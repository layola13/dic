imagin:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ | v. |想象，设想 |To form a mental picture or idea of something; to conceive of something.  |例句：I can imagine the difficulties you faced. 我能想象你面临的困难。|例句：Imagine a world without technology. 想象一个没有科技的世界。|近义词：conceive, envision, visualize |反义词：know, experience


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I imagine he's tired. = 我猜想他累了。
She imagines herself as a famous actress. = 她想象自己是一位著名的女演员。


一般过去时:
I imagined I was flying. = 我想象我正在飞翔。
They imagined a better future. = 他们设想了一个更美好的未来。


现在完成时:
I have imagined many scenarios. = 我已经设想了许多情况。
She has imagined this moment countless times. = 她无数次地想象过这一刻。


过去完成时:
I had imagined it would be easier. = 我曾以为它会更容易。
They had imagined the journey would be shorter. = 他们曾以为旅程会更短。


将来时:
I will imagine a solution. = 我将会设想一个解决方案。
She will imagine what it's like to be on Mars. = 她将会想象在火星上是什么样的感觉。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could imagine a way to solve this problem. = 我希望我能想象出一个解决这个问题的方法。
If I could imagine the future, I would. = 如果我能想象未来，我会的。


被动语态:
The future is often imagined as utopian. = 未来常常被想象成乌托邦。
A better world is imagined by many. = 许多人想象着一个更美好的世界。


疑问句:
Can you imagine what it's like? = 你能想象那是什么样的吗？
Do you imagine this will work? = 你认为这会奏效吗？


否定句:
I can't imagine why he did that. = 我无法想象他为什么那样做。
She doesn't imagine the consequences. = 她没有设想后果。


PHRASE->
imagine that = 想象一下
imagine if = 想象一下如果
just imagine = 想象一下
can you imagine = 你能想象吗
imagine doing something = 想象做某事
