nonconscriptions:/ˌnɒnkənˈskrɪpʃənz/ | n. | 非征兵；免除兵役 | The absence of conscription; a system or policy where individuals are not compelled to serve in the military.  This term often refers to a state of affairs where military service is voluntary, not mandatory. | Example:  Many countries have embraced nonconscriptions, allowing citizens to choose whether or not to join the armed forces.  许多国家已经采用非征兵制，允许公民选择是否加入军队。 |近义词：voluntary military service, exemption from conscription | 反义词：conscription, compulsory military service | 名词
