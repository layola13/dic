词源:
The word "imperishable" is derived from the prefix "im-" (not) + "perish" (to die or decay) + "-able" (capable of).  Therefore, "imperishable" means "not capable of perishing" or "not subject to decay."  "Imperishables" is the noun form, referring to things that are imperishable.
