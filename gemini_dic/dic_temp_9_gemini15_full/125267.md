multiethnic:/ˌmʌltiˈeθnɪk/ | adj. | 多民族的，多种族的 | Relating to or consisting of many ethnic groups.  It describes a society, community, or group that comprises people from diverse ethnic backgrounds. | Example: The United States is a multiethnic society with people from all over the world.  美国是一个由来自世界各地的人民组成的多民族社会。  The school has a multiethnic student body, reflecting the diversity of the neighborhood. 这所学校的学生来自多个民族，反映了社区的多样性。|近义词：multicultural, diverse, heterogeneous | 反义词：monolithic, homogeneous
