ses:/sɛz/ | n. | 会议；课程；学期 | sessions are periods of time set aside for a specific activity, such as a meeting, class, or legislative body.  |例句：The committee held several sessions to discuss the proposal.  委员会举行了数次会议讨论该提案。|例句：The summer session of classes begins next week.  暑期课程下周开始。|近义词：meeting, class, period |反义词：break, recess


USAGE->

This class session is very interactive. = 这次课非常互动。
