imprudently:/ɪmˈpruːdəntli/ | adv. | 不谨慎地；鲁莽地 | acting in a way that shows lack of good judgment; rashly. | He imprudently invested all his savings in a risky venture. 他不谨慎地将他所有的积蓄都投资到一个高风险的项目中。 | recklessly, rashly, thoughtlessly | cautiously, prudently, wisely | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imprudently spends his money. = 他不谨慎地花钱。
She imprudently shares her personal information online. = 她不谨慎地在网上分享个人信息。

一般过去时:
They imprudently ignored the warnings. = 他们不谨慎地忽视了警告。
He imprudently drove through the red light. = 他鲁莽地闯红灯。

现在完成时:
She has imprudently revealed a company secret. = 她不谨慎地泄露了一个公司秘密。
They have imprudently borrowed too much money. = 他们不谨慎地借了太多钱。

过去完成时:
He had imprudently promised more than he could deliver. = 他不谨慎地许诺了他无法兑现的事情。
They had imprudently invested in a failing company. = 他们不谨慎地投资了一家快要倒闭的公司。

将来时:
They will imprudently risk everything on one gamble. = 他们将不谨慎地把一切都押在一场赌博上。
He will imprudently reveal his plans to his competitors. = 他将不谨慎地向他的竞争对手透露他的计划。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't acted so imprudently, he wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果他不那么鲁莽行事，他就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I hadn't acted so imprudently. = 我真希望我没有那么鲁莽行事。

被动语态:
The decision was imprudently made without consulting experts. = 这个决定是不谨慎地做出的，没有咨询专家。
The project was imprudently undertaken without proper planning. = 该项目是不谨慎地启动的，没有进行适当的规划。

疑问句:
Did he imprudently reveal the company's secret? = 他是否不谨慎地泄露了公司的秘密？
Wasn't it imprudent to trust him so implicitly? =  如此完全地相信他难道不是不谨慎的吗？

否定句:
He did not act imprudently; he was careful in his approach. = 他没有鲁莽行事；他的方法很谨慎。
They did not imprudently disclose sensitive information. = 他们没有不谨慎地泄露敏感信息。
