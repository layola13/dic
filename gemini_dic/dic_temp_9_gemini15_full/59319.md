earfuls:/ˈɪrfʊlz/| n.|一大堆话，教训；令人厌烦的冗长谈话|A large amount of information, often unwanted or unpleasant, given in a continuous stream.  It implies a feeling of being overwhelmed or bombarded with words.|例句：I got an earful from my boss about my tardiness.  我因为迟到受到了老板的一顿训斥。


I received an earful from my mother about my messy room. 我因为房间乱被妈妈狠狠地训了一顿。|近义词：scolding, reprimand, lecture, tirade|反义词：praise, compliment, commendation|词性 n.
