pastured:/ˈpɑːstʃərd/ | adj. | 放牧的；以牧草喂养的 |  Used to describe animals that are kept in a field and fed on grass, or land used for grazing.  | Example: The pastured cattle were healthy and strong.  那些放牧的牛很健康强壮。  The farm's pastured lamb is known for its superior flavor. 这个农场的放牧羔羊以其优越的风味而闻名。|近义词：grazed, range-fed | 反义词：confined, stall-fed | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer pastures his sheep in the valley. = 农民在山谷里放牧他的羊。
The cows are pastured on the hillside. = 奶牛在山坡上放牧。


一般过去时:
The shepherd pastured his flock on the green hills. = 牧羊人把羊群放牧在绿色的山上。
They pastured their horses in the meadow last summer. = 去年夏天他们把马放牧在草地上。


现在完成时:
The farmer has pastured his cattle in this field for many years. = 农民多年来一直在这个田地里放牧他的牛。
We have pastured the sheep in the north pasture since spring. = 自春天以来，我们一直在北边牧场放牧羊群。


过去完成时:
They had pastured the horses in the lower fields before moving them to the higher ground. = 他们在把马移到较高的地方之前，已经把它们放在较低的田地里放牧了。
The farmer had pastured his sheep there for generations. = 农民世世代代都在那里放牧他的羊。


将来时:
We will pasture the goats in the newly seeded field next week. = 我们下周将在新播种的田地里放牧山羊。
The rancher will pasture his herd on the eastern slopes this spring. = 这位牧场主今年春天将在东坡放牧他的牛群。


虚拟语气:
If we pastured the sheep in a different field, they might be healthier. = 如果我们将羊群放在不同的田地里放牧，它们可能会更健康。
I wish we had pastured the horses closer to the barn. = 我希望我们把马放牧得离谷仓更近一些。


被动语态:
The sheep were pastured in the best fields. = 羊群被放牧在最好的田地里。
The cattle are being pastured on the rich lowlands. = 牛群正在肥沃的低地放牧。


疑问句:
Are the cows being pastured in the north field? = 奶牛是在北边的田地里放牧吗？
Where were the sheep pastured last year? = 羊群去年在哪里放牧？


否定句:
The farmer did not pasture his cows in that field. = 农民没有在他的田地里放牧他的奶牛。
They haven't pastured the horses there this year. = 他们今年没有在那里放牧马。
