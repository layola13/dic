outbrazen:/ˌaʊtˈbreɪzən/| vt.|以厚颜无耻胜过；以大胆超过|To surpass in boldness or impudence.  It implies exceeding someone else's brazen behavior.|例句：He tried to outbrazen the bully, but it didn't work. 他试图以厚颜无耻胜过那个恶霸，但没成功。

例句：She outbrazened her opponent in the debate, winning the argument through sheer audacity.  她在辩论中以大胆超过了对手，凭借其大胆获得了胜利。


近义词：outshine, outface, outdare|反义词：yield, submit, cower|动词
