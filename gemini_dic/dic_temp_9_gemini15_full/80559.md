goodwin:/ˈɡʊdwɪn/| n. |古德温（姓氏）|Goodwin is a surname of English origin.  It is derived from the Old English personal name Godwine, composed of the elements "god" (god) and "wine" (friend).  Therefore, it literally means "God's friend". The surname is relatively common in England and has spread to other English-speaking countries through migration.|例句：Many famous people share the surname Goodwin.  许多名人都有古德温这个姓氏。|近义词: (无直接近义词，因为是姓氏)|反义词: (无直接反义词，因为是姓氏)|名词
