intracoastal: /ˌɪntrəˈkoʊstl/ | adj. | 内陆沿海的；沿内陆水道的 | Relating to or situated on a waterway, such as a sound or bay, that lies parallel to the coast and is partially enclosed by land.  It refers to a waterway that runs parallel to the coastline, often sheltered from the open ocean. | The intracoastal waterway provides a sheltered route for boats. 内陆水道为船只提供了一条安全的航线。 | coastal, littoral |  oceanic, offshore | adj.
