refrigerated:/rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtɪd/| adj.|冷藏的，冷冻的| Kept at a low temperature to preserve food.  Refrigerated trucks transport perishable goods across long distances.|近义词：chilled, cooled, frozen|反义词：heated, warmed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The restaurant keeps its food refrigerated. = 这家餐厅将食物冷藏保存。
This milk needs to be refrigerated. = 这牛奶需要冷藏。


一般过去时:
The leftovers were refrigerated after dinner. = 晚饭后剩菜被冷藏了。
The scientist refrigerated the samples immediately. = 科学家立即冷藏了样本。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
