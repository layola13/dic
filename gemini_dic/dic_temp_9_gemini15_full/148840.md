overwander:/ˌoʊvərˈwɑːndər/ | v. | 漫游，闲逛，徘徊 | To wander or roam beyond a certain limit or for an extended period.  It implies a lack of purpose or direction, often suggesting a state of being lost or aimless. |  例句：The lost hiker overwandered for days before finally finding his way back to civilization. 迷路的徒步旅行者在最终找到回文明社会的路之前徘徊了好几天。


例句：He overwandered the countryside, losing track of time and direction. 他在乡下漫无目的地游荡，迷失了时间和方向。


近义词：ramble, roam, stray, wander, drift


反义词：stay put, remain stationary
