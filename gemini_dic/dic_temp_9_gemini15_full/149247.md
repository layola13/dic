oyez:/ˈoʊjeɪ/| int.| 肃静；听我说 |  A call for attention, especially in a court of law.  It is an archaic form of the French word "oyez," meaning "hear."  | Example:  "Oyez, oyez, oyez!  All persons having business before this honorable court, draw near and give attention." = 肃静！肃静！肃静！凡有公务在尊贵的法庭上的人，请靠近并注意。 | 近义词：Hear ye!, Listen!, Attention! | 反义词：None. |  int.
