muler:/ˈmjuːlər/ | n. |骡子；骡夫；（尤指）走私者 | A mule is a hybrid animal, the offspring of a male donkey (jack) and a female horse (mare).  The term can also refer to a person who transports illegal goods, especially drugs. |例句：The farmer used mules to plow his fields.  这个农民用骡子耕地。

The smugglers used mules to transport the cocaine across the border. 走私者用骡子将可卡因运过边境。|近义词：hybrid, pack animal, transporter, courier |反义词：horse, donkey (as pure breeds)
