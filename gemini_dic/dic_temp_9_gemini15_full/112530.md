love's:/lʌvz/| n.|爱的；爱情的|The possessive form of "love," indicating belonging or possession.  It's used to show that something belongs to or is associated with love.|例句：Love's labor's lost. =  爱的劳动是徒劳的。|例句：Love's young dream. = 青涩的爱情。|近义词：love's own,  heart's|反义词：hate's, loathing's|名词


USAGE->
This word is a possessive form and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It functions as an adjective modifying a noun.


PHRASE->
love's labour's lost =  徒劳的爱
love's tender grace = 爱的温柔恩典
love's sweet song = 爱的甜蜜歌声
love's first bloom = 初恋
love's bitter truth = 爱的残酷真相
