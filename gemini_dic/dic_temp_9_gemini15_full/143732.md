outloose:/ˌaʊtˈluːs/| adj.|放荡的；不受约束的；自由奔放的|Unrestrained; free from the usual restraints of convention or law; dissolute.  The word suggests a lack of control and often implies negative connotations, particularly in relation to behavior.|例句：His outloose lifestyle led to many problems.他的放荡生活导致了许多问题。


例句：The outloose horses galloped across the plains.  不受约束的马匹在平原上飞奔。


近义词：unrestrained, unbridled, licentious, dissolute


反义词：restrained, controlled, disciplined, proper


词性：形容词
