parachronism:/ˈpærəˌkrɒnɪzəm/| n. |年代错置；时代错误；时代不符|Anachronism in time; placing something in the wrong time period.  It refers to an error in chronology, where something is placed in a time period where it doesn't belong. This could be an object, event, or idea. |Example: The depiction of a cell phone in a historical drama set in the 18th century is a parachronism.|近义词：anachronism|反义词：chronological accuracy
