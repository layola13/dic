detonating:/ˈdɛtəneɪtɪŋ/| v. |引爆；爆炸；使爆炸|The act of causing something to explode violently.  It often refers to the process of initiating an explosion in a controlled manner, such as with explosives. |例句：The bomb detonated unexpectedly, causing significant damage. 炸弹意外爆炸，造成了巨大的损失。

The engineers carefully detonated the remaining explosives. 工程师们小心地引爆了剩余的炸药。


近义词：exploding, blasting, igniting|反义词：defusing, disabling
