misalloting: /ˌmɪsˈæləʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 错误分配，误分配 | The act of assigning or allocating something incorrectly or inappropriately.  It implies a mistake in the distribution of resources, tasks, or responsibilities. | 例句：The company's misalloting of funds led to a significant budget shortfall. 公司资金分配错误导致预算严重短缺。

The project manager's misalloting of tasks resulted in delays and inefficiencies. 项目经理任务分配错误导致延误和效率低下。 | 近义词：misappropriating, misdistributing, maldistributing | 反义词：allocating properly, distributing fairly
