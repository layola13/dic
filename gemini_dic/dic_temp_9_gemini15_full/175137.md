raftsman:/ˈræftsmən/| n. |筏工；木排工；（尤指）木匠|A raftsman is a person who builds or works with rafts.  They are skilled in constructing and navigating rafts, often on rivers or lakes. The term can also be used more broadly to refer to a skilled carpenter, particularly one who works with wood in a less refined or specialized manner than a cabinetmaker or joiner. |Example: The raftsman skillfully guided the raft down the rushing river. 筏工熟练地将木筏引向下游奔腾的河水。|近义词：raftsperson, riverman, boatman, carpenter (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying refined woodworking skills could be considered contrasting.
