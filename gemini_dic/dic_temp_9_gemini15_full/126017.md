mosquito:/məˈskiːtoʊ/| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect with a long proboscis for sucking blood, typically found near water and biting humans and animals.  Mosquitoes are vectors for diseases such as malaria, dengue fever, and Zika virus.|例句：The mosquito buzzed annoyingly around my ear.  这只蚊子在我耳边嗡嗡作响，真烦人。|例句：Several species of mosquito are found in this region.  这个地区发现了多种蚊子。|近义词：gnat (smaller)|反义词:None
