pathogenesis:/ˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |发病机制；致病机理|The development of a disease; the biological mechanisms that lead to a diseased state.  It encompasses the factors that contribute to the initiation and progression of a disease.  Pathogenesis can involve genetic predisposition, environmental factors, infectious agents, and immune responses.|Example: The pathogenesis of Alzheimer's disease is still not fully understood.|近义词：etiology, pathophysiology|反义词：cure, remission
