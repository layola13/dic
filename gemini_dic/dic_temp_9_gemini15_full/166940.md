prestocking:/ˈpriːˌstɑːkɪŋ/| n. |预先储存，预先备货|The act of stocking something in advance of anticipated demand.  It's a proactive inventory management strategy aimed at preventing stockouts and ensuring sufficient supply to meet customer demands.  |例句：The supermarket engaged in prestocking to prepare for the holiday rush.  超市进行预先备货以应对节假日购物高峰。


例句：The company's prestocking strategy prevented a shortage of essential components during peak production. 公司的预先备货策略避免了在生产高峰期出现关键部件短缺。


近义词：pre-positioning, advance stocking, forward stocking |反义词：just-in-time stocking, reactive stocking |名词
