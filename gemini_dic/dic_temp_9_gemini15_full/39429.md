connected:/kəˈnɛktɪd/| adj.|连接的；相关的；互联的|Being joined or linked together; showing a relationship or connection.|例句：The two towns are connected by a new bridge. 这两个城镇由一座新桥连接。|例句：His success is connected to his hard work. 他的成功与他的努力有关。|近义词：linked, related, associated, joined|反义词：disconnected, separate, unrelated, isolated

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cities are connected by a high-speed rail. = 这些城市由一条高速铁路连接。
The events are connected in a surprising way. = 这些事件以一种令人惊讶的方式联系在一起。


一般过去时:
The two computers were connected via a network cable. = 两台电脑通过网线连接。
The dots were connected to reveal a hidden message. = 将点连接起来以揭示隐藏的消息。


现在完成时:
The researchers have connected the two phenomena. = 研究人员已经将这两种现象联系起来。
We have connected to the Wi-Fi network successfully. = 我们已经成功连接到Wi-Fi网络。


过去完成时:
The police had connected the suspect to the crime scene. = 警方已将嫌疑人与犯罪现场联系起来。
They had connected all the wires before testing the circuit. = 他们在测试电路之前已经连接好所有电线。


将来时:
The new highway will connect the two regions. = 新高速公路将连接这两个地区。
The two companies will be connected through a merger. = 这两家公司将通过合并而联系起来。


虚拟语气:
If the wires were connected properly, the device would work. = 如果电线连接正确，设备就会工作。
I wish the two ideas were more connected. = 我希望这两个想法能更紧密地联系起来。


被动语态:
The houses are connected to the main water supply. = 房屋连接到主要供水系统。
The system was connected remotely by a technician. = 该系统由技术人员远程连接。


疑问句:
Are the two events connected? = 这两个事件有联系吗？
Is your computer connected to the internet? = 你的电脑连接到互联网了吗？


否定句:
The two concepts are not connected. = 这两个概念没有联系。
The printer is not connected to the computer. = 打印机没有连接到电脑。
