erne:/ˈɜːrn/| n.|海雕|A large bird of prey, typically with a brown body and a white head and tail.  Ernes are known for their powerful talons and sharp beaks, which they use to hunt fish and other animals.|Example: The erne soared high above the cliffs, its sharp eyes scanning the ocean below.  那只海雕在悬崖峭壁上空翱翔，它锐利的目光扫描着下面的海洋。|近义词：eagle, hawk|反义词：None specifically
