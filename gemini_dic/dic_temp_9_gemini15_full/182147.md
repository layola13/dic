remixes:/ˈriːmɪksɪz/| n.|混音版本；混音作品|A remix is a modified version of a song or piece of music, often created by a different artist or DJ. It typically involves changing the arrangement, tempo, and instrumentation of the original track.|例句：The DJ played a remix of the popular song.  DJ播放了这首流行歌曲的混音版本。|例句：This album includes several remixes by different artists. 这张专辑包含了几位不同艺术家制作的混音版本。|近义词：re-edits, versions, adaptations|反义词：originals
