out-greened:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnd/| v. | 被比下去，被超越(在环保方面) |  To surpass or exceed someone else in terms of environmental friendliness or sustainability. This is a relatively new word, formed by adding the prefix "out-" to the verb "greened," implying to surpass someone in their efforts concerning environmental protection.  | Example:  "Their new eco-friendly factory completely out-greened the competition." = "他们新的环保工厂在环保方面完全超越了竞争对手。" | 近义词: outdo, surpass, outperform, eclipse | 反义词: be outdone, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company out-greens its competitors in sustainable practices. = 这家公司在可持续发展实践方面胜过其竞争对手。
Our new initiative out-greens all previous attempts at environmental conservation. = 我们新的倡议在环境保护方面超越了以往所有尝试。


一般过去时:
Last year, our project out-greened all other similar projects in the region. = 去年，我们的项目在该地区所有其他类似项目中脱颖而出。
Their innovative approach out-greened the traditional methods of waste management. = 他们创新的方法超越了传统的废物管理方法。


现在完成时:
They have out-greened their rivals in the race to develop eco-friendly technology. = 在开发环保技术的竞赛中，他们已经超越了竞争对手。
Our city has out-greened many other cities by implementing a comprehensive recycling program. = 通过实施全面的回收计划，我们的城市在环保方面已经超越了许多其他城市。


过去完成时:
By the end of the decade, the company had out-greened its competitors in terms of carbon footprint reduction. = 到十年结束时，该公司在减少碳足迹方面已经超越了竞争对手。
Before the new regulations were implemented, the factory had not out-greened its rivals. = 在实施新的法规之前，这家工厂并没有超越其竞争对手。


将来时:
We will out-green our competitors by investing heavily in renewable energy. = 我们将通过大力投资可再生能源来超越我们的竞争对手。
Their ambitious plan promises that they will out-green the current industry standard. = 他们雄心勃勃的计划承诺他们将超越目前的行业标准。


虚拟语气:
If we invested more in green technology, we would out-green the competition. = 如果我们对绿色技术投资更多，我们将超越竞争对手。
I wish our company had out-greened its rivals sooner. = 我希望我们公司早点超越竞争对手。


被动语态:
Our efforts were out-greened by the innovative approach of a smaller startup. = 我们的努力被一家小型初创公司的创新方法所超越。
Their sustainability initiatives were out-greened by a competitor's bold new program. = 他们的可持续发展倡议被竞争对手的大胆新计划所超越。


疑问句:
Did their new strategy really out-green the previous one? = 他们的新战略真的比之前的更好吗？
Will our new project out-green the existing infrastructure? = 我们新的项目会比现有的基础设施更环保吗？


否定句:
Their efforts did not out-green those of their main competitor. = 他们的努力没有超越其主要竞争对手。
Our company has not yet out-greened the industry leader in terms of sustainable practices. = 就可持续发展实践而言，我们公司尚未超越行业领导者。


PHRASE->
out-greened the competition  = 超越竞争对手
out-greened the market = 超越市场
out-greened the expectations = 超越预期
out-greened the standards = 超越标准
out-greened the industry = 超越行业
