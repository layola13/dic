rewardless:/rɪˈwɔːrdləs/| adj.|无益的；无回报的；不值得的 | Not providing or resulting in any reward.  |例句：He found the job rewardless and demoralizing. 他发现这份工作毫无回报且令人沮丧。

例句：Their efforts proved rewardless in the end. 他们的努力最终证明是徒劳的。


近义词：unrewarding, unproductive, fruitless, unprofitable, thankless

反义词：rewarding, profitable, fruitful, worthwhile
