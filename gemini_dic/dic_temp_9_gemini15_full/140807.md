on-field: /ˌɒnˈfiːld/ | adj. | 实况的；现场的；球场上的 |  Relating to or occurring on the field of play, especially in a sport.  It emphasizes the action happening directly within the playing area, as opposed to off-field matters like management or strategy. |

例句：

The on-field performance of the team was disappointing, despite their strong off-field preparations. = 球队的场上表现令人失望，尽管他们在场下做了充分的准备。

The on-field injuries have depleted the team's strength. = 球场上的伤病削弱了球队的实力。


近义词：live, in-game, in-play


反义词：off-field


词性：形容词
