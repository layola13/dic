noncommunicatively:/ˌnɒnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪvli/ | adv. | 不善于沟通地；沉默寡言地 | In a way that does not communicate or fails to convey information or feelings.  It describes actions or behavior that lack communication or expression. | He sat noncommunicatively in the corner, refusing to answer any questions. 他默默地坐在角落里，拒绝回答任何问题。 | communicatively, expressively | uncommunicative, taciturn


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  Its usage is always to modify a verb, describing how an action is performed.


Example 1:
He responded noncommunicatively to the difficult question. = 他对那个难题的回答含糊其辞。

Example 2:
She stared noncommunicatively at the painting. = 她默默地凝视着那幅画。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "noncommunicatively".  Its usage is generally standalone to modify a verb describing behavior.
