premiering:/prɪˈmɪəriŋ/| v.| 首映，首次公演 | To present (a film, play, etc.) for the first time publicly.  |例句：The new movie is premiering tonight at the local cinema.  今晚新电影将在当地电影院首映。

例句：The play premiered on Broadway last week and received rave reviews.  该剧上周在百老汇首演，并获得好评如潮。


近义词：debuting, opening, launching, unveiling

反义词：closing, ending


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The film is premiering next week. = 电影下周首映。
They are premiering their new play at the festival. = 他们在节日上首演他们的新剧。

一般过去时:
The movie premiered last night. = 电影昨晚首映。
The band premiered their new album at a sold-out concert. = 乐队在爆满的音乐会上首演了他们的新专辑。

现在完成时:
The director has premiered three films this year. = 导演今年已经首映了三部电影。
They have premiered their new musical to critical acclaim. = 他们首演的新音乐剧获得了评论界的赞誉。

过去完成时:
The play had premiered several times before it reached Broadway. = 这部剧在百老汇演出之前已经首演过几次了。
Before the concert, the band had premiered the new song at a smaller venue. = 音乐会之前，乐队在一个较小的场馆首演了这首新歌。

将来时:
The show will be premiering in London next month. = 该剧将于下个月在伦敦首映。
He will premiere his new collection at Paris Fashion Week. = 他将在巴黎时装周上首映他的新系列。


虚拟语气:
If the movie premiered earlier, it would have been more successful. = 如果电影早点首映，它会更成功。
I wish the play had premiered to a larger audience. = 我希望这部戏能有更多观众观看首映。

被动语态:
The film was premiered at the Cannes Film Festival. = 这部电影在戛纳电影节上首映。
The new song is being premiered on the radio this morning. = 这首新歌今天早上正在电台上首映。

疑问句:
When is the new season premiering? = 新剧季什么时候首映？
Where will the play be premiering? = 这部戏将在哪里首映？

否定句:
The film did not premiere as scheduled. = 电影没有按计划首映。
The play has not yet premiered in this country. = 这部剧还没有在这个国家首映。
