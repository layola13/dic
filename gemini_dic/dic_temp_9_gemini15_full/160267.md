pony-tail: /ˈpəʊni teɪl/| n. |马尾辫|A hairstyle in which the hair is pulled back and tied at the nape of the neck, resembling a pony's tail.|She wore her hair in a high pony-tail.|近义词：pigtail, ponytail|反义词:loose hair
