similarity:/ˌsɪməˈlærəti/| n. |相似性，类似 |The similarity between the two paintings is striking.  两幅画之间的相似之处令人惊叹。|近义词：likeness, resemblance, correspondence |反义词：difference, dissimilarity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The similarity between A and B is obvious. = A和B之间的相似性很明显。
There is a strong similarity in their approaches. = 他们的方法有很强的相似性。

一般过去时:
The similarity between the two events was noted by the historians. = 历史学家们注意到了这两个事件之间的相似性。
A striking similarity existed between the two cases. = 这两个案例之间存在着惊人的相似之处。

现在完成时:
Researchers have discovered a significant similarity in DNA sequences. = 研究人员已经发现了DNA序列中显著的相似性。
We have noticed many similarities in their personalities. = 我们已经注意到他们性格中的许多相似之处。

过去完成时:
By the end of the investigation, they had found many similarities between the suspects. = 到调查结束时，他们已经发现了嫌疑人之间的许多相似之处。
Scientists had already observed similarities in the behavior of the two species. = 科学家们已经观察到这两个物种行为上的相似之处。

将来时:
Future studies will likely reveal further similarities. = 未来的研究可能会揭示更多的相似之处。
We will find more similarities as we continue our research. = 当我们继续研究时，我们将发现更多的相似之处。


虚拟语气:
If there were more similarities, we could draw a stronger conclusion. = 如果有更多相似之处，我们就能得出更强的结论。
I wish there were fewer similarities between the two products, to avoid confusion. = 我希望这两个产品之间的相似之处更少，以避免混淆。

被动语态:
The similarities were highlighted in the report. = 报告中强调了这些相似之处。
Many similarities are being studied by scientists. = 许多相似之处正在被科学家们研究。

疑问句:
What are the similarities between these two theories? = 这两个理论之间有哪些相似之处？
Are there any similarities between your project and mine? = 你的项目和我的项目之间有什么相似之处吗？

否定句:
There is no similarity between the two events. = 这两件事之间没有任何相似之处。
The report failed to show any significant similarities. = 该报告未能显示任何显著的相似之处。
