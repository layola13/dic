refrigerate:/rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪt/ | vt. | 冷藏；冷冻 | To make or keep something cold, typically by using a refrigerator.  |例句：Please refrigerate the milk after opening.  请开封后冷藏牛奶。

例句：We need to refrigerate the leftovers to prevent them from spoiling. 我们需要冷藏剩菜剩饭以防止它们变质。


近义词：chill, cool, freeze | 反义词：heat, warm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I refrigerate the milk every day. = 我每天都把牛奶冷藏。
He refrigerates the leftovers after dinner. = 他晚饭后把剩菜剩饭冷藏起来。

一般过去时:
I refrigerated the cake before the party. = 我在派对之前把蛋糕冷藏起来了。
She refrigerated the soup to cool it down. = 她把汤冷藏起来使其冷却。

现在完成时:
I have refrigerated the medicine as instructed. = 我按照指示把药冷藏起来了。
They have refrigerated the ingredients for the cake. = 他们已经把蛋糕的配料冷藏起来了。

过去完成时:
I had refrigerated the wine before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前我已经把酒冷藏好了。
She had refrigerated the vegetables overnight. = 她已经把蔬菜冷藏了一夜。

将来时:
I will refrigerate the pizza after it cools. = 比萨冷却后我会把它冷藏起来。
He will refrigerate the leftovers for tomorrow's lunch. = 他会把剩菜剩饭冷藏起来作为明天的午餐。

虚拟语气:
If I had refrigerated the meat, it wouldn't have spoiled. = 如果我把肉冷藏起来，它就不会变质了。
I wish I had refrigerated the drinks earlier. = 我真希望我早点把饮料冷藏起来。

被动语态:
The milk should be refrigerated. = 牛奶应该冷藏。
The food is being refrigerated to maintain its freshness. = 食物正在被冷藏以保持新鲜。

疑问句:
Should I refrigerate this salad? = 我应该把这个沙拉冷藏吗？

否定句:

词源:
The word "refrigerate" comes from the Latin word "refrigerare," meaning "to cool."  "Refrigerare" is formed from "re-" (again, back) and "frigidus" (cold).  Thus, the word literally means "to make cold again."
