词源:
The word "daughter-in-law" is a compound word formed from "daughter" and "in-law."  "Daughter" refers to a female offspring. "In-law" is a term used to denote a relative by marriage.  The "in" signifies the relationship is established through marriage, specifically the marriage of one's son.
