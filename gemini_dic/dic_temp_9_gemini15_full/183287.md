reprise:/rɪˈpriːz/| n. v.| 重演；重播；重复；再现 |To repeat a performance or piece of music, or to repeat something from the past.  |The orchestra gave a stunning reprise of the first movement.  乐团对第一乐章进行了精彩的重演。  |The singer gave a powerful reprise of her hit song. 歌手对其热门歌曲进行了强有力的再演。| repetition, reiteration | original, first time |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band reprises its popular song at every concert. = 乐队在每场演唱会上都会重演其热门歌曲。
She reprises her role as the leading actress in the sequel. = 她在续集中再次扮演女主角。


一般过去时:
The actor reprised his famous role from the original play. = 这位演员重演了他从原创剧中扮演的著名角色。
They reprised the successful marketing campaign from last year. = 他们重现了去年成功的营销活动。


现在完成时:
The company has reprised its successful product line. = 公司已经重新推出了其成功的产品线。
The director has reprised many themes from his previous films. = 该导演在其之前的电影中重现了许多主题。


过去完成时:
The artist had reprised his most famous painting in a new style. = 这位艺术家以一种新的风格重现了他最著名的绘画作品。
Before the concert, the band had reprised some of their old songs for practice. = 音乐会在开始之前，乐队已经练习过一些旧歌。


将来时:
The musician will reprise his role in the upcoming musical. = 音乐家将在即将上演的音乐剧中重演他的角色。
The company will reprise its advertising campaign next year. = 公司将在明年重新启动其广告宣传活动。


虚拟语气:
If they reprised the play, it would likely be a success. = 如果他们重演这部戏剧，很可能会成功。
I wish they would reprise that amazing concert. = 我希望他们能重演那场精彩的音乐会。


被动语态:
The song was reprised by several other artists. = 这首歌被其他几位艺术家重演。
The classic scene from the movie was reprised in the sequel. = 电影中的经典场景在续集中被重演。


疑问句:
Will the band reprise any of its older songs? = 乐队会重演任何旧歌吗？
Did the actress reprise her famous role in the new movie? = 女演员在新电影中重演了她著名的角色吗？


否定句:
The band did not reprise any of its older songs. = 乐队没有重演任何旧歌。
They chose not to reprise the experiment. = 他们选择不重复实验。
