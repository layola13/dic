patriotism:/ˈpeɪtriəˌtɪzəm/| n. |爱国主义；爱国心 |The feeling of love, devotion, and sense of attachment to one's country.  It often involves a sense of national pride and a willingness to sacrifice for the good of one's nation.  |例句：Her patriotism led her to join the army.  她的爱国心促使她参军。
例句：The speech was intended to inspire patriotism. 这篇演讲旨在激发爱国热情。|近义词：nationalism, loyalty, allegiance |反义词：treason, sedition

词源:
From Middle French *patriotisme, from Latin patria ("fatherland," "native country") +‎ -ism.  The Latin word *patria itself is derived from pater ("father").  The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, system, or practice.
