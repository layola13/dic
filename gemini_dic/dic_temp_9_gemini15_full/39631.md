consequent-to:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt tuː/| adj. |由于…；作为…的结果|resulting from; as a consequence of|The consequent-to decision was to postpone the meeting.  由于…的决定是推迟会议。|The project's failure was consequent-to poor planning. 项目的失败是由于计划不周造成的。|resulting from, stemming from, arising from|inconsequent, unrelated


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The illness is consequent to a viral infection. = 这种疾病是由病毒感染引起的。
His success is consequent to hard work and dedication. = 他的成功源于努力和奉献。


一般过去时:
The accident was consequent to reckless driving. = 事故是由于鲁莽驾驶造成的。
Her happiness was consequent to receiving good news. = 她的快乐是由于收到好消息而引起的。


现在完成时:
The damage has been consequent to the storm. = 损害是由风暴造成的。
His improved health has been consequent to a change in diet. = 他的健康改善是由于改变饮食造成的。


过去完成时:
The delay had been consequent to unforeseen circumstances. = 延误是由于不可预见的情况造成的。
Her anxiety had been consequent to the uncertainty. = 她的焦虑是由于不确定性造成的。


将来时:
The increase in prices will be consequent to rising inflation. = 物价上涨将是通货膨胀上升的结果。
The success of the project will be consequent to effective teamwork. = 项目的成功将取决于有效的团队合作。


虚拟语气:
If the project were consequent to sufficient funding, it would succeed. = 如果项目有足够的资金支持，它就会成功。
If the decision were consequent to careful consideration, it would be better. = 如果这个决定是经过仔细考虑做出的，那就好了。


被动语态:
The decision was consequent to a thorough investigation. = 该决定是经过彻底调查后做出的。
The delay was consequent to unexpected problems. = 延误是由于意外问题造成的。


疑问句:
Was the failure consequent to negligence? = 失败是由于疏忽造成的吗？
Is this problem consequent to the recent changes? = 这个问题是最近的变化造成的吗？


否定句:
The success was not consequent to luck alone. = 成功并非仅仅是运气好。
The accident was not consequent to mechanical failure. = 事故并非由于机械故障造成的。
