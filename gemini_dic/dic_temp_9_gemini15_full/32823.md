chokers:/ˈtʃoʊkərz/ | n. | 颈圈；扼杀者 | A choker is a piece of jewelry worn around the neck, often tight-fitting.  It can also refer to something that restricts or suffocates. |例句：She wore a beautiful pearl choker to the gala. 她戴着一条美丽的珍珠项链参加了盛会。  The thick smoke acted as a choker in the burning building.浓烟在燃烧的建筑物里像扼杀者一样。|近义词： necklace, collar, strangler |反义词：  loosener (in the sense of something that restricts)
