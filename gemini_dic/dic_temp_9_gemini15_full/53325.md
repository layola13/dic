disacceptance:/dɪsækˈseptəns/| n. |不接受，拒收，否定|The act of not accepting something; rejection.  It can refer to the refusal to accept a bill of exchange, a proposed idea, or a person's behavior. |例句：The bank's disacceptance of the check caused a financial crisis for the small business.  银行拒收支票导致这家小企业陷入财务危机。


The company's disacceptance of our proposal was a major setback. 公司拒绝我们的提案是一个重大挫折。|近义词：rejection, refusal, repudiation |反义词：acceptance, approval
