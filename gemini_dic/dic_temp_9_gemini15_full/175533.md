rancher:/ˈræntʃər/| n.|牧场主，农场主|A rancher is a person who owns or works on a ranch, especially one raising livestock.  Ranchers manage large areas of land, often in rural or western settings, and are responsible for the care and raising of animals such as cattle, sheep, or horses.|例句：The rancher proudly showed off his prize-winning cattle.  这位牧场主骄傲地展示了他获奖的牛群。

例句：Many ranchers in the region are facing challenges due to drought.  该地区的许多牧场主都面临着干旱带来的挑战。|近义词：farmer, cattleman, stockman|反义词：city dweller
