releasable:/rɪˈliːsəbl/ | adj. | 可释放的；可解开的；可发布的 | Capable of being released or discharged.  Something that is releasable can be let go, set free, or made available to the public. |例句：The software is now releasable to the public.  这个软件现在可以向公众发布了。
The emergency brake is easily releasable. 紧急刹车很容易释放。

近义词：dischargeable, free-able, publishable
反义词：unreleasable, fixed, irretrievable
