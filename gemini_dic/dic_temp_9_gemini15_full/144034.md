outride:/ˌaʊtˈraɪd/| v.|超越，胜过，骑马超过|To ride faster or farther than (someone or something).  It can also imply surpassing someone in skill or achievement.|例句：He outrode all his competitors in the race. 他在比赛中超越了所有竞争对手。

例句：The experienced cyclist easily outrode the novice.  经验丰富的自行车手轻松地超过了新手。


近义词：outspeed, surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：be outridden by, be surpassed by

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outrides his opponents every time. = 他每次都超越他的对手。
She outrides the challenges presented to her. = 她克服了她面临的挑战。


一般过去时:
They outrode the storm. = 他们顶住了暴风雨。
He outrode the others in the cycling competition. = 他在自行车比赛中超过了其他人。


现在完成时:
She has outridden many difficulties in her life. = 她一生中克服了许多困难。
He has outridden the competition to secure first place. = 他在竞争中胜出，获得了第一名。


过去完成时:
They had outridden the initial setbacks and were on track to success. = 他们克服了最初的挫折，并走上了成功的道路。
She had outridden her fears and finally faced her challenge. = 她克服了恐惧，最终面对了挑战。


将来时:
They will outride the current economic downturn. = 他们将会克服当前的经济低迷。
He will outride his competitors and win the championship. = 他将超越竞争对手，赢得冠军。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he might outride his rivals. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他可能会超越他的对手。
If she had prepared better, she could have outridden the storm. = 如果她准备得更好，她就能克服这场暴风雨。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
