/ˌoʊvərˈhiːps/ | adj. |堆积如山的；过多的 | Extremely numerous or abundant; excessive.  | The garden was over-heaps with wildflowers. 花园里野花堆积如山。|近义词：abundant, overflowing, excessive | 反义词：scarce, deficient, meager

USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in your example.  The usage focuses on describing the quantity of something.

例句1: There were over-heaps of toys scattered across the floor. 地板上散落着堆积如山的玩具。
例句2:  The farmer's harvest was over-heaps this year, leading to lower prices. 今年农民的收成过多，导致价格下降。


PHRASE->
over-heaps of...  = 堆积如山的...
例句1:  She had over-heaps of homework to do. 她有堆积如山的作业要做。
例句2: The store had over-heaps of Christmas decorations. 商店里堆积如山的圣诞装饰品。

over-heaps with... =  充满...；堆满了...
例句1:  The table was over-heaps with food. 桌子上堆满了食物。
例句2: The room was over-heaps with books. 房间里堆满了书。

over-heaps and beyond = 多到超出...
例句1: His kindness went over-heaps and beyond expectations. 他的善良超出了预期。
例句2:  The damage was over-heaps and beyond repair. 损坏程度难以修复。


词源:
The word is a compound adjective formed from "over" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) and "heaps" (meaning a large, untidy pile).  It's a relatively informal word.
