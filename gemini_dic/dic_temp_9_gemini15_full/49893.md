demurrage:/dɪˈmʌrɪdʒ/ | n. |滞期费；装卸费 |  The charge levied for the detention of a vessel or cargo beyond the time allowed.  Demurrage compensates the owner for the loss of use of their asset. | The shipping company charged us hefty demurrage for the late arrival of the goods.  = 船公司因货物到达延误而向我们收取了巨额滞期费。 The importer was responsible for paying the demurrage. = 进口商负责支付滞期费。|  delay charges, detention charges |  prompt payment, on-time delivery | 名词
