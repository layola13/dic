cresses:/ˈkrɛsɪz/| n.|豆瓣菜，水田芥|Small edible plants with a peppery taste, often eaten in salads.  They are a type of cruciferous vegetable. |例句：I added some cresses to my salad for extra flavor. 我在我的沙拉里加了一些豆瓣菜来增加风味。

例句：The cresses were freshly picked from the garden. 豆瓣菜是从花园里新鲜采摘的。

近义词：watercress (更具体的指一种豆瓣菜)

反义词：无
