prepavements:/ˈpriːˌpeɪvmənts/| n.|预制路面；预制铺面|Prepavements are sections of pavement constructed off-site and then installed.  They are often used in large-scale projects to speed up construction.|例句：The construction crew used prepavements to quickly lay down the new road.  施工队使用预制路面来快速铺设新路。|近义词：precast pavement, prefabricated pavement|反义词：in-situ pavement|名词
