/ˌɡæstroʊdɪsk/| n. |胃镜检查|A gastrodisc is a medical device used to perform a gastroscopy, a procedure involving inserting a thin, flexible tube with a camera on the end into the stomach through the esophagus to visualize and examine the stomach lining.  This helps diagnose various stomach conditions. |Example: The doctor recommended a gastrodisc to examine the source of the patient's persistent stomach pain.  医生建议进行胃镜检查以检查病人持续性胃痛的根源。|近义词:gastroscopy, endoscopy (of the stomach)|反义词:|N
