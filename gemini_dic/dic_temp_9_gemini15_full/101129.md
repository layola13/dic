investigates:/ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪts/| v.|调查，研究|To carry out a systematic or formal inquiry to discover and examine the facts of an incident, allegation, or claim.|例句：The police are investigating the murder. 警方正在调查这起谋杀案。|例句：Scientists are investigating the causes of climate change. 科学家们正在调查气候变化的原因。|近义词：examines, explores, probes, inquires|反义词：ignores, neglects


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The detective investigates the crime scene carefully. = 侦探仔细调查了犯罪现场。
She investigates the matter thoroughly before making a decision. = 她会在做决定之前彻底调查此事。

一般过去时:
The committee investigated the allegations of misconduct. = 委员会调查了不当行为的指控。
He investigated the problem and found a solution. = 他调查了这个问题并找到了解决办法。

现在完成时:
The police have investigated several leads but haven't found the suspect yet. = 警方已经调查了几条线索，但尚未找到嫌疑人。
Scientists have investigated the effects of pollution on marine life for decades. = 科学家们几十年来一直在研究污染对海洋生物的影响。

过去完成时:
They had investigated the issue extensively before presenting their findings. = 他们在提交调查结果之前已经广泛地调查了这个问题。
The company had investigated the safety concerns before launching the product. = 公司在推出产品之前已经调查了安全问题。

将来时:
The authorities will investigate the plane crash thoroughly. = 当局将彻底调查这起飞机坠毁事件。
She will investigate the possibility of a new marketing strategy. = 她将研究新的营销策略的可能性。

虚拟语气:
If the police investigated more thoroughly, they might find the culprit. = 如果警方更彻底地调查，他们可能会找到罪犯。
I wish the government investigated corruption more aggressively. = 我希望政府更积极地打击腐败。

被动语态:
The case is being investigated by the FBI. = 此案正在由联邦调查局调查。
The matter will be investigated by an independent panel. = 此事将由一个独立小组进行调查。

疑问句:
Is the police investigating the robbery? = 警方正在调查抢劫案吗？
Are they still investigating the accident? = 他们还在调查事故吗？

否定句:
The government did not investigate the rumors properly. = 政府没有正确调查这些谣言。
They haven't investigated all the angles yet. = 他们还没有调查所有方面。
