occupations:/ˌɒkjuˈpeɪʃənz/ /ˌɑːkjuˈpeɪʃənz/ | n. |职业；占领；工作 |The word "occupations" refers to the jobs or professions that people have.  It can also refer to the act of occupying a place or space. |例句：He listed his previous occupations on his resume. 他在他的简历上列出了他以前的职业。

例句：The enemy's occupation of the city lasted for five years. 敌人的城市占领持续了五年。


近义词：jobs, professions, careers, employments | 反义词：leisure, idleness | 词性：名词
