deify:/ˈdiːɪfaɪ/| vt.|神化；尊为神 |To elevate to divine status; to regard or treat as a god.|例句：Many cultures deify their ancestors.  许多文化都将他们的祖先神化。|例句：The media tends to deify successful athletes. 媒体倾向于将成功的运动员神化。|近义词：idolize, apotheosize, canonize|反义词：desecrate, profane, vilify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deify the king. = 他们把国王神化。
We deify our heroes. = 我们把我们的英雄神化。

一般过去时:
