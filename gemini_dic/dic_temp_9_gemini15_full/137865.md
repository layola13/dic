nonvirtue:/ˈnɒnˈvɜːtʃuː/| n.|非美德；恶德|The absence of virtue; a vice or fault.  It often refers to a specific immoral or undesirable character trait.|例句：His arrogance is a nonvirtue that hinders his progress. 他的傲慢是一种阻碍他进步的非美德。|例句：Greed is considered a nonvirtue in many cultures. 在许多文化中，贪婪被认为是一种恶德。|近义词：vice, fault, flaw, defect |反义词：virtue, merit, excellence
