relive:/ˌriːˈlɪv/| vt.|重新体验；使复活；重温|To experience (something) again, especially a past event or emotion.|例句：He relived the accident in his mind again and again. 他一遍遍地在脑海中重温那次事故。
例句：The film relives the glory days of the 1960s. 这部电影重温了20世纪60年代的辉煌岁月。|近义词：re-experience, revisit, re-enact|反义词：forget, ignore|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relives his childhood memories every time he visits his hometown. 他每次回故乡都会重新体验童年记忆。
They relive the excitement of their wedding day every anniversary. 他们每逢结婚纪念日都会重温婚礼当天的兴奋。


一般过去时:
She relived the horror of the car crash in therapy.  她在治疗中重温了车祸的恐怖经历。
He relived his happiest moments with his late wife. 他重温了他与已故妻子的最快乐时光。


现在完成时:
I have relived that embarrassing moment countless times in my head. 我无数次在脑海中重温过那个尴尬的时刻。
She has relived the joy of her graduation day many times since. 从那以后，她多次重温了毕业日的喜悦。


过去完成时:
Before he died, he had relived his entire life in his mind.  在他死前，他已在脑海中重温了他的一生。
Having relived the trauma, she felt a sense of closure.  在重温了创伤之后，她感到一种解脱感。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
