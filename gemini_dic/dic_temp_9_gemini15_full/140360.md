oil:/ɔɪl/| n. |油，石油|A viscous liquid that is flammable and typically dark in color; used as fuel, lubricant, etc.  It can also refer to paints or other substances with an oily texture.|例句：We need to change the oil in the car. = 我们需要换汽车机油。|例句：The painting is done in oil. = 这幅画是用油画颜料画的。|近义词：petroleum, grease, lubricant|反义词：dryness, water


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine needs oil regularly. = 机器需要定期加油。
This engine uses a lot of oil. = 这台引擎耗油量很大。


一般过去时:
They oiled the door hinges yesterday. = 他们昨天给门铰链上了油。
The car leaked oil during the long drive. = 汽车在长途驾驶过程中漏油了。


现在完成时:
We have oiled the squeaky door. = 我们已经给吱吱作响的门上了油。
The car has used a lot of oil since the last change. = 自上次换油以来，汽车已经消耗了大量的机油。


过去完成时:
The mechanic had already oiled the engine before I arrived. = 我到达之前，技工已经给发动机上过油了。
They had used all the oil before starting the project. = 他们开始项目之前就已经用完了所有的油。


将来时:
We will oil the bicycle chain before the race. = 我们将在比赛前给自行车链条上油。
They will need to add more oil to the machine soon. = 他们很快需要给机器加更多的油。


虚拟语气:
If we had more oil, we could continue the work. = 如果我们有更多的油，我们就可以继续工作了。
I wish I had oiled the hinges before they rusted. = 我真希望铰链生锈之前我给它们上了油。


被动语态:
The car's engine is oiled every 5,000 miles. = 汽车的发动机每行驶5000英里就上一次油。
The machine parts are oiled automatically. = 机器零件会自动上油。


疑问句:

否定句:
The machine doesn't need oil at the moment. = 机器目前不需要加油。
