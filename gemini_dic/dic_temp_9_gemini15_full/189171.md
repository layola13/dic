sacksful:/ˈsæksfʊl/| n.|一袋…；满满一袋…|A sacksful is a quantity or amount that fills a sack. It implies a substantial quantity.  The word is often used figuratively to describe a large number of something.|例句：He carried sacksful of potatoes from the field. 他从田里扛回了好几袋土豆。

例句：The room was filled with sacksful of letters. 房间里堆满了满满一袋袋的信件。


近义词：bagful, load, quantity


反义词：少量，一点点 (a small amount, a little)


词性:名词
