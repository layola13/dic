impartable:/ɪmˈpɑːrtəbl/ | adj. | 不可传授的，不可沟通的 | Incapable of being imparted or communicated; incapable of being shared or passed on.  This word often describes knowledge or feelings that are deeply personal or inherently difficult to express. | Example:  The artist's unique vision was almost impartable; his techniques could be copied, but the essence of his work remained elusive.  艺术家独特的视野几乎无法传达；他的技巧可以被模仿，但作品的精髓仍然难以捉摸。| 近义词：incommunicable, inexpressible, uncommunicative | 反义词：communicable, transferable, shareable |  形容词
