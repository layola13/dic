over-positive: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːzɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过度积极的，过于乐观的 | Excessively positive or optimistic; tending to see the best in everything, even when it's unrealistic.  It often implies a lack of realism or a tendency to ignore potential problems. | 例句：His over-positive attitude prevented him from seeing the flaws in his business plan. 他过于乐观的态度使他看不到商业计划中的缺陷。

She was over-positive about the outcome of the surgery, ignoring the potential risks.  她对手术的结果过于乐观，忽略了潜在的风险。| 近义词：excessively optimistic, overly enthusiastic, unrealistic, Pollyannaish | 反义词：pessimistic, negative, realistic, cynical | 词性：形容词
