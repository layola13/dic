paraffining:/ˈpærəfaɪnɪŋ/| n. v.|石蜡处理；涂蜡|The process of applying paraffin wax to a surface.  This can be for waterproofing, preserving, or creating a smooth finish. |例句：The furniture was protected by paraffining.  家具通过涂蜡得到了保护。  The process of paraffining the wooden floors took several hours.  给木地板涂蜡的过程花了几个小时。|近义词：waxing|反义词：removing wax
