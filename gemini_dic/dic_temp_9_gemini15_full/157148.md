pinealocyte:/ˌpaɪniˈæləsaɪt/| n. |松果体细胞|A pinealocyte is a specialized type of cell found in the pineal gland.  These cells are responsible for the production and secretion of melatonin, a hormone that regulates sleep-wake cycles and other biological processes.  They are characterized by their unique morphology and the presence of specific intracellular structures involved in melatonin synthesis.|Example:  Researchers are studying the role of pinealocytes in the development of sleep disorders.|近义词:pineal cell|反义词: N/A
