mismatching:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃɪŋ/| adj.|不匹配的，不相称的|Not matching; incongruous.  It describes things that don't fit together well or are not compatible.|例句：The mismatching colors in the room were distracting. 房间里不协调的颜色令人分心。

例句：His mismatching socks and shoes showed a lack of attention to detail. 他不配套的袜子和鞋子显示出他对细节不够重视。


近义词：incompatible, discordant, incongruous, disparate

反义词：matching, compatible, harmonious


词性：形容词
