outbled:/ˌaʊtˈblɛd/| vt.|大量出血，流血过多 |To bleed more profusely than someone or something else.  This word is a relatively rare and somewhat informal term.|例句：The injured soldier outbled his comrades, requiring immediate transfusion. 受伤的士兵比他的战友流血更多，需要立即输血。|近义词：exsanguinate, hemorrhage excessively |反义词：stop bleeding, stanch
