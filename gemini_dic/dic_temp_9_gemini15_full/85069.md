hard-hearted:/ˌhɑːrdˈhɑːtɪd/ | adj. | 铁石心肠的，冷酷无情的 |  Lacking in sympathy or compassion; cruel.  |例句：He was hard-hearted and unfeeling toward the plight of the homeless. 他对无家可归者的困境冷酷无情。

例句：The hard-hearted landlord refused to lower the rent for his struggling tenants.  这个铁石心肠的房东拒绝降低他那些挣扎的租户的租金。


近义词：cruel, ruthless, merciless, unfeeling, callous, pitiless

反义词：kind, compassionate, merciful, sympathetic, tender-hearted


词源：源自古英语 *heard，意为“心”，加上形容词后缀 -hearted，表示“心的状态”。  "Hard" signifies a lack of softness or tenderness, hence a lack of compassion.
