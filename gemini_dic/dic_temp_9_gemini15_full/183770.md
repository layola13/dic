rescribe:/prɪˈskraɪb/| vt.|开处方；规定；描述；嘱咐|To prescribe means to authorize or order the use of something, typically a medicine or treatment.  It can also mean to lay down a rule or direct something to be done.|例句：The doctor prescribed me some antibiotics. 医生给我开了些抗生素。|例句：The rules prescribe that all members must pay dues. 规定所有会员都必须缴纳会费。|近义词：order, dictate, recommend, specify|反义词：proscribe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes antibiotics for the infection. = 医生为感染开出抗生素。
She prescribes regular exercise for better health. = 她建议经常锻炼以增强体质。

一般过去时:
The physician prescribed rest and medication. = 医生开了休息和药物的处方。
He prescribed a strict diet for his patient. = 他为病人制定了严格的饮食方案。

现在完成时:
The committee has prescribed new guidelines. = 委员会已经制定了新的指导方针。
She has prescribed this treatment many times before. = 她之前多次使用过这种疗法。

过去完成时:
The doctor had prescribed the medication before the patient's arrival. = 在病人到达之前，医生已经开了药。
They had prescribed a detailed plan of action. = 他们已经制定了一个详细的行动计划。


将来时:
The specialist will prescribe a course of physiotherapy. = 专科医生将开具物理治疗方案。
I think they will prescribe a stronger medicine. = 我认为他们会开更强的药。

虚拟语气:
If the doctor prescribed stronger medicine, the patient might recover faster. = 如果医生开了更强的药，病人可能会恢复得更快。
I wish the doctor had prescribed a different treatment. = 我希望医生能开出不同的治疗方案。

被动语态:
Antibiotics were prescribed for the bacterial infection. = 抗生素被用来治疗细菌感染。
A strict diet was prescribed by the nutritionist. = 营养师制定了严格的饮食方案。

疑问句:
What did the doctor prescribe for your headache? = 医生给你头痛开了什么药？
What measures were prescribed to prevent the spread of the disease? = 为防止疾病传播而采取了什么措施？

否定句:
The doctor did not prescribe any medication. = 医生没有开任何药。
The regulation does not prescribe a specific timeframe. = 该规定没有规定具体的时间范围。
