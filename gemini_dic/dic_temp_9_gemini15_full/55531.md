distributing: /dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 分发，分配，散布 | The act of giving something out to several people or places.  It implies spreading something over a wide area or among a large number of recipients. |例句：The company is distributing free samples of its new product. 公司正在分发其新产品的免费样品。

例句：The teacher is distributing the exam papers to the students. 老师正在把试卷分发给学生们。


近义词： dispensing, allocating, spreading, sharing, scattering | 反义词：collecting, gathering, concentrating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They distribute newspapers every morning. = 他们每天早上分发报纸。
The company distributes its products worldwide. = 这家公司在全球分销其产品。


一般过去时:
We distributed flyers in the neighborhood. = 我们在附近分发了传单。
The organization distributed aid to the victims of the disaster. = 该组织向灾难受害者分发了援助物资。


现在完成时:
They have distributed all the invitations. = 他们已经分发了所有的请柬。
The government has distributed millions of dollars in aid. = 政府已经分发了数百万美元的援助款项。


过去完成时:
They had distributed the questionnaires before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经分发了问卷。
She had distributed the workload among her team members. = 她已在她的团队成员之间分配了工作量。


将来时:
We will distribute the profits among the shareholders. = 我们将把利润分配给股东。
The company will distribute the new software next week. = 公司下周将发布新软件。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would distribute the tasks more efficiently. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更有效地分配任务。
I wish I had distributed the information more effectively. = 我希望我更有效地分发了信息。


被动语态:
The leaflets were distributed throughout the city. = 传单被分发到整个城市。
The aid was distributed fairly among the refugees. = 援助公平地分发给了难民。


疑问句:
How will the resources be distributed? = 资源将如何分配？
Who is responsible for distributing the materials? = 谁负责分发材料？


否定句:
They did not distribute the goods properly. = 他们没有正确地分发货物。
The company has not yet distributed the dividends. = 公司尚未分发股息。


PHRASE->
distribute evenly = 均匀分配
distribute widely = 广泛分发
distribute freely = 自由分发
distribute among = 在...之间分配
distribute to = 分发给
