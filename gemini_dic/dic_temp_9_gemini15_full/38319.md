concede:/kənˈsiːd/ | vt. |承认；让步；给予 | To admit that something is true or that someone else is right, especially unwillingly.  |例句：He conceded that he had made a mistake. 他承认自己犯了一个错误。|例句：She conceded defeat after the election results were announced.  选举结果公布后，她承认失败。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant |反义词：deny, refuse, contest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He concedes defeat gracefully. 他优雅地承认失败。
They concede the point. 他们承认这一点。


一般过去时:
She conceded the argument. 她承认了争论。
The government conceded to the protesters' demands. 政府向抗议者的要求让步。


现在完成时:
He has conceded that his plan was flawed. 他已经承认他的计划有缺陷。
They have conceded the match. 他们已经认输了。


过去完成时:
They had conceded before the final evidence was presented. 在最终证据出示之前，他们就已经认输了。
She had conceded the point earlier in the debate.  她在辩论的早期就已经承认了这一点。


将来时:
He will concede the election if the polls are accurate. 如果民调准确，他将承认选举失败。
They will concede the territory under pressure. 他们将在压力下让出领土。


虚拟语气:
If he conceded the error, the situation might improve. 如果他承认错误，情况可能会好转。
I wish he would concede the point. 我希望他会承认这一点。


被动语态:
The point was conceded by the opposition.  这个观点被反对派承认了。
The victory was conceded to the other team. 胜利被让给了另一支队伍。


疑问句:
Will he concede the election? 他会承认选举失败吗？
Did they concede the game? 他们认输了吗？


否定句:
He wouldn't concede an inch. 他寸步不让。
She refused to concede the possibility. 她拒绝承认这种可能性。
