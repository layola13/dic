foist:/fɔɪst/| vt.|强加；硬塞|To impose or inflict something on someone, especially something unwanted or unwelcome.  |例句：They foisted a worthless painting on him. 他们硬塞给他一幅毫无价值的画。

例句：He tried to foist his responsibilities onto his colleagues. 他试图把他的责任强加给他的同事。


近义词：impose, thrust, palm off, pass off|反义词：remove, withdraw, take back|动词
