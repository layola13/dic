longshoremen:/ˈlɒŋʃɔːrmən/ /ˈlɔːŋʃɔːrmən/ | n. |码头工人，搬运工人 |A longshoreman is a laborer who works on a dock, loading and unloading ships.  They are often members of a labor union and play a crucial role in the shipping industry. |例句：The longshoremen went on strike, disrupting port operations. 码头工人罢工，扰乱了港口运作。
The longshoremen worked tirelessly to unload the cargo before the storm hit. 码头工人夜以继日地卸货，以防风暴来临。|近义词：dockworker, stevedore |反义词：None  |词性：名词
