parapsychologist:/ˌpærəsaɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/| n. |超心理学专家|A parapsychologist is a scientist who studies parapsychological phenomena, such as extrasensory perception (ESP), psychokinesis, and other purported abilities that go beyond the known laws of physics.  They use scientific methods to investigate these claims, although the field is highly controversial and often considered pseudoscience. |Dr. Smith, a renowned parapsychologist, has dedicated her career to researching telepathy. 史密斯博士是一位著名的超心理学专家，她毕生致力于研究心灵感应。|psychologist, psychic researcher|scientist (in a related field focusing on conventional psychology)|n.
