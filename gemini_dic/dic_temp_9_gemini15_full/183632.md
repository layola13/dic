rereadings:/ˌriːˈriːdɪŋz/| n. |再读；重读；反复阅读|The professor assigned rereadings of key chapters for the upcoming exam.  教授布置了考试前关键章节的再读作业。|近义词：re-readings, reviews, repetitions|反义词：first readings, initial readings


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The student rereads the textbook chapter regularly. = 学生定期重读课本章节。
His rereadings of the classic novel deepen his understanding. = 他反复阅读这部经典小说，加深了对它的理解。


一般过去时:
She reread the email to make sure she understood it. = 她重读了邮件以确保自己理解了。
They reread the contract before signing it. = 他们在签署合同之前又读了一遍。


现在完成时:
I have reread that book several times. = 我已经读过那本书好几遍了。
He has reread all of Shakespeare's plays. = 他已经重读了莎士比亚的所有戏剧。


过去完成时:
She had reread the instructions before starting the project. = 在开始项目之前，她已经重读了说明。
They had reread the report several times before presenting it. = 他们在提交报告之前已经读过好几遍了。


将来时:
I will reread the chapter before the test. = 我考试前会重读这一章节。
She will reread the manuscript after editing it. = 她编辑完手稿后会再读一遍。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reread "War and Peace". = 如果我有更多时间，我会重读《战争与和平》。
I wish I had reread the instructions more carefully. = 我真希望我更仔细地重读说明。


被动语态:
The essay was reread by the professor for grammatical errors. = 教授重读了这篇文章，检查语法错误。
The document will be reread by the legal team. = 该文件将由法律团队重读。


疑问句:
Have you reread the assigned articles? = 你重读了指定的文章了吗？
Will they reread the script before the performance? = 他们在演出之前会重读剧本吗？


否定句:
He did not reread the letter. = 他没有重读这封信。
She has not reread the book since college. = 从大学毕业后她就再没读过这本书了。


PHRASE->
rereadings of classic literature = 经典文学的再读
rereadings for better comprehension = 为更好地理解而重读
critical rereadings = 批判性重读
close rereadings = 仔细研读
rereadings and annotations = 重读和注释
