outspins:/aʊtˈspɪnz/| v.|超过，胜过（尤指旋转速度）|To surpass or exceed, especially in speed of rotation.|The new turbine outspins the old one by a significant margin. 新型涡轮机的旋转速度明显超过旧型。|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outperforms |反义词：underperforms, lags behind

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The top spins faster than the bottom one. = 上面的陀螺转得比下面的快。
This fan outspins all others in its class. = 这款风扇的转速超过同类产品中的所有其他风扇。

一般过去时:
The old record player outspun the new one in terms of sound quality. = 就音质而言，旧唱机胜过新唱机。
The competitor's car outspun ours during the race. = 比赛中，竞争对手的车转速超过了我们的车。

现在完成时:
The company has outspun its competitors in the market. = 这家公司在市场上已经超过了竞争对手。
This new technology has outspun all previous attempts to solve the problem. = 这项新技术已经超越了所有先前解决这个问题的尝试。

过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outspun all of his opponents. = 比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有对手。
Before the final lap, she had already outspun her rivals. = 在最后一圈之前，她已经超过了她的对手。

将来时:
We believe that this design will outspin the current model. = 我们相信这个设计将超过目前的型号。
They predict their new product will outspin the competition. = 他们预测他们的新产品将胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If the motor were more powerful, it would outspin the current model. = 如果电机更强大，它将超过目前的型号。
I wish my car could outspin the others on the track. = 我希望我的车能够在赛道上超过其他车辆。

被动语态:
The old model was outspun by the new one in terms of efficiency. = 就效率而言，旧型号被新型号超越了。
The rival company's product was outspun in the recent market analysis. = 在最近的市场分析中，竞争对手公司的产品被超越了。

疑问句:
Does this motor outspin the previous version? = 这个马达的转速是否超过了以前的版本？
Can this new design outspin the current market leader? = 这个新设计能否超过目前的市场领导者？

否定句:
The smaller engine did not outspin the larger one. = 小发动机没有超过大发动机的转速。
Our team's strategy did not outspin the opponents'. = 我们团队的策略没有超过对手的策略（在速度或效率方面）。
