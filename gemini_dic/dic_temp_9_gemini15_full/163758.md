predelegated: /ˌpriːˈdɛlɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先委派的，预先授权的 |  Predelegated refers to something that has been delegated or authorized beforehand. It implies that the authority or responsibility was given in advance, before a specific situation or need arises.  | Example: The predelegated authority allowed the team to proceed with the project immediately.  预先授权使团队能够立即启动项目。 | 近义词：preauthorized, beforehand assigned | 反义词：undelegated, unassigned |  形容词
