pebblestone:/ˈpɛblˌstoʊn/| n. |卵石；鹅卵石 |A pebblestone is a small, smooth, rounded stone, typically found on a beach or in a riverbed.  It's formed by the action of water eroding a larger rock over time.|例句：The children collected colorful pebblestones on the beach.孩子们在海滩上收集五颜六色的鹅卵石。
The path was lined with smooth, grey pebblestones.  小路两旁排列着光滑的灰色鹅卵石。|近义词：pebble, cobble, stone |反义词：boulder, rock (larger stones)
