interfacing:/ˈɪntərfeɪsɪŋ/| n. |接口；界面；相互作用|The process of connecting two or more systems or components, often involving a shared boundary or communication method.  It can refer to physical connections, software interfaces, or interactions between people or organizations. |例句：The software provides a user-friendly interfacing for controlling the machine.  该软件提供了一个用户友好的界面来控制机器。

例句：The successful project required seamless interfacing between the engineering and marketing teams.  项目的成功需要工程和市场团队之间的无缝合作。


近义词：connection, interaction, interface, link, communication|反义词：separation, disconnection, isolation|名词
