positive:/ˈpɒzətɪv/ /ˈpɑːzətɪv/| adj.|积极的；肯定的；正面的；真实的|

英文解析：Expressing or characterized by affirmation, approval, or confidence; optimistic or cheerful.  Also referring to a result that confirms the presence of something, such as a disease or condition in a medical test.  In mathematics, it refers to a number greater than zero.

例句：

She has a positive attitude towards life.  = 她对生活抱有积极的态度。

The test results were positive for the virus. = 病毒检测结果呈阳性。


近义词：optimistic, cheerful, affirmative, confident, certain, upbeat


反义词：negative, pessimistic, doubtful, uncertain, gloomy


词性:形容词
