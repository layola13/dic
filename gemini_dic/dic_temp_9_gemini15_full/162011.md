praxinoscope:/ˈpræksɪnoʊskoʊp/ | n. | 幻影镜 | A praxinoscope is an optical toy that produces the illusion of movement by reflecting a series of images from a rotating cylinder.  It was a precursor to the cinematograph, offering a more refined view of moving images compared to its predecessor, the zoetrope. | The praxinoscope was a popular toy in the late 19th century.  Children loved watching the seemingly animated images. | 近义词：zoetrope (though less advanced), kinetoscope | 反义词：无 |  名词
