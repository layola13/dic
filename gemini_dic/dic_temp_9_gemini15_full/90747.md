hummingbird:/ˈhʌmɪŋbɜːrd/| n. |蜂鸟|A tiny bird with brightly colored feathers that feeds on nectar from flowers and hovers in the air by rapidly beating its wings.  Hummingbirds are known for their remarkable ability to fly backward and upside down.| 例句：The hummingbird zipped from flower to flower, sipping nectar.  蜂鸟从一朵花飞到另一朵花，吸食花蜜。

The hummingbird hovered in front of the red flower before it darted away. 蜂鸟在红花前盘旋了一会儿，然后飞走了。|近义词：None perfectly equivalent, but perhaps "small bird" or "flying insect" depending on context.|反义词：None directly opposite in meaning.|名词
