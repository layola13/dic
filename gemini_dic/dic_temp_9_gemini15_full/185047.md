rethink:/ˌriːˈθɪŋk/| vt.|重新考虑，再考虑|To think again about (something), typically in order to improve it or avoid a mistake.|例句：We need to rethink our strategy. 我们需要重新考虑我们的策略。|近义词： reconsider, review, reassess |反义词：accept, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rethink my decisions frequently. = 我经常重新考虑我的决定。
They rethink the project plan every week. = 他们每周都重新考虑项目计划。

一般过去时:
She rethought her answer after a moment's pause. = 她停顿片刻后重新考虑了她的答案。
He rethought his whole life after the accident. = 事故后他重新思考了他的一生。

现在完成时:
I have rethought my approach to the problem. = 我已经重新考虑了解决这个问题的方法。
They have rethought their marketing strategy several times. = 他们已经多次重新考虑了他们的营销策略。

过去完成时:
I had rethought my initial plan before presenting it. = 在提交方案之前，我已经重新考虑了我的最初计划。
She had rethought her position on the issue after hearing new evidence. = 在听到新的证据后，她已经重新考虑了她对这个问题的立场。

将来时:
I will rethink my options before making a final decision. = 在做出最终决定之前，我会重新考虑我的选择。
We will rethink the design if the test results are unsatisfactory. = 如果测试结果不令人满意，我们将重新考虑设计。

虚拟语气:
If I had rethought my actions, I might have avoided the mistake. = 如果我当时重新考虑了我的行为，我本可以避免这个错误。
I wish I had rethought my investment strategy. = 我真希望我当时重新考虑了我的投资策略。

被动语态:
The plan needs to be rethought. = 这个计划需要重新考虑。
The decision was rethought after further discussion. = 经过进一步讨论后，这个决定被重新考虑了。

疑问句:
Should we rethink our current approach? = 我们应该重新考虑我们目前的方法吗？
Have you rethought your decision to quit your job? = 你是否重新考虑了你辞职的决定？

否定句:
He didn't rethink his impulsive decision. = 他没有重新考虑他冲动的决定。
They didn't rethink their strategy despite the setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，他们并没有重新考虑他们的策略。
