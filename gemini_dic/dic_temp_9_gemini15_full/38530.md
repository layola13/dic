concisest:/kənˈsaɪsist/| adj.|最简洁的，最精练的 |Most brief or to the point; expressing much in few words.  It is the superlative form of concise.|例句：His concisest explanation was still too long for the impatient audience.  他最简洁的解释对那些不耐烦的听众来说还是太长了。


例句：The concisest summary of the report highlighted the key findings. 报告最简洁的总结突出了关键发现。


近义词：most succinct, most brief, most terse


反义词：most verbose, most rambling, most prolix


词性：形容词
