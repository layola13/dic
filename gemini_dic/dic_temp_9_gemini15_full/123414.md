monoplane:/ˈmɒnəpleɪn/| n. |单翼飞机|A monoplane is an airplane with only one main wing.  It contrasts with biplanes and other multi-winged aircraft.  Monoplanes are the most common type of airplane in use today.|The Wright brothers' first successful airplane was a monoplane.|近义词：single-wing aircraft|反义词：biplane, triplane
