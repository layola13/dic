forgot: /fərˈɡɑːt/ /fəˈɡɒt/ | vt. | 忘记 | To fail to remember something.  |  I forgot my keys. 我忘记带钥匙了。  I forgot to call her. 我忘记给她打电话了。 | remembered, recalled | remembered, recalled | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I forget things easily. = 我很容易忘记事情。
He forgets his appointments often. = 他经常忘记约会。


一般过去时:
I forgot my umbrella yesterday. = 我昨天忘记带伞了。
She forgot to lock the door. = 她忘记锁门了。


现在完成时:
I have forgotten his name. = 我忘记了他的名字。
We have forgotten our anniversary. = 我们忘记了我们的周年纪念日。


过去完成时:
I had forgotten all about the meeting. = 我把会议完全忘记了。
She had forgotten to buy milk. = 她忘记买牛奶了。


将来时:
I will forget all about it soon. = 我很快就会忘记这一切。
They will probably forget our request. = 他们可能会忘记我们的请求。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't forgotten my phone, I would have called you. = 如果我没有忘记我的手机，我就给你打电话了。
I wish I hadn't forgotten my homework. = 我真希望我没有忘记我的家庭作业。


被动语态:
The appointment was forgotten. = 约会给忘记了。
The instructions were forgotten. = 说明被忘记了。


疑问句:
Did you forget your wallet? = 你忘记带钱包了吗？
Have you forgotten what I told you? = 你忘记我告诉你的事情了吗？


否定句:
I didn't forget to send the email. = 我没有忘记发送邮件。
He didn't forget about the party. = 他没有忘记聚会。


PHRASE->
forget about it = 别担心；没关系
forget-me-not = 勿忘我（花）
don't forget to = 不要忘记...
forget oneself = 忘乎所以
to forget all about something = 完全忘记某事

词源:

The word "forgot" is the past tense of the verb "forget."  "Forget" itself comes from Old English *forgietan, composed of *for- ("completely," "away") and *gietan ("to get," "to obtain").  The meaning evolved from "to lose something" to "to lose the memory of something."
