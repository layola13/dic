semifossilized:/ˌsemiˈfɑːsəlaɪzd/| adj.|半石化的；半化石的|Partially fossilized; in a transitional state between organic matter and a fossil.  The term implies that the process of fossilization is incomplete or only partially complete.  It often describes organic remains that retain some original organic material while exhibiting signs of fossilization. |例句：The semifossilized wood still showed faint traces of its original cellular structure. 半石化的木头仍然显示出其原始细胞结构的微弱痕迹。

The semifossilized remains of the ancient reptile were found embedded in the rock.  古代爬行动物的半石化遗骸被发现嵌在岩石中。|近义词：partially fossilized, partially petrified|反义词：fully fossilized, completely petrified
