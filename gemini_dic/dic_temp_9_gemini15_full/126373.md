myrioscope:/ˈmɪriəˌskoʊp/| n. |万花筒；幻灯机 |A myrioscope is an optical device that projects multiple images onto a screen, creating a dazzling display of changing pictures.  It was popular in the 19th century as a form of entertainment. |The myrioscope was a marvel of Victorian-era technology, captivating audiences with its shifting kaleidoscope of images.  |近义词：stereoscope, slideshow |反义词：无
