pairwise:/ˈpeə(r)waɪz/| adj.|成对的；两两的|Considering each pair of items separately.  It involves comparing or processing elements in pairs.|例句：The data was analyzed pairwise to identify correlations. 数据被成对分析以识别相关性。  The students were paired pairwise for the group project. 学生们两两配对进行小组项目。|近义词：in pairs, two by two, by twos|反义词：individually, singly
