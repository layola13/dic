prescribing:/prɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/| v. |开处方；规定；指定；建议 | The act of recommending or ordering a medicine or treatment for someone.  It also means to lay down a rule or order. |例句：The doctor is prescribing antibiotics for the infection. 医生正在为感染开抗生素处方。

例句：The rules prescribe that all members must attend the meeting.  规定规定所有成员都必须参加会议。


近义词：prescripting, ordaining, dictating, recommending | 反义词：prohibiting, forbidding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes medication for his patients. = 医生为病人开药。
He prescribes a strict diet for weight loss. = 他规定了严格的节食减肥方法。


一般过去时:
The physician prescribed rest and fluids. = 医生开了休息和喝流体的处方。
She prescribed a course of physiotherapy. = 她开了物理治疗疗程。


现在完成时:
The committee has prescribed new guidelines for safety. = 委员会已经制定了新的安全指南。
They have prescribed a new set of rules for the game. = 他们已经为游戏制定了一套新的规则。


过去完成时:
The doctor had prescribed the medicine before the patient arrived. = 病人到达之前，医生已经开了药。
They had prescribed a strict regimen for the athlete. = 他们已经为运动员制定了严格的训练计划。


将来时:
The specialist will prescribe a stronger medication if needed. = 如果需要，专家将开更强的药。
The judge will prescribe a sentence for the criminal. = 法官将对罪犯判刑。


虚拟语气:
If I were the doctor, I would prescribe a different treatment. = 如果我是医生，我会开不同的治疗方案。
If she had prescribed the right medicine, he would have recovered faster. = 如果她开了正确的药，他就会恢复得更快。


被动语态:
The medication was prescribed by the doctor. = 药物是由医生开的。
New rules were prescribed by the government. = 新规是政府制定的。


疑问句:
What medication has the doctor prescribed? = 医生开了什么药？
What punishment will be prescribed for this crime? = 对这项罪行将处以什么惩罚？


否定句:
The doctor did not prescribe any antibiotics. = 医生没有开任何抗生素。
The judge did not prescribe a harsh sentence. = 法官没有判处严厉的刑罚。


PHRASE->
prescribe medication = 开药
prescribe a course of action = 制定行动方案
prescribe a treatment = 制定治疗方案
prescribe limits = 设定限制
prescribe rules = 制定规则
