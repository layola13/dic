overspecification: /ˌoʊvərˌspɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |过度规范；过度详细说明|The act of specifying more details than are necessary or desirable.  This can lead to inflexibility, increased complexity, and wasted effort.  It's often seen in software design, engineering, and project management where unnecessary constraints are imposed. |例句：The overspecification of the requirements led to delays in the project. 过度详细的规定导致了项目的延误。


The overspecification of the design made it difficult to implement efficiently. 设计的过度规范使得高效实施变得困难。|近义词：overdefinition, overdetermination, superfluity|反义词：underspecification, generality, simplicity|名词
