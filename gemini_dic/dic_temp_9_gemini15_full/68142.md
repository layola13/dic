fail-safed:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪft/| adj.|失效安全的，故障安全的|Designed or built to prevent failure or to ensure safety in the event of a failure.  A fail-safed system is one that automatically switches to a backup system or takes a safe action if the primary system fails.|例句：The software includes a fail-safed mechanism to prevent data loss in case of a power outage. 该软件包含一个失效安全机制，以防止电源中断时数据丢失。|例句：The aircraft's braking system is fail-safed, ensuring safe landings even if one component fails.  飞机的制动系统是失效安全的，即使一个部件发生故障也能确保安全着陆。|近义词：safeguarded, protected, secure|反义词：unsafe, vulnerable, risky|形容词
