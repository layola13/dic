odds-endses:/ˈɑːdzˌɛndz/ | n. | 零碎的东西，边角料 |  Odds and ends are small, leftover items of various kinds.  It refers to miscellaneous, unimportant things.  The plural form "odds and ends" is much more common than "odds-endses". |例句：I spent the afternoon tidying up the odds and ends in the garage.  我下午花时间整理车库里的零碎东西。

例句：She packed a few odds and ends for her weekend trip. 她为周末旅行打包了一些零碎物品。


近义词：miscellaneous items, scraps, remnants, trifles | 反义词：essentials, necessities, main items


词性：名词
