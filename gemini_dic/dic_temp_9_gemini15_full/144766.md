over-digested: /ˌoʊvərˈdaɪdʒɛstɪd/ | adj. | 消化不良的，过度消化的 |  Describes food that has been subjected to excessive digestive processes, often resulting in discomfort or negative health consequences.  This can be due to prolonged digestion time or excessive enzyme activity.  It often implies a state beyond simply being digested, suggesting an imbalance or malfunction in the digestive system. | Example: The over-digested meal left him with stomach cramps.  过度消化的食物让他胃抽筋了。 |近义词:  indigested, poorly digested | 反义词: easily digested, well-digested | 词性: adjective
