permanently:/ˈpɜːrmənəntli/| adv.|永久地，持久地|in a way that lasts forever or for a very long time;  constantly or continuously.|例句：The tattoo is permanently etched onto his skin. 这纹身永久地刻在他的皮肤上。

例句：He permanently damaged his hearing by listening to loud music. 他长期听大声音乐永久性地损害了他的听力。


近义词：eternally, perpetually, forever, constantly


反义词：temporarily, briefly, transiently


词性: 副词
