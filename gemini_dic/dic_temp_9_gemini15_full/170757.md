pseudocarp:/ˈsuːdəʊkɑːrp/| n. |假果；伪果|A pseudocarp is a fruit in which the fleshy part is derived not from the ovary but from some other floral part or parts, such as the receptacle or the calyx.  In essence, it's a fruit that appears to be a fruit but isn't formed in the typical way from the ovary.  Many common fruits are pseudocarps.|例句：Apples, pears, and strawberries are all examples of pseudocarps. 苹果、梨和草莓都是假果的例子。

The pineapple is a multiple pseudocarp, formed from the fusion of many individual fruits. 菠萝是由许多单个果实融合而成的多重假果。|近义词：false fruit, accessory fruit|反义词：true fruit
