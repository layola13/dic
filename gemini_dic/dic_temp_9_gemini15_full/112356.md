lorn:/lɔːrn/| adj.|孤单的，凄凉的，被遗弃的|sad and lonely, especially because someone you love has died or left you|例句：She felt forlorn and abandoned after her husband died. 她丈夫死后感到孤单无助。

例句：The forlorn child wandered the streets alone.  那个可怜的孩子独自在街上游荡。


近义词：desolate, lonely, forsaken, abandoned, desolate, wretched|反义词：happy, joyful, cheerful, content


词源:源自古英语 *lōrnian “丧失的，被遗弃的”，与古英语 *lēosan “丢失”有关。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels forlorn. = 他感到孤单。
She looks forlorn. = 她看起来很凄凉。


一般过去时:
The dog looked forlorn after its owner left. = 主人离开后，这只狗看起来很孤单。
I felt forlorn when my friend moved away. = 当我的朋友搬走时，我感到很孤单。


现在完成时:
She has felt forlorn ever since her breakup. = 自从分手以来，她一直感到很孤单。
He has been forlorn since the loss of his job. = 自从失去工作以来，他一直很沮丧。


过去完成时:
I had felt forlorn before I met my current partner. = 在遇到我现在的伴侣之前，我一直感到很孤单。
She had been forlorn for years before finding a new friend. = 在找到新朋友之前，她已经孤单多年了。


将来时:
I will feel forlorn if my cat dies. = 如果我的猫死了，我会感到很悲伤。
He will likely be forlorn if his plan fails. = 如果他的计划失败，他可能会很沮丧。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would feel forlorn. = 如果我处于他的境地，我会感到很孤单。
I wish I weren't so forlorn. = 我希望我不那么孤单。


被动语态:
(lorn本身就表示一种状态，一般不用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Does he feel forlorn? = 他感到孤单吗？
Does she look forlorn? = 她看起来很凄凉吗？


否定句:
He does not feel forlorn. = 他并不感到孤单。
She doesn't look forlorn at all. = 她一点也不显得凄凉。


PHRASE->
a forlorn hope = 一线希望 (a hopeless but desperate attempt)
a forlorn figure = 孤单的身影 (a lonely and sad-looking person)
forlornly beautiful = 凄美的 (beautifully sad)
forlorn attempt = 徒劳的尝试 (a futile or unsuccessful attempt)
forlorn expression = 忧郁的表情 (a sad and dejected expression)
