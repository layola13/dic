disponee:/dɪspəˈniː/| n. |受让人，受托人 |A disponee is a person to whom property or rights are transferred or conveyed.  They are the recipient of a disposition, often in a legal context.  For example, in a trust, the disponee would be the beneficiary. |例句：The disponee received the property according to the terms of the will. 受让人根据遗嘱条款收到了房产。

The disponee of the trust inherited a substantial estate. 信托的受让人继承了一大笔遗产。|近义词：recipient, beneficiary, grantee |反义词：dispositor, grantor
