froglet:/ˈfrɒɡlɪt/ | n. |小青蛙，蝌蚪后期|A young frog, especially one that has recently developed legs but still retains some tadpole characteristics.  Froglets are typically smaller than adult frogs and may have smoother skin.|例句：The pond was teeming with froglets, tiny replicas of their adult counterparts.  池塘里挤满了小青蛙，它们是成年青蛙的微型复制品。

The froglets hopped playfully around the lily pads.  小青蛙们在睡莲叶子上嬉戏地跳来跳去。|近义词：young frog, tadpole (in the later stages) |反义词：adult frog
