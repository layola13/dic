scrunchy:/ˈskrʌntʃi/| n.|发圈，松紧带|A scrunchy is a hair accessory made of fabric or elastic that is used to hold hair in place.  It is often made of stretchy material and is typically worn around a ponytail or bun.|例句：She tied her hair up with a colorful scrunchy. 她用一个彩色发圈扎起了头发。

例句：I bought a new scrunchy at the drugstore. 我在药店买了个新的发圈。|近义词：hair tie, ponytail holder|反义词：无
