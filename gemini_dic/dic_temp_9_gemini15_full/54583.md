dismalize:/ˈdɪzməlaɪz/| vt.|使沮丧；使阴沉；使凄凉|To make or become dismal; to depress or sadden.|例句：The constant rain dismalized the picnic.  持续的雨水使野餐变得凄凉。 The news dismalized her completely.  这个消息使她完全沮丧。|近义词：depress, sadden, gloom, dispirit|反义词：cheer, uplift, brighten, gladden|动词
