prefigurements:/priːˈfɪɡjərmənts/| n. |预兆；前兆；预示|Things that foreshadow or prefigure something else; indications or signs of something to come.  Prefigurements often suggest a future event or development, providing clues or hints about what might happen.|The strange weather patterns were seen as prefigurements of the impending storm.  奇怪的天气模式被视为即将到来的暴风雨的预兆。| portents, omens, foreshadowings, harbingers|—|名词
