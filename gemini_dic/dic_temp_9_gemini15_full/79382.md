glidder:/ˈɡlaɪdər/| n. |滑翔机；滑翔者 | A glider is an aircraft without an engine that relies on air currents to stay aloft.  It's often launched by being towed by another aircraft or by being launched from a slope. |例句：The glider soared gracefully through the sky.  滑翔机优雅地翱翔于天空。

例句：He learned to pilot a glider at a young age. 他年轻时学会了驾驶滑翔机。

近义词：sailplane, hang glider


反义词：powered aircraft, airplane (with engine)
