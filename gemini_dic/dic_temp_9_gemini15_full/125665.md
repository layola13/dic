mums:/mʌmz/| n.| 妈妈们；菊花 |The word "mums" is the plural of "mum," a shortened form of "mother." It can also refer to chrysanthemum flowers.|例句：My mums are coming to visit next week.  我的妈妈们下周要来拜访。

例句：We planted mums in the garden. 我们在花园里种了菊花。

近义词：mothers, moms (for "mother")，chrysanthemums (for "flowers")
反义词：无
