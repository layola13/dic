fluttery: /ˈflʌtəri/ | adj. | 颤抖的；飘动的；慌乱的 |

英文解析:Characterized by or suggesting a light, rapid, irregular motion; trembling or wavering.  Suggesting nervousness or excitement.

例句:

Her hands were fluttery as she waited for the results.  她的手在她等待结果时颤抖不已。

The butterfly's wings made a fluttery dance in the sunlight.  蝴蝶的翅膀在阳光下翩翩起舞。


近义词:tremulous, shaky, quivering, unsteady, nervous, agitated

反义词:steady, calm, still, composed
