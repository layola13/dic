ionises:/ˈaɪənaɪzɪz/| v. |电离；使离子化 |To ionize means to convert an atom or molecule into an ion by adding or removing charged particles, usually electrons.  This process is fundamental in various chemical and physical phenomena. |The high voltage ionises the air, creating ozone.|近义词：ionize, electrify |反义词：neutralize
