in-grouper: /ˈɪnˌɡruːpər/ | n. |群体成员，内群体成员 | A member of one's own group, especially in contrast to an out-group.  Someone who belongs to a specific social group and identifies with its members. | Example:  The in-groupers were quick to defend their shared beliefs.  内群体成员很快为他们共同的信仰辩护。|近义词: member, insider | 反义词: outsider, out-group member | 名词
