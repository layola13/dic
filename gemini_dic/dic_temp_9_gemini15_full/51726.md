devocation:/ˌdeɪvoʊˈkeɪʃən/| n. |献身；奉献；爱好；职业|Devotion to a cause or activity;  a strong feeling of love, admiration, or loyalty.|例句：Her devocation to her work is admirable.  她的工作奉献精神令人钦佩。


例句：His devocation to painting is evident in his many works. 他对绘画的热爱在他的许多作品中都显而易见。

近义词：dedication, commitment, passion, pursuit

反义词：neglect, indifference, apathy

词性:名词

词源:
