carbonometer:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. | 碳素计，碳量计 | A device used to measure the amount of carbon in a substance, typically in the form of carbon dioxide or carbon monoxide.  It's often used in environmental monitoring and industrial processes to assess carbon emissions or the carbon content of materials. | Example: The factory uses a carbonometer to monitor its carbon dioxide emissions and ensure compliance with environmental regulations.  工厂使用碳量计监测其二氧化碳排放量，确保符合环境法规。|近义词：carbon analyzer, carbon detector | 反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其功能相反的设备，例如氧气计) | 名词
