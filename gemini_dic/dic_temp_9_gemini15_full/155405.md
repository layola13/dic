phasis:/ˈfeɪsɪs/| n.|相位；阶段；方面|A particular stage in a process or cycle; an aspect or a particular feature of something.|The moon goes through different phases. 月亮经历不同的月相。  The project is in its final phase.  这个项目正处于最后阶段。|近义词：stage, aspect, stage, period |反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The moon is in its full phase. = 月亮处于满月阶段。
This project has many phases. = 这个项目有很多阶段。


一般过去时:
The project went through several phases last year. = 这个项目去年经历了几个阶段。
The disease had three distinct phases. = 这种疾病有三个不同的阶段。


现在完成时:
The construction has entered its final phase. = 建设已经进入最后阶段。
We have completed several phases of the research. = 我们已经完成了研究的几个阶段。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the experiment had already entered its second phase. = 我到达时，实验已经进入第二阶段了。
They had finished the initial phase before the funding ran out. = 在资金耗尽之前，他们已经完成了初始阶段。


将来时:
The project will enter its next phase next month. = 该项目将于下个月进入下一阶段。
The negotiations will likely have several phases. = 谈判可能会分几个阶段进行。


虚拟语气:
If the project were in its initial phase, we would have more time. = 如果项目处于初始阶段，我们将有更多时间。
I wish the project had been in a better phase. = 我希望项目处于更好的阶段。


被动语态:
The different phases of the process were clearly defined. = 过程的不同阶段被明确定义。
Each phase of the project will be carefully managed. = 项目的每个阶段都将被仔细管理。


疑问句:
What phase is the moon in tonight? = 今晚月亮处于哪个月相？
What phase are we in the development process? = 我们在开发过程的哪个阶段？


否定句:
The project is not yet in its final phase. = 项目尚未进入最后阶段。
The research hasn't progressed beyond the initial phase. = 研究尚未超过初始阶段。


PHRASE->
in phase:  同步；协调
out of phase:  不同步；失调
phase out:  逐步淘汰；逐渐取消
phase in:  逐步引进；逐渐实施
all phases: 所有阶段
