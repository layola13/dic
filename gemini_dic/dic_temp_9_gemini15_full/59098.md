dyewood:/ˈdaɪˌwuːd/| n. | 染料木，色木 | A type of wood that yields a dye.  The dye is typically extracted by boiling wood chips in water.  Different dyewoods produce different colors. | Example:  Logwood is a type of dyewood used to create a dark purple or black dye.  = 苏木是一种用于制造深紫色或黑色染料的染料木。|近义词：stain wood, coloring wood|反义词：None specifically.
