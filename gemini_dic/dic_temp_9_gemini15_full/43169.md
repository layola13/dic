cpm:/ˌsiːpiːˈɛm/|/ˈsiːpiːˈɛm/| n. |每千次展现成本|Cost Per Mille (or sometimes Cost Per Thousand),  a common metric in advertising representing the cost an advertiser pays for one thousand views or impressions of an advertisement.  It's used to measure the effectiveness and cost-efficiency of advertising campaigns across various platforms.|Example: The CPM for this video ad campaign was $10.  这个视频广告活动的CPM是10美元。|近义词：cost per thousand (CPT), impressions cost|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The CPM for this banner ad is relatively low. = 这个横幅广告的CPM相对较低。
Our CPM on social media is higher than expected. = 我们在社交媒体上的CPM高于预期。


一般过去时:
The CPM for last month's campaign was $5. = 上个月活动的CPM是5美元。
Their CPM decreased significantly after the website redesign. = 网站改版后，他们的CPM大幅下降。


现在完成时:
We have seen a steady increase in our CPM this quarter. = 本季度我们的CPM稳步增长。
They have reduced their CPM through targeted advertising. = 他们通过定向广告降低了CPM。


过去完成时:
Before the optimization, their CPM had been much higher. = 优化之前，他们的CPM要高得多。
By the end of the year, the CPM had fallen below our target. = 到年底，CPM已低于我们的目标。


将来时:
We expect the CPM to increase next month due to increased competition. = 我们预计下个月由于竞争加剧，CPM将上升。
Their CPM will likely decrease if they improve their ad targeting. = 如果他们改进广告投放目标，他们的CPM可能会下降。


虚拟语气:
If the CPM were lower, we would run more ads. = 如果CPM更低，我们会投放更多广告。
I wish the CPM were more predictable. = 我希望CPM更可预测。


被动语态:
The CPM is calculated based on ad impressions. = CPM是根据广告展示次数计算的。
Our CPM was significantly affected by the recent algorithm changes. = 最近的算法更改严重影响了我们的CPM。


疑问句:
What is the CPM for this specific ad placement? = 这个特定广告位的CPM是多少？
How is the CPM calculated for different platforms? = 不同平台的CPM是如何计算的？


否定句:
The CPM is not as high as we initially projected. = CPM并不像我们最初预测的那样高。
They did not achieve their target CPM for the quarter. = 他们没有达到本季度的目标CPM。
