malarial:/məˈleəriəl/| adj.|疟疾的；患疟疾的| Relating to or caused by malaria.  The word describes something connected to or resulting from the disease malaria. | Example:  He suffered from malarial fever. 他患有疟疾。  The malarial swamp bred mosquitoes. 疟疾沼泽滋生蚊子。|近义词：paludal, miasmic|反义词：healthy, well
