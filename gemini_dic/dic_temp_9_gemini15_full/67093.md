explore:/ɪkˈsplɔːr/| /ɛkˈsplɔr/| vt.|探索；探测；探险|To travel in or through (a place) in order to learn about it; investigate.  |例句：We explored the Amazon rainforest. 我们探索了亚马逊雨林。|例句：The scientists explored the possibility of a new cure. 科学家们探索了一种新疗法的可能性。|近义词：investigate, examine, search, delve into|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They explore the cave. = 他们在探索山洞。
We explore new ideas. = 我们探索新的想法。

一般过去时:
He explored the ancient ruins. = 他探索了古代遗址。
She explored the possibilities. = 她探索了各种可能性。

现在完成时:
They have explored the entire island. = 他们已经探索了整个岛屿。
We have explored many different options. = 我们已经探索了许多不同的选择。

过去完成时:
They had explored the area before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经探索过那个区域了。
She had explored many career paths before finding her niche. = 在找到自己的位置之前，她已经探索过许多职业道路。

将来时:
We will explore the underwater world. = 我们将探索水下世界。
They will explore the implications of the new law. = 他们将探讨新法律的影响。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would explore the Andes Mountains. = 如果我有时间，我会去探索安第斯山脉。
I wish I could explore the Sahara Desert. = 我希望我能探索撒哈拉沙漠。

被动语态:
The Amazon rainforest is being explored by scientists. = 亚马逊雨林正在被科学家们探索。
The new possibilities are being explored by the team. = 团队正在探索新的可能性。

疑问句:
Have you ever explored the Grand Canyon? = 你曾经探索过大峡谷吗？
Will they explore the subject further? = 他们会进一步探索这个主题吗？

否定句:
We didn't explore the option. = 我们没有探索这个选项。
They haven't explored all the possibilities. = 他们还没有探索所有可能性。
