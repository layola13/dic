furthering:/ˈfɜːðərɪŋ/ | v. |促进；推进；增进|The act of making something better or more advanced; promoting growth or development.  |例句：Furthering education is crucial for personal growth. 促进教育对个人成长至关重要。

例句：The company is furthering its research into renewable energy. 公司正在推进其对可再生能源的研究。


近义词：advancing, promoting, developing, fostering | 反义词：hindering, obstructing, impeding | 词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is furthering his career by taking additional courses. 他通过修读额外课程来提升他的事业。
They are furthering their understanding of the subject through research. 他们通过研究来增进对该主题的理解。

一般过去时:
She furthered her knowledge of history by visiting museums. 她通过参观博物馆来增进她的历史知识。
The government furthered its economic agenda by implementing new policies. 政府通过实施新政策来推进其经济议程。

现在完成时:
We have furthered our goals significantly this year. 今年我们已经显著地推进了我们的目标。
They have furthered their understanding of the problem through collaboration.  他们通过合作增进了对这个问题的理解。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had furthered his research on the topic. 在演示之前，他已经对该主题进行了进一步的研究。
By the time the project ended, they had furthered their skills considerably.  到项目结束时，他们的技能已经有了相当大的提高。

将来时:
She will further her education by pursuing a master's degree. 她将通过攻读硕士学位来深造。
We will further our investigation into the matter next week. 下周我们将进一步调查此事。

虚拟语气:
If he had furthered his studies, he would have a better job now. 如果他继续深造，他现在就会有一份更好的工作。
I wish I had furthered my knowledge of computer programming years ago. 我真希望几年前我就已经提升我的电脑编程知识了。

被动语态:
His career was furthered by his hard work and dedication. 他的事业因他的努力和奉献而得到提升。
The project was furthered by the generous donations received.  该项目因收到的慷慨捐款而得以推进。

疑问句:
How are you furthering your research? 你如何推进你的研究？
What steps are being taken to further the company's goals?  正在采取哪些步骤来推进公司的目标？

否定句:
He did not further his education after graduating from college. 他大学毕业后没有继续深造。
The lack of funding furthered the project. 资金不足没有推进该项目。
