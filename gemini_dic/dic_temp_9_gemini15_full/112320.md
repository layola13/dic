lore:/lɔːr/| n. |传说，知识，学问 |Traditional knowledge and beliefs passed down within a group or community; a body of knowledge or traditions relating to a particular subject.  |Example:  The ancient lore of the tribe included stories of mythical creatures and powerful spirits.  古老部落的传说包括神话生物和强大精灵的故事。
The lore surrounding the lost city is fascinating.  关于失落之城的传说非常迷人。|folklore, tradition, legend|fact, reality|名词
