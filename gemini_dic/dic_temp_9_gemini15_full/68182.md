faintishness:/ˈfeɪntɪʃnəs/| n. |虚弱；微弱；快要昏厥的状态|A feeling of faintness, weakness, or dizziness, often preceding unconsciousness.  It suggests a temporary and less severe state than a full faint.  The condition might be caused by various factors, such as low blood sugar, dehydration, or emotional stress.|例句：The heat and lack of food caused a feeling of faintishness.|近义词：weakness, dizziness, lightheadedness, giddiness|反义词：strength, vigor, robustness
