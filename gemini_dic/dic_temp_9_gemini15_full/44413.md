cross-member:/ˈkrɒsˌmɛmbər/| n. |横梁，横撑，横杆 |A structural member extending across a frame or structure, providing support and stability.  It connects two other members, typically at right angles. |例句：The chassis of the car included a strong cross-member to reinforce the frame. 这辆车的底盘包含一个坚固的横梁以增强车架强度。

The bridge's cross-members distributed the weight evenly. 桥梁的横梁均匀地分配了重量。

|近义词：transverse member, brace, tie bar|反义词：longitudinal member |名词
