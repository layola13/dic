reinvesting:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛstɪŋ/ | v. | 再投资；重新投资 | The act of putting profits or returns from an investment back into the same or a similar investment.  It's a strategy to generate further growth and returns. | Example: The company is reinvesting its profits into research and development.  这家公司正在将其利润再投资于研发。  The fund manager is reinvesting the dividends received to purchase more shares. 基金经理正在将收到的股息再投资以购买更多股票。 | 近义词：reapplying, re-employing, recycling (in a financial context) | 反义词：disinvesting, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reinvesting their earnings in new equipment. = 他们正在将他们的收入再投资于新设备。
We reinvest a portion of our profits each year. = 我们每年都会将一部分利润再投资。


一般过去时:
She reinvested her bonus in the stock market. = 她将奖金再投资于股市。
He reinvested the proceeds from the sale of his house. = 他将出售房屋的收益再投资。


现在完成时:
They have reinvested all their profits this year. = 他们今年已经将所有利润都再投资了。
The company has reinvested heavily in its workforce. = 公司已大力投资于其员工队伍。


过去完成时:
They had reinvested their savings before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经将储蓄再投资了。
She had reinvested her inheritance in a diversified portfolio. = 她已将继承的遗产再投资于多元化投资组合。


将来时:
We will reinvest the surplus in expanding our business. = 我们将把盈余再投资于扩大我们的业务。
They will reinvest their profits in a new project next year. = 他们明年将把利润再投资于一个新的项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would reinvest it in real estate. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会把它再投资于房地产。
If they reinvested wisely, they would see greater returns. = 如果他们明智地再投资，他们将获得更大的回报。


被动语态:
The profits were reinvested in the business. = 利润被再投资于企业。
The funds are being reinvested in sustainable energy projects. = 资金正在被再投资于可持续能源项目。


疑问句:
Are you reinvesting your savings? = 你正在将储蓄再投资吗？
Will they reinvest the money in the company? = 他们会把钱再投资到公司吗？


否定句:
They are not reinvesting their profits this year. = 他们今年没有将利润再投资。
She did not reinvest the money in the same business. = 她没有将钱再投资于同一业务。


PHRASE->

reinvest capital = 再投资资本
