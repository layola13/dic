greyer:/ˈɡreɪər/| adj.|更灰的，更苍老的|More gray;  becoming grayer. It describes a gradual increase in grayness or a process of becoming gray.  This word is primarily used to describe hair, but can also be used metaphorically to describe other things losing vibrancy or becoming dull.|例句：His hair is getting greyer each year. = 他每年头发都越来越灰白。
The once vibrant city felt greyer and more desolate after the war. = 战后，曾经充满活力的城市显得更加灰暗和荒凉。|近义词：grayish, hoary, silvered|反义词：blacker, brighter, more colorful|形容词
