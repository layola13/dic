preconversations:/ˌpriːkɒnˈvɜːseɪʃənz/ | n. |预先对话；会谈前的交流 | Preliminary discussions or exchanges that take place before a formal conversation or meeting.  These conversations often serve to establish rapport, set the agenda, or clarify expectations. | Example: The preconversations between the diplomats helped to smooth the way for the official negotiations.  预先对话帮助外交官们为正式谈判铺平了道路。 |近义词： preliminary talks, preparatory discussions, initial exchanges | 反义词：post-conversation, follow-up discussions |名词
