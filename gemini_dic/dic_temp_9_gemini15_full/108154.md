latitat: /ˈlætɪtæt/ | n. |潜逃；躲藏；躲避；(法律)潜逃令 |  A writ ordering the arrest of a person who has failed to appear in court.  It is now largely obsolete.  The term also refers to the act of hiding or absconding. |例句：The defendant issued a latitat to avoid being arrested.|近义词：absconding, hiding, fleeing, evasion |反义词：appearance, surrender


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hides from the police. = 他躲避警察。
She avoids her creditors. = 她躲避债主。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
They have been hiding from the authorities for months. = 他们已经躲避当局几个月了。
She has evaded capture for years. = 她多年来一直逃避抓捕。
