irrevealable: /ˌɪrɪˈviːləbl/ | adj. | 不可揭露的，不可泄露的 |  Unable to be revealed or disclosed; kept secret.  This word implies a secrecy that is inherent and cannot be overcome.  | Example: The agent's mission was irrevealable, for revealing it would jeopardize national security.  特工的任务是不可泄露的，因为泄露它会危及国家安全。 | 近义词: undisclosed, confidential, secret, hidden | 反义词: revealable,  disclosable
