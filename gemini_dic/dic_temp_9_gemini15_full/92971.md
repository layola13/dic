idio:/ˈɪdiəʊ/ | n. |习语，成语，方言|A phrase or expression whose meaning cannot be understood from the meanings of its individual words. Idioms are often figurative and culturally specific. |例句： "Kick the bucket" is an idiom meaning "to die".  “一脚踢桶”是表示“死亡”的习语。

|例句：He used a lot of colorful idioms in his speech. 他在演讲中使用了许多生动的习语。|近义词：expression, phrase, saying |反义词：literal meaning
