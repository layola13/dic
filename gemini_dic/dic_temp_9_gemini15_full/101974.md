isosceles: /aɪˈsɒsəliːz/ | adj. | 等腰的 | Having two sides of equal length.  An isosceles triangle has at least two sides of equal length. | Example: The isosceles triangle has two sides of 5cm and one side of 8cm.  这个等腰三角形有两条边长5厘米，一条边长8厘米。| 近义词:equilateral (if all three sides are equal) | 反义词:scalene (if all three sides are unequal) | 形容词
