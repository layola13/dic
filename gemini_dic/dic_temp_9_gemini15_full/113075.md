luna:/ˈluːnə/| /ˈluːnɑː/ | n. |月亮；鲁娜（女子名） | Luna is the Roman goddess of the moon, and the word itself comes from the Latin word for moon.  It's used as a name, often for girls, and can also refer to the moon itself.  | Example: The full moon, Luna, shone brightly in the night sky.  满月，鲁娜，在夜空中明亮地照耀着。  The name Luna is very popular. 名字鲁娜非常流行。 |近义词：moon, satellite (as a celestial body) |反义词：sun (as an opposite celestial body)
