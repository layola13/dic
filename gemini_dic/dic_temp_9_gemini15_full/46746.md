dandruffs:/ˈdændrʌfs/| n.|头皮屑|Dead skin cells from the scalp that appear as white flakes. Dandruff is a common condition that can affect people of all ages.|例句：He suffers from severe dandruff and needs to use a medicated shampoo. 他患有严重的 头皮屑，需要使用药用洗发水。  He has a lot of dandruff on his shoulders.他的肩膀上有很多头皮屑。|近义词：scalp scales, scurf|反义词:clean scalp
