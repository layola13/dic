restricter: /rɪˈstrɪktər/ | n. | 限制器，约束物 | A device or mechanism that restricts something; something that limits or controls.  A person or thing that restricts. |例句：The valve acts as a restricter, controlling the flow of water.  阀门充当限制器，控制水流。

The speed restricter on the car prevented it from going over 80 mph. 车上的速度限制器阻止其速度超过80英里/小时。


近义词：limiter, regulator, controller, constraint, restraint | 反义词：enhancer, facilitator, expander


词源:  Derived from the verb "restrict," ultimately from the Latin "restrictus," the past participle of "restringere," meaning "to bind back."  The suffix "-er" indicates an agent or instrument.
