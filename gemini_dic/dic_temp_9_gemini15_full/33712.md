circadian:/sərˈkeɪdiən/| adj.|生理节律的；昼夜节律的|relating to, or occurring in, roughly 24-hour cycles; relating to the biological processes that occur within a 24-hour cycle.  Circadian rhythms regulate various physiological processes, including sleep-wake cycles, hormone release, and body temperature.|例句：Our circadian rhythm is affected by light exposure.我们的昼夜节律受光照影响。

例句：Disruptions to the circadian rhythm can lead to health problems. 昼夜节律紊乱会导致健康问题。


近义词：diurnal (if referring specifically to daily cycles), rhythmic, cyclical|反义词：None directly opposite, but a disrupted or arrhythmic cycle could be considered an antonym in context. |词性:形容词
