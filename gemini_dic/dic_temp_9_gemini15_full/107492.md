lancer:/ˈlɑːnsər/ | n. | 骑枪兵； lancia 汽车；（尤指）长矛兵；| A lancer is a soldier who fights on horseback, armed with a lance.  The term can also refer to a type of car (Lancia).  Historically, lancers were known for their cavalry charges. | Example:  The lancer charged across the battlefield.  骑枪兵冲过战场。


The regiment consisted largely of lancers.  这个团大部分是由骑枪兵组成的。


近义词：cavalier, horseman, knight  | 反义词：foot soldier, infantryman | 词性: 名词


Etymology:  The word "lancer" comes from the Old French word "lanceor," which itself derives from the Latin word "lancea," meaning "lance" or "spear."  The evolution reflects the historical association of the lancer with the use of the lance as a primary weapon.
