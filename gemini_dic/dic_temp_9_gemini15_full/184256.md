resonances:/ˈrezənənsɪz/| n.|共振；共鸣；回响|The phenomenon where a vibrating system or external force drives another system to oscillate with greater amplitude at a specific preferential frequency.  This occurs when the frequency of the driving force is equal or close to a natural frequency of the driven system.  Resonances can be found in various fields, including acoustics, electronics, and mechanics. |例句：The singer's voice filled the hall with beautiful resonances.  演唱者的歌声在礼堂中回荡着美丽的共鸣。

The bridge collapsed due to resonances caused by strong winds.  由于强风引起的共振，桥梁倒塌了。


近义词：vibration, echo, reverberation, sympathy|反义词：silence, deadness, damping|名词
