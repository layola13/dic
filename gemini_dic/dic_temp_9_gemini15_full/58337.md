drywalls:/ˈdraɪwɔːlz/ | n. |轻质隔墙，石膏板墙 |  Pre-fabricated panels made of gypsum, used in the construction of interior walls.  They are lightweight, easy to install, and provide good sound insulation. |例句：The contractor installed drywall in the new addition to the house.  承包商在新扩建的房子里安装了石膏板墙。

例句：We need to repair the damaged drywall after the accident. 事故后我们需要修理损坏的石膏板墙。


近义词：plasterboard, gypsum board | 反义词：load-bearing wall (承重墙)
