patronizingly:/ˈpætɹənaɪzɪŋli/ | adv. | 以一种屈尊俯就的语气；摆出恩赐的姿态 |  Speaking or behaving in a way that shows you think you are superior to someone else.  It implies a condescending and often insincere attitude. | 例句：He patronizingly offered to help me with my homework, as if I were a child. 他以一种屈尊俯就的语气主动提出帮我做作业，仿佛我是个孩子。


例句：She spoke to him patronizingly, as if he were incapable of understanding. 她对他说话的态度很傲慢，仿佛他什么都不懂。


近义词：condescendingly, condescending, superiorly, superciliously


反义词：respectfully, humbly, sincerely
