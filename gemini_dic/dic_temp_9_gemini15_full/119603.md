mid-way:/ˌmɪdˈweɪ/| adj.|中途的，半路上的|Halfway; at the middle point.|例句：We met midway between our two homes. 我们在两个家之间中途相遇。|近义词：halfway, halfway point|反义词：fully, completely|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting is midway through its agenda. = 会议进行到议程的一半。
The project is midway between planning and execution. = 项目正处于规划和执行的中途。


一般过去时:
The hikers stopped midway up the mountain. = 登山者在半山腰停了下来。
The car broke down midway to the destination. = 汽车在去目的地的半路上抛锚了。


现在完成时:
We have completed midway through the project. = 我们已经完成了项目的一半。
They have reached midway point of the marathon. = 他们已经到达了马拉松比赛的中途点。


过去完成时:
They had already passed the midway point before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经过了中途点。
We had finished midway through the book when the lights went out. = 灯灭的时候，我们已经读完了书的一半。


将来时:
We will stop midway for a rest. = 我们将在中途停下来休息。
They will meet midway between two cities. = 他们将在两座城市之间会面。


虚拟语气:
If we had started earlier, we would have reached midway by now. = 如果我们早点出发，现在就应该到达中途了。
I wish we were midway through the journey already. = 我希望我们已经走到了旅程的一半。


被动语态:
The meeting was interrupted midway. = 会议中途被打断了。
The construction was paused midway due to the rain. = 由于下雨，施工中途暂停了。


疑问句:
Are we midway through the presentation? = 我们是否完成了演示的一半？
Is the journey midway completed? = 旅程进行到一半了吗？


否定句:
The flight was not midway yet. = 飞机还没到中途。
The work was not finished midway. = 工作没有完成一半。
