interfacer:/ˈɪntərfeɪsər/ | n. |接口器，界面器 | A device or program that connects two different systems or devices, enabling them to communicate and exchange data.  It acts as a mediator or translator between incompatible systems. |Example: The interfacer allowed the old printer to work with the new computer.  接口器允许旧打印机与新电脑一起工作。|近义词：adapter, connector, mediator |反义词：separator, divider |名词
