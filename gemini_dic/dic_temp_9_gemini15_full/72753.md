fogged:/fɒɡd/ /fɑːɡd/| v. adj.|  使充满雾；模糊不清的；困惑的 |To cover or obscure with fog; to make unclear or indistinct; to confuse or bewilder. |例句：The valley was fogged in. 山谷被大雾笼罩。The details of the plan were fogged. 计划的细节模糊不清。|近义词：hazy, misty, obscured, confused |反义词：clear, distinct, lucid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fog often fogs the valley. = 雾经常笼罩山谷。
He fogs the mirror with his breath. = 他用呼吸使镜子起雾。


一般过去时:
The heavy fog fogged the airport yesterday. = 浓雾昨天使机场关闭。
The window fogged up quickly. = 窗户很快就起雾了。


现在完成时:
The cold weather has fogged my glasses. = 寒冷的天气使我的眼镜起雾了。
The long journey has fogged my mind. = 漫长的旅程让我头脑昏昏沉沉。


过去完成时:
The driver had fogged the rear window, making it difficult to see. = 司机已经使后车窗起雾，使得难以看清。
They had fogged the issue with unnecessary details. = 他们用不必要的细节使问题变得模糊。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that fog will fog the coast tomorrow. = 预报预测明天浓雾将笼罩海岸。
I think the cold air will fog up the windows tonight. = 我认为今晚冷空气会使窗户起雾。


虚拟语气:
If the weather had been clear, the valley wouldn't have been fogged. = 如果天气晴朗，山谷就不会被雾笼罩。
If I hadn't been so tired, I wouldn't have been so fogged. = 如果我不那么累，我就不会那么迷糊。


被动语态:
The window was fogged by my breath. = 我的呼吸使窗户起雾了。
The area was fogged in by a thick mist. = 该地区被浓雾笼罩。


疑问句:
Is the window fogged? = 窗户起雾了吗？
Was the airport fogged in yesterday? = 昨天机场被雾封了吗？


否定句:
The road wasn't fogged. = 道路没有被雾笼罩。
The details weren't fogged; they were simply unclear. = 细节并非模糊不清；它们只是不清楚。


PHRASE->
fogged in:  被雾笼罩
fogged up: 起雾；模糊不清
fogged out: 被雾挡住视线
fogged over: 模糊起来；变得不清晰
fogged memory: 模糊的记忆
