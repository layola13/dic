self-centredness:/ˈsɛlfˈsɛntədnəs/| n. |自我中心，利己主义 |The state of being self-centered; preoccupation with oneself and one's own interests.  It often involves a lack of consideration for the feelings or needs of others. |例句：His self-centredness made him unpopular with his colleagues.  他的自我中心让他在同事中不受欢迎。

例句：Her self-centredness prevented her from seeing the impact her actions had on others.  她的自我中心让她看不到自己的行为对他人造成的影响。


近义词：egotism, narcissism, selfishness, egocentricity |反义词：altruism, selflessness, consideration


词源：

* **self-**:  Old English *self*, meaning "self, same."
* **-centred**:  From "centre," ultimately from Old French *centre*, from Latin *centrum*, meaning "center." The "-ed" suffix indicates an adjective form ("centered on oneself").
* **-ness**:  Old English *nes*, a suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or state.
