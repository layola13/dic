hatches:/ˈhætʃɪz/| n. v.|舱口；孵化；|A hatch is an opening, especially one in the deck of a ship or aircraft, or in a floor or wall.  It can also refer to the act of hatching eggs or emerging from an egg. |例句：The ship's hatches were sealed during the storm.  暴风雨期间，船的舱口被封住了。 The hen is patiently hatching her eggs. 母鸡正耐心地孵蛋。|近义词：opening, port, hatchway, incubate|反义词：close, seal|名词，动词
