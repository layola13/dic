mangling:/ˈmæŋɡlɪŋ/| n. |严重损坏；糟蹋；乱七八糟 |The mangling of the text made it almost unintelligible.  文本被严重损坏，几乎无法理解。|近义词：mutilation, destruction, disfigurement |反义词：repair, restoration, preservation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is mangling the fabric. = 机器正在损坏织物。
He mangles the English language. = 他把英语说得一塌糊涂。


一般过去时:
The storm mangled the trees. = 暴风雨摧毁了树木。
She mangled the recipe, resulting in a inedible dish. = 她把菜谱搞砸了，做出来的菜没法吃。


现在完成时:
They have mangled the original design. = 他们已经破坏了最初的设计。
The critics have mangled his reputation. = 批评家们已经毁了他的名声。


过去完成时:
He had mangled the car before the accident. = 事故发生前他已经把车撞坏了。
The thieves had mangled the lock before entering the house. = 小偷在进入房子之前已经弄坏了锁。


将来时:
