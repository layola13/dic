overpersecuting: /ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrsɪkjuːtɪŋ/ | v. |过度迫害，残酷迫害 |  The act of persecuting someone excessively or unjustly.  It implies a more severe and prolonged persecution than simply "persecuting." |例句：The government was accused of overpersecuting religious minorities. 政府被指控过度迫害宗教少数群体。

The historical persecution of the Rohingya people is a tragic example of overpersecuting a minority group. 罗兴亚人的历史迫害是过度迫害少数群体的悲剧例子。


近义词：oppressing, tyrannizing, tormenting, brutalizing | 反义词：protecting, defending, shielding, supporting | 词性:动词
