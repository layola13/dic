crabbing:/ˈkræbɪŋ/| n. v.|捕蟹；抱怨；螃蟹状行走|The act of catching crabs;  the act of complaining in a grumpy or irritable way; to move sideways like a crab. |例句：He spent the afternoon crabbing on the beach. 他在海滩上度过了一下午捕蟹。 He's been crabbing about the weather all day. 他整天都在抱怨天气。|近义词：fishing (for crabs), grumbling, complaining, grumping|反义词：praising, complimenting, rejoicing

He is constantly crabbing about his job. = 他一直在抱怨他的工作。
