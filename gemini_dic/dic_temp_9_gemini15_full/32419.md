chilopoda:/kɪˈlɒpədə/| n. |蜈蚣类动物|Chilopoda is the class of myriapods that contains centipedes.  They are elongated, flattened animals with one pair of legs per segment.  They are carnivorous predators, typically hunting insects and other small invertebrates. |Example:  Many species of chilopoda are found in tropical regions.  许多种类的蜈蚣都发现于热带地区。|近义词:centipede (though centipede refers to a specific member of the class)|反义词:None|名词
