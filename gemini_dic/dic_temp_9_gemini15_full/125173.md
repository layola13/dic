multibusiness:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪznɪs/| adj. n.|多业务的；多行业经营的 | Relating to or involving many different businesses or types of business.  A company with multiple lines of business. |例句：The multibusiness corporation diversified its investments to mitigate risk. 这家多业务公司将其投资多元化以降低风险。  Their multibusiness strategy led to significant growth. 他们的多业务战略带来了显著增长。|近义词：diversified, multinational, conglomerate |反义词：single-business, specialized, focused
