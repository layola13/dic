compensates:/ˈkɒmpənseɪts/ /ˈkɑːmpənseɪts/ | vt. |补偿，赔偿；抵消；弥补|To make up for something bad or undesirable; to offset or counterbalance something.|The company compensates its employees for their hard work.  公司补偿员工的辛勤工作。|The beautiful scenery compensates for the long journey. 美丽的景色弥补了漫长的旅程。|reimburse, recompense, offset |detract, worsen |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company compensates its employees well. = 公司给员工很好的报酬。
He compensates for his lack of experience with hard work. = 他用努力工作来弥补经验不足。

一般过去时:
The insurance company compensated the victim for his injuries. = 保险公司赔偿受害者的伤亡。
They compensated me for the damaged goods. = 他们赔偿了我损坏的货物。

现在完成时:
The government has compensated the farmers for their crop losses. = 政府已经赔偿农民的作物损失。
She has compensated for her initial mistakes. = 她已经弥补了她最初的错误。

过去完成时:
He had compensated the landlord before moving out. = 他在搬出去之前已经赔偿了房东。
They had compensated the workers for the lost wages.  = 他们已经赔偿了工人们损失的工资。

将来时:
The company will compensate the customers for the faulty product. = 公司将赔偿顾客有缺陷的产品。
We will compensate you for your time and effort. = 我们将补偿你的时间和精力。

虚拟语气:
If the company compensated its employees fairly, there would be less turnover. = 如果公司公平地补偿员工，人员流动就会减少。
I wish they had compensated me for the inconvenience. = 我希望他们能补偿我造成的麻烦。

被动语态:
The employees are compensated fairly by the company. = 员工受到公司的公平报酬。
The damage will be compensated for by the insurance company. = 损失将由保险公司赔偿。

疑问句:
Does the company compensate its employees for overtime? = 公司是否补偿员工的加班费？
How will the company compensate for the lost revenue? = 公司将如何弥补损失的收入？

否定句:
The company does not compensate its employees for travel expenses. = 公司不报销员工的差旅费。
They did not compensate the customer for the defective product. = 他们没有赔偿客户有缺陷的产品。
