perms:/pɜːmz/| n.|权限，许可；(头发的)烫发|Permissions, authorizations, or entitlements granted to a user or system.  It often refers to the ability to access, modify, or delete data or resources. In the context of hair, it refers to a permanent wave.|例句：The administrator has the perms to access all files.  管理员有权限访问所有文件。|例句：She got a perms to add volume to her hair. 她烫了发增加发量。|近义词：privileges, authorizations, access rights|反义词：restrictions, limitations, denials|名词
