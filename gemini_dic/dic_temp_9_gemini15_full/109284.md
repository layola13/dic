lemonlike:/ˈlɛmənlaɪk/| adj. |像柠檬的；柠檬似的|Resembling a lemon in color, shape, or taste.  The word combines "lemon" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The candy had a lemonlike tartness.  这糖果有一种柠檬般的酸味。

The curtains were a pale lemonlike yellow. 窗帘是浅柠檬黄色的。


近义词：lemony, citrusy, acidulous


反义词：sweet, sugary


词性:形容词
