metrician:/mɛˈtrɪʃən/| n. |测量员；计量学家|A metrician is a specialist in measurement, often in a specific field like surveying, construction, or engineering. They use various tools and techniques to obtain precise measurements.  |Example: The metrician carefully surveyed the land before construction began. 测量员在施工开始前仔细测量了土地。 |近义词：measurer, surveyor, gauger|反义词：None specifically.
