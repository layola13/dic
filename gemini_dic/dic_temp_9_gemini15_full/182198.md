remonetization: /ˌriːˌmʌnɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 再货币化；重新货币化 | The process of returning something to a monetary value or system after it has been demonetized or removed from a monetary system.  It often involves assigning a new value or converting it back into a usable currency. | 例句：The remonetization of the old currency notes caused significant disruption to the economy.  再货币化旧钞票给经济带来了严重混乱。

The government is considering the remonetization of certain assets to boost economic activity. 政府正在考虑对某些资产进行再货币化以促进经济活动。


近义词：revaluation, restoration of monetary value | 反义词：demonetization

词性：名词
