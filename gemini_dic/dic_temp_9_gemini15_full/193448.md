selassie:/səˈlæsi/| n. |塞拉西；海莱·塞拉西一世|A proper noun referring to Haile Selassie I, Emperor of Ethiopia (1892-1975),  a prominent figure in Ethiopian and Pan-African history.  The name is also used in Rastafarianism, where Selassie is revered as God incarnate.|Example: Many Rastafarians believe Haile Selassie I was the returned Christ. 许多拉斯特法里教徒相信海莱·塞拉西一世是复活的基督。|近义词: Emperor (of Ethiopia),  Jah (in Rastafarian context)|反义词: None.
