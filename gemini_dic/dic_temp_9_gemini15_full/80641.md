gopher:/ˈɡoʊfər/ | n. |囊鼠；地鼠；(计算机) गोफर程序 | A gopher is a small rodent that lives underground and is known for its cheek pouches.  In computing, a gopher is a program that retrieves information from the Internet. |例句：The gopher dug a deep burrow in the ground.  这只囊鼠在地上挖了一个很深的洞。|近义词：pocket gopher, ground squirrel,  (computing) web browser|反义词：None specifically.
