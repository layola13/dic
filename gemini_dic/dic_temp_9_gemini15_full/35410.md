co-options:/ˌkoʊˈɑːpʃənz/| n. | 合作选择；挪用；兼并；|The adoption or adaptation of something for a new purpose.  Co-option can refer to the process by which a pre-existing structure or pathway is recruited for a new function, often in evolution or development.|例句：The company's co-option of existing technology proved very successful. 这家公司对现有技术的兼并证明非常成功。
The evolution of feathers from scales is a classic example of co-option. 羽毛从鳞片进化而来的例子是合作选择的经典案例。|近义词：appropriation, adoption, recruitment |反义词：invention, creation
