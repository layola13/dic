outsmarted:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrtɪd/| v. |智胜，比…更聪明| Past tense and past participle of outsmart.  It means to be more clever or intelligent than someone else, especially in a way that leads to their defeat or failure. |例句：The detective outsmarted the cunning thief.  侦探智胜了狡猾的小偷。

The cunning fox outsmarted the hunter. 狡猾的狐狸智胜了猎人。


近义词：outwit, outmaneuver,  trick, deceive |反义词：be outwitted, be tricked, be defeated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often outsmarts his opponents in chess. 他经常在国际象棋中智胜对手。
She outsmarts her rivals in business deals.  她在商业交易中智胜对手。


一般过去时:
