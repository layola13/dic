photoinducible: /ˌfoʊtoʊɪnˈduːsəbl/ | adj. | 光诱导的，光诱发的 | Capable of being induced or activated by light.  This adjective describes processes or systems that are triggered or initiated by exposure to light.  The term combines "photo" (relating to light) and "inducible" (capable of being induced).


例句：

The photoinducible system allowed for precise control of gene expression.  光诱导系统允许对基因表达进行精确控制。

Researchers developed a photoinducible dimerization system for controlling protein interactions. 研究人员开发了一种光诱导的二聚化系统来控制蛋白质相互作用。


近义词：light-activated, light-triggered, photosensitive


反义词：light-insensitive, dark-induced


词性: adjective
