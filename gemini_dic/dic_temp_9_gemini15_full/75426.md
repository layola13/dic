fruitarian:/fruːˈteəriən/| n.|水果食者；素食者(只吃水果)|A fruitarian is a person who eats only fruits, nuts, seeds, and other plant products that do not require the killing of the plant to harvest.  This diet is often considered extreme and may lead to nutritional deficiencies. |Example:  She adopted a fruitarian diet for ethical reasons. 她出于伦理原因采用了水果食者的饮食。|近义词：vegan, vegetarian|反义词：carnivore
