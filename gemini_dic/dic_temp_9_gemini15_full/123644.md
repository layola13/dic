monts:/mɒnts/| n.|月份，山脉|Months are periods of time used in calendars.  A range of mountains can also be referred to as monts.|例句：There are twelve months in a year. 一年有十二个月。|例句：The Alps are a majestic range of monts. 阿尔卑斯山脉是一座雄伟的山脉。|近义词：periods, ranges|反义词：day, single peak


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The month of August is usually very hot. = 八月份通常很热。
Many months pass before we see each other again. = 好几个月后我们才能再见面。

一般过去时:
Last month was very busy. = 上个月非常忙碌。

将来时:
Next month, I will be on vacation. = 下个月我将休假。
We will have finished the project within the next few months. = 我们将在未来几个月内完成该项目。


虚拟语气:
