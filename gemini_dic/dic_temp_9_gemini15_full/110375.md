lignin:/ˈlɪɡnɪn/| n.|木质素|A complex polymer that is a major component of the cell walls of plants, particularly in woody tissue.  It provides rigidity and strength to plant structures.| 例句：The lignin content of the wood affected its burning properties. 木材中的木质素含量影响了它的燃烧性能。

The presence of lignin makes wood strong and durable. 木质素的存在使木材坚固耐用。|近义词： none perfectly synonymous, but  related terms include  cellulose, hemicellulose |反义词：none directly opposite, but terms implying absence of rigidity could be used in contrast, like  softness, flexibility|名词
