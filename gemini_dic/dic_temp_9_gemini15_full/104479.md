kaizens:/kaɪˈzɛnz/| n.|改进；改善；改善措施|The plural form of "kaizen,"  referring to continuous improvement efforts or the practices involved. It emphasizes incremental, ongoing changes rather than radical transformations.  Kaizen is a Japanese word meaning "change for the better" or "improvement."|例句：The company implemented several kaizens to streamline its production process.  这家公司实施了几项改进措施以简化其生产流程。|近义词：improvements, enhancements, optimizations|反义词：deterioration, decline, worsening|名词
