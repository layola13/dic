re-digesting:/ˌriːdaɪˈdʒɛstɪŋ/| v.|重新消化；反复思考|The process of reconsidering or re-evaluating previously digested information or experiences.  It implies a deeper, more thorough processing than the initial digestion.|例句：After the initial shock, she spent weeks re-digesting the implications of the news.  =  最初的震惊过后，她花了几个星期的时间反复思考这个消息的含义。


例句：He kept re-digesting the lecture notes to fully grasp the concepts. = 他不断地重新消化讲义笔记，以完全掌握这些概念。


近义词：reconsidering, reviewing, ruminating, pondering


反义词：ignoring, dismissing, overlooking


词性：动词

USAGE->

He re-digests the information before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前会重新消化信息。
She re-digests her experiences to learn from them. = 她重新消化自己的经历以便从中学习。


一般过去时:
