intangle:/ɪnˈtæŋɡəl/| vt.|使纠缠；使复杂化；|to twist together or become twisted together into a confused mass; to involve someone or something in difficulties or complications|The bird became entangled in the fishing net.  鸟被渔网缠住了。

The political situation is increasingly entangled. 政治局势越来越复杂。|entangle, complicate, involve| disentangle, simplify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often entangles himself in complex situations. 他经常把自己卷入复杂的境地。
The vines entangle the trees in the garden. 葡萄藤缠绕着花园里的树木。


一般过去时:
The fisherman untangled the fishing net after it became entangled in seaweed. 渔夫在渔网被海藻缠住后解开了渔网。
The argument entangled them in a bitter dispute. 那场争论使他们卷入了一场激烈的争端。


现在完成时:
The project has become entangled in bureaucratic red tape.  这个项目已被官僚主义的繁文缛节所拖累。
She has entangled herself in a web of lies. 她把自己卷入了一个谎言的圈套。


过去完成时:
Before the police arrived, the thieves had entangled themselves in a chase. 在警察到达之前，小偷们已经互相追逐纠缠在一起了。
The witnesses had entangled themselves in conflicting accounts of the accident. 目击者们在事故的描述中互相矛盾，彼此纠缠不清。


将来时:
The negotiations will likely entangle the two countries in a prolonged dispute.  谈判很可能会使这两个国家陷入长期的争端。
I fear that this decision will entangle us in more problems. 我担心这个决定会让我们陷入更多的问题。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't entangled himself in that business deal, he would be much richer now. 如果他没有卷入那笔生意，他现在会富有得多。
I wish I hadn't entangled myself in this complicated project. 我真希望我没有卷入这个复杂的项目。


被动语态:
The climber was entangled in the ropes. 登山者被绳索缠住了。
The issue was entangled with many other problems. 这个问题与许多其他问题纠缠在一起。


疑问句:
Is the wire entangled in the machine? 电线缠在机器上了吗？
Are you entangled in any other projects right now? 你现在还参与其他项目吗？


否定句:
The two issues are not entangled. 这两个问题没有纠缠在一起。
He didn't want to entangle himself in their family affairs. 他不想卷入他们的家庭事务。
