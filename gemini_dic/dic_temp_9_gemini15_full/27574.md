The process involves excavating, shaping, and lining waterways to facilitate the transport of water or goods. |例句：The canalling project transformed the region's transportation infrastructure. 运河建设项目改变了该地区的交通基础设施。  The canalling of the river improved irrigation for the surrounding farmland.  河流的沟渠化改善了周边农田的灌溉。|近义词：canalization, channelization |反义词：damming (阻拦), natural waterway (自然水道) |名词
