desalinize:/ˈdiːˌsælɪnaɪz/| vt.|脱盐；淡化|To remove salt from (something, especially seawater)  |例句：The plant desalinizes seawater to provide fresh water for the city.  该工厂将海水淡化以供应城市淡水。
例句：They are experimenting with new methods to desalinize water more efficiently. 他们正在试验更有效率的海水淡化方法。|近义词：desalt, purify|反义词：salinize, salt|动词
