overnationalize: /ˌoʊvərˈnæʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. |使超越国界；使国际化；过度强调民族主义|To place under the control or influence of an international or supranational body; to make something transcend national boundaries or interests.  The process often involves transferring authority from national governments to international organizations.|

例句：

The European Union has attempted to overnationalize certain economic policies.  = 欧洲联盟试图将某些经济政策置于超国家控制之下。

Some argue that overnationalizing environmental regulations could lead to ineffective enforcement. = 有人认为过度强调国际环保条例可能会导致执行不力。


近义词：internationalize, globalize

反义词：nationalize, localize


词性:动词
