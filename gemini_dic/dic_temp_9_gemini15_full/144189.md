outsiderism:/ˈaʊtsaɪdərɪzəm/| n. |局外人思想；局外人的观点；边缘文化；离经叛道|The belief or practice of regarding oneself or a group as an outsider, often with a sense of alienation or opposition to mainstream society or culture.  It can encompass artistic, political, or social perspectives. |例句：His paintings are a powerful expression of outsiderism, reflecting his alienation from the art establishment. 他的画作有力地表达了局外人思想，反映了他与艺术界的疏离。

例句：The band's music is characterized by its outsiderism, blending genres and rejecting conventional pop structures.  这个乐队的音乐以其离经叛道的风格为特色，融合了各种类型，并拒绝了传统的流行音乐结构。


近义词：marginality, nonconformity, unconventionality, dissent

反义词：conformity, mainstream, orthodoxy
