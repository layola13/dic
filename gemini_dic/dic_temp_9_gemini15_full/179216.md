redismissal: /ˌriːdɪsˈmɪsəl/ | n. | 再解雇，重新解雇 | The act of dismissing someone again after they have been previously dismissed.  It implies a reinstatement followed by a second dismissal. | Example: The employee's redismissal was deemed unfair due to lack of sufficient evidence.  员工的再次解雇由于缺乏足够的证据而被认为是不公平的。|近义词：rejection,reinstatement followed by dismissal | 反义词：hiring, employment, reinstatement
