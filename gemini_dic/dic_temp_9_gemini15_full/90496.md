hucksterdom:/ˈhʌkstərdəm/| n. |江湖骗子行径；欺骗；兜售行为 |The practice or conduct of a huckster; the state of being a huckster;  cheap or fraudulent salesmanship.  It often implies dishonesty and manipulation. |例句：The hucksterdom of the used car salesman was evident in his exaggerated claims.  这辆二手车的推销员的江湖骗子行径在他夸大的说法中显而易见。


The constant barrage of advertising is a form of hucksterdom.  广告的持续轰炸是一种欺骗行为。|近义词：racket, swindle, fraud, deception,  salesmanship (negative connotation)|反义词：honesty, integrity, truthfulness
