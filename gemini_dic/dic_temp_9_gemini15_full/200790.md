slickens:/ˈslɪkənz/| n.|擦痕，镜面构造|polished surfaces produced by the movement of rock masses against each other.  These are often found in fault zones and are indicative of significant tectonic activity.|Example: The slickensides on the fault plane showed evidence of significant horizontal displacement.|近义词：slickenside, polished fault surface|反义词：rough surface
