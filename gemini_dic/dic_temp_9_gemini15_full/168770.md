prodigious:/prəˈdɪdʒəs/| adj.|巨大的；惊人的；异常的；天才的 |  remarkably or impressively great in extent, size, or degree.  |例句：He possessed a prodigious memory. 他拥有惊人的记忆力。

例句：She showed a prodigious talent for music at a young age. 她很小的时候就展现了非凡的音乐天赋。


近义词：enormous, immense, vast, tremendous, extraordinary, amazing, remarkable, marvelous


反义词：tiny, insignificant, minuscule, negligible


词源:  From Middle French *prodigieux, from Latin *prodigiosus, from prodigio (prodigious event, omen), from prodigium (“an omen, portent, prodigy,” literally “something shown forth”).  The word's development reflects its connection to the idea of something so remarkable it seems almost supernatural or miraculous.
