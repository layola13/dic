reminiscences:/ˌremɪˈnɪsnsəz/| n.|回忆录；回忆；往事|The act or process of remembering past experiences; memories or recollections, often written down.  They can be personal accounts of past events, experiences, or people. |例句：Her reminiscences of childhood were filled with warmth and humor.  她的童年回忆充满了温暖和幽默。


例句：His book of reminiscences is a fascinating read. 他的回忆录读起来很吸引人。


近义词：memories, recollections, memoirs, recollections, reflections


反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion


词性: 名词
