shallot:/ʃəˈlɒt/| n. |小香葱；细香葱 |A shallot is a type of onion with a milder, more delicate flavor than a regular onion.  It is commonly used in cooking as a flavoring agent.  Shallots are typically smaller than onions and have a cluster of small bulbs. |例句：The recipe calls for finely chopped shallots. 这个食谱需要切碎的小香葱。

例句：I added some shallots to the soup for extra flavor. 我在汤里加了一些小香葱来增加香味。


近义词：onion, garlic (but with a milder flavor)

反义词：无


词源:The word "shallot" comes from the French word "échalote," which itself is ultimately derived from the Latin word "Ascalonia," referring to Ascalon, a city in ancient Palestine where shallots were believed to have originated.
