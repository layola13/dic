reconfrontation: /ˌriːkənfrʌnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 再对质；再次对抗 | The act of confronting someone or something again, often after a period of time or a change in circumstances.  It implies a renewed or repeated confrontation, suggesting unresolved issues or a continuation of conflict. |例句：The reconfrontation with his past traumas led to a breakthrough in his therapy.  再次面对过去的创伤，导致了他的治疗取得了突破。

The reconfrontation between the two countries over the border dispute heightened tensions.  两国就边界争端再次对抗加剧了紧张局势。

近义词：rematch, renewed conflict, second confrontation, fresh confrontation | 反义词：reconciliation, resolution, settlement
