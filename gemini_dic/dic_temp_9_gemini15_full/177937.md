rechristens:/ˌriːˈkrɪsən/| vt.|重新命名，改名|To give a new name to something or someone.  The act of giving a new name, often implying a significant change in identity or purpose.|例句：The company rechristened its product to better reflect its improved features. 公司为其产品重新命名，以更好地反映其改进的功能。

例句：The old building was rechristened as a museum after extensive renovations.  经过大规模翻修后，这座旧建筑被重新命名为博物馆。


近义词：rename, retitle, redesignate|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to the original name or purpose.|词性:动词
