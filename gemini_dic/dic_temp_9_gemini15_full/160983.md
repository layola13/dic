postalveolar:/ˌpoʊstælˈviːələr/| adj.|后齿龈的|Situated or produced at the back of the alveolar ridge, behind the teeth.  This refers to the area in the mouth where sounds are articulated.  The term is primarily used in phonetics and linguistics to describe sounds made with the tongue contacting the area just behind the alveolar ridge.|The 'sh' sound in 'ship' is a postalveolar fricative.|近义词：post-alveolar|反义词：alveolar, bilabial
