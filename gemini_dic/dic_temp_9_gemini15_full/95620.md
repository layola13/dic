incompass:/ɪnˈkʌmpəs/| vt.| 包含；包围；围绕 | To include or contain; to encircle or surround.  |例句：The study encompassed a wide range of topics. 这项研究涵盖了广泛的主题。

例句：The vast forest encompassed the small village.  这片广阔的森林环绕着小山村。

近义词：include, enclose, surround, embrace

反义词：exclude, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project encompasses many different tasks. = 这个项目包含许多不同的任务。
Her work encompasses several disciplines. = 她的工作涵盖多个学科。

一般过去时:
The treaty encompassed all the major issues. = 该条约涵盖了所有主要问题。
The ancient city encompassed a vast area. = 古代城市占地辽阔。

现在完成时:
His influence has encompassed the whole field. = 他的影响已经波及整个领域。
Our investigation has encompassed all available evidence. = 我们的调查已经涵盖了所有现有证据。

过去完成时:
The empire had encompassed most of Europe by that time. = 到那时为止，帝国已经涵盖了欧洲大部分地区。
The original plan had encompassed a much larger project. = 原计划包含一个更大的项目。

将来时:

Their future plans encompass world travel. = 他们的未来计划包括环球旅行。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "incompass" comes from the Old French "encompasser,"  meaning "to surround" or "to enclose."  The prefix "en-" means "in" or "into," and "compasser" is related to the word "compass," meaning a tool for drawing circles, thus highlighting the sense of surrounding or enclosing.
