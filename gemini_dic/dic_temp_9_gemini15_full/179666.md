reenjoy:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɔɪ/| vt.|再次享受，重新享受|To enjoy something again, after a period of not enjoying it.|例句：After a long break, I'm reenjoying my hobby of painting.  在长时间的休息后，我重新享受着我的绘画爱好。

例句：We reenjoyed the beautiful scenery on our second visit.  我们在第二次访问时再次欣赏了美丽的风景。


近义词：rediscover, relish, savor


反义词：dislike, abhor, detest


词性：动词
