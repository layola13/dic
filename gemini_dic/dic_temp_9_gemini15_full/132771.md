nondriver:/ˈnɑːnˌdraɪvər/| n. |非驾驶员，非司机|A person who does not drive a vehicle.  This term often refers to passengers in a car or other forms of transportation.|Example: The nondrivers in the car enjoyed the scenic route.  车上的非驾驶员们欣赏了风景优美的路线。The children were nondrivers on the school bus. 孩子们是校车上非驾驶员。|近义词：passenger, rider|反义词：driver, chauffeur
