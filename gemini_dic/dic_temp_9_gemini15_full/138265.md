not turn a hair:/nɑːt tɜːrn ə hɛər/| phrasal verb|泰然自若；毫不动容|To remain calm and composed in a difficult or stressful situation.  It implies a lack of visible emotional reaction.|例句：He faced the angry mob without turning a hair. 他面对愤怒的暴民毫不动容。

例句：She heard the bad news but didn't turn a hair. 她听到坏消息却毫不动容。


近义词：remain calm, keep one's cool, stay composed, show no emotion

反义词：lose one's composure, panic, freak out


词性: phrasal verb
