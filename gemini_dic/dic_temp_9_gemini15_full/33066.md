chowry:/ˈtʃaʊri/| n.|拂尘，牦牛尾扇|A chowry is a whisk or fly-flap made of yak's tail hair, used to keep away flies and other insects, especially in hot climates.  It is often used ceremonially.|The priest used a chowry to keep the flies away during the ceremony.  祭司用拂尘驱赶仪式期间的苍蝇。|fly whisk, fly-flap|  |n.
