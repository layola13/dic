predefinition:/ˌpriːˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən/ | n. |预定义；预先定义 | A predefinition is a definition made or established before something else. It's a specification or explanation of something before its actual implementation or use.  This often involves setting up parameters, variables, or structures in advance. | The software requires a predefinition of the data types before compilation.  这个软件需要在编译之前预定义数据类型。|近义词：prior definition, advance definition, preliminary definition |反义词：postdefinition (not a common word)
