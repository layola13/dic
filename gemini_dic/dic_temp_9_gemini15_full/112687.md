lowlands:/ˈloʊlændz/ | n. |低地，低洼地 | Areas of land that are lower than the surrounding areas, often characterized by flat or gently rolling terrain and frequently containing marshes, swamps, or other wetlands.  | Example: The fertile lowlands of the Nile River supported ancient Egyptian civilization.  尼罗河肥沃的低地孕育了古埃及文明。  The lowlands are prone to flooding after heavy rainfall. 低地容易在暴雨后发生洪水。 |近义词：plains, valleys, flats |反义词：highlands, uplands, mountains
