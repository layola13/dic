deoxy:/ˌdiːˈɒksi/| /ˌdiːˈɑːksi/ | adj. |脱氧的；去氧的 |lacking oxygen;  referring to a chemical compound that has lost an oxygen atom.  It is often used in the context of organic chemistry, particularly in biochemistry. |Example: Deoxyribonucleic acid (DNA) is a crucial molecule in all living organisms. 脱氧核糖核酸（DNA）是所有生物体中至关重要的分子。|近义词： oxygen-free, deoxygenated |反义词： oxygenated
