didn't:/ˈdɪdnt/| v. |没有，没做|The past tense of "did not". It is a contraction of "did not", used as the past tense of the auxiliary verb "do" in negative sentences.  It is used to form the past simple negative in all persons.|

例句：I didn't go to the party last night. = 我昨晚没去参加派对。

例句：She didn't see him at the meeting. = 她没在会上看到他。


近义词：did not

反义词：did


词性:动词(助动词)
