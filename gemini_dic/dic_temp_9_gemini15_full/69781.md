fermentative:/ˌfɜːrmɛnˈteɪtɪv/| adj. |发酵的；引起发酵的| Relating to or involving fermentation.  The process of fermentation is an anaerobic metabolic process that converts sugars into acids, gases, or alcohol. It's often used in food production and other industrial processes. |例句：The fermentative bacteria produced lactic acid.  发酵细菌产生了乳酸。

The yogurt has a distinctly fermentative smell.  酸奶有一种明显的酵香味。


近义词：fermenting, zymotic| 反义词：sterile, non-fermentative
