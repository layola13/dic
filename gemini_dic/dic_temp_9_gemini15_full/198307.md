shove:/ʃʌv/| vt. n.|猛推，挤；乱塞；| to push someone or something forcefully.|例句：He shoved the box under the bed. 他把箱子塞到了床底下。|近义词：push, thrust, shove|反义词：pull


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoves the door open. = 他猛地推开了门。
They shove their way onto the bus. = 他们挤上了公共汽车。


一般过去时:
She shoved the papers into her bag. = 她把文件塞进了包里。
He shoved me aside and ran. = 他把我推到一边跑了。


现在完成时:
I have shoved all the furniture against the wall. = 我已经把所有的家具都推到了墙边。
They have shoved the blame onto others. = 他们把责任推给了别人。


过去完成时:
He had shoved the evidence under the carpet. = 他已经把证据藏了起来。
She had shoved the food into her mouth. = 她已经狼吞虎咽地把食物吃掉了。


将来时:
I will shove this problem aside for now. = 我现在先把这个问题搁置一边。
They will shove the cart down the hill. = 他们将把车推下山坡。


虚拟语气:
If I shoved him, he would fall. = 如果我推他，他会摔倒。
I wish I hadn't shoved him so hard. = 我真希望我没有那么用力地推他。


被动语态:
The box was shoved under the bed. = 箱子被塞到了床底下。
The blame was shoved onto me. = 责任被推到了我身上。


疑问句:
Did he shove you? = 他推了你吗？
Who shoved the chair? = 谁推的椅子？


否定句:
I didn't shove him. = 我没有推他。
She didn't shove the food down. = 她没有把食物咽下去。


PHRASE->
shove off:离开
shove it:别管它
shove around: 随意摆布
shove down:吞咽
shove in:插入
