bust-up: /ˈbʌst ʌp/ | n., v. | 争吵；冲突；破坏；垮台 |

英文解析:A bust-up is a noisy quarrel or fight.  It can also refer to the act of breaking something or the sudden failure of something.

例句:

n. 争吵：
There was a terrible bust-up between the two brothers. = 两个兄弟之间发生了一场可怕的争吵。

n. 破坏：
The party ended in a bust-up, with furniture overturned and windows broken. = 派对以一场破坏告终，家具被推倒，窗户被打破。


v.  发生争吵：
They bust up over a disagreement about money. = 他们因为金钱上的分歧而大吵了一架。

v. 破坏：
The vandals bust up the school windows. = 那些破坏分子砸碎了学校的窗户。


近义词:  quarrel, fight, argument, altercation, row, fracas, breakdown (for sudden failure), demolition (for destruction)

反义词: agreement, harmony, peace, reconciliation

词性:  noun, verb
