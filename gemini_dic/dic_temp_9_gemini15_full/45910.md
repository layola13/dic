cutling:/ˈkʌtlɪŋ/| n.|插条，扦插苗|A cutting is a piece of a plant stem or root that is cut off and planted to produce a new plant.  It is a method of vegetative propagation.|例句：I propagated the rose bush from a cutting. = 我用插条繁殖了这棵玫瑰灌木。


例句：The gardener took several cuttings from the hydrangea. = 园丁从绣球花上取了好几根插条。


近义词：slip, sprig


反义词：seed (as a method of propagation)


词性:名词
