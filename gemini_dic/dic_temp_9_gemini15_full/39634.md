consequentialities:/ˌkɒnsɪkwɛnʃiˈælɪtiz/ | n. | 结果；后果；影响 | The word "consequentialities" refers to the state or quality of having consequences; the importance or significance of consequences.  It emphasizes the multiple or complex nature of the outcomes.  It is less frequently used than "consequences" and often implies a more significant or far-reaching impact. | The consequentialities of his actions were far-reaching and unforeseen.  他的行为带来了深远且无法预料的后果。
