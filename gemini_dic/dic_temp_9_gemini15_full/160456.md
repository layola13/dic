population:/ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən/ /ˌpɑːpjʊˈleɪʃən/| n. |人口，居民；种群；(动植物的)群体|The total number of people living in a particular place or area.  It can also refer to a group of animals or plants of the same kind living in a particular place.|例句：The world's population is growing rapidly. 全球人口正在迅速增长。

例句：The deer population in the forest has decreased significantly. 森林里的鹿群数量显著减少了。

近义词：inhabitants, citizenry, community

反义词：depopulation (n.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The population of this city is about one million. = 这个城市的人口大约一百万。
The insect population fluctuates throughout the year. = 昆虫数量全年都在波动。

一般过去时:
The population of the town was much smaller fifty years ago. = 五十年前，这个镇的人口要少得多。
The bird population declined sharply after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，鸟类的数量急剧下降。

现在完成时:
The population has increased dramatically in recent years. = 近年来，人口急剧增加。
The whale population has recovered slightly since the ban on whaling. = 自从禁止捕鲸以来，鲸鱼的数量略有恢复。

过去完成时:
By the time the census was taken, the population had already grown significantly. = 到人口普查进行时，人口已经显著增长了。
The rabbit population had been decimated by the disease before the hunters arrived. = 在猎人到来之前，兔子的数量已经被疾病摧毁了。

将来时:
The population is expected to reach two billion by 2050. = 预计到2050年，人口将达到20亿。
The city's population will likely double in the next decade. = 这个城市的人口可能在未来十年内翻一番。

虚拟语气:
If the population were smaller, the environmental impact would be less severe. = 如果人口较少，对环境的影响就会减轻。
I wish the endangered species population were larger. = 我希望濒危物种的数量更多。

被动语态:
The population figures are collected every ten years. = 人口数据每十年收集一次。
The population growth is being closely monitored by the government. = 人口增长正受到政府的密切监控。

疑问句:
What is the population of China? = 中国的人口是多少？
How has the global population changed over the past century? = 在过去的一个世纪里，全球人口发生了怎样的变化？

否定句:
The population is not evenly distributed across the country. = 人口在全国分布并不均匀。
The island's population hasn't increased significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这个岛屿的人口并没有显著增长。
