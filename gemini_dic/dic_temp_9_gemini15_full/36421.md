coking:/ˈkoʊkɪŋ/ | n. v. |焦化；炼焦|The process of heating coal or petroleum to drive off volatile matter and produce coke.  It can also refer to the coke produced through this process. |例句：The coking process is crucial for steel production. 炼焦过程对钢铁生产至关重要。

The coking plant emitted a lot of pollution.  焦化厂排放了大量的污染物。|近义词：carbonization, charring |反义词：无
