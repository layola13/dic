insure:/ɪnˈʃʊr/ | vt. | 保险；确保 | To protect oneself against financial loss by paying a premium to an insurance company.  |  例句：We need to insure our house against fire and theft. 我们需要为我们的房子投保火灾和盗窃险。 |例句：He insured his car for a significant amount. 他为他的车投保了一大笔金额。| 近义词：assure, guarantee, protect | 反义词：risk, endanger | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I insure my car annually. = 我每年为我的车投保。
They insure their business against liability. = 他们为他们的业务投保责任险。

一般过去时:
She insured her new painting last week. = 她上周为她的新画作投保。
He insured his life when he was young. = 他年轻的时候就投保了人寿保险。

现在完成时:
I have insured my property for many years. = 我已经为我的房产投保多年了。
We have insured against all possible risks. = 我们已经为所有可能的风险投保。

过去完成时:
He had insured the cargo before it shipped. = 货物装运前，他已经为其投保。
They had insured their house months before the flood. = 洪水来临前几个月，他们就已经为房子投保了。

将来时:
I will insure my new motorcycle next week. = 我下周将为我的新摩托车投保。
They will insure the building before the grand opening. = 他们将在盛大开幕前为大楼投保。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would insure my boat. = 如果我有更多钱，我会为我的船投保。
I wish I had insured my business against bankruptcy. = 我真希望我当时为我的企业投保了破产险。

被动语态:
The house was insured against fire. = 这房子投保了火灾险。
The shipment will be insured by our company. = 这批货物将由我们公司投保。

疑问句:
Is your car insured? = 你的车投保了吗？
Are the goods insured against damage? = 货物投保了损坏险吗？

否定句:
He didn't insure his bicycle. = 他没有为他的自行车投保。
They didn't insure their business properly. = 他们没有为他们的企业妥善投保。



PHRASE->
insure against = 投保…险
life insurance = 人寿保险
health insurance = 健康保险
car insurance = 汽车保险
property insurance = 房产保险
