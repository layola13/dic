measuring:/ˈmɛʒərɪŋ/ | v. |测量；衡量；估量 |The act of determining the size, amount, or degree of something.  It involves using instruments or methods to obtain a quantitative assessment. |例句：She is measuring the length of the table. = 她正在测量桌子的长度。

例句：We need to measuring the impact of the new policy. = 我们需要衡量新政策的影响。


近义词：gauging, assessing, evaluating, calculating | 反义词：estimating (in the sense of roughly guessing)
