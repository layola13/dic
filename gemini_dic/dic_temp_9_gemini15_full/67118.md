exponent:/ˈɛkspəʊnənt/| n. |指数，幂；拥护者，倡导者|A number showing how many times a base number is multiplied by itself.  It is written as a small number to the upper right of the base number. For example, in 2³, 3 is the exponent.|例句：In the expression 10², 2 is the exponent, meaning 10 is multiplied by itself twice (10 x 10 = 100).  在表达式10²中，2是指数，这意味着10自乘两次（10 x 10 = 100）。|近义词：power, index|反义词：base (in mathematical context)
