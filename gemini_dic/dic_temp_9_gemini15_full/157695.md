pixellates:/ˈpɪksəleɪts/| vt.|使像素化，使成像素状|To break an image down into pixels, often resulting in a loss of detail and a blocky appearance.  The process can be intentional, for stylistic effect, or unintentional, due to low resolution or compression.|例句：The image pixellates when you zoom in too far.  当您过度放大时，图像会像素化。

The photograph pixellated after being heavily compressed.  照片在被大量压缩后变得像素化了。


近义词：rasterize, digitize (in a specific context)|反义词：sharpen, smooth|动词
