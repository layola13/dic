crossing:/ˈkrɒsɪŋ/ /ˈkrɔːsɪŋ/| n. |交叉点；横渡；穿越|A place where a road, railway, etc. goes across something else; the act of going across something.|例句：The pedestrian crossing is at the corner. 行人过街处在拐角处。

例句：We made a daring crossing of the river. 我们大胆地渡过了河。|近义词：intersection, passage, transit|反义词：meeting, convergence

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train is crossing the bridge. = 火车正在过桥。
He crosses the street every day. = 他每天都过马路。

一般过去时:
They crossed the finish line together. = 他们一起冲过终点线。
She crossed the ocean on a large ship. = 她乘大船横渡了大洋。

现在完成时:
I have crossed the Alps twice. = 我已经两次穿越阿尔卑斯山脉了。
We have crossed the river safely. = 我们已经安全渡过了河。

过去完成时:
They had crossed the desert before nightfall. = 他们在天黑前已经穿越了沙漠。
She had crossed the road before the car arrived. = 汽车到达之前，她已经过了马路。

将来时:
We will cross the bridge when we reach it. = 我们到达桥的时候就会过桥。
They will cross the Atlantic next month. = 他们下个月将横渡大西洋。

虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would cross the ocean in a day. = 如果我有翅膀，我就能一天内横渡大洋。
I wish I could cross that river without a boat. = 我希望我能不用船就能渡过那条河。

被动语态:
The street will be crossed by many people. = 这条街会被很多人穿过。
The bridge is being crossed by a train. = 一列火车正在过桥。

疑问句:
Where does the road crossing begin? = 道路交叉点从哪里开始？
Are you crossing the street now? = 你现在正在过马路吗？

否定句:
He didn't cross the street because of the traffic. = 他因为交通堵塞而没有过马路。
They did not cross the border illegally. = 他们没有非法越境。
