chandra:/ˈtʃʌndrə/ | n. |钱德拉；月神；光辉|  A Sanskrit word meaning "moon" or "shining one", often used as a name or part of a name.  It is associated with the Hindu moon god Chandra and also appears in astronomical contexts.  |Example:  The Chandra X-ray Observatory is a powerful space telescope. 钱德拉X射线天文台是一个强大的空间望远镜。|近义词:Luna, Moon, Luminary|反义词:Darkness, Shadow|名词
