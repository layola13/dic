hybridity:/ˌhaɪˈbrɪdəti/ | n. | 混合性；杂交；混合体 | The state or condition of being hybrid; the combination of different elements or characteristics.  It often refers to the mixing of different cultures, races, or genres. | 例句：The novel showcases a fascinating hybridity of genres, blending science fiction with historical fiction. 这部小说展现了引人入胜的多种类型混合，将科幻小说与历史小说融合在一起。

例句：The city's cultural hybridity is evident in its diverse cuisine and architecture.  这座城市的文化混合性在其多元化的烹饪和建筑中显而易见。


近义词：mixture, blend, amalgamation, fusion, heterogeneity | 反义词：purity, homogeneity, uniformity
