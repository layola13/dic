ford:/fɔːrd/| n. v.|福特（汽车公司）；浅滩；涉水；渡口；强行通过|The word "ford" has two main meanings.  As a noun, it refers to a shallow place in a river or stream that can be crossed on foot or by vehicle. It can also refer to the Ford Motor Company. As a verb, it means to wade across (a river or shallow body of water).|例句：We forded the river at the shallowest point. 我们在河中最浅的地方涉水过河。|例句：The Ford Mustang is a popular sports car. 福特野马是一款受欢迎的跑车。|近义词：(n.) shallows, crossing; (v.) wade, cross|反义词：(n.) deep water, bridge; (v.) none specifically
