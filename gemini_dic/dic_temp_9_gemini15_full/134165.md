nonimputatively:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtətɪvli/ | adv. |非归咎地，无过错地 |  Not implying blame or fault.  This adverb indicates that something is done or said without assigning blame or responsibility to anyone.  | 例句：The report was written nonimputatively, focusing on the facts without assigning blame. 报告是客观撰写的，专注于事实而不指责任何人。


The findings were presented nonimputatively; no individual was singled out for criticism.  调查结果被客观地呈现；没有针对任何个人进行批评。 | 近义词：blamelessly, impartially, objectively | 反义词：culpably, blamefully
