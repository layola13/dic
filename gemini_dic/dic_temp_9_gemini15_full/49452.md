deltas:/ˈdɛltəz/| n.|三角洲；增量；变化量|A delta is a landform created by the deposition of sediment that is carried by a river as the flow leaves its mouth and enters slower-moving or stagnant water.  This occurs where a river enters an ocean, sea, estuary, lake, or reservoir.  The term is also used in mathematics to refer to a change or increment in a variable.|例句：The Nile Delta is one of the most fertile regions in the world. 尼罗河三角洲是世界上最肥沃的地区之一。

例句：The delta of the Mississippi River is a vast wetland. 密西西比河的三角洲是一个巨大的湿地。


近义词：alluvial fan (in some contexts), estuary (in some contexts)
反义词：无
词性:名词
