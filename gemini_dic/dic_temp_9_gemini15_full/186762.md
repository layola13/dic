riled:/raɪld/| adj.|激怒的，恼火的|Irritated; angry.  The word often implies a feeling of being mildly annoyed rather than intensely furious.|例句：He was riled by her constant criticisms. 他被她不断的批评激怒了。|例句：The noisy neighbours really riled me. 吵闹的邻居真让我生气。|近义词：annoyed, irritated, angered, vexed|反义词：calm, peaceful, serene, composed|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is riled by the traffic. = 他被交通堵塞激怒了。
The situation riles her. = 这种情况让她生气。


一般过去时:
I was riled by his rudeness. = 我被他粗鲁的态度激怒了。
The news riled him considerably. = 这个消息让他相当生气。


现在完成时:
I have been riled by his behavior all day. = 我一整天都被他的行为激怒了。
She has riled easily lately. = 她最近很容易生气。


过去完成时:
He had been riled for hours before he finally exploded. = 他生气了好几个小时，最后终于爆发了。
I had been riled by her comments before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始我就已经被她的评论激怒了。


将来时:

疑问句:
