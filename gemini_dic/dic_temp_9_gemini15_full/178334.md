reconditions:/ˌriːˈkənˈdɪʃənz/| vt.| 再修复；翻新；使恢复原状 | To restore something to its original condition or to a good working order, often after damage or wear.  | 例句：The mechanic reconditioned the engine. = 机械师修复了发动机。

The company reconditions used cars before selling them. = 公司在出售二手车之前会对其进行翻新。


近义词：renovate, refurbish, overhaul, rehabilitate | 反义词：damage, destroy, ruin | 动词
