shopwalker:/ˈʃɒpˌwɔːkə(r)/| n.|商店店员，（尤指大型百货商店的）男店员|A shopwalker is a male shop assistant, especially in a large department store.  The term is largely outdated. |Example: In the old days, a shopwalker would greet customers at the door and guide them to the appropriate department.  在过去，商店店员会在门口迎接顾客，并引导他们到相应的部门。|近义词：shop assistant, salesman, sales associate|反义词：customer, shopper
