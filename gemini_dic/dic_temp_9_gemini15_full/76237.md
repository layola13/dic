futurisms:/ˈfjuːtʃərɪzəm/| n.|未来主义；未来主义艺术|Futurism is an artistic and literary movement that originated in Italy in the early 20th century.  It emphasized speed, technology, youth, and violence, and rejected tradition.|The Futurists celebrated the dynamism of modern life and the beauty of machines.  Their art often depicted motion and speed.|近义词：avant-gardism, modernism|反义词：traditionalism, classicism
