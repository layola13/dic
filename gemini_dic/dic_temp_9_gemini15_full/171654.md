pulse:/pʌls/|n. v.|脉搏；脉冲；强烈的感情；推动；|The rhythmic expansion and contraction of the arteries, especially as felt at the wrist or neck.  A short burst of energy or activity.|例句：I could feel my pulse racing. 我能感觉到我的脉搏在加速。
The doctor checked her pulse. 医生检查了她的脉搏。|近义词：heartbeat, throb, beat|反义词：stillness, calm
