misappointed:/ˌmɪs.əˈpɔɪn.tɪd/ | adj. | 失望的，未被任命的 | Feeling disappointed because of something not meeting expectations or because of a failure to get a job or position.  | The misappointed candidate felt a deep sense of disappointment after losing the election.  失望的候选人在落选后感到深深的失望。| disappointed, frustrated, let down | appointed, satisfied, fulfilled | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is misappointed about the outcome of the project. = 他对项目的成果感到失望。
She is misappointed with her current job. = 她对目前的工作感到失望。


一般过去时:
The team was misappointed after their loss in the final game. = 他们在决赛中失利后感到失望。
He was misappointed when he didn't get the promotion. = 他没有得到晋升时感到失望。


现在完成时:
I have been misappointed by his behaviour. = 我一直对他这种行为感到失望。
They have been misappointed with the service they received. = 他们对收到的服务感到失望。


过去完成时:
We had been misappointed by the delay in the project before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，项目的延误让我们感到失望。
She had been misappointed many times before she finally succeeded. = 在她最终成功之前，她已经多次感到失望。


将来时:
I will be misappointed if the team doesn't win the next match. = 如果球队下一场比赛输了，我会很失望。
They will be misappointed if their plans don't work out. = 如果他们的计划失败了，他们将会很失望。


虚拟语气:
I would be misappointed if he didn't call me back. = 如果他不给我回电话，我会很失望。
She would have been misappointed if she hadn't passed the exam. = 如果她没有通过考试，她会很失望。


被动语态:
The team felt misappointed by the unfair judgment. = 球队对不公平的判决感到失望。
The students were misappointed by the cancellation of the trip. = 学生们对旅行取消感到失望。


疑问句:
Are you misappointed with the results? = 你对结果感到失望吗？
Is she misappointed about her performance? = 她对自己的表现感到失望吗？


否定句:
He wasn't misappointed by the criticism; he took it constructively. = 他并没有因为批评而感到失望；他以建设性的态度接受了它。
They weren't misappointed with the decision; they understood the reasons. = 他们对这个决定并不失望；他们理解其中的原因。
