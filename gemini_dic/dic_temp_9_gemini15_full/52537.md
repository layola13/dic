It often involves a feeling of insecurity and self-doubt, leading to hesitation in social situations or when expressing opinions. |Example: Her diffidence prevented her from speaking up in the meeting.  她的羞怯使她无法在会议上发言。|近义词：shyness, timidity, bashfulness, modesty|反义词：confidence, assurance, boldness
