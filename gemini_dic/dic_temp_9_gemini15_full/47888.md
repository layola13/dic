decemvirate:/ˌdesɪmˈvɛreɪt/| n. |十人执政团；十人委员会|A decemvirate is a governing body composed of ten people.  The term often refers to the board of ten magistrates who ruled ancient Rome for a period. |Example: The Roman decemvirate was established to codify Roman law.  罗马十人执政团是为了编纂罗马法而建立的。 The city council formed a temporary decemvirate to address the budget crisis.  市议会组建了一个临时的十人委员会来解决预算危机。|近义词：council, committee, board |反义词：monarchy, dictatorship
