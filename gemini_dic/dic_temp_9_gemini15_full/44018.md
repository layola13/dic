criminology:/ˌkrɪmɪˈnɒlədʒi/| n. |犯罪学|The scientific study of crime and criminals, including its causes, consequences, and control.  It examines the nature of crime, the social context in which crime occurs, and the responses of society to crime.|例句：He is studying criminology at university. 他在大学学习犯罪学。|例句：The professor's lecture focused on the sociological aspects of criminology. 教授的讲座重点关注犯罪学的社会学方面。|近义词：penology, criminal justice|反义词：N/A
