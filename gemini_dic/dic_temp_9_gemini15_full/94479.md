imporous: /ɪmˈpɔːrəs/ | adj. | 不透水的；不渗透的 | Impervious means not allowing something to pass through.  It often refers to liquids or gases, but can also be used figuratively. |  The raincoat is impervious to rain.  这件雨衣不透雨。 |  The politician remained impervious to criticism.  这位政治家对批评无动于衷。|  permeable, waterproof | porous, permeable  | 形容词
