photometrician:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈmɛtrɪʃən/| n. |测光员，光度测量专家| A photometrician is a specialist who measures and analyzes light.  They work with instruments to determine the intensity, distribution, and other properties of light sources. Their expertise is crucial in various fields like astronomy, lighting design, and photography.|The photometrician carefully calibrated the equipment before measuring the star's brightness.  测光员在测量恒星亮度之前仔细校准了设备。|luminary, photometrist|  (无明确反义词)|名词
