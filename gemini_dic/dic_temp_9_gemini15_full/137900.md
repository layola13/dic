/ˌnɒnˌvɪvɪˈpærətiːz/ | n. | 非胎生性；卵生 | The state of being oviparous, not viviparous;  the characteristic of producing eggs that hatch outside the body.  This term is primarily used in biological contexts to describe reproductive strategies. | Example:  The nonviviparity of most insect species is a well-established fact.  大多数昆虫物种的卵生是已被充分证实的。| 近义词: oviparity, egg-laying | 反义词: viviparity, live birth |  名词
