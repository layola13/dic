oxytocic:/ɒkˈsɪtɒsɪk/| adj. n.|催产的；催产素；催产剂|  An oxytocic is a substance that stimulates the contraction of the uterus, often used to induce or accelerate labor.  It can refer to a drug or a naturally occurring hormone. |例句：The doctor administered an oxytocic to induce labor. 医生施用了催产剂来催产。|例句：Oxytocin, a natural oxytocic, plays a crucial role in childbirth.催产素，一种天然催产剂，在分娩中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：ecbolic, uterotonic|反义词：None specifically, but substances that relax uterine muscles could be considered antonyms. |形容词，名词
