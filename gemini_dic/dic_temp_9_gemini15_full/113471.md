lyre:/ˈlaɪər/| n.|里拉琴，七弦琴|A lyre is a stringed musical instrument, typically having a body shaped like a shallow U and strings stretched between a crossbar and the body.  It is an ancient instrument and is associated with Greek mythology, notably Apollo.|Example: The ancient Greek poet played his lyre beautifully.  古代希腊诗人优美地演奏他的里拉琴。|近义词：harp, zither|反义词：None
