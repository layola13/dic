mewled:/mjuːld/| v.| 婴儿般地哭泣，低声哭泣 | to make a soft, crying sound, typically made by a baby  |例句：The baby mewled softly in its crib.  婴儿在婴儿床里轻声哭泣。

The newborn mewled weakly for its mother. 新生儿虚弱地哼哼唧唧地找妈妈。


近义词：whimpered, cried, wailed| 反义词：roared, shouted
