nonresuscitation: /ˌnɒnˌrɛsʌsɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不施行心肺复苏术 | The act of not performing cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) on a patient; a decision to withhold or withdraw life-sustaining treatment, particularly CPR.  This often involves a formal "Do Not Resuscitate" (DNR) order from a physician. | Example: The patient's advanced directive specified nonresuscitation in the event of cardiac arrest.  患者的预先指令规定，如果发生心脏骤停，则不进行心肺复苏。
