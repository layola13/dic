hamstrung:/ˈhæmstrʌŋ/| adj. v. |使…难以行动；使…受阻；削弱|Severely restricted or hampered.  To hamstring something is to make it ineffective or unable to function properly. This word originates from the act of cutting the hamstring tendons in animals, thus crippling them. |例句：The company's expansion plans were hamstrung by lack of funding. 公司的扩张计划由于缺乏资金而受阻。 The new regulations hamstrung the small businesses. 新的法规削弱了小企业。|近义词：crippled, impeded, hindered, hampered, disabled|反义词：empowered, enabled, facilitated


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bureaucracy hamstrings our efforts. = 官僚主义阻碍了我们的努力。
Lack of resources hamstrings the project. = 资源匮乏阻碍了这个项目。


一般过去时:
The storm hamstrung transportation. = 暴风雨阻碍了交通。
His injury hamstrung his athletic career. = 他的伤势毁了他的运动生涯。


现在完成时:
Years of neglect have hamstrung the school system. = 多年的忽视已经削弱了学校系统。
Budget cuts have hamstrung the hospital. = 预算削减已经削弱了医院。


过去完成时:
The previous administration's policies had hamstrung the economy. = 上届政府的政策已经削弱了经济。
Lack of planning had hamstrung the rescue effort. = 缺乏计划已经阻碍了救援工作。


将来时:
Further regulations will hamstring small businesses. = 进一步的法规将削弱小型企业。
The delay will hamstring the project's timeline. = 延迟将影响项目的进度。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't hamstrung the project, it would have been successful. = 如果他们没有阻碍这个项目，它本来会成功的。
If the government hadn't hamstrung the economy, we would be in better shape. = 如果政府没有削弱经济，我们的状况会更好。


被动语态:
The team was hamstrung by a lack of communication. = 该团队因缺乏沟通而受阻。
The negotiations were hamstrung by disagreements. = 谈判因分歧而受阻。


疑问句:

Is the company hamstrung by debt? = 公司是被债务所拖累的吗？


否定句:
The reforms did not hamstring the industry. = 改革并没有削弱这个行业。
The new technology did not hamstring the old system. = 新技术并没有削弱旧系统。
