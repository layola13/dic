precolluding: /ˌpriːkəˈluːdɪŋ/ | v. |预谋串通；暗中勾结 |  The act of secretly planning or conspiring with others, often for an illegal or unethical purpose.  It implies a degree of secrecy and advance planning before any overt action is taken. | The evidence suggests the two companies were precolluding to fix prices.  证据表明两家公司正在串通涨价。 | conspiring, colluding, conniving | opposing, acting independently, cooperating openly | 动词
