ferrites:/ˈfɛraɪts/| n. |铁氧体|Ferrite is a ceramic material composed of iron oxide and one or more other metallic elements.  They are commonly used in electronic devices due to their magnetic properties.  |例句：Many electronic devices utilize ferrites in their inductors and transformers.|近义词：magnetic ceramic, ferromagnetic material|反义词:无
