professionalising: /ˌprəˈfɛʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 专业化；使专业化 | The act of making something more professional or conforming to professional standards.  This often involves improving skills, adopting professional practices, and raising standards. |


例句：

The company is professionalising its image through a new marketing campaign. = 公司正在通过新的营销活动来提升其专业形象。

The government is professionalising the police force by improving training and equipment. = 政府正在通过改进培训和设备来使警务部门更加专业化。


近义词：professionalization,  formalizing, standardizing


反义词：amateurising, casualizing, informalizing


词性：动词
