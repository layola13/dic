dataria:/dəˈteəriə/| n.|档案室；档案馆；资料室|A dataria is a place where data, records, or documents are stored and managed, often in an organized and systematic way.  It may refer to a specific office or department within an organization or a more general storage facility.|Example: The Vatican dataria is responsible for managing papal documents.  梵蒂冈档案室负责管理教皇的文件。|近义词：archive, record office, repository|反义词：N/A
