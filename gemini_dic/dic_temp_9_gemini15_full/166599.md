presedentary: /ˌpriːˈsednəri/ | adj. |  久坐的，习惯久坐的 |  Relating to or characterized by prolonged sitting; sedentary. This term often implies a negative connotation, suggesting a lifestyle with insufficient physical activity.  | Example:  The presedentary lifestyle contributed to his health problems.  久坐的生活方式导致了他的健康问题。 | 近义词: sedentary, inactive,  | 反义词: active, mobile |  词性: 形容词
