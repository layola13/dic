pruderies:/ˈpruːdəriːz/| n.| 拘谨；假正经；过分端庄 |The excessive display of modesty or propriety; a tendency to be excessively modest or proper.  It often implies a hypocritical or affected display of virtue. |例句：Her pruderies prevented her from enjoying the lively party.  她的拘谨让她无法享受热闹的派对。

例句：The pruderies of Victorian society are now seen as rather quaint. 维多利亚时代的拘谨现在看来相当古怪。


近义词：modesty, primness, prissiness, puritanism, squeamishness

反义词：licentiousness, indecency, vulgarity, immodesty


词性:名词
