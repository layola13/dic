micropublish:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈpʌblɪʃ/| vt.|微出版，小规模出版|To publish something on a small scale, often online or through a limited distribution.|例句：She micropublished her poetry collection on a personal blog. 她在一个个人博客上微出版了她的诗集。|近义词：self-publish, independently publish|反义词：mass-publish, widely publish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He micropublishes his short stories online. 他在线微出版他的短篇小说。
They micropublish their research findings on their own website.  他们将研究成果在其自己的网站上微出版。


一般过去时:
She micropublished her novel through a small press last year. 去年她通过一家小型出版社微出版了她的长篇小说。
We micropublished the article on a dedicated platform. 我们在一个专门的平台上微出版了这篇文章。


现在完成时:
He has micropublished several essays on various topics. 他已经微出版了几篇关于不同主题的论文。
They have micropublished their work successfully and reached a niche audience. 他们成功地微出版了他们的作品，并接触到一个利基市场。


过去完成时:
Before launching the book widely, she had micropublished excerpts online to gauge reader interest. 在广泛发行这本书之前，她已经在网上微出版了节选内容来衡量读者的兴趣。
They had micropublished a beta version of the ebook before the official release. 在正式发布之前，他们已经微出版了电子书的测试版。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would micropublish my travelogue. 如果我有时间，我会微出版我的游记。
I wish I could micropublish my work more easily. 我希望我能更容易地微出版我的作品。

疑问句:
