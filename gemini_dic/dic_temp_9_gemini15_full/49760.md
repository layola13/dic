demolishing: /dɪˈmɒlɪʃɪŋ/ | /dɪˈmɑːlɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 拆毁；破坏；彻底击败 |  To destroy something completely, especially a building.  |

例句：
The old factory is being demolished to make way for a new shopping mall. =  旧工厂正在被拆除，以便为新的购物中心让路。

The hurricane demolished several houses in the coastal area. = 飓风摧毁了沿海地区的多栋房屋。


近义词：destroy, wreck, raze, dismantle, pulverize

反义词：construct, build, erect, create, establish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are demolishing the old building. = 他们正在拆除旧建筑。
The company demolishes old structures for redevelopment. = 这家公司拆除旧建筑物以进行重建。


一般过去时:
They demolished the old house last week. = 他们上周拆除了旧房子。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The construction team had demolished the old structure before starting the new one. = 建设团队在开始新的建筑之前已经拆除了旧建筑。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
