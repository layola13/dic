词源:
The word "simplistically" is derived from the adjective "simplistic," which itself comes from the noun "simplicity" and the suffix "-istic," indicating a characteristic or style.  "Simplicity" originates from the Old French "simplicite," ultimately tracing back to the Latin "simplicitas," meaning "plainness, straightforwardness."  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective into an adverb.
