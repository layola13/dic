lactoprotein:/ˌlæktoˈproʊtiːn/| n. |乳蛋白|A protein found in milk.  Lactoproteins are a diverse group of proteins, including casein and whey proteins, which are important components of milk and dairy products. They provide essential amino acids and contribute to the nutritional value of milk. | Example:  Whey protein is a type of lactoprotein that is popular among athletes.  乳清蛋白是一种深受运动员喜爱的乳蛋白。|近义词：milk protein, casein, whey protein|反义词：None
