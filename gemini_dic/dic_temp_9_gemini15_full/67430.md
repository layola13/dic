extincts:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/| adj.|灭绝的；绝种的|adj.  No longer existing; having died out.  Extinct species are those that have completely disappeared from the Earth.|例句：The dodo bird is extinct. = 渡渡鸟已经灭绝了。

The dinosaurs became extinct millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前灭绝了。

近义词：extinguished, defunct, vanished, gone, lost|反义词： extant, existing, surviving

词源:  From Latin *extinctus, past participle of extinguere "to extinguish, put out," from ex- "out" + stinguere "to extinguish," of unknown origin.  The sense development is from "put out" (a fire, for example) to "put an end to," ultimately to "cause to cease to exist."

现在完成时:

Many plant species have become extinct due to habitat loss. = 许多植物物种由于栖息地丧失而灭绝。


过去完成时:
The passenger pigeon had already become extinct by the time I was born. = 我出生的时候，旅鸽已经灭绝了。
Scientists believed the species had gone extinct long before their discovery. = 科学家认为该物种在他们发现之前很久就已经灭绝了。
