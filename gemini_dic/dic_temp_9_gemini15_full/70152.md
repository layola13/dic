fiascoes:/fiˈæskoʊz/| n.|惨败；彻底的失败；滑稽的失败|A fiasco is a complete and embarrassing failure.  It often involves a public event or undertaking that goes wrong in a spectacular and humorous way. The plural form, "fiascoes," simply refers to multiple instances of such failures.|The party was a complete fiasco; the cake fell on the floor, the music stopped working, and nobody showed up.|近义词：failure, disaster, debacle, catastrophe|反义词：success, triumph, victory
