inboard:/ˈɪnbɔːrd/| adj.|船内安装的；内部的|Situated or operating within the hull of a vessel; located inside.|The inboard engine is more protected from the elements.|近义词：internal, interior|反义词：outboard


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The engine is inboard. = 发动机在船内。
The system is inboard. = 系统在船内。


一般过去时:
The engine was inboard. = 发动机在船内。
The equipment was inboard. = 设备在船内。


现在完成时:
The inboard engine has been running smoothly. = 船内发动机运行平稳。
The cargo has been stowed inboard. = 货物已装入船内。


过去完成时:
The crew had already secured all inboard equipment before the storm. = 船员在暴风雨来临前已固定好所有船内设备。
The captain had ensured all inboard systems were functional. = 船长已确保所有船内系统都正常运行。


将来时:
The new inboard motor will be installed next week. = 新的船内发动机将于下周安装。
The goods will be transported inboard. = 货物将被运送到船内。


虚拟语气:
If the engine were inboard, it would be better protected. = 如果发动机在船内，它将得到更好的保护。
I wish the controls were inboard for easier access. = 我希望控制装置在船内，以便更容易操作。


被动语态:
The inboard system is being inspected. = 船内系统正在接受检查。
The cargo was efficiently loaded inboard. = 货物被有效地装入船内。


疑问句:
Is the engine inboard or outboard? = 发动机是船内安装的还是船外安装的？
Are the lifeboats stored inboard? = 救生艇是存放在船内吗？


否定句:
The auxiliary generator is not inboard; it's on the deck. = 辅助发电机不在船内；它在甲板上。
The spare parts are not inboard. = 备件不在船内。

词源:
The word "inboard" is a compound word formed from "in" (meaning inside or within) and "board" (referring to the side of a ship or the structure of a vessel).  Its meaning thus literally refers to something located within the structure of a ship.
