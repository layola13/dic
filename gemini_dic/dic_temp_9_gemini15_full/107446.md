lampoon:/læmˈpuːn/| n. vt.|讽刺文章；讽刺；挖苦|A lampoon is a written satire usually attacking a particular person or institution.  It is characterized by wit, humor, and often harsh criticism.|例句：The magazine published a lampoon of the president's foreign policy. 这本杂志发表了一篇讽刺总统外交政策的文章。|例句：He lampooned the mayor in his latest column. 他在他最新的专栏文章中讽刺了市长。|近义词：satire, parody, caricature, burlesque|反义词：praise, eulogy, commendation|名词，动词
