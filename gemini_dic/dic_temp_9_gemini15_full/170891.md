pseudoliberal:/ˈsjuːdoʊlɪbərəl/| adj.|伪自由主义的，假冒自由主义的|Appearing liberal but actually supporting conservative or authoritarian policies.  Someone described as a pseudoliberal might advocate for policies that seem progressive on the surface but ultimately benefit those in power or maintain existing inequalities.  This term is often used pejoratively.|例句：He's a pseudoliberal; he talks a good game about social justice, but his policies always favor the wealthy.|近义词：faux liberal, sham liberal|反义词：true liberal, genuine liberal
