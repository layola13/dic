hommocky:/ˈhɒməki/| adj.|起伏不平的，丘陵状的|Having a surface with many small mounds or hummocks.  The terrain was hommocky, making travel difficult.|近义词：undulating, bumpy, hilly|反义词：flat, level, smooth


例句：

The landscape was hommocky, a patchwork of low hills and hollows.

这片景观起伏不平，是由低矮的山丘和洼地组成的拼凑物。


The hommocky field made plowing difficult for the farmers.

起伏不平的田地使农民耕作困难。
