scunge:/skʌndʒ/| v.|清除污垢，擦洗；搜寻（尤指不干净的东西）|to clean something dirty, especially by rubbing it; to search for something, especially something dirty or unpleasant |例句：He spent the afternoon scunging the gutters. 他一下午都在清理排水沟。


例句：She scunged around in the attic for old toys.  她在阁楼里翻找旧玩具。


近义词：scrub, scour, rummage, ferret


反义词：clean, tidy


词性：动词
