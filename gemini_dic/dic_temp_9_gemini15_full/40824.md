convoys:/ˈkɒnˌvɔɪz/ | n. |护航队；车队；船队 |A convoy is a group of vehicles, ships, or aircraft travelling together, especially for protection.  |例句：A convoy of trucks transported the supplies across the desert.  一队卡车把物资运过沙漠。  例句：The naval convoy was escorted by destroyers.  海军护航队由驱逐舰护送。|近义词：fleet, escort, procession |反义词：None specifically.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The navy convoys ships across the ocean. = 海军护送船只横渡海洋。
He always convoys his children to school. = 他总是护送他的孩子们去上学。

一般过去时:
The merchant ships sailed in convoy during the war. = 商船在战争期间以护航队形式航行。
She convoyed the tourists to the museum. = 她护送游客到博物馆。

现在完成时:
The government has convoyed countless supplies to the disaster area. = 政府已将无数物资护送到灾区。
They have convoyed their goods safely to market. = 他们已安全地将货物护送到市场。


过去完成时:
