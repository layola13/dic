The term is also used to refer to a large amount of wealth or a rich source of something. |例句：The Klondike Gold Rush attracted thousands of prospectors.  克朗代克淘金热吸引了成千上万的淘金者。


The Klondike region is known for its stunning natural beauty. 克朗代克地区以其令人惊叹的自然美景而闻名。|近义词：gold rush, bonanza, jackpot |反义词：poverty, destitution
