hogshead:/ˈhɒɡzˌhed/ | n. |大酒桶；（旧时）63加仑的酒桶 |A hogshead is a large cask, traditionally used for storing liquids such as wine or beer.  The exact capacity varied historically but is generally considered to be around 63 gallons (227 liters).|The winemaker stored his finest vintage in a large hogshead.  酿酒师把最好的葡萄酒储存在一个大酒桶里。 |cask, barrel |small barrel |名词
