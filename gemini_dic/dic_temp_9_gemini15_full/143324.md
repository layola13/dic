/ˌaʊtˈiːtɪŋ/ | v. | 吃得比…多；胜过（尤指在吃东西方面） |  The act of eating more than someone else, often in a competitive context. It implies a contest of eating quantities of food.  |  例句：He outeated everyone at the buffet. 他在自助餐上吃得比任何人都多。  |近义词：outdo, surpass (in eating)| 反义词：undereating | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outeat me every time we go to a pizza place. = 每次我们去比萨店，他们都比我吃得多。
He outeats his brother at every meal. = 他每顿饭都比他弟弟吃得多。


一般过去时:
Last night, I outeated my friends at the all-you-can-eat restaurant. = 昨晚，我在吃到饱的餐厅里吃得比我的朋友们都多。
She outeated him in the hot dog eating contest. = 在热狗比赛中，她吃得比他多。


现在完成时:
We have outeaten the entire buffet. = 我们已经把整个自助餐都吃完了。
She has outeaten everyone in the family this week. = 这周她吃得比家里的每个人都多。


过去完成时:
By the time the dessert arrived, he had already outeaten everyone else. = 甜点送来的时候，他已经比其他人吃得多了。
They had outeaten their quota by midday. = 中午之前，他们就已经超过了他们的配额。


将来时:
I think I will outeat him in the pie-eating contest. = 我想我会在吃馅饼比赛中赢过他。
They will likely outeat us if we don't start eating faster. = 如果我们不吃快一点，他们很可能会吃得比我们多。


虚拟语气:
If he ate less, he wouldn't outeat everyone. = 如果他少吃点，他就不会比所有人吃得都多。
I wish I could outeat my hunger. = 我希望我能吃饱。


被动语态:
The champion was outeaten by a newcomer. = 冠军被一个新人超过了。
The buffet was completely outeaten by the guests. = 自助餐被客人们吃光了。


疑问句:
Did he outeat the competition? = 他在比赛中吃得比对手多吗？
Can he outeat his opponent? = 他能吃得过他的对手吗？


否定句:
I didn't outeat him; he ate more than me. = 我没有吃得比他多；他比我吃得多。
She didn't outeat her siblings at Thanksgiving. = 感恩节她没有吃得比她的兄弟姐妹们多。
