egotistic: /ˌiːɡoʊˈtɪstɪk/ | adj. | 自我中心的，自负的 | Excessively conceited or absorbed in oneself; self-centered.  Characterized by or expressing excessive self-importance. |  例句：His egotistic behavior alienated many of his colleagues.  他的自负行为疏远了他的许多同事。

例句：She delivered an egotistic speech, boasting endlessly about her achievements. 她发表了一篇自负的演讲，没完没了地吹嘘自己的成就。


近义词：narcissistic, self-absorbed, vain, conceited, self-important

反义词：humble, modest, unselfish, selfless


词性: adjective
