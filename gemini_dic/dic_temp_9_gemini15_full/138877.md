nurture:/ˈnɜːrtʃər/| vt. n.|培育；培养；滋养|To care for and encourage the growth or development of someone or something.|例句：She nurtured her children with love and care. 她用爱和关怀抚养她的孩子。  例句：The company nurtured a culture of innovation. 公司培养了一种创新文化。|近义词：cultivate, foster, nourish|反义词：neglect, ignore|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They nurture their plants carefully. = 他们细心地培育他们的植物。
She nurtures her talent through practice. = 她通过练习来培养她的才能。


一般过去时:
He nurtured his dream for many years. = 他多年来一直培养着自己的梦想。
The teacher nurtured the students' creativity. = 老师培养了学生们的创造力。


现在完成时:
We have nurtured a strong team. = 我们已经培养了一支强大的团队。
She has nurtured her skills over the years. = 多年来她一直在培养自己的技能。


过去完成时:
They had nurtured the seedlings before transplanting them. = 他们在移植幼苗之前已经培育了它们。
He had nurtured his ambition since childhood. = 他从小就培养了自己的雄心壮志。


将来时:
We will nurture the next generation of leaders. = 我们将培养下一代领导者。
The company will nurture its employees' potential. = 公司将培养员工的潜力。


虚拟语气:
If they nurtured their talent, they would achieve more. = 如果他们培养自己的才能，他们会取得更大的成就。
I wish I had nurtured my interests more when I was young. = 我真希望我年轻时能更多地培养自己的兴趣。


被动语态:

The children were nurtured in a loving home. = 孩子们在一个充满爱的家庭中长大。


疑问句:

否定句:
