in-the-works: /ɪn ðə wɜːrks/ | adj. |正在进行中，筹备中 |  This phrase describes something that is currently being developed, planned, or created but is not yet complete or ready for release. It implies progress is being made.  | 例句：The new software is in the works; we expect to release it next quarter.  新的软件正在开发中；我们预计下个季度发布。

The sequel to the popular movie is in the works.  这部热门电影的续集正在筹备中。


近义词：under development, underway, in progress, being developed,  in the pipeline


反义词：completed, finished, done, concluded


词性:形容词
