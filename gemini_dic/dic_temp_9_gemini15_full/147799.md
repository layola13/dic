overproud: /ˌoʊvərˈpraʊd/ | adj. | 过于骄傲的，自负的 | Excessively proud; arrogant.  It implies an excessive or unwarranted degree of pride, often to the point of being objectionable to others. |例句：He was overproud of his achievements and looked down on others. 他过于骄傲自己的成就，看不起其他人。

例句：Her overproud demeanor alienated many of her colleagues. 她过于骄傲的态度疏远了许多同事。


近义词：arrogant, haughty, conceited, vain, supercilious | 反义词：humble, modest, unassuming


词性: 形容词


USAGE->

He is overproud of his intelligence. = 他过于骄傲自己的智力。
She is overproud of her beauty. = 她过于骄傲自己的美貌。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

词源:
The word "overproud" is a simple combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the adjective "proud."  The root of "proud" can be traced back to Old English *prūde, related to Old High German *pruoti "proud, haughty," and ultimately to Proto-Germanic *prūþiz.
