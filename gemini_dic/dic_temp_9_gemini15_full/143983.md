outrank: /ˌaʊtˈræŋk/ | vt. | 超过…的军衔；职位高于 | To exceed in rank or position.  |例句：The general outranked all the other officers.  将军的军衔高于所有其他军官。

例句：Her experience outranks his in this field. 在这个领域，她的经验比他丰富。


近义词：surpass, exceed, supersede, outrates

反义词：subordinate to, be ranked below


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outranks me in the company. = 他在公司职位比我高。
The captain outranks the lieutenant. = 上尉的军衔高于中尉。


一般过去时:
She outranked him in the previous project. = 在之前的项目中，她的职位比他高。
The general outranked all other officers in the battle. = 在战斗中，将军的军衔高于所有其他军官。


现在完成时:
He has outranked her since the promotion. = 自从晋升以来，他的职位就比她高了。
This strategy has outranked all others in effectiveness. = 这种策略在有效性方面超过了所有其他策略。


过去完成时:
She had outranked him before the restructuring. = 在重组之前，她的职位就比他高了。
The older general had outranked his younger counterpart for many years. = 年长的将军多年来一直比他的年轻同僚军衔高。


将来时:
He will outrank everyone else in the department next year. = 明年他将在部门里职位高于所有人。
This technology will outrank all competitors in the market. = 这项技术将在市场上超越所有竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he were promoted, he would outrank his current boss. = 如果他晋升了，他的职位就会高于他现在的老板。
I wish I outranked him so I could make this decision. = 我希望我的职位比他高，这样我就能做出这个决定了。


被动语态:
He was outranked by the new CEO. = 他被新任首席执行官的职位超越了。
This product is outranked by its competitors in terms of sales. = 此产品在销售方面被竞争对手超越了。


疑问句:
Does the colonel outrank the major? = 上校的军衔高于少校吗？
Does this design outrank the previous one in terms of efficiency? = 这个设计在效率方面是否优于之前的那个？


否定句:
He doesn't outrank her; they are of equal rank. = 他的职位不高于她；他们的职位相同。
This method doesn't outrank the traditional one in practicality. = 这种方法在实用性方面并不优于传统方法。


PHRASE->
outrank in experience = 经验比…丰富
outrank in seniority =资历比…老
outrank in ability =能力比…强
outrank in performance =表现比…好
outrank in status =地位比…高


词源:
The word "outrank" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "to exceed" or "surpass") and the noun "rank" (meaning "a position in a hierarchy").  Therefore, it literally means "to exceed in rank."
