commercialization:/ˌkəməːʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 商业化；商品化 | The process of introducing a new product or service into the market.  It involves adapting a product or service for sale and distributing it to consumers. This often includes marketing and advertising to increase consumer awareness and demand. | Example: The commercialization of the internet has revolutionized communication.  互联网的商业化彻底改变了沟通方式。 |近义词：commodification, marketization |反义词：preservation, conservation
