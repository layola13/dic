culturing:/ˈkʌltʃərɪŋ/| v.|培养；种植；(细胞)培养|The process of growing or developing something, especially microorganisms or cells in a controlled environment.  It can also refer to the fostering of a particular attitude, behavior, or skill.|例句：They are culturing bacteria in the lab.他们在实验室里培养细菌。|例句：The teacher is culturing a love of reading in her students.老师正在培养学生对阅读的热爱。|近义词：cultivating, growing, developing|反义词：destroying, neglecting
