reinfiltrating:/ˌriːɪnˈfɪltreɪtɪŋ/| v. |再次渗透；重新渗入|The process of entering or penetrating something again, often secretly or illicitly, after having been removed or expelled.|The enemy is reinfiltrating the border region. 敌人正在重新渗透边境地区。|The escaped prisoners were reinfiltrating the city. 越狱的囚犯正在重新渗入城市。|近义词：infiltrate again, penetrate anew, re-enter secretly|反义词：expel, remove, eject
