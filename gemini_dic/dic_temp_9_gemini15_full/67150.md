exposer:/ɪkˈspoʊzər/ | n. |揭露者；曝光者；陈列品 | A person or thing that exposes something.  Someone who reveals something secret or scandalous.  An object or exhibit that is displayed. | Example: The journalist acted as an exposer of corruption.  这位记者充当了揭露腐败的揭露者。  The museum's new exposer showcases ancient artifacts.  博物馆的新展览展示了古代文物。 |近义词：revealer, informant, display | 反义词：concealer, protector
