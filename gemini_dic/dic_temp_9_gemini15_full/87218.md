hereinto:/ˌhɪərˈɪntuː/| adv.|到此，在此之内，进入其中|This refers to something that is included or contained within a particular document, situation, or discussion. It indicates movement or inclusion into a specific place or context.|例句：The details are set forth hereinto.  细节在此文中阐明。

例句：All relevant information has been incorporated hereinto. 所有相关信息都已包含在此。


近义词：therein, within, inside


反义词：therefrom, outside, beyond


词性:副词
