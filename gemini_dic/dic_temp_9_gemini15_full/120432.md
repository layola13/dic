minke whale:/ˈmɪŋk weɪl/ | n. |小须鲸 |A small baleen whale of the family Balaenopteridae, characterized by its relatively small size and streamlined body.  Minke whales are found in all oceans except the Arctic. They are known for their acrobatic displays, often breaching and leaping out of the water. | Example: We saw a minke whale surfacing near the boat. 我们在船附近看到一头小须鲸浮出水面。|近义词：baleen whale, small whale |反义词：blue whale (in terms of size)
