east:/iːst/ | adj., n., adv. | 东方的；东方；向东 |  East refers to the direction that the sun rises.  It can be an adjective, noun, or adverb.  As an adjective, it describes something located in the east. As a noun, it refers to the east direction or region. As an adverb, it indicates movement or direction towards the east. |例句：The sun rises in the east. (adj.)  太阳从东方升起。 The Far East is a fascinating region. (n.) 远东地区是一个迷人的区域。  We travelled east for three days. (adv.) 我们向东走了三天。 |近义词：oriental, eastern |反义词：west
