freakishly:/ˈfriːkɪʃli/ | adv. | 异常地；古怪地；怪异地 |  In an unusual or strange way; oddly.  The adverb describes something as being exceptionally unusual, strange, or bizarre. It often implies something is unsettling or unexpected. | 例句：He was freakishly tall for his age. 他和他年龄的人相比，身高异常高。


例句：The flowers grew freakishly large after the rain. 雨后花长得异常大。


近义词：strangely, oddly, unusually, abnormally, bizarrely


反义词：normally, typically, usually, regularly


词性: 副词
