monoglacial:/ˌmɒnəʊˈɡleɪʃəl/| adj. | 单冰川的；由单一冰川形成的 | Relating to or denoting a landform or deposit shaped by a single glacier.  Monoglacial landforms are often characterized by their simpler, less complex morphology compared to those shaped by multiple glacial advances. | Example: The valley showed clear monoglacial features, indicating a single period of glaciation.  这个山谷显示出明显的单冰川特征，表明只有一个冰川期。 |近义词：single-glaciated |反义词：polyglacial
