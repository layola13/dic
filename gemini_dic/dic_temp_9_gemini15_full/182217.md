remonstrator: /ˈrɛməˌstreɪtər/ | n. |抗议者，反对者 | A person who remonstrates; one who protests or objects.  | The demonstrators marched through the streets, protesting the government's policies.  抗议者们走上街头，抗议政府的政策。| protester, objector, dissenter | supporter, conformist | 名词

一般现在时:
The demonstrator protests against the new law. = 示威者抗议新法律。
He remonstrates with his boss about unfair treatment. = 他向老板抗议不公平待遇。

一般过去时:
The demonstrators remonstrated loudly against the decision. = 示威者大声抗议该决定。
She remonstrated with her son about his bad behavior. = 她因儿子的不良行为而责备他。

现在完成时:
The group has remonstrated repeatedly against the proposed changes. = 该团体已多次抗议拟议的变更。
He has remonstrated with the company about their poor customer service. = 他已向公司抗议其糟糕的客户服务。

过去完成时:
They had remonstrated several times before the issue was finally addressed. = 在问题最终得到解决之前，他们已经抗议过几次了。
She had remonstrated with him many times, but he ignored her. = 她已经多次责备他，但他不理她。

将来时:
The workers will remonstrate against the new working conditions. = 工人们将抗议新的工作条件。
She will remonstrate with him about his lateness. = 她会因为他的迟到而责备他。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would remonstrate with the manager. = 如果我是你，我会向经理抗议。
I wish I had remonstrated sooner about the injustice. = 我希望我早点就这种不公正行为提出抗议。

被动语态:
The government's policies were remonstrated against by many citizens. = 许多公民抗议政府的政策。
His complaints were remonstrated upon by his colleagues. = 他的抱怨受到了同事的谴责。

疑问句:

Did anyone remonstrate with the teacher about the difficult exam? = 有人向老师抗议那场难考试吗？


否定句:
He did not remonstrate against the new rules. = 他没有抗议新规。
She didn't remonstrate with him, choosing to ignore the situation. = 她没有责备他，而是选择忽视这种情况。
