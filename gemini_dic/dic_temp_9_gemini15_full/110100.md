licked:/lɪkt/| v.|舔；轻微打击；击败；|to pass the tongue over (a surface); to defeat or overcome easily.|例句：The dog licked its paw.  狗舔了舔它的爪子。|例句：The team completely licked their opponents.  这个队完全击败了他们的对手。|近义词：lap, swipe, defeat, beat|反义词：None specifically, depends on context (e.g., "to be defeated" is an antonym in the sense of being beaten)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He licks his ice cream. = 他舔着他的冰淇淋。
She licks the stamp. = 她舔邮票。

一般过去时:
The cat licked the milk from the bowl. = 猫舔干净了碗里的牛奶。
He licked his wounds after the defeat. = 失败后，他舔舐着伤口。

现在完成时:
The dog has licked the bowl clean. = 狗已经把碗舔干净了。
She has licked the problem and found a solution. = 她攻克了这个问题并找到了解决方法。

过去完成时:
He had licked the envelope before he put on the stamp. = 他在贴邮票之前已经舔过信封了。
She had licked her wounds after the painful breakup. = 痛心的分手后，她已经疗伤一段时间了。

将来时:
The baby will lick the spoon clean. = 宝宝会把勺子舔干净的。
I think she will lick the competition. = 我认为她会赢得比赛。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would lick my fur all day. = 如果我是一只猫，我会整天舔我的毛。
I wish I could lick my worries away. = 我希望我能把烦恼舔掉。

被动语态:
The stamp was licked before being stuck on the envelope. = 邮票在粘到信封上之前就被舔过了。(比较少用)
The ice cream was licked clean. = 冰淇淋被舔干净了。(比较少用)

疑问句:
Did the dog lick the floor? = 狗舔地板了吗？
Has she licked the bowl? = 她舔过碗了吗？

否定句:
He didn't lick the ice cream. = 他没有舔冰淇淋。
The cat didn't lick the cream. = 猫没有舔奶油。



PHRASE->

lickety-split = 非常快地
a lick and a promise = 草率地；敷衍了事
lick the dust = 彻底失败
