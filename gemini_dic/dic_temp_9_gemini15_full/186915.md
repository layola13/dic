riparians:/rɪˈpeəriənz/| n.|河岸居民；沿河土地所有者|People or entities who own land bordering a river or other body of water and who therefore have certain rights and responsibilities concerning that water.  Riparians often have legal rights to use the water for various purposes, but also have responsibilities regarding its protection and management.|例句：The riparian landowners are responsible for maintaining the banks of the river.  = 沿河土地所有者有责任维护河岸。
The dispute centered on the riparian rights to water usage. = 争端围绕着河岸居民用水权展开。|近义词：riverbank owners, waterfront owners|反义词：inland residents
