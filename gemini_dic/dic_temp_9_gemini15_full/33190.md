chromophotograph: /ˌkroʊmoʊˈfoʊtəɡræf/ | n. |彩色照片|A photograph produced using a process that involves the use of color.  It is an older method of color photography, predating modern color film and digital photography.| The chromophotograph showed vibrant colors, a testament to the skill of the photographer.  彩色照片展现出鲜艳的色彩，证明了摄影师的技艺。|近义词：color photograph, colored picture |反义词：monochrome photograph, black and white photograph
