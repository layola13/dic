dollarise:/ˈdɒləraɪz/|/ˈdɑːləraɪz/| vt. |美元化；以美元计价|To adopt the United States dollar as the official currency of a country or region, or to conduct financial transactions primarily in US dollars.|The country decided to dollarise its economy to stabilize its currency and attract foreign investment.|近义词：dollarize|反义词：de-dollarize

一般现在时:
The government dollarises the economy to control inflation. = 政府实行美元化以控制通货膨胀。
Many businesses dollarise their transactions to avoid currency risks. = 许多企业将其交易美元化以避免汇率风险。


一般过去时:
Ecuador dollarised its economy in 2000. = 厄瓜多尔在2000年实现了经济美元化。
The company dollarised its prices after the currency devaluation. = 该公司在货币贬值后将其价格美元化。


现在完成时:
Several countries have dollarised their economies in recent years. = 近年来，一些国家已经实现了经济美元化。
