mogul:/ˈmoʊɡəl/| n.|巨头，大亨|A person who has great power and influence in a particular field, especially business.  |例句：He's a media mogul with a vast empire. 他是一位拥有庞大帝国的媒体大亨。|近义词：tycoon, magnate, baron|反义词：nobody, insignificant person

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The media mogul controls several newspapers. = 这位媒体大亨控制着几家报纸。
That businessman is a rising mogul in the tech industry. = 那位商人是科技行业冉冉升起的大亨。

一般过去时:
He was a powerful oil mogul in the 1980s. = 他在20世纪80年代是一位强大的石油大亨。
The real estate mogul lost a significant portion of his fortune. = 这位房地产大亨损失了他大部分的财富。

现在完成时:
She has become a fashion mogul in a remarkably short time. = 她只用了很短的时间就成为了时尚大亨。
Several entrepreneurs have become tech moguls through innovative ideas. = 通过创新的想法，几位企业家已经成为了科技大亨。

过去完成时:
Before the scandal, he had been a respected business mogul. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他是一位受人尊敬的商业大亨。
They had already established themselves as media moguls before launching the new channel. = 在推出新频道之前，他们已经确立了自己作为媒体大亨的地位。

将来时:
He will likely become a major mogul in the entertainment industry. = 他很可能会成为娱乐业的主要大亨。
With her current trajectory, she will be a leading mogul in the future. = 按照她目前的轨迹，她将来会成为一位领导级的大亨。

虚拟语气:
If he had been a smarter mogul, he would have avoided the financial crisis. = 如果他是一位更聪明的巨头，他就会避免这场金融危机。
I wish I were a movie mogul and could produce my own films. = 我希望我是一位电影大亨，可以制作我自己的电影。

被动语态:
The company was founded by a former technology mogul. = 这家公司是由一位前科技大亨创立的。
The industry is being dominated by a few powerful moguls. = 这个行业正被少数几个强大的巨头所主导。

疑问句:
Is he really a powerful mogul as people say? = 他真的像人们说的那样是一位强大的大亨吗？
Who are the leading moguls in the automotive industry? = 汽车行业的主要大亨是谁？

否定句:
He is not a mogul; he's just a small business owner. = 他不是大亨；他只是一个小型企业主。
She wasn't a particularly influential mogul in her field. = 她并不是她领域里一个特别有影响力的大亨。
