opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/ | adj. | 不透明的；难以理解的；迟钝的 |  Not allowing light to pass through; not transparent.  Also used figuratively to describe something difficult to understand or someone who is mentally slow. | Example: The glass is opaque, so I can't see through it.  玻璃是不透明的，所以我无法看穿它。 The professor's lecture was opaque to me; I couldn't understand a word. 教授的讲座对我来说太难懂了；我一个字都听不懂。|近义词：impenetrable, obscure, obtuse |反义词：transparent, clear, lucid
