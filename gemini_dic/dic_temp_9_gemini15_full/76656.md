galumphing:/ˈɡælʌmfɪŋ/| v.|笨拙地行走，蹒跚而行|To walk in a clumsy, heavy-footed way.  The word suggests a ponderous, somewhat comical gait.|例句：The giant galumphed through the forest, his footsteps shaking the ground.  巨人笨拙地穿过森林，脚步震动着地面。


例句：He was galumphing along the road, singing loudly off-key. 他在路上笨拙地走着，大声跑调地唱歌。


近义词：stumbling, lurching, lumbering, plodding


反义词：gliding, skipping, dancing


词性：动词
