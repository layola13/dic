outstayed:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪd/| v. |逗留过久；逾期停留 |To remain or stay longer than intended, expected, or permitted.  |例句：I outstayed my welcome at the party.  我晚会待的时间太长了。
He outstayed his visa and faced deportation. 他签证过期了，面临遣返。|近义词：overstay, linger, tarry |反义词：leave early, depart promptly |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outstays his welcome at every party. 他在每个聚会上都待得太久了。
They often outstay their visas.他们经常逾期居留。


一般过去时:
We outstayed our welcome at the dinner party. 我们在晚宴上待的时间太长了。
The guests outstayed their invitation. 客人们待的时间超过了邀请的时间。


现在完成时:
I have outstayed my welcome many times. 我多次待的时间太长了。
She has outstayed her visa by several months. 她的签证已经过期好几个月了。


过去完成时:
They had outstayed their visa before the authorities noticed.  当局注意到之前，他们的签证就已经过期了。
We realized we had outstayed our welcome when the host started yawning. 当主人开始打哈欠时，我们意识到我们待的时间太长了。


将来时:
I think we will outstay our welcome if we don't leave soon. 如果我们不尽快离开，我想我们会待的时间太长。
If they continue to stay, they will outstay their invitation. 如果他们继续待下去，他们就会超过邀请的时间。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been so engrossed in conversation, I wouldn't have outstayed my welcome. 如果我没有那么专注于谈话，我就不会待得太久了。
I wish I hadn't outstayed my welcome at the party. 我真希望我没有在派对上待得太久。


被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice)
Their visit was outstayed its welcome. 他们的访问时间过长，不受欢迎。（比较生硬，不常用）


疑问句:
Did we outstay our welcome? 我们是不是待的时间太长了？
Have they outstayed their visa? 他们的签证过期了吗？


否定句:
We didn't outstay our welcome. 我们没有待的时间太长。
They didn't want to outstay their welcome. 他们不想待的时间太长。


PHRASE->
outstay one's welcome: 待得太久（最常用）
outstay a visit: 访问时间过长
outstay an invitation:  超过邀请的时间
outstay a deadline:  超过截止日期
outstay one's time: 超出时间
