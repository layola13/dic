lade:/leɪd/| vt.|装载，装满；使沉重；|to put a load on or into (something); to burden or weigh down.|例句：They laded the ship with cargo. 他们用货物装载了船。

例句：The heavy snow laded the branches of the trees. 厚厚的积雪压弯了树枝。

近义词：load, burden, freight |反义词：unload, lighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lades the truck every day. 他每天都装载卡车。
She lades the boxes onto the shelf. 她把箱子装到架子上。


一般过去时:
They laded the ship with grain. 他们用谷物装满了船。
He laded the donkey with heavy sacks. 他用沉重的麻袋装满了驴子。


现在完成时:
They have laded all the goods onto the train. 他们已经把所有货物装上了火车。
She has laded the washing machine with clothes. 她已经把洗衣机装满了衣服。


过去完成时:
The workers had laded all the cargo before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，工人们已经装完了所有的货物。
He had laded the truck before he went home. 他回家之前已经装好了卡车。


将来时:
They will lade the ship tomorrow. 他们明天将装载这艘船。
She will lade the cart with hay. 她将用干草装满手推车。


虚拟语气:
If he laded the truck carefully, there would be no problems. 如果他仔细装载卡车，就不会有问题了。
I wish I had laded the boxes more securely. 我希望我把箱子装得更牢固一些。


被动语态:
The ship was laded with oil. 这艘船装满了石油。
The truck is being laded with bricks. 卡车正在装砖。


疑问句:
How much cargo was laded onto the ship? 船上装了多少货物？
Have they laded the van yet? 他们已经装好货车了吗？


否定句:
They did not lade the boat properly. 他们没有正确地装载船只。
She doesn't lade the shelves very carefully. 她装货架不太仔细。


PHRASE->
lade with: 装满...
lade down: 放下
lade upon: 加在…上
lade out: 铺开
lade in: 装入


词源:
源自古英语 *ladan，意为“装载”。  与load 有关。
