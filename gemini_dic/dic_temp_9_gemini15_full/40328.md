contraband:/ˈkɒntrəbænd/ /ˈkɑːntrəbænd/| n. adj.|违禁品；走私货|Goods that are prohibited by law from being imported or exported.  It can also refer to illegal activities.|例句：The police seized a large quantity of contraband.  警方查获了大量违禁品。

The smugglers were caught trying to transport contraband across the border. 走私者试图越境运输违禁品时被捕。|近义词：smuggled goods, illicit goods, prohibited goods|反义词：legal goods, permitted goods|名词，形容词
