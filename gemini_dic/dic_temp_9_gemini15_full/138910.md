nutraceuticals:/nuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəlz/| n. |营养保健品；功能食品|Foods or food products that provide health benefits beyond basic nutrition.  Nutraceuticals are often used to prevent or treat diseases.  They can include vitamins, minerals, herbs, and other natural substances.|例句：Many people take nutraceuticals to improve their overall health and well-being. 很多人服用营养保健品来改善他们的整体健康和福祉。

The market for nutraceuticals is booming, with consumers increasingly seeking natural ways to enhance their health. 营养保健品市场蓬勃发展，消费者越来越寻求自然的方式来增强他们的健康。
