interpretive:/ɪnˈtɜːrpɹətɪv/| adj.|解释性的；诠释性的 | Relating to or involving interpretation, especially of artistic works or texts.  It suggests an explanation or understanding that goes beyond the literal meaning. |例句：The museum offers an interpretive tour that explains the historical significance of the artifacts.  博物馆提供了解释性导览，解释了这些文物的历史意义。

例句：Her interpretive dance conveyed a powerful message about loss and grief.她的诠释性舞蹈传达了关于失去和悲伤的有力信息。


近义词：explanatory, analytical, explicative, expository


反义词：literal, factual, descriptive (in the sense of purely descriptive without interpretation)

词性:形容词
