preresolution:/ˌpriːˌrezəˈluːʃən/| n.|预决议，预先决议|A preresolution is a preliminary resolution or decision, often made before a formal vote or official decision is taken.  It serves as a preparatory step, outlining the key points to be considered in the final resolution.  It may be subject to further discussion and amendment before final adoption.|The committee drafted a preresolution outlining the main points of the upcoming environmental protection bill.|近义词：preliminary decision, draft resolution|反义词：final resolution, conclusive decision
