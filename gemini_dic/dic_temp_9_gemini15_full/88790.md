hoe's:/hoʊz/| n. |锄头；(非正式)女人|A hoe is a tool used for gardening, typically with a flat blade attached to a long handle.  In informal contexts, it's a derogatory slang term for a woman who is promiscuous.|例句：He used a hoe to till the soil. 他用锄头耕地。|近义词：spade, cultivator|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer uses a hoe to weed the garden. = 农民用锄头除草。
She hoes the rows of vegetables every morning. = 她每天早上都用锄头锄菜地。


一般过去时:
He hoed the field all afternoon. = 他整个下午都在锄地。
They hoed the potatoes yesterday. = 他们昨天锄土豆。


现在完成时:
She has hoed the entire garden. = 她已经锄了整个菜园。
The farmers have hoed their fields for the season. = 农民们已经锄完了他们这季的田地。


过去完成时:
They had hoed the corn before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经锄完了玉米。
She had hoed the vegetables before lunch. = 午餐前她已经锄完了蔬菜。


将来时:
He will hoe the garden tomorrow. = 他明天将要锄草。
They will hoe the field after the rain stops. = 雨停后他们将要锄地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a hoe, I would hoe the garden. = 如果我有一把锄头，我就会锄草。
I wish I had hoed the garden yesterday. = 我真希望昨天就把草锄了。


被动语态:
The garden was hoed by the gardener. = 菜园是被园丁锄过的。
The fields are being hoed by the farm workers. = 田地正在被农场工人锄。


疑问句:
Did he hoe the field? = 他锄地了吗？
Has she finished hoeing the vegetables? = 她锄完菜了吗？


否定句:
He didn't hoe the garden today. = 他今天没有锄草。
She hasn't hoed the potatoes yet. = 她还没锄土豆。
