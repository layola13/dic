cropsick:/ˈkrɒpsɪk/| adj. | 晕船的，乘车(船)晕的 | Suffering from motion sickness, typically on a boat or ship.  This term is less common than "seasick" but can refer to motion sickness in any vehicle. | Example:  He felt cropsick after the bumpy car ride. 他在颠簸的汽车旅途中感到晕车。 | 近义词：seasick, carsick, airsick, travel sick | 反义词：well, healthy
