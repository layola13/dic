grammaticize:/ˈɡræmətɪsaɪz/| vt.| 使合乎语法；语法化 | To make something conform to the rules of grammar.  The process of incorporating a word or phrase into the grammatical system of a language. |例句：He tried to grammaticize the slang he used in his writing. 他试图使他写作中使用的俚语符合语法规范。|例句：Over time, the phrase "gonna" has been grammaticized in informal speech. 随着时间的推移，“gonna”这个词组在非正式语境中已经被语法化了。|近义词：formalize, standardize |反义词：ungrammaticalize (非标准词)
