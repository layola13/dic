replenishments:/rɪˈplɛnɪʃmənts/| n. |补充；补充物；再填充|The act of replenishing something; the things used to replenish something.|例句：The company needs to focus on replenishments to meet the increased demand. 公司需要专注于补充以满足日益增长的需求。

例句：Regular replenishments of stock are crucial for a smooth business operation. 定期补充库存对于业务的顺利运营至关重要。


近义词：replacements, refills, restockings|反义词：depletions, exhaustions|名词
