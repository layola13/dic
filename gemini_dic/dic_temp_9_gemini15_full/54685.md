disordering: /dɪsˈɔːrdərɪŋ/ | v. n. | 扰乱；混乱；失调 | The act of disrupting or throwing into disorder; a state of being disordered.  | 例句：The storm caused widespread disordering of the power grid.  暴风雨导致电网大范围混乱。 The disordering of his thoughts prevented him from concentrating.  他思绪混乱，无法集中精力。 | 近义词：disrupting, disorganizing, confusing, unsettling | 反义词：ordering, organizing, arranging, stabilizing | 动词/名词


USAGE->

The loud music is disordering the meeting. = 嘈杂的音乐扰乱了会议。
He is constantly disordering his room. = 他不断地把房间弄乱。
