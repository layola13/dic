roll-feeding: /ˈroʊlˌfiːdɪŋ/ | n. | 卷筒送料；卷筒进纸 | The process of feeding a material, such as paper or film, from a roll.  This method is commonly used in printing, packaging, and other industrial processes. | Example: The printer uses roll-feeding to continuously supply paper for high-volume printing.  卷筒送料打印机使用卷筒进纸来连续供应纸张进行大批量打印。


近义词：reel feeding, sheet-fed (反义词)


反义词：sheet feeding (单个纸张送料)
