reinterprets:/ˌriːɪnˈtɜːrprɪts/ | vt. |重新解释；重新诠释 | To interpret something again or in a new way.  This often involves a shift in perspective, understanding, or meaning. |例句：The artist reinterprets classic myths in a modern context.  艺术家在一个现代语境中重新诠释了经典神话。

The critic reinterprets the novel's ending, suggesting a different meaning. 批评家对小说的结尾进行了重新诠释，暗示了不同的含义。|近义词：reinterpret, re-evaluate, re-examine, re-assess |反义词：misinterpret, misunderstand |动词
