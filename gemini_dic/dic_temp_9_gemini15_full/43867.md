cresol:/ˈkriːzɔːl/| n. |甲酚|A group of three isomeric methylphenols, all of which are colorless to light yellow liquids with a phenolic odor.  They are used as disinfectants, antiseptics, and in the production of other chemicals.|例句：Cresol is a common disinfectant used in hospitals. 甲酚是医院常用的消毒剂。|例句：Many synthetic resins are produced using cresol as a component. 许多合成树脂是用甲酚作为成分生产的。|近义词：methylphenol, hydroxytoluene|反义词:|
