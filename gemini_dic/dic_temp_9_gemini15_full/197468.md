sheer:/ʃɪər/| adj.|纯粹的，绝对的；陡峭的；透明的；轻薄的 |  Sheer refers to something complete, absolute, or very steep. It can also describe something transparent or very thin. | 例句：The sheer cliff dropped hundreds of feet to the sea below.  悬崖峭壁陡峭地直落数百英尺到下面的海里。

The sheer audacity of his claim surprised everyone. 他说法的大胆程度让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词： utter, absolute, transparent, sheer, thin, steep | 反义词：opaque, thick, gradual, slight
