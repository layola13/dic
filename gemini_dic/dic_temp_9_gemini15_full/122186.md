misunderstand:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/| vt.|误解，误会|to fail to understand correctly; interpret wrongly.|例句：I misunderstood his instructions and did the wrong thing. 我误解了他的指示，做了错事。|例句：Don't misunderstand me, I'm not criticizing you. 别误会我，我不是在批评你。|近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue|反义词：understand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misunderstands my intentions. = 他误解了我的意图。
They misunderstand the rules of the game. = 他们误解了游戏的规则。

一般过去时:
I misunderstood the question. = 我误解了这个问题。
She misunderstood the email. = 她误解了邮件的内容。

现在完成时:
I have misunderstood you all along. = 我一直以来都误解你了。
We have misunderstood the instructions. = 我们误解了指示。

过去完成时:
I had misunderstood his signals. = 我误解了他的信号。
They had misunderstood the agreement. = 他们误解了协议。

将来时:
He will misunderstand my silence. = 他会误解我的沉默。
They will misunderstand the situation. = 他们会误解这种情况。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misunderstood him, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我没有误解他，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I hadn't misunderstood her feelings. = 我真希望我没有误解她的感受。

被动语态:
His words were misunderstood. = 他的话被误解了。
The instructions were misunderstood by many. = 许多人误解了指示。

疑问句:
Do you think they misunderstood me? = 你认为他们误解了我吗？
Did I misunderstand what he said? = 我是不是误解了他所说的话？

否定句:
I didn't misunderstand the message. = 我没有误解信息。
She didn't misunderstand the assignment. = 她没有误解作业。
