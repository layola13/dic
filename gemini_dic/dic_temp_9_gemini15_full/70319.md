field:/fiːld/| n. v.|田地，领域，场地|A field is an area of open land, especially one used for growing crops or keeping animals.  It can also refer to a particular branch of study or activity. |例句：The farmer worked hard in his field.  这位农民在他的田里辛勤劳作。|例句：She's an expert in the field of medicine. 她是医学领域的专家。|近义词：area, domain, sphere, expanse|反义词：building, structure, indoors|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer plants seeds in the field. = 农民在田里播种。
He works in the marketing field. = 他在市场营销领域工作。


一般过去时:
They harvested the wheat from the field. = 他们从田里收割了小麦。
She studied physics in the field of quantum mechanics. = 她研究量子力学的物理学领域。


现在完成时:
We have finished working in the field. = 我们已经完成了田间的工作。
She has made significant contributions to the field of biology. = 她为生物学领域做出了重大贡献。


过去完成时:
They had plowed the field before planting. = 他们在播种前已经犁好了田地。
He had already established himself in the field before receiving the award. = 他在获奖之前就已经在该领域站稳了脚跟。


将来时:
We will harvest the crops from the field next month. = 我们下个月将从田里收割庄稼。
She will pursue a career in the field of engineering. = 她将在工程领域追求事业。


虚拟语气:
If I had a larger field, I could grow more crops. = 如果我有一个更大的田地，我就能种植更多的庄稼。
If she were in a different field, she might be happier. = 如果她在不同的领域，她可能会更快乐。


被动语态:
The field was plowed by the tractor. = 田地被拖拉机犁了。
The research was published in a leading field journal. = 这项研究发表在一本领先的领域期刊上。


疑问句:
Is this field suitable for growing rice? = 这块地适合种植水稻吗？
What is the most promising field for future development? = 未来发展最有希望的领域是什么？


否定句:
That field is not suitable for planting corn. = 那块地不适合种植玉米。
He is not interested in the field of finance. = 他对金融领域不感兴趣。
