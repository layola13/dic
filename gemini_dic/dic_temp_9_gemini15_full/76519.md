galeopithecus:/ˌɡeɪliˈoʊpɪθɪkəs/| n. |鼯猴属|Galeopithecus is a genus of mammals belonging to the family Cynocephalidae, commonly known as colugos or flying lemurs. They are arboreal mammals found in Southeast Asia, characterized by their patagium, a membrane that stretches from their neck to their limbs and tail, enabling them to glide between trees.  They are not true lemurs nor do they truly fly, but rather glide.|The colugo (Galeopithecus volans) is a fascinating creature.|近义词: colugo, flying lemur|反义词:  (无直接反义词)|名词
