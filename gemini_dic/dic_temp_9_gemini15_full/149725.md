palmate:/ˈpælmeɪt/| adj.|掌状的，五指并生的|Having a hand-like or palmate structure, resembling a hand with fingers spread out.  The term is often used to describe leaves or veins in plants.|例句：The maple leaf is palmate, with five lobes radiating from a central point.  枫叶是掌状的，五个裂片从中心点辐射出来。|例句：The veins in this plant's leaf are palmate. 这株植物叶脉是掌状的。|近义词：digitate, hand-shaped|反义词：pinnate
