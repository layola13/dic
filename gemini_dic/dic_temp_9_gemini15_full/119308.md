microbacteria:/ˌmaɪkroʊbækˈtɪəriə/| n.|微细菌属|Microbacteria are a genus of Gram-positive bacteria belonging to the family Microbacteriaceae.  They are typically found in soil and water environments and are often associated with plant roots.  Some species are also known to be opportunistic pathogens in humans and animals. |例句：Researchers are studying the role of Microbacteria in plant growth promotion. 研究人员正在研究微细菌在促进植物生长中的作用。|近义词：bacteria, actinobacteria|反义词：macrobacteria (not a commonly used term)
