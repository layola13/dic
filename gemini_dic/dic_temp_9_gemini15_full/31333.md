characterful: /ˈkærəktərfʊl/ | adj. | 富有个性; 特色鲜明;  引人注目的 | Having a strong and interesting personality; full of character.  |


例句：

He's a very characterful actor, bringing a unique flair to every role. = 他是一位非常有个性的演员，每个角色都展现出独特的风格。

The old house had a characterful charm, with its crooked windows and mismatched doors. = 这座老房子有着独特的魅力，有着歪斜的窗户和不配套的门。


近义词： distinctive, individualistic, quirky, eccentric, spirited

反义词： bland, uninteresting, dull, commonplace, ordinary

词性：形容词
