cripplingly:/ˈkrɪplɪŋli/| adv. |严重地，严重到使人残废地，极度地 |severely; to the point of crippling; extremely.  It describes something so severe that it severely impairs or disables something or someone.  |例句：His cripplingly high debt left him unable to afford basic necessities. 他巨额的债务使他无力承担基本生活必需品。


例句：The company suffered a cripplingly bad recession. 这家公司遭受了严重的经济衰退。


近义词：severely, extremely, devastatingly, overwhelmingly, debilitatingly


反义词：slightly, mildly, marginally


词性:副词
