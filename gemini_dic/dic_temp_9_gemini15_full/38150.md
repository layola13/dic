compression:/kəmˈprɛʃən/| n. |压缩；压紧；浓缩|The act or process of reducing the size or volume of something.  It can refer to physical compression (like squeezing a sponge) or data compression (like reducing the size of a file).|例句：The compression of the gas increased its temperature. 气体的压缩提高了它的温度。

例句：Data compression is essential for efficient storage and transmission. 数据压缩对于高效存储和传输至关重要。|近义词：condensation, compaction, squeezing |反义词：expansion, decompression, enlargement |名词
