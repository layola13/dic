printery:/ˈprɪntəri/| n. |印刷厂，印刷所|A printery is a place where printing is done, often on a large scale. It's a workshop or factory equipped with printing presses and other necessary equipment.|例句：The old printery still uses traditional letterpress methods.  这个古老的印刷厂仍然使用传统的凸版印刷方法。|近义词：printing house, print shop, press|反义词：None.
