over-egg:/ˌoʊvərˈɛɡ/| vt. |过度强调；画蛇添足；做过头|To exaggerate or overemphasize something; to add too much of something, often to the detriment of the overall effect.|例句：He over-egged his description of the accident. 他对事故的描述过于夸张。|例句：Don't over-egg the pudding; a little spice is all it needs. 不要在布丁里放太多调料；只需要一点调料就够了。|近义词：exaggerate, overstate, overdo|反义词：understate, downplay
