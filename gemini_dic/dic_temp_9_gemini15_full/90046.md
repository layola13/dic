hospitalising: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 将…送入医院；住院治疗 | The act of admitting someone to a hospital for treatment.  |例句：The doctor decided to hospitalise the patient after the accident.  医生在事故后决定将病人送入医院。|例句：She was hospitalised for a week due to pneumonia. 她因肺炎住院一周。|近义词：admitting to hospital, hospitalizing | 反义词：discharging from hospital, releasing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hospitalise patients with severe injuries. = 他们将严重受伤的病人送入医院。
The hospital usually hospitalises around 100 patients daily. = 这家医院通常每天收治约100名病人。


一般过去时:
The paramedics hospitalised him immediately after the car crash. = 车祸后，医护人员立即将他送往医院。
They hospitalised her for observation after the surgery. = 手术后，他们将她送院观察。


现在完成时:
They have hospitalised over 500 patients this year. = 今年他们已经收治了500多名病人。
The hospital has hospitalised many patients with the flu recently. = 最近医院收治了许多患流感的病人。


过去完成时:
By the time the ambulance arrived, they had already hospitalised the injured child.  = 救护车到达时，他们已经将受伤的孩子送往医院了。
She had been hospitalised three times before this incident. = 在这次事件之前，她已经被送往医院三次了。


将来时:
The doctor thinks he will need to be hospitalised for further treatment. = 医生认为他需要住院进一步治疗。
They will hospitalise him if his condition worsens. = 如果他的病情恶化，他们会将他送往医院。


虚拟语气:
If his condition deteriorated, they would hospitalise him immediately. = 如果他的病情恶化，他们会立即将他送往医院。
I wish they had hospitalised him sooner. = 我希望他们早点将他送往医院。


被动语态:
He was hospitalised for several days. = 他住院好几天了。
Many patients were hospitalised due to the outbreak. = 由于疫情爆发，许多病人住院了。


疑问句:
Will she need to be hospitalised? = 她需要住院吗？
Was he hospitalised after the accident? = 事故后他住院了吗？


否定句:
They did not hospitalise him because his injuries were minor. = 他们没有将他送往医院，因为他的伤势较轻。
The doctor decided not to hospitalise her at that time. = 医生当时决定不让她住院。
