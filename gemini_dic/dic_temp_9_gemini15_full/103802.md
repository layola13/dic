joviality:/dʒoʊviˈæləti/ | n. |欢乐；愉悦；快活 |The feeling of being jovial; cheerfulness and friendliness.  It often involves boisterous merriment and good humor. |例句：Their joviality was infectious, and soon everyone was laughing and joking. 他们的欢乐是具有感染力的，很快每个人都笑了起来，开玩笑了起来。

例句：The party was filled with joviality and good cheer.  派对充满了欢乐和喜庆。


近义词：mirth, gaiety, cheerfulness, merriment, hilarity |反义词：gloom, sadness, depression, melancholy, moroseness


词源:  From Middle English *jovialite, from Old French *jovialité, from *jovial (see jovial).  Ultimately derived from Jupiter, the Roman god associated with joy and festivity.
