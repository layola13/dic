frontal:/ˈfrʌntəl/ | adj. |额头的；正面的；直接的；主要的| relating to the forehead or front part of something; direct and open;  of primary importance |例句：The frontal lobe of the brain controls many important functions.  大脑额叶控制着许多重要功能。

The frontal assault on the enemy position was successful. 对敌方阵地的正面进攻取得了成功。

近义词：forehead, anterior, direct, leading, primary |反义词：rear, back, indirect, secondary, minor
