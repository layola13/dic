electro-acoustics:/ˌɪˌlɛktroʊəˈkuːstɪks/| n. |电子声学|The branch of acoustics dealing with the recording, reproduction, and amplification of sound using electronic devices.  It encompasses the design, development, and application of electronic systems for the generation, transmission, reception, and reproduction of sound.|例句：Electro-acoustics is crucial in the design of high-fidelity audio systems. 电子声学在高保真音响系统的设计中至关重要。

例句：Modern electro-acoustic techniques allow for the creation of immersive soundscapes in movies and video games. 现代电子声学技术允许在电影和游戏中创建身临其境的音景。
