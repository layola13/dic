hierarchism:/ˈhaɪərɑːrkɪzəm/| n. |等级制度；等级森严；等级观念|The belief in or practice of hierarchical organization; a system or structure in which things are ranked one above the other.|The company's rigid hierarchism stifled creativity and innovation.  这家公司的等级森严扼杀了创造力和创新。  The strict hierarchism of the military prevented open dissent.  军队的严格等级制度阻止了公开的异议。|hierarchy, stratification, ranking|egalitarianism, democracy|名词
