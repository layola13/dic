rabbitry:/ˈræbɪtri/| n. |兔场；养兔场；兔舍|A rabbitry is a place where rabbits are bred and kept.  It can refer to a building, a farm, or even a specific area dedicated to raising rabbits.  The term often implies a more organized and possibly commercial operation than simply keeping a few pet rabbits.|例句：He built a large rabbitry to house his growing colony of Angora rabbits. 他建造了一个大型兔场来容纳他不断增长的安哥拉兔群。

例句：The local rabbitry supplies rabbits to pet shops across the region. 当地兔场向该地区各宠物店供应兔子。


近义词：rabbit farm, hutch (for smaller scale operations)|反义词：无
