词源:

half- + deafening.  "Deafening" derives from "deaf" (meaning unable to hear),  + "-ening" (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "making or tending to make").  Therefore, "half-deafening" literally means "making someone half-deaf," or "almost deafening".
