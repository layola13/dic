lost out: /lɒst aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 错失，失去机会，处于不利地位 | To fail to achieve something or to miss an opportunity.  |例句：He lost out on the promotion because he didn't have enough experience. 他因为经验不足而错失了晋升机会。

He lost out to a younger, more dynamic candidate. 他输给了更年轻更有活力的候选人。


近义词：miss, forfeit, fail to achieve, be disadvantaged, be at a disadvantage | 反义词：gain, win, succeed, achieve, triumph | 词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They often lose out on good deals because they don't act quickly enough. = 他们经常因为行动不够迅速而错失良机。
We lose out if we don't invest in new technology. = 如果我们不投资新技术，我们将处于不利地位。


一般过去时:
She lost out on the scholarship because of her low GPA. = 由于GPA低，她错失了奖学金。
The small business lost out to the larger competitors. = 这家小企业输给了更大的竞争对手。


现在完成时:
I've lost out on several job opportunities recently. = 我最近错失了好几个工作机会。
They have lost out on significant profits due to the market downturn. = 由于市场低迷，他们损失了大量的利润。


过去完成时:
He realized he had lost out on a great chance to travel the world. = 他意识到他错失了一个环游世界的大好机会。
We discovered that we had lost out on a crucial piece of information. = 我们发现我们错过了一条至关重要的信息。


将来时:
If we don't hurry, we'll lose out on the best seats. = 如果我们不快点，我们将错失最好的座位。
They will lose out on the contract if they don't submit their proposal on time. = 如果他们不按时提交提案，他们将失去合同。


虚拟语气:
If he had applied earlier, he wouldn't have lost out on the job. = 如果他早点申请，他就不会错过这份工作了。
I wish I hadn't lost out on that investment opportunity. = 我真希望我没有错过那个投资机会。


被动语态:
Many small businesses are lost out by the economic crisis. = 许多小企业都受到了经济危机的冲击而处于不利地位。
The team felt they were lost out by the unfair judging. = 该团队认为他们受到了不公平的评判而处于不利地位。


疑问句:
Will we lose out if we don't attend the meeting? = 如果我们不参加会议，我们会错过什么吗？
Did they lose out on anything significant during the negotiations? = 他们在谈判中错过了什么重要的事情吗？


否定句:
We didn't lose out on anything important. = 我们没有错过任何重要的事情。
They didn't lose out on the deal despite fierce competition. = 尽管竞争激烈，他们也没有失去这笔交易。


PHRASE->
lose out on a deal: 错失交易
lose out on an opportunity: 错失机会
lose out to someone: 输给某人
lose out in the competition: 在竞争中失败
lose out because of...: 因为...而失败
