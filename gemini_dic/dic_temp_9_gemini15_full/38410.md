concernedness:/kənˈsɜːrndnəs/ | n. | 担忧；关心；焦虑 | The feeling of worry or anxiety about something.  It often implies a sense of responsibility or involvement. |例句：Her concernedness for her son's well-being was evident.  她对儿子的安危非常担忧。

例句：The teacher's concernedness about the failing grades was palpable. 老师对不及格成绩的担忧显而易见。


近义词：worry, anxiety, apprehension, solicitude, care | 反义词：indifference, apathy, unconcern


词源:  Derived from the adjective "concerned," which comes from the Old French "concerner" (to concern), ultimately from the Latin "concernere" (to weave together, to relate to).  The suffix "-ness" is a common English suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
