overperforms:/ˌoʊvərpərˈfɔːrmz/ | v. | 超额完成；表现优异 | To perform beyond expectations or targets.  |The company overperformed in the last quarter, exceeding all sales projections.  这家公司上个季度业绩超预期，超过了所有的销售预测。| outperform, exceed, surpass | underperform


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fund consistently overperforms its benchmark. = 这只基金持续跑赢基准。
Our sales team overperforms every month. = 我们的销售团队每个月都超额完成任务。

一般过去时:
The stock overperformed last year. = 这只股票去年表现优异。
The athlete overperformed in the competition. = 这名运动员在比赛中表现出色。

现在完成时:
The investment strategy has overperformed the market. = 这种投资策略跑赢了市场。
The project has overperformed its initial goals. = 这个项目已经超额完成了最初的目标。

过去完成时:
The team had overperformed expectations before the merger. = 在合并之前，团队已经超出了预期。
The company had overperformed its targets for three consecutive years. = 这家公司连续三年超额完成了目标。

将来时:
Analysts predict the company will overperform next year. = 分析师预测该公司明年将表现优异。
We expect the new product to overperform in the market. = 我们预计新产品将在市场上表现出色。

虚拟语气:
If the economy improves, the company would overperform. = 如果经济好转，公司业绩将会超预期。
I wish the team had overperformed in the final game. = 我希望球队在决赛中能有更好的表现。

被动语态:
The expectations were overperformed by the team. = 团队超额完成了预期。（略显不自然，主动语态更常用）
The sales targets were overperformed by a significant margin. = 销售目标被大幅超额完成。（略显不自然，主动语态更常用）

疑问句:
Did the investment overperform its benchmarks? = 这项投资是否跑赢了基准？
Does the new marketing campaign overperform the old one? = 新的营销活动是否比旧的营销活动效果更好？

否定句:
The project did not overperform as expected. = 项目并没有像预期的那样超额完成。
The stock did not overperform the market index. = 这只股票没有跑赢市场指数。


PHRASE->
overperform the market = 跑赢市场
overperform expectations = 超出预期
significantly overperform = 大幅超额完成
consistently overperform = 持续超额完成
dramatically overperform = 剧烈超额完成
