preclude:/prɪˈkluːd/| vt.|阻止；排除；妨碍|To prevent something from happening; to make something impossible.|The heavy rain precluded a picnic.  大雨阻止了野餐。|His age precluded him from joining the army. 他的年龄使他无法参军。|近义词：prevent, avoid, hinder, impede|反义词：allow, permit, enable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain precludes a picnic. = 大雨阻止了野餐。
His busy schedule precludes him from attending the meeting. = 他繁忙的日程安排使他无法参加会议。

一般过去时:
The storm precluded our travel plans. = 暴风雨打乱了我们的旅行计划。
His illness precluded him from competing in the race. = 他的病使他无法参加比赛。

现在完成时:
The bad weather has precluded any outdoor activities. = 恶劣的天气已经阻止了任何户外活动。
His injury has precluded him from playing football this season. = 他的伤势使他本赛季无法踢足球。

过去完成时:
The earlier accident had precluded her from attending the conference. = 早些时候的事故使她无法参加会议。
A previous engagement had precluded him from accepting the invitation. = 此前的约会使他无法接受邀请。

将来时:
The strike will preclude the delivery of goods. = 罢工将阻止货物的交付。
A lack of funding will preclude the project from moving forward. = 资金不足将阻止该项目继续推进。

虚拟语气:
If he had not been ill, nothing would have precluded his success. = 如果他不生病，没有什么能阻止他的成功。
If the weather had been better, it wouldn't have precluded our hike. = 如果天气更好，它就不会阻止我们的徒步旅行。

被动语态:
The game was precluded by the bad weather. = 比赛因恶劣天气而取消。
His participation was precluded by his age. = 他因为年龄原因而被取消参赛资格。

疑问句:
Does his age preclude him from getting the job? = 他的年龄是否会妨碍他得到这份工作？
Would a lack of funding preclude the project's completion? = 资金不足是否会阻止项目的完成？

否定句:
Nothing will preclude me from achieving my goals. = 没有任何事情能阻止我实现我的目标。
The bad weather did not preclude us from enjoying our holiday. = 恶劣的天气并没有阻止我们享受假期。


PHRASE->

preclude the chance of = 排除...的机会
preclude any further = 阻止进一步
preclude all hope = 彻底破灭希望
