embitters:/ɪmˈbɪtərz/| vt.|使痛苦；使受苦；使怀恨|To cause (someone) to feel bitter or resentful.  The constant criticism embittered her.  |The constant failures embittered him towards life.|使人痛苦；使人怨恨|to make someone feel bitter and resentful|近义词：sour, exasperate, gall|反义词：soothe, comfort, gladden|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news embitters him. = 这个消息使他痛苦。
Her constant complaints embitter their relationship. = 她不断的抱怨使他们的关系变得痛苦。

一般过去时:
The loss of his job embittered him. = 失去工作使他痛苦。
The betrayal embittered her towards her former friend. = 背叛使她对以前的朋友怀恨在心。

现在完成时:
Years of hardship have embittered her. = 多年的苦难使她痛苦不堪。
His failures have embittered him against the world. = 他的失败使他怨恨世界。

过去完成时:
He had been embittered by years of disappointment. = 他多年来一直失望，这使他痛苦不堪。
She had been embittered by the injustice she had suffered. = 她遭受的不公正待遇使她痛苦不堪。

将来时:
This decision will embitter many people. = 这个决定将会使许多人痛苦。
Further setbacks will only embitter the situation. = 进一步的挫折只会使情况恶化。

虚拟语气:
If I knew this would embitter him, I wouldn't have told him. = 如果我知道这会让他痛苦，我就不告诉他了。
I wish this news hadn't embittered her so much. = 我希望这个消息没有让她如此痛苦。

被动语态:
He was embittered by the unfair treatment. = 他因为不公平的待遇而痛苦。
Many were embittered by the outcome of the election. = 许多人对选举的结果感到痛苦。

疑问句:
What events embittered him so deeply? = 什么事件让他如此痛苦？
Will this decision embitter the public? = 这个决定会让公众感到痛苦吗？

否定句:
The victory did not embitter him. = 胜利并没有让他痛苦。
The good news didn't embitter her mood. = 好消息并没有让她心情不好。
