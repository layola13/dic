dirigibles:/ˈdɪrɪdʒəbəlz/| n.|飞艇；飞船|A dirigible is a type of airship, a lighter-than-air aircraft that is propelled by engines and steered with control surfaces.  They are characterized by their rigid or semi-rigid structure, unlike balloons.|Example: The Hindenburg was a famous dirigible that tragically caught fire.  希特勒号是一艘著名的飞艇，它悲惨地发生了火灾。  The Goodyear Blimp is a well-known example of a modern dirigible.  固特异飞艇是现代飞艇的一个著名例子。|近义词：airship, zeppelin|反义词：airplane, helicopter|名词
