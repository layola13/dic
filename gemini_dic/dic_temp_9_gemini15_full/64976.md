estimations:/ˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃənz/| n. |估计，估算，评价|The act of forming an opinion or judgment about something; an approximate calculation of the value, number, quantity, or extent of something.|The estimations of the project cost varied widely.|近义词：calculations, appraisals, assessments|反义词：precisions, exactitudes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His estimations of the market are usually accurate. = 他对市场的估计通常很准确。
Their estimations for the project are always too high. = 他们对项目的估算总是太高。


一般过去时:
The estimations from the previous year were incorrect. = 去年做的估算是不正确的。
Her estimations of the damage were surprisingly accurate. = 她对损失的估算出奇地准确。


现在完成时:
We have completed all the estimations for the upcoming year. = 我们已经完成了明年所有的估算工作。

过去完成时:
