hatchway:/ˈhætʃweɪ/| n.|舱口，天窗|A hatchway is an opening in a deck, floor, or roof, especially one that is fitted with a hatch.  It provides access to a lower deck or compartment on a ship or aircraft, or to a cellar or basement in a building.|例句：The sailors opened the hatchway to let in fresh air. 水手们打开舱口让新鲜空气进来。

The crew climbed down through the hatchway to the engine room. 船员们从舱口爬到机舱。|近义词：opening, doorway, access point|反义词：closed, sealed
