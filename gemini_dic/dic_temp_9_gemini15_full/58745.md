dungeon:/ˈdʌnʒən/| n.|地牢；牢狱|A dungeon is a dark, underground prison, especially in a castle.  It is often associated with imprisonment, torture, and suffering. |例句：The prisoner was thrown into a dark and damp dungeon.  囚犯被扔进了一个黑暗潮湿的地牢。

例句：The legend tells of a terrifying monster lurking in the castle dungeon. 传说讲述了一个可怕的怪物潜伏在城堡地牢里。


近义词：prison, jail, cell, underground chamber|反义词：paradise, heaven, freedom
