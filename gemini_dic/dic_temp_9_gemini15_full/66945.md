experi: /ɪkˈspɪəriən/ | n. |经验；体验；阅历 | Experience; the practical contact with and observation of facts or events.  It often implies learning or personal growth from the encounter. | 例句：My experience in customer service has been invaluable. 我的客户服务经验非常宝贵。  He gained valuable experience working abroad. 他在国外工作中获得了宝贵的经验。 |近义词：experience, practice, knowledge | 反义词：inexperience, naiveté


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My experience helps me in my current role. = 我的经验帮助我胜任目前的工作。
This experience teaches me a valuable lesson. = 这段经历教会我宝贵的教训。

一般过去时:
My previous experience included managing a team. = 我之前的经验包括管理一个团队。
The experience shaped my future career path. = 那次经历塑造了我未来的职业道路。

现在完成时:
I have had many positive experiences in my life. = 我生活中有很多积极的体验。
She has gained significant experience in this field. = 她在这个领域积累了丰富的经验。

过去完成时:
By the time I started this job, I had already gained five years of experience. = 我开始这份工作时，我已经积累了五年的经验。
He had never had such a challenging experience before. = 他以前从未有过如此具有挑战性的经历。

将来时:
I will gain more experience in the next few years. = 在接下来的几年里，我将获得更多经验。
This internship will provide valuable experience for my future career. = 这段实习将为我未来的职业生涯提供宝贵的经验。

虚拟语气:
If I had more experience, I would apply for the promotion. = 如果我有更多经验，我会申请晋升。
I wish I had more experience in project management. = 我希望我在项目管理方面有更多经验。

被动语态:
His experience was highly valued by the company. = 他的经验受到公司的极度重视。

疑问句:
