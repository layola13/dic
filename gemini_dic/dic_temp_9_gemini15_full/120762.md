misapply:/ˌmɪsæˈplaɪ/| vt.|误用，错用，滥用|To use something incorrectly or for the wrong purpose.  |例句：He misapplied the rules and caused a lot of confusion. 他误用了规则，导致了很多混乱。|例句：The company misapplied its resources, leading to financial losses.  公司滥用资源，导致了财务损失。|近义词：misuse, abuse, mishandle|反义词：apply correctly, utilize properly|动词

They misapply the principles of fairness. = 他们误用了公平原则。
