prefertile:/prɪˈfɜːrtaɪl/| adj.|  适于生育的，潜在肥沃的 |  Potentially fertile; capable of becoming fertile.  The term often refers to land or soil that has the potential to become fertile with proper management or improvement.  It suggests a state of being *almost* fertile, but not yet fully so. | Example: The prefertile land showed promise after irrigation.  灌溉后，这块潜在肥沃的土地展现出希望。  The prefertile soil needs more organic matter to support plant growth. 这块适于生育的土壤需要更多有机质来支持植物生长。| 近义词：potentially fertile, improvable, promising | 反义词：infertile, barren, unproductive |  形容词
