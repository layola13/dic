echinital: /ˌɛkɪˈnaɪtəl/ | adj. | 海胆的；似海胆的 | Relating to or resembling a sea urchin. The term often refers to structures or substances with a spiny or prickly appearance.  | 例句：The echinital structures on the fossil suggested a close relationship to sea urchins.  化石上的海胆状结构表明它与海胆有密切关系。


近义词：spiny, prickly, sea-urchin-like | 反义词：smooth, glabrous


词源：源自希腊语单词“echinos”（海胆）加上后缀“-ital”，表示“与……相关的”或“类似于……”。
