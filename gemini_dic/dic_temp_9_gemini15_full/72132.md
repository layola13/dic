floodgate:/ˈflʌdɡeɪt/| n.|水闸；闸门；泛滥之源；(情感等的)闸门|A floodgate is a gate used to control the flow of water, especially in a river or canal.  It can also refer to something that prevents or releases a large amount of something, such as emotions or information. |例句：The floodgates of the dam were opened to release the excess water.  水坝的闸门被打开以释放多余的水。

The opening of the floodgates unleashed a torrent of criticism.  闸门的打开引发了一连串的批评。|近义词：sluice gate, dam, barrier |反义词：lock, dam (when closed)

词源:

The word "floodgate" is a compound word. "Flood" comes from the Old English word *flod*, meaning "river, stream, flood."  "Gate" comes from the Old English word *geat*, meaning "gate, opening."  Therefore, "floodgate" literally means a gate that controls the flow of a flood.
