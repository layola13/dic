sink:/sɪŋk/| n., v.|水槽；下沉；使下沉 | A basin, typically made of porcelain, for washing dishes or hands;  to go down below the surface of water or another liquid; to become submerged. |例句：The kitchen sink is clogged. 厨房水槽堵住了。The ship began to sink. 船开始下沉。|近义词：basin, submerge, drown |反义词：float, rise, ascend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water sinks slowly.  水慢慢下沉。
He sinks the ship. 他使船下沉。


一般过去时:
The Titanic sank in 1912.  泰坦尼克号于1912年沉没。
She sank the basket into the water. 她把篮子沉入水中。


现在完成时:
The ship has sunk. 船已经沉没了。
They have sunk the treasure. 他们已经沉没了宝藏。


过去完成时:
The ship had sunk before help arrived.  在救援到达之前，船已经沉没了。
The diver had sunk the case before he resurfaced. 潜水员在浮出水面之前已经沉没了箱子。


将来时:
The boat will sink if it takes on too much water.  如果船进水太多，它就会沉没。
They will sink the old car tomorrow. 他们明天将把旧车沉入水中。


虚拟语气:
If the ship had not sunk, many lives would have been saved. 如果船没有沉没，许多生命就能得救。
I wish I hadn't sunk that money into the business. 我真希望我没有把那笔钱投入到这个生意中。


被动语态:
The ship was sunk by a torpedo.  这艘船被鱼雷击沉。
The treasure was sunk years ago. 宝藏几年前就被沉没了。


疑问句:
Will the boat sink? 这艘船会沉吗？
Does the sink drain properly? 水槽排水顺畅吗？


否定句:
The boat didn't sink. 船没有沉。
The stone did not sink.石头没有下沉。
