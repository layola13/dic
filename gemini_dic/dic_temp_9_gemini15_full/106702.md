kyanize:/ˈkaɪənɑɪz/| vt.|用氰化物处理；用铬酸盐处理|To treat (wood, etc.) with potassium dichromate to preserve it against decay.  This process was formerly used to protect wood from decay and insect damage.  The term is now largely obsolete.|The old fence posts were kyanized to extend their lifespan.|近义词：treat, preserve|反义词：decay, rot|动词
