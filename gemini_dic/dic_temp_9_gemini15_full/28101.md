capitalising: /ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 大写；利用；获利 | The act of writing or printing a letter or word in a capital letter.  To take advantage of a situation or opportunity. To convert something into capital. | Example:  He was capitalising on his opponent's mistake. 他正在利用对手的失误。 |近义词：utilizing, exploiting, profiting from | 反义词：neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is capitalising on the current market trends. 他正在利用当前的市场趋势。
They are capitalising every opportunity to expand their business. 他们正在利用每一个机会来扩张他们的业务。


一般过去时:
She capitalised her name in the document. 她在文件中将她的名字大写。
The company capitalised on the success of its new product.  这家公司利用其新产品的成功。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
