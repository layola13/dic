sealing:/ˈsiːlɪŋ/ | v. n. |密封；盖章；决定；批准 | to close something tightly so that air or liquid cannot enter or escape; to officially confirm or approve something. |例句：He carefully sealed the envelope. 他仔细地封好了信封。 |近义词：closing, fastening, securing | 反义词：opening, unsealing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seals the packages every day. = 他每天都封包。
They seal the documents before sending them. = 他们在寄送文件之前会密封它们。


一般过去时:
She sealed the letter with wax. = 她用蜡封了信。
The company sealed the deal last week. = 公司上周达成了协议。


现在完成时:
We have sealed all the cracks in the wall. = 我们已经封好了墙上的所有裂缝。
They have sealed their fate by their actions. = 他们因自己的行为而决定了自己的命运。


过去完成时:
He had sealed the envelope before he mailed it. = 他在寄信之前已经封好了信封。
The committee had sealed the decision before the meeting. = 委员会在会议之前就已经做出了决定。


将来时:
She will seal the container before shipping it. = 她会在发货前封好容器。
They will seal the agreement tomorrow. = 他们明天将签署协议。


虚拟语气:
If he sealed the deal, we would celebrate. = 如果他完成了交易，我们将庆祝。
I wish I had sealed the documents properly. = 我真希望我当时把文件妥善密封了。


被动语态:
The letter was sealed with a special adhesive. = 这封信是用特殊的粘合剂封好的。
The agreement will be sealed by the board of directors. = 该协议将由董事会批准。


疑问句:
Has the package been sealed yet? = 包裹封好了吗？
Will the documents be sealed before sending? = 文件会在寄送前密封吗？


否定句:
He didn't seal the container properly. = 他没有正确地封好容器。
The deal was not yet sealed. = 交易尚未敲定。


PHRASE->
sealing wax:封蜡
sealing tape:封箱胶带
sealing compound:密封剂
airtight sealing:气密密封
watertight sealing:防水密封

词源:
From Middle English selen, from Old English *selian, from Proto-Germanic *sēljōn (to seal).  The meaning evolved from simply closing something to include the idea of making it secure and airtight.
