preconcentrating:/ˌpriːˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtɪŋ/|/ˈpriːkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪŋ/| v. |预浓缩，预富集 |The process of increasing the concentration of a substance before further analysis or processing.  This is often done to improve the detection limits or sensitivity of an analytical method.  |例句：The sample was preconcentrating before gas chromatography analysis. 样品在气相色谱分析之前进行了预浓缩。
The researchers used a solid-phase microextraction technique for preconcentrating the volatile organic compounds. 研究人员使用固相微萃取技术对挥发性有机化合物进行预富集。

|近义词：concentrating, enriching, amplifying |反义词：diluting, dispersing |动词
