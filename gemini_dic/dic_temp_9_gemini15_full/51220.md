destocks:/dɪˈstɒks/| vt.|减仓，抛售库存|To sell off a large amount of stock, typically to reduce inventory or raise capital.  This often happens in response to market changes or financial pressures.|例句：The company destocked its inventory to improve cash flow. = 公司减仓以改善现金流。|例句：The retailer destocked its seasonal items after the holiday season. = 零售商在节假日过后抛售了季节性商品。|近义词：liquidate, sell off, reduce inventory|反义词：stock up, accumulate inventory|动词
