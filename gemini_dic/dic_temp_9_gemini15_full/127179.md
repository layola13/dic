naturalizers:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪzərz/ | n. |归化者，归化植物|People or plants that have been naturalized.  Naturalizers are individuals who have acquired citizenship in a new country, or plants that have adapted to a new environment and spread successfully without human intervention. |例句：Many immigrants become naturalizers after several years of residency. 许多移民在居住几年后成为归化公民。|例句：The dandelion is a successful naturalizer in many parts of the world.蒲公英在世界许多地方都是成功的归化植物。|近义词：immigrants (for people), invasives (for plants) |反义词：natives (for plants), citizens by birth (for people) |名词
