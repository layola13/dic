galvanist:/ˈɡæl.vənɪst/| n.|电流疗法医师，电流疗法使用者|A person who practices galvanism or uses galvanic currents, especially for medical purposes.  A practitioner of galvanism, a now-obsolete medical treatment involving the application of electric current.| Example:  The galvanist applied a mild current to the patient's injured limb. 电疗师将微弱的电流施加到病人的受伤肢体上。|近义词：electrotherapist|反义词：无|名词
