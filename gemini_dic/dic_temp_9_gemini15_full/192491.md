scrunch-drying: /skrʌnʃˈdraɪɪŋ/ | n. | 揉搓吹干 (头发) | The act of drying hair by scrunching it with your hands while using a hairdryer. This technique is often used to create volume and texture, particularly in curly or wavy hair.  | 例句：Scrunch-drying is a great way to enhance natural curls. = 揉搓吹干是一种增强自然卷发的绝佳方法。

She prefers scrunch-drying her hair to using a straightener. = 她更喜欢揉搓吹干头发而不是用直发器。| 近义词：air-drying (风干), diffusing (扩散吹干) | 反义词：straightening (拉直), blow-drying (直吹) |  词性:名词
