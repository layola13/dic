overperemptorily:/ˌoʊvərˈpɛrəmptərɪli/ | adv. | 过于专横地；极其武断地 |  In an excessively or arbitrarily decisive manner.  It implies a rejection of other options or opinions without sufficient consideration.  | The judge overperemptorily dismissed the case, ignoring crucial evidence.  法官过于专横地驳回了此案，无视了关键证据。


近义词: arbitrarily, autocratically, dictatorially, imperiously, dogmatically

反义词: considerately, diplomatically, patiently, thoughtfully, leniently

词性: 副词
