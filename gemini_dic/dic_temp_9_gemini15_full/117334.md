medevac:/ˈmɛdəvæk/| n. v.|医疗后送；医疗撤离|Medical evacuation; the process of evacuating someone who is injured or ill, typically by air.|The soldier required a medevac after being injured in the battle.  士兵在战斗中受伤后需要医疗后送。|The helicopter performed a medevac operation to rescue the climber. 直升机进行了医疗后送行动以营救攀登者。|evacuation, airlift|—|n.
