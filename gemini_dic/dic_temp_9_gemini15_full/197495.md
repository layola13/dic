sheikh:/ʃeɪk/| n. |酋长，谢赫|A male leader of an Arab tribe or family, often with significant religious or political authority.  The term is also used as a title of respect.|例句：The sheikh welcomed the delegation to his palace.  这位酋长欢迎代表团到他的宫殿。|例句：He is a respected sheikh in his community. 他是社区里一位受人尊敬的谢赫。|近义词：chieftain, emir, leader|反义词：subject, follower|名词
