re-fenced:/ˌriːˈfɛnst/| v.|重新围栏，重新圈地 |To enclose (an area of land) again with a fence.  This implies a previous fencing that was removed or damaged, and is now being replaced or repaired.|例句：The farmer re-fenced the pasture after the storm damaged the old fence.  农民在暴风雨损坏旧栅栏后重新围起了牧场。

The council decided to re-fence the park to improve security.  市议会决定重新围起公园以提高安全性。|近义词：refence, re-erect, enclose |反义词：remove fence, open up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-fence the fields every spring. = 他们每年春天都重新围栏田地。
The farmer re-fences his property regularly. = 这位农民定期为他的房产重新围栏。


一般过去时:
They re-fenced the garden after the dog escaped. = 狗逃走后，他们重新围起了花园。
The contractor re-fenced the entire property last week. = 承包商上周重新围起了整个房产。


现在完成时:
They have re-fenced the entire property. = 他们已经重新围起了整个房产。
The city has re-fenced the playground for safety. = 城市为了安全已经重新围起了操场。


过去完成时:
They had re-fenced the area before the cattle arrived. = 在牛群到达之前，他们已经重新围起了该区域。
The farmers had re-fenced the fields by the time the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，农民们已经重新围起了田地。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
