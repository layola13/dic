delimitation's:/ˌdiːlɪmɪˈteɪʃənz/| n.|界限的规定；划界；限定|The act of setting the limits or boundaries of something.  It refers to the process of defining and marking the boundaries of something, whether it's a geographical area, a concept, or a task. |例句：The delimitation of the new national park is still under discussion. 新国家公园的划界仍在讨论中。
The delimitation of responsibilities between departments is crucial for efficient workflow. 部门之间责任的界定对于高效的工作流程至关重要。|近义词： demarcation, definition, limitation, specification |反义词：unification, integration
