feigningly: /ˈfeɪnɪŋli/ | adv. | 假装地，虚伪地 |  In a way that is pretending; insincerely.  | 例句：He spoke feigningly of his love for her, but his actions told a different story. 他假装深爱着她，但他的行为却表明了另一番景象。

He answered feigningly, avoiding direct eye contact. 他假装回答，避免直接的眼神接触。


近义词：pretendly, affectedly, artificially, deceptively | 反义词：genuinely, sincerely, truthfully | 词性：副词


USAGE->  (Feigningly being an adverb, it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  The tense is determined by the verb it modifies.)

例句1 (Present Simple): He feigningly agreed to help, but never did. 他假装同意帮忙，但从未这样做。
例句2 (Past Simple): She feigningly smiled, hiding her true feelings. 她假装微笑，掩盖了她的真实感受。
例句3 (Future Simple): He will feigningly apologize, hoping to avoid punishment. 他会假装道歉，希望避免惩罚。
例句4 (Present Perfect):  He has feigningly played the victim for years. 他多年来一直假装自己是受害者。
例句5 (Past Perfect): She had feigningly expressed interest in his work before the meeting. 会议前，她假装对他的工作表示了兴趣。
例句6 (Future Perfect): By next week, he will have feigningly completed the project. 到下周，他将假装完成了这个项目。

例句7 (Present Continuous): He is feigningly collaborating on the report. 他正在假装合作撰写报告。
例句8 (Past Continuous): She was feigningly listening to the lecture. 她当时假装在听讲座。


PHRASE->
There aren't common set phrases directly using "feigningly".  The word itself functions as a modifier to verbs describing actions performed deceptively.  Examples:

feigningly polite: 假装有礼貌的
feigningly cheerful: 假装开朗的
feigningly unconcerned: 假装漠不关心的
feigningly innocent: 假装无辜的
feigningly humble: 假装谦逊的


词源:

From feign + -ingly.  "Feign" comes from the Old French *feindre, from Latin fingere "to fashion, shape, invent, feign."  The suffix "-ingly" forms adverbs.
