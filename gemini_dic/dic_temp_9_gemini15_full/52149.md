diaphanoscope:/daɪˈæfənəskoʊp/| n. |透光镜；透视镜|An instrument for examining translucent objects by transmitted light, such as skin or other tissues.  It is essentially a simple form of transilluminator.  |The doctor used a diaphanoscope to examine the patient's skin for any abnormalities.|近义词：transilluminator|反义词：opaque instrument
