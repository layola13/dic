idiopathic:/ˌɪdiːoʊˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 特发性的；原因不明的 |  Of unknown cause; spontaneous.  The term is used primarily in medicine to describe diseases or conditions that arise without any discernible cause. | Example:  The doctor diagnosed her pain as idiopathic, meaning the cause is unknown.  医生诊断她的疼痛为特发性疼痛，这意味着病因不明。 | 近义词: spontaneous, unexplained, cryptogenic | 反义词: symptomatic, caused, known


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The condition is idiopathic. = 这种情况是特发性的。
His rash is idiopathic. = 他的皮疹是特发性的。


一般过去时:
The doctor said the illness was idiopathic. = 医生说这种疾病是特发性的。
Her fatigue was diagnosed as idiopathic. = 她的疲劳被诊断为特发性的。


现在完成时:
Researchers have found no cause for the idiopathic disease. = 研究人员没有找到这种特发性疾病的原因。
Scientists have determined this condition to be idiopathic. = 科学家已经确定这种情况是特发性的。


过去完成时:
Before the diagnosis, it had been assumed the disease was idiopathic. = 在诊断之前，人们曾认为这种疾病是特发性的。
They had initially believed the symptoms were idiopathic. = 他们最初认为这些症状是特发性的。


将来时:
Future research will hopefully explain the idiopathic disorder. = 未来的研究有望解释这种特发性疾病。
We will continue to study this idiopathic condition. = 我们将继续研究这种特发性疾病。


虚拟语气:
If the condition were idiopathic, further investigation would be necessary. = 如果这种情况是特发性的，则需要进一步调查。
I wish we knew the cause of this idiopathic pain. = 我希望我们知道这种特发性疼痛的原因。


被动语态:
The disease was classified as idiopathic. = 这种疾病被归类为特发性的。
The origin of the problem is believed to be idiopathic. = 这个问题的起源被认为是特发性的。


疑问句:
Is the patient's condition idiopathic? = 病人的病情是特发性的吗？
Could this unexplained symptom be idiopathic? = 这种无法解释的症状可能是特发性的吗？


否定句:
The disease is not idiopathic; a cause has been identified. = 这种疾病不是特发性的；已经找到了原因。
The symptoms are not idiopathic; they are caused by a viral infection. = 这些症状不是特发性的；它们是由病毒感染引起的。
