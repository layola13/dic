respicing:/rɪˈspaɪsɪŋ/| n. |再次调味；重新加香料|The act of adding spices again to food, often to enhance or adjust the flavor after initial seasoning.  Respicing is a common practice in cooking, particularly when a dish needs a flavor boost or a correction in seasoning.|例句：The chef did some respicing before serving the curry. = 大厨在端上咖喱之前又加了一些香料。|例句：After tasting the stew, she decided it needed respicing. = 尝过炖菜后，她决定需要重新加香料。|近义词：reseasoning, re-flavoring|反义词：under-seasoning, blanding|名词
