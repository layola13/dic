platforms:/ˈplætˌfɔrmz/| n.|平台；讲台；站台|A raised level surface, often used for a specific purpose.  It can refer to a physical structure like a train platform or a conceptual structure like a software platform.|例句：The train arrived at the platform. 火车到达站台了。

例句：We built a new platform for our online store. 我们为我们的在线商店建立了一个新的平台。

近义词：stage, base, foundation, system

反义词：N/A

词性：名词
