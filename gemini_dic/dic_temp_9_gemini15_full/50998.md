desirousness:/dɪˈzaɪrəsnəs/| n. |渴望；热望；欲望|The state or quality of being desirous; a strong feeling of wanting something.  It often implies a longing or yearning for something considered desirable or pleasant.|例句：Her desirousness for success drove her to work tirelessly.  她的成功渴望驱使她不知疲倦地工作。

例句：The desirousness of the children for candy was evident in their excited chatter. 孩子们对糖果的渴望从他们兴奋的谈话中显而易见。


近义词：longing, yearning, craving, eagerness, avidity|反义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern|词性:名词
