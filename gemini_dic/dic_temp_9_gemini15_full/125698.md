muneration:/ˌmjuːnəˈreɪʃən/| n. |酬金；薪酬；报酬 |The act of paying someone, or the amount paid;  remuneration.  It often refers to payment for professional services or work done. |例句：His remuneration was significantly less than he expected. 他的报酬远低于他的预期。

例句：The company offers competitive remuneration packages to attract top talent.  这家公司提供具有竞争力的薪酬方案以吸引顶尖人才。


近义词：compensation, payment, salary, wages, recompense

反义词：None directly opposite, but words suggesting lack of payment like "underpayment" or "poverty" could be considered contextual opposites.

词性：名词
