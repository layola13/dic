marginally:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəli/| adv. |轻微地；略微地；勉强地 |Slightly; to a small degree; barely.  The word implies a small or insignificant change or difference. |例句：The product's sales increased marginally last quarter.  上个季度产品销量略有增长。

例句：He marginally passed the exam. 他勉强通过了考试。


近义词：slightly, somewhat, barely, minimally, insignificantly

反义词：significantly, substantially, considerably, greatly


词源：源自于名词margin，意为“边缘，界限”。 "-ally" 是一个副词后缀，表示“以…的方式”。 因此，marginally 的字面意思是“在边缘地，在界限地”，引申为“轻微地”。


USAGE->

The price marginally increases each year. = 价格每年略微上涨。
She marginally improves her performance. = 她略微提高了她的表现。


一般过去时:

将来时:
