modernisation: /ˌmɒdərnʌɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌmɑːdərnʌɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 现代化 | The process of making something modern; the act of modernizing.  The process involves updating, improving, and adapting something to contemporary standards, technologies, or ideas. |


例句：

The modernisation of the factory increased productivity. = 工厂的现代化提高了生产力。

The city is undergoing a period of rapid modernisation. = 这个城市正在经历一个快速现代化的时期。


近义词：modernization, updating, improvement, renovation


反义词：antiquation, obsolescence


词性：名词
