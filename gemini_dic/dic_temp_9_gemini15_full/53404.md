disambiguate:/dɪsæmˈbɪɡjuˌeɪt/| vt.|消除歧义；明确；解释|To remove ambiguity from something, making its meaning clear and precise.  It often involves clarifying the meaning of a word, phrase, or concept that has multiple possible interpretations. |例句：The professor's explanation helped to disambiguate the complex terminology. 教授的解释有助于消除复杂的术语歧义。

例句：The context of the sentence disambiguated the meaning of the ambiguous word.  句子的语境消除了歧义词的含义。


近义词：clarify, elucidate, explain, resolve, specify

反义词：confuse, obscure, obfuscate

词性：动词
