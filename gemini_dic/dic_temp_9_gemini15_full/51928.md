diabete:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/| n.|糖尿病|A chronic metabolic disorder characterized by elevated levels of glucose in the blood.  This is due to either the pancreas not producing enough insulin, or the body's cells not responding properly to the insulin that is produced.|例句：He was diagnosed with type 2 diabetes. 他被诊断出患有2型糖尿病。|例句：She manages her diabetes through diet and exercise. 她通过饮食和运动来控制她的糖尿病。|近义词：hyperglycemia, glycosuria|反义词：N/A|名词
