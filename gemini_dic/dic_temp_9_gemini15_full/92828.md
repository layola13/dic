iconologists:/ˌaɪkənˈɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |图像学家；圣像学者| Scholars who study icons and their cultural significance. They analyze the symbolism, history, and artistic techniques associated with religious or secular images.  |The icono­logists debated the meaning of the Byzantine mosaic. 图像学家们就拜占庭马赛克的含义进行了辩论。| art historians, semioticians |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
