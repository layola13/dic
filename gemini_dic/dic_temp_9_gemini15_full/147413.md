overnormalizing: /ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度标准化；过度规范化 |  The act of excessively applying normalization techniques, leading to the loss of important information or nuance.  It often refers to the oversimplification or flattening of complex data or processes in an attempt to make them conform to a standard. This can result in inaccurate or incomplete representations. | 例句：Overnormalizing the data resulted in a loss of crucial variations in the patient population.  过度规范化数据导致患者群体中关键差异的丢失。


例句：The overnormalization of the database led to inaccurate predictions.  数据库的过度标准化导致了不准确的预测。


近义词：oversimplification, overgeneralization, standardization (in a negative context)


反义词：individualization, diversification, nuance


词性：动词 (gerund form, present participle)
