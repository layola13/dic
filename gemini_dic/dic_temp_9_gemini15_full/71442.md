flambeing:/flæmˈbeɪɪŋ/| n. v.| flambé（法式菜肴烹调法）,  flambéing（用烈酒点燃的烹调方法）|The act of flambéing involves adding liquor to a dish and igniting it, often for a dramatic effect.  Flambéing is a cooking technique in which alcohol is added to a dish and then ignited.  The alcohol burns off, leaving behind its flavor and aroma. |例句：The chef expertly performed the flambéing, setting the crêpes Suzette alight with a flourish.  厨师熟练地进行了 flambé，用华丽的动作点燃了可丽饼苏塞特。

The flambéing of the bananas added a delightful sweetness to the dessert.  香蕉的 flambé 为甜点增添了令人愉悦的甜味。|近义词：igniting, flaming|反义词：quenching, extinguishing|动词
