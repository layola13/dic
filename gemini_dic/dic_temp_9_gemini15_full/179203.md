rediscovery:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌvəri/| n. |重新发现；再次发现 |The rediscovery of the lost city was a major archaeological event.  重新发现失落的城市是一件重大的考古事件。|近义词：reinvention, recovery, revival |反义词：loss, oblivion


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The scientist is making a rediscovery of ancient techniques. = 科学家正在重新发现古代技术。
We rediscover the joy of learning everyday. = 我们每天都在重新发现学习的乐趣。


一般过去时:
They rediscovered the old manuscript in the attic. = 他们在阁楼上重新发现了那份旧手稿。
Historians rediscovered a lost painting by a famous artist. = 历史学家们重新发现了一幅著名艺术家的失传画作。


现在完成时:
Archaeologists have rediscovered a lost civilization in the Amazon. = 考古学家们已经在亚马逊重新发现了一个失落的文明。
She has rediscovered her passion for painting after many years. = 多年之后，她重新燃起了对绘画的热情。


过去完成时:
By the time the expedition ended, they had rediscovered several ancient ruins. = 探险队结束时，他们已经重新发现了几个古代遗址。
Before the renovation, they had rediscovered the original fireplace hidden behind the wall. = 在翻新之前，他们重新发现了隐藏在墙后的原有壁炉。


将来时:
Researchers believe they will rediscover many more lost species in the future. = 研究人员相信他们将来会重新发现更多失落的物种。
We hope to rediscover the beauty of our natural world. = 我们希望重新发现我们自然世界的美丽。


虚拟语气:
If they had better equipment, they might have rediscovered the artifact sooner. = 如果他们有更好的设备，他们可能会更早地重新发现文物。
I wish I could rediscover the feeling of childlike wonder. = 我希望我能重新找回孩童般的好奇心。


被动语态:
The lost city was rediscovered by a team of explorers. = 这个失落的城市被一支探险队重新发现。
Many old traditions are being rediscovered by younger generations. = 许多古老的传统正在被年轻一代重新发现。


疑问句:
When was the lost painting rediscovered? = 失传的画作是什么时候被重新发现的？
Have they rediscovered the missing documents yet? = 他们是否已经重新发现了丢失的文件？


否定句:
They did not rediscover the treasure despite their efforts. = 尽管他们努力了，但他们并没有重新发现宝藏。
The lost manuscript was never rediscovered. = 那份丢失的手稿从未被重新发现。
