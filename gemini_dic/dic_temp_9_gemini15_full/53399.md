disallowances:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊənsɪz/| n. |不允许；拒绝；扣除额；(尤指)费用扣除|The disallowances in the account led to a lower final balance. 账户中的扣除额导致最终余额较低。|近义词：rejections, deductions, prohibitions|反义词：allowances, approvals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disallows smoking in the office. = 公司不允许在办公室吸烟。
He disallows any form of dishonesty. = 他不允许任何形式的不诚实行为。


一般过去时:
The judge disallowed the evidence. = 法官驳回了证据。
They disallowed his application for a visa. = 他们拒绝了他的签证申请。


现在完成时:
The bank has disallowed several fraudulent transactions. = 银行已经拒绝了几笔欺诈性交易。
The committee has disallowed the proposed amendment. = 委员会否决了提议的修正案。


过去完成时:
They had disallowed the claim before he even submitted the paperwork. = 在他提交文件之前，他们就已经驳回了索赔。
The referee had disallowed the goal due to offside. = 裁判因为越位而取消了进球。


将来时:
The board will disallow any attempt to manipulate the results. = 董事会将不允许任何操纵结果的企图。
They will disallow the use of personal devices during the exam. = 考试期间他们将不允许使用个人电子设备。


虚拟语气:
If the court disallowed the appeal, they would have to accept the verdict. = 如果法院驳回上诉，他们就必须接受判决。
I wish they disallowed loud music after midnight. = 我希望他们在午夜后禁止大声播放音乐。


被动语态:
His application was disallowed due to insufficient documentation. = 他的申请由于文件不足而被驳回。
The extra expenses were disallowed by the auditor. = 审计员否决了额外支出。


疑问句:
Will they disallow the evidence presented by the defense? = 他们会驳回辩方提出的证据吗？
Were his claims disallowed because of a lack of proof? = 他的索赔是因为缺乏证据而被驳回的吗？


否定句:
The regulations don't disallow the use of mobile phones in this area. = 规定没有禁止在这个区域使用手机。
The committee didn't disallow the proposal; they approved it. = 委员会没有否决该提案；他们批准了它。


PHRASE->
disallow a claim = 驳回索赔
disallow an appeal = 驳回上诉
disallow evidence = 驳回证据
disallow a goal = 取消进球
disallow a transaction = 拒绝交易

词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + allow (允许) + -ance (名词后缀)  表示“不允许”，“拒绝”或“扣除”。
