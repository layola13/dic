compartmentalised: /kəmˈpɑːtmɛntaɪzd/ | adj. | 分隔的；划分区域的；（思想、情感等）隔绝的 | Divided into separate compartments; isolated or separated from other things.  | The office was highly compartmentalised, with each department working independently.  办公室被严格地划分区域，每个部门独立运作。| separated, isolated, segregated | integrated, unified, interconnected | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company compartmentalises its operations geographically. = 这家公司按地域划分其业务。
His brain compartmentalises information effectively. = 他的大脑有效地对信息进行分类。

一般过去时:
They compartmentalised the project into manageable tasks. = 他们将项目分解成易于管理的任务。
The government compartmentalised its intelligence operations. = 政府将其情报行动分隔开来。

现在完成时:
She has compartmentalised her emotions since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，她一直将自己的情绪隔离开来。
The team has compartmentalised its efforts to improve efficiency. = 团队已将工作分隔开来以提高效率。

过去完成时:
He had compartmentalised his personal and professional lives for years. = 他多年来一直将个人生活和职业生活分隔开来。
They had compartmentalised the data before analysis. = 他们在分析之前已经将数据分隔开来。


将来时:
We will compartmentalise the data for easier access. = 我们将对数据进行分隔，以便更容易访问。
The company will compartmentalise its marketing strategy. = 公司将把其营销策略分隔开来。

虚拟语气:
If we compartmentalised the problem, it would be easier to solve. = 如果我们把问题分开，就会更容易解决。
I wish I had compartmentalised my feelings better. = 我真希望我当时能更好地控制自己的情绪。

被动语态:
The project was compartmentalised into different phases. = 该项目被分成不同的阶段。
The information was compartmentalised to protect sensitive data. = 信息被分隔开来以保护敏感数据。

疑问句:

否定句:
