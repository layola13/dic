otorrhea:/ˌoʊtəˈriːə/| n. |耳流脓，耳漏 | A discharge of pus or other fluid from the ear.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including infection, inflammation, or injury to the ear.  | Example: The doctor diagnosed the patient with otorrhea after examining his ear. 医生检查了他的耳朵后，诊断他患有耳流脓。|近义词：ear discharge, aural discharge |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered the opposite of a healthy, dry ear. |名词
