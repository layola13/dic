debarred:/dɪˈbɑːrd/ | vt. | 禁止；阻止；取消资格 | To officially prevent someone from doing something, or prevent something from happening.  |例句：He was debarred from practicing law after being found guilty of fraud. 他因被判犯有欺诈罪而被禁止执业。
例句：The company was debarred from bidding on government contracts. 这家公司被禁止参与政府合同的投标。|近义词：exclude, prohibit, ban, disqualify |反义词：allow, permit, enable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They debar him from entering the building. = 他们禁止他进入大楼。
The club debars members who violate the rules. =  俱乐部禁止违反规定的会员。


一般过去时:
The judge debarred the lawyer from the case. = 法官将律师排除在案件之外。
She was debarred from driving for six months. = 她被禁止驾车六个月。


现在完成时:
He has been debarred from participating in the competition. = 他已经被禁止参加比赛。
The university has debarred several students for cheating. = 大学因作弊而禁止了几名学生。


过去完成时:
They had already debarred him before the evidence emerged. =  证据出现之前，他们就已经禁止他了。
The committee had debarred her from voting prior to the scandal. = 丑闻发生之前，委员会已经禁止她投票。


将来时:
The court will debar him from holding public office. = 法院将禁止他担任公职。
They will likely debar her from future competitions. = 他们很可能会禁止她参加未来的比赛。


虚拟语气:
If he had not cheated, he would not have been debarred. = 如果他没有作弊，就不会被禁止。
I wish I hadn't been debarred from the competition. = 我真希望我没有被禁止参加比赛。


被动语态:
He was debarred from the meeting. = 他被禁止参加会议。
She was debarred from entering the country. = 她被禁止入境。


疑问句:
Was he debarred from the race? = 他被禁止参加比赛了吗？
Will she be debarred from voting? = 她会被禁止投票吗？


否定句:
They did not debar him from the project. = 他们没有禁止他参与项目。
She was not debarred from attending the ceremony. = 她没有被禁止参加仪式。


PHRASE->
be debarred from = 被禁止...
debar someone from = 禁止某人...
debar oneself from =  (主动)避免...
debarred list = 禁止名单
debarred from participation = 禁止参与
