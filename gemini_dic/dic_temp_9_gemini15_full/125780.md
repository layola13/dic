murex:/ˈmjʊreks/| n.|骨螺，穆雷克斯螺|A murex is a sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Muricidae.  They are known for their elaborate, spiny shells.  Many species produce a purple dye, once highly valued.|例句：The ancient Phoenicians used dye extracted from murex snails to create their famous Tyrian purple.  古代腓尼基人使用从骨螺中提取的染料来创造他们著名的提尔紫。

例句：The intricate spiny shell of the murex is a collector's item. 骨螺复杂多刺的贝壳是收藏家的珍品。|近义词：sea snail, shellfish|反义词：无
