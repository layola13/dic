preaccorded:/ˌpriːəˈkɔːrdɪd/ | adj. | 预先同意的；预先商定的 |  Pre-accorded refers to something that has been agreed upon or arranged beforehand. It implies a prior agreement or understanding.  | Example: The preaccorded terms of the contract were accepted by both parties.  预先商定的合同条款被双方接受。 |近义词：prearranged, pre-determined, agreed-upon | 反义词：unplanned, unexpected, impromptu |  词性：形容词
