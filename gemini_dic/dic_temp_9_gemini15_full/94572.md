impoverish:/ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃ/| vt.|使贫穷；使枯竭；使恶化 |To make (someone or something) poor or less wealthy. To deplete or exhaust the strength or richness of. |例句：The war impoverished the country.  战争使这个国家贫穷。
The drought impoverished the soil. 干旱使土壤贫瘠。|近义词：impecunious, bankrupt, deplete |反义词：enrich, enhance, improve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory's closure impoverishes the local community. = 工厂倒闭使当地社区贫困化。
This policy impoverishes the quality of education. = 这一政策降低了教育质量。


一般过去时:
The famine impoverished the entire region. = 饥荒使整个地区贫困化。
Years of neglect impoverished the once-fertile land. = 多年的忽视使曾经肥沃的土地贫瘠。


现在完成时:
Years of drought have impoverished the farmland. = 多年的干旱使农田贫瘠。
The continuous exploitation has impoverished the natural resources. =  持续的开采已经耗尽了自然资源。


过去完成时:
The mismanagement had impoverished the company before the merger. = 经营不善在合并之前已经使公司陷入困境。
Decades of war had impoverished the nation's infrastructure. = 数十年的战争使国家的基础设施破败不堪。


将来时:
Without investment, this policy will impoverish the future generations. = 如果没有投资，这项政策将会使子孙后代贫困。
Continued pollution will impoverish the water quality of the river. =  持续的污染将会恶化河流的水质。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't intervened, the crisis would have impoverished millions. = 如果政府没有干预，这场危机将使数百万人陷入贫困。
If we continue this practice, it will impoverish the soil and reduce yields. = 如果我们继续这种做法，它将会使土壤贫瘠并减少产量。


被动语态:
The people were impoverished by the economic crisis. = 人民被经济危机弄得贫困潦倒。
The land was impoverished by years of over-farming. = 这片土地因多年的过度耕作而贫瘠。


疑问句:
Has the ongoing conflict impoverished the local population? =  持续的冲突是否使当地居民贫困？
Will this new law impoverish small businesses? = 这项新法律会使小型企业陷入困境吗？


否定句:
The government's efforts prevented the crisis from impoverishing the nation. = 政府的努力阻止了这场危机使国家陷入贫困。
Sustainable farming practices help prevent impoverishing the soil. = 可持续的农业实践有助于防止土壤贫瘠。
