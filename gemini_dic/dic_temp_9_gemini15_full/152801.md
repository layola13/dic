pectinibranch:/ˈpektɪnɪˌbræŋtʃ/| n.| 栉鳃类动物 |A pectinibranch is a type of gastropod mollusk characterized by a comb-like gill.  They are a diverse group with various shell shapes and lifestyles.  They are primarily marine organisms.| Example: Many species of pectinibranchs inhabit the intertidal zone.  许多栉鳃类动物栖息在潮间带。|近义词:  gastropod (more general term)|反义词: N/A
