humour: /ˈhjuːmər/ /ˈhjuːmər/ | n. |幽默；诙谐；滑稽；心情；奇想|

英文解析：The quality of being amusing or comic, especially as expressed in literature or speech.  It can also refer to a person's state of mind or disposition.

例句：

His speech was full of humour. = 他的演讲充满了幽默。

She has a dry sense of humour. = 她有一种冷幽默。


近义词：wit, comedy, funniness, amusement, mirth

反义词：seriousness, gravity, solemnity
