libament:/ˈlɪɡəmənt/| n. |韧带|A tough, fibrous band of connective tissue that unites bones or cartilages, serving to support and strengthen joints.  Ligaments are crucial for maintaining joint stability and preventing excessive movement.|Example: The ankle injury involved a severely sprained ligament.  踝关节受伤涉及严重扭伤的韧带。|近义词：tendon (though tendons connect muscle to bone)|反义词：无|名词
