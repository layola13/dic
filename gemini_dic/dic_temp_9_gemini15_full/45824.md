customer-facing: /ˈkʌstəmərˌfeɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 面向客户的，与客户直接接触的 | Directly interacting with or serving customers.  This adjective describes roles, tasks, or processes that involve direct contact and interaction with customers. | 例句：The company is investing heavily in improving its customer-facing technology. 这家公司正在大力投资改进其面向客户的技术。

例句：Many customer-facing roles require excellent communication skills. 许多面向客户的角色都需要优秀的沟通技巧。


近义词：client-facing, customer-oriented, customer-centric

反义词：internal, back-office, administrative


词性：形容词
