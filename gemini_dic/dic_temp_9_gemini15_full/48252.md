decoration:/ˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən/| n.|装饰，装修|The act of decorating or the state of being decorated; something that decorates.|例句：The Christmas decorations were beautiful. 圣诞装饰很漂亮。

例句：The room's decoration is quite modern.  房间的装饰很现代化。


近义词：adornment, ornamentation, embellishment|反义词：plainness, simplicity|词性：名词


词源:  From Old French *decoracion, from Latin decoratio, from decorare "to decorate," from decōrus "becoming, proper, graceful."
