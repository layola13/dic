funner:/ˈfʌnər/| adj.|更有趣的，比较级|More fun; a comparative form of "fun".  It is considered non-standard English.|例句：This game is funner than the last one.  这个游戏比上一个更有趣。|例句：That party was way funner than I expected.  那个派对比我想象的有趣得多。|近义词：more enjoyable, more entertaining|反义词：less enjoyable, boring|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This ride is funner than I thought. = 这个游乐设施比我想象的更有趣。
That movie is funner than the book. = 那部电影比那本书更有趣。


一般过去时:
The party was funner than last year's. = 今年的派对比去年的更有趣。
That concert was funner than I anticipated. = 那场音乐会比我预期的更有趣。


现在完成时:
This trip has been funner than any I’ve taken before. = 这趟旅行比我之前去的任何一次都更有趣。
Everything we've done today has been funner than I expected. = 我们今天所做的一切都比我预期的更有趣。


过去完成时:
The previous game had been funner than this one. = 上一场游戏比这场更有趣。
I had thought the earlier movie was funner. = 我曾认为之前的电影更有趣。


将来时:
The next vacation will be funner than this one. = 下一次假期会比这次更有趣。
I think the upcoming festival will be funner than last year's. = 我认为即将到来的节日会比去年的更有趣。


虚拟语气:
If it were funner, I would go. = 如果它更有趣，我会去。
If only the movie had been funner. = 但愿电影更有趣一点就好了。


被动语态:
(This adjective doesn't typically use passive voice.  The passive voice would apply to the noun the adjective describes, not the adjective itself.)  The game was considered funner than the previous one.  这场游戏被认为比上一场更有趣。


疑问句:
Isn't this funner than that? = 这不是比那更有趣吗？
Is this activity funner than the last one? = 这个活动比上一个更有趣吗？


否定句:
The second movie wasn't funner than the first. = 第二部电影不如第一部有趣。
This game isn't funner than I expected. = 这个游戏没有我想象的那么有趣。


PHRASE->
(The word "funner" itself isn't used in common phrases.  Phrases would use "fun" or "more fun.")
It's more fun to... = 做...更有趣
It's always more fun with...= 和...一起总是更有趣
Having more fun than...= 比...更有趣
What's more fun is...= 更有趣的是...
More fun than a barrel of monkeys = 比猴子桶还有趣（极其有趣）
