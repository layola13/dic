catch-cold:/kætʃ ˈkoʊld/| v.|感冒，患感冒 |To become ill with a cold.  This is an idiomatic expression, meaning to contract a common cold.|例句：I caught a cold last week. 我上周感冒了。
I think I'm catching a cold. 我觉得我快感冒了。|近义词：get a cold, come down with a cold |反义词：be healthy, be well

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He catches a cold every winter. = 他每年冬天都感冒。
She catches colds easily. = 她很容易感冒。

一般过去时:
I caught a bad cold last month. = 我上个月得了重感冒。
We caught colds after the concert. = 音乐会后我们都感冒了。


现在完成时:
I have caught a cold twice this year. = 今年我已经感冒两次了。
She has not caught a cold all winter. = 她整个冬天都没感冒。


过去完成时:
He had caught a cold before the trip. = 他在旅行前就已经感冒了。
They had caught colds several times that year. = 那一年他们已经感冒好几次了。


将来时:
I think I will catch a cold if I go out without a jacket. = 我想如果我不穿外套出去就会感冒。
If it rains, we might catch a cold. = 如果下雨，我们可能会感冒。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't gone out in the rain, I wouldn't have caught a cold. = 如果我没有在雨中出去，我就不会感冒了。
I wish I hadn't caught a cold.  = 我真希望我没有感冒。


被动语态: (This verb phrase is rarely used in the passive voice)
*Not applicable in a natural way.  The passive voice doesn't work well with this idiom.*


疑问句:
Did you catch a cold? = 你感冒了吗？
Have you caught a cold recently? = 你最近感冒了吗？


否定句:
I didn't catch a cold despite the cold weather. = 尽管天气寒冷，我还是没有感冒。
She didn't catch a cold this time. = 她这次没有感冒。


PHRASE->
