scripture:/ˈskrɪptʃər/ | n. |圣经；经典著作；经文 |The word "scripture" refers to sacred writings, especially those considered to be divinely inspired and authoritative within a particular religion.  It can also refer to any writings considered to be of great importance or literary merit. | Example:  The Bible is considered scripture by Christians.  圣经被基督徒视为圣经。

The ancient Greek philosophers' writings are considered scripture by some.  一些人认为古希腊哲学家的著作是经典著作。


近义词：sacred writings, holy writ, canonical texts, divine revelation | 反义词：heresy, apocrypha
