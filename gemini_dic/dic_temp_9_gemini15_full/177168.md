rearguing:/ˌriːˈɑːrɡjuːɪŋ/| v.| 再次争论；重新辩论 | To argue again; to discuss something again, often with the intention of changing a previous decision or conclusion. |例句：They spent hours rearguing the terms of the contract. 他们花了几个小时重新商讨合同条款。 They are rearguing the case in the appeals court. 他们在上诉法院重新辩论此案。|近义词：redebating, reexamining, reconsidering |反义词：agreeing, accepting, conceding |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rearguing the details of the agreement. = 他们正在再次争论协议的细节。
We are rearguing the case in the supreme court. = 我们在上诉法院重新辩论此案。


一般过去时:
They reargued the points raised in the meeting. = 他们再次争论了会议上提出的论点。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
