shmooze:/ʃmuːz/ | v. |闲聊；奉承；套近乎 | To chat in a friendly, informal, and often ingratiating way, especially to gain favor or advantage.  |例句：He spent the afternoon shmoozing with potential clients. 他下午花时间和潜在客户闲聊。|例句：She tried to shmooze her way into a better seat. 她试图通过奉承得到一个更好的座位。|近义词：chat, converse, schmooze, network,  cajole |反义词：ignore, snub, avoid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shmoozes with his colleagues every day. = 他每天都和同事们闲聊。
They shmooze with important people to get ahead. = 他们和重要人物套近乎以求晋升。

一般过去时:
She shmoozed the manager to get a raise. = 她通过奉承经理得到了加薪。
We shmoozed for hours at the party. = 我们在派对上闲聊了好几个小时。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
