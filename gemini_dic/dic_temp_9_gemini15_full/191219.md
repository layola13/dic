scarphing: /ˈskɑːrfɪŋ/ | v. |吞咽；狼吞虎咽|The act of swallowing something quickly and greedily.  It often implies a lack of elegance or refinement in eating. |例句：He was scarphing down his food, barely chewing. 他狼吞虎咽地吃着食物，几乎没有咀嚼。|例句：She scarfed her sandwich down before the meeting started.  她在会议开始前迅速吃掉了三明治。|近义词：gobbling, devouring, wolfing, gulping |反义词：savoring, relishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scarfs down his breakfast every morning. = 他每天早上都狼吞虎咽地吃早餐。
They scarf their lunch at their desks. = 他们在办公桌前匆匆吃午餐。

一般过去时:
She scarfed the pizza down in minutes. = 她几分钟内就狼吞虎咽地吃完了披萨。
The children scarfed their ice cream before it melted. = 孩子们在冰淇淋融化前迅速吃掉了它。

现在完成时:
He has scarfed down three burgers already. = 他已经狼吞虎咽地吃了三个汉堡包。
She has scarfed her way through the entire cake. = 她已经把整个蛋糕都狼吞虎咽地吃掉了。

过去完成时:
He had scarfed down his dinner before the movie started. = 电影开始前他已经狼吞虎咽地吃完了晚餐。
They had scarfed all the cookies before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经把所有饼干都吃光了。

将来时:
I will scarf down this sandwich before the train leaves. = 火车离开前我会迅速吃掉这个三明治。
She will scarf down her lunch quickly to get back to work. = 她会迅速吃完午餐然后回去工作。

虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would scarf down that entire pie. = 如果我饿了，我会把整个馅饼都狼吞虎咽地吃掉。
I wish I hadn't scarfed down that spicy curry; my stomach hurts! = 我真希望我没有狼吞虎咽地吃那个辣咖喱，我的肚子痛！

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The food was scarfed down quickly by the hungry guests. = 饥饿的客人迅速吃掉了食物。(不太自然)
The cake was scarfed down before anyone could get a piece. = 蛋糕在任何人能拿到一块之前就被吃光了。(不太自然)

疑问句:
Did he scarf down the entire plate of pasta? = 他把整盘意大利面都狼吞虎咽地吃掉了？
Will they scarf down all the food before we get there? = 我们到那里之前他们会把所有的食物都吃光吗？

否定句:
He didn't scarf down his food; he ate it slowly and carefully. = 他没有狼吞虎咽地吃东西；他吃得很慢很仔细。
She didn't scarf her meal; she savored each bite. = 她没有狼吞虎咽地吃饭；她细细品味每一口。
