nontemporal:/ˌnɒnˈtɛmpərəl/ | adj. |非时间的；与时间无关的 |Not relating to or involving time; existing outside of or independent of time.  The concept is often used in philosophy and physics to describe things that are not subject to the constraints of time. |Example:  Many physicists believe that certain fundamental laws of nature are nontemporal.  许多物理学家认为某些自然的基本规律是非时间的。


The concept of a nontemporal being is explored in many theological discussions.  在许多神学讨论中探讨了非时间存在的概念。


近义词: timeless, atemporal, eternal | 反义词: temporal, time-bound
