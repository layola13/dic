rebooted:/ˌriːˈbuːtɪd/| v. |重启，重新启动|To restart a computer or other electronic device.  This often involves turning it off and then on again.|例句：I rebooted my computer after it froze.  我电脑死机后重启了它。|例句：The server was rebooted to fix the software bug. 服务器重启以修复软件错误。|近义词：restart, reset|反义词：shutdown


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reboots his computer every morning. = 他每天早上都重启电脑。
She reboots the system regularly. = 她定期重启系统。

一般过去时:
I rebooted my phone after the update. = 更新后我重启了我的手机。
They rebooted the server last night. = 他们昨晚重启了服务器。

现在完成时:
We have rebooted the machine several times. = 我们已经重启了机器好几次。
She has rebooted her computer twice today. = 她今天已经重启电脑两次了。

过去完成时:
He had rebooted the system before the technician arrived. = 技术人员到达之前，他已经重启了系统。
They had rebooted the server multiple times before finding the solution. = 他们在找到解决方案之前已经多次重启服务器了。

将来时:
I will reboot my computer after I finish this work. = 我完成这项工作后将重启我的电脑。
We will reboot the network tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将重启网络。

虚拟语气:
If the system crashed, we would reboot it immediately. = 如果系统崩溃，我们会立即重启它。
I wish I had rebooted my computer sooner. = 我真希望我早点重启电脑。

被动语态:
The computer was rebooted by the technician. = 电脑由技术人员重启。
The system is being rebooted remotely. = 系统正在远程重启。

疑问句:
Has the router been rebooted? = 路由器重启了吗？
Should we reboot the server now? = 我们现在应该重启服务器吗？

否定句:
I didn't reboot my computer yesterday. = 我昨天没有重启电脑。
They haven't rebooted the system yet. = 他们还没有重启系统。


PHRASE->
