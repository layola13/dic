experimentarian: /ɪkˌspɛrɪmənˈtɛəriən/ | n. |实验者；经验主义者 | A person who conducts experiments, especially in science.  Someone who relies on experience as the basis of their knowledge. |例句：The experimentarian carefully documented every step of the process.  实验者仔细记录了过程中的每一步。

The renowned experimentarian, Dr. Smith, published her findings in a leading scientific journal. 著名的实验者史密斯博士在领先的科学期刊上发表了她的研究成果。

|近义词：experimenter, researcher, investigator |反义词：theorist (in the context of relying on experience vs. theory) |词性:n.
