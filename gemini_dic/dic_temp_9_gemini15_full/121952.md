missionary:/ˈmɪʃənɛri/ | n. |传教士 | A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to foreign countries to convert people to a particular religion.  |  Many missionaries traveled to Africa to spread Christianity.  许多传教士前往非洲传播基督教。  | evangelist, preacher | atheist, secularist | 名词


USAGE->

The missionary teaches the local children. = 传教士教当地孩子。
He works tirelessly as a missionary. = 他作为一名传教士不知疲倦地工作。

The missionary visited remote villages. = 传教士访问了偏远的村庄。
