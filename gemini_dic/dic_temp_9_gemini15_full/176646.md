reaccuse:/ˌriːəˈkjuːz/| vt.|重新指控，再次控告|To accuse someone again of the same crime or offense.  This often implies that the previous accusation was either unsuccessful, dismissed, or deemed insufficient.|例句：The prosecutor decided to reaccuse the defendant after new evidence emerged.  检察官在出现新的证据后决定再次起诉被告。

例句：The judge allowed the prosecution to reaccuse the suspect of fraud.  法官允许检方再次指控嫌疑人犯有欺诈罪。
