propensities:/prəˈpɛnsɪtiz/| n. |倾向，习性，癖好 | a natural inclination or tendency |His propensities for gambling led to his ruin. 他的赌博嗜好导致了他的破产。|近义词：tendencies, inclinations, predispositions |反义词：aversions, dislikes
