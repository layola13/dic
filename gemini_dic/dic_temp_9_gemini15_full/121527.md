mismanage:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒ/ | vt. | 经营不善；管理不当 | To manage badly or inefficiently. |例句：The company was severely criticized for its mismanagement of funds.  公司因资金管理不善而受到严厉批评。

例句：He completely mismanaged the project, leading to its failure. 他完全管理不当这个项目，导致了它的失败。


近义词：misadminister, mishandle, botch,  bungle

反义词：manage well, administer effectively


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mismanage the resources every year. = 他们每年都管理资源不善。
The government mismanages the economy. = 政府对经济的管理不善。


一般过去时:
He mismanaged his finances and went bankrupt. = 他理财不善，破产了。
The team mismanaged the game and lost. = 球队比赛中管理不当，输了。


现在完成时:
They have mismanaged the project from the start. = 从一开始他们就管理不当这个项目。
The company has mismanaged its reputation. = 公司损害了自己的声誉。


过去完成时:
They had mismanaged the funds before the audit. = 审计之前他们就已经管理不当资金了。
The school had mismanaged its resources for years. = 学校多年来一直管理不当资源。


将来时:
They will mismanage the situation if they don't change their approach. = 如果他们不改变方法，他们将会把情况搞糟。
The company will mismanage the crisis if they don't act quickly. = 如果他们不迅速行动，公司将会管理不当这场危机。


虚拟语气:
If they had managed the project properly, it wouldn't have failed. = 如果他们正确地管理了项目，它就不会失败了。
If he hadn't mismanaged his money, he would be rich now. = 如果他不乱花钱，他现在就会很富有。


被动语态:
The resources were mismanaged by the team. = 资源被团队管理不善。
The company's finances were mismanaged leading to its downfall. = 公司的财务管理不善，导致了它的垮台。


疑问句:
Did they mismanage the funds? = 他们管理不善资金了吗？
Were the resources mismanaged? = 资源管理不善了吗？


否定句:
They did not mismanage the project. = 他们没有管理不当这个项目。
The company didn't mismanage its assets. = 公司没有管理不善它的资产。
