penitential:/ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnʃəl/| adj.|忏悔的；悔罪的；表示悔恨的|Relating to or expressing penitence or repentance.  It often describes actions or feelings associated with remorse for wrongdoing.|例句：He undertook a penitential pilgrimage to Rome. 他进行了前往罗马的忏悔之旅。


例句：Her penitential tears showed her genuine remorse. 她的悔恨之泪表明了她真正的悔恨。


近义词：repentant, contrite, remorseful


反义词：unrepentant, impenitent, remorseless


词性：形容词
