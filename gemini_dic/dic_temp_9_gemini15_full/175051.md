radiolarian:/ˌreɪdiəˈlɛəriən/| n.|放射虫|A single-celled, usually microscopic, marine protist with a delicate, usually siliceous skeleton.  Radiolaria are zooplankton, meaning they drift in the ocean currents. Their skeletons, often intricate and beautiful, contribute significantly to ocean sediments.|例句：Many radiolarian fossils are found in deep-sea sediments.  许多放射虫化石都发现在深海沉积物中。|例句：The intricate skeletons of radiolaria are a marvel of natural engineering. 放射虫复杂的骨骼是自然工程的奇迹。|近义词：protozoa (general term)|反义词：None specifically|名词
