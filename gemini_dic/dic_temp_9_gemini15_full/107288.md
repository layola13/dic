lam-bastes:/læmˈbeɪsts/| vt.|痛斥；严厉批评；猛烈抨击 |To criticize severely or angrily.  |例句：The coach lambasted the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队糟糕的表现。|例句：The newspaper lambasted the politician for his corrupt dealings. 报纸猛烈抨击了这位政客的腐败行为。|近义词：rebuke, berate, castigate, upbraid |反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lambastes his opponents in every debate. = 他在每次辩论中都痛斥他的对手。
She lambastes the government's policies regularly. = 她经常严厉批评政府的政策。

一般过去时:
The teacher lambasted the student for cheating on the exam. = 老师严厉批评了那个考试作弊的学生。
He lambasted the company's unethical practices in his speech. = 他在演讲中痛斥了该公司的违反道德的行为。

现在完成时:
The critic has lambasted the film for its lack of originality. = 批评家一直严厉批评这部电影缺乏原创性。
She has lambasted the company for its environmental record. = 她一直痛斥这家公司糟糕的环境记录。

过去完成时:
The manager had lambasted the team before the game. = 经理在比赛前痛斥了球队。
They had lambasted the proposal before it was even presented. = 他们在提案提出之前就严厉批评了它。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he were to lambaste me again, I would walk away. = 如果他再次痛斥我，我会走开。
I wish he wouldn't lambaste his colleagues so publicly. = 我希望他不要在公开场合如此痛斥他的同事。

被动语态:
The team was lambasted for their poor performance. = 球队因为糟糕的表现而受到了严厉批评。
His ideas were lambasted by the critics. = 他的想法受到了评论家的严厉批评。

疑问句:
Will the coach lambaste the players after the match? = 比赛后教练会痛斥球员吗？
Does he often lambaste his employees for minor mistakes? = 他是否经常因为小错误而痛斥他的员工？

否定句:
He didn't lambaste the employee, instead, he offered constructive criticism. = 他没有痛斥员工，而是提供了建设性批评。
The review didn't lambaste the novel, but it pointed out some flaws. = 这篇评论并没有痛斥这部小说，但它指出了某些缺陷。
