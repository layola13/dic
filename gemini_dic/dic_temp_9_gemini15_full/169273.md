prominences:/ˈprɒmɪnənsɪz/| n. |突出部分；显著；卓越|The word "prominences" refers to parts that stick out or are more noticeable than their surroundings.  It can also refer to the state of being prominent or outstanding.  The plural form of "prominence".|例句：The mountain range's prominences were clearly visible from the valley. 山脉的突出部分从山谷里清晰可见。

例句：Her achievements in science brought her considerable prominence.  她在科学上的成就使她获得了相当的声誉。|近义词：projections, elevations, highlights, eminence, distinction|反义词：insignificance, obscurity, humility
