depositary:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəri/| n. |保管人；保存处；存款机构|A depositary is a person or institution that holds something in trust for another.  It can refer to a person entrusted with something, or a place where something is stored securely.  The term often appears in legal and financial contexts.|例句：The bank acts as a depositary for the company's funds.  这家银行充当这家公司的资金保管人。|例句：The museum serves as a depositary for historical artifacts.  这个博物馆充当历史文物的保存处。|近义词：custodian, repository, trustee|反义词：disburser, spender
