overmilked:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlkt/| adj.|过度挤奶的；被过度利用的|Excessively exploited or used to the point of exhaustion or diminishing returns.  The term often implies a negative consequence from overuse.|例句：The gold mine was overmilked, leading to its eventual closure. 这座金矿被过度开采，最终导致关闭。

The marketing campaign was overmilked, resulting in audience fatigue.  这次营销活动过度宣传，导致观众疲劳。|近义词：overused, depleted, exhausted, exploited|反义词：underutilized, untapped, fresh
