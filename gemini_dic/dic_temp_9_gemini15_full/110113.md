lickspittle:/ˈlɪkspɪtəl/| n.|拍马屁者，谄媚者|A person who tries to win favor from wealthy or influential people by flattering them excessively.  They are often insincere and opportunistic.|例句：He was nothing more than a lickspittle, always agreeing with his boss, regardless of his opinion.|近义词：flatterer, sycophant, yes-man, toady|反义词：sincere person, honest person, independent person
