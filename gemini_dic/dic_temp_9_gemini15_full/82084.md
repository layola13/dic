gro:/ɡroʊ/| n.|咕噜声；（喉咙）哽咽声|a low, guttural sound, often expressing displeasure or discomfort.  |例句：The old man let out a low gro from deep within his chest. 老人从胸腔深处发出一声低沉的咕噜声。|例句：She made a gro of annoyance when she saw the mess. 她看到那一片狼藉，不耐烦地发出一声咕噜声。|近义词：grunt, growl, grumble |反义词：scream, shout
