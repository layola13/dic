dust:/dʌst/| n. vt.|灰尘，尘土|Fine particles of earth or other matter that are carried or deposited by air.|例句：A thick layer of dust covered the furniture. 厚厚一层灰尘覆盖着家具。|近义词：powder, dirt, grime|反义词：cleanliness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind dusts the road. = 风把路吹上灰尘。
Dust settles on the furniture. = 灰尘落在家具上。


一般过去时:
He dusted the table. = 他擦了桌子上的灰尘。
She dusted off her old books. = 她掸掉了她旧书上的灰尘。


现在完成时:
I have dusted the entire house. = 我已经打扫了整个房子。
The wind has dusted the car with sand. = 风在车上扬起了沙尘。


过去完成时:
The car had been dusted with snow. = 车上已经落了一层雪。
They had dusted the shelves before the guests arrived. = 客人来之前他们已经擦过架子上的灰尘了。


将来时:
I will dust the room later. = 我一会儿会打扫房间。
The wind will dust the fields with pollen. = 风会把花粉吹到田野里。


虚拟语气:
If I had dusted the furniture yesterday, it wouldn't be so dirty now. = 如果我昨天擦了家具，现在就不会这么脏了。
I wish I had dusted the shelves before the party. = 我真希望在聚会前把架子上的灰尘擦掉。


被动语态:
The furniture was dusted yesterday. = 家具昨天被擦干净了。
The car is being dusted with a special cleaning solution. = 汽车正在用特殊的清洁剂除尘。


疑问句:
Has the room been dusted? = 房间打扫干净了吗？
Do you dust your furniture regularly? = 你经常擦家具上的灰尘吗？


否定句:
I didn't dust the floor this morning. = 我今天早上没有扫地。
She doesn't dust her house often enough. = 她不经常打扫房子。


PHRASE->

dust to dust = 尘归尘，土归土
