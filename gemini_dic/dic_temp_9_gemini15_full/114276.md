mainframe:/ˈmeɪnfreɪm/| n. |大型计算机，主机|A mainframe is a very large and powerful computer, typically used by large organizations for critical applications requiring high performance and reliability.  They are known for their ability to handle massive amounts of data and transactions simultaneously.|例句：Many banks rely on mainframe computers to process millions of transactions daily.  许多银行依靠大型计算机每天处理数百万笔交易。

例句：The company's mainframe system went down, causing significant disruption. 公司的大型计算机系统宕机，造成了严重的混乱。


近义词：large computer, central computer, host computer|反义词：personal computer, microcomputer|词性:名词
