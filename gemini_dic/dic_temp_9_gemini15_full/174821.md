racer:/ˈreɪsər/| n.|赛车手；竞赛者；快速移动的人或物|A person or thing that races; someone who competes in races, especially car races.  It can also refer to something that moves very quickly.|例句：He's a professional racer. 他是一位职业赛车手。

例句：The cheetah is a natural racer. 猎豹是天生的赛跑能手。


近义词：competitor, contestant, speedster |反义词：sluggard, laggard


词源:源自动词race（比赛），加上后缀-er（表示人或物），表示从事比赛的人或物。  The word ultimately derives from the Old French word "race," meaning "course, running."
