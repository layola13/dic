overconsciousness: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/ | n. |过度敏感；过分在意；神经质 | Excessive self-consciousness; an excessive awareness of oneself and one's actions.  It often implies anxiety or discomfort related to being observed or judged by others.  | Example: Her overconsciousness prevented her from fully enjoying the party.  她的过度敏感让她无法尽情享受派对。  |近义词：self-consciousness, hyperawareness, anxiety, nervousness | 反义词： unconcern, indifference, composure, nonchalance |名词
