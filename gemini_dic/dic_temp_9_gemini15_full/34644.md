clenches:/klɛntʃɪz/| vt.| 紧握；握紧；（使）皱缩；|To close or press (something) tightly.  To contract or tighten a muscle or muscles.|例句：He clenched his fists in anger. 他愤怒地握紧了拳头。

He clenched his jaw. 他紧咬着牙关。


近义词：grip, grasp, squeeze, compress|反义词：release, loosen, open
