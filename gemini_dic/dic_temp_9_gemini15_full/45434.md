curability:/ˌkjʊərəˈbɪləti/| n. |可治愈性，治愈率|The curability of a disease refers to the likelihood or possibility that it can be cured.  It often depends on factors such as early diagnosis, the type and stage of the disease, and the patient's overall health.  |Example: The curability of early-stage breast cancer is significantly higher than that of advanced-stage disease.早期乳腺癌的可治愈性明显高于晚期乳腺癌。

The curability of this infection is quite high with prompt treatment.这种感染如果及时治疗，治愈率相当高。|近义词：treatability, recoverability |反义词：incurability
