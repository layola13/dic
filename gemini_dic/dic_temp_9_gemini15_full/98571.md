institutional:/ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/| adj.|体制的；制度的；机构的；学院的|Relating to or characteristic of an established institution or institutions.|The institutional reforms were met with resistance.|近义词：systematic, organized, established|反义词：individual, personal, informal


例句：

The institutional changes were designed to improve efficiency.  =  这些体制改革旨在提高效率。

He criticized the institutional racism within the police force. = 他批评了警队内部的制度性种族歧视。
