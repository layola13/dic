redos:/ˈriːduːz/| n.|重做，返工|The act of repeating a process, often to correct errors or improve results. It often implies a more thorough or careful approach than a simple redo.|例句：The redos on the software took much longer than expected.  软件的返工比预期的要长得多。|近义词：redo, rework, revision|反义词：completion, finalization


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team redoes the design every week. = 团队每周都重新设计。
He redoes his homework every day. = 他每天都重做作业。


一般过去时:
She redid the presentation after receiving feedback. = 她收到反馈后重新制作了演示文稿。
They redid the entire project. = 他们重做了整个项目。


现在完成时:
They have redone the website several times. = 他们已经多次重新设计了网站。
We have redone the calculations to ensure accuracy. = 我们已经重新计算以确保准确性。


过去完成时:
They had redone the experiment before publishing the results. = 他们在发表结果之前已经重做了实验。
She had redone the painting several times before she was satisfied. = 她在她满意之前已经重画了好几次。


将来时:
We will redo the test next week. = 我们下周将重做测试。
He will redo the assignment if he fails. = 如果他不及格，他将重做作业。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would redo the entire project. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重做整个项目。
I wish I had redone the report before submitting it. = 我希望我在提交报告之前已经重做了它。


被动语态:
The document was redone by the editor. = 文件由编辑重新制作。
The bridge will be redone next year. = 这座桥明年将被重建。


疑问句:
Will the work be redone? = 工作会被重做吗？
Did they redo the calculations? = 他们重新计算了吗？


否定句:
They did not redo the experiment. = 他们没有重做实验。
We will not redo the assignment. = 我们不会重做作业。
