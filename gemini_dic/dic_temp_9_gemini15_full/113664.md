machismos:/mɑːˈtʃɪzˌmɔːz/ | n. | 男性沙文主义；大男子主义 | Masculine chauvinism; a set of beliefs and behaviors that are characteristic of machismo.  It often involves the assertion of male dominance and the subjugation of women.  The term is often used to describe a culture or system of beliefs that privileges men and undervalues women. | Example:  His behavior is a blatant display of machismo. 他的行为是赤裸裸的大男子主义表现。

The pervasive machismo in the community made it difficult for women to advance.  这个社区普遍存在的大男子主义使得女性难以进步。


近义词：male chauvinism, sexism, misogyny, patriarchy


反义词：feminism, egalitarianism


词性：名词
