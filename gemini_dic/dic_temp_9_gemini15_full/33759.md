circulary:/ˈsɜːrkjələri/| adj.|循环的；圆形的；环状的|Circular means having a round shape or relating to a circle.  It can also refer to something that is sent to many people, like a circular letter.|例句：The garden was laid out in a circular pattern. 花园呈圆形布局。|近义词：round, rotational, cyclical|反义词：linear, straight, rectangular|
