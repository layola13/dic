obligatos:/ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtoʊz/| n. |(音乐)  obbligato乐段；必须演奏的乐段 |An obligato is a musical passage that is essential to the performance and must be played. It's usually a solo part that interacts with the main melody.  |例句：The oboe obligato in the third movement is particularly beautiful. 第三乐章中的 oboe obbligato 特别优美。

The violin obligato enhanced the emotional impact of the aria. 小提琴 obbligato 增强了咏叹调的情感冲击力。


近义词：solo, essential part |反义词：optional part, ad lib
