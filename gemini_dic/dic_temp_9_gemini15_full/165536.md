premanufactures:/ˌpriːmænjuˈfæktʃərz/ | v. |预先制造，预制|To manufacture something in advance, often in parts or components, for later assembly or completion.|The company premanufactures many of its components to speed up production.  这家公司预先制造许多零部件以加快生产速度。|The factory premanufactures modular housing units. 工厂预制模块化住房单元。 |近义词：prefabricate, preassemble |反义词：assemble, manufacture (in the final stages)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They premanufacture engine parts for the car assembly line. = 他们为汽车装配线预先制造发动机零件。
The company premanufactures furniture components. = 这家公司预先制造家具组件。

一般过去时:
The builder premanufactured the window frames before construction. = 建筑商在施工前预先制造了窗框。
They premanufactured the steel structures for the bridge. = 他们为桥梁预先制造了钢结构。

现在完成时:
We have premanufactured all the necessary components for the project. = 我们已经为该项目预先制造了所有必要的组件。
The factory has premanufactured thousands of units this year. = 工厂今年已预先制造了数千个单元。

过去完成时:
They had premanufactured the parts before the strike began. = 罢工开始前，他们已预先制造好零件。
The team had premanufactured the modules for the quick deployment. = 团队为快速部署预先制造了模块。

将来时:
We will premanufacture the chassis for the next model. = 我们将为下一款车型预先制造底盘。
The plant will premanufacture the panels for the new building. = 工厂将为新建筑预先制造面板。

虚拟语气:
If we premanufactured the components, assembly would be much faster. = 如果我们预先制造组件，组装速度会快得多。
I wish we had premanufactured the parts; we're behind schedule. = 我希望我们已经预先制造了零件；我们进度落后了。

被动语态:
The parts were premanufactured overseas. = 零件是在海外预先制造的。
The modules are being premanufactured for the upcoming project. = 模块正在为即将到来的项目预先制造。

疑问句:
Are the parts premanufactured or assembled on-site? = 零件是预先制造的还是在现场组装的？
Were the sections premanufactured before shipment? = 部件在发货前是否预先制造？

否定句:
They did not premanufacture the entire structure. = 他们没有预先制造整个结构。
We won't premanufacture the product without testing it. = 我们不会在未经测试的情况下预先制造产品。
