epistemologist:/ɪˌpɪstɪˈmɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 认识论学者 | A person who studies epistemology, the branch of philosophy concerned with the nature and scope of knowledge.  |  She's a renowned epistemologist, specializing in the problem of justified belief.  她是一位著名的认识论学者，专门研究论证性信仰的问题。 | philosopher, theorist |  (No direct antonym, but opposing views within epistemology could be considered antithetical.) | 名词
