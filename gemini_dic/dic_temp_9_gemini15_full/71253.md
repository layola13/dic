five-a-sides:/faɪv ə saɪdz/| n. |五人制足球赛|A five-a-side football match is a match played with five players on each team, typically on a smaller pitch than a full-size football pitch.  It's a popular form of football, often played informally or in leagues.|Example: We're playing a five-a-side match this weekend.  这个周末我们有一场五人制足球赛。  They organized a five-a-side tournament for charity. 他们组织了一场为慈善事业举办的五人制足球锦标赛。|近义词: five-a-side football, indoor football (if played indoors)|反义词: eleven-a-side football (standard football)|名词
