divulges:/dɪˈvʌldʒɪz/| vt.|泄露；透露；公开|To divulge something means to reveal it, often something that was previously secret or confidential.  The verb implies a deliberate act of sharing information, sometimes against the wishes of others.|例句：He divulged the secret to his friend. 他向他的朋友泄露了秘密。|例句：The company refused to divulge its financial figures.  这家公司拒绝公开其财务数据。|近义词：reveal, disclose, uncover, betray|反义词：conceal, hide, secrete, keep secret|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She divulges company secrets to the press. = 她向媒体泄露公司机密。
He divulges his innermost thoughts in his diary. = 他在日记里倾诉他内心最深处的想法。

一般过去时:
The whistleblower divulged the illegal activities to the authorities. = 举报人向当局揭露了非法活动。
He divulged the location of the hidden treasure. = 他泄露了隐藏宝藏的地点。

现在完成时:
She has divulged too much information already. = 她已经泄露了太多信息。
The journalist has divulged several important details about the case. = 记者已经透露了关于此案的几个重要细节。

过去完成时:
He had divulged the plan before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始他就泄露了计划。
They had divulged the secret to several people before realizing their mistake. = 他们在意识到错误之前已经向几个人泄露了秘密。

将来时:
She will divulge the truth in her upcoming book. = 她将在即将出版的书中揭露真相。
The government will not divulge the details of the negotiation. = 政府不会透露谈判的细节。

虚拟语气:
If he had not divulged the information, the plan would have succeeded. = 如果他没有泄露信息，计划就会成功。
I wish he hadn't divulged my secret to everyone. = 我真希望他没有把我的秘密告诉所有人。

被动语态:
The information was divulged to the public. = 信息被公诸于众。
The location of the meeting was divulged by an insider. = 会议地点被内部人士泄露。

疑问句:
Will he divulge the details of the investigation? = 他会透露调查的细节吗？
Did she divulge the source of her information? = 她泄露了信息来源吗？

否定句:
He did not divulge any sensitive information. = 他没有泄露任何敏感信息。
The witness refused to divulge the suspect's identity. = 证人拒绝透露嫌疑人的身份。
