semi-uncial:/ˌsɛmiˈʌnʃəl/| adj.|半盎司体(一种书写体)|A semi-uncial script is a style of handwriting that developed from the uncial script. It is characterized by its rounded letters and smaller size compared to the uncial script.  It was widely used in the late Roman Empire and the early Middle Ages for writing books and documents.|The Lindisfarne Gospels are a famous example of a book written in semi-uncial script.|近义词：cursive (in a broad sense)|反义词：uncial (in a comparative sense),  majuscule (in a broad sense)|词性：形容词
