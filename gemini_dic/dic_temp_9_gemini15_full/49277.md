deliberations:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |商议；审议；考虑|Careful and thorough consideration or discussion of something, especially in order to make a decision.|The jury's deliberations lasted for several days before reaching a verdict.|近义词：considerations, discussions, consultations, debates|反义词：impulsiveness, haste

Their deliberations continue throughout the night. = 他们的商议持续了一整夜。
The committee's deliberations focus on budget allocation. = 委员会的商议集中在预算分配上。

一般过去时:
The council's deliberations concluded with a unanimous decision. = 理事会的商议以一致决定告终。

Deliberations have taken longer than anticipated due to the complexity of the issue. = 由于问题的复杂性，商议比预期的要长。

Further deliberations will be required before a final decision is made. = 在做出最终决定之前，还需要进一步的商议。
