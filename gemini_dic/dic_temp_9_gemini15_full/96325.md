indignance:/ɪnˈdɪɡnəns/| n. |愤慨；义愤；愤怒 |A feeling of annoyance, displeasure, or anger at something considered unjust, unworthy, or offensive.  It often implies a sense of moral outrage. |例句：His indignation at the injustice was palpable.  他的愤怒之情溢于言表。

例句：The blatant disregard for safety rules filled her with indignation. 这种公然无视安全规则的行为让她感到愤怒。


近义词：anger, wrath, resentment, outrage, fury, ire


反义词：acceptance, complacency, indifference


词性：名词
