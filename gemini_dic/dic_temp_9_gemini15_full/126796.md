nanotechnologies:/ˌnænəʊˌtɛknɒlədʒiz/|/n.|纳米技术；纳米科技|The scientific study and application of extremely small things.  Nanotechnologies involve manipulating matter at the atomic and molecular level to create new materials and devices with unique properties.|例句：Researchers are exploring the potential of nanotechnologies in medicine. 研究人员正在探索纳米技术在医学领域的潜力。|例句：The development of nanotechnologies has led to significant advancements in various fields. 纳米技术的发展导致了各个领域的重大进步。|近义词：nanoscience, nanotechnology (sing.)|反义词：macrotechnology (less common and often used in contrast)|名词
