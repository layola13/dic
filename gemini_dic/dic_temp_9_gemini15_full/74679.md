freed:/friːd/| v. |释放；解放；使自由|to release from confinement, oppression, or restraint; to liberate.|例句：The prisoners were freed after many years of imprisonment.  犯人们在监禁多年后被释放了。
例句：The new law freed businesses from unnecessary regulations. 新法律使企业摆脱了不必要的规章制度。|近义词：liberated, released, untied |反义词：imprisoned, confined, enslaved |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frees the birds from their cages every morning. 他每天早上都把鸟从笼子里放出来。
They free their minds from worry by meditating. 他们通过冥想来使自己的思想摆脱担忧。

一般过去时:
She freed the trapped animal from the net. 她把被困住的动物从网里解救了出来。
The soldiers freed the city from the enemy's control. 士兵们解放了这座城市，使其摆脱了敌人的控制。

现在完成时:
He has freed himself from the shackles of addiction. 他已经摆脱了沉迷的枷锁。
The movement has freed many people from oppression.  这场运动已经解放了许多受压迫的人民。

过去完成时:

She had freed herself from the constraints of her family. 她已经摆脱了家庭的束缚。

将来时:
