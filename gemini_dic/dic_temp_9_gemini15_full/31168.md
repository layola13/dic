change:/tʃeɪndʒ/ |/tʃeɪndʒ/ | n./v. |改变；变化；更换 |To make or become different.  |The change in weather was sudden. 天气突变。| The company changed its policy. 公司改变了政策。| alteration, modification, transformation | sameness, constancy, stability |名词/动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather changes frequently. = 天气经常变化。
He changes his mind often. = 他经常改变主意。


一般过去时:
The company changed its logo last year. = 公司去年更换了标识。
She changed her dress before the party. = 她派对前换了衣服。


现在完成时:
They have changed their plans. = 他们已经改变了计划。
Things have changed a lot since I was a child. = 自我小时候以来，事情变化很大。


过去完成时:
He had changed his mind before I could persuade him. = 在我劝说他之前，他已经改变了主意。
We had already changed our tickets before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，我们已经换了票。


将来时:
The company will change its strategy next quarter. = 公司下个季度将改变策略。
I will change my clothes after I finish exercising. = 我运动完后会换衣服。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would change my car. = 如果我有更多钱，我会换车。
I wish I could change the past. = 我希望我能改变过去。


被动语态:
The schedule was changed without notice. = 时间表在未经通知的情况下被更改了。
The policy has been changed several times. = 该政策已经更改过几次了。


疑问句:
Has the situation changed? = 情况改变了吗？
Will they change their decision? = 他们会改变决定吗？


否定句:
Nothing has changed. = 什么都没变。
They didn't change their plans. = 他们没有改变计划。



PHRASE->
