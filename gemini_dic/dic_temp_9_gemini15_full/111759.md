lodestone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n. |磁石，吸铁石 |A lodestone is a naturally occurring magnet, a piece of magnetite with the property of attracting iron.  It was historically significant as a navigational aid due to its ability to align itself with the Earth's magnetic field. |The lodestone guided the sailors across the treacherous seas. 磁石引导水手们穿越危险的海域。The ancient mariner used a lodestone to navigate. 古代水手用磁石导航。|magnet, magnetite|non-magnet, diamagnetic material|名词
