populaces:/ˈpɒpjʊləsɪz/| n.|民众，大众|The word "populaces" refers to the common people of a country or region, considered as a whole. It is often used to emphasize the mass of people as opposed to the elite or ruling class.  The plural form of populace. |例句：The government's policies were unpopular with the populaces. 政府的政策不受民众欢迎。|例句：The populaces of many nations are demanding greater political participation.|许多国家的民众都在要求更大的政治参与。|近义词：masses, people, commoners|反义词：elite, aristocracy, ruling class|名词
