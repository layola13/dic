hospitaler:/ˌhɒspɪˈtælər/|/ˈhɑːspɪtələr/| n. |医院护理人员；好客的人；圣约翰骑士团成员|A person who works in a hospital, especially a nurse or other caregiver.  Also, historically, a member of a religious order, particularly the Knights Hospitaller, known for their charitable work and hospitality.|例句：The hospitaler was kind and compassionate to all her patients. = 医院护理人员对所有病人都很和蔼可亲。  The old innkeeper was known as a true hospitaler, always welcoming travelers with warmth and generosity. =  这位老旅馆老板以好客闻名，总是热情慷慨地欢迎旅行者。|近义词：nurse, caregiver, humanitarian, benefactor|反义词：inhospitable, unfriendly, cruel
