rafterless:/ˈræftərləs/| adj.| 无椽的，无屋椽的 | Lacking rafters; without rafters.  The term describes a structure or building that is designed or built without the typical sloping beams (rafters) used to support a roof.  This could imply a flat roof, a different roofing system, or a structure not intended to have a traditional roof. | Example: The modern, minimalist design featured a rafterless roof, supported instead by a series of innovative steel beams.  = 现代极简主义的设计采用了一个无椽的屋顶，它由一系列创新的钢梁支撑。|近义词：roofless (if referring to the overall lack of roof), unroofed |反义词：raftered
