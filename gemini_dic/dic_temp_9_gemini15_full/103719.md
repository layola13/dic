josh:/dʒɒʃ/| /dʒɑːʃ/| v. n.|开玩笑；戏弄；猛推；（尤指男性名字）乔什|To tease or make fun of someone playfully; to push or shove forcefully.  The word can also function as a proper noun, representing a masculine given name.|例句：He was just joshing you, don't take it seriously. 他只是和你开玩笑，别当真。|例句：Don't josh me, I'm serious.  别和我开玩笑，我是认真的。|近义词：tease, kid, joke, prank, shove|反义词：be serious, be sincere|动词，名词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He joshes his friends often. = 他经常和朋友开玩笑。
She joshes him about his new haircut. = 她取笑他新剪的发型。

一般过去时:
They joshed him mercilessly. = 他们无情地戏弄他。
He joshed her about her clumsy dance. = 他取笑她笨拙的舞姿。

现在完成时:
I have joshed him about it several times. = 我已经多次和他开过这个玩笑。
She has joshed him relentlessly for weeks. = 她几周来一直无情地取笑他。

过去完成时:
He had joshed her before, so she didn't believe him. = 他以前和她开过玩笑，所以她不相信他。
They had joshed him so much he was embarrassed. = 他们开他玩笑开得太多了，他感到很尴尬。

将来时:
I will josh him about his new car. = 我将会和他开玩笑，关于他的新车。
He will josh her about her singing. = 他将会取笑她的歌声。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't josh him so much. = 如果我是你，我不会和他开那么多玩笑。
I wish I hadn't joshed him so harshly. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地取笑他。


被动语态:
He was joshed relentlessly by his classmates. = 他被同学们无情地取笑。
She was joshed about her accent. = 她因为口音被取笑。


疑问句:
Are you joshing me? = 你在和我开玩笑吗？
Was he joshing when he said that? = 他说这话的时候是在开玩笑吗？


否定句:
I wasn't joshing; I was completely serious. = 我不是在开玩笑；我是完全认真的。
He didn't josh her; he was genuinely concerned. = 他没有和她开玩笑；他确实是真心关心的。
