coati:/ˈkoʊɑːti/| n. |卷尾猴|A coati is a small mammal of the raccoon family, with a long, ringed tail and a pointed snout.  They are known for their inquisitive nature and their ability to climb trees.  Coatis are found in the forests and woodlands of Central and South America.|The coati sniffed around the base of the tree, searching for insects.  这只卷尾猴在树根周围嗅来嗅去，寻找昆虫。|近义词：raccoon, kinkajou|反义词：None
