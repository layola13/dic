darkens:/ˈdɑːrkənz/| v.|使变暗；使变黑；使忧郁|To make or become dark or darker.  It implies a gradual process of becoming darker, not an instantaneous change.|例句：The sky darkened as the storm approached.  天空在暴风雨来临之际变暗了。

The room darkened when the lights went out.  灯熄灭后房间变暗了。|近义词：darkens, dims, shades, obscures|反义词：brightens, lightens

USAGE->

The sky darkens every evening. = 天空中每天傍晚都会变暗。
He darkens the room with curtains. = 他用窗帘使房间变暗。

一般过去时:
The storm darkened the sky. = 暴风雨使天空变暗。
