foursomes:/ˈfɔːrsʌmz/| n. |四人一组；四人赛；四人同游|A foursome is a group of four people, often playing a game together, especially golf.  It can also refer to a romantic pairing of two couples.|例句：We played a foursome of golf this morning. 今天早上我们打了一场四人高尔夫球赛。|例句：They went on a foursome to the beach. 他们四人一起去海滩玩。|近义词：quartet, group of four|反义词：solo, duo, trio
