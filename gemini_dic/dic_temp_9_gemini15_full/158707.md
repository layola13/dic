ploughboy: /ˈplaʊbɔɪ/ | n. | 犁农，乡下小伙子 | A young man who works on a farm, especially one who ploughs the land.  The term often carries connotations of rustic simplicity and lack of sophistication. | Example:  The ploughboy whistled a merry tune as he guided his oxen. 犁农一边赶着牛，一边愉快地吹着口哨。  He was a simple ploughboy, untouched by city life. 他是个单纯的犁农，未受城市生活的影响。 | 近义词：farmhand, farm laborer, peasant boy | 反义词：gentleman, aristocrat
