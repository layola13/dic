cyprinoid:/ˈsɪprɪnɔɪd/ | adj. n. |鲤科的；鲤科鱼类 | Belonging to or characteristic of the Cyprinidae, a large family of freshwater fish including carps, minnows, and barbels.  |例句：Many cyprinoid species are important food sources in Asia.  许多鲤科鱼类是亚洲重要的食物来源。

例句：The study focused on the reproductive behavior of cyprinoid fishes. 这项研究关注鲤科鱼类的繁殖行为。


近义词：carp-like (仅指像鲤鱼的)


反义词：无


词性：形容词，名词
