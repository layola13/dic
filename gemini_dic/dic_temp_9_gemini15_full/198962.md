sight-reading:/ˈsaɪtˌriːdɪŋ/| n. |视奏；看谱演奏|The act of reading and performing music at the same time, without prior preparation or rehearsal.  It requires a high level of musical skill and sight-reading ability.|例句：Her sight-reading skills are exceptional.她的视奏技巧非常出色。

例句：The pianist demonstrated impressive sight-reading skills during the audition.  这位钢琴家在试听中展现了令人印象深刻的视奏技巧。|近义词：reading at sight, playing from the score|反义词：memorized performance, rehearsal
