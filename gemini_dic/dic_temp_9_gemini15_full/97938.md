innovation:/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən/ | n. |创新，革新 |The introduction of new methods, ideas, or products.  It refers to the process of translating an idea or invention into a good or service that creates value or for which customers will pay.  |例句：The company is known for its commitment to innovation. 这家公司以其对创新的承诺而闻名。

例句：This new technology represents a significant innovation in the field of medicine. 这项新技术代表了医学领域的一项重大创新。


近义词：invention, creativity, novelty, originality |反义词：stagnation, tradition, convention


词性: 名词

Their company focuses on continuous innovation. = 他们的公司专注于持续创新。

过去完成时:

词源:  The word "innovation" comes from the Latin word "innovatio," which means "renewal."  It's derived from the verb "innovare," meaning "to make new," "to renew," or "to change."
