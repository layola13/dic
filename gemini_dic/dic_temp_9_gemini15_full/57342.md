The term often refers to those affected by nuclear fallout.|例句：The downwinders suffered long-term health problems due to the nuclear testing.  下风地区的居民由于核试验而遭受了长期的健康问题。|例句：Studies on downwinders revealed increased rates of cancer. 对下风地区居民的研究揭示了癌症发病率的增加。|近义词：victim, sufferer|反义词：upwinder|名词
