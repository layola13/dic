saleswomen:/ˈseɪlzˌwɪmɪn/| n. |女销售员，女推销员 |A saleswoman is a woman who sells goods or services.  Saleswomen work in various settings, from retail stores to online platforms. They are often responsible for customer interaction, product demonstrations, and closing sales.|例句：The saleswoman showed me several different dresses.  这位女销售员给我展示了几件不同的裙子。

The saleswomen in that store are very helpful and friendly.  那家店的销售员都非常乐于助人和友好。|近义词：salesclerk, sales representative, vendor |反义词：customer, buyer
