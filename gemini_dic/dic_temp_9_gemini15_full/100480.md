intervarietal: /ˌɪntərˈvɑːriɪtəl/ | adj. | 品种间的，种间 | Relating to or occurring between different varieties of the same species, especially of plants.  | Example: Intervarietal competition for resources can significantly impact crop yield. 种间竞争资源会显著影响作物产量。 | 近义词: interspecific (when referring to species rather than varieties) | 反义词: intravarietal (within the same variety) | 词性: adjective
