one-shot:/ˈwʌnʃɑːt/| adj. n.|一次性的；一击必杀；单次拍摄|A single attempt or action intended to achieve a result quickly and decisively.  It can refer to a single, decisive action, a single take in film or photography, or a single-serving product.|例句：He achieved a one-shot victory in the tournament. 他在锦标赛中一举获胜。

例句：The director preferred a one-shot scene to multiple takes. 导演更喜欢单次拍摄的场景而不是多次拍摄。


近义词：single, instantaneous, immediate, decisive|反义词：repeated, multiple, prolonged, drawn-out|词性: adjective, noun
