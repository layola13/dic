scherif:/ʃeˈrɪf/ | n. |警长|Sheriff is a law enforcement officer with powers that vary depending on jurisdiction.  They are typically responsible for maintaining order in a county.  In some areas, they also run the county jail.|例句：The sheriff arrested the suspect. 警长逮捕了嫌疑犯。|The sheriff's department investigated the crime. 警长部门调查了这起犯罪案件。|近义词：police chief, constable, marshal|反义词：criminal, outlaw
