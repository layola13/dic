hellishly:/ˈhɛlɪʃli/| adv.|非常；极度地；地狱般地|extremely; to an intense or excessive degree; in a hellish manner|例句：The heat was hellishly intense.  这热度非常强烈。  The work was hellishly difficult. 这工作难极了。|近义词：extremely, intensely, terribly, awfully|反义词：mildly, slightly, gently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works hellishly hard. 他工作非常努力。
She speaks hellishly fast. 她说话飞快。


一般过去时:
They partied hellishly last night. 他们昨晚玩得很疯狂。
It rained hellishly hard yesterday. 昨天雨下得非常大。


现在完成时:
She has worked hellishly hard on this project. 她为这个项目付出了巨大的努力。
It has rained hellishly hard this week. 这周雨下得非常大。


过去完成时:
They had already worked hellishly hard before the deadline. 截止日期前他们已经非常努力地工作了。
It had rained hellishly hard all morning.  整个上午雨下得非常大。


将来时:
She will work hellishly hard to achieve her goal. 她将非常努力地工作以实现她的目标。
It will probably rain hellishly hard tomorrow. 明天可能会下很大的雨。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the work would be hellishly difficult, I wouldn't have taken it. 如果我知道工作会这么难，我就不会接了。
If it rained hellishly hard tomorrow, we'd have to postpone the picnic. 如果明天雨下得很大，我们就得推迟野餐。


被动语态:
The task was made hellishly difficult by the unclear instructions. 模糊的指示使任务变得极其困难。
The situation was made hellishly complicated by the unexpected events. 出乎意料的事件使情况变得极其复杂。


疑问句:
Was the exam hellishly difficult?  考试难到极点了吗？
Is the weather going to be hellishly hot this weekend? 这个周末天气会非常热吗？


否定句:
The task wasn't hellishly difficult, it was just challenging. 任务并不难，只是具有挑战性。
The weather wasn't hellishly hot, it was just warm. 天气并不炎热，只是温暖。
