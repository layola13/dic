hysterically:/hɪˈstɛrɪkli/ | adv. |歇斯底里地，情绪激动地 |  In a wildly emotional or uncontrolled way, often characterized by laughter or crying.  |例句：She laughed hysterically at the comedian's jokes. 她听到喜剧演员的笑话后歇斯底里地大笑。|例句：He sobbed hysterically after hearing the news. 听到这个消息后，他歇斯底里地哭了起来。|近义词：frantically, wildly, uncontrollably |反义词：calmly, serenely, composedly

词源:
