rushingly:/ˈrʌʃɪŋli/ | adv. |仓促地，匆忙地 |In a hurried or hasty manner.  The work was done rushingly and poorly. 工作做得仓促而粗劣。|近义词：hurriedly, hastily, speedily |反义词：leisurely, slowly, deliberately |


例句：

He rushingly finished his homework before going out. 他匆忙地完成了作业然后出去。

She rushingly ate her lunch and ran to catch the bus. 她匆匆忙忙地吃了午饭，然后跑去赶公共汽车。


词源:

Derived from the adjective "rushing," which itself comes from the verb "rush," meaning to move or act quickly or hastily.  The suffix "-ingly" transforms the adjective into an adverb, indicating the manner in which something is done.


用法说明:

Rushingly is an adverb that modifies verbs, describing the way in which an action is performed. It emphasizes the speed and lack of carefulness involved.  It often implies a sense of urgency or pressure.  The word carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting that the action may have been performed too quickly and perhaps with a lack of attention to detail.
