semi-basement: /ˈsɛmiˌbeɪsmənt/ | n. |半地下室 | A semi-basement is a room or rooms partially below ground level, typically with some windows above ground.  Unlike a full basement, it has at least some portion of its walls above ground level, often providing more natural light and ventilation. |例句：The apartment had a charming semi-basement with large windows overlooking the garden. 这套公寓有一个迷人的半地下室，有大窗户可以俯瞰花园。

例句：We rented a semi-basement apartment because it was more affordable. 我们租了一套半地下室公寓，因为更便宜。


近义词：lower level, partially underground room |反义词：attic, penthouse, upper floor |词性：名词
