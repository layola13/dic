kill-off:/kɪlˈɔːf/| vt.|消灭，清除，杀光|To kill a large number of people or animals in a particular place or area; to eliminate or destroy something completely.|例句：The pesticide killed off most of the insects.  杀虫剂杀死了大部分昆虫。|例句：The disease killed off a large portion of the population.  这种疾病消灭了很大一部分人口。|近义词：eradicate, exterminate, eliminate, wipe out|反义词：preserve, protect, conserve, foster


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government kills off harmful insects every year. = 政府每年都会消灭有害昆虫。
The company kills off its failing projects. = 公司淘汰其失败的项目。


一般过去时:
The frost killed off all the flowers in the garden. = 霜冻杀死了花园里所有的花。
The wildfire killed off most of the wildlife in the area. = 野火消灭了该地区大部分野生动物。


现在完成时:
The new policy has killed off many small businesses. = 新政策已经消灭了许多小型企业。
The drought has killed off much of the vegetation. = 干旱已经消灭了大部分植被。


过去完成时:
The previous administration had killed off the funding for the project. = 前一届政府已经取消了该项目的资金。
The prolonged war had killed off the hope for peace. = 长期的战争已经消灭了和平的希望。


将来时:
They will kill off the infected plants to prevent the disease from spreading. = 他们将清除受感染的植物以防止疾病蔓延。
The new technology will kill off the demand for the old product. = 新技术将淘汰对旧产品的需求。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't killed off the program, we would have had better results. = 如果他们没有取消该项目，我们会取得更好的结果。
If the government killed off the subsidies, many farmers would go bankrupt. = 如果政府取消补贴，许多农民将破产。


被动语态:
Many species were killed off by habitat destruction. = 许多物种因栖息地破坏而灭绝。
The old system was killed off by the new reforms. = 旧制度被新的改革所淘汰。


疑问句:
Will the new regulations kill off small businesses? = 新规会不会消灭小型企业？
Did the storm kill off the entire crop? = 暴风雨是否消灭了全部农作物？


否定句:
The pesticide didn't kill off all the weeds. = 杀虫剂并没有杀死所有的杂草。
The company hasn't killed off its research and development department. = 公司没有淘汰其研发部门。


PHRASE->
kill off competition = 消灭竞争对手
kill off a rumour = 辟谣
kill off an idea = 否决一个想法
kill off a character = 杀死一个角色（在小说或电影中）
kill off a disease = 根除一种疾病
