postemergency:/ˌpoʊstɪˈmɜːrdʒənsi/ | adj. | 紧急情况后的；灾后 | Relating to the period after an emergency or disaster.  It describes activities, planning, or situations that occur following an emergency. | Example: Postemergency relief efforts were hampered by the severe weather.  紧急救援工作因恶劣天气而受阻。 |近义词: post-disaster, post-crisis | 反义词: pre-emergency
