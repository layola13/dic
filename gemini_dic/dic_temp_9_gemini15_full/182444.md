rennet:/ˈrɛnɪt/| n.|凝乳酶；凝乳酵素|A substance containing rennin, used to curdle milk in cheesemaking.  It is traditionally obtained from the stomach lining of unweaned calves, but now also produced using microbial fermentation.|例句：The cheesemaker added rennet to the milk to start the coagulation process.  奶酪制造者添加了凝乳酶到牛奶中以开始凝结过程。|近义词：rennin, chymosin|反义词:无
