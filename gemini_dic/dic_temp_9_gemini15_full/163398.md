preconsolidate:/ˌpriːkənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/| v.|预先合并，预先巩固|To consolidate beforehand; to strengthen or combine things in advance.|The company decided to preconsolidate its assets before the merger to streamline the process. 公司决定在合并之前预先合并资产以简化流程。|近义词：prepare, consolidate, integrate|反义词：separate, divide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preconsolidate their resources every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会预先整合资源。
The team preconsolidates its data before analysis. = 团队在分析之前预先整合数据。

一般过去时:
The bank preconsolidated its branches last year. = 银行去年预先合并了分行。
We preconsolidated our efforts to achieve the goal. = 我们预先整合了我们的努力以实现目标。

现在完成时:
They have preconsolidated their investments for the upcoming market downturn. = 他们已经为即将到来的市场低迷预先整合了投资。
The government has preconsolidated its emergency response plans. = 政府已经预先整合了其应急响应计划。

过去完成时:
They had preconsolidated their positions before the stock market crash. = 他们在股市崩盘前已经预先巩固了他们的立场。
She had preconsolidated her notes before the presentation. = 她在演示文稿之前已经预先整理了笔记。

将来时:
The company will preconsolidate its operations in the next fiscal year. = 公司将在下一个财政年度预先整合其业务。
We will preconsolidate our marketing strategies for the new product launch. = 我们将为新产品的发布预先整合我们的营销策略。

虚拟语气:
If we had preconsolidated our resources, we would have been more successful. = 如果我们预先整合了资源，我们会更成功。
It would be wise to preconsolidate our efforts before launching the project. = 在启动项目之前预先整合我们的努力将是明智之举。

被动语态:
The debts were preconsolidated by the company to improve its credit rating. = 公司预先合并了债务以提高信用评级。
The data will be preconsolidated before being sent to the server. = 数据将在发送到服务器之前预先整合。

疑问句:
Will the company preconsolidate its departments before the restructuring? = 公司会在重组之前预先合并部门吗？
Have they preconsolidated their financial statements? = 他们已经预先合并了财务报表了吗？

否定句:
They did not preconsolidate their holdings before the market crash. = 他们在市场崩盘之前没有预先整合他们的资产。
We will not preconsolidate our marketing efforts without further analysis. = 我们不会在没有进一步分析的情况下预先整合我们的营销工作。
