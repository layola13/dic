out-laboring: /ˌaʊtˈleɪbərɪŋ/ | v. |  超过…的工作量;胜过…的工作 |  To surpass in labor or effort; to outdo in work. This is a relatively uncommon word, primarily used in a comparative sense.  It emphasizes exceeding someone else's work output or effort.


例句：

The new machine out-labored the older model by a significant margin. = 新机器的工作效率比旧机器高出很多。

He out-labored his colleagues, consistently exceeding their productivity targets. = 他比他的同事们工作更努力，始终超过他们的生产目标。


近义词：outwork, surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform

反义词：underperform, lag behind, fall short


词性:动词
