inquirer:/ɪnˈkwaɪərər/ | n. | 询问者；调查者；探究者 | A person who asks questions or makes inquiries; someone who investigates.  | Example: The police interviewed several  inquirers about the robbery.  警方采访了几位关于抢劫案的询问者。 |近义词: questioner, investigator, enquirer |反义词: respondent, answerer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inquirer asks many questions. = 询问者问了很多问题。
He inquires about the price. = 他询问价格。


一般过去时:
The inquirer asked a crucial question. = 询问者问了一个关键问题。
She inquired about the lost package. = 她询问了丢失的包裹。


现在完成时:
The inquirer has asked all the important questions. = 询问者已经问了所有重要的问题。
He has inquired about the job opening several times. = 他已经多次询问了职位空缺。


过去完成时:
The inquirer had already asked that question. = 询问者已经问过那个问题了。
She had inquired about the meeting time before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，她已经询问过会议时间。


将来时:
The inquirer will ask more questions later. = 询问者稍后会问更多问题。
He will inquire about the delivery status tomorrow. = 他明天将询问送货状态。


虚拟语气:
If the inquirer were here, he would know the answer. = 如果询问者在这里，他会知道答案。
I wish the inquirer had asked clearer questions. = 我希望询问者问得更清楚一些。


被动语态:
Many questions were asked by the inquirer. = 询问者问了很多问题。(被动语态，更强调问题被问)
The price was inquired about by the customer. = 客户询问了价格。(被动语态，更强调价格被询问)


疑问句:
Is the inquirer satisfied with the answers? = 询问者对答案满意吗？
Who is the inquirer? = 询问者是谁？


否定句:
The inquirer did not ask any further questions. = 询问者没有再问任何问题。
He did not inquire about the details. = 他没有询问细节。


PHRASE->
curious inquirer  好奇的询问者
persistent inquirer  坚持不懈的询问者
thorough inquirer  彻底的询问者
meticulous inquirer  一丝不苟的询问者
knowledgeable inquirer  知识渊博的询问者

词源:
The word "inquirer" comes from the verb "inquire," which is derived from the Old French word "enquérir," meaning "to seek information, to make an inquiry."  This in turn has roots in the Latin word "inquirere," which combines the prefix "in-" (into) and the verb "quirere" (to seek).
