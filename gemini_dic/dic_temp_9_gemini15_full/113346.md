luxuriety: /lʌɡˈʒʊəriəti/ | n. |奢侈；豪华；奢侈品 |The state of great comfort and extravagant living.  It often refers to something expensive and luxurious.  |例句：The hotel offered unrivaled luxuriety and comfort.  这家酒店提供无与伦比的奢华和舒适。

例句：She lived a life of great luxuriety, surrounded by expensive possessions. 她过着奢华的生活，周围环绕着昂贵的物品。


近义词：luxury, opulence, extravagance, lavishness |反义词：austerity, frugality, simplicity


词源:  The word "luxuriety" is derived from the Latin word "luxuria," meaning "luxuriousness," "abundance," or "excess."  It's a less common variant of "luxury," sharing the same root and essentially the same meaning.
