knucklehead:/ˈnʌkəlˌhed/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A knucklehead is a foolish or stupid person.  It is an informal term. |例句：He's such a knucklehead; he forgot his own birthday. 他真是个笨蛋，竟然忘了自己的生日。

例句：Don't be a knucklehead and leave the door unlocked. 不要傻乎乎地把门锁着。


近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, moron, blockhead

反义词：genius, intellectual, wise, clever, bright

词性:名词
