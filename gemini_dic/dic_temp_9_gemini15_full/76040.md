furler's:/ˈfɜːrlərz/| n.|卷帆器，卷索器|A furler is a device used to furl (roll up) a sail or other flexible item, such as a cable or hose.|例句：The boat was equipped with a furling system for easy sail handling. 这艘船配备了卷帆系统，方便操控帆。  The furler on the mainsail jammed, requiring manual intervention. 主帆上的卷帆器卡住了，需要人工干预。|近义词：roller, winder|反义词：unfurler
