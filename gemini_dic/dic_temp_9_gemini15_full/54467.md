disintegrative:/dɪsɪnˈtɛɡrətɪv/| adj.| 瓦解的；崩溃的；解体的 | Tending to disintegrate; causing disintegration.  It describes something that is breaking down or falling apart, either physically or metaphorically. |例句：The disintegrative effects of the war were felt throughout the country.  战争的瓦解效应波及全国。

例句：His disintegrative personality led to the collapse of his relationships. 他支离破碎的性格导致了他的关系破裂。

近义词：destructive, disruptive, decomposing, decaying

反义词：integrative, unifying, cohesive, constructive

词性：形容词
