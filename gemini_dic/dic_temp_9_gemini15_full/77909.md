genista:/dʒəˈnaɪstə/| n. |金雀花属植物|A genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae, commonly known as brooms.  They are characterized by their showy, pea-like flowers and often spiny branches. Many species are cultivated as ornamentals.|The genista bushes were ablaze with yellow flowers.|近义词：broom (某些种类),  Cytisus (某些种类)|反义词:无
