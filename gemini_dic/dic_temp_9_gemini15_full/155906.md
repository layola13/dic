photochromy:/ˈfoʊtoʊkroʊmi/| n. |光致变色；感光变色|The reversible change of color in a substance when exposed to light, often used in self-darkening lenses.|例句：Photochromy is the principle behind many self-darkening sunglasses.  光致变色是许多自动变色太阳镜背后的原理。|例句：The research team is exploring new materials for improved photochromy in eyewear. 研究小组正在探索用于改进眼镜光致变色的新材料。|近义词：phototropism (光致向性，指植物对光的反应，并非颜色变化)|反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑“无光致变色”等表达)|名词
