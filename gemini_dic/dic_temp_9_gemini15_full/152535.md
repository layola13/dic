pc:/piːˈsiː/| n.|个人电脑；个人计算机|A personal computer is a small, relatively inexpensive computer designed for individual use.|例句：I bought a new pc last week. 我上周买了一台新电脑。|近义词：computer, personal computer|反义词：mainframe, supercomputer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My pc runs Windows 10. = 我的电脑运行的是Windows 10系统。
This pc is very fast. = 这台电脑很快。


一般过去时:
My old pc crashed yesterday. = 我的旧电脑昨天崩溃了。
We used to have a pc in the classroom. = 我们过去教室里有一台电脑。


现在完成时:
I have had this pc for three years. = 我已经拥有这台电脑三年了。
She has upgraded her pc several times. = 她已经升级过好几次电脑了。


过去完成时:

将来时:
