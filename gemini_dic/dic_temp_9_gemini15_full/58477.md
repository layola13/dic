duded:/duːd/| interj.|家伙，哥们儿(俚语)|An informal term of address used among close friends, typically males.  It's a shortened and slightly altered version of "dude."|例句：Hey, duded, what's up? 嘿，哥们，怎么了？  Duded, that's awesome!  哥们，太棒了！|近义词：dude, man, pal, buddy|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
This word is primarily used in informal speech and does not have verb, noun, or adjective forms. Therefore, grammatical tenses, voice, or negation do not apply.  It functions solely as an interjection.


PHRASE->
This word doesn't readily form phrases in the same way as verbs or nouns. Its use is limited to its interjectional function.  However, it can be used with other informal phrases:

Duded, seriously? = 哥们，说真的？
Duded, no way! = 哥们，不可能！
Duded, I'm telling you. = 哥们，我跟你说。
What's up, duded? =  哥们，最近怎么样？
That's crazy, duded! =  太疯狂了，哥们！
