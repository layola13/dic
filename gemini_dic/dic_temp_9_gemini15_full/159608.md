polling:/ˈpoʊlɪŋ/| n. v.|民意调查；投票；轮询；定期检查|The act of conducting a poll or survey to gather opinions or data from a sample population.  It can also refer to the act of regularly checking or testing something. |例句：The polling station was busy on election day.  选举日投票站很忙。|例句：The system performs regular polling of the sensors. 系统定期轮询传感器。|近义词：survey, referendum, sounding, canvassing |反义词：census (in the sense of a complete count)|名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We are polling voters on their opinions about the new law. = 我们正在就选民对新法律的意见进行民意调查。
The system polls the server every minute. = 系统每分钟轮询服务器一次。

一般过去时:
They polled the public on their views regarding the proposed tax increase. = 他们就公众对拟议增税的看法进行了民意调查。
The engineer polled the network devices to check their status. = 工程师轮询网络设备以检查其状态。

现在完成时:
We have polled over 1000 people for this survey. = 我们已经为此调查采访了1000多人。
The monitoring system has been polling the temperature sensors continuously. = 监控系统一直在持续轮询温度传感器。

过去完成时:
They had polled the students before making the final decision. = 他们在做出最终决定之前已经对学生进行了民意调查。
The program had already polled the database before the error occurred. = 在发生错误之前，程序已经轮询了数据库。

将来时:
We will poll the employees about their satisfaction with the new benefits package. = 我们将对员工就他们对新的福利方案的满意度进行民意调查。
The automation system will poll the sensors every second. = 自动化系统将每秒轮询传感器一次。

虚拟语气:
If we polled the entire population, we would have a more accurate result. = 如果我们对整个人口进行民意调查，我们将得到更准确的结果。
It would be helpful if the system polled the sensors more frequently. = 如果系统更频繁地轮询传感器，将会有所帮助。

被动语态:
The voters were polled about their preferred candidate. = 选民们被问及他们最喜欢的候选人。
The network devices are polled regularly for performance monitoring. = 网络设备定期进行轮询以进行性能监控。

疑问句:
Will you be polling the community on this issue? = 你会就这个问题对社区进行民意调查吗？
How often does the system poll the database? = 系统多久轮询一次数据库？

否定句:
We did not poll the residents before starting construction. = 我们在开始施工前没有对居民进行民意调查。
The monitoring system does not poll the sensors during power outages. = 监控系统在停电期间不轮询传感器。
