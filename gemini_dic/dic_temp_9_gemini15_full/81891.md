griff:/ɡrɪf/| n.|狮鹫，鹰头狮身怪兽；(非正式) 抓住，握住；(苏格兰) 粗暴的人；|A griffin is a mythical creature with the head and wings of an eagle and the body of a lion.  The word can also informally refer to grasping or seizing something, or in Scotland, to a rough or surly person.|例句：The griffin on the coat of arms was magnificent.  纹章上的狮鹫非常雄伟。|例句：He had a firm griff on the rope. 他紧紧抓住绳子。|近义词：mythical creature, monster, beast|反义词：N/A
