hammerer's: /ˈhæmərərz/ | n. | 打锤者(的)；锤击者(的) |  The word "hammerer's" is a possessive form of the noun "hammerer," indicating belonging or association with a hammerer.  It describes something that belongs to, is associated with, or is done by a person who hammers.  For example, "hammerer's tools" would refer to the tools used by someone who hammers. |  例句：The hammerer's hands were calloused from years of work.  (打锤工人的手因多年的工作而长满了老茧。)  The hammerer's strike was powerful and precise. (打锤工人的一击既有力又精确。) | 近义词：smith's (if referring to a blacksmith),  metalworker's | 反义词：N/A (possessive form doesn't have antonyms) |  词性：名词(所有格)
