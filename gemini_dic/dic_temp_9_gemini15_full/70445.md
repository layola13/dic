figuratively:/ˈfɪɡjʊrətɪvli/| adv.|比喻地；象征性地|In a figurative way; not literally.  Figurative language uses figures of speech, such as metaphors and similes, to create imagery and express ideas indirectly.  |例句：He was figuratively drowning in paperwork. 他被文件淹没了。(比喻)

例句：She figuratively soared to the top of her field. 她事业上获得了巨大的成功。(比喻)


近义词：symbolically, metaphorically, poetically|反义词：literally, exactly
