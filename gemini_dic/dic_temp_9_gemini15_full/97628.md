inherence:/ɪnˈhɪərəns/| n. |内在性；固有性；内在属性|The inherent nature or quality of something; something that is a permanent, essential, or characteristic part of something else.  It emphasizes the intrinsic nature of a quality, rather than something acquired or added. |例句：The inherence of beauty in nature is undeniable. 自然之美是不可否认的。


The inherence of risk is a crucial factor in investment decisions. 风险的内在性是投资决策中的一个关键因素。


近义词：intrinsic nature, inherent quality, essential attribute, integral part|反义词：extrinsic, external, acquired, accidental
