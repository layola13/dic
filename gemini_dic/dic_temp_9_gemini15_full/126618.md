nail-biter:/ˈneɪlˌbaɪtər/ | n. |令人紧张的事物；令人焦虑的比赛或事件|A nail-biter is something that causes great excitement and suspense, especially a close competition or event where the outcome is uncertain until the very end.  It refers to the act of biting one's nails due to nervousness. |例句：The election was a real nail-biter; the results were too close to call until the last minute.  这场选举真是让人捏一把汗；直到最后一刻结果才尘埃落定。

The final game of the championship was a nail-biter, keeping everyone on the edge of their seats.  冠军赛的最后一场比赛让人紧张万分，让每个人都屏住呼吸。|近义词：thriller, cliffhanger, heart-stopper |反义词：predictable event, anticlimactic event |名词
