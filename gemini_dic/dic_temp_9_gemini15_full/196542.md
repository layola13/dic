sesqui:/ˈsɛskwaɪ/| adj.| 一又二分之一的；倍半的 |  From Latin *sesqui-, combining form of sesqui- "one and a half," from *semis "half" + unus "one."  It indicates "one and a half times" or "one and a half."  The word is used as a prefix to form compound words. |例句：A sesquicentennial celebration marks 150 years.  (一百五十周年庆典)


A sesquipedalian word is a very long word. (一个长篇大论的词)
