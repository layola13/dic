grappas:/ˈɡræpɑːz/| n.|意大利白兰地酒|A grappa is an Italian brandy made from the pomace (skins, seeds, and stems) of grapes after winemaking.  It's known for its strong flavor and high alcohol content.| Example:  He enjoyed a glass of grappa after dinner. 他晚餐后享用了一杯意大利白兰地。|近义词：brandy, eau-de-vie|反义词：无
