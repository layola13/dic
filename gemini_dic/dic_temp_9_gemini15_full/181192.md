reinjured:/ˌriːˈɪndʒərd/| v.|再次受伤；复发|To injure (a previously injured part of the body) again.  This verb implies a previous injury to the same body part.|例句：He reinjured his knee during the game. 他在比赛中再次伤了他的膝盖。
例句：After recovering from a broken leg, she reinjured it while skiing. 在腿骨折痊愈后，她在滑雪时再次受伤了。|近义词：re-wound, re-hurt|反义词：heal, recover


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He reinjures his ankle frequently. = 他经常扭伤脚踝。
They reinjure themselves during training. = 他们在训练中再次受伤。

一般过去时:
She reinjured her shoulder lifting weights. = 她举重时再次伤了肩膀。
He reinjured his back while gardening. = 他在园艺时再次伤了背。


现在完成时:
I have reinjured my wrist three times this year. = 今年我已经三次扭伤了手腕。
She has reinjured her knee playing basketball. = 她打篮球时再次伤了膝盖。


过去完成时:
He had reinjured his hamstring before the final race. = 在决赛之前，他已经再次拉伤了腿筋。
She had reinjured her ankle and couldn't continue the hike. = 她已经再次扭伤了脚踝，无法继续远足。


将来时:
I fear I will reinjure my knee if I run too soon. = 我担心如果我太早跑步会再次伤到膝盖。
He thinks he might reinjure his shoulder if he lifts heavy weights again. = 他认为如果再次举重可能会再次伤到肩膀。


虚拟语气:
If I had listened to the doctor, I wouldn't have reinjured my foot. = 如果我听了医生的建议，我就不会再次伤到脚了。
I wish I hadn't reinjured my back lifting that box. = 我真希望我没有搬那个箱子而再次伤到背。


被动语态:
His ankle was reinjured during the match. = 他的脚踝在比赛中再次受伤了。
The knee was reinjured in a fall. = 膝盖在一次跌倒中再次受伤了。


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't reinjure his arm. = 他没有再次伤到胳膊。
