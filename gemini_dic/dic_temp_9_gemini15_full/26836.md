cahoots:/kəˈhuːts/| n.|同谋；勾结；串通|Secret or conspiratorial cooperation or partnership, often for an illegal or illicit purpose.  The term implies a hidden or clandestine nature to the collaboration.|例句：They were in cahoots to defraud the company. 他们串通起来欺骗公司。

例句：The two politicians were suspected of being in cahoots. 这两位政治家涉嫌串通一气。


近义词：conspiracy, collusion, collaboration, partnership (in a negative context)

反义词：openness, transparency, honesty, independence


词性:名词
