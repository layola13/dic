词源:
mis- (错误的) + construction (建造，解释)  The word combines the prefix "mis-" indicating a wrong or bad action, with "construction," which can refer to the act of building something or interpreting something.  Therefore, "misconstruction" literally means a wrong interpretation or understanding.
