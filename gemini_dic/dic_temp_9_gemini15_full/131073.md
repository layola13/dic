nonbeatific:/ˌnɒnbiːˈæfɪk/| adj. |非幸福的，不快乐的 |Not characterized by bliss or blessedness; unhappy or unhappy-making.  It suggests a lack of the serene joy or happiness often associated with religious or spiritual experiences.  |The nonbeatific expression on his face suggested deep worry.  他的脸上没有幸福的表情，暗示着深深的担忧。|近义词：unhappy, miserable, unhappy, glum, sorrowful |反义词：beatific, blissful, joyful, happy
