penannular:/ˌpɛnænˈjʊlər/ | adj. |近乎环状的，半环形的 |Almost ring-shaped; having the form of a ring that is not quite closed.  The term often describes archaeological finds, particularly jewelry or tools. | Example: The archaeologist unearthed a penannular brooch, a characteristic piece of Celtic jewelry. 考古学家发现了一个半环形胸针，这是凯尔特珠宝的典型作品。|近义词：semicircular, incomplete, partial |反义词：circular, complete, full
