immortelle: /ˌɪmɔːrˈtɛl/ | n. |不凋花，长生花| a flower that retains its color and form even after being picked and dried, symbolizing immortality.  Immortelles are often used in dried flower arrangements and crafts. |例句：The immortelle flowers in the bouquet still looked vibrant weeks after being picked.  这束花中的不凋花在采摘几周后仍然看起来鲜艳。|近义词：everlasting flower, strawflower |反义词：wilting flower, ephemeral bloom
