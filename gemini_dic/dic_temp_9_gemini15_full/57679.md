drawl:/drɔːl/| /drɑːl/| vt.& n.|慢吞吞地说，拖长音调说；缓慢的语调|to speak slowly with prolonged vowels, especially in a Southern US accent.  A drawl is a slow, relaxed way of speaking characterized by the lengthening of vowels.|例句：He drawled his answer, making it hard to understand. 他慢吞吞地回答，让人难以理解。

例句：The Texan drawl was unmistakable. 得克萨斯州的慢吞吞的语调是无法误认的。

近义词：slur, drag, drone|反义词：rush, hurry, mumble|动词，名词
