revalue:/ˌriːˈvæljuː/| vt.|重新估价；重新评估；再评价 |To reassess the value of something, typically an asset or currency.|例句：The company decided to revalue its assets after the market downturn.  公司决定在市场低迷后重新评估其资产。|例句：The government is considering revaluing the national currency. 政府正在考虑重新评估国家货币。|近义词： reassess, reappraise, reevaluated |反义词： undervalue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They revalue their inventory annually. = 他们每年重新评估他们的库存。
The bank revalues its foreign currency holdings daily. = 银行每天重新评估其外币资产。


一般过去时:
The company revalued its property last year. = 公司去年重新评估了其房产。
The government revalued the currency in 1990. = 政府在1990年重新评估了货币。


现在完成时:
They have revalued their assets several times this year. = 他们今年已经几次重新评估了他们的资产。
The central bank has revalued the currency to combat inflation. = 中央银行已经重新评估了货币以对抗通货膨胀。


过去完成时:
They had already revalued the equipment before the audit. = 审计之前他们已经重新评估了设备。
The company had revalued its shares before the merger. = 公司在合并之前已经重新评估了其股票。


将来时:
They will revalue the property next month. = 他们下个月将重新评估房产。
The government will revalue the currency if inflation rises too high. = 如果通货膨胀过高，政府将重新评估货币。


虚拟语气:
If they revalued the assets now, the result would be different. = 如果他们现在重新评估资产，结果将会不同。
It would be wise if the company revalued its intellectual property. = 如果公司重新评估其知识产权将是明智的。


被动语态:
The assets were revalued by an independent auditor. = 资产由独立审计师重新评估。
The currency will be revalued by the central bank. = 货币将由中央银行重新评估。


疑问句:
Will the company revalue its stock options? = 公司会重新评估其股票期权吗？
Have they revalued the land recently? = 他们最近重新评估了土地吗？


否定句:
The company did not revalue its assets last year. = 公司去年没有重新评估其资产。
They haven't revalued the property yet. = 他们还没有重新评估房产。
