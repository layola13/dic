recategorizing:/ˌriːˈkætəɡəraɪzɪŋ/| v.|重新分类，重新归类 |The act of assigning something to a different category or group.  It implies a systematic reassessment and reallocation based on new information or criteria. |例句：The librarian is recategorizing the books according to the new Dewey Decimal System. 图书管理员正在根据新的杜威十进制系统重新分类书籍。


例句：The company is recategorizing its products to better target specific customer demographics.  公司正在重新分类其产品，以更好地针对特定客户群体。


近义词：re classifying, reclassifying, reorganizing, reordering


反义词：categorizing (without implying change), maintaining the same categorization


词性：动词(现在分词)
