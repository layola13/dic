faculty:/ˈfækəlti/| n.|能力；全体教员；系；学院|The mental or physical power to do something.  A body of teachers in a department of a university or college. A natural aptitude or skill.|例句：He has a faculty for languages. 他有语言天赋。|例句：The faculty of science is located in the new building. 理学院位于新楼。|近义词：ability, skill, staff, department|反义词：inability, weakness
