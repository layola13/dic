reconfigure:/ˌriːkənˈfɪɡjər/| vt.|重新配置；重新组态|To change the structure or organization of something, especially a system or device.|例句：The IT department will reconfigure the network to improve speed.  IT 部门将重新配置网络以提高速度。
例句：We need to reconfigure the software to meet the new requirements. 我们需要重新配置软件以满足新的需求。|近义词：rearrange, reorganize, restructure|反义词：maintain, preserve
