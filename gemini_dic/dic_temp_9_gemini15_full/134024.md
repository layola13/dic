nonhumans:/ˈnɑːnˌhjuːmənz/| n. |非人类，动物|  Nonhumans refers to all living beings that are not human. This term is often used in scientific contexts, particularly in fields like zoology, ecology, and animal behavior, to encompass all animals, plants, and other organisms excluding humans.  |Example: The study focused on the social interactions of nonhumans in their natural habitat. 这项研究关注的是非人类在其自然栖息地中的社会互动。|近义词：animals, creatures, beings|反义词：humans, people
