dis-contentedly:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdli/| adv. |不满地，不高兴地|Expressing or characterized by discontent; unhappily.  It describes the manner in which something is done, reflecting dissatisfaction or unhappiness.|He grumbled discontentedly about the long wait.|近义词：unhappily, grumblingly, resentfully | 反义词：happily, contentedly, cheerfully
