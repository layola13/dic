principling:/ˈprɪnsɪplɪŋ/ | v. | 原则化；基于原则的行动 |  The act of applying principles; basing actions or decisions on principles.  It often implies a systematic or rigorous application of principles, sometimes to the point of rigidity. |  He approached the problem with rigorous principling, refusing to compromise on his core beliefs. 他以严格的原则性来处理这个问题，拒绝在核心信仰上妥协。 The company's principling in its environmental policy is commendable.  这家公司在其环保政策中的原则性值得称赞。 |  principled,  rule-based | unprincipled, arbitrary, capricious
