mous:/maʊs/| n. |老鼠 |A small rodent that typically has a pointed snout, relatively large ears, and a long tail.  Mouses are often considered pests. |例句：I saw a mouse in the kitchen. 我在厨房里看到一只老鼠。|例句：The cat caught a mouse. 猫抓到了一只老鼠。|近义词：rodent, vermin |反义词：cat (predator) |名词
