drama:/ˈdrɑːmə/ /ˈdræmə/| n.|戏剧；戏剧性事件；引人入胜的事|a play intended for the theatre; a serious of exciting events; something that is exciting or interesting|The play was a gripping drama. 这出戏是一部扣人心弦的戏剧。
The events of the past week have been full of drama.  过去一周的事件充满了戏剧性。|play,  theater,  show, spectacle|comedy,  routine|noun
