chaise-longue:/ʃeɪz ˈlɒŋ/| n. |躺椅，长沙发|A chaise-longue is a long, upholstered chair, typically with a curved backrest that supports the head and shoulders, often used for reclining.  |例句：She relaxed on the chaise-longue by the window, reading a novel. 她躺在窗边的躺椅上，读着一本小说。  |近义词：recliner, daybed|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a stiff, upright chair.
