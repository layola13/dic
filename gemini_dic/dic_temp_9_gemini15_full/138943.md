nuttiness:/ˈnʌtinɪs/| n. |愚蠢；古怪；疯狂；坚果味|The quality of being nutty; silliness, craziness, or eccentricity. It can also refer to the flavor of nuts.|例句：His nuttiness was legendary among his friends. 他古怪的性格在朋友中是出了名的。
例句：The nuttiness of the peanut butter added a unique flavor to the cake. 花生酱的坚果味为蛋糕增添了独特的风味。|近义词：silliness, craziness, eccentricity, absurdity, lunacy|反义词：sanity, sense, rationality, normalcy|词性:名词
