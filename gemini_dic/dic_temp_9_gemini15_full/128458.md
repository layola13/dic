newsflashes: /ˈnuːzˌflæʃɪz/ | n. |新闻快讯，突发新闻 | Brief reports of important news, often broadcast unexpectedly.  Newsflashes typically cover breaking news events that require immediate attention. |

例句：

The newsflashes announced the sudden resignation of the prime minister. 新闻快讯宣布了首相的突然辞职。

Several newsflashes interrupted the regular programming to report on the developing storm.  几条新闻快讯打断了正常的节目播放，报道正在发展的风暴。


近义词： breaking news, bulletins, alerts


反义词： in-depth reports, background information


词性: 名词
