nonevangelical: /ˌnɒnˌɪˌvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/ | adj. |非福音派的；非基督教福音派的 | Not associated with evangelical Christianity; not belonging to or characteristic of evangelicalism.  This term describes individuals, groups, or movements that do not adhere to the tenets or practices of evangelical Christianity.  It often implies a more liberal or less traditional approach to religious belief and practice. | Example: The nonevangelical churches in the city held a joint service for peace.  这个城市里的非福音派教会为和平举行了一次联合礼拜。  | Near synonyms: non-evangelical, liberal (in a religious context), mainline (Protestant) | Antonyms: evangelical, fundamentalist |
