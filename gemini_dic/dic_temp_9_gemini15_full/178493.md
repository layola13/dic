recontemplating:/ˌriːkənˈtɛmpleɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新考虑，再思考 |To think again about (something); reconsider.  |例句：After careful consideration, I am recontemplating my decision to move.  在仔细考虑之后，我正在重新考虑搬家的决定。

例句：She spent hours recontemplating her options before making a final choice. 她花了数小时重新考虑她的选择，然后才做出最终决定。


近义词：reconsidering, reviewing, rethinking, re-evaluating

反义词：deciding, concluding, resolving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is recontemplating his career path. = 他正在重新考虑他的职业道路。
They are recontemplating their travel plans. = 他们正在重新考虑他们的旅行计划。


一般过去时:
She recontemplated her life choices. = 她重新思考了她的人生选择。
He recontemplated his decision to quit his job. = 他重新考虑了他辞职的决定。


现在完成时:
I have recontemplated my options many times. = 我已经多次重新考虑了我的选择。
They have recontemplated their investment strategy. = 他们已经重新考虑了他们的投资策略。


过去完成时:
She had recontemplated her relationship before ending it. = 在结束这段关系之前，她已经重新考虑过。
He had recontemplated his future before making a major life change. = 在做出重大人生改变之前，他已经重新考虑过他的未来。


将来时:
I will recontemplate my answer after further research. = 我会在进一步研究之后重新考虑我的答案。
They will recontemplate their offer after receiving feedback. = 他们会在收到反馈后重新考虑他们的提议。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would recontemplate my decision. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新考虑我的决定。
I wish I had recontemplated my actions before. = 我希望我之前能重新考虑我的行为。


被动语态:
The proposal is being recontemplated by the committee. = 该提案正在由委员会重新考虑。
His application was recontemplated after new information surfaced. = 在出现新的信息后，他的申请被重新考虑了。


疑问句:

否定句:
He is not recontemplating his retirement plans. = 他并没有重新考虑他的退休计划。
She did not recontemplate her decision, despite the new information. = 尽管有了新的信息，她也没有重新考虑她的决定。
