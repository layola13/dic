saccharinely:/ˈsækərɪnli/| adj.|甜腻地；过分甜美地；虚假地甜美地|Excessively sweet or sentimental in a way that is not genuine or sincere; affectedly sweet.|例句：She spoke saccharinely about her love for her ex-husband, which felt disingenuous given their bitter divorce. 她甜腻地谈论着对前夫的爱，考虑到他们痛苦的离婚，这感觉不真诚。|例句：The overly saccharine music made the romantic scene feel cheesy and unconvincing.  过于甜腻的音乐使得浪漫场景感觉俗套且缺乏说服力。|近义词：sweetly, cloyingly, sentimentally, affectedly|反义词：bitterly, harshly, frankly, sincerely
