cocounseling:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/| n. |共同咨询，互助辅导|A form of counseling where two people, often peers, provide mutual support and guidance to each other.  It emphasizes self-help and shared responsibility.| Example:  They participated in a cocounseling session to help each other work through their relationship problems.  他们参加了一次共同咨询会议，以帮助彼此解决人际关系问题。|近义词：mutual support, peer counseling, self-help|反义词：professional therapy (in the sense that it's not conducted by a licensed professional)
