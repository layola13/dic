landman:/ˈlændmæn/ | n. |土地测量员；土地管理人员；矿业地质勘探员 | A landman is a professional who works in the oil and gas industry, primarily responsible for securing land rights and mineral leases for exploration and production. They handle legal and logistical aspects of acquiring land for energy projects. |例句：The landman negotiated a favorable lease agreement with the landowner.  土地测量员与地主协商达成了一份有利的租赁协议。|例句：Experienced landmen are in high demand in the current energy market.  经验丰富的土地测量员在当前的能源市场上非常抢手。|近义词：mineral rights negotiator, lease broker, land agent |反义词：landowner
