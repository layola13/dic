cardamoms:/ˈkɑːdəməmz/| n. |豆蔻(复数)|The dried seed pods of several plants in the ginger family, used as a spice.  They have a warm, aromatic flavor.|例句：The recipe calls for a teaspoon of ground cardamoms. 这道菜谱需要一茶匙的研磨豆蔻。|例句：She added some whole cardamoms to the simmering curry. 她往炖着的咖喱里加了一些整颗豆蔻。|近义词：spice, aromatic seeds|反义词：无
