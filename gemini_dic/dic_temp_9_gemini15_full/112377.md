losers:/ˈluːzərz/| n.|失败者，输家|People who have not succeeded in a particular competition, situation, or activity.|例句：The losers of the election conceded defeat.  选举的失败者承认了失败。

例句：He felt like a loser after failing the exam.  考试不及格后，他觉得自己像个失败者。


近义词：underachievers, failures, also-rans|反义词：winners, victors
