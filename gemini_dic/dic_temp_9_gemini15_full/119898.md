militarize:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| vt.|军事化；使具有军事性质|To give military characteristics to; to equip for or convert to military use.  The process of transforming something into a military context, often involving the deployment of troops, weapons, and military strategies.|例句：The country is rapidly militarizing its border regions. 这个国家正在迅速地将其边境地区军事化。
例句：They plan to militarize the police force. 他们计划将警察部队军事化。|近义词：weaponize, arm, fortify|反义词：demilitarize, pacifize
