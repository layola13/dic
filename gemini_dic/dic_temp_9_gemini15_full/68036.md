factorizing: /ˈfæktəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 因式分解，找出因数 | To find the factors of a number or algebraic expression.  This involves expressing the number or expression as a product of its factors.  | Example:  Factorizing 12 gives 2 x 2 x 3.  | 近义词: factoring, resolving into factors | 反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym, the opposite would be a process of combining factors)
