redrilling:/ˌriːˈdrɪlɪŋ/| n. |再钻孔，重新钻孔|The act of drilling a hole again, often to correct a previous error or to deepen an existing hole.  It can also refer to the process of repeating a drilling operation. |例句：The redrilling of the well was necessary after the initial attempt failed.  = 初次尝试失败后，需要重新钻井。

The faulty component required redrilling to fit the new connector. = 有缺陷的部件需要重新钻孔才能安装新的连接器。|近义词：reboring, reaming |反义词：filling, sealing
