ruminantia:/ˌruːmɪˈnæntiə/| n. |反刍动物|The order Ruminantia includes mammals that have a four-chambered stomach and chew their cud.  They are herbivores known for their efficient digestion of plant matter.  The process of regurgitating and rechewing food is crucial to their survival. |Example: Cows, sheep, and goats are all examples of ruminants.  奶牛、绵羊和山羊都是反刍动物的例子。|近义词: cud-chewers, herbivores |反义词: carnivores
