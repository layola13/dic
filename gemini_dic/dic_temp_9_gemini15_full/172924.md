quakingly:/ˈkweɪkɪŋli/| adv.|颤抖地，战栗地|In a way that shows trembling or shaking, often from fear or cold.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.|例句：He spoke quakingly, his voice barely a whisper. 他颤抖着说话，声音几乎是耳语。
例句：The old woman clutched her shawl quakingly as the storm raged.  老妇人颤抖地抓住她的披肩，暴风雨肆虐着。|近义词：tremulously, shakily, nervously|反义词：calmly, steadily, confidently|
