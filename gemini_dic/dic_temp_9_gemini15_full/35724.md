coccolith:/ˈkɒkəlɪθ/ | n. |颗石，球石 | A tiny calcite plate found in the cell walls of coccolithophores, a type of single-celled phytoplankton.  Coccoliths are important components of marine sediments. | Example:  The ocean floor is covered in billions of coccoliths, the remnants of microscopic algae.  海洋底部覆盖着数十亿颗颗石，是微小藻类的残骸。|近义词：calcite plate (less specific)|反义词：无
