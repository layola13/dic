semirurally:/ˌsɛmiˈrʊrəli/| adj.| 半乡村的，介于乡村和城市之间的 | Partially rural; having some characteristics of rural areas.  It describes a place or lifestyle that is neither fully urban nor fully rural, blending elements of both.  | Example: The semirural community offered a peaceful atmosphere while still being within commuting distance of the city.  这个半乡村社区提供宁静的氛围，同时仍在城市通勤范围内。 | 近义词：suburban, semi-rural | 反义词：urban, completely rural | 词性：形容词
