intice:/ɪnˈtaɪs/| vt.|引诱；诱惑；吸引|To persuade or tempt someone to do something.|The sweet smell of freshly baked bread enticed him into the bakery.  甜面包的香味把他引诱进了面包店。|The beautiful scenery enticed tourists to visit the island.美丽的风景吸引游客来岛上观光。| allure, tempt, attract | repel, deter | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sweet aroma entices customers into the shop. = 甜美的香味吸引顾客进店。
He constantly tries to entice her with gifts. = 他不断试图用礼物引诱她。


一般过去时:
The advertisement enticed many people to buy the product. = 广告吸引许多人购买该产品。
The promise of wealth enticed him to take the risk. = 致富的承诺诱使他冒险。


现在完成时:
The company has enticed many talented individuals to join. = 公司已经吸引了许多有才能的人加入。
The success of the film has enticed many sequels. = 这部电影的成功已经吸引了许多续集。


过去完成时:
They had enticed the bird into the cage with seeds. = 他们用种子诱使鸟儿进了笼子。
She had been enticed by the false promises of the salesman. = 她被推销员的虚假承诺所诱骗。


将来时:
The new marketing campaign will entice more customers. = 新的营销活动将会吸引更多顾客。
We hope the prize will entice many people to enter the competition. = 我们希望这个奖品会吸引许多人参加比赛。


虚拟语气:
If the offer were more enticing, I would accept it. = 如果提议更诱人，我会接受。
I wish they would entice him to come with us. = 我希望他们能诱使他跟我们一起去。


被动语态:
Many people were enticed by the low prices. = 许多人被低价吸引。
She felt enticed by the prospect of travel. = 她对旅行的前景感到很心动。


疑问句:
What enticed you to apply for this job? = 什么吸引你申请这份工作？
Does the prospect of a promotion entice you? = 升职的前景是否吸引你？


否定句:
Nothing could entice him to leave his home. = 什么也无法诱使他离开家。
The low salary didn't entice many applicants. = 低薪没有吸引许多申请者。



PHRASE->

entice sb. into doing sth.  引诱某人做某事
entice sb. away from sth. 引诱某人离开某事
entice sb. with sth. 用某物引诱某人
be enticed by sth. 被某事所吸引
entice sb. to do sth. 诱惑某人做某事
