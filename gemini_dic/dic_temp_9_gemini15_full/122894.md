mollycoddling: /ˈmɒlikɒdlɪŋ/ | v. |过分溺爱；娇惯 | Excessively or excessively protecting or caring for someone, often to the point of hindering their independence and development.  |例句：She accused her parents of mollycoddling her brother. 她指责她的父母过分溺爱她的弟弟。

例句：The teacher was criticized for mollycoddling the students. 老师因为过分溺爱学生而受到批评。


近义词：pampering, spoiling, coddling, overprotecting | 反义词：neglecting, ignoring, toughening up

词源:
