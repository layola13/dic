lagers:/ˈlɑːɡərz/| n. |淡色啤酒|A lager is a type of beer that is brewed at low temperatures using a bottom-fermenting yeast.  This results in a crisp, clean taste.  Many lagers are light-bodied and pale in color.|例句：He prefers lagers to ales. 他更喜欢淡色啤酒而不是艾尔啤酒。
例句：The brewery produces a wide variety of lagers, from light to dark. 这家啤酒厂生产各种各样的淡色啤酒，从淡色到深色。|近义词：beer, ale (although ales are brewed differently)|反义词：ale (in terms of brewing method)|名词
