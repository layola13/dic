reengraving:/ˌriːɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/| n.|重雕；再雕刻|The act of engraving something again, often to improve upon or correct a previous engraving.  It might involve reworking an existing engraving or creating a new engraving based on the old one.|例句：The artist undertook a reengraving of the damaged print, carefully restoring the details.  艺术家对损坏的版画进行了重雕，仔细修复了细节。


The museum commissioned a reengraving of the antique plate for better reproduction.  博物馆委托对古董版重新雕刻以获得更好的复制品。|近义词：re-etching, re-engraving, reworking|反义词：first engraving, original engraving
