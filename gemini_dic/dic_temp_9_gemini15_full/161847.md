practises:/ˈpræktɪsɪz/| v.|练习，实践，执业|To perform or carry out an activity repeatedly in order to improve one's skill or ability.  It can also refer to the regular work of a professional.|例句：She practises the piano every day. = 她每天练习钢琴。

例句：He practises law in New York. = 他在纽约执业律师。|近义词：exercise, rehearse, train, perform|反义词：neglect, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He practises yoga every morning. = 他每天早上练习瑜伽。
She practises medicine in a small town. = 她在一个小镇上行医。

一般过去时:
I practised the guitar for hours yesterday. = 我昨天练习吉他练了好几个小时。
They practised their English conversation skills. = 他们练习了他们的英语会话能力。

现在完成时:
She has practised law for over ten years. = 她执业律师已经超过十年了。
He has practised the piano since he was a child. = 他从小就练习钢琴。

过去完成时:
I had practised the speech many times before the presentation. = 我在演讲之前已经练习了很多次了。
They had practised the dance routine for weeks. = 他们已经排练了几个星期的舞蹈。

将来时:
I will practise my French before my trip to Paris. = 我会在去巴黎旅行之前练习我的法语。
She will practise her driving skills tomorrow. = 她明天将练习她的驾驶技能。

虚拟语气:
If I practised more, I would be a better musician. = 如果我多练习，我会成为一个更好的音乐家。
I wish I had practised harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更努力地练习过。

被动语态:  (Practise is rarely used in the passive voice.  A similar verb like "be practiced" would be used instead.)
The technique is practiced widely. = 这项技术被广泛应用。
The new method is being practiced by many doctors. = 许多医生正在使用这种新方法。


疑问句:
Does he practise every day? = 他每天都练习吗？
How often do you practise your instrument? = 你多久练习一次你的乐器？

否定句:
I don't practise enough. = 我练习得不够。
She didn't practise for the test. = 她没有为考试做练习。


PHRASE->
practise makes perfect = 熟能生巧
practise what you preach = 言行一致
in practice = 实际上
out of practice = 荒疏了
legal practice = 律师业务
