junkie:/ˈdʒʌŋki/| n. |瘾君子，迷恋者|A person who is addicted to a drug, especially heroin.  Someone who is excessively enthusiastic about or devoted to something.|He became a heroin junkie after losing his job. 他失业后成了海洛因瘾君子。 She's a real fitness junkie; she works out every day. 她是个十足的健身狂，每天都锻炼。|近义词：addict, devotee, enthusiast |反义词：non-addict, abstainer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a junkie. = 他是个瘾君子。
She is a chocolate junkie. = 她是个巧克力迷。


一般过去时:
He was a junkie in his youth. = 他年轻时是个瘾君子。
She was a work junkie before she retired. = 退休前她是个工作狂。


现在完成时:
He has been a junkie for many years. = 他做瘾君子很多年了。
She has become a serious coffee junkie. = 她已经变成了一个严重的咖啡瘾君子。


过去完成时:
He had been a junkie before he went to rehab. = 他去戒毒所之前是个瘾君子。
She had been a fitness junkie for years before she got injured. = 在受伤之前，她已经是个健身狂好多年了。


将来时:
He will remain a junkie unless he gets help. = 除非他得到帮助，否则他将继续做瘾君子。
She will likely become a social media junkie if she isn't careful. = 如果不小心，她很可能会成为一个社交媒体迷。


虚拟语气:
If he were not a junkie, he would have a better life. = 如果他不是个瘾君子，他会过得更好。
If she weren't a work junkie, she would have more time for her family. = 如果她不是个工作狂，她就会有更多的时间陪伴家人。


被动语态:
He was considered a junkie by many. = 许多人认为他是个瘾君子。
She is often labeled a junkie by her friends. = 她的朋友们经常把她贴上瘾君子的标签。


疑问句:
Is he a junkie? = 他是个瘾君子吗？
Is she a serious technology junkie? = 她是个严重的科技迷吗？


否定句:
He is not a junkie. = 他不是个瘾君子。
She is not a junkie; she just enjoys her hobbies. = 她不是瘾君子；她只是喜欢她的爱好。
