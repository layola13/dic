impersonate:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənˌeɪt/ | vt. |扮演；模仿；假冒 | To assume the character or appearance of; to represent or act the part of.  |例句：He impersonated the president on stage. 他在舞台上扮演总统。 |例句：The actor skillfully impersonated a famous detective.  这位演员巧妙地扮演了一位著名的侦探。|近义词：represent, mimic, simulate, enact |反义词：be oneself, be authentic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impersonates famous actors. = 他模仿著名的演员。
She impersonates a variety of characters. = 她扮演各种各样的角色。

一般过去时:
He impersonated Elvis Presley last night. = 他昨晚模仿猫王。
The comedian impersonated several politicians. = 喜剧演员模仿了几位政治家。

现在完成时:
She has impersonated many historical figures. = 她已经扮演过许多历史人物。
He has never impersonated anyone before. = 他以前从未模仿过任何人。

过去完成时:
He had impersonated the mayor before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经扮演过市长。
She had never impersonated a villain before that role. = 在那个角色之前，她从未扮演过反派。

将来时:
He will impersonate a famous singer at the concert. = 他将在音乐会上扮演一位著名的歌手。
She will impersonate a historical figure for her school play. = 她将为学校戏剧扮演一个历史人物。

虚拟语气:
If I could impersonate anyone, I would choose Shakespeare. = 如果我能模仿任何人，我会选择莎士比亚。
I wish I could impersonate a bird and fly away. = 我希望我能变成一只鸟飞走。

被动语态:
The famous singer was impersonated by many people. = 许多人都模仿这位著名的歌手。
The character was skillfully impersonated by the young actor. = 这个角色被年轻的演员巧妙地扮演了。

疑问句:
Who are you impersonating? = 你在模仿谁？
Does he often impersonate other people? = 他经常模仿别人吗？

否定句:
He doesn't impersonate anyone. = 他不模仿任何人。
She didn't impersonate the queen. = 她没有模仿女王。
