pectinate:/ˈpektɪneɪt/| adj. |梳状的；齿状的|Resembling a comb in having a row of barbs or teeth.  The term is used to describe structures with closely arranged, parallel projections, resembling the teeth of a comb.|例句：The leaves of this plant have pectinate venation. 这株植物的叶脉呈梳状排列。 The comb-like structure of the antennae is pectinate.  触角的梳状结构是梳状的。|近义词：comb-like, toothed, serrated|反义词：smooth, entire
