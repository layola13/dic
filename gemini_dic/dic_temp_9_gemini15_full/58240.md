drum:/drʌm/| n. v.|鼓；鼓声；隆隆声|A drum is a musical instrument, typically cylindrical, with a skin stretched over one or both ends, that is beaten with sticks or the hands to produce a sound.  It can also refer to a container or other things that resemble a drum in shape or sound.|例句：The drummer played a powerful beat on his drum.  鼓手在他的鼓上演奏了一个强劲的节奏。|例句：The distant thunder sounded like a drum. 远处的雷声听起来像鼓声。|近义词：percussion instrument, tympani|反义词：silence
