influential: /ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 有影响力的；有权势的 |  Having great influence on someone or something.  |例句：She is an influential figure in the art world. 她是一位在艺术界有影响力的人物。|例句：His influential book changed the way people think about education. 他那本有影响力的书改变了人们对教育的看法。|近义词：powerful, important, potent, weighty |反义词：powerless, unimportant, insignificant
