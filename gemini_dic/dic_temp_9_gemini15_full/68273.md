fakes:/feɪks/ | n. v. |伪造品；假货；谎言；伪装；伪造；假装 |英文解析：A fake is something that is made to look like something else, usually to deceive someone.  It can be a noun referring to a counterfeit object or a verb describing the act of creating a forgery or pretending. |例句：He was selling fake designer handbags. 他在卖假名牌手袋。

The artist skillfully faked the old master's signature.  艺术家巧妙地伪造了大师的签名。

近义词：counterfeits, forgeries, imitations, shams, hoaxes |反义词：genuines, originals, authentic
