half-volley:/ˈhɑːfˌvɒli/| n. |半截球(网球、羽毛球等)|A half-volley in tennis, badminton, or other racquet sports is a shot played after the ball has bounced once but before it has reached the ground.  It's hit between a volley (a shot hit in the air before the ball bounces) and a groundstroke (a shot hit after the ball has bounced).|例句：He played a brilliant half-volley to win the point. 他打了一个精彩的半截球赢得了这一分。 He returned the serve with a powerful half-volley. 他用一个强有力的半截球回击发球。|近义词：mid-volley|反义词：volley, groundstroke|名词
