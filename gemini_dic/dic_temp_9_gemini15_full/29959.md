cavernous:/ˈkævərnəs/| adj.|巨大的，洞穴般的；空旷的，辽阔的 |  resembling a cavern; large, vast, and hollow.  |例句：The cavernous hall echoed with the sound of their voices.  巨大的大厅回荡着他们说话的声音。

例句：The old house had a cavernous living room with high ceilings.  这座老房子有一个高高的天花板的空旷的起居室。


近义词：spacious, vast, immense, huge, hollow

反义词：small, tiny, cramped, confined


词源:  From Middle English *caverneus, from Old French caverneus, from Latin caverna "cave".
