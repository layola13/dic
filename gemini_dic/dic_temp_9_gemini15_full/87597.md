heterotactous: /ˌhɛtərəʊˈtæktəs/ | adj. |异向的，反向的|  Referring to structures or arrangements in which corresponding parts are not similarly oriented or arranged.  It implies a lack of symmetry or parallelism.  The term is often used in biology and crystallography to describe the arrangement of molecules or structures within a larger system. | Example: The heterotactous arrangement of the protein subunits resulted in an asymmetric structure.  异向排列的蛋白质亚基导致了不对称的结构。| 近义词: asymmetric, dissimilar, unsymmetrical | 反义词: homotactic, symmetrical
