overintellectualizing: /ˌoʊvərɪntɛlektʃuəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度思考；过度诠释 | To overthink or overanalyze something, often to the point of obscuring its simplicity or missing the essential point.  It implies an excessive reliance on intellectual analysis to the detriment of other perspectives or common sense. |

例句：

He's overintellectualizing the situation; it's just a simple misunderstanding.  他过度思考了这种情况；这只是一个简单的误会。


She overintellectualizes her feelings, making it difficult to connect with her emotionally. 她过度诠释自己的感受，这使得很难在情感上与她产生共鸣。


近义词：overanalyze, overthink, overinterpret, intellectualize excessively


反义词：simplify,  take at face value,  intuitively understand


词性：动词 (verb)
