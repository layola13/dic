recarbonize:/ˌriːˈkɑːrbənaɪz/| vt.|重新碳化；使再碳化|To restore carbon to something, especially to increase the carbon content of steel or iron.  The process often involves heating the material in a carbon-rich environment.|例句：The blacksmith decided to recarbonize the old sword to restore its strength.  铁匠决定对旧剑进行再碳化以恢复其强度。

例句：The factory is experimenting with new methods to recarbonize the steel. 工厂正在试验新的方法来对钢铁进行再碳化。


近义词：re-carbonize, recarburize|反义词：decarbonize
