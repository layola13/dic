reemphases:/ˌriˈɛmfəsɪz/ | n. | 再强调，再次强调 | The reemphases on safety regulations are crucial for preventing accidents.  再次强调安全条例对于预防事故至关重要。 | 近义词：reinforcement, reiteration | 反义词：de-emphasis


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor reemphasizes key concepts. = 教授再次强调关键概念。
He reemphasizes the importance of punctuality. = 他再次强调准时的重要性。


一般过去时:
She reemphasized her commitment to the project. = 她再次强调了她对项目的承诺。
They reemphasized the need for collaboration. = 他们再次强调了合作的必要性。


现在完成时:
He has reemphasized his point several times. = 他已经多次重申了他的观点。
She has reemphasized the urgency of the situation. = 她已经再次强调了情况的紧急性。


过去完成时:
The speaker had reemphasized the dangers before the accident. = 演讲者在事故发生前已经再次强调了危险性。
After the initial explanation, the teacher had reemphasized the instructions. = 在最初的解释之后，老师再次强调了说明。


将来时:
The manager will reemphasize the company's goals at the meeting. = 经理将在会议上再次强调公司目标。
She will reemphasize the importance of teamwork in her next presentation. = 她将在下一次演示中再次强调团队合作的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reemphasize the deadline. = 如果我是你，我会再次强调截止日期。
It is essential that he reemphasize the significance of the findings. = 至关重要的是，他必须再次强调研究结果的重要性。


被动语态:
