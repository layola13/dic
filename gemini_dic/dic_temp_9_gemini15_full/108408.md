lawman:/ˈlɔːmæn/ | n. |执法人员，警官 |A lawman is a person who enforces the law, typically a police officer or sheriff.  Lawmen are responsible for maintaining order and upholding justice within their communities. |Example: The lawman apprehended the suspect after a high-speed chase.  这位执法人员在高速追逐后逮捕了嫌疑犯。|近义词：police officer, sheriff, peace officer, constable |反义词：criminal, outlaw
