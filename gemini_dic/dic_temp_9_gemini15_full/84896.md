hankerer:/ˈhæŋkərər/| n. |渴望者；热望者|One who has a strong desire or longing for something.  A person who hankers after something is strongly desirous of it.|He was a hankerer after fame and fortune.|近义词：yearner, craver, longers|反义词：indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hankers after a quiet life in the countryside. = 他渴望在乡下过平静的生活。
They hanker after power and influence. = 他们渴望权力和影响力。

一般过去时:
She hankered after her childhood home. = 她渴望回到她童年的家。
He hankered after the days of his youth. = 他怀念他年轻时的日子。

现在完成时:
I have hankered after a new car for months. = 我已经渴望拥有一辆新车好几个月了。
They have hankered after success for many years. = 他们多年来一直渴望成功。

过去完成时:
She had hankered after that painting for years before finally buying it. = 在最终买下那幅画之前，她已经渴望拥有它多年了。
He had hankered after a promotion before he finally received one. = 在最终获得晋升之前，他一直渴望晋升。

将来时:
I will hanker after this feeling of peace for many years to come. = 未来许多年，我都会怀念这种平静的感觉。
She will hanker after her family while she is away. = 她不在的时候，会想念她的家人。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would not hanker after a new house. = 如果我有钱，我就不会渴望拥有新房子了。
I wish I didn't hanker after things I can't afford. = 我真希望我不那么渴望买不起的东西。


被动语态:
(The word "hankerer" is rarely used passively.  The verb "hanker" is more commonly used.)


疑问句:
What do you hanker after most in life? = 你一生中最渴望的是什么？
Does he still hanker after that job? = 他还渴望得到那份工作吗？


否定句:
He doesn't hanker after material possessions. = 他不渴望物质财富。
She doesn't hanker after fame or recognition. = 她不渴望名利。


PHRASE->
hanker after: 渴望；热望
hanker for: 渴望；向往
hanker to: 渴望去做某事
long to hanker:  非常渴望
hanker and yearn:  渴望和向往
