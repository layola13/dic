coupler:/ˈkʌplər/ | n. |联轴器，耦合器，接头 | A device that connects two things, especially parts of a machine, allowing them to move together.  |例句：The mechanic replaced the faulty coupler on the engine. 机械师更换了发动机上故障的联轴器。

例句：We need a coupler to join these two sections of pipe. 我们需要一个接头来连接这两段管道。


近义词：connector, joint, link, coupling

反义词：separator, disconnector


词性：名词


词源:  The word "coupler" comes from the verb "couple," meaning "to join or link together."  This verb itself has roots in the Old French word "copuler," ultimately deriving from the Latin word "copulare," meaning "to join together."
