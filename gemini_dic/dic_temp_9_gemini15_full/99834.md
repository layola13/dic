intermit:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪt/| v.|间歇，中断|To stop for a time; cease or discontinue temporarily.|The rain intermitted for a while, allowing us to continue our hike. 雨间歇了一会儿，让我们可以继续远足。|近义词：pause, cease, discontinue, suspend|反义词：continue, persist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine intermittently makes a clicking sound. = 机器间歇性地发出咔哒声。
He intermittently checks his phone during meetings. = 他在会议期间间歇性地查看手机。


一般过去时:
The train service intermittently stopped yesterday due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，火车服务昨天间歇性地停止了。
The power intermittently failed throughout the night. = 整个晚上电力间歇性地中断。


现在完成时:
The project has intermittently progressed due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，项目进展一直断断续续。
The rain has intermittently fallen all day. = 雨今天断断续续地下了一整天。


过去完成时:
The engine had intermittently sputtered before finally giving out completely. = 发动机在最终完全熄火之前一直断断续续地发出嘶嘶声。
They had intermittently worked on the project for months before finalizing the design. = 他们在最终确定设计之前断断续续地工作了几个月。


将来时:
The lights will intermittently flicker during the storm. = 暴风雨期间灯光会间歇性地闪烁。
The factory's production will intermittently slow down next month due to maintenance. = 下个月工厂的生产由于维护将间歇性地减缓。


虚拟语气:
If the signal intermittently failed, we would have to reschedule the broadcast. = 如果信号间歇性地中断，我们就不得不重新安排广播。
I wish the traffic light didn't intermittently malfunction. = 我希望交通信号灯不会间歇性地发生故障。


被动语态:
The signal was intermittently interrupted by static. = 信号被静态间歇性地中断。
The meeting was intermittently disrupted by phone calls. = 会议被电话间歇性地打断。


疑问句:
Does the light intermittently blink? = 灯光会间歇性地闪烁吗？
Did the system intermittently crash last night? = 系统昨晚间歇性地崩溃了吗？


否定句:
The music did not intermittently stop; it played continuously. = 音乐没有间歇性地停止；它持续播放。
The machine did not intermittently malfunction; it stopped completely. = 机器没有间歇性地发生故障；它完全停止了。
