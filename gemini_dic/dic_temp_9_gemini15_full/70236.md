fico:/ˈfiːkoʊ/| n. |费斯科信用评分|A FICO score is a credit score developed by the Fair Isaac Corporation (FICO). It is used by lenders to assess the creditworthiness of individuals and is based on several factors, including payment history, amounts owed, length of credit history, new credit, and credit mix.  A higher FICO score generally indicates a lower risk to lenders. |My FICO score is 780, which is considered excellent.|近义词：credit score, credit rating|反义词：无
