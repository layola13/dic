preconclude:/ˌpriːkənˈkluːd/| vt.|预先下结论，预断|To form an opinion or make a judgment about something before having enough information to do so properly.  It often implies a negative judgment or a biased conclusion.|例句：Don't preconclude his guilt before hearing all the evidence. 不要在他所有证据都还没听完之前就预断他有罪。


例句：She preconcluded that the project would fail without even reviewing the proposal. 她甚至都没看过提案就预先断定项目会失败。


近义词：prejudge, prejudge, prejudicate, assume, presume


反义词：conclude fairly, objectively assess


词性:动词
