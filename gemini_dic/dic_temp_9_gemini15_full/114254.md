maim:/meɪm/| vt.|使残废；使受伤；严重损坏|To injure someone so severely that part of their body is permanently damaged or destroyed.  The word often implies a violent or brutal injury.|The explosion maimed several people. 爆炸使几个人残废了。
The accident maimed his left arm. 事故使他的左臂残废了.|cripple, disable, injure, wound|heal, cure, restore|动词


USAGE->

He maims his opponents in the debate. 他在辩论中使他的对手处于不利地位。
The careless driver maims many pedestrians every year.  粗心的司机每年都会致残许多行人。

一般过去时:
The war maimed countless soldiers.  战争使无数士兵残废。
