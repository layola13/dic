embattle:/ɪmˈbætəl/| vt.|使...列阵；使...处于战斗状态|To arrange (troops, etc.) in battle array; to engage in battle; to place in a position of conflict or difficulty.|例句：The general embattled his troops before the attack. 将军在进攻前将他的部队列阵。

例句：The political parties embattled each other in the media.各政党在媒体上互相攻击。


近义词：array, deploy, engage


反义词：disband, withdraw, disarm


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The general embattles his troops before every battle. = 将军每次战斗前都将他的部队列阵。
He embattles himself against the difficulties. = 他与困难作斗争。


一般过去时:
The army embattled its forces on the hill. = 军队在山上列阵。
They embattled themselves against the storm. = 他们与暴风雨搏斗。


现在完成时:
The company has embattled itself against its competitors. = 这家公司一直在与竞争对手抗衡。
They have embattled their arguments fiercely.  = 他们激烈地辩论。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had embattled themselves before the enemy's arrival. = 士兵们在敌人到来之前就已经列阵。
She had embattled her emotions throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，她一直在压制自己的情绪。


将来时:
The commander will embattle his troops at dawn. = 指挥官将在黎明时分将他的部队列阵。
We will embattle ourselves against the challenges ahead. = 我们将迎接未来的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If the king had embattled his army sooner, he might have won the war. = 如果国王早点把军队列阵，他可能就赢得了战争。
I wish I had embattled myself against my fears sooner. = 我真希望我早点克服恐惧。


被动语态:
The troops were embattled on the plains. = 部队在平原上列阵。
His reputation was embattled by the scandal. = 他的名誉因丑闻而受损。


疑问句:
Were the soldiers embattled before the attack? = 进攻前士兵们是否已列阵？
Will the opposing sides embattle themselves tomorrow? = 对立双方明天会互相攻击吗？


否定句:
The general did not embattle his troops quickly enough. = 将军没有及时将他的部队列阵。
They did not embattle themselves against the criticism. = 他们没有反驳批评。


PHRASE->
embattle oneself against = 与...对抗
embattle for = 为...而战
embattle in = 在...中列阵
embattle under = 在...指挥下列阵
embattle against the odds =  逆境中奋战


词源:
The word "embattle" comes from the Old French "en bataille," meaning "in battle array."  The prefix "en-" became "em-" in Middle English.  The word reflects the historical importance of military formations in warfare.
