cabrio:/ˈkæbriˌoʊ/ | n. |敞篷车；敞篷轿车 |A car with a removable roof.  A convertible. |例句：He bought a red cabrio for his birthday. 他买了一辆红色的敞篷车作为生日礼物。

例句：The cabrio was perfect for a sunny day drive. 这辆敞篷车非常适合在阳光明媚的日子里驾驶。


近义词：convertible, roadster |反义词：sedan, coupe


词源:  From Italian *cabriolet*, diminutive of *cabriole*, "a leap, a caper", ultimately from French *cabriolet*, perhaps influenced by *cabriole*, "a playful leap".  The original meaning referred to a type of light, two-wheeled carriage.  The automotive use is a later extension of the meaning.
