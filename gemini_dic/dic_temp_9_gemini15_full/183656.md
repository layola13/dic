/ˌriːˈriːlist/| v. |重新发行，再次发布|To release something again, typically a film, album, or software.|The band rereleased their debut album to celebrate their 20th anniversary. 乐队重新发行他们的首张专辑以庆祝他们的20周年纪念。|release, reissue|withdraw, cancel|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They rerelease the film every Christmas. = 他们每年圣诞节都重新发行这部电影。
The company rereleases its popular software annually. = 这家公司每年都会重新发行其流行的软件。

一般过去时:
The studio rereleased the classic movie last year. = 制片厂去年重新发行了这部经典电影。
They rereleased the album with bonus tracks. = 他们重新发行了这张专辑，并增加了额外曲目。

现在完成时:
They have rereleased three albums this year. = 他们今年已经重新发行了三张专辑。
The game has been rereleased on several platforms. = 这个游戏已经在多个平台上重新发行。

过去完成时:
The director had rereleased the film before the sequel was announced. = 在续集发布之前，导演已经重新发行了这部电影。
The company had rereleased the software to fix bugs before the complaints reached a critical point. = 在投诉达到临界点之前，该公司已经重新发布了该软件以修复错误。


将来时:
