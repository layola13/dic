/ˌnɑːnˌprɒlɒŋˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. |不延长；不续约；终止 |The absence of prolongations; the act or instance of not extending something, especially a contract or agreement.  Nonprolongations refer to situations where something that was previously in effect is allowed to expire without renewal or extension. This often applies to contracts, agreements, or periods of time. |Example: The company announced nonprolongations of several key contracts, leading to uncertainty among its employees. 公司宣布几份关键合同不再续约，导致员工们感到不确定。|近义词：terminations, expirations, cancellations |反义词：renewals, extensions
