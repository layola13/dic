giving-up: /ˈɡɪvɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 放弃；认输 |  The act of ceasing to try or to continue; surrendering; relinquishing.  It can also refer to the state of having ceased to try. |

例句：

He's giving up smoking. = 他正在戒烟。

After many attempts, she finally gave up. = 经过多次尝试后，她最终放弃了。


近义词：abandoning, surrendering, relinquishing, ceasing, quitting


反义词：persevering, persisting, continuing


词性: 动词(verb)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are giving up their dreams. = 他们在放弃他们的梦想。
He is giving up his job. = 他正在放弃他的工作。

一般过去时:
I gave up hope. = 我放弃了希望。
She gave up trying. = 她放弃了尝试。

现在完成时:
He has given up smoking several times. = 他已经戒烟好几次了。
They have given up on the project. = 他们已经放弃了那个项目。

过去完成时:
I had given up before he arrived. = 在他到达之前我已经放弃了。
She had given up hope long ago. = 她很久以前就放弃了希望。

将来时:
I will give up if it gets too hard. = 如果太难我会放弃。
He will give up his seat for an elderly person. = 他会把座位让给一位老人。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would give up. = 如果我是你，我会放弃。
I wish I hadn't given up so easily. = 我真希望我没有那么轻易放弃。

被动语态:  (This is less common with "giving up" as it's usually an active process.)
The project was given up due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该项目被放弃了。
The search was given up after several days. = 搜寻几天后被放弃了。

疑问句:
Are you giving up? = 你要放弃了吗？
Will he give up easily? = 他会轻易放弃吗？

否定句:
I will not give up. = 我不会放弃。
She didn't give up easily. = 她没有轻易放弃。


PHRASE->
give up hope = 放弃希望
give up trying = 放弃尝试
give up the ghost = 死去
give up smoking = 戒烟
give oneself up = 自首
