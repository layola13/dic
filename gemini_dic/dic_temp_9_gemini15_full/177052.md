reannounces:/ˌriːəˈnaʊnsɪz/| vt.|重新宣布，再次宣布|To announce something again, often after a period of time or with more emphasis.|The company reannounced its commitment to sustainability.  公司再次承诺致力于可持续发展。|The mayor reannounced his plans for the new park. 市长再次宣布了他关于新公园的计划。|announce, proclaim|withdraw, retract|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reannounces his candidacy every year. 他每年都重新宣布他的候选资格。
The company reannounces its new product line every quarter. 公司每个季度都会重新发布其新的产品线。

一般过去时:
She reannounced her decision to leave the company. 她再次宣布她要离开公司的决定。
They reannounced the concert date after a venue change. 他们更换演出场地后再次宣布了演唱会日期。

现在完成时:
The government has reannounced its commitment to economic reform. 政府已经再次承诺进行经济改革。
The school has reannounced its policy on student attendance. 学校已经再次宣布了其关于学生出勤的政策。

过去完成时:
They had reannounced the winner after a tie-breaker. 决胜局之后，他们再次宣布了获胜者。
The president had reannounced the emergency measures before the storm hit.  总统在暴风雨来临之前再次宣布了紧急措施。

将来时:
She will reannounce her plans for a world tour next month. 她将在下个月再次宣布她的世界巡回演唱计划。
The company will reannounce its financial results after the audit. 公司将在审计之后再次公布其财务业绩。

虚拟语气:
If they reannounced the policy, there would be less confusion. 如果他们重新宣布这项政策，就会减少混乱。
I wish they had reannounced the changes earlier. 我希望他们早点重新宣布这些变化。

被动语态:
The new policy was reannounced to the public. 新政策再次向公众宣布。
The winner's name was reannounced after a recount. 重新计票后再次宣布了获胜者的名字。

疑问句:
Will the company reannounce its earnings forecast? 公司会重新宣布其收益预测吗？
Has the government reannounced its position on immigration? 政府是否再次宣布其关于移民的立场？

否定句:
The spokesperson did not reannounce the product launch date. 发言人没有重新宣布产品发布日期。
The candidate did not reannounce his intention to run for office. 该候选人没有再次宣布他竞选公职的意图。
