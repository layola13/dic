peacefully:/ˈpiːsfəli/ | adj. |平静地；安详地；和睦地 |In a peaceful manner; calmly; quietly; without violence or disturbance.  |例句：The birds sang peacefully in the trees.  鸟儿在树上平静地歌唱。|例句：The family lived peacefully in the countryside.  这家人在乡下平静地生活着。|近义词：tranquilly, serenely, calmly, quietly |反义词：violently, noisily, turbulently, aggressively


USAGE->

They live peacefully together. = 他们和平地生活在一起。
