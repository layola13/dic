glochidium:/ɡləˈkɪdiəm/| n. |钩介幼虫；（某些软体动物的）钩状幼虫 |A glochidium is the larval stage of certain freshwater mussels and clams.  These microscopic larvae have tiny hooks that allow them to attach to the gills or fins of fish, where they complete their development as parasites. | Example: The glochidia of this mussel species are particularly adept at attaching to the gills of salmon.  这个贻贝物种的钩介幼虫特别擅长附着在鲑鱼的鳃上。|近义词: larva (general term)|反义词: adult mussel|名词
