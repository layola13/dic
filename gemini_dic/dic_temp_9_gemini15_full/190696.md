saurognathous: /ˌsɔːroʊˈnæθəs/ | adj. | 蜥蜴颌的；具有蜥蜴式下颌的 |  Having a jaw structure characteristic of lizards, typically with a single lower jaw bone (dentary) that articulates directly with the quadrate bone of the skull. This contrasts with the more complex jaw structure of mammals and some other groups.  |  The fossil remains showed saurognathous characteristics, indicating a reptilian ancestry.  | 近义词: reptilian-jawed (informal) | 反义词: mammal-jawed (less common, more descriptive than an antonym)
