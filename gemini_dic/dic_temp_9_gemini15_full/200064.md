sketchiest: /ˈskɛtʃiɪst/ | adj. | 最粗略的；最可疑的；最不靠谱的 |  The most unreliable, suspicious, or questionable.  It suggests something is poorly done, incomplete, or potentially dangerous.  | 例句：That's the sketchiest neighborhood in town.  = 那是城里最不靠谱的街区。


The plan seemed sketchy at best. =  这个计划充其量也只是粗略的计划。


近义词：dodgy, shady, suspicious, unreliable, questionable, dubious, suspect


反义词：reliable, trustworthy, dependable, safe, secure


词性:形容词
