scriptwriting:/ˈskrɪptˌraɪtɪŋ/| n.| 剧本创作 |The art or process of writing scripts for films, television, plays, or other media.  It involves developing storylines, characters, dialogue, and scenes to create a compelling narrative.|例句：She's studying scriptwriting at university. = 她正在大学学习剧本创作。|例句：His scriptwriting skills are exceptional. = 他的剧本创作技巧非凡。|近义词：screenwriting, playwriting|反义词：None
