pugilism:/ˈpjuːdʒɪlɪzəm/| n.|拳击；拳术|The art or practice of fighting with the fists.  Pugilism is a sport that requires skill, strength, and endurance.|例句：He was a renowned pugilist in his youth. 他年轻时是一位著名的拳击手。

例句：The match showcased the best of modern pugilism. 这场比赛展示了现代拳击的精髓。


近义词：boxing, fisticuffs|反义词：None specifically, but words implying peace or non-violence could be considered antonyms.


词源:
From Middle English pugilisme, from Late Latin pugilismus, from Latin pugil "boxer," from pugnus "fist."  The word's history reflects the long tradition of fist-fighting as a form of combat and sport.
