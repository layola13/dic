mammalians:/mæˈmeɪliən/| n. |哺乳动物|Mammals are warm-blooded vertebrate animals characterized by the presence of mammary glands in females, which produce milk to nourish their young. They are also typically characterized by hair or fur, three middle ear bones, and a neocortex region in the brain.|Example:  Humans, dogs, and whales are all examples of mammals. 人类、狗和鲸鱼都是哺乳动物的例子。|近义词：mammals|反义词：none
