repositories:/rɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːriz/| n. |存储库；知识库；资料库 | A repository is a place where things are stored.  It can refer to a physical location or a digital space.  In computing, it often refers to a central location for storing code, data, or other digital assets. |Example:  The national museum has a vast repository of historical artifacts. 国家博物馆收藏了大量的历史文物。

The company uses a cloud-based repository for storing its customer data. 这家公司使用基于云的存储库来存储其客户数据。|近义词：storehouse, archive, database |反义词：disposal, destruction
