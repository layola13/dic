/ɪmˈblɑːsəm/ | v. | 开花；蓬勃发展 | To develop and open flowers; to flourish and thrive. | The cherry trees are beginning to emblossom.  樱花树开始开花了。  The business is emblossoming with new ideas and growth. 这家公司正随着新的创意和发展而蓬勃发展。 | flourish, bloom, blossom | wither, fade | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers emblossom in spring. = 花在春天盛开。
The company emblossoms under his leadership. = 在他的领导下，公司蓬勃发展。


一般过去时:
The roses emblossomed beautifully last summer. = 玫瑰花去年夏天开得非常漂亮。
The town emblossomed with tourists during the festival. = 节日期间，小镇涌入了大量的游客。
