greenwash:/ˈɡriːnwɒʃ/ | v. |漂绿；环保虚伪宣传 | To make misleading claims about the environmental benefits of a product, service, or company in order to create a positive public image.  | Example:  The company was accused of greenwashing after it was revealed that their products were not as environmentally friendly as they claimed.  这家公司被指控进行“漂绿”宣传，因为后来发现他们的产品并不像他们声称的那样环保。|近义词：misrepresent,  deceive,  hype | 反义词：transparency, honesty
