garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/| n. |花环，花冠，花圈 |A garland is a string or circle of flowers, leaves, or other material used for decoration or adornment.  It's often used in celebrations or ceremonies. |Example: She wore a garland of roses in her hair. 她头上戴着玫瑰花环。|近义词：wreath, chaplet|反义词：None
