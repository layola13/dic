manic-depressive: /ˈmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. | 躁狂抑郁的；情感紊乱的 |  Describing a person affected by manic-depressive illness, characterized by alternating periods of mania (excessive excitement, energy, and irritability) and depression (sadness, loss of interest, and low energy).  The term is now largely replaced by "bipolar disorder". |  He suffered from manic-depressive illness, experiencing extreme highs and lows. 他患有躁狂抑郁症，经历了极度的高潮和低谷。 | 近义词：bipolar, cyclothymic | 反义词：emotionally stable, balanced
