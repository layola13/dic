perbromic: /ˌpɜːrˈbroʊmɪk/ | adj. |高溴酸的，含高溴酸根的 | Perbromic refers to compounds containing the perbromate anion (BrO₄⁻),  a relatively unstable oxyanion of bromine in its highest oxidation state (+7).  It describes the acid and its salts.  The perbromate ion itself is a powerful oxidizer. | Example: Perbromic acid is a strong oxidizing agent. 高溴酸是一种强氧化剂。|近义词：hyperbromic (less common) |反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑低价态溴的化合物如bromide.
