foraying:/fɔːˈreɪɪŋ/ | v. | 突袭；尝试；涉足 |  Making a sudden attack or incursion into enemy territory;  to make a first venture into a field of activity. | 例句：The company is foraying into the renewable energy market.  这家公司正在进军可再生能源市场。

例句：The army made a daring foray into enemy territory.  军队大胆地突袭了敌方领土。


近义词：raid, incursion, invasion, venture, attempt


反义词：retreat, withdrawal, surrender


词性：动词
