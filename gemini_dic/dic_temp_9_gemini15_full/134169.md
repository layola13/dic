nonincandescently: /ˌnɒnɪnˌkændˈɛsntli/ | adv. | 非白炽地；不发光地 |  Not incandescently; not giving off light.  The term describes a process or state that lacks the characteristic glow of incandescence.

例句：

The LEDs illuminated the room nonincandescently, providing a cool, efficient light. = LED灯非白炽地照亮了房间，提供了凉爽高效的光线。

The display screen emitted light nonincandescently, utilizing a different technology altogether. = 显示屏非白炽地发光，使用了完全不同的技术。


近义词： dimly, faintly, without incandescence

反义词： incandescently, brightly, brilliantly


词性：副词
