inventing:/ɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/ | v. |发明；创造；虚构 | The act of creating something new,  especially a device, process, or idea.  It can also refer to making something up, such as a story. |例句：She is inventing a new type of engine. 她正在发明一种新型发动机。

例句：He was inventing excuses to avoid going to work. 他在编造借口以避免去上班。


近义词：creating, devising, fabricating, concocting | 反义词：destroying, dismantling, abolishing | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are inventing new ways to communicate. = 他们正在发明新的沟通方式。
Scientists are inventing new medicines every day. = 科学家们每天都在发明新药。

一般过去时:
Edison invented the light bulb. = 爱迪生发明了电灯泡。
They invented a new game for the children. = 他们为孩子们发明了一个新游戏。

现在完成时:
She has invented a revolutionary new computer chip. = 她发明了一种革命性的新型电脑芯片。
Scientists have invented many life-saving drugs. = 科学家们发明了许多救命的药物。

过去完成时:
Before the war, they had invented a new type of weapon. = 战争爆发前，他们发明了一种新型武器。
He had invented several stories to explain his absence. = 他编造了几个故事来解释他的缺席。

将来时:
They will invent even more amazing technologies in the future. = 未来他们将发明更多令人惊叹的技术。
We hope they will invent a cure for cancer soon. = 我们希望他们很快能发明出治疗癌症的方法。

虚拟语气:
If I had invented time travel, I would have visited the dinosaurs. = 如果我发明了时间旅行，我就会去拜访恐龙。
I wish I had invented that amazing device. = 我真希望是我发明了那个神奇的装置。

被动语态:
The telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell. = 电话是由亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔发明的。
A new method for producing clean energy is being invented. = 一种生产清洁能源的新方法正在被发明。

疑问句:
Who invented the airplane? = 谁发明了飞机？
What are they inventing in the lab today? = 他们今天在实验室里发明什么？

否定句:
He didn't invent the wheel. = 他没有发明轮子。
They haven't invented a solution to this problem yet. = 他们还没有发明出解决这个问题的办法。
