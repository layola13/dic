crowd-surfer:/ˈkraʊdˌsɜːrfər/ | n. | 冲浪人群中的人 | A person who is carried by a crowd of people, typically at a concert or other large event.  They are usually lifted and passed above the heads of the crowd. | Example: The lead singer encouraged the crowd-surfers to be careful and to enjoy the experience. 主唱鼓励冲浪人群中的人小心并享受体验。|近义词：concertgoer, attendee |反义词：performer, musician
