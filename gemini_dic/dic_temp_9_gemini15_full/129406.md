nominalize:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪz/ /ˈnɑːmɪnəlaɪz/| vt.|使名词化；用名词表达|To convert (a verb, adjective, or other part of speech) into a noun.  This often involves adding suffixes like "-tion," "-ment," "-ance," or "-ness."  The process can sometimes lead to less concise or less direct writing.|


The committee decided to nominalize the verb "investigate" into the noun "investigation."  委员会决定将动词“调查”名词化成“调查”。

The report nominalized several key findings. 报告将几个关键发现名词化了。


近义词：nounify, substantivize

反义词：verbify (to convert into a verb)


词性：动词
