self-adhesive:/ˌsɛlf əˈhiːsɪv/| adj.|自粘的，粘性的|Having an adhesive backing that allows it to stick to surfaces without the need for additional adhesive.  It describes materials that stick to surfaces on their own, due to an adhesive already present on their back.|例句：The self-adhesive labels were easy to apply to the jars.  这些自粘标签很容易贴到罐子上。

例句：The poster was made with self-adhesive vinyl. 这张海报是用自粘乙烯基材料制作的。

近义词：sticky, pressure-sensitive

反义词：non-adhesive
