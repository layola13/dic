communicators:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtərz/ | n. |传播者；沟通者 |Those who effectively convey information or ideas.  People skilled in communication. |Example:  The company hired several experienced communicators to improve its public image.  这家公司聘请了几位经验丰富的沟通专家来改善其公众形象。|近义词：speakers,  intermediaries,  liaisons |反义词：isolates,  hermits
