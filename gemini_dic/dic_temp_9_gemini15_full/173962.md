quasi-sovereign:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈsɒvrən/ | adj. | 准主权的，半主权的 |  Having or exercising power that resembles but does not fully constitute sovereignty.  It implies a degree of autonomy but subject to limitations imposed by a higher authority.  | Example:  The region enjoys quasi-sovereign status within the federation. 这个地区在联邦内享有准主权地位。


| Example: Some indigenous groups maintain quasi-sovereign control over their ancestral lands.  一些土著群体对其祖先土地保持着准主权控制。


| 近义词: semi-sovereign, autonomous, self-governing | 反义词: fully sovereign, dependent, subordinate |  词性: adjective
