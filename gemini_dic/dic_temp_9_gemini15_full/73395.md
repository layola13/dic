forehorse: /ˈfɔːrhɔːrs/ | n. |领头马，先锋 |The forehorse is the horse that leads a team of horses. It is typically the strongest and most experienced horse in the team, and it sets the pace for the others.  |例句：The forehorse pulled the heavy wagon with great effort.  领头马吃力地拉动沉重的马车。

The forehorse in a team of four is typically the most powerful and experienced.  四匹马的队伍中，领头马通常是最强壮、最有经验的。


近义词：leader, lead horse | 反义词：rear horse, last horse | 词性: noun


USAGE->
This word is a noun, and as such does not have verb conjugations.  Therefore, tenses like past, present, future, perfect etc. are not applicable.  The word itself describes a state of being, a position in a team.


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "forehorse".  The word is largely used independently to describe the leading horse in a team.
