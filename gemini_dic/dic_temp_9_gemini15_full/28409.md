carbonator:/ˈkɑːrbənˌeɪtər/| n. |碳酸化器；苏打水制造机 |A device that adds carbon dioxide to liquids, typically to make carbonated drinks.  |Example:  The restaurant uses a carbonator to make its own sparkling water. 这家餐馆用碳酸化器自制苏打水。|近义词：carbonation machine, soda maker |反义词：decarbonator (removing carbon dioxide)
