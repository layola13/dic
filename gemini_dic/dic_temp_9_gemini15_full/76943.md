gapped:/ɡæpt/ | adj. | 有缺口的；有间隙的；不完整的 | Having gaps or spaces; incomplete.  |例句：The report was gapped, lacking crucial information.  这份报告有缺漏，缺少关键信息。

例句：The road was gapped due to recent flooding.  这条路由于最近的洪水而出现缺口。


近义词：incomplete, imperfect, fragmented, broken, interrupted | 反义词：complete, whole, entire, unbroken
