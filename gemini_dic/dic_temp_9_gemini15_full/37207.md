comet:/ˈkɒmɪt/ /ˈkɑːmɪt/| n. |彗星|A celestial body moving about the sun, usually in a highly eccentric orbit, that consists of a head and a tail and is often associated with a meteor shower.|例句：Halley's Comet is a famous periodic comet.  哈雷彗星是一个著名的周期性彗星。|例句：The comet streaked across the night sky, leaving a brilliant trail. 彗星划过夜空，留下了一条明亮的轨迹。|近义词：meteor|反义词: 无
