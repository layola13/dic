consort:/ˈkɒnsɔːt/ /ˈkɑːnsɔːrt/| n. v.| 配偶；协同；陪伴 | A spouse, usually a wife; to associate with; to accompany.  |例句：The queen consort sat beside the king.  王后坐在国王身边。


The king consorted with his advisors before making a decision. 国王在做决定之前与他的顾问们商议。|近义词：spouse, partner, companion, associate|反义词：enemy, opponent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consorts with the wealthy elite. 他与富有的精英们交往。
She consorts with her friends every weekend. 她每个周末都和朋友们一起玩。

一般过去时:
They consorted together in secret. 他们秘密地在一起密谋。
The prince consorted with various individuals from different social classes. 王子与来自不同社会阶层的各种人士交往。

现在完成时:
She has consorted with many influential people throughout her career.  在她整个职业生涯中，她与许多有影响力的人交往过。
He has consorted with criminals, putting himself in danger. 他与罪犯交往，把自己置于危险之中。

过去完成时:
Before the scandal, he had consorted with many corrupt officials. 在丑闻爆发之前，他与许多腐败官员交往。
They had consorted for years before their relationship became public. 在他们的关系公开之前，他们已经交往多年了。

将来时:
She will consort with her colleagues to finish the project. 她将与她的同事一起完成这个项目。
He will consort with the lawyers to prepare for the trial. 他将与律师们商议准备审判。

虚拟语气:
If she consorted with wiser people, she would avoid many mistakes. 如果她与更聪明的人交往，她就能避免许多错误。
I wish he had not consorted with such disreputable characters. 我希望他没有与那些声名狼藉的人物交往。

被动语态:
He was consorted with by many admirers. 他被许多仰慕者陪伴着。
The plan was consorted with great secrecy. 这个计划是秘密制定的。

疑问句:
Does he consort with dangerous people? 他是否与危险人物交往？
With whom does she consort regularly? 她经常与谁交往？

否定句:
She does not consort with dishonest people. 她不与不诚实的人交往。
He did not consort with them after the argument. 争吵之后，他不与他们来往了。


PHRASE->
consort with: 与…交往
royal consort: 王室配偶
consort of: …的配偶
to consort together:  共同密谋
to consort with evil: 与邪恶势力为伍


词源:
From Old French consorter, from Latin consors "partner," from com- "with" + sors, sortis "lot, fate."  The sense of "marriage partner" developed later.
