paved:/peɪvd/| adj. |铺好的；已铺设的|Covered with a hard, smooth surface, typically of stone, brick, or concrete.  The road was paved with smooth stones. 这条路是用光滑的石头铺成的。|近义词：surfaced, asphalted|反义词：unpaved, unsurfaced|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The path is paved with bricks. = 这条路是用砖铺成的。
The street is paved with asphalt. = 这条街是用沥青铺成的。

一般过去时:
They paved the driveway last week. = 他们上周铺了车道。
The workers paved the road quickly and efficiently. = 工人们又快又有效地铺好了路。

现在完成时:
They have paved the entire street. = 他们已经铺好了整条街。
The city has paved many new roads this year. = 今年市里铺设了许多新路。

过去完成时:
They had paved the road before the race began. = 比赛开始前他们已经铺好了路。
The construction crew had paved most of the highway by sunset. = 到日落时分，建筑队已经铺设了大部分高速公路。

将来时:
They will pave the new sidewalk next month. = 他们下个月将铺设新的路边人行道。
The government will pave the rural roads to improve transportation. = 政府将铺设农村道路以改善交通状况。

虚拟语气:
If they paved the road, traffic would flow more smoothly. = 如果他们铺了路，交通就会更顺畅。
I wish they had paved the parking lot; it's so bumpy. = 我真希望他们已经铺好了停车场；它太颠簸了。

被动语态:
The road was paved by a professional team. = 这条路是由一个专业的团队铺设的。
The new walkway is being paved at the moment. = 新的人行道正在铺设中。

疑问句:
Is the road paved? = 这条路铺好了吗？
Has the driveway been paved yet? = 车道铺好了吗？

否定句:
The path is not paved; it's just dirt. = 这条路没有铺好；只是泥土路。
They didn't pave the street before the festival. = 他们在节日之前没有铺路。
