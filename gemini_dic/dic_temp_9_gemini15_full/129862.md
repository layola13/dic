non-Indo-Europeans: /ˌnɑːnˈɪndoʊjʊərəpiːənz/ | n. | 非印欧语系民族 | People or groups of people whose languages do not belong to the Indo-European language family.  This term refers to a broad range of populations across the globe and emphasizes linguistic classification rather than racial or ethnic identity.  |  The genetic and cultural diversity of non-Indo-Europeans is immense.  | 近义词: non-Indo-European speakers, people of non-Indo-European descent | 反义词: Indo-Europeans | 名词
