fluter:/ˈflʌtər/| v.|飘动，拍打，激动|to move lightly and quickly or irregularly, or to make something do this; to beat rapidly, as the heart. |例句：The butterfly fluttered its wings. 蝴蝶拍打着翅膀。
The bird fluttered down from the tree. 鸟儿从树上飞了下来。|近义词：flutter, flap, vibrate, quiver|反义词：still, motionless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flag flutters in the wind. = 旗帜在风中飘扬。
The hummingbird flutters its wings rapidly. = 蜂鸟快速地拍打着翅膀。


一般过去时:
The leaves fluttered to the ground. = 叶子飘落到地上。
My heart fluttered when I saw him. = 当我看到他时，我的心跳加速了。


现在完成时:
The bird has fluttered away. = 鸟儿已经飞走了。
My heart has fluttered all day. = 我整天都心跳加速。


过去完成时:
The butterfly had fluttered away before I could take a picture. = 蝴蝶在我拍照之前就飞走了。
She had fluttered her eyelashes nervously before she spoke. = 她紧张地眨了眨眼睛才开口说话。


将来时:
The leaves will flutter in the breeze tomorrow. = 明天的微风会吹动树叶。
I think my heart will flutter when I meet her. = 我想当我见到她时，我的心跳会加速。


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew harder, the flag would flutter more dramatically. = 如果风更大，旗帜就会飘得更剧烈。
If I hadn't seen her, my heart wouldn't have fluttered so much. = 如果我没有看到她，我的心跳就不会加速那么多。


被动语态:
The flag was fluttered by the strong wind. = 旗帜被强风吹得飘动。
Her heart was fluttered by the exciting news. = 她激动人心的消息让她心跳加速。


疑问句:
Does the flag flutter in the wind? = 旗帜在风中飘动吗？
Does your heart flutter when you are nervous? = 你紧张的时候心跳会加速吗？


否定句:
The flag didn't flutter in the still air. = 旗帜在静止的空气中没有飘动。
My heart didn't flutter at all. = 我的心跳一点也没有加速。
