crumpled:/ˈkrʌmpəld/ | adj. |褶皱的；弄皱的；垮了的|Describes something that is wrinkled or creased, often due to being squeezed or crushed. It can also describe something that has lost its shape or strength, like a person who is defeated or disheartened. |例句：The old newspaper lay crumpled on the floor.  旧报纸皱巴巴地扔在地板上。
The crumpled shirt needed ironing.  那件皱巴巴的衬衫需要熨烫。

近义词：creased, wrinkled, crushed, rumpled,  

反义词：smooth, unwrinkled, ironed
