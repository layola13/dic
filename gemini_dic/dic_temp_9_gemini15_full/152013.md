patches:/ˈpætʃɪz/| n.|补丁；斑块；碎片；小块土地|A patch is a small area that is different from the area around it.  It can be a piece of material used to repair a hole, a small area of a different color or texture, or a small area of land. |例句：She sewed a patch onto her jeans to repair a hole. 她缝了一块补丁到她的牛仔裤上修补一个洞。

She had patches of freckles across her nose. 她的鼻子上有一些雀斑。|近义词：mend, repair, spot, section|反义词：whole, complete


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The gardener patches the holes in the fence. = 园丁修补围栏上的洞。
He patches up the cracks in the wall. = 他修补墙上的裂缝。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
The tailor has patched many clothes. = 裁缝已经缝补了很多衣服。

过去完成时:

They had patched the tire before continuing their journey. = 他们在继续旅程之前已经修补好了轮胎。

虚拟语气:
