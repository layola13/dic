dingbat:/ˈdɪŋbæt/| n. |傻瓜；古怪的人；(印刷术语)铅字；花体字| A dingbat is a foolish or eccentric person; also, a printer's term for a small decorative design or symbol, or a piece of type containing such a design.  |例句：He's a real dingbat; he can't even boil an egg. 他真是个傻瓜，连鸡蛋都不会煮。  |近义词：fool, idiot, eccentric, goofball |反义词：genius, intellectual, sage |名词
