counter-tide: /ˈkaʊntərtaɪd/ | n. | 逆流；反潮流 | A current or flow that opposes the main current or trend.  It refers to something that goes against the prevailing trend or opinion. | Example:  The small independent bookstore is a counter-tide in the age of e-books. 这家小型独立书店在电子书时代是一股逆流。  The artist's work is a counter-tide to the prevailing minimalist aesthetic.  这位艺术家的作品与流行的极简主义美学背道而驰。 | 近义词：countercurrent, opposing force, opposite trend | 反义词：mainstream, prevailing trend, current
