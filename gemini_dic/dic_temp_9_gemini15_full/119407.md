microfiches:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪʃiz/ | n. | 微缩胶片 | Microfiches are sheets of film containing miniaturized images of documents.  They are a form of microform used for storing and archiving large amounts of information in a compact way. | The library archives its old newspapers on microfiches. 图书馆将旧报纸保存在微缩胶片上。 |  microfilm, microform |  None |
