prematureness:/ˌpriːmətʃʊrˈnɛs/ | n. |过早；早产；不成熟 | The state of being premature; happening or arriving before the expected or usual time.  It often implies a negative consequence due to insufficient development or preparation. |例句：The prematureness of his retirement surprised everyone.  他的过早退休让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：The prematureness of the baby's birth resulted in health complications.  婴儿早产导致了健康并发症。


近义词：prematurity, hastiness, rashness, immaturity

反义词：maturity, timeliness, lateness (in the sense of being delayed)


词性：名词
