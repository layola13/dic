putrefactions:/ˌpjuːtrɪˈfækʃənz/ | n. |腐败；腐烂；分解 | The process of decay or rotting in organic matter, often accompanied by foul smell.  It involves the breakdown of complex organic molecules into simpler ones by microorganisms. |Example: The putrefactions of the corpse were evident from the strong odor.  尸体腐烂的迹象从强烈的臭味中可以看出。

The advanced state of putrefaction made identification difficult.  高度腐烂使得辨认变得困难。|近义词：decomposition, decay, rot, spoilage |反义词：preservation, conservation
