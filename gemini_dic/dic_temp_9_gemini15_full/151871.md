passive-aggressions:/ˈpæsɪv əˈɡreʃənz/ | n. | 被动攻击行为 |  The act of indirectly expressing negative feelings through seemingly harmless actions. This behavior involves expressing negativity subtly, avoiding direct confrontation.  It often manifests as subtle digs, sarcasm, or procrastination. | Example:  He passively aggressively left the dirty dishes in the sink instead of directly asking for help. 他被动地把脏盘子留在水槽里，而不是直接求助。


Example: Her passive-aggressive silence after the argument spoke volumes. 争吵后她被动攻击的沉默说明了一切。


近义词: indirect aggression, subtle hostility, covert aggression


反义词: direct confrontation, open communication, assertive behavior


词性: 名词 (复数)
