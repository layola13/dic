governess:/ˈɡʌvənəs/| n.|女家庭教师|A governess is a female teacher who provides private education to children, typically in their home.|例句：The governess taught the children French and music.  这位家庭教师教孩子们法语和音乐。|例句：She hired a governess to educate her daughter at home. 她雇了一位家庭教师在家教育她的女儿。|近义词：tutor, teacher, instructor|反义词：pupil, student
