metrology:/mɛˈtrɒlədʒi/ | n. |计量学，测量学 |The science of measurement and its applications.  It involves the principles and methods of obtaining, analyzing, and interpreting measurement data.  Metrology encompasses various aspects, from the definition of units to the calibration of instruments and the assessment of measurement uncertainty. |例句：Accurate metrology is crucial in manufacturing to ensure product quality. 精确的计量学对于确保产品质量至关重要。

例句：The national metrology institute is responsible for maintaining the country's measurement standards. 国家计量研究院负责维护国家的测量标准。


近义词：measurement science, measurement technology |反义词：None (no direct antonym) |词性:noun
