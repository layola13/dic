redispositions:/ˌriːdɪspəˈzɪʃənz/| n. |重新安排；重新布置；新的倾向；新的安排|The act of arranging or distributing something again; a new arrangement or distribution; a new tendency or inclination.|例句：The redisposition of troops improved the defensive position.  军队重新部署改善了防御态势。

例句：The redisposition of furniture gave the room a fresh look. 家具的重新摆放使房间焕然一新。


近义词：rearrangement, reorganization, realignment, shift

反义词：retention, maintenance, status quo
