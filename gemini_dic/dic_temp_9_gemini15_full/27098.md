caliduct:/ˈkælɪdʌkt/| n. |导热管，热导管 |A caliduct is a pipe or duct used to conduct heat.  It's often used in industrial settings or heating systems to transfer heat from one place to another. |Example: The factory used a network of caliducts to distribute heat efficiently throughout the building.  工厂用一套导热管网络来高效地将热量分布到整个建筑物。|近义词：heat pipe, conduit (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying cooling could be considered opposite in function.|名词
