nonmilitants:/ˌnɑːnˈmɪlɪtənts/| n. |非战斗人员，和平主义者|People or groups who do not participate in or support military actions.  They often advocate for peaceful solutions to conflict.|例句：The nonmilitants organized a peace march to protest the war. 非战斗人员组织了一次和平游行以抗议战争。

例句：Many nonmilitants believe in diplomacy and dialogue as a means of resolving disputes. 许多非战斗人员相信外交和对话是解决争端的途径。


近义词：pacifists, peace activists, civilians


反义词：militants, combatants, warriors


词性：名词
