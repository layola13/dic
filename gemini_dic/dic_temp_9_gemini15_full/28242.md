capsuling: /ˈkæpsjuːlɪŋ/ | v. | 概括；胶囊化；总结 | To encapsulate something is to summarize it concisely, or to enclose it in a capsule.  | Example:  He capsulated the entire meeting in a single sentence. 他用一句话概括了整个会议。  |近义词：summarizing, abstracting, condensing | 反义词：detailing, elaborating | 动词

She capsulates the medicine into small doses. 她把药胶囊化成小剂量。
