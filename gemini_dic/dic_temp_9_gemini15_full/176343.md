re-evaluates:/ˌriːɪˈvæljueɪts/| vt.|重新评估；重新评价|To reconsider and assess (something) again.|The company re-evaluates its strategy annually. 公司每年都会重新评估其战略。| reassess, reconsider, review | accept, confirm |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-evaluates his options every week. 他每周都会重新评估自己的选择。
The committee re-evaluates the proposal regularly. 委员会定期重新评估该提案。


一般过去时:
She re-evaluated her life choices after the accident. 事故后，她重新评估了她的人生选择。
The manager re-evaluated the employee's performance. 经理重新评估了员工的表现。


现在完成时:
They have re-evaluated the risks involved in the project. 他们已经重新评估了该项目中涉及的风险。
We have re-evaluated our marketing strategy several times. 我们已经多次重新评估了我们的营销策略。


过去完成时:
He had re-evaluated the situation before making his decision. 他在做出决定之前已经重新评估了情况。
The team had re-evaluated the data before submitting their report. 团队在提交报告之前已经重新评估了数据。


将来时:
They will re-evaluate the budget next month. 他们下个月将重新评估预算。
The government will re-evaluate the environmental impact assessment. 政府将重新评估环境影响评估。


虚拟语气:
If they re-evaluated their approach, they might find a better solution. 如果他们重新评估他们的方法，他们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish they had re-evaluated the plan before implementing it. 我希望他们在实施计划之前重新评估过该计划。


被动语态:
The policy will be re-evaluated by the board next year. 该政策明年将由董事会重新评估。
The project's progress is being re-evaluated by the supervisor. 项目的进展正在由主管重新评估。


疑问句:
Will the company re-evaluate its pricing strategy? 公司会重新评估其定价策略吗？
Have they re-evaluated the safety procedures yet? 他们已经重新评估安全程序了吗？


否定句:
They did not re-evaluate their assumptions. 他们没有重新评估他们的假设。
The team will not re-evaluate the findings until next week. 团队在下周之前不会重新评估这些发现。


PHRASE->
re-evaluate the situation = 重新评估形势
re-evaluate the evidence = 重新评估证据
re-evaluate the risks = 重新评估风险
re-evaluate the options = 重新评估选择
re-evaluate the impact = 重新评估影响
