foreplan:/ˈfɔːrplæn/| n. |预先计划，预案|A plan made in advance; a preliminary plan.|例句：The foreplan for the project included a detailed timeline and budget.  项目预案包括详细的时间表和预算。|例句：We need a solid foreplan to mitigate potential risks.我们需要一个可靠的预案来减轻潜在的风险。|近义词：preliminary plan, advance plan, preparatory plan|反义词：improvisation, spur-of-the-moment plan


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company develops a foreplan for each new product. = 公司为每种新产品制定预案。
He always makes a foreplan before starting a big project. = 他在开始大型项目之前总是制定预案。

一般过去时:
They developed a detailed foreplan last year. = 他们去年制定了一个详细的预案。
She foreplanned her trip meticulously. = 她精心预先计划了她的旅行。

现在完成时:
We have foreplanned the logistics for the conference. = 我们已经预先计划好了会议的物流安排。
They have completed the foreplan for the upcoming marketing campaign. = 他们已经完成了即将到来的营销活动的预案。

过去完成时:

The team had foreplanned the strategy for the negotiation. = 该团队已经预先计划好了谈判策略。

将来时:
We will foreplan the next steps after the meeting. = 会议结束后我们将预先计划下一步行动。
She will foreplan her holiday meticulously. = 她将精心预先计划她的假期。

虚拟语气:
If we had foreplanned better, we wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果我们预先计划得更好，我们就不会陷入这种情况。
It would have been helpful if they had foreplanned the project more thoroughly. = 如果他们更彻底地预先计划了项目，那将会有所帮助。

疑问句:
