mongoose:/ˈmʌŋɡuːs/ | n. | 獴 | A small, carnivorous mammal of the family Herpestidae, found in Africa, Asia, and southern Europe.  Mongooses are known for their ability to kill venomous snakes. | Example: The mongoose bravely faced the cobra.  这只獴勇敢地面对眼镜蛇。 |近义词：herpestid |反义词：无
