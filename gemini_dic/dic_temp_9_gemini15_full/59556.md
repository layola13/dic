eavesdrops:/ˈiːvzdrɒps/| v.|偷听，窃听|To listen secretly to a private conversation.|例句：I overheard them eavesdropping on my conversation. 我听到他们在偷听我的谈话。|近义词：overhear, listen in, spy|反义词：broadcast, announce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eavesdrops on his neighbors' conversations. = 他偷听邻居的谈话。
She often eavesdrops on her colleagues' gossip. = 她经常偷听同事们的闲话。


一般过去时:
I eavesdropped on their argument last night. = 昨晚我偷听了他们的争吵。
They eavesdropped on the meeting in the next room. = 他们偷听了隔壁房间的会议。


现在完成时:
He has eavesdropped on us several times. = 他已经多次偷听我们的谈话了。
She has never eavesdropped on anyone before. = 她以前从未偷听过任何人的谈话。


过去完成时:
I realized that someone had eavesdropped on our private discussion. = 我意识到有人偷听了我们的私人谈话。
They discovered that someone had eavesdropped on their secret plan. = 他们发现有人偷听了他们的秘密计划。


将来时:
I will not eavesdrop on their phone call. = 我不会偷听他们的电话。
He promises he will never eavesdrop on others again. = 他保证他再也不会偷听别人的谈话了。


虚拟语气:
If I had known they were eavesdropping, I wouldn't have said that. = 如果我知道他们在偷听，我不会那样说的。
I wish I hadn't eavesdropped on their conversation. = 我真希望我没有偷听他们的谈话。


被动语态:
Their conversation was eavesdropped on by a stranger. = 他们的谈话被一个陌生人偷听了。
The meeting was eavesdropped on by someone outside the room. = 会议被房间外面的人偷听了。


疑问句:
Were you eavesdropping on our conversation? = 你在偷听我们的谈话吗？
Did anyone eavesdrop on the meeting? = 有人偷听会议了吗？


否定句:
I didn't eavesdrop on their conversation.  = 我没有偷听他们的谈话。
She said she never eavesdrops on others' conversations. = 她说她从不偷听别人的谈话。
