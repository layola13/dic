perisarc:/ˈpɛrɪsɑːrk/| n. |围鞘；外骨骼|The perisarc is a protective outer layer secreted by the coelenterate, often chitinous and transparent, that surrounds the polyp or medusa. It provides protection against predators and environmental stressors.|例句：The perisarc of the hydroid colony provided a tough outer covering.  围鞘为水螅群体提供了坚硬的外层覆盖。

The perisarc of this species is particularly thick and robust.  这个物种的围鞘特别厚实结实。|近义词：sheath, covering, cuticle|反义词：endoderm, core
