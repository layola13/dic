onomastics:/ˌɒnəˈmæstɪks/ | n. |命名学，人名学 |The study of proper names, including personal names, place names, and the like.  It explores their origins, meanings, and historical development.  Onomastics encompasses various subfields, such as anthroponymy (personal names), toponymy (place names), and ethnonymy (names of ethnic groups).| Example:  The professor's research focuses on the onomastics of ancient Celtic tribes.  教授的研究集中在古代凯尔特部落的命名学上。|近义词：prosopography, name study |反义词：无
