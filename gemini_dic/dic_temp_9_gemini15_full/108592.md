lead-free:/ˈliːdfriː/| adj.| 无铅的 | Free from lead.  This adjective describes materials, products, or processes that do not contain lead.  | Example:  Many countries now require lead-free gasoline. 许多国家现在都要求使用无铅汽油。  The new solder is lead-free and environmentally friendly. 新的焊料是无铅的，而且对环境友好。|近义词：leadless |反义词：lead-containing
