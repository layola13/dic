longtail:/ˈlɒŋteɪl/ /ˈlɔːŋteɪl/| n. |长尾；长尾关键词；长尾效应|The long tail refers to a strategy of targeting a large number of niche markets with a relatively low volume of sales for each.  It contrasts with the "head," which focuses on a few popular products or services with high sales volume.  The long tail is particularly relevant in online businesses, where the vastness of the internet allows for the marketing and sales of many niche products.| Example:  Amazon's success is partially attributed to its long tail strategy, offering a vast selection of niche products.|近义词：niche market, specialized market|反义词：head, mainstream market|名词
