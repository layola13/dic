porers:/ˈpɔːrərz/ | n. | 仔细阅读者；钻研者 | People who pore over something, studying it intently.  They are diligent and focused in their examination. | Example: The porers in the library were completely absorbed in their books. 图书馆里那些仔细阅读的人完全沉浸在书中。 | 近义词：students, scholars, researchers | 反义词：skimmer,  superficial reader
