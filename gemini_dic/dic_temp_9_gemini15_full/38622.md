concretized:/ˈkɒŋkriˌtaɪzd/| /ˈkɑːŋkriˌtaɪzd/| v.|具体化；使具体化；使凝固|To make (something abstract) more concrete or real; to give definite form to; to solidify.|The architect concretized his initial sketch into a detailed blueprint. 建筑师将最初的草图具体化成详细的蓝图。

The government's promises were finally concretized in a new set of laws. 政府的承诺最终在新的法律中具体化了。|近义词：materialize, realize, solidify, embody|反义词：abstract, generalize, idealize|动词
