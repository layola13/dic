mortgagee:/ˌmɔːrɡəˈdʒiː/| n. |抵押权人，受押人 |The mortgagee is the lender who receives the mortgage as security for a loan.  抵押权人是接受抵押作为贷款担保的贷款人。|The bank, as the mortgagee, foreclosed on the property after the homeowner defaulted on the loan. 作为抵押权人的银行，在房主拖欠贷款后对该房产进行了止赎。| mortgagor, lender | debtor, borrower | 名词
