shares:/ʃɛrz/ | n. | 股份；股票；份额 |  A share is one of the equal parts into which the ownership of a company or corporation is divided.  Shares can be bought and sold on the stock market. | Example: He owns 100 shares of Apple stock. 他拥有100股苹果股票。  |近义词:stock, portion, part | 反义词:whole, entirety | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shares his toys with his brother. = 他和弟弟分享他的玩具。
She shares a flat with her friend. = 她和朋友合租一套公寓。


一般过去时:
They shared a pizza for dinner. = 他们晚餐一起分享了一个披萨。
We shared our experiences during the trip. = 我们分享了旅行中的经历。


现在完成时:
I have shared my ideas with the team. = 我已经和团队分享了我的想法。
She has shared her concerns with her manager. = 她已经和她的经理分享了她的担忧。


过去完成时:
He had shared his secret with her before the party. = 他在派对前已经和她分享了他的秘密。
They had shared the profits equally before the company went bankrupt. = 公司破产前，他们已经平均分配了利润。


将来时:
We will share the cost of the project. = 我们将分担项目的成本。
They will share their findings with the scientific community. = 他们将与科学界分享他们的发现。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would share my story with you. = 如果我有更多时间，我会和你分享我的故事。
I wish I could share my happiness with everyone. = 我希望我能与所有人分享我的快乐。


被动语态:
The profits were shared equally among the partners. = 利润在合伙人之间平均分配。
The information will be shared with all employees. = 信息将与所有员工共享。


疑问句:
Do you want to share your thoughts? = 你想分享你的想法吗？
Will you share the responsibility with me? = 你愿意和我一起承担责任吗？


否定句:
He doesn't share his food with anyone. = 他不和任何人分享他的食物。
She didn't share her concerns with anyone. = 她没有和任何人分享她的担忧。


PHRASE->

market share: 市场份额
share capital: 股本
