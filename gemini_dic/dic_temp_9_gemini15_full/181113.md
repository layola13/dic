reinfect:/ˌriːɪnˈfɛkt/| vt.|再次感染；重新感染|To infect (someone or something) again, after a previous infection.|例句：The virus could reinfect the patient if precautions are not taken.  病毒如果不采取预防措施可能会再次感染病人。  例句：  He was reinfected with the flu after a few weeks. 几周后他又再次感染了流感。|近义词：infect again, re-infect|反义词：cure, heal|动词
