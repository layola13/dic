limelighting:/ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtɪŋ/| n.|聚光灯照射；出风头；|The act of attracting attention or being the center of attention, often in a theatrical or dramatic way.  It can refer to the literal use of a limelight (a type of bright stage lighting) or the figurative sense of being the focus of attention.|例句：The singer enjoyed the limelighting during the concert. 歌手在音乐会上享受着聚光灯的照射。

例句：Her constant boasting felt like blatant limelighting. 她不断的吹嘘感觉像是赤裸裸地抢风头。|近义词：spotlight, attention-seeking, showboating|反义词：obscurity, anonymity, background
