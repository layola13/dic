loll:/lɒl/| /lɑːl/| vi. n.|懒洋洋地躺着；闲荡；（舌头）垂下|To lie or sit in a relaxed or lazy way; to hang or droop.  |例句：The dog lolled in the shade.  狗狗懒洋洋地躺在树荫下。

例句：He lolled back in his chair, completely relaxed. 他懒洋洋地靠在椅子上，完全放松了。


近义词：lounge, recline, sprawl, dangle|反义词：stand straight, be alert, be attentive|动词
