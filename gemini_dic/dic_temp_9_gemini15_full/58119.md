dropping: /ˈdrɒpɪŋ/ /ˈdrɑːpɪŋ/ | v. n. | 下落；滴下；放弃；下降 |

英文解析:
As a verb, "dropping" signifies the act of something falling or being let fall. It can also refer to a decrease in something, such as temperature or price, or the act of abandoning something or someone. As a noun, "dropping" is usually used as a gerund (verb acting as a noun), referring to the act of dropping something or the process of something dropping.

例句:

Verb:
The leaves are dropping from the trees.  = 树叶从树上掉下来。

The temperature is dropping rapidly. = 温度正在迅速下降。


Noun:
The dropping of the bomb caused widespread devastation. = 炸弹的投下造成了大范围的破坏。

The sudden dropping of prices surprised everyone. = 价格的突然下跌让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词:
Verb: falling, descending, declining, abandoning, relinquishing, letting go

Noun: fall, decrease, decline, abandonment


反义词:
Verb: rising, ascending, increasing, retaining, keeping, lifting

Noun: increase, rise, ascent
