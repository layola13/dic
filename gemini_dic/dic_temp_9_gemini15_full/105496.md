kilns:/kɪlnz/| n.|窑，炉|A kiln is a thermally insulated chamber, a type of oven, used for the firing of pottery, bricks, and other articles.  Kilns are used to achieve high temperatures that are necessary to harden or change the chemical composition of materials.|The potter carefully loaded the kiln with his latest creations.|ovens, furnaces|  (no direct antonym)|名词
