florulent:/ˈflɔːrʊlənt/| adj.|开花的；繁茂的；华丽的|Flowering abundantly; characterized by a profusion of flowers; ornate or flowery in style.|The garden was florulent with a riot of color. 花园里鲜花盛开，色彩缤纷。|The florulent prose of the novel was difficult to follow. 小说的华丽辞藻难以理解。|abundant, profuse, lush|barren, sparse, plain|形容词
