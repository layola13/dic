consists:/kənˈsɪsts/| v.|包含；组成；在于|To be composed or made up of; to be formed from.|The cake consists of flour, sugar, and eggs.  这个蛋糕由面粉、糖和鸡蛋组成。|The problem consists in a lack of funding.  问题在于资金不足。|comprises, includes, comprises of|differs from, excludes

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This team consists of five players. 这个队由五名队员组成。
The book consists of three chapters. 这本书由三章组成。

一般过去时:
The meal consisted of soup, salad, and dessert. 这顿饭包括汤、沙拉和甜点。
The old building consisted of several small rooms. 这座旧建筑由几个小房间组成。

现在完成时:
The committee has consisted of the same members for many years.  委员会多年来一直由相同的成员组成。
The collection has consisted primarily of paintings.  收藏主要由绘画组成。

过去完成时:
Before the merger, the company had consisted of two separate entities. 在合并之前，该公司由两个独立的实体组成。
The display had consisted of various artifacts from the region. 展览曾展出该地区各种文物。

将来时:
The new team will consist of experienced professionals. 新团队将由经验丰富的专业人员组成。
The program will consist of lectures and workshops. 该项目将包括讲座和研讨会。

虚拟语气:
If the project consisted only of research, it would be easier. 如果项目只包括研究，就会更容易。
I wish the report consisted of fewer technical terms. 我希望报告中包含较少的专业术语。

被动语态:
The team is consisted of highly skilled individuals.  该团队由高技能人才组成。(注意：consist本身不常用被动语态，此句略显不自然，更好的表达是The team is made up of…)
The mixture is consisted of several different chemicals.  混合物由几种不同的化学物质组成。(注意：consist本身不常用被动语态，此句略显不自然，更好的表达是The mixture is made up of…)


疑问句:
What does the mixture consist of?  混合物由什么组成？
What does this course consist of? 这门课程包括什么？

否定句:
The solution does not consist of simply adding more water.  解决方法并非仅仅是添加更多水。
The problem does not consist in a lack of resources.  问题不在于缺乏资源。


PHRASE->
consist of = 包含，由…组成
consist in = 在于
consist with = 与…一致
consistently = 一贯地，始终如一地
to consist entirely of = 完全由……组成
