meander:/miˈændər/| v.|蜿蜒，漫步，闲逛 |to follow a winding course:  The river meanders through the valley. 河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。|例句：The path meandered through the woods. 小路蜿蜒穿过树林。The speaker meandered from one topic to another. 演讲者从一个话题漫谈到另一个话题。|近义词：wind, wander, ramble, stray|反义词：straighten, direct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river meanders through the valley. = 河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。
He meanders aimlessly through life. = 他漫无目的地游荡于人生。


一般过去时:
The hikers meandered along the mountain trail. = 徒步旅行者沿着山间小路漫步。
She meandered through the antique shops. = 她漫步在古董店里。


现在完成时:
The river has meandered for centuries. = 这条河蜿蜒流淌了几个世纪。
We have meandered through many towns on this journey. = 在这次旅程中，我们漫游了许多城镇。


过去完成时:
The river had meandered through the plains before reaching the sea. = 河流在到达大海之前已经蜿蜒流经平原。
They had meandered for hours before finding the lost path. = 他们在找到丢失的小路之前已经漫步了好几个小时。


将来时:
The hikers will meander along the coast tomorrow. = 徒步旅行者明天将在海岸边漫步。
The stream will meander its way to the ocean. = 溪流将蜿蜒流向大海。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would meander through the art museum. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会在艺术博物馆里漫步。
I wish I could meander through the countryside. = 我真希望我能漫步在乡间。


被动语态:
The path cannot be meandered easily. = 这条路不容易蜿蜒通行。 (被动语态使用较少)
The route is often meandered by tourists. = 这条路线经常被游客漫步。 (被动语态使用较少)


疑问句:
Does the road meander far ahead? = 这条路前面蜿蜒延伸得很远吗？
Will they meander along the riverbank? = 他们会在河岸边漫步吗？


否定句:
The road does not meander; it goes straight. = 这条路不蜿蜒，它是笔直的。
He didn't meander; he walked directly to his destination. = 他没有漫步，而是直接走到目的地。
