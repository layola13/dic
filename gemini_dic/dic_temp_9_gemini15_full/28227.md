capsized:/ˈkæpsʌɪzd/| vt. |倾覆；翻船|To overturn or turn upside down, especially of a boat.  |例句：The boat capsized in the storm.  船在暴风雨中倾覆了。|例句：The strong winds capsized several small fishing boats. 强风导致几艘小型渔船倾覆。|近义词：overturn, upset, sink|反义词：right (a boat), stabilize


USAGE->

The strong current capsizes many small boats every year. = 强劲的水流每年都会使许多小船倾覆。
He carefully capsizes the toy boat in the bathtub. = 他小心地把玩具船在浴缸里翻了过来。

词源:
The word "capsize" comes from the Dutch word "kapseizen," which literally means "to turn the cap."  The "cap" refers to the top or head of something, implying an overturning.
