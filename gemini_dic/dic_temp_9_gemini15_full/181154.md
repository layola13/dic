reinforcing:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrsɪŋ/| v. |加强；加固；强化 | To strengthen or support something, making it more effective or more resistant to damage.  |例句：The government is reinforcing its border security. 政府正在加强边境安全。

例句：The engineer reinforced the bridge's structure to withstand earthquakes. 工程师加固了桥梁结构以承受地震。


近义词：strengthen, fortify, bolster, consolidate | 反义词：weaken, undermine, sabotage | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reinforcing the walls of the castle. = 他们正在加固城堡的城墙。
The teacher is reinforcing the students' understanding of grammar. = 老师正在强化学生对语法的理解。


一般过去时:
The soldiers reinforced their defensive positions. = 士兵们加强了他们的防御阵地。
She reinforced her arguments with new evidence. = 她用新的证据加强了她的论点。


现在完成时:
They have reinforced the bridge after the recent floods. = 最近的洪水过后，他们已经加固了桥梁。
The company has reinforced its commitment to sustainability. = 公司已经加强了其对可持续发展的承诺。


过去完成时:
The team had reinforced their strategy before the final game. = 在决赛之前，团队已经加强了他们的策略。
He had reinforced his understanding of the topic by reading several books. = 他通过阅读几本书强化了他对这个主题的理解。


将来时:
They will reinforce the team with new players. = 他们将用新的球员加强球队。
The government will reinforce its efforts to combat crime. = 政府将加强打击犯罪的努力。


虚拟语气:
If they reinforced the structure, it would be more stable. = 如果他们加固了结构，它会更稳定。
I wish they had reinforced the dam before the storm. = 我希望他们在暴风雨来临之前加固了水坝。


被动语态:
The bridge is being reinforced to withstand heavier traffic. = 桥梁正在被加固以承受更大的交通流量。
The security measures are being reinforced to prevent further incidents. = 安全措施正在被加强以防止进一步的事件发生。


疑问句:
Are they reinforcing the building's foundations? = 他们正在加固建筑的地基吗？
Will the company reinforce its marketing campaign? = 公司会加强其营销活动吗？


否定句:
They did not reinforce the damaged section of the road. = 他们没有加固道路受损的路段。
The government has not reinforced its sanctions against the country. = 政府没有加强其对该国的制裁。
