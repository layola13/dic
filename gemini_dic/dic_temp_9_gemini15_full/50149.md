denunciation:/ˌdɛnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/| n. |谴责；告发；公开指责 |The public denunciation of the dictator's crimes was met with widespread approval. 公开谴责独裁者的罪行受到了广泛赞同。|近义词：condemnation, criticism, accusation |反义词：praise, commendation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government issues a denunciation of the illegal activity. = 政府发表声明谴责非法活动。
Her denunciation of his behavior shocks everyone. = 她对他的行为的谴责震惊了所有人。


一般过去时:
The journalist's denunciation of corruption led to an investigation. = 记者对腐败的谴责导致了一项调查。
His denunciation of the treaty was highly controversial. = 他对条约的谴责极具争议。


现在完成时:
The company has faced numerous denunciations for its unethical practices. = 这家公司因其不道德行为而面临多次谴责。
She has issued a public denunciation of the violence. = 她公开谴责了暴力行为。


过去完成时:
By the time the scandal broke, many denunciations had already been made. = 在丑闻爆发时，许多谴责已经发出。
The court had already heard several denunciations before the trial began. = 在审判开始之前，法庭已经听取了几项谴责。


将来时:
The activist plans to issue a denunciation of the new law next week. = 该活动家计划下周发表谴责新法的声明。
We anticipate further denunciations of the policy in the coming months. = 我们预计未来几个月将会有更多对该政策的谴责。


虚拟语气:
If only there had been a stronger denunciation of the injustice. = 如果当时对这种不公正行为有更强烈的谴责就好了。
I wish the denunciation had been more effective. = 我希望谴责更有效。


被动语态:
The politician's actions were met with widespread denunciation. = 这位政治家的行为受到了广泛的谴责。
His ideas were subjected to fierce denunciation. = 他的想法受到了激烈的谴责。


疑问句:
What is the purpose of this denunciation? = 这次谴责的目的是什么？
Have you heard the denunciation of the company's actions? = 你听说过对该公司行为的谴责吗？


否定句:
There was no denunciation of the crime. = 没有对犯罪行为的谴策。
The meeting lacked any denunciation of the proposed policy. = 会议中没有任何对拟议政策的谴责。


PHRASE->
a public denunciation = 公开谴责
a formal denunciation = 正式谴责
a strong denunciation = 强烈的谴责
a written denunciation = 书面谴责
a vehement denunciation = 激烈的谴责


词源:
From Old French *denonciation, from Latin denunciatio, from denunciāre "to announce, report, denounce," from de- "down, away, from" + nuntiāre "to announce, proclaim," from nuntius "messenger."
