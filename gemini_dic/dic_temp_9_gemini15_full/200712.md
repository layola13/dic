sleepwalking: /ˈsliːpwɔːkɪŋ/ | n. |梦游|The act of walking or performing other actions while asleep.|例句：He has a history of sleepwalking, so we keep his bedroom door locked. 他有梦游史，所以我们一直锁着他的卧室门。  He was sleepwalking when he fell down the stairs. 他梦游时从楼梯上摔了下来。|近义词：somnambulism|反义词：N/A

He sleepwalks every night. = 他每晚都梦游。
She often sleepwalks when she's stressed. = 她压力大的时候经常梦游。

一般过去时:
I sleepwalked last night. = 我昨晚梦游了。

否定句:

PHRASE->
sleepwalking episode: 梦游发作

词源:
sleep + walking  (两个词的组合)  sleep 表示睡眠，walking 表示行走。  因此，sleepwalking 字面意思是“睡眠行走”。
