neglecter:/nɪˈɡlektər/ | n. | 忽视者，疏忽者 | A person who neglects their duties or responsibilities.  Someone who fails to care for something or someone properly. |  The neglecter of his garden was fined for violating local ordinances.  这个忽视了他花园的人因违反当地法令而被罚款。| 近义词：neglector, disregarder, abuser | 反义词：caretaker, protector
