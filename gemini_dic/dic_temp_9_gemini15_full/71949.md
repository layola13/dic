fliest:/flaɪz/| v. |飞(flies的第三人称单数形式)|The bird flies high in the sky.  鸟儿在高空中飞翔。|flies, fly|walks, crawls|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flies a kite every afternoon. 他每天下午放风筝。
The plane flies from New York to London. 飞机从纽约飞往伦敦。

一般过去时:
The bird flew away quickly. 鸟儿飞走了。
She flew to Paris last week. 她上周飞去了巴黎。

现在完成时:
The bird has flown away. 鸟儿已经飞走了。
He has flown many times to Asia. 他多次飞往亚洲。

过去完成时:
The bird had flown away before I arrived. 我到达之前，鸟已经飞走了。
They had flown to Rome before the meeting. 他们在开会之前已经飞到了罗马。

将来时:
The bird will fly south for the winter. 鸟儿将飞往南方过冬。
I will fly to Beijing next month. 我下个月将飞往北京。

虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would fly. 如果我有翅膀，我会飞。
I wish I could fly like a bird. 我真希望我能像鸟一样飞。

被动语态:
The kite was flown by a child. 风筝是一个孩子放飞的。
The plane is flown by a skilled pilot. 飞机由一名熟练的飞行员驾驶。

疑问句:
Does the bird fly high? 鸟飞得高吗？
Does the plane fly on time? 飞机准时起飞吗？

否定句:
The bird doesn't fly very far. 鸟飞不远。
The plane didn't fly because of the bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，飞机没有起飞。
