monocultural:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj. |单一文化的；同质文化的 |Relating to or characterized by a single culture; lacking diversity.  A monocultural society is one where there is a lack of diversity in culture, ethnicity, or ideas.|例句：The company's monocultural environment led to a lack of innovation. 公司单一文化的环境导致缺乏创新。
例句：His monocultural upbringing didn't prepare him for the diverse perspectives of the global workforce. 他单一文化的成长背景没有让他为全球劳动力队伍的多元化视角做好准备。|近义词：homogeneous, uniform, undiversified |反义词：multicultural, diverse, heterogeneous |词性：形容词
