improfitable:/ˌɪmˈprɒfɪtəbl/| adj.|无利可图的，不赚钱的| not profitable; yielding no profit.  The business venture proved to be unprofitable and was eventually shut down.|近义词：unprofitable, losing, unremunerative|反义词：profitable, lucrative, rewarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The investment is unprofitable this year. = 今年这项投资没有盈利。
This project is currently unprofitable. = 这个项目目前没有盈利。


一般过去时:
The restaurant was unprofitable last year. = 去年这家餐馆没有盈利。
The mining operation proved unprofitable in the past. = 过去的采矿作业证明是不盈利的。


现在完成时:
The venture has been unprofitable since its inception. = 自从成立以来，这项投资一直没有盈利。
The farm has been unprofitable for several seasons. = 这个农场已经亏损了好几个季节了。


过去完成时:
The company had been unprofitable for years before it finally closed. = 在公司最终倒闭之前，已经亏损多年了。
It had been unprofitable to continue the project. = 继续这个项目是不合算的。


将来时:
The new business plan will likely be unprofitable. = 新的商业计划很可能会亏损。
The project will be unprofitable unless we cut costs. = 除非我们削减成本，否则该项目将无利可图。


虚拟语气:
If we continue this way, the business will be unprofitable. = 如果我们继续这样下去，生意将无法盈利。
I wish the investment were not unprofitable. = 我希望这项投资不是无利可图的。


被动语态:
The project was deemed unprofitable by the board. = 该项目被董事会认定为无利可图。
The strategy is considered unprofitable by many experts. = 许多专家认为这项策略是不盈利的。


疑问句:
Is this venture truly unprofitable? = 这项投资真的没有盈利吗？
Are we sure this business model is unprofitable? = 我们确定这个商业模式没有盈利吗？


否定句:
The company is not unprofitable; it's making a small profit. = 这家公司并非没有盈利；它正在小幅盈利。
The investment isn't unprofitable; it's just breaking even. = 这项投资并非不赚钱；它只是保本。
