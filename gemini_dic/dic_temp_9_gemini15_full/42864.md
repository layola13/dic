courtesy: /ˈkɜːrtəsi/ | /ˈkɝːtəsi/ | n. | 礼貌；客气；恩惠；免费赠送 |  Politeness and consideration for others; a polite gesture or act.  |

例句：He showed great courtesy to his guests. 他对他的客人非常有礼貌。

例句：It was a courtesy to offer her a seat.  给她让座是一种礼貌。


近义词：politeness, civility, respect, consideration, kindness

反义词：rudeness, discourtesy, impoliteness, incivility


词源：源自古法语 *curtesie，最终源自拉丁语 *cortis (“court”)，指宫廷礼仪。


USAGE->

Her courtesy is appreciated by all. = 大家都欣赏她的礼貌。

一般过去时:
He showed great courtesy to the elderly woman. = 他对那位老太太非常有礼貌。

现在完成时:

将来时:
