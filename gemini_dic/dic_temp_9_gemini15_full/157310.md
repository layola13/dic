pinworm:/ˈpɪnwɜːrm/| n. |蛲虫 |A small, threadlike intestinal worm (Enterobius vermicularis) that is a common parasite in humans, especially children.  Pinworms cause anal itching and can spread easily through fecal contamination.|例句：My child has been diagnosed with pinworms and we're treating it with medication. 我的孩子被诊断出患有蛲虫病，我们正在用药物治疗。|例句：The doctor confirmed the presence of pinworms in the stool sample. 医生确认粪便样本中存在蛲虫。|近义词：threadworm |反义词:|
