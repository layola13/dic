cudgelling:/ˈkʌdʒəlɪŋ/| v.|苦思冥想；猛击| Continuously thinking hard about something; to strike repeatedly with a cudgel. |例句：He spent the afternoon cudgelling his brains for a solution. 他一下午都在苦思冥想解决方案。|例句：The angry mob cudgelled the thief mercilessly.  愤怒的暴民无情地殴打小偷。|近义词：pondering, racking one's brains, beating, thrashing|反义词：relaxing, resting
