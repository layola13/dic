half-grown:/ˌhæfˈɡroʊn/| adj.|半大的，未成熟的|Partially grown; not fully developed.  This adjective typically describes animals or plants, but can also be used figuratively for people.|例句：The half-grown puppy was already quite playful. 这只半大的小狗已经很顽皮了。  The half-grown sapling needed more support. 这棵半大的树苗需要更多支撑。|近义词：partially grown, underdeveloped, immature|反义词：full-grown, mature, developed
