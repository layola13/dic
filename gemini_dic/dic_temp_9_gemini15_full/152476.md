payables:/ˌpeɪˈeɪbəlnəsəz/| n.|应付账款；应付债务|Amounts of money a company owes to others.  These are typically recorded as liabilities on a company's balance sheet.  Payables can include accounts payable (money owed to suppliers), salaries payable (money owed to employees), taxes payable (money owed to the government), and other short-term debts.|例句：The company's payables increased significantly this quarter due to increased inventory purchases.  这个季度的应付账款由于库存增加而大幅增长。|例句：Managing payables efficiently is crucial for maintaining a healthy cash flow.  有效管理应付账款对于维持健康的现金流至关重要。|近义词：liabilities, debts, obligations|反义词：receivables, assets|名词
