hands-out: /hændzˈaʊt/ | n. |分发，施舍，讲义 |  A distribution of something, typically to a large number of people;  a handout can also refer to something given freely, often as charity.  It can also refer to printed materials given out, like course notes. | 例句：The teacher gave out the hands-out at the beginning of class. =老师在课堂开始时分发了讲义。

The charity organized a hands-out of food and clothing to the homeless. =慈善机构组织了向无家可归者分发食物和衣物的活动。


近义词：distribution, handout, allocation, dispensation | 反义词：retention, withholding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher hands out the tests. =老师分发试卷。
The volunteers hand out flyers. =志愿者们分发传单。


一般过去时:

The organization has handed out thousands of meals this year. =该组织今年已分发了数千份餐食。
He has handed out all the questionnaires. =他已经分发完了所有问卷。


过去完成时:
They had handed out all the programs before the concert began. =音乐会开始前，他们已分发完所有节目单。

将来时:
