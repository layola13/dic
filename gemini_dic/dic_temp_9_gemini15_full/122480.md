mobs:/mɒbz/| n.|暴民，乌合之众|A mob is a large, disorderly crowd of people, especially one that is violent or uncontrolled.  |例句：The mob surged forward, breaking through the police lines. 暴民蜂拥而上，冲破了警察的防线。|例句：A mob of angry protesters gathered outside the government building. 一群愤怒的抗议者聚集在政府大楼外。|近义词：crowd, throng, rabble, horde|反义词：order, calm, peace


USAGE->

The mob gathers in the square. = 暴民聚集在广场上。

The mob attacked the police station. = 暴民袭击了警察局。
