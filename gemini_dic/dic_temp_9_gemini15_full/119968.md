millennium:/mɪˈlɛniəm/| n. |千年；千禧年 |A millennium is a period of 1,000 years.  The term is often used to refer to a specific 1,000-year period, such as the first millennium CE.  It also commonly refers to the period around the year 2000, often called the "millennium." |The year 2000 marked the beginning of a new millennium.  = 2000年标志着一个新的千禧年的开始。 The Roman Empire lasted for many centuries, spanning several millennia. = 罗马帝国持续了几个世纪，跨越了几个千年。|century, decade |(近义词无明确的反义词，可考虑使用“a short period”, “a few years”等表达相对较短的时间段)|名词
