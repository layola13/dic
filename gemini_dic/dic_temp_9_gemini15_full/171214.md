psychosocial:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈsoʊʃəl/| adj.|心理社会的；社会心理的|relating to or denoting the interrelation of social factors and individual thought and behavior.  It emphasizes the complex interplay between psychological and social factors in influencing human behavior, development, and well-being.|例句：The study explored the psychosocial factors contributing to adolescent depression. 这项研究探讨了导致青少年抑郁的心理社会因素。  The program addresses the psychosocial needs of homeless individuals. 该项目满足无家可归者的社会心理需求。|近义词：sociopsychological, socioemotional|反义词：biological (in the sense of focusing solely on biological factors)
