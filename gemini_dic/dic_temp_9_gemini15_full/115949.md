marriageable:/ˈmærɪdʒəbl/| adj. |适婚年龄的；适于结婚的 | Suitable for marriage; of an age or in a state to be married.  |例句：She's reached marriageable age. 她到了适婚年龄。|例句：He's a very marriageable young man. 他是一位非常适婚的年轻人。|近义词：eligible, suitable |反义词：unmarriageable
