replates:/riˈpleɪts/ | v. | 再铺设，重新铺设 | To lay or place something again or anew, especially paving or flooring.  | Example: The city council decided to replate the damaged sidewalks. 市议会决定重新铺设受损的人行道。|近义词：relay, replace, repave | 反义词：remove, tear up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replate the roads every five years. = 他们每五年重新铺设一次道路。
The workers replate the damaged section of the floor. = 工人们正在重新铺设地板受损的部分。

一般过去时:
The team replated the entire kitchen floor last week. = 团队上周重新铺设了整个厨房的地板。
They replated the worn-out tiles in the bathroom. = 他们重新铺设了浴室里磨损的瓷砖。

现在完成时:
The city has replated many streets this year. = 今年市里已经重新铺设了许多街道。
They have replated the damaged area of the roof. = 他们已经重新铺设了屋顶受损的区域。


过去完成时:
They had replated the bridge before the heavy rains. = 大雨来临之前，他们已经重新铺设了桥面。
The contractors had replated the walkway by the time the event started. = 活动开始时，承包商已经重新铺设了走道。

将来时:
The company will replate the parking lot next month. = 公司下个月将重新铺设停车场。
They will replate the damaged section of the highway. = 他们将重新铺设高速公路受损的路段。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would replate the entire driveway. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会重新铺设整个车道。
It would be ideal if they replated the steps before winter. = 如果他们在冬天之前重新铺设台阶，那就最好了。


被动语态:
The old tiles were replated with new ones. = 旧瓷砖被新的瓷砖重新铺设了。
The road is being replated due to recent damage. = 由于最近的损坏，道路正在被重新铺设。


疑问句:
Will they replate the street in front of my house? = 他们会重新铺设我家门前的街道吗？
When are they going to replate the sidewalk? = 他们什么时候要重新铺设人行道？


否定句:
They did not replate the driveway this year. = 他们今年没有重新铺设车道。
The contractor refused to replate the damaged area without payment. = 承包商拒绝在没有付款的情况下重新铺设受损区域。
