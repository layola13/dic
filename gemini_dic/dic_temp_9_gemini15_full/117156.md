meanly:/ˈminli/| adv.|卑鄙地；吝啬地；卑劣地 |In a mean manner; ungenerously; badly.  It describes actions or behavior characterized by selfishness, unkindness, or stinginess. |例句：He treated his employees meanly, paying them barely minimum wage. 他待雇员很刻薄，只付给他们勉强够最低工资的薪水。
例句：She meanly refused to share her candy with her friends. 她吝啬地拒绝与朋友分享她的糖果。|近义词：shabbily, stingily, selfishly, ungenerously |反义词：generously, kindly, charitably, magnanimously
