elf:/ɛlf/ | n. |精灵，小妖精 | A mythical creature of folklore, typically having magical powers and inhabiting forests.  Elves are often depicted as having human-like features but possessing supernatural abilities. They may be benevolent or malevolent, depending on the specific tradition. |Example:  The children believed that elves lived in the enchanted forest. 孩子们相信精灵住在魔法森林里。|近义词：fairy, sprite, pixie |反义词：giant, ogre
