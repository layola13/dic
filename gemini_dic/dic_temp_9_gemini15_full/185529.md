revalorized:/ˌriːˈvæləraɪzd/| v. |重新评估；重新定价；重新重视 |To re-evaluate or reassess the value or worth of something.  It implies a change in perspective or understanding leading to a new valuation.|The company revalorized its assets after a successful year. 公司在成功的一年后重新评估了其资产。| re-evaluate, reassess, reappraise | undervalue, depreciate | 动词
