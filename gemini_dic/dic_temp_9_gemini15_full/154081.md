perichaeth:/pɛrɪˈkiːθ/| n. | 蒴萼；颈鞘 | The perichaeth is a sheath of leaves surrounding the base of a moss capsule.  蒴萼是围绕苔藓孢蒴基部的叶鞘。|  The perichaetial leaves are often larger and more conspicuous than the other leaves of the moss plant. 蒴萼叶通常比苔藓植物的其他叶子更大更显眼。| 近义词：calyptra (部分情况下) | 反义词：无
