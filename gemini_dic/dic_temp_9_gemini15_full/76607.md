gallivanting:/ˈɡælɪvæntɪŋ/| v.|四处游荡；寻欢作乐；|兴高采烈地到处闲逛；四处游玩；|例句：They spent their holidays gallivanting around Europe.  他们把假期花在了游遍欧洲上。

例句：He's been gallivanting around the country for months. 他已经到处闲逛好几个月了。


近义词：roaming, rambling, touring,  traveling,  holidaying


反义词：staying put, remaining stationary


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They gallivant around the world. = 他们在世界各地闲逛。
We gallivant to different cities every weekend. = 我们每个周末都去不同的城市游玩。


一般过去时:
They gallivanted across the country. = 他们纵贯全国四处游玩。
We gallivanted through Europe last summer. = 我们去年夏天在欧洲四处游玩。


现在完成时:
She has gallivanted all over South America. = 她已经游遍了整个南美洲。
They have gallivanted extensively throughout their careers. = 他们职业生涯中到处游历。


过去完成时:
They had gallivanted through several countries before settling down. = 他们在定居之前已经游历了好几个国家。
She had gallivanted across the continent before she found her home. = 在找到家之前，她已经游历了整个大陆。


将来时:
We will gallivant through the countryside next week. = 我们下周将在乡间四处游玩。
They will gallivant across Asia for their anniversary. = 他们将在亚洲四处游玩庆祝周年纪念日。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would gallivant around the world. = 如果我有钱，我会周游世界。
I wish I could gallivant to exotic locations. = 我希望我能去异国他乡游玩。


被动语态:

The celebrities were gallivanted around by their entourage. = 名人们被他们的随行人员带到各地游玩。


疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "gallivanting" emerged in the 19th century.  It's believed to be of uncertain origin, possibly a combination of "gallop" (a fast ride) and an unknown element related to wandering or roaming.  Its usage suggests a lively, somewhat frivolous, and carefree style of travel.
