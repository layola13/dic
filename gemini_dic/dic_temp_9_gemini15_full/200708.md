sleepwalk: /ˈsliːpwɔːk/ | v. |梦游|To walk around while asleep.  This usually occurs during the lighter stages of sleep and the person is often unaware of their actions. |例句：He sleepwalked down the stairs and into the kitchen last night. 他昨晚梦游下楼走进了厨房。|例句：She has been known to sleepwalk since childhood. 她从小就有梦游的毛病。|近义词：somnambulate |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be being fully awake and aware.
