pre-cleaner:/ˌpriːˈkliːnər/| n. |预清洁器，预处理设备|A pre-cleaner is a device or system used to remove larger contaminants or unwanted materials from a substance before further processing or cleaning.  It's often a preliminary step to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of subsequent cleaning stages.  For example, in industrial settings, a pre-cleaner might remove large debris from grain before it goes through a more refined cleaning process.| The factory uses a pre-cleaner to remove stones and sticks from the harvested wheat before milling. = 工厂在磨面之前使用预清洁器去除收割的小麦中的石头和树枝。|近义词：pre-processor, initial cleaner|反义词：final cleaner, polisher
