portaging:/pɔːrˈteɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. |搬运；背负（货物等）越过陆地 |The act of carrying or transporting goods, especially over land, typically by carrying them on one's back or shoulder.  It often involves carrying canoes or boats and their contents across portages. |例句：We spent the afternoon portaging our canoes around the falls.  我们下午花时间把独木舟搬运过瀑布。  They were portaging supplies to the remote village. 他们在向偏远村庄搬运物资。 |近义词：carrying, transporting, hauling |反义词：shipping (by water)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They portage their canoes around the rapids. = 他们把独木舟搬运过激流。
We portage supplies to the mountain climbers. = 我们把物资搬运给登山者。

一般过去时:
The explorers portaged their equipment across the mountain. = 探险家们把他们的装备搬运过山。

现在完成时:
They have portaged several canoes today. = 他们今天已经搬运了几艘独木舟。
We have portaged all the supplies to the campsite. = 我们已经把所有物资搬运到营地。

过去完成时:

We had portaged across the swamp before the rain started. = 我们在雨开始下之前已经搬运过了沼泽。
