inciters: /ɪnˈsaɪtərz/ | n. |煽动者，挑拨者 | Those who incite or instigate.  People who encourage or stir up violent or unlawful behavior. |例句：The police are investigating the inciters of the riot.  警方正在调查骚乱的煽动者。

例句：The inciters of the violence will be brought to justice. 暴力事件的煽动者将被绳之以法。


近义词：agitators, instigators, provocateurs, troublemakers | 反义词：peacemakers, mediators


词源:  From the verb "incite," which comes from the Old French "inciter," meaning "to urge on, stimulate."  The Old French word itself has roots in the Latin "incitare," from "in-" (in, into) + "citare" (to summon, rouse, excite).


用法说明：

inciters 通常作名词使用，指那些煽动他人采取暴力或非法行为的人。  它强调的是行为的激发和鼓励，而非仅仅是表达意见。


需要注意的是，"inciter" 这个词本身带有负面含义，因为它与暴力或不法行为联系在一起。  使用时需要谨慎，避免误解。
