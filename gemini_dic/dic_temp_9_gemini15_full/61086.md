ells:/ɛlz/ | n. |肘，(织物)段，(建筑)翼廊 | A projecting part of a building, often forming a covered passage.  Also, a unit of measurement for cloth.  In some dialects, it refers to the elbow. |例句：The old house had charming ells extending from the main structure.  这栋老房子有从主建筑延伸出来的迷人侧廊。|近义词：wing, extension,  cubit (as a measure)|反义词：N/A
