challenges:/ˈtʃælɪndʒɪz/ | n. |挑战，难题 | Difficulties or obstacles that must be overcome.  Challenges can be physical, mental, or emotional. |例句：The company faces many challenges in the current economic climate. 这家公司在当前的经济环境下面临许多挑战。
例句：Climbing Mount Everest presents significant challenges. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰带来了巨大的挑战。|近义词：difficulties, obstacles, trials, hardships |反义词：ease, simplicity


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The project challenges our team's abilities. = 这个项目挑战着我们团队的能力。
These new regulations challenge existing practices. = 这些新规挑战了现有的做法。

一般过去时:
The athlete challenged his own personal best. = 运动员挑战了他自己的最好成绩。
The argument challenged my assumptions. = 争论挑战了我的假设。

现在完成时:
The changing market has challenged many businesses. = 变化的市场已经挑战了许多企业。
She has challenged the status quo throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中一直在挑战现状。

过去完成时:
They had challenged the decision before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就对该决定提出了质疑。
The evidence had challenged the initial theory. = 证据已经挑战了最初的理论。

将来时:
The upcoming election will challenge the incumbent president. = 即将到来的选举将挑战现任总统。
We will challenge the unfair treatment. = 我们将挑战这种不公平的待遇。

虚拟语气:
If I were to challenge him, I would need strong evidence. = 如果我要挑战他，我需要有力的证据。
I wish I could challenge the system, but I lack the power. = 我希望我能挑战这个制度，但我缺乏权力。

被动语态:
The team was challenged by the unexpected problem. = 团队受到了意外问题的挑战。
His authority was challenged by the younger generation. = 他的权威受到了年轻一代的挑战。

疑问句:
What challenges do you face in your work? = 你在工作中面临什么挑战？
Does this new technology challenge traditional methods? = 这项新技术是否挑战了传统方法？

否定句:
The argument didn't challenge my beliefs. = 争论并没有挑战我的信仰。
He didn't challenge the authority's decision. = 他没有质疑当局的决定。


PHRASE->
