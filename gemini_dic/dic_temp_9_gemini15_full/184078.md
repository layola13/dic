resignedly:/rɪˈzaɪnɪdli/| adv.|顺从地；认命地；无奈地 |Showing or expressing resignation; passively accepting something unpleasant.  It implies a sense of yielding to something inevitable or unavoidable, often with a sense of weariness or hopelessness. |例句：She resignedly accepted the punishment. 她无奈地接受了惩罚。

He resignedly shrugged his shoulders. 他无奈地耸了耸肩。


近义词：submissively, passively, meekly, hopelessly, uncomplainingly


反义词：rebelliously, defiantly, protestingly, aggressively


词源：源自动词resign，意思是“辞职；放弃；顺从”。  "-edly" 是一个副词后缀，表示“以…的方式”。因此，resignedly 意味着“以顺从的方式”。
