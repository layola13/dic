lodged:/lɒdʒd/ | /lɑːdʒd/ | v. | 寄宿；提出；卡住；嵌合 | To place something firmly in a particular place.  To formally register or submit something. To become fixed or stuck. |例句：He lodged a complaint with the police. 他向警方投诉。


例句：The bullet lodged in his shoulder. 子弹卡在他的肩膀里。


近义词：submit, file, embed, lodgement


反义词：remove, extract, dislodge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lodges a complaint every week. 他每周都提出投诉。
She lodges with her aunt. 她寄宿在她阿姨家。


一般过去时:
They lodged a formal protest. 他们提出了正式抗议。
The key lodged in the lock. 钥匙卡在锁里了。


现在完成时:
He has lodged several complaints this month. 这个月他已经投诉了好几次了。
She has lodged with us for several years. 她已经和我们同住好几年了。


过去完成时:
They had lodged their application before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前提交了申请。
The bird had lodged itself in the chimney. 鸟把自己卡在了烟囱里。


将来时:
She will lodge a formal objection to the plan. 她将对该计划提出正式异议。
He will lodge at a hotel near the conference center. 他将在会议中心附近的一家酒店住宿。


虚拟语气:
If I lodged a complaint, they might take it seriously. 如果我投诉，他们可能会认真对待。
I wish I had lodged a complaint sooner. 我真希望我早点投诉。


被动语态:
A complaint was lodged against the company. 对该公司提出了投诉。
The application was lodged successfully. 申请已成功提交。


疑问句:
Where did you lodge your passport? 你把护照寄存在哪里了？
Have you lodged a complaint yet? 你投诉了吗？


否定句:
He didn't lodge a complaint. 他没有投诉。
The key didn't lodge in the lock. 钥匙没有卡在锁里。
