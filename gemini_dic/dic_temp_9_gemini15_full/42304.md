counter-offer:/ˈkaʊntər ˈɔːfər/ | n. |还价；反报价 | A counter-offer is a response to an offer, proposing different terms.  It's a revised offer made in response to an initial offer, typically in negotiations. |Example:  They offered me $50,000, but I made a counter-offer of $60,000.他们给了我5万美元，但我还价6万美元。|近义词：counterproposal,  revised offer |反义词：acceptance, initial offer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes a counter-offer every time. = 他每次都还价。
She often gives counter-offers in negotiations. = 她经常在谈判中还价。


一般过去时:
They made a counter-offer after considering the initial offer. = 他们考虑了最初的报价后，进行了还价。
I made a counter-offer, but they refused it. = 我还价了，但他们拒绝了。


现在完成时:
We have made several counter-offers, but they still haven't agreed. = 我们已经还价好几次了，但他们仍然没有同意。
She has received a counter-offer from another company. = 她收到了另一家公司的还价。


过去完成时:
They had already made a counter-offer before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，他们就已经还价了。
Before accepting the offer, he had considered making a counter-offer. = 在接受报价之前，他曾考虑过还价。


将来时:
I will make a counter-offer if they don't meet my expectations. = 如果他们不满足我的期望，我会还价。
They will likely make a counter-offer to our proposal. = 他们可能会对我们的提案进行还价。


虚拟语气:
If I had made a counter-offer, I might have gotten a better deal. = 如果我当时还价了，也许会得到更好的交易。
I wish I had made a counter-offer; the original offer was too low. = 我真希望我当时还价了；最初的报价太低了。


被动语态:
A counter-offer was made by the buyer. = 买方进行了还价。
The initial offer was countered with a lower price. = 最初的报价被以更低的价格反驳了。


疑问句:
Will you make a counter-offer? = 你会还价吗？
Have they made a counter-offer yet? = 他们还价了吗？


否定句:
I didn't make a counter-offer. = 我没有还价。
They didn't receive a counter-offer. = 他们没有收到还价。


PHRASE->
make a counter-offer = 还价
receive a counter-offer = 收到还价
reject a counter-offer = 拒绝还价
consider a counter-offer = 考虑还价
negotiate a counter-offer = 协商还价
