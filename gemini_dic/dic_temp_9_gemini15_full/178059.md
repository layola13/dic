reclassifying:/ˌriːˈklæsɪfaɪɪŋ/| v.|重新分类，重新归类 |The act of classifying something again, often with different criteria or into a different system.  It implies a change in the existing classification.|例句：The company is reclassifying its employees based on new skill sets. 公司正在根据新的技能组合重新对员工进行分类。

例句：The government is reclassifying certain types of waste to improve recycling efforts. 政府正在重新分类某些类型的废物以改善回收工作。


近义词：reorganizing, recategorizing, reordering


反义词：classifying (in its original sense)


词性：动词 (verb, present participle)
