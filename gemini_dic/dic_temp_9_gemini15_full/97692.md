inhumation:/ˌɪnhuˈmeɪʃən/| n. |埋葬，土葬|The act of burying a body; interment.  Inhumation is the traditional practice in many cultures.|例句：The inhumation of the body took place at dawn.  = 尸体在黎明时分下葬。|例句：Ancient inhumation practices varied widely across different cultures. = 不同文化古代的土葬习俗差异很大。|近义词：burial, interment, sepulture|反义词：exhumation, cremation
