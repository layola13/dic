iconophilist:/aɪˈkɒnəfɪlɪst/ | n. |圣像崇拜者；偶像崇拜者 | A person who venerates or admires icons or images, often religious ones.  They believe in the power and significance of images, often seeing them as a pathway to spiritual connection. | The iconophilist meticulously cleaned and restored the ancient religious icons.  圣像崇拜者一丝不苟地清洁和修复古老的宗教圣像。|  image-worshipper, idolater | iconoclast | 名词
