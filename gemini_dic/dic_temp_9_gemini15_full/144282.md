outsparkles:/ˌaʊtˈspɑːrkəl/| v.|胜过，比…更闪耀|To surpass in brilliance or splendor.  This word is a relatively rare and literary usage, suggesting a surpassing of brilliance that goes beyond mere brightness.|例句：Her diamond necklace outsparkled all the other jewelry in the room. = 她的钻石项链比房间里其他所有的珠宝都更闪耀。The performance outsparkled all expectations. = 演出超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：outshine, exceed, surpass, eclipse|反义词：be outshone by, be eclipsed by
