imitates:/ˈɪmɪteɪts/| v. |模仿，效仿|To copy someone or something's behaviour, speech, or work.|The child imitates her mother's actions.  孩子模仿她母亲的动作。|The comedian imitates famous people.  喜剧演员模仿名人。|simulates, copies, replicates|differs, originates|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imitates his father's mannerisms. = 他模仿他父亲的举止。
Birds imitate the songs of other birds. = 鸟类模仿其他鸟类的鸣叫。

一般过去时:
She imitated the actress's accent perfectly. = 她完美地模仿了那位女演员的口音。
The student imitated the teacher's example. = 学生模仿了老师的例子。

现在完成时:
He has imitated many famous artists throughout his career. = 他在他职业生涯中模仿了许多著名的艺术家。
The parrot has imitated many different sounds. = 那只鹦鹉模仿了许多不同的声音。

过去完成时:
Before the performance, she had imitated the dance moves countless times. = 演出前，她无数次地模仿过舞蹈动作。
He had imitated that style for years before he developed his own. = 在他形成自己风格之前，他已经模仿那种风格很多年了。

将来时:
She will imitate the style of famous painters for her next project. = 她将在她的下一个项目中模仿著名画家的风格。
The robot will imitate human movements with greater precision. = 机器人将更精确地模仿人类的动作。

虚拟语气:
If he imitated her style, he would be successful. = 如果他模仿她的风格，他就会成功。
I wish I could imitate his talent. = 我希望我能模仿他的才能。

被动语态:
His style was imitated by many aspiring artists. = 他的风格被许多有抱负的艺术家所模仿。
The painting was cleverly imitated. = 这幅画被巧妙地模仿了。

疑问句:
Does he imitate anyone in particular? = 他特别模仿谁吗？
Who are you imitating? = 你在模仿谁？

否定句:
He doesn't imitate others; he creates his own style. = 他不模仿别人；他创造自己的风格。
The singer did not imitate any other artists on her album. = 这位歌手在她的专辑中没有模仿任何其他艺术家。
