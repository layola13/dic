overdries:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪz/ | v. |使过度干燥；晒干过度 | To dry something excessively, resulting in brittleness, cracking, or damage.  |例句：The intense sun overdried the plants, leaving them brittle and brown.  烈日把植物晒干了，使它们变得脆弱而枯黄。|例句：The laundry was left in the dryer too long and overdried. 洗衣机里的衣服放的时间太长，烘干过度了。|近义词：dehydrate excessively, desiccate excessively |反义词：underdry, overmoisten
