enchanting:/ɪnˈtʃæntɪŋ/| adj. |迷人的，令人陶醉的 |pleasing and delightful; charming; captivating.  It suggests a magical or enchanting quality, often associated with beauty, charm, and a sense of wonder.|例句：The enchanting melody filled the air.  迷人的旋律充满了空气。

The garden was enchanting, with flowers of every color imaginable. 花园迷人，有着各种可以想象到的鲜花。


近义词：charming, delightful, captivating, magical, alluring, enchanting

反义词：repulsive, unpleasant, unappealing, boring


词源：源自动词enchant，意为“施魔法于；使着迷”。  The suffix "-ing" transforms the verb into an adjective.  The root "chant" relates to singing or incantations, suggesting a magical or captivating quality.
