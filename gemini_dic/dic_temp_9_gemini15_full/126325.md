myopic:/ˈmaɪɒpɪk/ /ˈmaɪoʊpɪk/| adj.| 近视的；缺乏远见的；目光短浅的 | lacking foresight or intellectual insight; short-sighted.  |例句：His myopic vision prevented him from seeing the bigger picture. 他的近视眼使他无法看到全局。

例句：The company's myopic focus on short-term profits led to its eventual downfall.  公司的目光短浅，只注重短期利润，最终导致了它的垮台。


近义词：short-sighted, nearsighted, narrow-minded, unperceptive

反义词：far-sighted, prescient, insightful, long-sighted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is myopic. = 他是近视的。
She is myopic in her business decisions. = 她在商业决策上目光短浅。


一般过去时:
He was myopic. = 他过去是近视的。
Their plan was myopic and ultimately failed. = 他们的计划目光短浅，最终失败了。


现在完成时:
He has been myopic since childhood. = 他从小就是近视眼。
The government has been myopic in its approach to environmental issues. = 政府在处理环境问题上目光短浅。


过去完成时:
He had been myopic for many years before he got glasses. = 他戴眼镜之前近视了很多年。
They had been myopic in their assessment of the market. = 他们在评估市场时目光短浅。


将来时:
He will likely remain myopic. = 他很可能仍然是近视的。
Their strategy will likely prove myopic in the long run. = 从长远来看，他们的战略很可能会被证明是目光短浅的。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
