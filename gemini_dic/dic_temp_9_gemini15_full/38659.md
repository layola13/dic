concussed:/kənˈkʌst/| adj.|脑震荡的；受震荡的| suffering from a concussion; having been hit hard enough to cause temporary brain dysfunction.  A concussed person may experience symptoms such as headache, dizziness, confusion, and memory loss. |例句：The boxer was concussed in the third round. 拳击手在第三回合脑震荡了。

例句：After the car accident, she was concussed and had to be hospitalized.  车祸后，她脑震荡并不得不住院。


近义词：injured, dazed, stunned, shaken up

反义词：unharmed, unscathed, fine


词性：形容词
