misled:/mɪsˈliːd/| vt.| 误导，使误信 | To give someone the wrong idea or impression, causing them to believe something that is untrue. | 例句：The misleading advertisement caused many customers to buy the faulty product.  误导性的广告导致许多顾客购买了有缺陷的产品。

The politician's speech misled the public about his true intentions. 这位政治家的讲话误导了公众对他真实意图的认识。


近义词：deceive, delude, confuse, mislead, betray | 反义词：enlighten, clarify, guide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misleads his employees with false promises. 他用虚假承诺误导他的员工。
The advertisement misleads consumers about the product's effectiveness. 广告误导消费者对产品有效性的认识。


一般过去时:
The map misled us, and we got lost. 地图误导了我们，我们迷路了。
He was misled by the cunning salesman. 他被狡猾的推销员误导了。


现在完成时:
They have been misled by the fake news. 他们一直被假新闻误导。
I have misled you unintentionally, I apologize. 我无意中误导了你，我道歉。


过去完成时:
I had been misled by his charming demeanor. 我被他迷人的举止误导了。
She realized she had misled her friends about her plans. 她意识到她误导了朋友关于她的计划。


将来时:
The lack of clarity will mislead many people in the future.  缺乏清晰度将来会误导很多人。
I fear that this information will mislead the investigators. 我担心这个信息会误导调查人员。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been misled, I would have made a different decision. 如果我没有被误导，我会做出不同的决定。
I wish I hadn't been misled by his promises. 我真希望我没有被他那些承诺误导。


被动语态:
Many people were misled by the false information. 许多人被虚假信息误导了。
The audience was misled by the speaker's rhetoric. 观众被演讲者的辞藻误导了。


疑问句:
Was the witness misled by the questioning? 证人是被提问误导了吗？
Were the investors misled by the company's financial reports? 投资者是被公司的财务报告误导了吗？


否定句:
The instructions were clear, so no one was misled. 说明很清楚，所以没有人被误导。
He tried to mislead me, but I wasn't fooled. 他试图误导我，但我没有上当。


PHRASE->
be misled by = 被……误导
misled into = 被误导去做…
grossly misled = 严重误导
completely misled = 完全误导
seriously misled = 严重误导


词源:
mis- (错误地) + lead (引导)  The prefix "mis-" indicates a wrong or bad action, and "lead" means to guide or direct.  Therefore, "mislead" means to guide someone wrongly.
