overgloominess:/ˌoʊvərˈɡluːminɪs/| adj. |过度忧郁；极度阴沉|Excessive gloominess; extreme depression or dejection.  It implies a level of sadness or pessimism that goes beyond typical unhappiness.|The overgloominess of his outlook prevented him from seeing the positive aspects of the situation.  他的观点过于悲观，以至于看不到事情积极的一面。 The overgloominess in the room was palpable. 屋子里弥漫着一种令人窒息的阴郁气氛。|近义词：despondency, melancholy, dejection|反义词：cheerfulness, optimism, buoyancy
