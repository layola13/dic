nonexploratory:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɒrətəri/| adj.|非探索性的，非探测性的|Not involving or intended for exploration or investigation.  It describes something that doesn't aim to discover or learn new things, but rather focuses on established knowledge or methods.|The study was nonexploratory, focusing solely on replicating previous findings. 这项研究是非探索性的，仅仅专注于复制之前的发现。|近义词：investigative, experimental|反义词：exploratory, investigative
