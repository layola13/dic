competitors:/kəmˈpɛtɪtərz/ | n. |竞争者，对手 |People or businesses competing for the same thing.  |例句：Our company faces tough competitors in the market.  我们的公司在市场上面临着强大的竞争对手。

例句：The two teams are fierce competitors. 这两支队伍是激烈的竞争对手。


近义词： rivals, contenders, opponents | 反义词：collaborators, allies


词源:  The word "competitor" comes from the Latin word "competere," meaning "to strive together" or "to be suitable."  It evolved through Old French "concurrente" and Middle English "competitour."
