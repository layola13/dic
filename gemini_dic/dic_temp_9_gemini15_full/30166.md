celebrations:/ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃənz/| n. |庆祝活动，庆祝 |The act of celebrating; a festive gathering or activity in honor of a special occasion.  Celebrations often involve food, music, dancing, and other forms of merrymaking. |例句：The wedding celebrations lasted for three days. 婚礼庆祝活动持续了三天。

例句：New Year's celebrations are always exciting. 新年庆祝活动总是令人兴奋的。


近义词：festivities, commemorations, observances |反义词：mourning, lamentations |词性:名词
