nonreality:/ˌnɒnriˈæləti/| n. |非现实；虚幻；不真实|The concept refers to anything that does not exist in physical reality, encompassing things like dreams, fantasies, illusions, and fictional worlds.  It often contrasts with tangible reality and objective truth.  |Example: The characters and events in the novel are pure nonreality, existing only in the author's imagination.  小说中的人物和事件纯粹是虚构的，只存在于作者的想象中。|近义词：illusion, fantasy, unreality, fiction |反义词：reality, truth, actuality
