joker:/ˈdʒoʊkər/| n.|小丑，爱开玩笑的人，玩笑，王牌|A joker is a person who makes jokes, especially as a profession.  It can also refer to a playing card with a humorous image, or something unexpected that disrupts a situation. |例句：He's a real joker, always cracking jokes. 他是个十足的小丑，总是开玩笑。|例句：The joker in the pack is the wild card.  一副牌中的小丑牌是王牌。|近义词：clown, jester, prankster, buffoon|反义词：serious person, straight-laced person
