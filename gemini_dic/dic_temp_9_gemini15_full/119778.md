mighty:/ˈmaɪti/ | adj. |强大的，巨大的；非凡的 | Powerful, strong, great; possessing great power or strength.  |例句：The mighty river carved a deep canyon.  这条强大的河流刻凿出一个深深的峡谷。

例句：She possessed a mighty spirit. 她拥有强大的精神。


近义词：powerful, strong, great, tremendous, immense | 反义词：weak, feeble, insignificant, puny


词源:  Old English *mihtig, from miht "might, power," related to Old High German maht "power," Old Norse mekt "power," Gothic mahts "power." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *magtiz.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is mighty strong. = 他非常强壮。
The engine is mighty powerful. = 这引擎非常强劲。

一般过去时:
The army was mighty and invincible. = 这支军队强大且不可战胜。
The storm was mighty and destructive. = 暴风雨强大且具有破坏性。

现在完成时:
She has become mighty wealthy. = 她已经变得非常富有。
They have achieved mighty success. = 他们取得了巨大的成功。

过去完成时:
The king had been mighty in his youth. = 国王年轻时非常强大。
The empire had grown mighty over centuries. = 这个帝国经过几个世纪的增长变得非常强大。

将来时:
The nation will be mighty in the future. = 这个国家将来会很强大。
The project will be mighty expensive. = 这个项目将会非常昂贵。

虚拟语气:
If I were mighty, I would help the poor. = 如果我很有权势，我会帮助穷人。
If he had been mighty, he wouldn't have failed. = 如果他很强大，他就不会失败了。

被动语态:
The castle was deemed mighty by all. = 这座城堡被所有人认为非常强大。
The problem was considered mighty complex. = 这个问题被认为非常复杂。

疑问句:
Is he mighty enough to lift that rock? = 他足够强大能举起那块石头吗？
Was the earthquake mighty enough to cause damage? = 地震强大到足以造成破坏吗？

否定句:
He is not mighty enough to win the competition. = 他不够强大无法赢得比赛。
