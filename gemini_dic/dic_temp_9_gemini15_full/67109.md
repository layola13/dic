explosions:/ɪkˈsploʊʒənz/ | n. | 爆炸；爆发 | A sudden and violent bursting or breaking apart of something, especially with a loud noise.  The rapid release of energy from a confined space, resulting in a sudden expansion of volume and often accompanied by intense heat, light, and sound. |例句：The explosions shook the city to its foundations. 爆炸震动了这座城市的地基。

例句：Several explosions were heard in the distance. 远处传来几声爆炸声。


近义词： blasts, detonations, eruptions

反义词： implosions
