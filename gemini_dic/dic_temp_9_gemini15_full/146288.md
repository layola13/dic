overfastidiousness: /ˌoʊvər fæˈstɪdiəsnəs/ | n. | 过分挑剔；吹毛求疵 | Excessive attention to detail; meticulousness to an excessive degree.  This can manifest as an inability to tolerate imperfection or a constant striving for unattainable levels of precision.  It often leads to frustration and inefficiency. |


例句：

Her overfastidiousness prevented her from finishing the project on time.  她的过分挑剔导致她无法按时完成项目。

His overfastidiousness in cleaning his apartment made it difficult for him to relax. 他打扫公寓的过分挑剔让他难以放松。


近义词：perfectionism, meticulousness, scrupulousness, over-precision


反义词：sloppiness, carelessness, laxity, negligence


词性：名词
