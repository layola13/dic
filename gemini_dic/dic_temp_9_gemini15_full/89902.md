horrifies:/ˈhɒrɪfaɪz/ /ˈhɔːrɪfaɪz/| vt.|使恐惧；使惊骇；|to fill with horror; to shock greatly.|The news of the accident horrified everyone.  事故的消息使每个人都感到恐惧。|The gruesome details of the crime horrified the jury.  这起犯罪的可怕细节让陪审团感到震惊。|terrifies, frightens, shocks|amuses, delights|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The movie horrifies the audience. = 这部电影使观众感到恐惧。
The thought of failure horrifies him. = 失败的想法让他感到恐惧。

一般过去时:
The sight of the accident horrified me. = 事故的景象让我感到恐惧。
The news horrified her parents. = 这个消息让她父母感到震惊。

现在完成时:
The recent events have horrified the nation. = 最近发生的事件震惊了全国。
His actions have horrified many people. = 他的行为让许多人感到震惊。

过去完成时:
I had never been so horrified in my life. = 我一生中从未如此恐惧过。
They had been horrified by what they saw. = 他们被他们所看到的东西吓坏了。

将来时:
The results of the experiment will likely horrify many scientists. = 实验的结果可能会让许多科学家感到震惊。
The upcoming news report will horrify the public. = 即将来临的新闻报道将使公众感到震惊。

虚拟语气:
If I saw that again, it would horrify me. = 如果我再次看到那样的事情，我会感到恐惧。
I wish the news hadn't horrified me so much. = 我希望这个消息没有让我如此恐惧。

被动语态:
The audience was horrified by the ending. = 观众被结局吓坏了。
Many people are horrified by the violence in the city. = 许多人对城市中的暴力感到恐惧。

疑问句:
Does the thought of public speaking horrify you? = 公开演讲的想法会让你感到恐惧吗？
What aspects of the story horrified you the most? = 故事中哪些方面最让你感到恐惧？

否定句:
The film didn't horrify me as much as I expected. = 这部电影并没有像我预期的那样让我感到恐惧。
The situation didn't horrify them at all. = 这种情况并没有让他们感到恐惧。


PHRASE->
