mooningly:/ˈmuːnɪŋli/| adv.| 月亮般地；沉思地；茫然地 | dreamily or pensively; in a moonlit way.  |例句：She gazed mooningly out the window. 她茫然地凝视着窗外。

例句：He sat mooningly by the fire, lost in thought. 他沉思地坐在火炉旁，陷入沉思。

近义词：dreamily, pensively, wistfully, reflectively

反义词：actively, energetically, briskly


词性:副词
