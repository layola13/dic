querulous:/ˈkwɛrələs/| adj.|抱怨的；爱发牢骚的；易怒的 | Given to complaining; peevish; fretful.  |例句：The querulous child cried until he got what he wanted.  这个爱抱怨的孩子哭着直到他得到了他想要的东西。

例句：Her querulous tone annoyed everyone in the room. 她爱抱怨的语气惹恼了房间里的每个人。


近义词：complaining, grumbling, peevish, fretful, irritable, complaining, whiny


反义词：cheerful, pleasant, amiable, good-natured, content


词源:  From Medieval Latin *querulosus, from Latin querulus "complaining," from queror "I complain."  The ultimate origin is likely Proto-Indo-European *kʷer- "to cry out."
