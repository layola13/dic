minor-league: /ˈmaɪnərliːɡ/ | adj. n. | 小联盟的；次要的；不太重要的 |  Relating to or denoting minor league baseball, or a similar level of competition in other sports.  It can also refer to something of lesser importance or significance. |

例句：

He played in the minor leagues for several years before making it to the majors. 他在小联盟打了几年球，然后才进入大联盟。

The company's minor-league affiliates are struggling financially. 这家公司的下属小公司正在财务上挣扎。


近义词： secondary, lesser, subordinate, inferior

反义词： major-league, principal, primary, major


词性：形容词，名词

词源：

* **minor:**  From Latin *minor*, comparative of *minus* "less".  Meaning "smaller" or "lesser".

* **league:** From Old English *lēg*, "a bond, a tie, a covenant".  In the context of sports, it refers to an association of teams competing against each other.
