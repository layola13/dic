carcinogens:/kɑːrˈsɪnədʒənz/| n. |致癌物|Substances that cause cancer.  Carcinogens can be chemicals, radiation, or certain biological agents.  Exposure to carcinogens increases the risk of developing cancer.|Many studies have linked cigarette smoke to lung cancer because it contains numerous carcinogens.  许多研究将香烟烟雾与肺癌联系起来，因为它含有许多致癌物。|mutagens, oncogens|benign substances.
