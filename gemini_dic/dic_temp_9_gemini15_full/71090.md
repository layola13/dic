fish-farms:/ˈfɪʃfɑːrmz/| n.| 养鱼场 |A fish farm, also known as an aquaculture farm, is a facility that raises fish commercially.  It involves cultivating fish in a controlled environment, often tanks, ponds, or cages, to produce food for human consumption.  These farms can range in size from small operations to large-scale industrial enterprises.|例句：Many coastal communities rely on fish farms for jobs and economic stability.  许多沿海社区依靠养鱼场来维持就业和经济稳定。


例句：The fish farm uses sustainable practices to minimize its environmental impact. 这个养鱼场采用可持续措施来最大限度地减少其对环境的影响。


近义词：aquaculture farms, fish hatcheries|反义词：wild fisheries
