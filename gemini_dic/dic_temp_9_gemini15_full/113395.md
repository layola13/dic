lying: /ˈlaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 说谎；位于；躺着 | To make a statement that one knows is untrue; to be in a horizontal position; to be situated. |  例句：He was lying about his age. 他在撒谎关于他的年龄。|近义词：fabricating, fibbing, deceiving,  reclining, situated |反义词：telling the truth, standing, upright


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is lying on the sofa. = 他躺在沙发上。
She is lying about her qualifications. = 她在说谎她的资历。


一般过去时:
He lied to me about the accident. = 他向我撒了关于事故的谎。
I was lying in bed when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候我躺在床上。


现在完成时:
He has been lying to us for years. = 他多年来一直在欺骗我们。
She has lain there for hours. = 她在那里躺了几个小时。


过去完成时:
He had been lying in wait for his prey. = 他一直在等待猎物。
She had lain down to rest before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临之前她已经躺下休息了。


将来时:
I will be lying on the beach tomorrow. = 我明天将在海滩上躺着。
He will lie to you again if you give him the chance. = 如果你给他机会，他会再次欺骗你。


虚拟语气:
If I were lying, I would feel guilty. = 如果我在说谎，我会感到内疚。
I wish I hadn't lied to her. = 我真希望我没有对她撒谎。


被动语态:
The body was found lying in the street. = 尸体被发现躺在街上。
The truth is often buried, and lies are often told.  真相往往被掩盖，谎言往往被诉说。(被动含义隐含)


疑问句:
Is he lying? = 他在说谎吗？
Where is the book lying? = 书放在哪里？


否定句:
He isn't lying; he's telling the truth. = 他没有说谎；他说的是实话。
The object wasn't lying where it should be. = 物体不在它应该在的地方。


PHRASE->
lie down = 躺下
lie in wait = 埋伏
lie about = 对…撒谎
tell a lie = 说谎
a white lie = 善意的谎言
