modeller:/ˈmɒdələr/| n. |模型制作者；建模者；造型师|A person who creates models, often in fields like fashion, architecture, or computer graphics.  They design, build, and refine models to represent real-world objects or systems. |He's a skilled modeller, creating intricate miniature buildings. 他是一位技艺精湛的模型制作师，制作精巧的微型建筑。She's a successful fashion modeller, known for her unique style. 她是一位成功的时尚模特，以其独特的风格而闻名。|designer, creator, architect (in specific contexts)|deconstructor, destroyer (in contrast to the creation aspect)|名词
