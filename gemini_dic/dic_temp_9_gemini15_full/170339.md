province:/ˈprɒvɪns/ /ˈprɑːvɪns/| n. |省份，行政区；领域，范围|A province is a main administrative division of certain countries or empires.  It can also refer to a particular area or field of activity.|Example: Ontario is a province in Canada. 安大略是加拿大的一個省份。|Example:The province of medicine has seen huge advances in recent years.医学领域近年来取得了巨大进步。|近义词：territory, region, area|反义词：nation, country (in the context of being a larger political entity)|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This province produces a lot of wheat. = 这个省份生产大量的麦子。
Which province are you from? = 你是哪个省份的人？


一般过去时:
That province suffered a severe drought last year. = 那个省份去年遭受了严重的干旱。
He lived in that province for many years. = 他在那省住了很多年。


现在完成时:
This province has experienced significant economic growth. = 这个省份经历了显著的经济增长。
The government has invested heavily in infrastructure in this province. = 政府在这个省份大力投资基础设施。


过去完成时:
Before the war, that province had enjoyed a period of peace. = 在战争爆发前，那个省份享受过一段和平时期。
They had already left the province before the news arrived. = 消息传来之前，他们已经离开了那个省份。


将来时:
This province will likely see an increase in tourism next year. = 这个省份明年可能会看到旅游业的增长。
What will the government do to help this province? = 政府将如何帮助这个省份？


虚拟语气:
If this province were to become independent, it would face many challenges. = 如果这个省份独立，它将面临许多挑战。
I wish this province had better public transportation. = 我希望这个省份有更好的公共交通。


被动语态:
The province is governed by a provincial government. = 该省由省政府管辖。
Many new laws were passed by the province last year. = 去年该省通过了许多新法律。


疑问句:
Which province has the highest population? = 哪一个省份人口最多？
What are the main industries in this province? = 这个省份的主要产业是什么？


否定句:
That province does not produce much oil. = 那个省份不生产多少石油。
He has never been to that province before. = 他以前从未去过那个省份。
