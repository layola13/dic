oriented:/ˈɔːriɛntɪd/ | /ˈɔːriˌɛntɪd/ | adj. | 以…为方向的；面向…的；定向的 |  Having a specified direction, position, or orientation.  It can also refer to someone's beliefs, interests, or attitudes. | 例句：The building is oriented towards the south.  这栋建筑朝南。  He's very career-oriented. 他非常注重事业。 | 近义词：directed, focused, aimed, aligned | 反义词：disoriented, confused, unfocused


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The compass is oriented north. = 指南针指向北方。
Her research is oriented towards finding a cure. = 她的研究方向是寻找治疗方法。


一般过去时:
The map was oriented incorrectly. = 地图方向标注错误。
The company was oriented towards customer satisfaction. = 公司以客户满意为导向。


现在完成时:
The satellite has been oriented correctly. = 卫星已经正确定向。
My priorities have been oriented towards family. = 我的优先考虑已经转向家庭。


过去完成时:
The ship had been oriented towards the storm before it hit. = 船在风暴来临之前已经朝向风暴方向。
The team had been oriented towards the project's goals. = 团队之前已经确定了项目目标。


将来时:
The telescope will be oriented towards the nebula. = 望远镜将指向星云。
The company will be oriented towards sustainability. = 公司将致力于可持续发展。


虚拟语气:
If the antenna were oriented correctly, we would receive a stronger signal. = 如果天线方向正确，我们会收到更强的信号。
If the company were more customer-oriented, it would have more loyal customers. = 如果公司更注重客户，它将会有更多忠实客户。


被动语态:
The furniture was oriented to maximize space. = 家具摆放是为了最大限度地利用空间。
The curriculum is oriented towards practical skills. = 教学大纲侧重于实用技能。


疑问句:
Is the camera properly oriented? = 相机方向正确吗？
Is the discussion oriented towards a solution? = 讨论的方向是寻求解决方案吗？


否定句:
The map is not oriented correctly. = 地图方向标注不正确。
His approach is not results-oriented. = 他的方法不是以结果为导向的。


PHRASE->

goal-oriented: 目标导向的
future-oriented: 未来导向的
service-oriented: 服务导向的
