fatuitous:/ˈfætʃuəs/| adj.|愚蠢的；轻率的；荒谬的 | Foolish or inane; silly.  Characterized by foolishness or lack of seriousness; absurd. |例句：His fatuous remarks annoyed everyone.  他愚蠢的言论惹恼了所有人。

例句：That's a fatuous idea; it will never work. 那是個愚蠢的想法；它永遠不會奏效。


近义词：silly, foolish, absurd, inane, ridiculous, nonsensical

反义词：sensible, intelligent, wise, judicious, rational


词性：形容词

词源:
The word "fatuous" ultimately derives from the Latin word "fatuus," meaning "foolish" or "stupid."  The word entered English in the 16th century.
