re-arm:/ˌriːˈɑːrm/| vt.|重新武装；再次装备|To arm again; to equip with weapons again.  The word is formed by the prefix "re-" meaning "again" or "anew" and the verb "arm" meaning "to equip with weapons".|例句：The army will re-arm with the latest technology.  军队将装备最新技术重新武装。

The country decided to re-arm after the attack.  该国在遭到袭击后决定重新武装。|近义词：re-equip, refit|反义词：disarm, demilitarize|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-arm their forces every year. = 他们每年都重新武装部队。
The company re-arms its products with new features. = 公司用新功能重新武装其产品。

一般过去时:
The rebels re-armed themselves after the truce failed. = 叛军在停火失败后重新武装自己。
The nation re-armed in response to the growing threat. = 面对日益增长的威胁，国家重新武装起来。

现在完成时:
They have re-armed their military with advanced weaponry. = 他们已经用先进武器重新武装了军队。
The country has re-armed significantly since the conflict. = 自冲突以来，该国已经进行了大规模的重新武装。

过去完成时:
The army had re-armed before the invasion began. = 军队在入侵开始之前就已经重新武装了。
They had re-armed themselves secretly before the uprising. = 他们在起义之前秘密地重新武装了自己。


将来时:
The government will re-arm its defense forces next year. = 政府明年将重新武装其国防部队。
We will re-arm our position with more evidence. = 我们将用更多证据来巩固我们的立场（重新武装我们的论点）。


虚拟语气:
If they had re-armed sooner, the outcome might have been different. = 如果他们早点重新武装，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish we had re-armed before the attack. = 我希望我们在袭击前就重新武装了。


被动语态:
The troops were re-armed with modern rifles. = 部队配备了现代步枪重新武装。
The outdated weapons were re-armed with new parts. = 过时的武器用新零件重新装备。


疑问句:
Will the country re-arm its navy? = 这个国家会重新武装它的海军吗？
Has the army been re-armed with better equipment? = 军队是否配备了更好的装备重新武装？


否定句:
The government refused to re-arm its military. = 政府拒绝重新武装军队。
They did not re-arm their defenses adequately. = 他们没有充分地重新武装他们的防御力量。
