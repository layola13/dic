dismantling: /dɪsˈmæntliŋ/ | v. | 拆卸；废除；瓦解 | The process of taking something apart, especially a structure or system; to take apart a machine or structure.  |例句：The old building is being dismantled. 这座旧建筑正在被拆除。


例句：The government is dismantling the old social welfare system. 政府正在废除旧的社会福利制度。


近义词：disassembling, taking apart, demolishing, destroying, abolishing


反义词：assembling, constructing, building, establishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are dismantling the old bridge. = 他们正在拆除旧桥。
We dismantle the machine every year for maintenance. = 我们每年都会拆卸机器进行维护。


一般过去时:
The workers dismantled the factory equipment. = 工人们拆除了工厂设备。
They dismantled the old regime. = 他们推翻了旧政权。


现在完成时:
They have dismantled the entire system. = 他们已经拆除了整个系统。
We have dismantled the old car engine. = 我们已经拆卸了旧汽车发动机。


过去完成时:
They had dismantled the bomb before it exploded. = 他们在炸弹爆炸前已经拆除了它。
The rebels had dismantled the government's communication network. = 反叛者已经破坏了政府的通讯网络。


将来时:
They will dismantle the statue next week. = 他们下周将拆除这座雕像。
The company will dismantle its operations in that country. = 公司将撤销在那个国家的业务。


虚拟语气:
If they dismantled the wall, they would have a better view. = 如果他们拆除了这堵墙，他们就会有更好的视野。
I wish they had dismantled the outdated equipment sooner. = 我希望他们早点拆除过时的设备。


被动语态:
The old car is being dismantled for parts. = 旧车正在被拆解成零件。
The outdated system was dismantled last year. = 过时的系统去年被拆除了。


疑问句:
Are they dismantling the building? = 他们正在拆除建筑物吗？
Is the old system being dismantled? = 旧系统正在被拆除吗？


否定句:
They are not dismantling the machine yet. = 他们还没拆卸机器。
The government did not dismantle the program. = 政府没有取消该计划。
