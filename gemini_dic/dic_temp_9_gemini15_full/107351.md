lamella:/ləˈmɛlə/| n.|薄片；层片；片层|A thin layer, scale, or plate-like structure, especially one of a parallel series in animal or plant tissue.|例句：The bone structure showed many lamellae arranged in a concentric pattern. 骨骼结构显示许多同心排列的薄片。|例句：The geologist examined the rock's lamellae to determine its age. 地质学家检查了岩石的薄片以确定其年代。|近义词：layer, plate, scale, stratum|反义词：mass, block
