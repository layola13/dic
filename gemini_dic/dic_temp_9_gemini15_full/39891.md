constitution:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtʃuːʃən/| n. |宪法；章程；构成；体格；体质 |The fundamental principles or established precedents according to which a state or other organization is acknowledged to be governed.  It can also refer to the physical structure of a person or thing. |例句：The US Constitution guarantees freedom of speech. 美国宪法保障言论自由。 The constitution of the club is very strict. 这个俱乐部的章程非常严格。He has a strong constitution. 他体格强壮。|近义词：charter, framework, makeup, physique |反义词：destruction, demolition
