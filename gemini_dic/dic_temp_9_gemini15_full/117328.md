meddlesomeness:/ˈmɛdəlsəmˌnɛsɪz/ | n. |爱管闲事；干涉；多管闲事 | The meddlesomeness of her neighbors drove her crazy.  |近义词：interference, intrusion, intervention |反义词：noninterference, apathy, unconcern


英文解析:  Meddlesomeness refers to the quality or state of being meddlesome; the tendency to interfere in other people's affairs or business without being asked or needed.  It implies an excessive and often unwelcome involvement in matters that do not concern the person.


例句:

Her constant meddlesomeness in family matters caused considerable friction.  她的不断干涉家庭事务引起了相当大的摩擦。


The teacher's meddlesomeness extended to correcting the students' personal lives. 老师的干涉甚至延伸到纠正学生们的私生活。


近义词: interference, intrusion, intervention, officiousness


反义词: noninterference, apathy, unconcern, indifference


词性：名词 (noun)


注意：此词为meddlesome的名词形式，meddlesome意为爱管闲事的，多管闲事的。  meddlesomeness则强调这种爱管闲事的行为或状态。  没有提供语法时态表格，因为meddlesomeness本身是名词，不具备动词的时态变化。
