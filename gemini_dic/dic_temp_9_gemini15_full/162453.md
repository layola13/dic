preadopting: /ˌpriːəˈdɒptɪŋ/ | v. | 预先收养；提前收养 |  The act of adopting a child before the legal process is complete.  This often involves a period of fostering or pre-placement care. | 例句：The couple were preadopting a baby from a local orphanage, hoping to finalize the adoption soon. 这对夫妇正在从当地孤儿院预先收养一个婴儿，希望很快能完成收养手续。


例句：The agency supported preadopting families with counseling and resources. 该机构为预先收养的家庭提供咨询和资源。


近义词：fostering (in the context of pre-adoption care), pre-placement care


反义词：post-adoption


词性：动词
