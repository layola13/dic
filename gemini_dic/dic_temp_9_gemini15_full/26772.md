caesarians:/sɪˈzəriən/| n.|剖腹产，剖宫产|The surgical delivery of a baby by incision through the mother's abdomen and uterus.|例句：She had a caesarean section after experiencing complications during labor. 她分娩时出现并发症，进行了剖腹产手术。

例句：The doctor recommended a caesarean because the baby was breech. 因为胎儿臀位，医生建议进行剖腹产。


近义词：cesarean section, C-section|反义词：natural childbirth, vaginal delivery|词性:名词
