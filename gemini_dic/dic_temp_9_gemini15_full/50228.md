depend on:/dɪˈpend ɒn/| phrasal verb|依靠，依赖，取决于|To rely on someone or something for support, help, or supply.|例句：I depend on my family for emotional support. 我依靠我的家人获得情感支持。

例句：The success of the project depends on the weather.  项目的成功取决于天气。|近义词：rely on, count on, hinge on|反义词：be independent of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I depend on my car to get to work. = 我依靠我的车上班。
We depend on the rain for our crops. = 我们依靠雨水种植庄稼。

一般过去时:
She depended on her parents for financial help. = 她依赖父母的经济资助。
They depended on each other for support during difficult times. = 他们在困难时期互相依靠。

现在完成时:
I have depended on public transport for years. = 多年来我一直依靠公共交通工具。
He has depended on his savings to survive. = 他一直依靠积蓄生存。

过去完成时:
She had depended on him to finish the project, but he failed. = 她曾依赖他完成项目，但他失败了。
They had depended on the forecast to plan their trip, but it was wrong. = 他们曾依赖天气预报来计划他们的旅行，但预报错了。

将来时:
I will depend on you to help me move. = 我将依靠你帮我搬家。
We will depend on the market research to guide our decisions. = 我们将依靠市场调研来指导我们的决策。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't depend on my job, I would travel the world. = 如果我不依赖我的工作，我会周游世界。
I wish I didn't depend so much on my phone. = 我希望我不那么依赖我的手机。

被动语态:
The outcome of the election depends on many factors. = 大选的结果取决于许多因素。(被动语态表达不常见，通常用主动语态)
The success of the business is depended on its employees' hard work. =  企业的成功取决于员工的努力工作。(被动语态表达不常见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Does the result depend on the experiment? = 结果取决于实验吗？
Can we depend on him to finish the job on time? = 我们能指望他按时完成工作吗？

否定句:
You can't depend on the weather to be good all the time. = 你不能指望天气一直好。
The outcome does not depend on a single factor. = 结果并不取决于单一因素。


PHRASE->
