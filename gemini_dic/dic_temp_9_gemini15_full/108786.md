least:/liːst/| adj., pron., n. |最少的；最小；最少量的；最低限度|The word "least" is an adjective, pronoun, and noun that signifies the smallest amount or degree. It is the superlative form of "little."  As an adjective, it modifies nouns; as a pronoun, it stands in place of a noun phrase; and as a noun, it represents the smallest quantity or degree. |例句：This is the least expensive option. (adj.) 这是最便宜的选择。  At least, we tried. (adv.) 至少，我们尝试过了。  Of the three, he did the least work. (pron.) 在三个人中，他做的工作最少。|近义词：minimum, smallest, fewest |反义词：most, greatest, maximum


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats the least amount of food. = 他吃的食物最少。
She drinks the least coffee in the office. = 她在办公室喝咖啡最少。

一般过去时:
They spent the least money on their trip. = 他们在旅行中花了最少的钱。
He complained the least about the situation. = 他对这种情况抱怨最少。

现在完成时:
She has done the least work out of the team. = 在团队中，她做的工作最少。
He has received the least amount of support. = 他收到的支持最少。

过去完成时:
I had done the least amount of preparation for the test. = 我对考试的准备工作最少。
They had received the least information before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前收到的信息最少。


将来时:
We will spend the least time on that project. = 我们将在那个项目上花费最少的时间。
He will make the least effort to pass the exam. = 他将为通过考试付出最少的努力。


虚拟语气:
If I had the least doubt, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我有一点点怀疑，我就不会那样做了。
I wish I had the least idea of what to do next. = 我希望我至少知道下一步该做什么。


被动语态:
The least attention was paid to this issue. = 这个问题受到了最少的关注。
The least amount of time was allocated for the meeting. = 分配给会议的时间最少。


疑问句:
Which option requires the least effort? = 哪个选项需要最少的努力？
Who did the least amount of work? = 谁做的工作最少？


否定句:
He did not do the least bit of work. = 他一点工作都没做。
She showed the least amount of interest in the topic. = 她对这个话题表现出最少的兴趣。
