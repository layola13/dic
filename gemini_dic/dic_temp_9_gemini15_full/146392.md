overflown:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊn/| v. |泛滥的；溢出的；充满的 | Past participle of overflow.  Describes a state where something has exceeded its capacity and spilled over. It can refer to liquids, emotions, or even information. |例句：The river overflown its banks after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。

The inbox overflown with spam emails.收件箱里塞满了垃圾邮件。

|近义词：inundated, flooded, brimming, overwhelmed |反义词：drained, emptied, depleted |动词
