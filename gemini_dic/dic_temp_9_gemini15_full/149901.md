palissy:/pəˈlisi/| n.|帕利西(人名)|Bernard Palissy (c. 1510 – c. 1590) was a French potter, inventor, and writer.  He is renowned for his highly distinctive and elaborately decorated earthenware, often featuring naturalistic depictions of plants and animals. His work significantly influenced the development of ceramic art.|The Palissy ware is highly collectible.|近义词:None|反义词:None
