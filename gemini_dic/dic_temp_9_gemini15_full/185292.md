retreater:/rɪˈtriːtər/ | n. | 撤退者；退却者；隐居者 | A person who retreats, especially from a battle or difficult situation.  Someone who withdraws from society or a particular activity to find peace or solitude. | Example: The general ordered a retreat, and the retreaters quickly fell back to their lines.  将军下令撤退，撤退者迅速退回到他们的防线上。 |近义词：recluse, hermit, deserter | 反义词：attacker, aggressor, leader


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He retreats from conflict. = 他回避冲突。
She retreats to her room to read. = 她退回到房间里看书。


一般过去时:
The army retreated after the battle. = 战斗结束后，军队撤退了。
He retreated into himself after the loss. = 失败后，他变得沉默寡言。


现在完成时:
They have retreated from their earlier positions. = 他们已经放弃了之前的立场。
She has retreated from public life. = 她已经退出了公众生活。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had retreated before the enemy arrived. = 敌人到达之前，士兵们已经撤退了。
He had retreated to his cabin before the storm hit. = 风暴来临之前，他已经退回到自己的小屋里了。


将来时:
We will retreat if necessary. = 必要时我们将撤退。
She will retreat to the mountains for a peaceful vacation. = 她将到山区去度过一个宁静的假期。


虚拟语气:
If they had retreated earlier, they would have survived. = 如果他们早点撤退，他们就能活下来了。
I wish I had retreated from that deal. = 我真希望我没有参与那笔交易。


被动语态:
The army was retreated by the general. = 军队被将军下令撤退了。 (This is less common;  the active voice is preferred.)
The position was retreated from strategically. = 战略上放弃了这个阵地。


疑问句:
Did they retreat? = 他们撤退了吗？
Will he retreat from the challenge? = 他会逃避挑战吗？


否定句:
They did not retreat. = 他们没有撤退。
He will not retreat from his beliefs. = 他不会放弃自己的信仰。


PHRASE->

spiritual retreat:灵性静修
silent retreat:静修
retreat from reality:逃避现实
strategic retreat:战略性撤退
tactical retreat:战术性撤退
