gunning:/ˈɡʌnɪŋ/| v.|  积极寻找；努力争取；(口)寻衅；瞄准 |To search actively for something; to strive to achieve something; (informal) to look for a fight; to aim a gun. |例句：He's gunning for the promotion. 他正在努力争取晋升。|近义词：searching, striving, aiming, seeking |反义词：neglecting, ignoring, avoiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are gunning for the top spot in the league. = 他们在努力争取联赛的榜首位置。
He is always gunning for trouble. = 他总是惹是生非。


一般过去时:
She gunned for the scholarship. = 她努力争取奖学金。
The hunter gunned for the deer. = 猎人瞄准了鹿。


现在完成时:
They have been gunning for him since the incident. = 自那件事发生以来，他们一直在找他的麻烦。
He has gunned for that position for years. = 多年来，他一直在努力争取那个职位。


过去完成时:
They had been gunning for the company's CEO for months before he resigned. = 在CEO辞职前几个月，他们就一直在对他下手。
She had gunned for a better job before finally finding one. = 在最终找到一份更好的工作之前，她一直在努力争取。


将来时:
They will be gunning for revenge. = 他们将会报复。
He will gun for the championship next year. = 他明年将会争取冠军。


虚拟语气:
If I were gunning for the job, I would have prepared better. = 如果我要争取这份工作，我会做得更好准备。
I wish I hadn't been gunning for a fight that night. = 我真希望那天晚上我没有去惹事。


被动语态:
The CEO was gunned for by his rivals. = 首席执行官受到了竞争对手的攻击。
The position was gunned for by many ambitious employees. = 许多有抱负的员工都在争取这个职位。


疑问句:
Is he gunning for my job? = 他是在觊觎我的工作吗？
Are they gunning for a fight? = 他们是在寻衅滋事吗？


否定句:
He wasn't gunning for a fight; he was just defending himself. = 他不是在寻衅滋事，他只是在自卫。
She wasn't gunning for the promotion; she was happy with her current position. = 她并没有努力争取晋升；她对现在的工作很满意。


PHRASE->
gunning for someone = 找某人麻烦
gunning for a job/promotion = 努力争取工作/晋升
gunning for trouble = 惹是生非
gunning down = 射杀
gunning for the top spot= 争夺第一名
