conditionings:/kənˈdɪʃənɪŋz/ | n. | 条件反射；制约；训练；环境因素 | The process of learning through association, where a neutral stimulus becomes associated with a significant stimulus, eventually eliciting a similar response.  Conditioning can be classical (Pavlovian) or operant (instrumental). | 例句：The dog's salivation is a result of classical conditioning.  狗的流口水是经典条件反射的结果。

His fear of spiders is a type of conditioning acquired in childhood. 他对蜘蛛的恐惧是在童年时期获得的一种条件反射。| 近义词：training, learning, habituation | 反义词：unlearning, extinction
