megaphonically: /ˌmɛɡəˈfɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 扩音器般地；大声地，清晰地 | In a manner characteristic of a megaphone; loudly and clearly. | 例句：He addressed the crowd megaphonically, ensuring everyone could hear his message. 他用扩音器般的声音向人群讲话，确保每个人都能听到他的信息。


例句：The announcement was made megaphonically across the stadium.  这个通知是在体育场里用扩音器大声宣布的。


近义词：loudly, audibly, forcefully, boomingly


反义词：softly, quietly, inaudibly


词性：副词
