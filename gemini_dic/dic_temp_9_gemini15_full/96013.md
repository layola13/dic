incurvity:/ɪnˈkɜːrvɪti/ | n. |弯曲；曲率 |The lack of straightness; curvature.  In geometry, it refers to the degree of curvature.  The term is less common than "curvature" and often used in more specialized contexts. |例句：The incurvity of the road made driving difficult. 这条路的弯曲使驾驶变得困难。

The high incurvity of the lens caused significant distortion. 透镜的高曲率导致了明显的畸变。


近义词：curvature, flexure, bend


反义词：straightness, rectilinearity


词性：名词

词源:  The word "incurvity" is derived from the Latin prefix "in-" (meaning "in" or "into") and "curvitas" (meaning "curvature").  It's a less frequently used synonym of "curvature," emphasizing the inward or concave aspect of the bending.
