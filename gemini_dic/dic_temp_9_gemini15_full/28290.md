capturing: /ˈkæptʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 捕获；拍摄；吸引；表达 |  The act of capturing something involves taking possession of it, often by force or skill.  It can also refer to recording something, like an image or sound, or expressing a feeling or idea. |

例句：

The photographer spent hours capturing the perfect sunset.  = 摄影师花了数小时拍摄完美的日落。

The police successfully captured the thief. = 警方成功抓获了小偷。


近义词： seizing, trapping, catching, recording, expressing, conveying

反义词： releasing, freeing, losing, missing


词性：动词
