carve-ups:/ˈkɑːrv ʌps/| n.|分割，瓜分，分配|The carve-up of the company's assets was contentious. 公司资产的分割存在争议。|近义词：division, distribution, sharing |反义词：unification, consolidation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee carves up the budget every year. = 委员会每年都会分割预算。
How do they carve up the profits? = 他们如何分配利润？


一般过去时:
They carved up the land among themselves. = 他们私下瓜分了土地。
The politicians carved up the spoils of victory. = 政客们瓜分了胜利的果实。


现在完成时:
They have carved up the territory between them. = 他们已经瓜分了这片领土。
The company has carved up its assets among its shareholders. = 公司已经将其资产分配给股东。


过去完成时:
They had already carved up the cake before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经把蛋糕分好了。
The empire had been carved up by the victorious powers. = 这个帝国已经被战胜国瓜分了。


将来时:
They will carve up the estate after the will is read. = 遗嘱宣读后，他们将分割遗产。
How will they carve up the market share? = 他们将如何瓜分市场份额？


虚拟语气:
If they carved up the land fairly, there would be less conflict. = 如果他们公平地分配土地，冲突就会减少。
I wish they had not carved up the company so hastily. = 我真希望他们没有这么匆忙地瓜分公司。


被动语态:
The profits were carved up amongst the partners. = 利润被合伙人瓜分了。
The territory was carved up by the warring factions. = 这片领土被交战各派瓜分了。


疑问句:
How will the inheritance be carved up? = 遗产将如何分配？
Who will carve up the pie? = 谁来分派？


否定句:
They did not carve up the land equitably. = 他们没有公平地分配土地。
The resources were not carved up efficiently. = 资源没有被有效地分配。
