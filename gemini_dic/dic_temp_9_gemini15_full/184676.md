restrung:/riːˈstrʌŋ/| v. |重新穿弦；重新组织；重新调整|To string something again; to reorganize or rearrange something.|例句：The tennis player restrung his racquet after the match.  网球运动员在比赛后重新穿了他的球拍。
例句：She restrung her argument to make it more persuasive. 她重新组织了自己的论点，使其更有说服力。|近义词：reorganize, rearrange, restring, rewire|反义词：disorganize, disarrange
