skunking:/ˈskʌŋkiŋ/| n. |臭鼬行为；使…难堪；搞砸|The act of a skunk spraying its foul-smelling musk;  to ruin something; to make someone feel embarrassed or humiliated.|例句：The skunk's skunking kept the dog away. 臭鼬的喷射驱赶了狗。  The team's poor performance was a real skunking. 球队的糟糕表现真是太糟糕了。|近义词：sabotage, spoiling, ruining, embarrassing|反义词：success, triumph


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is skunking the project. = 他正在搞砸这个项目。
They are skunking the competition. = 他们在使竞争对手难堪。


一般过去时:
The skunk skunked the dog. = 臭鼬喷了狗一身臭气。
She skunked her presentation. = 她把她的演示搞砸了。


现在完成时:
He has skunked three projects this year. = 他今年已经搞砸了三个项目。
They have skunked the opposition repeatedly. = 他们已经多次使对手难堪。


过去完成时:
They had skunked the deal before we arrived. =在我们到达之前，他们已经把交易搞砸了。
She had skunked her exam by not studying. =她因为没有学习而考试不及格。


将来时:

They will skunk their rivals with their new product. =他们将用他们的新产品击败竞争对手。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
