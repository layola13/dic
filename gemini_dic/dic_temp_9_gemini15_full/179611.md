reenact:/ˌriːˈækt/| vt.|再现；重演；重新制定|To perform or represent again; to act out a past event.|例句：The students reenacted the historical battle. 学生们重演了那场历史战役。

例句：The play reenacted the events of the French Revolution. 这部戏剧重现了法国大革命的事件。


近义词：reproduce, replay, recreate, restage|反义词：destroy, abolish, undo|动词
