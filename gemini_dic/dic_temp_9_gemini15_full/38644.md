concur:/kənˈkɜːr/| vi.|同时发生；一致；同意 |To happen or exist at the same time; to agree or be in accord. |例句：Their opinions concurred. 他们的意见一致。 The two events concurred. 这两件事同时发生。|近义词：agree, coincide, correspond |反义词：disagree, differ, conflict |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two events concur. = 这两件事同时发生。
We concur on this matter. = 在这件事上我们意见一致。


一般过去时:
Their opinions concurred. = 他们的意见一致。
The meetings concurred with the holidays. = 会议与假期同时举行。


现在完成时:
The studies have concurred on the effectiveness of the treatment. = 多项研究已经证实这种治疗方法的有效性。
Our findings have concurred with previous research. = 我们的研究结果与之前的研究结果一致。


过去完成时:
The results had concurred with the hypothesis before the final data analysis. = 在最终数据分析之前，结果与假设相符。
All the evidence had concurred to point to his guilt. = 所有证据都指向了他的罪行。


将来时:
The astronomers predict that the two planets will concur next month. = 天文学家预测这两个行星下个月将同时出现。
I believe that we will concur on a solution soon. = 我相信我们很快就会达成一致。


虚拟语气:
If the data concurred with the theory, it would revolutionize the field. = 如果数据与理论相符，它将彻底改变该领域。
I wish the committee members would concur on a decision. = 我希望委员会成员们能达成一致意见。


被动语态:
It was concurred that the plan was feasible. = 人们一致认为该计划是可行的。(注意：concur本身不常用于被动语态)
It was agreed that the project should proceed. = 大家同意项目应该继续进行。(用agree代替，更自然)


疑问句:
Do your results concur with our findings? = 你的结果与我们的发现一致吗？
Do the dates of the two conferences concur? = 这两个会议的日期相同吗？


否定句:
Their opinions did not concur. = 他们的意见不一致。
The two events did not concur. = 这两件事没有同时发生。


PHRASE->
concur with: 与…一致
concur in: 同意…
concur on: 就…达成一致
to concur that: 一致认为…
concurring opinion:  concurring opinion: 同意意见
