obsessing:/əbˈsɛsɪŋ/| v.|痴迷，困扰，念念不忘|To think about something too much or too often, often to the point of obsession.|例句：He's been obsessing over his exam results all week. 他整个星期都在一直想着考试成绩。
例句：She's obsessing about her weight. 她一直担心自己的体重。|近义词：preoccupying, dwelling on, fixated on|反义词：ignoring, disregarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obsesses over his work. = 他沉迷于他的工作。
I obsess over small details. = 我对细节很执着。

一般过去时:
She obsessed over her appearance. = 她过去很在意自己的外表。
They obsessed over the missing documents. = 他们过去一直为丢失的文件而苦恼。

现在完成时:
I have been obsessing over this problem for days. = 我已经为这个问题苦恼好几天了。
He has obsessed over his health since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，他就一直很担心自己的健康。

过去完成时:
She had obsessed over the decision for weeks before finally making it. = 在最终做出决定之前，她已经为此苦恼了好几周。
They had obsessed over the details of the plan, leaving little time for execution. = 他们过去一直纠结于计划的细节，几乎没有时间去执行。

将来时:
I will probably obsess over this new project for a while. = 我可能会沉迷于这个新项目一段时间。
She will likely obsess over her performance in the upcoming competition. = 她可能会一直担心她在即将到来的比赛中的表现。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't obsess over every little thing, I would be much less stressed. = 如果我不为每一件小事都烦恼，我就会轻松得多。
I wish I didn't obsess over what others think of me. = 我希望我不那么在意别人怎么看我。

被动语态:  (The verb "obsess" is rarely used in the passive voice because it's usually followed by a prepositional phrase.)


疑问句:
Does he obsess over anything in particular? = 他对什么特别着迷吗？
Are you obsessing over this issue unnecessarily? = 你是不是在不必要地纠结这个问题？

否定句:
I don't obsess over things that are outside my control. = 我不会为我无法控制的事情而烦恼。
He doesn't obsess over his appearance like some people do. = 他不像有些人那样在意自己的外表。


PHRASE->
obsess over something = 对某事念念不忘
obsess about something = 对某事过度担心
obsess with someone = 迷恋某人
be obsessed with = 对…痴迷
obsessed with the idea = 痴迷于这个想法
