plower:/ˈplaʊər/| n. |犁地者；犁|A plowman; someone who uses a plow.  The word evokes a sense of hard work and connection to the land.  |例句：The plower worked tirelessly from sunrise to sunset. 犁地者从日出到日落辛勤工作。

例句：The old plower knew the land intimately.  老犁地者对这片土地非常熟悉。


近义词：ploughman, farmer

反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider terms suggesting lack of agricultural labor.


词性:名词
