pecans:/pɪˈkɑːnz/| n. |美洲山核桃|A pecan is a sweet, edible nut from the pecan tree, native to North America.  They are commonly used in desserts, candies, and savory dishes. |Example:  I love pecan pie.  我喜欢山核桃派。

Example: We bought a bag of pecans to use in our baking. 我们买了一袋山核桃用来烘焙。
