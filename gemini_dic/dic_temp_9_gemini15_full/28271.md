captivating: /ˈkæptɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人着迷的，迷人的 |  Attracting and holding the attention completely; fascinating.  |例句：The captivating story kept me glued to my seat.  令人着迷的故事让我一直坐在座位上。

The captivating performance left the audience speechless.  令人着迷的演出让观众说不出话来。|近义词：enthralling, enchanting, mesmerizing, fascinating |反义词：boring, dull, uninteresting, repulsive | 词性: 形容词
