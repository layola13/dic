distinctively:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪvli/ | adv. | 独特地；显著地 |  In a way that is clearly different from others; noticeably.  |例句：His style is distinctively modern. 他的风格明显是现代的。|例句：The bird's song was distinctively different from any I'd heard before.  这只鸟的叫声与我以前听到的任何一种都截然不同。|近义词：uniquely, markedly, noticeably, conspicuously |反义词：indistinguishably, similarly, vaguely

词源:
The word "distinctively" is derived from the adjective "distinct," which comes from the Latin word "distinctus," the past participle of "distinguere," meaning "to distinguish."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb, indicating manner or way.
