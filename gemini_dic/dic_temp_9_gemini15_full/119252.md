michaelmas:/ˈmaɪkəlmæs/| n. |圣米迦勒节|The feast day of St. Michael the Archangel, celebrated on September 29th. It is a quarter-day in England and Wales, traditionally marking the end of the harvest season and the beginning of the autumn term in some schools and universities.  |Example: Michaelmas is a significant date in the academic calendar.  圣米迦勒节是学术日历上的一个重要日期。|近义词:St. Michael's Day|反义词:N/A|名词
