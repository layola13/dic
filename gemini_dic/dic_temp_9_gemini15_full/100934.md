inuloid:/ˈɪnjuˌlɔɪd/| n. |类菊糖；菊糖样物质|A substance resembling inulin, a type of polysaccharide found in certain plants, particularly those of the Compositae family (daisies, sunflowers, etc.).  Inuloids often have similar properties to inulin, such as solubility in water and ability to form gels.
