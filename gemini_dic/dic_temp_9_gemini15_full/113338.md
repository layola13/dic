luxuriantly:/ˈlʌɡʒəriəntli/| adv.|繁茂地；茂盛地；奢华地 | Abundantly and richly; in a luxurious manner.  It describes something that is growing abundantly or is characterized by richness and abundance. |例句：The garden grew luxuriantly, overflowing with flowers and vibrant green leaves. 花园里植物生长茂盛，鲜花和翠绿的叶子遍地都是。

The vines luxuriantly covered the walls of the old house.  藤蔓茂盛地覆盖着老房子的墙壁。


近义词：profusely, abundantly, lavishly, opulently

反义词：sparsely, meagerly, scantily


词源:  From "luxuriant" + "-ly".  "Luxuriant" originates from the Latin word "luxuriāns," the present participle of "luxuriāri," meaning "to grow luxuriantly" or "to thrive."  This, in turn, is related to "luxus," meaning "luxury" or "abundance."
