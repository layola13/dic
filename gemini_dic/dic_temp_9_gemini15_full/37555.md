commonwealth: /ˈkɒmənˌwɛlθ/ /ˈkɑːmənˌwɛlθ/ | n. |联邦，英联邦；共和政体；公共财产 |

英文解析:A commonwealth is a self-governing country or group of countries that is a member of the Commonwealth of Nations.  It can also refer to a political system based on the common good, or to the shared property of a community.

例句:
The Commonwealth of Nations includes many former British colonies. = 英联邦包括许多前英国殖民地。

Australia is a member of the Commonwealth. = 澳大利亚是英联邦成员国。


近义词: federation, republic, community

反义词: monarchy (in the sense of a political system), private property (in the sense of ownership)

词性: noun
