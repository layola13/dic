quasi-punished:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈpʌnɪʃt/ | adj. |准惩罚的，似惩罚的 |  Describes a situation or action that resembles punishment but doesn't fully meet the legal or formal definition of punishment.  It often implies a consequence that is negative, restrictive, or disadvantageous, though not necessarily intended as a formal penalty. | Example:  The company's decision to deny him a promotion was a quasi-punished for his outspoken criticism. 公司拒绝给他升职的决定是对其直言不讳的批评的一种准惩罚。|近义词：penalized, sanctioned, disadvantaged, reprimanded |反义词：rewarded, commended, benefited
