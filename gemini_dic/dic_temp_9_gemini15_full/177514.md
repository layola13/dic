reblooming:/ˌriˈbluːmɪŋ/| adj.| повторное цветение; 复花; 再开花 |  Describing a plant that produces flowers more than once in a single growing season.  It implies a capacity for multiple flowering cycles. |Example:  The rose bush is reblooming, producing a second flush of blossoms.  这棵玫瑰灌木正在再次开花，产生第二批花朵。|近义词：reflowering, repeat-blooming|反义词：non-reblooming, single-blooming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rose is reblooming beautifully this year. = 今年玫瑰花开得非常漂亮。
This variety of rose is reblooming continuously throughout the summer. = 这个品种的玫瑰在整个夏天都在不断地复花。

一般过去时:
The azaleas rebloomed last month after a period of dormancy. = 杜鹃花在休眠一段时间后，上个月又开了花。
The plant rebloomed vigorously following a period of heavy rain. = 这株植物在经过一场大雨后，又旺盛地开花了。

现在完成时:
The hydrangeas have rebloomed twice this season. = 绣球花这个季节已经复花两次了。
The garden has rebloomed spectacularly after the spring frost. = 春霜过后，花园里的花又盛开了。

过去完成时:
The tulips had rebloomed before the first frost arrived. = 郁金香在第一次霜冻来临之前已经复花了。
The roses had rebloomed several times by the end of summer. = 到夏天结束时，玫瑰已经复花好几次了。


将来时:
The lilies will rebloom next month, if the weather stays warm. = 如果天气保持温暖，百合花下个月将会再次开放。
My clematis will rebloom in the autumn, producing a second display of flowers. = 我的铁线莲将在秋天再次开花，展现第二次花朵盛开。


虚拟语气:
If the plant were properly cared for, it would rebloom more profusely. = 如果这株植物得到适当的照顾，它会开得更茂盛。
I wish my orchids would rebloom as beautifully as they did last year. = 我希望我的兰花能像去年一样美丽地再次开放。


被动语态:
The flowers are being encouraged to rebloom by regular watering. = 通过定期浇水，鼓励花朵再次开放。
The roses were regularly pruned to encourage reblooming. = 玫瑰被定期修剪以促进复花。


疑问句:
Will the daffodils rebloom this year? = 水仙花今年还会再开花吗？
Does this type of iris rebloom after its initial flowering? = 这种鸢尾花在第一次开花后还会再开花吗？


否定句:
The daylilies did not rebloom this year due to the drought. = 由于干旱，今年萱草没有复花。
That particular rose variety doesn't rebloom reliably in our climate. = 在我们这种气候下，那个特定品种的玫瑰无法可靠地复花。
