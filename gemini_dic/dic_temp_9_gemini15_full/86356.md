hectograph:/ˈhɛktoɡræf/| n.|油印机；油印本|A hectograph is a method of duplicating documents using a gelatin-based pad.  The image is transferred to the pad, then multiple copies are made by pressing paper onto the pad.|Example:  In the past, teachers often used a hectograph to make multiple copies of worksheets.|近义词：duplicator, copying machine|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "original" or "unique" could be considered opposites in the context of creating multiple copies.|名词
