dmg:/dæmɪdʒ/| n.|损坏，损害|Damage refers to physical harm or injury to something, often resulting in a reduction in its value or functionality.  It can also refer to harm or impairment to reputation or relationships.|例句：The storm caused significant damage to the houses. 暴风雨对房屋造成了严重的损坏。|例句：His careless words caused irreparable damage to their relationship. 他不小心说的话严重损坏了他们之间的关系。|近义词：harm, injury, impairment, destruction|反义词：repair, restoration, improvement|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The accident damages his car. = 事故损坏了他的车。
The loud music damages my hearing. = 大声的音乐损害我的听力。

一般过去时:
The earthquake damaged many buildings. = 地震损坏了许多建筑物。
The flood damaged the crops. = 洪水毁坏了庄稼。


现在完成时:
The storm has damaged the roof. = 暴风雨已经损坏了屋顶。
Years of neglect have damaged the building's structure. = 多年的忽视已经损坏了建筑物的结构。


过去完成时:
The previous owner had damaged the engine before selling the car. = 前任车主在卖车之前已经损坏了发动机。
They had damaged the reputation of the company. = 他们已经损害了公司的声誉。


将来时:
The heavy rain will damage the crops. = 大雨将损害庄稼。
The continued pollution will damage the environment. = 持续的污染将损害环境。


虚拟语气:
If you drop it, you will damage it. = 如果你把它掉下来，你就会损坏它。
I wish I hadn't damaged the vase. = 我真希望我没有损坏花瓶。


被动语态:
The car was damaged in the accident. = 这辆车在事故中受损了。
The building was damaged by the fire. = 建筑物被大火损坏了。


疑问句:
Was the computer damaged? = 电脑损坏了吗？
How was the bridge damaged? = 桥是怎么损坏的？


否定句:
The accident didn't damage the car. = 事故没有损坏汽车。
The rain didn't damage the crops. = 雨没有损坏庄稼。


PHRASE->
cause damage = 造成损害
repair the damage = 修复损坏
assess the damage = 评估损失
limit the damage = 限制损失
collateral damage = 附带损害
