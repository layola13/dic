changer:/ˈtʃeɪndʒər/ | n.  |兑换者；改变者；转换器| A person or thing that causes change or makes something different.|The young activist is a changer in her community.  这位年轻的活动家是她社区的改变者。|The new software is a real changer for our workflow. 这款新软件彻底改变了我们的工作流程。|altering agent, transformer, reformer |stabilizer, preserver|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine changes the paper size automatically. 这台机器会自动更改纸张尺寸。
He changes his mind frequently. 他经常改变主意。


一般过去时:
The policy changed last year.  这项政策在去年改变了。
She changed her clothes before going out. 她出门前换了衣服。


现在完成时:
They have changed the rules of the game. 他们已经改变了比赛规则。
He has changed a lot since I last saw him. 自从我上次见到他以来，他变化很大。


过去完成时:
They had changed their plans before the storm arrived.暴风雨来临之前，他们已经改变了计划。
The company had changed its logo several times before settling on the current one. 公司在最终确定当前的标识之前，已经更换过几次标识了。


将来时:
The government will change its approach to the problem. 政府将改变其处理这个问题的方法。
They will change the date of the meeting. 他们将更改会议日期。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would change my strategy. 如果我是你，我会改变我的策略。
I wish I could change the past. 我真希望我能改变过去。


被动语态:
The date of the meeting was changed. 会议日期被更改了。
The rules are being changed. 规则正在被更改。


疑问句:
Has the schedule changed?  日程安排改变了吗？
Will they change their decision? 他们会改变他们的决定吗？


否定句:
The situation hasn't changed.  情况没有改变。
They didn't change their minds. 他们没有改变主意。
