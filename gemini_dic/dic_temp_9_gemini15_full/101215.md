invisibleness: /ˌɪnˈvɪzəbəlnəs/ | n. |不可见性，隐形|The state or quality of being invisible; inability to be seen.  It refers to the characteristic of something that cannot be perceived by the eye. |例句：The magician's invisibleness was a marvel to the audience. 魔术师的隐身对观众来说是一个奇迹。

例句：The invisibleness of the air makes it difficult to appreciate its presence. 空气的不可见性使得人们难以体会到它的存在。


近义词：invisibility, unseenness, imperceptibility

反义词：visibility, perceptibility


词源：

* **in-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **visibl-**:  from "visible,"  derived from Latin "visibilis" (capable of being seen), from "vidēre" (to see)
* **-ness**: suffix forming nouns of state or quality
