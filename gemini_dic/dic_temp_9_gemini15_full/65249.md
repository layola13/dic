etymological:/ˌɛtɪməˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 词源学的；词源的 | Relating to the origin of words and the way in which their meanings have changed throughout history.  It explores the historical development of words, tracing their origins and evolution through different languages and time periods. | Example: The professor gave an etymological explanation of the word "nice," showing how its meaning has shifted over centuries. | 近义词：derivational, originary | 反义词：N/A
