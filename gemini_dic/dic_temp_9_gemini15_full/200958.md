slitting: /ˈslɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 切割，撕裂 | The act of cutting or tearing something into long, thin strips.  It often refers to a continuous process, such as cutting fabric or metal. |例句：The machine is slitting the fabric into narrow strips. 这台机器正在将织物切割成窄条。

例句：They are slitting the metal sheet to make smaller pieces. 他们正在切割金属板以制造更小的零件。


近义词：cutting, slashing, tearing, ripping | 反义词：joining, mending, patching | 词性：动名词
