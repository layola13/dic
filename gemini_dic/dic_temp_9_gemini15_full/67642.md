extrications:/ˌɛkstrɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 解救；脱险；解脱 | The act of freeing someone or something from a constraint or difficulty.  It refers to the process of removing something or someone from a difficult or dangerous situation.  |例句：The firefighters performed daring extrications to save the victims from the burning building. 消防队员进行了大胆的营救行动，将受害者从燃烧的建筑物中救出。

例句：The extrications of the trapped miners took several days. 营救被困矿工的行动持续了好几天。


近义词：rescues, liberations, salvages | 反义词：imprisonments, entrapments | 词性：名词
