photocopiers:/ˈfoʊtoʊˌkɑːpiərz/ | n. |复数名词：复印机 | Machines that make copies of documents.  Photocopiers are essential office equipment.  |The office has several new photocopiers. 办公室有几台新的复印机。 We need to repair the photocopiers.我们需要修理复印机。|printers, copiers |  (无明确反义词，可考虑manual copying 手动复印) | noun

词源:
The word "photocopier" is a combination of "photo" (from Greek phōs, phot- "light") and "copier" (from the verb "to copy"). It refers to a machine that uses light to create copies of documents.
