opercular:/ɒˈpɜːrkjʊlə(r)/| adj.|鳃盖的；像鳃盖的；盖状的|Relating to or resembling an operculum; lid-like.  The term often refers to the bony flap covering the gills of fish.|例句：The opercular bones protect the delicate gills of the fish.  鳃盖骨保护着鱼类娇嫩的鳃。

The opercular cavity is where the gills are located. 鳃腔是鳃所在的地方。


近义词：gill covering, lid-like, flap-like|反义词：open, uncovered


词源：From Latin *operculum ("a cover, lid"), from operire ("to cover").  The suffix "-ar" indicates an adjective.
