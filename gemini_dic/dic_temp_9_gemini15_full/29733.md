catmint: /ˈkætˌmɪnt/ | n. | 猫薄荷 | A plant of the mint family, *Nepeta cataria*, whose leaves have a strong odor that attracts cats.  It is known for its euphoric effect on cats. |例句：My cat went crazy when she smelled the catmint. 我的猫闻到猫薄荷后就发疯了。
The catnip toy was filled with dried catmint. 猫薄荷玩具里装满了干燥的猫薄荷。


近义词：catnip (though technically a different species within the same genus)


反义词：无


词性：名词
