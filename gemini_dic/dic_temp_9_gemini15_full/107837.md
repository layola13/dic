larches:/ˈlɑːrtʃɪz/| n. |落叶松属植物|Larches are deciduous coniferous trees belonging to the genus Larix. They are known for their needle-like leaves that turn golden yellow in autumn before falling off.  They are found in the cooler temperate and boreal regions of the Northern Hemisphere. |例句：The mountains were ablaze with the golden hues of the larches in autumn. 秋天，山上的落叶松呈现出金黄色的色彩，十分绚丽。|例句： We planted several larches in the garden to provide shade and beauty. 我们在花园里种了几棵落叶松，以提供阴凉和美景。|近义词：larch (单数)|反义词：N/A
