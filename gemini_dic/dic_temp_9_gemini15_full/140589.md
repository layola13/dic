olivaceous:/ˌɒlɪˈveɪʃəs/ | adj. |橄榄色的，橄榄绿的 | Of the color of an olive; grayish-green.  The term describes a color that is a mixture of gray, green, and brown, similar to the color of olives. | The painting featured an olivaceous landscape, evoking a sense of calmness and tranquility.  The bird's plumage was a muted olivaceous hue. |近义词：greenish-gray, grayish-green, olive-green | 反义词：bright, vivid
