re-excitation: /ˌriːˌɛkˌsaɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 再激发；再激励 | The process of exciting something again, especially after a period of inactivity or decline.  It often refers to the renewed stimulation of an excited state in a physical or biological system. |

例句：

The re-excitation of the neuron led to another action potential.  神经元的再激发导致了另一个动作电位。

The economy showed signs of re-excitation after a period of stagnation.  经济在一段时间的停滞之后出现了复苏的迹象。


近义词：restimulation, reactivation, rekindling

反义词：deactivation, inhibition, suppression

词性：名词
