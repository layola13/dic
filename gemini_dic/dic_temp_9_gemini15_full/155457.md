/fɪˈnɑːlədʒiz/ | n. |物候学；物候现象|The study of periodic biological phenomena, especially in relation to climate and weather.  It examines the timing of natural events in plant and animal life cycles, such as flowering, migration, and hibernation, and how these events are influenced by environmental factors. | Example:  The study of phenologies helps us understand the impact of climate change on ecosystems.  物候学研究有助于我们了解气候变化对生态系统的影响。|近义词:seasonal changes, biological timing|反义词: N/A
