counselled: /ˈkaʊnsəld/ | v. | 劝告；建议；指导 | To give advice or guidance to (someone).  |例句：The therapist counselled the patient on coping with stress. 治疗师建议病人如何应对压力。  |近义词：advised, guided, directed |反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The therapist counsels her patients daily. = 治疗师每天都会为她的病人提供咨询。
He counsels people facing difficult life choices. = 他为面临艰难人生抉择的人们提供咨询。

一般过去时:
She counselled him to seek professional help. = 她劝他寻求专业帮助。
The lawyer counselled his client before the trial. = 律师在审判前为他当事人提供咨询。

现在完成时:
I have counselled many people through difficult times. = 我已经帮助许多人度过难关。
She has counselled him on several occasions. = 她已经多次为他提供咨询。

过去完成时:
He had counselled her extensively before she made her decision. = 在她做出决定之前，他已经对她进行了广泛的咨询。
They had counselled with each other for hours before reaching an agreement. = 他们在达成协议之前已经互相商讨了好几个小时。

将来时:
I will counsel him on his career options. = 我将就他的职业选择给他提供咨询。
She will counsel the grieving family. = 她将为悲痛的家庭提供咨询。

虚拟语气:
If I had counselled him earlier, things might have been different. = 如果我早点劝他，情况可能就不一样了。
I wish I had counselled her to be more cautious. = 我真希望我早点劝她谨慎些。

被动语态:
He was counselled by a team of experts. = 他得到了一组专家的指导。
The students were counselled on the importance of studying hard. = 学生们接受了关于努力学习重要性的指导。

疑问句:
Did the teacher counsel the disruptive student? = 老师是否对那个扰乱课堂秩序的学生进行了劝导？
Has the lawyer counselled you on your legal options? = 律师是否就你的法律选择向你提供了咨询？

否定句:
They did not counsel the young man about his problems. = 他们没有就他的问题对那个年轻人进行劝导。
She wouldn't counsel anyone against their better judgment. = 她不会劝任何人去做违背他们判断的事情。


PHRASE->
counsel against = 劝阻
counsel someone to do something = 劝告某人做某事
seek counsel = 寻求建议
give counsel = 提供建议
take counsel with someone = 与某人商量


词源:
From Old French *conseiller, from Latin *consiliāre, frequentative of consulēre “to consult, deliberate,” from *consul “counselor.”  The word ultimately derives from the Latin word *consul, which refers to a Roman magistrate.
