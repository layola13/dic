raceme:/ˈreɪsiːm/| n.|总状花序|An unbranched, indeterminate inflorescence with pedicellate flowers arranged along an elongated, unbranched axis.  The flowers are attached individually to the main stem, not clustered in groups. |例句：The lilac bush displayed a beautiful raceme of fragrant purple flowers.  紫丁香灌木展现出一串美丽的紫色芬芳花朵总状花序。  The botanist carefully examined the raceme to identify the plant species. 植物学家仔细检查了总状花序以鉴定植物种类。|近义词：spike, panicle|反义词:None.
