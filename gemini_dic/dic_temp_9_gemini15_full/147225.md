overmaturity:/ˌoʊvərməˈtʃʊrɪti/ | n. | 过熟；成熟过度 | The state of being overripe or past the optimal stage of development or ripeness.  It can refer to fruits, vegetables,  financial instruments, or even concepts.  | Example: The overmaturity of the mangoes resulted in a mushy texture and unpleasant taste.  芒果过度成熟导致质地软烂，味道不佳。

The overmaturity of the bonds led to a significant loss of value. 债券过度成熟导致了巨大的价值损失。|近义词：overripeness, senescence |反义词：undermaturity, immaturity
