hugging: /ˈhʌɡɪŋ/ | v. |拥抱，紧抱|The act of holding someone closely in one's arms, typically to show affection or comfort.  |例句：She hugged her child tightly. 她紧紧地抱着她的孩子。|例句：The two friends hugged each other goodbye. 这两个朋友互相拥抱告别。|近义词：embracing, cuddling, clasping|反义词：pushing away, rejecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hugs his daughter every night. = 他每天晚上都拥抱他的女儿。
They are hugging each other happily. = 他们快乐地互相拥抱。

一般过去时:
She hugged her friend after the good news. = 她听到好消息后拥抱了她的朋友。
I hugged my dog when I came home. = 我回家时拥抱了我的狗。

现在完成时:
I have hugged her many times today. = 我今天已经拥抱她很多次了。
They have hugged each other since they met. = 自从他们见面以来，他们就一直在拥抱对方。

过去完成时:
He had hugged her before she left. = 她离开之前，他已经拥抱了她。
She had hugged her cat before it ran away. = 她家的猫跑掉之前，她已经拥抱了它。

将来时:
I will hug you when I see you. = 我见到你时会拥抱你。
They will hug each other at the airport. = 他们将在机场互相拥抱。

虚拟语气:
If I saw her, I would hug her. = 如果我看到她，我会拥抱她。
I wish I could hug my grandma right now. = 我真希望我现在能拥抱我的奶奶。

被动语态:
The child was hugged by his mother. = 这个孩子被他的母亲拥抱了。
The teddy bear was hugged tightly by the little girl. = 小女孩紧紧地抱着泰迪熊。

疑问句:
Are you hugging your pet? = 你在拥抱你的宠物吗？
Did he hug her goodbye? = 他拥抱她道别了吗？

否定句:
He didn't hug her. = 他没有拥抱她。
I don't usually hug strangers. = 我通常不拥抱陌生人。
