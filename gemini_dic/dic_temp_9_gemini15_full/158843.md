plumelet: /ˈpluːmlɛt/ | n. | 羽状小枝；小羽枝 | A small feather or a small plume-like structure.  It often refers to a small, feathery appendage on a plant or a small, delicate feather. | Example:  The botanist carefully examined the plumelets on the rare orchid. 植物学家仔细检查了这种稀有兰花上的羽状小枝。


近义词:  small feather, feathery appendage, down


反义词:  (no direct antonym, but words suggesting opposite characteristics could be used, such as) large branch, coarse structure


词性: noun
