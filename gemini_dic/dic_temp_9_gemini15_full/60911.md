elephantiasis:/ˌɛləfənˈtaɪəsɪs/| n. |象皮病|A chronic condition characterized by massive swelling of the limbs and other body parts, caused by obstruction of the lymphatic system, typically by parasitic worms.  Elephantiasis is a severe form of lymphedema. |例句：The disease caused severe elephantiasis in his legs.  这种疾病导致他的腿严重患上了象皮病。|例句：Years of untreated filariasis led to elephantiasis.  多年未治疗的丝虫病导致了象皮病。|近义词：lymphedema, lymphatic obstruction |反义词：N/A
