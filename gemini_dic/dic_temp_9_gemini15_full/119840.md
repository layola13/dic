mildew:/ˈmɪlduː/ | n. |霉；霉菌；霉污 | A fungus that grows on damp surfaces, causing discoloration and a musty smell.  It often appears as a fuzzy, whitish coating. |例句：The damp basement was covered in mildew.潮湿的地下室长满了霉菌。

例句：We need to remove the mildew from the shower tiles.我们需要清除淋浴瓷砖上的霉菌。


近义词：mold, fungus, moldiness | 反义词：cleanliness, sterility


词源:  From Middle English *mildew, from Old English *melugu, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *meeldauw and German *Mehltau, literally "meal-dew".  The name reflects the appearance of the fungus, resembling a fine, powdery coating.
