resketching:/ˌriːˈskɛtʃɪŋ/ | v. |重新描绘；重新勾勒；重新构思 | The act of sketching something again, often to improve it or to explore different possibilities.  It implies a revision or refinement of a previous sketch. | Example:  He spent the afternoon resketching the landscape, trying to capture the light more accurately. 他花了一下午重新描绘风景，试图更准确地捕捉光线。 |近义词：redrawing, re-drafting, revising, refining |反义词：finishing, completing
