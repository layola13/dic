kneecapping:/ˈniːkæpɪŋ/| n. v.|膝盖骨打击；使…丧失活力；破坏；|The act of shooting someone in the kneecap, often as a form of intimidation or punishment.  It can also refer to the act of severely damaging or undermining something.|例句：The gang was known for its brutal kneecapping of rivals.  这个帮派以残酷地打击对手的膝盖而闻名。

The company's new strategy kneecapped its competitors.  公司的新的策略破坏了其竞争对手。|近义词：crippling, disabling, undermining, sabotaging|反义词：empowering, strengthening, supporting

USAGE->
