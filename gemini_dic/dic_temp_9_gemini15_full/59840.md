ectopia:/ɛkˈtoʊpiə/| n. |异位，异位症 |The displacement of an organ or other part from its normal position.  It refers to the presence of an organ or tissue in an abnormal location.  This can occur during embryonic development or later in life due to injury or disease.|例句：The patient presented with ectopia cordis, a rare congenital defect.|近义词：displacement, malposition |反义词：normal position, in situ
