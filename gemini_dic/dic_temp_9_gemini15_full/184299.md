resourcing:/rɪˈsɔːrsɪŋ/| n. |资源配置；资源管理；资源获取|The process of identifying, acquiring, allocating, and managing resources needed to achieve organizational goals.  This includes human resources, financial resources, and material resources.|例句：Effective resourcing is crucial for project success.  有效的资源配置对于项目的成功至关重要。|例句：The company is reviewing its resourcing strategy to improve efficiency. 公司正在审查其资源配置策略以提高效率。|近义词：resource allocation, resource management|反义词：resource depletion, mismanagement|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company resources its projects carefully. = 公司仔细地为其项目配置资源。
We are resourcing the new department with skilled staff. = 我们正在为新部门配备熟练的员工。


一般过去时:
They resourced the campaign effectively. = 他们有效地为活动配置了资源。
The project was poorly resourced from the start. = 这个项目从一开始就资源匮乏。


现在完成时:
We have resourced the team adequately for this task. = 我们已经为这个团队提供了足够的资源来完成这项任务。
The company has resourced several new initiatives this year. = 公司今年已经为多个新项目配置了资源。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
