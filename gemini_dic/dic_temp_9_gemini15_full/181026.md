reinaugurates:/ˌriːɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪts/ | vt. |重新就职；再次启用；重新开始 |To inaugurate again; to install or begin again in office.  The verb implies a formal or ceremonial act of restarting something, often after an interruption or a period of inactivity. |例句：The president was reinaugurated for a second term.  总统再次就职，开始了第二任期。

The newly renovated museum was reinaugurated with a grand ceremony.  经过重新装修的博物馆隆重开幕了。


近义词：restart, recommence, resume, inaugurate (again) | 反义词：terminate, conclude, finish, decommission | 词性：动词
