hyperactivity: /ˌhaɪpərækˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 多动；活动过度 | Excessive physical activity or restlessness.  It is often a symptom of a condition such as ADHD. |  His hyperactivity made it difficult for him to sit still in class. 他上课时坐不住，因为他太好动了。  The doctor suspects hyperactivity as a contributing factor to his poor sleep. 医生怀疑活动过度是导致他睡眠不好的一个因素。 | restlessness, overactivity | calmness, inactivity | 名词

词源:

The word "hyperactivity" is a modern coinage, combining the Greek prefix "hyper-" (meaning "over," "above," or "excessive") with the word "activity."  Therefore, it literally means "excessive activity."
