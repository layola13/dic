lemons:/ˈlɛmən/| n.|柠檬|The fruit of a lemon tree, typically oval-shaped and having a yellow rind and very sour juice.|例句：I added some lemon juice to the fish. 我在鱼里加了一些柠檬汁。|例句：She squeezed the lemons to make lemonade. 她挤压柠檬来制作柠檬汁。|近义词：citrus fruit, lime|反义词：sweet fruit
