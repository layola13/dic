outplotting:/ˌaʊtˈplɒtɪŋ/ | v. |智胜，巧妙地超过|To surpass or outmaneuver someone through clever planning or strategy.  It implies a more cunning and strategic approach than simply "outdoing" someone.|例句：The detective outplotted the cunning criminal, leading to his arrest.  侦探巧妙地智胜了狡猾的罪犯，导致了他的逮捕。

例句：The opposing team's outplotting of our strategy led to our defeat. 对手巧妙地超过了我们的策略，导致了我们的失败。


近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit,  surpass

反义词：being outmaneuvered, being outsmarted
