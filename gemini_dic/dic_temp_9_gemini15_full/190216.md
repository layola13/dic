sapientious: /seɪpiˈɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 富有智慧的；明智的；有见识的 | Possessing or showing great wisdom or understanding.  It suggests a deep and insightful understanding of life and the world, often gained through experience and reflection.  The word is relatively uncommon in modern English. | Example:  The sapientious old woman offered sage advice to the troubled young man.  这位富有智慧的老妇人给那个苦恼的年轻人提供了明智的建议。


近义词: wise, sagacious, judicious, insightful, perceptive, intelligent, discerning

反义词: foolish, unwise, imprudent, unintelligent, ignorant, obtuse


词性：形容词
