marketeer:/ˌmɑːrkɪˈtɪər/ | n. | 市场营销人员，营销专家 | A person who is skilled in marketing and promoting products or services.  | Example:  The marketeer developed a creative campaign to boost sales.  这位营销人员制定了一个富有创意的活动来提升销量。 |近义词：marketer, salesperson, advertiser | 反义词：consumer, buyer
