resuspend:/ˌriːsəˈspɛnd/| vt.|重新悬浮；重新溶解|To suspend again; to put (a settled substance) back into suspension in a liquid.|例句：The sediment needs to be resuspended before analysis.  沉淀物需要在分析前重新悬浮。

例句：The scientist resuspended the cells in fresh media. 科学家用新鲜培养基重新悬浮细胞。


近义词：redisperse,re-suspend,resuscitated

反义词：settle,precipitate
