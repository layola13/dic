open-necked:/ˈoʊpən nekt/ | adj. |敞领的；不系领带的 |Having a neckline that is not fastened or closed.  It typically refers to shirts or blouses where the top buttons are undone, revealing the chest. |例句：He wore an open-necked shirt to the barbecue. 他在烧烤派对上穿着一件敞领衬衫。  |例句：The open-necked blouse looked comfortable and stylish. 这件敞领衬衫看起来舒适又时尚。|近义词：unbuttoned, unfastened|反义词： buttoned, fastened
