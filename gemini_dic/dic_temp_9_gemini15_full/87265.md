herling:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/ | n. |  hurling (运动)；猛掷；用力投掷 |  Hurling is a traditional Irish field game played with a wooden stick (hurley) and a small ball (sliotar).  Hurling involves hitting the ball with the hurley to score goals. |  例句：He excels at hurling. 他擅长 hurling。  He made a mighty herling of the stone. 他用力地投掷了那块石头。 | 近义词：throwing, flinging, pitching | 反义词：receiving, catching
