humbles:/ˈhʌmbəlz/ | v. | 使谦卑；使逊顺；降低（某事物的地位） | To make someone feel humble; to reduce the importance or status of something. |例句：The experience humbled him.  这次经历使他变得谦逊。
例句：The discovery humbled previous theories.  这一发现使之前的理论相形见绌。|近义词：humiliate, lower, deflate |反义词：exalt, inflate, aggrandize
