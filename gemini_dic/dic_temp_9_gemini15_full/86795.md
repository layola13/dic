hemangioblastomas:/ˌhiːmænˌdʒiːoʊˈblæstəməz/| n. |血管母细胞瘤|A hemangioblastoma is a rare, benign tumor that originates from hemangioblasts, cells that can differentiate into both blood vessels and blood cells.  These tumors are most commonly found in the cerebellum (a part of the brain) but can also occur in the spinal cord and retina.| Example: The patient was diagnosed with a cerebellar hemangioblastoma and underwent surgery for its removal.  病人被诊断出患有小脑血管母细胞瘤，并接受了手术切除。|近义词：cerebellar hemangioblastoma (if specifying location)|反义词：malignant brain tumor (as opposed to benign)|名词
