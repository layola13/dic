oilskin:/ˈɔɪlskɪn/| n.|油布；油皮；防水布|A fabric treated with oil to make it waterproof.  It is often used for clothing and coverings.|例句：The fisherman wore an oilskin coat to protect himself from the rain.  渔夫穿着一件油布外套以防雨。

例句：She covered her furniture with oilskin during the storm. 她在大雨中用油布盖住了家具。


近义词：waterproof fabric, oiled cloth|反义词：porous fabric, permeable material|词性:名词
