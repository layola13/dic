marsupial:/mɑːrˈsuːpiəl/| n. |有袋动物|A marsupial is a mammal that carries its young in a pouch.  Marsupials are characterized by their pouch, where their young complete their development.  Many marsupials are found in Australia and surrounding islands.|Example: Kangaroos and koalas are examples of marsupials.|近义词：pouched mammal|反义词：placental mammal
