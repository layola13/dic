quasi-continually: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈtɪnjuəli/ | adj. | 准连续地，几乎连续不断地 | Almost continuously; frequently and regularly, though not constantly.  The word implies a near-continuous state, with possible brief interruptions. | Example: The machine operated quasi-continually for three days before requiring maintenance. 这台机器在需要维护之前几乎连续工作了三天。 |近义词：nearly continuously, almost constantly, frequently, regularly | 反义词：intermittently, sporadically, discontinuously
