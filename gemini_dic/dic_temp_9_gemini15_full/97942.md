innovative:/ˈɪnəveɪtɪv/ | adj. |创新的，革新的 |  Showing creativity and originality in thinking and doing things; introducing new ideas, methods, or inventions.  | 例句：His innovative approach to teaching resulted in higher student engagement.  他的创新教学方法提高了学生的参与度。

例句：The company is known for its innovative products. 这家公司以其创新产品而闻名。


近义词：creative, groundbreaking, original, inventive, novel

反义词：conventional, traditional, outdated, old-fashioned


词源:  From the verb "innovate," which comes from the Latin word "innovare," meaning "to renew," "to change," or "to make new."  The prefix "in-" means "in" or "into," and the root word "novare" means "to make new."
