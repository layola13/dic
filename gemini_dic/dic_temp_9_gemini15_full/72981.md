It implies a lack of caution and disregard for potential consequences. |Examples: His foolhardinesses in mountain climbing nearly cost him his life.  他的登山鲁莽行为差点要了他的命。  The general's foolhardinesses led to significant losses for his army.  将军的鲁莽行为导致他的军队损失惨重。 |近义词：recklessnesses, rashnesses, temerities |反义词：cautiousness, prudence, carefulness |名词
