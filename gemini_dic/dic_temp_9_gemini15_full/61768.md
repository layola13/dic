empathizer:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzər/| n. |移情者，共情者 |A person who is able to understand and share the feelings of another.  Someone who readily shows empathy. |例句：She is a natural empathizer, always able to understand others' perspectives.  她是一个天生的移情者，总是能够理解别人的观点。


例句：He proved to be a good empathizer, offering support and understanding during a difficult time. 他证明自己是一个很好的移情者，在困难时期提供支持和理解。


近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person, understanding person

反义词：apathy, unfeeling person, unsympathetic person
