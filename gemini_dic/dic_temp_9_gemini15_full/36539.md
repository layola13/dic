collaborator:/kəˈlæbəreɪtər/| n. |合作者，协作者，同谋|A collaborator is a person who works jointly with others, especially in a artistic or scientific endeavor.  It can also refer to someone who aids the enemy during wartime.|例句：He was a key collaborator on the project. 他是该项目的主要合作者。|例句：The artist collaborated with a renowned musician.艺术家与一位著名的音乐家合作。|近义词：coworker, partner, accomplice|反义词：opponent, adversary, rival|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collaborates with several artists. = 他与几位艺术家合作。
She collaborates on numerous projects. = 她参与多个项目合作。

一般过去时:
They collaborated on a new song. = 他们合作创作了一首新歌。
He collaborated with a famous scientist. = 他与一位著名的科学家合作。

现在完成时:
We have collaborated on several successful projects. = 我们已经合作完成了几项成功的项目。
She has collaborated with many leading figures in the industry. = 她已经与业内许多领导人物合作过。

过去完成时:
They had collaborated extensively before the final product was released. = 在最终产品发布之前，他们已经进行了广泛的合作。
The team had collaborated for years on this research. = 这个团队多年来一直致力于这项研究。

将来时:
We will collaborate on a new book next year. = 我们明年将合作出版一本新书。
They will collaborate with international experts. = 他们将与国际专家合作。

虚拟语气:
If he collaborated with us, the project would be completed sooner. = 如果他与我们合作，项目就能更快完成。
I wish she had collaborated with us earlier. = 我希望她早点与我们合作。

被动语态:
The project was collaborated on by many talented individuals. = 该项目由许多有才华的人共同完成。
The research was collaborated on by scientists from different countries. = 这项研究是由来自不同国家的科学家合作完成的。

疑问句:
Who did you collaborate with on this project? = 你在这个项目中与谁合作？
Have you collaborated with anyone on this design? = 你在这个设计上与任何人合作过吗？

否定句:
He did not collaborate with the competitor. = 他没有与竞争对手合作。
She refused to collaborate on the project. = 她拒绝参与该项目的合作。


PHRASE->
