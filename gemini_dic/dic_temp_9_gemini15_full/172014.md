purged:/pɜːrdʒd/ | v. |清除；净化；肃清 | To remove or eliminate something completely, especially something unwanted or harmful.  |例句：The dictator purged his enemies from the government. 独裁者将他的敌人从政府中清除。  |例句：She purged her system of toxins by drinking plenty of water. 她通过大量饮水来清除体内的毒素。|近义词：cleanse, purify, eliminate, expunge |反义词：contaminate, pollute, introduce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He purges his computer of viruses regularly. = 他定期清除电脑病毒。
They purge the water supply of contaminants. = 他们清除供水中的污染物。

一般过去时:
The government purged the opposition party. = 政府肃清了反对党。
She purged the old files from her computer. = 她从电脑中删除了旧文件。

现在完成时:
The company has purged its database of duplicate entries. = 公司已经清除了数据库中的重复条目。
He has purged himself of all guilt. = 他已经摆脱了所有的罪恶感。

过去完成时:
They had purged the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经销毁了证据。
She had purged her diet of sugar before the competition. = 比赛前，她已经清除了饮食中的糖分。

将来时:
The authorities will purge the city of illegal immigrants. = 当局将清除城市中的非法移民。
He will purge the data from the hard drive. = 他将从硬盘中清除数据。

虚拟语气:
If I purged my diet of processed foods, I would feel healthier. = 如果我清除了饮食中的加工食品，我会感觉更健康。
I wish I had purged my email inbox yesterday. = 我真希望我昨天清除了我的邮箱收件箱。

被动语态:
The list of names was purged from the database. = 名单已从数据库中删除。
The contaminated area is being purged of hazardous materials. = 受污染的区域正在清除有害物质。

疑问句:
Has the system been purged of malware? = 系统是否已清除恶意软件？
Will the old data be purged from the server? = 旧数据将从服务器中删除吗？

否定句:
He did not purge the files from his computer. = 他没有从电脑中删除文件。
The company hasn't purged its outdated inventory. = 公司没有清除过时的库存。


PHRASE->
purge oneself of = 摆脱…
purge the system of = 清除系统中的…
purge the ranks of = 清除…队伍中的…
purge the body of = 清除身体中的…
purge a country of = 清除一个国家中的…

词源:
The word "purge" comes from the Old French word "purger," meaning "to cleanse, purify," which in turn derives from the Latin word "purgare," having the same meaning.  The Latin word is formed from "purus" (pure) + "agere" (to do, drive).
