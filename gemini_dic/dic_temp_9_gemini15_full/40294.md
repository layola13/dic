continuos:/kənˈtɪnjuəs/ | adj. | 连续的；持续不断的 |  Continuous means uninterrupted in time or sequence. It describes something that happens without stopping or pausing. |例句：The continuous rain caused flooding.  持续的降雨导致了洪水。

例句：He made a continuous effort to improve his English. 他持续努力提高他的英语。

近义词：uninterrupted, constant, unceasing, persistent | 反义词：intermittent, discontinuous, sporadic | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine runs continuously. = 机器持续运转。
The river flows continuously. = 河水持续流动。

一般过去时:
The rain continued continuously for three days. = 雨持续下了三天。
The engine ran continuously for hours. = 发动机持续运转了数小时。

现在完成时:
It has been raining continuously since morning. = 从早上开始就一直在下不停的雨。
She has worked continuously on this project for weeks. = 她已经连续几周致力于这个项目。

过去完成时:
The machine had been running continuously before it broke down. = 机器在坏掉之前一直在持续运转。
They had worked continuously for days without rest. = 他们连续工作了好几天没休息。

将来时:
The factory will operate continuously throughout the night. = 工厂将整夜持续运转。
We will continuously monitor the situation. = 我们将持续监测情况。

虚拟语气:
If the power supply were continuous, the machine would work perfectly. = 如果电源持续供应，机器就能完美运转。
I wish the music were continuous, not interrupted by commercials. = 我希望音乐是连续的，而不是被广告打断。

被动语态:
The process is continuously monitored by sensors. = 这个过程由传感器持续监控。
The data is continuously updated. = 数据持续更新。

疑问句:
Is the supply of electricity continuous? = 供电是持续的吗？
Is the noise continuous or intermittent? = 噪音是持续的还是间歇性的？

否定句:
The supply of water was not continuous. = 水的供应不是持续的。
The sound was not continuous; it stopped and started. = 声音不是持续的；它时断时续。
