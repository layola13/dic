concrescence:/kɒnˈkrɛsəns/| n. | 并生；融合；生长在一起 | The process of growing together; the state of being grown together; the fusion of separate elements into a unified whole.  It often refers to the gradual merging or unification of separate parts into a single entity.  The term is used in various fields including philosophy, biology, and geology. | Example: The concrescence of the two cells resulted in a single organism.  两个细胞的融合导致了一个单一的有机体。|近义词：fusion, coalescence, amalgamation, unification |反义词：separation, division, fragmentation | 名词
