rolling:/ˈroʊlɪŋ/ /ˈrəʊlɪŋ/ | adj., v. |滚动的；旋转的；起伏的；连续不断的 |  Describes something that is moving or turning continuously, or something that is continuous and ongoing.  |例句：The rolling hills stretched as far as the eye could see.  连绵起伏的山丘一直延伸到视野尽头。
The ball kept rolling down the hill. 球一直滚下山坡。|近义词：rotating, tumbling, continuous, ongoing |反义词：stationary, still, discontinuous, intermittent |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is rolling steadily towards the sea. 河水稳定地流向大海。
The wheels are rolling smoothly along the track. 车轮沿着轨道平稳地滚动。


一般过去时:
The stones rolled down the mountainside. 石头滚下山坡。
She rolled the dough into a thin sheet. 她把面团擀成薄片。


现在完成时:
The snow has been rolling in all day.  雪整天都在飘落。
The ball has been rolling for ten minutes. 球已经滚了十分钟了。


过去完成时:
The ball had rolled under the sofa before I could catch it.  在我抓住球之前，球已经滚到沙发底下了。
After the earthquake, the rocks had rolled down the cliff. 地震过后，岩石从悬崖上滚落下来。


将来时:
The snow will be rolling in later this evening. 今晚晚些时候将下雪。
The stones will roll down the hill if you disturb them. 如果你扰乱它们，石头就会滚下山。


虚拟语气:
If the ball rolled faster, it would reach the bottom sooner. 如果球滚得更快，它就会更快到达底部。
I wish the clouds would stop rolling in. 我希望云彩停止飘来。


被动语态:
The dough was rolled thinly before baking. 面团在烘烤前被擀薄。
The dice were rolled and the result was a six. 骰子被掷出，结果是六点。


疑问句:
Is the ball still rolling? 球还在滚动吗？
Are the clouds rolling in? 云彩在飘来吗？


否定句:
The wheels aren't rolling. 车轮没有转动。
The stones didn't roll far. 石头没有滚得很远。


PHRASE->
rolling hills：连绵起伏的丘陵
rolling pin：擀面杖
rolling stone：滚石 (比喻漂泊不定的人)
rolling thunder：隆隆的雷声
rolling stock：铁路车辆
