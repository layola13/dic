duellists:/ˈduːəlɪsts/| n. |决斗者；对决者|People who engage in duels.  The term often implies skill and experience in dueling.|例句：The two duellists faced each other, pistols drawn.  两位决斗者面对面站着，拔出了手枪。

例句：The duellists were renowned for their precision and deadly aim.  这些决斗者以其精准的射击和致命的瞄准而闻名。

近义词：combatants, duelists, fighters

反义词：peacemakers, mediators
