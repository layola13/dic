omening: /ˈoʊmənɪŋ/ | n. | 预兆，征兆 | An omening is an indication or sign of something that is going to happen, often something bad.  It suggests the presence of a future event, typically a negative one, based on current observations or events. | The dark clouds were an omening of the coming storm.  乌云是暴风雨即将来临的预兆。The unsettling silence was an omening of trouble.  令人不安的寂静预示着麻烦。| foreshadowing, presage, portent | auspicious, favorable | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dark clouds are omening a storm. = 乌云预示着一场风暴。
That uneasy feeling is omening trouble. = 那种不安的感觉预示着麻烦。


一般过去时:
The broken mirror omened bad luck. = 打碎的镜子预示着厄运。
The strange noises omened danger. = 奇怪的声音预示着危险。


现在完成时:
The recurring nightmares have omened disaster. = 反复出现的噩梦预示着灾难。
Recent events have omened a downturn in the economy. = 最近的事件预示着经济衰退。


过去完成时:
The villagers believed the crows had omened the famine. = 村民们相信乌鸦预示着饥荒。
She realized the strange dream had omened her accident. = 她意识到那个奇怪的梦预示着她的意外。


将来时:
The astrologer says the stars will omen a period of change. = 占星家说星星将预示着一时期变革。
Experts believe the current trends will omen a significant shift in the market. = 专家们认为目前的趋势将预示着市场发生重大转变。


虚拟语气:
If the signs omene disaster, we should prepare. = 如果迹象预示着灾难，我们应该做好准备。
If only the dreams hadn't omened such sadness. = 但愿梦境没有预示如此悲伤。


被动语态:
The coming war was omened by the political tensions. = 即将到来的战争是由政治紧张局势预示的。
A decline in sales was omened by the recent market reports. = 最近的市场报告预示着销售额下降。


疑问句:
Does this unusual weather omen something bad? = 这异常的天气预示着什么不好的事情吗？
What does the oracle omen for the future? = 神谕预示着未来的什么？


否定句:
The clear sky did not omen any rain. = 晴朗的天空没有预示任何降雨。
The positive signs did not omen any difficulties. = 积极的迹象并没有预示任何困难。
