oblatum: /oʊˈbleɪtəm/ | n. | 供奉物，奉献物 | A thing offered, especially as a gift to a church or a deity.  It is often used in a religious context to refer to an offering made to God or a sacred object.  | Example: The oblatum was presented at the altar during the mass.  供奉物在弥撒中被呈献在祭坛上。 | 近义词：offering, donation, sacrifice | 反义词：taking, seizure
