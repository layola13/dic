safari:/səˈfɑːri/|/ˈsæfɑːri/| n. |狩猎旅行；观光旅行；（电脑）浏览器| A safari is a journey or expedition, especially one for hunting game animals in East Africa.  The term is now also used for any adventurous journey or sightseeing trip.|例句：We went on a safari in Kenya and saw lions, elephants, and giraffes. 我们在肯尼亚进行了一次狩猎旅行，看到了狮子、大象和长颈鹿。|例句：The company offers guided safaris to various national parks. 这家公司提供去各个国家公园的导游狩猎旅行。|近义词：expedition, excursion, journey, tour |反义词：stay-at-home, sedentary activity
