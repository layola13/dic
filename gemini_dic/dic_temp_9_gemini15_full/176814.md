readopted:/ˌriːˈɑːptɪd/| v. |重新采用，再次收养 |To adopt again; to take up or accept again.  |例句：The city council readopted the old zoning laws.  市议会重新采用了旧的区域规划法。
例句：After the initial adoption failed, the couple readopted the child.  第一次收养失败后，这对夫妇再次收养了孩子。|近义词：re-establish, reinstate, resume|反义词：abandon, reject, forsake|动词
