shifter:/ˈʃɪftər/| n. v.|换档器；换挡者；改变者；转移者 |A shifter is a device or person that shifts or changes something.  It can refer to a mechanical device (like a gear shifter in a car), a person who changes things, or something that causes a change or shift. |例句：He slammed the gear shifter into reverse. 他猛地把换档杆挂入倒档。

The political landscape is a constant shifter, with power dynamics always changing.  政治格局是一个不断变化的因素，权力动态总是在变化。|近义词：changer, changer,  mover,  transformer |反义词：stabilizer,  maintainer
