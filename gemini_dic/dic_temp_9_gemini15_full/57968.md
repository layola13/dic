dripstone:/ˈdrɪpstoʊn/| n. |滴石，钟乳石|Calcium carbonate deposits formed by dripping water in caves.  These formations can take many shapes, including stalactites (hanging from the ceiling) and stalagmites (rising from the floor).|The cave was filled with stunning dripstone formations, some reaching towards the ceiling like ancient, frozen waterfalls.|近义词：stalactite, stalagmite|反义词：无
