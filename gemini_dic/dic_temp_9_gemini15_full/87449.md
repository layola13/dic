hesitatory: /ˈhezɪteɪtəri/ | adj. |犹豫的，迟疑的 | Showing or feeling hesitancy.  Characterized by or given to hesitation. |

例句：

He gave a hesitant answer. = 他给了一个犹豫的回答。

Her hesitant steps showed her nervousness. = 她犹豫的步伐显示了她的紧张。


近义词：vacillating, wavering, uncertain, irresolute, faltering

反义词：decisive, resolute, confident, certain


词源:

The word "hesitatory" is derived from the verb "hesitate," which comes from the Latin word "hæsĭtāre," meaning "to stick, be slow, stammer."  The suffix "-tory" transforms the verb into an adjective, indicating a quality or characteristic of hesitation.
