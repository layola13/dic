ooze-out:/uːz aʊt/| v.|渗出，慢慢流出 |To slowly flow or leak out; to gradually emerge or become known.|The honey oozed out of the broken jar.蜂蜜从破了的罐子里渗了出来。|The truth slowly oozed out after the investigation.真相在调查之后慢慢地浮出水面。| seep, leak, exude |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境选择如：remain, stay)| 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water oozes out of the crack in the pipe. = 水从管道裂缝中渗出来。
The news slowly oozes out to the public. = 消息慢慢地传到了公众那里。


一般过去时:
The blood oozed out of the wound. = 血从伤口渗了出来。
The secret oozed out during the party. = 秘密在聚会上泄露了。


现在完成时:
The oil has oozed out of the engine. = 机油已经从发动机里渗出来了。
The truth has oozed out over the past few weeks. = 真相在过去几周里逐渐浮出水面。


过去完成时:
The ink had oozed out all over the page before I could stop it. = 我还没来得及阻止，墨水就已经渗透了整页纸。
The details of the scandal had oozed out before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，丑闻的细节就已经泄露了。


将来时:
The paint will ooze out if you apply it too thickly. = 如果你涂得太厚，油漆就会渗出来。
The information will eventually ooze out to the media. = 这些信息最终会泄露给媒体。


虚拟语气:
If the dam were to break, the water would ooze out into the valley. = 如果大坝决堤，水就会渗入山谷。
I wish the news wouldn't ooze out so quickly. = 我希望消息不要这么快就泄露出去。


被动语态:
The syrup was oozed out of the container. = 糖浆从容器中渗了出来。（此被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）
The information was slowly oozed out by an anonymous source. = 信息由一个匿名消息来源慢慢泄露出去。


疑问句:
Is the oil oozing out of the engine? = 机油是从发动机里渗出来的吗？
Is the truth oozing out yet? = 真相已经泄露了吗？


否定句:
The water didn't ooze out of the pipe. = 水没有从管道里渗出来。
The secret didn't ooze out despite our efforts to keep it hidden. = 尽管我们努力保密，但秘密还是没有泄露。



PHRASE->
ooze out of the cracks = 从裂缝中渗出
ooze out from under = 从下面渗出
ooze out slowly = 慢慢地渗出
ooze out of control = 失去控制
ooze out information = 泄露信息
