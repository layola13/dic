dislocates: /ˈdɪsloʊkeɪts/ | vt. |使脱位；使错位；扰乱；使混乱 | To put (something, especially a bone) out of its normal place or position.  |例句：The fall dislocated his shoulder.  摔倒使他的肩膀脱臼了。 |例句：The new policy dislocated the entire production process. 新政策扰乱了整个生产流程。|近义词：displace, derange, disarrange |反义词：locate, align, stabilize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The accident dislocates his jaw. = 事故使他的下巴脱臼了。
The new system dislocates the workflow. = 新系统扰乱了工作流程。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:
