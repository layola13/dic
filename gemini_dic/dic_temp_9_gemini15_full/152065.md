patera:/ˈpætərə/| n.|浅盘状凹地；酒杯状陨石坑；|A shallow, broad, saucer-like depression, especially on the surface of a planet or moon.  Often used to describe a type of crater with a shallow, flat bottom and relatively low rim. |Example: The Martian surface is marked by numerous paterae, indicating past volcanic activity.|近义词：crater, depression, basin|反义词：peak, mountain
