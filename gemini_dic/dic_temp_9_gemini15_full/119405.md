microfiche:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfiʃ/| n. |缩微胶片|A microfiche is a sheet of film containing microimages of documents, typically reduced to about 1/24th of their original size.  It's a method of storing large amounts of information in a compact format.|例句：The library archives are stored on microfiche. 图书馆的档案保存在缩微胶片上。  The researcher spent hours examining the microfiche. 研究人员花了数小时检查缩微胶片。|近义词：microfilm|反义词：full-size print
