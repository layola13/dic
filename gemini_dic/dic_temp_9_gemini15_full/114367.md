majorities:/məˈdʒɒrɪtiz/ | n. |多数；大多数 |The word "majorities" refers to the greater number or part of something.  It implies a plurality, exceeding half.  It's often used in the context of votes, opinions, or populations. |例句：The bill passed with a large majority.  这项法案以压倒性多数通过。

The proposed changes received the support of the majority of shareholders.  提议的变更得到了大多数股东的支持。


近义词：pluralities, major portions, greater numbers |反义词：minorities
