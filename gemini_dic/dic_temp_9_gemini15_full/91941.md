hypermetropia:/ˌhaɪpərˈmiːtriə/| n.|远视，远视眼|Hypermetropia, also known as farsightedness, is a refractive error of the eye where parallel rays of light focus behind the retina instead of directly on it.|She was diagnosed with hypermetropia and needed glasses.|近义词：farsightedness|反义词：myopia
