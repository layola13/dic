saltern:/ˈsɔːltən/| n.|盐田；晒盐场|A saltern is an area of land where seawater is evaporated to produce salt.  It's typically a shallow basin or series of basins where seawater is channeled and left to evaporate naturally under the sun. The salt is then harvested.|The ancient salterns of the Mediterranean are a testament to centuries of salt production.  那些地中海古老的盐田证明了几个世纪以来盐的生产。|saltworks, salt pan|freshwater pond
