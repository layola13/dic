showboats:/ˈʃoʊboʊts/| n.|炫耀者；卖弄的人；花里胡哨的船；（尤指）戏船|A showboat is a person who shows off or boasts about their accomplishments or possessions.  It can also refer to a type of boat used for theatrical performances, especially in the past.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying excessive vanity or a lack of substance. |例句：He's such a showboat, always bragging about his achievements. 他真是个爱炫耀的人，总是吹嘘自己的成就。


He bought a fancy showboat and sailed it around the lake. 他买了一艘花哨的游船，在湖上航行。


近义词：boaster, braggart, exhibitionist|反义词：humble, modest|名词
