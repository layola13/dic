goodbyes:/ˌɡʊdˈbaɪz/| n.|告别；再见|The plural form of "goodbye," referring to instances of saying farewell.  It can also refer to the act of leaving or the feeling associated with separation. |例句：We exchanged tearful goodbyes at the airport.我们在机场依依不舍地告别。
例句：Saying goodbyes is always difficult.  告别总是很难。|近义词：farewells, leave-takings|反义词：greetings, hellos|名词
