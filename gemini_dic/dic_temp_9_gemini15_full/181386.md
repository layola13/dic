reinvolvement:/ˌriːɪnˈvɒlvmənt/| n. |再次参与；重新参与；卷入 |The act or process of becoming involved again in something;  a return to participation or engagement.  |例句：His reinvolvement in the project was crucial to its success. 他的重新参与对项目的成功至关重要。

例句：After a period of absence, her reinvolvement in the community was welcomed.  一段时间缺席后，她重新参与社区活动受到了欢迎。


近义词：re-engagement, resumption, return, participation

反义词：withdrawal, disengagement, abandonment

词性：名词
