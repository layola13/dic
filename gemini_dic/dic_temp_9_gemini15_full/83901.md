half-awake: /ˌhɑːf əˈweɪk/ | adj. | 半醒的，昏昏沉沉的 |  Partially awake; in a state between being fully awake and fully asleep.  |例句：I felt half-awake all day after a restless night.  我一夜没睡好，整天都昏昏沉沉的。

例句：He stumbled through the presentation, clearly half-awake. 他磕磕绊绊地完成了演示，显然是昏昏沉沉的。


近义词：dozy, drowsy, sleepy, lethargic

反义词：wide-awake, alert, vigilant


词源:  The word is a compound of "half" (meaning one of two equal parts) and "awake" (meaning not asleep).  Its meaning is a straightforward combination of these two elements.
