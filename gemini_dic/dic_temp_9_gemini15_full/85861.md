head-off:/hedˈɔːf/ | vt. | 预防；阻止；提前出发 | To prevent something from happening; to leave before someone or something else. | 例句：We need to head off potential problems before they arise.  我们需要在问题出现之前就将其扼杀在摇篮里。

We headed off early to avoid the traffic. 我们早早出发以避免交通堵塞。


近义词：prevent, avert, anticipate, preempt | 反义词：allow, encourage, facilitate | 词性：动词


USAGE->

They head off to work early every morning. 他们每天早上很早就出发去上班。
