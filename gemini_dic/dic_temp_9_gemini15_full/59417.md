earthiness:/ˈɜːrθɪnəs/| n. |泥土气息；质朴；粗俗；现实性 |The quality of being earthy;  a characteristic of something that is down-to-earth, unrefined, or natural.  It can refer to a literal connection to the earth, or a figurative connection to simple, unpretentious things.  The connotation can be positive (suggesting authenticity and naturalness) or negative (suggesting crudeness or vulgarity).|例句：The painting captured the earthiness of the rural landscape. 这幅画捕捉到了乡村景观的泥土气息。The earthiness of her humor sometimes offended more refined sensibilities. 她幽默中的粗俗有时会冒犯更精致的感受。|近义词：rusticity, naturalness, realism, crudeness, vulgarity |反义词：refinement, sophistication, artificiality
