gossipy:/ˈɡɑːsɪpi/| adj. |爱说闲话的；爱搬弄是非的 |Given her gossipy nature, she always knows the latest news, often before it's officially announced.  考虑到她爱嚼舌根的本性，她总是知道最新的消息，通常是在消息正式公布之前。|近义词：chatty, tattling, scandal-mongering |反义词：discreet, reserved, quiet
