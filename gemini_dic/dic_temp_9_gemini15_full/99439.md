interest:/ˈɪntrəst/| n. v.|兴趣；利息；利益；使感兴趣|

英文解析:
Noun:  A feeling of wanting to know or learn about something or someone; a subject that someone is interested in;  the money paid regularly at a particular rate for the use of money lent. Verb: To attract someone's attention and make them want to know more; to affect someone or something favorably or unfavorably.


例句:
My main interest is history. = 我的主要兴趣是历史。

He has a strong interest in music. = 他对音乐有浓厚的兴趣。


The bank charges a high rate of interest. = 银行收取高额利息。

The interest on the loan is 5% per year. = 这笔贷款的利息是每年5%。


The new policy interests many people. = 这项新政策引起了许多人的兴趣。


The teacher interests her students with exciting stories. = 老师用激动人心的故事吸引了学生们的兴趣。


近义词:
Noun: fascination, curiosity, concern, involvement, benefit, profit.
Verb: engage, attract, appeal, intrigue.


反义词:
Noun: boredom, apathy, disinterest, indifference, loss.
Verb: bore, repel.


词性:
Noun and Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My sister interests herself in painting. = 我妹妹对绘画感兴趣。
This topic interests many people. = 这个话题吸引了许多人。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
Has the new project interested any investors? = 新项目有没有吸引到任何投资者？
This book has interested me for years. = 这本书多年来一直让我着迷。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish this article interested more people. = 我希望这篇文章能吸引更多的人。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
