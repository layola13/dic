falsificator:/ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪtər/ | n. |伪造者，篡改者 | A person who falsifies something, especially documents or evidence.  Someone who creates or presents something false as genuine. | The falsificator was caught red-handed when the police found discrepancies in his documents. 伪造者在警方发现其文件中的差异时被当场抓住。  The falsificator attempted to deceive the court with fabricated evidence. 伪造者试图用伪造的证据欺骗法庭。| fabricator, counterfeiter, forger | authenticator, verifier | 名词
