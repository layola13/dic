disheir:/dɪsˈhɛər/| vt.|剥夺继承权|To deprive (someone) of the right of inheritance.  The word implies a deliberate act of exclusion, often due to wrongdoing or disfavor.|例句：The king disinherited his rebellious son. 国王剥夺了他叛逆儿子的继承权。|例句：She was disinherited because of her scandalous behavior. 因为她的丑闻行为，她被剥夺了继承权。|近义词：dispossess, deprive, divest|反义词：inherit, bequeath|动词
