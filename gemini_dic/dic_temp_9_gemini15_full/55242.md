dissimilate:/dɪˈsɪmɪleɪt/| vt.| 使不同; 使异化; 使不相似 | To make or become different; to cause to lose similarity or resemblance.  The process of becoming dissimilar. |例句：The melting pot effect aims to dissimilate different cultures into a unified whole, but this can also lead to the loss of unique cultural identities.  熔炉效应旨在将不同的文化融合成一个统一的整体，但这也会导致独特文化身份的丧失。

例句：Over time, the two species dissimilated, developing distinct characteristics.  随着时间的推移，这两个物种变得不同，发展出独特的特征。


近义词：differentiate, distinguish, diversify, alienate


反义词：assimilate, homogenize, unify


词源：dis- (否定前缀) + similate (来自拉丁语 similis, meaning "like" or "similar")
