pelleting:/ˈpɛlətɪŋ/| n. |制粒；压制成颗粒|The process of making pellets;  the action of forming something into pellets.  It is commonly used in agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing. |例句：The farmer uses a pelleting machine to process the feed for his livestock.  这位农民用制粒机加工牲畜饲料。


The company improved the efficiency of its pelleting process.这家公司提高了其制粒工艺的效率。|近义词：granulation, compression, forming|反义词：pulverizing, grinding, crushing|名词
