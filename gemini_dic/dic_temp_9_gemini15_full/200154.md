skim:/skɪm/| vt. n.|略读；撇去（液体表面）的漂浮物；|快速阅读以抓住要点；从液体表面去除漂浮物|例句：I skimmed the article to get the main points. 我快速浏览了这篇文章以抓住要点。  例句：She skimmed the cream from the top of the milk. 她撇去了牛奶表面的奶油。|近义词：scan, glance, peruse|反义词：read thoroughly, pore over|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skims the newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上浏览报纸。
She skims the cream off the milk. = 她把牛奶表面的奶油撇去。


一般过去时:
I skimmed the chapter before the exam. = 我考试前快速浏览了这一章。
The cook skimmed the impurities from the soup. = 厨师撇去了汤里的杂质。


现在完成时:
I have skimmed several articles this morning. = 我今天早上浏览了几篇文章。
She has skimmed the fat from the broth. = 她已经撇去了肉汤里的脂肪。


过去完成时:
He had skimmed the report before the meeting. = 他在会议前快速浏览了报告。
They had skimmed the oil from the surface of the water. = 他们已经从水面撇去了油。


将来时:
I will skim the book before buying it. = 我买书之前会先浏览一下。
She will skim the surface of the pond for leaves. = 她将从池塘表面撇去树叶。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would skim the whole book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会通读这本书。
I wish I had skimmed the instructions before starting. = 我希望我在开始之前浏览过说明书。


被动语态:
The article was skimmed by many readers. = 这篇文章被许多读者快速浏览过。
The impurities were skimmed from the liquid. = 杂质从液体中被撇去。


疑问句:
Did you skim the email before replying? = 你在回复邮件之前浏览过吗？
Have they skimmed all the debris from the pool? = 他们把游泳池里的所有碎片都撇掉了吗？


否定句:
I didn't skim the contract; I read it carefully. = 我没有浏览合同；我仔细阅读了它。
She doesn't skim the news; she reads every detail. = 她不浏览新闻；她阅读每一个细节。
