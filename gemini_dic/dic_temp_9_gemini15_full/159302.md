pointer:/ˈpɔɪntər/| n.| 指针；指示器；提示者 | A pointer is something that indicates or directs attention to something else, such as a device used to indicate direction, a person who guides or directs, or a symbol in computer programming.  |例句：He used a laser pointer to highlight the key points on the slide. 他用激光笔突出幻灯片上的要点。

例句：The dog acted as a pointer, leading the hunters to the pheasant. 这只狗充当了指示犬，带领猎人们找到了雉鸡。

近义词：indicator, guide, index, cursor

反义词：obscurer, concealer

词源:
The word "pointer" comes from the Middle English word "poyntour," meaning "one who points," ultimately derived from the Old French word "pointe" meaning "point."  The sense of a physical object used for pointing predates the modern computer science usage.
