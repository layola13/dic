radiocarbon-date:/ˌreɪdiəʊˈkɑːrbən deɪt/| n. |放射性碳测年法；碳十四测年法 |The process of determining the age of an object containing organic material by measuring the amount of carbon-14 remaining.  This method is used in archaeology and other fields to date artifacts and fossils. |例句：Scientists used radiocarbon dating to determine the age of the ancient artifact. 科学家们使用放射性碳测年法来确定古代文物的年代。

例句：The radiocarbon date of the bone fragment placed the remains in the late Bronze Age. 骨骼碎片的放射性碳测年结果表明遗骸属于青铜时代晚期。


近义词：carbon dating, radiocarbon analysis


反义词：无


词性：名词
