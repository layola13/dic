colorise:/ˈkʌləraɪz/|/ˈkʌlərˌaɪz/| vt.|着色，使彩色化|To add color to (something, especially a black and white photograph or film)  |例句：He colorised the old black and white photos. 他给旧黑白照片着色了。|例句：The movie was colorised for its re-release. 这部电影为了重新发行而被着色了。|近义词：colorize, colourize|反义词：decolorize, desaturate|动词
