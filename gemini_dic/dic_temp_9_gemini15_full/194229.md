self-managing: /ˌsɛlfˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我管理的，自主管理的 |  Describes a system, process, or person capable of managing itself or themselves without external control or intervention.  It emphasizes independence and proactivity in handling tasks and responsibilities. |


例句：

The self-managing team successfully completed the project ahead of schedule. = 这个自我管理的团队成功地提前完成了项目。

This software uses a self-managing database to optimize performance. = 这个软件使用自我管理的数据库来优化性能。


近义词：autonomous, independent, self-governing, self-regulating


反义词：dependent, controlled, managed


词性：形容词
