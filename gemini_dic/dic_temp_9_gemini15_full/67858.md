fabella:/fəˈbɛlə/| n.|小骨；籽骨|A small, sesamoid bone, most commonly found behind the lateral condyle of the femur (thigh bone).  It's an accessory bone that varies greatly in size and presence among individuals.  Its function is not fully understood, but it may assist in reducing friction or providing mechanical advantage during knee flexion.|例句：The presence of a fabella is a common anatomical variation.  = 籽骨的存在是一种常见的解剖学变异。
