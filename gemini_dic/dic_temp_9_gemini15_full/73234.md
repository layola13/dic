forcipes: /ˈfɔːrsɛps/ | n. | 镊子，钳子 |  A surgical instrument with two blades that can be squeezed together to grasp or hold something.  For example, surgeons use forceps to hold tissues during an operation, or dentists might use them to remove teeth. | The surgeon used forceps to carefully remove the shard of glass.  外科医生用镊子小心地取出玻璃碎片。

The dentist used forceps to extract the damaged tooth. 牙医用镊子拔掉了受损的牙齿。


近义词：tweezers, pliers | 反义词：None (no direct antonym) | 词性: n.
