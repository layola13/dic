cinematographers:/ˌsɪnəməˈtɑːɡrəfərz/ | n. | 电影摄影师 |  A cinematographer is a person who is skilled in the art of cinematography and responsible for the visual aspects of a film or video production. They work closely with the director to create the visual storytelling of the movie.  |  Roger Deakins is a highly acclaimed cinematographer known for his stunning visuals. 罗杰·狄金斯是一位以其令人惊叹的视觉效果而闻名的备受赞誉的电影摄影师。 The cinematographer carefully chose the lighting to enhance the mood of the scene. 电影摄影师仔细选择灯光来增强场景的情绪。 |  director of photography (DP), cameraman |  (no direct antonym)
