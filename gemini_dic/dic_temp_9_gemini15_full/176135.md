razure:/ˈreɪʒər/| n. |涂抹；刮擦；删除；涂改|The act of erasing or scratching out something written or printed; a mark or blemish resulting from such erasure.|例句：The document showed signs of razure. 这份文件上有涂改的痕迹。

例句：The razure on the contract made it invalid. 合同上的涂改使其无效。


近义词：erasure, deletion, obliteration|反义词：addition, insertion|词性:名词


词源:

From Old French *rasure, from raser "to scrape, shave," from Vulgar Latin *rasāre, from Latin radere "to scrape."  The English word retains the sense of scraping away to remove something written or printed.
