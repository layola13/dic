divorce:/dɪˈvɔːrs/| n. v.|离婚；使离婚|The legal dissolution of a marriage.  The act of ending a marriage.|例句：They are getting a divorce. 他们正在离婚。

例句：He divorced his wife last year. 他去年和他妻子离婚了。


近义词：separation, dissolution|反义词：marriage, union|词性：n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They divorce frequently in this city. = 这个城市离婚率很高。
He divorces his wife because of her bad temper. = 他因为妻子的坏脾气而与她离婚。


一般过去时:
They divorced after twenty years of marriage. = 他们结婚20年后离婚了。
She divorced him because of infidelity. = 她因为他的不忠而与他离婚。


现在完成时:
They have divorced for five years. = 他们离婚五年了。
She has divorced twice. = 她结过两次婚，离过两次婚。


过去完成时:
They had divorced before he met her. = 在他遇到她之前，他们就已经离婚了。
She had divorced him long before he even knew her. = 在他认识她很久以前，她就和他离婚了。


将来时:
They will divorce soon. = 他们很快就要离婚了。
He will divorce her if she continues to cheat on him. = 如果她继续欺骗他，他将会和她离婚。


虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't divorce. = 如果我是他，我不会离婚。
I wish they hadn't divorced. = 我希望他们没有离婚。


被动语态:
The marriage was divorced by the court. = 这段婚姻被法院判决离婚。
Their divorce was finalized last month. = 他们的离婚在上个月定案了。


疑问句:
Are they going to divorce? = 他们要离婚吗？
Has she divorced her husband yet? = 她和她丈夫离婚了吗？


否定句:
They did not divorce. = 他们没有离婚。
She will not divorce him easily. = 她不会轻易和他离婚。
