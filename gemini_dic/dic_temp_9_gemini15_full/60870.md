elegies:/ˈɛlədʒiz/| n.|挽歌；哀歌 | A poem of serious reflection, typically a lament for the dead. Elegies often explore themes of loss, grief, and mortality, but can also address broader themes of human existence. |例句：She wrote a beautiful elegy for her late husband. 她为她已故的丈夫写了一首美丽的挽歌。 |例句：The poet's elegy touched on the fragility of life and the inevitability of death.  诗人的挽歌触及了生命的脆弱和死亡的不可避免性。|近义词：lament, dirge, threnody |反义词：celebration, eulogy (depending on context)
