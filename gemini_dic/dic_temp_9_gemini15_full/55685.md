ditty:/ˈdɪti/| n. |小曲；小调；歌曲 | A short, simple song, often of a light or cheerful nature.  | 例句：She hummed a cheerful ditty as she worked. 她一边工作一边哼着快乐的小曲。 | 例句：The children sang a ditty about a little bird. 孩子们唱了一首关于小鸟的小歌。|近义词：song, tune, air, ballad |反义词：symphony, opera


USAGE->
