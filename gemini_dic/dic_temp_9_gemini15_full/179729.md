reenvision:/ˌriːɪnˈvɪʒən/| vt.|重新构想，重新设想 |To imagine or plan something again in a different way.|例句：We need to reenvision our marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. 我们需要重新构想我们的营销策略以接触更广泛的受众。

例句：The architect reenvisioned the building's design to incorporate more natural light. 建筑师重新设计了建筑物，以融入更多的自然光。


近义词：reimagine, reconceive, rethink, revamp|反义词：maintain, perpetuate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reenvision their business model every year. = 他们每年都会重新构想他们的商业模式。
She reenvisions her future goals regularly. = 她定期重新构想她未来的目标。

一般过去时:
The company reenvisioned its brand identity last year. = 公司去年重新构想了它的品牌形象。
He reenvisioned the plot of his novel after receiving feedback. = 他在收到反馈后重新构想了他的小说的情节。

现在完成时:
We have reenvisioned our approach to customer service. = 我们已经重新构想了我们对客户服务的方法。
They have reenvisioned the project several times. = 他们已经多次重新构想这个项目了。

过去完成时:
They had reenvisioned the plan before presenting it to the board. = 他们在向董事会提交计划之前已经重新构想过了。
She had reenvisioned her career path after a period of reflection. = 在一段时间的反思之后，她已经重新规划了自己的职业道路。


将来时:
We will reenvision the curriculum next year. = 我们明年将重新构想课程大纲。
They will reenvision their marketing campaign in the spring. = 他们将在春天重新构想他们的营销活动。

虚拟语气:
If we reenvisioned our strategy, we might succeed. = 如果我们重新构想我们的策略，我们可能会成功。
I wish they had reenvisioned their approach earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新构想他们的方法。

被动语态:
The project was reenvisioned by the team. = 该项目由团队重新构想。
The marketing strategy is being reenvisioned by the department. = 营销策略正在由部门重新构想。

疑问句:
Will they reenvision the product design? = 他们会重新设计产品吗？
Have you reenvisioned your life goals recently? = 你最近重新构想你的生活目标了吗？

否定句:
They did not reenvision their approach to the problem. = 他们没有重新构想他们解决这个问题的方法。
We will not reenvision the plan unless necessary. = 除非必要，否则我们不会重新构想这个计划。
