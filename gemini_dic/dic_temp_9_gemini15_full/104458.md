kainozoic:/ˌkaɪnəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n. |新生代的；新生代 |The Kainozoic Era, also known as the Cenozoic Era, is the current and most recent of the three Phanerozoic geological eras.  It began 66 million years ago with the Cretaceous–Paleogene extinction event which marked the end of the Mesozoic Era. The term "Kainozoic" is derived from Greek words meaning "new life". This era is characterized by the diversification of mammals, birds, and flowering plants.  | Example: The evolution of primates occurred during the Kainozoic Era. 新生代时期发生了灵长类动物的进化。|近义词：Cenozoic |反义词：Mesozoic, Paleozoic
