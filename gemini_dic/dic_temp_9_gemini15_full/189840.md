samaritan:/ˌsæməˈreɪtən/| n.| 撒玛利亚人；乐于助人的人 | A Samaritan is a person from Samaria, a region in ancient Israel.  The term is now commonly used to refer to someone who is kind and helpful to those in need, especially strangers.  The usage stems from the parable of the Good Samaritan in the Bible. | Example:  He stopped to help the stranded motorist; he was a true Samaritan. 他停下来帮助遇险的司机；他真是个乐于助人的人。|近义词：Good neighbor, benefactor, altruist |反义词：misanthrope, egoist
