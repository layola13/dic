/ˈɛpɪt/| n. |墓志铭，悼词；精华，典型；(诗歌中的)附加诗句 | A short inscription in praise of a deceased person, often inscribed on a tombstone;  the embodiment of something; a short poem added to a longer one. | Example:  The epitaph on the tombstone was simple and moving.  墓碑上的墓志铭简洁而感人。 The novel is an epitaph to a bygone era. 这部小说是对逝去时代的悼词。|近义词：inscription, eulogy, epitaph |反义词：none
