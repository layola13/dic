hydrocyanide:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈsaɪənaɪd/| n. |氰化氢；氢氰酸 |A highly toxic and volatile liquid or gas, used in fumigation and other industrial processes. It is also a component of some pesticides.|例句：The accidental release of hydrocyanide gas caused widespread panic.  意外释放的氰化氢气体引起了广泛恐慌。|例句：Exposure to hydrocyanide can lead to serious health problems, even death. 接触氰化氢可能导致严重的健康问题，甚至死亡。|近义词：prussic acid|反义词：无|词性：名词

词源:

The word "hydrocyanide" is a combination of "hydro-" (meaning "water" or "hydrogen") and "cyanide" (from the Greek "kyanos," meaning "dark blue," referring to the color of some cyanide compounds).  Therefore, it literally means "hydrogen cyanide."
