rebutment:/rɪˈbʌtmənt/| n. |反驳；驳斥；反证|The act of refuting or contradicting a statement or argument.  It involves presenting evidence or reasoning to show that the original statement is incorrect or invalid. |例句：The lawyer presented a strong rebutment to the witness's testimony.  律师对证人的证词提出了强有力的反驳。

例句：His rebutment of the accusations was unconvincing. 他对指控的反驳令人难以信服。


近义词：refutation, contradiction, denial, counterargument |反义词：confirmation, corroboration, affirmation
