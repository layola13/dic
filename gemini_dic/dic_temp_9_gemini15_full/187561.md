romant:/roʊˈmænt/| adj.|浪漫的，多情的|Romantic refers to something characterized by or suggesting the idealized qualities of romance, such as love, passion, excitement, and adventure. It can also describe things associated with the Romantic movement in art and literature.|例句：The romantic dinner was a perfect ending to the evening. 这顿浪漫的晚餐为夜晚画上了完美的句号。|近义词：passionate, amorous, idyllic|反义词：realistic, pragmatic
