rummaged:/ˈrʌmɪdʒd/| v.|翻找；搜寻|To search unsystematically and thoroughly through something.|例句：She rummaged in her handbag for her keys. 她翻遍了她的手提包找钥匙。|例句：He rummaged through the drawer looking for his passport. 他翻箱倒柜地找他的护照。|近义词：search, hunt, delve, ferret|反义词：organize, tidy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rummages through his pockets every morning. 他每天早上都翻找他的口袋。
She rummages in her closet for a suitable outfit. 她在衣橱里翻找合适的衣服。

一般过去时:
I rummaged through the attic looking for old photos. 我翻遍了阁楼寻找旧照片。
They rummaged in the box of toys, searching for the missing piece. 他们在玩具箱里翻找，寻找丢失的零件。

现在完成时:
She has rummaged through all her drawers but still hasn't found her glasses. 她已经翻遍了所有的抽屉，但仍然没找到她的眼镜。
He has rummaged through the archives for hours but found nothing relevant. 他在档案室里翻找了几个小时，但什么都没找到。

过去完成时:
I had rummaged through the suitcase before I realised I had the wrong one. 我在意识到拿错了行李箱之前已经翻找过它了。
They had rummaged through the entire house before calling the police. 他们在报警之前已经翻遍了整个房子。

将来时:
I will rummage in the cupboard for some snacks later. 我晚点会在橱柜里翻找一些零食。
She will rummage through the old bookshelves tomorrow. 她明天会翻找旧书架。


被动语态:
The attic was rummaged through by the children. 阁楼被孩子们翻找过。
The drawers were rummaged through, but nothing of value was found. 抽屉被翻找过，但没有发现任何有价值的东西。


疑问句:
Were you rummaging through my things? 你是不是在翻我的东西？
Has he been rummaging in the garbage again? 他是不是又在垃圾桶里翻东西了？


否定句:
He didn't rummage through the bag. 他没有翻找那个包。
She didn't rummage in the desk. 她没有翻找桌子。


PHRASE->
rummage through: 翻找；搜寻
rummage sale: 旧货出售
rummage around: 到处翻找
rummage for: 寻找
rummage in: 在...里翻找
