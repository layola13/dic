USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in the example.  It describes a state or feeling.

词源:
The word "fozy" is a variant spelling of "cozy," which itself is derived from the Dutch word "koozie," meaning "small, snug dwelling."  The "f" in "fozy" is likely a dialectal variation or a spelling error that became established.
