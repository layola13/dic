evolution: /ˌɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. |进化，演变，发展 | The gradual development of something, especially from a simple to a more complex form.  | 例句：The theory of evolution explains the diversity of life on Earth.  进化论解释了地球上生命的多样性。

例句：The evolution of language took thousands of years. 语言的演变花了数千年时间。


近义词：development, progression, advancement | 反义词：regression, devolution

词源:
The word "evolution" comes from the Late Latin word *evolutionem* (nominative *evolutio*), a noun of action from the verb *evolvere*, meaning "to unroll, unfold, disclose."  This reflects the idea of gradual unfolding or development from a simpler to a more complex state.
