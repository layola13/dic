outdwell:/ˌaʊtˈdwel/| v.|居住时间更长，比…住得久|To remain or stay longer than (someone or something).  It implies a surpassing in duration of residence.|例句：The old couple outdwelt all their neighbors in the quiet village. 老夫妇比他们安静村庄里的所有邻居住得都久。

例句：The ancient stones outdwelt the civilizations that built them.  古老的石头比建造它们的文明存在的时间更长。


近义词：outlast, surpass, exceed|反义词：leave, depart, vacate|词性:动词
