guillemet:/ˈɡɪləmɛt/| n. |书名号，角括号|French word for "quotation marks," referring to the angular brackets ⟨⟩ used in some languages to indicate quotations.  These are distinct from the more common double quotes (" ") or single quotes (' ').|Example: The original text was enclosed in guillemets. 原文用书名号括起来了。|近义词：angle brackets, chevrons |反义词：N/A
