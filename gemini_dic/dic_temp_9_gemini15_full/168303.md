pro-Norwegian: /ˌproʊ ˈnɔːrwiːdʒən/ | adj. | 亲挪威的，支持挪威的 | Favoring or supporting Norway or Norwegian interests.  The term often appears in historical and political contexts, referring to sentiments or actions that promote Norwegian independence, autonomy, or cultural identity.  It can also describe individuals or groups actively advocating for Norway's interests in international affairs. | 例句：The pro-Norwegian sentiment in the region was strong during the union with Sweden.  这个地区在与瑞典联盟期间，亲挪威情绪强烈。


The pro-Norwegian press actively campaigned for independence. 亲挪威媒体积极宣传独立。| 近义词：pro-Norway, Norwegian-supporting | 反义词：anti-Norwegian, pro-Danish (in historical context) | 词性：形容词
