incorporator:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtər/| n. |法人设立者；公司发起人；并入者| A person who incorporates a company or organization.  An incorporator is responsible for filing the necessary paperwork to legally establish a corporation. They are often the initial directors or officers of the newly formed entity. |例句：The incorporator signed the articles of incorporation and filed them with the state. 法人设立者签署了公司章程并向州政府提交。

例句：John was the sole incorporator of the new tech startup.  约翰是这家新科技创业公司的唯一法人设立者。


近义词：founder, organizer, promoter |反义词：dissolver, liquidator |词性:noun
