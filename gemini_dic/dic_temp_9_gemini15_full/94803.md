impulses:/ˈɪmpʌlsɪz/| n.|冲动；刺激；脉冲|A sudden strong urge or desire to act.  Impulses can be emotional, physical, or even electrical in nature. They are often unplanned and can lead to both positive and negative consequences.|例句：He acted on impulse and bought the car. 他一时冲动买了那辆车。|例句：The electrical impulses sent a signal to the brain. 电脉冲向大脑发送信号。|近义词： urges, instincts, whims, stimuli |反义词： deliberation, premeditation, reflection
