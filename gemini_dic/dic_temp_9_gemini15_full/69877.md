ferryable: /ˈfɛriəbl/ | adj. | 可渡运的，可渡口的 | Capable of being ferried; suitable for ferrying.  This word describes something that can be transported by ferry.  | Example: The small boat was ferryable across the narrow strait. 这艘小船可以渡过狭窄的海峡。  The large cargo ship was too large to be ferryable across the river. 这艘大型货船太大了，无法渡过这条河。 | 近义词：transportable, conveyable | 反义词：impassable, unnavigable |  形容词
