distaffs: /ˈdɪstæfs/ | n. |纺车|A distaff is a staff, typically of wood, on which flax or wool is wound for spinning.  It is a traditional tool used in spinning yarn. The term also refers, figuratively, to the female line of a family. | The old woman sat by the fire, spinning wool from her distaff.  老妇人坐在火炉旁，用纺车纺羊毛。  |近义词：spindle (in some contexts) |反义词：None.
