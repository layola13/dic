ensues:/ɪnˈsuːz/ | v. | 接着发生；随之而来 | To happen or occur afterward or as a result.  It implies a consequence or a following event. | The argument ensued after a heated disagreement.  争吵在激烈的分歧之后发生。


After the announcement, a period of uncertainty ensued.  公告之后，一段不确定的时期随之而来。| follow, result, succeed | precede  | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What ensues after the storm? = 暴风雨过后会发生什么？
Something good always ensues when we work hard. = 当我们努力工作时，总会有好事发生。

一般过去时:
A fierce debate ensued after his speech. = 他演讲后发生了一场激烈的辩论。
Chaos ensued when the power went out. = 当停电时，混乱随之而来。

现在完成时:
Much confusion has ensued from his unclear instructions.  他的指示不清，导致了很多混乱。
No significant change has ensued since the agreement.  自从协议达成以来，没有发生任何重大变化。

过去完成时:
A lengthy silence had ensued before he finally spoke.  在他最终开口之前，长时间的沉默已经持续了。
Little progress had ensued despite their best efforts. 尽管他们尽了最大努力，但进展甚微。

将来时:
What will ensue if we fail to act? = 如果我们不采取行动，将会发生什么？
A severe drought will likely ensue if it doesn't rain soon. = 如果不久不下雨，很可能会发生严重的干旱。


虚拟语气:
If he had confessed, a different outcome might have ensued. = 如果他认罪了，结果可能会不同。
If we were to ignore the problem, serious consequences would ensue. = 如果我们忽视这个问题，就会产生严重的后果。

被动语态:
A great deal of suffering was ensued by the war. = 战争带来了巨大的痛苦。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
A new era was ensued after the revolution. = 革命之后开始了新纪元。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)

疑问句:
What will ensue from this decision? = 这个决定会带来什么后果？
What ensued after the earthquake? = 地震之后发生了什么？

否定句:
No problems ensued from the change of plans. = 计划变更没有带来任何问题。
Nothing untoward ensued during the operation.  手术期间没有发生任何意外情况。


PHRASE->
ensue from = 源于...
ensue upon = 紧随...之后
ensue in = 在...中发生
ensue after = 在...之后发生
ensue with = 伴随...发生
