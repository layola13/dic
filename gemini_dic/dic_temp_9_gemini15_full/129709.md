non-effective:/ˌnɒnɪˈfektɪv/| adj.|无效的，不起作用的|Not producing any significant or desired effect.  It refers to something that fails to achieve its intended purpose or result. |例句：The medicine proved non-effective in treating the illness. 这药治疗这种疾病无效。

例句：His attempts to persuade her were non-effective. 他试图说服她，但没有成功。

近义词：ineffective, useless, fruitless, unproductive, unavailing

反义词：effective, successful, productive, fruitful, potent

词性:形容词
