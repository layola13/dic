outhunt:/aʊtˈhʌnt/| vt.|胜过（尤指在狩猎中）;超过|To surpass or exceed, especially in hunting or a competitive activity.|例句：The experienced hunter outhunted the newcomer, bagging twice as many deer.  经验丰富的猎人胜过了新手，猎获的鹿是新手的两倍。

例句：The rival company outhunted our team in securing the new client.  竞争对手公司在争取新客户方面胜过了我们团队。


近义词：outsmart, outdo, surpass, excel|反义词：be outdone, be surpassed, be defeated|动词
