north-eastern:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːstən/ | adj. |东北的，来自东北的 |  Describing something situated in or coming from the northeast direction.  It combines "north" and "east" to indicate a direction between north and east. | The north-eastern states experienced heavy snowfall. 东北部各州经历了大雪。|  northeastern, north-east | southwestern,south-western | 形容词
