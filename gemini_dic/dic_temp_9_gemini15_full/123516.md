monotones:/ˈmɒnəʊtəʊnz/| n.|单调的音调；单色调；单调乏味的事物|Sounds or colors that are monotonous lack variety and are dull.  Monotones can refer to a range of things from a speaker's voice to a painter's palette. |例句：The speaker's monotone voice put the audience to sleep.  演讲者的单调乏味的嗓音让听众睡着了。
The artist preferred to work in monotones, using only shades of gray.这位艺术家喜欢用单色调作画，只使用灰色调。|近义词：monotony, uniformity, sameness|反义词：variety, diversity, colorfulness
