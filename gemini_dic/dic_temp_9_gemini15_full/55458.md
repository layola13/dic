distinguishing: /dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 杰出的；显著的；有区别的 | Serving to distinguish; characteristic; marking a difference.  |例句：The bird's distinguishing feature is its bright red crest. 这只鸟最显著的特征是它鲜红的羽冠。
The distinguishing characteristic of this wine is its oaky flavor.  这款酒最显著的特点是其橡木味。|近义词：characteristic, distinctive, remarkable, prominent |反义词：indistinguishable, indistinct, commonplace
