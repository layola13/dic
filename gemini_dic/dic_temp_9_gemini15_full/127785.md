neighbors:/ˈneɪbərz/| n.|邻居们|People who live near you.|The neighbors threw a party last night.|近义词：residents, people nearby|反义词: strangers, foreigners|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My neighbors often have barbecues. = 我的邻居经常举行烧烤派对。
The neighbors are very friendly. = 邻居们非常友好。


一般过去时:
My neighbors helped us move last weekend. = 上周末我的邻居们帮我们搬家。
The neighbors complained about the noise. = 邻居们抱怨噪音。


现在完成时:
The neighbors have lived here for twenty years. = 邻居们在这里住了二十年了。
We have known our neighbors for a long time. = 我们认识我们的邻居很久了。


过去完成时:
We had already met our neighbors before we moved in. = 我们搬进去之前就已经认识了邻居。
The neighbors had complained several times before they called the police. = 邻居们在报警之前已经抱怨过几次了。


将来时:
We will visit our neighbors this weekend. = 我们这个周末将拜访我们的邻居。
The neighbors will be having a party next Saturday. = 邻居们下周六将举行一个派对。


虚拟语气:
If I had good neighbors, I would feel safer. = 如果我有好的邻居，我会感觉更安全。
I wish my neighbors were quieter. = 我希望我的邻居们安静一点。


被动语态:
Our privacy was invaded by the neighbors. = 我们的隐私被邻居侵犯了。
The noise complaint was filed against our neighbors. = 噪音投诉是针对我们的邻居提出的。


疑问句:
Do you know your neighbors? = 你认识你的邻居吗？
Have you met your new neighbors yet? = 你见过你的新邻居了吗？


否定句:
We don't know our neighbors very well. = 我们不太了解我们的邻居。
My neighbors didn't seem to notice the problem. = 我的邻居似乎没有注意到这个问题。


PHRASE->
