preexchanging:/ˌpriːɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/| adj.| 事先交换的；预先交换的 |  Describes an action or process of exchanging something before a main event or action. It implies preparation or anticipation. | Example: The preexchanging of information ensured a smooth negotiation.  事先交换信息确保了谈判顺利进行。 |近义词：prior exchange, advance exchange |反义词：postexchanging
