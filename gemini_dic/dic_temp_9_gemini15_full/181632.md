relaxatory: /rɪˈlæksətɔːri/ | adj. |令人放松的，松弛的 | Tending to relax or soothe; having a relaxing effect.  | The spa offered a relaxatory atmosphere, easing tension and promoting well-being.  水疗中心提供轻松愉快的氛围，缓解压力，促进身心健康。| soothing, calming, tranquilizing | tense, stressful, stimulating | 形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  The following examples demonstrate its usage in different sentence structures.


肯定句:
The music had a relaxatory effect on me. = 这音乐对我有着放松的效果。
The massage was incredibly relaxatory. = 按摩非常放松。


否定句:
The loud noises were not relaxatory at all. = 嘈杂的声音一点也不令人放松。
The film wasn't relaxatory; it was quite stressful. = 这部电影一点也不令人放松；它相当令人紧张。


疑问句:
Is this environment relaxatory enough for you? = 这个环境对你来说足够放松吗？
Was the aromatherapy session relaxatory? = 香薰疗程令人放松吗？


PHRASE->
relaxatory atmosphere = 放松的氛围
relaxatory environment = 放松的环境
relaxatory music = 舒缓的音乐
relaxatory activities = 放松的活动
