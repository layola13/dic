encroached:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃt/ | v. | 侵占；侵蚀；蚕食 | To intrude on (a person's territory, rights, etc.); to advance gradually beyond usual or proper limits. |例句：The sea encroached upon the land after the storm.  风暴过后，海水侵蚀了陆地。  |例句：The new shopping mall encroached on the historic district. 新建的购物中心侵占了历史街区。 |近义词：invade, trespass, infringe, impinge |反义词：recede, withdraw, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government encroaches on our freedoms. = 政府侵犯了我们的自由。
The desert slowly encroaches on the farmland. = 沙漠慢慢地侵蚀着农田。

一般过去时:
The forest encroached on the village over many years. = 多年来，森林侵占了村庄。
The sea encroached significantly after the tsunami. = 海啸过后，海水严重侵蚀了陆地。


现在完成时:
The city has encroached further into the countryside. = 城市进一步侵入了乡村。
The weeds have encroached upon the garden path. = 杂草已经蔓延到了花园小路上。


过去完成时:
The developers had encroached upon the wetlands before the regulations were put in place. = 开发商在法规实施之前就已经侵占了湿地。
The ice had encroached so far that the village was isolated. = 冰雪蔓延得如此之远，以至于村庄与世隔绝。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will encroach on coastal communities. = 海平面上升将侵蚀沿海社区。
The ambitious corporation plans to encroach upon the market share of its competitors. = 这家雄心勃勃的公司计划侵占其竞争对手的市场份额。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't encroached on their rights, the people would have been happier. = 如果政府没有侵犯他们的权利，人民会更幸福。
I wish the construction company hadn't encroached on the park. = 我真希望建筑公司没有侵占公园。


被动语态:
Their land was encroached upon by the expanding city. = 他们的土地被扩张的城市侵占了。
The villagers' rights were encroached upon by the powerful corporation. = 村民的权利被强大的公司侵犯了。


疑问句:
Has the building encroached on the historical landmark? = 这座建筑物是否侵占了历史地标？
Did the floodwaters encroach upon the residential areas? = 洪水是否侵入了居民区？


否定句:
The government did not encroach on the people's right to protest. = 政府没有侵犯人民的抗议权。
The new highway did not encroach on the existing wildlife preserve. = 新高速公路没有侵占现有的野生动物保护区。


PHRASE->
encroach upon/on = 侵犯；侵占
encroachment of = …的侵占
the encroachment of sth = 某事的侵蚀
encroachment on sth = 对…的侵犯
gradual encroachment = 逐渐侵占
