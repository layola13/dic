mullah:/ˈmʌlə/| n.|毛拉，伊斯兰教神职人员|A mullah is a Muslim religious leader, teacher, or scholar, often one who leads prayers in a mosque.  The term's usage and connotations vary across different Islamic cultures.|例句：The mullah delivered a powerful sermon on the importance of charity.  这位毛拉发表了一篇关于施舍重要性的有力布道。

例句：Many mullahs opposed the government's new policies. 许多毛拉反对政府的新政策。
