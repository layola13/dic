looses:/ˈluːsɪz/ | adj. | 松的；宽松的；不精确的；不严密的 | Loose describes something that is not tight or firmly fixed. It can also refer to something that is not precise or strict.  | 例句：His trousers are too loose. = 他的裤子太松了。

The definition of the term was rather loose. = 这个术语的定义相当宽泛。| 近义词：slack, baggy, relaxed | 反义词：tight, firm, strict
