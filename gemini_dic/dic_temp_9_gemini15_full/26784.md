cafe:/ˈkæfeɪ/| n. |咖啡馆，小餐馆|A cafe is a small restaurant, typically one that serves coffee and light meals.|例句：We met at a cozy cafe near the park. 我们在公园附近一家舒适的咖啡馆见面了。|近义词：coffee shop, bistro, diner|反义词：large restaurant, formal dining establishment
