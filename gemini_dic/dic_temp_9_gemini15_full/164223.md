predominantly:/prɪˈdɒmɪnəntli/ | adv. | 主要地；显著地；大部分地 |  Primarily or chiefly; for the most part.  It emphasizes that something is the most noticeable or important feature. | 例句：The city's population is predominantly Hispanic.  这座城市的人口主要为西班牙裔。

The conference was predominantly attended by women.  会议主要由女性参加。


近义词：mainly, largely, chiefly, mostly, primarily | 反义词：minimally, slightly, scarcely, rarely
