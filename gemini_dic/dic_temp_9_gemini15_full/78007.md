gentlemen:/ˈdʒɛntlmən/| n. |先生们；绅士们 |A collective term of address for a group of men, often implying politeness and respect.  It can also refer to men of refined manners and high social standing. |Examples: Gentlemen, please be seated. 先生们，请坐。 The gentlemen of the club enjoyed a quiet evening.  俱乐部的先生们享受了一个宁静的夜晚。|近义词： Sirs, men,  |反义词：ladies (in contrast)
