词源:
The word "goofiness" derives from the word "goof,"  which emerged in American English in the late 19th or early 20th century.  The exact origin of "goof" is uncertain, but it's likely related to informal or slang terms for foolishness or a foolish person. The suffix "-iness" is added to create the noun form, indicating a state or quality.
