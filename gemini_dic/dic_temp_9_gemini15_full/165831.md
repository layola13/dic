preoccupied:/ˌpriːˈɒkjupaɪd/| adj.|全神贯注的；心事重重的；被占据的|Engrossed in thought; deeply engaged with something; filled or occupied by something.|例句：She seemed preoccupied with her own thoughts. 她似乎心事重重。
He was so preoccupied with work that he forgot his appointment. 他工作太投入了，忘了约会。|近义词：absorbed, engrossed, engrossed, lost in thought|反义词：unconcerned, unconcerned, detached, carefree


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is preoccupied with his studies. 他全神贯注于他的学习。
She is preoccupied by worries about her job. 她为工作上的担忧而心烦意乱。


一般过去时:
He was preoccupied during the meeting. 他在会议期间心不在焉。
She was preoccupied with a personal matter. 她当时正忙于一件私事。


现在完成时:
He has been preoccupied with this project for weeks.  他几周来一直忙于这个项目。
She has been preoccupied lately; I don’t know what’s wrong. 她最近心事重重；我不知道出了什么事。


过去完成时:
He had been preoccupied all day, so he didn't notice the email. 他整天都心不在焉，所以没有注意到邮件。
She had been preoccupied with her illness, so she missed the deadline. 她一直为自己的病情担忧，所以错过了截止日期。


将来时:
He will be preoccupied with his new job next month. 下个月他将忙于他的新工作。
She will likely be preoccupied with wedding plans. 她很可能会忙于婚礼计划。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so preoccupied, he would have noticed the problem sooner. 如果他不那么心不在焉，他就会早点发现这个问题。
I wish I weren't so preoccupied; I could really enjoy this party. 我希望我不那么心事重重；这样我就能真正享受这个派对了。


被动语态:
His mind was preoccupied by anxieties. 他的思绪被焦虑占据。
The room was preoccupied with the aroma of coffee.房间里弥漫着咖啡的香味。（此例被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动）


疑问句:
Is he preoccupied with something? 他是不是心事重重？
Are you preoccupied with your work? 你是不是工作太忙了？


否定句:
He wasn't preoccupied at all; he was just bored. 他一点也不心不在焉；他只是无聊。
She wasn't preoccupied; she was simply listening intently. 她并不心不在焉；她只是专心致志地听着。


PHRASE->
be preoccupied with: 专注于…；为…而心烦意乱
become preoccupied: 开始心事重重
remain preoccupied: 保持心事重重
feel preoccupied: 感到心事重重
seem preoccupied: 看起来心事重重
