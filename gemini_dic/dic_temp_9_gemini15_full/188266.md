/ˈkɔːrpəs/ | n. | 语料库 | A large and structured set of texts (or speech) used for linguistic research.  A corpus may be composed of written or spoken material, or both.  It is used to study language patterns, grammar, vocabulary, and other linguistic features.  | Example: The researchers used a large corpus of English novels to analyze changes in vocabulary over time.  研究人员使用大量的英文小说语料库来分析词汇随时间的变化。 | 近义词：collection, database, archive | 反义词：N/A
