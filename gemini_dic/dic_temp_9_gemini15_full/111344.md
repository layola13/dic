liveliness:/ˈlaɪvlinəs/ | n. | 活泼；生气；生动 | The liveliness of the party was infectious.  派对的热闹气氛极具感染力。|  animation, vivacity, sprightliness | dullness, lethargy, apathy | 名词


例句：

The children's liveliness filled the room with energy. 孩子们的活泼给房间带来了活力。

Her performance was full of liveliness and charm. 她的表演充满了活力和魅力。


词源：

源自live（活着的，有生命的）+ -liness（名词后缀，表示“性质”、“状态”）。  表示“充满生命力的状态”。


近义词：

animation, vivacity, sprightliness, energy, enthusiasm, vibrancy


反义词：

dullness, lethargy, apathy, sluggishness, inactivity, lifelessness
