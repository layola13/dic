retransferred:/ˌriːtrænsˈfɜːrd/| v. |再转让，重新转移|To transfer something again; to transfer something that has already been transferred.  This implies a second transfer of ownership, possession, or responsibility.|例句：The property was retransferred to the original owner after a legal dispute.  房产在法律纠纷后被重新转让给了原业主。

The rights were retransferred to the subsidiary company. 权利被重新转让给了子公司。|近义词：retransfer, reassign, reconvey|反义词：retain, keep,  acquire initially
