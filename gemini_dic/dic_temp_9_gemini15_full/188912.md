rut:/rʌt/ | n. |车辙；惯例；刻板的套路| A rut is a deep track made by the repeated passage of wheels or other vehicles.  It can also refer to a fixed, habitual routine, often one that is dull or unproductive. |例句：The car wheels left deep ruts in the muddy track. 车轮在泥泞的小路上留下了深深的车辙。

The company is stuck in a rut and needs to innovate. 这家公司墨守成规，需要创新。|近义词：groove, track, routine, habit |反义词：innovation, change, novelty |词性：名词
