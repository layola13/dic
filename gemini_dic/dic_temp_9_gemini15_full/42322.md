counter-salient: /ˈkaʊntərˈseɪliənt/ | n. | 反突出部，反突出点 | A salient that projects toward the enemy from a main defensive line.  It is a projecting part of a battle line that is opposite to and faces a salient on the enemy's line.  It's strategically important because it allows for a counter-attack or defense against an enemy's salient.  | Example: The counter-salient was heavily fortified to prevent enemy penetration.  反突出部设有重兵防守以阻止敌人渗透。 | 近义词: reverse salient, opposing salient | 反义词: salient (突出部) |  词性: 名词
