parameterize:/pəˈræmɪtəraɪz/ | vt. |使参数化；用参数表示|To express or represent (something, such as a curve or surface) in terms of parameters.  To assign parameters to (a model, system, etc.).|

例句：

We need to parameterize the curve to perform the calculation. = 我们需要将曲线参数化才能进行计算。

The engineers parameterized the simulation model with various input values. = 工程师们用不同的输入值对仿真模型进行了参数化。


近义词：parametrize, specify, define

反义词：deparameterize (less common)


词性：动词


词源：parameter + -ize


英文解析：The verb "parameterize" combines the noun "parameter" (a variable or arbitrary constant) with the suffix "-ize," which means "to cause to be" or "to treat as."  Therefore, to parameterize something is to express it using parameters, essentially representing it in a more flexible or adjustable way.  This is common in mathematics, computer science, and engineering.  It often involves defining a system or model in terms of variables that can be changed to alter the system's behavior.
