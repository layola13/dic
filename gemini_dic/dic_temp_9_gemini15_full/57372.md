doyen:/ˈdɔɪən/ | n. |资深人士；老前辈；权威；元老 |A doyen is a senior and respected member of a group or profession.  It often implies a long history of experience and influence within that field.  |例句：He is the doyen of British architects. 他是英国建筑师中的元老。  |例句：She is considered the doyen of the fashion industry. 她被认为是时尚界的权威。 |近义词：veteran, elder, leader, authority |反义词：novice, beginner, newcomer |名词
