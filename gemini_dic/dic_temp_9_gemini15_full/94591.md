imprecate:/ˈɪmprɪkeɪt/| vt.|诅咒；咒骂；恳求；祈求|To invoke (evil) upon; to curse.  To call upon (a deity) to inflict evil upon; to curse. To beseech earnestly.|例句：He imprecated curses upon his enemies. 他诅咒他的敌人。

例句：They imprecated the gods for a good harvest. 他们向上天祈祷丰收。


近义词：curse, execrate, denounce, anathematize|反义词：bless, praise, commend


词源:
From Late Latin *imprecari ("to invoke evil upon," "to curse"), from Latin prex, precationem ("prayer, entreaty") + in- ("upon, against").  The original sense involved a prayer, but one directed at bringing harm.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imprecates his rivals daily. = 他每天诅咒他的竞争对手。
She imprecates misfortune on those who wronged her. = 她诅咒那些冤枉她的人倒霉。


一般过去时:
The witch imprecated a terrible fate upon the village. = 女巫诅咒村庄遭受可怕的命运。
He imprecated vengeance on his betrayer. = 他诅咒背叛他的人遭到报应。


现在完成时:
They have imprecated divine wrath upon their enemies for generations. = 世世代代他们都诅咒神灵降怒于他们的敌人。
She has imprecated misfortune upon her former friends. = 她已经诅咒她的前朋友们倒霉。


过去完成时:
The villagers had imprecated a curse on the land before the drought. = 在干旱之前，村民们已经诅咒了这片土地。
He had imprecated a swift and painful death upon his enemies. = 他曾诅咒他的敌人迅速而痛苦地死去。


将来时:
They will imprecate a curse upon anyone who dares to enter. = 他们将诅咒任何胆敢进入的人。
She will imprecate the gods for mercy. = 她将向上帝祈祷怜悯。（注意这里implicate更接近祈求的意思）


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not imprecate such a fate on him. = 如果我是你，我不会对他下这样的诅咒。
I wish I hadn't imprecated such harsh words upon her. = 我真希望我没有对她说过这么严厉的话。


被动语态:
His enemies were imprecated by the wronged man. = 他的敌人被那个受冤枉的人诅咒了。
The land was imprecated upon by the angry god. = 这片土地受到了愤怒之神的诅咒。


疑问句:
Did he imprecate evil upon his enemies? = 他诅咒他的敌人吗？
Would you imprecate a curse on someone who hurt you? = 你会诅咒伤害你的人吗？


否定句:
He did not imprecate curses on his opponents. = 他没有诅咒他的对手。
She would not imprecate a curse, even on those who deserved it. = 她不会诅咒任何人，即使是那些应该受到诅咒的人。
