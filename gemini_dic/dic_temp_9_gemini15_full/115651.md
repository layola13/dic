marcus:/ˈmɑːrkəs/| n. |马库斯（男子名）|Marcus is a popular masculine given name of Latin origin.  It is derived from the Roman family name Marcus, which is believed to be related to the Latin word "Mars," the god of war.  The name suggests strength, courage, and masculinity.|例句：My friend's name is Marcus. 我的朋友名叫马库斯。|近义词：Mark (similar sound and meaning)|反义词：无|名词
