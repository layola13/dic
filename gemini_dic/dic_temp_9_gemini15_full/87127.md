herbaria:/hɜːˈbeəriə/| n. |植物标本馆；植物标本集|A herbaria is a collection of preserved plant specimens and associated data used for scientific study.  Herbaria are important resources for botanists, taxonomists, and ecologists. They provide a record of plant diversity through time and space, and are crucial for understanding plant evolution, distribution, and conservation. |例句：The university boasts a world-renowned herbaria with millions of specimens.  大学拥有一个世界知名的植物标本馆，馆藏数百万份标本。

The Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, houses one of the largest herbaria in the world.  英国皇家植物园邱园拥有世界上最大的植物标本馆之一。|近义词：herbarium (单数形式), botanical collection |反义词：无
