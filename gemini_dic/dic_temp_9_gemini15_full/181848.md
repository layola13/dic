relocated:/ˌriːˈloʊkeɪtɪd/| v. |重新安置；迁移；搬迁 | To move to a new place and establish oneself there.  |例句：The company relocated its headquarters to a larger city. 公司将其总部迁至更大的城市。

例句：After the flood, many families were relocated to temporary shelters. 洪水过后，许多家庭被安置到临时住所。


近义词：moved, transferred, transplanted, shifted

反义词：remained, stayed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company relocates its offices annually. = 公司每年都搬迁办公室。
He relocates frequently for his job. = 他因为工作经常搬家。


一般过去时:
They relocated to the countryside last year. = 他们去年搬到了乡下。
We relocated our business to a more convenient location. = 我们把生意搬到了一个更方便的地方。


现在完成时:
She has relocated three times in the last five years. = 在过去的五年里，她已经搬了三次家。
They have relocated their operations to a new facility. = 他们已将其业务转移到新的设施中。


过去完成时:
Before they moved to California, they had relocated to Texas. = 在他们搬到加利福尼亚州之前，他们搬到了德克萨斯州。
He had relocated several times before settling down in London. = 他在伦敦定居之前已经搬过好几次家了。


将来时:
We will relocate to a new apartment next month. = 我们下个月将搬到一间新公寓。
The factory will relocate to a region with lower labor costs. = 工厂将搬迁到劳动力成本较低的地区。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would relocate to a warmer climate. = 如果我有钱，我会搬到气候温暖的地方。
I wish I could relocate and start over. = 我希望我能搬家并重新开始。


被动语态:
The employees were relocated to different departments. = 员工们被调到了不同的部门。
The factory is being relocated to a new location. = 工厂正在搬迁到新的地点。


疑问句:
Have they relocated yet? = 他们搬家了吗？
When will the company be relocated? = 公司什么时候搬迁？


否定句:
They did not relocate their business this year. = 他们今年没有搬迁他们的公司。
We have not yet decided when we will relocate. = 我们还没有决定什么时候搬家。


PHRASE->
relocate to = 搬迁到...
relocate oneself = 重新安置自己
relocate a business = 搬迁企业
relocate staff = 重新安置员工
relocate assets = 重新安置资产
