enjoyed:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪd/| v. |享受过的；喜欢过的 |The past tense and past participle of "enjoy".  It indicates that someone experienced pleasure or satisfaction from something in the past. |例句：I enjoyed the concert last night.  昨晚我享受了音乐会。

例句：She enjoyed reading the book. 她喜欢读这本书。

近义词：liked, relished, savored | 反义词：disliked, loathed, detested | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I enjoy playing tennis. = 我喜欢打网球。
She enjoys reading novels. = 她喜欢读小说。

一般过去时:
I enjoyed the movie. = 我喜欢这部电影。
They enjoyed their vacation. = 他们享受了他们的假期。

现在完成时:
I have enjoyed many wonderful experiences. = 我已经享受了许多美好的经历。
She has enjoyed living in this city. = 她一直很喜欢住在这个城市。

过去完成时:
I had enjoyed the meal before the unexpected guests arrived. = 不速之客到来之前，我已经享用过这顿饭了。
She had enjoyed her time at the beach before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，她已经在海滩上玩得很开心了。

将来时:
I will enjoy my retirement. = 我将享受我的退休生活。
We will enjoy the beautiful scenery. = 我们将欣赏美丽的风景。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would enjoy painting. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会喜欢绘画。
I wish I enjoyed public speaking more. = 我希望我更喜欢公开演讲。

被动语态:
The concert was enjoyed by everyone. = 音乐会受到每个人的喜爱。
The book is enjoyed by readers of all ages. = 这本书受到各个年龄段读者的喜爱。

疑问句:
Did you enjoy the party? = 你喜欢这个派对吗？
Did she enjoy her trip to Paris? = 她喜欢她去巴黎的旅行吗？

否定句:
I didn't enjoy the food. = 我不喜欢这食物。
He didn't enjoy the movie at all. = 他一点也不喜欢这部电影。
