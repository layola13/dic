dominancy:/ˈdɒmɪnənsi/ /ˈdɑːmɪnənsi/| n. |支配；统治；优势；主导地位 | The state or fact of being dominant; power or influence over others.  It refers to the power or influence of a person, group, or idea that is more important or stronger than others. |  例句：The company's dominance in the market is undeniable. 这家公司在市场上的主导地位是不可否认的。

例句：His dominance over his siblings was clear from a young age.  他从小就明显地支配着他的兄弟姐妹。


近义词：predominance, supremacy, ascendancy, hegemony, authority

反义词：subordination, subservience, inferiority


词源:  From Latin *dominantia, from dominans, present participle of dominari "to rule, dominate," from dominus "master, lord."
