pixilates:/ˈpɪksəleɪts/| v.|像素化，使成像素状|To break an image or video into distinct pixels, often resulting in a loss of detail and a blocky appearance.  This usually happens when an image is scaled down or compressed too aggressively.|The old photograph pixilated when I zoomed in.  老照片当我放大时像素化了。
The video pixilated during the fast-paced action sequence.  在快节奏的动作场景中，视频出现了像素化。|近义词：pixelate, rasterize|反义词：sharpen, enhance|动词
