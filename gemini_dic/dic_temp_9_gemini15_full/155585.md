philopolemical:/ˌfɪləʊpəˈlɛmɪkəl/| adj.|好战的；好辩的；喜欢争论的 |  Relating to or characterized by a love of argument or controversy.  It combines "philo-" (love) and "polemic" (controversy, argument).  The word suggests a fondness for engaging in debate and argument, often for the sake of intellectual sparring rather than necessarily to achieve a specific outcome. |The philopolemical professor delighted in lengthy debates with his students.  教授热衷于和学生进行冗长的辩论。|近义词：argumentative, contentious, disputatious |反义词：pacific, conciliatory, peaceable
