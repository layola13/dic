effection:/ɪˈfɛkʃən/| n.|喜爱，爱慕；感情；效力|A feeling of fondness or liking; affection is a positive feeling of liking someone or something.  It often implies a tender or warm feeling, rather than passionate love.|例句：She showed great affection for her grandchildren. 她对她的孙子孙女表现出极大的喜爱。

例句：The children felt a deep affection for their teacher.孩子们对他们的老师怀有深厚的感情。


近义词：fondness, love, attachment, liking, tenderness|反义词：hate, dislike, aversion, animosity, indifference|词性：名词


词源:  From Old French *afeccion, from Latin affectio "state of mind, feeling, disposition, passion," from afficere "to affect, influence," from ad- "to" + facere "to do, make."  The word's evolution reflects the idea that affection is a state of being affected or influenced by positive feelings towards someone or something.
