clarino:/kləˈriːnoʊ/| n.|小号；亮色皮革|A clarino is a type of small trumpet, often used in historical performance.  It can also refer to a type of bright-colored leather, especially used for bookbinding.|例句：The orchestra used a clarino for the high notes. 乐队用小号演奏高音。 The book was bound in a beautiful clarino leather. 这本书是用漂亮的亮色皮革装订的。|近义词：trumpet (for the instrument), calfskin (for the leather)|反义词：bassoon (for the instrument), dull leather (for the leather)|名词
