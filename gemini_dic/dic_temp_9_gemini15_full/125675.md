munches:/mʌntʃɪz/| v.| 嚼，咀嚼 | To chew (food) noisily.  |例句：He munched on an apple. 他大口地嚼着苹果。

例句：The children munched happily on their snacks. 孩子们津津有味地吃着零食。


近义词：chew, gnaw, crunch | 反义词：sip, swallow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He munches his food slowly. = 他慢慢地嚼着食物。
She munches on carrots every afternoon. = 她每天下午都嚼胡萝卜。


一般过去时:
He munched his way through a whole bag of chips. = 他吃掉了一整袋薯片。
The horse munched contentedly on the hay. = 马儿心满意足地啃着干草。


现在完成时:
He has munched his way through half the cake. = 他已经吃掉了半块蛋糕。
She has munched all the cookies. = 她把所有的饼干都吃掉了。


过去完成时:
He had munched all the popcorn before the movie started. = 电影开始前他已经把爆米花都吃掉了。
They had munched on snacks all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在吃零食。


将来时:
He will munch on some nuts while he watches TV. = 他看电视的时候会吃一些坚果。
They will munch their way through the picnic basket. = 他们会把野餐篮里的东西都吃光。


虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would munch on an apple. = 如果我饿了，我会吃个苹果。
I wish I could munch on something sweet right now. = 我真希望现在能吃点甜的东西。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The apples were munched on by the children. = 苹果被孩子们吃掉了。
The carrots were munched by the rabbit. = 胡萝卜被兔子吃掉了。


疑问句:
Is he munching on something? = 他在吃东西吗？
What are you munching on? = 你在吃什么？


否定句:
He doesn't munch loudly. = 他咀嚼食物时声音不大。
She didn't munch on anything at the party. = 她在派对上什么也没吃。


PHRASE->
munch on = 大口吃
munch through = 吃光
munch away = 持续地吃
munch happily = 快乐地咀嚼
munch quietly = 安静地咀嚼
