what:/wɒt/|ˈhwɑːt/| pron.|什么；多么；怎么|An interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something.  It can refer to things, actions, qualities, reasons, or situations.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What time is it? 现在几点？


近义词：which (用于选择), how (用于方式), why (用于原因)|反义词：nothing, none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么事？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到恶心之前吃了什么？
What had they already discussed? = 他们之前已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会怎样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望我知道该做什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built there? = 那里正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What is the problem? = 问题是什么？
What color is your car? = 你的车是什么颜色的？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
I didn't see what happened. = 我没看到发生了什么事。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about…? = ……怎么样？
What if…? = 如果……怎么办？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
