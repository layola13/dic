correlating:/ˈkɔːrəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |使相关；使相互关联 | Relating or connecting two or more things.  The study correlated smoking with lung cancer.  This verb describes the action of establishing a relationship between things. It implies a mutual influence or connection. |例句：Researchers are correlating data from different studies to draw broader conclusions. 研究人员正在将来自不同研究的数据关联起来，以得出更广泛的结论。

例句：The increase in temperature correlated with a rise in sea levels.气温升高与海平面上升相关。


近义词：connecting, associating, linking, relating | 反义词：disassociating, separating, isolating | 词性：动词
