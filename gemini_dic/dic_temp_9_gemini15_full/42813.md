courageous:/kəˈreɪdʒəs/| adj. |勇敢的，无畏的 | possessing or characterized by courage; brave.  The courageous firefighter rescued the child from the burning building.  |近义词：brave, valiant, bold, intrepid |反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is courageous in the face of danger. = 他面对危险时很勇敢。
She is a courageous leader. = 她是一位勇敢的领导者。


一般过去时:
The soldiers were courageous in battle. = 士兵们在战斗中表现得很勇敢。
He acted courageously during the crisis. = 在危机期间，他表现得很勇敢。


现在完成时:
She has been courageous throughout her illness. = 她在她生病的整个过程中都表现得很勇敢。
They have shown courageous resilience in the face of adversity. = 面对逆境，他们展现了勇敢的韧性。


过去完成时:
He had been courageous in his fight against injustice. = 他在反对不公正的斗争中一直很勇敢。
They had courageously defended their beliefs. = 他们勇敢地捍卫了自己的信仰。


将来时:
She will be courageous in the face of future challenges. = 她将勇敢地面对未来的挑战。
He will courageously pursue his goals. = 他将勇敢地追求他的目标。


虚拟语气:
If I were courageous, I would speak my mind. = 如果我勇敢的话，我会说出我的想法。
I wish I had been more courageous in that situation. = 我希望我在那种情况下更勇敢一些。


被动语态:
His courage was admired by everyone. = 他的勇气受到所有人的钦佩。(注意：courageous本身不能用被动语态)
Her bravery was considered courageous. = 她的勇敢被认为是勇敢的。(此处用bravery代替courageous，方便使用被动语态)


疑问句:
Is he courageous enough to take the risk? = 他有足够的勇气冒险吗？
Are they courageous enough to face the truth? = 他们有足够的勇气面对真相吗？


否定句:
He was not courageous enough to confess his mistake. = 他没有足够的勇气承认自己的错误。
She wasn't courageous enough to stand up to the bully. = 她不够勇敢，无法对抗那个恶霸。



PHRASE->
a courageous act 一个勇敢的行为
a courageous decision 一个勇敢的决定
a courageous spirit 一种勇敢的精神
courageous defense 勇敢的防御
courageous resistance 勇敢的抵抗
