misvalued:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuːd/| adj.| 估值过低的；被低估的 |  Incorrectly or unfairly assessed in value.  Something that is misvalued is considered to be worth less than it actually is, often due to a lack of understanding or a biased assessment. | Example: The company's stock is currently misvalued by the market.  这家公司的股票目前被市场低估了。  |近义词：undervalued, underpriced, underestimated |反义词：overvalued, overpriced, overestimated |


USAGE->

The market misvalues this stock. = 市场低估了这只股票。
This painting is misvalued. = 这幅画被低估了。
