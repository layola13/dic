re-indexed:/ˌriːˈɪndɛkst/ | v. |重新编制索引 | To re-index means to create a new index for something, often a database or a file system, after it has been modified or updated. This process involves rebuilding the index to reflect the current state of the data.  | Example: The database was re-indexed after the recent software update.  数据库在最近的软件更新后重新编制了索引。

The website's search functionality improved significantly after the content was re-indexed. 网站内容重新编制索引后，搜索功能得到了显著改善。|近义词:reindex,rebuild index |反义词: delete index, remove index |动词
