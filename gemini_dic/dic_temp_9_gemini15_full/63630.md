They are often found in arid and semi-arid regions.|例句：The vast expanse of the desert was characterized by dramatic eolians, sculpted by centuries of wind erosion.  这片广袤的沙漠以经受了数百年风蚀侵蚀而形成的壮观的风成地貌为特征。|近义词：wind-formed deposits, wind-blown sediments|反义词：aquatic deposits, glacial deposits|名词
