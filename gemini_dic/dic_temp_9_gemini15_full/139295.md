obliviousness: /əˈblɪviəsnəs/ | n. | 疏忽；健忘；不注意；茫然 | The state of being unaware or unmindful; forgetfulness.  It often implies a lack of awareness of something significant or important. |


例句：

Her obliviousness to the danger was alarming.  她的对危险的漠不关心令人担忧。

His obliviousness to his surroundings made him easy to pickpocket. 他对周围环境的漠不关心使他很容易被扒窃。


近义词： unawareness, inattention, heedlessness, unconcern, forgetfulness


反义词： awareness, mindfulness, attentiveness, heedfulness, concern


词性： 名词
