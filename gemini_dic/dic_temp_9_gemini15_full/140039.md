oenology:/ˌiːnɒˈlɒdʒi/| n. |酿酒学；葡萄酒学|The scientific study of wine and winemaking, encompassing all aspects from grape growing to wine tasting and appreciation.  |例句：He studied oenology at university and now works as a winemaker. 他在大学学习酿酒学，现在是一名酿酒师。|例句：Her passion for oenology led her to open her own wine bar. 她对酿酒学的热情促使她开了自己的酒吧。|近义词：viniculture, viticulture (referring more to grape growing)|反义词：无|名词
