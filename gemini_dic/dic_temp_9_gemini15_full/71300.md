fixture:/ˈfɪkstʃər/| n.|固定装置；固定物；定期比赛；成员|A fixture is something that is fixed in place, or a person or thing that is a permanent or regular part of something.|The bathroom fixtures are all new. 浴室的固定装置都是新的。The local derby is a fixture in the football calendar. 当地德比是足球赛历中的定期比赛。|fitting, installation, component|temporary, movable


USAGE->

This fixture is a permanent part of the building. = 这个固定装置是建筑物的永久组成部分。
