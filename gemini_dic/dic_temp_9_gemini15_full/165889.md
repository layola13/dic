preoperator:/ˌpriːˈɑːpəreɪtər/| n. |术前医生，术前准备者|A preoperator is a healthcare professional who performs tasks related to preparing a patient for surgery.  This might include tasks such as reviewing the patient's medical history, ensuring the patient is properly anesthetized, or preparing the surgical site.|例句：The preoperator meticulously checked the patient's chart before surgery. 术前医生在手术前仔细检查了病人的病历。
The preoperator prepared the operating room and the patient for the procedure. 术前准备者为手术室和病人准备了手术。|近义词：surgical technician, operating room nurse, anesthesiologist (in some aspects)|反义词：postoperator|词性 n.
