competency:/ˈkɑːmpɪtənsi/ /ˈkɒmpɪtənsi/ | n. |能力；胜任；权限 |The ability to do something successfully or efficiently.  It often refers to a skill or set of skills that enable someone to perform a particular task or job effectively.  Competency can also refer to the authority or power to act in a certain way. |例句：He demonstrated exceptional competency in handling the crisis. 他在处理危机方面表现出非凡的能力。

The company values competency and experience in its employees.  这家公司重视员工的能力和经验。


近义词：ability, capability, skill, proficiency, expertise, aptitude |反义词：incompetence, incapability, inability
