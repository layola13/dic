morrowless: /ˈmɔroʊləs/ | adj. | 无明日的；没有未来的；绝望的 |  Lacking a tomorrow; hopeless; despairing. The word suggests a state of hopelessness and despair, implying a lack of expectation for the future. It evokes a sense of impending doom or finality.  | Example:  The bleak landscape felt morrowless, devoid of any hope for a brighter future.  = 荒凉的景色让人感觉毫无希望，没有未来。 The morrowless feeling overwhelmed him after receiving the terrible news. =  收到噩耗后，他感到绝望。 | 近义词: hopeless, despairing, bleak, grim, desolate | 反义词: hopeful, optimistic, promising, bright
