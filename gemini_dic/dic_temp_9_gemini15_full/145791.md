overdebilitated:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪbɪlɪteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 极度虚弱的，衰弱的 | Extremely weakened or debilitated; excessively weakened.  The word suggests a state beyond simple weakness, implying a significant and potentially dangerous decline in physical or mental strength. | Example: After weeks of illness, he was overdebilitated and required extensive medical care.  =  几周的疾病后，他虚弱不堪，需要大量的医疗护理。 | 近义词：extremely weak, severely weakened, prostrated, enfeebled | 反义词：strong, robust, vigorous, healthy
