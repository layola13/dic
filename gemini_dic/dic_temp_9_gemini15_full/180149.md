reforge:/ˌriːˈfɔːrdʒ/| vt.|重铸；改造；重建|To shape or create something again by heating and hammering, or to fundamentally change something.|The blacksmith reforged the broken sword.  铁匠重铸了那把断剑。|They decided to reforge their business strategy. 他们决定重塑他们的商业战略。|reform, reconstruct, remake|destroy, ruin|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He reforges old swords. = 他重铸旧剑。
She reforges her ideas regularly. = 她定期重塑自己的想法。

一般过去时:
The artist reforged the sculpture. = 艺术家重铸了雕塑。
They reforged their alliance after the conflict. = 冲突后他们重建了联盟。

现在完成时:
He has reforged his career after the scandal. = 丑闻后他重塑了自己的职业生涯。
She has reforged her identity since moving to a new country. = 自从搬到新的国家后，她已经重塑了自己的身份。

过去完成时:
They had reforged their relationship before the wedding. = 他们婚前已经修复了彼此的关系。
The company had reforged its image before the launch of the new product. = 公司在新产品发布前已经重塑了自身形象。

将来时:
We will reforge the broken bridge. = 我们将重建那座断桥。
The government will reforge its economic policies next year. = 政府明年将重塑其经济政策。

虚拟语气:
If I had the skills, I would reforge the ancient artifact. = 如果我有这技术，我会重铸这件古代文物。
I wish I could reforge my past mistakes. = 我希望我能弥补我过去的错误。

被动语态:
The old tools were reforged by the skilled craftsman. = 这些旧工具由技艺精湛的工匠重铸。
The company's image is being reforged through a new marketing campaign. = 公司的形象正在通过新的营销活动得到重塑。

疑问句:
Will they reforge the contract? = 他们会重签合同吗？
Can the broken vase be reforged? = 破碎的花瓶可以重铸吗？

否定句:
He did not reforge the damaged engine. = 他没有重修损坏的引擎。
They could not reforge their broken trust. = 他们无法修复他们破碎的信任。
