psyche:/ˈsaɪkiː/| n. |心灵；精神；灵魂；蝴蝶|The psyche is the human soul, mind, or spirit.  It is often used to refer to the deeper, unconscious aspects of the mind.  |例句：Understanding the human psyche is a complex undertaking.  理解人类心灵是一项复杂的工作。

例句：Exploring the psyche of a criminal can help in understanding their motives.  探索罪犯的心理会帮助理解他们的动机。


近义词：mind, soul, spirit, ego, self


反义词：body (in the sense of physical body as opposed to the mind)


词性：名词
