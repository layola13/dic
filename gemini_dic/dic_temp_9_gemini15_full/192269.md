screeners:/ˈskriːnərz/| n.|筛选者；审查员；预审员|People or systems that screen or examine something, often to filter out undesirable elements.  For example, this could refer to people who pre-screen applicants for a job, or a software program that filters emails for spam.|例句：The airport screeners checked all passengers' luggage carefully. 机场安检人员仔细检查了所有乘客的行李。

The movie screeners gave the film a positive review before its release. 电影预审员在电影发行前给予了正面评价。|近义词：inspectors, reviewers, examiners|反义词：approvers (in some contexts)|名词
