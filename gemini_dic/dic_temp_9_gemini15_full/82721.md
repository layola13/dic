guidebooks:/ˈɡaɪdbʊks/ | n. |指南，旅行指南 |Books providing information and advice for travelers or visitors to a particular place. They often include maps, itineraries, and practical tips. | Example:  I bought a few guidebooks before my trip to Italy. 我在去意大利旅行之前买了几本指南。  I consulted several guidebooks to plan my hiking route. 我查阅了几本指南来规划我的徒步路线。|近义词：travel guides, handbooks, manuals |反义词：None
