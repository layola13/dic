rebathe:/riːˈbeɪð/ | vt. | 再洗一次；重新沐浴 | To bathe again; to bathe a second time.  |例句：I decided to rebathe the baby after he rolled around in the dirt. 我决定孩子在泥地里滚了一圈后给他再洗一次澡。

例句：After a long and sweaty hike, I rebathed to wash away the grime. 长途跋涉后，我重新洗了个澡，洗去身上的污垢。


近义词：rewash, rinse again, wash again | 反义词：dry, dehydrate
