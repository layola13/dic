monopersonal:/ˌmɒnəʊˈpɜːsənl/|/ˌmɑːnoʊˈpɜːrsənəl/| adj.| 单个人的；涉及一个人的；单人的 | Relating to or involving only one person.  It often refers to grammatical structures where the subject and object are the same person. |  The monopersonal verb "repent" requires only one participant.  这个单人称动词“悔过”只需要一个参与者。


The sentence "She hurt herself" demonstrates a monopersonal action.  句子“她伤了自己”展示了一个单人行为。


近义词：single-person, solitary, individual


反义词：plural, multipersonal, interpersonal

词性:形容词
