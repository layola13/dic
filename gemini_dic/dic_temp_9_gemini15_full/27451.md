camouflages:/ˈkæməflɑːʒɪz/ | n. |伪装；掩饰 |The art or practice of concealing the presence of something by means of disguise, camouflage, or other means.  Camouflage is used to make something blend in with its surroundings.  |例句：The army uses camouflage to hide its tanks from enemy aircraft. = 军队使用伪装来将坦克隐藏在敌机视野之外。

The chameleon's camouflage helped it survive in the jungle. = 变色龙的伪装帮助它在丛林中生存。|近义词：disguise, concealment, masking |反义词：exposure, revelation |
