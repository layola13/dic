homophone:/ˈhɒməfoʊn/ /ˈhɑːməfoʊn/| n. |同音词 |A word that is pronounced the same as another word but differs in meaning, and may differ in spelling.| 例句：  "Their" and "there" are homophones.  “Their”和“there”是同音词。


例句： "to", "too", and "two" are homophones. “to”, “too”和“two”是同音词。


近义词：同义词(如果指意义相近的同音词)


反义词：无


词性:名词


词源:  From Greek *homo- ("same") + -phone ("sound").  The word was coined in the 19th century.
