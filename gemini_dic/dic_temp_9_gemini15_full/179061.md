redenied:/ˌriːˈdeɪnd/| v.|再次拒绝；再次驳回|To deny something again; to reject something a second time.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") to the verb "denied".|The bank redenied his loan application. = 银行再次拒绝了他的贷款申请。
The judge redenied the defendant's request for a new trial. = 法官再次驳回了被告人关于重新审判的要求。|refuse, reject, turn down|accept, approve|动词
