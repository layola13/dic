replanted:/ˌriˈplæntɪd/| v. |重新种植；再植|To plant (something) again after it has been removed or destroyed.  This word is the past participle of the verb "replant".|例句：The farmer replanted the damaged crops after the storm.  农民在暴风雨后重新种植了受损的庄稼。

The gardener replanted the flowers in a larger pot. 园丁把花重新栽种在一个更大的花盆里。


近义词：replant, reset, reseed|反义词：uproot, remove, destroy|动词
