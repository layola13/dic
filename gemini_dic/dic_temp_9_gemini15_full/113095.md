luncheon:/ˈlʌntʃən/| n. |午餐，便餐|A light meal, typically eaten in the middle of the day.|例句：We had a luncheon meeting at the club. 我们在俱乐部举行了一次午餐会。

例句：The luncheon was followed by a short business meeting. 午餐后举行了一次简短的商务会议。

近义词：lunch, midday meal|反义词：dinner, supper|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a luncheon every day. = 他每天都吃午餐。
They often have a business luncheon. = 他们经常举行商务午餐。


一般过去时:
We had a delicious luncheon yesterday. = 昨天我们吃了一顿美味的午餐。
She attended a luncheon in honor of the mayor. = 她参加了一个为市长举行的午餐会。


现在完成时:
I have already had my luncheon. = 我已经吃过午餐了。
They have had several luncheons this week. = 他们本周已经参加了好几次午餐会。


过去完成时:
They had finished their luncheon before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经吃完了午餐。
She had never attended such a lavish luncheon before. = 她以前从未参加过如此奢华的午餐会。


将来时:

They will be having a luncheon at the restaurant. = 他们将在餐厅吃午餐。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have a longer luncheon. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会吃更长时间的午餐。
