headstone:/ˈhɛdstoʊn/ | n. |墓碑|A headstone is a stone that is placed at the head of a grave, usually inscribed with the deceased's name and dates of birth and death.  It often includes other details about the person's life.|Example:  The headstone was weathered and worn, making some of the inscription illegible.  墓碑风化严重，一些碑文已无法辨认。|近义词：gravestone, tombstone|反义词：无
