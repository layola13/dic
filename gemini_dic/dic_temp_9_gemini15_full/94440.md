imploring: /ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 恳求的；哀求的 |  Expressing or characterized by earnest entreaty.  It describes a manner or tone that conveys intense pleading or begging. |


例句：

His imploring gaze pleaded for mercy.  = 他恳求的目光乞求怜悯。

The child's imploring eyes tugged at her heart. =  孩子的哀求眼神牵动着她的心。


近义词：beseeching, pleading, supplicating, entreating

反义词：demanding, commanding, ordering


词性：形容词
