psalms:/sɑːmz/| n.|诗篇；圣诗|The Psalms are a collection of 150 Hebrew poems that are part of the biblical book of Psalms.  They are known for their diverse range of emotions, from praise and thanksgiving to lament and despair.  Many are attributed to King David.|例句：He read a psalm from the Bible. 他读了圣经中的一篇诗篇。|例句：The choir sang beautiful psalms during the service. 唱诗班在仪式上演唱了优美的圣诗。|近义词：hymns, odes, canticles|反义词：prose (as an antonym to poetic form)|名词
