non-chargeable:/ˌnɒnˈtʃɑːrdʒəbl/ | adj. | 不可收费的，免费的 | Not subject to a charge or fee.  | Example: The consultation was non-chargeable.  这次咨询是免费的。 |近义词：free, complimentary, gratis | 反义词：chargeable, payable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The service is non-chargeable. = 这项服务是免费的。
This item is non-chargeable for members. = 此商品会员免费。


一般过去时:
The repairs were non-chargeable under the warranty. = 保修期内维修是免费的。
That service was non-chargeable last year. = 去年这项服务是免费的。


现在完成时:
The company has made this service non-chargeable. = 公司已将这项服务免费。
All consultations have been non-chargeable this month. = 本月所有咨询都是免费的。


过去完成时:
The repairs had been non-chargeable before the policy changed. = 在政策改变之前，维修是免费的。
The service had been non-chargeable until recently. = 直到最近这项服务都是免费的。


将来时:
The new software will be non-chargeable for the first year. = 新软件第一年将免费。
The service will be non-chargeable for students. = 此项服务对学生免费。


虚拟语气:
If the service were non-chargeable, more people would use it. = 如果这项服务是免费的，更多的人会使用它。
I wish the consultation were non-chargeable. = 我希望这次咨询是免费的。


被动语态:
The service is considered non-chargeable by many. = 这项服务被许多人认为是免费的。
The fee was declared non-chargeable after the investigation. = 调查后，费用被宣布为免费的。


疑问句:
Is this service non-chargeable? = 这项服务是免费的吗？
Are these items non-chargeable? = 这些商品是免费的吗？


否定句:
The service is not non-chargeable; there is a small fee. = 这项服务并非免费；需要支付少量费用。
That item is not non-chargeable; you have to pay for it. = 那件商品不是免费的；你必须付费。
