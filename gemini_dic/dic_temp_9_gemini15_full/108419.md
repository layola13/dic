lawns:/lɔːnz/| n.|草坪，草地|An area of short, mown grass, especially in a garden or park.|例句：The children were playing on the lawns.孩子们在草坪上玩耍。|近义词：grass, turf, sward|反义词：pavement, concrete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mows the lawns every Saturday. = 他每周六都修剪草坪。
The lawns need watering. = 草坪需要浇水。


一般过去时:
They watered the lawns yesterday. = 他们昨天浇了草坪。
We played croquet on the lawns last summer. = 去年夏天我们在草坪上玩槌球。


现在完成时:
They have just finished mowing the lawns. = 他们刚刚修剪完草坪。
We have had a picnic on the lawns several times this year. = 今年我们已经在草坪上野餐好几次了。


过去完成时:
The lawns had been watered before the party started. = 派对开始前草坪已经浇过水了。
They had already mowed the lawns when the rain began. = 下雨的时候他们已经修剪好草坪了。


将来时:
I will mow the lawns tomorrow. = 我明天会修剪草坪。
They will water the lawns before the guests arrive. = 客人到达之前他们会浇草坪。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would tend the lawns more often. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更经常地打理草坪。
I wish the lawns were greener. = 我希望草坪更绿一些。


被动语态:
The lawns are mowed weekly. = 草坪每周都被修剪。
The lawns were damaged by the storm. = 草坪被暴风雨破坏了。


疑问句:
Are the lawns ready for the party? = 草坪准备好派对了吗？
Whose lawns are those? = 那些是誰家的草坪？


否定句:
They didn't mow the lawns last week. = 他们上周没有修剪草坪。
The lawns weren't watered because of the drought. = 因为干旱，草坪没有浇水。
