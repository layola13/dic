distill: /dɪˈstɪl/ | vt. & n. | 蒸馏；提炼；浓缩；使…精炼 | to purify a liquid by a process of heating and cooling; to extract the essential elements of something | 近义词：refine, purify, concentrate | 反义词：dilute, mix


英文解析:
As a verb, "distill" means to purify a liquid by heating it until it becomes a vapor, then cooling the vapor to condense it into a liquid. This process removes impurities.  It can also mean to extract the essential elements or meaning from something complex, leaving behind the less important parts.  This metaphorical use is common in contexts like literature, ideas, or experiences.

As a noun, "distillate" refers to the purified liquid produced by distillation.


例句:
They distilled the alcohol to make it stronger. = 他们蒸馏酒精使其更浓。

The writer distilled years of experience into a single, powerful novel. = 作家将多年的经验浓缩成一部有力的长篇小说。
