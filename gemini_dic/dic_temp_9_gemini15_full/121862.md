misreprinted:/ˌmɪsˈprɪntɪd/| adj.|印刷错误的；误印的 |Printed incorrectly.  The information was misreprinted in the newspaper, leading to confusion among readers. |近义词：erroneously printed, incorrectly printed, misprinted |反义词：correctly printed, accurately printed


例句:

The book was misreprinted, with several pages missing. 这本书印刷错误，缺少几页。

The flyer was misreprinted with the wrong date. 传单印刷错误，日期不对。


词源:

mis- (错误地) + reprinted (再印刷的)  "reprinted" itself comes from "re-" (again) + "printed" (printed).


用法说明:

misreprinted是一个形容词，用来描述印刷错误的东西。它通常用来形容印刷品，如书籍、杂志、传单等。  它强调的是印刷过程中出现的错误，而不是内容本身的错误。


由于misreprinted 是一个形容词，它本身不具有动词的各种时态变化。  它通常用来修饰名词，例如：a misreprinted book (一本印刷错误的书)，a misreprinted article (一篇印刷错误的文章)。
