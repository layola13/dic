disinterest:/ˈdɪsɪntrəst/ | n., adj. | 不感兴趣；冷漠；公正 | Lack of interest; impartiality.  | The judge showed complete disinterest in the case.  法官对这个案子表现出完全的漠不关心。|  apathy, indifference | interest, concern | 名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His disinterest in politics is evident. = 他对政治的不感兴趣是显而易见的。
Their disinterest in the project is concerning. = 他们对这个项目的不感兴趣令人担忧。


一般过去时:
Her disinterest in the offer was surprising. = 她对这个提议的不感兴趣令人惊讶。
His disinterest in the meeting was noted by his boss. = 他的老板注意到了他对会议的不感兴趣。


现在完成时:
My disinterest in sports has grown over the years. = 我对体育运动的兴趣随着时间的推移而减弱了。
Their disinterest in the charity event has been disappointing. = 他们对慈善活动的漠不关心令人失望。


过去完成时:
His disinterest in the company had already been apparent before the merger. = 在合并之前，他对公司的漠不关心就已经很明显了。
Her disinterest in the subject had led to her poor grades. = 她对这个科目的不感兴趣导致了她成绩差。


将来时:
His disinterest in the future of the company is a serious problem. = 他对公司未来的漠不关心是一个严重的问题。
Their disinterest in the upcoming election is worrying. = 他们对即将到来的选举漠不关心令人担忧。


虚拟语气:
If only there were less disinterest in environmental issues. = 但愿对环境问题的漠不关心少一些。
I wish there was less disinterest among young people in reading. = 我希望年轻人对阅读的漠不关心少一些。


被动语态:
The proposal was met with disinterest. = 这个提议受到了冷遇。
His disinterest in the matter was discussed by the committee. = 委员会讨论了他对这件事的漠不关心。


疑问句:
Is there any disinterest in the new policy? = 对这项新政策有什么不感兴趣吗？
Was his disinterest in the project genuine? = 他对这个项目的不感兴趣是真的吗？


否定句:
There was no disinterest shown in the presentation. = 演示并没有受到冷遇。
Her interest in the subject, not disinterest, was evident. = 她对这个主题的兴趣，而不是不感兴趣，是显而易见的。
