prod's:/prɒdz/| n.|产品，制品|Products are goods or services that are offered for sale.|例句：The company launched its new line of products last week.  这家公司上周推出了其新的产品线。|近义词：goods, merchandise, wares|反义词：services (in some contexts)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces many products. = 工厂生产很多产品。
This company sells various products. = 这家公司销售各种产品。

一般过去时:
They produced a new car model last year. = 他们去年生产了一款新车型。
We purchased several products online yesterday. = 我们昨天在网上购买了几种产品。

现在完成时:
They have produced over 10,000 units this year. = 他们今年已经生产了一万多件产品。
We have purchased all the necessary products for the project. = 我们已经购买了项目所需的所有产品。

过去完成时:
They had produced the prototype before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经生产出了原型。
We had already ordered all the products before the store closed. = 商店关门之前我们已经订购了所有产品。

将来时:
They will produce more sustainable products in the future. = 他们将来会生产更多可持续的产品。
We will buy the products next week. = 我们下周会购买这些产品。

虚拟语气:
If they produced higher quality products, they would sell more. = 如果他们生产更高质量的产品，他们就能卖出更多。
I wish they had produced a better product. = 我希望他们能生产出更好的产品。

被动语态:
Many products are manufactured in China. = 许多产品都在中国制造。
The new product is being tested now. = 新产品正在测试中。

疑问句:
What products does the company offer? = 公司提供什么产品？
Which products are most popular? = 哪些产品最受欢迎？

否定句:
The company doesn't produce that product anymore. = 公司不再生产那种产品了。
We didn't buy any products today. = 我们今天没有买任何产品。
