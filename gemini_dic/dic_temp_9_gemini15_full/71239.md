fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/| n./adj./v.|适合的；安装；配件|Suitable, appropriate; the act of installing or adjusting; a component part.|例句：The dress is a perfect fitting. 这件衣服非常合身。|近义词：suitable, appropriate, proper, adjustment, installation|反义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, improper

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new carpet fits the room perfectly. = 新地毯非常适合这个房间。
This job fits my skills and experience. = 这份工作符合我的技能和经验。


一般过去时:
The mechanic fitted the new tire quickly. = 技工很快就装上了新轮胎。
The tailor fitted the suit to his measurements. = 裁缝根据他的尺寸修改了西装。


现在完成时:
They have fitted a new lock on the door. = 他们已经给门装上了新锁。
We have fitted all the new kitchen cabinets. = 我们已经安装好了所有新的厨房橱柜。


过去完成时:
He had fitted the pipes before the plumber arrived. = 水管工到达之前，他已经安装好了管道。
She had fitted the dress before the party started. = 派对开始之前，她已经试穿了那件衣服。


将来时:
They will fit the windows tomorrow. = 他们明天将安装窗户。
We will fit the new engine next week. = 我们下周将安装新的发动机。


虚拟语气:
If the shoes fitted better, I would buy them. = 如果鞋子更合脚，我会买下它们。
I wish this dress fitted me perfectly. = 我希望这件衣服能完美地适合我。


被动语态:
The new carpet was fitted yesterday. = 新地毯昨天安装好了。
The engine is being fitted at the moment. = 发动机正在安装中。


疑问句:
Does this shirt fit me? = 这件衬衫适合我吗？
Does the part fit correctly? = 这个零件安装正确吗？


否定句:
The shoes don't fit properly. = 这双鞋不太合脚。
The new component didn't fit into the machine. = 新组件无法安装到机器上。


PHRASE->
a fitting tribute:  恰当的敬意
a fitting end:  合适的结局
a fitting occasion: 合适的场合
a perfect fit:  完美的契合
a poor fit:  不太合适


词源：
源自古英语 *fittan，意思是“适合，装备”。  其动词和形容词用法都源于此词根。 名词用法是后来发展出来的，指安装好的部件或适合的物品。
