hitless:/ˈhɪtləs/| adj. |未击中的；无成绩的；失败的 |Without any hits or successes.  Describes a situation or performance lacking in success or impact. |例句：The baseball team had a hitless game. =  棒球队一场比赛都没打出安打。

The boxer delivered a hitless performance, failing to land a single punch. = 拳击手表现糟糕，一拳都没打中。

|近义词：unsuccessful, fruitless, unproductive, barren |反义词：successful, fruitful, productive
