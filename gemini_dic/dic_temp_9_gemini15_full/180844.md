rehearsable:/rɪˈhɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可排练的，可预演的 | Capable of being rehearsed; suitable for rehearsal.  This word describes something that can be practiced or prepared for a performance or presentation. | Example: The play is fully rehearseable, given enough time and resources. 这出戏是可以排练的，只要有足够的时间和资源。  The new dance routine is highly rehearseable and easy to learn. 新的舞蹈套路很容易排练和学习。 | 近义词：rehearsable, practicable, feasible | 反义词：unprepared, impossible
