ricins: /ˈrɪsɪnz/ | n. |蓖麻毒素|Ricin is a highly toxic protein found in the seeds of the castor bean plant.  It is a potent toxin that can cause severe illness and death if ingested, inhaled, or injected.  The symptoms of ricin poisoning can include nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, internal bleeding, and ultimately, organ failure.  There is no specific antidote for ricin poisoning, and treatment focuses on supportive care. | Example:  The terrorist plot involved using ricin to poison the water supply. = 恐怖分子的阴谋涉及使用蓖麻毒素来毒害供水系统。


The accidental ingestion of even a small amount of ricin can be lethal. = 即使少量意外摄入蓖麻毒素也可能是致命的。


近义词: toxin, poison, venom | 反义词: antidote (in terms of effect)
