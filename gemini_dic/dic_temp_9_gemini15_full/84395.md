hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːrk/| n. v.|标志，特征；鉴定；盖上印记|A hallmark of a good teacher is patience.  |例句：The company's products are known for their high quality and attention to detail, which are hallmarks of their brand.  该公司的产品以其高质量和对细节的关注而闻名，这些都是其品牌的标志。|近义词：characteristic, trait, sign, mark|反义词：defect, flaw


hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːrk/| v.|给…盖上印记；成为…的标志|例句：The gold was hallmarked to verify its purity.  黄金被盖上印记以验证其纯度。|近义词：stamp, brand, characterize, distinguish|反义词：None

This building's architecture hallmarks the era. = 这座建筑的风格是那个时代的标志。
His kindness hallmarks his personality. = 他的善良是其性格的标志。


一般过去时:

This invention will surely hallmark a new age of technology. = 这项发明无疑将标志着新时代的科技。


虚拟语气:
