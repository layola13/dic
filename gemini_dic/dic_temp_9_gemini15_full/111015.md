lirella:/lɪˈrɛlə/| n. |细长条纹；裂缝| A lirella is a long, narrow, often linear or slightly curved, and usually dark-colored slit or fissure, especially one found on the surface of a fungus or lichen.  It is typically filled with spores. |Example: The lichen showed a distinct lirella running along its thallus.  这种地衣在其叶状体上显示出一条明显的细长条纹。|近义词：fissure, crack, slit |反义词：无
