saprophyte:/ˈsæprəfaɪt/| n.|腐生生物|An organism, especially a fungus or bacterium, that feeds on dead organic matter.|例句：Many fungi are saprophytes, playing a vital role in decomposition. 许多真菌是腐生生物，在分解过程中起着至关重要的作用。


例句：Saprophytes are essential for recycling nutrients in the ecosystem. 腐生生物对于生态系统中养分的循环至关重要。


近义词：decomposer, saprobe


反义词：parasite, autotroph


词性:名词
