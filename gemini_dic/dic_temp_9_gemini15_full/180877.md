rehospitalizations: /ˌriːˌhɒspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 再住院，二次住院 | The act or instance of being hospitalized again after a previous discharge.  It often refers to a patient readmitted to the hospital within a specific timeframe after an initial discharge, usually for the same or a related condition.  |例句：The high rate of rehospitalizations among heart failure patients is a major concern.  多次心力衰竭患者再住院率高是一个主要问题。

The study investigated the factors contributing to 30-day rehospitalizations following hip replacement surgery. 这项研究调查了髋关节置换术后30天内再住院的 contributing factors。


近义词：readmissions, repeat hospitalizations | 反义词：initial hospitalization, first admission

词性：名词
