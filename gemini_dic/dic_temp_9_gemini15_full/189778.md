salutary:/ˈsæljuˌtɛri/| adj. |有益健康的；有益的；有益的；使人健康的|  Beneficial to health, well-being, or morals; wholesome.  The word implies a positive effect that promotes improvement or restoration. |

例句：The doctor prescribed a salutary regime of exercise and diet.  医生开了有益健康的运动和饮食方案。

例句：The crisis served as a salutary lesson in the importance of preparedness.  这场危机为我们上了一堂宝贵的关于做好准备的重要性课程。


近义词：beneficial, advantageous, wholesome, healthful, helpful, therapeutic

反义词：detrimental, harmful, deleterious, pernicious


词源：源自拉丁语 *salus, salutis ("health, safety, well-being")，与其相关的词根salv-也出现在诸如salvation, salvage等词中。
