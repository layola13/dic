leave:/liːv/| /liːv/ | vt. vi. n.|离开；留下；委托；让；使处于某种状态| to go away from a place; to depart; to allow something to remain; to entrust something to someone; to permit something to remain untouched|例句：I'm leaving for London tomorrow. = 我明天去伦敦。

例句：She left her keys on the table. = 她把钥匙留在桌上了。


近义词：depart, go, quit, forsake, abandon|反义词：arrive, return, stay, remain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I leave for work at 8 a.m. every day. = 我每天早上8点去上班。
He leaves the office early on Fridays. = 他星期五早早下班。

一般过去时:
We left the party early. = 我们早早离开了聚会。
She left her job last month. = 她上个月辞了职。

现在完成时:
I have left my wallet at home. = 我把钱包忘在家里了。
They have left already. = 他们已经离开了。

过去完成时:
I had left the house before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前我已经离开了家。
He had left the company before I joined. = 我加入公司之前他已经离开了。

将来时:
We will leave tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上离开。
She will leave for Paris next week. = 她下周去巴黎。

虚拟语气:
I wish I had left earlier. = 我真希望我早点离开。
If I had known, I would have left sooner. = 如果我知道的话，我会早点离开。

被动语态:
The meeting was left unfinished. = 会议没有完成。
The door was left open. = 门是开着的。

疑问句:
When are you leaving? = 你什么时候离开？
Has he left yet? = 他离开了么？

否定句:
I didn't leave my phone in the car. = 我没有把手机留在车里。
He didn't leave until late. = 他直到很晚才离开。


PHRASE->
leave behind = 留下，遗留
leave out = 省略，遗漏
leave alone = 不管，别碰
leave off = 停止
leave it to me = 交给我吧
