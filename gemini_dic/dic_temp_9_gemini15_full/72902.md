folly:/ˈfɒli/ /ˈfɑːli/| n.|愚蠢；荒唐事；华丽的建筑|Lack of good sense; foolishness; a costly and foolish undertaking; a building or structure, typically one of a decorative or ornamental nature.|The folly of his actions was evident to everyone.  他的行为的愚蠢显而易见。

The construction of that extravagant mansion was a folly. 建造那座奢华的宅邸是一件蠢事。


近义词：silliness, absurdity, foolishness, extravagance|反义词：wisdom, sense, prudence
