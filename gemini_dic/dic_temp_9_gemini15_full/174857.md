raciologist: /ˌreɪʃiˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |种族学家|A raciologist is a scholar who studies race and racism, often from a scientific or sociological perspective.  However, the term is now largely considered outdated and problematic due to its historical association with racist pseudoscience.  The field of study is more appropriately addressed through terms like "racial studies scholar," "sociologist of race," or "anthropologist of race."|例句：While the term "raciologist" was once used, it's now generally avoided due to its problematic history.|近义词：racial studies scholar, sociologist of race, anthropologist of race|反义词：N/A
