demilitarization:/ˌdiːˌmɪlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |非军事化；裁军；解除武装|The process of removing military forces or weapons from a place.  It can refer to a specific area, a country, or even a particular type of weapon.|例句：The demilitarization of the Korean Peninsula has been a long and complex process. 朝鲜半岛的非军事化是一个漫长而复杂的过程。

例句：After the war, the demilitarization of the occupied territories was a priority. 战争结束后，被占领地区的非军事化成为优先事项。


近义词：disarmament, neutralization, demilitarisation (British spelling) |反义词：militarization, armament
