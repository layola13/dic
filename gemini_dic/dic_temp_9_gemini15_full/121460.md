misjudgements:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒmənts/ | n. |误判，错误判断 | Incorrect judgments or assessments.  These can stem from a lack of information, bias, or flawed reasoning. |例句：His misjudgements in the early stages of the project led to significant delays. 他的项目早期误判导致了严重的延误。

例句：The jury's misjudgements resulted in a wrongful conviction. 陪审团的误判导致了错误的定罪。


近义词：errors, mistakes, miscalculations, misinterpretations, blunders

反义词：accurate judgments, correct assessments, sound decisions


词源: mis- (wrongly) + judgment (the ability to make considered decisions or come to sensible conclusions)  The word "judgment" itself originates from Old French "juggement" and ultimately from Latin "judicium".
