pseudoamateurish: /ˌsuːdoʊˌæməˈtɜːrɪʃ/ | adj. | 似是而非的业余的；装模作样的业余的 |  appearing amateurish but intending to give the impression of professionalism or skill.  It suggests a pretense of skill or expertise that is not genuinely present.  The word combines "pseudo" (false, pretended) with "amateurish" (lacking skill or expertise).|

例句：His pseudoamateurish attempt at fixing the car only made things worse.  他的那次装模作样的修车尝试只会让情况更糟。

例句：The painting, while seemingly amateurish, had a certain pseudoamateurish charm that made it appealing.  这幅画虽然看起来业余，但却有一种似是而非的业余魅力，使其颇具吸引力。
