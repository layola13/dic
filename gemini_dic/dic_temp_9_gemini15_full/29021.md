cartography:/kɑːˈtɑːɡrəfi/| n.|地图学，制图学|The science or art of making maps.  It involves the study of the Earth's surface, its features, and the methods used to represent them on maps.|Example: Cartography is a fascinating field that combines art, science, and technology.  地图学是一个迷人的领域，它结合了艺术、科学和技术。|近义词：mapmaking, chartmaking|反义词：N/A
