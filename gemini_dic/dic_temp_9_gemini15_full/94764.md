improvisation:/ˌɪmprəvɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 即兴创作；即兴表演 | The act of improvising; something improvised.  It refers to the creation of something spontaneously, without prior preparation.  This can apply to music, theatre, comedy, or even everyday conversation. | Example:  The jazz musician's improvisation was breathtaking.  这位爵士乐手的即兴演奏令人叹为观止。

The comedian's improvisation kept the audience laughing throughout the show.  这位喜剧演员的即兴表演让观众在整场演出中都笑个不停。|近义词：extemporization, ad-libbing, impromptu |反义词：premeditation, planning, rehearsal |
