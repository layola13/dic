reopened:/ˌriːˈoʊpənd/|/ˈriːəʊpənd/| v. |重新开放|To open something again after it has been closed.  This verb implies a previous closure.|例句：The shop reopened after a week of renovations. 商店经过一周的装修后重新开业了。


The museum reopened to the public last Saturday. 博物馆上周六重新向公众开放。


近义词：reactivate, resume, restart|反义词：close, shut down, suspend|动词
