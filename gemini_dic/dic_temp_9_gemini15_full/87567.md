heterophagi: /ˌhɛtərəˈfædʒi/ | n. |异食癖；异嗜症 | The consumption of non-nutritive substances or materials.  This can range from eating clay (geophagy) to consuming ice (pagophagia) to ingesting substances like paint or paper.  The causes can be varied, including nutritional deficiencies, psychological factors, and even cultural practices. | Example:  Her heterophagia manifested as a craving for chalk.  她的异食癖表现为对粉笔的渴望。  |近义词: pica (a more common synonym) |反义词:  (no direct antonym;  the opposite would be normal eating habits) | 名词
