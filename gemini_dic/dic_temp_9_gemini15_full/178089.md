reclothing:/ˌriːˈkloʊðɪŋ/| n. |再穿衣；重新穿衣；翻新衣物|The act of putting on clothes again, or the process of renewing or refurbishing clothing.  It can also refer to providing new clothing, particularly to those in need.|例句：After the rain, the children needed reclothing. 雨后，孩子们需要重新穿衣服。|例句：The charity focuses on reclothing homeless individuals.  这家慈善机构专注于为无家可归者提供新衣物。|近义词：re-dressing, refitting, re-equipping|反义词：undressing, discarding
