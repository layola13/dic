grommet:/ˈɡrɑːmɪt/ | n. |金属环；护圈；衬套 | A grommet is a small metal or plastic ring used to reinforce a hole in a material, such as fabric or sheet metal, to prevent tearing or wear.  It can also be a similar ring used as a decorative element. |  The grommets prevented the canvas from tearing at the eyelets.  金属环防止帆布在穿孔处撕裂。 We used grommets to reinforce the holes in the tarp. 我们用金属环加固帆布上的孔。| washer, eyelet |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
