gigabit's:/ˈɡɪɡəbɪts/| n. |吉比特(复数所有格)|The possessive form of "gigabit," referring to something belonging to or associated with a gigabit.  It indicates ownership or relationship. For example,  "the gigabit's speed" refers to the speed of a gigabit connection.|The network's gigabit's speed was impressive.  这个网络的吉比特速度令人印象深刻。|megabit's, terabit's|  (无明确反义词，因为是名词所有格)|名词所有格
