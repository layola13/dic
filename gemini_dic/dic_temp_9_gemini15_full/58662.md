dumfounder:/dʌmˈfaʊndər/| vt.| 使惊呆；使目瞪口呆 | To make someone speechless with amazement or shock.  |例句：The magician's trick completely dumbfounded the audience. 魔术师的戏法完全让观众惊呆了。
The unexpected news dumbfounded her.  这个意外的消息让她惊呆了。|近义词：astonish, amaze, bewilder, astound |反义词：enlighten, clarify
