negritude:/ˈneɡrɪtjuːd/| n. |黑人性；黑人意识；黑人文化|The quality or fact of being black; a movement in French-speaking Africa and the Caribbean promoting black pride and cultural identity.  It emphasizes the cultural and historical experiences of black people, often in contrast to colonial or Western influences. |Example:  The Negritude movement celebrated African culture and heritage. 黑人性运动颂扬非洲文化和遗产。|近义词: blackness, black consciousness |反义词: whiteness
