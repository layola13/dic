hermitries:/ˈhɜːrmɪtriz/| n.|隐居地；隐士住所；隐修院|Places of seclusion inhabited by hermits; also, the practice or state of being a hermit.|例句：The hermitries nestled deep within the mountains were rarely visited.  隐居在深山里的隐修院很少有人来访。

例句：Many ancient hermitries have been converted into museums. 许多古老的隐修院已经被改建成博物馆。


近义词：hermitages, retreats, monasteries (in some contexts)|反义词：bustling places, crowded areas
