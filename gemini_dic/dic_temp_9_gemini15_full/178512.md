recontracted:/ˌriːkənˈtræktɪd/| adj.| 重新签订合同的；再次签约的 |  Describes something that has been contracted again, often implying a renewal or a modification of a previous contract.  | Example: The company recontracted with its supplier for a lower price. 公司以较低的价格与供应商重新签订了合同。 | 近义词：renewed, renegotiated | 反义词：terminated, cancelled |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company recontracts with its suppliers annually. = 公司每年都与供应商重新签订合同。
He recontracts his insurance policy every year. = 他每年都重新签订他的保险单。


一般过去时:
They recontracted the project last month. = 他们上个月重新签订了该项目的合同。
The musicians recontracted with a new record label. = 这些音乐家与一家新的唱片公司重新签约了。


现在完成时:
We have recontracted with the same construction firm for the new building. = 我们已经与同一家建筑公司重新签订了新建筑的合同。
She has recontracted her services to the organization for another three years. = 她已经与该组织续签了三年的服务合同。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had recontracted the services of a consultant. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经重新聘用了顾问的服务。
The team had recontracted with the sponsor before the championship game. = 在冠军赛之前，球队已经与赞助商重新签订了合同。


将来时:
They will recontract the lease on their office space next year. = 他们明年将重新签订办公室租赁合同。
The government will recontract the construction of the bridge. = 政府将重新签订桥梁建设合同。


虚拟语气:
If they had recontracted earlier, they would have avoided the penalty. = 如果他们早点重新签订合同，他们就可以避免处罚。
I wish we had recontracted with a more reliable supplier. = 我真希望我们能与更可靠的供应商重新签订合同。


被动语态:
The contract was recontracted under new terms. = 合同已根据新条款重新签订。
The agreement was recontracted after lengthy negotiations. = 在经过长时间的谈判后，协议重新签订了。


疑问句:
Has the company recontracted with its previous vendor? = 公司是否已与之前的供应商重新签订合同？
Will the project be recontracted with a different firm? = 该项目是否会与不同的公司重新签订合同？


否定句:
The company did not recontract with its former supplier. = 公司没有与之前的供应商重新签订合同。
They haven't recontracted the services of the marketing agency. = 他们没有重新聘用营销机构的服务。

recontract a project = 重新签订项目合同
recontract employment = 重新签订雇佣合同
