lectures:/ˈlɛktʃərz/| n.|讲座，演讲|Formal talks or speeches given to an audience, especially for instruction.|The professor gave a series of lectures on ancient history.  教授做了一系列关于古代历史的讲座。|talks, speeches, presentations, addresses|silence, quiet


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He gives lectures on philosophy every week. 他每周都做哲学讲座。
The professor lectures on various aspects of literature. 教授讲授文学的各个方面。


一般过去时:
She lectured on the importance of education yesterday. 她昨天讲授了教育的重要性。
We attended a fascinating lecture last night. 我们昨晚参加了一个精彩的讲座。


现在完成时:
He has lectured at many universities across the country. 他在这个国家的许多大学都做过讲座。
She has lectured extensively on climate change. 她对气候变化做了广泛的演讲。


过去完成时:
