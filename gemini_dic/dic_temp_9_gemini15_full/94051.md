impartible: /ɪmˈpɑːrtɪbl/ | adj. | 不可分割的，不能分享的 | Incapable of being divided or shared.  The word emphasizes the inability to separate or distribute something, often due to its inherent nature or legal restrictions. | Example: The indivisible nature of the atom was once considered impartible, but modern physics has shown this to be false.  原子不可分割的特性一度被认为是不可分割的，但现代物理学已经证明这是错误的。|近义词：indivisible, inseparable, unshareable | 反义词：divisible, shareable
