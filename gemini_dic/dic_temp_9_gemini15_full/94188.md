imperator:/ɪmˈpɛrəˌtɔːr/| n. |皇帝，统治者|The title "Imperator" was a Roman military title originally signifying supreme military command, later evolving to denote the emperor's supreme authority.  It emphasized the emperor's role as supreme commander of the Roman legions and his ultimate power.|例句：Julius Caesar was granted the title of Imperator for his military victories. 凯撒大帝因其军事胜利而获得“Imperator”称号。


例句：Augustus, the first Roman emperor, consolidated the power of the Imperator. 奥古斯都，罗马的第一位皇帝，巩固了皇帝的权力。


近义词：emperor, sovereign, ruler, monarch|反义词：subject, citizen
