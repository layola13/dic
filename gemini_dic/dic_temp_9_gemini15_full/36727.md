collodion:/kəˈloʊdiən/| n. |火棉胶；胶棉|A viscous, flammable solution of nitrocellulose in a mixture of ether and alcohol, formerly used as a base for photographic film and in surgery to seal wounds.  It's a clear, syrupy liquid that dries to form a tough, transparent film.|Example:  In the 19th century, collodion was a crucial component in early photography.  十九世纪，火棉胶是早期摄影的关键组成部分。|近义词：nitrocellulose solution |反义词：无
