mincers:/ˈmɪnsərz/ | n. |绞肉机；碎肉机；（尤指）说话含糊不清的人 | A mincer is a machine used to mince meat, or, less commonly, a person who speaks in an unclear or indistinct way.  | Example:  She used a hand-mincer to prepare the meat for the meatballs. 她用手动绞肉机准备做肉丸的肉。  | Example: He was a mincer, his words difficult to understand. 他说话含糊不清，很难理解。|近义词：meat grinder, chopper (for the machine)|反义词：None directly applicable.
