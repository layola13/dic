primitivism:/ˈprɪmətɪvɪzəm/| n. |原始主义；原始状态；简陋；粗俗|The belief in the value and beauty of primitive cultures and art, often manifested in artistic styles that imitate or evoke such cultures. It can also refer to a state of being primitive or undeveloped.  |例句：The painting exhibited a strong sense of primitivism, with its bold colors and simplified forms. 这幅画展现出强烈的原始主义，其色彩大胆，形式简化。

例句：His primitivism in technology showed in his reliance on outdated tools. 他在技术上的原始性表现在他依赖过时的工具上。|近义词：simplicity, naiveté, rusticity |反义词：sophistication, refinement, complexity
