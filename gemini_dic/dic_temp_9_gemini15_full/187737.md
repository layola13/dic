rooter:/ˈruːtər/ | n. |狂热支持者，捣乱者，(美)地下电缆探测器 | A rooter is someone who enthusiastically supports a particular team, especially in sports.  It can also refer to someone who deliberately causes trouble or damage, or (in American English) a device used to locate underground cables. |例句：He's a die-hard rooter for the Yankees. 他是扬基队的铁杆粉丝。 |近义词：fan, supporter, vandal (for the trouble-making sense), cable locator (for the technical sense) |反义词：opponent, detractor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roots for the local football team. = 他为当地足球队加油。
She roots for her favorite basketball player. = 她为她最喜欢的篮球运动员加油。


一般过去时:
They rooted for the underdog team. = 他们为弱势球队加油。
He rooted for his friend in the race. = 他在比赛中为他的朋友加油。


现在完成时:
She has rooted for this team for many years. = 她为这支球队加油多年了。
They have rooted for the same political party their whole lives. = 他们一生都支持同一个政党。


过去完成时:
We had rooted for them since the beginning of the season. = 我们从赛季开始就一直为他们加油。
She had rooted for her brother's success all along. = 她一直以来都支持她弟弟的成功。


将来时:
I will root for you in the competition. = 我会在比赛中为你加油。
They will root for their team no matter what the score is. = 不管比分如何，他们都会为他们的球队加油。


虚拟语气:
If I were there, I would root for you. = 如果我在那里，我会为你加油。
I wish I could root for both teams. = 我希望我能同时为两支球队加油。


被动语态:
The team was rooted for by thousands of fans. = 这支球队得到了成千上万球迷的支持。
(Note: Passive voice is less common with "root" in this sense)


疑问句:
Who do you root for in this election? = 在这次选举中你支持谁？
Which team are you rooting for? = 你支持哪支球队？


否定句:
I don't root for any particular team. = 我不特别支持任何一支球队。
He didn't root for the winning team. = 他并没有为获胜的球队加油。


PHRASE->
root for:  为...加油
root out: 根除；肃清
root around: 搜寻；翻找
take root: 生根；扎根
get to the root of: 找到...的根源
