forwardness:/ˈfɔːrwədnəs/ | n. |冒失；鲁莽；放肆；大胆；直率 |The quality or state of being forward; boldness; impudence; presumption.  It can also refer to the act of advancing or progressing. |例句：Her forwardness surprised everyone at the party.  她的冒失让派对上的每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：The company's forwardness in adopting new technology gave them a competitive edge. 公司在采用新技术的积极性使其获得了竞争优势。


近义词：boldness, impudence, audacity, presumption, assertiveness, frankness


反义词：timidity, shyness, diffidence, reserve, modesty


词源:  From Middle English *forwardnesse, from forward + -nesse.  "Forward" itself derives from Old English *forweard, from for- ("forth, forward") + weard ("direction, ward").
