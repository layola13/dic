restrainers:/rɪˈstreɪnərz/ | n. | 约束物；抑制物；限制器 | Something that restrains or restricts.  A restrainer can be a physical device or a force that limits movement, action, or growth. | Example: The seat belts are important safety restrainers in a car. 安全带是汽车中重要的安全约束装置。  The government imposed new economic restrainers to control inflation. 政府实施新的经济抑制措施以控制通货膨胀。 |近义词：constraints, limitations, controls, checks |反义词：enablers, stimulants
