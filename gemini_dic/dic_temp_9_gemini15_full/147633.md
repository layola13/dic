overpoise:/ˈoʊvərˈpɔɪz/| v.| 举止过于庄重；过于稳重；使…失去平衡|To be or behave in an excessively dignified or self-possessed manner; to cause to lose balance.|例句：He overpoised himself with excessive formality, making the conversation stilted. 他过于庄重，使谈话显得拘谨。

例句：The heavy weight overpoised the small table, causing it to tip over.沉重的重量使小桌子失去平衡，导致它翻倒。|近义词：overbalance, affect, unbalance|反义词：underpoise, casualness, informality|词性:动词
