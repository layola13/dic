silencer:/ˈsaɪlənsər/| n.|消音器；压制者|A device for silencing or muffling a noise, especially the sound of a firearm.  It is also used figuratively to describe something or someone that suppresses or silences something else.|例句：The motorcycle had a custom silencer installed. 这辆摩托车安装了一个定制消音器。

例句：He acted as a silencer, preventing the scandal from becoming public. 他充当了压制者，阻止了丑闻公开。|近义词：muffler, suppressor, damper|反义词：amplifier, intensifier|名词
