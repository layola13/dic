self-reproaches:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈproʊtʃɪz/| n.|自我责备，自责|The act of blaming or criticizing oneself.  It often involves feelings of guilt, shame, or regret over one's actions or inactions.|例句：His self-reproaches were constant after the accident.  事故发生后，他不断地自责。

例句：Overwhelmed by self-reproaches, she couldn't sleep.  被自责压得喘不过气，她睡不着觉。


近义词：self-criticism, remorse, regret, self-condemnation, repentance


反义词：self-satisfaction, complacency, self-justification


词性:名词


词源:

* **self-**:  Prefix indicating oneself or one's own.  From Old English *self, *sylf.

* **reproach**: Verb meaning to blame or criticize.  From Old French *reprochier, from Latin *reprobare ("to disapprove").  The noun form "reproach" developed later.
