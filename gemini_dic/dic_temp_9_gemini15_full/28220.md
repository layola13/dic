capsheaf: /ˈkæpʃiːf/ | n. | 穗子；麦束顶端 | A capsheaf is the topmost sheaf or bundle of grain in a harvest. It is often considered the best or most representative part of the harvest.  It can also refer to a crowning achievement or final element that completes something. | Example: The golden capsheaf of wheat stood tall above the rest.  这株金黄色的麦穗高高地矗立在其他麦穗之上。 The new software update is the capsheaf of a year's worth of development.  新的软件更新是一年开发工作的最终成果。 | 近义词: culmination, climax, pinnacle | 反义词: beginning, start, commencement | 名词
