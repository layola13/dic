complimentarity:/ˌkɒmplɪmɛnˈtærɪti/| n. |互补性，互补关系|The state of being complementary; the way two or more things work well together because of their differences.  It often refers to the relationship between two entities where each entity's weaknesses are compensated for by the other's strengths.  This concept is applicable across various fields, including biology, physics, and economics.| Example: The  complimentarity between the two companies led to a successful merger. 两个公司的互补性促成了成功的合并。|近义词：complementarity, synergy, interdependence|反义词：opposition, conflict, antagonism|名词
