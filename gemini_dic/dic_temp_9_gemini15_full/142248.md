orotund: /ˈɔːrəˌtʌnd/ | adj. | 声音洪亮的；言辞浮夸的；夸张的 |  Characterized by fullness and richness of sound; pompous or bombastic in speech or style.  | 例句：His orotund pronouncements were met with polite but unenthusiastic applause. 他洪亮的宣布受到了礼貌但缺乏热情的掌声。

例句：The orator delivered an orotund speech that captivated the audience.  那位演说家发表了一篇声音洪亮，引人入胜的演讲。


近义词：sonorous, resonant, pompous, bombastic, magniloquent

反义词：muted, subdued, quiet, understated, laconic


词源：From Middle French *orotund, from Latin orotundus, from os, oris "mouth" + tundus, past participle of tundere "to swell, puff up".  The word literally means "having a swollen mouth," suggesting a full, resonant voice.
