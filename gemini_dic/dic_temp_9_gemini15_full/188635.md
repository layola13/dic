ruminatively:/ˈruːmɪneɪtɪvli/| adj. |沉思地；反刍地；默想地 |Engaging in or characterized by deep thought; reflective and pensive.  It suggests a slow, deliberate process of contemplation, often involving a turning over of ideas or memories in the mind. |例句：He stared ruminatively out the window, lost in thought. 他沉思地望着窗外，陷入沉思。

例句：She ruminatively chewed her lip, considering the implications of her decision. 她沉思地咬着嘴唇，考虑着她决定的影响。


近义词：thoughtfully, contemplatively, reflectively, pensively, meditatively

反义词：rashly, impulsively, hastily, thoughtlessly


词性：形容词
