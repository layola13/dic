copings:/ˈkoʊpɪŋz/| n. |盖板；coping stone；压顶石|Protective covering or facing, typically of stone or concrete, on a wall or other structure. Copings are used to protect the top edge of a wall from the weather and to provide a finished appearance.|例句：The brick copings on the wall were crumbling.  墙上的砖砌压顶石正在崩塌。|例句：The new copings around the pool were made of granite. 泳池周围的新压顶石是用花岗岩制成的。|近义词：capstone, capping, coping stone|反义词： (无明确反义词，可考虑与“exposed edge”相对)|名词
