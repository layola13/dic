physiognosy:/ˌfɪziˈɒɡnəsi/| n. |相面术；观相术|The study of a person's character or personality from their outer appearance, especially the face.  Physiognomy is the practice of determining character or personality from outward appearances, especially the face.  It is considered a pseudoscience. |Example:  He believed in physiognosy and judged people based on their facial features. 他相信相面术，并根据人们的面部特征来判断他们。|近义词：face reading, character reading |反义词：N/A
