precommunicates:/ˌpriːkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪts/ | vt. |预先沟通；提前沟通 | To communicate beforehand; to communicate in advance.  | The team precommunicates their strategies before every major competition.  团队在每次重大比赛前都会预先沟通他们的策略。 |近义词：inform beforehand, give advance notice, brief in advance |反义词： keep secret, withhold information


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He precommunicates his schedule to his assistant every morning. 他每天早上都会提前告知他的行程给他的助理。
The company precommunicates its marketing plans to its stakeholders. 公司提前向利益相关者沟通其营销计划。

一般过去时:
She precommunicated her travel plans to her family. 她提前告知家人她的旅行计划。
The manager precommunicated the changes in policy to his team. 经理提前向他的团队沟通了政策变化。

现在完成时:
They have precommunicated their decision to the board. 他们已经向董事会提前告知了他们的决定。
We have precommunicated the potential risks to the investors. 我们已经向投资者提前告知了潜在的风险。

过去完成时:
The speaker had precommunicated his key points to the audience. 演讲者已提前向观众传达了他的要点。
Before the meeting, the CEO had precommunicated his expectations. 会议之前，首席执行官已经提前传达了他的期望。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he had precommunicated his concerns, the problem could have been avoided. 如果他事先表达了他的担忧，这个问题本来是可以避免的。
