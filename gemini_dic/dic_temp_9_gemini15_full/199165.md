silicioidea: /ˌsɪlɪˈsɔɪdiə/ | n. |硅藻类|硅藻纲，是一类单细胞藻类，具有硅质细胞壁。它们是重要的浮游植物，对海洋生态系统至关重要。|Example: Diatoms belong to the class Bacillariophyceae, also known as silicioidea.  (硅藻属于杆藻纲，也称为硅藻纲。)


The silicioidea are microscopic, single-celled algae with cell walls made of silica (silicon dioxide).  They are a crucial component of the phytoplankton in many aquatic environments, playing a significant role in the global carbon cycle.  Their intricate and beautiful glass-like shells are a subject of scientific and artistic interest.
