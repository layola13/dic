mailshot:/ˈmeɪlʃɒt/| n. |邮寄广告，批量邮寄|A mailshot is a marketing technique where a company sends the same advertising material to many people by post.  It's often used to promote products or services to a large target audience. |Example: The company sent out a mailshot announcing their new product line. 这家公司寄出了宣传其新产品线的邮寄广告。|近义词：bulk mailing, direct mail campaign |反义词：personal contact, individual communication
