chairperson:/ˈtʃeəpɜːrsən/| n.|主席；会长；主持人|A chairperson is the person who presides over a meeting or organization.  They typically lead discussions, ensure order, and make sure decisions are reached fairly.  The term is often used as a gender-neutral alternative to "chairman".|The chairperson opened the meeting with a brief welcome.|president, chairman, moderator, presiding officer|None|noun
