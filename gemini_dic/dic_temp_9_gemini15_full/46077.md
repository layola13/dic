cyclopaedia:/saɪkləˈpiːdiə/| n. |百科全书|A book or set of books containing information on many subjects or on a particular subject.  It is an extensive compilation of knowledge, typically arranged alphabetically.|Example: The Encyclopaedia Britannica is a well-known cyclopaedia.|近义词：encyclopedia, compendium|反义词：None|名词
