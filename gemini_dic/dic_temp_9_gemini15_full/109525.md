lesions:/ˈliːʒənz/| n.|损害；损伤；病灶|An area of damaged tissue in the body.  Lesions can be caused by a variety of factors, including injury, infection, or disease. They can vary in size, shape, and appearance, depending on the underlying cause.|例句：The doctor examined the lesions on her skin.医生检查了她皮肤上的损害。The brain lesions were the cause of his paralysis. 脑损伤是他瘫痪的原因。|近义词：injury, wound, damage, trauma, ulcer, sore|反义词：healing, recovery, health
