fieldful:/ˈfiːldfʊl/| n. |田地满的；一田地之量|A fieldful is a quantity or amount that fills a field.  It implies a large, perhaps overflowing, quantity of something.  The word often emphasizes the abundance or fullness. |例句：The farmer harvested a fieldful of golden wheat.  农夫收割了一整田地的金黄小麦。


The field was a fieldful of vibrant wildflowers.  田里开满了鲜艳的野花。|近义词：field's worth, plenty, abundance |反义词：scarcity, dearth
