lactin: /ˈlæktɪn/ | n. | 乳汁，泌乳素 | A hormone produced by the pituitary gland that stimulates the production of milk in mammals.  It plays a crucial role in lactation. |  The mother's body produces lactin to nourish her newborn.  母亲的身体会产生泌乳素来喂养新生儿。 | prolactin |  (no direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of  a hormone that *inhibits* milk production) | 名词
