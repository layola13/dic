plebification:/ˌplɛbɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|平民化；庸俗化；低俗化 |The process of becoming plebeian or common; the act of making something common or vulgar.|例句：The plebification of popular culture is a concern for many intellectuals.  大众文化的平民化让许多知识分子担忧。|例句：The rapid plebification of the once-exclusive art form is evident in its wider accessibility.  这种曾经专属的艺术形式迅速平民化，从其更广泛的可及性中可见一斑。|近义词：debasement, vulgarization, degradation|反义词：refinement, elevation, ennoblement|名词
