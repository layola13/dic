homogenise:/ˈhɒmədʒənaɪz/ /ˈhoʊmədʒənaɪz/ | vt. | 使均匀；使同质化 | To make something uniform or homogeneous in composition or character.  | The milk was homogenised to prevent the cream from separating. 牛奶经过均质处理以防止奶油分离。 The company aims to homogenise its marketing strategies across different regions.  公司旨在使其营销策略在不同地区保持一致。 |  uniform, standardize, equalize | diversify, differentiate, stratify | 动词
