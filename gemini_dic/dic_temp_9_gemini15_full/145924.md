overdirect:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrɛkt/ | vt. | 过度指导；指挥过度 | To give excessive or unnecessary direction or guidance to someone or something.  It implies interfering too much, potentially hindering rather than helping. | 例句：The manager overdirected the new employee, stifling their initiative.  经理过度指导新员工，扼杀了他们的主动性。

The coach overdirected the team, leading to confusion and poor performance. 教练过度指挥球队，导致混乱和糟糕的表现。| 近义词：micromanage, supervise excessively, control excessively | 反义词：underdirect, delegate, empower
