nanocomposite:/ˌnænəʊkəmˈpɒzɪt/ |/ˌnænoʊkəmˈpɑːzɪt/| n. |纳米复合材料|A nanocomposite is a multiphase solid material where one of the phases has one, two or three dimensions of less than 100 nanometers (nm).  These materials often exhibit enhanced mechanical, electrical, thermal, or other properties compared to their individual components.  The unique properties arise from the nanoscale interactions between the different phases. |Example: Carbon nanotube-reinforced polymer nanocomposites are used in high-strength, lightweight applications.  碳纳米管增强聚合物纳米复合材料用于高强度轻量化应用。  |近义词:nanomaterial, composite material |反义词:homogeneous material
