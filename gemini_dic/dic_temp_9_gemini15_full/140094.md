off-grid:/ˈɔːf ɡrɪd/|/ˈɑːf ɡrɪd/| adj.| 离网的；不依赖公共设施的 | Not connected to or dependent on public utilities or services, especially the electricity grid.  | 例句：They live off-grid in a remote cabin, generating their own power.  他们住在偏远的小屋里，过着离网生活，自己发电。| 例句：The off-grid lifestyle requires self-sufficiency and resourcefulness.  离网的生活方式需要自给自足和足智多谋。|近义词：self-sufficient, independent, unconnected |反义词：on-grid, connected
