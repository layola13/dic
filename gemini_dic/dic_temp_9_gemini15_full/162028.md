prayingly:/ˈpreɪɪŋli/| adv. |祈祷般地，恳求地 |In a manner suggesting prayer or earnest entreaty.  It implies a fervent, hopeful, and often anxious desire for a positive outcome. |例句：She looked at him prayingly, hoping he would agree. 她祈祷般地看着他，希望他会同意。
例句：He waited prayingly for the doctor's diagnosis. 他祈祷般地等待着医生的诊断。|近义词：supplicantly, beseechingly, imploringly |反义词：indifferently, nonchalantly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have the typical verb conjugations of a verb.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.  You would not use it in a sentence with a verb conjugated for tense (present, past, future, etc.).  Instead, the tense is dictated by the verb it modifies.

Example sentences illustrating different tenses using the adverb "prayingly":


Present Simple: He waits prayingly for the results. 他祈祷般地等待着结果。
Present Continuous: She is looking prayingly at the sky. 她祈祷般地望着天空。

Past Simple: They listened prayingly to the news. 他们祈祷般地听着新闻。
Past Continuous:  He was watching prayingly as his son crossed the finish line. 他祈祷般地看着儿子冲过终点线。

Future Simple: They will prayingly await the decision. 他们将祈祷般地等待决定。
Future Continuous: She will be watching prayingly throughout the surgery.  她将在手术期间祈祷般地关注着。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "prayingly".  The word itself functions as a descriptive adverb modifying verbs.
