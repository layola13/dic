overwilling:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪlɪŋ/| adj. |过分乐意的，极其愿意的| excessively willing; more willing than is desirable or appropriate.  This word suggests an eagerness that might be excessive or even problematic.|He was overwilling to take on extra responsibilities, leading to burnout.|近义词：eager, enthusiastic, keen|反义词：reluctant, unwilling, hesitant
