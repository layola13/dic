self-trained:/ˌselfˈtreɪnd/| adj.| 自学的，自主训练的 |  Describes someone or something that has learned or been trained without formal instruction or guidance from others.  It emphasizes the independent nature of the learning process. | 例句：He is a self-trained programmer, having learned coding through online resources and personal projects. 他是一位自学的程序员，通过在线资源和个人项目学习编程。

He achieved fluency in Japanese through self-trained study. 他通过自学掌握了日语流利表达。


近义词：self-taught, independently trained, autodidactic


反义词：formally trained, professionally trained


词性：形容词
