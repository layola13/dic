potting:/ˈpɒtɪŋ/ | v. | 盆栽；栽种；(射杀)击中 | The act of planting something in a pot; also refers to the act of shooting and hitting a target.  | 例句：She enjoys potting her own herbs. = 她喜欢自己种草药。  | 例句：The hunter successfully potted the deer. = 猎人成功射中了鹿。 | 近义词：planting, setting, shooting | 反义词：uprooting, removing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pots his tomatoes every year. = 他每年都种植西红柿。
She pots her geraniums in colorful containers. = 她把天竺葵种在色彩鲜艳的花盆里。

一般过去时:
They potted the seedlings yesterday. = 他们昨天栽种了幼苗。
He potted the clay pigeon with a single shot. = 他一枪打中了粘土靶。

现在完成时:
I have potted all the new plants. = 我已经把所有新植物都种好了。
She has potted several awards this year. = 她今年获得了几个奖项。(引申义:获得)

过去完成时:
They had potted the flowers before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经把花种好了。
He had potted three birds before lunch. = 中午之前他已经打死了三只鸟。(引申义:射杀)

将来时:
We will pot the herbs next week. = 我们下周将种植草药。
He will pot the target from a long distance. = 他将从远处射中目标。

虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger pot, I would pot that rose bush. = 如果我有一个更大的花盆，我会把那棵玫瑰丛种进去。
If he had aimed better, he would have potted the bird. = 如果他瞄准得更好，他就能射中那只鸟了。

被动语态:
The seedlings were potted carefully. = 幼苗被仔细地栽种了。
The clay pigeon was potted by the experienced shooter. = 粘土靶被经验丰富的射手击中了。

疑问句:
Are you potting the plants today? = 你今天种植物吗？
Did he pot the bird? = 他射中那只鸟了吗？

否定句:
He didn't pot the fish. = 他没有射中鱼。
They haven't potted the seeds yet. = 他们还没播种。
