rescrutiny: /rɪˈskruːtəni/ | n. | 重新审查；再审查 | The act of examining something again, typically with more care or attention than before.  It implies a previous examination that was deemed insufficient or flawed. |例句：The government's decision was subject to intense rescrutiny after the scandal broke. 丑闻爆发后，政府的决定受到了严厉的重新审查。

例句：After the initial report was criticized, the data underwent further rescrutiny. 初步报告受到批评后，数据进行了进一步的重新审查。


近义词：re-examination, review, re-evaluation, second look | 反义词：acceptance, approval


词性: 名词
