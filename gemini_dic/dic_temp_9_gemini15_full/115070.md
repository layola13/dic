manes:/meɪnz/| n.|鬃毛；长发|The long, flowing mane of the lion was impressive.  狮子的长长的飘逸的鬃毛令人印象深刻。|The horse's mane was braided beautifully. 马的鬃毛编得非常漂亮。|近义词：hair, tresses|反义词：baldness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse's mane is long and flowing. = 这匹马的鬃毛又长又飘逸。
His mane is usually neatly combed. = 他的鬃毛通常梳理得很整齐。


一般过去时:
The lion's mane was magnificent. = 狮子的鬃毛非常华丽。
She brushed her long mane before going out. = 她出门前梳理了她那长长的秀发。


现在完成时:
The wind has tangled the horse's mane. = 风把马的鬃毛弄乱了。
He has never let anyone cut his mane.  = 他从未让任何人剪过他的长发。


过去完成时:
The knight had braided his horse's mane before the battle. = 骑士在战斗前已经编好了马的鬃毛。
She had washed her mane before applying conditioner. =  她在涂护发素之前已经洗过头发了。


将来时:
I will braid my daughter's mane for the wedding. = 我将在婚礼上为女儿编辫子。
The stylist will style his mane for the photoshoot. = 发型师将为他的头发造型拍照。


虚拟语气:

I wish my mane was as thick as hers. = 我希望我的头发像她一样浓密。
