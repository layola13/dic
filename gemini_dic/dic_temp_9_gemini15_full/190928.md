scaffold:/ˈskæfəʊld/| n. v.| 脚手架；支架；（尤指为处死犯人而搭建的）绞刑台；（比喻）支架；基础；|A scaffold is a temporary structure used to support workers and materials during the construction or repair of a building.  It can also refer to a platform for executions. Figuratively, it can mean a framework or support. |例句：The builders erected a scaffold around the building. 建筑工人搭建了一个脚手架围绕着建筑物。


The scaffold was used to support the workers while they painted the high walls. 脚手架用来支撑工人粉刷高墙。|近义词：framework, structure, support, platform |反义词：ground, base (in some figurative senses)
