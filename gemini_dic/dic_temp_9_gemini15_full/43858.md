crescent:/ˈkrɛsnt/| n. adj.| 新月；月牙形；渐增的 |A crescent is a shape like the curved part of the moon when it is less than half full.  It can also refer to something that is gradually increasing. |例句：The new moon was a thin crescent in the sky. 新月在天空中是一弯细细的月牙。The company experienced a period of crescent growth. 公司经历了一段增长期。|近义词：half-moon, sickle, waxing|反义词：full, waning
