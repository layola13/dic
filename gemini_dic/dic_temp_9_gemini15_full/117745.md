melanomas:/ˌmɛləˈnoʊməz/ | n. | 黑色素瘤（复数） | Malignant tumors that develop from melanocytes, the cells that produce melanin.  They are the most serious type of skin cancer. | Example:  Early detection of melanomas is crucial for successful treatment.  早期发现黑色素瘤对于成功治疗至关重要。 | 近义词: malignant skin cancers | 反义词: benign skin lesions
