daws:/dɔːz/| n.|寒鸦|A daw is a type of crow, a black bird with a slightly shorter beak than a crow.  They are known for their gregarious nature and often seen in flocks. |Example: A flock of daws swooped down to snatch the discarded bread.一群寒鸦俯冲下来抢夺丢弃的面包。|近义词：crow, rook|反义词：无
