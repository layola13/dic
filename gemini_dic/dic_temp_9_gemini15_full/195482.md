semiorganic:/ˌsɛmiˈɔːrɡænɪk/| adj.|半有机；部分有机的|Partially organic; containing both organic and inorganic components.  It describes substances or processes that are partly derived from living organisms but also involve synthetic or non-organic materials or methods.|The farmer uses semi-organic methods, combining natural fertilizers with some synthetic pesticides.|近义词：partially organic|反义词：fully organic, completely inorganic
