intergenerational: /ˌɪntərˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl/ | adj. |几代人的；跨代的 | Relating to or involving several generations.  |例句：The company implemented an intergenerational mentorship program.  这家公司实施了一个跨代的指导项目。

例句：There are significant intergenerational differences in attitudes towards technology.  在对待技术的态度上存在着显著的代际差异。


近义词：transgenerational, multigenerational

反义词：intragenerational (within a single generation)


词性:形容词
