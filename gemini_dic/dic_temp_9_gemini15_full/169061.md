/ˈprəʊɡræməbəl/ /ˈproʊɡræməbəl/ | adj. | 可编程的，可程序控制的 | Capable of being programmed; able to have its operations controlled by a program.  This adjective describes devices, systems, or software that can be instructed to perform different tasks by changing their internal instructions. | Example:  Many modern appliances are programmable, allowing users to schedule tasks like washing clothes or cooking meals.  许多现代家用电器都是可编程的，允许用户安排洗衣或做饭等任务。


Example: This programmable thermostat allows you to set different temperatures for different times of the day. 这个可编程温控器允许您为一天中的不同时间设置不同的温度。


近义词: configurable, customizable, adaptable


反义词: fixed, inflexible, unchangeable


词性: adjective
