polaristic: /pəʊˈlærɪstɪk/ | adj. | 两极分化的；对立的 | Relating to or characterized by polar opposites or extremes; exhibiting polarization.  | The political climate has become increasingly polaristic in recent years. 近年来，政治气候日益两极分化。 |近义词：polarized, dichotomous, dualistic |反义词：unified, harmonious, integrated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The political landscape is polaristic. = 政治格局是两极分化的。
This issue presents a polaristic view. = 这个问题呈现出两极分化的观点。


一般过去时:
The debate was highly polaristic. = 辩论非常两极分化。
Society was more polaristic in the past. = 过去社会更加两极分化。


现在完成时:
The country has become increasingly polaristic. = 这个国家已经变得越来越两极分化。
Their opinions have remained polaristic. = 他们的观点仍然是对立的。


过去完成时:
The situation had already become polaristic before the election. = 在选举之前，情况已经变得两极分化了。
The discussion had been quite polaristic until the mediator intervened. = 在调解员介入之前，讨论一直相当两极分化。


将来时:
The discussions will likely remain polaristic. = 讨论可能会继续保持两极分化。
The future looks polaristic in many aspects. = 未来在许多方面看起来都是两极分化的。


虚拟语气:
If the situation weren't so polaristic, we could find common ground. = 如果情况不是那么两极分化，我们就能找到共同点。
I wish the public discourse were less polaristic. = 我希望公众舆论不那么两极分化。


被动语态:
The issue is often presented in a polaristic way. = 这个问题经常以两极分化的方式呈现。
The debate was perceived as being overly polaristic. = 辩论被认为过于两极分化。


疑问句:
Is the current situation truly polaristic? = 目前的情况真的是两极分化吗？
Why is this topic so polaristic? = 为什么这个话题如此两极分化？


否定句:
The group's views were not polaristic; they found common ground. = 该组织的观点并非两极分化；他们找到了共同点。
The discussion was not as polaristic as expected. = 讨论并不像预期的那样两极分化。
