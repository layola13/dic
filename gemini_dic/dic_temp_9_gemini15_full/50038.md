denotative:/ˈdiːnoʊteɪtɪv/| adj.|指称的；词义的；语义的|Relating to the literal or dictionary meaning of a word or phrase, as opposed to its connotative or implied meaning.  It focuses on the explicit, objective meaning, free from emotional associations or subjective interpretations.|The denotative meaning of "home" is a place of residence.|近义词：literal, explicit|反义词：connotative, implied|形容词
