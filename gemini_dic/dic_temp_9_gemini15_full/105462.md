kill-the-clock: /kɪl ðə klɒk/ | v. | 消磨时间 | To engage in an activity, often a trivial or unproductive one, to pass the time.  This phrase implies that the activity itself is not the primary goal; rather, the goal is simply to fill time until something else happens. | 例句：We killed the clock playing cards before the movie started. 我们在电影开始前打牌消磨时间。


例句：They killed the clock by watching reruns of old sitcoms.  他们通过观看旧情景喜剧重播来消磨时间。


近义词：pass the time, while away the time, dawdle, procrastinate


反义词：make the most of time, utilize time efficiently


PHRASE->
kill time: 消磨时间
kill some time: 消磨一些时间
kill a few hours: 消磨几个小时
kill the time until: 消磨时间直到...
a time killer: 消磨时间的活动


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They kill the clock by playing video games. = 他们通过玩电子游戏来消磨时间。
We kill time chatting online. = 我们通过在线聊天来消磨时间。

一般过去时:
We killed the clock watching TV last night. = 我们昨晚看电视消磨时间。
They killed some time browsing the internet. = 他们浏览互联网消磨了一些时间。

现在完成时:
We have killed the clock playing cards for hours. = 我们打牌消磨了好几个小时。
She has killed time reading magazines all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在读杂志消磨时间。

过去完成时:
They had killed the clock by watching a movie before the party started. = 派对开始前，他们看了一部电影来消磨时间。
We had killed some time chatting before the meeting began. = 会议开始前，我们聊天消磨了一些时间。

将来时:
We will kill time by going for a walk before dinner. = 晚饭前我们会去散步消磨时间。
They will kill the clock playing board games this evening. = 今晚他们会玩棋盘游戏来消磨时间。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we wouldn't have killed the clock watching TV. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们就不会看电视消磨时间。
I wish I hadn't killed so much time playing games yesterday. = 我真希望昨天我没有花那么多时间玩游戏。

被动语态: (This phrase is rarely used in the passive voice)  The time was killed by playing games. = 时间是通过玩游戏来消磨掉的。(不自然)
The waiting time was killed by reading a book. = 等待的时间是通过看书来消磨掉的。(更自然)


疑问句:
How do you usually kill the clock on weekends? = 你通常周末如何消磨时间？
What are they doing to kill time? = 他们在做什么来消磨时间？

否定句:
We didn't kill time by watching TV; we were busy working. = 我们没有看电视消磨时间；我们忙着工作。
They didn't want to kill the clock; they had important things to do. = 他们不想消磨时间；他们有重要的事情要做。
