pigpen:/ˈpɪɡpen/| n.|猪圈；猪栏|A pen or enclosure for pigs.  A pigpen is often a dirty and smelly place.|例句：The pigs were rooting around in the mud in the pigpen. 猪们在猪圈里的泥里拱来拱去。

例句：The farmer cleaned out the pigpen every week.  农民每周都会清理猪圈。|近义词：pigsty, hogpen|反义词：mansion, palace
