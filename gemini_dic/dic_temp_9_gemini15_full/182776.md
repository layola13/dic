repatriate:/ˌriˈpeɪtrieɪt/| vt. n.|遣返；归还；汇回；|To return (someone) to their own country.|The government is working to repatriate its citizens from the war-torn region. 政府正在努力将公民从饱受战争蹂躏的地区遣返。|近义词：relocate, return, send back|反义词：expatriate, exile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government repatriates refugees to their home countries. = 政府将难民遣返回他们的祖国。
They repatriate profits earned overseas. = 他们将海外赚取的利润汇回国内。


一般过去时:
The company repatriated its employees after the crisis. = 危机过后，公司遣返了其员工。
She repatriated her savings from the foreign bank. = 她从外国银行汇回了自己的存款。


现在完成时:
The army has repatriated all the prisoners of war. = 军队已经遣返了所有战俘。
He has repatriated his assets to his home country. = 他已将其资产汇回祖国。


过去完成时:
They had repatriated the artifacts before the museum opened. = 在博物馆开放之前，他们已经将文物送回。
The company had repatriated its funds before the currency devaluation. = 在货币贬值之前，公司已经将资金汇回。


将来时:
The government will repatriate the remaining citizens next month. = 政府将于下个月遣返其余公民。
We will repatriate the profits to our headquarters in the US. = 我们将利润汇回我们在美国的总部。


虚拟语气:
If the situation improved, they would repatriate the workers. = 如果情况好转，他们就会遣返工人。
I wish they would repatriate the stolen artworks. = 我希望他们能将被盗的艺术品送回。


被动语态:
The refugees were repatriated to their villages. = 难民被遣返回他们的村庄。
The funds are being repatriated to avoid further losses. = 正在将资金汇回以避免进一步损失。


疑问句:
Will the company repatriate its employees soon? = 公司会很快遣返员工吗？
When will the government repatriate the citizens? = 政府什么时候遣返公民？


否定句:
The government did not repatriate the dissidents. = 政府没有遣返异议人士。
The company will not repatriate its profits this year. = 公司今年不会将利润汇回。
