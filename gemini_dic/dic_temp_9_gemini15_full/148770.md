overunionizes: /ˌoʊvərˈjuːnjənaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度工会化 | To excessively or improperly organize into labor unions.  This implies either too many workers are unionized in a given setting, or that the unionization process itself was flawed or unfair. | Example: The company feared that overunionizing would cripple its operations.  公司担心过度工会化会削弱其运营。  The aggressive unionizing campaign led to accusations of overunionizing and unfair labor practices.  激进的工会化运动导致了过度工会化和不公平劳动行为的指控。 | 近义词: overorganize, excessively unionize | 反义词:  deunionize, underunionize
