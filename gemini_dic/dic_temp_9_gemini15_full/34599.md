cleavage:/ˈkliːvɪdʒ/ | n. |裂缝；分裂；解理；乳沟 | The act of splitting or separating something; a split, crack, or fissure;  in geology, the tendency of a mineral to split along definite planes; the exposed area of a woman's breasts between the breasts. |例句：The rock showed signs of cleavage. 这块岩石显示出解理的迹象。

The geological cleavage was impressive. 地质上的裂缝令人印象深刻。


例句：Her low-cut dress revealed a lot of cleavage. 她的低胸礼服露出了很多乳沟。


近义词：fission, split, rift, fracture, separation (for general meaning); décolletage (for exposed breasts) | 反义词：union, fusion, joining, cohesion
