disjunctive:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋktɪv/| adj.|析取的；选择的；分离的|  Relating to or denoting a logical connective (such as 'or') that expresses an alternative between two or more propositions.  It implies that at least one of the propositions must be true, but not necessarily all of them.  The term also describes something that is disconnected or separated.|

The disjunctive proposition "it will rain or it will snow" is true if either or both events occur.

The disjunctive clauses in the contract allowed for flexibility in payment terms.


近义词：alternative, separating, disjointed,  disconnected

反义词：conjunctive, connected, unified, continuous


词性：adjective
