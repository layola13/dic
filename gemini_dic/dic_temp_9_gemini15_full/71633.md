flatly:/ˈflætli/ | adv. | flatly | 断然地；平淡地；完全地；直截了当地 | The statement was made flatly and without emotion.  陈述是直截了当地，毫无感情地提出的。| He flatly refused to cooperate. 他断然拒绝合作。|  emphatically, bluntly, directly | indirectly, vaguely, subtly | 副词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He flatly denies the accusations. 他断然否认这些指控。
She flatly refuses to compromise. 她断然拒绝妥协。


一般过去时:
He flatly rejected my offer. 他断然拒绝了我的提议。
She flatly contradicted her brother. 她断然反驳了她的哥哥。


现在完成时:
He has flatly refused to answer any questions. 他一直断然拒绝回答任何问题。
She has flatly ignored all my emails. 她一直完全无视我所有的邮件。


过去完成时:
He had flatly stated his position before the meeting. 他在会议之前就已断然陈述了他的立场。
She had flatly denied any involvement in the incident. 她之前已断然否认参与了这起事件。


将来时:
He will flatly refuse any further negotiations. 他将断然拒绝进一步谈判。
She will flatly contradict anything you say. 她会断然驳斥你说的任何话。


虚拟语气:
If he were asked, he would flatly refuse. 如果问他，他会断然拒绝。
If she were here, she would flatly deny it. 如果她在场，她会断然否认。


被动语态:
His claims were flatly rejected. 他的说法被断然拒绝了。
The proposal was flatly ignored. 这项提议被完全忽略了。


疑问句:
Did he flatly refuse? 他是断然拒绝了吗？
Will she flatly deny it? 她会断然否认吗？


否定句:
He didn't flatly refuse, he just hesitated. 他并没有断然拒绝，他只是犹豫了一下。
She didn't flatly contradict him, she just expressed her reservations. 她并没有断然反驳他，她只是表达了自己的保留意见。
