helmed:/helm/| vt.|掌舵；领导；担任…的领导|To control or manage something, especially a ship or organization.|例句：She helmed the company through a difficult period. 她带领公司度过了艰难时期。|例句：The captain helmed the ship through the storm. 船长驾船驶过暴风雨。|近义词：lead, manage, steer, direct|反义词：follow, be led


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He helms the project. = 他领导这个项目。
She helms the ship skillfully. = 她熟练地掌舵。

一般过去时:
He helmed the company for ten years. = 他领导这家公司十年了。
She helmed the boat across the lake. = 她驾船渡过湖面。

现在完成时:
He has helmed the department since 2010. = 自2010年以来，他一直领导该部门。
She has helmed many successful campaigns. = 她领导过许多成功的战役。

过去完成时:
He had helmed the ship before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他掌舵过那艘船。
She had helmed the organization for five years before retiring. = 她退休前领导了该组织五年。

将来时:
He will helm the new initiative. = 他将领导这项新计划。
She will helm the aircraft carrier. = 她将指挥航空母舰。

虚拟语气:
If he helmed the project, it would be successful. = 如果他领导这个项目，它将会成功。
If she had helmed the ship differently, the accident might have been avoided. = 如果她当时掌舵方式不同，事故也许可以避免。

被动语态:
The project was helmed by a very experienced manager. = 这个项目由一位经验丰富的经理领导。
The ship was skillfully helmed through the narrow passage. = 船只熟练地通过狭窄的通道。

疑问句:
Who helmed the company during that crisis? = 在那次危机期间是谁领导这家公司？
Who helmed the expedition? = 谁领导这次探险？

否定句:
He did not helm the project. = 他没有领导这个项目。
She did not helm the ship during the storm. = 暴风雨期间她没有掌舵。


PHRASE->
