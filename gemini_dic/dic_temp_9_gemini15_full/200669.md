sledges:/ˈslɛdʒɪz/| n.|雪橇；(尤指长而重的)雪橇|A sledge is a vehicle used for travelling over snow and ice, typically consisting of a long, flat base with runners.  It is often pulled by animals or people.|例句：The children enjoyed sledging down the snowy hill. 孩子们喜欢在雪坡上滑雪橇。
例句： We used a sledge to transport supplies across the frozen lake. 我们用雪橇把物资运过冰冻的湖面。|近义词：sleigh, toboggan|反义词：None specifically.  (Antonyms would depend on the context, e.g.,  car if contrasting modes of transportation)
