overripened:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpənd/| adj. |过熟的，熟透了的|  Beyond the optimal stage of ripeness; excessively ripe.  The fruit has become soft and mushy due to being overripened.  |The overripened bananas were thrown away.|近义词：rotten, spoiled, decayed|反义词：underripe, unripe
