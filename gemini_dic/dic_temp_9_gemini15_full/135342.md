nonpenetration: /ˌnɒnˌpɛnɪˈtreɪʃən/ | n. | 非穿透；未穿透；未渗透 | The lack of penetration; the state of not penetrating.  This term is often used in technical contexts, particularly in fields like medicine, engineering, and materials science.  It describes a situation where something (e.g., a projectile, a fluid, a signal) does not pass through or into a substance or material. |


例句：

The nonpenetration of the bullet into the armor saved the soldier's life. = 子弹未穿透装甲，挽救了士兵的生命。

The study investigated the nonpenetration of water into the newly developed sealant. = 这项研究调查了新开发的密封剂对水的阻隔性。


近义词：impermeability, impenetrability, resistance


反义词：penetration, permeability


词性: 名词
