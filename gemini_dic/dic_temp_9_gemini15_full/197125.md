shandies:/ˈʃændiz/| n. |香槟酒加姜汁汽水或柠檬水的混合饮料 |A shandy is a mixed alcoholic drink that typically consists of beer (often lager or ale) and a non-alcoholic beverage, most commonly lemonade or ginger ale.  The proportions vary depending on preference, but it's usually a roughly equal mix or slightly more beer than the other ingredient.  It's a refreshing drink, particularly popular in warmer climates. |He ordered a shandy to cool down on the hot afternoon. 他在炎热的下午点了一杯香槟酒加姜汁汽水来降温。|  |  | noun
