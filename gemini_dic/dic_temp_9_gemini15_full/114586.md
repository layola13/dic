malcontent:/ˈmælˌkɒntɛnt/| n. adj.|不满者；牢骚者；不满的；不满足的|A person who is dissatisfied and rebellious.  Someone who is persistently unhappy and complains a lot.|例句：He was a malcontent who constantly criticized the government. 他是一个不断批评政府的不满分子。


例句：The malcontent employee finally quit his job.  这位心怀不满的员工最终辞职了。


近义词：dissident, disgruntled, disaffected, resentful


反义词：contented, satisfied, happy


词性: noun, adjective
