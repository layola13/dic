mix:/mɪks/| n., v.|混合；混合物|To combine or blend into one mass or mixture.|The chef mixed the ingredients together.  厨师把配料混合在一起。|blend, combine, mingle|separate, unmix|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mixes the paint carefully. 他仔细地混合颜料。
She mixes the flour and sugar. 她混合面粉和糖。

一般过去时:
They mixed the cement yesterday. 他们昨天混合了水泥。
I mixed up the ingredients. 我把配料弄混了。

现在完成时:
I have mixed the batter. 我已经混合好面糊了。
She has mixed the drinks for the party. 她已经为派对调好了饮料。

过去完成时:
He had mixed the chemicals before the experiment. 他在实验前已经混合好了化学药品。
They had mixed the colors incorrectly. 他们已经错误地混合了颜色。

将来时:
We will mix the ingredients tomorrow. 我们明天将混合配料。
She will mix the salad dressing. 她将调制沙拉酱。

虚拟语气:
If I mixed the colors differently, the result would be better. 如果我以不同的方式混合颜色，结果会更好。
I wish I had mixed the batter more thoroughly. 我希望我当初把面糊搅拌得更彻底些。

被动语态:
The ingredients were mixed by the machine. 配料是由机器混合的。
The colors are being mixed now. 颜色现在正在混合。

疑问句:
How do you mix the cake batter? 你是怎么混合蛋糕糊的？
Have you mixed the drinks yet? 你已经混合好饮料了吗？

否定句:
Don't mix the oil and water. 不要混合油和水。
He didn't mix the ingredients properly. 他没有正确地混合配料。


PHRASE->
mix up: 弄混
mix and match: 混搭
a mixed bag: 各种各样的
a mixed blessing: 喜忧参半
mix it up:  (俚语)大打出手


词源:
源自古英语 *micsian,  与拉丁语 miscere 和古希腊语 μίγνυμι (mignumi)  相关，意思都是“混合”。
