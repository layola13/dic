frustr: /ˈfrʌstræt/ | v. | 使沮丧；使受挫；阻挠 | to prevent someone from achieving something; to make someone feel annoyed or discouraged because they cannot achieve something |  The constant delays frustrated our plans.  持续的延误使我们的计划受挫。 |  The traffic frustrated me. 交通让我感到沮丧。 | annoy, irritate, impede | encourage, help, assist | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frustrates me with his constant complaining. = 他不停的抱怨让我很沮丧。
The bad weather frustrates our outdoor plans. = 恶劣的天气阻碍了我们的户外计划。


一般过去时:
The exam frustrated her because she didn't prepare well. = 考试让她很沮丧，因为她没有好好准备。
The broken machine frustrated the workers' efforts. = 那台坏掉的机器阻碍了工人们的工作。


现在完成时:
The project has frustrated us for months. = 这个项目已经让我们沮丧了好几个月了。
Her lack of cooperation has frustrated the team's progress. = 她缺乏合作精神已经阻碍了团队的进展。


过去完成时:
He had been frustrated by repeated failures before he finally succeeded. = 在他最终成功之前，他一直被反复的失败所挫败。
They had been frustrated by the lack of resources for years. = 多年来，他们一直因为缺乏资源而感到沮丧。


将来时:
The new regulations will frustrate many small businesses. = 新的法规将会让许多小型企业感到沮丧。
I think the long journey will frustrate the children. = 我认为漫长的旅程会让孩子们感到沮丧。


虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't been frustrated by that setback, I would have finished the project earlier. = 如果我没有被那次挫折打败，我就能早点完成项目了。
I wish I hadn't been so frustrated by his behavior. = 我真希望我不会因为他那样做而感到沮丧。


被动语态:
Many people are frustrated by the slow pace of change. = 许多人对缓慢的改变速度感到沮丧。
His ambitions were frustrated by a lack of opportunity. = 他的抱负由于缺乏机会而受挫。


疑问句:
What frustrates you the most about your job? = 你工作中最让你沮丧的是什么？
Does the complexity of the problem frustrate you? = 这个问题的复杂性是否让你感到沮丧？


否定句:
The simple instructions didn't frustrate the children. = 简单易懂的说明并没有让孩子们感到沮丧。
Nothing frustrated his determination to succeed.  = 没有任何事情能够动摇他成功的决心。


PHRASE->

deeply frustrated = 深感沮丧
become frustrated = 变得沮丧
