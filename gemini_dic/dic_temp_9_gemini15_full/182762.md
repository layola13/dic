repassant:/ˌrɛpəˈsɑːnt/| adj.|再次经过的，再次走过的|Passing again; passing by a second time.  It describes something that is happening or has happened again, often in a similar way to a previous occurrence.|例句：The train made a repassant journey through the valley. 这列火车再次经过山谷。|近义词：recurring, repeated, revisited|反义词：initial, first, unprecedented
