fabricates:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪts/| vt.|捏造；伪造；编造|To invent or create something false.  To make or manufacture something, especially on a large scale.|The journalist fabricated the story to sell more newspapers.  记者编造了这个故事以卖出更多报纸。The company fabricates car parts. 这家公司制造汽车零件。|creates, invents, manufactures, constructs|reveals, discloses, verifies, authenticates|动词


USAGE->

The factory fabricates electronic components. = 工厂制造电子元件。
