meant:/ment/| v.|意思是，打算，意指|To have something as one's purpose or intention; to signify or express.|例句：I meant to call you earlier, but I forgot. 我本来打算早点给你打电话，但我忘了。|近义词：intended, signified, implied|反义词：unintended, accidental


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He means well, but he's not very good at expressing himself. = 他本意是好的，但他不太擅长表达自己。
What does this word mean? = 这个词是什么意思？


一般过去时:
I meant to go to the store, but I ran out of time. = 我本来打算去商店，但我没时间了。
She meant no harm. = 她并非有意伤害。


现在完成时:
I have meant to write to you for weeks, but I've been so busy. = 我几周来一直想给你写信，但我一直很忙。
What have you meant to say all along? = 你一直想说什么？


过去完成时:
I had meant to finish the project before the deadline, but I was delayed. = 我本来打算在截止日期前完成项目，但我被耽搁了。
She had meant to call, but she lost her phone. = 她本来打算打电话，但她丢了手机。


将来时:
I will mean to finish my work by tonight. = 我打算今晚完成我的工作。
He will mean to study hard for the exam. = 他打算努力学习考试。


虚拟语气:
If I had known you were coming, I would have meant to cook something special. = 如果我知道你要来，我本来打算做些特别的东西。
I wish I had meant to be more helpful. = 我希望我本来可以更有帮助。


被动语态:
The message was meant to be encouraging. = 这条消息的用意是鼓励人的。
His words were meant to be taken seriously. = 他的话应该认真对待。


疑问句:
What did you mean by that? = 你这是什么意思？
What does this symbol mean? = 这个符号是什么意思？


否定句:
I didn't mean to offend you. = 我无意冒犯你。
That wasn't meant to be sarcastic. = 那并非讽刺。


PHRASE->
be meant to do sth = 应该做某事
what is meant by = …是什么意思
meant to be = 天生注定
mean well = 好心好意
well-meant = 善意的
