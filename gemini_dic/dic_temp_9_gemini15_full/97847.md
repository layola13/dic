inlard:/ɪnˈlɑːrd/| vt.| 掺杂；在…中加入脂肪；使肥沃|To mix or incorporate fat, especially lard, into (something, such as meat).  |例句：The butcher inlarded the roast with garlic and herbs.  屠夫在烤肉中加入了大蒜和香草。|例句：The farmer inlarded the dry soil with manure to improve its fertility.  农民用肥料使干旱的土壤肥沃。|近义词：interlard, intersperse, enrich|反义词：lean, deplete|动词
