seeps:/siːps/| v.|渗出，渗漏|to flow or leak slowly through small openings or pores.  |例句：Water seeped through the crack in the wall. 水从墙上的裂缝中渗了出来。|例句：The oil seeped into the soil, contaminating the groundwater.  石油渗入土壤，污染了地下水。|近义词：ooze, trickle, percolate, filter|反义词：gush, pour, flood


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water seeps through the cracks in the dam. = 水从水坝的裂缝中渗出。
Oil slowly seeps from the damaged pipeline. = 油慢慢地从受损的管道中渗出。

一般过去时:
The rain seeped into the tent, making us wet. = 雨水渗入了帐篷，让我们淋湿了。
The dye seeped into the fabric, creating a beautiful pattern. = 染料渗入了织物，创造出美丽的图案。

现在完成时:
The toxins have seeped into the groundwater, causing serious pollution. = 毒素已经渗入地下水，造成了严重的污染。
The stain has seeped into the carpet, and I can't get it out. = 污渍已经渗入地毯，我无法清除它。

过去完成时:
The water had seeped into the basement before we noticed the leak. = 我们注意到漏水之前，水已经渗入了地下室。
By the time we arrived, the oil had seeped several feet into the ground. = 当我们到达时，石油已经渗入了地下几英尺。

将来时:
The rising tide will seep into the low-lying areas. = 上涨的潮水将会渗入低洼地区。
If we don't fix the roof, rain will seep into the attic. = 如果我们不修理屋顶，雨水将会渗入阁楼。

虚拟语气:
If the dam were damaged, water would seep into the valley. = 如果水坝受损，水就会渗入山谷。
I wish the oil hadn't seeped into the river. = 我希望石油没有渗入河流。

被动语态:
The basement was seeped with water after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，地下室被水浸透了。
The fabric was seeped with dye, giving it a rich color. = 织物被染料浸透，呈现出丰富的颜色。

疑问句:
Is the water seeping into the foundation? = 水是否正在渗入地基？
Does the oil seep through the cracks in the container? = 油是否从容器的裂缝中渗出？

否定句:
The water didn't seep through the waterproof material. = 水没有渗透防水材料。
The liquid didn't seep into the porous rock. = 液体没有渗入多孔岩石。
