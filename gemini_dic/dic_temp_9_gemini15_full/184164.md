resizing: /ˌriːˈsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 尺寸调整；大小调整 | The act of changing the size of something, typically an image or window on a computer screen.  It involves scaling the dimensions of an object proportionally or non-proportionally. | 例句：The image requires resizing before uploading. 图片上传前需要调整大小。

例句：The software allows for easy resizing of photos. 该软件允许轻松调整照片大小。


近义词：scaling, rescaling, adjusting, altering


反义词：None (The opposite would be "keeping the same size," not a single word)


词性：动名词 (gerund)  (派生自动词 resize)
