geeked:/ɡiːkt/| adj.|兴奋的，狂热的，很兴奋的 | Extremely excited; enthusiastic.  |例句：I'm totally geeked about the concert! 我对这场音乐会超级兴奋！

例句：She was geeked to see her favorite band. 她很兴奋能看到她最喜欢的乐队。


近义词：excited, thrilled, stoked, hyped

反义词：bored, apathetic, unenthusiastic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am geeked about the new video game. = 我对这款新的电子游戏很兴奋。
He is geeked to meet his idol. = 他很兴奋能见到他的偶像。


一般过去时:
I was geeked after I heard the good news. = 我听到好消息后很兴奋。
She was geeked to receive the award. = 她收到奖项后很兴奋。


现在完成时:
I have been geeked all day. = 我兴奋了一整天。
They have been geeked since they won the competition. = 自从他们赢得比赛以来，他们就一直很兴奋。


过去完成时:
I had been geeked for weeks before the event. = 在活动开始前的几周里，我一直都很兴奋。
She had been geeked all morning, anticipating the announcement. = 她兴奋了一上午，期待着宣布。


将来时:
I will be geeked when I finally see them. = 当我最终见到他们时，我会很兴奋。
He will be geeked to start his new job. = 他将很兴奋开始他的新工作。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would be geeked. = 如果我中了彩票，我会非常兴奋。
I wish I were geeked about my work. = 我希望我对我的工作感到兴奋。


被动语态: (This word is rarely used passively)  The crowd was geeked up by the performer's energy.  表演者的活力让观众兴奋起来。(This is a more idiomatic use, not a direct passive of "geeked")
The audience was worked into a geeked frenzy. 观众被煽动得非常兴奋。(This again uses a related idiom)


疑问句:
Are you geeked about the trip? = 你对这次旅行兴奋吗？
Is he geeked for the party? = 他对派对兴奋吗？


否定句:
I'm not geeked about going to the dentist. = 我对去看牙医不兴奋。
She wasn't geeked about the movie. = 她对这部电影不兴奋。
