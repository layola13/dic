madam:/ˈmædəm/| n.|夫人，女士|A polite or formal title for a woman.  It is often used in formal settings or when addressing a woman whose name you don't know.|例句：Excuse me, madam, could you help me?  对不起，夫人，您能帮我一下吗？|例句：The madam of the house greeted us warmly.  房子的女主人热情地接待了我们。|近义词：ma'am, lady, missus|反义词：sir
