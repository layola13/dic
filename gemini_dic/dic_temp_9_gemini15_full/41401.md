corncrib:/ˈkɔːrnkrɪb/ | n. | 谷仓；玉米仓 | A small, often simple structure used for storing corn.  Corncribs are traditionally built to allow for air circulation to prevent spoilage. | The farmer stored his harvest in the corncrib.  农夫把收成储存在玉米仓里。  The old corncrib was falling apart.  那个旧玉米仓快要塌了。 | granary, crib |  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑与“open field”相对，意为“没有储存的玉米田”) | 名词
