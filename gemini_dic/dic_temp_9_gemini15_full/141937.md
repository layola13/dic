ordinator:/ɔːr.dəˈneɪ.tər/| n. |协调者；组织者|A person who coordinates or organizes something.  An ordinator is responsible for the smooth running of a process or event by bringing together different elements and ensuring they work together effectively.|例句：The project ordinator ensured all tasks were completed on time. 项目协调员确保所有任务都按时完成。|例句：The wedding ordinator managed all the details, from the flowers to the catering. 婚礼组织者负责所有细节，从鲜花到餐饮。|近义词：coordinator, organizer, manager |反义词：disorganizer, disruptor
