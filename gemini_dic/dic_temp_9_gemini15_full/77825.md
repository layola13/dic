generative:/ˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 生成的；创生的；有生产力的 | Relating to or capable of generating something.  It often refers to the ability of a system to create new content, such as text, images, or code, based on learned patterns. |  The generative model produced realistic-looking images of cats.  生成的模型产生了看起来很逼真的猫的图像。| creative, productive | destructive, unproductive |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The generative model generates new text. = 生成的模型生成新的文本。
This process generates a lot of waste. = 这个过程产生了很多废物。


一般过去时:
The artist generated a lot of interest in his new work. = 艺术家对他的新作品产生了浓厚的兴趣。
The storm generated strong winds. = 暴风雨产生了强风。


现在完成时:
AI has generated significant advancements in various fields. = 人工智能在各个领域取得了显著进展。
They have generated a substantial profit this year. = 他们今年获得了可观的利润。


过去完成时:
The company had generated a large debt before it went bankrupt. = 公司破产前已背负巨额债务。
The writer had generated several drafts before submitting his manuscript. = 作家在提交手稿之前已经写了好几个草稿。


将来时:
This technology will generate many new job opportunities. = 这项技术将创造许多新的就业机会。
The new policy is expected to generate more revenue. = 新政策有望带来更多收入。


虚拟语气:
If we had a better system, we could generate more accurate results. = 如果我们有一个更好的系统，我们可以生成更准确的结果。
I wish I could generate more ideas. = 我希望我能产生更多想法。


被动语态:

否定句:
