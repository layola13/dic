contrate:/kənˈtreɪt/| v.|集中；专心；浓缩 |To concentrate means to focus your attention or effort on something.  It can also refer to making something denser or stronger by removing water or other substances. |例句：I need to concentrate on my studies. 我需要专注于我的学习。  |例句：The factory concentrates orange juice before packaging. 工厂在包装前浓缩橙汁。|近义词：focus, center,  |反义词：distract, scatter, dilute.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She concentrates on her work every day. = 她每天都专心工作。
He concentrates his efforts on finishing the project. = 他集中精力完成项目。

一般过去时:
I concentrated hard on the exam. = 我在考试中认真集中注意力。
They concentrated the solution by boiling it. = 他们通过煮沸来浓缩溶液。

现在完成时:
She has concentrated on her career for many years. = 她多年来一直专注于她的事业。
The company has concentrated its resources on new product development. = 公司已将资源集中在新产品开发上。

过去完成时:
I had concentrated on my studies before the test. = 我在考试前已经集中精力学习了。
They had concentrated the syrup before bottling it. = 他们之前已经浓缩了糖浆才装瓶。

将来时:
I will concentrate on learning a new language next year. = 我明年将专注于学习一门外语。
The team will concentrate its efforts on resolving the issue. = 团队将集中精力解决这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If I had concentrated harder, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我当时更专注一点，我就通过考试了。
If she concentrated more, she would achieve better results. = 如果她更专注，她会取得更好的成绩。

被动语态:
The solution was concentrated by evaporation. = 溶液通过蒸发被浓缩。
All efforts are being concentrated on finding a solution. = 所有努力都集中在寻找解决方案上。

疑问句:
Do you concentrate well when studying? = 你学习的时候注意力集中吗？
Is the juice concentrated enough? = 果汁浓缩得够了吗？

否定句:
I couldn't concentrate because of the noise. = 因为噪音我无法集中注意力。
The company didn't concentrate its resources effectively. = 公司没有有效地集中资源。
