nonequivalently:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwɪvələntli/ | adv. |非等效地，不等价地 |  The word describes a situation where two things are not equivalent or equal in value, effect, or meaning. It emphasizes the lack of equivalence. | Example:  The two approaches, while seemingly similar, operate nonequivalently in practice. 这两种方法虽然表面相似，但在实践中却作用不等价。 | 近义词：differently, unequally, dissimilarly | 反义词：equivalently
