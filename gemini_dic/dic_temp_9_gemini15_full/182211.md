remonstrating:/rɪˈmɒnstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 规劝；抗议；强烈反对 |  To remonstrate means to protest or argue strongly against something.  It implies a forceful expression of disapproval or dissent. |

例句：

He remonstrated with his friend about the dangers of smoking. = 他规劝他的朋友戒烟的危害。

The workers remonstrated against the unfair treatment. = 工人们强烈抗议不公平待遇。


近义词：protest, object, complain, expostulate, rebuke

反义词：agree, approve, consent, acquiesce

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remonstrates with his boss about the new policy. = 他向老板抗议新的政策。
She frequently remonstrates against injustice. = 她经常抗议不公正。


一般过去时:
He remonstrated with the manager about the poor service. = 他向经理投诉糟糕的服务。
They remonstrated against the proposed tax increase. = 他们反对拟议的税收增加。


现在完成时:
She has remonstrated several times about the noise. = 她已经多次抗议噪音。
They have remonstrated against the government's actions. = 他们已经抗议政府的行为。


过去完成时:
He had remonstrated before, but nothing changed. = 他以前就抗议过，但什么也没改变。
They had remonstrated many times, to no avail. = 他们抗议过很多次，但没有用。


将来时:
He will remonstrate with her about her behaviour. = 他将就她的行为规劝她。
They will remonstrate at the meeting about the budget cuts. = 他们将在会议上抗议预算削减。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would remonstrate against such unfairness. = 如果我是你，我会抗议这种不公平。
I wish I had remonstrated sooner. = 我真希望我早点抗议。


被动语态:
His complaints were constantly remonstrated against. = 他的抱怨不断遭到抗议。
The decision was remonstrated against by many people. = 许多人反对这一决定。


疑问句:
Why are you not remonstrating against this? = 你为什么不反对这个？
Will they remonstrate about the new rules? = 他们会抗议新规吗？


否定句:
He did not remonstrate against the decision. = 他没有反对这个决定。
She wouldn't remonstrate even when she was wronged. = 即使受了委屈，她也不会抗议。


PHRASE->
remonstrate against = 反对
remonstrate with = 规劝，抗议
remonstrate loudly = 高声抗议
remonstrate earnestly = 认真地抗议
remonstrate vehemently = 强烈抗议


词源:
From Old French *remonstrer, from Latin remonstrāre "to show again, demonstrate," from re- "again" + monstrāre "to show."
