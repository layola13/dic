carabineer:/ˌkærəˈbɪnɪr/| n. |登山扣；蟹扣；卡宾枪|A carabineer is a strong metal loop with a spring-loaded gate, used in climbing, mountaineering, and other activities for connecting ropes, harnesses, and other equipment.  It's crucial for safety.| Example: He clipped the rope to the carabineer before descending the cliff. 他在下降悬崖之前将绳索扣到登山扣上。|近义词：snap hook, quick link|反义词：无
