lime:/laɪm/| n. adj.|酸橙；石灰；绿色的|A lime is a small, round, green or yellow fruit similar to a lemon, but smaller and more acidic.  It can also refer to the tree that bears this fruit, or to calcium oxide (quicklime), a white powder used in construction and other industries.  As an adjective, it describes a vivid yellowish-green color.|例句：We used limes in the mojitos. 我们在莫吉托酒里用了酸橙。|例句：The walls were painted a bright lime green. 墙壁漆成了鲜艳的酸橙绿。|近义词：lemon, citron, quicklime|反义词：N/A (depending on context)|名词，形容词
