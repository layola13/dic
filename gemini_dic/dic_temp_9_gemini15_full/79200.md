glances:/ɡlænsɪz/ | n. | 一瞥，匆匆一看 | A quick look. |例句：She glanced at the clock. 她瞥了一眼钟。|近义词：glance, peep, peek |反义词：stare, gaze


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glances at his phone frequently. = 他经常看一眼他的手机。
She glances around the room nervously. = 她紧张地环顾了一下房间。


一般过去时:
He glanced at the newspaper before leaving. = 他离开前瞥了一眼报纸。
She glanced up when she heard the noise. = 她听到声音时抬头看了一眼。


现在完成时:
I have glanced at the report, but haven't read it thoroughly. = 我浏览了一下报告，但还没有仔细阅读。
She has glanced at the menu several times. = 她已经多次看过菜单了。


过去完成时:
He had glanced at the letter before throwing it away. = 他在扔掉信之前匆匆看了一眼。
She had glanced at her watch several times during the meeting. = 在会议期间，她好几次看了一眼手表。


将来时:
He will glance at the map before starting his journey. = 他会在出发前快速看一下地图。
She will glance at the recipe before she begins cooking. = 她会在开始烹饪前快速看一下食谱。


虚拟语气:
If I had glanced at the instructions, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我当时看了一眼说明书，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had glanced at the weather forecast before leaving. = 我真希望我在离开前看了一眼天气预报。


被动语态:
The painting was glanced at by many visitors. = 那幅画被许多参观者匆匆看了一眼。
The document should be glanced at before signing. = 文件应该在签字前浏览一下。


疑问句:
Did he glance at the message? = 他看了一眼信息吗？
Has she glanced at the email yet? = 她看一眼邮件了吗？


否定句:
He didn't glance at me during the conversation. = 在谈话期间，他根本没看我一眼。
She didn't glance at the price tag. = 她没有看一眼价签。


PHRASE->
a quick glance = 匆匆一瞥
a sidelong glance = 斜眼看
a fleeting glance = 一闪而过
to steal a glance = 偷偷地看一眼
to exchange glances = 交换眼神
