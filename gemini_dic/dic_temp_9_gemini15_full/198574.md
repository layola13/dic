sticky:/ˈstɪki/ | adj. | 粘的，粘性的；棘手的，困难的 |  Something that is sticky is covered with or has a substance that makes things stick to it.  It can also refer to a situation that is difficult or awkward. |例句：The glue is very sticky. 这胶水很粘。

The situation is sticky; we need to act carefully.  情况很棘手；我们需要谨慎行事。|近义词：adhesive, glutinous, tacky, difficult, problematic |反义词：dry, smooth, easy, straightforward |
