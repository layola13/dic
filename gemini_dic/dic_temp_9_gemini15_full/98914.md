intensives:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪvz/| n.|强调词，强化词|Words or phrases that add emphasis or intensity to the meaning of other words or phrases. They can be adverbs, adjectives, or even whole phrases.|例句：He's incredibly intelligent. 他非常聪明。  She's absolutely gorgeous. 她非常漂亮。|近义词：emphasizers, intensifiers|反义词：downplayers, weakeners|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun shines intensely today. = 太阳今天强烈地照耀着。
He works intensively on his project. = 他在项目上努力工作。


一般过去时:
She studied intensively for the exam. = 她为考试努力学习。
The rain fell intensely during the storm. = 暴风雨期间雨下得很大。


现在完成时:
We have worked intensely on this problem. = 我们一直在努力解决这个问题。
They have intensely debated the issue. = 他们对这个问题进行了激烈的辩论。


过去完成时:
They had intensely prepared for the competition. = 他们为比赛做了充分的准备。
She had intensely disliked him since childhood. = 她从小就非常讨厌他。


将来时:
We will intensively train for the marathon. = 我们将为马拉松进行强化训练。
He will intensively study the topic next week. = 他下周将深入研究这个话题。


虚拟语气:
If I were intensely focused, I would succeed. = 如果我非常专注，我就会成功。
I wish I had intensely prepared for the presentation. = 我希望我为演示做了充分的准备。


被动语态:
The problem was intensely debated. = 这个问题被激烈地讨论了。
The issue was intensely scrutinized by the committee. = 这个问题受到了委员会的严格审查。


疑问句:
Is he intensely committed to this project? = 他对这个项目非常投入吗？
Was she intensely moved by the movie? = 她被电影深深打动了吗？


否定句:
He wasn't intensely interested in the topic. = 他对这个话题并不十分感兴趣。
She didn't intensely care about the outcome. = 她并不十分关心结果。
