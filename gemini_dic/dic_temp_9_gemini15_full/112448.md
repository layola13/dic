louder:/ˈlaʊdər/| adj.| 更大声的，更响亮的 |  Comparative adjective of loud;  signifying a greater degree of loudness. It describes a sound that is more intense or has a higher volume. | 例句：Please speak louder; I can't hear you.  请大声点儿；我听不见你说话。

例句：The music got louder and louder. 音乐越来越响亮。


近义词： louder, stronger, higher, more intense | 反义词： quieter, softer, fainter


词性：形容词
