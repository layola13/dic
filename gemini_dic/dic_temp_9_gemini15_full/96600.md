inductiveness:/ɪnˈdʌktɪvnəs/ | n. |归纳性；感应性 |The quality or state of being inductive; the tendency to infer general principles from particular instances.  It refers to the ability to draw general conclusions from specific observations.  In physics, it relates to the property of a substance to induce an electromotive force in a nearby conductor. |例句：The study demonstrated a high degree of inductiveness in the participants' reasoning. 这项研究表明参与者的推理具有高度的归纳性。

The experiment showed the inductiveness of the coil. 实验显示了线圈的感应性。|近义词：inference, deduction (in some contexts),  induction |反义词：deductiveness
