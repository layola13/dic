hawks:/hɔːks/| n. |鹰；鹰派人士|A hawk is a bird of prey with a hooked beak and talons.  Hawks are known for their sharp eyesight and hunting skills.  The term "hawks" can also refer to people who favor a more aggressive or interventionist approach, particularly in foreign policy. |例句：The hawk circled high above the valley.  这只鹰在山谷上空盘旋。|例句：The hawks in the government advocated for immediate military action.  政府中的鹰派人士主张立即采取军事行动。|近义词：eagle, falcon, raptor,  (for the political sense) hard-liner, interventionist|反义词：(for the political sense) dove, pacifist|名词
