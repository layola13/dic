revised:/rɪˈvaɪzd/ | adj. v. | 修订的；修改的；校订的 |  Having been changed or improved; amended.  |例句：The revised edition of the textbook includes new chapters.  修订版教科书包含新章节。

The revised plan is more efficient.  修改后的计划更高效。|近义词：amended, corrected, altered, modified |反义词：original, unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revises his work carefully. 他仔细地修改他的作品。
She revises her essay before submitting it. 她提交论文前修改了她的论文。


一般过去时:
They revised the contract last night. 他们昨晚修改了合同。
The editor revised the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前修改了手稿。


现在完成时:
I have revised my presentation several times. 我已经修改了我的演示文稿好几次了。
She has revised her opinion on the matter. 她已经改变了她对这件事的看法。


过去完成时:
He had revised the document before he sent it. 他发送文件之前已经修改了文件。
They had revised their strategy before the meeting. 他们在会议之前已经修改了他们的策略。


将来时:
We will revise the budget next week. 我们下周将修改预算。
She will revise her thesis before graduation. 她毕业前将修改她的论文。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would revise my essay again. 如果我有时间，我会再次修改我的论文。
I wish I had revised my application more carefully. 我希望我当时更仔细地修改了我的申请。


被动语态:
The document was revised by the team leader. 文件由团队领导修改。
The policy is being revised at the moment. 政策目前正在修改中。


疑问句:
Has the report been revised? 报告修改了吗？
Will the schedule be revised? 时间表会修改吗？


否定句:
He didn't revise his paper before handing it in. 他提交论文前没有修改他的论文。
They haven't revised their plans yet. 他们还没有修改他们的计划。
