out-beams:/ˈaʊtbiːmz/| v.|超越，胜过|To surpass or excel.|The new skyscraper out-beams all the other buildings in the city.  这座新摩天大楼超越了城市里所有其他的建筑物。|近义词：surpass, excel, overshadow|反义词：underperform, fall short


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He out-beams his rivals in terms of creativity. = 他在创造力方面胜过他的竞争对手。
The sun out-beams the moon in brightness. = 太阳的光芒胜过月亮。


一般过去时:
Her performance out-beamed all expectations. = 她的表演超出了所有人的预期。
The champion out-beamed his opponent in the final match. = 冠军在决赛中胜过了他的对手。


现在完成时:
His latest novel has out-beamed his previous works in terms of sales. = 他的最新小说在销量方面已经超过了他之前的作品。
The company has out-beamed its competitors in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额方面已经超越了它的竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, she had out-beamed all other runners. = 比赛结束时，她已经超过了所有其他跑步者。
The team had out-beamed their rivals in the previous games. = 这支球队在之前的比赛中已经战胜了他们的对手。


将来时:
The new technology will out-beam all existing methods. = 新技术将超越所有现有的方法。
We believe our product will out-beam the competition. = 我们相信我们的产品将超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he might out-beam his colleagues. = 如果他更努力工作，他可能会超过他的同事。
I wish I could out-beam my own self-doubt. = 我希望我能克服自己的自卑。


被动语态:
His achievements were out-beamed by his younger brother's. = 他的成就被他的弟弟超越了。
The old record was out-beamed by the new one. = 旧纪录被新纪录超越了。


疑问句:

Will the new strategy out-beam the old one? = 新战略会胜过旧战略吗？


否定句:
His efforts did not out-beam the challenges he faced. = 他的努力并没有克服他面临的挑战。
The small company could not out-beam the larger corporation. = 这家小公司无法超越更大的公司。
