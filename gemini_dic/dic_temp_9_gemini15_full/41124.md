coprophagous:/kɒˈprɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食粪的；粪食性的 |  Eating or feeding on dung or excrement.  Coprophagous animals play an important role in nutrient cycling within ecosystems.  | 例句：Many insects are coprophagous, helping to break down waste and recycle nutrients. = 许多昆虫是食粪的，有助于分解废物和回收养分。


Some beetles are coprophagous and consume animal dung. = 一些甲虫是食粪的，并以动物粪便为食。| 近义词：dung-eating, scatophagic | 反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous


词源:

* **copro-**:  From Greek *kopros* meaning "dung" or "excrement".
* **-phagous**: From Greek *-phagos* meaning "eating" or "devouring".

因此，coprophagous字面意思是“吃粪的”。
