highnesses:/haɪˈnəsɪz/| n. |殿下们，诸位殿下|The plural of "highness," a title of respect used to address or refer to royalty or other high-ranking individuals.  It is typically used in formal settings. |Their Royal Highnesses, the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge, visited the hospital.  = 剑桥公爵和公爵夫人殿下访问了医院。 The Highnesses were gracious hosts. = 诸位殿下是热情好客的主人。|royalty, dignitaries|commoners, subjects|名词
