intoxication:/ˌɪntɒksɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 中毒；醉酒；兴奋；陶醉 | The state of being intoxicated, especially by alcohol or drugs; a feeling of excitement, elation, or exhilaration.  |例句：The driver was arrested for driving under the influence of intoxication. 司机因酒后驾车被捕。

例句：Her intoxication with fame was evident in her extravagant lifestyle. 她对名利的陶醉在她的奢华生活方式中显而易见。


近义词：inebriation, inebriety, drunkenness, exhilaration | 反义词：sobriety, calmness


词源:  Comes from the verb "intoxicate," which itself originates from the Late Latin word "intoxicare," meaning "to poison."  This in turn is derived from the Latin word "toxicum," meaning "poison," ultimately from the Greek word "toxikon," referring to poison from arrows.
