haversack:/ˈhævəsæk/| n. |背包，旅行包 |A haversack is a type of bag, typically made of canvas or other strong material, used for carrying supplies or equipment, especially on a journey.  It often has a single strap to sling it over the shoulder. |He packed his haversack with food and water for the hike. 他把背包里装满了食物和水，准备去远足。|rucksack, knapsack, backpack |purse (in the sense of lacking a bag)|名词
