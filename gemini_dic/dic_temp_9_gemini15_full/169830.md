prosecuted:/ˈprɒsɪkjuːtɪd/ | v. | 起诉，检举 | To institute legal proceedings against (a person or organization).  |例句：The company was prosecuted for polluting the river. = 这家公司因污染河流而被起诉。

例句：He was prosecuted for tax evasion. = 他因逃税而被起诉。


近义词：indicted, sued, charged | 反义词：acquitted, exonerated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government prosecutes criminals. = 政府起诉罪犯。
She prosecutes cases of fraud. = 她起诉欺诈案件。

一般过去时:
The police prosecuted him for speeding. = 警方以超速为由起诉了他。
They prosecuted the case vigorously. = 他们大力起诉此案。

现在完成时:
The authorities have prosecuted several individuals involved in the scandal. = 当局已经起诉了参与丑闻的几个人。
They have prosecuted this case for many years. = 他们已经起诉这个案子很多年了。

过去完成时:
The judge had already prosecuted similar cases before. = 法官之前已经审理过类似的案件。
Before the trial, they had prosecuted him several times for minor offenses. = 在审判之前，他们曾几次以轻微罪名起诉他。

将来时:
The authorities will prosecute anyone found guilty. = 当局将起诉任何被判有罪的人。
They will prosecute the case next week. = 他们将在下周起诉此案。

虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would prosecute him. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会起诉他。
I wish the police would prosecute these vandals. = 我希望警方能起诉这些破坏分子。

被动语态:
He was prosecuted for theft. = 他因盗窃罪被起诉。
The case was prosecuted with great efficiency. = 此案审理效率很高。

疑问句:
Will he be prosecuted for his actions? = 他会因自己的行为而被起诉吗？
Has anyone been prosecuted in connection with this crime? = 有人因这起罪行而被起诉吗？

否定句:
They did not prosecute the case due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，他们没有起诉此案。
The company was not prosecuted despite the clear evidence of wrongdoing. = 尽管有明显的违规证据，该公司仍未被起诉。


PHRASE->
prosecute a case = 起诉一个案件
prosecute a war = 发动战争
prosecute an inquiry = 进行调查
prosecute an appeal = 提出上诉
prosecute with vigor = 积极地起诉


词源:
From Old French *prosecuter, from Latin prosequi "to follow up, pursue, prosecute," from pro- "forward" + sequi "to follow."  The legal sense developed in the 14th century.
