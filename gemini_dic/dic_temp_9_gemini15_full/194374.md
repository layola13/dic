self-preservation: /ˌsɛlfˌprɛzərˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 自我保护，自保 | The act of protecting oneself from harm or danger.  It often involves actions taken to ensure one's continued existence or well-being.  This can encompass physical, emotional, and social aspects. |  例句：The instinct for self-preservation is strong in most animals.  大多数动物都有很强的自我保护本能。

The bear's self-preservation instincts kicked in when it sensed danger. 熊在感觉到危险时本能地进行了自我保护。


近义词：survival, self-defense, protection | 反义词：self-destruction, self-sacrifice | 词性：名词
