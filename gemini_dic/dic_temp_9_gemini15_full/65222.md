etiology:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒi/|/ˌiːtiˈɑːlədʒi/| n. |病因学；病因；原因学 |The study of the causes or origins of diseases.  Etiology encompasses the factors that contribute to the development of a disease, including genetic predispositions, environmental exposures, and lifestyle choices. |例句：The etiology of Alzheimer's disease is still not fully understood. 阿尔茨海默病的病因尚未完全了解。

例句：Researchers are investigating the etiology of the recent outbreak of flu. 研究人员正在调查最近流感爆发的病因。

近义词：causation, pathogenesis, origin


反义词：cure, treatment, resolution


词性:名词
