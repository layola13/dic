midwinterly:/ˈmɪdˌwɪntərli/| adj.|仲冬的，隆冬的，严冬的|Characterized by or suggestive of midwinter; relating to the middle of winter.  It evokes a sense of cold, darkness, and the stillness often associated with the depth of winter.|例句：The midwinterly landscape was stark and beautiful.  仲冬的景色荒凉而美丽。
The air held a midwinterly chill.  空气中弥漫着仲冬的寒意。

近义词：wintery, wintry, hibernal

反义词：summery,estival
