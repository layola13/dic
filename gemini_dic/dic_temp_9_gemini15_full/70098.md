feverishly:/ˈfiːvərɪʃli/| adv.|狂热地；焦急地；兴奋地|In a feverish or agitated manner; with great energy or excitement, often to the point of being frantic.|例句：He worked feverishly to finish the project before the deadline. 他为了赶在最后期限前完成项目而拼命工作。

例句：She feverishly searched for her lost keys. 她焦急地寻找她丢失的钥匙。


近义词：frantically, frantically, excitedly, urgently|反义词：calmly, leisurely, slowly


词源:源于fever (发烧) + -ish (形容词后缀) + -ly (副词后缀)。最初指因发烧而产生的激动不安的状态，后来引申为更广泛的“狂热地”、“焦急地”等含义。
