rambutans:/ˈræmˌbʊtænz/| n. |红毛丹|Rambutans are small, oval fruits with a reddish, hairy rind and sweet, translucent white flesh. They are native to Southeast Asia and are popular throughout the region. |例句：The rambutan trees were laden with ripe, red fruit. 红毛丹树上挂满了成熟的红色果实。  |例句：I bought some fresh rambutans at the market. 我在市场上买了一些新鲜的红毛丹。|近义词：lychee, mangosteen|反义词：无
