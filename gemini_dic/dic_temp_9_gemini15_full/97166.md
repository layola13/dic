infirmarian:/ˌɪnfɜːrˈmeəriən/| n.|医务人员，保健员|A person who cares for the sick or infirm, especially in a hospital or similar institution.  The term is now largely obsolete, replaced by more specific titles like nurse or medical assistant. |例句：The infirmarian tended to the patients' needs with care and compassion.  医务人员细心体贴地照顾病人的需要。|近义词：nurse, medical assistant, caregiver |反义词：patient
