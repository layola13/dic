sector:/ˈsɛktər/| n. |部门；领域；扇形；区域|A sector is a part of a circle between two radii. It can also refer to a particular area of activity, business, or society.|例句：The finance sector is booming. 金融业正在蓬勃发展。|例句：The pizza was cut into eight sectors. 比萨被切成八块扇形。|近义词：area, division, segment, field|反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company operates in several sectors. = 这家公司在几个领域运营。
This sector contributes significantly to the economy. = 这个部门对经济贡献巨大。

一般过去时:
That sector experienced rapid growth last year. = 去年该部门经历了快速增长。
The agricultural sector faced many challenges. = 农业部门面临诸多挑战。

现在完成时:
The technology sector has undergone a major transformation. = 科技行业已经经历了重大变革。
This sector has attracted significant investment. = 这个部门吸引了大量投资。

过去完成时:
Before the crisis, the banking sector had been very profitable. = 危机爆发前，银行业一直非常有利可图。
The construction sector had already declined before the recession hit. = 在经济衰退来临之前，建筑业就已经开始衰退了。

将来时:
The energy sector will face significant changes in the coming years. = 能源行业将在未来几年面临重大变化。
This sector will likely see increased competition. = 这个部门可能会面临日益激烈的竞争。

虚拟语气:
If the government invested more, this sector would thrive. = 如果政府加大投资，这个部门将会蓬勃发展。
I wish the agricultural sector had received more support. = 我希望农业部门能得到更多支持。

被动语态:
This sector is heavily regulated by the government. = 这个部门受到政府的严格监管。
The sector was significantly impacted by the pandemic. = 该部门受到了疫情的严重影响。

疑问句:
Which sector are you working in? = 你从事哪个行业？
What sector is most affected by climate change? = 受气候变化影响最大的部门是哪个？

否定句:
That sector is not performing well. = 那个部门表现不佳。
The company does not operate in that sector. = 这家公司不在那个领域运营。
