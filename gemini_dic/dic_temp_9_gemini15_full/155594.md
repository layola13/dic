philosopher:/fɪˈlɒsəfər/ | n. |哲学家 |A person who studies or writes about philosophy; someone engaged in philosophical inquiry.  |Example: Socrates is considered one of the greatest philosophers of ancient Greece. 苏格拉底被认为是古希腊最伟大的哲学家之一。 |近义词:thinker, scholar, sage |反义词:ignoramus, simpleton
