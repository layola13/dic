greed:/ɡriːd/ | n. |贪婪，贪心 | excessive desire to acquire or possess more than one needs or deserves.  It often involves a selfish desire for wealth, power, or food. |例句：His greed led him to ruin. 他的贪婪导致了他破产。

例句：Her greed for power was insatiable. 她对权力的贪婪是无法满足的。


近义词：avarice, covetousness, rapacity, cupidity | 反义词：generosity, altruism, selflessness
