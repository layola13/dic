midwife:/ˈmɪdwɪf/| n.|助产士，稳婆|A midwife is a person, typically a woman, trained to assist women in childbirth.  They provide care to pregnant women and newborns, often working in hospitals, birthing centers, or in the woman's home. |例句：The midwife helped the mother deliver her baby safely.  助产士帮助母亲安全分娩。|例句：She chose to have a midwife attend her home birth. 她选择在家分娩，并请了一位助产士。|近义词：obstetrician (医生), birth attendant|反义词：无
