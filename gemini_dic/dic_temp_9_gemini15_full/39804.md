consortiums:/kənˈsɔːrʃiəm/| n.|财团；协会；联合体|A consortium is an association of two or more individuals, companies, organizations or governments that is formed for the purpose of pursuing a common goal.  Consortiums are often used for large-scale projects that require significant resources and expertise.|例句：Several companies formed a consortium to bid on the construction project.  几个公司组成了一个财团来竞标这个建筑项目。|近义词：group, alliance, partnership|反义词：individual, solitary
