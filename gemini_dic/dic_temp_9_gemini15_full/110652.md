/lɪŋk/ | n. | 链接，联系 | A link is a connection between two things.  It can be physical, like a chain link, or metaphorical, like a link between two ideas. | Example:  There's a link between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间存在联系。  The two cities are linked by a railway. 这两座城市由铁路连接。 |近义词：connection, bond, tie |反义词：separation, disconnection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chain links together securely.  这条链子牢固地连接在一起。
This website links to many other resources.  这个网站链接到许多其他资源。

一般过去时:
He linked the two events together in his mind. 他在他的脑海里将这两件事联系起来。
The researchers linked the disease to a specific gene. 研究人员将这种疾病与特定基因联系起来。

现在完成时:
They have linked the suspect to the crime. 他们已经将嫌疑犯与罪行联系起来。
Scientists have linked climate change to rising sea levels. 科学家们已经将气候变化与海平面上升联系起来。

过去完成时:
She had linked the two stories before she realized their connection. 在她意识到两者之间的联系之前，她已经将这两个故事联系起来。
The police had linked the burglaries to the same suspect. 警方已经将这些入室盗窃案与同一个嫌疑犯联系起来。

将来时:
The bridge will link the two islands. 这座桥将连接这两个岛屿。
The company will link its systems for better efficiency. 公司将连接其系统以提高效率。

虚拟语气:
If the two countries were linked by a stronger alliance, there would be less conflict. 如果两国由更强大的联盟联系起来，冲突就会减少。
I wish they had linked the relevant documents together. 我希望他们把相关的文件连接在一起。

被动语态:
The two buildings are linked by a covered walkway. 这两座建筑物由一条有顶棚的走道连接。
The database is linked to the accounting system. 数据库与会计系统相连。

疑问句:
Does this paragraph link well to the previous one?  这部分与前一部分衔接得好吗？
How are the two concepts linked? 这两个概念是如何联系起来的？

否定句:
The two ideas are not logically linked. 这两个想法在逻辑上没有联系。
The study failed to link the two variables. 这项研究未能将这两个变量联系起来。
