nondisposable:/ˌnɒndɪˈspoʊzəbəl/| adj. |不可丢弃的；一次性用品以外的|Not designed to be thrown away after use; reusable.  The term often contrasts with "disposable," highlighting the product's durability and intended longevity.  |例句：This is a nondisposable diaper, you can wash and reuse it. 这是一款可重复使用的尿布，你可以清洗后重复使用。

例句： We need to invest in nondisposable containers to reduce waste.我们需要投资购买不可丢弃的容器来减少浪费。


近义词：reusable, recyclable, durable, permanent

反义词：disposable, transient, ephemeral


词性：形容词

词源:
The word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "disposable" (meaning "intended to be thrown away after use").  "Disposable" itself comes from the verb "dispose," meaning "to get rid of," ultimately deriving from Old French *disposer.
