confessers:/kənˈfɛsərz/| n.|忏悔者；承认者|Those who confess, typically religious individuals who confess their sins to a priest or other religious authority.  It can also refer to those who admit to a crime or wrongdoing.|例句：The confessers lined up to receive absolution.  忏悔者排队接受赦免。|例句：The confessers were granted immunity in exchange for their testimony. 承认者被给予豁免权以换取他们的证词。|近义词：penitents, admitters, declarers |反义词：deniers, concealers|名词
