principal:/ˈprɪnsɪpəl/| adj. n.|主要的；校长；本金|

英文解析:As an adjective, "principal" means first in importance, rank, or degree. As a noun, it refers to the head of a school or the main amount of a debt or investment.  It can also refer to a fundamental truth or proposition.

例句:

The principal reason for his resignation was stress.  他辞职的主要原因是压力。

The principal of the school addressed the students. 学校校长向学生们讲话。


近义词:main, chief, primary, headmaster, headmistress, capital (as a noun)

反义词:secondary, minor, subordinate


词性:adjective, noun
