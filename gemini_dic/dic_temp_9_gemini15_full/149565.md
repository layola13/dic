pagod:/ˈpæɡəd/| n. |宝塔，佛塔|A pagoda is a tiered tower, usually a Buddhist temple.  Pagodas are typically found in East and Southeast Asia, and are often ornate and multi-storied structures.  They are used for religious purposes, and often house relics or statues of Buddha.|例句：The ancient pagoda stood majestically on the hill. 这座古老的宝塔雄伟地矗立在山上。|例句：We visited a beautiful pagoda in Kyoto. 我们参观了京都一座美丽的宝塔。|近义词：temple, shrine, stupa|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a secular building.
