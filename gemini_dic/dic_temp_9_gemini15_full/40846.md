coo's:/kuːz/| n. |咕咕声，(鸽子等的) 柔和的叫声 |The soft sounds made by doves or similar birds.  |例句：The coo's of the pigeons filled the square.  鸽子的咕咕声充满了广场。|例句：I love listening to the coo's of the turtledoves in the evening. 我喜欢在晚上听斑鸠的咕咕声。|近义词：murmur, purr, cooing |反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doves coo constantly. = 鸽子不停地咕咕叫。
The baby coos at its mother. = 婴儿对着母亲发出咕咕声。

一般过去时:
The pigeons cooed softly all night. = 鸽子整夜轻轻地咕咕叫。
The baby cooed happily in its crib. = 婴儿在摇篮里高兴地发出咕咕声。

The birds have cooed all morning. = 鸟儿整个上午都在咕咕叫。

过去完成时:
