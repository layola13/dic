precertifying:/ˌpriːsɜːrˈtɪfaɪɪŋ/| v. |预先认证，预先批准 |The act of certifying something in advance.  It implies a process of verification or approval that takes place before the main event or process.  |例句：The precertifying process for the new software took several weeks. 预先认证新软件的过程耗费了数周时间。

The bank requires precertifying all large transactions. 银行要求预先认证所有大额交易。


近义词：pre-approving, pre-authorizing


反义词：post-certifying, ratifying (after the fact)

词性：动词
