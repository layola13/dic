copse:/kɒps/|n.|小树林，矮树丛|A copse is a small group of trees smaller than a wood or forest.  It is often dense and tangled.|例句：We took a shortcut through the copse. 我们抄近路穿过小树林。|例句：A small copse of birch trees stood beside the river. 河边矗立着一小片桦树林。|近义词：grove, thicket, woodlet|反义词：forest, woodland
