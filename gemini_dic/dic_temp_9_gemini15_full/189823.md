salving:/ˈsælvɪŋ/| v.|涂药膏；治疗；缓解；补救|The act of applying a salve or ointment to a wound or sore;  to soothe or relieve pain, distress, or a difficult situation; to rescue or salvage something from a bad situation. |例句：She was salving the burn with a soothing ointment. 她正在用舒缓的药膏涂抹烧伤处。

例句：He tried to salve his conscience by donating to charity. 他试图通过捐款来抚慰自己的良心。


近义词：healing, soothing, relieving, remedying, rescuing

反义词：aggravating, worsening, harming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He salves his wounds every night. = 他每晚都涂药膏治疗伤口。
She salves her anxieties with yoga. = 她通过瑜伽来缓解焦虑。

一般过去时:

虚拟语气:
