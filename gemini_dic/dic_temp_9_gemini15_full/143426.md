outflies:/ˌaʊtˈflaɪz/| v.|超过（在飞行速度或距离上）|to fly faster or further than (something or someone)|The eagle outflies the hawk.  老鹰比鹰飞得快。|The jet outflew all other aircraft.  这架喷气式飞机比所有其他飞机飞得都远。|outpaces, surpasses|lags behind


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bird outflies all others in the flock.  这只鸟比鸟群里其他鸟飞得都快。
He consistently outflies his competitors in air races. 他在航空比赛中总是比他的竞争对手飞得更远。


一般过去时:
The plane outflew the storm.  飞机飞过了风暴。
She outflew her opponent in the final race.  在决赛中，她比对手飞得更远。


现在完成时:
The new model has outflown all previous versions.  新款已经超过了所有以前的版本。
He has outflown every other pilot in the competition. 他在比赛中比其他每位飞行员飞得都远。


过去完成时:
The old plane had outflown many other aircraft in its time. 这架旧飞机在其服役期间超过了许多其他飞机。
By the time they reached the finish line, she had outflown all her rivals. 当他们到达终点线时，她已经超过了所有对手。


将来时:
I predict that this new aircraft will outfly all others. 我预测这款新型飞机将超过所有其他飞机。
They expect the new design to outfly its predecessor. 他们预计新设计将超过其前身。


虚拟语气:
If only I could outfly the wind! 要是我能飞得比风还快就好了！
I wish I could outfly my worries. 我希望我能摆脱我的烦恼。


被动语态:
The old record was outflown by a young pilot.  旧记录被一位年轻飞行员打破了。
In the race, he was outflown by a competitor from another team. 在比赛中，他被另一个队的竞争对手超过了。


疑问句:
Does this plane outfly the others? 这架飞机比其他飞机飞得快吗？
Can this new engine outfly the old one?  这个新引擎能超过旧引擎吗？


否定句:
The small plane did not outfly the larger one.  小型飞机没有超过大型飞机。
Unfortunately, our team did not outfly the opposing team. 不幸的是，我们的团队没有超过对方团队。
