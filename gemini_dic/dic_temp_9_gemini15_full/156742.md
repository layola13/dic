piepoudre:/ˈpiːpʊdr/| n.|尘土法庭，即决法庭|A court of summary jurisdiction formerly held in fairs and markets to deal with disputes arising there.  The name derives from the dusty conditions of such courts.|例句：The piepoudre court was known for its swift justice.  尘土法庭以其迅速的审判而闻名。|近义词：summary court, market court|反义词：high court, supreme court
