repossess:/ˌriːpəˈsɛs/ | vt. |收回，取回，收回（财产） | To take back possession of (something, especially property) that has been lent, sold, or pledged as security.  | The bank repossessed his car because he failed to make the payments.  银行因为他未能按时付款而收回了他的汽车。 | reclaim, retrieve, recover | relinquish, surrender, cede | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank repossesses cars regularly. = 银行定期收回汽车。
She repossesses her old toys. = 她收回她旧的玩具。

一般过去时:
They repossessed the house last week. = 他们上周收回了房子。
The landlord repossessed the apartment. = 房东收回了公寓。

现在完成时:
The company has repossessed several vehicles this month. = 这个月公司已经收回了多辆车。
He has repossessed the land after a long legal battle. = 在一场漫长的法律诉讼后，他收回了土地。

过去完成时:
The bank had already repossessed the property before he could pay. =  在他能够付款之前，银行已经收回了房产。
She had repossessed the painting before the auction. = 拍卖之前，她已经取回了画作。

将来时:
They will repossess the equipment if the payments are not made. = 如果不付款，他们将收回设备。
The lender will repossess the house next month. = 贷款人下个月将收回房屋。

虚拟语气:
If he didn't pay, the bank would repossess his car. = 如果他不付款，银行就会收回他的汽车。
I wish they hadn't repossessed my furniture. = 我真希望他们没有收回我的家具。

被动语态:
His car was repossessed by the bank. = 他的车被银行收回了。
The house is being repossessed due to unpaid mortgage. = 由于未偿还抵押贷款，房子正在被收回。

疑问句:
Will the bank repossess the property? = 银行会收回房产吗？
Has the landlord repossessed the apartment yet? = 房东已经收回公寓了吗？

否定句:
They did not repossess the vehicle. = 他们没有收回车辆。
The company will not repossess the equipment this time. = 公司这次不会收回设备。
