realign:/ˌriːəˈlaɪn/| vt.|重新调整，重新排列，使…重新一致|To adjust or arrange (something) again, especially in a more accurate or effective way.  It implies a change to improve something that was previously misaligned or inefficient.|例句：The company decided to realign its marketing strategy to better target its customers. 公司决定重新调整其营销策略，以便更好地定位其客户。

例句：The government is trying to realign its economic policies to stimulate growth. 政府正在努力调整其经济政策以刺激增长。


近义词：rearrange, readjust, realign, reorganize |反义词：misalign, disorganize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We realign our strategies every quarter. = 我们每个季度都会重新调整我们的策略。
They realign the furniture in the office. = 他们重新排列办公室的家具。


一般过去时:
The team realigned its priorities after the setback. = 团队在遭遇挫折后重新调整了优先级。
She realigned the images for better presentation. = 她重新排列图像以获得更好的演示效果。


现在完成时:
The company has realigned its organizational structure. = 公司已经重新调整了其组织结构。
We have realigned our goals to better reflect the market demands. = 我们已经重新调整了我们的目标，以更好地反映市场需求。


过去完成时:
They had realigned their resources before launching the new product. = 他们在推出新产品之前已经重新分配了资源。
The company had realigned its marketing budget before the campaign started. = 公司在活动开始之前已经重新调整了营销预算。


将来时:
We will realign our production schedule next month. = 我们下个月将重新调整我们的生产计划。
They will realign the equipment to increase efficiency. = 他们将重新调整设备以提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If we realigned our processes, we would be more efficient. = 如果我们重新调整流程，效率会更高。
I wish they had realigned the budget earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新调整预算。


被动语态:
The marketing strategy was realigned to focus on younger consumers. = 营销策略进行了重新调整，以专注于年轻消费者。
The production line was realigned to improve output. = 生产线进行了重新调整以提高产量。


疑问句:
Will they realign the schedule? = 他们会重新调整时间表吗？
Have they realigned their priorities? = 他们是否重新调整了优先级？


否定句:
The company did not realign its resources effectively. = 公司没有有效地重新分配资源。
We will not realign our strategy without a thorough analysis. = 我们不会在没有进行彻底分析的情况下重新调整我们的策略。


PHRASE->
realign the economy = 调整经济
realign the political landscape = 改变政治格局
realign the company's structure = 重组公司架构
realign the priorities = 重新安排优先顺序
realign the budget = 重新调整预算

词源:
re- (prefix indicating again or anew) + align (to bring into line or agreement)  来自法语"aligner" (使成一直线), 最终来自拉丁语 "ad" (to) + "linea" (line).
