bustiness:/ˈbʌstinəs/| n. |繁忙；热闹；喧嚣|The bustling energy of the city was infectious.  城市的喧嚣活力具有感染力。|近义词：activity, hustle, bustle|反义词：quiet, stillness, calm|

英文解析:Bustiness refers to a state of being busy, lively, and full of activity, often with a sense of excitement or commotion. It implies a vibrant and energetic atmosphere.


例句:

The bustiness of the marketplace was overwhelming. 市场上的喧嚣令人难以招架。

The restaurant was filled with the bustiness of lunchtime diners. 餐厅里挤满了午餐就餐者的喧嚣。


词源:

Derived from the word "bustle," meaning to move about in a busy and energetic way.  "Bustle" itself has roots in Middle English and is likely related to words suggesting movement or activity.
