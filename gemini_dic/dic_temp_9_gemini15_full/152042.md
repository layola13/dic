patellae:/pəˈtɛliː/| n.|髌骨（复数）|The patellae are the kneecaps; small, flat, triangular bones that protect the knee joint.  They are essential for effective knee extension.|例句：The physiotherapist examined the patient's patellae for any signs of damage. 理疗师检查了病人的髌骨是否有任何损伤迹象。|例句：  During the fall, he fractured both his patellae.  在跌倒时，他摔断了两个髌骨。|近义词：kneecaps|反义词：无
