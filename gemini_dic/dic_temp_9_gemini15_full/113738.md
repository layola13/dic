macrofauna:/ˈmækroʊfɔːnə/| n. |大型动物群落，大型底栖动物|Animals large enough to be seen without magnification, typically referring to organisms in a specific habitat, such as the benthic macrofauna of a lake or ocean floor.| 例句：The researchers analyzed the macrofauna composition of the sediment core.  研究人员分析了沉积岩芯中的大型动物群落组成。| 例句： Changes in the macrofauna community structure indicated environmental pollution. 大型动物群落结构的变化表明了环境污染。|近义词：megafauna |反义词：microfauna
