non-endemic: /ˌnɒnɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 非地方性的；非流行性的 | Not regularly found in a particular region or population; not endemic.  | Example:  The disease is non-endemic in this country, meaning it doesn't occur regularly here.  这个疾病在这个国家是非地方性的，意味着它不会经常在这里发生。

| Example: The non-endemic species was introduced accidentally.  这个非地方性的物种是意外引入的。| 近义词: exotic, introduced, alien | 反义词: endemic, indigenous, native |
