nonsyndication: /ˌnɒnˌsɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 非联合发行；非同步发行 | The absence of syndication; the state of not being syndicated.  This term refers to the practice of not distributing content or programming through a syndication network.  It implies that the material is only available through its original source. | Example:  The show enjoyed high ratings but remained in nonsyndication for years. 这部剧收视率很高，但多年来一直没有联合发行。 | 近义词:  exclusive distribution, non-syndicated distribution | 反义词: syndication
