reborn:/ˌriːˈbɔːrn/| adj. v. |重生；复生的；革新的|

英文解析:  Born again; experiencing a renewal or revival.  It can refer to a spiritual rebirth, a personal transformation, or the revival of something that was previously considered dead or obsolete. As a verb, it means to be born again or to experience a significant transformation.


例句:

He felt reborn after his recovery from a serious illness. = 他在大病初愈后感觉自己重生了。

The company was reborn with a new CEO and a revised business strategy. = 在有了新的首席执行官和修改后的商业战略后，这家公司焕发了新生。


近义词: regenerated, revitalized, resurrected, renewed


反义词: deceased, dead, extinct


词性:  adjective, verb
