nonpalatable: /ˌnɒnˈpælətəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnˈpælətəbəl/ | adj. |令人不愉快的；难以下咽的；不受欢迎的 | Unpleasant to the taste or mind; distasteful.  Something that is nonpalatable is not pleasant to eat or drink, or is not acceptable or agreeable. |

例句：

The medicine had a nonpalatable taste.  = 这药味道难以下咽。

His proposal was deemed nonpalatable to the board. = 他的提案被董事会认为难以接受。


近义词：unpleasant, distasteful, disagreeable, offensive, repulsive, unacceptable

反义词：pleasant, palatable, agreeable, delicious, welcome


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **palatable**:  adjective meaning "pleasant to the taste"  (from Latin *palatum* "palate")

用法说明:

nonpalatable 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某种事物令人不愉快或难以接受，既可以指食物的味道，也可以指意见、建议、计划等。
