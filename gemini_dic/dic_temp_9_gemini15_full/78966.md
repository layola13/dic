girlie:/ˈɡɜːli/| n. adj.|女孩；少女；女性的；轻浮的|A young woman or girl;  often used in a slightly condescending or informal way.  It can also describe something considered to be feminine or associated with girls. |例句：She's such a girlie girl, always wearing pink. 她真是个小女生，总是穿着粉色。

例句：That's a bit girlie for my taste. 这对我来说有点女性化了。


近义词：girl, lass, woman,  (adj.) feminine, girly


反义词： (n.) man, boy  (adj.) masculine, manly


词性:名词，形容词
