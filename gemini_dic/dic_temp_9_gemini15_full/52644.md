digitalises: /ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |数字化；使...数字化 | To convert information into a digital format.  This typically involves converting analog data (such as images, sound, or text) into a series of binary digits (0s and 1s) that can be processed by a computer. |例句：The library is digitalising its entire collection. 图书馆正在将其全部藏书数字化。

The company digitalised its records to improve efficiency.  这家公司将其记录数字化以提高效率。|近义词：convert, digitize |反义词：analogize
