词源:
From Late Latin *dogmatismus, from Greek dogma ("opinion, doctrine, tenet"), from dokein ("to seem, to think, to appear").  The suffix "-ism" denotes a system, practice, or doctrine.  Therefore, dogmatism literally means "the system of doctrines."
