frightened:/ˈfraɪtnd/| adj.|害怕的，受惊的| feeling fear or anxiety; scared.|The child was frightened by the loud noise.  孩子被巨大的噪音吓到了。|She was frightened to go out alone at night. 她害怕晚上独自外出。|scared, terrified, alarmed|calm, brave, unafraid|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is frightened of spiders. 他害怕蜘蛛。
The dog is frightened by the fireworks.  这只狗被烟花吓到了。


一般过去时:
I was frightened by the sudden storm.  我被突如其来的暴风雨吓到了。
She was frightened when she saw the snake. 她看到蛇的时候吓坏了。


现在完成时:
I have been frightened many times in my life. 我一生中被吓到过很多次。
He has been frightened by dogs ever since he was a child. 从小他就害怕狗。


过去完成时:
I had been frightened before, so I knew what to expect. 我以前被吓过，所以我预料到了。
She had been frightened by the news, so she couldn't sleep. 她被这个消息吓坏了，所以睡不着觉。


将来时:
I will be frightened if I see a ghost. 如果我看到鬼，我会害怕。
She will be frightened if the dog barks at her. 如果狗冲她叫，她会害怕。


虚拟语气:
If I were frightened, I would run away. 如果我害怕，我会跑掉。
I wish I weren't frightened of heights. 我希望我不恐高。


被动语态:
(被动语态在此词语中不常用，因为frightened本身就是被动含义)


疑问句:
Are you frightened of the dark? 你怕黑吗？
Is he frightened by the thunder? 他被雷声吓到了吗？


否定句:
I am not frightened of snakes. 我不怕蛇。
He was not frightened by the dog's barking. 他没有被狗叫声吓到。


PHRASE->
be frightened of: 害怕...
frightened to death: 吓得要死
frightened stiff: 吓得僵硬
absolutely frightened: 非常害怕
terribly frightened: 非常害怕
