goshawk:/ˈɡɒs.hɔːk/| n. |游隼，苍鹰|A goshawk is a large bird of prey with a short, broad tail and rounded wings, known for its speed and agility in hunting.|例句：The goshawk swooped down and snatched the rabbit. 苍鹰俯冲下来抓住了兔子。  The goshawk is a powerful hunter in the forest. 苍鹰是森林里强大的猎手。|近义词：hawk, falcon, eagle|反义词：无
