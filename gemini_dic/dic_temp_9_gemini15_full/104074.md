It often refers to a fascination with unusually large or oversized objects, animals, or events.|例句：The town's annual jumbomania festival featured a giant parade of oversized floats and costumes. 这个小镇一年一度的狂欢节以巨型彩车和服装组成的盛大游行作为特色。|近义词：gigantomania, megalomania|反义词：micromania
