confessionalist:/kənˈfɛʃənəlɪst/| n. |忏悔者；自白者；坦白者 |A confessionalist is someone who practices confession, either as a religious act or as a way of self-expression.  They may be a person who regularly confesses their sins to a priest, or a writer or artist who openly reveals personal details in their work. |Example: The author's confessionalist style made the book both compelling and unsettling.  这位作者坦白直率的写作风格使这本书既引人入胜又令人不安。|近义词：penitent, self-revealer |反义词：secretive person, reserved person
