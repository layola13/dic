helminthic:/hɛlˈmɪnθɪk/| adj.|蠕虫的；寄生虫的|Relating to or caused by helminths (parasitic worms).  The term encompasses diseases caused by a variety of parasitic worms, including nematodes, cestodes, and trematodes.  |例句：The child's lethargy was diagnosed as a helminthic infection.  这个孩子的嗜睡被诊断为蠕虫感染。

例句：The research focused on the prevalence of helminthic diseases in the region. 这项研究关注该地区蠕虫病的流行情况。


近义词：parasitic, worm-borne

反义词：sterile, germ-free


词源:From "helminth" + "-ic".  "Helminth" comes from the Greek word "hélmins," meaning "worm."
