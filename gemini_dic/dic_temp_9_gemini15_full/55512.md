distressed:/dɪˈstrest/ | adj. | 痛苦的；忧虑的；境况艰难的 | Feeling extreme anxiety, sorrow, or pain.  It can also describe something worn or damaged. |例句：She was deeply distressed by the news of her father's illness. 她听到她父亲生病的消息后非常痛苦。

The old building was distressed, showing signs of age and neglect. 这座古老的建筑破旧不堪，显露出年久失修的迹象。|近义词：upset, worried, anxious, troubled, afflicted, damaged |反义词：calm, relaxed, peaceful, happy, comfortable, undamaged

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is distressed by the recent events. 他对最近发生的事件感到苦恼。
She is always distressed when she's alone. 她独自一人时总是感到焦虑。


一般过去时:
He was distressed to hear about the accident. 他听到事故的消息后感到非常难过。
She was deeply distressed after losing her job. 失去工作后，她非常痛苦。


现在完成时:
I have been distressed by your behavior. 我一直对你行为感到苦恼。
They have been distressed since their move. 他们搬家后一直很焦虑。


过去完成时:
She had been distressed for weeks before she finally sought help. 在她最终寻求帮助之前，她已经焦虑了好几个星期。
He had been distressed about his failing health for months.  几个月来，他一直为自己的健康状况恶化而焦虑。


将来时:
I will be distressed if I fail the exam. 如果考试不及格，我会很焦虑。
She will be distressed when she hears the news. 当她听到这个消息时，她会很痛苦。


虚拟语气:
I would be distressed if anything happened to my family. 如果我的家人发生什么事，我会非常痛苦。
If I were in her shoes, I would be distressed. 如果我处在她的位置，我会很焦虑。


被动语态:
He was distressed by the criticism. 他受到了批评而感到苦恼。（被动语态较少用于此词）
The fabric was distressed to give it an antique look. 布料经过做旧处理，使其具有古董外观。(被动语态在此处更常见)


疑问句:
Are you distressed about something? 你是不是有什么烦心事？
Is she distressed by the situation? 她对这种情况感到焦虑吗？


否定句:
He wasn't distressed by the noise. 他并不被噪音所打扰。
She wasn't distressed at all by the news. 她对这个消息一点也不难过。
