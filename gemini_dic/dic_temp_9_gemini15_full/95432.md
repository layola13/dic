incinerators:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtərz/ | n. |焚烧炉；垃圾焚烧场 | A device or place used for incinerating waste.  Incinerators burn waste at high temperatures, reducing its volume and destroying harmful substances. |The city council is considering building a new incinerator to deal with the growing waste problem.  市议会正在考虑建造一座新的焚烧炉来处理日益严重的垃圾问题。| cremators, furnaces | landfills, recycling plants | 名词
