over-sensitize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsaɪz/ | vt. | 使过度敏感；使过于敏感 | To make someone or something excessively sensitive.  This often implies making someone react too strongly or negatively to stimuli that wouldn't normally cause such a reaction.  | 例句：The constant negative feedback over-sensitized her to criticism.  持续的负面反馈使她对批评过于敏感。

The media's over-sensitized the public to the dangers of the virus. 媒体夸大了病毒的危险性，使公众过于敏感。| 近义词：hypersensitive, overreact, overstimulate | 反义词：desensitize, toughen, harden |
