prerequisite:/ˌpriːˈrɛkwɪzɪt/ | n. |先决条件，必备条件 | A prerequisite is something that must exist or happen before something else can exist or happen.  It is a condition that must be fulfilled before something else can be done or happen. |

Example 1:  A college degree is a prerequisite for many jobs.  | 大学学位是许多工作的先决条件。

Example 2:  Passing the exam is a prerequisite to graduation. | 通过考试是毕业的先决条件。


近义词：requirement, precondition, necessity, essential


反义词：nonessential, optional, unnecessary


词性：名词
