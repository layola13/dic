pleases:/ˈpliːziz/ | v. | 请；使高兴 |  The word "pleases" is the third-person singular simple present tense form of the verb "please,"  meaning to make someone happy or satisfied, or to be agreeable or acceptable. It can also function as an imperative, meaning "please do something". |  例句：He pleases his parents by studying hard. 他努力学习使父母高兴。  The color scheme pleases the eye.  这个配色方案赏心悦目。| 近义词：gratifies, delights, satisfies | 反义词：displeases, offends, annoys | 动词
