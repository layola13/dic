popper:/ˈpɒpə(r)/, /ˈpɑːpər/| n., v.|砰的一声；爆竹；流行乐；炫耀者；轻率的人；（使）发出爆裂声|A popper is something that makes a popping sound, or a person who shows off or boasts.  It can also refer to a type of firework or a genre of music.|例句：The champagne cork popped with a loud bang. 香槟酒瓶塞砰的一声开了。The firecrackers were popping all night long.  鞭炮整夜都在噼啪作响。|近义词：cracker, firecracker,  show-off,  boaster|反义词：quiet person, humble person


USAGE->

The champagne cork pops with a loud bang. = 香槟酒瓶塞砰的一声开了。
He pops balloons for the kids. = 他为孩子们扎破气球。
