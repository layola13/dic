pickerel:/ˈpɪkərəl/| n.|梭鱼，狗鱼|A pickerel is a small, slender freshwater fish of the pike family, known for its aggressive predatory nature and elongated body.  They are popular game fish. |例句：He caught a large pickerel in the lake. 他在湖里钓到一条大梭鱼。|例句：The pickerel darted away as the fisherman cast his line.  渔夫抛线时，梭鱼迅速逃走了。|近义词：pike (a larger relative),  jackfish|反义词：None directly applicable.
