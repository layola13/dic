orbitals:/ˈɔːrbɪtəlz/| n. |轨道（原子轨道）|Regions of space around an atom's nucleus where there is a high probability of finding an electron.  Orbitals are described by quantum numbers and have specific shapes and energy levels.|  Electrons occupy orbitals within an atom's electron shell.|电子占据原子电子壳层内的轨道。|atomic orbitals, molecular orbitals|nucleus, particle.
