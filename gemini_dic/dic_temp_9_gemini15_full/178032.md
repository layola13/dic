reckons with:/ˈrɛkənz wɪð/| v. |认真对待；处理；面对；考虑|to consider something seriously and take it into account; to deal with a difficult or challenging situation or person|The company reckons with the possibility of a recession.  这家公司正在认真考虑经济衰退的可能性。|He finally reckoned with his past mistakes. 他最终直面了他过去的错误。|deal with, confront, address, consider|ignore, neglect, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reckons with the challenges of his new job. 他正在认真对待新工作的挑战。
She reckons with the possibility of failure. 她正在考虑失败的可能性。


一般过去时:
They reckoned with the difficult situation and found a solution. 他们认真对待了困境并找到了解决办法。
He reckoned with his fear and faced it head-on. 他直面了自己的恐惧。


现在完成时:
She has reckoned with her grief and is moving on. 她已经处理好了悲伤，并且正在继续生活。
We have reckoned with the consequences of our actions. 我们已经承担了我们行为的后果。


过去完成时:
He had reckoned with the potential problems before starting the project. 他在开始项目之前就已经考虑到了潜在的问题。
They had reckoned with the opposition's strategy before the debate. 他们在辩论之前就已经考虑到了对手的策略。


将来时:
We will reckon with the results of the election next week. 我们下周将认真对待选举结果。
She will reckon with the criticism she received. 她将认真对待她收到的批评。


虚拟语气:
If he reckoned with his mistakes earlier, things would have been different. 如果他早点正视自己的错误，情况就会有所不同。
I wish I had reckoned with the potential risks beforehand. 我真希望我之前已经考虑到了潜在的风险。


被动语态:
The problem must be reckoned with immediately. 这个问题必须立即得到处理。
The consequences of their actions were reckoned with later. 他们行为的后果后来得到了处理。


疑问句:
Does she reckon with the challenges ahead? 她是否正在认真对待未来的挑战？
How will he reckon with the criticism? 他将如何应对批评？


否定句:
He didn't reckon with the unexpected delays. 他没有考虑到意外的延误。
They didn't reckon with the strength of their opponent. 他们没有考虑到对手的实力。
