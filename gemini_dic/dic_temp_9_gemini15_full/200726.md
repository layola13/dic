sleeting:/ˈsliːtɪŋ/| n. |雨夹雪；冻雨 |The phenomenon of rain and snow falling simultaneously, or rain freezing as it falls.  It is characterized by a mixture of rain and snow, or by rain that freezes upon contact with surfaces. |例句：The sleeting made driving conditions extremely hazardous. 雨夹雪使得驾驶环境极其危险。
例句： We experienced several hours of continuous sleeting before the storm finally broke.  暴风雨最终停止前，我们经历了数小时的持续雨夹雪。|近义词：freezing rain, snow mixed with rain|反义词：sunny, clear

Sleeting often occurs in winter. = 雨夹雪经常发生在冬天。
