chalkstone:/ˈtʃɔːkstoʊn/| n. |白垩石；石灰岩|A chalkstone is a nodule or concretion of calcium carbonate, often found in chalk formations.  They are typically hard and white or light-colored. |例句：The farmer found several chalkstones while plowing his field.  农民在耕地时发现了几块白垩石。|近义词：calcite nodule, limestone concretion |反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与柔软的石头对比)|名词
