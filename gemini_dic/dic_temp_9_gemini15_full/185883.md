revolter:/rɪˈvoʊltər/ | n. | 反叛者，造反者 | A person who revolts against authority or established systems.  A revolter is someone who actively participates in a rebellion or uprising against the existing power structure. They may use violence or other means to express their dissent and attempt to overthrow the government or authority. | Example: The revolters stormed the palace gates.  反叛者冲进了宫殿大门。 The young revolter was arrested for participating in the protests. 年轻的反叛者因参与抗议活动而被捕。|近义词：rebel, insurgent, mutineer | 反义词：loyalist, conformist | 名词
