oophoritis:/ˌoʊəfəˈraɪtɪs/| n. |卵巢炎|Inflammation of an ovary.  The condition can be caused by various factors, including infections (bacterial, viral, or fungal), autoimmune diseases, or pelvic inflammatory disease (PID). Symptoms can range from mild discomfort to severe pain, depending on the severity and cause.  Treatment depends on the underlying cause and may include antibiotics, pain relievers, or surgery.|The doctor diagnosed her with oophoritis after several tests.|近义词：ovaritis|反义词：None specifically;  the antonym would describe a healthy ovary.
