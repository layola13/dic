ghettoization:/ˌɡetəʊaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |贫民窟化；隔离；排斥 |The process of concentrating a particular group of people, often a minority or marginalized group, into a specific geographic area, typically characterized by poverty, social disadvantage, and limited opportunities.  This can result from discriminatory practices, economic inequalities, or other social forces.  It often leads to social isolation and limited access to resources and services. |例句：The ghettoization of the immigrant community led to increased social tensions and limited access to education.  贫民窟化使移民社区的社会紧张局势加剧，并限制了他们获得教育的机会。|近义词：segregation, marginalization, isolation, confinement |反义词：integration, inclusion, dispersion
