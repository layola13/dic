diplomats:/ˈdɪpləˌmætts/| n.|外交官；外交家|Persons who represent their governments in foreign countries, engaging in negotiations and promoting their nation's interests.  Diplomats are skilled in diplomacy and international relations.|例句：The diplomats worked tirelessly to negotiate a peace treaty.  外交官们不懈努力地谈判了一份和平条约。

例句：Several diplomats attended the international summit.  几位外交官出席了国际峰会。


近义词：ambassadors, envoys, negotiators|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be someone actively undermining international relations. |词性:名词
