inhaler:/ɪnˈheɪlər/ | n. |吸入器|A device used to administer medication, typically in the form of an aerosol spray or powder, into the lungs.  It is commonly used to treat respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.|例句：He used his inhaler to relieve his wheezing. 他用吸入器缓解喘息。|例句：The doctor prescribed a new inhaler for her asthma. 医生给她开了新的哮喘吸入器。|近义词：nebulizer, metered-dose inhaler (MDI)|反义词：N/A
