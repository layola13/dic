concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/| n. vt.|关心；担忧；涉及；关系到| concern  as a noun refers to a feeling of worry or anxiety,  a matter that needs attention, or a business or organization. As a verb, it means to worry, affect, or be relevant to something or someone. |例句：My main concern is my children's education.  我的主要担忧是我的孩子的教育。  The meeting concerned the future of the company.  会议涉及公司的未来。|近义词： worry, anxiety, involvement, regard |反义词： indifference, unconcern, apathy |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My biggest concern is the environment. = 我最大的担忧是环境。
This doesn't concern me. = 这与我无关。


一般过去时:
The news concerned him deeply. = 这个消息让他深感担忧。
The meeting concerned the budget. = 会议讨论的是预算。


现在完成时:
This issue has concerned me for years. = 这个问题困扰我多年了。
The project has concerned many people. = 这个项目牵涉到很多人。


过去完成时:
I had already concerned myself with the problem before he mentioned it. = 在他提到这个问题之前，我已经关注过这个问题了。
They had concerned themselves with the matter for a long time. = 他们对此事关注已久。


将来时:
This problem will concern us in the future. = 这个问题将来会困扰我们。
The new regulations will concern all businesses. = 新的法规将影响所有企业。


虚拟语气:
If I knew it concerned him, I would have done something. = 如果我知道这会让他担心，我就会做点什么。
I wish this didn't concern my family. = 我希望这不会牵涉到我的家人。


被动语态:
He was concerned about the situation. = 他对这种情况感到担忧。
The matter was concerned with the company's future. = 这件事与公司的未来有关。


疑问句:
Does this concern you? = 这让你担忧吗？
What concerns you most? = 你最担心什么？


否定句:
It doesn't concern me. = 这与我无关。
The problem doesn't concern him at all. = 这个问题根本不关他的事。
