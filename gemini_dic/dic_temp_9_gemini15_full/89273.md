homocercy:/hoʊˈsɜːrsi/| n. |同形性，同形现象|The phenomenon where different species develop similar structures or forms due to similar environmental pressures, not due to shared ancestry.  This is an example of convergent evolution.| Example: The streamlined bodies of sharks and dolphins are an example of homocercy, as they evolved independently to suit similar aquatic environments.  鲨鱼和海豚流线型的身体是同形性的一个例子，因为它们是独立进化以适应类似的水生环境。|近义词:convergent evolution, analogy|反义词:homology,divergent evolution|名词
