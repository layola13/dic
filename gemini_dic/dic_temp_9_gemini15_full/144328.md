outspurting:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. |喷出；涌出 |The act of spurting or gushing out; a sudden forceful ejection of a liquid or other substance.  |例句：Lava was outspurting from the volcano.  火山岩浆正喷涌而出。|例句：Water was outspurting from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道里喷涌而出。|近义词：gushing, spurting, erupting |反义词：trickling, seeping


USAGE->

Blood outspurts from the wound. 血从伤口喷涌而出。
