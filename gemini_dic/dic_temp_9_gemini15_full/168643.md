procommunism: /ˌproʊˈkɒmjʊnɪzəm/ | adj. | 亲共的，支持共产主义的 |  Supporting or favoring communism.  The term describes individuals, groups, or policies that advocate for or express a positive view towards communist ideologies and systems. |  例句：His procommunism was evident in his writings and speeches.  他的亲共立场在他的著作和演讲中显而易见。

例句：The government accused the opposition party of procommunism. 政府指控反对党亲共。


近义词：communist-sympathizing, pro-Soviet (in historical context), leftist (in some contexts)


反义词：anticommunist, pro-capitalist


词性：形容词
