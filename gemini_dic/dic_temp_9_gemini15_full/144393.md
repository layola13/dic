outstriding: /ˌaʊtˈstraɪdɪŋ/ | v. |超越，胜过，超过|To surpass or exceed in achievement, ability, or extent.|例句：Her talent outstrides that of her peers.  她的才能超过了她的同龄人。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, transcend |反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outstrides his competitors in the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中超越了他的竞争对手。
The company's success outstrides its initial projections.  公司的成功超过了最初的预期。


一般过去时:
She outstrode all her rivals in the race. 她在比赛中超越了所有对手。
The team outstrode their opponents in terms of innovation.  这个团队在创新方面超越了他们的对手。


现在完成时:
He has outstrode many challenges in his career. 他在他职业生涯中克服了许多挑战。
The project has outstrode expectations in terms of its impact.  这个项目的成果超出了预期。


过去完成时:
They had outstrode all obstacles before reaching the summit. 他们在到达山顶之前已经克服了所有障碍。
The company had outstrode its competitors by the time the market crashed.  市场崩盘时，该公司已经超越了竞争对手。


将来时:
She will outstride her current limitations with hard work. 她将通过努力工作克服目前的局限性。
The technology will outstride current capabilities in the near future.  这项技术在不久的将来将超越目前的水平。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outstride his rivals. 如果他训练更努力，他就能超越对手。
I wish I could outstride my fears and pursue my dreams. 我希望我能克服恐惧，追求我的梦想。


被动语态:
His achievements were outstrode by no one in the field.  他在该领域成就无人能及。
The plan was outstrode by unforeseen circumstances.  计划被不可预见的情况所超越。


疑问句:
Does his ambition outstride his capabilities? 他的雄心壮志超过了他的能力吗？

否定句:
His efforts did not outstride the challenges he faced. 他的努力并没有克服他面临的挑战。
The company failed to outstride its competitors in the market.  该公司未能超越市场上的竞争对手。
