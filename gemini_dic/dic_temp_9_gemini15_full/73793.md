forman:/ˈfɔːrmən/| n. |工头，领班|A foreman is a skilled worker who supervises other workers.|例句：The foreman instructed the workers to start the project. 工头指示工人开始项目。|近义词：supervisor, supervisor, overseer|反义词:worker, subordinate.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The foreman oversees the construction site. = 工头监管着建筑工地。
He directs the team's daily tasks. = 他指导团队的日常工作。

一般过去时:
The foreman checked the quality of the work yesterday. = 工头昨天检查了工作质量。
He supervised the installation of the new equipment. = 他监督了新设备的安装。

现在完成时:
The foreman has completed the project on time. = 工头按时完成了项目。
He has managed the team successfully for years. = 多年来他成功地管理着这个团队。

过去完成时:
The foreman had already finished his inspection before the workers arrived. = 工人在到达之前，工头已经完成了他的检查。
He had prepared a detailed report before the meeting. = 他在会议前准备了一份详细的报告。

将来时:
The foreman will inspect the site tomorrow. = 工头明天将视察工地。
He will assign new tasks to the workers next week. = 他下周将分配新的任务给工人。

虚拟语气:
If the foreman were here, he would solve the problem. = 如果工头在这里，他会解决这个问题。
I wish the foreman had given us clearer instructions. = 我希望工头给了我们更清晰的指示。

被动语态:
The work was supervised by the foreman. = 工作由工头监督。
The project was completed under the foreman’s guidance. = 项目在工头的指导下完成。

疑问句:
Is the foreman available? = 工头有空吗？
Who is the foreman on this project? = 这个项目的工头是谁？

否定句:
The foreman didn't approve the plan. = 工头没有批准这个计划。
He wasn't satisfied with the workers' performance. = 他对工人的表现不满意。
