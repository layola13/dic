goodliest:/ˈɡʊdliɪst/| adj.|最美好的，最优秀的，最令人愉悦的|Most excellent; finest; most pleasing.  The word is an archaic superlative form of "goodly," meaning beautiful or pleasant.|例句：The goodliest of the knights stepped forward. 最优秀的骑士向前迈了一步。
The garden boasted the goodliest of flowers. 花园里盛开着最美丽的花朵。|近义词：finest, best, most beautiful|反义词：worst, ugliest, least pleasing
