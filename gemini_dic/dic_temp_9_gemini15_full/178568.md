recording:/rɪˈkɔːrdɪŋ/| n. |录音，记录|The act or process of recording sound or data; something that is recorded.|例句：The recording of the concert was excellent.  这场音乐会的录音非常好。|例句：She kept a detailed recording of her expenses. 她详细记录了自己的开支。|近义词：registration, documentation, transcription |反义词：deletion, erasure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He records his lectures. = 他录制他的讲座。
She is recording a new album. = 她在录制一张新专辑。

一般过去时:
They recorded the meeting. = 他们记录了会议。
I recorded a song yesterday. = 我昨天录了一首歌。

现在完成时:
We have recorded all the interviews. = 我们已经录完了所有的采访。
She has recorded three albums this year. = 她今年已经录制了三张专辑。

过去完成时:
They had recorded the song before the concert. = 他们已经在音乐会之前录好了歌。
He had recorded his testimony before the trial. = 他在审判前已经录下了他的证词。

将来时:
They will record the show tomorrow. = 他们明天将录制这个节目。
We will be recording the podcast next week. = 我们下周将录制播客。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better microphone, I would record more often. = 如果我有一个更好的麦克风，我会更频繁地录音。
I wish I had recorded the speech. = 我真希望我录下了那次演讲。

被动语态:
The meeting was recorded for posterity. = 会议被记录下来以供后人参考。
The song is being recorded in a professional studio. = 这首歌正在专业的录音棚里录制。

疑问句:
Is the concert being recorded? = 音乐会正在录制吗？
Was the evidence recorded? = 证据被记录了吗？

否定句:
They didn't record the conversation. = 他们没有记录谈话。
She hasn't recorded anything yet. = 她还没有录制任何东西。
