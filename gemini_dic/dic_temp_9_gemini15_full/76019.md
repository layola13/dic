furfuran:/ˈfɜːrfjʊræn/| n.|糠醛|Furfural is a heterocyclic aldehyde derived from the dehydration of pentoses, often obtained from agricultural byproducts like corn cobs and oat hulls.  It's a colorless liquid that darkens upon exposure to air. It has a characteristic pungent odor.|例句：Furfural is used as a solvent in various industrial processes. 糠醛被用作各种工业过程中的溶剂。|例句：The researchers analyzed the furfural content in the biomass sample. 研究人员分析了生物质样品中的糠醛含量。|近义词：furfuraldehyde|反义词：无
