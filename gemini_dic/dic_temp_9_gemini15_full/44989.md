crystallizer: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzər/ | n. |结晶器；结晶装置 |A crystallizer is a piece of equipment used in chemical engineering to create crystals from a solution.  It works by carefully controlling the conditions (like temperature, pressure, and concentration) under which a solution is cooled or evaporated, allowing crystals to form.  The size and shape of the crystals can be controlled by adjusting these parameters. | Example: The chemical plant uses a large crystallizer to produce high-purity salt crystals.  这家化工厂使用大型结晶器生产高纯度盐晶体。  The new crystallizer design improved the efficiency of the process by 20%. 新的结晶器设计使工艺效率提高了20%。 |近义词: crystallizing apparatus, precipitation unit |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与溶解相关的词汇，如dissolver) |名词
