bylining:/ˈbaɪˌlaɪnɪŋ/| n.|署名，作者署名行|The act of writing a byline; a byline itself.  A byline is a line of text at the beginning or end of an article, usually in a newspaper or magazine, that indicates the author's name. |Example: The article's bylining clearly identified the author as a Pulitzer Prize winner. 这篇文章的署名清楚地表明作者是普利策奖得主。|近义词：credit line, attribution|反义词：anonymity
