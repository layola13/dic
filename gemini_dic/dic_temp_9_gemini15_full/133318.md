nonexplosiveness: /ˌnɒnɪkˈspləʊsɪvnəs/ | /ˌnɑːnɪkˈsploʊsɪvnəs/ | n. |非爆炸性；不易爆炸；不爆炸的性质 | The lack of explosiveness; the characteristic of not being explosive.  This term describes a substance or situation that is unlikely to explode or cause an explosion.  It emphasizes the absence of explosive potential. | Example: The nonexplosiveness of the material made it safe to handle.  这种材料不易爆炸，所以处理起来很安全。 The engineers ensured the nonexplosiveness of the fuel mixture. 工程师们确保了燃料混合物的非爆炸性。 | 近义词:  safety, stability, inertness | 反义词: explosiveness, volatility, instability |  形容词
