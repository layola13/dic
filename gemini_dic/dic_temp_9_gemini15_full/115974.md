marry-off:/ˈmæri ɒf/ | vt. |嫁掉；把…嫁出去 | To give someone in marriage; to arrange a marriage for someone, especially a daughter.  |例句：Her parents tried to marry her off to a wealthy businessman.  她的父母试图把她嫁给一个富有的商人。|例句：They were determined to marry their daughter off before she turned thirty. 他们决心在女儿三十岁之前把她嫁出去。|近义词：wed, give in marriage |反义词：keep unmarried


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They marry their daughters off early. = 他们早早地把女儿嫁出去了。
He tries to marry off his daughter to a rich man. = 他试图把女儿嫁给一个有钱人。

一般过去时:
My parents married me off at a young age. = 我父母在我很年轻的时候就把我嫁出去了。
She married her daughter off to a doctor. = 她把女儿嫁给了一名医生。

现在完成时:
They have already married their daughter off. = 他们已经把女儿嫁出去了。
He hasn't yet married his daughter off. = 他还没有把女儿嫁出去。

过去完成时:
They had already married their daughter off before they moved. = 他们在搬家之前就已经把女儿嫁出去了。
She had hoped to marry her daughter off before the war broke out. = 她曾希望在战争爆发前把女儿嫁出去。

将来时:
They will marry their daughter off next year. = 他们明年将把女儿嫁出去。
He plans to marry his daughter off to a lawyer. = 他计划把女儿嫁给一名律师。

虚拟语气:
If I were her father, I wouldn't marry her off so young. = 如果我是她父亲，我不会把她这么年轻就嫁出去。
I wish I hadn't married my daughter off to him. = 我真希望我没有把我女儿嫁给他。

被动语态:
She was married off at sixteen. = 她十六岁就被嫁出去了。
His daughter was successfully married off to a kind man. = 他的女儿成功地嫁给了一个善良的男人。


疑问句:
Will they marry their daughter off soon? = 他们会很快把女儿嫁出去吗？
Why did they marry her off so young? = 为什么他们把她这么年轻就嫁出去了？

否定句:
They didn't marry their daughter off. = 他们没有把女儿嫁出去。
She refused to be married off against her will.  = 她拒绝被强迫嫁人。
