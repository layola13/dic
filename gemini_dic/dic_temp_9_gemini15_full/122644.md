modify:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɑːdɪfaɪ/| vt.|修改，更改，修饰|To change something slightly, usually to improve it or to make it more suitable for a particular purpose.  It involves making adjustments or alterations.|

例句：The architect modified the plans to include a larger window. 建筑师修改了图纸，以便包含一个更大的窗户。

例句：We need to modify our strategy to achieve better results. 我们需要修改我们的策略以取得更好的结果。


近义词：alter, change, adjust, amend, revise

反义词：maintain, preserve, keep


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He modifies his writing style to suit the audience. 他修改他的写作风格以适应读者。
They modify their behavior to fit in. 他们修改他们的行为以融入其中。


一般过去时:
She modified the recipe to make it healthier. 她修改了食谱，使其更健康。
The engineer modified the design to improve efficiency. 工程师修改了设计以提高效率。


现在完成时:
They have modified the software to fix the bugs. 他们已经修改了软件以修复错误。
I have modified my approach to the problem. 我已经修改了我解决这个问题的方法。


过去完成时:
He had modified the car before selling it. 他在卖车之前已经改装了这辆车。
She had modified her plans after the unexpected event.  在她遇到意外事件后，她修改了她的计划。


将来时:
They will modify the system next week. 他们下周将修改系统。
We will modify our response depending on the situation. 我们将根据情况修改我们的回应。


虚拟语气:
If I modified the code, it might work. 如果我修改代码，它可能会运行。
I wish I had modified my answer earlier. 我希望我早点修改我的答案。


被动语态:
The document was modified by the editor. 这份文件是由编辑修改的。
The rules were modified to be more fair. 规则被修改得更公平了。


疑问句:
Did they modify the contract? 他们修改合同了吗？
Will you modify the design? 你会修改设计吗？


否定句:
He did not modify his opinion. 他没有修改他的意见。
They will not modify the schedule. 他们不会修改日程安排。
