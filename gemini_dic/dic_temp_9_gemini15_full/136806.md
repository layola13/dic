nonshattering: /ˌnɒnˈʃætərɪŋ/ | adj. |不易碎的，耐摔的 | Not easily broken or shattered.  The term describes materials that resist fracturing or breaking when subjected to impact or stress. | Example: The nonshattering glass is ideal for use in high-traffic areas.  不易碎的玻璃非常适合在人流量大的区域使用。|近义词：unbreakable, shatterproof, impact-resistant |反义词：shattering, fragile, brittle
