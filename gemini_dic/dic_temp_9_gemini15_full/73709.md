forgotten:/fərˈɡɒtən/ | /fərˈɡɑːtən/ | adj. | 被遗忘的；被忽略的 |  Forgotten refers to something that has been overlooked, neglected, or is no longer remembered. It implies a lapse in memory or attention.  | Example: The old photograph lay forgotten in the attic.  古老的照片被遗忘在阁楼上。

The forgotten toys were covered in dust. 被遗忘的玩具上蒙了一层灰尘。| neglected, overlooked, unheeded, lost to memory | remembered, recalled, remembered, present in mind | 形容词
