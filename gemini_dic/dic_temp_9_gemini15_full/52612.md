digestif:/ˈdɪdʒɛstɪf/| n.|助消化酒，开胃酒|A digestif is an alcoholic beverage, typically a spirit or liqueur, consumed after a meal to aid digestion.|例句：After a rich dinner, he enjoyed a glass of brandy as a digestif.  一顿丰盛的晚餐后，他享用了一杯白兰地作为助消化酒。|例句：Many people believe that herbal liqueurs make excellent digestives. 许多人认为草本利口酒是很好的助消化酒。|近义词：after-dinner drink, liqueur|反义词：appetizer (as a contrasting category)|名词
