The word evokes a sense of someone who is loud and possibly disruptive.|例句：He was a real chowter at the party, shouting and laughing all night. 他在派对上真是个大吵大闹的人，整晚又喊又笑。|近义词：brawler, loudmouth, chatterbox |反义词：quiet person, introvert
