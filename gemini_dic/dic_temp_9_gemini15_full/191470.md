schizophrenia:/ˌskɪtsəˈfreɪniə/| n. |精神分裂症|A chronic brain disorder that affects a person's ability to think, feel, and behave clearly.  Symptoms include hallucinations, delusions, disorganized speech, and difficulty with social interaction.  It's a severe mental illness that requires ongoing treatment. |He was diagnosed with schizophrenia at a young age and has been receiving treatment ever since. 他年轻时被诊断出患有精神分裂症，从那以后一直在接受治疗。|  psychosis, mental illness |  sanity, normalcy |名词
