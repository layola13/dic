lapdogs:/ˈlæpˌdɔːɡz/| n. | 膝上犬，娇生惯养的人 |Small dogs kept as pets, often small enough to be held in one's lap.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe someone who is overly submissive or dependent on another. |例句：She treats her little dog like a lapdog, dressing it in tiny clothes. 她把小狗当作膝上犬一样对待，给它穿上小小的衣服。

例句：He's become a lapdog to the company's CEO, agreeing with everything he says. 他成了公司首席执行官的跟屁虫，对他说的每件事都表示赞同。


近义词：pet, darling, minion, sycophant |反义词：independent person, leader
