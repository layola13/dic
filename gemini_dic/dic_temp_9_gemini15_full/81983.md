grindstones:/ˈɡraɪndˌstoʊnz/| n. |磨石；砂轮|A grindstone is a rotating stone wheel used for sharpening or grinding tools or other objects.  It's typically made of a coarse, abrasive material.|例句：The blacksmith used a grindstone to sharpen his tools.  铁匠用磨石磨快他的工具。
The old farmer sharpened his scythe on a large grindstone. 老农夫在一个大磨石上磨他的镰刀。|近义词：whetstone, sharpening stone|反义词：N/A
