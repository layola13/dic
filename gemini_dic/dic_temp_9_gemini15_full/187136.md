roaching:/ˈroʊtʃɪŋ/| v.|蟑螂出没；（非正式）大量涌入；|to infest with roaches; to be present in large numbers|例句：The kitchen was roaching badly after the hurricane.  飓风过后，厨房里蟑螂肆虐。
例句：Fans roached the stadium hours before the game.  比赛开始前几个小时，球迷们就涌进了体育场。|近义词：infesting, swarming, crowding |反义词：clean, empty


USAGE->

The kitchen is roaching. = 厨房里蟑螂很多。
Roaches are roaching all over the place. = 蟑螂到处乱窜。
