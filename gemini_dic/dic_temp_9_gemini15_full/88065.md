highlanders:/ˈhaɪləndərz/| n. |苏格兰高地居民；高地人|People who live in the Highlands of Scotland.  The term can also refer to people from mountainous regions in other parts of the world, or figuratively to people perceived as strong, independent, and perhaps somewhat isolated.|例句：The Highlanders are known for their strong sense of community. 苏格兰高地居民以其强烈的社区意识而闻名。

例句：The film "Highlander" features immortal warriors battling each other. 电影《高地人》讲述了不朽的战士互相战斗的故事。


近义词：mountaineers (in a literal sense), independent people (in a figurative sense)|反义词：lowlanders (in a literal sense), dependent people (in a figurative sense)|词性：名词
