crocking:/ˈkrɒkɪŋ/| v. | 裂开；弄破；（使）磨损；（尤指）弄脏；|The verb "crock" means to crack or break something, often pottery. It can also refer to the gradual wearing away or damaging of something, or to soil or stain something.  The word often implies a gradual process of deterioration. |例句：The vase was crocked when it fell to the floor.  花瓶掉到地上摔裂了。

The constant rubbing against the rocks crocked the bottom of the boat.  不断摩擦岩石使船底磨损了。


近义词：crack, break, damage, wear, stain |反义词：repair, mend, restore
