handling: /ˈhændlɪŋ/ | n. v. | 处理；操控；应付 |

英文解析:The noun "handling" refers to the way something is dealt with or managed.  As a verb, "handle" means to deal with, manage, or control something. It can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts.

例句:

(Noun) The handling of the crisis was exemplary.  =  危机处理堪称典范。

(Noun)  Poor handling of the delicate situation led to further complications. =  对棘手局面的处理不当导致了进一步的复杂化。


(Verb) He handles stress very well. = 他很好地应对压力。

(Verb)  She expertly handles the high-performance sports car. = 她熟练地驾驶着这辆高性能跑车。


近义词: (n.) management, control, treatment, operation; (v.) manage, deal with, cope with, control, operate

反义词: (n.) neglect, mismanagement; (v.) ignore, neglect


词性: noun, verb
