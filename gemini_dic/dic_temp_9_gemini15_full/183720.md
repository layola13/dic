resampled:/riːˈsæmpld/ | v. |重新采样，重采样 | To resample something is to take a new sample from it, often to improve its quality or to make it suitable for a different purpose.  This often involves changing the sampling rate or the number of data points.  |例句：The audio was resampled to a higher bitrate for better sound quality.  音频被重新采样到更高的比特率以获得更好的音质。

The image was resampled to a lower resolution for faster loading times. 图片被重新采样到较低的解析度以加快加载速度。|近义词：re-sample, resubmit|反义词：N/A
