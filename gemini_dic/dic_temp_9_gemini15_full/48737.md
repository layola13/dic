definable:/dɪˈfaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可定义的，可确定的 | Capable of being defined or explained clearly and precisely.  Something that is definable has clear boundaries or characteristics that allow it to be categorized or understood. | The terms of the contract were clearly definable. 合同条款清晰可定义。|  The line between right and wrong isn't always easily definable.  是非界限并不总是容易界定的。 | definable, clear, explicit | undefinable, vague, ambiguous | 形容词
