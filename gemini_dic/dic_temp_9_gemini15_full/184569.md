restocking:/riːˈstɒkɪŋ/| v.|重新进货，补充库存|The act of replenishing a supply of goods that have been sold or used.  It involves bringing new items into stock to replace those that have been depleted.|例句：The store is currently restocking its shelves with new merchandise. 这家商店目前正在补充货架上的新商品。
例句：We need to restock the pantry before the weekend. 我们需要在周末前补充食品储藏室的食品。|近义词：replenishing, refilling, supplying|反义词：depleting, exhausting, selling out
