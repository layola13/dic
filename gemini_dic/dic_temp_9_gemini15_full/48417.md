deducement: /dɪˈduːsmənt/ | n. | 推论；推断；结论 | The process of deducing something; a conclusion reached by reasoning.  It is rarely used in modern English and is often considered archaic.  | Example:  The detective arrived at a deducement based on the evidence presented.  侦探根据提供的证据得出了一个推论。  | 近义词：inference, conclusion, deduction | 反义词：induction (though not a direct antonym, induction is the opposite reasoning process) | 名词
