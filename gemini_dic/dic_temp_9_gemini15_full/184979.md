retelephoned:/ˌriːˈtɛləfoʊnd/| vt.|再打电话，重新打电话|To telephone someone again.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning again) to the verb "telephoned".|例句：I retelephoned him to confirm the meeting time. 我给他又打了个电话，确认会议时间。

例句：She retelephoned the office to check on the delivery status. 她再次打电话给办公室，查询送货状态。


近义词：called back, phoned again


反义词：无


词性：动词
