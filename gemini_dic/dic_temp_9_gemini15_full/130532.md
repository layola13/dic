nonaccumulating:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːmjʊleɪtɪŋ/| adj. |非累积的，不积累的 | Not accumulating; not tending to gather or increase in quantity.  This adjective describes something that doesn't build up or collect over time.  | Example:  The nonaccumulating fund is designed to distribute profits immediately.  非累积基金旨在立即分配利润。


The company uses a nonaccumulating pension plan. 这家公司使用的是非累积养老金计划。|近义词：non-additive, non-cumulative |反义词：accumulating, cumulative
