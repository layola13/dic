punctuates:/ˈpʌŋktʃueɪts/ | vt. | 加标点符号；强调；使明显 | To mark (writing) with punctuation marks. To interrupt or break the continuity of (something).  | 例句：She punctuates her sentences with dramatic pauses. 她用戏剧性的停顿来强调她的句子。  | 例句：The sudden silence punctuated the otherwise cheerful atmosphere. 突如其来的寂静打破了原本欢乐的气氛。 | 近义词：interrupts, emphasizes, highlights | 反义词：continues, flows, uninterrupted
