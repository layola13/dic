finches:/ˈfɪntʃɪz/| n.|雀科鸟类；雀类|Small passerine birds, typically having a stout bill.  Finches are known for their diverse and often colorful plumage and melodious songs. They are found worldwide, except for Antarctica and Australia. |例句：Many species of finches inhabit the Galapagos Islands.  许多种类的雀科鸟类栖息在加拉帕戈斯群岛。

例句：We watched the finches flitting amongst the flowers. 我们看着雀鸟在花丛中飞舞。


近义词：songbirds, passerines
反义词：无


词性：名词
