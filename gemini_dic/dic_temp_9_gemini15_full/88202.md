himself:/hɪmˈsɛlf/ | pron. | 他自己 | himself 指的是主语本身，通常用于强调主语的独立行为或状态。它是一个反身代词，其主语必须是第三人称单数男性。| He hurt himself playing football. 他踢足球时伤到了自己。| himself | other people, others | pron.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He teaches himself English. 他自学英语。
He prepares himself for the exam. 他为考试做准备。


一般过去时:
He dressed himself quickly. 他迅速地穿好了衣服。
He blamed himself for the accident. 他为这次事故责备自己。


现在完成时:
He has taught himself to play the guitar. 他自学了吉他。
He has prepared himself well for the interview. 他为面试做了充分的准备。


过去完成时:
He had already dressed himself before I arrived. 我到达之前他已经穿好衣服了。
He had blamed himself for the mistake before anyone else mentioned it. 在其他人提到之前，他已经责备自己犯了错误。


将来时:
He will teach himself programming next year. 他明年将自学编程。
He will prepare himself thoroughly for the competition. 他将为比赛做好充分的准备。


虚拟语气:
If he were to do it himself, he would do a better job. 如果他亲自去做，他会做得更好。
I wish he had taken care of himself better. 我希望他更好地照顾自己。


被动语态:
(himself cannot be used in passive voice directly.  The sentence structure would need to be changed.)  The work was done by himself. 这工作是他自己做的。(This avoids using "himself" in the passive voice directly.)


疑问句:
Did he do it himself?  是他自己做的吗？
Could he have hurt himself seriously? 他可能严重受伤了吗？


否定句:
He didn't do it himself.  这不是他自己做的。
He didn't prepare himself adequately for the test. 他没有为考试做好充分的准备。


PHRASE->
by himself: 独自地
for himself: 为他自己
to himself: 自言自语
in himself: 本身
of himself: 自动地
