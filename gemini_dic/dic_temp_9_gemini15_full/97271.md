influence: /ˈɪnflʊəns/ | n., v. |影响；势力；权势 | to affect or change (someone or something) in an indirect but usually significant way. |近义词：effect, impact, sway | 反义词：neglect, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His opinions greatly influence public opinion. = 他的观点极大地影响着公众舆论。
The weather influences our plans for the weekend. = 天气影响着我们周末的计划。


一般过去时:
The artist's work was influenced by his travels abroad. =这位艺术家的作品受到了他出国旅行的影响。
The decision was influenced by political pressure. = 这个决定受到了政治压力的影响。


现在完成时:
Her work has influenced generations of artists. = 她的作品影响了几代艺术家。
The new technology has influenced the way we communicate. = 新技术已经影响了我们的交流方式。


过去完成时:
He had been influenced by his father's political views. = 他受过他父亲政治观点的影响。
The painting had been influenced by earlier Renaissance masters. = 这幅画受到了早期文艺复兴大师的影响。


将来时:
Future technologies will influence our lives in unforeseen ways. = 未来的科技将以我们无法预料的方式影响我们的生活。
The new policy will influence how businesses operate. = 新政策将影响企业的运营方式。


虚拟语气:
If he had not influenced me, I wouldn't be where I am today. = 如果他没有影响我，我今天就不会像现在这样了。
I wish my advice had influenced her decision. = 我希望我的建议能影响她的决定。


被动语态:
The company's image was influenced by a negative news report. = 公司的形象受到了负面新闻报道的影响。
He was influenced by his friends to try smoking. = 他受朋友的影响尝试吸烟。


疑问句:
What influences your decisions? = 什么影响你的决定？
What factors influenced the outcome of the election? = 哪些因素影响了选举的结果？


否定句:
His arguments did not influence my opinion. = 他的论点没有影响我的意见。
The weather did not influence our decision to go hiking. = 天气并没有影响我们去远足的决定。


PHRASE->
under the influence of: 在……的影响下
have an influence on: 对……有影响
a strong influence: 强烈的影响
political influence: 政治影响
social influence: 社会影响
