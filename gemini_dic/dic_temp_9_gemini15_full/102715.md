jangled:/ˈdʒæŋɡld/ | vt. |使烦躁；使紧张；使神经紧张；拨弄（琴弦等）|To make (someone) nervous or upset; to cause a harsh, discordant sound by striking or jarring.|The loud music jangled his nerves.  吵闹的音乐让他神经紧张。|The child jangled the keys on the ring. 孩子拨弄着钥匙链上的钥匙。|irritate, upset, agitate |calm, soothe |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud noises jangle my nerves. = 嘈杂的声音让我心烦意乱。
He jangles the keys in his pocket. = 他把口袋里的钥匙弄得叮当响。


一般过去时:
