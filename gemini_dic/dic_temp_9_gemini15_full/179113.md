redevelopment:/ˌriːˈdɛvələpmənt/ | n. | 再开发；重建；改造 | The process of improving an area by replacing old buildings with new ones, or by improving existing ones.  It often involves a comprehensive plan to revitalize a neglected or deteriorated area. |  例句：The city council approved a plan for the redevelopment of the waterfront.  市议会批准了一项滨水区重建计划。

例句：The redevelopment project transformed the abandoned factory site into a vibrant community center. 再开发项目将废弃的工厂场地改造成一个充满活力的社区中心。


近义词：reconstruction, regeneration, renewal, revitalization | 反义词：demolition, decay, deterioration | 词性：名词
