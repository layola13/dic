oversecures:/ˌoʊvər.səˈkjʊr/| v.|过度保护；过度防范|To protect or secure something excessively, often to the point of hindering its natural development or function.  This often implies unnecessary or counterproductive measures.|例句：The company oversecured its data, making it difficult for employees to access necessary files. 公司过度保护其数据，导致员工难以访问必要的档案。
例句：He oversecured his bicycle with multiple locks, despite living in a safe neighborhood. 他用多个锁过度保护他的自行车，尽管他住在安全的社区。|近义词：overprotect, overguard, safeguard excessively|反义词：neglect, abandon, underprotect|
