devalue:/ˌdiːˈvæljuː/ | vt. |贬值；轻视；降低…的价值 | To reduce the value or worth of something.  |例句：The government's policies devalued the currency. 政府的政策导致货币贬值。

例句：He felt devalued by his boss's constant criticism. 他觉得老板不断批评他让他很受轻视。


近义词：depreciate, lessen, diminish, undervalue | 反义词：appreciate, enhance, increase, value


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government devalues the currency. = 政府正在贬值货币。
They devalue their employees' contributions. = 他们轻视员工的贡献。


一般过去时:
The company devalued its assets last year. = 公司去年低估了其资产。
Inflation devalued savings. = 通货膨胀贬低了储蓄的价值。


现在完成时:
The dollar has devalued significantly against the euro. = 美元兑欧元大幅贬值。
He has felt devalued since his promotion was denied. = 自从他的晋升被拒绝后，他就感到自己被轻视了。


过去完成时:
The company had devalued its stock before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经低估了其股票的价值。
They had devalued his expertise until it was too late. = 他们直到为时已晚才意识到他的专业知识的价值。


将来时:
The central bank will devalue the currency to boost exports. = 中央银行将贬值货币以促进出口。
They will devalue the importance of his role. = 他们将贬低他的角色的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If they devalued the currency, inflation would increase. = 如果他们贬值货币，通货膨胀将会上升。
I wish they hadn't devalued my efforts. = 我希望他们没有贬低我的努力。


被动语态:
The currency was devalued by the government. = 货币被政府贬值了。
His contributions were devalued by his superiors. = 他的贡献被他的上司轻视了。


疑问句:
Has the government devalued the currency recently? = 最近政府是否贬值了货币？
Do they devalue the opinions of younger employees? = 他们是否轻视年轻员工的意见？


否定句:
The government did not devalue the currency. = 政府没有贬值货币。
They did not devalue the importance of the project. = 他们没有贬低这个项目的重要性。
