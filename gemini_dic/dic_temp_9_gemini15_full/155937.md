photoelectric:/ˌfoʊtoʊɪˈlɛktrɪk/| adj.|光电的；光电效应的|relating to the emission of electrons when light hits a material.  The photoelectric effect is the basis of many technologies, including solar panels and photodiodes.|例句：The photoelectric effect is a fundamental concept in physics. 光电效应是物理学中的一个基本概念。

例句：This device uses a photoelectric sensor to detect light. 这个装置使用光电传感器来检测光线。
