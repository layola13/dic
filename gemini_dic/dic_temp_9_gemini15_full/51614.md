developabilities:/ˌdɛvɛləpəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |发展能力，可发展性|The inherent potential for growth and improvement;  capacities that can be developed.  This refers to the potential for something to be developed, rather than something that is already developed. |例句：The company recognized the developabilities of its young employees and invested in training programs.  这家公司认识到其年轻员工的发展潜力，并投资了培训项目。

例句：His developabilities in leadership were evident from his early career. 他在领导方面的潜力在他职业生涯早期就显而易见。


近义词：potentials, capabilities, capacities, promise


反义词：limitations, constraints, inabilities


词性:名词
