flexes:/ˈflɛksɪz/| v. |弯曲；屈伸；运用；发挥；|To bend or become bent, especially repeatedly.  It can also refer to the act of using or applying something skillfully.|The cat flexed its claws.  The company flexed its muscles in the market. |近义词：bend, curve,  strain, utilize|反义词：straighten, stiffen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flexes his biceps every morning. = 他每天早上都弯曲他的二头肌。
The yoga instructor flexes her body into various poses. = 瑜伽教练把她的身体弯曲成各种姿势。


一般过去时:
She flexed her fingers before starting to type. = 她开始打字前弯曲了她的手指。
