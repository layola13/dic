crucify:/ˈkruːsɪfaɪ/ | vt. |钉死在十字架上；严厉批评；折磨 | To put someone to death by nailing them to a cross.  Also, to criticize someone severely. |例句：They crucified Jesus on a hill outside Jerusalem.  他们把耶稣钉死在耶路撒冷郊外的一座山上。

例句：The critics crucified his new novel. 批评家们严厉批评了他的新小说。


近义词：execute, condemn, castigate, lambaste, denounce | 反义词：praise, commend, exalt
