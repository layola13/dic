fantasy:/ˈfæntəsi/ | n. |幻想；空想；幻想作品 |Imaginative creation, especially one involving supernatural or unreal elements; a product of the imagination.|例句：Her novels are full of fantasy and magic.  她的小说充满了幻想和魔法。

例句：He spent hours lost in fantasy, dreaming of faraway lands. 他花了好几个小时沉浸在幻想中，梦想着遥远的国度。


近义词：dream, imagination, illusion, daydream | 反义词：reality, truth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fantasizes about winning the lottery. = 他幻想中彩。
She fantasizes about traveling the world. = 她幻想环游世界。


一般过去时:
I fantasized about being a famous actress. = 我曾经幻想成为一名著名的女演员。
He fantasized about flying. = 他曾经幻想飞行。


现在完成时:
She has fantasized about this moment for years. = 她多年来一直幻想这一刻。
They have fantasized about living in a castle. = 他们一直幻想住在城堡里。


过去完成时:
I had fantasized about meeting him before we actually met. = 在我们真正见面之前，我曾经幻想过和他见面。
She had fantasized about becoming a doctor since childhood. = 她从小就幻想成为一名医生。


将来时:
I will fantasize about my future career. = 我将幻想我未来的职业。
He will fantasize about his next adventure. = 他将幻想他的下一个冒险。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would fantasize about a life on a tropical island. = 如果可以，我会幻想在热带岛屿上的生活。
I wish I could fantasize more often. = 我希望我能更频繁地幻想。


被动语态:  (Fantasy is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Does he often fantasize about escaping reality? = 他经常幻想逃避现实吗？
What do you fantasize about? = 你幻想什么？


否定句:
I don't fantasize about unrealistic things. = 我不会幻想不切实际的事情。
He didn't fantasize about revenge. = 他没有幻想复仇。
