meditated:/ˈmɛdɪteɪtɪd/| v. |沉思；冥想；考虑|To think deeply and calmly, especially about spiritual matters; to reflect on. |例句：I meditated on the meaning of life. 我沉思生命的意义。  She meditated for an hour each morning. 她每天早上冥想一个小时。|近义词：contemplated, pondered, reflected, ruminated|反义词：acted, rushed, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He meditates every day. = 他每天冥想。
They meditate to relieve stress. = 他们冥想以减压。

一般过去时:
I meditated on the problem for hours. = 我花了几个小时思考这个问题。
She meditated before making a decision. = 她做决定前先冥想了一会儿。

现在完成时:
I have meditated regularly for years. = 我多年来定期冥想。
She has meditated on the consequences of her actions. = 她已经思考了她行为的后果。

过去完成时:
He had meditated for a long time before he spoke. = 他在说话之前冥想了很久。
They had meditated on the proposal before rejecting it. = 他们仔细考虑了这个提案之后才拒绝它。

将来时:
I will meditate tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会冥想。
She will meditate before the important meeting. = 她将在重要的会议前冥想。

虚拟语气:
If I had meditated more, I might have made a better decision. = 如果我当时冥想更多，我可能会做出更好的决定。
I wish I had meditated before reacting so impulsively. = 我真希望我在如此冲动地反应之前先冥想一下。

被动语态:
The problem was meditated upon by the committee. = 委员会对这个问题进行了深思熟虑。
The solution to the issue was carefully meditated on. = 这个问题的解决方案经过了仔细的思考。


疑问句:
Do you meditate regularly? = 你经常冥想吗？
Has she ever meditated before? = 她以前冥想过吗？

否定句:
He did not meditate before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前没有冥想。
They didn't meditate enough to solve the problem. = 他们没有充分思考来解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
meditate on = 沉思…
meditate upon = 深思熟虑…
guided meditation = 引导式冥想
mindful meditation = 正念冥想
transcendental meditation = 超越冥想
