crystallogeny:/krɪˌstælˈɒdʒənɪ/ | n. |晶体成因学|The study of the origin and formation of crystals.  It encompasses the processes by which crystals grow, the conditions under which they form, and the factors that influence their morphology and properties.  |The crystallogeny of this mineral deposit is complex, involving multiple stages of hydrothermal activity.  该矿床的晶体成因很复杂，涉及多个阶段的热液活动。|petrology, mineralogy|amorphous geology|
