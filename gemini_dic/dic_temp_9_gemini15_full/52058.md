dialogically:/ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 对话式地，对话般地 | In a way that involves or is characteristic of dialogue; through dialogue.  This adverb emphasizes the interactive and communicative nature of an action or process. It suggests a back-and-forth exchange of ideas, perspectives, or viewpoints. | Example:  The problem was solved dialogically through a series of collaborative discussions.  这个问题通过一系列合作讨论以对话的方式得到解决。 | 近义词：interactively, conversationally, communicatively | 反义词：monologically, unilaterally, silently
