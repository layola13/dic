revarnished:/ˌriːˈvɑːrnɪʃt/| v. |重新上漆，重新涂漆 |To varnish something again.  This implies a previous application of varnish that has worn off or needs refreshing. |例句：The antique table was revarnished to restore its shine.  = 古董桌子重新上漆以恢复光泽。

The old wooden floor was revarnished after years of wear and tear. =  旧木地板经过多年的磨损后重新上漆。


近义词： refinish, recoat


反义词：  unvarnished (lacking varnish)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revarnishes his boats every year. = 他每年都给他的船重新上漆。
She revarnishes the furniture regularly. = 她定期给家具重新上漆。


一般过去时:
They revarnished the deck last weekend. = 他们上周末重新粉刷了甲板。
I revarnished the old chest of drawers. = 我重新给旧的抽屉柜上了漆。


现在完成时:
He has revarnished the doors several times. = 他已经几次重新粉刷了门。
She has revarnished the entire house. = 她已经重新粉刷了整栋房子。


过去完成时:
They had revarnished the car before selling it. = 他们在卖车之前已经重新给车上了漆。
We had revarnished the table before the party. = 我们在聚会之前已经重新给桌子上了漆。


将来时:
We will revarnish the floor next week. = 我们下周将重新粉刷地板。
They will revarnish the chairs tomorrow. = 他们明天将重新粉刷椅子。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would revarnish the fence. = 如果我有时间，我会重新粉刷栅栏。
If she had more money, she would revarnish the antique table. = 如果她有钱一点，她会重新粉刷古董桌子。


被动语态:
The boat was revarnished by a professional. = 这艘船是由专业人士重新上漆的。

疑问句:

否定句:
