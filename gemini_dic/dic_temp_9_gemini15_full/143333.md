outedges:/ˈaʊtˌɛdʒɪz/| n. |出边, 出射边 |The outedges of a graph are the edges that connect to vertices with no outgoing edges.  In other words, they are the edges pointing away from "sink" nodes (nodes with only incoming edges).  They represent the final stages or outputs of a process depicted by the graph.|例句：Analyzing the outedges of this network reveals the key distribution points. 分析这个网络的出射边揭示了关键的分发点。

The outedges of the decision tree indicate the final outcomes. 决策树的出射边指示最终结果。|近义词：outgoing edges, terminal edges |反义词：inedges, incoming edges
