grafter:/ˈɡræftər/| n. |努力工作的人；贪污受贿者；嫁接者|A grafter is someone who works very hard.  It can also refer to someone who is corrupt and accepts bribes. In horticulture, a grafter is someone who grafts plants.|例句：He's a real grafter; he works long hours every day. 他是个实实在在的苦干者；他每天工作很长时间。
He was exposed as a grafter taking bribes from construction companies. 他被揭露为一个收受建筑公司贿赂的贪污者。|近义词：hard worker, diligent worker, (for corrupt meaning) corrupt official, bribe-taker |反义词：idler, loafer, honest official
