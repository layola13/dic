highmost:/ˈhaɪməʊst/ | adj. | 最高的，最高的 | Highest in position, degree, or amount.  This word is less common than "highest" and is often considered archaic or overly formal.  | Example: From the highmost peak, he surveyed the kingdom. 从最高的山峰上，他俯瞰着王国。 | 近义词：highest, uppermost | 反义词：lowest, bottommost
