restabilizing: /ˌriːˈsteɪbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使重新稳定；使恢复稳定 |  The process of restoring stability to something that has been disrupted or destabilized.  It involves bringing a system, situation, or structure back to a state of equilibrium or balance. |

例句：

The government implemented new policies to restabilize the economy after the financial crisis. = 政府实施了新的政策，以在金融危机后稳定经济。

The doctor administered medication to restabilize the patient's heart rate. = 医生施用了药物以稳定病人的心率。


近义词：stabilize, re-establish, consolidate, reinforce

反义词：destabilize, disrupt, upset
