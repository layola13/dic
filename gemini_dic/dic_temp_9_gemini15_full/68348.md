fall-upon:/fɔːl əˈpɒn/|/ˈfɔːl əˈpɑːn/| phrasal verb|偶然发现；降临；袭击|To unexpectedly discover or encounter something or someone; to happen unexpectedly; to attack or assail. |例句：I fell upon an old photograph of my grandfather. 我偶然发现了一张我祖父的旧照片。|例句：A sudden illness fell upon him.  一场突如其来的疾病降临在他身上。|近义词：stumble upon, happen upon, befall, attack, assail|反义词：plan, expect, avoid


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The responsibility falls upon me. = 责任落在我身上。
Unexpected problems often fall upon us during the project. = 项目期间我们经常遇到意想不到的问题。


一般过去时:
A great opportunity fell upon her. = 一个绝好的机会降临在她身上。
The enemy suddenly fell upon the village. = 敌人突然袭击了村庄。


现在完成时:
Many difficulties have fallen upon the company recently. = 最近公司遭遇了许多困难。
Good fortune has fallen upon him. = 好运降临在他身上。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, a terrible silence had fallen upon the town. =暴风雨来临之前，可怕的寂静笼罩着小镇。
The responsibility had fallen upon him before he even realized it. = 他甚至还没意识到责任就已经落在了他身上。


将来时:
A heavy snowstorm will fall upon this area tomorrow. = 明天一场大雪将袭击这个地区。
Great changes will fall upon our society in the next decade. = 未来十年，巨大的变化将会降临到我们的社会。


虚拟语气:
If only more opportunities would fall upon us. = 但愿有更多机会降临在我们身上。
If a crisis were to fall upon our nation, we would need to be prepared. = 如果危机降临到我们国家，我们就需要做好准备。


被动语态:
The town was fallen upon by the enemy. = 城镇遭到了敌人的袭击。
The task was fallen upon by the team.  = 任务由团队承担。


疑问句:
What will fall upon us next? = 下一步会发生什么？
When will this burden fall upon me? = 这负担什么时候会落到我身上？


否定句:
No misfortune fell upon them during their journey. = 在旅途中，他们没有遭遇任何不幸。
Nothing unusual fell upon the city that day. = 那天城市没有发生任何异常的事情。


PHRASE->
fall upon someone unexpectedly = 意外地降临到某人身上
fall upon someone with (criticism) = 对某人（批评）猛烈攻击
fall upon a solution = 偶然找到解决办法
fall upon one’s enemies = 袭击某人的敌人
fall upon hard times = 陷入困境
