opprobrium:/əˈproʊbriəm/| n. | 谴责；耻辱；恶名 | Public condemnation; disgrace; infamy.  It refers to the harsh criticism or disapproval that is directed at someone or something, often resulting in shame and disgrace. | His actions brought him considerable opprobrium.  他的行为给他带来了很大的谴责。 |The scandal brought opprobrium on the entire family. 这丑闻给全家人带来了耻辱。|  condemnation, disgrace, shame, ignominy | praise, honor, glory | 名词
