huh:/hʌ/| int.| 嗯？啊？|表示疑惑、不确定或轻微的否定。常用于回应对方所说的话，表示没有听清或不明白对方的意思。|例句：Huh? What did you say? 嗯？你刚才说什么？|例句：Huh, I don't think so. 嗯，我不这么认为。|近义词：what?, um|反义词:  无


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't follow typical grammatical tenses.  It's used in informal contexts to express uncertainty or a lack of understanding.  Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Huh? What was that? = 嗯？什么？
Huh-uh = 不
What the huh? = 搞什么鬼？
I don't know, huh. = 我不知道，嗯。
Is that so, huh? = 是这样吗，嗯？
