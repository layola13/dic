epiphanize:/ɪˈpɪfənaɪz/| vt.|顿悟；领悟；使显现|To experience or cause to experience a sudden realization or understanding; to make something manifest or evident.  The word suggests a sudden and profound insight, often of a spiritual or transformative nature.|例句：The artist epiphanized the meaning of his life through his latest painting.  艺术家通过他最新的绘画作品领悟了他人生的意义。The evidence epiphanized the truth of the matter. 证据使事情的真相显而易见。|近义词：realize, understand, comprehend, enlighten|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, obfuscate|动词
