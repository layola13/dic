redocument:/ˌriːˈdɒkjʊmɛnt/| vt.|重新编写文档，重新记录|To document something again, often with improvements or corrections.  It implies a previous documentation existed and is being revised or replaced.|例句：We need to redocument the entire process to ensure clarity. 我们需要重新编写整个流程的文档以确保清晰明了。

例句：The software's documentation was riddled with errors, so we had to redocument it completely. 该软件的文档充满了错误，因此我们不得不完全重新编写它。
