mainframes:/ˈmeɪnfreɪmz/| n.|大型机，主机|A mainframe is a very large and powerful computer, typically used by large organizations for critical applications requiring high transaction processing and data storage capacity.|例句：Many banks still rely on mainframes for their core banking systems. 许多银行仍然依赖大型机来运行其核心银行系统。|近义词：supercomputer, server (in some contexts)|反义词：personal computer, workstation
