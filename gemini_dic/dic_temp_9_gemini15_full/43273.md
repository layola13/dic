crafting:/ˈkræftɪŋ/| v.|制作；精工细作；创造；设计|The act of making something skillfully, especially by hand; the process of carefully planning and creating something.|例句：She is crafting a beautiful wooden bird. 她正在制作一只精美的木鸟。
例句：He spent years crafting his novel. 他花了数年时间创作他的小说。|近义词：making, creating, designing, constructing|反义词：destroying, ruining, demolishing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is crafting a new strategy. = 他正在制定一个新的策略。
They craft beautiful pottery. = 他们制作精美的陶器。

一般过去时:
She crafted a compelling argument. = 她构思了一个有说服力的论点。
We crafted a plan to escape. = 我们制定了一个逃跑计划。

现在完成时:
He has crafted several successful businesses. = 他已经创建了几家成功的企业。
They have crafted a masterpiece. = 他们创作了一部杰作。

过去完成时:
She had crafted a detailed report before the meeting. = 她在会议前已经起草了一份详细的报告。
They had crafted a solution to the problem. = 他们已经为这个问题找到了一个解决方案。

将来时:
We will be crafting a new website next month. = 我们下个月将要制作一个新的网站。
She will craft a response to the letter. = 她将要起草一封回信。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would craft a better presentation. = 如果我有更多时间，我会制作一个更好的演示文稿。
I wish I could craft a more persuasive argument. = 我希望我能构思一个更有说服力的论点。

被动语态:
The policy was carefully crafted by the committee. = 政策是由委员会仔细制定的。
The sculpture was crafted from marble. = 这尊雕塑是用大理石雕刻而成的。

疑问句:
What are you crafting right now? = 你现在正在制作什么？
How is the new product being crafted? = 新产品是如何制作的？

否定句:
He didn't craft a convincing explanation. = 他没有构思出一个令人信服的解释。
They haven't crafted a solution yet. = 他们还没有找到解决方案。
