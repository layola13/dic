collegial:/kəˈliːdʒiəl/| adj.|学院的；同事间的；合作的|Characterized by or exhibiting collegiality; relating to or characteristic of a college or its members.  The collegial atmosphere fostered collaboration among the faculty. 学院式的氛围促进了教师之间的合作。|近义词：cooperative, collaborative, collegiality|反义词：uncooperative, adversarial, individualistic
