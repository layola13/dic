causa: /ˈkɔːzə/ | n. | 原因，起因，诉讼理由 | The underlying cause of the problem is a lack of communication.  这个问题的根本原因是缺乏沟通。|近义词：reason, cause, origin |反义词：effect, result


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the cause of the accident? = 事故的原因是什么？
The cause of the delay is unknown. = 延误的原因不明。


一般过去时:
What was the cause of the fire? = 火灾的原因是什么？
The cause of the problem was identified. = 问题的原因已经查明。


现在完成时:
What has been the cause of your stress? = 导致你压力的原因是什么？
The cause of the conflict has been resolved. = 冲突的原因已经解决。


过去完成时:
What had been the cause of the argument before they reconciled? = 他们在和好之前争吵的原因是什么？
The cause of the damage had been obvious all along. = 损坏的原因一直都很明显。


将来时:
What will be the cause of the next economic crisis? = 下一次经济危机的起因是什么？
We will investigate what the cause of the malfunction will be. = 我们将调查故障的原因是什么。


虚拟语气:
If the cause of the disease were known, a cure could be found. = 如果知道这种病的病因，就能找到治疗方法。
I wish I knew the cause of his unhappiness. = 我真希望知道他为什么不快乐。


被动语态:
The cause of the accident is being investigated. = 事故原因正在调查中。
The true cause of the war has never been fully understood. = 战争的真正原因从未被完全理解。


疑问句:
What is the cause? = 原因是什么？
Could you explain the cause of this issue? = 你能解释一下这个问题的原因吗？


否定句:
There is no known cause for this illness. = 这种疾病没有已知原因。
The cause of the accident was not immediately apparent. = 事故的原因并没有立即显现。


PHRASE->
causa causans: 直接原因
causa sine qua non: 必要条件
proximate cause: 近因
cause and effect: 因果关系
root cause: 根源


词源:
From Latin *causa ("cause, reason, case, lawsuit"), from Proto-Italic *kausa, from Proto-Indo-European *kaweh₂- ("to strive, to fight").  The word shares a root with words like “combat” and “cause.”
