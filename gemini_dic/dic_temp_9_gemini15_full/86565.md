helicograph:/ˈhɛlɪkəɡræf/| n. |螺旋仪，螺旋描图器 |A helicograph is a device that uses a rotating cylinder to create a spiral or helical pattern, often used in writing or drawing.  It can also refer to the resulting spiral drawing or writing produced by such a device. |Example: The inventor demonstrated his new helicograph, which produced intricate spirals with remarkable speed.  这个发明家演示了他的新型螺旋仪，它能以惊人的速度绘制出复杂的螺旋图案。|近义词：spiralograph (for the drawing tool)|反义词：None specifically, but words implying straight lines or non-spiral patterns could be considered antonyms. |名词
