over-garrisoned: /ˌoʊvərˈɡærɪsənd/ | adj. | 驻军过多的；防守过严的 | Excessively garrisoned; having more troops stationed than necessary.  | The fortress was over-garrisoned, leading to logistical problems and strained resources.  堡垒驻军过多，导致后勤问题和资源紧张。| under-garrisoned | adequately garrisoned |
