recaps:/ˈriːkæps/| v.|总结，概括，回顾|To summarize or briefly review something.  |例句：The teacher recapped the main points of the lesson. 老师总结了课程要点。|例句：Let's recap what we've learned so far. 让我们回顾一下到目前为止我们学到了什么。|近义词：summarize, review, summarize, recount|反义词：detail, elaborate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recaps the news every evening. = 他每天晚上都总结新闻。
She recaps the meeting's key decisions. = 她总结了会议的重点决定。


一般过去时:
I recapped the events of the day in my diary. = 我在我的日记里总结了当天发生的事情。
The professor recapped the lecture's main arguments. = 教授总结了讲座的主要论点。


现在完成时:
She has recapped all the important information. = 她已经总结了所有重要的信息。
He has recapped the project's progress to date. = 他已经总结了项目的最新进展。


过去完成时:
They had recapped the previous chapter before starting the next one. = 他们在开始下一章之前已经总结了前一章的内容。
After the storm, she had recapped the damage to her property. = 暴风雨过后，她总结了对其财产造成的损失。


将来时:
I will recap the meeting for those who missed it. = 我会为错过会议的人总结会议内容。
The journalist will recap the election results. = 记者将总结选举结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would recap the entire book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会总结整本书。
I wish I could recap the entire conference. = 我希望我能总结整个会议。


被动语态:
The key points of the presentation were recapped at the end. = 演示文稿的要点在最后进行了总结。
The meeting's conclusions will be recapped in an email. = 会议的结论将在电子邮件中进行总结。


疑问句:
Will you recap the instructions for us? = 你会为我们总结一下指示吗？
Can you recap what happened last night? = 你能总结一下昨晚发生的事情吗？


否定句:
He didn't recap the details of the accident. = 他没有总结事故的细节。
She failed to recap the important points of the presentation. = 她未能总结演示文稿的要点。
