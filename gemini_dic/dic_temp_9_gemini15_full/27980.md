canvas:/ˈkæn.vəs/| n. |帆布，画布；幕布；(电脑)画布|A canvas is a piece of strong cloth used by artists for painting on, or a similar material used for other purposes.  It can also refer to a background or surface for visual display on a computer screen. |例句：The artist painted a beautiful landscape on the canvas.  艺术家在画布上画了一幅美丽的风景画。

The photographer used a large canvas backdrop for the photoshoot. 摄影师在拍摄中使用了大型帆布背景。


近义词：cloth, surface, backdrop |反义词：N/A |词性：名词

词源:
The word "canvas" ultimately derives from the Old French word "canevas," which in turn comes from the Latin "cannabis," meaning "hemp."  Originally, canvas was made from hemp fibers, and the word retained this association even as other materials began to be used for its production.
