hypocretin: /ˌhaɪpoʊˈkrɛtɪn/ | n. | 促醒素 |  A neuropeptide that regulates arousal, wakefulness, and appetite. It is primarily produced in the hypothalamus.  A deficiency in hypocretin is associated with narcolepsy. |例句：Research suggests a link between hypocretin levels and sleep disorders.  研究表明促醒素水平与睡眠障碍之间存在联系。|例句：The study investigated the role of hypocretin in regulating appetite.  这项研究调查了促醒素在调节食欲中的作用。|近义词：orexin (it is also known as orexin)|反义词：None specifically, but substances that promote sleep could be considered antonyms in function.|名词
