redone:/ˌriːˈdʌn/| v. |重新制作；翻新；修改|To do something again, often in a better or improved way.  It implies a previous attempt that was not satisfactory.|例句：The software has been redone to fix the bugs.  软件已被重新制作以修复错误。|例句：The old house was redone with modern amenities. 老房子经过翻新，配备了现代化设施。|近义词：remade, revised, refurbished|反义词：destroyed, ruined


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He redones his website every year. 他每年都会重新制作他的网站。
They redo the garden every spring. 他们每年春天都会重新整理花园。


一般过去时:
She redid her makeup before the party.  她在聚会前重新化了妆。
He redid the assignment after receiving feedback. 他在收到反馈后重新做了作业。


现在完成时:
I have redone that presentation three times. 我已经三次重新制作了那个演示文稿。
They have redone the kitchen since they moved in. 他们搬进来后就重新装修了厨房。


过去完成时:
She had redone the report before submitting it to her boss.  她在向老板提交报告之前已经重新做了报告。
They had redone the experiment several times before achieving success. 他们在取得成功之前已经多次重复了实验。


将来时:
We will redo the contract next week. 我们下周将重新制定合同。
He will redo the painting if he has time. 如果他有时间，他会重画这幅画。
