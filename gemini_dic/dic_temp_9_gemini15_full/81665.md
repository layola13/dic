green-manured:/ˈɡriːnˌmænʊrd/| adj.|绿肥的；用绿肥施肥的|  Using green manure to fertilize the soil.  This method involves growing and then plowing under certain plants to enrich the soil with nutrients.|近义词：soil-enriched, organically fertilized|反义词：chemically fertilized
