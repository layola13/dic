earmark:/ˈɪərmɑːrk/| vt. n.|预先拨款；指定用途；作标记；特征|To designate (funds or resources) for a particular purpose; to set aside for a specific use; to mark in a distinctive way.|例句：The committee earmarked $1 million for the project.  委员会为该项目拨款 100 万美元。

The unique markings earmarked the antique chair as a valuable piece. 独特的标记表明这把古董椅子是一件珍贵的物品。|近义词：allocate, designate, reserve, identify|反义词：unspecified, undetermined|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They earmark funds for education each year. = 他们每年都为教育拨款。
The company earmarks a portion of its profits for charity. = 这家公司将部分利润捐献给慈善机构。


一般过去时:
The government earmarked a large sum of money for infrastructure development. = 政府为基础设施建设拨付了一大笔资金。
She earmarked several passages in the book for further study. = 她为进一步研究而标记了书中的几段话。


现在完成时:
They have earmarked significant resources for disaster relief. = 他们已经为救灾拨付了大量资源。
We have earmarked this area for future expansion. = 我们已经将这块区域指定用于未来的扩张。


过去完成时:
They had earmarked the funds before the project even began. = 项目开始之前，他们就已经拨款了。
She had earmarked that specific chapter for her presentation. = 她已经将那一章内容指定用于她的演示文稿。


将来时:
The council will earmark additional funds for community projects. = 理事会将为社区项目拨付额外资金。
We will earmark a section of the report for recommendations. = 我们将报告的一部分内容专门用于建议。


虚拟语气:
If they earmarked more money, the project would be completed sooner. = 如果他们拨更多款项，项目就能更快完成。
I wish they had earmarked more resources for research. = 我希望他们为研究拨付更多资源。


被动语态:
The funds were earmarked for the construction of a new hospital. = 这笔资金被指定用于新建医院。
A large portion of the budget has been earmarked for environmental protection. = 预算的很大一部分已被指定用于环境保护。


疑问句:
Have you earmarked any funds for this initiative? = 你为这项倡议拨款了吗？
What portion of the budget is earmarked for marketing? = 预算的哪一部分用于营销？


否定句:
They did not earmark enough money for the repairs. = 他们没有为维修拨够款。
The company has not earmarked any funds for new equipment. = 公司没有为新设备拨款。


PHRASE->
earmark for = 为…拨款
earmark funds = 拨款
earmark something for something = 将某物指定用于某事
earmarked for = 专款用于
earmarked budget = 专款预算
