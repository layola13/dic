homopolic:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈpɒlɪk/ | adj. | 同族的；同源的 | Relating to or denoting a chemical compound in which the constituent atoms are all of the same element.  A homopolic compound is composed entirely of one type of atom, such as a homonuclear diatomic molecule like O2 (oxygen) or a chain of identical atoms like sulfur (S8). | Example:  The study focused on the properties of homopolic sulfur chains.  这项研究的重点是同族硫链的特性。| 近义词: homonuclear, monatomic (in some contexts) | 反义词: heteropolic, heteronuclear
