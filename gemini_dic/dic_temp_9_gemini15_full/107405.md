laminators:/ˈlæmɪneɪtərz/| n.|层压机；层压工|A laminator is a machine or person that laminates.  It can refer to a machine that bonds layers of material together, often using heat and pressure, or a person who operates such a machine.|例句：The company invested in a new laminator to improve efficiency. 这家公司投资了一台新的层压机以提高效率。

The skilled laminator carefully aligned the sheets before pressing them.  熟练的层压工在压制之前仔细对齐了薄片。|近义词：laminating machine, press|反义词：None specifically, but words implying separation or dismantling could be considered antonyms in a contextual sense.|名词
