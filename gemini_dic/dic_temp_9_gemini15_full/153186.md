pellets:/ˈpɛləts/ | n. |颗粒；小球；药丸 |Small, compressed grains or particles of a substance.  They can be made of various materials and used for different purposes. |例句：The farmer fed the chickens with pellets of feed.  农民用饲料颗粒喂鸡。

例句：The doctor prescribed him some sleeping pellets. 医生给他开了些安眠药丸。

近义词：granules, globules, beads |反义词：powder (in some contexts)
