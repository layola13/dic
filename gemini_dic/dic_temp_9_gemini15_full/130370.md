non-unity: /ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪti/ | adj. | 非统一性，不统一 | Lack of unity; a state of not being united or uniform.  It refers to a situation where things are diverse, disparate, or not working together harmoniously. | The non-unity of the team led to their defeat.  团队的不团结导致了他们的失败。 |  disunity, fragmentation, discord | unity, harmony, coherence | adj.
