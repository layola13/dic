federalize:/ˈfɛdərəlaɪz/ | vt. |使联邦化；联邦化 | To bring under the control of or unite into a federal government.  This involves consolidating power at a national level, often from previously independent states or regions. |例句：The states attempted to federalize the militia. 各州试图将民兵联邦化。|例句：The movement aimed to federalize the railway system. 该运动旨在将铁路系统联邦化。|近义词：nationalize, centralize |反义词：decentralize, regionalize
