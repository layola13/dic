palliatively:/ˈpæliətivli/| adv.|缓解地；姑息性地|In a way that relieves symptoms without curing the underlying disease.  It aims to improve the quality of life for patients with serious illnesses.|例句：The patient is being treated palliatively for her advanced cancer.  = 这位病人正在接受姑息治疗以缓解其晚期癌症的症状。|例句：They provided palliative care to ease her suffering. = 他们提供姑息治疗以减轻她的痛苦。|近义词：soothingly, comfortingly, supportively|反义词：curatively, radically

词源:
