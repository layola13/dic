latinist:/ˈlætɪnɪst/| n. |拉丁学者；拉丁语专家|A Latinist is a scholar or expert in the Latin language and its literature.  They often study classical Latin texts and may specialize in areas such as linguistics, history, or literature related to the Roman world. |Example:  He is a renowned Latinist, having published several influential books on Roman poetry.|近义词：classicist (if focusing on classical literature), philologist (if focusing on language)|反义词：无
