peg:/pɛɡ/| n. vt.|木钉；桩；销子；记号；(衣服的)撑子；(价格等的)固定；理解；记住| A peg is a small, usually cylindrical piece of wood or metal used to fasten something or to hang something on.  It can also refer to a fixed level or standard, or to the act of fixing something at a particular level.|例句：He hammered a peg into the wall. 他把钉子钉进墙里。

He used pegs to hang his clothes. 他用衣架挂衣服。

近义词：pin, spike, dowel|反义词：unfasten, loosen|名词,动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I peg the clothes on the line. = 我把衣服挂在绳子上。
He pegs the price at $10. = 他把价格定在10美元。


一般过去时:
She pegged the tent to the ground. = 她把帐篷钉在地上。
They pegged their hopes on a successful outcome. = 他们把希望寄托在成功的结果上。


现在完成时:
I have pegged the washing on the line. = 我已经把洗好的衣服晾在绳子上了。
They have pegged the inflation rate at 2%. = 他们已将通货膨胀率确定为2%。


过去完成时:
He had pegged the picture to the wall before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，他已经把画钉在墙上了。
They had pegged their hopes on a miracle. = 他们曾寄希望于奇迹。


将来时:
I will peg the notice on the board. = 我将把通知钉在布告栏上。
She will peg the price according to market demand. = 她将根据市场需求定价。


虚拟语气:
If I pegged the price higher, I would have made more profit. = 如果我把价格定得更高，我就会赚更多钱。
I wish I had pegged the market correctly. = 我真希望我当初正确地预测了市场。


被动语态:
The tent was pegged securely to the ground. = 帐篷牢固地钉在地上。
The price was pegged at $50. = 价格定在50美元。


疑问句:
Where did you peg the map? = 你把地图钉在哪里了？
How did you peg the value of the property? = 你是如何评估该房产价值的？


否定句:
I didn't peg him as a thief. = 我没把他看作小偷。
They didn't peg the correct price for the product. = 他们没有为该产品定出正确的价格。


PHRASE->
peg away at:  坚持不懈地做某事 (to keep working steadily at something)
peg down: 固定；确定 (to fix or determine something)
peg out: 死亡 (to die)
peg something to something: 把…与…联系起来 (to link or connect something to something else)
peg one's hopes on: 把希望寄托于… (to place one's hopes on something)


词源:
The word "peg" originates from Old English *pæc, meaning "a pointed stick," which is related to the Old Norse word *pakki, meaning "bundle," and ultimately derived from Proto-Germanic *pakō,  meaning "a pointed stake or peg".  The sense of "to fasten with a peg" developed later.
