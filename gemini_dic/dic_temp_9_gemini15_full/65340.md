eukaryotes:/ˈjuːkæri.oʊts/ | n. |真核生物| Organisms whose cells contain a nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles.  They are contrasted with prokaryotes, which lack these structures. Eukaryotes include animals, plants, fungi, and protists. | Example:  Eukaryotes are characterized by their complex cellular organization. 真核生物的特征是其复杂的细胞组织。

The study of eukaryotes is crucial to understanding the diversity of life on Earth.  对真核生物的研究对于理解地球上生命的多样性至关重要。|近义词:  higher organisms (less precise)|反义词: prokaryotes
