internuncios: /ˌɪntərˈnʌnʃioʊz/ | n. | 调停者；中间人 |  A person who acts as a mediator or messenger between two parties.  The word is rarely used in modern English and is largely obsolete.  It suggests a more formal or official intermediary than simply a mediator. |  The internuncios worked tirelessly to broker a peace agreement between the warring factions.  调停者不懈努力促成交战各派之间的和平协议。| mediator, intermediary, negotiator | antagonist, opponent | 名词
