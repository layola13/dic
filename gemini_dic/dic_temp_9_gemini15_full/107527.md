landfilling:/ˈlændˌfɪlɪŋ/| n. v.|垃圾填埋；填埋场|The process of disposing of waste material by burying it in the ground.  It's a common method of waste disposal, but it has environmental drawbacks.|例句：The city is considering expanding its landfilling operations. = 该城市正在考虑扩大其垃圾填埋业务。

例句：Improper landfilling can contaminate groundwater. = 不当的垃圾填埋会污染地下水。

近义词：dumping, burying, disposal|反义词：recycling, composting, reusing|词性:n./v.
