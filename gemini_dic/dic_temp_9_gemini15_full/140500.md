oldly:/ˈoʊldli/ | adv. | 老式地；古老地；年迈地 | In a way that is characteristic of the past; in an old-fashioned manner.  The word describes actions or states performed or existing in an old-fashioned way. |例句：She dressed oddly, in a style oldly reminiscent of the Victorian era. 她穿着古怪，风格古老地让人想起维多利亚时代。

She spoke oddly, using language oldly fashioned and rarely heard. 她说话古怪，使用古老的、很少听到的语言。


近义词：antiquely, archaically,  in an old-fashioned way | 反义词：newly, modernly, contemporarily | 词性:副词
