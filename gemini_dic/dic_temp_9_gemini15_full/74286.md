fracking:/ˈfrækɪŋ/| n. v.|水力压裂法，压裂采油|The process of injecting liquid at high pressure into subterranean rocks, boreholes, etc., so as to fracture the rock and release natural gas or petroleum.|例句：Fracking has revolutionized natural gas production in the United States. 水力压裂法彻底改变了美国的天然气生产。
例句：Environmental concerns surrounding fracking remain a significant issue.  围绕水力压裂法的环境问题仍然是一个重要的问题。|近义词：hydraulic fracturing, hydrofracturing|反义词：conventional drilling (常规钻井)|名词,动词
