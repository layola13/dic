saccharinate:/ˈsækəreɪn/| vt. adj.|使甜；加糖精于；甜的，过于甜的|To make something sweet, often excessively so, typically using artificial sweeteners.  It can also refer to something overly sentimental or excessively sweet in a mannered way.|例句：He saccharinated his coffee with an artificial sweetener. 他用人工甜味剂使咖啡变甜。  The movie's ending was saccharine and predictable. 电影的结尾过于甜腻且可预测。|近义词：sweeten, sugar, oversweeten |反义词：bitter, unsweetened
