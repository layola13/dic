homewards:/ˈhoʊmwərdz/| adv.|朝家的；回家方向地|Moving or directed toward home.|例句：The birds flew homewards at sunset.  傍晚时分，鸟儿们飞回了家。
例句：We set off homewards after a long day.  漫长的一天后，我们动身回家了。|近义词：homeward, homeward bound|反义词：away from home


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They walk homewards every evening. = 他们每天晚上都步行回家。
He always travels homewards by train. = 他总是乘火车回家。


一般过去时:
The family drove homewards after the picnic. = 野餐后，一家人开车回家。
She hurried homewards, fearing a storm. = 她匆忙赶回家，害怕暴风雨来临。


现在完成时:
We have walked homewards for hours. = 我们已经步行回家好几个小时了。
The children have been running homewards since school finished. = 放学后孩子们一直在跑回家。


过去完成时:
They had already started homewards before the rain began. = 下雨之前他们就已经开始回家了。
She had walked homewards for miles when she realized she’d lost her keys. = 她走了好几英里才意识到自己丢了钥匙。


将来时:
We will travel homewards tomorrow. = 我们明天将要回家。
They will set off homewards at dawn. = 他们将在黎明时出发回家。


虚拟语气:
If only I were homewards bound now! = 但愿我现在正回家！
I wish I were homewards, away from this noise. = 我希望我已经在家了，远离这个噪音。


被动语态:
(Homewards is an adverb and cannot be used in the passive voice.  A related phrase could be used, however.)  The journey home was completed. = 回家的旅程完成了。
The route homewards was clearly marked. = 回家的路线标示得很清楚。


疑问句:
Are you going homewards now? = 你现在要回家吗？
Is the train going homewards? = 火车是回家的吗？


否定句:
They didn't go homewards immediately. = 他们没有立即回家。
He wasn't walking homewards; he was going in the opposite direction. = 他不是往家走；他朝相反的方向走去。
