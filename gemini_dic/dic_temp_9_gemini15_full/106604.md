kuffiyeh:/ˈkʊfiːjə/| n.|头巾，阿拉伯头巾|A kufiyeh is a traditional Arab headdress, typically a checkered scarf worn by men.  It's often made of cotton or wool and is folded and worn in various ways. |He wore a red and white kufiyeh. 他戴着一块红白相间的头巾。  The old man's kufiyeh was slightly dusty.  老人的头巾有点脏。|keffiyeh, shemagh|  (无明确反义词)|名词
