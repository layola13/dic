mistrusters:/mɪsˈtrʌstərz/ | n. |不信任者，怀疑者 |Those who habitually distrust others; people prone to suspicion and disbelief.  |例句：The company's recent scandals have turned many of its loyal customers into mistrusters. 公司最近的丑闻让许多忠实顾客变成了怀疑者。

例句：Mistrusters often find it difficult to form close relationships. 怀疑者通常难以建立密切的关系。


近义词：skeptics, doubters, cynics, disbelievers | 反义词：believers, trusters
