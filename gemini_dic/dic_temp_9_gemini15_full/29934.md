cavalryman:/ˈkævəlriːmæn/ | n. |骑兵，骑兵队员 |A soldier who serves in a cavalry unit, typically mounted on a horse.  |例句：The cavalryman charged bravely into battle.  这名骑兵勇敢地冲入战场。|例句：A seasoned cavalryman, he knew the terrain well.  作为一名经验丰富的骑兵，他对地形非常熟悉。|近义词：trooper, horseman, cavalry soldier |反义词：infantryman
