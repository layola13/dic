pseudoapprehensive:/ˌsuːdoʊæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/| adj.|似是而非的忧虑，装作焦虑的 | seemingly apprehensive; pretending to be anxious or worried.  The word combines "pseudo" (false, pretended) with "apprehensive" (anxious or fearful). It describes a state where someone displays anxiety or worry, but this feeling might not be genuine.  |例句：His pseudoapprehensive glances at the exam paper were a transparent attempt to garner sympathy. 他假装焦虑地瞥着试卷，企图博得同情。

例句：Her pseudoapprehensive demeanor didn't fool anyone; we knew she was secretly thrilled. 她装作焦虑的样子骗不了任何人；我们知道她暗地里很兴奋。|近义词：feigned anxious, affectedly worried |反义词：calm, unconcerned, relaxed |词性: adjective
