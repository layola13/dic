ingrowing:/ˈɪŋɡroʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 向内长的；嵌入的 |  Growing inward; embedded in the surrounding tissue.  This often refers to hairs or nails. | Example:  He had an ingrowing toenail that was very painful. 他有一个非常疼痛的嵌甲。


Example: The surgeon removed the ingrowing hair. 外科医生去除了嵌入的毛发。


Near synonyms: embedded, inward-growing


Antonyms: outward-growing, protruding


Word type: Adjective
