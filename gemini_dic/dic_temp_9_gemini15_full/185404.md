retrospective:/ˌretrəˈspektɪv/| adj., n.|回顾性的；回顾展|Relating to or considering things past.|The museum is holding a retrospective of the artist's work. |近义词：retroactive, review, survey|反义词：prospective, future


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This exhibition is retrospective of her early works. = 这个展览回顾了她早期的作品。
The study takes a retrospective look at the events. = 这项研究回顾性地审视了这些事件。


一般过去时:
The critics gave a retrospective review of the film. = 批评家对这部电影进行了回顾性的评论。
They held a retrospective on the artist's life and career. = 他们举办了一个关于这位艺术家生平和事业的回顾展。


现在完成时:
We have had a retrospective look at our company's history. = 我们已经回顾了我们公司历史。
She has completed a retrospective analysis of the data. = 她已经完成了对数据的回顾性分析。


过去完成时:
By the time he died, he had already planned a retrospective of his career. = 他去世时，他已经计划好了一场回顾他职业生涯的展览。
They had finished the retrospective study before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了回顾性研究。


将来时:
The gallery will hold a retrospective exhibition next year. = 画廊明年将举办一个回顾展。
