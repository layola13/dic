garnishing:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 装饰；点缀；(菜肴的)装饰 | The act of decorating or embellishing something, especially food.  It often involves adding small items to enhance the visual appeal. | Example: The chef added a garnishing of fresh herbs to the dish.  厨师在菜肴上加了一些新鲜香草作为装饰。 |近义词：decoration, embellishment, ornamentation |反义词：simplification, plainness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef garnishes the plates with parsley. = 厨师用欧芹装饰盘子。
She garnishes her cakes with chocolate shavings. = 她用巧克力屑装饰她的蛋糕。


一般过去时:
He garnished the salad with croutons. = 他用面包丁装饰沙拉。
They garnished the cocktails with cherries. = 他们用樱桃装饰鸡尾酒。


现在完成时:
I have garnished the soup with a swirl of cream. = 我已经用一圈奶油装饰了汤。
She has garnished the dessert with edible flowers. = 她已经用食用花装饰了甜点。


过去完成时:
He had garnished the main course with roasted vegetables before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经用烤蔬菜装饰了主菜。
They had garnished the appetizer with microgreens for an elegant touch. = 为了增添优雅的感觉，他们已经用微型蔬菜装饰了开胃菜。


将来时:
I will garnish the pizza with fresh basil. = 我将用新鲜罗勒装饰披萨。
She will garnish the steak with a peppercorn sauce. = 她将用胡椒粒酱汁装饰牛排。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would garnish the cake more elaborately. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更精心地装饰蛋糕。
I wish I had garnished the fish with lemon slices. = 我真希望我用柠檬片装饰了鱼。


被动语态:
The dish was garnished with finely chopped onions. = 这道菜用切碎的洋葱装饰。
The cocktails are garnished with lime wedges. = 鸡尾酒用酸橙角装饰。


疑问句:
How do you garnish this pasta dish? = 你如何装饰这道意大利面？
What are you going to garnish the soup with? = 你要用什么来装饰汤？


否定句:
I didn't garnish the pie, it's simple. = 我没有装饰馅饼，它很简单。
She did not garnish the tacos, opting for a minimalist approach. = 她没有装饰玉米卷，选择了一种极简主义的方法。
