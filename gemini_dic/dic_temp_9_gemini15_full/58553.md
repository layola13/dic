duke:/dʒuːk/| n. |公爵|A male ruler of a duchy; a nobleman holding the highest hereditary title below a prince.|The Duke of Wellington commanded the British army at Waterloo.  威灵顿公爵在滑铁卢指挥英国军队。|Prince, Earl|Commoner, peasant|名词
