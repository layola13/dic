magister:/ˈmædʒɪstər/| n.|硕士；导师；大师|A magister is a master or teacher, particularly one holding a master's degree or a high academic rank.  The term is often used in historical or academic contexts.  In some contexts, it can also refer to a magistrate or a person of authority.|例句：He earned his magister degree in history. 他获得了历史学硕士学位。|He was a magister of rhetoric, known throughout the land. 他是一位雄辩大师，名扬天下。|近义词：master, teacher, professor, expert|反义词：student, novice, apprentice
