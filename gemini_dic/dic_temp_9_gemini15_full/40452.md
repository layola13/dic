contravallation:/ˌkɒntrəvæˈleɪʃən/| n. |包围；防御工事；围攻工事 |The act of surrounding a place with fortifications; a system of fortifications surrounding a besieged place.  It refers to the encircling of a fortified position by a line of defensive works.  |例句：The army constructed a contravallation around the enemy city, cutting off supplies and reinforcements. = 军队在敌城周围修建了防御工事，切断了敌军的补给和增援。

例句：The siege was successful due to the effective contravallation built by the besieging army. = 由于围攻军队修建了有效的包围工事，围攻取得了成功。


近义词：circumvallation, investment, blockade |反义词：relief, evacuation
