pretendingly: /prɪˈtɛndɪŋli/ | adv. | 假装地，做作地 | In a way that suggests pretense or feigning; affectedly.  | 例句：He spoke pretendingly, avoiding direct eye contact. 他说话做作，避免直接的眼神接触。  She smiled pretendingly, hiding her true feelings. 她假装微笑，掩饰她真实的情感。| 近义词：affectedly, artificially, falsely | 反义词：genuinely, sincerely, authentically
