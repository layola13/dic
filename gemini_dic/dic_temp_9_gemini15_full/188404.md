rubylike: /ˈruːbiˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 类似红宝石的；像红宝石的 | Resembling a ruby in color, brilliance, or other qualities.  The term often implies a deep red color with a rich, lustrous appearance. | 例句：The gemstone had a rubylike brilliance. 这颗宝石有着像红宝石一样的光泽。

例句：Her lipstick was a deep, rubylike shade. 她的口红是一种深邃的，像红宝石一样的颜色。


近义词：ruby-red, crimson, scarlet, garnet


反义词：pale, dull, lackluster


词性:形容词
