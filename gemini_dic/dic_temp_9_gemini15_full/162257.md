pre-warn:/priːˈwɔːrn/| vt.|预先警告，提前警告 |To warn someone in advance about something that might happen.  |例句：The company pre-warned its employees about the potential layoffs. 公司提前警告员工可能会有裁员。

例句：They pre-warned us about the severe weather conditions. 他们提前警告我们恶劣的天气状况。


近义词：forewarn, alert, notify, advise


反义词：surprise, ambush


词性:动词
