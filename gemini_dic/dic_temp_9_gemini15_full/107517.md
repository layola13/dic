landbased:/ˈlændˌbeɪst/| adj.|陆基的，陆地的|Based on or located on land, as opposed to being sea-based or air-based.  |例句：The landbased missile defense system is highly effective.  陆基导弹防御系统非常有效。

例句：The company is expanding its landbased operations. 这家公司正在扩大其陆地业务。


近义词：terrestrial, onshore

反义词：sea-based, ocean-based, air-based
