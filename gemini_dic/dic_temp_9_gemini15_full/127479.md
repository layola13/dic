necessitation:/ˌnɛsɛsɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |必要性；需要；强制；必然性 |The necessitation of the reform is undeniable. 改革的必要性不可否认。|The necessitation for immediate action is clear.  立即行动的必要性是显而易见的。|近义词：requirement, need, compulsion, obligation |反义词：optionality, choice, freedom


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation necessitates a change in policy. = 这种情况需要改变政策。
This job necessitates long hours. = 这份工作需要长时间工作。

一般过去时:
The war necessitated rationing. = 战争导致了配给制。
The emergency necessitated immediate action. = 紧急情况需要立即采取行动。

现在完成时:
Recent events have necessitated a review of our security procedures. = 最近发生的事件需要我们审查安全程序。
The crisis has necessitated a change of plans. = 危机迫使我们改变计划。

过去完成时:
The previous administration's actions had necessitated a complete overhaul of the system. = 前一届政府的行动迫使我们对系统进行了彻底的改革。
The delay had necessitated a postponement of the meeting. = 延误迫使会议推迟。

将来时:
Future developments will necessitate further investment. = 未来的发展将需要进一步的投资。
The project will necessitate significant resources. = 该项目将需要大量的资源。

虚拟语气:
It would necessitate a complete rewrite of the code. = 这将需要对代码进行完整的重写。
If we did that, it would necessitate a lot of extra work. = 如果我们那样做，那就需要很多额外的工作。

被动语态:
A change in policy was necessitated by the crisis. = 危机迫使政策发生变化。
Further investment is necessitated by the expansion of the business. = 业务扩张需要进一步的投资。

疑问句:
Does this situation necessitate immediate action? = 这种情况需要立即采取行动吗？
What does this project necessitate in terms of resources? = 就资源而言，这个项目需要什么？


否定句:
The situation does not necessitate drastic measures. = 这种情况不需要采取严厉措施。
This task doesn't necessitate any special skills. = 这项任务不需要任何特殊技能。
