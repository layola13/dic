reattachable: /ˌriːəˈtætʃəbl/ | adj. | 可重新连接的，可重新附着的 | Able to be attached again.  This adjective describes something that can be fastened or connected again after being detached.  | 例句：The cable is reattachable, so don't worry if you disconnect it accidentally.  这条电缆是可以重新连接的，所以不用担心你意外断开它。

The clasp on the necklace is reattachable, making it easy to wear and remove.  项链上的扣子是可以重新连接的，使得佩戴和取下都很方便。


近义词：reconnectable, refastenable | 反义词：permanently attached, irremovable | 词性：形容词

词源:  The word is formed by combining "re-" (again) + "attach" (to fasten) + "-able" (capable of being).  Therefore, it literally means "capable of being attached again."
