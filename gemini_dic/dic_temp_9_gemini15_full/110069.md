licheniform:/ˈlaɪkənɪfɔːrm/| adj.|苔藓状的，地衣样的|Resembling lichen, especially in its flat, crusty appearance.  The term is often used to describe skin lesions. |例句：The rash on his arm presented a licheniform appearance. 他胳膊上的皮疹呈现苔藓状外观。 |例句：The chronic dermatitis exhibited a characteristic licheniform pattern. 慢性皮炎表现出特征性的苔藓状模式。|近义词：scaly, crusty,  papular|反义词：smooth, unblemished
