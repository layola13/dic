sizer:/ˈsaɪzər/| n. |尺寸调整器；分级器；筛分机 |A sizer is a device or tool used to determine or adjust the size of something.  This can refer to various applications, from sorting materials by size to adjusting the size of something during manufacturing.  |例句：The factory uses a sophisticated sizer to sort the grains by size before packaging. 工厂在包装前使用精密的分级器对谷物进行分级。
