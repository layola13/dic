nontransferability: /ˌnɒnˌtrænsfərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可转让性 | The inability to transfer something, such as ownership, rights, or benefits.  This refers to the state or quality of being nontransferable.  | Example: The nontransferability of the license prevents its sale or assignment to another person.  不可转让的许可证阻止其出售或转让给他人。 | 近义词：inalienability, untransferability | 反义词：transferability
