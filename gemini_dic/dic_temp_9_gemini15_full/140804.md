on-draft: /ˌɒnˈdrɑːft/ | adj. | 生啤的，现酿的 |  Describes beer that is served directly from a keg, rather than from a bottle or can. This typically implies a fresher taste and often a lower price. |  例句：The pub has a great selection of beers on draft. 这家酒吧有各种各样的生啤。

例句：This brewery offers several unique beers on draft. 这家啤酒厂提供几种独特的生啤。


近义词：unbottled, from the tap, keg


反义词：bottled, canned


词性:形容词
