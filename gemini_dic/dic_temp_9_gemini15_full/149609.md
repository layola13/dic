/ˈpeɪnˌkɪlər/| n. |止痛药；镇痛剂 |A painkiller is a medicine that reduces or eliminates pain.  It can be an over-the-counter medication or a prescription drug, depending on the strength and type of pain. |例句：She took a painkiller for her headache. 她服用了止痛药来缓解头痛。

例句：The doctor prescribed a strong painkiller to manage his post-operative pain. 医生开了强效止痛药来控制他的术后疼痛。

近义词：analgesic, pain reliever |反义词：pain inducer (无确切的反义词，通常用描述性词语表达)|名词
