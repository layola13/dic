embargoed:/ɛmˈbɑːrɡoʊd/ | adj. | 被禁运的，被禁止的 |  Officially prevented from trade or other activity; subject to an embargo.  | The shipment was embargoed due to international sanctions. 这批货物由于国际制裁而被禁运。 The information was embargoed until after the press conference.  该信息在新闻发布会后才解禁。|  banned, prohibited, restricted | unrestricted, permitted, allowed | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government embargoes certain goods. = 政府禁止某些商品贸易。
This country embargoes weapons. = 这个国家禁止武器贸易。

一般过去时:
The country embargoed oil exports last year. = 该国去年禁止石油出口。
They embargoed the news until the investigation was complete. = 他们将消息禁运到调查完成为止。

现在完成时:
The UN has embargoed arms sales to the region. = 联合国已经禁止向该地区出售武器。
Several countries have embargoed goods from that nation. = 几个国家已经禁止了来自那个国家的商品。

过去完成时:
The company had embargoed the report before the leak. = 公司在泄露之前就已经禁运了该报告。
They had embargoed the information for years. = 他们已经禁运该信息多年了。

将来时:
The government will embargo the sale of certain chemicals. = 政府将禁止销售某些化学品。
They will embargo the new technology until it's fully tested. = 他们将在新技术完全测试完毕之前将其禁运。

虚拟语气:
If the president embargoed the trade, the economy would suffer. = 如果总统禁止贸易，经济就会遭受损失。
I wish they hadn't embargoed the information. = 我真希望他们没有禁运该信息。

被动语态:
The goods were embargoed by the government. = 这些货物被政府禁运了。
The news release was embargoed until Monday. = 新闻稿在星期一前都被禁运。

疑问句:
Are these goods embargoed? = 这些货物被禁运了吗？
Was the information embargoed? = 该信息被禁运了吗？

否定句:
The goods were not embargoed. = 这些货物没有被禁运。
The information was not embargoed. = 该信息没有被禁运。


PHRASE->
embargo on arms = 武器禁运
embargo on trade = 贸易禁运
news embargo = 新闻禁运
under embargo = 在禁运之下
to lift an embargo = 解除禁运
