countermanifesto:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænɪfɛstoʊ/| n. |反宣言；对宣言的反驳|A countermanifesto is a public declaration that contradicts or refutes the claims or arguments presented in a previous manifesto.  It often arises in response to a political or ideological statement, offering an opposing viewpoint or perspective.  |Example: The dissident group released a countermanifesto, directly challenging the government's proposed reforms.  = 异见团体发布了一份反宣言，直接挑战政府提出的改革方案。|近义词：rebuttal, refutation, counter-statement|反义词：manifesto
