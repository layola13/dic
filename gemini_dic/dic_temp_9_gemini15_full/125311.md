multigroup:/ˈmʌltiɡruːp/| n.|多组的；多群的|Relating to or involving multiple groups.  The term often appears in statistical analysis and social science contexts, referring to the analysis of data from multiple groups simultaneously.|例句：The researchers used multigroup analysis to compare the effectiveness of the treatment across different age groups.  研究人员使用多组分析来比较不同年龄组治疗的有效性。|近义词：multifaceted, polygroup|反义词：single-group, monogroup

词源:
The word "multigroup" is a compound word formed from "multi-" (meaning many or multiple) and "group".  The prefix "multi-" has Latin origins (multus, meaning "many"), while "group" is derived from the Old French word "groupe," ultimately from the Italian word "gruppo".
