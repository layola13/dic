experimentations:/ˌɛkspɛrɪmenˈteɪʃənz/| n. |实验；试验；尝试|The plural form of "experimentation," referring to multiple instances of conducting experiments or trying new things.  It emphasizes the process of exploring and testing various approaches. |例句：The scientist's experimentations with new materials led to a breakthrough. 科学家对新材料的实验取得了突破。  His experimentations in the kitchen resulted in a delicious, albeit unexpected, dish. 他在厨房里的尝试做出了美味的，虽然意想不到的菜肴。|近义词：trials, tests, explorations, investigations|反义词：routine, standardization
