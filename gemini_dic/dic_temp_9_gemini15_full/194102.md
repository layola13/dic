self-impairing: /ˌsɛlfɪmˈpɛərɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我损害的，自我破坏的 |  Describes actions, behaviors, or conditions that harm or damage oneself. It implies a deliberate or unintentional act that negatively impacts one's well-being, physical health, or mental state. |


例句：

His self-impairing behavior, such as excessive drinking, led to serious health problems. = 他自我损害的行为，例如酗酒，导致了严重的健康问题。

The study explored the factors contributing to self-impairing tendencies in adolescents. = 这项研究探讨了导致青少年自我损害倾向的因素。


近义词：self-destructive, self-defeating, harmful, damaging


反义词：self-enhancing, beneficial, helpful, constructive


词性：形容词
