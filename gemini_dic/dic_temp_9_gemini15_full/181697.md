relevantly:/ˈrɛləvəntli/ | adv. | 相关地；贴切地；适当地 | In a way that is relevant.  It means something is connected to or appropriate for a particular situation or subject. | Example: He spoke relevantly to the issue at hand. 他恰当地谈到了手头的问题。 |近义词：appropriately, pertinently, aptly | 反义词：irrelevantly, inappropriately
