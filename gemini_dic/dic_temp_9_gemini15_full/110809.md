linkwork:/ˈlɪŋkˌwɜːrk/ | n. |联动装置；联动机构；链条工作 |Linkwork refers to a system of interconnected parts that work together, often involving chains, levers, or other mechanisms to transmit motion or force.  It's frequently used in mechanical engineering to describe complex systems of moving parts.  |Example: The intricate linkwork of the grandfather clock ensured its precise timekeeping.  复杂的座钟的联动装置确保了其精确的计时。  |近义词：mechanism, linkage, gearing |反义词：N/A
