shamateur:/ʃæmətˈɜːr/ | adj. | 半吊子，冒牌专家 | A sham amateur is someone who pretends to be skilled or knowledgeable in a particular field but actually lacks genuine expertise.  They often display a superficial understanding and may even be incompetent.  This term combines "sham" (meaning fake or false) and "amateur" (meaning someone who engages in an activity for pleasure rather than professionally). | He's a shamateur when it comes to cooking; his dishes look impressive but taste terrible. 他在烹饪方面是个半吊子；他的菜看起来很不错，但味道很糟糕。 |  pretender, dilettante, poser | expert, professional, proficient |
