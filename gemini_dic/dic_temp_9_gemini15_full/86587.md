heliochromy:/ˌhiːliˈkroʊmi/| n. |日光显色；日照显色|The process by which colors are produced or modified by the action of sunlight.  It often refers to the change in color of certain substances when exposed to sunlight.  |Example: The heliochromy of the paint was remarkable, shifting subtly from azure to violet in different light conditions.  日光使颜料的颜色发生了显著的变化，在不同的光线下，它会从蔚蓝色微妙地转变为紫色。|近义词：photochromism, solarization|反义词：None specifically, but concepts like "color stability" or "color constancy" could be considered opposites.|名词
