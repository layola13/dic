led:/led/ | v. n. adj. |领导，引导；照亮；导致；发光二极管 |

英文解析：As a verb, "led" is the past tense and past participle of "lead."  It means to guide, direct, or conduct someone or something. As a noun, it refers to a light-emitting diode, a semiconductor device that emits light when an electric current is passed through it. As an adjective, it can be used to describe something that is prominent or leading.

例句：

The general led his troops into battle. = 将军带领他的军队投入战斗。

She led the way through the forest. = 她带路穿过森林。


The LED lights are energy-efficient. = LED灯很节能。

This company is a leader in the tech industry.  = 这家公司是科技行业的领导者。

近义词：
(verb) guided, conducted, directed, managed
(noun) diode, light


反义词：
(verb) followed, trailed


词性:
verb (past tense and past participle of lead), noun, adjective
