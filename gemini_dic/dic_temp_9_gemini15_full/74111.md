fought:/fɔːt/| v.|战斗，搏斗，争论|The past tense of "fight".  It describes a past action of engaging in a fight, struggle, or argument.|例句：The soldiers fought bravely in the battle.  士兵们在战斗中英勇作战。|例句：They fought over the last piece of cake. 他们为最后一块蛋糕争吵。|近义词：battled, struggled, contended|反义词：cooperated, surrendered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fight every day. = 他们每天都打架。
He fights for justice. = 他为正义而战。


一般过去时:
We fought hard to win the game. = 我们努力拼搏赢得了比赛。
She fought against the injustice. = 她与不公正作斗争。


现在完成时:
They have fought many battles. = 他们打过许多仗。
He has fought for his beliefs all his life. = 他为自己的信仰奋斗了一生。


过去完成时:
They had fought for hours before they finally surrendered. = 在他们最终投降之前，他们已经战斗了几个小时。
She had fought tirelessly for equality before she achieved it. = 在她获得平等之前，她为此不知疲倦地奋斗过。


将来时:
We will fight for our rights. = 我们将为我们的权利而战。
They will fight to the end. = 他们将战斗到最后。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would have fought harder. = 如果我更强壮，我会更努力地战斗。
I wish I had fought for what I believed in. = 我希望我曾经为我所信仰的东西而战。


被动语态:
The battle was fought fiercely. = 这场战斗打得很激烈。

疑问句:

否定句:
