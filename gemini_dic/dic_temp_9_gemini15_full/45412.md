cuppas:/ˈkʌpəz/| n. |茶(复数)|Cuppas is a colloquial and informal plural of "cuppa," a shortened form of "cup of tea." It refers to multiple cups of tea.  | Example: "Let's have some cuppas and chat." =  "让我们喝几杯茶聊聊。" |近义词:cups of tea, infusions|反义词:None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We have cuppas every afternoon. = 我们每天下午都喝茶。
They drink cuppas with biscuits. = 他们喝茶配饼干。


一般过去时:
We had cuppas after the game. = 比赛结束后我们喝了茶。
She made cuppas for everyone. = 她为每个人都泡了茶。


现在完成时:
I've had several cuppas today. = 我今天已经喝了好几杯茶了。
They've finished their cuppas. = 他们已经喝完茶了。


过去完成时:
We had already had our cuppas before they arrived. = 他们到达之前我们已经喝完茶了。
She had made cuppas for the guests before they came. = 客人来之前她已经为他们泡好了茶。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had some cuppas right now. = 我真希望现在能喝点茶。
