/ˈdʒɛŋɡɪs/| n.|成吉思汗|Genghis Khan, also known as Chinggis Khan, was the founder and first Great Khan of the Mongol Empire, which became the largest contiguous land empire in history. He united many of the nomadic tribes of Northeast Asia and led them to conquer much of Eurasia.|Genghis Khan united the Mongol tribes and conquered vast territories. |近义词：Chinggis Khan|反义词：无
