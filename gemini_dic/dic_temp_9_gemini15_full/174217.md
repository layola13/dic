quenches:/kwɛntʃɪz/| vt.| 熄灭；解渴；抑制；满足|To satisfy (thirst, hunger, etc.); to extinguish (fire, etc.); to suppress (feeling, etc.).|例句：He quenched his thirst with a glass of water. 他喝了一杯水解渴。|例句：The firefighters quickly quenched the fire. 消防员迅速扑灭了大火。|近义词：satisfy, extinguish, suppress, slake|反义词：ignite, inflame, stimulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cool water quenches my thirst. = 凉水解渴。
This drink quenches my thirst on a hot day. = 在炎热的一天，这种饮料解我的渴。

一般过去时:

The firefighters have quenched the blaze. = 消防员已经扑灭了大火。

过去完成时:
They had quenched their thirst before they began the climb. = 他们在开始攀登之前已经解渴了。
The rain had quenched the flames before the firefighters arrived. = 在消防员到达之前，雨水已经扑灭了火焰。

将来时:
The new sprinkler system will quickly quench any fires. = 新的洒水系统将迅速扑灭任何火灾。
