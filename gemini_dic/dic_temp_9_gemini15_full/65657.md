evaporometer:/ɪˌvæpəˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. |蒸发器，蒸发计 |An instrument for measuring the rate of evaporation of water.  It typically consists of a container of water with a known surface area, from which the evaporation is measured over a period of time. |Example:  The evaporometer recorded a high rate of evaporation yesterday due to the heat and wind.  昨天蒸发计记录到了很高的蒸发速率，因为天气炎热且风大。|近义词：evaporation gauge, atmometer |反义词：None directly opposite, but terms like "hydrometer" (measuring liquid density) could be considered contrasting in function.
