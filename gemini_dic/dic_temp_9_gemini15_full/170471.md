词源:

The word "prunelle" comes from the Old French word *prunelle*, which itself ultimately derives from the Latin word *pruna*, meaning "a burning coal" or "a plum". The connection lies in the resemblance of the dark pupil to a small, dark, glowing ember or a dark plum.
