fractious:/ˈfrækʃəs/| adj.|易怒的；脾气暴躁的；难驾驭的；难以控制的 |  easily annoyed or angered; irritable; unruly; difficult to control. | The fractious child threw a tantrum in the store.  那个易怒的孩子在商店里大发脾气。|The fractious debate continued for hours.  这场激烈的辩论持续了数小时。| irritable, unruly, troublesome, contentious | calm, docile, placid, agreeable
