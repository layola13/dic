ipecacuanha: /ɪˌpɛkəˈkwɑːnə/ | n. | 吐根 | Ipecacuanha is a plant whose root is used to induce vomiting.  It is a powerful emetic, historically used in medicine, but now largely replaced by safer alternatives due to its toxicity. |  The doctor prescribed ipecacuanha to induce vomiting after the child ingested poison.  医生开了吐根以在孩子摄入毒药后催吐。|  emetic |  antidote (in the context of its counteraction by antidotes)
