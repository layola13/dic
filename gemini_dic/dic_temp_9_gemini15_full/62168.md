encoders:/ɪnˈkoʊdərz/ | n. |编码器，编码装置 |A device or system that converts information into a code. Encoders are used in various applications to translate data into a format suitable for transmission, storage, or processing.  |Example: The video encoder compressed the high-resolution footage into a smaller file size.  视频编码器将高分辨率的素材压缩成更小的文件大小。|  converter, translator, transcoder | decoder | 名词
