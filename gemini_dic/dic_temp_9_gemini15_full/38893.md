USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confers firmness to the argument. 他使论点更加坚实。
The teacher confers structure to the lesson. 老师使课程更有条理。

一般过去时:
The process conferred stability to the structure.  该过程使结构更加稳定。
The agreement conferred legitimacy to the organization. 该协议使该组织合法化。

现在完成时:
The new policy has conferred greater efficiency on the company. 新政策已使公司效率更高。
The recent reforms have conferred more power to the executive branch. 最近的改革赋予了行政部门更大的权力。

过去完成时:
They had conferred additional strength to the bridge before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，他们已经加强了桥梁的强度。

将来时:
The new technology will confer significant advantages to our business. 新技术将为我们的业务带来显著优势。
The upcoming election will confer the power to a new president.  即将到来的选举将把权力赋予一位新总统。

虚拟语气:
If the project were completed, it would confer a great benefit to the community. 如果项目完成，它将给社区带来巨大的好处。
If we had acted sooner, we could have conferred more stability on the market. 如果我们早点采取行动，我们本可以使市场更稳定。

被动语态:
Stability was conferred upon the nation by the new leader. 新领导人给国家带来了稳定。
Greater efficiency was conferred to the system by the upgrade. 系统升级带来了更高的效率。

疑问句:
What benefits will this new treatment confer?  这种新疗法将带来什么好处？

否定句:

The weak foundation did not confer sufficient stability to the building. 脆弱的地基没有给建筑物提供足够的稳定性。
