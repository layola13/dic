employs:/ɪmˈplɔɪz/| vt.|雇佣；使用；采用|To make use of something, someone's services, or someone's abilities.|例句：The company employs over 500 people. 这家公司雇佣了500多名员工。|例句：This method employs a new technology.  这种方法采用了一种新技术。|近义词：hires, uses, utilizes|反义词：fires, dismisses, releases|动词
