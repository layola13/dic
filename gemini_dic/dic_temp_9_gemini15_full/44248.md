croesus:/ˈkruːsəs/| n.|克洛伊索斯；巨富；暴发户|A fabulously wealthy person;  a very rich person, often implying that wealth was acquired quickly or unexpectedly.|例句：He lives like a Croesus, spending lavishly on luxury goods. 他像克洛伊索斯一样生活，挥霍无度地购买奢侈品。|近义词：millionaire, billionaire, tycoon|反义词：pauper, beggar
