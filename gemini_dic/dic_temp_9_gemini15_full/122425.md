moanfully:/ˈmoʊnfʊli/ | adj. | 悲叹地；痛苦地；抱怨地 | Expressing or characterized by a moan; mournful.  It describes something done or said in a way that expresses sadness, pain, or complaint. |例句：She spoke moanfully of her lost love. 她悲痛地谈论着她失去的爱人。

例句：He looked moanfully at the ruined house. 他悲哀地望着那座毁坏的房子。


近义词： sadly, sorrowfully, dolefully, mournfully, plaintively


反义词： joyfully, happily, cheerfully, merrily


词性：形容词
