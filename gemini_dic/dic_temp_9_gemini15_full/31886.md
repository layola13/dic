cheery-bye:/ˈtʃɪəribaɪ/| int.|愉快的再见，欢快的道别|A cheerful way of saying goodbye, often used informally. It combines the feeling of "cheery" (happy and cheerful) with the action of saying "bye".|例句：We exchanged a cheery-bye before heading off in different directions. 我们在朝不同方向出发前互相愉快的道别了。


例句：It was a cheery-bye, filled with promises to meet again soon.  这是一个愉快的道别，充满了很快再相见的承诺。


近义词：cheerful goodbye, happy farewell, bright farewell |反义词：sad farewell, somber goodbye


词性:感叹词
