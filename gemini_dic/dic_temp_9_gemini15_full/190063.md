sandstorms:/ˈsændˌstɔːrmz/| n. |沙尘暴 |A sandstorm is a strong wind that carries a large amount of sand and dust, reducing visibility and causing significant environmental and health impacts.  Sandstorms often occur in arid and semi-arid regions.|例句：The sandstorm reduced visibility to almost zero. 沙尘暴导致能见度几乎为零。

The fierce sandstorm buried the village under tons of sand.  猛烈的沙尘暴将村庄埋在了数吨的沙子下。|近义词：dust storm, sand blizzard |反义词: calm, breeze
