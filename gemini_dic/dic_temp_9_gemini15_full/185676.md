reverify:/ˌriːˈvɛrɪfaɪ/| vt.|再次验证；复查|To verify something again; to check something again to ensure its accuracy or validity.  |例句：We need to reverify the data before submitting the report.我们需要在提交报告之前再次验证数据。|例句：The police decided to reverify the witness's testimony.警方决定再次核实证人的证词。|近义词：recheck, double-check, confirm, validate|反义词：disprove, invalidate
