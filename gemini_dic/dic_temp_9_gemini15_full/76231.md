future-proofing:/ˈfjuːtʃəpruːfɪŋ/ | n. v. |未雨绸缪；使免受未来冲击 |  The act of making something resistant to future changes or challenges.  It can refer to a process, a plan, or a product. |例句：The company is future-proofing its operations by investing in renewable energy.  这家公司正在通过投资可再生能源来为其运营做好未来的准备。

The company's future-proofing strategy includes developing adaptable products and diversifying its market. 这家公司的未来防范战略包括开发适应性产品和多元化市场。| 近义词：preparation, precaution, safeguarding | 反义词：neglect, unpreparedness

We are future-proofing our software. = 我们正在使我们的软件免受未来冲击。
They future-proof their investments carefully. = 他们仔细地为他们的投资做好未来的准备。
