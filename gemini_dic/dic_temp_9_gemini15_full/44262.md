croissant:/krɔɪˈsɑːnt/ | n. |羊角面包|A crescent-shaped roll of rich, buttery pastry, typically made with layered dough.  It is a popular breakfast pastry in many countries.|例句：I bought a delicious croissant from the bakery this morning.  今天早上我在面包店买了一个美味的羊角面包。|近义词：pastry, roll|反义词：无
