例句：The classic lines of the coupe are timeless.  这辆双门轿跑车的经典线条永不过时。|近义词：sports car, roadster (depending on features)|反义词：sedan, station wagon|名词

词源:
The word "coupe" comes from the French word "coupé," the past participle of the verb "couper," meaning "to cut" or "to shorten."  This refers to the shortened, more streamlined body style of the car compared to a sedan.
