jeering:/ˈdʒɪərɪŋ/| n.|嘲笑，讥讽|The act of making fun of someone or something in a scornful or contemptuous way.  It often involves loud, mocking laughter or shouts.|例句：The jeering from the crowd was deafening.  人群的嘲笑声震耳欲聋。
例句：He ignored the jeering of his classmates. 他无视了同学们的嘲笑。|近义词：scoffing, mocking, taunting, derision|反义词：praise, applause, admiration|名词
