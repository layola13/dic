slippages:/ˈslɪpɪdʒɪz/| n.|滑移；差距；疏忽；下降|The difference between planned and actual performance, often used in project management and financial contexts.  It refers to the failure to meet targets or expectations.  It can also refer to a gradual decline or deterioration.|例句：The project experienced significant slippages in its timeline due to unforeseen circumstances.  项目由于不可预见的情况导致时间表出现重大延误。

The company's market share showed noticeable slippages in the last quarter.  公司上季度市场份额出现明显下降。|近义词：delays, shortfalls, failures, deviations|反义词：improvements, advances, successes
