self-disservices:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈsɜːrvɪsɪz/| n.|自我损害的行为；自我服务的行为导致的不良后果|Acts that harm oneself; negative consequences resulting from self-serving actions.  The term often implies a lack of self-awareness or consideration for the potential negative impacts of one's actions.|例句：His self-disservices led to his downfall. = 他的自我损害行为导致了他的垮台。 His constant self-disservices in the workplace, like missing deadlines and neglecting tasks, ultimately cost him his job. = 他在工作中不断自我损害的行为，例如错过最后期限和忽视任务，最终让他丢了工作。|近义词：self-sabotage, self-destruction, self-harm|反义词：self-care, self-preservation|名词
