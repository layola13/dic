kabal:/ˈkeɪbəl/| n.|秘密组织；阴谋集团|A secret group of people, usually involved in something evil or illegal.  The term often implies a clandestine and powerful nature, often with occult or conspiratorial overtones.|例句：The journalist uncovered a kabal of corrupt officials.  记者揭露了一个腐败官员的秘密组织。  The rumor of a powerful kabal controlling the government spread like wildfire.  控制政府的强大秘密组织的谣言像野火一样蔓延。|近义词：clique, cabal, conspiracy, ring, mafia|反义词：open group, public organization
