overtrusts:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌsts/| v.|过度信任，过分相信|To place excessive or unwarranted trust in someone or something; to have too much faith in. |例句：He overtrusts his employees and often suffers the consequences. 他过度信任他的员工，经常为此付出代价。  He overtrusts his instincts and often makes poor decisions. 他过度依赖直觉，经常做出糟糕的决定。|近义词：trust excessively, rely too heavily on, have blind faith in|反义词：distrust, doubt, suspect
