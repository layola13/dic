pot-hole:/ˈpɑːtˌhoʊl/ /ˈpɒtˌhəʊl/| n.|坑洞，路面坑洼|A pothole is a hole in a road surface, caused by wear and tear or damage.  It's usually caused by water seeping into cracks in the road and freezing, expanding and breaking up the road surface.|例句：The car hit a pothole and blew a tire.  车子撞到一个坑洞，轮胎爆了。|例句：The city is responsible for repairing potholes on its streets. 市政府有责任维修街道上的坑洞。|近义词：crater, cavity, rut|反义词：smooth surface, level ground|名词
