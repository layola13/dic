laconism:/ˈlækənɪzəm/| n. |简洁; 简洁的风格; 寡言; |Conciseness or brevity of expression.  A laconism is a short, pithy saying or statement. It often involves the use of few words to convey a significant meaning.  The term is derived from the ancient Spartans, who were known for their brevity and terseness in speech.|例句：His speech was characterized by laconism; he got straight to the point.  他的演讲以简洁著称；他直奔主题。

例句：The poem's laconism belied its profound emotional depth. 这首诗的简洁掩盖了其深刻的情感深度。


近义词：brevity, terseness, succinctness, pithiness |反义词：verbosity, wordiness, prolixity
