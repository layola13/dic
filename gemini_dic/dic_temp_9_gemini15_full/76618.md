gallop:/ˈɡæləp/| v. n. |飞奔；疾驰|To run or ride at a gallop.  A fast pace of a horse involving all four legs off the ground at once.|The horse galloped across the field.  那匹马飞奔穿过田野。|The rider enjoyed the exhilarating gallop. 骑手享受着令人兴奋的疾驰。|canter, run, dash|walk, stroll|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gallops his horse every morning. = 他每天早上都骑马飞奔。
The horses gallop across the plains. = 马儿在平原上飞驰。

一般过去时:
She galloped her horse to victory. = 她策马飞奔取得了胜利。
They galloped through the forest. = 他们飞驰穿过森林。

现在完成时:
We have galloped across the desert. = 我们已经飞驰过沙漠。
The jockey has galloped many a race. = 骑师参加过许多赛马。

过去完成时:
He had galloped many miles before he stopped. = 他飞奔了许多英里才停下来。
They had galloped until they were exhausted. = 他们一直飞奔到筋疲力尽。

将来时:
I will gallop through the countryside tomorrow. = 我明天将在乡间飞驰。
The riders will gallop across the finish line. = 骑手们将飞驰过终点线。


虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would gallop across the fields. = 如果我有一匹马，我会在田野上飞驰。
I wish I could gallop like the wind. = 我希望我能像风一样飞驰。

被动语态:
The race was galloped at a furious pace. = 赛马以惊人的速度进行。
The horse was galloped to the stables. = 马被飞驰着送回了马厩。

疑问句:
Did the horse gallop fast enough? = 马跑得够快吗？
Can you gallop a horse well? = 你能熟练地驾驭飞奔的马吗？

否定句:
The horse didn't gallop very far. = 马没有跑多远。
He couldn't gallop because his horse was lame. = 他不能飞奔，因为他的马瘸了。
