propitious:/prəˈpɪʃəs/| adj.| 吉利的；顺利的；有利的 | Favorable or advantageous; auspicious.  It suggests a situation or time that is likely to lead to success or good fortune.  | 例句：The propitious weather conditions allowed the climbers to reach the summit.  有利的天气条件使登山者能够到达山顶。

例句：The propitious market conditions led to a surge in investment.  有利的市场环境导致投资激增。


近义词：auspicious, favorable, opportune, fortunate, auspicious, promising


反义词：unfavorable, inauspicious, ill-omened, unlucky


词性：形容词
