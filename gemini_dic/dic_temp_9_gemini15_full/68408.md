falsest:/ˈfɔːlsɪst/| adj.|最虚假的，最不真实的|Most untrue; most deceptive.  It is the superlative form of "false".|例句：His falsest claim was that he had never lied. 他最虚假的声明是他从未撒谎过。

例句：The falsest smiles often hide the deepest resentments. 最虚伪的笑容往往掩盖着最深的怨恨。


近义词：most untrue, most deceptive, most fabricated, most sham

反义词：truest, most genuine, most authentic

词性:形容词
