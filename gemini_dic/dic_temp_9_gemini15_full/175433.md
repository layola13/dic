ramen:/ˈrɑːmən/ | n. |拉面|A Japanese noodle soup consisting of wheat noodles in a light broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and typically topped with sliced pork, dried seaweed, and green onions.  It's a popular and widely-consumed dish. |例句：I had a delicious bowl of ramen for lunch.  我午餐吃了一碗美味的拉面。|近义词：noodle soup, broth|反义词：无
