postinfluenzal: /ˌpoʊstɪnflʊˈɛnzəl/ | adj. | 流行性感冒后的；流感后的 |  Following or relating to the period after an attack of influenza.  It describes the symptoms, conditions, or complications that occur after a person has recovered from the flu. | Example: Postinfluenzal fatigue can persist for weeks after the acute phase of the illness. 流感后的疲劳可能会在疾病的急性期后持续数周。 | 近义词: postviral, post-flu | 反义词: preinfluenzal
