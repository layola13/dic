flower:/ˈflaʊər/ | n. | 花 | A plant's reproductive organ, typically having colorful petals surrounding the reproductive parts. Flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.  | Example: The rose is a beautiful flower. 这朵玫瑰花很漂亮。 The garden is full of colorful flowers. 花园里盛开着五彩缤纷的花朵。 |近义词：blossom, bloom |反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent, e.g., weed if referring to unwanted growth)
