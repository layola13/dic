menace:/ˈmɛnəs/| n., v.| 威胁；危险；讨厌的人或事 |A person or thing that is likely to cause harm; a threat or danger. |例句：The storm clouds were a menace to the small fishing boats.暴风雨云对小渔船构成威胁。|例句：The gangs are a menace to society.这些帮派是社会的祸害。|近义词：threat, danger, peril, hazard |反义词：safety, security, protection

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog menaces the cat. = 这只狗威胁着这只猫。
That loud music menaces my peace and quiet. = 那吵闹的音乐威胁着我的平静。


一般过去时:
The bully menaced the smaller children. = 那个恶霸威胁了那些较小的孩子。
The dark figure menaced her from the shadows. = 黑影从阴影中威胁着她。


现在完成时:
The rising floodwaters have menaced the coastal towns. = 上涨的洪水威胁着沿海城镇。
His erratic behavior has menaced his career. = 他反复无常的行为已经威胁到他的事业。


过去完成时:
The approaching storm had menaced the sailors for days. = 逼近的风暴几天来一直威胁着水手们。
The criminal had menaced the witness before the trial. = 罪犯在审判前威胁过证人。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will menace coastal communities. = 海平面上升将威胁沿海社区。
The approaching hurricane will menace the island. = 逼近的飓风将威胁该岛屿。


虚拟语气:
If he menaced me again, I would call the police. = 如果他再次威胁我，我会报警。
I wish he hadn't menaced the children. = 我真希望他没有威胁孩子们。


被动语态:
The town was menaced by the approaching fire. = 城镇受到逼近大火的威胁。
She felt menaced by his aggressive behavior. = 她感到他的侵略性行为对她构成威胁。


疑问句:
Does the dog menace strangers? = 这只狗威胁陌生人吗？
What menaces the environment most? = 什么对环境的威胁最大？


否定句:
The situation doesn't menace us at present. = 目前情况并不威胁我们。
He didn't menace anyone with a weapon. = 他没有用武器威胁任何人。


PHRASE->
a menace to society  社会的祸害
a constant menace  持续的威胁
a potential menace  潜在的威胁
a public menace  公共危害
to pose a menace to 对…构成威胁
