silhouetting:/ˌsɪlhuˈɛtɪŋ/| v.|轮廓线描绘；使成轮廓状 |To represent or show (someone or something) as a dark shape against a light background.  The process of creating a silhouette.|The setting sun silhouetted the mountains against the sky. 夕阳将山峦的轮廓映衬在天空背景上。


The trees were silhouetted against the twilight sky. 树木在暮色苍茫的天空中呈现出剪影。|近义词：shadowing, outlining |反义词：highlighting, detailing

词源:
