ketone:/ˈkiːtoʊn/| n. |酮 |A ketone is an organic compound with a carbonyl group (=C=O) bonded to two carbon atoms.  Ketones are important in many biological processes and are found in various natural products and industrial chemicals. |例句：Acetone is the simplest ketone. 丙酮是最简单的酮。|例句： Many fruits contain various ketones that contribute to their aroma.许多水果含有各种酮，这些酮有助于它们的香气。|近义词：carbonyl compound |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，需要根据语境来确定)
