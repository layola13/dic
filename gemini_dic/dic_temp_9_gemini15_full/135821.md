nonpromulgation:/ˌnɒnˌprɒmʌlˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |不公布；未公布 |The act or instance of not promulgating; the state of not being promulgated.  It often refers to the withholding of information or laws from public knowledge.  |Example: The nonpromulgation of the new regulations led to widespread confusion.  不公布新的规章导致了广泛的混乱。  The nonpromulgation of the findings was a deliberate attempt to conceal the truth.  未公布调查结果是蓄意隐瞒真相的企图。|近义词：suppression, concealment, withholding |反义词：promulgation, publication, dissemination |名词
