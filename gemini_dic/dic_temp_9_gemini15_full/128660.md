nicolaitan:/ˌnɪkəˈleɪtən/| n. |尼哥拉党人；尼哥拉派；放荡的人|A member of a heretical sect mentioned in the Book of Revelation, associated with idolatry, immorality, and the teaching of false doctrines.  The exact nature of the Nicolaitans is debated by scholars.|The Book of Revelation condemns the Nicolaitans for their practices.  The passage suggests that they tolerated or even encouraged idolatry and sexual immorality. |近义词：heretic, immoralist |反义词：saint, moralist
