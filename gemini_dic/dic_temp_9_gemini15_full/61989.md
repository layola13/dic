enacts:/ɪˈnækts/| vt.|制定，颁布（法律等）；扮演；演出 |To make a law officially valid.  To perform (a role or part) in a play or film.| The government enacts new laws every year. 政府每年都颁布新法律。|The actor enacts the role of Hamlet with great skill. 这位演员技巧娴熟地扮演哈姆雷特的角色。|近义词：promulgate, establish, perform, represent |反义词：repeal, abolish


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government enacts new laws regularly. = 政府定期颁布新法律。
She enacts the role of a queen in the play. = 她在剧中扮演女王的角色。


一般过去时:
Congress enacted the Civil Rights Act of 1964. = 国会于1964年颁布了民权法案。
He enacted the scene perfectly. = 他完美地演绎了这场戏。


现在完成时:
They have enacted several important pieces of legislation this year. = 今年他们已经颁布了几项重要的立法。
She has enacted many different roles throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她扮演过许多不同的角色。


过去完成时:
The play had already been enacted before the critics arrived. = 剧评家到达之前，戏已经演完了。
By the time the bill was passed, the government had already enacted temporary measures. = 法案通过时，政府已经颁布了临时措施。


将来时:
The legislature will enact a new tax law next month. = 立法机关下个月将颁布一项新的税法。
The director believes he will perfectly enact the scene. = 导演相信他会完美地演绎这场戏。


虚拟语气:
If they enacted stricter regulations, the problem would be solved. = 如果他们制定更严格的法规，这个问题就能解决。
If the play had enacted the scene differently, the audience would have understood the plot better. = 如果剧中对这场戏的演绎有所不同，观众就会更好地理解剧情。


被动语态:
New laws are enacted by the parliament. = 新法律由议会颁布。
The scene is enacted with great emotion. = 这场戏演绎得非常感人。


疑问句:
When will the new environmental laws be enacted? = 新的环境法何时会颁布？
Who will enact the lead role in the upcoming movie? = 谁将在即将上映的电影中扮演主角？


否定句:
The government did not enact the proposed legislation. = 政府没有颁布拟议的立法。
He could not enact the character convincingly. = 他无法令人信服地扮演这个角色。


PHRASE->

enact a law = 制定法律
enact a policy = 制定政策
enact a scene = 表演一场戏
enact a role = 扮演一个角色
enact a drama = 演绎一出戏剧


词源:

From Middle English *enacten, from Old French enacter, from en- + acter (to act).  The modern meaning encompassing legislative action developed later.
