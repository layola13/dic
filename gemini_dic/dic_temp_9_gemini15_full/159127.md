poco:/ˈpoʊkoʊ/| adv.| 少量；一点；略微 |A little; somewhat; slightly.  It is often used in music to indicate a slight decrease in tempo or volume.|例句：Add a poco of salt to the soup.  给汤加一点盐。

例句：The music slowed down a poco. 音乐稍微慢了下来。


近义词：slightly, a little, somewhat, a bit

反义词：much, a lot, greatly


词源：意大利语，意思是“little”


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats poco for lunch. = 他午餐吃得少。
She drinks poco coffee. = 她喝的咖啡很少。


一般过去时:
They slept poco last night. = 他们昨晚睡得很少。
He ate poco at the party. = 他在派对上吃得很少。


现在完成时:
She has studied poco this semester. = 这个学期她学习得很少。
They have eaten poco today. = 他们今天吃得很少。


过去完成时:
He had worked poco before the deadline. = 截止日期前他工作得很少。
She had slept poco the previous week. = 她前一周睡得很少。


将来时:
I will eat poco tomorrow. = 我明天会吃得少。
They will travel poco this year. = 他们今年会旅行很少。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would work poco. = 如果我有更多时间，我会少工作。
I wish I had studied poco less. = 我希望我少学习一点。


被动语态:
Poco is consumed by the majority. = 少量被大多数人消费。
Poco is known about the incident. = 关于这起事件知之甚少。


疑问句:
Did you eat poco? = 你吃得少吗？
Did she work poco? = 她工作很少吗？


否定句:
I didn’t eat poco. = 我没吃得少。(我吃得很多)
She didn’t work poco. = 她没工作很少。(她工作很多)



PHRASE->
poco a poco: gradually, little by little  (逐渐地，一点一点地)
un poco de: a little bit of (一点…)
poco más: a little more (多一点)
poco menos: a little less (少一点)
a poco que: if only (只要)
