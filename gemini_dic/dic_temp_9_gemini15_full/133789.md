nongenetical: /ˌnɒn.dʒəˈnetɪkəl/ | adj. | 非遗传的；非基因的 | Not relating to or resulting from genes or heredity.  This term describes traits or characteristics that are not passed down from parent to offspring through genetic material.  These traits can be influenced by environmental factors, epigenetic modifications, or other non-hereditary mechanisms. |

例句：

Environmental factors can have a significant impact on nongenetic traits, such as height and weight.  环境因素会对身高和体重等非遗传性状产生重大影响。


Nongenetic diseases are often caused by external factors, unlike genetic disorders.  与遗传性疾病不同，非遗传性疾病通常是由外部因素引起的。


近义词：acquired, environmental, epigenetic (in some contexts)


反义词：genetic, hereditary, inherited


词性: adjective
