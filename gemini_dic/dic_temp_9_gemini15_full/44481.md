crossbows:/ˈkrɒsbəʊz/| n.|弩|A crossbow is a ranged weapon consisting of a bow mounted horizontally on a stock.  It is usually cocked manually and fired by releasing the string.  The arrow, called a bolt, is often shorter and heavier than an arrow used with a regular bow. |Example:  The medieval soldier aimed his crossbow at the approaching enemy.  中世纪士兵用弩瞄准了逼近的敌人。

The hunter used a powerful crossbow to bring down the deer. 猎人用一把强力的弩射倒了鹿。|近义词：bow (in a historical or figurative sense)|反义词：None directly applicable.
