furzechat:/ˈfɜːrzætʃ/| n. |欧石鵒|A small, migratory passerine bird of the family Muscicapidae, found in southwestern Europe and northwestern Africa. It is characterized by its reddish-brown upperparts, a distinctive dark mask across its eyes, and a yellow breast.  It nests in gorse bushes (furze) hence its name.|The furzechat is a delightful bird to watch as it flitts through the gorse bushes.|近义词：None precisely, but other similar-sized passerines could be considered.|反义词：None|名词
