carpet-bomb:/ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒm/| vt.|地毯式轰炸|To bomb a large area indiscriminately.  This term evokes the image of a carpet being laid down, covering a wide expanse.|例句：The enemy forces carpet-bombed the city, causing widespread destruction. 敌军对城市进行了地毯式轰炸，造成了大范围的破坏。The air force decided against carpet-bombing the residential areas. 空军决定不對住宅區進行地毯式轟炸。|近义词：blanket bomb, saturate bomb|反义词：surgical strike, precision bombing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They carpet-bomb the area. = 他们对该地区进行地毯式轰炸。
The military carpet-bombs enemy territory. = 军方对敌方领土进行地毯式轰炸。


一般过去时:
They carpet-bombed the city last night. = 他们昨晚对该城市进行了地毯式轰炸。
The planes carpet-bombed the forest. = 飞机对森林进行了地毯式轰炸。


现在完成时:
They have carpet-bombed several villages. = 他们已经对好几个村庄进行了地毯式轰炸。
The army has carpet-bombed the region repeatedly. = 军队已经多次对该地区进行了地毯式轰炸。


过去完成时:
They had carpet-bombed the area before the troops advanced. = 部队推进之前，他们已经对该地区进行了地毯式轰炸。
The enemy had carpet-bombed the town before the rescue mission began. = 在救援行动开始之前，敌人已经对城镇进行了地毯式轰炸。

虚拟语气:
