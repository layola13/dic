possessioned:/pəˈzɛʃənd/| adj.|拥有…的；被…控制的|Possessed means controlled or dominated by a supernatural being, such as a demon or spirit.  It can also refer to being strongly influenced by a particular feeling or idea.|例句：He was possessed by a dark spirit and acted erratically. 他被邪灵附身，行为异常。  She seemed possessed by a sudden rage. 她似乎被突如其来的怒火控制住了。|近义词：haunted, obsessed, controlled|反义词：unpossessed, free, liberated|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is possessed by jealousy. = 他被嫉妒吞噬。
The house is possessed by evil spirits. = 这房子被恶灵附身。


一般过去时:
She was possessed by a desire for revenge. = 她被复仇的欲望控制了。
The artist was possessed by his muse. = 艺术家被他的缪斯女神所掌控。


现在完成时:
I have been possessed by this idea for weeks. = 我几周来一直被这个想法困扰。
The town has been possessed by a strange fear. = 这个小镇笼罩着一种奇怪的恐惧。


过去完成时:
He had been possessed by grief since his wife's death. = 自妻子去世以来，他一直沉浸在悲伤之中。
They had been possessed by the notion that they were invincible. = 他们一直坚信自己是不可战胜的。


将来时:
She will be possessed by her ambition. = 她将被她的雄心壮志所驱使。
The country will be possessed by chaos. = 这个国家将陷入混乱。


虚拟语气:
If he were possessed by such a powerful force, he would be unstoppable. = 如果他被如此强大的力量控制，他将势不可挡。
I wish I were not possessed by such self-doubt. = 我希望我不那么自卑。


被动语态:
The painting seemed to be possessed by a strange energy. = 这幅画似乎充满了奇怪的能量。
The building is said to be possessed by ghosts. =据说这栋建筑闹鬼。


疑问句:
Is he possessed by demons? = 他被恶魔附身了吗？
Is the land possessed by anyone? = 这块地有人拥有吗？


否定句:
He was not possessed by greed. = 他并没有被贪婪所控制。
The house was not possessed, merely old. = 这房子不是闹鬼，只是很旧。


PHRASE->
be possessed of = 拥有（某种品质）
be possessed with = 被…控制
possessed by = 被…附身
a possessed look = 疯狂的表情
a possessed manner = 狂乱的态度
