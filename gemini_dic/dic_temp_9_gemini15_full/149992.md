palming:/ˈpɑːmɪŋ/|/ˈpɑːmɪŋ/| n. v.|手掌；用手掌做；暗中给予|The act of concealing something in the palm of one's hand, or the act of using one's palm in a specific way.  It can refer to a sleight of hand technique, a method of concealing something, or a type of massage.|例句：He was skilled in palming cards during the game. 他在游戏中擅长用手法藏牌。

The magician expertly palmed the coin. 魔术师熟练地用手掌藏起了硬币。|近义词：concealing, hiding, secreting|反义词：revealing, showing, displaying|动词
