immorally:/ɪˈmɔːrəli/ | adv. | 不道德地，邪恶地 | Acting in a way that is considered wrong or wicked according to accepted standards of morality.  | Example: He acted immorally by stealing money from his employer. 他不道德地从雇主那里偷钱。 | 近义词：wickedly, wickedly, unethically, dishonestly | 反义词：morally, ethically, virtuously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts immorally. = 他行为不道德。
They behave immorally. = 他们行为不道德。

一般过去时:
She acted immorally in that situation. = 她在那样的情况下行为不道德。
He behaved immorally towards his friends. = 他对朋友的行为不道德。

现在完成时:
They have acted immorally on numerous occasions. = 他们多次行为不道德。
He has behaved immorally throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中行为不道德。

过去完成时:
She had acted immorally before anyone discovered it. = 在任何人发现之前，她就已经行为不道德了。
They had behaved immorally for many years. = 他们不道德的行为持续了很多年。

将来时:
He will act immorally again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次行为不道德。
She will probably behave immorally under pressure. = 在压力下她可能会行为不道德。

虚拟语气:
If he acted immorally, he would face consequences. = 如果他行为不道德，他会面临后果。
I wish he hadn't acted so immorally. = 我真希望他不要行为如此不道德。

被动语态:
His actions were considered immoral. = 他的行为被认为是不道德的。
The decision was seen as immorally motivated. = 这一决定被认为是不道德的动机。

疑问句:
Did he act immorally? = 他行为不道德吗？
Was the decision made immorally? = 这一决定是不道德的吗？

否定句:
He did not act immorally. = 他没有行为不道德。
She acted morally, not immorally. = 她的行为是道德的，而不是不道德的。
