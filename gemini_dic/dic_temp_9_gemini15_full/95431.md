incinerator:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtər/ | n. |焚烧炉；焚化炉 |A furnace or apparatus for burning waste material, especially at high temperatures.|例句：The city is planning to build a new incinerator to handle its growing waste problem.  这个城市计划建造一座新的焚烧炉来处理日益增长的垃圾问题。

例句：The incinerator was emitting a foul smell.  焚烧炉散发出一股恶臭。


近义词： cremator, destructor | 反义词：None (No direct antonym exists.  The opposite concept would be recycling or composting.)
