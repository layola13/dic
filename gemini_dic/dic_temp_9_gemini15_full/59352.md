earmarked:/ˈɪərmɑːrkt/| v. adj. |指定用途的；预留的；拨款给…的|To designate something for a particular purpose; to set aside or reserve something for a specific use.  |例句：The funds have been earmarked for the new hospital. 这笔资金已拨给新建医院。  The land has been earmarked for development. 这块地已被指定用于开发。|近义词：allocated, designated, reserved, set aside|反义词：unallocated, unspecified

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government earmarks funds for education. = 政府拨款用于教育。
They earmark a portion of their profits for charity. = 他们将部分利润捐赠给慈善机构。

一般过去时:
The company earmarked a specific budget for marketing. = 公司为市场营销预留了一笔特定的预算。
He earmarked a section of his book for the biography. = 他在书中专门留出一部分写传记。

现在完成时:
The city council has earmarked several sites for new parks. = 市议会已为新的公园选址。
They have earmarked a significant sum for research and development. = 他们已为研发拨出巨款。

过去完成时:
They had earmarked the building for demolition before the discovery. = 在发现之前，他们已将该建筑物指定用于拆除。
The committee had earmarked the funds for a different project previously. = 委员会先前已将这笔资金拨给另一个项目。

将来时:
We will earmark resources for disaster relief. = 我们将拨出资源用于救灾。
The company plans to earmark a substantial amount for expansion. = 公司计划拨出大量资金用于扩张。

虚拟语气:
If we earmarked more resources, we could achieve more. = 如果我们拨出更多资源，我们就能取得更多成就。
I wish they had earmarked the funds for a more worthy cause. = 我希望他们能将资金拨给更有价值的事业。

被动语态:
The funds were earmarked for the construction of a new school. = 这笔资金被指定用于建造一所新学校。
The land has been earmarked for residential development. = 这块土地已被指定用于住宅开发。

疑问句:
Has the money been earmarked for this project yet? = 这笔钱是否已指定用于这个项目？
What resources have been earmarked for this initiative? = 为这项倡议预留了哪些资源？

否定句:
The funds were not earmarked for that purpose. = 这笔资金并非为此目的而拨款。
They haven't earmarked sufficient resources for the project. = 他们没有为该项目预留足够的资源。
