hapuku:/ˈhæpʊku/| n.|  深海石斑鱼 |A hapuku is a large, deep-water grouper fish found in the waters of New Zealand and Australia.  It is highly prized for its firm, white flesh and is considered a delicacy.|  例句：The restaurant is famous for its grilled hapuku. 这家餐厅以烤深海石斑鱼而闻名。 The hapuku population has been declining in recent years due to overfishing. 近年来，由于过度捕捞，深海石斑鱼的数量一直在下降。|近义词：groper,  (depending on species) bass|反义词：无|名词
