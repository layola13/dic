misdials:/mɪsˈdaɪəlz/ | v. |拨错号码，打错电话 |To dial the wrong number unintentionally. |例句：I misdialed and accidentally called my ex. 我拨错号码，不小心打给了我的前任。|例句：He often misdials when he's in a hurry. 他赶时间的时候经常拨错号码。|近义词：err, mistake |反义词：dial correctly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misdials frequently. = 他经常拨错号码。
She misdials almost every time she calls him. = 她每次给他打电话几乎都会拨错号码。


一般过去时:
I misdialed her number yesterday. = 我昨天拨错了她的号码。
They misdialed the emergency services number. = 他们拨错了紧急服务号码。


现在完成时:
I have misdialed several times today. = 我今天已经拨错好几次号码了。
She has misdialed that number before. = 她以前拨错过那个号码。


过去完成时:
I realized I had misdialed after the call ended. = 通话结束后我才意识到我拨错了号码。
He discovered he had misdialed the wrong extension. = 他发现他拨错了分机号码。


将来时:
I think I will misdial again if I don't write down the number. = 如果我不把号码记下来，我想我会再次拨错。
He's likely to misdial if he continues to rush. = 如果他继续匆忙，他很可能会拨错号码。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misdialed, I would have reached him. = 如果我没有拨错号码，我就能联系到他了。
If she hadn't misdialed, the emergency services would have arrived sooner. = 如果她没有拨错号码，急救服务就会更快到达。


被动语态:
The number was misdialed by mistake. = 号码被误拨了。
The wrong number was frequently misdialed. = 经常拨错号码。


疑问句:
Did you misdial the number? = 你是不是拨错号码了？
Have you ever misdialed a crucial number? = 你有没有拨错过什么重要的号码？


否定句:
I didn't misdial; the line was busy. = 我没有拨错号码；线路忙。
She didn't misdial; she just didn't get an answer. = 她没有拨错号码；只是没有得到回应。


PHRASE->
