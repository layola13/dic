词源:
The word "nonsubmersible" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the word "submersible," which comes from the verb "submerge" (to put underwater).  "Submerge" has Latin roots:  "sub-" (under) + "mergere" (to dip or plunge).  Therefore, "nonsubmersible" literally means "not capable of being submerged."
