one-off:/ˈwʌnɒf/| adj. n. |一次性的；仅此一次的；独特的|  A one-off event or item is unique and not likely to be repeated.  It can describe something that is made or done only once, or something unusual or exceptional. |例句：This was a one-off event, not a regular occurrence. 这是一次性事件，并非经常发生。

This sculpture is a one-off piece; there are no others like it. 这件雕塑是独一无二的，没有其他类似的。


近义词：unique, single, exceptional, isolated

反义词：recurring, regular, repeated, common


词性：形容词，名词
