shit-scared: /ʃɪtˈskɛərd/ | adj. | 非常害怕的，吓得要死的 | Extremely frightened; terrified. |例句：I was shit-scared when the dog started barking fiercely.  我当时吓得要死，因为狗开始凶猛地叫。

例句：She was shit-scared to death when the earthquake hit. 地震来临时，她吓得魂飞魄散。


近义词：terrified, petrified, scared stiff, deathly afraid,  panicked

反义词：unafraid, calm, composed, fearless


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is shit-scared of spiders. 他非常害怕蜘蛛。
They are shit-scared of the dark. 他们非常害怕黑暗。


一般过去时:
I was shit-scared during the storm.  暴风雨期间我吓得要死。
She was shit-scared to go alone. 她害怕独自一人去。


现在完成时:
I have been shit-scared of heights ever since I fell from a tree.自从我从树上摔下来后，我就一直非常害怕高处。
He has been shit-scared of public speaking his whole life. 他一生都很害怕公开演讲。


过去完成时:
I had been shit-scared before the interview. 面试前我一直很害怕。
She had been shit-scared of the exam all week.  她整个星期都很害怕考试。


将来时:
I will be shit-scared if I have to speak in front of a large crowd. 如果我必须在很多人面前演讲，我会非常害怕。
They will be shit-scared if they see a ghost. 如果他们看到鬼，他们会吓得要死。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a snake, I would be shit-scared. 如果我看到蛇，我会吓得要死。
I wish I wasn't shit-scared of flying. 我希望我不那么害怕飞行。


被动语态: (This adjective is rarely used passively)  此形容词很少用被动语态


疑问句:

否定句:
I wasn't shit-scared, just a little nervous. 我并不害怕，只是有点紧张。
She wasn't shit-scared; she handled the situation calmly. 她并不害怕；她冷静地处理了这种情况。
