shelducks: /ˈʃɛldʌks/ | n. | 潜鸭 |  Shelducks are a group of dabbling ducks belonging to the genus Tadorna.  They are characterized by their often brightly colored plumage and their tendency to nest in burrows or other cavities. They are found in various parts of the world, including Europe, Asia, and Australia.  | Example: The shelducks were feeding peacefully by the riverbank.  河边的潜鸭正在平静地觅食。|近义词:  Tadorna (genus name), dabbling ducks | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, consider contrasting terms like diving ducks)|  词性: 名词
