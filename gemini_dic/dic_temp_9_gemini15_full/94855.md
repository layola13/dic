in-a-fix:/ɪn ə fɪks/| adj. phrase|困境中，陷入麻烦|in a difficult, troublesome, or embarrassing situation.|例句：He's in a fix because he lost his passport. 他因为丢了护照而陷入困境。
例句：The company is in a fix after the recent scandal. 这家公司在最近的丑闻后陷入困境。|近义词：in trouble, in a predicament, in hot water|反义词：in good shape, out of trouble


USAGE->
This phrase is an adjective phrase and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is", "was", "will be", etc.  It describes a state of being.  Therefore, tenses are expressed through the verb describing the state of being in the fix.


Example Sentences demonstrating different tenses:

Present Simple: He is in a fix because he missed the deadline. 他因为错过截止日期而陷入困境。
Past Simple: She was in a fix after her car broke down. 她的车坏了之后，她陷入了困境。

Present Perfect: They have been in a fix since the market crashed. 自从市场崩盘以来，他们就一直处于困境中。
Past Perfect:  I had been in a fix before, but this situation was worse. 我以前也曾陷入困境，但这种情况更糟。

Future Simple: He will be in a fix if he doesn't find a new job soon. 如果他不尽快找到新工作，他将陷入困境。
Future Perfect: By next week, she will have been in a fix for a month. 到下周，她将陷入困境一个月了。


PHRASE->
get into a fix = 陷入困境
be in a fix = 处于困境中
work oneself out of a fix = 摆脱困境
a real fix = 真正的困境
a terrible fix = 糟糕的困境
