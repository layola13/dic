reexpressed:/ˌriːɪkˈsprest/| v. |重新表达；再次表达 |To express something again, often in a different way or with more clarity.  It implies a previous expression that was perhaps unclear, insufficient, or needed further explanation. |例句：The scientist reexpressed his findings in simpler terms for the public audience.  科学家用更简单的术语向公众重新表达了他的研究结果。

例句：He reexpressed his concerns to the committee after the initial meeting. 他在第一次会议后向委员会再次表达了他的担忧。


近义词：restate, reformulate, paraphrase, reiterate |反义词：suppress, conceal
