reshow:/ˌriːˈʃoʊ/| vt.|重播，再演，重新展示|To show or perform something again.|例句：The movie will be reshown next week.  这部电影下周将重播。|例句：The museum decided to reshow the popular exhibit. 博物馆决定重新展出这个受欢迎的展览。|近义词：replay, rebroadcast, repeat|反义词：cancel, remove
