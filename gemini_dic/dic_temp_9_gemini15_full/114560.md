malawian:/məˈlɑːwiən/| adj. n.|马拉维的；马拉维人|Of or relating to Malawi or its people.  A native or inhabitant of Malawi.|例句：She is a Malawian citizen.  她是一位马拉维公民。

例句：He spoke about his experiences living in Malawian villages. 他讲述了他居住在马拉维村庄的经历。

近义词：Malawite (less common)


反义词：None (as it describes a nationality/origin)
