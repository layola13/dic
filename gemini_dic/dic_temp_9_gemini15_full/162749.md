prebenefitting: /ˌpriːˈbɛnɪfɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先受益，提前获益 |  The act of receiving benefits before they are officially granted or earned.  This word is not standard English and is likely a newly coined word or a misspelling. It combines "pre-" (before) and "benefitting" (receiving benefits). A more appropriate term might be "preemptively benefiting" or "receiving advance benefits." |  The company is prebenefitting from the new tax laws by investing early. = 公司通过提前投资从新的税法中预先获益。 The project is designed to prebenefit the community by providing early access to resources. = 该项目旨在通过提前提供资源让社区预先受益。 |  preemptively benefiting, receiving advance benefits |  losing benefits, suffering disadvantages |  verb
