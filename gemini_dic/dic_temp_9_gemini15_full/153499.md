pensionable:/ˈpɛnʃənəbl/| adj.| 可领取养老金的；符合领取养老金条件的 |Eligible to receive a pension.  This usually refers to having worked for a sufficient period of time to qualify for a pension. |Example: He's finally pensionable after 30 years of service. 他在服务30年后终于可以领取养老金了。  She reached pensionable age last year. 她去年达到领取养老金的年龄。|近义词：retirement-eligible, pension-eligible|反义词：non-pensionable


USAGE->

He is pensionable this year. = 他今年可以领取养老金了。
She is not yet pensionable. = 她还不能领取养老金。

一般过去时:

He will be pensionable next year. = 他明年可以领取养老金了。
