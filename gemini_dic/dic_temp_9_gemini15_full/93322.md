ill-prepared:/ˌɪlprɪˈpɛərd/| adj. |准备不足的，未做好准备的 |Lacking adequate preparation; insufficiently prepared.  It implies a deficiency in planning, resources, or knowledge necessary for a task or situation.| The team was ill-prepared for the final game, resulting in a crushing defeat.  球队对决赛准备不足，导致惨败。| well-prepared, ready, equipped | well-prepared, fully prepared


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ill-prepared for the upcoming exam. = 他对即将到来的考试准备不足。
They are ill-prepared to handle the crisis. = 他们对处理这场危机准备不足。


一般过去时:
She was ill-prepared for the interview. = 她对面试准备不足。
We were ill-prepared for the sudden change in weather. = 我们对突如其来的天气变化准备不足。


现在完成时:
They have been ill-prepared for this project from the start. = 从一开始他们就对这个项目准备不足。
The company has been ill-prepared to adapt to the changing market. = 这家公司对适应不断变化的市场准备不足。


过去完成时:
He had been ill-prepared for the presentation, which led to a disastrous outcome. = 他对演示准备不足，导致了灾难性的结果。
The hikers had been ill-prepared for the difficult terrain, and became lost. = 登山者对艰难的地形准备不足，迷路了。


将来时:
They will be ill-prepared for the competition if they don't practice more. = 如果他们不加紧练习，他们将无法应对比赛。
The students will be ill-prepared for the final exam without further revision. = 如果不进一步复习，学生们将无法应对期末考试。


虚拟语气:
If he had been better prepared, he would have passed the test. = 如果他准备充分的话，他就能通过考试了。
I wish I had been better prepared for the meeting. = 我希望我对会议准备得更好一些。


被动语态:
The students were found to be ill-prepared for the assignment. = 发现学生们对作业准备不足。
The team was deemed ill-prepared for the tournament. = 该队被认为对锦标赛准备不足。


疑问句:
Were they ill-prepared for the journey? = 他们对旅程准备不足吗？
Is he ill-prepared to take on this responsibility? = 他承担这项责任准备不足吗？


否定句:
The candidate was not ill-prepared; she had studied diligently. = 候选人并非准备不足；她学习非常勤奋。
They were not ill-prepared for the emergency; they had a detailed plan. = 他们对紧急情况并非准备不足；他们有一个详细的计划。
