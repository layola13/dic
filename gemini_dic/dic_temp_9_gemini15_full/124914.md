mudguards:/ˈmʌdɡɑːrdz/| n.|挡泥板，挡泥板|Mudguards are devices fitted to vehicles to prevent mud and water from splashing onto the body of the vehicle and other vehicles or pedestrians.|例句：The bicycle's mudguards were rusty and needed replacing.  自行车的挡泥板生锈了，需要更换。|例句：The car's mudguards helped keep it clean during the muddy drive. 这辆车的挡泥板在泥泞的驾驶过程中帮助保持了车身清洁。|近义词：fender (for cars), splash guard|反义词：无
