colostomy:/koʊˈlɒstəmi/| n. |结肠造口术；结肠造口；人工肛门|A colostomy is a surgical procedure that brings one end of the large intestine (colon) out through the abdominal wall, creating an opening called a stoma.  Waste then passes out of the body through the stoma into a bag worn on the abdomen.|例句：The surgeon explained the procedure for a colostomy to the patient.  外科医生向病人解释了结肠造口术的过程。

The patient needed a colostomy after the bowel surgery. 病人在肠道手术后需要进行结肠造口术。|近义词：stoma, ileostomy (if involving the ileum)|反义词：colostomy reversal (the surgical procedure to close the stoma)|词性 n.
