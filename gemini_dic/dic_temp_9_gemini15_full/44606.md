crowd-surf:/ˈkraʊdˌsɜːrf/| v.| 冲浪式人群冲浪 | To be carried by a crowd of people at a concert or similar event.  This involves being lifted and passed over the heads of the audience. | 例句：The lead singer encouraged the audience to crowd-surf during the encore. 主唱鼓励观众在返场演出期间进行人群冲浪。

The band was so popular that several people tried to crowd-surf.  乐队如此受欢迎以至于有几个人尝试了人群冲浪。|近义词：ride the crowd, get carried by the crowd |反义词：N/A
