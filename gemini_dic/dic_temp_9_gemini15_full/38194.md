compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|强迫性的；难以抑制的；强制性的 | characterized by or resulting from an irresistible urge; compelling; mandatory |例句：He has a compulsive need to check his phone every few minutes. 他有一种每隔几分钟就要查看手机的强迫性需求。|例句：The evidence was compulsive enough to secure a conviction. 证据确凿，足以定罪。|近义词：obsessive, irresistible, uncontrollable |反义词：voluntary, optional, controllable

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is compulsive about cleaning. = 他对清洁有强迫症。
She is compulsive in her pursuit of knowledge. = 她追求知识的劲头很猛。

一般过去时:
His gambling was compulsive. = 他以前有赌博成瘾。
Her spending habits were compulsive in her youth. = 她年轻时花钱大手大脚。

现在完成时:
He has been compulsive about exercise for years. = 他多年来一直对运动有强迫性。
She has become compulsive about checking her email. = 她现在变得很强迫地检查她的邮件。

过去完成时:
He had been compulsive about work before his breakdown. = 在他崩溃之前，他一直沉迷于工作。
She had been compulsive about dieting, leading to health problems. = 她一直强迫节食，导致健康问题。

将来时:
He will likely remain compulsive about his routines. = 他很可能会继续坚持他的日常工作。
She will probably become compulsive about social media if she's not careful. = 如果她不小心，她可能会沉迷于社交媒体。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't compulsive, he would be much happier. = 如果他不那么强迫，他会快乐得多。
If she hadn't been compulsive, she would have avoided those problems. = 如果她不那么强迫，她就能避免那些问题。

被动语态:
His behavior was described as compulsive. = 他的行为被描述为强迫性的。
Her actions were considered compulsive by her therapist. = 她的行为被她的治疗师认为是强迫性的。

疑问句:
Is his behavior compulsive? = 他的行为是强迫性的吗？
Are her habits compulsive? = 她的习惯是强迫性的吗？

否定句:
His actions were not compulsive; he simply enjoyed them. = 他的行为并非强迫性的；他只是单纯地享受它们。
Her spending was not compulsive; she was simply generous. = 她花钱并非强迫性的；她只是慷慨大方。
