一般现在时:
The road serpentines through the valley. 这条路蜿蜒穿过山谷。
The river serpentines towards the ocean. 这条河流蜿蜒流向海洋。

一般过去时:
The path serpentined through the forest.  小路蜿蜒穿过森林。
The trail serpentined up the mountain. 小路蜿蜒向上攀登山峰。

现在完成时:
The road has serpentined for miles. 这条路已经蜿蜒了几英里。
The river has serpentined its way to the lake. 这条河已经蜿蜒流到了湖泊。

过去完成时:
The road had serpentined before it reached the village. 这条路在到达村庄之前就已经蜿蜒了。
The river had serpentined through the canyons before emptying into the sea. 这条河在流入大海之前已经蜿蜒穿过峡谷了。

虚拟语气:
If the road serpentined less, the journey would be shorter. 如果这条路不那么蜿蜒，旅程就会短一些。
