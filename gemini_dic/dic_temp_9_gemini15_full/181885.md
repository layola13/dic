rely-on:/rɪˈlaɪ ɒn/ | v. |依赖，依靠 | to depend on someone or something | I rely on my friend for support.  我依靠我的朋友的支持。|  depend on, count on |  ignore, neglect | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rely on my alarm clock to wake me up. = 我依靠闹钟叫醒我。
We rely on the internet for information. = 我们依靠互联网获取信息。

一般过去时:
She relied on her parents for financial assistance. = 她依靠父母的经济资助。
They relied on hearsay, not facts. = 他们依赖道听途说，而不是事实。

现在完成时:
I have relied on public transport for years. = 我多年来一直依赖公共交通工具。
We have relied on him for many years. = 我们多年来一直依赖他。

过去完成时:
He had relied on his intuition before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前依赖了自己的直觉。
They had relied on a faulty system, leading to the error. = 他们依赖一个有缺陷的系统，导致了错误。

将来时:
I will rely on your help to finish this project. = 我将依靠你的帮助完成这个项目。
She will rely on her experience to guide her. = 她将依靠她的经验来指导她。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would rely on my own judgment. = 如果我是你，我会依靠自己的判断。
I wish I could rely on him more. = 我希望我能更依赖他。

被动语态:
His success cannot be relied upon. = 他的成功不可靠。
The project is heavily relied upon by the company. = 这个项目被公司高度依赖。

疑问句:
Can you rely on the information? = 你能相信这些信息吗？
Who can we rely on in this situation? = 在这种情况下，我们可以依靠谁？

否定句:
Don't rely on luck; prepare well. = 不要依赖运气；做好准备。
You can't rely on him to be on time. = 你不能指望他准时。


PHRASE->
rely on oneself = 依靠自己
rely on others = 依靠他人
rely on experience = 依靠经验
rely on intuition = 依靠直觉
rely on evidence = 依靠证据
