grumped:/ɡrʌmpt/| v.|变得脾气暴躁；抱怨；发牢骚|To become grumpy or bad-tempered; to complain or grumble. |例句：He grumped about the weather all day. 他整天抱怨天气。|例句：She grumped at me for being late. 她因为我迟到而对我发牢骚。|近义词：complained, grumbled, groused, sulked|反义词：smiled, cheered, rejoiced|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grumps about the traffic every morning. = 他每天早上都抱怨交通。
She often grumps when she's tired. = 她累了的时候经常发牢骚。

一般过去时:
He grumped about the long wait. = 他抱怨等了很久。
She grumped at the waiter for the slow service. = 她因为服务慢而对服务员发牢骚。

现在完成时:
He has grumped all afternoon. = 他整个下午都在抱怨。
She has grumped about her job recently. = 她最近一直在抱怨她的工作。

过去完成时:
He had grumped about the food before he even tasted it. = 他甚至还没尝就抱怨食物了。
She had grumped all day, so no one wanted to talk to her. = 她整天都在抱怨，所以没有人想和她说话。

将来时:
He will grump if the game is cancelled. = 如果比赛取消，他会抱怨的。
