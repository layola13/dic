notedly:/ˈnoʊtɪdli/ | adv. | 尤其，显著地，值得注意地 | Notably, the study found a significant correlation between the two variables.  尤其值得注意的是，这项研究发现这两个变量之间存在显著的相关性。| 近义词：significantly, remarkably, especially, particularly | 反义词：insignificantly, unnoticeably
