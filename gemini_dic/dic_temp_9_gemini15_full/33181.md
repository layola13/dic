chromogenic:/ˌkroʊməˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|显色的；生色团的；产色反应的|Producing or capable of producing color.  Chromogenic reactions are often used in diagnostic tests to visualize the presence of a specific substance.|The chromogenic substrate turned blue in the presence of the enzyme.|近义词：color-producing,  pigment-forming|反义词：achromic, colorless
