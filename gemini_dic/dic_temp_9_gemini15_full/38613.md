concreting:/kənˈkriːtɪŋ/| v.|混凝土浇筑；铺设混凝土|The act of placing and consolidating concrete.  It involves mixing, transporting, placing, and finishing concrete to create a solid structure.|例句：The concreting of the new bridge is expected to be completed by next month.  新的桥梁的混凝土浇筑预计下个月完成。

例句：They are concreting the driveway today. 他们今天正在浇筑车道混凝土。


近义词：cementing, paving


反义词：demolishing, removing


词性：动词
