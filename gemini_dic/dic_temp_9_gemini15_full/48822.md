defocuses:/ˌdiːˈfoʊkəsɪz/| v.|使散焦；使模糊；使失去焦点|To make something less sharp or clear, especially an image or a person's mind.|The blurry photo shows the photographer defocused the image intentionally. 这张模糊的照片显示摄影师故意让图像散焦。  The long working hours defocused him from his family life. 长时间的加班使他疏忽了家庭生活。|blur, unfocus, soften|focus, sharpen|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The photographer defocuses the background to highlight the subject. = 摄影师使背景散焦以突出主体。
He defocuses easily when under pressure. = 他在压力下很容易分心。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
