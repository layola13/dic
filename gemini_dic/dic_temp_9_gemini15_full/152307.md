/ˈpætərsən/| n. |帕特森（姓氏）|Patterson is a surname of Scottish origin.  It is a habitational name from any of several places called Patterson, which are named after a personal name, Patersoun.  The personal name is derived from the elements "Peter" and "son".|The Patterson family has a long and distinguished history.  |近义词：Paterson (variant spelling)|反义词：无
