honeymooning:/ˈhʌnimuːnɪŋ/ | v. | 蜜月旅行；享受蜜月期 |  The act of taking a honeymoon; the period of time newlyweds spend together after their wedding. It can also refer to a period of intense happiness or success, similar to the feeling of a honeymoon. | Example: They are honeymooning in Hawaii. 他们在夏威夷度蜜月。  They are honeymooning in their new jobs. 他们在新工作中享受着蜜月期。 | 近义词：vacationing, holidaying, celebrating | 反义词：None directly applicable, but could be considered opposite to a period of difficulty or strife.
