clothespin:/ˈkloʊðˌspɪn/ | n. | 衣夹 | A clothespin is a device used to fasten clothes to a clothesline while they are drying.  It typically consists of two pieces of wood or plastic joined by a spring. | Example: I used a clothespin to hang the washed socks on the line. 我用衣夹把洗好的袜子挂在晾衣绳上。|近义词：clothes peg |反义词：None
