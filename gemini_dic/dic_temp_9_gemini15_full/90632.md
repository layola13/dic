humanitarian:/ˌhjuːmænɪˈteəriən/ | adj., n. |人道主义的；人道主义者 | Relating to or concerned with humanity; charitable; benevolent.  A person who works actively to promote human welfare. |

例句：
The organization provides humanitarian aid to victims of natural disasters.  该组织向自然灾害的受害者提供人道主义援助。

Many humanitarian workers are risking their lives to help those in need.  许多人道主义工作者正在冒着生命危险帮助需要帮助的人。


近义词：benevolent, charitable, philanthropic, compassionate

反义词：inhuman, cruel, heartless, uncaring


词性：形容词，名词
