词源:

The word "footloose" is a compound word.  "Foot" is the Old English word *fōt, meaning "foot," and "loose" comes from the Old English word *lōs, meaning "free" or "unbound."  Therefore, the word literally means "free of foot," implying freedom of movement and lack of restrictions.
