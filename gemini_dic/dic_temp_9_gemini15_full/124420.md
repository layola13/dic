motorcaded:/ˈmoʊtərkeɪdɪd/ | adj. | 汽车游行的；乘汽车车队行驶的 |  Having traveled or being conveyed in a motorcade.  This word is relatively rare and primarily used in descriptive contexts. | 例句：The newly elected president arrived at the capitol in a motorcaded procession.  新当选的总统乘坐汽车车队抵达国会大厦。

The visiting dignitaries were motorcaded through the city streets.  来访的贵宾们乘坐汽车车队穿过城市街道。


近义词：convoyed, processioned (less common) | 反义词： walking, cycling (implying alternative modes of travel)


词性：形容词
