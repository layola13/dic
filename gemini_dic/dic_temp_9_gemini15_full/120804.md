misasserting:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrtɪŋ/| v.|错误地声明；断言错误|To state something incorrectly or falsely; to make a false claim or assertion.  |例句：He was misasserting the company's financial position. 他错误地声明了公司的财务状况。|例句：Misasserting facts can lead to serious consequences.  错误地陈述事实会导致严重后果。|近义词：misrepresenting, misstating, falsifying|反义词：asserting correctly, verifying, confirming|动词
