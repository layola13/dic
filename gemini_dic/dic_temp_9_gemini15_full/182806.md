repayments:/riˈpeɪmənts/| n. |还款，偿还|The act of paying back money that has been borrowed or owed.  It often refers to a series of payments made over time, such as those made on a loan or mortgage.|例句：The repayments on my student loan are quite high. 我的学生贷款还款额相当高。

例句：She is struggling to keep up with her mortgage repayments. 她正在努力维持她的抵押贷款还款。


近义词：reimbursements, payments, settlements|反义词：non-payments, defaults


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank processes repayments weekly. = 银行每周处理还款。
He makes regular repayments on his car loan. = 他定期偿还他的汽车贷款。

一般过去时:
She made all her repayments on time. = 她按时偿还了所有款项。
They completed the repayments last year. = 他们去年完成了还款。

现在完成时:
We have completed all our repayments. = 我们已经完成了所有的还款。
She has made several repayments this month. = 她这个月已经还了几次款了。

将来时:
