reutilizing:/ˌriːˈjuːtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 再利用；重复利用 |  The act of using something again after it has been used once. This involves finding a new purpose for an item or resource that would otherwise be discarded.  | Example:  The company is focusing on reutilizing waste materials to reduce its environmental impact. = 这家公司正专注于重复利用废料以减少其对环境的影响。

The city is reutilizing old shipping containers as affordable housing units. = 这个城市正在将旧集装箱重新利用作经济适用房单元。


近义词：recycling, repurposing, reusing | 反义词：discarding, wasting, destroying | 词性：动词
