nomadism:/ˈnoʊmədɪzəm/| n. |游牧生活；游牧制；流浪；漂泊 |The practice of moving from place to place to find food and resources, typically with livestock. It is characterized by a lack of permanent settlements and a reliance on mobility for survival.  | Example: Nomadism was a way of life for many tribes before the advent of agriculture.  游牧生活是农业出现之前许多部落的生活方式。|近义词：migrancy, itinerancy |反义词：sedentarism, settlement
