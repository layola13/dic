enchanter:/ɪnˈtʃɑːntər/| n. |魔法师，施魔法者|A person who enchants or casts spells; a magician.|例句：The enchanter waved his wand, and the dragon vanished.  魔法师挥舞着魔杖，龙消失了。
例句：The old enchanter lived in a secluded tower, brewing powerful potions.  那位老魔法师住在偏僻的塔楼里，酿造着强大的药剂。|近义词：magician, sorcerer, wizard|反义词：de-enchanter (非正式用法，表示解除魔法的人或事物)|
