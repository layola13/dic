laxativeness:/læksˈeɪtɪvnəs/ | n. |松弛；懈怠；放纵；通便性 |The state or quality of being lax; looseness; negligence; a tendency to be lax.  It often refers to a relaxed or loose state of the bowels, resulting in frequent bowel movements.  |例句：His laxativeness in managing the project led to missed deadlines. 他的项目管理松懈导致错过最后期限。

例句：The laxativeness of the security measures allowed intruders to gain access. 安全措施的松懈使得入侵者得以进入。


近义词：slackness, negligence, looseness, remissness, leniency |反义词：strictness, rigor, firmness, tautness, stringency


词源：源自于拉丁语 *laxus,意思是“松弛的，宽松的”。  The suffix "-ness" is added to form a noun, indicating a state or quality.
