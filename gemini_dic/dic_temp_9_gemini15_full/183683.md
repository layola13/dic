rerolling:/ˌriːˈroʊlɪŋ/| v.|重新滚动；再掷骰子|The act of rolling something again, typically dice or a similar object in a game.  It implies a previous attempt that was unsatisfactory.|例句：He wasn't happy with his first character, so he spent hours rerolling. 他对自己的第一个角色不满意，所以花了几个小时重新掷骰子。

例句：After failing the initial roll, she decided to reroll.  第一次掷骰失败后，她决定重新掷骰。


近义词：re-rolling, trying again, restarting


反义词：accepting, keeping


词性:动词
