damnum:/ˈdæmnəm/| n.|损害，损失，危害|Damage, loss, harm;  in Roman law, a general term for any loss or injury.  It encompasses both material and non-material harm. |例句：The storm caused considerable damnum to the crops.  暴风雨对庄稼造成了相当大的损失。  The damnum resulting from the accident was significant. 事故造成的损害是巨大的。|近义词：injury, harm, damage, loss|反义词：benefit, gain, profit|名词
