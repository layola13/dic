chills:/tʃɪlz/| n. v.|寒意，寒战；使…感到寒冷|A sudden feeling of cold, often accompanied by shivering; to cause someone to feel cold.|例句：The chills ran down my spine.  一阵寒意从我的脊椎滑落。|例句：The cold wind chilled me to the bone.  寒冷的风使我感到彻骨的寒冷。|近义词：shivers, shakes, tremors|反义词：warmth, heat


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cold wind chills me. = 冷风使我感到寒冷。
He chills the wine before serving. = 他上菜前先冰镇葡萄酒。

一般过去时:
I chilled to the bone last night. = 我昨晚冷得彻骨。
She chilled the soup before putting it in the refrigerator. = 她把汤冰镇后再放进冰箱。

现在完成时:
The cold weather has chilled me to the bone. = 寒冷的天气让我冷得彻骨。
The rain has chilled the ground. = 雨水使地面变凉了。

过去完成时:
I had chilled the champagne before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我已经把香槟冰镇好了。
The food had chilled significantly by the time we ate it. = 等我们吃的时候，食物已经凉得相当厉害了。

将来时:
I will chill the drinks before the party. = 聚会之前我会把饮料冰镇好。
The weather forecast says it will chill down tonight. = 天气预报说今晚会变冷。

虚拟语气:
If I had worn a warmer coat, I wouldn't have chilled so much. = 如果我穿了件更暖和的外套，我就不会这么冷了。
I wish I hadn't chilled the beer so much; it's too cold now. = 我真希望我没有把啤酒冰得那么凉，现在太冷了。

被动语态:
The soup was chilled before serving. = 汤在上菜前被冰镇了。
My bones were chilled by the icy wind. = 我被冰冷的风吹得寒气直透骨髓。


疑问句:

Were the vegetables chilled properly? = 蔬菜冰镇得够好吗？


否定句:
The room wasn't chilled enough. = 房间不够凉爽。
I didn't chill the wine, so it's warm. = 我没有冰镇葡萄酒，所以它很暖和。
