nectarine:/ˈnɛktəriːn/| n. |油桃|A nectarine is a fruit similar to a peach, but with smooth skin.  It is typically smaller and firmer than a peach and has a sweeter, more intense flavor. Nectarines are a good source of vitamins and fiber. |Example: I bought a basket of juicy nectarines from the farmer's market. 我从农贸市场买了一篮子多汁的油桃。|近义词：peach, plum|反义词：无
