leadership: /ˈliːdəʃɪp/ | n. |领导力，领导才能，领导地位 | The ability to lead and guide others; the process of leading a group or organization.  It involves setting a vision, motivating people, and achieving common goals. |例句：Her leadership skills are exceptional.  她的领导能力非常出色。

例句：The company needs strong leadership to navigate these challenging times. 这家公司需要强大的领导力来应对这些充满挑战的时期。


近义词：guidance, direction, command, management, superintendence

反义词：followership, subordination

词源:
The word "leadership" is derived from the Old English word "lǣdan," meaning "to lead," plus the suffix "-ship," indicating "the state or quality of being."  The evolution reflects the development of the concept of leadership from a simple act of guiding to a complex set of skills and qualities.
