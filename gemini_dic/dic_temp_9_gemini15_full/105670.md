kingmaking:/ˈkɪŋˌmeɪkɪŋ/| n.|造王者；幕后操纵者；(对政治人物的)推举；|The act of influencing the selection of a leader, especially a king or other powerful figure, often behind the scenes.  It often involves manipulating events or people to achieve a desired outcome. |例句：The behind-the-scenes kingmaking ensured that the party's chosen candidate won the election. 幕后操纵确保了该党选择的候选人赢得了选举。

例句：His years of subtle kingmaking finally paid off when his protégé ascended to the throne. 他多年的幕后操纵终于得到了回报，他的门生登上了王位。


近义词：power broker, puppet master, political maneuvering, influence peddling, wire-pulling

反义词：passivity, inaction, impartiality


词性:名词
