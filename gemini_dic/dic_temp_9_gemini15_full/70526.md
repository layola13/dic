filialness: /ˈfɪliəlˌnɛs/ | n. |孝顺；孝敬；子女应尽的义务 | The quality or state of being filial; dutiful respect and care for one's parents.  Filialness is a virtue highly valued in many cultures.  |  例句：Her filialness was evident in her constant care for her aging parents.  她的孝顺体现在她对年迈父母的悉心照料中。


例句：The family prized filialness above all other virtues.  这个家庭把孝顺看得高于一切其他美德。


近义词：piety, devotion, obedience, respect

反义词：disrespect, disobedience, neglect


词性：名词
