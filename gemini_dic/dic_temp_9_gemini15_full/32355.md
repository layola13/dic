childlikeness: /ˈtʃaɪldlaɪknəs/ | n. |孩子气；天真；幼稚 | The quality or state of being childlike; the characteristics or behavior typical of a child.  This can refer to positive traits such as innocence and wonder, or negative traits such as immaturity and naiveté.  The context determines the connotation. |例句：Her childlikeness was charming, a refreshing contrast to the cynicism of the adults around her.  她的孩子气很迷人，与周围成年人的愤世嫉俗形成了令人耳目一新的对比。


例句：The painting captures a sense of childlikeness and wonder. 这幅画捕捉到了一种孩子般的天真和惊奇。


近义词：innocence, naiveté, simplicity, childishness


反义词：maturity, sophistication, worldliness


词源：child (孩子) + -like (像…一样) + -ness (名词后缀)  The word is a combination of "childlike" and the suffix "-ness" to form a noun describing the state or quality of being childlike.
