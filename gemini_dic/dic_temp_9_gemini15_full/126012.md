musquash: /ˈmʌskwɑːʃ/ | n. |麝鼠|A large, semi-aquatic rodent with dark brown fur, a broad, flat tail, and webbed hind feet.  It is found in North America and is known for its musky odor. |例句：The trappers set their snares hoping to catch musquash for their fur. 捕兽者设置陷阱，希望能捕捉麝鼠获取它们的毛皮。

The musquash built its lodge near the riverbank.  麝鼠在河岸边建造了它的巢穴。


近义词：water rat (though not strictly accurate, as it's a different species)| 反义词：None directly applicable.
