cucumber:/ˈkjuːkʌmbə(r)/| n. |黄瓜|A long, thin, green fruit with smooth skin and watery flesh, eaten raw in salads or pickled.  It is botanically a fruit, but culinarily considered a vegetable.|例句：I added sliced cucumber to my sandwich. 我在我的三明治里加了黄瓜片。|例句：She loves pickled cucumbers. 她喜欢腌黄瓜。|近义词：gherkin (small pickled cucumber)|反义词：None
