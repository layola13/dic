samplings:/ˈsæmplɪŋz/| n.|样本，抽样，采样|The act of taking samples; a representative selection.|例句：The researchers conducted samplings of soil to analyze its composition. 研究人员对土壤进行了采样以分析其成分。|例句：The music album includes samplings from various artists. 这张音乐专辑包含了来自不同艺术家的采样片段。|近义词：specimens, extracts, selections|反义词：whole, totality
