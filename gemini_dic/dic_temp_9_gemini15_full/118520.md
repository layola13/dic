mesocolon:/ˌmɛsoʊˈkoʊlən/| n. |结肠中段|The mesocolon is the part of the peritoneum that attaches the large intestine to the posterior abdominal wall.  It is a double layer of peritoneum that contains blood vessels, nerves, and lymphatic vessels that supply the large intestine.|The transverse mesocolon supports the transverse colon.  |近义词：mesentery (more general term)|反义词：无
