quark:/kwɔːrk/|ˈkwɑːrk/| n. |夸克|A type of elementary particle and a fundamental constituent of matter. Quarks are never found alone but always in combinations, forming hadrons such as protons and neutrons.  |Example:  Protons and neutrons are made up of three quarks each.|近义词: elementary particle, fundamental particle|反义词:None
