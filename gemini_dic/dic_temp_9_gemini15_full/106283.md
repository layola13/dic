knuckled:/ˈnʌkəld/ | adj. v. |  指关节的；用指关节敲打；屈服 |  Having or relating to knuckles; to hit with the knuckles; to yield or submit. |例句：He knuckled down to his studies. 他认真学习。  He knuckled under to the pressure. 他屈服于压力。|近义词：bent, yielded, submitted | 反义词：resisted, defied


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He knuckles down to his work every day. 他每天都认真工作。
She knuckles under to her boss's demands. 她屈服于老板的要求。

一般过去时:
He knuckled his fist against the table. 他用拳头敲桌子。
They knuckled under to the threat. 他们屈服于威胁。

现在完成时:
He has knuckled down and finished the project. 他认真完成了项目。
She has knuckled under to the pressure of the deadline. 她屈服于截止日期的压力。

过去完成时:
He had knuckled under to the pressure before the deadline. 他在截止日期前就屈服于压力了。
She had knuckled down to her studies for months before the exam. 考试前几个月她一直在认真学习。

将来时:
He will knuckle down to the task tomorrow. 他明天将认真完成这项任务。
She will knuckle under if they continue to pressure her. 如果他们继续施压，她将会屈服。

虚拟语气:
If he knuckled down, he would pass the exam. 如果他认真学习，他就能通过考试。
If she hadn't knuckled under, the situation would have been different. 如果她没有屈服，情况就会不同。

被动语态:
The project was knuckled down to by the team.  团队认真完成了项目。（被动语态略显不自然，此句为解释用法）
The decision was knuckled under to reluctantly.  这个决定是不情愿地接受的。（被动语态略显不自然，此句为解释用法）


疑问句:
Will he knuckle down and finish the report? 他会认真完成报告吗？
Did she knuckle under to their demands? 她屈服于他们的要求了吗？

否定句:
He didn't knuckle down to his studies. 他没有认真学习。
She didn't knuckle under to their threats. 她没有屈服于他们的威胁。
