caraway:/ˈkærəweɪ/| n. |葛缕子，香旱芹籽|A small, aromatic plant with grayish-green leaves and tiny, oblong fruits (seeds) that have a pungent, slightly sweet flavor.  The seeds are used as a spice in baking and cooking.|例句：The bread contained caraway seeds for a distinctive flavor. 这面包里加了葛缕子籽，味道很独特。|例句：She sprinkled caraway seeds over the rye bread. 她往黑麦面包上撒了葛缕子籽。|近义词：cumin, anise|反义词:|N
