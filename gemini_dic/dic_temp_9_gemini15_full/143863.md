outplot:/ˈaʊtplɒt/| v.|智胜，巧妙地计划超过|To outplot someone means to surpass them in planning or strategy, typically in a clever or cunning way. It suggests a more intricate and intellectual outmaneuvering than simply outsmarting.|例句：The detective outplotted the cunning criminal, anticipating his every move and ultimately bringing him to justice.  侦探巧妙地智胜了狡猾的罪犯，预料到了他的每一个举动，最终将他绳之以法。|近义词：outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver |反义词：be outplotted, be outsmarted
