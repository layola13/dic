recedes:/rɪˈsiːdz/ | v. | 逐渐消失；后退；减弱 | To move back or away from a previous position; to become less intense or noticeable.  | The tide recedes. 潮水退去。| The pain gradually receded. 疼痛逐渐减轻。|近义词：withdraw, retreat, fade, diminish | 反义词：advance, approach, increase, intensify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water recedes slowly. = 水慢慢退去。
His anger recedes with time. = 他的怒气随着时间消退。

一般过去时:
The flood waters receded after several days. = 洪水几天后退去了。
The symptoms receded after taking the medication. = 服药后症状消退了。

现在完成时:
The pain has receded significantly. = 疼痛已经明显减轻。
The threat of war has receded. = 战争的威胁已经消退。

过去完成时:
The storm had receded before we arrived. = 我们到达之前风暴已经过去了。
His fear had receded once he saw his family. = 一旦看到家人，他的恐惧就消失了。

将来时:
The tide will recede in the evening. = 晚潮将退去。
Hopefully, the inflammation will recede soon. = 希望炎症很快就会消退。

虚拟语气:
If the flood waters receded, we could go home. = 如果洪水退去，我们就能回家了。
I wish my worries would recede. = 我希望我的担忧能够消失。

被动语态:
The problem was receded by the new policy. = 新政策解决了这个问题。(此处被动语态略显生硬，通常不用被动)
The symptoms are being receded by the treatment. = 治疗正在使症状消退。(此处被动语态略显生硬，通常不用被动)


疑问句:
Is the pain receding? = 疼痛正在减轻吗？
Does the shoreline recede during low tide? = 退潮时海岸线会后退吗？

否定句:
The tide is not receding. = 潮水没有退去。
The swelling hasn't receded yet. =肿胀还没有消退。


PHRASE->
recede into the background = 退居幕后
recede from memory = 从记忆中消失
recede into the distance = 渐渐远去
recede into obscurity = 渐渐被人遗忘
recede from view = 从视野中消失
