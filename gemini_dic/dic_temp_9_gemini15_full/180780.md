regulize:/ˈrɛɡjʊleɪz/ | vt. | 使规范化；使规则化；调整；管理 | To make something conform to rules or a standard.  To bring something under control or regulation. | 例句：The government is trying to regularize the informal economy. 政府正试图使非正规经济规范化。 The company needs to regularize its accounting practices. 这家公司需要规范其会计制度。 | 近义词：standardize, normalize, regulate, control | 反义词：disorder, disorganize
