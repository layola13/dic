envisioning:/ɪnˈvɪʒənɪŋ/| v. |展望；设想；想象|The act of forming a mental image of something, typically something that is not yet real or has not yet happened.  It involves imagining future possibilities, scenarios, or outcomes.|例句：The architect spent hours envisioning the new building. 建筑师花了数小时设想新的建筑物。

例句：She was envisioning a bright future for her children. 她正在设想她孩子们的光明未来。


近义词：imagining, visualizing, conceiving, contemplating, forecasting


反义词：ignoring, disregarding, overlooking


词性：动词
