no-balls:/ˈnoʊbɔːlz/| n. |坏球，无效球|A no-ball is a delivery in cricket that does not meet the legal requirements for a fair delivery, giving the batting team an advantage.|Example: The bowler bowled a no-ball, giving the batsman an extra run. 投球手投了一个坏球，给了击球手额外一分。|近义词：illegal delivery, foul ball|反义词：fair ball, legal delivery
