quadrivalence:/ˈkwɑːdrɪvələns/| n. |四价；四价态；四价化合物|The state or quality of having a valence of four; the property of an atom or element of having four valency electrons available for bonding.  Quadrivalence is a crucial concept in chemistry, particularly in understanding the bonding behaviour of certain elements like carbon.  |例句：Carbon exhibits quadrivalence, forming four covalent bonds. 碳表现出四价，形成四个共价键。  The quadrivalence of silicon is responsible for its ability to form complex structures. 硅的四价使其能够形成复杂的结构。|近义词：tetravalence|反义词：univalence, bivalence, trivalence
