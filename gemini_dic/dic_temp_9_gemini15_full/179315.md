redoing:/ˌriːˈduːɪŋ/| v. |重新做，再做|The act of doing something again; repeating a task or process.  It is the present participle of the verb "redo."|例句：He's redoing the kitchen. 他正在重新装修厨房。

例句：She's redoing her college application.  她在重新填写她的大学申请表。


近义词：remaking, revising, repeating, reworking|反义词：finishing, completing|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are redoing the bathroom. = 他们在重新装修浴室。
We are redoing the presentation. = 我们正在修改演示文稿。


一般过去时:
He redid the assignment. = 他重做了作业。
She redid the entire project. = 她重做了整个项目。


现在完成时:
I have redone the calculations. = 我已经重新计算过了。
They have redone the experiment. = 他们已经重复了实验。


过去完成时:
He had redone the painting before the exhibition. = 展览前他已经重画过这幅画了。
She had redone her research before submitting the paper. = 在提交论文之前，她已经重新做了研究。


将来时:
We will redo the test next week. = 我们下周将重新考试。
They will redo the website design. = 他们将重新设计网站。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would redo the exam. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重考。
It would be better if we redid the whole thing. = 如果我们重做整个事情会更好。


被动语态:
The report is being redone. = 报告正在被重新制作。
The software will be redone next year. = 软件明年将被重新制作。


疑问句:
Are you redoing the garden? = 你要重新整理花园吗？
Will they be redoing the contract? = 他们会重新签订合同吗？


否定句:
I am not redoing that work. = 我不会重做那项工作。
They did not redo the experiment. = 他们没有重复实验。
