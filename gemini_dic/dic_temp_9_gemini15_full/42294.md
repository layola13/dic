counter-intelligence:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/| n. |反情报；反间谍 |The activities undertaken to protect an organization or nation from espionage, sabotage, and other threats.  Counter-intelligence involves detecting, preventing, and neutralizing hostile intelligence activities. |例句：The agency's counter-intelligence division uncovered a plot to assassinate the president.  该机构的反情报部门发现了一项暗杀总统的阴谋。


例句：Effective counter-intelligence measures are crucial for national security.  有效的反情报措施对国家安全至关重要。|近义词：espionage countermeasures, intelligence security,  |反义词：espionage, sabotage, subversion|名词
