floods:/flʌdz/|/flʌd z/| n. v.|洪水；泛滥；涌入|To cover or overflow with water; an overwhelming quantity or amount.|The heavy rain caused floods in many areas.  大雨导致许多地区发生洪水。

The river flooded its banks after the storm.  暴风雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。


近义词：deluge, inundation, overflow|反义词：drought


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river floods every spring. = 河水每年春天都会泛滥。
Flooding is a common problem in this region. = 洪涝是这个地区常见的问题。


一般过去时:
The city flooded after the dam broke. = 大坝决堤后，城市被洪水淹没了。
The streets were flooded during the heavy rain. = 大雨期间，街道被淹了。


现在完成时:
The area has flooded several times this year. = 今年这个地区已经多次被洪水淹没。
The basement has flooded because of a leak in the pipe. = 由于管道泄漏，地下室被水淹了。


过去完成时:
The house had flooded before the emergency services arrived. = 紧急服务人员到达之前，房子就已经被淹了。
The river had flooded its banks several times that year. = 那一年河水已经多次漫过河岸。


将来时:
The meteorologist predicts that the area will flood if the rain continues. = 气象学家预测如果雨继续下，该地区将被淹没。
The low-lying areas will likely flood if the storm hits. = 如果风暴来袭，低洼地区很可能会被淹没。


虚拟语气:
If the dam had not broken, the city would not have flooded. = 如果大坝没有决堤，城市就不会被淹。
If it rained heavily, the fields would flood. = 如果下大雨，田地就会被淹。


被动语态:
The streets were flooded by the torrential rain. = 街道被暴雨淹没了。
The houses were flooded, causing extensive damage. = 房屋被淹，造成了巨大的损失。


疑问句:
Did the floods cause significant damage? = 洪水造成了很大的损失吗？
Has the river flooded yet? = 河水泛滥了吗？


否定句:
The river didn't flood this year. = 今年河水没有泛滥。
The basement hasn't flooded since the repairs. = 自从维修后，地下室就没有再被淹过。
