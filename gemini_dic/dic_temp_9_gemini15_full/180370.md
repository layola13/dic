refurnishing: /ˌriːˈfɜːrnɪʃɪŋ/ | n. |重新装修；重新布置 | The act of furnishing again; renewing or replacing furniture and fittings.  |例句：The refurnishing of the hotel cost a fortune.  酒店的重新装修花费了一大笔钱。

例句：The refurnishing of the apartment gave it a modern feel.  公寓的重新装修使它有了现代感。


近义词：redecoration, renovation, refurbishment | 反义词：None (antonyms would describe the opposite of the action, like "leaving as is") | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel is refurnishing its rooms. = 酒店正在重新装修房间。
She refurnishes her apartment every few years. = 她每隔几年就会重新装修她的公寓。


一般过去时:
They refurnished the house last year. = 他们去年重新装修了房子。
We refurnished the living room with new furniture. = 我们用新的家具重新布置了客厅。


现在完成时:
They have refurnished their kitchen. = 他们已经重新装修了他们的厨房。
She has refurnished her office since she got promoted. = 自从她升职以来，她已经重新装修了她的办公室。


过去完成时:
They had refurnished the entire building before the new tenants moved in. = 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经重新装修了整栋楼。
She had refurnished her bedroom before her daughter's visit. = 在她女儿来访之前，她已经重新装修了她的卧室。


将来时:
They will refurnish their bathroom next month. = 他们下个月将重新装修浴室。
We will refurnish the entire house after we move in. = 我们搬进去后将重新装修整栋房子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would refurnish my apartment. = 如果我有更多钱，我会重新装修我的公寓。
I wish I could refurnish my living room. = 我真希望我能重新装修我的客厅。


被动语态:
The house is being refurnished. = 房子正在被重新装修。
The office was refurnished last week. = 办公室上周被重新装修了。


疑问句:
Are they refurnishing their house? = 他们正在重新装修他们的房子吗？
When will you refurnish your bedroom? = 你什么时候会重新装修你的卧室？


否定句:
They did not refurnish their kitchen. = 他们没有重新装修他们的厨房。
We are not refurnishing the living room this year. = 我们今年不重新装修客厅。
