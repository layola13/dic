/dæŋ/ | n. |危险；风险；困境 | danger refers to a situation that could cause harm or injury. It implies a potential threat or peril. |例句：The hikers were in great danger when the storm hit.  登山者在暴风雨来临时处于极大的危险之中。|近义词：peril, hazard, risk, threat |反义词：safety, security


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bridge is in danger of collapsing. = 这座桥有倒塌的危险。
There is a danger of fire. = 有发生火灾的危险。

一般过去时:
The city was in danger during the war. = 战争期间，这座城市处于危险之中。
He narrowly escaped danger. = 他侥幸逃脱了危险。

现在完成时:
We have been in danger several times. = 我们多次处于危险之中。
The situation has improved; the danger has passed. = 情况有所好转；危险已经过去了。

过去完成时:
They had been in danger before the rescue arrived. = 在救援到来之前，他们一直处于危险之中。
The country had faced danger from invasion. = 这个国家曾面临入侵的危险。

将来时:
The dam may be in danger if the river floods. = 如果河水泛滥，大坝可能会面临危险。
We will be in danger if we don't take precautions. = 如果我们不采取预防措施，我们将处于危险之中。

虚拟语气:
If we hadn't acted quickly, we would have been in great danger. = 如果我们没有迅速行动，我们将处于极大的危险之中。
I wish I hadn't put myself in such danger. = 我真希望我没有让自己处于如此危险的境地。

被动语态:
The town was put in danger by the hurricane. = 这个小镇被飓风置于危险之中。
Many lives were saved from danger. = 许多生命被从危险中拯救出来。

疑问句:
Is there any danger? = 有危险吗？
What is the danger? = 危险是什么？

否定句:
There is no danger. = 没有危险。
He was not in danger. = 他没有处于危险之中。


PHRASE->
in danger = 处于危险之中
out of danger = 脱离危险
a danger to = 对...构成危险
at one's own danger = 自担风险
face danger = 面对危险
