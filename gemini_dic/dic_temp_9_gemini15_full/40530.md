controlling interests:/kənˈtroʊlɪŋ ˈɪntrəsts/ | n. | 控制性权益 |  Ownership that gives the holder the power to govern the financial and operating policies of an entity.  This typically involves owning more than 50% of the voting shares, but can also be achieved with less than 50% through various control mechanisms such as voting agreements or special share classes. | Example:  The acquisition gave the investor controlling interests in the company.  这次收购使投资者获得了该公司的控制性权益。


The major shareholder has controlling interests in the firm. 主要股东拥有该公司控制性权益。


近义词：majority ownership, controlling stake, dominant position | 反义词：minority interest, passive investment
