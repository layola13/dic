detasselled:/ˌdiːˈtæsld/ | adj. | 去雄穗的；去雄蕊的 |  Having had the tassels (the male flower parts) removed, typically from a corn plant. This process is done to prevent unwanted pollination and improve the yield of kernels. | Example: The farmer detasselled the corn field to improve the quality of the corn.  农民去除了玉米田的雄穗以提高玉米质量。The detasselled corn plants were healthier and produced more ears. 去雄穗的玉米植株更健康，结的玉米棒更多。| 近义词： emasculated (in the context of plants) | 反义词： tasseled
