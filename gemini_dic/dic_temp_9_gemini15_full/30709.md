certitudes:/ˈsɜːrtɪtjuːdz/| n.|确定的事物；确信；确实|Certainties; things that are known to be true or that are considered certain.  The state of being certain; conviction.|例句：Despite the overwhelming evidence, there are still some who question the certitudes of science. 尽管有压倒性的证据，仍然有一些人质疑科学的确定性。

例句：Her certitudes about her future plans were shaken by the unexpected job loss. 她对未来计划的确定性因意外失业而动摇了。


近义词：certainties, convictions, assurances, facts

反义词：uncertainties, doubts, ambiguities

词性: 名词
