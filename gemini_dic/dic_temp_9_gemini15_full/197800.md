shingler:/ˈʃɪŋɡlər/| n. |盖屋顶的人；制瓦工人；（尤指）铺设木瓦的人 |A shingler is a person who installs shingles, typically on roofs.  They are skilled in working with various types of roofing materials, including wood, asphalt, and composite shingles.  |The shingler worked quickly and efficiently, completing the roof in just a few days.  盖屋顶的人快速有效地工作，几天就完成了屋顶。| roofer, tiler |  (no direct antonym, but could be described as the opposite of someone who *removes* shingles) |名词
