dreadlocks:/ˈdrɛdlɑːks/| n. |脏辫；螺纹状发辫 | Thick, ropelike strands of hair formed by twisting or braiding. Dreadlocks are a hairstyle associated with various cultures and often require significant time and effort to maintain.  | Example:  She wore her dreadlocks in a beautiful braid. 她把她的脏辫编成了一个漂亮的辫子。  |近义词： locs, braids (if referring to the braided style of creation) |反义词：straight hair, short hair
