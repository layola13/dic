medevacing: /ˌmɛdəˈveɪsɪŋ/ | v. |医疗后送；医疗撤离 | The act of evacuating someone for medical reasons, typically by air or other rapid transport.  It is a blend of "medical" and "evacuating". | 例句：The injured soldiers were medevaced to a field hospital. 受伤的士兵被医疗后送至野战医院。  The civilian population was medevaced from the war zone.平民百姓从战区撤离。| 近义词：evacuating, airlifting, transporting (for medical reasons) | 反义词：leaving behind, abandoning (in a medical emergency)
