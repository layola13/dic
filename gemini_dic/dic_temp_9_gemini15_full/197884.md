ships:/ʃɪps/ | n. | 船舶；舰船 |  A vessel, especially one of considerable size, used for transporting people or goods by sea.  |例句：Many ships were damaged in the storm.  许多船只在风暴中受损。

例句：The cargo ship is carrying containers to Asia. 这艘货船正运载集装箱前往亚洲。


近义词：vessels, boats,  liners


反义词：N/A


词性：名词
