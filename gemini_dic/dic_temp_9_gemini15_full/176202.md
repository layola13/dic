re-attack:/ˌriːəˈtæk/| vt.|再次攻击，反击|To attack again; to launch a renewed attack.|例句：The enemy re-attacked at dawn. 敌人黎明时再次发动进攻。|例句：The army re-attacked after regrouping.  军队重整旗鼓后再次发起进攻。|近义词：counterattack, retaliate|反义词：surrender, retreat


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army re-attacks the enemy positions regularly. = 军队定期对敌军阵地进行反击。
He re-attacks the same argument in his new book. = 他在新书中再次攻击了同样的论点。


一般过去时:
The rebels re-attacked the city after a brief respite. = 叛军短暂休整后再次攻击了这座城市。
She re-attacked the problem from a different angle. = 她从不同的角度再次尝试解决这个问题。


现在完成时:
The general has re-attacked the strategic point three times. = 将军已经三次攻击了那个战略要地。
They have re-attacked the issue of climate change in their latest report. = 他们在最新的报告中再次讨论了气候变化的问题。


过去完成时:
The troops had re-attacked before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，部队已经再次发动了进攻。
He had re-attacked the argument several times before finally convincing his opponent. = 在最终说服对手之前，他已经多次反驳了对方的论点。


将来时:
The enemy will re-attack as soon as they are prepared. = 敌人一旦准备好就会再次发动进攻。
We will re-attack the problem with a new approach. = 我们将用一种新的方法再次尝试解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If they re-attacked, we would be ready. = 如果他们再次进攻，我们将做好准备。
I wish he had not re-attacked the same old ideas. = 我希望他没有再次攻击那些老掉牙的想法。


被动语态:
The city was re-attacked last night. = 该城市昨晚再次遭到袭击。
The argument was re-attacked by several scholars. = 这一论点被几位学者再次驳斥。


疑问句:
Will they re-attack tomorrow? = 他们明天会再次进攻吗？
Has the proposal been re-attacked since the last meeting? = 自上次会议以来，这个提案是否再次遭到攻击？


否定句:
They did not re-attack after their initial defeat. = 他们在第一次失败后没有再次进攻。
He did not re-attack the evidence presented. = 他没有再次攻击所提供的证据。
