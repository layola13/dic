近义词：laugh riot,  joke,  comedy,  witticism,  funny thing

词源:  The word is a compound word. "Rib" refers to the ribs, and "-tickler" is a suffix suggesting something that causes a tickling sensation, in this case, laughter.  The image is of laughter causing a tickling sensation in the ribs.
