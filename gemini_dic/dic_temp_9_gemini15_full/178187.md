recombine:/ˌriːkəmˈbaɪn/| vt.|重新组合，重新结合|To combine or unite again; to combine (genetic material) in a new way.|例句：The software allows you to recombine the audio and video tracks.  软件允许你重新组合音频和视频轨道。
例句：Scientists are studying how genes recombine during meiosis. 科学家们正在研究基因在减数分裂过程中如何重新组合。|近义词：reunite, merge, recompose|反义词：separate, divide, disintegrate|动词
