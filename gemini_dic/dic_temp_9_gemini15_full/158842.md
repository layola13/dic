plumeless:/ˈpluːmləs/| adj.|无羽毛的；无饰的|Without feathers; lacking plumes or ornamental feathers.  The word describes something devoid of feathers or similar decorative plumes.|例句：The plucked chicken looked rather plumeless and pathetic.  拔光毛的鸡看起来相当无毛且可怜。

例句：The bald eagle, though powerful, appeared rather plumeless from a distance.  虽然强大，但从远处看，秃鹰看起来相当无毛。


近义词：featherless, unfeathered, bare


反义词：feathered, plumed
