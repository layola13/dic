extinct:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. |灭绝的，绝种的 |  No longer existing.  A species is extinct when there are no more living members of that species. | Example: The dodo bird is extinct.  渡渡鸟已经灭绝了。  The dinosaurs became extinct millions of years ago. 恐龙在数百万年前灭绝了。|近义词：extinguished, defunct, vanished, lost | 反义词： extant, existing, surviving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The species is extinct. = 这个物种已经灭绝了。
The plant is extinct in the wild. = 这种植物在野外已经灭绝了。

一般过去时:
The woolly mammoth went extinct thousands of years ago. = 长毛象在几千年前灭绝了。
The passenger pigeon became extinct in the early 20th century. = 旅鸽在20世纪初灭绝了。

现在完成时:
Many species have gone extinct due to human activity. = 许多物种由于人类活动而灭绝了。
Several bird species have become extinct in recent decades. = 近几十年来，有几种鸟类已经灭绝了。

过去完成时:
The dinosaurs had already become extinct before humans evolved. = 在人类进化之前，恐龙就已经灭绝了。
Scientists believe that many species had gone extinct before they were even discovered. = 科学家认为许多物种在被发现之前就已经灭绝了。

将来时:
If we don't act now, more species will become extinct. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，更多物种将灭绝。
Scientists predict that several plant species will go extinct in the next century. = 科学家预测，下个世纪几种植物物种将灭绝。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, perhaps some species would not be extinct today. = 如果我们早点采取行动，也许有些物种今天就不会灭绝了。
I wish that the dodo bird were not extinct. = 我希望渡渡鸟没有灭绝。

被动语态:
Many species are threatened with extinction. = 许多物种面临灭绝的威胁。(被动语态描述物种所面临的状态)
The passenger pigeon was driven to extinction by overhunting. = 旅鸽由于过度捕猎而灭绝了。(被动语态描述导致灭绝的原因)

疑问句:
Is the Tasmanian tiger extinct? = 袋狼灭绝了吗？
Are there any extinct species that have been brought back to life? = 有任何灭绝的物种被复活了吗？

否定句:
That species is not yet extinct. = 那个物种尚未灭绝。
The panda is not extinct, but it is endangered. = 熊猫没有灭绝，但它濒临灭绝。


PHRASE->
threatened with extinction = 濒临灭绝

functional extinction = 功能性灭绝
mass extinction = 大规模灭绝
