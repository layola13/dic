The term can also refer to the gillnets themselves or other tools used for trapping. | 例句：The gillers set their nets at dawn.  诱捕者在黎明时分设置渔网。  The illegal gillers were caught and fined. 非法捕鱼者被抓获并处以罚款。 | 近义词：fishermen, trappers, netters | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with conservationists or animal rights activists. | 名词
