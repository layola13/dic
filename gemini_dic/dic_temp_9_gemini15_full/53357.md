disaffected:/ˌdɪs.əˈfektɪd/| adj.|不满的；离心的；疏远的 | Feeling or showing dissatisfaction and discontent | The disaffected youth turned to crime.  不满的青年转向犯罪。|  alienated, disgruntled, disenchanted | satisfied, content, loyal | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The workers are disaffected with their low wages. 工人们对他们低廉的工资感到不满。
He is disaffected from the political process. 他对政治进程感到疏远。


一般过去时:
The employees were disaffected after the company announced layoffs. 公司宣布裁员后，员工们感到不满。
She was disaffected with her job and decided to quit. 她对自己的工作感到不满，决定辞职。


现在完成时:
Many young people have become disaffected with the current government. 许多年轻人对现政府感到不满。
The community has been disaffected by years of neglect.  多年被忽视，这个社区已经离心离德了。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had been disaffected for months before the mutiny.  兵变之前，士兵们已经不满了好几个月了。
They had become disaffected with the leadership long before the crisis.  早在危机爆发之前，他们就对领导层感到不满。


将来时:
The government fears that more people will become disaffected if the economy continues to worsen. 如果经济继续恶化，政府担心会有更多的人变得不满。
If the reforms fail,  many citizens will likely be disaffected. 如果改革失败，许多公民可能会感到不满。


虚拟语气:
If the workers were not so disaffected, they might be more productive. 如果工人们没有那么不满，他们可能会更有生产力。
I wish the students weren't so disaffected with their studies. 我希望学生们不要对学习如此不满。


被动语态:
The government's policies have left many citizens feeling disaffected. 政府的政策使许多公民感到不满。
The new management is trying to address the issues that have disaffected the workforce. 新管理层正试图解决导致员工不满的问题。


疑问句:
Are the voters disaffected with the current political climate? 选民们对目前的政治气候不满吗？
Is there anything that could be done to alleviate the disaffected population? 有什么办法可以缓解不满的人群吗？


否定句:
The employees are not disaffected; they are simply overworked. 员工们并不不满；他们只是工作过度。
The community is not disaffected; they are actively involved in local politics. 这个社区并不离心离德；他们积极参与地方政治。
