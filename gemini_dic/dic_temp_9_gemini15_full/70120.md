feyness:/ˈfeɪnɪs/| n. |古怪；精灵古怪；超凡脱俗；神秘感|A whimsical or capricious quality; an otherworldly or magical charm.  It suggests a lighthearted strangeness, often associated with fairies or folklore.  The feeling can be enchanting or slightly unsettling, depending on the context. |例句：There was a certain feyness about her, a mischievous glint in her eye.  她身上有一种精灵古怪的气质，眼中闪烁着淘气的神采。

There's a feyness to the old house, as if it holds secrets from another time. 这栋老房子有一种超凡脱俗的气质，仿佛隐藏着另一个时代的秘密。|近义词：whimsicality, capriciousness, eccentricity, otherworldliness|反义词：ordinariness, normality, mundanity
