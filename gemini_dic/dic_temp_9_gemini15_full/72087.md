floatiest: /ˈfloʊtiɪst/ | adj. | 最漂浮的，最轻盈的 | Most buoyant; most likely to float.  It is the superlative form of the adjective "floaty".  | Example: The floatiest cloud drifted lazily across the sky.  最轻盈的云朵懒洋洋地飘过天空。 | Near synonyms: lightest, airiest, most buoyant | Antonyms: heaviest, densest, most grounded |  形容词
