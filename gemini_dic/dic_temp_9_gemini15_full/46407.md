daffodils:/ˈdæfədɪlz/| n. |水仙花|A daffodil is a yellow flower with a long trumpet-shaped center.  They are often associated with spring and new beginnings. |The daffodils were in full bloom in the meadow.|近义词：narcissus|反义词:无
