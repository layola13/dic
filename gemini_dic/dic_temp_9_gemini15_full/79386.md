glided:/ˈɡlaɪdɪd/ | v. | 滑翔；滑动；优雅地移动 | To move smoothly and effortlessly, as if floating.  |例句：The bird glided effortlessly through the air.  这只鸟轻盈地滑翔在空中。

例句：The dancer glided across the stage. 舞者优雅地滑过舞台。


近义词：slid, floated, sailed, swept


反义词：bumped, stumbled, crashed


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird glides effortlessly through the air. = 鸟儿轻盈地滑翔在空中。
He glides through life with little effort. 他毫不费力地生活着。

虚拟语气:
