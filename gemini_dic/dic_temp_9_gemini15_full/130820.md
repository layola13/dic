nonappealability:/ˌnɒnəˌpiːləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可上诉性，不服上诉 | The inability to appeal a decision or ruling.  This often refers to a legal context where a judgment or order cannot be challenged in a higher court.  | Example: The court ruled that the decision was final and subject to nonappealability.  法院裁定该决定是最终的，不可上诉。 |近义词：finality, unappealable nature | 反义词：appealability
