outreasoning: /ˌaʊtˈriːzənɪŋ/ | v. | 以理服人，胜过…的推理 |  To defeat or surpass someone in reasoning or argument.  It implies a superior intellect or skill in logical argumentation. |  例句：He completely outreasoned his opponent in the debate, leaving them speechless. 他在辩论中完全以理服人，让对手哑口无言。

例句：The detective outreasoned the criminal, leading to his confession.  侦探以理服人，导致罪犯认罪。


近义词：outwit, outsmart, outargue, surpass in logic

反义词：be out reasoned by, be defeated in argument


词性：动词
