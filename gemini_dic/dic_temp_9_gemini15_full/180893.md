rehumiliates: /ˌriːhjuːˈmɪlieɪts/ | vt. | 再度羞辱；再次使蒙羞 | To humiliate someone again; to subject someone to a second or repeated instance of humiliation. |例句：The constant criticism rehumiliates him, chipping away at his self-esteem.  不断的批评再次让他蒙羞，一点一点地磨损了他的自尊心。

The public apology, instead of helping, only rehumiliated the already disgraced politician. 公开道歉并没有起到帮助作用，反而再次羞辱了这位已经声名狼藉的政客。


近义词：再次羞辱，再次使丢脸，再次使难堪 |再次贬低，再次使卑微 |  mortify again, shame again, abase again, degrade again

反义词：抬举，赞扬，尊敬 | uplift, praise, honor


词性：动词
