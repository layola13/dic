dunfish:/ˈdʌnˌfɪʃ/| n. |干鱼，盐渍鱼 |Dried or salted fish, especially cod.  Dunfish is a type of preserved fish that has been dried and/or salted to extend its shelf life.  It's often associated with cod, but other fish can be prepared in this manner. |例句：The fishermen sold their dunfish to merchants for export. 渔民们把他们的干鱼卖给了出口商。|例句：The traditional recipe called for dunfish and potatoes.  传统的食谱需要干鱼和土豆。|近义词：dried fish, salted fish, cured fish |反义词：fresh fish
