kph:/keɪpiːeɪtʃ/|abbr.|公里每小时|kilometers per hour, a unit of speed.|例句：The car was traveling at 100 kph. 这辆车正以每小时100公里的速度行驶。|近义词：km/h|反义词：无


USAGE->
This abbreviation doesn't have verb conjugations as it's not a verb.  It's a unit of measurement.  Therefore, grammatical tenses and moods are not applicable.


PHRASE->
kph is typically used independently and doesn't form common phrases.  It's primarily used in numerical contexts to express speed.  For example: "The speed limit is 80 kph."  "The train reached a top speed of 200 kph."
