diagraming:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræmɪŋ/ | v. | 绘制图表；图解 | The act of creating diagrams; illustrating or explaining something using diagrams.  | He is diagraming the circuit to understand its function. 他正在绘制电路图以了解其功能。 | charting, graphing, sketching |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境用undrawing等表达相反含义) | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am diagraming the sentence structure. = 我正在绘制句子的结构图。
She diagrams complex equations for her students. = 她为学生绘制复杂的方程式图表。

一般过去时:
We diagrammed the process step by step. = 我们一步一步地绘制了这个过程的图表。
He diagrammed the play's plot on the board. = 他在黑板上绘制了该剧的情节图。

现在完成时:
I have diagrammed several different approaches to the problem. = 我已经绘制了几种解决这个问题的不同方法的图表。
They have diagrammed the entire system for better understanding. = 为了更好地理解，他们已经绘制了整个系统的图表。

过去完成时:
We had diagrammed the initial design before the meeting. = 我们在会议之前已经绘制了最初的设计图。
She had diagrammed the data before analyzing it. = 在分析数据之前，她已经绘制了数据的图表。
