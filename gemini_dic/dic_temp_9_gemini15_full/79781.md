glutted:/ˈɡlʌtɪd/| adj.| 充满的；过多的；吃饱了的 |  Overfilled; excessively supplied; satiated.  |例句：The market is glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|例句：After the large Thanksgiving meal, everyone felt glutted. 感恩节大餐后，每个人都感觉吃饱了。|近义词：saturated, crammed, stuffed, surfeited |反义词：deficient, lacking, starved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The market is glutted with cheap products. = 市场充斥着廉价产品。
He feels glutted after eating a huge meal. = 他吃了一顿大餐后感觉饱了。

一般过去时:
The shops were glutted with Christmas toys last December. = 去年12月商店里堆满了圣诞玩具。
I felt glutted after the three-course dinner. = 三道菜的晚餐后我感觉吃饱了。

现在完成时:
The city has been glutted with tourists this summer. = 今年夏天这个城市挤满了游客。
We have glutted ourselves on delicious pastries. = 我们吃掉了很多美味的糕点，吃撑了。

过去完成时:
The warehouse had been glutted with unsold goods for months. = 仓库里堆积了数月的滞销商品。
They had glutted themselves on candy before dinner. = 他们晚饭前吃撑了糖果。


将来时:
The market will be glutted with new smartphones next year. = 明年市场将充斥着新型智能手机。
I think I will be glutted if I eat another slice of cake. = 我想如果再吃一块蛋糕我会吃撑的。

虚拟语气:
If the market were glutted, prices would fall. = 如果市场饱和，价格就会下跌。
I wish I hadn't glutted myself on that spicy food. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多辣的食物。


被动语态:
The market was glutted by the influx of cheap goods. = 市场因廉价商品涌入而饱和。
The audience was glutted with information during the long presentation. = 观众在冗长的演示中信息过载。

疑问句:
Is the market glutted with similar products? = 市场是否充斥着类似的产品？
Are you feeling glutted after that huge lunch? = 你吃过那顿丰盛的午餐后感觉饱了吗？


否定句:
The market is not glutted; there is still demand. = 市场并不饱和；仍然存在需求。
I am not glutted; I could eat more. = 我还没吃饱；我还可以多吃点。
