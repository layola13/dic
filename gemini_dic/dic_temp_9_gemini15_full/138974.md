nyctophile:/ˈnɪktəˌfaɪl/| n. |恋夜者，夜猫子 |A person who loves or is attracted to the night.  Someone who prefers the night to the day and feels more comfortable and alert at night.  |Example:  She's a confirmed nyctophile; she rarely ventures out during the day. 她是个不折不扣的夜猫子；她很少在白天出门。|近义词：night owl, nocturnal person |反义词：heliophile
