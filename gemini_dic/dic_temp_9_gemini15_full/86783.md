hem-in:/ˈhɛmɪn/| vt. |包围，围困，限制|To surround and restrict someone's movement or options.|The troops hemmed in the enemy.  部队包围了敌人。|The high walls hemmed in the small garden. 高墙包围着那个小花园。|enclose, surround, confine|free, liberate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tall buildings hem in the small park. = 高楼大厦包围着这个小公园。
They hem me in with their constant demands. = 他们不断的要求限制了我。


一般过去时:
The police hemmed in the suspect. = 警察包围了嫌疑犯。

现在完成时:
The city has hemmed in the countryside. = 城市已经包围了乡村。

过去完成时:
