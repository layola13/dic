division:/dɪˈvɪʒən/ | n. | 分配；部门；除法；隔阂 | The act of dividing or separating something into parts; a section or group within a larger organization; the mathematical process of finding how many times one number goes into another; a difference or separation between people or groups. |例句：The division of the land was a complex process. 土地的分割是一个复杂的过程。  The marketing division is responsible for advertising. 市场部负责广告宣传。|近义词：section, department, partition, separation |反义词：union, combination, unification


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company divides its profits among its shareholders. = 公司将其利润分配给股东。
She divides her time between work and family. = 她把时间分配在工作和家庭之间。


一般过去时:
We divided the cake into eight slices. = 我们把蛋糕切成八块。
The army divided its forces into three groups. = 军队将其兵力分成三组。


现在完成时:
They have divided the inheritance equally among the siblings. = 他们已将遗产平均分配给兄弟姐妹。
She has divided her attention between two projects. = 她把注意力分散在两个项目上。


过去完成时:
They had divided the spoils before the others arrived. = 其他人到达之前，他们已经瓜分了战利品。
Before the meeting, they had divided themselves into working groups. = 会议之前，他们已经分成几个工作小组。


将来时:
We will divide the tasks among the team members. = 我们将把任务分配给团队成员。
The company will divide its resources between two projects. = 公司将把资源分配到两个项目上。


虚拟语气:
If we divided the workload fairly, we could finish earlier. = 如果我们公平地分配工作量，我们可以早点完成。
I wish I had divided my time more wisely. = 我希望我当时能更明智地分配时间。


被动语态:
The cake was divided into six pieces. = 蛋糕被切成六块。
The work was divided equally among the employees. = 工作被平均分配给员工。


疑问句:
How is the company divided into departments? = 公司是如何划分部门的？
How many ways can we divide this problem? = 我们有多少种方法可以解决这个问题？


否定句:
They did not divide the responsibility fairly. = 他们没有公平地分配责任。
The class was not divided into smaller groups. = 班级没有分成更小的组。
