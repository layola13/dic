pluperfect:/ˈpluːpɜːrfɪkt/| adj.|过去完成时|The pluperfect tense is a verb tense used to describe an action that was completed before another action in the past.  It emphasizes the completion of one past action before another past action occurred. It is formed using the past participle of the main verb and an auxiliary verb "had".|

例句：
She had finished her work before he arrived. = 她在他到达之前已经完成了她的工作。

They had eaten dinner when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候，他们已经吃过晚饭了。


近义词：past perfect (完全一样的意思)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，与一般过去时相对)


词性：形容词 (通常用来修饰动词时态)
