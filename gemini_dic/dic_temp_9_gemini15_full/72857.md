folklorists:/ˌfoʊkˈlɔːrɪsts/| n. |民间传说学者；民俗学家|Scholars who study folklore, the traditional beliefs, customs, and stories of a community or culture. They research, collect, and analyze folklore to understand cultural heritage and traditions.|The folklorists presented their findings on the origins of the local ghost story.  民间传说学者们展示了他们关于当地鬼故事起源的研究成果。|anthropologists, ethnologists|—|
