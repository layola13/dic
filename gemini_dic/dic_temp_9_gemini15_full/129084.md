nitroprusside:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈprʌsaɪd/| n. |硝普钠|Sodium nitroprusside is a potent vasodilator used to treat hypertensive emergencies. It works by relaxing the smooth muscles in blood vessels, causing them to widen and lowering blood pressure.  Nitroprusside is administered intravenously and its effects are rapid and short-lived.
