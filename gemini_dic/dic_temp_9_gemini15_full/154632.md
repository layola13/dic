persisting:/pərˈsɪstɪŋ/| v. adj.|持续的；坚持不懈的|Continuing to exist or happen, especially in spite of difficulty or opposition.|例句：Despite the challenges, she persisted in her efforts.  尽管面临挑战，她仍然坚持努力。|近义词：continuing, enduring, lasting|反义词：ceasing, stopping, ending|动词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem persists. = 问题持续存在。
He persists in his denial. = 他坚持否认。


一般过去时:
The pain persisted for several hours. = 疼痛持续了好几个小时。
She persisted with her studies despite the distractions. = 尽管分心，她仍然坚持学习。


现在完成时:
The drought has persisted throughout the summer. = 干旱持续整个夏季。
He has persisted in his belief despite overwhelming evidence to the contrary. = 尽管有压倒性的证据反驳，他仍然坚持自己的信念。


过去完成时:
The rain had persisted all night. = 雨整夜都在下。
She had persisted in her efforts for years before finally succeeding. = 在最终成功之前，她已经努力多年了。


将来时:
The cold weather will persist into next week. = 寒冷的天气将持续到下周。
I believe this trend will persist for many years to come. = 我相信这种趋势将在未来许多年持续下去。


虚拟语气:
If only he would persist in his efforts. = 但愿他会坚持他的努力。
If I had persisted, I might have succeeded. = 如果我坚持下去，也许会成功。


被动语态:
The policy was persisted with despite opposition. = 尽管遭到反对，这项政策仍然坚持实行。(注意：被动语态在此用法略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The issue is persistently ignored. =  这个问题一直被忽视。(此句用persist的形容词形式更自然)


疑问句:
Will this feeling persist? = 这种感觉会持续吗？
Does the pain still persist? = 疼痛还在持续吗？


否定句:
The symptoms did not persist. = 症状没有持续。
He refused to persist in his foolish behavior. = 他拒绝继续他愚蠢的行为。


PHRASE->
persist in: 坚持
persist with: 坚持做
persistently oppose: 持续反对
persist in doing: 坚持做某事
persist in one's belief: 坚持某人的信念


词源:
From Old French persister, from Latin persistere (to stand firm), from per- (completely) + sistere (to stand).
