minaret:/ˌmɪnəˈrɛt/| n. |尖塔|A tall, slender tower, typically part of a mosque, having a balcony from which the muezzin calls the faithful to prayer.|The minaret of the mosque was visible from miles away.  清真寺的尖塔从几英里外就能看到。|近义词：tower, steeple|反义词：N/A
