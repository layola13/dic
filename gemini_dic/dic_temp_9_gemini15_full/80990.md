grade:/ɡreɪd/ | n. v. |等级；分数；成绩；年级；分级；评级；给…分等级 |  A grade is a level of quality or achievement.  It can refer to a score on a test, a level in school, or a classification of something.  |例句：He received a good grade on his exam. 他考试得了好成绩。|例句：The apples are graded according to size. 苹果按大小分级。|近义词：level, rank, mark, score |反义词：ungraded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher grades the papers. = 老师批改试卷。
She grades the students' essays carefully. = 她仔细批改学生的论文。


一般过去时:
He graded the exams last night. = 他昨晚批改了考试卷。
They graded the apples into three sizes. = 他们把苹果分成了三个大小等级。


现在完成时:
I have graded all the assignments. = 我已经批改完所有的作业了。
She has graded the students' projects and given them feedback. = 她已经批改了学生的项目并给了他们反馈。


过去完成时:
The teacher had graded the tests before the students arrived. = 学生到达之前，老师已经批改完试卷了。
By the time the deadline arrived, he had graded all the applications. = 截止日期到来时，他已经批改完所有的申请了。


将来时:
She will grade the papers tomorrow. = 她明天将批改试卷。
The committee will grade the entries based on originality and creativity. = 委员会将根据原创性和创造力对参赛作品进行评级。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would grade the papers more carefully. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更仔细地批改试卷。
If I were the teacher, I would grade the exams more leniently. = 如果我是老师，我会更宽松地批改考试。


被动语态:
The exams were graded by the computer. = 考试卷由电脑批改。
The students' work is being graded right now. = 学生们的作业正在被批改。


疑问句:
What grade did you get on the test? = 你考试得了多少分？
How are the students' projects graded? = 学生们的项目是如何评级的？


否定句:
I haven't graded the essays yet. = 我还没批改论文。
The teacher didn't grade the papers on time. = 老师没有按时批改试卷。


PHRASE->
grade inflation = 成绩膨胀
grade point average (GPA) = 平均绩点
grade level = 年级水平
A grade = A等
failing grade = 不及格


词源:
From Old French *grade, from Latin gradus "step, degree," from PIE *gred- "to walk, step."  The meaning evolved from a physical step to a level or stage of something.
