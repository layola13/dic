condemner:/kənˈdɛmnər/| n. |谴责者，责难者 |A condemner is a person who strongly disapproves of something and expresses their disapproval.  They may publicly criticize or denounce actions, policies, or individuals. | Example: The condemner of the war voiced his strong opposition in a public forum.  谴责这场战争的人在公开论坛上表达了他的强烈反对。  The judge, a staunch condemner of violence, sentenced the defendant to a lengthy prison term.  法官，一位坚决谴责暴力的法官，判处被告长期监禁。|近义词：critic, censurer, denouncer, reprover |反义词：supporter, approver, defender
