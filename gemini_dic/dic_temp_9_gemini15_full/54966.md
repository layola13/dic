disproportion:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃən/| n.|不成比例，不相称|The state or fact of being out of proportion; lack of balance or symmetry.|例句：There is a disproportion between the number of men and women in the company. 公司男女员工比例失调。|例句：The punishment was disproportionate to the crime. 惩罚与罪行不相称。|近义词：imbalance, disparity, inequality|反义词：proportion, balance, symmetry|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The workload is disproportionate to the pay. = 工作量与报酬不成比例。
The representation of women in politics shows a disproportion. = 政治领域女性的代表性显示出一种不成比例。


一般过去时:
The response to the crisis was disproportionate to the actual threat. = 对危机的反应与实际威胁不成比例。
The punishment seemed disproportionate to the offense. = 惩罚似乎与罪行不成比例。


现在完成时:
The media has disproportionately focused on this aspect of the story. = 媒体对故事的这个方面关注过度。
The government has disproportionately allocated resources to this project. = 政府对这个项目分配了不成比例的资源。


过去完成时:
The damage had been disproportionate to the initial impact. = 损害与最初的影响不成比例。
They realized that their efforts had been disproportionate to the results achieved. = 他们意识到他们的努力与取得的成果不成比例。


将来时:
The impact of climate change will be disproportionately felt by developing countries. = 气候变化的影响将由发展中国家不成比例地承受。
The cost of the project is likely to be disproportionate to its benefits. = 该项目的成本可能与其效益不成比例。


虚拟语气:
If the resources were allocated more fairly, the results would not be so disproportionate. = 如果资源分配更公平，结果就不会如此不成比例。
I wish the attention given to this issue were not so disproportionate. = 我希望对这个问题的关注不要如此不成比例。


被动语态:
Resources are disproportionately allocated to urban areas. = 资源不成比例地分配给城市地区。
The problem was disproportionately highlighted in the media. = 这个问题在媒体中被不成比例地强调。


疑问句:
Is the representation of minorities in the media disproportionate? = 少数族裔在媒体中的代表性是否不成比例？
Are the penalties disproportionate to the offenses committed? = 惩罚与所犯的罪行是否不成比例？


否定句:
The distribution of wealth was not disproportionate. = 财富分配并非不成比例。
The company's response to the complaints was not disproportionate. = 公司对投诉的回应并非不成比例。


PHRASE->
disproportionate allocation = 不成比例的分配
disproportionate representation = 不成比例的代表性
disproportionate impact = 不成比例的影响
disproportionate burden = 不成比例的负担
disproportionate growth = 不成比例的增长


词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + proportion (比例)  来自拉丁语 *proportio,  由pro- (向前) + portio (部分)构成。
