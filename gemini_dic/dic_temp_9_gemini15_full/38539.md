conclude:/kənˈkluːd/ | vt. |结束；总结；推断；缔结|To bring something to an end; to summarize; to deduce or infer; to finalize an agreement. |例句：The meeting concluded at 5 pm.  会议在下午5点结束。  |例句：She concluded her speech with a powerful quote. 她用一句有力的话语结束了她的演讲。|近义词：finish, end, summarize, infer, deduce |反义词：begin, start, commence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He concludes his presentation every week. 他每周都结束他的演示。
The study concludes that smoking is harmful. 研究得出结论，吸烟有害健康。

一般过去时:
The judge concluded the trial. 法官结束了审判。
We concluded the meeting after three hours. 我们三个小时后结束了会议。

现在完成时:
They have concluded their investigation. 他们已经完成了他们的调查。
She has concluded that the hypothesis is incorrect. 她已经得出结论，假设是错误的。

过去完成时:
He had concluded his work before the deadline. 他在截止日期前完成了他的工作。
By then, they had concluded that there was no solution. 到那时，他们已经得出结论，没有解决办法。

将来时:
The conference will conclude tomorrow. 会议将于明天结束。
We will conclude the negotiations soon. 我们很快就会结束谈判。

虚拟语气:
If he concluded the deal, he would be promoted. 如果他完成了这笔交易，他就会被提升。
I wish I had concluded the report earlier. 我希望我早点完成报告。

被动语态:
The meeting was concluded early. 会议提前结束了。
The experiment was concluded successfully. 实验成功结束了。

疑问句:
When will the meeting conclude? 会议什么时候结束？
How did you conclude that? 你是怎么得出那个结论的？

否定句:
He did not conclude his speech on time. 他没有按时结束他的演讲。
The scientists haven't concluded their research yet. 科学家们还没有完成他们的研究。


PHRASE->
conclude with: 以...结束
conclude from: 从...推断出
conclude that: 得出结论
jump to conclusions: 妄下结论
in conclusion: 总之


词源:
From Latin *concludere, from com- ("together") + claudere ("to shut, close").  The original sense was "to shut up, enclose," evolving to its current meanings of bringing something to an end, summarizing, or reaching a conclusion through reasoning.
