misinference:/ˌmɪsˈɪnfərəns/| n. |误解，错误推断 |The act of making a wrong inference; a mistaken deduction.  It involves drawing an incorrect conclusion from available evidence or information.  |例句：His misinference of the data led to a flawed conclusion. 他的数据误解导致了错误的结论。  The misinference stemmed from a misunderstanding of the instructions. 误解源于对指示的误解。|近义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation, false conclusion, erroneous deduction |反义词：correct inference, accurate deduction, sound reasoning
