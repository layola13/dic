pesticides:/ˈpɛstɪsaɪdz/| n. |杀虫剂；农药|Substances used to kill pests, including insects, rodents, weeds, and other organisms harmful to crops, livestock, or human health.  Pesticides are widely used in agriculture, public health, and household pest control. However, their use can have negative environmental and health consequences.|The farmer sprayed pesticides on his crops to protect them from insects.|近义词：insecticides, herbicides, rodenticides|反义词：natural pest control
