fudged:/fʌdʒd/| v.|篡改，伪造，敷衍了事 | To alter or falsify (something) in a way that is intended to deceive; to do something badly or carelessly.  |例句：He fudged the figures to make the company look more profitable. 他篡改了数字，让公司看起来更赚钱。

例句：She fudged her way through the exam, hoping to pass without really understanding the material. 她敷衍了事地完成了考试，希望能通过考试而不用真正理解材料。


近义词：doctor, falsify, fake, manipulate, cheat, bungle, botch,  mishandle

反义词：verify, confirm, authenticate, improve, perfect
