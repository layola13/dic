hoarier:/ˈhoʊriər/| adj. |更苍老的；更灰白的；更古老的 |More grayish white;  more aged; showing the effect of age.  The word suggests a covering of something resembling hoar frost, implying age and sometimes a slightly negative connotation of shabbiness or decay. |The hoarier parts of the building showed signs of neglect.  这栋建筑物年代久远的部分显露出被忽视的迹象。


The hoarier chapters of the book were less engaging than the others. 这本书中年代久远的部分不如其他部分引人入胜。


近义词：whiter, older, grayer, antique


反义词：younger, newer, fresher
