disordered:/dɪsˈɔːrdərd/ | adj. | 无序的；紊乱的；混乱的 | Lacking order or organization; in a state of confusion.  |The disordered room reflected his chaotic mind.  凌乱的房间反映了他混乱的头脑。 The patient presented with disordered thinking. 病人表现出思维紊乱。 | ordered, organized, systematic | orderly, neat, tidy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The papers are disordered on his desk. = 他的桌子上文件杂乱无章。
Her thoughts are disordered and hard to follow. = 她的思绪混乱，难以理解。

一般过去时:
The library was disordered after the party. = 聚会后图书馆一片混乱。
His schedule was disordered due to the unexpected event. = 由于意外事件，他的日程安排被打乱了。

现在完成时:
The situation has become disordered since the accident. = 事故发生后，情况变得混乱不堪。
My sleep schedule has been disordered lately due to stress. = 最近由于压力，我的睡眠时间很不规律。

过去完成时:
The files had been disordered before the new system was implemented. = 新系统实施之前，文件就已经很混乱了。
His life had been disordered long before he sought help. = 在他寻求帮助很久之前，他的生活就已经很混乱了。

将来时:
The room will be disordered if we don't clean it. = 如果我们不打扫，房间就会乱成一团。
The market will likely be disordered by the new regulations. = 新规很可能会扰乱市场秩序。

虚拟语气:
If the system were disordered, it would crash. = 如果系统混乱，它就会崩溃。
I wish my life weren't so disordered. = 我希望我的生活不要这么混乱。

被动语态:
The documents were disordered by the flood. = 文件被洪水冲乱了。
The data was disordered during transmission. = 数据在传输过程中出现了紊乱。

疑问句:
Is your schedule disordered? = 你的日程安排混乱吗？
Are the files disordered in the cabinet? = 文件柜里的文件杂乱无章吗？

否定句:
His life is not disordered. = 他的生活井然有序。
The room was not disordered; it was meticulously organized. = 房间并非凌乱，而是井井有条。
