inhaling:/ɪnˈheɪlɪŋ/| v.|吸入，吸气|The act of drawing breath into the lungs.|例句：He was inhaling deeply, trying to calm himself. 他深深地吸了一口气，试图让自己平静下来。  例句：The doctor warned him about the dangers of inhaling asbestos dust. 医生警告他吸入石棉粉尘的危险。|近义词：breathing in, aspirating|反义词：exhaling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inhales deeply before speaking. = 他在说话之前深吸一口气。
She inhales the fresh mountain air. = 她吸入新鲜的山间空气。

一般过去时:
He inhaled deeply and then began to sing. = 他深吸一口气，然后开始唱歌。
She inhaled the sweet scent of roses. = 她吸入了玫瑰花甜美的香味。

现在完成时:
I have inhaled too much smoke. = 我吸入了太多的烟。
She has inhaled the pollen from the flowers. = 她吸入了花朵的花粉。

过去完成时:
He had inhaled the toxic fumes before he realised the danger. = 他在意识到危险之前已经吸入了有毒烟雾。
She had inhaled the perfume before she entered the room. = 她进房间前已经闻到了香水味。

将来时:
He will inhale the anesthetic before the surgery. = 他将在手术前吸入麻醉剂。
She will inhale deeply to prepare for the dive. = 她将深吸一口气为潜水做准备。

虚拟语气:
If he inhaled deeply, he would feel better. = 如果他深吸一口气，他会感觉更好。
I wish I had not inhaled that dust. = 我真希望我没有吸入那灰尘。

被动语态:
The smoke was inhaled by the children. = 烟雾被孩子们吸入了。
The anesthetic is inhaled by the patient. = 麻醉剂被病人吸入。

疑问句:
Is he inhaling the poison? = 他在吸入毒药吗？
Are they inhaling the fumes? = 他们正在吸入烟雾吗？

否定句:
He did not inhale the gas. = 他没有吸入气体。
She does not inhale cigarette smoke. = 她不吸烟。
