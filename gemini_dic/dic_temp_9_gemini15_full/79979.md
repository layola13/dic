go-cold:/ɡoʊ kəʊld/ | adj. |变冷；冷淡；(感情)冷却|  Describes something that becomes cold or loses warmth, or, metaphorically, describes a relationship or feeling that becomes distant or less enthusiastic.  |例句：The coffee went cold while I was on the phone.  咖啡在我打电话的时候凉了。|例句：Their relationship went cold after the argument. 他们争吵后关系变得冷淡了。|近义词：cool down, become frigid, lose warmth |反义词：heat up, warm up, become passionate
