It implies a forceful and deliberate response aimed at neutralizing or overcoming the initial action. |例句：The general planned a series of counterstrokes to regain lost territory.  将军计划了一系列的反击行动以收复失地。

The government launched a series of economic counterstrokes to combat inflation. 政府发起了一系列经济反击措施来对抗通货膨胀。|近义词：retaliation, counterattack, riposte, rejoinder |反义词：submission, surrender, capitulation |名词
