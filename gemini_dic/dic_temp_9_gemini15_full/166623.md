presentational:/ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃənəl/| adj. | 演示的；陈述的；表现的 | Relating to or involving presentation; characterized by presentation.  It often describes something designed to present information effectively, or the style of presenting something. |例句：The presentational aspects of the report were well-handled.  报告的演示部分处理得很好。

例句：Her presentational skills were evident in her clear and concise speech.  她在清晰简洁的演讲中展现了她的表达能力。


近义词：expository, demonstrative, illustrative, expressive


反义词：unclear, inexpressive, obscure, hidden


词性：形容词
