It often refers to a lack of skill or expertise, especially in a particular area, accompanied by a lack of self-awareness.|例句：His dufferism in the kitchen resulted in a burnt dinner.  他的厨房笨手笨脚导致晚餐烧焦了。|近义词：ineptitude, incompetence, ineptitude, clumsiness, awkwardness|反义词：expertise, skill, proficiency, dexterity
