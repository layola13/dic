slighted:/ˈslaɪtɪd/ | vt. |轻视；忽略；怠慢 | To treat (someone) with disdain or indifference; to disregard or ignore.  |例句：I felt slighted when they didn't invite me to the party.  我因为他们没有邀请我参加聚会而感到受冷落。

例句：He slighted his responsibilities and the project failed. 他忽略了他的责任，导致项目失败。|近义词：ignore, neglect, disregard, snub, insult |反义词：respect, honor, cherish, value |动词
