inflict:/ɪnˈflɪkt/| vt.| 造成（痛苦、伤害等）；使遭受 | to cause (something unpleasant or painful) to be suffered by someone or something | The attack inflicted heavy casualties.  这次袭击造成了重大伤亡。 | 近义词：impose, deal, administer | 反义词：relieve, alleviate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inflicts pain on others. = 他对他人施加痛苦。
The government inflicts new taxes on the people. = 政府向人民征收新税。

一般过去时:
The storm inflicted severe damage on the city. = 暴风雨给城市造成了严重的破坏。
She inflicted a terrible wound on him. = 她给他造成了可怕的伤口。

现在完成时:
The war has inflicted untold suffering on the population. = 战争给人民带来了难以言喻的痛苦。
Years of drought have inflicted severe damage on the crops. = 多年的干旱给庄稼造成了严重的损害。

过去完成时:
The earthquake had inflicted irreparable damage before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，地震已经造成了无法弥补的损失。
Before the doctor's intervention, the accident had inflicted serious injuries. = 在医生介入之前，事故已经造成了严重的伤害。

将来时:
The new policy will inflict hardship on many families. = 新政策将给许多家庭带来困难。
The upcoming hurricane is likely to inflict significant damage on coastal areas. = 即将来临的飓风可能会对沿海地区造成重大破坏。

虚拟语气:
If the government imposed this tax, it would inflict great hardship. = 如果政府征收这项税，它将造成巨大的困难。
I wish he wouldn't inflict his bad mood on everyone. = 我希望他不把他的坏情绪强加给每个人。

被动语态:
Great damage was inflicted by the flood. = 大洪水造成了巨大的损失。
Severe injuries were inflicted on the victims of the accident. = 事故的受害者受到了严重的伤害。

疑问句:
What kind of suffering was inflicted on the prisoners? = 对囚犯施加了什么样的痛苦？
What damage did the fire inflict on the building? = 火灾对建筑物造成了什么损坏？

否定句:
The punishment did not inflict any lasting damage. = 惩罚没有造成任何持久性的损害。
They tried to avoid inflicting harm on innocent people. = 他们试图避免伤害无辜的人。


PHRASE->
inflict punishment = 施加惩罚
inflict pain = 造成痛苦
inflict damage = 造成损害
inflict injury = 造成伤害
inflict suffering = 造成痛苦
