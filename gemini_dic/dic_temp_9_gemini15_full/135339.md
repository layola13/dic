nonpenetrable: /ˌnɒnˈpɛnɪtrəbl/ | adj. | 不可穿透的，难以理解的 | Impervious to penetration; incapable of being pierced or entered.  Also describes something difficult to understand or comprehend. | 例句：The fortress walls were nonpenetrable.  堡垒的城墙是不可穿透的。

The professor's lecture was nonpenetrable to most of the students. 教授的讲座对大多数学生来说难以理解。


近义词：impenetrable, impervious, unyielding, opaque


反义词：penetrable, permeable, transparent, clear


词性：形容词
