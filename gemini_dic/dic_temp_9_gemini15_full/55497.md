distractor:/dɪˈstræktər/ | n. | 干扰物；干扰因素；转移注意力的东西 | Something that distracts attention from something else;  a misleading or confusing item, especially in a test or experiment.  | Example: The loud music was a major distractor during the exam.  考试期间大声的音乐是一个主要的干扰因素。

The brightly colored toys served as distractors, preventing the child from focusing on their homework.  鲜艳的玩具起到了分散注意力的作用，使孩子无法专注于作业。 | 近义词：diversion, interruption, distraction | 反义词：focus, aid, help |
