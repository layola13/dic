endways:/ˈɛndweɪz/ | adv. | 末端朝前地；竖着；一头朝前地 |  On end; with the end foremost.  It describes the orientation of something, typically an object, with its end pointing forward or upward. |例句：The log was lying endways in the stream.  木头竖着躺在溪流里。

例句：He pushed the box endways into the corner. 他把箱子竖着推到角落里。


近义词：on end, upright, vertically | 反义词：sideways, horizontally
