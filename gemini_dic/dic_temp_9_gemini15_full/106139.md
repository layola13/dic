knittings:/ˈnɪtɪŋz/ | n. | 编织物；针织品 | The art, process, or occupation of knitting.  Knittings can refer to the items produced through knitting, such as sweaters, scarves, or blankets, or to the act of knitting itself.  |例句：Her knittings were displayed at the local craft fair.她的编织品在当地工艺品博览会上展出。

例句：The shop sells a wide variety of knittings, from simple scarves to intricate sweaters.这家商店出售各种各样的针织品，从简单的围巾到复杂的毛衣。


近义词：knitted items, handiwork, needlework | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with woven fabrics or machine-made items. | 词性:n.


词源:  Derived from the verb "knit," which ultimately comes from Old English *cnittan, meaning "to knot" or "to tie together."  The suffix "-ings" indicates the products or results of the action of knitting.
