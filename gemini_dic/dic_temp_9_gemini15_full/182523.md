rental: /ˈrɛntəl/ | n. | 租赁；租金；出租物 |  The act of renting something; something that is rented; the price paid for renting something.  |  例句：The rental cost of the apartment is high. 这套公寓的租金很高。

例句：He's looking for a rental car for his vacation. 他正在为假期寻找一辆租赁汽车。


近义词：lease, hire, letting | 反义词：purchase, ownership


词源:  From Middle English *rental, from Old French *rental, from rent “rent” + -al.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rental property is available now. = 这套出租物业现在可以租用。
He pays a high rental fee each month. = 他每月支付高额租金。


一般过去时:
The rental agreement expired last month. = 租赁协议上个月到期了。
They rented a car for their road trip. = 他们租了一辆车去公路旅行。


现在完成时:
We have rented this apartment for three years. = 我们已经租了这套公寓三年了。
She has just finished paying off her rental car. = 她刚刚付清了她的租车费用。


过去完成时:
They had already paid the rental deposit before moving in. = 他们搬进去之前已经支付了租金押金。
We had rented a house in the countryside before we found this apartment. = 在我们找到这套公寓之前，我们已经在乡下租了一所房子。


将来时:
We will rent a bigger apartment next year. = 我们明年将租一套更大的公寓。
They will be renting a boat for their fishing trip.  = 他们将为他们的钓鱼之旅租一艘船。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would rent a bigger house. = 如果我有更多钱，我会租一栋更大的房子。
I wish I could afford a luxury rental car. = 我希望我能负担得起一辆豪华租车。


被动语态:
The car was rented by a tourist. = 这辆车被一名游客租走了。
The apartment is being rented out to a young couple. = 这套公寓正在出租给一对年轻夫妇。


疑问句:
Is the rental property pet-friendly? = 这套出租物业允许养宠物吗？
How much is the monthly rental? = 每月租金是多少？


否定句:
They did not renew their rental agreement. = 他们没有续签租赁协议。
We don't want to rent a car; we will use public transportation. = 我们不想租车；我们将使用公共交通工具。
