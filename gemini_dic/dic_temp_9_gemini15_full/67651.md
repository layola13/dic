extrorse:/ˈɛkstrɔːrs/| adj.|外向的，向外的；外倾的|Turning or directed outwards;  referring to anthers that open outwards.  The term is commonly used in botany to describe the direction of anthers (the pollen-bearing part of a stamen) in relation to the flower.|例句：The anthers of this flower are extrorse, opening away from the center of the flower.  这个花的药囊是外向的，向远离花心的方向打开。|The anthers of many lilies are extrorse. 许多百合的药囊是外向的。|近义词：outward, external, everted |反义词：introrse, inward
