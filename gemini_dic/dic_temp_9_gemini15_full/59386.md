earrings:/ˈɪəriŋz/| n. |耳环|Ornamental jewelry worn in the pierced lobes of the ears.  Earrings come in a vast array of styles, materials, and designs, reflecting personal taste and cultural influences.|例句：She wore beautiful diamond earrings to the gala. 她戴着漂亮的钻石耳环参加了盛会。

例句：Those dangling earrings are quite striking. 那些垂坠的耳环非常引人注目。


近义词：earbobs, pendants (if worn on the earlobe)
反义词：None (no direct antonym)
词性：名词

词源:
The word "earrings" comes from the Old English word "ēarehring," a compound word formed from "ēare" (ear) and "hring" (ring).  It literally meant "ear-ring." The modern spelling and pronunciation evolved gradually over time.
