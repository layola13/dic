pressured:/ˈprɛʃərd/| adj.|有压力的，被迫的，受压的|Feeling strong pressure or stress.|The pressured schedule left him exhausted.  他紧张的日程安排让他筋疲力尽。|He felt pressured into accepting the job. 他感到被迫接受这份工作。|近义词：stressed, strained, burdened|反义词：relaxed, calm, unburdened

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels pressured by his demanding boss. 他感到老板要求严格给他带来了压力。
The students are pressured to achieve high grades. 学生们面临着取得高分的压力。

一般过去时:
She felt pressured to conform to societal expectations. 她感到有压力要符合社会期望。
I was pressured into signing the contract. 我被迫签署了合同。

现在完成时:
They have been pressured into making a decision too quickly. 他们一直被迫过快地做出决定。
He has felt pressured since he started his new job. 自从他开始新工作以来，他就一直感到压力很大。

过去完成时:
She had been pressured for weeks before she finally gave in. 在她最终屈服之前，她已经承受了数周的压力。
They had felt pressured to agree to the deal before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前感到被迫同意这项交易。

将来时:
He will be pressured to perform well in the upcoming competition. 他将在即将到来的比赛中承受表现良好的压力。
She will feel pressured to meet the tight deadline. 她会感到有压力要赶上紧张的截止日期。

虚拟语气:
If I weren't pressured, I would take more time to make a decision. 如果我没有压力，我会花更多时间做决定。
I wish I weren't pressured to work overtime every day. 我希望我不必每天加班。

被动语态:
He was pressured into revealing his secret. 他被迫泄露了他的秘密。
The company was pressured by investors to increase profits. 公司受到投资者的压力，要求增加利润。

疑问句:
Are you feeling pressured by your workload? 你是否感到工作量过大给你带来了压力？
Is she pressured to work overtime frequently? 她经常被迫加班吗？

否定句:
He wasn't pressured into making a decision. 他没有被迫做出决定。
She doesn't feel pressured by the upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试没有感到压力。

PHRASE->
under pressure: 在压力下
feel pressured: 感到有压力
put pressure on: 对…施加压力
come under pressure: 受到压力
pressure someone into: 迫使某人做…
