jaw-dropper:/ˈdʒɔːˌdrɒpər/| n. |令人震惊的事物；令人惊叹的事物 |Something so surprising or impressive that it makes one's jaw drop in astonishment.  It can refer to an event, a sight, a piece of news, or an object. | Example: The magician's final trick was a real jaw-dropper. 魔术师的最后戏法真是令人瞠目结舌。 The view from the top of the mountain was a jaw-dropper. 从山顶看到的景色令人惊叹。|近义词：showstopper, stunner, spectacle|反义词：disappointment, letdown
