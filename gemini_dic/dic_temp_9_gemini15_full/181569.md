relapser:/rɪˈlæpsər/| n. |复发者，再次染上恶习者|A person who has relapsed, especially from a disease or addiction.  Someone who has returned to a previous state or behavior, particularly a negative one.  For example, a relapser might be someone who has relapsed into drug addiction after a period of sobriety. |Example:  He was a relapser, having struggled with alcoholism for years. 他是个复发者，多年来一直与酗酒作斗争。  She is a relapser, having gone back to smoking after quitting for six months. 她是个复发者，戒烟六个月后又复吸了。|近义词：recidivist, backslider|反义词：recoverer, abstainer|名词
