overseeding:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdɪŋ/ | n. |过量播种；补播；再播种 |The act of sowing seeds on top of an existing lawn or crop to thicken it or improve its quality.  This is often done to improve the density of a lawn, repair bare patches, or establish a new type of grass. |例句： Overseeding in the fall is a common practice for improving lawn health. 秋天补播是改善草坪健康状况的常见做法。

例句：The golf course underwent overseeding to improve the putting greens. 高尔夫球场进行了补播以改善果岭状况。


近义词：reseeding, topdressing (if including additional material) |反义词：thinning (removing plants) |词性：名词
