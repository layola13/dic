lordosis:/lɔːrˈdoʊsɪs/ | n. |脊柱前凸，腰椎前凸 |An inward curvature of the spine, typically in the lumbar region.  It's a normal curve in the spine, but excessive lordosis is considered a medical condition. |Example:  She developed excessive lordosis after her pregnancy. 她怀孕后出现了过度腰椎前凸。|近义词：swayback, hyperlordosis |反义词：kyphosis
