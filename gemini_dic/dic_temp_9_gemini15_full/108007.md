lassoes: /læˈsoʊz/ | n. |套索；套马索 | A lasso is a long rope with a loop at one end, used for catching animals, especially cattle and horses.  It's typically thrown in a way that the loop ensnares the animal. | The cowboy skillfully threw the lasso, catching the runaway calf.  牛仔熟练地扔出套索，套住了逃跑的小牛。  The rancher used a lasso to secure the wild horse. 牧场主用套索套住了野马。| noose, snare |  (无明确反义词，可考虑“release”, “free”) | 名词
