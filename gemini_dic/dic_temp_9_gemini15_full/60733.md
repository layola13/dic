electrocardiographically:/ˌɪˌlɛktrəʊˌkɑːrdiəˈɡræfɪkəli/| adv.|心电图地，用心电图方法|Adverb describing something done using an electrocardiograph or related to its results.  It indicates a process or observation made through the recording and interpretation of electrical activity of the heart.|The patient's heart rhythm was monitored electrocardiographically throughout the surgery.|近义词：by electrocardiogram, using ECG|反义词：None


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, traditional verb tenses are not applicable.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.

例句1: The doctor diagnosed the arrhythmia electrocardiographically. 医生通过心电图诊断出心律失常。

例句2:  The data was analyzed electrocardiographically to identify any abnormalities. 数据通过心电图分析以识别任何异常。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "electrocardiographically".  It's a relatively technical term used in a specific medical context.
