sculpted:/ˈskʌlptɪd/| adj. | 雕刻的；雕塑的 | Formed or shaped by sculpting.  Having the appearance of being sculpted. |例句：The sculpted figures on the temple were breathtaking.  寺庙上雕刻的人物令人叹为观止。

The sculpted ice formations were stunning.  雕刻的冰雪造型令人惊叹。|近义词：carved, molded, modeled, shaped |反义词：unformed, shapeless
