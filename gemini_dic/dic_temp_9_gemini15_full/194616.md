self-teacher: /ˈsɛlf ˈtiːtʃər/ | n. | 自学者 | A person who teaches themselves a subject or skill, often without formal instruction.  | Example: He's a self-teacher in programming, learning entirely through online resources. 他是一位编程自学者，完全通过在线资源学习。 | 近义词：autodidact | 反义词：student (in a formal setting)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a self-teacher of French. = 他是法语自学者。
She self-teaches herself piano. = 她自学钢琴。


一般过去时:
He was a self-teacher of history. = 他过去是历史自学者。
She self-taught herself guitar. = 她过去自学吉他。


现在完成时:
He has been a self-teacher for many years. = 他已经自学了很多年了。
She has self-taught several languages. = 她自学了几门外语。


过去完成时:
He had been a self-teacher before he enrolled in college. = 在他上大学之前，他一直是自学者。
She had self-taught coding before she got the job. = 在她找到工作之前，她自学了编程。


将来时:
He will be a self-teacher of Japanese. = 他将成为日语自学者。
She will self-teach herself photography. = 她将自学摄影。


虚拟语气:
If I were a self-teacher, I would learn faster. = 如果我是自学者，我会学得更快。
I wish I were a better self-teacher. = 我希望我是一个更好的自学者。


被动语态:
Many skills can be self-taught. = 许多技能都可以自学。
The course can be self-taught. = 这门课程可以自学。


疑问句:
Is he a self-teacher? = 他是自学者吗？
Are you a self-teacher? = 你是自学者吗？


否定句:
He is not a self-teacher; he has a tutor. = 他不是自学者，他有个导师。
She did not self-teach, she took classes. = 她没有自学，她上了课。
