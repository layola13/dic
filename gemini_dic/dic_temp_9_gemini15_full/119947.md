mill:/mɪl/| n. v. |磨坊；工厂；磨碎；碾碎；| A mill is a building in which grain is ground into flour.  A factory producing something. To grind or crush something. |例句：The old water mill is still in operation. 这座古老的水磨坊仍在运行。

The textile mill employed hundreds of workers. 这家纺织厂雇佣了数百名工人。

He milled the grain into flour. 他把谷物磨成面粉。

She milled the spices before adding them to the stew.  她在把香料添加到炖菜之前先磨碎了它们。|近义词：factory, factory, grind, pulverize |反义词：refine, polish
