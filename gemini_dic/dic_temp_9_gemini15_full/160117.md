pommels:/ˈpɒməlz/| n.|  pommelage | 1. (通常复数) 马鞍前部的隆起部分；2. 剑柄的球形或圆形顶部；3. (少量用法) 果实的梗；4.(少量用法)  装饰性球形突出物|英文解析：The pommel is typically a rounded or knob-like projection, often found at the top of a hilt or handle.  In the case of a saddle, it's a raised part at the front. |例句：The knight gripped the pommel of his sword.  =骑士握住剑柄的球形顶部。

The saddle's high pommel offered good support. = 马鞍高耸的前部提供了良好的支撑。|近义词：knob, head, top, boss |反义词：base, bottom
