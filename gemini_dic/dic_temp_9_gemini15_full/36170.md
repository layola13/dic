coffered:/ˈkɒfəd/ | adj. |有壁龛的；有凹槽的；雕花装饰的 | Having a series of recessed panels in a ceiling or wall.  The term often refers to decorative architectural features. |例句：The library ceiling was beautifully coffered, creating a sense of depth and elegance. 图书馆的天花板装饰着精美的壁龛，营造出一种深度和优雅的感觉。

The dining room featured a coffered ceiling with intricate detailing. 餐厅的天花板装饰着复杂的凹槽。

近义词：panelled, recessed, ornamented |反义词：plain, flat, undecorated |词性：形容词


Etymology:  The word "coffered" derives from the Old French word "coffrer," meaning "to make a chest or box." This is because coffered ceilings and walls resemble a series of sunken boxes or coffers.  The architectural feature likely originated in ancient Roman and Greek architecture.
