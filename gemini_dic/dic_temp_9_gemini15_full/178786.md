recuperated:/rɪˈkuːpəreɪtɪd/| v.|恢复健康；复原；重新获得|To regain or recover something lost or taken away, especially health or strength.|例句：After a week in bed, he finally recuperated.  一周卧床休息后，他终于恢复了健康。|例句：The company recuperated its losses within a year. 公司在一年内收回了损失。|近义词：recover, regain, convalesce|反义词：deteriorate, worsen|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recuperates quickly after illness. = 他生病后恢复得很快。
She recuperates her energy after a long day. = 她长久工作一天后恢复精力。

一般过去时:
He recuperated quickly after his surgery. = 他手术后恢复得很快。
The economy recuperated after the recession. = 经济在衰退后复苏了。

现在完成时:
She has recuperated well from her injury. = 她从伤病中恢复得很好。
The company has recuperated its losses. = 公司已经收回了损失。

过去完成时:
He had recuperated significantly before the race. = 他在比赛前已经恢复得相当不错了。
The market had recuperated somewhat before the crash. = 市场在崩溃前已有所恢复。

将来时:
He will recuperate fully within a few weeks. = 他几周内就能完全康复。
The economy is expected to recuperate next year. = 预计经济将在明年复苏。

虚拟语气:
If he had recuperated sooner, he could have competed. = 如果他恢复得早一点，他就能参赛了。
I wish I had recuperated more quickly after the operation. = 我希望我手术后恢复得更快一些。

被动语态:
His strength was slowly recuperated. = 他的体力慢慢恢复了。
The lost documents were never recuperated. = 丢失的文件从未找回。

疑问句:
Has he recuperated yet? = 他恢复了吗？
Will the company recuperate from this setback? = 公司能从这次挫折中恢复过来吗？

否定句:
He did not recuperate as quickly as expected. = 他恢复的速度没有预期的快。
The company has not yet recuperated its losses. = 公司尚未收回损失。


PHRASE->
recuperated from an illness = 从疾病中恢复
recuperated his strength = 恢复了他的体力
recuperated his losses = 弥补了他的损失
fully recuperated = 完全康复
rapidly recuperated = 快速恢复


词源:
From Medieval Latin recuperatus, past participle of recuperare "to recover, regain," from re- "again" + curare "to care for, attend to".  The ultimate origin is in the Proto-Indo-European root *kwer- "to protect, care for".
