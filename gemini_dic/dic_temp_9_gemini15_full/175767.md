rarebit:/ˈrɛəbɪt/| n. |奶酪烤面包|A Welsh rarebit is a hot open sandwich consisting of melted cheese (usually Cheddar) on toasted bread, often with additions such as mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and beer.  It's a classic comfort food. |Example:  He ordered a Welsh rarebit for dinner. 他晚餐点了一份威尔士奶酪烤面包。 |近义词: cheese toast, melted cheese sandwich|反义词:  (no direct antonym, but perhaps a cold sandwich)|词性:n.
