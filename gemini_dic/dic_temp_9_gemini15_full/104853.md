keelrake: /ˈkiːlreɪk/ | n. |龙骨耙, 船底耙 | A keelrake is a tool used to clean the bottom of a ship's hull, particularly the keel.  It's typically a long, heavy rake with strong tines designed to remove barnacles, seaweed, and other marine growth. | Example: The divers used a keelrake to remove the stubborn barnacles from the ship's keel.  潜水员用龙骨耙清除船底顽固的藤壶。|近义词:  hull cleaner, bottom scraper | 反义词: N/A
