cooling:/ˈkuːlɪŋ/| n. adj. v.|冷却；降温；凉爽的|The process of reducing temperature.  It can refer to a noun (the act of cooling), an adjective (describing something that cools), or a verb (the act of making something cool).|例句：The cooling system in my car is broken. 我的车上的冷却系统坏了。

The air conditioning provides cooling during the summer heat. 空调在夏季高温下提供降温。


近义词：refrigeration, chilling, refreshing|反义词：heating, warming
