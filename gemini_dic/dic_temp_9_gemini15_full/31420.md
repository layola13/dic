chariot:/ˈtʃæriət/| n.|战车，轻便马车|A two-wheeled horse-drawn vehicle, used in ancient warfare and races.  |例句：The ancient Romans used chariots in battle. 古罗马人在战斗中使用战车。

例句：The chariot race was a thrilling spectacle.  战车比赛是一场激动人心的景象。


近义词：carriage, cart, wagon|反义词：None specifically.
