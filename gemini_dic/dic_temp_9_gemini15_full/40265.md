continu:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ | v. |继续，延续 | To proceed without interruption; to go on. |例句：The meeting continued for another hour. 会议又持续了一个小时。|例句：Please continue reading where you left off. 请从你上次读到的地方继续读下去。|近义词：proceed, resume, persist |反义词：stop, cease, end |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He continues to work hard. = 他继续努力工作。
She continues her studies. = 她继续她的学业。

一般过去时:
The rain continued throughout the night. = 雨整夜不停地下着。
He continued to speak even after being interrupted. = 即使被打扰，他仍然继续讲话。

现在完成时:
They have continued to support us. = 他们一直支持我们。
She has continued to improve her skills. = 她一直在提高她的技能。

过去完成时:
He had continued working until he was exhausted. = 他一直工作到筋疲力尽。
The problem had continued for many years. = 这个问题已经持续了很多年。

将来时:
We will continue our journey tomorrow. = 我们明天将继续我们的旅程。
The project will continue into next year. = 这个项目将持续到明年。

虚拟语气:
If he continued to study, he would succeed. = 如果他继续学习，他就会成功。
I wish he continued his efforts. = 我希望他继续努力。

被动语态:
The work will be continued next week. = 工作将在下周继续进行。
The meeting was continued after a short break. = 短暂休息后，会议继续进行。

疑问句:
Will you continue to help me? = 你会继续帮助我吗？
Does the film continue after the intermission? = 电影中场休息后还会继续吗？

否定句:
He did not continue the conversation. = 他没有继续谈话。
They will not continue with the project.  = 他们不会继续进行这个项目。


PHRASE->
continue on = 继续前进
continue with = 继续做某事
continue to = 继续做某事
continue doing = 继续做某事
continue reading = 继续阅读
