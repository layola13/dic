post-Aristotelian: /ˌpoʊstˌærɪstəˈtiːliən/ | adj. | 亚里士多德之后的；后亚里士多德的 |  Referring to the philosophical period and thought following the work of Aristotle (384-322 BC). This era saw a shift away from Aristotelian thought, encompassing various schools and perspectives that challenged, expanded upon, or reacted against his ideas.  | Example: Post-Aristotelian philosophy saw the rise of skepticism and Neoplatonism.  亚里士多德之后的哲学见证了怀疑论和新柏拉图主义的兴起。| 近义词: postclassical, later | 反义词: Aristotelian
