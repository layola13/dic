north-east: /ˌnɔːrθˈiːst/ | adj., n., adv. | 东北，东北方的 |  Describes a direction, location, or wind that is between north and east.  As a noun, it refers to the northeast region or direction. As an adjective, it modifies nouns to indicate location or direction. As an adverb, it modifies verbs to indicate direction. | Example:  The wind was blowing from the north-east.  东北风在吹。 The north-east of England is known for its beautiful coastline. 英格兰东北部以其美丽的海岸线而闻名。 We travelled north-east towards the mountains. 我们向东北方向，朝着山的方向行驶。| 近义词： northeastern | 反义词：south-west
