/mæts/ | n. | 垫子；席子 | A piece of thick, heavy material used as a floor covering, or for other purposes such as protecting a surface. |  例句：The cat sat on the mat.  猫坐在垫子上。


例句：I need a new mat for my kitchen door. 我需要一块新的门垫。


近义词：rug, carpet, doormat


反义词：无


词性：名词
