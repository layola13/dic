ground-plans:/ˈɡraʊnd plænz/| n. |平面图，底层平面图 |A ground plan is a drawing showing the arrangement of rooms, etc., in a building, viewed from above.| 例句：The architect presented the ground plan for the new house. 建筑师展示了新房子的平面图。

The ground plan of the museum was confusing. 博物馆的平面图令人费解。


近义词：floor plan, layout |反义词：elevation (立面图)
