multinucleate:/ˌmʌltiˈnjuːkliɪt/| adj. |多核的，多细胞核的 |Having or containing more than one nucleus.  Multinucleate cells are common in certain tissues and organisms, often resulting from cell fusion or incomplete cytokinesis.  | Example:  The skeletal muscle fibers are multinucleate. 骨骼肌纤维是多核的。|近义词：polynuclear, multinucleated |反义词：uninucleate, mononucleate
