pseudobulbous:/ˌsuːdəʊˈbʌlbəs/| adj.|假球茎的；具假球茎的|Having or resembling a pseudobulb.  A pseudobulb is a thickened stem, often swollen with stored water, found in some orchids and other plants. It serves as a water and nutrient reserve, enabling the plant to survive dry periods.|例句：Many orchid species are pseudobulbous, allowing them to withstand drought conditions.  许多兰花物种都是假球茎的，这使它们能够承受干旱条件。|近义词：bulbous (but less specific)|反义词：non-pseudobulbous
