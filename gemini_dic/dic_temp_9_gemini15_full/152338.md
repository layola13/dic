paulownia:/pɔːˈloʊniə/|/pɔːˈləʊniə/| n. |泡桐树；皇后木|Paulownia is a genus of seven species of deciduous trees native to central and western China.|The paulownia tree is known for its large, heart-shaped leaves and beautiful, fragrant flowers.|例句：The park is famous for its rows of majestic paulownia trees. 公园以其成排的宏伟泡桐树而闻名。|例句：The vibrant purple flowers of the paulownia tree are a stunning sight in spring. 泡桐树鲜艳的紫色花朵在春天是一道令人惊叹的景象。|近义词：princess tree, empress tree|反义词：无
