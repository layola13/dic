coarsening: /ˈkɔːrsənɪŋ/ | n. | 粗糙化；变粗糙；变粗劣 | The process of becoming coarser or rougher in texture, quality, or appearance.  It can refer to a gradual decline in quality or refinement. |

例句：

The coarsening of the fabric made it less comfortable to wear.  =  织物的粗糙化使其穿着起来不太舒服。

The coarsening of the image is due to low resolution. = 图像的粗糙化是由于低分辨率造成的。


近义词：roughening, deterioration, degradation

反义词：refinement, smoothing, improvement

词性：名词
