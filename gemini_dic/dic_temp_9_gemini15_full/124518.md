mountain:/ˈmaʊntən/| n.|山，山脉|A large natural elevation of the earth's surface rising abruptly from the surrounding level; a large mass of rock, earth, etc., rising conspicuously above the surrounding area.|例句：The climbers reached the top of the mountain. 登山者到达了山顶。

例句：The mountain range stretched as far as the eye could see. 山脉绵延到视线所及之处。

近义词：hill, peak, range|反义词：valley, plain


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mountain stands tall. = 这座山巍峨耸立。
Mountains are formed by geological processes. = 山脉是由地质过程形成的。

一般过去时:
We climbed the mountain yesterday. = 我们昨天爬了那座山。
They saw a snow-capped mountain in the distance. = 他们看到远处有一座雪山。

现在完成时:
I have climbed that mountain twice. = 我已经爬过那座山两次了。
She has never seen such high mountains before. = 她以前从未见过这么高的山。

过去完成时:
They had already reached the summit before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经到达山顶了。
We had climbed for hours before we found a suitable place to rest. = 我们爬了好几个小时才找到一个合适的地方休息。

将来时:
We will climb Mount Everest next year. = 我们明年将攀登珠穆朗玛峰。
They will be hiking in the mountains next week. = 他们下周将在山区远足。

虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I could fly over the mountain. = 如果我是一只鸟，我就能飞过这座山。
I wish I could see the mountain from here. = 我希望我能从这里看到那座山。

被动语态:
The mountain was formed millions of years ago. = 这座山是在几百万年前形成的。
The path up the mountain is rarely used, so it is often overgrown. = 通往山顶的路很少有人使用，所以经常杂草丛生。

疑问句:
Is that mountain visible from here? = 从这里能看到那座山吗？
Which mountain is the highest in the region? = 这个地区哪座山最高？

否定句:
That is not a mountain, it's a hill. = 那不是山，那是小山。
We did not reach the summit of the mountain. = 我们没有到达山顶。
