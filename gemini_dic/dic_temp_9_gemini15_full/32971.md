chore:/tʃɔːr/| n. |家务杂事；苦差事 | a routine task, especially a household one |例句：Doing the dishes is a daily chore.  洗碗是每天的家务杂事。|例句：He considers fixing the car a real chore. 他认为修理汽车是一件苦差事。|近义词：task, job, errand |反义词：pleasure, recreation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I do the chores every Saturday. = 我每个星期六做家务。
She cleans the house as her daily chore. = 她每天打扫房子作为家务。


一般过去时:
I did the chores yesterday. = 我昨天做了家务。
He finished all his chores before dinner. = 他在晚饭前完成了所有家务。


现在完成时:
I have already done the chores. = 我已经做完家务了。
She has completed most of her chores for the week. = 她已经完成了本周的大部分家务。


过去完成时:
I had finished my chores before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经做完家务了。
She had done all the chores before going out. = 她出去之前已经完成了所有家务。


将来时:
I will do the chores tomorrow. = 我明天要做家务。
She will help with the chores this weekend. = 她这个周末会帮忙做家务。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would do all the chores myself. = 如果我有更多时间，我会自己做所有家务。
I wish I didn't have so many chores to do. = 我希望我没有那么多家务要做。


被动语态:
The chores were done quickly. = 家务很快就做完了。
The house chores are being done by the maid. = 家务活正在由女佣做。


疑问句:
Have you done the chores yet? = 你做完家务了吗？
What chores need to be done today? = 今天需要做什么家务？


否定句:
I didn't do the chores today. = 我今天没有做家务。
She hasn't finished her chores yet. = 她还没做完家务。


PHRASE->
