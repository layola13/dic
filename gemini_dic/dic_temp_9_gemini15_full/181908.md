remainer:/rɪˈmeɪnər/| n. | 剩余物；留下来的人；(英国)脱欧留欧派 | A person who remained in or supported remaining in the European Union.  Those who voted to remain in the EU are often called Remainers.  |The remainder of the cake was eaten by the children.  剩下的蛋糕被孩子们吃掉了。|  The Remainers campaigned vigorously against Brexit.  留欧派积极地反对脱欧。 | supporter, advocate | leaver (反义词，指脱欧派)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The remainder of the food remains in the fridge. = 剩下的食物留在冰箱里。
Many people remain undecided. = 许多人仍然犹豫不决。


一般过去时:
The majority of the students remained silent. = 大多数学生保持沉默。
He remained in his office until late. = 他一直待在办公室直到很晚。


现在完成时:
She has remained loyal to her principles. = 她一直忠于自己的原则。
The problem has remained unsolved. = 问题仍然没有解决。


过去完成时:
After everyone else had left, he remained. = 其他人都离开后，他留了下来。
The building had remained untouched for years. = 这座建筑多年来一直未被触动。


将来时:
I will remain here until you return. = 我会一直待在这里直到你回来。
The situation will remain unchanged. = 情况将保持不变。


虚拟语气:
If he remained silent, the situation would be better. = 如果他保持沉默，情况会更好。
I wish I had remained at home. = 我真希望我待在家里。


被动语态:
The decision remained unchanged. = 决定保持不变。(注意remain本身是主动词，少用被动)
The issue was left to remain unresolved. = 这个问题被搁置未解决。


疑问句:
Will you remain in the country? = 你会留在国内吗？
Does this problem remain unsolved? = 这个问题仍然没有解决吗？


否定句:
He did not remain long. = 他没有待多久。
The situation did not remain stable. = 情况没有保持稳定。


PHRASE->

the remainder of = ...的剩余部分
remain silent = 保持沉默
remain calm = 保持冷静
remain seated = 保持坐姿
remain to be seen = 有待观察
