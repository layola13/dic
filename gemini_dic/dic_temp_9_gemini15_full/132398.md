nondemonstratively: /ˌnɒnˌdɛmənˈstreɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 不炫耀地；不引人注目地 |  Without ostentation or display; subtly.  It describes actions or behavior that are done quietly and without drawing attention to themselves. |

例句：He accepted the award nondemonstratively, simply nodding his head. 他不露声色地接受了奖项，只是点了点头。

例句：She quietly and nondemonstratively helped those in need. 她默默地、不引人注目地帮助那些需要帮助的人。


近义词：subtly, unobtrusively, inconspicuously, quietly, modestly

反义词：ostentatiously, demonstratively, conspicuously, flamboyantly


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".
* **demonstrative**: adjective meaning "showing feelings openly; expressive".  It comes from the verb "demonstrate," which ultimately derives from Latin *demonstrare* ("to point out," "to show").

用法说明:

该词主要用作副词，修饰动词，形容词或整个句子，描述一种不张扬，不炫耀的行为方式。
