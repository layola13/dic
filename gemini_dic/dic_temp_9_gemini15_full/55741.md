diver:/ˈdaɪvər/ | n. |潜水员，跳水运动员| A person who dives, especially underwater or from a height.  |例句：The diver found a sunken treasure chest. 潜水员找到一个沉没的宝箱。|例句：The diver performed a graceful dive from the high board. 跳水运动员从高台上表演了一个优美的跳水动作。|近义词：submariner, swimmer, plunger|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who stays on land.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dives every weekend. = 他每个周末都潜水。
She dives into the pool gracefully. = 她优雅地跳入游泳池。

一般过去时:
They dived into the ocean yesterday. = 他们昨天潜入大海。
He dived from the ten-meter platform. = 他从十米高的跳台上跳水。

现在完成时:
I have dived in the Great Barrier Reef. = 我曾在澳大利亚大堡礁潜水。
She has never dived before. = 她以前从未潜水过。

过去完成时:
He had dived many times before he became a professional. = 在他成为职业选手之前，他已经潜过很多次了。
They had dived to great depths before encountering the whale. = 在遇到鲸鱼之前，他们已经潜到了很深的地方。

将来时:
We will dive in the Caribbean next year. = 我们明年将在加勒比海潜水。
She will dive from the highest platform. = 她将从最高的跳台上跳水。

虚拟语气:
If I were a diver, I would explore the ocean depths. = 如果我是一个潜水员，我将探索海洋深处。
I wish I could dive with dolphins. = 我希望我能和海豚一起潜水。

被动语态:
The sunken ship was dived on by many divers. = 那艘沉船被许多潜水员潜水探查过。
The coral reef should be dived on responsibly. = 珊瑚礁应该负责任地进行潜水探查。

疑问句:
Do you dive often? = 你经常潜水吗？
Has he ever dived before? = 他以前潜水过吗？

否定句:
I don't dive. = 我不潜水。
She didn't dive yesterday. = 她昨天没有潜水。

词源:
The word "diver" comes from the verb "dive," which ultimately derives from the Old English word "dūfan," meaning "to dip" or "to plunge."  The suffix "-er" is an agent noun suffix indicating a person who performs the action.
