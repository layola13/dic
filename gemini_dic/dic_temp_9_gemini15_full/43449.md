/kreɪz/ | n. | 裂缝；裂痕；疯癫；脾气暴躁 | A crase is a crack or fissure, especially in a surface. It can also refer to a state of madness or a bad temper.  |例句：There was a crase in the vase.  花瓶上有一道裂缝。  The old woman was in a crase.  老太太脾气暴躁。 |近义词：crack, fissure, rift, frenzy, fit |反义词：smoothness, calmness, sanity
