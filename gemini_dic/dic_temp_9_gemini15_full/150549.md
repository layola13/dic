papa:/pəˈpɑː/ | n. |爸爸，爹地 | A term of endearment for father.  It is a common and informal way to address one's father or a similar paternal figure. | Example: "Papa, can I have some more ice cream?"  爸爸，我能再吃点冰淇淋吗？|近义词：dad, daddy, father, pop, pa |反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My papa cooks dinner every night. = 我爸爸每晚都做饭。
Papa reads stories to me before bed. = 爸爸睡前给我读故事。

一般过去时:
Papa took me to the park yesterday. = 爸爸昨天带我去公园了。
Papa helped me with my homework last night. = 爸爸昨晚帮我做了作业。

现在完成时:
Papa has made many delicious meals for us. = 爸爸为我们做了很多美味的食物。
Papa has always been supportive of my dreams. = 爸爸一直都很支持我的梦想。

过去完成时:
Papa had already left for work when I woke up. = 我醒来时爸爸已经去上班了。
Papa had prepared breakfast before we got up. = 我们起床前爸爸已经准备好了早餐。

将来时:
Papa will take us to the beach next weekend. = 爸爸下周末会带我们去海滩。
Papa will help me with my science project tomorrow. = 爸爸明天会帮我做科学作业。

虚拟语气:
If Papa were here, he would help us. = 如果爸爸在这里，他会帮助我们。
I wish Papa could come to my graduation. = 我希望爸爸能来参加我的毕业典礼。

被动语态:
Dinner is often cooked by Papa. = 晚饭通常是由爸爸做的。
Stories are usually read to me by Papa. = 故事通常是由爸爸给我读的。

疑问句:
Has Papa finished work yet? = 爸爸下班了吗？
Where is Papa going? = 爸爸去哪儿？

否定句:
Papa doesn't work on weekends. = 爸爸周末不上班。
Papa didn't go to the meeting. = 爸爸没有去参加会议。
