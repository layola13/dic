词源：

The word "repostulate" is formed from the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and the verb "postulate."  "Postulate" comes from the Latin word "postulatum," a past participle of "postulare," meaning "to demand," "to request," or "to assume." In its modern usage, "postulate" means to assume or suggest the existence of something as a basis for reasoning or argument.
