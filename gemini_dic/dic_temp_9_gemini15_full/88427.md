hispanicists:/ˌhɪspænɪˈsɪsts/| n. |西班牙语文学研究者；西班牙语专家| Scholars specializing in Hispanic studies, encompassing literature, language, and culture of Spain, Latin America, and other Spanish-speaking regions.  They often conduct research, teach, and translate works related to Hispanic cultures. | Example: Many hispanicists are currently researching the evolution of the Spanish language in different regions. 许多西班牙语文学研究者目前正在研究西班牙语在不同地区的演变。|近义词： Hispanophiles, specialists in Hispanic studies |反义词：无
