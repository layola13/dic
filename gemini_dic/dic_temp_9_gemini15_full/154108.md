periecians:/pɛriˈeɪʃənz/| n.|周边居民，邻国居民|People who live in the surrounding area or neighboring countries.  The term often implies a less direct relationship than "neighbors" and can refer to inhabitants of a wider region.|例句：The periecians of Athens were granted certain rights but lacked full citizenship. 雅典的周边居民享有某些权利，但缺乏完全的公民权。|近义词：neighbors, surrounding inhabitants, border residents|反义词：citizens (in the context of a specific polity)
