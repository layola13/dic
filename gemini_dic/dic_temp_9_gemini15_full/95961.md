inculcate:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪt/ | vt. |灌输；谆谆教诲 |To inculcate means to teach someone an attitude, idea, or habit by persistent repetition.  It suggests a forceful or persistent effort to instill something into someone's mind. |The teacher inculcated in her students the importance of hard work.  老师向她的学生灌输了努力工作的重要性。|  He tried to inculcate a sense of responsibility in his son. 他试图在他儿子心中灌输责任感。| instill, implant, impress |  - | v.
