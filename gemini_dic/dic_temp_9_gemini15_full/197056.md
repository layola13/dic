shamanic:/ʃəˈmænɪk/| adj.|萨满教的；萨满巫师的|Relating to or characteristic of shamanism.  The term often describes practices, rituals, or beliefs associated with shamanic traditions.|例句：The shamanic journey involved a deep trance state. 萨满之旅涉及到一种深度恍惚状态。

例句：Her paintings depicted shamanic symbols and imagery. 她的画描绘了萨满教的符号和意象。


近义词：spiritual, mystical, occult

反义词：scientific, rational, secular
