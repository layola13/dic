pro-cathedral:/ˌproʊˈkæθɪdrəl/ | n. |主教座堂副堂，代主教座堂 | A pro-cathedral is a church that serves as a temporary or secondary cathedral, often in a diocese that doesn't yet have a full cathedral.  It usually holds the same liturgical significance as a cathedral but lacks the official status. | The pro-cathedral hosted the bishop's annual Christmas Eve mass.  主教座堂副堂举办了主教每年的平安夜弥撒。 | cathedral, church |  — |
