import:/ɪmˈpɔːrt/| vt.| 导入，引入，进口 | To bring (goods or services) into a country from abroad for sale.  |例句：The company imports high-quality coffee beans from Colombia. 这家公司从哥伦比亚进口优质咖啡豆。|例句：They import a wide range of products from China. 他们从中国进口各种各样的产品。|近义词：bring in, introduce,  |反义词：export


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We import various fruits from different countries. = 我们从不同的国家进口各种水果。
The company imports raw materials for its production. = 这家公司进口生产所需的原材料。


一般过去时:
They imported a large quantity of textiles last year. = 他们去年进口了大量的纺织品。
The country imported less oil this year than last year. = 今年该国进口的石油比去年少。


现在完成时:
We have imported over 1000 tons of steel this year. = 今年我们已经进口了超过1000吨的钢铁。
The government has recently imported new medical equipment. = 政府最近进口了新的医疗设备。


过去完成时:
Before the trade war, they had imported most of their goods from that country. = 在贸易战之前，他们的大部分商品都从那个国家进口。
The company had imported significant amounts of timber prior to the ban. = 在禁令之前，该公司已经进口了大量的木材。


将来时:
We will import more sustainable products next year. = 我们明年将进口更多可持续产品。
The company plans to import advanced technology from Europe. = 公司计划从欧洲进口先进技术。


虚拟语气:
If we imported more locally, we would support our farmers. = 如果我们进口更多本地产品，我们将支持我们的农民。
I wish we had imported that technology sooner. = 我希望我们早点引进这项技术。


被动语态:
A large quantity of goods is imported every day. = 每天都有大量的货物被进口。
The new software was imported successfully into our system. = 新软件已成功导入到我们的系统中。


疑问句:
Do you import any goods from overseas? = 你从海外进口任何商品吗？
Does the company import components from other countries? = 这家公司从其他国家进口零部件吗？


否定句:
We don't import products that are made using child labor. = 我们不进口使用童工制造的产品。
The company doesn't import that specific type of machine anymore. = 公司不再进口那种特定类型的机器。


PHRASE->
import data = 导入数据
import module = 导入模块 (编程)
import license = 进口许可证
import duty = 进口税
import quota = 进口配额
