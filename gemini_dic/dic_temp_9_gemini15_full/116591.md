matriculants:/məˈtrɪkjʊlənts/| n. |入学新生；注册者|People who have been admitted to a school or college.  They are usually new students who have successfully completed the matriculation process.|The matriculants were excited to begin their university studies.|freshmen, registrants|dropouts, non-students|名词
