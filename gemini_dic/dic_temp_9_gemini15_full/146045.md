overdriving:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪvɪŋ/ | v. |超速驾驶；过度使用；强求；| Driving at a speed exceeding the legal limit or safe speed.  It can also refer to overuse or forcing something beyond its capacity. |例句：He was overdriving on the highway and got a speeding ticket. 他在高速公路上超速驾驶，被开了一张超速罚单。

例句：The company is overdriving its employees to meet the deadline. 公司为了赶截止日期，过度压榨员工。


近义词：speeding, exceeding, overworking, straining


反义词：underspeeding, underworking
