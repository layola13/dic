existentially:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəli/ | adv. | 存在地；实存地；从存在角度来看 |  Adverb describing something relating to existence or the nature of existence. It emphasizes the fundamental aspects of being and living.  |例句：He questioned the meaning of life existentially. 他从存在主义的角度质疑生命的意义。

例句：The artist explored existential themes in her work.  这位艺术家在她作品中探讨了存在主义主题。


近义词： fundamentally, ontologically, philosophically


反义词： superficially, empirically, practically


词性：副词
