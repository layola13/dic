midnight:/ˈmɪdnaɪt/| n.|午夜，子夜|The time when it is twelve o'clock at night.  It marks the end of one day and the beginning of the next.|例句：We'll meet at midnight. 我们将在午夜见面。|例句：The clock struck midnight.  钟敲响了午夜。|近义词：mid-night, twelve o'clock at night|反义词：noon, midday
