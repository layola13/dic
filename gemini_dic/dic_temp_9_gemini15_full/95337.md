incensing: /ɪnˈsɛnsɪŋ/ | v. | 使生气；使愤怒；焚香 | The constant noise was incensing him.  | to make someone very angry | infuriating, provoking, angering | pleasing, calming | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smell of incense is incensing the neighbor. = 檀香的味道正在惹恼邻居。
His behavior incenses his parents daily. = 他的行为每天都惹恼他的父母。


一般过去时:
The news incensed the public. = 这则新闻激怒了公众。
Her rudeness incensed me. = 她的粗鲁让我很生气。


现在完成时:
The constant interruptions have incensed me. =  不断的打断让我很生气。
His actions have incensed many people. = 他的行为激怒了许多人。


过去完成时:
They had been incensed by his lies. = 他们被他谎言激怒了。
I had been incensed by her betrayal. = 我已经被她的背叛激怒了。


将来时:
That news will incense many voters. = 那则新闻将会激怒许多选民。
His arrogance will incense everyone. = 他的傲慢将会激怒所有人。


虚拟语气:
If he knew what she said, he would be incensed. = 如果他知道她说了什么，他会很生气。
I wish I hadn't done that; it would have incensed him. = 我真希望我没有那样做；那会惹恼他的。


被动语态:
The public was incensed by the government's decision. = 公众被政府的决定激怒了。
He was incensed by their lack of respect. = 他被他们的不尊重激怒了。


疑问句:
Does this situation incense you? = 这种情况让你生气吗？
What incenses you the most? = 什么事最让你生气？


否定句:
The news didn't incense her. = 这条新闻并没有让她生气。
