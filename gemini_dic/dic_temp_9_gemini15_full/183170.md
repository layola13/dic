repractice:/ˌriːˈpræktɪs/ | v. |再练习，重新练习 |To practice again; to practice something again.  |例句：I need to repractice the piano piece before the concert. 我需要在音乐会之前重新练习这首钢琴曲。

例句：She decided to repractice her presentation after receiving feedback.  她收到反馈后决定重新练习她的演示文稿。


近义词：rehearse, review, revise | 反义词：neglect, abandon
