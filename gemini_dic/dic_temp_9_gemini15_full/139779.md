octane:/ˈɒkteɪn/ /ˈɑːkteɪn/ | n. |辛烷；辛烷值|A saturated hydrocarbon with eight carbon atoms; the main constituent of gasoline.  It is also a measure of the anti-knock qualities of gasoline. |例句：The octane rating of this gasoline is 95. 这汽油的辛烷值为95。|例句：High-octane fuel is recommended for this car.  这款车建议使用高辛烷值燃油。|近义词：isooctane|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑低辛烷值燃料)|名词
