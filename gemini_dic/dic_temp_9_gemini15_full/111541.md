lob:/lɒb/| /lɑːb/ | vt. n. |轻抛，轻击；（高尔夫球）轻击；大球；裂片|To throw or hit something gently, especially a ball.|例句：He lobbed the ball over the net. 他轻轻地将球抛过网。|例句：The fisherman lobbed the bait into the water. 渔夫轻轻地把鱼饵抛入水中。|近义词：toss, pitch, hurl|反义词：slam, throw hard


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He lobs the ball gently. = 他轻轻地抛球。
She lobs the letter into the mailbox. = 她把信轻轻地投入邮箱。

一般过去时:
He lobbed the ball over the fence. = 他把球抛过了栅栏。
She lobbed a question to the speaker. = 她向演讲者提了一个问题。

现在完成时:
He has lobbed the ball many times. = 他已经抛过很多次球了。
She has lobbed several suggestions during the meeting. = 她在会议期间提出了好几个建议。

过去完成时:
He had lobbed the ball before the game started. = 在比赛开始前，他已经抛过球了。
She had lobbed the idea to her boss before the presentation. = 在演示之前，她已经向老板提出了这个主意。

将来时:
He will lob the ball to his teammate. = 他将把球抛给他的队友。
She will lob a grenade at the enemy position. = 她将向敌军阵地投掷手榴弹。

虚拟语气:
If he lobbed the ball harder, he might have won the game. = 如果他更用力地抛球，他可能就赢了比赛。
If she had lobbed the bomb earlier, the building would have collapsed. = 如果她早点投掷炸弹，楼房就会倒塌了。

被动语态:
The ball was lobbed over the wall. = 球被抛过了墙。
The question was lobbed at the panel. = 这个问题被抛给了专家小组。

疑问句:
Did he lob the ball successfully? = 他成功地抛球了吗？
Will she lob the package over the fence? = 她会把包裹抛过栅栏吗？

否定句:
He didn't lob the ball far enough. = 他没有把球抛得足够远。
She didn't lob the ball accurately. = 她没有准确地抛球。
