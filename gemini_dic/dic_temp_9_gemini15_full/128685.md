nictitation:/ˌnɪktɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|瞬膜, 第三眼睑的眨眼|The involuntary or voluntary closing and opening of the nictitating membrane.  Nictitation is a reflex action protecting the eye from bright light or foreign bodies.|例句：The cat exhibited rapid nictitation in response to the bright light.  猫对强光做出了迅速的瞬膜眨眼反应。


The slow, blinking movement of the third eyelid is called nictitation.|例句：Birds often use nictitation to keep their eyes moist and clean while flying. 鸟类经常在飞行时利用瞬膜眨眼来保持眼睛湿润和清洁。|近义词：blinking, winking (in the context of the third eyelid)|反义词：None directly opposite, but  "keeping the eyes wide open" could be considered a contrast.|名词
