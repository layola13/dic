sequined:/ˈsiːkwɪnd/| adj.|缀满亮片的；饰有亮片的|Covered with sequins.  Sequined clothing is often worn for special occasions.|The sequined dress shimmered under the stage lights.  = 那件缀满亮片的礼服在舞台灯光下闪闪发光。|glittery, sparkling|plain, unadorned

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress is sequined. = 这件衣服是缀满亮片的。
Her shoes are sequined and sparkly. = 她的鞋子是缀满亮片且闪闪发光的。

一般过去时:
The dancer wore a sequined costume. = 舞者穿着一套缀满亮片的服装。
They decorated the cake with sequined frosting. = 他们用缀满亮片的糖霜装饰蛋糕。

现在完成时:
She has worn that sequined top to several parties. = 她已经把那件缀满亮片的衬衫穿去好几个派对了。
I have never seen such a beautifully sequined gown. = 我从未见过如此美丽的缀满亮片的礼服。

过去完成时:
She had already put on her sequined dress before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经穿上了她的缀满亮片的礼服。
They had carefully applied the sequins to the fabric. = 他们已经小心地将亮片粘贴到织物上。

将来时:
She will wear a sequined outfit to the New Year's Eve party. = 她将在新年派对上穿一件缀满亮片的服装。
They will decorate the room with sequined streamers. = 他们将用缀满亮片的彩带装饰房间。

虚拟语气:
If I had a sequined dress, I would wear it to the party. = 如果我有一件缀满亮片的礼服，我会穿它去参加派对。
I wish I had a sequined bag to match my shoes. = 我希望我有一个与我的鞋子相配的缀满亮片的包包。

被动语态:
The curtains were sequined by hand. = 窗帘是手工缝上亮片的。
The costumes are being sequined for the upcoming show. = 服装正在为即将到来的演出缝上亮片。

疑问句:
Is your jacket sequined? = 你的夹克是缀满亮片的？
Are those shoes sequined or just sparkly? = 那些鞋子是缀满亮片的还是只是闪闪发光的？

否定句:
The shirt is not sequined; it's just plain. = 这件衬衫不是缀满亮片的；它只是普通的。
Her simple dress was not sequined. = 她简单的连衣裙没有缀满亮片。

sequined handbag = 缀满亮片的包包

词源:
From sequin + -ed.  Sequin itself comes from the Italian word *sequina*, a diminutive of *sega*, meaning "saw".  This refers to the shape of early sequins.
