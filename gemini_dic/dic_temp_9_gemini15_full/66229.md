excitingly:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋli/ | adv. |令人兴奋地，激动地 |  The adverb "excitingly" describes something done in an exciting manner. It indicates a state of excitement or exhilaration.  |例句：He spoke excitingly about his adventures in the Amazon. 他兴奋地讲述了他亚马逊河探险的经历。
The news was excitingly revealed at the press conference.  这个消息在新闻发布会上激动人心地公布了。|近义词：thrillingly, enthusiastically, passionately |反义词：dully, boringly, apathetically |词性：副词
