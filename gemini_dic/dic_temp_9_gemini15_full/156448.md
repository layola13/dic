piacular:/ˈpaɪəkjuːlər/| adj.|赎罪的；涤罪的；祈求宽恕的| Relating to expiation or atonement for sin or wrongdoing.  It suggests an act or offering designed to appease a deity or to obtain forgiveness for a transgression.|The piacular sacrifice was offered to appease the angry gods.  赎罪祭是为了平息愤怒的神灵而献上的。|近义词：expiatory, atoning, propitiatory|反义词：unrepentant, blameworthy
