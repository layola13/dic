co-officials:/ˌkoʊ əˈfɪʃəlz/ | n. | 同僚官员，同事官员 |  Refers to officials who work together in the same organization or on the same project.  This term emphasizes the collaborative aspect of their roles. | Example: The co-officials met to discuss the new policy.  同事官员们会面讨论新政策。  The co-officials collaborated on the report. 同事官员们合作撰写了报告。|近义词：colleagues, fellow officials | 反义词：None  |
