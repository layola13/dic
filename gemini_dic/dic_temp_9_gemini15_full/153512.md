pensively:/ˈpɛnsɪvli/| adv.|沉思地，忧郁地 |Thoughtfully and sadly; in a pensive manner.  It suggests a state of quiet, often melancholic reflection. |例句：She sat pensively by the window, gazing at the rain. 她坐在窗边，沉思地望着雨。

例句：He pensively stroked his chin, lost in thought. 他沉思地抚摸着下巴，陷入沉思。


近义词：thoughtfully, reflectively, contemplatively, dreamily, wistfully


反义词：carefree, cheerfully, lightheartedly, joyfully


词性:副词
