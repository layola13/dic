faultfinder:/ˈfɔːltfaɪndər/| n. |挑剔者；吹毛求疵者 | A person who is always finding fault with others or things.  Someone who habitually points out flaws and shortcomings. | He's a real faultfinder; he never seems satisfied. 他是个真正的挑剔者；他似乎永远都不满意。| critic, censurer, nitpicker |  supporter, praiser | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finds fault with everything. = 他对一切都吹毛求疵。
She is always a faultfinder. = 她总是挑剔别人。


一般过去时:
He found fault with my work. = 他挑剔我的工作。
She found fault with the food. = 她挑剔食物。


现在完成时:
He has found fault with my every suggestion. = 他对我的每一个建议都表示不满。
She has been a faultfinder all her life. = 她一辈子都是个挑剔的人。


过去完成时:
He had found fault with the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议前就对计划提出了异议。
She had found fault with his behavior many times. = 她多次挑剔过他的行为。


将来时:
He will find fault with the new design. = 他会挑剔新的设计。
She will probably find fault with anything I do. = 她可能会挑剔我做的任何事情。


虚拟语气:
If he were not such a faultfinder, we would get along better. = 如果他不是那么挑剔，我们会相处得更好。
I wish she weren't such a faultfinder. = 我希望她不要那么挑剔。


被动语态:
His work was constantly found fault with by his boss. = 他的工作经常受到老板的批评。
The new policy was found fault with by many people. = 许多人对这项新政策表示不满。


疑问句:
Is he always a faultfinder? = 他总是挑剔吗？
Are you a faultfinder? = 你是个挑剔的人吗？


否定句:
He is not a faultfinder, he's just very detail-oriented. = 他不是个挑剔的人，他只是非常注重细节。
She didn't find fault with the presentation. = 她没有挑剔这个演示文稿。
