logged-off: /lɒɡd ˈɒf/ | adj. | 注销的，已下线的 |  Describes a state where a user has ended their session on a system or network.  It implies a deliberate action to disconnect. | Example:  My account is logged-off. 我的账号已注销。 The system automatically logged-off after 10 minutes of inactivity. 系统在10分钟无活动后自动注销。|近义词:disconnected, offline, signed out | 反义词:logged-in, online, signed in |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is logged off the system. 他已从系统注销。
She is logged off her email account. 她已从邮箱账户注销。

一般过去时:
I logged off the computer last night. 我昨晚关掉了电脑。
They logged off their accounts before leaving. 他们离开前注销了他们的账户。

现在完成时:
He has logged off his social media accounts. 他已经注销了他的社交媒体账户。
She has logged off for the night. 她已经下线休息了。

过去完成时:
I had logged off before the server crashed. 服务器崩溃之前我已经下线了。
They had logged off their accounts before the security breach. 安全漏洞发生前他们已经注销了账户。

将来时:
I will log off when I finish my work. 我完成工作后就会下线。
She will log off at 5 pm. 她将在下午5点下线。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would log off immediately. 如果我是你，我会立即下线。
I wish I had logged off earlier. 我真希望我早点下线。

被动语态:
The account was logged off remotely. 帐户被远程注销。
The session was logged off due to inactivity. 会话由于无活动而被注销。

疑问句:
Are you logged off yet? 你下线了吗？
Is the system logged off? 系统已注销了吗？

否定句:
I am not logged off yet. 我还没下线。
She was not logged off when the hacker attacked. 黑客攻击时她没有下线。


PHRASE->
log off the system: 从系统注销
log off the network: 从网络注销

force logged off: 强制注销
being logged off: 正在注销
