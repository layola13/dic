enduro:/ɛnˈdʊroʊ/| n. |耐力赛；耐力；持久力 |A long and difficult race, typically for motorcycles or cars, testing the endurance of both the machine and the driver or rider.  It also refers to the ability to withstand hardship or strain.|例句：The Dakar Rally is a grueling enduro. 达喀尔拉力赛是一场艰苦的耐力赛。

例句：His enduro through the desert proved his resilience. 他穿越沙漠的耐力赛证明了他的韧性。


近义词：endurance race, marathon, stamina


反义词：sprint, briefness, fragility


词性：名词
