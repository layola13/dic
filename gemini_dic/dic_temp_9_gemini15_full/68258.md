faithlessly:/ˈfeɪθləsli/| adv.|不忠实地；背信地；不诚实地|Without faith or loyalty; treacherously.  It implies a betrayal of trust or commitment.|例句：He acted faithlessly towards his friends. 他对朋友不忠。

例句：She faithlessly broke her promise. 她不忠实地违背了她的诺言。


近义词：treacherously, disloyally, unfaithfully, perfidiously

反义词：faithfully, loyally, honestly, truly


词性：副词
