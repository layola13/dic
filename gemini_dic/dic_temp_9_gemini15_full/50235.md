dependances: /dɪˈpɛndənsɪz/ | n. | 依赖；附属物；从属关系 |  The state of being dependent; reliance.  Also refers to things that are dependent or subordinate to something else, such as buildings or territories. |例句：His dependances on drugs led to his downfall.  他的药物依赖导致了他的垮台。

例句：The island's dependances include several smaller islets. 这个岛屿的附属物包括几个较小的岛屿。


近义词：reliance, dependence, subordination, subsidiary


反义词：independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency


词性：名词


词源：源自法语“dépendance”，意为“依赖”。最终源自拉丁语“dependere”，意为“悬挂；依靠”。
