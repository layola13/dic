latch:/lætʃ/| n., v.|门闩；锁；抓住；卡住|A latch is a simple fastening device, typically a bar or bolt that can be moved to secure a door or gate.  It can also refer to the act of fastening something with a latch or the act of catching or seizing something.|例句：He latched the gate securely. 他牢牢地闩上了大门。


The child latched onto her mother's leg.  孩子抓住了她母亲的腿。|近义词：fasten, catch, secure|反义词：unlock, unfasten
