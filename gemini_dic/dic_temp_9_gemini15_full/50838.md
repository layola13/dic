desecrated:/ˈdɛsɪkreɪtɪd/| v. adj.|亵渎，玷污，破坏（神圣）|To violate the sacredness of (something); to treat with disrespect.  The vandals desecrated the ancient temple. |近义词：violate, profane, defile, pollute|反义词：sanctify, consecrate, hallow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They desecrate the church every Sunday. = 他们每个星期天都亵渎教堂。
He desecrates the memory of his ancestors. = 他亵渎了他祖先的记忆。

一般过去时:
The tomb was desecrated by grave robbers. = 坟墓被盗墓贼亵渎了。
The soldiers desecrated the village. = 士兵们亵渎了这个村庄。

现在完成时:
They have desecrated the sacred grounds. = 他们已经亵渎了神圣的土地。
The vandals have desecrated the monument. = 暴徒们已经亵渎了纪念碑。

过去完成时:
The artifacts had been desecrated before they were discovered. = 文物在被发现之前就已经被亵渎了。
The temple had been desecrated centuries ago. = 那座寺庙几个世纪前就被亵渎了。

将来时:
They will desecrate the statue next week. = 他们下周将亵渎这座雕像。
He will desecrate the oath he has taken. = 他将违背他所宣的誓言。

虚拟语气:
If they desecrated the holy site, they would face severe punishment. = 如果他们亵渎了圣地，他们将面临严厉的惩罚。
I wish they hadn't desecrated the national flag. = 我希望他们没有亵渎国旗。

被动语态:
The holy site was desecrated by the intruders. = 圣地被入侵者亵渎了。
The ancient ruins were desecrated over many years. = 古代遗址多年来一直被亵渎。

疑问句:
Was the cemetery desecrated? = 墓地被亵渎了吗？
Have the sacred texts been desecrated? = 这些神圣的文本被亵渎了吗？

否定句:
They did not desecrate the temple. = 他们没有亵渎寺庙。
We will not allow them to desecrate our history. = 我们不会允许他们亵渎我们的历史。


PHRASE->
desecrated land = 亵渎的土地
desecrated grave = 被亵渎的坟墓
desecrated temple = 被亵渎的寺庙
desecrated memory = 被玷污的记忆
desecrated symbol = 被亵渎的象征


词源:
From Old French *desecrer, from Latin *desecrāre, from de- "away from" + sacrāre "to consecrate."  The word carries a strong sense of reversing or undoing a sacred act.
