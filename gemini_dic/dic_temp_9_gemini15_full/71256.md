five-star:/faɪvˈstɑːr/| adj.|五星级的；极好的|Describes something of the highest quality, typically a hotel or restaurant,  often indicating luxury and excellence.|例句：The hotel offered five-star service and amenities. 这家酒店提供五星级的服务和设施。|例句：That restaurant is a five-star dining experience. 那家餐厅是一家五星级的餐饮体验。|近义词：excellent, superb, first-rate, top-notch|反义词：one-star, poor, substandard|
