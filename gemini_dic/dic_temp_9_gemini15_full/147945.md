overrepresented:/ˌoʊvərˈreprɪˌzɛntɪd/ | adj. | 过度代表的，比例过高的 |  Excessively represented; present in greater numbers or proportions than expected or average.  This usually implies a disproportionate representation compared to a reference group or population. | 例句：Women are overrepresented in the nursing profession. = 女性在护理行业中比例过高。

例句：Minorities are overrepresented in the prison population. = 少数族裔在监狱人口中比例过高。


近义词：disproportionately represented, excessively represented, overweighted


反义词：underrepresented, underweighted


词源: over- (prefix indicating excess) + represent (to stand for or act on behalf of) + -ed (past participle suffix)
