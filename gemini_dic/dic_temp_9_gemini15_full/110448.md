likes:/laɪks/| n. v. |喜好，喜爱；喜欢做某事|英文解析：The noun "likes" refers to things a person enjoys or finds pleasant. The verb "like" expresses preference or enjoyment.  |例句：His likes include hiking and photography. (他的爱好包括远足和摄影。)  I like pizza. (我喜欢披萨。) |近义词：loves, enjoys, prefers, appreciates |反义词：dislikes, hates, detests |词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He likes playing basketball. = 他喜欢打篮球。
She likes to read novels. = 她喜欢读小说。

一般过去时:
They liked the movie. = 他们喜欢这部电影。
I liked ice cream when I was a child. = 我小时候喜欢吃冰淇淋。

现在完成时:
I have liked this song for years. = 我喜欢这首歌很多年了。
She has liked him since they first met. = 从他们第一次见面起她就喜欢他了。

过去完成时:
He had liked her for a long time before he finally confessed. = 在他最终表白之前，他已经喜欢她很久了。
We had liked the idea before we learned about the cost. = 在我们了解成本之前，我们很喜欢这个主意。

将来时:
I think I will like living in the countryside. = 我想我会喜欢住在乡下的。
They will like the gift you bought them. = 他们会喜欢你给他们买的礼物。

虚拟语气:
I wish I liked vegetables more. = 我希望我更喜欢蔬菜。
If I liked spicy food, I would try that dish. = 如果我喜欢辛辣食物，我会尝试那道菜。
