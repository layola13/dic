force:/fɔːrs/| /fɔːrs/ | n./vt.|力，力量；武力；军队；势力；强行；迫使|Physical strength or energy;  the strength or energy as an attribute of physical action or movement; a natural power or agency; a body of armed people; compelling power;  to compel by force; to cause (something) to happen or be done by using force or authority.|例句：The force of the wind blew down the tree.  风的力道把树吹倒了。|例句：The police were forced to use force to arrest the suspect. 警方被迫使用武力逮捕嫌疑人。|近义词：strength, power, might, compulsion|反义词：weakness, impotence


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The wind forces the door open. 风把门吹开了。
She forces him to work overtime. 她强迫他加班。


一般过去时:
They forced their way through the crowd. 他们强行挤过人群。
The storm forced the plane to land. 暴风雨迫使飞机降落。


现在完成时:
They have forced the government to change its policy.  他们迫使政府改变政策。
The pressure has forced him to resign.  压力迫使他辞职。


过去完成时:

将来时:
