metanotum:/ˌmɛtəˈnoʊtəm/| n. |并胸腹节|The metanotum is the dorsal sclerite of the metathorax, the third thoracic segment of an insect.  It's often fused with other segments and can be difficult to distinguish.  The structure and appearance of the metanotum vary greatly across insect orders.|The dragonfly's metanotum is particularly robust.|近义词:posterior notum (less precise)|反义词:pronotum, mesonotum (referring to other thoracic segments)|名词
