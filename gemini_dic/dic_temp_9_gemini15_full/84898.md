hankering:/ˈhæŋkərɪŋ/| n.|渴望，热望|A strong desire or longing for something.|I have a hankering for a chocolate milkshake. 我非常想喝巧克力奶昔。|近义词：craving, yearning, longing|反义词：disinterest, apathy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a hankering for spicy food. = 他渴望吃辛辣的食物。
She always has a hankering for her childhood home. = 她总是渴望回到她童年的家。

一般过去时:
I had a hankering for pizza last night. = 昨晚我非常想吃披萨。
They had a hankering for adventure in their youth. = 他们年轻时渴望冒险。

现在完成时:
I have had a hankering for that book for months. = 我几个月来一直渴望得到那本书。
She has had a hankering for a change of scenery lately. = 她最近一直渴望改变一下环境。

过去完成时:
Before the trip, I had had a hankering for a relaxing vacation. = 旅行前，我一直渴望一个轻松的假期。
They had had a hankering for a quiet life in the countryside for years. = 他们多年来一直渴望在乡下过平静的生活。

将来时:
I will have a hankering for some sweets after dinner. = 晚饭后我会想吃些甜食。
She thinks she will have a hankering for a cup of tea later. = 她认为过会儿她会想喝杯茶。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would satisfy my hankering for travel. = 如果我有钱，我会满足我旅行的渴望。
I wish I could satisfy my hankering for a new car. = 我真希望我能满足我买新车的渴望。


被动语态:
His hankering for fame was never truly satisfied. = 他对名利的渴望从未真正得到满足。
Her hankering for a better life was not easily ignored. = 她对更好生活的渴望不容易被忽视。


疑问句:
What is your current hankering? = 你现在渴望什么？
Do you have any hankering for a particular dessert? = 你渴望吃某种特别的甜点吗？


否定句:
I don't have a hankering for anything right now. = 我现在什么都不想吃。
She didn't have a hankering for social gatherings. = 她并不渴望参加社交聚会。
