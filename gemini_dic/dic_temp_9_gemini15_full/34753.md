cliff:/klɪf/| n.|悬崖，峭壁|A steep rock face, especially at the edge of the sea.|例句：The hikers carefully approached the edge of the cliff.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地靠近悬崖边缘。
例句：The waves crashed against the cliff face. 海浪拍打着悬崖峭壁。|近义词： precipice, crag, bluff|反义词：plain, valley


USAGE->
