monohemerous:/ˌmɒnəʊˈhɛmərəs/| adj. |单日生的；一日生的|  lasting only one day; occurring only once a day.  Referring to flowers that bloom and wither within a single day. | Example: Many desert flowers are monohemerous, opening in the morning and closing at night.  许多沙漠花卉都是单日生的，早晨开放，晚上闭合。| 近义词: ephemeral, short-lived | 反义词: perennial, long-lasting | 形容词
