rebandages:/ˌriːˈbændɪdʒɪz/| v.|重新包扎；再包扎 |To bandage (a wound, etc.) again.  The verb implies a previous bandaging that is being redone, often because the first bandage has become loose, soiled, or ineffective. |例句：The nurse had to rebandage the patient's leg after the original dressing became soaked with blood. 护士不得不重新包扎病人的腿，因为原来的敷料被血浸透了。

例句：After the intense workout, he rebandaged his sprained ankle. 剧烈运动后，他重新包扎了他扭伤的脚踝。


近义词：redress, re-dress, bandage again|反义词：unbandage, remove bandage


词性：动词
