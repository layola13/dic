ravenala:/ˌrævəˈnɑːlə/ | n. |旅人蕉 | A large plant of the genus Ravenala, native to Madagascar, with large, fan-like leaves arranged in two rows on a thick stem.  It is also known as the traveler's palm because its leaf sheaths collect rainwater, which can be drunk. | Example: The traveler's palm, or ravenala, is a striking sight in the Madagascar landscape.  旅人蕉，或称旅人掌，是马达加斯加景观中引人注目的景象。|近义词：traveler's palm |反义词：无
