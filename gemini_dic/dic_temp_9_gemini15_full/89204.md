homespun:/ˈhoʊmspʌn/| adj.|家纺的；土制的；朴实的|Made or produced at home; simple and unpretentious.  The word evokes a sense of rustic charm and simplicity, often associated with traditional crafts and rural life.|例句：Her homespun dress was made from fabric she wove herself.  她的土布连衣裙是她自己织的布做的。

例句：The speaker's homespun wisdom resonated with the audience. 演讲者朴实的智慧引起了听众的共鸣。|近义词：homemade, rustic, simple, unpretentious|反义词：sophisticated, refined, elegant, elaborate|
