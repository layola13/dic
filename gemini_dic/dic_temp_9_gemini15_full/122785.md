molasseses:/məˈlæsiːz/| n.|糖蜜(复数)|Molasseses is the plural of molasses, a thick, dark syrup produced during the refining of sugarcane or sugar beets.  It's a viscous, sweet substance often used in cooking and baking.  The use of the plural "molasseses" is considered nonstandard and generally avoided; "molasses" is usually used for both singular and plural. |The baker used molasseses in his gingerbread recipe.|近义词：treacle|反义词：无

词源:
