indicavit:/ɪndɪˈkævɪt/| v.|指示；表明；显示；指出|This word is the third-person singular perfect indicative active of the Latin verb *indicare*, meaning "to point out," "to show," or "to indicate."  It's rarely used in modern English except in specialized contexts, particularly legal or historical ones, referring to a specific action or declaration made in the past.  |例句：The document indicavit the ownership of the land.  该文件表明了土地的所有权。


The historical record indicavit a significant shift in public opinion. 历史记录表明公众舆论发生了重大转变。|近义词：showed, demonstrated, revealed, proved|反义词：concealed, hidden, obscured|动词
