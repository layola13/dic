self-serving: /ˌsɛlfˈsɜːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 自私的；只顾自己利益的 |  serving one's own interests, often at the expense of others.  It implies a lack of consideration for others' needs or feelings. |


例句：

His self-serving actions alienated many of his colleagues.  他的自私行为疏远了他许多同事。

The report was criticized for being self-serving and lacking objectivity. 这份报告因自私且缺乏客观性而受到批评。


近义词：selfish, egoistic, narcissistic, self-centered,  opportunistic


反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish, benevolent


词性：形容词
