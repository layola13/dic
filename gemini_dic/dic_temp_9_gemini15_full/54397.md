disimpark:/dɪsɪmˈpɑːrk/| v. |驶离停车位；离开停车位|To move a vehicle out of a parking space.  The word is a blend of "dis-" (meaning "away from" or "opposite of") and "impark" (a back-formation from "parking").|例句：He disimparked his car quickly to avoid a ticket. 他迅速地把车从停车位开走以免被罚款。

例句：After the meeting, everyone disimparked their cars and headed home. 会议结束后，每个人都把车从停车位开走回家了。

近义词：depart from parking space, leave a parking spot, drive out of parking|反义词：park,  enter a parking space|动词
