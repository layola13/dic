precomplicate:/ˌpriːˈkɒmplɪkeɪt/| v.|使预先复杂化；预先使复杂|To make something more complicated than it needs to be, often unnecessarily or prematurely.|The project was precomplicated by unrealistic deadlines.  这个项目因不切实际的期限而预先复杂化了。|近义词：overcomplicate, complicate,  pre-plan|反义词：simplify, clarify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They precomplicate the design with unnecessary features. = 他们用不必要的特性使设计预先复杂化。
He precomplicates his work by overthinking. = 他过度思考，使他的工作预先复杂化。


一般过去时:
The architect precomplicated the building plan with excessive details. = 建筑师用过多的细节使建筑图纸预先复杂化了。
She precomplicated the situation by revealing too much information too soon. = 她过早地透露了太多信息，使情况预先复杂化了。


现在完成时:
We have precomplicated the process by introducing too many variables. = 我们引入了太多变量，使这个过程预先复杂化了。
They have precomplicated the negotiations by making unreasonable demands. = 他们提出了不合理的要求，使谈判预先复杂化了。


过去完成时:
He had precomplicated the recipe by adding too many spices before he even started cooking. = 他甚至还没开始做饭就加了太多的香料，使菜谱预先复杂化了。
They had precomplicated the game by introducing too many rules before the players could even begin. = 他们在玩家开始游戏之前就引入了太多规则，使游戏预先复杂化了。


将来时:
I will precomplicate the matter if I don't think carefully. = 如果我不仔细考虑，我会使事情预先复杂化。
They will precomplicate the trip by not booking accommodations in advance. = 如果他们不提前预订住宿，他们将会使旅行预先复杂化。


虚拟语气:
