contacts:/ˈkɒntækts/ /ˈkɑːntækts/| n.|联系方式；联系人；隐形眼镜|People or organizations with whom one maintains contact; a person's connections.  Also refers to lenses worn on the eyes.|例句：I need to update my contacts. 我需要更新我的联系方式。

例句：She exchanged contacts with several potential employers at the job fair. 她在招聘会上与几位潜在雇主交换了联系方式。


近义词：connections, acquaintances, associates, lenses

反义词：isolation, separation
