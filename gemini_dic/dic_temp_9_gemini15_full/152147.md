patin:/ˈpætɪn/| n.|  铜绿；（尤指金属表面的）暗淡的保护层；|A patina is a surface layer, usually green or brown, that forms on objects made of copper, bronze, or other metals exposed to the elements. It's often considered aesthetically pleasing and protective.|例句：The old copper kettle had developed a beautiful green patina. 这口旧铜壶上形成了一层漂亮的绿色铜绿。 The statue's patina was a testament to its age and exposure to the elements.  雕像上的铜绿证明了它的年代久远以及经受风吹雨打。|近义词：tarnish, verdigris, film|反义词：polish, shine
