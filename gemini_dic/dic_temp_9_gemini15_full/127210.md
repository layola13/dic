/ˈnɔːtiər/ | adj. |更顽皮的，更淘气的 | More mischievous or badly behaved than usual.  It is the comparative form of "naughty". |例句：He's been naughtier than usual lately.  他最近比平时更淘气了。

近义词：more mischievous, more disobedient, more unruly | 反义词：more well-behaved, more obedient
