sash:/ˈsæʃ/| n.|腰带；窗扇；绶带|A sash is a long piece of cloth worn around the waist or over the shoulder, often as part of a uniform or ceremonial dress. It can also refer to a frame holding a windowpane.|例句：She wore a sash in the school colors. 她系着学校颜色的腰带。|例句：The sash of the window was stuck. 窗户的窗扇卡住了。|近义词：belt, ribbon, band|反义词：无
