preconsider:/priːkənˈsɪdə(r)/| vt.|预先考虑，事先考虑|To think about or consider something beforehand.  It implies careful thought and planning before taking action.|例句：We need to preconsider the potential risks before launching the project.  我们需要在启动项目之前预先考虑潜在的风险。

例句：She preconsidered all possible outcomes before making her decision. 她做出决定之前预先考虑了所有可能的结果。


近义词：consider beforehand, contemplate beforehand, think over in advance, plan for


反义词：ignore, overlook, neglect


词性：动词
