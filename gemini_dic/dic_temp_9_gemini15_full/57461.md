draftee:/dræfˈtiː/| n. |被征召入伍者，应征入伍者|A draftee is a person who has been conscripted into military service.  This term is often associated with compulsory military service, particularly during wartime.  |Example: Many young men became draftees during the Vietnam War.  许多年轻人在越南战争期间成为被征召入伍者。

Example:  He was a reluctant draftee, preferring civilian life. 他是不情愿的应征入伍者，更喜欢平民生活。|近义词：conscript, inductee |反义词：volunteer (in the context of military service)
