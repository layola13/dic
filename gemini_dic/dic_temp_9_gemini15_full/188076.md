round-shouldered: /ˈraʊndˈʃoʊldərd/ | adj. | 圆肩的，驼背的 | Having or characterized by rounded shoulders; stooped.  This describes a posture where the shoulders are slumped forward, often due to poor posture or physical condition.  |例句：He had a round-shouldered posture from years of hunching over his desk. 他多年来一直伏案工作，所以驼背了。

例句：The doctor advised her to improve her posture to correct her round-shouldered appearance. 医生建议她改善姿势以纠正驼背的外观。


近义词：stooped, hunched, slouched, bowed

反义词：straight-backed, upright
