court:/kɔːrt/| /kɔːt/ | n. v. | 法院；球场；朝廷；奉承；追求 | A court is a place where legal cases are heard.  It can also refer to a playing area, such as a tennis court, or a royal court.  As a verb, it means to try to win someone's affection or favor. | 例句：The court found him guilty. 法院判决他有罪。 The tennis court was in excellent condition. 网球场状况极佳。 | 近义词： tribunal, yard,  (v.) woo, pursue | 反义词： (n.) prison, wilderness (v.) reject, ignore | 名词，动词
