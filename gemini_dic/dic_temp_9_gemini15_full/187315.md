rockery:/ˈrɒkəri/| n. |假山；石堆|A rockery is an artificial arrangement of rocks and stones, often with plants growing among them, created as an ornamental feature in a garden.  It is designed to mimic a natural rock formation.|例句：The gardener created a beautiful rockery in the corner of the garden. 园丁在花园角落里建造了一个美丽的假山。|例句：We spent the afternoon building a rockery in the backyard. 我们下午都在后院堆砌假山。|近义词：rock garden, stone garden|反义词:  plain, flat ground
