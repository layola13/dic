oiler:/ˈɔɪlər/| n. |注油工；加油机；润滑油；（机器的）油杯| a person or thing that oils something; a container for oil; lubricating oil. |例句：He works as an oiler in a factory. 他在一家工厂做注油工。 The oiler kept the machine running smoothly.  加油机使机器平稳运行。|近义词：lubricator, greaser |反义词：de-oiler (非正式)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The oiler lubricates the engine regularly. = 注油工定期润滑发动机。
He oils the bicycle chain every week. = 他每周都给自行车链条上油。


一般过去时:
The mechanic oiled the car engine yesterday. = 机械师昨天给汽车发动机加油了。
The old oiler leaked oil onto the floor. = 旧的油杯漏油到地板上了。


现在完成时:
They have oiled the machinery for years. = 他们多年来一直为机械加油。
The factory has used this type of oiler for decades. = 该工厂几十年来一直在使用这种类型的加油机。


过去完成时:
The engine had already been oiled before the mechanic arrived. = 机械师到达之前，发动机已经被加油了。
They had used that old oiler for many years before it broke. = 在它坏掉之前，他们已经用了那个旧油杯很多年了。


将来时:
We will oil the car before the long trip. = 长途旅行之前，我们会给汽车加油。
The company will install a new automatic oiler next week. = 公司下周将安装一个新的自动加油机。


虚拟语气:
