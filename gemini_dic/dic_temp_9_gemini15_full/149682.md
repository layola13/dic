pajamas:/pəˈdʒɑːməz/ | n. | 睡衣 | Loose-fitting clothing worn in bed, typically consisting of a top and bottoms.  | Example: I put on my pajamas and went to bed. 我穿上睡衣然后上床睡觉了。|近义词：nightclothes, sleepwear |反义词：day clothes, street clothes
