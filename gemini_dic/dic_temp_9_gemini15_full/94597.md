impreciseness:/ɪmˈprɛsɪsnəs/ | n. | 不精确；含糊；模糊 | Lack of exactness; vagueness.  It refers to the quality or state of being imprecise.  The impreciseness of the instructions led to confusion.  |The impreciseness of his language made it difficult to understand his point.|近义词：vagueness, ambiguity, inexactness, inaccuracy |反义词：precision, accuracy, exactness|
