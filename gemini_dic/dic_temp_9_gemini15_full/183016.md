replica:/ˈrɛplɪkə/| n.|复制品，仿制品|A replica is a very accurate copy of something.|The museum displayed a replica of the ancient statue.  博物馆展出了那尊古代雕像的复制品。|copy, duplicate, imitation|original, authentic|名词

The museum owns a replica of the Mona Lisa. = 博物馆拥有一幅蒙娜丽莎的复制品。
This watch is a replica of a famous brand. = 这块手表是名牌的仿制品。
