rationalize: /ˈræʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. |使合理化；为…找借口；使符合理性 | To attempt to justify or explain (one's own or another's behavior or feelings) in a way that seems reasonable.  |  例句：He tried to rationalize his behavior by saying he was under a lot of stress. 他试图通过说自己压力很大来为自己的行为辩解。


例句：She rationalized her decision to quit her job by saying the commute was too long. 她以通勤时间太长为由，为她辞职的决定找借口。


近义词：justify, explain, excuse, vindicate | 反义词：irrationalize


词源：源自拉丁语 "rationalis" (理性的),  由 "ratio" (理性，比例) 派生而来。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rationalizes his mistakes. = 他为他的错误找借口。
They rationalize their spending habits. = 他们为他们的消费习惯找借口。

一般过去时:
She rationalized her fear of heights. = 她为她对高处的恐惧找借口。
He rationalized his decision to invest in that company. = 他为投资那家公司的决定找借口。

现在完成时:
I have rationalized my procrastination. = 我已经为我的拖延找了借口。
They have rationalized their actions to the board.  = 他们已经向董事会解释了他们的行为。

过去完成时:
She had rationalized her anger before confronting him. = 在与他对质之前，她已经为她的愤怒找了借口。
He had rationalized his previous failures before starting the new project. = 在开始新项目之前，他已经为之前的失败找了借口。

将来时:
I will rationalize my choice to my parents. = 我会向我的父母解释我的选择。
They will rationalize the changes to the employees. = 他们将向员工解释这些改变。

虚拟语气:
If he rationalized his behavior, he might feel better. = 如果他能为自己的行为找借口，他可能会感觉好一些。
If she had rationalized her fear earlier, she would have been less stressed. = 如果她早点为自己的恐惧找借口，她就不会那么焦虑了。

被动语态:
His actions were rationalized by his lawyer. = 他的行为得到了他的律师的辩护。
The company's decisions were rationalized to the public. = 公司的决定向公众作了解释。

疑问句:
How did he rationalize his actions? = 他如何为他的行为辩解？
How can we rationalize such a significant loss? = 我们如何解释如此巨大的损失？

否定句:
He didn't rationalize his poor performance. = 他没有为他的糟糕表现找借口。
She couldn't rationalize her feelings of guilt. = 她无法为她的负罪感找借口。


PHRASE->
