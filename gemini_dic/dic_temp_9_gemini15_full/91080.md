hutting:/ˈhʌtɪŋ/| n. |居住在小屋中；建小屋；小屋居住区 |The act of living in a hut or huts; the process of building huts; a collection of huts.  |例句：The nomadic tribe practiced hutting during their seasonal migrations. 流动部落在季节性迁徙期间过着居住在小屋里的生活。 |例句：The construction workers were engaged in hutting to provide temporary shelter for the project. 建筑工人正在建造小屋，为项目提供临时住所。|近义词：dwelling, sheltering, lodging |反义词：palatial living, luxurious accommodation.
