possets:/ˈpɒsɪts/| n.|暖牛奶饮料，蛋奶酒|A posset is a hot drink traditionally made with milk, ale, and spices, sometimes with added eggs or wine.  It was a popular drink in medieval and Tudor England.  |Example:  She warmed herself with a comforting posset before bed. 她睡前喝了一杯暖和的蛋奶酒来暖身。|近义词：hot milk drink, spiced milk|反义词：None specifically.
