founds:/faʊndz/| v. |创立；建立；发现；(found的过去式和过去分词)|To establish something, such as an organization or institution, or to discover something.|例句：She founded a charity to help homeless people. 她创办了一个慈善机构来帮助无家可归的人。|例句：The archaeologists founded an ancient city buried beneath the desert. 考古学家发现了一个埋在沙漠下的古代城市。|近义词：establish, create, discover, initiate|反义词：destroy, abolish, dismantle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They found new evidence in the case. = 他们在案子中发现了新的证据。
He funds a new school every year. = 他每年资助一所新学校。

一般过去时:
The company was founded in 1980. = 这家公司成立于1980年。
The explorers founded a new colony. = 探险家们建立了一个新的殖民地。

现在完成时:
They have founded several successful businesses. = 他们已经创办了几家成功的企业。
Archaeologists have found many ancient artifacts. = 考古学家们已经发现了许多古代文物。


过去完成时:
Before the war, they had founded a thriving community. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经建立了一个繁荣的社区。
By the time I arrived, they had found a solution. = 当我到达时，他们已经找到了解决办法。


将来时:
She will found a new museum next year. = 她明年将创办一个新的博物馆。
They will found the lost treasure soon. = 他们很快就会找到失落的宝藏。


虚拟语气:
If I had found the key, I would have opened the door. = 如果我找到了钥匙，我就已经开了门。
I wish I had founded my own business years ago. = 我真希望几年前就自己创业了。


被动语态:
The school was founded by a wealthy philanthropist. = 这所学校是由一位富有的慈善家创办的。
The lost city has been recently found. = 失落的城市最近被发现了。


疑问句:
When was the organization founded? = 组织是什么时候成立的？
Have they found the missing documents? = 他们找到丢失的文件了吗？


否定句:
They did not find any evidence of wrongdoing. = 他们没有发现任何不当行为的证据。
She did not found a company. = 她没有创办公司。


PHRASE->
found a company = 创办公司
found a family = 成立家庭
found a school = 创办学校
found guilty = 被判有罪
found wanting = 发现不足之处
