misbehaving:/ˌmɪsbiˈheɪvɪŋ/| v. |行为不端，捣乱|Acting in a way that is not good or acceptable.  This word describes actions that are unruly, disobedient, or contrary to expectations of proper conduct. It often refers to children's bad behavior but can apply to adults as well.|例句：The children were misbehaving in class. 孩子们在课堂上行为不端。

The dog was misbehaving by chewing on the furniture. 狗乱咬家具，行为不端。

近义词：acting up, playing up, causing trouble, being naughty, being unruly|反义词：behaving well, being well-behaved, obeying, complying
