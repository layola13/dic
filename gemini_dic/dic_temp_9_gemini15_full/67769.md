eyehole:/ˈaɪhoʊl/| n. |窥视孔，钥匙孔|A small hole, especially in a door or wall, that allows someone to see through.  |例句：He peered through the eyehole of the door. 他透过门上的窥视孔窥视。

例句：The keyhole was so small it was hard to insert the key. 钥匙孔太小，很难插入钥匙。

近义词：peephole, keyhole |反义词：wide opening
