imputing:/ɪmˈpjuːtɪŋ/ | v. |归咎于；归因于；(尤指错误地)指控；|To attribute (something) to someone or something.  Often implies blame or responsibility. It can also refer to assigning a value or characteristic.|例句：He imputed his failure to bad luck. 他把他的失败归咎于运气不好。

例句：They imputed the rise in crime to the government's policies. 他们将犯罪率的上升归咎于政府的政策。


近义词：attribute, ascribe, assign, charge


反义词：exonerate, absolve


词源：源自拉丁语“imputare”，意为“to charge, reckon, or ascribe”。  "im-" 是一个前缀，表示“进入”或“到...里面”； "putare" 意思是“认为，计算”。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
To whom do you impute the responsibility? = 你把责任归咎于谁？

否定句:
I do not impute blame to anyone. = 我不责怪任何人。
They did not impute the accident to negligence. = 他们没有将事故归咎于疏忽。
