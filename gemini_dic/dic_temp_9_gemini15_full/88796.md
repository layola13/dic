hoeing:/ˈhoʊɪŋ/| v.|锄地，除草|The act of using a hoe to cultivate the soil or remove weeds.|例句：He spent the afternoon hoeing the vegetable patch. 他一下午都在菜地里锄草。|例句：The farmer was hoeing the rows of corn. 农民正在玉米行间锄地。|近义词：tilling, weeding|反义词：planting (in the sense of covering up weeds), leaving untended


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She hoes the garden every morning. = 她每天早上都锄地。
He hoes the weeds between the rows of plants. = 他锄掉植物行间的杂草。

一般过去时:
They hoed the field yesterday. = 他们昨天锄了地。
I hoed the garden until sunset. = 我锄地直到日落。


现在完成时:
We have hoed all the weeds in the garden. = 我们已经锄掉了花园里的所有杂草。
She has hoed the potatoes. = 她已经锄了土豆。


过去完成时:
They had hoed the entire field before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经锄完了整个地。
The farmer had hoed the rows before planting the seeds. = 农民在播种之前已经锄过行了。


将来时:
I will hoe the garden tomorrow. = 我明天将要锄地。
They will hoe the field next week. = 他们下周将要锄地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a hoe, I would hoe the garden. = 如果我有一把锄头，我会锄地的。
I wish I had hoed the garden earlier. = 我希望我早点锄地。


被动语态:
The field was hoed by the farmer. = 这块地是由农民锄的。
The weeds are being hoed by the gardener. = 园丁正在锄草。


疑问句:
Have you hoed the garden yet? = 你锄地了吗？
Will they hoe the field today? = 他们今天会锄地吗？


否定句:
He didn't hoe the garden yesterday. = 他昨天没有锄地。
They haven't hoed the field yet. = 他们还没锄地。
