outpatient:/ˈaʊtˌpeɪʃənt/| n. |门诊病人，门诊患者 |A patient who receives medical treatment without being admitted to a hospital.  |例句：She is an outpatient at the clinic. 她是在诊所看门诊的病人。|例句：The doctor saw several outpatients this morning. 医生今天上午接诊了好几位门诊病人。|近义词：ambulatory patient |反义词：inpatient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an outpatient at the hospital. = 他是医院的门诊病人。
She receives outpatient care. = 她接受门诊护理。


一般过去时:
He was an outpatient last week. = 他上周是门诊病人。
She received outpatient treatment for her injury. = 她因伤接受了门诊治疗。


现在完成时:
He has been an outpatient for six months. = 他已经做了六个月的门诊病人。
She has received excellent outpatient care. = 她接受了极好的门诊护理。


过去完成时:
He had been an outpatient before his condition worsened. = 在他的病情恶化之前，他一直是门诊病人。
She had received outpatient physiotherapy before the surgery. = 手术前她接受了门诊理疗。


将来时:
He will be an outpatient next week. = 他下周将是门诊病人。
She will receive outpatient surgery. = 她将接受门诊手术。


虚拟语气:
If he were an outpatient, he could go home. = 如果他是门诊病人，他就可以回家了。
I wish I were an outpatient instead of being hospitalized. = 我希望我是门诊病人而不是住院病人。


被动语态:
He was treated as an outpatient. = 他被当作门诊病人治疗。
Outpatient services are provided here. = 这里提供门诊服务。


疑问句:
Is he an outpatient or an inpatient? = 他是门诊病人还是住院病人？
Are outpatient appointments available today? = 今天有门诊预约吗？


否定句:
He is not an outpatient; he is hospitalized. = 他不是门诊病人；他住院了。
She did not receive outpatient care. = 她没有接受门诊护理。


PHRASE->
outpatient department = 门诊部
