reporters:/rɪˈpɔːrtərz/ | n. |记者们，报道者们 |People who gather and report news for newspapers, magazines, television, or radio.  Reporters investigate events, interview people, and write or broadcast stories. | The reporters rushed to the scene of the accident. 记者们迅速赶到事故现场。  Many reporters covered the presidential election. 许多记者报道了总统大选。 | journalists, correspondents, newsmen |  (无明显反义词，可考虑non-reporters或ordinary people) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Reporters write articles every day. = 记者们每天都写文章。
Reporters interview witnesses to gather information. = 记者们采访证人以收集信息。


一般过去时:
Reporters covered the breaking news last night. = 记者们昨晚报道了突发新闻。
Reporters filed their stories before the deadline. = 记者们在截止日期前提交了他们的报道。


现在完成时:
Reporters have interviewed many important people. = 记者们已经采访了许多重要人物。
Reporters have exposed several scandals this year. = 记者们今年揭露了几起丑闻。


过去完成时:
The reporters had already left before the press conference began. = 在新闻发布会开始之前，记者们已经离开了。
The reporters had gathered all the facts before writing the article. = 记者们在写文章之前已经收集了所有事实。


将来时:
Reporters will cover the upcoming summit. = 记者们将报道即将举行的峰会。
Reporters will file their reports by the end of the week. = 记者们将在周末前提交他们的报告。


虚拟语气:
If the reporters were more careful, they would not have made that mistake. = 如果记者们更仔细一些，他们就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish the reporters had given me more time to answer their questions. = 我希望记者们给了我更多时间回答他们的问题。


被动语态:
The story was reported by several reporters. = 这则新闻是由几位记者报道的。
Many details were omitted from the reporters' accounts. = 许多细节被记者的报道省略了。


疑问句:
Were the reporters accurate in their reporting? = 记者们的报道准确吗？
How many reporters attended the press conference? = 多少记者参加了新闻发布会？


否定句:
The reporters did not have access to all the information. = 记者们无法获得所有信息。
The reporters denied the allegations. = 记者们否认了这些指控。
