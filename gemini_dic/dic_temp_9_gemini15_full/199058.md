significator:/ˈsɪɡnɪfɪkeɪtər/| n. |指示物；象征；预兆|A significator is something that signifies or represents something else, often something abstract or intangible.  It can be a symbol, a sign, or an indicator.  In astrology, a significator is a planet or other celestial body considered to represent a particular person or thing. |Example: The dark clouds served as a significator of the impending storm.  乌云是即将到来的暴风雨的预兆。 The king's crown is a significator of his power and authority. 国王的王冠象征着他的权力和权威。|近义词：symbol, sign, indicator, emblem, token|反义词： insignificator (not a common word)|名词
