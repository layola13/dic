martlemas:/ˈmɑːrtlɪməs/| n.|圣马丁节|Martinmas is a feast day celebrated on November 11th in honor of Saint Martin of Tours.  It marks the beginning of winter in many cultures and was historically associated with various customs and traditions, including the end of the harvest season and the beginning of the livestock wintering period.|The farmers celebrated Martlemas with a feast after the harvest.|近义词：Saint Martin's Day|反义词：无
