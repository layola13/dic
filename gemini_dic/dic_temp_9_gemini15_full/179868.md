reexpose:/ˌriːɪkˈspoʊz/| vt.|重新揭露；再次暴露；使再次遭受 |To expose again or anew; to subject again to exposure.|例句：The media reexposed the politician's past scandals.  媒体再次曝光了这位政客过去的丑闻。|例句：The repeated reexposure to sunlight damaged her skin.  反复暴露在阳光下损坏了她的皮肤。|近义词：reveal, unveil, disclose|反义词：conceal, hide, suppress|动词
