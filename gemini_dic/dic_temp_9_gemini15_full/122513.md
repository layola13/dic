mockingbirds:/ˈmɑːkɪŋbɜːrdz/ /ˈmɒkɪŋbɜːdz/| n. |嘲鸫（鸟）|A mockingbird is a type of bird known for its ability to mimic the sounds of other birds and animals.  They are often found in North America.|The mockingbird sang a beautiful song, imitating the calls of several other birds.|近义词：mimic bird, songbird |反义词：无
