rejuvenating: /rɪˈdʒuːvənˌeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 使恢复活力的；使年轻的；使复兴的 |  Making someone or something feel or look younger, fresher, or more lively.  The process of renewing or restoring something. |

例句：

The spa offers a range of rejuvenating treatments.  = 这个水疗中心提供一系列恢复活力的护理。

The rejuvenating effect of the mountain air was noticeable. = 山间空气的恢复活力效果是显而易见的。


近义词：invigorating, refreshing, revitalizing, restorative, renewing

反义词：debilitating, aging, exhausting, draining
