holmes:/hoʊmz/| n. |福尔摩斯（姓氏）|Holmes is a surname, most famously associated with the fictional detective Sherlock Holmes created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.  The name itself is of English origin and likely derives from Old English elements.  It can be used as a proper noun referring to the character or the surname. |The famous detective, Sherlock Holmes, solved many mysteries.  |近义词:  (as a surname, no direct synonyms)|反义词: (as a surname, no direct antonyms)|名词
