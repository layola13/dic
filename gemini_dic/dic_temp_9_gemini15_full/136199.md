nonrelevant:/ˌnɒnˈreləvənt/| adj. |不相关的；无关紧要的 |Not relevant; not connected with the subject at hand.  |例句：His comments were completely nonrelevant to the discussion. 他的评论与讨论完全无关。
例句：The information provided was largely nonrelevant and unhelpful. 提供的信息大多无关紧要且无益。|近义词：irrelevant, immaterial, extraneous|反义词：relevant, pertinent, germane


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and therefore doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It only changes in form to agree with the noun it modifies (e.g., nonrelevant information, nonrelevant details).


PHRASE->
nonrelevant information: 不相关的信息
nonrelevant details: 不相关的细节
nonrelevant data: 不相关的数据
nonrelevant discussion: 不相关的讨论
nonrelevant arguments: 不相关的论点
