sinologist:/ˌsaɪˈnɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |汉学家；中国问题专家 | A sinologist is a scholar who studies China, its language, history, culture, or society.  Sinologists often specialize in a particular area, such as Chinese literature, history, or politics. | Example: Professor Li is a renowned sinologist specializing in Tang Dynasty poetry. 李教授是一位研究唐诗的著名汉学家。 |近义词：China scholar, expert on China |反义词：无
