carbon-dated:/ˈkɑːrbənˌdeɪtɪd/ | adj. |碳定年法的；经碳定年法测定的 | Determined using carbon dating, a method used to determine the age of an object containing organic material by using the properties of radiocarbon (a radioactive isotope of carbon).  | 例句：The ancient artifact was carbon-dated to 3000 BC.  这件古代文物经碳定年法测定为公元前3000年。

The shroud was carbon-dated, revealing its actual age.  裹尸布经过碳十四年代测定，揭示了它的实际年龄。


近义词：radiocarbon-dated

反义词：N/A


词源:

* **carbon:** From Latin *carbo* "coal, charcoal".  This refers to the carbon isotope used in the dating process.

* **-dated:**  The suffix "-dated" indicates something is assigned a date, often through a specific method.  It's derived from the verb "to date," which in this context means to determine the age of something.
