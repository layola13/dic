resave:/ˌriːˈseɪv/| vt.|重新保存，再次保存|To save something again, typically after it has been accidentally deleted or changed.  This implies a previous saving action.|例句：I had to resave the document after my computer crashed. 我电脑崩溃后不得不重新保存文档。

例句：Please resave the file with a different name. 请用不同的文件名重新保存文件。


近义词：save again, re-save, save anew|反义词：delete, erase|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resaves his work every ten minutes. = 他每十分钟就重新保存一次他的工作。
She resaves the document before closing the program. = 她关闭程序前重新保存文档。

一般过去时:
I resaved the file after making the changes. = 我做了修改后重新保存了文件。
They resaved the project before the power outage. = 停电前他们重新保存了项目。

现在完成时:
I have resaved the presentation several times. = 我已经多次重新保存了演示文稿。
She has resaved the image after editing it. = 她编辑图片后重新保存了它。

过去完成时:

将来时:
