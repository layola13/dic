circ:/sɜːrk/| n.|圆周，环行，循环|a roughly circular line, route, or movement.|The planets move in circles around the sun. 行星绕太阳做圆周运动。|circle, orbit, cycle|straight line


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water circulates in the system.  = 水在这个系统中循环。
The blood circulates throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环。


一般过去时:
The news circulated quickly. = 新闻迅速传开。
The rumor circulated among the students. = 谣言在学生中流传。


现在完成时:
The rumor has circulated widely. = 谣言已经广泛流传。
The information has circulated throughout the company. = 信息已在公司内部广泛传播。


过去完成时:
The story had circulated for years before I heard it. = 我听到这个故事之前，它已经流传多年了。
The news had circulated before the official announcement. = 官方宣布之前，消息就已经传开了。


将来时:
The article will circulate widely online. = 这篇文章将在网上广泛传播。
The information will circulate to all employees. = 信息将传达给所有员工。


虚拟语气:
If the news circulated faster, we could have reacted sooner. = 如果消息传播得更快，我们本可以更快地做出反应。
I wish the information had circulated more efficiently. = 我希望信息能更有效地传播。


被动语态:
The pamphlet was circulated among the attendees. = 小册子在与会者中分发。
The document is being circulated for review. = 文件正在分发以供审查。


疑问句:
Does the air circulate properly in this room? = 这个房间的空气流通是否良好？
How is the information circulated within the organization? = 信息在组织内部是如何传播的？


否定句:
The blood doesn't circulate properly in his leg. = 他的腿部血液循环不好。
The rumor didn't circulate beyond our small group. = 谣言没有在我们的小圈子之外传播。



PHRASE->

circulate a rumor = 散布谣言
