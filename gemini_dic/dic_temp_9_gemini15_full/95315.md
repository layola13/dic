incautiousness:/ˌɪnˈkɔːʃəsnəs/| n. |轻率；不谨慎；鲁莽|Lack of caution; carelessness; recklessness.  It often leads to undesirable consequences due to a lack of forethought or consideration of potential risks.|His incautiousness in handling the chemicals resulted in a minor explosion.  他的处理化学品时轻率导致了一次小爆炸。|近义词：rashness, recklessness, carelessness, heedlessness|反义词：caution, prudence, carefulness
