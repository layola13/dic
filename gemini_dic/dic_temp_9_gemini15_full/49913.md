denar:/ˈdɛnɑːr/| n. |第纳尔（货币单位）|A denar is a monetary unit, historically used in several countries, most notably in the former Yugoslavia and currently in North Macedonia.  It's also the name of a historical Roman coin.|Example:  The Macedonian denar is the official currency of North Macedonia.  马其顿第纳尔是北马其顿的官方货币。|近义词：dinar (similar sounding, often used interchangeably)|反义词：None (as it's a unit of currency, not something with direct opposites)|
