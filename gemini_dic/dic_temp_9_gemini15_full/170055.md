proteranthous:/ˌproʊtəˈrænθəs/ | adj. |先花的；先开花的 |Producing flowers before leaves.  This term describes plants that develop flowers before their leaves fully emerge.  The flowers are often prominent and showy, attracting pollinators early in the growing season. | Example:  Many bulbous plants are proteranthous, their blooms appearing before the leaves unfurl.  许多球茎植物是先开花的，它们的花在叶子展开之前就出现了。|近义词：precocious (in flowering)|反义词：posteranthous (leaves before flowers)
