nonlyricalnesses:/ˌnɒnˈlɪrɪkəlˌnɛsɪz/| n.|非抒情性，非抒情特征|The absence of lyrical qualities; the state or quality of being nonlyrical.  It refers to a lack of qualities associated with lyric poetry, such as emotional expressiveness, musicality, and personal feeling.|例句：The nonlyricalnesses of the poem made it difficult to connect with the speaker's emotions. 这首诗缺乏抒情性，难以与说话者的情感产生共鸣。 The piece, despite its musicality, lacked the emotional depth and personal expression associated with lyrical works; its nonlyricalnesses were quite striking. 这作品虽然具有音乐性，但却缺乏与抒情作品相关的深度情感和个人表达；其非抒情性非常引人注目。|近义词：unpoeticness, prosaicness, matter-of-factness|反义词：lyricism, musicality, expressiveness|名词
