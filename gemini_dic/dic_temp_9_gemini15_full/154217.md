peripheric:/pɛrɪˈfɛrɪk/ | adj. | 周围的；外围的；次要的 | Relating to or situated on the edge or periphery of something.  Peripheral things are not of central importance. | Example: The peripheric villages were largely unaffected by the city's growth.  周边的村庄在很大程度上不受城市发展的影響。  |近义词：marginal, outer, secondary | 反义词：central, core, essential
