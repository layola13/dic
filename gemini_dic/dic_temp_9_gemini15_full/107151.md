laetere:/læˈtɛəri/| v.|欢欣鼓舞，高兴|Rejoice; be glad.  The word is a Latin imperative, meaning "rejoice!" or "be glad!". It is often used in religious contexts.|例句：Laetere in Domino semper iterum dicam, Laetere! = Rejoice in the Lord always; again I say, Rejoice! (Rejoice in the Lord always; I say it again, Rejoice!)  |近义词：rejoice, exult, jubilate |反义词：mourn, grieve, lament |动词
