microprocessor:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈprɑːsɛsər/|/ˈmaɪkrəʊˈprəʊsɛsə(r)/| n. |微处理器|A microprocessor is a central processing unit (CPU) on a single integrated circuit.  It is the brain of a computer, responsible for executing instructions and performing calculations.|Example: Modern smartphones contain powerful microprocessors that enable a wide range of applications.  现代智能手机包含功能强大的微处理器，可以实现各种应用。|近义词：CPU, central processing unit, chip |反义词：None  (no direct antonym)|名词
