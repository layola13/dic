learning:/ˈlɜːrnɪŋ/ | n. |学习；学习过程；知识；技能 | The process of acquiring knowledge or skills.  It can refer to the act of studying, the knowledge gained, or the skills developed. |例句：Learning a new language takes time and effort. 学习一门外语需要时间和精力。

例句：His years of learning culminated in a successful career. 他多年的学习最终成就了他成功的事业。


近义词：study, acquisition, education, training | 反义词：ignorance, unlearning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Learning is a lifelong process. = 学习是一个终生的过程。
She is learning to play the piano. = 她正在学习弹钢琴。


一般过去时:
I learned a lot from that experience. = 我从那次经历中学到了很多。
He learned to swim when he was five. = 他五岁的时候学会了游泳。


现在完成时:
I have learned a lot about history. = 我已经学习了很多关于历史的知识。
She has been learning English for three years. = 她学习英语已经有三年了。


过去完成时:
I had learned all the vocabulary before the test. = 我在考试前已经学习了所有的词汇。
They had been learning the song for weeks before the concert. = 他们在音乐会之前已经学习这首歌好几个星期了。


将来时:
I will be learning French next semester. = 下个学期我将学习法语。
She will have learned all the material by the end of the course. = 到课程结束时，她将已经学习完所有材料。


虚拟语气:
If I had learned more, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我学得更多，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had learned to play the guitar when I was younger. = 我真希望我年轻的时候学会了弹吉他。


被动语态:
The lesson was learned the hard way. = 这课是吃尽苦头才学到的。
New skills are constantly being learned throughout life. = 终其一生，人们都在不断学习新的技能。


疑问句:
What are you learning at school? = 你在学校学什么？
Is she learning anything useful? = 她学到什么有用的东西了吗？


否定句:
He wasn't learning anything new. = 他没有学习任何新东西。
They haven't been learning effectively. = 他们学习效率不高。
