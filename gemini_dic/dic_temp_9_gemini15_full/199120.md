silencers:/ˈsaɪlənsərz/| n.|消音器，消声器|A silencer is a device fitted to a firearm to reduce the noise of the gunshot.  It works by muffling the sound of the expanding gases. |例句：The assassin used a silencer on his pistol to avoid detection.  刺客在他的手枪上使用了消音器以避免被发现。

例句：Many countries have strict regulations on the use of silencers.  许多国家对消音器的使用有严格的规定。|近义词：suppressor, muffler|反义词：amplifier
