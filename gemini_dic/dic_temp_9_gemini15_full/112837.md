lucimeter:/luːˈsɪmɪtər/ | n. |照度计，测光计 | An instrument used to measure the intensity of light, especially in photography or scientific research.  | Example: The photographer used a lucimeter to ensure the correct exposure for the photograph. 摄影师使用照度计来确保照片的正确曝光。  |近义词：photometer, light meter | 反义词：无
