diagram:/ˈdaɪəɡræm/| n. |图表，示意图 |A diagram is a simplified drawing showing the main features of something, typically used to explain how something works or is structured.  It visually represents information or data. |Example: The teacher drew a diagram on the board to explain the process of photosynthesis.  老师在黑板上画了一个图表来解释光合作用的过程。|近义词：chart, graph, illustration, sketch|反义词：description (detailed verbal explanation)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The diagram shows the parts of a flower. = 这个图表显示了花的各个部分。
This diagram explains how the engine works. = 这个图表解释了引擎的工作原理。

一般过去时:
The engineer presented a diagram of the new bridge. = 工程师展示了新桥的示意图。
We studied the diagram carefully before starting the project. = 我们在开始项目之前仔细研究了图表。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
I had already studied the diagram before the lecture. = 我在讲座之前已经研究过图表了。
