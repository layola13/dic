disemployment: /ˌdɪsɪmˈplɔɪmənt/ | n. | 失业；解雇 | The state of being unemployed; the act of dismissing someone from employment.  |例句：The recent economic downturn has led to widespread disemployment. 近期经济衰退导致了广泛的失业。

例句：The factory closure resulted in mass disemployment among the workers. 工厂倒闭导致大量工人失业。


近义词：unemployment, joblessness, redundancy, dismissal


反义词：employment, hiring, recruitment

词性：名词
