mudir:/muːˈdɪər/ | n. |警长，主管，负责人 |The word "mudir" is an Arabic word meaning "director," "manager," "supervisor," or "head."  It can refer to a person in charge of a specific department or area, often in a governmental or administrative context.  The specific meaning can vary depending on the context. |例句：The mudir of the police station oversaw the investigation.  警察局局长负责调查。  The school mudir announced the new rules. 学校主管宣布了新规章。 |近义词：director, manager, supervisor, superintendent, chief |反义词：subordinate, employee
