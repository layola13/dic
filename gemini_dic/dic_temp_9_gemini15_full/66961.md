experimentalist:/ɪkˌsperɪˈmɛntəlɪst/ | n. |实验者，实验主义者 | A person who conducts experiments, especially in science.  Someone who emphasizes experimentation in their approach to a field of study or practice. | Example:  The experimentalist carefully documented the results of each trial.  实验者仔细记录了每次试验的结果。  |近义词: researcher, scientist, investigator |反义词: theorist
