词源:

The word "lintel" comes from Old French *lintel*,  ultimately deriving from Latin *limen, līminis* meaning "threshold," "door-sill,"  or "lintel."  The semantic shift from threshold to lintel is a natural one, as both are horizontal structural elements near a doorway.
