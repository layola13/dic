elitism:/ɪˈliːtɪzəm/| n. |精英主义；精英统治|The belief that a society or system should be led by an elite.  This ideology often involves the idea that only a select few possess the necessary intelligence, talent, or moral character to govern effectively. It can manifest in various forms, including social, political, and economic hierarchies.  |Example: The country's education system has been criticized for its elitism, favoring students from wealthy backgrounds.  这个国家的教育体系因其精英主义而受到批评，它偏袒富裕家庭的学生。|近义词：aristocracy, snobbery, classism |反义词：egalitarianism, populism, meritocracy|
