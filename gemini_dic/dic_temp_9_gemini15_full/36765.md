colluding:/kəˈluːdɪŋ/ | v. |共谋；串通；勾结 |  Secretly cooperating for an illegal or dishonest purpose.  | The executives were accused of colluding to fix prices.  高管们被指控串通起来操纵价格。 | conspiring, plotting, conniving | cooperating honestly, acting independently | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are colluding to defraud the government. = 他们正在串通起来欺诈政府。
We are not colluding; we are working independently. = 我们没有串通；我们是在独立工作。


一般过去时:
The two companies colluded to monopolize the market. = 这两家公司串通起来垄断市场。
He colluded with his brother to steal the money. = 他与他的兄弟串通起来偷钱。


现在完成时:
They have colluded to hide the evidence. = 他们串通起来隐藏证据。
The investigation has revealed that they have been colluding for years. = 调查显示他们多年来一直在串通。


过去完成时:
It turned out that they had colluded before the deal was signed. = 事实证明，他们在交易签署之前就已经串通了。
The police discovered that the suspects had colluded to commit the crime. = 警方发现嫌疑人串通犯案。


将来时:
They will collude to manipulate the election results. = 他们将串通起来操纵选举结果。
We suspect they will collude to suppress the truth. = 我们怀疑他们会串通起来压制真相。


虚拟语气:
If they had not colluded, the scandal would not have happened. = 如果他们没有串通，就不会发生丑闻。
I wish they hadn't colluded to deceive us. = 我真希望他们没有串通起来欺骗我们。


被动语态:
The evidence suggests that the price was colluded upon. = 证据表明价格是经过串通的。
It is believed that the witnesses were colluded against. = 人们相信证人受到了串通陷害。


疑问句:
Were they colluding to get rid of their competitor? = 他们是不是串通起来除掉他们的竞争对手？
Are the officials colluding with the criminals? = 官员们是否与罪犯勾结？


否定句:
The investigation found no evidence that they were colluding. = 调查没有发现他们串通的证据。
We deny any accusations of colluding with our rivals. = 我们否认与竞争对手串通的任何指控。
