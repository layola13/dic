kaffiyehs:/kæfiːjɛz/ | n. | 卡菲耶（阿拉伯头巾） | A kaffiyeh is a traditional Arab headdress, usually made of a checkered cotton scarf. It is often worn by men in the Middle East and is associated with Palestinian identity.  |  He wore a traditional kaffiyeh. 他戴着一顶传统的卡菲耶。  |  keffiyeh, shemagh |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
