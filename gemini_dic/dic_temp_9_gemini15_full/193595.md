self-assertively:/ˌsɛlf əˈsɜːrtɪvli/| adv. |自信地，坚定地，果断地 |Acting in a self-assured and confident manner, often to express one's needs and opinions without being aggressive.  |例句：She self-assertively demanded a raise, outlining her contributions to the company. 她自信地要求加薪，并列举了她对公司的贡献。

例句：He self-assertively defended his ideas in the meeting, despite opposition from others. 他在会议上自信地捍卫了自己的想法，尽管遭到其他人的反对。


近义词：confidently, decisively, assertively, boldly

反义词：submissively, timidly, hesitantly, meekly
