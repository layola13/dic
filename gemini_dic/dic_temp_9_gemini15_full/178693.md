recrossing:/ˌriːˈkrɒsɪŋ/ | n. | 再穿过，重新越过 | The act of crossing again;  passing over or through something a second time.  |例句：The recrossing of the river proved more difficult than the initial crossing.  重新渡河比最初渡河更困难。

例句：After a brief detour, they made a recrossing of the mountain trail.  短暂绕行后，他们重新翻越了山路。


近义词：repassing, returning across, crossing again | 反义词：initial crossing, first passage | 词性: 名词
