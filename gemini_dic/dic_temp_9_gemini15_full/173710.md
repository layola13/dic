quasi-miraculous: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ mɪˈrækjʊləs/ | adj. | 近乎奇迹的，不可思议的 |


英文解析:  Describing something that is almost miraculous; exhibiting characteristics that are very close to being miraculous, though perhaps not quite reaching the level of a true miracle.  It implies an element of surprise, unexpectedness, and often a sense of wonder or amazement.


例句:

The plant's recovery was quasi-miraculous, considering the extent of the damage.
考虑到损坏程度，植物的恢复堪称奇迹。


His escape from the burning building was a quasi-miraculous event.
他从燃烧的建筑物中逃脱是一件近乎奇迹的事件。


近义词:  amazing, astonishing, incredible, improbable, extraordinary, phenomenal, marvelous


反义词:  ordinary, commonplace, expected, predictable, mundane


词性: adjective
