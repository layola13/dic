goatherd:/ˈɡoʊθərd/| n.|牧羊人（专门放山羊的）|A goatherd is a person who herds goats.  It is a relatively simple occupation, but one that requires patience and skill in managing a flock of animals. Goatherds often live a nomadic or semi-nomadic lifestyle, following their herds to areas with fresh pasture.  |例句：The goatherd led his flock across the mountain.  牧羊人带领他的羊群穿过山脉。

The old goatherd told fascinating stories of his life in the mountains. 年迈的牧羊人讲述了他令人着迷的山区生活故事。|近义词：shepherd (if tending sheep), herdsman|反义词：None specifically, but words implying a sedentary lifestyle could be considered antonyms.  |名词
