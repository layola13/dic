escritoire:/ˈɛskrɪtwɑːr/| n. |写字台；化妆台|A writing desk, especially one with a sloping top and drawers.  An escritoire is a type of writing desk, often with a fall-front or hinged lid that conceals compartments for writing materials. They are often elegant and ornate pieces of furniture.|The antique escritoire was a beautiful piece of craftsmanship.|近义词：writing desk, secretary, bureau|反义词：None.
