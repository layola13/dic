garfish:/ˈɡɑːrfɪʃ/| n.|尖嘴鱼；长颌鱼|A garfish is a slender, long-snouted fish with a long, thin jaw.  They are found in both saltwater and freshwater environments.|例句：The garfish darted through the seaweed.  尖嘴鱼在海藻中穿梭。|例句：We caught several garfish while fishing near the shore.我们在岸边钓鱼时钓到几条尖嘴鱼。|近义词：needlefish,  billfish|反义词：无
