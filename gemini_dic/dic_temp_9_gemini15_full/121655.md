misphrases:/ˈmɪsˌfreɪzɪz/ | n. | 措辞不当；语句错误；表达不当 | Incorrect or inappropriate phrasing; a wrongly worded statement or expression.  Misphrases are often caused by poor word choice, grammatical errors, or a lack of clarity.  They can lead to misunderstandings and misinterpretations. | 例句：His misphrases during the interview made a bad impression. 他在面试中的措辞不当留下了不好的印象。

The repeated misphrases in her essay revealed a lack of editing.  她文章中反复出现的措辞错误暴露了她缺乏编辑能力。 | 近义词：malapropisms, solecisms, blunders, misstatements | 反义词：accurate phrasing, precise wording, clear expression |  n.
