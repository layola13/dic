garron:/ˈɡærən/| n.|劣等马；老马|A garron is a small, inferior horse, often old and worn out.  It is typically a type of pony, suggesting a less valuable and less refined horse.|例句：The farmer rode his old garron to market.  这个农民骑着他那匹老劣马去市场。|The old man's garron was tired and slow.  老人的劣马又累又慢。|近义词：nag, cob, pony|反义词：stallion, thoroughbred
