give-away:/ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ | n., v. |赠品；泄露；赠送；放弃 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "giveaway" refers to something given free, often as a promotional item.  It can also refer to something that unintentionally reveals a secret or truth. As a verb, "give away" means to give something to someone free of charge, or to unintentionally reveal a secret.

例句:
The company offered free giveaways at the trade show. 公司在贸易展上提供免费赠品。

The nervous twitch in his eye was a giveaway that he was lying. 他眼睛里紧张的抽搐暴露了他正在撒谎。


She decided to give away her old clothes to charity. 她决定把旧衣服捐给慈善机构。

He gave away the ending of the movie. 他泄露了电影的结局。


近义词:
(n.) freebie, handout, present, clue, indication
(v.) donate, distribute, reveal, divulge, betray


反义词:
(n.) purchase, secret, mystery
(v.) keep, conceal, hide, retain


词性:
Noun, Verb
