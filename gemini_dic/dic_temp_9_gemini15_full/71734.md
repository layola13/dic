flaws:/flɔːz/| n. |缺点；瑕疵；缺陷 |A flaw is an imperfection or weakness and can refer to a physical defect, a mistake, or a moral failing.  It implies something is not perfect or ideal, often hindering its function or value. |例句：The painting has a few flaws, but it's still beautiful. 这幅画有一些瑕疵，但它仍然很漂亮。

The diamond has a tiny flaw that is barely visible. 这颗钻石有一个几乎看不见的小瑕疵。


近义词：defect, imperfection, fault, blemish, shortcoming |反义词：perfection, excellence
