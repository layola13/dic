lodestar:/ˈloʊdstɑːr/| n. | 指北星；向导；目标 | A lodestar is a star, especially the North Star, that guides sailors.  It can also refer to a guiding principle or a person who inspires and guides others. | Example:  The North Star served as a lodestar for ancient mariners. 指北星曾是古代水手的向导。 The principles of justice were his lodestar in life. 正义原则是他人生的指路明灯。 |近义词：guiding star, beacon, guide, inspiration |反义词：misdirection, hindrance, obstacle |
