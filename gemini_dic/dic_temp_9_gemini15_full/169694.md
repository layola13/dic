propounded:/prəˈpaʊndɪd/| v.|提出，阐述|To propound is to put forward or offer (an idea, theory, or plan) for consideration or discussion.  It suggests a formal or serious presentation. |The professor propounded a new theory on the origin of the universe. 教授提出了一种关于宇宙起源的新理论。|He propounded his views on education to the committee. 他向委员会阐述了他对教育的看法。|近义词：put forward, propose, suggest, advance|反义词：suppress, withhold, conceal|动词
