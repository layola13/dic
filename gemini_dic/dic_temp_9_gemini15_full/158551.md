plete:/pliːt/|adj.|完整的，完全的，彻底的|complete means having all the necessary parts or elements; finished or ended.|The report is complete. = 报告已经完成了。|  The task is now complete. = 任务现在完成了。|incomplete, unfinished, partial|finished, perfect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The project is complete. = 项目完成了。
The form is complete. = 表格填写完整了。

一般过去时:
They completed the task on time. = 他们按时完成了任务。
She completed her studies last year. = 她去年完成了学业。

现在完成时:
We have completed the first phase of the project. = 我们已经完成了项目的第一个阶段。
He has completed his marathon training. = 他已经完成了马拉松训练。

过去完成时:
They had completed the report before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经完成了报告。
She had completed her homework before watching TV. = 她做完家庭作业之后才看电视。

将来时:
We will complete the project next week. = 我们下周将完成这个项目。
They will complete their journey soon. = 他们很快就会完成他们的旅程。

虚拟语气:
If he completed the assignment, he would pass the course. = 如果他完成了作业，他就能通过考试。
I wish I had completed the project earlier. = 我希望我早点完成这个项目。

被动语态:
The project was completed on schedule. = 项目按计划完成了。
The building was completed last year. = 这栋建筑是去年建成的。

疑问句:
Is the application complete? = 申请表填写完整了吗？
Are the instructions complete? = 说明完整吗？


否定句:
The report is not complete. = 报告不完整。
The task is not yet complete. = 任务尚未完成。
