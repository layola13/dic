mis-practicing:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪsɪŋ/| v. |误诊，渎职，行为不端 |The act of engaging in unprofessional or unethical conduct, especially in a professional context, such as medicine or law.  It often involves negligence, incompetence, or deliberate wrongdoing. |例句：The doctor was accused of mis-practicing and lost his license.  医生被指控渎职并吊销了执照。

The lawyer was found guilty of mis-practicing after falsifying evidence. 律师在伪造证据后被判犯有渎职罪。


近义词：malpractice, misconduct, wrongdoing, professional negligence|反义词：professionalism, ethical conduct, best practice
