chondropterygian:/ˌkɒndrəˈtɛəriɡiən/| adj.|软骨鱼类的|Relating to or characteristic of the Chondrichthyes, a class of fish characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton.  The term encompasses sharks, rays, and chimaeras.| Example: Chondropterygian fishes have skeletons made of cartilage, not bone. 软骨鱼类的骨骼由软骨而非骨头构成。|近义词: cartilaginous fish|反义词: osteichthyan (bony fish)
