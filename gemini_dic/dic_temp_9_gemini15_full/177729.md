recaption:/ˌriːˈkæpʃən/| n.|重新夺回；再捕获；重新占据|The act of recapturing something, especially a person or territory previously lost or escaped.  It can also refer to a summary or repetition of something. |例句：The police are conducting a recaption operation to recover the stolen goods. 警方正在进行重新夺回被盗物品的行动。

例句：The professor gave a recaption of the main points of the lecture. 教授对讲座的主要内容进行了总结。


近义词：recapture, recovery, retake, summary, recapitulation|反义词：loss, surrender, release|名词
