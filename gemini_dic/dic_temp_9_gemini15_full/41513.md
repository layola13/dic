corollet:/kəˈrɒlət/ | n. | 小花冠；副花冠 | A small corolla, especially one of the florets of a composite flower head.  It is a modified petal structure found in some flowers. | Example: The individual florets in a sunflower head each possess a tiny corollet.  向日葵花头中的单个小花都具有一个微小的副花冠。|近义词：corolla (in some contexts), ray floret|反义词：无
