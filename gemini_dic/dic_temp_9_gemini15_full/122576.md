moderator: /ˈmɒdəreɪtə(r)/, /ˈmɑːdəreɪtər/ | n. |主持人，调解人，版主| A person who leads or guides a discussion, meeting, or online forum; someone who moderates.  They ensure order and fairness, often by controlling the flow of conversation and preventing disruptions.  |例句：The moderator kept the debate focused on the key issues. 主持人让辩论集中在关键问题上。

例句：The online forum moderator removed offensive posts.  在线论坛版主删除了攻击性帖子。

近义词：chairperson, facilitator, arbiter, mediator, administrator

反义词：disruptor, agitator
