octodont:/ˈɒktədɒnt/| n. |八齿动物|A rodent belonging to the family Octodontidae, characterized by having eight lower incisors.  These animals are typically found in South America and are known for their distinctive appearance and burrowing habits.|Example: The degu is a common example of an octodont found in Chile.|近义词： rodent (a broader term)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with animals lacking this dental characteristic.
