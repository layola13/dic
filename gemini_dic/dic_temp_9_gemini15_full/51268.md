desulphurize:/ˌdiːsʌlˈfjʊəraɪz/| vt.|脱硫；除硫|To remove sulfur from (a substance).  The process often involves chemical reactions or physical methods to separate sulfur compounds from the material. |例句：The refinery uses a complex process to desulphurize the crude oil.  这家炼油厂使用复杂工艺来脱硫原油。
The coal needs to be desulphurized before burning to reduce air pollution. 这煤在燃烧前需要脱硫以减少空气污染。|近义词：desulfurize, sulfur removal|反义词：sulfurize, add sulfur|动词
