settles:/ˈsɛtəlz/ | vt. vi. n.|解决；定居；沉淀；安顿；平静下来|to reach a solution to a problem or dispute; to establish oneself in a place; to sink to the bottom of a liquid; to become calm or quiet|例句：The dispute was finally settled out of court. 争端最终庭外和解了。  The birds settled on the branches. 鸟儿落在树枝上。|近义词：resolve, establish, subside, calm |反义词：agitate, disturb, escalate, unsettle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He settles his accounts every month. 他每月结算账目。
The dust settles slowly in the still air. 灰尘在静止的空气中慢慢沉降。

一般过去时:
They settled in a small village after retirement. 退休后，他们在一个小村庄定居。
The argument finally settled down after an hour.  争论一个小时后终于平息了。

现在完成时:
The issue has been settled amicably. 问题已友好地解决了。
The family has settled into their new home. 这家人已经适应了他们的新家。

过去完成时:
The matter had been settled before the meeting. 会议之前此事已解决。
By the time the police arrived, the crowd had settled down. 等警察到达时，人群已经平静下来了。

将来时:
The problem will settle itself in time. 这个问题迟早会自行解决。
We will settle in the countryside next year. 我们明年将在乡下定居。

虚拟语气:
If he settled his debts, he would feel much better. 如果他偿还了债务，他会感觉好得多。
I wish this noise would settle down. 我希望这个噪音能安静下来。

被动语态:
The matter was settled quickly and efficiently. 这件事迅速有效地解决了。
The dispute was settled by arbitration. 争议通过仲裁解决。

疑问句:
Has the argument been settled? 争论解决了吗？
Will they settle in the city or the countryside?  他们会在城市定居还是在乡下定居？

否定句:
The problem hasn't been settled yet. 问题还没有解决。
The dust didn't settle for hours. 灰尘好几个小时都没沉淀下来。
