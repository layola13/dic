heals:/hiːlz/| v.|治愈，愈合|To restore to health or soundness; cure; to become healthy or sound again.|例句：The wound is slowly healing. 伤口正在慢慢愈合。|例句：The medicine helped to heal his infection. 这药帮助治愈了他的感染。|近义词：cure, mend, recover|反义词：injure, harm, worsen


USAGE->

This cream heals dry skin effectively. = 这款面霜有效治愈干燥的皮肤。
