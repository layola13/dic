outsell:/ˌaʊtˈsɛl/| vt.|胜过…的销量，比…卖得好 |To sell more of something than another person or company.|例句：Our new product outsells all competitors. 我们的新产品销量超过所有竞争对手。

例句：Last year, that model outselled all others in its class.  去年，那款车型销量超过了同级别所有其他车型。


近义词：outsold, surpass, exceed, top|反义词：undersell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This book outsells all others in its category. = 这本书的销量超过了同类其他所有书籍。
Their new phone consistently outsells its predecessor. = 他们的新款手机销量持续超过其前代产品。


一般过去时:
That song outselled every other song on the album. = 那首歌的销量超过了专辑中其他所有歌曲。
The smaller company outselled the larger one in the last quarter. = 在上个季度，这家较小的公司销量超过了较大的公司。


现在完成时:
This artist has outselled millions of records worldwide. =这位艺术家在全球的唱片销量已达数百万张。
The company has outselled its rivals for three consecutive years. =该公司连续三年销量超过竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had outselled their initial sales projections. =到年底，他们的销量已经超过了最初的销售预测。
Before the new marketing campaign, they had outselled their competitors by a small margin. =在新营销活动开始之前，他们的销量略微超过了竞争对手。


将来时:
Analysts predict that this product will outsell its competitors next year. =分析师预测这款产品明年将超过竞争对手的销量。
We believe our new strategy will enable us to outsell the market leader. =我们相信我们的新战略将使我们能够超越市场领导者的销量。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we might have outselled our rivals. =如果我们投资更多，我们本可以超过竞争对手的销量。
I wish our product had outselled theirs. =我希望我们的产品销量能超过他们的。


被动语态:
Their product was outselled by our innovative design. =他们的产品被我们的创新设计在销量上超越了。
The old model was quickly outselled by the new one. =旧款很快就被新款在销量上超越了。


疑问句:
Does this brand outsell its competitors in this region? =这个品牌在这个地区是否销量超过竞争对手？
Will our new marketing campaign help us outsell the competition? =我们的新营销活动能否帮助我们超越竞争对手的销量？


否定句:
Our sales figures show that we did not outsell the market leader. =我们的销售数据显示我们没有超过市场领导者的销量。
Unfortunately, our product failed to outsell its main competitor. =不幸的是，我们的产品未能超过其主要竞争对手的销量。


PHRASE->
outsell the competition = 超过竞争对手的销量
outsell the market = 占据市场销量领先地位
outsell expectations = 超出预期销量
significantly outsell = 大幅领先销量
consistently outsell = 持续领先销量
