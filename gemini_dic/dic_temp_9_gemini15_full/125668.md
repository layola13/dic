munch:/mʌntʃ/| vt. vi.|大声咀嚼；用力嚼；|To chew noisily or with force.|He munched on an apple.|近义词：chew, gnaw, masticate|反义词：sip, nibble

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He munches his food loudly. = 他大声咀嚼食物。
She munches on carrots all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在吃胡萝卜。


一般过去时:
The dog munched on the bone happily. = 狗高兴地啃着骨头。
I munched a sandwich during the break. = 我在休息时间吃了一个三明治。


现在完成时:
He has munched through half the bag of chips. = 他已经吃掉了半袋薯片。
She has munched her way through a whole box of chocolates. = 她已经吃掉了一整盒巧克力。


过去完成时:
They had munched all the cookies before the party started. = 派对开始前他们已经吃掉了所有饼干。
The children had munched their snacks before their nap. = 孩子们在午睡前吃完了零食。


将来时:
I will munch on popcorn while watching the movie. = 看电影的时候我会吃爆米花。
They will munch their way through the buffet. = 他们会尽情享用自助餐。


虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would munch on an apple. = 如果我饿了，我会吃个苹果。
I wish I could munch on this delicious cake without gaining weight. = 我希望我能吃这个美味的蛋糕而不会长胖。


被动语态:
The apples were munched on by the children. = 苹果被孩子们吃掉了。
The entire plate of cookies was munched clean. = 整盘饼干都被吃光了。


疑问句:
Are you munching on something? = 你在吃什么东西吗？
What are they munching on so noisily? = 他们在嚼什么东西那么大声？


否定句:
He didn't munch on the vegetables. = 他不吃蔬菜。
She doesn't munch her food loudly. = 她不大声咀嚼食物。
