dictate:/dɪkˈteɪt/ | vt. | 口述；命令；支配；决定 | To speak or read aloud (words) for another person to write down. To order or impose something forcefully. To influence or determine (something). |例句：The teacher dictated a letter to the students. 老师给学生们口述了一封信。
The dictator dictated his terms. 独裁者强加了他的条件。


例句：The conditions dictated our actions. 环境决定了我们的行动。


近义词：pronounce, prescribe, impose, determine | 反义词：obey, follow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dictates letters to his secretary every day. 他每天都口述信件给他的秘书。
The boss dictates the work schedule. 老板决定工作安排。


一般过去时:
She dictated the terms of the contract. 她口述了合同条款。
The general dictated his orders to the troops. 将军向部队下达了命令。


现在完成时:
The author has dictated the entire manuscript. 作者已经口述了整篇手稿。
She has dictated the fashion trends for years. 她多年来一直主导时尚潮流。


过去完成时:
He had dictated the speech before the meeting. 他在会议前已经口述了演讲稿。
The government had dictated the economic policy for decades. 政府几十年来一直掌控着经济政策。


将来时:
The CEO will dictate the company's future direction. 首席执行官将决定公司的未来方向。
I will dictate my will to my lawyer. 我将把我的遗嘱口述给我的律师。


虚拟语气:
If the market dictated the price, it would be lower. 如果市场决定价格，价格就会更低。
I wish he wouldn't dictate my actions. 我希望他不必控制我的行动。


被动语态:
The terms of the agreement were dictated by the stronger party.  协议条款是由实力较强的一方决定的。
His opinion was dictated by his prejudices. 他的意见受他的偏见所左右。


疑问句:
Does the government dictate the educational curriculum? 政府是否决定教育课程？
Will the weather dictate our plans for the weekend? 天气会影响我们周末的计划吗？


否定句:
The teacher didn't dictate the answers. 老师没有口述答案。
The company does not dictate its employees' personal lives. 公司不会干涉员工的私人生活。


PHRASE->
dictate to  向…口述
dictate terms  规定条件
dictate policy 制定政策
dictate events 左右局势
dictate one's will  立遗嘱
