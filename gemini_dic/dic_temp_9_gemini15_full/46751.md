dandyise:/ˈdændiˌaɪz/| v.|使变得花哨；打扮得漂漂亮亮 |To make something or someone look excessively fashionable or stylish, often in a way that is considered ostentatious or affected.|例句：He dandyised his appearance for the party. 他为了派对打扮得花枝招展。 He dandyised the room with unnecessary ornaments. 他用不必要的装饰品把房间装点得过于华丽。|近义词：embellish, adorn,  frivolish |反义词：simplify, plain, unadorned
