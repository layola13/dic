previolated: /ˌpriːˈvaɪəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 受到侵犯的；被违反的 |  Describes something that has been violated or broken, often referring to a law, agreement, or right.  It implies a previous state of integrity that has been compromised. |

例句：

His rights were previolated by the unfair arrest. = 他在不公平的逮捕中受到了侵犯。

The treaty was previolated, leading to international tensions. = 条约被违反，导致国际紧张局势。


近义词：infringed, violated, transgressed, breached


反义词：respected, upheld, honored, obeyed


词性：形容词
