jinping:/tʃɪnˈpɪŋ/| n. |习近平|This is a proper noun referring to Xi Jinping, the current General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, President of the People's Republic of China, and Chairman of the Central Military Commission.|Xi Jinping is a powerful leader. 习近平是一位强大的领导人。|  |  | noun


USAGE->
This word is a proper noun and thus doesn't conjugate like verbs or adjectives.  Grammatical usage is determined by the sentence structure in which it appears.  Examples below demonstrate its usage in different sentence types:

Declarative Sentence:
Xi Jinping is the President of China.  = 习近平是中国国家主席。

Interrogative Sentence:
Is Xi Jinping attending the summit? = 习近平会出席峰会吗？

Exclamatory Sentence:
Xi Jinping!  What a powerful figure! = 习近平！多么有权势的人物！

Passive Voice (using a verb phrase including "jinping"):
Policies were implemented under Xi Jinping's leadership.  = 这些政策是在习近平领导下实施的。


PHRASE->

Xi Jinping Thought: 习近平思想 (This is a significant phrase referring to his political ideology.)
Xi Jinping's leadership: 习近平的领导 (This phrase refers to his role as a leader.)
Xi Jinping era: 习近平时代 (This refers to the period of his leadership.)
under Xi Jinping: 在习近平领导下 (This prepositional phrase indicates something happened during his tenure.)
the Xi Jinping administration: 习近平政府 (This refers to the government under his leadership.)
