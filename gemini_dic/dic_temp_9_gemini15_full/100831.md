introductorily:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌktərəli/ | adv. | 导言式地；引介式地；作为介绍 | In a way that serves as an introduction or beginning.  It suggests a preliminary or initial approach to a topic or subject. | Example: He spoke introductorily about the project before delving into the details. 他在深入探讨细节之前，简单介绍了这个项目。 | 近义词：preliminarily, initially, by way of introduction | 反义词：conclusively, finally
