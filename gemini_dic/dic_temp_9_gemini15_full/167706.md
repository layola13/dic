pries:/praɪz/| vt.|撬，撬动，费力地弄开|to lever or force open (something), especially with a tool.|例句：He pried the lid off the jar. 他费力地撬开了罐子的盖子。|例句：She pried the window open with a screwdriver. 她用螺丝刀撬开了窗户。|近义词：lever, pry open, force open|反义词：close, shut, seal
