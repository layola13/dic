shrugged:/ʃrʌɡd/| v.|耸肩，表示不在乎|To raise one's shoulders slightly to express indifference, lack of knowledge, or doubt.|He shrugged his shoulders when asked about the missing money.  他被问及丢失的钱时耸了耸肩。|She shrugged off the criticism. 她对批评不屑一顾。|近义词：flick, twitch|反义词:  None specifically, but antonyms might include showing clear agreement or understanding.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shrugs his shoulders when he doesn't understand. = 他不明白的时候耸耸肩。
They shrug off their responsibilities. = 他们逃避责任。


一般过去时:
She shrugged her shoulders in response. = 她耸了耸肩作为回应。
He shrugged off the insult. = 他对侮辱置之不理。


现在完成时:
He has shrugged off many challenges in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多挑战。
She has shrugged off the rumors. = 她对谣言不屑一顾。


过去完成时:
He had shrugged off the warning signs. = 他对警告信号置之不理。
She had shrugged off the pain until it became unbearable. = 她一直忍着疼痛，直到疼痛难以忍受。


将来时:
He will shrug off the criticism, as usual. = 他会像往常一样对批评置之不理。
She will probably shrug and walk away. = 她可能会耸耸肩然后走开。


虚拟语气:
If he knew the answer, he wouldn't shrug his shoulders. = 如果他知道答案，他就不会耸肩了。
I wish I hadn't shrugged off his advice. = 我真希望我没有忽视他的建议。


被动语态:
The question was shrugged off by the speaker. = 这个问题被演讲者忽略了。
His concerns were shrugged off by his colleagues. = 他的担忧被他的同事们忽略了。


疑问句:
Did he shrug his shoulders when you asked him? = 当你问他的时候，他耸肩了吗？
Why did she shrug off the offer? = 她为什么拒绝了这个提议？


否定句:
He didn't shrug; he answered directly. = 他没有耸肩；他直接回答了。
She didn't shrug off the responsibility; she accepted it. = 她没有逃避责任；她接受了它。


PHRASE->
shrug off:  忽略，不理会
shrug something aside:  把…放在一边
shrug one's shoulders: 耸肩
give a shrug:  耸耸肩
a shrug of the shoulders:  耸肩的动作
