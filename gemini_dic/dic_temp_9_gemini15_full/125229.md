multiculturalist: /ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | n. |多元文化主义者；多元文化论者 | A person who advocates for or supports multiculturalism.  Someone who believes that different cultures should coexist and be respected within a society. |  例句：Many multiculturalists argue that embracing diversity enriches society. 许多多元文化主义者认为，拥抱多样性会丰富社会。


例句：She's a strong multiculturalist and believes in the importance of cultural exchange. 她是一位坚定的多元文化主义者，相信文化交流的重要性。


近义词：cultural pluralist, integrationist


反义词：nativist, assimilationist, isolationist


词性:名词
