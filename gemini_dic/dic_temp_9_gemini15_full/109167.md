legitimation: /ləˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 使合法化；合法性；证明合法 | The act of making something legitimate or acceptable; the state of being legitimate.  |The legitimation of the new government was crucial for stability. 新政府的合法化对于稳定至关重要。 The court questioned the legitimation of the evidence. 法院质疑证据的合法性。 | justification, validation, authorization | illegitimacy, invalidity, invalidation | 名词

一般现在时:
The government works on the legitimation of its policies. = 政府致力于使其政策合法化。
This process requires careful legitimation. = 这个过程需要仔细地合法化。

一般过去时:
The king's marriage legitimized his heir. = 国王的婚姻使他的继承人合法化。
The company's actions were later legitimized by the court. = 该公司的行为后来被法院合法化。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
