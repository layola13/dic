outgreening:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnɪŋ/| v.|超越，胜过（在环保方面）|To surpass or exceed in environmental friendliness or sustainability.|The new building's design outgreens all previous attempts at sustainable architecture.|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass |反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company outgreens its competitors in waste reduction. = 这家公司在垃圾减量方面胜过其竞争对手。
Our new policy outgreens the previous one in environmental protection. = 我们的新政策在环境保护方面胜过之前的政策。


一般过去时:
Their project outgreened all other entries in the sustainability competition. = 他们的项目在可持续性竞赛中胜过了所有其他参赛作品。
Last year's efforts outgreened the previous year's in terms of carbon footprint reduction. = 去年在减少碳足迹方面的努力胜过了前年。


现在完成时:
Our city has outgreened many other cities in its commitment to renewable energy. = 我们的城市在对可再生能源的承诺方面已经超越了许多其他城市。
The company has outgreened its rivals by implementing a comprehensive recycling program. = 通过实施全面的回收计划，该公司已经超越了其竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the decade, they had outgreened their initial sustainability goals. = 到十年末，他们已经超越了他们最初的可持续性目标。
Before the new regulations, no company had outgreened the industry leader in water conservation. = 在新的法规出台之前，没有一家公司在节水方面能够超越行业领导者。


将来时:
We will outgreen our competitors with our innovative green technology. = 我们将凭借我们的创新绿色技术超越我们的竞争对手。
This new initiative will outgreen all previous attempts at urban greening. = 这项新的倡议将超越所有以往的城市绿化尝试。


虚拟语气:
If we invested more in renewable energy, we would outgreen our neighboring cities. = 如果我们对可再生能源投资更多，我们将超越我们邻近的城市。
I wish our company had outgreened its rivals sooner. = 我希望我们公司早点超越竞争对手。


被动语态:
Our city was outgreened by another city in terms of air quality. = 我们的城市在空气质量方面被另一个城市超越。
The company's efforts were outgreened by a smaller startup. = 该公司的努力被一家较小的初创公司超越。


疑问句:
Does our new strategy outgreen our previous efforts? = 我们的新战略是否超越了我们之前的努力？
Will this new technology outgreen existing solutions in energy efficiency? = 这项新技术能否在能源效率方面超越现有解决方案？


否定句:
Their efforts did not outgreen the industry standards. = 他们的努力没有超越行业标准。
The project failed to outgreen the previous attempts. = 该项目未能超越之前的尝试。


PHRASE->

outgreen the competition = 超越竞争对手
outgreen the industry standard = 超越行业标准
outgreen previous efforts = 超越之前的努力
striving to outgreen = 努力超越
outgreen in sustainability = 在可持续性方面超越
