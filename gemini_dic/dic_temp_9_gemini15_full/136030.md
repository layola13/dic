nonreappointment:/ˌnɒnˌriːəˈpɔɪntmənt/| n.|未续聘，未再次任命|The act of not reappointing someone to a position or role.  It implies a decision not to renew a previous appointment.|例句：His nonreappointment to the board came as a surprise to many. 他的董事会职位未获续聘令许多人感到意外。


例句：The professor's nonreappointment sparked protests from students. 教授未获续聘引发了学生的抗议。|近义词：nonrenewal, dismissal (in some contexts), termination|反义词：reappointment, renewal, reappointment|词性 n.
