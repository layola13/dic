defuse:/diˈfjuːz/ | vt. |化解（危机）；使（炸弹等）失效；缓和（紧张局势）| To remove the fuse from (a bomb, etc.); to make (a dangerous situation) less dangerous or tense. |例句：The police successfully defused the bomb.  警察成功地拆除了炸弹。

例句：The mediator helped defuse the tense situation between the two countries. 调解员帮助缓和了这两个国家之间的紧张局势。

近义词：neutralize, disarm, alleviate, mitigate

反义词：escalate, inflame, aggravate, exacerbate

词性:动词

词源:
The word "defuse" comes from the verb "fuse," meaning to melt or unite by heat.  The prefix "de-" indicates removal or reversal.  Therefore, "defuse" originally meant to remove a fuse, and the meaning has expanded to encompass the broader sense of removing or lessening danger or tension.
