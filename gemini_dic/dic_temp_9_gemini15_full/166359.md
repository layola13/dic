preremunerate:/ˌpriːrɪˈmjuːnəreɪt/| vt.|预先酬谢；预付报酬|To pay or reward someone in advance for services rendered or to be rendered.  The word implies a payment made before the work is completed, often as an incentive or guarantee.|例句：The company preremunerated the consultants for their work on the project. = 公司预先支付了顾问们项目的报酬。


The company preremunerated the artist a significant sum before the completion of the mural. = 公司在壁画完工前预付了艺术家一大笔钱。|近义词：prepay, advance, compensate in advance|反义词：post-pay, delay payment|动词
