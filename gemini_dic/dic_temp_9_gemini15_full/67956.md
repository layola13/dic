facileness: /ˈfæsɪlnəs/ | n. |容易；轻而易举；熟练 |The state or quality of being facile; ease or dexterity.  It implies a lack of depth or effort.  |例句：The facileness with which he solved the problem surprised everyone. 他轻而易举地解决了这个问题，让每个人都感到惊讶。


例句：The surprising facileness of her response suggested a lack of genuine understanding. 她反应的轻率暗示了她缺乏真正的理解。


近义词：ease, fluency, dexterity, readiness, simplicity


反义词：difficulty, complexity, arduousness, laboriousness


词性：名词
