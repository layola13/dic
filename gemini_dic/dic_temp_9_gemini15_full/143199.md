outcourting:/ˌaʊtˈkɔːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 超越；胜过 |  The act of surpassing or exceeding someone or something, especially in a competitive context.  It implies a significant degree of superiority. This word is not commonly used and may be considered archaic or newly coined.  | 例句：Their outcourting of the rival team was undeniable, securing them the championship. = 他们超越对手队的表现是无可否认的，为他们赢得了冠军。
Their outcourting in innovation led to a market dominance. = 他们在创新方面的超越导致了市场的主导地位。| 近义词：outshining, surpassing, outperforming, excelling | 反义词：underperforming, failing, losing
