percutaneously:/ˌpɜːrkjuˈteɪniəsli/| adv. |经皮地，穿透皮肤地 |Through the skin.  This adjective describes a medical procedure or process that is performed through the skin, without the need for a surgical incision. | Example: The medication was administered percutaneously.  药物是经皮给药的。|近义词：transdermally, through the skin |反义词：surgically, intravenously
