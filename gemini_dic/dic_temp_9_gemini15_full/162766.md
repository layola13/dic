prebiotic:/ˌpriːˈbaɪɒtɪk/|/ˌpriːˈbaɪɑːtɪk/| n. |益生元| A non-digestible food ingredient that promotes the growth of beneficial microorganisms in the intestines.  Prebiotics are typically fibers that feed the "good" bacteria in your gut, helping them thrive and improve your digestive health. | Example:  Many fruits and vegetables contain prebiotics that support gut health. 许多水果和蔬菜都含有益生元，有助于肠道健康。|近义词：probiotic (though technically different), dietary fiber|反义词：antibiotic
