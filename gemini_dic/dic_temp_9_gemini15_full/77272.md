gastrostomy:/ɡæsˈtrɒstəmi/| n. |胃造口术；胃造口| A surgical procedure in which a feeding tube is placed through the abdominal wall and into the stomach. This allows for the delivery of nutrition and medication directly into the stomach when a person cannot eat or drink normally. | Example: The doctor recommended a gastrostomy for the patient who was unable to swallow due to a stroke.  医生建议为因中风而无法吞咽的病人进行胃造口术。|近义词：gastric feeding, percutaneous endoscopic gastrostomy (PEG)|反义词：N/A
