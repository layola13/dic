outcome: /ˈaʊtˌkʌm/ | n. |结果，结局，成果 |The final outcome of the election is still uncertain.  选举的最终结果仍然不确定。|近义词：result, consequence, effect |反义词：cause, origin


The outcome is the result or consequence of a process or event. It refers to what happens as a result of something else.  It can be a positive or negative result, depending on the context.


例句：

The outcome of the meeting was a decision to postpone the project.  会议的结果是决定推迟项目。

The positive outcome of her hard work was a promotion. 她努力工作的积极结果是升职。


词性：名词
