centerboards:/ˈsɛntərˌbɔːrdz/ | n. |中板（船）|A retractable board or plate fitted to the keel of a sailboat to prevent sideways drift.|The centerboard on our sailboat needs to be lowered before we can sail upwind.  我们的帆船上的中板需要放下才能逆风航行。|近义词: keel, fin |反义词: rudder (in terms of function, not direct antonym)
