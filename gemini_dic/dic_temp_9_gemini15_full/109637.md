lettings:/ˈlɛtɪŋz/| n. |出租，租赁（尤指房产）|The act of renting out property, typically residential or commercial.  It can also refer to the properties themselves that are available for rent.  |例句：The lettings agent found us a suitable apartment.  = 中介帮我们找到了一套合适的公寓。

例句：Our lettings business is booming this year. = 今年我们的租赁业务蓬勃发展。


近义词：rentals, leases, tenancies |反义词：purchases, sales |词性:名词
