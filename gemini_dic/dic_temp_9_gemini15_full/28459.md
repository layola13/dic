carburised:/ˈkɑːrbəraɪzd/|/ˈkɑːrbəraɪzd/| adj.|渗碳的；渗碳处理过的| Treated with carbon to increase hardness.  This process is typically used to harden the surface of steel components while maintaining a softer core.|The gears were carburised to improve their wear resistance. = 齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐磨性。The carburised layer on the surface provided excellent protection against abrasion. = 表面上的渗碳层提供了极好的抗磨损保护。| case-hardened, surface-hardened|soft, unhardened|
