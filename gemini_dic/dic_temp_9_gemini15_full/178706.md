recruitable: /rɪˈkruːtəbəl/ | adj. | 可招募的，适于征召的 |  capable of being recruited; suitable for recruitment.  This word describes individuals or resources that are eligible and suitable for recruitment into a particular organization, group, or cause.  It implies a willingness or suitability to join,  often based on skills, qualifications, or other desirable attributes. |


例句：

The company is looking for recruitable candidates with strong technical skills.  这家公司正在寻找拥有强大技术技能的可招募候选人。

The army needs a large pool of recruitable young men and women.  军队需要大量适于征召的青年男女。


近义词：eligible, suitable, qualified, available


反义词：unsuitable, ineligible, unavailable


词性：形容词
