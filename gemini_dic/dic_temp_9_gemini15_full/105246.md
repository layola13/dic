khalifa:/kəˈliːfə/| n. |哈里发，卡里发|The title of the head of state in some Muslim countries, especially those where the ruler is considered to be a successor to the Prophet Muhammad.  It's also used as a surname.|例句：Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan was the Emir of Abu Dhabi and President of the United Arab Emirates.|近义词：caliph, imam (in some contexts)|反义词：None (the word itself doesn't have a direct antonym).
