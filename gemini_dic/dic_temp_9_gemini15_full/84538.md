hamitic:/hæˈmɪtɪk/| adj.|含米特的；閃含語系的|Relating to the Hamitic languages, a proposed family of Afro-Asiatic languages spoken in North Africa and parts of East Africa. The classification is now largely considered outdated and inaccurate by linguists.|The proposed Hamitic languages included Berber, Cushitic, and Chadic languages.  However, this classification is now widely rejected.|近义词：Afro-Asiatic (in a broader, more accepted context)|反义词：N/A
