nonhereditariness:/ˌnɒnˌhɛrɪtəˈrɛrɪnəs/| n. |非遗传性，非遗传|The lack of inheritance; the characteristic of not being inherited genetically.  It refers to traits or characteristics that are not passed down from parents to offspring through genes.  This can be due to environmental factors, learned behaviors, or other non-genetic influences.|例句：The study explored the nonhereditariness of certain behavioral traits in the population. 这项研究探讨了人群中某些行为特征的非遗传性。


The nonhereditariness of the disease suggests that environmental factors play a significant role.  这种疾病的非遗传性表明环境因素起着重要作用。|近义词：noninheritance, acquiredness|反义词：heritability, inheritance
