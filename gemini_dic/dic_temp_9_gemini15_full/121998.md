misspend:/mɪsˈspend/| vt.|滥用，浪费|To use or spend something badly or wrongly, often in a way that is wasteful or harmful.|例句：He misspent his youth on drugs and alcohol. 他年少时沉迷于毒品和酒精，浪费了青春。|例句：Don't misspend your money on frivolous things. 不要把钱浪费在毫无意义的事情上。|近义词：waste, squander, fritter away|反义词：save, conserve, utilize wisely|动词
