overdecorating: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度装饰；装饰过度 |The act of decorating something excessively, to the point of being cluttered or gaudy.  It implies that the decoration detracts from the overall aesthetic rather than enhancing it. |The living room was overdecorated; every surface was covered with knick-knacks.  客厅装饰过度；每个表面都摆满了小摆设。| decorating, embellishing |minimalism, simplicity |动词
