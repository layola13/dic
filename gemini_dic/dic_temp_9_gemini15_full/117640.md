megathere:/ˈmɛɡəθɪər/| n. |巨型动物|A megathere is any member of the extinct order of mostly large, ground-dwelling mammals, including giant ground sloths, glyptodonts, and related forms.  They were predominantly herbivores, although some may have been omnivores.  Megatheres flourished during the Pleistocene epoch and went extinct at the end of that era, likely due to climate change and human hunting.|The megatheres of South America were truly impressive creatures.|近义词：giant ground sloth (for specific examples)|反义词：None (as it refers to an extinct group)
