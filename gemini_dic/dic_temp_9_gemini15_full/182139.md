remitter:/rɪˈmɪtər/| n. |汇款人，支付者|The person or entity sending money.  A remitter is the individual or organization initiating a payment transaction, typically through a bank or other financial institution.|例句：The remitter of the check was identified as John Smith. = 支票的汇款人是约翰·史密斯。
例句：The bank needs the remitter's address to process the transaction. = 银行需要汇款人的地址来处理交易。|近义词：payer, sender, payer, originator|反义词：payee, recipient

词源:
The word "remitter" comes from the verb "remit," meaning "to send or transmit (money)."  "Remit" itself ultimately derives from the Latin word "remittere," meaning "to send back, let go, release, forgive."  The suffix "-er" signifies the agent or doer of the action.
