prearrested:/ˌpriːəˈrɛstɪd/| adj.| 预先逮捕的，未经正式逮捕的 |Prearrested refers to a situation where someone is detained or held before a formal arrest is made.  This often happens in situations where there is suspicion of wrongdoing but not yet enough evidence for a full arrest.  It can also describe a state of being held before an official arrest warrant is issued.|例句：The suspect was prearrested at the airport and questioned for hours before being formally charged.  嫌疑人在机场被预先逮捕，并在正式指控前被讯问数小时。


The suspect remained prearrested overnight pending further investigation. 嫌疑人被预先拘留了一夜，等待进一步调查。|近义词：detained, held, apprehended (before formal arrest)|反义词：released, acquitted, freed
