craftiness:/ˈkræftinəs/| n. |狡猾；灵巧；精明 |Skill in deception; cunning; cleverness.  It refers to the skill in achieving one's ends by deceit or evasion.  This can involve cleverness, skill, and resourcefulness, but always with a connotation of trickery or dishonesty.|例句：His craftiness allowed him to outsmart his opponents.  他的狡猾使他能够智胜对手。

The politician's craftiness was evident in his carefully worded speeches.  这位政治家的狡猾在他的措辞谨慎的演讲中显而易见。|近义词：shrewdness, cunning, guile, slyness |反义词：honesty, straightforwardness, ingenuousness
