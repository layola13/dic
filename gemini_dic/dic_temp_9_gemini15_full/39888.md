constituters:/kənˈstɪtʃuːtərz/| n. |构成者，组成部分|Those who constitute something; the components or elements that make up a whole.  The word refers to the individuals or things that form or create a larger entity. |例句：The diverse group of artists were the main constituters of the vibrant art scene.  那些多元化的艺术家群体是充满活力的艺术场景的主要构成者。The various molecules are the constituters of this complex compound. 各种分子是这种复杂化合物的构成部分。|近义词：components, constituents, elements, ingredients, factors|反义词：destroyers, dismantlers, disintegrators|名词
