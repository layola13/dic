invaginated: /ɪnˈvædʒɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 内陷的；内套的；内褶的 |  Describes a structure that has been turned inside out or folded inward.  It often refers to a biological structure, such as a part of an organ or a cell membrane. |

例句：
The invaginated gut of the embryo develops into the intestines. = 胚胎内陷的肠道发育成为肠。

The invaginated cell membrane formed a vesicle. = 内陷的细胞膜形成了一个囊泡。


近义词：infolded, inturned, recessed

反义词：everted, evaginated, protruded


词性：形容词
