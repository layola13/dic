proscience:/ˈproʊsaɪəns/| adj.|亲科学的，支持科学的|Supporting or advocating for science and its methods.  It emphasizes the importance of evidence-based reasoning and the scientific method in decision-making and policy.|例句：The proscience movement actively combats misinformation and promotes scientific literacy. 亲科学运动积极打击虚假信息并推广科学素养。

例句：Her proscience stance led her to support funding for scientific research.  她支持科学的立场促使她支持科学研究的经费。

近义词：scientific, evidence-based, rational, empirical

反义词：antiscience, pseudoscientific, irrational


词性:形容词
