nervomuscular: /ˌnɜːrvoʊˈmʌskjʊlər/ | adj. | 神经肌肉的 | Relating to or affecting both nerves and muscles.  This term describes the interaction between the nervous system and the muscular system. It often refers to the connection between a motor neuron and a muscle fiber at the neuromuscular junction, where nerve impulses trigger muscle contraction.

例句：

Nervomuscular diseases can cause weakness and fatigue. = 神经肌肉疾病会导致虚弱和疲劳。

The neuromuscular junction is crucial for voluntary movement. = 神经肌肉接头对于随意运动至关重要。


近义词：neuromuscular (synonym)


反义词：None (No direct antonym exists, as it describes a specific physiological relationship.)


词性：形容词
