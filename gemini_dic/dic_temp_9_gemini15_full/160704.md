portmanteaux:/ˌpɔːrtˈmænˌtoʊz/ | n. | 行李箱；（两个词合并而成的）混成词 | A portmanteau is a large suitcase, typically one that opens into two compartments.  It also refers to a word blending the sounds and combining the meanings of two others.  For example, "smog" is a portmanteau of "smoke" and "fog". | Example:  She packed her belongings into her portmanteau before the journey. 她旅行前把她的东西都装进了她的旅行箱。 The word "brunch" is a portmanteau of "breakfast" and "lunch".  “早午餐”这个词是“早餐”和“午餐”的混成词。 | 近义词：suitcase, bag, blend, hybrid | 反义词：N/A
