multihomer:/ˈmʌltiˌhoʊmər/ | adj. | 多宿主；多家庭的 | Having or using multiple home networks or connections.  A multihomer is a computer or network device that has multiple connections to different networks, often for redundancy, increased bandwidth, or to access different network resources. |  Example: The server is a multihomer, connected to both the internal network and the internet.  这个服务器是一个多宿主，连接到内部网络和互联网。| 近义词：multi-homed,  多连接的 | 反义词：single-homed 单连接的 |  形容词
