coldblooded:/ˈkoʊldˈblʌdɪd/| adj.|冷血的；残酷的；(动物)冷血的|Having or showing a lack of feeling or sympathy; cruel.  It can also refer to animals that are ectothermic (relying on external sources for body heat).|例句：The murderer showed a coldblooded disregard for human life.  凶手表现出对人命的冷酷无情。|例句：Snakes are coldblooded creatures. 蛇是冷血动物。|近义词：heartless, ruthless, callous, unfeeling |反义词：compassionate, empathetic, warmhearted
