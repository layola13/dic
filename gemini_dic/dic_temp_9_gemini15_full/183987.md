reshipment's:/riːˈʃɪpməntz/| n. |再装运；再发货；再运送 |The process of shipping something again, typically after an initial shipment has encountered a problem.  It refers to the act of reshipping or the goods being reshipped.  The 's' indicates possessive.  For example, "the reshipment's delay" refers to the delay of a reshipment. |The reshipment's arrival was delayed due to bad weather.  = 由于恶劣天气，再发货延误了。 The customer requested a reshipment's tracking number. = 客户要求提供再发货的追踪号码。|shipment, delivery, forwarding|no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with "cancellation" or "return"|n.
