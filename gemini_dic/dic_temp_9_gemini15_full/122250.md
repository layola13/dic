mite:/maɪt/| n.| 螨虫；极少量；可怜的人 | A mite is a very small arachnid, related to spiders and ticks.  It can also refer to a tiny amount of something or a small, insignificant person. | Example: The dust mite is a common allergen.  尘螨是一种常见的过敏原。  There's only a mite of sugar left. 只剩下一点点糖了。 |近义词：arachnid, speck, particle, bit |反义词：abundance, plethora
