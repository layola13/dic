dreamtime:/ˈdriːmtaɪm/ | n. | 梦时；创世时期；澳洲原住民的神话时代 |  Dreamtime refers to the Aboriginal Australian concept of a timeless period in which ancestral beings created the world and everything in it. It's not just a time in the past, but a spiritual realm that exists alongside the present, influencing the present world.  It encompasses creation stories, mythology, and the spiritual connection between people and the land. | Example: The Dreamtime stories explain the origin of the landscape and the animals.  梦时故事解释了景观和动物的起源。|近义词：creation myth, ancestral period, spiritual realm |反义词：现实(present reality)
