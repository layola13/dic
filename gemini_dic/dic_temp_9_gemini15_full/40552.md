controverters:/kənˈtrɒvɜːtərz/| n.|争论者，辩论者|Those who engage in controversy or debate, often expressing opposing views.  People who like to argue or dispute.|例句：The controversial new law brought forth many heated arguments from controverters on both sides.  这项有争议的新法律引发了双方争论者的许多激烈争论。


例句：The meeting became a shouting match between the two controverters. 会议变成了两个争论者之间的喊叫比赛。


近义词：debaters, disputants, arguers, antagonists


反义词：agreeing parties, conciliators


词性：名词
