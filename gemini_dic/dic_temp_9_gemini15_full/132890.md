nonelectives:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktɪvz/ | n. | 非选修课 | Non-electives refer to courses or subjects that are required as part of a curriculum and cannot be chosen by the student.  They are mandatory components of a program of study. | Example:  Math and science are nonelectives in this engineering program.  这个工程项目中，数学和科学是非选修课。 |近义词：required courses, compulsory subjects, mandatory classes |反义词：electives, optional courses
