purchased:/ˈpɜːrtʃəst/| v.|购买，购得|To buy something.|I purchased a new car last week. 我上周买了一辆新车。|bought, acquired|sold, forfeited|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He purchases groceries every Saturday. 他每周六都买杂货。
She purchases her coffee from a local shop. 她从当地一家商店买咖啡。


一般过去时:
They purchased a house in the suburbs. 他们在郊区买了一栋房子。
I purchased a new phone online. 我在网上买了一部新手机。


现在完成时:
She has purchased all the necessary ingredients. 她已经购买了所有必要的食材。
We have purchased tickets to the concert. 我们已经买好了音乐会的票。


过去完成时:
He had purchased the tickets before the prices went up. 价格上涨之前，他已经买好了票。
They had purchased the land several years ago. 几年前他们就买下了那块地。


将来时:
I will purchase a new laptop next month. 我下个月会买一台新的笔记本电脑。
They will purchase a new car when they save enough money. 他们攒够钱后会买一辆新车。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would purchase a bigger house. 如果我有更多钱，我会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish I had purchased that dress when I saw it. 我真希望我看到那件衣服时就买下来了。


被动语态:
The tickets were purchased online. 票是在网上买的。
The house was purchased for a high price. 这栋房子以高价售出。


疑问句:
Did you purchase the item? 你买了那件商品吗？
Where did they purchase the materials? 他们在哪里购买的材料？


否定句:
I did not purchase anything today. 我今天什么也没买。
She hasn't purchased a new dress yet. 她还没买新衣服。
