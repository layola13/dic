misdesert:/ˌmɪsˈdɛzərt/| n.|应得的惩罚；应得的报应|Lack of desert; ill desert.  It refers to a punishment or misfortune deserved because of wrongdoing or bad actions.  It emphasizes the deserved nature of the negative consequence.|例句：His misdesert was a life of poverty and hardship. 他的应得的惩罚是贫困和艰辛的生活。

His misdeserts followed him throughout his life.他的恶报伴随了他的一生。|近义词：punishment, retribution, recompense|反义词：reward, blessing, good fortune|名词
