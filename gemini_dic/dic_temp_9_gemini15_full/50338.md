deployed:/dɪˈplɔɪd/ | vt. | 部署；展开；调动；运用 | To put (something) into action or use; to move (troops or equipment) into position for military action.  | 例句：The army deployed its troops along the border.  军队沿边境部署了部队。

例句：The company deployed a new marketing strategy. 公司实施了一项新的营销策略。


近义词：implement, utilize, dispatch, station | 反义词：withdraw, recall, remove
