docusoaps:/ˈdɒkjuˌsoʊps/| n. |纪实肥皂剧|A genre of television programming that blends documentary-style filming with the dramatic storytelling techniques of soap operas.  These shows often feature real people in real-life situations, but with elements of editing and narrative structure that enhance the drama and conflict. |Example:  The success of "Laguna Beach" helped popularize the docusoap genre.  《橘子郡男孩》的成功帮助普及了纪实肥皂剧这一类型。|近义词：reality TV, reality show |反义词：fiction, scripted drama
