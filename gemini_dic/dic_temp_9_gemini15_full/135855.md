nonprosperity:/ˌnɒnprɒsˈpɛrɪti/| n. |非繁荣；不景气；萧条|The state or condition of not being prosperous; lack of prosperity; adversity.  It often refers to economic hardship or decline.  |例句：The country is experiencing a period of nonprosperity due to the recent economic crisis.  这个国家由于最近的经济危机而经历了一段不景气时期。|例句：Years of nonprosperity have left the community struggling.  多年的萧条让这个社区苦苦挣扎。|近义词：adversity, hardship, depression, recession, downturn |反义词：prosperity, affluence, boom, growth
