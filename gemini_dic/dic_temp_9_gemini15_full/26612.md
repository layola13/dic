cachexic:/kæˈsɛksɪk/| adj.|恶病质的，消瘦的|extremely thin and weak, usually because of illness|The cachexic patient was barely able to stand.|近义词：emaciated, wasted, gaunt|反义词：robust, healthy, well-nourished


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is cachexic due to his long illness. = 他因为长期生病而消瘦。
The patient presents as cachexic. = 病人表现出恶病质。


一般过去时:
She was cachexic after months of battling cancer. = 她与癌症斗争数月后变得非常消瘦。
The doctor noted that the patient was cachexic. = 医生注意到病人消瘦。


现在完成时:
He has become cachexic since the onset of the disease. = 自从患病以来，他变得非常消瘦。
She has been cachexic for several years now. = 她已经消瘦好几年了。


过去完成时:
He had been cachexic for some time before his diagnosis. = 在确诊之前，他已经消瘦了一段时间。
The patient had already become cachexic by the time treatment began. = 在治疗开始时，病人已经非常消瘦了。


将来时:
Without treatment, he will likely become cachexic. = 不治疗，他很可能会变得非常消瘦。
She will probably remain cachexic unless her condition improves. = 除非她的病情好转，否则她可能会一直消瘦。


虚拟语气:
If he received proper nutrition, he wouldn't be cachexic. = 如果他得到适当的营养，他就不会如此消瘦。
I wish he weren't so cachexic. = 我希望他不那么消瘦。


被动语态:
The patient was found to be cachexic. = 发现病人消瘦。
His condition was described as cachexic. = 他的病情被描述为恶病质。


疑问句:
Is the patient cachexic? = 病人消瘦吗？
Does he appear cachexic to you? = 他看起来消瘦吗？


否定句:
He is not cachexic; he's just thin. = 他不是恶病质，只是瘦。
She is not currently cachexic. = 她目前不消瘦。
