grandpas:/ˈɡrænpɑːz/| n. |爷爷们；外公们|The plural form of "grandpa," referring to multiple grandfathers or grandfathers in general.  It is a collective noun.  |My grandpas always told me stories about their childhood. 我的爷爷们总是给我讲他们童年时代的故事。|grandfathers, old men|young people


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My grandpas play golf every weekend. = 我的爷爷们每个周末都打高尔夫球。
The grandpas in the park are feeding the pigeons. = 公园里的爷爷们正在喂鸽子。


一般过去时:
My grandpas visited us last Christmas. = 我的爷爷们去年圣诞节来看我们了。
The grandpas watched the children play in the sandbox. = 爷爷们看着孩子们在沙箱里玩耍。

The grandpas have already left for the day. = 爷爷们已经离开一天了。


过去完成时:
