leptothrix:/ˈlɛptoθrɪks/| n.|纤毛菌属|Leptothrix is a genus of bacteria known for their ability to oxidize iron and manganese, often forming sheaths around their cells.  These bacteria are found in various aquatic environments and play a significant role in biogeochemical cycles.|Example: Leptothrix species are often found in iron-rich waters, contributing to the formation of iron deposits. 纤毛菌属通常存在于富含铁的水域中，有助于铁矿的形成。|近义词：Gallionella (similar iron-oxidizing bacteria)|反义词：无
