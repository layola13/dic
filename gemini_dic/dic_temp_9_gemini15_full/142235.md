orograph:/ˈɔːrəɡræf/ | n. |地形图；山岳地形|A representation or description of the mountains or hills of a region.  Orography is the scientific study of mountains, their formation, and their features. The term can also refer to a map or chart showing the mountains and hills of an area. |Example: The orograph clearly showed the rugged terrain of the region.  地形图清晰地显示了该地区的崎岖地形。|近义词：topographical map, relief map |反义词：planimetric map (a map showing only flat features)
