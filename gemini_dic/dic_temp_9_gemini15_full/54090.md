disembay:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbeɪ/| v.|使…脱离海湾；使…与海湾隔绝|To remove from a bay or to separate from a bay; to enclose or shut off from access to a bay.|The new dam disembayed the once-accessible fishing grounds. 新建的水坝使以前易于到达的渔场与海湾隔绝。|近义词：isolate, separate, enclose|反义词：open, connect
