foraged:/fɔːrɪdʒd/ | v. | 觅食；搜寻；搜集 | To search for food or provisions.  | 例句：The animals foraged for food in the forest. 动物们在森林里觅食。
The hikers foraged for berries along the trail. 徒步旅行者沿着小路寻找浆果。| 近义词：search, hunt, gather, scrounge | 反义词：provide, supply

词源:
The word "forage" comes from the Old French word "fourrage," meaning "fodder" or "forage."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "fodrum," which also means "fodder."  The shift in meaning from fodder to the act of searching for food is a natural semantic extension.
