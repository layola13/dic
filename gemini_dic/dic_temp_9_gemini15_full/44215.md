crocidolite:/ˈkrɒsɪdəlaɪt/| n. |蓝石棉|A blue fibrous amphibole mineral, a variety of riebeckite, often containing inclusions of other minerals.  It is a type of asbestos and is known for its potential to cause serious health problems, including lung cancer and asbestosis. |The old factory was found to be contaminated with crocidolite, leading to a major cleanup operation.|近义词：riebeckite, blue asbestos|反义词：无
