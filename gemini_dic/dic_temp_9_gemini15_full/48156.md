decommercialize: /ˌdiːkəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪz/ | vt. |使非商业化；取消商业化 | To remove from commercial influence or control; to eliminate commercial aspects or practices from.  |例句：The government is attempting to decommercialize healthcare, making it more accessible to the poor. 政府正试图使医疗保健非商业化，使其更容易为穷人所及。 | The city council voted to decommercialize the waterfront, preventing further development of private businesses. 市议会投票决定使滨水区非商业化，以防止私人企业进一步发展。|近义词：discommercialize,  deregulate (in some contexts)|反义词：commercialize, monetize | 动词
