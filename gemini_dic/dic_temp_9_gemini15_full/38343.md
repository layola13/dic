conceivers:/kənˈsiːvərz/ | n. | 构想者；设想者；孕育者 | Those who conceive, imagine, or plan something.  They can be inventors, artists, parents, or anyone who brings something new into existence. | The conceivers of the project were finally able to see it come to fruition.  这个项目的构想者们终于看到它实现了。

The brilliant conceivers behind the new technology deserve recognition.  创造这项新技术的杰出构想者们值得认可。


近义词：originators, inventors, creators, planners, designers

反义词：destroyers, dismantlers


词性：名词
