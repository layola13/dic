/ˈmʌltɪplaɪ/ | v. | 乘；繁殖；增加 | To increase in number or amount; to perform the mathematical operation of multiplication. | The bacteria multiplied rapidly in the warm broth. 细菌在温暖的肉汤中迅速繁殖。  The company's profits multiplied after the successful launch of their new product. 公司在新产品成功推出后利润倍增。 | increase, augment, reproduce | decrease, diminish | 动词


USAGE->
