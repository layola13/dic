segmental:/ˌseɡˈmɛntl/| adj.| 分节的；分段的；音段的 | Relating to or consisting of segments; specifically, in phonetics, relating to the sounds of speech that are produced by the articulation of the vocal tract.  | Example: Segmental phonology studies the individual sounds of a language.  音段语音学研究语言的单个音素。|近义词：sectional, divided, discrete |反义词：suprasegmental, continuous
