rammy:/ˈræmi/| n.|争吵；骚乱|A noisy quarrel or commotion; a brawl.  It often implies a somewhat disorganized and possibly humorous fight or argument.|例句：There was a bit of a rammy outside the pub last night. 昨晚酒吧外发生了一点小争吵。|例句：The kids got into a right rammy over the last cookie. 孩子们为了最后一块饼干大吵了一架。|近义词：brawl, fracas, scuffle, row|反义词：peace, calm, tranquility
