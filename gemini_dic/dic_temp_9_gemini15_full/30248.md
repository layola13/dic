cellulose:/ˈsɛljuˌloʊs/| n. |纤维素|A complex carbohydrate that forms the main structural component of the cell walls of plants.|例句：Wood is largely composed of cellulose. 木材主要由纤维素构成。

例句：Cotton fibers are almost pure cellulose. 棉花纤维几乎是纯纤维素。


近义词：fiber, carbohydrate|反义词：无
