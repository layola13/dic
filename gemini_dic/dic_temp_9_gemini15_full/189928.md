sanatoria: /ˌsænəˈtɔːriə/ | n. |疗养院（复数）|Sanatoria are institutions providing medical care and treatment, especially for those with chronic illnesses or needing rehabilitation.  They often have a focus on rest and recovery in a therapeutic environment. |The sanatoria in the mountains were known for their fresh air and restorative qualities.  那些山上的疗养院以其新鲜的空气和恢复活力而闻名。|  hospitals, clinics, health resorts | prisons, factories
