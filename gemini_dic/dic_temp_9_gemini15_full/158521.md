plenums:/ˈpliːnəmz/| n. |全体会议；全体成员；(建筑) 充气空间；(电气) 腔室|A plenum is a large, usually enclosed, space into which air is blown for ventilation or other purposes. It can also refer to a full assembly or meeting of a group.  |例句：The plenum of the HVAC system distributes conditioned air throughout the building.  (该暖通空调系统的充气空间将调节后的空气输送到整个建筑物。)  The plenum of the board of directors voted on the new policy. (董事会全体成员对新政策进行了投票。) |近义词：assembly, meeting, chamber, space|反义词：vacancy, emptiness
