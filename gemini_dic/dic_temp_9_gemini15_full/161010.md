postboy:/ˈpoʊstbɔɪ/| n. |邮差（旧称）|A postboy was a person, typically a young man, employed to deliver mail, especially on horseback, before the establishment of organized postal services.  The term is now largely archaic. |Example: In the 18th century, postboys were a common sight riding along country lanes delivering letters.|近义词：mailman, postman (modern equivalents)|反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The postboy delivers the mail every day. = 邮差每天送信。
He carries the mailbag on his horse. = 他把邮袋放在马上。

一般过去时:
The postboy rode through the countryside. = 邮差骑马穿过乡村。
He delivered the important letter on time. = 他准时送达了那封重要的信。

现在完成时:
The postboy has delivered all the mail. = 邮差已经送完了所有的邮件。
He has ridden many miles today. = 他今天骑了好多英里。

过去完成时:
The postboy had already left before the letter arrived. = 信到达之前，邮差已经离开了。
He had delivered all the mail before sunset. = 他在日落之前已经送完了所有的邮件。

将来时:
The postboy will deliver the mail tomorrow. = 邮差明天送信。
He will ride his horse across the mountains. = 他将骑马越过群山。


虚拟语气:
If the postboy had arrived earlier, the letter would have been delivered sooner. = 如果邮差早点到，信就会早点送达。
I wish the postboy were faster. = 我希望邮差能更快一些。

被动语态:
The mail was delivered by the postboy. = 邮件是由邮差送达的。
The horse was ridden by the postboy across the fields. = 邮差骑着马穿过田野。

疑问句:
Did the postboy deliver the package? = 邮差送包裹了吗？
When will the postboy arrive? = 邮差什么时候到？

否定句:
The postboy didn't deliver the mail today. = 邮差今天没有送信。
He wasn't riding his usual horse. = 他没有骑他通常骑的那匹马。


PHRASE->

There are limited common phrases directly using "postboy" due to its archaic nature.  However, we can illustrate phrases related to the job:

riding the mail = 送邮件（骑马）
delivering the post = 投递邮件
on horseback = 骑马
country lane = 乡间小路
mail route = 邮件路线
