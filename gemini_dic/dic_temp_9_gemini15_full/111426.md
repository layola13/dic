lizards:/ˈlɪzərdz/| n. |蜥蜴 |Lizards are reptiles that are characterized by their scaly skin, four legs (although some species are legless), and long tails. They are found in a wide variety of habitats around the world. |例句：Many lizards are harmless and beneficial to the environment, controlling insect populations.  许多蜥蜴无害，对环境有益，控制着昆虫的数量。|例句：The chameleon is a type of lizard known for its ability to change color. 变色龙是一种以其变色能力而闻名的蜥蜴。|近义词：reptiles, saurians |反义词：无
