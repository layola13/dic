downtime:/ˈdaʊntaɪm/| n. |停机时间；闲置时间；空闲时间 |The period when a machine, system, or process is not operational or working.  It can also refer to a period of inactivity or leisure. |例句：The server experienced unexpected downtime yesterday. = 服务器昨天出现了意外停机。

例句：We used the downtime to catch up on emails. = 我们利用停机时间处理邮件。

近义词：idle time, interruption, outage, break, inactivity

反义词：uptime, operation, productivity
