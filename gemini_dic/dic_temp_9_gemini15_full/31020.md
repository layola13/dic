challengingly:/ˈtʃælən(d)ʒɪŋli/| adv.| 挑战性地，要求很高地 |  Describes something that presents a significant challenge or difficulty. It suggests that something is demanding, difficult, or requires considerable effort to overcome. |例句：The project was challengingly complex, requiring a multidisciplinary team.  这个项目极具挑战性，需要一个多学科团队。

The athlete trained challengingly hard for the upcoming Olympics.这位运动员为即将到来的奥运会进行了高强度训练。


近义词：demandingly, strenuously, rigorously, taxingly


反义词：easily, simply, effortlessly
