humblehead:/ˈhʌmbəlhed/| n.|卑微的头脑，谦卑的人|A humblehead is a person who is excessively humble or self-deprecating, often to the point of being annoying or ineffective.  It's a less common term, often used informally and sometimes with a slightly negative connotation.  The term combines "humble" (meaning modest or unassuming) with "head," suggesting a person dominated by humility.|例句：He's such a humblehead; he always downplays his accomplishments. 他真是个谦卑过头的人；他总是轻描淡写自己的成就。

He's a bit of a humblehead, always apologizing even when he's not at fault. 他有点谦卑过头了，即使不是他的错也总是道歉。|近义词：meek, self-effacing, modest, unassuming|反义词：arrogant, boastful, conceited, self-important|名词
