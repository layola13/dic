complementary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/| adj.|互补的，补充的|Something that is complementary completes or enhances something else.|The colors in the painting are complementary.|近义词：supplementary, additional, supporting|反义词：contradictory, opposing, conflicting|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These two skills are complementary. = 这两种技能是互补的。
His experience complements her expertise. = 他的经验补充了她的专业知识。


一般过去时:
The two ideas complemented each other perfectly. = 这两个想法完美地互补。
Her outfit complemented her accessories. = 她的服装搭配她的配饰很协调。


现在完成时:
The new features have complemented the existing software. = 新功能完善了现有软件。
Their efforts have complemented each other well. = 他们的努力互相补充得很好。


过去完成时:
The previous research had complemented their findings. = 先前的研究补充了他们的发现。
His earlier work had complemented the current project. = 他早期的工作补充了当前的项目。


将来时:
The upcoming expansion will complement the current facilities. = 即将来临的扩张将补充现有的设施。
The new product will complement our existing line. = 新产品将补充我们现有的产品线。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, they would complement our current efforts. = 如果我们有更多资源，它们将补充我们目前的努力。
If the colors were complementary, the painting would be more harmonious. = 如果颜色是互补的，这幅画就会更和谐。


被动语态:
His work was complemented by his colleagues' contributions. = 他的工作得到了同事的贡献的补充。
The design was complemented by the use of natural materials. = 该设计通过使用天然材料得到了补充。


疑问句:
Are these colors complementary? = 这些颜色是互补的吗？
Do these skills complement each other? = 这些技能是互补的吗？


否定句:
The two arguments were not complementary. = 这两个论点并不互补。
The music did not complement the film's mood. = 音乐并不符合电影的氛围。
