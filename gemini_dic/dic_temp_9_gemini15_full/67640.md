extricating: /ˈɛkstrɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 解救；使摆脱；抽出 | The act of freeing someone or something from a constraint or difficulty.  |例句：Firefighters were busy extricating people from the burning building. 消防队员正忙着将人们从燃烧的建筑物中救出来。

例句：She spent hours extricating herself from the complicated legal situation. 她花了数小时才摆脱复杂的法律纠纷。


近义词：freeing, liberating, rescuing, releasing, disentangling | 反义词：entrapping, ensnaring, involving


词源:  From Middle English *extricaten, from Late Latin *extricare, from Latin ex- ("out") + tricare ("to trouble, to rub").  The original meaning involved untangling or freeing something from a tangled or difficult situation.
