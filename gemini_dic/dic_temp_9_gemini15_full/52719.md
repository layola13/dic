dihedral:/ˈdaɪˌhiːdrəl/| n.|二面角；二面体|A dihedral angle is the angle between two intersecting planes.  It is also used in chemistry to describe the angle between two planes defined by groups of atoms in a molecule.|例句：The dihedral angle between the two wings of the aircraft is crucial for its stability.  飞机两个机翼之间的二面角对其稳定性至关重要。

The dihedral angle of a molecule significantly affects its properties. 分子的二面角会显著影响其性质。|近义词：interfacial angle|反义词:|名词
