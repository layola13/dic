preadvertises:/ˌpriːˈædvərtaɪzɪz/| vt.|预先做广告，提前宣传|To advertise something before it is available or released.|The company preadvertises its products to generate excitement before the launch.|近义词：promote beforehand, preannounce|反义词：post-advertise


USAGE->

They preadvertise their new products every year. = 他们每年都为他们的新产品预先做广告。
The store preadvertises its holiday sales. = 这家商店预先宣传它的假日促销活动。
