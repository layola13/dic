prompted:/ˈprɑːmptɪd/ /ˈprɒmptɪd/| vt.|提示；促使；激起；提示（演员）|to encourage someone to speak or act, especially by reminding them of what they should say or do, or to cause a particular reaction.|The teacher prompted the students to think critically.老师提示学生进行批判性思考。|suggested, induced, triggered|hindered, prevented|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The actor's forgotten line prompts the stage manager to whisper the cue.  演员忘记了台词，提示员低声提示了提示词。
He prompts his students to participate in class discussions. 他提示他的学生参与课堂讨论。


一般过去时:
The news report prompted widespread public outrage. 新闻报道引发了公众的广泛愤怒。
A sudden noise prompted her to look around.  一声巨响促使她环顾四周。


现在完成时:
Recent events have prompted a reassessment of our security policies. 最近发生的事件促使我们重新评估安全政策。
The success of the project has prompted further investment. 项目的成功促使了进一步的投资。


过去完成时:
The earlier warnings had prompted him to take precautions. 早期警告促使他采取了预防措施。
What had prompted her to make such a drastic decision? 是什么促使她做出如此 drastic 的决定？


将来时:
The upcoming election will prompt many debates on various issues.  即将到来的选举将引发关于各种问题的许多辩论。
This new evidence will prompt a thorough investigation. 这项新证据将促使进行彻底调查。


虚拟语气:
If I were prompted, I would have answered differently. 如果有人提示我，我会给出不同的答案。
I wish I had been prompted sooner. 我真希望早点有人提示我。


被动语态:
The students were prompted to answer the questions. 学生们被提示回答问题。
He was prompted by a sudden urge to confess. 他被一种突然的冲动促使去坦白。


疑问句:
What prompted you to say that?  是什么促使你说那样的话？
What prompted this sudden change of plans?  是什么促使计划突然改变？


否定句:
Nothing prompted him to act. 没有什么促使他采取行动。
The evidence didn't prompt any further investigation.  证据没有促使进一步的调查。
