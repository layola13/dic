epauletted:/ˈɛpəlɛtɪd/| adj.|  缀有肩章的；佩带肩章的 |  Wearing epaulets.  The word describes something or someone adorned with epaulets, which are ornamental shoulder pieces on a uniform. |例句：The epauletted officer strode confidently into the room.  这位佩带肩章的军官自信地走进房间。

The general, epauletted and imposing, addressed the troops.  这位身着肩章、气宇轩昂的将军向部队讲话。


近义词：shouldered (in the sense of having shoulder ornaments), ornamented


反义词：unadorned, plain


词性:形容词
