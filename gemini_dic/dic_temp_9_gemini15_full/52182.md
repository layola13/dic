diaries:/ˈdaɪəriːz/| n.|日记；日记簿|A diary is a record of events, experiences, and reflections kept on a daily basis.  It can be a personal journal or a more formal record of events. |例句：She keeps a diary to record her daily thoughts and feelings. 她写日记来记录她每天的想法和感受。|例句：The doctor asked the patient to keep a food diary. 医生让病人记录饮食日记。|近义词：journals, logs|反义词：None
