fellas:/ˈfɛləz/| n.|伙计们，家伙们|A colloquial term for men or guys.  It's often used in a friendly or informal setting.|例句：Hey fellas, let's grab a beer.  嘿，伙计们，咱们去喝杯啤酒吧。|例句：Those fellas over there are causing trouble.  那边那些家伙在惹麻烦。|近义词：guys, men, blokes, chaps|反义词：ladies, women
