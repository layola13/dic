electric:/ɪˈlɛktrɪk/ | adj. | 电的；电动的；令人兴奋的 | Relating to or operated by electricity; exciting or thrilling. | 例句：The electric car is very popular. 这款电动汽车非常受欢迎。  | 例句：The concert was electric! 音乐会太棒了！ | 近义词：electrical, electrifying | 反义词：manual, mechanical


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The electric fan cools the room. = 电风扇使房间凉爽。
This electric kettle boils water quickly. = 这个电热水壶烧水很快。

一般过去时:
The lights went electric during the storm. = 暴风雨期间灯突然亮了。
He drove an electric car last year. = 他去年开的是电动汽车。

现在完成时:
We have installed electric heating in our house. = 我们已经在房子里安装了电热系统。
She has driven an electric car for three years. = 她已经开电动汽车三年了。

过去完成时:
The factory had used electric power for decades. = 这个工厂几十年来一直使用电力。
Before the invention of the electric light bulb, people used candles. = 在发明电灯泡之前，人们使用蜡烛。

将来时:
They will install electric car charging stations. = 他们将安装电动汽车充电站。
We will use electric scooters to get around. = 我们将使用电动滑板车四处走动。

虚拟语气:
If I had an electric car, I would save money on gas. = 如果我有一辆电动汽车，我会省下汽油钱。
I wish I had an electric bike to commute to work. = 我希望我有一辆电动自行车上班通勤。

被动语态:
The room is lit by electric lights. = 房间由电灯照明。
The car is powered by an electric motor. = 这辆车由电动机驱动。

疑问句:
Is this a electric toothbrush? = 这是电动牙刷吗？
Does your house use electric heating? = 你的房子使用电热吗？

否定句:
This device is not electric; it's manual. = 这个设备不是电动的；它是手动。
The old factory didn't use electric lights. = 老工厂不使用电灯。
