can't:/kænt/| v. |不能，不会|Contraction of "cannot," indicating inability or unwillingness to do something. It's used in various tenses and sentence structures.|例句：I can't go to the party tonight. = 我今晚不能去参加派对。|例句：She can't believe he lied to her. = 她不敢相信他对她撒谎了。|近义词：cannot, am unable to,  is unable to|反义词：can, able to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I can't believe it! = 我简直不敢相信！
We can't afford a new car. = 我们买不起新车。

一般过去时:
I couldn't sleep last night. = 我昨晚睡不着觉。
They couldn't find their keys. = 他们找不到他们的钥匙了。

现在完成时:
I haven't been able to contact him. = 我一直联系不上他。(can't的现在完成时通常用haven't/hasn't been able to代替)
She hasn't been able to finish the project. = 她一直没能完成这个项目。(can't的现在完成时通常用haven't/hasn't been able to代替)

过去完成时:
I couldn't have done it without your help. = 没有你的帮助，我做不到。(can't的过去完成时通常用couldn't have done代替)
They couldn't have predicted the outcome. = 他们不可能预测到结果。(can't的过去完成时通常用couldn't have done代替)


将来时:
I won't be able to come tomorrow. = 我明天来不了。(can't的将来时通常用won't be able to代替)
We can't possibly finish it by Friday. = 我们不可能在周五之前完成它。(can't的将来时通常用won't be able to代替)

虚拟语气:
I wish I could go to the concert. = 我真希望我能去参加音乐会。
If I could, I would help you. = 如果我能，我会帮助你。

被动语态:
It can't be done. = 这事做不成。
The problem can't be solved easily. =这个问题不容易解决。

疑问句:
Can't you hear me? = 你听不见我说话吗？
Can't you see what's happening? = 你没看见发生了什么事吗？

否定句:
I can't help you. = 我帮不了你。
He can't understand English. = 他不懂英语。


PHRASE->
can't help but: 不禁…
can't wait to: 迫不及待地想…
can't stand: 受不了
can't seem to: 好像不能
can't believe: 不敢相信
