daavid:/ˈdeɪvɪd/| n. |大卫| A common masculine given name of Hebrew origin, meaning "beloved" or "friend".  The name is associated with King David in the Bible, a renowned figure known for his piety, musical talent, and kingship.  |Example: My friend's name is David. 我的朋友名叫大卫。|近义词:Dave (short form)|反义词: N/A|名词
