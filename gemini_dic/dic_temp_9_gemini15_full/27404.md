camerade:/ˌkæmərˈɑːd/| n.|同志，朋友，战友|A comrade is a friend, especially one who shares your political beliefs or is involved in the same struggle.  It often implies a close bond based on shared goals and experiences.|Example: He fought alongside his comrades in the revolution. 他在革命中与他的同志们并肩作战。|近义词：friend, companion, pal, ally|反义词：enemy, opponent, adversary|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
My comrade works hard. = 我的同志工作很努力。
She is a close comrade to many. = 她是许多人的亲密战友。


一般过去时:
My comrade fought bravely. = 我的同志英勇作战。
They were comrades in arms. = 他们曾是战场上的战友。


现在完成时:
We have been comrades for many years. = 我们多年来一直是同志。
She has supported her comrades throughout the struggle. = 在整个斗争中，她一直支持她的同志们。


过去完成时:
They had been comrades since childhood. = 他们从小就是同志。
He had already established a strong bond with his comrades before the mission. = 在执行任务之前，他已经与他的同志们建立了牢固的联系。


将来时:
We will remain comrades until the end. = 我们将一直是同志，直到最后。
They will be comrades in arms during the upcoming conflict. = 在即将到来的冲突中，他们将成为战场上的战友。


虚拟语气:
If I had a comrade like him, I would feel safer. = 如果我有像他一样的同志，我会感到更安全。
I wish I had more comrades to help me. = 我希望我有更多同志来帮助我。


被动语态:
He was betrayed by his comrades. = 他被他的同志们背叛了。
The comrades were celebrated for their bravery. = 这些同志因其勇敢而受到表彰。


疑问句:
Is he your comrade? = 他是你的同志吗？
Were they comrades during the war? = 他们在战争期间是同志吗？


否定句:
He is not my comrade. = 他不是我的同志。
They were not comrades in that fight. = 他们在那一场战斗中并非同志。
