organises:/ˈɔːɡənaɪzɪz/ | v. |组织；安排；筹备 | To put things or people into a particular order, system, or structure; to arrange or plan (an event, activity, etc.).| 例句：She organises all the company's events. 她组织公司所有的活动。

例句：He organises his time very efficiently. 他非常有效率地安排自己的时间。


近义词：arrange, plan, coordinate, structure | 反义词：disorganise, disarrange, disrupt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She organises the office party every year. = 她每年都组织办公室聚会。
He organises his thoughts before he speaks. = 他说话前先整理思绪。


一般过去时:
They organised a fundraising event last month. = 他们上个月组织了一场筹款活动。
She organised her desk before leaving for the day. = 她下班前整理了她的办公桌。


现在完成时:
We have organised everything for the conference. = 我们已经为会议准备好了所有东西。
She has organised several successful charity events. = 她已经组织了几次成功的慈善活动。


过去完成时:
They had organised the logistics before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经安排好了后勤工作。
He had organised a surprise party for his wife's birthday. = 他为他妻子的生日组织了一个惊喜派对。


将来时:
We will organise a trip to the beach next summer. = 我们明年夏天将组织一次海滩旅行。
They will organise a meeting to discuss the project. = 他们将组织一次会议来讨论这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would organise a bigger party. = 如果我有更多时间，我会组织一个更大的派对。
I wish I could organise my life better. = 我希望我能更好地安排我的生活。


被动语态:
The event was organised by a professional events company. = 这场活动是由一家专业的活动公司组织的。
The meeting is being organised for next week. = 会议正在安排在下周举行。


疑问句:
Who organised the meeting? = 谁组织了这次会议？
How will you organise the transportation? = 你将如何安排交通？


否定句:
He didn't organise the trip properly. = 他没有妥善安排这次旅行。
They haven't organised the accommodation yet. = 他们还没有安排住宿。
