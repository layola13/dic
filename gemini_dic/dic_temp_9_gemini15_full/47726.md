decadent:/ˈdɛkədənt/| adj.|颓废的；堕落的；享乐主义的|decadent describes something or someone that shows moral or cultural decline, often associated with excessive luxury and self-indulgence.  It suggests a loss of values and a focus on pleasure over responsibility.|The decadent aristocracy lived a life of luxury, oblivious to the suffering of the common people.  颓废的贵族过着奢侈的生活，对普通人的苦难视而不见。|debauched, depraved, dissipated|moral, virtuous, upright
