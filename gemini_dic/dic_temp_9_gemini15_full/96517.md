indoctrinated:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪtɪd/ | v. |灌输思想，教唆 |To teach (someone) a set of beliefs, often in an unquestioning manner.  This often implies a forceful or manipulative method of teaching, resulting in the acceptance of ideas without critical examination. |例句：The cult indoctrinated its members to believe that only they possessed the truth.邪教灌输其成员相信只有他们拥有真理。The children were indoctrinated with nationalist ideas from a young age.孩子们从小就被灌输了民族主义思想。|近义词：brainwash, proselytize, inculcate |反义词：educate (in a critical and open-minded way), enlighten
