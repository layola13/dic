moulter:/ˈmaʊltər/| n. |换毛者；蜕皮者|A moulter is an animal that is shedding its feathers, skin, or other outer covering.  This term is often used in reference to birds shedding their feathers. |The bird is a moulter, its feathers are falling out. 这只鸟正在换毛，羽毛正在脱落。|shedder, shedders|non-moulter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird moults its feathers every year. = 这只鸟每年换一次羽毛。
He regularly moults his skin. = 他定期蜕皮。


一般过去时:
The snake moulted its skin last night. = 蛇昨晚蜕皮了。
The eagle moulted its feathers in the autumn. = 鹰在秋天换了羽毛。


现在完成时:
The caterpillar has moulted several times. = 毛毛虫已经蜕皮好几次了。
The bird has finished moulting its feathers. = 这只鸟已经完成了换毛。


过去完成时:
The snake had moulted its skin before I saw it. = 我看到它之前，蛇已经蜕皮了。
The bird had moulted its feathers before the migration. = 在迁徙之前，鸟已经换好了羽毛。


将来时:
The snake will moult its skin next week. = 蛇下周将要蜕皮。
The bird will begin moulting soon. = 这只鸟很快就要开始换毛了。


虚拟语气:
If the bird moulted its feathers earlier, it would be better prepared for winter. = 如果这只鸟早点换毛，它就能更好地为冬天做好准备。
I wish the snake would moult its skin quickly. = 我希望蛇能快点蜕皮。


被动语态:
The feathers were moulted by the bird. = 羽毛是被鸟换掉的。
The skin is being moulted by the snake. = 蛇正在蜕皮。


疑问句:
Does the snake moult its skin often? = 蛇经常蜕皮吗？
Is the bird currently moulting? = 这只鸟现在正在换毛吗？


否定句:
The bird doesn't moult its feathers in the spring. = 这只鸟春天不换羽毛。
The snake hasn't moulted its skin yet. = 蛇还没蜕皮。
