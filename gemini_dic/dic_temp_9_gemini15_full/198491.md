shrimpish:/ˈʃrɪmpɪʃ/| adj.|像虾的；胆小的；懦弱的|Resembling a shrimp; timid; cowardly.  The word suggests smallness, fragility, and a lack of assertiveness.|例句：He was too shrimpish to stand up for himself. 他太胆小了，不敢为自己辩护。
例句：The shrimpish creature scurried away at the slightest noise.  那只像虾一样小的生物听到一点声音就赶紧逃跑了。|近义词：timid, cowardly, fainthearted, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, bold, courageous, dauntless|
