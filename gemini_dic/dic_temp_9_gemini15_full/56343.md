dogsled:/ˈdɒɡˌslɛd/|/ˈdɑːɡˌslɛd/| n. |狗拉雪橇|A sled pulled by dogs, typically used for transportation over snow and ice.|Example:  The musher expertly guided his dogsled across the snowy landscape.  驾驭员熟练地驾驶着狗拉雪橇穿过雪景。

The Inuit people have used dogsleds for centuries.  因纽特人使用狗拉雪橇已有数百年历史。


近义词：dog team, sled


反义词：无


词性：名词
