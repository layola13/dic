chaperons:/ˈʃæpərɔːnz/ | n. | 陪伴者；监护人 | A chaperone is a person who accompanies and supervises young people, especially at social events, to ensure their proper behavior.  | Example:  The school arranged chaperones for the students' trip to the museum. 学校为学生们的博物馆之旅安排了陪伴者。  |近义词：attendant, escort, guardian | 反义词：None specifically, but contextually could be words implying unsupervised freedom.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She chaperones the young girls at the dance. = 她担任年轻女孩在舞会上的陪伴者。
He chaperones his younger sister to the party. = 他陪着他的妹妹去参加派对。


一般过去时:
My aunt chaperoned us on our class trip. = 我的阿姨在我们班级旅行中担任我们的陪伴者。
She chaperoned the teenagers at the concert. = 她担任青少年在音乐会上的陪伴者。


现在完成时:
She has chaperoned many school events over the years. = 多年来，她担任过许多学校活动的陪伴者。
They have chaperoned their children's social events since they were young. = 从孩子们很小的时候起，他们就一直担任孩子们社交活动的陪伴者。


过去完成时:
My parents had chaperoned me at every school dance before I was sixteen. = 在我十六岁之前，我的父母每次学校舞会都陪着我。
She had chaperoned countless events before retiring. = 在退休之前，她担任过无数活动的陪伴者。


将来时:
She will chaperone the students' prom next month. = 下个月她将担任学生毕业舞会的陪伴者。
They will chaperone the youth group's camping trip. = 他们将担任青年团露营旅行的陪伴者。


虚拟语气:
If only I had a chaperone at that party! = 要是我在那次派对上有位陪伴者就好了！
I wish my parents had chaperoned me more when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候父母能更多地陪伴我。


被动语态:
The students were chaperoned by several teachers. = 学生们由几位老师陪同。
The group was well chaperoned throughout the tour. = 在整个旅游过程中，该小组受到了很好的陪同。


疑问句:
Will you be chaperoning the event? = 你会担任这次活动的陪伴者吗？
Who will be chaperoning the children? = 谁将担任孩子们的陪伴者？


否定句:
She did not chaperone the party. = 她没有担任这次派对的陪伴者。
They were not chaperoned on their trip. = 他们的旅行没有陪伴者。
