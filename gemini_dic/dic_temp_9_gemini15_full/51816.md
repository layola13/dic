dewdrop:/ˈdjuːdrɒp/| n. |露珠|A small drop of water that forms on cool surfaces at night, especially on plants, from condensation of atmospheric vapor.| 例句：The spiderweb glittered with countless dew drops.  蜘蛛网布满了无数闪闪发光的露珠。

The dewdrop clung to the petal. 露珠粘在花瓣上。

近义词：drop, droplet | 反义词：无
