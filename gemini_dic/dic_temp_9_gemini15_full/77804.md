generalizing: /ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |概括；归纳；推广 | To make a general statement or form a general opinion, often without considering all the details or exceptions.  It involves drawing broad conclusions from specific instances. |


例句：
He's generalizing from a very small sample size. = 他从一个非常小的样本量中进行概括。

She's generalizing about men based on her experience with one. = 她根据与一个男人的经历来概括所有男人。


近义词：summarizing, abstracting, synthesizing, globalizing


反义词：specifying, detailing, particularizing


词性：动词
