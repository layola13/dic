shortlists:/ˈʃɔːrtlɪsts/| n. v.|候选名单；入围名单；将…列入候选名单|A shortlist is a list of candidates or items that have been selected from a larger group for further consideration.  The verb form means to put someone or something on a shortlist.|

例句：
The company shortlisted ten candidates for the job. = 公司为这份工作选出了十名候选人。

The judges shortlisted three paintings for the final round. = 评委们为决赛选出了三幅画作。


近义词：
candidates, nominees, finalists

反义词：
longlist (a list of all candidates before narrowing it down)


词性:
noun, verb
