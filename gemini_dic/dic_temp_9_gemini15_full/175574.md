randomise: /ˈrændəmaɪz/ | vt. | 使随机化；随机分配 | To arrange or select something in a random order; to make something random.  | 例句：The participants were randomised into two groups.  参与者被随机分成两组。

例句：The software randomises the questions on each test.  该软件使每次测试的题目顺序随机化。


近义词：shuffle, jumble, scramble | 反义词：order, arrange systematically | 词性: 动词


USAGE->
