companionable:/kəmˈpænɪənəbəl/ | adj. | 愉快的；友好的；易于相处的 |  Pleasant company; sociable and friendly.  Someone who is companionable is easy to get along with and enjoy being around. They are friendly, sociable, and make good company. | Example: He was a companionable man, always ready with a joke or a story. 他是一个很友善的人，总是乐于讲笑话或故事。


Example:  The atmosphere at the party was very companionable; everyone was chatting and laughing. 晚会的氛围非常融洽；每个人都在聊天和大笑。


近义词: sociable, friendly, agreeable, convivial, affable | 反义词: unfriendly, unsociable, disagreeable, aloof, solitary | 词性: adjective
