lands:/lænz/ | n. |土地，国土，地域 | Lands refers to an expanse of ground, especially a country or region considered as a whole. It often implies a sense of ownership or possession. |例句：The fertile lands produced abundant crops.  肥沃的土地出产丰富的农作物。

例句：The king ruled over vast lands. 国王统治着广阔的土地。


近义词：territory, country, region, estate | 反义词：seas, oceans


词源:  From Old English *land, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch land and German Land, ultimately from a Proto-Indo-European root meaning "to stretch" or "to extend."  The plural form "lands" emphasizes multiple distinct areas or territories.
