desideration:/ˌdɛsɪdəˈreɪʃən/| n. |渴望；需要；要求 |A desideratum is something that is desired or needed.  It often refers to a requirement or condition that is considered essential.  The plural is desiderata. |例句：The desideration for peace is universal. 全世界都渴望和平。


例句：Their primary desideration was a peaceful solution. 他们的首要要求是和平解决。


近义词：desire, need, requirement, want, demand


反义词：indifference, apathy, unconcern


词性：名词
