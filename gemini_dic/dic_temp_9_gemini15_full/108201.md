laud:/lɔːd/| vt.|赞扬，赞美，歌颂|to praise someone or something highly, especially in a public context.|例句：The critics lauded his performance as brilliant. 批评家们高度赞扬了他的精彩表演。|例句：The mayor lauded the city's new park. 市长赞扬了城市的新公园。|近义词：praise, commend, extol, acclaim|反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce, disparage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They laud her achievements every year. = 他们每年都赞扬她的成就。
We laud the efforts of the volunteers. = 我们赞扬志愿者的努力。


一般过去时:
The teacher lauded the student's hard work. = 老师赞扬了学生的努力工作。
The newspaper lauded the hero's bravery. = 报纸赞扬了英雄的勇敢。


现在完成时:
She has been lauded for her contributions to science. = 她因对科学的贡献而受到赞扬。
They have lauded the new policy for its effectiveness. = 他们因其有效性而赞扬了这项新政策。


过去完成时:
The company had lauded the employee before the award ceremony. = 在颁奖典礼之前，公司已经赞扬了这位员工。
The public had lauded the artist's work before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，公众已经赞扬了这位艺术家的作品。


将来时:
The president will laud the soldiers' sacrifice in his speech. = 总统将在他的演讲中赞扬士兵的牺牲。
The community will laud the efforts to rebuild the town. = 社区将赞扬重建城镇的努力。


虚拟语气:
If I were the judge, I would laud her courage. = 如果我是法官，我会赞扬她的勇气。
I wish they would laud the importance of education. = 我希望他们能赞扬教育的重要性。


被动语态:
His work was lauded by many critics. = 他的作品受到许多评论家的赞扬。
The project was lauded for its innovative design. = 该项目因其创新设计而受到赞扬。


疑问句:
Will they laud the team's victory? = 他们会赞扬球队的胜利吗？
Did they laud his efforts to help the poor? = 他们赞扬了他帮助穷人的努力吗？


否定句:
They did not laud his behavior. = 他们没有赞扬他的行为。
The committee did not laud the proposal. = 委员会没有赞扬该提案。



PHRASE->
laud to the skies = 竭力赞扬
laud somebody/something highly = 高度赞扬某人/某事
earn/win/gain/deserve laud = 赢得赞扬
be lauded for = 因……而受赞扬
laud somebody's efforts = 赞扬某人的努力


词源:
源自于拉丁语 *laudāre  “to praise”，与英语单词“praise”同源。
