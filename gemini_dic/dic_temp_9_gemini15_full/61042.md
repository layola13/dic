elixirs:/ɪˈlɪksərz/| n.|灵丹妙药；仙丹；(尤指)使人精力充沛的饮料|a magical or medicinal potion.  A elixir is often believed to have the power to cure diseases or prolong life. It can also refer to something that invigorates or revitalizes.|例句：Many people believe that herbal elixirs can cure various ailments. 许多人相信草药灵丹妙药可以治愈各种疾病。

The alchemist spent years searching for the elixir of life.炼金术士花了数年时间寻找长生不老药。|近义词：potion, remedy, tonic|反义词：poison, toxin|名词
