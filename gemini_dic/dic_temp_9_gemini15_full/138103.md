norms:/nɔːmz/| n.|规范，标准，准则|Rules or standards that are considered to be typical or expected behavior within a particular group or society.|The social norms of this community are quite strict.|近义词：standards, rules, guidelines, conventions|反义词：anarchy, chaos|名词


例句：

The social norms of this community are quite strict.  这个社区的社会规范非常严格。

We must uphold the highest ethical norms in our profession. 我们必须在我们的职业中坚持最高的道德规范。


词源:

The word "norm" comes from the Latin word "norma," meaning "carpenter's square," "rule," or "pattern."  Its meaning evolved to encompass the idea of a standard or model.


用法说明：

norms 通常指社会、群体或组织中被普遍接受的行为准则或标准。  它们可以是正式的法律或规则，也可以是非正式的、约定俗成的行为方式。  norms 可以影响个人的行为、态度和价值观。


PHRASE->

behavioral norms 行为规范
group norms 集体规范
