pentamerism:/pɛnˈtæməˌrɪzəm/| n. |五体性，五辐射对称|The arrangement of parts in fives or multiples of five, especially in the radial symmetry of echinoderms.  This refers to a body plan where five similar parts are arranged around a central axis.  For example, starfish exhibit pentamerism. |Example:  The starfish's pentamerism is a defining characteristic of its morphology.  海星的五辐射对称是其形态的决定性特征。 The striking pentamerism of the sea anemone is clearly visible. 海葵醒目的五体性清晰可见。|近义词：radial symmetry (in the context of five-part symmetry)|反义词：bilateral symmetry|名词
