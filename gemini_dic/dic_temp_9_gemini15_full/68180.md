faintingly:/ˈfeɪntɪŋli/| adv.|虚弱地；快要昏倒地；微弱地 |adverb describing an action performed weakly or as if about to faint.  It suggests a lack of strength or energy, often due to illness, emotion, or exhaustion.|例句：She smiled faintly, her strength failing. 她无力地笑了笑，力气快用尽了。 He spoke faintly, barely audible above the din. 他声音微弱，在嘈杂声中几乎听不见。|近义词：weakly, feebly, faintly, barely|反义词：strongly, powerfully, forcefully|副词
