mudskipper:/ˈmʌdˌskɪpər/| n. |弹涂鱼|A fish that can live both in water and on land, using its pectoral fins to "skip" across mudflats.|例句：Mudskippers are amazing creatures that can survive out of water for extended periods. 弹涂鱼是令人惊叹的生物，它们可以在水中和陆地上生存很长时间。|近义词：amphibious fish|反义词:None
