fantail:/ˈfænˌteɪl/| n.|扇尾，扇尾鸽|A fantail is a bird with a tail that spreads out like a fan, or a breed of pigeon with such a tail.|例句：The peacock displayed its magnificent fantail.  孔雀展示了它华丽的扇尾。

例句：The fantail pigeon is known for its graceful movements. 扇尾鸽以其优雅的动作而闻名。


近义词： None (specific to the bird's tail)

反义词：None (no direct opposite)

词性:Noun
