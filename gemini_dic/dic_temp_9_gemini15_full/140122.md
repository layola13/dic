off-stage:/ˌɒfˈsteɪdʒ/ /ˌɑːfˈsteɪdʒ/| adj.|幕后；台下；不在舞台上|Situated or occurring off the stage of a theatre.  It can also refer to something happening outside the main focus or attention.|例句：The actors waited off-stage before their cue.  演员们在提示前在后台等待。

The sound effects were created off-stage. 音效是在后台制作的。|近义词：backstage, behind the scenes|反义词：on-stage, onstage|


USAGE->

The actors are off-stage during the intermission. = 中场休息期间，演员们都在后台。
The orchestra is off-stage preparing for the next piece. = 乐队在后台准备下一首乐曲。
