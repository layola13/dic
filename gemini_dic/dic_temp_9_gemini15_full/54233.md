disengage:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/| vt.|脱离，解脱，使脱离，使自由|To release from engagement or connection; to disconnect or separate.  |例句：He disengaged the safety catch on the gun. 他解开了枪上的保险。

例句：The two armies disengaged after a long battle.  两军经过一场长时间的战斗后脱离了接触。


近义词：disconnect, detach, separate, release, uncouple

反义词：engage, connect, attach


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disengages the gears smoothly. = 他顺利地挂空挡。
She disengages herself from the project. = 她从项目中脱身。


一般过去时:
The soldiers disengaged from the enemy. = 士兵们与敌人脱离了接触。
He disengaged the car's transmission. = 他挂上了汽车的空挡。


现在完成时:
They have disengaged from the argument. = 他们已经停止了争论。
She has disengaged herself from her former life. = 她已经摆脱了她以前的生活。


过去完成时:
The train had disengaged from the carriages before the accident. = 事故发生前火车已经与车厢脱钩了。
They had disengaged from the deal before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经退出交易了。


将来时:
He will disengage the clutch before starting the engine. = 他将在启动发动机之前松开离合器。
They will disengage themselves from the company soon. = 他们很快就会脱离这家公司。


虚拟语气:
If he disengaged the brakes, the car would roll down the hill. = 如果他松开刹车，汽车就会滚下山坡。
I wish I could disengage myself from this situation. = 我希望我能摆脱这种情况。


被动语态:
The machine was disengaged from the power supply. = 机器与电源断开了连接。
The coupling was disengaged by the technician. = 联轴器被技师拆开了。


疑问句:
Has the machinery disengaged properly? = 机械是否已正确脱离？
Will they disengage their forces immediately? = 他们会立即撤兵吗？


否定句:
He couldn't disengage the stubborn lock. = 他无法打开那把顽固的锁。
The two parties failed to disengage from the conflict. = 双方未能脱离冲突。


PHRASE->
disengage from: 脱离……
disengage oneself from:  使自己摆脱……
disengage the clutch: 松开离合器
disengage the gears: 挂空挡
disengage from combat: 脱离战斗


词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + engage (使从事，使参与)  The word ultimately derives from the Old French word "engager," meaning "to pledge," "to guarantee," or "to bind."
