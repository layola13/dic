perfumy:/pərˈfjuːmi/| adj.|芬芳的，香的，有香味的|Having a strong or sweet smell, like perfume.  The word suggests a somewhat overwhelming or artificial scent.|The room was perfumy with the scent of lilies.  房间里弥漫着百合花的芬芳。The perfumy air made me sneeze.  香气扑鼻让我打喷嚏。| fragrant, aromatic, scented |musky, pungent(if the smell is unpleasant) |形容词


USAGE->

The room is perfumy. = 房间里很香。
This soap smells perfumy. = 这块肥皂闻起来很香。
