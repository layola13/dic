ejecta:/ɪˈdʒɛktə/| n. |喷出物，喷射物|Material that has been ejected, especially from a volcano or crater.  The ejecta from the volcano covered the surrounding area in ash and rock. 火山的喷出物覆盖了周围地区的火山灰和岩石。|近义词：ejected material, debris, fallout |反义词：N/A
