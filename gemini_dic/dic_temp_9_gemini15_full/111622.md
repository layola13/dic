localising: /ˈloʊkəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使本地化；使适应当地情况 | The process of adapting something (software, a product, etc.) to a particular location or culture.  It involves translating text, adjusting images, and modifying functionality to meet the specific needs and preferences of a target market. | Example:  The company is localising its software for the Japanese market. 公司正在将其软件本地化以适应日本市场。 | 近义词: adapting, customizing, regionalizing | 反义词: globalizing, standardizing

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are localising the website for the French market. = 他们正在将网站本地化为法语市场。
We localise our products to meet customer needs. = 我们对产品进行本地化以满足客户需求。

一般过去时:
The team localised the game for several countries. = 该团队将游戏本地化到多个国家。
They localised the software last year. = 他们去年对软件进行了本地化。

现在完成时:
We have localised the app for over ten countries. = 我们已经将该应用程序本地化到十多个国家。
They have localised their marketing materials. = 他们已经将他们的营销材料本地化了。

过去完成时:
They had localised the software before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经将软件本地化了。
We had localised the website before the international conference. = 我们在国际会议之前已经将网站本地化了。

将来时:
