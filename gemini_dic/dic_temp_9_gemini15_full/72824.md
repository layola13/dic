foliar:/ˈfoʊliər/ | adj. | 叶的；叶状的；叶面的 | Relating to leaves or the leaves of plants.  Foliar application refers to the application of substances directly to the leaves of plants. | Example: Foliar feeding is a method of supplying nutrients directly to the leaves.  叶面施肥是直接向叶片提供养分的一种方法。|近义词：leafy |反义词：root, stem
