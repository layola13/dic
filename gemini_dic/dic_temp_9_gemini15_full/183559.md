request:/rɪˈkwɛst/| n. & vt.|请求，要求；申请|An expression of a wish or desire for something to be done; a formal application for something.|例句：I made a request for a transfer to another department. 我请求调到另一个部门。|例句：He requested a meeting with the manager. 他请求与经理会面。|近义词：demand, plea, application, petition|反义词：offer, provide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He requests a new car. = 他请求一辆新车。
They request more information. = 他们请求更多信息。

一般过去时:
She requested a leave of absence. = 她请求休假。
He requested that I attend the meeting. = 他要求我参加会议。

现在完成时:
I have requested a refund. = 我已经申请退款。
They have requested my assistance.  = 他们已经请求我的帮助。

过去完成时:
She had requested the documents before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已经请求了文件。
He had requested a promotion several times. = 他已经多次请求晋升。

将来时:
I will request a copy of the report. = 我将请求一份报告副本。
They will request an extension of the deadline. = 他们将请求延期。

虚拟语气:
I requested that he be present. = 我要求他到场。(that从句用虚拟语气)
It is requested that everyone participate. = 要求每个人都参加。(It is requested that...从句用虚拟语气)


被动语态:
A meeting was requested by the employees. = 员工请求了一次会议。
The information was requested by the authorities. = 信息是由当局请求的。

疑问句:
Did you request anything else? = 你还请求了什么其他的东西吗？
What did they request from you? = 他们向你请求了什么？


否定句:
I did not request that change. = 我没有请求那个改变。
They didn't request any assistance. = 他们没有请求任何帮助。



PHRASE->
at the request of = 应…的要求
on request = 应要求
in request = 受欢迎的，吃香的
make a request = 提出请求
grant a request = 准予请求
