nonmystical: /ˌnɒnˈmɪstɪkəl/ | adj. |非神秘的；非神秘主义的 | Not relating to or suggesting mysticism;  ordinary or commonplace.  It describes something that lacks mystical or supernatural elements, being straightforward and easily understood. |

例句：
The scientist offered a nonmystical explanation for the phenomenon. = 科学家对这种现象提供了一个非神秘的解释。

The nonmystical approach to problem-solving involves logic and reason. =  非神秘的解决问题的方法涉及逻辑和推理。


近义词：rational, realistic, secular, mundane, earthly, prosaic

反义词：mystical, supernatural, occult, esoteric, magical
