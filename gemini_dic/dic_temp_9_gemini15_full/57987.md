driveled:/ˈdraɪvəld/| adj.|愚笨的；迟钝的；无趣的 |  Describes someone who is slow-witted, dull, or boring.  It implies a lack of intelligence or imagination. |He gave a driveled speech that bored everyone to tears. 他发表了一个令人昏昏欲睡的无聊演讲。|近义词：stupid, dull, obtuse,  uninspired |反义词：intelligent, bright, witty, clever
