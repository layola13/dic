mocap:/ˈmoʊkæp/| n. |动作捕捉，动态捕捉|Motion capture is the process of recording the movement of objects or people.  It's often used in filmmaking, video games, and animation to create realistic movements for digital characters.|例句：The film used advanced mocap technology to create the realistic movements of the dragon. 这部电影使用了先进的动作捕捉技术来创造逼真的龙的动作。|例句：The actors wore mocap suits to record their performances for the video game. 演员们穿着动作捕捉服来录制他们为电子游戏表演的动作。|近义词：motion capture, performance capture|反义词：N/A
