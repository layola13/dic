hymenopteran:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərən/| n. |膜翅目昆虫|A hymenopteran is an insect belonging to the order Hymenoptera, characterized by two pairs of membranous wings and a constricted waist.  They include ants, bees, wasps, and sawflies.  Many hymenopterans are social insects, living in colonies with a complex social structure.  |例句：Ants, bees, and wasps are all examples of hymenopterans.  蚂蚁、蜜蜂和黄蜂都是膜翅目昆虫的例子。

例句：The study focused on the communication methods of various hymenopteran species. 这项研究侧重于各种膜翅目昆虫的交流方式。
