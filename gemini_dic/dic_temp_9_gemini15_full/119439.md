microlight:/ˈmaɪkroʊlaɪt/ | n. |微型飞机，超轻型飞机 |A microlight is a very light aircraft, typically with one or two seats and a small engine.  They are known for their simple design and ease of operation. |例句：He took a scenic flight in a microlight over the countryside. 他乘坐微型飞机在乡间进行了观光飞行。

例句：Microlights are becoming increasingly popular as a recreational activity. 微型飞机作为一种休闲活动正变得越来越受欢迎。


近义词：ultralight aircraft, very light aircraft |反义词：heavy aircraft, jumbo jet |词性：名词

词源:
The word "microlight" is a compound word.  "Micro" comes from the Greek word  μικρός (mikrós) meaning "small," and "light" refers to the aircraft's lightweight nature.  Therefore, "microlight" literally means "small light aircraft".
