redesigned:/ˌriːdɪˈzaɪnd/| adj. |重新设计的，改版设计的 |重新设计过的；经过重新设计的；改头换面的|The redesigned website is much easier to navigate.  重新设计的网站更容易浏览。|The company redesigned its logo to appeal to a younger audience. 公司重新设计了其标志以吸引更年轻的受众。|restyled, revamped, re-engineered|original, unchanged


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The website is redesigned every year. 网站每年都会重新设计。
This car model is redesigned with improved fuel efficiency.  这款汽车经过重新设计，燃油效率有所提高。


一般过去时:
They redesigned the kitchen last year.  他们去年重新设计了厨房。
The software was redesigned to fix several bugs. 软件经过重新设计以修复几个错误。


现在完成时:
The company has redesigned its marketing strategy.  该公司已经重新设计了其营销策略。
We have redesigned the product packaging to make it more appealing. 我们已经重新设计了产品包装，使其更具吸引力。


过去完成时:
They had redesigned the website before the launch. 他们在发布之前已经重新设计了网站。
The architect had redesigned the building plans before the final approval. 建筑师在最终批准之前已经重新设计了建筑计划。


将来时:
They will redesign the logo next month.  他们下个月将重新设计标志。
The app will be redesigned to improve user experience. 该应用程序将被重新设计以改善用户体验。


虚拟语气:
If they redesigned the system, it would be more efficient. 如果他们重新设计系统，它将更有效率。
I wish they had redesigned the interface; it’s so clunky. 我希望他们重新设计了界面；它太笨拙了。


被动语态:
The product was redesigned by a team of engineers. 该产品由工程师团队重新设计。

疑问句:

否定句:
