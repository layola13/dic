pantomimicry:/ˌpæntəˈmɪmɪkri/| n.|哑剧模仿；模仿；装腔作势|The imitation of actions or events, especially by means of gestures and facial expressions, without speech.  It often involves exaggerated movements and expressions for comedic or dramatic effect.  Pantomimicry can be used in theatrical performances, mime, or even in everyday communication.|例句：The actor's pantomimicry of a drowning man was hilarious.  这个演员模仿溺水者的样子很滑稽。

例句：Her pantomimicry perfectly captured the essence of the scene.  她的哑剧表演完美地捕捉到了场景的精髓。


近义词：mimicry, imitation, impersonation|反义词：authenticity, genuineness|名词
