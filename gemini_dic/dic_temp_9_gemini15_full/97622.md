inharmony:/ɪnˈhɑːrməni/ | adj. | 不和谐的，不协调的 | Lacking harmony; discordant.  It describes a state or condition where things are not in agreement or balance, often resulting in conflict or unpleasantness. | 例句：Their musical performance was marred by moments of inharmony. = 他们的音乐表演因一些不和谐的时刻而大打折扣。

Their relationship is marked by frequent periods of inharmony. = 他们的关系经常处于不和谐的状态。| 近义词：discordant, dissonant, incongruous, conflicting | 反义词：harmonious, concordant, consistent
