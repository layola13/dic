rowing-boat: /ˈroʊɪŋ boʊt/ | n. |划艇，小划船| A small boat propelled by oars.  Rowing boats are typically used for recreation, fishing, or short-distance travel on calm waters. They can vary in size and design depending on their intended use. |例句：We rented a rowing-boat and spent the afternoon on the lake. 我们租了一艘划艇，在湖上度过了下午。

例句：The children enjoyed a rowing-boat ride on the river. 孩子们喜欢在河上划船。


近义词：rowboat, dinghy (smaller), skiff (lighter)

反义词：motorboat (powered boat), sailboat (sail-powered boat)

词性：名词
