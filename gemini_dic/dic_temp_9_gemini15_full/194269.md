self-oblivious:/ˌsɛlf əˈblɪviəs/ | adj. |  茫然不知的；忘我的；未察觉的 | Unmindful of oneself or one's surroundings; absorbed in thought or activity.  | 例句：He worked with self-oblivious concentration. 他全神贯注地工作，全然不知周围的一切。  | 例句：Lost in her book, she was completely self-oblivious to the noisy party going on around her.  她沉浸在书中，完全没有注意到周围喧闹的聚会。| 近义词：unaware, unconscious, absorbed, engrossed | 反义词：self-aware, mindful, conscious
