cowcatcher: /ˈkaʊˌkætʃər/ | n. |除雪犁；防撞器|A metal structure at the front of a locomotive designed to push aside obstacles, such as cattle or debris, on the tracks.  It's also known as a pilot or snowplow, depending on its primary function.|The cowcatcher on the old steam engine cleared the tracks of fallen branches.|近义词：pilot, snowplow |反义词：None
