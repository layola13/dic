relearns:/ˌriːˈlɜːrnz/ | v. |重新学习，再学习 |To learn something again after having forgotten it or not understanding it previously.  |He relearns the multiplication tables every summer to keep his skills sharp.|近义词：revise, review, restudy |反义词：forget, unlearn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relearns Spanish every year. = 他每年都重新学习西班牙语。
She relearns the piano pieces before the concert. = 她在音乐会之前重新学习钢琴曲。

一般过去时:
I relearned the poem for the recitation. = 我为了朗诵重新学习了这首诗。
They relearned the safety procedures after the accident. = 事故发生后，他们重新学习了安全规程。

现在完成时:
She has relearned how to ride a bike after her accident. = 她事故之后重新学会了骑自行车。
He has relearned the material for the exam. = 他已经为考试重新学习了材料。

过去完成时:
I had relearned the song before the karaoke night. = 卡拉OK之夜之前我已经重新学习了这首歌。
They had relearned the dance steps before the performance. = 他们演出之前已经重新学习了舞步。

将来时:
I will relearn calculus next semester. = 下学期我将重新学习微积分。
She will relearn the French vocabulary before her trip. = 她将在旅行前重新学习法语词汇。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would relearn Latin. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新学习拉丁语。
I wish I could relearn how to play the guitar. = 我真希望我能重新学会弹吉他。

被动语态:
The material needs to be relearned. = 材料需要重新学习。
The lesson will be relearned by the students. = 学生们将重新学习这节课。


疑问句:
Will you relearn the material for the test? = 你会为考试重新学习材料吗？
Have you relearned the rules of grammar? = 你重新学习语法规则了吗？

否定句:
He did not relearn the history lesson. = 他没有重新学习历史课。
She will not relearn the song for the performance. = 她不会为演出重新学习这首歌。
