outskirts:/ˈaʊtskɜːrts/| n.|郊区，边缘地带|The outer limits or edges of a city or town.|例句：We live on the outskirts of London. 我们住在伦敦郊区。|近义词：suburbs, periphery, borders|反义词：center, downtown


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory is located on the outskirts. = 工厂位于郊区。
They live on the outskirts of the city. = 他们住在城市的郊区。

一般过去时:
He lived on the outskirts when he was young. = 他年轻的时候住在郊区。
We walked to the outskirts of the town. = 我们走到小镇的郊区。

现在完成时:
They have lived on the outskirts for many years. = 他们已经在郊区住了很多年了。
She has just moved to the outskirts. = 她刚刚搬到郊区。

过去完成时:
They had already left the outskirts before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经离开了郊区。
We had lived on the outskirts for ten years before we moved. = 我们在搬家之前已经在郊区住了十年。

将来时:
They will move to the outskirts next year. = 他们明年将搬到郊区。
We will explore the outskirts of the forest tomorrow. = 我们明天将探索森林的边缘地带。

虚拟语气:
If I lived in the city, I would prefer to live on the outskirts. = 如果我住在城市里，我会更喜欢住在郊区。
I wish I could live on the outskirts, but it's too far from my work. = 我希望我能住在郊区，但是离我的工作地点太远了。

被动语态:
The new housing development is being built on the outskirts. = 新的住宅开发项目正在郊区建设。
The outskirts were largely ignored by the city council. = 郊区在很大程度上被市议会忽视了。

疑问句:
Do you prefer living in the city center or on the outskirts? = 你更喜欢住在市中心还是郊区？
Are there many shops on the outskirts? = 郊区有很多商店吗？

否定句:
He doesn't live in the city center, he lives on the outskirts. = 他不住在市中心，他住在郊区。
They didn't find any restaurants on the outskirts. = 他们在郊区没有找到任何餐馆。
