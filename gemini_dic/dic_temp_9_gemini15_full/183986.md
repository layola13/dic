reshipment:/ˌriːˈʃɪpmənt/ | n. | 再装运；重新装运；再发货 | The act of shipping something again;  the goods shipped again.  It often implies a previous shipment had a problem, requiring reshipment. | 例句：Due to damage during transit, we require a reshipment of the order. 由于运输途中损坏，我们需要重新发货。

The customer requested a reshipment after receiving the wrong item. 客户收到错误的商品后要求重新发货。


近义词：re-shipment, second shipment, resending


反义词：arrival, receipt


词性: 名词
