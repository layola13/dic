national:/ˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. |国家的；民族的；全国性的 | Relating to or characteristic of a nation; common to or characteristic of the whole of a nation.  |例句：The national anthem was played.  国歌奏响了。|例句：She felt a strong sense of national pride. 她感到强烈的民族自豪感。|近义词：nationalistic, state, federal |反义词：international, global, local
