icing:/ˈaɪsɪŋ/| n.|糖霜，结冰 | A sweet, creamy mixture used as a topping or filling for cakes and other desserts.  It can also refer to the process or result of something becoming covered with ice. |例句：The cake was decorated with delicious chocolate icing.  这蛋糕装饰着美味的巧克力糖霜。

例句：The roads were dangerous due to the overnight icing.  由于一夜的结冰，道路变得危险。

近义词：frosting, glaze

反义词：None directly opposite, but 'bare', 'unfrosted' could be considered contextual opposites.
