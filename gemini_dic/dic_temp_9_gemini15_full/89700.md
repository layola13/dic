hoovered:/ˈhuːvərd/| v. |吸尘；彻底清理|To clean (a surface, such as a floor or carpet) with a vacuum cleaner.  The word is often used as a verb even if a specific brand of vacuum cleaner wasn't used.|例句：I hoovered the carpets this morning. = 今天早上我吸了地毯。

I hoovered up all the broken glass after the accident. = 事故后我把所有碎玻璃都吸起来了。

近义词：vacuum, clean, suction


反义词：mess, dirty, litter


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She hoovers the house every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都吸尘。
He hoovers the car after every trip. = 他每次旅行后都会吸车内。


一般过去时:
I hoovered the stairs yesterday. = 我昨天吸了楼梯。
They hoovered the living room before the party. = 他们在派对前吸了客厅。


现在完成时:
She has hoovered the entire house. = 她已经把整个房子都吸尘了。
He has hoovered up all the crumbs. = 他已经把所有的面包屑都吸起来了。


过去完成时:
I had hoovered the carpet before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前我已经吸过地毯了。
They had hoovered the car before they left for the airport. = 他们出发去机场前已经吸过车了。


将来时:
I will hoover the bedroom later. = 我一会儿会吸卧室。
She will hoover the kitchen after dinner. = 她晚饭后会吸厨房。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would hoover the floors. = 如果我有时间，我会吸地板。
I wish I had hoovered before the party. = 我希望派对前我已经吸过尘了。


被动语态:
The carpets are hoovered weekly. = 地毯每周都吸尘。
The car was hoovered after the dog rode in it. = 狗坐过车后，车被吸尘了。


疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->
