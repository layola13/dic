necks:/neks/| n.|脖子，颈部；瓶颈；狭窄路段|The narrow part of a body between the head and the shoulders; a narrow part of something. |例句：He has a long neck. 他脖子很长。

例句：The bottle has a narrow neck. 这个瓶子瓶颈很窄。


近义词：throat, nape

反义词：无


词源:古英语 *hnecca
