The word "overappraisal" is a compound word formed from:

* **appraisal**:  A noun meaning "the act of assessing something."  This comes from the verb "appraise," which has roots in Old French *apriser* ("to set a price on").  The ultimate origin traces back to Latin *appreciare* ("to set a value on").
