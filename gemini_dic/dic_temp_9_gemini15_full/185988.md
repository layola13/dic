rewatering:/ˌriːˈwɔːtərɪŋ/| n.|重新注水；补水；复水|The act of supplying water again to something that has become dry or depleted.  This often refers to environmental restoration projects, aiming to restore water levels in depleted ecosystems, such as wetlands or rivers.|例句：The rewatering of the dried-up marsh is a crucial step in its ecological recovery.  重新注水干涸的沼泽是其生态恢复的关键步骤。


The rewatering project aims to restore the river's natural flow.  重新注水项目旨在恢复河流的自然水流。|近义词：rehydration, irrigation, replenishment|反义词：dewatering, desiccation
