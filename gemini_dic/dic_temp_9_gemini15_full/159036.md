pneumatocele: /njuːˈmætəˌsiːl/ | n. | 气肿; 气囊肿 | A pneumatocele is a collection of air within the lung tissue or other organ, often caused by trauma or infection.  It can appear as a cyst-like structure on imaging studies.  | Example: The chest X-ray revealed a large pneumatocele in the right lung.  胸部X光片显示右肺有一个大的气肿。 |近义词: air cyst, emphysema (in some contexts) |反义词:  None directly antonymous, but terms describing solid masses would be contrasting. |  名词
