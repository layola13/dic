equinoxes:/ˈiːkwɪnɒksɪz/| n.|春分秋分，昼夜平分点|The equinoxes are the two days of the year when day and night are of approximately equal length.|例句：The autumn equinox is usually around September 22 or 23. 秋分通常在9月22日或23日左右。 The spring equinox marks the beginning of spring. 春分标志着春天的开始。|近义词：equinoctial points|反义词：solstice

一般现在时:
The equinoxes occur twice a year. = 春分秋分一年发生两次。
Day and night are of equal length during the equinoxes. = 在春分秋分期间，昼夜等长。
