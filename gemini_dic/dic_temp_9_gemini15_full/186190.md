rhetorics:/ˈrɛtərɪks/| n.|修辞学；雄辩术；华丽的辞藻|The art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing, especially the use of figures of speech and other compositional techniques.|例句：His speech was full of empty rhetoric. 他的演讲充满了空洞的辞藻。|例句：The course covers the basics of rhetoric and composition. 这门课程涵盖了修辞学和写作的基础知识。|近义词：eloquence, oratory, discourse|反义词：silence, taciturnity
