gymnolaema:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈliːmə/| n. |裸子植物|A gymnolaema is a plant belonging to the Gymnolaemata, a class of bryozoans characterized by a non-calcified, leathery or membranous zooecium (the individual chamber of an animal in a bryozoan colony).  These are often found in marine environments. |例句：The research focused on the diversity of gymnolaemata in the coastal waters. 研究集中在沿海水域裸子植物的多样性上。|近义词：bryozoan (more general term)|反义词：N/A|名词
