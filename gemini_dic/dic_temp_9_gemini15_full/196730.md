sewing:/ˈsoʊɪŋ/|/ˈsəʊɪŋ/| n. v. |缝纫；缝合|The act of stitching fabric together with a needle and thread; the craft or occupation of sewing.  It can also refer to the act of uniting things together, metaphorically.|例句：She enjoys sewing as a hobby. 她喜欢缝纫作为一项爱好。

She is sewing a button onto her shirt.  她在衬衫上缝一个纽扣。


近义词：stitching, needlework, tailoring|反义词：unsewing, ripping, tearing|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I sew every Sunday. = 我每个星期天都缝纫。
She sews beautiful dresses. = 她缝制漂亮的衣服。


一般过去时:
I sewed a new dress last week. = 我上周缝了一件新衣服。
They sewed the tear in the fabric. = 他们缝补了布料上的裂缝。


现在完成时:
I have sewn many quilts in my life. = 我一生缝制了许多棉被。
She has sewn all the buttons back on. = 她已经把所有的纽扣都缝回去了。


过去完成时:
I had sewn the dress before the party. = 我在派对之前已经缝好了这件衣服。
They had sewn the costumes before the play began. = 在戏剧开始之前，他们已经缝好了戏服。


将来时:
I will sew a new curtain tomorrow. = 我明天会缝制一个新的窗帘。
She will sew the buttons on later. = 她稍后会把纽扣缝上。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sew a quilt. = 如果我有更多时间，我会缝制一件棉被。
I wish I could sew as well as my grandmother. = 我希望我能像我祖母一样擅长缝纫。


被动语态:
The buttons were sewn on securely. = 纽扣被牢固地缝上了。
The dress is being sewn by a professional tailor. = 这件衣服正在由专业的裁缝缝制。


疑问句:
Do you sew? = 你会缝纫吗？
Are you sewing a dress? = 你正在缝制一件衣服吗？


否定句:
I don't sew very often. = 我不经常缝纫。
She didn't sew the button on properly. = 她没有把纽扣缝好。
