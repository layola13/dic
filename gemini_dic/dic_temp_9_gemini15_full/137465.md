nontitular: /ˌnɒnˈtaɪtjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非名义上的；非头衔的 | Not holding a titular position; not bearing a title.  It describes something that is not officially or nominally designated by a title or name. | The nontitular head of the organization exerted considerable influence.  这个组织的非名义领导人施加了相当大的影响力。 |近义词： unofficial, informal, de facto | 反义词： titular, official
