nonrefrigerated:/ˌnɒnriˈfrɪdʒəreɪtɪd/|/ˌnɑːnriˈfrɪdʒəreɪtɪd/| adj. |未冷藏的，未冷冻的 |Not kept cold by refrigeration.  This term applies to food and other items that are not stored at low temperatures to prevent spoilage.| 例句：The nonrefrigerated milk had soured. = 未冷藏的牛奶变酸了。 The nonrefrigerated leftovers should be eaten within a few hours. = 未冷藏的剩菜应该在几小时内吃完。|近义词：unrefrigerated, chilled (反义), frozen (反义)|反义词：refrigerated, chilled, frozen|形容词
