handgun:/ˈhændɡʌn/| n. |手枪|A firearm designed to be held and fired with one hand.  Handguns are typically smaller and lighter than other firearms, such as rifles or shotguns. They are widely used for self-defense, law enforcement, and sport shooting.|例句：He pulled out a handgun and pointed it at the intruder. 他拔出手枪指着入侵者。

例句：The police officer confiscated the illegal handgun. 警官没收了那支非法手枪。


近义词：pistol, revolver|反义词：rifle, shotgun|名词
