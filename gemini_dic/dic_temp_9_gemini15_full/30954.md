/ʃɛz/ | n. |轻便椅子，躺椅|A chaise is a light, comfortable chair, often with arms and a padded seat and back, sometimes designed for reclining.  It can be made of various materials, such as wood, wicker, or metal. Chaises longues, particularly, are designed for reclining.|The old chaise lounge sat by the fireplace, a perfect spot for a quiet afternoon.|  armchair, lounge chair |  stool, hard chair | 名词
