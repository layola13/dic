dunk:/dʌŋk/| vt. n.|猛按；浸；投篮|To submerge something quickly in a liquid; to force something down; to throw (a basketball) through the hoop. |例句：He dunked the cookie in his milk. 他把饼干浸在牛奶里。 He dunked the basketball with style. 他潇洒地投了个篮。|近义词：submerge, plunge, slam|反义词：lift, raise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dunks the ball every game. = 他每场比赛都投篮。
She dunks her donut in coffee. = 她把甜甜圈浸在咖啡里。


一般过去时:
He dunked the basketball and scored. = 他投篮得分了。
She dunked the biscuit in her tea. = 她把饼干浸在茶里。


现在完成时:
He has dunked the ball many times this season. = 这个赛季他投篮很多次了。
She has dunked her cookies in milk many times. = 她多次把饼干浸在牛奶里。


过去完成时:
He had dunked the ball before the buzzer. = 在终场哨响之前他已经投篮了。
She had dunked the bread in the soup before she ate it. = 在吃之前她已经把面包浸在汤里了。


将来时:
He will dunk the ball at the next game. = 他将在下一场比赛投篮。
She will dunk her cake in the ice cream. = 她会把蛋糕浸在冰淇淋里。


虚拟语气:
If he were taller, he could dunk the ball easily. = 如果他更高，他就能轻松投篮。
If I had more time, I would dunk my cookies more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地浸饼干。
