planterdom:/ˈplæntərdəm/| n. |种植者阶层；种植园主社会|The world of planters; the collective body of planters.  It often implies a social system or community dominated by planters, especially in historical contexts like the antebellum South.  |例句：The planterdom of the antebellum South held significant political and economic power.  南方战前的种植园主阶层掌握着巨大的政治和经济权力。|近义词：plantocracy, plantation elite|反义词：None specifically, but concepts like "yeomanry" or "laboring class" represent contrasting social groups.|名词
