hanch:/hɑːntʃ/| n.|直觉；预感；(人的)臀部|A hunch is a feeling or guess that something is true, even though you have no proof.  It's an intuitive understanding or suspicion.|例句：I had a hunch that he was lying. 我预感他在说谎。|例句：She sat down, her hanch against the wall. 她坐下来，臀部靠着墙。|近义词：feeling, intuition, suspicion|反义词：certainty, proof


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have a hunch he'll be late. = 我预感他会迟到。
She often gets hunches about things. = 她经常对事情有预感。

一般过去时:
I had a hunch that something was wrong. = 我预感有什么不对劲。
He had a hunch she was hiding something. = 他预感她在隐瞒什么。


现在完成时:
I've had a hunch about this project for weeks. = 我对这个项目已经有了几周的预感。
She's had many hunches that turned out to be correct. = 她有很多预感后来被证明是正确的。


过去完成时:
I had had a hunch that he would propose, so I wasn't surprised. = 我之前就预感他会求婚，所以我不惊讶。
She had had a hunch about the accident before the news report. = 在新闻报道之前，她就对事故有了预感。


将来时:
I think I'll have a hunch about the winner soon. = 我想我很快就会对获胜者有预感。
She believes she will have a hunch about the solution tomorrow. = 她相信明天她会对解决方案有预感。


虚拟语气:
If I had a hunch, I would tell you. = 如果我有预感，我会告诉你的。
I wish I had a hunch about the future. = 我希望我对未来有预感。


被动语态:
(This word is rarely used in passive voice because it describes a feeling)  


疑问句:
Do you have a hunch about who did it? = 你对谁干的这件事有预感吗？
Has she ever had a hunch that proved to be false? = 她曾经有过被证明是错误的预感吗？


否定句:
I don't have a hunch about what will happen next. = 我对接下来会发生什么没有预感。
She didn't have a hunch that it would rain. = 她没有预感会下雨。



PHRASE->
a hunch that = 一个...的预感
follow your hunch = 凭直觉去做
have a hunch = 有预感
a gut hunch = 一种直觉
act on a hunch = 凭直觉行事
