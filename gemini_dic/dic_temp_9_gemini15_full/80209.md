godmothers:/ˈɡɑːdmʌðərz/ | n. | 教母；女教父 |  A godmother is a woman who acts as a sponsor in a Christian baptism, promising to guide the child's spiritual development.  The term also refers to a woman who acts as a mentor or supportive figure in a child's life, similar to a godmother in role but without the formal religious context. |  She is my godmother and has been a guiding influence in my life. 她是我的教母，一直以来对我的人生影响深远。 My godmother gave me a beautiful necklace for my graduation. 我的教母送给我一条漂亮的项链作为毕业礼物。|近义词：sponsor, mentor, guardian |反义词：godfather (男教父)
