peptonuria: /ˌpɛptəˈnjʊəriə/| n. | 胨尿症 | The presence of peptones in the urine, indicating significant protein breakdown. This condition can be a sign of various medical issues, including severe infections, kidney damage, or digestive disorders.  | Example:  The patient's peptonuria was a key indicator of the advanced stage of their kidney disease.  | 近义词:  proteinuria (though peptonuria is a more specific type of proteinuria) | 反义词:  absence of peptones in urine
