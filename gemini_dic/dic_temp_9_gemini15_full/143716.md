英文解析: This word is a relatively uncommon adjective implying something lasted longer than something else, or beyond a expected time.  It combines the prefix "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) with the verb "linger" (meaning to stay or continue for a longer time than expected).


例句:

The joy of the victory outlingered the pain of the struggle.  胜利的喜悦比奋斗的痛苦持续的时间更长。


The effects of the medication outlingered the initial dose.  药物的效果持续的时间比最初的剂量预期的更长。
