preevolutional: /ˌpriːˌɛvəˈluːʃənəl/ | adj. |进化前的；进化之前的| Existing or occurring before the evolution of a particular species or group.  Describing a period, state, or condition that existed before evolutionary changes. |例句：The preevolutional forms of these insects were quite different. 这些昆虫进化前的形态大相径庭。

The preevolutional environment was markedly different from today's. 进化前的环境与今天大相径庭。


近义词：prehistoric, antecedent, primordial

反义词：post-evolutionary, evolved


词性：形容词
