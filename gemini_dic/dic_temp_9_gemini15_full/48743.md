definers:/dɪˈfaɪnərz/ | n. | 定义者，界定者 | Those who establish or determine the meaning or boundaries of something.  They clarify, specify, or explain concepts, ideas, or terms. | The scientists acted as definers of the new species' characteristics. 科学家充当了对这种新物种特征的界定者。 The judges were the ultimate definers of the competition's rules. 评委是比赛规则的最终界定者。 | classifiers, specifiers, interpreters | undefiners (反义词并非完全对立，而是相对缺乏明确界定) | 名词
