skiffs:/skɪfs/| n.|小船，轻舟|A small, light boat, often used for fishing or recreation.  Skiffs are typically rowed or sailed, and are often quite shallow.|例句：We spent the afternoon sailing in a small skiff. 我们在一条小船上度过了下午。

例句：The fishermen used their skiffs to navigate the shallow waters. 渔民们用他们的轻舟在浅水中航行。


近义词：dinghy, shallop, punt


反义词：battleship, ocean liner, tanker


词性:名词
