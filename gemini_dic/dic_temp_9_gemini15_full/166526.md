presbyopic: /ˌprɛzbiˈɒpɪk/ | adj. | 老视的，远视的 |  Relating to or characteristic of presbyopia; affected by presbyopia.  Presbyopia is a gradual loss of your eyes' ability to focus on nearby objects. It's a natural part of aging. |  例句：He needs reading glasses because he's become presbyopic. 他需要老花镜，因为他得了老花眼。  |  例句：Many people experience presbyopic vision changes as they get older. 许多人在变老的过程中会经历老视的视力变化。 | 近义词：farsighted (in the context of presbyopia) | 反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but the opposite would be having excellent near vision)
