invectively:/ɪnˈvektivli/| adv.| 痛骂地；辱骂地；尖刻地 |In a way that uses strong criticism or insults.  It describes the manner in which something is said or done, characterized by harsh, abusive language.|例句：He invectively criticized the government's policies. 他尖刻地批评了政府的政策。|例句：She responded invectively to his accusations. 她愤怒地回应了他的指控。|近义词：abusively, vituperatively, scathingly|反义词：complimentarily, flatteringly, kindly|副词
