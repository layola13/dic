damneder:/ˈdæmnədər/| adj.|更该死的，更遭殃的|A comparative form of "damned," intensifying the negative aspect.  It expresses a higher degree of condemnation, misfortune, or unpleasantness.  It's considered informal and often used for emphasis.|例句：This project is a damneder mess than I initially thought.  这个项目比我最初想的更糟糕。The weather is getting damneder by the minute. 天气每分每秒都在恶化。|近义词：worse, more cursed, more infernal|反义词：better, improved, blessed
