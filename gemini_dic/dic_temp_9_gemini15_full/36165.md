coffeepot:/ˈkɔːfɪpɒt/| n. |咖啡壶|A container, usually made of metal or ceramic, used to make and serve coffee.  It typically has a handle, a spout, and a lid.|例句：She poured the coffee from the coffeepot into our mugs. 她把咖啡壶里的咖啡倒进我们的杯子里。|例句：The coffeepot whistled merrily, signaling that the coffee was ready. 咖啡壶愉快地吹着口哨，表示咖啡已经煮好了。|近义词：coffee maker, percolator|反义词：None|名词
