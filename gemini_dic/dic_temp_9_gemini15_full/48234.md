decontaminated:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|已净化，已消毒的|Removed of contaminants; made clean and safe.|The decontaminated area was reopened to the public.  已净化的区域重新向公众开放。|近义词：purified, sterilized, disinfected|反义词：contaminated, polluted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital decontaminates all surgical instruments. = 医院对所有手术器械进行消毒。
This process decontaminates the water supply. = 此过程使供水得到净化。


一般过去时:
They decontaminated the site after the chemical spill. = 化学品泄漏后，他们对现场进行了净化处理。
The emergency services decontaminated the victims. = 紧急救援人员对受害者进行了消毒处理。


现在完成时:
The team has decontaminated the building. = 小组已经对建筑物进行了净化处理。
The authorities have decontaminated the affected area. = 当局已对受影响区域进行了消毒。


过去完成时:
The workers had decontaminated the equipment before starting the repairs. = 工人们在开始维修之前已经对设备进行了净化处理。
The scientists had decontaminated the lab before the experiment. = 科学家们在实验之前已经对实验室进行了消毒。


将来时:
The crew will decontaminate the spacecraft upon its return. = 飞船返回后，机组人员将对其进行净化处理。
The government will decontaminate the water source. = 政府将对水源进行净化处理。


虚拟语气:
If they had decontaminated the area sooner, the damage would have been less severe. = 如果他们早点对该地区进行净化处理，损失就不会那么严重。
It's vital that the equipment be decontaminated before use. = 使用前对设备进行消毒至关重要。


被动语态:
The contaminated water was decontaminated using advanced technology. = 受污染的水使用先进技术进行了净化处理。
The area was decontaminated by the specialized team. = 该区域由专业团队进行了净化处理。


疑问句:
Has the area been decontaminated yet? = 该区域是否已经净化处理？
Will the equipment be decontaminated before transport? = 设备在运输前是否会进行净化处理？


否定句:
The area has not yet been fully decontaminated. = 该区域尚未完全净化处理。
They did not decontaminate the tools properly. = 他们没有正确地对工具进行消毒。
