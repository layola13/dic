revindicate:/ˈrɛvɪndɪkeɪt/| vt.| 维护；证明…正确；为…辩护 | To claim or regain something lost or taken away; to justify or vindicate.  |例句：The lawyer worked hard to revindicate his client's innocence. 律师努力为其当事人的清白辩护。

例句：The government's new policy is intended to revindicate its commitment to environmental protection. 政府的新政策旨在维护其对环境保护的承诺。


近义词：vindicate, justify, uphold, defend, assert |反义词：condemn, refute, deny, invalidate
