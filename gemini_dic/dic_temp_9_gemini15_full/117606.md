megaphones:/ˈmɛɡəfoʊnz/ | n. |扩音器，喇叭 | A megaphone is a cone-shaped device used to amplify the voice of a speaker, making it audible over a greater distance.  It works by concentrating sound waves. |例句：The coach used a megaphone to address the team from across the field. 教练用扩音器对着球场另一端的队员讲话。


The protesters used megaphones to make their voices heard. 抗议者使用扩音器来让他们的声音被听到。|近义词：loudspeaker, bullhorn |反义词：whisper, murmur
