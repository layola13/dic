ready-made:/ˈrediˌmeɪd/| adj.|现成的；预制的；制作好的|Already made and available for immediate use; not requiring further preparation or assembly.|例句：I bought a ready-made suit for the wedding.  我为婚礼买了一套现成的西装。|例句：She prefers ready-made meals to cooking from scratch. 她更喜欢现成的饭菜而不是自己从头做起。|近义词：prefabricated, prepared, off-the-shelf|反义词：homemade, custom-made, tailor-made


USAGE->

The store sells many ready-made clothes. = 这家商店卖很多现成的衣服。
These ready-made meals are very convenient. = 这些现成的饭菜很方便。


一般过去时:
