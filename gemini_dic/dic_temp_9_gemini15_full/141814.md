orbited:/ˈɔːrbɪtɪd/ | v. | 绕轨道运行；环绕  | To move in an orbit around a celestial body. |例句：The moon orbited the Earth. 月球绕地球运行。

The satellite orbited the planet for several years.  这颗卫星绕这颗行星运行了好几年。


近义词：revolved, circled, circled around | 反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The moon orbits the Earth. = 月球绕地球运行。
The planets orbit the sun. = 行星绕太阳运行。

一般过去时:
The satellite orbited the planet for a year. = 这颗卫星绕这颗行星运行了一年。
Hubble orbited the Earth for many years. = 哈勃望远镜绕地球运行了许多年。

现在完成时:
The spacecraft has orbited Mars since last year. = 自去年以来，这艘宇宙飞船一直在绕火星运行。
The satellite has orbited the Earth for over a decade. = 这颗卫星绕地球运行已超过十年。

过去完成时:

The astronauts had orbited the moon several times before landing. = 宇航员在着陆之前已经绕月球运行了好几次。

将来时:

The telescope will orbit the sun, gathering data. = 望远镜将绕太阳运行，收集数据。

虚拟语气:
