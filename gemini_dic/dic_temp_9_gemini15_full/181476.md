rejoint:/ˌriːˈdʒɔɪnt/| v.|重新连接；重新加入；接合|To rejoin means to join again; to come together again after being separated.  It can also refer to connecting two things that were previously disconnected. |例句：The two pieces of the vase were carefully rejoined.  (花瓶的两块被仔细地重新粘合在一起。)

The separated hikers rejoined the group after an hour. (分开的一小时后，分散的徒步旅行者重新加入了队伍。)


近义词：reconnect, reunite, rejoin, recombine, reattach|反义词：separate, disconnect, disjoin, sever|动词
