hide-out:/ˈhaɪdaʊt/| n. |隐匿处，藏身处 |A secret or secluded place used for hiding.  A hideout is a place where someone can hide, often from danger or the law.  |例句：The robbers made their hideout in an abandoned mine.  强盗们在一个废弃的矿井里藏身。

The police raided the suspected drug dealer's hideout. 警察突袭了那个涉嫌贩毒者的藏匿处。|近义词：hiding place, secret place, refuge, sanctuary |反义词：open space, public place |名词
