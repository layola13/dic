hiller:/ˈhɪlər/| n. |丘陵地带耕作者；土豆收获机| A hiller is a person who cultivates hilly land, or a machine used for harvesting potatoes.  Hillers are often used in agriculture to create ridges in the soil, which helps with drainage and weed control.  They can also be used to harvest potatoes by lifting them from the ground.|The farmer used a hiller to create neat rows in his potato field.  农民用土豆收获机在他的土豆田里整理出整齐的垄。| cultivator, planter | plain, flat land | 名词
