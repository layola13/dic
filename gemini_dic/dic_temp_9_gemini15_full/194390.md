self-promotion: /ˌsɛlf prəˈmoʊʃən/ | n. |自我推销，自我宣传 | The act of promoting oneself or one's work, often to gain recognition or advancement.  It involves actively highlighting one's skills, accomplishments, and value to others. This can be done through various methods, including networking, creating a strong personal brand, and showcasing one's work.  |

例句：

He engaged in self-promotion by posting his achievements on social media. = 他通过在社交媒体上发布自己的成就来进行自我推销。

Her self-promotion tactics included giving presentations at conferences and publishing articles in relevant journals. = 她的自我推销策略包括在会议上做演讲以及在相关期刊上发表文章。


近义词：self-advertisement, promotion, publicity, marketing oneself

反义词：self-deprecation, humility, modesty
