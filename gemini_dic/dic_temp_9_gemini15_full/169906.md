prospects:/ˈprɒspektz/| n. |前景；可能性；期望；顾客；(尤指有潜力的)客户|The word "prospects" refers to the possibility or likelihood of something happening, especially something good.  It can also refer to potential customers or clients. |例句：The prospects for economic growth are good.  经济增长的前景良好。

The company is looking for new prospects. 这家公司正在寻找新的客户。|近义词：opportunities, possibilities, chances, outlook, clients, customers |反义词：impossibilities, uncertainties, bleak outlook
