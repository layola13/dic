grillroom:/ˈɡrɪlruːm/| n.|烧烤室；烤肉餐厅|A room or restaurant equipped with grills for cooking and serving grilled food.  It often implies a more casual and informal dining atmosphere than a formal restaurant.|例句：We had dinner at the grillroom, enjoying delicious grilled steaks. 我们在烧烤室吃了晚餐，享用了美味的烤牛排。


例句：The hotel's grillroom is known for its excellent seafood. 这家酒店的烧烤室以其出色的海鲜而闻名。


近义词：steakhouse, barbecue restaurant, barbecue joint


反义词：formal dining room, fine dining restaurant


词性：名词
