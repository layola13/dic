sigillarid:/ˈsɪɡɪləˌrɪd/| n. |似印章的；印章状的|Relating to or characteristic of sigillaria, an extinct genus of arborescent lycopods with distinctive, vertically arranged leaf scars resembling seals.  The term often refers to the characteristic leaf scars themselves.|The sigillarid fossils found in this area are remarkably well-preserved.|近义词：scaly (in reference to the appearance), patterned|反义词：smooth, unmarked
