saturated:/ˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 饱和的；浸透的；充满的 |  Completely soaked or filled with something.  |例句：The sponge was saturated with water.  海绵浸透了水。

例句：The market is saturated with similar products. 市场充斥着类似的产品。

近义词：soaked, drenched, permeated, imbued | 反义词：dry, unsaturated, deficient

词源：源自拉丁语 *saturare，意为“to fill”，由 *satis（足够）+ *are（动词后缀）构成。

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soil is saturated with water after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，土壤饱和了水分。
The market is saturated with cheap smartphones. = 市场充斥着廉价智能手机。


一般过去时:
The fabric was saturated with dye. = 布料被染料浸透了。
The sponge was saturated with cleaning solution. = 海绵被清洁剂浸透了。


现在完成时:
The ground has been saturated by weeks of rain. = 地面已经被几周的雨水浸透了。
The news has saturated the media for days. = 这条新闻已经占据了媒体好几天了。


过去完成时:
The soil had been saturated before the landslide. = 山体滑坡前，土壤已经饱和了水分。
The market had been saturated with that product before they launched a new one. = 在他们推出新产品之前，市场已经被该产品饱和了。


将来时:
The soil will be saturated if it continues to rain. = 如果继续下雨，土壤将会饱和。
The market will likely be saturated with electric vehicles in the near future. = 未来不久，电动汽车市场可能会饱和。


虚拟语气:
If the soil were saturated, the plants would thrive. = 如果土壤饱和了水分，植物就会茁壮成长。
If the market were saturated, their new product wouldn't sell well. = 如果市场已经饱和，他们的新产品就不会卖得好。


被动语态:
The fabric is saturated with a special solution. = 布料被一种特殊的溶液浸透了。
The air was saturated with the smell of freshly cut grass. = 空气中弥漫着新割草的香味。（空气被新割草的香味饱和了）


疑问句:
Is the sponge saturated? = 海绵饱和了吗？
Is the market saturated with this type of product? = 市场对这种产品饱和了吗？


否定句:
The soil is not yet saturated. = 土壤尚未饱和。
The market is not saturated with this niche product. = 这个利基产品市场并未饱和。
