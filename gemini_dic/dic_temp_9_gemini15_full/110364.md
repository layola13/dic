lightweight: /ˈlaɪtweɪt/ | adj. | 轻量级的；轻便的；不重要的 |  Something that is lightweight is not heavy. It can also refer to something that is not important or significant. | 例句：This new laptop is lightweight and easy to carry. 这台新笔记本电脑轻便易携。

例句：His argument was lightweight and unconvincing. 他的论点轻飘飘的，缺乏说服力。


近义词：light, flimsy, insubstantial, unimportant | 反义词：heavyweight, substantial, important


词源:  The word "lightweight" is a compound word formed from "light" and "weight".  "Light" meaning not heavy, and "weight" referring to the heaviness of an object.  The combination creates an adjective describing something of little weight or importance.
