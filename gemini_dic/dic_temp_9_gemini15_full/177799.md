receive:/rɪˈsiːv/ | vt. |接收，收到，获得 |to get or be given something |例句：I received a letter from my friend. 我收到我朋友的一封信。|例句：She received a promotion at work.她在工作中获得了晋升。|近义词：get, obtain, accept |反义词：send, transmit, give


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I receive many emails every day. = 我每天收到很多邮件。
They receive a salary at the end of the month. = 他们月底领工资。

一般过去时:
He received a package yesterday. = 他昨天收到一个包裹。
She received good news about her exam. = 她收到了关于考试的好消息。

现在完成时:
I have received your email. = 我已经收到你的邮件了。
They have received several awards this year. = 他们今年已经获得了好几个奖项。

过去完成时:
He had received the news before I called. = 我打电话之前他已经收到消息了。
She had received many letters of support. = 她已经收到了很多支持信。

将来时:
I will receive my results next week. = 我下周会收到我的成绩。
They will receive their bonuses soon. = 他们很快就会收到奖金。

虚拟语气:
If I received a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我收到一百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I had received a better education. = 我希望我接受过更好的教育。

被动语态:
The package was received yesterday. = 包裹昨天收到了。
The award will be received by the winner next month. = 奖项将于下个月由获奖者领取。

疑问句:
Did you receive my letter? = 你收到我的信了吗？
Have they received the payment yet? = 他们收到付款了吗？

否定句:
I did not receive your message. = 我没有收到你的消息。
She hasn't received an answer to her question. = 她还没有收到她问题的答案。


PHRASE->
