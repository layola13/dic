experiment:/ɪkˈsperɪmənt/ | n., v. | 实验；试验 |  A test done in order to learn something or discover if something works.  |  The scientist conducted an experiment to test his hypothesis.  科学家进行了一项实验来检验他的假设。  | trial, test, study | observation, control | 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists conduct many experiments. = 科学家们进行许多实验。
We experiment with new recipes. = 我们尝试新的食谱。


一般过去时:
They experimented with different methods. = 他们尝试了不同的方法。
I conducted an experiment yesterday. = 我昨天做了一个实验。


现在完成时:
She has experimented with various techniques. = 她尝试过各种技巧。
Scientists have conducted numerous experiments on this topic. = 科学家们在这个课题上进行了无数的实验。


过去完成时:
They had already experimented with that approach before. = 他们之前已经尝试过那种方法了。
We had completed the experiment by nightfall. = 我们在夜幕降临前完成了实验。


将来时:
We will conduct a series of experiments next week. = 我们下周将进行一系列实验。
They will experiment with a new drug. = 他们将尝试一种新药。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would conduct more experiments. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进行更多实验。
It would be interesting to see what would happen if we experimented with a different approach. = 如果我们尝试不同的方法，看看会发生什么将会很有趣。


被动语态:
The experiment was carefully designed. = 实验被精心设计。
Many experiments are being conducted in the lab. = 实验室里正在进行许多实验。


疑问句:
What kind of experiment are you conducting? = 你正在进行什么实验？
Have you ever done this experiment before? = 你以前做过这个实验吗？


否定句:
