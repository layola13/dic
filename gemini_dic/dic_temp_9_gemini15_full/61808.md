emphysemas:/ˈɛmfɪˈsiːməz/| n. |肺气肿（复数）|A plural form of emphysema, referring to multiple instances of the condition characterized by the abnormal enlargement of the air sacs in the lungs, leading to shortness of breath and other respiratory problems.|例句：The doctor diagnosed him with severe emphysemas, requiring immediate treatment. 医生诊断他患有严重的肺气肿，需要立即治疗。|近义词：pulmonary disease, COPD (chronic obstructive pulmonary disease)|反义词：healthy lungs
