melanins: /ˈmɛlənɪnz/ | n. | 黑色素 | Dark brown to black pigments occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals.  They are responsible for the color of skin, hair, and eyes, and protect against harmful ultraviolet (UV) radiation from the sun. |  The high concentration of melanins in his skin protected him from sunburn.  他皮肤中黑色素的浓度很高，这保护了他免受晒伤。 |  pigments | albinism (反义词，缺乏黑色素)
