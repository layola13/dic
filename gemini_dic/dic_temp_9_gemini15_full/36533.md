collaboration:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃən/| n. |合作，协作|The act of working with someone or a group of people to achieve a common goal.  It involves joint effort, shared responsibility, and mutual contribution.|例句：The success of the project depended on close collaboration between the team members.  这个项目的成功取决于团队成员之间的密切合作。

例句：The artists' collaboration resulted in a stunning masterpiece.  艺术家们的合作创作出一件令人惊叹的杰作。


近义词：cooperation, teamwork, joint effort, partnership

反义词：competition, antagonism, conflict


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We collaborate on many projects. = 我们合作许多项目。
They collaborate closely with their partners. = 他们与合作伙伴密切合作。


一般过去时:
We collaborated on a research paper last year. = 去年我们合作撰写了一篇研究论文。
The two companies collaborated to develop a new product. = 这两家公司合作开发了一种新产品。


现在完成时:
We have collaborated on several successful projects. = 我们已经合作完成了几项成功的项目。
They have collaborated extensively in the field of medicine. = 他们在医学领域开展了广泛的合作。


过去完成时:
They had collaborated for years before they published their findings. = 他们在发表研究成果之前已经合作多年了。
We had collaborated on the design before the final presentation. = 我们在最终演示之前就已经合作完成了设计。


将来时:
We will collaborate on a new marketing strategy next quarter. = 下个季度我们将合作制定新的营销策略。
They will collaborate with international experts on this research. = 他们将在该研究中与国际专家合作。


虚拟语气:
If we collaborated more effectively, we would achieve better results. = 如果我们更有效地合作，我们将取得更好的成果。
I wish we had collaborated earlier on this project. = 我希望我们在这个项目上早点合作。


被动语态:
The project was completed through collaboration between several departments. = 该项目是通过几个部门的合作完成的。
The design was created through a collaboration of artists and engineers. = 该设计是通过艺术家和工程师的合作创作的。


疑问句:
Will you collaborate with us on this project? = 你会在这个项目上与我们合作吗？
Are they collaborating with any other companies? = 他们正在与其他公司合作吗？


否定句:
We did not collaborate with them on the previous project. = 我们在上一个项目中没有与他们合作。
They refused to collaborate with their competitors. = 他们拒绝与竞争对手合作。
