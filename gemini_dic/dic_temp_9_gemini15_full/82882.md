gulped:/ɡʌlpt/| v.|吞咽；大口喝；|to swallow (something) quickly and with difficulty, often because of fear or nervousness|例句：He gulped down the water in one go. 他一口喝光了水。

She gulped, unable to speak. 她哽咽着，说不出话来。


近义词：swallow, gulp down, bolt down|反义词：spit out, reject|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gulps his food down quickly. 他很快地把食物吞了下去。
She gulps nervously before speaking. 她说话前紧张地吞咽着。

一般过去时:
He gulped the wine. 他一口喝了酒。
She gulped down the medicine. 她把药一口吞了下去。

现在完成时:
He has gulped down three glasses of water. 他已经喝光了三杯水。
She has gulped all the soup. 她已经把汤都喝光了。

过去完成时:
He had gulped the news before he reacted. 他在做出反应之前已经吞下了这个消息。
She had gulped the entire bottle of water. 她已经喝光了整瓶水。

将来时:
He will gulp down the entire glass of juice. 他会把整杯果汁一口喝完。
She will gulp the medicine as soon as the doctor instructs.  医生一吩咐，她就会把药吞下去。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not gulp down the medicine so quickly. 如果我是你，我不会这么快地把药吞下去。
I wish I hadn't gulped down that glass of water so quickly. 我真希望我没有那么快地喝完那杯水。

被动语态:
The medicine was gulped down quickly by the patient. 药物被病人迅速吞咽。
The entire pie was gulped down in a few minutes. 整块派几分钟就被吃光了。

疑问句:
Did he gulp the entire pizza? 他把整个披萨都吃光了吗？
Will she gulp down the whole thing? 她会把所有东西都吞下去吗？

否定句:
He didn't gulp his food. 他没有狼吞虎咽地吃东西。
She didn't gulp down the drink. 她没有一口喝下饮料。


PHRASE->
gulp down:  吞咽；大口喝下
gulp up:  迅速吞咽
gulp something back:  强忍住（眼泪、情绪等）
a gulp of air:  一口空气
a gulp of water:  一口水
