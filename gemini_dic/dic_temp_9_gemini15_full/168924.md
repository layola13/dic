professionally:/prəˈfɛʃənəli/ | adv. | 专业地，职业地 | In a manner characteristic of a professional; competently and skillfully.  |例句：She handled the difficult situation professionally and calmly. 她专业而冷静地处理了这个棘手的情况。

例句：The surgeon performed the operation professionally and with precision. 外科医生专业而精确地进行了手术。


近义词：competently, skillfully, expertly, adeptly

反义词：amateurishly, incompetently, clumsily
