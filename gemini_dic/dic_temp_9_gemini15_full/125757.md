murderable: /ˈmɜːrdərəbl/ | adj. | 应受处死的；极度令人厌恶的；极易激怒的 |  Worthy of being murdered; extremely annoying or irritating; easily provoked to anger. |

例句：

His behavior was simply murderable; I wanted to strangle him.  = 他的行为简直令人难以忍受；我想掐死他。

That movie was so bad, it was murderable. = 那部电影太烂了，简直让人想杀人。


近义词：abhorrent, detestable, infuriating, maddening, provoking

反义词：adorable, delightful, pleasant, charming


词性: 形容词
