mowable: /ˈmoʊəbəl/ | adj. | 可修剪的，可割的 | Capable of being mown or cut with a mowing machine.  | Example:  The lawn is mowable now that the rain has stopped.  现在雨停了，草坪可以修剪了。  The field is mowable, so we can harvest the hay. 这块地可以割草，所以我们可以收割干草。| 近义词：cuttable,  trimmable | 反义词：unmowable, uncuttable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lawn is mowable. = 草坪是可以修剪的。
That field is mowable. = 那块地是可以修剪的。

一般过去时:
The grass was mowable yesterday. = 昨天的草是可以修剪的。
The wheat was mowable last week. = 上周的小麦是可以收割的。

现在完成时:
The field has been mowable all week. = 这块地一整周都是可以收割的。
The lawn has become mowable after the rain. = 雨后草坪可以修剪了。

过去完成时:
The grass had been mowable before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前草是可以修剪的。
The hay had become mowable by the end of summer. = 到夏天结束时，干草可以收割了。

将来时:
The lawn will be mowable tomorrow. = 草坪明天可以修剪了。
The wheat will be mowable next week. = 小麦下周可以收割了。

虚拟语气:
If the grass were mowable, I would cut it. = 如果草可以修剪，我会把它剪掉。
If the field were mowable, we would harvest it. = 如果田地可以收割，我们会收割它。

被动语态:
The lawn is considered mowable by the gardener. = 园丁认为草坪是可以修剪的。
The wheat is deemed mowable by the farmer. = 农民认为小麦是可以收割的。

疑问句:
Is the lawn mowable? = 草坪可以修剪吗？
Is the field mowable today? = 今天这块地可以修剪吗？

否定句:
The lawn is not mowable yet. = 草坪现在还不能修剪。
The field is not mowable because it's too wet. = 田地太湿了，不能修剪。
