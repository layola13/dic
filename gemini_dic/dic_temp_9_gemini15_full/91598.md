hyperaggregating: /ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 超级聚合；过度聚合 | The process of combining or grouping things together to an excessive degree, often leading to inefficiency or loss of granularity.  This term is often used in the context of data processing, network management, and cloud computing. | 例句：The hyperaggregating of network traffic caused a significant slowdown.  = 网络流量的过度聚合导致了严重的减速。

The company's hyperaggregating of customer data led to privacy concerns. = 公司过度聚合客户数据导致了隐私问题。


近义词：overaggregating, consolidating (in an excessive way), clumping


反义词：disaggregating, separating, distributing


词性：动词
