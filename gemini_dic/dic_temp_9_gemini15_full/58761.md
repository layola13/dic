dunked:/dʌŋkt/| vt.|浸，蘸，猛力投掷|To submerge briefly in a liquid; to throw or plunge forcefully.|例句：He dunked the cookie in his milk. 他把饼干浸在牛奶里。

例句：The basketball player dunked the ball with great force.  篮球运动员用力地灌篮。


近义词：dip, immerse, plunge, slam|反义词：lift, raise, retrieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dunks his donut in coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都把甜甜圈浸在咖啡里。
She dunks the vegetables in boiling water before cooking. = 她在烹饪之前将蔬菜浸在沸水中。


一般过去时:
I dunked the basketball and scored! = 我灌篮得分了！
He dunked the cookie into his tea. = 他把饼干浸到茶里。


现在完成时:
He has dunked the ball several times in this game. = 在这场比赛中他已经灌篮好几次了。
She has dunked the bread in the egg mixture before frying. =  她在油炸之前将面包浸在蛋液里。


过去完成时:
They had dunked the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他们已经销毁了证据。
He had dunked the potatoes in oil before serving them. = 他在端上土豆之前已经把它们浸在油里了。


将来时:
I will dunk the chips in the salsa. = 我会把薯条蘸萨尔萨酱。
She will dunk her biscuit in the gravy. = 她将把饼干浸在肉汁里。


虚拟语气:
If I were taller, I would dunk the basketball. = 如果我更高的话，我会灌篮。
If he had more time, he would have dunked the cookies longer. = 如果他有更多时间，他会把饼干浸泡更久。


被动语态:
The cookies were dunked in chocolate. = 饼干浸在巧克力里了。
The basketball was dunked with amazing skill. = 篮球被技巧性地灌篮了。


疑问句:
Did you dunk the bread in the soup? = 你把面包浸在汤里了吗？
Will he dunk the ball during the game? = 他会在比赛中灌篮吗？


否定句:
I didn't dunk the cookies in the milk.  = 我没有把饼干浸在牛奶里。
He didn't dunk the ball successfully. = 他没有成功灌篮。


PHRASE->
dunk a cookie = 蘸饼干
dunk a basketball = 灌篮
dunk in milk = 浸在牛奶里
dunk in chocolate = 浸在巧克力里
dunk someone's head = 把某人的头按进水里


词源:
The word "dunk" originates from the Middle English word "dunken," meaning "to dip" or "to thrust."  The modern meaning, especially in the context of basketball, developed later.
