mockle:/ˈmɒkl/| n.|模仿，嘲弄|To mock or mimic someone or something, often in a playful or teasing way.  It often implies a less aggressive form of mockery than "mock".|例句：The children were mockling the teacher's accent.孩子们在模仿老师的口音。|例句：He mockled his friend's clumsy dance moves. 他模仿了他朋友笨拙的舞步。|近义词：mimic, imitate, ape|反义词：praise, compliment|名词
