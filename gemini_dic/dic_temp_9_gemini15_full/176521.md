re-raise:/ˌriːˈreɪz/| vt.|再次提出，重新提出，加价|To raise something again; to increase a bid or offer.|例句：The committee decided to re-raise the issue of funding. 委员会决定再次提出资金问题。|例句：He re-raised his bid to $10,000. 他把出价提高到一万美元。|近义词：reintroduce, reiterate, raise again|反义词：withdraw, retract, lower


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-raise the question every meeting. = 他们每次会议都会重新提出这个问题。
The company re-raises its prices annually. = 公司每年都会重新提高价格。


一般过去时:
The lawyer re-raised the objection in court. = 律师在法庭上再次提出异议。
She re-raised her concerns about the project. = 她再次表达了她对该项目的担忧。


现在完成时:
The government has re-raised the minimum wage. = 政府已经再次提高了最低工资。
They have re-raised the topic several times. = 他们已经多次重新提起这个话题。


过去完成时:
He had re-raised the issue before the final decision was made. = 在最终决定做出之前，他已经重新提出了这个问题。
The board had re-raised its objections to the merger. = 董事会已再次对合并提出异议。


将来时:
They will re-raise the matter at the next meeting. = 他们将在下次会议上再次提出此事。
The CEO will re-raise the salary cap next year. = 首席执行官明年将再次提高薪资上限。


虚拟语气:
If they re-raised the funding, the project could continue. = 如果他们再次提供资金，项目就可以继续进行。
I wish they would re-raise the issue of safety regulations. = 我希望他们能再次提出安全规章的问题。


被动语态:
The question was re-raised by the opposition party. = 这个问题由反对党再次提出。
The offer was re-raised by the buyer. = 买方再次提高了报价。


疑问句:
Will they re-raise the price of gas? = 他们会再次提高汽油价格吗？
Has the issue been re-raised recently? = 最近这个问题是否被再次提出？


否定句:
They did not re-raise the topic during the discussion. = 他们在讨论中没有再次提起这个话题。
The company will not re-raise salaries this year. = 公司今年不会再次提高薪水。
