condonable: /kənˈdoʊnəbl/ | adj. | 可原谅的，可宽恕的 |  Forgivable; excusable.  Something that is condonable can be forgiven or overlooked. |


例句：

His minor mistakes were condonable. = 他的小错误是可以原谅的。

Her impulsive behavior, while regrettable, was ultimately condonable given the circumstances. = 考虑到当时的境况，她冲动的行为虽然令人遗憾，但最终是可以原谅的。


近义词：forgivable, excusable, pardonable, justifiable


反义词：unforgivable, inexcusable, unpardonable


词性：形容词


词源:  Comes from the verb "condone," meaning to forgive or overlook something.  "Condone" itself has roots in the Old French "condoner" and ultimately the Latin "condonare," meaning "to grant, forgive."
