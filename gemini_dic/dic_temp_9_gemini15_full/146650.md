overharvest:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrvɪst/ | vt. |过度采伐；过度捕捞；过度收获 | To harvest (a crop, resource, etc.) to the point of depletion or damage.  It implies taking more than is sustainable.  |例句：Overharvesting of fish stocks has led to a decline in their populations. 过度捕捞导致鱼类数量下降。

例句：The overharvesting of timber in the rainforest is causing deforestation. 雨林中过度采伐木材导致森林砍伐。


近义词：deplete, exhaust, exploit, overexploit | 反义词：conserve, preserve, protect
