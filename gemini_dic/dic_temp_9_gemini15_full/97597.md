inhabiter:/ɪnˈhæbɪtər/| n. |居住者，居民|A person or animal that lives in or occupies a place.  An inhabiter is someone who dwells in a particular place, making it their home.|例句：The early inhabitants of this island were skilled fishermen. 这座岛屿的早期居民是熟练的渔民。

The city's inhabitants are a diverse mix of cultures. 这个城市的居民来自不同的文化背景，十分多元化。


近义词：resident, occupant, dweller, resident, denizen|反义词：outsider, stranger, alien|词性：名词
