plain's:/pleɪnz/| n. |平原；简朴；坦率| plains refers to a large area of flat land with few trees.  It can also refer to something simple and unadorned, or to speaking in a straightforward and honest manner. |例句：The vast plains stretched as far as the eye could see.  辽阔的平原一直延伸到视线所能及的地方。  She spoke in plain terms about the situation. 她坦率地谈论了这种情况。|近义词：fields, meadows, prairies, straightforward, simple|反义词：mountains, hills, complex, ornate, deceitful
