reassess:/ˌriːəˈses/| vt.|重新评估；重新考虑|To assess something again, often in the light of new information.|The company will reassess its strategy after the market downturn. 公司将在市场低迷后重新评估其战略。|evaluate, review, reconsider|accept, ignore|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reassesses the situation every morning. 他每天早上都会重新评估形势。
The government reassesses the economic policy annually. 政府每年都会重新评估经济政策。

一般过去时:
She reassessed her priorities after the accident. 事故后，她重新评估了自己的优先事项。
They reassessed the damage caused by the storm. 他们重新评估了风暴造成的损失。

现在完成时:
We have reassessed the project's budget several times. 我们已经多次重新评估了项目的预算。
The team has reassessed its goals for the year.  团队已经重新评估了今年的目标。

过去完成时:
After the new data arrived, they had reassessed their previous conclusions. 新数据到达后，他们重新评估了之前的结论。
She had reassessed her options before making a decision.  她在做决定之前重新评估了自己的选择。

将来时:
They will reassess the risks involved before proceeding. 他们在继续之前将重新评估所涉及的风险。
The committee will reassess the application next week. 委员会下周将重新评估申请。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reassess my approach to the problem. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新评估我解决这个问题的方法。
It is crucial that they reassess the situation immediately. 至关重要的是，他们必须立即重新评估形势。

被动语态:
The proposal was reassessed by the board of directors. 该提案被董事会重新评估了。
The project's timeline will be reassessed next month. 项目的时间表将在下个月重新评估。

疑问句:
Will the company reassess its marketing strategy? 公司会重新评估其营销策略吗？
Has the committee reassessed the budget yet? 委员会是否已经重新评估了预算？

否定句:
The manager did not reassess the employee's performance. 经理没有重新评估员工的表现。
They will not reassess the plan until next quarter. 他们直到下个季度才会重新评估计划。
