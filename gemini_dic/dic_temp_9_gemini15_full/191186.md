scarier:/ˈskɛəriər/| adj.|更可怕的，更吓人的|More frightening or terrifying than something else.  It is the comparative form of "scary."|例句：The haunted house was scarier than I expected.  闹鬼的房子比我预料的更可怕。

例句：That movie was scarier than the last one we saw. 那部电影比我们上次看的更恐怖。


近义词：more frightening, more terrifying, more alarming, more dreadful

反义词：less frightening, less terrifying, less alarming, less dreadful

词性：形容词
