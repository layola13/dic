loch:/lɒx/| n.|湖泊，海湾|A loch is a Scottish Gaelic word for a narrow inlet of the sea or a freshwater lake.  It's often used to describe the deep, narrow, often fjord-like lakes found in Scotland.  |Example: Loch Ness is famous for the legend of the monster.  尼斯湖以水怪传说而闻名。  The boat trip on Loch Lomond was beautiful.  在洛蒙德湖的船游很漂亮。|近义词：lake, bay, fjord, inlet|反义词：None specifically|名词
