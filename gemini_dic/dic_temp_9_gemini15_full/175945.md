rationalizing: /ˈræʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使合理化；为…找借口 | To attempt to justify (one's own or another's behaviour or decision) with logical, plausible, or credible reasons, even if these are false or only partially true.  | 例句：He rationalized his decision to quit his job by saying he needed more time with his family. 他以需要更多时间陪伴家人为由，为辞职的决定辩解。


例句：She tried to rationalize her spending by claiming it was necessary for her business. 她试图通过声称这笔支出对她生意有必要来为自己的消费找借口。


近义词：justify, explain away, excuse, vindicate | 反义词：condemn, criticize, denounce

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rationalizes his actions to avoid blame. = 他为自己的行为找借口以避免责备。
They rationalize their decisions based on market trends. = 他们根据市场趋势为自己的决定找理由。

一般过去时:
She rationalized her poor performance by blaming her colleagues. = 她通过责怪同事来为自己的糟糕表现找借口。
He rationalized his mistakes after the project failed.  = 项目失败后，他为自己的错误找借口。

现在完成时:
They have rationalized their investment strategies over the years. = 多年来，他们一直在为自己的投资策略找理由。
He has rationalized his behaviour to feel better about himself. = 他一直在为自己的行为找借口，好让自己感觉更好。

过去完成时:
She had rationalized her actions before confessing the truth. = 在坦白真相之前，她已经为自己的行为找好了借口。
He had rationalized his decision to leave, even though he regretted it later. = 他当时已经为离开的决定找好了借口，即使后来他后悔了。

将来时:
He will rationalize his choices to his boss. = 他将向老板解释他的选择。
They will rationalize their spending habits to save money. = 他们将为自己的消费习惯找理由以节省开支。

虚拟语气:
If I were to rationalize my mistakes, I would blame the circumstances. = 如果我要为我的错误找借口，我会怪罪环境。
I wish I hadn't rationalized my procrastination; I would have finished the project on time. = 我真希望我没有为我的拖延找借口；那样我就能按时完成项目了。

被动语态:
His actions were rationalized by his superiors. = 他的行为被他的上司合理化了。
The company's decision was rationalized in a press release. = 公司的决定在新闻稿中得到了解释。

疑问句:
How can you rationalize such unethical behavior? = 你怎么能为这种不道德的行为找借口？
Why did they rationalize their failure to meet deadlines? = 他们为什么为未能按时完成任务找借口？

否定句:
He didn't rationalize his actions; he took full responsibility. = 他没有为自己的行为找借口；他承担了全部责任。
They refused to rationalize their mistakes; they admitted their fault. = 他们拒绝为自己的错误找借口；他们承认了自己的错误。
