leucocytogenesis:/ˌluːkoʊsaɪtəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |白细胞生成，白血球生成 |The process of formation of white blood cells.  Leucocytogenesis is a complex process involving several stages and different types of cells. |The bone marrow is the primary site of leucocytogenesis in adults. 骨髓是成人白细胞生成的主要场所。  Disorders of leucocytogenesis can lead to various immune deficiencies. 白细胞生成的紊乱会导致各种免疫缺陷。|haemopoiesis, hematopoiesis|leukocytopenia (反义词并非完全对立，而是指白细胞减少症，并非指白细胞生成的缺乏)|名词
