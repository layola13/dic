pithlike:/ˈpɪθlaɪk/| adj.|髓质似的；干巴巴的；无趣的；|Resembling pith;  characterized by a lack of substance or force.  Often used to describe something that is dull, insipid, or uninspired.|The pithlike speech lacked any real substance.  这篇干巴巴的演讲缺乏任何实际内容。|The arguments presented were rather pithlike and unconvincing.  提出的论点相当空洞无力，令人难以信服。|insipid, vapid, jejune|substantial, forceful, compelling|形容词
