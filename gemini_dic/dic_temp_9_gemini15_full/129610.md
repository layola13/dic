non-conductor:/ˌnɑːn kənˈdʌktər/| n. adj.|非导体，绝缘体|A non-conductor is a material that does not readily transmit electricity, heat, or sound.|例句：Wood is a good non-conductor of electricity.  木材是不良导电体。

例句：Rubber is a non-conductor of heat. 橡胶是隔热体。


近义词：insulator, dielectric|反义词：conductor
