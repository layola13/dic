russophile:/ˈrʌsəfaɪl/| n.|亲俄人士，俄罗斯通|A person who is fond of or greatly admires Russia, its people, culture, or politics.|例句：Many historians are russophiles and have spent years studying Russian history. 许多历史学家是亲俄人士，并花费数年时间研究俄罗斯历史。
例句：His russophile tendencies were evident in his extensive collection of Russian art. 他对俄罗斯的喜爱在他的大量俄罗斯艺术收藏中显而易见。|近义词：philorussian|反义词：Russophobe|名词
