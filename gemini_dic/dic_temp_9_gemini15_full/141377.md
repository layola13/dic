The patient was prepped in the operatory before the surgery. 病人在手术前在手术室里做了准备。

词源:
The word "operatory" is derived from the Latin word "operari," meaning "to work" or "to operate."  The suffix "-tory" indicates a place or thing where an action is performed.  Therefore, "operatory" literally means "a place where operations are performed."
