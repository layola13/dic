preissued:/ˌpriːˈɪʃuːd/| adj.| 预先发行的；预先发布的 | Previously issued or published.  Something preissued has been released or distributed in advance. |例句：The preissued tickets sold out quickly. 预售票很快售罄了。
例句：The company released a preissued press release to manage expectations. 公司发布了一份预先发布的新闻稿来管理预期。|近义词：advance, prior, beforehand |反义词：post-issued, subsequent
