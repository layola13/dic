extorting: /ɪkˈstɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. |勒索；敲诈；强夺 | Obtaining something, especially money, through force or threats.  |例句：He was extorting money from his victims. 他正在敲诈他的受害者。|近义词：blackmailing,  coercing,  pressuring |反义词：giving, donating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are extorting money from the businessman. = 他们正在勒索那个商人钱财。
The government is extorting taxes from its citizens. = 政府正在向公民强行征税。


一般过去时:
The criminals extorted a large sum of money from the bank. = 罪犯从银行敲诈了一大笔钱。
He extorted a confession from the suspect. = 他从嫌疑人那里逼供。


现在完成时:
The mafia has extorted millions of dollars from businesses. = 黑手党已经从企业勒索了数百万美元。
She has extorted information from her colleagues. = 她已经从同事那里强取信息。


过去完成时:
The police discovered that he had extorted money from several people. = 警察发现他曾从几个人那里勒索过钱。
They realized that they had been extorted for years. = 他们意识到他们多年来一直被敲诈。


将来时:
The authorities believe he will continue to extort money. = 当局认为他会继续勒索钱财。
They fear that the gang will extort more businesses in the future. = 他们担心该团伙将来会敲诈更多的企业。


虚拟语气:
If he had not extorted money, he would not be in jail. = 如果他没有勒索钱财，他就不会在监狱里。
I wish they had not extorted that information from me. = 我希望他们没有从我这里强取那份信息。


被动语态:
Money was extorted from the wealthy businessman. = 钱财是从那个富有的商人那里勒索来的。
Information was extorted from the witness under duress. = 信息是在胁迫下从证人那里强取的。


疑问句:
Were they extorting money from the tourists? = 他们是否在勒索游客的钱财？
Is the company extorting its employees? = 公司是否正在敲诈它的员工？


否定句:
He did not extort money from anyone. = 他没有从任何人那里勒索钱财。
The company denies extorting its customers. = 公司否认敲诈它的顾客。
