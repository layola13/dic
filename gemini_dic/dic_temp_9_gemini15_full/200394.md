skylining: /ˈskaɪlaɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 天际线轮廓；天际线景色 | The act of creating or enhancing a skyline, often through the construction of tall buildings.  It can also refer to the visual impact of a skyline. |例句：The new skyscraper dramatically changed the city's skylining.  新的摩天大楼极大地改变了城市的天际线轮廓。  The photographer captured the stunning skylining of the Manhattan cityscape. 摄影师捕捉到了曼哈顿城市天际线的壮丽景色。|近义词：skyline, cityscape, panorama |反义词：flatland, plains
