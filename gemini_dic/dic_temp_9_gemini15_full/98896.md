intensest:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪst/ | adj. | 最强烈的；最紧张的 |  The most extreme or intense form of something.  It is the superlative form of "intense". |例句：The intensest heat of the summer was unbearable. 夏天最热的时候让人难以忍受。

例句：He felt the intensest pressure to succeed. 他感到巨大的成功压力。


近义词：most intense, extreme, utmost, maximal | 反义词：mildest, weakest, least intense | 词性：形容词
