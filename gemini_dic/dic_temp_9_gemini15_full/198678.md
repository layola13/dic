shying:/ʃaɪɪŋ/| v.|害羞，躲避；|to move suddenly away from something that frightens or surprises you; to avoid something.|例句：The horse shied at the loud noise.  马听到巨响受惊了。|例句：He shied away from responsibility. 他逃避责任。|近义词： flinch, recoil, shrink|反义词： confront, face, embrace|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shies away from conflict. = 他回避冲突。
The horse shies at sudden movements. = 马对突然的移动很敏感。


一般过去时:
She shied away from the camera. = 她躲避了摄像机。
The dog shied at the fireworks. = 狗被烟花吓到了。


现在完成时:
He has shied away from public speaking his whole life. = 他一生都回避公开演讲。
The cat has shied away from me ever since I accidentally scared it. = 自从我不小心吓到它之后，猫就一直躲着我。


过去完成时:
She had shied away from commitment in previous relationships. = 在之前的感情中，她一直回避承诺。
He had shied away from the truth for years. = 他多年来一直回避真相。


将来时:
I think he will shy away from the challenge. = 我认为他会回避挑战。
She will shy away from any confrontation. = 她会回避任何冲突。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not shy away from expressing your opinion. = 如果我是你，我不会回避表达你的意见。
I wish I hadn't shied away from that opportunity. = 我真希望我没有回避那个机会。


被动语态:
The issue was shied away from by the politician. = 政治家回避了这个问题。（略显不自然，很少用被动语态）
The truth was shied away from for too long. = 真相被回避了太久。（略显不自然，很少用被动语态）


疑问句:
Does he shy away from difficult conversations? = 他会回避艰难的谈话吗？
Why does the animal shy away from me? = 为什么动物躲着我？


否定句:
He doesn't shy away from hard work. = 他不回避努力工作。
She didn't shy away from expressing her concerns. = 她没有回避表达她的担忧。


PHRASE->
shy away from = 回避
shy of = 略少于
shy glance = 羞怯的一瞥
shy smile = 羞涩的微笑
to be shy = 害羞
