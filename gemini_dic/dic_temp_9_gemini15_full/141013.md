onethird:/wʌnˈθɜːrd/| n. adj.|三分之一；三之一|One-third refers to one part out of three equal parts of a whole.  It can function as both a noun and an adjective. As a noun, it represents the quantity; as an adjective, it modifies a noun.|例句：One-third of the students passed the exam. = 三分之一的学生通过了考试。

One-third of the cake is left. = 还剩下三分之一的蛋糕。|近义词：a third, 33.33%|反义词：two-thirds, three-quarters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
One-third of the apples are red. = 三分之一的苹果是红色的。
One-third of the population lives in poverty. = 三分之一的人口生活在贫困中。


一般过去时:
One-third of the land was flooded. = 三分之一的土地被淹没了。
One-third of the participants left early. = 三分之一的参与者提前离开了。


现在完成时:
One-third of the project has been completed. = 三分之一的项目已经完成了。
One-third of the books have been sold. = 三分之一的书已经卖掉了。


过去完成时:
One-third of the work had been finished before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，三分之一的工作已经完成了。
One-third of the budget had already been spent. = 预算的三分之一已经花掉了。


将来时:
One-third of the city will be renovated next year. = 明年三分之一的城市将被翻新。
One-third of the students will graduate next month. = 下个月三分之一的学生将毕业。


虚拟语气:
If one-third of the votes were cast for him, he would win. = 如果三分之一的选票投给他，他就会赢。
I wish one-third of my problems were solved. = 我希望我的三分之一的问题能够得到解决。


被动语态:
One-third of the profits are shared among the partners. = 三分之一的利润在合伙人之间共享。
One-third of the cake was eaten by the children. = 三分之一的蛋糕被孩子们吃掉了。


疑问句:
Is one-third of the pizza enough for you? = 三分之一的比萨饼够你吃吗？
What fraction is one-third equivalent to? = 三分之一相当于什么分数？


否定句:
One-third of the class did not understand the lesson. = 三分之一的班级没有理解这节课。
More than one-third of the population did not vote. = 超过三分之一的人口没有投票。


PHRASE->
one-third of the total = 总数的三分之一
one-third of the time = 三分之一的时间
one-third less than = 比…少三分之一
one-third more than = 比…多三分之一
one-third increase = 增加三分之一


词源:
The word "one-third" is a compound word.  "One" is from Old English "ān," meaning "one."  "Third" comes from Old English "þridda,"  the ordinal number indicating the third position in a sequence.  The use of the hyphen indicates that it functions as a single unit, representing a fraction.
