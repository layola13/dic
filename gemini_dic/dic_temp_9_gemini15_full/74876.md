fresh:/freʃ/| adj.| 新鲜的；清新的；无经验的 |  Newly made, gathered, or produced; not stale or spoiled.  Characterized by or suggestive of youth, vigor, or freshness. Lacking experience. | 例句：The bread is fresh from the oven.  这面包刚出炉。

The air smells fresh after the rain. 雨后空气清新。


近义词：new, recent, novel, unblemished | 反义词：stale, old, spoiled, experienced
