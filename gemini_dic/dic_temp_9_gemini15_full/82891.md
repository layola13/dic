gulps:/ɡʌlp/| vt. & n.|吞咽；大口喝；|to swallow something quickly and with difficulty, often because you are nervous or thirsty.|例句：He gulped down the water. 他咕咚咕咚地喝着水。
He gulped nervously before speaking. 他在说话前紧张地吞咽了一下。|近义词：swallow, gulp down|反义词：sip, savor
