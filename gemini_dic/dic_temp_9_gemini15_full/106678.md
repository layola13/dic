kvetchers:/ˈkvɛtʃərz/| n. |抱怨者，牢骚满腹的人 |A kvetch is someone who complains a lot.  Kvetchers are often characterized by their negativity and tendency to dwell on problems.  The plural form, "kvetchers," refers to multiple people who engage in this behavior.|例句：The office is full of kvetchers today; everyone seems to have a complaint. 办公室今天充满了抱怨的人；每个人似乎都有怨言。

The meeting was dominated by kvetchers who seemed intent on finding fault with every proposal. 会议上充斥着抱怨者，他们似乎一心要挑剔每一个提案。|近义词：complainer, grumbler, whiner, moaners|反义词：optimist, Pollyanna


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kvetchs about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
They kvetch constantly about their jobs. = 他们不断抱怨他们的工作。


一般过去时:
She kvetched about the long lines at the store. = 她抱怨商店的长队。
He kvetched to his friend about his boss. = 他向他的朋友抱怨他的老板。


现在完成时:
They have kvetched about this problem for months. = 他们已经抱怨这个问题好几个月了。
She has kvetched incessantly since the incident. = 自从那件事发生以来，她就一直在不停地抱怨。


过去完成时:
He had kvetched for hours before anyone listened. = 他抱怨了好几个小时才有人听。
They had kvetched repeatedly, but nothing changed. = 他们反复抱怨，但什么也没改变。


将来时:
I know he will kvetch about the new rules. = 我知道他会抱怨新规定的。
She will kvetch endlessly if we don't go. = 如果我们不去，她会没完没了地抱怨。


虚拟语气:
If he kvetched less, people might listen more. = 如果他少抱怨一些，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't kvetch so much. = 我希望他不那么抱怨。


被动语态:
His complaints are often kvetched about by others.  = 他的抱怨经常被别人抱怨。 (This is awkward, the passive voice is not naturally used with this verb)


疑问句:
Does he kvetch a lot? = 他抱怨很多吗？
Why are they always kvetching? = 他们为什么总是抱怨？


否定句:
He doesn't kvetch as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样抱怨那么多了。
They didn't kvetch at all during the trip. = 他们在旅行期间一点也没抱怨。



PHRASE->
(The verb "kvetch" doesn't readily form common idiomatic phrases in the same way many other verbs do. Its usage is primarily as a stand-alone verb describing complaining.)  Therefore, instead of phrases with "kvetch," here are phrases related to the act of complaining:

constantly kvetching:  不停地抱怨
to kvetch about something:  抱怨某事
a habitual kvetch: 一个习惯性抱怨的人
kvetching incessantly: 没完没了地抱怨
a bundle of kvetchers:一群抱怨的人
