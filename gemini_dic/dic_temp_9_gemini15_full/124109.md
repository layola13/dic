mortalities:/mɔːˈtælɪtiz/| n. |死亡率；死亡人数|The number of deaths in a particular place or period, or from a particular cause.  It often refers to a large-scale event or population. |例句：The mortalities from the flu epidemic were alarmingly high. 流感疫情的死亡人数高得惊人。|例句：Infant mortalities have decreased significantly in developed countries. 发达国家的婴儿死亡率已显著下降。|近义词：death rate, fatalities,casualties|反义词：survival rate, births
