coexisting: /ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstɪŋ/ | adj. | 并存的；共存的 | Existing at the same time or in the same place.  |例句：Many different cultures are coexisting peacefully in this city.  这个城市里许多不同的文化和平共处。|近义词：concurrent, simultaneous, compatible |反义词：conflicting, incompatible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two species are coexisting in the same habitat. = 这两种物种在同一栖息地共存。
These ideas coexist within the same philosophical framework. = 这些思想在同一哲学框架内共存。


一般过去时:
The two companies coexisted peacefully for many years before merging. = 两家公司在合并之前和平共处多年。
Different belief systems coexisted in the ancient world. = 在古代世界，不同的信仰体系并存。


现在完成时:
These two cultures have coexisted for centuries. = 这两种文化已经共存了几个世纪。
The old and new traditions have coexisted peacefully in this community. = 在这个社区里，古老的和新的传统和平共处。


过去完成时:
Before the war, different political factions had coexisted in relative harmony. = 在战争爆发之前，不同的政治派别相对和谐地共存。
The two kingdoms had coexisted for decades before the conflict began. = 在冲突开始之前，这两个王国已经共存了几十年。


将来时:
The two groups will coexist if they can find common ground. = 如果这两个群体能找到共同点，他们将能够共存。
We hope that these two competing systems will coexist in the future. = 我们希望这两个竞争系统将来能够共存。


虚拟语气:
If these two species could coexist, the ecosystem would be more stable. = 如果这两种物种能够共存，生态系统将更加稳定。
If only these conflicting ideologies could coexist peacefully! = 但愿这些冲突的意识形态能够和平共处！


被动语态:
The different opinions were allowed to coexist in the debate. = 在辩论中，不同的观点被允许共存。
The various elements in the design were carefully planned to coexist harmoniously. = 设计中的各种元素经过精心规划，以和谐共存。


疑问句:
Can these two political systems coexist peacefully? = 这两种政治制度能够和平共处吗？
Do you think these conflicting ideas can coexist in the same theory? = 你认为这些相互冲突的思想能够在同一理论中共存吗？


否定句:
The two ideas could not coexist within the same framework. = 这两种思想无法在同一个框架内共存。
The two species did not coexist, leading to competition for resources. = 这两种物种没有共存，导致了资源竞争。
