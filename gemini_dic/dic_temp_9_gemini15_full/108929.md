leechlike:/ˈliːtʃlaɪk/| adj.|像水蛭的；贪婪的；寄生性的|Resembling a leech; clinging or sucking like a leech; parasitic; excessively greedy or exploitative.|例句：His leechlike grip on power prevented any reform.  他的权力控制得像水蛭一样，阻止了任何改革。

例句：The leechlike company drained the resources of the small town. 这家像水蛭一样的公司榨干了小镇的资源。

近义词：parasitic, clinging, clinging, greedy, rapacious, exploitative

反义词：generous, altruistic, helpful
