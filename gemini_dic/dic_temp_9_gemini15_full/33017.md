chorology:/kəˈrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 空间分布学；地理分布学 | The study of the spatial distribution of organisms or phenomena.  It involves analyzing patterns of distribution across geographical areas and examining the factors that influence these patterns.  Chorology is closely related to biogeography and geography. | Example:  The chorology of the rare orchid species is poorly understood due to its limited distribution.  稀有兰花物种的空间分布学由于其分布有限而鲜为人知。|近义词：biogeography, geographical distribution | 反义词：N/A
