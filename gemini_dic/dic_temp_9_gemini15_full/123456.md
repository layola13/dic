monorails:/ˈmɒnəˌreɪlz/ /ˈmɑːnəˌreɪlz/| n. |单轨铁路，单轨电车|A monorail is a railway system that uses a single rail for its vehicles to run on.  It's often elevated and used in urban areas for transportation.|例句：The city is planning to build a new monorail system to alleviate traffic congestion.  这座城市计划建造一个新的单轨铁路系统以缓解交通拥堵。

The monorail at the theme park was a popular attraction. 主题公园的单轨电车是一个很受欢迎的景点。|近义词：railway, transit system |反义词：multi-rail system
