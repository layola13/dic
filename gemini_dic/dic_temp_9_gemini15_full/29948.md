caved-in: /keɪvd ˈɪn/ | adj. | 坍塌的；凹陷的 | collapsed inward; sunken | The caved-in ceiling threatened to crush the trapped miners.  坍塌的天花板威胁着要压垮被困的矿工。 The old building had a caved-in roof.  那座旧建筑的屋顶塌陷了。 | sunken, collapsed, depressed | intact, undamaged, upright | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ceiling is caved in. = 天花板塌陷了。
The road is caved in several places. = 道路在几处塌陷了。

一般过去时:
The mine caved in during the storm. = 矿井在暴风雨中坍塌了。
The tunnel caved in after the explosion. = 爆炸后隧道坍塌了。


现在完成时:
The roof has caved in after years of neglect. = 屋顶由于多年的忽视而坍塌了。
Several sections of the highway have caved in. = 高速公路的几个路段已经塌陷了。


过去完成时:
The support beams had caved in before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，支撑梁已经坍塌了。
The ground had caved in, creating a large sinkhole. = 地面已经塌陷，形成一个巨大的陷坑。


将来时:
The old bridge will likely cave in under heavy traffic. = 在繁重的交通压力下，这座旧桥很可能会坍塌。
If the pressure increases, the tunnel will cave in. = 如果压力增加，隧道将会坍塌。


虚拟语气:
If the supports had been stronger, the roof wouldn't have caved in. = 如果支撑更坚固，屋顶就不会坍塌了。
I wish the road hadn't caved in; it would have saved us a lot of trouble. = 我真希望道路没有塌陷；这将节省我们很多麻烦。


被动语态:
The tunnel was caved in by the earthquake. = 隧道在地震中坍塌了。
The roof was caved in by the weight of the snow. = 屋顶被雪的重量压塌了。


疑问句:
Has the ceiling caved in? = 天花板塌了吗？
Did the wall cave in during the storm? = 暴风雨期间墙塌了吗？


否定句:
The building hasn't caved in yet. = 建筑物还没有坍塌。
The ground didn't cave in despite the heavy rain. = 尽管雨很大，地面也没有塌陷。


PHRASE->

cave in completely:  完全坍塌
partially caved in: 部分坍塌
severely caved in:  严重坍塌
