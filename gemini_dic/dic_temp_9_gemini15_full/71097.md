fish-up:/ˈfɪʃʌp/| v.| 捞鱼；捕捞；(非正式) 找出；挖掘出 | To catch fish, especially by dragging a net through the water; to find or discover something, usually after some effort. |例句：The fishermen fish-up many fishes every day. 渔民们每天捕捞很多鱼。They fish-up some old photos in the attic.他们在阁楼里翻出一些旧照片。|近义词：fish for, dredge up, unearth |反义词：sink, discard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fish up salmon in this river. = 他们在这条河里捕捞鲑鱼。
We fish up old memories during family reunions. = 我们在家庭聚会上挖掘出旧日的回忆。


一般过去时:
