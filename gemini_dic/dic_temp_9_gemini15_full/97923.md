innocents:/ˈɪnəsənts/| n.|无辜的人；天真；清白|The state or quality of being innocent; freedom from guilt or wrongdoing; lack of experience or knowledge of the world;  a person who is innocent.|例句：The innocents were spared from the massacre. 无辜的人们免遭了屠杀。|例句：The child's innocence was captivating. 孩子的天真烂漫令人着迷。|近义词：innocence, guiltlessness, naivete, purity|反义词：guilt, wickedness, culpability, experience
