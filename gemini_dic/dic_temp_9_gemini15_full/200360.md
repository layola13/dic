skydive:/ˈskaɪdaɪv/| v.|跳伞|To jump from an aircraft with a parachute.  This activity involves freefall before the parachute opens.|例句：He decided to skydive for his 50th birthday. 他决定在他50岁生日那天跳伞。

例句：She skydived over the beautiful Swiss Alps. 她跳伞飞越美丽的瑞士阿尔卑斯山脉。


近义词：parachute, plummet

反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider "stay on the ground" as a contrasting activity.

词源:
The word "skydive" is a compound word, combining "sky" (referring to the heavens or atmosphere) and "dive" (meaning to plunge or descend rapidly).  It emerged in the early 20th century with the development of parachuting as a sport and activity.
