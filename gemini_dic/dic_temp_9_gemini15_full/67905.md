face-out: /ˈfeɪs aʊt/ | adj. | 面朝外的；面向外部的 |  Describes something positioned or arranged so that its front or most prominent side is facing outward or away from a central point.  | 例句：The shelves are face-out, allowing customers to easily browse the products. 货架是面朝外的，让顾客可以轻松浏览产品。

The books were arranged face-out on the display table. 书本面朝外摆放在展示台上。


近义词： outward-facing, exposed, visible | 反义词：face-in, inward-facing, concealed


词性: 形容词
