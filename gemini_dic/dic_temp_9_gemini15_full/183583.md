requirements: /rɪˈkwaɪərmənts/ | n. | 要求；需求；必需品 | The requirements for the job are listed below. 这份工作的要求列在下面。 | 近义词：needs, demands, specifications | 反义词：unnecessary things


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project requires careful planning. = 这个项目需要仔细的规划。
What are the requirements for this course? = 这门课程的要求是什么？


一般过去时:
The client required a quick turnaround. = 客户要求快速完成。
The old system had different requirements. = 旧系统有不同的要求。


现在完成时:
They have met all the requirements. = 他们已经满足了所有要求。
Have you fulfilled all the requirements for graduation? = 你已经满足所有毕业要求了吗？


过去完成时:
We had already met the requirements before the deadline. = 我们在截止日期之前就已经满足了要求。
The company had not met the safety requirements. = 公司没有达到安全要求。


将来时:
The new software will require a powerful computer. = 新软件将需要一台强大的电脑。
What requirements will the project have in the future? = 这个项目将来会有什么要求？


虚拟语气:
If the requirements were less stringent, we could finish sooner. = 如果要求不那么严格，我们就能更快完成。
It is essential that all requirements be met. = 必须满足所有要求。


被动语态:
The requirements were clearly stated in the contract. = 合同中清楚地说明了要求。
All requirements are being reviewed by the committee. = 所有要求都正在由委员会审查。


疑问句:
What are the main requirements? = 主要要求是什么？
What requirements do you need to meet? = 你需要满足什么要求？


否定句:
The product doesn't meet the safety requirements. = 该产品不符合安全要求。
His application didn't fulfill all the requirements. = 他的申请没有满足所有要求。
