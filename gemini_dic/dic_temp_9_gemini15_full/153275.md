penalizes:/ˈpiːnəlaɪzɪz/| vt.|惩罚，处罚|To impose a penalty on; punish.|The referee penalized the player for a foul.  裁判因犯规而惩罚了该球员。

The company penalizes employees for lateness. 公司会惩罚迟到的员工。|punish, sanction, chastise|reward, pardon|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government penalizes tax evasion. = 政府惩罚逃税行为。
The school penalizes cheating on exams. = 学校惩罚考试作弊行为。

一般过去时:
The judge penalized the defendant for contempt of court. = 法官因藐视法庭而惩罚了被告。
The teacher penalized the student for disruptive behavior. = 老师因扰乱课堂秩序而惩罚了学生。

现在完成时:
The team has been penalized repeatedly for rough play. = 这支球队因粗暴的比赛而多次受到处罚。
He has penalized himself for his mistakes. = 他因自己的错误而惩罚自己。

过去完成时:
The athlete had been penalized before, so the new penalty was harsher. = 该运动员之前曾受过处罚，所以新的处罚更严厉。
They had penalized the company for violating environmental regulations. = 他们因违反环境法规而对该公司进行了处罚。

将来时:
The league will penalize any team found guilty of doping. = 联盟将惩罚任何被发现使用兴奋剂的球队。
The court will penalize him for perjury. = 法院将因伪证罪而惩罚他。

虚拟语气:
If they penalized him more severely, perhaps he wouldn't repeat the offense. = 如果他们对他处罚更严厉一些，也许他就不会重犯了。
I wish the government penalized pollution more effectively. = 我希望政府更有效地惩罚污染行为。

被动语态:
The player was penalized for unsportsmanlike conduct. = 该球员因不公平竞争行为而受到处罚。
The company was penalized heavily for violating safety regulations. = 该公司因违反安全条例而受到严厉处罚。

疑问句:
Will the team be penalized for this incident? = 这支球队会因为这件事受罚吗？
How will the court penalize the offender? = 法院将如何惩罚罪犯？

否定句:
The judge did not penalize the witness for her honest testimony. = 法官没有因为证人的老实证词而惩罚她。
The company does not penalize employees for minor infractions. = 公司不会因为轻微的违规行为而惩罚员工。
