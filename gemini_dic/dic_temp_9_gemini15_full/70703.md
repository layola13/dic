financer:/faɪˈnænsər/| n. | 融资者；出资人；财政家 | A person or organization that provides funds for a project or business.  |The project was finally completed thanks to the generous financer.  这个项目最终得以完成，多亏了一位慷慨的融资者。| investor, funder, backer | debtor, borrower | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company finances its operations through various means. = 公司通过各种方式为其运营提供资金。
He finances his education with scholarships and part-time jobs. = 他靠奖学金和兼职工作支付学费。


一般过去时:
They financed the construction of a new hospital. = 他们为新建医院提供了资金。
She financed her trip to Europe with savings from her previous job. = 她用之前工作攒的钱资助了她的欧洲之旅。


现在完成时:
The government has financed several major infrastructure projects. = 政府已为几项重大基础设施项目提供了资金。
We have financed the acquisition of a new building. = 我们已经为购买新大楼提供了资金。


过去完成时:
They had financed the research before the grant application was approved. = 他们已在拨款申请获批之前为该研究提供了资金。
The company had financed its expansion before the market downturn. = 公司在市场低迷之前为其扩张提供了资金。


将来时:
The bank will finance the purchase of the new equipment. = 银行将为购买新设备提供资金。
We will finance our retirement with investments and savings. = 我们将通过投资和储蓄来支付退休金。


虚拟语气:
If they had financed the project earlier, it would have been completed on time. = 如果他们早些为项目提供资金，它本来就能按时完成。
I wish we had financed that venture; it would have been profitable. = 我真希望我们当时投资了那个项目；它本来会很赚钱的。


被动语态:
The construction of the bridge was financed by the city government. = 这座桥的建设由市政府提供资金。
The movie was financed by several independent producers. = 这部电影由几家独立制片公司投资拍摄。


疑问句:
Who financed the expedition to the Antarctic? = 谁为南极探险提供了资金？
How will you finance your studies abroad? = 你将如何资助你在国外的学习？


否定句:
They did not finance the proposed expansion project. = 他们没有为拟议的扩张项目提供资金。
The company was unable to finance its operations without additional capital. = 没有额外资金，公司无法维持运营。
