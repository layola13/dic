naughtly:/ˈnɔːtɪli/| adj. |顽皮的，淘气的|Mischievous; disobedient; playful in a naughty way.|例句：The children were naughtly and wouldn't listen to their parents.  孩子们很顽皮，不肯听父母的话。|例句：He got into trouble for his naughtly behavior. 他因为淘气而惹上了麻烦。|近义词：mischievous, naughty, impish, unruly|反义词：obedient, well-behaved, docile
