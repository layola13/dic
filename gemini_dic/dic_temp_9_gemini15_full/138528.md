nowhereness:/ˈnoʊˌhwernəs/ | n. |无处可去；虚无；不存在感 | The feeling or state of being nowhere, lacking direction or purpose; a sense of meaninglessness or insignificance.  It often implies a feeling of isolation, displacement, or lack of belonging. | Example:  The vastness of the desert emphasized the profound nowhereness of their situation.  沙漠的广袤突出了他们处境中深刻的无处可去感。

The crushing nowhereness of his life after losing his job weighed heavily on him.  失去工作后，他生活中那种压抑的虚无感让他不堪重负。


近义词：meaninglessness, pointlessness, nothingness, void, emptiness

反义词：somewhere, purpose, meaning, significance, belonging
