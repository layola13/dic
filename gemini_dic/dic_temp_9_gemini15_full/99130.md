intercepted:/ˌɪntərˈsɛptɪd/ | v. |截取；拦截；截获 | To interrupt or stop something from continuing on its course.  |例句：The police intercepted a shipment of illegal drugs.  警察截获了一批非法毒品。

例句：The enemy's message was intercepted. 敌人的信息被截获了。


近义词：blocked, stopped, seized, captured | 反义词：allowed, permitted, delivered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government intercepts suspicious emails. = 政府拦截可疑邮件。
She intercepts her friend's calls. = 她拦截朋友的电话。


一般过去时:
The police intercepted the stolen car. = 警察拦截了被盗车辆。
He intercepted a pass during the football game. = 他在足球比赛中拦截了一次传球。


现在完成时:
They have intercepted several coded messages. = 他们截获了几条密码信息。
The authorities have intercepted the smuggled goods. = 当局已截获走私货物。


过去完成时:
The spy had intercepted the secret documents before the enemy could. = 间谍在敌人之前截获了秘密文件。
She had intercepted the letter before it reached her husband. = 在信件到达她丈夫之前，她已经截获了它。


将来时:
We will intercept the package before it reaches its destination. = 我们将在包裹到达目的地之前将其拦截。
They will intercept the missile before it hits the target. = 他们将在导弹击中目标之前拦截它。


虚拟语气:
If we had intercepted the information earlier, we could have prevented the attack. = 如果我们早点截获信息，就能阻止袭击。
I wish I had intercepted that email.  = 我真希望我当时截获了那封邮件。


被动语态:
The message was intercepted by the enemy. = 信息被敌人截获了。
The package was intercepted at the border. = 包裹在边境被截获了。


疑问句:
Was the letter intercepted? = 信件被拦截了吗？
Were the communications intercepted? = 通信被拦截了吗？


否定句:
The signal was not intercepted. = 信号没有被拦截。
They did not intercept the enemy's plan. = 他们没有拦截敌人的计划。


PHRASE->
intercept a call = 接听（电话）
intercept a pass = 拦截传球
intercept a message = 截取信息
intercept a missile = 拦截导弹
