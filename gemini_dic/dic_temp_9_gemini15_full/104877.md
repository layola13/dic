keep:/kiːp/| vt. vi. n.|保持，保留，遵守|to continue to have or possess something; to prevent something from being lost, damaged, or destroyed; to prevent oneself from doing something.|例句：I keep my passport in a safe place. 我把护照放在安全的地方。  I tried to keep calm during the emergency.我在紧急情况下试图保持冷静。|近义词：retain, maintain, preserve|反义词：lose, abandon, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I keep my room clean. = 我保持我的房间干净。
She keeps a diary. = 她坚持写日记。

一般过去时:
He kept his promise. = 他遵守了他的诺言。
They kept the secret. = 他们保守了秘密。

现在完成时:
I have kept my word. = 我信守了我的诺言。
She has kept the same hairstyle for years. = 她多年来一直保持着同样的发型。

过去完成时:
He had kept the money for a long time. = 他已经保存那笔钱很久了。
They had kept quiet about the matter. = 他们对这件事一直保持沉默。


将来时:
I will keep you informed. = 我会让你随时了解情况。
She will keep trying. = 她会继续尝试。

虚拟语气:
If I kept practicing, I would improve. = 如果我一直练习，我会进步。
I wish I had kept my appointment. = 我真希望我没有错过我的约会。

被动语态:
The secret was kept well. = 秘密被保守得很好。
The documents are kept in the vault. = 文件保存在保险库中。

疑问句:
Do you keep a pet? = 你养宠物吗？
Will you keep your appointment? = 你会赴约吗？

否定句:
I don't keep up with the latest trends. = 我跟不上最新的潮流。
He didn't keep his appointment. = 他没有赴约。
