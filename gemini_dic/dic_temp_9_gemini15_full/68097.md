fadlike:/ˈfædlaɪk/| adj.|像时尚的；短暂流行的；昙花一现的|resembling a fad; existing or popular only for a short time.  The fadlike nature of the product led to its quick decline in sales.|近义词：trendy, ephemeral, fleeting, momentary|反义词：lasting, enduring, permanent


例句：

The fadlike popularity of fidget spinners quickly faded. =  这种像时尚一样流行的指尖陀螺迅速过气了。

His fadlike obsession with collecting stamps lasted only a few months. = 他这种短暂的集邮爱好只持续了几个月。
