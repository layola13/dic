remise:/rɪˈmiːz/| n. v.|放弃；赦免；补偿；（房屋等的）附属建筑|To give up or surrender (something); to forgive or pardon; to compensate for; a shed or outbuilding attached to a main building.  |The prisoner was granted a remise of his sentence. 犯人被赦免了刑期。|The landlord offered a remise for the damage to the property. 房东为房产损坏提供了赔偿。|近义词：remission, pardon, compensation, relinquishment|反义词：retention, punishment, withholding


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He remits his rent every month. = 他每月汇寄房租。
The company remits payments to its suppliers. = 公司向供应商汇款。

一般过去时:
She remitted the payment after a delay. = 她延后支付了款项。
The court remitted the case to a lower court. = 法院将此案发回下级法院。


现在完成时:
They have remitted all the outstanding debts. = 他们已经偿还了所有未清债务。
He has remitted the money via bank transfer. = 他已通过银行转账汇款。


过去完成时:
The judge had remitted the sentence before the appeal. = 法官在上诉前已减轻了判决。
She had remitted the funds before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已汇款。


将来时:
We will remit the balance of the invoice next week. = 我们下周将汇付发票余额。
They will remit the payment upon receipt of the goods. = 收到货物后，他们将付款。


虚拟语气:
If the judge remitted the sentence, he would be free. = 如果法官减轻判决，他将获得自由。
I wish he would remit the debt he owes me. = 我希望他能偿还欠我的债务。


被动语态:
The sentence was remitted by the judge. = 判决被法官减轻了。
The payment will be remitted to your account shortly. = 款项将很快汇入您的账户。


疑问句:
Will the company remit the payment on time? = 公司会按时付款吗？
Did they remit the funds as promised? = 他们是否如约汇款？


否定句:
He did not remit the payment on time. = 他没有按时付款。
The judge refused to remit the sentence. = 法官拒绝减轻判决。


PHRASE->

remise of sentence: 赦免刑罚
remise of debt: 债务豁免
in remise: 在豁免中
to remit a payment: 汇款
to remit a fine: 缴纳罚款
