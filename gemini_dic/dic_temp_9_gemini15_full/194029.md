self-focussing: /ˈsɛlf ˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ | adj. |  自我关注的，以自我为中心的 |  Concentrating primarily on oneself; self-absorbed.  It emphasizes a preoccupation with one's own thoughts, feelings, and experiences, often to the exclusion of others. |  例句：His self-focussing behaviour made it difficult to have a meaningful conversation.  他的自我中心行为使得很难进行有意义的谈话。


例句：Her self-focussing nature prevented her from seeing the needs of her team. 她的自我中心的天性让她无法看到团队的需求。


近义词：self-centered, egocentric, narcissistic, self-absorbed


反义词：other-centered, altruistic, selfless, empathetic
