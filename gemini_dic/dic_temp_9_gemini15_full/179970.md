/riːˈfaɪts/| n. |再战，重战 |A refight is a renewed battle or conflict, often after a previous defeat or stalemate.  It implies a continuation or resumption of fighting. |例句：The armies engaged in a bloody refight after a brief truce.  军队在短暂休战后再次展开血腥战斗。

The refight for control of the city lasted for weeks.  争夺城市控制权的再次战斗持续了数周。|近义词：rematch, renewed battle, second battle |反义词：peace, truce, armistice|名词
