postcontemporary: /ˌpoʊstˌkənˈtɛmpəreri/ | adj. | 后当代的；后现代以后的 |  Describing the period, trends, or phenomena that follow postmodernism, often characterized by a critical engagement with postmodernity's legacy and a move towards new forms of expression and understanding.  It suggests a reflection upon and reaction against postmodern thought and practice. | Example:  The artist's work is considered postcontemporary, exhibiting a synthesis of digital media and traditional techniques while questioning the very nature of originality.  =这位艺术家的作品被认为是后当代的，它融合了数字媒体和传统技法，同时质疑原创性的本质。| 近义词:  late postmodern, transmodern | 反义词:  premodern, modern, postmodern
