roque:/rok/| n.|洛可可式花园；槌球|A game played outdoors, similar to croquet, but using mallets and balls on a much larger court.  The game is played on a large lawn with hoops, and the objective is to hit the balls through the hoops in a specific order.|例句：The family enjoyed a leisurely game of roque on the expansive lawn.  这家在广阔的草坪上享受了一场悠闲的槌球游戏。|近义词：croquet|反义词：无|名词
