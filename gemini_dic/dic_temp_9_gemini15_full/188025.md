roughens:/ˈrʌfənz/| vt.|使粗糙；使粗砺|To make something rough or uneven in texture or surface.  The constant friction roughens the skin.|例句：The wind roughened the sea. 风使大海变得波涛汹涌。
The sandpaper roughened the wood. 砂纸磨粗了木头。|近义词：coarsens, makes uneven, abrades|反义词：smooths, polishes
