carport:/ˈkɑːrpɔːrt/| n. |车棚，汽车棚 |A carport is an open-sided structure for sheltering a car or other vehicles.  It typically has a roof but no walls. |例句：We built a carport to protect our car from the elements. 我们建了一个车棚来保护我们的车免受风雨侵蚀。

例句：The carport is attached to the house, making it convenient to enter and exit the vehicle. 车棚与房子相连，进出车辆很方便。


近义词：garage (if enclosed), shelter, car shelter |反义词：None directly applicable.  A carport is the opposite of being completely exposed to the elements, but there isn't a single-word antonym.

词性:名词
