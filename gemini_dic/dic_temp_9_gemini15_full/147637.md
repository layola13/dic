overpolice:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊlis/| v.|过度警务，警察过度执法|To police excessively or oppressively; to subject to excessive police surveillance or intervention.|例句：The community felt overpoliced and unfairly targeted by law enforcement. 社区感到过度执法，执法部门不公平地针对他们。|例句：The increase in police presence has led to accusations of overpolicing in the neighborhood. 警力增加导致该社区被指控过度执法。|近义词：oppress, harass, surveil excessively|反义词：underpolice, neglect, ignore|动词
