noncirculating: /ˌnɒnˈsɜːkjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不流通的，非流通的 | Not circulating; not in general use or circulation.  This adjective describes something that is not in common use or distribution, often referring to money, resources, or information.  |


例句：

The museum holds a collection of noncirculating commemorative coins. = 博物馆收藏了一批不流通的纪念币。

The library has a section for noncirculating archival materials. = 图书馆设有非流通档案资料专区。


近义词：restricted, unavailable, inaccessible, uncirculated


反义词：circulating, common, widespread, accessible


词性：形容词
