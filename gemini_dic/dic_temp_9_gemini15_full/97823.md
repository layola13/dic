inking:/ˈɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. |墨水；墨迹；着墨|The act of applying ink; the process of printing or writing with ink; a stain or mark made with ink.|例句：The inking of the contract finalized the deal.  合同的签署敲定了这笔交易。|例句：There was inking on his fingers from the pen.  他的手指上有笔墨。|近义词：staining, marking, writing|反义词：erasing, removing
