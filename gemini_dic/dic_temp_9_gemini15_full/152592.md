peacefulness:/ˈpiːsfʊlnəs/ | n. |平静；安宁；宁静 |The state or quality of being peaceful; tranquility; calmness.  It refers to a state of freedom from disturbance; quiet and calm. |例句：The peacefulness of the countryside was a welcome change after the city's noise.  乡村的宁静是城市喧嚣后令人欣慰的改变。

例句：The sunset cast a lovely peacefulness over the lake. 日落给湖面蒙上了一层美丽的宁静。


近义词：tranquility, serenity, calm, quietude, stillness |反义词：turbulence, chaos, turmoil, unrest, agitation |词性：名词
