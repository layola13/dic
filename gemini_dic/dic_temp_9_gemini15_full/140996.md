onepage:/ˈwʌnpeɪdʒ/| adj.|单页的；一页的|A document or presentation that can be contained on a single page.|例句：This is a onepage summary of the project. 这是一份项目的单页摘要。|近义词：single-page, concise|反义词：multipage, lengthy

This summary fits onto one page. = 这个摘要可以放在一页纸上。
