shrewder:/ˈʃruːdər/| adj.|更精明的，更狡猾的|More astute or sharper in judgment; showing more cunning or shrewdness.|He was shrewder than he appeared, quickly spotting the flaw in the argument. 他比看起来更精明，很快就发现了论证中的缺陷。| astute, clever, sharp, cunning|naive, foolish, gullible, simple|形容词
