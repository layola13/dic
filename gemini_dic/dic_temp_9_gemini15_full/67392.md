extermination:/ɪkˌstɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 消灭；根除；灭绝 | The complete destruction of something, especially a group of people or animals.  |  例句：The extermination of the rats was a priority for the city health department.  城市卫生部门的首要任务是消灭老鼠。

例句：The planned extermination of the entire species is morally reprehensible. 计划灭绝整个物种在道德上是令人谴责的。


近义词：annihilation, eradication, destruction, elimination | 反义词：preservation, conservation, protection | 词性：名词
