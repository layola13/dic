reproves:/rɪˈpruːvz/| vt.|责备；谴责；训斥|To express disapproval of; to rebuke or reprimand.  It implies a more serious or formal expression of disapproval than simply criticizing. |例句：The teacher reproved the student for talking in class. 老师因学生在课堂上讲话而责备他。

例句：His father reproved him for his bad behavior. 他的父亲因他的不良行为而责备他。


近义词：rebuke, reprimand, reprove, chide, scold, admonish|反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproves his son for lying. = 他因儿子撒谎而责备他。
She reproves her employees for their carelessness. = 她因员工的粗心大意而责备他们。


一般过去时:
The judge reproved the lawyer for his inappropriate remarks. = 法官因律师的不得体言论而责备他。
My mother reproved me for breaking the vase. = 妈妈因为我打破了花瓶而责备我。


现在完成时:
He has reproved her many times for her lateness. = 他已经多次因她的迟到而责备她了。
The teacher has reproved the students for their disruptive behavior. = 老师已经因学生们的扰乱行为而责备他们了。


过去完成时:
She had reproved him before, but he continued to misbehave. = 她以前就责备过他，但他还是继续不规矩。
The principal had reproved the students for their cheating before the exam. = 校长在考试前就因为他们的作弊行为而责备了学生们。


将来时:
The manager will reprove the staff for their inefficiency. = 经理将会因员工的效率低下而责备他们。
I know he will reprove me for my mistakes. = 我知道他会因为我的错误而责备我。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reprove him for his rudeness. = 如果我是你，我会因为他粗鲁无礼而责备他。
I wish I had reproved him earlier. = 我真希望我早点责备他。


被动语态:
He was reproved for his actions. = 他因自己的行为而受到责备。
The students were reproved for their lack of discipline. = 学生们因缺乏纪律而受到责备。


疑问句:
Will you reprove him for his behavior? = 你会因为他行为而责备他吗？
Should I reprove my child for this minor offense? = 我应该因为这件小错而责备我的孩子吗？


否定句:
I did not reprove him for his mistake. = 我没有因为他的错误而责备他。
She never reproves her children, which is a mistake. = 她从不责备她的孩子，这是一个错误。
