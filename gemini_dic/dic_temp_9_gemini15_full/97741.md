initializations:/ɪˌnɪʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |初始化，初始设定 |The process of setting initial values for variables, parameters, or data structures at the start of a program or system.  It prepares the system for operation. |Example: The initialization of the database took several hours. 数据库的初始化花了几个小时。|近义词：setup, configuration, instantiation|反义词：termination, shutdown
