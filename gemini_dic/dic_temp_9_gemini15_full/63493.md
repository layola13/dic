entwined:/ɪnˈtwaɪnd/ | adj. v. | 交织的；缠绕的 |  Intertwined or twisted together.  |例句：The destinies of the two countries were entwined.  两国的命运交织在一起。

例句：The branches of the trees were entwined overhead. 树枝在头顶上缠绕在一起。


近义词：intertwined, interwoven, interlaced, tangled, braided | 反义词：separated, untied, disentangled | 词性：adj., v.
