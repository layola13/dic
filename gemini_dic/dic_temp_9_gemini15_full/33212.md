chronicle:/ˈkrɒnɪkl/ /ˈkrɑːnɪkl/| n., v.|编年史；记录；逐一记载|a factual written account of important or historical events; a record of events in order of their occurrence|The historian meticulously chronicled the reign of the emperor.|近义词：record, annals, history|反义词：oblivion, forgetfulness|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chronicles the daily events of his life in a journal. = 他在一个日记本里记录他每天的生活事件。
She chronicles the progress of her research in regular reports. = 她定期在报告中记录她的研究进展。


一般过去时:
The monk chronicled the history of the monastery for centuries. = 这个僧侣几个世纪以来一直在记录修道院的历史。
They chronicled the events leading up to the war in a detailed book. = 他们在一本详尽的书中记录了导致战争发生的事件。


现在完成时:
She has chronicled the changing landscape of the city for over twenty years. = 二十多年来，她一直在记录这座城市不断变化的景象。
Historians have chronicled the rise and fall of many empires. = 史学家们已经记录了许多帝国的兴衰。


过去完成时:
Before the publication, they had chronicled the entire process of writing the book. = 在出版之前，他们已经记录了写这本书的整个过程。
The ancient scrolls had chronicled the lives of the Pharaohs for generations. = 古代卷轴记录了世世代代法老的生活。


将来时:
She will chronicle the expedition to the Amazon rainforest in her upcoming book. = 她将在即将出版的书中记录亚马逊雨林探险的经历。
The journalist will chronicle the upcoming election campaign. = 记者将记录即将到来的竞选活动。


虚拟语气:
If I had a time machine, I would chronicle the events of ancient Rome. = 如果我有一台时间机器，我将记录古罗马的事件。
I wish I could chronicle the lives of my ancestors. = 我希望我能记录我祖先的生活。


被动语态:
The events of the revolution were chronicled by many different historians. = 革命的事件被许多不同的历史学家记录了下来。
The king's life has been chronicled extensively in many books. = 国王的生平在许多书中都有广泛的记载。


疑问句:
Will the newspaper chronicle the court case? = 报纸会记录这个法庭案件吗？
Did the author chronicle the lives of ordinary people? = 作者记录了普通人的生活吗？


否定句:
The ancient civilization's history was not fully chronicled. = 古代文明的历史没有被完全记录下来。
He did not chronicle his travels in a diary. = 他没有在日记中记录他的旅行。



PHRASE->
chronicle of events = 事件记录
chronicle the history = 记录历史
chronicle of the war = 战争编年史
a detailed chronicle = 详细的编年史
chronicle the rise = 记录兴起
