colonitis:/ˌkɒləˈnaɪtɪs/ | n. |结肠炎|Inflammation of the colon, the large intestine.  Symptoms can include abdominal pain, cramping, diarrhea, and sometimes bloody stool.  The cause can vary, including infections, inflammatory bowel disease (like ulcerative colitis or Crohn's disease), and ischemic colitis. Treatment depends on the underlying cause.| Example:  He was diagnosed with colonitis after experiencing severe abdominal pain and bloody diarrhea. 他在经历剧烈腹痛和血便后被诊断出患有结肠炎。|近义词：colitis, large bowel inflammation |反义词：None (It describes a condition, not something with a direct opposite).
