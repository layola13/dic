flexuous:/ˈflɛkʃuəs/| adj.|弯曲的，蜿蜒的，柔软的 | Bending readily; pliant; supple; characterized by a series of curves or bends.  The word describes something that curves or bends repeatedly in a graceful or sinuous way. |例句：The flexuous willow branches swayed gently in the breeze.  柳树柔软的枝条在微风中轻轻摇曳。

The road followed a flexuous path through the mountains. 这条路蜿蜒穿过群山。


近义词：winding, curving, serpentine, sinuous, tortuous |反义词：straight, direct, rigid, inflexible


词性: 形容词
