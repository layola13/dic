pre-addressed:/ˌpriː əˈdrɛst/| adj. | 预先写好地址的 | Already having an address written on it.  This typically refers to envelopes, packages, or labels that have the recipient's address already printed or written on them, making them ready for mailing. |Example:  I sent a pre-addressed envelope with my donation. 我用预先写好地址的信封寄了我的捐款。


The company sent pre-addressed return labels with the order. 公司随订单附送了预先写好地址的回邮标签。


近义词：addressed in advance, pre-labeled


反义词：unaddressed, blank


词性：形容词
