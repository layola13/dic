karroo:/kəˈruː/| n.|卡鲁（南非干旱地区）|A semi-desert region in South Africa, characterized by its arid climate and sparse vegetation.  The Karoo is divided into several subregions, including the Great Karoo and the Little Karoo. |The Karoo landscape is stunning, with its dramatic rock formations and unique flora.|近义词：veld (broader term for open country)|反义词：无|名词
