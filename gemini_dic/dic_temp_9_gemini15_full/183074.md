reponder:/rɪˈpɒndə(r)/| n. |应答者，回答者 |A person who responds or answers.  |例句：The responder quickly dispatched the emergency call.|应答者迅速处理了紧急呼叫。|近义词：answerer, respondent|反义词：initiator, questioner |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The responder answers the phone. = 应答者接电话。
He is a quick responder in emergencies. = 他是紧急情况下的快速应答者。


一般过去时:
The responder answered the call promptly. = 应答者迅速接听了电话。
She was a dedicated responder for many years. = 她多年来一直是一位尽职的应答者。


现在完成时:
The responder has already answered the question. = 应答者已经回答了这个问题。
He has been a responder since 2000. = 自2000年以来，他一直是应答者。

将来时:
