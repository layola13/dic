intersubsistence:/ˌɪntər səbˈsɪstəns/| n. |相互依存，共同生存|The concept refers to the mutual dependence and interconnectedness of beings or entities, where their survival and well-being are intertwined and rely on each other.  It emphasizes the relational aspect of existence, highlighting how individual entities cannot exist or thrive in isolation. | Example: The intersubsistence of the various species in the rainforest ecosystem is crucial for its stability. 雨林生态系统中各种物种的相互依存对于其稳定性至关重要。|近义词：interdependence, mutual dependence, symbiosis |反义词：independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency|名词
