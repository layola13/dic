racemose:/rəˈmoʊs/| adj.|总状的；穗状的；|Of or relating to a type of inflorescence in which the flowers are arranged in a cluster along an unbranched or branched axis.  It refers to a flower arrangement where individual flowers are borne on short stalks along a central stem, resembling a bunch of grapes.|例句：The racemose inflorescence of the lilac is quite striking.|近义词：paniculate, clustered|反义词：solitary
