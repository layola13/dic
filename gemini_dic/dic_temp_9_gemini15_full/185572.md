revegetate:/ˌriːˈvɛdʒɪteɪt/| vt.|使重新长满植被；使复绿|To re-establish vegetation on (an area of land).  The process involves planting seeds, seedlings, or other plant materials to restore plant life to a previously barren or degraded area. |例句：The hillside was revegetated after the fire.  火灾后山坡重新长满了植被。

例句：The government is working to revegetate the mined land. 政府正在努力使矿区重新长满植被。


近义词：replant, afforest, reforest|反义词：devegetate, denude
