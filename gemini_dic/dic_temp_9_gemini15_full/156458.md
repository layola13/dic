pianissimos:/ˌpjænɪˈsɪmoʊz/| n.| 最弱音(复数) |The plural form of pianissimo, indicating multiple instances of playing very softly.  It refers to a musical direction instructing a performer to play as softly as possible.| 例句：The pianist played several pianissimos in the delicate passage.  钢琴家在轻柔的段落中演奏了几个最弱音。 The repeated pianissimos created a sense of hushed mystery. 重复的最弱音营造了一种神秘的寂静感。|近义词：softest, whispers, faintest|反义词：fortissimos, loudest
