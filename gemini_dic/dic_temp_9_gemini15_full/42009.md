costermonger:/ˈkɒstəˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 小贩，水果蔬菜商 | A costermonger is a street vendor, typically selling fruit and vegetables. 词源:
The word "costermonger" comes from the Middle English "coster," meaning "costard" (a type of apple), and "monger," meaning "trader" or "dealer."  Thus, a costermonger was originally a dealer in costard apples, but the term broadened to include any street vendor selling fruit and vegetables.
