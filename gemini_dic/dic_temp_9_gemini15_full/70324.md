field-testing: /ˈfiːldˌtɛstɪŋ/ | n. | 现场测试，实地测试 | The process of testing a product or system in a real-world environment to evaluate its performance and identify any potential problems.  |  例句：The company conducted extensive field-testing of the new software before its release.  该公司在发布新软件之前进行了广泛的现场测试。

例句：The new car underwent rigorous field-testing in various terrains. 这辆新车在各种地形进行了严格的实地测试。


近义词：real-world testing, practical testing, in-situ testing | 反义词：laboratory testing, simulation testing | 词性：名词
