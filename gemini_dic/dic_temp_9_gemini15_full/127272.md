navbar:/ˈnævˌbɑːr/| n. |导航栏|A navigation bar is a graphical control element that provides navigation links for a website or application. It typically appears at the top or side of the screen and contains buttons or links that allow users to access different sections or features.  |Example:  The website's navbar made it easy to find the contact page. 网站的导航栏方便用户找到联系页面。|近义词：navigation menu, menu bar|反义词：无
