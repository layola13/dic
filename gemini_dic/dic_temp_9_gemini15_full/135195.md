nonorientation:/ˌnɒnˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən/ | n. |  非定向；缺乏方向感；迷失方向 | Lack of orientation; a state of being disoriented or lost.  It can refer to a lack of understanding, direction, or purpose. | Example: The hiker experienced nonorientation in the dense fog.  徒步旅行者在浓雾中迷失了方向。  The student's nonorientation to the new curriculum caused difficulties. 学生对新课程的不了解导致了困难。 | 近义词：disorientation, confusion, bewilderment | 反义词：orientation, guidance, direction
