/kræk/| n. v.|裂缝；破裂；（使）破裂；|A crack is a line on the surface of something where it has broken, but not completely separated.  To crack something means to break it, causing a line or lines to appear on its surface. |例句：There's a crack in the wall. 墙上有一条裂缝。

The vase cracked when it fell. 花瓶掉下来摔裂了。|近义词：fissure, split, fracture|反义词：mend, repair, whole|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vase cracks under pressure. = 花瓶在压力下裂开了。
He cracks jokes all the time. = 他总是开玩笑。


一般过去时:
The ice cracked under my weight. = 冰在我体重下裂开了。
The old woman cracked a smile.  = 老太太露出笑容。


现在完成时:
The drought has cracked the earth. = 干旱使土地龟裂。
He has cracked the code. = 他破译了密码。


过去完成时:
The building had cracked before the earthquake. = 这座建筑在地震前就已经裂开了。
She had cracked under the pressure of the job. = 她承受不住工作压力而崩溃了。

被动语态:

否定句:
