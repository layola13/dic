gastronomist:/ˈɡæstrənɒmɪst/ | n. |美食家；烹饪专家 |A gastronomist is an expert in gastronomy, the art or science of good eating.  They possess extensive knowledge of food, its preparation, and its cultural significance. Gastronomists may be chefs, food writers, critics, or researchers who study the history and culture of food. | Example:  The renowned gastronomist, renowned for his insightful critiques,  published a new book on the culinary traditions of Southeast Asia.  著名的美食家以其富有见地的评论而闻名，出版了一本关于东南亚烹饪传统的新书。|近义词：gourmet, epicure, food connoisseur |反义词：None specifically, but someone with little interest in food could be considered an antonym.
