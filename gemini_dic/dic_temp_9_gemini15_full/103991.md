juggles:/ˈdʒʌɡlz/| vt.|玩杂耍；同时应付；巧妙处理|To keep several objects in motion in the air at the same time, typically by throwing and catching them repeatedly; to manage many tasks or responsibilities at the same time. |例句：He juggles three balls with amazing skill. 他以惊人的技巧玩弄三个球。

例句：She skillfully juggles her career and family responsibilities. 她巧妙地兼顾事业和家庭责任。|近义词：balances, manages, maneuvers, handles |反义词: neglects, ignores, drops


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He juggles three balls every day. = 他每天玩三个球的杂耍。
She juggles work and family life. = 她同时处理工作和家庭生活。


一般过去时:
The clown juggled brightly colored balls. = 小丑玩弄色彩鲜艳的球。
He juggled his schedule to fit in the meeting. = 他调整了日程安排以参加会议。


现在完成时:
He has juggled his finances carefully for years. = 多年来，他小心地处理他的财务状况。
She has juggled multiple projects simultaneously. = 她同时处理多个项目。


过去完成时:
He had juggled several jobs before finding his current position. = 在找到现在的工作之前，他做过几份工作。
She had juggled her commitments for months before she finally cracked. = 在她最终崩溃之前，她已经处理了几个月的承诺。


将来时:
I will juggle my responsibilities to make time for the trip. = 我将调整我的责任，为旅行腾出时间。
He will juggle work and studies next semester. = 下学期他将同时处理工作和学习。


虚拟语气:
If I could juggle my time better, I would achieve more. = 如果我能更好地安排我的时间，我会取得更多成就。
I wish I could juggle all these tasks effectively. = 我希望我能有效地处理所有这些任务。


被动语态:
The balls were juggled expertly by the performer. = 球被表演者熟练地抛接。

疑问句:
