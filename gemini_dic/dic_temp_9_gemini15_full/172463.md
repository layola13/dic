what:/wɑːt/| pron.|什么，什么东西，多么|英文解析：Interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something or someone.  It can also be used to express the degree or extent of something.|例句：What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What time is it? = 现在几点了？|近义词：which, who, whom|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到不舒服之前吃了什么？
What had they already discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What happened to your car? = 你的车怎么了？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She didn't say what she wanted. = 她没说她想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What about...? = ...怎么样？
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
No matter what = 无论如何
