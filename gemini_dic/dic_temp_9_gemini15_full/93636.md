imboss:/ɪmˈbɒs/| vt. n.|压印；凸印；浮雕|To raise a design in relief on (a surface), typically by pressing from the back.|The artist embossed the leather with a floral pattern.  艺术家用花卉图案压印皮革。|The embossed logo looked elegant on the cover.  压印的标志在封面上看起来很优雅。|emgrave, impress|flatten, smooth|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He embosses leather goods. = 他压印皮革制品。
The company embosses corporate logos on stationery. = 公司在文具上压印公司标志。

一般过去时:
She embossed the invitation cards with gold leaf. = 她用金箔压印请柬。
They embossed the metal plate with a detailed image. = 他们用精细的图像压印金属板。

现在完成时:
The artist has embossed hundreds of pieces of artwork. =这位艺术家已经压印了数百件艺术品。
We have embossed the trophies for the winners. = 我们已经为获奖者压印了奖杯。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, they had embossed the certificates. = 在演示文稿之前，他们已经压印了证书。
She had embossed the invitations weeks before the wedding. = 婚礼前几周，她已经压印好了请柬。


将来时:

被动语态:
The leather was embossed with a delicate pattern. = 皮革被压印上了精致的图案。
The awards were embossed with the company's insignia. = 奖项被压印上了公司的徽章。


疑问句:

否定句:
