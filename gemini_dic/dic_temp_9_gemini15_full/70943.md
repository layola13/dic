firefight:/ˈfaɪərfaɪt/| n. v.|灭火战斗；冲突；激烈争论|The act of fighting a fire; a battle or struggle, especially a brief but intense one; a heated argument or dispute.|例句：The firefighters bravely fought the firefight. 消防员勇敢地进行了灭火战斗。

例句：The two companies engaged in a fierce firefight over market share. 两家公司为了市场份额展开了激烈的争夺。|近义词：battle, struggle, conflict, altercation|反义词：peace, harmony, agreement|名词,动词
