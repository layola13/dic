nonsentence:/ˈnɑːnsɛntəns/| n. |非句子；不成句子的词语|A nonsentence is a sequence of words that does not conform to the grammatical rules of a sentence.  It may lack a subject, verb, or complete thought.  It is often used in informal speech or writing.|Example: "Nice day." (This is a nonsentence because it lacks a verb and a complete thought.)|近义词：phrase, fragment, incomplete sentence|反义词：sentence
