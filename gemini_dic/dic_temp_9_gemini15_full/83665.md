haffle:/ˈhæfəl/ | v. |使困惑；使为难；使犹豫 |to confuse or bewilder someone; to make someone hesitate |例句：The complex instructions haffled him. 复杂的指令把他弄糊涂了。|例句：The unexpected question haffled the speaker.  出乎意料的问题使演讲者犹豫了。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, perplex, puzzle |反义词：clarify, enlighten
