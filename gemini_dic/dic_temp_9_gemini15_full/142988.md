outbarter:/ˈaʊtˈbɑːrtər/| vt.|以物易物；用…交换|To exchange goods or services without the use of money.|They outbartered their extra vegetables for some eggs from their neighbor.|近义词：trade, exchange, swap|反义词：buy, sell

We outbarter services with our friends to save money. = 我们和朋友们互相交换服务以节省开支。
