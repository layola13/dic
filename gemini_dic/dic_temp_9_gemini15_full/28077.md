capibara:/ˌkæpɪˈbɑːrə/| n. |水豚|A large, semi-aquatic, herbivorous rodent native to South America.  Capybaras are known for their docile nature and are the largest living rodents in the world.|例句：The capybara peacefully grazed on the riverbank.  水豚平静地啃食着河岸边的草。|例句： We observed a family of capybaras swimming in the lake. 我们观察到一群水豚在湖里游泳。|近义词：rodent (general term)|反义词：None specifically, but words describing small or aggressive animals could be considered antonyms in context. |
