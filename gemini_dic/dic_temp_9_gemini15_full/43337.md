crampfish:/ˈkræmpˌfɪʃ/| n.|电鳐|Electric ray; a type of fish that can generate a strong electric shock.  Crampfish are known for their ability to produce electricity to stun prey or defend themselves. They are flat-bodied rays found in various oceans around the world. | The fisherman received a powerful shock from a crampfish.  渔夫被电鳐电了一下。|  electric ray, numbfish |  (no clear antonym)|名词
