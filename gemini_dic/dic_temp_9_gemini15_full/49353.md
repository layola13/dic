delinquency:/dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi/| n. |违法行为；失职；怠忽；不良行为 |The act or instance of failing to fulfill an obligation or perform a duty; a lapse or omission of duty. It often refers to crimes committed by young people.  |例句：His delinquency led to his arrest. 他的违法行为导致了他的逮捕。|例句：The company's delinquency in paying taxes resulted in a fine. 公司拖欠税款导致被罚款。|近义词：misconduct, neglect, offense, crime |反义词：obedience, compliance, diligence
