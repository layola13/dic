reasonings: /ˈriːzənɪŋz/ | n. | 推理；论证；理由 | The process of forming conclusions, judgments, or inferences from facts or premises.  It involves a series of logical steps to arrive at a conclusion. |


例句：

His reasonings were flawed and led to incorrect conclusions.  他的推理有缺陷，导致了错误的结论。

The judge carefully considered the reasonings presented by both sides before delivering the verdict.  法官仔细考虑了双方提出的论证后，才宣判。


近义词：arguments, inferences, deductions, justifications, rationales

反义词：illogic, irrationality, nonsense


词性：名词
