decoct:/dɪˈkɒkt/| vt.|煎煮(药材等)，熬制|To extract the essence of (a substance) by boiling.  The term often refers to the preparation of medicinal substances from plants. |例句：She decocted herbs to make a soothing tea. 她煎煮草药来泡制舒缓的茶。

例句：The pharmacist decocted the ingredients according to the ancient recipe. 药剂师按照古老的配方煎煮药材。


近义词：boil, brew, simmer|反义词：infuse (in the context of herbal preparation, where infusion uses hot water without boiling)|词性：动词
