corroborator:/kəˈrɑːbəreɪtər/ | n. | 佐证者；证实者 | A person who corroborates a statement or theory.  Someone who provides supporting evidence. | The witness's testimony served as a corroborator for the defendant's alibi.  证人的证词为被告的不在场证明提供了佐证。| confirmer, verifier, supporter | contradictor, denier | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He corroborates the story with evidence. 他用证据证实了这个故事。
She corroborates her claims with data. 她用数据证实了她的说法。


一般过去时:
The expert corroborated the findings of the initial report. 专家证实了初步报告的调查结果。
He corroborated his statement with documents. 他用文件证实了他的说法。


现在完成时:
The police have corroborated the suspect's alibi. 警方已经证实了嫌疑人的不在场证明。
Scientists have corroborated the theory with new research. 科学家们用新的研究证实了这个理论。


过去完成时:
They had corroborated the information before releasing the statement. 他们在发布声明之前已经证实了信息。
The investigators had corroborated the witness's testimony. 调查人员已经证实了证人的证词。


将来时:
Experts will corroborate the results of the study. 专家们将证实这项研究的结果。
We will corroborate the findings with further analysis. 我们将用进一步的分析来证实这些发现。


虚拟语气:
If he could find more evidence, he would corroborate the theory. 如果他能找到更多证据，他就能证实这个理论。
If she had more time, she would corroborate the claims with more research. 如果她有更多时间，她就能用更多的研究来证实这些说法。


被动语态:
The story was corroborated by several witnesses. 这个故事得到了几位证人的证实。
The findings were corroborated by independent researchers. 这些发现得到了独立研究人员的证实。


疑问句:
Does this evidence corroborate the suspect's alibi? 这证据是否证实了嫌疑人的不在场证明？
Will further testing corroborate the initial results? 进一步的测试是否会证实初步结果？


否定句:
The witness's testimony did not corroborate the defendant's claim. 证人的证词并没有证实被告的说法。
The new evidence does not corroborate the existing theory. 新证据并没有证实现有的理论。
