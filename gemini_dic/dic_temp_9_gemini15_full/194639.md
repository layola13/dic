self-training:/ˌsɛlfˈtreɪnɪŋ/| n.|自我训练，自主学习|The process of learning or improving a skill or ability without the guidance of a teacher or instructor.  It often involves setting personal goals, finding resources, and monitoring one's own progress.|例句：She undertook a program of self-training to improve her coding skills. 她进行了一套自我训练计划来提高她的编程技能。

例句：The athlete relied on self-training to prepare for the competition.  这位运动员依靠自我训练来为比赛做准备。|近义词：self-education, independent study, autodidacticism|反义词：formal training, instructed learning|名词
