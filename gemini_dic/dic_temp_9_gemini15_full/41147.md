copulae:/ˈkɒpjʊleɪ/ |/ˈkɑːpjʊleɪ/| n. |系动词；联结词| Linking verbs; words that connect a subject to a predicate.  Copulae are verbs that don't express action but link the subject of a sentence to a predicate that describes or identifies the subject.  Common examples include "be," "seem," "appear," "become," and "remain." |例句：The sky is blue. (天空是蓝色的。)  The dog seems happy. (那条狗看起来很开心。) |近义词：linking verbs, connecting verbs |反义词：action verbs


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flower is beautiful. = 这朵花很漂亮。
He seems tired. = 他看起来很累。


一般过去时:
She was a teacher. = 她是一位老师。
The movie appeared boring. = 那部电影看起来很无聊。


现在完成时:
They have become friends. = 他们已经成为朋友了。
It has remained unchanged. = 它保持不变。


过去完成时:
The situation had become critical. = 情况已经变得危急。
He had seemed happy earlier. = 他早些时候看起来很开心。


将来时:
The weather will be sunny. = 天气将会晴朗。
She will become a doctor. = 她将会成为一名医生。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go. = 如果我是你，我会去。
It would seem unfair. = 这看起来不公平。


被动语态:
The problem is being solved. = 问题正在被解决。
The book is considered a classic. = 这本书被认为是经典之作。


疑问句:
Is he coming? = 他来了吗？
Was she at home? = 她在家吗？


否定句:
The answer is not correct. = 答案不正确。
It doesn't seem likely. = 这看起来不太可能。


PHRASE->
There is/are... = 有...
It is/was...that... = 是...
It seems that... = 看起来...
It appears that... = 看起来...
It remains to be seen... = 有待观察...
