godwinson:/ˈɡɒdˌwɪnsən/| n. |戈德温森（姓氏） |Godwinson is a surname of Anglo-Saxon origin. It is a patronymic surname, meaning it is derived from a personal name.  The name is composed of the elements "Godwin" and "son," indicating "son of Godwin." Godwin itself is a compound name meaning "God's friend" or "God's victory."  The surname is historically associated with the powerful Godwin family of Anglo-Saxon England.  |Harold Godwinson, the last Anglo-Saxon king of England, was the most famous bearer of this surname.  |近义词:|无 |反义词:|无 |名词
