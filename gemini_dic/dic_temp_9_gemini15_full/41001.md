cop-out:/ˈkɒpˌaʊt/| n. v.|逃避责任；找借口；敷衍了事|An act of avoiding a responsibility or difficult task; a way of avoiding something unpleasant or difficult.|例句：He tried to cop out of doing the dishes. 他试图逃避洗碗的责任。|例句：His excuse was a cop-out. 他的借口是找的托词。|近义词：excuse, evasion, avoidance|反义词：responsibility, accountability, commitment

She cops out whenever things get tough. = 她每当事情变得艰难时就逃避。
