groundcovers:/ˈɡraʊndˌkʌvərz/| n.|地被植物|Plants that cover the ground, typically low-growing and spreading.  They are used in landscaping to suppress weeds, prevent soil erosion, and provide visual interest.|Example:  We planted various groundcovers to prevent erosion on the hillside. 我们种植了各种地被植物来防止山坡上的侵蚀。|近义词：creepers, spreading plants, low-growing plants|反义词：trees, tall plants
