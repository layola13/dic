nonperishables:/ˌnɒnˈpɛrɪʃəbəlz/| n. |不易腐烂的食品；耐用品|Nonperishables are food items that have a long shelf life and do not spoil easily.  They are typically stored in a cool, dry place.|We stocked up on nonperishables like canned goods and bottled water before the hurricane.|近义词：staples, provisions|反义词：perishables
