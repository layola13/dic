intensifies:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪz/ | v. | 加剧；增强；变强烈 | To become or make more intense.  | 例句：The storm intensified as the night went on.  暴风雨在夜间加剧了。| 例句：He intensified his efforts to find a solution. 他加紧努力寻找解决方案。| 近义词：escalate, aggravate, heighten, strengthen | 反义词：weaken, diminish, lessen, abate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The conflict intensifies daily. = 冲突日益加剧。
The sun intensifies the heat. = 太阳增强了热量。

一般过去时:
The pain intensified after the surgery. = 手术后疼痛加剧了。
The argument intensified until it became a shouting match. = 争论越来越激烈，最后演变成一场喊叫。

现在完成时:
The drought has intensified over the past few months. = 干旱在过去几个月里加剧了。
The economic crisis has intensified, causing widespread unemployment. = 经济危机加剧，导致大范围失业。

过去完成时:
The situation had intensified before the intervention. = 干预之前，局势已经恶化了。
Tensions had intensified significantly before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前，紧张局势已大大加剧。

将来时:
The pressure will intensify as the deadline approaches. = 随着截止日期的临近，压力将会加大。
The competition will intensify in the coming years. = 未来几年竞争将会加剧。

虚拟语气:
If the government intensified its efforts, the problem could be solved. = 如果政府加大力度，这个问题就能解决。
I wish the government would intensify its efforts to combat poverty. = 我希望政府能加大力度打击贫困。

被动语态:
The security measures were intensified after the attack. = 袭击之后，安全措施加强了。
The search efforts are being intensified to find the missing child. = 正在加紧搜寻失踪儿童。

疑问句:
Will the pressure intensify further? = 压力还会进一步加大吗？
Does the heat intensify in the afternoon? = 下午热度会增强吗？

否定句:
The government did not intensify its efforts to control inflation. = 政府没有加大力度控制通货膨胀。
The effects of the medication did not intensify. = 药物的效果没有增强。
