The ancient prophecies forespeak a time of great upheaval.  古代预言预示着一个动荡的时代。|近义词：predict, foreshadow, foretell, portend|反义词：postdict

词源:

The word "forespeak" is a compound word formed from the prefix "fore-" (meaning "before" or "in advance") and the verb "speak".  It's a relatively uncommon word, retaining a strong archaic or literary flavor.  Its etymology directly reflects its meaning: to speak of something before it happens.
