rhetoricians: /rɛˈtɒrɪʃənz/ | n. |修辞学家；雄辩家 | Experts in rhetoric, the art of persuasive speaking or writing.  They study and teach the principles and techniques of effective communication. |  The rhetoricians debated the effectiveness of different persuasive strategies.  修辞学家们就不同说服策略的有效性进行了辩论。|  orators, speakers, writers |  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑与缺乏表达技巧的人对比)  unskilled communicators
