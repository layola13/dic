pirouetting:/ˌpɪruˈɛtɪŋ/ | v. |旋转，快速旋转 | The act of pirouetting;  a spinning or whirling movement, typically done by a dancer on one leg.  |例句：The ballerina pirouetted gracefully across the stage.  芭蕾舞演员优雅地在舞台上旋转。

The dancer pirouetted effortlessly, her movements fluid and precise. 舞者轻盈地旋转，动作流畅精准。


近义词：spinning, twirling, whirling, revolving | 反义词：standing still
