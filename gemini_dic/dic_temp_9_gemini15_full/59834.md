ectolecithal: /ˌɛktoʊˈlɛsɪθəl/ | adj. | 外卵黄的 |  Describing an egg in which the yolk is located outside the embryo, or in which the yolk is relatively small and distributed throughout the cytoplasm.  This is in contrast to eggs where the yolk is concentrated in the center (macrolecithal) or evenly distributed (isolecithal).|  The insect egg is typically ectolecithal, with the yolk distributed throughout the cytoplasm.  昆虫卵通常是外卵黄的，卵黄分布在整个细胞质中。|近义词：peripheral yolk (informal)|反义词：endolecithal, macrolecithal


词源：

* **ecto-**:  From Greek *ektós*, meaning "outside," "external."
* **lecith-**: From Greek *lékithos*, meaning "egg yolk."
* **-al**:  Suffix forming adjectives.


用法说明：

ectolecithal 主要用于描述卵细胞的卵黄分布情况，是一个生物学专业术语。  它不常用在日常生活中。  在学术文章，尤其是胚胎学或发育生物学相关的论文中，会经常出现。


例句：

1.  Many insects possess ectolecithal eggs, facilitating their development outside the mother's body. 许多昆虫拥有外卵黄卵，这有利于它们在母亲体外发育。

2.  The research compared the development of ectolecithal and endolecithal eggs under different environmental conditions. 这项研究比较了在不同环境条件下外卵黄卵和内卵黄卵的发育情况。


由于ectolecithal是一个专业术语，其语法用法仅限于在句子中作定语修饰名词（例如，ectolecithal eggs）。  没有特别的时态、语态或语气变化需要额外说明。  也没有常用的词组搭配。
