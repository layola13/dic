monogenist:/ˈmɒnədʒənɪst/| n. | 一元论者；相信人类起源于单一祖先的人 | A monogenist is someone who believes that all humans originated from a single pair of ancestors.  This theory contrasts with polygenism, which posits multiple origins for humanity.  Monogenists often cite religious or philosophical reasons for their belief. | Example:  The debate between monogenists and polygenists raged for centuries. = 一元论者和多元论者之间的争论持续了几个世纪。|近义词：unigenist |反义词：polygenist |名词
