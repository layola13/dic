needed:/ˈniːdɪd/| adj.|需要的，必要的|Required or essential; something that is necessary.|The tools needed for the job were scattered on the floor.  需要这工作的工具散落在地板上。|  The additional funds are needed to complete the project.  完成这个项目需要额外的资金。|necessary, essential, requisite|unnecessary, superfluous, unwanted|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project needs more funding. = 这个项目需要更多资金。
We need to leave now. = 我们现在需要离开。


一般过去时:
She needed help with her homework. = 她需要帮助完成作业。
They needed a larger space for the event. = 他们需要一个更大的空间来举办活动。


现在完成时:
We have needed a new car for a long time. = 我们很久以前就需要一辆新车了。
He has needed medical attention since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，他就一直需要医疗护理。


过去完成时:
I had needed a break before the meeting. = 我在会议前需要休息一下。
They had needed more time to prepare. = 他们需要更多时间来准备。


将来时:
We will need more volunteers next year. = 我们明年将需要更多志愿者。
They will need to reschedule the meeting. = 他们将需要重新安排会议。


虚拟语气:
If I needed help, I would ask. = 如果我需要帮助，我会请求。
It would be helpful if we needed less paperwork. = 如果我们需要的文书工作更少，那就太好了。


被动语态:
More resources are needed for the research. = 研究需要更多资源。
Additional staff is needed to handle the workload. = 需要额外的人员来处理工作量。


疑问句:
What is needed to complete the task? = 完成这项任务需要什么？
Is anything else needed? = 还需要什么吗？


否定句:
No further information is needed at this time. = 目前不需要更多信息。
He didn't need my help after all. = 他最终不需要我的帮助。


PHRASE->
