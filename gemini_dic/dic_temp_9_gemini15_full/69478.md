felanders:/ˈfɛləndərz/| n.|飞鼠|A felander is a small, arboreal marsupial belonging to the family Petauridae, characterized by its gliding membrane and nocturnal habits. They are found in Australia and surrounding islands.|例句：The sugar glider is a type of felander.  =  飞鼠是一种飞鼯。|近义词：flying squirrel (though not a true marsupial)|反义词:None.
