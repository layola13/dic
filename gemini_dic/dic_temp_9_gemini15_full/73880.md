Its use should be avoided in modern contexts due to its strong negative connotations and judgmental tone. | She was branded a fornicatress by the puritanical community. 她被清教徒社区贴上了淫妇的标签。 |  slut, whore, strumpet |  (no direct antonym, but terms implying chastity or virtue could be considered opposite) | 名词
