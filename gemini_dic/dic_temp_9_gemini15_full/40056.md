consumptives:/kənˈsʌmptɪvz/| n. adj.| 肺病患者；消耗性的；患肺病的 | A person suffering from consumption (tuberculosis).  The word often carries a historical connotation, referring to those suffering from tuberculosis in the 19th century.  It can also describe something that consumes or wastes resources excessively.|例句：In the 19th century, many consumptives were sent to sanatoriums in search of a cure.  十九世纪，许多肺病患者被送往疗养院寻求治疗。  The project became a consumptive endeavor, draining resources and time without yielding results.  这个项目变成了一个消耗性的努力，耗费了资源和时间，却没有任何成果。|近义词：tubercular patients (n.), wasteful (adj.)|反义词：healthy (adj.), productive (adj.)
