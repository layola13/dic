genius:/ˈdʒiːniəs/| n. adj.|天才；天赋；天才人物|A person of extraordinary intellectual and creative power.  A genius is someone with exceptional intellectual or creative ability. |例句：Einstein was a genius in the field of physics. 爱因斯坦是物理学领域的天才。  She possesses a genius for painting. 她具有绘画的天赋。|近义词：prodigy, mastermind, talent, gifted |反义词：idiot, dunce, fool


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a genius. = 他是个天才。
This invention shows true genius. = 这项发明展现了真正的天才。


一般过去时:
Da Vinci was a genius. = 达芬奇是个天才。
The composer showed great genius in his early works. = 这名作曲家在早期作品中展现了非凡的天才。


现在完成时:
She has shown her genius in many ways. = 她已经以多种方式展现了她的天才。
Scientists have discovered a new genius in the field of medicine. = 科学家们在这个医学领域发现了一个新的天才。


过去完成时:
They had not realised his genius until after his death. = 直到他死后，他们才意识到他的天才。
The artist had already shown signs of genius as a child. = 这名艺术家从小就已展现出天才的迹象。


将来时:
He will be remembered as a genius. = 他将被人们铭记为天才。
Future generations will marvel at her genius. = 后代将惊叹于她的天才。


虚拟语气:
If he were a genius, he would solve this problem easily. = 如果他是个天才，他会轻松解决这个问题。
I wish I had the genius to write a novel like that. = 我希望我有写出那样一部小说的天才。


被动语态:
His genius was recognised late in his life. = 他的天才在他晚年才被认可。
The painting is considered a work of genius. = 这幅画被认为是天才之作。


疑问句:
Is he a genius? = 他是天才吗？
Was her work truly a product of genius? = 她的作品真的是天才之作吗？


否定句:
He is not a genius, but he is very clever. = 他不是天才，但他非常聪明。
The painting does not reflect the genius of the artist. = 这幅画没有体现出艺术家的天才。


PHRASE->
a genius at sth  = 在某方面很有天赋
a stroke of genius = 一时灵感
a person of genius = 天才人物
display genius = 展现天才
genius loci = 地灵
