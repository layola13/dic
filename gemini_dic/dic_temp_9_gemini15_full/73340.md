forefence:/ˈfɔːrfɛns/| n. |前栅栏，前篱笆 |A fence at the front of a property.  It is typically used to define the boundary of a property and provide some level of security and privacy. |例句：The forefence was made of weathered wood and needed repainting. 前栅栏是用风化的木材制成的，需要重新粉刷。  The new forefence significantly improved the curb appeal of the house. 新的前栅栏大大提高了房子的外观。|近义词：front fence, boundary fence, perimeter fence |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered the opposite of an open, undefended area. |名词
