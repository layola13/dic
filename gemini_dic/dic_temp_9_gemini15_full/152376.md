pavans:/ˈpɑːvɑːnz/| n.|帕万舞曲|A pavane is a slow, stately dance of the 16th and 17th centuries, typically in 4/4 time, characterized by its measured pace and dignified style.  It was often performed by couples in a slow, gliding motion.|例句：The dancers gracefully performed a pavane in period costume. 舞者们身着古装优雅地表演了帕万舞曲。

The musicians played a lovely pavane that filled the hall. 音乐家们演奏了一首优美的帕万舞曲，充满了整个大厅。|近义词：dance, minuet|反义词:jig, lively dance.
