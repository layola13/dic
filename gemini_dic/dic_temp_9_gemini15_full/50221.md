depauperize:/dɪˈpɔːpəraɪz/| vt.|使贫困；使枯竭；使贫瘠|To make poor; to impoverish; to exhaust or deplete.  The prolonged drought depauperized the land, leaving it barren and unproductive.  长期干旱使土地贫瘠，使其变得贫瘠且不生产。|近义词：impoverish, deplete, impoverish|反义词：enrich, enhance
