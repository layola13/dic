colemanite:/ˈkoʊlmənˌaɪt/| n. |硼酸钙|A hydrous borate mineral, calcium borate,  with the chemical formula Ca₂B₆O₁₁·5H₂O. It is a common ore of boron.  It's typically found in evaporite deposits. |Colemanite is a valuable source of borax and other boron compounds, used extensively in various industries.  |近义词：borate mineral|反义词：无
