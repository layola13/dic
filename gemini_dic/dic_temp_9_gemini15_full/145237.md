overbites:/ˈoʊvərbaɪts/ | n. | 上颚突出，暴牙 | An overbite is a condition where the upper teeth significantly overlap the lower teeth.  It's a common dental malocclusion. | Example:  She had a noticeable overbite that made her self-conscious. 她有一个明显的暴牙，让她很自卑。 |近义词：prognathism, malocclusion | 反义词：underbite
