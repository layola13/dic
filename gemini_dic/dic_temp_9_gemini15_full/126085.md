musty's:/ˈmʌsti/| adj.|发霉的，霉臭的|Having a stale, damp smell.  The smell suggests decay or dampness.|例句：The old house smelled musty.  这栋旧房子闻起来很霉。|例句：The basement has a musty odor. 地下室有一股霉味。|近义词：moldy, fusty, mildewed|反义词：fresh, sweet-smelling

USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and thus doesn't have tenses like verbs.  The usage changes depending on the sentence structure.

一般现在时:
The room smells musty. = 房间闻起来很霉。
The old books are musty. = 这些旧书闻起来很霉。

一般过去时:
The attic smelled musty yesterday. = 阁楼昨天闻起来很霉。
The clothes were musty after being stored in the damp basement. = 衣服放在潮湿的地下室后，闻起来很霉。

现在完成时:
The closet has become musty since we moved in. = 自从我们搬进来后，壁橱就变得很霉了。
The old chest has remained musty for years. = 这只旧箱子多年来一直很霉。

过去完成时:
The library had been musty before they renovated it. = 在他们翻新之前，图书馆就已经很霉了。
The papers had grown musty in the damp archives. = 这些文件在潮湿的档案馆里已经变得很霉了。


将来时:
The basement will likely become musty if we don't fix the leak. = 如果我们不修好漏水的地方，地下室很可能会变得很霉。
The stored grain will probably be musty by next spring. = 到明年春天，储存的谷物可能会发霉。

被动语态:
The musty smell was noticed by everyone. = 每个人都注意到了霉味。
The musty books were discarded. = 那些发霉的书被丢弃了。


疑问句:
Is the air musty in here? = 这里空气发霉吗？
Does this old coat smell musty? = 这件旧外套闻起来很霉吗？


否定句:
The air isn't musty in this new house. = 这栋新房子的空气不发霉。
The clothes were not musty after being washed. = 洗完衣服后，衣服没有发霉。


PHRASE->
a musty smell:一股霉味
