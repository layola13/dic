redoubts:/rɪˈdaʊts/| n. |防御工事；堡垒；避难所 |A redoubt is a strong defensive position, typically a small fort.  It is often used in a military context, but can also refer to a place of refuge or security. |例句：The soldiers held their position in the redoubt against repeated attacks. 士兵们在防御工事中抵抗了反复的攻击。

例句：She retreated to her redoubt of books and quiet solitude. 她退回到她书籍和宁静孤独的避难所。

近义词：fort, stronghold, fortification, refuge, sanctuary |反义词：open ground, vulnerability, exposure |词性：名词
