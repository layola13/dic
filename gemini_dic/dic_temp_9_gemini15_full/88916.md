hold-the-bag: /hoʊld ðə bæɡ/ | phrasal verb | 承担责任；独自承担后果 |  To be left with the negative consequences of a situation or deal.  It implies that others involved avoided responsibility. | 例句：After the company went bankrupt, the investors were left holding the bag. 公司破产后，投资者们只能独自承担后果。

The small business owner ended up holding the bag for his partner's bad decisions.  这位小企业主最终为其合伙人的错误决定承担了责任。


近义词：take the blame, shoulder the responsibility, bear the brunt, face the music


反义词：escape responsibility, shirk responsibility, evade consequences


PHRASE->
hold the bag for someone: 为某人承担责任
hold the bag on something: 为某事承担责任
be left holding the bag: 独自承担后果
end up holding the bag: 最终承担责任
get stuck holding the bag: 陷入独自承担后果的境地


词源: The phrase is idiomatic, originating in informal English.  The image is of someone being left with a bag containing undesirable items or consequences, while others have escaped.  It doesn't have a direct traceable etymology to a specific time or text, but its meaning and usage have evolved naturally through common parlance.

USAGE->(The following examples demonstrate various tenses and sentence structures, but "hold the bag" itself doesn't conjugate like a standard verb.)

一般现在时:
He always holds the bag when things go wrong. = 当事情出错时，他总是承担责任。
They hold the bag for their mismanagement. = 他们为自己的管理不善承担责任。


一般过去时:
She held the bag after her business partner ran away. = 她的商业伙伴逃跑后，她独自承担了责任。
We held the bag for the failed project. = 我们为失败的项目承担了责任。


现在完成时:
He has held the bag for years because of his brother's debts. = 多年来，他一直因他兄弟的债务而承担责任。
They have held the bag since the scandal broke. = 自丑闻爆发以来，他们一直在承担责任。


过去完成时:
The investors had held the bag for months before the lawsuit was settled. = 在诉讼解决之前，投资者们已经独自承担了后果好几个月了。
The company had held the bag for the defective products. = 公司已经为有缺陷的产品承担了责任。


将来时:
If the deal falls through, I will be holding the bag. = 如果交易失败，我将独自承担后果。
They will be holding the bag for their reckless actions. = 他们将为其鲁莽的行为承担责任。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the risks, I wouldn't have held the bag. = 如果我知道风险，我就不会承担责任了。
I wish I hadn't held the bag for him. = 我真希望我没有为他承担责任。


被动语态:(  "Hold the bag" is not typically used in the passive voice because it implies active participation, even if unwilling)  A more suitable phrasing would be needed to express a passive meaning.


疑问句:
Who's holding the bag in this situation? = 在这种情况下，谁在承担责任？
Will you be holding the bag if the project fails? = 如果项目失败，你会承担责任吗？


否定句:
He didn't hold the bag; he passed the blame onto others. = 他没有承担责任；他把责任推给了别人。
They didn't want to hold the bag, so they ran away. = 他们不想承担责任，所以他们逃跑了。
