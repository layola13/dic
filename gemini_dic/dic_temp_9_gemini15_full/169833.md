prosecution:/ˌprɒsɪˈkjuːʃən/| /ˌprɑːsɪˈkjuːʃən/| n.|起诉；检察；检方|The legal process of accusing someone of a crime and bringing them to trial.  It involves investigating the alleged crime, gathering evidence, and presenting the case in court.|例句：The prosecution presented compelling evidence against the defendant. 检方提供了令人信服的证据指控被告。|例句：The prosecution rested its case after calling its final witness. 检方在传唤了最后一名证人后结案陈词。|近义词：indictment, lawsuit, legal action|反义词：defense, acquittal
