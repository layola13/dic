fledgling:/ˈfledʒlɪŋ/| adj. n.|雏鸟；新手；刚开始发展的|A fledgling is a young bird that has recently acquired its feathers and is learning to fly.  It can also refer to something new and inexperienced.  |例句：The fledgling company struggled in its early years. 这家新公司在创业初期举步维艰。

例句：The fledgling bird took its first flight.  这只雏鸟进行了它的第一次飞行。


近义词：novice, beginner, tyro, budding


反义词：experienced, veteran, seasoned


词性：adj., n.
