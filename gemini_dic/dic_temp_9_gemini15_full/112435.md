lotto:/ˈlɒtoʊ/| n. |彩票；乐透彩票 |A lottery, especially one in which the winners are determined by the random selection of numbers.  It's a game of chance where players buy tickets with numbers, and if their numbers match the winning numbers drawn, they win a prize.  |例句：He bought a lotto ticket, hoping to win the jackpot. 他买了一张彩票，希望能赢得头奖。

例句：The lotto winnings were shared among the three winners. 彩票奖金由三位获奖者平分。


近义词：lottery, raffle |反义词：None |词性:名词
