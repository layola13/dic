hereafterward:/ˌhɛrˈæftərwərd/| adv.|今后，此后 |  This word is a less common variant of "hereafter," meaning "from this time on; in the future." It emphasizes the continuation of a state or action after a specific point in time.  It's largely archaic or literary. | Example:  Hereafterward, all meetings will be held in the new conference room.  今后，所有会议都将在新的会议室举行。|近义词：hereafter, henceforth, subsequently, later |反义词：beforehand, previously, earlier |副词
