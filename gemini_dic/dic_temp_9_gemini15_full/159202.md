poe:/poʊ/| n.|诗歌；诗作；诗意；诗人|Poetry, in its broadest sense, is a form of literature that uses aesthetic and rhythmic qualities of language—such asphonaesthetics, sound symbolism, and metre—to evoke meanings in addition to, or in place of, the prosaic ostensible meaning.|例句：He wrote a poem about love. 他写了一首关于爱情的诗。|例句：The poem is full of imagery and emotion. 这首诗充满了意象和情感。|近义词：verse, rhyme, poetry|反义词：prose

The poet writes a poem every week. = 这个诗人每周都写一首诗。
She composes beautiful poetry. = 她创作优美的诗歌。
