namaste:/ˈnɑːməsteɪ/| int.|你好；问候语；合十礼|A Hindu greeting meaning "I bow to you," expressing respect and reverence.  It is typically accompanied by the gesture of pressing the palms together in front of the chest.|例句：Namaste, it's a pleasure to meet you.  你好，很高兴见到你。|例句：She greeted everyone with a warm namaste. 她用温暖的合十礼向每个人打招呼。|近义词：hello, greetings, salaam|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I say namaste to my yoga instructor. = 我向我的瑜伽老师问好（合十礼）。
They often say namaste to each other. = 他们经常互相问好（合十礼）。

一般过去时:
I said namaste to the monk. = 我向那位僧侣问好（合十礼）。
She namaste'd her teacher. = 她向她的老师行合十礼。


现在完成时:
I have said namaste many times today. = 我今天已经多次行过合十礼了。
We have namaste'd to everyone in the class. = 我们已经向班里的每个人行过合十礼了。


过去完成时:
I had already said namaste before he arrived. = 在他到达之前我已经行过合十礼了。
They had namaste'd to the statue before leaving. = 他们离开之前已经向雕像行过合十礼了。


将来时:
I will say namaste when I see him. = 我见到他时会行合十礼。
We will namaste to the guru tomorrow. = 我们明天将向大师行合十礼。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would say namaste. = 如果我见到他，我会行合十礼。
I wish I had said namaste to her earlier. = 我真希望我早点向她行合十礼。


被动语态:
Namaste is often used in yoga classes. = 合十礼经常在瑜伽课上使用。
A namaste was given to the guest speaker. = 给嘉宾致以合十礼。


疑问句:
Did you say namaste? = 你行过合十礼了吗？
Have you ever said namaste before? = 你以前曾经行过合十礼吗？


否定句:
I didn't say namaste. = 我没有行合十礼。
She didn't namaste to anyone. = 她没有向任何人行合十礼。


PHRASE->
Namaste and good morning = 问好加早安
Namaste, my friend = 朋友你好
Namaste and welcome = 问好加欢迎
Namaste, I am glad to meet you = 问好加很高兴认识你
A warm namaste = 温暖的问好
