goodie:/ˈɡʊdi/| n.|好东西，美味佳肴|A goodie is something good to eat, especially a sweet treat.  It can also refer to something pleasant or desirable.|例句：The children were excited to receive their goodie bags after the party.  孩子们在派对结束后很高兴收到他们的礼品袋。

例句：She baked a batch of goodies for the bake sale. 她为义卖烘焙了一批美味佳肴。|近义词：treat, delicacy, sweet, reward|反义词：rubbish, trash, junk
