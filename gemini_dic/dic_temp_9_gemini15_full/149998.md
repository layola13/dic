/ˈpɑːlmɪstriːz/ | n. | 手相学 | The study of the lines on the palms of hands to supposedly determine character or predict the future.  |  She was fascinated by palmistry and spent hours studying the lines on her own hands. 她对算命很着迷，花了好几个小时研究自己手上的掌纹。| fortune-telling, chiromancy |  (No direct antonym, as it's not a descriptive term with a direct opposite.
