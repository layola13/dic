provent:/prɪˈvɛnt/| vt.|预防，阻止，妨碍|To prevent something from happening means to stop it from happening.|例句：We need to prevent accidents from happening.我们需要防止事故发生。

例句：Regular exercise can prevent heart disease. 定期锻炼可以预防心脏病。


近义词：avoid, hinder, avert, preclude|反义词：allow, permit, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prevents accidents by driving carefully. 他小心驾驶以防止事故发生。
The government prevents pollution through strict regulations. 政府通过严格的法规来防止污染。

一般过去时:
The rain prevented us from going to the park.  雨水阻止了我们去公园。
She prevented him from making a mistake. 她阻止了他犯错。

现在完成时:
The new law has prevented many crimes. 新法律已经阻止了许多犯罪。
His quick thinking has prevented a disaster. 他的快速思考避免了一场灾难。

过去完成时:
The heavy snow had prevented them from reaching their destination. 大雪阻止了他们到达目的地。
He had prevented the spread of the rumour by acting swiftly. 他迅速行动阻止了谣言的传播。

将来时:
The new security system will prevent unauthorized access. 新的安全系统将防止未经授权的访问。
We hope the vaccine will prevent the spread of the disease. 我们希望疫苗能够阻止疾病的传播。

虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have prevented the fire. 如果我们早点采取行动，就能阻止这场火灾。
I wish I had prevented him from leaving. 我真希望我阻止了他离开。

被动语态:
The spread of the disease was prevented by vaccination.  疾病的传播通过疫苗接种得到了阻止。
Access to the building is prevented by a security guard.  保安阻止了进入大楼。

疑问句:
What can we do to prevent further damage? 我们能做些什么来防止进一步的损失？
How can we prevent this from happening again? 我们如何才能防止这种情况再次发生？

否定句:
He didn't prevent the accident from happening. 他没有阻止事故的发生。
The measures didn't prevent the problem from worsening.  这些措施并没有阻止问题的恶化。


PHRASE->
prevent from:  阻止…
prevent...from happening: 阻止…发生
prevent the spread of: 防止…的传播
prevent accidents: 预防事故
prevent disease: 预防疾病
