cannibalic:/ˌkænɪˈbælɪk/| adj.|食人肉的；残忍的；破坏性的|Relating to or characteristic of cannibalism;  marked by the destruction of something to which one is connected or indebted.  It implies a destructive or self-destructive consumption or exploitation.|例句：The cannibalic tendencies of the tribe were well-documented.  该部落的食人习性有据可查。 The company's cannibalic marketing strategy, while initially successful, ultimately undermined its long-term growth. 该公司的食人营销策略虽然最初取得了成功，但最终损害了其长期增长。|近义词：anthropophagous, predatory, destructive, self-destructive|反义词：nourishing, beneficial, constructive

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's strategy is cannibalic. = 这家公司的策略具有破坏性。
His behavior is cannibalic towards his own success. = 他对自身成功的行为具有破坏性。


一般过去时:
Their actions were cannibalic to their own reputation. = 他们的行为损害了他们自己的声誉。
The marketing campaign was cannibalic to previous products. = 营销活动对之前的产品造成了损害。


现在完成时:
The project has been cannibalic to other initiatives. = 该项目已经对其他项目造成了损害。
The new product has cannibalized sales of the old one. = 新产品蚕食了旧产品的销售额。


过去完成时:
The old system had been cannibalized by the new one. = 旧系统已被新系统取代。
The previous strategy had become cannibalic to the company's growth. = 先前的策略已经损害了公司的增长。


将来时:
The new policy will be cannibalic to the existing market. = 新政策将蚕食现有市场。
This approach will be cannibalic to our own resources. = 这种方法将损害我们自己的资源。


虚拟语气:
If the company continued this strategy, it would be cannibalic. = 如果公司继续这一策略，它将具有破坏性。
If they had invested wisely, the project wouldn't have been so cannibalic. = 如果他们明智地投资，该项目就不会如此具有破坏性。


被动语态:
The market share was cannibalized by the competitor. = 市场份额被竞争对手蚕食。
The old product line was cannibalized by the new one. = 旧产品线被新产品线取代。


疑问句:
Is this marketing approach cannibalic? = 这种营销方法具有破坏性吗？
Are their actions cannibalic to their own goals? = 他们的行为会损害他们自己的目标吗？


否定句:
The strategy is not cannibalic; it's actually quite innovative. = 该策略并非具有破坏性；它实际上相当具有创新性。
Their actions were not cannibalic to the team's overall success. = 他们的行为并没有损害团队的整体成功。
