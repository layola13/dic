qualify:/ˈkwɑːlɪfaɪ/ | /ˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/ | vt. & vi. | 使合格；具有…的资格；证明…合格 | To fulfill the requirements or conditions for something; to meet a standard. |例句：He qualified as a doctor last year. 他去年获得了医生资格。

He didn't qualify for the competition because he was too young. 他因为年龄太小而没有资格参加比赛。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Does this product qualify for a discount? = 这产品符合打折资格吗？
How many people qualify for the program this year? = 今年有多少人符合该计划的资格？


一般过去时:
She qualified for the Olympics in 2016. = 她2016年获得了奥运会参赛资格。
The judge qualified the witness's testimony as unreliable. = 法官认定证人的证词不可靠。


现在完成时:
He has qualified for the finals. = 他已经获得了决赛资格。
Have you qualified for the scholarship yet? = 你已经获得奖学金资格了吗？


过去完成时:
She had already qualified for the next round before the last match. = 在最后一局比赛之前，她就已经获得了下一轮的资格。
They had qualified for the tournament by winning the regional playoffs. = 他们通过赢得地区季后赛获得了锦标赛的资格。


将来时:
Will she qualify for the next level? = 她能晋级下一轮吗？
I believe he will qualify for the position. = 我相信他会胜任这个职位。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have qualified for the team. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能进入球队了。
I wish I had qualified for the grant. = 我真希望我获得了那笔助学金。


被动语态:
The candidates were qualified by the committee. = 候选人由委员会认定合格。
He was qualified to give expert testimony. = 他有资格作专家证词。


疑问句:
Do you qualify for this job? = 你符合这份工作的资格吗？
How many applicants qualified for the interview? = 有多少申请者获得了面试资格？


否定句:
He didn't qualify for the loan. = 他没有资格获得贷款。
Their application didn't qualify under the new regulations. = 他们的申请不符合新的规定。
