interlaying: /ˌɪntərˈleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 交织；层叠；穿插 |  The act of weaving or placing things together in an interwoven or layered manner.  It implies a complex arrangement where elements are intertwined or superimposed. |


例句：

The artist created a stunning effect by interlaying different fabrics in her tapestry.  = 艺术家通过在她的挂毯中交织不同的织物创造出令人惊叹的效果。

The software interlays multiple audio tracks to create a rich soundscape. = 该软件将多个音频轨道层叠在一起以创建丰富的音景。


近义词：interweaving, intertwining, layering, overlapping

反义词：separating, disentangling, isolating


词性：动词
