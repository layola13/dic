replowed:/rɪˈplaʊd/| v.| 再耕,重新耕作 | To plow (land) again.  This typically involves plowing land that has already been plowed, often to prepare it for a new planting season or to improve soil conditions. | Example: The farmer replowed the field after the heavy rain to improve drainage.  农民在暴雨后重新耕作了田地以改善排水。The dry soil needed to be replowed before planting. 干燥的土壤在播种前需要重新耕作。|近义词：retill, cultivate again|反义词：leave fallow, harvest
