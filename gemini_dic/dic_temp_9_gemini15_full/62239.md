encrusted:/ɪnˈkrʌstɪd/| adj.| 附有硬壳的；结硬壳的；| Covered with a hard crust or layer of something.  The word implies a process of gradual accumulation, often resulting in a thick, hard coating.|例句：The old teapot was encrusted with years of tea stains.  古老的茶壶上沾满了多年的茶渍。|例句：The ancient ruins were encrusted with lichen.  古老的废墟上覆盖着地衣。|近义词：coated, covered, crusted,  incrusted|反义词：clean, bare, smooth
