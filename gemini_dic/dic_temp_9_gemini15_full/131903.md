noncontention:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛnʃən/| n. |无竞争；不争论；和谐|The absence of contention or competition; a state of agreement or harmony.  It often describes a situation where resources are readily available, or where there's a cooperative rather than competitive atmosphere.|Example: The surprisingly smooth rollout of the new software was due to the noncontention among the development teams.  令人惊讶的是，新软件的顺利推出是由于开发团队之间没有竞争。|近义词：harmony, agreement, cooperation, accord|反义词：contention, conflict, competition, strife|词性：名词
