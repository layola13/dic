prefaded: /ˌpriːˈfeɪdɪd/ | adj. | 褪色的，略微褪色的 | Slightly faded; showing signs of having faded.  The word combines "pre-" (before, beforehand) and "faded," suggesting a state of having faded somewhat before the current observation. | 例句：The prefaded jeans gave her a vintage look. = 这条略微褪色的牛仔裤让她看起来很有复古感。

The prefaded photograph was still recognizable. = 这张略微褪色的照片仍然可以辨认。


近义词：faded, pale, washed-out, bleached


反义词：vibrant, bright, vivid


词性: adjective
