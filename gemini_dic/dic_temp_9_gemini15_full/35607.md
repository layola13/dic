coast-is-clear:/koʊst ɪz klɪr/| phrase | 风平浪静；危机已过；万事大吉 |  The phrase means that a dangerous or difficult situation has passed, and it is now safe to proceed. It originates from the nautical context, where a clear coast meant that there were no obstacles or dangers visible, allowing for safe passage. |例句：The coast is clear, let's go. 风平浪静，我们走吧。


例句：We waited until the coast was clear before entering the building. 我们等到风平浪静之后才进入大楼。


近义词：safe, secure, all clear


反义词：dangerous, risky, perilous


词性: phrase
