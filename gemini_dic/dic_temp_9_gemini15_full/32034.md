cherishers:/ˈtʃerɪʃərz/| n.|珍爱者；珍惜的人|Those who cherish something hold it dear and protect it.  They value it highly and treat it with care and affection. Cherishers demonstrate love, appreciation, and a commitment to preserving what they hold dear. |例句：The cherishers of old traditions kept the ancient customs alive. 珍爱古老传统的人们使古老的习俗得以延续。  The museum is full of cherishers of art and history.  博物馆里充满了珍爱艺术和历史的人。|近义词：protectors, keepers, lovers, guardians|反义词：destroyers, neglecters, abusers|名词
