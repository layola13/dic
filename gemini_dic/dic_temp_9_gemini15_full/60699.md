electrization:/ˌɪˌlɛktrɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.| 电气化；充电；激奋| The process of charging an object with electricity, or of converting something to use electricity.  It can also refer to the act of thrilling or exciting someone. |例句：The electrization of the railway system improved transportation significantly.  铁路系统的电气化极大地改善了交通状况。

例句：Her electrifying speech left the audience breathless. 她激动人心的演讲让听众屏息凝神。

近义词： electrification, energization, excitation

反义词：de-electrification, de-energization
