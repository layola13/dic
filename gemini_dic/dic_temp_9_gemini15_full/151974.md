pastry:/ˈpeɪstri/| n. |糕点，面点| Baked goods made from dough or batter.  Pastries can include sweet items like cakes, cookies, and pies, as well as savory items like quiches and turnovers. |例句：The bakery offers a wide variety of pastries, from croissants to éclairs. 这家面包店提供各种各样的糕点，从羊角面包到泡芙。
例句：For dessert, we had a selection of delicious pastries. 甜点，我们吃了一些美味的糕点。|近义词：baked goods, confectionery|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with  savory dishes or meals. |名词
