howl:/haʊl/| v., n.| 嚎叫；怒吼 | to make a long, loud, and usually mournful cry, like a wolf or dog.  |例句：The wolves howled at the moon. 狼对着月亮嚎叫。|例句：The wind howled through the trees. 风在树林中呼啸。|近义词：wail, yell, scream |反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog howls every night. = 这条狗每晚都嚎叫。
The wind howls fiercely. = 风猛烈地呼啸。

一般过去时:
The baby howled when he fell down. = 宝宝摔倒后大声哭喊。
The siren howled in the distance. = 警笛在远处鸣叫。

现在完成时:
The dog has howled all night. = 这条狗整晚都在嚎叫。
The storm has howled for hours. = 暴风雨已经持续了好几个小时。

过去完成时:
The wolf had howled before the hunter arrived. = 在猎人到达之前，狼已经嚎叫过了。
The wind had howled all night, making it difficult to sleep. = 风整夜呼啸，让人难以入睡。

将来时:
The baby will howl if he is hungry. = 如果宝宝饿了，他会哭喊。
The storm will howl throughout the night. = 暴风雨将持续整夜呼啸。

虚拟语气:
If a wolf howled nearby, I would be scared. = 如果附近有狼嚎叫，我会害怕。
I wish the wind wouldn't howl so loudly. = 我希望风不要呼啸得那么大声。


被动语态:
A mournful howl was heard in the night. = 夜晚听到一声凄凉的嚎叫。
The song was described as a howling wind.  = 这首歌被形容为呼啸的风。


疑问句:
Does the dog howl often? = 这条狗经常嚎叫吗？
Why is the wind howling so loud? = 为什么风呼啸得这么大声？

否定句:
The dog didn't howl last night. = 这条狗昨晚没有嚎叫。
The wind wasn't howling when I left. = 我离开的时候风没有呼啸。


PHRASE->
