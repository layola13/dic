一般现在时:
The foliage provides shade in the summer. = 枝叶在夏天提供阴凉。
The leaves of the tree are changing color. = 树叶正在变色。


一般过去时:
The foliage was thick and green last spring. = 去年春天树叶茂密翠绿。
The leaves fell from the trees in autumn. = 秋天树叶从树上掉落。


现在完成时:
The foliage has grown significantly this year. = 今年的树叶长得相当茂盛。
