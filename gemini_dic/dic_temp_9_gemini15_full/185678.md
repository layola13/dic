revering:/rɪˈvɪərɪŋ/ | v. | 尊敬；崇敬 | Showing deep respect and admiration for someone or something.  |例句：Many people revere the Dalai Lama for his wisdom and compassion. 许多人尊敬达赖喇嘛的智慧和慈悲。|例句：She revered her grandmother for her strength and resilience. 她敬仰她祖母的坚强和韧性。|近义词：respecting, venerating, honouring, idolizing |反义词：despising, scorning, contemning |动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They revere their ancestors. = 他们尊敬他们的祖先。
We revere the memory of those who fought for freedom. = 我们敬仰为自由而战的人们的记忆。

一般过去时:
The ancient Romans revered their emperors. = 古代罗马人崇敬他们的皇帝。
She revered her teacher for his profound knowledge. = 她敬仰她老师渊博的知识。

现在完成时:
People have revered this mountain for centuries. = 人们几个世纪以来一直敬畏这座山。
We have revered his contributions to science. = 我们一直敬仰他对科学的贡献。

过去完成时:
They had revered the king before the revolution. = 革命前他们一直敬仰国王。
The villagers had revered the old tree as sacred. = 村民们一直将那棵老树视为神圣。

将来时:
Future generations will revere her achievements. = 后代将敬仰她的成就。
They will revere him as a national hero. = 他们将把他尊为民族英雄。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would revere my elders. = 如果我是你，我会尊敬我的长辈。
I wish I could revere such a talented artist. = 我真希望我能敬仰这样一位有才华的艺术家。

被动语态:
His work is revered by many scholars. = 他的作品受到许多学者的尊敬。
Her paintings are revered throughout the world. = 她的画作在全世界受到崇敬。

疑问句:
Do you revere anyone in particular? = 你特别尊敬谁吗？
Who do they revere as their leader? = 他们尊敬谁作为他们的领导者？

否定句:
They do not revere violence. = 他们不崇尚暴力。
I did not revere his methods, but I respected his results. = 我不崇敬他的方法，但我尊重他的成果。


PHRASE->
to be revered as = 被尊为...
deeply revered = 深受尊敬的
widely revered = 广受尊敬的
to hold someone in reverence = 非常尊敬某人
to show reverence to = 向...表示敬意
