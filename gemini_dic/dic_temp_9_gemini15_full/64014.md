epitapher:/ɪˈpɪtəfər/ | n. |墓志铭撰写者；墓志铭作者 | A person who writes epitaphs.  Epitaphers craft concise and often poetic inscriptions for gravestones, commemorating the deceased.  |Example:  The renowned poet was also a skilled epitapher, his words capturing the essence of each life he memorialized.  墓志铭撰写者是一位著名的诗人，他用文字捕捉了他所纪念的每一种人生的精髓。 |近义词: inscription writer, memorial writer |反义词: N/A
