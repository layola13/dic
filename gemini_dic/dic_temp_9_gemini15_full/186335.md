rhubarb:/ˈruːbɑːrb/|/ˈruːbɑːr/| n. |大黄|A plant with thick stalks that are used in cooking, typically in pies and crumbles, and often with added sugar to offset its tartness.  The stalks are usually red or reddish-pink. |例句：I made a delicious rhubarb pie for dessert.  我做了一个美味的大黄派作为甜点。|例句：The rhubarb crumble was slightly tart, but very tasty.  大黄碎饼干有点酸，但很好吃。|近义词： (no perfect synonym, but depending on context): sorrel, gooseberry|反义词： (no direct antonym)|词性:名词
