hurdled:/ˈhɜːrld/| /ˈhɜːr.dəld/| v.|跨越（障碍）；克服（困难）|To jump over a hurdle; to overcome a difficulty or obstacle.|例句：He hurdled the fence with ease. 他轻松地跨过了栅栏。|例句：The company hurdled a major financial crisis.  这家公司克服了一场重大的金融危机。|近义词：leap over, overcome, surmount|反义词：fail, stumble


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hurdles the fence every day. = 他每天都跨过栅栏。
She hurdles obstacles in her career. = 她克服事业上的障碍。

一般过去时:

They hurdled the many challenges they faced. = 他们克服了他们面临的许多挑战。
