ill-treatments: /ˌɪlˈtriːtmənts/ | n. |虐待；恶劣对待 |  The cruel and unfair treatment of someone.  It refers to actions that cause physical or emotional harm, neglect, or exploitation. |例句：The report detailed the horrific ill-treatments suffered by the prisoners.  报告详细描述了囚犯遭受的可怕虐待。

The ill-treatments of animals in the factory caused public outrage.  工厂虐待动物的行为引起了公众的愤怒。|近义词：abuse, maltreatment, mistreatment, cruelty, oppression |反义词：kindness, compassion, care, good treatment
