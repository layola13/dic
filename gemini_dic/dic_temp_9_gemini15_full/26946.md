calcarine:/ˈkælkəraɪn/| n.|距状沟的；距状沟的皮质|The calcarine sulcus is a prominent landmark in the occipital lobe of the brain, marking the border between the visual cortex areas V1 and V2. It is named for its resemblance to a spur (calcar) in shape.|The calcarine sulcus is involved in visual processing. Damage to this area can cause visual field defects.|近义词:visual cortex, striate cortex|反义词:N/A
