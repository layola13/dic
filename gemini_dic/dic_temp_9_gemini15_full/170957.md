pseudopatriotically:/ˌsuːdəʊˈpætriɒtɪkli/| adj. |伪爱国地，假爱国地 | Displaying or characterized by a pretended or insincere love of one's country.  It suggests actions or expressions of patriotism that are superficial, self-serving, or intended to manipulate public opinion rather than stemming from genuine national pride. |例句：The politician spoke pseudopatriotically, using nationalistic rhetoric to distract from his corrupt dealings.  这位政客伪爱国地发言，用民族主义言论来转移人们对他腐败行为的注意力。


The candidate's speech was filled with pseudopatriotic slogans designed to appeal to the masses.  这位候选人的演讲充满了旨在迎合大众的伪爱国口号。


近义词：jingoistically, chauvinistically, nationalistically (but with a stronger emphasis on insincerity) |反义词：patriotically (genuinely), sincerely


词源:

* **pseudo-**:  From Greek *pseudo-* meaning "false," "sham," or "pretended."
* **patriotic**: From  *patriot* (from French *patriote*, from Latin *patriota*, from Greek *patriothēs*, from *patris* "fatherland").  *Patriotic* means "of, relating to, or characteristic of a patriot; inspired by love of one's country."
* **-ally**:  Adverbial suffix indicating "in a ... manner."
