freebooter:/ˈfriːˌbuːtər/| n.| 海盗；掠夺者 | A freebooter is a pirate or plunderer, especially one who operates without official sanction. They are often associated with raiding coastal areas and engaging in acts of piracy for personal gain.  | The freebooter plundered the coastal villages, stealing valuables and supplies.  这个海盗洗劫了沿海村庄，偷走了贵重物品和补给。 | pirate, privateer, buccaneer, raider | law-abiding citizen, peaceful inhabitant
