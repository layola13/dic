postulant:/ˈpɒstʃələnt/| n. |申请者；候选人；修道院见习修士|A postulant is a person who is applying to join a religious order, especially a monastic order, and is undergoing a period of probation before taking vows.  It can also refer to an applicant for any position or privilege. |Example:  She is a postulant at the convent, hoping to become a nun. 她是一位修道院的申请者，希望成为一名修女。


Example: He is a postulant for the prestigious scholarship. 他是申请这份有声望的奖学金的候选人。|近义词：applicant, candidate, applicant, seeker|反义词：member (in the context of a religious order)
