lignites:/ˈlɪɡnaɪts/| n.|褐煤|A type of coal that is relatively low in carbon content and high in moisture. It is the lowest rank of coal and is considered a relatively young fossil fuel.|Example: Lignite is often used in power plants due to its abundance and low cost. 褐煤因其丰富和低廉的成本，常用于发电厂。|近义词：brown coal|反义词：anthracite
