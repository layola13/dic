reinvested:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | v. | 再投资；重新投资 | To invest (money) again, typically the profit or returns from a previous investment.  |例句：The company reinvested its profits into research and development.  公司将其利润再投资于研发。


例句：They reinvested their earnings in new equipment. 他们将收益再投资于新设备。


近义词：reapplied, reused, repurposed | 反义词：withdrew, liquidated

一般现在时:
The company reinvests its profits annually. = 公司每年将利润再投资。
He reinvests his earnings in the stock market. = 他将自己的收入再投资于股市。

一般过去时:
She reinvested her bonus in a new property. = 她将奖金再投资于一处新房产。
They reinvested the proceeds from the sale of their business. = 他们将出售业务的收益再投资。

现在完成时:
We have reinvested all our savings in the family business. = 我们已将所有积蓄再投资于家族企业。
The fund has reinvested its capital in high-growth stocks. = 该基金已将其资本再投资于高增长股票。

过去完成时:
They had reinvested their profits before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经将利润再投资了。
She had reinvested her inheritance in various ventures. = 她已将其继承的遗产再投资于各种事业。

将来时:
He will reinvest his winnings from the lottery. = 他将把彩票奖金再投资。
The government plans to reinvest tax revenue in infrastructure projects. = 政府计划将税收收入再投资于基础设施项目。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would reinvest it in my business. = 如果我有更多钱，我会将它再投资于我的生意。
If they had reinvested their earnings wisely, they would be richer now. = 如果他们明智地将收益再投资，现在就会更富有。

被动语态:
The profits were reinvested in new equipment. = 利润被再投资于新设备。
The funds are being reinvested in sustainable energy projects. = 资金正在被再投资于可持续能源项目。

疑问句:
Will the company reinvest its profits this year? = 公司今年会将利润再投资吗？
Have they reinvested the money they received? = 他们已经将收到的钱再投资了吗？

否定句:
They did not reinvest their earnings. = 他们没有将收益再投资。
The company chose not to reinvest its profits this quarter. = 公司选择在本季度不将利润再投资。
