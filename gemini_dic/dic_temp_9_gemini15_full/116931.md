mayweed:/ˈmeɪwiːd/| n. |野菊,母菊|A common weed, usually with white or yellow flowers, belonging to the daisy family.  It is often considered a nuisance in gardens and lawns. |The mayweed was spreading rapidly across the neglected lawn.|近义词：chamomile (some species), dog fennel|反义词: none.
