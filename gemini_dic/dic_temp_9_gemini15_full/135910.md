nonpublic: /ˌnɒnˈpʌblɪk/ | adj. | 不公开的；非公开的 | Not open to or known by the general public.  It refers to something that is private, confidential, or restricted.  | 例句：The company's nonpublic financial data is confidential. 这家公司的非公开财务数据是保密的。

The nonpublic meeting was attended only by board members. 只有董事会成员参加了非公开会议。


近义词：private, confidential, secret, restricted, undisclosed | 反义词：public, open, accessible


词性: adjective (形容词)

词源:  The word "nonpublic" is a compound word. It combines the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "public" (meaning "of or concerning the people as a whole; open to or shared by all").  The root of "public" traces back to the Latin word "publicus," meaning "of the people."
