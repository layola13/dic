forker:/ˈfɔːrkər/| n. |叉子；分叉器；（程序员）分支者| A forker is a tool or device that creates forks or branches; in programming, it refers to a person who creates a fork of a software project.  It can also refer to a person who uses a fork (eating utensil). | Example: He used a forker to eat his pasta. 他用叉子吃意大利面。  The programmer is a prolific forker, contributing to many open-source projects.  这位程序员是一位多产的分支者，为许多开源项目做出了贡献。|近义词：brancher, divider (in some contexts)|反义词：joiner, merger (in some contexts)
