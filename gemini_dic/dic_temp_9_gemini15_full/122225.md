miswiring:/ˈmɪsˌwaɪərɪŋ/| n.|错误布线；接线错误|The incorrect or faulty connection of wires or circuits.  This can lead to malfunctions or failures in electrical systems, computer networks, or other interconnected components.|例句：The miswiring of the electrical system caused a fire. = 电路系统的错误布线导致了火灾。

The miswiring in the computer network resulted in data loss. = 计算机网络的错误布线导致了数据丢失。|近义词：faulty wiring, incorrect wiring, bad connection|反义词：correct wiring, proper wiring|名词
