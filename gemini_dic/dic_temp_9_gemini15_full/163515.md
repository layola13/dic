Events, preparations, and anticipation leading up to the coronation of a monarch.  |Example: The precoronation celebrations included a grand parade through the city.  加冕前的庆祝活动包括一场盛大的城市游行。|近义词：antecedent, prelude, preliminary|反义词：postcoronation|名词
