saucy: /ˈsɔːsi/ | adj. | 冒失的；厚颜无耻的；活泼的；调皮的；(食物)加有调味酱的 |

英文解析:  Saucy can describe someone who is bold, impudent, or cheeky, often in a playful or charming way. It can also refer to food that has been seasoned with a sauce, making it flavorful and appetizing.


例句:

She gave him a saucy wink.  = 她对他俏皮地眨了眨眼。

The saucy child stuck his tongue out at the teacher. = 那个调皮的孩子朝老师吐舌头。


近义词: cheeky, impudent, impertinent, bold, sassy, piquant (for food)


反义词: timid, shy, demure, insipid (for food)


词性: adjective
