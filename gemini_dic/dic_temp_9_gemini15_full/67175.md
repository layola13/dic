exposure:/ɪkˈspoʊʒər/| n. |曝光；暴露；揭露；接触 |The act of exposing something or someone to something else; the state of being exposed.  It can refer to physical exposure to elements, vulnerability to risk, unveiling of secrets, or experience gained from something. |例句：The photographer’s work shows a remarkable exposure of human suffering.  摄影师的作品令人惊叹地揭露了人类的苦难。

例句：  Prolonged exposure to the sun can cause skin cancer. 长时间暴晒会导致皮肤癌。


近义词： revelation, unveiling, disclosure, contact, experience |反义词： concealment, protection, shielding |词性：名词
