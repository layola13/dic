inro:/ˈɪnroʊ/| n. |入门，引论，序言 |A brief introductory section or passage, especially at the beginning of a book or other work.|例句：The book's inro captivated the reader from the start. 这本书的引言从一开始就吸引了读者。|近义词：introduction, preface, prologue, prelude|反义词：conclusion, epilogue
