skeg:/skeɡ/| n.|龙骨；尾舵；突出物|A skeg is a small fin or projection on the bottom of a boat or aircraft, typically near the stern or tail.  It provides stability and sometimes steering. In woodworking, a skeg refers to a projecting piece of wood.|例句：The boat's skeg helped to keep it stable in rough seas. 这艘船的龙骨帮助它在汹涌的海面上保持稳定。The aircraft's skeg aided in directional control during landing.  飞机的尾舵在着陆时辅助方向控制。|近义词：fin, keel, rudder (in some contexts)|反义词：None directly applicable.
