nonfallacious: /ˌnɒnfəˈleɪʃəs/| adj. |非谬误的，正确的，合理的|Free from fallacy; sound; valid.  It refers to reasoning or arguments that are not flawed or deceptive.  The opposite of fallacious. |例句：His argument, while unconventional, was ultimately nonfallacious. 他的论点虽然非常规，但最终是正确的。

例句：The judge's decision, based on solid evidence, was deemed nonfallacious.  法官的判决基于确凿的证据，被认为是合理的。


近义词：sound, valid, logical, rational, justifiable, coherent


反义词：fallacious, flawed, illogical, irrational, unsound, incoherent
