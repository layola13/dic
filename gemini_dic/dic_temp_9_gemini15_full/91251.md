hydroelectricity:/ˌhaɪdroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsəti/| n. |水力发电|Electricity generated by using the power of water, typically by using a dam to create a reservoir of water that is then used to turn turbines.  Hydroelectricity is a clean and renewable source of energy. |Example:  Many countries rely on hydroelectricity for a significant portion of their energy needs.  许多国家依靠水力发电来满足其大部分能源需求。  The hydroelectric dam provides power to the entire region.  这个水电大坝为整个地区提供电力。|近义词：water power|反义词：fossil fuel power
