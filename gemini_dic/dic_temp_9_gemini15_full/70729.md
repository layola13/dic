findable: /ˈfaɪndəbl/ | adj. | 可找到的，可获得的 |  Easily found or obtained. Something that is findable is easy to locate or discover. |

例句：

The lost keys were surprisingly findable under the sofa.  丢失的钥匙出人意料地在沙发底下找到了。

The information you need is easily findable online. 你需要的信息很容易在网上找到。


近义词：discoverable, accessible, retrievable, obtainable

反义词：unfindable, inaccessible, elusive, lost


词源:

The word "findable" is derived from the verb "find," meaning to discover something unexpectedly or after searching.  The suffix "-able" is added to create an adjective, indicating something that can be found. The word's history is directly linked to the Old English verb "findan," meaning "to find."
