colored:/ˈkʌləd/ /ˈkʌlɚd/| adj.|有色的，着色的；肤色较深的|Having color; possessing a hue or shade.  The term can be considered outdated and potentially offensive when referring to people of color, as it is associated with historical racial discrimination.  More appropriate terms include "people of color" or specific ethnicities.|例句：The colored pencils are vibrant and bright. 彩色铅笔鲜艳明亮。

The walls were painted in various colored shades. 墙壁被漆成各种颜色。|近义词：pigmented, tinted, hued, diverse (when referring to people)|反义词：colorless, uncolored, monochrome, white (in some contexts)|词性:形容词
