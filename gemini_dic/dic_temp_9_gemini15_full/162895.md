precentennial: /ˌpriːsɛnˈtɛniəl/ | adj. | 百年纪念前的；接近百年的 | Relating to the period of time immediately preceding a centennial celebration or anniversary.  It often implies the anticipation or build-up to a major anniversary. |例句：The precentennial events for the city's founding included a historical exhibit and a series of lectures.  这座城市建城前的百年纪念活动包括历史展览和一系列讲座。


The precentennial preparations for the school's 100th anniversary were already underway. 学校百年校庆的筹备工作已经开始。


近义词：antecentennial (less common) | 反义词：postcentennial | 词性：形容词
