reassures:/ˌriːəˈʃʊrz/ | vt. |使安心；使确信；使放心 | To restore confidence or courage to; to comfort.  |例句：The doctor's words reassured her about her health. 医生的话让她对自己的健康放心了。|例句：His calm demeanor reassured the frightened children. 他镇定的举止使害怕的孩子们安心了。|近义词：comfort, console, soothe, alleviate |反义词：alarm, frighten, terrify, unsettle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reassures his clients daily. = 他每天都让他的客户安心。
The teacher reassures the nervous students. = 老师让紧张的学生们安心。

一般过去时:
The news reassured me greatly. = 这个消息让我非常安心。
She reassured him that everything would be alright. = 她向他保证一切都会好起来的。

现在完成时:
This information has reassured me. = 这信息让我安心了。
The doctor's explanation has reassured the parents. = 医生的解释让父母们安心了。

过去完成时:
Having reassured herself, she proceeded with the plan. = 在使自己安心后，她继续执行计划。
He had reassured her many times before. = 他之前已经多次让她安心了。

将来时:
I will reassure you that everything will be fine. = 我会让你放心，一切都会好起来的。
The manager will reassure the investors about the company's future. = 经理会让投资者对公司的未来放心。

虚拟语气:
If I could reassure you, I would. = 如果我能让你安心，我会的。
I wish I could reassure her about the situation. = 我希望我能让她对这种情况安心。

被动语态:
She was reassured by his kind words. = 她被他友善的话语所安慰。
The public was reassured by the government's response. = 公众对政府的回应感到放心。

疑问句:
Does this information reassure you? = 这信息让你安心吗？
Will his promise reassure the stakeholders? = 他的承诺会让利益相关者安心吗？

否定句:
Nothing could reassure her about her son's safety. = 没有什么能让她安心儿子的安全。
His words did not reassure the anxious crowd. = 他的话语并没有让焦虑的人群安心。


PHRASE->
reassure oneself = 使自己安心
reassure somebody about something = 使某人对某事安心
reassure somebody that = 向某人保证
fully reassure = 完全使安心
partially reassure = 部分使安心


词源:
re- (again) + assure (to make certain; to guarantee)  来自古法语 *aseurer,  源自拉丁语 *ad- + *secūrus (safe, secure).
