prebestows:/ˌpriːbɪˈstoʊz/| v.|预先给予；提前授予|To grant or bestow something in advance or beforehand.|The king prebestowed the land upon his loyal knight before his death. 国王在死前预先将土地赐给了他忠诚的骑士。|近义词：grant in advance, give beforehand, bestow beforehand|反义词：withhold, retain
