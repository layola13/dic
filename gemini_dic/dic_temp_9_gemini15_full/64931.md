estate:/ɪˈsteɪt/| n.|地产，财产，庄园，身份，状态|A large area of land in the country that is owned by one person or family.  It often includes a large house.|例句：He inherited a large estate from his uncle. 他从叔叔那里继承了一大笔遗产。|例句：The estate agent showed us several properties.  房产经纪人给我们看了几处房产。|近义词：property, possession, holding|反义词：nothingness, poverty
