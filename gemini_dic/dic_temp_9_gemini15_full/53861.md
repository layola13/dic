discontinuation:/ˌdɪsˌkɒntɪnjuˈeɪʃən/ | n. |停止；中断；废止 |The act of discontinuing something; the state of being discontinued.  It refers to the ceasing of something, such as a product, service, or practice. |例句：The discontinuation of the bus route caused great inconvenience to the residents.  公交线路的停运给居民带来了极大的不便。

例句：The company announced the discontinuation of its flagship product due to declining sales.  由于销量下降，该公司宣布停止生产其旗舰产品。


近义词：cessation, termination, discontinuance, suspension

反义词：continuation, resumption, commencement
