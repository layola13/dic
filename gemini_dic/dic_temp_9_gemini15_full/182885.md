repercussion:/ˌriːpərˈkʌʃn/| n. |反响，影响，恶果 |The consequences, especially bad ones, of an action or event.|The repercussions of the scandal were far-reaching.  这场丑闻的影响深远。|The government’s decision had unforeseen repercussions. 政府的决定产生了无法预料的影响。|近义词：consequence, effect, ramification, outcome |反义词：cause, origin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The decision has repercussions for the entire company. = 这个决定对整个公司都有影响。
His actions have serious repercussions. = 他的行为造成了严重的后果。


一般过去时:
The earthquake had devastating repercussions. = 地震造成了毁灭性的后果。
The scandal had significant repercussions for his career. = 这场丑闻严重影响了他的事业。


现在完成时:
The reforms have had unforeseen repercussions. = 改革产生了意想不到的影响。
The policy changes have had positive repercussions. = 政策变化产生了积极的影响。


过去完成时:
They hadn't anticipated the repercussions of their actions. = 他们没有预料到自己行为的后果。
It turned out that the repercussions of the decision had been underestimated. = 事实证明，对该决定后果的估计不足。


将来时:
The new law will have significant repercussions for small businesses. = 新法律将对小型企业产生重大影响。
We must consider the potential repercussions before acting. = 我们必须在采取行动之前考虑潜在的影响。


虚拟语气:
If we had foreseen the repercussions, we would have acted differently. = 如果我们预见了后果，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish we hadn't underestimated the repercussions of our decision. = 我真希望我们没有低估我们决定的后果。


被动语态:
The repercussions of the crisis were felt worldwide. = 危机的后果波及全球。
The potential repercussions are being carefully assessed. = 正在仔细评估潜在的影响。


疑问句:
What were the repercussions of the war? = 战争的后果是什么？
What repercussions might this decision have? = 这个决定可能带来什么后果？


否定句:
There were no negative repercussions from the changes. = 这些变化没有带来负面影响。
The project had no significant repercussions. = 该项目没有产生重大影响。


PHRASE->
negative repercussions = 负面影响

unintended repercussions = 不经意的后果
immediate repercussions = 立即的影响
serious repercussions = 严重的影响
