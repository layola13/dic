cantrip:/ˈkæn.trɪp/| n. |小把戏；戏法；咒语 |A cantrip is a minor magic spell, often playful or mischievous, used in games, stories, or fantasy settings. It's typically not very powerful or dangerous.  |例句：The wizard cast a simple cantrip to light the way.  这位巫师施了一个简单的咒语来照亮道路。


The mischievous fairy used a cantrip to turn the prince's shoes into frogs.  调皮的小仙子用了一个小咒语把王子的鞋子变成了青蛙。|近义词：trick, spell, charm |反义词：powerful magic, serious spell
