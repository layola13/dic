/mɛsɪdʒɪz/| n. |信息，消息，讯息 |Messages are plural of message, referring to multiple pieces of information or communication.  They can be sent through various mediums, such as text, email, or phone.| Examples: I received several messages on my phone. 我收到了几条手机短信。 He left a series of messages on my answering machine. 他在我的答录机上留了一连串的留言。|news, notices, communications|silence, absence of communication|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The system sends many messages every day. = 系统每天发送许多信息。
Messages arrive constantly throughout the day. = 信息全天都在不断到达。


一般过去时:
I read all the messages yesterday. = 我昨天读了所有的信息。
She sent three messages before lunch. = 她午饭前发了三条信息。


现在完成时:
I have received several important messages. = 我已经收到几条重要的信息。
He has sent many messages but hasn't received a reply. = 他发了好多信息，但没收到回复。


过去完成时:
They had already read the messages before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经读了这些信息。
I had sent the messages before realizing my mistake. = 我在意识到我的错误之前就已经发送了信息。


将来时:
I will send you a message later. = 我稍后会给你发信息。
They will receive the messages by tomorrow. = 他们明天会收到这些信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had seen the messages, I would have replied. = 如果我看到了信息，我就回复了。
I wish I had received the messages earlier. = 我真希望我早点收到这些信息。


被动语态:
The messages were delivered promptly. = 信息被迅速送达。
All messages are being processed. = 所有信息正在处理中。


疑问句:
Are there any new messages? = 有什么新信息吗？
What messages did you receive? = 你收到了什么信息？


否定句:
I haven't received any messages today. = 我今天没有收到任何信息。
He didn't send any messages yesterday. = 他昨天没有发任何信息。
