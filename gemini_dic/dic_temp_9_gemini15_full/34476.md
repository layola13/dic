clay:/kleɪ/| n.|黏土，泥土|A natural earthy material that is plastic when moist but hard when fired, consisting essentially of hydrated aluminum silicates.|The sculptor molded the clay into a beautiful vase.  雕塑家把黏土塑造成一个美丽的瓶子。|The potter's hands worked the clay with practiced ease. 陶工的手熟练地揉捏着黏土。| 近义词：mud, loam, earth | 反义词：stone, rock
