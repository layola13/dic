cabernets:/ˌkæbərˈneɪz/| n. |赤霞珠葡萄酒（复数）|Cabernets are a type of red wine made from Cabernet Sauvignon grapes.  They are known for their full-bodied flavor, often with notes of black currant, cedar, and vanilla.| 例句：The restaurant boasts an extensive wine list, featuring several excellent cabernets. 这家餐厅拥有丰富的酒单，其中包括几种极好的赤霞珠葡萄酒。


The cabernets we tasted at the vineyard were exceptional. 我们在葡萄园里品尝的赤霞珠葡萄酒非常棒。|近义词：red wines, Bordeaux wines|反义词：white wines
