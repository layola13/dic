pseudomalaria:/ˌsuːdəʊməˈleəriə/| n. |假疟疾|A condition that mimics the symptoms of malaria but is not caused by the malaria parasite.  It is often caused by other infections, such as influenza or other viral or bacterial illnesses. |Example:  The patient presented with symptoms resembling malaria, but further tests ruled out the parasite, diagnosing it as pseudomalaria.  病人表现出类似疟疾的症状，但进一步的测试排除了寄生虫，诊断为假疟疾。|近义词：malarialike illness,  simulated malaria |反义词：malaria
