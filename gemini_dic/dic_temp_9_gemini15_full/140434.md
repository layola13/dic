okey-doke:/ˈoʊkiːˈdoʊki/ | int. | 好吧，行，没问题 | An informal affirmative expression indicating agreement or acceptance. It's a playful and slightly emphatic way of saying "okay."  | Example: "Okey-doke, let's get started." = 好吧，咱们开始吧。 |近义词：okay, alright, all right, sure, yep | 反义词：no, nope


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Okey-doke is not typically used in phrases.  It stands alone as an informal affirmation.
