inventoried: /ˈɪnvəntɔːrid/ | v. | 盘点；入库登记 | To have made a detailed list of items, typically for inventory purposes.  | The warehouse manager inventoried all the new shipments.  仓库管理员对所有新到的货物进行了盘点。 | listed, cataloged, recorded |  uninventoried |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team inventories the supplies every week. = 团队每周都会盘点物资。
We inventory the stock at the end of each month. = 我们每个月底都会盘点库存。


一般过去时:
They inventoried the entire museum collection. = 他们清点过整个博物馆的藏品。
The accountant inventoried the company's assets. = 会计盘点了公司的资产。


现在完成时:
We have inventoried all the damaged goods. = 我们已经清点过所有损坏的货物了。
She has inventoried the library's books several times. = 她已经多次清点过图书馆的书籍了。


过去完成时:
They had inventoried the warehouse before the audit. = 审计之前，他们已经对仓库进行了盘点。
The team had inventoried the equipment before the move. = 搬迁前，团队已经清点过设备了。


将来时:
We will inventory the new arrivals tomorrow. = 我们明天将盘点新到的货物。
They will inventory the store's stock next week. = 他们下周将盘点商店的库存。


虚拟语气:
If we had inventoried the stock earlier, we would have noticed the shortage. = 如果我们早点盘点库存，就会发现短缺了。
It would be helpful if they inventoried the supplies regularly. = 如果他们定期盘点物资，那就有帮助了。


被动语态:
The entire collection was inventoried last year. = 整个藏品去年都已盘点过了。
The warehouse is inventoried monthly. = 仓库每月都会进行盘点。


疑问句:
Has the shipment been inventoried yet? = 货物已经盘点了吗？
When will the inventory be inventoried? = 什么时候盘点库存？


否定句:
They did not inventory the equipment properly. = 他们没有正确地盘点设备。
The warehouse has not been inventoried this month. = 这个月还没有对仓库进行盘点。
