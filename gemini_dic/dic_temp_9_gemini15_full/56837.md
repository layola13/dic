dorothy:/ˈdɔːrəθi/|n.|多萝西|A female given name of Greek origin, meaning "gift of God".  It's a popular name, particularly associated with the character Dorothy Gale in L. Frank Baum's "The Wizard of Oz".|例句：My aunt's name is Dorothy.  我的阿姨名叫多萝西。|例句：Dorothy is a classic and timeless name. 多萝西是一个经典而永恒的名字。|近义词：Doris, Thea|反义词：无
