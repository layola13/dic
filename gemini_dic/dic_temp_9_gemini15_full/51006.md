desistive: /dɪˈsɪstɪv/ | adj. | 制止的；遏制的；停止的 |  Relating to or tending to cause cessation or stoppage; inhibitory.  The word often describes something that stops or prevents an action or process.  |


例句：

The desistive measures taken by the government had a noticeable impact on inflation. = 政府采取的遏制措施对通货膨胀产生了显著的影响。

His desistive attitude towards the project ultimately led to its failure. = 他对该项目消极怠工的态度最终导致了它的失败。


近义词：inhibitory, suppressive, deterrent, restrictive

反义词：stimulative, encouraging, promoting

词性：形容词
