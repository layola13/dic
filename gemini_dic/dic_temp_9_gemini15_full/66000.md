examination:/ɪɡˌzæməˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 检查；考试；审查 |  The process of inspecting or testing something systematically.  It can refer to a formal test of knowledge or skills, a medical or physical check-up, or a detailed review of something. |例句：The doctor conducted a thorough examination.医生进行了彻底的检查。

例句：The final examination is next week.期末考试在下周。


近义词：inspection, test, scrutiny, review | 反义词：neglect, oversight


USAGE->

The doctor examines the patient carefully. = 医生仔细检查病人。
We examine the data before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前检查数据。


一般过去时:
