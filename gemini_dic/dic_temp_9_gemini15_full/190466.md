sassenachs:/ˈsæsənæks/| n. |低地人；外地人；（苏格兰盖尔语对低地苏格兰人的蔑称）|A derogatory term used by Scottish Gaels to refer to Lowlanders, particularly those of English descent.  It implies a lack of understanding of Gaelic culture and traditions. The term carries a historical weight, reflecting centuries of tension between the Highlands and Lowlands of Scotland.|The sassenachs built a new road through the glen, disregarding the ancient paths.|近义词：Lowlander, outsider, foreigner|反义词：Gael, Highlander
