rehaul:/ˌriːˈhɔːl/| vt.|彻底检修；彻底改革；大修|To repair or renovate something thoroughly; to reorganize or revamp something completely.|例句：The old engine needed a complete rehaul. 这台旧发动机需要彻底检修。|例句：The company underwent a major rehaul to improve efficiency.  这家公司进行了大规模的改革以提高效率。|近义词：overhaul, renovate, revamp, reorganize|反义词：neglect, deteriorate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rehaul the engine every year. = 他们每年都对发动机进行检修。
The government rehauls its policies regularly. = 政府定期调整其政策。

一般过去时:
We rehauled the old house last summer. = 我们去年夏天彻底翻修了旧房子。
The team rehauled their strategy after the loss. = 比赛失利后，球队调整了他们的策略。

现在完成时:
They have rehauled the entire system. = 他们已经彻底改造了整个系统。
The company has rehauled its marketing campaign. = 公司已经彻底改进了其营销活动。

过去完成时:
They had rehauled the car before selling it. = 他们在卖车之前已经修理好了车。
The government had rehauled the education system before the new school year. = 政府在新学年之前已经改革了教育体系。

将来时:
We will rehaul the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将彻底翻修厨房。
The company will rehaul its product line next year. = 公司明年将彻底改头换面其产品线。

虚拟语气:
If they had rehauled the brakes, the accident might have been avoided. = 如果他们当时检修了刹车，事故也许可以避免。
It's time we rehauled the outdated regulations. = 是时候我们彻底改革过时的规章制度了。


被动语态:
The engine is being rehauled at the moment. = 发动机正在检修中。
The outdated laws are being rehauled by the parliament. = 过时的法律正在由议会进行修订。


疑问句:
Will they rehaul the bridge soon? = 他们很快会对桥梁进行大修吗？
Is the company planning to rehaul its image? = 公司计划重塑其形象吗？


否定句:
They didn't rehaul the equipment. = 他们没有检修设备。
The government has not yet rehauled its immigration policy. = 政府尚未修改其移民政策。


PHRASE->
