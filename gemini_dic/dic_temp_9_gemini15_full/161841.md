practicum:/ˈpræktɪkəm/| n.|实习；实践课；实习作业| A practicum is a course, especially in a professional school, that involves supervised practical application of a subject.  It often involves hands-on experience and the application of theoretical knowledge in a real-world setting.|例句：The nursing practicum gave students valuable hands-on experience. 护理实习让学生获得了宝贵的实践经验。|例句：My psychology practicum involved observing and interviewing patients. 我的心理学实习包括观察和采访病人。|近义词：internship, fieldwork, apprenticeship|反义词：theory, lecture
