daunted:/ˈdɔːntɪd/| adj.|畏惧的；胆怯的；受挫的|Feeling intimidated or discouraged, often by the magnitude of a task or challenge.|The hikers were daunted by the steep, rocky climb ahead. 登山者被前面陡峭崎岖的山路吓倒了。|近义词：discouraged, intimidated, dismayed, disheartened|反义词：encouraged, emboldened, confident


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is daunted by the size of the project. 他被项目的规模吓倒了。
The task daunts many people.  这项任务使许多人望而却步。


一般过去时:
I was daunted by the complexity of the problem. 我被问题的复杂性吓倒了。
She felt daunted by the prospect of public speaking. 她对公开演讲的前景感到害怕。


现在完成时:
They have been daunted by repeated failures. 他们屡屡失败，感到灰心丧气。
The team has not been daunted by the challenges.  团队并没有被挑战吓倒。


过去完成时:
He had been daunted by previous setbacks, but he persevered. 他曾因之前的挫折而气馁，但他坚持了下来。
She had never been daunted before, but this time it was different. 她以前从未感到气馁，但这一次不一样了。


将来时:
The upcoming exam will daunt many students.  即将到来的考试会让许多学生感到害怕。
I am sure she will not be daunted by the difficult task ahead. 我确信她不会被面前的困难任务吓倒。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't daunted by the challenge, I would try it. 如果我不害怕这个挑战，我会尝试一下。
I wish I weren't so daunted by the prospect of failure. 我希望我不那么害怕失败。


被动语态:
He was daunted by the immense workload. 他被巨大的工作量吓倒了。
Many were daunted by the difficult questions. 许多人被难题难住了。


疑问句:
Are you daunted by the task? 你被这项任务吓倒了吗？
Was she daunted by the criticism? 她被批评吓倒了吗？


否定句:
He was not daunted by the criticism. 他没有被批评吓倒。
She wasn't daunted by the challenge. 她没有被挑战吓倒。


PHRASE->

be daunted by: 被……吓倒
feel daunted: 感到害怕
seem daunted: 看起来害怕
remain undaunted: 毫不气馁
undaunted spirit: 不屈不挠的精神
