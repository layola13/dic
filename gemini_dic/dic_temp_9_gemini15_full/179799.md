reexamine:/ˌriːɪɡˈzæmɪn/| vt.|重新检查；再审查|To examine again; to reconsider.|例句：The committee will reexamine the evidence. 委员会将重新审查证据。  例句：Let's reexamine our assumptions. 让我们重新审视我们的假设。|近义词：review, reconsider, reinvestigate|反义词：accept, ignore|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexamine the data every year. = 他们每年都重新审查数据。
We reexamine our strategies regularly. = 我们定期重新审查我们的策略。

一般过去时:
The judge reexamined the witness. = 法官再次讯问了证人。
She reexamined her life choices. = 她重新审视了她的人生选择。

现在完成时:
They have reexamined the case several times. = 他们已经多次重新审查了这个案子。
We have reexamined our budget and found some savings. = 我们已经重新审查了我们的预算，并发现了一些节省之处。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had reexamined the documents before the trial. = 律师在审判前已经重新检查了文件。
They had reexamined their options before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经重新考虑了他们的选择。

将来时:
The scientists will reexamine the results of the experiment. = 科学家们将重新检查实验的结果。
The board will reexamine the proposal next week. = 董事会下周将重新审查该提案。

虚拟语气:
If we reexamined the evidence, we might find new clues. = 如果我们重新审查证据，我们可能会发现新的线索。
I wish I had reexamined my assumptions earlier. = 我希望我早点重新审视我的假设。

被动语态:
The report will be reexamined by the experts. = 这份报告将由专家重新审查。
The evidence was reexamined and found to be unreliable. = 证据被重新审查，发现不可靠。

疑问句:
Will the committee reexamine the proposal? = 委员会会重新审查该提案吗？
Have they reexamined the witnesses' statements? = 他们是否重新审查了证人的证词？

否定句:
They did not reexamine the evidence carefully enough. = 他们没有仔细地重新审查证据。
He refused to reexamine his position on the issue. = 他拒绝重新审视他在这个问题上的立场。
