orewood:/ˈɔːrˌwʊd/| n.|矿木，矿藏木材|Orewood refers to wood that is impregnated with or contains mineral deposits, often found in areas with significant mineral activity.  It's not a commonly used word and lacks widespread dictionary entries.|例句：The miners discovered a vein of orewood deep within the mountain.  矿工们在深山中发现了一条矿木脉。|近义词：mineralized wood, petrified wood (if fossilized)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be 'plain wood' or 'unmineralized wood'.|名词
