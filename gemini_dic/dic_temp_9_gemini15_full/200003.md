skee:/skiː/| v.|令人不快；令人厌恶；使人感到不适|To feel disgusted or uneasy; to make someone feel disgusted or uneasy.|例句：That smell really skees me out.  那个气味真让我恶心。|近义词：disgust, repel, nauseate|反义词：delight, please
