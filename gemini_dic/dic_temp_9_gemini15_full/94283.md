impersonation: /ɪmˌpɜːrsənɪˈfeɪʃən/ | n. |拟人化；模仿；假冒 | The attribution of human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.  It's a figure of speech where an inanimate object or abstract concept is given human qualities. | Example: The wind whispered secrets through the trees. (The wind, an inanimate object, is given the human ability to whisper.) | 近义词：personification,anthropomorphism | 反义词：dehumanization
