nonsurvivor:/ˌnɑːn.sɚˈvaɪvɚ/ | n. |未幸存者，遇难者 |A nonsurvivor is someone who dies in an accident, disaster, or illness.  This term emphasizes the lack of survival. | Example: The report listed the names of all the nonsurvivors of the plane crash.  这份报告列出了飞机失事中所有遇难者的姓名。|近义词: casualty, victim, fatality |反义词: survivor
