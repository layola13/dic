employe:/ɛmˈplɔɪiː/ | n. |雇员，职工 | A person employed for wages or salary.  | Example: The company employs over 500 employees. 这家公司雇佣了500多名员工。 |近义词：worker, staff member, personnel |反义词：employer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company employs many skilled workers. = 这家公司雇佣了许多熟练工人。
She employs a part-time assistant. = 她雇佣了一名兼职助理。

一般过去时:
They employed him last year. = 他们去年雇佣了他。
The factory employed hundreds of people. = 这个工厂雇佣了数百人。

现在完成时:
We have employed three new engineers this year. = 今年我们已经雇佣了三名新的工程师。
The firm has employed her for over a decade. = 这家公司已经雇佣了她十多年了。

过去完成时:
They had employed him before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经雇佣了他。
The company had employed her for five years before she resigned. =  在她辞职之前，该公司已经雇佣了她五年。

将来时:
We will employ more staff next quarter. = 下个季度我们将雇佣更多员工。
They will employ a new marketing manager soon. = 他们很快就会雇佣一位新的市场经理。

虚拟语气:
If they employed more people, they could increase production. = 如果他们雇佣更多的人，他们就能提高产量。
I wish they employed someone with more experience. = 我希望他们雇佣一个更有经验的人。

被动语态:
He was employed by a large corporation. = 他被一家大公司雇佣了。
Many new graduates are employed each year. = 每年都有很多应届毕业生被录用。

疑问句:
Is she employed full-time or part-time? = 她是全职还是兼职？
Are they employed by the government? = 他们是政府雇员吗？

否定句:
He is not employed at the moment. = 他目前没有工作。
She wasn't employed at that company. = 她不在那家公司工作。
