personifications:/pɜːrsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|拟人化；拟人手法|The attribution of human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.  It's a literary device used to create vivid imagery and enhance understanding.|例句：The poet's use of personification, giving the wind a voice and personality, made the poem more engaging.  诗人运用拟人手法，赋予风以声音和个性，使诗歌更引人入胜。|近义词：anthropomorphism, embodiment, personification|反义词：dehumanization, depersonalization|名词
