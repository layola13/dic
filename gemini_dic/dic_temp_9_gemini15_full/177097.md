reapplied:/ˌriːəˈplaɪd/| v.|重新申请，再次使用|To apply again; to apply something again.|例句：He reapplied for the job after being rejected the first time. 他第一次被拒绝后再次申请了这份工作。
She reapplied the sunscreen after swimming. 游泳后她再次涂抹防晒霜。|近义词：renew, reapply for|反义词：withdraw, abandon
