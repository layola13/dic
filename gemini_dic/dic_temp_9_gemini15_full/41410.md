cornelians:/kɔːˈniːliənz/| n. |红玉髓； carnelian |红玉髓是一种不透明的红色或红棕色的玉髓，通常用于制作珠宝。Its color ranges from a pale orange to a deep, almost brownish red. The color is due to the presence of iron oxide. Cornelian has been used for thousands of years in jewelry and decorative objects.|例句：The necklace was made of beautiful cornelians. 这条项链是用美丽的红玉髓制成的。


The ancient Egyptians used cornelian extensively in their jewelry and amulets. 古埃及人广泛地将红玉髓用于他们的珠宝和护身符。|近义词：carnelian, chalcedony |反义词:  无
