extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/| vt.|挤压；突出；逐出|To force, press, or push out (something) through a small opening.  The process involves shaping a material by forcing it through a die of the desired cross-section.|例句：The machine extrudes plastic to create various shapes. 这台机器挤压塑料以创造各种形状。

例句：The volcano extruded a massive amount of lava. 火山喷发出大量的熔岩。|近义词：protrude, project, expel, force out|反义词：retract, withdraw, suck in|
