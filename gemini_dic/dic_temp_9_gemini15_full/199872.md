sitz:/sɪts/| n.|坐，静坐；疗程|a session of sitting, especially one for medical treatment.  It can also refer to a period of quiet reflection or meditation.|例句：He had a sitz bath to soothe his aching muscles. 他洗了个坐浴来缓解酸痛的肌肉。  She took a sitz for an hour to calm her nerves. 她静坐了一个小时来平复心情。|近义词：sitting, rest, bath (in context of sitz bath)|反义词：standing, activity
