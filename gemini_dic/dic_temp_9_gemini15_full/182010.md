remediable:/ˈrɛmɪdiəbl/ | adj. | 可补救的；可治愈的 | capable of being remedied; curable.  The situation, while serious, is still remediable. | 近义词：curable, correctable, fixable | 反义词：irremediable, incurable, irreparable

例句：

The damage to the car is remediable with some minor repairs.  汽车的损坏可以通过一些小修补来修复。

The teacher believes that the student's poor grades are remediable with extra tutoring. 老师相信学生的低分可以通过额外的辅导来补救。
