lactation:/lækˈteɪʃən/| n. |泌乳；哺乳期|The process of producing and secreting milk from the mammary glands.  This process is essential for nourishing newborns.  It is regulated by hormones, primarily prolactin and oxytocin. |例句：Breastfeeding is a natural form of lactation. = 母乳喂养是哺乳的自然形式。

The mother's lactation period lasted for six months. = 这位母亲的哺乳期持续了六个月。


近义词：milk production, breastfeeding, suckling|反义词：None directly antonymous, but terms implying absence of milk production could be used, such as "agalactia" or "hypogalactia".|名词
