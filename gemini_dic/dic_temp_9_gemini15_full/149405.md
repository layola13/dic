packages:/ˈpækɪdʒɪz/| n.|包裹；套装；组件|A package is a collection of things wrapped or boxed together.  It can refer to a physical grouping of items, like a parcel sent through the mail, or a software bundle, or a set of related services.|例句：I received a package from my family yesterday.  昨天我收到了家人的包裹。

例句：The software package includes a word processor and a spreadsheet program.  这个软件包包括一个文字处理器和一个电子表格程序。|近义词：parcel, bundle, kit|反义词：loose items, individual components|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company packages its products carefully. = 公司仔细包装其产品。
He packages the food for delivery. = 他打包食物准备送货。


一般过去时:
They packaged the gifts beautifully. = 他们把礼物包装得很漂亮。
She packaged the documents for shipping. = 她把文件打包好准备邮寄。


现在完成时:
We have packaged all the orders for today. = 我们已经打包了今天所有的订单。
They have packaged and shipped the goods. = 他们已经打包并运送了货物。


过去完成时:
He had packaged the items before the courier arrived. = 快递员到达之前，他已经打包好物品了。
She had packaged everything before leaving for the airport. = 她去机场之前已经把所有东西都打包好了。


将来时:
We will package the new product next week. = 我们下周将包装新产品。
They will package the order and ship it tomorrow. = 他们将打包订单并在明天发货。


虚拟语气:
If we packaged the goods differently, they might sell better. = 如果我们采用不同的包装方式，这些商品可能会卖得更好。
I wish we had packaged the fragile items more carefully. = 我真希望我们当初能更仔细地包装易碎物品。


被动语态:
The goods are packaged in sturdy boxes. = 货物用坚固的箱子包装。
The software is packaged with a comprehensive manual. = 该软件附带一本详尽的手册。


疑问句:
How are the products packaged? = 产品是如何包装的？
Are the items packaged individually or together? = 这些物品是单独包装还是一起包装？


否定句:
They didn't package the order correctly. = 他们没有正确地包装订单。
The company does not package its products in plastic. = 公司不使用塑料包装其产品。
