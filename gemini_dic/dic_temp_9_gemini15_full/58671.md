dummier:/ˈdʌmiər/ | adj. | 比较笨的；更愚蠢的 | More foolish or stupid; a comparative form of "dummy".  It implies a higher degree of stupidity than "dummy" alone. |  He felt like a dummier than ever after failing the test again. 他再次考试不及格后，感觉比以往任何时候都更笨。|近义词:  dumber, stupider, sillier | 反义词: smarter, cleverer, brighter
