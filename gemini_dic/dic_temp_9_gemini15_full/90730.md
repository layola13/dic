humiliations:/ˌhjuːmɪliˈeɪʃənz/| n. |羞辱；耻辱；丢脸的事 |The plural form of "humiliation," referring to instances or feelings of shame, disgrace, or embarrassment.  It emphasizes multiple occurrences of such experiences. |例句：His repeated failures resulted in a series of humiliating experiences. 他屡屡失败，导致了一连串的屈辱经历。

例句：The public humiliations he suffered damaged his reputation. 他遭受的公开羞辱损害了他的名誉。


近义词：disgraces, shames, mortifications, indignities |反义词：honors, glories, triumphs |词性:名词
