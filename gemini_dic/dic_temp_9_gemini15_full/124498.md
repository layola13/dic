mouline:/muːˈliːn/| n.|穆里尼线, 细棉线|A very fine, tightly twisted thread, often made of cotton, used for embroidery and other fine sewing.  It is known for its strength and smoothness.|例句：The intricate embroidery was created using a fine mouline thread. 这幅复杂的刺绣是用细穆里尼线制作的。
The artist used several shades of mouline to create depth in the design.艺术家使用了多种色调的穆里尼线来为设计创造深度。|近义词：embroidery floss, thread|反义词：thick yarn, coarse thread|
