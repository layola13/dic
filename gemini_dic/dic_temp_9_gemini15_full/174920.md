rade:/reɪd/| n. |袭击，突袭；等级；(尤指商品的)等级；| a sudden attack on a place by a group of people, usually to steal something or to cause damage.  It can also refer to a specific rank or grade, especially in the context of goods. |例句：The police conducted a raid on the suspected drug dealer's house.  警方对涉嫌贩毒者的住所进行了突袭。

The apples are graded according to size. 苹果按大小分级。|近义词：attack, assault, raid, foray, level, rank |反义词：defense, protection
