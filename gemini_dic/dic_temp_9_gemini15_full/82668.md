guessing:/ˈɡɛsɪŋ/ | v. |猜测；推测 |The act of forming an opinion or making a judgment based on incomplete information.  It often involves an element of uncertainty or speculation. |例句：He was guessing at the answer. 他在猜测答案。

例句：Her guessing game was quite entertaining. 她的猜谜游戏很有趣。


近义词：speculating, conjecturing, surmising, estimating | 反义词：knowing, understanding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am guessing the answer. = 我正在猜测答案。
He is guessing the price of the car. = 他正在猜测汽车的价格。

一般过去时:
I guessed the answer correctly. = 我猜对了答案。
She guessed that he was lying. = 她猜到他在撒谎。

现在完成时:
I have guessed all the answers. = 我已经猜到了所有答案。
They have been guessing for hours. = 他们已经猜了几个小时了。

过去完成时:
I had guessed the solution before he explained it. = 在他解释之前，我已经猜到了答案。
She had been guessing all day, but still didn't know. = 她猜了一整天，但仍然不知道。

将来时:
I will guess the number of candies in the jar. = 我将猜罐子里有多少糖果。
We will be guessing the winner of the contest. = 我们将猜测比赛的获胜者。

虚拟语气:
If I were guessing, I'd say it's going to rain. = 如果要我猜，我会说要下雨了。
I wish I hadn't guessed so badly. = 我真希望我不要猜得这么糟糕。

被动语态:
The answer was guessed by many people. = 许多人都猜到了答案。
The winner will be guessed by the audience. = 获胜者将由观众猜测。

疑问句:
Are you guessing the meaning of this word? = 你在猜这个词的意思吗？
What are you guessing about? = 你在猜什么？

否定句:
I am not guessing; I know the answer. = 我不是在猜，我知道答案。
He didn't guess the right answer. = 他没有猜对答案。


PHRASE->
