impound:/ɪmˈpaʊnd/| vt.|扣押；没收；充公|To seize and hold (something, especially property) under the authority of a law.  This typically involves taking something into legal custody, often as a result of a legal process or violation.|The police impounded his car because he was driving without a license. |警察扣押了他的车，因为他无证驾驶。


The city impounded the abandoned vehicles. |市政府没收了那些被遗弃的车辆。


近义词：confiscate, seize, appropriate|反义词：release, return


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police impound illegal drugs. = 警方扣押非法毒品。
The government impounds assets suspected of being involved in criminal activities. = 政府扣押涉嫌参与犯罪活动的资产。

一般过去时:
They impounded the suspect's car. = 他们扣押了嫌疑人的汽车。
Customs officials impounded the shipment of counterfeit goods. = 海关官员没收了那批假冒商品。

现在完成时:
The authorities have impounded several vehicles this week. = 当局本周已扣押了几辆车。
The court has impounded the documents as evidence. = 法院已将文件作为证据扣押。

过去完成时:
The police had impounded the evidence before the trial began. = 审判开始前，警方已将证据扣押。
The company had impounded the faulty products before they reached the market. = 该公司在产品上市前已将有缺陷的产品扣押。

将来时:
They will impound the car if the owner doesn't pay the parking fines. = 如果车主不缴纳停车罚款，他们将扣押他的车。
The court will impound the assets until the legal dispute is resolved. = 法院将扣押资产，直到法律纠纷解决。

被动语态:
His car was impounded for illegal parking. = 他的车因违章停车被扣押。
The stolen goods were impounded by the police. = 赃物被警方扣押。

疑问句:
Will my car be impounded if I park here? = 如果我把车停在这里，我的车会被扣押吗？
Were the drugs impounded legally? = 这些毒品是否合法地被扣押？

否定句:
They did not impound the vehicle because there was insufficient evidence. = 由于证据不足，他们没有扣押车辆。
The judge refused to impound the documents. = 法官拒绝扣押文件。


PHRASE->
impound property = 扣押财产
impound evidence = 扣押证据
impound assets = 扣押资产
impound vehicles = 扣押车辆
impound funds = 扣押资金


词源:
From Old French empeigner ("to pledge"), from em- + empeigne ("pledge, pawn"), from Vulgar Latin *impendere ("to hang over, threaten"), from Latin pendere ("to hang").  The meaning shifted over time from "to pledge" to "to seize and hold."
