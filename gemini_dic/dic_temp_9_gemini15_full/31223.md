channel:/ˈtʃænəl/ | n. v. | 频道；渠道；通道；海峡 | A channel is a route or way through which something is passed or sent.  It can refer to a television or radio station, a waterway, or a means of communication. | 例句：The television channel showed a documentary about wildlife.  电视频道播放了一部关于野生动物的纪录片。| 例句：The company uses various channels to market its products. 公司使用各种渠道销售其产品。|近义词：route, conduit, passage, waterway |反义词：obstacle, barrier |名词，动词
