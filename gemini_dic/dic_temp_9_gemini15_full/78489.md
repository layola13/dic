gharial:/ˈɡeəriəl/| n. |恒河鳄|A gharial is a critically endangered crocodilian species found in the rivers of the Indian subcontinent.  It is characterized by its extremely long and slender snout, adapted for catching fish. |例句：The gharial's long snout is perfectly adapted for catching fish. 恒河鳄的长鼻子非常适合捕捉鱼类。  The conservation efforts aim to protect the remaining gharial population. 保育工作旨在保护剩余的恒河鳄种群。|近义词：crocodile (广义), gavial (同义)|反义词: N/A
