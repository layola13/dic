skepticisms:/ˈskɛptɪsɪzəmz/| n. |怀疑主义；怀疑态度|The plural form of skepticism, referring to multiple instances or types of doubt or questioning beliefs. It encompasses a range of doubts about claims, assertions, or systems of belief.|例句：His arguments were met with widespread skepticisms from the scientific community. 他的论点遭到了科学界的广泛质疑。  例句：The new policy has generated various skepticisms among the public. 新政策在公众中引起了各种各样的怀疑。|近义词：doubts, uncertainties, incredulity|反义词：beliefs, certainties, convictions|名词
