ngo:/ˌɛnˌdʒiˈoʊ/ | n. | 非政府组织 | Non-governmental organization; a non-profit organization that operates independently of any government.  NGOs are often involved in humanitarian work, development projects, advocacy, and research. | Example:  The NGO provided vital medical supplies to the disaster-stricken area.  这个非政府组织向灾区提供了重要的医疗用品。 | 近义词：nonprofit, charity, voluntary organization | 反义词：government agency, governmental organization
