disliken:/dɪsˈlaɪkən/| vt.|不喜欢，厌恶|To feel or express a lack of liking for something or someone.| I dislike the taste of olives. 我不喜欢橄榄的味道。|近义词：dislike, hate, abhor|反义词:like, love, adore|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dislikes spicy food. 他不喜欢辛辣的食物。
She dislikes loud noises. 她不喜欢吵闹的声音。


一般过去时:
I disliked that movie. 我不喜欢那部电影。
They disliked the new policy. 他们不喜欢新的政策。


现在完成时:
I have disliked broccoli since I was a child. 从小我就讨厌西兰花。
She has disliked him ever since that incident. 从那件事后她就讨厌他了。


过去完成时:
He had disliked her for years before they finally met. 在他们最终见面之前，他已经讨厌她好多年了。
I had disliked the job long before I quit. 在我辞职之前很久我就讨厌这份工作了。


将来时:
I will dislike this assignment once I start working on it. 一旦我开始做这项任务，我就会讨厌它。
They will dislike the changes that are coming. 他们会不喜欢即将到来的变化。


虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't dislike public speaking so much. 我真希望我不那么讨厌公开演讲。
If I disliked my job less, I would stay longer. 如果我不那么讨厌我的工作，我会待更久。


被动语态:
(The passive voice is rarely used with "dislike".)  不喜欢这个词很少用被动语态。
His work is disliked by many. 他的作品不受很多人欢迎。(较为生硬的表达)


疑问句:
Do you dislike anything about your job? 你对你的工作有什么不满吗？
Does she dislike cats? 她讨厌猫吗？


否定句:
I don't dislike him, I just don't understand him. 我并不讨厌他，我只是不理解他。
She doesn't dislike the idea, she just needs more time to consider it. 她并不讨厌这个主意，只是需要更多时间考虑。


PHRASE->
have a dislike for 对...反感
take a dislike to 开始讨厌
show a dislike of 表现出对...的不喜欢
express a dislike for 表达对...的不喜欢
deep-seated dislike 根深蒂固的厌恶
