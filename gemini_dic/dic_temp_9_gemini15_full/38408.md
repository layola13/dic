concerned:/kənˈsɜːrnd/| adj.|担心的；相关的；关切的| worried, anxious, relating to, involving |例句：I'm concerned about your health. 我担心你的健康。|例句：The report is concerned with the effects of pollution.这份报告与污染的影响有关。|近义词： worried, anxious, involved, related |反义词： unconcerned, indifferent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is concerned about the exam. = 他担心考试。
She is concerned with environmental issues. = 她关注环境问题。

一般过去时:
They were concerned about the safety of their children. = 他们担心孩子们的安全。
He was concerned that the project might fail. = 他担心项目可能失败。

现在完成时:
I have been concerned about this matter for a long time. = 我长期以来一直关注此事。
We have been concerned that the situation might worsen. = 我们一直担心情况可能会恶化。

过去完成时:
She had been concerned about him before he went on the trip. = 在他旅行之前，她一直很担心他。
They had been concerned that the deadline would not be met. = 他们一直担心无法按时完成。

将来时:
I will be concerned if he doesn't call. = 如果他不打电话，我会担心。
They will be concerned about the consequences. = 他们会担心后果。

虚拟语气:
I would be concerned if I were in your shoes. = 如果我是你，我会担心。
She would have been concerned if she had known. = 如果她知道的话，她会担心的。

被动语态:
His health was concerned by the doctor.  = 他的健康状况受到了医生的关注。
The matter is concerned with the board. = 此事与董事会有关。


疑问句:
Are you concerned about the delay? = 你担心延误吗？
Is the article concerned with climate change? = 这篇文章是关于气候变化的吗？

否定句:
I am not concerned about the noise. = 我不担心噪音。
He wasn't concerned with the details. = 他不关心细节。


PHRASE->
as concerned as = 和...一样担心
be concerned with = 与…有关；参与…
concerned about = 担心…；忧虑…
as far as I'm concerned = 就我而言
deeply concerned = 深感担忧
