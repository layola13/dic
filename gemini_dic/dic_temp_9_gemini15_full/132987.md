nonenemies: /ˌnɒnˈɛnəmiz/ | n. |非敌人，非敌对者 | Those who are not enemies; those with whom there is no conflict or antagonism.  The term often implies a neutral or potentially friendly relationship, rather than simply the absence of enmity. | Example:  Despite past disagreements, they are now considered nonenemies and are working towards a collaborative future. = 尽管过去有过分歧，他们现在被认为是非敌人，并且正在努力走向合作的未来。 |近义词：neutrals, noncombatants, allies (potentially), friends (potentially) | 反义词：enemies, adversaries, opponents | 词性：名词
