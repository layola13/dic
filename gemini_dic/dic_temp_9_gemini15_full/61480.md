embrave:/ɪmˈbreɪv/| vt.|使勇敢；鼓励；壮胆|To give (someone) courage or confidence.  To make someone brave or bolder.|例句：The coach's speech emboldened the team to play their best. 教练的演讲鼓舞了球队发挥最佳水平。

例句：The support of her friends emboldened her to face her fears. 朋友的支持使她勇敢地面对恐惧。


近义词：encourage, inspire, hearten, embolden |反义词：discourage, intimidate, daunt


词性:动词
