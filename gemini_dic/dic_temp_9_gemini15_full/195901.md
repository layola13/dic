senselessly:/ˈsɛnsləsli/| adv.|无意义地，愚蠢地，荒谬地|In a senseless way; without reason or purpose.|例句：He senselessly threw away his chances of success. 他愚蠢地放弃了他成功的机会。

例句：They argued senselessly about trivial matters. 他们为一些琐碎的事情无意义地争吵。


近义词：meaninglessly, foolishly, absurdly, pointlessly

反义词：purposefully, meaningfully, rationally, intelligently


USAGE->
