chancelleries:/ˈtʃænsəlriːz/| n.|总理府；外交部；政府部门|The offices and staff of a head of state or government, especially those concerned with foreign affairs.  Chancelleries are often grand buildings that symbolize power and diplomacy.|例句：The chancelleries of Europe worked tirelessly to prevent war.  欧洲各国总理府为防止战争而夜以继日地工作。|例句：The new policy was announced from the chancellery. 新政策是由总理府宣布的。|近义词：government offices, ministries, diplomatic missions|反义词：无
