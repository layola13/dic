dan:/dæn/| n. |丹，（男子名）|A short form of Daniel.  A man's name of Hebrew origin meaning "God is my judge".|My friend Dan is a talented musician.  我的朋友丹是一位才华横溢的音乐家。|近义词:Daniel|反义词:无


USAGE->

Dan plays the guitar. = 丹弹吉他。
