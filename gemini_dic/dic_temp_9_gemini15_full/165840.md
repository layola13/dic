preoccupying: /ˌpriːˈɑːkjupaɪɪŋ/ | adj. |令人担忧的；使人焦虑的；占据心思的 | Something that is preoccupying is causing someone to think about it a lot, often to the exclusion of other things. It suggests a state of being engrossed or burdened by a thought or worry.  | 例句：The looming deadline was preoccupying him, preventing him from enjoying his vacation.  迫在眉睫的最后期限让他忧心忡忡，无法享受假期。

The news of the accident was preoccupying for everyone involved.  事故的消息让所有相关人员都感到担忧。


近义词：worrying, disturbing, troubling, anxious, concerning | 反义词：reassuring, comforting, calming, peaceful

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem is preoccupying her. = 这个问题让她很担忧。
His work is preoccupying him these days. =  这些日子他的工作让他很操心。

一般过去时:
The news preoccupied him for days. = 这个消息让他忧心忡忡了好几天。
The argument preoccupied their minds. =  那场争论占据了他们的思绪。

现在完成时:
This issue has been preoccupying me for weeks. =  这个问题已经困扰我几周了。
The thought of the exam has preoccupied her all day. =  考试的念头让她整天都心神不宁。

过去完成时:
He had been preoccupied with his own problems, so he hadn't noticed hers. = 他一直忙于自己的问题，所以没有注意到她的问题。
They had been preoccupied with preparations for the wedding. =  他们一直忙于婚礼的准备工作。


将来时:
I'm sure this will preoccupy the committee for months to come. = 我确信这将使委员会在未来几个月内都为此操心。
The project will preoccupy him for the next few years. =  这个项目将在未来几年内占据他的大部分时间。

虚拟语气:
If I weren't preoccupied with work, I would have gone to the party. = 如果我没有忙于工作，我就去参加聚会了。
I wish I weren't so preoccupied with my studies. = 我真希望自己不要这么忙于学业。

被动语态:
He was preoccupied by his worries. = 他被自己的忧虑所困扰。
She was preoccupied with her thoughts. = 她被自己的思绪所占据。


疑问句:
Is anything preoccupying you at the moment? = 你现在有什么烦心事吗？
What's preoccupying him lately? = 他最近在担心什么？


否定句:
He wasn't preoccupied with the details. = 他并没有被细节所困扰。
She wasn't preoccupied by the noise. = 她没有被噪音所打扰。


PHRASE->

preoccupied expression = 心事重重的神情
