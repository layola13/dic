fleeringly:/ˈflɪərɪŋli/| adv.| 嘲弄地；冷笑地；嬉皮笑脸地 |  In a mocking or sneering manner; with a scornful smile.  |例句：He looked at me fleeringly, a cruel smile twisting his lips. 他嘲弄地望着我，嘴角带着残酷的微笑。


例句：She spoke fleeringly about his attempts to impress her. 她轻蔑地谈论着他试图给她留下印象的举动。


近义词：scornfully, mockingly, sneeringly, derisively


反义词：seriously, sincerely, respectfully, earnestly


词性: 副词
