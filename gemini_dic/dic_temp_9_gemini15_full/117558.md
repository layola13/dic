megacosm:/ˈmɛɡəkɒzəm/| n. |宏观世界；宇宙；巨大系统 | A megacosm is a vast or immense system, often used to describe the universe or a very large and complex entity. It's contrasted with a microcosm, which represents a smaller system mirroring the larger one.  |Example:  The city was viewed as a megacosm of human society, reflecting both its triumphs and its flaws.  这个城市被看作是人类社会的宏观世界，反映了它的成就和缺陷。|近义词：universe, cosmos, macrocosm |反义词：microcosm
