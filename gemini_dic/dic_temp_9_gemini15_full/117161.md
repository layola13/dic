means:/miːnz/| /miːns/| v.|意思是，意味着；打算；有…的意义|To express or represent something; to intend or plan something; to have a particular significance or effect.|The sign means "no parking". 这个标志表示“禁止停车”。|The word "love" means many different things. “爱”这个词有许多不同的含义。| signifies, implies, indicates | doesn't mean,  is opposite to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This sign means "no entry". = 这个标志表示“禁止通行”。
What does this word mean? = 这个词是什么意思？


一般过去时:
The meeting meant a lot to him. = 那次会议对他意义重大。
What did her silence mean? = 她的沉默意味着什么？


现在完成时:
His actions have meant a great deal to the community. = 他的行为对社区意义重大。
What have your efforts meant to the project? = 你的努力对项目意味着什么？


过去完成时:
I didn't understand what the letter had meant. = 我不明白那封信的意思。
What had his words meant to her? = 他的话对她意味着什么？


将来时:
This discovery will mean a lot for future research. = 这一发现对未来的研究意义重大。
What will this decision mean for our company? = 这个决定对我们公司意味着什么？


虚拟语气:
If it meant sacrificing our principles, we wouldn't do it. = 如果这意味着牺牲我们的原则，我们不会这么做。
I wish it meant something more to him. = 我希望它对他更有意义。


被动语态:
It was meant to be a surprise. = 本来打算是一个惊喜。
What is meant by this phrase? = 这句话是什么意思？


疑问句:
What does "ubiquitous" mean? = “普遍存在的”是什么意思？
What do you mean by that? = 你这是什么意思？


否定句:
That doesn't mean what you think it means. = 那并不像你认为的那样。
This doesn't mean we can't try. = 这并不意味着我们不能尝试。
