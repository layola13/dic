nontreatment: /ˌnɑːnˈtriːtmənt/ | n. | 不治疗；未经治疗 | The absence of treatment or intervention;  failure to provide medical care or other forms of treatment.  | 例句：The nontreatment of his injury led to serious complications.  他的伤势未得到治疗，导致了严重的并发症。

The patient's nontreatment was a major factor in his deteriorating condition. 病人的未治疗是其病情恶化的主要因素。


近义词：lack of treatment, neglect, absence of care | 反义词：treatment, care, intervention | 词性: noun
