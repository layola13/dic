creepy:/ˈkriːpi/| adj.|令人毛骨悚然的，怪异的，令人不舒服的|Something creepy is strange and frightening in a way that makes you feel uncomfortable. It often suggests something slightly sinister or unsettling, evoking a feeling of unease or disgust.  It can refer to things, places, or people. |例句：The old house had a creepy atmosphere. 这栋老房子有一种令人毛骨悚然的氛围。

The movie had some creepy scenes that scared me. 这部电影有一些令人毛骨悚然的场景吓到我了。


近义词：eerie, spooky, scary, unsettling, weird, bizarre, sinister, unnerving |反义词：pleasant, comforting, reassuring, normal, friendly
