matamata:/ˌmætəˈmɑːtə/|/ˈmætəˌmɑːtɑː/| n. |马塔马塔龟|A large, freshwater turtle of the family Chelidae, found in South America, characterized by its flattened, triangular head, numerous dermal flaps, and cryptic coloration.  It is a ambush predator. |例句：The matamata turtle is a master of camouflage, blending seamlessly with its swampy habitat. 马塔马塔龟是伪装大师，能与沼泽栖息地完美融合。|例句：The zoo recently acquired a rare albino matamata turtle. 动物园最近获得了一只罕见的患白化症的马塔马塔龟。|近义词：  (No perfect synonyms, but other freshwater turtles)|反义词： (No direct antonyms)|名词
