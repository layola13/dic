pilfering:/ˈpɪlfəriŋ/| v.| 偷窃（少量东西）；顺手牵羊 |The act of stealing things of little value, usually in small quantities.  It often implies a casual or opportunistic nature, rather than a planned or large-scale theft. |例句：He was caught pilfering stationery from the office. 他被抓到偷办公室的文具。

例句：The child was caught pilfering cookies from the jar.  孩子被抓到从罐子里偷饼干。


近义词：stealing, thieving, shoplifting, petty larceny


反义词：donating, giving, contributing


词源：源自古英语 *pilfian，意为“偷窃”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pilfers supplies from the warehouse. = 他从仓库偷取物资。
They pilfer small items from the store. = 他们从商店偷窃小件物品。


一般过去时:
She pilfered a pen from the desk. = 她从桌子上偷了一支笔。
The thief pilfered money from the cash register. = 小偷从收银机里偷了钱。


现在完成时:
He has pilfered several items over the years. = 多年来他偷了好几件东西。
They have pilfered information from the company's database. = 他们从公司的数据库中窃取了信息。


过去完成时:
Before the audit, they had pilfered thousands of dollars. = 在审计之前，他们已经偷走了数千美元。
She had pilfered small amounts of money for years before getting caught. = 在被抓之前，她多年来一直少量地偷钱。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
