pileworm: /ˈpaɪlwɜːrm/ | n. |管虫; 环节动物 | A marine polychaete worm that lives in a tube or burrow.  They are typically found in mud or sand.  |例句：The pileworm built its tube out of sand grains. 管虫用沙粒建造它的管子。

例句：Researchers studied the pileworm's unique feeding mechanism. 研究人员研究了管虫独特的摄食机制。


近义词：tube worm (although not all tube worms are pileworms)


反义词：无


词性: 名词
