gastriloquist:/ˈɡæstrɪləkwɪst/| n. |腹语表演者|A ventriloquist is a performer who can make their voice appear to come from another source, usually a puppet or dummy.  They achieve this illusion through skill and practice in manipulating their vocal cords and breath control.  Gastriloquism is often performed as a form of entertainment, often involving humor and storytelling.|The gastriloquist amazed the audience with his lifelike puppet.|ventriloquist,  puppet master| (no direct antonym, but perhaps "silent performer" could be considered a contextual opposite)|名词
