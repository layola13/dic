paralysis:/pəˈræləsɪs/| n.|瘫痪；麻痹；停顿|The loss of the ability to move (a body part), often caused by illness or injury.  It can also refer to a state of inactivity or stagnation.|例句：The accident left him with paralysis in his legs.  事故导致他的腿瘫痪了。

例句：The political paralysis prevented any meaningful reforms.  政治僵局阻碍了任何有意义的改革。

近义词：paralyzation, inactivation, immobility, stagnation

反义词：mobility, activity, animation
