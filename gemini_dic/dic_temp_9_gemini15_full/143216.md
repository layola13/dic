outcull:/aʊtˈkʌl/| vt.|超过（产量）|To surpass in amount produced or harvested; to exceed in quantity.|The farmer's new technique outculled his previous methods by 20%.  这位农民的新技术使他的产量比以前增加了20%。|近义词：outproduce, surpass, exceed, outstrip |反义词：underproduce, fall short of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new factory outculls the old one in production. = 新工厂的产量超过旧工厂。
This apple tree outculls all the others in the orchard. = 这棵苹果树的产量超过果园里其他所有的苹果树。

一般过去时:
Last year's harvest outculled the previous year's. = 去年收成超过前一年。
The team outculled their rivals in terms of goals scored. = 这支球队进球数超过了他们的对手。

现在完成时:
The company has outculled its competitors in the market. = 这家公司在市场上已经超过了它的竞争对手。
This year's wheat production has outculled all expectations. = 今年的小麦产量超出了所有预期。

过去完成时:
By the end of the season, they had outculled all other teams in the league. = 到赛季结束时，他们已经超过了联赛中所有其他球队。
Before the new technology was implemented, they had never outculled their production target. = 在采用新技术之前，他们从未超过他们的生产目标。


将来时:
We anticipate that next year's yield will outcull this year's. = 我们预计明年的产量将超过今年。
With the new equipment, we expect to outcull our competitors significantly. = 使用新设备，我们预计将大大超过我们的竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we had better equipment, we would outcull our rivals. = 如果我们有更好的设备，我们就能超过我们的竞争对手。
I wish our farm could outcull the neighboring farm in this year's harvest. = 我希望我们的农场今年的收成能够超过邻近的农场。


被动语态:
Their production was outculled by a smaller, more efficient company. = 他们的产量被一家更小、更高效的公司超过了。
Last year's record was outculled by this year's exceptional harvest. = 去年的纪录被今年的丰收打破了。


疑问句:
Does this new method outcull the traditional one? = 这种新方法的产量比传统方法高吗？
Will our new technology outcull the competition? = 我们的新技术能否超过竞争对手？


否定句:
Our team did not outcull the opposing team. = 我们的球队没有超过对方球队。
The factory failed to outcull its production target this quarter. = 这个工厂本季度未能超过其生产目标。
