pledge:/pledʒ/| n. v.|保证；誓言；抵押；许诺|A solemn promise or undertaking.  A thing given as security for a loan etc. To make a solemn promise of; to give (something) as security for a loan.|例句：He pledged his support for the project. 他保证支持这个项目。
He pledged his house to the bank. 他把房子抵押给了银行。|近义词：promise, vow, guarantee, commit|反义词：break (a pledge), renege


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pledge my allegiance to the flag. = 我宣誓效忠国旗。
She pledges her support to the charity. = 她承诺支持这家慈善机构。


一般过去时:
He pledged to donate a significant sum. = 他承诺捐赠一大笔款项。
They pledged their loyalty to the king. = 他们向国王宣誓效忠。


现在完成时:
They have pledged their support for the campaign. = 他们已经承诺支持这项运动。
She has pledged to help us with the project. = 她已经承诺帮助我们完成这个项目。


过去完成时:
He had pledged his entire savings to the cause. = 他已经把他所有的积蓄都捐给了这个事业。
They had pledged their allegiance before the war began. = 在战争开始之前，他们就已经宣誓效忠了。


将来时:
We will pledge our resources to the relief effort. = 我们将把我们的资源投入到救济工作中。
She will pledge to work harder next semester. = 她将承诺下个学期更加努力学习。


虚拟语气:
If he pledged his support, we would have a better chance of success. = 如果他承诺支持，我们成功的几率就会更大。
I wish he had pledged to help us earlier. = 我希望他早点承诺帮助我们。


被动语态:
The land was pledged as security for the loan. = 这块土地被抵押作为贷款的担保。
His support was pledged by many influential people. = 许多有影响力的人承诺支持他。


疑问句:
Did he pledge to complete the task on time? = 他是否承诺按时完成任务？
Will they pledge their financial resources to the project? = 他们会为这个项目提供资金支持吗？


否定句:
He did not pledge his support to the opposition. = 他没有承诺支持反对派。
They refused to pledge allegiance to the new government. = 他们拒绝向新政府宣誓效忠。


PHRASE->
pledge allegiance = 宣誓效忠
pledge one's word = 保证；承诺
pledge of support = 支持的承诺
pledge to do something = 承诺做某事
break a pledge = 违背诺言
