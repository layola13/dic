partitionism:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 分裂主义，分割主义 | The advocacy or practice of dividing a territory or group into separate parts, often based on ethnic, religious, or political differences.  It often involves the belief that a particular group should have its own independent state or territory. | Example: The rise of partitionism in the region led to violent conflict and the displacement of millions.  地区分裂主义的兴起导致了暴力冲突和数百万人流离失所。|近义词：separatism, sectionalism | 反义词：unification, integration | 名词
