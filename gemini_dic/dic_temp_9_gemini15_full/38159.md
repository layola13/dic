comprised:/kəmˈpraɪzd/| v. |包含，构成，组成|To consist of; be made up of.  It emphasizes the combination of parts to form a whole.  |The team is comprised of experts from various fields.  这个团队由来自各个领域的专家组成。|composed of, constituted of, made up of|separated, divided


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee is comprised of five members. = 委员会由五名成员组成。
This book is comprised of three chapters. = 这本书由三章组成。


一般过去时:
The team was comprised of experienced professionals. = 这个团队由经验丰富的专业人士组成。
The collection was comprised of rare stamps. = 这个收藏由珍稀邮票组成。


现在完成时:
The project has been comprised of several stages. = 该项目已由几个阶段组成。
The report has been comprised of various data points. = 该报告由各种数据点组成。


过去完成时:
The agreement had been comprised of several key clauses. = 该协议已包含若干关键条款。
The presentation had been comprised of slides and a Q&A session. = 演示文稿由幻灯片和问答环节组成。


将来时:
The new product will be comprised of innovative features. = 新产品将包含创新功能。
The team will be comprised of both experienced and new members. = 该团队将由经验丰富的成员和新成员组成。


虚拟语气:
If the team were comprised of more experienced members, the project would be completed sooner. = 如果团队由更有经验的成员组成，项目将会更快完成。
I wish the report were comprised of more relevant information. = 我希望这份报告包含更多相关信息。


被动语态:
The group is comprised of many different people. = 这个小组由许多不同的人组成。(This sentence is already in the passive voice)
The solution was comprised of several key elements. = 解决方案由几个关键要素组成。(This sentence is already in the passive voice)


疑问句:
Is the class comprised of students from different backgrounds? = 这个班是由来自不同背景的学生组成的吗？
What is the cake comprised of? = 蛋糕是由什么组成的？


否定句:
The team is not comprised of only engineers. = 这个团队并非只由工程师组成。
The dessert is not comprised of any nuts. = 甜点不含任何坚果。


PHRASE->
be comprised of = 由……组成

comprising = 包含；构成

comprise a large part of = 构成很大一部分
