embryonic:/ˌɛmbrɪˈɑːnɪk/| adj.|胚胎的；萌芽期的；早期的|Relating to an embryo or the embryonic stage of development.  It suggests something is in its earliest stage of development and has the potential for future growth.|例句：The embryonic stage of the project is crucial for its future success.  这个项目的胚胎阶段对其未来的成功至关重要。

例句：The company is still in its embryonic stage, with only a few employees. 这家公司仍处于萌芽阶段，只有少数员工。


近义词：primordial, rudimentary, nascent, incipient|反义词：mature, developed, advanced
