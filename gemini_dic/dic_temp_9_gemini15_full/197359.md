sheafing: /ʃiːfɪŋ/ | v. |捆扎；成捆；束缚|The act of binding or gathering together into sheaves (bundles).  It often refers to the process of gathering stalks of grain into bundles for harvesting. |例句：The farmers were busy sheafing the wheat after the harvest.  农民们收割后忙着捆麦子。

The process of making sheaves is called sheafing. 收割捆扎的过程叫做捆扎。|近义词：bundling, binding, gathering|反义词：scattering, dispersing|动词
