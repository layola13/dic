fundamentals:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlz/ | n. |基础知识，基本原理，根本 | The basic principles, facts, or assumptions underlying a subject or field of study.  |例句：A solid understanding of the fundamentals is essential for success in this field.  扎实的基础知识对于在这个领域取得成功至关重要。

例句：We need to go back to the fundamentals and rebuild our strategy. 我们需要回到基础，重建我们的战略。


近义词：basics, essentials, foundations, principles | 反义词：superficialities, complexities, details


词源：源自于拉丁语“fundamentum”，意为“foundation”（基础）。
