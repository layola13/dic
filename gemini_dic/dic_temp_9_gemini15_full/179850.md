reexplains:/ˌriːɪkˈspleɪnz/| v. |重新解释；再次解释 |To explain something again, often in more detail or a different way.  |The teacher reexplained the complex equation, using simpler terms.  老师用更简单的术语重新解释了这个复杂的方程式。| explains, clarifies, elucidates | obfuscates, confuses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reexplains the concept every week. 他每周都会重新解释这个概念。
She reexplains the rules to the new employees. 她向新员工重新解释规则。


一般过去时:
The professor reexplained the theory after several students asked questions.  教授在几个学生提问后重新解释了这个理论。
He reexplained his position to the committee. 他向委员会重新解释了他的立场。


现在完成时:
The tutor has reexplained the difficult passages multiple times.  家教已经多次重新解释了这些难懂的段落。
She has reexplained the instructions because many people misunderstood them. 她已经重新解释了说明，因为很多人误解了它们。


过去完成时:
After the initial confusion, the speaker had reexplained the key points. 在最初的混乱之后，发言人重新解释了要点。
They had reexplained the plan several times before everyone understood. 在每个人都理解之前，他们已经多次重新解释了这个计划。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reexplain the entire process. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新解释整个过程。
