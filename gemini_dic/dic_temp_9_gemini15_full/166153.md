prepromoting:/ˌpriːprəˈmoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先推广，提前宣传 |  The act of promoting something before its official launch or release, aiming to generate early interest and anticipation.  It involves creating hype and buzz around a product, service, or event in advance. | 例句：The company is prepromoting its new product line through social media campaigns. 公司正在通过社交媒体活动预先推广其新的产品线。


The marketing team is prepromoting the concert by releasing exclusive clips. 市场团队正在通过发布独家片段来预先宣传音乐会。


近义词：advance promotion, early promotion, pre-launch marketing, teaser campaign | 反义词：post-launch marketing, delayed promotion
