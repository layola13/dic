outtrading: /ˌaʊtˈtreɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 胜过；在交易中超过 | To surpass or exceed someone or something in trading or business dealings.  |例句：Our company outtraded its competitors by focusing on customer service. = 我们公司通过专注于客户服务而胜过竞争对手。 |近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, outperform |反义词：underperforming, being outdone


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outtrade their rivals consistently. = 他们始终胜过竞争对手。
The small business outtrades larger competitors in niche markets. = 这家小企业在利基市场上胜过更大的竞争对手。

一般过去时:
Last quarter, we outtraded all expectations. = 上个季度，我们超出了所有预期。

They have consistently outtraded their rivals this year. = 今年他们一直胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
