lilliputian:/ˌlɪləˈpjuːʃən/| adj. n.|小矮人；微小的；极小的|Extremely small; tiny.  The term refers to the inhabitants of Lilliput, an island in Jonathan Swift's satirical novel *Gulliver's Travels*, where the people are only six inches tall.|The jewelry was lilliputian in size.|近义词：minuscule, miniature, tiny, diminutive|反义词：gigantic, enormous, colossal, immense|形容词，名词
