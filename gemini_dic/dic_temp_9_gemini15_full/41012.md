coparceny:/kəˈpɑːrsəni/| n.| 均分继承（尤指女性继承人间的）|The legal right of several heirs to inherit property equally, especially among female heirs.  Coparcenary is a system of inheritance where property is divided equally among multiple heirs, typically daughters or sisters, rather than passing to a single heir.|例句：Under the old law, the estate passed to the daughters in coparcenary.  在旧法下，遗产由女儿们均分继承。|The sisters inherited the land in coparcenary.  姐妹们以均分继承的方式继承了这块土地。|joint tenancy, inheritance|sole inheritance
