moonshine:/ˈmuːnʃaɪn/| n. |月光；私酿酒；空话；胡说八道|  Moonshine is light from the moon.  It can also refer to illegally distilled liquor, or nonsense. |例句：The moonshine bathed the landscape in silver light. 月光给景色镀上银色的光辉。

The moonshiners were caught by the police. 私酒酿造者被警察抓住了。

That's just moonshine; don't believe it. 那只是胡说八道；别信它。

近义词：moonlight, illicit liquor, nonsense, rubbish|反义词：daylight, legal liquor, truth, sense|名词
