delivering: /dɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/ | v. |递送；交付；发表；履行；分娩 |The act of conveying or transporting something to a destination;  giving a speech or performance; fulfilling a promise or obligation; giving birth. |例句：The courier is delivering the package now.  快递员正在送包裹。

The speaker was delivering a powerful speech. 演讲者正在发表一篇有力的演讲。


近义词：conveying, transmitting, presenting, fulfilling, producing | 反义词：receiving, accepting, withholding

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is delivering the goods. 他正在送货。
She delivers inspiring lectures. 她发表令人振奋的演讲。

一般过去时:
They delivered the pizza on time. 他们准时送来了披萨。
The doctor delivered the baby safely. 医生安全地接生了婴儿。

现在完成时:
He has delivered three speeches this week. 他本周已经发表了三次演讲。
She has delivered the project on time. 她已按时完成了项目。

过去完成时:
They had delivered the package before I arrived. 我到达之前他们已经送了包裹。
She had delivered the baby before the ambulance arrived. 救护车到达之前她已经生下孩子了。

将来时:
He will deliver the keynote speech tomorrow. 他明天将发表主旨演讲。
They will deliver the furniture next week. 他们下周将送来家具。

虚拟语气:
If he delivered the speech, it would be a success. 如果他发表演讲，那将会成功。
I wish she had delivered the baby earlier. 我希望她能早点生下孩子。

被动语态:
The package was delivered yesterday. 包裹昨天送到了。
The speech is being delivered now. 演讲正在进行中。

疑问句:
Is he delivering the goods now? 他现在正在送货吗？
When will the package be delivered? 包裹什么时候送达？

否定句:
He didn't deliver the goods on time. 他没有准时送货。
She didn't deliver the baby naturally. 她没有自然分娩。
