oversauce:/ˈoʊvərˌsɔːs/ | v. | 过量加调味汁；过度调味 | To add too much sauce to something.  |  例句：He oversauced the pasta, making it too soggy. 他在意大利面中加了过多的酱汁，导致它太湿了。  | 近义词：overseason, overdo | 反义词：undersauce
