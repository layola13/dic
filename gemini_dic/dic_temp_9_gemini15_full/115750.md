marimba:/məˈrɪmbə/| n. |马林巴琴|A musical instrument consisting of a set of tuned wooden bars struck with mallets to produce musical tones.  The bars are arranged in chromatic order and the instrument often has a resonant chamber beneath.|例句：The marimba player produced a beautiful melody. 马林巴琴演奏者演奏出一首优美的旋律。
The orchestra included a marimba solo in its performance.  管弦乐队在演出中加入了马林巴琴独奏。|近义词：xylophone, vibraphone|反义词:|
