gastralgia:/ɡæsˈtrælʒə/| n. |胃痛；胃部疼痛|Stomach ache or pain in the stomach region.  Gastralgia can be caused by various factors, including indigestion, ulcers, infections, and stress. It can range from mild discomfort to severe, sharp pain.|例句：He suffered from severe gastralgia after eating spicy food. 他吃辛辣食物后患上了严重的胃痛。|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to relieve her gastralgia. 医生开了药来缓解她的胃痛。|近义词：stomachache, abdominal pain|反义词：gastric comfort (胃部舒适)|名词
