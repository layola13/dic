punchinello: /ˌpʌntʃɪˈnɛloʊ/ | n. | 庞奇内洛（滑稽剧角色）| A stock character in Italian commedia dell'arte, a mischievous and often boastful hunchback.  Punchinello is known for his slapstick comedy, witty remarks, and generally clumsy behavior. He is often depicted as a servant or a commoner, and his antics are usually directed towards those in authority.  | Example: The play featured a hilarious Punchinello who constantly outsmarted the pompous doctor.  庞奇内洛的滑稽表演让这个自大的医生总是被耍得团团转。| 近义词：harlequin, buffoon, clown | 反义词：serious character, straight man
