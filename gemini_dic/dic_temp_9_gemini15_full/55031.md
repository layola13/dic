disquieted:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪd/| adj.|不安的；烦扰的；焦虑的| feeling or showing anxiety and worry.|The news disquieted her greatly.  这个消息让她非常不安。|The uncertain future disquieted many people. 不确定的未来让许多人感到不安。|restless, anxious, uneasy|calm, peaceful, tranquil|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is disquieted by the news. 他对这个消息感到不安。
The situation disquiets the public.  这种情况让公众不安。
