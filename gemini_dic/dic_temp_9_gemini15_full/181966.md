remaster:/ˌriːˈmɑːstər/|/ˈriːmæstər/ | vt. |重新制作母带；重新掌握；重新发行 |To create a new, improved version of a sound recording or video, often with higher quality audio or video. This typically involves enhancing the original source material using modern technology.  |例句：The band remastered their classic album for its 25th anniversary. 乐队为庆祝经典专辑发行25周年对其进行了重新制作母带。

The studio remastered the film for a Blu-ray release.  工作室为蓝光发行重新制作了这部电影。|近义词：remake, refurbish, enhance |反义词：destroy, ruin
