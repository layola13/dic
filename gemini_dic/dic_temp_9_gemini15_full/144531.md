outvenom: /ˌaʊtˈvɛnəm/ | vt. |胜过…的毒性；毒性超过 | To surpass in venom; to be more venomous than. |例句：The black mamba's venom outvenoms that of the cobra. 黑曼巴蛇的毒性超过眼镜蛇。The criticism outvenomed the praise. 批评胜过赞扬。 |近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo |反义词：be less venomous than


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The black mamba outvenoms the cobra. = 黑曼巴蛇的毒性超过眼镜蛇。
His anger outvenoms his reason. = 他的愤怒胜过他的理智。

一般过去时:

Her spite outvenomed her regret. = 她的恶意超过了她的后悔。

现在完成时:

Her actions have outvenomed any words she could have spoken. = 她的行为比她任何话语都更具恶意。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
