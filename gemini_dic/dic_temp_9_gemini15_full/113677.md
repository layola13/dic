mack:/mæk/| n. v.|大衣；外套；猛击；打击；|A mack is a type of heavy, waterproof coat, often associated with fishermen or outdoor workers.  As a verb, it means to strike or hit forcefully. |例句：He wore a mackintosh in the rain. 他在雨中穿着一件防水衣。 He maced the intruder. 他用警棍击打入侵者。|近义词：(n.) coat, raincoat, jacket; (v.) hit, strike, punch |反义词：(v.) caress, soothe
