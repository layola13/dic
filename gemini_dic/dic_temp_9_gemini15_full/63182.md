entangle:/ɪnˈtæŋɡəl/ | vt.  |使纠缠；使复杂化 | To twist together or become twisted together in a confused mass.  |The wires became entangled. 电线纠缠在一起了。|He became entangled in a web of lies. 他陷入了谎言的泥潭。| disentangle, untangle | separate, simplify | v.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fishermen entangle the net. = 渔民们把渔网缠在一起。
The vines entangle the trees. = 爬山虎缠绕着树木。

一般过去时:
The climber entangled himself in the ropes. = 登山者把自己缠在了绳索上。
The dog entangled its leash in the fence. = 狗的牵引绳缠在了栅栏上。

现在完成时:
The children have entangled themselves in the blankets. = 孩子们把自己裹在了毯子里。
The situation has become increasingly entangled. = 情况变得越来越复杂。

过去完成时:
He had entangled himself in a difficult legal battle. = 他卷入了一场艰难的法律诉讼。
She had entangled her hair in the branches. = 她的头发缠在了树枝上。

将来时:
The strong winds will entangle the power lines. = 强风将会缠绕住电线。
I fear the two issues will entangle further. = 我担心这两个问题将会进一步纠缠。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been so careless, I wouldn't have entangled myself in this mess. = 如果我不那么粗心，我就不会陷入这个困境。
If he had not intervened, the situation would have become even more entangled. = 如果他没有干预，情况会变得更加复杂。

被动语态:
The fishing net was entangled in the rocks. = 渔网被缠在了岩石上。
The argument was further entangled by the introduction of new evidence. = 新证据的出现使争论更加复杂。

疑问句:
Is the rope entangled? = 绳子缠住了吗？
Are the wires entangled with each other? = 电线互相缠绕了吗？

否定句:
The threads did not entangle easily. = 这些线不容易缠绕。
He tried to untangle the situation, but he couldn't disentangle the truth. = 他试图理清局势，但他无法解开真相。


PHRASE->
entangle oneself in = 卷入...
become entangled = 陷入困境
entangled in = 缠绕在...
entangle with = 与...纠缠
severely entangled = 严重缠绕


词源:
Old French *entangler, from en- + tangler "to tangle," from Old North French tangler.  Ultimately of Germanic origin.
