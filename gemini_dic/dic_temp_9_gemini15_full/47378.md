dead:/ded/| adj.|死的，无生命的；沉闷的，无趣的；完全的，彻底的 |The word "dead" signifies the cessation of life.  It can also describe something completely lacking in vitality or interest.  It can further be used to emphasize the absolute nature of something. |例句：The flower is dead. 这朵花死了。 The party was dead.  派对很沉闷。 He's dead tired. 他累极了。|近义词：deceased, lifeless, dull, extinct, defunct |反义词：alive, lively, vibrant, active, functioning


USAGE->

一般过去时:
