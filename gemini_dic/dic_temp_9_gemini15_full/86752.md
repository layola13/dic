helpful:/ˈhɛlpfəl/| adj.|有帮助的，有益的|Providing assistance; useful.|His advice was very helpful.  他的建议非常有帮助。|近义词：useful, beneficial, advantageous|反义词：harmful, unhelpful, detrimental

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His suggestions are helpful to me. = 他的建议对我有帮助。
This book is helpful for learning English. = 这本书对学习英语很有帮助。

一般过去时:
Your help was very helpful yesterday. = 你的帮助昨天非常有帮助。
The medicine was helpful in relieving my pain. = 这药有助于缓解我的疼痛。

现在完成时:
His advice has been helpful in many situations. = 他的建议在很多情况下都很有帮助。
This course has been helpful to my career development. = 这门课程对我的职业发展很有帮助。

过去完成时:
I had already found a helpful solution before he called. = 在他打电话之前我已经找到了一个有帮助的解决方案。
The information they had provided was helpful in understanding the problem. = 他们提供的信息有助于理解这个问题。


将来时:
This new software will be helpful in our work. = 这个新软件将有助于我们的工作。
I believe this training will be helpful for your future. = 我相信这次培训对你的未来会有帮助。


虚拟语气:
It would be helpful if you could provide more details. = 如果你能提供更多细节，那就太好了。
I wish I had had a more helpful teacher. = 我真希望我有一个更有帮助的老师。


被动语态:
The information was found helpful by the researchers. = 研究人员发现这些信息很有帮助。
The new method is considered helpful by many experts. = 许多专家认为这种新方法很有帮助。


疑问句:
Is this information helpful to your research? = 这个信息对你的研究有帮助吗？
Will this software be helpful for our project? = 这个软件对我们的项目有帮助吗？


否定句:
His advice was not helpful at all. = 他的建议一点帮助都没有。
The instructions were not helpful and quite confusing. = 说明书毫无帮助，而且非常混乱。


PHRASE->

prove helpful = 证明有帮助
