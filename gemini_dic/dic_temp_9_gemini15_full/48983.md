deghost:/diːˈɡoʊst/| vt.|去除重影；消除重影；去除幻影|To remove a ghost or ghosting effect from an image or signal.  This often involves image processing techniques to reduce noise or artifacts that resemble ghosts.|例句：The software can deghost images, improving clarity and detail.  该软件可以去除图像重影，从而提高清晰度和细节。|例句：The engineer deghosted the signal to isolate the true data.工程师去除了信号中的重影以分离真实数据。|近义词：remove ghosting, eliminate artifacts, clean up|反义词：ghost, introduce artifacts
