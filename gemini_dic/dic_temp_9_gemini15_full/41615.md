corral:/kəˈræ l/| n., vt.|畜栏；围栏；(使)集合；(使)聚集 | a fenced enclosure for confining livestock; to gather or confine (livestock) within a corral; to force into submission or a confined space. |例句：The cowboys drove the cattle into the corral. 牛仔们把牛赶进畜栏。
The police corralled the protesters. 警察控制住了抗议者。|近义词：pen, enclosure, round up, gather |反义词：scatter, disperse
