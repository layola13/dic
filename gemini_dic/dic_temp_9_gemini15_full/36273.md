cohabitee:/ˌkoʊhæbɪˈtiː/| n. |同居者|A person who lives with another person, especially a person who is not their spouse.  This term is often used in legal contexts to describe unmarried couples living together. |Example:  The cohabitee was named in the will.  这位同居者被列入遗嘱。
They are registered as cohabitees with the local council. 他们以同居者的身份在当地议会登记。|近义词：partner, live-in partner, domestic partner |反义词：spouse (in the context of married couples) |名词
