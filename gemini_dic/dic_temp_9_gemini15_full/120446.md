minnows:/ˈmɪnoʊz/| n. |小型鱼，小人物 |Small fish, especially young ones.  They are often used as bait for fishing.  The term can also be used metaphorically to refer to insignificant people or things. |例句：The children caught several minnows in the stream.孩子们在小溪里捉到几条小鱼。

The minnows darted around in the shallow water. 小鱼在浅水中快速游动。

|近义词：small fish, fry,  small fry |反义词：large fish, whales, giants |名词
