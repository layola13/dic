gazillionaires:/ˌɡæzɪljəˈneərz/| n. |亿万富翁们 |A humorous or informal term for a large number of billionaires or extremely wealthy people.  It combines "gazillion" (an informal word for a huge, unspecified number) with "billionaires".|例句：The gazillionaires of Silicon Valley are known for their philanthropic endeavors.  硅谷的亿万富翁们以他们的慈善事业而闻名。 The gazillionaires attending the gala were dressed impeccably.参加晚会的亿万富翁们衣着讲究。|近义词：billionaires,multi-millionaires,the super-rich|反义词：poor people,the impoverished
