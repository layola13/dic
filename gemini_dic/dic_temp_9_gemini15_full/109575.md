let-someone-have-it: /lɛt ˈsʌmwʌn ˈhæv ɪt/ | phrasal verb |  痛打某人；狠狠教训某人；严厉批评某人 | To attack someone violently or verbally; to severely criticize or reprimand someone. | 例句：The bully let the smaller boy have it.  那个恶霸狠狠地揍了那个个子小的男孩。


例句：The teacher let the student have it for cheating on the exam. 老师因为学生考试作弊而狠狠地批评了他。


近义词：attack, assault, scold, rebuke, berate, lambaste


反义词：praise, commend, compliment, forgive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lets his brother have it when he misbehaves. = 他弟弟行为不端时，他会狠狠地教训他。
She lets her employees have it if they miss deadlines. = 员工错过最后期限，她会严厉批评他们。


一般过去时:
The coach let the team have it after their terrible performance. = 队伍表现糟糕后，教练狠狠地训斥了他们。
He let his friend have it for breaking his promise. = 他朋友违背承诺后，他狠狠地批评了他。


现在完成时:
She has let her frustrations have it on her poor husband. = 她把所有的挫败感都发泄在她可怜的丈夫身上。
He has let his anger have it on his co-worker several times this week. = 这周他已经好几次把怒气发泄在他同事身上了。


过去完成时:
She had let her anger have it on him before realizing he wasn't at fault. = 她先发泄了怒火，然后才意识到他并没有错。
He had let his disappointment have it on his team before they had a chance to explain. = 在队员们有机会解释之前，他已经把失望发泄在他们身上了。


将来时:
I will let him have it if he does that again. = 如果他再那样做，我会狠狠地教训他。
She will let her feelings have it when she sees him. = 她见到他时就会发泄她的感情。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would let him have it. = 如果我看到他，我会狠狠地教训他。
I wish I had let him have it when he deserved it. = 我真希望在他该挨骂的时候，我就狠狠地教训了他。


被动语态: *(This phrase is rarely used in the passive voice because it emphasizes the active action of "letting someone have it.")*  


疑问句:
Will you let him have it? = 你会教训他吗？
Should I let him have it? = 我应该教训他吗？


否定句:
I didn't let him have it, even though I wanted to. = 虽然我想，但我并没有教训他。
She didn't let her anger have it, despite feeling very upset. = 尽管感到非常沮丧，她并没有发泄她的怒火。


PHRASE->
let someone have it good = 狠狠地教训某人
let someone have it straight = 直截了当地批评某人
let someone have it to them = 当面狠狠地教训某人
let the whole truth have it = 讲出全部真相
let the chips fall where they may = 听天由命
