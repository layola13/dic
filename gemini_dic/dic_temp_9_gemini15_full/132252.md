nondeclaratively:/ˌnɒndɪˈklærətɪvli/ | adv. |非陈述性地|  Adverbial form of nondeclarative, indicating a manner of communication or expression that is not declarative; that is, not making a statement or assertion. It often suggests an implicit or indirect way of conveying information or instructions.  | Example: The software operates nondeclaratively, using procedural code to achieve its goals.  该软件以非声明式的方式运行，使用过程代码来实现其目标。 | 近义词:implicitly, indirectly, procedurally | 反义词:declaratively, explicitly
