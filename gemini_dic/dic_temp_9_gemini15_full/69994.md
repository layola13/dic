fetchers:/ˈfɛtʃərz/| n.|取物者，提取器|Those who or that which fetches something.  It can refer to people, animals, or mechanisms designed to retrieve objects.|例句：The dogs are excellent fetchers; they always bring back the ball.  这些狗是优秀的取物者；它们总是把球带回来。  The automated system is a reliable fetcher of data from the server. 这个自动化系统是一个可靠的从服务器提取数据的提取器。|近义词：retrievers, gatherers, collectors|反义词：losers, abandoners


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dogs fetch the ball every day. = 狗每天都去取球。
The system fetches data from the database. = 系统从数据库中提取数据。

一般过去时:
He fetched the newspaper from the mailbox. = 他从邮箱里取了报纸。
The robot fetched the parts from the storage. = 机器人从仓库取来了零件。

现在完成时:
She has fetched the children from school. = 她已经把孩子们从学校接回来了。
The program has fetched all the necessary files. = 程序已经获取了所有必要的文档。

过去完成时:
He had fetched the tools before starting the repair. = 他在开始修理之前已经取来了工具。
The machine had fetched the data before the crash. = 机器在崩溃之前已经获取了数据。

将来时:
I will fetch you a glass of water. = 我会给你拿一杯水。
They will fetch the luggage from the airport. = 他们将去机场取行李。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would fetch help immediately. = 如果我是你，我会立即去寻求帮助。
I wish I had fetched an umbrella before leaving. = 我真希望离开前带了把伞。

被动语态:
The data was fetched automatically. = 数据是自动获取的。
The information will be fetched from the cloud. = 信息将从云端获取。

疑问句:
Will you fetch the mail? = 你会去取邮件吗？
Can the robot fetch the heavy box? = 机器人能取那个沉重的箱子吗？

否定句:
He did not fetch the package on time. = 他没有按时取包裹。
The program could not fetch the required data. = 程序无法获取所需数据。


PHRASE->
fetch and carry:  跑腿，服侍 (to do someone's errands)
go fetch: 去取 (used to tell someone to retrieve something)
fetch up:  到达，最终成为 (to arrive at a particular place or situation)
fetch someone something: 为某人取来某物
fetch a price:  卖到...价钱 (to be sold for a specified amount)
