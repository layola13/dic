lawful: /ˈlɔːfəl/ | adj. | 合法的，法律允许的 | Conforming to or allowed by law; legal.  |例句：The police officer ensured the arrest was lawful. 警官确保逮捕是合法的。

例句：It is lawful to vote at the age of 18 in most countries. 在大多数国家，18岁有权投票是合法的。


近义词：legal, legitimate, rightful | 反义词：illegal, unlawful, illegitimate


词源:  From Old English *lāhwful, from lāh "law" + -ful.
