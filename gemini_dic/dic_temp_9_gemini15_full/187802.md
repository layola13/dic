rosaniline:/ˌroʊzˈænəlɪn/| n. |罗丹明，品红|A red triphenylmethane dye, the parent compound of many other dyes.  It's a basic dye used in various applications, including staining biological samples.|Example:  Rosaniline is used as a stain in microscopy to highlight certain cellular structures.  罗丹明在显微镜下用作染色剂，以突出某些细胞结构。|近义词：magenta, fuchsine|反义词：无
