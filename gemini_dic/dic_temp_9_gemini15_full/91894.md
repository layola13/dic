hyperlethal: /ˌhaɪpərˈliːθəl/ | adj. | 超致死的，极度致命的 | Extremely lethal; causing death far beyond what is normally expected.  | 例句：The newly developed toxin was hyperlethal, causing immediate death in test subjects. 新研发的毒素具有超强的致死性，在测试对象中引起立即死亡。


The hyperlethal dose of radiation exceeded all previous records.  辐射的超致死剂量超过了以往的所有记录。

近义词：extremely deadly, highly toxic, extraordinarily lethal, ultra-lethal | 反义词：harmless, benign, innocuous


词源:  The word "hyperlethal" is a combination of "hyper-" (meaning "excessive," "above," or "beyond") and "lethal" (meaning "causing or capable of causing death").  It's a relatively modern coinage, reflecting the need for a term to describe levels of lethality exceeding typical expectations.
