castleward:/ˈkɑːslwɔːrd/| n. |城堡领地，城堡管辖区 |The area under the jurisdiction of a castle, including the surrounding land and its inhabitants.  This term often refers to a historical administrative unit or a specific area controlled by a castle.  |Example: The castleward stretched for miles, encompassing several villages and farms.  城堡领地延伸数英里，包括几个村庄和农场。|近义词:domain, territory, demesne |反义词:  (lacks a direct antonym, but could be contrasted with terms like "open land", "uncontrolled territory")
