counterconventions:/ˌkaʊntərkənˈvɛnʃənz/| n.|违反惯例；反传统的事物|Things that go against established norms or traditions.  They challenge or defy conventional practices, beliefs, or expectations.|例句：Her avant-garde fashion choices were considered counterconventions in the conservative town.  她前卫的时尚选择在这个保守的小镇被认为是违反常规的。

例句：The artist's counterconventions in painting style sparked a heated debate among critics.  这位艺术家在绘画风格上的反传统做法引发了评论家们激烈的争论。


近义词： unconventional practices, nonconformities, heresies, innovations, departures from the norm


反义词： conventions, traditions, norms, orthodoxies


词性:名词
