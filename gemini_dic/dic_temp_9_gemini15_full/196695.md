severest:/ˈsɪvɪrəst/| adj.|最严重的；最严厉的；最剧烈的 | Most extreme in degree, intensity, or severity.  It is the superlative form of "severe".|例句：The severest punishment was given to the criminal. 最严厉的惩罚被判给了罪犯。

例句：The severest winter in decades brought widespread power outages. 数十年来最严酷的冬天导致了大范围的停电。


近义词：most serious, most harsh, most rigorous, most extreme

反义词：mildest, gentlest, least severe


词性：形容词
