skateable: /ˈskeɪtəbəl/ | adj. | 可滑旱冰的，适合滑旱冰的 | Suitable for skating.  Describes a surface or area that is smooth and even enough to skate on. | 例句：The ice was perfectly skateable. 冰面非常适合滑冰。


The new skate park has several skateable areas. 新建的滑板公园有几个适合滑旱冰的区域。


近义词：skatable, smooth, even | 反义词：rough, bumpy, uneven
