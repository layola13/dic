flagships:/ˈflæɡʃɪps/ | n. |旗舰；佼佼者 | A flagship is the best or most important product, service, or model offered by a company. It is often a leading example or a symbol of the company's brand and quality.  It can also refer to the leading ship in a fleet. |例句：Apple's iPhone is considered a flagship product.苹果的iPhone被认为是一款旗舰产品。 |例句：The aircraft carrier is the flagship of the navy. 这艘航空母舰是海军旗舰。|近义词：leading product, top model, premier offering |反义词：inferior product, low-end model
