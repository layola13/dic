noma:/ˈnoʊmə/ | n. |游牧生活，游牧民族 |A way of life in which people move from place to place instead of living in one location; a group of people who live this way.  |Example: The nomadic tribes migrated with the seasons, following the herds of animals.  游牧部落随着季节迁移，追随动物群。|近义词：migration, wandering, itinerant |反义词：sedentary, settled
