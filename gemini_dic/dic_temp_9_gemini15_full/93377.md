illiberal:/ɪˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. | 不自由的；专制的；狭隘的 | Not liberal; characterized by or advocating adherence to traditional or orthodox values and attitudes; often associated with authoritarianism or intolerance.  | The illiberal government suppressed dissent and curtailed freedom of speech.  这个不自由的政府压制异见并限制言论自由。 | authoritarian, intolerant, restrictive, repressive | liberal, democratic, tolerant, open-minded |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government's policies are illiberal. = 政府的政策是不自由的。
He holds illiberal views on social issues. = 他对社会问题持有狭隘的观点。


一般过去时:
The regime was notoriously illiberal. = 该政权臭名昭著地专制。
Their actions were considered illiberal by many. = 他们的行为被许多人认为是不自由的。


现在完成时:
The country has become increasingly illiberal in recent years. = 近年来，这个国家变得越来越专制。
Society has witnessed a rise in illiberal attitudes. = 社会见证了狭隘态度的上升。


过去完成时:
The people had suffered under an illiberal government for decades. = 人民在专制政府下受苦了几十年。
They had already experienced the consequences of illiberal policies. = 他们已经体验到不自由政策的后果。


将来时:
The situation will likely become more illiberal without reform. = 没有改革，情况可能会变得更加不自由。
We fear the country will become more illiberal under the new leadership. = 我们担心在新的领导下，这个国家会变得更加专制。


虚拟语气:
If the government were more liberal, the people would have more freedom. = 如果政府更自由，人民就会有更多自由。
I wish the society were not so illiberal. = 我希望社会不要如此狭隘。


被动语态:
His views were considered illiberal by the majority. = 他的观点被大多数人认为是狭隘的。
Dissent is often suppressed by illiberal regimes. = 异见常常被专制政权压制。


疑问句:
Are the proposed laws illiberal? = 提出的法律是不自由的吗？
Is this an illiberal interpretation of the law? = 这是对法律的一种狭隘的解释吗？


否定句:
The society is not illiberal; it's quite open-minded. = 这个社会并非不自由；它相当开放。
His actions were not illiberal; he simply acted cautiously. = 他的行为并非不自由；他只是谨慎行事。
