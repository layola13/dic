complain:/kəmˈpleɪn/ | v. |抱怨，投诉 | to express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something.  |例句：He complained about the noise. 他抱怨噪音。 |例句：She complained to the manager about the poor service. 她向经理投诉糟糕的服务。|近义词：protest, grumble, object |反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complains about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
They complain frequently about their workload. = 他们经常抱怨工作量。


一般过去时:
She complained to the teacher about the unfair test. = 她向老师抱怨考试不公平。
He complained bitterly about the long wait. = 他抱怨漫长的等待时间，语气很苦涩。


现在完成时:
I have complained several times about the broken window. = 我已经多次抱怨那扇破窗户了。
They have complained to the landlord about the leak. = 他们已经向房东投诉漏水问题。


过去完成时:
He had complained many times before they finally fixed the problem. = 在他们最终解决问题之前，他已经抱怨过很多次了。
She had complained about the noise all night before falling asleep. = 在睡着之前，她整晚都在抱怨噪音。


将来时:
I will complain to the authorities if the problem persists. = 如果问题持续存在，我将向当局投诉。
He will complain again if he doesn't get a refund. = 如果他不获得退款，他还会再次投诉。


虚拟语气:
I wish I hadn't complained so much. = 我真希望我不要抱怨那么多。
If I were you, I would complain to the manager. = 如果我是你，我会向经理投诉。


被动语态:
The poor service was complained about by many customers. = 许多顾客都抱怨服务差。
The problem has been complained about repeatedly. =  这个问题已经被反复投诉了。


疑问句:
What are you complaining about? = 你在抱怨什么？
Why did she complain to the boss? = 她为什么向老板投诉？


否定句:
He didn't complain about the food. = 他没有抱怨食物。
She didn't complain even though she was very tired. = 即使她非常疲倦，她也并没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
complain about = 抱怨…
complain to = 向…投诉
lodge a complain = 正式投诉
make a complain = 提出投诉
file a complaint = 提交投诉
