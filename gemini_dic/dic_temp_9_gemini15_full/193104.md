secular:/ˈsɛkjələr/| adj.|世俗的；非宗教的；现世的|Not connected with religious or spiritual matters.  Secular activities or institutions are those that are not controlled by a church or other religious organization. |例句：The school is secular, not religious. 这所学校是世俗的，而非宗教的。

例句：His approach to the problem was purely secular, focusing on practical solutions rather than spiritual guidance. 他解决问题的方法纯粹是世俗的，侧重于实际的解决方案而不是精神指导。


近义词：worldly, temporal, earthly, profane|反义词：religious, spiritual, sacred


词源:  From Latin *saecularis ("of an age, lasting an age, worldly"), from saeculum ("age, generation, world").  The original meaning referred to something lasting a generation or an age, hence gradually coming to mean "worldly" as opposed to "spiritual" or "religious".
