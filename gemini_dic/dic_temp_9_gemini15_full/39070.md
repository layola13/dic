conformists:/kənˈfɔːrmɪsts/ | n. | 顺从者，墨守成规者 | People who conform to the established norms, rules, or beliefs of a group or society. They tend to avoid challenging the status quo and prioritize fitting in. | Example:  The conformists in the company readily accepted the new dress code, without question. 公司里的顺从者们欣然接受了新的着装规范，毫无疑问。  Many conformists followed the trend of wearing the same type of shoes. 许多墨守成规的人追随了穿同一种鞋的潮流。|近义词：traditionalists, conventionalists, followers |反义词：nonconformists, rebels, mavericks
