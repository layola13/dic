self-protections:/self-prəˈtɛkʃənz/| n. |自我保护措施；自我防卫机制 | Self-protections are mechanisms or actions taken by an individual or organization to safeguard themselves from harm, risk, or threat.  This can include physical, emotional, or legal measures. |例句：The company implemented new self-protections against cyberattacks.  这家公司实施了新的网络攻击自我保护措施。 |例句：His aggressive behavior was actually a form of self-protections stemming from deep-seated insecurities. 他的侵略性行为实际上是一种源于根深蒂固的不安全感的自我保护。|近义词：defense mechanisms, safeguards, precautions |反义词：self-destruction, vulnerability, exposure |名词
