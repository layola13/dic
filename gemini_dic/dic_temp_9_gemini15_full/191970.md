scorch:/skɔːrtʃ/| vt. vi. n. adj.| 烧焦；枯萎；炙热；极度炎热；| to burn the surface of something |The heat scorched the paint on the car. 热量把汽车上的油漆烤焦了。 The sun scorched the earth. 太阳晒焦了大地。 |  burn, sear, singe | quench, extinguish |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun scorches the land daily. = 太阳每天都炙烤着这片土地。
He scorches the meat too long. = 他把肉烤过头了。


一般过去时:
The fire scorched the wooden fence. = 大火烧焦了木栅栏。
The intense heat scorched the crops. = 强烈的热浪烤焦了庄稼。


现在完成时:
The drought has scorched the fields. = 干旱已经烤焦了田地。
The sun has scorched my skin. = 太阳晒伤了我的皮肤。


过去完成时:
The farmer had scorched the weeds before the rain. = 农民在下雨之前已经烧焦了杂草。
They had scorched the bread.  = 他们已经把面包烤焦了。


将来时:
The intense sun will scorch the paint. = 强烈的阳光会把油漆晒焦。
The wildfire will scorch the forest. = 野火将烧焦森林。


虚拟语气:
If the sun shone brightly, it would scorch the plants. = 如果阳光强烈，它会把植物晒焦。
I wish the sun wouldn't scorch the crops. = 我希望太阳不会把庄稼晒焦。


被动语态:
The bread was scorched in the oven. = 面包在烤箱里烤焦了。
The leaves were scorched by the frost. = 叶子被霜冻烧焦了。


疑问句:
Did the fire scorch the house? = 大火烧焦了房子吗？
Has the sun scorched the grass? = 太阳晒焦了草地了吗？


否定句:
The fire didn't scorch the building. = 大火没有烧焦建筑物。
The sun didn't scorch the plants this year. = 今年太阳没有晒焦植物。
