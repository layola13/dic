disinvests: /ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛsts/ | v. |撤资；停止投资 | To withdraw investment from (a company, industry, or asset).  |例句：The company is disinvesting in its coal-fired power plants. 这家公司正在撤出其燃煤发电厂的投资。|  例句：The government decided to disinvest from the failing project. 政府决定停止对这个失败的项目投资。|近义词：divest, withdraw, pull out |反义词：invest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disinvests in failing businesses. = 这家公司对亏损企业撤资。
He disinvests regularly to diversify his portfolio. = 他定期撤资以使他的投资组合多元化。


一般过去时:
They disinvested from the stock market last year. = 他们去年撤出了股市投资。
The government disinvested in the public transportation system. = 政府停止了对公共交通系统的投资。


现在完成时:
The fund has disinvested from several high-risk ventures. = 该基金已撤出了几项高风险投资。
She has disinvested in her property portfolio recently. = 她最近撤出了房产投资组合的投资。


过去完成时:
They had disinvested in the project before the losses became substantial. = 在损失变得巨大之前，他们已经撤出了该项目的投资。
The company had disinvested from the market before the crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，该公司已经撤出了市场投资。


将来时:
They will disinvest in the oil industry next year. = 他们明年将撤出石油行业的投资。
The investor plans to disinvest from the technology sector soon. = 投资者计划很快撤出科技行业的投资。


虚拟语气:
If they disinvested earlier, they would have avoided significant losses. = 如果他们早点撤资，他们就能避免重大损失。
I wish they had disinvested before the market downturn. = 我希望他们在市场低迷之前就撤资了。


被动语态:
Millions of dollars were disinvested from the project. = 数百万美元的资金从该项目撤出。
The company's assets are being disinvested gradually. = 该公司的资产正在逐步撤资。


疑问句:
Will the government disinvest in renewable energy? = 政府会撤出可再生能源的投资吗？
Why did they disinvest from that promising startup? = 他们为什么撤出那家有前途的初创企业的投资？


否定句:
The company did not disinvest in its core business. = 该公司没有撤出其核心业务的投资。
They haven't disinvested from the real estate market yet. = 他们还没有撤出房地产市场的投资。


PHRASE->
disinvest in = 撤资于…
disinvest from = 从…撤资
strategic disinvestment = 战略性撤资
partial disinvestment = 部分撤资
