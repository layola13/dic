nonproliferation:/ˌnɒnprəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |不扩散；防止扩散 |The prevention of the spread of nuclear weapons or other weapons of mass destruction.  It often refers to international efforts to control the spread of such weapons.  |Example: The international community is committed to nuclear nonproliferation. 国际社会致力于核不扩散。


The Treaty on the Non-Proliferation of Nuclear Weapons (NPT) is a landmark international treaty whose objective is to prevent the spread of nuclear weapons and weapons technology, to promote cooperation in the peaceful uses of nuclear energy, and to further the goal of achieving nuclear disarmament and general and complete disarmament.  国际社会致力于核武器不扩散条约（NPT）是一个具有里程碑意义的国际条约，其目标是防止核武器及武器技术的扩散，促进和平利用核能的合作，并进一步实现核裁军和普遍彻底裁军。

|近义词：prohibition, prevention, containment |反义词：proliferation |
