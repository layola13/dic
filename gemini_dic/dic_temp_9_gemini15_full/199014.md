signaled:/ˈsɪɡnəld/ | vt. | 发信号；标志；预示 | To give a signal; to indicate or suggest something.  |例句：The red light signaled us to stop.  红灯示意我们停车。

The flickering lights signaled a power outage. 闪烁的灯光预示着停电。


近义词：indicated, showed, suggested, implied | 反义词：concealed, hid, masked | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He signals his intentions clearly. 他清楚地表达了他的意图。
The bird signals its distress with loud cries. 鸟儿用大声叫喊来表示它的痛苦。


一般过去时:
She signaled to her friend across the room. 她向房间对面的朋友打手势。
The captain signaled for the ship to turn. 船长发出信号让船转向。


现在完成时:
They have signaled their agreement to the proposal. 他们已经表示同意该提案。
The scientists have signaled a breakthrough in their research. 科学家们已经预示了他们在研究方面的突破。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had signaled their retreat before the enemy arrived. 士兵们在敌人到达之前已经发出了撤退的信号。
He had signaled his dissatisfaction with the project weeks ago.几周前他就已经表示对这个项目的不满。


将来时:
We will signal our readiness when we are prepared. 我们准备好后会发出准备就绪的信号。
The system will signal an error if any problems occur. 如果出现任何问题，系统将发出错误信号。


虚拟语气:
If he had signaled his needs earlier, we could have helped him. 如果他早点发出需求信号，我们就能帮他了。
I wish she had signaled her concerns before it was too late. 我希望她在为时已晚之前就已经表达了她的担忧。


被动语态:
The message was signaled using Morse code. 信息是用摩尔斯电码发出的。
The emergency was signaled by a loud alarm. 紧急情况是由响亮的警报发出的。


疑问句:
Did he signal for help? 他有没有发出求救信号？
What did the flashing lights signal? 闪烁的灯光预示着什么？


否定句:
He didn't signal his intention to leave. 他没有表示要离开的意图。
The driver didn't signal before making the turn. 司机在转弯前没有打转向灯。

词源:
The word "signaled" comes from the Middle English word "signalen," which is derived from the Old French word "signalier," meaning "to make a sign."  Ultimately, it traces back to the Latin word "signum," meaning "sign."
