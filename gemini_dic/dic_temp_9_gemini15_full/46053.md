cyclist:/ˈsaɪklɪst/| n. |自行车骑行者，骑自行车的人|A person who rides a bicycle.  Cyclists should wear helmets for safety.  骑自行车的人应该为了安全戴头盔。|近义词：biker, bicycle rider|反义词：pedestrian


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cycles to work every day. = 他每天骑自行车上班。
She cycles around the park on weekends. = 她周末在公园里骑自行车。

一般过去时:
I cycled to school when I was a child. = 我小时候骑自行车上学。
They cycled along the coast yesterday. = 他们昨天沿着海岸骑自行车。

现在完成时:
He has cycled across the country. = 他已经骑自行车横穿了全国。
She has cycled many kilometers this week. = 她本周已经骑了很多公里的自行车。

过去完成时:
I had cycled ten miles before I stopped for a rest. = 我休息之前已经骑了十英里的自行车。
They had cycled for hours before they reached their destination. = 他们到达目的地之前已经骑了几个小时的自行车。

将来时:
I will cycle to the beach tomorrow. = 我明天将骑自行车去海滩。
We will cycle through the countryside next week. = 我们下周将骑自行车穿过乡村。

虚拟语气:
If I had a bicycle, I would cycle to work. = 如果我有一辆自行车，我会骑自行车上班。
I wish I could cycle faster. = 我希望我能骑得更快。

被动语态:
The bicycle was cycled by the child.  = 这辆自行车被孩子骑了。(This sentence is less common and sounds unnatural.  Passive voice is rarely used with this verb.)
The race was won by the experienced cyclist. = 比赛由经验丰富的自行车手赢得。

疑问句:
Do you cycle to work? = 你骑自行车上班吗？
Does she cycle regularly? = 她经常骑自行车吗？

否定句:
I don't cycle very often. = 我不经常骑自行车。
He didn't cycle to the party. = 他没有骑自行车去参加聚会。


PHRASE->
avid cyclist = 狂热的自行车骑行者
experienced cyclist = 经验丰富的自行车骑行者
competitive cyclist = 竞技自行车骑行者
professional cyclist = 专业自行车骑行者
casual cyclist = 普通的自行车骑行者
