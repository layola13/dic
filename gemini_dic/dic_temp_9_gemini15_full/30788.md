cetaceous:/sɪˈteɪʃəs/| adj.|鲸类的；鲸鱼的| Relating to or characteristic of whales, dolphins, and porpoises.  These marine mammals share common ancestry and characteristics.|例句：Cetaceous mammals are highly intelligent.  鲸类哺乳动物非常聪明。

例句：The museum has a fascinating exhibit on cetaceous life.  这个博物馆有一个关于鲸类生命的迷人展览。


近义词：whale-like (in some contexts)


反义词：None directly opposite, but terms describing land mammals could be considered contrastive.
