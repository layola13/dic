decimal:/ˈdesɪməl/ | adj. n. | 十进制的；小数 | Relating to or denoting a system of numbers and arithmetic based on the number ten, tenth parts, and powers of ten. 词源:
The word "decimal" comes from the Late Latin word "decimalis," meaning "tenth."  This in turn derives from the Latin word "decem," meaning "ten."  The connection is clear:  a decimal system is based on powers of ten.
