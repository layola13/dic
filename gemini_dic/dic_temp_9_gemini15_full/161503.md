potentials:/pəˈtɛnʃəlz/ | n. |潜能；可能性；潜力 |  Potentials refer to the inherent capabilities or possibilities of something or someone.  They represent what could be achieved or developed given the right circumstances.  This word often implies unrealized capabilities waiting to be tapped. |例句：The company has enormous growth potentials in the Asian market. 这家公司在亚洲市场拥有巨大的增长潜力。

The students showed great potentials in their final exams. 学生们在期末考试中展现了很大的潜力。


近义词： possibilities, capabilities, prospects, promise | 反义词：limitations, incapacities, impossibilities | 词性:n.
