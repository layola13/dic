reexposition:/ˌriːˌɛkspəˈzɪʃən/| n. |再阐述，重述，重新解释 |A re-explanation or restatement of something previously explained.  It often involves clarifying or expanding on a previous explanation to ensure better understanding. |The professor provided a reexposition of the complex theorem, addressing the students' questions. 教授对复杂的定理进行了重新解释，解答了学生的问题。|explanation, clarification, reiteration | obfuscation, misrepresentation |名词
