rejig:/ˌriːˈdʒɪɡ/| vt.|重新调整；改组；修补|To reorganize or rearrange something, often in order to improve it.|例句：We need to rejig the schedule to accommodate the new client. 我们需要重新调整日程安排以适应新客户。|例句：The company rejigged its marketing strategy after a poor sales quarter. 公司在销售业绩不佳的一个季度后重新调整了其营销策略。|近义词：rearrange, reorganize, revamp, restructure|反义词：disorganize, disarrange


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rejig their marketing plans every year. = 他们每年都会调整他们的营销计划。
He rejigs his schedule to make time for exercise. = 他调整他的日程安排，腾出时间锻炼。


一般过去时:
She rejigged her essay after receiving feedback. = 她收到反馈后修改了她的论文。
The team rejigged their strategy following the loss. = 比赛失利后，团队调整了策略。


现在完成时:
They have rejigged the website to improve user experience. = 他们已经重新设计了网站以改善用户体验。
The government has rejigged its economic policies. = 政府已经调整了其经济政策。


过去完成时:
They had rejigged the budget before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前已经调整了预算。
She had rejigged her plans after the unexpected delay. = 由于意外延误，她已经重新安排了她的计划。


将来时:
We will rejig the office layout next month. = 我们下个月将重新调整办公室布局。
They will rejig their product line to meet new demands. = 他们将调整产品线以满足新的需求。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would rejig the entire project. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新调整整个项目。
I wish I had rejigged my presentation earlier. = 我真希望我早点修改我的演示文稿。


被动语态:
The schedule was rejigged to fit the new deadline. = 日程安排被调整以符合新的截止日期。
The marketing campaign is being rejigged for better results. = 营销活动正在被调整以获得更好的结果。


疑问句:

否定句:
