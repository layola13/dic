hazers:/ˈheɪzərz/| n.| 造成薄雾的人或物；使模糊不清的人或物 |Those who or that which causes haze.  It can refer to atmospheric conditions, pollutants, or even metaphorical obscurations.|例句：The factory hazers polluted the air, reducing visibility.  工厂的排放物污染了空气，降低了能见度。  The political hazers made it difficult to understand the truth. 政治上的混淆使真相难以理解。|近义词：foggers, obscurers, confounders |反义词：clarifiers, illuminators
