mourns:/mɔːrnz/| v.|哀悼，悲伤|To feel or express great sadness, especially because of someone's death.  The verb "mourn" implies a deep and prolonged feeling of sorrow.|例句：The family mourns the loss of their beloved son.  这个家庭为他们心爱的儿子去世而悲伤。
She mourns the passing of her grandmother. 她哀悼祖母的去世。|近义词：grieve, lament, bewail|反义词：celebrate, rejoice

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mourns the loss of his friend every day. 他每天都在哀悼失去朋友的痛苦。
They mourn silently, their faces etched with grief.  他们默默地哀悼，脸上刻着悲伤。


一般过去时:
She mourned deeply after her husband died. 她丈夫去世后深感悲痛。
The nation mourned the death of its leader. 全国人民为领导人的逝世而哀悼。


现在完成时:
We have mourned the loss of many loved ones over the years. 多年来，我们为许多亲人的离去而悲伤。
She has mourned his passing for many months. 她为他的去世哀悼了几个月。


过去完成时:
They had mourned the loss of their home before the storm even hit.  暴风雨来临之前，他们就已经为失去家园而悲伤了。
She had mourned the end of their relationship for years before she met someone new. 在遇到新的人之前，她已经为他们关系的结束哀悼了好几年。


将来时:
They will mourn the loss of their pet for a long time. 他们将为失去宠物而悲伤很长时间。
The country will mourn the fallen soldiers. 全国人民将为阵亡士兵哀悼。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have mourned with them. 如果我知道的话，我会和他们一起哀悼。
I wish I hadn't caused her such pain; she still mourns our quarrel. 我真希望我没有给她带来如此大的痛苦；她仍然为我们的争吵而悲伤。


被动语态:
The loss of the soldiers was mourned by the entire country. 全国人民为士兵的牺牲而哀悼。（注意：mourn 通常不用于被动语态，此句略显不自然，更自然的表达方式是：The entire country mourned the loss of the soldiers.）
The death of the artist was mourned across the globe. 艺术家的去世在全球受到哀悼。（同样，更自然的表达是：The globe mourned the death of the artist）


疑问句:
Does she mourn the loss of her childhood? 她为失去童年而悲伤吗？
How long will they mourn the passing of their father? 他们将为父亲的去世哀悼多久？


否定句:
He did not mourn the loss of his job. 他并没有为失去工作而悲伤。
She doesn't mourn easily; she keeps her feelings hidden. 她不容易悲伤；她把自己的感情隐藏起来。
