ligature:/ˈlɪɡəˌtʃʊr/| n. |连字，扎带，结扎 |A ligature is two or more letters joined together as a single character.  It can also refer to a surgical suture or a tie used to bind something. |Example: The "æ" in "æsthetic" is a ligature.  The surgeon used a ligature to close the wound. |近义词：tying, bond, joining |反义词：separation, disjunction |名词
