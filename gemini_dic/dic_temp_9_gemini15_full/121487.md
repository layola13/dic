mislaying: /ˌmɪsˈleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 丢失；放错地方 | To put something in a place where one cannot easily find it again; to lose temporarily.  |例句：I'm always mislaying my keys. = 我总是找不到我的钥匙。|例句：He mislaid the important document and couldn't find it for days. = 他把重要的文件放错了地方，好几天都没找到。|近义词：misplacing, losing |反义词：finding, recovering |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often mislay my glasses. = 我经常找不到我的眼镜。
He mislays his wallet every week. = 他每周都找不到他的钱包。

一般过去时:
She mislaid her phone yesterday. = 她昨天找不到她的手机了。
They mislaid the contract during the move. = 搬家的过程中他们找不到合同了。

现在完成时:
I have mislaid my passport; I can't find it anywhere. = 我把护照弄丢了，到处都找不到。
He has mislaid his keys several times this month. = 这个月他已经好几次找不到钥匙了。

过去完成时:
She realized she had mislaid her notes before the presentation. = 演示之前她才意识到自己把笔记弄丢了。
They discovered they had mislaid the crucial evidence weeks ago. = 他们发现几周前就弄丢了关键证据。

将来时:
I'm afraid I'll mislay my ticket. = 我担心我会把票弄丢。
He thinks he might mislay his luggage on the trip. = 他认为旅行中可能会把行李弄丢。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't mislaid the instructions, I could have finished the project earlier. = 如果我没有把说明书弄丢，我就能早点完成项目了。
I wish I hadn't mislaid my camera during the vacation. = 我真希望假期期间我没有把相机弄丢。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The documents were mislaid by someone in the office. = 文件被办公室的某人放错了地方。
The keys were mislaid, and we had to get new ones. = 钥匙弄丢了，我们不得不重新配一把。


疑问句:
Have you ever mislaid anything important? = 你有没有把什么重要的东西弄丢过？
Did he mislay the report before the meeting? = 他在会议之前把报告弄丢了吗？

否定句:
I haven't mislaid my wallet, I know where it is. = 我没有把钱包弄丢，我知道它在哪里。
He didn't mislay the book; he deliberately hid it. = 他没有把书弄丢；他故意藏起来了。
