cantankerous: /kænˈtæŋkərəs/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的；爱争吵的；不好相处的 |

英文解析:  Bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative.  Describes someone who is likely to be grumpy, irritable, and prone to quarreling.


例句:

He's a cantankerous old man who's always complaining.  = 他是个脾气暴躁的老人，总是抱怨。

The cantankerous neighbor constantly argued with everyone on the street. =  那个脾气暴躁的邻居不断地和街上的每个人争吵。


近义词: grumpy, irritable, bad-tempered, quarrelsome, argumentative, churlish, crotchety, irascible


反义词: amiable, agreeable, good-natured, pleasant, easygoing


词源:  The word comes from the Middle English word "cantanker," meaning "quarrelsome person," possibly of obscure origin, perhaps related to a now-obsolete word "canker," meaning "sore, ulcer," suggesting a metaphorical connection to a festering ill-temper.
