russify:/ˈrʌsɪfaɪ/| vt.|使俄化；用俄语表达|To make something Russian in character or style; to translate into Russian.|例句：The government attempted to russify the schools by imposing Russian language and culture.  政府试图通过强加俄语和文化来使学校俄化。

例句：He decided to russify his novel for a wider Russian audience. 他决定将他的小说俄化，以吸引更广泛的俄罗斯读者。


近义词：sovietize,  russianize


反义词：westernize, americanize


词性:动词
