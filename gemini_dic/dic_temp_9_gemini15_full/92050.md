英文解析：Hyperpigmentation is a condition characterized by darkened patches of skin.  It occurs when an area of skin produces more melanin than usual.  This excess melanin can result from various factors, including sun exposure, hormonal changes, inflammation, or certain medications.  The appearance of hyperpigmentation varies, ranging from light brown to dark brown or even black spots or patches.
