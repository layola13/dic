evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ | adj. | 逃避的；闪烁其词的；回避的 | tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding indirectly.  |例句：He gave an evasive answer to my question. 他对我的问题闪烁其词。|例句：Her evasive tactics prevented us from getting the information we needed.  她的回避策略使我们无法获得所需信息。|近义词：evasive, elusive, ambiguous, equivocal |反义词：direct, frank, forthright, straightforward | 形容词

词源:
The word "evasive" comes from the verb "evade," which originates from the Old French word "evader," meaning "to escape, avoid."  The suffix "-ive" is added to form the adjective, signifying a tendency or quality of evading.
