distrib:/ˈdɪstrɪb/| n. v.|分配；分发；分布|The act of distributing something; the way in which something is distributed.|例句：The distribution of wealth is uneven. 财富分配不均。|近义词：allocation, apportionment, dispersal|反义词：concentration, centralization


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company distributes its products worldwide. = 这家公司在全球范围内分销其产品。
He distributes flyers around the neighborhood. = 他在附近散发传单。


一般过去时:
They distributed food to the victims of the flood. = 他们向洪灾受害者分发了食物。
The teacher distributed the exam papers. = 老师分发了试卷。


现在完成时:
The company has distributed over a million copies of its new book. = 这家公司已经分发了超过一百万册新书。
She has distributed her time effectively among her various projects. = 她有效地分配了她在各个项目上的时间。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had distributed the agenda to all participants. = 会议之前，他们已将议程分发给所有与会者。
By the end of the day, she had distributed all the gifts. = 到一天结束时，她已经分发了所有的礼物。


将来时:
We will distribute the profits among the shareholders. = 我们将把利润分配给股东。
The organization will distribute aid to the affected areas. = 该组织将向受灾地区提供援助。


虚拟语气:
If the company distributed its products more efficiently, it would make more profit. = 如果该公司更高效地分销产品，它将获得更多利润。
I wish they had distributed the information earlier. = 我希望他们早点分发信息。


被动语态:
The leaflets were distributed throughout the city. = 传单在全城分发。
The new software will be distributed to all employees next week. = 新软件将于下周分发给所有员工。


疑问句:
How will the resources be distributed? = 资源将如何分配？
Who is responsible for distributing the materials? = 谁负责分发材料？


否定句:
The company did not distribute its products to that region. = 该公司没有向该地区分销其产品。
He hasn't distributed the questionnaires yet. = 他还没分发问卷。


PHRASE->
distribution center 分销中心
