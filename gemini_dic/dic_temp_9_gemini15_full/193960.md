self-evidencing: /ˌsɛlfˈɛvɪdən(t)sɪŋ/ | adj. | 不证自明的，显而易见的 |  Clearly true and requiring no proof or explanation.  The evidence is inherent within the statement or situation itself. | Example: The rising sun is a self-evidencing fact.  日出是不证自明的事实。The absurdity of the plan was self-evidencing.  这个计划的荒谬之处显而易见。| 近义词: obvious, apparent, manifest, clear, undeniable | 反义词: obscure, ambiguous, debatable, questionable |  词性: 形容词

词源:
