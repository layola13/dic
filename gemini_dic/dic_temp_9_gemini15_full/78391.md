get-across:/ˌɡɛt əˈkrɒs/ | vt. |使理解；使清楚明白；传达；越过 |to successfully communicate an idea or message |例句：I tried to get my point across, but he just wouldn't listen. 我试图让他明白我的观点，但他就是不听。  例句：The bridge is too narrow for a large truck to get across. 这座桥太窄，大型卡车过不去。|近义词：convey, communicate, transmit |反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets his ideas across very well. = 他很擅长表达自己的想法。
The teacher gets the lesson across clearly. = 老师把课程讲得很清楚。


一般过去时:
She got her message across to the audience. = 她成功地向观众传达了她的信息。
The speaker failed to get his point across. = 演讲者未能成功表达他的观点。


现在完成时:
I have finally got my point across to him. = 我终于让他明白了我的观点。
We have gotten the message across through various channels. = 我们已经通过各种渠道传达了信息。


过去完成时:
He had got his point across before the meeting ended. = 会议结束前他已经表达了他的观点。
By then, she had already gotten the news across to her family. = 到那时，她已经把消息告诉了她的家人。


将来时:
I will get my proposal across to the board tomorrow. = 我明天将把我的提案提交给董事会。
The company will get the new product across to the target market. = 公司将把新产品推向目标市场。


虚拟语气:
If I could get my ideas across, I would feel much better. = 如果我能表达我的想法，我会感觉好得多。
I wish I had gotten my point across earlier. = 我希望我早点表达我的观点。


被动语态:
The message was successfully got across to the public. = 信息成功地传达给了公众。
The information needs to be got across effectively. = 信息需要有效地传达。


疑问句:
Did you get your message across? = 你成功地传达你的信息了吗？
Will the company get its new strategy across to its employees? = 公司能否将其新战略传达给员工？


否定句:
I couldn't get my point across. = 我无法表达我的观点。
He didn't get the message across clearly. = 他没有把信息表达清楚。


PHRASE->
get across to = 传达给…
get across the message = 传达信息
get across the point = 表达观点
get across the idea = 传达想法
get across the difficulties = 克服困难
