capful:/ˈkæpfʊl/| n.|满杯的量；一满杯|The amount that fills a cap.  It often refers to a bottle cap or a container's lid.|例句：Add a capful of bleach to the washing machine.  = 在洗衣机里加入一瓶盖漂白剂。

例句：She took a capful of medicine. = 她喝了一瓶盖的药。


近义词：a cap's worth, a spoonful(if referring to a small amount)


反义词：无


词性：名词
