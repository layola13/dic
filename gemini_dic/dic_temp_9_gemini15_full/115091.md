manganate:/ˈmæŋɡənət/| n. |锰酸盐|A salt or ester of manganic acid (H₂MnO₄). Manganates are typically dark green in color and are strong oxidizing agents.  They are used in various industrial applications, including in the production of certain pigments and as oxidizing agents in chemical reactions.| Example: Potassium manganate(VI) is a dark green compound used in some chemical processes. 钾锰酸盐（VI）是一种深绿色的化合物，用于某些化学过程中。|近义词:permanganate (though chemically distinct)|反义词: none specifically.
