fawner:/ˈfɔːnər/ | n. | 谄媚者，奉承者 | A person who fawns; someone who tries to win favor from influential people by flattery.  | Example: He was a fawner, always seeking the approval of his superiors. 他是个谄媚者，总是寻求上司的认可。|近义词:flatterer, sycophant, yes-man | 反义词:critic, opponent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog fawns over its owner. = 这只狗对它的主人很谄媚。
He fawns on the boss to get a promotion. = 他奉承老板以求升职。


一般过去时:
She fawned over the celebrity at the party. = 她在派对上对那位名人献尽殷勤。
He fawned on the rich businessman hoping for an investment. = 他奉承那位富有的商人，希望能得到投资。


现在完成时:
He has fawned over his superiors for years. = 他多年来一直奉承他的上司。
She has fawned on every influential person she's met. = 她逢人便拍马屁。


过去完成时:
He had fawned over the director for months before getting the part. = 在得到这个角色之前，他奉承了导演几个月。
They had fawned over the potential investor before he finally agreed to fund their project. = 在这位潜在投资者最终同意为他们的项目提供资金之前，他们一直对他阿谀奉承。


将来时:
She will fawn over the new CEO at the upcoming meeting. = 她将在即将召开的会议上讨好新任CEO。
He will fawn on his clients to secure a large contract. = 他将奉承他的客户以确保获得一份大合同。


虚拟语气:
If he fawned less, people might respect him more. = 如果他不那么谄媚，人们可能会更尊重他。
I wish he wouldn't fawn over everyone he meets. = 我希望他不必逢人就拍马屁。


被动语态:
The new employee was fawned over by the senior managers. = 新员工受到了高级经理的奉承。
The politician was constantly fawned upon by his supporters. = 这位政治家不断受到支持者的奉承。


疑问句:
Does he always fawn over powerful people? = 他总是奉承有权势的人吗？
Why does she fawn over everyone she meets? = 为什么她逢人就拍马屁？


否定句:
He didn't fawn over the critic, even though she was powerful. = 即使这位评论家很有影响力，他也没有奉承她。
She didn't fawn on the boss; she earned her promotion through hard work. = 她没有奉承老板；她是通过努力工作获得晋升的。


PHRASE->
fawn over someone = 讨好某人
fawn upon someone = 奉承某人
fawn all over someone = 极其谄媚某人
fawn on someone = 巴结某人
fawn to someone = 向某人献殷勤
