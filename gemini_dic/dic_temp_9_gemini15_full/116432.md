matchable:/ˈmætʃəbəl/| adj.| 可匹配的，可比较的 | Capable of being matched; comparable.  Something that is matchable can be paired or compared with something else because they share similar qualities or characteristics. |  例句：The two samples are matchable in terms of color and texture. 这两个样品在颜色和质地上是匹配的。

例句：These fabrics are almost matchable, but there's a slight difference in shade.  这些布料几乎可以匹配，但色调略有不同。


近义词：comparable, compatible, alike, similar

反义词：incomparable, incompatible, dissimilar, different

词性：形容词
