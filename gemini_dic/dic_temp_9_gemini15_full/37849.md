competitively:/kəmˈpɛtətɪvli/ | adv. | 有竞争力地；竞争性地 | In a competitive manner; striving to surpass others.  | 例句：They priced their products competitively to gain market share. 他们以具有竞争力的价格定价产品以获得市场份额。

例句：She played competitively throughout her career. 她职业生涯中一直都在竞技比赛。


近义词：contendingly, aggressively, ambitiously

反义词：uncompetitively, passively, unambitiously


词性：副词
