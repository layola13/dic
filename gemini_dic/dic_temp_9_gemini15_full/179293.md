redleg:/ˈrɛdˌlɛɡ/| n.|红腿(动物);患红腿病的人|A redleg is a person or animal with red legs. The term is most commonly used to refer to certain species of frogs and other amphibians with reddish legs, or to people suffering from a condition known as "redleg" (a bacterial infection).|例句：The researcher observed several redlegs in the swamp. 研究人员在沼泽地观察到几只红腿动物。  The doctor diagnosed the patient with redleg. 医生诊断病人患有红腿病。|近义词：amphibian (for the animal sense), erysipelas (for the disease sense)|反义词：无|名词
