corroders:/kəˈroʊdərz/| n.|腐蚀剂；腐蚀物|Substances or agents that cause corrosion.|The acid rain acted as corroders on the ancient statues.|近义词：erodents, corrosives|反义词：protectors, preservatives|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acid corrodes the metal. = 酸腐蚀金属。
These chemicals corrode the pipes. = 这些化学物质腐蚀管道。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
