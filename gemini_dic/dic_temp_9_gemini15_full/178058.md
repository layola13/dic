reclassify:/ˌriːˈklæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|重新分类，重新归类 |To classify something again, often using a different system or criteria.  |例句：The government decided to reclassify the drug as a controlled substance.  政府决定将该药物重新归类为管制药品。

例句：The librarian reclassified the books according to the Dewey Decimal System. 图书管理员根据杜威十进制系统重新对书籍进行了分类。


近义词：re-categorize, recategorise, reassign, reorganize


反义词：classify (in the original way)


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reclassify the data annually. = 他们每年对数据进行重新分类。
The company reclassifies its employees based on performance. = 公司根据业绩对员工进行重新分类。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
