go-at:/ɡoʊ æt/| phrasal verb|猛攻；努力尝试；着手处理|To make a determined effort to do something difficult or challenging; to attack or assault vigorously.|例句：He went at the problem with great energy. 他精力充沛地着手解决这个问题。|例句：The soldiers went at the enemy with fierce determination. 士兵们以强烈的决心猛攻敌人。|近义词：attack, tackle, assault, strive for|反义词：avoid, neglect, ignore|词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They go at their work with enthusiasm every day. = 他们每天都热情地投入工作。
He goes at the puzzle, trying different approaches. = 他尝试不同的方法来解这个谜题。

一般过去时:
She went at the mountain with determination, finally reaching the summit. = 她决心登上那座山，最终到达了山顶。
They went at the project with renewed vigor after the setback. = 挫折之后，他们以新的活力投入项目。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

He had gone at the painting many times, refining his techniques. = 他多次尝试作画，不断完善自己的技巧。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:  (This phrasal verb is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
How will they go at the negotiations? = 他们将如何进行谈判？
Will he go at the project alone or with a team? = 他会独自进行这个项目还是与团队一起进行？


否定句:
He didn't go at the problem effectively. = 他没有有效地解决这个问题。
They didn't go at the task with the necessary enthusiasm. = 他们没有以必要的热情投入工作。
