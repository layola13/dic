name-calling: /ˈneɪmˌkɔːlɪŋ/ | n. |辱骂；人身攻击 | The act of insulting someone by using offensive or derogatory names.  It is a form of verbal abuse and often involves stereotyping or prejudice. | 例句：The children engaged in name-calling during the argument. 孩子们在争吵中互相辱骂。

例句：He was subjected to relentless name-calling online. 他在网上遭受了无情的辱骂。


近义词：insulting, verbal abuse, abuse, taunting, heckling, vilification

反义词：praise, compliment, commendation


词性：名词
