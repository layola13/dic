It can manifest in various ways, from procrastination to a general lack of ambition.  | Example: His indolence prevented him from finishing the project on time.  他的懒惰使他无法按时完成项目。 |近义词：laziness, sloth, lethargy, idleness |反义词：diligence, industry, activity, energy |名词
