comfit:/ˈkɒmfɪt/| n.|蜜饯；糖衣果仁|A comfit is a small, sweet confection, typically a sugared nut or seed.  Comfits are made by coating a core ingredient (such as nuts, seeds, or spices) with multiple layers of sugar syrup. This process creates a hard, candy-like coating. They have a long history, dating back to ancient times.|Example: The dessert included a variety of comfits, including sugared almonds and ginger.|近义词：candy, sweetmeat|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could include unsweetened food or bitter substances.|名词
