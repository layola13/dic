robbery:/ˈrɒbəri/ /ˈrɑːbəri/| n. |抢劫；盗窃|The unlawful taking of property from a person or place by force or threat of force.|例句：The bank robbery was a terrifying experience for the customers.  银行抢劫对顾客来说是一次可怕的经历。

例句：Police are investigating the robbery of a local jewelry store. 警方正在调查当地一家珠宝店的抢劫案。


近义词：theft, burglary, heist, hold-up|反义词：donation, gift
