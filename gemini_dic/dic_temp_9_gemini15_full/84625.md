hand-and-foot:/ˌhænd ænd ˈfʊt/| adj.|  竭尽全力地；尽心尽力地； |Completely and totally; with every possible effort.  This phrase describes someone who gives their all, doing everything possible to help or complete a task. |例句：She helped me hand-and-foot with the project. = 她尽心尽力地帮我完成了这个项目。  She waited on him hand and foot. = 她对他照顾得无微不至。|近义词：wholeheartedly, completely, thoroughly, diligently|反义词：negligently, carelessly, halfheartedly|形容词
