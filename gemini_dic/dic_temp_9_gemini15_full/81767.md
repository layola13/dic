gregarious:/ɡrɪˈɡɛəriəs/ | adj. | 好交际的；群居的；爱社交的 |  Fond of company; sociable; tending to form groups.  |  He's a gregarious person and enjoys parties. 他是一个好交际的人，喜欢参加聚会。She's a gregarious bird that lives in flocks.  她是一种群居的鸟类，成群生活。 | sociable, outgoing, convivial | solitary, reclusive, introverted | adj.
