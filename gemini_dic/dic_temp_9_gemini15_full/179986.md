refilm:/ˌriːˈfɪlm/| vt.|重拍，重新拍摄|To film (something) again; to make a new film of (something).  This often involves re-shooting scenes, using a different cast, or updating the story.|例句：The director decided to refilm the entire sequence after the initial footage was deemed unsatisfactory.  导演决定在最初的素材被认为不令人满意后重新拍摄整个片段。

例句：They refilmed the movie with a new lead actor.  他们用新的男主角重拍了这部电影。


近义词：reshoot, remake, redo|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to simply leave the original film as is.|词性:动词
