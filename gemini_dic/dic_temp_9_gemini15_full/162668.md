preassign:/ˌpriːəˈsaɪn/| vt.|预先分配，预先指定|To assign (something) beforehand or in advance.  It implies assigning something before it is needed or before a specific event.|例句：The teacher preassigned seats for the students in the classroom. 老师预先为学生分配了教室的座位。

例句：The project manager preassigned tasks to each team member before the project kickoff meeting. 项目经理在项目启动会议之前预先分配了每个团队成员的任务。


近义词：allocate beforehand, predetermine, assign in advance, allot in advance

反义词：postassign (This is not a standard word, but it conveys the opposite meaning)

词性:动词
