It often implies a careful and deliberate process of showing the way.|例句：The museum offered a manuduction to the new exhibit, explaining the historical context and artistic significance.  博物馆为新展览提供了引导，解释了历史背景和艺术意义。 The manuduction of the young apprentice was thorough and patient.  对年轻学徒的指导是彻底而耐心的。|近义词：guidance, conduction, leading, escorting|反义词：abandonment, neglect
