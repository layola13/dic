overbilled:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪld/| adj. v. |被多收费的；多收费|Charged more than the actual cost or amount due.  This describes a situation where someone has been charged more money than they should have been.  The term can refer to a bill, an invoice, or any other form of charge.|例句：I was overbilled for my phone service. = 我的电话费被多收了。

The restaurant overbilled us for the wine. = 餐厅的酒水价格多收了我们的钱。|近义词：overcharged, excessively charged|反义词：underbilled, undercharged|
