monodynamic:/ˌmɒnəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. | 单动力性的，单向的 |  Relating to or characterized by a single driving force or system.  The term is often used in fields like engineering and physics to describe systems with a single source of power or energy.  It contrasts with polydynamic systems which have multiple driving forces. | 例句：The monodynamic system showed a predictable and consistent energy output.  单动力系统显示出可预测且一致的能量输出。

The engine's monodynamic design simplified maintenance.  该发动机的单动力设计简化了维护。


近义词：unidirectional, single-powered, single-source | 反义词：polydynamic, multidynamic
