infarct:/ˈɪnfɑːrkt/ | n. |梗塞；(组织)坏死 | The death of tissue due to lack of blood supply.  This is usually caused by a blockage in an artery.  |Example: A myocardial infarct is a heart attack. 心肌梗塞就是心脏病发作。

The infarct caused significant damage to the brain.  梗塞对大脑造成了严重的损伤。


|近义词：necrosis, ischemia |反义词：reperfusion, healing |词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
An infarct develops slowly in some cases.  在某些情况下，梗塞发展缓慢。
The infarct affects the functionality of the organ. 梗塞影响器官的功能。


一般过去时:
A large infarct occurred in the patient's lung. 病人肺部发生了大面积梗塞。
The doctor diagnosed an infarct after reviewing the scans. 医生在查看扫描结果后诊断为梗塞。


现在完成时:
Several infarcts have occurred in this region recently. 最近该地区发生了好几起梗塞。
The patient has experienced a significant infarct. 病人经历了一次严重的梗塞。


过去完成时:
The patient had already suffered a silent infarct before the symptoms appeared. 在症状出现之前，病人已经遭受过一次无声的梗塞。
The doctor explained that the patient had experienced a series of small infarcts. 医生解释说，病人经历了一系列的小梗塞。


将来时:
The doctor predicts that the patient will develop further infarcts if the condition is not treated. 如果不治疗，医生预测病人将会发展出进一步的梗塞。
If he continues his current lifestyle, he will likely suffer a future infarct. 如果他继续目前的生活方式，他很可能会在将来遭受梗塞。


虚拟语气:
If the blood supply had been restored sooner, the infarct might have been smaller. 如果血液供应能够更快地恢复，梗塞可能就会更小。
It is hypothetical that the infarct would have been prevented with earlier intervention. 假设早期干预可以预防梗塞。


被动语态:
The area was severely damaged by the infarct. 该区域受到了梗塞的严重破坏。
The infarct was confirmed by a subsequent MRI scan. 随后的核磁共振扫描证实了梗塞。


疑问句:
What caused the infarct? 导致梗塞的原因是什么？

否定句:
