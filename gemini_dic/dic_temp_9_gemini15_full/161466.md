potashery:/ˈpɒtæʃəri/| n. |粗钾肥厂；制碱厂|A potashery is a factory or plant where potash, a potassium-rich fertilizer, is produced or processed.  It often involves the extraction and refining of potassium salts from natural sources like mines or brine. |The old potashery, once a bustling center of industry, now stands silent and abandoned.  古老的制碱厂，曾经是繁忙的工业中心，现在却静悄悄地废弃了。|近义词：potash works, fertilizer plant |反义词：N/A
