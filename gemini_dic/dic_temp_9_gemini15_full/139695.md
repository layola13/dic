oceanias:/ˌoʊʃiˈeɪniəz/| n.|大洋洲（复数）|Oceania is a vast region encompassing numerous islands and countries in the central and southwestern Pacific Ocean.  It's often considered a continent, although its geographical definition is debated.  The term "Oceania" is used to refer to the collective group of islands and countries, rather than a single entity.|例句：The islands of Oceania boast incredible biodiversity. 大洋洲的岛屿拥有令人难以置信的生物多样性。  The history of Oceania is rich and complex. 大洋洲的历史丰富而复杂。|近义词：Pacific Islands, South Pacific|反义词：无|名词
