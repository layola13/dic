overbuild:/ˈoʊvərˌbɪld/| vt.|过度建设；过度建造|To build something to a greater extent than is necessary or desirable.  It often implies unnecessary expense, complexity, or capacity. |例句：They overbuilt the shopping mall, resulting in many empty stores. 他们过度建造了购物中心，导致许多商店空置。|例句：The city overbuilt its infrastructure during the boom years, leading to significant debt.  在经济繁荣时期，该市基础设施建设过度，导致巨额债务。|近义词：overconstruct, overdevelop|反义词：underbuild, underdevelop|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overbuild their houses with unnecessary features. = 他们为房子增加了不必要的设施而过度建设。
The company overbuilds its products to meet anticipated demand. = 公司过度生产产品以满足预期的需求。

一般过去时:
The developers overbuilt the housing complex, leading to vacancies. = 开发商过度建造了住宅小区，导致空置。
They overbuilt the bridge, making it unnecessarily strong and expensive. = 他们过度建造了桥梁，使其变得不必要地坚固和昂贵。

现在完成时:
The government has overbuilt many public facilities in recent years. = 政府近年来过度建设了许多公共设施。
They have overbuilt their capacity, leading to lower profits. = 他们过度建设产能，导致利润下降。

过去完成时:
They had overbuilt the factory before the market demand decreased. = 在市场需求下降之前，他们已经过度建设了工厂。
The city had overbuilt its transportation system before the population declined. = 在人口减少之前，该市已经过度建设了交通系统。

将来时:
They will overbuild the new airport if they don't carefully plan. = 如果他们不仔细规划，他们将会过度建设新机场。
The company will overbuild its production line, leading to waste. = 公司将过度建设生产线，导致浪费。

虚拟语气:
If they had not overbuilt the stadium, they wouldn't have so much empty space. = 如果他们没有过度建设体育馆，就不会有这么多的空位。
I wish they hadn't overbuilt the highway; it's now underutilized. = 我希望他们没有过度建设高速公路；它现在使用不足。

被动语态:
The project was overbuilt due to poor planning. = 由于规划不当，该项目被过度建设。
The office building was overbuilt, resulting in high operating costs. = 办公楼被过度建设，导致运营成本高昂。

疑问句:
Was the bridge overbuilt? = 这座桥是否过度建造了？
Did they overbuild the factory to accommodate future growth? = 他们是否过度建设了工厂以适应未来的增长？

否定句:
They did not overbuild the small apartment complex. = 他们没有过度建设小型公寓楼。
The company did not overbuild its production capacity this year. = 公司今年没有过度建设其生产能力。

词源:
The word "overbuild" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "build."  Its meaning is a direct extension of the base word, signifying the act of building beyond what is needed or appropriate.
