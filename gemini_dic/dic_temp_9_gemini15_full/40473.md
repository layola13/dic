contributes:/kənˈtrɪbjuːts/ | vt. |贡献，促成 | to give something, especially money, in order to help achieve or provide something:  |例句：He contributes regularly to the charity. 他定期向慈善机构捐款。|例句：The new bridge will greatly contribute to the economic development of the area. 新桥将极大地促进该地区经济发展。|近义词：donate, give, offer, assist |反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contributes to the local newspaper. = 他为当地报纸撰稿。
She contributes to the team's success. = 她为团队的成功做出了贡献。

一般过去时:
They contributed generously to the fundraising campaign. = 他们慷慨地捐款给筹款活动。
He contributed a chapter to the book. = 他为这本书贡献了一章。

现在完成时:
She has contributed significantly to the field of medicine. = 她对医学领域做出了重大贡献。
He has contributed articles to several magazines. = 他已向几家杂志投稿。

过去完成时:
They had contributed all they could before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经尽力贡献了。
She had contributed to the project for many years before retiring. = 她在退休前为这个项目贡献了很多年。

将来时:
We will contribute to the discussion tomorrow. = 我们明天将参与讨论。
He will contribute his expertise to the project. = 他将把他的专业知识贡献给该项目。

虚拟语气:
If he contributed more, the project would be completed sooner. = 如果他贡献更多，项目就能更快完成。
I wish he had contributed more to the team effort. = 我希望他为团队的努力贡献更多。

被动语态:
A significant amount was contributed by anonymous donors. = 一笔可观的款项是由匿名捐赠者捐赠的。
Much progress has been contributed to by dedicated researchers. = 许多进步是由敬业的研究人员做出的。

疑问句:
How much did you contribute to the cause? = 你为这项事业贡献了多少？
Will you contribute your ideas to the meeting? = 你会在会议上提出你的想法吗？

否定句:
He did not contribute to the conversation. = 他没有参与谈话。
She didn't contribute anything to the solution. = 她对解决问题没有任何贡献。


PHRASE->
contribute to = 促成；导致
contribute something to something = 向…贡献…
contribute towards = 捐助
make a contribution to = 对…做出贡献
contributing factor = 促成因素
