drawhead: /ˈdrɔːhed/ | n. |拉丝头，拉丝模头 | A drawhead is a tool or die used in wire drawing to reduce the diameter of a wire. It typically has a conical shape with a precisely controlled reduction in diameter to shape the wire.  | Example: The drawhead was carefully adjusted to achieve the desired wire diameter.  拉丝模头被仔细调整以达到所需的线径。 |近义词:drawing die, wire die |反义词: N/A
