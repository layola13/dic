computer-literate:/kəmˈpjuːtərˌlɪtərət/| adj.|懂电脑的，精通计算机的|Having a good understanding of computers and their uses.|She is computer-literate and can easily troubleshoot software issues. 她懂电脑，能轻松排除软件问题。|tech-savvy, computer-proficient|computer-illiterate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is computer-literate. = 他懂电脑。
She is computer-literate and can use many software programs. = 她懂电脑，可以使用很多软件程序。

一般过去时:
He was computer-literate even as a child. = 他从小就懂电脑。
My grandfather wasn't computer-literate until a few years ago. = 我爷爷直到几年前才开始懂电脑。

现在完成时:
She has become computer-literate in recent years. = 她近年来变得懂电脑了。
They have been computer-literate for many years. = 他们懂电脑很多年了。

过去完成时:
I had been computer-literate for five years before I got this job. = 我在这份工作之前已经懂电脑五年了。
Before the course, she hadn't been very computer-literate. = 在上课之前，她不太懂电脑。

将来时:
He will be computer-literate by the end of the course. = 他到课程结束时就会懂电脑了。
They will become more computer-literate with practice. = 他们通过练习会变得更懂电脑。

虚拟语气:
If I were computer-literate, I would build my own website. = 如果我懂电脑，我会自己建网站。
I wish I were computer-literate so I could fix my computer. = 我希望我懂电脑，这样我就能修好我的电脑了。

被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be used.)
The training program aims to make students computer-literate. = 培训项目旨在让学生掌握计算机知识。
The employees are expected to be computer-literate. = 员工应该精通电脑。


疑问句:
Is he computer-literate? = 他懂电脑吗？
Are you computer-literate enough for this job? = 你懂电脑够胜任这份工作吗？


否定句:
He is not computer-literate. = 他不懂电脑。
Many elderly people are not computer-literate. = 许多老年人不懂电脑。
