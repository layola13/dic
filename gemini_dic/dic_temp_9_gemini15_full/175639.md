rannel:/ˈrænəl/| n.|小河, 小溪, 浅滩|A small stream or channel, especially one that is shallow and narrow; a sandbank or shallow area in a river or sea.|The rannel was barely deep enough to float a small boat.  这条小河水浅，几乎不足以让小船漂浮。|The ship ran aground on a sand rannel. 船搁浅在一处沙洲上。|近义词：brook, rill, creek, stream|反义词：ocean, sea
