eradicating: /ɪˈrædɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 根除；消灭；扑灭 |  The act of removing or destroying something completely, especially something undesirable, such as a disease, pest, or harmful practice.  |

例句：

The government is committed to eradicating poverty. = 政府致力于消除贫困。

Scientists are working hard to eradicate this deadly disease. = 科学家们正在努力根除这种致命的疾病。


近义词：eliminating, destroying, removing, exterminating, abolishing

反义词：spreading, propagating, fostering, cultivating


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are eradicating weeds from the garden. = 他们在花园里除草。
The company is eradicating harmful chemicals from its products. = 公司正在从其产品中清除有害化学物质。


一般过去时:
They eradicated the disease from the village. = 他们从村庄根除了这种疾病。
The farmer eradicated the pests from his crops. = 农民根除了他庄稼上的害虫。


现在完成时:
They have eradicated polio from most parts of the world. = 他们已经从世界大部分地区根除了小儿麻痹症。
We have eradicated most of the bugs in the software. = 我们已经消除了软件中的大部分错误。


过去完成时:
They had eradicated the problem before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经解决了这个问题。
The team had eradicated all traces of the virus. = 该团队已经清除病毒的所有痕迹。


将来时:
They will eradicate the invasive species from the ecosystem. = 他们将从生态系统中清除入侵物种。
The government will eradicate corruption within the police force. = 政府将清除警队内部的腐败。


虚拟语气:
If we acted now, we could eradicate hunger in our community. = 如果我们现在采取行动，我们就能消除我们社区的饥饿。
I wish they would eradicate the noise pollution. = 我希望他们能消除噪音污染。


被动语态:
The disease was eradicated in the 1980s. = 这种疾病在20世纪80年代被根除了。
The problem is being eradicated gradually. = 这个问题正在逐渐被解决。


疑问句:
Can we eradicate this social injustice? = 我们能消除这种社会不公吗？
How will they eradicate the harmful effects of pollution? = 他们将如何消除污染的有害影响？


否定句:
They haven't eradicated the problem yet. = 他们还没有解决这个问题。
We cannot eradicate poverty overnight. = 我们无法一夜之间消除贫困。
