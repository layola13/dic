observational:/ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃənəl/|adj.|观察的；观察性的；基于观察的|relating to or based on observation.  The study used observational data to reach its conclusions.  This adjective describes something that is related to or based on the act of observing something.|例句：The study relied on observational data rather than experimental results. 这项研究依赖于观察数据而不是实验结果。

例句：His observational skills were crucial to solving the mystery.  他的观察能力对解开这个谜团至关重要。


近义词：empirical, experimental (in some contexts), based on observation


反义词：theoretical, hypothetical, conjectural


词源：源自observe (观察) + -ational (形容词后缀)  The word "observational" is derived from the verb "observe," meaning to watch, notice, or perceive something carefully. The suffix "-ational" transforms the verb into an adjective, signifying "relating to" or "characterized by" observation.
