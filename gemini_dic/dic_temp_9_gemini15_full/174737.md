rabato:/ræˈbɑːtoʊ/| n. |褶边领; 衬领|A stiff, pleated linen collar worn by men and women in the 16th and 17th centuries.  It was often starched and decorated.  |例句：The portrait shows the nobleman wearing a magnificent, lace-trimmed rabato. 这幅肖像画显示这位贵族穿着一件华丽的、饰有蕾丝的褶边领。|近义词：ruff, collar, neck ruff|反义词：None specifically;  antonyms relate to the absence of a rabato (e.g., open neck)|名词
