hostile:/ˈhɒstaɪl/ /ˈhɑːstaɪl/| adj.|敌对的；不友好的；敌意的 |Showing strong opposition or dislike; unfriendly.  |例句：The hostile crowd threw stones at the police.  敌对的人群向警察扔石头。

例句：He received a hostile reception from the audience. 他受到了观众的冷淡接待。


近义词：antagonistic, unfriendly, aggressive, belligerent, inimical

反义词：friendly, amicable, welcoming, hospitable


词源：From Old French *hostil, from Latin hostilis "of or belonging to an enemy," from hostis "enemy."  The word's development reflects the long history of warfare and conflict in human societies.
