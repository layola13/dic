corianders:/ˈkɒriændəz/| n. |芫荽, 香菜|Corianders are the seeds of the coriander plant, used as a spice.  They have a distinctive citrusy flavor.| 例句：The stew was flavored with coriander and cumin. 这道炖菜用芫荽和孜然调味。| 例句：She added a sprinkle of fresh coriander to the dish. 她往菜里撒了一点新鲜的芫荽。|近义词：cilantro (leaves), parsley|反义词：无
