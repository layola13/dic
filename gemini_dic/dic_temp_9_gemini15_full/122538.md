model:/ˈmɒdl/ /ˈmɑːdl/| n. |模型；模式；模范；榜样；模特儿|A model is a small copy of something, often used to show how something will look or work. It can also refer to a person who poses for artists or photographers, or a person or thing that serves as an example to be imitated.|例句：The architect presented a model of the new building. 建筑师展示了新建筑的模型。|例句：She's a successful model who has worked for many top designers. 她是一位成功的模特，曾为许多顶级设计师工作。|近义词：prototype, pattern, exemplar, ideal|反义词：original, real, prototype (in some contexts)
