intervenes:/ˌɪntərˈviːnz/| v.|干预；介入；调停|To come between so as to prevent or alter a result or course of events; to interfere.  |例句：The government intervened to prevent a strike. 政府介入以阻止罢工。|例句：The teacher intervened when the children started fighting. 当孩子们开始打架时，老师进行了干预。|近义词：interfere, mediate, arbitrate|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government intervenes in the economy regularly. = 政府定期干预经济。
He intervenes in his children's arguments frequently. = 他经常干预孩子们的争吵。


一般过去时:
The United Nations intervened in the conflict. = 联合国介入冲突。
She intervened to stop the fight. = 她介入阻止了这场争斗。


现在完成时:
The central bank has intervened several times this year. = 央行今年已经介入几次了。
He has intervened on my behalf many times. = 他多次为我出面。


过去完成时:
The army had intervened before the situation escalated. = 在局势升级之前，军队已经介入。
They had intervened to prevent a riot. = 他们已经介入以防止暴乱。


将来时:
The international community will intervene if necessary. = 如果必要，国际社会将介入。
I will intervene if the situation gets out of hand. = 如果情况失控，我会介入。


虚拟语气:
If the doctor had intervened earlier, the patient might have survived. = 如果医生早点介入，病人可能就活下来了。
It would have been better if he hadn't intervened. = 如果他没有介入就好了。


被动语态:
The dispute was intervened by a third party. = 纠纷由第三方进行了调解。
The situation was intervened in by the police. = 警方向事件进行了介入。


疑问句:
Will the government intervene in this matter? = 政府会介入此事吗？
Did anyone intervene during the argument? = 在争吵中有人介入吗？


否定句:
The government did not intervene in the crisis. = 政府没有介入这场危机。
He chose not to intervene in their personal affairs. = 他选择不干预他们的私事。


PHRASE->
intervene in = 介入…
intervene on behalf of = 代表…出面
intervene to prevent = 介入以阻止
intervene with = 向…干预
intervene in the affairs of = 干涉…的事务
