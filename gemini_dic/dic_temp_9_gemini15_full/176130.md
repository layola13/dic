razorbills:/ˈreɪzərˌbɪlz/ | n. | 海雀 | Razorbill is a type of auk, a seabird characterized by its sharply pointed bill, hence the name. They are found in the North Atlantic Ocean and are known for their diving abilities to catch fish.  | Example:  The razorbill skillfully dived into the ocean to catch a fish.  海雀熟练地潜入海中捕鱼。
