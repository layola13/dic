complained:/kəmˈpleɪnd/| v.|抱怨，投诉 | to express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something. |例句：He complained about the noise. 他抱怨噪音。|例句：She complained to the manager about the poor service. 她向经理投诉糟糕的服务。|近义词：grumbled, protested, grumbled, whined |反义词：praised, complimented


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complains about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
They complain constantly about their jobs. = 他们不断抱怨他们的工作。


一般过去时:
She complained to the teacher about the unfair test. = 她向老师抱怨考试不公平。
We complained loudly about the long wait. = 我们大声抱怨漫长的等待。


现在完成时:
He has complained several times about the broken elevator. = 他已经多次抱怨电梯坏了。
They have complained repeatedly about the lack of support. = 他们反复抱怨缺乏支持。


过去完成时:
She had complained many times before they finally fixed the problem. = 在他们最终解决问题之前，她已经抱怨过很多次了。
He had complained bitterly about his treatment before he resigned. = 他辞职前曾苦苦抱怨自己的待遇。


将来时:
I will complain to the authorities if the problem isn't fixed. = 如果问题不解决，我会向有关部门投诉。
They will complain again if they don't receive a refund. = 如果他们没有收到退款，他们会再次投诉。


虚拟语气:
I would complain if I were in your position. = 如果我处在你的位置，我会抱怨。
If she had complained sooner, the issue might have been resolved earlier. = 如果她早点抱怨，这个问题可能早就解决了。


被动语态:
The poor service was complained about by many customers. = 许多顾客都抱怨服务差。
The noise was complained about by the residents. = 噪音受到了居民的抱怨。


疑问句:
Did he complain about anything? = 他抱怨了什么吗？
What did she complain about? = 她抱怨了什么？


否定句:
He didn't complain about the food. = 他没有抱怨食物。
They didn't complain at all. = 他们根本没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
complain about = 抱怨…
complain to = 向…投诉
lodge a complaint = 正式投诉
make a complaint = 提出投诉
have a complaint = 有意见/不满
