plug away:/plʌɡ əˈweɪ/| v.| 坚持不懈地工作；努力地做 | To work persistently and steadily at something, often despite difficulties.  |例句：He plugged away at his essay until he finished it. 他坚持不懈地写论文直到完成。

例句：She plugged away at the problem for hours before finding a solution. 她花了几个小时努力解决这个问题才找到答案。


近义词：persevere, persist, continue, strive


反义词：give up, quit, abandon, cease


词性:动词
