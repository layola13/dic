/ˌɛlˈbiːˈdʌbljuː/|/ˈɛlbiːˈdʌbljuː/ | n. |腿球(板球)|Leg before wicket (LBW) in cricket is a method of dismissing a batsman.  It occurs when the ball would have hit the wickets (the three stumps behind the batsman), but the batsman's leg is obstructing the ball's path. The umpire then declares the batsman "leg before wicket" (LBW), and the batsman is out.|The batsman was given LBW.  击球员被判腿球出局。|  out, bowled |  not out | 名词
