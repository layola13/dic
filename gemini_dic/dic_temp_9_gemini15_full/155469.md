It refers to the characteristics of experience that make it seem real and immediate, often emphasizing the subjective and qualitative aspects of consciousness.  | Example: The phenomenality of the Northern Lights captivated the onlookers. 北极光的现象性迷住了旁观者。The phenomenality of her singing voice was undeniable. 她歌声的显著性是不可否认的。 | 近义词：remarkableness, extraordinariness, significance | 反义词：ordinariness, insignificance | 名词
