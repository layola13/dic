passing-on:/ˈpæsɪŋ ɔːn/| v.|传递，去世，继续|The act of transferring something to someone else, or the act of dying.|例句：He's passing on his knowledge to his students. 他正在把他的知识传授给他的学生们。|例句：His passing-on was a great loss to the community. 他的去世是社区的一大损失。|近义词：transmitting, handing down, bequeathing, decease, demise|反义词：receiving, keeping, retaining, living, surviving|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He passes on his wisdom to his children. = 他把他的智慧传给他的孩子们。
They pass on the information to their colleagues. = 他们把信息传递给他们的同事。

一般过去时:
She passed on the secret to me. = 她把秘密告诉我了。
My grandfather passed on last year. = 我的祖父去年去世了。

现在完成时:
He has passed on the family business to his son. = 他已经把家族企业传给了他的儿子。
The news has passed on quickly through the village. = 这个消息很快就在村子里传开了。

过去完成时:
She had passed on the message before I arrived. = 我到达之前，她已经传达了这个信息。
The tradition had passed on for generations. = 这个传统已经传承了几代人。

将来时:
They will pass on their property to their heirs. = 他们将把他们的财产留给他们的继承人。
I will pass on this opportunity to someone else. = 我将把这个机会让给别人。

虚拟语气:
If he passed on the information earlier, we would have been prepared. = 如果他早点把信息传递过来，我们就做好准备了。
I wish he hadn't passed on so suddenly. = 我真希望他没有这么突然去世。


被动语态:
The knowledge was passed on through generations. = 知识代代相传。
The message was passed on to the authorities. = 消息已转达给了当局。


疑问句:
Will you pass on the message? = 你会传递这个消息吗？
Did he pass on peacefully? = 他走得安详吗？

否定句:
He didn't pass on the bad news. = 他没有传递坏消息。
She didn't pass on the inheritance. = 她没有继承遗产。
