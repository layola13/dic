curving:/ˈkɜːrvɪŋ/ | adj. |弯曲的，曲线形的 |  Describing something that bends or curves; having a curved shape or form. |例句：The road had a sharp curving bend.  这条路有一个急弯。

The river follows a curving course through the valley. 这条河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。

|近义词：bending, winding, meandering, sinuous |反义词：straight, direct, linear |形容词
