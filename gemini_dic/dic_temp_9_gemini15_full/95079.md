词源:

The word "inaffectation" is formed by adding the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") to the word "affectation."  "Affectation" comes from the Latin word "affectatio," meaning "a striving after," "an attempt to impress," or "artificiality."  Therefore, "inaffectation" literally means "not an affectation" or "the absence of artificiality."
