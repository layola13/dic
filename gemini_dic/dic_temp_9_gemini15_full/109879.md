liability:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. |责任；债务；倾向 | The state of being legally responsible for something.  It can refer to legal responsibility for something,  a debt or financial obligation, or an inherent disadvantage or weakness. |例句：The company faces substantial liability for environmental damage. 这家公司面临因环境破坏造成的巨额赔偿责任。

例句：His reckless driving resulted in significant liability for the accident. 他的鲁莽驾驶导致了事故的重大责任。


近义词：responsibility, obligation, debt,  burden, drawback, weakness

反义词：asset, advantage, strength


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company accepts full liability for the product defect. = 公司对产品缺陷承担全部责任。
What is his liability in this case? = 在这种情况下，他的责任是什么？


一般过去时:
Their liability for the accident was proven in court. = 他们的事故责任在法庭上得到证实。
The company's liability ended after the contract expired. = 合同到期后，公司的责任就结束了。


现在完成时:
The government has accepted liability for the disaster relief. = 政府已经承担了救灾责任。
The court has determined their liability for the damages. = 法院已经确定了他们对损害的责任。


过去完成时:
They had already accepted liability before the lawsuit began. = 在诉讼开始之前，他们就已经承担了责任。
The company had fulfilled its liability before the deadline. = 公司在截止日期前履行了其责任。


将来时:
The new law will increase the liability of employers. = 新法律将增加雇主的责任。
They will face significant liability if the project fails. = 如果项目失败，他们将面临巨大的责任。


虚拟语气:
If they had acted responsibly, their liability would have been less. = 如果他们行事负责，他们的责任就会小一些。
I wish the company had accepted liability sooner. = 我希望公司早点承担责任。


被动语态:
Liability for the accident was assigned to the driver. = 事故责任被判给司机。
The damages were covered by the insurance company's liability. = 损失由保险公司的责任承担。


疑问句:
What is the extent of their liability? = 他们的责任范围有多大？
Does the contract define their liability? = 合同是否界定了他们的责任？


否定句:
The company denied any liability for the incident. = 公司否认对该事件有任何责任。
He was found not to have any liability in the matter. = 他被发现在这件事上没有任何责任。


PHRASE->
strict liability = 严格责任
limited liability = 有限责任
product liability = 产品责任
liability insurance = 责任保险
potential liability = 潜在责任


词源:
liability源自于拉丁语“ligare”（to bind），经过古法语“liable”演变而来。  它最初的意思是“被约束的”或“有义务的”，逐渐演变成现代英语中的含义。
