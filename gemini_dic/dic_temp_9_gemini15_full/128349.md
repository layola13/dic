neutralizer:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzər/ | n. | 中和剂，抵消物 | A substance or agent that neutralizes something, especially an acid or alkali.  It can also refer to something that counteracts or cancels out the effect of something else. | Example:  The chemist added a neutralizer to the acid to make it safe.  化学家向酸中加入中和剂使其安全。 | 近义词：counteractant, antidote, inhibitor | 反义词：activator, enhancer
