masalas:/məˈsɑːləz/| n. |香料混合物，咖喱粉|A blend of ground spices used in Indian cooking.  Masalas can vary greatly in their composition depending on the region and specific dish.|例句：The chef added a fragrant masala to the chicken curry.  厨师在鸡肉咖喱中加入了芳香的香料混合物。

例句：This masala blend includes cumin, coriander, and turmeric. 这种香料混合物包括孜然、芫荽和姜黄。
