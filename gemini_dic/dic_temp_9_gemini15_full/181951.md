remarque:/rəˈmɑːrk/ | n. | 备注；评语；谈论；言论 | A remark is a statement or comment.  It can be a brief observation, a critical opinion, or a casual comment. |例句：He made a rude remark about her dress. 他对她衣服做了粗鲁的评论。

例句：The professor's remarks on the essay were helpful. 教授对这篇论文的评语很有帮助。


近义词：comment, observation, statement, note | 反义词：silence


词源：来自法语“remarquer”，意为“to notice, to observe”。  The word ultimately derives from the Old French prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "marquer" (to mark).


用法说明：


remarque作为名词，可以指：

1.简短的评论或陈述：This is a general use, encompassing casual observations to more pointed critiques.

2.正式的评论或意见：This use often implies a more considered and perhaps critical assessment.

3.值得注意的事情：In this case, a remarque is something that stands out or deserves attention.



例句补充：


1.  He made a thoughtful remarque about the painting. 他对这幅画作了意味深长的评论。


2. The critic's remarque was scathing. 批评家的评论非常尖刻。


3.  Her remarque went largely unnoticed.  她的评论基本上没有引起注意。


4. The author included a brief remarque in the book's introduction. 作者在书的引言中添加了一段简短的评语。


5. The detective noted a significant remarque in the victim's apartment.  侦探注意到受害者公寓里一个重要的细节（备注）。



PHRASE->
make a remark: 作出评论
pass a remark:  顺便说一句
remark on something: 对某事发表评论
remark about something:  谈论某事
a telling remark:  意味深长的评论


注意：remarque没有动词形式，其动词形式通常使用动词“remark”。
