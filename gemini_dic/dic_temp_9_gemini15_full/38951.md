confides:/kənˈfaɪdz/| vt.|吐露(秘密)，信赖|to tell someone something secret or personal, trusting them not to repeat it; to have confidence or trust in someone or something. |例句：She confided her troubles to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的烦恼。|例句：I confide in my sister about everything. 我把我的一切都告诉我的姐姐。|近义词：entrust, trust, reveal|反义词：conceal, hide, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confides in his diary every night. = 他每晚都向他的日记倾诉。
She confides her secrets to her mother. = 她向她母亲吐露她的秘密。

一般过去时:
I confided my deepest fears to my therapist. = 我向我的治疗师倾诉了我最深的恐惧。
He confided in his colleague about the project's difficulties. = 他向他的同事倾诉了项目中的困难。

现在完成时:
She has confided in me about her relationship problems. = 她向我倾诉了她的感情问题。
He has confided all his secrets to his wife. = 他已经把他所有的秘密都告诉了他的妻子。

过去完成时:
I had confided in him before, so he knew what to expect. = 我以前曾向他倾诉过，所以他知道该期待什么。
She had confided her plans to her family before announcing them publicly. = 在公开宣布之前，她已经向家人透露了她的计划。

将来时:
I will confide in you about my future plans. = 我会向你倾诉我的未来计划。
She will confide her feelings to him soon. = 她很快就会向他倾诉她的感受。

虚拟语气:
If I had a trusted friend, I would confide in them. = 如果我有一个值得信赖的朋友，我会向他们倾诉。
I wish I could confide in someone about this. = 我希望我能向某人倾诉这件事。

被动语态:
The secret was confided to only a few people. = 这个秘密只被少数人知道。
His worries were confided to his journal. = 他的担忧被倾诉在他的日记里。

疑问句:
In whom do you confide your deepest secrets? = 你向谁倾诉你最深的秘密？
Does she confide in anyone about her problems? = 她会向任何人倾诉她的问题吗？

否定句:
He did not confide in anyone about his illness. = 他没有向任何人倾诉他的病情。
She wouldn't confide her fears to strangers. = 她不会向陌生人倾诉她的恐惧。
