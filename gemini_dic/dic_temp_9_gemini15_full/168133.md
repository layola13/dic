pro-Asian:/ˌproʊˈeɪʒən/| adj.|亲亚裔的，支持亚裔的|Supporting or favoring Asian people or Asian interests.  The term is often used in the context of social and political movements advocating for Asian rights and representation.|例句：The pro-Asian sentiment is growing in response to recent hate crimes.  亲亚裔情绪因最近的仇恨犯罪而日益增长。|近义词：pro-Asian American, Asia-supportive|反义词：anti-Asian, anti-Asian American
