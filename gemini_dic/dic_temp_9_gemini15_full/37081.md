combin:/ˈkɒmbaɪn/| /kəmˈbaɪn/| vt. n.|结合，联合；联合收割机|To bring or join together (things, ideas, etc.).|例句：The two companies combined their resources. 两家公司合并了它们的资源。|例句：He combined work with pleasure. 他把工作和娱乐结合起来。|近义词：unite, merge, blend|反义词：separate, divide, sever|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They combine flour and water to make dough. = 他们把面粉和水混合在一起做成面团。
She combines her love of art with her business acumen. = 她把对艺术的热爱与她的商业头脑结合起来。

一般过去时:
The artist combined various colors to create a masterpiece. = 艺术家将各种颜色混合在一起创作了一幅杰作。
He combined several different techniques in his presentation. = 他在演讲中结合了几种不同的技巧。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
