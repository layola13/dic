go-after:/ɡoʊˈæftər/ | vt. |追逐；追求；设法得到|To pursue or try to obtain something or someone.  The phrase implies a determined effort to achieve a goal, often involving some degree of competition or challenge. |例句：The police are going after the robbers. = 警察正在追捕强盗。

The company is going after a larger market share. = 公司正在争取更大的市场份额。

近义词：pursue, chase, seek, hunt, strive for | 反义词：ignore, neglect, abandon, forsake | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes after his dreams every day. = 他每天都在追逐他的梦想。
They go after the best talent in the industry. = 他们追求业内最优秀的人才。

一般过去时:
She went after the thief but lost him in the crowd. = 她追赶小偷，但在人群中失去了他。
The detective went after the clues meticulously. = 侦探一丝不苟地追查线索。

现在完成时:
They have gone after several funding opportunities. = 他们已经争取了几次融资机会。
He has gone after that promotion for months. = 他已经争取那个晋升职位好几个月了。

过去完成时:
She had gone after the job for a year before she finally got it. = 在她最终得到这份工作之前，她已经追求了一年。
The company had gone after that contract for a long time, without success. = 公司长期追求那份合同，但没有成功。

将来时:
I will go after my goals with determination. = 我将坚定地追求我的目标。
They will go after the escaped prisoner. = 他们将追捕逃犯。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go after my dreams. = 如果我是你，我会去追逐我的梦想。
I wish I had gone after that job opportunity. = 我真希望我当时争取了那个机会。

被动语态:
The suspect is being gone after by the police. = 嫌疑人正在被警方追捕。(注意：被动语态略显不自然，通常使用其他表达方式更佳)
The award was gone after by many talented artists. = 许多有才华的艺术家都在争取这个奖项。(注意：被动语态略显不自然，通常使用其他表达方式更佳)

疑问句:
Are you going after that promotion? = 你在争取那个晋升吗？
What are they going after now? = 他们现在在追求什么？

否定句:
He is not going after that job anymore. = 他不再追求那份工作了。
She didn't go after the lost wallet. = 她没有去追寻丢失的钱包。
