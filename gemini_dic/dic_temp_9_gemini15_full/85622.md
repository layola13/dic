haulier:/ˈhɔːliər/ | n. | 货运承运人，运输商 | A haulier is a person or company that transports goods by road.  They typically own and operate a fleet of trucks or other vehicles. | Example: The haulier delivered the goods on time despite the bad weather.  货运承运人尽管天气恶劣，还是准时送达了货物。|近义词：carrier, transporter, trucker |反义词：无

The haulier transports goods across the country. = 货运承运人运输货物遍布全国。
He hauls furniture from the factory to the stores. = 他将家具从工厂运送到商店。
