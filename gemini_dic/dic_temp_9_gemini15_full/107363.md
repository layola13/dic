lamelliferous:/ˌlæməˈlɪfərəs/| adj.|具薄片的；具叶片的|Bearing or producing lamellae (thin plates or layers).  The term is often used in biological contexts to describe structures with thin, plate-like components. |例句：The fungus exhibited lamelliferous gills, characteristic of its species.  这种真菌展现出其物种特征的叶状菌褶。

例句：The lamelliferous structure of the bone allowed for increased strength and flexibility. 骨骼的叶片状结构增加了强度和柔韧性。


近义词：plate-like, lamellar, foliated


反义词：amorphous, structureless


词性:形容词
