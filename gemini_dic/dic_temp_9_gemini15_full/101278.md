involuntary:/ɪnˈvɑːləntəri/ | adj. | 不由自主的；无意的；非自愿的 | Not done of one's own free will; compelled.  |例句：His involuntary twitch made him the subject of much teasing. 他的不由自主的抽搐让他成为大家取笑的对象。

例句：The involuntary muscle contractions caused him significant pain. 不由自主的肌肉收缩给他带来了巨大的痛苦。


近义词：unintentional, unconscious, automatic, reflex

反义词：voluntary, deliberate, intentional


词源:  The word "involuntary" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "voluntary," which itself comes from the Latin word "voluntarius" (meaning "willing" or "of one's own free will").  Therefore, "involuntary" literally means "not of one's own free will."
