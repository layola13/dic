heresiarchy:/hɪˈriːziˌɑːrki/| n. |异端统治；异端等级制度 |The system or structure of power within a heretical group or movement.  It refers to the organization and hierarchy of those who hold and propagate heretical beliefs.  The term emphasizes the aspect of power and control within a heretical system, rather than just the beliefs themselves. |Example:  The early Christian church fought against the heresiarchy of Gnosticism, which posed a significant threat to its authority.  早期基督教会与诺斯替教的异端统治作斗争，这对其权威构成了重大威胁。|近义词：heresy, heterodoxy, schism |反义词：orthodoxy, conformity, unity |名词
