nonincumbents:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈkʌmbənts/| n. |现任者以外的人；非现任者|People who are not currently holding a particular office or position.  They are often challengers in an election.|例句：The nonincumbents in this year's election are all relatively unknown. 今年选举中的非现任者都相对不为人知。  The nonincumbents mounted a strong challenge to the ruling party.  非现任者对执政党发起了强有力的挑战。|近义词：challengers, outsiders, newcomers |反义词：incumbents
