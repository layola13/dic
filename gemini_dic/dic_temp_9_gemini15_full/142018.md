organizations:/ˌɔːɡənəˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |组织，机构 | Organizations are groups of people working together for a common purpose.  组织是为共同目标而努力工作的人们的群体。|例句：Many international organizations are working to combat climate change. 许多国际组织正在努力应对气候变化。

Many non-profit organizations rely on donations. 许多非营利组织依靠捐款。|近义词：groups, institutions, bodies, associations |反义词：individuals, chaos
