foster:/ˈfɒstər/ /ˈfɔːstər/| vt. n. adj.|培养；抚养；促进；收养|To nurture, encourage, or promote the growth of something.  It can also refer to taking a child into one's home for a period of time without formally adopting them. |例句：She fostered a love of reading in her children. 她培养了孩子们对阅读的热爱。


例句：The couple decided to foster a child for a year. 这对夫妇决定收养一个孩子一年。


近义词：cultivate, nurture, promote, raise, cherish |反义词：neglect, hinder, suppress, discourage


词性:动词，名词，形容词


USAGE->
