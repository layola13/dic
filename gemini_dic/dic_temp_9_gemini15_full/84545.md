hamlets:/ˈhæmlɪts/| n.|小村庄；村落|A hamlet is a small village.  It is typically smaller than a village and often consists of a few houses or farms clustered together.|例句：We drove through several picturesque hamlets on our journey.  我们在旅途中穿过好几个风景如画的小村庄。|例句：The hamlet was so quiet, you could hear a pin drop.  这个小村庄非常安静，你甚至能听到针掉的声音。|近义词：village, settlement, community|反义词：metropolis, city
