sequestered:/sɪˈkwɛstərd/ | adj. | 与世隔绝的；隐蔽的；隔离的 | secluded; isolated; kept apart from others;  removed from the outside world. |  The sequestered jury deliberated for days before reaching a verdict.  陪审团与世隔绝，经过数日的审议才做出裁决。|  isolated, secluded, secluded, remote, hidden | public, open, accessible


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The jury is sequestered in a hotel. = 陪审团被隔离在一间酒店里。
This area is sequestered from the public. = 这个区域与公众隔绝。

一般过去时:
The family sequestered themselves in their mountain cabin. = 这家人把自己隔离在他们位于山上的小屋里。

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
