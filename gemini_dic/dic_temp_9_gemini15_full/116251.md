mass-produces:/ˌmæsˈprɒdʒuːsiːz/| v.|大规模生产，批量生产|To produce something in large quantities, typically using an assembly line or other efficient methods.|例句：The factory mass-produces clothing for major retailers.  这家工厂为大型零售商大规模生产服装。
例句：Many companies mass-produce inexpensive electronics.许多公司大规模生产廉价电子产品。|近义词：manufacture, produce en masse, churn out|反义词：handmade, custom-made|动词

They mass-produce cars in that factory. = 那个工厂大规模生产汽车。
