post-paid:/ˌpoʊstˈpeɪd/ | adj. | 邮资已付的；货到付款的 | Paid after delivery or use.  This term describes goods or services where the payment is made after the delivery or usage, rather than beforehand.  | Example: The package was post-paid, so I didn't have to pay anything at the post office.  这个包裹是邮资已付的，所以我不用在邮局付钱。 | 近义词：paid-in-advance(反义词)| 反义词：pre-paid | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shipping is post-paid. = 运费已付。
This service is post-paid. = 这项服务是货到付款的。


一般过去时:
The bill was post-paid last month. = 上个月账单已付清。
The letter was post-paid. = 这封信是邮资已付的。


现在完成时:
They have post-paid all their invoices. = 他们已经付清了所有的发票。
I have post-paid the electricity bill. = 我已经付清了电费账单。


过去完成时:
We had post-paid the order before it shipped. = 我们在订单发货前已付款。
She had post-paid the package before sending it. = 她寄包裹前已付邮资。


将来时:
I will post-paid the package tomorrow. = 我明天将支付包裹邮费。
We will post-paid the rent at the end of the month. = 我们将在月底支付房租。


虚拟语气:
If the service was post-paid, I would use it. = 如果这项服务是货到付款的，我会使用它。
If the package had been post-paid, I wouldn't have had to pay extra. = 如果包裹是邮资已付的，我就不用额外付费了。


被动语态:
The bill is post-paid monthly. = 账单每月货到付款。
The expenses were post-paid by the client. = 费用由客户后付。


疑问句:
Is the postage post-paid? = 邮费已付了吗？
Is this service post-paid or pre-paid? = 这项服务是货到付款还是预付？


否定句:
The delivery was not post-paid; I had to pay extra. = 送货不是货到付款的；我不得不额外支付。
The service is not post-paid; payment is required upfront. = 这项服务不是货到付款的；需要预先付款。
