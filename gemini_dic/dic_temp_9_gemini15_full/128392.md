new-wave:/nuːˈweɪv/| adj. |新浪潮；新潮的|  Relating to a late 20th-century movement in various art forms, characterized by a rejection of previous styles and experimentation with new forms and ideas.  It often involves a revival of earlier styles but with a modern twist.  The term is applied most prominently to music, cinema, and art. |例句：The band's music is described as new-wave pop. 这支乐队的音乐被描述为新浪潮流行音乐。

例句：The gallery showcased several paintings from the new-wave art movement. 画廊展出了新浪潮艺术运动的几幅画作。


近义词：avant-garde, innovative, modern, experimental|反义词：traditional, conventional, classic, outdated|词性:adjective
