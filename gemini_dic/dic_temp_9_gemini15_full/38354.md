concentrates:/ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪts/ /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪts/| v. n.|集中；浓缩物；浓缩；全神贯注|To direct one's attention or efforts toward a particular activity, subject, or goal;  to increase the concentration of a substance.|例句：He concentrates on his studies. 他专心学习。  The factory concentrates orange juice. 工厂浓缩橙汁。|近义词：focus, center,  |反义词：distract, disperse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He concentrates on his work every day. = 他每天都专心工作。
She concentrates her energy on her career. = 她把精力集中在事业上。


一般过去时:
I concentrated on the lecture yesterday. = 我昨天专心听讲座了。
They concentrated their efforts on finding a solution. = 他们集中精力寻找解决方案。


现在完成时:
She has concentrated on improving her skills. = 她一直致力于提高自己的技能。
He has concentrated his investments in technology. = 他一直把投资集中在科技领域。


过去完成时:
They had concentrated their resources before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经集中了资源。
She had concentrated on her studies before the exam. = 她在考试前一直专心学习。


将来时:
I will concentrate on finishing this project next week. = 我下周将集中精力完成这个项目。
We will concentrate our marketing efforts on social media. = 我们将把营销重点放在社交媒体上。


虚拟语气:
If I had concentrated harder, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我当时更专心的话，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had concentrated more on my studies. = 我真希望我当时学习更专心点。


被动语态:
The orange juice is concentrated by the factory. = 橙汁由工厂浓缩。
All efforts should be concentrated on solving the problem. = 所有努力都应该集中在解决问题上。


疑问句:
Does he concentrate well during meetings? = 他开会时能集中注意力吗？
What are you concentrating on right now? = 你现在在专注什么？


否定句:
I couldn't concentrate on the book because of the noise. = 因为噪音我无法集中精力看书。
He doesn't concentrate easily in a noisy environment. = 在嘈杂的环境中他很难集中注意力。
