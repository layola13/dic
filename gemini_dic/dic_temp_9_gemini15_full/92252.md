hyperurbanism:/ˌhaɪpərˈɜːrbənɪzəm/| n. |超城市化；过度城市化 |The extreme concentration of population and activity in cities, often leading to negative consequences such as overcrowding, pollution, and social inequality.  It refers to a condition beyond urbanization, characterized by extreme density, interconnectedness, and often unsustainable practices. | Example: The rapid hyperurbanism of megacities in developing countries presents significant challenges to urban planning and infrastructure.  超发达国家的大都市快速超城市化给城市规划和基础设施带来了巨大的挑战。|近义词：extreme urbanization, megacity sprawl |反义词：ruralization, deurbanization |名词
