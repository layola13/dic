outechoed:/ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊd/| v. |大声回应，盖过…的声音|To exceed in loudness or intensity; to surpass in sound.|The band's performance outechoed the cheering crowd.乐队的表演盖过了欢呼的人群。|近义词：overshadow, surpass, exceed|反义词：understated, muted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The speaker outechoes the audience.  演讲者的声音盖过了观众。
The machine outechoes the surrounding noise. 机器的声音盖过了周围的噪音。

一般过去时:
Her laughter outechoed the music.她的笑声盖过了音乐。
The thunder outechoed the distant train. 雷声盖过了远处火车的声音。

现在完成时:
The news has outechoed all other stories. 这条新闻已经盖过了所有其他的新闻。
The protest has outechoed the government's response. 抗议活动已经盖过了政府的回应。

过去完成时:
The final chord had outechoed the previous melodies.  最后的和弦盖过了之前的旋律。
The applause had outechoed the singer's last note. 掌声盖过了歌手的最后一个音符。

将来时:
The fireworks will outecho the city's sounds. 烟花将盖过城市的声音。
The concert will outecho the quiet of the night. 音乐会将盖过夜晚的宁静。

虚拟语气:
If the singer sang louder, she would outecho the instruments.如果歌手唱得更大声，她就能盖过乐器的声响。
I wish my voice could outecho the wind. 我希望我的声音能盖过风声。

被动语态:
The singer's voice was outechoed by the roaring crowd. 歌手的歌声被喧闹的人群盖过了。
The small sound was outechoed by the larger machine. 小的声音被更大的机器盖过了。


疑问句:
Does the stadium's sound system outecho the crowd's cheers? 体育馆的音响系统是否盖过了观众的欢呼声？
Will the new amplifier outecho the old one? 新的放大器会比旧的响吗？

否定句:
The whisper did not outecho the traffic noise. 低语声没有盖过交通噪音。
The quiet music did not outecho the lively party. 轻柔的音乐没有盖过热闹的派对。
