gilled:/ɡɪld/| adj.|有鳃的；长着鳃的|Having gills.  Gilled fungi are characterized by their gill-like structures underneath the cap.|例句：The fish has bright red gilled. 这条鱼有鲜红色的鳃。

The gills of the mushroom are delicate.  蘑菇的鳃很娇嫩。|近义词：branchiate|反义词：gill-less
