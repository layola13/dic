dun:/dʌn/| v. n. adj.|催促，敦促；暗淡的；深褐色的；|to demand payment of (a debt)|例句：The landlord dunned him for the rent. 房东催他还租金。|近义词：urge, press, importune|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He duns his tenants for rent every month. = 他每个月都催促他的房客交租。
They dun their customers for overdue payments. = 他们催促他们的客户支付逾期付款。


一般过去时:
The creditor dunned him repeatedly for the debt. = 债权人反复催他还债。
She dunned me for the money I owed her. = 她催我还她欠她的钱。


现在完成时:
The company has dunned me several times for the outstanding invoice. = 公司已经几次催促我支付未付的发票。
He has dunned his friend for the loan for months. = 他已经几个月催促他的朋友还贷款了。


过去完成时:
They had dunned him relentlessly before he finally paid. = 在他最终付款之前，他们无情地催促了他。
The bank had dunned her for years before she declared bankruptcy. = 在她宣布破产之前，银行已经催促了她多年。


将来时:
The collector will dun you if you don't pay on time. = 如果你不按时付款，收款人将会催促你。
I will dun him again next week if he still hasn't paid. = 如果他下周还没还钱，我还会再催他一次。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't dun him so aggressively. = 如果我是你，我不会那么咄咄逼人地催促他。
I wish I hadn't dunned him so harshly; it damaged our friendship. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地催促他；这损害了我们的友谊。


被动语态:
He was dunned relentlessly by his creditors. = 他被债权人无情地催促。
The debtor was dunned for payment repeatedly. = 债务人被反复催促付款。


疑问句:
Will they dun me for the late payment? = 他们会因为我付款迟到而催促我吗？
Are you going to dun him for the money he owes you? = 你打算催他还欠你的钱吗？


否定句:
They didn't dun him for the small debt. = 他们没有因为那笔小债务催促他。
I won't dun you again if you promise to pay soon. = 如果你答应很快付款，我不会再催你了。


PHRASE->
dun for payment = 催促付款
dun somebody relentlessly = 无情地催促某人
dun somebody for a debt = 催促某人还债
to be dunned for money = 被催促还钱
dun somebody until they pay = 一直催促某人直到他们付款
