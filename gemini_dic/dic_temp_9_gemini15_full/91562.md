hyper-romantic:/ˌhaɪpərroʊˈmæntɪk/ | adj. |极度浪漫的，过于浪漫的 | Extremely romantic; excessively or unrealistically romantic.  It suggests a level of romance that is perhaps idealized or impractical. |例句：The novel presented a hyper-romantic view of love, ignoring the complexities of real relationships. 这部小说对爱情描绘得过于浪漫化，忽略了真实关系的复杂性。


The movie's plot was hyper-romantic, filled with improbable coincidences and grand gestures. 这部电影的情节过于浪漫，充满了不可能的巧合和夸张的姿态。|近义词：idealistic, idyllic, sentimental |反义词：realistic, pragmatic, cynical
