/ˌmaɪkrəʊˈmeɪniə/| n. |极度细微的事物；极度琐碎的事物；对微小细节的过度关注|A preoccupation with minute details or trivial matters.  It is often associated with obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) and can manifest as an inability to make decisions due to overthinking the small aspects of a situation. Micromania can also refer to an excessive focus on insignificant details in one's work or personal life.|She suffers from micromania, spending hours agonizing over the placement of a single comma.|近义词：perfectionism, obsession, meticulousness|反义词：negligence, carelessness, indifference|名词
