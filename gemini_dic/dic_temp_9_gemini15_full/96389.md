indiscriminately:/ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnətli/| adv.|不加区别地；随意地；任意地 |Without careful judgment or distinction; randomly.  It implies a lack of selectivity or consideration for the consequences. |例句：The soldiers fired indiscriminately into the crowd.  士兵向人群胡乱射击。|例句：The company indiscriminately laid off workers, regardless of their performance. 公司不分青红皂白地解雇员工，不管他们的表现如何。|近义词：randomly, haphazardly, arbitrarily, carelessly |反义词：selectively, carefully, deliberately, thoughtfully
