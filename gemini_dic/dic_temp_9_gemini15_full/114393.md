make amends:/meɪk əˈmɛndz/ | v. | 补偿；赔偿；弥补 | To make amends means to compensate for a wrong or injury done; to attempt to repair damage or restore good relations.  It involves taking action to rectify a mistake or apologize for a hurtful action. |  例句：He tried to make amends for his hurtful words by sending flowers. 他试图通过送花来弥补他伤人的话。

例句：She made amends to her friend by apologizing sincerely and offering help. 她真诚地道歉并主动提供帮助来弥补对朋友的伤害。


近义词：compensate, atone, reconcile, redress, repair

反义词：aggravate, worsen, exacerbate
