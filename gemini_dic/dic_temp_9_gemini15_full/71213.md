fitchet:/ˈfɪtʃɪt/| n.| 鼬; 狡猾的人 | A fitchet is a small, slender mammal of the weasel family, especially the polecat.  The word is now largely archaic or dialectal, but it also carries a figurative meaning referring to a cunning or deceitful person. | Example:  The cunning fitchet evaded the traps.  狡猾的鼬躲过了陷阱。 The lawyer, a sly fitchet, twisted the facts to his client's advantage. 那个律师，一个狡猾的人，歪曲事实以利于他的委托人。 | 近义词：polecat, weasel, ferret, rogue, knave | 反义词：innocent, honest
