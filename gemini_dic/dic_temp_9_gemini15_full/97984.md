inoculating: /ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 接种；灌输；使免疫 | The act of introducing a vaccine or other substance into the body to produce immunity to a disease;  to introduce (an idea, belief, etc.) into the mind of someone; to treat (a person or animal) with a vaccine or similar preparation to protect them against disease. | 例句：The nurse is inoculating the baby against polio. 护士正在给婴儿接种脊髓灰质炎疫苗。

The professor is inoculating his students with a love of literature. 教授正在给他的学生灌输对文学的热爱。| 近义词：vaccinating, immunizing, implanting | 反义词：infecting, contaminating
