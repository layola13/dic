polled:/poʊld/ | v. | 投票；民意调查；修剪；剪短 | To collect and count votes in an election or to ask people for their opinions on a subject.  |例句：The results of the recent poll show that the public strongly supports the new policy. 最近民调结果显示公众强烈支持这项新政策。

例句：The farmer polled his sheep. 农民剪了他的羊毛。


近义词：surveyed, questioned, voted, shorn | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They poll the voters every four years. = 他们每四年对选民进行民意调查。
The shepherd polls his flock regularly. = 牧羊人定期修剪他的羊群。


一般过去时:
The town polled its citizens on the new park proposal. = 该镇就新的公园提案对公民进行了民意调查。
The gardener polled the roses to improve their shape. = 园丁修剪玫瑰以改善其形状。


现在完成时:
They have polled the students on their opinions about the new curriculum. = 他们已经就新课程对学生进行了民意调查。
The farmer has polled his sheep twice this year. = 农民今年已经两次剪羊毛了。


过去完成时:
Before the election, they had polled all the registered voters. = 大选前，他们已经对所有登记选民进行了民意调查。
By autumn, the gardener had polled all the hedges. = 到秋天，园丁已经修剪了所有树篱。


将来时:
They will poll the public on this issue next month. = 下个月他们将就这个问题对公众进行民意调查。
The florist will poll the hydrangeas to encourage more blooms. = 花商将修剪绣球花以促进更多开花。


虚拟语气:
If they had polled more people, the results would have been more accurate. = 如果他们调查更多的人，结果会更准确。
If the farmer had polled his sheep earlier, the wool would have been longer. = 如果农民早点剪羊毛，羊毛就会更长。


被动语态:
The voters were polled on their preferences. = 选民们被问及他们的偏好。
The roses were polled to promote branching. = 玫瑰被修剪以促进分枝。


疑问句:
Were the citizens polled before the decision was made? = 在做出决定之前，公民们是否进行了民意调查？
Have the sheep been polled this year? = 今年羊毛剪了吗？


否定句:
They did not poll enough people to get a representative sample. = 他们调查的人数不足以获得具有代表性的样本。
The gardener did not poll the trees, as they were already well-shaped. = 园丁没有修剪树木，因为它们已经造型很好。
