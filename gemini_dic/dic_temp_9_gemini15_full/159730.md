polyclinics:/ˈpɒliklɪnɪks/| n.|多科诊所，联合诊所|A polyclinic is a medical facility that provides a wide range of healthcare services under one roof, often including outpatient consultations, diagnostic testing, and minor surgical procedures.  It's typically larger and more comprehensive than a single-specialty clinic.|例句：The new polyclinic offers a comprehensive range of services, from general check-ups to specialist consultations. 新的多科诊所提供全面的服务，从一般体检到专家咨询。|例句：Many people prefer polyclinics because they offer convenience and access to various specialists in one location. 许多人更喜欢多科诊所，因为它们在一个地方提供便利和接触各种专家的机会。|近义词：medical center, health center, outpatient clinic|反义词：specialist clinic, single-specialty clinic
