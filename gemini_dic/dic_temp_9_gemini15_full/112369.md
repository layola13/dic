lose ground:/luːz ɡraʊnd/| v.|失去阵地；失去优势；衰退；落后|To lose ground means to become less successful, powerful, or popular.  It often implies a gradual decline or loss of advantage. |例句：The company is losing ground to its competitors. 这家公司正在失去市场份额给竞争对手。

例句：Our team lost ground in the second half of the game.  我们的球队在下半场输掉了比赛。


近义词：decline, fall behind, slip, retrogress, weaken

反义词：gain ground, advance, progress, improve, strengthen

词性：动词
