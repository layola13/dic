industrialise:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪz/| vt.|使工业化；使产业化|To develop industry in (a country or region) on a large scale.|例句：The government is committed to industrialising the country. 政府致力于使国家工业化。|例句：Many developing nations are striving to industrialise rapidly.许多发展中国家正努力迅速工业化。|近义词：industrialize|反义词：deindustrialize

一般现在时:
The government industrialises the economy through various policies. = 政府通过各种政策使经济工业化。
They industrialise their farming practices to increase efficiency. = 他们使他们的农业实践工业化以提高效率。


一般过去时:
The country industrialised rapidly during the 19th century. = 这个国家在19世纪迅速工业化。
They industrialised the production process to cut costs. = 他们使生产过程工业化以降低成本。


现在完成时:
China has industrialised significantly in recent decades. = 中国近几十年来已经实现了显著的工业化。
Many countries have industrialised their textile industries. = 许多国家已经将他们的纺织工业工业化。


过去完成时:
By the time the war ended, the country had industrialised extensively. = 到战争结束时，这个国家已经进行了广泛的工业化。
Before the economic boom, they had industrialised only a small part of their agriculture. = 在经济繁荣之前，他们只将一小部分农业工业化。


将来时:
The nation will industrialise further in the coming years. = 这个国家将在未来几年进一步工业化。
We believe this region will industrialise rapidly due to its resources. = 我们相信由于其资源，这个地区将迅速工业化。


虚拟语气:
If the government invested more, the country would industrialise more quickly. = 如果政府投入更多资金，国家将更快地工业化。
I wish the process of industrialisation hadn't been so damaging to the environment. = 我希望工业化过程没有对环境造成如此大的破坏。


被动语态:
The economy was industrialised by the government's policies. = 经济被政府的政策所工业化。
The production line is being industrialised to increase output. = 生产线正在工业化以提高产量。


疑问句:
How will the country industrialise its agricultural sector? = 这个国家将如何使其农业部门工业化？
When did this region begin to industrialise? = 这个地区何时开始工业化？


否定句:
The government did not industrialise the country as quickly as they had hoped. = 政府没有像他们希望的那样迅速地使国家工业化。
They chose not to industrialise their fishing industry. = 他们选择不将他们的渔业工业化。
