overlean: /ˌoʊvərˈliːn/ | v. | 倾斜过度；过度依赖 | To lean or incline too far; to rely too heavily on something or someone. | 例句：The tower overleans dangerously. 塔身危险地倾斜过度。  The company overleans on a single supplier. 这家公司过度依赖单一供应商。 | 近义词：tilt excessively, over-rely | 反义词：stand upright, be independent
