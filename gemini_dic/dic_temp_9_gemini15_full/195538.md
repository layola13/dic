semipendulousness:/ˌsemiˈpendʒələsnəs/| n. |半下垂状态，半悬垂状态 |The state of being partly hanging down or drooping; a condition between fully erect and fully pendulous.  It often describes the growth habit of plants or other objects. |Example: The flowers displayed a charming semipendulousness, neither fully upright nor completely drooping.  这朵花展现出迷人的半下垂状态，既不是完全直立也不是完全下垂。|近义词：partial pendulence, slight droop |反义词：erectness, uprightness |名词
