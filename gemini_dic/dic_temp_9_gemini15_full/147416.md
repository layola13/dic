overnourished: /ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃt/ | adj. |营养过剩的，喂养过度的|  Excessively nourished; having received more nourishment than is needed or desirable.  The condition of having consumed excessive amounts of nutrients, leading to potential health problems.|

例句：

The child, overnourished from birth, suffered from obesity in later life.  = 这个孩子从出生就被喂养过度，成年后患上了肥胖症。

His overnourished plants were beginning to show signs of stress. = 他营养过剩的植物开始出现压力迹象。


近义词：overfed, overfed, surfeited


反义词：undernourished, malnourished


词源:

The word "overnourished" is a compound word formed from the prefix "over-" meaning "too much" or "excessively," and the adjective "nourished," which derives from the verb "nourish."  "Nourish" comes from the Old French "norrir" and ultimately from the Latin "nutrire," meaning "to nourish," "to feed," or "to foster."
