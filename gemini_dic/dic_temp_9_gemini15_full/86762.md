helplessness:/ˈhɛlpləsnəs/ | n. | 无助；无能为力 | The feeling or state of being unable to do anything to help oneself or another person.  It often involves a sense of powerlessness and despair. |  例句：Her helplessness in the face of such overwhelming grief was heartbreaking. 面对如此巨大的悲伤，她的无助令人心碎。

例句：The feeling of helplessness overwhelmed him after the accident. 事故发生后，无助感将他淹没。


近义词：powerlessness, vulnerability, inability, desperation | 反义词：self-reliance, empowerment, capability
