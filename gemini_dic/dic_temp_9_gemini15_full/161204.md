postinfection: /ˌpoʊstɪnˈfɛkʃən/ | adj. | 感染后，感染之后的 | Relating to the period or events following an infection.  It describes the consequences or effects that occur after an infection has taken place. |

例句：

Postinfection complications are a major concern in treating this disease. = 感染后的并发症是治疗这种疾病的主要担忧。

The study examined postinfection immune responses in mice. = 这项研究检查了小鼠感染后的免疫反应。


近义词：postinfectious,  after-infection


反义词：preinfection


词性：形容词
