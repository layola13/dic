perfumes:/ˈpɜːrfjuːmz/ | n. | 香水；香气 | Fragrant substances used to impart a pleasant scent to the body or objects.  | Example: She sprayed her favorite perfumes before going out. 她出门前喷了她最喜欢的香水。  |近义词：fragrances, scents, aromas | 反义词：stenches, odors
