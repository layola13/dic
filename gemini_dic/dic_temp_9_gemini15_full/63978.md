epistemic:/ˌɛpɪˈstɛmɪk/| adj.|认识论的；知识论的；与知识相关的|Relating to knowledge or justified belief; concerned with the nature and scope of knowledge.  The term often appears in discussions of epistemology, the branch of philosophy that deals with the theory of knowledge.|

The epistemic status of the claim is questionable.  这个说法的认识论地位值得怀疑。

The debate centered on epistemic issues.  这场辩论的中心是认识论问题。

近义词:cognitive, intellectual, theoretical

反义词:practical, empirical (in some contexts)
