free-form:/friː fɔːrm/|/ˈfriː fɔːrm/| adj.| 自由形式的；非正式的 |  Not following a set pattern or structure; informal and unrestrained.  It implies a lack of pre-defined rules or limitations, allowing for improvisation and individual expression. |

例句：The teacher encouraged free-form writing in the class, allowing students to express their ideas freely.  老师鼓励课堂上进行自由写作，让学生自由表达他们的想法。

例句：The artist's free-form sculpture was a striking contrast to the more traditional pieces in the gallery. 艺术家的自由形式雕塑与画廊中更传统的作品形成了鲜明的对比。


近义词：unstructured, informal, improvisational, spontaneous

反义词：structured, formal, rigid, conventional


词性：形容词
