mediums:/ˈmiːdiəm/| n.|媒介；方法；灵媒；中庸；中等|A medium is a channel or means of communication, expression, or transmission.  It can also refer to a person believed to have the ability to communicate with spirits.  In art, it refers to the material or technique used.|例句：Television is a powerful medium of communication. 电视是一种强大的传播媒介。

She uses mixed media in her art.  她使用混合媒介进行艺术创作。


例句：The psychic claimed to be a medium.  那个通灵者声称自己是一个灵媒。


近义词：channel, instrument, vehicle, spirit medium|反义词：extreme, end
