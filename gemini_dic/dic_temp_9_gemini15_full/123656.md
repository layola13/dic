monuments:/ˈmɒnjʊmənts/ /ˈmɑːnjumənts/ | n. |纪念碑，纪念物 |A monument is a structure or object built to commemorate a person, event, or idea.  Monuments are often significant landmarks and can take many forms, from statues and memorials to buildings and structures. |例句：The Washington Monument is a prominent landmark in Washington D.C. 华盛顿纪念碑是华盛顿特区的一个著名地标。

The pyramids of Egypt are ancient monuments that attract millions of visitors each year.  埃及的金字塔是每年吸引数百万游客的古代纪念碑。|近义词：memorial, landmark, memorial, statue |反义词：nothingness, insignificance
