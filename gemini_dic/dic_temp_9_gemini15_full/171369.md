publishing: /ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 出版业；出版；发行 | The act of making something (such as a book, magazine, or piece of music) available to the public.  It encompasses all the processes involved in bringing a work to the public, from writing and editing to printing and distribution. | Example: The publishing of her new novel was a major success.  她的新小说出版大获成功。

Example:  Self-publishing is becoming increasingly popular. 自我出版越来越受欢迎。


近义词：publication, distribution, printing  | 反义词：suppression, censorship | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This publishing house releases many bestsellers annually. = 这家出版社每年发行许多畅销书。
They are publishing a new edition of the textbook. = 他们正在出版该教科书的新版本。


一般过去时:
The company published its financial report last week. = 公司上周发布了财务报告。
They published the article in a leading scientific journal. = 他们在领先的科学期刊上发表了这篇文章。


现在完成时:
She has published several articles on the subject. = 她已经发表了几篇关于这个主题的文章。
The author has published three novels so far. = 这名作者迄今已出版了三部小说。


过去完成时:
They had published the book before the conference. = 他们在大会在召开之前已经出版了这本书。
The editor had already published the corrected manuscript. = 编辑已经出版了校正后的手稿。


将来时:
They will publish the results of the study next month. = 他们将在下个月公布这项研究的结果。
The new magazine will be publishing its first issue in spring. = 这本新杂志将在春天发行第一期。


虚拟语气:
If they published the book now, it would be a bestseller. = 如果他们现在出版这本书，它将成为畅销书。
I wish they had published my poem. = 我希望他们能发表我的诗歌。


被动语态:
The book was published by a small independent press. = 这本书由一家小型独立出版社出版。
The article is being published in several languages. = 这篇文章正在被翻译成多种语言出版。


疑问句:
When will the book be published? = 这本书什么时候出版？
Who is publishing this controversial biography? = 谁在出版这本有争议的传记？


否定句:
They did not publish the findings of the research. = 他们没有发表这项研究的结果。
The manuscript will not be published until next year. = 这份手稿要到明年才会出版。

词源:
The word "publishing" comes from the verb "publish," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "publicare," meaning "to make public."  The suffix "-ing" turns the verb into a gerund, functioning as a noun.
