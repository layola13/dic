owned:/oʊnd/| v.|拥有的；占有的|to have something as one's own; to possess something.|例句：He owned a large house in the countryside. 他在乡下拥有一栋大房子。|例句：The company owned several factories. 这家公司拥有几家工厂。|近义词：possessed, held, controlled|反义词：rented, leased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I own a car. = 我拥有一辆车。
She owns a successful business. = 她拥有一家成功的企业。

一般过去时:
They owned a small farm. = 他们曾经拥有一个小农场。
He owned the land for many years. = 他拥有那块土地很多年了。

现在完成时:
We have owned this house for ten years. = 我们拥有这栋房子十年了。
She has owned that painting since 1990. = 她从1990年就拥有那幅画了。

过去完成时:
They had owned the shop before they moved to the city. = 他们在搬到城市之前就拥有那家商店了。
She had owned several cars before buying her current one. = 在买她现在这辆车之前，她曾经拥有过几辆车。

将来时:
I will own my own business one day. = 我有朝一日会拥有自己的企业。
They will own a yacht in the future. = 他们将来会拥有一艘游艇。


虚拟语气:
If I owned a yacht, I would sail around the world. = 如果我拥有一艘游艇，我会环游世界。
I wish I owned a bigger house. = 我希望我有一栋更大的房子。

被动语态:
The land was owned by the government. = 这块土地归政府所有。
The building is owned by a private company. = 这栋建筑物归一家私人公司所有。

疑问句:
Do you own a pet? = 你养宠物吗？
Does she own a car? = 她有车吗？

否定句:
I don't own a bicycle. = 我没有自行车。
They didn't own their home. = 他们没有自己的房子。


PHRASE->
take ownership of = 对...负责
feel owned = 感觉被束缚或控制
self-owned = 自有的
jointly owned = 共有的
government-owned = 国有的
