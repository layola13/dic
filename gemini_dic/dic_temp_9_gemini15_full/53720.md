It often implies a deliberate action to undermine someone's reputation or authority. |例句：The scandal led to a significant disclout of the politician.  丑闻导致这位政治家失去了相当的影响力。
The company experienced a disclout after the product recall.  产品召回后，公司影响力下降了。|近义词：depowering, discrediting, undermining, delegitimizing |反义词：empowerment, enhancement, boosting
