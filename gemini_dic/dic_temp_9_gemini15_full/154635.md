persistively:/pərˈsɪstəntli/| adv. |坚持地；执拗地 | persistently means in a way that continues to exist or is repeated for a long time, even when this is difficult or annoying.  It implies a determined and unwavering continuation of an action or state. |He persistently pursued his dream of becoming a writer. 他坚持不懈地追求成为作家的梦想。|She persistently ignored his calls. 她一直无视他的来电。 | consistently, steadfastly, resolutely | intermittently, casually, sporadically | 副词
