left-over:/ˈlɛftˌoʊvər/| adj., n.|剩余的；剩菜剩饭|Remaining after a quantity has been used or consumed.|There are some left-over cakes from the party.  派对上还有一些剩下的蛋糕。|The leftover food was delicious reheated.  剩下的食物加热后很好吃。|remaining, surplus|freshly cooked, newly prepared

He often has leftovers for lunch. = 他经常吃剩菜当午餐。
