prescreening:/ˈpriːˌskriːnɪŋ/| n. |预选；预审；初步筛选 |The process of screening or evaluating something or someone before a more thorough or formal process.  This often involves a preliminary assessment to determine eligibility or suitability. For example, in hiring, it might involve reviewing resumes to identify candidates for interviews.  |例句：The prescreening of applications helped to narrow down the pool of candidates. 预选申请帮助缩小了候选人的范围。

The prescreening process eliminated many unqualified applicants. 预选过程淘汰了许多不合格的申请人。

|近义词：preliminary screening, initial screening, preselection |反义词：final screening, ultimate selection |名词
