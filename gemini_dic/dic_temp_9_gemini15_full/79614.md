glory:/ˈɡlɔːri/|/ˈɡlɔːri/| n.|荣誉，光荣，辉煌|Great praise and honor received for achievement.|例句：He achieved glory on the battlefield. 他在战场上获得了荣誉。|例句：The glory of the Roman Empire lasted for centuries. 罗马帝国的辉煌持续了几个世纪。|近义词：honor, fame, renown|反义词：shame, disgrace, dishonor|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team enjoys its glory after winning the championship. = 这支球队在赢得冠军后享受着荣誉。
Her singing brings her much glory. = 她的歌声给她带来了很多荣誉。

一般过去时:
The athletes reveled in their glory after the Olympics. = 运动员们在奥运会后沉浸在他们的荣誉中。
The general basked in the glory of his victory. = 将军沉浸在他胜利的光荣中。

现在完成时:
He has achieved considerable glory in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中获得了相当大的荣誉。
She has brought glory to her family and country. = 她为她的家庭和国家带来了荣耀。

过去完成时:
They had already tasted glory before this competition. = 在这次比赛之前，他们已经尝到了胜利的滋味。
The empire had long since lost its former glory. = 这个帝国早已失去了昔日的辉煌。

将来时:
Future generations will remember his glory. = 后代将铭记他的功勋。
She hopes to achieve glory in her chosen field.  = 她希望在所选择的领域取得辉煌成就。

虚拟语气:
If he had won, he would have basked in the glory. = 如果他赢了，他就会沉浸在胜利的光荣中。
I wish I could share in your glory. = 我希望我能分享你的荣耀。

被动语态:
His name was enshrined in glory. = 他的名字被载入史册。
The city was bathed in the glory of the setting sun. = 城市沐浴在夕阳的余晖中。

疑问句:
What is the glory of this moment? = 这时刻的辉煌是什么？
Does he seek glory or true achievement? = 他追求的是荣誉还是真正的成就？

否定句:
He didn't seek personal glory. = 他不追求个人荣誉。
The battle brought no glory to either side. = 这场战斗双方都没有赢得荣誉。
