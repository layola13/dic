colorized:/ˈkʌləraɪzd/ | adj. |着色的，彩色的|  Giving something color, especially a picture or video that was originally black and white.  |例句：The old film was colorized for a modern audience.  这部旧电影经过着色处理，以适应现代观众。|近义词：colored, tinted, enhanced|反义词：monochrome, black-and-white|形容词
