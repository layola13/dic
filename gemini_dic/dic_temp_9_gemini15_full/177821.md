recensionist:/rɪˈvɪʒənɪst/| n. |评论员；评论家；校订者|A person who writes reviews, especially of books or other publications;  a critic or editor who revises or corrects a text.|The recensionist praised the novel's intricate plot and compelling characters.  这位评论员称赞了这部小说的复杂情节和引人入胜的人物。|critic, reviewer, editor|propagandist(针对特定观点的评论家)|名词
