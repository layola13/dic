reassignment:/ˌriːəˈsaɪnmənt/| n. |重新分配；重新指派 |The act of assigning something or someone again; the state of being assigned again.  It often implies a change of task, role, or responsibility. |例句：The reassignment of tasks improved team efficiency.  重新分配任务提高了团队效率。

例句：His reassignment to a different department was unexpected. 他被重新分配到不同的部门，这很出乎意料。


近义词：reassignment, reallocation, redeployment, transfer


反义词：original assignment, initial allocation


词性:名词
