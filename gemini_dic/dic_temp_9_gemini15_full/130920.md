英文解析：Nonascertainablenesses is the plural form of "nonascertainableness," which means the state or quality of being impossible to ascertain or determine with certainty. It refers to things that are unknowable or impossible to find out definitively.  The word is formed by combining "non-" (not), "ascertain" (to find out for certain), "-able" (capable of being), and "-ness" (state or quality).
