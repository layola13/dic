cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/| n. |茧；保护层；庇护所 |A cocoon is a protective casing, usually of silk, spun by a larva (such as a moth or butterfly) before it pupates.  It also refers to a place or situation that provides protection or shelter.  |例句：The caterpillar spun a silken cocoon around itself. 毛毛虫把自己裹在一个丝茧里。


The butterfly emerged from its cocoon. 蝴蝶从茧中飞出。|近义词：chrysalis, casing, shelter, refuge |反义词：exposure, vulnerability
