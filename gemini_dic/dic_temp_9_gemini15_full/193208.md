seduces:/səˈdʒuːsɪz/ | vt. | 引诱；诱惑；迷惑；勾引 | To entice or persuade (someone) to do something, typically something morally wrong or unwise.  | 例句：The siren song seduced the sailors to their doom.  =  海妖的歌声引诱水手走向灭亡。

The salesman seduced the customer into buying an expensive car. =  推销员诱使顾客买了一辆昂贵的汽车。


近义词：tempt, entice, allure, charm, attract | 反义词：repel, deter, discourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seduces women with his charm. = 他用魅力诱惑女人。
The advertisement seduces consumers to buy the product. = 广告诱使消费者购买该产品。


一般过去时:
She seduced him with promises of wealth. = 她用财富的承诺诱惑了他。
The beautiful scenery seduced me into staying longer. = 美丽的景色诱使我多待了一会。


现在完成时:
He has seduced many women throughout his life. = 他一生中诱惑过许多女人。
The marketing campaign has seduced a large number of customers. =  营销活动吸引了大量的顾客。


过去完成时:
She had seduced him before he even realized it. = 在他意识到之前，她就已诱惑了他。
The company had seduced investors with false promises. = 公司用虚假承诺诱惑了投资者。


将来时:
He will seduce her with his romantic gestures. = 他将用浪漫的姿态诱惑她。
The new product will seduce the market with its innovative features. = 新产品将以其创新功能吸引市场。


虚拟语气:
If he seduced her, he would lose everything. = 如果他诱惑了她，他将失去一切。
I wish he hadn't seduced her with his lies. = 我真希望他没有用谎言诱惑她。


被动语态:
Many men have been seduced by her beauty. = 许多男人都被她的美貌所吸引。
The consumers were seduced by the clever advertising campaign. = 消费者被巧妙的广告宣传所吸引。


疑问句:
Did he seduce her? = 他诱惑了她吗？
Was she seduced by his promises? = 她被他的承诺诱惑了吗？


否定句:
He did not seduce her; she came willingly. = 他没有诱惑她；她自愿来的。
The advertisement did not seduce many customers. = 广告没有吸引很多顾客。


PHRASE->
seduce someone into doing something = 诱使某人做某事
be seduced by = 被…所诱惑
seduce away = 诱走；勾引走
seduce from = 从…引诱出来
seduce with = 用…诱惑

词源:
源自中古英语 *seducen，源自古法语 *seduire，源自拉丁语 *ducere（引导，带领）。  前缀 *se- 表示“离开，分开”，因此字面意思为“引导离开”。
