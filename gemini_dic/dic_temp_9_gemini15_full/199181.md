silicones:/ˈsɪlɪkəʊnz/ | n. |硅酮，硅橡胶 |A silicone is a synthetic polymer containing silicon together with carbon, hydrogen, oxygen, and other elements. Silicones are known for their heat resistance, water repellency, and flexibility.  They are used in a wide variety of applications, from cosmetics to industrial products. |Example:  Many hair conditioners contain silicones to make hair feel smoother. 许多护发素含有硅酮，使头发感觉更顺滑。

The new sealant is made of silicone.  新型密封胶是由硅酮制成的。|近义词：polymer, elastomer |反义词：无

词源:
The word "silicone" is derived from "silicon," the chemical element, and the suffix "-one," which is often used to denote organic compounds containing oxygen.  The name reflects the chemical structure of these polymers, which contain silicon-oxygen bonds.
