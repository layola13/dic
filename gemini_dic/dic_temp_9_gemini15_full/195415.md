seminarist:/ˈsɛmɪnərɪst/| n. |神学院学生；研讨会参加者|A person who attends or studies at a seminary (a college for training clergy).  It can also refer to a participant in a seminar.|例句：He was a bright seminarist at the theological seminary. 他是神学院里一位聪明的学生。


例句：Several seminarists attended the workshop on biblical interpretation.  几位神学院学生参加了圣经解释研讨会。

近义词：seminarian, theological student, seminar participant

反义词：N/A

词性：名词
