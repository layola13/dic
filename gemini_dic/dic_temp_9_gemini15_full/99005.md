interacting:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪŋ/| adj. |相互作用的，相互影响的 |Describes something or someone that is involved in a process of reciprocal action or influence.  It emphasizes the dynamic and mutual relationship between elements. |Example:  The interacting molecules formed a complex structure.  相互作用的分子形成了一个复杂的结构。 The interacting characters in the novel create a compelling narrative. 小说中相互影响的人物创造了一个引人入胜的故事。 |近义词：interacting, reciprocal, interdependent, mutual |反义词：independent, isolated, separate |形容词
