coerce:/koʊˈɜːrs/| vt.|强迫，胁迫|To persuade (an unwilling person) to do something by using force or threats.|例句：The dictator coerced the people into obedience. 独裁者强迫人民服从。|例句：They were coerced into signing the contract. 他们被迫签署了合同。|近义词：compel, force, pressure|反义词：persuade (in a gentle way), encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He coerces his employees to work overtime. = 他强迫他的员工加班。
The government coerces citizens into compliance. = 政府强迫公民遵守规定。

一般过去时:
The police coerced a confession from the suspect. = 警方从嫌疑人那里逼供。
She coerced him into giving her money. = 她强迫他给她钱。

现在完成时:
They have coerced the witness into silence. = 他们已经强迫证人保持沉默。
The company has coerced its workers into accepting lower wages. = 公司已经强迫员工接受较低的工资。

过去完成时:
He had coerced her into marrying him years ago. = 多年前他就强迫她嫁给他了。
The regime had coerced the population into submission. = 该政权已经强迫人民屈服。

将来时:
They will coerce him into revealing the secrets. = 他们将会强迫他泄露秘密。
The authorities will coerce compliance with the new law. = 当局将会强迫人们遵守新法律。

虚拟语气:
If they coerced me, I would refuse. = 如果他们强迫我，我会拒绝。
I wish they hadn't coerced him into confessing. = 我希望他们没有强迫他认罪。

被动语态:
He was coerced into confessing a crime he didn't commit. = 他被迫承认了一项他未犯的罪行。
The workers were coerced into accepting the unfair contract. = 工人们被迫接受不公平的合同。

疑问句:
Were they coerced into making that decision? = 他们是被迫做出那个决定的吗？
Did anyone coerce you into signing the document? = 有人强迫你签署文件吗？

否定句:
The police didn't coerce the suspect into admitting guilt. = 警方并没有强迫嫌疑人承认有罪。
She wasn't coerced into participating in the project. = 她并没有被迫参与这个项目。


PHRASE->
coerce into doing something = 强迫做某事
coerce a confession = 逼供
coerce compliance = 强制遵守
coerce obedience = 强制服从
under coercion = 在胁迫下
