fews:/fjuːz/ | n. |少量，少数 |A small number of something.  It often implies a disappointingly small number.  Note that "few" is more common and generally preferred.|例句：There were only a fews people at the meeting.  会议上只有少量的人。|近义词：few, a handful, several (though "several" implies more than "fews") |反义词：many, numerous, a lot


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are a fews apples left. = 还剩下几个苹果。
There are fews problems with this plan. = 这个计划几乎没有问题。


一般过去时:
There were a fews cars in the parking lot. = 停车场里只有几辆车。
There were fews complaints about the service. = 关于服务方面的抱怨很少。


现在完成时:
There have been a fews accidents on this road. = 这条路上发生过几起事故。
There have been fews changes since last year. = 自从去年以来变化很少。


过去完成时:
There had been a fews delays before the flight departed. = 在飞机起飞前已经有一些延误了。
There had been fews opportunities for advancement. = 升迁的机会很少。


将来时:
There will be a fews guests at the party. = 聚会上将会有几个客人。
There will be fews chances to win the lottery. = 中彩票的机会很少。


虚拟语气:
If there were a fews more volunteers, we could finish the project. = 如果有更多志愿者，我们就能完成这个项目。（此处few用作可数名词复数）
I wish there were a fews more sunny days this month. = 我希望这个月有更多晴天。（此处few用作可数名词复数）


被动语态:
A fews mistakes were made in the report. = 报告中出现了一些错误。
Few problems were reported by the users. = 用户报告的问题很少。


疑问句:
Are there a fews people waiting outside? = 外面有几个人在等吗?
Are there fews reasons to worry? = 有什么理由担心吗？


否定句:
There weren't a fews people at the party; there were many. = 聚会上并非只有少量的人；而是很多人。
There weren't fews problems; there were many. =  问题不是很少；而是很多。
