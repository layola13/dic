high-courts:/ˈhaɪ kɔːrts/| n.|高等法院|A high court is a court of law with superior jurisdiction, typically in a specific region or for a particular type of case.  They often hear appeals from lower courts and have the authority to make significant legal decisions. | Example: The high court overturned the lower court's decision.  高等法院推翻了下级法院的裁决。 The case will be heard in the high court next month.  此案将于下个月在高等法院审理。|近义词：supreme court, appellate court |反义词：lower court, magistrate's court|名词
