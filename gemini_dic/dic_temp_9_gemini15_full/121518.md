mislocating: /ˌmɪsloʊˈkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 放错地方；放错位置 | Putting something in the wrong place; failing to locate something correctly. | 例句：I'm constantly mislocating my keys. = 我总是找不到我的钥匙。


例句：He admitted mislocating the important documents. = 他承认把重要的文件放错了地方。


近义词：misplacing, placing wrongly, putting in the wrong place


反义词：locating correctly, finding, retrieving


词性：动词
