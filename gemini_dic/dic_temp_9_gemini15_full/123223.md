monoconsonantal: /ˌmɒnəʊkɒnˈsɒnəntəl/ | adj. | 单辅音的 |  Having or consisting of only one consonant.  This term is primarily used in linguistics to describe a syllable or word that contains only one consonant sound.  It contrasts with words containing multiple consonants (multiconsonantal).


例句：

The syllable "a" is monoconsonantal. = 音节“a”是单辅音的。

"Oh" is a simple, monoconsonantal word. = “哦”是一个简单的单辅音词。


近义词：unisonant (less common)


反义词：multiconsonantal, polyconsonantal


词性: 形容词
