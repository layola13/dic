migrate:/maɪˈɡreɪt/ | v. | 迁移；移居；迁徙 | To move from one place to another, especially to a new place to live.  | Example: Birds migrate south for the winter. 鸟类在冬天向南迁徙。  Many people migrate to cities in search of work. 许多人为了找工作而迁移到城市。 |近义词：emigrate, immigrate, relocate |反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Birds migrate south every year. = 鸟类每年都向南迁徙。
People migrate to find better opportunities. = 人们为了寻找更好的机会而迁移。

一般过去时:
My family migrated to Canada in 1980. = 我的家人在1980年移居加拿大。
The nomads migrated across the desert. = 游牧民族穿越沙漠迁移。

现在完成时:
Millions of people have migrated to urban areas. = 数百万的人口已经迁移到城市地区。
They have migrated to a new country for a better future. = 为了更好的未来，他们已经移居到一个新的国家。

过去完成时:
The birds had already migrated before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，鸟类已经迁徙了。
The family had migrated several times before settling down. = 在定居之前，这个家庭已经迁移了好几次。

将来时:
We will migrate to the countryside next year. = 我们明年将移居乡村。
The company plans to migrate its servers to a new data center. = 公司计划将其服务器迁移到新的数据中心。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would migrate to a warmer climate. = 如果我有钱，我会迁移到气候更温暖的地方。
I wish I could migrate to a different country. = 我希望我能移居到另一个国家。

被动语态:
The data was migrated to the new server successfully. = 数据成功迁移到新服务器。
Many animals are being migrated due to climate change. = 许多动物由于气候变化而被迫迁移。

疑问句:
Do birds migrate at night? = 鸟类晚上迁徙吗？
When will the company migrate its data? = 公司什么时候迁移其数据？

否定句:
The birds did not migrate this year due to the unusual weather. = 由于异常的天气，鸟类今年没有迁徙。
He decided not to migrate to another city. = 他决定不迁移到另一个城市。


PHRASE->
migrate to = 迁移到...
migrate from = 从...迁移
mass migration = 大规模迁移
seasonal migration = 季节性迁移
forced migration = 强制迁移
