hipness:/ˈhɪpnəs/| n. |时髦；酷；现代感 |The quality or state of being hip;  stylishness, coolness, and a trendy awareness of current fads and culture.  It often implies an understanding and appreciation of what is considered fashionable or avant-garde. |例句：The band's hipness attracted a large following. 这支乐队的酷劲儿吸引了众多粉丝。

例句：Her clothes and attitude showcased her hipness.  她的穿着和态度都展现了她的时尚感。


近义词：coolness, trendiness, stylishness, modernity, sophistication

反义词：unfashionableness, outdatedness, dullness, backwardness


词性：名词
