refuses:/rɪˈfjuːzɪz/| v.|拒绝，拒不|To decline or reject (something offered, proposed, or requested).|He refuses to admit his mistake. 他拒绝承认自己的错误。|She refused the offer of a job. 她拒绝了这份工作。|accept, decline|agree, consent|verb
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refuses to cooperate. 他拒绝合作。
They refuse my request. 他们拒绝了我的请求。

一般过去时:
She refused to answer my questions. 她拒绝回答我的问题。
He refused the invitation politely. 他礼貌地拒绝了邀请。

现在完成时:
I have refused all offers so far. 到目前为止，我已经拒绝了所有的提议。
She has refused to comment on the situation. 她拒绝就此事发表评论。

过去完成时:
He had refused to believe it until he saw it with his own eyes. 直到他亲眼所见，他才相信。
They had refused to help previously. 他们之前拒绝过帮忙。

将来时:
She will refuse to sign the contract. 她将拒绝签署合同。
They will refuse any further negotiations. 他们将拒绝任何进一步的谈判。

虚拟语气:
I wish he would not refuse my help. 我希望他不拒绝我的帮助。
If he refused, what would we do? 如果他拒绝了，我们该怎么办？

被动语态:
His request was refused. 他的请求被拒绝了。
My application was refused. 我的申请被拒绝了。

疑问句:
Will he refuse to go? 他会拒绝去吗？
Does she refuse to comply? 她拒绝遵守吗？

否定句:
He doesn't refuse to help. 他并不拒绝帮忙。
She didn't refuse my invitation. 她没有拒绝我的邀请。
