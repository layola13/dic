credibly:/ˈkrɛdəbli/ | adj. | 可信地；可靠地 |  believable; in a way that can be believed.  It suggests that something is likely to be true because it is supported by evidence or appears reasonable. |  His story sounded credibly detailed. 他的故事听起来非常详细可信。The witness gave credibly testimony.目击者作证可信。| reliably, plausibly, convincingly | unbelievably, unconvincingly, dubiously
