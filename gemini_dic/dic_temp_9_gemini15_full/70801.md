fingernails:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌneɪlz/ | n. |指甲|The hard keratin covering on the tips of the fingers.  They protect the sensitive fingertips and are used in various tasks requiring dexterity and precision.| 例句：She painted her fingernails a bright red. 她把指甲涂成了鲜红色。

例句：He bit his fingernails nervously. 他紧张地咬着指甲。
