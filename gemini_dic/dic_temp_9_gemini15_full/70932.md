firebug:/ˈfaɪərˌbʌɡ/|/ˈfaɪəbʌɡ/| n. |纵火犯；萤火虫|A firebug is someone who deliberately sets fires, often for reasons of pyromania.  It can also refer to a tool for debugging web applications.|例句：The police arrested a suspected firebug after a series of arson attacks.  警方在一系列纵火袭击后逮捕了一名涉嫌纵火的嫌疑人。


The firebug quickly extinguished the small blaze. 纵火犯迅速扑灭了小火。|近义词：arsonist, pyromaniac|反义词：firefighter, fire marshal
