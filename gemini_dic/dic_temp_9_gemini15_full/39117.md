confucianisms:/kənˈfjuːʃənɪzəm/| n. |儒家思想，儒教|The philosophical and religious system developed from the teachings of Confucius.  It emphasizes social harmony, ethical conduct, and good governance. Confucianism has profoundly influenced East Asian culture and continues to shape values and practices in many parts of the world. |Example:  Confucianisms  played a central role in shaping the social and political structures of ancient China.|近义词：Confucian thought, Confucian ethics|反义词：无
