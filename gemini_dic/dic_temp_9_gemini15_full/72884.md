follow-through: /ˈfɑːloʊ θruː/ | /ˈfɒləʊ θruː/ | v. | 完成；贯彻；坚持到底；(体育)后续动作 |

英文解析:To follow through means to complete something that has been started, to see a task or project to its end, or to persist in an action until its conclusion. In sports, it specifically refers to the continuation of a movement after contact, crucial for power and accuracy.

例句:

He followed through on his promise to donate to the charity.  = 他信守了向慈善机构捐款的承诺。

She lacked the follow-through to finish the project on time. = 她缺乏坚持到底的精神，没能按时完成项目。


近义词: complete, finish, persevere, persist, conclude

反义词: abandon, quit, give up, neglect
