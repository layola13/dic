constriction:/kənˈstrɪkʃən/| n. |收缩；狭窄；约束 | The act of constricting or the state of being constricted; narrowing or tightening.  It can refer to physical narrowing, like a constriction in a blood vessel, or a metaphorical narrowing, like a constriction of opportunities. | Example: The constriction of the artery reduced blood flow to the heart.  动脉收缩减少了血液流向心脏。  The economic constriction forced many businesses to close. 经济收缩迫使许多企业倒闭。|近义词：contraction, narrowing, restriction, compression |反义词：dilation, expansion, widening |名词
