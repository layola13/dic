gene-rated: /ˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 产生的；生成的 |  Produced or created; brought into existence.  The term often implies a process or system that automatically creates something. | 例句：The software generated a report summarizing the data.  该软件生成了一个总结数据的报告。

The system generated an error message. 系统生成了一个错误消息。
