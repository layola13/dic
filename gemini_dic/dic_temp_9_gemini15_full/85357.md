harrow:/ˈhærəʊ/| n. vt.|耙；痛苦；折磨|A harrow is a tool used in agriculture for breaking up and smoothing the soil.  It can also refer to something that causes distress or suffering.|例句：The farmer used a harrow to prepare the field for planting. 农民用耙子耕地准备播种。The experience was a harrowing ordeal. 这段经历是一场痛苦的磨难。|近义词：rake, torment, distress|反义词：soothe, comfort, ease|
