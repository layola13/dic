haywires:/ˈheɪwaɪrz/| adj.|混乱的；失控的；错乱的 |  Describes a situation or a system that is malfunctioning, chaotic, or out of control.  It suggests a state of disarray and unpredictability. |例句：The whole system went haywire after the power surge.  电力激增后，整个系统都乱套了。

The plan went haywire when the key component failed. 计划的关键部件失效后，计划就乱套了。|近义词：chaotic, disordered, erratic, malfunctioning |反义词：orderly, controlled, functioning smoothly |词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is haywire. = 机器失灵了。
The situation is haywire. = 情况混乱不堪。


一般过去时:
The computer went haywire yesterday. = 昨天电脑失灵了。
Everything went haywire during the storm. = 暴风雨期间一切陷入混乱。


现在完成时:
The engine has gone haywire. = 发动机已经失灵了。
My schedule has been haywire all week. = 我整个星期的日程都乱套了。


过去完成时:
The project had gone haywire before they could fix it. = 项目在他们能修复它之前就已经乱套了。
The communication system had gone haywire, resulting in delays. = 通信系统已经失灵，导致延误。


将来时:
The system will go haywire if we don't fix it. = 如果我们不修理它，系统就会失灵。
I fear the negotiations will go haywire. = 我担心谈判会失败。


虚拟语气:
If the software were haywire, the system would crash. = 如果软件失灵，系统就会崩溃。
I wish my life wasn't so haywire. = 我希望我的生活不要这么混乱。


被动语态:
The electrical system was found to be haywire. = 发现电力系统失灵了。
The experiment was completely haywire. = 实验完全失败了。


疑问句:
Is the network haywire? = 网络失灵了吗？
Is everything haywire? = 一切都乱套了吗？


否定句:
The equipment isn't haywire; it's just old. = 设备没有失灵；它只是旧了。
The plan isn't haywire; it's just complex. = 计划并没有乱套；它只是很复杂。


PHRASE->
go haywire = 变得混乱；失控
get haywire = 变得混乱；失灵
drive someone haywire = 使某人发疯；使某人抓狂
send something haywire = 使某物失灵；使某物混乱
a haywire system = 一个失灵的系统

词源:
The term likely originated from the image of hay (dried grass for animal feed) becoming tangled and disordered, thus "wire" likely refers to nerves or systems becoming similarly tangled and out of order.  The exact origin is difficult to pinpoint with certainty.
