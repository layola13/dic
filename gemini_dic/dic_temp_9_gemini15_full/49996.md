denizenize:/ˈdenɪzənaɪz/ | vt. |使成为居民；使归化；使习惯于 |To grant the rights and privileges of a citizen to; to naturalize.  To accustom or habituate to a particular place or environment. |例句：The government denizenized the refugees, granting them citizenship. 政府使难民成为公民，给予他们公民身份。

The newly arrived immigrants quickly denizenized themselves to the local culture. 新来的移民很快适应了当地的文化。|近义词：naturalize, acclimatize, habituate |反义词：expatriate, alienate, estrange
