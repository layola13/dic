pseudoanemia:/ˌsuːdoʊəˈniːmiə/| n. | 假性贫血 | A condition in which the blood appears anemic (low in red blood cells or hemoglobin) due to factors other than actual blood loss or destruction of red blood cells.  It's characterized by a reduced red blood cell count, but without the typical symptoms of anemia, such as fatigue or shortness of breath.  The reduced red blood cell count is often due to increased plasma volume. |  The patient presented with pseudoanemia, showing a low hematocrit but no signs of anemia.  这位病人表现出假性贫血，血细胞比容低，但没有贫血症状。  | anemia, low hematocrit |  true anemia, polycythemia |  名词
