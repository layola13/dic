operettas:/ˌɒpəˈretəz/| n. |轻歌剧；小歌剧 |A short opera, typically a light-hearted work with spoken dialogue, singing, and often a comedic plot. Operettas are known for their blend of music, drama, and humor, often featuring romantic storylines and catchy melodies. They are typically less serious and grand in scale than full-fledged operas. |Example: The Gilbert and Sullivan operettas are famous for their witty lyrics and memorable tunes. 吉尔伯特和萨利文创作的轻歌剧以其诙谐的歌词和令人难忘的曲调而闻名。|近义词：light opera, musical comedy |反义词：grand opera
