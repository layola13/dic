defenseman:/dɪˈfɛnsmən/| n. |后卫，防守队员 |A defenseman is a player in certain team sports, such as ice hockey, whose primary responsibility is to defend their team's goal.  They prevent the opposing team from scoring. |Example:  The defenseman blocked the shot and saved the goal.  后卫挡住了射门并保住了球门。

The team's defensemen played a crucial role in their victory. 球队的后卫们在他们的胜利中发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：defender, back, rearguard |反义词：forward, attacker

WORD ORIGIN->
The word "defenseman" is a compound word formed from "defense" and "man."  "Defense" comes from the Old French word "defense," meaning "defense, protection." "Man" is an Old English word. Therefore, "defenseman" literally means "a man who defends."
