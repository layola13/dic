pre-Thanksgiving:/ˌpriːˈθæŋksˌɡɪvɪŋ/| adj.|感恩节前的|The period of time immediately before Thanksgiving. It often involves preparations for the holiday, such as shopping for groceries, cooking, and decorating.|例句：We're busy with pre-Thanksgiving preparations. 我们正忙着感恩节前的准备工作。|近义词：ante-Thanksgiving, pre-holiday|反义词：post-Thanksgiving
