procrastinatively:/prəˈkræstɪneɪtɪvli/| adv.|拖延地，磨蹭地|In a manner characterized by procrastination; delaying or postponing something.|He completed the project procrastinatively, leaving everything to the last minute. 他拖延地完成了项目，把所有事情都留到最后一刻。|例句：She worked procrastinatively, finishing her assignment just before the deadline. 她拖拖拉拉地工作，直到截止日期前才完成作业。|近义词：lazily, tardily, dilatorily|反义词：promptly, expeditiously, diligently
