finis:/ˈfaɪnɪs/| n.|结束，终结，结尾|The end or conclusion of something.|The author wrote "finis" at the end of the book. 作者在书的结尾写了“finis”。|近义词：end, conclusion, termination|反义词：beginning, commencement


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The play finishe at 10 pm. = 戏剧在晚上十点结束。
The project finishes next week. = 项目下周结束。


一般过去时:
The meeting finished early. = 会议提前结束了。
The race finished in a tie. = 比赛以平局结束。


现在完成时:
He has finished his work. = 他已经完成了他的工作。
She has finished reading the book. = 她已经读完了那本书。


过去完成时:
The film had finished before we arrived. = 我们到达之前电影已经结束了。
They had finished eating when I called. = 我打电话的时候他们已经吃完了。


将来时:
The concert will finish at midnight. = 音乐会将在午夜结束。
The game will finish soon. = 游戏很快就会结束。


虚拟语气:
If the war had finished earlier, many lives would have been saved. = 如果战争早点结束，许多生命就能得救。
I wish the project had finished on time. = 我希望项目能按时完成。


被动语态:
The work was finished quickly. = 工作很快完成了。
The meeting was finished before noon. = 会议在中午之前结束了。


疑问句:
Has the game finished yet? = 比赛结束了吗？
When will the concert finish? = 音乐会什么时候结束？


否定句:
The work is not finished yet. = 工作还没完成。
The book hasn't finished yet. = 书还没读完。
