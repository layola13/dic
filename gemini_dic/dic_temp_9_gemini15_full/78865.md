gingival:/dʒɪnˈdʒaɪvəl/| adj.|牙龈的，齿龈的|Relating to the gums.  The word refers to the tissues surrounding the teeth.|例句：The dentist examined the gingival tissues for signs of inflammation. 牙医检查了牙龈组织是否有炎症迹象。

例句：He experienced gingival recession due to aggressive brushing. 由于用力刷牙，他出现了牙龈萎缩。

近义词：gum, gum tissue

反义词：None that directly oppose the meaning.  Words describing conditions *opposite* to healthy gingiva might be used, such as "inflamed," "recessed," or "diseased."
