conflux:/ˈkɒnflʌks/ /ˈkɑːnflʌks/| n. |汇合；合流；融合 |A conflux is the merging or flowing together of two or more things, especially rivers or streams. It can also refer to a gathering or confluence of people or ideas.  |Example: The confluence of the two rivers created a large, fertile delta.  两条河流的汇合形成了一个巨大的肥沃三角洲。

Example: The confluence of political and economic factors led to the crisis. 政治和经济因素的汇合导致了这场危机。|近义词：junction, confluence, merger, convergence |反义词：divergence, separation, division |名词
