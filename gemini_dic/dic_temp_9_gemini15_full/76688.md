galvanopuncture:/ˌɡælˌvænəˈpʌŋkʧər/| n. |电流针刺疗法|A therapeutic technique that combines the principles of acupuncture and the application of a direct electrical current.  It involves inserting needles into acupuncture points and then passing a weak electrical current through the needles.  This is believed to stimulate the body's natural healing processes and reduce pain.| Example:  The patient underwent galvanopuncture treatment for chronic back pain.  病人接受了电流针刺疗法治疗慢性背痛。|近义词：electroacupuncture (电针)|反义词：None directly applicable.
