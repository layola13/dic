halieutics:/ˌhæliˈjuːtɪks/| n. |渔业学，渔业；捕鱼技术|The scientific study of fisheries, including their biology, ecology, and management.  It encompasses various aspects of fishing, from the biology of fish to the socio-economic impacts of the fishing industry.|例句：Halieutics research is crucial for sustainable fishing practices. 渔业学研究对于可持续的捕捞实践至关重要。|近义词：fisheries science, pisciculture (in part)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite could be considered land-based agriculture.
