indulges:/ɪnˈdʌldʒɪz/ | vt. |纵容；沉溺；享受 | To allow oneself to enjoy a luxury, or to do something that one knows is not good for one.  |例句：She indulges in chocolate every now and then. 她偶尔会纵容自己吃巧克力。
He indulged his love for classical music by attending concerts regularly. 他定期参加音乐会，以此来沉溺于他对古典音乐的热爱。|近义词：pamper, spoil, gratify, humor |反义词：deny, restrain, curb, discipline |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He indulges in video games every evening. = 他每天晚上都沉迷于电子游戏。
She indulges her sweet tooth with regular ice cream treats. = 她经常用冰淇淋来满足她对甜食的渴望。

一般过去时:
I indulged in a long bath after a hard day's work. = 辛苦工作一天后，我好好泡了个澡。
They indulged themselves with a luxurious vacation in the Caribbean. = 他们在加勒比海享受了一次奢华的假期。

现在完成时:
He has indulged in too many sweets lately and feels unwell. = 他最近吃太多甜食，感觉不舒服。
She has indulged her artistic talents by taking painting classes. = 她通过参加绘画课程来发挥她的艺术才能。

过去完成时:
Having indulged in a large meal, he felt too full to go for a walk. = 因为吃了一顿大餐，他感觉太饱了，不想去散步。
They had indulged in several glasses of wine before driving home, which was very dangerous. = 他们在开车回家前喝了几杯酒，这非常危险。

将来时:
I will indulge myself with a massage after the marathon. = 马拉松结束后，我要给自己做个按摩。
We will indulge in some fine dining at a Michelin-starred restaurant. = 我们将在米其林星级餐厅享用美食。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would indulge in painting landscapes. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会沉迷于画风景画。
I wish I could indulge in a long vacation, but I have too much work. = 我真希望我能休一个长假，但我工作太多。

被动语态:
His love for sweets was indulged by his parents. = 他对甜食的喜爱被他的父母纵容了。
The children were indulged with expensive toys.  = 孩子们被昂贵的玩具宠坏了。

疑问句:
Does she often indulge in unhealthy snacks? = 她经常吃不健康的小吃吗？
Do you ever indulge in daydreaming? = 你会做白日梦吗？

否定句:
He didn't indulge in any alcohol at the party. = 他在派对上没有喝酒。
She doesn't indulge herself with expensive clothes. = 她不会买昂贵的衣服来取悦自己。
