penninerved:/ˈpɛnɪnɜːvd/| adj.|羽状脉的，羽状叶脉的|Having veins arranged like a feather, with smaller veins branching off a central vein.  The term is primarily used in botany to describe the venation pattern of leaves.|例句：The leaves of the oak tree are penninerved, exhibiting a clear central vein with smaller veins branching out.|近义词：pinnate-veined|反义词：palmate-veined
