plutocracies:/ˈpluːtəkrəsiːz/| n.|寡头政治，财阀统治|A plutocracy is a society or system ruled or controlled by the wealthy.  It's a government where power is held by the small minority of wealthy elite.  The term often implies that this power is exerted to maintain and enhance their own wealth and power, often at the expense of the wider population.|例句：Some critics argue that the current political system is drifting towards a plutocracy, where the voices of the wealthy drown out those of the average citizen.  一些评论家认为，目前的政治制度正在向寡头政治转变，富人的声音压过了普通公民的声音。|近义词：oligarchy, aristocracy, kleptocracy|反义词：democracy, meritocracy
