salons:/səˈlɒnz/| n.|沙龙；客厅；展览室|A salon is a social gathering, typically of intellectuals or artists, at which topics of common interest are discussed.  It can also refer to a shop providing a specific service, such as a hair salon. |例句：She hosted regular salons in her elegant apartment. = 她定期在她优雅的公寓里举办沙龙。|例句：I got my hair cut at a new salon downtown. = 我在市中心的一家新美发沙龙剪了头发。|近义词：gathering, meeting, shop|反义词：None specifically, depends on context.
