/ˈreɪdər/| n. v.| 袭击者；侵略者；抢劫者；突袭者 | A person who attacks or invades a place, especially suddenly or unexpectedly; someone who commits robbery or raids.  |例句：The raiders stole all the valuable jewels. 袭击者偷走了所有贵重的珠宝。|例句：The Viking raiders attacked the coastal villages. 维京海盗袭击了沿海村庄。|近义词：attacker, invader, robber, plunderer |反义词：defender, protector


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The raider attacks the village. = 袭击者袭击了村庄。
He raids the bank every Tuesday. = 他每周二都抢劫银行。


一般过去时:
The raider attacked the village last night. = 袭击者昨晚袭击了村庄。
They raided the museum and stole a priceless painting. = 他们洗劫了博物馆，偷走了一幅无价的画。


现在完成时:
The raiders have already escaped. = 袭击者已经逃跑了。
The police have investigated several bank raids. = 警方已经调查了几起银行抢劫案。


过去完成时:
The villagers had already hidden their valuables before the raiders arrived. = 在袭击者到达之前，村民们已经藏起了他们的贵重物品。
The thieves had planned the raid for months. = 窃贼们策划了数月的抢劫。


将来时:
The army will raid the enemy's base tomorrow. = 军队明天将袭击敌人的基地。
They will likely raid the store again soon. = 他们很可能会很快再次抢劫商店。


虚拟语气:
If the raiders had been caught, they would be in jail now. = 如果袭击者被抓住了，他们现在就会在监狱里。
I wish the raiders hadn't destroyed the ancient artifacts. = 我真希望袭击者没有破坏那些古代文物。


被动语态:
The village was raided by the bandits. = 村庄被土匪袭击了。
The bank was robbed last night. = 银行昨晚被抢劫了。


疑问句:
Who were the raiders? = 袭击者是谁？
When did the raid take place? = 袭击发生在什么时候？


否定句:
The raiders did not steal anything of value. = 袭击者没有偷走任何有价值的东西。
He did not participate in the raid. = 他没有参与这次袭击。
