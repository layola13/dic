goodly:/ˈɡʊdli/| adj.|相当大的；美好的；漂亮的；令人愉悦的|  Signifying something of considerable size, beauty, or excellence. It often implies a pleasing abundance or a substantial amount.|例句：They harvested a goodly amount of wheat this year. 他们今年收割了大量的麦子。

例句：The garden boasted a goodly number of roses in full bloom. 花园里盛开着许多玫瑰花。


近义词：considerable, substantial, ample, fine, beautiful, pleasant|反义词：meager, insufficient, paltry, ugly, unpleasant|词性:形容词
