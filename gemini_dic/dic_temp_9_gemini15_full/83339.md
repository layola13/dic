gypsying:/ˈdʒɪpsɪŋ/| n. |游荡；流浪；吉普赛式的生活 |The act of traveling and living a nomadic lifestyle, often associated with Romani people (Gypsies).  It implies a carefree and adventurous spirit, often involving improvisation and spontaneity. |例句：They spent the summer gypsying around Europe in a camper van.  他们夏天开着房车在欧洲四处游荡。

They embraced a life of gypsying, moving from place to place with no fixed abode. 他们过着四处游荡的生活，居无定所。|近义词：wandering, roaming, rambling, traveling |反义词：settling down, staying put
