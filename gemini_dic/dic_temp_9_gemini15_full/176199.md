re-assertions:/ˌriːəˈsɜːrʃənz/ | n. | 重申；再次声明 | Repeated statements or declarations, often emphasizing a previous claim.  The re-assertions of his innocence were met with skepticism.  | 近义词：reaffirmations, reiterations, reiterations, confirmations | 反义词：retractions, denials | 名词


例句：

His re-assertions of loyalty to the company were unconvincing after his recent actions.
他的再次声明对公司的忠诚在他最近的行为之后是令人难以置信的。

The government's re-assertions of its commitment to economic reform were welcomed by investors.
政府再次承诺进行经济改革，受到了投资者的欢迎。
