overmoralize:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəlaɪz/ | vt. | 过分强调道德；说教；道德说教 | To moralize excessively or to an inappropriate degree; to preach or lecture excessively on morality. |例句：He overmoralized the simple act of sharing a cigarette. 他对仅仅是分享一支香烟的行为进行了过分的道德说教。


例句：The teacher overmoralized the story, losing the children's interest. 老师对故事进行了过度的道德说教，孩子们失去了兴趣。


近义词：preach, sermonize, moralize excessively, lecture


反义词：understate, downplay, ignore moral implications


词性：动词
