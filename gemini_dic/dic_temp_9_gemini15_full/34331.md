clashers:/ˈklæʃərz/ | n. |冲突者，发生冲突的人或物 |People or things that clash or are in conflict.  The term often refers to players or groups in a competitive game, especially in the context of the mobile game "Clash of Clans".| Example: The clashers fought fiercely for control of the central resource.  冲突者为了争夺中心资源而激烈战斗。 The two armies were fierce clashers. 这两支军队是激烈的冲突者。|近义词：combatants, opponents, rivals |反义词：allies, collaborators, peacemakers |名词
