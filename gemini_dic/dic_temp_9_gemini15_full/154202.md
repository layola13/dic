peripatetic: /ˌpɛrɪpəˈtɛtɪk/ | adj. |巡游的；流动的；四处游历的 |  A peripatetic person is someone who travels from place to place, often working or living in various locations for relatively short periods.  The term often implies a wandering lifestyle, not necessarily tied to a single home or job.  It can also describe something that is characterized by movement or change. | Example:  He led a peripatetic life, teaching at universities across the country. 他过着四处游历的生活，在全国各地的大学任教。


Example: Her peripatetic career as a freelance journalist took her to many exotic locations.  她作为自由撰稿人的四处奔波的职业生涯带她去了许多异国情调的地方。


近义词: itinerant, wandering, nomadic, transient


反义词: sedentary, stationary, settled


词性: adjective
