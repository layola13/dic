self-idolatries:/ˌsɛlf ˈaɪˈdɑːlətriz/| n.|自我崇拜；自恋；自我中心|The excessive admiration of oneself;  a preoccupation with one's own importance or achievements, often to the exclusion of others' needs and feelings.  It can manifest as narcissism, vanity, or egotism.|例句：His self-idolatries prevented him from seeing his flaws and accepting constructive criticism.  他的自我崇拜使他看不到自己的缺点，无法接受建设性批评。


例句：Her constant boasting about her accomplishments revealed a deep-seated self-idolatry. 她不断吹嘘自己的成就，暴露了她根深蒂固的自我崇拜。


近义词：narcissism, egotism, vanity, self-absorption, self-centeredness


反义词：humility, selflessness, modesty


词性:noun
