loins:/lɔɪnz/| n.|腰部，臀部；（动物的）腰里脊肉|The lower part of the back, between the ribs and the hips; also, the corresponding part of an animal, often used as meat.|例句：He felt a sharp pain in his loins. 他感到腰部一阵剧痛。|例句：The roast was made from the finest cuts of lamb loins. 烤肉是用最好的羊腰肉做的。|近义词：waist, back, flank|反义词：无
