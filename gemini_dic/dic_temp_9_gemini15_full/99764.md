intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/| adj.| 中级的，中间的；中级的 |  Lying between two extremes; of an intermediate stage or degree.  It refers to something that is between a beginner and advanced level. For example, an intermediate level of English proficiency means the person can understand and use the language better than a beginner but is not yet fluent. |例句：He's an intermediate level swimmer. 他是中级游泳选手。

She is taking an intermediate course in accounting. 她正在上中级会计课程。


近义词：middling, average, moderate | 反义词：beginner, advanced, elementary, expert


词源:  From Middle French *intermediat, from Latin *intermediatus, past participle of intermèdia-re "to be between," from inter "between" + medius "middle."
