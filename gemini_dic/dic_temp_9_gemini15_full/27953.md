cantonese:/ˌkæn.təˈniːz/| n. |粤语，广东话 |The Cantonese language is a Chinese dialect spoken primarily in Guangdong and Guangxi provinces of southern China, as well as Hong Kong and Macau. It's known for its tonal nature and unique vocabulary. |Example:  She speaks fluent Cantonese and Mandarin. 她能流利地说粤语和普通话。|近义词：Guangdong dialect, Yue Chinese |反义词:  (No direct antonym;  other Chinese dialects could be considered contrastive)|名词
