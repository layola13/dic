skulking:/ˈskʌlkiŋ/| v.|潜行；偷偷摸摸；躲躲藏藏|To move or behave in a secretive or furtive manner, typically with a sense of guilt or wrongdoing.  It implies a sense of stealth and avoidance.|例句：The cat was skulking in the shadows. 猫在阴影中偷偷摸摸地走动。

The thief was skulking around the back of the house. 小偷在房子后面鬼鬼祟祟地转悠。


近义词：sneaking, slinking, lurking, creeping, prowling|反义词：striding, swaggering, parading


词源:源自古英语 *sculcian，意为“拖着脚走，懒散地走”。  This reflects the slow, secretive movement often associated with skulking.  The modern meaning developed over time to encompass the secretive and furtive aspects of the movement.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He skulks around the corridors. 他在走廊里偷偷摸摸地走动。
She skulks in the shadows, avoiding detection. 她躲在阴影里，避免被发现。


一般过去时:
The dog skulked away when it saw me. 那条狗看到我后偷偷溜走了。
He skulked behind the tree, hoping to remain unseen. 他躲在树后，希望能不被看见。


现在完成时:
He has been skulking around all day. 他整天都在偷偷摸摸地转悠。
She has skulked away to avoid responsibility. 她偷偷溜走了以逃避责任。


过去完成时:
The burglar had skulked away before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，窃贼已经偷偷溜走了。
They had been skulking in the alley for hours. 他们已经在巷子里躲躲藏藏好几个小时了。


将来时:
He will skulk away when he hears the police sirens. 他听到警笛声就会偷偷溜走。

虚拟语气:
If he skulked any longer, he would be caught. 如果他再躲下去，就会被抓住。
I wish he wouldn't skulk around so much. 我希望他不要总是偷偷摸摸的。


被动语态: (Skulking is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Was he skulking in the garden? 他是不是在花园里偷偷摸摸的？

否定句:
He didn't skulk away; he walked confidently. 他没有偷偷溜走；他自信地走着。
She didn't skulk; she faced the problem head-on. 她没有躲躲藏藏；她直接面对了问题。
