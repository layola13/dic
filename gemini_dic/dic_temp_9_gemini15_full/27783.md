cannibals:/ˈkænɪbəlz/| n. |食人族；食人者 |People who eat human flesh.  Cannibals are often portrayed in fiction as savage and violent, but the practice of cannibalism has existed in various cultures throughout history for different reasons, including ritualistic practices, survival, and warfare. |Example: The explorers encountered a tribe of cannibals deep in the jungle. 探险家们在丛林深处遇到了一支食人族。|近义词：man-eaters, anthropophages |反义词：vegetarian, herbivore
