misdirecting: /ˌmɪsdaɪˈrektɪŋ/ | adj. | 误导性的，使人误解的 | Leading someone to believe something that is untrue or misleading; causing someone to go in the wrong direction. | 例句：The advertisement was misdirecting, promising benefits it couldn't deliver. 这则广告具有误导性，承诺了它无法兑现的好处。

The misleading information caused the hikers to take a misdirecting path, leading them far off course.  这些误导性信息导致徒步旅行者走错了路，使他们偏离了路线很远。| 近义词：deceptive, misleading, confusing, deceptive, ambiguous | 反义词：accurate, truthful, straightforward, clear, unambiguous | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advertisement is misdirecting consumers. = 广告正在误导消费者。
That sign is misdirecting people towards the wrong building. = 那个标志正在把人们引向错误的建筑物。


一般过去时:
The map misdirected us. = 地图误导了我们。
His instructions were misdirecting, leading to much confusion. = 他的指示是误导性的，导致了很多混乱。


现在完成时:
The campaign has misdirected public opinion. = 该活动已误导了公众舆论。
The rumors have misdirected the investigation. = 谣言已经误导了调查。


过去完成时:
They had misdirected the funds before the audit. = 他们在审计之前已经挪用了资金。(虽然“misdirect funds”更常用于主动语态，但此句为了符合要求而使用被动语态，意思略有不同。)
The detective realized he had been misdirected by the suspect's false statement. = 侦探意识到他被嫌疑人的虚假陈述误导了。


将来时:
The new policy will misdirect resources. = 新政策将导致资源错配。
I fear that the changes will misdirect the team's efforts. = 我担心这些变化会使团队的努力方向错位。


虚拟语气:
If the instructions weren't so misdirecting, we would have arrived on time. = 如果指示不是那么误导性，我们就会准时到达。
I wish the map hadn't been so misdirecting; we would have found the trail much sooner. = 我真希望地图没有那么误导性，我们会早得多找到小路。


被动语态:
The public was misdirected by false promises. = 公众被虚假承诺所误导。
The funds were misdirected, leading to a financial crisis. = 资金被错用，导致了金融危机。


疑问句:
Is the sign misdirecting people? = 这个标志是否在误导人们？
Were the instructions misdirecting? = 指示有误导性吗？


否定句:
The instructions were not misdirecting; they were perfectly clear. = 指示没有误导性；它们非常清楚。
The map was not misdirecting; we simply misread it. = 地图没有误导我们；我们只是读错了它。
