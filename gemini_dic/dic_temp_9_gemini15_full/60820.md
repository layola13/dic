electroplates:/ɪˌlɛkˈtroʊpleɪts/ | vt. | 电镀 | To coat (a metal object) with a layer of another metal by electrolysis.  This process uses an electric current to deposit a thin layer of metal onto a conductive surface. | 例句：They electroplated the jewelry with gold to enhance its appearance.  他们用金电镀首饰以增强其外观。

They electroplated the car parts to prevent rust. 他们对汽车零件进行电镀以防止生锈。


近义词：plate, coat, galvanize | 反义词：strip, remove
