pentagrams:/ˈpɛntəˌɡræmz/| n.|五角星，五芒星|A pentagram is a five-pointed star, often associated with magic and mysticism.  It is formed by drawing a continuous line that intersects itself five times.|例句：The artist incorporated pentagrams into the design of the album cover.  艺术家在专辑封面的设计中加入了五角星。 The ancient symbol of the pentagram was found carved into the stone.古老的五角星符号被发现刻在石头上。|近义词：five-pointed star, pentacle|反义词：None specifically.
