nonserials:/ˈnɒnˈsɪəriəlz/ | n. |非连续剧集；非连续剧|  Items or programs that are not part of a continuous series or sequence.  These are often individual works, unlike those belonging to a series with a continuing storyline or set of characters.|Example: The library has a great selection of nonserials, including many stand-alone novels and documentaries.|近义词：standalone works, individual items, independent productions|反义词：series, serials, sequels
