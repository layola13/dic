innuendo:/ɪˈnjuːɛndoʊ/ | n. |影射；暗讽；弦外之音 | A suggestion or hint of something bad or unpleasant.  It often involves indirect or subtle references to something inappropriate or taboo. | 例句：His speech was full of innuendo. 他的演讲充满了影射。  The comedian's jokes were filled with sexual innuendo.  这位喜剧演员的笑话充满了性暗示。 | 近义词：insinuation, implication, suggestion, hint | 反义词：direct statement, explicit statement |  名词
