flamenco:/fləˈmɛŋkoʊ/| n. |弗拉明戈舞（一种西班牙舞蹈）|Flamenco is a passionate and expressive dance form originating from Andalusia, Spain. It is characterized by its rhythmic footwork, hand claps, and emotional vocals.|The flamenco dancer captivated the audience with her powerful movements and intense emotions.  弗拉明戈舞者以其强劲的动作和强烈的情感吸引了观众。|Spanish dance, Andalusian dance|ballet, classical dance|n.
