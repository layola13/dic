cacher:/kæʃeɪr/| v. |隐藏；储藏；缓存|To hide or store something, especially in a secret place.  It also refers to storing data in a cache for faster retrieval.|The spy cacher the documents in a hollow tree. 间谍把文件藏在一棵空心树里。|The browser caches frequently accessed webpages. 浏览器缓存经常访问的网页。|conceal, hide, secrete|reveal, expose|动词
