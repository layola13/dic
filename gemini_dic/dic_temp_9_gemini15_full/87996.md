high-ranking:/ˈhaɪˌræŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | 高级别的，高级别的；地位高的 |  Describes a person or position that holds a high level of authority or importance within an organization or system.  |  She held a high-ranking position in the government. 她在一个政府部门担任要职。|  senior, superior, top-level | low-ranking, junior, subordinate |

That employee holds a high-ranking position. = 那个员工担任要职。
