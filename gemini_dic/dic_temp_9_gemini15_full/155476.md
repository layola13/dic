phenomenology:/ˌfɪˌnɒmɪˈnɒlədʒi/|/ˌfɛnɑːmɪˈnɑːlədʒi/| n. |现象学|The study of structures of consciousness as experienced from the first-person point of view.  Phenomenology focuses on describing the essential structures of experience, rather than explaining their underlying causes. |Example:  The philosopher's work explored phenomenology, focusing on the lived experience of time.  哲学家的作品探讨了现象学，重点是时间的体验。|近义词：descriptive psychology, experiential analysis |反义词：positivism, empiricism
