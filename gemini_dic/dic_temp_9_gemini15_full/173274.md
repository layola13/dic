quasi-constructively:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈstrʌktɪvli/ | adv. | 准构造性地，近乎构造性地 |  Describes a method or process that resembles constructive methods but doesn't strictly adhere to all their requirements. It might involve non-constructive steps or rely on certain assumptions that aren't fully justified constructively.  |  The proof was presented quasi-constructively, relying on a non-constructive lemma in one step.  |  constructively, effectively |  destructively, non-constructively | 副词
