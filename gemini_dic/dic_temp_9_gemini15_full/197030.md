shallon:/ˈʃælən/| n.|美洲茶藨子；美洲越橘|A type of evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America, known for its edible berries.  The berries are often used in jams, jellies, and other food products.|例句：The trail led through a dense thicket of shallon bushes.  这条小路穿过一片茂密的沙龙灌木丛。

例句：We collected a basketful of ripe shallon berries for making jam. 我们收集了一篮子成熟的沙龙浆果用来做果酱。


近义词：salal (similar evergreen shrub with edible berries)|反义词：无


词性：名词
