igniferous:/ɪɡˈnɪfərəs/| adj.|含火的，火成的，火山喷发的|Bearing or containing fire; produced by fire; of volcanic origin.  The word describes something that is characterized by or related to fire, particularly volcanic fire.|The igniferous rocks of the region provided evidence of past volcanic activity.|近义词：fiery, volcanic, fiery, igneous|反义词：watery, ice-cold
