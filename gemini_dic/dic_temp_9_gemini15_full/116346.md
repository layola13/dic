masterwork:/ˈmɑːstərˌwɜːrk/| n. |杰作，名作 |A masterwork is a work of art, especially one of outstanding skill or artistry.  It is a piece of work that shows a high level of skill and artistry and is considered to be of exceptional quality. |例句：Shakespeare's Hamlet is considered a masterwork of English literature.莎士比亚的《哈姆雷特》被认为是英国文学的杰作。

例句：The Sistine Chapel ceiling is a breathtaking masterwork of Renaissance art. 西斯廷教堂的天花板是文艺复兴时期艺术的令人惊叹的杰作。

近义词：masterpiece, classic, magnum opus |反义词：duffer, flop, botch
