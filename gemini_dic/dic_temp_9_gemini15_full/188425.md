rud:/rʌd/| adj.|粗鲁的；无礼的；粗糙的|rude means showing a lack of consideration for others; not polite.|例句：It's rude to interrupt someone when they're speaking.  打断别人说话是不礼貌的。|例句：The surface of the wood was very rud, making it difficult to paint. 木头的表面很粗糙，很难上漆。|近义词：impolite, discourteous, coarse, rough|反义词：polite, courteous, refined, smooth|形容词


USAGE->

She is always rude to waiters. = 她总是对服务员很粗鲁。
