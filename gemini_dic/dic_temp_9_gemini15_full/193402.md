seismograph:/ˈsaɪzməɡræf/| n. |地震仪|An instrument that measures and records the intensity and direction of seismic waves caused by earthquakes or other ground vibrations.|The seismograph recorded a magnitude 7.0 earthquake.|近义词：seismometer|反义词：无
