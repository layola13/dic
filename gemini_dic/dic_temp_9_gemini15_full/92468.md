hypostome:/ˈhaɪpəstoʊm/| n. |下口, 口基|The hypostome is the mouth or oral opening of certain invertebrates, particularly cnidarians (like jellyfish and sea anemones) and some other animals.  It's often located on the underside of the body.  |Example: The sea anemone extended its hypostome to capture a passing shrimp. 海葵伸出下口捕捉一只经过的虾。|近义词：oral opening, mouth|反义词：N/A
