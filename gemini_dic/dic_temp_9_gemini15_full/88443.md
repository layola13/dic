hissing:/ˈhɪsɪŋ/| adj. v.|嘶嘶声，发出嘶嘶声|making a hissing sound;  a sound like that made by a snake or escaping steam.|例句：The snake hissed menacingly.  那条蛇发出威胁的嘶嘶声。 The escaping steam hissed from the pipe. 从管道里喷出的蒸汽发出嘶嘶声。|近义词：sizzling, whispering, spitting|反义词：booming, roaring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The kettle is hissing. = 水壶在嘶嘶作响。
The tires are hissing as the car brakes. = 汽车刹车时轮胎发出嘶嘶声。

一般过去时:
The snake hissed at me. = 蛇朝我嘶嘶作响。
The radiator hissed all night. = 暖气片整夜都在嘶嘶作响。

现在完成时:
The engine has been hissing since this morning. = 引擎从今天早上就开始嘶嘶作响了。
The pressure cooker has hissed and released steam. = 高压锅嘶嘶作响并释放了蒸汽。

过去完成时:
The hissing had stopped before I opened the door. = 我开门之前嘶嘶声就停了。
The tires had been hissing for miles before they blew. = 轮胎在爆胎前已经嘶嘶作响了好几英里。

将来时:
The pressure cooker will hiss when the steam builds up. = 当蒸汽积聚时，高压锅会嘶嘶作响。
The escaping gas will hiss as it vents. = 泄漏的气体会嘶嘶作响。

虚拟语气:
If the snake hissed again, I would run away. = 如果蛇再次嘶嘶作响，我会跑开。
I wish the faulty pipe wouldn't hiss so loudly. = 我希望有问题的管道不会发出这么大的嘶嘶声。

被动语态:
The sound of hissing was heard in the distance. = 远处传来嘶嘶的声音。
A hissing sound was detected by the sensor. = 传感器检测到嘶嘶声。

疑问句:
Is that a snake hissing? = 那是不是蛇在嘶嘶作响？
What is causing that hissing sound? = 这是什么声音？

否定句:
The engine wasn't hissing; it was just a leak. = 引擎没有嘶嘶作响；只是漏水了。
The tires didn't hiss when I braked gently. = 我轻踩刹车时轮胎没有嘶嘶作响。
