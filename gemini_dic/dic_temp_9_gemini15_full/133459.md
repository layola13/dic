词源:
The word "nonfeasibilities" is derived from the word "feasible," meaning capable of being done or accomplished.  The prefix "non-" means "not," thus "nonfeasibilities" refers to things that are not feasible or possible.  The "-ilities" suffix forms a plural noun indicating a quality or state.
