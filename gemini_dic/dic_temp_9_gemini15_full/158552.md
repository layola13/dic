plethora:/ˈplɛθərə/| n.|过量；过剩；大量|An excessive amount of something.  It often implies an overwhelming or excessive quantity.|There is a plethora of books on the subject.  这个主题有很多书。|abundance, profusion, superfluity|scarcity, dearth, paucity|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is a plethora of options. =  有很多选择。
A plethora of problems exists. =  存在着大量的问题。
