muffler:/ˈmʌflər/| n. |消音器；围巾；マフラー|A muffler is a device fitted to the exhaust pipe of an internal combustion engine to reduce the noise of the exhaust gases.  It can also refer to a scarf worn around the neck to keep warm. |例句：The car's muffler was rusty and needed replacing.  这辆车的消音器生锈了，需要更换。

例句：She wrapped a warm wool muffler around her neck. 她围了一条温暖的羊毛围巾。|近义词：silencer (for exhaust), scarf, shawl |反义词：amplifier (opposite function of a muffler as a silencer)
