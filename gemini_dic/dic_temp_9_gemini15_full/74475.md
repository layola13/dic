frankfurter:/ˈfræŋkfɜːrtər/|/ˈfræŋkfɜːrtɚ/| n. |法兰克福香肠；热狗|A frankfurter is a type of sausage, typically made of beef, pork, or a combination of both, often served in a hot dog bun.  It's named after Frankfurt, Germany, where it originated.|例句：I ordered a frankfurter with mustard and relish. 我点了一份加芥末和调味酱的法兰克福香肠。|例句：He ate three frankfurters at the baseball game. 他在棒球比赛中吃了三根热狗。|近义词：hot dog, sausage, wiener|反义词：无
