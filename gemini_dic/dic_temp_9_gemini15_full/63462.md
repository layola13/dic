entrepreneurs:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːrz/| n. |企业家，创业者| A person who organizes and manages a business undertaking, especially one with considerable initiative and risk.  They are individuals who identify opportunities, gather resources, and take risks to create and manage a business. |Many entrepreneurs started their businesses in their garages.|近义词：businessperson, founder, innovator|反义词：employee, worker


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Entrepreneurs often face many challenges. = 企业家经常面临许多挑战。
She identifies new market opportunities as an entrepreneur. = 她作为一名企业家，能够发现新的市场机会。


一般过去时:
He was a successful entrepreneur in the tech industry. = 他是科技行业一位成功的企业家。
Many entrepreneurs participated in the startup competition. = 许多企业家参加了创业比赛。


现在完成时:
She has built several successful businesses as an entrepreneur. = 她作为一名企业家已经建立了几家成功的企业。
Numerous entrepreneurs have changed the world through innovation. = 许多企业家通过创新改变了世界。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
