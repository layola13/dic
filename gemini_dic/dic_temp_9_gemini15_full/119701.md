midnights:/ˈmɪdnaɪts/| n.|午夜；半夜|The time of twelve o'clock at night.  It can also refer to the period around midnight.|例句：We arrived at our destination just before midnights. 我们在午夜前到达目的地。

例句：The party went on until midnights. 派对一直持续到午夜。


近义词：midnight hour, twelve o'clock at night
反义词：noon, midday


词性:名词
