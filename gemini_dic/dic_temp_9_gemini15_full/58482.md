dudgeons:/ˈdʌdʒənz/| n.|怨恨，怒气|A state of sulky displeasure or resentment.|He stormed off in a fit of dudgeon. 他怒气冲冲地走了。|近义词：resentment, anger, displeasure|反义词：pleasure, contentment, happiness
