mirid:/ˈmɪrɪd/| n. |盲蝽科昆虫|A mirid is a small insect belonging to the family Miridae, a large family of true bugs.  They are often plant feeders, with some species being considered pests. |Example:  Many mirids are plant bugs that feed on sap. 许多盲蝽是吸食植物汁液的昆虫。|近义词: plant bug, capsid bug |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑用捕食盲蝽的昆虫作为间接反义词，例如：predatory insect)|名词
