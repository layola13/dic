glassiness:/ˈɡlæsinɪs/| adj.|玻璃状；呆滞；无光泽|Having the appearance of glass; smooth and shiny like glass.  It can also describe a dull, lifeless appearance in the eyes, suggesting lack of emotion or awareness. |例句：The glassiness of the glaze on the pottery was exquisite. 陶瓷上的釉面的玻璃质感非常精美。

The glassiness in her eyes suggested she was deeply saddened. 她眼神中的呆滞表明她非常悲伤。|近义词：smoothness, shininess, dullness, lifelessness|反义词：brightness, vibrancy, sparkle, animation
