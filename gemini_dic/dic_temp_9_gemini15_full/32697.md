chlorate:/ˈklɔːreɪt/| n.|氯酸盐|A salt of chloric acid (HClO3), containing the chlorate ion (ClO3−). Chlorates are strong oxidizing agents and are used in various applications, including matches, fireworks, and bleaching agents.|例句：Potassium chlorate is a common oxidizing agent used in fireworks. 氯酸钾是烟花中常用的氧化剂。  Sodium chlorate is used as a herbicide. 氯酸钠用作除草剂。|近义词：perchlorate|反义词：无
