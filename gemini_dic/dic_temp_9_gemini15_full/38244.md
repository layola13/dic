computerizes:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪz/| vt.|使电脑化；用计算机处理|To equip (a system, organization, etc.) with computers or computer technology.|The company computerizes its accounting system to improve efficiency.  这家公司将其会计系统电脑化以提高效率。|The factory is being computerized. 这家工厂正在实现电脑化。|automate, digitize|manual, hand-operated|动词


USAGE->

The company computerizes its data. = 公司将数据电脑化。
They computerize their payroll system. = 他们将工资系统电脑化。

We computerized the entire process last year. = 我们去年将整个流程电脑化了。
