modificative:/ˌmɒdɪfɪˈkeɪtɪv/| adj. |修饰的，起修饰作用的| Having the function or effect of modifying; relating to or involving modification.  It describes a word, phrase, or clause that modifies or changes the meaning of another word or phrase.  | 例句：The modificative adjective "small" changes the meaning of the noun "house".  修饰性形容词“small”改变了名词“house”的意思。


The modificative clause provides extra information about the subject.  这个修饰性从句提供了关于主语的额外信息。| 近义词：qualifying, descriptive, modifying | 反义词：independent (in the context of clauses)
