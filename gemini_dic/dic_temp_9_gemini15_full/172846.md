quadruped:/ˈkwɒdruped/ /ˈkwɑːdruped/| n. |四足动物|A quadruped is an animal that walks on four legs.  It refers to animals with four feet used for locomotion.  The term encompasses a wide range of species, including mammals, reptiles, amphibians, and insects. | Example: Horses, dogs, and cats are all quadrupeds.  马、狗和猫都是四足动物。 Elephants are massive quadrupeds.  大象是巨大的四足动物。|近义词：four-footed animal |反义词：biped
