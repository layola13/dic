mutacism:/ˈmjuːtəsɪzəm/| n. | 变音，换音；语音转换 | The substitution of one sound for another, especially the substitution of a voiced sound for a voiceless sound or vice versa. This often occurs in speech disorders or as a dialectal feature.  | Example: The pronunciation of "pin" as "bin" is an example of mutacism.  变音，例如将“pin”发音为“bin”就是变音的一个例子。|近义词：sound substitution, phonetic change | 反义词：N/A
