radix:/ˈreɪdɪks/ | n. |基数，根，根本 |The radix of a number system is the base of that system,  indicating the number of digits used to represent numbers. For example, the decimal system has a radix of 10, while the binary system has a radix of 2.  It also refers to the root or origin of something. |例句：The radix of the decimal system is 10. 十进制系统的基数是10。 The radix of the problem lies in poor planning.  问题的根源在于计划不周。|近义词：base, root, foundation, origin |反义词：outcome, result, effect
