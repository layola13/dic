invite:/ɪnˈvaɪt/ | vt. |邀请；恳请；招致 |To ask (someone) to come to a social event. To formally request the presence of (someone) at an event. To request (someone) to do something.  |例句：I invited her to my birthday party. 我邀请她参加我的生日派对。

They invited us to dinner. 他们邀请我们吃晚饭。


近义词：ask, request, summon | 反义词：refuse, reject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I invite you to my wedding. 我邀请你参加我的婚礼。
We invite applications for this position. 我们邀请申请这个职位。


一般过去时:
She invited me to her house for tea. 她邀请我去她家喝茶。
They invited him to join their club. 他们邀请他加入他们的俱乐部。


现在完成时:
I have invited all my friends to the party. 我已经邀请所有朋友参加聚会了。
She has invited him to speak at the conference. 她已经邀请他去会议上发言了。


过去完成时:
I had invited them to the concert, but they couldn't make it. 我已经邀请他们去音乐会了，但他们来不了了。
She had invited him to dinner before realizing he was busy. 她邀请他共进晚餐，然后才意识到他很忙。


将来时:
I will invite you to my next party. 我会邀请你参加我下次的派对。
We will invite the minister to speak at the ceremony. 我们将邀请部长在仪式上发言。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had invited her earlier. 我真希望我早点邀请她。
If I were you, I would invite him to the meeting. 如果我是你，我会邀请他参加会议。


被动语态:
I was invited to the party. 我被邀请参加派对了。
He was invited to give a speech. 他被邀请发表演讲。


疑问句:
Were you invited to the party? 你被邀请参加派对了吗？
Will I be invited to the wedding? 我会被邀请参加婚礼吗？


否定句:
I didn't invite him to the party. 我没有邀请他参加派对。
They haven't invited her to the meeting. 他们还没有邀请她参加会议。


PHRASE->
invite someone to dinner  邀请某人共进晚餐
invite trouble 招惹麻烦
invite criticism 招致批评
invite applications 邀请申请
a warm invitation 热情的邀请

词源:
invite  源自古法语 inviter，  最终源自拉丁语 invitare，意思是“to call, beckon”，由in- (表示“进入”) + vitare (表示“道路，方式”)构成。  因此，invite 的本义是“引导，召唤”。  它逐渐演变为现代意义上的“邀请”。
