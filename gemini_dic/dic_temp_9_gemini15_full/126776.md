nanodiamonds:/ˌnænoʊˈdaɪəməndz/| n. |纳米金刚石|Extremely small diamonds, typically less than 100 nanometers in diameter, with unique properties due to their size.  They are used in various applications, including medicine, electronics, and industrial processes.|例句：Researchers are exploring the use of nanodiamonds in targeted drug delivery.  研究人员正在探索纳米金刚石在靶向药物递送中的应用。|近义词：ultrafine diamonds, microdiamonds (depending on size)|反义词：macroscopic diamonds, large diamonds|名词
