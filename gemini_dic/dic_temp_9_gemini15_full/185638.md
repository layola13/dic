reverberators:/rɪˈvɜːrbəreɪtərz/| n. |混响器，回声发生器|A reverberator is a device or system that creates reverberation,  the persistence of sound after the original sound has stopped.  It's used in audio engineering to add depth and spaciousness to recordings or live performances. |Example: The recording studio used a digital reverberator to enhance the vocals.  录音棚使用数字混响器来增强人声。 The concert hall's natural reverberation was impressive, almost like a natural reverberator.  音乐厅的自然混响令人印象深刻，几乎就像一个天然的混响器。|近义词：resonator, echo generator |反义词：absorber, silencer
