microbiologist:/ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |微生物学家|A scientist who studies microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa.  They investigate the structure, function, and behavior of these organisms, as well as their roles in various ecosystems and their impact on human health. |Dr. Lee is a renowned microbiologist who specializes in antibiotic resistance.|近义词：microbiologist, bacteriologist (if specializing in bacteria)|反义词:None.
