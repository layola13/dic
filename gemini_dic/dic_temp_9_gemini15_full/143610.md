outinventing:/ˌaʊtɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/ | v. |胜过…的发明；超过…的创造力 |  To surpass someone or something in inventiveness or creativity. This is a relatively rare word, formed by combining "out-" (meaning to surpass) and "inventing." It implies a significant degree of innovative superiority. |  例句：His design outinvented all the previous attempts, leading to a revolutionary product. 他的设计超过了以前所有的尝试，创造出了一种革命性的产品。

The company's new marketing strategy outinvented its competitors', leading to a significant increase in sales.  这家公司新的营销策略胜过了竞争对手，导致销售额大幅增长。


近义词：outshine, outstrip, exceed, surpass


反义词：be outdone, be surpassed, be inferior to


词性:动词
