reconceptualized:/ˌriːkənˈsɛptʃuəlaɪzd/| v. |重新概念化；重新构想 |To rethink and reformulate a concept or idea. It involves a fundamental shift in understanding, going beyond a simple modification or refinement.  |The researchers reconceptualized the problem of climate change, focusing on its social and economic dimensions. 研究人员重新构想了气候变化的问题，重点关注其社会和经济层面。|reimagine, reformulate, rethink |  (无明确反义词，可使用 "misunderstand," "misinterpret" 来表达与之相反的结果)|动词
