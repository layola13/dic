orthorrhaphous:/ˌɔːrθəˈræfəs/| adj.|正缝合的；直缝合的|  Relating to or characterized by a straight suture or seam, particularly in the context of bone plates or surgical incisions.  It implies a suture line that is linear and without significant deviation or irregularity.|  The surgeon opted for an orthorrhaphous closure to minimize scarring.  外科医生选择正缝合闭合以最大限度地减少疤痕。|近义词：straight sutured, linear sutured|反义词：irregular sutured, zigzag sutured
