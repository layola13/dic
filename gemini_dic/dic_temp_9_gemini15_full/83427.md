habit:/ˈhæbɪt/ | n. |习惯，习性|A settled or regular tendency or practice, especially one that is hard to give up.|例句：He has a habit of biting his nails. 他有咬指甲的习惯。|例句：It's a bad habit to stay up late. 熬夜是个坏习惯。|近义词：custom, practice, routine|反义词：uncustomary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a good habit of reading before bed. = 他有睡前阅读的好习惯。
She maintains a healthy habit of exercising regularly. = 她保持着定期锻炼的健康习惯。

一般过去时:
I had a bad habit of procrastinating. = 我以前有拖延的坏习惯。
He broke the habit of smoking. = 他改掉了抽烟的习惯。

现在完成时:
I have developed a habit of waking up early. = 我养成了早起的习惯。
She has broken the habit of overeating. = 她改掉了暴饮暴食的习惯。

过去完成时:
I had already formed the habit of drinking coffee before I moved to Italy. = 在我搬到意大利之前，我已经养成了喝咖啡的习惯。
She had given up the habit of biting her nails by the time she turned ten. = 到她十岁的时候，她已经改掉了咬指甲的习惯。

将来时:
I will try to develop a habit of saving money. = 我将尝试养成储蓄的习惯。
She will break the habit of spending too much money. = 她将改掉花钱太多的习惯。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better habit of time management, I would be less stressed. = 如果我有更好的时间管理习惯，我会不那么有压力。
I wish I had the habit of exercising more. = 我希望我有更多运动的习惯。

被动语态:
Good habits are often formed unconsciously. = 好习惯通常是无意识地养成的。
Bad habits can be broken with conscious effort. = 通过有意识的努力，坏习惯是可以被改掉的。

疑问句:
What is your most annoying habit? = 你最讨厌的习惯是什么？
Do you have any bad habits you'd like to break? = 你有什么想改掉的坏习惯吗？

否定句:
He doesn't have a habit of drinking alcohol. = 他没有喝酒的习惯。
She doesn't have the habit of being late. = 她没有迟到的习惯。


PHRASE->
develop a habit 养成习惯
break a habit 改掉习惯
get into a habit 养成…的习惯
have a habit of 有…的习惯
out of habit 出于习惯
