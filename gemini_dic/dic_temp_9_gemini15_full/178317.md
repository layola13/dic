recondemning:/ˌriːkənˈdɛmɪŋ/| v.|再次谴责，重新谴责 |To condemn again; to condemn anew.  It implies a renewed or intensified condemnation, often after a previous attempt at resolution or appeasement has failed.|例句：The international community is recondemning the use of chemical weapons. 国际社会再次谴责使用化学武器。

例句：After the new evidence emerged, the court recondemned the defendant to a harsher sentence. 新证据出现后，法院再次判处被告更严厉的刑罚。


近义词：reproach, denounce, criticize, condemn


反义词：praise, commend, approve


词性：动词
