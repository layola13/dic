random's:/ˈrændəmz/| n.|随机的；任意的|The possessive form of "random," indicating something belonging to or associated with something random.|例句：The random's occurrences surprised everyone.  随机事件让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：chance, haphazard, arbitrary|反义词：deliberate, planned, intentional|名词


USAGE->
This word is a possessive form and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It shows possession and thus doesn't follow typical verb tense rules.  Its usage depends entirely on the context of the noun it modifies.

Example Sentences illustrating possessive usage:

1.  The random's effect was unexpected.  随机的影响是出乎意料的。
2.  We analyzed the random's data set. 我们分析了随机的数据集。
3.  The study of the random's distribution was complex. 对随机分布的研究很复杂。
4. The random's selection process was flawed. 随机的选择过程是有缺陷的。
5.  The random's nature of the experiment made the results uncertain. 实验的随机性使结果不确定。

PHRASE->
Because "random's" is a possessive form, it doesn't have common phrases in the same way as a verb or adjective.  Phrases would involve the noun it modifies. For example:

1. random's choice: 随机的选择
2. random's selection: 随机的选择
3. random's sample: 随机样本
4. random's generator: 随机数生成器
5. random's variable: 随机变量
