rebrand:/ˌriːˈbrænd/| vt.|重新塑造品牌；更换品牌|To change the image or identity of a company or product, especially in order to make it more appealing to customers.|例句：The company decided to rebrand itself to attract a younger audience. 公司决定重新塑造品牌形象以吸引更年轻的受众。|近义词：reposition, relaunch, revamp|反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rebrand their products every year. = 他们每年都重新设计他们的产品品牌。
The company regularly rebrands to stay current. = 公司定期重新塑造品牌以保持潮流。

一般过去时:
The cafe rebranded last month. = 这家咖啡馆上个月重新设计了品牌。
They rebranded their image after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，他们重新塑造了品牌形象。


现在完成时:
The company has rebranded several times since its inception. = 自成立以来，该公司已经多次更换品牌。
They have rebranded their logo to reflect a new direction. = 他们已经重新设计了他们的标志以反映新的方向。


过去完成时:
They had rebranded before the merger was announced. = 在合并宣布之前，他们已经完成了品牌重塑。
The company had rebranded its website prior to the launch. = 公司在网站上线之前已经重新设计了品牌。


将来时:
The company will rebrand next year. = 公司明年将重新塑造品牌。
We will rebrand our products to better target our consumers. = 我们将重新设计我们的产品品牌以更好地定位我们的消费者。


虚拟语气:
If they rebranded, they might attract more customers. = 如果他们重新塑造品牌，他们可能会吸引更多顾客。
It would be wise if they rebranded their outdated image. = 如果他们重新塑造过时的品牌形象，将会明智得多。


被动语态:
The product line was rebranded to appeal to a wider market. = 产品线经过重新设计品牌以吸引更广泛的市场。
The company's image is being rebranded as part of a larger strategy. = 作为更大战略的一部分，公司形象正在被重新塑造。


疑问句:
Will the company rebrand its products? = 公司会重新设计其产品品牌吗？
Has the company rebranded recently? = 公司最近是否重新塑造了品牌？


否定句:
The company did not rebrand its logo. = 公司没有重新设计其标志。
They decided against rebranding at this time. = 他们决定目前不重新塑造品牌。


PHRASE->
rebrand a company = 重新塑造公司品牌
rebrand a product = 重新塑造产品品牌
rebrand an image = 重新塑造形象
rebrand a message = 重新塑造信息
rebrand for a new market = 为新市场重新塑造品牌
