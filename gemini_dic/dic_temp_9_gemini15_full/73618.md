forewish:/fɔːrˈwɪʃ/| v.| 预祝，预先祝愿 | To express a wish or hope for something in advance.  | Example: I forewish you a happy birthday. 我预祝你生日快乐。| 近义词：wish in advance, anticipate with good wishes | 反义词：curse, jinx


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I forewish them good luck on their journey. = 我预祝他们旅途顺利。
She forewishes her friend success in the competition. = 她预祝她的朋友在比赛中取得成功。


一般过去时:
He forewished her a speedy recovery. = 他预祝她早日康复。
They forewished the team victory in the final match. = 他们预祝球队在决赛中获胜。


现在完成时:
I have forewished them all the best for their future endeavors. = 我已经预祝他们未来的事业一切顺利。
She has forewished him many happy returns of the day. = 她已经预祝他生日快乐。


过去完成时:
He had forewished her good fortune before she embarked on her project. = 在她开始项目之前，他已经预祝她好运。
They had forewished the couple a happy marriage before the ceremony. = 在仪式之前，他们已经预祝这对夫妇婚姻幸福。


将来时:
I will forewish him success in his upcoming exams. = 我将预祝他在即将到来的考试中取得成功。
She will forewish them a safe and pleasant trip. = 她将预祝他们旅途安全愉快。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had forewished them a safe journey. = 我真希望我当时预祝他们旅途安全。
If I had known, I would have forewished her all the best. = 如果我知道的话，我会预祝她一切顺利。


被动语态:
They were forewished good luck by their families. = 他们的家人预祝他们好运。
The success of the project was forewished by many people. = 许多人都预祝该项目成功。


疑问句:

否定句:
