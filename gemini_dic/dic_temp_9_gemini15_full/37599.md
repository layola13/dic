communicated:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪd/ | v. | 传达；沟通；交流 | To successfully convey information or ideas to someone.  | He communicated his concerns to the manager. 他向经理表达了他的担忧。 | conveyed, imparted, transmitted | misunderstood, misconstrued | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He communicates effectively in meetings. 他在会议上有效地沟通。
She communicates her ideas clearly. 她清晰地表达了她的想法。


一般过去时:
They communicated their decision to the team. 他们向团队传达了他们的决定。
He communicated the bad news as gently as possible. 他尽可能温柔地传达了坏消息。


现在完成时:
We have communicated with them several times. 我们已经和他们联系过几次了。
She has communicated her intentions to the board. 她已经向董事会传达了她的意图。


过去完成时:
He had communicated his concerns before the meeting. 他在会议之前已经表达了他的担忧。
They had communicated the plan to the stakeholders. 他们已经向利益相关者传达了计划。


将来时:
I will communicate the results as soon as they are available. 结果一旦出来，我就会传达。
She will communicate the new policy to her staff tomorrow. 她明天将向她的员工传达新的政策。


虚拟语气:
If he had communicated better, the misunderstanding would have been avoided. 如果他沟通更好，误解就可以避免了。
I wish I had communicated my feelings more clearly. 我希望我当时能更清晰地表达我的感受。


被动语态:
The information was communicated to all employees. 信息已传达给所有员工。
The decision was communicated through email. 决定是通过电子邮件传达的。


疑问句:
How did you communicate with him? 你是怎么和他沟通的？
Has the message been communicated effectively? 信息是否有效地传达了？


否定句:
He did not communicate his feelings adequately. 他没有充分表达他的感受。
The message was not communicated clearly. 信息没有传达清楚。


PHRASE->
communicate effectively  有效沟通
communicate clearly 清晰沟通
communicate with sb 与某人沟通
means of communication 沟通方式
open communication  公开沟通
