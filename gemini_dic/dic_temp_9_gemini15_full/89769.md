horde:/hɔːrd/| n.|部落，人群，兽群|A large group of people, animals, or things.|例句：A horde of tourists descended on the small town.  一大群游客涌进了这个小镇。|例句：A horde of locusts devoured the crops.  一大群蝗虫吞噬了庄稼。|近义词：crowd, throng, swarm, mass|反义词：few, handful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A horde of fans gathers outside the stadium. = 一大群粉丝聚集在体育场外。
A horde of locusts destroys the crops every year. = 每年一大群蝗虫都会毁坏庄稼。

一般过去时:
A horde of barbarians attacked the city. = 一大群野蛮人袭击了这座城市。
A horde of refugees fled the war-torn country. = 一大群难民逃离了饱受战争摧残的国家。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The villagers had seen a horde of wolves approaching their village. = 村民们看到了一大群狼正朝他们的村庄走来。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
