fillies:/ˈfɪliz/| n.|小母马，年轻女子|A filly is a young female horse.  The term is often used to evoke a sense of youth, energy, and grace.|例句：The filly raced past the finish line with incredible speed. 这匹小母马以惊人的速度冲过终点线。

例句：She's a spirited filly, full of life and mischief. 她是个充满活力和淘气的年轻女子。|近义词：young mare, young woman|反义词：stallion, mare (in the context of horses)
