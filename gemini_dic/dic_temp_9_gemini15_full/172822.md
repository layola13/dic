quadriplegics:/kwɑːdrɪˈplɛdʒɪks/ | n. | 四肢瘫痪者 |  People with paralysis in all four limbs.  Quadriplegics often require extensive medical care and assistance with daily living activities. | Example:  The rehabilitation center provides specialized support for quadriplegics.  康复中心为四肢瘫痪者提供专业的支持。


The support group offers a safe space for quadriplegics to share their experiences. 支持小组为四肢瘫痪者提供了一个分享经验的安全空间。|近义词：paralyzed people (with quadriplegia), tetraplegics | 反义词：able-bodied people

词源:
From quadri- (four) + plegic (paralyzed).  The word ultimately derives from the Greek word "plege," meaning "stroke" or "blow," referring to the stroke or injury causing paralysis.
