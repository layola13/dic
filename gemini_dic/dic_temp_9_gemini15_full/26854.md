cairny: /ˈkɛːrni/ | n. |多石的；多碎石的 | Characterized by the presence of many cairns (piles of stones) or loose stones.  The term often describes rough, rocky terrain. | 例句：The cairny path was difficult to traverse. = 多石的小路很难通行。 The hikers encountered a cairny hillside. = 徒步旅行者遇到一个多石的山坡。 | 近义词：stony, rocky, rough, bouldery | 反义词：smooth, level, even
