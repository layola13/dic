reexperiences:/ˌriːɪkˈspɪəriənsɪz/ | n. | 再体验，重新经历 | The act of experiencing something again; a renewed experience.  |例句：Her reexperiences of childhood trauma led her to seek therapy. 她童年创伤的再次经历促使她寻求治疗。  |近义词：reliving, revisiting, recalling | 反义词：forgetting, ignoring | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reexperiences his childhood every time he visits his hometown. = 他每次回故乡都会重新体验他的童年。
She reexperiences the joy of her wedding anniversary each year. = 她每年都会重新体验结婚纪念日的喜悦。

一般过去时:
He reexperienced the thrill of the race again when he watched the video. = 当他观看录像时，他又重新体验了比赛的刺激。
She reexperienced the pain of her breakup when she saw his picture. = 当她看到他的照片时，她又重新体验了分手的痛苦。

现在完成时:
I have reexperienced that feeling of overwhelming joy several times since then. = 从那以后，我已经好几次重新体验到了那种难以言喻的喜悦。
She has reexperienced the loss of her pet many times in her dreams. = 在梦中，她已经多次重新体验到失去宠物的悲伤。

过去完成时:
By the time the movie ended, he had reexperienced the entire emotional journey of the protagonist. = 到电影结束时，他已经重新体验了主角的整个情感历程。
Before the reunion, she had reexperienced the anxieties of her school days. = 在同学聚会之前，她已经重新体验了学生时代的焦虑。

将来时:
He will reexperience the magic of the place when he returns next year. = 他明年回来的时候将会再次体验这个地方的魅力。
She will reexperience the excitement of the concert when she listens to the recording. = 当她听录音时，她将再次体验音乐会的兴奋。

虚拟语气:
If I were to go back, I would reexperience the happiness of my youth. = 如果我能回去，我会重新体验青春的快乐。
I wish I could reexperience that moment again. = 我真希望能够再次体验那一刻。

被动语态:  (This word is rarely used in the passive voice)
The emotions of the past were reexperienced by the patient during the therapy session. = 在治疗过程中，患者重新体验了过去的感情。(较为生硬)
The trauma was reexperienced vividly through the sensory details. = 通过感官细节，创伤被生动地重新体验了。(较为生硬)

疑问句:
Will you reexperience the same feelings if you go back there? = 如果你回到那里，你会重新体验同样的感觉吗？
Have you ever reexperienced a past trauma? = 你曾经重新体验过过去的创伤吗？

否定句:
He did not reexperience the same level of fear this time. = 这一次他并没有重新体验到同样的恐惧程度。
She couldn't reexperience the joy of that day no matter how hard she tried. = 不管她多么努力，她都无法重新体验那一天的快乐。
