ma's:/mɑːz/| n.|妈妈的；妈妈们|The possessive form of "ma," a shortened form of "mama" or "mother". It indicates possession or relationship.  |Example:  That's ma's car. =那是妈妈的车。|近义词:mother's, mum's|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
This word is a possessive form and doesn't have verb conjugations like other parts of speech.  It functions solely to show possession.  Therefore, typical verb tenses are not applicable.

PHRASE->
ma's place = 妈妈家
ma's cooking = 妈妈的厨艺
ma's advice = 妈妈的建议
go to ma's = 去妈妈家
ma's birthday = 妈妈的生日
