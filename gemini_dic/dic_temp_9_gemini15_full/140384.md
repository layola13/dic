oillet:/ˈɔɪlɪt/| n.| 小孔；装饰孔；(旧式)衬裙上的装饰孔 | A small hole, especially one used for ornamentation or ventilation.  Historically, it referred to an eyelet in a garment, particularly a decorative eyelet on a woman's petticoat. | Example: The dressmaker carefully stitched the oillets around the neckline. (裁缝仔细地缝制了领口周围的小孔。)  The old book had several oillets punched in its cover. (那本旧书的封面上打了好几个小孔。) | 近义词：eyelet, hole, perforation | 反义词：solid, unbroken
