copsewood:/ˈkɒpswʊd/| n. |小树林；矮树丛|A copsewood is a small wood or thicket of trees.  It often implies a dense growth of relatively small trees. |例句：We walked through a dense copsewood, the sunlight barely filtering through the leaves. 我们穿过一片茂密的矮树林，阳光几乎无法穿透树叶。|例句：A small copsewood sheltered the hikers from the afternoon rain. 一小片矮树林为远足者遮挡了下午的雨水。|近义词：grove, thicket, woodlet|反义词：forest (in terms of size), open field|名词
