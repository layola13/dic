cater:/ˈkeɪtər/| vt.|供应饮食；迎合；满足需要|to provide food and drinks for a social event|例句：The company caters for all kinds of events, from weddings to corporate dinners.  这家公司承办各种活动，从婚礼到公司晚宴。|例句：The restaurant caters to a younger clientele. 这家餐馆迎合年轻顾客。|近义词：supply, provide, satisfy, accommodate|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company caters to large events. = 这家公司承办大型活动。
She caters to her clients' every need. = 她满足客户的每一个需求。


一般过去时:
They catered the wedding last weekend. = 他们上周末承办了婚礼。
The chef catered to the king's preferences. = 厨师迎合了国王的喜好。


现在完成时:
We have catered many events this year. = 今年我们已经承办了许多活动。
She has always catered to her family’s wishes. = 她一直都满足家人的愿望。


过去完成时:
They had catered several parties before this one. = 在这次聚会之前，他们已经承办过几次派对了。
The restaurant had catered to the elite for many years. = 这家餐馆多年来一直为精英阶层服务。


将来时:
We will cater the conference next month. = 我们下个月将承办这次会议。
She will cater to the needs of the disabled guests. = 她将满足残疾客人的需求。


虚拟语气:
If they catered to the public's demands, they'd be more successful. = 如果他们迎合公众的需求，他们会更成功。
I wish they had catered a vegetarian option. = 我希望他们提供素食选择。


被动语态:
The event was catered by a renowned company. = 这场活动由一家知名公司承办。
The needs of the guests are always catered for. = 客人的需求总是得到满足。


疑问句:
Does this restaurant cater to dietary restrictions? = 这家餐厅是否能满足饮食限制的要求？
Will they cater the party next week? = 他们下周会承办这个派对吗？


否定句:
They don't cater to large groups. = 他们不承办大型聚会。
The hotel doesn't cater to specific dietary needs. = 这家酒店不满足特定的饮食需求。


PHRASE->
cater to: 迎合；满足…的需要
cater for: 为……提供饮食
cater an event: 承办一个活动
high-end catering: 高端餐饮服务
mobile catering: 流动餐饮服务
