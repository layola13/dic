hearthrug: /ˈhɑːθrʌɡ/| n. |壁炉地毯| A rug placed in front of a fireplace.  It is typically large and made of a durable material to withstand wear and tear from people sitting or standing on it while enjoying the warmth of the fire. |The hearthrug was worn in places from years of use.  壁炉地毯有些地方因多年的使用而磨损了。|  floor rug, fireside rug |  none |noun
