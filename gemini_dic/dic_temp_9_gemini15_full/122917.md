molybdate:/ˈmɒlɪbdeɪt/| n. |钼酸盐|Molybdate is a salt or ester of molybdic acid. It contains the molybdate anion, MoO₄²⁻.  Molybdates are used in various applications, including pigments, catalysts, and corrosion inhibitors.|Example: Ammonium molybdate is a common reagent used in analytical chemistry. 钼酸铵是分析化学中常用的试剂。|近义词:molybdenum compound|反义词: N/A
