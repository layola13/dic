self-tolerant:/ˈsɛlf ˈtɑːlərənt/| adj.|自身耐受的，自身容忍的|Tolerant of one's own tissues or cells; not reacting against them.  This term is primarily used in the context of immunology and refers to a state where the immune system does not attack the body's own cells and tissues.|例句：The patient's immune system showed a degree of self-tolerance, preventing autoimmune disease.  这位病人的免疫系统表现出一定程度的自身耐受性，从而预防了自身免疫性疾病。|近义词：immunotolerant|反义词：autoimmune, self-reactive
