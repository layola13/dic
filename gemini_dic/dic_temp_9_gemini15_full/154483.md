peroxidation:/ˌpɛrɑːksɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |过氧化作用，过氧化|The chemical process of adding oxygen to a molecule, often resulting in the formation of peroxides.  This process can be damaging to cells and tissues.  |例句：Lipid peroxidation is a major contributor to cellular damage in oxidative stress. 脂质过氧化是氧化应激中细胞损伤的主要因素。
|例句：The peroxidation of unsaturated fatty acids leads to the production of malondialdehyde. 不饱和脂肪酸的过氧化作用导致丙二醛的产生。|近义词：oxidation, autoxidation |反义词：reduction |词性：名词
