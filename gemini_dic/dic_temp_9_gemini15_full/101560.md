irons:/ˈaɪərnz/ | n. |熨斗；镣铐；铁器；(常用复数)铁链；(pl.)熨衣架 |  A tool used for smoothing clothes by pressing them with a heated surface; shackles or chains used to restrain someone; things made of iron.  |例句：She used an iron to press her shirt. = 她用熨斗熨衬衫。


例句：The prisoner was bound with irons. = 囚犯被铁链锁着。
