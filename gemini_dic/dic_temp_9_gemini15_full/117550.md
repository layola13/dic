megabytes:/ˈmɛɡəbaɪts/| n. |兆字节，百万字节|A megabyte (MB) is a unit of information or computer storage equal to one million bytes.|例句：The file is too large; it's over 100 megabytes.  这个文件太大了；超过100兆字节。|例句：My computer has a hard drive with 500 gigabytes of storage, which is equivalent to 500,000 megabytes. 我的电脑硬盘有500千兆字节的存储空间，相当于500,000兆字节。|近义词：gigabyte, kilobyte|反义词：bit (though not a direct opposite, as it's a smaller unit)|词性：名词
