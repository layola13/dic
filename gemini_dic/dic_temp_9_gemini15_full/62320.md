endeavorment:/ɪnˈdɛvərmənt/ | n. |努力；尽力；尝试 | An endeavorment is a serious attempt or effort to achieve something. It emphasizes the sustained nature of the effort. |例句：Her endeavorment to learn Mandarin was admirable.  她的努力学习普通话令人钦佩。

例句：The endeavorment to climb Mount Everest was a testament to their determination. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰的努力证明了他们的决心。

近义词：effort, attempt, striving, exertion

反义词：negligence, idleness, apathy
