circumscriber:/sɜːrkəmˈskraɪbər/| n. |限定者；限制者；画圆的人| One who circumscribes; one who limits or restricts; someone who draws a circle around something.  The term often implies a limitation of power or authority. |The dictator was a circumscriber of freedom. 独裁者限制了自由。| limiter, restrictor, demarcator |expander, liberator|
