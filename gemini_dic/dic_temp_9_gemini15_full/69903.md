fertilization:/ˌfɜːrtlɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |受精；施肥；肥沃化 |The process by which a sperm unites with an egg to initiate the development of a new organism; the application of fertilizers to soil to enhance plant growth. |例句：The fertilization of the egg by the sperm marks the beginning of a new life. 精子与卵子的结合标志着新生命的开始。The farmer ensured successful crop yields through regular fertilization of the fields. 农民通过定期施肥确保了成功的作物产量。|近义词：impregnation, insemination, fecundation |反义词：sterility
