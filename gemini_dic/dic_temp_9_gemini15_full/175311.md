raise:/reɪz/ | vt. n. | 举起；提高；筹集；抚养；引起；提升 | To lift or move something to a higher position; to increase the amount, level, or value of something; to bring up children; to collect money for a cause; to cause something to happen. | 例句：He raised his hand to ask a question. 他举手提问。  They raised the price of gasoline. 他们提高了汽油价格。

近义词：lift, elevate, increase, collect, rear, breed, arouse, bring about | 反义词：lower, decrease, diminish, suppress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He raises his voice. = 他提高了他的嗓音。
They raise money for charity. = 他们为慈善事业筹款。

一般过去时:
She raised her hand. = 她举起了手。
The company raised salaries. = 公司提高了薪水。

现在完成时:
They have raised three children. = 他们已经养育了三个孩子。
We have raised enough money for the project. = 我们已经为这个项目筹集了足够的资金。

过去完成时:
He had raised the issue at the previous meeting. = 他在之前的会议上已经提出了这个问题。
They had raised their concerns before the decision was made. = 他们在做出决定之前已经表达了他们的担忧。

将来时:
We will raise the flag tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将升旗。
The government will raise taxes next year. = 政府明年将提高税收。

虚拟语气:
If I raised my concerns, they might listen. = 如果我提出我的担忧，他们可能会听。
I wish I had raised my hand sooner. = 我希望我早点举手。

被动语态:
The flag was raised at sunrise. = 旗帜在日出时升起。
The issue was raised by several members. = 这个问题由几位成员提出。

疑问句:
Did he raise his hand? = 他举手了吗？
Will they raise the rent? = 他们会涨房租吗？

否定句:
They didn't raise any objections. = 他们没有提出任何异议。
He didn't raise his voice. = 他没有提高嗓音。


PHRASE->
raise a question = 提出问题

词源:
Old English *rǣsan, of Germanic origin; related to Old High German *reizan ‘to ride, stir up’.  The sense development is from ‘to lift up’ to ‘to arouse or excite’.
