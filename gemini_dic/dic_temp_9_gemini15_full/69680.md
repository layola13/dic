fends: /fɛndz/ | v. | 击退；防御；维持生计 | To ward off (attack, danger, etc.); to defend oneself against; to provide for oneself |  例句：The warrior fended off the attacker with his sword.  战士用剑挡开了袭击者。

例句：She fends for herself and her two children. 她独自抚养她和她的两个孩子。


近义词：defend, protect, ward off, resist, support

反义词：surrender, succumb, yield, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fends off criticism with ease. 他轻松地化解了批评。
The small business fends for itself in a competitive market. 这家小企业在一个竞争激烈的市场中维持生计。


一般过去时:
The knight fended off the dragon's fiery breath.  骑士挡住了巨龙的烈焰。
They fended for themselves in the wilderness. 他们在荒野中独自生存。


现在完成时:
She has fended off numerous attacks on her policy. 她已经成功抵御了多次对其政策的攻击。
We have fended for ourselves since our parents passed away. 自从父母去世后，我们一直靠自己生活。


过去完成时:
He had fended off several challenges before finally winning the competition. 在最终赢得比赛之前，他已经克服了好几个挑战。
They had fended for themselves for months before rescue arrived.  在获救之前，他们已经独自生存了几个月。


将来时:
He will fend off any attempts to undermine his authority. 他将抵御任何企图削弱其权威的企图。
We will fend for ourselves until we find a new job. 我们会自力更生，直到找到新工作。


虚拟语气:
If he had fended off the attack earlier, the outcome might have been different. 如果他早点挡住攻击，结果可能会有所不同。
If they had fended for themselves better, they would not have been so hungry. 如果他们能更好地自力更生，就不会这么饿了。


被动语态:
The attack was fended off by the brave soldiers.  勇敢的士兵们击退了攻击。
Their needs were fended for by the charitable organization. 他们的需求由慈善组织提供。


疑问句:
Can he fend off the challenges ahead? 他能克服未来的挑战吗？
How long can they fend for themselves in this harsh environment? 在这种恶劣的环境中，他们能独自生存多久？


否定句:
He couldn't fend off the blow. 他无法挡住这一击。
They failed to fend for themselves properly. 他们没能妥善地自力更生。


PHRASE->
fend for oneself: 自力更生
fend off: 击退；避开
fend for: 抚养；照顾
fend a blow: 挡住一击
fend an attack: 抵御攻击


词源:
源自古英语 *fēndan，意为“抓住，抓住”。  其含义演变为“防御，抵御”。
