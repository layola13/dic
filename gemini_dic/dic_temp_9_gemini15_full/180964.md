reimbursed:/ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrst/| v. |报销，偿还 | To repay (a person or organization) a sum of money that has been spent.  |The company reimbursed me for my travel expenses. 公司报销了我的差旅费。|refund, repay, compensate |owe,  deficit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reimburses employees for business travel. = 公司报销员工的公务旅行费用。
They reimburse clients for damaged goods. = 他们赔偿客户损坏的货物。


一般过去时:
I was reimbursed for the cost of the repairs. = 我收到了修理费用的报销。
She reimbursed her friend for the concert tickets. = 她向朋友报销了演唱会门票的钱。


现在完成时:
We have been reimbursed for all our expenses. = 我们所有的费用都已报销。
The insurance company has reimbursed me for the stolen items. = 保险公司已经赔偿了我被盗物品的损失。


过去完成时:
He had been reimbursed before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经收到了报销。
They had already been reimbursed for the project costs. = 他们已经报销了项目的费用。


将来时:
They will reimburse you once you submit the receipt. = 你提交收据后，他们会报销给你。
The government will reimburse farmers for crop losses. = 政府将赔偿农民的作物损失。


虚拟语气:
If they reimbursed me promptly, I would be happy. = 如果他们及时报销，我会很高兴。
I wish they had reimbursed me sooner. = 我希望他们早点报销给我。


被动语态:
His expenses were reimbursed by the company. = 他的费用由公司报销。
The damages will be reimbursed by the responsible party. = 损失将由责任方赔偿。


疑问句:
Will you be reimbursed for your travel expenses? = 你的差旅费会报销吗？
Were you reimbursed for the damaged goods? = 你损坏的货物得到赔偿了吗？


否定句:
They did not reimburse me for the parking fees. = 他们没有报销我的停车费。
The insurance company did not reimburse her for the full amount. = 保险公司没有全额赔偿她。


PHRASE->
be reimbursed for = 因…而获得报销
reimburse someone for something = 赔偿某人某物
reimbursement policy = 报销政策
request reimbursement = 申请报销
reimbursement claim = 报销申请
