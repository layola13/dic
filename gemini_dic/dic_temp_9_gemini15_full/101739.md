irresistibly:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbli/| adj. | 不可抗拒地，无法抵挡地 |  In an irresistible manner; so tempting or attractive as to be impossible to resist.  | The aroma of freshly baked bread was irresistibly appealing.  新鲜烘焙的面包的香味令人无法抗拒。| irresistibly charming, overwhelmingly, compellingly | resistible, avoidable |
