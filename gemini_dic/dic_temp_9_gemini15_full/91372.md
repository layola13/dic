hydrostatics:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˈstætɪks/| n. |流体静力学|The branch of physics that deals with the equilibrium of liquids and gases and the pressure exerted by liquids and gases at rest.| Example:  Hydrostatics is crucial for understanding the behavior of submarines. 流体静力学对于理解潜艇的行为至关重要。|近义词:fluid statics|反义词:hydrodynamics
