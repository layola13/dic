repumping: /rɪˈpʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 再泵送；重新抽送 | The act of pumping something again, typically a fluid, after it has already been pumped once.  It often implies a process of recirculation or re-use. | 例句：The repumping of the contaminated water was necessary to ensure its proper treatment.  再抽送受污染的水是确保其得到适当处理的必要措施。

The repumping system ensures continuous circulation of coolant in the reactor. 再泵送系统确保反应堆中冷却剂的持续循环。


近义词：re-circulation, recirculation, re-pumping


反义词：discharge, draining


词性:名词

词源:  The word "repumping" is a combination of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and the verb "pumping" (the act of using a pump).  Therefore, it literally means "pumping again."
