revenge:/rɪˈvɛndʒ/ | n. vt.| 报仇，复仇 |  The act of harming someone in return for an injury or wrong that they have inflicted on you.  |例句：He swore revenge on his enemies. 他发誓要向敌人报仇。

例句：She sought revenge for the death of her father. 她为父亲的死报仇。


近义词：retribution, vengeance, retaliation | 反义词：forgiveness, pardon | 词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seeks revenge on his rivals. = 他向他的对手报仇。
They take revenge for the injustice. = 他们为不公正报仇。

一般过去时:
She revenged herself on her betrayer. = 她向背叛她的人报了仇。
He revenged the death of his friend. = 他为朋友的死报了仇。

现在完成时:
They have revenged the insults they suffered. = 他们已经报复了他们遭受的侮辱。
She has taken revenge on those who wronged her. = 她已经报复了那些冤枉她的人。

过去完成时:
He had revenged himself before they could react. = 在他们反应过来之前，他已经报仇了。
She had sought revenge for years before finally succeeding. = 在最终成功之前，她已经寻求报复多年了。

将来时:
I will revenge this insult. = 我将报复这种侮辱。
They will surely revenge this injustice. = 他们一定会报复这种不公正。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would revenge myself on him. = 如果我是你，我会向他报仇。
I wish I could revenge my family’s honor. = 我希望我能报复我家族的荣誉。

被动语态:
Revenge will be taken on those responsible. = 将会对责任人进行报复。
The insult was revenged swiftly and decisively. = 这种侮辱得到了迅速而果断的报复。

疑问句:
Will you seek revenge? = 你会报仇吗？
How will you revenge yourself? = 你将如何报仇？

否定句:
He didn't seek revenge. = 他没有报仇。
She will not revenge herself on him. = 她不会向他报仇。
