demephitize: /ˌdiːˈmɛfɪtaɪz/ | vt. | 除去硫磺味；去除硫磺；净化 | To remove the smell or presence of sulfur or sulfur compounds.  The process often involves chemical treatment to neutralize or eliminate the sulfurous odor or substance. | 例句：The winemaker used a special process to demephitize the wine, removing its unpleasant sulfurous notes.  酿酒师用一种特殊的方法来去除葡萄酒中的硫磺味，去除其令人不快的硫磺味。

The factory implemented new measures to demephitize the air, reducing the sulfur dioxide emissions. 工厂实施了新的措施来净化空气，减少二氧化硫的排放。 | 近义词：desulfurize, purify, deodorize | 反义词：sulfurize, contaminate
