declaw:/dɪˈklɔː/| vt.| 去爪 | To surgically remove the claws of an animal, especially a cat.  This procedure is often performed to prevent scratching.  The removal of claws can significantly alter a cat's natural behavior and ability to defend itself. | The vet declawed my cat to prevent it from scratching the furniture.  兽医为我的猫做了去爪手术，以防止它抓坏家具。
The cat was declawed as a kitten. 这只猫还是小猫的时候就被去爪了。|近义词：remove claws, onychectomy |反义词：leave claws intact
