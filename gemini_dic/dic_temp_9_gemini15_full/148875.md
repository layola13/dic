overweigh:/ˌoʊvərˈweɪ/| vt. adj.|重量超过；使…过重；过于重视|to weigh more than; to exceed in weight or importance|例句：The suitcase overweighs the limit. 这个行李箱超重了。  The evidence overweighs the defendant's claims. 证据比被告的主张更有分量。|近义词：outweigh, exceed|反义词：underweigh


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The package overweighs the limit. = 这个包裹超重了。
This argument overweighs all others. = 这一论点比其他所有论点都更有分量。

一般过去时:
The box overweighed the capacity of the truck. = 箱子超过了卡车的承载能力。
He overweighed the importance of the details. = 他过分重视细节。

现在完成时:
The evidence has overweighed the initial suspicions. = 证据已经压倒了最初的怀疑。
They have overweighed the risks involved.  = 他们已经过分重视了所涉及的风险。

过去完成时:
The initial findings had overweighed the subsequent research. = 最初的发现已经压倒了随后的研究。
She had overweighed the negative aspects of the situation. = 她已经过分强调了情况的负面因素。


将来时:
The shipment will overweigh the allowed weight. = 这批货物将超过允许的重量。
I think this factor will overweigh all others in the decision. = 我认为这个因素将在决策中压倒所有其他因素。

虚拟语气:
If the package overweighed the limit, we would have to pay extra. = 如果包裹超重，我们就得额外付费。
If he had overweighed the risks, he would not have invested. = 如果他当时过分重视了风险，他就不会投资了。

被动语态:
The importance of the issue was overweighed by the media. = 这个问题的重要性被媒体过分夸大了。
The evidence was overweighed in favour of the prosecution. = 证据对检方有利。

疑问句:
Does the package overweigh the limit? = 这个包裹超重了吗？
Does this factor overweigh the others in your opinion? = 你认为这个因素是否比其他因素更重要？

否定句:
The benefits do not overweigh the costs. = 收益不超过成本。
The positive aspects do not overweigh the negative ones. = 正面因素并不能压倒负面因素。
