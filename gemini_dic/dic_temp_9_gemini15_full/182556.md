reobjected:/ˌriːəbˈdʒɛktɪd/| v.|再反对，再次拒绝|To object again; to raise an objection a second time.  It implies a previous objection was made and is now being reiterated or a new objection is being added to a previous one.|例句：The judge reobjected to the admission of the evidence, citing new concerns about its authenticity. = 法官再次反对接受证据，理由是其真实性存在新的疑虑。 The defendant reobjected to the line of questioning, arguing it was irrelevant. = 被告再次反对这一系列的提问，认为它与案件无关。|近义词：rebut, protest, oppose|反义词：accept, approve, concede|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reobjects to the proposal every time it's mentioned. = 他每次提到这个提议就再次反对。
They reobject to the new policy on a regular basis. = 他们定期地再次反对这项新政策。

一般过去时:
The lawyer reobjected to the witness's testimony. = 律师再次反对证人的证词。
She reobjected to the terms of the contract after reviewing them. = 她审查合同条款后再次反对。


现在完成时:
He has reobjected to the plan on several occasions. = 他已经多次再次反对这项计划。
They have reobjected to the proposed changes, citing safety concerns. = 他们再次反对拟议的更改，理由是安全问题。


过去完成时:
The committee had reobjected to the budget before the final vote. = 在最终投票之前，委员会已经再次反对该预算。
Before the judge ruled, the defense had reobjected to the prosecution's evidence. = 在法官做出裁决之前，辩方已经再次反对控方的证据。


将来时:
He will reobject to the decision at the next meeting. = 他将在下次会议上再次反对该决定。
They will reobject if the amendment is not withdrawn. = 如果不撤回修正案，他们将会再次反对。


虚拟语气:
If he were to hear the proposal again, he would reobject. = 如果他再次听到这个提议，他会再次反对。
I wish they hadn't reobjected to the plan; it would have been approved. = 我真希望他们没有再次反对这项计划；它本来会被批准的。


被动语态:
The motion was reobjected to by several members. = 该动议遭到几名成员的再次反对。
His argument was reobjected to by the opposing counsel. = 他的论点遭到对方律师的再次反对。


疑问句:
Will they reobject to the changes? = 他们会再次反对这些改变吗？
Did the defendant reobject to the judge's ruling? = 被告对法官的裁决再次提出异议了吗？


否定句:
He did not reobject to the suggestion. = 他没有再次反对这个建议。
They did not reobject to the proposed solution. = 他们没有再次反对拟议的解决方案。


PHRASE->
reobject to a decision = 再次反对一个决定
reobject to a proposal = 再次反对一个提议
reobject on grounds of… = 以…为由再次反对
reobject vehemently = 强烈地再次反对
