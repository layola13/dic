decongestants:/ˌdiːkənˈdʒɛstənts/| n. |减充血剂|Substances that reduce congestion, typically in the nasal passages.  They work by constricting blood vessels, reducing swelling and mucus production.  Decongestants are available as nasal sprays, oral medications, and in some combination medications for colds and allergies.|例句：Many over-the-counter cold remedies contain decongestants to relieve stuffy noses.  许多非处方感冒药含有减充血剂以缓解鼻塞。

Many people use decongestants to alleviate the symptoms of a common cold. 许多人使用减充血剂来缓解普通感冒的症状。|近义词：nasal decongestants, antihistamines (for allergy-related congestion)|反义词：congestants (substances that cause congestion)
