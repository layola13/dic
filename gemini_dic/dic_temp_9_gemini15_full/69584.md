felstone:/ˈfɛlstəʊn/| n. |霏石，霏砂岩|A felstone is a type of fine-grained, usually light-coloured igneous rock, often porphyritic, that is rich in feldspar.  It is a variety of rhyolite, and is sometimes used as a building stone.|The old church was built from local felstone.|近义词：rhyolite, porphyry|反义词：无
