graminaceous:/ˌɡræmɪˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|禾本科的；草本的|Of or relating to the grass family (Gramineae or Poaceae).  The term refers to plants possessing characteristics typical of grasses, such as jointed stems, long narrow leaves, and flowers arranged in spikelets.|The field was covered in graminaceous plants.|近义词：grassy, herbaceous|反义词：woody, arboreal
