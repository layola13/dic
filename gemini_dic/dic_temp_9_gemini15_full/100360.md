/ˌɪntərˈstɪmjʊlaɪ/| n. |刺激间期，刺激间隔|The time interval between two stimuli.  This term is primarily used in psychology and neuroscience to describe the period between the presentation of two separate stimuli.  The length of the interstimulus interval can significantly affect the response of the subject. |Example: The experiment measured reaction times as a function of the interstimulus interval.  实验测量了反应时间与刺激间期的关系。|近义词：stimulus interval, gap between stimuli|反义词：simultaneous stimuli|名词
