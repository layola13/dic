nonspontaneously:/ˌnɒnspɒnˈteɪniəsli/| adv.|非自发地，非自动地|Occurring without spontaneity or natural inclination; not occurring naturally or automatically.  It emphasizes a lack of natural or automatic occurrence, often implying external intervention or force.|例句：The reaction proceeded nonspontaneously, requiring the addition of a catalyst.  反应非自发进行，需要添加催化剂。

例句：The crystals formed nonspontaneously under high pressure. 晶体在高压下非自发形成。


近义词：artificially, deliberately, intentionally, mechanically

反义词：spontaneously, naturally, automatically


词性:副词
