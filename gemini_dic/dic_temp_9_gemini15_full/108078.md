laterite:/ˈlætəraɪt/ | n. |红土，砖红壤 | A red, brown, or yellowish-brown soil, rich in iron and aluminum oxides, formed by the weathering of rocks in hot, wet climates.  Laterite is often hard and impermeable when dry, but becomes soft and plastic when wet.  It is used in construction as a building material. | The building was constructed from laterite blocks. 这座建筑是用红土砖块建造的。 The road was made of compacted laterite. 这条路是用压实的红土建成的。 | clay, soil |  (no direct antonym, but contrasting terms might be fertile soil, well-drained soil) | 名词
