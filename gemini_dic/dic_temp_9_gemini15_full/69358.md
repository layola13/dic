federating: /ˈfɛdəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 使联合；使结盟；联合起来 |  The act of uniting or combining into a federation or league; the process of forming a federation.  | 例句：The states are federating to form a new nation.  各州正在联合起来组建一个新的国家。

The process of joining together to form a federation is ongoing.  联合起来组成联邦的过程正在进行中。

近义词：uniting, consolidating, unifying, amalgamating | 反义词：separating, dividing, disuniting | 词性：动词
