larceny:/ˈlɑːrsəni/| n.|盗窃罪；偷窃|The unlawful taking, carrying away, and secreting of the personal property of another with the intent to permanently deprive the owner of its possession.  Larceny is a crime that involves the theft of personal property. |例句：He was arrested for grand larceny after stealing a car. 他因偷车而被逮捕，罪名是重窃罪。

例句：The small larceny of a candy bar led to a much larger problem.  偷一块糖果的小偷小摸导致了更大的问题。


近义词：theft, stealing, robbery, pilfering|反义词：giving, donating


词源:  Middle English: from Old French larcin, from Latin latrocinium ‘robbery’, from latro ‘robber’.
