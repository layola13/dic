perchlorate:/ˈpɜːrklɔːreɪt/| n.|高氯酸盐|A salt or ester of perchloric acid.  Perchlorates are strong oxidizing agents and can be dangerous. They are also found naturally in some environments and are used in various applications, such as rocket propellant and fireworks.|例句：Ammonium perchlorate is a common component of solid rocket propellant. 硝酸铵是固体火箭推进剂的常见成分。|例句：Exposure to high levels of perchlorate can be harmful to human health.  接触高浓度的高氯酸盐对人体健康有害。|近义词：oxidizer|反义词：无
