mislearnt: /ˌmɪsˈlɜːnt/ | v. | 学错，误学 | To learn incorrectly or imperfectly.  | He mislearnt the rules of grammar. 他把语法规则学错了。 | learned correctly, understood correctly |  misunderstood, misconstrued | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I mislearn new words frequently. = 我经常把新单词学错。
He mislearns the pronunciation regularly. = 他经常把发音读错。

I mislearnt the capital of Australia. = 我把澳大利亚的首都不记得了。(误记)
She mislearnt the song lyrics. = 她把歌的歌词记错了。


现在完成时:
I have mislearnt many things in my life. = 我一生中有很多事情都学错了。
He has mislearnt the formula several times. = 他已经几次把公式弄错了。


过去完成时:
I had mislearnt the directions, so I got lost. = 我把路线搞错了，所以迷路了。
She had mislearnt the recipe, and the cake was a disaster. = 她把菜谱搞错了，蛋糕做得很糟糕。
