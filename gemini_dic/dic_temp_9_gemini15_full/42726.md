counterweights:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪts/| n. |平衡重，配重|A counterweight is a weight used to balance another weight, often used in machinery or structures to compensate for an imbalance or to provide stability.|例句：The crane uses counterweights to lift heavy objects. 起重机使用平衡重来提升重物。 The seesaw needs counterweights to balance properly. 跷跷板需要配重才能保持平衡。|近义词：balance, weight, equilibrium|反义词：imbalance, unbalance
