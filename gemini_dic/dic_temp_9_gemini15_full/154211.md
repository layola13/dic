peripheral:/pəˈrɪfərəl/ | adj., n. | 周边的；外围的；次要的；外围设备 |  Relating to or situated on the edge or periphery of something.  As a noun, it refers to an external device attached to a computer. |  The peripheral vision allows you to see objects at the edge of your visual field.  周边视野使你能够看到视野边缘的物体。

The new computer system includes several peripheral devices, such as a printer and a scanner. 新的电脑系统包括几个外围设备，例如打印机和扫描仪。|  marginal, secondary, outlying | central, main, primary | 形容词，名词

词源:
