caponiere:/ˌkæpəˈnjɛər/| n. |卡波尼尔（防御工事）|A small, fortified structure, typically underground or partially buried, used for defense.  It often contains loopholes for firing weapons.  Caponiers are particularly associated with star forts and other types of fortifications. |The caponier allowed the defenders to fire on attackers approaching from any direction. 卡波尼尔允许防御者向从任何方向接近的攻击者射击。|  fort, redoubt | (none readily available) |名词
