self-corrections:/ˌsɛlf kəˈrɛkʃənz/| n.|自我修正，自我纠正|The act of correcting one's own mistakes or errors.  It can refer to various processes, from minor grammatical adjustments in writing to significant changes in behavior or thinking. |例句：The software performs self-corrections automatically.  软件会自动进行自我修正。

例句：Her self-corrections during the presentation showed her commitment to accuracy.  她在演讲中进行的自我修正显示了她对准确性的承诺。|近义词：amendments, revisions, rectifications|反义词：perseverance of error,  uncorrected mistakes|名词
