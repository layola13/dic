USAGE->
一般现在时:
The likelihoods of rain are high today. = 今天下雨的可能性很高。
What are the likelihoods of them arriving on time? = 他们准时到达的可能性有多大？


一般过去时:
The likelihoods of success were low last year. = 去年成功的可能性很低。
What were the likelihoods of finding a job in that market? = 在那个市场上找到工作的可能性有多大？


现在完成时:
The likelihoods of a deal have increased recently. = 最近达成协议的可能性增加了。
What likelihoods have been assessed for project completion? = 对项目完成的可能性进行了哪些评估？


过去完成时:

将来时:
The likelihoods of a recession will increase if inflation continues. = 如果通货膨胀持续下去，经济衰退的可能性将会增加。
What will the likelihoods of a successful launch be? = 成功发射的可能性有多大？


虚拟语气:
If the likelihoods were higher, we would invest more. = 如果可能性更高，我们会投入更多。
I wish the likelihoods of a cure were greater. = 我希望治愈的可能性更大。


被动语态:
The likelihoods of success were underestimated. = 成功的可能性被低估了。

疑问句:

否定句:
