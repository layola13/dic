giraffid: /dʒɪˈræfɪd/ | adj. n. |长颈鹿科的；长颈鹿科动物| Relating to or characteristic of the Giraffidae, a family of even-toed ungulates that includes giraffes and okapis.  The term can refer to the family itself or to any member of the family. |例句：Giraffids are known for their long necks and legs. 长颈鹿科动物以其长脖子和长腿而闻名。 The okapi is a member of the giraffid family. 奥卡皮是长颈鹿科的成员。|近义词：giraffe-like (adj.)|反义词：无
