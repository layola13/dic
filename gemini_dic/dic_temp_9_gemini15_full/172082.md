purplest:/ˈpɜːrpəlist/| adj.|最紫的，极紫的|Most purple; to the highest degree purple.  The word is the superlative form of "purple," indicating the greatest or most intense degree of purple.  It can be used to describe color, but also figuratively to describe something intensely emotional or dramatic.|例句：The purplest sunset I've ever seen painted the sky. 我见过的最紫的日落给天空涂上了颜色。


The flowers in her garden were the purplest imaginable. 她花园里的花是想象中能想到的最紫的。|近义词：deepest purple, most violet|反义词：palest, colorless
