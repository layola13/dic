devastavit:/ˌdɛvəˈsteɪvɪt/| v.|破坏；挥霍；浪费|To devastate; to lay waste; to ruin; to squander.|例句：The flood devastated the town.  洪水摧毁了这个城镇。
例句：He devastated his inheritance through gambling. 他因赌博而挥霍了他的遗产。|近义词：destroy, ruin, squander, waste|反义词：build, construct, preserve, conserve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devastates his health by smoking. = 他吸烟毁了自己的健康。
They devastate the environment with their actions. = 他们的行为破坏了环境。

一般过去时:
The earthquake devastated the city. = 地震摧毁了这座城市。
The fire devastated the forest. = 大火烧毁了森林。

现在完成时:
The storm has devastated the coastal areas. = 暴风雨已经摧毁了沿海地区。
He has devastated his savings through reckless spending. = 他因挥霍无度而花光了积蓄。

过去完成时:
The company had devastated its reputation before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公司就已经毁掉了自己的声誉。
They had devastated the landscape with their mining operations. = 他们的采矿作业已经破坏了这片土地。

将来时:
The hurricane will devastate the island. = 飓风将摧毁这个岛屿。
If he continues like this, he will devastate his family's finances. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会毁掉他家人的经济状况。

虚拟语气:
If he had listened to advice, he wouldn't have devastated his career. = 如果他听了劝告，就不会毁掉他的事业了。
I wish I hadn't devastated my trust fund. = 我真希望我没有挥霍我的信托基金。

被动语态:
The village was devastated by the typhoon. = 村庄被台风摧毁了。
The economy has been devastated by the crisis. = 经济已经被危机摧毁了。

疑问句:
Has the drought devastated the crops? = 干旱是否摧毁了庄稼？
Will the war devastate the country? = 战争会摧毁这个国家吗？

否定句:
The government did not devastate the environment. = 政府没有破坏环境。
He didn't devastate his inheritance. = 他没有挥霍他的遗产。


PHRASE->
devastate the land = 破坏土地
devastate the economy = 摧毁经济
devastate the environment = 破坏环境
devastate someone's life = 毁掉某人的生活
devastate a city = 摧毁一座城市


词源:
From Old French *devasté, past participle of dévaster "to lay waste," from Latin vastāre "to lay waste," from vastus "waste, desolate."  The "de-" prefix intensifies the meaning.
