colly: /ˈkɒli/ | adj. | 煤黑的；黑色的；（旧）带有煤灰的 |  Describing something as dark or black, often with connotations of soot or coal dust. The word is now largely archaic or dialectal. |例句：Her colly eyes shone in the dim light. = 她乌黑的眼睛在昏暗的灯光下闪闪发光。

The term is also used, less commonly, as a noun referring to coal dust or smut.  |例句：The miner's face was covered in colly. = 矿工的脸上沾满了煤灰。|近义词：black, dark, sooty |反义词：white, light, bright

The coal dust is colly. = 煤尘是黑色的。
Her eyes are colly. = 她的眼睛是黑色的。

The blacksmith's hands were colly. = 铁匠的手是黑色的。

否定句:
