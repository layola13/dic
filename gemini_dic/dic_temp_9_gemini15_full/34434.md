claustrophobically:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbɪkli/| adj. |幽闭恐怖地，令人感到幽闭恐惧地 |Feeling or causing a feeling of claustrophobia (extreme or irrational fear of confined places).  The adjective describes a state or situation that evokes claustrophobia. |例句：The small, dark room felt claustrophobically oppressive.  那个狭小黑暗的房间感觉压抑得令人窒息。

The narrow corridor felt claustrophobically confined.  狭窄的走廊感觉令人窒息地狭窄。


近义词：suffocatingly, oppressively, stiflingly |反义词：openly, freely, spaciousl
