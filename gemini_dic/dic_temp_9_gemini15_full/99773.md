intermediation:/ˌɪntərˌmiːdiˈeɪʃən/| n. |中介；调解；斡旋 |The act of mediating between parties; intervention.  It involves a third party facilitating communication and negotiation between two or more disputing parties to help them reach a mutually agreeable solution.  |例句：The UN played a crucial role in the intermediation of the peace talks.联合国在和平谈判的调解中发挥了至关重要的作用。

例句：Successful intermediation requires trust and impartiality from the mediator.成功的调解需要调解员的信任和公正。


近义词：mediation, arbitration, reconciliation |反义词：direct negotiation, unilateral action |词性：名词
