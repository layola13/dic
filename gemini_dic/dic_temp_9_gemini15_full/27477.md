campaniles:/ˌkæmpəˈniːlz/| n.|钟楼，高塔|A campanile is a bell tower, especially one freestanding and often associated with an Italian church.|例句：The campanile of the church dominates the skyline. 教堂的钟楼耸立在城市天际线上。  The old campanile leaned precariously.  古老的钟楼摇摇欲坠。|近义词：bell tower, steeple|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with low buildings or structures without bells.
