holiest:/ˈhoʊliəst/ | adj. | 最神圣的，最圣洁的 | Most sacred or holy.  The holiest day of the year is Christmas for Christians.  |近义词：most sacred, most consecrated |反义词：most profane, most unholy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temple is the holiest place in the city. = 这座庙宇是城里最神圣的地方。
This land is considered the holiest ground. = 这块土地被认为是最神圣的土地。


一般过去时:
The holiest relic was kept in a secure vault. = 最神圣的圣物被保存在一个安全的地下室里。
Yesterday was the holiest day of the year. = 昨天是一年中最神圣的日子。


现在完成时:
They have visited the holiest sites in Jerusalem. = 他们已经参观了耶路撒冷最神圣的遗址。
We have always considered this mountain the holiest peak. = 我们一直认为这座山是最神圣的山峰。


过去完成时:
Before the ceremony, they had prepared the holiest garments. = 在仪式之前，他们已经准备好了最神圣的衣物。
The priest had already blessed the holiest objects. = 祭司已经祝福了最神圣的物品。


将来时:
We will visit the holiest shrine next year. = 我们明年将参观最神圣的神社。
They will celebrate the holiest festival in the spring. = 他们将在春天庆祝最神圣的节日。


虚拟语气:
If I were to visit a holy site, I would choose the holiest one. = 如果我去参观一个圣地，我会选择最神圣的一个。
I wish I could visit the holiest city in the world. = 我希望我能参观世界上最神圣的城市。


被动语态:
The holiest scriptures were carefully preserved. = 最神圣的经文被小心地保存着。
The holiest relics are protected by armed guards. = 最神圣的圣物受到武装警卫的保护。


疑问句:

否定句:
That is not the holiest place in the region. = 那不是该地区最神圣的地方。

词源:
From Old English *hāliȝost, superlative of hālig “holy.”  The suffix -est indicates the superlative degree.
