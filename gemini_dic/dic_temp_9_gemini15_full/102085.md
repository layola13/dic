isopodous:/ˌaɪsəˈpɒdəs/| adj.| 等足类的；具等足的 | Having legs of approximately equal length.  The term typically refers to crustaceans in the order Isopoda, which includes woodlice and pillbugs.  | Example:  Isopodous crustaceans are characterized by their seven pairs of similar legs. 等足类甲壳动物的特点是它们有七对相似的腿。|近义词：uniform-legged (informal)|反义词：anisodactylous (having unequal toes), heterodactylous (having digits of different shapes)
