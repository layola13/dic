parodist:/ˈpærədɪst/| n.| 模仿者；戏仿者 | A parodist is someone who creates parodies, which are humorous imitations of the style and content of another work.  They often use satire to comment on or critique the original work. | Example: The comedian is a brilliant parodist, known for his spot-on imitations of famous politicians.  这位喜剧演员是一位才华横溢的模仿者，以其对著名政治家的精准模仿而闻名。|近义词：imitator, satirist, mimic | 反义词：original creator
