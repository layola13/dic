confute:/kənˈfjuːt/| vt.|驳斥，反驳|To prove (a person, statement, etc.) to be wrong; refute.  |例句：He confuted his opponent's arguments with irrefutable evidence. 他用无可辩驳的证据驳斥了对手的论点。|例句：The scientist's data confuted the prevailing theory. 科学家的数据驳斥了流行的理论。|近义词：refute, disprove, rebut, controvert |反义词：confirm, corroborate, support, verify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confutes his opponent's arguments every debate. = 他在每次辩论中都驳斥对手的论点。
The evidence confutes the defendant's claim of innocence. = 证据驳斥了被告无罪的主张。


一般过去时:
She confuted the rumors with facts. = 她用事实驳斥了谣言。
The professor confuted the student's flawed logic. = 教授驳斥了学生有缺陷的逻辑。


现在完成时:
Scientists have confuted many previously held beliefs. = 科学家们已经驳斥了许多先前被人们普遍接受的观点。
The new research has confuted the old theory. = 新的研究驳斥了旧理论。


过去完成时:
They had confuted the opposing team's strategy before the final game. = 在决赛之前，他们已经驳斥了对方球队的策略。
The author had confuted several criticisms of his work before publishing the revised edition. = 作者在出版修订版之前已经驳斥了他作品的几项批评。


将来时:
The lawyer will confute the witness's testimony. = 律师将驳斥证人的证词。
She will confute the opposing arguments in her presentation. = 她将在她的演讲中驳斥反对的论点。


虚拟语气:
If he had more evidence, he could confute the claims. = 如果他有更多证据，他就能驳斥这些说法。
I wish I could confute the misinformation spreading online. = 我希望我能驳斥网上流传的错误信息。


被动语态:
His arguments were confuted by the opposing team. = 他的论点被对方团队驳斥了。
The theory was confuted by recent findings. = 该理论被最近的研究结果驳斥了。


疑问句:
Can you confute his statement? = 你能驳斥他的说法吗？
How can we confute this false claim? = 我们如何才能驳斥这个错误的说法？


否定句:
He could not confute the evidence against him. = 他无法驳斥针对他的证据。
They failed to confute the prosecution's case. = 他们未能驳斥检方的指控。
