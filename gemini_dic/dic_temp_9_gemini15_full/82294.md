grouper:/ˈɡruːpər/| n.|石斑鱼|A grouper is any of numerous species of marine fish belonging to the family Serranidae. They are popular game fish and are often caught for food.  |例句：The fisherman caught a large grouper. 渔夫钓到了一条大石斑鱼。|例句：Many different species of grouper are found in coral reefs. 许多不同种类的石斑鱼在珊瑚礁中被发现。|近义词：sea bass, snapper|反义词:无
