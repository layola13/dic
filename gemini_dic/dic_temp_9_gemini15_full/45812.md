custom-made:/ˈkʌstəmˌmeɪd/ | adj. | 定制的，量身定做的 | Made to the specifications of a particular customer.  It implies a higher level of personalization and often a higher price than mass-produced items. | 例句：I ordered a custom-made suit for my wedding. = 我为婚礼订做了一套西装。

例句：She wore a custom-made gown to the gala. = 她穿着一件定制礼服参加了盛典。


近义词：tailor-made, bespoke, made-to-order, personalized

反义词：mass-produced, ready-made, off-the-rack


词源：来自“custom” (习惯，风俗；客户的订单) + “made” (制作的)。  “Custom” 在这里指的是客户的特殊要求或订单。
