patroller:/pəˈtroʊlər/ | n. |巡逻者，巡逻兵 |A person or thing that patrols.  A patroller is someone or something that regularly travels over an area to watch for trouble or to protect it. |例句：The park patroller spotted a group of teenagers vandalizing the benches. 公园巡逻员发现一群青少年正在破坏长凳。The border patroller stopped the car for a routine check. 边境巡逻员拦下汽车进行例行检查。|近义词：guard, watchman, police officer |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who is sedentary or inactive.
