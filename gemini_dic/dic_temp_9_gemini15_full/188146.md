roussette:/ruːˈsɛt/| n.|狐蝠；果蝠|A roussette is a type of megabat, specifically a fruit bat belonging to the family Pteropodidae.  They are known for their large size relative to other bats, their fox-like faces, and their frugivorous diet. Many species are found in tropical and subtropical regions of the world. |Example: The researchers observed a colony of roussettes feeding on mangoes.  研究人员观察到一群狐蝠在吃芒果。|近义词：fruit bat, flying fox|反义词：N/A
