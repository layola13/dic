nulling:/ˈnʌlɪŋ/| v.|使无效；取消；抵消|The process of making something ineffective or invalid.  It often involves actively suppressing or canceling out a signal or effect.|例句：The experiment involved nulling the gravitational effect.  实验包括消除重力效应。
例句：The treaty effectively nulled the previous agreements. 这项条约实际上使之前的协议失效。|近义词：canceling, annulling, invalidating, negating|反义词：validating, confirming, establishing
