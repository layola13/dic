calistheneum: /ˌkælɪsˈθiːniəm/ | n. |健身房；体育馆；体操馆 | A calistheneum is a place equipped for callisthenic exercises.  It's essentially a gymnasium designed specifically for bodyweight training.  The term is less common now than "gymnasium". | Example:  The ancient Greeks valued physical fitness, and their calistheneums were centers of athletic training.  古代希腊人重视身体健康，他们的体操馆是体育训练的中心。 | 近义词：gymnasium, fitness center | 反义词：None specifically.
