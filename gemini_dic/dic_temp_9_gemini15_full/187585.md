romantics:/rəʊˈmæntɪks/| n.|浪漫主义者；浪漫的人|People who are romantic, or who are interested in romanticism.  Romantics are often characterized by their idealism, emotional intensity, and appreciation for beauty and nature. |例句：Many young romantics dream of finding their soulmate. 许多年轻的浪漫主义者都梦想找到他们的灵魂伴侣。|例句：The novel explores the lives of several passionate romantics. 这部小说探讨了几个充满激情的浪漫主义者的生活。|近义词：dreamers, idealists, enthusiasts|反义词：realists, pragmatists
