myologist:/maɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |肌学家，肌肉学家 | A myologist is a specialist in myology, the scientific study of muscles.  They study the structure, function, and diseases of muscles. | The myologist presented her research findings on the effects of exercise on muscle growth. 肌学家展示了她关于运动对肌肉生长影响的研究结果。 |  anatomist, physiologist |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
