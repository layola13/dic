mis-acting:/ˌmɪsˈæktɪŋ/| adj. |拙劣的表演；行为不端|Poor or improper acting; behaving badly.  |例句：The mis-acting in the play was painful to watch.  这部戏中的拙劣表演让人难以忍受。
The child's mis-acting led to a time-out.  孩子的行为不端导致了禁闭。|近义词：malperformance, misconduct, bad acting|反义词：excellent acting, good behavior
