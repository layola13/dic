paralyzing: /ˈpærəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. |令人麻痹的；使瘫痪的；令人震惊的 | causing or able to cause paralysis; overwhelming and stunning.  |  The paralyzing fear gripped her. 令人麻痹的恐惧紧紧抓住她。The news was absolutely paralyzing.  这个消息真是令人震惊。|  crippling, debilitating, stunning, shocking | invigorating, stimulating, energizing | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news paralyzes the city.  新闻使这座城市瘫痪。
This fear paralyzes me.  这种恐惧使我瘫痪。


一般过去时:
The accident paralyzed him from the waist down. 事故导致他从腰部以下瘫痪。
The shock paralyzed her for a moment.  震惊使她瘫痪了一瞬间。


现在完成时:
The storm has paralyzed transportation in the region. 暴风雨已使该地区的交通瘫痪。
Fear has paralyzed her ability to act. 恐惧已经让她丧失了行动能力。


过去完成时:
The earthquake had paralyzed the city before help arrived. 在救援到来之前，地震已经使城市瘫痪。
She had been paralyzed by indecision. 她因优柔寡断而陷入瘫痪状态。


将来时:
The strike will paralyze the economy. 罢工将使经济瘫痪。
The coming winter will paralyze many transportation links. 即将来临的冬天将使许多交通线路瘫痪。


虚拟语气:
If the virus spread, it would paralyze the country. 如果病毒蔓延，它将使全国瘫痪。
I wish the pain wouldn't paralyze me. 我希望疼痛不会让我瘫痪。


被动语态:
The city was paralyzed by the blizzard. 这座城市被暴风雪瘫痪了。
His leg was paralyzed in the accident. 他的腿在事故中瘫痪了。


疑问句:
Is the city paralyzed by the storm?  城市被暴风雨瘫痪了吗？
Will the strike paralyze the entire industry?  罢工会使整个行业瘫痪吗？


否定句:
The cold didn't paralyze him.  寒冷并没有使他瘫痪。
The threat did not paralyze her resolve.  威胁并没有削弱她的决心。
