laurasias: /ˌlɔːrˈeɪʒəz/ | n. |劳亚古陆 |  A large, ancient landmass in the Northern Hemisphere that included most of the present-day continents of North America, Europe, and Asia.  It broke apart during the Mesozoic Era. |The breakup of Laurasia contributed to the diversity of plant and animal life on Earth.  劳亚古陆的裂解促成了地球上动植物生命的多样性。|Gondwana (反义词需结合语境,  因为不是严格的反义词，而是指另一块古陆)|名词
