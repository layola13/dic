franciscanism:/ˌfrænɪsˈkeɪnɪzəm/| n. |方济各会主义；方济各会精神|The principles and practices of the Franciscan Order, emphasizing poverty, humility, and service to the poor.  It encompasses a way of life characterized by simplicity, compassion, and dedication to God.|The Franciscanism of the friars was evident in their simple robes and charitable works.|近义词：fraternalism, asceticism|反义词：materialism, hedonism
