reminded:/rɪˈmaɪndɪd/| v.|提醒，使想起 |To cause (someone) to remember something.  |例句：He reminded me of my appointment. 他提醒了我我的约会。|例句：The picture reminded her of her childhood. 这张照片使她想起了童年。|近义词：remembered, recalled, brought to mind|反义词：forgot, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reminds me to take my medicine every day. 他每天提醒我吃药。
She reminds him of his promises. 她提醒他他的承诺。

一般过去时:
I reminded him about the meeting. 我提醒了他会议的事。
She reminded me to lock the door. 她提醒我锁门。

现在完成时:
I have reminded him several times. 我已经提醒他好几次了。
She has reminded me of my responsibilities. 她已经提醒了我我的责任。

过去完成时:
I had reminded him before he left. 他离开之前我已经提醒过他了。
She had reminded me about the deadline. 她已经提醒了我截止日期。

将来时:
I will remind him tomorrow. 我明天会提醒他。
She will remind me before the test. 她考试前会提醒我。

虚拟语气:
I wish I had reminded him earlier. 我真希望我早点提醒他。
If I had reminded her, she wouldn't have forgotten. 如果我提醒了她，她就不会忘记了。

被动语态:
He was reminded of his duties. 他被提醒了他的职责。
I was reminded to bring my passport. 我被提醒要带上我的护照。

疑问句:
Did you remind her? 你提醒她了吗？
Has he been reminded of the importance of this task? 他是否已经被提醒了这项任务的重要性？

否定句:
I didn't remind her to call. 我没有提醒她打电话。
He wasn't reminded of the meeting. 他没有被提醒会议的事。
