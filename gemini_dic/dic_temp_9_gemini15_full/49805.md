demonomist: /ˌdiːməˈnɒmɪst/ | n. | 恶魔学家 | A demonologist is a person who studies demons, their nature, and their activities.  They often investigate alleged demonic possessions or influences.  | Example: The demonologist examined the artifacts found at the scene, searching for clues to the nature of the supposed demonic presence.  | 近义词:  demonic scholar, supernatural investigator | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but concepts like theologian or exorcist could be considered contrasting) | 名词
