roundups:/ˈraʊndʌps/| n.|总结，综述，围捕|A roundup is a summary of news or other information, or the act of gathering together a group of animals or people.|例句：The newspaper published a roundup of the week's most important events.  报纸刊登了本周最重要事件的综述。|近义词：summary, overview, compilation, collection|反义词：detail, specifics
