pageful:/ˈpeɪdʒfʊl/| n.|一页纸的内容；满满一页|A pageful is an amount of writing or information that fills a page.  It suggests a substantial amount, not just a few lines.|例句：I wrote a pageful of notes during the lecture. 我在讲座上记了一页纸的笔记。

例句：The artist filled the pageful with intricate details.  艺术家用复杂的细节填满了这一页。


近义词：page, sheetful


反义词：fragment, snippet


词性:名词
