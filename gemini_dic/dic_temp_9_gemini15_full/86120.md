heartburn: /ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/ | n. |胃灼热，烧心| A burning sensation in the chest, often caused by acid reflux.  This occurs when stomach acid flows back into the esophagus. | Example:  I suffered from severe heartburn after eating that spicy curry.  我吃了那份辣咖喱后，胃灼热得很厉害。|近义词：acid indigestion, pyrosis |反义词：None
