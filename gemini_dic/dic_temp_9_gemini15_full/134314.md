noninstinctual: /ˌnɒnɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəl/ | adj. | 非本能的，非天生的 | Not instinctive; not based on instinct.  It refers to behaviors, actions, or reactions that are learned or acquired rather than innate. |  The child's ability to use tools was noninstinctual, demonstrating learned behavior.  孩子使用工具的能力是非本能的，这证明了后天习得的行为。


近义词: learned, acquired, deliberate, conscious, volitional
反义词: instinctive, innate, natural, reflex, automatic

词性: adjective (形容词)
