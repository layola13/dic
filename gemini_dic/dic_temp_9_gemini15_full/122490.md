mocha:/ˈmoʊkə/| n. adj.|摩卡咖啡，摩卡色|A type of coffee drink that is made with espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk.  It can also refer to a color, a dark brown with reddish-brown hues.|例句：I ordered a mocha with extra whipped cream. 我点了一杯加了额外鲜奶油的摩卡咖啡。|例句：Her dress was a beautiful mocha color. 她的裙子是美丽的摩卡色。|近义词：latte, cappuccino (for coffee); brown, auburn (for color)|反义词：None specifically, depends on context (light color for color)|名词，形容词
