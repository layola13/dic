revolvers:/rɪˈvɒlvərz/ | n. |左轮手枪 | A revolver is a handgun that has a revolving cylinder containing multiple cartridges.  It is typically loaded by rotating the cylinder to align each chamber with the barrel. | Example: The outlaw pulled out his revolver and fired.  歹徒拔出左轮手枪并开枪了。  The detective examined the revolver carefully for fingerprints.  侦探仔细检查左轮手枪上的指纹。|近义词：pistol, handgun |反义词：无
