reposed:/rɪˈpoʊzd/ | v. | 静止；安息；存放；依赖 | to be in a state of rest or quiet; to place (something) in a particular place for safekeeping; to place confidence or trust in someone or something. |例句：The cat reposed peacefully on the sunbeam.  猫安详地躺在阳光下。
The documents were reposed in the vault. 文件被存放在金库里。|近义词：rested, lay, placed, relied |反义词：active, restless, agitated

She reposes her head on the pillow. 她把头枕在枕头上。
