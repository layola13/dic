gulpers:/ˈɡʌlpərz/| n.|吞咽者；大口喝水者|A gulper is someone or something that gulps, often referring to a creature that swallows its prey whole or someone who drinks quickly and noisily.|例句：The anglerfish is a deep-sea gulper, known for its enormous mouth.|近义词：swallower, guzzler|反义词：sipper


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The whale gulps huge amounts of water. = 鲸鱼吞下大量的海水。
He gulps his food down quickly. = 他狼吞虎咽地吃东西。

一般过去时:
She gulped the water in one go. = 她一口喝光了水。
The bird gulped down the worm. = 鸟儿吞下了虫子。

现在完成时:
They have gulped down three bottles of beer. = 他们已经喝光了三瓶啤酒。
The shark has gulped its prey whole. = 鲨鱼已经把猎物整个吞了下去。

虚拟语气:
