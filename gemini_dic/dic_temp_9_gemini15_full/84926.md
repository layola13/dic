hanseatic:/ˌhænziˈætɪk/| adj.|汉萨同盟的；汉萨城市的；|Relating to the Hanseatic League, a medieval commercial and defensive confederation of towns in northern Germany and surrounding areas.  The term often evokes images of maritime trade, mercantile power, and a specific architectural style.|例句：The Hanseatic cities were renowned for their wealth and influence.  汉萨同盟城市以其财富和影响力而闻名。

The Hanseatic architecture of Lübeck is a testament to the league's power.  吕贝克的汉萨风格建筑是该同盟力量的证明。|近义词：Germanic (in a historical context), mercantile|反义词：None specifically, but words implying weakness or lack of trade power could be considered antonyms in context.
