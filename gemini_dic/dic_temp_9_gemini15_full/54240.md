digestion:/daɪˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 消化；领悟 | The process of breaking down food in the body into a form that can be absorbed and used.  This includes mechanical and chemical processes.  It also refers to the mental process of understanding something. | Example:  The digestion of food is essential for obtaining nutrients. 消化食物对于获取营养至关重要。


The slow digestion of complex ideas requires patience and reflection.  复杂思想的缓慢消化需要耐心和反思。| assimilation, absorption | indigestion, malabsorption | noun
