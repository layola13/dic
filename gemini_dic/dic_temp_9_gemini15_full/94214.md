imperforable:/ɪmˈpɔːrfərəbl/ | adj. | 不可穿透的，无法渗透的 | Incapable of being pierced or penetrated.  The material is imperforable, making it ideal for protective gear.  这种材料不可穿透，非常适合制作防护装备。 | 近义词：impenetrable, impervious, unpierceable | 反义词：permeable, penetrable |  形容词

词源:
The word "imperforable" comes from the prefix "im-" (not), "perforate" (to pierce), and the suffix "-able" (capable of).  Therefore, it literally means "not able to be pierced."
