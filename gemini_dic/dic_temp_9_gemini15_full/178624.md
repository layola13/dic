recoverableness: /rɪˌkʌvərəˈbɪlnəs/ | n. | 可恢复性，可挽回性 | The ability to be recovered or regained.  It refers to the degree to which something can be restored to its previous state after damage, loss, or failure.  | Example: The recoverableness of the data after the hard drive crash was questionable.  硬盘崩溃后数据的可恢复性令人怀疑。  The recoverableness of the antique vase after it fell was surprisingly high. 古董花瓶跌落后，其可恢复性出奇地高。| 近义词：recoverability, retrievability | 反义词：irrecoverability, irretrievability | 名词
