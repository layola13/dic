half-made:/ˌhɑːfˈmeɪd/| adj. |半成品的，未完成的 |Partially completed; unfinished.  It describes something that is only partly finished or made. |例句：The half-made cake sat on the counter, covered in a dusting of flour.  = 半成品的蛋糕放在柜台上，上面撒着一些面粉。

The half-made project was abandoned due to lack of funding. = 由于资金不足，这个未完成的项目被放弃了。|近义词：incomplete, unfinished, uncompleted, partly finished |反义词：complete, finished, perfected
