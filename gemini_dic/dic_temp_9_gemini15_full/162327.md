preach:/priːtʃ/| vt. vi. n.|布道；宣扬；竭力鼓吹|To deliver a sermon or religious address; to advocate or promote (a belief, cause, etc.) earnestly.|例句：The priest preached a sermon on forgiveness.  牧师作了一篇关于宽恕的布道。|例句：He preached the gospel of hard work. 他宣扬努力工作的福音。|近义词：proclaim, advocate, sermonize|反义词：silence, suppress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preaches every Sunday. 他每个星期天都布道。
She preaches tolerance and understanding. 她宣扬宽容和理解。

一般过去时:
The pastor preached a powerful sermon last week. 牧师上周发表了一篇有力的布道。
They preached against violence and hatred. 他们谴责暴力和仇恨。

现在完成时:
He has preached to thousands of people. 他已经向成千上万的人布道。
She has preached this message for years. 她多年来一直宣扬这个信息。

过去完成时:
He had preached the same sermon many times before. 他以前多次讲过同样的布道。
They had preached against the war for months. 他们几个月来一直反对这场战争。

将来时:
He will preach at the cathedral tomorrow. 他明天将在教堂布道。
She will preach about the importance of education. 她将宣讲教育的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If he preached with more passion, more people would listen. 如果他布道更热情一些，就会有更多人倾听。
I wish he would preach less about politics. 我希望他少谈论一些政治。

被动语态:
The sermon was preached to a large congregation. 布道是对一个大型会众进行的。
Tolerance and understanding are preached in many religions. 宽容和理解在许多宗教中都被宣扬。

疑问句:
Does he preach often? 他经常布道吗？
What did he preach about? 他讲的是什么？


否定句:
He doesn't preach hatred. 他不宣扬仇恨。
They did not preach violence. 他们没有宣扬暴力。


PHRASE->
preach to the choir 对牛弹琴
preach about 宣讲...

preach at sb. 对某人说教
