obdurated:/ˈɒbdʒʊreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 顽固的；固执的；冷酷无情的 | hardened or made hard; stubbornly persistent in wrongdoing |  The obdurated criminal refused to confess, even in the face of overwhelming evidence.  这个顽固的罪犯拒绝认罪，即使面对压倒性的证据。 | 近义词：stubborn, obstinate, inflexible, intractable | 反义词：yielding, compliant, flexible
