intemperance:/ɪnˈtɛmpərəns/| n. |放纵；过度；不节制 |Lack of moderation or restraint; excessive indulgence.  It often refers to excessive consumption of alcohol, but can also apply to other areas of life like eating, spending, or working.|例句：His intemperance led to his downfall.他的放纵导致了他的垮台。  Her intemperance in spending left her deeply in debt. 她的过度消费让她负债累累。|近义词：excess, immoderation, extravagance |反义词：temperance, moderation, restraint
