mapped:/mæpt/| v. | 绘制地图；规划；对应；|to represent (something) on a map; to plan or arrange systematically; to establish a correspondence between.|例句：The explorers mapped the new territory.  探险家们绘制了新领地的地图。|例句：The project was carefully mapped out.  这个项目被仔细地规划出来了。|近义词：charted, plotted, planned, correlated|反义词：unmapped, unplanned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maps the area for the new road. = 他为新路绘制该区域的地图。
They map out their marketing strategy. = 他们规划他们的营销策略。

一般过去时:
She mapped the coastline. = 她绘制了海岸线的地图。
We mapped the entire project timeline. = 我们规划了整个项目的进度表。


现在完成时:
They have mapped the entire genome. = 他们已经绘制了整个基因组图谱。
We have mapped out a detailed plan for the conference. = 我们已经为会议制定了一个详细的计划。


过去完成时:
They had mapped the region before the expedition. = 他们在探险之前已经绘制了该地区的地图。
She had mapped out her escape route before the fire started. = 大火开始之前，她已经规划好了逃生路线。


将来时:
He will map the stars for his astronomy project. = 他将为他的天文项目绘制星图。
They will map out the new city's infrastructure. = 他们将规划新城市的交通基础设施。


虚拟语气:
If we had mapped the terrain, we wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我们事先绘制了地形图，我们就不会迷路了。
I wish I had mapped out my career path earlier. = 我真希望我早点规划好我的职业道路。


被动语态:
The city was mapped in detail. = 这个城市被详细地绘制成地图。
The project plan was mapped out by the team leader. = 项目计划是由团队领导制定的。


疑问句:
Has the area been mapped yet? = 该区域的地图绘制好了吗？
How was the project mapped out? = 项目是如何规划的？


否定句:
They didn't map the entire forest. = 他们没有绘制整个森林的地图。
We haven't mapped out the details of the budget yet. = 我们还没有规划好预算的细节。
