rille:/rɪl/| n.|细沟，小槽|A narrow, shallow groove or channel, especially on the surface of a celestial body.  |例句：Lunar rilles are long, narrow channels on the Moon's surface. 月球沟壑是月球表面狭长而深的沟槽。|例句：The geologist carefully examined the rilles in the rock face. 地质学家仔细检查了岩壁上的细沟。|近义词：groove, furrow, channel|反义词：ridge, crest
