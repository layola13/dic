in-side:/ˌɪnˈsaɪd/| adj. adv. n.|内部的；里面；内部|Inside means within or into the interior of something. It can be used as an adjective, adverb, or noun.|例句：The inside of the house was beautifully decorated. (adj.)  房子的内部装饰得很漂亮。 The cat ran inside when it saw the dog. (adv.) 猫看到狗就跑进屋里了。  Let's look at the inside of the box. (n.) 我们来看看盒子的里面。|近义词：inner, interior, within|反义词：outside, exterior
