jejunum:/dʒɪˈdʒuːnəm/| n. |空肠|The jejunum is the middle section of the small intestine, between the duodenum and the ileum.  It's responsible for the majority of nutrient absorption.  |Example: The surgeon carefully examined the jejunum during the laparoscopy. 外科医生在腹腔镜检查中仔细检查了空肠。|近义词：midgut (less specific)|反义词：duodenum, ileum
