intelligencer:/ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənsər/ | n. |情报员，情报机构 |A person who gathers information secretly, especially for a government.  An organization that gathers and analyzes intelligence. |例句：The intelligencer infiltrated the enemy camp and obtained valuable information.  情报员渗透到敌营并获得了宝贵的情报。

The military intelligencer was highly skilled in espionage.  这位军事情报员非常擅长间谍活动。|近义词：spy, agent, scout |反义词：informant (if referring to someone who reveals information),  civilian (in the context of military intelligence) |名词
