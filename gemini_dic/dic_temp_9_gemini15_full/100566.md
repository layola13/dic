interwoven:/ˌɪntərˈwoʊvn/| adj.| 交织的；错综复杂的 |  Interwoven describes something where things are closely connected or mixed together, often in a complex way.  It implies a degree of intricacy and perhaps even inseparability. |  The stories of the characters were beautifully interwoven throughout the novel.  = 小说中人物的故事巧妙地交织在一起。

The threads of the tapestry were so finely interwoven that it was difficult to distinguish individual strands. = 挂毯的线织得如此精细，很难区分出单个线。| intricately connected, intertwined, entangled | separated, distinct, unconnected | adjective
