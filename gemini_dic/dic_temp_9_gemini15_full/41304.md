corduroy:/ˈkɔːrdərɔɪ/| n.|灯芯绒|A durable fabric with a distinctive raised wale pattern, typically made of cotton.  It is known for its softness and texture. |Example:  She wore a corduroy jacket. 她穿着一件灯芯绒外套。|近义词：velveteen, velvets|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps smooth fabrics like silk or satin.|名词

词源:
The word "corduroy" originates from the French phrase "corde du roi," meaning "king's cord" or "king's cloth."  It was originally a heavy, durable fabric used for workwear and clothing.  The raised ribbed pattern is created by the weaving process.
