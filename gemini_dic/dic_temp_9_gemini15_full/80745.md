gosling:/ˈɡɒzlɪŋ/ | /ˈɡɑːzlɪŋ/ | n. | 鹅雏，小鹅 | A young goose.  | Example: The mother goose protectively watched over her goslings. 母鹅保护性地守护着她的鹅雏。  The farmer collected the goslings and put them in a pen. 农民收集了鹅雏并将它们放在围栏里。|近义词：young goose, cygnet (if referring to a swan) |反义词：adult goose

USAGE->

A gosling follows its mother. = 小鹅跟着它的妈妈。
The goslings swim in the pond. = 小鹅们在池塘里游泳。
