prehesitate:/ˌpriːˈhɛzɪteɪt/| v.|犹豫不决，迟疑|To hesitate or be reluctant before doing something; to show signs of uncertainty or doubt before acting.|例句：He prehesitated before answering the difficult question. 他在回答那个难题之前犹豫了一下。|例句：She prehesitated, clearly unsure of what to say next. 她犹豫了一下，显然不知道接下来该说什么。|近义词：vacillate, waver, falter|反义词：rush, bolt, plunge|动词
