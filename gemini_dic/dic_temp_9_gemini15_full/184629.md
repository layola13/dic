restrengthens:/ˌriːˈstrɛŋθənz/ | v. |  重新增强；使恢复活力 | To make stronger again; to revitalize.  | The new policy restrengthens the economy. 新政策增强了经济实力。|  reinforce, strengthen, revitalize | weaken, debilitate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
