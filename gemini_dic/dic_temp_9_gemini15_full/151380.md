parleying:/ˈpɑːrliɪŋ/| n. | 谈判；会谈；交涉 | The act of engaging in a discussion, especially a negotiation or an attempt to settle a dispute.  |例句：The two sides are engaged in lengthy parleying. 双方正在进行冗长的谈判。|例句：The parleying between the warring factions continued for hours. 交战各派之间的谈判持续了数小时。|近义词：negotiation, discussion, conference, deliberation |反义词：conflict, fighting, battle, war |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are parleying with the terrorists. = 他们在和恐怖分子谈判。
The diplomats are parleying to find a peaceful solution. = 外交官们正在谈判以寻找和平解决办法。


一般过去时:
The negotiators parleyed for hours before reaching an agreement. = 谈判人员谈判了数小时才达成协议。
The two leaders parleyed on the phone last night. = 两位领导人昨晚通了电话进行会谈。


现在完成时:
They have been parleying for days, but haven't reached a conclusion yet. = 他们已经谈判了好几天，但还没有达成结论。
The warring parties have parleyed several times, but the conflict persists. = 交战各方已经谈判过几次，但冲突仍在持续。


过去完成时:
They had parleyed extensively before the final agreement was reached. = 在最终协议达成之前，他们已经进行了广泛的谈判。
The general had parleyed with the enemy commander several times before the decisive battle. = 在决定性战役之前，将军曾多次与敌军指挥官谈判。


将来时:
The president will parley with the opposition leader tomorrow. = 总统明天将与反对派领导人会谈。
We will parley with our creditors to restructure our debts. = 我们将与债权人谈判以重组我们的债务。


虚拟语气:
If they had parleyed earlier, they might have avoided the war. = 如果他们早点谈判，也许就能避免战争。
I wish they would parley and find a peaceful resolution. = 我希望他们能进行谈判并找到和平的解决办法。


被动语态:
The matter was parleyed over for weeks. = 这件事被讨论了好几个星期。
A peace agreement was parleyed into existence. = 通过谈判达成了和平协议。


疑问句:
Are they parleying with the enemy? = 他们正在与敌人谈判吗？
Will they parley before resorting to violence? = 在诉诸暴力之前，他们会谈判吗？


否定句:
They refused to parley with the rebels. = 他们拒绝与叛军谈判。
The government will not parley under duress. = 政府不会在胁迫下谈判。
