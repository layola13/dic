hyperoxygenated: /ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 富氧的，高氧的 | Containing or having an abnormally high level of oxygen.  This term usually refers to blood that has a higher-than-normal oxygen saturation. | Example: Hyperoxygenated blood is crucial for efficient tissue function. 富氧血液对有效的组织功能至关重要。 |近义词：oxygen-rich, highly oxygenated | 反义词：hypoxic, deoxygenated
