contemptible:/kənˈtɛmptəbəl/| adj.|令人鄙视的；卑劣的；可鄙的|Deserving contempt; despicable.  Something contemptible is worthy of scorn and disgust because it is morally reprehensible,  shameful, or base.|His behavior was contemptible and showed a lack of respect for others.  他的行为令人鄙视，表现出对别人的不尊重。|despicable, vile, shameful, base, mean|admirable, respectable, honorable, laudable
