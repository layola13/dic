reilluminate: /ˌriːɪˈluːmɪneɪt/ | vt. |重新照亮；使…重焕光明；使…重新闪耀 | To illuminate again; to shed light on something anew; to brighten or revive something.  |例句：The newly installed lights reilluminated the dark alley. 新安装的灯重新照亮了黑暗的小巷。

The sunrise reilluminated the landscape after the storm.  暴风雨过后，日出重新照亮了这片风景。|近义词：relight, brighten, revitalize |反义词：darken, extinguish, dim
