gybe:/dʒaɪb/| v.|改变方向；(船)改变航向；(非正式)讽刺；挖苦|To change direction, especially of a sailing vessel by turning the boat's bow through the wind.  It also means to jeer or taunt someone.|例句：The captain gybed the ship to avoid the oncoming storm. 船长改变船的航向以躲避即将到来的风暴。

例句：He gybed at her clumsy attempt to dance. 他嘲笑她笨拙的舞蹈尝试。|近义词：tack, veer, jibe (same spelling, different pronunciation), taunt, mock|反义词：praise, compliment
