seborrhoeic:/ˌsɛbəˈroʊɪk/| adj.|溢脂性的，脂溢性的| Relating to or characteristic of seborrhoea (excessive secretion of sebum from the sebaceous glands).  The term often describes skin conditions associated with this overproduction of oil.|例句：He suffers from seborrhoeic dermatitis. 他患有脂溢性皮炎。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as seborrhoeic eczema. 医生诊断她的病情为脂溢性湿疹。


词源:
From seborrhoea + -ic.  Seborrhoea itself comes from Latin *sebum ("tallow, grease") + Greek -rhoia ("flow, discharge").


近义词:
oily, greasy


反义词:
dry (in the context of skin)


词性:
形容词
