prevolunteer:/ˌpriːˈvɒlənˌtɪər/ | adj. | 预先志愿的；提前志愿的 |  Describing someone who volunteers before an event or need officially arises.  It suggests proactive volunteering rather than reactive.  | Example: The prevolunteer firefighters were already on the scene before the alarm sounded. 预先志愿的消防员在警报响起之前就已经到达现场了。 |近义词：proactive, anticipatory | 反义词：reactive, belated
