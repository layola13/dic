life:/laɪf/ | n. |生命，生活，人生 | The life of a plant is short. 植物的生命短暂。  |  近义词：existence, living, livelihood | 反义词：death


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Life is beautiful. = 生命是美丽的。
He lives in London. = 他住在伦敦。


一般过去时:
She lived in Paris for five years. = 她曾在巴黎住了五年。
We lived a simple life in the countryside. = 我们在乡下过着简朴的生活。


现在完成时:
I have lived here all my life. = 我住在这里一辈子了。
They have lived through many hardships. = 他们经历了许多苦难。


过去完成时:
He had lived in New York before moving to Los Angeles. = 他在搬到洛杉矶之前住在纽约。
She had lived a difficult life before she met him. = 在遇到他之前，她过着艰难的生活。


将来时:
I will live a better life in the future. = 我将来会过上更好的生活。
We will live to see the 22nd century. = 我们将活到22世纪。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would live a more comfortable life. = 如果我有更多钱，我会过上更舒适的生活。
I wish I could live forever. = 我希望我能永远活着。


被动语态:
His life was changed forever by the accident. = 他的生活因事故而永远改变了。
The story of his life is being written. = 他的生平故事正在被撰写。


疑问句:
What is the meaning of life? = 生命的意义是什么？
How long will he live? = 他还能活多久？


否定句:
There is no life on Mars. = 火星上没有生命。
He doesn't live a healthy life. = 他没有过健康的生活。
