go-kart:/ˈɡoʊkɑːrt/| n. |小型赛车；卡丁车 |A small, open racing car, typically for one person, with low-slung chassis and powerful engine.  It's designed for speed and maneuverability on tracks. |例句：My son loves to race his go-kart every weekend.  我的儿子每个周末都喜欢开他的卡丁车比赛。

例句：He won first place in the go-kart competition. 他在卡丁车比赛中获得了第一名。|近义词：kart, racing kart |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑大型赛车等作为对比)|词性:名词
