shopkeepers:/ˈʃɑːpˌkiːpərz/ | n. | 店主们，店主（复数） | People who own or manage shops.  Shopkeepers are vital to the local economy. 店主对当地经济至关重要。|近义词：retailers, merchants |反义词：customers, shoppers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shopkeepers open their shops at 8 a.m. = 店主们早上8点开门。
These shopkeepers sell fresh produce. = 这些店主卖新鲜农产品。

一般过去时:
The shopkeepers closed early yesterday. = 店主们昨天早早关门了。
The shopkeepers helped the customer find what they needed. = 店主帮助顾客找到他们需要的东西。

现在完成时:
The shopkeepers have increased their prices recently. = 店主们最近涨价了。
The shopkeepers have been working hard all day. = 店主们整天都在努力工作。

过去完成时:
The shopkeepers had already closed when I arrived. = 我到达时，店主们已经关门了。
The shopkeepers had prepared for the busy holiday season. = 店主们已经为繁忙的假日季节做好了准备。

将来时:
The shopkeepers will open a new branch next month. = 店主们下个月将开设一个新分店。
The shopkeepers will be attending the trade show. = 店主们将参加贸易展。

虚拟语气:
If the shopkeepers had advertised more, they would have sold more goods. = 如果店主们多做些广告，他们就会卖出更多商品。
I wish the shopkeepers would lower their prices. = 我希望店主们能降价。

被动语态:
The shopkeepers were robbed last night. = 店主们昨晚被抢劫了。
The shop windows were cleaned by the shopkeepers. = 店主们擦洗了橱窗。

疑问句:
Are the shopkeepers friendly? = 店主们友善吗？
Do the shopkeepers accept credit cards? = 店主们接受信用卡吗？

否定句:
The shopkeepers didn't open on Sundays. = 店主们星期天不开门。
The shopkeepers don't offer discounts. = 店主们不提供折扣。
