semi-direct:/ˌsɛmiˈdaɪrɛkt/| adj.|半直接的，间接的（但接近直接）|Partially direct; not completely indirect.  It often refers to methods or approaches that combine elements of both direct and indirect methods.  For example, in language teaching, a semi-direct method might involve some direct instruction but also incorporate communicative activities.|例句：The teacher used a semi-direct approach to teaching grammar, combining explanation with practice.|近义词：indirect, partly direct|反义词：direct
