immobilizing: /ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 使动弹不得的；使无法移动的 | Rendering immobile; making incapable of movement.  | The immobilizing effect of the anesthesia was immediate. 麻醉药的固定作用是立即发生的。The accident left him immobilized, unable to move his legs.  事故使他动弹不得，无法移动双腿。|  paralyzing, crippling, restraining | mobilizing, freeing, liberating |  形容词
