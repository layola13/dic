fluey:/ˈfluːi/| adj.|伤风的；患感冒的；不舒服的|Feeling unwell, typically with a cold or flu.  The term often implies a mild illness, not necessarily a full-blown flu.|例句：I feel a bit fluey today; I think I'm coming down with something.  我今天感觉有点不舒服，我想我快感冒了。|例句：She's been feeling fluey all week, so she stayed home from work. 她整个星期都感觉不舒服，所以她没去上班。|近义词：under the weather, poorly, unwell, slightly ill|反义词：healthy, well
