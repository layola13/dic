cephalopode:/ˈsɛfələpɒd/| n. |头足类动物|A marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, characterized by a prominent head and arms or tentacles that usually surround the mouth.  They include octopuses, squid, cuttlefish, and nautiluses.|例句：The octopus is a fascinating example of a cephalopode.  这只章鱼是头足类动物的一个迷人例子。

例句：Scientists are studying the complex nervous systems of cephalopodes. 科学家们正在研究头足类动物复杂的神经系统。
