invigilations:/ɪnˈvɪdʒɪleɪʃənz/| n.|监考；监视|The act of supervising examinations or other activities to prevent cheating or ensure fairness.  It refers to the process of overseeing and monitoring a test, exam, or other formal activity to maintain order and integrity.|例句：The invigilations were strictly enforced during the final exams. 期末考试期间严格执行监考制度。

例句：The invigilations ensured a fair and unbiased testing environment.  监考确保了公平公正的考试环境。


近义词：supervision, monitoring, overseeing, proctoring
反义词：non-supervision, neglect


词源:  From the verb "invigilate," which comes from the Late Latin *in- "in" + vigilare "to watch, be awake."  The word thus literally means "to watch over."
