rhodic:/ˈroʊdɪk/ | adj. |罗迪克的；（土壤）富含铁的，呈红色的 | Relating to or characteristic of rhodic soils, which are red-colored soils rich in iron and aluminum oxides.  These soils are typically found in humid tropical and subtropical regions. | The rhodic soil profile showed clear evidence of intense weathering.  罗迪克土壤剖面显示出强烈风化的明显证据。|近义词:ferruginous, lateritic |反义词:albic
