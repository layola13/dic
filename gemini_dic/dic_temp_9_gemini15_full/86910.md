hemlocks:/ˈhɛmlɑːk/| n.|铁杉；毒芹|Evergreen coniferous trees of the genus Tsuga, with short needles and cones.  The name also refers to a highly poisonous plant, Conium maculatum, also known as poison hemlock.|例句：The hemlock trees provided shade for the hikers.  铁杉为远足者提供了阴凉。|例句：Ancient Greeks used hemlock to execute criminals. 古希腊人用毒芹处决罪犯。|近义词：conifer, evergreen|反义词：无
