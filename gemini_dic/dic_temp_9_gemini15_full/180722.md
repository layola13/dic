regroove:/ˌriːˈɡruːv/| vt.|重新开槽；改进；翻新|To create a new groove or grooves in something, often to improve its function or appearance.  It can also refer to reviving or updating something, giving it a new lease on life. |例句：The engineer regrooved the worn-out record. 工程师重新开槽了磨损的唱片。

The band regrooved their old hit song with a modern sound. 乐队用现代的音效翻新了他们以前的热门歌曲。|近义词：renovate, revamp, refurbish, update|反义词：destroy, ruin, damage
