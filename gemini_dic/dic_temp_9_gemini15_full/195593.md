semiprovincial:/ˌsɛmiprəˈvɪnʃəl/| adj.| 半乡下气的；有些土气的；不够世故的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a person or place that is somewhat provincial; lacking sophistication or worldly experience.  It suggests a blend of rural simplicity and a touch of awkwardness in social settings. | Example:  He had a semiprovincial charm that was both endearing and slightly unsettling. 他有一种半乡下气的魅力，既可爱又有点让人不安。  |近义词：unsophisticated, naive, provincial, rustic |反义词：sophisticated, cosmopolitan, worldly
