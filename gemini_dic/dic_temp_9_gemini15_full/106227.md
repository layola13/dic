knotty:/ˈnɒti/ /ˈnɑːti/| adj.| 棘手的；复杂的；难解的；多节的|  Difficult to solve or understand; full of knots.  The word derives from "knot," referring to the tangled nature of something complex. |例句：The problem proved to be knotty and required considerable thought.  这个问题证明很棘手，需要仔细考虑。

The detective faced a knotty case with many unanswered questions.  这位侦探面临一个棘手的案件，有很多悬而未决的问题。|近义词：complex, intricate, tangled, perplexing, convoluted|反义词：simple, easy, straightforward, uncomplicated|
