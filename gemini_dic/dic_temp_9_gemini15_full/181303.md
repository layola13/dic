reinterpreted:/ˌriːɪnˈtɜːprɪtɪd/| v.|重新诠释，重新解释 |To interpret (something) in a new or different way.  To give a new meaning or understanding to something.|例句：The painting has been reinterpreted by several art historians.  几名艺术史学家对这幅画作进行了重新诠释。

例句：The old data was reinterpreted in light of new discoveries. 基于新的发现，旧数据被重新解读。


近义词：re-explained, re-examined, re-evaluated, reanalyzed


反义词：misinterpreted, misunderstood


词性：动词 (过去分词)
