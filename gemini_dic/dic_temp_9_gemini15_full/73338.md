forefeel:/fɔːrˈfiːl/ | v. | 预感，预料 | To have a feeling or intuition about something before it happens; to anticipate.  |例句：I forefelt that the meeting would be difficult. 我预感到会议会很难。

例句：She forefelt trouble brewing between the two brothers. 她预感到两兄弟之间会有麻烦。


近义词：anticipate, foresee, presage, prefigure | 反义词：be surprised by, be unaware of | 词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I forefeel a storm coming. = 我预感到暴风雨即将来临。
They forefeel a change in the market. = 他们预感到市场将要发生变化。

一般过去时:
I forefelt the earthquake before it happened. = 地震发生前我预感到了。
She forefelt that her project would fail. = 她预感到她的项目会失败。

现在完成时:
I have forefelt this moment for years. = 我多年来一直预感到这一刻。
They have forefelt the consequences of their actions. = 他们已经预感到他们行为的后果。

过去完成时:
I had forefelt the danger, so I avoided the area. = 我预感到危险，所以我避开了那个区域。
She had forefelt his rejection, so she wasn't surprised. = 她预感到他会拒绝，所以并不惊讶。

将来时:
I will forefeel the outcome of the election. = 我将预感到选举的结果。
They will forefeel the impact of the new policy. = 他们将预感到新政策的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had forefelt the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预感到后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had forefelt the storm. = 我真希望我预感到暴风雨。

被动语态: (被动语态用在此处不常见，因为forefeel 通常是主观感受)
The coming disaster was forefelt by many. = 许多人预感到即将到来的灾难。(较为生硬)
The impending danger was not forefelt by the authorities. = 当局没有预感到迫在眉睫的危险。(较为生硬)


疑问句:
Do you forefeel any problems with the project? = 你预感到这个项目有什么问题吗？
Did she forefeel the argument before it started? = 争吵开始前她预感到吗？

否定句:
I didn't forefeel the sudden change in weather. = 我没有预感到天气突然变化。
He didn't forefeel the company's bankruptcy. = 他没有预感到公司会破产。


PHRASE->

forefeel the consequences = 预感到后果
forefeel something bad = 预感到不好的事情
