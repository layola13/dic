leafstalk:/ˈliːfˌstɔːk/| n. |叶柄|The stalk that supports a leaf.  It connects the leaf blade to the stem. Leafstalks can vary greatly in length, shape, and thickness depending on the plant species.  |例句：The leafstalk of the sunflower is quite thick and sturdy. 向日葵的叶柄相当粗壮结实。

The vibrant red leafstalks contrasted beautifully with the green leaves.鲜红的叶柄与绿叶形成了美丽的对比。|近义词：petiole|反义词：None. |名词
