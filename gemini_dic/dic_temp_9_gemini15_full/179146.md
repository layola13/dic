redictating: /ˌriːˈdɪkteɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重复口述，重新听写 |  The act of dictating something again, often to correct errors or for clarification. This implies a previous dictation session.  | 例句：The boss asked his secretary to redictate the letter after finding several grammatical errors.  老板发现信中有一些语法错误，要求秘书重新口述这封信。

例句：After the first dictation proved unsatisfactory, the speaker decided to redictate the entire document. 第一次口述不令人满意后，演讲者决定重新口述整个文件。


近义词：re-dictate, repeat dictation, re-record dictation | 反义词：  N/A  | 词性:动词
