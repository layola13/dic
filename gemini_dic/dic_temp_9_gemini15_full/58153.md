droughtier:/ˈdraʊtiər/| adj.|更干旱的，更干燥的|More dry or arid.  The adjective "droughtier" is the comparative form of "drought" meaning more affected by or experiencing drought.|例句：The droughtier regions of the country are facing severe water shortages.  这个国家比较干旱的地区正面临严重的缺水问题。

The western states are becoming increasingly droughtier each year. 西部各州每年都变得越来越干旱。|近义词：dryer, drier, arid, parched|反义词：wetter, more humid
