phobias:/ˈfoʊbiəz/|/ˈfəʊbiəz/| n. |恐惧症，恐惧；对某事物或某类事物的强烈恐惧|Phobias are persistent, excessive, and unrealistic fears of specific objects, activities, or situations.|例句：Many people suffer from phobias, such as arachnophobia (fear of spiders) and claustrophobia (fear of enclosed spaces).  很多人患有恐惧症，例如蜘蛛恐惧症（对蜘蛛的恐惧）和幽闭恐惧症（对封闭空间的恐惧）。|近义词：fear, dread, anxiety|反义词：courage, bravery
