self-ease:/ˌsɛlfˈiːz/| n.| 自在，安心，舒适| The feeling of being comfortable and at ease with oneself; a state of relaxed self-acceptance.  It implies a sense of inner peace and confidence, free from self-doubt or anxiety. |例句：After years of therapy, she finally found her self-ease.  多年治疗后，她终于找到了自在感。|例句：He cultivated self-ease through meditation and mindfulness. 他通过冥想和正念培养了内心的自在。|近义词：self-acceptance, composure, serenity, tranquility, peace of mind|反义词：anxiety, self-doubt, unease, discomfort, stress|名词
