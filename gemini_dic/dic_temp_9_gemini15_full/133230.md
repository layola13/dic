nonexclamatory: /ˌnɒnɪkˈsklæməˌtɔːri/ | adj. |非感叹的，陈述的 | Not expressing strong emotion; declarative.  A nonexclamatory sentence is a statement, question, or command, but not an exclamation. | Example:  The cat sat on the mat. (This is a nonexclamatory sentence.)  | 近义词：declarative, affirmative, assertive | 反义词：exclamatory, interjectory
