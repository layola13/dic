expeditioners:/ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃənərz/ | n. |探险队员们 |People who are part of an expedition.  The term is a plural noun, referring to a group of individuals undertaking a journey or exploration, typically to a remote or challenging location. |The expeditioners faced many hardships during their trek across the Antarctic. 探险队员们在穿越南极洲的旅程中面临许多困难。  The expeditioners returned with invaluable data about the region's flora and fauna. 探险队员们带回了关于该地区动植物的宝贵数据。 | explorers, adventurers | settlers, residents | 名词
