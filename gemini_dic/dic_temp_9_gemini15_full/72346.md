floweret:/ˈflaʊərɛt/| n.|小花，小朵|A small flower, especially one that is part of a larger flower head or inflorescence.|例句：The daisy is composed of many tiny flowerets.  雏菊由许多小花组成。
例句：The inflorescence was a dense cluster of small, delicate flowerets. 花序是一簇密集的小巧精致的花朵。|近义词：floret, small flower|反义词:large flower, blossom
