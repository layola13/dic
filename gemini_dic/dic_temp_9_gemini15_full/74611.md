freckles:/ˈfrekəlz/| n.|雀斑；斑点|Small, brownish spots on the skin, especially on the face and arms, caused by exposure to the sun.  They are often considered a charming feature.|例句：She has adorable freckles across her nose. 她鼻子上布满了可爱的雀斑。
例句：The sun gave him a scattering of freckles on his shoulders. 太阳晒得他肩膀上长满了雀斑。|近义词：spots, speckles|反义词：clear skin|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has many freckles. = 他有很多雀斑。
She always gets freckles in the summer. = 她在夏天总是长雀斑。

一般过去时:
He had freckles as a child. = 他小时候有雀斑。

现在完成时:

I've developed more freckles after my vacation. = 度假后我长了更多雀斑。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
