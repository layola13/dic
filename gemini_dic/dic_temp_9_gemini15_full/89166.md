homemaker:/ˈhoʊmˌmeɪkər/| n. |家庭主妇，家庭主婦|A person, typically a woman, who manages a home and family.  This role traditionally involves cooking, cleaning, childcare, and managing the household budget.  However, the modern definition is more inclusive and can include men and individuals in same-sex relationships who take on the primary responsibility of managing the household.|例句：She is a dedicated homemaker who prioritizes her family's well-being. 她是一位尽职尽责的家庭主妇，她优先考虑家人的幸福。|例句：He's a stay-at-home homemaker who loves cooking and gardening. 他是一位全职家庭主妇，热爱烹饪和园艺。|近义词：housewife, homebody, stay-at-home parent|反义词：career woman, breadwinner|名词
