come-play:/kʌm pleɪ/ | v. | 来玩 | This is not a single word in standard English dictionaries.  It's a compound of two verbs, "come" and "play," often used informally as an invitation or suggestion. | Come play with me! = 来和我一起玩！ Let's come play outside. = 让我们到外面玩吧！ |  arrive, join, participate |  refuse, stay, work | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Come play with me now. = 现在来和我一起玩。
They come play basketball every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都来打篮球。

一般过去时:
We came play hide-and-seek yesterday. = 我们昨天玩捉迷藏。
She came play the piano for us. = 她来为我们弹钢琴。

现在完成时:
They have come play here many times. = 他们来这里玩过很多次了。
We have come play in this park before. = 我们以前在这个公园玩过。

过去完成时:
Before I arrived, they had come played a game of chess. = 我到达之前，他们已经下了一盘棋。
They had come played several rounds before we joined. = 在我们加入之前，他们已经玩了几轮了。

将来时:
I will come play with you tomorrow. = 我明天会和你一起玩。
They will come play at the beach this summer. = 他们今年夏天会去海滩玩。

虚拟语气:
If only they would come play with us! = 但愿他们会来和我们一起玩！
I wish they would come play more often. = 我希望他们能更经常地来玩。


被动语态:
(The phrase "come play" is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action initiated by the subject.)  A game was arranged to be played; everyone was invited to come and play. =  安排了一场游戏；每个人都被邀请来玩。
The children were encouraged to come play outside. = 孩子们被鼓励到外面去玩。

疑问句:
Will you come play with us? = 你会来和我们一起玩吗？
Why didn't they come play with us? = 为什么他们没有和我们一起玩？

否定句:
They didn't come play today. = 他们今天没来玩。
I won't come play if it rains. = 如果下雨，我就不来玩了。
