hurrays:/həˈreɪz/| n.|欢呼声，喝彩声|Exclamations of joyous approval or celebration.  A plural noun referring to multiple shouts of "hurrah".|例句：The crowd erupted in hurrays as the winning team took the field.  人群在获胜队伍进入赛场时爆发出阵阵欢呼声。

例句：The hurrays for the new president were deafening.  对新总统的欢呼声震耳欲聋。


近义词：cheers, shouts of joy, acclamations, applause


反义词：groans, boos, hisses


词性：名词
