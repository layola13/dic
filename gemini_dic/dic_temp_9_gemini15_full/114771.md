malthusian:/mælˈθuːʒən/| adj.|马尔萨斯式的；马尔萨斯主义的|relating to or denoting the theory that population growth will always tend to outrun the food supply and that unless it is checked by moral restraint or disaster (such as war, famine, or disease) there will be unending poverty and misery.|例句：The Malthusian theory predicts a future of widespread famine and societal collapse. 马尔萨斯理论预测未来将出现大范围饥荒和社会崩溃。

例句：His concerns about overpopulation reflect a Malthusian perspective on resource scarcity. 他对人口过多的担忧反映出一种马尔萨斯式的资源短缺观点。

近义词：overpopulationist

反义词：optimistic (regarding resource availability)
