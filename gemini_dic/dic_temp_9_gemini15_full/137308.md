nontactic: /ˌnɒnˈtæktɪk/ | adj. | 非战术的，非策略的 | Not relating to or involving tactics.  This term is primarily used in military and strategic contexts to describe actions or plans that are not carefully planned or executed to achieve a specific military or political objective.  It often implies a lack of strategic thinking or foresight. | Example: The general criticized the nontactic troop movements, which left them vulnerable to attack.  将军批评了那些非战术性的部队调动，这使得他们容易受到攻击。| 近义词: unstrategic, unplanned, haphazard, improvident | 反义词: tactical, strategic, planned, calculated |
