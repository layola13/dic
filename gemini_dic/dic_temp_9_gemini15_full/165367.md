prejudication:/ˌpriːˈdʒuːdɪkeɪʃən/ | n. | 预断；偏见；先入之见 | The act of prejudging or forming an opinion before having sufficient knowledge; a preconceived judgment or opinion, often based on bias or insufficient information.  | Example:  The judge's prejudication of the case led to a mistrial.  法官对案件的预断导致了审判无效。

The quick prejudication of the defendant's guilt hindered a fair trial. 对被告有罪的快速预断妨碍了公平审判。|近义词：prejudgment, bias, prejudice, preconception |反义词：fair judgment, impartiality, objectivity |名词
