reenlisted:/ˌriːˈɛnlɪstɪd/| v. |重新入伍，再服役 |To re-enlist is to sign up for another term of service in the military after one's previous term has ended.  It implies a voluntary return to military service. |Example: After his first tour in Afghanistan, he reenlisted for another five years.  他第一次阿富汗之旅结束后，又重新入伍服役五年。


Example:  Many soldiers reenlisted after the war ended, wanting to continue serving their country. 许多士兵在战争结束后重新入伍，想要继续为国家效力。

近义词：re-enrol, rejoin, sign up again

反义词：discharge, retire, leave the service
