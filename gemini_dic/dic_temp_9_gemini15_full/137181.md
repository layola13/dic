nonsudsing: /ˈnɑːnsʌdʒɪŋ/ | adj. |不产生泡沫的，不冒泡的| Not producing or forming a lather or foam.  This adjective describes substances, typically cleaning agents, that lack the ability to create suds or bubbles when agitated with water. | Example:  The nonsudsing detergent was effective at cleaning, despite its lack of foam.  = 不产生泡沫的清洁剂尽管没有泡沫，但清洁效果很好。|近义词：foamless, sudsless |反义词：sudsing, foamy
