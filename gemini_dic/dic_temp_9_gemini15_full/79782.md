glutting:/ˈɡlʌtɪŋ/| v.|使充满；使过量；狼吞虎咽|To fill or supply with something to excess.|The market is glutting with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价进口商品。|近义词：oversaturating, inundating, overwhelming |反义词：starving, depriving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is glutting himself with sweets. = 他正在狼吞虎咽地吃糖果。
The company is glutting the market with its new product. = 公司正在用它的新产品充斥市场。

一般过去时:
She glutted herself on chocolate. = 她吃掉了大量的巧克力。
The festival glutted the town with tourists. = 节日使小镇挤满了游客。

现在完成时:
They have glutted the market with cheap goods. = 他们已经用廉价商品充斥了市场。
He has glutted himself on information. = 他已经吸收了过多的信息。

过去完成时:
The farmers had glutted the market before the price dropped. = 在价格下跌之前，农民们已经使市场饱和了。
She had glutted herself on cake before dinner. = 晚饭前她已经吃掉了大量的蛋糕。

将来时:
They will glut the market with their new invention. = 他们将用他们的新发明充斥市场。
I will glut myself on books during my vacation. = 假期期间我将大量阅读书籍。

虚拟语气:
If they glutted the market, the price would drop. = 如果他们使市场饱和，价格就会下降。
I wish I hadn't glutted myself on that spicy food. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多辛辣食物。

被动语态:
The market was glutted with cheap products. = 市场被廉价产品充斥着。
The audience was glutted with information. = 观众被信息淹没了。

疑问句:
Is the market being glutted with similar products? = 市场是否被类似产品充斥着？
Are you glutting yourself with work? = 你是不是工作太多了？

否定句:
We didn't glut the market with our product. = 我们没有用我们的产品充斥市场。
He didn't glut himself on junk food. = 他没有暴饮暴食垃圾食品。
