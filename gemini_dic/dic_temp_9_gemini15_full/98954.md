inter-dominion: /ˌɪntərˈdɒmɪnjən/ | n. | 域间，区域间 | Relating to or affecting relations between different dominions or territories, especially in a political or administrative context.  The term often implies a level of interaction or conflict between these areas. |  The inter-dominion trade agreement fostered economic cooperation.  域间贸易协定促进了经济合作。 The inter-dominion disputes over resource allocation led to political instability.  关于资源分配的域间争端导致了政治不稳定。 | 近义词：inter-regional, transnational | 反义词：intra-dominion, internal | 名词
