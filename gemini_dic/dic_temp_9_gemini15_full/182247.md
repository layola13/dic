remotest:/rɪˈmoʊtɪst/ | adj. | 最远的；最偏僻的；最微小的；最不可能的 | Most distant in space or time; furthest.  Suggesting the smallest or least likely possibility. |例句：The remotest village in the mountains was finally reached.  山区最偏远的村庄终于被到达了。

The remotest possibility of success kept him going. 最微小的成功希望支撑着他继续下去。

|近义词：farthest, furthest, most distant, slightest, most unlikely |反义词：nearest, closest, most likely | 词性:形容词
