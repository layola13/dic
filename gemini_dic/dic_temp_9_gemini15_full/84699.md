handheld:/ˈhændheld/| adj.|手持的，便携式的|Something that is handheld is small and light enough to be held and used easily in one hand.  It is designed for convenient, one-handed operation.|例句：This handheld device is easy to use. 这款手持设备易于使用。|例句：She prefers a handheld vacuum cleaner for quick cleanups. 她更喜欢手持式吸尘器来快速清洁。|近义词：portable, mobile, compact|反义词：stationary, fixed
