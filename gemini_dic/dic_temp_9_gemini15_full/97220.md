inflaters:/ɪnˈfleɪtərz/ | n. |充气机，膨胀物 |A device or substance that inflates something, such as a balloon or tire.  It can also refer to something that causes inflation (in economics).|例句：The mechanic used an inflator to fill the car tire.  机械师用充气机给汽车轮胎充气。|例句：Rising energy prices are inflaters of the economy.  不断上涨的能源价格是经济膨胀的推动力。|近义词：inflater, pump, blower |反义词：deflator
