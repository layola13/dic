privatizations: /ˌpraɪvətaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 私有化（行为）；私营化（过程） | The process of transferring ownership of a business, enterprise, agency, or public service from the public sector to the private sector.  It involves the sale or lease of state-owned assets to private individuals or companies. |

例句：

The recent privatizations of state-owned industries have led to increased competition. 最近国有企业的私有化导致了竞争加剧。

The government's privatization policies have sparked considerable debate. 政府的私有化政策引发了相当大的争议。


近义词：denationalization, commercialization

反义词：nationalization, socialization


词性：名词 (plural of privatization)
