misunderstanders:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændərz/| n. |误解者，理解错误的人|People who misunderstand or have a wrong understanding of something.  Those who fail to grasp the true meaning or intent.|例句：The misunderstanders of the agreement were quick to point out its flaws.  (协议的误解者很快就指出了它的缺陷。)

The misunderstanders failed to see the humor in the situation. (那些误解情况的人没有看出事情中的幽默之处。)

近义词：misinterpreters, those who err in understanding. |反义词：those who understand correctly, experts. |名词
