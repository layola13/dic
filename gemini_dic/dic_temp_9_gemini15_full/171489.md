puggish:/ˈpʌɡɪʃ/| adj.| 顽皮的；像巴哥犬的；粗鲁的；傲慢的 |Pugnacious, impudent, or surly.  The word evokes the image of a pug dog, known for its wrinkled face and sometimes stubborn or grumpy demeanor. |例句：He had a puggish expression on his face. 他脸上带着一种顽皮的表情。

例句：Her puggish refusal to cooperate angered everyone. 她蛮横地拒绝合作激怒了所有人。


近义词：stubborn, surly, impudent, pugnacious, grumpy

反义词：obedient, docile, polite, agreeable


词性:形容词
