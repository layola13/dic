lev:/lev/| n.|杠杆；提升；举起|A lever is a simple machine consisting of a rigid bar resting on a pivot, used to lift or move a heavy weight at one end by applying force to the other.|例句：He used a lever to pry open the lid. 他用杠杆撬开了盖子。|近义词：handle, crank|反义词:depress, lower
