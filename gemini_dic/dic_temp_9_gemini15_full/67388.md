exterminate:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 消灭，根除 | To destroy completely; to get rid of something completely.  |例句：The government launched a campaign to exterminate rats in the city. 政府发起了一场在城市中消灭老鼠的运动。

例句：They tried to exterminate the entire population of the island. 他们试图消灭岛上的全部居民。


近义词：annihilate, eradicate, eliminate, destroy | 反义词：preserve, protect, conserve | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We exterminate pests in our homes regularly. = 我们定期灭杀家里的害虫。
The company exterminates rodents for businesses. = 这家公司为企业灭鼠。


一般过去时:
The farmer exterminated the weeds in his field. = 农民除掉了田里的杂草。
They exterminated the cockroaches with poison. = 他们用毒药消灭了蟑螂。


现在完成时:
They have exterminated most of the insects in the area. = 他们已经消灭了该地区的大部分昆虫。
The government has exterminated a significant number of pests. = 政府已经消灭了大量的害虫。


过去完成时:
The town had exterminated all the rats before the disease spread. = 在疾病蔓延之前，小镇已经消灭了所有老鼠。
Before the new insecticide, they had exterminated pests using traditional methods. = 在新的杀虫剂出现之前，他们用传统方法灭杀害虫。


将来时:
We will exterminate the remaining weeds tomorrow. = 我们明天将消灭剩下的杂草。
The authorities will exterminate the invasive species to protect the ecosystem. = 当局将消灭入侵物种以保护生态系统。


虚拟语气:
If we had exterminated the pests earlier, the damage would have been less. = 如果我们早点消灭害虫，造成的损害就会少一些。
I wish we could exterminate all the mosquitoes. = 我希望我们能够消灭所有蚊子。


被动语态:
The rats were exterminated by the poison. = 老鼠被毒药消灭了。
The invasive plant species is being exterminated by the local authorities. = 当地当局正在消灭入侵植物物种。


疑问句:
Will the pesticide exterminate all the insects? = 这种杀虫剂会消灭所有昆虫吗？
Have they exterminated the termites successfully? = 他们成功地消灭白蚁了吗？


否定句:
We cannot exterminate all the insects without harming beneficial ones. = 我们不能在不伤害益虫的情况下消灭所有昆虫。
The previous attempt to exterminate the weeds failed. = 之前消灭杂草的尝试失败了。


PHRASE->
exterminate pests = 消灭害虫
exterminate insects = 消灭昆虫
exterminate weeds = 除草
exterminate rodents = 灭鼠
exterminate a species = 消灭一个物种

词源:
exterminate comes from the Latin words "ex" (out, from) and "terminare" (to end, to limit).  It literally means "to bring to an end completely".
