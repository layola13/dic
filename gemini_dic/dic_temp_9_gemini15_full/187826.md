roselet:/ˈroʊzlət/ | n. | 小玫瑰；玫瑰花丛 | A small rose; a diminutive rose.  It can also refer to a cluster or small group of roses. | Example:  The garden was filled with delicate roselets of various colors. 花园里盛开着各种颜色娇嫩的小玫瑰。 The hillside was covered in a profusion of roselets. 山坡上覆盖着大量的小玫瑰花丛。 | 近义词：small rose, miniature rose | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a large rose bush or a full-blown rose. | noun
