digitalizations:/ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |数字化；数据化；数字化转型 | The process of converting information into a digital format.  This includes the conversion of analog data, such as text, images, or sound, into a digital representation that can be stored and processed by computers.  Digitalization is transforming industries and impacting various aspects of our lives. |例句：The digitalization of healthcare records has improved patient care.  医疗记录的数字化改善了患者护理。

例句：The company's digitalization strategy aims to increase efficiency and productivity. 该公司的数字化战略旨在提高效率和生产力。


近义词：digitization, computerization, automation |反义词：analogization
