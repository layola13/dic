precharting:/priːˈtʃɑːrtɪŋ/| n.|预先制图，预先图表|The act of creating charts or graphs before an event or process, often to aid in planning or prediction.  It involves anticipating data and visualizing potential outcomes.|例句：The team did precharting of expected sales to better allocate resources.  团队进行了预期销售额的预先制图，以便更好地分配资源。


例句：Precharting the project timeline helped the manager anticipate potential delays. 预先绘制项目时间表帮助经理预料到了潜在的延误。


近义词：forecasting, planning, projecting


反义词：retrospective analysis, post-mortem analysis


词性:名词
