irrespectively:/ˌɪrɪˈspektɪvli/ | adv. | 不论如何；不管；不顾 | Regardless of other factors or considerations.  The decision will be made irrespective of cost.  | The company will proceed irrespective of the shareholders' opinions. 公司将不顾股东意见继续进行。 | regardless of, notwithstanding, notwithstanding, regardless, anyway | dependently, dependently |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He attends the meeting irrespective of the weather. = 不管天气如何，他都参加会议。
We will go ahead irrespective of their objections. = 不管他们反对与否，我们都会继续进行。

一般过去时:
She voted irrespective of party lines. = 她不顾党派立场进行了投票。
They continued the project irrespective of the funding issues. = 不管资金问题，他们继续了项目。

现在完成时:
They have completed the task irrespective of the difficulties. = 不管困难有多大，他们都完成了任务。
We have supported them irrespective of their background. = 不管他们的背景如何，我们都支持他们。

过去完成时:
They had already made the decision irrespective of the new information. = 不管新的信息如何，他们已经做出了决定。
She had finished the work irrespective of the late hour. = 不管时间已经很晚了，她已经完成了工作。

将来时:
We will proceed irrespective of the outcome. = 不管结果如何，我们都会继续进行。
They will continue the research irrespective of the funding cuts. = 不管经费削减与否，他们都会继续研究。


虚拟语气:
Even if the situation were different, we would proceed irrespective of the risks. = 即使情况不同，我们也会不顾风险继续进行。
I would have helped him irrespective of his past mistakes, had I known. = 如果我知道的话，我会不顾他过去的错误帮助他。


被动语态:
The decision was made irrespective of public opinion. = 决定是不顾公众舆论做出的。
The project will be completed irrespective of the delays. = 项目将不顾延误而完成。


疑问句:
Will they continue the project irrespective of the cost? = 不管成本多少，他们会继续这个项目吗？
Should we proceed irrespective of the potential risks? = 我们应该不顾潜在的风险继续进行吗？


否定句:
They did not complete the task irrespective of the pressure. = 他们并没有不顾压力完成任务。
The decision was not made irrespective of the evidence. = 决定并非不顾证据做出的。
