fanner:/ˈfænər/| n.|煽动者；扇子；（尤指谷物）播种机|A person who fans something, especially a person who stirs up trouble or excitement.  Also, a device used for winnowing grain or spreading seed.|例句：He's a fanner of discontent, always stirring up trouble. 他是个煽动不满的人，总是挑起事端。

例句：The farmer used a fanner to spread the seeds evenly across the field.  农民用播种机将种子均匀地撒在田里。|近义词：agitator, inciter, instigator|反义词：peacemaker, conciliator|词性:名词
