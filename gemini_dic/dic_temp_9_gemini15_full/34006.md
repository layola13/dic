cited:/ˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被引用的；被提及的 |  Mentioned or referred to as an example, support, or proof.  | Example:  The cited study showed a strong correlation.  (被引用的研究显示出很强的相关性。)  The article cited several experts in the field. (这篇文章引用了几位该领域的专家。) |  quoted, mentioned, referenced | uncited |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article cites several experts. = 这篇文章引用了几位专家。
This study is cited frequently. = 这项研究经常被引用。


一般过去时:
The professor cited a relevant case study. = 教授引用了一个相关的案例研究。
They cited several sources in their report. = 他们在报告中引用了几个来源。


现在完成时:
This theory has been cited many times. = 这个理论已经被引用多次。
The author has cited this work extensively. = 作者广泛引用了这项工作。


过去完成时:
The lawyer had cited previous legal precedent. = 律师先前已引用过先前的法律先例。
They had cited the same article in their earlier publications. = 他们在早期的出版物中引用了同一篇文章。


将来时:
She will cite this research in her upcoming presentation. = 她将在即将到来的演讲中引用这项研究。
The researcher will cite relevant studies to support his argument. = 研究人员将引用相关的研究来支持他的论点。


虚拟语气:
If I were to write this paper, I would cite these sources. = 如果我要写这篇论文，我会引用这些来源。
I wish I had cited all the necessary sources. = 我希望我已经引用了所有必要的来源。


被动语态:
The article was cited in numerous publications. = 这篇文章在许多出版物中被引用。
This research has been widely cited in the field. = 这项研究在该领域被广泛引用。


疑问句:
Was this source properly cited? = 这个来源被正确引用了吗？
Are all the citations accurate? = 所有引用都准确吗？


否定句:
The student did not cite his sources correctly. = 学生没有正确引用他的来源。
The paper failed to cite relevant research. = 这篇论文未能引用相关的研究。


PHRASE->
frequently cited = 经常被引用
properly cited = 正确引用
highly cited = 高被引
well-cited = 引用充分的
poorly cited = 引用不足的
