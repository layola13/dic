juried:/ˈdʒʊəriːd/| adj.|评审的；经评审的；|judged or selected by a jury or panel of judges.  It often refers to art competitions or exhibitions where works are chosen for inclusion based on the judgment of experts.|例句：The juried art show featured only the best works submitted.  这场评审的艺术展只展出了提交的最佳作品。

例句：Only juried entries will be considered for the prize. 只有经过评审的参赛作品才会被考虑获奖。


近义词：judged, selected, reviewed, screened

反义词：unjudged, unselected


词性：形容词
