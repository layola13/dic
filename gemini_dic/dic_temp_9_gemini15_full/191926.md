scoopers:/ˈskuːpərz/| n.|勺子；铲子；新闻记者；（尤指冰淇淋）盛勺| a person or thing that scoops.  A scoop is a tool used for scooping.  In journalism, a scoop is an important piece of news obtained before other journalists.|例句：He used a scoop to serve the ice cream. 他用勺子盛冰淇淋。|近义词：spoon, shovel, dipper, journalist|反义词：N/A

She scoops the snow from the driveway. = 她用铲子铲走车道上的雪。
