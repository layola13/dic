shrivel:/ˈʃrɪvəl/| v.|枯萎；皱缩|To become wrinkled and dry, especially due to heat, cold, or age; to cause something to do this.  |例句：The leaves shrivelled in the intense heat. 叶子在酷暑中枯萎了。|例句：The fruit began to shrivel after being left out in the sun. 水果放在太阳下后开始干瘪。|近义词：wither, wrinkle, shrink|反义词：flourish, expand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The grapes shrivel in the sun. = 葡萄在阳光下枯萎。
The skin shrivels with age. = 皮肤随着年龄增长而起皱。


一般过去时:
The plant shrivelled after the drought. = 干旱过后，植物枯萎了。
The apple shrivelled in my lunchbox. = 我的午餐盒里的苹果干瘪了。


现在完成时:
The leaves have shrivelled due to lack of water. = 由于缺水，叶子已经枯萎了。
The fruit has shrivelled and is no longer edible. = 水果已经干瘪了，不能再吃了。


过去完成时:
The flowers had shrivelled before I could water them. = 我还没来得及浇水，花儿就已经枯萎了。
The leaves had shrivelled completely by the end of summer. = 到夏天结束时，叶子已经完全枯萎了。


将来时:
The fruit will shrivel if left in the sun. = 如果放在太阳下，水果会干瘪。
The plants will shrivel without regular watering. = 没有定期浇水，植物会枯萎。


虚拟语气:
If the plant had been watered, it wouldn't have shrivelled. = 如果植物被浇过水，它就不会枯萎了。
I wish my skin wouldn't shrivel so much as I get older. = 我希望我的皮肤不会随着年龄增长而皱得那么多。


被动语态:
The grapes were shrivelled by the intense heat. = 葡萄被酷暑烤干了。
The fruit was shrivelled before it could be harvested. = 水果在收割之前就干瘪了。


疑问句:
Have the leaves shrivelled already? = 叶子已经枯萎了吗？
Will the flowers shrivel in this cold weather? = 这些花会在这么冷的天气里枯萎吗？


否定句:
The leaves didn't shrivel because it rained. = 叶子没有枯萎，因为下雨了。
The fruit didn't shrivel because it was kept in the refrigerator. = 水果没有干瘪，因为它被放在冰箱里了。
