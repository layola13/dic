rebakes:/ˌriːˈbeɪks/ | v. | 再烘烤；重新烘焙 | To bake (something) again. This usually implies improving the original baking or correcting a flaw.  |  例句：I had to rebake the cake because it was undercooked. 我不得不重新烘烤蛋糕，因为它没烤熟。

例句：The bread was slightly burnt, so I rebaked it at a lower temperature. 面包有点烤焦了，所以我用较低的温度重新烘烤了一下。


近义词：reheat, bake again | 反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of 'discard' or 'throw away'. | 动词
