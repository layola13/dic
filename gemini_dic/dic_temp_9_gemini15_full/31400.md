chargehand:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒhænd/ | n. |工头，领班|A chargehand is a worker who is in charge of a small group of other workers.  They are responsible for overseeing their work, ensuring it is done correctly and efficiently, and reporting to a higher supervisor.|The chargehand instructed the team to start working on the new section.|foreman, supervisor, team leader|subordinate, worker|名词
