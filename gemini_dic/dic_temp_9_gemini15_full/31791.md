checking-upon: /ˈtʃɛkɪŋ əˈpɑːn/ | v. | 检查；察看；探望 | To make sure something is correct, in good order, or is making progress as expected; to visit someone unexpectedly or briefly. | 例句：I'm checking upon the progress of the project. 我正在检查项目的进度。 I'm checking upon my grandmother this afternoon. 我今天下午要去看望我的祖母。 | 近义词：monitoring, overseeing, inspecting, visiting | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting
