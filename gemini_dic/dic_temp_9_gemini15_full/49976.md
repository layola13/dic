denigrators:/ˈdɛnɪɡeɪtərz/| n.|贬低者，诋毁者|Those who belittle or speak ill of someone or something.  People who denigrate often aim to diminish the reputation or importance of their target.|例句：The denigrators of his work failed to acknowledge his significant contributions to the field.  诋毁他作品的人未能承认他对该领域的重大贡献。

例句：His political opponents were relentless denigrators, constantly attacking his character and policies. 他的政治对手是不遗余力地贬低他，不断攻击他的品格和政策。


近义词：critics, detractors, belittlers, disparagers

反义词：praisers, supporters, advocates, champions

词性: 名词
