phasel:/ˈfeɪz/| n. |阶段；方面；相位|a distinct period or stage in a process of development or growth.|The project is in its initial phase.  这个项目处于初期阶段。|stage, step, period |conclusion, end|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This project is in its planning phase. = 这个项目正处于规划阶段。
The moon goes through different phases. = 月亮经历不同的月相。


一般过去时:
The first phase of the construction was completed last month. = 上个月完成了施工的第一阶段。
We went through several phases of grief after losing our pet. = 失去宠物后，我们经历了几个悲伤的阶段。


现在完成时:
They have completed the first phase of the experiment. = 他们已经完成了实验的第一阶段。
We have been through many phases in our relationship. = 在我们的关系中，我们经历了许多阶段。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the initial phase had already ended. = 当我们到达时，初始阶段已经结束了。
They had already gone through several phases of negotiations before reaching an agreement. = 在达成协议之前，他们已经进行了好几轮谈判。


将来时:
The next phase will begin next week. = 下一阶段将于下周开始。
We will be entering a new phase of our lives soon. = 我们很快将进入人生的新阶段。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we could have completed all phases of the project. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们就能完成项目的各个阶段。
I wish we had foreseen the challenges in the later phases. = 我希望我们能预见到后期阶段的挑战。


被动语态:
The different phases of the research were carefully documented. = 研究的不同阶段都被仔细记录下来。
The final phase is being planned at the moment. = 最终阶段目前正在规划中。


疑问句:
What phase of the project are we in now? = 我们现在处于项目的哪个阶段？
What are the different phases involved in this process? = 这个过程中涉及哪些不同的阶段？


否定句:
The project hasn't reached its final phase yet. = 项目尚未进入最终阶段。
We didn't anticipate so many problems in the early phases. = 我们没有预料到早期阶段会有这么多问题。


PHRASE->

out of phase = 不同步的；不协调的
lunar phase = 月相
phase transition = 相变
