resides:/rɪˈzaɪdz/| v.|居住，存在，在于|To live permanently in a place.  Also, to exist or be present in something.|例句：He resides in a small village in the countryside. 他住在乡下一个小村庄里。

例句：The problem resides in a lack of communication.  问题在于缺乏沟通。


近义词：lives, dwells, inhabits, exists

反义词：leaves, departs, vacates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resides in London. = 他住在伦敦。
She resides at 123 Main Street. = 她住在主街123号。


一般过去时:
They resided in Paris for five years. = 他们在巴黎住了五年。
My grandparents resided in that house for decades. = 我的祖父母在那所房子里住了几十年。


现在完成时:
She has resided in New York since 2010. = 她自2010年以来一直住在纽约。
He has resided in many countries throughout his career. = 他职业生涯中居住过许多国家。


过去完成时:
Before moving here, he had resided in California. = 在搬到这里之前，他住在加利福尼亚。
They had resided in that apartment for two years before it was sold. = 那套公寓卖掉之前，他们在那里住了两年。


将来时:
He will reside in a new apartment next month. = 他下个月将住进一套新公寓。
They will reside in the city for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，他们将居住在这个城市。


虚拟语气:
If he resided closer, he would visit more often. = 如果他住得近些，他会更经常来访。
I wish I resided in a warmer climate. = 我希望我住在气候更温暖的地方。


被动语态:
The power resides in the people. = 权力掌握在人民手中。(此处被动语态较少用)
The responsibility resides with the management team. = 责任在于管理团队。(此处被动语态较少用)


疑问句:
Where do you reside? = 你住在哪里？
Does he reside in the city or the suburbs? = 他住在城里还是郊区？


否定句:
He does not reside in this country. = 他不住在这个国家。
They did not reside in that house for long. = 他们在那所房子里没住多久。


PHRASE->
reside in = 住在...
reside with = 与...同住；归属于...
reside at = 住在（特定地址）
reside among = 居住在...之中
reside within = 存在于...之中
