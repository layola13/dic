coopers:/ˈkuːpərz/| n.|桶匠；制桶工人；(Cooper's)人名|A cooper is a person who makes or repairs barrels, casks, and other wooden containers.  They are skilled craftspeople who use specialized tools and techniques to create these vessels. | The cooper carefully inspected the barrel for leaks before filling it with wine.  桶匠在往酒桶里灌酒之前仔细检查是否有漏。  | barrel maker, cask maker |  (无明确反义词，可考虑与现代工业化制桶方式相对)| 名词
