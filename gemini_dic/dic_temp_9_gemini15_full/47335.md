de-seeds:/diːˈsiːdz/ | vt. |去籽；去除种子 | To remove seeds from something. |例句：She de-seeds the tomatoes before making the sauce. 她做酱汁之前先把西红柿去籽。 |例句：The machine efficiently de-seeds the grapes for wine production. 这台机器高效地去除酿酒葡萄的籽。 |近义词：pit, core, hull |反义词：seed
