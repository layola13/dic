nondelegations:/ˌnɒnˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. |不可转让；非委任；授权的限制|The inability to delegate a specific task or responsibility.  This often arises from legal restrictions, contractual obligations, or the inherent nature of the task.  For example, a captain's ultimate responsibility for the safety of a ship cannot be delegated.|例句：The regulations imposed strict nondelegations on the board of directors regarding financial matters.  该规章对董事会关于财务事项的权力作出了严格的不可转让规定。

The company's policy of nondelegations ensured that only senior management could approve major acquisitions. 公司的不可转让政策确保只有高级管理层才能批准重大收购。|近义词：intransferability, restriction, limitation |反义词：delegation, authorization, empowerment
