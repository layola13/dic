pseudo-entity:/ˈsuːdoʊˈɛntɪti/|/ˈsjuːdəʊˈɛntɪti/| n. |伪实体；假实体 |A pseudo-entity is something that appears to be a distinct entity or object but lacks genuine independent existence or substance. It might be a concept, a label, or a construct that is useful for certain purposes but doesn't correspond to a real, objective thing in the world. For example, in software engineering, a pseudo-entity might be a data structure that represents a complex relationship between several actual entities.  |The marketing department created a pseudo-entity called "brand loyalty" to track customer behavior. |近义词：artifact, construct, abstraction |反义词：entity, reality
