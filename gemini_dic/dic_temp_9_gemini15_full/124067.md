morphology:/mɔrˈfɑːlədʒi/ | n. |形态学，词法；形态|The study of the forms and structures of things.  It can refer to the forms of words (linguistic morphology) or the forms of organisms (biological morphology).|例句：The morphology of the plant is unique to its species.  这植物的形态特征是其物种独有的。|例句：Linguistic morphology studies how words are formed and how their forms relate to their meanings.  语言形态学研究词语是如何构成的以及它们的形态与意义之间的关系。|近义词：structure, form, shape |反义词：无
