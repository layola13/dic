piece:/piːs/| n.|一片；一件；作品；乐曲|a portion or fragment of something larger; a single item or unit; a work of art, music, or literature.|例句：He ate a piece of cake. 他吃了一块蛋糕。  He wrote a beautiful piece of music. 他创作了一首优美的乐曲。|近义词：part, portion, section, item, work|反义词：whole


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This puzzle has 1000 pieces. = 这个拼图有1000片。
She needs a new piece of clothing. = 她需要一件新衣服。

一般过去时:
He broke a piece of glass. = 他打破了一块玻璃。
They played a piece of music by Mozart. = 他们演奏了一首莫扎特的乐曲。

现在完成时:
I've finished the last piece of my homework. = 我完成了最后一件作业。
She has written several pieces of poetry. = 她写了几首诗歌。

过去完成时:
He had already eaten a piece of the pie before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经吃掉了一块馅饼。
They had lost a valuable piece of jewelry. = 他们丢失了一件贵重的珠宝。

将来时:
I will cut a piece of the bread. = 我会切一块面包。
She will write a piece about climate change. = 她将会写一篇关于气候变化的文章。

虚拟语气:
If I had another piece of cake, I would eat it. = 如果我还有另一块蛋糕，我会把它吃掉。
I wish I had a piece of mind. = 我希望我能安心。

被动语态:
The cake was cut into several pieces. = 蛋糕被切成了几块。
A new piece of legislation is being drafted. = 一项新的立法正在起草中。

疑问句:
How many pieces are there? = 有多少块？
Do you have another piece of information? = 你还有其他信息吗？

否定句:
I don't have a spare piece of paper. = 我没有多余的纸。
He didn't want another piece of advice. = 他不想再听任何建议。
