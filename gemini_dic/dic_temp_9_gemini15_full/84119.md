half-lunatic: /ˈhɑːf ˈluːnətɪk/ | adj. | 半疯的，有点疯癫的 |  Describes someone who exhibits some, but not all, of the characteristics of a lunatic; slightly mad or eccentric.  The term implies a less severe form of madness than "lunatic." | 例句：He was acting rather half-lunatic, running around the garden shouting at squirrels. = 他行为有点疯疯癫癫的，在花园里跑来跑去冲着松鼠大喊大叫。

He's a bit of a half-lunatic, always coming up with bizarre schemes. = 他有点疯疯癫癫的，总是想出一些古怪的计划。

| 近义词：slightly mad, eccentric, whimsical, unbalanced, erratic | 反义词：sane, rational, normal, composed, balanced | 词性: adjective
