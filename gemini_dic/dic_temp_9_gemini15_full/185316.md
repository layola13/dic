retributer:/rɪˈtrɪbjuːtər/ | n. | 报应者；惩罚者 | One who inflicts retribution or punishment.  A person who gives punishment or revenge for wrongdoing. | Example: The vengeful retributer sought justice for the crimes committed against his family. 报复心强的惩罚者为针对其家族犯下的罪行寻求正义。|近义词：avenger, punisher, chastiser | 反义词：forgiver, pardoner
