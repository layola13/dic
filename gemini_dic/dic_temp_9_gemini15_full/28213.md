caproate:/ˈkæproʊeɪt/| n. |己酸酯|Caproate is a salt or ester of caproic acid.  It is a six-carbon fatty acid, often found in various natural oils and fats.  The term can refer to the general class of caproates or to specific caproate esters.|The aroma of caproate esters can be described as cheesy, goaty, or waxy, depending on the specific ester.|  Ethyl caproate, for example, contributes to the aroma of certain cheeses.|近义词：hexyl hexanoate (for specific esters)|反义词：无
