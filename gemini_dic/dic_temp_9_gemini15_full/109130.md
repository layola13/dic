legionnaire:/ˈliːdʒənɛər/| n. |军团士兵；患有军团病的人 | A member of a legion, especially a Roman legion.  Also, a person suffering from Legionnaires' disease. |例句：He was a legionnaire in the Roman army. 他是罗马军队中的一名士兵。

例句：She was diagnosed with Legionnaires' disease after a stay at the hotel.  她在酒店住过之后被诊断出患有军团菌病。


近义词：soldier, veteran (for the military meaning); patient (for the disease meaning)
反义词：civilian (for the military meaning); healthy (for the disease meaning)


词源:  From Late Latin *legionarius, from legio "legion". The connection to Legionnaires' disease comes from an outbreak traced back to a legion of American veterans in 1976.
