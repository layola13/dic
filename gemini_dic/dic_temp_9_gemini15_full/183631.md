rereading:/ˌriːˈriːdɪŋ/| n.|重读；再读|The act of reading something again.|例句：The rereading of the contract revealed a crucial clause we'd missed. 重读合同揭示了我们错过的关键条款。|近义词：reviewing, re-perusal|反义词：first reading


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am rereading the book. = 我正在重读这本书。
He rereads the email every day. = 他每天都重读这封邮件。


一般过去时:
I reread the chapter last night. = 我昨晚重读了那一章。
She reread the letter several times. = 她重读了那封信好几次。


现在完成时:
I have reread this article three times. = 我已经重读这篇文章三次了。
She has reread the entire novel. = 她已经重读了整部小说。


过去完成时:
I had reread the instructions before I started. = 我在开始之前已经重读了说明书。
They had reread the proposal before submitting it. = 他们在提交提案之前已经重读了它。


将来时:
I will reread the book next week. = 我下周将重读这本书。
She will reread the letter before replying. = 她将在回复之前重读这封信。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would reread your manuscript. = 如果我有时间，我会重读你的手稿。
I wish I had reread the email more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地重读过那封邮件。


被动语态:
The document was reread by the manager. = 文件由经理重读了。
The instructions are being reread by the employees. = 说明书正在被员工重读。


疑问句:
Have you reread the report yet? = 你重读报告了吗？
Will he reread the contract before signing? = 他会在签字之前重读合同吗？


否定句:
I did not reread the article. = 我没有重读那篇文章。
She will not reread the book again. = 她不会再重读这本书了。


PHRASE->
rereading for comprehension = 为理解而重读
careful rereading = 仔细重读
rereading and annotating = 重读并做注释
a rereading of the text = 对文本的重读
critical rereading = 批判性重读
