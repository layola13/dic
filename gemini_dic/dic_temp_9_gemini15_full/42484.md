counterforces: /ˈkaʊntərˌfɔːrsɪz/ | n. | 反作用力；对抗力量 | Forces that oppose or counteract other forces.  They can be physical, political, social, or ideological.  The term often implies a struggle or conflict between opposing forces. |

例句：

The government deployed counterforces to quell the rebellion.  政府部署了对抗力量来平息叛乱。

Economic sanctions were used as a counterforce to the aggressor nation's actions.  经济制裁被用作对抗侵略国行动的反制力量。


近义词：opposition, resistance, countermeasures, countervailing forces


反义词：reinforcement, support, assistance


词性：名词
