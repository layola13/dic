consciencelessly: /ˌkɒnʃiˈensləsli/ | adv. | 没有人性的；不道德地；无情地 | Acting without a conscience; showing a complete lack of moral principles.  | 例句：He acted consciencelessly, stealing from his own family to fund his gambling habit. 他行为不道德，偷窃自己的家庭来资助他的赌博习惯。

例句：The company consciencelessly exploited its workers, paying them minimal wages and providing unsafe working conditions.  这家公司无情地剥削工人，付给他们最低工资并提供不安全的工作条件。


近义词：ruthlessly, mercilessly, unfeelingly, remorselessly, heartlessly


反义词：conscientiously, scrupulously, ethically, morally
