overharvesting: /ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrvɪstɪŋ/ | n. |过度捕捞；过度采伐 | The act of harvesting a resource at a rate that exceeds its ability to replenish itself, leading to depletion or extinction.  It applies to both living resources (fish, trees, etc.) and non-living resources (minerals, etc.).  | 例句：Overharvesting of fish stocks has led to a decline in many fish populations.  过度捕捞导致许多鱼类数量下降。

Overharvesting of forests has contributed to deforestation and soil erosion. 过度采伐森林导致了森林砍伐和水土流失。| 近义词： depletion, exploitation, overexploitation | 反义词： conservation, sustainable harvesting, replenishment |  词性: 名词
