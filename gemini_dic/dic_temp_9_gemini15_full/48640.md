defendress:/dɪˈfɛndrɛs/| n.|女辩护人，女保护者|A woman who defends someone or something.  The term is now largely archaic and considered somewhat sexist, often replaced by "defender" or more specific terms.|例句：She acted as the defendress of the accused. 她担任被告的辩护人。|例句：The lioness was a fierce defendress of her cubs. 母狮凶猛地保护着她的幼崽。|近义词：defender, protector, advocate|反义词：attacker, assailant
