pre-evolutionary: /ˌpriːˌɛvəˈluːʃənəri/ | adj. |进化前的；在进化之前的|  Describing something that existed or occurred before the process of biological evolution as we understand it, often referring to the period before the emergence of life or before significant evolutionary changes.  It can also refer to concepts or theories that predate the modern understanding of evolution. |

例句：

The pre-evolutionary Earth was a vastly different place. = 进化前的地球是一个截然不同的地方。

Some scientists study pre-evolutionary chemical processes that may have led to life. = 一些科学家研究进化前的化学过程，这些过程可能导致了生命的产生。


近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding, earlier

反义词：post-evolutionary, subsequent, following


词性：形容词
