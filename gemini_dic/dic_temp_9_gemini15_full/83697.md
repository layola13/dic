hagglers:/ˈhæɡlərz/| n.|讨价还价者，议价者|People who argue about prices, especially in a market or bazaar.  They try to get the best possible price by negotiating and bargaining.|例句：The hagglers in the marketplace were trying to get the best price for their carpets.集市上的讨价还价者试图为他们的地毯争取最好的价格。

The tourists were easily out-haggled by the experienced hagglers. 经验丰富的讨价还价者很容易就把游客的价钱压下来了。|近义词：bargainers, negotiators, traders|反义词：buyers (who readily accept prices)
