kneebrush: /ˈniːbrʌʃ/ | n. |膝盖刷；擦膝盖的刷子| A brush used to clean or brush knees, typically used in cleaning or grooming.  This word is less common and may be considered a compound word. |例句：The stable hand used a kneebrush to clean the mud off the horse's knees.  马夫用膝盖刷擦掉马膝盖上的泥巴。


近义词：knee cleaner (less common)


反义词：无
