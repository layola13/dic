nihility:/naɪˈɪləti/| n. |虚无；不存在；无效|The state or quality of being nonexistent or of having no value or importance; nothingness.  Nihility is a philosophical concept referring to the ultimate meaninglessness or emptiness of existence. It often explores themes of despair, absurdity, and the lack of inherent value in the universe. |例句：The vastness of space emphasized the insignificance and nihility of human existence.  宇宙的浩瀚突显了人类存在的不重要性和虚无。

The novel explores the theme of nihility, depicting characters grappling with the meaninglessness of their lives. 这部小说探讨了虚无的主题，描绘了人物在与人生的毫无意义作斗争。|近义词：nothingness, void, emptiness, nullity |反义词：existence, reality, significance, meaning
