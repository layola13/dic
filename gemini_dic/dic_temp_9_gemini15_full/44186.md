croaked:/kroʊkt/| /krəʊkt/ | vt. vi. n.| 呱呱叫；嘶哑地说；(嗓音沙哑地)说；死亡|to make a deep rough sound like a frog; to speak in a low, rough voice, especially because of a sore throat; the sound of a frog or a similar sound|例句：The frog croaked loudly in the pond.青蛙在池塘里大声呱呱叫。 The old man croaked his last words. 老人用嘶哑的声音说了最后一句话。|近义词：cackle, croak, rasp,  |反义词：sing, whisper.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The frog croaks all night. = 青蛙整夜呱呱叫。
He croaks when he speaks. = 他说话声音嘶哑。


一般过去时:
The raven croaked ominously. = 乌鸦不祥地呱呱叫。
She croaked out a reply. = 她嘶哑地回答。


现在完成时:
The frog has croaked all night. = 青蛙已经叫了一整夜了。
He has croaked since he caught a cold. = 自从他感冒后，他的声音就一直嘶哑。


过去完成时:
The frog had croaked all night before it went silent. = 在它停止叫声之前，青蛙已经叫了一整夜了。
She had croaked so much that her throat was sore. = 她已经嘶哑地说了很多话，嗓子都疼了。


将来时:
The frog will croak again at sunset. = 日落时，青蛙还会再叫。
He will probably croak if he doesn't get treatment for his throat. = 如果他不治疗喉咙，他可能会声音嘶哑。


虚拟语气:
If he had drunk less, he wouldn't have croaked so badly. = 如果他少喝点酒，就不会声音这么嘶哑了。
I wish I hadn't croaked so loudly yesterday. = 我真希望昨天我没有大声喊叫。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His last words were croaked out weakly. = 他的遗言无力地被嘶哑地说出。
(This is unusual;  "croaked" is generally used actively.)


疑问句:
Did the frog croak? = 青蛙叫了吗？
Does he often croak when he's tired? = 他累的时候经常声音嘶哑吗？


否定句:
The frog didn't croak last night. = 青蛙昨晚没叫。
He didn't croak, he whispered. = 他没有嘶哑地说，而是低声说。


PHRASE->
croak out = 嘶哑地说出
croak up = (嗓音沙哑地)开始说话
die a croaker = 咽气（俚语）
to croak something out = 勉强说出
croaking death = 死亡的低沉声音


词源:
The word "croak" ultimately derives from the Old French word "croquer," meaning "to crackle, crunch," and possibly onomatopoeic in origin, mimicking the sound of a frog's call.  The meaning shifted over time to refer specifically to the sound made by frogs and later to a raspy human voice.
