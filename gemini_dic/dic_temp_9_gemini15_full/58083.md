drooper:/ˈdruːpər/| n. |萎靡不振的人；垂头丧气的人；（植物的）下垂部分|A person who is depressed or dejected; something that droops.  |例句：After the loss, he was a real drooper. 比赛失利后，他变得非常萎靡不振。|例句：The droopers of the wilting flower needed water. 枯萎的花朵下垂的部分需要浇水。|近义词：dejected person, downcast individual, wilted part|反义词：energetic person, lively individual, upright part|
