nondepravation:/ˌnɒnˌdeprəˈveɪʃən/ | n. |  非剥夺；非匮乏 | The state or condition of not being deprived or lacking something essential.  It often refers to studies or experiments where subjects are not deprived of essential resources or stimuli. | Example: The study used a nondepravation control group to compare against the effects of sleep deprivation.  该研究使用了非剥夺对照组来比较睡眠剥夺的影响。 | 近义词：absence of deprivation, lack of deprivation | 反义词：deprivation, lack, want
