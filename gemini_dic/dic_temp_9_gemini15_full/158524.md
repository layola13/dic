pleochromatic:/ˌpliːoʊkroʊˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 多色的，多色调的 | Exhibiting or characterized by a variety of colors; multicolored.  This term often refers to minerals or gemstones that show different colors depending on the direction of light. | Example: The pleochroic tourmaline showed different colors depending on the angle of view.  该多色性电气石根据视角的不同呈现不同的颜色。|近义词：multicolored, polychromatic, iridescent | 反义词：monochromatic
