milled:/mɪld/| v. adj. |碾磨的；加工过的；充满的|To grind or cut into small pieces; to process (grain, etc.) in a mill;  having been milled.|例句：The wheat was milled into flour. 小麦被磨成面粉。The fabric has a milled finish. 这织物具有磨光处理的表面。|近义词：ground, pulverized, processed |反义词：unprocessed, whole


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mill the rice every day. = 他们每天碾米。
The factory mills wheat into flour. = 工厂把小麦磨成面粉。

一般过去时:
The farmer milled the corn last week. = 农民上周磨了玉米。
They milled the grain before transporting it. = 他们在运输谷物之前先加工了它。

现在完成时:
They have milled all the wheat. = 他们已经把所有的小麦都磨了。
The company has milled millions of tons of grain. = 这家公司已经加工了数百万吨的谷物。

过去完成时:
They had milled the rice before the festival. = 他们在节日之前已经把米磨好了。
The miller had milled the flour before the bakery opened. = 磨坊主在面包店开门之前已经磨好了面粉。


将来时:
They will mill the oats tomorrow. = 他们明天将碾磨燕麦。
The mill will mill the barley next week. = 磨坊下周将磨大麦。

虚拟语气:
If they milled the grain properly, the flour would be better. = 如果他们正确地磨制谷物，面粉就会更好。
I wish they had milled the corn more finely. = 我希望他们把玉米磨得更细一些。

被动语态:
The wheat is milled into flour. = 小麦被磨成面粉。
The grain was milled before being shipped. = 谷物在运输前被磨制。

疑问句:
Is the rice milled yet? = 米磨好了吗？
Has the grain been milled? = 谷物磨制好了吗？

否定句:
They didn't mill the corn yesterday. = 他们昨天没有磨玉米。
The wheat hasn't been milled yet. = 小麦还没有被磨制。
