gunocracy:/ɡʌˈnɒkrəsi/| n. |枪杆子统治；军政府；军阀统治 |A system of government where power is held by the military or other armed groups.  It implies rule by force and often a lack of democratic processes.  |Example: The country descended into gunocracy after the coup d'état.  这个国家在政变后陷入了枪杆子统治。|近义词：military dictatorship, autocracy, tyranny |反义词：democracy, republic
