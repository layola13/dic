envisages:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒɪz/ | vt. | 预想；设想；想象 | To form a mental picture of (something not yet existing or experienced); imagine.  | The architect envisages a building that will be both functional and aesthetically pleasing. 建筑师设想一座既实用又美观的建筑。

The company envisages a significant increase in profits next year. 公司预计明年利润将大幅增长。 | foresee, contemplate, imagine |  |  verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He envisages a bright future for his children. = 他设想他的孩子们拥有光明的未来。
She envisages a successful career in medicine. = 她设想自己在医学领域取得成功。


一般过去时:
They envisaged a different outcome to the project. = 他们预想的是项目会有不同的结果。
The planners envisaged a city with green spaces and efficient transport. = 规划者们设想了一个拥有绿色空间和高效交通的城市。


现在完成时:
We have envisaged many scenarios for the upcoming election. = 我们已经设想了即将到来的选举的许多情景。
She has envisaged this problem for some time. = 她已经设想过这个问题一段时间了。


过去完成时:
They had envisaged a much simpler solution before encountering the unexpected challenges. = 在遇到意想不到的挑战之前，他们设想了一个更简单的解决方案。
I had envisaged a quiet evening at home, but the neighbours had a party. = 我原本设想在家安静地度过一个晚上，但邻居们却举办了派对。


将来时:
The government will envisage new policies to address climate change. = 政府将设想新的政策来应对气候变化。
Scientists will envisage a future where energy is clean and sustainable. = 科学家们将设想一个能源清洁和可持续的未来。


虚拟语气:
If I envisaged a successful outcome, I would have invested more. = 如果我预见到成功的结果，我会投入更多。
I wish I had envisaged the potential difficulties before starting the project. = 我希望我在开始项目之前就预见到潜在的困难。


被动语态:
A bright future was envisaged for the company by the investors. = 投资者们为这家公司设想了一个光明的未来。
A new strategy for the marketing campaign was envisaged by the team. = 该团队为营销活动设想了一个新的策略。


疑问句:
What future do you envisage for yourself? = 你为自己设想了怎样的未来？
What kind of society do they envisage for the future? = 他们设想未来是什么样的社会？


否定句:
I didn't envisage such a negative response to my proposal. = 我没有预料到我的提议会得到如此负面的回应。
They didn't envisage the complications that would arise. = 他们没有预料到将会出现的复杂情况。


PHRASE->

envisage a scenario = 设想一种情景
envisage the possibilities = 设想可能性
envisage the consequences = 设想后果
envisage a solution = 设想一个解决方案
