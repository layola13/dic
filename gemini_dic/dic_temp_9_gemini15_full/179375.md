redressible:/rɪˈdrɛsəbl/ | adj. | 可补救的；可矫正的 | Capable of being remedied or corrected.  Something that is redressible can be fixed, improved, or made right. | The damage to the car is redressible with some body work. 这辆车的损坏可以通过一些钣金修理来修复。  The situation, while serious, is still redressible.  虽然情况严重，但仍然是可以补救的。 | remediable, correctable, rectifiable | irremediable, irreparable
