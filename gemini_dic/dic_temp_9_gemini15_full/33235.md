chronographs:/ˈkrɒnəɡrɑːfs/| n. |计时器，计时码表|A chronograph is a watch or other device that includes a stopwatch function, typically with multiple subdials to measure elapsed time, and often featuring other complications like a calendar or a tachymeter.|例句：His chronograph precisely measured the runner's finishing time. 他的计时码表精确地测量了跑步者的完成时间。
例句：The diver used his chronograph to monitor his ascent.  潜水员用他的计时码表监测他的上升过程。|近义词：stopwatch, timer|反义词：None specifically.  |名词
