presweeten:/ˌpriːˈswiːtn/| v.|预先加糖，事先加糖 | To add sugar to something before it is used or consumed.  This is typically done to enhance the sweetness or to mask unpleasant flavors. |例句：The cereal is presweetened, so you don't need to add any extra sugar.  这麦片是预先加糖的，所以你不需要额外加糖。

例句：The company presweetened the yogurt to make it more appealing to children. 公司预先给酸奶加糖，以使其对儿童更具吸引力。


近义词：sweeten beforehand, pre-sweeten


反义词：leave unsweetened


词性:动词
