clearinghouse: /ˈklɪərɪŋhaʊs/ | n. |清算所，结算中心 | A clearinghouse is an institution that facilitates transactions between parties by acting as an intermediary.  It typically handles the settlement of financial obligations, such as checks or securities, and reduces risks associated with direct transactions.  | 例句：The New York Stock Exchange operates a clearinghouse to ensure the timely and efficient settlement of trades.  纽约证券交易所运营着一个清算所，以确保交易的及时和高效结算。


The central bank acts as a clearinghouse for the nation's banks. 中央银行充当全国银行的清算所。|近义词：settlement center, exchange, intermediary |反义词：N/A
