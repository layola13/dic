preinvesting: /ˌpriːɪnˈvɛstɪŋ/ | v. | 预先投资，提前投资 |  The act of investing before a project or venture officially begins, often to secure a position or gain an advantage.  This involves making investments in anticipation of future returns.  | 例句：The company is preinvesting in renewable energy sources to ensure a sustainable future. = 这家公司正在预先投资可再生能源，以确保可持续的未来。


The early adoption of new technologies often requires preinvesting in research and development. = 新技术的早期采用通常需要预先投资于研发。


近义词：advance funding, upfront investment, early-stage investment | 反义词：post-investment, delayed investment | 词性：动词
