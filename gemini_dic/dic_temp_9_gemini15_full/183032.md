replied:/rɪˈplaɪd/| v.|回答，答复|To give an answer in words or writing to a question, statement, or letter.|例句：He replied to my email promptly. 他很快回复了我的邮件。
She replied that she was fine. 她回答说她很好。|近义词：answered, responded, returned|反义词：ignored, neglected|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replies to emails quickly. 他很快回复邮件。
She replies with a smile. 她笑着回应。


一般过去时:
I replied to his question. 我回答了他的问题。
They replied that they understood. 他们回答说他们明白了。


现在完成时:
She has replied to all the emails. 她已经回复了所有邮件。
He has replied to my request. 他已经回复了我的请求。


过去完成时:
I had already replied before he called.  在他打电话之前我已经回复了。
They had replied negatively to the proposal. 他们已经对该提案作出了否定的答复。


将来时:
I will reply to your message tomorrow. 我明天会回复你的消息。
She will reply as soon as possible. 她会尽快回复。


虚拟语气:
If he had replied sooner, we would have avoided the problem. 如果他早点回复，我们就不会遇到这个问题了。
I wish she had replied to my letter. 我希望她能回复我的信。


被动语态:
His question was replied to promptly. 他的问题得到了迅速的答复。
The email was replied to yesterday. 邮件昨天得到了回复。


疑问句:
Did he reply to your message? 他回复你的消息了吗？
Has she replied yet? 她回复了吗？


否定句:
He didn't reply to my letter. 他没有回复我的信。
She hasn't replied yet. 她还没回复。


PHRASE->
reply to: 回复
reply in kind: 以牙还牙
reply sharply: 尖锐地回答
reply promptly: 迅速回复
in reply to:  作为对…的回答
