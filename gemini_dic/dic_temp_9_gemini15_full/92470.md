hypostyle:/ˈhaɪpəʊstaɪl/| n.|柱式大厅；多柱大厅|A hypostyle hall is a large room or building with a roof supported by a large number of columns.  The columns are often arranged in rows and create a visually impressive space. |The ancient Egyptian temples often featured magnificent hypostyle halls.  埃及古庙通常都设有宏伟的柱式大厅。


The Karnak Temple's Great Hypostyle Hall is a stunning example of hypostyle architecture. 卡纳克神庙的大柱式大厅是柱式建筑的惊人范例。|近义词：columned hall, pillared hall|反义词：无|名词
