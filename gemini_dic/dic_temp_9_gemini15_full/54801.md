dispenses:/dɪˈspɛnsz/ | vt. | 分配；分发；提供；免除 | To give out something, especially in portions or shares; to distribute.  | The pharmacy dispenses prescriptions. 药房配药。| The machine dispenses drinks. 这台机器供应饮料。 | distributes, provides, gives out |  hoards, keeps, retains | 动词


USAGE->

The machine dispenses drinks. = 这台机器供应饮料。
The teacher dispenses knowledge to her students. = 老师把知识传授给学生。
