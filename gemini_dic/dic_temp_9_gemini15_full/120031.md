millionaire:/ˌmɪljəˈneər/| n. |百万富翁，百万富婆 |A person who has assets worth at least one million dollars or the equivalent in other currencies.  |He dreams of becoming a millionaire one day. 他梦想有一天成为百万富翁。|  She inherited a considerable fortune and became a millionaire overnight. 她继承了一笔可观的财富，一夜之间成了百万富翁。| multimillionaire, billionaire | pauper, beggar | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a millionaire. = 他是一个百万富翁。
They are millionaires. = 他们是百万富翁。


一般过去时:
He was a millionaire last year. = 去年他是一个百万富翁。
She was a millionaire before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，她是一个百万富翁。


现在完成时:
He has been a millionaire for many years. = 他已经是一个百万富翁很多年了。
She has become a millionaire through hard work. = 她通过努力工作成为了百万富翁。


过去完成时:
He had been a millionaire before he lost his fortune. = 在他失去财富之前，他是一个百万富翁。
She had already been a millionaire when she started her charity. = 当她开始做慈善事业时，她已经是百万富翁了。


将来时:
He will be a millionaire someday. = 他总有一天会成为百万富翁。
She will become a millionaire if her business succeeds. = 如果她的生意成功，她将会成为百万富翁。


虚拟语气:
If I were a millionaire, I would travel the world. = 如果我是一个百万富翁，我会环游世界。
I wish I were a millionaire. = 我希望我是一个百万富翁。


被动语态:
(This word is rarely used in the passive voice.  The passive voice would focus on *becoming* a millionaire, not being one.)  He was declared a millionaire by Forbes magazine.  福布斯杂志宣布他为百万富翁。
She was made a millionaire by her inheritance.  她因为继承而成为百万富翁。


疑问句:
Is he a millionaire? = 他是百万富翁吗？
Are they millionaires? = 他们都是百万富翁吗？


否定句:
He is not a millionaire. = 他不是百万富翁。
They are not millionaires. = 他们不是百万富翁。
