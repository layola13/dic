classicalism:/ˈklæsɪsɪzəm/| n. |古典主义；古典风格|The adherence to the principles and styles of classical art, literature, or music, especially that of ancient Greece and Rome.  Classicalism emphasizes order, balance, harmony, and restraint.  It often features clear structure, formal language, and a focus on reason and intellect.|The building's architecture exemplifies classicalism, with its symmetrical facade and use of columns.|Neoclassicism, humanism|Baroque, Romanticism|名词
