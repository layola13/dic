reliction:/rɪˈlɪkʃən/| n.|遗物；遗迹；放弃；遗弃|The relinquishment or abandonment of a right, claim, or possession.  It often refers to something left behind or abandoned, especially something of historical or archaeological significance.  The term can also refer to the legal abandonment of a claim or right.|例句：The ancient ruins are a powerful relic of a bygone civilization. 这片古老的废墟是昔日文明的有力遗迹。

The discovery of the reliction provided valuable insights into the region's history. 遗物的发现为了解该地区的歷史提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：remnant, vestige, leftover, abandon, relinquishment|反义词：acquisition, retention
