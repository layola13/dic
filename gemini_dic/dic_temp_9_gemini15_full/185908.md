revolutionizer:/ˌrevəˈluːʃənaɪzər/| n. |革命者；改革者；创新者|A person or thing that causes a revolution or great change.  Someone or something that brings about a significant and positive change.|The invention of the internet was a true revolutionizer of communication.  互联网的发明是通讯的真正革命者。| innovator, reformer, trailblazer | reactionary, conservative | 名词
