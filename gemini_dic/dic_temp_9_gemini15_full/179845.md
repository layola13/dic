reexperimented:/ˌriːɪkˈspɛrɪmɛntɪd/ | v. | 再实验；重新实验 | To perform an experiment again, often with modifications or under different conditions.  |  The scientists reexperimented with a new catalyst to improve the reaction yield. 科学家们用新的催化剂重新进行了实验，以提高反应产率。 | experimented, retested |  (No direct antonym, but the opposite action would be to cease experimenting.)


USAGE->

They reexperiment regularly to refine their methods. = 他们定期重新实验以改进他们的方法。
The team reexperiments with different variables to find the optimal solution. = 团队正在尝试不同的变量以找到最佳解决方案。

She reexperimented with the formula after the initial results were inconclusive. = 在最初的结果不确定后，她重新进行了实验。
