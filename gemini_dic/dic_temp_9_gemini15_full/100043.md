interplane:/ˈɪntərpleɪn/| n. |中间平面，翼间，(飞机的)机翼间|A surface or region between two planes, especially the space between the wings of an airplane.  The term is primarily used in aviation and aeronautical engineering. |例句：The mechanic inspected the interplane struts for any damage. 机械师检查了机翼间的支柱是否有损坏。

例句：Airflow between the wings, in the interplane, affects lift. 机翼间的空气流动影响升力。


近义词：wingspan (部分情况下), space between wings|反义词：无


词性:名词
