end-in-smoke: /ɛndɪnˈsmoʊk/ | phrasal verb |化为乌有；以失败告终；烟消云散|  To fail completely; to disappear without a trace; to come to nothing.  The idiom originates from the visual image of something burning and disappearing completely as smoke. |


例句：

His ambitious plans ended in smoke.  他的雄心勃勃的计划化为泡影。

All our efforts ended in smoke. 我们所有的努力都付诸东流。


近义词：fail, collapse, fall through, come to naught, vanish


反义词：succeed, triumph, materialize, come to fruition


词性: phrasal verb
