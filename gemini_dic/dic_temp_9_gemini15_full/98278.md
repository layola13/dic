insets:/ˈɪnˌsɛts/| n. |插图；镶嵌物；嵌入物 |Something inserted into something else, such as a picture in a frame or a smaller piece of material in a larger one.  |Example: The book contains beautiful color insets. 这本书包含精美的彩色插图。

The magazine had several insets advertising new products. 这本杂志有几张广告新产品的插页。


|近义词：inserts, additions, embellishments |反义词：omissions, removals |名词
