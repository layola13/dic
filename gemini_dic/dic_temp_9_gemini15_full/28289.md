captures:/ˈkæptʃərz/| vt. n.|捕获；夺取；吸引；表达；(照片、影像)捕捉；|to take something into one's possession, especially by force; to represent or express something accurately or vividly; the act of capturing something.|例句：The police captured the thief.  警察抓住了小偷。|例句：The camera captures the beauty of the landscape. 相机捕捉到了这片美丽的风景。|近义词：seize, grab, catch,  obtain,  convey,  represent|反义词：release, liberate, free, miss, fail to represent|动词


USAGE->

The photographer captures stunning images. = 摄影师捕捉到令人惊叹的图像。
The artist captures the essence of human emotion. = 艺术家捕捉到了人类情感的本质。
