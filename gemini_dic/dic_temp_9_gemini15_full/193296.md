seemliness:/ˈsiːmliːnəs/| n. |得体；合宜；体面|The quality or state of being seemly; propriety; decorum.  It refers to behavior, appearance, or actions that are appropriate and proper for a particular situation or context.  |例句：The seemliness of her attire was impeccable.  她的穿着非常得体。

例句：He conducted himself with great seemliness throughout the ceremony. 他在整个仪式上举止得体。


近义词：propriety, decorum, appropriateness, fitness, decency

反义词：indecency, impropriety, unseemliness, inappropriateness
