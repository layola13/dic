maistre:/ˈmɑːstər/| n. |大师，师傅；（旧时）男管家|A maistre is a master, teacher, or skilled craftsman; historically, it also referred to a male housekeeper or steward.  The word carries connotations of skill, authority, and often a certain age and experience.|例句：He was a maistre of his craft, renowned for his skill in carpentry. 他是技艺大师，以其精湛的木工技艺而闻名。|近义词：master, teacher, expert, craftsman|反义词：novice, apprentice, unskilled worker|名词
