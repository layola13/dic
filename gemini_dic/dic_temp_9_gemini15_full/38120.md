comprehendingly:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhendɪŋli/ | adv. | 理解地；明白地 | In a way that shows understanding.  It implies a thorough grasp of the situation or information. | He listened comprehendingly to her explanation. 他理解地听着她的解释。 | understandingly, intelligently | unintelligently, incomprehensibly | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and its usage is straightforward. It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.  There's no specific tense conjugation involved as it's an adverbial modifier.

Example 1: She nodded comprehendingly. 她理解地点点头。
Example 2: He smiled comprehendingly, indicating he understood the joke. 他理解地笑了，表示他明白了这个笑话。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "comprehendingly".  Its use is generally stand-alone to modify a verb.  However, phrases incorporating the root word "comprehend" are common:
