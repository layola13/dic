self-contradicting: /ˌsɛlf kɒntrəˈdɪktɪŋ/ | adj. | 自相矛盾的 |

英文解析:  Describing a statement, argument, or action that contains or implies a contradiction; inherently inconsistent.  Something self-contradicting contradicts itself.

例句:

His argument was self-contradicting, making it difficult to follow.  他的论点自相矛盾，难以理解。

The politician's statement was self-contradicting, undermining his credibility.  这位政治家的声明自相矛盾，损害了他的信誉。


近义词: inconsistent, contradictory, paradoxical, self-defeating, illogical

反义词: consistent, logical, coherent


词性: adjective (形容词)
