picketing:/ˈpɪkɪtɪŋ/| n. |纠察；罢工纠察；抗议示威|The act of picketing involves standing outside a place of work or other location to protest something, often as part of a labor dispute or other demonstration.  It often involves carrying signs or banners. |例句：The workers engaged in picketing outside the factory to demand better wages. 工人们在工厂外进行纠察，要求提高工资。

例句：The students organized a picketing event to protest the university's new policy. 学生们组织了一次抗议活动来反对大学的新政策。


近义词：demonstration, protest, protest march, sit-in|反义词：compliance, acceptance, agreement|名词
