district:/ˈdɪstrɪkt/ | n. | 区；地区；区域 | A district is a particular area of a country or town, especially one that has some particular feature.  | The school is located in a residential district. 这所学校位于住宅区。The police are investigating the crime in the district. 警方正在调查该地区的犯罪活动。 | region, area, zone, section |  (近义词无明显反义词，可根据语境选择如：rural/urban)
