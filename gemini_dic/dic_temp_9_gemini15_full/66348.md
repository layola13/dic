excursioned:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒənd/| v.|短途旅行；远足|The word "excursioned" is the past tense and past participle of the verb "excursion," meaning to go on an excursion or short trip.  It's less common than using "went on an excursion" or a similar phrasing.|例句：Last summer, we excursioned to the mountains. 去年夏天，我们去山里远足了。
They excursioned to the coast for the weekend. 他们周末去了海边短途旅行。|近义词：travelled, journeyed, toured, tripped|反义词：stayed, remained
