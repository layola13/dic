decoded:/diˈkoʊdid/| adj. |解码的；已译解的 |  Decoded means having been converted from a code or cipher into plain text or understandable form. It implies a successful process of deciphering or interpreting encrypted information. | Example: The decoded message revealed the secret plan. 解码后的信息揭示了秘密计划。  The decoded signal showed a clear pattern. 解码的信号显示出清晰的模式。|近义词：decrypted, deciphered |反义词：encoded, encrypted
