champagne:/ʃæmˈpeɪn/| n. |香槟酒|A sparkling wine made in the Champagne region of France, typically from Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, and Pinot Meunier grapes.|例句：We celebrated with a bottle of champagne. 我们用一瓶香槟酒庆祝。|近义词：sparkling wine|反义词:still wine
