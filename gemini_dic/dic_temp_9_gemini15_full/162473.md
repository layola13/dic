preadvised:/ˌpriːədˈvaɪzd/| adj.| 预先告知的；预先劝告的 |  Pre-advised means something has been advised or warned about in advance. It implies prior notification or caution given before an event or action. | Example:  The pre-advised safety procedures were followed meticulously. = 预先告知的安全程序得到了细致的遵守。  The hikers were pre-advised of the potential dangers of the trail. = 徒步旅行者们已预先被告知小径上潜在的危险。 | 近义词：forewarned, pre-informed, cautioned | 反义词：unwarned, uninformed
