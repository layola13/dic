preindemnifying: /ˌpriːɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 预先赔偿；预先补偿 |  The act of compensating or securing someone against future loss or damage before the loss or damage occurs.  This is typically done through contracts or insurance policies.  | 例句：The contract included a clause for preindemnifying the contractor against potential liability. = 合同中包含了一项条款，用于预先赔偿承包商可能承担的责任。


The company's preindemnifying agreement protected them from financial ruin. = 公司的预先赔偿协议保护他们免受财务破产。| 近义词：prepaying, securing, protecting, insuring | 反义词：post-indemnifying, leaving liable
