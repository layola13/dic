nonspheric:/ˌnɒnˈsfɪərɪk/| adj. |非球面的|Not spherical; having a surface that is not perfectly round.  This term describes shapes that deviate from a perfect sphere, exhibiting irregularities or asymmetries.  It's often used in optics and lens design to describe lenses that are not perfectly spherical. | Example: The telescope's nonspheric mirror provided superior image quality.  望远镜的非球面镜提供了更高的图像质量。|近义词：aspherical, non-spherical|反义词：spherical
