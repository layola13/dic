irrigates:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪts/ | vt. | 灌溉；冲洗；清除 (污物) | To supply (land) with water artificially.  To wash or cleanse with a fluid. |例句：The farmer irrigates his fields with a complex system of canals. 农民用复杂的运河系统灌溉他的田地。

The doctor irrigates the wound to remove debris. 医生冲洗伤口以清除碎屑。


近义词：water, flush, cleanse | 反义词：dehydrate, dry out | 词性:动词
