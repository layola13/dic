chapatti:/tʃəˈpætɪ/| n. |印度薄饼|A thin unleavened flatbread made from whole wheat flour, commonly eaten in India, Pakistan, and other parts of South Asia.  It is typically cooked on a griddle or flat pan.| 例句：For dinner, we had delicious chicken curry served with chapattis.  晚餐我们吃了美味的鸡肉咖喱配印度薄饼。| 例句：She rolled out the dough and cooked the chapatti until it was perfectly browned. 她擀开面团，把印度薄饼煎到完美焦黄。|近义词：roti, chapati, phulka|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
