circumambulate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbjuleɪt/| v. |绕行；环绕行走|To walk around something, especially a building or sacred place, typically as a ritual or demonstration.|The pilgrims circumambulated the holy shrine.|近义词：encircle, perambulate, go around|反义词：traverse directly
