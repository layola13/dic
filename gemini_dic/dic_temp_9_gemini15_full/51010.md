desk: /dɛsk/ | n. | 书桌，办公桌 | A piece of furniture with a flat top and usually drawers, used for writing or working at.  |  例句：He sat at his desk, working on a report. 他坐在他的书桌前，写报告。


例句：There's a lamp on the desk. 书桌上有一盏灯。


近义词：table, writing table, worktable | 反义词：None


词源:  The word "desk" comes from Old French "desque,"  itself derived from Old French "desca," meaning "a writing table."  Ultimately, the word's roots trace back to the Latin word "desca," meaning "a table" or "a board."
