mesophyll:/ˈmɛsəˌfɪl/| n. |叶肉|The internal tissue of a leaf, containing cells with chloroplasts that carry out photosynthesis.|The mesophyll is the primary site of photosynthesis in plants. 叶肉是植物进行光合作用的主要场所。|近义词：chlorenchyma |反义词: epidermis
