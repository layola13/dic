/ˈkædi/| n.|球童；行李员；茶水桶；高尔夫球车|A caddy is a person who carries a golfer's clubs during a game.  It can also refer to a small container, typically for tea or other beverages.  In some contexts, it refers to a small cart used to carry golf clubs.|例句：He hired a caddy to carry his golf clubs. 他雇了一个球童来扛他的高尔夫球杆。

例句：She poured tea from the caddy. 她从茶叶罐里倒茶。
