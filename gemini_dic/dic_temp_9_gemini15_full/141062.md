onomantic: /ˌɒnəˈmæntɪk/ | adj. |  命名学的；与名字相关的；名字占卜的 | Relating to or involving the significance or supposed magical power of names.  The word combines "onoma" (name) and "mantic" (divination). It suggests a belief in the inherent power or influence of a name on a person's life or destiny.  |  The onomantic tradition holds that names are not arbitrary labels but rather possess inherent power.  | 近义词:  nominative, appellative | 反义词:  nameless, anonymous |  形容词
