corrected:/kəˈrektɪd/ | adj. | 被改正的；正确的 |  Corrected means having been made accurate or right; free from errors.  | Example: The corrected version of the report is now available.  已更正的报告现已可用。 |近义词：amended, rectified, improved | 反义词：incorrect, wrong, flawed |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The corrected text is clear and concise. = 更正后的文本清晰简洁。
The corrected answer is on the board. = 正确答案在黑板上。


一般过去时:
He corrected the spelling mistakes in his essay. = 他纠正了他论文中的拼写错误。
She corrected her previous statement. = 她纠正了她之前的陈述。


现在完成时:
I have corrected all the errors in the document. = 我已经纠正了文档中的所有错误。
They have corrected the inaccurate data. = 他们已经纠正了不准确的数据。


过去完成时:
He had corrected the manuscript before submitting it. = 他在提交手稿之前已经更正了它。
She had corrected the figures before presenting them. = 她提交这些数字之前已经纠正了它们。


将来时:
I will correct the mistakes tomorrow. = 我明天将更正这些错误。
They will correct the error in the next update. = 他们将在下次更新中更正错误。


虚拟语气:
If I had corrected the mistake earlier, the problem would not have occurred. = 如果我早点纠正错误，问题就不会发生了。
I wish I had corrected my answer before submitting the test. = 我希望我在提交测试之前纠正了我的答案。


被动语态:
The errors were corrected by the editor. = 错误由编辑更正。
The document is being corrected at the moment. = 文档正在被更正。


疑问句:
Have the errors been corrected? = 错误已更正了吗？
Were the figures corrected accurately? = 数字被准确地更正了吗？


否定句:
The errors were not corrected. = 错误没有被更正。
He did not correct his mistakes. = 他没有纠正他的错误。
