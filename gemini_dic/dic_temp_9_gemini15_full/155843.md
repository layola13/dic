The adjective "phosphorescent" describes something that glows in the dark after being exposed to light.  The adverb "phosphorescently" modifies a verb, describing how the action is performed.  |The fireflies blinked phosphorescently in the night.  萤火虫在夜空中磷光闪烁。

The clock's hands glowed phosphorescently in the dark room.  时钟的指针在黑暗的房间里发出磷光。
