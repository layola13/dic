overloads:/ˈoʊvərloʊdz/| vt. n.|超载；过载；使超载|To overload something means to put too much weight or too many things on or into it.  It can also refer to assigning too many tasks or responsibilities to someone or something.  As a noun, it refers to an instance of overloading.|例句：The truck was overloaded with cargo. 这辆卡车超载货物了。

The programmer overloaded the function with too many parameters. 程序员给函数添加了过多的参数，导致函数超载。


近义词：overburden, overwhelm, overtax|反义词：underload, underburden


USAGE->
