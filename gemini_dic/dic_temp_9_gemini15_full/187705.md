roommate:/ˈruːmˌmeɪt/ | n. |室友 |A person who shares a room or apartment with another person.  |Example: My roommate and I share the rent and utilities.我的室友和我共同承担房租和水电费。|近义词：housemate, flatmate |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My roommate cleans the kitchen every week. = 我的室友每周打扫厨房。
He is a considerate roommate. = 他是一位体贴的室友。

一般过去时:
My roommate cooked dinner last night. = 我的室友昨晚做了晚饭。
We had a great time with our old roommate. = 我们和以前的室友玩得很开心。

现在完成时:
I have known my roommate for two years. = 我认识我的室友两年了。
My roommate has already paid the rent. = 我的室友已经付了房租。

过去完成时:
I had never met my roommate before moving in. = 在搬进来之前，我从未见过我的室友。
They had lived together as roommates for five years before the argument. = 他们在争吵之前已经作为室友一起生活了五年。

将来时:
My roommate will be moving out next month. = 我的室友下个月将搬出去。
We will find a new roommate soon. = 我们很快就会找到一个新的室友。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better roommate, I would be happier. = 如果我有一个更好的室友，我会更快乐。
I wish my roommate would clean up after himself. = 我希望我的室友能自己收拾干净。

被动语态:
The room was shared by two roommates. = 这个房间由两个室友合住。
The rent was split fairly between the roommates. = 房租在室友之间公平分配。

疑问句:
Do you like your roommate? = 你喜欢你的室友吗？
Does your roommate mind if I borrow his car? = 你的室友介意我借他的车吗？

否定句:
I don't get along with my roommate. = 我和我的室友相处不好。
My roommate doesn't like loud music. = 我的室友不喜欢大声的音乐。
