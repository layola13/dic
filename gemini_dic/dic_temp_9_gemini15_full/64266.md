equipper:/ɪˈkwɪpər/| n. |装备者；配备者 |A person or thing that equips or furnishes. Someone or something that provides what is needed for a particular purpose or activity.  |例句：The skilled equipper prepared the soldiers for battle. 熟练的装备者为士兵们准备了战斗物资。|例句：The company is a leading equipper of military vehicles. 这家公司是主要的军用车辆装备商。|近义词：supplier, provider, furnisher |反义词：disarmer, stripper


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The equipper prepares the team for the expedition. = 装备者为团队准备远征物资。
He equips his backpack with essential survival tools. = 他用必要的生存工具装备他的背包。

一般过去时:
