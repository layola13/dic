drifter:/ˈdrɪftər/| n.|漂泊者，流浪者，游民|A person who has no fixed home or job and travels from place to place.|He was a drifter, moving from town to town, finding odd jobs to survive. 他是个流浪汉，在城镇间漂泊，打零工为生。|vagrant, wanderer, nomad|settler, resident|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The drifter wanders the streets. = 流浪汉在街上游荡。
He drifts from job to job. = 他四处漂泊，更换工作。


一般过去时:
The drifter slept under the bridge last night. = 流浪汉昨晚睡在桥下。
She drifted through life without a purpose. = 她漫无目的地度过一生。


现在完成时:
He has drifted through many countries. = 他已经漂泊过许多国家。
She has drifted in and out of relationships. = 她一直处于不稳定的恋爱关系中。


过去完成时:
Before he found his calling, he had drifted for years. = 在找到自己的事业之前，他已经漂泊多年了。
The boat had drifted far from the shore. = 船已漂离海岸很远。


将来时:
He will drift further and further from reality. = 他将越来越脱离现实。
The river will drift the log downstream. = 河流将把原木冲向下游。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't drifted so much, he might have had a family. = 如果他不那么四处漂泊，也许会有一个家庭。
I wish I hadn't drifted apart from my old friends. = 我真希望我没有和老朋友疏远。


被动语态:
The boat was drifted by the current. = 船被水流冲走了。
He was drifted into a life of crime. = 他被卷入了犯罪的生活。


疑问句:
Is he a drifter? = 他是一个流浪者吗？
Where does the drifter sleep? = 流浪者在哪里睡觉？


否定句:
He is not a drifter; he has a stable job. = 他不是流浪汉；他有一份稳定的工作。
She didn't drift aimlessly; she had a plan. = 她没有漫无目的地游荡；她有计划。
