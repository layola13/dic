centrifuged:/ˈsɛntrfjuːdʒd/| v. |离心的；用离心机处理的 | Past participle of centrifuge.  It describes something that has been spun rapidly in a centrifuge.  |例句：The blood sample was centrifuged to separate the plasma from the cells.  血液样本经过离心处理以分离血浆和细胞。

例句：The urine sample had been centrifuged before analysis. 尿液样本在分析前已进行离心处理。


近义词：spun, rotated, whirled


反义词：None directly opposite, but  'unseparated' could be considered a contextual antonym.


词性：verb (past participle)
