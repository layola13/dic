inamovable: /ɪˈnæmuːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可移动的，固定的 |  Unable to be moved; fixed firmly in place.  | The heavy safe was inamovable without specialized equipment.  这个沉重的保险箱没有专用设备是无法移动的。 The mountain seemed inamovable, a permanent fixture in the landscape.  这座山看起来不可移动，是风景中永久的固定物。 | immovable, fixed, stationary | movable, removable, portable | 形容词

词源:
The word "inamovable" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "movable" (capable of being moved).  "Movable" ultimately derives from the Old French word "movable," which in turn comes from the Latin word "mobilis," meaning "able to be moved."
