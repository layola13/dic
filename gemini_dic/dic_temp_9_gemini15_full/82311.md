/ˈɡraʊzərz/ | n. |抱怨者；牢骚满腹的人；滑雪靴 | A person who complains frequently or habitually.  Grousers often express dissatisfaction or annoyance.  The term can also refer to a type of heavy, insulated boot, typically used for skiing or mountaineering. | Example:  He's a real grouser; he's always complaining about something. 他是个爱抱怨的人；他总是抱怨一些事情。

He bought new grousers for his upcoming ski trip. 他为即将到来的滑雪旅行买了新的滑雪靴。| 近义词：complainer, whiner, grumbler | 反义词：optimist, complaisant
