hyperdevelopmental:/ˌhaɪpərˌdɪˌvɛləpˈmentəl/ | adj. | 超速发展的；过度发展的 | Excessively developed or advanced, often beyond what is typical or expected for a given age or stage.  This term often implies an accelerated rate of development, sometimes with potential negative consequences. |  The child showed hyperdevelopmental skills in mathematics, mastering calculus at the age of eight.  这个孩子在数学方面表现出超速发展的技能，八岁时就掌握了微积分。  |近义词：advanced, precocious, accelerated | 反义词：underdeveloped, retarded, delayed |  形容词
