pass-out: /ˈpæs aʊt/ | v. | 昏过去；失去知觉；分发；通过 |

英文解析:To pass out can mean to lose consciousness, to distribute something, or to successfully complete a test or course.  The meaning depends heavily on context.

例句:

He passed out from the heat. = 他热晕过去了。

The teacher passed out the exams. = 老师分发了试卷。

She passed out her nursing school exams. = 她顺利通过了护士学校的考试。


近义词: faint, collapse, distribute, succeed, graduate

反义词: regain consciousness, fail
