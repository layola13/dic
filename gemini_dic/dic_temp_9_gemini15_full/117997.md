memorializes:/məˈmɔːriəlaɪz/|/ˈmɛmərɪəlaɪz/| vt. |纪念；使不朽；作为纪念|To keep the memory of (someone or something) alive or to create a lasting record of (something).  The verb "memorializes" signifies the act of creating a lasting tribute or commemoration to honor or remember a person, event, or idea.  It implies a formal or significant act of remembrance.|

例句：
The statue memorializes the fallen soldiers. = 这座雕像纪念阵亡的士兵。

The museum memorializes the history of the city. = 这个博物馆纪念这座城市的历史。


近义词：commemorates, celebrates, honors, remembers

反义词：forgets, ignores, neglects

词性:动词
