galleries:/ˈɡæləriːz/| n.|画廊；美术馆；长廊|A gallery is a room or building for the display or sale of works of art, or a covered passageway.|例句：The museum's galleries are filled with masterpieces.  博物馆的画廊里充满了杰作。|例句：We strolled along the long gallery admiring the architecture. 我们沿着长廊漫步，欣赏着建筑。|近义词：museum, showroom, arcade|反义词：None specifically.
