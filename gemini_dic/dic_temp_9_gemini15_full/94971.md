in-the-air: /ɪn ði ˈɛr/ | adj. | 空中的；未决定的；谣传的 |  Existing or happening in the air; undecided or uncertain; rumored. |


例句：

The birds are in the air. = 鸟儿在空中飞翔。

The proposal is still in the air. = 这个提案仍在讨论中，尚未决定。


近义词： airborne, aloft, unsettled, undecided, rumored


反义词： settled, decided, confirmed


词性：形容词
