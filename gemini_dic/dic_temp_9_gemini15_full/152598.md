peaceless:/ˈpiːsləs/| adj.|不安宁的；动荡的；混乱的 |Without peace; disturbed; unsettled.  The adjective "peaceless" describes a state or situation lacking peace, tranquility, or harmony. It suggests a condition of unrest, turmoil, or conflict. |例句：The country remained peaceless despite the peace treaty.  这个国家尽管签订了和平条约，仍然不安宁。

例句：The peaceless atmosphere in the city prevented the festival from taking place.  城市里不安宁的气氛导致节日无法举行。
