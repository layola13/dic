redact:/ˈrɛdækt/| vt.|编辑；修改；删节|To edit or revise (a text), especially by removing sensitive or confidential information.|The government redacted sensitive information from the report before releasing it to the public. 政府在向公众发布报告之前删除了敏感信息。|近义词：edit, revise, expunge, censor|反义词：reveal, disclose|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor redacts the manuscript. = 编辑修改手稿。
They redact sensitive information from the document. = 他们从文件中删除敏感信息。

一般过去时:
He redacted the names of the witnesses. = 他删去了证人的名字。
She redacted the offensive passages from the book. = 她从书中删去了冒犯性的段落。

现在完成时:
They have redacted all the confidential details. = 他们已经删除了所有机密细节。
The team has redacted the report for public release. = 该团队已修改报告以便公开发布。

过去完成时:
He had redacted the letter before sending it. = 他在寄出信之前已经修改了信件。
She had redacted the document to protect the sources. = 为保护消息来源，她已修改了文件。

将来时:
They will redact the document before publication. = 他们将在出版前修改该文件。
We will redact any personally identifiable information. = 我们将删除任何个人身份信息。

虚拟语气:
If they redacted the names, it would be safer. = 如果他们删除了姓名，就会更安全。
It would be better if they redacted the sensitive parts. = 如果他们删去敏感部分会更好。

被动语态:
The document was redacted by the security team. = 该文件由安全团队修改。
The report is being redacted for release. = 报告正在修改以便发布。

疑问句:
Has the report been redacted? = 报告是否已被修改？
Will the names be redacted from the list? = 姓名会从名单中删除吗？

否定句:
They did not redact the controversial section. = 他们没有修改有争议的部分。
He has not yet redacted the entire document. = 他尚未修改整份文件。


PHRASE->
