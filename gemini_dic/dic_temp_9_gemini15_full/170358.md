provisionalities: /ˌprɒvɪʒəˈnælətiz/ | n. | 临时措施；临时性规定；暂定条款 |  Provisionalities refer to temporary arrangements or measures that are put in place until a more permanent solution can be found.  They are often characterized by their impermanent nature and subject to change.  | Example: The government implemented several provisionalities to address the immediate crisis, but these will be replaced by more comprehensive long-term policies.  政府实施了一些临时措施来应对眼前的危机，但这些措施将被更全面的长期政策所取代。 | 近义词：temporary measures, interim arrangements, provisional solutions | 反义词：permanencies, final arrangements, permanent solutions | 名词
