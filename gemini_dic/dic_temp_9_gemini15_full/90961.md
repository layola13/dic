hurraying:/həˈreɪɪŋ/| v.|欢呼；喝彩|The act of shouting "hurray" repeatedly or enthusiastically.  It expresses great joy, excitement, or approval. |例句：The crowd was hurraying for the winning team.  人群为获胜的队伍欢呼。

例句：The children were hurraying with delight at the arrival of Santa Claus. 孩子们高兴地为圣诞老人的到来欢呼。


近义词：cheering, shouting, applauding, celebrating

反义词：mourning, lamenting, grieving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are hurraying for the national team. = 他们在为国家队欢呼。
We hurray at every good news we receive. = 我们每收到好消息都会欢呼。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
