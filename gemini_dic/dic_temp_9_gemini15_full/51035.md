desmodont:/ˈdɛzmədɒnt/| n. |脱齿兽|A desmodont is any of three species of bat in the family Desmodontidae, characterized by their specialized dentition for feeding on blood.  These bats are commonly known as vampire bats.|Example: The common vampire bat is a well-known desmodont.  普通吸血蝙蝠是一种众所周知的脱齿兽。|近义词：vampire bat (吸血蝙蝠)|反义词：无
