misallocates:/ˌmɪsˈæləkeɪts/| vt.|错误分配，错置|To allocate (resources, funds, etc.) inefficiently or inappropriately.  This implies a failure to optimize the distribution of something, often leading to negative consequences.|例句：The company misallocated its resources, leading to project delays and financial losses. 公司资源配置错误，导致项目延误和财务损失。
例句：The government misallocated funds intended for education, causing schools to lack essential supplies. 政府错误分配了用于教育的资金，导致学校缺乏必要的物资。|近义词：mismanage, misapply, waste|反义词：allocate efficiently, optimize, prioritize|动词
