remarriages: /ˌriːˈmærɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 再婚；复婚 |  The plural form of remarriage, referring to multiple instances of getting married again after a previous marriage has ended.  It encompasses situations where either or both partners have been married before. |

例句：
The statistics show a rising number of remarriages in recent years.  近年来，再婚的统计数字不断上升。

Many remarriages involve children from previous relationships. 许多再婚都涉及到来自以前关系的孩子。


近义词：second marriages, subsequent marriages

反义词：first marriages, initial marriages


词性：名词 (plural)
