copyrighting:/ˈkɒpiraɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːpraɪtɪŋ/| v.|版权登记；版权保护；撰写版权；创作；|The act of securing a copyright for a creative work, or the process of creating written content, often for marketing purposes.|  They are copyrighting their new software. 他们正在为他们的新软件申请版权。|He's busy with copyrighting the company's new slogan. 他正忙着为公司的新标语申请版权。|registering, securing|infringing, violating|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She copyrights her novels. = 她为她的小说申请版权。
He copyrights all his original work. = 他为所有原创作品申请版权。

一般过去时:
They copyrighted the song last year. = 他们去年为这首歌申请了版权。
The author copyrighted the book before publication. = 作者在出版前为这本书申请了版权。

现在完成时:
She has copyrighted several books. = 她已经为几本书申请了版权。
He has copyrighted his invention. = 他已经为他的发明申请了版权。

过去完成时:
They had copyrighted the design before the competitor did. = 在竞争对手之前，他们已经为该设计申请了版权。
The company had copyrighted the logo for many years. = 这家公司已经拥有该商标的版权多年了。

将来时:
We will copyright the film next month. = 我们下个月将为这部电影申请版权。
She will copyright her paintings soon. = 她很快就会为她的画作申请版权。


虚拟语气:
If I had copyrighted my invention earlier, I would have made more money. = 如果我早点为我的发明申请版权，我会赚更多钱。
I wish I had copyrighted my music before releasing it. = 我真希望在发布我的音乐之前就已申请了版权。

被动语态:
The song was copyrighted in 2022. = 这首歌的版权是在2022年注册的。
The software is being copyrighted now. = 软件正在申请版权。

疑问句:
Has the book been copyrighted yet? = 这本书已经申请版权了吗？
Is he planning to copyright his photographs? = 他计划为他的照片申请版权吗？

否定句:
They did not copyright the article. = 他们没有为这篇文章申请版权。
He hasn't copyrighted his latest work. = 他还没有为他最新的作品申请版权。


PHRASE->
copyright infringement = 版权侵犯
copyright holder = 版权持有者
copyright notice = 版权声明
copyright protection = 版权保护
copyright law = 版权法
