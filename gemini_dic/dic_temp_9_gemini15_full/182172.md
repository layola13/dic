remodification:/ˌriːˌmɒdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |再修改；重新修改；二次修改 |The act of modifying something that has already been modified.  It implies a second or subsequent alteration.  This often involves refining or improving upon a previous modification. |Example: The software underwent remodification to address user feedback. 软件经过二次修改以解决用户反馈。The remodification of the engine increased its efficiency. 引擎的重新修改提高了效率。|近义词：revise, amend, alter, readjust|反义词：originality, creation
