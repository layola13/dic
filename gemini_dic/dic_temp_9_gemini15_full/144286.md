outspeed:/ˌaʊtˈspiːd/| vt.|超过速度；比…跑得快|To surpass in speed; to exceed in velocity.|例句：The new car outspeeds all its competitors. 这辆新车速度超过所有竞争对手。
例句：The cheetah can outspeed most other land animals. 猎豹的速度超过大多数其他陆地动物。|近义词：outpace, outrun, exceed|反义词：underspeed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sports car outspeeds all others on the track. = 这辆跑车在赛道上速度超过其他所有车辆。
He outspeeds his opponents in the race. = 他在比赛中速度超过了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The greyhound outsped the rabbit in the race. = 在比赛中，猎犬的速度超过了兔子。
The rocket outsped all previous models. = 这枚火箭的速度超过了所有以前的型号。


现在完成时:
The new train has outsped all expectations in terms of speed. = 新型火车在速度方面已经超过了所有预期。
She has outsped her personal best time in the marathon. = 她马拉松比赛的成绩已经超过了她个人的最好成绩。


过去完成时:
By the time the police arrived, the thief had outsped them. = 当警察到达时，小偷已经超过了他们。
The plane had outsped the storm before it hit the coast. = 飞机在风暴袭击海岸线之前已经超过了风暴。


将来时:
The new jet will outspeed all existing commercial aircraft. = 新型喷气式飞机将超过所有现有的商用飞机的速度。
We expect that the technology will outspeed previous methods. = 我们预计这项技术将超过以前的方法的速度。


虚拟语气:
If the horse had outsped the others, it would have won the race. = 如果那匹马的速度超过了其他马，它就赢得了比赛。
I wish I could outspeed the traffic jam. = 我希望我能超过交通堵塞。


被动语态:
The older model was outsped by the newer one. = 旧型号被新型号的速度超过了。
The record was outsped by a young athlete. = 这项纪录被一位年轻运动员打破了。


疑问句:
Can this engine outspeed that one? = 这个引擎的速度能超过那个吗？
Does the new car outspeed the old one? = 新车速度比旧车快吗？


否定句:
The small car couldn't outspeed the truck. = 小型车无法超过卡车的速度。
He did not outspeed his opponent in the final lap. = 他在最后一圈没有超过对手。
