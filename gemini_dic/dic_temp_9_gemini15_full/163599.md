precursory:/prɪˈkɜːrsəri/| adj.|预兆的；先驱的；先行的| preceding or foreshadowing something else.  It suggests something that comes before and indicates or announces the arrival of something else, often something more significant.|例句：The tremors were precursory to the earthquake.  地震前的震动是地震的预兆。|例句：His early works were precursory to his later, more famous paintings. 他的早期作品预示着他后来更著名的绘画。|近义词：antecedent, preliminary, introductory, prior, foreshadowing|反义词：subsequent, following, consequent
