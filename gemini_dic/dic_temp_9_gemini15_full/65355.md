eulogium:/juːˈləʊdʒiəm/| n. |颂词；赞辞；悼词 |A formal expression of praise, especially of a deceased person.  It often involves a glowing tribute to the person's achievements and character. |The eulogium delivered at the funeral was both moving and insightful.  葬礼上所致的颂词既感人又富有洞察力。| panegyric, tribute, encomium | invective, diatribe | 名词
