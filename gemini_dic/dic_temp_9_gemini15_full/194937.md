semi-lethal:/ˈsɛmiˈliːθəl/| adj.|半致死的，准致死的 |  Nearly, but not completely, fatal; causing partial death or serious injury.  |例句：The dose of poison was semi-lethal, leaving the victim severely weakened but alive.  这剂毒药是半致死的，使受害者严重虚弱但还活着。|近义词：partially lethal, sublethal, near-fatal |反义词：lethal, harmless, innocuous
