skinheads:/ˈskɪnhedz/| n.|光头党|A subculture, originally associated with working-class youth in Britain, characterized by shaved heads, specific clothing styles (often Doc Martens boots and certain brands of clothing), and sometimes far-right political views.  Skinhead culture has diverse facets, with some groups embracing working-class solidarity and others holding racist and violent ideologies. |The skinheads were known for their aggressive behavior and shaved heads.|近义词：Oi!, rude boys (in some contexts)|反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with  "hippies" or other counter-cultural groups)|名词
