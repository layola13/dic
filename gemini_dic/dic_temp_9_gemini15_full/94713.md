improperly:/ɪmˈprɑːpərli/ | adv. | 不正确地，不恰当地 | In a way that is not correct or appropriate.  It indicates a deviation from accepted standards, rules, or norms. |例句：He improperly filed his taxes, resulting in a penalty. 他不正确地报税，导致被罚款。

例句：The building was improperly constructed, leading to structural problems. 这栋建筑施工不当，导致结构问题。


近义词：incorrectly, inappropriately, wrongly, inadequately


反义词：properly, correctly, appropriately


词性: 副词
