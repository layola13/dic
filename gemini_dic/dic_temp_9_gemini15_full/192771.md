sealer:/ˈsiːlər/ | n. | 密封剂；封口机；防水剂 | A substance used to seal something, making it airtight or waterproof; a machine or tool used for sealing.  |例句：We used a silicone sealer to fill the gaps in the window frame. 我们用硅酮密封剂填充窗框的缝隙。

例句：The factory uses a high-speed sealer to package its products.  这家工厂使用高速封口机包装其产品。|近义词： sealant, adhesive, caulking |反义词：opener (in the context of a container), unsealer |词性 n.
