self-damnation:/ˌsɛlfˌdæmˈneɪʃən/| n. |自我谴责，自我毁灭|The act of condemning oneself; the state of being condemned by oneself; self-destruction.  It often implies a feeling of intense guilt and remorse, leading to self-destructive behavior.|例句：His self-damnation was evident in his constant self-criticism and reckless actions.  他的自我谴责在他持续不断的自我批评和鲁莽行为中显而易见。

例句：Driven by a sense of self-damnation, he isolated himself from friends and family.  受自我谴责感的驱使，他把自己与朋友和家人隔离开来。


近义词：self-condemnation, self-reproach, self-destruction, damnation


反义词：self-acceptance, self-forgiveness, redemption


词性: 名词
