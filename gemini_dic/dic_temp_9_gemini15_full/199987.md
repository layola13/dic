skateparks:/ˈskeɪtˌpɑːrks/| n.|滑板公园|A public area specifically designed for skateboarding, rollerblading, BMX biking, and other wheeled sports.  It typically features ramps, rails, and other obstacles.|Example: The new skatepark is a popular spot for local youth.|近义词：skate area,  BMX park|反义词: None
