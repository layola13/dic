self-exertions:/selfɪˈzɜːrʃənz/| n. |自我努力；尽力而为|  Self-exertions refer to the efforts one makes on their own behalf, without the help of others.  It emphasizes individual initiative and hard work. |Example:  Through sheer self-exertions, she managed to finish the marathon. 通过自身的努力，她设法完成了马拉松比赛。

His success was due to his own self-exertions, not to luck. 他的成功是由于他自己的努力，而不是运气。|近义词：endeavors, efforts, struggles, striving |反义词：passivity, idleness, inaction
