insulates: /ˈɪnsəleɪts/ | vt. | 使隔绝；使绝缘；使免受影响 | To protect something from the effect of something else, especially heat or cold, or to protect someone from an unpleasant influence. | 例句：The building is well insulated against the cold. 这栋建筑物隔热性能良好，不怕冷。

The thick walls insulate the house from outside noise. 厚厚的墙壁隔绝了来自外部的噪音。


近义词：isolate, protect, shield | 反义词：expose, connect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wool insulates the sheep from the cold. 羊毛使羊免受寒冷。
This material insulates against heat.  这种材料隔热。


一般过去时:
The government insulated the economy from global shocks. 政府使经济免受全球冲击。
They insulated the wires to prevent short circuits. 他们给电线包上绝缘层以防止短路。


现在完成时:
We have insulated the attic to improve energy efficiency. 我们已经给阁楼加了隔热层以提高能源效率。
The company has insulated itself from criticism. 这家公司已经让自己免受批评。


过去完成时:
They had insulated the pipes before the winter arrived. 他们在冬天到来之前已经给管道加了保温层。
The builders had insulated the walls before plastering them. 建造者在粉刷墙壁之前已经进行了隔热处理。


将来时:
We will insulate the house before moving in. 我们将在搬进去之前给房子做好隔热。
The engineers will insulate the power lines to improve safety. 工程师们将对电力线进行绝缘处理以提高安全性。


虚拟语气:
If we insulated the windows better, we would save more energy. 如果我们把窗户隔热做得更好，我们会节省更多的能源。
I wish they had insulated the house properly. 我真希望他们当初把房子隔热处理好。


被动语态:
The house is insulated with fiberglass. 这栋房子用玻璃纤维隔热。
The cables are insulated with plastic. 电缆用塑料绝缘。


疑问句:
Is the house properly insulated? 这房子隔热好吗？
Are the pipes insulated against freezing temperatures? 管道是否进行了防冻绝缘处理？


否定句:
The building isn't well insulated. 这栋建筑隔热性能不好。
They didn't insulate the attic properly. 他们没有正确地给阁楼进行隔热处理。


PHRASE->

词源:
Insulate comes from the Latin word "insulare," meaning "to make into an island."  This reflects the idea of separating something from its surroundings.
