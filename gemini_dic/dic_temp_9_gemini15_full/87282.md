hermeneutical:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪkəl/ | adj. | 解释学的；诠释学的 | Relating to the branch of knowledge that deals with interpretation, especially of the Bible or literary texts.  It emphasizes the interpreter's role in understanding the meaning. | The hermeneutical approach to the text revealed new insights.  解释文本的诠释学方法揭示了新的见解。 | interpretative, exegetical |  uninterpretable  |  adj.
