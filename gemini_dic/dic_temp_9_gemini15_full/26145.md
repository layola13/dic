busto:/ˈbʌstoʊ/| n.|半身像，胸像|A sculpted or painted representation of a person from the chest up.  |例句：The museum houses a magnificent busto of Julius Caesar.  博物馆收藏着一尊朱利叶斯·凯撒的精美半身像。|例句：She commissioned a busto of her late husband. 她委托制作了她已故丈夫的半身像。|近义词：bust, portrait (upper body)|反义词：full-length portrait|名词
