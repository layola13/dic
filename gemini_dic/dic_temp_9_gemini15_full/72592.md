fly-by:/ˈflaɪˌbaɪ/| n. |飞越；快速浏览；短暂访问 | A fly-by is a quick flight past something, often for observation or reconnaissance.  It can also refer to a brief visit or a cursory examination. |例句：The pilot performed a spectacular fly-by of the stadium. 飞行员在体育场进行了壮观的飞越表演。


例句：We only had a fly-by of the museum; we didn't have time for a proper visit. 我们只是匆匆参观了一下博物馆，没有时间好好参观。|近义词：flyover, quick look, brief visit |反义词：in-depth study, thorough examination |名词

The plane performs a fly-by every day. = 飞机每天都进行一次飞越表演。
He does a fly-by of the project reports. = 他快速浏览一下项目报告。

一般过去时:
The fighter jet executed a daring fly-by. = 战斗机进行了大胆的飞越表演。
She did a fly-by of the data before the presentation. = 她在演示文稿之前快速浏览了一下数据。

现在完成时:
The team has completed several fly-bys of the affected area. = 团队已经完成了受灾地区几次飞越。

过去完成时:

She had done a fly-by of the documents before the meeting. = 她在会议之前已经快速浏览了文件。

虚拟语气:
If the weather permitted, we would have done a fly-by. = 如果天气允许，我们会进行飞越。
I wish I had done a fly-by of the market research before investing. = 我希望在投资之前快速浏览过市场调研。
