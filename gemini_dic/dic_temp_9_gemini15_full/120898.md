miscalculation:/ˌmɪsˌkælkjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |误算；错误的计算；错误估计 |An incorrect calculation or judgment.  It refers to a mistake made in calculating something, leading to an inaccurate result or a wrong decision. |Example: The project's failure was due to a serious miscalculation of costs.  这个项目的失败是由于严重低估了成本造成的。

The company's miscalculation of the market demand led to significant losses. 公司对市场需求的错误估计导致了巨大的损失。|近义词：error, mistake, blunder, misjudgment |反义词：accurate calculation, correct assessment
