metempsychosis:/ˌmɛtɛmˈsaɪkoʊsɪs/| n. |灵魂转世；轮回|The belief or doctrine that the soul, after death, passes into another body.  This concept is central to many religions and philosophies, particularly those originating in Eastern cultures.  It often involves a cycle of birth, death, and rebirth, with the soul's experiences in past lives influencing its future incarnations.|Example:  Many ancient cultures believed in metempsychosis, believing their souls would be reborn into new bodies.  许多古代文化相信灵魂转世，认为他们的灵魂会转世到新的身体里。|近义词：reincarnation, transmigration|反义词：annihilation, extinction|名词
