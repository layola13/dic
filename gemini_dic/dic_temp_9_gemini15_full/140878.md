on-the-track-of:/ˌɒn ðə træk ɒv/|/ˌɑːn ðə træk əv/| adj. phrase|追踪；追查；调查|This phrase describes the state of being in pursuit of something, typically a person or a piece of information. It implies a focused and determined effort to find or discover the target. |The police are on the track of the escaped prisoner.  警察正在追捕逃犯。

The detective is on the track of a major drug operation.  侦探正在调查一个大型贩毒集团。

|近义词：pursuing, investigating, searching for, hunting down |反义词：losing, abandoning, giving up
