evolved:/ɪˈvɒlvd/ | /ɪˈvɑːlvd/ | v. |进化；发展；演变 | To undergo gradual development, typically from a simple to a more complex form.  |例句：Humans evolved from apes. 人类从猿类进化而来。 |例句：The company has evolved significantly over the past decade. 这家公司在过去十年中发展显著。|近义词：developed, progressed, advanced |反义词：degenerated, regressed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The species evolves slowly over time. = 物种随着时间的推移缓慢进化。
He evolves his strategies continuously. = 他不断地改进他的策略。


一般过去时:
The bird evolved from a reptile. = 鸟类由爬行动物进化而来。
The plan evolved over several meetings. = 这个计划在几次会议中逐渐完善。


现在完成时:
The technology has evolved significantly in recent years. = 近年来技术已经有了显著发展。
The situation has evolved since yesterday. = 自从昨天以来，情况已经发生了变化。


过去完成时:
The theory had evolved before the publication of the book. = 在这本书出版之前，这个理论就已经发展成熟了。
By that point, their relationship had evolved into a deep friendship. = 到那时，他们的关系已经发展成深厚的友谊。


将来时:
The company will evolve its product line next year. = 公司明年将改进其产品线。
We expect the situation to evolve in the coming weeks. = 我们预计这种情况将在未来几周内有所发展。


虚拟语气:
If the situation had evolved differently, the outcome would have been different. = 如果情况发展不同，结果也会不同。
I wish the technology had evolved faster. = 我希望技术发展得更快。


被动语态:
The design was evolved over many years. = 这个设计经过多年的改进。
The system is being evolved to meet new requirements. = 该系统正在改进以满足新的需求。


疑问句:
How did the universe evolve? = 宇宙是如何演化的？
Has the company's strategy evolved recently? = 公司的战略最近有变化吗？


否定句:
The plan did not evolve as expected. = 计划没有按预期发展。
The species hasn't evolved much in the past million years. = 在过去的百万年中，该物种的变化不大。


PHRASE->
evolved from = 源于...
evolved into = 演变成...
evolved over time = 随着时间的推移而发展
evolved a new strategy = 制定了一个新的策略
evolved a new method = 发展了一种新方法
