noddingly:/ˈnɒdɪŋli/ | adv. | 点头地；表示赞同地 |  In a way that shows agreement or understanding by nodding.  It describes an action or manner of speaking that indicates assent or agreement through nodding. | He nodded his head approvingly. 他点头表示赞同。She listened noddingly to the story. 她听得频频点头。|  affirmatively, approvingly, assenting | disapprovingly, dissenting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nods approvingly. 他赞许地点头。
She listens noddingly to the teacher. 她点头听着老师讲课。


一般过去时:
He nodded his head approvingly yesterday. 他昨天点头表示赞同。
She listened noddingly to the music. 她一边点头一边听音乐。


现在完成时:
He has nodded his head in agreement many times. 他已经多次点头表示同意。
She has listened noddingly to all my complaints. 她耐心地听我倾诉所有抱怨。


过去完成时:
He had already nodded in agreement before I finished speaking. 我还没说完话他就已经点头同意了。
She had listened noddingly throughout the entire meeting. 整个会议期间她一直都在点头倾听。


将来时:
He will nod approvingly when he hears the news. 他听到这个消息会赞许地点头。
She will listen noddingly while we explain the plan. 我们解释计划的时候，她会点头听着。


虚拟语气:
If he understood, he would nod approvingly. 如果他理解了，他会赞许地点头。
If she had been paying attention, she would have listened noddingly. 如果她当时注意听了，她就会点头听着。


被动语态:
(This adverb is rarely used in passive voice.  A passive construction would be awkward.)


疑问句:
(This adverb doesn't typically form a question.)


否定句:
He didn't nod approvingly; he looked unconvinced. 他没有赞许地点头；他看起来不太相信。
She didn't listen noddingly; she seemed quite bored. 她并没有点头表示认同；她看起来很无聊。
