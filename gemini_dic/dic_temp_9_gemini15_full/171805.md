punctuating:/ˈpʌŋktʃueɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 加标点符号；强调；使显著 |  The act of adding punctuation marks to written text to clarify meaning and improve readability.  It can also mean to interrupt or emphasize something. |


例句：

She was punctuating her speech with dramatic pauses. = 她用戏剧性的停顿来强调她的演讲。

The sudden rain punctuated the otherwise sunny day. = 突如其来的雨水使原本阳光明媚的一天变得有所不同。


近义词：interspersing, interrupting, emphasizing


反义词：omission


词性：动词
