northman:/ˈnɔːrθmæn/| n. |北欧人，斯堪的纳维亚人 |A Northman is a person from the Scandinavian countries, especially a Viking.  The term often evokes images of seafaring warriors and explorers from the Viking Age. |例句：The Northman sailed across the stormy seas.  北欧人乘船横渡暴风雨的海面。


例句：Many historical accounts detail the raids of Northmen on coastal settlements.  许多历史记载详细描述了北欧人对沿海定居点的袭击。


近义词：Scandinavian, Viking (in the context of a warrior)


反义词：Southerner (in a broad geographical sense)


词性：名词
