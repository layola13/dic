prereceiving: /ˌpriːrɪˈsiːvɪŋ/ | v. | 预先接收；提前收到 |  The act of receiving something before it is officially scheduled or expected.  This word is a relatively rare and less common word, often used in specific contexts relating to logistics, inventory management, or data processing. |


例句：

The warehouse implemented a prereceiving system to streamline the inbound process. = 仓库实施了预先接收系统以简化入库流程。

The company's prereceiving procedures ensure that all incoming shipments are properly documented before arrival. = 公司的预先接收程序确保所有到货货物在到达之前都得到妥善记录。


近义词：pre-receiving, advance receiving, early receiving


反义词：post-receiving, delayed receiving
