frizzles:/ˈfrɪzəlz/| n. v.| 卷曲；(头发)蓬松；|To make something curly or to become curly, especially hair.|The wind frizzled her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。|Her hair frizzled in the humidity.  她的头发在潮湿的天气里蓬松起来。|curls, crisps|straight, smooth|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat frizzles its fur when it's scared. = 猫害怕时会竖起毛来。
She often frizzles her hair with a curling iron. = 她经常用卷发器卷头发。


一般过去时:
The wool frizzled when it was exposed to the flame. = 羊毛接触火焰时卷曲了。
He frizzled the eggs until they were crispy. = 他把鸡蛋煎至酥脆。


现在完成时:
The sun has frizzled the paint on the car. = 太阳晒得汽车漆都卷曲了。
I have never frizzled my hair before. = 我以前从未烫过卷发。


过去完成时:
The heat had frizzled the leaves before the rain came. = 下雨前，高温已经把树叶烤卷了。
She had frizzled her hair for the special occasion. = 她为了特别的场合烫了卷发。


将来时:
The intense heat will frizzle the crops. = 强烈的热浪会把庄稼烤焦。
I will frizzle my hair tomorrow for the party. = 我明天要为了聚会烫卷头发。


虚拟语气:
If I had frizzled my hair, I would have looked different. = 如果我烫了卷发，我会看起来不一样。
I wish my hair would frizzle naturally. = 我希望我的头发能自然卷曲。


被动语态:
The fabric was frizzled by the heat of the iron. = 布料被熨斗的热度烫卷了。
His hair was frizzled by the wind. = 他的头发被风吹乱了。


疑问句:
Does the heat frizzle the plastic? = 高温会使塑料卷曲吗？
Will you frizzle your hair for the wedding? = 你会在婚礼上烫卷头发吗？


否定句:
The iron didn't frizzle the fabric. = 熨斗没有把布料烫卷。
I don't want to frizzle my hair today. = 我今天不想烫卷头发。


PHRASE->
frizzle out: (Informal) to stop working; to fail. 停止工作；失败
frizzle up: to become crispy or curled.变得酥脆或卷曲
heat frizzles: 热量使…卷曲
frizzle and sizzle:  发出咝咝的响声并卷曲(通常指食物)
frizzle away:逐渐消失；逐渐减少
