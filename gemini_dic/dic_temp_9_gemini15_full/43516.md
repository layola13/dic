craves:/kreɪvz/| vt.|渴望，热切地想要|To have a strong desire or longing for something.|例句：He craves the taste of his mother's cooking. 他渴望吃到他母亲做的菜。|例句：She craves excitement and adventure. 她渴望刺激和冒险。|近义词：yearns, longs for, desires|反义词：dislikes, abhors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He craves chocolate every day. = 他每天都渴望巧克力。
She craves attention from her friends. = 她渴望得到朋友们的关注。

一般过去时:
I craved a cold drink after my workout. = 锻炼后我渴望喝一杯冷饮。
They craved peace and quiet after the hectic week. = 忙碌的一周后，他们渴望平静与安宁。

现在完成时:
He has craved freedom since he was a child. = 从小他就渴望自由。
She has craved recognition for her hard work. = 她一直渴望得到对她辛勤工作的认可。

过去完成时:
I had craved a vacation for months before I finally booked one. = 在我最终预订假期之前，我已经渴望度假好几个月了。
They had craved a change in their lives for years. = 他们多年来一直渴望改变生活。

将来时:
I will crave comfort food when I am feeling stressed. = 当我感到压力大的时候，我会渴望吃安慰食物。
She will crave adventure as she gets older. = 随着年龄的增长，她会渴望冒险。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would crave success more than anything. = 如果我是他，我会比任何事情都渴望成功。
I wish I didn't crave sugary drinks so much. = 我希望我不那么渴望含糖饮料。

被动语态:  (The verb "crave" is rarely used in the passive voice.)
His attention was craved by everyone in the room. = 房间里的每个人都渴望他的关注。(This sounds unnatural)


疑问句:
Does he crave spicy food? = 他渴望吃辛辣食物吗？
What does she crave most in life? = 她一生中最渴望的是什么？

否定句:
He doesn't crave fame or fortune. = 他不渴望名利。
She doesn't crave sweets anymore. = 她不再渴望甜食了。


PHRASE->
crave for sth = 渴望某物
crave something intensely = 强烈渴望某物
crave attention = 渴望关注
crave excitement = 渴望刺激
crave solitude = 渴望独处
