nonreciprocals:/ˌnɒnˌrɛsɪˈprɒkəlz/ | n. |非互惠关系，单向关系 |Things that are not reciprocal; relationships or actions where one party gives or does something without receiving something comparable in return.  It often implies an imbalance of power or obligation. | Example: The relationship between the two countries was characterized by nonreciprocals, with one consistently providing aid while the other offered little in return.  这个两个国家之间的关系以非互惠为特征，一方持续提供援助，而另一方几乎没有回报。 |近义词：one-sided relationships, unilateral actions, unbalanced exchanges |反义词：reciprocals, mutual relationships, balanced exchanges | 名词
