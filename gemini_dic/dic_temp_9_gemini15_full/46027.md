cybersquatter:/ˈsaɪbəˌskwɒtər/ | n. |网络域名抢注者 |A person who registers domain names (especially those that are valuable or well-known) in the hope of selling them at a profit to the rightful owners or others.  | Example:  He's a cybersquatter who registered the domain name "applecomputers.com" hoping to sell it to Apple. 他是一个网络域名抢注者，他注册了域名“applecomputers.com”，希望能卖给苹果公司。 |近义词：domain name pirate, internet squatter |反义词：N/A
