pneumatically: /njuːˈmætɪkli/ | adj. | 气动的；用压缩空气操作的 | Operated by compressed air.  | The door opened pneumatically.  门是气动开启的。 The factory uses pneumatically powered tools.  这家工厂使用气动工具。 |  air-powered, compressed-air | manually operated, mechanically operated | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system operates pneumatically. = 这个系统是气动的。
The machine functions pneumatically. = 这台机器用压缩空气操作。


一般过去时:
The valve was operated pneumatically. = 这个阀门是气动操作的。
The drill was pneumatically powered. = 钻头是用压缩空气驱动的。


现在完成时:
They have pneumatically controlled the process. = 他们已经用压缩空气控制了这个过程。
We have installed a pneumatically driven pump. = 我们安装了一个气动泵。


过去完成时:
The system had been pneumatically controlled before the failure. = 系统在故障前一直是气动控制的。
The workers had already pneumatically tested the equipment. = 工人们已经对设备进行了气动测试。


将来时:
The new factory will use pneumatically operated machinery. = 新工厂将使用气动机械。
We will pneumatically inflate the tires. = 我们将用压缩空气给轮胎充气。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
pneumatic  源自希腊语  pneuma (πνεῦμα)  meaning "breath, spirit, wind".  "-ic" 是形容词后缀， "-ally" 是副词后缀。 所以pneumatically 表示“气动地”。
