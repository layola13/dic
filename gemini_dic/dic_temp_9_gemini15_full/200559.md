slathers:/ˈslæðərz/| vt.|涂抹，乱涂；大量使用|to spread (something, especially a viscous substance) thickly over a surface.  It also implies doing something excessively or liberally.|例句：She slathered butter on her toast. 她把黄油厚厚地涂在吐司上。

She slathered sunscreen all over her body before going to the beach. 她去海滩前在全身涂抹了防晒霜。|近义词：smear, spread, daub|反义词：dab, sprinkle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slathers peanut butter on his sandwich. = 他把花生酱涂在三明治上。
She slathers lotion on her dry skin. = 她把乳液涂在干燥的皮肤上。

一般过去时:
I slathered paint on the canvas. = 我把颜料涂在画布上。
They slathered the cake with icing. = 他们用糖霜厚厚地涂抹蛋糕。

现在完成时:
He has slathered the walls with wallpaper paste. = 他已经用壁纸浆糊涂抹了墙壁。
She has slathered her lips with lip balm. = 她已经用润唇膏厚厚地涂抹了嘴唇。

过去完成时:

She had slathered the cookies with chocolate before serving them. = 她上菜之前已经用巧克力厚厚地涂抹了饼干。
