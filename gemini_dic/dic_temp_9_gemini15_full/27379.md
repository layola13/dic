came-in-for:/keɪmˈɪnfɔːr/| phrasal verb|招致；遭受；引起；获得|This phrase means to receive something, usually something negative such as criticism, blame, or attack. It can also mean to be subjected to something or to be the recipient of something.|例句：His new policy came in for a lot of criticism.  他的新政策招致了很多批评。

例句：The company came in for heavy criticism after the accident. 事故发生后，该公司受到了严厉批评。


近义词：received, attracted, incurred, underwent|反义词：avoided, escaped, evaded|词性:phrasal verb
