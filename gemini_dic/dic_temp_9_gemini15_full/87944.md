higgledy-piggledy: /ˈhɪɡəldiˈpɪɡəldi/ | adj. | 杂乱的；乱七八糟的；混乱的 |  In a confused or disordered manner; haphazardly.  The word suggests a chaotic jumble, lacking order or system. |

例句：

The toys were scattered higgledy-piggledy across the floor. = 玩具散落在地板上，乱七八糟的。

The papers lay higgledy-piggledy on the desk. = 文件杂乱地堆在桌子上。


近义词：jumbled, chaotic, disordered, messy, muddled, helter-skelter, topsy-turvy


反义词：orderly, organized, neat, tidy, systematic


词源:

The origin of "higgledy-piggledy" is uncertain, but it's believed to be a reduplicative compound, meaning it's formed by repeating a similar-sounding element.  The individual components, "higgledy" and "piggledy," don't have independent established meanings.  The word likely arose from onomatopoeia (words imitating sounds) or from a playful combination of sounds evoking the feeling of disorder.  The first recorded use dates back to the 16th century.
