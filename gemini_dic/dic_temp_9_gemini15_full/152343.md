paunch:/pɔːntʃ/| n.|大肚子，腹部；隆起的肚子|A paunch is a large, protruding belly.  It often suggests overweight or obesity. |例句：He had a considerable paunch hanging over his belt. 他有一个相当大的肚子，挂在腰带上。

例句：The old man's paunch jiggled as he walked.  老人走路时肚子晃动。


近义词：potbelly, belly, abdomen


反义词：flat stomach, toned abdomen


词源：源自古法语 *panche,  意为“腹部”，最终源自拉丁语 *pancia。
