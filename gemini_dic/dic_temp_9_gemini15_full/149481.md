padlock:/ˈpædlɒk/|/ˈpædlɑːk/| n. |挂锁，密码锁 |A padlock is a detachable lock having a shackle that passes through a staple or loop and is secured by a hasp.  It's typically used to secure doors, gates, or other objects. |例句：He used a padlock to secure the shed. 他用挂锁锁上了棚屋。|例句：The padlock was rusted and difficult to open.  这把挂锁生锈了，很难打开。|近义词：lock, clasp |反义词：unlock, open
