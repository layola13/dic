fosterage:/ˈfɒstərɪdʒ/| n. |寄养；抚养；保育；养育|The act of fostering; the state of being fostered.  It refers to the care and upbringing of a child by someone other than their biological parents, typically on a temporary basis. |例句：The fosterage of the child was arranged by the social services.  寄养孩子的安排是由社会服务机构做出的。


例句：The long-term fosterage of abandoned animals requires significant resources. 长期收养被遗弃的动物需要大量资源。


近义词：fostering, care, upbringing, nurture


反义词：abandonment, neglect


词性：名词
