over-claim:/ˈoʊvərˌkleɪm/| vt.|过分声称；夸大；索赔过高|To claim or assert something in excess of what is true or justified.  It often implies exaggeration or a dishonest attempt to gain something.|例句：He over-claimed his experience on his resume. 他在简历上夸大了自己的经验。|例句：The insurance company accused him of over-claiming the damages to his car. 保险公司指控他夸大了汽车损失。|近义词：exaggerate, inflate, overstate, hype|反义词：understate, downplay


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He over-claims his abilities in interviews. = 他在面试中夸大自己的能力。
They over-claim the benefits of their product. = 他们夸大了他们产品的益处。


一般过去时:
She over-claimed the amount of work she had completed. = 她夸大了她已经完成的工作量。
The contractor over-claimed the costs of the project. = 承包商夸大了项目的成本。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
