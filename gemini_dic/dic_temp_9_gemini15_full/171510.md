puisny:/ˈpjuːzni/| adj.| 年少的；下级的；次要的 |  Of or relating to a younger or less important person or thing; junior. The word often implies a lack of experience or authority.  | Example: He was a puisny clerk in the law firm, handling only minor tasks. 他是律师事务所里一个年轻的职员，只处理一些小事。 |近义词：junior, subordinate, minor, lesser | 反义词：senior, superior, major, principal |
