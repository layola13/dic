oversleeping:/ˌoʊvərˈsliːpɪŋ/| v. |睡过头，睡懒觉|The act of sleeping beyond one's intended wake-up time.  It often results in being late for work or other commitments.|例句：Oversleeping made me miss my important meeting. 睡过头让我错过了重要的会议。

例句：I'm always late for work because of oversleeping. 我总是因为睡过头而上班迟到。


近义词：sleeping in, late rising|反义词：waking up early, getting up on time|词性：动名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is oversleeping every morning. 他每天早上都睡过头。
She often oversleeps on weekends. 她周末经常睡懒觉。


一般过去时:
I overslept this morning and missed my train. 我今天早上睡过头了，错过了火车。
They overslept yesterday and were late for school. 他们昨天睡过头了，上学迟到了。


现在完成时:
I have overslept three times this week. 这周我已经睡过头三次了。

过去完成时:
I had overslept, so I was already behind schedule. 我睡过头了，所以我已经落后于进度了。
They had overslept, causing them to miss their flight. 他们睡过头了，导致他们错过了航班。


将来时:
