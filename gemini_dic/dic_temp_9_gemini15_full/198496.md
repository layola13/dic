shrine:/ʃraɪn/ | n. |圣地，圣坛；神殿；珍藏处 | A shrine is a place that is considered sacred, often dedicated to a particular deity or person.  It can be a building, a location, or even an object. Shrines are often places of pilgrimage and worship. | Example:  Many pilgrims visit the shrine to pay their respects. 许多朝圣者前往圣地表达敬意。 |近义词：sanctuary, temple, chapel |反义词：profanation, desecration


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The shrine attracts many visitors every year. = 这个圣地每年吸引许多游客。
This shrine houses a sacred relic. = 这个神殿供奉着圣物。


一般过去时:
They visited the ancient shrine last week. = 他们上周参观了古老的圣地。
The villagers built a shrine to honor their ancestors. = 村民们建造了一座圣坛来祭祀他们的祖先。


现在完成时:
Pilgrims have visited this shrine for centuries. = 朝圣者们几个世纪以来一直参观这个圣地。
The family has maintained the shrine for generations. = 这个家族世世代代守护着这个圣地。


过去完成时:
Before the earthquake, the shrine had stood for over 500 years. = 地震前，这座圣地已经屹立了500多年。
They had already prayed at the shrine before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前，他们已经在圣地祈祷过了。


将来时:
We will visit the shrine next month. = 我们下个月将参观这座圣地。
The government will restore the damaged shrine. = 政府将修复受损的圣地。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would visit the shrine. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去参观圣地。
I wish I could visit the shrine next year. = 我希望明年能去参观圣地。


被动语态:
The shrine was built in the 17th century. = 这座圣地建于17世纪。
The shrine is being renovated at the moment. = 目前这座圣地正在翻修。


疑问句:
Where is the nearest shrine located? = 最近的圣地在哪里？
Is this shrine open to the public? = 这个圣地对公众开放吗？


否定句:
The shrine was not damaged in the fire. = 圣地没有在火灾中受损。
He did not go to the shrine that day. = 他那天没有去圣地。



PHRASE->

shrine to:  对…的象征；对…的纪念碑 (Example: The museum is a shrine to the artist's life and work. 这个博物馆是艺术家生平和作品的纪念馆。)
shrine of:  …的圣地 (Example: The mountain is a shrine of peace and tranquility.  这座山是宁静祥和的圣地。)
sacred shrine: 神圣的圣地
ancient shrine: 古老的圣地
Buddhist shrine: 佛教圣地
