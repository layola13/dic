explain:/ɪkˈspleɪn/ | vt. |解释，说明|To make something clear or easy to understand, typically by giving reasons or details.|例句：Can you explain this problem to me? 你能向我解释这个问题吗？|近义词：interpret, clarify, elucidate |反义词：confuse, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He explains the rules clearly. = 他清楚地解释了规则。
She explains the process step by step. = 她一步一步地解释了这个过程。


一般过去时:
The teacher explained the lesson carefully. = 老师仔细地讲解了课程。
He explained his absence to his boss. = 他向老板解释了他的缺席原因。


现在完成时:
I have explained this to you several times. = 我已经向你解释过好几次了。
She has explained the situation thoroughly. = 她已经彻底解释了情况。


过去完成时:
I had explained the situation before he arrived. = 在他到达之前，我已经解释了情况。
They had explained the plan to the team earlier. = 他们早些时候已经向团队解释了计划。


将来时:
I will explain it to you later. = 我稍后会向你解释。
She will explain the new policy tomorrow. = 她明天将解释新的政策。


虚拟语气:
If I had explained it better, he would have understood. = 如果我解释得更好，他就会明白了。
I wish I had explained myself more clearly. = 我希望我当时解释得更清楚一些。


被动语态:
The rules were explained to the students. = 规则向学生们解释了。
The situation will be explained in detail later. = 情况稍后将详细解释。


疑问句:
Can you explain it again? = 你能再解释一下吗？
Will you explain why you did that? = 你能解释一下你为什么那样做吗？


否定句:
I couldn't explain why I was late. = 我无法解释为什么迟到了。
He didn't explain the instructions properly. = 他没有正确地解释说明。


PHRASE->
explain oneself = 解释自己的行为
explain away = 搪塞，解释掉
explain in detail = 详细解释
explain the reason = 解释原因
to explain something to someone = 向某人解释某事
