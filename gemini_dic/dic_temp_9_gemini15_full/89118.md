home:/hoʊm/|n.|家，家乡，住所|A person's house or family; the place where one lives permanently, especially with one's family.|例句：My home is in the countryside. 我的家在乡下。|例句：She longed for the comforts of home. 她渴望家的舒适。|近义词：house, residence, dwelling|反义词：abroad, away


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I go home every day. = 我每天回家。
He lives at home with his parents. = 他和父母住在一起。

一般过去时:
We went home late last night. = 我们昨晚很晚才回家。
She stayed home all day yesterday. = 她昨天一整天都待在家里。

现在完成时:
I have been home for three hours. = 我已经在家三个小时了。
They have just arrived home. = 他们刚到家。


过去完成时:
I had already gone home when he called. = 他打电话的时候我已经回家了。
She had been home for a week before she felt better. = 她在家一个星期后才感觉好些。

将来时:
I will go home after work. = 我下班后回家。
We will be home by dinner time. = 我们晚餐前就能到家。

虚拟语气:
I wish I were home right now. = 我真希望我现在在家。
If I had known you were coming, I would have stayed home. = 如果我知道你要来，我就待在家了。

被动语态:
The house was broken into while they were away from home. = 他们不在家的时候，房子被闯入了。
The home was decorated beautifully. = 房子装饰得很漂亮。

疑问句:
Is he home yet? = 他回家了吗？
Where is your home? = 你的家在哪里？

否定句:
I am not home right now. = 我现在不在家。
He did not go home yesterday. = 他昨天没有回家。
