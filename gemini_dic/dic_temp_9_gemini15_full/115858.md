markup:/ˈmɑːrkʌp/| /ˈmɑːrkʌp/| n. |标记，加价，稿件标注|The process of adding markup to a document involves annotating it with instructions for formatting and presentation.|The markup on the item was excessive.|近义词：annotation, markdown, pricing |反义词：discount, reduction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor applies markup to the manuscript. = 编辑在稿件上添加标记。
The store adds a significant markup to their products. = 商店在其产品上加价很多。
