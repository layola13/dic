huaracho: /wɑːˈrɑːtʃoʊ/ | n. | 瓦拉乔（一种墨西哥凉鞋） | A huaracho is a type of sandal traditionally worn in Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.  They are typically made from a single piece of leather or other material, often with a woven or braided design.  They are usually simple and practical footwear. | Example:  My grandmother used to wear huaraches when she worked in the fields.  我的祖母过去在田里工作时穿瓦拉乔。


近义词: sandal,  thong sandal


反义词: boot, shoe (in the sense of being enclosed footwear)


词性: Noun
