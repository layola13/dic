re-adding:/ˌriːˈædɪŋ/| v. |重新添加，再次添加 |The act of adding something again after it has been removed or lost.  It implies a previous addition that was undone. |例句：After deleting the file by mistake, I had to re-add it to the project.  不小心删除了文件后，我不得不把它重新添加到项目中。


例句：We are re-adding the feature that users requested. 我们正在重新添加用户请求的功能。


近义词：readding,reincorporating,reintroducing


反义词：removing,deleting,subtracting


词性:动词
