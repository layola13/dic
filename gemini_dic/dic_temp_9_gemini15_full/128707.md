niece:/niːs/| n. |侄女；外甥女|A niece is the daughter of one's brother or sister, or the daughter of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law.| My niece is graduating from college this year.  我的侄女今年大学毕业。|My sister's daughter is my niece. 我的姐姐的女儿是我的侄女。|  granddaughter, granddaughter-in-law | son, nephew
