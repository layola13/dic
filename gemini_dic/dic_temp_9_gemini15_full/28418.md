carbonises:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/|/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/| vt. |使碳化；使煤化 |To convert (something) into carbon or charcoal; to become charred. The process involves the removal of volatile constituents from organic matter, leaving behind a carbon residue. |例句：The intense heat carbonised the wood.  剧烈的热量使木材碳化了。

The fire rapidly carbonised the debris. 火迅速将碎片碳化。


近义词：char, scorch, incinerate |反义词：hydrate
