scroggy:/ˈskrɒɡi/| adj.|瘦弱的；粗糙的；杂乱的；|形容植物、人或物体的状态，指其瘦弱、粗糙、不整洁或杂乱无章。It can describe a person who is thin and unhealthy, or a plant that is stunted and poorly grown. It can also describe something that is rough or uneven in texture. |例句：The scroggy dog looked like it hadn't eaten in days.  这只瘦弱的狗看起来好几天没吃东西了。

The scroggy field was overgrown with weeds.  杂草丛生的田地里长满了杂草。|近义词：gaunt, ragged, rough, unkempt, scraggly|反义词：plump, healthy, smooth, neat, well-groomed|
