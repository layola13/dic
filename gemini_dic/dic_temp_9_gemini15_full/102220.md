itchily:/ˈɪtʃɪli/| adv.| 痒痒地；焦躁不安地 | In a way that shows or suggests an itch or a feeling of restlessness or impatience.  It describes an action or state characterized by an irritating sensation or a feeling of unease.| 例句：He scratched his arm itchily. 他痒痒地抓着胳膊。  She paced the room itchily, waiting for the phone to ring. 她焦躁不安地在房间里踱步，等着电话铃响。|近义词：restlessly, impatiently, anxiously | 反义词：calmly, patiently, serenely | 副词
