custodianships:/kʌsˈtoʊdiənʃɪps/| n. |监护权；保管；管理 |The responsibilities and duties associated with guarding, protecting, and managing something or someone.  It often refers to legal guardianship of a person or property. |例句：The court awarded the custodianship of the child to its aunt. 法院将孩子的监护权判给了她的姑姑。

The museum is responsible for the custodianship of many valuable artifacts.  博物馆负责许多珍贵文物的保管。|近义词：guardianship, care, keeping, charge |反义词：neglect, abandonment
