reappropriation:/ˌriːəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 挪用；重新诠释；盗用 | The act of taking something that was originally used by one group or culture and using it for a different purpose, often by a different group or culture. This can be positive, negative, or neutral depending on the context and intent.  | Example: The band's use of Native American imagery in their music video is a controversial example of cultural reappropriation.乐队在音乐录影带中使用美洲原住民的意象，是文化挪用的一个有争议的例子。 |近义词:misappropriation, appropriation, adoption|反义词:original creation, preservation
