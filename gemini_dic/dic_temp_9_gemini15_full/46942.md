darnest:/ˈdɑːrnɪst/| adj.|该死的，最…的，极度的|Superlative form of "darn," intensifying an adjective or adverb.  It's an informal and often emphatic way to express a high degree of something.|例句：That's the darnest thing I've ever seen! = 那是我看过的最奇怪的事情！  He ran the darnest race I've ever witnessed. = 他跑了场我见过的最精彩的比赛。|近义词：most, awfully, terribly, incredibly|反义词：least, mildly, slightly
