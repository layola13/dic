corals:/ˈkɔːrəlz/| n.|珊瑚；珊瑚虫|Coral is a hard stony substance secreted by certain marine coelenterates.  It forms the skeletons of coral reefs. Corals are also the small animals that build these reefs.|例句：The Great Barrier Reef is made of billions of tiny corals.  大堡礁是由数十亿个微小的珊瑚构成。

例句：Divers were mesmerized by the vibrant colors of the corals. 潜水员被珊瑚鲜艳的颜色迷住了。


近义词：coral reefs, polyps (referring to the animals)


反义词：None directly applicable, but could consider terms like "rock" or "sand" in contrast to their living, reef-building nature.


词性：名词
