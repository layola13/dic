/dɪˈsprɛdə(r)/| n. |施肥机；撒布机；传播者|A machine used to spread fertilizer, seed, or other materials over a large area.  It can also refer to something or someone that spreads something, like a disease or information.|例句：The farmer used a dispreader to evenly distribute fertilizer across his fields.  农民用撒布机均匀地将肥料撒在田地上。

例句：The internet has become a powerful dispreader of both accurate and false information.互联网已经成为传播准确信息和虚假信息的强大工具。|近义词：spreader, distributor, disseminator |反义词：collector, concentrator
