preoccupiers:/ˌpriːˈɒkjʊpaɪərz/ | n. | 全神贯注者；使人操心的事物 | Those who are engrossed in thought or activity; things that occupy one's mind and cause worry. |  例句：The preoccupied students barely noticed the fire alarm.  全神贯注的学生几乎没有注意到火警。 The constant demands of her job were major preoccupiers for her. 她工作的持续需求是她主要操心的事。| 近义词：worriers, thinkers, concerns | 反义词：nonchalants, carefree people, distractions


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The preoccupiers constantly distract him from his work.  = 全神贯注的事物不断地分散他的注意力。
His preoccupiers are his family and his business. = 让他操心的事物是他的家庭和他的事业。


一般过去时:
Her worries were her preoccupiers yesterday. = 她的担忧是她昨天操心的事。
The students' preoccupiers were their upcoming exams. = 学生们操心的事是即将到来的考试。


现在完成时:
His financial problems have been his main preoccupiers. = 他的财务问题一直是他主要操心的事。
The project's delays have preoccupied the team. = 项目的延误使团队分心。


过去完成时:
His past failures had been preoccupiers before the interview. = 他过去的失败在面试前一直让他操心。
The team had many preoccupiers before the launch. = 团队在发布前有很多操心的事。


将来时:
The upcoming election will be a major preoccupier for the country. = 即将到来的选举将成为国家主要操心的事。
His health concerns will preoccupy him in the future. = 他的健康问题将来会让他操心。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't have so many preoccupiers, he would be more productive. = 如果他没有那么多操心的事，他会更有生产力。
I wish I didn't have so many preoccupiers weighing on my mind. = 我希望我没有那么多让我操心的烦恼。


被动语态:
He was preoccupied by his worries. = 他被他的担忧所困扰。（被动语态更侧重于“被占据”而非主动的“操心”）
The meeting was overshadowed by various preoccupiers. = 会议被各种操心的事所掩盖。（被动语态更侧重于“被占据”而非主动的“操心”）


疑问句:
What are your main preoccupiers at the moment? = 你现在主要操心什么？
What preoccupiers are affecting your performance? = 什么让你分心影响了你的表现？


否定句:
He wasn't preoccupied with trivial matters. = 他没有被琐事所困扰。
She wasn't distracted by any preoccupiers during the presentation. = 演示期间，没有任何事情分散她的注意力。
