misplay:/ˈmɪsˌpleɪ/| v.|误传，误用；打错；(体育)失误|To play incorrectly or unsuccessfully; to make a mistake in playing a game or a musical instrument; to handle or use something incorrectly.|例句：He misplayed the crucial pass, leading to a goal for the opposing team. 他关键时刻传球失误，导致对方进球。

例句：The musician misplayed a note during the concert, but quickly recovered.  音乐家在音乐会上弹错了一个音符，但他很快恢复了。


近义词：mismanage, mishandle, blunder, err, fumble|反义词：master, excel, succeed
