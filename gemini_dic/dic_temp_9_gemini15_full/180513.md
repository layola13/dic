regent:/ˈriːdʒənt/ | n. |摄政王，摄政者，校董 | A person appointed to govern a country during the minority, absence, or incapacity of the sovereign.  A member of a governing board, especially of a college or university. |例句：The regent ruled the kingdom in the young king's stead.  摄政王代理年轻国王统治王国。

The university's board of regents approved the new building plan. 大学董事会批准了新的建筑计划。 |近义词：governor, ruler, trustee |反义词：subject, subordinate
