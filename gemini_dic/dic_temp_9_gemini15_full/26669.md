cactus: /ˈkæktəs/ | n. |仙人掌| A cactus is a succulent plant that is adapted to arid conditions and typically has spines or bristles instead of leaves. Cacti are known for their ability to store water in their stems and roots. They are found in deserts and other dry regions around the world.  | Example: The desert landscape was dotted with various species of cacti.  沙漠景观点缀着各种仙人掌。  |近义词：succulent (in some contexts)|反义词：无
