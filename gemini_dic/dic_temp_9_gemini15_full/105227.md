keystroking:/ˈkiːˌstroʊkɪŋ/ | n. |按键，击键；键盘输入|The act of pressing keys on a keyboard, often rapidly or repeatedly.  It can refer to the act itself or the resulting data.|例句：The keystroking was so fast, I could barely see what he was typing.  按键速度太快，我几乎看不清他在打什么。

例句：The hacker gained access by monitoring the keystroking patterns of the employee. 黑客通过监控员工的按键模式获得了访问权限。


近义词：typing, key pressing, inputting | 反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable) | 词性：Noun
