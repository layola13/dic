scarce:/skɛərs/ | adj. | 稀少的；缺乏的；不足的 |  Scarce means something is in short supply; there is not enough of it. It often refers to resources or commodities. | Example:  Food was scarce during the war.  战争期间食物匮乏。

| Example:  Job opportunities are scarce in this region.  这个地区就业机会稀少。|近义词：rare, uncommon, infrequent, limited |反义词：abundant, plentiful, ample, copious |词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The resources are scarce in this area. = 这个地区的资源匮乏。
Water is scarce in the desert. = 沙漠里缺水。


一般过去时:
Goods were scarce during the economic crisis. = 经济危机期间商品匮乏。
Fuel was scarce last winter. = 去年冬天燃料短缺。


现在完成时:
Resources have become increasingly scarce. = 资源变得越来越稀缺。
Opportunities have become scarce in recent years. = 近年来机会变得稀少。


过去完成时:
They had already exhausted the scarce resources. = 他们已经耗尽了稀少的资源。
The situation had become scarce before we realized it. = 在我们意识到之前，情况已经变得紧张了。


将来时:
Water will become increasingly scarce in the future. = 未来水资源将越来越匮乏。
Resources will be scarce in the coming decades. = 未来几十年资源将会匮乏。


虚拟语气:
If resources were not scarce, we wouldn't have this problem. = 如果资源不匮乏，我们就不会有这个问题。
If only time were not scarce, we could do more. = 要是时间不匮乏就好了，我们就能做更多的事。


被动语态:
The materials were found to be scarce. = 发现这些材料稀缺。
The resources are considered scarce by many experts. = 许多专家认为这些资源稀缺。


疑问句:
Are resources scarce in your country? = 你们国家资源匮乏吗？
Is clean water scarce in this region? = 这个地区清洁水资源匮乏吗？


否定句:
Resources are not scarce in that area. = 那个地区资源并不匮乏。
Food is not scarce in this city. = 这个城市食物并不匮乏。



PHRASE->
