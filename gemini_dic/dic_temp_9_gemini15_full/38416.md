concert-goer:/ˈkɒnsətˌɡəʊər/| n. |音乐会观众，听众 |A concert-goer is a person who attends concerts.  This word implies a regular or habitual attendee, someone who frequents concerts. |Example: The concert-goer rushed to buy tickets as soon as they went on sale.  这位音乐会观众一放票就赶紧去买了。|近义词：concert attendee, music lover, audience member |反义词：performer, musician
