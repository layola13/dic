self-committal:/ˌsɛlf kəˈmɪtəl/| n.|自我约束；自我承诺；自行承担责任|The act of committing oneself to something; a pledge or promise made to oneself. It implies a strong sense of responsibility and dedication to a particular course of action or belief.  |例句：Her self-committal to the project ensured its success. 她对项目的自我承诺确保了它的成功。

例句：His self-committal to daily meditation brought him inner peace. 他对每日冥想的自我承诺给他带来了内心的平静。


近义词：self-discipline, dedication, commitment, pledge, resolve


反义词：irresponsibility, recklessness, procrastination, indecision


词性:名词
