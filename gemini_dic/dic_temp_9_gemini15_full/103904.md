judder:/ˈdʒʌdər/| v. n.|震动；抖动；颠簸|To shake or vibrate violently and irregularly.  A judder is a violent, irregular shaking or vibration.|The old car juddered violently as we drove over the rough road. 这辆旧车在我们驶过崎岖不平的道路时剧烈地抖动。 The engine started to judder, then died. 引擎开始抖动，然后熄火了。|shake, vibrate, tremble|smooth, steady|动词，名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The engine judders constantly. = 引擎不断地抖动。
The train judders to a halt. = 火车抖动着停了下来。

一般过去时:
The car juddered as it went over the pothole. = 汽车经过坑洼时颠簸了一下。
The ground juddered from the explosion. = 地面因爆炸而震动。

现在完成时:
The machine has been juddering all morning. = 这台机器整个上午都在抖动。
The building has juddered slightly since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，建筑物轻微震动。

过去完成时:
The bridge had juddered alarmingly before it collapsed. = 桥梁在倒塌之前已经发出令人担忧的震动。
The plane had juddered violently during the turbulence. = 飞机在颠簸中剧烈震动。

将来时:
The car will judder if you don't get it fixed. = 如果你不修理，汽车将会抖动。
The building will judder during the earthquake. = 建筑物在地震期间会震动。


虚拟语气:
If the engine juddered again, I would stop driving. = 如果引擎再次抖动，我就会停止驾驶。
I wish the train wouldn't judder so much. = 我希望火车不要抖动得这么厉害。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
