fisticuffs:/ˈfɪstɪkʌfs/| n. |拳打脚踢，斗殴 |A fight involving fists.  It typically implies a relatively informal and perhaps unsophisticated brawl, rather than a formal boxing match. |例句：The argument escalated into fisticuffs.  这场争论升级成了拳打脚踢。|例句：The two men engaged in a brief but vigorous bout of fisticuffs. 这两个人进行了一场短暂而激烈的拳打脚踢。|近义词：brawling, fighting, scuffling, a free-for-all |反义词：peace, harmony, agreement
