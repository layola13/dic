m-learning:/ˈɛmˈlɜːrnɪŋ/ | n. |移动学习，无线学习 |Mobile learning, also known as m-learning, refers to learning that takes place using portable computing devices such as mobile phones, tablet PCs, and PDAs. It leverages the capabilities of mobile devices to access learning resources, participate in learning activities, and collaborate with others.  | Example: Many universities now offer m-learning resources to supplement traditional classroom teaching. 许多大学现在提供移动学习资源来补充传统的课堂教学。|近义词：mobile learning, wireless learning |反义词：traditional learning, classroom learning
