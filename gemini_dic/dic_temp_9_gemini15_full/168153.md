pro-Brazilian: /ˌproʊ ˈbrəˈzɪliən/ | adj. | 亲巴西的；支持巴西的 |  Supporting or favoring Brazil or Brazilian interests.  The term indicates a positive stance towards Brazil, its people, culture, or policies.  It's often used in political or social contexts to describe individuals, groups, or policies that are in favor of Brazil.


例句：

He holds pro-Brazilian views on trade agreements. = 他对贸易协定持有亲巴西的观点。

The pro-Brazilian lobby actively campaigned for the new legislation. = 亲巴西的游说团体积极为新法案奔走。


近义词：Brazilian-supporting, Brazil-friendly

反义词：anti-Brazilian


词性：形容词
