dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n.|木销，榫；圆木钉|A dowel is a pin, usually of wood, used to join pieces of wood together.  It's cylindrical and fits into pre-drilled holes.|例句：The carpenter used wooden dowels to secure the joints. 木匠用木销固定接缝。

The furniture was assembled using dowels and glue. 这件家具是用木销和胶水组装的。|近义词：peg, pin, tenon|反义词:None.
