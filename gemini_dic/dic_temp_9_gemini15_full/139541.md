obtrudes:/əbˈtruːdz/| v.|突出；强加；闯入|To push oneself or something forward in an unwelcome or intrusive way; to become noticeable in an unwelcome way.|例句：His loud laughter obtruded on the quiet of the library.  他的大声笑声扰乱了图书馆的宁静。|例句：Don't obtrude your opinions on others. 不要把你的意见强加于人。|近义词：intrude, impose, thrust, project|反义词：withdraw, retreat, recede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obtrudes his opinions into every conversation. = 他在每次谈话中都强加自己的意见。
The building obtrudes on the landscape. = 这座建筑物破坏了风景。


一般过去时:
She obtruded herself into their private lives. = 她闯入了他们的私生活。
The noise obtruded upon the peaceful atmosphere. = 噪音扰乱了宁静的氛围。


现在完成时:
His arrogance has obtruded itself upon everyone. = 他的傲慢已经强加于所有人。
The new regulations have obtruded on our freedom. = 新规章限制了我们的自由。


过去完成时:
He had obtruded his views before anyone else had a chance to speak. = 他在其他人有机会发言之前就强加了自己的观点。
The unexpected guest had obtruded upon their quiet evening. = 不速之客扰乱了他们宁静的夜晚。


将来时:
The new factory will obtrude on the local environment. = 新工厂将破坏当地环境。
I fear that his interference will obtrude upon our plans. = 我担心他的干涉会影响我们的计划。


虚拟语气:
If he obtruded again, I would ask him to leave. = 如果他再次强加于人，我会请他离开。
I wish he wouldn't obtrude his unsolicited advice. = 我希望他不必强加他多余的建议。


被动语态:
His ideas were obtruded upon the group. = 他的想法被强加于该小组。
Their privacy was obtruded upon by the paparazzi. = 他们的隐私被狗仔队侵犯了。


疑问句:
Does his behaviour obtrude on others? = 他的行为是否冒犯了别人？
Why does this problem obtrude itself upon us now? = 为什么这个问题现在困扰着我们？


否定句:
He did not obtrude his presence on the meeting. = 他没有在会议上强加自己的存在。
They tried not to obtrude their grief on others. = 他们尽量不向别人表现他们的悲伤。


PHRASE->
obtrude oneself = 擅自介入
obtrude upon = 打扰；侵犯
obtrude into = 闯入；介入
obtrude on = 妨碍；打扰
obtrude one's opinion = 强加意见
