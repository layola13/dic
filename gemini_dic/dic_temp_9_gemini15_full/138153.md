northing:/ˈnɔːrθɪŋ/| n. |向北的距离，北向分量|The northward distance of a point from a reference line, typically used in surveying and mapping. It represents the distance north of a chosen origin.  |Example: The northing of the survey marker was recorded as 1234.5 meters.  测量标记的北向分量记录为1234.5米。|近义词：northward displacement, north coordinate|反义词：southing
