contradictorily:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktərɪli/|/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktərili/| adv.|矛盾地，自相矛盾地|In a way that is self-contradictory or inconsistent.|His statements were contradictorily vague and specific at the same time.  他的陈述同时含糊不清又具体明确，自相矛盾。

The evidence presented contradictorily supports and refutes the defendant's claim. 提出的证据自相矛盾地支持和反驳了被告的声明。


近义词：inconsistently, paradoxically, contrarily

反义词：consistently, logically, coherently

词性:副词
