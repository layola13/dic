heathenized:/ˈhiːðənʌɪzd/| vt. |使异教化；使野蛮化；使粗俗化 |To convert or influence someone to paganism or to adopt pagan practices or beliefs; to make something crude, uncivilized, or barbarous.  |例句：The invading army heathenized the conquered land.  侵略军使被征服的土地野蛮化了。

The local culture was heathenized by the influx of foreign influences. 当地文化因涌入的外来影响而变得粗俗化了。

|近义词：paganize, barbarize, brutalize |反义词：civilize, refine, cultivate |动词
