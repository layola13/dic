resembles:/rɪˈzɛmbəlz/| v.|类似，像，酷似|To be similar or alike in appearance, character, or quality.|The child resembles his father. 这个孩子长得像他父亲。|近义词：resembles, is similar to, is like, looks like|反义词：differs from, is unlike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting resembles a masterpiece. = 这幅画很像一件杰作。
His writing style resembles that of Hemingway. = 他的写作风格类似于海明威。

一般过去时:
The building resembled a castle. = 这栋建筑物像一座城堡。
The situation resembled a disaster. = 情况类似于一场灾难。

现在完成时:
The new model has resembled the old one in many ways. = 新型号在许多方面与旧型号相似。
His behavior has resembled that of a child recently. = 他最近的行为像个孩子。

过去完成时:
The evidence resembled what we had expected before the trial. = 在审判之前，证据与我们预期的相似。
The situation resembled what we had feared several years ago. = 情况类似于几年前我们所担心的情况。

将来时:
The new product will resemble the old one in its functionality. = 新产品的功能将与旧产品相似。
The finished project will resemble the initial sketch. = 完成的项目将与最初的草图相似。

虚拟语气:
If the painting resembled a Monet, it would be worth a fortune. = 如果这幅画像莫奈的画作，它将价值连城。
If the suspect resembled the description, he would be arrested immediately. = 如果嫌疑犯符合描述，他将立即被逮捕。

被动语态:
The two cars are resembled in design. = 这两辆车的车型很相似。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The painting is resembled by many others of that style. = 这幅画与许多其他同风格的画相似。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)

疑问句:
Does the new design resemble the old one? = 新设计与旧设计相似吗？
Does his behavior resemble that of a responsible adult? = 他的行为像个负责任的成年人吗？

否定句:
The two houses do not resemble each other at all. = 这两所房子一点也不相似。
The witness's statement does not resemble the actual events. = 目击者的证词与实际事件不符。


PHRASE->
resemble in appearance = 在外观上相似
resemble each other = 相互相似
resemble closely = 非常相似
resemble in character = 性格相似
strongly resemble = 酷似
