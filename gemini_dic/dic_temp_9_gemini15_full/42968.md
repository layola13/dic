covert:/ˈkʌvərt/| adj. |秘密的，隐蔽的；暗中的| hidden, disguised, or secret;  secretive;  (of an operation) designed to be kept secret. |例句：The covert operation was successful.  这次秘密行动很成功。|例句：He made a covert attempt to steal the money. 他暗中企图偷钱。|近义词：secret, clandestine, stealthy, hidden |反义词：overt, open, public, revealed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The agency conducts covert operations. = 该机构进行秘密行动。
He makes covert observations. = 他进行秘密观察。

一般过去时:
They conducted a covert investigation. = 他们进行了一项秘密调查。
She made a covert comment. = 她发表了一条隐晦的评论。

现在完成时:
The government has engaged in many covert activities. = 政府参与了许多秘密活动。
He has made several covert attempts to contact her. = 他多次秘密尝试联系她。


过去完成时:
They had already completed the covert mission. = 他们已经完成了秘密任务。
She had made covert preparations for the trip. = 她已为这次旅行做了秘密准备。

将来时:
We will launch a covert surveillance operation. = 我们将启动一项秘密监视行动。
They will make covert recordings. = 他们将进行秘密录音。


虚拟语气:
If they had known about the covert plan, they would have stopped it. = 如果他们知道这个秘密计划，他们就会阻止它。
I wish I had known about the covert meeting. = 我真希望我知道那个秘密会议。


被动语态:
The covert operation was carefully planned. = 秘密行动经过精心策划。
The information was obtained through covert means. = 信息是通过秘密手段获得的。


疑问句:
Is this a covert operation? = 这是秘密行动吗？
Were the meetings covert? = 会议是秘密的吗？


否定句:
The meeting was not covert; it was open to the public. = 会议并非秘密；它是公开的。
Their actions were not covert; everyone knew what they were doing. = 他们的行动并非秘密；每个人都知道他们在做什么。
