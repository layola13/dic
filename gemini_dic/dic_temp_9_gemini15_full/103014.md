Jehovah's:/dʒəˈhoʊvəz/| n.|耶和华的；耶和华见证人的|Jehovah's is the possessive form of the name Jehovah, referring to something belonging to or associated with Jehovah, the Hebrew name for God in the Old Testament.  It is also commonly used to refer to Jehovah's Witnesses, a Christian denomination.|例句：Jehovah's Witnesses are known for their door-to-door ministry. 耶和华见证人以挨家挨户传教而闻名。|近义词：God's, Yahweh's|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
This word is primarily a possessive noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It functions as an adjective modifying other nouns.  Therefore, traditional tense conjugations don't apply.  Instead, usage varies depending on the sentence structure.
