hooked:/hʊkt/ | adj. |着迷的；沉迷的；上瘾的 |  Completely captivated or addicted to something.  |例句：I'm hooked on this new TV series. 我迷上了这部新电视剧。

例句：He's completely hooked on video games. 他完全沉迷于电子游戏。


近义词：addicted, captivated, engrossed, obsessed | 反义词：uninterested, indifferent, detached | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am hooked on coffee. = 我对咖啡上瘾了。
She is hooked on that new reality show. = 她迷上了那个新的真人秀节目。


一般过去时:
I was hooked after the first episode. = 第一集之后我就迷上了。
He was hooked on gambling for years. = 他沉迷于赌博多年了。


现在完成时:
I have been hooked on this book for days. = 我迷上这本书好几天了。
She has been hooked on social media since high school. = 她从高中时代就开始沉迷于社交媒体。


过去完成时:
I had already been hooked before I even knew it. = 我甚至在意识到之前就已经上瘾了。
She had been hooked on painkillers for months before she sought help. = 在寻求帮助之前，她已经服用止痛药成瘾数月了。


将来时:
I think I will be hooked after I try it. = 我想试过之后就会上瘾了。
He thinks he will be hooked on this new sport. = 他认为他会迷上这项新运动。


虚拟语气:
If I tried it, I think I would be hooked. = 如果我尝试一下，我想我会上瘾的。
I wish I wasn't hooked on sugar. = 我真希望我不那么喜欢糖。


被动语态:  (Not typically used in the passive voice for this meaning)


疑问句:
Are you hooked on anything at the moment? = 你现在对什么东西上瘾了吗？
Is he hooked on that new game? = 他迷上那个新游戏了吗？


否定句:
I'm not hooked on anything. = 我对任何东西都不上瘾。
She wasn't hooked on the story, so she stopped reading. = 她对这个故事不感兴趣，所以她停止阅读了。



PHRASE->
hooked on:  沉迷于...
get hooked:  上瘾
become hooked:  开始沉迷
be hooked on: 迷上...
stay hooked:  持续沉迷
