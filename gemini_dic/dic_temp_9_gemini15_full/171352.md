publicity:/pʌˈblɪsəti/| n. |公众宣传，公开；名声 |The act of making something known publicly; attention given to someone or something by the media or public.  |例句：The company launched a major publicity campaign to promote its new product. 这家公司发起了一场大型的宣传活动来推广其新产品。

例句：Negative publicity surrounding the scandal damaged the company's reputation.围绕丑闻的负面宣传损害了公司的声誉。


近义词：promotion, advertising,coverage, notoriety |反义词：secrecy, obscurity, anonymity |词性:名词
