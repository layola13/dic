declaration:/ˌdekləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 声明；宣告；申报；公告 | A formal statement or announcement.  It can be a public statement of intentions, opinions, or facts.  It can also be a statement declaring something to be true or existing.  |例句：The government issued a declaration of war. 政府发布了战争宣言。

The company made a declaration of its commitment to environmental protection. 公司声明致力于环境保护。


近义词：proclamation, announcement, statement, assertion | 反义词：concealment, secrecy, withholding | 词性：名词


词源:  From Old French *declaracioun, from Latin *declaratio, from declarare "to make clear, explain," from de- "completely" + clarus "clear."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government declares its policy on immigration. = 政府声明其移民政策。
She declares her love for him. = 她宣告她爱他。

一般过去时:
He declared his innocence. = 他宣称自己无罪。
They declared independence from Britain. = 他们宣布脱离英国独立。

现在完成时:
She has declared her candidacy for president. = 她已经宣布竞选总统。
He has declared his assets to the tax authorities. = 他已向税务机关申报了他的资产。

过去完成时:
They had declared war before the first battle. = 他们第一场战斗前就已宣战。
She had declared bankruptcy before the creditors arrived. = 在债权人到达之前，她已经宣布破产。

将来时:
He will declare his intentions next week. = 他下周将宣布他的意图。
The court will declare the verdict tomorrow. = 法院明天将宣判。

虚拟语气:
If he declared his love, she would be very happy. = 如果他表白，她会非常高兴。
I wish he had declared his intentions earlier. = 我希望他早点表明他的意图。

被动语态:
The war was declared in 1914. = 战争于1914年爆发。
The winner will be declared at the end of the race. = 比赛结束后将宣布获胜者。

疑问句:
Has the company declared a dividend? = 公司是否已宣布分红？
Will they declare war? = 他们会宣战吗？

否定句:
He did not declare his income. = 他没有申报他的收入。
She refused to declare her allegiance. = 她拒绝宣誓效忠。
