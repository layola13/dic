knots:/nɒts/|n.|结，节，难题|A knot is a fastening made by tying together pieces of string, rope, or other flexible material.  It can also refer to a hard lump or swelling, or a difficult problem.|例句：She tied a knot in the rope. 她把绳子打了个结。|The sailor skillfully tied many knots. 水手熟练地打了很多结。|近义词：tie, tangle, complication|反义词：untying, solution
