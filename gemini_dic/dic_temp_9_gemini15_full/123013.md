monasteries:/ˈmɒnəstəriːz/| n.|修道院（复数）|Monasteries are religious communities where monks or nuns live and work.|例句：Many ancient monasteries are located in remote areas. 许多古老的修道院位于偏远地区。|例句：The monasteries of Tibet are famous for their unique architecture.  西藏的修道院以其独特的建筑风格而闻名。|近义词：convents, abbeys|反义词：secular buildings
