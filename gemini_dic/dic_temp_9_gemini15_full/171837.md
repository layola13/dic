pungy:/ˈpʌŋɡi/| adj.| 刺激性气味的；强烈的；辛辣的 | Having a strong, sharp, and often unpleasant smell.  |例句：The cheese had a pungy smell. 这奶酪有一股刺激性的气味。|例句：The air was filled with the pungy odor of garlic.  空气中弥漫着浓烈的蒜味。|近义词：strong-smelling, acrid, fetid, pungent |反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling, odorless
