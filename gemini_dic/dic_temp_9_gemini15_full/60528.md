elaidate: /ˈɛlədeɪt/ | n. |油酸乙酯|Elaidate is the ethyl ester of elaidic acid, a trans unsaturated fatty acid. It is a colorless liquid with a mild odor. It is used in various applications, including as a solvent and in the production of certain types of plastics and coatings.  |例句：The study examined the effects of elaidate on cholesterol levels in mice. 这项研究考察了油酸乙酯对小鼠胆固醇水平的影响。|近义词：ethyl oleate (近似物，但并非完全相同)|反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑其顺式异构体油酸乙酯（ethyl oleate）作为对比|名词
