ichorhaemia:/ˌaɪkɔːrˈhiːmiə/| n. |脓血症，腐败血症 |A pathological condition characterized by the presence of ichor (a thin watery discharge from wounds) in the blood.  It is a severe and often fatal condition.  The term is rarely used in modern medical terminology, having been largely superseded by more specific diagnoses. |例句：The patient presented with symptoms consistent with ichorhaemia, including fever, chills, and septic shock.  患者表现出与脓血症一致的症状，包括发烧、寒战和败血性休克。|近义词：septicemia, bacteremia, toxemia |反义词：asepsis, sterility |名词
