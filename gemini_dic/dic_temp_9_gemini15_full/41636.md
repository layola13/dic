correction:/kəˈrekʃən/| n.|改正；纠正；修正|The act of correcting something; the state of being corrected.  It can refer to the process of identifying and fixing errors, or the resulting change.|例句：The teacher made several corrections to my essay.老师在我的论文中做了几次修改。|例句：The correction of the faulty wiring prevented a fire.  错误线路的修正防止了火灾。|近义词：amendment, rectification, adjustment|反义词：error, mistake, fault|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor makes corrections to the manuscript. = 编辑正在修改稿件。
She routinely performs corrections on her students' work. = 她定期更正学生的作业。

一般过去时:
He made a correction to the spelling error. = 他纠正了一个拼写错误。
They corrected the inaccurate data in the report. = 他们纠正了报告中不准确的数据。

现在完成时:
The team has made several corrections to the software. = 团队已经对软件做了几次修改。
She has corrected all the grammatical errors in the document. = 她已经纠正了文档中所有的语法错误。

过去完成时:
He had already made the correction before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前就已经做了修改。
They had corrected the mistake before anyone noticed it. = 他们已经在任何人注意到之前纠正了错误。

将来时:
We will make the necessary corrections before publication. = 我们将在出版前进行必要的修改。
The engineer will correct the malfunctioning system. = 工程师将纠正故障系统。

虚拟语气:
If I had known the error, I would have made the correction. = 如果我知道错误，我就会进行修改。
It would be helpful if they made corrections to their approach. = 如果他们能改进方法就好了。

被动语态:
The errors were corrected by the proofreader. = 校对员纠正了错误。
The mistake is being corrected by the technician. = 技术员正在纠正错误。

疑问句:
Have you made all the necessary corrections? = 你已经做了所有必要的修改吗？
What corrections need to be made to the plan? = 计划需要做哪些修改？

否定句:
He didn't make any corrections to his work. = 他没有对他的作品做任何修改。
She failed to correct the inaccuracies in her report. = 她没有纠正报告中的不准确之处。
