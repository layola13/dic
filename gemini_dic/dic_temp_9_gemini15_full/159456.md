polewig:/ˈpoʊlɪɡ/| n. |蝌蚪|A polewig is the larval stage of a frog or toad.  It is an aquatic creature with a tail and gills, lacking legs.  It undergoes metamorphosis to become a frog or toad.|例句：The pond was teeming with polewigs.  池塘里挤满了蝌蚪。|近义词：tadpole|反义词：frog, toad
