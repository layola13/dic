/ˌpriːɪnˈvɒlvd/ | adj. | 预先参与的；预先卷入的 | Previously involved; already implicated or connected to something.  The term suggests prior involvement, often implying a degree of responsibility or knowledge. |例句：The preinvolved individuals were questioned extensively by the police. = 预先卷入的个人受到了警方的仔细盘问。

例句：He was preinvolved in the scandal, having provided information to the perpetrators beforehand. = 他预先卷入了丑闻，事先向肇事者提供了信息。


近义词：implicated, involved, connected, implicated in


反义词：uninvolved, unconnected, extraneous


词性：形容词
