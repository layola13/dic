sclerotitis:/ˌsklɛrəˈtaɪtɪs/| n. |巩膜炎|Inflammation of the sclera, the white of the eye.  It can be caused by various factors, including infections, autoimmune diseases, and trauma. Symptoms can include redness, pain, and blurred vision. |The patient presented with sclerotitis, characterized by severe redness and pain in the eye.  这位病人出现了巩膜炎，其特征是眼睛严重发红和疼痛。|uveitis, episcleritis|healthy sclera|名词
