precontemplate:/ˌpriːkənˈtɛmpleɪt/ | vt. |预先思考，预想 | To think about or consider (something) beforehand.  |例句：She precontemplated the challenges of starting her own business. 她预先思考了创业的挑战。

例句：He precontemplated his speech for the upcoming conference. 他预先构思了他即将在会议上发表的演讲。


近义词：foresee, anticipate, contemplate beforehand, preconsider | 反义词：neglect, disregard, overlook | 词性：动词

词源:
The word "precontemplate" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before" or "in advance") and the verb "contemplate" (meaning "to think deeply about").  Therefore, it literally means "to think about beforehand".
