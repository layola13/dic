The word lacks widespread acceptance in formal writing or speech. |例句：The villain murderized his enemies with a gruesome efficiency.  这个恶棍以令人毛骨悚然的效率残忍杀害了他的敌人。The storm murderized the landscape.  暴风雨摧毁了这片土地。|近义词：slaughtered, massacred, butchered, annihilated|反义词：saved, rescued, protected
