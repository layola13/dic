puzzle:/ˈpʌzəl/| n. v. |难题；谜；困惑；使困惑 |a problem that is difficult to solve; to confuse or bewilder someone|例句：This crossword puzzle is very difficult.  这个纵横字谜游戏很难。

The mystery of the missing money puzzled the police. 丢失的钱款之谜使警方困惑不解。


近义词：mystery, enigma, riddle, conundrum | 反义词：solution, answer
