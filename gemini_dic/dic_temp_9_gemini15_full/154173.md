periodicalisms:/ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkəlɪzəm/| n.|期刊特色；期刊习语；期刊风格|Characteristics of style or expression typical of periodicals.  These might include the use of certain vocabulary, sentence structures, or journalistic conventions.  The term often implies a somewhat formulaic or predictable style, but not necessarily negatively.|例句：The article exhibited certain periodicalisms, such as the use of short, declarative sentences and frequent subheadings. 这篇文章展现出某些期刊特色，例如使用简短的陈述句和频繁的小标题。|近义词：journalistic style, magazine style, periodical conventions|反义词：literary style (in contrast)|名词
