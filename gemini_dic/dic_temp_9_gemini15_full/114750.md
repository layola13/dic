malonic: /məˈlɒnɪk/ | adj. |丙二酸的|Malonic acid is a dicarboxylic acid with the formula CH₂(CO₂H)₂. It is a white crystalline powder that is soluble in water.  It's named after malonyl, which is derived from malic acid. Malonic acid is used in the synthesis of many other chemicals, including pharmaceuticals and polymers.|  Malonic acid is a key intermediate in the biosynthesis of fatty acids. |近义词: dicarboxylic |反义词: N/A
