opiniator:/əˈpɪnieɪtər/| n. |固执己见的人，武断的人|A person who is unreasonably attached to their own opinions and beliefs, often unwilling to consider others' viewpoints.  They tend to be dogmatic and inflexible in their thinking.|He's such an opiniator; he never listens to anyone else's perspective. 他是个固执己见的人；他从来不听别人的意见。|近义词：dogmatist, bigot, tyrant, autocrat |反义词：open-minded person, tolerant person
