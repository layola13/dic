chambering:/ˈtʃeɪmbərɪŋ/ | v. | 装入弹膛；使进入房间 |The act of loading a firearm with ammunition.  It also refers to the process of entering or placing something into a chamber or enclosed space. |例句：He carefully chambered a round into the rifle. 他小心翼翼地将一颗子弹装入步枪膛内。|例句：The guests were chambered into the grand ballroom. 客人们被引入了宏伟的舞厅。|近义词：loading, inserting, housing |反义词：unloading, ejecting
