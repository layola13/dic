schoolkid:/ˈskuːlkɪd/| n. |小学生|A child who attends school.  A schoolkid is a young person who is of school age and attends school.  The term is informal and often used in a slightly condescending or humorous way.|例句：The schoolkid spilled his juice all over his homework.  这个小学生把果汁洒了他所有的家庭作业上。

例句：That schoolkid is always getting into trouble. 那个小学生总是惹麻烦。


近义词：schoolchild, pupil, student (more formal)|反义词：adult, grown-up
