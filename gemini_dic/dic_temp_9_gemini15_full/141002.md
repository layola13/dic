onerous:/ˈoʊnərəs/| adj.|繁重的；费力的；令人厌烦的| burdensome, difficult, and unpleasant|The task proved to be more onerous than anticipated.  这项任务被证明比预期的更加繁重。

The project was onerous, requiring long hours and considerable effort. 这个项目很费力，需要长时间工作和付出相当大的努力。


近义词：burdensome, arduous, laborious, taxing, demanding

反义词：easy, effortless, simple, light


词源:  From Old French *onerous, from Latin onus "burden".  The word entered English in the 15th century.
