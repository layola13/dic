describe:/dɪˈskraɪb/ | vt. |描述，形容 |To give an account in words of (someone or something), including all the relevant characteristics or features.  |例句：Please describe the suspect to the police. 请向警察描述嫌疑人的特征。 |例句：Can you describe what happened? 你能描述一下发生了什么吗？|近义词：portray, depict, delineate, recount |反义词：misrepresent, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He describes the scene vividly. 他生动地描述了这个场景。
She describes her childhood as happy. 她把她的童年描述成快乐的。

一般过去时:
The witness described the attacker accurately. 目击者准确地描述了袭击者。
He described the process in detail. 他详细地描述了这个过程。

现在完成时:
She has described the painting beautifully. 她已经漂亮地描述了这幅画。
They have described the situation clearly. 他们已经清楚地描述了情况。

过去完成时:
He had described the problem before the meeting. 他在会议前已经描述了这个问题。
She had described her feelings to her friend. 她已经向她的朋友描述了她的感受。

将来时:
I will describe my trip to you later. 我稍后会向你描述我的旅行。
He will describe the experiment in his report. 他将在他的报告中描述这个实验。

虚拟语气:
If I could describe it, I would. 如果我能描述它，我会的。
I wish I could describe the feeling. 我希望我能描述这种感觉。

被动语态:
The event was described in the newspaper. 这件事在报纸上被描述了。
The painting is described as a masterpiece. 这幅画被描述为杰作。

疑问句:
How would you describe your job? 你如何形容你的工作？
Can you describe the feeling? 你能描述一下这种感觉吗？

否定句:
I cannot describe the beauty of the sunset. 我无法描述日落的美丽。
He didn't describe the accident properly. 他没有恰当地描述事故。


PHRASE->
describe as: 描述为...
describe in detail: 详细描述
describe the scene: 描述场景
give a description of: 给出...的描述
accurate description: 准确的描述
