sipunculacea:/ˌsɪpʌŋkjuˈleɪsiə/| n. |腕足动物门 | Sipunculacea is a phylum of marine invertebrates, commonly known as peanut worms.  They are characterized by their unsegmented bodies, a retractable proboscis, and a U-shaped gut.  They inhabit various marine environments, from shallow waters to deep seas. | Example:  The research team discovered a new species of sipunculacean in the deep-sea trench.  研究团队在深海海沟发现了一种新的腕足动物。|近义词:None (it's a phylum)|反义词:None|名词
