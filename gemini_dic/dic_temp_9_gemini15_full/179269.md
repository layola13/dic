redistributed:/ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪd/| v. |重新分配，再分配 |To distribute again or anew; to allocate (resources, etc.) again.  The term implies a previous distribution that is being altered or modified.|


例句：
The government redistributed the land to the farmers. = 政府将土地重新分配给了农民。

The company redistributed its profits among its employees. = 公司将利润重新分配给了员工。


近义词：reallocate, reallot, redistribute


反义词：concentrate, centralize


词性：动词


USAGE->

The government redistributes resources annually. = 政府每年重新分配资源。
The company redistributes profits based on performance. = 公司根据业绩重新分配利润。
