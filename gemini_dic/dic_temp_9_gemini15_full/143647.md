outkills:/ˌaʊtˈkɪlz/| vt.|胜过，超越|To surpass or exceed in skill or ability.|例句：His skill outkills all others in the competition.  他的技能胜过比赛中的所有其他人。|例句：Her dedication outkills her talent.  她的奉献精神胜过她的天赋。|近义词：surpasses, excels, outperforms, overshadows |反义词：underperforms, is inferior to


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outkills his opponents in every game. = 他在每一场比赛中都胜过对手。
She consistently outkills her colleagues in sales. = 她在销售方面始终胜过她的同事。

一般过去时:
