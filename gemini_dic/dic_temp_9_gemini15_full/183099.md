reported:/rɪˈpɔːrtɪd/ | adj. v. | 报道的；据报道的；报告；汇报 |  The word "reported" can function as both an adjective and a verb. As an adjective, it means something that has been reported or stated. As a verb, it means to give a spoken or written account of something.  | 例句：The reported accident caused a major traffic jam.  据报道的事故导致了严重的交通堵塞。 The police officer reported the incident to his superior. 警官向上级报告了这一事件。 | 近义词：announced, declared, recounted, stated | 反义词：unreported, undisclosed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reports the news every evening. = 他每晚报道新闻。
She reports directly to the CEO. = 她直接向CEO汇报。


一般过去时:
They reported the crime to the police. = 他们向警方报案。
He reported his findings in a detailed document. = 他在一份详细的文档中报告了他的发现。


现在完成时:
She has reported the problem to the IT department. = 她已经向IT部门报告了这个问题。
They have reported record profits this year. = 他们今年创下了创纪录的利润。


过去完成时:
He had reported the incident before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经报告了这一事件。
They had reported their findings to the committee. = 他们已经向委员会报告了他们的调查结果。


将来时:
She will report her progress next week. = 她下周将汇报她的进度。
They will report the results of the study tomorrow. = 他们明天将报告研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If he had reported the issue earlier, it could have been avoided. = 如果他早点报告这个问题，就可以避免了。
I wish I had reported the incident sooner. = 我真希望我早点报告这件事。


被动语态:
The accident was reported in all the major newspapers. = 这起事故被所有主要报纸报道了。
The findings were reported to the board of directors. = 调查结果已报告给董事会。


疑问句:
Did you report the incident? = 你报告了这件事吗？
Has the suspect been reported to the authorities? = 嫌犯已被报告给当局了吗？


否定句:
He did not report the theft. = 他没有报告盗窃案。
The results have not yet been reported. = 结果尚未报告。


PHRASE->
report on = 报道...
report to = 向...汇报
report back = 汇报
report a crime = 报案
file a report = 提交报告
