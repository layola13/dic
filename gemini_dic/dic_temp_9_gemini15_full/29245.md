cassette:/kəˈsɛt/ | n. |盒式磁带|A cassette is a plastic case containing a magnetic tape used for recording and playing sound or data.  Cassettes were popular for music and data storage before the advent of CDs and digital formats. | I found an old cassette tape of my favorite band from the 1980s.  我找到了一盘我80年代最喜欢的乐队的旧盒式磁带。

|He played the cassette on his portable player. 他在他的便携式播放器上播放磁带。|  compact disc, tape | record (as in a vinyl record) | 名词
