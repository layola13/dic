obviating:/ˈɒbvieɪtɪŋ/ | v. |消除；避免；使…不必要 | To remove a need or difficulty.  To make unnecessary. |例句：The new system obviates the need for manual data entry.  新的系统消除了手动数据录入的需要。

例句：The new bridge obviates the long detour around the mountains. 新桥避免了绕山的漫长绕路。


近义词：avoid, prevent, eliminate, preclude, avert, circumvent

反义词： necessitate, require, demand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new software obviates many previous problems. = 新软件消除了许多以前的问题。
This solution obviates the need for further discussion. = 这个解决方案使进一步讨论变得没有必要。


一般过去时:
The invention of the airplane obviated the need for long sea voyages. = 飞机的发明消除了长途海运的必要性。
His quick thinking obviated a potential disaster. = 他敏捷的思维避免了一场潜在的灾难。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have obviated many manual tasks. = 技术进步已经消除了许多手工任务。
This new policy has obviated the previous confusion. = 这项新政策消除了之前的混乱。


过去完成时:
They had obviated the problem before it escalated. = 他们在问题升级之前就解决了这个问题。
The careful planning had obviated any potential delays. = 周密的计划避免了任何潜在的延误。


将来时:
This new technology will obviate the need for human intervention. = 这项新技术将消除对人工干预的需求。
The new bridge will obviate the current traffic congestion. = 新桥将消除目前的交通拥堵。


虚拟语气:
If we adopted this strategy, it would obviate many difficulties. = 如果我们采取这个策略，它将消除许多困难。
I wish this solution could obviate all the problems. = 我希望这个解决方案能够解决所有问题。


被动语态:
Many problems were obviated by the new system. = 新系统消除了许多问题。
The need for extra staff was obviated by improved efficiency. = 提高效率消除了对额外员工的需求。


疑问句:
Does this new technology obviate the need for training? = 这项新技术是否消除了培训的需要？
Will this measure obviate the risk of failure? = 这一措施能否避免失败的风险？


否定句:
The new regulations did not obviate the need for further investigation. = 新规章并没有消除进一步调查的必要。
The current system does not obviate the potential for errors. = 当前系统无法避免出错的可能性。
