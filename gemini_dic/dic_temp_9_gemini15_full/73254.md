fore-alleged:/ˌfɔːr əˈlɛdʒd/| adj.|预先声称的，先前指控的 |Previously alleged; mentioned or claimed before a formal accusation or trial.  The term implies that the allegation is still under investigation or has not yet been proven in court. |例句：The fore-alleged embezzlement was the subject of a lengthy investigation.  = 预先声称的挪用公款是长期调查的主题。

The fore-alleged fraud led to the company's bankruptcy. = 预先指控的欺诈导致公司破产。


近义词：previously accused, earlier claimed, beforehand alleged


反义词： subsequently alleged, newly accused


词性：形容词
