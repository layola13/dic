nonanesthetics:/ˌnɒnænɛsˈθɛtɪks/| n.|非麻醉剂|Substances that do not have anesthetic properties; they do not induce loss of sensation or consciousness.  They might be used in conjunction with anesthetics or for purposes other than anesthesia.|Example:  The study compared the effects of anesthetics and nonanesthetics on wound healing.  这项研究比较了麻醉剂和非麻醉剂对伤口愈合的影响。|近义词：analgesics (if referring to pain relief),  antiseptics (if referring to sterilization)|反义词：anesthetics|名词
