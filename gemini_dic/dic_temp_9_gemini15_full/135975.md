nonradiances:/ˌnɒnˈreɪdiənsɪz/ | n. |非辐射 |The absence of radiant energy or emission.  It refers to areas or objects that do not emit radiation, in contrast to those that do (radiances).  The term is often used in physics, astronomy, and related fields. | Example: The deep space between galaxies exhibits significant nonradiances. 深层星系间的空间显示出显著的非辐射现象。|近义词:darknesses, obscurations|反义词:radiances, emissions|名词
