downweigh:/ˈdaʊnweɪ/| vt.|轻视；低估；贬低|To give less importance or value to something.  It implies a deliberate act of minimizing something's significance.|The study downweighed the importance of early childhood education. 这项研究低估了幼儿教育的重要性。|近义词：underestimate, undervalue, devalue, belittle |反义词：overestimate, overvalue, emphasize, highlight|动词
