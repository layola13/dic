dementia:/dɪˈmɛnʃə/|/ˈdɛmɛnʃə/| n. |痴呆；老年性痴呆|A chronic or persistent disorder of the mental processes caused by brain disease or injury and marked by memory disorders, personality changes, and impaired reasoning.|例句：Her grandmother suffered from dementia for many years.  她祖母患痴呆症多年了。

例句：The doctor diagnosed him with vascular dementia. 医生诊断他患有血管性痴呆。


近义词：senility, cognitive impairment, mental deterioration|反义词：sanity, lucidity, mental sharpness|词性：名词
