nerviness:/ˈnɜːvɪnəs/| n. |紧张不安；焦虑|A feeling of anxiety or nervousness. It can stem from various sources like upcoming events, social interactions, or stressful situations.  The feeling is often accompanied by physical symptoms like increased heart rate, sweating, or trembling. |例句：Her nerviness was evident in her trembling hands.  她的双手颤抖，显露出了她的紧张。

例句：The nerviness before the big presentation was palpable.  大型演示之前的紧张气氛是显而易见的。


近义词：anxiety, apprehension, jitters, unease, worry|反义词：calmness, composure, serenity, tranquility|词性:名词
