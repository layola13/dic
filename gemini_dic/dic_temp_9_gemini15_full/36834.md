colonists:/ˈkɑːlənɪsts/| n. |殖民者，移民 |People who settle in a new country or region, often establishing a colony under the control of their home country.  Colonists often face challenges such as adapting to a new environment, establishing governance, and interacting with indigenous populations.  Their actions and experiences shape the history and culture of the newly settled area. |The American colonists fought for independence from Great Britain.  美国殖民者为争取独立而与大不列颠作战。|settlers, pioneers, immigrants |natives, indigenous people |名词
