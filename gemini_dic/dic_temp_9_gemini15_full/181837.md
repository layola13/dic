reliving:/ˌriːˈlɪvɪŋ/| v.|重温，再体验| experiencing again something that happened in the past, often with strong emotions.  |例句：She was reliving her childhood memories. 她正在重温儿时的记忆。|例句：Reliving that traumatic experience was incredibly difficult.  重温那次创伤经历非常困难。|近义词：recalling, remembering, recapturing|反义词：forgetting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relives his childhood memories every day. = 他每天都在重温童年记忆。
They are reliving their honeymoon in their minds. = 他们在脑海中重温蜜月时光。

一般过去时:
She relived the accident in her mind. = 她脑海中重现了事故发生的情景。
We relived the best moments of our trip. = 我们重温了旅行中最美好的时刻。

现在完成时:
He has relived that moment countless times. = 他无数次地重温了那一刻。
She has relived the joy of her wedding day. = 她已经无数次重温了婚礼当天的喜悦。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had relived his speech many times. = 演讲之前，他已经多次排练（重温）了自己的演讲。
Having relived the entire incident, she felt exhausted. = 在重温了整件事之后，她感到筋疲力尽。

将来时:
I will relive this amazing experience for years to come. = 未来几年我都会重温这段美好的经历。
They will relive their victory in the coming years. = 未来几年他们都会重温他们的胜利。


被动语态:
The happy memories were relived through old photos. = 通过旧照片重温了快乐的回忆。
The events of that day are relived in the film. = 电影重现了那一天发生的事件。


PHRASE->
reliving the past = 重温过去
reliving the moment = 重温那一刻
reliving a dream = 重温一个梦
reliving a nightmare = 重温噩梦
reliving the glory days = 重温辉煌岁月
