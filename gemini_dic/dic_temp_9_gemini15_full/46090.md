cyclostomi: /ˌsaɪkləˈstoʊmi/ | n. | 圆口纲 | Cyclostomi is a class of jawless vertebrates including lampreys and hagfish.  They are characterized by their eel-like bodies, lack of jaws, and circular mouths.  They are considered to be the most primitive vertebrates. | Example:  The study of cyclostomi provides valuable insights into the early evolution of vertebrates.  圆口纲的研究为早期脊椎动物的进化提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词: Agnatha (although this is a superclass, it encompasses Cyclostomi)|反义词:  Gnathostomata (jawed vertebrates)
