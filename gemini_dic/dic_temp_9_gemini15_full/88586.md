hitlerites:/ˈhɪtləraɪts/| n. |希特勒支持者，纳粹分子 |A derogatory term for supporters of Adolf Hitler and Nazism.  The word carries strong negative connotations and is used to express contempt or disgust for those associated with the Nazi regime and its ideology. |例句：The historian condemned the actions of the hitlerites during World War II.  历史学家谴责了二战期间希特勒支持者的行为。|近义词：Nazis, fascists,  Third Reich supporters|反义词：anti-Nazis, resistance fighters|名词
