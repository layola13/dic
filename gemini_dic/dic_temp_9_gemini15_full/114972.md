manchuko:/ˈmæntʃuːkoʊ/| n. |满洲国|Manchukuo was a Japanese puppet state in Manchuria (northeastern China) from 1932 to 1945.  It was established by the Empire of Japan after the Mukden Incident, a staged event used as a pretext for the invasion and occupation of Manchuria.|The establishment of Manchukuo was a key step in Japan's expansionist ambitions in Asia. The puppet emperor was Puyi, the last emperor of China.|近义词:puppet state, occupied territory|反义词:independent nation
