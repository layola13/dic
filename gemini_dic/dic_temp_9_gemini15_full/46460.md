dahlia:/ˈdeɪljə/| n. |大丽花|A dahlia is a plant of the daisy family, with brightly coloured, often double flowers.  Many varieties are cultivated as garden plants.|The dahlias in her garden were a stunning display of color.|近义词：flower, bloom|反义词:无
