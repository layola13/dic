dehydrator:/diːˈhaɪdreɪtər/| n. |脱水机，干燥机|A kitchen appliance that removes moisture from food to preserve it.  It works by circulating warm air around the food. |例句：I use my dehydrator to make fruit leather and jerky. 我用我的脱水机做水果皮和牛肉干。

例句：The dehydrator is a great way to preserve summer's bounty. 脱水机是保存夏季收获的好方法。
