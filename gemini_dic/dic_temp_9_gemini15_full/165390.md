preknit:/ˈpriːnɪt/| adj.| 预先编织的；预先缝合的 |Preknit refers to fabric or garments that have been knitted before being incorporated into a larger garment or product.  It often implies a component that is ready-made and attached to other parts, rather than knitted in place.|例句：The sleeves of this jacket are preknit for ease of assembly. 这件夹克的袖子是预先编织的，便于组装。


The collar is made from preknit fabric.  衣领是用预先编织好的面料制成的。|近义词：pre-made, ready-made|反义词：knitted in place, hand-knitted
