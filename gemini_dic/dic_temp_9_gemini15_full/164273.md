predrive:/ˈpriːdraɪv/| n.|预驱动，预先驱动|A predrive is a preliminary drive or test run, often used in the context of testing computer hardware or software before full implementation.  It's a process of preparing a system or component for its main operation.|例句：The engineers conducted a predrive test of the new engine before mass production. 工程师在批量生产之前对新发动机进行了预驱动测试。
例句：The software team performed a predrive to check for bugs before releasing the update. 软件团队在发布更新之前进行了预驱动测试以检查错误。|近义词：test run, trial run, pre-test|反义词：final run, main run|名词
