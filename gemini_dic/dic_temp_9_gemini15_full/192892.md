seatbelts:/ˈsiːtˌbɛlts/| n. |安全带|A strap or belt designed to secure a person in a vehicle's seat, reducing the risk of injury in an accident.  |Example: Always fasten your seatbelts before driving.系好安全带后再开车。|近义词：safety belts, restraints|反义词：None
