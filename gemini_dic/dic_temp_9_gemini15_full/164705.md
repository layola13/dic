preextract: /ˌpriːˈɛkstrækt/ | v. | 预先提取；预先萃取 | To extract something beforehand or in advance.  It implies a preparation or preliminary step before a main extraction process. | The researchers preextracted the essential oils before performing the chromatography. 研究人员在进行色谱分析之前预先提取了精油。  They preextracted the data from the database for further analysis. 他们预先从数据库中提取数据以便进一步分析。 |  pre-extract (no direct synonyms, the meaning is conveyed by phrases like "extract in advance," "preliminary extraction") |  post-extract (antonym describes the opposite timing of extraction) | verb
