qualities:/ˈkwɑːlətiz/ | n. |品质，特性，特质 | Qualities are the distinctive attributes or characteristics of a person, object, or thing.  They can be physical, mental, moral, or other attributes that define something.  |例句：His best qualities are his kindness and generosity. 他最好的品质是他的善良和慷慨。

例句：The qualities of this material make it ideal for clothing.  这种材料的特性使其成为理想的服装面料。


近义词：attributes, characteristics, traits, features, properties

反义词：defects, flaws, shortcomings


词性：名词
