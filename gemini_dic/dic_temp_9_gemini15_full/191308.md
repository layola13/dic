scene-of-crime:/siːn əv kraɪm/| n. |犯罪现场|The scene of crime refers to the location where a crime has been committed. It is the area where evidence related to the crime may be found and preserved.  Forensic investigators meticulously examine the scene to gather evidence, such as fingerprints, DNA, and other clues that may help solve the crime.|The police cordoned off the scene of crime.  警方封锁了犯罪现场。|crime scene, location of crime|peaceful scene, safe place|名词
