renaming:/ˌriːˈneɪmɪŋ/| n. |改名，重新命名 |The act of giving a new name to something.  This often happens with files, software, or even people or places.  It involves changing the existing name to a different one. |例句：The renaming of the street caused some confusion.  街道改名造成了些许混乱。

The renaming of the project is necessary to reflect its new direction. 项目改名是为了反映其新的方向。|近义词：rechristening, retitling |反义词：maintaining the original name


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company is renaming its product line. = 公司正在为其产品线改名。
She renames her files regularly. = 她定期重命名她的文件。


一般过去时:
They renamed the building after a famous scientist. = 他们以一位著名科学家的名字重新命名了那座建筑物。
He renamed his dog after his favorite character. = 他以他最喜欢的角色的名字重新命名了他的狗。


现在完成时:
They have renamed the stadium several times. = 他们已经几次改了体育馆的名字。
The developers have renamed the application to improve its branding. = 开发人员已重命名该应用程序以改进其品牌形象。


过去完成时:
Before the announcement, they had renamed the company secretly. = 在宣布之前，他们秘密地改了公司名。
We had renamed the file before realizing it was already correct. = 我们在意识到它已经正确之前就重命名了文件。


将来时:
They will rename the street next month. = 他们下个月将改这条街的名字。
The board will rename the company at its next meeting. = 董事会将在下次会议上为公司改名。


虚拟语气:
If they renamed the product, it might sell better. = 如果他们为产品改名，它可能会卖得更好。
I wish they had renamed the software earlier. = 我希望他们早点为软件改名。


被动语态:
The software was renamed by the developers. = 该软件由开发人员重命名。
The street is being renamed to honor a local hero. = 这条街正在改名，以纪念一位当地英雄。


疑问句:
Will the company be renaming its products soon? = 公司会很快为其产品改名吗？
Have they renamed the file yet? = 他们已经重命名该文件了吗？


否定句:
They did not rename the project. = 他们没有为项目改名。
She will not rename her company without consulting her partners. = 她不会在不咨询她的合伙人的情况下为公司改名。
