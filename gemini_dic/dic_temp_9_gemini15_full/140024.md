/oʊˈɛmz/ | n. |原始设备制造商|Original Equipment Manufacturer; a company that produces parts or products that are used by another company to produce its own products.  OEMs typically don't sell directly to consumers. |Example:  Many car manufacturers rely on several OEMs to supply parts like engines and tires.  许多汽车制造商依赖几家原始设备制造商来供应发动机和轮胎等零部件。|近义词: supplier, manufacturer, vendor |反义词: retailer, end-user
