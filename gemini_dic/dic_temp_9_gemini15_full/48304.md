decrepit:/dɪˈkrɛpɪt/| adj.|破旧的；衰老的；衰弱的| weakened and worn out by age, illness, or overuse; dilapidated.|The decrepit old house was falling apart.  这栋破旧的老房子快要塌了。|The decrepit old man could barely walk.  这位衰弱的老人几乎无法行走。|run-down, dilapidated, broken-down, feeble, weak|strong, robust, vigorous, new, sturdy
