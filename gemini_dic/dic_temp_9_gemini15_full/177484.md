rebind:/ˌriːˈbaɪnd/| vt.|重新装订；重新绑定；重新结合|To bind again; to fasten or tie again.  It can also refer to re-establishing a connection or link, particularly in programming. |例句：The librarian had to rebind the damaged book. 图书管理员不得不重新装订那本损坏的书。

例句：The programmer had to rebind the application after updating the libraries. 程序员在更新库后不得不重新绑定应用程序。|近义词：retie, refasten, reconnect|反义词：unbind, unfasten, disconnect|动词
