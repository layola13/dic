navigated:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtɪd/| v.|导航；驾驶；顺利通过；克服（困难）|To find one's way; to steer a course.|例句：The captain skillfully navigated the ship through the storm. 船长熟练地驾驶着船只穿过暴风雨。|例句：She navigated the complex bureaucracy to get her permit. 她克服了复杂的官僚程序，拿到了许可证。|近义词：steer, guide, pilot, manage|反义词：founder, flounder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He navigates the city streets with ease. 他轻松地驾驭着城市街道。
We navigate the website to find the information we need. 我们浏览网站以查找所需信息。


一般过去时:
The explorers navigated the treacherous river. 探险家们成功地航行了那条险峻的河流。
They navigated the complex legal issues with the help of a lawyer. 他们在律师的帮助下解决了复杂的法律问题。


现在完成时:
She has navigated many challenges in her career. 她已经在职业生涯中克服了许多挑战。
We have navigated the difficult terrain and reached the summit. 我们已经克服了艰难的地形并到达了山顶。


过去完成时:
He had navigated the maze before, so he knew the way out. 他以前走过这个迷宫，所以他知道怎么出去。
They had navigated the political landscape carefully before making their decision. 他们在做出决定之前仔细地权衡了政治局势。


将来时:
We will navigate the new software system next week. 我们下周将学习使用新的软件系统。
The ship will navigate the narrow strait tomorrow. 船只将于明天航行通过狭窄的海峡。


虚拟语气:
If I had a map, I would have navigated the forest more easily. 如果我有地图，我就能更容易地穿过森林。
If only I had navigated that relationship better.  但愿我当时能更好地处理那段关系。


被动语态:
The ship was navigated by an experienced captain. 这艘船由一位经验丰富的船长驾驶。
The project was successfully navigated through the various regulatory hurdles. 项目成功地克服了各种监管障碍。


疑问句:
How do you navigate this complicated software? 你如何使用这个复杂的软件？
Can you navigate the website using a screen reader? 你可以使用屏幕阅读器浏览网站吗？


否定句:
He couldn't navigate the unfamiliar roads. 他无法驾驭不熟悉的道路。
The pilot failed to navigate the plane safely. 飞行员未能安全地驾驶飞机。


PHRASE->
navigate through: 渡过；克服 (navigate through difficulties)
navigate around: 绕过；避开 (navigate around the problem)
navigate to: 导航到；前往 (navigate to the website)
navigate by: 根据…导航 (navigate by the stars)
successfully navigate: 成功地克服；成功地驾驭 (successfully navigate the challenges)
