noncontemptuous: /ˌnɒnkənˈtɛmptʃuəs/ | adj. | 不轻蔑的，不鄙视的 | Not expressing or feeling contempt; respectful.  | 例句：Her tone was noncontemptuous, even friendly. 她的语气并不轻蔑，甚至很友好。


例句：He offered a noncontemptuous explanation for his mistake. 他对自己的错误做出了不轻蔑的解释。


近义词：respectful, polite, courteous, considerate, civil


反义词：contemptuous, scornful, disdainful, disrespectful, insulting


词源:  The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "contemptuous," which derives from the Latin word "contemptus," meaning "despised" or "scorned."  The Latin word itself is a past participle of the verb "contemnere," meaning "to despise" or "to scorn."  Therefore, "noncontemptuous" literally means "not despising" or "not scornful."
