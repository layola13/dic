misjudge:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒ/| vt.|误判，错估；曲解|To form an incorrect judgment or opinion of (someone or something).  To fail to understand or assess correctly.|例句：I completely misjudged the situation. 我完全误判了形势。

例句：He misjudged the distance and crashed into the wall. 他判断距离错误，撞到了墙上。


近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand, miscalculate, underestimate, overestimate

反义词：understand correctly, assess accurately, judge correctly

词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misjudges people easily. 他很容易误判别人。
I often misjudge the time it takes to complete a task. 我经常误判完成一项任务所需的时间。


一般过去时:
She misjudged his intentions. 她误解了他的意图。
The jury misjudged the defendant's character. 陪审团误判了被告的品性。


现在完成时:
I have misjudged him many times. 我多次误判了他。
We have misjudged the market's potential. 我们误判了市场的潜力。


过去完成时:
They had misjudged the strength of the opponent. 他们之前低估了对手的实力。
I realized I had misjudged her abilities. 我意识到我误判了她的能力。


将来时:
He will probably misjudge the situation again. 他很可能会再次误判形势。
I fear that they will misjudge the consequences of their actions. 我担心他们会误判他们行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misjudged him, I would have trusted him more. 如果我没有误判他，我会更信任他。
I wish I hadn't misjudged her talent. 我真希望我没有误判她的才能。


被动语态:
The situation was misjudged by the authorities.  当局误判了形势。
His capabilities were misjudged by his former employer. 他以前雇主误判了他的能力。


疑问句:
Did I misjudge her reaction? 我是不是误判了她的反应？
Could we have misjudged the risks involved? 我们是否低估了其中涉及的风险？


否定句:
I didn't misjudge her intentions; I understood them perfectly. 我并没有误判她的意图；我完全理解了。
They didn't misjudge the weather conditions; they were well-prepared. 他们没有误判天气情况；他们准备充分。
