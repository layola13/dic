natural:/ˈnætʃərəl/| adj.|自然的，天然的；天生的，天赋的；正常的，自然的|Existing in or caused by nature; not made or caused by humankind.  It also refers to something inherent or innate to a person or thing.|例句：The natural beauty of the countryside is breathtaking.  乡村的自然景色令人叹为观止。
例句：She has a natural talent for music. 她天生具有音乐天赋。|近义词：inherent, innate, spontaneous, unartificial|反义词：artificial, unnatural, synthetic, contrived

The water is naturally clear. = 这水自然清澈。
This plant grows naturally in the wild. = 这种植物在野外自然生长。

一般过去时:
