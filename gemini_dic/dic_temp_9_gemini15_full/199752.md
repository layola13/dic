sirening:/ˈsaɪrənɪŋ/| n.| 汽笛声；警报声；诱惑；迷惑 | The sound of a siren, often used figuratively to describe something alluring or dangerous.  It can also refer to the act of using a siren's call to lure someone. |例句：The siren's song was so captivating that it led the sailors to their doom.  = 汽笛的歌声如此迷人，以至于它导致水手们走向灭亡。

The distant siren was a haunting sound. = 远处汽笛声令人难忘。|近义词：wailing, alluring, tempting, enchanting |反义词：silence, calmness,  repulsion


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The siren is wailing. = 警报声在鸣叫。
The ship's siren sounds every hour. = 船上的汽笛每小时鸣响一次。


一般过去时:
The siren wailed throughout the night. = 警报声整夜鸣叫。
The siren sounded just before the storm. = 暴风雨来临前汽笛响了。


现在完成时:
The siren has been wailing for hours. = 警报声已经鸣叫了好几个小时了。
The factory siren has sounded its noon whistle. = 工厂的汽笛已经响起了中午的汽笛声。


过去完成时:
The siren had already sounded before we realized there was a problem. = 在我们意识到有问题之前，警报已经响了。
The emergency siren had been malfunctioning for weeks. = 紧急警报已经故障几周了。


将来时:
The siren will sound at 6 a.m. = 警报将在早上6点响起。
The police siren will likely wail as they approach. = 警车临近时很可能会鸣响警报。


虚拟语气:
If the siren sounded, we would evacuate immediately. = 如果警报响了，我们将立即撤离。
I wish the siren hadn't sounded so unexpectedly. = 我真希望警报没有这么突然地响起。


被动语态:
The siren was heard from miles away. = 几英里外都能听到警报声。
The siren was tested this morning. = 警报今天早上进行了测试。


疑问句:
Did you hear the siren? = 你听到警报声了吗？
What kind of siren is that? = 那是哪种警报器？


否定句:
The siren didn't sound during the fire. = 火灾期间警报没有响起。
I didn't hear the siren at all. = 我根本没有听到警报声。
