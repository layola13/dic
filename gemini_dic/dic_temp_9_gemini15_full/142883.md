out-of-step: /ˌaʊt əv stɛp/ | adj. | 不合拍的；不同步的；格格不入的 | Not in agreement or harmony with something else; not keeping pace with others.  | 例句：His ideas were completely out-of-step with the company's goals. 他的想法与公司的目标完全不符。

例句：The drummer was out-of-step with the rest of the band.  鼓手和其他乐队成员节奏不一致。


近义词：discordant, out of sync, misaligned, uncoordinated, at odds

反义词：in step, synchronized, harmonious, coordinated, consistent


词性：形容词
