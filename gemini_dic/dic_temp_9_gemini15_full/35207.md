clubster:/ˈklʌbstər/| n. |俱乐部成员；俱乐部式的；（尤指）小型豪华跑车|A clubster is a member of a club, or something (especially a small, luxurious sports car) that is characteristic of a club or its members.  The term often implies exclusivity and a certain level of sophistication.|例句：He's a clubster at the exclusive golf club. 他是那个高尔夫俱乐部里的会员。  The new car is a real clubster, sleek and powerful. 这辆新车真是辆豪华跑车，线条流畅，动力强劲。|近义词：member, clubman (for member aspect);  sports car, luxury car (for car aspect)|反义词：non-member (for member aspect);  economy car (for car aspect)
