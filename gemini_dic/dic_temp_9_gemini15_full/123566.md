monsterpiece:/ˈmɒnstəpiːs/| n. |杰作，旷世巨作|A monsterpiece is a work of art, literature, music, etc., that is exceptionally good or impressive; it's often used informally and hyperbolically to emphasize the greatness of something.|例句：His new novel is a monsterpiece! 他的新小说是一部杰作！  例句：That painting is a total monsterpiece, isn't it? 那幅画真是太棒了，不是吗？|近义词：masterpiece, magnum opus|反义词：flop, failure|名词
