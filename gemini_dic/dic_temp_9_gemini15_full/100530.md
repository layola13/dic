intervisiting: /ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪtɪŋ/ | v. |互相访问，互访 |The act of people from different organizations or groups visiting each other's workplaces or facilities for the purpose of learning, exchanging ideas, or building relationships.  This is often done to foster collaboration, share best practices, or improve understanding between different entities. |例句：Several schools participated in an intervisiting program to share teaching methodologies.  几个学校参与了一个互访项目以分享教学方法。


例句：The two companies engaged in intervisiting to improve their manufacturing processes. 两家公司进行互访以改进其制造流程。


近义词：mutual visits, reciprocal visits, cross-visits |反义词：isolation, seclusion |词性：动词
