deifies:/ˈdiːɪfaɪz/| v.|神化；尊为神|To regard or represent as a god or goddess; to ascribe divine attributes to.|例句：Many cultures deify their ancestors. 许多文化都将他们的祖先神化。|例句：The media often deifies successful athletes. 媒体经常将成功的运动员神化。|近义词：idolize, apotheosize, elevate|反义词：denigrate, vilify, belittle|动词
