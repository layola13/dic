lilywort:/ˈlɪliwɜːrt/| n. |百合草，一种百合科植物|A lilywort refers to any plant of the lily family, Liliaceae.  The term is somewhat archaic and not often used in modern botany, often replaced by more specific names for individual species.  It encompasses a wide range of plants known for their flowers.|The meadow was filled with various lilyworts, their vibrant colors a testament to the season.|近义词：lily (more specific), liliaceous plant|反义词：无
