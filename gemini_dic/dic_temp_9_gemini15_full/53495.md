disassembled:/ˌdɪs.əˈsɛm.bəld/ | vt. | 拆卸；分解；解体 | To take something apart into its separate components.  | The mechanic disassembled the engine to repair it. 技工拆卸了发动机以便修理。  The old building was disassembled brick by brick.  那座旧建筑被一块砖一块砖地拆卸。 | assembled, put together | assembled, constructed | v.


USAGE->

They disassemble the machine every year for maintenance. = 他们每年都拆卸机器进行维护。
We disassemble the toy to understand how it works. = 我们拆卸玩具以了解它的工作原理。


一般过去时:
