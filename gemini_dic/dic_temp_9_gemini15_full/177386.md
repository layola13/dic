reauthorizes:/ˌriˈɔːθəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |重新授权；再次授权 | To give authority or power to someone or something again.  It implies a previous grant of authority that has either expired or needs renewal. |例句：The committee reauthorizes the project for another year. 委员会再次批准该项目延长一年。

The Senate reauthorizes the transportation bill, ensuring continued funding for infrastructure projects. 参议院重新授权交通法案，确保基础设施项目的持续资金。


近义词：renew, re-empower, reinstate, revalidate | 反义词：revoke, cancel, annul, invalidate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government reauthorizes the program annually. = 政府每年都会重新授权该项目。
They reauthorize the permits every five years. = 他们每五年重新授权一次许可证。


一般过去时:
Congress reauthorized the act last month. = 国会在上个月重新授权了该法案。
The board reauthorized the expenditure. = 董事会重新授权了这笔支出。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The committee had reauthorized the policy prior to the incident. = 在事件发生之前，委员会已经重新授权了该政策。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
