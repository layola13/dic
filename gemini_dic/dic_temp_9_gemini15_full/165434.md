prelector: /prɪˈlɛktər/ | n. |宣读者；演讲者；朗读者 | A person who reads aloud, especially a reader of lectures or other public readings.  |Example: The prelector delivered a compelling reading of the ancient poem.  宣读者以引人入胜的方式朗读了这首古老的诗歌。|近义词：reader, lecturer, orator |反义词：listener, audience


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prelector reads aloud every morning. = 宣读者每天早上大声朗读。
He prelects on ancient history. = 他宣讲古代历史。


一般过去时:
The prelector read a passage from Shakespeare. = 宣读者朗读了一段莎士比亚的片段。
He prelected eloquently on the subject. = 他雄辩地就这个话题发表了演讲。


现在完成时:
The prelector has read many classic works. = 宣读者已经朗读过许多经典作品。
He has prelected at numerous universities. = 他曾在许多大学做过演讲。


过去完成时:
The prelector had already read the entire manuscript before the event. = 在活动开始前，宣读者已经读完了整篇稿件。
He had prelected on this topic several times before. = 他之前已经多次就这个话题发表过演讲。


将来时:
The prelector will read from the new novel tomorrow. = 宣读者明天将朗读新小说。
He will prelect at the conference next week. = 他下周将在会议上发表演讲。


虚拟语气:
If the prelector were here, he would read to us. = 如果宣读者在这里，他会给我们朗读。
I wish the prelector had prelected more clearly. = 我希望宣读者讲得更清楚些。


被动语态:
The poem was prelected beautifully by the student. = 这首诗被学生优美地朗读了。
The lecture was prelected with great passion. = 这场演讲充满激情地进行了。


疑问句:
Will the prelector read the entire book? = 宣读者会朗读整本书吗？
Who will prelect at the graduation ceremony? = 谁将在毕业典礼上发表演讲？


否定句:
The prelector did not read the last chapter. = 宣读者没有朗读最后一章。
He did not prelect on that particular topic. = 他没有就那个特定话题发表演讲。
