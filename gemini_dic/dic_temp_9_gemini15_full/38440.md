concertmaster:/ˈkɒnsətmɑːstə(r)/|/ˈkɑːnsərtˌmæstər/| n. |首席小提琴手|The concertmaster is the leader of the first violin section in an orchestra and often acts as the soloist in certain pieces.  The concertmaster is responsible for tuning the orchestra and often assists the conductor in rehearsals. |The concertmaster gave a stunning solo performance. 首席小提琴手作了一场令人惊叹的独奏表演。 The orchestra's concertmaster is renowned for her precision and artistry.  乐队的首席小提琴手以其精准和艺术造诣而闻名。|leader, first violinist|
