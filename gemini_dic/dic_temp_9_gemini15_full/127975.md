neoterist:/ˈniːəʊtərɪst/| n. |新事物爱好者，新词语爱好者|A neoterist is someone who is enthusiastic about new things, especially new words and phrases.  They often adopt and use newly coined words and expressions. |例句：He's a neoterist, always incorporating the latest slang into his conversations. 他是个新词语爱好者，总是把最新的俚语融入他的谈话中。|近义词：innovator, modernizer,  early adopter |反义词：traditionalist, conservative, antiquarian |名词
