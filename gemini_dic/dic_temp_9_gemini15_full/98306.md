insightfully:/ˈɪnsaɪtfʊli/ | adv. | 富有洞察力地；有见地地 | Showing a deep understanding of people and situations.  It suggests a perceptive and insightful way of thinking or acting. |  He insightfully analyzed the political situation. 他富有洞察力地分析了政治局势。  She insightfully addressed the complex issues. 她有见地地解决了这些复杂的问题。 | perceptively, shrewdly, acutely | obtusely, blindly, unperceptively | 副词
