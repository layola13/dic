penalises:/ˈpiːnəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |惩罚，处罚 | To punish or inflict a penalty on someone or something.  | The company penalises employees for lateness. 这家公司会惩罚迟到的员工。 | punish, sanction, chastise | reward, pardon | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school penalises students who cheat. = 学校惩罚作弊的学生。
The government penalises companies that pollute the environment. = 政府惩罚污染环境的公司。

一般过去时:
The judge penalised the defendant for contempt of court. = 法官因藐视法庭而惩罚了被告。
The referee penalised the player for a foul. = 裁判因犯规而惩罚了球员。

现在完成时:
The team has been penalised heavily for their poor performance. = 该队因表现不佳而受到了严厉的处罚。
The company has penalised several employees for misconduct. = 公司已因行为不端而惩罚了几名员工。

过去完成时:
He had been penalised before for the same offense. = 他以前曾因同样的罪行受到惩罚。
The team had already been penalised twice before the end of the game. = 在比赛结束前，该队已经被罚了两次。

将来时:
The court will penalise the offender severely. = 法院将严厉惩罚罪犯。
They will be penalised if they break the rules. = 如果他们违反规定，他们将受到惩罚。

虚拟语气:
If he had followed the rules, he wouldn't have been penalised. = 如果他遵守规则，他就不会受到惩罚。
I wish they hadn't been penalised so harshly. = 我希望他们没有受到如此严厉的惩罚。

被动语态:
The driver was penalised for speeding. = 司机因超速而受到处罚。
The team was penalised for a late substitution. = 球队因迟迟未换人而受到处罚。

疑问句:
Will the company penalise him for his mistake? = 公司会因他的错误而惩罚他吗？
Were the players penalised for arguing with the referee? = 球员们因与裁判争论而受到惩罚了吗？

否定句:
The judge did not penalise the defendant. = 法官没有惩罚被告。
The school does not penalise students for minor infractions. = 学校不会因轻微违规而惩罚学生。
