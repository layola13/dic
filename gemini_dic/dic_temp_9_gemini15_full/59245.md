e-float:/ˈiːfloʊt/ | n. | 电子浮标；电子浮子 | An e-float is a type of electronic buoy used for various purposes, often in marine environments.  It typically incorporates sensors and communication systems to collect and transmit data.  | Example: The researchers deployed an e-float to monitor ocean currents and water temperature.  研究人员部署了一个电子浮标来监测洋流和水温。 |近义词: electronic buoy, sensor buoy | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其功能相反的设备，例如：fixed sensor) | 名词
