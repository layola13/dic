plunderer:/ˈplʌndərər/| n. |掠夺者，抢劫者 |A plunderer is someone who steals goods, especially during a war or raid. They take things by force and violence.  |例句：The Viking plunderers sacked the coastal villages. 维京掠夺者洗劫了沿海村庄。|例句：The pirates were notorious plunderers of the seas. 海盗是臭名昭著的海上掠夺者。|近义词：looter, robber, raider, thief |反义词：benefactor, giver, protector

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plunderer steals valuable artifacts.  = 掠夺者偷窃珍贵的文物。
He plunders the city for gold. = 他洗劫这座城市以获取黄金。

一般过去时:
The plunderer stole all the gold. = 掠夺者偷走了所有的金子。
They plundered the village and left it in ruins. = 他们洗劫了村庄，把它夷为平地。

现在完成时:
The plunderers have plundered many cities. = 掠夺者已经洗劫了许多城市。
He has plundered the treasury of its riches. = 他已经掠夺了国库的财富。

过去完成时:
The villagers had already hidden their valuables before the plunderers arrived. = 在掠夺者到来之前，村民们已经藏起了他们的贵重物品。
The army had plundered the city before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，军队已经洗劫了这座城市。

将来时:
The pirates will plunder the merchant ships. = 海盗将掠夺商船。
He will plunder the museum for priceless paintings. = 他将洗劫博物馆以获取无价的绘画作品。

虚拟语气:
If he were a plunderer, he would take everything. = 如果他是一个掠夺者，他会拿走一切。
I wish the plunderers hadn't destroyed the temple. = 我希望掠夺者没有破坏寺庙。

被动语态:
The city was plundered by the invaders. = 这个城市被入侵者洗劫了。
The museum's treasures were plundered centuries ago. = 博物馆的珍宝几个世纪前被洗劫一空。

疑问句:
Who was the plunderer? = 掠夺者是谁？
Were the treasures plundered by the same group? = 这些财宝是被同一伙人洗劫的吗？

否定句:
He is not a plunderer. = 他不是掠夺者。
They did not plunder the town. = 他们没有洗劫这座城镇。
