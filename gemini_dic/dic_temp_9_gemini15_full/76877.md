gangrene:/ˈɡæŋɡriːn/ | n. |坏疽，坏死 | The death of body tissue due to lack of blood flow or infection.  It is characterized by discoloration, swelling, and pain.  Severe cases can lead to amputation. |例句：The untreated wound became infected and developed gangrene.  未经处理的伤口感染并发展成坏疽。

例句：The diabetic patient suffered from gangrene in his toes.  这位糖尿病患者的脚趾患有坏疽。


近义词：necrosis, mortification | 反义词：healing, recovery | 词性：名词

词源:
From Late Latin *gangraena, from Greek γάγγραινα (gánggraina), from γάγγραινον (gánggrainon), neuter present participle of γαγγραίνω (gangrainō) "to eat like a sore," related to γάγγραινα (gánggraina) "ulcer, sore."  The ultimate origin is uncertain, but may be pre-Greek.
