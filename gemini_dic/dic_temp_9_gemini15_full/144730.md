over:/ˈoʊvər/ /ˈəʊvə(r)/ | adv., prep., adj., v. |超过，在…之上，结束，遍及 |  英文解析：Over has multiple meanings depending on its grammatical function. As an adverb, it means above, across, or finished. As a preposition, it means above, across, or during. As an adjective, it means finished or ended. As a verb, it means to cover or to overcome. |例句：The bird flew over the house. (adv.) 鸟飞过了房子。  The meeting is over. (adj.) 会议结束了。|近义词：above, across, finished, beyond, throughout |反义词：under, below, beneath, unfinished, started
