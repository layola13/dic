polyzoa:/pɒliˈzoʊə/| n. |苔藓动物|Polyzoa is a taxonomic term, now largely obsolete, referring to a group of aquatic invertebrate animals that are now classified within the phylum Bryozoa.  They are characterized by their colonial nature and tiny, often microscopic, individual zooids. |例句：The study examined the distribution of polyzoa in the coastal waters. 这项研究考察了沿海水域中苔藓动物的分布。|近义词：Bryozoa (preferred term)|反义词：无|名词
