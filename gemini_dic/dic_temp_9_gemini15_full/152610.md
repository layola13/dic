peach:/piːtʃ/| n.|桃子；桃色|A peach is a round fruit with sweet, juicy flesh and fuzzy skin.  It is usually yellowish or reddish in color.|例句：I ate a delicious peach for dessert. 我吃了一个美味的桃子作为甜点。

例句：The peach tree blossomed beautifully in spring. 桃树在春天开得非常漂亮。

近义词：nectarine, apricot|反义词：无|词性:名词

词源:
The word "peach" comes from the Old French "peche," which ultimately derives from the Latin "persica," meaning "Persian." This reflects the fruit's origin in Persia (modern-day Iran), from where it spread to other parts of the world.
