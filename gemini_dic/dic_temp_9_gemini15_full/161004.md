postbags: /ˈpoʊstˌbæɡz/ | n. |邮袋；邮件包| Bags used to carry mail.  The term is now largely archaic, referring to the bags used to transport mail before modern postal systems.  |Example: The postman sorted the postbags before delivering the mail.  邮递员在投递邮件之前整理了邮袋。|近义词：mailbags, postal bags |反义词：无
