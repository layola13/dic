plied:/plaɪd/| v. |使用；运用；从事；恳求；折叠；编织|to use, employ, or work with something; to apply oneself diligently to a task; to earnestly request or implore; to fold or intertwine (as in fabric); to weave or plait|例句：He plied his trade skillfully. 他熟练地从事他的行业。|例句：She plied him with questions. 她向他提了一连串问题。|近义词：used, employed, applied, utilized, worked, requested, pleaded|反义词：neglected, ignored, abandoned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plies his trade as a carpenter. = 他以木匠为生。
She plies her needle with great skill. = 她非常熟练地使用针线。


一般过去时:
The sailors plied their oars through the rough sea. = 水手们在汹涌的大海中奋力划桨。
He plied the hammer all day long. = 他整天都在使劲敲锤子。


现在完成时:
They have plied their skills to build a beautiful home. = 他们运用他们的技能建造了一个美丽的房子。
She has plied her craft for over twenty years. = 她从事她的手艺已经有二十多年了。


过去完成时:
He had plied his trade for years before he retired. = 他退休前从事他的行业已经很多年了。
They had plied the waters with their fishing nets all day. = 他们一整天都在水里用渔网捕鱼。


将来时:
She will ply her skills in the new job. = 她将在新工作中运用她的技能。
He will ply the roads with his new truck. = 他将用他的新卡车行驶在路上。


虚拟语气:
If he plied his talents more diligently, he would succeed. = 如果他更努力地发挥自己的才能，他就会成功。
I wish I had plied my trade more effectively. = 我希望我以前能更有效地从事我的行业。


被动语态:
The wood was plied with a sharp chisel. = 这块木头是用锋利的凿子雕刻的。
The fabric was plied and woven into a beautiful tapestry. = 这块织物被折叠和编织成一幅美丽的挂毯。


疑问句:
How does he ply his trade? = 他是如何从事他的行业的？
What tools does she ply in her work? = 她在工作中使用什么工具？


否定句:
He didn't ply his trade effectively that day. = 他那天没有有效地从事他的行业。
She didn't ply the oars hard enough to reach the shore. = 她划桨不够用力，无法到达岸边。
