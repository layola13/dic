overfrieze:/ˈoʊvərfriːz/| n. |装饰带，檐口饰带|A decorative band, typically of carved stone or plaster, running along the top of a wall or building, just below the roofline or cornice.  It often features sculpted figures, patterns, or other ornamentation. | The overfrieze of the ancient temple was intricately carved with scenes of mythical creatures.  古庙的檐口饰带精细地雕刻着神话生物的场景。| frieze, cornice, molding |  (无明确反义词，因为是描述性词语)|名词
