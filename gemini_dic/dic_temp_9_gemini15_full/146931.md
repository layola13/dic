overintensifications:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪfɪkeɪʃənz/| n.|过度强化；过分强调 |The excessive strengthening or emphasis of something, often leading to negative consequences.  It refers to situations where something is amplified beyond its necessary or optimal level.  The term often implies a distortion or exaggeration of the original meaning or effect. |例句：The overintensifications of security measures created an atmosphere of fear and distrust.  过度强化的安全措施造成了恐惧和不信任的氛围。 The overintensifications in the marketing campaign backfired, alienating potential customers.  营销活动中的过度强化适得其反，疏远了潜在客户。|近义词：exaggeration, overemphasis, hyperbole|反义词：understatement, downplaying, minimization|名词
