erostrate:/ˈɛrəstreɪt/ | vt. |纵火焚烧（尤指建筑物）|To set fire to (a building, especially a prominent one), deliberately and maliciously.  The term often implies a deliberate act of vandalism or an attack on a symbol of power or authority.|Example: The protesters threatened to erostrate the government building.  抗议者威胁要纵火焚烧政府大楼。|近义词：incinerate, burn, firebomb |反义词：extinguish, put out
