carbohydride:/ˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdraɪd/| n. |碳氢化合物|A compound of carbon and hydrogen only.  Carbohydrides are the simplest organic compounds and form the basis for many other organic molecules. They can be saturated (alkanes), unsaturated (alkenes and alkynes), or cyclic.|Example: Methane (CH4) is a simple carbohydride.|近义词：hydrocarbon|反义词：无
