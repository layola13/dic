rubs:/rʌbz/ | vt. n.|擦，摩擦；揉搓；(使)恼火|to move one's hand or another object backwards and forwards over a surface|He rubs his eyes sleepily. 他睡眼惺忪地揉了揉眼睛。|近义词：wipe, scrub, chafe|反义词：polish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rubs his hands together. = 他搓搓手。
She rubs the table clean. = 她把桌子擦干净了。


一般过去时:
I rubbed the lotion into my skin. = 我把润肤露擦进皮肤里。
He rubbed the stain until it disappeared. = 他擦拭污渍直到它消失。


现在完成时:
I have rubbed the dirt off my shoes. = 我已经把鞋上的泥土擦掉了。
She has rubbed the paint until it's smooth. = 她已经把油漆擦到光滑为止。


过去完成时:
He had rubbed the candle wax off the tablecloth before his guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把桌布上的蜡烛油擦掉了。
She had rubbed her eyes until they were red. = 她揉眼睛揉到眼睛都红了。


将来时:
I will rub the car clean tomorrow. = 我明天会把车擦干净。
She will rub some ointment on the wound. = 她会在伤口上涂一些药膏。


虚拟语气:
If I rubbed this magic lamp, I might find a genie. = 如果我摩擦这个神灯，我可能会找到一个精灵。
I wish I could rub away my worries. = 我真希望我能消除我的烦恼。


被动语态:
The table was rubbed clean. = 桌子被擦干净了。
The lotion was rubbed into his skin. = 润肤露被擦进他的皮肤里了。


疑问句:
Are you rubbing your eyes? = 你在揉眼睛吗？
Why are you rubbing that stain? = 你为什么要擦拭那个污渍？


否定句:
He didn't rub the mud off his boots. = 他没有把靴子上的泥巴擦掉。
She didn't rub the lotion on her sunburn. = 她没有把润肤露涂在晒伤的皮肤上。
