exemplary:/ɪɡˈzɛmpləri/ | adj. | 模范的；值得效仿的；典范的 |  Worthy of imitation; serving as a model; excellent.  It describes something or someone that serves as an excellent example or model, setting a high standard for others to follow. |例句：Her work is exemplary, showing great skill and dedication.  她的作品堪称典范，展现了精湛的技艺和奉献精神。


例句：His commitment to environmental protection is exemplary. 他对环境保护的承诺是模范的。


近义词：model, ideal, perfect, outstanding, excellent


反义词：bad, poor, inferior, substandard
