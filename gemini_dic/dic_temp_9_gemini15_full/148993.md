ovoviviparism:/ˌoʊvoʊvaɪˈvɪpərɪzəm/| n. |卵胎生|The reproductive method in which eggs develop and hatch within the mother's body, but without a placental connection; the young are nourished by the egg yolk.  卵胎生，一种繁殖方式，胚胎在母体内存活和孵化，但没有胎盘连接；幼体由卵黄提供营养。| Example:  Many species of sharks exhibit ovoviviparism. 许多鲨鱼物种表现出卵胎生。|近义词: viviparity (in some contexts),  matrotrophy (when referring to nourishment)|反义词: oviparity
