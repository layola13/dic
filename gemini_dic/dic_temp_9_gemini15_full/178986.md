redefeat:/ˌriːdɪˈfiːt/ | vt. |再次击败；再次战胜|To defeat again; to overcome a second time.  |例句：The champion was redefeated in the final match. 冠军在决赛中再次败北。|例句：After a period of recovery, the team redefeated their rivals. 经过一段时间的恢复，球队再次击败了他们的对手。|近义词：defeat again, overcome again, vanquish again |反义词：be defeated, be overcome, be vanquished
