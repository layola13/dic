scoutingly:/ˈskaʊtɪŋli/ | adv. |侦察般地，探测般地 |In a manner suggestive of scouting or exploration; cautiously and carefully.  |例句：He moved scoutingly through the undergrowth, his eyes scanning for any sign of danger. 他小心翼翼地穿过灌木丛，眼睛扫描着任何危险的迹象。


例句：She looked scoutingly around the room before entering. 她进入房间前侦察般地环顾四周。


近义词： cautiously, carefully, watchfully, investigatively


反义词：recklessly, carelessly, heedlessly


词性：副词
