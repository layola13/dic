moore:/mʊr/| n. |摩尔；沼泽；荒野|The word "moore" refers to a person from the Moorish people, historically inhabiting parts of North Africa and the Iberian Peninsula.  It can also refer to a type of dark, boggy land, or a vast, uncultivated area.  The usage depends heavily on context.|例句：He studied the history of the Moors in Spain. 他研究了西班牙摩尔人的历史。|近义词：moorland, heath, bog, wasteland |反义词：cultivated land, farmland
