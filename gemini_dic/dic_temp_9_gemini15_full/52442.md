die-hard:/ˈdaɪˌhɑːrd/| adj.|顽固的；坚定的；忠实的|Someone who is die-hard is extremely loyal or committed to something, even when faced with difficulties or opposition.  They are unwilling to change their beliefs or allegiances.|例句：He's a die-hard fan of the team, even when they lose. 他是这个队的铁杆球迷，即使他们输了比赛。|例句：She's a die-hard supporter of the government's policies. 她是政府政策的坚定支持者。|近义词：stubborn, resolute, committed, staunch, unwavering|反义词：wavering, fickle, irresolute, apathetic
