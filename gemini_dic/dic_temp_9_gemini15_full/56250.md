dog-tired: /dɒɡˈtaɪəd/ | adj. | 非常疲惫的，精疲力竭的 | Extremely tired; exhausted.  |  例句：After a long day of hiking, I was dog-tired.  = 长途跋涉一天后，我累得筋疲力尽。

例句：I'm dog-tired after working overtime all week. =  加班一周后我累坏了。


近义词：exhausted, worn out, beat, wiped out, fatigued


反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated


词源:  The origin of "dog-tired" is unclear, but it's likely a figurative expression. "Dog" here doesn't refer to literal dogs, but rather implies a state of being utterly exhausted, like a dog after a long chase.  The image evokes a sense of complete physical and mental depletion.  The term likely emerged in the late 19th or early 20th century, becoming part of colloquial English.
