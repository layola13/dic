plesiomorphous:/ˌpliːziəˈmɔːrfəs/ | adj. | 祖征的，原始的 |  Referring to a primitive character state,  a trait that is inherited from a distant ancestor and has not changed significantly over evolutionary time.  It is the ancestral character state, and may be retained or lost in descendants.  | Example: The five-fingered hand is a plesiomorphous trait for mammals.  哺乳动物的五指手是祖征。 | 近义词：ancestral, primitive | 反义词：apomorph, derived, specialized
