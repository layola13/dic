mormonist:/ˈmɔːrmənɪst/| n. |摩门教徒；摩门教信徒|A member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.  The term is sometimes considered offensive by members of the church, who prefer to be called Latter-day Saints.|例句：He is a devout Mormonist and actively participates in church activities. 他是一位虔诚的摩门教徒，积极参与教会活动。


例句：Many Mormonists dedicate their lives to missionary work. 许多摩门教徒将一生奉献给传教工作。


近义词：Latter-day Saint, member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints


反义词：None (This is a religious affiliation, not something with a direct opposite.)


词源: From "Mormon," a term derived from the Book of Mormon, a central religious text of the Latter-day Saints.  The suffix "-ist" indicates a follower or believer.
