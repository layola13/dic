overclassify:/ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|过度分类，分类过度|To classify something too much or in too much detail, often leading to unnecessary complexity or confusion.  This often results in a loss of useful information due to excessive categorization.|例句：The librarian overclassified the books, making it difficult to find what we needed. 图书管理员对书籍进行了过度分类，使得我们很难找到所需书籍。

例句：The researchers overclassified the data, obscuring important patterns. 研究人员对数据进行了过度分类，掩盖了重要的模式。


近义词：overcategorize, overcompartmentalize


反义词：underclassify, generalize

词性：动词
