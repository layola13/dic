licentiateship:/laɪˈsɛnʃɪeɪtʃɪp/| n. |特许资格；执照；准许|The status or right granted to a person who has been licensed to practice a profession.  It often involves passing an examination or fulfilling specific requirements.  |例句：After years of study, she finally achieved her licentiateship in medicine.  多年学习后，她终于获得了行医执照。

例句：His licentiateship in law allows him to practice independently. 他作为律师的资格允许他独立执业。


近义词：license, authorization, permission, qualification, accreditation

反义词：prohibition, disqualification, ban

词性：名词
