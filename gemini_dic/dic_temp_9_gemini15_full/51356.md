detentions:/dɪˈtenʃənz/ | n. | 拘留；留堂；滞留 | The act of detaining someone or something; a period of being kept in custody or confinement, often for disciplinary reasons in school;  a delay or holding back.  |例句：The student received detentions for misbehavior.  这个学生因为行为不端受到了留堂处分。

例句：The ship experienced several detentions due to bad weather. 这艘船因为恶劣天气经历了几次滞留。


近义词：confinements, arrests, delays, restraints | 反义词：releases, freedoms


词源：源自于动词 "detain",  由古法语 "detenir"而来，其词根是拉丁语的 "tenēre" (to hold).
