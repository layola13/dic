non-collection: /ˌnɒn kəˈlɛkʃən/ | adj. n. | 非收集；未收集 |  Describes something that is not a collection or the act of not collecting something.  It can function as an adjective modifying a noun or as a noun referring to the state of not being collected. |

例句：

The museum's non-collection items were stored in the basement. = 博物馆的非收藏品被储存在地下室。

His non-collection of stamps was surprising, given his interest in philately. =  鉴于他对集邮的兴趣，他没有收集邮票这令人惊讶。


近义词：lack of collection, absence of collection


反义词：collection


词性：adj. n.
