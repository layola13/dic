connote:/kənˈnoʊt/| vt.|暗示；隐含；意味着|to suggest or imply in addition to the literal or primary meaning |The word "home" connotes warmth and security.  这个词“家”暗示着温暖和安全。|近义词：imply, suggest, indicate|反义词：denote


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The word "childlike" connotes innocence. = “孩子气”这个词暗示着天真无邪。
This painting connotes a sense of sadness. = 这幅画暗示着一种悲伤的感觉。


一般过去时:
The poem connoted a feeling of loss. = 这首诗暗示了一种失落感。
Her silence connoted disapproval. = 她的沉默暗示着反对。


现在完成时:
His actions have connoted a lack of respect. = 他的行为暗示着缺乏尊重。
The symbolism in the novel has connoted a deeper meaning. = 小说中的象征意义暗示了更深层次的含义。


过去完成时:
The old photograph had connoted a sense of nostalgia. = 那张旧照片曾暗示着一种怀旧之情。
His previous behaviour had connoted a pattern of deceit. = 他之前的行为暗示了一种欺骗的模式。


将来时:
The new advertising campaign will connote luxury and sophistication. = 新的广告宣传活动将暗示奢华和精致。
His upcoming speech will connote a shift in political strategy. = 他即将发表的演讲将暗示政治策略的转变。


虚拟语气:
If the painting connoted joy, it would be more appealing. = 如果这幅画暗示着快乐，它就会更吸引人。
If his words had connoted sincerity, she would have believed him. = 如果他的话暗示着真诚，她就会相信他。


被动语态:
Innocence is connoted by the word "childlike". = 天真无邪是由“孩子气”这个词暗示的。
A sense of unease was connoted by the strange noises. = 奇怪的声音暗示了一种不安的感觉。


疑问句:
What does this symbol connote? = 这个符号暗示着什么？
What feelings does the music connote? = 这音乐暗示着什么情感？


否定句:
The word "cheap" does not connote quality. = “便宜”这个词并不暗示质量。
The painting did not connote any negative emotions. = 这幅画并没有暗示任何负面情绪。
