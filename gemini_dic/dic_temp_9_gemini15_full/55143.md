dissecting:/dɪˈsɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 解剖；仔细分析 | To cut up (a body, plant, or other organism) in order to study its internal parts.  To analyze something in detail in order to understand its nature or structure. | Example: The students were dissecting frogs in biology class. 学生们在上生物课时正在解剖青蛙。The article dissects the political motives behind the decision. 这篇文章仔细分析了该决定背后的政治动机。|近义词：analyzing, examining, scrutinizing | 反义词：synthesizing, combining, ignoring
