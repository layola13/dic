delivers:/dɪˈlɪvərz/| vt.|递送；发表；履行；交付；给予；生（孩子）|To bring or take something to a person or place.  To give a speech or performance. To fulfill a promise or expectation. To give birth to a baby. |例句：The postman delivers the mail every morning. = 邮递员每天早上送邮件。

The speaker delivered a powerful speech. = 演讲者发表了一篇精彩的演讲。


近义词：conveys, transmits, presents, produces, gives birth to|反义词：receives, collects, withholds|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He delivers newspapers every day. = 他每天送报纸。
She delivers speeches with great passion. = 她充满激情地发表演讲。

一般过去时:
The courier delivered the package yesterday. = 快递员昨天送来了包裹。
The doctor delivered the baby safely. = 医生安全地接生了婴儿。

现在完成时:
She has delivered three babies this year. = 她今年已经接生了三个婴儿。
He has delivered many inspiring lectures. = 他已经发表了许多鼓舞人心的演讲。

过去完成时:
They had delivered the goods before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已交付了货物。
She had delivered a baby only a week before. = 她仅仅在一周前才生了一个孩子。

将来时:
The company will deliver the product next week. = 公司下周将交付产品。
He will deliver a keynote speech at the conference. = 他将在会议上发表主题演讲。

虚拟语气:
If he delivered the speech earlier, he would have more time. = 如果他早点发表演讲，他就会有更多的时间。
If she had delivered the baby at home, it would have been dangerous. = 如果她在家里生孩子，那就危险了。

被动语态:
The package was delivered to the wrong address. = 包裹送到了错误的地址。
The speech was delivered with great clarity. = 演讲表达得非常清晰。

疑问句:
Does he deliver the goods himself? = 他自己送货吗？
When will the results be delivered? = 结果什么时候公布？

否定句:
He didn't deliver the speech as planned. = 他没有按照计划发表演讲。
The company failed to deliver the goods on time. = 公司未能按时交付货物。


PHRASE->
deliver a blow = 给予打击
deliver a message = 传达信息
deliver a verdict = 宣判
deliver the goods = 履行承诺；做到应做的事
deliver a punch = 打一拳
