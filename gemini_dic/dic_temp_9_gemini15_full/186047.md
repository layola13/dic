reworker: /ˌriːˈwɜːrkər/ | n. |返工者，返修者，修改者 | A person who reworks something, such as a piece of writing or a manufactured item.  Someone who performs rework tasks, often correcting errors or improving quality. | Example: The reworker meticulously corrected the flaws in the manuscript.  返工者一丝不苟地纠正了稿件中的缺陷。|近义词： reviser, editor, corrector, modifier |反义词： originator, creator
