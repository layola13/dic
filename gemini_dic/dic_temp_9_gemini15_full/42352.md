counteragents: /ˌkaʊntərˈeɪdʒənts/ | n. |对抗剂；反作用剂；对立物 |

英文解析:A counteragent is a substance or agent that opposes or neutralizes the effects of another substance or agent.  It often refers to something that works against or counteracts a specific action, effect, or substance.  In some contexts, it can also refer to a person or group who opposes another.

例句:

The antidote acted as a counteragent to the poison.  解毒剂作为毒药的对抗剂。

The government's new policies are seen by some as counteragents to economic growth.  一些人认为政府的新政策是对经济增长的反作用剂。


近义词: antidote, antagonist, counteractant, opponent


反义词:  protagonist, synergist (in the context of substances working together)


词性: noun
