preloads:/ˈpriːloʊdz/ | n. |预加载；预先加载的内容 |  The process of loading resources (like images, scripts, or stylesheets) before they are actually needed, to improve website performance.  Preloads can significantly reduce page load times and enhance user experience. | Example: The website uses preloads to ensure fast loading of essential images. 网站使用预加载来确保快速加载重要的图像。  The game preloads assets to minimize loading screens during gameplay. 游戏预加载资源以最大限度地减少游戏过程中的加载屏幕。 |近义词：prefetch, caching | 反义词：postload (not a common word) | 名词
