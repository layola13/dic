clads:/klædz/ | v. | 穿衣；覆盖 | To cover or clothe (something or someone).  The verb "clad" is often used in a more literary or poetic context, emphasizing the act of being covered or adorned. | Example: The mountains were clad in snow.  群山披着白雪。  The children were clad in bright, colorful clothes. 孩子们穿着鲜艳多彩的衣服。 | 近义词：clothe, dress, cover | 反义词：undress, uncover | 动词

The snow clads the mountains. = 雪覆盖着山峦。
She clads herself in elegant gowns. = 她穿着优雅的礼服。
