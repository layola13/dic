scansion:/ˈskænʃən/| n.|音节扫描，音步分析|The analysis of verse into metrical units, especially feet.|The poem's scansion reveals a consistent iambic pentameter. 这首诗的音步分析显示出始终如一的五音步抑扬格。|近义词：metrical analysis, rhythmic analysis|反义词：None
