excluders:/ɪkˈskluːdərz/| n.|排除者；排斥者；隔离器|Those who practice exclusion or discrimination; devices that prevent something from entering or passing through.|例句：The new immigration laws are creating more excluders in society. 新的移民法正在社会中制造更多的排斥者。|例句：The air filter acts as an excluder, preventing dust from entering the room. 空气过滤器充当隔离器，防止灰尘进入房间。|近义词：discriminators, separators, barriers|反义词：includers, integrators


USAGE->

The school excluders bully the new students. = 学校里的排斥者欺负新生。
These filters act as excluders of unwanted particles. = 这些过滤器充当不需要的颗粒的排除器。
