reselecting: /ˌriːsəˈlɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 重新选择，再选择 |  The act of selecting again; choosing something or someone a second time, often after a previous selection has been deemed unsatisfactory or needs replacement.  |例句：The committee is reselecting its chair after the previous one resigned.  委员会主席辞职后，委员会正在重新选举主席。

例句：After the initial candidates failed to meet the requirements, the company is reselecting applicants for the position.  在最初的候选人未能满足要求后，公司正在重新选择该职位申请人。


近义词：rechoosing, selecting again, choosing anew

反义词：deselecting, rejecting


词性：动词


USAGE->
