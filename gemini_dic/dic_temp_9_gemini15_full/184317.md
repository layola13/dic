respecifying: /ˌriːˈspɛsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |重新指定，重新说明 | The act of specifying again; restating or clarifying something previously specified.  It often implies a correction or refinement of the initial specification. | 例句：Respecifying the project requirements after the initial feedback resulted in a more robust design.  重新说明项目需求在获得初步反馈后，导致了更强大的设计。


例句：The engineer is respecifying the parameters of the system to improve its performance. 工程师正在重新指定系统的参数以提高其性能。


近义词：redefining, redefining, clarifying, restating, revising


反义词：vaguely defining, obscuring


词性：动词
