riversides:/ˈrɪvərˌsaɪdz/| n.|河边，河畔|The area along the banks of a river.  It often refers to a scenic or residential area located near a river.|例句：We had a picnic by the riversides. 我们在河边野餐。|例句：Many charming houses line the riversides of the Thames.  许多迷人的房屋排列在泰晤士河畔。|近义词：riverbanks, waterfront, riverside areas|反义词：inland, upcountry
