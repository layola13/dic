rinsed:/rɪnst/| v. |冲洗；漂洗|To wash something, typically with a quick, light flow of water.|例句：I rinsed the dishes before putting them in the dishwasher. 我把盘子冲洗干净后再放进洗碗机。


例句：She rinsed her hair with conditioner after shampooing. 洗发后，她用护发素冲洗头发。

近义词：washed, cleaned, purified | 反义词：soiled, dirty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rinse the dishes every day. = 我每天都冲洗碗碟。
He rinses his mouth after brushing his teeth. = 他刷牙后漱口。

一般过去时:
I rinsed the mud off my shoes. = 我把鞋子上的泥冲掉了。
She rinsed the lettuce before adding it to the salad. = 她把生菜冲洗干净后再添加到沙拉中。

现在完成时:
I have rinsed the paintbrushes. = 我已经冲洗了画笔。
She has rinsed her hair with cold water. = 她用冷水冲洗了头发。

过去完成时:
I had rinsed the vegetables before I started cooking. = 我在开始做饭之前已经冲洗了蔬菜。
They had rinsed the glasses before putting them away. = 他们把杯子收起来之前已经冲洗过了。

将来时:
I will rinse the clothes before putting them in the dryer. = 我会把衣服冲洗干净再放进烘干机。
She will rinse her hair with apple cider vinegar. = 她会用苹果醋冲洗头发。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rinse the car. = 如果我有更多时间，我会冲洗汽车。

被动语态:
