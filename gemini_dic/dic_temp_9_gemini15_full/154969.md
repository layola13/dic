pestling:/ˈpɛstliŋ/| n.|研磨；捣碎；杵臼|The act of pounding or grinding something, usually with a pestle.  It often refers to the process of crushing herbs, spices, or other ingredients.|例句：The pestling of the garlic released its fragrant aroma.  捣蒜释放出它的芬芳香味。|例句：The rhythmic pestling of the mortar and pestle filled the kitchen.  研钵和杵的节奏性研磨声充满了厨房。|近义词：grinding, crushing, pounding|反义词：None specifically antonymous, but could be considered the opposite of  "leaving whole".|名词
