overorganizes:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/| v. |过度组织；安排过度|To organize something to an excessive degree, often resulting in inefficiency or unnecessary complexity.  The excessive planning and scheduling can lead to stress and reduced effectiveness. | 例句：He overorganizes every aspect of his life, leaving little room for spontaneity. 他把生活的方方面面都安排得过于细致，几乎没有留下任何空间去即兴发挥。


例句：She overorganizes her projects, creating unnecessary complexity and delaying completion. 她把项目安排得过于复杂，导致不必要的复杂性和延误完成。


近义词：overplan, micromanage, overschedule


反义词：underorganize, haphazard, disorganized


词性:动词
