second-handness: /ˌsekəndˈhændnəs/ | n. | 二手性质；间接性；转述；间接获得的事物 | The quality or state of being second-hand.  This can refer to the condition of something being previously owned, or to information being received indirectly. |

例句：

The second-handness of the information made it unreliable.  信息的间接性使其不可靠。

The car's second-handness was evident in its worn upholstery. 这辆车的二手性质在其磨损的内饰中显而易见。


近义词：usedness, indirectness, secondhand, pre-owned

反义词：newness, originality, firsthand

词性: 名词
