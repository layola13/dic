disintegrators:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtərz/| n. |粉碎机；崩解器；瓦解者|A machine or device that breaks something down into small pieces or causes it to decay.  It can refer to devices used in various industries, from food processing to waste management, to break down materials.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe something that causes disintegration or breakdown.|例句：The disintegrators in the factory pulverize the raw materials into fine powder. 工厂里的粉碎机将原材料粉碎成细粉。

The disintegrator reduced the waste to a manageable size.  粉碎机将垃圾减少到易于管理的尺寸。|近义词：pulverizer, crusher, grinder, destroyer|反义词：integrator, unifier, assembler|
