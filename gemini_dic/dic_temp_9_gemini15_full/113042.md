luminous:/ˈluːmɪnəs/| adj.|发光的；明亮的；照耀的|Emitting or reflecting light; shining.  The luminous dial glowed in the dark.  |The moon was luminous in the night sky. 月亮在夜空中发出光芒。 The fireflies put on a luminous display. 萤火虫上演了一场发光表演。|bright, radiant, shining|dark, dull, obscure|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The moon is luminous tonight. = 今晚月亮很明亮。
The city lights are luminous from afar. = 从远处看，城市灯火通明。


一般过去时:
The fireflies were luminous in the garden. = 萤火虫在花园里发出光芒。
The stars were luminous in the clear night sky. = 在晴朗的夜空中，星星闪闪发光。


现在完成时:
The painting has remained luminous for centuries. = 这幅画几个世纪以来一直保持着鲜艳的色彩。
The diamonds have always been luminous and captivating. = 钻石一直闪耀迷人。


过去完成时:
The aurora borealis had been luminous before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，北极光曾发出耀眼的光芒。
The sunset had been luminous and breathtaking. = 日落时分，景色绚丽夺目。


将来时:

The comet will be luminous as it approaches the Earth. = 当彗星接近地球时，它将发出耀眼的光芒。


虚拟语气:
If the sun were luminous, we would have daylight. = 如果太阳发光，我们就会有白天。
If the stars were more luminous, we would see them better. = 如果星星更明亮，我们会看得更清楚。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Is the gemstone luminous? = 这宝石会发光吗？

否定句:
