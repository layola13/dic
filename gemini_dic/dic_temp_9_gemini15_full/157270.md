pinole:/pɪˈnoʊli/| n.|皮诺里（一种美洲原住民食品）|A beverage or food made from ground roasted grain, typically corn, often mixed with water or milk and sometimes other ingredients.  It's a traditional food of various indigenous cultures in the Americas.|The hikers sustained themselves with pinole during their long trek across the desert.  徒步旅行者在穿越沙漠的漫长旅程中靠皮诺里维持生命。|近义词：porridge, gruel|反义词：无
