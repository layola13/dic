nazism:/ˈnɑːtsizm/| n. |纳粹主义|A set of political beliefs associated with the Nazi Party in Germany, characterized by extreme nationalism, racism (especially antisemitism), totalitarian control, and the belief in the superiority of the Aryan race.  It led to the Holocaust and World War II.|The rise of Nazism in Germany led to devastating consequences for the world.|近义词：fascism, totalitarianism|反义词：democracy, liberalism
