delint:/dɪˈlɪnt/| vt.|去除棉绒；整理；清除杂质|To remove lint or fluff from something, typically fabric.  The process involves removing unwanted particles or impurities.|例句：I need to delint this sweater before I wear it.  我需要在穿这件毛衣之前去除上面的棉绒。

例句：The technician delinted the optical fiber to improve signal transmission. 技术人员清理了光纤以改善信号传输。

近义词：clean, purify, remove, fluff

反义词：dirty, contaminate, pollute

词性:动词
