oligo:/ˈɒlɪɡoʊ/| /ˈɑːlɪɡoʊ/ |prefix|寡，少，少量|A combining form meaning “few,” “little,” or “small,” used in the formation of compound words.|例句：Oligonucleotide is a short nucleic acid polymer.寡核苷酸是一种短链核酸聚合物。例句：Oligotrophic lakes have low nutrient levels.贫营养湖泊的营养物质含量低。|近义词：few, little, small, scanty|反义词：poly-, multi-|prefix


USAGE->
This word is a prefix, not a standalone word, so it doesn't have conjugations like verbs or tenses.  It combines with other words to create new words with the meaning of "few," "little," or "small."
