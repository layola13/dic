housewifing:/ˈhaʊsˌwaɪfɪŋ/| n. |家庭主妇的工作；操持家务|The act of managing a household, typically performed by a housewife; encompassing cooking, cleaning, childcare, and other domestic tasks.  It can also refer to the lifestyle or role of a housewife.|例句：She found housewifing to be both rewarding and challenging. 她发现操持家务既有益又充满挑战。|例句：Many women in the past dedicated their lives to housewifing.|许多过去的女人都将一生奉献给了家庭主妇的工作。|近义词：homemaking, home management|反义词：career, professional work|名词
