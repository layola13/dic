estrange:/ɪˈstreɪndʒ/ | vt. |使疏远；使隔阂；使不友好 | To make (someone) feel isolated or alienated.  |例句：The long separation estranged the brothers. 长时间的分别使兄弟俩疏远了。

例句：His recent behavior has estranged him from his friends. 他最近的行为使他与朋友们疏远了。


近义词：alienate, isolate, separate, distance

反义词：unite, reconcile, connect,  close


词性:动词
