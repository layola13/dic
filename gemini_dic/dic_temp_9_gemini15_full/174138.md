queasy:/ˈkwiːzi/| adj.| 恶心的；反胃的；不适的 | Feeling sick in the stomach; nauseous.  The rough boat ride made me feel queasy.  | 近义词：nauseous, nauseated, sick, ill | 反义词：well, comfortable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel queasy after eating that spoiled food. = 吃了那腐烂的食物后，我感到恶心。
The smell makes me feel queasy. = 这个气味让我感觉反胃。


一般过去时:
She felt queasy during her pregnancy. = 她怀孕期间感到恶心。
He became queasy after riding the roller coaster. = 他坐过山车后感到恶心。


现在完成时:
I have felt queasy all morning. = 我整个上午都感到恶心。
She has been queasy since she took the medicine. = 自从她吃了药后就一直感到恶心。


过去完成时:
I had felt queasy before I even got to the doctor's office. = 我甚至还没到医生办公室就感到恶心了。
They had been feeling queasy all day before they finally vomited. = 他们在最终呕吐之前一整天都感到恶心。


将来时:
I think I will feel queasy if I eat any more of this. = 我想如果再吃这个的话我会感到恶心。
She believes she will be queasy on the long flight. = 她认为在漫长的飞行中她会感到恶心。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't eaten so much, I wouldn't feel so queasy. = 如果我没有吃那么多，我就不会感到这么恶心。
I wish I hadn't eaten that; I feel terribly queasy. = 我真希望我没有吃那个；我感觉非常恶心。


被动语态: (This adjective is rarely used passively.)  A passive construction would be unnatural.


疑问句:
Are you feeling queasy? = 你感觉恶心吗？
Does the smell make you feel queasy? = 这个气味让你感觉恶心吗？


否定句:
I don't feel queasy at all. = 我一点也不恶心。
He wasn't feeling queasy after the operation. = 手术后他感觉不恶心。


PHRASE->
feel queasy = 感到恶心
become queasy = 变得恶心
a queasy stomach = 一个不舒服的胃
queasy feeling = 恶心的感觉
slightly queasy = 有点恶心
