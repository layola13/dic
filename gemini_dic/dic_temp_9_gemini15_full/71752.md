fle:/fleɪ/| n. | 蚤 | A small wingless jumping insect that is parasitic on mammals and birds. Fleas are known for their ability to bite and suck blood, causing itching and irritation.  They can also transmit diseases. | Example:  The dog was covered in fleas.  这只狗身上长满了跳蚤。 | 近义词: louse, insect | 反义词:  N/A
