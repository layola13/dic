pleurodynia:/ˌplʊərəˈdaɪniə/| n. |胸痛，肋间神经痛 |A viral infection characterized by sharp, stabbing pains in the chest and abdomen.  It is also known as Bornholm disease. |例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as pleurodynia after examining her symptoms. 医生在检查了她的症状后，诊断她的病情为胸痛。|例句：He suffered from severe pleurodynia, making it difficult to breathe deeply. 他患有严重的胸痛，使他难以深呼吸。|近义词：Bornholm disease, epidemic myalgia |反义词：无
