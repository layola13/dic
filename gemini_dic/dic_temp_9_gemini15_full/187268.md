robotization:/ˌroʊbətaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |机器人化；自动化|The process of replacing human workers with robots or automated systems.  This often involves the integration of robotics, AI, and other technologies to automate tasks and processes across various industries. |例句：The robotization of the factory led to increased efficiency and lower production costs. 工厂的机器人化导致效率提高和生产成本降低。

例句：The increasing robotization of manufacturing is a major trend in the global economy. 制造业日益增长的机器人化是全球经济的一个主要趋势。


近义词：automation, mechanization|反义词：manual labor, humanization
