fissioned:/ˈfɪʃənd/| v.|裂变的；分裂的|To cause or undergo fission.  The process of splitting an atomic nucleus into two or more fragments, releasing a large amount of energy.|The uranium atom was fissioned in the reactor. 反应堆中铀原子发生了裂变。 The cell fissioned, creating two daughter cells. 细胞分裂，产生了两个子细胞。|近义词：split, divided, cleaved|反义词：fused, united, merged
