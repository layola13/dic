macaroons:/ˌmækəˈruːnz/| n. |马卡龙|A small, sweet meringue-based confection made with egg whites, icing sugar, and almond flour, often sandwiched together with buttercream or jam.  Macaroons are known for their delicate texture and vibrant colors.|例句：The bakery is famous for its delicious almond macaroons. 这家面包店以其美味的杏仁马卡龙而闻名。  She bought a box of colorful macaroons as a gift. 她买了一盒五颜六色的马卡龙作为礼物。|近义词：macarons (often used interchangeably, though technically different), meringue cookie|反义词：无
