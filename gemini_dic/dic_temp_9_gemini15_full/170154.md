proton:/ˈproʊtɒn/ /ˈproʊtɑːn/| n. |质子|A proton is a stable subatomic particle occurring in all atomic nuclei, with a positive electric charge equal in magnitude to that of an electron, but with a much larger mass.|例句：Protons are positively charged particles found in the nucleus of an atom.  质子是带正电荷的粒子，存在于原子的原子核中。|例句：The number of protons in an atom's nucleus determines its atomic number. 原子核中质子的数量决定了它的原子序数。|近义词：nucleon|反义词：electron
