kobellite:/ˈkoʊbəlaɪt/| n. |钴铅矿|A sulfide mineral consisting of lead, bismuth, and sulfur, often containing cobalt and other trace elements.  It is typically found in hydrothermal veins and is an important ore of bismuth.|Kobellite crystals are usually found in association with other sulfide minerals.|近义词： bismuthinite (when bismuth is the primary focus)|反义词：无
