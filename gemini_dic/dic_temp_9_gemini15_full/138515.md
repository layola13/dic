nowadays:/ˈnaʊədeɪz/| adv. |现今，如今，现在|Nowadays, people rely heavily on technology. 如今，人们非常依赖科技。|近义词：currently, presently, at present, these days |反义词：formerly, previously, in the past


例句：

Nowadays, many people work from home.  如今，许多人都在家工作。

Nowadays, communication is much faster than before.  如今，沟通比以前快得多。
