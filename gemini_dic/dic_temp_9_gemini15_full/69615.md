feminine:/ˈfemɪnɪn/| adj.|女性的；温柔的；秀美的| Relating to or characteristic of women or girls.  It can also describe things that are considered delicate, graceful, or aesthetically pleasing in a way associated with femininity.|例句：She has a very feminine appearance. 她长相非常女性化。  The poem has a delicate, feminine quality. 这首诗有一种细腻、女性化的气质。|近义词：womanly, girlish, delicate, graceful|反义词：masculine, manly, strong, robust|词性:形容词
