resurfacers:/rɪˈsɜːrfəsər/| n. |路面修复机；翻新机 |A resurfacer is a machine used to repair or resurface roads, pavements, or other surfaces.  It typically involves removing the old surface and applying a new layer of material. |Example: The road crew used a large resurfacer to repair the potholes.  路面施工队用大型路面修复机修补了坑洼的路面。|近义词：pavement repair machine, road planer |反义词：road destroyer (非正式)
