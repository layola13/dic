reindicting:/ˌriːˈɪndaɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再起诉，重新起诉 | The act of bringing charges against someone again after a previous indictment has been dismissed or a trial has ended.  This often happens if new evidence is discovered or if there are procedural errors in the original indictment. | Example: The prosecutor is considering reindicting the defendant on more serious charges. = 检察官正在考虑对被告提起更严重的指控。 The case was dismissed, but the possibility of reindicting the suspect remains. = 此案已被驳回，但重新起诉嫌疑人的可能性依然存在。|近义词：recharging, reprosecuting | 反义词：dismissing, acquitting | 动词
