overflowing: /ˌoʊvərˈfloʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 溢出的；充满的；洋溢的 |  Describes something that is so full that its contents are spilling over the edges; also used figuratively to describe an abundance or overwhelming feeling. | 例句：The river was overflowing its banks after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。

The overflowing generosity of the donors surprised everyone.  捐赠者的慷慨大方令所有人惊讶。

| 近义词：abundant, brimming, copious, overflowing with, replete | 反义词：empty, lacking, deficient, scarce | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bathtub is overflowing. = 浴缸溢出来了。
The bowl is overflowing with fruit. = 碗里装满了水果。


一般过去时:
The river overflowed its banks last night. = 河水昨晚漫过了河岸。
My inbox overflowed with emails this morning. = 我今天早上收件箱里邮件爆满了。


现在完成时:
The river has overflowed several times this year. = 河水今年已经泛滥过几次了。
My heart has overflowed with joy since I heard the good news. = 自从听到这个好消息后，我的心充满了喜悦。


过去完成时:
The dam had overflowed before the engineers could repair it. = 在工程师们修好之前，水坝已经决堤了。
I had overflowed my budget by the end of the month. = 到月底我的预算超支了。


将来时:
The river will overflow if it rains heavily again. = 如果再次下大雨，河水就会泛滥。
I think my email inbox will overflow again tomorrow. = 我想我明天的邮箱又会爆满了。


虚拟语气:
If the cup were overflowing, it would spill. = 如果杯子满了，它就会溢出来。
If my heart overflowed with happiness, I would shout it from the rooftops. = 如果我的心充满了幸福，我会把它大声喊出来。


被动语态:
The streets were overflowed by the floodwater. = 街道被洪水淹没了。
The hospital was overflowed with patients after the accident. = 事故发生后，医院挤满了病人。


疑问句:
Is the sink overflowing? = 水槽溢了吗？
Is your schedule overflowing with appointments? = 你的日程安排是不是满满的？


否定句:
The glass wasn't overflowing; it was only half full. = 杯子并没有溢出来；它只有半满。
Her patience wasn't overflowing; she was starting to get frustrated. = 她的耐心已经快要用尽了；她开始感到沮丧。
