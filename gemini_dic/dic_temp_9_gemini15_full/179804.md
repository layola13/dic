reexcavated:/ˌriːˈɛkskəveɪtɪd/| v. |重新挖掘；再次挖掘 |To excavate again or anew.  This implies a previous excavation at the same site. |例句：The archaeologists reexcavated the site after new evidence emerged. 考古学家在出现新的证据后重新挖掘了该遗址。

例句：The team reexcavated the tomb, hoping to find more artifacts.  团队重新挖掘了坟墓，希望能找到更多文物。


近义词：re-explore, reinvestigate, reexamine


反义词：bury, cover, conceal


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexcavate the site every year. = 他们每年都重新挖掘该遗址。
Archaeologists reexcavate ancient cities to learn more about the past. = 考古学家重新挖掘古代城市以了解更多关于过去的信息。


一般过去时:
The team reexcavated the area last summer. = 团队去年夏天重新挖掘了该区域。
They reexcavated the ruins and found a hidden chamber. = 他们重新挖掘了废墟，发现了一个隐藏的密室。


现在完成时:
They have reexcavated the site three times. = 他们已经重新挖掘了该遗址三次。
Scientists have reexcavated the dinosaur fossil to analyze it further. = 科学家们已经重新挖掘了恐龙化石以进一步分析它。


过去完成时:
They had reexcavated the tomb before the museum opened. = 在博物馆开放之前，他们已经重新挖掘了坟墓。
Before the new discovery, they had reexcavated the site twice without success. = 在新的发现之前，他们已经两次重新挖掘了该遗址，但没有成功。


将来时:
They will reexcavate the ancient city next year. = 他们明年将重新挖掘这座古城。
The researchers will reexcavate the area to look for more evidence. = 研究人员将重新挖掘该区域以寻找更多证据。


虚拟语气:
If they had more funding, they would reexcavate the entire site. = 如果他们有更多资金，他们就会重新挖掘整个遗址。
I wish they would reexcavate the area and investigate further. = 我希望他们能重新挖掘该区域并进行进一步调查。


被动语态:
The site was reexcavated last month. = 该遗址上个月被重新挖掘了。
The ancient ruins are being reexcavated by a team of experts. = 古代遗址正在由一个专家团队重新挖掘。


疑问句:
Will they reexcavate the site next year? = 他们明年会重新挖掘该遗址吗？
Has the area been reexcavated since the last discovery? = 自上次发现以来，该区域是否被重新挖掘过？


否定句:
They did not reexcavate the site this year. = 他们今年没有重新挖掘该遗址。
The archaeologists have not yet reexcavated the entire area. = 考古学家尚未重新挖掘整个区域。
