cauterism:/ˈkɔːtəriˌzəm/ | n. | 烙术；烧灼法 | The application of a heated instrument or caustic substance to burn tissue to stop bleeding or destroy diseased tissue.  It is a medical procedure used to cauterize wounds. | Example:  The surgeon used cauterism to stop the bleeding. 外科医生用烙术止血。


Example: The doctor recommended cauterism to remove the wart. 医生建议用烧灼法去除疣。


近义词：cauterization, searing, burning | 反义词：healing, soothing
