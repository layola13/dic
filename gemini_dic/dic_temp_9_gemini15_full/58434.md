duckboards:/ˈdʌkbɔːrdz/| n.|鸭板路，木板路|A duckboard is a walkway made of wooden planks, often used in wet or muddy areas.  They are typically raised slightly above the ground to keep feet dry.|例句：The soldiers marched across the muddy field on duckboards.士兵们沿着鸭板路穿过泥泞的田野。
The garden path was made of duckboards to prevent mud from being tracked into the house. 花园小路是用鸭板铺成的，以防止泥土被带进屋里。|近义词：walkway, gangplank, platform|反义词:None specifically.
