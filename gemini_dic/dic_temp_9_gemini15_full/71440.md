flambeau:/ˈflæmbəʊ/ | n. |火把，火炬 | A flambeau is a large, typically handheld torch, often used in ceremonies or processions.  It is usually made of wood or metal and has a large wick that burns brightly. | The parade was illuminated by the flickering flambeaux carried by the participants.  这场游行被参与者们拿着的摇曳的火把照亮了。


|近义词：torch, lantern, |反义词：darkness |
