hero:/ˈhɪərəʊ/ /ˈhiːroʊ/| n. |英雄，男主角|A person who is admired for their courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities.|例句：He became a national hero after saving the child from the fire. 他在救孩子脱离火灾后成为民族英雄。

例句：The movie's hero is a charming rogue. 这部电影的男主角是一个迷人的流氓。


近义词：champion, idol, protagonist |反义词：villain, antagonist, coward |词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a hero to many people. = 他是许多人的英雄。
She is the hero of this story. = 她是这个故事的女主角。


一般过去时:
He was a hero in the war. = 他在战争中是一位英雄。
She was the hero of the day. = 她是那天的大功臣。


现在完成时:
He has become a hero in my eyes. = 他在我眼中已成为英雄。
She has always been a hero to her family. = 她一直是她家庭的英雄。


过去完成时:
He had already been a hero before the incident. = 在事件发生前，他已经是一位英雄了。
She had become a local hero by saving the drowning child. = 她通过救起溺水儿童而成为当地英雄。


将来时:
He will be a hero someday. = 他总有一天会成为英雄。
She will be remembered as a hero. = 她将被人们铭记为英雄。


虚拟语气:
If he were a hero, he would save the world. = 如果他是英雄，他会拯救世界。
If she had been a hero, she would have stopped the crime. = 如果她是英雄，她就会阻止犯罪。


被动语态:
He was hailed as a hero by the media. = 他被媒体誉为英雄。
She was considered a hero by her community. = 她被她的社区视为英雄。


疑问句:
Is he a hero or a villain? = 他是英雄还是恶棍？
Who is the hero in this novel? = 这部小说中的主人公是谁？


否定句:
He is not a hero, he's just lucky. = 他不是英雄，他只是运气好。
She is not the hero of this story; she is a supporting character. = 她不是这个故事的女主角，她是一个配角。
