prospered:/ˈprɒspəd/| v.|繁荣，兴旺，成功|To be successful and wealthy; to thrive.|The business prospered under his leadership.  他的领导下，生意兴隆。|The city prospered after the discovery of gold.  在发现黄金后，这座城市繁荣起来。|flourished, thrived, succeeded|failed, declined, suffered
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prospers in his new job. = 他在新工作中很成功。
The company prospers despite the economic downturn. = 尽管经济低迷，这家公司仍然兴旺发达。

一般过去时:
The village prospered for many years. = 这个村庄繁荣了很多年。
Our business prospered after we launched the new product. = 我们推出新产品后，生意兴隆。

现在完成时:
They have prospered since they moved to the city. = 自从他们搬到城市以来，他们就过得很富裕。
The country has prospered greatly in recent decades. = 近几十年来，这个国家取得了巨大的进步。

过去完成时:
The kingdom had prospered until the war began. = 在战争爆发之前，王国一直很繁荣。
The family had prospered before the economic crisis hit. = 在经济危机爆发之前，这个家庭一直很富裕。


将来时:
The company will prosper if they continue to innovate. = 如果他们继续创新，公司将会兴旺发达。
I believe that our community will prosper in the future. = 我相信我们的社区未来会繁荣昌盛。


虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he might have prospered. = 如果他更努力工作，他可能已经成功了。
I wish I had prospered in my career. = 我希望我的事业能够成功。


被动语态:
The town was prospered by the new industry. = 新兴产业使这个城镇繁荣起来。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The region was not prospered by the drought. = 干旱并没有使这个地区繁荣。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Did the business prosper last year? = 去年生意兴隆吗？
Will the project prosper in the long run? = 从长远来看，这个项目会成功吗？


否定句:
The farm didn't prosper after the flood. = 洪水过后，农场没有恢复元气。
His business did not prosper due to poor management. = 由于管理不善，他的生意没有兴隆。


PHRASE->
prosper greatly = 大大繁荣
prosper under = 在...的领导下繁荣
prosper in business = 商业兴隆
prosper from = 得益于...而繁荣
prosper through = 通过...而繁荣
