/ˈɛfəmərəl/ | adj. |短暂的；转瞬即逝的；朝生暮死的|Fleeting, short-lived, ephemeral, transient, momentary; lasting for a very short time.  Ephemeral things are temporary and do not last long. They exist only for a brief period. The term often refers to something beautiful but fleeting, like a beautiful sunset or a brief moment of happiness.|The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a few weeks.  樱花的美丽是短暂的，只持续几周。| lasting, enduring, permanent | permanent, lasting |形容词


例句：

The ephemeral nature of life is a recurring theme in many works of art.  生命的短暂性是许多艺术作品中反复出现的主题。

His fame proved to be quite ephemeral; he was forgotten within a year. 他的名声证明是相当短暂的；一年之内就被遗忘了。


近义词：transient, fleeting, momentary, short-lived, evanescent

反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring, eternal
