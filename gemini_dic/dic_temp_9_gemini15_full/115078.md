maneuvered:/məˈnuːvərd/| vt.|  调动；操纵；设法使…通过 | To move skillfully or carefully, especially in a confined space or difficult situation; to guide or manipulate cleverly.  |例句：The captain skillfully maneuvered the ship through the narrow channel. 船长熟练地驾驶着船通过狭窄的海峡。

例句：She cleverly maneuvered herself into a position of power. 她巧妙地让自己获得了权力。


近义词：manipulate, steer, navigate, guide, direct |反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maneuvers the car expertly. = 他熟练地驾驶汽车。
She maneuvers her way through the crowd. = 她设法穿过人群。

一般过去时:
The general maneuvered his troops into a strategic position. = 将军把他的部队调动到一个战略位置。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
The team had maneuvered themselves into a winning position before the final quarter. = 在最后一节比赛之前，球队已经把自己调动到了一个获胜的位置。
He had maneuvered the conversation away from the sensitive topic. = 他已将谈话从敏感话题上转移开。

将来时:
The pilot will maneuver the aircraft for a smooth landing. = 飞行员将操纵飞机平稳着陆。
We will maneuver the project to meet the deadline. = 我们将调整项目以赶上截止日期。

虚拟语气:
