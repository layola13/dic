centrifugally:/sɛnˈtrɪfjuɡəli/ | adv. |离心地，向外地 |Moving or directed away from the center.  The effect is produced by centrifugal force. | 例句：The water is flung centrifugally outwards. 水被离心力甩向外侧。

例句：The centrifuge spun the blood sample centrifugally, separating the components.离心机离心地旋转血液样本，分离其成分。


近义词：radially, outwardly

反义词：centripetally


词性：副词
