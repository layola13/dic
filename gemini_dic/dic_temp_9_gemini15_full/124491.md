mouldiness:/ˈmoʊldinəs/| n. |霉味；霉烂；霉菌 |The state or quality of being mouldy; the presence of mould.  It refers to the condition of something covered in or affected by mould, often accompanied by a musty smell. |例句：The old book smelled strongly of mouldiness. 这本旧书散发着浓重的霉味。|例句：The mouldiness on the bread made it inedible. 面包上的霉菌使它不能食用。|近义词：mustiness, dampness, mildew |反义词：freshness, cleanliness
