hyde:/haɪd/| n. v.|隐匿；兽皮；（人名）海德|The act of hiding something or someone; a person's skin, especially an animal's;  a surname.|例句：He went into hiding to evade the police. 他躲起来躲避警察。  例句：The coat is made of a fine hyde. 这件外套是用上好的兽皮做的。|近义词：conceal, secrete, skin |反义词：reveal, expose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hides his feelings well. = 他把自己的感情隐藏得很好。
They hide the truth from the public. = 他们向公众隐瞒真相。


一般过去时:
She hid the letter in a drawer. = 她把信藏在一个抽屉里。
The thief hid behind a tree. = 小偷藏在一棵树后面。


现在完成时:
He has hidden his money in a Swiss bank. = 他已将钱藏在瑞士银行。
They have hidden the evidence. = 他们已经隐藏了证据。


过去完成时:
She had hidden the key before she left. = 她离开前已经把钥匙藏起来了。
They had hidden the truth for years. = 他们隐瞒真相多年了。


将来时:
He will hide the present until Christmas. = 他会把礼物藏到圣诞节。
They will hide the evidence from the police. = 他们会把证据藏起来不让警察找到。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would hide my feelings. = 如果我是你，我会隐藏我的感情。
I wish I had hidden my money better. = 我真希望我把钱藏得更好一些。


被动语态:
The truth was hidden from us. = 真相被我们隐瞒了。
The evidence has been hidden by the police. = 证据已被警方隐藏起来。


疑问句:
Where did he hide the money? = 他把钱藏在哪里了？
Why did she hide the truth? = 她为什么隐瞒真相？


否定句:
He didn't hide his disappointment. = 他没有掩饰他的失望。
They didn't hide the fact that they were married. = 他们没有隐瞒他们结婚的事实。
