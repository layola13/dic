resublime:/ˌriːsəˈblaɪm/| v.|使再次升华；重新升华|To sublime again; to subject to sublimation a second time.  This word is less common and primarily used in a scientific or technical context, particularly in chemistry.|例句：The chemist attempted to resublime the purified compound to remove any remaining impurities. 化学家试图再次升华纯化的化合物以去除任何残留的杂质。|近义词：refine, purify|反义词：degrade, corrupt|动词
