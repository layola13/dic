feuding:/ˈfjuːdɪŋ/| v.|不和，争斗；世仇 |持续的争吵或争斗，通常在家庭或家族之间。|例句：The two families have been feuding for generations. 这两个家族世仇已久。|例句：The feuding between the neighbors escalated into a physical fight. 邻居间的争吵升级为肢体冲突。|近义词：quarreling, bickering, warring |反义词：harmonizing, reconciling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are feuding over a property dispute. = 他们在为财产纠纷而争斗。
The families are constantly feuding. =  这些家庭不断地争吵。


一般过去时:
The Montagues and Capulets were feuding. =  蒙太古家族和卡普莱特家族一直争斗不休。
They feuded bitterly for years. = 他们多年来一直激烈地争吵。


现在完成时:
They have been feuding since childhood. = 他们从孩提时代就开始争吵了。
The two countries have feuded over territory for centuries. = 这两个国家几个世纪以来一直在争夺领土。


过去完成时:
They had been feuding for years before they finally reconciled. = 在他们最终和解之前，他们已经争吵多年了。
The families had feuded over the inheritance for decades. =  几十年来，这些家族一直在为遗产争吵。


将来时:
I predict they will be feuding for many years to come. = 我预测他们未来还会争吵很多年。
They will likely feud over the will. = 他们很可能会为遗嘱而争吵。


虚拟语气:
If they weren't feuding, they could work together. = 如果他们不争吵，他们就能一起工作。
I wish they weren't feuding. = 我希望他们不要争吵。


被动语态:
The issue was often feuded over. =  这个问题经常成为争吵的焦点。
The land has been feuded over for generations. = 这块土地世世代代都有人争夺。


疑问句:
Are they still feuding? = 他们还在争吵吗？
Why are those two families feuding? = 为什么那两个家族在争吵？


否定句:
They are not feuding anymore. = 他们不再争吵了。
The brothers were not feuding, despite rumors to the contrary. = 尽管有相反的传闻，兄弟俩并没有争吵。
