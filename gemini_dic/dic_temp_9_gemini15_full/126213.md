my-bad:/maɪ bæd/| int.|对不起，是我的错|An informal expression used to apologize for a mistake or oversight.  It is a shortened version of "My bad," which itself is a contraction of "It's my bad." It's typically used in casual settings and implies a less formal apology than "I'm sorry".|例句：My bad, I didn't realize I was blocking the door.  对不起，我没意识到我在挡着门。


例句：My bad, I forgot to send you the email. 对不起，我忘了给你发邮件。


近义词：sorry, excuse me, my mistake, oops


反义词：N/A (It's an apology, not a statement with a direct opposite.)


词性:感叹词(interjection)
