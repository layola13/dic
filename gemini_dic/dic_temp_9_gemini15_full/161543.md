potlike:/ˈpɒtlaɪk/ | adj. | 像罐子的；像盆的 | Resembling a pot in shape or appearance.  The word combines "pot" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance. |例句：The small, potlike structure housed the delicate seedlings.  这个小巧的、像罐子一样的结构容纳着娇嫩的幼苗。

The vase had a potlike shape, wide at the base and tapering towards the top.  这个花瓶形状像个罐子，底部宽，向上逐渐变细。


近义词：pot-shaped, urn-shaped, vessel-like | 反义词：flat, planar,  (no direct antonym, as it describes a resemblance)


词源:  The word is a straightforward combination of the Old English word "potte" (pot) and the suffix "-like," which has Germanic roots and signifies "similar to" or "resembling."  The suffix's evolution can be traced back to Old English "-lic" and ultimately to Proto-Germanic *-līkaz.
