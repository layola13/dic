re-formats:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmæts/ | vt. |重新格式化 |To change the format of (something), especially a computer file or disk.  This involves rearranging the data to a new structure or layout.  |例句：I need to re-format my hard drive to install the new operating system. 我需要重新格式化我的硬盘才能安装新的操作系统。
例句：The software automatically re-formats the document to fit the printer's settings. 该软件会自动重新格式化文档以适应打印机的设置。|近义词： reformat, restructure |反义词： leave as is, maintain original format |动词
