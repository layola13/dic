reeligible: /ˌriːˈɛlɪdʒəbəl/ | adj. | 可再次当选的，符合再次选举条件的 | Eligible to be elected again.  This term is used to describe someone who meets the qualifications to run for office or a position a second time, after having previously held that position. |


例句：

The mayor is reeligible for another term.  市长可以再次竞选另一届。

After completing his first term successfully, the senator is reeligible.  参议员成功完成第一届任期后，可以再次竞选。


近义词：re-electable, eligible again


反义词：ineligible, disqualified


词性：形容词
