mountainous:/ˈmaʊntənəs/ | adj. | 多山的；巨大的；大量的 | Characterized by mountains; having many mountains.  The adjective also can be used figuratively to describe something vast or immense. | 例句：The region is mountainous and difficult to traverse. 这个地区多山，难以通行。

The Himalayas are a mountainous range. 喜马拉雅山脉是一片山区。


近义词：hilly, rugged, extensive, immense | 反义词：flat, plain, level


词源:  From Middle English *monteinous, from Old French *monteigneus, from *montagne "mountain" (from Vulgar Latin *montanea, from Latin *montanus, from mons, montis "mountain").  The suffix -ous is a common adjective-forming suffix in English, indicating "full of" or "characterized by".


用法说明:

mountainous通常用作形容词，修饰名词，描述地形或数量。

例句：

1.The mountainous terrain made the journey challenging.  多山的地形使旅程充满挑战。
2.The company faced a mountainous task of restructuring.  公司面临着艰巨的重组任务。(比喻用法)

3.He inherited a mountainous debt. 他继承了一笔巨额债务。(比喻用法)
4.They had a mountainous amount of work to do.  他们有大量的工作要做。(比喻用法)
