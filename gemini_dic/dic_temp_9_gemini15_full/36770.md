collusively:/kəˈluːzɪvli/ | adv. | 阴谋地；秘密勾结地 | Acting in a collusive manner; secretly cooperating for an illegal or dishonest purpose.  This adverb describes actions done in secret agreement, often for illicit gain. | Example: The two companies collusively fixed prices, driving smaller competitors out of business.  两个公司秘密串通涨价，把规模较小的竞争对手挤出了市场。|近义词：secretly, conspiratorially, clandestinely | 反义词：openly, transparently, honestly


USAGE->

The companies collusively inflate their prices. = 这些公司串通起来抬高价格。
