keyboards:/ˈkiːbɔːrdz/| n.|键盘；键盘乐器|A keyboard is an input device used to enter data into a computer or other electronic device.  It typically consists of a set of keys that can be pressed to produce characters, numbers, or commands.|例句：I need a new keyboard for my computer. 我需要一个新的电脑键盘。|例句：The pianist played a beautiful melody on the keyboard. 钢琴家在钢琴上演奏了一首优美的旋律。|近义词：keypad, console|反义词：mouse, trackpad
