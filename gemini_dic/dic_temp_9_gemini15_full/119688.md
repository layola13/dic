midis:/ˈmɪdiːz/| n.|MIDI文件，MIDI数据|MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface) files are digital audio files that contain musical information rather than raw audio data. They are smaller in size than WAV or MP3 files.  |Example: I downloaded some midis for my new synthesizer. 我下载了一些MIDI文件到我的新合成器上。|近义词：MIDI files, digital music files|反义词：WAV files, MP3 files, raw audio files
