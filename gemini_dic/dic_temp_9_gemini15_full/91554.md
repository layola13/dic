hyper-linked:/ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋkt/ | adj. | 超链接的 |  Containing or relating to a hyperlink; able to be clicked on to access another document or website.  A hyperlinked word or image allows users to jump to another location within a document or to a different website. | Example: The report contains many hyper-linked references to supporting documents.  这份报告包含许多超链接的参考文献。  |近义词：linked, connected | 反义词：unlinked, disconnected | 形容词
