preadvocate:/ˌpriːˈædvəkeɪt/ | v. | 预先倡导；提前拥护 | To advocate or support something before it becomes widely known or accepted.  |  例句：She preadvocated for the use of renewable energy sources long before they became mainstream. 她早在可再生能源成为主流之前就预先倡导使用它们。 | 近义词：precursor, forerunner, pioneer | 反义词：oppose, contradict
