lawmaker:/ˈlɔːˌmeɪkər/|/ˈlɔːˌmeɪkɚ/| n. |立法者；议员|A lawmaker is a person who makes laws, typically a member of a legislature such as a parliament or congress.  They are involved in the process of drafting, debating, and voting on legislation.|The lawmakers are currently debating a new tax bill.  立法者们目前正在辩论一项新的税收法案。|legislator, representative, congressman, senator|criminal, lawbreaker|名词
