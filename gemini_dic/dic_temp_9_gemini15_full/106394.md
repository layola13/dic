kookaburras:/ˌkuːkəˈbærəz/| n. |笑翠鸟（复数）|A kookaburra is a type of bird belonging to the kingfisher family, known for its distinctive, loud laughing call.  They are native to Australia and New Guinea.|Example:  The kookaburras' laughter echoed through the Australian bush. 笑翠鸟的笑声在澳大利亚的灌木丛中回荡。  We heard a chorus of kookaburras at dawn. 我们在黎明时分听到了一群笑翠鸟的叫声。|近义词：kingfisher (a broader term)|反义词：无|名词
