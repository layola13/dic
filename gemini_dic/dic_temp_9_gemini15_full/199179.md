silicones:/ˈsɪlɪkoʊnz/| n.|硅酮，硅橡胶|A silicone is a synthetic polymer made from silicon, oxygen, carbon, and other elements.  They are known for their heat resistance, water repellency, and flexibility.  Silicones are used in a wide variety of applications, from cosmetics to industrial manufacturing.|例句：This sealant is made from silicone, so it's very durable.  这个密封胶是由硅酮制成的，所以非常耐用。|例句：Many hair conditioners contain silicones to make hair smoother and shinier. 许多护发素含有硅酮，使头发更光滑更有光泽。|近义词：polymer, elastomer|反义词：无
