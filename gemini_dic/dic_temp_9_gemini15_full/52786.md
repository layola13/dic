dilettanteish:/ˌdɪləˈtɑːntɪʃ/| adj.|业余爱好者的；半瓶醋的；浅尝辄止的|Having the characteristics of a dilettante; superficial or amateurish in one's approach to a subject or activity.  It suggests a lack of serious commitment or expertise.|The dilettanteish attempt at painting was clearly lacking in skill and depth.|近义词：amateurish, superficial, superficial, unskillful|反义词：expert, professional, skilled
