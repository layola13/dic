patronymic: /pætˈrɒnɪmɪk/ /pætˈrɑːnɪmɪk/ | n. | 来源于父系的名字；父名；父称 | A patronymic is a name derived from the name of one's father or ancestor.  It's a type of surname that indicates lineage.  Many patronymics have evolved over time and may no longer clearly show their original meaning. | Example:  The surname "Johnson" is a patronymic, meaning "son of John". | 近义词：surname, family name | 反义词：matronymic (来源于母系的名字) | 名词
