denudes: /dɪˈnuːdz/ | vt. | 使裸露；剥去…的覆盖物；使荒芜 | To make bare or expose; to strip of covering or protection; to make desolate. | The relentless wind denuded the trees of their leaves.  狂风无情地吹落了树叶。| The deforestation denuded the hillsides.  滥砍滥伐使山坡变得荒凉。| 近义词：strip, expose, uncover, bare | 反义词：clothe, cover, conceal, protect | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind denudes the land of its topsoil. = 风剥蚀了土地表层的土壤。
He denudes the forest of its trees for profit. = 他为了利益而砍伐森林中的树木。


一般过去时:
The flood denuded the riverbanks of vegetation. = 洪水冲走了河岸上的植被。
Years of drought denuded the fields. = 多年的干旱使田地变得荒芜。


现在完成时:
The harsh winter has denuded the landscape. = 严酷的冬天使这片土地变得荒凉。
Overgrazing has denuded the pasture of grass. = 过度放牧使牧场上的草都消失了。


过去完成时:
The miners had denuded the mountainside of its ore before the inspectors arrived. = 矿工们在检查员到来之前已经挖光了山坡上的矿石。
The prolonged war had denuded the country of its resources. = 长期的战争耗尽了国家的资源。


将来时:
The construction project will denude the area of its natural beauty. = 建设项目将破坏该地区的自然美景。
Continued pollution will denude the river of its fish. = 持续的污染将使河里的鱼类消失殆尽。


虚拟语气:
If they continued this practice, they would denude the forest completely. = 如果他们继续这种做法，他们将彻底毁掉这片森林。
I wish the developers hadn't denuded the coastline of its dunes. = 我真希望开发商没有破坏海岸线上的沙丘。


被动语态:
The mountain was denuded by years of mining. = 这座山被多年的采矿活动破坏了。
The land was denuded of its forests by careless logging. = 这片土地由于不谨慎的伐木而失去了森林。


疑问句:
What factors denuded the topsoil from the farmland? = 哪些因素导致农田表土流失？
Has the company denuded the area of its wildlife? = 这家公司是否破坏了该地区的野生动物？


否定句:
The government didn't allow the project that would denude the valley of its trees. = 政府不允许实施会破坏山谷树木的项目。
They didn't denude the forest completely, leaving some trees untouched. = 他们并没有完全砍伐森林，留下了一些树木。


PHRASE->
denude of:  剥夺…；使…缺乏
denude the land:  使土地荒芜
denude the forest:  使森林荒芜
denude the coast: 使海岸线裸露
denude the soil: 使土壤贫瘠
