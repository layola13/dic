microalgae: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈælɡiː/ | n. | 微藻 | Microscopic algae.  Single-celled or colonial algae, invisible to the naked eye.  They are a diverse group of photosynthetic organisms found in various aquatic environments. |  Researchers are studying microalgae as a potential source of biofuel. 研究人员正在研究微藻作为生物燃料的潜在来源。

The rapid growth of microalgae makes them attractive for biofuel production. 微藻的快速生长使其成为生物燃料生产的有吸引力的选择。|近义词：phytoplankton (if referring to the planktonic variety) | 反义词：macroalgae (large algae visible to the naked eye) | 名词
