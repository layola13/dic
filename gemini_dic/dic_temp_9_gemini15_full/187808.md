rose:/roʊz/|ˈrəʊz/| n. v.|玫瑰；上升；脸红|A rose is a prickly bush or climbing plant that has fragrant flowers with many petals.  The word can also refer to the flower itself. As a verb, it means to rise or increase gradually.|例句：The roses in her garden are blooming beautifully. 她花园里的玫瑰开得非常漂亮。
The sun rose in the east. 太阳从东方升起。|近义词：flower, blossom, climb, ascend, blush|反义词：fall, descend, pale


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Roses bloom in spring. = 玫瑰在春天盛开。
The price of gas rises steadily. = 油价稳步上涨。

一般过去时:
She rose from her chair. = 她从椅子上站起来。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

被动语态:

疑问句:
