nps:/ɛnpiːˈɛs/| n.|净推荐值, 网络推广满意度|Net Promoter Score, a metric used to measure customer experience and loyalty.  It is calculated by subtracting the percentage of detractors (customers who would not recommend a company) from the percentage of promoters (customers who would recommend a company).|Example: Our company's NPS is 70, indicating high customer satisfaction.|近义词：customer satisfaction, loyalty score|反义词：customer dissatisfaction
