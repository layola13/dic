reliquefied: /ˌriːˈlɪkwɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 再液化的；重新液化的 |  Having been liquefied again after having been solidified.  This term describes a substance that has been solidified and then returned to a liquid state. |例句：The solidified gas was reliquefied using a special cooling process.  固化的气体通过特殊的冷却过程重新液化了。


The reliquefied nitrogen was used in the cryogenic experiment. 再液化的氮气被用于低温实验。|近义词：reliquefied, remelted (if applicable to solids)|反义词：solidified, frozen
