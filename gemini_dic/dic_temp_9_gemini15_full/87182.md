herding:/ˈhɜːrdɪŋ/| v.|放牧，牧羊，群体管理 |The act of gathering and controlling a group of animals, especially sheep or cattle, for movement or protection.  |例句：The shepherd was skilled in herding sheep across the mountains. 牧羊人擅长将羊群赶过山。|例句：The manager implemented new strategies for herding the employees towards the company goals. 经理实施了新的策略来引导员工朝公司目标努力。|近义词：shepherding, mustering, driving|反义词：scattering, dispersing|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He herds cattle every day. = 他每天放牧牛群。
She herds her children to school. = 她把孩子们赶去上学。


一般过去时:
The farmer herded his sheep into the barn. = 农民把羊赶进了谷仓。
They herded the crowd into the stadium. = 他们把人群赶进了体育场。


现在完成时:
He has herded sheep for over twenty years. = 他放牧羊已经有二十多年了。
She has successfully herded the team through several crises. = 她成功地带领团队度过了几次危机。


过去完成时:
The shepherd had herded the flock before the storm hit. = 牧羊人在暴风雨来临之前就把羊群赶走了。
They had herded the refugees to safety before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临之前将难民转移到了安全地带。


将来时:
The rancher will herd the cattle to a new pasture tomorrow. = 牧场主明天将把牛群赶到新的牧场。
We will herd the students into the auditorium for the assembly. = 我们将把学生们赶到礼堂参加集会。


虚拟语气:
If I were a shepherd, I would herd my sheep peacefully. = 如果我是一个牧羊人，我会平静地放牧我的羊。
If she had herded the team better, they would have achieved more. = 如果她能更好地领导团队，他们就能取得更大的成就。


被动语态:
The sheep were herded into the pen. = 羊群被赶进了羊圈。
The protesters were herded away by police. = 抗议者被警察驱散了。


疑问句:
Do you herd animals for a living? = 你靠放牧动物为生吗？
How do you herd such a large group of people? = 你是如何管理这么大一群人的？


否定句:
He doesn't herd sheep anymore. = 他不再放牧羊了。
The manager failed to herd the employees towards a common goal. = 经理未能引导员工朝着共同的目标前进。
