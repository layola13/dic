buzzer:/ˈbʌzər/ | n. |蜂鸣器，门铃 |A buzzer is a device that makes a buzzing sound, often used as a signaling device.  It can be an electric buzzer, a hand-held buzzer, or a part of a larger system like a doorbell or alarm. |例句：The buzzer rang, signaling the end of the round.  蜂鸣器响了，标志着回合结束。
The referee used a hand-held buzzer to stop the fight. 裁判用一个手持式蜂鸣器来停止比赛。|近义词：beeper, horn, alarm |反义词：silence
