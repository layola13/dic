megaphoning:/ˈmɛɡəfoʊnɪŋ/| v.|扩音，用扩音器喊话|The act of using a megaphone to amplify one's voice.|例句：The coach was megaphoning instructions to the players from the sidelines. 教练在边线用扩音器向队员喊叫指示。|近义词：amplifying, broadcasting, announcing|反义词：whispering, muttering

They megaphone their message across the square. = 他们在广场上用扩音器传播他们的信息。
