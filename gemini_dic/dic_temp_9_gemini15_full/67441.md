extirp:/ˈɛkstɜːrp/| vt.|根除；消灭；拔除|To pull up by the roots; to root out; to destroy totally.|例句：The government is trying to extirpate corruption. 政府正试图根除腐败。

例句：We need to extirpate this invasive weed from our garden. 我们需要将这种入侵杂草从我们的花园中清除干净。

近义词：eradicate, eliminate, abolish, exterminate|反义词：cultivate, foster, nurture|词性:动词

词源:
