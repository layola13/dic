letter-perfect:/ˈlɛtər ˌpɜːrfɪkt/ | adj. |完美的，一丝不苟的 | Exactly as written or required; faultless.  The term implies accuracy down to the smallest detail, often in written or memorized material. | 例句：His recitation of the poem was letter-perfect.  他的诗歌朗诵完美无缺。


例句：The student's essay was letter-perfect; not a single error could be found.  学生的文章完美无瑕；找不到任何错误。


近义词：flawless, impeccable, perfect, faultless, accurate


反义词：flawed, imperfect, erroneous, inaccurate


词性:形容词
