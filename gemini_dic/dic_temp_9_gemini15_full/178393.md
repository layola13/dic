reconnecter:/ˌriːkəˈnɛktər/| n. |重新连接器，恢复连接者 |A device or system that re-establishes a connection that has been interrupted.  It can refer to physical connections (like wires) or digital connections (like network links).|例句：The network administrator used a special reconnecter to restore internet access after the server crash. 网络管理员使用了一个特殊的重新连接器来在服务器崩溃后恢复互联网访问。
The reconnecter automatically re-established the connection to the database. 重新连接器自动重新建立与数据库的连接。|近义词：restorer, reconnector device |反义词：disconnector, interruptor |名词
