felafel:/ˈfɛləfəl/| n. | falafel (也作felafel)| 炸豆泥丸子，鹰嘴豆泥球 | Fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, typically served in pita bread.  Felafel is a popular Middle Eastern and Mediterranean street food, known for its savory and slightly spicy flavor. | 例句：I ordered a felafel pita for lunch. = 我午餐点了一份炸豆泥皮塔饼。

例句：The felafel was crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. = 炸豆泥丸子外酥里嫩。


近义词：chickpea patty, bean patty | 反义词：无
