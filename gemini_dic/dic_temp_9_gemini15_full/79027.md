give-a-damn:/ɡɪv ə dæm/| phrasal verb|不在乎，不关心|This phrase expresses a lack of care or concern.  It is informal and often used in a negative context. The "damn" is an expletive, adding emphasis to the lack of concern.|例句：I don't give a damn what you think. 我不在乎你认为什么。

例句：He just gave a damn about his own work. 他只在乎自己的工作。


近义词：couldn't care less, don't care, not give a hoot, not give a shit


反义词：care, concern, worry


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I don't give a damn about his opinion. = 我不在乎他的意见。
They don't give a damn about the rules. = 他们不在乎规则。


一般过去时:
I didn't give a damn what people said. = 我不在乎人们说什么。
She didn't give a damn about the consequences. = 她不在乎后果。


现在完成时:
I haven't given a damn about his threats. = 我一直不在乎他的威胁。
They haven't given a damn about the environment. = 他们一直不在乎环境。


过去完成时:
I hadn't given a damn about the deadline until it was too late. = 直到为时已晚我才不在乎截止日期。
They hadn't given a damn about the warning signs. = 他们之前一直不在乎警告信号。


将来时:
I won't give a damn what they say. = 我不在乎他们说什么。
He won't give a damn about the criticism. = 他不会在乎批评。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't give a damn, I wouldn't be here. = 如果我不在乎，我就不会在这里。
I wish I didn't give a damn about his feelings. = 我真希望我不在乎他的感受。


被动语态: (This phrase is rarely used passively)


疑问句:
Do you give a damn about the outcome? = 你在乎结果吗？(This implies the opposite, usually meaning "Do you care?")
Does he give a damn about the project? = 他在乎这个项目吗？ (This implies the opposite, usually meaning "Does he care?")


否定句:
I don't give a damn about your problems. = 我不在乎你的问题。
He doesn't give a damn about the rules. = 他不在乎规则。


PHRASE->
give a damn about = 在乎……
not give a damn about = 不在乎……
give a damn what = 不在乎……
couldn't give a damn = 完全不在乎
I don't give a damn = 我一点也不在乎
