magmatic:/mæɡˈmætɪk/| adj.|岩浆的；火成岩的|Relating to or produced by magma.  Magmatic rocks are formed from the cooling and solidification of molten rock (magma).|例句：The volcanic eruption released a large amount of magmatic gases into the atmosphere.  火山爆发将大量的岩浆气体释放到大气中。

例句：The island is primarily composed of magmatic rocks formed millions of years ago.  这个岛屿主要由数百万年前形成的火成岩构成。


近义词：igneous (in the context of rocks)
反义词：sedimentary, metamorphic (in the context of rocks)

词性：形容词
