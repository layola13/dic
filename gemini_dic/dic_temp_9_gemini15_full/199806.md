sister:/ˈsɪstər/| n.|姐妹，修女|A female sibling; a woman in the same religious order.|例句：My sister is a doctor. 我的姐姐是医生。

例句：She entered a convent and became a sister. 她进入修道院成为了一名修女。


近义词：sibling,  (in a religious order) nun


反义词：brother


词性：名词

词源:
The word "sister" comes from Old English * *swēostor*, which is of Proto-Germanic origin.  Its cognates can be found in other Germanic languages, demonstrating a shared ancestry.  The meaning has remained consistent throughout its history, referring to a female sibling.
