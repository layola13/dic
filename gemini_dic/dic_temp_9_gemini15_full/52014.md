diagrammed:/ˈdaɪəɡræmd/ | v. | 绘制图表；用图表表示 |  To represent (something) in the form of a diagram.  It involves creating a visual representation using lines, symbols, and labels to illustrate relationships or processes. | The engineer diagrammed the complex system for better understanding. 工程师绘制了复杂系统的图表以便更好地理解。  The teacher diagrammed the sentence structure on the board. 老师在黑板上画出了句子的结构图。| charted, graphed, sketched | undescribed, unillustrated | 动词
