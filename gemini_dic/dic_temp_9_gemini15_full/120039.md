millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/| n. |千足虫，马陆|A myriapod with a long segmented body and many legs.  Millipedes are usually herbivores and harmless to humans, although some species can secrete irritating fluids.|例句：I saw a millipede crawling across the path. 我看到一条千足虫在小路上爬行。

例句：The children were fascinated by the millipede they found under a log. 孩子们对他们在原木下发现的千足虫很着迷。


近义词：centipede (although technically different)|反义词：无


词源:  The word "millipede" comes from the Latin words "mille" (thousand) and "pes" (foot).  Although they don't have a thousand legs, the name reflects the large number of legs they possess.
