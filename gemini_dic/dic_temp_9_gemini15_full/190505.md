satchelfuls:/ˈsætʃəlˌfʊlz/| n. |满满一旅行包；许多|The word "satchelfuls" is a plural noun formed by adding the suffix "-fuls" to the noun "satchel." It signifies multiple quantities filled to the capacity of a satchel.  It implies a large number of things, not necessarily a precise count.  |例句：She carried several satchelfuls of books. 她提着好几包书。

She brought satchelfuls of goodies to the picnic. 她带了满满几包好吃的东西去野餐。


近义词：bagsful, loads, heaps, quantities


反义词：a few, a little, scarcely any


词性：名词
