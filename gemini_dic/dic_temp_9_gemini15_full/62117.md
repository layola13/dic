词源:
The word "enchilada" comes from the Spanish word "enchilada," which itself derives from the Nahuatl (Aztec) word "chilli."  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "covered in," thus, "enchilada" literally means "something covered in chili."  The dish's origins are firmly rooted in Mexican cuisine.
