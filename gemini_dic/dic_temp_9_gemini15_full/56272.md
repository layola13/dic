dogfish:/ˈdɒɡfɪʃ/ /ˈdɑːɡfɪʃ/| n.|狗鱼，一种小型鲨鱼|A dogfish is a small shark, typically found in coastal waters.  They are known for their slender bodies and pointed snouts. Many species are commercially fished.|例句：The fisherman caught several dogfish in his net. = 渔夫在网里捕到几条狗鱼。
The spiny dogfish is a common species found in the Atlantic. = 棘狗鱼是大西洋中常见的一种。|近义词：shark, small shark |反义词：whale, large fish
