minute: /ˈmɪnɪt/ | n. |分钟 | A minute is a unit of time equal to 60 seconds.  It's a small fraction of an hour.


例句：

There are sixty minutes in an hour. = 一小时有六十分钟。

The meeting lasted for fifteen minutes. = 会议持续了十五分钟。


近义词：moment (in the sense of a short period)


反义词：hour, day (longer periods of time)


词性: Noun
