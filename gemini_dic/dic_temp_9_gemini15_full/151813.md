passed away: /pɑːst əˈweɪ/ | adj. phrasal verb |去世，过世 |This phrase is used to describe the death of a person in a gentle and euphemistic way. It avoids the harshness of words like "died" or "dead".  |例句：His grandfather passed away peacefully in his sleep.  他的祖父在睡梦中安详地去世了。

例句：She learned that her aunt had passed away last week. 她上周得知她姑姑去世了。


近义词：died, deceased, expired, departed

反义词：born, alive


词性: phrasal verb (动词短语)
