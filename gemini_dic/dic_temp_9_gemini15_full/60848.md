electrotype:/ɪˈlɛktrətaɪp/| n.|电铸版，电版|A process of reproducing printing plates by electroplating a mold of the original type or image with metal, typically copper or nickel.  The resulting metal plate is then used for printing.|The printer used an electrotype to create multiple copies of the rare book.|近义词：stereoplate|反义词：original type
