sermoneer:/ˌsɜːrməˈnɪər/ | v. |说教；长篇大论地训斥 | To deliver a sermon or preach; to lecture or moralize tediously. |例句：He likes to sermoneer about the evils of modern life. 他喜欢长篇大论地训斥现代生活的弊端。|例句：The teacher sermoneered the students about the importance of studying.老师就学习的重要性对学生们说教。|近义词：preach, lecture, moralize |反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sermoneers to anyone who will listen. = 他对任何愿意听的人说教。
She sermoneers about the importance of healthy eating. = 她对健康饮食的重要性说教。


一般过去时:
The priest sermoneered on the dangers of sin. = 牧师就罪恶的危险性说教。
My father sermoneered me for coming home late. = 我父亲因为我回家晚了而训斥我。


现在完成时:
He has sermoneered at length about the decline of morals. = 他已经冗长地教训了道德的沦丧。
She has sermoneered to her children countless times about responsibility. = 她已经无数次地教训她的孩子要负责任。


过去完成时:
He had sermoneered to them before, so they were not surprised. = 他以前就对他们说过教，所以他们并不感到惊讶。
She had sermoneered at him many times about his poor study habits. = 她已经多次就他的糟糕学习习惯训斥他了。


将来时:
He will sermoneer again next Sunday. = 他下星期天还会再说教。
She will sermoneer to the congregation about charity. = 她将向会众讲解慈善。


虚拟语气:
If he sermoneered less, people would listen more. = 如果他不那么爱说教，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't sermoneer so much. = 我希望他不那么爱说教。


被动语态:
The students were sermoneered about their behavior. = 学生们因为他们的行为受到了训斥。
The congregation was sermoneered on the importance of faith. = 会众受到了关于信仰重要性的教诲。


疑问句:
Does he often sermoneer? = 他经常说教吗？
Will she sermoneer at the meeting? = 她会在会议上说教吗？


否定句:
He didn't sermoneer at the meeting. = 他在会议上没有说教。
She doesn't usually sermoneer to her friends. = 她通常不会对她朋友说教。


PHRASE->
sermoneer at someone = 对某人说教
sermoneer about something = 就某事说教
sermoneer endlessly = 没完没了地说教
sermoneer with conviction = 坚定地训斥
sermoneer from the pulpit = 从讲坛上布道
