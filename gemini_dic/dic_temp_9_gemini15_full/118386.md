mermaid:/ˈmɜːrmeɪd/| n. |美人鱼|A mythical sea creature with the head and upper body of a woman and the tail of a fish.  They are often depicted as beautiful and enchanting, sometimes associated with danger or magic. |Example: The little girl dreamed of becoming a mermaid and swimming in the ocean.  小女孩梦想成为美人鱼并在海洋中游泳。|近义词：siren (often implying danger), sea nymph |反义词：merman
