retrenching:/rɪˈtrentʃɪŋ/| v.|紧缩；裁减；减少；退却 |To reduce expenses or spending; to cut back; to withdraw or retreat.  |例句：The company is retrenching due to declining profits. 公司由于利润下降而正在裁员。|例句：The army is retrenching from its forward positions. 军队正在从前沿阵地撤退。|近义词：cut back, reduce, economize, curtail, withdraw|反义词：expand, increase, augment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is retrenching its workforce. = 公司正在裁员。
They are retrenching their spending on marketing. = 他们在减少市场营销方面的支出。


一般过去时:
The government retrenched spending on social programs. = 政府削减了社会福利项目的支出。
The army retrenched after suffering heavy losses. = 军队在遭受重大损失后撤退了。


现在完成时:
The company has retrenched significantly in the last year. = 公司在过去一年中大幅度缩减了规模。
They have retrenched their operations to focus on core businesses. = 他们已经缩减业务范围，专注于核心业务。


过去完成时:
The company had retrenched before the economic crisis hit. = 在经济危机爆发前，公司就已经开始裁员了。
They had retrenched their marketing budget before launching the new product. = 在推出新产品之前，他们已经削减了市场营销预算。


将来时:
The company will retrench if profits continue to fall. = 如果利润继续下降，公司将裁员。
They will retrench their overseas operations. = 他们将缩减海外业务。


虚拟语气:
If the company had retrenched earlier, it might have avoided bankruptcy. = 如果公司早点裁员，或许就能避免破产。
If they had retrenched their spending, they would have more money now. = 如果他们当时削减了开支，现在就会有更多的钱。


被动语态:
Spending on education was retrenched by the government. = 教育经费被政府削减了。
The company's operations were retrenched due to the pandemic. = 由于疫情，公司的业务受到了缩减。


疑问句:
Is the company retrenching its staff? = 公司正在裁员吗？
Will they retrench their investment in that project? = 他们会减少对那个项目的投资吗？


否定句:
The company is not retrenching at this time. = 公司目前没有裁员。
They did not retrench their marketing efforts despite the low sales. = 尽管销售额低，他们也没有减少市场营销工作。

forced retrenchment=被迫裁员
