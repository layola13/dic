outbuildings:/ˈaʊtˌbɪldɪŋz/| n. |附属建筑，外屋|Buildings located on a property separate from the main house, such as a garage, shed, or barn.  These structures often serve as storage, workshops, or other supplementary spaces. |Example: The old farmhouse had several outbuildings, including a stable and a chicken coop.  老农舍有几座附属建筑，包括一个马厩和一个鸡舍。


The old barn, one of the outbuildings, was in desperate need of repair.  旧谷仓，其中一座附属建筑，急需修理。|近义词：sheds, dependencies, annexes |反义词：main house, principal building
