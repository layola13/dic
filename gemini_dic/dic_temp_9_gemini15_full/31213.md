channel-hop:/ˈtʃænəl hɒp/| v.|频道跳跃，转台|Rapidly switching between different television or radio channels.|例句：He channel-hopped through dozens of channels looking for something interesting. 他快速切换数十个频道寻找有趣的内容。

例句：I was channel-hopping last night and stumbled upon a great documentary. 我昨晚一直在换台，偶然看到一部很棒的纪录片。


近义词：flick through, switch channels, surf channels|反义词：stick with, remain on


词源:  The word is a compound of "channel" and "hop." "Channel" refers to a television or radio station or frequency, and "hop" implies a quick, repetitive movement.  The meaning is thus a direct combination of these two words.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I channel-hop when I'm bored. = 我无聊的时候会换台。
He channel-hops constantly during commercials. = 他在广告期间不停地换台。


一般过去时:
She channel-hopped until she found a movie she liked. = 她一直换台，直到找到一部她喜欢的电影。
They channel-hopped all evening, never settling on one program. = 他们整个晚上都在换台，从未在一个节目上停留。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
Before the game started, he had channel-hopped through every channel on cable. = 在比赛开始之前，他已经把有线电视上的每一个频道都换了一遍。
She had channel-hopped so much that she felt dizzy. = 她换台换得太多了，感觉头晕。
