misadvising:/ˌmɪsædˈvaɪzɪŋ/| v.|误导，提供错误建议 | Giving wrong or harmful advice.  |例句：He was misadvising the young people on financial matters. 他在财务问题上误导了年轻人。|例句：The company was accused of misadvising its clients about the risks involved.该公司被指控在涉及的风险问题上误导了客户。|近义词：misguiding, misleading, ill-advising |反义词：advising well, guiding correctly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is misadvising his clients on investment strategies. = 他正在就投资策略误导他的客户。
They are constantly misadvising their children about the dangers of drugs. = 他们不断地误导他们的孩子关于毒品的危险。


一般过去时:
She misadvised him about the legal implications of his actions. = 她误导了他关于他行为的法律含义。
The lawyer misadvised his client, leading to a costly mistake. = 律师误导了他的委托人，导致了一个代价高昂的错误。


现在完成时:
The advisor has misadvised many investors over the years. = 多年来，这位顾问误导了许多投资者。
They have misadvised us on several crucial decisions. = 他们在几个关键决策上误导了我们。


过去完成时:

I discovered that I had misadvised my team on the project's requirements. = 我发现我误导了我的团队关于项目的需要。


将来时:
They will misadvise the public if they continue to spread misinformation. = 如果他们继续散布虚假信息，他们将误导公众。
She fears that he will misadvise the committee on the budget. = 她担心他会误导委员会关于预算。


虚拟语气:
If I had known better, I would not have misadvised him. = 如果我早知道，我就不会误导他了。
I wish I hadn't misadvised my friend on that important matter. = 我真希望我没有在我的朋友那个重要的事情上误导他。


被动语态:
The client was misadvised by his lawyer, resulting in a lost case. = 委托人被他的律师误导了，导致案件败诉。
The investors were misadvised about the potential risks of the investment. = 投资者被误导了关于投资的潜在风险。


疑问句:

Are they misadvising the public regarding the safety of the product? = 他们是否正在就产品的安全性误导公众？


否定句:
He did not misadvise her; he gave her the best advice he could. = 他没有误导她；他给了她他能给的最好的建议。
The expert did not misadvise the group on the technical aspects of the problem. = 专家没有在问题的技术方面误导小组。
