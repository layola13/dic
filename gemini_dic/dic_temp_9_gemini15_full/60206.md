efflorescence:/ˌɛfləˈrɛsəns/ | n. |开花；繁荣；涌现；风化 |The process of flowering; a blossoming or flourishing; a sudden increase or appearance of something.  It can also refer to the powdery deposit formed on the surface of a substance due to the loss of water of crystallization. |例句：The efflorescence of the Renaissance saw a flourishing of art and literature. 文艺复兴的繁荣时期见证了艺术和文学的兴盛。

The efflorescence on the brickwork indicated the presence of soluble salts. 砖墙上的风化表明存在可溶性盐。


近义词：flowering, blossoming, flourishing, boom, surge |反义词：decline, decay, withering, stagnation
