reimbursability:/ˌriːɪmbɜːrsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可报销性，报销能力 | The ability of an expense to be reimbursed.  The state of being eligible for reimbursement. | Example: The reimbursability of travel expenses is dependent on company policy.  旅行费用的报销能力取决于公司政策。  The  project proposal needs to address the reimbursability of all costs. 项目提案需要说明所有费用的报销能力。| 近义词：refundability, repayability | 反义词：non-reimbursability
