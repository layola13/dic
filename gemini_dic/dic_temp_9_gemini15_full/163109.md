precollector: /ˌpriːkəˈlɛktər/ | n. | 预收集器，预收集程序 | A precollector is a component in some garbage collection systems that performs an initial scan of memory to identify potentially unreachable objects before the main garbage collection process begins. This helps to improve the efficiency of the garbage collection by reducing the amount of work the main collector needs to do.  | Example: The precollector significantly reduced the pause times during garbage collection.  预收集器显著减少了垃圾回收过程中的暂停时间。|近义词：initial scanner, preliminary collector |反义词：main collector, final collector
