sieur: /sər/ | n. |先生|A title of respect used to address or refer to a man, especially in French.  It is a shortened form of "monsieur."| Example:  "Bonjour, monsieur."  = "你好，先生。" |近义词：mister, sir, gentleman |反义词：madam, miss, lady


USAGE->
This word is primarily a title and doesn't have verb conjugations like "to be," "to do," or "to have." Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.  The word itself is not used in a sentence structure that would require tense.


PHRASE->
Monsieur le président =  先生总统
Messieurs les juges = 先生们法官
Cher monsieur = 亲爱的先生
Monsieur et madame = 先生和夫人
Mon cher monsieur = 我亲爱的先生
