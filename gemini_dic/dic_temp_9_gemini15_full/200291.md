skonce:/skɒns/| n.| 壁灯；壁饰 | A sconce is a light fixture, typically for a wall, often with a decorative bracket.  It can also refer to a decorative wall bracket that doesn't necessarily hold a light. | Example:  The antique sconces cast a warm glow on the dining room walls.  古老的壁灯在餐厅的墙壁上投射出温暖的光芒。  The hallway was adorned with ornate sconces.  走廊装饰着华丽的壁饰。|近义词：wall bracket, wall lamp|反义词：ceiling light, floor lamp|
