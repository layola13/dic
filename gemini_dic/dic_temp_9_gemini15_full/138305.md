noteboard:/ˈnoʊtˌbɔːrd/ | n. |记事板，留言板 |A noteboard is a board or surface used for displaying notes, messages, or announcements. It can be made of various materials, such as cork, whiteboard, or even fabric.  Noteboards are commonly used in offices, classrooms, and homes to organize information and reminders. | Example:  The teacher used the noteboard to write the day's schedule. 老师用记事板写下当天的日程安排。


The team left a note on the noteboard to remind everyone of the meeting. 团队在留言板上留下一张便条，提醒大家开会。|近义词：bulletin board, message board, notice board |反义词：None  |名词
