colourways:/ˈkʌləweɪz/ | n. |配色方案，色彩组合 |A colourway refers to a specific combination of colors used in a design, product, or artwork. It often describes the various color options available for a particular item.  For example, a shoe might be available in several colourways, each featuring a different palette. |The new sneaker is available in three exciting colourways.  这双新运动鞋有三种令人兴奋的配色方案。  The artist experimented with different colourways for the mural.  这位艺术家尝试了壁画的不同配色方案。 | color schemes, color palettes |monochrome (反义词并非完全对立，而是指单色，与多种颜色组合相对)
