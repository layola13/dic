matriculate:/məˈtrɪkjuleɪt/| v. |入学，注册(大学)|To officially enroll as a student at a college or university.  |He matriculated at Oxford University in 1998. 他于1998年进入牛津大学学习。|enroll, register, enter|expel, drop out|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She matriculates at Harvard this fall. = 她今年秋天在哈佛大学入学。
Students matriculate at the beginning of each semester. = 学生们在每个学期的开始入学。


一般过去时:
He matriculated at Cambridge last year. = 他去年在剑桥大学入学。
My brother matriculated from a prestigious university. = 我哥哥毕业于一所著名的大学。


现在完成时:
She has matriculated at the University of California, Berkeley. = 她已在加州大学伯克利分校入学。
Many students have matriculated since the new program started. = 自从新项目启动以来，许多学生已经入学。


过去完成时:
They had matriculated before the scholarship deadline. = 他们在奖学金截止日期之前已经入学。
He had matriculated at the medical school by the time his brother graduated. = 当他弟弟毕业时，他已经在医学院入学了。


将来时:
I will matriculate at Stanford next year. = 我明年将在斯坦福大学入学。
She will matriculate in the fall semester. = 她将在秋季学期入学。


虚拟语气:
If I had the grades, I would matriculate at Yale. = 如果我有足够的成绩，我就会进入耶鲁大学学习。
I wish I could matriculate at a top-tier university. = 我希望我能进入一所顶尖大学学习。


被动语态:
Many students are matriculated every year. = 每年都有许多学生入学。
He was matriculated into the engineering program. = 他被录取进入工程专业。


疑问句:
When did you matriculate? = 你是什么时候入学的？
Will she matriculate this year? = 她今年会入学吗？


否定句:
He did not matriculate at that university. = 他没有在那所大学入学。
She will not matriculate until next year. = 她要到明年才会入学。
