limburg:/ˈlɪmbɜːrɡ/ | n. |林堡（比利时城市）；林堡奶酪 | Limburg is a province in Belgium and the Netherlands, and also refers to a type of cheese.  The city of Limburg in Belgium is known for its rich history and picturesque setting.  Limburg cheese is a soft, creamy cheese with a distinctive pungent aroma. |例句：We visited the charming city of Limburg in Belgium. 我们参观了比利时风景如画的林堡市。  The Limburg cheese was quite strong, but delicious. 林堡奶酪味道很浓，但是很好吃。|近义词：province (for the region), cheese (for the cheese) |反义词：无
