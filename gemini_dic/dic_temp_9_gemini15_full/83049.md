gurgled:/ˈɡɜːrɡld/|/ˈɡɜːrɡəld/| vt.|咕噜咕噜地响；发出汩汩声 |To make a sound like water running quickly through a narrow space.  It often describes the sound of liquid flowing, bubbling, or being poured.|例句：The water gurgled down the drain. 水咕噜咕噜地流进了下水道。

The stream gurgled merrily over the smooth stones. 小溪愉快地哗哗流过光滑的石头。


近义词：bubble, gurgle, gurgle, splash, swish|反义词：hush, silence
