nickering:/ˈnɪkərɪŋ/| n.|得得的叫声；低声的笑声|The sound a horse makes, a soft, gentle whinny.  It can also refer to a soft, suppressed laugh.|例句：The horse responded with a soft nickering.  这匹马发出轻轻的得得声。

The children giggled, their laughter a low nickering. 孩子们咯咯地笑着，笑声低低的。|近义词：whinnying, snickering|反义词：roaring, bellowing
