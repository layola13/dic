monotherapy:/ˌmɒnəʊˈθɛrəpi/| n. |单药疗法|The use of a single drug to treat a disease or condition.  This approach contrasts with combination therapy, which involves using multiple drugs simultaneously.|Example:  The doctor prescribed monotherapy for his hypertension. 医生给他开了单药治疗高血压的药。The patient responded well to monotherapy with a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI).  患者对选择性血清素再摄取抑制剂(SSRI)的单药疗法反应良好。|近义词: single-drug therapy |反义词: combination therapy, polytherapy
