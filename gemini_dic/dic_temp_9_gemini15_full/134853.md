nonmodernness:/ˌnɒnˈmɒdərnəs/| n. |非现代性，非现代感|The lack of modern characteristics or qualities; the state of being nonmodern.  It often refers to a deliberate rejection of modern trends or a nostalgic attachment to older styles or ways of life. |例句：The building's nonmodernness was striking, a stark contrast to the sleek skyscrapers around it. 这座建筑物的非现代感很突出，与周围光滑的摩天大楼形成鲜明对比。

The museum's exhibit showcased the nonmodernness of traditional crafts. 博物馆的展览展示了传统工艺的非现代性。|近义词：antimodernism, traditionalism, archaism|反义词：modernity, modernism
