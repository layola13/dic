It is bordered by the Republic of Benin in the west, Niger in the north, Chad and Cameroon in the east, and the Gulf of Guinea in the south.  Nigeria is a diverse country with over 250 ethnic groups and numerous languages, with English as the official language.  Its economy is largely dependent on oil production, but it also has a significant agricultural sector.  The country has faced challenges with corruption, political instability, and conflict in different regions.
