commandlet:/ˈkəˈmændlɛt/ | n. | 命令程序，命令子程序 | A commandlet is a lightweight command in PowerShell that performs a single, well-defined operation.  It's a fundamental building block for creating more complex PowerShell scripts and automation.  Unlike full-fledged applications, commandlets are designed for integration within the PowerShell environment and typically interact with other cmdlets through pipelines. | Example:  Get-ChildItem lists files and folders.  Set-Location changes the current directory. | 近义词：PowerShell command, function (in a broader sense) | 反义词：N/A
