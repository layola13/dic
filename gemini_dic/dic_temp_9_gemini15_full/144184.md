outsideness: /ˈaʊtsaɪdnəs/ | n. | 外部性；外部状态；局外人的地位 | The state or quality of being outside; the condition of being an outsider.  It can refer to physical location, social standing, or a feeling of detachment. |


例句：

He felt a sense of outsideness at the party, knowing no one. = 他在派对上感到格格不入，因为他不认识任何人。

The outsideness of the building was striking, with its unusual architecture. = 建筑物的外部非常醒目，其建筑风格独特。


近义词：externality, alienation, separation, detachment, exclusion

反义词：inclusion, integration, belonging, insider status, intimacy


词性：名词
