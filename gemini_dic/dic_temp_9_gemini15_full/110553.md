limestone:/ˈlaɪmstəʊn/| n. |石灰石|A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate (CaCO3), usually in the form of calcite, and often containing fossils.|例句：The pyramids were built from limestone blocks.  金字塔是用石灰石块建造的。

例句：The cliffs are made of a type of hard, grey limestone.  悬崖是由一种坚硬的灰色石灰石构成的。


近义词：calcium carbonate, carbonate rock | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "limestone" comes from the Old English words "lim" (meaning "glue" or "cement") and "stān" (meaning "stone").  This reflects its historical use as a source of lime, a binding agent in mortar and cement.
