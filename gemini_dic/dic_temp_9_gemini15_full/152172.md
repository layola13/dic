patriarchally:/ˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkəli/| adv.| 父权地；家长式地 | In a manner characteristic of a patriarch or patriarchy; dominated by men.  |例句：Many societies operate patriarchally, with men holding most positions of power. 许多社会运作模式是父权制的，男性占据着大多数权力职位。
例句：The family was run patriarchally, with the father making all the important decisions. 这个家庭是由父亲家长式地管理的，所有重要的决定都由父亲做出。|近义词：paternalistically, autocratically |反义词：matriarchally, egalitarianly
