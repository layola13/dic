gaskets:/ˈɡæskɪts/| n. |垫圈；密封垫|A gasket is a mechanical seal, typically made of rubber, plastic, or metal, that is used to create a tight seal between two surfaces.  It prevents leaks of liquids, gases, or other materials.  Gaskets are commonly used in engines, pipes, and other machinery.|例句：The mechanic replaced the worn-out gaskets in the engine. 机械师更换了发动机里磨损的垫圈。


The car's leaking because the head gasket is blown. 汽车漏油是因为缸垫爆了。|近义词：seal, washer, packing|反义词: opening, gap
