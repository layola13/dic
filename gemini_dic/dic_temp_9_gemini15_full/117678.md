meiotic:/maɪˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 减数的；减数分裂的 | Relating to or involving meiosis.  Meiotic division is a type of cell division that reduces the chromosome number by half, creating gametes (sex cells). | Example: Meiotic cell division is essential for sexual reproduction.  减数细胞分裂对于有性生殖至关重要。 The meiotic process ensures genetic diversity. 减数过程确保遗传多样性。|近义词：gametic |反义词：mitotic
