refires:/ˌriːˈfaɪərz/| v.|重新点燃，再次点火|To ignite or set on fire again.  The word combines "re-" (again) and "fires" (the third-person singular present tense of "fire," meaning to ignite).|例句：The firefighters refired the controlled burn to ensure complete combustion. 消防员重新点燃了受控焚烧以确保完全燃烧。
The pilot refired the engine after the initial attempt failed.  飞行员在第一次尝试失败后重新启动了引擎。|近义词：reignite, rekindle|反义词：extinguish, quench|动词
