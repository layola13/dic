primitiveness:/prɪˈmɪtɪvnəs/| n. |原始性；简陋；粗糙|The state or quality of being primitive; lack of sophistication or development.  It often refers to something simple, undeveloped, or belonging to an early stage of development. This can apply to technology, societies, or even individual behaviors.|例句：The primitiveness of their tools was evident in the crude craftsmanship. 他们工具的原始性在粗糙的工艺中显而易见。

例句：The primitiveness of the living conditions shocked the visitors.  居住条件的简陋震惊了参观者。|近义词：simplicity, crudeness, rusticity, rawness|反义词：sophistication, advancement, refinement, complexity|词性 n.
