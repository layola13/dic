scripted:/ˈskrɪptɪd/ | adj. | 编剧的；有剧本的；按剧本表演的 |  Written or planned in advance, as for a play or film; following a script.  | Example: The play was entirely scripted, with no room for improvisation. 这出戏完全是按照剧本表演的，没有即兴发挥的空间。  The actors delivered their lines with perfect scripted timing. 演员们以完美的预设时间表来表演他们的台词。 | 近义词：prearranged, preplanned, predetermined | 反义词：improvised, spontaneous, unscripted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The play is scripted by a famous playwright. = 这部剧是由一位著名的剧作家编剧的。
The actors are scripted to say those lines. = 演员们被要求说那些台词。


一般过去时:
The movie was scripted last year. = 这部电影是去年编剧的。
The director scripted the ending scene. = 导演编写了结尾场景。


现在完成时:
The script has been scripted and ready for rehearsals. = 剧本已经编写完成并准备好排练了。
They have scripted several scenes already. = 他们已经编写了好几个场景了。


过去完成时:
The play had been scripted before the actors arrived. = 在演员到达之前，剧本就已经写好了。
The screenwriter had scripted the entire movie before he started editing. = 编剧在开始剪辑之前就已经写好了整部电影的剧本。


将来时:
The new TV series will be scripted by a talented newcomer. = 这部新的电视剧将由一位有才华的新人编剧。
The play will be fully scripted by next week. = 这部戏将在下周完全编剧完成。


虚拟语气:
If the play were scripted differently, it might have been more successful. = 如果这出戏的剧本不同，它可能会更成功。
I wish the movie had been better scripted. = 我希望这部电影的剧本更好一些。


被动语态:
The dialogue was scripted by the director himself. = 对白是由导演本人编写的。
The scenes are being scripted as we speak. = 场景正在被编写。


疑问句:
Is the speech scripted? = 这篇演讲是有剧本的吗？
Was the interview scripted? = 采访是有剧本的吗？


否定句:
The performance wasn't scripted; it was completely improvised. = 演出不是有剧本的；它是完全即兴创作的。
The comedian's jokes weren't scripted. = 喜剧演员的笑话不是事先写好的。
