pucker:/ˈpʌkər/| v. n.|皱缩，噘嘴|To gather or draw together into wrinkles or folds; to purse one's lips.|例句：The old leather puckered with age.  这块旧皮革因年代久远而皱缩。
例句：She puckered her lips in disapproval. 她不满地噘起了嘴。|近义词：wrinkle, crease, purse|反义词：smooth, flatten|动词
