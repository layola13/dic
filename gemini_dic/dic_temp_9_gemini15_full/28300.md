capybara:/ˈkæpɪbɑːrə/ | n. |水豚|A large, semi-aquatic, herbivorous rodent native to South America.  Capybaras are known for their docile nature and are the largest living rodents in the world. They are social animals and live in groups. |例句：The capybara peacefully grazed near the riverbank.  这只水豚平静地吃着河边的草。|例句：We saw a family of capybaras swimming in the lake. 我们看到一群水豚在湖里游泳。|近义词：rodent (a more general term)|反义词：None specifically.
