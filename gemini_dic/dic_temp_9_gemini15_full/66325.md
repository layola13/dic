excreting:/ɪkˈskriːtɪŋ/ | v. | 排泄；分泌 | The act of excreting waste products from the body.  It includes processes like urination, defecation, and sweating.  The process is essential for maintaining homeostasis. |例句：The kidneys are excreting toxins from the blood.  肾脏正在排出血液中的毒素。|例句：The sweat glands are excreting excess water and salt. 汗腺正在排出多余的水分和盐分。|近义词：eliminating, discharging, secreting |反义词：absorbing, retaining |动词
