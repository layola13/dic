overstretches: /ˌoʊvərˈstrɛtʃɪz/ | vt. |过度伸展；过度使用；使过度劳累 | To stretch or extend too far; to use or strain excessively; to overwork. |例句：He overstretched himself trying to finish the project on time. 他为了按时完成项目而过度劳累。

例句：The company overstretched its resources by expanding too quickly. 公司扩张过快，导致资源过度使用。


近义词：overextend, strain, overtax, exhaust

反义词：underuse, conserve, rest
