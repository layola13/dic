preelectric: /ˌpriːɪˈlɛktrɪk/ | adj. |电气化之前的，前电力的|  Existing or occurring before the widespread use of electricity.  This word describes a time or condition prior to the common availability and use of electricity. |例句：Preelectric homes relied on gaslight for illumination.  前电力时代的房屋依靠煤气灯照明。


例句：The preelectric era saw widespread use of candles and oil lamps.  前电力时代广泛使用蜡烛和油灯。


近义词：pre-electrical, ante-electric


反义词：post-electric, electric


词性：形容词
