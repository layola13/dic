overmedicated:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|用药过度的；药物滥用的|  Receiving or having received more medication than is necessary or advisable.  This condition can stem from various factors, including doctor error, patient misunderstanding, or intentional misuse of prescription drugs.  It can lead to serious health consequences.|例句：He was overmedicated after his surgery and experienced several adverse side effects. 他手术后用药过度，经历了几种不良反应。

The patient's overmedicated state contributed to her confusion and disorientation. 病人的用药过度导致她精神错乱和迷失方向。|近义词：polypharmacy, drugged, overdosed|反义词：undermedicated, appropriately medicated
