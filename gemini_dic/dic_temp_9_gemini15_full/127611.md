neediness:/ˈniːdinəs/ | n. |需要；匮乏；依赖性 |The state of needing or wanting something, often excessively; a strong desire for something.  It can also refer to a lack of something essential, or an excessive dependence on others. |例句：His neediness stemmed from a lack of parental affection in his childhood.他的需要源于童年缺乏父母的爱。

例句：Her constant neediness pushed him away. 她不断的需要把他推开了。


近义词：dependence, desperation, craving, want, urgency |反义词：self-sufficiency, independence, self-reliance |词性: 名词
