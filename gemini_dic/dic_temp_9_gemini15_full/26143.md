bustling: /ˈbʌs(ə)lɪŋ/ | adj. | 熙熙攘攘的；热闹的；繁忙的 | Full of energy and activity; lively and busy.  | 例句：The bustling city streets were filled with people.  熙熙攘攘的城市街道上挤满了人。

The bustling market was a feast for the senses. 热闹的市场是一场感官盛宴。| 近义词：lively, busy, vibrant, thriving, animated | 反义词：quiet, calm, still, deserted, stagnant | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The market is bustling with shoppers. = 市场挤满了购物者。
The city center is always bustling with activity. = 市中心总是熙熙攘攘的。


一般过去时:
The streets were bustling with activity during the festival. = 节日期间街道上熙熙攘攘。
The cafe was bustling with customers last night. = 昨晚咖啡馆里顾客盈门。


现在完成时:
The town has become bustling since the new shopping mall opened. = 自从新的购物中心开业以来，这个小镇变得热闹非凡。
The area has been bustling with tourists all summer. = 整个夏天这个地区一直游人如织。


过去完成时:

The restaurant had been bustling earlier in the evening. = 晚上早些时候餐厅里人声鼎沸。

虚拟语气:
