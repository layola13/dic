full:/fʊl/|adj.|满的；完全的；彻底的；饱的|Completely or entirely filled or occupied; containing as much or as many as possible.  |例句：The glass is full of water. 这杯子装满了水。|例句：He gave a full account of the incident. 他对事件做了详尽的叙述。|近义词：complete, entire, total, absolute|反义词：empty, partial, incomplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bag is full. = 袋子满了。
The room is full of people. = 房间里挤满了人。

一般过去时:
The tank was full yesterday. = 昨天油箱是满的。
The stadium was full of excited fans. = 体育场里挤满了兴奋的球迷。

现在完成时:
The bottle has been full all day. = 瓶子一整天都是满的。
She has had a full day at work. = 她今天工作了一整天。

过去完成时:
The glass had been full before he drank from it. = 他喝之前杯子是满的。
They had a full understanding of the project before starting. = 在开始之前他们对这个项目有了充分的了解。

将来时:
The pool will be full tomorrow. = 游泳池明天将被注满。
The theatre will be full for the premiere. = 首映式剧院将座无虚席。

虚拟语气:
If the tank were full, we could travel further. = 如果油箱是满的，我们就能走得更远。
I wish my schedule wasn't so full. = 我希望我的日程安排不要那么满。

被动语态:
The container is filled full. = 容器装满了。
The room was filled full of balloons. = 房间里装满了气球。

疑问句:
Is the cup full? = 杯子满了吗？
Is your schedule full this week? = 你这周日程安排很满吗？

否定句:
The jar isn't full yet. = 罐子还没满。
The auditorium wasn't full. = 礼堂没有坐满。


PHRASE->
in full = 全部地；完全地
full of = 充满…的
to the full = 充分地；彻底地
full stop = 句号
full time = 全职的
