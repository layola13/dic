ringer:/ˈrɪŋər/| n.|模仿者；替身；非常相似的人或物|A ringer is a person or thing that closely resembles another.  It can refer to someone who substitutes for another person, especially in a competition, or something that is a very close copy or imitation.|例句：He was a ringer for his father. 他和他父亲长得一模一样。

例句：The painting was such a good ringer, it fooled the experts. 这幅画仿造得如此逼真，连专家都被骗了。

近义词：double, substitute, copy, imitation

反义词：original, genuine
