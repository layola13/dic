prorebel:/ˈproʊˌrɛbəl/| n. |叛乱分子，造反者|A prorebel is a person who supports or participates in a rebellion or uprising against an established authority.  They often advocate for significant political or social change through unconventional and sometimes violent means. |例句：The prorebel group launched a surprise attack on the government forces. 叛乱分子对政府军发起了突然袭击。|例句：Many prorebels were arrested after the failed coup attempt.  政变未遂后，许多叛乱分子被捕。|近义词：insurgent, rebel, revolutionary|反义词：loyalist, conformist|名词
