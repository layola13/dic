interlanguage:/ˈɪntərˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ/| n. |中间语言，过渡语言|A language system used by a second language learner which is neither their first language nor the target language; it is characterized by features from both languages and sometimes new features not present in either.|Example:  The learner's interlanguage showed a mixture of English and Spanish grammatical structures.  学习者的中间语言显示出英语和西班牙语语法结构的混合。|近义词：pidgin, transitional language|反义词：native language, target language
