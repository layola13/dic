left-hander:/ˈlɛftˌhændər/| n. adj.| 左撇子；惯用左手的人；左撇子的| A person who uses their left hand more skillfully than their right hand.  It can also refer to things designed for left-handed use. | Example: He's a left-hander and has to use special scissors. 他是个左撇子，必须用特制的剪刀。  She writes with her left hand; she is a left-hander. 她用左手写字；她是个左撇子。|近义词：southpaw|反义词：right-hander|名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a left-hander. = 他是个左撇子。
She uses her left hand more often. = 她更经常使用左手。


一般过去时:
He was a left-hander, even as a child. = 他从小就是个左撇子。
She wrote with her left hand yesterday. = 昨天她用左手写字。


现在完成时:
He has always been a left-hander. = 他一直都是个左撇子。
She has written many letters with her left hand. = 她用左手写过许多信。


过去完成时:
He had been a left-hander for as long as anyone could remember. =  从任何人都记得的时候起，他就是一个左撇子。
She had already written the letter with her left hand before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，她已经用左手写完了信。


将来时:
He will continue to be a left-hander. = 他将继续是左撇子。
She will write this essay with her left hand. = 她将用左手写这篇论文。


虚拟语气:
If he were a left-hander, he would use left-handed scissors. = 如果他是左撇子，他会使用左撇子剪刀。
I wish I were a left-hander; it would make some things easier. = 我希望我是个左撇子；那样一些事情会更容易。


被动语态:
Left-handed scissors are often preferred by left-handers. = 左撇子剪刀通常受到左撇子的青睐。
The letter was written with a left hand. = 这封信是用左手写的。


疑问句:
Is he a left-hander? = 他是左撇子吗？
Are you a left-hander? = 你是左撇子吗？


否定句:
He is not a left-hander; he's right-handed. = 他不是左撇子；他是右撇子。
She doesn't use her left hand for writing. = 她不用左手写字。


PHRASE->

left-handed throw = 左手投球
a left-handed batsman = 左撇子击球手
left-handed drive = 左侧行驶
