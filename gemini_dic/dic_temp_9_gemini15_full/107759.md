lapidarian:/ˌlæpɪˈdɛəriən/ | adj. | 简洁的；精炼的；(尤指)碑文似的 | Concise and precise, especially in style;  resembling or characteristic of inscriptions on stone.  It often implies a sense of formality and elegance. | The lapidarian inscription on the tomb was both beautiful and deeply moving.  墓碑上的碑文简洁而动人。  The professor's lapidarian prose style was admired by his students. 教授简洁的散文风格受到学生的赞赏。 | terse, succinct, pithy | verbose, rambling, diffuse | 形容词
