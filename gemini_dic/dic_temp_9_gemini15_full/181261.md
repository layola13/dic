reinstator: /ˌriːɪnˈsteɪtər/ | n. |恢复职位者；恢复原状者 | One who reinstates someone or something.  A person or thing that restores something to its former state. |例句：The manager acted as a reinstator, bringing back the unjustly fired employee.  经理充当了恢复职位者的角色，让被不公平解雇的员工复职。  |近义词：restorer, reinstaller |反义词：remover, displacer
