mooched:/muːtʃt/| v.| 蹭吃蹭喝；骗取；游手好闲地度过 | To get something by begging or borrowing, especially without intending to repay or return it; to spend time idly or lazily. |例句：He mooched a cigarette from his friend. 他向朋友蹭了一根烟。 He mooched around all afternoon. 他整个下午都游手好闲地混日子。|近义词：scrounge, sponge, cadge, bum |反义词：earn, work for


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mooches money from his parents. = 他向父母要钱。
She mooches rides to work. = 她蹭车去上班。

一般过去时:
I mooched a sandwich from the office kitchen. = 我从办公室厨房里顺走了一个三明治。
They mooched around the town all day. = 他们整天在镇上闲逛。

现在完成时:
He has mooched off his friends for years. = 他多年来一直靠朋友养活。
She has mooched several books from the library without returning them. = 她从图书馆借走了几本书，一直没还。

过去完成时:

They had mooched around for hours before finding a place to eat. = 他们在找到吃饭的地方之前已经闲逛了好几个小时。
