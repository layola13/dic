non-entrepreneurial:/ˌnɒnˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːriəl/ | adj. |非创业的；缺乏创业精神的 | Not having the characteristics or qualities of an entrepreneur; lacking initiative or a willingness to take risks in business.  | Example: The company's non-entrepreneurial culture stifled innovation. 这家公司缺乏创业精神的文化扼杀了创新。|近义词：unenterprising, unadventurous, risk-averse | 反义词：entrepreneurial, enterprising, adventurous
