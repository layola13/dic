pseudo-emotional: /ˈsuːdoʊɪˈmoʊʃənəl/ | adj. | 假装情绪化的；伪装情感的 |  Seeming or pretending to have emotions, often in a superficial or insincere way.  It describes behavior or expressions that mimic genuine emotion but lack genuine feeling. |


例句：

His pseudo-emotional apology felt hollow and unconvincing.  他的假惺惺道歉听起来空洞且缺乏说服力。

She delivered a pseudo-emotional speech filled with clichés and lacking authenticity. 她发表了一篇充满陈词滥调且缺乏真实感的假情假意演讲。


近义词：affected, artificial, insincere, contrived, theatrical, feigned, simulated

反义词：genuine, authentic, sincere, heartfelt, real, true


词性: adjective
