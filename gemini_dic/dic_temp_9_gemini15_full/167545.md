prevues:/ˈpruːvjuːz/| n.| 预告片；预展 |Short films or excerpts shown in advance of the main feature, typically in a movie theater.  They give a glimpse of upcoming films or events.|例句：The movie previews were exciting and made me want to see the films.|近义词：trailers, previews, sneak peeks|反义词：full film, complete showing

USAGE->

The theater shows previews before every movie. = 电影院每部电影前都放映预告片。
These previews highlight the best scenes. = 这些预告片突出了最佳场景。

一般过去时:

The previews captivated the audience. = 预告片吸引了观众。
