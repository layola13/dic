reimburse:/ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrs/|/ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrs/| vt.|偿还；报销；补偿|To repay a person for expenses incurred.  To give someone back the money that they have spent.|The company will reimburse you for your travel expenses.  公司将报销您的差旅费。|The insurance company reimbursed me for the damage to my car. 保险公司赔偿了我汽车的损失。|refund, repay, compensate|defraud,  underpay|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reimburses employees for business-related expenses. = 公司报销员工的业务相关费用。
He regularly reimburses his clients for their travel costs. = 他定期向客户报销差旅费。

一般过去时:
They reimbursed me for the cost of the hotel. = 他们报销了我的酒店费用。
The government reimbursed the victims for their losses. = 政府赔偿了受害者的损失。

现在完成时:
She has reimbursed the company for all outstanding expenses. = 她已经向公司报销了所有未结费用。
Have you been reimbursed for your travel expenses yet? = 你的差旅费报销了吗？

过去完成时:
They had reimbursed him before he left for his trip. = 他出发旅行前，他们已经向他报销了费用。
The company had already reimbursed her for the damage. = 公司已经赔偿了她造成的损失。

将来时:
We will reimburse you as soon as we receive the invoice. = 我们收到发票后会立即向您报销。
They will reimburse the participants for their transportation costs. = 他们将报销参与者的交通费用。

虚拟语气:
If the company reimbursed me promptly, I would be very happy. = 如果公司及时地向我报销，我会非常高兴。
I wish they had reimbursed me sooner for the repairs. = 我希望他们能早点赔偿我的维修费用。

被动语态:
His expenses were reimbursed by his employer. = 他的费用由他的雇主报销。
The damaged goods were reimbursed by the supplier. = 损坏的货物由供应商赔偿。

疑问句:
Will the company reimburse me for these expenses? = 公司会报销这些费用吗？
Does your insurance policy reimburse for medical expenses? = 你的保险政策是否报销医疗费用？

否定句:
The company did not reimburse him for the extra costs. = 公司没有报销他的额外费用。
They refused to reimburse me for the faulty product. = 他们拒绝赔偿我因产品故障造成的损失。
