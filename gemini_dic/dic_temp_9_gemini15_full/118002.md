memories:/ˈmeməriːz/| n.|回忆，记忆 |Plural of memory.  Memories are things you remember from the past. They can be happy, sad, or neutral.  |例句：I have many happy memories of my childhood. = 我有很多关于童年的美好回忆。

例句：The old photograph brought back many fond memories. = 这张旧照片唤起了许多美好的回忆。

近义词：recollections, reminiscences |反义词：forgetfulness, amnesia |词性:名词
