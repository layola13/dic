half-jokingly: /ˌhæfˈdʒoʊkɪŋli/ | adv. | 半开玩笑地，开玩笑似地 |  The adverb "half-jokingly" describes something said or done in a way that is partly serious and partly intended as a joke.  It implies a mixture of seriousness and humor, making it unclear whether the speaker or actor is entirely serious. | 例句：He half-jokingly suggested we should quit our jobs and travel the world. 他半开玩笑地建议我们辞掉工作去环游世界。


He half-jokingly asked if I'd won the lottery. 他开玩笑似地问我是否中了彩票。| 近义词：playfully, teasingly, jokingly | 反义词：seriously, earnestly
