planisher:/ˈplænɪʃər/| n. |平滑机，压平机 |A planisher is a machine used to smooth or flatten a surface, often used in metalworking.  It can refer to various types of machinery depending on the industry and application, but generally involves using pressure to achieve a smooth finish. |Example: The metal sheet was fed into the planisher to achieve a perfectly smooth surface.  压平机将金属板送入以达到完美的平滑表面。|近义词：smoother, leveler, flattener |反义词：roughener, texturizer |名词

词源:
The word "planisher" is derived from the verb "to planish," meaning to smooth or flatten a surface, especially metal.  The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or instrument.  The ultimate origin likely traces back to Old French words related to flattening or smoothing.
