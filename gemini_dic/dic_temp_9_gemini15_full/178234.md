recompensated:/ˌrekəmˈpɛnseɪtɪd/| adj. | 补偿的，报答的 |  Having received compensation or reward.  |例句：The company recompensed the employee for their extra work. 公司因员工的额外工作而给予补偿。

例句：He felt recompensed by the gratitude shown by the people he'd helped. 他因为得到帮助过的人的感激而感到欣慰。


近义词：rewarded, compensated, repaid | 反义词：uncompensated, unrewarded | 词性：形容词 (过去分词作形容词)


词源:

* **re-**: prefix indicating "again" or "back"
* **compensate**: from Latin *compensare* "to weigh equally, make amends for," from *com-* "together" + *pensare* "to weigh," from *pendere* "to hang, weigh."


用法说明：

recompensated是动词compensate的过去分词形式，用作形容词。它表示某人或某物已经得到了补偿或报答。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They compensate their employees fairly. = 他们公平地补偿员工。
The company compensates for lost time. = 公司补偿损失的时间。


一般过去时:
She compensated him for the damage. = 她赔偿了他造成的损失。
The insurance company compensated the victim. = 保险公司赔偿了受害者。


现在完成时:
He has been compensated for his injuries. = 他因受伤已获得赔偿。
The government has compensated the farmers for their losses. = 政府已经赔偿了农民的损失。


过去完成时:
They had already compensated the workers before the protest. = 在抗议之前，他们已经赔偿了工人们。
The company had compensated the customer for the faulty product. = 公司已经赔偿了顾客有缺陷的产品。


将来时:
They will compensate you for the inconvenience. = 他们将赔偿您造成的麻烦。
The court will compensate the plaintiff for their damages. = 法院将赔偿原告的损失。


虚拟语气:
If they compensated me properly, I would be satisfied. = 如果他们妥善地赔偿我，我会满意的。
I wish they had compensated us for the delay. = 我希望他们能赔偿我们因延误造成的损失。


被动语态:
The employees are compensated monthly. = 员工每月获得报酬。
The damage will be compensated by the insurance company. = 损失将由保险公司赔偿。


疑问句:
Are the workers compensated for overtime? = 工人们加班有补偿吗？
Will the company compensate us for the delay? = 公司会赔偿我们因延误造成的损失吗？


否定句:
The company did not compensate the employees for their extra work. = 公司没有因员工的额外工作而给予补偿。
He hasn't been compensated for his injuries yet. = 他还没有因受伤而获得赔偿。


PHRASE->
compensate for = 补偿...
make compensation for = 对...作出赔偿
receive compensation = 得到补偿
adequate compensation = 足够的补偿
financial compensation = 经济补偿
