sequin:/ˈsiːkwɪn/| n. |亮片，小金属片|A small, shiny disk used for ornamentation, typically sewn onto clothing.  Sequins are often made of metal, plastic, or glass and come in a variety of colors and sizes.|例句：The dancer's costume was covered in sequins.  舞者的服装上缀满了亮片。|例句：She added sequins to her dress to make it more festive. 她给她的裙子加上了亮片，使它更喜庆。|近义词：spangle, paillettes|反义词：None.
