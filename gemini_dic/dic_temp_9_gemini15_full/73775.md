formalistically:/fɔːˈmælɪstɪkli/ | adv. |形式主义地，表面上 |  Adverb describing something done in a formal or superficial way, emphasizing form over substance or meaning. It often implies a lack of genuine feeling or understanding.  |例句：He approached the problem formalistically, applying rules without considering the context. 他形式化地处理这个问题，应用规则而不考虑背景。  |例句：The painting was criticized for being too formalistically arranged, lacking emotional depth. 这幅画因过于形式化地安排而受到批评，缺乏情感深度。 |近义词：conventionally, superficially, mechanically |反义词：substantively, meaningfully, organically
