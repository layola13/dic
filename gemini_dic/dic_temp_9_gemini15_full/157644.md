pittances:/ˈpɪtənsɪz/| n.|少量钱；微薄的薪水；少量食物|A pittance is a very small amount of money, especially wages or a payment.  It suggests inadequacy and often implies unfairness or insufficient compensation.|例句：He was paid a pittance for his hard work. 他辛辛苦苦工作却只得到微薄的薪水。|例句：The family survived on pittances.  这个家庭靠着微薄的收入生存。|近义词：alms, trifle, meager allowance, paltry sum|反义词：fortune, windfall, abundance, opulence|名词
