preconceived:/ˌpriːkənˈsiːvd/| adj.| 预先构思的；先入为主的 | formed or conceived beforehand; existing in the mind before encountering the thing referred to |The preconceived notions about his profession were completely wrong. 他对他的职业的先入为主的观念是完全错误的。|近义词：predetermined, preordained, prior |反义词：unforeseen, unexpected


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He has preconceived ideas about politics. = 他对政治有先入为主的想法。
She holds preconceived notions about people from other countries. = 她对来自其他国家的人持有先入为主的观念。


一般过去时:
I had preconceived notions about the job interview.  = 我对这次工作面试有先入为主的想法。
They preconceived a plan to win the game. = 他们预先制定了一个赢得比赛的计划。


现在完成时:
We have preconceived a strategy for the marketing campaign. = 我们已经为营销活动预先制定了一个策略。
She has preconceived a negative opinion of him based on rumors. = 她根据谣言对他有了负面的先入为主的看法。


过去完成时:
I had preconceived that the meeting would be boring. = 我预先认为会议会很无聊。
They had preconceived a route before starting their journey. = 他们出发前已经预先规划好了路线。


将来时:
We will preconceive a design for the new building. = 我们将预先设计新建筑的设计。
They will preconceive a solution to the problem before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前会预先想出一个解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If I had not preconceived the outcome, I might have been surprised. = 如果我没有预先设想结果，我可能会感到惊讶。
I wish I hadn't preconceived my opinion on the matter. = 我真希望我没有对这件事先入为主。


被动语态:
The plan was preconceived months in advance. = 计划是提前几个月就预先制定的。
The design was preconceived by the architect. = 这个设计是由建筑师预先构思的。


疑问句:
Were the assumptions preconceived or based on evidence? = 这些假设是预先设定的还是基于证据的？
Had they preconceived any strategies for the negotiation? = 他们在谈判之前是否预先制定了任何策略？


否定句:
The solution wasn't preconceived; it was developed organically. = 这个解决方案不是预先设定的；它是自然发展起来的。
Their approach to the problem wasn't preconceived; they were open to new ideas. = 他们解决问题的方法并非预先设定的；他们对新想法持开放态度。


PHRASE->

preconceived ideas/notions:  先入为主的想法/观念
preconceived opinions: 先入为主的观点
preconceived judgments: 先入为主的判断
preconceived biases: 先入为主的偏见
preconceived plan: 预先制定的计划
