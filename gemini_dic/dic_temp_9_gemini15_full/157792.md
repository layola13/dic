placid:/ˈplæsɪd/| adj. |平静的；安详的；温和的|calm and peaceful; tranquil; undisturbed.  The placid lake reflected the clear blue sky.  平静的湖面映照着湛蓝的天空。|近义词：calm, tranquil, serene, peaceful |反义词：turbulent, agitated, stormy, restless
