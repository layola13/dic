hemidactyl:/ˈhɛmɪˌdæktɪl/| n. |半趾动物，半指动物|A hemidactyl is an animal with only half the normal number of toes or fingers on a limb.  This often refers to a condition where a digit is partially formed or missing.  The term is used primarily in zoology and comparative anatomy. |Example:  The fossil evidence suggests the presence of hemidactylous species in this region.  这个地区的化石证据表明存在半趾动物物种。|近义词:  partially-toed, incompletely-fingered (these are less precise)|反义词:  penta(five)-dactyl(具有五趾的)
