fabricatress:/ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪtrɪs/| n.|女制造者；女伪造者|A woman who fabricates things, especially a woman who makes cloth or other textiles.  It can also refer to a woman who creates something false or deceptive.|例句：The skilled fabricatress wove intricate patterns into the tapestry.  熟练的女织工在挂毯上织出了复杂的图案。The journalist suspected the witness was a fabricatress of lies.  记者怀疑证人是在编造谎言。|近义词：female fabricator, woman manufacturer, female forger|反义词：consumer, destroyer


USAGE->

The company fabricates car parts. = 这家公司制造汽车零件。
