manifesting:/ˈmænɪfɛstɪŋ/ | v. | 显现；表现；证明；表明 |  To make something known or obvious; to show something clearly; to become real or apparent.  | 例句：Her talent is manifesting itself in her artwork.  她的天赋在她的艺术作品中显现出来。

例句：The symptoms of the disease manifested themselves quickly.  疾病的症状很快显现出来。


近义词：showing, revealing, demonstrating, exhibiting, displaying | 反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing, obscuring


词源：源自于拉丁语 "manifestāre"，意思是“使明显”，由 "manifestus"  (明显的) 派生而来。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is manifesting his anger through aggressive behavior. = 他正在通过侵略性行为表现他的愤怒。
She manifests great kindness to everyone she meets. = 她对遇到的每一个人都表现出极大的善良。


一般过去时:
The prophecy manifested itself in a surprising way. = 预言以一种令人惊讶的方式实现了。
He manifested a strong desire to succeed. = 他表现出强烈的成功欲望。


现在完成时:
Your hard work has manifested itself in your excellent grades. = 你的努力学习已经体现在你优异的成绩上。
They have manifested a commitment to environmental protection. = 他们已经展现出对环境保护的承诺。


过去完成时:
By the time the police arrived, the suspect had already manifested his guilt. = 等警察到达时，嫌疑人已经表现出他的罪行了。
She had manifested her disapproval by leaving the room early. = 她通过早退表达了她的不满。


将来时:
The effects of climate change will manifest themselves in extreme weather events. = 气候变化的影响将通过极端天气事件表现出来。
The project is expected to manifest significant economic benefits. = 预计该项目将带来可观的经济效益。


虚拟语气:
If he manifested more interest, she might have given him a chance. = 如果他表现出更多的兴趣，她可能会给他一个机会。
I wish he would manifest some understanding of my situation. = 我希望他能表现出对我的处境的理解。


被动语态:
His genius was manifested in his groundbreaking inventions. = 他的天才体现在他开创性的发明中。
The results of the experiment will be manifested in a detailed report. = 实验结果将在详细报告中体现。


疑问句:
How does your belief manifest itself in your daily life? = 你的信仰如何在你的日常生活中体现？
Does this problem manifest in any other way? = 这个问题还有其他表现形式吗？


否定句:
The symptoms did not manifest themselves immediately. = 症状并没有立即显现。
He didn't manifest any signs of illness. = 他没有任何生病的迹象。


PHRASE->
manifest destiny: 天命论
manifest function: 显性功能
manifest content: 表面内容 (精神分析)
manifest error: 明显错误
to manifest itself: 表现出来；显现出来
