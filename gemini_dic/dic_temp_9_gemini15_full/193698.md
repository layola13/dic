self-condemning: /ˌsɛlf kənˈdɛmɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我谴责的；注定失败的 |

英文解析:  Describing something that is inherently flawed or destined to fail, often because of its own nature or design. It implies a built-in mechanism for its own downfall or criticism.

例句:

His self-condemning arrogance alienated his allies.  他的自我谴责的傲慢疏远了他的盟友。

The project was self-condemning from the start, lacking a viable business plan. 这个项目从一开始就注定要失败，因为它缺乏可行的商业计划。


近义词: self-defeating, doomed, failing, flawed, ill-conceived

反义词: successful, promising, viable, well-designed


词性: adjective
