get-even: /ˌɡɛtˈiːvən/ | adj. v. | 报复的；扯平的；报仇的 | To retaliate; to take revenge; to achieve equal standing or balance. |  例句：He planned to get even with his rivals. 他计划向他的对手报复。 He finally got even with his brother for stealing his bike. 他终于报复了他偷他自行车的兄弟。| 近义词：revenge, retaliate, redress | 反义词：forgive, pardon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They get even with their enemies. = 他们向敌人报复。
We get even by winning the game. = 我们通过赢得比赛来报复/扯平。


一般过去时:
She got even with him for his insults. = 她报复了他对她的侮辱。
They got even by revealing his secret. = 他们通过揭露他的秘密来报复/扯平。


现在完成时:
He has got even with his former boss. = 他已经报复了他的前任老板。
They have got even for the unfair treatment. = 他们已经为不公平待遇报复/扯平了。


过去完成时:
She had got even before he could react. = 在他反应过来之前，她已经报复了。
They had got even by the end of the year. = 到年底他们已经报复/扯平了。


将来时:
I will get even with you someday. = 我总有一天会报复你。
We will get even with them for their treachery. = 我们将报复他们的背叛。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would get even with them. = 如果我是你，我会报复他们。
I wish I had gotten even sooner. = 我希望我早点报复。


被动语态:
Revenge should not be gotten. = 不应该报复。
His betrayal was gotten even for. = 他的背叛得到了报应。


疑问句:
How will you get even? = 你将如何报复？
Did he ever get even with his tormentors? = 他有没有报复过他的折磨者？


否定句:
He didn't get even with his bullies. = 他没有报复他的欺凌者。
She chose not to get even, despite the provocation. = 尽管受到了激怒，她还是选择不报复。



PHRASE->
get even with someone = 向某人报复
get even for something = 为某事报复
get square with someone = 与某人扯平
get back at someone = 报复某人
even the score = 扳回比分/报复
