dispensing:/dɪˈspɛnsɪŋ/| v.|分发；配药；给予；施予 |The act of giving out or distributing something, often in small amounts.  It can also refer to the preparation and distribution of medicine. |例句：The pharmacist is dispensing medication. 药剂师正在配药。

例句：The machine is dispensing drinks. 这台机器正在分发饮料。


近义词：distributing, allotting, administering, supplying

反义词：receiving, collecting, withholding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pharmacy is dispensing flu vaccines. = 药房正在分发流感疫苗。
He dispenses wisdom freely. = 他慷慨地给予智慧。


一般过去时:
The teacher dispensed the test papers. = 老师分发了试卷。
The doctor dispensed the medicine. = 医生开了药。


现在完成时:
The hospital has dispensed thousands of doses of the vaccine. = 医院已经分发了数千剂疫苗。
She has dispensed sound advice throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她一直提供合理的建议。


过去完成时:
They had dispensed all the food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经分发了所有的食物。
The pharmacist had dispensed the wrong medication. = 药剂师开了错药。


将来时:
The company will dispense bonuses to its employees. = 公司将向员工发放奖金。
They will dispense justice fairly. = 他们将公平地执行正义。


虚拟语气:
If the machine were dispensing correctly, we wouldn't have this problem. = 如果机器正常运行，我们就不会有这个问题。
I wish the government would dispense more aid to the needy. = 我希望政府能向穷人提供更多援助。


被动语态:
The medicine was dispensed by the doctor. = 药是由医生开的。
The prizes were dispensed fairly among the winners. = 奖品在获胜者中公平地分配。


疑问句:
Is the machine dispensing properly? = 机器运行正常吗？
Are they dispensing the medicine correctly? = 他们正确地配药了吗？


否定句:
The machine is not dispensing tickets. = 机器没有出票。
The doctor did not dispense the prescribed medication. = 医生没有开出处方药。
