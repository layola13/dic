hauntedness:/ˈhɔːntɪdnəs/ | n. | 阴森感；鬼气；萦绕不去的感觉 | The feeling of being haunted or pursued by something, often something from the past;  a sense of unease or dread associated with a place or situation.  It can refer to a physical feeling of a presence or a psychological feeling of being troubled by memories or anxieties. | 例句：The old house had an undeniable hauntedness. 这栋老房子有一种无法否认的阴森感。

The hauntedness of her memories kept her awake at night.  她挥之不去的回忆让她整夜睡不着觉。


近义词：eerie feeling, unease, dread, gloom, oppression, spectrality


反义词：peace, tranquility, serenity, calm, composure


词性：名词
