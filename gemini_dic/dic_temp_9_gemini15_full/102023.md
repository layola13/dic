isohaemagglutination:/ˌaɪsoʊhiːməˌɡlætjuˈneɪʃən/| n. |同族凝集|The clumping together of red blood cells from the same blood group due to the presence of naturally occurring antibodies. This is a phenomenon that can occur in certain individuals and can have implications for blood transfusions.|例句：Isohaemagglutination is a rare but potentially serious complication of blood transfusions. 同族凝集是输血罕见但可能严重的并发症。|近义词：agglutination, clumping|反义词：none|名词
