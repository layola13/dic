involucrum:/ɪnˈvɒljʊkrəm/| n. |总苞，外被，包膜 |A structure that encloses or surrounds another structure, particularly in botany, referring to a bract or group of bracts that surrounds a flower or inflorescence.  In anatomy, it can refer to a sheath or covering. |例句：The involucrum of the sunflower is a ring of modified leaves surrounding the flower head.  向日葵的总苞是围绕花头的变态叶环。

The involucrum of the nerve is a protective sheath. 神经的包膜是一个保护套。|近义词：sheath, covering, envelope, wrapper |反义词：interior, core
