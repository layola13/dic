immaterialism: /ɪˌmætəriəlɪzəm/ | n. | 非物质主义；精神主义 | The philosophical doctrine that denies the existence of matter, or that regards mind as the only reality.  It emphasizes the primacy of mind or spirit over matter. | Example:  Immaterialism is a metaphysical position that contrasts sharply with materialism.  非物质主义是一种与唯物主义形成鲜明对比的形而上学立场。


| Example: Some religious beliefs are rooted in immaterialism, positing a spiritual realm as more fundamental than the physical world. 一些宗教信仰根植于非物质主义，认为精神领域比物质世界更根本。 | 近义词：idealism, spiritualism, mentalism | 反义词：materialism, physicalism |
