/ˈdʒoʊkiː/ | n. | 骑师，玩笑 | A jockey is a person who rides horses in races.  A joke is something said or done to cause laughter. | 例句：The jockey skillfully guided his horse to victory.  骑师熟练地引导他的马获胜。  That joke was so corny!  那个笑话太老套了！| 近义词：rider (for jockey),  gag, jest, prank (for joke) | 反义词： (for jockey)  none directly applicable, (for joke) serious statement
