howzit:/ˈhaʊzɪt/| int.|你好，怎么样？|Informal greeting, common in South Africa and other regions.  Similar to "How's it going?" or "What's up?".|例句：Howzit, mate! How's your day going?  你好，伙计！你今天过得怎么样？|例句：Howzit! Long time no see!  你好！好久不见！|近义词：hi, hey, what's up, how's it going|反义词: N/A


USAGE->
This word is primarily an interjection, and doesn't have tenses in the same way verbs do.  It functions as a greeting. Therefore, typical tense examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
howzit going? = 怎么样？
howzit bru = 你好吗，兄弟？（在南非语境下）
howzit my man = 你好吗，我的朋友？（在南非语境下）
howzit boss = 你好吗，老板？（在南非语境下）
howzit my friend = 你好吗，我的朋友？
