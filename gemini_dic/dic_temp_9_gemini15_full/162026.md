prayerwise:/ˈpreərwaɪz/ | adv. | 就祈祷而言；从祈祷的角度来看 |  This word is an adverb formed by combining "prayer" and "wise." It indicates a perspective or consideration related to prayer.  It suggests looking at a situation from a religious or spiritual standpoint, emphasizing faith, devotion, or seeking divine intervention. | Example: Prayerwise, things are looking up.  从祈祷的角度来看，情况正在好转。 | 近义词：spiritually, religiously, devotionally | 反义词：worldly, materially, secularly
