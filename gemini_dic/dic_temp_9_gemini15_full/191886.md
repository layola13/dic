scolding:/ˈskoʊldɪŋ/|/ˈskəʊldɪŋ/| n. |责骂；训斥 |The act of scolding someone severely.  It often involves criticism and reprimand. |例句：Her scolding made the child cry.  她的责骂使孩子哭了起来。|例句：The teacher gave the student a scolding for being late.老师因学生迟到而训斥了他。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, reproach, admonition|反义词：praise, commendation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scolds his children frequently. = 他经常责骂他的孩子们。
She scolds him for his mistakes. = 她因他的错误而责备他。

一般过去时:
My mother scolded me for breaking the vase. = 妈妈因为我打破花瓶而责骂我。
The teacher scolded the students for their bad behavior. = 老师因为学生们的恶劣行为而责骂他们。

现在完成时:
She has scolded him several times this week. = 本周她已经责骂了他好几次。
He has never scolded me before. = 他以前从未责骂过我。

过去完成时:
The principal had already scolded him before the parents arrived. = 校长在家长到达之前就已经责骂过他了。
She had scolded him many times before he finally changed his behavior. = 在他最终改变行为之前，她已经责骂过他许多次了。

将来时:
I think she will scold him if he does it again. = 我想如果他再这样做，她会责骂他的。
He will be scolded by his boss if he is late again. = 如果他再次迟到，他会受到老板的责骂。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't scold the child so harshly. = 如果我是你，我就不会这么严厉地责骂孩子。
I wish I hadn't scolded him so angrily. = 我真希望我没有那么生气地责骂他。

被动语态:
The child was scolded for his bad manners. = 这个孩子因为他的坏习惯而受到责骂。
He was scolded by his teacher for cheating on the test. = 他因为考试作弊而被老师责骂。

疑问句:
Was the child scolded for his behavior? = 孩子因为他的行为而受到责骂了吗？
Will he be scolded for his lateness? = 他会因为迟到而受到责骂吗？

否定句:
She didn't scold him for his mistake. = 她没有因为他的错误而责骂他。
He wasn't scolded by his parents. = 他没有受到父母的责骂。


PHRASE->
a harsh scolding  严厉的训斥

receive a scolding  受到责骂
deserve a scolding  应该受到责骂
give someone a scolding  训斥某人
