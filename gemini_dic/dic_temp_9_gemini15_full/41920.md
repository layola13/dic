cosmographic:/ˌkɒzməˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|宇宙描绘的；宇宙学的|Describing or relating to the mapping or description of the universe.  It often refers to older, pre-telescopic models of the universe.|Example: The cosmographic model presented in the book was based on the geocentric view of the universe. 这本书中提出的宇宙描绘模型是基于地心宇宙观。|近义词：cosmical, universal|反义词：microscopic, terrestrial
