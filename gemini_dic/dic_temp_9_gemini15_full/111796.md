lofty:/ˈlɒfti; ˈlɔːfti/| adj.|崇高的；高耸的；傲慢的；自大的|  lofty refers to something that is of great height, or to something that is elevated in character or status. It can also describe someone who is arrogant or haughty.|

例句：
The mountains were lofty and majestic. =  山峰高耸而雄伟。

The professor held lofty ideals about education. =这位教授对教育抱有崇高的理想。


近义词：high, elevated, towering, grand, noble, superior, arrogant, haughty

反义词：low, humble, modest, down-to-earth, lowly


词源:
源自中古英语 *lofty，源于古英语 *lōft “空中，天花板”，与古高地德语 *luft “空气”同源。
