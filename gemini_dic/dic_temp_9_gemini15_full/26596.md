cacaos:/ˈkækeɪoʊz/| n.|可可豆，可可粉|The word "cacaos" is the plural form of "cacao," referring to the dried and fully fermented seeds of the cacao tree, from which cocoa powder and chocolate are made.  It can also refer to the powder itself. |Example:  The chocolatier carefully sourced fine cacaos from Ecuador. 巧克力师傅仔细地从厄瓜多尔采购上等的可可豆。


The farmer harvested a bountiful crop of cacaos this year. 今年农民收获了大量可可豆。|近义词：cocoa beans, cocoa powder|反义词：无|名词
