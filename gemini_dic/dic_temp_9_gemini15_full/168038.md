private-detectives: /ˈpraɪvət dɪˈtɛktɪvz/ | n. | 私家侦探们 | Private detectives are investigators who work independently, often hired by individuals or companies to conduct investigations outside of law enforcement.  They investigate cases such as infidelity, missing persons, background checks, and corporate espionage. | The private detectives found the missing documents. 私家侦探找到了丢失的文件。  The company hired private detectives to investigate the leak. 公司雇佣私家侦探调查泄密事件。 | investigators, inquiry agents | law enforcement officers
