lissencephaly:/lɪˈsɛnsɪfəli/| n. |脑回减少症，脑发育不全|A condition in which the brain's surface lacks the normal folds and grooves (gyri and sulci). This results in a smooth-appearing brain surface.  It's a serious neurological disorder that can cause significant intellectual disability and seizures.| Example: The infant was diagnosed with lissencephaly at birth.|近义词：agyria|反义词：None (describes a condition, not a trait with a direct opposite)|名词
