pagan:/ˈpeɪɡən/| n. adj.|异教徒；异教的；|A pagan is a person who follows a polytheistic religion, or a religion that does not involve belief in a single God.  The term is often used to describe people who lived before the rise of Christianity, Judaism, or Islam, and their practices.|例句：Many ancient civilizations were pagan.  许多古代文明都是异教的。|例句：The festival has pagan origins. 这个节日起源于异教。|近义词：heathen, idolater|反义词：Christian, Muslim, Jew
