quasi-logical:/ˈkwɑːziˈlɒdʒɪkəl/|/ˈkwɑːzaɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj. |似逻辑的，准逻辑的 | Seemingly logical but not strictly conforming to the rules of logic; appearing logical but lacking rigorous logical structure or justification.  It suggests a resemblance to logical reasoning but falls short of true logical rigor. |The argument, while quasi-logical, contained several unsupported assumptions.  这个论点虽然看似合乎逻辑，但却包含几个未经证实的假设。|近义词：plausible, seemingly logical, superficially logical |反义词： illogical, fallacious, nonsensical
