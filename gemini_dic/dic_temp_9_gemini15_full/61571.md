emer:/ɪˈmɜːr/ | v. |浮现；出现；显露；(问题、困难等)发生|To come forth into view; appear; become manifest; arise (as a problem or difficulty).|The sun emerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云后出现了。|The truth will eventually emerge. 真相最终会水落石出。|近义词：appear, arise, surface |反义词：disappear, vanish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emerges from behind the clouds every morning. = 太阳每天早上从云后出现。
New problems emerge constantly in this project. = 在这个项目中不断出现新的问题。


一般过去时:
The singer emerged from the backstage. = 歌手从后台出来了。
A new challenge emerged during the negotiations. = 在谈判过程中出现了一个新的挑战。


现在完成时:
The truth has finally emerged after years of investigation. = 经过多年的调查，真相终于大白。
Several issues have emerged since the last meeting. = 自上次会议以来出现了一些问题。


过去完成时:
After the storm had subsided, the island emerged from the mist. = 暴风雨过后，岛屿从雾中显现出来。
It had emerged that the company was in serious financial trouble before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，该公司已经陷入严重的财务困境。


将来时:
New evidence will emerge during the trial. = 在审判期间将出现新的证据。
I believe the truth will emerge in time. = 我相信真相最终会水落石出。


虚拟语气:
If the truth emerged, many people would be shocked. = 如果真相大白，许多人都会感到震惊。
I wish the solution would emerge soon. = 我希望解决方案能尽快出现。


被动语态:
The problem was first emerged in the early stages of the project. = 这个问题在项目初期就出现了。
The details of the accident are yet to be emerged. = 事故的细节尚未披露。


疑问句:
When will the truth emerge? = 真相何时会水落石出？
What new challenges might emerge? = 可能出现哪些新的挑战？


否定句:
The results did not emerge as expected. = 结果与预期不符。
No new evidence has emerged in the case. = 此案中没有出现新的证据。


PHRASE->
emerge victorious = 赢得胜利
emerge from = 从...中出现
emerge unscathed = 安然无恙
emerge into = 进入
emerge as = 成为
