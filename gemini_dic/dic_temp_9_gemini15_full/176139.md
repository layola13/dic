razzia:/ˈræziə/| n.|突袭；抢劫；搜捕|A sudden raid, typically by soldiers or police, to seize people or property.  The word often implies a swift and forceful action, often characterized by violence or intimidation. |例句：The police conducted a razzia on the suspected drug dealer's house.  警方对涉嫌贩毒者的住所进行了突袭。

例句：The rebels launched a surprise razzia against the enemy's supply lines. 反叛者对敌人的补给线发起了突然袭击。

近义词：raid, incursion, foray, attack, swoop

反义词：withdrawal, retreat
