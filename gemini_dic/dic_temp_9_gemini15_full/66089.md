excavations:/ˌɛkskəˈveɪʃənz/ | n. |挖掘；发掘；出土文物 | The process of digging up and removing earth or other material to reveal something hidden beneath the surface, often for archaeological or construction purposes.  The results of such digging, often including artifacts and structures. |例句：The archaeologists conducted extensive excavations at the ancient Roman site. 考古学家在古罗马遗址进行了广泛的发掘。

例句：The museum's collection includes many fascinating artifacts from recent excavations. 博物馆的藏品包括许多来自最近发掘的迷人文物。


近义词：digs, explorations, unearthings | 反义词：constructions, buildings, erections | 词性: 名词
