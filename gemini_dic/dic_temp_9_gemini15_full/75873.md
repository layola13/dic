functioned:/ˈfʌŋkʃənd/| v.|运作；起作用；行使职能|To perform or operate in a proper or expected manner.  It describes something fulfilling its intended purpose or role.|The machine functioned perfectly.|近义词：operated, worked, performed|反义词：malfunctioned, failed
