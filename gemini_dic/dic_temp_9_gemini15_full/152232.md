patrollers:/pəˈtroʊlər/ | n. |巡逻者，巡查员 |People or groups of people who patrol an area to maintain order or security.  Patrollers are responsible for monitoring a specific region, often on foot or in vehicles, to ensure safety and prevent criminal activity. |例句：The park patrollers are responsible for keeping the park safe and clean. 公园巡逻员负责维护公园的安全和清洁。


The coastal patrollers spotted a suspicious vessel.  海岸巡逻人员发现了一艘可疑船只。

近义词：guards, watchmen, inspectors, wardens |反义词：None directly applicable;  context-dependent antonyms might include criminals or vandals.

词性：名词
