renovator:/ˈrɛnəveɪtər/| n. |翻新者；修缮者；整修者 |A renovator is a person or company that renovates buildings or other structures.  They restore, modernize, or improve existing properties. |Example: The experienced renovator transformed the dilapidated house into a stunning modern home.  经验丰富的翻新者将破旧的房子改造成了一座令人惊叹的现代住宅。|近义词：remodeler, refurbisher, restorer |反义词：demolisher, destroyer |名词
