non-Roman:/ˌnɒnˈroʊmən/ | adj. |非罗马的；非罗马字母的|Not relating to or characteristic of ancient Rome or its people, language, or culture.  It can also refer to writing systems other than the Roman alphabet.|例句：The inscription was in a non-Roman script.  这铭文是用非罗马字母书写的。|例句：Many cultures around the world use non-Roman writing systems. 世界各地许多文化使用非罗马文字系统。|近义词：alien, foreign, non-Latin|反义词：Roman, Latin
