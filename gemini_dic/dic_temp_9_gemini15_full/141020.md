ongoing: /ˈɒŋɡəʊɪŋ/ | /ˈɑːŋɡoʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 正在进行的；持续的 | Continuing to exist or develop.  |例句：The investigation is ongoing.  调查正在进行中。

例句：There is ongoing debate about the best way to address climate change. 关于解决气候变化的最佳方法，目前仍在进行辩论。

近义词：continuing, current, in progress, underway

反义词：completed, finished, concluded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is ongoing. = 项目正在进行中。
The research is ongoing. = 研究正在进行中。

一般过去时:
The repairs were ongoing when the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临时，维修工作正在进行中。
The negotiations were ongoing throughout the night. = 谈判持续了一整夜。

现在完成时:
The renovations have been ongoing for months. = 装修已经持续了好几个月了。
The discussions have been ongoing since last week. = 讨论从上周就一直持续到现在。

过去完成时:
The project had been ongoing for years before it was finally completed. = 该项目在最终完成之前已经进行了多年。
The argument had been ongoing for some time before they finally reached a compromise. = 他们最终达成妥协之前，争论已经持续了一段时间。

将来时:
The construction will be ongoing for another year. = 建设工程还将持续一年。
The talks will be ongoing until a solution is found. = 直到找到解决方案，谈判才会结束。

虚拟语气:
If the negotiations were ongoing, we would have more time to reach an agreement. = 如果谈判还在进行中，我们将有更多时间达成协议。
I wish the repairs were ongoing so that the problem could be fixed quickly. = 我希望维修工作正在进行中，以便能够快速解决问题。

被动语态:
The matter is being ongoingly reviewed. = 这个问题正在持续审查中。(Although less common to use "ongoingly")
The situation is being actively addressed, as the investigation is ongoing. = 由于调查正在进行中，正在积极处理这种情况。


疑问句:
Is the project still ongoing? = 项目还在进行吗？
Are the negotiations still ongoing? = 谈判还在进行吗？

否定句:
The project is not ongoing. = 项目已经停止了。
The meeting is not ongoing. = 会议已经结束了。


PHRASE->
ongoing process = 持续的过程
ongoing debate = 持续的辩论
ongoing investigation = 持续的调查
ongoing support = 持续的支持
ongoing concern = 持续的担忧
