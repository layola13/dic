considerer:/kənˈsɪdərər/| n. |考虑者，细想者 |A considerer is someone who thinks carefully about something before making a decision.  They weigh the pros and cons and contemplate various options.| Example:  The committee appointed a skilled considerer to review the proposals.  委员会任命了一位熟练的考虑者来审查这些提案。|近义词：thinker, ponderer, deliberator |反义词：impulsive person, rash person
