dramas:/ˈdrɑːməz/| n. |戏剧；戏剧性事件；一系列不幸事件 |Plays intended for the stage; a genre of literature;  a series of exciting, unpleasant, or unexpected events. |例句：Shakespeare wrote many famous dramas.  莎士比亚创作了许多著名的戏剧。
|例句：The family went through a series of dramas after the accident. 事故后，这个家庭经历了一系列的戏剧性事件。|近义词：plays, tragedies, comedies, melodramas |反义词：comedy (in the sense of lighthearted entertainment)
