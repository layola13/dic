missteps:/ˈmɪsˌstɛps/ | n. |失误；错误的步骤 | A misstep is a wrong step or a mistake.  It refers to a single instance of making a wrong move, physically or metaphorically.  |例句：His misstep in handling the negotiations cost the company millions.  他的谈判失误给公司造成了数百万美元的损失。

例句：The dancer recovered gracefully from her misstep.  舞者优雅地从她的失误中恢复过来。


近义词：mistake, blunder, error, slip, faux pas | 反义词：success, triumph, accomplishment | 词性：名词
