pecan:/pɪˈkæn/| n. |美洲山核桃|A pecan is a sweet edible nut from the pecan tree,  a species of hickory native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.  It has a rich, buttery flavor and is often used in desserts and confections. |例句：The pie was made with a pecan crust and a creamy caramel filling.  这馅饼是用山核桃皮和奶油焦糖馅做的。|例句：He cracked open the pecan shell to reveal the delicious nut inside. 他敲开了山核桃壳，露出了里面美味的果仁。|近义词：hickory nut, walnut|反义词：无
