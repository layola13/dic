frau:/fraʊ/| n.|夫人，太太，女子|A woman, especially a married woman.  The word often implies a sense of sophistication, elegance, or authority, sometimes with a slightly negative connotation of being manipulative or scheming.  It's primarily used in German, but its usage has spread to other languages.|例句：The frau of the house greeted us warmly.  这家夫人的热情地迎接了我们。|例句：She was a formidable frau, known for her sharp wit and business acumen. 她是一位令人敬畏的夫人，以其敏锐的才智和商业头脑而闻名。|近义词：woman, wife, lady |反义词：man, gentleman
