fall-in-with:/ˌfɔːlˈɪn wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 偶然遇到；同意；赞成；与…相处融洽 | To meet someone unexpectedly or by chance; to agree with someone or something; to become friendly with someone; to conform to or comply with something. |

例句：

I fell in with an old friend at the market. = 我在市场上偶然遇到了一个老朋友。

We fell in with their plans for the weekend. = 我们同意了他们周末的计划。


近义词：encounter, agree with, concur with, get along with, comply with

反义词：disagree with, oppose, conflict with
