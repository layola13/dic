micrograph:/ˈmaɪkrəɡræf/| n. |显微照片；显微图像 |A micrograph is a photograph or image taken through a microscope, showing a greatly magnified view of a small object or structure.  It's commonly used in scientific fields to visualize microscopic details. |Example: The micrograph clearly showed the intricate structure of the cell wall.  显微照片清晰地显示了细胞壁的复杂结构。 The electron micrograph revealed the virus's unique morphology. 电子显微照片揭示了病毒独特的形态。|近义词：microphotograph |反义词：macrophotograph
