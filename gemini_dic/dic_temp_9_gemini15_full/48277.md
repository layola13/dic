decouples:/ˈdiːkʌpəlz/ | vt. | 解耦合；使分离 | To decouple something means to separate it from something else, often to make it independent or more flexible.  This term is frequently used in engineering, software design, and economics. | Example: The new design decouples the power supply from the main circuit board, improving reliability. 新的设计将电源与主电路板分离，提高了可靠性。


The modular design decouples the different components, making maintenance easier. 模块化设计将不同的组件分离，使维护更容易。| 近义词：separate, disconnect, disengage, isolate | 反义词：couple, connect, integrate, link | 动词
