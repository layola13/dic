quailed:/kweɪld/| vi.|畏缩；胆怯；丧失勇气|To lose courage in the face of danger or difficulty; to cower.  The verb often implies a sudden loss of courage, a shrinking back from something frightening or challenging.|例句：He quailed at the sight of the snake. 他看到蛇后畏缩了。|例句：Facing the fierce storm, the small boat quailed. 面对猛烈的风暴，小船畏缩了。|近义词：cower, flinch, shrink, recoil|反义词：brave, bold, courageous|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quails at the thought of public speaking. = 他一想到公开演讲就胆怯。
She often quails before challenges. = 她经常在挑战面前畏缩。


一般过去时:
The soldiers quailed before the enemy's advance. = 士兵们在敌人进攻面前胆怯了。
He quailed at the harsh criticism. = 他对严厉的批评感到畏缩。


现在完成时:
She has never quailed in the face of adversity. = 她从未在逆境面前胆怯过。
He has quailed before responsibility in the past. = 他过去在责任面前畏缩过。


过去完成时:
The knight had quailed before the dragon's fiery breath. = 骑士曾在巨龙的烈焰面前胆怯过。
They had quailed at the news of the impending war. = 他们听到即将到来的战争的消息后感到畏缩。


将来时:
I fear he will quail at the sight of blood. = 我担心他看到血会害怕。
They will likely quail under pressure. = 他们很可能会在压力下畏缩。


虚拟语气:
If he had not quailed, he might have succeeded. = 如果他没有胆怯，他也许会成功。
I wish I hadn't quailed at the interview. = 我真希望我在面试时没有畏缩。


被动语态:
(The verb "quail" is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a state of being rather than an action performed on someone.)


疑问句:
Did he quail at the task ahead? = 他对眼前的任务感到畏缩了吗？
Will she quail in the face of opposition? = 她会在反对面前畏缩吗？


否定句:
He did not quail in the face of danger. = 他面对危险没有胆怯。
She never quails from a challenge. = 她从来不会回避挑战。


PHRASE->
quail before = 在...面前畏缩

to quail and shrink = 畏缩和退缩
quail in one's boots = 吓得浑身发抖
