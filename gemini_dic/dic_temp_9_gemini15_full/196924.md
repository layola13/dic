shadowing: /ˈʃædoʊɪŋ/ | n. v. | 跟踪；跟随；模仿；影子；阴影 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "shadowing" refers to the act of following someone closely, often secretly.  It can also refer to a shadowy or darkened area, or a representation of something else (e.g., a shadowing of doubt).  As a verb, "shadowing" means to follow someone closely, to cast a shadow on something, or to imitate someone's speech or actions.

例句:
The detective spent days shadowing the suspect.  = 侦探花了几天时间跟踪嫌疑犯。

The tall buildings cast long shadows across the street. = 高楼大厦在街上投下长长的阴影。

The student improved his English skills through shadowing native speakers. = 学生通过模仿母语人士的口语来提高英语技能。


近义词:
Noun:  following, tracking, trailing, surveillance, shade, gloom.
Verb: following, tracking, imitating, mimicking, mirroring.


反义词:
Noun:  illumination, brightness, clarity.
Verb:  leading, guiding.


词性:
Noun and Verb
