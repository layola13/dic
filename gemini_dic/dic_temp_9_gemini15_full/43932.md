crewmanship:/ˈkruːmənʃɪp/| n. |船员技艺；船员合作；全体船员的工作能力|The skill and teamwork of a ship's crew; the collective ability of a crew.|例句：The captain praised the crewmanship displayed during the storm. 船长称赞船员在暴风雨中展现出的娴熟技艺和团队合作精神。  例句：Their excellent crewmanship ensured the safe arrival of the cargo.  他们优秀的船员技艺确保了货物的安全到达。|近义词：teamwork, cooperation, skill|反义词：incompetence, disorganization
