It is typically shaped like a four-leaf clover.|例句：The cloverleaf interchange caused significant traffic congestion during rush hour.  苜蓿叶形立交桥在高峰时段造成了严重的交通拥堵。 The driver took the exit ramp at the cloverleaf. 司机在苜蓿叶形立交桥处下了出口匝道。|近义词：interchange, highway interchange|反义词：none
