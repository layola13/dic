creeped:/kriːpt/| v.|潜行；缓慢移动；逐渐蔓延|To move slowly and quietly, often in a stealthy or sneaky way; to gradually spread or increase.|例句：The cat creeped silently through the grass. 猫悄悄地穿过草地。

The feeling of unease creeped into my heart. 不安的感觉逐渐涌上我的心头。|近义词：crawled, slithered, inched|反义词：dashed, rushed, sprinted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He creeps around the house at night. 他晚上在房子周围偷偷摸摸地走动。
The ivy creeps up the walls. 常春藤爬上了墙壁。


一般过去时:
The thief creeped into the room. 小偷潜入了房间。
The cold slowly creeped into the house. 寒气慢慢地渗入了屋子。


现在完成时:
The plant has creeped across the entire garden. 植物已经蔓延到整个花园。
Fear has creeped into her heart. 恐惧已经爬上了她的心头。


过去完成时:
The feeling of dread had creeped over me before the exam.  考试前我已经感到害怕。
The vine had creeped up the entire wall before we noticed it.  在我们注意到之前，藤蔓已经爬满了整面墙。


将来时:
The darkness will creep in as the sun sets.  太阳下山后，黑暗将会降临。
The rumors will creep through the town. 流言蜚语将会在小镇上蔓延。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't so tired, I wouldn't have creeped into bed so early. 如果我不那么累，我就不会这么早就爬上床睡觉了。
If he had creeped more quietly, he wouldn't have been caught. 如果他更安静地潜行，就不会被抓住了。


被动语态:
The house was creeped into by a burglar.  房子被窃贼潜入了。
The garden is being creeped over by weeds. 花园正被杂草覆盖。


疑问句:
Did the mouse creep into the cupboard? 老鼠是不是偷偷溜进了橱柜？
Does that feeling of anxiety ever creep into your mind? 你是否曾经感到焦虑？


否定句:
He didn't creep into the room; he walked in openly. 他没有偷偷溜进房间，而是大大方方地走进去。
The cold didn't creep into the house because the windows were closed tightly.  因为窗户关得很紧，所以寒气没有渗入屋子。


PHRASE->
creep up on someone =  悄悄接近某人
creep into something = 潜入某物；逐渐产生
creep out = 令人毛骨悚然
creep along =  缓慢移动
creep away = 偷偷溜走
