overtrimmed:/ˌoʊvərˈtrɪmd/ | adj. | 修剪过度的；过于修饰的 | Excessively trimmed; too much has been removed or cut away.  The hedges were overtrimmed, looking unnatural and stark.  | 近义词：overcut, excessively pruned | 反义词：untrimmed, unkempt
