redbud:/ˈrɛdbʌd/| n.|紫荆花树|A redbud is a small tree or large shrub belonging to the genus Cercis, known for its early spring blossoms of pink, red, or purple.  These blossoms appear directly on the branches before the leaves emerge.|例句：The redbud tree in our yard is blooming beautifully.  我们院子里的紫荆花树开得真漂亮。
例句：We planted a Eastern redbud near the fence. 我们在栅栏附近种了一棵美国紫荆。|近义词：Judas tree (a type of redbud)|反义词:  (no direct antonym,  but contrasting trees could be oak, maple etc.)
