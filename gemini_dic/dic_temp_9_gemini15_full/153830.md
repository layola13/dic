percentages:/pərˈsɛntɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 百分比，百分率 | Percentages are used to express proportions. 百分比用来表示比例。  | The percentage of students who passed the exam was 90%.  考试及格的学生比例为90%。 | ratios, proportions |  fractions (in some contexts) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The percentage of unemployed people is increasing. = 失业人口的百分比正在上升。
What percentage of the budget is allocated to education? = 预算的多少百分比分配给了教育？


一般过去时:
The percentage of smokers decreased last year. = 去年吸烟者的百分比下降了。
What percentage of the votes did the candidate receive? = 候选人获得了多少百分比的选票？


现在完成时:
The percentage of women in the workforce has risen significantly. = 职场女性的比例显著上升。
What percentage of the project has been completed so far? = 到目前为止，项目完成了多少百分比？


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the percentage of defective products had fallen. = 到年底，次品率已经下降。
The percentage of shares they owned had decreased before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们拥有的股份比例已经下降。


将来时:
The percentage of electric vehicles on the road will increase in the coming years. = 未来几年，道路上电动汽车的比例将会增加。
What percentage of the population will be over 65 in 2050? = 到2050年，人口中65岁以上的人口比例将是多少？


虚拟语气:
If the percentage of errors were lower, the results would be more reliable. = 如果错误率更低，结果会更可靠。
I wish the percentage of absenteeism were lower in this class. = 我希望这个班的缺勤率更低。


被动语态:
The percentage of profit is calculated annually. = 利润百分比每年计算一次。
The percentage of market share was significantly impacted by the new competitor. = 新竞争对手对市场份额百分比产生了重大影响。


疑问句:
What percentage of the population speaks English? = 百分之多少的人口说英语？
What is the percentage increase in sales this quarter? = 本季度销售额增长了百分之几？


否定句:
The percentage of participation wasn't high enough. = 参与比例不够高。
The percentage of successful applicants was not what we expected. = 成功申请人的比例并非我们预期的那样。


PHRASE->
percentage point = 百分点
percentage change = 百分比变化
percentage yield = 百分比产量
high percentage = 高比例
low percentage = 低比例
