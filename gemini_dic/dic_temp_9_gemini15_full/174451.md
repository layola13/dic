quinch: /kwɪntʃ/ | v. | 熄灭（火焰）；压制（感情）；痛饮；猛喝 | to extinguish (a flame); to suppress (emotion); to drink deeply; to gulp |  例句：He quenched the fire with a bucket of water. 他用一桶水灭了火。 He tried to quench his thirst with a glass of water. 他试图用一杯水解渴。 | 近义词：extinguish, suppress, slake, gulp | 反义词：ignite, inflame, encourage
