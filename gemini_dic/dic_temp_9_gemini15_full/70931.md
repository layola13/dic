firebricks:/ˈfaɪərˌbrɪks/| n.|耐火砖|Firebricks are bricks that are resistant to high temperatures and are used in fireplaces, furnaces, and kilns.  They are typically made from refractory materials such as clay, silica, or alumina.|例句：The fireplace was lined with firebricks to withstand the intense heat.壁炉内衬有耐火砖以承受高温。|例句：The kiln was rebuilt using high-quality firebricks.窑炉是用高质量的耐火砖重建的。|近义词：refractory bricks, heat-resistant bricks|反义词：None specifically, but bricks made of materials that are not heat-resistant could be considered antonyms in context.|名词
