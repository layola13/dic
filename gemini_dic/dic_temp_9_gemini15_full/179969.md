refighting:/ˌriːˈfaɪtɪŋ/| v. | 重战，再战 | The act of fighting again; renewing a fight or battle.  It can refer to a literal physical fight or a metaphorical struggle. | 例句：The refighting of the Civil War in popular culture continues to fascinate.  =  大众文化中对内战的重温继续吸引着人们。

The refighting of old grievances led to further conflict. =  旧怨的重燃导致了进一步的冲突。

近义词：re-engagement, renewed fighting, restarting the battle

反义词：ceasefire, truce, peace
