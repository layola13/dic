conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/| v.|符合，遵照，适应|To comply with rules, standards, or laws; to behave according to accepted norms.  |例句：He refused to conform to the company's dress code. 他拒绝遵守公司的着装规范。|例句：She conformed to the pressure from her peers and agreed to the plan. 她屈服于同龄人的压力，同意了这个计划。|近义词：comply, abide by, adhere to|反义词：rebel, defy, deviate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conforms to the rules. = 他遵守规则。
They conform to social expectations. = 他们符合社会期望。

一般过去时:
She conformed to the tradition. = 她遵循传统。
He conformed to his parents' wishes. = 他顺从了父母的意愿。

现在完成时:
We have conformed to the new regulations. = 我们已经遵守了新的规章制度。
They have conformed to the majority opinion. = 他们已经遵从了大多数人的意见。

过去完成时:
He had conformed to the pressure before he finally agreed. = 在他最终同意之前，他已经屈服于压力了。
She had conformed to societal norms all her life. = 她一生都遵循社会规范。

将来时:
They will conform to the new policy. = 他们将遵守新的政策。
He will conform to the company's standards. = 他将符合公司的标准。

虚拟语气:
If he conformed to the rules, he wouldn't be in trouble. = 如果他遵守规则，他就不会有麻烦。
I wish he would conform to the dress code. = 我希望他能遵守着装规范。

被动语态:
The design must conform to safety standards. = 该设计必须符合安全标准。(被动)
His behavior was expected to conform to company policy. = 预计他的行为将符合公司政策。(被动)

疑问句:
Does he conform to the rules? = 他遵守规则吗？
Will they conform to the new regulations? = 他们会遵守新的规章制度吗？

否定句:
He does not conform to the rules. = 他不遵守规则。
She did not conform to societal expectations. = 她不符合社会期望。


PHRASE->
conform to = 符合，遵照
conform with = 与…一致
in conformity with = 按照，根据
out of conformity with = 不符合
to conform oneself to = 使自己适应
