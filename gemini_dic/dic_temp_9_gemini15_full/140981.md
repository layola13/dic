Oneiromancy is an ancient form of divination. |Example:  She practiced oneiromancy, believing her dreams held clues to her destiny. = 她练习解梦术，相信她的梦境蕴含着关于她命运的线索。|近义词：dream interpretation, divination,  dream analysis|反义词：N/A
