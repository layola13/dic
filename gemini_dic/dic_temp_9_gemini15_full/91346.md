hydropneumatic:/ˌhaɪdroʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 水气动的；液压气动的 | Relating to or denoting a system or device that uses both water and compressed air to operate.  This typically involves using compressed air to pressurize a water system, leveraging the incompressibility of water for power transmission. | The car's suspension system is hydropneumatic, providing a remarkably smooth ride. 这辆车的悬挂系统是水气动的，提供了非常平稳的乘坐体验。| pneumatic, hydraulic |  mechanically operated  | adj.
