fourfold:/ˈfɔːrfəʊld/|/ˈfɔːrfəʊld/| adj. |四倍的；四重的；四方面的|Having four parts or elements; four times as great or many.|The company's profits showed a fourfold increase. 公司的利润增长了四倍。|近义词：quadruple, four times as much|反义词：single, onefold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plan is fourfold in its approach. = 这个计划在方法上是四方面的。
This year's profits are fourfold what they were last year. = 今年的利润是去年的四倍。


一般过去时:
The population increased fourfold during that decade. = 在那十年间，人口增长了四倍。
His efforts resulted in a fourfold improvement in the product's quality. = 他的努力使产品的质量提高了四倍。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
By the end of the project, costs had increased fourfold. = 到项目结束时，成本已经增加了四倍。

将来时:
The company projects that sales will increase fourfold next year. = 公司预计明年销售额将增长四倍。
We anticipate the number of participants will increase fourfold in the next conference. = 我们预计下一次会议的参与者人数将增加四倍。


虚拟语气:
If the project were successful, the profits would be fourfold. = 如果项目成功，利润将是四倍。
I wish my investment had increased fourfold. = 我希望我的投资已经增长了四倍。


被动语态:
The problem was described as fourfold in nature. = 这个问题被描述为具有四方面的性质。
The effect of the new policy was seen to be fourfold. = 新政策的影响被认为是四方面的。


疑问句:

否定句:
The impact was not fourfold, but only twofold. = 影响不是四倍，而是两倍。
