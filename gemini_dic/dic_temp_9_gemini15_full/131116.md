nonbiologically:/ˌnɒnˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. |非生物地；非生物学地 |  Not relating to or involving biology or living organisms.  It describes something that occurs or exists outside the realm of living things or biological processes. | Example: The process of crystal formation is nonbiologically driven.  这个晶体形成的过程是由非生物驱动的。 | 近义词：abiotically, inorganicly | 反义词：biologically
