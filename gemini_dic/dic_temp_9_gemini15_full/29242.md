casseroled:/kæsəˈroʊld/| v.|炖(在砂锅里)|To cook (food) in a casserole dish.|例句：She casseroled the chicken and vegetables for a hearty dinner. 她把鸡肉和蔬菜放在砂锅里炖了一顿丰盛的晚餐。|近义词：stew, braise|反义词：fry, grill
