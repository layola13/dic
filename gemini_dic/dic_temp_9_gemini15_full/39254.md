congregationalism:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪʃənəlɪzəm/|/ˌkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |公理会制；公理会主义|A system of church government in which each local church is independent and self-governing.  Congregationalism emphasizes the autonomy of the local congregation and the authority of the Bible. |Many Protestant denominations, such as the Congregational Church, practice congregationalism.|近义词：Independent church governance, self-governance|反义词：Episcopacy, Presbyterianism
