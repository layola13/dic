expounding:/ɪkˈspaʊndɪŋ/| v.|详细解释，阐述|To explain or discuss something in detail.  |例句：The professor spent an hour expounding on the theory of relativity. 教授花了一个小时详细解释相对论。|例句：She expounded her views on the matter at length. 她详细阐述了她对这件事的看法。|近义词：explaining, elaborating, elucidating, clarifying|反义词：summarizing, abbreviating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expounds his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地阐述了他的想法。
She expounds on the benefits of exercise regularly. = 她定期阐述运动的好处。

一般过去时:
The speaker expounded the principles of democracy. = 演讲者阐述了民主的原则。
He expounded his theory in a recent publication. = 他在最近的一篇出版物中阐述了他的理论。

现在完成时:
She has expounded this topic in several lectures. = 她已经在几场讲座中阐述了这个主题。
They have expounded their position to the committee. = 他们已经向委员会阐述了他们的立场。

过去完成时:
He had expounded the same argument many times before. = 他之前已经多次阐述过同样的论点。
The author had expounded the historical context in the introduction. = 作者在引言中阐述了历史背景。

将来时:
The professor will expound on the latest research findings. = 教授将阐述最新的研究结果。
She will expound her vision for the company at the meeting. = 她将在会议上阐述她对公司的愿景。

虚拟语气:
If he expounded his ideas more clearly, people would understand him better. = 如果他更清晰地阐述他的想法，人们就会更好地理解他。
I wish he had expounded on that point further. = 我希望他进一步阐述那一点。

被动语态:
His ideas were expounded in detail in the book. = 他的想法在书中得到了详细的阐述。
The theory is expounded in a series of lectures. = 该理论在一系列讲座中得到阐述。

疑问句:
How will he expound his arguments? = 他将如何阐述他的论点？
Will she expound on the challenges facing the company? = 她会阐述公司面临的挑战吗？

否定句:
He didn't expound on the details. = 他没有详细阐述细节。
She didn't expound her ideas effectively. = 她没有有效地阐述她的想法。
