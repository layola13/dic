chalk:/tʃɔːk/| n.|粉笔|A soft, white, or colored limestone, used to write or draw on a blackboard or similar surface.  |The teacher wrote the equation on the chalkboard with chalk.|近义词：crayon, limestone|反义词：None

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The artist uses chalk to create beautiful drawings. =艺术家用粉笔创作美丽的图画。
We often use chalk on the blackboard in class. = 我们经常在课堂上用粉笔在黑板上写字。

一般过去时:
She chalked the score on the board. = 她用粉笔在板上记下了分数。
He chalked out a plan for the project. = 他草拟了一个项目计划。
