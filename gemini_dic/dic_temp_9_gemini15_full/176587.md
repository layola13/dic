reacceding:/ˌriːˈæksɛdɪŋ/| v.|重新加入，重新取得|The act of regaining or resuming access to something previously lost or relinquished.  It implies a return to a previous state or position. |例句：After a period of absence, she is reacceding to her position on the board.  在一段时间的缺席后，她重新担任董事会成员。

例句：The country is reacceding to the international trade agreement. 这个国家正在重新加入国际贸易协定。


近义词：rejoining, resuming, regaining, recovering


反义词：leaving, abandoning, relinquishing, forfeiting


词性:动词
