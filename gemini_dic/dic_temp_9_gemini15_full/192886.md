seasonings:/ˈsiːzənɪŋz/| n.|调味料，佐料|Substances used to add flavor to food, such as herbs, spices, salt, and pepper.| Example: The chef added a blend of seasonings to enhance the dish's flavor.  厨师加入多种调味料来增强菜肴的风味。|近义词：spices, herbs, condiments|反义词：blandness
