preschool:/ˈpriːskuːl/ | n. |学前班；幼儿园；学龄前儿童 |A preschool is an educational institution or program for children before they enter kindergarten or elementary school.  It typically provides activities designed to prepare children for formal schooling. | My daughter attends a preschool near our home.  我的女儿在我们家附近的一家幼儿园上学。| kindergarten, nursery school | school (for older children) |名词
