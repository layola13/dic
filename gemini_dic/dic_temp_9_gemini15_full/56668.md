doodah:/ˈduːdɑː/| n.|小玩意儿，小东西，无名小卒|A doodah is a thingamajig, a gizmo, or any small object whose name you don't know or can't remember. It often implies something insignificant or unimportant.  |例句：I've got a whole box full of doodahs in the garage.  我车库里有一整箱子的小玩意儿。

例句：He's just a doodah, not someone to be taken seriously. 他只不过是个无名小卒，不值得认真对待。|近义词：thingamajig, gizmo, gadget, whatchamacallit |反义词：important item, significant object
