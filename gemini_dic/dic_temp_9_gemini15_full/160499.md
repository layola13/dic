porcupine:/ˈpɔːrkjupaɪn/| n. |豪猪|A rodent covered in quills.  Porcupines are known for their defensive mechanism of raising their quills when threatened. They are primarily herbivores, feeding on plants, bark, and roots.|The porcupine scurried up the tree to escape the predator.  这只豪猪窜上树去躲避捕食者。|hedgehog, quill pig| (no direct antonym, perhaps "smooth-skinned rodent")|名词
