excretory:/ɪkˈskriːtəri/ | adj. | 排泄的；分泌的 | Relating to or concerned with excretion.  The excretory system removes waste products from the body. | The kidneys are part of the excretory system.  肾脏是排泄系统的一部分。 The excretory process is vital for maintaining homeostasis. 排泄过程对于维持体内平衡至关重要。| secretory, eliminatory | retentive
