promuscis:/proˈmʌskɪs/| n. |长鼻；喙；象鼻 |A long, flexible snout, especially that of an insect or other invertebrate.  It often refers to a proboscis used for feeding or sensory perception.  |Example: The butterfly used its delicate proboscis to sip nectar from the flower. 蝴蝶用它细长的喙吸食花蜜。|近义词：snout, trunk, rostrum |反义词：None specifically, but words implying a lack of such an appendage could be considered antonyms in context.  |名词
