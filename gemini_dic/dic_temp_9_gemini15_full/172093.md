purportedly:/pərˈpɔːrɪdli/|/ˈpɜːrpɔːtɪdli/| adv. |据称；据说；声称地 |The man purportedly stole the painting.  据称此人偷了这幅画。 |The company's profits were purportedly higher than reported.  该公司声称其利润高于报告中的数字。| allegedly, reportedly |actually, truly |副词

USAGE->

He purportedly owns a yacht. = 他据说拥有一艘游艇。
They purportedly plan to retire next year. = 他们据说计划明年退休。
