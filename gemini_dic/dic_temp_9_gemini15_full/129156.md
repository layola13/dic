no-no's:/ˈnoʊnoʊz/| n. |禁忌，不允许的事|Things that are forbidden or unacceptable.  The plural form of "no-no."|例句：Smoking is a no-no in this building.  在这个建筑物里吸烟是禁忌。
例句：Using your phone during the meeting is a definite no-no. 在会议期间使用手机绝对不行。|近义词：taboo, prohibition, forbidden act|反义词：permitted act, acceptable practice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That action is a no-no. = 那个行为是禁忌。
This behavior is a no-no in our company. = 这种行为在我们公司是禁忌。

一般过去时:
That was a no-no back then. = 那在当时是禁忌。
His actions were considered no-no's by many. = 他的行为被许多人认为是禁忌。

现在完成时:
That has been a no-no for years. = 那多年来一直是禁忌。
We have learned that this is a no-no. = 我们已经知道这是禁忌。

过去完成时:
It had been a no-no before the new rules were introduced. = 在新规章出台之前，它一直是禁忌。
They had already understood that it was a no-no. = 他们已经明白那是个禁忌。

将来时:
That will be a no-no in the future. = 那将来将会是禁忌。
Using this equipment will be a no-no after the update. = 更新后使用此设备将是禁忌。

虚拟语气:
If that were a no-no, I would have done something different. = 如果那是禁忌，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish that hadn't been a no-no. = 我希望那不是禁忌。

被动语态:
That was considered a no-no. = 那被认为是禁忌。
Those actions are being treated as no-no's by the authorities. = 这些行为正被当局视为禁忌。

疑问句:
Is that a no-no? = 那是禁忌吗？
Are those actions no-no's in this context? = 在这种情况下，那些行为是禁忌吗？

否定句:
That isn't a no-no. = 那不是禁忌。
Those actions weren't considered no-no's at the time. = 那些行为当时不被认为是禁忌。
