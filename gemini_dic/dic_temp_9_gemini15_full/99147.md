interchain:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪn/| n. |链间，互链| A system or protocol that allows communication and interaction between different blockchains.  It facilitates the transfer of assets, data, or information across separate blockchain networks.| Example:  The new interchain protocol allows for seamless token transfers between different blockchains.  新的链间协议允许在不同的区块链之间无缝转移代币。|近义词：cross-chain, blockchain bridge|反义词：intrachain
