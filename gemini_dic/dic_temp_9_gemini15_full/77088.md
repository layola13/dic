garrisoned:/ˈɡærɪsənzd/| v. |驻军于；配备守备部队|To provide (a place) with a garrison; to station troops in (a place) for defense.|The fort was garrisoned by a thousand soldiers.  这座堡垒驻扎着一千名士兵。|The city was heavily garrisoned during the war.  战争期间，这座城市驻扎了大量军队。|defend, occupy, fortify|evacuate, abandon|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army garrisons the border town. = 军队驻守边境小镇。
They garrison the fort every year. = 他们每年都在要塞驻军。

一般过去时:
The castle was garrisoned by Roman soldiers. = 这座城堡曾由罗马士兵驻守。
They garrisoned the island during the conflict. = 冲突期间，他们在岛上驻扎军队。

现在完成时:
The army has garrisoned the city since the revolution. = 自从革命以来，军队一直驻扎在这个城市。
They have garrisoned the base for many years. = 他们多年来一直在该基地驻军。

过去完成时:
The fort had been garrisoned for centuries before it fell. = 要塞在陷落之前已经驻守了几个世纪。
They had garrisoned the town for a decade before the enemy attacked. = 在敌人袭击之前，他们已经驻守该镇十年了。

将来时:
The government will garrison the newly built base next year. = 政府明年将在新建的基地驻军。
They will garrison the strategic point to prevent invasion. = 他们将在战略要地驻军以防止入侵。

虚拟语气:
If they garrisoned the city, the rebellion would be crushed. = 如果他们驻守这座城市，叛乱就会被镇压。
I wish they had garrisoned the border more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地驻守边境。

被动语态:
The town was garrisoned by experienced soldiers. = 该镇由经验丰富的士兵驻守。
The fort is being garrisoned as we speak. = 正如我们所说，要塞正在驻军。

疑问句:
Was the city garrisoned during the war? = 战争期间这座城市有驻军吗？

否定句:
The enemy did not garrison the town. = 敌人没有驻守该镇。
The island was not garrisoned adequately. = 该岛的驻军不足。
