nonhereditary: /ˌnɒn.hɪˈrɛdɪtəri/ | adj. | 非遗传的，不遗传的 | Not transmitted from parent to offspring.  It refers to traits or characteristics that are not passed down through genes. | 例句：The disease is nonhereditary, meaning it's not passed from parents to their children.  这种疾病是非遗传性的，这意味着它不会从父母传给他们的孩子。

The condition is nonhereditary; it's caused by environmental factors. 这种情况是非遗传性的；它是由环境因素引起的。| 近义词：acquired, environmental, nongenetic | 反义词：hereditary, genetic, inherited |
