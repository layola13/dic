codeveloping: /ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/ | v. | 共同开发，合作开发 |  The act of developing something together, often implying a collaborative effort between two or more parties. This term emphasizes the shared responsibility and contribution in the development process. | 例句：The two companies are codeveloping a new software platform. = 两家公司正在共同开发一个新的软件平台。

The research team is codeveloping a new treatment for cancer with a pharmaceutical company. = 研究团队与一家制药公司正在共同开发一种新的癌症治疗方法。


近义词：collaboratively developing, jointly developing, co-creating | 反义词：developing independently, solely developing | 词性:动词
