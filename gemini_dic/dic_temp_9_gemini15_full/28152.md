capivi:/kæˈpiːvi/| n. |卡皮瓦拉（一种大型啮齿动物）|Capybara, also known as chigüiro, is a large semi-aquatic rodent native to South America. It is the largest living rodent in the world.  |例句：The capybara is a remarkably social animal. 卡皮瓦拉是一种非常群居的动物。|例句：We saw a family of capybaras relaxing by the river. 我们看到一群卡皮瓦拉在河边休息。|近义词：rodent, semi-aquatic mammal |反义词：无
