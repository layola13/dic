jackhammer:/ˈdʒækˌhæmər/| n.| 冲击钻，碎石机 |A jackhammer is a pneumatic tool used for breaking up hard surfaces like concrete or asphalt. It uses compressed air to repeatedly strike a chisel-like bit. |例句：The workers used a jackhammer to break up the old pavement. 工人们用冲击钻凿碎旧路面。  The constant noise of the jackhammer was deafening. 冲击钻的持续噪音震耳欲聋。|近义词：pneumatic drill, demolition hammer|反义词：None
