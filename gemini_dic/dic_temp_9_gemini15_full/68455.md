familiarised:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzd/ | v. | 使熟悉；使了解 | To make someone or something familiar or well-known.  |  例句：The teacher familiarised the students with the new vocabulary.  老师让学生们熟悉新的词汇。|  例句：He familiarised himself with the company's policies before starting his new job. 他在开始新工作前熟悉了公司的政策。|近义词：acquaint, accustom, introduce | 反义词：estrange, alienate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They familiarise themselves with the new software. = 他们熟悉新的软件。
The company familiarises its employees with safety regulations. = 公司让员工熟悉安全条例。


一般过去时:
She familiarised herself with the city map before her trip. = 她旅行前熟悉了城市地图。
The trainer familiarised the athletes with the new training techniques. = 教练让运动员熟悉新的训练技术。


现在完成时:
I have familiarised myself with the project details. = 我已经熟悉了项目细节。
They have familiarised the public with the new product through advertising. = 他们通过广告让公众熟悉了新产品。


过去完成时:
He had familiarised himself with the research papers before writing his dissertation. = 他在撰写论文前已经熟悉了研究论文。
The team had familiarised themselves with the competitor's strategies. = 团队已经熟悉了竞争对手的策略。


将来时:
She will familiarise herself with the operating system before installing the software. = 她将在安装软件之前熟悉操作系统。
The company will familiarise its customers with the updated terms and conditions. = 公司将让客户熟悉更新后的条款和条件。


虚拟语气:
If I had familiarised myself with the instructions, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我熟悉了说明书，我就不会犯那个错误。
It would be helpful if you familiarised yourself with the company culture before the interview. = 如果你在面试前熟悉一下公司文化，将会很有帮助。


被动语态:
The students were familiarised with the historical context. = 学生们被告知了历史背景。
The new employees are being familiarised with the company procedures. = 新员工正在熟悉公司流程。


疑问句:
How did you familiarise yourself with the subject matter? = 你是如何熟悉这个主题的？
Have they familiarised the public with the risks involved? = 他们是否让公众熟悉了其中涉及的风险？


否定句:
He didn't familiarise himself with the safety procedures, which resulted in an accident. = 他没有熟悉安全程序，导致了事故。
They haven't familiarised the users with the updated software features. = 他们没有让用户熟悉更新的软件功能。
