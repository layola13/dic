plumps:/plʌmps/| v. n. adj.|使…丰满；鼓起；猛地放下；丰满的；圆鼓鼓的；|to become or make something fuller and rounder; to suddenly drop something.|例句：The rain plumped the soil. 雨水使土壤变得肥沃。The bird plumped down on the branch. 鸟儿猛地落在树枝上。She has plump cheeks. 她有丰满的脸颊。|近义词：swell, puff up, plump out, chubby, full |反义词：thin, skinny, lean, flat

USAGE->
