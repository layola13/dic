/nemz/| n.|越南春卷|A nem is a type of spring roll, typically from Vietnamese cuisine.  It's often made with rice paper wrappers filled with various ingredients, such as vermicelli noodles, pork, shrimp, vegetables, and herbs.  These ingredients are then wrapped and often served with a dipping sauce.|例句：I ordered some fresh spring rolls (nems) for lunch. 我午餐点了一些新鲜的越南春卷。|近义词：spring roll, fresh roll|反义词：无
