nondisparateness:/ˌnɒnˈdɪspærətɪs/| n. |非歧视性，无差别性 | The absence of disparateness;  lack of differentiation or distinction, particularly in a way that is unfair or discriminatory.  This term is often used in legal contexts, particularly regarding equal opportunity and fair treatment.  |Example: The company’s hiring practices were reviewed to ensure nondisparateness. 公司的招聘流程经过审查，以确保其没有歧视。|近义词：equality, impartiality, fairness, evenhandedness |反义词：discrimination, bias, prejudice, partiality
