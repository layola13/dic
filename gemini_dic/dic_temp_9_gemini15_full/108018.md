laste:/lɑːst/| adj. |最后的，最近的 |The last one. 最后一个。|近义词：final, ultimate, recent |反义词：first, initial, earliest|


英文解析：
The adjective "last" means coming after all others; final.  It can also refer to most recent in time or order.

例句：
This is the last chance you will get. 这是你最后的机会了。

The last time I saw him, he was very happy.  我上次见到他时，他非常高兴。


USAGE->

The last train leaves at midnight. = 末班车在午夜离开。
This is the last chapter of the book. = 这是这本书的最后一章。

一般过去时:
The last movie I saw was a comedy. = 我最后看的一部电影是喜剧片。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

I had already finished my last piece of work before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经完成了我的最后一件工作。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
