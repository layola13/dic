dirige:/dəˈriːʒ/| v.|指导，指挥，管理|To guide or direct; to manage or control.|The conductor directed the orchestra with a flourish. 指挥用华丽的动作指挥乐队。|guide, lead, conduct, manage|abandon, neglect, mismanage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He directs the company's marketing strategy. 他指导公司的营销策略。
She directs the choir with passion. 她充满激情地指挥合唱团。


一般过去时:
The general directed the troops into battle. 将军指挥军队投入战斗。
He directed the film with great skill. 他以精湛的技艺执导了这部电影。


现在完成时:
She has directed several successful films. 她执导过几部成功的电影。
He has directed the project since its inception. 自项目启动以来，他一直负责该项目。


过去完成时:
They had directed the resources to the most pressing needs. 他们已将资源导向最紧迫的需求。
Before the meeting, she had directed her team to prepare a presentation. 会议之前，她已指示她的团队准备一份演示文稿。


将来时:
He will direct the new play next season. 下个季度他将执导新剧。
She will direct the traffic flow during the event. 活动期间她将指挥交通流量。


虚拟语气:
If he directed the project, it would be more efficient. 如果他负责这个项目，效率会更高。
I wish he had directed the team more effectively. 我希望他更有效地指挥团队。


被动语态:
The orchestra was directed by a renowned conductor. 乐队由一位著名的指挥家指挥。
The company's marketing strategy is directed by the CEO. 公司的营销策略由首席执行官指导。


疑问句:
Who directed that award-winning movie? 谁执导了那部获奖电影？
Who is directing the project now? 现在谁负责这个项目？


否定句:
He did not direct the traffic properly. 他没有正确指挥交通。
She did not direct her energy towards the right goals. 她没有将精力投入到正确的目标上。
