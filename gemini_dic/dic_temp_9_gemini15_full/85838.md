hcf:/ˌeɪtʃˈsiːˈɛf/| n. |最高公因数|Highest Common Factor.  It is the largest positive integer that divides each of the integers without leaving a remainder.  For example, the HCF of 12 and 18 is 6. |The HCF of 12 and 18 is 6 because 6 is the largest number that divides both 12 and 18 without leaving a remainder.|近义词：greatest common divisor (GCD)|反义词：least common multiple (LCM)|名词
