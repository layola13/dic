resimmering:/ˌriːˈsɪmərɪŋ/| v.|再次炖煮；重新酝酿|The process of simmering something again, often to enhance flavor or texture.  It implies a previous simmering has already taken place.  |例句：After the initial simmer, she resimmered the sauce for another hour to deepen the flavor.  = 初次炖煮后，她又炖煮了酱汁一小时，以加深其风味。

例句：The chef resimmered the stew, adding more vegetables for a richer taste. = 厨师再次炖煮炖菜，加入更多蔬菜以获得更浓郁的味道。


近义词：reheating, simmering again, stewing again|反义词：boiling, frying, baking|词性：动词
