nonclient:/ˈnɑːnˌklaɪənt/ | adj. | 非客户的，非客户端的 | Not related to or part of a client or client application.  This term is often used in the context of computer operating systems and software, referring to areas or processes that are not directly controlled or managed by a client application. | Example: The nonclient area of a window includes the title bar and borders.  非客户区窗口包括标题栏和边框。

词源:

The word "nonclient" is a compound word formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "client."  The term emerged with the development of graphical user interfaces (GUIs) and the distinction between the parts of a window directly controlled by the application (client area) and those managed by the operating system (nonclient area).
