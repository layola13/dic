monopathy:/məˈnɒpəθi/| n. |单药疗法|The use of a single drug to treat a disease or condition.  It's often contrasted with polypharmacy (the use of multiple drugs).  Monotherapy may be chosen when a single agent is effective enough, or to minimize side effects.|Example:  The doctor prescribed monotherapy with a statin to lower the patient's cholesterol.  医生开了一种他汀类药物的单药疗法来降低病人的胆固醇。|近义词：single-drug therapy|反义词：polypharmacy
