crosswind:/ˈkrɒsˌwɪnd/|/ˈkrɔːsˌwɪnd/| n. |侧风，横风 |A wind blowing across the direction of travel.  It can affect the course or stability of a vehicle, aircraft, or vessel. |例句：The pilot had to correct for the strong crosswind during landing.  飞行员着陆时不得不修正强烈的侧风。

例句：The sailboat was slowed by the persistent crosswind. 帆船被持续的侧风减慢了速度。


近义词：side wind, lateral wind |反义词：headwind, tailwind |词性：名词
