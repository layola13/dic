fells:/felz/ | v. | 使倒下；砍伐；击倒；感觉；理解 | To cause to fall; to cut down (trees); to strike down; to perceive by touch or feel; to understand. | 例句：The wind felled the ancient oak. 风吹倒了古老的橡树。 The lumberjacks felled the trees to clear the land.  伐木工人砍伐树木以清理土地。 | 近义词：cut down, knock down, topple,  understand, comprehend | 反义词：erect, raise, build, misunderstand


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The wind fells the trees. = 风吹倒树木。
He fells the timber skillfully. = 他熟练地砍伐木材。


一般过去时:
The storm felled many trees. = 暴风雨吹倒了许多树木。
The axe felled the giant redwood. = 斧头砍倒了巨大的红杉树。


现在完成时:
The lumberjacks have felled all the trees in the forest. = 伐木工人已经砍倒了森林里所有的树木。
The heavy rain has felled many power lines. = 大雨已经吹倒了许多电线。


过去完成时:
The farmer had felled the old apple tree before the storm hit. = 农民在暴风雨来临之前砍倒了那棵老苹果树。
They had felled most of the trees by nightfall. = 他们到夜幕降临的时候已经砍倒了大部分树木。


将来时:
The workers will fell the remaining trees next week. = 工人们下周将砍伐剩余的树木。
The strong wind will likely fell more trees tonight. = 强风今晚可能会吹倒更多树木。


虚拟语气:
If the wind were stronger, it would fell more trees. = 如果风更大，它会吹倒更多的树木。
I wish the storm hadn't felled that beautiful oak. = 我真希望暴风雨没有吹倒那棵美丽的橡树。


被动语态:
Many trees were felled by the storm. = 许多树木被暴风雨吹倒了。
The old house was felled to make way for a new road. = 老房子被拆除，为新路让路。


疑问句:
Were the trees felled by the wind or by humans? = 树木是被风吹倒的还是被人砍倒的？
How many trees were felled last year? = 去年砍倒了多少棵树？


否定句:
The wind did not fell the trees. = 风没有吹倒树木。
They did not fell the tree because it was protected. = 他们没有砍倒那棵树，因为它受保护。


PHRASE->
fell a tree = 砍倒一棵树

fell short of = 达不到
fell in love = 爱上
fell upon = 袭击
