leis:/ˈleɪz/| n.|花环；花冠；项链|A lei is a garland or wreath, typically made of flowers, and worn around the neck.  It's often used in Hawaiian culture as a symbol of welcome, celebration, or respect.|例句：She wore a beautiful lei of orchids around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一条美丽的兰花花环。|例句：They presented him with a lei to show their appreciation. 他们送给他一条花环以示感谢。|近义词：garland, wreath, necklace|反义词：None specifically.
