nonrealism:/ˈnɒnˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. |非现实主义，反现实主义 |The rejection or denial of realism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy.  It encompasses various approaches that deviate from representing reality as it is perceived. Examples include surrealism, magical realism, and expressionism.  |Example:  The play's nonrealism was evident in its fantastical setting and characters. 这出戏的非现实主义体现在其奇幻的场景和人物中。|近义词：unrealism, fantasy, idealism |反义词：realism, naturalism
