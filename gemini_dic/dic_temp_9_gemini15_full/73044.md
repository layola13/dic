footings:/ˈfʊtɪŋz/ | n. |基础；立足点；根基；地位 |The foundation or base upon which something is built or established; a secure position or standing.  |例句：The house is built on solid footings. 这房子建在坚实的地基上。

例句：He needs to find his footing in his new job. 他需要在新工作中找到自己的立足点。


近义词：foundation, base, groundwork, basis, footing


反义词：instability, insecurity, weakness


词源:源自古英语fotung，由fot（脚）和-ung（名词后缀）构成，字面意思是“脚下的东西”，引申为基础或立足点。
