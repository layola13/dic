词源:
The word "co-operatively" is derived from the adjective "co-operative," which itself comes from the verb "co-operate."  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together," and "operate" comes from the Latin "operari," meaning "to work." Therefore, "co-operatively" literally means "working together."
