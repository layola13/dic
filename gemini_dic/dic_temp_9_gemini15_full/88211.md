hindbrains:/ˈhaɪndbreɪnz/ | n. |后脑; 延脑 |The hindbrain is the lower part of the brain, containing the cerebellum, pons, and medulla oblongata. It controls basic life functions such as breathing, heart rate, and sleep.  | Example: Damage to the hindbrain can have severe consequences, affecting vital functions.  后脑损伤可能导致严重后果，影响重要的生命功能。|近义词: rhombencephalon |反义词:forebrain, midbrain
