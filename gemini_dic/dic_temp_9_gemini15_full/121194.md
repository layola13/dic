misdoubtful: /ˌmɪsˈdaʊtfl/ | adj. | 怀疑的；不确定的 | Feeling or expressing doubt; uncertain.  | The misdoubtful expression on her face suggested she wasn't entirely convinced.  她脸上怀疑的表情表明她并不完全相信。 | doubtful, uncertain, suspicious | certain, sure, confident | 形容词


USAGE->
This word is quite rare and doesn't have common usage across different tenses.  The grammatical structures will be illustrated with the word "doubtful".


一般现在时:
He is doubtful about the success of the project. = 他对项目的成功表示怀疑。
They are doubtful whether they can finish on time. = 他们怀疑能否按时完成。


一般过去时:
She was doubtful of his intentions. = 她怀疑他的意图。
I was doubtful that he would arrive. = 我怀疑他会来。


现在完成时:
We have been doubtful about the weather forecast. = 我们一直对天气预报表示怀疑。
They have been doubtful of the company's claims. = 他们一直对公司的说法表示怀疑。


过去完成时:
He had been doubtful about his abilities before the competition. = 比赛前，他对自己的能力一直很怀疑。
She had been doubtful of his sincerity for years. = 多年来，她一直怀疑他的真诚。


将来时:
I will be doubtful of the outcome until I see the results. = 在看到结果之前，我都会对结果感到怀疑。
They will be doubtful about signing the contract without more information. = 没有更多信息，他们会犹豫是否签署合同。


虚拟语气:
If I were doubtful, I would ask for more information. = 如果我怀疑，我会要求更多信息。
I wish I weren't doubtful about his honesty. = 我希望我不怀疑他的诚实。


被动语态:
His motives were doubted by many people. = 许多人怀疑他的动机。
The results are doubted by the scientific community. = 科学界对结果表示怀疑。


疑问句:
Are you doubtful about his story? = 你怀疑他的故事吗？
Is she doubtful of her chances of winning? = 她怀疑自己获胜的机会吗？


否定句:
I am not doubtful about his abilities. = 我不怀疑他的能力。
She was not doubtful of her decision. = 她对自己的决定没有疑问。



PHRASE->
