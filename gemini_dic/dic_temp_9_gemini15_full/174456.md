quindecagon:/ˈkwɪndeɪkəɡɒn/| n. |十五边形|A quindecagon is a polygon with fifteen sides and fifteen angles.  It's also known as a pentadecagon.|The architect designed a building with a quindecagon-shaped base.  建筑师设计了一座底座为十五边形的建筑。|近义词：pentadecagon|反义词：triangle, square, pentagon (referring to the number of sides)|名词
