showiness:/ˈʃoʊɪnəs/ | n. |炫耀；华而不实；显眼 |The quality or state of being showy; ostentation; a display intended to attract notice.  It often implies a lack of substance or sincerity. |例句：The showiness of the ceremony overshadowed its true meaning.  典礼的炫耀喧宾夺主，掩盖了其真正的意义。

例句：He disliked the showiness of her new car, preferring something more understated. 他不喜欢她新车的炫耀，更喜欢低调一些的东西。


近义词：ostentation, flamboyance, gaudiness, conspicuousness |反义词：simplicity, modesty, understatement


词源：源自show (展示，表演) + -iness (名词后缀，表示“性质”、“状态”)，字面意思是“展示的性质”，引申为“炫耀”。
