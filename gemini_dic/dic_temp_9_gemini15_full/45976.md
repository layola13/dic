cyanin:/ˈsaɪənɪn/| n.|花青素|A water-soluble pigment that gives many flowers, fruits, and vegetables their blue, purple, or red color.  It's a type of flavonoid and an antioxidant.|Example: The vibrant blue color of blueberries is due to the presence of cyanins. 蓝莓的鲜艳蓝色是由于花青素的存在。 The red color of red cabbage is also caused by cyanins.  红甘蓝的红色也是由花青素引起的。|近义词：anthocyanin (更广泛的术语)|反义词：无|名词
