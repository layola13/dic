environmentalism:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪzəm/| n. |环境保护主义；环境论|The advocacy of or concern for the protection of the environment.  It encompasses a broad range of concerns, from pollution control to the conservation of natural resources and biodiversity. |例句：Environmentalism is gaining increasing support worldwide. 环境保护主义正在全世界获得越来越多的支持。

例句：The group's environmentalism extends to advocating for sustainable agriculture. 该组织的环境保护主义延伸到倡导可持续农业。


近义词：ecologism, environmental protection, conservationism |反义词：environmental destruction, pollution
