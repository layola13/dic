drupaceous:/druːˈpeɪʃəs/| adj.|核果的；带核果的|Having the characteristics of a drupe; relating to or resembling a drupe.  A drupe is a fleshy fruit with a hard shell (stone) enclosing a seed, such as a cherry, plum, or peach.|The drupaceous fruits were ripe and ready for picking.  这些核果已经成熟，可以采摘了。|juicy, stone-fruit| non-drupaceous
