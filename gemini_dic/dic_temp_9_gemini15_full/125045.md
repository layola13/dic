mulches:/ˈmʌltʃɪz/| n.|覆盖物，覆盖层|Material such as wood chips, straw, or plastic spread on the ground to protect plant roots and conserve moisture.  It helps retain soil moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.|例句：We spread a thick layer of mulches around the rose bushes to protect them from the harsh sun. 我们在玫瑰丛周围铺了一层厚厚的覆盖物，以保护它们免受强烈的阳光照射。|例句：The gardener used wood mulches to improve the soil structure and reduce erosion. 园丁使用木质覆盖物来改善土壤结构并减少侵蚀。|近义词：ground cover, topdressing |反义词：bare soil
