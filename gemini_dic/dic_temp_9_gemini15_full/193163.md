sedates:/ˈsɛdeɪts/| vt.|使镇静；使平静；使安宁|to make someone or something calmer and less anxious or excited|The doctor sedated the patient before the surgery.医生在手术前给病人注射了镇静剂。|近义词：calm, tranquilize, soothe|反义词：agitate, excite, stimulate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication sedates him effectively. = 这种药很有效地使他镇静。
This music sedates my nerves. = 这音乐使我的神经平静下来。


一般过去时:
