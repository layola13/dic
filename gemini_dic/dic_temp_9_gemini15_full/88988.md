holistically:/həˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 整体地；全盘地；从整体出发地 | Considering something as a whole; in a holistic way.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness of parts and their influence on the whole. | 例句：We need to approach the problem holistically, considering all its aspects.我们需要从整体上解决这个问题，考虑到它的所有方面。

例句：The doctor treated the patient holistically, considering both physical and mental health.医生从整体上治疗病人，兼顾身心健康。


近义词：integrally, comprehensively, completely, entirely | 反义词：partially, fragmentarily, incompletely | 词性: 副词
