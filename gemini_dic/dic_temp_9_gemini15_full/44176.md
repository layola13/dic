critiqued:/krɪˈtiːkt/| v. | 批评；评论；批判 | To analyze and judge the merits and faults of something (e.g., a piece of writing, a work of art, a performance).  | The professor critiqued the student's essay, pointing out both its strengths and weaknesses.  教授评论了学生的文章，指出了它的优点和缺点。 | analyzed, reviewed, evaluated, assessed | praised, lauded | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He critiques my work regularly. = 他经常评论我的作品。
She critiques movies for a living. = 她靠影评为生。


一般过去时:
The art critic critiqued the painting harshly. = 艺术评论家严厉地批评了这幅画。
They critiqued the proposal before submitting it. = 他们在提交提案之前对其进行了评论。


现在完成时:
She has critiqued hundreds of essays this semester. = 本学期她已经评论了几百篇文章。
He has critiqued my performance several times. = 他已经多次评论我的表现。


过去完成时:
The committee had critiqued the plan before the meeting. = 委员会在会议前已经对计划进行了评论。
She had critiqued his work extensively before giving her final assessment. = 在给出最终评价之前，她已经广泛地评论了他的作品。


将来时:
The panel will critique the submissions next week. = 评审团下周将对提交的作品进行评论。
He will critique the novel after he finishes reading it. = 他看完小说后会对其进行评论。


虚拟语气:
If he critiqued the project more thoroughly, we might have avoided the mistakes. = 如果他更彻底地评论了这个项目，我们本可以避免这些错误。
I wish he had critiqued my presentation before I gave it. = 我希望他在我做演示之前能评论一下我的演示文稿。


被动语态:
The student's work was critiqued by the professor. = 学生的作品受到了教授的评论。
The film was critiqued by several major publications. = 这部电影受到了几家主要出版物的评论。


疑问句:
Has the manuscript been critiqued yet? = 手稿已经被评论了吗？
Will the play be critiqued by professional critics? = 这部戏会被专业评论家评论吗？


否定句:
The director didn't critique the actor's performance. = 导演没有评论演员的表演。
The committee did not critique the proposal adequately. = 委员会没有充分评论该提案。


PHRASE->
critique someone's work = 批评某人的作品
constructive critique = 建设性批评
offer a critique = 提供评论
subject to critique = 受到批评
peer critique = 同行评议
