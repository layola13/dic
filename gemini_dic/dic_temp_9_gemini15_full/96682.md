词源：
The term "IndyCar" is a shortened form of "Indianapolis 500 race car".  It's a blend of "Indy" (short for Indianapolis) and "car".  The Indianapolis 500 is a prestigious annual auto race held at the Indianapolis Motor Speedway, which is a major event in the history of open-wheel racing and therefore heavily influences the term's meaning.
