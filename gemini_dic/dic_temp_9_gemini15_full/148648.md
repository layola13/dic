overthrongs:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋz/ | n. | 人潮涌动，挤满 | A large crowd of people filling or overflowing a place.  The word suggests a sense of overwhelming density and perhaps even chaos. | Example: The streets were overthronged with people celebrating the victory. 街道上挤满了庆祝胜利的人群。 |近义词：crowd, throng, swarm, teem | 反义词：deserted, empty, sparsely populated | 名词
