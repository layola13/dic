nonconsummation: /ˌnɒnkɒnsʌˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 未完成；未履行；(婚姻的)未圆房 | The failure to complete or fulfill something, especially a marriage through sexual intercourse.  It refers to a marriage that has not been consummated, meaning the couple has not had sexual intercourse.  The term is often used in legal contexts related to divorce or annulment. |

The grounds for annulment included nonconsummation of the marriage.  | 宣告婚姻无效的理由包括婚姻未圆房。
