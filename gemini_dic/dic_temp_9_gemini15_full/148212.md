overshot:/ˈoʊvərʃɑːt/| /ˈəʊvəʃɒt/| vt. adj.|射过，超过，高估|To go past or beyond (a target, point, or limit).  To exceed a specified or expected amount.|例句：The arrow overshot the target.  箭射过了靶子。
The plane overshot the runway.  飞机飞过了跑道。


例句：He overshot his budget by 10%. 他超支了10%。
The company overshot its sales projections. 公司的销售预测过高了。


近义词：exceed, surpass, overestimate |反义词：undershoot, underestimate


词性:动词，形容词

词源:
The word "overshot" is formed from the prefix "over-" (meaning "beyond" or "too much") and the past tense of the verb "shoot."  The original meaning was literally to shoot beyond a target.  Its meaning has since expanded to encompass exceeding any kind of limit or target.
