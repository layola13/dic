essential:/ɪˈsɛnʃəl/ | adj. |必要的，本质的，根本的 | extremely important; absolutely necessary.  |例句：Regular exercise is essential for good health.  规律的锻炼对健康至关重要。|例句：The essential point is that we must act quickly.  最根本的一点是我们必须迅速行动。|近义词：crucial, vital, indispensable, fundamental |反义词：unnecessary, unimportant, optional


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Food and water are essential for survival. = 食物和水对生存至关重要。
This step is essential to the process. = 这步对整个过程至关重要。

一般过去时:
It was essential that we finish the project on time. = 我们准时完成项目是至关重要的。
He considered his family's support essential to his success. = 他认为家人的支持对他成功至关重要。

现在完成时:
It has been essential to maintain a positive attitude throughout the challenge. = 在整个挑战过程中保持积极的态度一直是至关重要的。
They have found that collaboration is essential for innovation. = 他们发现合作对于创新至关重要。


过去完成时:
It had been essential that they secure funding before starting the project. = 在项目开始之前，他们必须确保资金到位，这至关重要。
She realized it had been essential to communicate more clearly with her team. = 她意识到更清晰地与团队沟通至关重要。

将来时:
It will be essential to have a backup plan. = 制定一个备用计划将至关重要。
Regular checkups will be essential to maintain good health. = 定期检查对于保持健康将至关重要。

虚拟语气:
It is essential that he be present at the meeting. = 他必须出席会议，这是必要的。
It would have been essential to contact them earlier. = 较早地联系他们本来是必要的。


被动语态:
The steps needed to complete the process are considered essential. = 完成该过程所需的步骤被认为是必要的。
A strong work ethic is often considered essential for success. = 强大的职业道德通常被认为是成功的必要条件。

疑问句:

否定句:
These features are not essential to the product's functionality. = 这些功能对产品的功能不是必要的。
The extra information is not essential for understanding the concept. = 额外的信息对于理解这个概念不是必要的。
