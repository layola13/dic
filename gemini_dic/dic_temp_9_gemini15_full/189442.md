Sailmaking requires specialized skills and knowledge of sail design and materials.|例句：The sailmaking process is complex, requiring precision and expertise. 帆布制作过程复杂，需要精确性和专业知识。

The old sailmaker passed his skills down to his son. 老帆匠把他的技艺传给了他的儿子。|近义词：sail manufacture, sail production|反义词: N/A
