incompatibles:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəblz/ | n. |不相容的事物，矛盾体 | Things that are incompatible are unable to exist or work together because of fundamental differences.  |The two operating systems are incompatibles; they cannot be used on the same computer. 这两个操作系统不相容；它们不能在同一台计算机上使用。|近义词：inconsistencies, contradictions |反义词：compatibles


USAGE->
This word is a noun, and thus does not conjugate like verbs.  The usage will vary depending on the context of the sentence.
