resectability: /ˌriːˌsɛktəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可切除性 | The state or quality of being resectable; the ability of a tumor or other lesion to be surgically removed.  | 例句：The surgeon assessed the tumor's resectability before recommending treatment.  外科医生在建议治疗方案之前评估了肿瘤的可切除性。


The patient's poor overall health compromised the resectability of the cancerous growth. 病人的整体健康状况不佳，影响了癌性组织的可切除性。| 近义词：removability, operability | 反义词：inoperability, unresectability |
