nuclear:/ˈnuːkliər/| adj.| 核子的；核能的；原子核的 | Relating to the nucleus of an atom, especially concerning the energy released by nuclear fission or fusion.  Nuclear energy is a powerful source of electricity but also carries significant risks. | 例句：Nuclear weapons pose a grave threat to global security. 核武器对全球安全构成严重威胁。

The nuclear family is becoming less common.  核心家庭越来越少见。| 近义词：atomic, atomic energy | 反义词：non-nuclear
