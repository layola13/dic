musquaw:/ˈmʌskwoː/| n.|麝鼠|A musquaw is a North American rodent of the genus Ondatra,  characterized by its dark brown fur and semi-aquatic lifestyle.  They are known for their ability to build lodges in wetlands. |例句：The musquaw built a large lodge in the marsh. 麝鼠在沼泽地里建造了一个大窝棚。|近义词：muskrat|反义词:无
