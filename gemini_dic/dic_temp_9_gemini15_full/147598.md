overplanting:/ˌoʊvərˈplæntɪŋ/| n.|过度种植，种植过密|The act of planting too many plants in a given area, leading to competition for resources and potentially reduced yields.  Overplanting can also refer to planting the same crop repeatedly in the same location, potentially depleting soil nutrients.|例句：Overplanting led to stunted growth and a poor harvest. 过度种植导致植物生长迟缓，收成不好。

例句：The farmer's overplanting of corn resulted in weak stalks and smaller ears.  农民玉米种植过密导致玉米杆细弱，玉米棒小。


近义词：overcrowding, excessive planting|反义词：underplanting, sparse planting|名词
