japed:/dʒeɪpt/| v.|开玩笑，嘲弄，戏弄 |To make fun of someone or something in a playful or teasing way.  |例句：He japed at her about her new haircut. 他拿她的新发型开玩笑。

例句：The children japed and played all afternoon. 孩子们一下午都在嬉戏玩闹。


近义词：teased, joked, kidded, mocked |反义词：praised, complimented,  serious
