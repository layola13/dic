deporting:/dɪˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. |驱逐出境，递解出境 |The act of forcing someone to leave a country, typically because they have violated immigration laws or other regulations.  This often involves sending someone back to their home country. |例句：The government is deporting illegal immigrants. 政府正在驱逐非法移民。

例句：He was facing deportation after his visa expired. 他的签证过期后，他面临被驱逐出境。


近义词：expelling, removing, extraditing


反义词：admitting, welcoming


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is deporting illegal immigrants. = 政府正在驱逐非法移民。
They are deporting him to his home country. = 他们正在将他驱逐回他的祖国。


一般过去时:
The authorities deported him last week. = 当局上周将他驱逐出境。

现在完成时:
The country has deported thousands of people this year. = 今年该国已驱逐数千人。
They have already been deported. = 他们已经被驱逐出境了。

虚拟语气:
