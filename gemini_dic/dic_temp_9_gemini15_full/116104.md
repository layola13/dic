martyrs:/ˈmɑːrtərz/| n.|殉道者；烈士|A martyr is a person who is killed because of their religious or other beliefs.  They are often seen as heroes or saints by their followers.|例句：Joan of Arc was a martyr for her beliefs.  圣女贞德为她的信仰而殉道。|例句：Many martyrs died for their faith during the early Christian era.  在早期基督教时代，许多殉道者为他们的信仰而死。|近义词：saint, hero, victim|反义词：persecutor, oppressor|名词
