forgets:/fərˈɡɛts/ | v. |忘记，遗忘 | To fail to remember something.  |例句：He forgets his wife's birthday every year. 他每年都忘记他妻子的生日。
He forgets to lock the door. 他忘记锁门了。|近义词：miss, overlook, neglect |反义词：remember, recall


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forgets his keys often. 他经常忘记带钥匙。
She forgets appointments sometimes. 她有时忘记约会。


一般过去时:
I forgot my umbrella yesterday. 昨天我忘记带伞了。
They forgot to invite her to the party. 他们忘记邀请她参加聚会了。


现在完成时:
I have forgotten his name. 我已经忘记了他的名字。
She has forgotten her promise. 她已经忘记了她的承诺。


过去完成时:
I had forgotten to call him before he called me. 他打电话给我之前我忘记给他打电话了。
We had forgotten about the meeting. 我们忘记了会议。


将来时:
I will forget all about it. 我会忘记这件事的。
They will forget their worries soon. 他们很快就会忘记他们的烦恼。


虚拟语气:
If I forgot my password, I would be unable to log in. 如果我忘记了密码，我就无法登录。
I wish I hadn't forgotten my appointment. 我真希望我没有忘记我的约会。


被动语态:
The details were forgotten. 细节被遗忘了。
The meeting was forgotten. 会议被忘记了。


疑问句:
Did you forget my birthday? 你忘记我的生日了吗？
Have you forgotten what I told you? 你忘记我告诉你的事了吗？


否定句:
I didn't forget to pack my suitcase. 我没有忘记收拾我的行李箱。
She doesn't forget her responsibilities. 她不会忘记她的责任。


PHRASE->
forget about it = 别担心; 没关系
forget oneself = 忘乎所以
forget it =算了吧
cannot forget = 难以忘记
to forget something = 忘记某事
