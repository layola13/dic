perturbate:/ˈpɜːrtəbeɪt/|/ˈpɜːrtuːbeɪt/| vt.|扰乱；使不安；使混乱 |To disturb or disquiet (someone's mind); to cause anxiety or concern.  It often implies a more significant or lasting disturbance than "disturb".|例句：The news of the accident perturbed her deeply.  意外的消息深深地扰乱了她。

The unexpected changes perturbed the carefully laid plans.  意外的变化扰乱了精心制定的计划。


近义词：disturb, upset, agitate, unsettle, discompose |反义词：calm, soothe, pacify, tranquilize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud noise perturbs the baby. = 吵闹声扰乱了婴儿。
The constant criticism perturbs him. =  不断的批评使他不安。


一般过去时:
The bad news perturbed her greatly. =  坏消息使她非常不安。
The unexpected visitor perturbed the meeting. =  不速之客扰乱了会议。


现在完成时:
The ongoing conflict has perturbed the region's economy. =  持续的冲突扰乱了该地区的经济。
Recent events have perturbed the stock market. =  最近的事件扰乱了股市。


过去完成时:
The earlier setbacks had perturbed the team's confidence. =  早些时候的挫折已经扰乱了团队的信心。
The rumors had perturbed the public before the announcement. =  在宣布之前，谣言已经扰乱了公众。


将来时:
The upcoming election will perturb the political landscape. =  即将到来的选举将会扰乱政治格局。
The expected storm will perturb the travel plans. =  预期的风暴将会扰乱旅行计划。


虚拟语气:
If the news were true, it would perturb many people. = 如果消息属实，它将会扰乱许多人。
I wish the situation wouldn't perturb my family so much. =  我希望这种情况不会让我的家人如此不安。


被动语态:
She was perturbed by the harsh criticism. = 她被严厉的批评弄得不安。
The market was perturbed by the sudden drop in oil prices. = 市场因油价突然下跌而受到扰乱。


疑问句:
Does the uncertainty perturb you? =  不确定性是否让你不安？
Will the changes perturb the project's timeline? =  这些变化会扰乱项目的进度吗？


否定句:
The news did not perturb him. =  这个消息并没有让他不安。
The situation did not perturb the experienced leader. =  这种情况并没有扰乱这位经验丰富的领导者。



PHRASE->
be perturbed by = 被…扰乱
highly perturbed = 非常不安
deeply perturbed = 深深不安
become perturbed = 变得不安
feel perturbed = 感到不安

词源:
From Latin *perturbatus, past participle of perturbare "to disturb, trouble," from per- "thoroughly" + turbare "to disturb, confuse," from turba "crowd, tumult."  The word ultimately reflects the idea of a crowd causing disorder.
