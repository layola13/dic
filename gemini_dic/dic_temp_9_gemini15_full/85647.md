haunts:/hɔːnts/| v.|萦绕，困扰，鬼魂出没 |To repeatedly come to mind; to trouble the mind persistently; to appear as a ghost.  |例句：The memory of that day haunts me. 那一天的记忆一直萦绕着我。|例句：The old house is said to be haunted.据说那栋旧房子闹鬼。|近义词：trouble, bother, plague, infest|反义词：soothe, comfort, calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ghost haunts the old house. = 鬼魂出没于那栋老房子。
This melody haunts my mind. = 这首旋律萦绕在我的脑海中。

一般过去时:
The memory of the accident haunted him for years. = 那次事故的记忆困扰了他多年。
The image of her face haunted his dreams. = 她的容貌萦绕在他的梦中。

现在完成时:
That song has haunted me all day. = 那首歌一整天都萦绕在我的心头。
The fear of failure has haunted her throughout her career. = 害怕失败一直困扰着她整个职业生涯。

过去完成时:
He had been haunted by guilt ever since the incident. = 自从那件事发生后，他就一直被内疚感所困扰。
The thought of what could have been had haunted her for months. = 对本可能发生的事情的思考已经困扰了她几个月。

将来时:
I fear this problem will haunt me for years to come. = 我担心这个问题会在未来几年一直困扰我。
The consequences of his actions will haunt him for the rest of his life. = 他行为的后果将会伴随他一生。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't made that mistake, it wouldn't haunt me now. = 如果我没有犯那个错误，它现在就不会困扰我了。
I wish those memories wouldn't haunt me so much. = 我希望那些记忆不会这么困扰我。

被动语态:
He was haunted by nightmares. = 他被噩梦缠绕着。
The city was haunted by the ghost of its former glory. = 这个城市被它昔日辉煌的幽灵所萦绕。

疑问句:
Does the idea of failure haunt you? = 失败的想法是否困扰着你？
What haunts you most about this situation? = 在这种情况下，什么最让你困扰？

否定句:
The problem didn't haunt me as much as I thought it would. = 这个问题并没有像我想象的那样困扰我。
The memories of childhood did not haunt her in her later years. = 童年的记忆并没有在她的晚年困扰她。


PHRASE->
haunt someone's dreams = 萦绕在某人的梦中

haunt the mind = 萦绕心头
haunt a place = 某地闹鬼
haunt someone's thoughts = 困扰某人的想法
