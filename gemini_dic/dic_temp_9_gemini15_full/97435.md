infuriating: /ɪnˈfjʊəriˌeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |令人愤怒的，激怒人的 | Extremely annoying or displeasing.  |例句：His constant interruptions were infuriating. 他不断的打断真是令人恼火。

例句：The traffic jam was infuriating; I was late for the meeting. 这场交通堵塞真让人火大，我开会迟到了。


近义词：enraging, maddening, exasperating, provoking, irritating |反义词：soothing, calming, pleasing
