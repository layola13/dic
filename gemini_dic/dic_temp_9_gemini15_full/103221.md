jetted:/ˈdʒɛtɪd/ | v. | 喷射；急速行进 | To move or travel very quickly, especially in a vehicle, or to propel something forward with great force.  |例句：The plane jetted across the sky. 飞机划过天空。

The car jetted away from the scene of the accident.  汽车迅速驶离事故现场。|近义词：sped, zoomed, shot |反义词：crept, crawled, inched |动词
