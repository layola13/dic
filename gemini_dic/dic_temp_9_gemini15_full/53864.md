discontinue:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/| vt.|停止，中断，终止|To stop or cease doing something; to bring to an end.|The company decided to discontinue the product due to low sales. 公司决定因销量低而停产该产品。|近义词：cease, stop, terminate, end|反义词：continue, resume, start


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They discontinue the service every winter. = 他们每年冬天都停止这项服务。
We discontinue the project because of funding issues. = 由于资金问题，我们终止了这个项目。


一般过去时:
The factory discontinued production last year. = 工厂去年停止生产了。
She discontinued her studies to take care of her family. = 她为了照顾家庭而中断了学业。


现在完成时:
They have discontinued the use of that chemical. = 他们已经停止使用那种化学物质了。
The airline has discontinued flights to that city. = 这家航空公司已经停飞到那个城市的航班了。


过去完成时:
The company had discontinued the product before the complaints started. = 在投诉开始之前，公司就已经停产该产品了。
They had discontinued the project before they realized the mistake. = 他们在意识到错误之前就已经停止了这个项目。


将来时:
They will discontinue the service next month. = 他们下个月将停止这项服务。
The school will discontinue the program next year. = 学校明年将取消该项目。


虚拟语气:
If they discontinued the product, sales would likely decrease. = 如果他们停产该产品，销量可能会下降。
I wish they had discontinued that noisy machine earlier. = 我真希望他们早点停掉那台吵闹的机器。


被动语态:
The product was discontinued due to safety concerns. = 该产品由于安全问题而被停产。
The service will be discontinued next week. = 该服务将于下周停止。


疑问句:
Will they discontinue the bus route? = 他们会取消这条公交线路吗？
Have they discontinued the magazine? = 他们停刊了吗？


否定句:
They did not discontinue the project despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，他们也没有停止这个项目。
The company will not discontinue the product until a replacement is found. = 在找到替代品之前，公司不会停产该产品。
