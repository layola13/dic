marine:/məˈriːn/| adj. n.| 海的；海军的；海生的；| Relating to the sea; existing in or produced by the sea. |例句：The marine biologist studied the coral reefs.  这位海洋生物学家研究珊瑚礁。|例句：He served in the marine corps. 他在海军陆战队服役。|近义词：nautical, maritime, sea, ocean|反义词：terrestrial
