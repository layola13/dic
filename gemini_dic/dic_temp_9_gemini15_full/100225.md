interrogator:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtər/| n. |审问者；讯问者|A person who interrogates someone, especially a suspect.  An interrogator is someone who asks questions in a thorough and often intense way, usually to obtain information, often from a suspect or prisoner.|例句：The interrogator relentlessly questioned the suspect for hours.  审问者不间断地审问了嫌疑犯数小时。|例句：The experienced interrogator knew how to get the truth from even the most resistant witnesses.  经验丰富的审问者知道如何从即使是最顽抗的证人那里得到真相。|近义词：examiner, questioner, investigator|反义词：respondent, interviewee
