firings:/ˈfaɪərɪŋz/| n.|解雇；开除；射击；发射|The act of dismissing someone from their job.  It can also refer to the act of shooting a weapon or firing a projectile.|例句：The recent firings at the company caused widespread anxiety among the employees. 近期公司的大规模解雇引发了员工的广泛焦虑。

例句：The rapid firings of the machine gun created a deafening roar. 机枪的快速射击制造出震耳欲聋的轰鸣声。

近义词：dismissals, discharges, sackings |反义词：hirings, employments |词性:n.
