flailing:/ˈfleɪlɪŋ/| v.|挥舞；挣扎；乱动 |To move wildly or in a disorganized way, typically arms or legs.  It can also refer to struggling ineffectually. |例句：He was flailing his arms wildly in the air. 他在空中疯狂地挥舞着手臂。

例句：The drowning man was flailing in the water.  溺水的人在水中挣扎。


近义词：struggling, thrashing, beating, swinging


反义词：calm, still, controlled


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flails his arms when he's angry. = 他生气时挥舞着手臂。
She flails around when she tries to dance. = 她跳舞时手舞足蹈。


一般过去时:
The bird flailed its wings before taking flight. = 鸟儿在起飞前拍打着翅膀。
He flailed desperately in the deep water. = 他在深水中拼命挣扎。


现在完成时:
He has flailed about trying to solve the problem. = 他为了解决这个问题而一直在乱搞。
The team has been flailing all season. = 这个队整个赛季都表现得很糟糕。


过去完成时:
He had flailed for hours before finally reaching the shore. = 他挣扎了好几个小时才最终到达岸边。
She had flailed against the current until she was exhausted. = 她逆流而上挣扎直到精疲力尽。


将来时:
He will flail around if he doesn't get what he wants. = 如果他得不到想要的东西，他会大发脾气。
The boat will flail wildly in the storm. = 船只会在暴风雨中剧烈摇晃。


虚拟语气:
If he had flailed less, he might have won the fight. = 如果他不那么乱挥舞，他可能就赢了这场战斗。
I wish I hadn't flailed so much in my presentation. = 我真希望我在演讲中不要那么手忙脚乱。


被动语态:
His arms were flailed about by the strong wind. = 他的手臂被强风吹得乱舞。
The boat was flailed by the waves. = 船被海浪冲击得剧烈摇晃。


疑问句:
Was he flailing in the water? = 他在水里挣扎吗？
Is she flailing her arms around? = 她在挥舞手臂吗？


否定句:
He didn't flail around like he usually does. = 他不像平时那样乱挥舞了。
She didn't flail when she heard the bad news.  = 她听到坏消息时没有惊慌失措。


PHRASE->
flail about: 乱动，乱挥舞
flail around:  乱挥舞，手忙脚乱
flail helplessly: 无助地挣扎
flail wildly:  疯狂地挥舞
flail in desperation: 绝望地挣扎
