geopolitist: /ˌdʒiːoʊpɒlətɪst/ | n. | 地缘政治学家 | A geopolitist is a scholar or expert who studies geopolitics, which is the study of international relations in terms of geography and material resources.  They analyze how geographical factors, such as location, resources, and topography, influence political power, international relations, and conflict.  Geopolitists often consider the impact of global events on national security and the distribution of power among nations. | Example:  The geopolitist presented a compelling analysis of the evolving geopolitical landscape in East Asia.  地缘政治学家对东亚不断变化的地缘政治格局进行了令人信服的分析。 |近义词: geostrategist, political scientist (with a focus on geopolitics) |反义词: N/A
