embraced:/ɪmˈbreɪst/ | vt. |拥抱；欣然接受；包含；围绕|To hold (someone) closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection.  To accept or support (a belief, theory, change, etc.) enthusiastically. To include or contain something within its scope.|例句：She embraced her son tightly. 她紧紧地拥抱了她的儿子。|例句：The company embraced new technology.  这家公司欣然接受了新技术。|近义词：hug, clasp, accept, welcome |反义词：reject, refuse, shun, spurn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embraces his children every night. = 他每晚都拥抱他的孩子。
She embraces new challenges with enthusiasm. = 她热情地迎接新的挑战。

一般过去时:
They embraced after a long separation. = 他们久别重逢后拥抱在一起。
He embraced the opportunity to travel. = 他欣然接受了旅行的机会。

现在完成时:
She has embraced a new lifestyle. = 她已经接受了一种新的生活方式。
The country has embraced democracy. = 这个国家已经接受了民主。

过去完成时:
They had embraced their fate before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经接受了他们的命运。
She had embraced the idea before it was presented. = 在这个想法被提出之前，她就已经接受了它。

将来时:
He will embrace the future with open arms. = 他将张开双臂拥抱未来。
They will embrace the changes coming their way. = 他们将欣然接受即将到来的变化。

虚拟语气:
If I embraced this opportunity, I would have a better life. = 如果我抓住这个机会，我会过上更好的生活。
I wish I had embraced my passion earlier. = 我希望我早点追求我的热情。

被动语态:
The new policy was embraced by the majority. = 新政策得到了大多数人的支持。
The idea was embraced enthusiastically by the team. = 这个想法受到了团队的热烈欢迎。

疑问句:

Will she embrace the new technology? = 她会接受这项新技术吗？

否定句:
