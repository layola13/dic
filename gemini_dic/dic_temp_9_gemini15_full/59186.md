dysmenorrhea:/ˌdɪsmɛnəˈriːə/| n. |痛经|Painful menstruation, characterized by cramps, pain in the lower abdomen, and sometimes back pain.  The pain can range from mild to severe and may be accompanied by other symptoms such as nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and fatigue.| 例句：Many women suffer from dysmenorrhea, especially during their younger years. 许多女性患有痛经，尤其是在她们年轻的时候。| 例句：She took over-the-counter pain relievers to manage her dysmenorrhea. 她服用非处方止痛药来缓解痛经。|近义词：menstrual cramps, period pain|反义词：painless menstruation
