neologization:/ˌniːoʊˈlɒdʒɪzeɪʃən/| n. | 新词创造；新词形成 | The process of creating new words or expressions.  It involves the introduction of new words into a language, often to reflect changes in society, technology, or culture.  This can involve combining existing words, borrowing from other languages, or creating entirely new words. | Example: The rapid neologization of the internet age has led to many new terms entering everyday speech.  互联网时代的快速新词创造导致许多新词进入了日常口语。|近义词：word formation, coinage, innovation |反义词：archaism, obsolescence |名词
