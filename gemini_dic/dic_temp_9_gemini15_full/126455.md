myth:/mɪθ/| n. |神话；虚构的故事；误解 |A myth is a traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events.  It can also refer to a widely held but false belief or idea. |例句：The myth of Persephone and Hades is a well-known Greek myth.  希腊神话中珀耳塞福涅和哈迪斯的传说广为人知。|例句：The belief that all swans are white is a myth.  认为所有天鹅都是白色的这种说法是错误的。|近义词：legend, fable, tale, misconception |反义词：reality, truth, fact
