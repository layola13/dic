deconglomerating: /ˌdiːkənˈɡlɒmərˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 解除企业集团化；使（企业集团）分解 | The process of separating a large, diversified company into smaller, more focused entities.  This often involves divesting subsidiaries or business units. | 例句：The company is deconglomerating, selling off its non-core businesses to focus on its main product line.  这家公司正在进行企业集团分解，出售非核心业务以专注于其主要产品线。


The company is currently undergoing a period of deconglomerating, aiming to improve efficiency and profitability. 这家公司目前正在经历企业集团分解时期，旨在提高效率和盈利能力。


近义词：disaggregating, divesting, breaking up | 反义词：conglomerating, merging, consolidating | 词性：动词
