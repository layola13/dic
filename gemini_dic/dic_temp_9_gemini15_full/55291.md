dissociative: /dɪˈsoʊʃieɪtɪv/ | adj. | 分离的；解离的；分离性的 | Relating to or characterized by dissociation, a mental process of disconnecting from one's thoughts, feelings, memories, or sense of self.  It often occurs as a coping mechanism in response to trauma or overwhelming stress. |


例句：

The patient exhibited dissociative symptoms, including amnesia and depersonalization.  病人表现出解离症状，包括失忆和人格解体。

Her dissociative disorder stemmed from childhood trauma. 她的解离性障碍源于童年创伤。


近义词：separative, detached, disconnected, disjointed


反义词：integrated, unified, connected, associated


词性：形容词
