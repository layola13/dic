nonshoppers: /ˈnɒnˌʃɒpərz/ | n. |非购物者；不购物的人 | People who do not engage in shopping activities.  They may choose not to shop for various reasons, including lack of interest, financial constraints, or philosophical objections to consumerism. | Example:  The marketing campaign failed to reach the nonshoppers, who remained unconvinced by the product's appeal.  = 这场营销活动未能吸引到非购物者，他们对产品的吸引力仍然不为所动。


近义词: non-buyers, consumers who avoid shopping, people who rarely shop

反义词: shoppers, consumers, buyers

词性: noun
