phytolithologist:/ˌfaɪtəˈlɪθɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |植物硅酸体学家|A scientist who studies phytoliths.  Phytoliths are microscopic silica structures found in plants.  Phytolitholigists analyze these structures to learn about past environments, plant species, and human activities.|The phytolithologist carefully examined the soil samples under a microscope.|paleobotanist, archaeobotanist| (no clear antonym)|名词
