cartway:/ˈkɑːrtweɪ/ | n. |车辙；车道；小路 | A cartway is a track or road suitable for carts or other vehicles.  It is often a rough, unpaved road. |例句：The old cartway was overgrown with weeds.  这条旧车道杂草丛生。|例句：The farmer used the cartway to transport his crops.  这位农民用这条车道运输他的庄稼。|近义词：track, path, lane, roadway |反义词：highway (in terms of quality and size)
