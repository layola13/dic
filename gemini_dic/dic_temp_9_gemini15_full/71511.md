flannel:/ˈflænəl/| n. adj.|法兰绒；法兰绒的|A soft woven fabric, typically of wool, cotton, or a wool-cotton blend, used to make shirts, pajamas, and other clothing.|例句：She wore a flannel shirt. 她穿着一件法兰绒衬衫。|例句：The baby's blanket is made of soft flannel. 婴儿的毯子是用柔软的法兰绒做的。|近义词：twill, corduroy|反义词：silk, satin
