manifesters:/ˈmænɪfɛstərz/| n.| 表明者；显示者；证明者 | Those who clearly show or demonstrate something; individuals or things that make something evident.  |例句：The protesters were the most vocal manifesters of public discontent.  抗议者是公众不满的最直言不讳的表达者。

例句：The rapid economic growth of the country is a manifestor of its successful policies.  该国的快速经济增长证明了其政策的成功。


近义词：demonstrators, exhibitors, revealers, indicators | 反义词：concealers, suppressors


词源:
The word "manifesters" derives from the verb "manifest," which comes from the Late Latin word *manifestāre*, meaning "to make clear," "to show," or "to reveal."  This in turn is derived from the Latin *manifestus*, meaning "evident," "clear," or "obvious."


用法说明:


一般现在时:
The rising sea levels manifest climate change. 海平面上升表明了气候变化。
These symptoms manifest a serious illness. 这些症状表明了一种严重的疾病。


一般过去时:
The experiment manifested the power of the new technology. 实验显示了新技术的威力。
His actions manifested his true character. 他的行为显露出了他真正的性格。


现在完成时:
Recent events have manifested a growing need for change. 近期事件表明了对变革日益增长的需求。
The data have manifested a clear trend. 数据显示了一个清晰的趋势。


过去完成时:
By then, the problems had already manifested themselves. 到那时，问题已经显现出来了。
The signs of the disease had manifested themselves weeks before.  疾病的症状在几周前就已经显现出来了。


将来时:
The effects of the policy will manifest themselves over time.  这项政策的影响将会随着时间的推移而显现出来。
The results of the study will manifest themselves in the coming months.  这项研究的结果将在接下来的几个月里显现出来。


虚拟语气:
If only the truth would manifest itself!  但愿真相能够显现出来！
If the symptoms manifested differently, the diagnosis would be different. 如果症状表现不同，诊断也会不同。


被动语态:
The problem was manifested in several ways.  这个问题以多种方式表现出来。
The company's success was manifested in its increased profits. 公司的成功体现在其利润的增长上。


疑问句:
How does this disease manifest itself?  这种疾病是如何表现出来的？
What are the ways this problem manifests?  这个问题有哪些表现形式？


否定句:
The results did not manifest the expected outcome.  结果并没有显示出预期的结果。
The symptoms did not manifest themselves until much later.  症状直到很久以后才显现出来。
