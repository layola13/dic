indiscrimination's: /ɪnˌdɪskrɪmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 不加区别；不加选择；任意；无差别 | The act of not making distinctions or showing favoritism; treating all things the same.  It's often used with a negative connotation, implying a lack of judgment or fairness. |例句：The indiscriminate bombing caused widespread devastation.  无差别轰炸造成了大范围的破坏。
The indiscriminate use of pesticides harms the environment.  滥用杀虫剂危害环境。|近义词：undifferentiation, lack of discernment, bias, prejudice |反义词：discrimination, selectivity, careful consideration
