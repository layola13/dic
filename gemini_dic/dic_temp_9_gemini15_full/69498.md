feline:/ˈfiːlaɪn/| n. adj.|猫科动物；猫一样的；猫的|A feline is a member of the cat family, characterized by their agility, hunting instincts, and independent nature.  The term can refer to both domestic cats and wild cats.  As an adjective, it describes something related to or characteristic of cats.|例句：The sleek feline gracefully leaped onto the fence.  这只优雅的猫科动物轻巧地跳到了栅栏上。 The feline eyes of the tiger watched its prey. 老虎那双猫科动物的眼睛注视着它的猎物。|近义词：catlike, catty|反义词：canine
