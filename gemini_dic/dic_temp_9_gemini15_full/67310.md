/ˌɛksˌsuːsɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 复苏；苏醒；复活 | The act or process of reviving or restoring to consciousness or life; resuscitation.  Exsuscitation refers to the process of bringing someone back from a state of unconsciousness, near-death, or apparent death.  It encompasses various medical interventions aimed at restoring vital functions. | Example: The paramedics performed exsuscitation techniques on the victim of the accident. 急救人员对事故受害者进行了复苏抢救。 | 近义词：resuscitation, revival, reanimation | 反义词：death, demise, expiration |  名词
