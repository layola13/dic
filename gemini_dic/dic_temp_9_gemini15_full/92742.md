ichthyocoprolite:/ˌɪkθiːoʊˈkɑːprəlaɪt/| n. |鱼类粪化石| fossilized fish excrement.  Ichthyocoprolites are valuable paleontological tools, providing insights into the diet and environment of extinct fish species. They can be found in various sedimentary rock formations.  | 例句：The discovery of numerous ichthyocoprolites in the limestone layer suggests a thriving fish population in this ancient ecosystem. = 在石灰岩层中发现大量的鱼类粪化石表明，在这个古老的生态系统中鱼类种群繁盛。

The analysis of ichthyocoprolite samples revealed the presence of scales and bones from various prey species, indicating a diverse diet for the fish. = 对鱼类粪化石样本的分析揭示了各种猎物种类的鳞片和骨骼的存在，表明鱼类的饮食多样化。| 近义词：fossil feces, coprolites (more general)| 反义词：None. | 名词
