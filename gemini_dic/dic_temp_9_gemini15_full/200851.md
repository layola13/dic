sliming:/ˈslaɪmɪŋ/| n.| 诽谤；诋毁；造谣；污蔑 | The act of spreading false or malicious rumors about someone to damage their reputation.  It often involves subtle or indirect methods. | 例句：The sliming campaign against the politician was relentless.  这场针对这位政治家的诽谤活动持续不断。

例句：The online sliming of the celebrity was shocking.  对这位名人的网络诽谤令人震惊。


近义词：smear, defamation, vilification, character assassination

反义词：praise, commendation, exoneration

词性：名词
