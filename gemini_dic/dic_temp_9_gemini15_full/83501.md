hackmatack:/ˈhækmətæk/| n. |美洲落叶松|American larch, a type of coniferous tree native to eastern North America, known for its reddish-brown wood and its deciduous nature (meaning it loses its needles in the fall).  It is also sometimes called tamarack.|The hackmatack trees turned golden in the autumn.  美洲落叶松在秋天变成金黄色。|近义词：tamarack, larch |反义词：无
