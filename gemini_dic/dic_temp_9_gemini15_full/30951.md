chaise:/ʃeɪz/| n. |轻便躺椅；(旧式)轻便马车|A chaise is a light, two-wheeled carriage, especially one pulled by a horse, or a comfortable chair, often with arms and a padded seat.|例句：She relaxed in the comfortable chaise by the fireplace. 她舒服地坐在壁炉旁的轻便躺椅上。|例句：In the old painting, a wealthy gentleman is shown riding in a chaise. 在那幅旧画中，一位富有的绅士正坐在一辆轻便马车里。|近义词：chair, lounge chair, recliner|反义词：None
