salvages:/ˈsæl.vɪdʒ/| vt. n.|抢救；打捞；挽救；(尤指从废墟或危险中)抢救出来之物 |To save something or someone from being destroyed or lost.  |例句：Firefighters salvaged some furniture from the burning building. 消防员从燃烧的建筑物中抢救出一些家具。

The crew salvaged the wrecked ship. 船员抢救了失事的船只。|近义词：rescue, recover, retrieve|反义词：destroy, lose, ruin|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They salvage valuable items from the wreckage. = 他们从残骸中打捞贵重物品。
The company salvages old computers for parts. = 这家公司回收旧电脑以提取零件。


一般过去时:
The divers salvaged the sunken treasure. = 潜水员打捞了沉没的宝藏。
We salvaged what we could from the flood. = 我们从洪水中抢救了我们能抢救的东西。


现在完成时:
They have salvaged many lives from the disaster. = 他们已经从灾难中拯救了许多生命。
The team has salvaged important data from the crashed server. = 团队已从崩溃的服务器中抢救出重要数据。


过去完成时:
The archaeologists had salvaged ancient artifacts before the site was destroyed. = 考古学家在遗址被破坏之前已经抢救出古代文物。
They had salvaged the documents before the fire spread. = 大火蔓延之前他们已经抢救了文件。


将来时:
The coast guard will salvage the damaged vessel. = 海岸警卫队将打捞受损的船只。
We will salvage the situation if possible. = 我们将尽力挽回局面。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have salvaged the project. = 如果我们早点行动，就能挽回这个项目。
I wish we could salvage the relationship. = 我希望我们能挽回这段关系。


被动语态:
The lost jewels were salvaged from the ocean floor. = 丢失的珠宝是从海底打捞上来的。
The damaged car was salvaged for parts. = 受损的汽车被拆解成零件。


疑问句:
Can they salvage the situation? = 他们能挽回局面吗？
Will the ship be salvaged? = 这艘船会被打捞吗？


否定句:
They couldn't salvage the damaged engine. = 他们无法修复受损的引擎。
We failed to salvage the situation. = 我们未能挽回局面。
