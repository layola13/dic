fattiness:/ˈfætɪnəs/| n. |肥胖；肥腻；脂肪过多 |The excessive accumulation of fat in the body or food.  It often refers to an undesirable level of fat, leading to obesity or making food unpleasantly greasy. |例句：Her fattiness contributed to her health problems.  她的肥胖导致了她的健康问题。

例句：The fattiness of the fried chicken made it difficult to eat a lot. 这炸鸡的油腻程度让人难以多吃。


近义词：obesity, corpulence, plumpness, greasiness

反义词：leanness, thinness, slimness
