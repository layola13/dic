ridgeling: /ˈrɪdʒəlɪŋ/ | n. |阉割的公畜，未完全阉割的公畜 | A male animal that has been castrated, often imperfectly.  A ridgeling is typically a male animal whose testicles have been removed or damaged, resulting in reduced or absent fertility.  The term often implies incomplete castration, leaving some residual masculine characteristics. | Example: The farmer examined the ridgeling, noting its slightly underdeveloped musculature.  这个农民检查了那只阉割的公畜，注意到它的肌肉略微发育不良。 |近义词: gelding, wether (for sheep), stag (for deer) | 反义词: stallion, bull, boar
