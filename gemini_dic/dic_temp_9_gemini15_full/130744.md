nonamenabilities: /ˌnɒnˌeɪməˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可调和性，不相容性 | The state or quality of being nonamenable;  inability to be persuaded or controlled; incompatibility.  This word refers to a lack of amenability, which is the willingness to be persuaded or controlled.  It often implies a deep-seated incompatibility or resistance to influence. |  The nonamenabilities between the two factions made compromise impossible.  = 两个派系之间的不可调和性使得妥协成为不可能。 The nonamenabilities of the data made statistical analysis difficult. = 数据的不相容性使得统计分析变得困难。|近义词：incompatibility, irreconcilability, intractability,  resistance | 反义词：amenability, compliance, tractability
