futurologies:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 未来学；未来研究 | The study of possible future developments and their implications.  Futurologies often involve the application of various methodologies to forecast and analyze potential scenarios.  It encompasses a wide range of topics, including technological advancements, social trends, environmental changes, and economic shifts. | Example:  Many futurologies predict a significant increase in automation in the workplace over the next decade.  许多未来学预测未来十年工作场所自动化程度将大幅提高。 | 近义词：forecasting, prediction, prospective studies | 反义词：retrospection, history, past studies | 名词
