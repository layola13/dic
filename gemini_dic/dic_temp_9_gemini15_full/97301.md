infomania:/ˌɪnfəˈmeɪniə/| n. |信息狂, 信息成瘾|An overwhelming desire to acquire and process information, often to the point of obsession and neglecting other aspects of life.|例句：His infomania led him to spend hours every day online, neglecting his family and work.  他的信息狂让他每天花数小时上网，忽视了家庭和工作。|近义词：information overload, data addiction, news junkie|反义词：information apathy, disinterest in news
