quasi-sentimentally:/ˈkwɑːziˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəli/| adj. |似是而非地伤感地；假装伤感地 |  The word combines "quasi," meaning seemingly or apparently, with "sentimentally," indicating a feeling or expression of tenderness, sadness, or nostalgia.  It describes an expression of emotion that is not entirely genuine or heartfelt, perhaps affected or superficial.  |  He spoke quasi-sentimentally about his lost youth, but I suspected he was mostly trying to impress us. 他似是而非地伤感地谈论着他逝去的青春，但我怀疑他主要是想给我们留下深刻印象。 | near-sentimentally, affectedly sentimental | genuinely sentimental, honestly emotional | adj.
