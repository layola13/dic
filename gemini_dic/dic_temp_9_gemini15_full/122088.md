mistier:/ˈmɪstiər/| adj.| 更加朦胧的，更加有雾的 | More misty; foggier.  The air grew mistier as the evening approached.  |近义词：foggier, hazier, cloudier |反义词：clearer, brighter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mountain is mistier today than yesterday. = 今天山比昨天更朦胧。
The valley is mistier in the morning. = 山谷在早晨更朦胧。


一般过去时:
The air was mistier last night. = 昨晚空气更朦胧。
The scene became mistier as the fog rolled in. = 随着雾的到来，景色变得更加朦胧。


现在完成时:
The weather has become mistier recently. = 最近天气变得更朦胧了。
The landscape has grown mistier since the rain started. = 自从下雨以来，风景变得更加朦胧了。


过去完成时:
The view had been mistier before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，景色更加朦胧。
The air had become mistier than I remembered. = 空气比我记得的更朦胧了。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that it will be mistier tomorrow. = 预报说明天会更朦胧。
The lake will be mistier in the autumn. = 秋天湖面会更朦胧。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were mistier, we wouldn't be able to see the mountains. = 如果天气更朦胧，我们就看不到山了。
I wish the atmosphere were less mistier. = 我希望气氛不那么朦胧。


被动语态:
The scene was made mistier by the falling snow. = 下落的雪花使景色变得更加朦胧。
The view was rendered mistier by the thick fog. = 厚厚的雾使景色变得更加朦胧。


疑问句:
Is the air mistier today? = 今天空气更朦胧了吗？
Isn't the future mistier than we think? = 未来难道不比我们想象的更朦胧吗？


否定句:
The air isn't mistier than usual. = 空气并不比平时更朦胧。
The future is not mistier than it used to be. = 未来并不比以前更朦胧。
