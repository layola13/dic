dealerships:/ˈdiːlərʃɪps/ | n. |经销商；经销权 | A dealership is a business that sells and often services vehicles, such as cars, trucks, or motorcycles, from a specific manufacturer.  It's the place where you buy a new or used car from an authorized seller. | 例句：He works at a Ford dealership. 他在福特经销店工作。 | 例句：The new car dealership opened last month.  这家新车经销店上个月开业了。 | 近义词：dealership network, agency, distributorship | 反义词：manufacture, production
