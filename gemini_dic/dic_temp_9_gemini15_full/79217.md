glanduliferous:/ˌɡlændjuˈlɪfərəs/| adj.|腺体的；含腺的；生腺的|Bearing or producing glands.  The term refers to a structure, organ, or organism that possesses or produces glands. |Example: The glanduliferous tissue secreted a viscous fluid.  含腺组织分泌出一种粘稠液体。 The leaf surface is glanduliferous, producing a sticky substance. 叶面长有腺体，能产生粘性物质。|近义词：glandular|反义词：aglandular
