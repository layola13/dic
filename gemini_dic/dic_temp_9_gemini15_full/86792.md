hemadynamics:/ˌhiːməʊˈdaɪnæmɪks/| n. |血液动力学|The study of blood flow and pressure in the circulatory system.  It involves the forces that influence the circulation of blood, including the heart's pumping action, the elasticity of blood vessels, and the blood's viscosity.|Example:  Understanding hemadynamics is crucial for diagnosing and treating cardiovascular diseases.  理解血液动力学对于诊断和治疗心血管疾病至关重要。|近义词：hemodynamics, circulatory dynamics |反义词：无
