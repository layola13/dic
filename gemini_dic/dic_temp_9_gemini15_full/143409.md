outflankers:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋkərz/ | n. |侧翼攻击者，包抄者 |Those who attack the flanks of an opponent's army or position.  They aim to outmaneuver and overcome the opponent by attacking from the sides or rear. |例句：The general sent outflankers to attack the enemy's exposed left wing.  将军派出了侧翼攻击部队去攻击敌人暴露的左翼。

The outflankers successfully encircled the enemy forces. 侧翼攻击部队成功地包围了敌军。


近义词：flanking units, flanking forces, enveloping forces |反义词：center,main force

词性：名词
