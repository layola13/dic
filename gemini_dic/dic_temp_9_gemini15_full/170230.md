protractor:/ˈproʊtræktər/| n. |量角器|A protractor is a tool used for measuring angles, typically in degrees.  It usually has a semicircular shape with markings from 0 to 180 degrees.| Example: I used a protractor to measure the angle of the triangle. 我用量角器测量了三角形的角度。|近义词：angle measurer|反义词：None

词源:
The word "protractor" comes from the Latin word "protrahere," meaning "to draw forth" or "to prolong."  This reflects its function of extending lines to measure angles.
