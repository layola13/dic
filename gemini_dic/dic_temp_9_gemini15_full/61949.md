emulsified:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪd/| adj.|乳化的；已乳化的|Made into or characterized by an emulsion; a mixture of two or more liquids that usually do not mix.  |例句：The mayonnaise is emulsified oil and egg yolk.  蛋黄酱是乳化的油和蛋黄。

例句：The homogenized milk is an emulsified product. 均质牛奶是一种乳化产品。


近义词：emulsified, blended, mixed

反义词：separated, unmixed


词性:形容词
