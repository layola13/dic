moods:/muːdz/| n.|情绪，心情；气氛；语气|The word "moods" refers to a temporary state of mind or feeling.  It can range from happy and cheerful to sad and depressed.  It can also describe the atmosphere or tone of something, like a piece of music or a painting.|例句：Her moods changed rapidly throughout the day.  她的情绪在一天中变化很快。
例句：The movie had a dark and brooding mood. 这部电影有一种黑暗而沉闷的气氛。|近义词：feelings, temperaments, states of mind, atmospheres|反义词：N/A (moods are not typically expressed as having direct opposites, but rather different types)|名词
