czardoms:/ˈtʃɑːrdəm/| n.|沙皇统治；沙皇国；专制统治|The word "czardom" refers to the rule or reign of a czar (tsar), an emperor or autocratic ruler, especially in Russia.  It also refers to the territory or empire ruled by a czar.|Example: The Romanov czardom lasted for over three centuries.  罗曼诺夫王朝统治了三个多世纪。 The czardom was characterized by its autocratic rule and vast territory. 沙皇统治以其专制统治和广阔的领土为特征。|近义词：empire, kingdom, autocracy|反义词：democracy, republic|名词
