debut:/ˈdeɪbjuː/| n. v.|首次亮相；初次登台；首次公开露面|The first public appearance of a performer or work.|例句：Her debut as a singer was a great success.  她的歌手首秀非常成功。

例句：The film's debut at the Cannes Film Festival was met with critical acclaim.  这部电影在戛纳电影节的首映获得了评论界的赞誉。


近义词：first appearance, introduction, launch, premiere|反义词：finale, conclusion, end


词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Her new novel debuts next month. = 她的新小说下个月首发。
The band debuts their new album tonight. = 乐队今晚首发他们的新专辑。


一般过去时:
The play debuted to a packed house. = 这部戏首演座无虚席。
She debuted her new collection at Fashion Week. = 她在时装周上首次发布了她的新系列。


现在完成时:
He has debuted several successful films. = 他已经推出了几部成功的电影。
The artist has debuted her work in several galleries. =这位艺术家已在几家画廊展出了她的作品。


过去完成时:
By the time the show debuted, the cast had rehearsed for months. = 在演出首演之前，演员们已经排练了几个月。
They had debuted their product at several trade shows before launching it nationwide. = 他们在全国推出产品之前，已经在几个贸易展会上展出了他们的产品。


将来时:
The new car will debut at the auto show next week. = 新车将于下周在车展上首次亮相。
The singer will debut her new single next month. = 这名歌手下个月将发行她的新单曲。


虚拟语气:
If the play had debuted earlier, it might have been more successful. = 如果该剧早点首演，可能会更成功。
I wish I had been there for her debut performance. = 我真希望我能去看她的首场演出。


被动语态:
The new product was debuted to great fanfare. = 新产品隆重推出。
The album was debuted at a special concert. = 专辑在一场特别的音乐会上首发。


疑问句:
When will the new phone debut? = 新手机什么时候发布？
Has the artist debuted her latest artwork yet? =这位艺术家已经展出她最新的艺术作品了吗？


否定句:
The product did not debut as well as expected. = 产品的推出并不像预期的那样好。
The film did not debut in theaters until next year. = 这部电影直到明年才会在影院上映。
