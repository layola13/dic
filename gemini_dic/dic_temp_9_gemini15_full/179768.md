reestimations:/ˌriːˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. |重新估算；重新评估 | The act of estimating something again, often after discovering new information or making corrections to previous estimates.  It involves revising initial calculations or judgments. |例句：The project's reestimations revealed a significant cost overrun.  项目重新估算显示成本超支严重。


例句：After reviewing the latest data, we need to perform reestimations of the project timeline.  在审查了最新数据后，我们需要对项目时间表进行重新估算。


近义词：recalculations, reassessments, revisions, reevaluations


反义词：initial estimations, original assessments


词性：名词
