duffle: /ˈdʌfl/ | n. |粗呢；旅行袋；(尤指)帆布旅行袋 | A thick, heavy woolen cloth, or a bag made from this cloth, often used for travel.  |例句：He packed his duffle bag for the weekend trip. 他为周末旅行打包了他的旅行袋。

例句：The coat is made of a thick duffle. 这件外套是用厚厚的粗呢做的。


近义词：bag, sack, rucksack | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "duffle" originates from Duffel, a town in Belgium, where a thick, woolen cloth was originally manufactured.  The name of the town then became associated with the cloth itself, and later, the bags made from it.
