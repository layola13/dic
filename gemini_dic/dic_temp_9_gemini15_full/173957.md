quasi-slanderously: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈslændərəsli/ | adj. | 近似诽谤地，似是而非地诽谤 |  Almost but not quite slanderous; suggesting slander but lacking the necessary proof or intent to be legally considered slander.  It implies a malicious or damaging statement that falls short of being a legally actionable slander. |

例句：

He spoke quasi-slanderously about his competitor, hinting at wrongdoing without explicitly accusing him. = 他近似诽谤地谈论他的竞争对手，暗示有不法行为，但没有明确指控他。


The article, while not explicitly libelous, was written in a quasi-slanderously manner, damaging the reputation of the subject. = 这篇文章虽然并非明确诽谤，但写作方式近似诽谤，损害了当事人的名誉。


近义词：defamatory, calumnious, scurrilous,  insinuatingly


反义词：complimentary, laudatory, praising


词性：形容词
