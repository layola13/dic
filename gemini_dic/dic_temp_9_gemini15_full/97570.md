ingrowth's:/ˈɪŋɡroʊθz/ | n. | 内生；内长；向内生长 | The inward growth of something, typically tissue or a plant.  It often refers to the abnormal growth of tissue into another structure.  The possessive 's' indicates ownership or belonging. | Example: The surgeon carefully removed the ingrowth's fibrous tissue. 外科医生仔细地切除了内生纤维组织。  |近义词：incursion, intrusion, proliferation |反义词：outgrowth, extrusion, regression |名词
