inserting:/ɪnˈsɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. |插入；嵌入；添加|The act of putting something into something else.  It often implies placing something within a larger structure or context.|例句：He was busy inserting the new chapter into the manuscript. 他正忙着将新章节插入手稿中。|例句：The surgeon carefully inserted the catheter into the vein. 外科医生小心地将导管插入静脉。|近义词：putting in, embedding, adding, introducing |反义词：removing, deleting, extracting|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inserting a picture into the document. = 他正在将一张图片插入文档。
She inserts a new paragraph every day. = 她每天插入一段新文字。

一般过去时:
I inserted a coin into the vending machine. = 我往自动售货机里投了一枚硬币。
They inserted the key into the ignition. = 他们把钥匙插进点火开关。

现在完成时:
She has inserted several new slides into her presentation. = 她已经在她演示文稿中插入了几张新幻灯片。
He has inserted the missing data into the spreadsheet. = 他已将丢失的数据插入电子表格。

过去完成时:
Before submitting, he had inserted all the necessary diagrams. = 在提交之前，他已经插入了所有必要的图表。
She had inserted the correct password before the system allowed access. = 在系统允许访问之前，她已经输入了正确的密码。

将来时:
I will insert the relevant details later. = 我稍后会插入相关细节。
They will insert a new advertisement in tomorrow's newspaper. = 他们将在明天的报纸上插入一则新广告。

虚拟语气:
If I were to insert this paragraph here, the flow would be better. = 如果我把这段话插入这里，行文会更好。
I wish I had inserted a better picture for the report. = 我希望我在报告中插入更好的图片。

被动语态:
The image was inserted into the wrong place. = 图片被插入了错误的位置。
A new section is being inserted into the contract. = 合同中正在插入一个新的部分。

疑问句:
Where did you insert the USB drive? = 你把U盘插在哪里了？
Where should I insert this paragraph? = 我应该把这段话插在哪里？

否定句:
He didn't insert the correct information. = 他没有插入正确的信息。
She did not insert the floppy disk properly. = 她没有正确插入软盘。
