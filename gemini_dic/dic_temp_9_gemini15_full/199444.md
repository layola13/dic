simulcasting:/ˈsɪmjʊlkæstɪŋ/| n. |同步广播；同时播放|The simultaneous broadcasting of a program on television and radio, or on multiple channels.|例句：The concert was simulcast on three different television channels. 这场音乐会在三个不同的电视频道上同时播放。|近义词：simultaneous broadcast, simultaneous transmission|反义词：delayed broadcast
