rive:/raɪv/| v.|撕裂，裂开；使激动；驱使|to tear or pull apart with force; to be deeply moved or excited; to drive or urge on.|例句：The strong wind rived the old oak tree. 狂风撕裂了那棵老橡树。 The news of her success rived her with joy. 她成功的消息使她欣喜若狂。|近义词：tear, split, rend, excite, propel|反义词：mend, repair, soothe, calm


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river rives the valley. = 河流冲刷着山谷。
The strong wind rives the leaves from the trees. = 强风把树叶从树上吹落。


一般过去时:
The earthquake rived the city. = 地震撕裂了这座城市。
The news of his death rived her heart. = 他去世的消息使她心碎。


现在完成时:
The constant friction has rived the rope. = 长期摩擦已经磨损了绳子。
Years of hardship have rived the family. = 多年的苦难使这个家庭支离破碎。


过去完成时:
The storm had rived the ship before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，暴风雨已经摧毁了那艘船。
The drought had rived the land, leaving it barren. = 干旱已经使这片土地干涸贫瘠。


将来时:
The flood will rive the riverbanks. = 洪水将冲毁河岸。
The relentless sun will rive the paint on the car. = 无情的阳光会把汽车上的油漆晒裂。


虚拟语气:
If the storm had been stronger, it would have rived the house. = 如果暴风雨更猛烈的话，它就会把房子摧毁。
I wish the wind hadn't rived my favorite tree. = 我真希望风没有吹坏我最喜欢的树。


被动语态:
The fabric was rived by the sharp object. = 布料被尖锐的物体撕裂了。
The land was rived by the earthquake. = 这片土地被地震撕裂了。


疑问句:
Does the current rive the boat downstream? = 水流是否把船冲向下游？
Will the force of the explosion rive the building apart? = 爆炸的力量会把建筑物炸裂吗？


否定句:
The material didn't rive easily. = 这种材料不容易撕裂。
The gentle breeze did not rive the leaves from the trees. = 柔和的微风没有把树叶从树上吹落。


PHRASE->

rive open: 撕开，裂开
rive asunder: 分裂，崩裂
rive in two: 分成两半
rive with: 充满（某种情感）
