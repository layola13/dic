insanest:/ɪnˈseɪnɪst/| adj.|最疯狂的，最荒谬的 | Most extreme in madness or folly; utterly foolish.  It is the superlative form of "insane".|例句：That's the insanest idea I've ever heard!  那是我听过最疯狂的想法！
 
例句：He drove at an insanest speed. 他以最疯狂的速度开车。


近义词：craziest, maddest, most deranged, most ludicrous


反义词：sane, rational, sensible, reasonable


词性:形容词
