scapulas:/ˈskæpjʊləz/| n.|肩胛骨（复数）|The scapulas are two large, triangular bones located on the back of the rib cage, one on each side. They form part of the shoulder girdle and provide attachment points for several important muscles.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's scapulas to check for any fractures.医生检查了病人的肩胛骨，查看是否有骨折。

例句：Strengthening the muscles around the scapulas can improve posture and reduce back pain.  加强肩胛骨周围的肌肉可以改善姿势并减轻背痛。


近义词：shoulder blades|反义词：无


词源:  From Late Latin *scapulae, plural of scapula, from Latin scapula "shoulder blade".  The Latin word is of uncertain origin, possibly related to a Proto-Indo-European root meaning "to dig" or "to scrape," reflecting the bone's shape.
