chuck:/tʃʌk/ | n./v.| 抛，扔；笑话；(非正式)家伙；切碎 |  To throw something carelessly or forcefully; a joke; an informal term for a person; to cut into small pieces. |例句：He chucked the ball to his friend. 他把球扔给了他的朋友。 He told a few chuckling jokes. 他讲了几个让人发笑的笑话。 That guy's a real chuck. 那家伙真是个家伙。 The chef chucked the vegetables for the stew. 厨师把蔬菜切碎做炖菜。|近义词：toss, throw, fling, jest, guy, fellow, chop, mince |反义词：catch, receive, keep, serious, formal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chucks the ball every day. = 他每天都扔球。
She chucks her old clothes away. = 她把旧衣服扔掉了。

一般过去时:
He chucked the stone into the river. = 他把石头扔进了河里。
They chucked a party last night. = 他们昨晚举办了一个派对。

现在完成时:
He has chucked away all his old toys. = 他已经扔掉了所有旧玩具。
She has chucked a lot of work lately. = 她最近做了很多工作。(chuck在此指“完成”)

过去完成时:
He had chucked the letter in the bin before I saw him. = 我看见他之前，他已经把信扔进了垃圾桶。
They had chucked the idea before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就放弃了那个主意。

将来时:
He will chuck the rubbish in the bin. = 他会把垃圾扔进垃圾桶。
She will chuck the old car. = 她会把旧车扔掉。


虚拟语气:
If he chucked his bad habits, he would be healthier. = 如果他改掉坏习惯，他会更健康。
I wish I had chucked that job sooner. = 我真希望我早点辞掉那份工作。


被动语态:
The ball was chucked across the field. = 球被扔过了田野。
The old furniture was chucked out. = 旧家具被扔掉了。


疑问句:
Did he chuck the ball far? = 他把球扔得远吗？
Will she chuck the project? = 她会放弃这个项目吗？


否定句:
He didn't chuck the ball hard enough. = 他没有用力把球扔出去。
She doesn't chuck her rubbish on the street. = 她不会把垃圾扔在街上。
