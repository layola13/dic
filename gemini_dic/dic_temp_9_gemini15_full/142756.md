otter:/ˈɒtər/ /ˈɑːtər/| n. |水獭|A semiaquatic mammal with webbed feet, a long body, and dense, waterproof fur. Otters are known for their playful nature and agility in the water.|例句：We saw an otter playing in the river. 我们看到一只水獭在河里玩耍。  The otter gracefully slid into the water. 水獭优雅地滑入水中。|近义词：mink, weasel|反义词:None
