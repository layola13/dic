grine:/ɡrɪn/| v.|咧嘴笑，露齿而笑|To smile broadly, showing one's teeth.|例句：She grinned at me mischievously. 她顽皮地对我咧嘴一笑。|例句：He grinned from ear to ear when he heard the news. 听到这个消息，他咧嘴大笑。|近义词：smile, beam, smirk|反义词：frown, scowl|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grins at the camera. = 他对着镜头咧嘴一笑。
She grins when she sees her friends. = 她看到朋友们时就咧嘴笑。

一般过去时:
He grinned at the joke. = 他对那个笑话咧嘴一笑。
She grinned broadly after winning the race. = 比赛获胜后，她咧嘴大笑。

现在完成时:
He has grinned at every joke told today. = 他今天对每一个笑话都咧嘴一笑。
She has grinned all day long. = 她一整天都在咧嘴笑。

过去完成时:
He had grinned before he realized the mistake. = 他在意识到错误之前就已经咧嘴笑了。
She had grinned nervously before the interview. = 面试前，她紧张地咧嘴笑了笑。

将来时:
He will grin when he sees his gift. = 他看到礼物时会咧嘴一笑。
She will grin with pride upon graduation. = 毕业时她会骄傲地咧嘴一笑。

虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would grin with satisfaction. = 如果我知道答案，我会满意地咧嘴一笑。
I wish I could grin more often. = 我希望我能更经常地咧嘴笑。
