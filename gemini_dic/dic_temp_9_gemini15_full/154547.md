perplexes:/pərˈpleksɪz/ | v. | 使困惑；使费解 | To cause someone to feel confused and uncertain.  | The complex instructions perplexed me.  复杂的指令使我困惑。 | confuses, baffles, puzzles | clarifies, simplifies | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The riddle perplexes me. = 这个谜语让我困惑。
This problem perplexes many scientists. = 这个问题困扰着许多科学家。

一般过去时:
The sudden change perplexed her. = 突然的变化使她困惑。
The instructions perplexed him, so he asked for help. = 说明书把他弄糊涂了，所以他寻求帮助。

现在完成时:
The mystery has perplexed investigators for years. = 这个谜团多年来一直困扰着调查人员。
This issue has perplexed me since I started working on it. = 自从我开始研究这个问题以来，它就一直困扰着我。

过去完成时:
He had been perplexed by the contradictory evidence. = 他一直被相互矛盾的证据弄糊涂了。
The detectives had been perplexed by the lack of clues. = 侦探们一直被缺乏线索所困惑。

将来时:
The new regulations will perplex many businesses. = 新规将使许多企业困惑。
I think this new software will perplex a lot of users. = 我认为这个新软件会让很多用户困惑。

虚拟语气:
If I understood the instructions, I wouldn't be perplexed. = 如果我理解了说明，我就不会困惑了。
I wish I weren't so perplexed by this situation. = 我希望我不至于对这种情况如此困惑。

被动语态:
Many people are perplexed by the government's decision. = 许多人对政府的决定感到困惑。
He was perplexed by the unexpected turn of events. = 他对事态的意外转变感到困惑。

疑问句:
What perplexes you about this project? = 你对这个项目感到困惑的是什么？
Does anything perplex you in this chapter? = 这章有什么让你困惑的吗？

否定句:
The explanation didn't perplex me at all. = 这个解释一点也没有让我困惑。
The simple instructions did not perplex her. = 简洁的说明并没有让她困惑。


PHRASE->
