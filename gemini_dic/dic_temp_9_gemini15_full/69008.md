fatted:/ˈfætɪd/| adj.| 喂肥的；长肥的 |  fed (feed的过去分词), implying that animals have been specially fed to make them fat.  It suggests a deliberate process of fattening for slaughter or other purposes. |例句：The fatted calf was prepared for the prodigal son's return.  肥牛犊已准备好迎接浪子回家。

例句：They had a fatted goose for Christmas dinner. 他们圣诞晚餐吃的是一只喂肥的鹅。


近义词：plump, obese, corpulent, well-fed

反义词：lean, thin, skinny, emaciated


词性:形容词
