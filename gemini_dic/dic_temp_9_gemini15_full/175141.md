rag-bags: /ˈræɡbæɡz/ | n. | 破烂衣服包，杂物包 | A rag-bag is a bag containing old clothes or other discarded items.  It often implies a disorganized collection of things. | Example:  The attic was filled with old trunks and rag-bags overflowing with forgotten treasures.  阁楼里堆满了旧箱子和装满被遗忘的宝藏的破烂衣服包。 | 近义词：jumble bag, odds and ends, junk bag | 反义词：organized collection, neatly packed bag
