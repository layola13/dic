sates:/seɪts/ | vt. |使满足；使厌腻；使饱足|To satisfy (a desire or appetite).  To surfeit; cloy. |例句：The delicious meal sated my hunger. = 这顿美味佳肴满足了我的饥饿感。

The rich dessert sated her sweet tooth, leaving her feeling slightly sick. =  那份丰盛的甜点满足了她的甜食渴望，让她感觉有点反胃。


近义词：satisfy, gratify, appease, fulfill | 反义词：starve, deprive, frustrate

词性:动词

词源:
The word "sate" originates from the Old French word "saier," meaning "to satisfy," which in turn derives from the Latin word "satiare,"  meaning "to satisfy," "to fill," or "to glut."  The connection to "satiety" (the state of being full) is clear.
