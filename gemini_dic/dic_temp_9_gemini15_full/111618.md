localing: /ˈloʊkəlɪŋ/ | n. |本地化，区域化 |The process of adapting a product or service to a particular geographic market or region.  It often involves translating text, adapting images and videos, and modifying functionality to suit local customs and preferences. |Example:  The company undertook extensive localing efforts before launching its new product in Japan.  公司在日本推出新产品之前进行了广泛的本地化工作。|近义词：localization, adaptation |反义词：globalization
