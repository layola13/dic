decolonizing: /ˌdiːˈkɒlənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 去殖民化；摆脱殖民主义影响 | The act of dismantling colonial structures and ideologies.  It involves challenging and overcoming the lasting effects of colonialism on various aspects of society, including political systems, economic structures, cultural practices, and psychological perspectives.  This process often necessitates critical self-reflection, acknowledging historical injustices, and actively working towards equity and justice. |

例句：Many universities are actively engaged in decolonizing their curricula. 许多大学正在积极参与去殖民化他们的课程。

例句：The decolonizing process requires a deep understanding of historical context and ongoing power dynamics. 去殖民化过程需要对历史背景和持续的权力动态有深刻的理解。


近义词：liberating, emancipating, freeing

反义词：colonizing, subjugating, oppressing
