joying:/ˈdʒɔɪɪŋ/ | v. | 享受；欣喜 |  To experience or express great pleasure or happiness.  | 例句：She was joying in the success of her project. = 她为她项目的成功而欣喜。| 例句：The children were joying in the sunshine and the warm weather. = 孩子们享受着阳光和温暖的天气。| 近义词：enjoying, delighting, reveling | 反义词：suffering, grieving


USAGE->

I am joying in this moment. = 我正在享受这一刻。
They are joying in their newfound freedom. = 他们正在享受他们新获得的自由。

一般过去时:
We joyed in the beautiful scenery. = 我们享受着美丽的景色。
She joyed in the accomplishment of her goal. = 她为实现目标而感到高兴。

I have joyed in many adventures throughout my life. = 我一生中享受过许多冒险。
