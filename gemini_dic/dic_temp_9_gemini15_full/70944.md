firefighter:/ˈfaɪərˌfaɪtər/| n. |消防员|A person whose job is to extinguish fires and rescue people from burning buildings.  Firefighters are trained professionals who respond to emergencies involving fire, hazardous materials, and other life-threatening situations. They often work long and irregular hours and face significant risks to their safety.|Example: The firefighters bravely entered the burning building to rescue the trapped residents.  消防员勇敢地冲进燃烧的建筑物去营救被困的居民。The firefighters battled the blaze for hours before bringing it under control. 消防员奋战数小时才控制住大火。|近义词：fireman, emergency responder|反义词：N/A
