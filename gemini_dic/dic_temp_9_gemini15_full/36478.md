coleading:/ˌkoʊˈliːdɪŋ/| adj.|共同领导的，协同领导的| jointly leading or managing.  The term often implies shared responsibility and authority. |例句：They are coleading the project, ensuring a balanced approach. 他们共同领导这个项目，确保采取平衡的方法。

例句：The two companies are coleading the development of a new drug. 两家公司共同领导一种新药的开发。


近义词：co-managing, jointly leading, collaborating


反义词：solely leading, independently managing
