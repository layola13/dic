convict:/kənˈvɪkt/| vt.|证明…有罪；使…确信|To declare (someone) guilty of a criminal offense by the verdict of a court; to persuade (someone) of the truth of something.|例句：The jury convicted him of murder. 陪审团裁定他犯有谋杀罪。|例句：I'm trying to convict you of the error of your ways. 我试图让你相信你行为的错误。|近义词：prove guilty, condemn, persuade|反义词：acquit, exonerate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court convicts the defendant. = 法院判决被告有罪。
She convicts him of lying. = 她认定他说谎。


一般过去时:
The jury convicted him of fraud. = 陪审团判定他犯有欺诈罪。
He was convicted of theft last year. = 他去年因盗窃罪被判有罪。


现在完成时:
They have convicted him on several charges. = 他们已经就多项指控判决他犯有罪行。
The police have convicted the suspect. = 警方已将嫌疑人定罪。


过去完成时:
The judge had convicted him before the appeal. = 法官在上诉之前已经判决他有罪。
It had been convicted that he was guilty. = 他有罪已被认定。


将来时:
The court will convict him next week. = 法院下周将判决他有罪。
They will probably convict him. = 他们很可能会判他罪。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, they would convict him. = 如果证据更充分，他们就会判他有罪。
If he had confessed, they would have convicted him easily. = 如果他认罪，他们就会很容易地判他罪。


被动语态:
He was convicted of conspiracy. = 他被判犯有共谋罪。
The criminal was convicted last month. = 这个罪犯上个月被判刑。


疑问句:
Will they convict the suspect? = 他们会判决嫌疑人有罪吗？
Has he ever been convicted before? = 他以前有没有被判过罪？


否定句:
The jury did not convict the defendant. = 陪审团没有判决被告有罪。
They could not convict him due to lack of evidence. = 由于证据不足，他们无法判他罪。


PHRASE->
