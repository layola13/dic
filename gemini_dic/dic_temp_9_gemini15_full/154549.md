perplexingly:/pərˈpleksɪŋli/ | adv. |令人困惑地，令人费解地 | in a way that is perplexing or confusing; bafflingly.  | The instructions were perplexingly vague.  | confusingly, bewilderingly, mystifyingly | clearly, understandably | 副词


例句：

The instructions were perplexingly vague. 指示令人困惑地含糊不清。

The situation was perplexingly complex.  情况令人费解地复杂。


词源：

源自动词 perplex，意思是“使困惑；使费解”。  perplex  本身来自拉丁语 perplexus，意思是“纠缠；困惑的”，由 prae-（“之前”）和 plectere（“编织；交织”）构成。


用法说明：

perplexingly 主要用作副词，修饰动词、形容词或整个句子，表示一种令人困惑或费解的状态。  它通常用于描述情况、事件或信息难以理解或解释。


近义词：

confusingly, bewilderingly, mystifyingly, bafflingly, puzzlingly


反义词：

clearly, understandably, simply, straightforwardly
