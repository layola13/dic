colors:/ˈkʌlərz/| n. |颜色，色彩|Colors are the visual perception of different wavelengths of light.|The rainbow displays a beautiful array of colors.|近义词：hues, shades, tints|反义词：monochrome, colorless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers are many colors. = 花儿有很多颜色。
Her dress is a vibrant color. = 她的裙子颜色鲜艳。

一般过去时:
The leaves turned many colors in autumn. = 秋天树叶变色了。
His face was a pale color after the accident. = 事故后他的脸色苍白。

现在完成时:
The artist has used a variety of colors in the painting. = 画家在这幅画中使用了各种颜色。
She has never seen such bright colors before. = 她以前从未见过如此鲜艳的色彩。

过去完成时:
He had already mixed the colors before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经调好了颜色。
The walls had been painted a different color before they moved in. = 他们搬进来之前墙已经漆成了不同的颜色。

将来时:
The walls will be painted a light color. = 墙壁将被漆成浅色。
We will use different colors in the presentation. = 我们将在演示文稿中使用不同的颜色。

虚拟语气:
If the colors were brighter, the painting would be more appealing. = 如果颜色更鲜艳，这幅画就会更吸引人。
I wish the colors were more harmonious. = 我希望颜色更和谐一些。

被动语态:
The colors were carefully chosen by the designer. = 颜色是由设计师精心挑选的。
The room is painted in calming colors. = 房间漆着令人平静的颜色。

疑问句:
What colors are your favorite? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What color is the car? = 车是什么颜色？

否定句:
The painting doesn't have many colors. = 这幅画颜色不多。
The room wasn't painted in bright colors. = 房间没有漆成鲜艳的颜色。
