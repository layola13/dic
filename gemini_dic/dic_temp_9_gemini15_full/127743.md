negotiations:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃənz/| n. |谈判，协商|The process of discussion between people or groups with different views, in order to reach an agreement.  Negotiations often involve compromise and give-and-take.|例句：The negotiations between the two countries lasted for months.  两国之间的谈判持续了数月。|例句：After lengthy negotiations, they finally reached a settlement.  经过长时间的谈判，他们最终达成了协议。|近义词：discussions, talks, consultations, bargaining|反义词：conflict, confrontation, war|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is holding negotiations with its employees. = 公司正在与员工进行谈判。
Negotiations often involve compromise. = 谈判通常需要妥协。

一般过去时:
The peace negotiations failed to produce a result. = 和谈未能取得成果。
They held several negotiations before reaching an agreement. = 他们在达成协议之前进行了多次谈判。

现在完成时:
They have completed negotiations on the new contract. = 他们已经完成了新合同的谈判。
We have been in negotiations for weeks. = 我们已经谈判了好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
Before the final agreement, they had already held several rounds of negotiations. = 在最终协议达成之前，他们已经进行了数轮谈判。
The parties had been in negotiations for months before the strike. = 罢工前各方已经进行了数月的谈判。

将来时:
Further negotiations are scheduled for next week. = 下周将安排进一步的谈判。
They will begin negotiations with the union tomorrow. = 他们明天将开始与工会进行谈判。

虚拟语气:
If the negotiations were successful, we would avoid a strike. = 如果谈判成功，我们将避免罢工。
I wish the negotiations had been more productive. = 我希望谈判更有成效。

被动语态:
The agreement was reached after lengthy negotiations. = 经过长时间的谈判，协议达成。
The terms of the contract are currently being negotiated. = 合同条款目前正在谈判中。

疑问句:
Are the negotiations still ongoing? = 谈判还在进行吗？
What is the status of the negotiations? = 谈判的现状如何？

否定句:
The negotiations did not lead to a resolution. = 谈判没有导致解决。
They failed to reach an agreement despite extensive negotiations. = 尽管进行了广泛的谈判，他们仍未能达成协议。
