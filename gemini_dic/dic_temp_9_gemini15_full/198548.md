shrubbage:/ˈʃrʌbɪdʒ/| n. |灌木丛；矮树丛 |A mass or collection of shrubs.  It often implies a somewhat unkempt or overgrown state. |The garden was overrun with shrubbage. 花园里长满了杂乱的灌木丛。 The path was barely visible beneath the dense shrubbage. 小路在茂密的灌木丛下几乎看不见了。|undergrowth, bushes, thicket |open field, grassland|名词
