reboil:/ˌriːˈbɔɪl/| vt.|再煮沸，重新煮沸|To boil again.|The chef reboiled the sauce to thicken it.  厨师再次煮沸酱汁使其变浓稠。|The soup needs to be reboiled. 这汤需要重新煮沸。|boil, simmer|freeze, cool
