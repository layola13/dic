non-quality:/ˈnɑːnˈkwɑːləti/| n.|非质量，非品质特性|lack of quality; characteristics that are not related to quality.|The report highlighted several areas of non-quality in the product.这份报告强调了产品中几个非质量方面的问题。|近义词：defect, flaw, imperfection|反义词：quality, excellence


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense variations aren't applicable.  The usage focuses on its role in a sentence.

Example 1:  The manufacturer addressed the non-quality issues promptly. 制造商迅速解决了非质量问题。

Example 2:  Excessive non-quality led to customer dissatisfaction. 过多的非质量导致客户不满。


PHRASE->
There aren't common idiomatic phrases directly using "non-quality."  However, related phrases could be:

lack of quality:  This directly conveys the meaning of non-quality.  缺乏质量
quality control: This is the opposite, focusing on ensuring quality.  质量控制
poor workmanship: This describes a result of non-quality.  粗制滥造
inferior product: This describes a product with non-quality aspects.  劣质产品
areas for improvement: This points out aspects that lack quality.  改进空间
