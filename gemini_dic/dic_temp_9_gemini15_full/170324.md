providence's:/ˈprɒvɪdənsɪz/ | n. | 天意；神意；远见；节俭；供应 | The guidance or care provided by God.  Providence often refers to God's protective care and guidance.  It can also mean foresight or prudence. | 例句：The success of the project was attributed to providence.  这个项目的成功归功于天意。


例句：Many believe their good fortune is due to divine providence. 许多人相信他们的好运是由于神意。


近义词：foresight, prudence, divine guidance, fate, destiny


反义词：negligence, carelessness, chance, accident


词性：名词 (possessive form of providence)
