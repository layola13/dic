novella:/nəˈvɛlə/ | n. |中篇小说 |A novella is a work of fiction longer than a short story but shorter than a novel, typically ranging from 17,000 to 40,000 words.  It often focuses on a single plotline or a limited number of characters, and may explore a theme or idea in greater depth than a short story. |Example:  "The Metamorphosis" by Franz Kafka is a famous example of a novella.  "变形记"是卡夫卡著名的中篇小说。|近义词：short story, novelette |反义词：novel
