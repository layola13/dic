despond:/dɪˈspɒnd/| adj.|沮丧的；失望的|Feeling or showing discouragement or depression.|He slumped into a chair, despondent and defeated. 他无力地瘫坐在椅子上，沮丧而失败。|近义词：dejected, depressed, disheartened|反义词：cheerful, optimistic, hopeful|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is despondent about his future. = 他对自己的未来感到沮丧。
She feels despondent after the exam. = 考试后她感到沮丧。


一般过去时:
He was despondent after losing his job. = 他失业后很沮丧。
They were despondent because of the bad news. = 因为这个坏消息他们很沮丧。


现在完成时:
She has been despondent since her dog died. = 自从她的狗死了以后，她一直很沮丧。
He has remained despondent despite our encouragement. = 尽管我们鼓励他，但他仍然沮丧。


过去完成时:
He had been despondent for weeks before he finally sought help. = 在他最终寻求帮助之前，他已经沮丧了好几个星期了。
They had become despondent after many failed attempts. = 经过多次失败的尝试后，他们变得沮丧了。


将来时:
I fear he will be despondent if he doesn't get the promotion. = 我担心如果他得不到晋升，他会很沮丧。
She will likely be despondent if her project fails. = 如果她的项目失败，她很可能会沮丧。


虚拟语气:
If I failed, I would be despondent. = 如果我失败了，我会很沮丧。
I wish I weren't so despondent. = 我希望我不那么沮丧。


被动语态:
(Despondent is rarely used passively.)  He was left feeling despondent. = 他被留下了沮丧的感觉。(这里是被动语态，但并非despondent本身被动)
(Despondent is rarely used passively.)  The situation left him despondent. = 这种情况使他感到沮丧。(这里是被动语态，但并非despondent本身被动)


疑问句:
Is he despondent about the results? = 他对结果感到沮丧吗？
Are you feeling despondent lately? = 你最近感到沮丧吗？


否定句:
He is not despondent; he's just tired. = 他并不沮丧；他只是累了。
She was not despondent, but rather determined. = 她并不沮丧，而是下定决心。


PHRASE->
