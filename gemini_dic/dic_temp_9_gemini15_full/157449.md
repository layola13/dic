pirouetted:/ˌpɪruˈɛtɪd/| v.|旋转（尤指芭蕾舞演员）|To spin or twirl, especially in ballet.  The dancer pirouetted gracefully across the stage.|近义词：twirled, spun, revolved|反义词：stood still, remained stationary
