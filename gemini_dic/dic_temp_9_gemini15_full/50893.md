desertify:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪ/| vt.|沙漠化；使变成沙漠|To transform (land) into desert; to make barren or unproductive.  The process involves the loss of fertile land, often due to deforestation, overgrazing, or unsustainable agricultural practices. |例句：Overgrazing has desertified vast areas of the Sahel region.  过度放牧使萨赫勒地区的大片土地沙漠化了。
例句：Years of drought have desertified the once-fertile farmland.  多年的干旱使曾经肥沃的农田变成了沙漠。|近义词：aridify, dry up|反义词：reforest, irrigate|动词
