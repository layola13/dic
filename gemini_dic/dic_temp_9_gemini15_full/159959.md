polypode:/ˈpɒlɪpəʊd/| n. |多足类植物，多足蕨 |A polypode is a type of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by creeping rhizomes and simple or pinnatifid fronds.  They are often epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks. |Example:  Many species of polypode thrive in humid, shaded environments. 许多种类的多足蕨在潮湿阴凉的环境中茁壮成长。|近义词：fern (泛指)|反义词：无
