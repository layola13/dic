finisher:/ˈfɪnɪʃər/| n.|完成者；收尾者；终结者|A person or thing that finishes something.  Someone or something that completes a task or process.|例句：He was a fast finisher and always completed his work ahead of schedule. 他是一个工作效率很高的人，总是提前完成工作。
He's a strong finisher in the race. 他是比赛中强劲的冲刺者。|近义词：completer,concluder|反义词：starter, beginner
