indorsable:/ɪnˈdɔːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可背书的，可认可的 | Capable of being endorsed;  worthy of approval or support.  It refers to something that can be signed on the back to transfer ownership or liability, or something that deserves endorsement or approval. | Example: The check was indorsable, allowing me to transfer it to another person.  这张支票是可以背书的，允许我把它转让给其他人。 The proposal was indorsable due to its innovative approach.  由于其创新的方法，该提案是值得认可的。|近义词：transferable, approvable, acceptable | 反义词：unacceptable, unapprovable, nontransferable
