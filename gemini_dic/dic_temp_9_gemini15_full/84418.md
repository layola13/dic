halloweens:/ˌhæləˈwiːnz/ | n. |万圣节(复数) |The word "Halloweens" is the plural form of "Halloween," referring to multiple instances of the festival.  It is less common than using "Halloweens" to refer to multiple years of celebration. |例句：Many people celebrate Halloweens with costumes and trick-or-treating. 许多人穿着戏服挨家挨户要糖果来庆祝万圣节。
例句：We've had some spooky Halloweens over the years.  这些年来，我们经历了一些令人毛骨悚然的万圣节。|近义词：All Hallows' Eve (singular)|反义词：无
