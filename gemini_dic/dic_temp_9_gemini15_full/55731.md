dive-bombed:/ˈdaɪvˌbɒmbd/ | v. |俯冲轰炸；突然猛扑|To attack a target from the air by diving steeply and releasing bombs.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a sudden and forceful attack or descent.|例句：The fighter plane dive-bombed the enemy position.  战斗机俯冲轰炸了敌军阵地。

The hawk dive-bombed the unsuspecting rabbit.  鹰突然猛扑向毫无防备的兔子。|近义词：bomb, attack, swoop, plummet|反义词：ascend, climb, soar|
