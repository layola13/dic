regatta:/rəˈɡætə/| n.|帆船比赛；划船比赛|A regatta is a series of boat races, especially sailing races.  It often involves multiple races over several days and can attract competitors from various locations.|例句：The annual regatta attracted hundreds of sailors from around the world.  一年一度的帆船比赛吸引了来自世界各地的数百名水手。|近义词：boat race, sailing competition, yachting event|反义词：None
