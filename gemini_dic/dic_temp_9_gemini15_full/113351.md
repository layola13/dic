luxurist: /ˈlʌkʃʊrɪst/ | n. | 奢侈品爱好者，沉迷奢侈的人 | A person who enjoys and often spends a lot of money on luxury goods and experiences.  They prioritize luxury and extravagance in their lifestyle. |  She's a luxurist who owns a collection of designer handbags and travels first class. 她是一位奢侈品爱好者，拥有众多名牌手袋，并且经常乘坐头等舱旅行。|近义词：connoisseur (of luxury), hedonist, spendthrift | 反义词：ascetic, minimalist | 名词
