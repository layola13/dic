export:/ˈɛkspɔːrt/| /ɪkˈspɔːrt/ | vt. n. |出口，输出；输出（数据）|To send goods or services to another country for sale.  |例句：China exports a large quantity of textiles to the United States. 中国向美国出口大量的纺织品。

例句：The software allows you to export your data to a spreadsheet. 该软件允许您将数据导出到电子表格。

近义词：ship, send, transmit

反义词：import


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We export high-quality products. = 我们出口高质量的产品。
The company exports goods to many countries. = 公司向许多国家出口商品。


一般过去时:
They exported a large amount of wheat last year. = 他们去年出口了大量的麦子。
The factory exported its products to Europe. = 工厂将其产品出口到欧洲。


现在完成时:
We have exported over 1000 units this month. = 我们这个月已经出口了1000多件产品。
The business has successfully exported its products to new markets. = 这家企业已成功将其产品出口到新的市场。


过去完成时:
They had exported all the goods before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经出口了所有货物。
The company had exported significant amounts of goods before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，该公司已经出口了大量的商品。


将来时:
We will export more goods next year. = 我们明年将出口更多商品。
The company will export its new product to Asia. = 公司将把它的新产品出口到亚洲。


虚拟语气:
If we exported more, we would make more profit. = 如果我们出口更多，我们将获得更多利润。
I wish we had exported more goods last year. = 我希望我们去年出口更多商品。


被动语态:
A large quantity of tea is exported from India. = 大量的茶叶从印度出口。
Many products are exported to developing countries. = 许多产品出口到发展中国家。


疑问句:
Do you export to other countries? = 你出口到其他国家吗？
What products does your company export? = 贵公司出口什么产品？


否定句:
We don't export to that country. = 我们不向那个国家出口。
The company didn't export any goods last month. = 公司上个月没有出口任何货物。
