pentastomida:/ˌpentəˈstɒmɪdə/| n. |舌形动物门 |Pentastomida is a phylum of parasitic arthropods, commonly known as tongue worms.  They are characterized by their elongated, worm-like bodies and their parasitic lifestyle in the respiratory tracts of vertebrates.
