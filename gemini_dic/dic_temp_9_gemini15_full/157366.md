pipes:/paɪps/| n.| 管子；管道；烟斗 | Tubes or conduits, usually cylindrical, used for conveying liquids, gases, or other materials.  Also refers to a tobacco pipe. |例句：The plumber repaired the pipes under the sink.  水管工修理了水槽下面的管道。

The old man enjoyed smoking his pipe after dinner. 晚饭后，这位老人喜欢抽他的烟斗。|近义词：tubes, conduits, ducts |反义词： (无直接反义词，可根据语境选择反义词，如open space)
