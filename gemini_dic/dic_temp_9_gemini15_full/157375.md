pipetting:/pɪˈpɛtɪŋ/ | n. |移液；移液操作 | The act of using a pipette to transfer a liquid.  This involves drawing a measured amount of liquid into a pipette and then dispensing it into another container. | Example: Accurate pipetting is crucial for successful experiments.  精确的移液对于成功的实验至关重要。  |近义词：liquid transfer, dispensing |反义词：N/A | 名词
