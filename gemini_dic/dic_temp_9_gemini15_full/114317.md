maintains:/meɪnˈteɪnz/ | vt. | 维持；保持；坚持；主张 | To keep something in good condition; to continue to support or claim something. |例句：He maintains a strict diet. 他保持严格的饮食。

例句：She maintains that she is innocent. 她坚持认为自己是无辜的。

近义词：preserve, uphold, sustain, assert | 反义词：neglect, abandon, relinquish, deny | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains a positive attitude. = 他保持积极的态度。
The company maintains high safety standards. = 公司保持着高安全标准。

一般过去时:
She maintained her composure during the crisis. = 在危机期间，她保持了镇静。
They maintained their innocence throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，他们都坚持自己是无辜的。

现在完成时:
He has maintained his weight for years. = 他多年来一直保持着体重。
The government has maintained its position on the issue. = 政府一直坚持其在该问题上的立场。

过去完成时:
She had maintained a diary for ten years. = 她坚持写日记十年了。
They had maintained their friendship despite many disagreements. = 尽管有很多分歧，他们还是保持了友谊。

将来时:
We will maintain regular contact. = 我们将保持定期联系。
The company plans to maintain its market share. = 公司计划保持其市场份额。

虚拟语气:
If he maintained his focus, he would succeed. = 如果他保持专注，他就会成功。
I wish he maintained a cleaner workspace. = 我希望他保持一个更干净的工作空间。

被动语态:
The equipment is maintained regularly. = 设备定期维护。
Order is maintained by the police. = 秩序由警察维持。

疑问句:
Does he maintain contact with his family? = 他与家人保持联系吗？
How do they maintain such high quality? = 他们如何保持如此高的质量？

否定句:
He doesn't maintain a healthy lifestyle. = 他没有保持健康的生活方式。
The company failed to maintain its profitability. = 公司未能保持盈利。

词源:
The word "maintain" comes from the Old French word "maintenir," which is composed of "main" (hand) and "tenir" (to hold).  Therefore, the original meaning literally implied "to hold in hand," evolving to encompass the sense of keeping something in existence, in good condition, or in support.
