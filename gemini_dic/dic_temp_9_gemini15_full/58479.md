dudes:/duːdz/| n.|家伙们，哥们儿们|A dude is a man, especially a young man, often used informally and sometimes playfully.  The plural form, "dudes," refers to a group of men.|Example: "Hey dudes, let's go surfing!"  嘿，哥们儿们，咱们去冲浪吧！|近义词：guys, fellas, men, blokes|反义词：ladies, women
