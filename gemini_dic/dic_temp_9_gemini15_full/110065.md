lichen:/ˈlaɪkən/| n.|地衣|A lichen is a composite organism consisting of a fungus and a photosynthetic partner, typically a green alga or cyanobacterium.  Lichens are found in a wide variety of habitats, from deserts to arctic regions. They play an important role in many ecosystems, contributing to soil formation and providing food and shelter for other organisms.|Example:  The rocks were covered in a variety of colorful lichens.|近义词:moss(苔藓),fungus(真菌)|反义词:None
