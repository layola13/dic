conchies:/ˈkɒntʃiz/| n.|海螺壳；（尤指）大型海螺；（非正式）吸毒者|The word "conchies" refers primarily to the shells of large sea snails, especially the queen conch.  However, it also has a slang meaning, particularly in the context of the Vietnam War, referring to those who avoided military service, often through conscientious objection. This usage carries a negative connotation.|例句：The beach was littered with beautiful conchies. 海滩上散落着许多美丽的贝壳。|近义词：shells, seashells|反义词：N/A
