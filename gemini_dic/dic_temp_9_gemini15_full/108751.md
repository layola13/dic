learned:/lɜːrnd/| adj. v. |  学习到的；有学问的；熟练的 | Learned is the past participle of the verb "learn,"  meaning acquired knowledge or skill through study, experience, or being taught. It can also function as an adjective describing someone with extensive knowledge or skill. |例句：He is a learned professor. 他是一位博学的教授。 She has learned to play the piano. 她学会了弹钢琴。|近义词：educated, scholarly, skilled, proficient |反义词：ignorant, unskilled, unlearned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He learns English every day. = 他每天学习英语。
She learns to cook quickly. = 她很快学会了做饭。


一般过去时:
I learned a lot from that experience. = 我从那次经历中学到了很多。
They learned to swim last summer. = 他们去年夏天学会了游泳。


现在完成时:
I have learned a lot about history. = 我学到了很多关于历史的知识。
She has learned to drive a car. = 她已经学会了开车。


过去完成时:
I had learned the song before the concert. = 我在音乐会之前就学会了这首歌。
They had learned Spanish before traveling to Mexico. = 他们在去墨西哥旅行之前就学会了西班牙语。


将来时:
I will learn to play the guitar next year. = 我明年将学习弹吉他。
She will have learned French by the time she graduates. = 到她毕业的时候，她就会学会法语了。


虚拟语气:
If I had learned harder, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我学习更努力的话，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had learned to play the violin when I was younger. = 我真希望我年轻的时候学会了拉小提琴。


被动语态:
The lesson was learned quickly. = 这节课很快就学会了。
The new software is being learned by the employees. = 员工们正在学习新的软件。


疑问句:
Have you learned anything new today? = 你今天学到什么新东西了吗？
Did she learn to ride a bicycle as a child? = 她小时候学会骑自行车了吗？


否定句:
I haven't learned how to use this software yet. = 我还没学会如何使用这个软件。
He didn't learn to read until he was seven. = 他直到七岁才学会阅读。
