microwaved:/ˈmaɪkroʊveɪvd/| adj. | 微波炉加热过的 | Cooked or heated in a microwave oven.  | Example: I had a microwaved dinner last night.  我昨晚吃的是微波炉加热的晚餐。|近义词：heated, warmed | 反义词：raw, uncooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He microwaves his lunch every day. = 他每天都用微波炉加热午餐。
She microwaves leftovers for a quick meal. = 她用微波炉加热剩菜做一顿快餐。

一般过去时:
I microwaved the soup for two minutes. = 我把汤在微波炉里加热了两分钟。
They microwaved the pizza until it was hot. = 他们把披萨放在微波炉里加热直到变热。

现在完成时:
We have microwaved the potatoes several times today. = 我们今天已经用微波炉加热了几次土豆了。
She has microwaved popcorn for the movie. = 她已经用微波炉爆了玉米花来看电影。

过去完成时:
He had microwaved the vegetables before adding them to the pasta. = 他在把蔬菜添加到意大利面之前已经用微波炉加热过了。
They had microwaved the coffee to warm it up. = 他们已经用微波炉加热咖啡来使其变暖。

将来时:
I will microwave the rice before serving. = 我会在上菜前用微波炉加热米饭。
She will microwave the fish for a quick lunch. = 她会用微波炉加热鱼做个快餐。

虚拟语气:
If I had a microwave, I would microwave the leftovers. = 如果我有微波炉，我会用微波炉加热剩菜。
I wish I had microwaved the pizza longer. = 我希望我把披萨在微波炉里加热更久一点。

被动语态:

The vegetables are microwaved briefly to retain nutrients. = 蔬菜在微波炉里快速加热以保留营养。

疑问句:
Did you microwave the vegetables? = 你用微波炉加热蔬菜了吗？

否定句:

词源:
The word "microwaved" is derived from "microwave," which combines "micro-" (meaning very small) and "wave" (referring to electromagnetic waves).  The term reflects the use of short electromagnetic waves in the cooking process.
