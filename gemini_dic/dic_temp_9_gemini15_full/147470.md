overoperating:/ˌoʊvərˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ/| v.|过度操作，超负荷运转|The excessive or improper use of something, often leading to malfunction or damage.  It implies operating beyond the recommended limits or in a way that causes strain or inefficiency.  |例句：Overoperating the machinery led to a breakdown.  过度操作机器导致了故障。

例句：The company's overoperating of its servers caused a system crash. 公司服务器过度运行导致系统崩溃。


近义词：overworking, overloading, overstressing, abusing


反义词：underoperating, idling, underutilizing


词性：动词
