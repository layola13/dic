fleeced:/fliːst/| vt.| 欺诈；剪（羊）毛；剥夺；|To fleece someone means to cheat them out of their money or possessions. It can also refer to shearing sheep.  |例句：He was fleeced out of his savings by a con artist. 他被一个骗子骗走了积蓄。

例句：The farmer fleeced his sheep in the autumn. 秋天，农夫剪了他的羊毛。


近义词：cheat, defraud, rob, swindle, shear| 反义词：enrich, benefit, help
