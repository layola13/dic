foley:/ˈfoʊli/| n. |音效制作；脚步声； Foley艺术|The creation of sound effects, especially in filmmaking, by using various props and techniques to mimic sounds.  This includes the creation of realistic sounds of footsteps, body movements, and environmental effects.|例句：The foley artist worked tirelessly to create realistic sound effects for the film.  音效师辛勤工作为电影制作逼真的音效。

The film's realistic sound of rain was achieved through meticulous foley work. 电影中逼真的雨声是通过细致的音效制作实现的。


近义词：sound effects, audio post-production|反义词：silence|词性：名词

词源:
The term "foley" is derived from the name of Jack Foley, a pioneering sound effects artist in Hollywood's early years.  He developed many of the techniques still used today.  The term became associated with the art form itself.
