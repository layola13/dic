outdweller:/ˌaʊtˈdwɛlər/| n. |居住在外的人，外居者|A person who lives outside of a particular area or community, often in a rural or remote location.|例句：The outdwellers often rely on their own resources for food and water.居住在外的人们通常依靠自己的资源获取食物和水。  例句：Many outdwellers commute long distances to work in the city.许多居住在外的人们要长途通勤到城市工作。|近义词：outsider, rustic, country dweller |反义词：inhabitant, city dweller, urbanite |名词
