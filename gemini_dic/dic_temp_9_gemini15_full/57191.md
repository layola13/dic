down-home:/daʊnˈhoʊm/| adj.|家常的；质朴的；乡土气息浓厚的 | Simple, unpretentious, and characteristic of rural life or the home; genuine and sincere.  |例句：The restaurant served down-home cooking that reminded me of my grandmother's. 这家餐馆供应的家常菜让我想起了我祖母做的菜。


例句：He has a down-home charm that makes people instantly like him. 他有一种质朴的魅力，让人立刻喜欢上他。


近义词：rustic, homely, folksy, unpretentious


反义词：sophisticated, pretentious, urban, cosmopolitan


词性:形容词
