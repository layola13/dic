infuscation:/ˌɪnfjuːˈskeɪʃən/| n. |使模糊；使黯淡；使困惑|The act of making something unclear, obscure, or confusing; darkening or obscuring something.  It can refer to the obscuring of meaning, vision, or understanding.|例句：The infuscation of the evidence made it difficult for the jury to reach a verdict. 证据的模糊化使得陪审团难以做出裁决。

The infuscation of the air by the smoke made it hard to breathe. 烟雾使空气变得模糊，难以呼吸。|近义词：obscuration, obfuscation, confusion, darkening |反义词：clarification, illumination, enlightenment
