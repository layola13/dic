hallway:/ˈhɔːlweɪ/ | n. |走廊，过道 | A hallway is a long passage or corridor inside a building, typically connecting rooms.  |例句：The hallway was dimly lit.  走廊灯光昏暗。|例句：We met in the hallway outside the classroom. 我们在教室外的走廊相遇。|近义词：corridor, passage, passageway |反义词：room
