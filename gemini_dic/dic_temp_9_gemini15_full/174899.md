racoon:/ræˈkuːn/|/ˈrækuːn/| n. |浣熊|A raccoon is a small to medium-sized mammal with a ringed tail and a masked face. They are known for their intelligence and dexterity, often using their paws to manipulate objects.  They are omnivores, feeding on a variety of foods. |Example:  The raccoon raided the garbage can last night.  昨晚浣熊袭击了垃圾桶。

The raccoon cleverly opened the jar. 浣熊巧妙地打开了罐子。|近义词：procyon (scientific name) |反义词：无
