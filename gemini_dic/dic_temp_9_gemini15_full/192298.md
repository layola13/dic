screwer:/ˈskruːər/| n., v.| 螺丝刀；拧螺丝的人；欺骗者；破坏者 | A screwer can refer to a tool used to drive screws, a person who screws things together, or someone who exploits or deceives others. It can also describe someone or something that causes damage or disruption. |例句：He used a screwdriver to tighten the screw. 他用螺丝刀拧紧螺丝。|近义词：screwdriver, cheat, saboteur |反义词：helper, benefactor

USAGE->

He screws the lid onto the jar. = 他把盖子拧到罐子上。
She screws the bolts into the wood. = 她把螺栓拧进木头里。
