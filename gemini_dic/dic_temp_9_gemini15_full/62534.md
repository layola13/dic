enduringly:/ɪnˈdʊərɪŋli/| adv. |持久地；永久地；经久地|  lasting for a long time; permanently.  The word emphasizes the lasting nature and impact of something.|例句：His influence on the art world is enduringly felt. 他的影响在艺术界持久地存在着。

例句：The city's enduringly beautiful architecture draws tourists from around the globe.  这座城市经久不衰的美丽建筑吸引着来自世界各地的游客。


近义词：lastingly, permanently, perpetually, incessantly


反义词：briefly, temporarily, fleetingly


词性: 副词
