off-site:/ˌɔːfˈsaɪt/| adj.|异地的；场外的；不在现场的|Located or occurring away from the usual or main place of work or activity.  It refers to something that happens outside of a company's main office or usual working location.|例句：The off-site meeting was held at a resort.  异地会议在度假村举行。

例句：We'll need to arrange for off-site data backup. 我们需要安排异地数据备份。


近义词：remote, external, away from the office, out-of-office

反义词：on-site, in-house, local


词性：形容词
