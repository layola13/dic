chunks:/tʃʌŋks/| n.|块；部分；大块；(数据的)数据块|Pieces or sections of something larger; a significant portion or amount.  It can also refer to a segment of data.|例句：He chopped the vegetables into chunks. 他把蔬菜切成块状。

例句：The article was broken down into manageable chunks. 这篇文章被分成易于管理的部分。


近义词：pieces, segments, portions, bits, fragments|反义词：whole, entirety
