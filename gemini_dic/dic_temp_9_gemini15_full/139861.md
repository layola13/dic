octylic:/ɒkˈtɪlɪk/ | adj. | 辛酸的，辛酯的 | Relating to or derived from octyl or caprylic acid, an eight-carbon saturated fatty acid.  Octylic compounds are often used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food additives. | Example:  The lotion contained octylic acid derivatives to moisturize the skin.  这款乳液含有辛酸衍生物以滋润肌肤。|近义词：caprylic | 反义词：无
