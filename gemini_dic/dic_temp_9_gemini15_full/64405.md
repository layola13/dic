ergat:/ˈɜːrɡæt/| n.|工蚁，职蚁|A sterile female ant that performs tasks other than reproduction, such as foraging, nest building, and caring for young.|Example:  The ergat tirelessly carried a crumb back to the nest.  工蚁不知疲倦地把面包屑带回巢穴。|近义词：worker ant|反义词：queen ant
