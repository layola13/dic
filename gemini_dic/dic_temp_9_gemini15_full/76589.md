gallinaceous:/ˌɡælɪˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|  gallinaceous | 鸡形的；雉科的；类似鸡的 | Relating to or characteristic of birds of the order Galliformes, which includes chickens, turkeys, pheasants, and quails.  These birds are typically ground-dwelling, have strong legs, and are often game birds. | Example:  Many gallinaceous birds are raised for their meat.  许多鸡形鸟类是为肉类而饲养的。|近义词：fowl-like, poultry-like |反义词：None specifically, but words describing birds of different orders could be considered antonyms depending on the context.
