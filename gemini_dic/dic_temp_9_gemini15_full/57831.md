dressed:/drɛst/ | adj. |穿着衣服的；打扮好的；盛装的 |  Wearing clothes; adorned or decorated.  |例句：The children were all dressed in their best clothes for the party. 孩子们都穿着最好的衣服参加聚会。 |例句：She was dressed in a beautiful red gown. 她穿着一条美丽的红色长袍。|近义词：clad, attired, adorned |反义词：undressed, naked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dressed in a suit. = 他穿着西装。
The table is dressed for a formal dinner. = 这张桌子布置好了正式晚餐。

一般过去时:
She was dressed in a white dress. = 她穿着白色连衣裙。
They were dressed for the cold weather. = 他们穿着保暖的衣服以应对寒冷天气。

现在完成时:
He has been dressed for hours. = 他已经穿好衣服好几个小时了。
The room has been dressed beautifully for the occasion. = 房间为这次场合装饰得很漂亮。

过去完成时:
She had been dressed and ready before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经穿戴整齐，准备好了。
The hall had been dressed with flowers before the ceremony. = 典礼前，大厅已经用鲜花装饰好了。

将来时:
He will be dressed in his uniform tomorrow. = 他明天将穿着制服。
The shop will be dressed for Christmas next week. = 商店下周将为圣诞节装饰起来。

虚拟语气:
If he were dressed properly, he wouldn't be so cold. = 如果他穿戴得体，他就不会这么冷了。
I wish I were dressed more appropriately for this event. = 我希望我穿得更适合这次活动。

被动语态:
The dolls are dressed by their owner. = 这些娃娃是由它们的主人打扮的。
The stage was dressed with lights and props. = 舞台用灯光和道具装饰好了。

疑问句:
Is she dressed for the occasion? = 她穿得适合这个场合吗？
Are the children all dressed? = 孩子们都穿好衣服了吗？

否定句:
He is not dressed for the weather. = 他穿的衣服不适合这个天气。
The cake was not dressed with icing. = 蛋糕没有涂上糖霜。
