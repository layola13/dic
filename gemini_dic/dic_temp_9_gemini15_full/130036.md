non-participating: /ˌnɒn pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不参与的，非参与的 | Not taking part or involved.  This adjective describes something or someone that is not involved in a particular activity, process, or group. | Example:  He remained non-participating in the heated debate. 他在激烈的辩论中保持不参与。The non-participating members were sent a summary of the meeting. 没有参加会议的成员收到了会议总结。 | 近义词：uninvolved, inactive, passive | 反义词：participating, involved, active


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The employees are non-participating in the company's new initiative. = 员工们没有参与公司的新计划。
He remains non-participating in most social events. = 他大部分社交活动都不参与。


一般过去时:
She was non-participating in the school's fundraiser. = 她没有参与学校的募捐活动。
They were non-participating observers during the experiment. = 他们在实验中是旁观者，没有参与。


现在完成时:
He has been non-participating in the project for several weeks. = 他几周来没有参与这个项目。
The company has been non-participating in industry conferences recently. = 公司最近没有参加行业会议。


过去完成时:
She had been non-participating in the discussions leading up to the decision. = 在做出决定之前的讨论中，她一直没有参与。
They had been non-participating for so long that their opinions were irrelevant. = 他们已经很久没有参与了，以至于他们的意见无关紧要。


将来时:
He will be non-participating in the upcoming marathon. = 他将不参加即将到来的马拉松比赛。
They will remain non-participating until further notice. = 在另行通知之前，他们将保持不参与。


虚拟语气:
If he were non-participating, the outcome would be different. = 如果他不参与，结果将会不同。
I wish she had been non-participating in that risky venture. = 我希望她没有参与那场冒险的投资。


被动语态:
His non-participation was noted by the organizers. = 组织者注意到了他的不参与。
Her non-participating status was confirmed by the committee. = 委员会确认了她不参与的地位。


疑问句:
Is he non-participating in the project? = 他没有参与这个项目吗？
Are they non-participating by choice or by force? = 他们是不参与，还是被迫不参与？


否定句:
He is not non-participating; he's actively involved. = 他并非不参与；他积极参与其中。
They were not non-participating; they were just quiet observers. = 他们并非不参与；他们只是安静的观察者。


PHRASE->
