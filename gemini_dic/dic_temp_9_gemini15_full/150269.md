panduriform:/pænˈdʒʊrɪfɔːrm/| adj. |琵琶形的；提琴形的|Resembling a pandura (a type of ancient stringed instrument) in shape;  usually applied to leaves or other elongated objects that are broadest near the base and taper towards the apex, often with a slight curve.|The panduriform leaves of the plant were easily identifiable.  这植物琵琶形的叶子很容易辨认。 The geologist described the unusual panduriform rock formation. 地质学家描述了这种奇特的琵琶形岩石构造。|近义词：fiddle-shaped, lyrate|反义词：linear, lanceolate
