scathingly:/ˈskeɪðɪŋli/| adv.|严厉地，尖刻地，刻薄地 |  severely or sharply critical; bitterly. It describes the manner in which something is said or done, conveying harshness and criticism. |例句：He scathingly criticized the government's handling of the crisis. 他严厉批评了政府处理危机的措施。

例句：She scathingly dismissed his proposal as unrealistic. 她尖刻地驳斥了他的提议，认为不切实际。|近义词：bitterly, sharply, harshly, severely, acrimoniously |反义词：mildly, gently, kindly, approvingly |词性：副词
