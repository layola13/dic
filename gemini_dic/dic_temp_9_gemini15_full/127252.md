nautilus:/ˈnɔːtɪləs/|/ˈnɔːtɪlʌs/| n. |鹦鹉螺；（尤指）船蛸|A nautilus is a marine mollusk with a spiral shell.  Nautiluses are known for their beautiful, chambered shells. The term can also refer specifically to the genus Nautilus, which includes several species. |例句：The nautilus shell is a marvel of natural engineering.  鹦鹉螺壳是自然工程的奇迹。

The deep-sea nautilus is a fascinating creature. 深海鹦鹉螺是一种迷人的生物。|近义词：cephalopod, mollusk |反义词：None specifically.
