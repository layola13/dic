reassimilating: /ˌriːəˈsɪməleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再同化；重新吸收 | The process of absorbing or integrating something again, often after a period of separation or exclusion.  This often refers to cultural or social integration. | 例句：The immigrant community is slowly reassimilating into the mainstream culture.  = 移民社区正在慢慢地重新融入主流文化。

The government is attempting to reassimilate refugees into society. = 政府正试图将难民重新融入社会。


近义词：reintegrating, reabsorbing, re-incorporating | 反义词：segregating, isolating, excluding | 词性：动词
