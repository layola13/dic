megascan:/ˈmɛɡəˌskæn/| n.|巨型扫描，大型扫描|A high-resolution 3D scan of a real-world object or environment, often used in computer graphics and game development.  It provides incredibly detailed textures and geometry.|Example:  The game developers used a megascan of a forest to create a realistic virtual environment. 游戏开发者使用森林的巨型扫描来创建逼真的虚拟环境。|近义词：high-resolution scan, detailed 3D scan|反义词：low-resolution scan, simple 3D model|名词
