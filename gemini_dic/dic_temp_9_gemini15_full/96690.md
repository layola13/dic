inebrious:/ɪˈniːbriəs/| adj.|醉醺醺的；令人陶醉的 | intoxicated; exhilarating; causing intoxication | The inebrious revelers sang loudly and danced wildly.  醉醺醺的狂欢者们大声唱歌，疯狂跳舞。 |The inebrious atmosphere of the party was infectious.  派对上令人陶醉的氛围极具感染力。|intoxicated, tipsy, drunk |sober, temperate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is inebrious after drinking too much wine. 他喝太多酒后醉醺醺的。
The atmosphere in the bar is inebrious. 酒吧里的气氛令人陶醉。


一般过去时:
She was inebrious after the celebratory dinner. 庆祝晚餐后她醉醺醺的。
The concert was inebrious, leaving the audience exhilarated. 音乐会令人陶醉，让观众兴奋不已。


现在完成时:
He has become inebrious on several occasions. 他曾多次醉酒。
The perfume has an inebrious scent that fills the room. 香水散发着令人陶醉的香味，充满整个房间。


过去完成时:
They had become inebrious before the accident happened. 事故发生前他们已经醉了。
She had been inebrious all night. 她整晚都醉醺醺的。


将来时:
He will be inebrious by the end of the party. 派对结束时他会醉了。
The new wine will have an inebrious effect on many people. 新酒会让很多人感到陶醉。


虚拟语气:
If he drank more, he would be inebrious. 如果他喝得更多，他会醉的。
If the music were more intense, the atmosphere would be more inebrious. 如果音乐更强烈，气氛会更令人陶醉。


被动语态:
He was inebriated by the potent cocktail. 他被烈性鸡尾酒灌醉了。(Note:  While "inebrious" is usually not used passively, this illustrates the concept.)
The audience was captivated by the inebrious performance. 观众被令人陶醉的表演迷住了。(Note:  Here "inebrious" modifies the performance, not the audience's state.)


疑问句:
Is he inebrious? 他醉了吗？
Is the aroma of the flowers inebrious?  花的香味令人陶醉吗？


否定句:
He is not inebrious; he's just tired. 他没醉，只是累了。
The atmosphere was not inebrious; it was rather subdued.  气氛并不令人陶醉，而是相当压抑。
