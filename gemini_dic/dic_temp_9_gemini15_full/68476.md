families:/ˈfæməliz/ | n. | 家庭；家族；族；科 | The plural form of "family," referring to multiple family units or groups of related individuals or things.  It can also refer to a group of related species (in biology). |例句：Many families attended the school picnic. 许多家庭参加了学校野餐。  The cat family includes lions, tigers, and domestic cats. 猫科动物包括狮子、老虎和家猫。|近义词： households, clans, kindreds |反义词：  (no direct antonym, but could use words implying isolation or lack of kinship, such as solitude, individual) | 名词
