megalopolis:/mɛɡəˈlɒpəlɪs/ | n. | 大都市区，超级城市 | A continuous urban area formed by the merging of several large cities and their suburbs.  It is characterized by high population density, extensive infrastructure, and complex social and economic systems. | Example: Tokyo-Yokohama is a sprawling megalopolis. 东京-横滨是一个庞大的大都市区。  The northeastern megalopolis of the United States stretches from Boston to Washington, D.C.  美国东北部的大都市区从波士顿延伸到华盛顿特区。|近义词：conurbation, metropolis |反义词：rural area, small town
