outpriced: /ˌaʊtˈpraɪst/ | adj. | 价格过高的，比…贵 |  More expensive than something else; exceeding in price.  | The new model is overpriced compared to its predecessor. 新款车型比旧款贵。 |  expensive, costly, dear | cheap, inexpensive, affordable | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The boutique is overpriced. = 这家精品店价格过高。
These shoes are overpriced. = 这些鞋价格过高。

一般过去时:
The tickets were overpriced for the event. = 这次活动的票价太贵了。
The hotel room was overpriced. = 这个酒店房间价格过高。

现在完成时:
They have overpriced their products this year. = 他们今年的产品定价过高。
The company has overpriced its services. = 这家公司对其服务定价过高。

过去完成时:
The restaurant had overpriced its menu items last month. = 上个月这家餐厅的菜单价格过高。
They had overpriced their goods, resulting in low sales. = 他们把商品定价过高，导致销量低迷。

将来时:
They will likely overpriced the next generation of smartphones. = 他们很可能会对下一代智能手机定价过高。
The tickets will be overpriced if they don't increase the seating capacity. = 如果他们不增加座位数量，票价将会过高。


虚拟语气:
If they had not overpriced the tickets, more people would have attended. = 如果他们没有把票价定得那么高，就会有更多人来参加。
I wish they hadn't overpriced the dress; I really wanted it. = 我真希望他们没有把那件裙子定价那么高；我真的很想要它。

被动语态:
The goods were overpriced by the retailer. = 这些商品被零售商定价过高。
The services are often overpriced by the company. = 这家公司的服务经常被定价过高。


疑问句:
Are these products overpriced? = 这些产品价格过高吗？
Is the service overpriced compared to its competitors? = 与竞争对手相比，这项服务价格过高吗？


否定句:
The restaurant is not overpriced, given its location and quality. = 考虑到这家餐厅的位置和质量，它的价格并不算高。
The items are not overpriced; they are reasonably priced. = 这些商品的价格并不算高；它们的价格合理。
