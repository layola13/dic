humaner: /ˈhjuːmənər/ | adj. | 更有人情味的；更仁慈的 | More humane; showing greater compassion or kindness. | The new policy is a humaner approach to the problem.  新的政策是对这个问题更有人情味的方法。 | 近义词：kinder, more compassionate, more merciful | 反义词：cruel, inhumane, brutal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is humaner than he used to be. 他比以前更有人情味了。
She acts humaner towards animals now. 她现在对动物更仁慈了。


一般过去时:
He was humaner in his judgment yesterday. 他昨天的判断更有人情味。
They were humaner to the prisoners than expected. 他们对囚犯比预期的更仁慈。


现在完成时:
The organization has become humaner in its approach. 这个组织在处理方法上变得更有人情味了。
The government has shown itself to be humaner in recent years. 政府近年来表现得更有人性化了。


过去完成时:
They had been humaner in their previous dealings. 他们之前的处理方式更有人情味。
She had become far humaner after her experience.  她经历之后变得更加仁慈。


将来时:
The judge will be humaner in his sentencing. 法官的判决将更有人情味。
The company will hopefully be humaner to its employees.  公司有望对员工更仁慈。


虚拟语气:
If he were humaner, he would understand. 如果他更有人情味，他会理解的。

被动语态:
