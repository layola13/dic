delve:/delv/| v.|深入研究；探究|To search deeply and thoroughly into a subject, question, etc.; to investigate thoroughly.|例句：She delved into the archives to find information about her family history. 她深入研究档案以查找有关家族历史的信息。

例句：The detective delved into the case, determined to find the truth.  侦探深入调查此案，决心找到真相。


近义词：investigate, explore, probe, examine|反义词：superficially examine, skim

He delves into the mysteries of the universe. = 他深入研究宇宙的奥秘。
She delves into old books for inspiration. = 她深入钻研旧书以寻找灵感。

一般过去时:

They delved into the problem until they found a solution. = 他们深入研究这个问题直到找到解决方案。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
