comfortable:/ˈkʌmftəbl/| adj.|舒适的，舒服的，自在的|Feeling physical ease and relaxation; not causing discomfort.|The armchair is very comfortable.|近义词：cozy, pleasant, restful|反义词：uncomfortable, uneasy, awkward


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sofa feels comfortable. = 这沙发感觉很舒服。
This chair is comfortable to sit in. = 这椅子坐起来很舒服。

一般过去时:
The bed was comfortable last night. = 昨晚床很舒服。
I felt comfortable in my new clothes. = 我穿着新衣服感觉很舒服。

现在完成时:
I have been comfortable since moving to this apartment. = 自从搬到这套公寓以来，我一直很舒适。
We have found a comfortable way to work from home. = 我们找到了一种舒适的在家工作方式。

过去完成时:
I had been comfortable until the air conditioning broke. = 直到空调坏了，我才感到不舒服。
They had felt comfortable in their old house before the renovations. = 在装修之前，他们一直觉得老房子很舒适。

将来时:
The new house will be very comfortable. = 新房子将会非常舒适。
I will feel more comfortable after I finish this project. = 完成这个项目后我会感觉更舒服。

虚拟语气:
If I had a comfortable bed, I would sleep better. = 如果我有一张舒服的床，我会睡得更好。
I wish I were more comfortable in social situations. = 我希望我在社交场合更自在些。

被动语态:
The room is made comfortable with soft lighting. = 房间用柔和的灯光营造出舒适的氛围。
The temperature was kept comfortable throughout the meeting. = 会议期间温度一直保持舒适。

疑问句:
Is the chair comfortable? = 这椅子舒服吗？
Are you comfortable with the arrangements? = 你对这些安排满意吗？

否定句:
The shoes are not comfortable to wear. = 这鞋子穿起来不舒服。
I don't feel comfortable asking him for help. = 我不太好意思向他求助。
