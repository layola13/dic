prefabricate:/ˌpriːˈfæbrɪkeɪt/| vt.|预制；预构；捏造；伪造|To manufacture (a component or structure) in advance, typically off-site, ready for assembly or installation.|The company prefabricates the houses in a factory before transporting them to the construction sites.|近义词：preassemble, construct beforehand|反义词：construct on-site, fabricate (in the sense of forging something false)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They prefabricate the walls in a factory. = 他们在工厂里预制墙体。
The company prefabricates modular homes. = 这家公司预制模块化房屋。

一般过去时:
The builders prefabricated the roof sections. = 建筑工人预制了屋顶部分。
We prefabricated the components for the bridge. = 我们预制了桥梁的构件。


现在完成时:
They have prefabricated all the necessary parts. = 他们已经预制了所有必要的部件。
The construction company has prefabricated hundreds of units. = 这家建筑公司已经预制了数百个单元。


过去完成时:
They had prefabricated the steel frames before the concrete was poured. = 在浇筑混凝土之前，他们已经预制好了钢架。
The team had prefabricated the modules for faster assembly. = 团队已经预制好了模块，以便更快地组装。


将来时:
We will prefabricate the building's exterior walls. = 我们将预制建筑物的外部墙体。
The factory will prefabricate thousands of units next year. = 工厂明年将预制数千个单元。


虚拟语气:
If we prefabricated the components, the construction would be quicker. = 如果我们预制构件，施工速度会更快。
It would be more efficient if they prefabricated the sections off-site. = 如果他们在场外预制构件，效率会更高。


被动语态:
The house's walls were prefabricated. = 房子的墙体是预制的。
The bridge sections are being prefabricated in the factory. = 桥梁部分正在工厂里预制。


疑问句:
Were the beams prefabricated? = 梁是预制的吗？
Are the components being prefabricated off-site? = 零部件是在场外预制的吗？


否定句:
They did not prefabricate the entire structure. = 他们没有预制整个结构。
The company doesn't prefabricate anything smaller than a module. = 这家公司不预制任何小于模块的东西。
