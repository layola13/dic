chooses:/ˈtʃuːzɪz/| v.|选择，挑选|To select or decide on something or someone from a number of possibilities.|She chooses her clothes carefully.|近义词：selects, picks, opts for|反义词：rejects, refuses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chooses a red apple. = 他选择了一个红苹果。

She chooses her words carefully. = 她仔细地选择措辞。


一般过去时:
They chose a new car last week. = 他们上周选了一辆新车。

I chose the blue dress. = 我选择了蓝色的裙子。


现在完成时:
She has chosen a career in medicine. = 她已经选择了医学作为职业。

He has chosen to remain silent. = 他选择保持沉默。


过去完成时:
They had chosen their team before the game started. = 比赛开始前他们已经选好了队伍。

She had chosen a different path in life. = 她已经选择了一条不同的生活道路。


将来时:
I will choose the best option. = 我将选择最好的方案。

They will choose a date for the wedding soon. = 他们很快就会选择婚礼的日期。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would choose differently. = 如果我是你，我会做出不同的选择。

I wish I had chosen a different major in college. = 我真希望我在大学里选择了不同的专业。


被动语态:
The winner was chosen by a panel of judges. = 获胜者由评审团选出。

A new president will be chosen next year. = 明年将选出一位新总统。


疑问句:
What did you choose for dessert? = 你选择了什么甜点？

Which color did she choose? = 她选择了什么颜色？


否定句:
He didn't choose the right path. = 他没有选择正确的道路。

They didn't choose to participate. = 他们没有选择参与。


PHRASE->
choose from = 从...中选择
choose to = 选择去做...
choose between = 在...之间选择
choose wisely = 明智地选择
cannot choose but = 不得不


词源:
The word "choose" comes from the Old English word "ceosan," meaning "to choose, select, pick."  This Old English word has Germanic roots, related to words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings.
