diploidic: /dɪˈplɔɪdɪk/ | adj. | 二倍体的；双倍体的 | Having two complete sets of chromosomes, one from each parent.  Diploid cells are characteristic of most somatic cells in multicellular organisms. |例句：Human somatic cells are diploid, containing 46 chromosomes. 人体体细胞是二倍体的，含有46条染色体。

例句：Most animals are diploid organisms, inheriting one set of chromosomes from each parent.  大多数动物是二倍体生物，从父母双方各继承一套染色体。


近义词：doubled, twinned

反义词：haploid, monoploid


词性：形容词
