pummelos:/ˈpʌmɛloʊz/| n. |柚子|A pomelo is a large citrus fruit, similar to a grapefruit but larger and sweeter, with a thick rind. It is believed to be one of the original citrus fruits from which other citrus fruits have developed.|The pomelos at the market were enormous and fragrant.  市场上的柚子又大又香。|grapefruit, shaddock|  |名词
