collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/ | v. |合作，协作 |to work jointly on an activity or project |例句：The two companies collaborated on the development of a new product. 两家公司合作开发了一种新产品。
例句：The scientists collaborated on a research project. 科学家们合作进行了一个研究项目。|近义词：cooperate, work together |反义词：compete, oppose |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We collaborate on many projects. = 我们合作许多项目。
They collaborate closely with their partners. = 他们与合作伙伴密切合作。

一般过去时:
We collaborated on a research paper last year. = 我们去年合作撰写了一篇研究论文。
The artists collaborated on a mural. = 这些艺术家合作创作了一幅壁画。

现在完成时:
We have collaborated with them for many years. = 我们与他们合作多年了。
They have collaborated on several successful films. = 他们合作拍摄了几部成功的电影。

过去完成时:
They had collaborated on several projects before this one. = 在这个项目之前，他们已经合作过几个项目了。
We had collaborated extensively before the final presentation. = 在最终演示之前，我们进行了广泛的合作。

将来时:
We will collaborate on a new marketing campaign next month. = 我们下个月将合作开展一个新的营销活动。
They will collaborate with international experts on the study. = 他们将在研究中与国际专家合作。

虚拟语气:
If we collaborated more effectively, we could achieve more. = 如果我们更有效地合作，我们可以取得更多成就。
It would be beneficial if we collaborated on this project. = 如果我们在这个项目上合作将会很有益处。

被动语态:
The project was collaborated on by several teams. = 该项目由几个团队合作完成。
The design was collaboratively developed. = 该设计是合作开发的。

疑问句:
Will you collaborate with me on this task? = 你愿意在这个任务上与我合作吗？
Do they often collaborate with other organizations? = 他们经常与其他组织合作吗？

否定句:
They did not collaborate on the project. = 他们没有参与这个项目的合作。
We chose not to collaborate with that company. = 我们选择不与那家公司合作。


PHRASE->
collaborate with = 与...合作
collaborate on = 合作做...
collaborate to = 合作以...
collaborative effort = 合作努力
collaborative work = 合作作品
