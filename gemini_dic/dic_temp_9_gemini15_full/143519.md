outgreened:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnd/| adj.| 比…更绿，胜过…的绿色 |  Describes something that surpasses another in terms of greenness, either in color intensity, lushness, or environmental friendliness. It implies a competition or comparison of greenness. |例句：The new lawn completely outgreened the old one after the renovation.  翻新后，新的草坪比旧的绿多了。

The rainforest outgreened the surrounding farmland by a significant margin. 雨林的绿色程度远超周围的农田。


近义词：outrivaled in greenness, more verdant, lusher

反义词：less green, browner, more barren


词性：形容词
