computerise:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪz/ | vt. | 使电脑化；用电脑控制 | To equip (a business, process, etc.) with computers or computer systems.  | The company decided to computerise its accounting system. 公司决定将其会计系统电脑化。 | automate, digitize | manual, hand-operated | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They computerise all their records. = 他们把所有记录都电脑化了。
The factory computerises the production line. = 工厂将生产线电脑化了。

一般过去时:
We computerised our office last year. = 我们去年将办公室电脑化了。
The bank computerised its transaction system in 2010. = 该银行在2010年将交易系统电脑化了。

现在完成时:
They have computerised their entire operation. = 他们已经将整个业务电脑化了。
The company has computerised its payroll system. = 公司已经将工资系统电脑化了。

过去完成时:
They had computerised the system before the merger. = 他们在合并之前已经将系统电脑化了。
The government had computerised its tax collection system by the end of the decade. = 到十年底，政府已将税收系统电脑化。


将来时:
We will computerise our marketing department next year. = 我们明年将把市场部电脑化。
The school will computerise its library system in the near future. = 学校不久将把图书馆系统电脑化。


被动语态:
The process is being computerised. = 这个过程正在被电脑化。
The data was computerised quickly and efficiently. = 数据被快速有效地电脑化了。


疑问句:
Have they computerised the billing system yet? = 他们已经将计费系统电脑化了吗？
Will the project be computerised on time? = 该项目能否按时电脑化？


否定句:
They haven't computerised their inventory system yet. = 他们尚未将库存系统电脑化。
