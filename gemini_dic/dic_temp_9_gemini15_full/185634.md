reverberation:/ˌrɛvərˈbeɪʃən/ | n. | 回声；反响；余波 | The persistence of a sound after its source has stopped, caused by repeated reflection or echoing.  It can also refer to the continuing impact or effect of an event or action. |例句：The canyon walls reverberated with the sound of our voices.  峡谷的墙壁回荡着我们的声音。

例句：The reverberations of the scandal continue to shake the government.  这起丑闻的余波仍在震动政府。


近义词：echo, resonance, repercussion, aftereffect

反义词：silence, stillness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sound reverberates through the hall. = 声音在大厅里回荡。
The news reverberates across the country. = 这消息在全国引起反响。


一般过去时:
The explosion reverberated for miles. = 爆炸声回响了几英里。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
