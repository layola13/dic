salvos:/ˈsælvōz/| n.|齐射；一连串；突然袭击|A salvo is a simultaneous discharge of a number of guns or missiles, or a burst of something such as laughter or applause.|例句：The artillery unleashed a salvo of shells.  炮兵发射了一连串炮弹。

例句：The comedian was greeted with a salvo of laughter. 喜剧演员受到了阵阵笑声的欢迎。|近义词：volley, barrage, burst|反义词：single shot, trickle
