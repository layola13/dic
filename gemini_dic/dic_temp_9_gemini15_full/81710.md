greenish:/ˈɡriːnɪʃ/| adj.|略带绿色的，发绿的|Slightly green in color; having a greenish tinge.  The word implies a less intense or pure green than "green".|例句：The sky was greenish-grey before the storm.暴风雨前天空呈灰绿色。

例句：His skin had a slightly greenish tinge after his illness. 他病后皮肤略带青绿色。


近义词：verdigris,  seagreen,  jade,  emerald (depending on shade)
反义词： reddish,  yellowish,  brownish

词性：形容词
