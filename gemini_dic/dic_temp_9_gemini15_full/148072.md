oversaturating: /ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 使过饱和；使过度饱和 |  To saturate to excess; to fill or supply beyond capacity.  The process involves exceeding the saturation point, resulting in a surplus or overflow. | 例句：The market is oversaturating with similar products. 市场充斥着类似的产品。(The market is oversaturated with similar products.  也可以使用过去分词形式)


The constant advertising is oversaturating the consumer.  持续不断的广告使消费者感到厌烦。(The constant advertising is oversaturating the consumer.  也可以使用过去分词形式)


近义词：flood, inundate, deluge, swamp


反义词：undersaturate, deplete

词性：动词
