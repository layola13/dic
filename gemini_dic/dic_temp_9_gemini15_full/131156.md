This term often appears in financial contexts, referring to individuals or entities without outstanding loans. | Example: The study focused on the savings habits of nonborrowers. 这项研究的重点是非借款人的储蓄习惯。|近义词：non-debtor,  unscheduled borrower (in specific contexts) |反义词：borrower, debtor
