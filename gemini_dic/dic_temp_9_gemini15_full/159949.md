polypier:/ˈpɒlɪpiər/| n. |珊瑚礁；珊瑚群体；珊瑚骨骼 |A polypier is the hard, calcareous skeleton or colony of coral polyps. It's formed by the accumulation of the skeletons of many individual polyps over time.  |Example: The diver marveled at the vast polypier, a breathtaking underwater city built by countless tiny creatures.  潜水员惊叹于巨大的珊瑚礁，这是一个由无数微小生物建造的令人惊叹的水下城市。|近义词：coral reef, coral colony |反义词：None
