nongermination: /ˌnɒnˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |  非萌发；不发芽 | The failure of a seed or spore to germinate.  This can be due to various factors such as unfavorable environmental conditions (temperature, moisture, light), seed dormancy, or damage to the seed. | The nongermination of the seeds resulted in a poor crop yield.  非萌发的种子导致歉收。 The high salinity levels caused nongermination of the seeds. 高盐度导致种子不发芽。| germination |  -  |  noun
