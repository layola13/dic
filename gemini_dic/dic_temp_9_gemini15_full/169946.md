prostomium:/ˈprɒstoʊmiəm/| n.|前口叶|The prostomium is the anterior-most segment of the body in many annelids (segmented worms), located above the mouth.  It is often a small, unsegmented lobe or projection that may bear sensory organs such as eyes or antennae. | Example: The prostomium of the earthworm is a small, fleshy lobe that overhangs the mouth.  蚯蚓的前口叶是一个小的肉质叶状体，覆盖在口器之上。|近义词：proboscis (in some contexts)|反义词：pygidium (posterior-most segment)|词性:noun
