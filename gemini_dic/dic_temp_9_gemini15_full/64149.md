equalization:/ˌiːkwəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 均衡；均衡化；平均化 | The process of making something equal or uniform.  It involves adjusting or balancing different elements to achieve a desired level of uniformity or fairness. |例句：The equalization of opportunity is a key goal of many social programs.  机会均等是许多社会项目的主要目标。

例句：Sound equalization can significantly improve the audio quality.  声音均衡可以显著改善音频质量。


近义词：balancing, leveling, adjustment, standardization | 反义词：discrimination, imbalance, disparity | 词性：名词
