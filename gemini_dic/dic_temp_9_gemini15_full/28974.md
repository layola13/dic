cars:/kɑːrz/| n.|汽车，车辆|Motor vehicles designed for carrying passengers on roads.|例句：He owns several luxury cars. 他拥有几辆豪华轿车。|例句：Many cars were damaged in the storm. 暴风雨中许多汽车受损。|近义词：vehicles, automobiles|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drives his car to work every day. = 他每天开车上班。
They have two cars in their garage. = 他们的车库里有两辆车。

一般过去时:
She drove her car to the airport yesterday. = 她昨天开车去了机场。
We bought a new car last month. = 我们上个月买了一辆新车。

现在完成时:
I have driven this car for five years. = 我已经开了这辆车五年了。
They haven't bought a new car yet. = 他们还没有买新车。

过去完成时:
He had driven the car for ten years before it broke down. = 他开了那辆车十年后，它才坏掉。
She had never driven a car before that day. = 在那一天之前，她从未开过车。

将来时:
I will buy a new car next year. = 我明年会买一辆新车。
They will be driving to the beach tomorrow. = 他们明天将开车去海滩。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would travel more. = 如果我有一辆车，我会多旅行。
I wish I had a new car. = 我真希望我有一辆新车。

被动语态:
The car was repaired last week. = 这辆车在上周进行了修理。
The cars are being cleaned now. = 汽车现在正在清洗。

疑问句:
Do you have a car? = 你有车吗？
What kind of car do you drive? = 你开什么类型的车？

否定句:
He doesn't own a car. = 他没有车。
They didn't drive to the party. = 他们没有开车去参加聚会。
