disarrange:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒ/| vt.|弄乱；扰乱；使混乱 |To put out of order; to derange; to throw into confusion.|例句：The storm disarranged the furniture in the room. 暴风雨弄乱了房间里的家具。|例句：Don't disarrange the papers on my desk. 不要弄乱我桌子上的文件。|近义词：disorder, confuse, jumble |反义词：arrange, organize, order
