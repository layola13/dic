frothing:/ˈfrɒðɪŋ/| v.|起泡沫，沸腾，狂怒|The verb "frothing" describes the action of producing froth or foam, or the state of being covered in froth.  It can also be used figuratively to describe someone who is extremely angry or excited, almost to the point of losing control.|例句：The beer was frothing in the glass.  啤酒在杯子里起泡沫了。|例句：He was frothing at the mouth with rage. 他气得咬牙切齿。|近义词：foaming, bubbling, lathering|反义词：calm, still
