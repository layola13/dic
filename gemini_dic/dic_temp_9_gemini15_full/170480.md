pruriency:/ˈprʊəriənsi/| n. |淫秽；好色；性欲|An excessive interest in or preoccupation with sex; lasciviousness.  It often involves a morbid fascination with sexual matters, often expressed in a way that is considered vulgar or offensive.  |例句：The novel was criticized for its gratuitous pruriency. 这部小说因其过度的淫秽内容而受到批评。
The magazine was condemned for its blatant pruriency. 这本杂志因其公然的淫秽内容而受到谴责。|近义词：lewdness, lasciviousness, salaciousness |反义词：chastity, purity, decency |名词
