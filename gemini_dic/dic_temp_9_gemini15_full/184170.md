reskilled:/ˌriːˈskɪld/| v. | 再培训的；重新掌握技能的 | To teach (someone) new skills, especially to enable them to do a different job.  |例句：The company reskilled its workforce to meet the demands of the new technology. 公司对员工进行了再培训以满足新技术的需要。

例句：Many workers were reskilled in order to adapt to the changing job market. 为了适应不断变化的就业市场，许多工人接受了再培训。


近义词： retrained, upskilled,  retooled


反义词： unskilled, untrained


词性：动词
