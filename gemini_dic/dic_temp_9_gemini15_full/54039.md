discussion: /dɪˈskʌʃən/ | n. |讨论，议论 |The act of talking about something with another person or group of people in order to reach a decision or to exchange ideas.  It involves a process of exploring different viewpoints and perspectives. |例句：We had a long discussion about the project. 我们就这个项目进行了长时间的讨论。

例句：The committee's discussion led to a compromise. 委员会的讨论达成了妥协。


近义词：debate, deliberation, conversation, conference, colloquy |反义词：silence, agreement (in the sense of a lack of discussion)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We discuss the problem every week. 我们每周都讨论这个问题。
They discuss their future plans regularly. 他们定期讨论他们的未来计划。


一般过去时:
We discussed the issue yesterday. 我们昨天讨论了这个问题。
They discussed the proposal thoroughly. 他们彻底讨论了这个提案。


现在完成时:
We have discussed this topic many times. 我们已经讨论过这个话题很多次了。
They have discussed the merger since last month. 自从上个月以来，他们一直在讨论合并事宜。


过去完成时:
They had discussed the contract before the meeting. 会议之前他们已经讨论过合同了。
We had discussed the options carefully before making a decision. 我们在做决定之前仔细讨论了各种方案。


将来时:
We will discuss the budget next week. 我们下周将讨论预算。
They will discuss the new policy at the meeting. 他们将在会议上讨论新的政策。


虚拟语气:
If we had discussed the matter earlier, we could have avoided the problem. 如果我们早点讨论这个问题，就能避免这个问题了。
I wish we had discussed the implications more thoroughly. 我希望我们能更彻底地讨论一下其影响。


被动语态:
The issue was discussed at length. 这个问题被详细讨论了。
The proposal will be discussed at the next meeting. 该提案将在下次会议上讨论。


疑问句:
What is being discussed in the meeting? 会议上正在讨论什么？
Was the matter ever discussed? 这件事曾经讨论过吗？


否定句:
We didn't discuss the sensitive topic. 我们没有讨论这个敏感的话题。
They haven't discussed the details yet. 他们还没有讨论细节。


PHRASE->
enter into discussion: 开始讨论
open discussion: 公开讨论
heated discussion: 激烈的讨论
fruitful discussion: 有成效的讨论
under discussion: 正在讨论中
