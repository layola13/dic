irrupt:/ɪˈrʌpt/| v.|突然闯入；突然爆发|To suddenly interrupt; to burst in forcefully; to erupt suddenly.|例句：The protesters irrupted into the meeting.  抗议者突然闯入会议。|例句：Violence irrupted in the streets. 暴力在街上突然爆发。|近义词：burst in, intrude, erupt, break in|反义词：withdraw, retreat, subside
