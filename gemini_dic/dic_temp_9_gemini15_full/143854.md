outplays:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪz/ | vt. |胜过；技高一筹 | To surpass or exceed in skill or performance.  |例句：The young pianist outplayed his opponent with effortless grace. 年轻的钢琴家以轻松优雅的姿态胜过了他的对手。  |例句：The veteran quarterback outplayed the rookie in the crucial game. 资深四分卫在关键比赛中技高一筹。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, excel |反义词：underperform, be outdone


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outplays his opponents every game. = 他每一场比赛都胜过对手。
The team consistently outplays their rivals. = 这个球队总是胜过他们的对手。

一般过去时:
