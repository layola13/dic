celebrater: /ˈsɛləˌbreɪtər/ | n. |庆祝者，庆祝会| A person who celebrates; an enthusiastic participant in a celebration.  |例句：He was a boisterous celebrater at the victory party. 他是庆功宴上一个喧闹的庆祝者。|近义词：celebrant, reveler |反义词：mourner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee celebrates the anniversary every year. = 委员会每年都庆祝周年纪念日。
She celebrates her birthday with a big party. = 她用一个盛大的派对庆祝她的生日。


一般过去时:
They celebrated their wedding anniversary last week. = 他们上周庆祝了结婚周年纪念日。
We celebrated his success with a dinner. = 我们用一顿晚餐庆祝了他的成功。


现在完成时:
We have celebrated many successes this year. = 今年我们庆祝了许多成功。
She has celebrated her graduation with her friends. = 她和朋友们一起庆祝了她的毕业典礼。


过去完成时:
They had celebrated their victory before the news reached the public. = 在消息传到公众之前，他们就已经庆祝了他们的胜利。
We had celebrated the new year before the power went out. = 断电之前我们已经庆祝了新年。


将来时:
We will celebrate our tenth wedding anniversary next year. = 我们明年将庆祝我们的十周年结婚纪念日。
They will celebrate the festival with traditional dances. = 他们将以传统舞蹈庆祝节日。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would celebrate wildly. = 如果我中了彩票，我会疯狂庆祝。
I wish I could celebrate my birthday with my family. = 我希望我能和家人一起庆祝我的生日。


被动语态:
The anniversary was celebrated with a grand feast. = 周年纪念日以盛大的宴会庆祝。
His achievements were celebrated by many people. = 他的成就受到了许多人的赞扬和庆祝。


疑问句:
How will you celebrate your birthday? = 你将如何庆祝你的生日？
Where will they celebrate the holiday? = 他们将在哪里庆祝节日？


否定句:

We didn't celebrate Christmas this year. = 今年我们没有庆祝圣诞节。
