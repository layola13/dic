piercers:/ˈpɪərsərz/| n.|穿孔者；穿孔器|A person who pierces body parts, or a tool used for piercing.|例句：She went to a professional piercer to get her ears pierced. 她去了一家专业的穿孔店穿耳洞。|近义词：body piercer, perforator|反义词：healer, repairer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The piercer uses a sterilized needle. = 穿孔师使用消毒针。
He pierces ears professionally. = 他专业穿耳洞。


一般过去时:
The piercer pierced my nose last week. = 穿孔师上周帮我打了鼻洞。
She pierced her own ears at home. = 她在家自己穿的耳洞。


现在完成时:
I have had my ears pierced twice. = 我已经穿了两次耳洞。
She has pierced her navel. = 她已经打了肚脐环。


过去完成时:
He had already pierced his eyebrow before he went to the party. = 他去参加派对之前已经穿了眉毛洞。
She had never had her tongue pierced before. = 她以前从未穿过舌环。


将来时:
I will get my ears pierced next month. = 我下个月会去穿耳洞。
She will pierce her septum next week. = 她下周会穿鼻中隔。


虚拟语气:
If I were brave enough, I would get my nose pierced. = 如果我足够勇敢，我会去穿鼻洞。
I wish I had pierced my ears earlier. = 我真希望我早点穿耳洞。


被动语态:
My ears were pierced by a professional. = 我的耳洞是由专业人士穿的。
Her nose will be pierced tomorrow. = 她的鼻子明天会被穿孔。


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't pierce his own ears. = 他没有自己穿耳洞。
She doesn't want to pierce her eyebrows. = 她不想穿眉毛洞。
