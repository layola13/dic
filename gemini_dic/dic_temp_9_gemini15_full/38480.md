conchite:/ˈkɒŋkaɪt/| n. | 贝壳石，文石 | Aragonite that has formed as a coating on a conch shell or other marine shell. It is often found in caves and other environments where calcium carbonate is precipitated from water.  | Example: The cave walls were adorned with beautiful formations of conchite.  洞穴墙壁上装饰着美丽的贝壳石构造。|近义词：aragonite, calcite |反义词：无
