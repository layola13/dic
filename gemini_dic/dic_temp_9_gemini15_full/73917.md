forswearing:/fɔːrˈswɛərɪŋ/| v.|发誓放弃；弃绝；否认；避免|The act of formally renouncing or abandoning something, especially a belief, habit, or claim.  It implies a strong and deliberate rejection.|例句：He made a solemn vow, forswearing all violence. 他郑重发誓，放弃一切暴力行为。

例句：She forswore her allegiance to the crown. 她放弃了对王室的效忠。


近义词：renouncing, abandoning, repudiating, abjuring, relinquishing|反义词：affirming, embracing, upholding, clinging to|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forswears smoking every day. = 他每天都发誓戒烟。
They forswear violence in their beliefs. = 他们的信仰中弃绝暴力。


一般过去时:
She forswore her past mistakes. = 她发誓不再犯以前的错误。
He forswore his loyalty to the organization. = 他放弃了他对该组织的忠诚。


现在完成时:
He has forsworn gambling since his losses. = 自从输钱后，他已戒掉了赌博。
She has forsworn all contact with her former associates. = 她已经与她以前的所有伙伴断绝了所有联系。


过去完成时:
He had forsworn alcohol before the accident. = 事故发生前他已经戒酒了。
She had forsworn her ambition to become a lawyer. = 她已经放弃了她成为律师的雄心壮志。


将来时:
He will forswear his claim to the inheritance. = 他将放弃他继承遗产的权利。
She will forswear all forms of revenge. = 她将放弃一切形式的报复。


虚拟语气:
If he forswore his bad habits, he would be a better person. = 如果他放弃他的坏习惯，他会成为更好的人。
I wish she had forsworn that dangerous path. = 我希望她当初没有走上那条危险的道路。


被动语态:
His past was forsworn by him in the interview. = 在采访中，他否认了他的过去。
The treaty was forsworn by the warring factions.  = 交战各方否认了该条约。


疑问句:

Has she forsworn her previous beliefs? = 她是否放弃了她之前的信仰？


否定句:
He did not forswear his right to appeal. = 他没有放弃他的上诉权。
She would not forswear her love for him. = 她不会放弃对他的爱。
