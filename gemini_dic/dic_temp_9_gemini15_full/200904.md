slippage:/ˈslɪpɪdʒ/ | n. |滑动；下滑；差距；疏忽 |A failure to maintain a standard or level; a decline.  The difference between an expected result and an actual result. |例句：There has been a significant slippage in exam results this year. 今年考试成绩出现明显下滑。

There was a slippage in the deadline for the project. 项目截止日期出现了偏差。


近义词：decline, decrease, drop, backsliding, lapse, deviation, shortfall |反义词：improvement, increase, gain, advancement
