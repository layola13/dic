foxfish:/ˈfɒksfɪʃ/ | n. |狐鱼 |A foxfish is a type of fish belonging to the family *Aplodactylidae*, characterized by its elongated body, large head, and often brightly colored.  They are typically found in rocky reefs and kelp forests in the Indo-Pacific region. They are not closely related to foxes. |例句：The diver spotted a vibrant orange foxfish darting among the coral.  潜水员看到一条鲜艳的橙色狐鱼在珊瑚间穿梭。  |近义词：None directly equivalent. |反义词：None directly applicable.
