mentorship: /ˈmɛntʃɔːrʃɪp/ | n. | 导师制；指导；教导 | The act or process of mentoring; guidance or training provided by a mentor.  |  例句：The company offers a robust mentorship program for new employees. 这家公司为新员工提供了一个强大的导师计划。

例句：He benefited greatly from his mentorship with a senior executive. 他从与一位高级主管的指导中受益匪浅。


近义词：guidance, tutelage, coaching, sponsorship

反义词：neglect, abandonment, disregard


词性：名词
