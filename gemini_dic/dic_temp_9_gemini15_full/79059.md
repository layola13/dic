givers:/ˈɡɪvərz/| n.|给予者，捐赠者|People or organizations that give something, such as gifts, money, or time.  It refers to those who are characterized by their generosity and willingness to share.|The anonymous givers donated a large sum of money to the charity. 匿名捐赠者向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。The givers of the awards were all prominent figures in the industry. 颁奖者都是业内知名人士。|donors, benefactors, contributors|receivers, takers|名词
