offerable: /ˈɒfərəbl/ | /ˈɔːfərəbl/ | adj. | 可提供的；可提出的；可供选择的 |

英文解析:Capable of being offered; suitable or fit to be offered.  Something that is offerable is something that can be given or proposed.

例句:

The company has many offerable positions for graduates. 公司有很多可供毕业生选择的职位。

Several solutions were offerable to resolve the conflict.  几个解决冲突的方案是可以提出的。


近义词: available,  proposable,  presentable

反义词: unavailable, unacceptable, unsuitable


词性: adjective
