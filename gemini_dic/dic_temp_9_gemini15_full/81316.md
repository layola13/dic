grapery:/ˈɡreɪpəri/| n. |葡萄园；葡萄种植园|A grapery is a place where grapes are grown, often under cover or in a controlled environment.  It can refer to a vineyard, but often implies a more specialized or intensive cultivation of grapes, perhaps for specific purposes like winemaking or table grapes.|例句：He inherited a beautiful grapery from his grandfather. 他从他祖父那里继承了一个美丽的葡萄园。|例句：The grapery produced award-winning grapes for the local winery. 这个葡萄种植园为当地酒厂生产出了获奖的葡萄。|近义词：vineyard, vineyard, orchard (if also growing other fruits)|反义词：N/A
