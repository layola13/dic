eyeshadow:/ˈaɪˌʃædoʊ/| n. |眼影|A cosmetic powder, cream, or gel used to color the eyelids.  It's applied to enhance the eyes and add depth and definition.|例句：She applied a shimmery eyeshadow to her eyelids. 她往眼睑涂抹了闪亮的 眼影。

例句：The eyeshadow palette contained a range of beautiful colors. 这款眼影盘包含各种美丽的颜色。


近义词：eye paint, eye color


反义词：无


词性：名词
