jazzed:/dʒæzd/| adj.|兴奋的，狂热的|Feeling very excited and enthusiastic.|例句：I'm jazzed about the upcoming trip! 我对即将到来的旅行感到兴奋！|例句：She was totally jazzed after winning the competition. 她在赢得比赛后非常兴奋。|近义词：excited, thrilled, energized, stoked|反义词：depressed, uninterested, bored, apathetic


USAGE->

I am jazzed about the concert. = 我对这场音乐会感到兴奋。
He is jazzed about his new job. = 他对他的新工作感到兴奋。
