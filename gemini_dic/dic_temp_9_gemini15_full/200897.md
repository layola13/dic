slipcover:/ˈslɪpkʌvər/| n. |沙发套，罩子 |A slipcover is a removable cover for furniture, especially sofas and armchairs, usually made of fabric.  It protects the furniture from wear and tear and can also be used to change the look of a room. |Example: She bought a new slipcover for her sofa to freshen up the living room. 她买了一个新的沙发套来装饰客厅。|近义词：cover, protector, casing |反义词：N/A
