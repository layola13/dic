handsomest:/ˈhændsəməst/| adj.|最英俊的，最漂亮的 |Most attractive or beautiful, especially in a conventionally masculine way.  The word implies a combination of physical attractiveness and appealing qualities of character.|例句：He is the handsomest man in the village. 他是村子里最英俊的男人。

例句：She considered him the handsomest actor she'd ever seen. 她认为他是她见过的最英俊的演员。


近义词：most attractive, most beautiful, most stunning, most gorgeous

反义词：ugliest, least attractive, plainest


词性：形容词
