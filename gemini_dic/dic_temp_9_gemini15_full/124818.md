mucinogen:/mjuːˈsɪnədʒən/ | n. |粘蛋白原 |A glycoprotein precursor that is converted into mucin.  Mucinogens are high-molecular-weight glycoproteins found in various secretory cells and are responsible for the production of mucins, which are crucial components of mucus. |Example: The goblet cells in the respiratory tract secrete mucinogen, which hydrates to form mucus and protects the airways.  呼吸道中的杯状细胞分泌粘蛋白原，其水合后形成粘液并保护气道。|近义词：mucus precursor, glycoprotein precursor |反义词：无
