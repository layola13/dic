rober:/ˈroʊbər/| n.|强盗，盗贼|A robber is a person who steals from others, especially by violence or threat.|例句：The robber was caught by the police.  强盗被警察抓住了。|近义词：thief, bandit, burglar|反义词：police officer, law-abiding citizen


USAGE->
This word does not have verb conjugations like "what".  It is a noun and therefore doesn't change tense.


一般现在时:
The robber stole the money. = 强盗偷走了钱。
That robber is dangerous. = 那个强盗很危险。


一般过去时:
The robber escaped last night. = 强盗昨晚逃跑了。
The robber had a gun. = 强盗有一把枪。


现在完成时:
The police have caught the robber. = 警察已经抓住了强盗。
The robber has been identified. = 强盗已被辨认出来。


过去完成时:
The police had already arrived when the robber fled. = 警察到达时，强盗已经逃跑了。
The bank had been robbed before we arrived. = 我们到达之前银行已经被抢劫了。


将来时:
The robber will be arrested soon. = 强盗很快就会被逮捕。
They think the robber will strike again. = 他们认为强盗会再次作案。


虚拟语气:
If I were a robber, I would be rich. = 如果我是强盗，我会很富有。
If the robber hadn't been so careless, he would have gotten away. = 如果强盗不那么粗心，他就会逃脱了。


被动语态:
The money was stolen by a robber. = 钱被强盗偷走了。
The robber was seen by a witness. = 强盗被目击者看到了。


疑问句:
Was the robber armed? = 强盗有武器吗？
Who was the robber? = 强盗是谁？


否定句:
He wasn't a robber. = 他不是强盗。
The robber didn't get away. = 强盗没有逃脱。


PHRASE->
armed robber 武装抢劫犯

词源:
The word "robber" comes from the Old French word "robeor," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "rapere," meaning "to seize" or "to snatch."  The evolution reflects a shift in meaning from simply seizing something to the more criminal act of stealing by force or threat.
