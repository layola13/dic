despoilers:/dɪˈspɔɪlərz/| n.|掠夺者，破坏者|Those who plunder or damage something, especially systematically.  They are often associated with theft, vandalism, or the exploitation of resources.|例句：The despoilers of the ancient city left little but rubble behind.  掠夺这座古城的破坏者只留下瓦砾。

例句：The logging companies were accused of being despoilers of the rainforest.  伐木公司被指控是雨林的破坏者。


近义词：plunderers, pillagers, vandals, destroyers, exploiters|反义词：preservers, protectors, conservators|名词
