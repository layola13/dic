nonproblems:/ˈnɑːnˈprɑːbləmz/| n.|非问题，伪问题|Problems that are not real problems, often stemming from misunderstanding or a lack of perspective.  They are issues that appear significant but are ultimately trivial or easily resolved.|例句：Many of the complaints were nonproblems, easily addressed with a little communication. 许多投诉都是非问题，只需一点沟通就能轻松解决。  The perceived crisis turned out to be a series of nonproblems.  所谓的危机结果是一系列的伪问题。|近义词：trivial matters, minor issues, insignificant problems|反义词：real problems, serious issues, major challenges|名词
