defensed:/dɪˈfɛnsd/| v. | 防御；保卫 | defended的过去式和过去分词 | The castle was well defensed against attack.  城堡防御良好，抵御了攻击。 | protected, guarded, shielded | attacked, assaulted | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defends his country. = 他保卫他的国家。
She defends her beliefs. = 她捍卫她的信仰。


一般过去时:
They defended the city bravely. = 他们勇敢地保卫了这座城市。
He defended himself against the accusations. = 他为自己辩护，反驳了指控。


现在完成时:
We have defended our position successfully. = 我们已成功地捍卫了我们的立场。
She has defended her title for three years. = 她已经连续三年卫冕了冠军头衔。


过去完成时:
They had defended the fort for months before surrendering. = 在投降之前，他们已经坚守堡垒数月了。
He had defended his innocence throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，他一直为自己辩护，证明自己的清白。


将来时:
We will defend our rights. = 我们将捍卫我们的权利。
They will defend the country against invasion. = 他们将保卫国家免受侵略。


虚拟语气:
If he were to defend himself, he would need a good lawyer. = 如果他要为自己辩护，他需要一个好律师。
I wish I had defended myself better. = 我真希望我当时能更好地为自己辩护。


被动语态:
The city was well defended. = 这座城市防御良好。
The proposal was defended by the committee. = 委员会为这项提案进行了辩护。


疑问句:
Did they defend their actions? = 他们为自己的行为辩护了吗？
How will we defend against this attack? = 我们将如何抵御这次攻击？


否定句:
He did not defend himself. = 他没有为自己辩护。
They did not defend the country effectively. = 他们没有有效地保卫国家。


PHRASE->
national defense = 国防
