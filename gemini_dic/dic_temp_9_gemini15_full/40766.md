convincedly:/kənˈvɪnsɪŋli/ | adv. |  确信地；令人信服地 |  In a way that shows one is certain or has no doubt.  It describes the manner in which something is said or done, emphasizing the speaker's or actor's strong belief. |  He spoke convincedly about the benefits of the new program. 他确信地谈论了新计划的好处。

She argued her case convincedly, leaving no room for doubt. 她令人信服地陈述了自己的观点，不容置疑。 | convincingly, persuasively | unconvincingly, doubtingly | 副词
