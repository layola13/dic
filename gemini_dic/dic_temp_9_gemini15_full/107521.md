lander:/ˈlændər/| n.|登陆器，着陆器|A vehicle or device designed to land on a celestial body, such as the Moon or Mars.  It can be crewed or uncrewed.|例句：The lunar lander touched down softly on the moon's surface. 月球着陆器轻轻地降落在月球表面。|例句：The Mars lander sent back stunning images of the Martian landscape.火星着陆器发回了火星景观的惊人图像。|近义词：module, descent module|反义词：launcher, ascender|名词
