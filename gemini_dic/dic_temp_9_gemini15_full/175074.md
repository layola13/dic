radioscopy:/reɪdiˈɒskəpi/ | n. | X射线透视检查；透视术 | The examination of the body by means of X-rays to detect abnormalities.  Radioscopy is a technique used to visualize internal structures in real-time using X-rays. | Example: The doctor performed a radioscopy to check for a fractured bone. 医生做了X射线透视检查以查看骨折。  The patient underwent radioscopy to examine their lungs. 病人接受了透视检查以检查他们的肺部。|近义词：fluoroscopy, X-ray examination | 反义词：N/A
