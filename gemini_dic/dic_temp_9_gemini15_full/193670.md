self-cognition:/ˌsɛlf kɒɡˈnɪʃən/| n. |自我认知，自我觉察 |The understanding and awareness of one's own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.  It involves introspection and self-reflection to understand one's own strengths, weaknesses, motivations, and beliefs. | Example:  Developing a strong sense of self-cognition is crucial for personal growth.  发展强烈的自我认知感对个人成长至关重要。|近义词：self-awareness, self-understanding |反义词：self-deception, unawareness |名词
