overtask:/ˌoʊvərˈtæsk/ | vt. |使承担过重的任务；使工作过度|To give someone more tasks than they can reasonably manage or complete.  It implies a burden or excessive workload.|例句：The manager overtasked his employees with the new project deadline. = 经理因为新的项目截止日期而给他的员工安排了过多的任务。


例句：She felt overtasked by the demands of her job and family. = 她感到工作和家庭的要求让她不堪重负。


近义词：overload, overburden, overwhelm


反义词：underwork, underutilize


词性：动词
