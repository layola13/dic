hyperbolically:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪkli/ | adv. |  夸张地，过分地 | In a hyperbolic manner; using hyperbole.  It describes something said or done in an exaggerated way to create emphasis or humor.  | 例句：He hyperbolically described the storm as the worst in a century. 他夸张地形容这场风暴是百年来最严重的。  | 例句：She spoke hyperbolically about her love for chocolate. 她夸张地谈论了她对巧克力的爱。 | 近义词：exaggeratedly, extravagantly, immoderately | 反义词：understatedly, mildly, moderately | 副词
