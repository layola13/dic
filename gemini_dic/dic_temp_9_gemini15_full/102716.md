jangler:/ˈdʒæŋɡlər/| n. v.| 喧闹的人；喋喋不休的人；（尤指乐器）发出叮当声的人或物 | A jangler is a person who talks incessantly and noisily, or something that makes a rattling or jingling sound.  |例句：The old car was a real jangler, making all sorts of noises as it drove down the street. 这辆旧车真是个吵闹家伙，行驶在街上发出各种噪音。  The noisy children were such janglers; I couldn't concentrate on my work. 那些吵闹的孩子真吵，我无法集中精力工作。|近义词：chatterbox, rattle, noisy person |反义词：silent person, quiet object.
