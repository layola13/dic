coupling:/ˈkʌplɪŋ/ | n., v. |联轴器；联接；结合 |The act of joining or connecting two or more things.  It can refer to a physical device that connects shafts or other mechanical parts, or to the act of joining things in a more abstract sense. |例句：The engine was connected to the wheels by a strong coupling.  发动机通过一个强力联轴器连接到车轮上。

The two companies announced their coupling in a joint press release. 两家公司在联合新闻发布会上宣布合并。|近义词：connection, joining, linkage, union, combination |反义词：separation, disconnection, disjunction
