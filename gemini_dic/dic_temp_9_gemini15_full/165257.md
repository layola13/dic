preinsulates: /ˌpriːˈɪnsjuleɪts/ | v. | 预先隔热 | To insulate beforehand or in advance.  This verb is formed by adding the prefix "pre-" to the verb "insulates," indicating an action performed before something else.  | The builder preinsulated the walls before adding the drywall.  建筑工人先给墙壁做了隔热处理，然后再加石膏板。|  insulate, protect |  (No direct antonym;  the opposite would be to leave uninsulated) | 动词
