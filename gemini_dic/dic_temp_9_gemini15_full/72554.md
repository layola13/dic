flutterable: /ˈflʌtərəbl/ | adj. | 颤抖的；飘动的；易于兴奋的 | Easily moved or agitated; tending to flutter.  The word suggests a lightness and a lack of stability, implying something that is easily swayed or excited. | 例句：The butterfly's wings were flutterable in the gentle breeze.  轻柔的微风中，蝴蝶的翅膀轻轻飘动。

The leaves of the aspen trees were flutterable in the autumn wind. 秋风中，白杨树的叶子飘动不已。


近义词：tremulous, wavering, flighty, skittish | 反义词：stable, steady, calm, composed | 词性：形容词

词源:
The word "flutterable" derives from the verb "flutter," which itself has Old English roots.  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.  The core sense of movement and lightness has remained consistent throughout its evolution.
