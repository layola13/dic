pre-Raphaelite:/ˌpriːræfəˈɪlaɪt/| adj.| 拉斐尔前派的；拉斐尔前派艺术的 | Relating to or denoting the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood, a group of 19th-century English painters who rejected the academic art of their time and sought inspiration in the art of the period before Raphael.  Their work is characterized by its intense detail, vibrant colors, and symbolic subject matter, often drawn from literature and mythology. | The Pre-Raphaelite paintings are renowned for their meticulous detail.  拉斐尔前派的绘画以其精细的细节而闻名。| 近义词：Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood (名词)| 反义词：post-Raphaelite (理论上存在，但无广泛使用)|
