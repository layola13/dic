sixday: /ˈsɪksdeɪ/ | adj. | 六天的；持续六天的 |  Six-day refers to something lasting for six days.  It is a compound adjective formed by combining "six" and "day". | 例句：The six-day cycling race was grueling.  六天自行车赛非常艰苦。


例句：They embarked on a six-day trek through the mountains. 他们开始了为期六天的山区徒步旅行。


近义词：six-days ( less common)


反义词：无


词性：形容词
