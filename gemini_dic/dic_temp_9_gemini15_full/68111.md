faff:/fæf/| v. n.|瞎忙；琐事；浪费时间 | To spend time in a disorganized or ineffective way; to fuss or trifle.  |例句：I spent the whole morning faffing about with the computer. 我整个上午都在瞎忙电脑。  |近义词：fuss, fiddle, potter, mess about |反义词：efficient, productive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He faffs around a lot. = 他经常瞎忙。
She faffs with her makeup every morning. = 她每天早上都在浪费时间化妆。


一般过去时:
I faffed around for hours trying to fix it. = 我花了几个小时瞎忙，试图修理它。
We faffed about in the garden all afternoon. = 我们整个下午都在花园里瞎忙。


现在完成时:
He's been faffing around all day. = 他已经瞎忙了一整天。
She's faffed about with that project for weeks. = 她已经为此项目瞎忙了好几个星期。


过去完成时:
I had faffed about for ages before I finally understood. = 我瞎忙了很久才最终明白。
They had faffed around with the plans for months before starting. = 他们在开始之前已经为计划瞎忙了几个月。


将来时:
I'll probably faff around tomorrow trying to get it done. = 我明天可能会瞎忙，试图完成它。
We'll be faffing about with the decorations all day. = 我们一整天都会在瞎忙装饰。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't faffed around, I would have finished on time. = 如果我没有瞎忙，我就会按时完成。
I wish I hadn't faffed about so much. = 我真希望我没有那么瞎忙。


被动语态:  (The verb "faff" is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Are you just faffing around? = 你只是在瞎忙吗？
Was he faffing about while I was working? = 我工作的时候他在瞎忙吗？


否定句:
I didn't faff around; I got it done quickly. = 我没有瞎忙；我很快就完成了。
She doesn't faff about when she's working. = 她工作时不会瞎忙。
