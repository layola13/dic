odyssey:/ˈɒdəsi/ /ˈɑːdəsi/| n.| 长途冒险；艰难的旅程；奥德赛（荷马史诗）|a long and eventful or adventurous journey or experience.  The word is often used metaphorically to describe a difficult or challenging experience.|例句：Her career has been a real odyssey of ups and downs. 她的职业生涯充满了起伏，是一段真正的历程。

例句：The novel follows the odyssey of a young woman searching for her lost brother. 这部小说讲述了一位年轻女子寻找失散兄弟的历程。


近义词：journey, voyage, adventure, quest, pilgrimage|反义词：stationary existence, sedentary life|词性：名词
