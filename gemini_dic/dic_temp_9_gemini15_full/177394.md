reavows:/ˌriːəˈvaʊz/| vt.|再次声明；重申；重新宣誓|To declare or affirm again; to renew a vow or promise.|例句：He reavowed his commitment to the cause. 他再次声明了他对事业的承诺。|例句：She reavowed her love for him after their quarrel. 他们吵架后，她重申了她对他的爱。|近义词：reaffirm, reaffirm, reiterate, restate|反义词：renounce, recant, retract|动词
