paniculated:/pænɪˈkjuːleɪtɪd/| adj.|  丛生的，圆锥花序的 | Having many branches or flower clusters arranged in a panicle.  A panicle is a branched, indeterminate inflorescence with flowers on short stalks along the main axis.  The term is often used to describe the branching pattern of plants. | Example: The paniculated inflorescence of the oat plant is easily recognizable.  燕麦植物的圆锥花序很容易辨认。 The paniculated hydrangea boasted a stunning display of blooms.  这株圆锥绣球花展现了令人惊艳的花朵。 | 近义词：branched, clustered | 反义词：unbranched, single
