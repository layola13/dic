hang-dog:/ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ | adj. | 垂头丧气的，沮丧的 | Having a dejected appearance; downcast.  The term evokes the image of a dog with its head hanging low, suggesting shame or dejection. |例句：He looked hang-dog after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后看起来垂头丧气的。

例句：She had a hang-dog expression after the argument. 争吵后她脸上露出沮丧的表情。


近义词：dejected, downcast, depressed, despondent, crestfallen

反义词：cheerful, upbeat, happy, joyful, elated

词性:形容词
