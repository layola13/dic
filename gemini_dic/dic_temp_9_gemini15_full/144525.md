outvalues:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuːz/| vt.|超过…的价值，胜过…的价值|to surpass in value or importance|The antique vase outvalues all the other items in the collection. 这件古董花瓶的价值超过收藏中的所有其他物品。|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outweighs|反义词： undervalues, underestimates|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outvalues his possessions over his family. 他把财产看得比家庭还重要。
This painting outvalues all the others in the gallery. 这幅画的价值超过画廊里的其他所有画作。


一般过去时:
The gold coin outvalued the silver ones. 这枚金币的价值超过了那些银币。
The old house outvalued the land it sat on. 这栋老房子的价值超过了它所占据的土地。


现在完成时:
The market has outvalued the company's initial projections. 市场已经超过了公司最初的预期。
This investment strategy has outvalued the traditional approach. 这种投资策略的收益已经超过了传统方法。


过去完成时:
The antique had outvalued the price they initially paid for it. 这件古董的价值已经超过了他们最初支付的价格。
She realized that her experience had outvalued her qualifications. 她意识到她的经验已经超过了她的资历。


将来时:
This technology will outvalue current methods in the long run. 从长远来看，这项技术将超过目前的方法。
The new building will outvalue the old one in terms of energy efficiency. 新建筑在能源效率方面将超过旧建筑。


虚拟语气:
If the property were renovated, it would outvalue the neighboring houses. 如果对房产进行翻修，它的价值将超过附近的房屋。
If he had invested wisely, his portfolio would have outvalued the market. 如果他投资明智，他的投资组合的价值将超过市场。


被动语态:
The old car was outvalued by the new model. 旧车被新款车在价值上超过了。
His contributions were outvalued by the team's overall performance. 他的贡献被团队的整体表现所超越。


疑问句:
Does this diamond outvalue that ruby? 这颗钻石的价值超过那颗红宝石吗？
Does experience outvalue education in this field? 在这个领域，经验的价值超过教育吗？


否定句:
The project didn't outvalue the investment made.  该项目没有超过所做的投资。
His efforts did not outvalue his lack of experience. 他的努力并没有弥补他缺乏经验的不足。


PHRASE->
outvalue significantly:  显著超过价值
outvalue by a large margin:  大幅度超过价值
outvalue in the long term:  长期来看价值更高
outvalue compared to: 与...相比价值更高
outvalue in terms of:  就...而言价值更高
