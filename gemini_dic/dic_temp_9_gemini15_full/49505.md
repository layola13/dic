demagogism:/ˈdɛməɡɑːɡɪzəm/| n. |煽动；煽动政治|The use of appealing to the emotions of the people to gain power, often by making false claims and promises.|例句：His speech was pure demagogism, designed to inflame the passions of the crowd.  他的演讲纯粹是煽动，旨在激起群众的激情。|近义词：incitement, agitation, rabble-rousing|反义词：statesmanship, leadership


例句：The rise of demagogism in the country led to widespread political instability.  该国煽动政治的兴起导致了广泛的政治不稳定。
