prebuys:/ˈpriːbaɪz/| n.|预购，预先购买|The act of buying something before it is officially released or available.  It often involves placing an order and paying in advance.|例句：The prebuys for the new video game were incredibly high, indicating strong consumer interest.  新视频游戏的预购量非常高，表明消费者兴趣浓厚。|近义词：advance purchase, early purchase|反义词：post-purchase
