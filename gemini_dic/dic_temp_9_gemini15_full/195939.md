sensitizes: /ˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使敏感；使过敏；使易感 | To make someone or something more sensitive or responsive to something.  | 例句：Exposure to sunlight sensitizes the skin to sunburn.  阳光照射会使皮肤更容易晒伤。 | 例句：The experience sensitized him to the plight of the homeless.  这次经历使他对无家可归者的困境变得敏感。 | 近义词：desensitize的反义词,  make aware,  alert,  condition | 反义词：desensitize,  numb,  deaden | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication sensitizes the patient to sunlight. = 这种药物使病人对阳光过敏。
This training sensitizes employees to workplace harassment. = 这项培训使员工对工作场所的骚扰更加敏感。

一般过去时:
The accident sensitized her to the dangers of speeding. = 那次事故使她意识到超速的危险。
The teacher sensitized the students to the importance of empathy. = 老师使学生们意识到同理心的重要性。

现在完成时:
Exposure to the toxin has sensitized many people in the area. =  接触这种毒素使该地区的许多人过敏。
Years of activism have sensitized her to social injustice. = 多年的行动主义使她对社会不公正现象更加敏感。

过去完成时:
He had sensitized himself to the cultural differences before the trip. = 他在旅行前已经让自己对文化差异更加敏感。
They had sensitized the public to the environmental crisis through their campaign. = 他们通过他们的活动已经使公众意识到环境危机。

将来时:
The new program will sensitize the public to the issue of climate change. = 新项目将使公众意识到气候变化问题。
This training will sensitize employees to the company's new policies. = 这项培训将使员工了解公司的新政策。

虚拟语气:
If we sensitized people to the problem earlier, we could have prevented it. = 如果我们早点让人们意识到这个问题，我们就能预防它。
I wish they had sensitized me to the risks involved. = 我真希望他们当时让我意识到所涉及的风险。

被动语态:
The public is being sensitized to the importance of vaccination. = 公众正在被教育了解疫苗接种的重要性。
The students were sensitized to the ethical implications of the research. = 学生们被教育了解这项研究的伦理意义。

疑问句:
What sensitized you to this issue? = 什么让你意识到这个问题？
How can we sensitize the younger generation to environmental protection? = 我们怎样才能使年轻一代意识到环境保护的重要性？

否定句:
The campaign failed to sensitize the public to the dangers. = 这项活动未能使公众意识到危险。
The advertisement did not sensitize consumers to the product's flaws. = 这则广告没有让消费者意识到该产品的缺陷。
