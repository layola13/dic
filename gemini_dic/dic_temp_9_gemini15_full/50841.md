desecrates:/ˈdɛsɪkreɪts/| vt.|亵渎；玷污；侵犯（圣地）|To violate or outrage the sacredness of (something); to treat with contempt or disrespect.|The vandals desecrated the cemetery by toppling tombstones.  暴徒们推倒墓碑，亵渎了墓地。

They desecrated the temple with graffiti. 他们用涂鸦亵渎了寺庙。

近义词：profane, defile, violate, pollute |反义词：revere, sanctify, consecrate
