sectors:/ˈsɛktərz/ | n. | 部门；领域；扇区 |  A sector is a distinct part or area of something, especially of a country, society, or industry.  It can also refer to a part of a circle or a similar geometric shape. |

例句：

The financial sector is experiencing a period of significant growth.  金融部门正在经历一个显著增长的时期。

The city was divided into several sectors for administrative purposes.  为了行政目的，这座城市被划分为几个区域。


近义词：division, area, part, field, segment

反义词：whole, entirety

一般现在时:
The company operates in several sectors. = 这家公司在多个领域运营。
This sector contributes significantly to the national economy. = 这个部门对国民经济贡献巨大。

一般过去时:
The government restructured several key sectors. = 政府重组了几个关键部门。
That sector experienced a sharp decline last year. = 去年该领域经历了大幅下降。

现在完成时:
The technology sector has grown exponentially. = 科技行业呈指数级增长。
Several sectors have already adapted to the new regulations. = 几个部门已经适应了新的规定。

过去完成时:
Before the crisis, the banking sector had enjoyed a long period of prosperity. = 危机爆发前，银行业经历了长期的繁荣。
The agricultural sector had already suffered from the drought. = 农业部门早已遭受干旱之苦。

将来时:
The energy sector will face significant challenges in the coming years. = 能源行业在未来几年将面临重大挑战。
We predict that this sector will boom in the next decade. = 我们预测该行业将在未来十年蓬勃发展。

虚拟语气:
If the government invested more in this sector, it would create more jobs. = 如果政府在这个领域投入更多资金，就能创造更多就业机会。
I wish the education sector received more funding. = 我希望教育部门能获得更多资金。

被动语态:
The sectors most affected were those related to tourism. = 受影响最大的部门是那些与旅游业相关的部门。
Many sectors are being reorganized to improve efficiency. = 许多部门正在进行重组以提高效率。

疑问句:
Which sectors are most vulnerable to climate change? = 哪些部门最容易受到气候变化的影响？
What sectors are you focusing on for investment? = 你投资的重点领域是什么？

否定句:
This company does not operate in that sector. = 这家公司不在该领域运营。
The report did not include data from all sectors. = 该报告并未包含所有部门的数据。
