findfault:/faɪndˈfɔːlt/ | vt. |挑剔；找茬；批评 |To criticize or complain about someone or something, often unfairly or excessively.  |例句：He's always finding fault with his colleagues' work. 他总是挑剔同事的工作。|例句：She's constantly finding fault with everything. 她总是对一切吹毛求疵。|近义词：criticize, faultfind, carp, censure |反义词：praise, commend, compliment |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finds fault with everything I do. = 他对我做的每一件事都吹毛求疵。
They find fault with the new policy. = 他们对这项新政策吹毛求疵。

一般过去时:
She found fault with my presentation. = 她对我的演示提出了批评。
He found fault with the restaurant's service. = 他对这家餐厅的服务提出了批评。

现在完成时:
They have found fault with the design numerous times. = 他们多次对这个设计提出了批评。
She has found fault with my work again. = 她又一次对我的工作提出了批评。

过去完成时:
He had found fault with the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就对这个计划提出了批评。
They had found fault with the report, delaying its submission. = 他们之前就对报告提出了批评，延误了它的提交。

将来时:
I think she will find fault with my cooking. = 我想她会挑剔我的厨艺。
He will find fault with anything, no matter how good it is. = 无论多么好，他都会挑剔。

虚拟语气:
If I were to submit the report now, they would find fault with it. = 如果我现在提交报告，他们会挑剔它。
I wish he wouldn't find fault with every little detail. = 我希望他不必事事都吹毛求疵。

被动语态:
My work was found fault with by my boss. = 我的工作受到了老板的批评。
The proposal was found fault with for its lack of detail. = 该提案因缺乏细节而受到批评。

疑问句:
Does she often find fault with others? = 她经常挑剔别人吗？
What aspects of the project did he find fault with? = 他对项目的哪些方面提出了批评？

否定句:
He didn't find fault with my report. = 他没有挑剔我的报告。
She didn't find fault with the food. = 她没有挑剔食物。
