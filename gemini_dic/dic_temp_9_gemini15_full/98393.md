insolubilities:/ˌɪnsɒljʊˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不溶解性；不可解性；无法解决的问题 | The insolubilities of the problem baffled the researchers.  这个问题的不可解性难倒了研究人员。 |近义词：insolubility, intractability |反义词：solubility, solvability


英文解析：The plural form of insolubility, referring to the state or quality of being insoluble;  incapable of being dissolved, solved, or explained.  It can refer to physical insolubility (like a substance not dissolving in a liquid) or to conceptual insolubility (like a problem that cannot be solved).


例句：

The chemical's insolubilities in water hindered its application.  这种化学物质在水中的不溶性阻碍了它的应用。

The numerous insolubilities in the case made it difficult for the detective to solve the crime.  案件中诸多无法解释之处使得侦探难以破案。


词性: 名词(Noun)
