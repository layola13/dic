kindergartens:/ˈkɪndəɡɑːrtn̩z/ | n. |幼儿园（复数） | Kindergartens are preschool educational institutions for young children, typically aged three to five. They provide a playful and engaging environment to foster early childhood development.  |Many kindergartens offer half-day and full-day programs.|近义词：preschools, nurseries |反义词：high schools, universities
