nonactors:/ˈnɑːnˌæktərz/ | n. |非演员；非行动者 |People or things that are not involved in a particular action or event.  Those who do not participate directly in a situation, event, or process.  |例句：The nonactors in the drama watched from the sidelines.  非戏剧中的演员们在旁边观望。

The nonactors were largely unaffected by the company's restructuring.  那些非行动者们很大程度上不受公司重组的影响。|近义词：bystanders, onlookers, spectators, passive observers |反义词：actors, participants, stakeholders
