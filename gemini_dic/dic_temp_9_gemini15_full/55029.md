disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/ | n./adj. |不安；焦虑；扰乱 | A feeling of anxiety and worry; to disturb someone's peace or tranquility. |例句：Her disquiet grew as the days passed without news.  她的不安随着没有消息的日子一天天过去而加剧。


The news of the impending storm caused considerable disquiet among the villagers. 即将来临的风暴的消息在村民中引起了相当大的不安。|近义词：unease, anxiety, worry, restlessness, perturbation |反义词：calm, peace, tranquility, serenity
