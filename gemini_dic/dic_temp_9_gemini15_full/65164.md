ethnocentricities:/ˌeθnoʊsɛnˈtrɪsətiz/| n. |民族中心主义倾向；民族优越感；各种族中心主义|The plural form of ethnocentricity, referring to multiple instances or types of ethnocentric beliefs or behaviors.  It highlights the diverse ways in which people prioritize their own cultural group and judge others based on their own cultural standards. |例句：The study revealed a range of ethnocentricities among the participants, from subtle biases to overt prejudice. 这项研究揭示了参与者之间存在各种各样的民族中心主义倾向，从细微的偏见到公开的偏见。|近义词：prejudices, biases, cultural biases, xenophobia |反义词：cosmopolitanism, multiculturalism
