iam:/aɪˈæm/ | pron. | 我 |  The first-person singular pronoun, indicating the speaker.  It is a contraction of "I am." |  I am a student. = 我是一个学生。 I am going home. = 我要回家了。 |  me, myself |  you, he, she, it, we, they | pron.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am a teacher. = 我是一名老师。
I am going to the park. = 我要去公园。


一般过去时:
I was at home yesterday. = 我昨天在家。
I was feeling sick. = 我感觉不舒服。


现在完成时:
I have finished my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
I have been to China. = 我去过中国。


过去完成时:
I had already eaten before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经吃过了。
I had never seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我从未见过如此美丽的日落。


将来时:
I will go to the cinema tomorrow. = 我明天要去电影院。
I will be twenty years old next year. = 明年我将二十岁。


虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would fly. = 如果我是一只鸟，我会飞。
I wish I were taller. = 我希望我更高。


被动语态:
I am loved by my family. = 我被我的家人所爱。
I was told to be quiet. = 我被告知要安静。


疑问句:
Am I right? = 我对吗？
Am I bothering you? = 我打扰你了吗？


否定句:
I am not hungry. = 我不饿。
I am not going to the party. = 我不去参加派对。


PHRASE->
I am sorry. = 对不起。
I am fine. = 我很好。
I am busy. = 我很忙。
I am happy. = 我很高兴。
I am afraid. = 我害怕。
