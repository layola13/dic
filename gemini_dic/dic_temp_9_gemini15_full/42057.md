cotangent:/ˈkoʊtændʒənt/| n. |余切 |The cotangent of an angle in a right-angled triangle is the ratio of the length of the side adjacent to the angle to the length of the side opposite to the angle.  It is also the reciprocal of the tangent.  In trigonometry, it's a crucial function used in various calculations involving angles and triangles.|The cotangent of 45 degrees is 1.  余切45度是1。|tangent, reciprocal|tangent |名词
