cyclopses:/ˈsaɪklɒpsiːz/| n. |独眼巨人复数|Cyclopes are mythical creatures, typically depicted as giants with a single eye in the center of their forehead.  They are often associated with strength, violence, and craftsmanship.  In Greek mythology, they were skilled smiths who forged Zeus's thunderbolts.|The cyclopes in Homer's Odyssey were fearsome creatures who imprisoned Odysseus.|近义词：giants, monsters|反义词：N/A
