detachment:/dɪˈtætʃmənt/ | n. | 分离；超然；冷漠；军队分遣队 | The state of being detached; separation.  It can refer to physical separation, emotional distance, or a military unit. |例句：The detachment of the old building's facade from the main structure was a sign of serious structural damage. 老建筑立面与主体结构的分离是严重结构性损坏的迹象。

例句：Her detachment from her family's emotional drama was remarkable, given her close upbringing. 考虑到她从小与家人关系密切，她对家庭情感剧的冷漠令人瞩目。

近义词：separation, disengagement, aloofness, indifference, objectivity, detachment (military unit) | 反义词：attachment, involvement, connection, engagement, empathy
