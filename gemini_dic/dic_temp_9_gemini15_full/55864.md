divinities:/dɪˈvɪnɪtiz/| n. |神性；神；神灵 |Divinities are supernatural beings, often worshipped as gods or goddesses.  They are believed to possess immense power and influence over the world and human affairs. |例句：Many ancient cultures had complex pantheons of divinities, each with its own unique attributes and stories.  许多古代文化都有复杂的神灵万神殿，每个神灵都有其独特的属性和故事。

例句：The artist depicted the divinities in a style reminiscent of classical sculpture.艺术家以古典雕塑风格描绘了这些神灵。


近义词：gods, goddesses, deities, supernaturals |反义词：mortals, humans, earthly beings |词性：名词
