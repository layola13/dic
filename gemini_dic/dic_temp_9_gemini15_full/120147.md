mincemeat:/ˈmɪnsmiːt/| n.|肉馅，碎肉；（尤指圣诞节食用的）果料馅|A mixture of finely chopped meat, usually beef or mutton, spices, and dried fruits, especially used as a filling for pies.  The term now commonly refers to a sweet mixture of dried fruits, spices, and suet, often used in mince pies, especially during the Christmas season.|例句：The traditional Christmas mince pies are filled with rich mincemeat.  传统的圣诞肉馅饼里充满了丰富的肉馅。|例句：She made a delicious mincemeat tart with apples and cinnamon. 她用苹果和肉桂做了一个美味的肉馅饼。|近义词：filling, stuffing|反义词：whole meat
