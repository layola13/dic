/kɔːk/ | n. | 堵塞物；软木塞；嵌缝料 | A substance used to seal gaps and cracks, typically a putty-like material.  |  He used caulk to seal the gap around the window. 他用嵌缝料封住了窗户周围的缝隙。 | sealant, filler |  (No direct antonym, but "gap" or "crack" could be considered opposites in the context of what caulk fills) | 名词
