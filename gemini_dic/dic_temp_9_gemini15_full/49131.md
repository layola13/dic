deistic:/ˈdiːɪstɪk/| adj.| 自然神论的；泛神论的 | Relating to or characteristic of deism; believing in a God who created the universe but does not intervene in it.  |  His philosophy is distinctly deistic, emphasizing the self-sufficiency of the natural world.  他的哲学明显是自然神论的，强调自然界的自给自足。  |近义词：theistic (in a broad sense), pantheistic |反义词：atheistic, agnostic
