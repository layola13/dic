mumblers:/ˈmʌmblərz/| n. |含糊不清地说的人；咕哝的人|People who speak indistinctly or mutter. They often speak quietly and unclearly, making it difficult for others to understand them.  |例句：The mumblers in the back of the room were distracting the speaker.  房间后面那些含糊不清说话的人分散了演讲者的注意力。

例句：I couldn't understand a word the mumblers were saying. 我听不懂那些咕哝的人在说什么。


近义词：mutterers, grumblers, whisperers|反义词：articulate speakers, clear speakers|名词
