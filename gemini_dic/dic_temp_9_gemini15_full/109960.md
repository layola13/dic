liberate:/ˈlɪbəreɪt/| vt.|解放；释放；使自由|To set someone or something free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression.|例句：The army liberated the city from enemy control.  军队从敌人手中解放了这座城市。|例句：She felt liberated after quitting her job.  辞职后，她感觉解脱了。|近义词：free, release, emancipate|反义词：enslave, imprison, oppress|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army liberates the city. = 军队解放了这座城市。
He liberates his mind from worries. = 他从担忧中解放了自己的思想。


一般过去时:
They liberated the prisoners. = 他们解放了囚犯们。
The movement liberated women from traditional roles. = 这场运动使妇女从传统角色中解放出来。


现在完成时:
The soldiers have liberated the town. = 士兵们已经解放了那个城镇。
She has liberated herself from the constraints of society. = 她已经从社会的束缚中解脱出来。


过去完成时:
They had liberated the country before the peace treaty was signed. = 他们签署和平条约之前已经解放了国家。
The rebels had liberated several villages by dawn. = 到黎明时，叛军已经解放了几个村庄。


将来时:
The rebels will liberate the city soon. = 叛军很快就会解放这座城市。
We hope that one day, technology will liberate us from manual labor. = 我们希望有一天，科技能使我们从体力劳动中解放出来。


虚拟语气:
If they had liberated the people earlier, fewer lives would have been lost. = 如果他们早些解放人民，就不会有那么多人丧生。
I wish they would liberate the political prisoners. = 我希望他们能释放政治犯。


被动语态:
The city was liberated by the allied forces. = 这座城市被盟军解放了。
The slaves were finally liberated after many years of struggle. = 奴隶们在多年的斗争后终于获得了解放。


疑问句:
Will the army liberate the town? = 军队会解放那个城镇吗？
Has the government liberated the political prisoners? = 政府已经释放政治犯了吗？


否定句:
The army did not liberate the city. = 军队没有解放这座城市。
They have not yet liberated the hostages. = 他们还没有释放人质。


PHRASE->
liberate someone from something: 从…中解放某人
liberate oneself from something: 从…中解脱自己
liberate a country: 解放一个国家
liberate a city: 解放一座城市
liberate resources: 释放资源
