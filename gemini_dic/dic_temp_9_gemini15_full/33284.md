chrysotype:/ˈkrɪsətaɪp/| n. |日光印相法；金黄印相法|A photographic printing process using a ferric oxalate solution to sensitize paper, resulting in a warm, golden-brown image. The process is known for its unique color and archival stability. |The chrysotype print displayed rich, golden tones. 日光印相法照片呈现出丰富的金色调。|近义词：cyanotype, platinum print|反义词：无
