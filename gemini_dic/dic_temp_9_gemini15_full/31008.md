chalky:/ˈtʃɔːki/ | adj. | 粉笔似的；灰白的；干巴巴的 |  Having the quality of chalk; dry, crumbly, and often white or pale in color.  The texture is often rough and easily crumbled. |例句：The soil is chalky and dry. 这土壤又干又白，像粉笔一样。

The cliffs were chalky white.悬崖是粉笔般洁白。


近义词：powdery, dusty, friable,  whitish


反义词：smooth, moist, oily,  rich (referring to soil)


词性:形容词
