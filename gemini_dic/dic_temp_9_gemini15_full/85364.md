harrowingly:/ˈhærəʊɪŋli/| adv.|令人恐惧地，令人痛苦地，令人心碎地 |  Describes something that causes intense fear, distress, or suffering.  It emphasizes the extreme emotional impact of an event or experience. |例句：The film depicts the harrowing experiences of refugees fleeing war. 这部电影描绘了难民逃离战争的痛苦经历。

The accident was a harrowing experience for everyone involved.  这次事故对所有参与者来说都是一次令人痛苦的经历。

近义词：terrifyingly, horrifyingly, distressingly, agonizingly | 反义词：pleasantly, comfortingly, soothingly
