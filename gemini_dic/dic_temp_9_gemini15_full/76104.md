further-education: /ˌfɜːðər ˌedʒuˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 进修，继续教育 | Continuing education beyond the compulsory stage, typically at a college or university.  It encompasses a wide range of educational pursuits undertaken after completing secondary school. | Example: She's pursuing further education in graphic design. 她正在进修平面设计。  He's considering further education options after graduating high school. 他正在考虑高中毕业后的继续教育选择。 | 近义词：advanced education, higher education, post-secondary education | 反义词：primary education, secondary education | 名词
