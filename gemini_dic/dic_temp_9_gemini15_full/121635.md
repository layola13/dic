mispay:/mɪsˈpeɪ/| vt.|付错款，付错钱|To pay the wrong amount of money, or to pay the correct amount to the wrong person.|例句：I mispaid my electricity bill by $10.  我电费多付了10美元。


例句：The company mispaid several employees, resulting in a payroll error. 公司给几位员工付错了工资，导致工资单出错。


近义词：pay incorrectly, miscalculate payment, err in payment


反义词：pay correctly, pay accurately


词性:动词
