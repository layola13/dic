moseying:/ˈmoʊziɪŋ/| v. |闲逛；漫步；慢吞吞地走|To move slowly and casually; to stroll.|例句：We spent the afternoon moseying through the park. 我们下午在公园里闲逛。|例句：He was moseying along the street, whistling a tune. 他慢吞吞地走在街上，吹着口哨。|近义词：strolling, ambling, sauntering|反义词：hurrying, rushing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are moseying down the street. = 他们在街上慢慢地走着。
We mosey around the town every Sunday. = 我们每个星期天都在镇上闲逛。


一般过去时:
She moseyed into the room and sat down. = 她慢悠悠地走进房间坐下。
They moseyed home after the festival. = 节日后他们慢慢地回家了。


现在完成时:
We have been moseying through the countryside all day. = 我们今天一直在乡下闲逛。
He has moseyed along quite a distance. = 他已经走了相当远的路了。


过去完成时:
They had moseyed into the bar before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经慢慢地进了酒吧。
She had moseyed through the entire museum before lunch. = 午餐前她已经慢慢地逛遍了整个博物馆。


将来时:
We will mosey down to the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天会慢慢地走到海滩去。
They will mosey through the market later. = 他们稍后会慢慢地逛逛市场。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mosey around the old town. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会在老城区闲逛。
I wish I could mosey through the fields all day. = 我希望我能整天在田野里闲逛。


被动语态:  (Moseying is rarely used passively.)
*The path was being moseyed along by many tourists. (This sounds unnatural.) = 许多游客正慢悠悠地走在这条路上。(这句话听起来不自然)
*The town square was often moseyed through by locals. (This is marginally better but still awkward.) = 当地人经常在镇中心广场闲逛。(这句话勉强说得通，但仍然略显别扭)


疑问句:
Are they moseying towards the river? = 他们是在朝河边走吗？
Will you be moseying along the coast? = 你会沿着海岸慢慢走吗？


否定句:
They didn't mosey; they ran. = 他们没有闲逛；他们跑了。
We won't mosey around today; we have too much to do. = 我们今天不会闲逛；我们有很多事情要做。


PHRASE->
moseying along = 慢慢地走着
moseying around = 闲逛
moseying through = 漫步穿过
moseying about = 闲逛
moseying off = 慢慢地离开
