paragenic: /ˌpærəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |伴生的，共生的|  relating to or denoting minerals that occur together in the same rock formation.  Paragenesis refers to the order in which minerals crystallize from a cooling magma or other geological process.  This adjective describes minerals or other geological features formed in this context. |


例句：

The paragenic relationships of the ore minerals were carefully studied. = 仔细研究了矿石矿物的共生关系。

The paragenic sequence indicates a complex history of hydrothermal alteration. = 共生序列表明了复杂的水热蚀变历史。


近义词：syngenetic, associated


反义词：epigenetic


词性：形容词
