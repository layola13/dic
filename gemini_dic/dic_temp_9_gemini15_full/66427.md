exegesist:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːsɪst/| n. |释经家，解经学者|A person who engages in exegesis, especially of biblical texts.  Someone who interprets and explains texts, particularly religious scriptures. |The exegesist offered a compelling interpretation of the obscure passage.  这位释经家对这段晦涩的经文提出了令人信服的解释。|interpreter, commentator, scholar|—|
