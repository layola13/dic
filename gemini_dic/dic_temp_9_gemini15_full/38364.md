concentric:/kənˈsentrɪk/| adj.|同心的，同中心的|Having a common center; arranged in a series of circles around a common center.  Concentric circles share the same center point.| 例句：The target had concentric circles of different colors.  靶子有不同颜色的同心圆。

例句：The ripples in the pond were concentric circles spreading outwards. 池塘里的涟漪是向外扩散的同心圆。


近义词：concentrically (adv.), coaxial, co-axial


反义词：eccentric


词性:形容词
