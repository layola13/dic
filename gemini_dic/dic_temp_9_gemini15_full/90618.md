humanhood:/ˈhjuːmənˌhʊd/| n. |人性；人类的本质；人道|The inherent qualities and characteristics of being human; the state or condition of being human.  It often encompasses aspects like morality, compassion, rationality, and social interaction.  The concept explores what it means to be human and how humans interact with the world and each other.|例句：The novel explores the complexities of humanhood and the search for meaning. 这部小说探讨了人性的复杂性和对意义的追求。

例句：Understanding humanhood requires empathy and a deep understanding of human behavior. 理解人性需要同理心和对人类行为的深刻理解。
