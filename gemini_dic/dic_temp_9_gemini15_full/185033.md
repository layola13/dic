rethaw:/ˌriːˈθɔː/| v.|解冻；再次解冻|To thaw something that has been frozen again.  This word is a combination of "re-" (again) and "thaw."|例句：We had to rethaw the meat after it froze again in the power outage.  我们停电后不得不再次解冻肉。

例句：The frozen vegetables needed to be rethawed before cooking.  冷冻蔬菜需要在烹饪前再次解冻。


近义词：defrost, thaw again|反义词：freeze, refrigerate


词性:动词
