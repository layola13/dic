meconidine:/mɪˈkɒnɪdiːn/| n. |麦可宁|A synthetic opioid analgesic, similar to codeine but more potent.  It is used to relieve moderate to severe pain. |例句：Meconidine is a potent analgesic used in some countries but not widely available in others.|近义词：codeine, oxycodone|反义词：analgesic (as a descriptive term for lack of pain-relieving properties, not as a direct antonym)|名词
