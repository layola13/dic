prestocked:/ˈpriːˌstɑːkt/| adj. |预先备货的，预先储备的 |Already stocked in advance; pre-supplied.  This usually refers to items that are stocked before a specific event or need. |例句：The emergency shelter was prestocked with food and water.  紧急避难所预先储备了食物和水。

例句：The store was prestocked with holiday decorations in October. 十月份商店就预先备好了节日装饰品。


近义词：pre-supplied, pre-stocked, pre-positioned, pre-arranged


反义词：unstocked, unprepared, lacking


词性：形容词
