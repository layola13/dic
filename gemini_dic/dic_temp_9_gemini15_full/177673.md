recalculations:/ˌriːkæl.kjəˈleɪʃənz/| n. |重新计算，再计算|The process of calculating something again, often to correct an error or to obtain a more accurate result.  It involves repeating a calculation, often with revised data or a different method.|例句：The engineer ordered recalculations after discovering a flaw in the initial design.工程师在发现初始设计中的缺陷后下令重新计算。

例句：Due to the new data, recalculations of the project's budget are necessary. 由于新的数据，项目预算需要重新计算。


近义词：recomputations, recalculating, recomputing|反义词：initial calculation, first calculation|名词
