nondualisms:/ˌnɒnˈdjuːəlɪzəmz/| n. |非二元论；非对立性 |The philosophical concept that reality is not composed of separate, independent entities, but rather a unified whole.  Nondualisms reject the duality of subject and object, mind and body, etc. |例句：Many Eastern philosophies embrace nondualisms, emphasizing interconnectedness. 许多东方哲学都接受非二元论，强调相互关联性。

例句：The study explored the implications of nondualisms for contemporary psychology. 这项研究探讨了非二元论对当代心理学的影响。


近义词：monism, unity, oneness, interconnectedness |反义词：dualism, dichotomy, separation, duality

词性:名词
