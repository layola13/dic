misshaped:/mɪsˈʃeɪpt/| adj.|畸形的，变形;形状不规则的 |Having a shape that is not normal or natural; deformed.  |例句：The accident left him with a misshapen leg. 事故导致他一条腿变形。

例句：The storm left the trees misshapen and broken. 风暴过后，树木都变形了，而且断了。


近义词：deformed, malformed, distorted, irregular, asymmetrical |反义词：well-shaped, shapely, symmetrical


词源:

* **mis-**:  A prefix indicating bad, wrong, or ill. (源自古英语 *mis-,  表示错误，坏的)
* **shape**:  The external form or appearance characteristic of someone or something. (源自古英语 *sceape,  表示形状，样子)

因此，misshapen 字面意思是“形状错误的”。
