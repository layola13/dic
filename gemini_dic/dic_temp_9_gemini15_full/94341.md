impingement:/ɪmˈpɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 妨碍；侵犯；撞击 | The act of impinging; encroachment or infringement.  It can refer to physical impact or a more abstract interference. |例句：The new highway's construction is causing impingement on the local wildlife habitat. 新高速公路的建设正在对当地野生动物栖息地造成影响。

例句：The constant noise from the factory was an impingement on their quality of life. 工厂持续不断的噪音影响了他们的生活质量。


近义词：interference, encroachment, intrusion, infringement, impact |反义词：assistance, support, facilitation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The construction work impinges on our daily routine.  施工影响了我们的日常生活。
The loud music impinges on my concentration.  嘈杂的音乐影响了我的注意力。


一般过去时:
The storm impinged on the coastal areas.  暴风雨袭击了沿海地区。
His aggressive behavior impinged on her personal space. 他的侵略性行为侵犯了她的私人空间。


现在完成时:
The expansion of the city has impinged on the surrounding farmland. 城市的扩张已经侵占了周围的农田。
Their constant complaints have impinged on my patience. 他们不断的抱怨已经磨光了我的耐心。


过去完成时:
The previous administration's policies had impinged on economic growth. 前一届政府的政策已经影响了经济增长。
The long drought had impinged on the water supply. 长期的干旱已经影响了供水。


将来时:
The new regulations will impinge on small businesses. 新的规章制度将会影响小型企业。
The rising sea levels will impinge on coastal communities. 海平面上升将影响沿海社区。


虚拟语气:
If the project were to proceed, it would impinge on the natural environment. 如果项目继续进行，它将影响自然环境。
I wish the construction noise wouldn't impinge on my sleep. 我希望施工噪音不会影响我的睡眠。


被动语态:
Their privacy was impinged upon by the paparazzi. 他们的隐私受到了狗仔队的侵犯。
The project was impinged upon by unexpected delays. 该项目受到了意外延误的影响。


疑问句:
Does the new policy impinge on our rights? 新政策是否侵犯了我们的权利？

否定句:
The decision did not impinge on our daily operations. 该决定没有影响我们的日常运作。

词源:
From the verb "impinge," which comes from the Middle English "impinge," meaning "to strike," ultimately derived from the Old French "empigner" and ultimately from Latin "impingere," meaning "to thrust in, strike."  The noun "impingement" is a later development.
