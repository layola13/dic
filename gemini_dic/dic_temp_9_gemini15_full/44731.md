cruisingly:/ˈkruːzɪŋli/| adv.|轻松自如地，流畅地，毫不费力地|easily and smoothly, without difficulty or effort.  The word implies a sense of effortless grace and control.|例句：He steered the boat cruisingly through the narrow channel. 他轻松自如地驾驶着船通过狭窄的水道。


例句：She completed the marathon cruisingly, finishing well ahead of schedule. 她轻松地完成了马拉松比赛，比预定的时间提前很多。


近义词：effortlessly, smoothly, easily, readily


反义词：laboriously, clumsily, with difficulty


词源：源自动词cruise，意为“巡航，漫游”。  "-ingly"是副词后缀，表示“以……的方式”。  因此cruisingly字面意思是“以巡航的方式”，引申为轻松自如地。
