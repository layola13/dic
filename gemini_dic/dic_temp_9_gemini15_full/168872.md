profanes:/ˈproʊfeɪnz/| v.|亵渎，污蔑，玷污|To treat something sacred with disrespect; to violate or abuse something considered holy or sacred.  |例句：He profaned the sacred scriptures by using them for his own purposes. 他为了自己的目的而亵渎神圣的经文。

例句：The graffiti profaned the beauty of the ancient temple. 涂鸦亵渎了古庙的美丽。


近义词：desecrate, defile, violate, abuse, contaminate

反义词：revere, sanctify, consecrate, honor

词源:
From Latin *profanāre, from pro- ("before," "in front of") + fānum ("temple").  The original meaning referred to placing something outside the temple, thus making it common or unworthy. Over time, the meaning shifted to imply disrespect towards something sacred.
