overclog:/ˌoʊvərˈklɒɡ/ | vt. |堵塞过度；阻塞过度 |To clog excessively; to block or obstruct to an excessive degree.  |例句：The heavy rain overclogged the drainage system, leading to flooding. 大雨过度阻塞了排水系统，导致洪水。  |例句：The excessive snow overclogged the roads, making travel impossible. 过多的积雪阻塞了道路，使交通瘫痪。|近义词：congest, block, obstruct |反义词：unclog, clear, open
