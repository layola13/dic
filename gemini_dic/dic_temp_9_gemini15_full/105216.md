keypads:/ˈkiːpædz/ | n. | 键盘；数字小键盘 | A keypad is a set of buttons used to input numbers, letters, or symbols, often found on phones, calculators, and other electronic devices.  Keypads are typically smaller and simpler than keyboards. | Example:  The ATM's keypad was difficult to use in the rain.  自动取款机的键盘在雨中很难使用。 The keypad on my phone is quite responsive. 我手机上的键盘反应很快。 | 近义词：keyboard (though usually larger and more complex), key panel | 反义词：None  |  名词
