enjambement:/ɪnˈdʒæmbmənt/ | n. | 诗歌中的换行 |  The continuation of a sentence without a pause beyond the end of a line, couplet, or stanza.  This creates a sense of flow and momentum, often used for emphasis or to create a specific effect.  | Example:  The apparition of these faces in the crowd; / Petals on a wet, black bough. (Ezra Pound) 这句话在人群中出现；/ 湿黑枝头的花瓣。(艾兹拉·庞德)  | 近义词: run-on line, continuous line | 反义词: end-stopped line
