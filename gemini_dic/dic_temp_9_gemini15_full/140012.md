oedemas:/ˈiːdiːməz/| n.|水肿（复数）|Swelling caused by fluid buildup in the body tissues.  Oedemas are often a symptom of underlying medical conditions.|例句：The patient presented with significant peripheral oedemas in both legs.  病人双腿出现明显的周围性水肿。|例句：  Heart failure can lead to oedemas in the lower extremities. 心力衰竭会导致下肢水肿。|近义词：swellings, edema (单数)|反义词：dehydration, dryness|名词
