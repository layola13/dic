gally:/ˈɡæli/| n.|厨房；船上的厨房；(尤指旧船上的)伙房|A galley is a kitchen, especially on a ship or boat.  Historically, it referred to the cooking area on a ship, often cramped and below deck.  More recently, the term can also be used for a small, compact kitchen in other contexts, such as a small apartment or a recreational vehicle.|例句：The ship's galley was small but well-equipped. 这艘船的厨房虽小，但设备齐全。

The galley on the yacht was surprisingly spacious.  游艇上的厨房出乎意料地宽敞。|近义词：kitchen, cookhouse|反义词：dining room, mess hall
