hutong:/ˈhuːtʊŋ/|/ˈhuːtɔŋ/| n. |胡同|A narrow alley or lane, especially one in a Chinese city.  Hutongs are typically found in older parts of Beijing and other Chinese cities, characterized by their narrow, winding pathways and traditional courtyard houses (siheyuan). They represent a significant part of China's cultural heritage.|例句：We spent the afternoon wandering through the charming hutongs of Beijing. 我们在北京迷人的胡同里度过了下午。|例句：The old hutongs are being renovated to preserve their historical significance. 老胡同正在被翻新以保留其历史意义。|近义词：alley, lane, backstreet|反义词：boulevard, avenue, highway
