cultivates:/ˈkʌltɪveɪts/ | vt. |耕作；培养；陶冶；养成|To prepare and use land for growing crops; to try to develop and improve something.|例句：They cultivate rice in the paddy fields. 他们在稻田里种植水稻。|例句：She cultivates a positive attitude. 她培养积极的态度。|近义词：cultivate, foster, nurture, develop|反义词：neglect, abandon, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cultivates a large vegetable garden. = 他耕种着一个很大的菜园。
Farmers cultivate the land to grow crops. = 农民耕种土地以种植农作物。


一般过去时:
She cultivated friendships with many influential people. = 她与许多有影响力的人培养了友谊。
They cultivated a small patch of land for their own use. = 他们耕种了一小块土地自用。


现在完成时:
He has cultivated a reputation as a skilled artist. = 他已经培养了作为一个熟练艺术家的声誉。
We have cultivated a strong bond of friendship. = 我们已经培养了牢固的友谊。


过去完成时:
They had cultivated the land for generations before the factory was built. = 工厂建成之前，他们世世代代耕种这片土地。
She had cultivated her talent through years of hard work. = 她通过多年的努力培养了自己的才能。


将来时:
We will cultivate a new variety of roses next year. = 我们明年将种植新品种的玫瑰。
The company will cultivate a better relationship with its customers. = 公司将与客户建立更好的关系。


虚拟语气:
If we cultivated more patience, we would be happier. = 如果我们培养更多的耐心，我们会更快乐。
I wish I had cultivated better study habits when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候培养更好的学习习惯。


被动语态:
The land was cultivated for centuries. = 这片土地耕种了几个世纪。
His talent was carefully cultivated by his teachers. = 他的天赋得到了老师的精心培养。


疑问句:
Do you cultivate any plants in your garden? = 你在花园里种植任何植物吗？
How long have they been cultivating this land? = 他们耕种这块地多久了？


否定句:
They do not cultivate wheat in this region. = 这个地区不种植小麦。
She did not cultivate a close relationship with her colleagues. = 她没有和同事培养密切的关系。
