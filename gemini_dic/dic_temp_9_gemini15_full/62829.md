enigmas:/ˈɛnɪɡməz/ | n. |谜，谜团，难以理解的事物 | something that is mysterious, puzzling, or difficult to understand.  |The disappearance of the painting remains an enigma.  这幅画的消失仍然是个谜。|riddle, puzzle, mystery | clarity, solution |名词

These symbols remain enigmas to us. = 这些符号对我们来说仍然是谜。

词源:
From the Greek word αἴνιγμα (aínigma), meaning "riddle," "puzzle."  The word ultimately derives from the verb αἴνισσομαι (aínissomai), meaning "to speak in riddles."
