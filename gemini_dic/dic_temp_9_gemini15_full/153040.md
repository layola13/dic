peels:/piːlz/ | n. v. |果皮；皮；剥皮；脱落 |The outer covering of fruits and vegetables; to remove the skin from something. |例句：The orange peels are a great source of vitamin C.  橘子皮是维生素C的重要来源。

例句：She carefully peels the potatoes before boiling them. 她仔细地削土豆皮后再煮。


近义词：skins, rinds, pare, shell  | 反义词：core(针对水果中心部分)

词性: 名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peels an apple every morning. 他每天早上削一个苹果。
She peels the potatoes for dinner. 她为晚饭削土豆。


一般过去时:
I peeled the banana and ate it. 我剥了香蕉然后吃了。
He peeled the garlic before chopping it. 他在切蒜之前剥了蒜。


现在完成时:
I have peeled all the carrots. 我已经把所有的胡萝卜都削好了。
She has peeled and sliced the onions. 她已经把洋葱剥好切好了。


过去完成时:
I had peeled the potatoes before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前我已经把土豆削好了。
She had peeled the oranges before the party started. 派对开始之前她已经剥好了橘子。


将来时:
I will peel the apples for the pie tomorrow. 我明天会为馅饼削苹果。
He will peel the oranges for the juice. 他会为果汁剥橘子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would peel all the vegetables myself. 如果我有更多的时间，我会自己削所有蔬菜。
I wish I had peeled the garlic earlier. 我真希望我早点剥蒜。


被动语态:
The potatoes are peeled and diced. 土豆被削皮并切丁。
The oranges were peeled before being juiced. 橘子在榨汁前被剥皮。


疑问句:
Do you peel your potatoes before boiling them? 你煮土豆之前会削皮吗？
Have you peeled the bananas yet? 你把香蕉剥好了吗？


否定句:
I didn't peel the oranges. 我没有剥橘子。
She doesn't peel her apples. 她不削苹果皮。



PHRASE->
peel off: 脱落；剥落
peel away: 剥离；逐渐消失
peel back: 揭开；剥开
sun-peeled tomatoes: 晒干的西红柿
orange peels: 橘子皮
