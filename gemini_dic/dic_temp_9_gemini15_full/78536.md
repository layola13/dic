ghostily:/ˈɡoʊstli/| adj.|幽灵般地，像幽灵一样地，隐隐约约地|In a way that suggests the presence of a ghost; faintly or subtly.  It often implies something eerie, unclear, or indistinct.|例句：The music faded ghostily into the night. 音乐幽灵般地消失在夜色中。

The house stood ghostily on the hill.  房子像幽灵般地矗立在山丘上。|近义词：eerie, spectral, faintly, subtly|反义词：clearly, distinctly, vividly
