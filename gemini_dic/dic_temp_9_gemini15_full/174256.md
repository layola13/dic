quest:/kwest/| n.|探求；任务；问题；寻找|A quest is a long and difficult search for something.  It can also refer to a search for knowledge or a particular goal.|例句：The knight embarked on a quest for the Holy Grail.  这位骑士踏上了寻找圣杯的征程。|例句：Her lifelong quest for knowledge led her to many universities. 她毕生追求知识，这让她去了许多大学。|近义词：search, pursuit, mission, inquiry|反义词：answer, solution


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He quests for knowledge. = 他追求知识。
She quests for a better life. = 她追求更好的生活。


一般过去时:
They quested for treasure in the mountains. = 他们在山里寻找宝藏。
He quested after truth. = 他追寻真理。


现在完成时:
She has quested for answers for years. = 她多年来一直在寻找答案。
They have quested after justice. = 他们一直在追求正义。


过去完成时:
He had quested for the lost city for many years before he found it. = 在找到失落的城市之前，他已经寻找了多年。
She had quested after happiness before she finally found it. = 在她最终找到幸福之前，她一直在追求幸福。


将来时:
We will quest for the lost artifact. = 我们将寻找失落的文物。
He will quest for answers to his questions. = 他将寻找他问题的答案。


虚拟语气:
If I were to quest for glory, I would face many dangers. = 如果我要追求荣耀，我将面临许多危险。
I wish I could quest for the truth without fear. = 我希望我能不畏惧地追寻真理。


被动语态:
The lost city was quested for by many adventurers. = 许多冒险家都在寻找这座失落的城市。
Justice is quested after by all good people. = 所有善良的人都在追求正义。


疑问句:
What are you questing for? = 你在寻找什么？
What did they quest after? = 他们追求什么？


否定句:
He did not quest for wealth. = 他没有追求财富。
She does not quest for power. = 她不追求权力。



PHRASE->

on a quest = 在寻找；在探求
a quest for = 对…的追求
a noble quest = 一个崇高的目标
a perilous quest = 一次危险的探险
a personal quest = 一次个人探索
