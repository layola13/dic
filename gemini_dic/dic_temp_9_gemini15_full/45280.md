cultural:/ˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 文化的；文化的；教养的 | Relating to the ideas, customs, and social behaviour of a society.  | Example: The museum showcases the cultural heritage of the region.  这个博物馆展示了该地区的文化遗产。 |近义词：civilizational, societal | 反义词：uncivilized, unrefined
