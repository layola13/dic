fuddy:/ˈfʌdi/| adj.|守旧的，迂腐的，古板的|Foolishly old-fashioned or conventional.  Characterized by or showing an adherence to old-fashioned ways; fuddy-duddy.  |例句：He's a bit of a fuddy-duddy, always wearing a suit and tie. 他有点守旧，总是穿着西装打领带。

例句：Her fuddy-duddy ideas about parenting are outdated. 她关于养育孩子的守旧观念已经过时了。


近义词：old-fashioned, antiquated, conservative, stuffy, conventional,  stuffy,  reactionary

反义词：modern, progressive, liberal, forward-thinking, avant-garde


词性:形容词
