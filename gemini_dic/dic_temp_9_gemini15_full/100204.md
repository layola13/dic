interrex:/ˈɪntərɛks/| n. |摄政王；代王|A person who temporarily holds royal power, especially during an interregnum.  An interrex was a temporary ruler in ancient Rome appointed during a period between the death of one king and the election of another.  |Example: During the interregnum, the interrex governed the Roman state. 在王位空缺期间，摄政王统治着罗马国。|近义词：regent, interim ruler|反义词：monarch (in a permanent sense)
