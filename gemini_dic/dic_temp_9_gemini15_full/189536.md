salary:/ˈsæləri/| n. |薪水，薪金 | a fixed regular payment, typically paid on a monthly or bi-weekly basis, made by an employer to an employee, especially a professional or white-collar worker.  |My salary is $5,000 per month. 我的月薪是5000美元。|  She received a substantial salary increase. 她获得了大幅度的加薪。 |wage, pay, remuneration | poverty, destitution |名词
