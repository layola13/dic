cotton:/ˈkɒtn/ /ˈkɑːtn/| n. |棉花；棉布|A soft white fluffy substance that grows around the seeds of the cotton plant, and is used to make cloth.  |例句：The shirt is made of 100% cotton. 这件衬衫是百分之百的棉布。 |例句：She planted cotton in her field. 她在田里种了棉花。|近义词：linen, silk, wool|反义词：N/A
