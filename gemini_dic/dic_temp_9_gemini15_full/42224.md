counselors: /ˈkaʊnsələr/ | n. | 顾问，辅导员 | A person who gives guidance on personal, social, or psychological problems.  |  She sought the counsel of a career counselor. 她寻求职业顾问的建议。 | advisor, consultant, therapist |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 opposite of guidance  ) | 名词


USAGE->

The counselors offer advice to students. = 辅导员为学生提供建议。
Our school employs several counselors. = 我们学校雇佣了几位辅导员。
