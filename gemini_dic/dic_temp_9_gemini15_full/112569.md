lovely:/ˈlʌvli/| adj.|可爱的，令人愉快的，美好的|Agreeable and attractive.  It can describe people, things, or experiences.|例句：She has a lovely smile. 她有一个可爱的微笑。

例句：The weather was lovely for the picnic.野餐的天气真好。


近义词：adorable, charming, delightful, pleasant, beautiful|反义词：ugly, unpleasant, awful, horrible|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers are lovely. = 这些花很漂亮。
The music is lovely. = 这音乐很动听。


一般过去时:
The cake was lovely. = 蛋糕很好吃。
The sunset was lovely. = 日落很美。


现在完成时:
We have had a lovely holiday. = 我们度过了一个愉快的假期。
They have bought a lovely house. = 他们买了一栋漂亮的房子。


过去完成时:
The house had been lovely before the fire. = 这房子在火灾发生前很漂亮。
We had had a lovely time before the rain started. = 下雨前我们玩得很开心。


将来时:
The trip will be lovely. = 这趟旅行将会很愉快。
The party will be lovely. = 派对将会很美好。


虚拟语气:
If it were lovely tomorrow, we would go to the beach. = 如果明天天气好，我们就去海滩。
I wish the weather were lovely. = 我希望天气好。


被动语态:
The garden was made lovely by the gardener. = 花园被园丁打理得很漂亮。
The room was decorated lovely. = 房间装饰得很漂亮。


疑问句:
Is the dress lovely? = 这件裙子漂亮吗？
Isn't the view lovely? = 景色不是很美吗？


否定句:
The food wasn't lovely. = 食物不好吃。
The painting isn't lovely. = 这幅画不漂亮。
