pavlova:/pævˈlɔːvə/| n. | 帕芙洛娃蛋糕 | A meringue-based dessert named after the Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova. It consists of a crisp meringue base and a soft, marshmallowy interior, often topped with whipped cream and fruit.  |  Example:  We enjoyed a delicious pavlova for dessert after dinner.  我们晚饭后享用了一块美味的帕芙洛娃蛋糕。 | 近义词: meringue cake, meringue roulade | 反义词: None
