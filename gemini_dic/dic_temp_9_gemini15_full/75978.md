funneled:/ˈfʌnld/| vt.|漏斗状输送；引导；集中；|To force or guide something into a narrow or confined space, like liquid through a funnel.|The donations were funneled to the charity through a third-party organization. 捐款通过第三方组织输送给了慈善机构。|The river funneled into a narrow gorge. 河流汇入狭窄的峡谷。|channeled, directed, guided|dispersed, scattered.
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They funnel the water into the reservoir. = 他们把水导入水库。
The company funnels its profits back into research and development. = 这家公司将其利润重新投入研发。
