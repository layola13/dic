illumination:/ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 照明；启发；阐明 | The act of illuminating or the state of being illuminated; the light cast by a source of illumination;  a sudden understanding or realization.  | 例句：The illumination of the cathedral was spectacular.  教堂的照明非常壮观。

The illumination of the text clarified the author's meaning.  对文本的阐明澄清了作者的意图。 | 近义词：lighting, enlightenment, elucidation | 反义词：darkness, obscurity, confusion |

词源:
illumination comes from the Latin word "illuminatio," a combination of "in-" (in, into) and "luminare" (to shine, to light).  The word evolved through Old French and Middle English before reaching its modern form.
