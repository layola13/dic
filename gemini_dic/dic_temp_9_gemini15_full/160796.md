positivists: /ˈpɒzɪtɪvɪsts/ | n. | 实证主义者 |  Adherents of positivism, a philosophical theory emphasizing empirical evidence and scientific methods as the primary sources of knowledge.  Positivists believe that knowledge should be based on observable facts and verifiable data, rejecting metaphysical speculation. |  The positivists argued that sociology should be a science based on observable facts.  实证主义者认为社会学应该是一门基于可观察事实的科学。| empiricists, scientists | idealists, metaphysicians | 名词
