saddling: /ˈsæd(ə)lɪŋ/ | n. v. | 骑乘；负重；使承担 |

英文解析: Saddling refers to the act of placing a saddle on an animal's back, or to the act of burdening someone or something with a task or responsibility.  As a noun, it refers to the process or state of being saddled. As a verb, it describes the action of saddling.

例句:

The cowboy expertly saddled his horse before the rodeo.  = 牛仔在牛仔竞技赛前熟练地给他的马鞍上马鞍。

The company is saddling itself with unnecessary debt. = 这家公司给自己带来了不必要的债务。


近义词: (verb) burdening, loading, weighting, encumbering

反义词: (verb) unloading, relieving, freeing


词性:  Noun & Verb
