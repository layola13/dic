It often implies a lack of maturity or sophistication, sometimes bordering on artificiality.|The maidishness of her demeanor made her seem out of place at the sophisticated party.  她的举止过于少女气，让她在那个高雅的派对上显得格格不入。|近义词：childishness, girlishness, coquettishness |反义词：maturity, sophistication, worldliness
