ident:/ˈaɪdɛnt/ | v. |识别，辨认 |To recognize or identify something or someone.  |The police identfied the suspect from security footage.|近义词：recognize, distinguish, pinpoint |反义词：misidentify, mistake


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software identifies the user based on their fingerprint. = 该软件根据用户的指纹识别用户。
He quickly identifies the problem with the engine. = 他很快地识别出了发动机的问题。

一般过去时:
The detective identified the killer through DNA evidence. = 侦探通过DNA证据认出了凶手。
She identified her lost dog from a picture on the website. = 她从网站上的照片认出了她丢失的狗。

现在完成时:
Scientists have identified a new species of plant. = 科学家们已经发现了一种新的植物物种。
They have identified several potential risks in the project. = 他们已经确定了该项目中的几个潜在风险。

过去完成时:
By the time the police arrived, they had already identified the perpetrator. = 当警察到达时，他们已经认出了罪犯。
She had identified the problem before anyone else did. = 在其他人之前，她就已经发现了这个问题。

将来时:
We will identify the best candidate for the job. = 我们将确定最适合这份工作的候选人。
The doctors will identify the cause of the illness soon. = 医生们很快就会查明疾病的原因。

虚拟语气:
If I identified the issue earlier, I could have avoided the trouble. = 如果我早点发现问题，我就能避免麻烦了。
I wish I had identified the flaw in the plan before it was implemented. = 我希望在计划实施之前，我已经发现了计划中的缺陷。

被动语态:
The thief was identified by a witness. = 小偷被一个目击者认了出来。
The problem was quickly identified and solved. = 问题很快就被发现并解决了。

疑问句:
Can you identify the suspect in the lineup? = 你能从列队中认出嫌疑犯吗？
How did you identify the source of the error? = 你是如何确定错误来源的？

否定句:
I couldn't identify the voice on the phone. = 我无法辨认电话里的声音。
They failed to identify the cause of the accident. = 他们未能查明事故原因。
