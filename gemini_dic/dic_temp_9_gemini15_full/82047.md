/ˈɡrɪstɪd/ | adj. | 磨碎的；碾碎的 |  Ground or crushed into small pieces, typically grain.  | The gristed wheat was ready for baking.  磨碎的小麦准备烘焙了。 | ground, pulverized | whole, unground |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The miller grinds the wheat. = 磨坊主磨碎小麦。
The machine grinds the coffee beans. = 机器磨碎咖啡豆。


一般过去时:
The farmer gristed the corn last week. = 农民上周碾碎了玉米。
They gristed the barley for beer. = 他们碾碎大麦酿啤酒。


现在完成时:
The mill has gristed tons of grain this year. = 今年这家磨坊已经碾碎了数吨谷物。
She has gristed all the spices for the curry. = 她已经把咖喱的所有香料都磨碎了。


过去完成时:
They had gristed the flour before the baker arrived. = 烘焙师到达之前，他们已经磨好了面粉。
The miller had gristed the oats before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，磨坊主已经磨好了燕麦。


将来时:
The farmer will grist the wheat tomorrow. = 农民明天将碾碎小麦。
The mill will grist the grain next week. = 磨坊下周将碾碎谷物。


虚拟语气:
If he had a mill, he would grist the grain himself. = 如果他有一家磨坊，他就会自己碾碎谷物。
I wish I had gristed the pepper earlier. = 我真希望我早点把胡椒磨碎了。


被动语态:
The wheat was gristed by the miller. = 小麦是由磨坊主磨碎的。
The corn was gristed using a modern machine. = 玉米是用现代机器磨碎的。


疑问句:
Was the grain gristed properly? = 谷物磨碎得妥当吗？
Has the pepper been gristed yet? = 胡椒磨碎了吗？


否定句:
The miller didn't grist the wheat today. = 磨坊主今天没有磨小麦。
They haven't gristed the barley yet. = 他们还没有碾碎大麦。
