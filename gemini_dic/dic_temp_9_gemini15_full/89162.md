homely:/ˈhoʊmli/| adj.|家常的；朴实的；其貌不扬的；舒适的 |

英文解析:Homely can have slightly different meanings depending on context.  It can describe something that is simple and comfortable, like a homely atmosphere. It can also describe someone's appearance as unattractive or plain, though this usage is becoming less common and can be considered offensive.  The original meaning referred to something characteristic of home or family life.

例句:

The small cottage had a warm and homely atmosphere. = 这间小别墅有着温暖舒适的氛围。

She was a homely woman, but kind and generous. = 她是一位其貌不扬的女子，但是善良慷慨。(This usage is less common and potentially offensive)

近义词:cozy, comfortable, unpretentious, plain (for appearance), simple

反义词:elegant, luxurious, glamorous, sophisticated (for appearance), showy
