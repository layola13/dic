recollects:/ˌrekəˈlektz/| v.| 回忆，想起 | To bring (something) back to mind; remember.  |例句：He recollects his childhood with fondness. 他怀着 fondness 回忆他的童年。|例句：I can't recollect where I put my keys. 我记不起我把钥匙放在哪里了。|近义词：remember, recall, remember,  |反义词：forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recollects the details of the accident clearly. 他清晰地回忆起事故的细节。
She recollects her grandmother's stories often. 她经常回忆起祖母的故事。

一般过去时:
I recollect meeting him at a conference last year. 我记得去年在一次会议上见过他。
They recollected the events of that fateful day. 他们回忆起那命运攸关的一天发生的事情。


现在完成时:
Have you recollected everything you need for the trip? 你已经记起旅行所需的一切了吗？
She has recollected the address now. 她现在已经想起了地址。


过去完成时:
I had recollected the password before I logged in. 我在登录之前已经记起了密码。
He had recollected the information before the meeting. 他在会议之前已经记起了信息。


将来时:
I will recollect the details when I have more time. 当我有更多时间的时候我会回忆细节。
She will recollect the poem once she sees the book. 一旦她看到书，她就会想起这首诗。


虚拟语气:
If I could recollect the answer, I would tell you. 如果我能想起答案，我会告诉你的。
I wish I could recollect what he said. 我真希望我能想起他说的话。


被动语态:
The incident was recollected by many witnesses. 这起事件被许多目击者回忆起来。
The details of the meeting were carefully recollected in the minutes. 会议的细节被仔细地记录在会议记录中。


疑问句:
Do you recollect the name of the restaurant? 你还记得那家餐馆的名字吗？
Can you recollect what happened next? 你能回忆起接下来发生的事情吗？


否定句:
I cannot recollect his name. 我记不起他的名字。
She doesn't recollect having met him before. 她不记得以前见过他。
