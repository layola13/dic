picas:/ˈpɪkəz/| n.|印刷单位，活字排印单位|A pica is a unit of measurement used in typography and typesetting, traditionally equal to 12 points.  It's used to measure the width of columns and the length of lines in printed material.  The exact size of a pica can vary slightly depending on the system used, but it's generally around 4.23 mm or 0.166 inches.|例句：The designer specified the column width in picas.|近义词：point, em|反义词：None
