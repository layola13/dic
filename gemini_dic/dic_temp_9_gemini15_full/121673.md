misplant:/ˌmɪsˈplænt/| vt.|误植，栽错地方|To plant in the wrong place or incorrectly.|The gardener misplanted the seedlings, resulting in poor growth. 园丁把幼苗栽错了地方，导致生长不良。|近义词：misplace, mislocate|反义词：plant correctly
