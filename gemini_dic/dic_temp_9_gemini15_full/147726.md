overprinted:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪntɪd/| adj.|印在…上面的；重印的|Printed on top of something else, often obscuring what was already there.  The term implies that the new printing is on top of existing print.|例句：The new address was overprinted on the old label. 新地址被印在了旧标签上。

The stamp was overprinted with a new value. 邮票上加盖了新的面值。


近义词：superimposed, overlaid, stamped

反义词：underprinted (less common)
