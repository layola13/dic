pheasantry:/ˈfiːzən(t)ri/| n.|养雉场；雉类养殖场|A pheasantry is a place where pheasants are bred and raised, often for hunting or sport.  It can also refer to a place where pheasants are kept for ornamental purposes.|例句：The pheasantry is home to hundreds of colourful pheasants.  这个养雉场是数百只五彩斑斓的雉类的家园。|The pheasantry was a popular tourist attraction.  这个养雉场是一个受欢迎的旅游景点。|近义词：game farm, aviary (if also housing other birds)|反义词：无
