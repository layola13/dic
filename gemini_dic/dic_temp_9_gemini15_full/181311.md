reinterrogating:/ˌriːɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtɪŋ/| v.|再审问，重新盘问|The act of questioning someone again, often to clarify previous answers or to obtain further information.  It implies a more thorough or detailed questioning than the initial interrogation.|例句：The police were reinterrogating the suspect about his alibi.  警方正在重新盘问嫌疑人关于他的不在场证明。

例句：The judge ordered the witness to be reinterrogated regarding inconsistencies in their testimony.  法官命令对证人进行再次盘问，以解决其证词中的不一致之处。


近义词：re-examine, question again, cross-examine, re-interview


反义词：dismiss, overlook, ignore


词性:动词
