slingshots: /ˈslɪŋʃɒts/ | n. | 弹弓；投石器 | A slingshot is a simple weapon consisting of a Y-shaped frame with an elastic band used to propel a projectile, typically a small stone or pellet.  |  例句：He used a slingshot to shoot down the annoying bird. 他用弹弓打下那只烦人的鸟。

例句：The boy built a slingshot from a forked stick and rubber bands. 男孩用树枝分叉和橡皮筋做了一个弹弓。


近义词：catapult, projectile weapon | 反义词：None
