lazily:/ˈleɪzɪli/| adv.|懒洋洋地，懒散地|In a lazy manner; without energy or enthusiasm.  |例句：He lazily stretched out on the sofa. 他懒洋洋地躺在沙发上。|例句：She lazily flipped through the pages of the magazine. 她懒洋洋地翻阅着杂志。|近义词：indolently, sluggishly, languidly|反义词：energetically, vigorously, diligently|副词
