percentile:/pərˈsɛntaɪl/| n. |百分位数|A percentile is a value below which a given percentage of observations in a group of observations falls.  For example, the 20th percentile is the value below which 20% of the observations can be found.|Example:  Her score was in the 90th percentile, meaning she scored higher than 90% of the test takers.  她的分数在百分位数的第90位，这意味着她的分数高于90%的考生。|近义词：quantile|反义词：无
