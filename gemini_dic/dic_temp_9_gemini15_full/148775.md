overurging:/ˌoʊvərˈɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | n. | 过分劝说；过度催促 | The act of urging or persuading someone excessively.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the urging is excessive, inappropriate, or even harmful. | Example:  The overurging of the child to eat more led to an aversion to food.  孩子被过度劝说多吃，导致厌食。| 近义词： importuning, pressing,  coaxing (in a negative context) | 反义词：  under-persuasion,  gentle encouragement
