doorless:/ˈdɔːrləs/ | adj. | 无门的；没有门的 | Lacking doors.  | Example: The old shed was doorless and exposed to the elements.  旧棚屋没有门，暴露在风吹雨打中。The car had a doorless design for easier access. 这辆车采用无门设计，方便进出。|近义词：unbarred (if referring to a lack of barrier), open (if referring to the absence of a closing mechanism)|反义词：doored
