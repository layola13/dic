scrolled:/skroʊld/|/skrəʊld/| v.|滚动；卷动；浏览；缓慢移动|To move smoothly over a surface; to read or look through something by moving it along.|He scrolled through the endless newsfeed on his phone. 他在手机上滚动浏览无穷无尽的新闻推送。|She scrolled the parchment to find the correct passage. 她卷动羊皮纸以找到正确的段落。|  unfurled, glided, perused | stopped, halted |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I scroll through social media every day. = 我每天都会浏览社交媒体。
He scrolls down the document to find the information he needs. = 他滚动文档以查找所需信息。


一般过去时:
She scrolled through the photos on her phone. = 她浏览了手机上的照片。
They scrolled down the list of names. = 他们向下滚动名单。


现在完成时:
I have scrolled through this website several times. = 我已经浏览过这个网站几次了。
He has scrolled past many irrelevant ads. = 他已经滚动浏览了很多不相关的广告。


过去完成时:
She had scrolled to the bottom of the page before she realized it. = 她直到意识到才滚动到页面底部。
They had scrolled through the entire document before finding the answer. = 他们浏览了整篇文档才找到答案。


将来时:
I will scroll through the images later. = 我稍后会浏览这些图片。
She will scroll down to the comments section. = 她将向下滚动到评论部分。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would scroll through all the comments. = 如果我有更多时间，我会浏览所有评论。
I wish I could scroll back in time. = 我希望我能回到过去。


被动语态:
The document was scrolled to the beginning. = 文件被滚动到开头。
The website is often scrolled through by users. = 网站经常被用户浏览。


疑问句:
Have you scrolled to the end yet? = 你已经滚动到结尾了吗？
Can you scroll up to show me the chart again? = 你能向上滚动再次给我显示图表吗？


否定句:
I didn't scroll through the whole article. = 我没有浏览整篇文章。
He couldn't scroll down any further. = 他无法再向下滚动。


PHRASE->
scroll down = 向下滚动
scroll up = 向上滚动
scroll through = 浏览
scroll to = 滚动到
infinite scroll = 无限滚动
