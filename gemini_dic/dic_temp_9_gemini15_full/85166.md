hardworking:/ˈhɑːrdˌwɜːrkɪŋ/ | adj. |勤奋的，努力工作的 |Diligent and persistent in one's work or efforts.  |例句：She is a hardworking student and always gets good grades. 她是一个勤奋的学生，成绩总是很好。
例句：He's a hardworking employee who always goes the extra mile. 他是一位勤奋的员工，总是加倍努力。|近义词：industrious, diligent, assiduous, dedicated |反义词：lazy, indolent, idle, slothful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hardworking. = 他很勤奋。
She is hardworking and ambitious. = 她勤奋又雄心勃勃。


一般过去时:
He was hardworking when he was young. = 他年轻的时候很勤奋。
They were hardworking throughout the project. = 他们在整个项目期间都很勤奋。


现在完成时:
She has been hardworking all year. = 她一年来一直很勤奋。
He has always been hardworking. = 他一直都很勤奋。


过去完成时:
She had been hardworking for months before she finally succeeded. = 她努力工作了几个月后终于成功了。
They had been hardworking all day before they took a break. = 他们工作了一整天后才休息。


将来时:
He will be hardworking in his new job. = 他在新工作中会很勤奋。
They will be hardworking to meet the deadline. = 他们会努力工作以赶上截止日期。


虚拟语气:
If he were hardworking, he would succeed. = 如果他勤奋的话，他会成功的。
If she had been hardworking, she would have passed the exam. = 如果她勤奋些，她就通过考试了。


被动语态:
(Hardworking is not typically used in the passive voice.)  勤奋通常不用于被动语态。


疑问句:
Is he hardworking? = 他勤奋吗？
Are they hardworking enough? = 他们够勤奋吗？


否定句:
He is not hardworking. = 他不勤奋。
She wasn't hardworking enough to finish the task on time. = 她不够勤奋，无法按时完成任务。


PHRASE->
a hardworking person = 一个勤奋的人
a hardworking student = 一个勤奋的学生
hardworking employee = 勤奋的员工
be hardworking = 勤奋
hardworking attitude = 勤奋的态度
