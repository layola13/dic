libration:/laɪˈbreɪʃən/| n. | 天平动；摆动；振动 | The slight oscillatory movement of the Moon as seen from the Earth, caused by variations in its orbital speed.  It's a rocking or swaying motion.  | Example: The libration of the Moon allows us to see slightly more than half its surface over time. 月球的秤动使我们能够随着时间的推移看到超过其表面一半的景象。 |近义词：oscillation, swaying, rocking |反义词：stability, stillness
