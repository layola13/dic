lago:/ˈleɪɡoʊ/| n. |泻湖；湖泊|A lagoon is a shallow body of water, often separated from a larger body of water by a barrier reef or a strip of land. Lagoons are typically calm and sheltered, making them popular for swimming, boating, and other recreational activities.  They can also be important habitats for various plant and animal species.|Example: The turquoise lagoon shimmered under the midday sun.  这个绿松石色的泻湖在中午的阳光下闪闪发光。 The resort boasted a beautiful lagoon perfect for swimming.  这家度假村拥有一个美丽的泻湖，非常适合游泳。|近义词：lake, pond, bay|反义词：ocean, sea
