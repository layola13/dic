directing:/dəˈrektɪŋ/ | v. |指导；导演；指示；管理 |The act of guiding or controlling something or someone. It implies leading, overseeing, and managing a process or people to achieve a specific goal.  |例句：She is directing the new marketing campaign. 她正在指导新的市场营销活动。

例句：The film director is directing the actors in a scene.  这位电影导演正在指导演员们拍摄一个场景。


近义词：managing, supervising, guiding, leading, overseeing | 反义词：following, obeying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He directs the orchestra with passion. 他充满激情地指挥乐队。
She directs her employees effectively. 她有效地指导她的员工。


一般过去时:
He directed the traffic around the accident. 他指挥交通绕过事故现场。
She directed the company through a difficult period. 她带领公司度过了艰难时期。


现在完成时:
He has directed several award-winning films. 他执导过几部获奖电影。
She has directed the project successfully. 她成功地指导了这个项目。


过去完成时:
He had directed the play before he started on the film. 他在开始拍电影之前就已经导演过这部戏剧了。
She had directed the team for five years before she retired. 她退休前已经指导这个团队五年了。


将来时:
He will direct the next movie in the franchise. 他将执导该系列电影的下一部。
She will direct the new marketing strategy. 她将指导新的营销策略。


虚拟语气:
If she directed the project, it would be finished on time. 如果她指导这个项目，它就能按时完成。
I wish he directed the company with more decisiveness. 我希望他以更果断的方式来管理公司。


被动语态:
The play was directed by a renowned director. 这部戏是由一位著名的导演执导的。
The team was directed to focus on the most important tasks. 团队被指示专注于最重要的任务。


疑问句:
Who is directing the film? 谁在执导这部电影？
Who directed the marketing campaign? 谁指导了这次市场营销活动？


否定句:
He didn't direct the traffic properly. 他没有正确地指挥交通。
She didn't direct the project effectively. 她没有有效地指导这个项目。
