personalize:/ˈpɜːrsənəlaɪz/ | /ˈpɜːrsənəlaɪz/ | vt. |使个性化；定制；专门为…制作 |To make or adapt (something) to suit a particular person or group of people.  | 例句：The software allows you to personalize your desktop.  软件允许你个性化你的桌面。

例句：We personalized the marketing campaign to target specific demographics. 我们对营销活动进行了个性化设置，以针对特定的目标群体。


近义词：customize, tailor, individualize, adapt

反义词：generalize, standardize


词性：动词


USAGE->

They personalize their marketing emails. = 他们个性化他们的营销邮件。
I personalize my workout routine every month. = 我每个月都会个性化我的锻炼计划。

词源:
The word "personalize" comes from the word "personal," meaning relating to a particular person.  The suffix "-ize" is a verb-forming suffix, meaning "to make or become."  Therefore, "personalize" means "to make personal" or "to adapt to a particular person."
