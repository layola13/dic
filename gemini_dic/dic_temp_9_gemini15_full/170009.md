protectingness:/prəˈtɛktɪŋnəs/ | n. |保护性；保护欲；关爱 |The feeling of wanting to protect someone or something.  It often implies a sense of care and responsibility.  This feeling can be positive, like the protectiveness a parent feels for their child, or negative, like the overprotectiveness that can stifle independence.|例句：Her protectiveness towards her younger brother was evident in her constant vigilance.  她对弟弟的保护欲在她持续的警觉中显而易见。

The intense protectiveness of the mother bear towards her cubs kept predators away. 母熊对幼崽的强烈保护欲使捕食者远离。|近义词： protectiveness, guardianship, care, shielding |反义词：neglect, indifference, abandonment |词性:名词
