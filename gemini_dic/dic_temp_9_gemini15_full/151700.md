partnerships:/ˈpɑːtnərʃɪps/ | n. |伙伴关系；合作关系 |A partnership is a relationship between two or more people, organizations, or countries who agree to work together.  It often involves sharing resources, responsibilities, and profits. |例句：The two companies formed a partnership to develop new software. 两家公司结成伙伴关系来开发新软件。  Our partnership with the local community has been very successful. 我们与当地社区的合作非常成功。 |近义词：collaboration, alliance, joint venture |反义词：competition, rivalry
