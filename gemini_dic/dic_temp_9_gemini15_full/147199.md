overmagnifying: /ˌoʊvərˈmæɡnɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 过度放大；夸大 |  The act of magnifying something excessively, often distorting its true nature or importance.  It implies an exaggeration that goes beyond simple magnification.  |  例句：He was overmagnifying the significance of a minor setback. 他过度夸大了一个小挫折的重要性。

例句：Her anxiety over the presentation was overmagnifying the potential problems. 她对演示的焦虑夸大了潜在的问题。


近义词：exaggerating, hyperbolizing, inflating, overemphasizing

反义词：downplaying, minimizing, understating

词性：动词
