expectancies:/ɪkˈspektənsiz/| n. |期望；预期；期待 |The plural of expectancy.  It refers to the things that are expected or anticipated.  It can also refer to the likelihood of something happening. |例句：The doctor's expectancies for the patient's recovery were high. 医生对病人康复的预期很高。  The high expectancies surrounding the new product launch put pressure on the team. 新产品发布会带来的高期望给团队带来了压力。|近义词：anticipations, expectations, hopes |反义词：disappointments, uncertainties |名词
