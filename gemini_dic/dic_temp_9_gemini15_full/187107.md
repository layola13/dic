rivulets:/ˈrɪvəlɪts/| n.|小溪；细流|Small streams or brooks.  Rivulets are typically smaller than rivers and often flow in a less defined channel.|例句：Tiny rivulets of water trickled down the mountainside.  =  细细的溪流从山坡上涓涓流下。
例句：After the rain, numerous rivulets formed in the fields. = 雨后，田野里形成了许多小溪。|近义词：streamlets, brooklets, creeks|反义词：river, ocean
