okeydokey:/ˈoʊkiˈdoʊki/ | int. | 好吧，行，可以 |  An informal and playful way of saying "okay." It emphasizes agreement or acceptance in a lighthearted manner.  | Example: "Okeydokey, I'll meet you there at 7." 好吧，我七点在那里见你。 | 近义词：okay, alright, all right, yes | 反义词：no, nope
