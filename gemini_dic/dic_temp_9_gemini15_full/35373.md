co-occur:/ˌkoʊəˈkɜːr/| v. |共同出现，同时发生 | To occur at the same time or in the same place.  |例句：The symptoms of the disease often co-occur.  这种疾病的症状往往同时出现。|例句：Stress and anxiety often co-occur. 压力和焦虑经常同时出现。|近义词：occur together, happen simultaneously, concur |反义词：exclude, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The symptoms co-occur frequently. = 这些症状经常同时出现。
Stress and depression co-occur in many individuals. = 许多人在压力和抑郁症同时出现。

一般过去时:
The earthquake and tsunami co-occurred in 2004. = 地震和海啸在2004年同时发生。
The two events co-occurred unexpectedly. = 这两件事出乎意料地同时发生。

现在完成时:
Several studies have shown that these two factors have co-occurred. = 几项研究表明这两个因素同时出现过。
These symptoms have co-occurred in many patients. = 许多患者都出现过这些症状。

过去完成时:

It was later discovered that the two phenomena had co-occurred for many years. = 后来发现这两种现象已经同时存在多年了。

将来时:
We expect that these events will co-occur next year. = 我们预计这些事件明年将同时发生。
The scientists predict that the two phenomena will co-occur under certain conditions. = 科学家预测这两种现象在特定条件下会同时发生。

虚拟语气:

It would be surprising if these events co-occurred simultaneously. = 如果这些事件同时发生，那将会令人惊讶。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
