sage:/seɪdʒ/| n. adj.|圣人；贤人；鼠尾草；明智的；|a wise person, especially one who is old; a plant with grayish-green leaves used in cooking and medicine.  |例句：He was a sage, revered for his wisdom and counsel. 他是一位圣人，以他的智慧和劝告而受人尊敬。|例句：The chef added a sprig of sage to the roast chicken. 厨师在烤鸡里加了一小枝鼠尾草。|近义词：wise man, philosopher, herb|反义词：fool, ignoramus


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a wise sage. = 他是一位睿智的圣人。
The sage grows well in this climate. = 鼠尾草在这种气候下生长良好。

一般过去时:
She consulted a sage for guidance. = 她向一位智者寻求指导。
They used sage to flavor the dish. = 他们用鼠尾草调味这道菜。

现在完成时:
He has become a respected sage in his community. = 他已成为社区中受人尊敬的智者。
We have grown sage in our garden for years. = 我们多年来一直在花园里种植鼠尾草。

过去完成时:
They had consulted the village sage before making a decision. = 在做决定之前，他们已经咨询过村里的智者。
She had already added sage to the stuffing before realizing she didn't have enough. = 在意识到鼠尾草不够之前，她已经把鼠尾草加到馅料里了。

将来时:
She will consult a sage about her future. = 她将向一位智者咨询她的未来。
We will plant more sage next spring. = 我们明年春天将种植更多的鼠尾草。

虚拟语气:
If I were a sage, I would know the answer. = 如果我是一位智者，我会知道答案。
I wish I had more sage for this recipe. = 我希望我还有更多鼠尾草来做这个食谱。

被动语态:

The decision was made after consulting a sage. = 在咨询了智者之后做出了决定。

疑问句:

否定句:
