hypermania:/ˌhaɪpəˈmeɪniə/ | n. |躁狂症，轻躁狂状态 | A state of abnormally elevated mood and energy, less severe than mania.  It is characterized by increased activity, talkativeness, and a decreased need for sleep.  Hypermania is often a symptom of bipolar disorder. | Example:  He experienced a period of hypermania characterized by impulsive spending and reckless behavior. 他经历了一段轻躁狂时期，其特征是冲动消费和鲁莽行为。 |近义词: hypomania, elevated mood |反义词: depression, apathy
