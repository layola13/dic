diplopia:/dɪˈploʊpiə/| n. |复视|The simultaneous perception of two images of a single object;  a condition in which a single object is perceived as two objects. This is often caused by issues with the eye muscles, nerves, or brain. |例句：He experienced diplopia after his head injury. 他头部受伤后出现了复视。|例句：The doctor diagnosed her diplopia as a result of muscle weakness. 医生诊断她的复视是肌肉无力造成的。|近义词：double vision |反义词：single vision
