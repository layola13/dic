fluoridization:/ˌflɔːrɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 氟化作用；加氟处理 | The process of adding fluoride to a substance, typically water or toothpaste, to prevent tooth decay.  | Example:  Water fluoridation is a public health measure to reduce cavities.  饮用水加氟是降低龋齿发生率的一项公共卫生措施。The fluoridization of toothpaste helps strengthen tooth enamel. 牙膏加氟有助于增强牙釉质。|近义词：fluorination |反义词：defluoridation
