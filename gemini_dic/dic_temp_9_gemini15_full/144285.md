outsped:/ˌaʊtˈspɛd/| vt.|超过(速度)|To surpass in speed; to exceed in velocity.|例句：The cheetah outsped the gazelle.  猎豹超过了瞪羚。|例句：The sports car outsped all the other vehicles on the highway. 这辆跑车超过了高速公路上所有其他车辆。|近义词：outstrip, outrun, exceed|反义词：lag behind, fall behind

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rabbit outspeds the tortoise. 兔子超过乌龟。
The train outspeds the bus. 火车比巴士快。


一般过去时:
The greyhound outsped the hare. 灰狗超过了野兔。
The horse outsped the carriage. 马跑得比马车快。


现在完成时:
The athlete has outsped all his competitors.  这位运动员已经超过了所有竞争对手。
The car has outsped the previous speed record. 这辆车已经超过了之前的速度记录。


过去完成时:
The cyclist had outsped the others before the final lap. 自行车手在最后一圈之前已经超过了其他人。
The runner had outsped his opponent by a considerable margin. 跑步者已经远远超过了他的对手。


将来时:
The rocket will outsped the previous record. 火箭将超过之前的记录。
I expect that the new model will outsped its predecessor. 我预计新车型将超过其前身。


虚拟语气:
If the wind were stronger, the plane would outsped its scheduled speed. 如果风更大，飞机的速度就会超过预定速度。
If he had trained harder, he might have outsped his rival. 如果他训练更努力，他可能会超过他的对手。


被动语态:
The record was outsped by the new car. 新车超过了记录。
The previous champion was outsped in the final race.  之前的冠军在决赛中被超过了。


疑问句:

否定句:
