sickie:/ˈsɪki/| n.|装病者；告假的人|A person who pretends to be ill in order to avoid work or school.  It's often used informally and carries a slightly negative connotation, implying dishonesty.|例句：He called in sick, but I suspect he was just taking a sickie. 他打电话说病了，但我怀疑他只是在装病。|近义词：malingerer, absentee, shirker|反义词：hard worker, diligent employee|名词
