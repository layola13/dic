frame:/freɪm/| n. v.|框架；结构；画面；给…装框 | To provide a structure or border for something; a single picture in a film or series of pictures; a particular perspective or way of thinking. |例句：The frame of the house is made of wood.  房子的框架是用木头做的。

The film consists of 24 frames per second.  这部电影每秒包含24帧画面。


近义词：border, structure, setting, context |反义词： shapelessness, formlessness|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frames the picture beautifully. 他把画框得好漂亮。
The window frame is made of oak. 窗框是用橡木做的。


一般过去时:
She framed the photo in a simple, elegant frame. 她用一个简单优雅的相框装裱了照片。
They framed the suspect for the crime. 他们诬陷嫌疑犯犯了罪。


现在完成时:
They have framed the painting. 他们已经给画装框了。
The media has framed the issue negatively. 媒体对这个问题的报道带有负面倾向。


过去完成时:
He had framed the painting before the exhibition. 他在展览前已经给画装框了。
The police had framed him before he even knew about the crime. 在他甚至不知道犯罪之前，警方已经陷害他了。


将来时:
I will frame this photo for my mother. 我将会把这张照片装裱起来送给我的母亲。
They will frame the new law to protect the environment. 他们将制定新的法律来保护环境。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better frame, I would display this painting. 如果我有一个更好的相框，我会把这幅画展示出来。
If they had not framed him, he would be free now. 如果他们没有陷害他，他现在就自由了。


被动语态:
The picture was framed by a professional. 这幅画是由专业人士装裱的。
The suspect was framed by a rival company. 嫌疑人被竞争对手公司陷害了。


疑问句:
How do you frame a picture perfectly? 你怎样才能完美地给照片装框？
What frame rate is this video recorded in? 这段视频是用什么帧率录制的？


否定句:
I didn't frame the photo yet. 我还没给照片装框。
He didn't frame the suspect, he only found the evidence. 他没有陷害嫌疑人，他只是发现了证据。
