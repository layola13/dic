persnickety:/pərˈsnɪkɪti/| adj.|吹毛求疵的，过分讲究的，挑剔的|Extremely fussy or fastidious, especially about small details.  The word suggests an excessive concern for minor details and a tendency to find fault. |例句：She's so persnickety about her food; she won't eat anything that isn't perfectly prepared. 她对食物非常挑剔；她不吃任何准备不完美的食物。

例句：He's a persnickety dresser, always making sure his tie is perfectly aligned. 他是一个非常讲究穿着的人，总是确保他的领带完全对齐。


近义词：fussy, fastidious, meticulous, picky, overcritical, nitpicky

反义词：easygoing, tolerant, lax, unconcerned

词性：形容词
