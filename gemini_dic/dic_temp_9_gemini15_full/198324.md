shovelling: /ˈʃʌvəlɪŋ/ | v. | 铲；铲运 |  The act of using a shovel to move something.  It often refers to moving loose materials like snow, dirt, or coal. | 例句：He spent the morning shovelling snow from the driveway. = 他上午花了时间铲除车道上的积雪。


例句：The construction workers were shovelling gravel into the trucks. = 建筑工人正在用铲子往卡车上铲砾石。


近义词：scooping, digging,  pitching


反义词：None directly opposite, but actions like "removing" or "clearing" could be considered contrasting in some contexts.


词性: 动词


USAGE->

He shovels snow every winter. = 他每年冬天都铲雪。
They shovel the grain into the silo. = 他们把谷物铲进筒仓。


一般过去时:
I shovelled the path yesterday. = 我昨天铲了路。

现在完成时:

They had shovelled the coal into the furnace before the power went out. =停电前，他们已将煤铲进熔炉。


将来时:
