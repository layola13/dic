reforestations:/ˌriːˌfɒrɪˈsteɪʃənz/ | n. | 再造林；重新造林 | The act or process of planting trees in a previously forested area that has been cleared or degraded.  It aims to restore the forest ecosystem and its associated benefits. | 例句：The government launched a large-scale reforestation project to combat desertification.  政府启动了一个大规模的重新造林项目来对抗沙漠化。

The reforestations in the Amazon have been significantly impacted by illegal logging. 亚马逊地区的重新造林工作受到了非法砍伐的严重影响。|近义词：afforestation, replanting, tree planting | 反义词：deforestation
