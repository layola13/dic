literatures:/ˈlɪtəreɪtʃərz/ | n. |文学作品；文献；著作 | The body of written works produced in a particular language, country, or period.  It encompasses various forms of creative writing, such as novels, poems, plays, and essays. |  例句：She is studying the literatures of the Victorian era. 她正在研究维多利亚时代的文学作品。 |例句：The professor's research focuses on the comparative literatures of East and West. 教授的研究重点是东西方比较文学。|近义词：writings, works, texts |反义词：None |名词
