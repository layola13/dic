polypetalous:/ˌpɒliˈpetələs/ | adj. | 多瓣的；多花瓣的 | Having many petals.  This term is used to describe flowers with numerous separate petals, as opposed to flowers with fused petals (gamopetalous). The petals are distinct and not joined together. | Example:  The rose is a polypetalous flower.  玫瑰花是多瓣花的。


The tulip displays a characteristic polypetalous structure.  郁金香展现出典型的多瓣结构。|近义词：many-petaled |反义词：gamopetalous
