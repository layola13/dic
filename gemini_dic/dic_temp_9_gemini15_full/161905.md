pragmatics:/præɡˈmætɪks/| n. |语用学|The branch of linguistics dealing with language in use and the contexts in which it is used, including its social, cultural, and cognitive aspects.|Example:  Pragmatics studies how context contributes to meaning.  语用学研究语境如何影响意义。|近义词：contextual linguistics, discourse analysis |反义词：semantics (in some contexts)
