punching-up: /ˈpʌntʃɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 润色，改进，增强喜剧效果 | To improve something, especially a piece of writing or a performance, by making it more exciting, humorous, or interesting.  |例句：He spent weeks punching up the script before the final rehearsal. 他花了几个星期的时间润色剧本，直到最终彩排。

例句：The comedian punched up his routine with some topical jokes.  这位喜剧演员用一些时事笑话来增强他的表演。


近义词：improving, enhancing, sharpening, refining

反义词：dumbing down, weakening, diluting
