outsteal:/ˌaʊtˈstiːl/|v.|胜过；技高一筹|To surpass someone in skill or ability, especially in stealing or deception.|例句：The master thief managed to outsteal his apprentice, proving his superior skills.  高手窃贼成功地胜过他的学徒，证明了他的高超技能。

The rival company tried to outsteal our market share by undercutting our prices. 对手公司试图通过压低价格来夺取我们的市场份额。|近义词：outdo, surpass, outmaneuver|反义词：be outdone, be surpassed

一般过去时:
