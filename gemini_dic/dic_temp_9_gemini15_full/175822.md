rasp:/ræsp/| n. v.|刮擦声；粗糙的嗓音；粗锉；用力刮擦| a rough, grating sound; a harsh voice; a coarse file; to scrape roughly|The old door rasped open.  这扇旧门嘎吱一声开了。|The speaker's rasping voice was hard to hear.  演讲者沙哑的声音很难听清。|近义词：scrape, grate, scratch|反义词：smooth, soft


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rasps his throat. 他擦拭他的喉咙。
The rusty hinge rasps. 生锈的铰链发出摩擦声。


一般过去时:
The metal rasped against the stone. 金属摩擦着石头。
She rasped a reply. 她粗声粗气地回答。


现在完成时:
He has rasped the wood smooth. 他已经把木头磨光滑了。
The constant coughing has rasped her voice.  不断的咳嗽使她的嗓子沙哑了。


过去完成时:
The old woman had rasped out her story before she fainted. 老妇人在晕倒前断断续续地讲完了她的故事。
The wind had rasped the paint from the old house. 风已将旧房子的油漆刮掉了。


将来时:
The dry leaves will rasp against the window. 干树叶会摩擦窗户。
He will rasp the paint off the wall. 他将把墙上的油漆刮掉。


虚拟语气:
If he rasped the wood any harder, he would break it. 如果他再用力刮木头，就会把它弄坏。
I wish he wouldn't rasp his voice so much. 我希望他不要那么沙哑地说话。


被动语态:
The wood was rasped smooth. 木头被磨光滑了。
The paint was rasped off the wall. 墙上的油漆被刮掉了。


疑问句:
Does the old door still rasp? 这扇旧门还会发出摩擦声吗？
Did the sound rasp your ears?  那个声音刺耳吗？


否定句:
The wood didn't rasp against the metal. 木头没有摩擦金属。
The engine didn't rasp. 发动机没有发出摩擦声。


PHRASE->
