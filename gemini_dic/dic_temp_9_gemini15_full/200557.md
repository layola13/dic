slathered:/ˈslæðərd/| vt.|涂抹，厚涂|To spread (a substance) thickly and smoothly over a surface.|例句：She slathered butter on the toast. 她把黄油厚厚地涂在吐司上。例句：He slathered sunscreen all over his body. 他把防晒霜涂抹在全身。|近义词：spread, smear, daub|反义词：scrape off, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slathers peanut butter on his sandwich. = 他在三明治上涂花生酱。
She slathers lotion on her skin. = 她把乳液涂抹在皮肤上。

一般过去时:
I slathered paint on the canvas. = 我把颜料涂在画布上。
They slathered mud on the tires. = 他们把泥巴涂在轮胎上。

现在完成时:
She has slathered sunscreen on her children. = 她已经给孩子们涂了防晒霜。
He has slathered frosting on the cake. = 他已经给蛋糕涂上了糖霜。

过去完成时:
The chef had slathered the meat in sauce before roasting it. = 大厨在烤肉之前已经把肉涂上了酱汁。
She had slathered the walls with wallpaper paste. = 她已经用壁纸胶把墙涂抹了。

将来时:
I will slather mayonnaise on my fries. = 我会在薯条上涂蛋黄酱。
They will slather honey on their pancakes. = 他们会在薄煎饼上涂蜂蜜。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would slather honey on my bread. = 如果我有更多时间，我会在面包上涂蜂蜜。
I wish I had slathered more paint on the wall. = 我希望我能在墙上多涂些油漆。

被动语态:
The bread was slathered with butter. = 面包涂上了黄油。
The cake was slathered with chocolate frosting. = 蛋糕涂上了巧克力糖霜。

疑问句:

Has she slathered the lotion on her face? = 她把乳液涂在脸上了吗？

否定句:
I didn't slather enough butter on the bread. = 我没有在面包上涂足够的黄油。
He didn't slather the paint evenly. = 他没有均匀地涂抹油漆。
