recenter:/ˌriːˈsɛntər/ | vt. |重新居中；重新定位；使…成为中心 | To reposition something so that it is in the center or to change the center of something.  |例句：The photographer had to recenter the image to correct the composition. 摄影师不得不重新居中图像以纠正构图。

例句：We need to recenter our marketing strategy on the needs of our customers. 我们需要将我们的营销策略重新定位在客户的需求上。


近义词：realign, reposition, refocus | 反义词：decentralize, displace


词性:动词
