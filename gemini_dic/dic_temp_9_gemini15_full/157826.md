plagiarisms:/ˈpleɪdʒəriˌzəm/| n.|剽窃；抄袭|The act of using another person's words or ideas without giving them credit.  It is a serious academic offense and can have serious consequences. |例句：The professor accused the student of plagiarism. 教授指控学生剽窃。

例句：The essay was full of plagiarisms, and the student failed the course. 这篇文章充满了剽窃内容，学生因此考试不及格。


近义词：theft, copying, infringement, appropriation

反义词：originality, innovation, creation


词性：名词
