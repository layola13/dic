quasi-patronizing: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈpætrənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 带有伪善的施舍意味的，假装关怀的 |


英文解析:  Appearing to be condescending or patronizing, but perhaps unintentionally or subtly so.  It suggests a slightly veiled attempt to be helpful or superior, often masked by a seeming kindness or concern.  The "quasi" prefix indicates that it's not fully or overtly patronizing, but possesses some of its characteristics.


例句:

His quasi-patronizing tone, while offering advice, actually made me feel more inadequate.  他的语气看似关切地提供建议，实际上却让我感觉更无能。

Her quasi-patronizing smile, though seemingly friendly, hinted at a sense of superiority.  她看似友好的微笑，实则暗含着优越感。


近义词: condescending, patronizing, slightly superior, subtly condescending, faintly patronizing


反义词: genuinely helpful, supportive, respectful, humble


词性: adjective (形容词)
