re-cement:/ˌriːsɪˈment/| vt.|重新胶合；巩固；加强|To cement again; to strengthen or reinforce.|The broken vase was carefully re-cemented.|近义词：reinforce, strengthen, consolidate|反义词：break, weaken, destroy|动词


USAGE->

We re-cement our friendship with regular phone calls. = 我们通过定期打电话来巩固我们的友谊。
