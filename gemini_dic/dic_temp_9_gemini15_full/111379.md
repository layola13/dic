livestock:/ˈlaɪvˌstɒk/ | n. |牲畜，家畜 |Farm animals kept for their products (meat, milk, etc.) or for their work.  |例句：The farmer raises livestock for meat and dairy products.  这位农民饲养牲畜以获取肉类和乳制品。|例句：The drought severely impacted the livestock population. 干旱严重影响了牲畜数量。|近义词：animals, cattle, beasts |反义词：wildlife, pets


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer raises livestock. = 农民饲养牲畜。
We keep livestock on our farm. = 我们在农场里养牲畜。

一般过去时:
They sold their livestock last year. = 他们去年卖掉了他们的牲畜。
The rancher moved his livestock to higher ground. = 牧场主把他的牲畜移到了较高的地方。

现在完成时:
Farmers have increased livestock production. = 农民们已经增加了牲畜的产量。
We have lost some livestock due to the storm. = 我们因为暴风雨损失了一些牲畜。

过去完成时:
They had already sold their livestock before the price dropped. = 价格下跌之前，他们已经卖掉了他们的牲畜。
The farmer had moved his livestock to safety before the flood. = 在洪水到来之前，农民已经把牲畜转移到了安全的地方。

将来时:
We will buy more livestock next spring. = 我们明年春天会买更多的牲畜。
They will transport the livestock to the market tomorrow. = 他们明天将把牲畜运送到市场。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had enough money to buy more livestock. = 我希望我有足够的钱买更多的牲畜。
