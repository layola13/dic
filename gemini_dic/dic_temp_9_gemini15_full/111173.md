lithofracteur:/ˈlɪθəʊˌfrækʃʊər/| n. |碎石机；岩石破碎机|A lithofracteur is a machine or device used to break up rocks, typically by using explosive charges or high-pressure hydraulics.  It is often used in mining, quarrying, and construction. |Example: The miners used a powerful lithofracteur to blast through the hard granite.  矿工们使用强大的碎石机炸开坚硬的花岗岩。 The construction crew employed a lithofracteur to break up the bedrock for the new foundation. 建设队使用碎石机破碎基岩用于新的地基。|近义词：rock breaker, stone breaker, rock splitter|反义词：rock consolidator (this is a less common and more technical counterpoint)|名词
