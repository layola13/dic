remast: /riˈmæstər/ | v. | 重制，重新制作 | To remaster something is to improve its quality, typically a sound recording or a film, by using modern techniques.  This often involves enhancing the audio or video quality, removing imperfections, or adding new features. | Example:  The band remastered their classic album for its 25th anniversary. 乐队为了庆祝他们的经典专辑发行25周年而对其进行了重新制作。  The film was remastered in 4K resolution. 这部电影已重新制作成4K分辨率。 |近义词:remake, refurbish,  re-edit|反义词:destroy, ruin|动词
