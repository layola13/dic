shatter:/ˈʃætər/ | vt. n. | 粉碎；破灭；使震惊 | To break (something) violently into many pieces; to destroy completely; to severely shock or upset someone. | 例句：The earthquake shattered the building. 地震摧毁了那座建筑物。

The news shattered her hopes.  这个消息粉碎了她的希望。


近义词：smash, crash, break, crush, destroy | 反义词：mend, repair, restore, build


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The earthquake shatters buildings. = 地震摧毁建筑物。
He shatters my dreams. = 他粉碎了我的梦想。


一般过去时:
The vase shattered when it fell. = 花瓶掉下来摔碎了。
The news shattered his confidence. = 这个消息击碎了他的信心。


现在完成时:
The storm has shattered many windows. = 暴风雨已经打碎了许多窗户。
The scandal has shattered his reputation. = 丑闻毁了他的名声。


过去完成时:
The glass had shattered before I could stop it. = 我还没来得及阻止，玻璃就已经碎了。
His hopes had shattered before the competition even began. = 比赛还没开始，他的希望就已经破灭了。


将来时:
The impact will shatter the glass. = 撞击会击碎玻璃。
The news will shatter her illusions. = 这个消息将粉碎她的幻想。


虚拟语气:
If I dropped it, it would shatter. = 如果我把它扔下去，它就会碎。
If I had known the truth, it would have shattered my world. = 如果我知道真相，它就会毁掉我的世界。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
