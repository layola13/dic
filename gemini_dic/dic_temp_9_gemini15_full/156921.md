piled:/paɪld/| v.|堆积，堆叠|To put (things) in a pile or heap; to accumulate.|The books were piled high on the table. 书堆在桌子上很高。|The snow piled up during the night. 夜里雪堆积起来了。|stack, heap, accumulate|scatter, disperse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He piles his clothes on the chair. = 他把衣服堆在椅子上。
They pile the wood neatly. = 他们整齐地堆放木头。


一般过去时:
She piled the dishes in the sink. = 她把盘子堆在水槽里。
The children piled up the toys after playing. = 孩子们玩完后把玩具堆了起来。


现在完成时:
He has piled up a lot of debt. = 他已经积累了很多债务。
They have piled the sandbags against the wall. = 他们已经把沙袋堆在墙边。


过去完成时:
The snow had piled up to the roof before the storm stopped. = 风暴停下来之前，雪已经堆到了屋顶。
They had piled all the leaves into a large heap. = 他们已经把所有的树叶堆成一堆。


将来时:
They will pile the lumber in the shed. = 他们会把木材堆放在棚子里。
We will pile all the dirty clothes into the washing machine. = 我们会把所有脏衣服都堆进洗衣机。


虚拟语气:
If I had piled the books more carefully, they wouldn't have fallen. = 如果我把书堆得更仔细一些，它们就不会掉下来了。
I wish I hadn't piled so much work onto myself. = 我真希望我没有给自己安排那么多工作。


被动语态:
The wood was piled neatly in the corner. = 木材被整齐地堆放在角落里。
The sandbags were piled high to protect the house from flooding. = 沙袋被堆得很高，以保护房子免受洪水侵袭。


疑问句:
Have you piled all the papers yet? = 你把所有的文件都堆好了吗？
Where did they pile the rubbish? = 他们把垃圾堆在哪里了？


否定句:
Don't pile your dirty clothes on the floor. = 不要把你的脏衣服堆在地板上。
He didn't pile the books properly, so they fell. = 他没有把书堆好，所以它们掉下来了。


PHRASE->
pile up: 堆积；积累
pile on:  大量增加；猛烈攻击
pile into:  挤进
pile high: 堆得很高
a pile of: 一堆
