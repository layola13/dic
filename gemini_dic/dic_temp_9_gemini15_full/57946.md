drip-dried:/ˈdrɪpdraɪd/| adj.|滴干的，晾干的|  Describes clothes or laundry that have been allowed to dry by dripping after being washed, rather than being machine-dried or put in a dryer. This method often results in less shrinkage and damage to the clothes. |例句：I prefer to drip-dry my delicates to prevent them from shrinking. 我喜欢把我的精致衣物滴干以防止它们缩水。

例句：The drip-dried shirts were still slightly damp. 滴干的衬衫还略微潮湿。

近义词：air-dried, line-dried

反义词：machine-dried, tumble-dried


词性：形容词
