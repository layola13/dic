deuteropathy:/djuːtəˈrɒpəθi/| n. |继发性疾病，继发病|A secondary disease or disorder resulting from a primary condition.  It refers to a disease that develops as a consequence of another disease or condition.|Example:  The patient's deuteropathy, a debilitating nerve condition, stemmed from the initial viral infection.  这位病人的继发性疾病，一种使人衰弱的神经疾病，源于最初的病毒感染。|近义词：secondary disease, complication, sequela|反义词：primary disease, initial condition|名词
