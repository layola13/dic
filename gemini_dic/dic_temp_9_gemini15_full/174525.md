quintal:/ˈkwɪntəl/| n. |百公斤；担；昆塔尔|A quintal is a unit of weight equal to 100 kilograms.  It is used in some countries, particularly in the context of agricultural products or bulk commodities.|例句：The farmer harvested a quintal of rice this season.  这个农民本季收获了一百公斤稻米。|近义词：hundredweight (cwt), metric hundredweight|反义词：无
