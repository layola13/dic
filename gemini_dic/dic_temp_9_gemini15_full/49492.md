delving:/ˈdɛlvɪŋ/| v. |深入探究；钻研|  The act of thoroughly investigating or exploring a subject or area.  It implies a deep and often time-consuming process of research and discovery. |例句：She was delving into the mysteries of ancient Egypt. 她正在深入探究古埃及的奥秘。

例句：He spent years delving into the archives to find the information. 他花了数年时间深入研究档案以查找信息。


近义词：investigating, exploring, researching, probing, scrutinizing

反义词：superficially examining, glancing over, skimming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is delving into the history of the Roman Empire. = 他正在钻研罗马帝国的历史。
She delves into complex philosophical concepts. = 她深入探究复杂的哲学概念。


一般过去时:
They delved into the matter thoroughly. = 他们彻底地研究了这个问题。
The detective delved into the case with great care. = 侦探非常仔细地调查了这个案子。


现在完成时:
She has delved deeply into the subject of quantum physics. = 她已经深入研究了量子物理学。
We have been delving into this problem for weeks. = 我们已经研究这个问题几周了。


过去完成时:
They had delved into the archives before they published their findings. = 他们在发表研究结果之前已经深入研究了档案。
She had delved into the family history before she discovered the truth. = 在她发现真相之前，她已经深入研究了家族史。


将来时:
I will delve into the origins of this tradition. = 我将深入探究这项传统的起源。
They will be delving into the data for the next few months. = 他们将在接下来的几个月里深入研究这些数据。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would delve into this topic further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更深入地研究这个话题。
I wish I could delve deeper into the subject. = 我希望我能更深入地研究这个主题。


被动语态:
The issue was delved into by the committee. = 这个议题被委员会深入研究了。
The ancient texts are being delved into by scholars worldwide. = 全球的学者们正在深入研究古代文本。


疑问句:
Are you delving into anything interesting at the moment? = 你最近在研究什么有趣的东西吗？
What are they delving into? = 他们在研究什么？


否定句:
He didn't delve into the details. = 他没有深入研究细节。
They chose not to delve into the controversial aspects of the issue. = 他们选择不深入研究该问题的争议方面。


PHRASE->
delve into: 深入研究
delve deep into: 深入钻研
delve in: 开始认真研究
delve for: 寻找（信息等）
delve among: 在...中寻找


词源:
The word "delving" comes from the Old English word "delfan," meaning "to dig."  The modern meaning reflects the idea of digging deep into a subject to uncover hidden knowledge or information.
