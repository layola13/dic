progresses:/prəˈɡresɪz/| v.|进展；进步；进行；|To move forward or onward; to develop or improve.  It implies a continuous movement toward a goal or a more advanced state.|例句：The project is progressing well. 项目进展顺利。

The construction progresses slowly due to the bad weather.  由于天气恶劣，施工进展缓慢。|近义词：advances, develops, proceeds, continues|反义词：regresses, deteriorates, declines


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant progresses steadily. = 植物稳步生长。
The negotiation progresses smoothly. = 谈判进展顺利。

一般过去时:
The research progressed rapidly. = 研究进展迅速。
The disease progressed slowly. = 疾病进展缓慢。

现在完成时:
The construction has progressed significantly. = 建设已经取得了显著进展。
The talks have progressed well so far. = 会谈到目前为止进展顺利。

过去完成时:
The project had progressed considerably by the deadline. = 到截止日期为止，项目已经取得了相当大的进展。
They had progressed to the next stage before the interruption. = 在中断之前，他们已经进展到下一阶段。

将来时:
The economy will progress steadily next year. = 经济明年将稳步发展。
The work will progress more quickly with additional staff. = 增加人手后，工作进展会更快。

虚拟语气:
If the negotiations progressed smoothly, we could reach an agreement. = 如果谈判进展顺利，我们就能达成协议。
I wish the project progressed more quickly. = 我希望项目进展更快。

被动语态:
Progress is being made on the new bridge. = 新桥的建设正在取得进展。
The plan was progressed by the committee. = 该计划由委员会推进。

疑问句:
How is the project progressing? = 项目进展如何？
Is the research progressing as planned? = 研究进展是否按计划进行？

否定句:
The project isn't progressing as expected. = 项目进展不如预期。
The talks didn't progress at all. = 会谈毫无进展。
