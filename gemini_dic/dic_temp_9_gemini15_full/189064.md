saboti:/ˈsæbəˌtiː/| n. |木底鞋；破坏行动|A sabot is a wooden shoe, traditionally worn by peasants in some parts of Europe.  The term also refers to an act of sabotage,  deliberately obstructing or destroying something, often machinery or production. |例句：The workers used sabots to disrupt the factory's production. 工人们用木底鞋破坏工厂的生产。


例句：The saboteur planted a bomb to cause damage to the infrastructure. 破坏分子安放炸弹以破坏基础设施。

近义词：sabotage, obstruction, disruption

反义词：cooperation, assistance, support

词性：名词
