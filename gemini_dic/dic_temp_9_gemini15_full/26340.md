buzzes:/ˈbʌzɪz/| v.|嗡嗡叫；忙碌；充满活力|To make a continuous low humming sound, like that of a bee; to be full of activity or excitement.|例句：The bees buzzed around the flowers. 蜜蜂在花朵周围嗡嗡叫。 The city buzzed with excitement before the game.比赛前，城市里充满了兴奋的喧嚣。|近义词：hum, whirr, thrum, vibrate|反义词：be silent, be still
