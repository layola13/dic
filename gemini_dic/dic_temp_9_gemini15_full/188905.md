rustly:/ˈrʌsli/ | adj. |沙沙作响的，瑟瑟作响的 | Making a rustling sound; characterized by a rustling sound.  |例句：The leaves rustled in the breeze.  树叶在微风中沙沙作响。
The silk dress rustled as she moved. 她移动时丝绸裙子发出沙沙声。|近义词：whispering, murmuring, crinkling |反义词：silent, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaves rustle in the wind. = 树叶在风中沙沙作响。
The fabric rustles when I move. = 我动的时候布料发出沙沙声。


一般过去时:
The wind rustled the leaves last night. = 昨晚风吹动树叶发出沙沙声。
The paper rustled as she opened the envelope. = 她打开信封时纸张发出沙沙声。


现在完成时:
The wind has rustled the leaves all morning. = 一上午风都在吹动树叶发出沙沙声。
The cat has rustled through the bushes. = 猫已经穿过灌木丛，发出沙沙声。


过去完成时:
The leaves had rustled before the storm arrived. = 在暴风雨来临之前，树叶已经沙沙作响。
The thief had rustled through the drawers before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷已经翻动抽屉发出沙沙声。

虚拟语气:
If the wind blew, the leaves would rustle. = 如果风吹了，树叶就会沙沙作响。
I wish the leaves would rustle more quietly. = 我希望树叶能更安静地沙沙作响。


被动语态:

The papers were rustled by the searching detectives. = 文件被搜查的侦探翻动发出沙沙声。


疑问句:
Do the leaves rustle in the wind? = 树叶在风中沙沙作响吗？
Does the fabric rustle when you move it? = 你移动布料时它会发出沙沙声吗？


否定句:
The leaves didn't rustle in the still air. = 在静止的空气中，树叶没有沙沙作响。
The fabric doesn't rustle; it's very quiet. = 这布料不发出沙沙声；它很安静。

词源:
The word "rustle" comes from the Middle English word "rustelen," which is of imitative origin, meaning to make a rustling sound.  The sound itself is onomatopoeic.
