get-it:/ˈɡɛtɪt/| phr. v.|明白了，懂了|It's a colloquial phrase expressing understanding or comprehension.  It often implies a previous lack of understanding that has now been resolved.|例句： "Oh, I get it now!" = "哦，我现在明白了！"  "I didn't get it at first, but now I understand." = "我一开始没明白，但现在我懂了。" |近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp|反义词：misunderstand, not understand
