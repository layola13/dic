campsites:/ˈkæmpˌsaɪts/| n. |营地；野营地 |Places designated for camping, often providing basic facilities like toilets and water.  |例句：We booked campsites in advance for our family camping trip. 我们提前预定了家庭露营旅行的营地。|例句：The campsites were full, so we had to find another place to pitch our tents. 营地满了，所以我们不得不另找地方扎帐篷。|近义词：camping grounds, campgrounds |反义词：hotel, motel
