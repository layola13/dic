recalled:/rɪˈkɔːld/|/ˈriːkɔːld/ | vt. | 回忆起；回想；召回；撤回 | To bring (something) back to mind; remember.  To officially order the return of something or someone. |例句：I recalled the day we first met. = 我回忆起了我们第一次见面的那一天。

She recalled the details of the accident. = 她回忆起了事故的细节。


例句：The manufacturer recalled the faulty product. = 制造商召回了有缺陷的产品。

The ambassador was recalled to his home country. = 大使被召回了他的祖国。


近义词：remember, recollect, retrieve, summon back | 反义词：forget, overlook

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recalls his childhood memories often. = 他经常回忆起童年往事。
She recalls the names of all her students. = 她记得所有学生的姓名。

一般过去时:
I recalled seeing him at the party last night. = 我记得昨晚在派对上见过他。
They recalled the important points of the meeting. = 他们回忆起会议的重点。

现在完成时:
Have you recalled everything you learned in school? = 你还记得学校里学到的所有东西吗？
She has recalled the incident many times. = 她已经多次回忆起这起事件。

过去完成时:
I had recalled the story before he asked. = 他问我之前我就已经回忆起那个故事了。
By the time she finished speaking, I had recalled the entire conversation. = 等她说完话，我已经回忆起了整个谈话内容。

将来时:
I will recall this day for the rest of my life. = 我将永远记住这一天。
We will recall the names of the winners later. = 我们稍后会宣布获奖者的名字。

虚拟语气:
If I recalled the password, I could log in. = 如果我记得密码，就能登录了。
I wish I could recall what I said. = 我真希望我能想起我说过什么。

被动语态:
The faulty toys were recalled by the company. = 有缺陷的玩具被公司召回了。
His statement was later recalled by the witness. = 他的证词后来被证人撤回了。

疑问句:
Do you recall what happened? = 你记得发生了什么事吗？
Can you recall the address? = 你能想起地址吗？

否定句:
I cannot recall his name. = 我记不起他的名字。
She didn't recall seeing the man before. = 她不记得以前见过这个人。


PHRASE->
recall to mind = 回忆起
recall sth to sb = 向某人回忆某事
recall a meeting = 召回会议
recall an order = 撤销命令
recall a product = 召回产品
