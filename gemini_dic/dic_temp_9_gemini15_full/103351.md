jiltish:/ˈdʒɪltɪʃ/| adj.|轻浮的；反复无常的；善变的|Characterized by fickleness or capriciousness; flighty.  It suggests a lack of seriousness or commitment, often in romantic relationships.|例句：Her jiltish behavior led to the breakup.  她的轻浮行为导致了分手。

例句：He was known for his jiltish nature and numerous failed relationships. 他以反复无常的性格和多次失败的恋情而闻名。


近义词：capricious, fickle, flighty, erratic, inconstant

反义词：constant, steadfast, reliable, dependable, faithful

词性：形容词
