precedential:/ˌprɛsɪˈdɛnʃəl/| adj.|具有先例的；可作先例的；创先例的|Creating a precedent; serving as a precedent.  Something that is precedential sets a pattern or example for future cases or situations. It establishes a legal precedent or a standard for future actions.|例句：The court’s decision in this case is precedential and will influence future rulings.  这个案件的法庭判决具有先例效力，并将影响未来的裁决。


例句：The new policy is precedential, setting a standard for all future projects. 这项新政策具有先例意义，为所有未来的项目设定了标准。


近义词：exemplary, authoritative, precedent-setting


反义词：unprecedented, novel, original


词性:形容词
