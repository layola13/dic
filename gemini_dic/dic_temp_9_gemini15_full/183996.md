reshooting:/ˌriːˈʃuːtɪŋ/| n.|重拍，重摄|The act of filming a scene or sequence again.  It's often done to correct mistakes, improve the quality, or change the scene's direction.|例句：The director decided on a reshooting of the final scene.  导演决定重拍最后一幕。|例句：The reshooting added significantly to the film's budget. 重拍大大增加了电影的预算。|近义词：refilming, retaking|反义词：first take, original shoot|名词
