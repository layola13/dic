interchangers: /ɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒərz/ | n. | 交换者；互换装置 |  People or things that exchange or are exchanged.  A device or system that facilitates the exchange of something, such as a currency exchange or a railway track switch. | 例句：The currency interchangers at the airport were busy with travelers. 机场的货币兑换处挤满了旅客。The railway interchangers allowed trains to switch tracks smoothly. 铁路交换机允许火车顺利切换轨道。 | 近义词：exchangers, converters, switches | 反义词：receivers (in the context of a one-way transfer)
