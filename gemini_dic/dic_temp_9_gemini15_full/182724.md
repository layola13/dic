repaired:/rɪˈpeəd/ | v. | 修理过的；修复过的 |  To restore something to good condition after damage or deterioration.  | The mechanic repaired my car.  机械师修理了我的车。| fixed, mended, restored | broken, damaged, destroyed | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repairs cars for a living. = 他以修理汽车为生。
She regularly repairs her own clothes. = 她定期修理自己的衣服。


一般过去时:
They repaired the roof after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们修理了屋顶。
I repaired the broken chair yesterday. = 我昨天修好了那把坏椅子。


现在完成时:
The team has repaired the bridge. =  队伍已经修好了桥。
She has repaired the fence several times this year. = 她今年已经几次修理过篱笆了。


过去完成时:
They had repaired the car before the race. = 他们在比赛前已经修好了车。
The plumber had repaired the leak before the water damage got worse. = 水管工在漏水情况恶化之前已经修好了漏水的地方。


将来时:
We will repair the computer tomorrow. = 我们明天将修理电脑。
The company will repair the damaged equipment soon. = 公司很快就会修理损坏的设备。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would repair the bicycle myself. = 如果我有工具，我会自己修理自行车。
It would have been better if they had repaired the road earlier. = 如果他们早点修路就好了。


被动语态:
The road is being repaired at the moment. = 道路正在维修中。
The broken window was repaired quickly. = 破损的窗户很快就被修理好了。


疑问句:
Has the car been repaired yet? = 车修好了吗？
Will the damage be repaired under warranty? = 保修期内会修理损坏的部分吗？


否定句:
They didn't repair the appliance. = 他们没有修理那个家用电器。
The problem hasn't been repaired yet. = 问题还没有解决。


PHRASE->
repair a relationship = 修复关系
repair damage = 修复损坏
in need of repair = 需要修理
under repair =  正在修理中
beyond repair = 无法修理


词源:
From Old French *reparer, from re- +parer "to prepare, make ready".  The sense development is from "to make ready, prepare" to "to make ready again, restore".
