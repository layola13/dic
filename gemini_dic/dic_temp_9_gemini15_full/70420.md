fighter-bombers:/ˈfaɪtərˌbɒmbərz/| n. |战斗轰炸机|A military aircraft designed to both engage in air-to-air combat and deliver bombs to ground targets.  Fighter-bombers combine the capabilities of fighter jets and bombers, offering versatility in military operations.|例句：The Air Force deployed its fighter-bombers to strike enemy positions.空军部署其战斗轰炸机打击敌方阵地。|近义词：attack aircraft, close-support aircraft|反义词：transport aircraft, reconnaissance aircraft
