medico:/ˈmɛdɪkoʊ/| n. |医生，内科医生|A medico is a doctor, especially a physician.  The term often implies a general practitioner or someone working in a hospital setting. |例句：The medico examined the patient carefully.|近义词：doctor, physician, general practitioner|反义词：patient
