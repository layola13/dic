pathologic:/ˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪk/| adj.|病理学的；病态的；患病的| relating to or caused by disease; diseased.  The report detailed the pathologic changes in the patient's lungs.|近义词：morbid, diseased, unhealthy|反义词：healthy, normal, sound


例句：

The doctor examined the pathologic tissue sample under a microscope.医生在显微镜下检查了病理组织样本。

The study focused on the pathologic effects of smoking on the respiratory system. 这项研究侧重于吸烟对呼吸系统的病理影响。
