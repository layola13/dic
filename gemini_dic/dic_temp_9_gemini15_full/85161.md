hardwares:/ˈhɑːrdˌweərz/| n.|硬件；硬件设备|Physical components of a computer system, such as the CPU, memory, and storage devices.  The term often refers to the tangible parts of a technology system, as opposed to software.|例句：The new computer has powerful hardwares. 这台新电脑有强大的硬件。

例句：We need to upgrade our hardwares to run the new software. 我们需要升级我们的硬件才能运行新软件。


近义词：equipment, apparatus, machinery, components


反义词：software


词性：名词
