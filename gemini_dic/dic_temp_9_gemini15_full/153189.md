pellicle:/ˈpɛlɪkəl/| n.|薄膜；表皮|A thin skin, membrane, or film.  For example, the pellicle on the surface of a liquid, or the outer membrane of a cell.|The wine had a thin pellicle on its surface. 这葡萄酒表面有一层薄膜。|The bacteria formed a pellicle on the agar. 细菌在琼脂上形成了一层薄膜。|membrane, film, cuticle|thick skin|名词
