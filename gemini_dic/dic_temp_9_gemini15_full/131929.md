noncontinuous: /ˌnɒnkənˈtɪnjuəs/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtɪnjuəs/ | adj. |非连续的，不连续的|Not continuous; interrupted; intermittent.  It refers to something that does not happen continuously or without interruption.  |例句：The rain was noncontinuous, falling in short bursts.  雨是断断续续地下，一阵一阵的。

例句：His work schedule is noncontinuous; he works some days and not others.他的工作时间是不连续的；他有的日子工作，有的日子不工作。

近义词：intermittent, discontinuous, sporadic, broken

反义词：continuous, uninterrupted, constant


词性：形容词
