safavid:/ˈsæfəvæd/| n.|萨非王朝|The Safavid dynasty was a powerful Iranian dynasty that ruled Persia from 1501 to 1736.  They were known for their strong centralized government, their support of Shia Islam, and their significant contributions to Persian art and architecture.|Example: The Safavid era saw a flourishing of Persian art and culture.  萨非王朝时期，波斯艺术和文化蓬勃发展。 The Safavid dynasty's influence extended far beyond Persia's borders. 萨非王朝的影响力远超波斯边界。|近义词：Timurid (related dynasty), Mughal (related dynasty)|反义词：None
