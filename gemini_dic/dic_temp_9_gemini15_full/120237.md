mingle:/ˈmɪŋɡəl/| v.|混合；交往；掺和|To move around and mix freely with others; to combine or blend.|例句：The guests mingled freely at the reception. 客人们在招待会上自由地互相交往。
例句：The sweet smell of flowers mingled with the salty air. 花的甜香与咸咸的海风混合在一起。|近义词：mix, blend, commingle|反义词：separate, segregate|动词


USAGE->

The guests mingle freely at the party. = 客人们在派对上自由地互相交往。
He always mingles with the locals when he travels. = 他旅行时总是和当地人交往。

一般过去时:
They mingled happily at the wedding. = 他们在婚礼上愉快地互相交往。
