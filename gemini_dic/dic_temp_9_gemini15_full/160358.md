pop-off:/ˈpɒpɒf/| n. v.|突然爆发；发脾气；(俚语)炫耀；弹出；脱落 | a sudden outburst of anger or excitement; to suddenly and unexpectedly leave or depart; to remove something quickly; (informal) to show off. |例句：He had a pop-off with his boss. 他和老板大吵了一架。  The lid popped off the jar. 罐子的盖子突然弹开了。|近义词：outburst, blowup, eruption, show off|反义词：calm, composure, restraint


USAGE->
