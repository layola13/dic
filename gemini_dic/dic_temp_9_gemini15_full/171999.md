purgation:/pɜːrˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|净化；清除；泻药；涤罪|The act of purging or cleansing;  the removal of something unwanted or impure.  It can refer to a physical cleansing (like using a laxative), a spiritual cleansing (like confession), or a metaphorical cleansing (like purging an organization of corruption).|例句：The purgation of the city's sewers was a necessary but unpleasant task.  城市的污水净化是一项必要但令人不快的任务。

例句：Spiritual purgation is a key aspect of many religious practices. 精神净化是许多宗教实践的关键方面。|近义词：cleansing, purification, expurgation, evacuation|反义词：contamination, pollution, corruption|词性：名词
