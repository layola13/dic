millipedes:/ˈmɪlɪpiːdz/| n.|千足虫，马陆|A myriapod invertebrate with a long segmented body and numerous legs.  They are herbivores and generally harmless to humans.|例句：We found several millipedes under the damp log. 我们在潮湿的原木下发现了许多千足虫。

例句：The children were fascinated by the millipede slowly crawling across the path. 孩子们被缓慢爬过小路的千足虫迷住了。|近义词：centipede (though they are different creatures)|反义词:None.
