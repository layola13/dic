donate: /ˈdoʊneɪt/ | vt. |捐赠，捐献 |To give something, especially money or goods, to a person or an organization in order to help them.  |例句：He donated a large sum of money to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。

例句：She donated her old clothes to a homeless shelter. 她把旧衣服捐给了流浪者收容所。


近义词：contribute, give, offer, bestow | 反义词：receive, take, accept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He donates blood regularly. = 他定期献血。
They donate to various charities. = 他们向不同的慈善机构捐款。


一般过去时:
She donated her time to the project. = 她为这个项目贡献了自己的时间。
We donated books to the school library. = 我们向学校图书馆捐赠了书籍。


现在完成时:
I have donated to this cause for many years. = 我多年来一直为这项事业捐款。
They have donated a significant amount of money. = 他们已经捐赠了一大笔钱。


过去完成时:
She had donated all her savings before the disaster. = 灾难发生前，她已经捐出了所有的积蓄。
He had donated his artwork to the museum previously. = 他之前已经把他的艺术品捐给了博物馆。


将来时:
I will donate to the Red Cross after the hurricane. = 飓风过后我将向红十字会捐款。
They will donate their profits to the hospital. = 他们将把利润捐给医院。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate to that organization. = 如果我有更多钱，我会向那个组织捐款。
I wish I could donate more to help the victims. = 我希望我能捐更多钱来帮助受害者。


被动语态:
A large amount of money was donated to the relief effort. = 大笔资金被捐赠给了救灾工作。
The paintings were donated to the national gallery. = 这些画作被捐赠给了国家美术馆。


疑问句:
Will you donate to the cause? = 你会为这项事业捐款吗？
How much did they donate? = 他们捐了多少钱？


否定句:
He did not donate anything to the campaign. = 他没有向竞选活动捐款。
She could not donate because she was broke. = 她因为没钱而无法捐款。


PHRASE->
donate blood = 献血
donate organs = 捐献器官
donate time = 捐献时间
donate money = 捐款
make a donation = 捐款
