fron:/frɒn/| /frʌnt/ | n. |前线，前部，正面 |The front of the building is impressive. 建筑的正面令人印象深刻。|近义词：forefront, face, exterior |反义词：rear, back

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The front of the house faces south. = 房子的正面朝南。
The soldiers are at the front. = 士兵们在最前线。

一般过去时:
The front door was locked. = 前门锁上了。
They fought bravely at the front. = 他们在前线英勇作战。


现在完成时:
The army has advanced to the front. = 军队已经推进到前线。
We have repainted the front of the house. = 我们已经重新粉刷了房子的正面。


过去完成时:
The battle had already begun at the front. = 战斗已经在前线开始了。
They had reached the front before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临之前到达了前线。


将来时:
We will arrive at the front tomorrow. = 我们明天将到达前线。
The new building will have a glass front. = 新建筑将有一个玻璃正面。


虚拟语气:
If the front door were unlocked, I could enter. = 如果前门没锁，我就能进去。
I wish the front of the house were painted a different color. = 我希望房子的正面漆成不同的颜色。


被动语态:
The front of the car was damaged in the accident. = 汽车的前面在事故中受损。
The front line was heavily defended. = 前线防御严密。


疑问句:
Is there a garden at the front of the house? = 房子前面有花园吗？

否定句:
