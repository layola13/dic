mashup:/ˈmæʃʌp/ | n. |混搭；混合；(音乐)混音 |A mashup is a creative work that combines elements from two or more sources.  In music, it often involves blending samples from different songs.  In other contexts, it can refer to a combination of different ideas, styles, or technologies. | Example: The DJ created a fantastic mashup of pop and hip-hop.  这个DJ创作了一个流行乐和嘻哈乐的精彩混搭。|近义词：blend, mix, combination, medley |反义词：separation, division
