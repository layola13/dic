giveback:/ˈɡɪvˌbæk/| n. vt.|归还；回馈；回报|The act of returning something that was borrowed or taken, or of contributing to a cause or community.|例句：The company's giveback program supports local charities. 公司的回馈计划支持当地慈善机构。例句：She made a generous giveback to the community. 她对社区做出了慷慨的回馈。|近义词：return, payback, contribution|反义词：keep, withhold, take


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives back the book to the library. = 他把书还给了图书馆。
They give back a portion of their profits to charity. = 他们把部分利润捐给了慈善机构。


一般过去时:
She gave back the money she owed. = 她还了她欠的钱。
We gave back the borrowed tools. = 我们把借来的工具还了。


现在完成时:
He has given back all the stolen goods. = 他已经归还了所有被盗的物品。
They have given back to the community for many years. = 他们多年来一直回馈社区。


过去完成时:
She had given back the necklace before her husband noticed it was missing. = 在她丈夫注意到项链丢失之前，她已经把它还了。
They had given back the car before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前把车还了。


将来时:
I will give back the car tomorrow. = 我明天会把车还回去。
They will give back the money as soon as possible. = 他们会尽快把钱还回来。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would give it back. = 如果我有钱，我会把它还回去。
I wish I had given back the book earlier. = 我真希望我早点把书还回去。


被动语态:
The stolen paintings were given back to the museum. = 被盗的画作被归还给了博物馆。
The money will be given back to the rightful owner. = 钱将会归还给 rightful owner。


疑问句:
Will you give back the money? = 你会把钱还回来吗？
Have they given back the borrowed equipment? = 他们把借来的设备还了吗？


否定句:
He didn't give back the keys. = 他没有把钥匙还回来。
She hasn't given back the library books. = 她还没有把图书馆的书还回来。


PHRASE->
give back to the community = 回馈社会
give something back = 归还某物
give oneself back to = 重新投入到...
give back control = 交还控制权
give back the favour = 偿还恩惠
