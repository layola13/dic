preevasion:/ˌpriːɪˈveɪʒən/| n. | 事先逃避；预先规避 | The act of avoiding something before it happens; proactive avoidance.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered a neologism or a rare coinage.  It combines "pre-" (before) and "evasion" (the act of avoiding).|例句：The company's preevasion strategies included lobbying efforts to prevent the new regulations from being passed. 公司的预先规避策略包括游说工作，以防止新规通过。


近义词：preemptive avoidance, proactive evasion, preventative measure | 反义词：confrontation, engagement, acceptance | 词性:noun
