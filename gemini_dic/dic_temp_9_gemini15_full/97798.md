injures:/ˈɪndʒərz/| vt.|受伤，损害|To do physical harm or damage to someone or something.|例句：He injured his leg playing football. 他踢足球时伤了腿。|例句：The storm injured many buildings. 暴风雨损坏了许多建筑物。|近义词：harm, hurt, wound, damage|反义词：heal, cure, repair


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He injures his knee every time he plays basketball. = 他每次打篮球都伤到膝盖。
The constant noise injures my ears. = 持续的噪音损伤我的耳朵。

一般过去时:
She injured her ankle during the hike. = 她在徒步旅行中扭伤了脚踝。
The accident injured several people. = 那次事故造成多人受伤。

现在完成时:
I have injured my back lifting that heavy box. = 我搬那个重箱子时伤了背。
The company has injured its reputation through unethical practices. = 公司因不道德行为而损害了其声誉。

过去完成时:
He had injured his hand before the competition. = 他在比赛前就伤了手。
The team had injured its star player in the previous game. = 该队在上场比赛中伤到了明星球员。

将来时:
I fear I will injure my foot if I continue running. = 我担心如果继续跑步我会伤到脚。
The construction work may injure the environment. = 建设工程可能会破坏环境。

虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have injured my knee. = 如果我更小心一点，就不会伤到膝盖了。
I wish I hadn't injured my wrist, so I could play tennis. = 我真希望我没有伤到手腕，这样我就能打网球了。

被动语态:
His leg was injured in a car accident. = 他的腿在车祸中受伤了。
The environment was injured by the factory's pollution. = 环境被工厂的污染破坏了。

疑问句:
Were any people injured in the explosion? = 爆炸中有人受伤吗？
How was your car injured? = 你的车是怎么坏的？

否定句:
The fall didn't injure him badly. = 那次跌倒并没有严重伤到他。
The company's actions did not injure its reputation this time. = 公司的行为这次没有损害其声誉。
