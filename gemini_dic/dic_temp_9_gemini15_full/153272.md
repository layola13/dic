penalize:/ˈpiːnəlaɪz/|/ˈpenəlaɪz/| vt. |惩罚；处罚；不利于|To subject to a penalty; punish.  To put at a disadvantage.|

The team was penalized for a foul. = 球队因犯规而受罚。

He was penalized for speeding. = 他因超速而受罚。


近义词：punish, sanction, fine, reprimand

反义词：reward, benefit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The referee penalizes the player for a foul. = 裁判因犯规而惩罚球员。
The company penalizes employees for tardiness. = 公司因迟到而惩罚员工。


一般过去时:
The judge penalized the defendant for perjury. = 法官因伪证罪惩罚被告。
The teacher penalized the student for cheating. = 老师因作弊而惩罚学生。


现在完成时:
The athlete has been penalized several times this season. = 这位运动员本赛季已被处罚几次。
The company has penalized its employees for violating safety regulations. = 公司因违反安全条例而惩罚其员工。


过去完成时:
The team had already been penalized before the game even started. =  比赛开始前球队就已经受到处罚了。
The driver had been penalized repeatedly for driving infractions. = 司机因多次违反交通规则而被反复处罚。


将来时:
The athlete will be penalized if he commits another foul. = 如果这位运动员再次犯规，他将受到处罚。
The court will penalize the company for environmental violations. = 法院将因环境违规而惩罚该公司。


虚拟语气:
If he had not broken the law, he would not have been penalized. = 如果他没有违法，就不会受到惩罚。
I wish they hadn't penalized her so harshly. = 我真希望他们没有对她如此严厉地惩罚。


被动语态:
The player was penalized for unsportsmanlike conduct. = 球员因不公平竞争行为而受到处罚。
The team was penalized for exceeding the time limit. = 球队因超过时间限制而受到处罚。


疑问句:
Will the team be penalized for this mistake? = 这支球队会因为这个错误而受到惩罚吗？
How much will he be penalized for this infraction? = 他因为这次违规将受到多少惩罚？


否定句:
The judge did not penalize the witness for his conflicting testimony. = 法官没有因为证人的证词矛盾而惩罚他。
The school did not penalize the students for being late. = 学校没有因为学生迟到而惩罚他们。


PHRASE->
penalize heavily = 严厉处罚
penalize severely = 严厉惩罚
penalize unfairly = 不公平地惩罚
penalize excessively = 过度惩罚
penalize disproportionately = 不成比例地惩罚


词源:
From Old French *penaliser, from Latin poena "penalty, punishment".
