olivines:/ˈɒlɪviːnz/ | n. |橄榄石（矿物）|Olivine is a magnesium iron silicate mineral.  It's a common mineral in the Earth's mantle and is also found in some meteorites.  Different varieties of olivine exist, varying in their magnesium and iron content.|例句：The volcanic rock contained numerous olivine crystals.  这块火山岩含有许多橄榄石晶体。

例句：Scientists analyzed the olivine composition in the meteorite sample. 科学家分析了陨石样本中的橄榄石成分。
