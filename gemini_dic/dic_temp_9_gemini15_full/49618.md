demibrigade:/ˈdɛmiˌbriɡeɪd/| n. |半旅|A military unit of less than a full brigade, typically half the size.  It is often a temporary formation or one composed of troops from different regiments.  The term suggests a smaller, less formally structured unit than a full brigade.|The general ordered the formation of a demibrigade to reinforce the flank.  将军下令组建一支半旅以加强侧翼。

The 7th Demibrigade played a crucial role in the battle. 第7半旅在战斗中发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：half-brigade, detachment, contingent|反义词：brigade, division|名词
