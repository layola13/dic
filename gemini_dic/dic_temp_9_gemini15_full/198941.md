sifters:/ˈsɪftərz/| n. |筛子；筛选者|A sifter is a tool used to separate fine particles from coarser ones, often used in cooking or construction. It can also refer to someone who carefully examines or sorts through information or materials.|例句：She used a sifter to add flour to the cake batter. 她用筛子把面粉筛到蛋糕糊里。|例句：The journalist acted as a sifter, carefully examining all the evidence before writing her report.  这位记者扮演了筛选者的角色，在撰写报告之前仔细审查了所有证据。|近义词：sieve, strainer, screener|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to mix or combine.
