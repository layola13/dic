fluctuations:/ˌflʌkʃuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. |波动；起伏；涨落 | Variations or changes in a value or quantity, especially in an irregular or unpredictable manner.  Fluctuations can refer to price changes, temperature shifts, or any other variable that changes over time. |例句：The stock market experienced significant fluctuations throughout the day. 股市全天经历了剧烈的波动。

例句：There were large fluctuations in temperature during the storm.暴风雨期间气温波动很大。


近义词：variations, changes, swings, oscillations, undulations | 反义词：stability, constancy, steadiness | 词性:noun

The price fluctuates daily. = 价格每天都在波动。
