memorializing: /məˈmɔːriəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 纪念；使不朽；追悼 |  The act of creating a lasting record or tribute to someone or something.  It often involves creating a monument, writing a biography, or holding a ceremony. |

例句：
The city is memorializing its fallen heroes with a new monument. =  这座城市正在用一座新的纪念碑来纪念阵亡的英雄。

The artist memorialized the tragic event in a powerful painting. = 艺术家在一幅强有力的画作中纪念了这场悲惨的事件。


近义词：commemorating, celebrating, remembering, honoring


反义词：forgetting, ignoring, neglecting


词性：动词
