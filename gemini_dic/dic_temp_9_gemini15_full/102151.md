issue:/ˈɪʃuː/ | n., vt. | 问题；发行；发布；提出；造成…；颁布 |  A matter or concern that needs to be addressed; to distribute or release something officially; to formally raise a point or question; to cause something to happen; to officially make something available or known. | 近义词：problem, topic, question, matter, publish, release, raise, create, produce | 反义词：solution, answer, resolution


例句(n.):

The main issue is the lack of funding.  = 主要问题是缺乏资金。

We need to address this issue immediately. = 我们需要立即解决这个问题。


例句(vt.):

The bank issued a statement denying the rumors. = 银行发表声明否认了谣言。

The government issued a new set of regulations. = 政府颁布了一套新的规章制度。


词源:

From Old French *issu, past participle of issir "to go out, issue forth," from Latin exire "to go out," from ex- "out" + ire "to go."  The meaning developed from "something that comes out" to "a point or matter arising".


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company issues a new product every quarter. = 公司每个季度都会发行一款新产品。
This factory issues a lot of pollution. = 这个工厂排放很多污染。


一般过去时:
They issued a warning about the storm. = 他们发布了关于暴风雨的警告。
The judge issued a ruling on the case. = 法官对该案作出了裁决。


现在完成时:
The government has issued several statements on the crisis. = 政府已经就这场危机发表了几份声明。
They have issued a new travel advisory. = 他们发布了新的旅行建议。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had issued a press release. = 会议之前，他们已经发布了一份新闻稿。
The court had already issued the warrant. = 法院已经签发了逮捕令。


将来时:
The company will issue a report next month. = 公司下个月将发布一份报告。
The bank will issue the new credit cards soon. = 银行很快就会发行新的信用卡。


虚拟语气:
If the company issued a recall, they would lose a lot of money. = 如果公司发出召回令，他们将损失很多钱。
I wish they had issued a warning sooner. = 我希望他们早点发出警告。


被动语态:
A new policy was issued by the government. = 政府发布了一项新政策。
The tickets were issued to the participants. = 门票发给了参与者。


疑问句:
What issues are you facing? = 你面临什么问题？
When will the new stamps be issued? = 新邮票什么时候发行？


否定句:
The company did not issue a statement. = 公司没有发表声明。
They did not issue any warnings. = 他们没有发出任何警告。


PHRASE->

at issue:  正在讨论中的；有争议的
the issue at hand: 目前的问题
take issue with: 对…表示异议
a matter of issue: 争论点
a burning issue: 迫切的问题
