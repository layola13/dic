moistener:/ˈmɔɪsnər/| n. |加湿器；湿润剂|A device or substance that adds moisture.  A moisturizer is a type of moistener used for skin.|Example:  The air conditioner has a built-in moistener to prevent dry air.  这个空调有一个内置的加湿器来防止空气干燥。|近义词：humidifier, humectant|反义词：dehydrator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The humidifier moistens the air. = 加湿器使空气湿润。
She uses a lip moistener regularly. = 她经常使用润唇膏。

一般过去时:
He moistened the soil before planting. = 他在种植前润湿了土壤。
We used a facial moistener last night. = 我们昨晚使用了面部保湿剂。

现在完成时:
I have moistened the sponge. = 我已经弄湿了海绵。
They have applied a hair moistener to their dry hair. = 他们已经往干燥的头发上涂抹了护发素。

过去完成时:
She had moistened her lips before speaking. = 她说话前润湿了嘴唇。
The gardener had moistened the seeds before sowing. = 园丁在播种前已经润湿了种子。

将来时:

He will use a plant moistener to water his plants. = 他将使用植物喷壶来浇灌他的植物。

虚拟语气:
