elvish:/ˈɛlvɪʃ/| adj.|精灵的；像精灵的；优美的；精致的|Relating to or characteristic of elves; suggesting the qualities of elves, such as grace, beauty, and magic.  It can also describe something delicate or refined.|例句：The elvish carvings on the ancient tomb were breathtaking. 古墓上精灵般的雕刻令人叹为观止。

例句：Her elvish features and graceful movements captivated everyone. 她精灵般的容貌和优雅的动作吸引了所有人。


近义词：fairy-like, ethereal, delicate, refined, magical

反义词：coarse, rough, crude, clumsy
