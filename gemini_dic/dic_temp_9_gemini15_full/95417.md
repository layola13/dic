incidentally:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentəli/ | adv. |顺便说一下；附带地；偶然地 |By the way; as a matter of minor or incidental importance.  It introduces a piece of information that is not the main point of the conversation but is related. |例句：Incidentally, I saw your brother yesterday. 顺便说一下，我昨天看见你哥哥了。  Incidentally, the meeting has been postponed. 顺便提一句，会议延期了。|近义词：by the way, incidentally, in passing, as an aside|反义词：importantly, significantly
