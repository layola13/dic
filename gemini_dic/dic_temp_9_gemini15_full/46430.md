daggier:/ˈdæɡər/| n.|匕首，短剑|A dagger is a short sword with a pointed blade, typically used as a weapon.  Daggers are often associated with stealth and assassination.|例句：The assassin used a dagger to kill his victim. 刺客用匕首杀死了他的受害者。|近义词：knife, poniard|反义词: broadsword, longsword


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, verb tenses and voice (active/passive) are not applicable.  The examples below show how "dagger" is used in different sentence structures.
