formalized:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzd/| v. | 使正式化；使制度化 | To give a definite and structured form to something that was previously informal or unstructured.  It implies establishing rules, procedures, or systems. |例句：The company formalized its employee handbook. 公司正式制定了员工手册。 |例句：They formalized their agreement in a written contract. 他们以书面合同的形式正式确定了他们的协议。|近义词：systematize, codify, standardize, institutionalize |反义词：informalize, loosen, casualize
