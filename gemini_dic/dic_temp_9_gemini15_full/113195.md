luring: /ˈlʊərɪŋ/ | adj. | 诱人的，吸引人的 |  Attractive or tempting; enticing.  It suggests something that is captivating and potentially dangerous or misleading.  | 例句：The luring scent of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.  诱人的新鲜烘焙面包香味充满了厨房。

The luring promise of easy money led many to invest in the fraudulent scheme.  轻松赚钱的诱人承诺导致许多人投资了欺诈计划。


近义词：attractive, tempting, enticing, captivating, alluring, seductive

反义词：repulsive, repellent, unattractive, unappealing


词源:  From the verb "lure," ultimately from Old Norse *lūra, meaning "to lie in wait," "to ambush," or "to lure."  The sense of attraction evolved from the idea of something enticing prey into a trap.
