multiculturalists:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪsts/| n. |多元文化主义者|Advocates for the inclusion and celebration of diverse cultures within a society.  They often support policies promoting cultural understanding, tolerance, and equality. |例句：Many multiculturalists believe that embracing diversity enriches society.  许多多元文化主义者认为，拥抱多元文化丰富了社会。|近义词：pluralists, inclusionists |反义词：nativists, isolationists
