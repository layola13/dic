collectible:/kəˈlɛktəbəl/ | adj. | 可收藏的，值得收藏的 |  Something collectible is worth collecting because it is rare, valuable, or interesting.  It often increases in value over time. | Example:  That vintage comic book is a highly collectible item. 那本复古漫画书是一件非常值得收藏的物品。  His stamp collection contains many rare and collectible stamps.他的邮票收藏中包含许多稀有和值得收藏的邮票。 | 近义词：collectable, valuable, rare | 反义词：common, worthless


USAGE->

一般现在时:
That antique car is collectible. = 那辆古董车是值得收藏的。
This rare coin is highly collectible. = 这枚稀有硬币非常值得收藏。


一般过去时:
That painting was collectible even fifty years ago. = 那幅画即使在50年前也值得收藏。
The baseball card was highly collectible in its day. = 这张棒球卡在当时非常值得收藏。


现在完成时:
Many people have collected stamps over the years. = 多年来，许多人都收集邮票。
She has amassed a significant collection of antique dolls. = 她已经积累了大量的古董娃娃收藏。


过去完成时:
Before the auction, he had already identified several collectible items. = 拍卖会之前，他已经确定了几件值得收藏的物品。
They had collected enough rare books to fill a library. = 他们已经收集了足够的珍本书，可以装满一个图书馆。


将来时:
This vintage toy will become more collectible in the future. = 这件复古玩具将来会更有收藏价值。
He will continue to collect antique furniture. = 他将继续收藏古董家具。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would buy that collectible vase. = 如果我有钱，我会买那个值得收藏的花瓶。
I wish I had started collecting stamps when I was younger. = 我真希望我年轻时就开始收集邮票了。


被动语态:
Many valuable items are collected by enthusiasts. = 许多有价值的物品都被爱好者收藏。
That rare stamp was recently collected by a museum. = 那枚稀有邮票最近被一家博物馆收藏。


疑问句:
Is this old record collectible? = 这张旧唱片值得收藏吗？
Are these antique cars still collectible today? = 这些古董车今天还有收藏价值吗？


否定句:
That mass-produced item isn't collectible. = 那件批量生产的物品不值得收藏。
These modern coins are not considered collectible. = 这些现代硬币不被认为是值得收藏的。
