pooching: /ˈpuːtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 偷窃；擅自挪用；非法猎捕 |  To steal or take something that doesn't belong to you; to appropriate something without authorization. It can also refer to the illegal hunting or poaching of animals. | 例句：He was accused of pooching funds from the company. 他被指控盗用公司资金。


例句：The poachers were caught red-handed, pooching protected birds.  偷猎者当场被抓住，他们正在非法捕猎受保护的鸟类。


近义词：stealing, pilfering, embezzling, appropriating, poaching (in the context of hunting)


反义词：donating, giving, returning


词性: 动词
