comparably:/ˈkɒmpərəbli/ /ˈkɑːmpərəbli/ | adv. | 相似地；相当地 | In a comparable way; to a similar extent.  The two products are comparably priced. | The results are comparably good.  | similarly, relatively | incomparably, dissimilarly | 副词


例句：

The two houses are comparably sized. = 这两所房子大小相当。

His performance was comparably better than last year's. = 他的表现比去年好得多。


近义词：

similarly, relatively, equally


反义词：

incomparably, dissimilarly, unequally


词源：

comparably源于形容词comparable，它是由compare（比较）加上后缀-able（可…的）构成。  compare的词根追溯到拉丁语的comparare，由com-（一起）和parare（平等）构成，字面意思是“使平等”。因此，comparably表示“以可比较的方式”，即“相似地，相当地”。
