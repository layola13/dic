preexplanations:/ˌpriːˌɛkspləˈneɪʃənz/| n. |预先解释，初步解释 |Preliminary explanations given before a detailed explanation.  They provide a basic understanding of a topic before delving into specifics.  |Example: The professor gave preexplanations of the complex theory before starting the lecture. 教授在开始讲座前对复杂的理论进行了预先解释。|近义词：introduction, preliminary remarks, overview |反义词：postscript, afterthought
