parcels:/ˈpɑːrsəlz/| n.|包裹；小包；一块地；份额|A parcel is a package that is wrapped and tied up, usually to be sent by post or carried by hand. It can also refer to a piece of land or a share of something.|例句：I received a parcel from my grandmother yesterday. 昨天我收到了祖母寄来的包裹。

例句：The land was divided into several parcels. 这块地被分成几块地。|近义词：package, packet, bundle|反义词：N/A
