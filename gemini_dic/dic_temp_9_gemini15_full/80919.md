gowns:/ɡaʊnz/| n.|长袍；礼服|A gown is a long, loose-fitting garment, typically worn by women, often for formal occasions or as a nightdress.  It can also refer to a robe worn by judges or academics.|例句：She wore a beautiful silk gown to the wedding. 她穿着一条美丽的丝绸礼服参加婚礼。|例句：The judge put on his black gown before entering the courtroom. 法官进入法庭前穿上了他的黑色长袍。|近义词：robe, dress, frock|反义词：None specifically, depends on context (e.g., casual clothing)|名词
