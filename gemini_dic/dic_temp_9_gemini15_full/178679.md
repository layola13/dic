recriminated:/rɪˈkrɪmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 反驳；反控；互相指责 | To accuse someone of wrongdoing in return for their accusation; to retaliate with an accusation. |例句：He recriminated, saying that she had started the fight. 他反驳道，说她是先动手打架的。


例句：They recriminated each other for the failure of the project. 他们互相指责对方导致项目失败。


近义词：counter-accuse, retort, retaliate | 反义词：excuse, forgive, pardon | 词性：动词


USAGE->

She recriminates whenever she is criticized.  她每当受到批评就会反驳。


一般过去时:
He recriminated against his brother for breaking his toy. 他指责他弟弟打破了他的玩具。
