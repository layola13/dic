imperilling:/ɪmˈperəlɪŋ/ | v. | 危及；使处于危险之中 |  To put something or someone in danger; to jeopardize. | The ongoing drought is imperilling the harvest.  持续的干旱正在危及收成。| The reckless driving imperilled the lives of the passengers.  鲁莽的驾驶危及了乘客的生命。| endangering, jeopardizing, threatening | safeguarding, protecting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm is imperilling the coastal towns. = 暴风雨正在危及沿海城镇。
He is imperilling his health by smoking. = 他吸烟正在危及他的健康。


一般过去时:
The fire imperilled the entire building. = 大火危及了整栋建筑物。
Her actions imperilled her career. = 她的行为危及了她的事业。


现在完成时:
The government's policies have imperilled the economy. = 政府的政策已经危及了经济。
His reckless behavior has imperilled his relationship. = 他鲁莽的行为已经危及了他的关系。


过去完成时:
They had imperilled the mission by their carelessness. = 他们因粗心大意而危及了任务。
The company had imperilled its reputation through unethical practices. = 这家公司因不道德行为而危及了其声誉。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will imperil many coastal communities. = 海平面上升将危及许多沿海社区。
His continued gambling will imperil his financial security. = 他持续的赌博将危及他的经济安全。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to drink, he would imperil his liver. = 如果他继续喝酒，他将危及他的肝脏。
I wish he hadn't imperilled the deal with his rash comments. = 我真希望他不要因为鲁莽的言论而危及这笔交易。


被动语态:
The project was imperilled by a lack of funding. = 该项目因缺乏资金而受到威胁。
The environment is being imperilled by pollution. = 环境正受到污染的威胁。


疑问句:
Is the current situation imperilling the peace talks? = 目前的情况是否危及了和平谈判？
Does his behavior imperil his future? = 他的行为是否危及他的未来？


否定句:
The new safety measures will not imperil the workers. = 新的安全措施不会危及工人的安全。
The government claims that their policies do not imperil the environment. = 政府声称他们的政策不会危及环境。


PHRASE->
seriously imperil = 严重危及
greatly imperil = 极大地危及
potentially imperil = 潜在地危及
directly imperil = 直接危及
imperil the future = 危及未来
