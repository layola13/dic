postepileptic: /ˌpoʊstɪˈpɪlɛptɪk/ | adj. | 痫后期的；癫痫发作后的 | Post-epileptic refers to the period of time immediately following an epileptic seizure.  It describes the symptoms, behaviors, or conditions that occur after the seizure has ended. |  The patient experienced post-epileptic confusion for several minutes after the seizure.  患者在癫痫发作后几分钟内出现痫后意识模糊。|近义词：post-ictal |反义词：pre-epileptic|形容词
