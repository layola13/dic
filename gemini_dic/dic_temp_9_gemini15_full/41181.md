It emphasizes repetition and imitation rather than originality or creativity. | Example: The company's advertising campaign was criticized for its blatant copyism, relying heavily on well-worn slogans and imagery.  这家公司的广告宣传活动因其公然的模仿而受到批评，严重依赖老套的口号和意象。|近义词：plagiarism, imitation, mimicry |反义词：originality, innovation, creativity | 名词
