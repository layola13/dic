serviette:/sɜːˈvjɛt/| n. |餐巾；餐巾纸|A serviette is a piece of cloth or paper used at a meal to wipe one's mouth or fingers.  It is a more formal term than "napkin".|The restaurant provided each diner with a crisp white serviette.|近义词：napkin, napkin|反义词:  无
