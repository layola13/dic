reinvention:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛnʃən/| n. | 再创造；改造；革新 | The act of changing something so much that it is essentially new.  It involves a significant transformation or reimagining of something existing.  This could apply to ideas, products, businesses, or even oneself. |  例句：The company's reinvention involved a complete overhaul of its marketing strategy. 公司的改造包括对其营销策略的彻底改革。


例句：His reinvention as a successful entrepreneur was remarkable. 他作为成功企业家的改造令人瞩目。


近义词：transformation, revolution, renewal, rebirth, revamp


反义词：destruction, demolition, stagnation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reinvents its products every year. = 公司每年都会对其产品进行改造。
She reinvents herself with a new career. = 她通过新的职业来重新塑造自己。


一般过去时:
He reinvented the wheel, literally.  = 他从字面上重新发明了轮子。
They reinvented their marketing campaign after poor sales. = 销售不佳后，他们对营销活动进行了重新设计。


现在完成时:
The artist has reinvented himself many times throughout his career. =这位艺术家在他职业生涯中多次重塑自我。
The company has reinvented its image to appeal to a younger audience. =该公司已经重塑了自己的形象，以吸引更年轻的受众。


过去完成时:
They had reinvented their business model before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，他们已经改变了他们的商业模式。
She had reinvented her life after a difficult divorce. = 经历了一次艰难的离婚后，她已经重塑了自己的生活。


将来时:
He will reinvent his brand next year. = 他明年将重新打造自己的品牌。
The company will reinvent its approach to customer service. = 公司将改变其客户服务方式。


虚拟语气:
If they reinvented their strategy, they might succeed. = 如果他们改变策略，他们可能会成功。
I wish I could reinvent myself and start over. = 我希望我能重塑自我并重新开始。


被动语态:
The product was reinvented to improve its functionality. = 该产品经过重新设计以提高其功能。
The company's image is being reinvented by a new marketing team. = 公司的形象正在由一个新的营销团队进行重塑。


疑问句:
Will they reinvent their business model? = 他们会改变他们的商业模式吗？
How can I reinvent myself? = 我该如何重塑自我？


否定句:
They did not reinvent the wheel; they simply improved it. = 他们并没有重新发明轮子；他们只是改进了一下。
The company hasn't reinvented itself enough to stay competitive. =该公司自我改造得还不够多，无法保持竞争力。
