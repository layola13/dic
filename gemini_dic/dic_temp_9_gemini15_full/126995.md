nasality:/næˈsæləti/| n. |鼻音，鼻音化|The quality of a sound produced with the nasal passages open, as in the pronunciation of /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/.  It can also refer to speech that is excessively nasal.  |例句：His speech had an unpleasant nasality. 他的说话带有难听的鼻音。

例句：The singer's nasality added a unique character to the song.  这位歌手的鼻音为歌曲增添了独特的特色。


近义词：nasal resonance, nasal quality |反义词：oral quality


词源:  From Middle French *nasalité, from Latin nasus "nose".  The suffix "-ity" is a common English suffix denoting a quality or state.
