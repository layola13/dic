ergometer:/ˈɜːrɡˌmiːtər/| n. |功率计；测功器|A device used to measure work or power, especially in the context of exercise or physical activity.  It often involves resistance to movement, allowing the measurement of energy expenditure. |Example: The cyclist used an ergometer to monitor their power output during training.  骑车人用功率计监测训练期间的功率输出。|近义词：power meter, work meter|反义词：None specifically, but concepts like "inaccurate measurement device" could be considered antonymic in context.|名词
