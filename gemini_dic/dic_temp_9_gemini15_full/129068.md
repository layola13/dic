nitrogen:/ˈnaɪtrədʒən/ | n. |氮 |Nitrogen is a chemical element, a colorless, odorless, tasteless gas that forms about 78 percent of the Earth's atmosphere. It is essential for life, being a component of amino acids and proteins.  | Example:  Plants absorb nitrogen from the soil to grow. 植物从土壤中吸收氮来生长。

The air we breathe is mostly nitrogen. 我们呼吸的空气大部分是氮气。 |近义词：none (no perfect synonym)|反义词：oxygen (in terms of atmospheric composition and biological function) |名词
