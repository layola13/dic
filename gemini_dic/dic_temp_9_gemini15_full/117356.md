medianly:/ˈmiːdiənli/ | adv. |中等地，适中地 |In a median or average manner; moderately.  The word suggests something neither extreme nor exceptional, but rather falls within the middle range.  |例句：He performed medianly on the test, scoring neither exceptionally high nor low. 他考试成绩中等，既不高也不低。

例句：The restaurant's prices are medianly priced, offering a good value for the money. 这家餐馆的价格适中，物有所值。


近义词：moderately, averagely, passably, reasonably


反义词：extremely, exceptionally, extraordinarily


词性: 副词
