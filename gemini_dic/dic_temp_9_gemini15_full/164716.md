prefabricated:/ˌpriːˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.| 预制的，预装的 |  Made or assembled from parts that have been made beforehand.  These components are often manufactured off-site and then transported to the construction site for final assembly. This method is commonly used in construction, manufacturing, and other industries to save time and resources. | The prefabricated house was assembled quickly and efficiently.  预制房屋快速高效地组装完成。  | Modular, ready-made, mass-produced | Custom-built, handcrafted

The factory prefabricates many houses every month. = 工厂每月预制许多房屋。
