shoulderless:/ˈʃoʊldərlɛs/ | adj. | 无肩的，露肩的 | Lacking shoulders; without shoulders.  The term often refers to clothing items that expose the shoulders. | Example: The dress was shoulderless, revealing her elegant neck and shoulders.  这条裙子是露肩的，露出了她优雅的颈部和肩膀。  |近义词：strapless, off-the-shoulder | 反义词：shouldered
