fanzine:/ˈfænzɪn/| n. |同人志，粉丝杂志|A fanzine is a non-professional magazine or publication produced by and for fans of a particular subject, typically science fiction, comics, music, or a particular celebrity.  It often features fan fiction, artwork, reviews, and news related to the subject.  Fanzines are usually created and distributed independently, often through informal networks. |Example:  She started a fanzine dedicated to her favorite band, sharing her artwork and interviews.|近义词：fan publication, amateur magazine|反义词：professional publication, commercial magazine
