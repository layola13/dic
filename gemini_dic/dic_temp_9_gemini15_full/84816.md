handyman:/ˈhændiˌmæn/| n. |修理工，技工|A handyman is a person skilled at doing a wide variety of practical tasks, typically around the home.  They are often capable of performing minor repairs, maintenance, and improvements. |例句：We hired a handyman to fix the leaky faucet. 我们雇了一个修理工来修理漏水的龙头。

例句：The handyman installed new shelves in the garage. 修理工在车库里安装了新的架子。


近义词：repairman, maintenance worker, handy person |反义词：None directly applicable.

词源:
The word "handyman" is a compound word.  "Handy" means skillful or convenient, deriving from Old English "hand" (hand) and a suffix implying skill or aptitude. "Man" simply refers to a person.  Therefore, a handyman is literally a "skillful person (with their hands)."
