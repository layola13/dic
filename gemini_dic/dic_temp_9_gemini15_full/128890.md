nimble:/ˈnɪmbəl/| adj.|灵活的，敏捷的，轻快的|Quick and light in movement or action; agile.  The word describes someone or something that moves with ease and speed.  It can also refer to the mind's quickness and dexterity.|例句：The dancer's nimble fingers flew across the keyboard.  这位舞者的灵活手指在键盘上飞舞。

The cat showed nimble reflexes, dodging the thrown ball.  这只猫展现了敏捷的反应，躲过了扔来的球。|近义词：agile, quick, deft, spry|反义词：clumsy, awkward, stiff, slow
