lobotomizing:/ˌloʊbəˈtɑːmɪzɪŋ/| v.| 脑叶切除术；摧毁…的思维能力|The act of performing a lobotomy, a surgical procedure that severs connections in the brain's prefrontal cortex.  This term often carries negative connotations, suggesting a brutal or destructive intervention that impairs cognitive function and personality. |例句：The doctor considered lobotomizing the patient to alleviate his severe mental illness.  医生考虑对病人进行脑叶切除术以减轻他的严重精神疾病。

The practice of lobotomizing patients was widely condemned as unethical and inhumane after its damaging effects became widely known.|对病人的脑叶切除术在它的破坏性影响被广泛了解后受到了广泛谴责为不道德和不人道的。|近义词：brain surgery (in a negative context), damaging, destroying (mentally)|反义词：healing, restoring, improving (mentally)
