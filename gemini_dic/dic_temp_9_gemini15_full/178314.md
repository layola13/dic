一般现在时:
The court recondemns the defendant. = 法院再次判决被告有罪。
Recondemnation is rarely used in modern legal systems. = 现代法律体系很少使用再次谴责。


一般过去时:
The judge recondemned the prisoner to life imprisonment. = 法官再次判处犯人无期徒刑。
The public recondemned the politician's actions. = 公众再次谴责了这位政治家的行为。


现在完成时:
The court has recondemned the criminal to a harsher sentence. = 法院已再次判处罪犯更严厉的刑罚。
The organization has recondemned violence against civilians. = 该组织已再次谴责针对平民的暴力行为。


过去完成时:
The jury had recondemned the accused before the new evidence was presented. = 在新的证据提交之前，陪审团已经再次谴责了被告。
The people had recondemned the policy long before its repeal. = 在政策废除很久以前，人们就已经再次谴责了这项政策。


将来时:
The court will recondemn the offender if new evidence emerges. = 如果出现新的证据，法院将再次谴责罪犯。
The international community will recondemn human rights abuses. = 国际社会将再次谴责侵犯人权的行为。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the court would recondemn the defendant. = 如果证据更充分，法院将再次判决被告有罪。
If they had known the consequences, they wouldn't have recondemned the treaty. = 如果他们知道后果，他们就不会再次谴责该条约了。


被动语态:
The criminal was recondemned to a longer prison term. = 罪犯被再次判处更长的监禁期限。
The proposal was recondemned by the committee. = 该提案被委员会再次否决。


疑问句:
Will the court recondemn him for his past crimes? = 法院会因他过去的罪行再次谴责他吗？
Has the government recondemned its previous policy? = 政府是否再次谴责其先前的政策？


否定句:
The court did not recondemn the defendant. = 法院没有再次谴责被告。
The public did not recondemn the actions of the police. = 公众没有谴责警方的行为。
