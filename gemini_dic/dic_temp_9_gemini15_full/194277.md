self-oiling: /ˈsɛlf ˈɔɪlɪŋ/ | adj. | 自动润滑的 | Self-oiling describes something that lubricates itself, requiring no external lubrication.  This usually refers to machinery or mechanisms where the design incorporates a system for automatic lubrication. | 例句：The self-oiling bearings in the machine require minimal maintenance. 这台机器上的自动润滑轴承需要最少的维护。

The self-oiling pump runs smoothly and quietly. 这台自动润滑泵运行平稳安静。


近义词：self-lubricating

反义词：requiring external lubrication, needing lubrication


词性：形容词

词源:

The word is a compound of "self-" (meaning "by itself") and "oiling" (the act of lubricating with oil).  It's a straightforward descriptive term that emerged with the development of self-lubricating machinery.
