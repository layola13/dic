luxuriously:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriəsli/| adv.|奢侈地，豪华地 |Describes something done or existing in a luxurious manner, characterized by extravagance and comfort.  |例句：They lived luxuriously in a mansion overlooking the ocean.他们住在俯瞰大海的豪宅里，生活奢侈。|例句：The hotel was luxuriously appointed, with every amenity imaginable. 这家酒店设备豪华，应有尽有。|近义词：opulently, lavishly, sumptuously, extravagantly|反义词：frugal, modestly, sparsely, simply|副词
