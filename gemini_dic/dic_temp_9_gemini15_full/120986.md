miscitation:/ˌmɪsˌsaɪˈteɪʃən/| n. | 误引用；错误引用 | The act of quoting something incorrectly or out of context.  This can include misrepresenting the meaning of the original source.  Miscitation can be accidental or intentional. | Example: The article contained a clear miscitation of the original research paper. 这篇文章明显错误引用了原始研究论文。 |近义词： misquotation, misattribution, misrepresentation | 反义词：accurate citation, correct quotation
