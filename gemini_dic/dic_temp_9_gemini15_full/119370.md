microcosmic: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈkɒzmɪk/ | adj. | 微观的；缩影的 | Reflecting or representing something on a smaller scale.  It refers to something that is a miniature representation of a larger system or the world.  | 例句：The family is often seen as a microcosm of society. 家庭常被视为社会的缩影。

The artist's work is a microcosm of his life experiences.  这位艺术家的作品是他生活经历的缩影。


近义词：miniature, epitome, paradigm, representative


反义词：macrocosmic, vast, immense


词性：形容词
