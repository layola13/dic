daffiest:/ˈdæfiɪst/| adj.|最傻的，最愚蠢的，最古怪的 |Most foolish or silly;  eccentric.  It is the superlative form of "daffy".|例句：He wore the daffiest hat I've ever seen. 他戴着我见过的最傻的帽子。

例句：Her daffiest idea was to paint the house purple and orange. 她最古怪的主意是把房子漆成紫色和橙色。


近义词： silliest, goofiest, craziest, most absurd, most ridiculous

反义词：most sensible, most serious, most reasonable

词性：形容词
