overpatriotism:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪtriəˌtɪzəm/| n. |极端的爱国主义；盲目爱国|Excessive or fanatical patriotism;  a love of one's country to an excessive degree, often leading to harmful actions or intolerance of differing views.  It often manifests as unquestioning support for one's government and a rejection of criticism or dissent. |例句：His overpatriotism led him to blindly support the war, even when it became clear it was unjust. 他的极端爱国主义使他盲目地支持这场战争，即使战争显然是不公正的。
例句：The government's policies were met with resistance from those who saw them as a sign of overpatriotism and suppression of free speech.  政府的政策遭到了那些认为这是极端爱国主义和压制言论自由迹象的人的抵制。|近义词：chauvinism, jingoism, nationalism (in extreme forms)|反义词：cosmopolitanism, internationalism
