pikelet:/ˈpaɪklɪt/| n. |小薄饼，小煎饼 |A small, slightly raised pancake, typically made with batter and served with butter or jam.  It's similar to a crumpet but smaller and often thinner. |例句：I enjoyed a delicious pikelet with strawberry jam for breakfast.  我早餐吃了一个美味的草莓酱小薄饼。

例句：She made a batch of pikelets for the afternoon tea. 她做了一批小薄饼作为下午茶。


近义词：crumpet, pancake, small pancake |反义词：None  |词性:名词
