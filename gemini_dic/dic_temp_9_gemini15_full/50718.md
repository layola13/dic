dernier:/dɛrˈnjeɪ/| adj.|最后的，最近的，终极的|From the Old French "dernier," meaning "last," ultimately from the Latin "de" (from, of) + "novissimus" (superlative of "novus," new).  It implies a finality or a position at the end of a sequence or period.|例句：This is the dernier cri in fashion. 这是最新的时尚潮流。The dernier chapitre of the book was disappointing. 这本书的最后一章令人失望。|近义词：last, final, ultimate, latest|反义词：first, initial, earliest|形容词
