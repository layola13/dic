non-efficient: /ˌnɒnɪˈfɪʃənt/ | adj. | 非高效的，低效的 | Not efficient; not achieving maximum productivity with minimum wasted effort or expense.  | The non-efficient system caused significant delays.  这个低效的系统造成了严重的延误。The non-efficient use of resources led to budget overruns. 资源的低效利用导致预算超支。| efficient, effective | inefficient, ineffective


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like a verb.  There are no tenses to illustrate.  However, we can show its use in different sentence structures.


肯定句:
The non-efficient process wasted valuable time.  低效的流程浪费了宝贵的时间。
His non-efficient methods resulted in poor quality work. 他低效的方法导致了劣质的工作。


否定句:
The process wasn't non-efficient; it was actually quite streamlined. 这个过程并非低效；它实际上相当简化。
The system wasn't non-efficient; it was highly optimized for productivity.  该系统并非低效；它针对生产力进行了高度优化。


疑问句:
Is the current system non-efficient? 当前的系统低效吗？
Are these non-efficient practices contributing to our losses?  这些低效的做法是否导致了我们的损失？


比较级/最高级:
The new system is far less non-efficient than the old one. 新系统远比旧系统效率更高。  (Note:  This uses "less" to express the opposite of "non-efficient" for comparison)
This is the most non-efficient method we've ever tried. 这是我们尝试过的最低效的方法。 (Note: While grammatically correct, it's less common to use superlatives with "non-" prefixes)


PHRASE->
There aren't common idiomatic phrases directly using "non-efficient."  However, phrases conveying similar meanings include:


a waste of time: 浪费时间
inefficiently managed: 管理不善
poorly organized: 组织混乱
lacking productivity: 缺乏生产力
underperforming: 表现不佳
