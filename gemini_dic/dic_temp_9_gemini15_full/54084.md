disembarking:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrkɪŋ/ | v.ing | 下船；下飞机；卸货 | The act of leaving a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  | Example: The passengers were busy disembarking from the cruise ship.  乘客正忙着下邮轮。  The process of unloading cargo from a ship or aircraft. | Example:  Disembarking the cargo took several hours.  卸货花了几个小时。 |近义词：leaving, departing, unloading | 反义词：embarking, boarding, loading | 动名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Passengers are disembarking from the train. = 乘客正在下火车。
The crew is disembarking the supplies. = 船员正在卸下补给品。


一般过去时:
We disembarked the plane in London. = 我们在伦敦下了飞机。
The soldiers disembarked the ship at dawn. = 士兵们在黎明时分下了船。


现在完成时:
The passengers have disembarked and gone to their hotels. = 乘客们已经下船并去了他们的酒店。
They have disembarked all the goods from the truck. = 他们已经把卡车上的所有货物卸下来了。


过去完成时:
We had disembarked before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，我们已经下船了。
The team had disembarked the equipment before the inspection. = 团队在检查之前已经卸下了设备。


将来时:
We will disembark at the next stop. = 我们将在下一站下车。
The cargo will disembark at the port tomorrow. = 货物将于明天在港口卸货。


虚拟语气:
If we disembarked earlier, we wouldn't have missed the bus. = 如果我们早点下船，就不会错过公交车了。
I wish we had disembarked at a different port. = 我真希望我们在不同的港口下船。


被动语态:
The passengers were disembarked quickly and efficiently. = 乘客们快速而有效地下了船。
The goods are being disembarked at the warehouse. = 货物正在仓库卸货。


疑问句:
When are we disembarking? = 我们什么时候下船？
Have all the passengers disembarked yet? = 所有乘客都下船了吗？


否定句:
The passengers were not yet disembarking. = 乘客们还没有下船。
We didn't disembark until the captain gave the order. = 直到船长下令，我们才下船。


PHRASE->
disembark from a ship = 从船上下来
disembark at the airport = 在机场下飞机
disembark the cargo = 卸货
disembark the troops = 卸载军队
disembark safely = 安全下船/飞机
