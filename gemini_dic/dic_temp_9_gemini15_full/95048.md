inactivities:/ˌɪnˈæktɪvɪtiz/| n. |不活动；懒散；消极怠工|A state of being inactive; lack of activity or involvement; idleness.  It often refers to a lack of engagement in work, social activities, or physical exercise.|例句：His inactivities led to a decline in his health. 他的不活动导致了他的健康状况下降。

例句：The company's inactivities in the market resulted in significant losses. 该公司在市场上的不作为导致了巨大的损失。


近义词：idleness, inactivity, lethargy, sluggishness, dormancy

反义词：activity, energy, dynamism, vigor, liveliness


词源:  The word "inactivities" is formed by adding the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") to the noun "activities."  "Activities" derives from the verb "to act," ultimately from the Latin word "agere" (to do, to drive, to act).
