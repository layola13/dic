purpureal:/pɜːrˈpjʊəriəl/ | adj. |紫红色的；紫色的 |  Of a deep purplish red color.  The word evokes a rich, deep shade, often associated with royalty and luxury.  | 例句：The sunset painted the sky with purpureal hues.  夕阳将天空染成了紫红色的色彩。

The purpureal robes of the cardinal signified his high rank.  枢机的紫红色长袍象征着他的高位。| 近义词：crimson, purple, violet, magenta | 反义词：pale, light, white

词源:
