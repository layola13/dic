coronoid:/ˈkɒrənɔɪd/|/ˈkɔːrənɔɪd/| n. |喙状突；冠状突 |A coronoid process is a bony projection shaped like a beak or a crow's beak.  It's found in several parts of the body, notably in the mandible (lower jaw) and ulna (forearm bone).|例句：The coronoid process of the mandible articulates with the temporal bone.|近义词：process (in the anatomical sense)|反义词：None specifically, but terms describing opposite anatomical features could be considered antonyms depending on the context.
