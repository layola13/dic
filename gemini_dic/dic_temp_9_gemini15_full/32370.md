chile:/ˈtʃɪli/ | n. |智利；辣椒|A country in South America.  Also, a type of spicy pepper. |例句：Chile is known for its copper mines. 智利以其铜矿而闻名。
例句：I added some chili to the soup to make it spicier. 我在汤里加了一些辣椒，使它更辣。|近义词：pepper (for the fruit), republic (for the country) |反义词： (country) none, (pepper) mild pepper
