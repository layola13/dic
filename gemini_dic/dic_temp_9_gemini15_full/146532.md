overglamorize: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməraɪz/ | vt. |过分渲染，过分美化 | To exaggerate the glamour or attractiveness of something; to make something seem more glamorous than it really is.  | 例句：The advertisement overglamorizes the product's features, making it seem far more effective than it actually is.  广告过分渲染了产品的特性，使其看起来比实际效果好得多。

The reality TV show overglamorizes the lifestyle of the wealthy, presenting a distorted picture of their daily lives.  这个真人秀节目过分美化了富人的生活方式，呈现了他们日常生活扭曲的画面。| 近义词：embellish, romanticize, idealize | 反义词：understate, downplay, minimize | 动词
