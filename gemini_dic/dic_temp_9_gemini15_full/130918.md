nonascertainable: /ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtənəbl/ | adj. | 不可确定的，无法查明的 |  Unable to be discovered, known, or verified.  It implies a lack of means or possibility to determine something definitively. | The cause of the accident remains nonascertainable.  事故原因仍然无法查明。 | ascertainable, determinable | ascertainable, verifiable |  形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in the example.  It describes a noun.

Example Sentences:

The exact number of casualties is nonascertainable.  伤亡的确切数字无法确定。

The witness's testimony offered no nonascertainable facts.  证人的证词没有提供任何无法确定的事实。(This example uses "no nonascertainable" to indicate the presence of ascertainable facts.)
