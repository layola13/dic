coincident-with:/ˌkoʊɪnˈsɪdənt wɪθ/| adj. |与…同时发生|Happening at the same time; simultaneous.  This adjective is used to describe events or circumstances that occur at the same time, often implying a connection or correlation, though not necessarily a causal relationship.|例句：The eruption of the volcano was coincident with a significant earthquake. 火山爆发与一次强烈地震同时发生。

The economic downturn was coincident with a rise in unemployment. 经济衰退与失业率上升同时发生。|近义词：simultaneous, concurrent, contemporaneous |反义词：disparate, separate, distinct
