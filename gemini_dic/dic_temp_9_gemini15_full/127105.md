nationalization:/ˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 国有化；民族化 | The process of transferring ownership of a company or industry from the private sector to the government.  This often involves the government taking control of assets and operations. | Example: The nationalization of the railway system led to improved infrastructure but also increased bureaucracy.  国有化铁路系统改善了基础设施，但也增加了官僚主义。 | 近义词：governmentalization, socialization | 反义词：privatization
