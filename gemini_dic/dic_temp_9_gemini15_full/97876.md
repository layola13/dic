inmost:/ˈɪnmoʊst/ | adj. | 最深处的；最内心的；最隐秘的 |  The innermost part of one's being; the most secret or private thoughts and feelings.  It refers to something furthest inside or deepest within a place, object, or person's mind. |  例句：He revealed his inmost secrets to his diary. 他向他的日记透露了他内心最深处的秘密。


例句：The inmost chamber of the castle was untouched for centuries. 城堡最里面的房间几个世纪都没有动过。


近义词：innermost, deepest, most secret | 反义词：outermost, superficial, external | 词性：形容词
