curation:/kjʊˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 策展；馆藏；管理；选择 | The careful selection and organization of items for exhibition or display.  It involves choosing, arranging, and presenting items in a meaningful and engaging way.  | 例句：The museum's curation of the Picasso exhibition was exceptional. = 博物馆对毕加索展览的策展工作非常出色。

例句：The online store's curation of vintage clothing is impressive. = 这家网店的复古服装精选令人印象深刻。


近义词：selection, arrangement, organization, exhibition, management | 反义词：neglect, disorganization, randomness
