predraws:/ˌpriːˈdrɔːz/| v.|预先绘制，预先画图|To make a preliminary drawing or sketch before the final version.  It often involves planning and outlining the main elements of a larger artwork or design.|例句：The architect predrew the building's facade before starting the detailed blueprints. 建筑师在开始详细蓝图之前预先绘制了建筑物的正面。

例句：The artist predrew the landscape to determine the composition before applying paint.艺术家在着色之前预先绘制了风景以确定构图。


近义词：sketch, outline, rough draft, preliminary sketch


反义词：finalize, complete, finish
