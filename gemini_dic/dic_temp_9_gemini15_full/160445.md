popularizer:/ˈpɑːpjʊləraɪzər/|/ˈpɒpjʊləraɪzər/| n. |普及者；推广者 |A popularizer makes complex ideas or subjects accessible and understandable to a wider audience.  They simplify information and present it in a clear and engaging way. |Example:  Carl Sagan was a popularizer of science, making astronomy engaging for the masses. 卡尔·萨根是一位科学普及者，他使天文学对大众来说引人入胜。|近义词：advocate, proponent, disseminator, expounder|反义词：obscurantist, obfuscator|名词
