immolation:/ɪməˈleɪʃən/| n. |焚祭；牺牲；毁灭|The act of immolating something or someone;  the destruction or sacrifice of something.  It often refers to the burning of a victim as a sacrifice to a deity, but can also refer to the destruction or sacrifice of something less literal, such as a career or reputation.|例句：The ancient ritual involved the immolation of a lamb. 古代仪式包括献祭一只羔羊。

The immolation of his career was a result of his reckless actions. 他职业生涯的毁灭是他鲁莽行为的结果。|近义词：sacrifice, destruction, obliteration |反义词：preservation, salvation, creation
