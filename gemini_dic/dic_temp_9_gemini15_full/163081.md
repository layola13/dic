precoding:/ˈpriːkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. |预编码，预编码技术 |The process of encoding data before transmission or storage to improve efficiency or performance.  Precoding is often used in communication systems to mitigate interference and improve signal quality.  |Example:  Precoding techniques are essential for maximizing data rates in MIMO systems. 预编码技术对于最大化MIMO系统的数据速率至关重要。|近义词：pre-processing, encoding |反义词：decoding
