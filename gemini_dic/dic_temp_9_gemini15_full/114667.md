PHRASE->
malice aforethought (蓄意谋杀):  A planned and intentional act of malice.
malice in fact (事实上的恶意): Malice that can be proven through evidence.
malice towards (对…的恶意): Feeling or showing ill will towards someone.
act of malice (恶意行为):  An action performed with the intention of causing harm.
full of malice (充满恶意的):  Showing a great deal of ill will.

词源:  The word "malicing" comes from the Middle English word "malicien," meaning "to act with malice."  The root is the Latin word "malitia," meaning "evil, wickedness, badness."
