obstructionistic: /ˌɒbˈstrʌkʃənɪstɪk/ | adj. | 故意阻挠的；妨碍的；设置障碍的 |  Characterized by or tending toward obstruction; deliberately hindering or delaying.  It implies a conscious and often malicious intent to impede progress or prevent something from happening. |

例句：

The obstructionistic tactics of the opposition party delayed the passage of the bill.  = 反对党的故意阻挠策略延误了法案的通过。

His obstructionistic behavior in the meeting frustrated everyone. = 他在会议上的故意阻挠行为让每个人都很沮丧。


近义词：

obstructive, hindering, impeding, delaying, thwarting, blocking, resistant


反义词：

cooperative, helpful, supportive, facilitating, assisting


词性：形容词
