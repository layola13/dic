festival:/ˈfɛstɪvəl/ | n. |节日，庆典 |A festival is a time of celebration, often with special events, activities, and traditions.  It can be religious, cultural, or secular. |例句：The town held a harvest festival to celebrate the end of the growing season.  这个小镇举办了一个丰收节来庆祝种植季节的结束。|例句：Many people travel to Rio de Janeiro to experience the Carnival festival. 许多人前往里约热内卢体验狂欢节。 |近义词：celebration, feast, holiday |反义词：mourning, solemnity
