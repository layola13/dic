词源:
FBI is an acronym for the Federal Bureau of Investigation.  The full name explains its meaning.  Its origins trace back to the establishment of the Bureau of Investigation in 1908, which underwent several name changes and reorganizations before becoming the Federal Bureau of Investigation in 1935.
