home-in-on:/hoʊmˈɪnɑːn/|/ˈhoʊmɪnɒn/| v. |瞄准；集中于；锁定；|to direct one's attention or efforts toward a particular target or goal.  This often implies a process of narrowing down possibilities until a specific focus is reached.|The police homed in on the suspect's car. |警察盯上了嫌疑人的车。
|The researchers homed in on a promising new treatment for the disease.|研究人员集中精力研究一种很有前景的治疗这种疾病的新疗法。|focus on, zero in on, target|digress, stray, wander


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They home in on the problem. = 他们专注于这个问题。
We home in on the target. = 我们瞄准目标。

一般过去时:
The missile homed in on its target. = 导弹击中了目标。
The detective homed in on a crucial clue. = 侦探找到了一个关键线索。


现在完成时:
The team has homed in on a solution. = 团队已经找到了解决方案。
She has homed in on her career goals. = 她已经专注于她的职业目标。


过去完成时:
The investigators had homed in on the suspect before the arrest. = 调查人员在逮捕之前已经锁定了嫌疑人。
They had homed in on the source of the problem by the end of the day. = 他们在当天结束前已经找到了问题的根源。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
