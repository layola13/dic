lurk:/lɜːk/|v.|潜伏；潜藏；隐匿|To be or remain hidden, so as to be unnoticed; to lie in wait, typically with harmful intent.  |例句：The thief lurked in the shadows, waiting for the right moment to strike.  小偷潜伏在阴影中，等待合适的时机下手。|例句：Danger lurks around every corner in this city.  在这个城市里，每个角落都潜藏着危险。|近义词：hide, conceal, slink, prowl|反义词：reveal, expose, show


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lurks in the alleyway. = 他潜伏在小巷里。
They lurk online, spreading misinformation. = 他们在网上潜伏，传播不实信息。


一般过去时:
The predator lurked in the tall grass. = 捕食者潜伏在高高的草丛中。
She lurked in the shadows, observing her target. = 她潜伏在阴影中，观察她的目标。


现在完成时:
He has lurked in the shadows for weeks. = 他潜伏在阴影中已经几周了。
They have lurked online, anonymously commenting. = 他们一直在网上潜伏，匿名评论。


过去完成时:
The burglar had lurked outside the house for hours. = 窃贼在房子外面潜伏了几个小时。
She had lurked in the bushes before surprising him. = 她先前躲在灌木丛中，然后给他一个惊喜。


将来时:
He will lurk in the dark until the time is right. = 他会潜伏在黑暗中，直到时机成熟。
They will lurk online, waiting for an opportunity. = 他们会潜伏在网上，等待机会。


虚拟语气:
If he lurked there longer, he would have been caught. = 如果他潜伏在那里更久，他就会被抓住了。
I wish I hadn't lurked in the shadows; it felt wrong. = 我真希望我没有潜伏在阴影里；感觉不对劲。


被动语态:
The danger was lurked upon by the unsuspecting traveler.  (This is less common and sounds awkward.  Better phrasing is needed.) =  毫无戒心的旅行者遭遇了潜伏的危险。(这个句子不太常见，听起来很别扭。需要更好的措辞。)
The threat was lurking unseen. =  威胁潜伏着，没有被看见。


疑问句:
Is he lurking somewhere? = 他是不是潜伏在某个地方？
Were they lurking in the background? = 他们是不是潜伏在幕后？


否定句:
He doesn't lurk in dark places. = 他不会潜伏在黑暗的地方。
They didn't lurk in the shadows. = 他们没有潜伏在阴影中。


PHRASE->
lurk in the shadows:潜伏在阴影中
lurk online: 网上潜伏
lurk behind:潜伏在…后面
lurk beneath the surface:潜藏于表面之下
a lurking suspicion:潜伏的怀疑
