resecuring:/ˌriːsɪˈkjʊərɪŋ/| v. |重新确保；再次获得安全|The act of securing something again, after it has been lost or compromised.  It implies restoring a previous state of security.|例句：The company is resecuring its data after a recent cyberattack.  公司在最近一次网络攻击后正在重新确保其数据安全。

例句：After the storm, the family worked to resecure their belongings. 暴风雨过后，这家人努力重新固定他们的财物。


近义词：reinforcing, strengthening, safeguarding, protecting|反义词：compromising, endangering, jeopardizing|词性：动词


USAGE->
