hulls:/hʌlz/| n.|船体；外壳；（谷物等的）壳；（船的）船身 |The outer covering of a fruit, seed, or nut.  The body of a ship. The main structure of something.|例句：The ship's hull was damaged in the storm.  暴风雨中船体受损了。 The peanut hulls were scattered on the ground. 花生壳散落在地上。|近义词：shell, casing, frame, body |反义词：interior, core, content
