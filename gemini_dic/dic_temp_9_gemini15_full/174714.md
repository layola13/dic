quotients:/ˈkwoʊʃənts/ | n. |商；结果 |The quotient of 12 divided by 3 is 4.  12除以3的商是4。|近义词：result, outcome |反义词：N/A


英文解析：A quotient is the result of division.  It's the number of times one number (the divisor) goes into another number (the dividend).


例句：

The quotient of 10 divided by 2 is 5. = 10除以2的商是5。

What is the quotient when you divide 20 by 4? = 当你把20除以4时，商是多少？


词源:

The word "quotient" comes from the Latin word "quotiens," meaning "how many times," or "as many times as."  This reflects its core function in mathematics as the answer to a division problem.
