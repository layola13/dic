chroma:/ˈkroʊmə/ /ˈkrəʊmə/| n. |色度，色彩强度，色调|The intensity, purity, or saturation of a color.  It refers to the degree to which a color is pure or diluted with white or gray.  For example, a highly saturated red has high chroma, while a pale pink has low chroma.|The artist used a high chroma blue to create a vibrant effect.  艺术家使用了高色度蓝色来创造鲜艳的效果。|saturation, intensity, purity|achromatic, grayscale|名词
