pacifying: /ˈpæsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 安抚的；使平静的 |  Having a calming or soothing effect; tending to pacify.  | 例句：The pacifying effect of the music helped to calm the crying baby.  安抚的音乐有助于使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。

例句：Her soft words had a pacifying effect on him. 她温柔的话语对他有安抚作用。


近义词：soothing, calming, appeasing, conciliatory | 反义词：agitating, provoking, inflaming, irritating | 词性：形容词
