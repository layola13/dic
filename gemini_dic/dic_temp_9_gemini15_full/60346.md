egotheism:/ˈiːɡoʊθiːɪzəm/| n. |自我中心主义；利己主义|The excessive belief in one's own importance and abilities; a preoccupation with oneself and one's own interests, often to the exclusion of others.  It involves a grandiose sense of self-importance and a disregard for the feelings and needs of others.  |例句：His egotheism made it impossible for him to work effectively in a team.  他的自我中心主义使他无法有效地团队合作。


例句：Her egotheism was so extreme that she couldn't understand why anyone wouldn't agree with her. 她的自我中心主义如此极端以至于她无法理解为什么任何人不会同意她。


近义词：narcissism, self-centeredness, egotism, selfishness, conceit |反义词：altruism, selflessness, humility

词源:
The word "egotheism" is a blend of "ego" (from Latin, meaning "I") and "theism" (from Greek, meaning "belief in a god").  It suggests a form of self-worship, treating oneself as a god.
