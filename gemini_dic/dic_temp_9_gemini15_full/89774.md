hordes:/hɔːrdz/| n.|兽群；人群；大量 | A horde is a large group of people, typically one that is considered to be disorganized and potentially dangerous.  It often implies a vast number moving together. | Example:  Hordes of tourists flocked to the beach during the summer. 夏季期间，成群结队的游客涌向海滩。

Example:  Hordes of locusts descended upon the crops, destroying everything in their path.  蝗虫群袭击了庄稼，摧毁了它们经过的一切。


近义词：crowd, throng, swarm, mass, multitude

反义词：few, handful, small number

词性：名词
