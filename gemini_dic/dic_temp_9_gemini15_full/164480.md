preentitled:/ˌpriːɪnˈtaɪtəld/ | adj. | 预先有权利的；预先被赋予权利的 |  Having a right or entitlement beforehand; granted a right or privilege in advance.  | The preentitled children expected to inherit the family fortune.  这些预先有权继承的孩子们期望继承家族财产。 | entitled, privileged |  disenfranchised, deprived |  adj.
