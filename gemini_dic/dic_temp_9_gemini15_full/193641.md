self-caricature: /ˌsɛlf ˈkærɪkəˌtʃʊr/ | n. | 自画像，自我讽刺 | A representation of oneself in an exaggerated or ludicrous manner, often for humorous effect.  It can be a drawing, a written description, or even a performance.  It involves highlighting one's own flaws or eccentricities in a self-deprecating way. | Example: His stand-up routine was a brilliant self-caricature, poking fun at his own awkwardness and anxieties.  他的单口喜剧表演是一场精彩的自我讽刺，嘲笑他自己的笨拙和焦虑。


近义词: self-parody, self-satire, lampoon (of oneself) | 反义词: idealized self-portrait, self-aggrandizement | 词性: noun
