fractionating:/ˈfrækʃəneɪtɪŋ/| v.|分馏；分离；分级|The process of separating a liquid mixture into its components by boiling and condensing the vapors at different temperatures.  This technique is widely used in the petroleum industry to separate crude oil into various fractions like gasoline, kerosene, and diesel.|例句：The crude oil is fractionated in a distillation column to obtain different petroleum products. 原油在蒸馏塔中进行分馏以获得不同的石油产品。|例句：The chemist carefully fractionated the sample to isolate the desired compound. 化学家仔细地分馏样品以分离所需的化合物。|近义词：separating, distilling, refining|反义词：combining, mixing
