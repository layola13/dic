reirrigated: /ˌriːˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | v. | 再灌溉；重新灌溉 | To irrigate again; to water again after a previous irrigation.  | The farmer reirrigated the field after the recent dry spell.  农民在最近的旱期后再次灌溉了田地。 | irrigate, water |  dehydrate, dry out |  动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reirrigate the crops regularly. = 他们定期给庄稼重新灌溉。
The farmer reirrigates his fields every week. = 这位农民每周都给他的田地重新灌溉。

一般过去时:
The gardener reirrigated the lawn after the hot weather. = 炎热的天气过后，园丁给草坪重新浇水。
We reirrigated the parched land last night. = 我们昨晚给干涸的土地重新灌溉了。

现在完成时:
The farmers have reirrigated the fields twice this month. = 这个月农民们已经给田地重新灌溉了两次。
They have reirrigated the garden since the drought began. = 自干旱开始以来，他们已经多次给花园重新灌溉。

过去完成时:
The plants had already been reirrigated before the inspection. = 在检查之前，植物已经被重新灌溉过了。
The crops had been reirrigated several times before the harvest. = 收获前，庄稼已经被重新灌溉过好几次了。

将来时:
They will reirrigate the fields next week. = 他们下周将再次灌溉田地。
