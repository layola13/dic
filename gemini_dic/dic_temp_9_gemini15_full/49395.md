delitigate: /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ | vt. | 委派；授权；代表 | To entrust (a task or responsibility) to another person, typically one who is less senior than oneself.  | 例句：The manager delegated the task to his assistant.  经理将任务委托给了他的助理。

例句：The committee delegated the responsibility of fundraising to a subcommittee. 委员会将筹款的责任委托给了一个小组委员会。


近义词：assign, entrust, authorize, commission | 反义词：retain, keep, reserve | 词性: Verb (动词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He delegates tasks efficiently. = 他有效地委派任务。
She delegates responsibilities to her team. = 她将责任委托给她的团队。

一般过去时:
The boss delegated the project to a younger employee. = 老板将项目委托给了一位年轻的员工。
They delegated the decision-making process to a special committee. = 他们将决策过程委托给了一个特别委员会。

现在完成时:
He has delegated all his important tasks for the day. = 他已经委托了今天所有重要的任务。
The CEO has delegated the responsibility of hiring to the HR department. = 首席执行官已将招聘责任委托给人力资源部门。

过去完成时:
She had delegated the work before leaving for vacation. = 她在休假前已委托了工作。
They had delegated the responsibility to someone else before the crisis hit. = 危机发生前，他们已将责任委托给了其他人。


将来时:
The manager will delegate the new project to his most capable employee. = 经理将把新项目委托给最能干的员工。
She will delegate the task to someone with more experience. = 她将把任务委托给更有经验的人。


虚拟语气:
If I were the manager, I would delegate more tasks. = 如果我是经理，我会委派更多任务。
I wish he had delegated the task earlier. = 我希望他早点委托这项任务。


被动语态:
The task was delegated to the junior staff. = 任务被委托给了初级员工。
The responsibility will be delegated to a suitable candidate. = 责任将委托给合适的候选人。


疑问句:
To whom did you delegate the project? = 你把项目委托给了谁？
Will the responsibility be delegated to the new team members? = 责任会委托给新的团队成员吗？


否定句:
He didn't delegate the tasks properly. = 他没有正确地委派任务。
She refused to delegate any of her work. = 她拒绝委托任何她的工作。


PHRASE->
delegate authority = 授权
delegate responsibility = 委派责任
delegate a task = 委派任务
delegate powers = 授权
delegate to someone = 委托给某人

词源:
源自拉丁语 *dēlēgāre，由dē-（表示“向下”或“离开”）和lĕgāre（“派驻；派遣”）构成，字面意思是“派遣”。
