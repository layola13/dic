indicts:/ɪnˈdaɪts/| vt.|正式起诉，控告|To formally accuse someone of a crime.|The grand jury indicted him on charges of murder. 大陪审团以谋杀罪正式起诉他。|近义词：accuse, charge, arraign|反义词：acquit, exonerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The grand jury indicts the defendant. = 大陪审团起诉被告。
He indicts his opponent for corruption. = 他指控他的对手腐败。

一般过去时:
The prosecutor indicted the suspect last week. = 检察官上周起诉了嫌疑人。
They indicted him for fraud. = 他们以诈骗罪起诉他。

现在完成时:
The court has indicted several individuals in this case. = 法院在本案中已经起诉了几个人。
The government has indicted the CEO on multiple counts of fraud.  = 政府已经以多项欺诈罪起诉了首席执行官。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, the judge had indicted the man for theft. = 在审判之前，法官已经以盗窃罪起诉了这个人。
The police had indicted him on drug charges before the evidence was found. = 在证据被发现之前，警方已经以贩毒罪起诉了他。

将来时:
The prosecutor will indict the suspect next week. = 检察官下周将起诉嫌疑人。
They will likely indict him on multiple charges. = 他们很可能会以多项罪名起诉他。


虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would indict him. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会起诉他。
If the jury indicted him, he would face years in prison. = 如果陪审团起诉他，他将面临多年的监禁。

被动语态:
He was indicted on charges of conspiracy. = 他被指控犯有共谋罪。
The defendant was indicted by the grand jury. = 被告被大陪审团起诉。

疑问句:
Will the grand jury indict the defendant? = 大陪审团会起诉被告吗？
Has the prosecutor indicted anyone yet? = 检察官已经起诉任何人了吗？

否定句:
The grand jury did not indict the defendant. = 大陪审团没有起诉被告。
The prosecutor chose not to indict him due to lack of evidence. = 由于证据不足，检察官选择不予起诉他。


PHRASE->
indict for (a crime) =  以（某种罪名）起诉
indict on (charges) =  以（指控）起诉
be indicted on = 被指控犯有…
indictment for = 对…的起诉书
grand jury indictment = 大陪审团起诉书
