moonlight:/ˈmuːnlaɪt/| n.|月光；月色|The light from the moon.  It can refer to the light itself, or the effect of the light on something.|例句：The moonlight illuminated the path. 月光照亮了小路。

例句：We sat on the porch, enjoying the soft moonlight. 我们坐在门廊上，享受着柔和的月光。


近义词：moonbeam, moonshine


反义词：sunlight, daylight


词性:名词
