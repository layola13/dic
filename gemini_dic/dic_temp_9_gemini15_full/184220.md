resolidifying: /ˌriːsəˈlɪdɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 再凝固；重新固化 | The process of a substance changing from a liquid or gaseous state back to a solid state.  This often involves a decrease in temperature or an increase in pressure. | Example: The molten metal was slowly resolidifying in the mold.  熔化的金属正在模具中慢慢重新凝固。 The melted wax was resolidifying after cooling.冷却后融化的蜡正在重新凝固。|近义词：re-solidification, solidifying again |反义词：melting, liquefying |动词
