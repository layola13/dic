monothecal: /ˌmɒnəˈθiːkəl/ | adj. | 单室的；单心皮的 | Having only one locule or cavity;  referring to a single-celled structure or a single-carpellate pistil in botany.  | 例句：The monothecal anther of the plant was examined under a microscope. 这株植物的单室花药在显微镜下被观察。  The ovary is monothecal, containing a single ovule. 子房是单室的，含有一个胚珠。 | 近义词：unicellular (in some contexts), uniloculate | 反义词：polythecal, multilocular
