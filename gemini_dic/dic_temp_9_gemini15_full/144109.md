outscolded: /ˌaʊtˈskoʊldɪd/ | v. |  大声斥责；超过…的斥责 |  To scold or rebuke someone more severely than someone else.  This word is the past participle of the verb "outscold". | 例句：The teacher outscolded the boy for his disruptive behavior.  老师因男孩的扰乱行为而大声斥责了他。


The principal outscolded the bully more severely than the victim. 校长比受害者更严厉地斥责了那个欺凌者。

近义词：outcry, berate, rebuke, reprimand, chastise | 反义词：praise, commend, compliment
