ornithichnite:/ɔːrnɪˈθɪknaɪt/| n. |鸟足迹化石|A fossilized bird footprint.  Ornithichnites are trace fossils, meaning they are evidence of an organism's activity rather than the organism itself.  They provide valuable information about the behavior, size, and locomotion of extinct birds.  The study of ornithichnites is important for paleontology and understanding the evolution of birds.|The discovery of ornithichnites in the sandstone confirmed the presence of birds in the area millions of years ago.  鸟足迹化石在砂岩中的发现证实了数百万年前该地区存在鸟类。| ichnofossil, trace fossil|  (无明显反义词)|名词
