chose: /tʃoʊz/ | v. | 选择，挑选 | To select or pick out from a number of possibilities.  |例句：I chose the red dress. 我选择了那件红裙子。|近义词：selected, picked, opted |反义词：rejected, refused |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chooses his words carefully. = 他仔细地选择他的话。
She chooses a new car every year. = 她每年都选择一辆新车。

一般过去时:
I chose the pasta for dinner. = 我选择意大利面作为晚餐。
They chose to stay home instead of going out. = 他们选择待在家里而不是出去。

现在完成时:
She has chosen a career in medicine. = 她已经选择了一个医学职业。
He has chosen to ignore the problem. = 他选择忽略这个问题。

过去完成时:
I had chosen my outfit before the party started. = 派对开始前我已经选好了我的衣服。
They had chosen their seats before the movie began. = 电影开始前他们就已经选好了座位。

将来时:
I will choose a gift for my friend's birthday. = 我将选择一件礼物作为朋友的生日礼物。
She will choose the best candidate for the job. = 她将选择最合适的候选人担任这份工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would choose the blue one. = 如果我是你，我会选择蓝色的那个。
I wish I had chosen a different path in life. = 我希望我人生中选择了不同的道路。

被动语态:
The winner was chosen by a panel of judges. = 获胜者由评审团选出。
The best option was chosen after careful consideration. =经过仔细考虑后选择了最佳方案。

疑问句:
Which dress did you choose? = 你选哪件裙子？
What career path have you chosen? = 你选择了什么职业道路？

否定句:
I didn't choose that option. = 我没有选择那个选项。
They didn't choose to participate in the event. = 他们没有选择参加这个活动。


PHRASE->
choose from = 从...中选择
choose to = 选择做...
choose between = 在...之间选择
have one's choice = 有选择的权利
make a choice = 做出选择

词源:
The word "chose" is the past tense of the verb "choose,"  ultimately deriving from Old English *ceosan, meaning "to choose, select."  This Old English word has cognates in other Germanic languages, reflecting a common ancestor within the Proto-Germanic language family.
