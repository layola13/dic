resodded:/ˌriːˈsɒdɪd/ | v. | 重新铺设草皮 | To resod is to replace the sod or turf of a lawn or other area with new sod.  This involves removing the old grass and soil and laying down fresh sod to create a new lawn. | Example:  The football field was resodded after the concert. 足球场在音乐会后重新铺设了草皮。  The park resodded the damaged areas after the heavy rains. 公园在暴雨后重新铺设了受损的区域。| 近义词: re-turf, relay, replace (turf)| 反义词:  remove sod, leave bare
