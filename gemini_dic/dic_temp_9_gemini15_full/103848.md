joystick:/ˈdʒɔɪstɪk/ | n. |操纵杆；游戏杆 | A joystick is a lever that is used to control the movement of a vehicle, machine, or computer cursor. It typically consists of a stick that can be moved in multiple directions, and buttons or switches for additional control. |Example: He expertly manipulated the joystick to guide the spaceship through the asteroid field. 他熟练地操纵操纵杆，引导宇宙飞船穿过陨石区。  |近义词: control stick, lever, game controller |反义词: None
