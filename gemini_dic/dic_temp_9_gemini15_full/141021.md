/ɒnˈɡəʊɪŋli/ | adj. | 不断地；持续地 | Continuously; in a way that continues without stopping.  | The project is ongoing, and we expect to complete it by the end of the year.  这个项目正在进行中，我们预计将在年底前完成。 | continuous, constant, persistent | intermittent, sporadic, temporary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The work is ongoing. = 工作正在持续进行。
The negotiations are ongoing. = 谈判正在持续进行中。

一般过去时:
The investigation was ongoing at the time of the report. = 报告撰写时，调查仍在进行中。
The project was ongoing until funding ran out. = 直到资金用尽，项目才停止。

现在完成时:
The renovations have been ongoing for months. = 装修已经持续了好几个月了。
The conflict has been ongoing since the beginning of the year. = 冲突从年初就一直持续着。

过去完成时:
The project had been ongoing for years before it was finally completed. = 该项目在最终完成之前已经持续了数年。
The research had been ongoing for several months before they published their findings. = 他们在发表研究结果之前已经进行了几个月的研究。

将来时:
The repairs will be ongoing for several more weeks. = 修理工作还将持续几周。
The development will be ongoing for the foreseeable future. =  发展在可预见的未来将持续进行。

虚拟语气:
If the problem were ongoing, we would have to take more drastic measures. = 如果问题持续存在，我们将不得不采取更严厉的措施。
I wish the improvements were ongoing at a faster pace. = 我希望改进能够更快地进行。

被动语态:
The investigation is being ongoingly monitored. = 调查正在持续进行监控。
The situation is being ongoingly assessed. = 情况正在持续评估中。

疑问句:
Is the construction ongoing? = 建筑工程还在进行吗？
Are the negotiations still ongoing? = 谈判还在继续吗？

否定句:
The project is not ongoing; it was completed last week. = 项目已经结束，上周就完成了。
The repairs are not ongoing at the moment. = 目前没有进行修理工作。

ongoing support: 持续的支持
