flatulency:/ˈflætʃələnsi/ | n. |肠胃气胀；放屁 |The expulsion of gas from the bowels through the anus.  It is often accompanied by a characteristic sound and odor.  The condition can be caused by various factors, including diet, digestive issues, and certain medical conditions. | Example:  The embarrassing flatulency during the presentation made him blush.  这次演讲中尴尬的放屁让他脸红了。  |近义词：flatus, breaking wind | 反义词：None directly applicable.
