remittent:/rɪˈmɪtənt/ | adj. | 间歇的；周期性发作的；反复的 | Recurring or appearing at intervals; intermittent.  |The patient experienced remittent fever, with periods of high temperature followed by periods of lower temperature.| 近义词：intermittent, periodic, recurring | 反义词：constant, continuous, persistent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fever remits intermittently. =  发烧间歇性地缓解。
This condition is remittent in nature. = 这种情况是间歇性的。


一般过去时:
The pain remitted after he took the medicine. = 他服药后疼痛缓解了。
Her symptoms remitted temporarily. = 她的症状暂时缓解了。


现在完成时:
His fever has remitted significantly. = 他的烧已经显著退了。
The symptoms have remitted since starting the new treatment. = 自从开始新的治疗以来，症状已经缓解了。


过去完成时:
The headache had remitted before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，头痛已经缓解了。
Her cough had remitted slightly by the end of the week. = 到周末时，她的咳嗽已经稍微好转了。


将来时:
The doctor hopes the symptoms will remit soon. = 医生希望症状很快会缓解。
We expect the inflammation to remit after the surgery. = 我们预计手术后炎症会消退。


虚拟语气:
If the fever remitted, he would feel much better. = 如果发烧退了，他会感觉好得多。
I wish the pain would remit quickly. = 我希望疼痛能很快缓解。


被动语态:
The symptoms were remitted by the new medication. = 新药使症状缓解了。
The inflammation is being remitted through physical therapy. = 通过物理疗法正在使炎症消退。


疑问句:
Does the fever remit regularly? = 发烧会定期缓解吗？
Has the pain remitted completely? = 疼痛完全缓解了吗？


否定句:
The fever did not remit easily. = 发烧不容易退。
The pain has not remitted despite the medication. = 尽管服药，疼痛仍未缓解。
