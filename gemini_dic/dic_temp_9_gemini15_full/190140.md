sanitariums:/ˌsænɪˈteəriəm/ | n. |疗养院，精神病院 | A sanitarium is an institution for the treatment of the sick, especially those with chronic illnesses, or a mental institution.  It is now largely an outdated term. | Example:  The family sent him to a sanitarium in the mountains to recover his health.  家人送他去山上的疗养院休养身体。  |近义词：hospital, asylum, clinic, health resort | 反义词：None directly applicable, but implies the opposite of a place of healing or recovery. |名词
