capital:/ˈkæpɪtl/| n. adj.|首都；资本；大写字母；资金；|英文解析:A capital city is the most important city in a country or region, often serving as the seat of government. Capital can also refer to wealth in the form of money or property, or to uppercase letters in writing.  |例句：London is the capital of England.  伦敦是英格兰的首都。

The company needs more capital to expand. 这家公司需要更多资金来扩张。

近义词：metropolis, main city, funds, resources, uppercase |反义词：province, countryside, lowercase, small town


USAGE->

The capital of France is Paris. = 法国的首都巴黎。
This project requires significant capital investment. = 这个项目需要大量的资金投入。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
