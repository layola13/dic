quasi-engagingly: /ˈkwɑːzi ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋli/ | adv. |似是而非地吸引人地; 模糊地吸引人地 |  Describes something that appears to be engaging or interesting, but only superficially or to a limited extent.  It suggests a lack of genuine depth or connection. |


例句：He spoke quasi-engagingly about his work, but I couldn't shake the feeling that he was bored. = 他似是而非地谈论他的工作，但我还是感觉他很无聊。

He presented the data quasi-engagingly, using flashy visuals but lacking substance. = 他用花哨的视觉效果似是而非地呈现数据，但缺乏实质内容。


近义词： superficially engaging, vaguely interesting, seemingly captivating, halfheartedly engaging

反义词： genuinely engaging, deeply captivating, truly interesting, compelling


词性: 副词
