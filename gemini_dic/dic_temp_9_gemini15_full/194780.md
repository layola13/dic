sells:/sɛlz/ | v. |出售，销售|To exchange (something) for money; to be sold at a particular price.  It can also refer to the act of persuading someone to buy something. |例句：The store sells a wide variety of goods. 这家商店出售各种各样的商品。

The company sells its products online and in stores. 这家公司在网上和实体店销售其产品。|近义词：trade, market, peddle, retail |反义词：buys


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sells cars for a living. = 他靠卖车为生。
She sells handmade jewelry online. = 她在线销售手工珠宝。


一般过去时:
They sold their house last year. = 他们去年卖掉了房子。
He sold me a faulty appliance. = 他卖给了我一件有故障的电器。


现在完成时:
We have sold all our tickets. = 我们已经卖完了所有的票。
She has sold over a thousand copies of her book. = 她的书已经卖出了超过一千本。


过去完成时:
They had sold most of their stock before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经卖掉了大部分库存。
The company had sold its assets before filing for bankruptcy. = 这家公司在申请破产前已经出售了其资产。


将来时:
I will sell my car next week. = 我下周将卖掉我的车。
They will sell their paintings at the auction. = 他们将在拍卖会上出售他们的绘画作品。


虚拟语气:
If I sold my house, I could afford a new car. = 如果我卖掉我的房子，我就能买一辆新车。
I wish I had sold my stocks before the crash. = 我真希望我在崩盘前卖掉了我的股票。


被动语态:
The tickets are sold out. = 票卖完了。
The house was sold for a good price. = 这房子以好价钱卖掉了。


疑问句:
Does this store sell books? = 这家商店卖书吗？
Where do they sell organic vegetables? = 他们在哪里卖有机蔬菜？


否定句:
I don't sell drugs. = 我不卖毒品。
The shop doesn't sell that item anymore. = 这家店不再卖那个商品了。


PHRASE->
sell out = 卖光；出卖
sell off = 清仓处理；廉价出售
sell short = 低估；做空
sell like hotcakes = 畅销
sell oneself = 自卖自夸
