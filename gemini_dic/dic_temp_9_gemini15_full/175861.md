rata:/ˈrɑːtə/ | n. |  比率，速度，等级 | A rata is a fixed rate or proportion, often used in financial contexts. It can also refer to a rate of speed or a classification or rank.  | Example: The interest rate on the loan was a fixed rata of 5%. 这笔贷款的利率是固定的5%。 The company's growth rate has been at a steady rata. 该公司的增长速度一直很稳定。 | 近义词：rate, proportion, ratio | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The interest rata is 5%. = 利率是5%。
What is the exchange rata today? = 今天的汇率是多少？


一般过去时:
The growth rata was high last year. = 去年增长率很高。
The inflation rata was 2% last month. = 上个月通货膨胀率为2%。


现在完成时:
The company has maintained a steady rata of growth. = 公司一直保持着稳定的增长速度。
Interest rates have remained at a low rata for several years. = 多年来利率一直保持在低位。


过去完成时:
The inflation rata had already risen before the government intervened. = 政府干预之前，通货膨胀率已经上升了。
The growth rata had slowed down before the economic crisis. = 经济危机之前，增长速度已经放缓了。


将来时:
The interest rata will likely increase next year. = 利率明年可能会上涨。
What will be the exchange rata tomorrow? = 明天的汇率是多少？


虚拟语气:
If the growth rata were higher, the economy would improve. = 如果增长率更高，经济就会好转。
I wish the interest rata were lower. = 我希望利率更低。


被动语态:
The rata was set by the central bank. = 比率是由中央银行设定的。
The growth rata is being closely monitored. = 增长率正受到密切监控。


疑问句:
What is the rata of exchange? = 汇率是多少？

否定句:
