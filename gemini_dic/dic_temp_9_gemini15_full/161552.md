pots:/pɒts/|n.|罐，壶，盆|A pot is a container, usually round and made of metal, clay, or plastic, used for cooking, storing, or serving food or drink.|例句：She put the flowers in a beautiful ceramic pot. 她把花放在一个漂亮的陶瓷花盆里。|例句：The soup is simmering gently in the pot. 汤在锅里慢慢地炖着。|近义词：container, vessel, urn|反义词：N/A
