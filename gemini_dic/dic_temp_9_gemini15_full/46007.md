cybercriminals:/ˈsaɪbərˈkrɪmɪnəlz/| n. |网络罪犯|People who commit crimes using computers and the internet.  They engage in various illegal activities, such as hacking, data theft, fraud, and cyberstalking.|Example: Cybercriminals are constantly developing new ways to exploit vulnerabilities in computer systems. 网络罪犯不断开发新的方法来利用计算机系统中的漏洞。|近义词：hackers, online criminals, internet criminals|反义词：cybersecurity professionals, law enforcement officers
