silaged:/ˈsaɪlɪdʒd/| adj.|青贮的；用青贮法保存的|preserved fodder (typically grass or corn) stored in airtight conditions;  the process of preserving fodder in this way.  It is a method of preserving forage for animal feed.|例句：The farmer silaged the corn for winter feed.  这位农民将玉米青贮起来作为冬天的饲料。
The silage was stored in a large silo.青贮饲料储存在一个大型筒仓里。|近义词：preserved, ensiled|反义词：fresh, unpreserved
