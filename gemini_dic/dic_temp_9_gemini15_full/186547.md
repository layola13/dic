riddler:/ˈrɪdlər/| n. |谜语者，谜题制造者 |A person who creates and poses riddles;  someone who is enigmatic and difficult to understand. |例句：The riddler left a trail of cryptic clues for the detective to follow.  谜语者留下了一连串神秘的线索供侦探追踪。  |近义词：enigmatist, puzzler, conundrum-maker |反义词：explainer, elucidator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He riddles me every day. = 他每天都给我出谜语。
The riddler poses many difficult questions. = 谜语者提出了许多难题。


一般过去时:
He riddled me with questions yesterday. = 他昨天用问题把我难住了。
The riddler posed a challenging riddle last night. = 谜语者昨晚提出一个具有挑战性的谜语。


现在完成时:
He has riddled me with his strange behavior. = 他古怪的行为让我百思不得其解。
The riddler has created many complex puzzles. = 谜语者已经创造了许多复杂的谜题。


过去完成时:
He had riddled me before, so I was prepared. = 他以前就难住过我，所以我做好了准备。
The riddler had already planted clues before the game started. = 在游戏开始之前，谜语者就已经埋下了线索。


将来时:
He will riddle me again tomorrow. = 他明天还会再给我出谜语。
The riddler will reveal the answer next week. = 谜语者下周将揭晓答案。


虚拟语气:
If he were a riddler, he would enjoy this game. = 如果他是个谜语者，他会喜欢这个游戏的。
I wish the riddler would give us a hint. = 我希望谜语者能给我们一点提示。


被动语态:
He was riddled with doubts. = 他充满了怀疑。
The answer was riddled with errors. = 答案充满了错误。


疑问句:
Is he a riddler? = 他是谜语者吗？
Was the message riddled with secrets? = 这条消息是否充满了秘密？


否定句:
He is not a riddler. = 他不是谜语者。
The case was not riddled with mysteries. = 此案并非充满神秘之处。


PHRASE->
riddler's game = 谜语游戏
riddler's clues = 谜语者的线索
riddler's question = 谜语者的提问
the Riddler (Batman villain) = 谜语人（蝙蝠侠反派）
riddled with = 充满...的 (e.g., riddled with problems)


词源:
From the word "riddle," which ultimately derives from Old English *rædel, meaning "a guessing game," related to words such as "read" and "rede" (counsel).  The "-er" suffix denotes an agent or doer of the action.
