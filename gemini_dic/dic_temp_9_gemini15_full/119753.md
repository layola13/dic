midwit:/ˈmɪdˌwɪt/| n.|中等智力的人，中等智慧的人|A person of middling or average intelligence, often implying a smugness or overconfidence disproportionate to their actual abilities.  The term often carries a derogatory or mocking connotation, suggesting a lack of self-awareness.|例句：He fancies himself an intellectual, but he's just a midwit spouting clichés. 他自认为是知识分子，但他只是一个只会说些陈词滥调的普通人。He's a midwit who thinks he's a genius. 他是一个自以为天才的普通人。|近义词：intellectual lightweight, average Joe, commoner|反义词：genius, intellectual, savant
