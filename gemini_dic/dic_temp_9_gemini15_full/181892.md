remagnetizing:/ˌriːˈmæɡnɪtaɪzɪŋ/| v.|重新磁化；再磁化 |The process of restoring or changing the magnetic properties of a material.  It involves realigning the magnetic domains within the material to create a new or stronger magnetic field.|例句：The hard drive needed remagnetizing after the power surge.  电源浪涌后，硬盘需要重新磁化。

例句：We are remagnetizing the steel components to enhance their magnetic strength. 我们正在重新磁化钢制部件以增强其磁性强度。


近义词：re-magnetize, remagnetise


反义词：demagnetizing
