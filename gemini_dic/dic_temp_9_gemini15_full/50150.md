denunciations:/ˌdɛnʌnsiˈeɪʃənz/| n.|公开谴责；告发；指责|The act of publicly condemning or accusing someone or something.  It often involves revealing wrongdoing or unacceptable behavior.|例句：The government faced numerous denunciations for its human rights abuses.  政府因其侵犯人权而面临无数谴责。

例句：His denunciations of corruption were met with both praise and hostility. 他对腐败的谴责既受到了赞扬，也受到了敌视。


近义词：condemnations, criticisms, accusations, indictments

反义词：praises, endorsements, approvals


词性：名词
