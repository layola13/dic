christendom:/ˈkrɪstəndəm/| n. |基督教世界；基督教徒；基督教信仰|The collective body of Christian believers and churches throughout the world; the entire Christian world.  It can also refer to the geographical areas predominantly Christian. |例句：The news spread rapidly throughout Christendom.  这个消息迅速传遍了整个基督教世界。

例句：For centuries, Christendom was divided into Eastern and Western branches.几个世纪以来，基督教世界分为东西方两支。


近义词：Christianity (as a religion), the Christian world


反义词：paganism, non-Christian world


词性:名词
