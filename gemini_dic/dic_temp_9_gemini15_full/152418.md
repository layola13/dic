pavonine:/ˈpævənaɪn/| adj.|孔雀石色的；华丽的；炫耀的|Resembling the plumage of a peacock; iridescent; ostentatious.  The adjective refers to the characteristic shimmering, iridescent colours of a peacock's feathers.|例句：The pavonine tail of the peacock shimmered in the sunlight. 孔雀那华丽的尾巴在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：Her pavonine jewelry drew admiring glances. 她炫目的珠宝吸引了羡慕的目光。


近义词：iridescent, resplendent, showy, flamboyant|反义词：dull, drab, understated


词源:  From Latin *pavo ("peacock") + -ine (suffix forming adjectives).  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *peh₂w-, meaning "peafowl".
