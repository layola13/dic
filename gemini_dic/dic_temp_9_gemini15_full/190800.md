savoyard:/ˈsævɔɪɑːrd/| adj. n.|萨伏伊人；萨伏伊的；萨伏伊菜|Of or relating to Savoy, a former region in southeastern France.  A Savoyard is a person from Savoy.  Savoyard cuisine is known for its use of cheese, potatoes, and other local ingredients.|The Savoyard cheese is delicious. 萨伏伊奶酪很好吃。|He is a proud Savoyard. 他是一个骄傲的萨伏伊人。|French, Swiss|No direct antonyms, but words like "French" or "Italian" could be used in contrast depending on the context.|
