semiexecutive: /ˌsemiɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv/ | adj. | 半行政的；准行政的 |  Relating to or denoting a position or job that is partly executive in nature, often involving some managerial responsibilities but lacking the full authority of an executive.  It suggests a position that bridges the gap between a purely administrative role and a full executive position. | Example:  He held a semiexecutive position, managing a small team but reporting directly to a senior executive. 他担任半行政职位，管理一个小团队，但直接向高级主管汇报。


近义词: quasi-executive, sub-executive

反义词: fully executive, purely administrative

词性: adjective
