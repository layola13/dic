discernible:/dɪˈsɜːrnəbl/ | adj. | 可辨别的，可识别的，看得见的 | Able to be perceived or understood; noticeable.  | The faint sound was barely discernible. 微弱的声音几乎难以辨别。The difference between the two products is clearly discernible.  两种产品之间的区别是显而易见的。 | perceptible, noticeable, detectable, apparent | indistinguishable, imperceptible, unnoticeable
