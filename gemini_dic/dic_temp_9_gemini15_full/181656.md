releasability: /ˌriˈliːzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可释放性；可发布性；可解除性 | The ability to be released or discharged.  This term often refers to the capability of something to be set free, made available, or let go of, depending on the context. It can apply to software, prisoners, information, or even physical objects. | 例句：The software's releasability is dependent on passing all the tests.  软件的可发布性取决于通过所有测试。


The high releasability of the prisoners raised concerns about public safety. 囚犯的高释放率引发了对公共安全的担忧。


近义词：  dischargeability,  availability,  accessibility | 反义词：  irreleasability (This is not a common word, often replaced with phrases like "inability to release")
