oceanographers:/ˌoʊʃəˈnɑːɡrəfərz/ | n. | 海洋学家 | Scientists who study the ocean.  Oceanographers explore various aspects of the ocean, including its physical properties, chemical composition, biological life, and geological features. They use a variety of tools and techniques to gather data and analyze the complex processes occurring within the ocean. |  Many oceanographers are working to understand the impact of climate change on the oceans.  许多海洋学家正在努力了解气候变化对海洋的影响。


The field of oceanography has expanded greatly in recent decades. 海洋学领域在近几十年来得到了很大的发展。|近义词：marine scientists, ocean scientists |反义词：N/A
