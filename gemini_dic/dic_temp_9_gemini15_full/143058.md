outboard motors:/ˈaʊtˌbɔːrd ˈmoʊtərz/| n. |舷外发动机；船外机|A motor that is mounted on the outside of a boat, typically on the stern (rear).  Outboard motors are commonly used to power small boats such as fishing boats, dinghies, and inflatable boats.|例句：He installed a new outboard motor on his fishing boat. 他在他的渔船上安装了一个新的舷外发动机。

例句：The outboard motor sputtered and died halfway across the lake.  舷外发动机在湖中心熄火了。


近义词：boat motor, outboard, stern motor|反义词：inboard motor (内置发动机)|词性：名词
