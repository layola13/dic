investitures:/ɪnˈvɛstɪtʃərz/| n. |授职仪式；授予；封授 |The formal act of investing someone with an office or dignity; the conferring of an office, rank, or dignity.  Investitures often involved symbolic gestures, such as the bestowal of a robe or staff. |例句：The king performed the investiture ceremony, bestowing the knighthood upon the brave warrior. 国王举行了授勋仪式，授予这位勇敢的战士爵位。

例句：The Archbishop conducted the investiture of the new bishop. 大主教主持了新主教的授职仪式。


近义词：conferment, installation, inauguration |反义词：removal, dismissal, deposition |词性：名词
