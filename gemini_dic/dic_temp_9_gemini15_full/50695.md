dermestes:/ˈdɜːrmɪstiːz/| n. |皮蠹属(昆虫)|A genus of beetles belonging to the family Dermestidae, known for their larvae's ability to consume various organic materials, including hides, wool, and other animal products.  They are often considered pests due to their destructive feeding habits. |例句：Dermestes lardarius, commonly known as the larder beetle, is a notorious pest in homes and museums. 皮蠹属中的腊虫是一种臭名昭著的家居和博物馆害虫。|近义词：hide beetle, skin beetle, carpet beetle (depending on the specific species)|反义词：无|名词
