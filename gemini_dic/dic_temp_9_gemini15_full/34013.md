citizen:/ˈsɪtɪzən/| n. |公民，居民 |A citizen is a legally recognized member of a country or state, entitled to its protection and possessing certain rights and responsibilities.|例句：He is a citizen of the United States. 他是美国公民。|例句：She actively participates in the life of her community as a responsible citizen. 她作为一名负责任的公民积极参与社区生活。|近义词：inhabitant, resident, subject|反义词：alien, foreigner, non-resident|名词
