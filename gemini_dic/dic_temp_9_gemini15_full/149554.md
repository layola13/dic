pages:/ˈpeɪdʒɪz/| n.|页，页码|The plural form of "page," referring to single sheets of paper in a book, document, or website.|例句：This book has 300 pages. 这本书有300页。|例句：Turn to page 10. 翻到第10页。|近义词：sheets, folios|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are many pages in this document. = 这份文档有很多页。
These pages contain important information. = 这些页面包含重要信息。


一般过去时:
The printer printed 100 pages yesterday. = 打印机昨天打印了100页。
I read 20 pages last night. = 我昨晚读了20页。


现在完成时:

They had printed the pages before the deadline. = 他们在大限前已经打印了这些页面。
