The term also refers to the swirling, teardrop-shaped pattern itself, often found on textiles.|例句：Her dress featured a beautiful paisley print.  她的连衣裙印着美丽的佩斯利图案。|近义词：patterned, figured, ornate|反义词：plain, simple
