homologize:/həˈmɒlədʒaɪz/ | vt. | 使同源；视为同源 | To declare or treat (something) as homologous; to establish homology between.  The process of identifying homologous structures or genes in different organisms. |  Scientists homologized the genes found in both species. 科学家将在这两个物种中发现的基因视为同源基因。  The researchers attempted to homologize the different anatomical structures. 研究人员试图将不同的解剖结构视为同源结构。 | synonym: equate, correlate | antonym: differentiate, distinguish | 动词
