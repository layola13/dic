flouts:/flaʊts/| vt.|蔑视；违反；嘲笑|To treat with contempt; to disregard or violate (a rule, law, etc.).  The verb often implies a deliberate and open defiance.|例句：He flouted the rules of the game. 他蔑视比赛规则。

例句：She openly flouted convention by wearing jeans to the wedding. 她穿着牛仔裤去参加婚礼，公然违反了传统习俗。


近义词：defy, scorn, mock, disregard, ignore|反义词：obey, respect, conform to|动词
