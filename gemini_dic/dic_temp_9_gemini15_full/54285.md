disguises:/dɪsˈɡaɪzɪz/ | n. |伪装；假扮；掩饰 | The act of concealing one's true identity or nature; a disguise.  |例句：She used a clever disguise to sneak into the party. 她用巧妙的伪装偷偷溜进了派对。

例句：The thief's disguises were so convincing that no one suspected him.  小偷的伪装如此逼真，以至于没有人怀疑他。


近义词：camouflage, mask, concealment | 反义词：revelation, exposure | 词性：名词
