feeds:/fiːdz/ | /fiːds/ | n. v. |饲料；信息来源；供应；喂养 | To provide with food; supply information; to provide data to a system; to give nourishment to. |例句：The farmer feeds the chickens every morning.  = 农民每天早上喂鸡。
The news website feeds me with the latest updates. = 新闻网站为我提供最新消息。 |近义词：nourishes, supplies, provides, informs|反义词：starves, deprives


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The farmer feeds the chickens every morning. = 农民每天早上喂鸡。
She feeds her dog twice a day. = 她一天喂两次狗。

一般过去时:
He fed the birds yesterday. = 他昨天喂了鸟。
They fed the stray cats last night. = 他们昨晚喂了流浪猫。

现在完成时:
I have fed the dog already. = 我已经喂过狗了。
She has fed the baby three times today. = 她今天已经喂了婴儿三次。

过去完成时:
The birds had already been fed before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们已经被喂过了。
He had fed the animals before he left for work. = 他上班前已经喂了动物。

将来时:
I will feed the cat when I get home. = 我回家后会喂猫。
She will feed the fish tomorrow. = 她明天会喂鱼。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would feed the stray dogs. = 如果我有时间，我会喂流浪狗。
I wish I had fed the birds earlier. = 我真希望我早点喂了鸟。

被动语态:
The animals are fed twice a day. = 动物们一天喂两次。
The data is fed into the computer system. = 数据被输入到电脑系统中。

疑问句:
Do you feed your pets regularly? = 你定期喂养你的宠物吗？
Does she feed the fish every day? = 她每天都喂鱼吗？

否定句:
I didn't feed the dog this morning. = 我今天早上没有喂狗。
She doesn't feed her cat processed food. = 她不喂猫加工过的食物。
