outchattering: /ˌaʊtˈtʃætərɪŋ/ | v. |  喧宾夺主地谈论；比…说得更多；盖过…的谈话声 |  The act of chattering more loudly or continuously than someone else, thus overpowering their voice or contribution to a conversation.  | 例句：The children's outchattering drowned out the teacher's instructions. 孩子们的喧闹声盖过了老师的指示。


The incessant outchattering of the social media influencers made it hard to hear the expert's opinion. 社交媒体网红们喋喋不休的谈话声使得很难听到专家的意见。| 近义词：outtalk, overshadow, drown out | 反义词：listen attentively, be silent
