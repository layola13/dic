personalization:/ˌpɜːrsənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |个性化，定制，个人化 |The act of customizing something to meet the needs or preferences of a particular individual or group.  It involves tailoring products, services, or experiences to be relevant and appealing to specific users. |例句：The website offers personalization options to enhance user experience.  网站提供个性化选项以增强用户体验。

例句：Many online stores use personalization to recommend products based on past purchases. 许多在线商店使用个性化来根据过去的购买推荐产品。

近义词：customization, individualization, tailoring |反义词：standardization, generalization
