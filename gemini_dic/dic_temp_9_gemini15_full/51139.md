despoiling:/dɪˈspɔɪlɪŋ/| v.|掠夺；破坏；剥夺|The act of despoiling something involves taking away its value, beauty, or significance, often through violence, theft, or destruction.  It implies a deliberate and often forceful act of ruining or plundering.|例句：The invading army despoiled the city, leaving it in ruins.  侵略军洗劫了这座城市，把它夷为平地。
The careless logging operation despoiled the forest of its ancient trees.  粗心的伐木作业破坏了森林中古老的树木。|近义词：plundering, pillaging, ravaging, ruining, destroying|反义词：preserving, protecting, restoring, enhancing

过去完成时:
