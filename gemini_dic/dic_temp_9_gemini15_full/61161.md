elsewise:/ˈɛlswaɪz/ | adv. |否则；不然；其他方面 | Otherwise; in another way; in other respects.  The word implies a contrasting or alternative situation or condition. |例句：You must finish your work on time; elsewise, you'll be punished.  你必须按时完成工作；否则，你将受到惩罚。

例句：He's not interested in sports; elsewise, he would have joined the team. 他对运动不感兴趣；否则，他会加入球队的。
