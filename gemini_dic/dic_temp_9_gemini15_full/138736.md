numbfish:/ˈnʌmˌfɪʃ/| n. |河豚；(非正式)迟钝的人|A numbfish is a type of fish, often referring to a pufferfish, known for its ability to inflate its body as a defense mechanism.  The term can also be used informally to describe a person who is slow-witted or insensitive.|例句：The numbfish inflated itself when threatened.  河豚受到威胁时会膨胀自己。|例句：He's such a numbfish; he doesn't understand the joke. 他真是个迟钝的人；他不懂这个笑话。|近义词：pufferfish, blowfish (for the fish); dullard, dolt (for the person)|反义词：sharp, quick-witted (for the person)|名词
