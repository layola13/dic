equaled:/ˈiːkwəld/| v.|等于，相当于 |The sum of 2 and 2 equaled 4.  2加2等于4。|The two teams equaled each other in skill. 两队实力相当。|近义词：equalled, amounted to |反义词：differed from


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Two plus two equals four. = 二加二等于四。
The number of boys equals the number of girls. = 男孩的人数等于女孩的人数。


一般过去时:
The temperature equaled zero degrees Celsius. = 温度降到了零摄氏度。
His efforts equaled those of his brother. = 他的努力和他哥哥的一样多。


现在完成时:
The total cost has equaled our budget. = 总成本已经达到了我们的预算。
The achievements of this year have equaled those of the previous year. = 今年的成就与去年相当。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, the scores had equaled. = 到比赛结束时，比分相等。
The amount of rain this month had equaled the rainfall of the entire previous season. = 这个月的降雨量与之前整个季节的降雨量相等。


将来时:
The total cost will equal ten thousand dollars. = 总成本将达到一万美元。
By next year, our production will equal that of our competitors. = 到明年，我们的产量将与竞争对手相当。


虚拟语气:
If the two sides equaled in strength, the battle would have been a draw. = 如果双方实力相当，这场战斗将是平局。
I wish my income equaled my expenses. = 我希望我的收入等于我的支出。


被动语态:
The two amounts were equaled. = 这两笔金额是相等的。
His efforts were equaled by his colleagues' hard work. = 他的努力与同事的辛勤工作不相上下。


疑问句:
Does the number of men equal the number of women? = 男女人数相等吗？
Do your skills equal those required for the job? = 你的技能符合这份工作要求吗？


否定句:
The quality of the product did not equal the price. = 产品的质量与价格不相符。
His performance did not equal his potential. = 他的表现没有发挥出他的潜力。
