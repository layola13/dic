manages:/ˈmænɪdʒɪz/ | v. | 管理；经营；设法做到 | To control or be in charge of something, especially a business or organization; to succeed in doing something difficult. |例句：She manages a team of ten people. 她管理着一个十人团队。 |例句：He manages to finish the work on time. 他设法按时完成了工作。|近义词：administers, directs, controls, handles |反义词：fails, neglects


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manages the project effectively. = 他有效地管理着这个项目。
The company manages its resources wisely. = 这家公司明智地管理着它的资源。


一般过去时:
She managed the store for five years. = 她经营这家商店五年了。
They managed to escape the fire. = 他们设法逃离了火灾。


现在完成时:
He has managed the team since 2010. = 自2010年以来，他一直管理着这个团队。
She has managed to overcome many challenges. = 她已经克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
They had managed to complete the report before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前设法完成了报告。
He had managed the company for ten years before retiring. = 他退休前经营这家公司十年了。


将来时:
She will manage the new branch next year. = 明年她将管理新分公司。
They will manage to find a solution. = 他们将设法找到一个解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If she managed the project, it would be successful. = 如果她管理这个项目，它将会成功。
I wish I managed my time better. = 我希望我更好地管理我的时间。


被动语态:
The company is managed by a skilled CEO. = 这家公司由一位熟练的首席执行官管理。
The project was managed efficiently. = 项目管理效率很高。


疑问句:
Does he manage the department? = 他管理这个部门吗？
How does she manage to do everything? = 她是怎么做到一切的？


否定句:
He doesn't manage his time well. = 他没有很好地管理他的时间。
They didn't manage to reach the summit. = 他们没有成功到达山顶。


PHRASE->
manage to do something = 设法做某事
manage without something = 没有…也能应付
manage one's time = 管理时间
