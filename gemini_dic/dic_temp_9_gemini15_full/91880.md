hyperintelligent: /ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt/ | adj. | 超级智能的，极度聪明的 | Extremely intelligent; possessing intelligence far exceeding the norm.  This term often refers to hypothetical artificial intelligence or future human evolution surpassing human-level intelligence significantly. | Example:  The hyperintelligent AI quickly solved the problem that had stumped human experts for years.  = 超级智能的AI迅速解决了困扰人类专家多年的问题。 The scientists speculated about the potential dangers of creating hyperintelligent machines. = 科学家们推测创造超级智能机器的潜在危险。 | 近义词：superintelligent, exceptionally intelligent, highly intelligent | 反义词：unintelligent, dull, stupid |  词性：形容词
