lastingness:/ˈlæstɪŋnəs/| n. |持久性，耐久性，持续时间|The lastingness of the paint is impressive.  这油漆的持久性令人印象深刻。|近义词：permanence, durability, endurance|反义词：transitoriness, ephemerality


英文解析：
Lastingness refers to the quality or state of lasting; the length of time something continues to exist or remain effective.  It emphasizes the enduring nature of something, its ability to withstand the passage of time.


例句：

The lastingness of their friendship was a testament to their shared values.  他们友谊的持久性证明了他们共同的价值观。

The company prides itself on the lastingness of its products. 这家公司以其产品的持久性而自豪。


近义词：

permanence:  the state or quality of lasting or remaining unchanged. 持久性，永恒性
durability: the ability to withstand wear, pressure, or damage. 耐久性，耐用性
endurance: the ability to withstand hardship or persevere. 忍耐力，持久力


反义词：

transitoriness: the state or quality of being transient; fleeting or short-lived. 短暂性，转瞬即逝
ephemerality: the state or quality of being short-lived or temporary. 短暂性，短暂


词性：名词 (noun)
