koeksister:/ˈkukˌsɪstər/| n.|油炸麻花(南非的一种甜点)|A koeksister is a traditional South African dessert made from fried dough, often soaked in syrup or honey.  It's a popular treat, particularly in the Western Cape. |Example:  My grandmother's koeksisters are the best I've ever tasted. 我祖母做的油炸麻花是我吃过的最好吃的。  I bought a box of koeksisters from the market. 我从市场上买了一盒油炸麻花。|近义词：fritter, cruller (though these are not exact equivalents)|反义词：无|名词
