例句：Her daffier ideas were often the most creative. 她那些更古怪的想法往往是最有创意的。


近义词：sillier, crazier, more eccentric, more foolish

反义词：sane, sensible, rational, reasonable


词源：源自daffy，其词源尚不明确，但可能是拟声词或来自方言。
