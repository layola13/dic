kidnap:/ˈkɪdnæp/| vt.|绑架，拐骗|To take someone away illegally by force, typically to demand a ransom.  |例句：The child was kidnapped from his school.  孩子从学校被绑架了。|例句：The police are investigating the kidnapping. 警方正在调查这起绑架案。|近义词：abduct, seize, carry off|反义词：release, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They kidnap children for ransom. = 他们绑架儿童勒索赎金。
The gang kidnaps people frequently. = 这个团伙经常绑架人。


一般过去时:
The police rescued the girl who had been kidnapped. = 警方救出了被绑架的女孩。
He was kidnapped last year. = 他去年被绑架了。


现在完成时:
They have kidnapped three victims this month. = 这个月他们已经绑架了三个受害者。
No one has been kidnapped in this area for years. = 多年来，这个地区没有人被绑架。


过去完成时:
The police discovered that the suspect had kidnapped several people before. = 警方发现嫌疑人之前绑架过几个人。
They realized that he had been kidnapped earlier that day. = 他们意识到他当天早些时候就被绑架了。


将来时:
The police believe that the suspect will kidnap again. = 警方认为嫌疑人还会再次绑架。
They fear that more people will be kidnapped in the near future. = 他们担心不久的将来会有更多人被绑架。


虚拟语气:
If they had not kidnapped him, he would be alive now. = 如果他们没有绑架他，他现在还活着。
I wish they had not kidnapped her. = 我真希望他们没有绑架她。


被动语态:
The victim was kidnapped last night. = 受害者昨晚被绑架了。
Several people have been kidnapped recently. = 最近有几个人被绑架了。


疑问句:
Was he kidnapped? = 他被绑架了吗？
Who was kidnapped? = 谁被绑架了？


否定句:
They did not kidnap anyone. = 他们没有绑架任何人。
The police said he was not kidnapped. = 警方说他没有被绑架。
