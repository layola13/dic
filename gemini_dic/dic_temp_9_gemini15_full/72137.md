floodlight:/ˈflʌdlaɪt/| n. |泛光灯；强光灯 |A strong light used to illuminate a large area.  Floodlights are often used in stadiums, airports, and construction sites. |例句：The stadium was brilliantly lit by powerful floodlights. 体育场被强大的泛光灯照得通亮。|例句：The floodlights illuminated the stage, making the performance more visible. 泛光灯照亮了舞台，使演出更清晰可见。|近义词：spotlight, searchlight |反义词：dim light, darkness
