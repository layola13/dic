entrees:/ˈɑːntreɪz/| n.|主菜，开胃菜|Main courses of a meal, often served after an appetizer.  The term can also refer to appetizers in some contexts, depending on regional usage and the specific menu.|例句：The entrees on the menu tonight include steak, salmon, and chicken. 今晚菜单上的主菜包括牛排、鲑鱼和鸡肉。

The restaurant offers a wide variety of delicious entrees. 这家餐厅提供各种美味的主菜。|近义词：main courses, dishes, courses|反义词：appetizers (in some contexts), desserts|词性:名词
