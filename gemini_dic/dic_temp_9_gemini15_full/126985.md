nary:/ˈnɛəri/| adj.|一个也没有，毫无|Not one; not a single one.  It is used to emphasize the complete absence of something.|例句：There wasn't nary a cloud in the sky. 天空中一丝云彩也没有。

例句：I saw nary a soul on the deserted beach.  我在荒凉的海滩上一个人也没看到。


近义词：no, not a single, not one, none

反义词：many, several, numerous

词性：形容词
