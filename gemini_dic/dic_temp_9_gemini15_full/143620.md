outjet:/aʊtˈdʒɛt/ | vt. |喷射而出，超过；胜过 | To surpass or exceed, especially in speed or power; to eject forcefully. |例句：The rocket outjetted the other vehicles in the race. 火箭在比赛中超过了其他车辆。|The volcano outjetted molten rock and ash. 火山喷射出熔岩和火山灰。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outrun |反义词：underperform, lag behind
