egalitarianism:/ɪˌɡælɪˈteəriənɪzəm/| n. |平等主义；平均主义|The belief in human equality especially with respect to social, political, and economic rights and opportunities.  It emphasizes the importance of equal rights and opportunities for all members of society, regardless of their background or social standing.  |例句：The movement championed egalitarianism and social justice.  这个运动拥护平等主义和社会正义。|例句：Many societies strive for egalitarianism, but true equality remains a distant goal. 许多社会都在争取平等主义，但真正的平等仍然是一个遥远的目标。|近义词：equalitarianism, equity, fairness |反义词：inequality, elitism, hierarchy
