ensear:/ɛnˈsɪər/| vt.| 烧焦；使焦黑；使麻木；使枯萎|To burn the surface of something; to scorch or sear.  The verb often implies a sudden, intense burning, leaving a charred or blackened surface. It can also be used figuratively to describe the numbing or withering effect of a strong emotion or experience.|例句：The intense heat enseared the grass.  烈火把草烧焦了。

The wildfire enseared the forest.  野火烧焦了森林。


近义词：scorch, sear, char, burn|反义词：soothe, heal, refresh|动词
