outproducing: /ˌaʊtprəˈduːsɪŋ/ | v. |超过产量，产量超过 |  Producing more than; surpassing in output.  |例句：This factory is outproducing all others in the region.这家工厂的产量超过该地区所有其他工厂。 |例句：Our team outproduced the competition, leading to a significant market share increase. 我们的团队产量超过竞争对手，导致市场份额大幅增加。|近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outperforming | 反义词：underproducing, lagging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory outproduces its competitors. = 这家工厂的产量超过竞争对手。
Our farm outproduces all the others in the area. = 我们的农场产量超过该地区所有其他农场。

一般过去时:
Last year, the company outproduced its rivals. = 去年，这家公司的产量超过了竞争对手。
The team outproduced their expectations. = 这个团队的产量超过了他们的预期。

现在完成时:
The new machine has outproduced the old one significantly. = 新机器的产量已显著超过旧机器。
They have outproduced their quota for the month. = 他们本月的产量已超过配额。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the factory had outproduced its target. = 到年底，这家工厂的产量已超过目标。
Before the upgrade, the system had outproduced its predecessor. = 在升级之前，该系统已经超过了其前身。

将来时:
We expect to outproduce our competitors next quarter. = 我们预计下个季度将超过我们的竞争对手。
With the new technology, we will outproduce the current market leader. = 使用新技术，我们将超过目前的市场领导者。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we would have outproduced them. = 如果我们投资更多，我们本来可以超过他们。
I wish we could outproduce our rivals so easily. = 我希望我们能如此轻松地超过竞争对手。

被动语态:
The small company was outproduced by the larger corporation. = 这家小公司被更大的公司在产量上超过。
Their efforts were outproduced by unexpected circumstances. = 他们的努力被意外情况超过了。

疑问句:
Is this factory outproducing the others? = 这家工厂的产量超过其他工厂吗？
Are we outproducing our targets this month? = 我们这个月的产量超过目标了吗？

否定句:
The farm didn't outproduce its neighbor's this year. = 这个农场今年没有超过邻居的产量。
We failed to outproduce our competitors in the last quarter. = 我们在上个季度未能超过竞争对手。
