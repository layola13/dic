prosiphon: /ˈproʊsaɪfən/ | n. |虹吸管，虹吸器| A siphon is a tube used to convey liquid upward from a reservoir and then downward to a lower level of its own accord, by maintaining a pressure difference between the two ends.  It works based on the principle of atmospheric pressure and gravity. |Example: The gardener used a prosiphon to empty the pond.  园丁用虹吸管清空了池塘。  The chemical prosiphon transferred the liquid safely. 化学虹吸管安全地转移了液体。|近义词：syphon, tube |反义词：pump (in the context of liquid transfer)|名词
