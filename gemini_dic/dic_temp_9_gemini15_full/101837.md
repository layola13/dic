irruption:/ɪˈrʌpʃən/| n. |突然爆发；闯入；涌入 |The sudden and violent entry of a large number of people or things.  A sudden, forceful outbreak or intrusion.|The irruption of the barbarians into the Roman Empire led to its decline.  蛮族突然入侵罗马帝国导致了它的衰落。The irruption of laughter broke the tense silence.  一阵笑声打破了紧张的沉默。|incursion, invasion, outbreak, eruption|withdrawal, retreat, calm, peace|名词
