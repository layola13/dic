enormousnesses:/ɪˈnɔːrməsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 巨大的集合；庞大之物 | The enormousnesses of the universe are difficult to comprehend.宇宙的浩瀚难以理解。 | 近义词：immensities, vastnesses | 反义词：smallnesses, littlenesses


词源:

* **enormous:**  源自法语 *énorme*,  最终源自拉丁语 *enormem* (巨大的，异常的)，由 *e-* (加强前缀) + *normis* (规则，标准) 构成。表示超出正常标准的巨大。

* **-ness:**  这是一个英语后缀，表示“性质”、“状态”或“程度”。加在形容词后构成名词。


英文解析:

`Enormousnesses` is the plural form of the noun `enormousness`. It refers to the multiple instances or aspects of something being extremely large, immense, or vast.  It's a relatively uncommon word, often used to emphasize the multitude of huge things or qualities.


例句:

The enormousnesses of the project's scope and budget were only revealed later.  项目的规模和预算的巨大程度直到后来才被揭示。

The sheer enormousnesses of the data collected overwhelmed the researchers. 收集到的海量数据让研究人员难以招架。
