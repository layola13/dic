dinettes:/daɪˈnet/| n. |小餐厅，小型餐厅；（通常指带餐桌椅的）小吃饭区|A dinette is a small dining area, often including a table and chairs, typically found in apartments or smaller homes.  It's a less formal and usually smaller version of a dining room.|例句：The apartment had a charming dinette overlooking the garden. 这套公寓有一个迷人的小餐厅，可以俯瞰花园。|例句：They enjoyed their breakfast in the cozy dinette. 他们在舒适的小餐厅享用早餐。|近义词：breakfast nook, kitchenette, eating area |反义词：dining hall, grand dining room
