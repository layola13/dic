require:/rɪˈkwaɪr/ | vt. | 需要，要求 | to need something; to make something necessary | The job requires a high level of skill. 这份工作需要很高的技能水平。  The law requires that all drivers wear seat belts. 法律规定所有司机都必须系安全带。| need, necessitate, demand |  give, offer | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He requires a lot of attention. 他需要很多关注。
The project requires careful planning. 这个项目需要仔细的计划。

一般过去时:
She required medical attention after the accident. 事故后她需要医疗护理。
The old house required extensive repairs. 这栋旧房子需要大规模的修理。

现在完成时:
The situation has required a change in our strategy.  情况需要我们改变策略。
The project has required more time than we anticipated.  这个项目比我们预期的花费了更多的时间。

过去完成时:
They had required a deposit before starting the work. 他们在开始工作之前要求支付押金。
It had required considerable effort to reach the summit.  到达山顶需要相当大的努力。

将来时:
The new regulations will require businesses to adapt. 新规将要求企业适应。
The trip will require a lot of preparation. 这趟旅行需要大量的准备。

虚拟语气:
It would require a miracle for us to win. 我们要赢需要奇迹。
If they required assistance, they should have asked. 如果他们需要帮助，他们应该提出要求。

被动语态:
A high level of accuracy is required.  需要高精度。
Further investigation is required to determine the cause. 需要进一步调查以确定原因。

疑问句:
Does the job require any special skills? 这份工作需要什么特殊技能吗？
What documents are required for this application?  这个申请需要什么文件？

否定句:
The task doesn't require any special tools. 这项任务不需要任何特殊的工具。
The project doesn't require your immediate attention.  这个项目不需要你立即关注。
