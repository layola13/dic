self-aggrandizements:/ˌsɛlf əˈɡrændɪzmənts/| n. |自夸；自我吹嘘；自我抬高|The act of exaggerating one's own importance or achievements.  It often involves boasting or self-promotion, sometimes to an excessive or inappropriate degree. |例句：His constant self-aggrandizements made him unpopular with his colleagues. 他不断自吹自擂，使他在同事中不受欢迎。

例句：Her self-aggrandizements in the interview backfired, making her appear arrogant and insincere.  她在面试中自我吹嘘适得其反，让她显得傲慢和虚伪。


近义词：self-promotion, boasting, vainglory, conceit, egotism


反义词：humility, modesty, self-deprecation


词性：名词
