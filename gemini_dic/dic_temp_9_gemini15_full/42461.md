counterfactual:/ˌkaʊntərˈfæktʃuəl/| adj. n.|反事实的；与事实相反的；假设的|Relating to or denoting a statement that describes a situation that is contrary to fact.  It often involves imagining what might have happened if something different had occurred. |例句：The counterfactual argument suggests that if he had studied harder, he would have passed the exam.  反事实论证表明，如果他学习更努力，他就能通过考试。

例句：Historians often explore counterfactual scenarios to better understand the past. 历史学家经常探索反事实情景以更好地理解过去。


近义词：hypothetical, speculative, imaginary, unreal|反义词：factual, real, actual|词性：adj. n.
