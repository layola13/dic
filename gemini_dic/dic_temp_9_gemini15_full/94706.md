impromptu:/ɪmˈprɑːmptjuː/| adj.|即兴的，无预备的 |  Done without being planned, organized, or rehearsed.  An impromptu speech is one given without preparation. |例句：He gave an impromptu speech at the wedding. 他在婚礼上即兴发表了演讲。
例句：The band played an impromptu concert in the park. 乐队在公园里举行了一场即兴音乐会。|近义词：improvised, spontaneous, extempore|反义词：rehearsed, planned, prepared|
