jink:/dʒɪŋk/| v.|突然转向；闪躲；欺骗|To move or turn suddenly and quickly to avoid something.|The driver jinked the car to avoid a collision.  司机突然转动方向盘以避免碰撞。|The rabbit jinked away from the fox. 兔子迅速躲开狐狸。|dodge, evade, swerve|straighten, continue|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often jinks to avoid obstacles. = 他经常闪躲以避开障碍物。
She jinks through the crowded market. = 她灵活地穿梭于拥挤的市场。

一般过去时:
The cyclist jinked sharply to avoid the pothole. = 骑自行车的人猛地一拐躲开了那个坑。
He jinked his head to avoid the blow. = 他猛地一歪头躲开了那一击。

现在完成时:
The football player has jinked past several defenders. = 足球运动员已经躲过了好几个防守队员。
She has jinked her way to the top of her profession. = 她凭借自己的灵活手段达到了职业顶峰。

过去完成时:
He had jinked to the left before the car hit him. = 汽车撞到他之前，他已经向左躲开了。
The runner had jinked past her opponent before crossing the finish line.  = 跑步者在冲过终点线之前已经躲过了她的对手。

将来时:
The driver will jink to avoid the oncoming traffic. = 司机将要突然转向以避开迎面而来的车辆。
She will jink and weave through the crowd. = 她将灵活地在人群中穿梭。

虚拟语气:
If he had jinked quicker, he would have avoided the accident. = 如果他躲得更快些，他就能避免事故了。
If she had jinked to the right, she might have won the race. = 如果她向右躲一下，她也许就能赢得比赛了。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The ball was jinked around the defender. = 球被巧妙地绕过了防守队员。(较少见)
The path was jinked to avoid the swamp. = 小路拐弯抹角地绕开了沼泽。(较少见)

疑问句:
Did he jink to avoid the punch? = 他躲开那一拳了吗？
Will she jink to the left or right? = 她会向左躲还是向右躲？

否定句:
He didn't jink in time to avoid the collision. = 他没有及时躲开以避免碰撞。
She didn't jink; she ran straight into the wall. = 她没有躲闪；她直接撞到了墙上。
