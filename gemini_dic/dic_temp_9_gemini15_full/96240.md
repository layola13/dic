indicate:/ˈɪndɪkeɪt/| vt.|指示；表明；象征；暗示|to show, point out, or make clear|The arrow indicates the direction to the beach.  箭头指示通往海滩的方向。|The study indicates a link between smoking and lung cancer.  这项研究表明吸烟与肺癌之间存在联系。|suggest, point to, signify|conceal, hide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He indicates his agreement by nodding. = 他点头表示同意。
The map indicates the location of the treasure. = 地图标示着宝藏的位置。

一般过去时:
She indicated that she was tired. = 她表示她累了。
The doctor indicated a problem with my heart. = 医生指出我的心脏有问题。

现在完成时:
All the evidence indicates that he is guilty. = 所有证据都表明他有罪。
Studies have indicated a rise in sea levels. = 研究表明海平面正在上升。

过去完成时:
The clues indicated that the thief had escaped through the window. = 线索表明小偷是从窗户逃走的。
Previous research had indicated a potential cure. = 先前的研究已经表明了一种潜在的疗法。

将来时:
The forecast indicates that it will rain tomorrow. = 预报显示明天将会下雨。
The signs indicate that the economy will improve next year. = 迹象表明经济明年将会好转。

虚拟语气:
If the symptoms indicate a serious problem, you should see a doctor immediately. = 如果症状表明存在严重问题，你应该立即去看医生。
The data indicates that, if we continue at this rate, we will be in trouble. = 数据表明，如果我们继续以这种速度发展，我们将陷入困境。

被动语态:
The route is indicated on the map. = 路线在地图上标示出来了。
His guilt was clearly indicated by the evidence. = 证据清楚地表明了他的罪行。

疑问句:
Does this symbol indicate danger? = 这个符号表示危险吗？
What does the blinking light indicate? = 闪烁的灯光表示什么？

否定句:
The research does not indicate any significant changes. = 研究没有显示任何重大变化。
The sign didn't indicate any parking restrictions. = 标志没有指示任何停车限制。


PHRASE->
