microphotometry:/ˌmaɪkroʊfoʊˈtɒmətri/ | n. |显微测光法 | The measurement of the light absorption or transmission of microscopic objects or areas.  This technique is used extensively in various fields, such as biology, materials science, and pathology, to quantitatively analyze the properties of small samples. | Example: Microphotometry was used to determine the density of cells in the tissue sample. 显微测光法被用来测定组织样本中细胞的密度。 | 近义词: microdensitometry | 反义词: N/A
