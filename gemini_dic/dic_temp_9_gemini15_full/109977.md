libertarians:/ˌlɪbərˈteəriənz/| n. |自由主义者|Supporters of libertarianism, a political philosophy that emphasizes individual rights, limited government, and free markets.  Libertarians advocate for maximizing individual autonomy and minimizing state intervention.|Many libertarians oppose government regulations on businesses and personal lives.  许多自由主义者反对政府对企业和个人生活的监管。|  Conservatives, anarchists |Authoritarians, socialists|名词
