glow-worm: /ˈɡloʊwˌwɜːrm/ | n. |萤火虫|A small, nocturnal insect that produces light.  Glow-worms are actually the larvae or females of certain beetle species; the males usually have wings and are less luminous.|例句：We saw several glow-worms twinkling in the grass. 我们看到几只萤火虫在草丛中闪烁。|近义词：firefly|反义词：无
