revolved:/rɪˈvɒlvd/ | /rɪˈvɑːlvd/| v. |旋转；围绕；围绕…旋转；以…为中心；仔细考虑|To move in a circle around a central point.  To think deeply and carefully about something.|The Earth revolves around the sun. 地球绕着太阳转。|The discussion revolved around the budget.  讨论围绕预算展开。|近义词：rotate, circle, orbit, ponder, contemplate |反义词：remain stationary

The planets revolve around their stars. = 行星围绕着它们的恒星旋转。
