flutters:/ˈflʌtərz/| v. n.|轻微颤动；激动；飘动|To move lightly and quickly; to flap wings rapidly; to feel a slight trembling, often from excitement or nervousness.|例句：The butterfly fluttered its wings. 蝴蝶拍打着翅膀。
The bird fluttered down from the tree.  鸟儿从树上轻飘飘地飞下来。

近义词：tremble, quiver, vibrate, flap|反义词：remain still, stay steady
