lished:/ˈlɪʃt/ | adj. | 已发表的；已出版的；已公布的 | Published works are those that have been officially released to the public.  已发表的作品是那些已正式向公众发布的作品。|例句：His new novel is published next month.他的新小说下个月出版。 |  例句：The research findings were published in a leading scientific journal. 这项研究结果发表在一份领先的科学期刊上。|近义词：released, issued, printed | 反义词：unpublished, unprinted


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The newspaper publishes articles daily. = 报纸每天出版文章。
She publishes her blog posts weekly. = 她每周发表她的博客文章。


一般过去时:
They published their findings last year. = 他们去年发表了他们的研究结果。
The company published a new report on market trends. = 公司发布了一份关于市场趋势的新报告。


现在完成时:
He has published several books on history. = 他已经出版了几本关于历史的书。
The author has published numerous articles in academic journals. = 这名作者在学术期刊上发表了大量的文章。


过去完成时:
Before the conference, she had published three papers on the topic. = 在会议之前，她已经发表了三篇关于这个主题的论文。
They had published the results before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前公布了结果。


将来时:
They will publish the book next spring. = 他们将在明年春天出版这本书。
The journal will publish his article in the next issue. = 该期刊将在下一期发表他的文章。


虚拟语气:
If he published his findings, he would become famous. = 如果他发表了他的研究结果，他就会成名。
I wish I had published my work sooner. = 我希望我早点发表我的作品。


被动语态:
The article was published in the New York Times. = 这篇文章发表在《纽约时报》上。
The book is being published by a major publishing house. = 这本书由一家主要出版社出版。


疑问句:
Has the journal published your research yet? = 该期刊是否已经发表你的研究？
When will the results be published? = 结果什么时候公布？


否定句:
The article was not published due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，这篇文章没有发表。
He hasn't published anything in years. = 他多年来什么都没发表。


PHRASE->

publish a book = 出版一本书
publish an article = 发表文章
publish a paper = 发表论文
publish online = 在线发布
publish findings = 公布研究结果
