interpenetrant: /ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪnt/ | adj. |相互渗透的，互相贯穿的 |  Describes two or more things that penetrate or pass into each other.  It implies a mutual interaction and intermingling, rather than simply one thing passing through another. |

例句：

The two polymer networks formed an interpenetrant polymer network (IPN). = 两种聚合物网络形成了互穿网络（IPN）。

The design features interpenetrant layers of different materials for improved strength. = 该设计具有相互渗透的不同材料层，以提高强度。


近义词：intermingled, interwoven, intertwined, permeating

反义词：separate, distinct, unconnected, disjoint


词性：形容词
