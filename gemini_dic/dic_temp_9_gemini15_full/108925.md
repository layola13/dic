leecher:/ˈliːtʃər/| n. |吸食者；寄生虫；盗取者|A leecher is someone who takes something (especially something valuable or needed by others) without giving anything in return, often excessively or unfairly.  They are often associated with parasitic behavior, exploiting others for personal gain.  This can apply to various contexts, including downloading copyrighted material illegally, taking advantage of others' generosity without reciprocation, or exploiting resources without contributing. |例句：He's a leech, always borrowing money and never paying it back. 他是个寄生虫，总是借钱却不还。

He's become a leecher on the company's resources, never contributing to the team's success. 他已经变成了公司资源的寄生虫，从未为团队的成功做出贡献。|近义词：parasite, freeloader, moocher, sponge |反义词：contributor, giver, benefactor
