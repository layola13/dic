maternalisms:/məˈtɜːnəlɪzəm/| n. |母性主义；母爱至上；母权主义|The belief in the inherent superiority of maternal qualities and roles, often associated with traditional gender roles and the prioritization of motherhood above other aspects of life.  It can also refer to policies and practices that favor mothers and motherhood. | Example:  The politician's speech was criticized for its overt maternalisms, suggesting women should prioritize family over career.  政治家的讲话因其明显的母性主义言论而受到批评，暗示女性应该将家庭置于事业之上。|近义词：maternalism, motherism|反义词：patriarchy, feminism
