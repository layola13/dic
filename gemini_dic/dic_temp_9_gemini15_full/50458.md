depredation:/ˌdɛprɪˈdeɪʃən/| n. |掠夺；破坏；损害 | The act of preying on or plundering; the act of destroying or damaging something.  It often implies a significant loss or damage, often involving a violation or abuse. |例句：The depredations of the invading army left the city in ruins.  入侵军队的掠夺使这座城市沦为废墟。

例句：Years of overfishing have led to the depredation of fish stocks. 多年的过度捕捞导致鱼类资源枯竭。


近义词：plundering, pillaging, ravaging, devastation, destruction | 反义词：preservation, conservation, protection, restoration | 词性:名词
