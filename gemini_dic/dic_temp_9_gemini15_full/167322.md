pretranslated: /ˌpriːˈtrænzleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先翻译的 | Already translated before being used or published.  |  例句：The pretranslated subtitles made the foreign film accessible to a wider audience. 预先翻译的字幕使这部外国电影能够被更广泛的观众所接受。 | 近义词：translated beforehand, beforehand translated | 反义词：untranslated, original
