lepisma:/ˈlɛpɪzmə/| n. |衣鱼；蠹鱼|A small, wingless insect, typically silvery-white, that feeds on paper, starch, and other carbohydrates.  They are often found in houses and libraries.|例句：The lepisma was discovered in the old library, feeding on the ancient books.  这只衣鱼被发现藏身于古老图书馆中，正在啃食古籍。|例句： We found several lepisma in the pantry, attracted by the spilled sugar. 我们在储藏室发现了数只衣鱼，它们是被洒在地上的糖吸引来的。|近义词：silverfish |反义词:  (无明确反义词)|名词

词源:
The word "lepisma" is derived from the Greek words "lepis" (λεπίς), meaning "scale," and "isma" (ἴσμα),  a suffix indicating a diminutive or small thing.  Therefore, "lepisma" literally means "small scaled one," referring to the insect's scaled body.
