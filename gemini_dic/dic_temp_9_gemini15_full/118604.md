message:/ˈmɛsɪdʒ/ | n. |信息，消息，音讯 |A message is a piece of information that is sent from one person or thing to another.  It can be written, spoken, or even implied through actions. |例句：I left a message on his answering machine. 我给他留言了。|例句：The message was clear: we need to work harder. 信息很明确：我们需要更努力地工作。|近义词：communication, note, information |反义词：silence
