nomadian:/noʊˈmædɪk/ | adj. | 游牧的；流浪的；漂泊的 | Relating to nomads or their way of life; characterized by moving from place to place in search of food, water, or pasture.  | Example:  The nomadic tribes migrated seasonally with their herds.  游牧部落随着它们的兽群季节性地迁徙。  |近义词：wandering, itinerant, roving, migratory | 反义词：settled, sedentary
