multiplexing:/ˈmʌltiplɛksɪŋ/| n. |多路复用|The process of combining multiple signals into one signal for transmission over a single channel, and then separating them at the receiving end.  This technique is used to efficiently utilize communication resources.  For example, in telecommunications, multiplexing allows many phone calls to be transmitted simultaneously over a single cable.|Example:  Time-division multiplexing (TDM) is a common technique used in digital communication systems.|近义词：multiplexing,  signal combining|反义词：demultiplexing
