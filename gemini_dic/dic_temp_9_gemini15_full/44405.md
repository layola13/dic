cross-kick:/krɔːs kɪk/| n. vt.|交叉踢腿；交叉腿法|A cross-kick is a martial arts technique where one leg is crossed over the other before delivering a kick.  It's often used to create a wider range of motion or to surprise an opponent.|例句：He executed a perfect cross-kick, knocking his opponent off balance. 他完美的执行了一个交叉踢腿，击倒了他的对手。

The cross-kick is a powerful technique in Taekwondo.  交叉踢腿是跆拳道中的一种强力技术。|近义词：spinning kick, roundhouse kick|反义词：straight kick
