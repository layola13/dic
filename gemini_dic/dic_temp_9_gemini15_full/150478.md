pantile:/ˈpæntaɪl/| n.|平瓦，筒瓦|A pantile is a type of roofing tile that is curved and S-shaped, overlapping to create a waterproof covering.  They are commonly used in Europe and are known for their distinctive appearance.|The old farmhouse had a roof covered in beautiful, weathered pantiles.|近义词：roof tile, tile|反义词：无
