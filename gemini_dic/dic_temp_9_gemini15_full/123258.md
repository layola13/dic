monodelphous:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfəs/| adj.|单体雄蕊的|Having stamens united into a single bundle or tube.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe the arrangement of stamens (male reproductive organs) in a flower.  The stamens are fused together, forming a single structure. | Example: The flower exhibits a monodelphous condition, with all its stamens joined into a single column.  这朵花呈现单体雄蕊的状态，所有雄蕊都联合成单一的柱状结构。|近义词：monadelphous (same meaning)|反义词：diadelphous, polyadelphous (stamens united into multiple bundles)
