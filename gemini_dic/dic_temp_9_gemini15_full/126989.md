nasalise:/ˈneɪzəlaɪz/ | vt. | 鼻化；使带鼻音 | To pronounce (a vowel or syllable) with nasal resonance, typically by lowering the velum.  | Example:  The singer nasalises the vowels in the final line. 歌手在最后一行中对元音进行了鼻化处理。  |近义词: nasalize,  vocalize with nasal resonance | 反义词: denasalize
