scandal:/ˈskændəl/| n. |丑闻，诽谤 |A scandal is an action or event that causes public outrage because it is considered morally wrong or shocking.  It often involves wrongdoing by people in positions of power or authority. |The Watergate scandal led to President Nixon's resignation.  水门事件导致尼克松总统辞职。|controversy, disgrace, outrage |praise, honor|名词
