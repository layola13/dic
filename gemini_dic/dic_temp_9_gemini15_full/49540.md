demasculinization: /ˌdiːmæskjʊlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去男性化；女性化 | The process of reducing or eliminating masculine characteristics.  This can refer to physical changes, behavioral changes, or social changes that lessen the expression of traditionally masculine traits.  The term is often used in discussions of gender identity, hormone therapy, and societal expectations. | Example: The demasculinization of certain professions, once considered exclusively male domains, is a sign of changing social norms.  去男性化某些职业，曾经被认为是男性独有的领域，是社会规范变化的标志。 | 近义词：feminization, effeminization | 反义词：masculinization
