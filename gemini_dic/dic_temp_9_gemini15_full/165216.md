/ˌpriːɪŋkwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 预审，预先审问 |  A preinquisition is a preliminary inquiry or investigation, often conducted before a more formal or extensive investigation. It aims to gather initial information and determine the need for further action.  The term often carries connotations of suspicion and potential legal ramifications. | The police conducted a preinquisition before issuing a warrant.  警方在签发搜查令之前进行了预审。 | preliminary investigation, initial inquiry, preliminary hearing |  final judgment, trial |  名词
