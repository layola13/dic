nonpsychoanalytic: /ˌnɒnˌsaɪkoʊæˈnælɪtɪk/ | adj. |非精神分析的| Not related to or based on the theories of psychoanalysis.  This adjective describes approaches, methods, or theories that do not originate from or align with the principles of psychoanalysis. | Example:  The therapist employed a nonpsychoanalytic approach to treating anxiety, focusing on cognitive behavioral techniques.  治疗师采用非精神分析的方法治疗焦虑，重点关注认知行为技术。|近义词: non-Freudian, behavioral, cognitive |反义词: psychoanalytic
