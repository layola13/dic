masterworks:/ˈmɑːstərwɜːrks/ | n. |杰作，名作 |Excellent works of art, music, or literature.  Masterworks are typically considered to be of exceptional quality and lasting significance. |例句：The museum boasts a collection of Renaissance masterworks.  这个博物馆拥有文艺复兴时期杰作的收藏。

例句：Shakespeare's plays are considered masterworks of English literature.  莎士比亚的戏剧被认为是英国文学的杰作。


近义词： masterpieces, classics, triumphs


反义词：trash, junk, dreck


词性: 名词
