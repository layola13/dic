making-for:/ˈmeɪkɪŋ fɔːr/| v. |导致；促成；造成|  This phrase describes an action or situation that leads to a particular result or consequence. It emphasizes the process of leading to something.  |The warm weather is making for a pleasant afternoon.  温暖的天气使得下午很惬意。|The lack of planning is making for a difficult situation.  缺乏计划导致了一种困境。|contributing to, leading to, resulting in| hindering, obstructing, preventing|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The current economic climate is making for increased uncertainty. = 目前的经济环境正在导致更大的不确定性。
This new software is making for a more efficient workflow. = 这款新软件正在促成更高效的工作流程。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain made for difficult driving conditions. = 大雨导致了艰难的驾驶条件。
His hard work made for a successful project. = 他的努力工作促成了一个成功的项目。


现在完成时:
The recent policy changes have made for a more competitive market. = 最近的政策变化已经导致了一个更具竞争力的市场。
Years of neglect have made for a dilapidated building. = 多年的忽视导致了建筑物破败不堪。


过去完成时:
The prior disagreements had made for a tense atmosphere at the meeting. = 先前的分歧导致了会议上紧张的气氛。
Her previous experience had made for a smooth transition into the new role. = 她之前的经验为她顺利过渡到新的角色创造了条件。


将来时:
The upcoming storm will make for challenging travel conditions. = 即将来临的风暴将导致出行困难。
Increased automation will make for higher productivity. = 自动化的增加将导致更高的生产率。


虚拟语气:
If we had planned better, it would have made for a smoother event. = 如果我们计划得更好，活动就会进行得更顺利。
If the weather had been better, it would have made for a more enjoyable picnic. = 如果天气更好，野餐就会更愉快。


被动语态:
A more efficient system is being made for by the new software. = 新软件正在创造一个更高效的系统。(被动语态略显不自然，此结构更常用主动语态)
Greater productivity is being made for by the new technology. = 新技术正在促成更高的生产力。(被动语态略显不自然，此结构更常用主动语态)


疑问句:
What is making for the delay? = 导致延误的原因是什么？
What factors are making for this success? = 哪些因素导致了这个成功？


否定句:
The lack of communication didn't make for a productive team. = 缺乏沟通并没有促成一个高效的团队。
The poor weather conditions did not make for a pleasant journey. = 恶劣的天气条件并没有带来愉快的旅程。


PHRASE->
make for + 名词/副词:  make for the exit (走向出口), make for home (回家), make for success (通向成功), make for the mountains (前往山区), make for trouble (惹麻烦)
