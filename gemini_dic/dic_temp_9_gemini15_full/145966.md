overdiverse:/ˌoʊvərˈdaɪvɜːrs/ | adj. | 过度多元化的；过于多样化的 |  Having excessive diversity;  too many different elements or types, to the point of being unmanageable or ineffective.  This term implies that the level of diversity is problematic, hindering rather than helping. | Example: The company's overdiverse product line made it difficult to focus marketing efforts and manage inventory effectively. 公司产品线过于多元化，导致难以集中营销精力和有效管理库存。 | 近义词: excessively diverse, overly heterogeneous | 反义词: homogeneous, uniform, monolithic | 词性: adjective
