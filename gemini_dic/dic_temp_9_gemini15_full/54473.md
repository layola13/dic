disinter:/dɪsˈɪntər/| vt.|掘出；挖出（尤指埋葬的东西）|To remove something, especially a body, from a grave.  |例句：They disinterred the bodies to perform further tests. 他们掘出尸体进行进一步的测试。

例句：The archaeologists disinterred ancient artifacts from the burial mound. 考古学家从坟丘中挖掘出古代文物。


近义词：exhume, unearth, uncover


反义词：bury, inter, entomb


词性：动词
