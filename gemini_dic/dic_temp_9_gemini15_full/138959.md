nvr:/ˈnɛvər/| adv.|从不，决不 | never means not ever; at no time. It's used to emphasize the complete absence of something.  |例句：I have never been to Paris. 我从未去过巴黎。  |例句：Never give up your dreams.  永远不要放弃你的梦想。|近义词：not ever, at no time|反义词：always, ever


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I never eat breakfast. = 我从不吃早餐。
She never goes to the movies. = 她从不看电影。

一般过去时:
He never called me back. = 他从未给我回电话。
They never visited their grandparents. = 他们从未探望过他们的祖父母。

现在完成时:
I have never seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我从未见过如此美丽的日落。
She has never traveled abroad. = 她从未出国旅行过。

过去完成时:
I had never met him before that day. = 在那一天之前我从未见过他。
We had never been to that restaurant before. = 我们以前从未去过那家餐馆。

将来时:
I will never forget your kindness. = 我永远不会忘记你的好意。
We will never compromise our values. = 我们永远不会妥协我们的价值观。

虚拟语气:
I wish I had never met him. = 我真希望我从未见过他。
If I had known, I would never have gone. = 如果我知道的话，我永远不会去了。

被动语态:
The question was never answered. = 这个问题从未得到解答。
The problem has never been solved. = 这个问题从未得到解决。

疑问句:
Have you ever been to London? = 你去过伦敦吗？ (This uses "ever", the opposite of "never")
Will you ever forgive me? = 你会原谅我吗？ (This uses "ever", the opposite of "never")

否定句:
I don't ever want to see him again. = 我再也不想见到他了。 (This uses "ever" in a negative context)
She didn't ever complain about her job. = 她从未抱怨过她的工作。 (This uses "ever" in a negative context)


PHRASE->
never mind = 不要紧
never again = 再也不
never before = 从未有过
nevertheless = 然而；尽管如此
never say never = 不要说永远不会
