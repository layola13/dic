/ˈɪndiː/| adj. |独立的；另类的；非主流的|Independent, unconventional, and often associated with underground or alternative culture.  Indie music, films, and fashion often challenge mainstream trends and aesthetics. |例句：She prefers indie films to mainstream Hollywood productions. 她更喜欢独立电影而不是主流的好莱坞电影。|近义词：independent, alternative, underground, unconventional |反义词：mainstream, conventional, established
