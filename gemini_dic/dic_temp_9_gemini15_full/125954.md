musician:/mjuːˈzɪʃən/| n. |音乐家，乐师|A person who plays a musical instrument or sings, especially professionally.  |例句：He's a talented musician, skilled in both piano and composition. 他是一位才华横溢的音乐家，擅长钢琴和作曲。|例句：Many musicians gathered for the annual music festival. 许多音乐家聚集在一起参加年度音乐节。|近义词：artist, performer, instrumentalist, vocalist|反义词：None specifically antonymous, but could be contrasted with "non-musician"|词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays the guitar every day. = 他每天弹吉他。
She sings in a choir. = 她在一个合唱团唱歌。


一般过去时:
The band played a fantastic concert last night. = 乐队昨晚演奏了一场精彩的音乐会。
He composed a beautiful symphony. = 他创作了一首美丽的交响曲。


现在完成时:
She has performed on many stages. = 她曾在许多舞台上演出。
He has written several successful albums. = 他创作了几张成功的专辑。


过去完成时:
They had already finished their rehearsal before the audience arrived. = 观众到达之前，他们已经完成了彩排。
The orchestra had played that piece many times before. = 乐队以前演奏过这首曲子很多次了。


将来时:
She will give a solo performance next week. = 她下周将进行独奏演出。
The band will tour Europe this summer. = 乐队今年夏天将巡回欧洲演出。


虚拟语气:
If I were a musician, I would travel the world. = 如果我是一个音乐家，我会周游世界。
I wish I could play the piano as well as he does. = 我希望我能像他一样弹钢琴。


被动语态:
The concert was attended by thousands of people. = 音乐会吸引了成千上万的人参加。
The song was written by a famous composer. = 这首歌是由一位著名的作曲家创作的。


疑问句:
Are you a musician? = 你是音乐家吗？
What instrument does he play? = 他演奏什么乐器？


否定句:
She is not a professional musician. = 她不是一名职业音乐家。
He doesn't play any instruments. = 他不会演奏任何乐器。
