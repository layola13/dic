outdesigned:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈzaɪnd/| v. |设计得更好，胜过设计 |To surpass in design or planning; to be superior in design.  This is a past participle used as an adjective or in passive voice.|例句：Their new product was outdesigned by the competitor's innovative model. 他们的新产品被竞争对手的创新型号设计得更好。

Their strategy was completely outdesigned; they hadn't anticipated our response.  他们的策略被完全超越了；他们没有预料到我们的回应。|近义词：outmaneuvered, surpassed, excelled|反义词：underdesigned, poorly designed
