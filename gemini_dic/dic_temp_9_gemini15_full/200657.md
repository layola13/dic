sled:/slɛd/| n. |雪橇|A vehicle used for sliding over snow or ice, typically consisting of a long, flat board or frame with runners.|例句：The children enjoyed sledding down the snowy hill.孩子们喜欢在雪坡上滑雪橇。|近义词：sleigh, toboggan|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sleds down the hill every winter. = 他每年冬天都在山上滑雪橇。
The children sled in the park. = 孩子们在公园里玩雪橇。

一般过去时:
We sledded down the hill yesterday. = 我们昨天在山上玩了雪橇。
She sled yesterday afternoon. = 她昨天下午玩了雪橇。

现在完成时:
I have sledded many times this winter. = 这个冬天我已经玩过很多次雪橇了。
They have never sledded before. = 他们以前从未玩过雪橇。

过去完成时:
He had sledded before, so he wasn't afraid. = 他以前滑过雪橇，所以他不害怕。
We had never sledded on such a steep hill before. = 我们以前从未在这么陡峭的山坡上滑过雪橇。

将来时:
We will sled tomorrow if it snows. = 如果明天下雪，我们将去玩雪橇。
They will sled at the mountain resort next week. = 他们下周将在山区度假村玩雪橇。

虚拟语气:
If I had a sled, I would go sledding. = 如果我有一辆雪橇，我会去滑雪橇。
I wish I could go sledding this weekend. = 我希望这个周末我能去滑雪橇。


被动语态:
The sled was pulled by a team of huskies. = 雪橇由一群哈士奇犬拉着。
The sled was damaged in the accident. = 雪橇在事故中受损。


疑问句:
Do you like sledding? = 你喜欢滑雪橇吗？
Have you ever been sledding before? = 你以前滑过雪橇吗？


否定句:
He doesn't have a sled. = 他没有雪橇。
They didn't go sledding last winter. = 他们去年冬天没有去滑雪橇。
