re-elect:/ˌriːɪˈlɛkt/ | vt. |重新选举 | To elect someone again to the same position.  |例句：The incumbent president was re-elected by a landslide.  现任总统以压倒性优势再次当选。|例句：The voters decided to re-elect their favourite mayor.选民决定再次选举他们最喜欢的市长。|近义词：reelect, choose again, select again |反义词：remove from office, unseat

一般现在时:
They re-elect the mayor every four years. = 他们每四年重新选举市长一次。
The company re-elects its board members annually. = 公司每年重新选举董事会成员。

一般过去时:
The citizens re-elected him as their representative. = 公民们再次选举他为他们的代表。
The club re-elected its president last month. = 俱乐部上个月重新选举了它的主席。

现在完成时:
They have re-elected the same senator for three terms. = 他们已经连续三届重新选举了同一位参议员。
The school has re-elected Mr. Smith as principal. = 学校已经再次选举史密斯先生为校长。

过去完成时:
Before the scandal, they had re-elected him twice. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经两次重新选举了他。
The board had re-elected the CEO before the financial crisis hit. = 在金融危机爆发之前，董事会已经再次选举了首席执行官。

将来时:
The people will re-elect him if he continues to perform well. = 如果他继续表现出色，人们将会再次选举他。
We expect they will re-elect the current governor next year. = 我们预计他们明年将再次选举现任州长。

虚拟语气:
If the people were satisfied, they would re-elect the current president. = 如果人民满意，他们就会再次选举现任总统。
I wish they would re-elect the mayor who improved our city. = 我希望他们能再次选举那位改善了我们城市的那位市长。

被动语态:
The mayor was re-elected by a large margin. = 市长以较大优势再次当选。
He was re-elected despite the controversy. = 尽管存在争议，他仍然再次当选。

疑问句:
Will the voters re-elect the incumbent? = 选民会再次选举现任者吗？
Did the party re-elect its leader? = 该党是否再次选举了其领导人？

否定句:
The governor was not re-elected in the last election. = 州长在上届选举中没有再次当选。
The public did not re-elect the unpopular candidate. = 公众没有再次选举那位不受欢迎的候选人。
