milieux:/ˈmiːljeʊz/| n.|环境；社会环境；氛围|The word "milieux" is the plural of "milieu," referring to a person's social environment, the circumstances surrounding one, or a particular physical environment. It often implies a specific cultural, social, or intellectual context.|例句：She studied the different milieux of Parisian artists.|近义词：environments, settings, contexts, circles|反义词：vacuums, voids
