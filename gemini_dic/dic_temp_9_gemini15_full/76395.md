gaffes:/ɡæfs/| n.|失礼；失言；尴尬；|A gaffe is a clumsy social error; a faux pas.  It's an unintentional mistake, often in speech or behavior, that causes embarrassment or offense. |例句：He made a terrible gaffe when he forgot the name of the prime minister. 他忘记了首相的名字，犯了一个严重的错误。

例句：Her comment about his weight was a major gaffe. 她关于他体重的评论是一个严重的失言。


近义词：faux pas, blunder, mistake, error, slip, oversight|反义词：tact, diplomacy, grace
