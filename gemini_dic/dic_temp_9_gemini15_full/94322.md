impetuous:/ɪmˈpetʃuəs/ | adj. | 冲动的；鲁莽的；性急的 | Acting or done quickly and without thought or care.  Characterized by sudden or rash action, emotion, or feeling. | 例句：His impetuous nature often led him into trouble.  他的冲动性格经常给他带来麻烦。

His impetuous decision to quit his job surprised everyone. 他冲动地决定辞职，让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词：rash, impulsive, hasty, reckless, precipitate | 反义词：cautious, deliberate, thoughtful, prudent, careful | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is impetuous in his actions. = 他做事冲动。
She is an impetuous speaker. = 她是个说话冲动的演说家。


一般过去时:
He was impetuous when he was younger. = 他年轻的时候很冲动。
She was impetuous in her response. = 她回应得很冲动。


现在完成时:
He has been impetuous throughout his life. = 他一生都很冲动。
She has been impetuous on several occasions. = 她有好几次都很冲动。


过去完成时:
He had been impetuous, which is why he regretted his decision. = 他之前很冲动，所以他后悔自己的决定。
She had been impetuous and acted without thinking. = 她之前很冲动，没有经过思考就采取了行动。


将来时:
He will be impetuous again, I'm sure. = 我肯定他会再次冲动。
She will likely be impetuous in her reaction. = 她很可能会冲动地做出反应。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so impetuous, he wouldn't make so many mistakes. = 如果他不那么冲动，就不会犯那么多错误。
If she hadn't been so impetuous, she would have avoided the argument. = 如果她不那么冲动，就能避免这场争吵。


被动语态:  (Impetuous is rarely used passively)  The decision was described as impetuous.  这个决定被描述为冲动的。
His actions were seen as impetuous. 他的行为被认为是冲动的。


疑问句:
Was his decision truly impetuous? = 他的决定真的那么冲动吗？
Is she always so impetuous? = 她总是这么冲动吗？


否定句:
He was not impetuous in his approach. = 他采取的方法并不冲动。
She was not at all impetuous in her reply. = 她的回答一点也不冲动。
