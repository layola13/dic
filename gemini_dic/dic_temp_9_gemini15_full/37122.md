combustion:/kəmˈbʌstʃən/| n.|燃烧，氧化|The rapid oxidation of a substance, producing heat and light.|例句：The combustion of fuel in a car engine produces energy. 汽车引擎中燃料的燃烧产生能量。|近义词：burning, incineration|反义词：extinction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Combustion releases energy. = 燃烧释放能量。
The engine relies on combustion to function. = 引擎依靠燃烧来运转。

一般过去时:
The wood underwent rapid combustion. = 木材发生了快速燃烧。
Combustion created a large amount of smoke. = 燃烧产生了大量的烟雾。

现在完成时:
The fire has caused significant combustion. = 大火造成了严重的燃烧。
Scientists have studied combustion for centuries. = 科学家们研究燃烧已经有好几个世纪了。

过去完成时:

The explosion had resulted from uncontrolled combustion. = 爆炸是由不受控制的燃烧造成的。

将来时:
