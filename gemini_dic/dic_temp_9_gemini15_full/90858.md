hungers:/ˈhʌŋɡərz/ | n. |饥饿；渴望；欲望 | The feeling of needing or wanting something very much, especially food.  |例句：The children's hungers were satisfied with a hearty meal. 孩子们吃了一顿丰盛的饭后，饥饿感得到了满足。|例句：His hunger for knowledge drove him to pursue further education. 他对知识的渴望驱使他继续深造。|近义词：appetite, craving, yearning |反义词：satiation, satisfaction |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hungers for power. = 他渴望权力。
She hungers for a simpler life. = 她渴望过一种更简单的生活。

一般过去时:
The refugees hungered for days before help arrived. = 难民们在得到帮助之前挨饿了好几天。
They hungered for news from home. = 他们渴望得到家里的消息。

现在完成时:
He has hungered for success his whole life. = 他一生都在渴望成功。
She has hungered for recognition for her work. = 她一直渴望得到对她工作的认可。

过去完成时:
They had hungered for weeks by the time rescue came. = 等到救援到来时，他们已经饿了好几个星期了。
Having hungered for so long, he devoured the food. = 饿了那么久，他狼吞虎咽地吃起了食物。

将来时:
They will hunger for freedom if their rights are suppressed. = 如果他们的权利受到压制，他们将会渴望自由。
He will hunger for revenge after what happened. = 发生那件事后，他会渴望复仇。

虚拟语气:
If he hungered for knowledge as much as he hungered for wealth, he'd be a great scholar. = 如果他对知识的渴望像对财富的渴望一样强烈，他就会成为一位伟大的学者。
I wish I didn't hunger for things I can't have. = 我真希望我不渴望我得不到的东西。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His hunger was satisfied. = 他的饥饿得到了满足。(较少用)
Their need was not being met; they hungered for justice.  = 他们的需求没有得到满足；他们渴望正义。(主动语态更自然)


疑问句:
Does he hunger for more power? = 他渴望获得更多权力吗？
Do they hunger for a better future? = 他们渴望拥有更美好的未来吗？

否定句:
He does not hunger for fame. = 他不渴望成名。
They did not hunger for power; they sought peace. = 他们不渴望权力；他们寻求和平。

the pangs of hunger = 饥饿的阵痛
