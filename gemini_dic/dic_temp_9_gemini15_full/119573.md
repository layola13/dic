microvillar:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈvɪlər/| adj.|微绒毛的；微绒毛状的| Relating to or characterized by microvilli.  Microvilli are tiny finger-like projections on the surface of some cells, increasing surface area for absorption or secretion. |The microvillar surface of the intestinal lining facilitates nutrient absorption.肠道内壁的微绒毛表面促进了营养物质的吸收。|近义词：microscopic, minute|反义词：macroscopic
