inconclusive:/ˌɪnkənˈkluːsɪv/| adj. | 不确定的；无定论的；不充分的 | Not leading to a definite decision or result; not conclusive.  |The evidence presented was inconclusive, so the jury was unable to reach a verdict.  证据不足，陪审团无法做出裁决。|conclusive, definitive | conclusive, decisive |


USAGE->

The evidence is inconclusive. = 证据不确定。
The results of the test are inconclusive. = 测试结果不确定。

一般过去时:
The investigation was inconclusive. = 调查结果没有定论。

现在完成时:

The discussions have been inconclusive so far. = 到目前为止，讨论还没有结果。

过去完成时:
The previous tests had been inconclusive. = 之前的测试结果没有定论。
The earlier analysis had yielded inconclusive data. = 早期分析得出的数据不确定。

虚拟语气:
