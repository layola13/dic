few:/fjuː/| adj.| 很少的，少数的 |  A small number of; not many.  It emphasizes the smallness of the quantity. |  There are few people in the park today.  今天公园里人很少。|  a little, several, some | many, numerous | 形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are few cars on the road. = 道路上的汽车很少。
Few students understand this concept. = 很少有学生理解这个概念。

一般过去时:
There were few opportunities available. = 几乎没有机会。
Few people attended the meeting. = 很少有人参加会议。

现在完成时:
Few people have ever seen such a thing. = 很少有人见过这样的事情。
Few changes have been made to the plan. = 对计划的改动很少。

过去完成时:
Few problems had been anticipated before the launch. = 在发布之前，很少有人预料到问题。
Few of the guests had arrived by the time the party started. = 聚会开始时，很少有客人到达。

将来时:
There will be few seats available. = 将会有很少的座位。
Few people will be surprised by the news. = 很少有人会对这个消息感到惊讶。

虚拟语气:
If there were few obstacles, the project would be easier. = 如果障碍很少，项目就会更容易。
I wish there were few distractions during my work. = 我希望在我工作期间干扰少一些。

被动语态:
Few mistakes were made during the presentation. = 演示过程中犯的错误很少。
Few details were revealed about the incident. = 关于这起事件的细节很少被透露。

疑问句:
Are there few resources available? = 可用的资源很少吗？
Were there few participants in the survey? = 调查参与者很少吗？

否定句:
There weren't few complaints about the service. = 对这项服务并非没有抱怨（有很多抱怨）。
It's not few challenges we face. = 我们面临的挑战并非很少（很多）。


PHRASE->
a few = 一些 (a small number, but more than one)
quite a few = 相当多 (more than a few)
very few = 非常少 (almost none)
but few = 但是很少
few and far between = 很少见，凤毛麟角
