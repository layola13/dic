eyeballing:/ˈaɪˌbɔːlɪŋ/ | v. |粗略估计，目测|To estimate something by looking at it, without using any tools or precise measurements.  It implies a quick, informal assessment. |例句：I eyeballed the distance and figured it was about a mile. 我目测了一下距离，估计大约一英里。

例句：We eyeballed the amount of paint needed for the project. 我们粗略估计了一下这个项目需要多少油漆。


近义词：estimating, judging, guessing, approximating | 反义词：measuring precisely, calculating accurately

She eyeballs the distance between the two trees. = 她目测两棵树之间的距离。
