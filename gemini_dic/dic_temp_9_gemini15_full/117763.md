melatonin:/ˌmɛləˈtoʊnɪn/ | n. |褪黑素|A hormone produced by the pineal gland in the brain, primarily at night, that regulates sleep cycles. Melatonin supplements are often used to treat insomnia and jet lag.|例句：Taking melatonin supplements can help regulate your sleep cycle. 服用褪黑素补充剂可以帮助调节你的睡眠周期。
例句：The body naturally produces melatonin to promote sleep. 身体自然产生褪黑素以促进睡眠。|近义词：sleep aid, hormone|反义词：stimulant|词性:名词
