conspired:/kənˈspaɪərd/ | v. |密谋；共谋；图谋|To plan secretly to do something wrong or illegal.  |例句：They conspired to overthrow the government. 他们密谋推翻政府。

例句：The evidence suggests that several people conspired to commit the crime.  证据表明有几个人共谋犯下此罪。


近义词：plotted, schemed, plotted against, colluded

反义词：cooperated, collaborated
