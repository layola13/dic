postcostal:/ˌpoʊstˈkɒstl/| adj.|肋骨后的，肋骨旁的|Situated behind or beside the ribs.  The term is primarily used in anatomical descriptions.|例句：The postcostal muscles support the abdominal wall. 肋骨后的肌肉支撑腹壁。|例句：The pain was located in the postcostal region. 疼痛位于肋骨旁区域。|近义词：posterior to ribs, paracostal|反义词：precostal
