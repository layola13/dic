onset:/ˈɒnset/ /ˈɑːnset/| n.| 开始，发作，初期 | The beginning or start of something.  It can refer to the beginning of a disease, an attack, a period of time, or a process. |例句：The onset of winter brought heavy snow. 冬天的来临带来了大雪。

例句：The onset of the disease was sudden and severe.  疾病的发作突然而严重。


近义词：beginning, commencement, start, initiation |反义词：conclusion, end, termination |词性:名词


词源:  Old French *onsete, from *on- (on) + sete (attack, assault), from Vulgar Latin *incursus (incursion).  The word's evolution reflects its original meaning relating to a military attack or assault, which later broadened to encompass the beginning of any event or process.
