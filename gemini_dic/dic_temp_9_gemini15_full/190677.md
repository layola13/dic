sauna:/ˈsɔːnə/| n. |桑拿浴；桑拿浴室 | A sauna is a small room or building designed for sweating, typically heated by a stove or heater.  It is used for relaxation and health benefits. | 例句：I went to the sauna to relax after a long day. 我在忙碌了一天后去桑拿房放松一下。  | 例句：The hotel sauna was incredibly hot and humid.  酒店的桑拿房非常闷热潮湿。|近义词：steam bath, sweat bath | 反义词：cold bath, ice bath
