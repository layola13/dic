halfhearted:/ˈhæfˈhɑːtɪd/| adj.|不热心的；敷衍的；三心二意的|Showing little enthusiasm or interest.|His halfhearted attempt at an apology was unconvincing. 他敷衍了事的道歉令人难以信服。|近义词：apathetic, unenthusiastic, lukewarm, perfunctory|反义词：enthusiastic, passionate, committed, ardent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is halfhearted about his work. = 他对工作不热心。
She is halfhearted in her efforts to learn the language. = 她学习这门语言的努力很敷衍。


一般过去时:

They were halfhearted about their participation in the project. = 他们对参与这个项目很不热心。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
