pprofessor:/prəˈfɛsər/| n. |副教授|A professor who holds a rank below a full professor.  A pprofessor typically has less seniority and teaching responsibilities than a full professor. They might be on a tenure track or a non-tenure track position. |Example: He's a pprofessor of history at a small liberal arts college. 他是一所小型文理学院的历史副教授。|近义词: associate professor|反义词: full professor
