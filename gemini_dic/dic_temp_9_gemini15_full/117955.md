member:/ˈmɛmbər/ | n. |成员，会员 |A person, animal, or thing that is a part of a larger group or organization.  |例句：He is a member of the club. 他是俱乐部的一员。 |例句：She's a valued member of our team. 她是我们团队中一位宝贵的成员。|近义词：participant, associate, constituent |反义词：outsider, non-member


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a member of the committee. = 他是委员会的成员。
They are members of the same club. = 他们都是同一个俱乐部的成员。

一般过去时:
She was a member of the parliament. = 她曾是议会成员。
He was a loyal member for many years. = 他多年来一直是忠实的会员。

现在完成时:
I have been a member of this gym for five years. = 我已经在这个健身房做会员五年了。
She has been a contributing member since the beginning. = 从一开始她就一直是贡献成员。

过去完成时:
He had been a member before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他曾是会员。
They had been members for over a decade. = 他们已经做了十多年的会员了。

将来时:
I will be a member of the board next year. = 明年我将成为董事会成员。
She will become a member of the society soon. = 她很快就会成为协会的成员。

虚拟语气:
If I were a member, I would have a say. = 如果我是会员，我会有一席之地。
I wish I were a member of that exclusive club. = 我真希望我是那个高级俱乐部的会员。

被动语态:
He was elected a member of the council. = 他被选为理事会成员。
She was appointed a member of the committee. = 她被任命为委员会成员。

疑问句:
Are you a member of the organization? = 你是这个组织的成员吗？
Is he still a member of the team? = 他还是团队成员吗？

否定句:
I am not a member of that group. = 我不是那个团体的成员。
He was never a member of the society. = 他从未是协会的成员。


PHRASE->
member of parliament (MP) = 国会议员
family member = 家庭成员
team member = 团队成员
club member = 俱乐部成员
community member = 社区成员
