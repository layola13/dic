microswitch:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌswɪtʃ/| n. |微型开关|A microswitch is a small, electrically operated switch that is activated by a small amount of pressure or movement.  They are commonly used in electronic devices and appliances to detect the presence or absence of something, or to control a circuit.  |例句：The door of the washing machine uses a microswitch to turn on the motor when closed.  洗衣机的门使用微型开关，在关门时启动马达。


The camera's shutter uses a microswitch to control the exposure time. 相机的快门使用微型开关来控制曝光时间。|近义词：miniature switch, small switch |反义词：macroswitch (less common, implies a large switch)
