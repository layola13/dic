duress:/dʒʊˈrɛs/| n. |胁迫；强迫；压力 |Unlawful constraint exercised upon a person whereby he is forced to act contrary to his will.  It involves the use of force, threats, or coercion to compel someone to do something against their will. |例句：He signed the confession under duress. 他在胁迫下签署了供词。  The witness testified under duress from the defendant.证人在被告的胁迫下作证。|近义词：coercion, compulsion, force, pressure |反义词：freedom, voluntariness |名词
