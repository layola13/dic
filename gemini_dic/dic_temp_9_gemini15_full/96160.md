independence: /ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəns/ | n. | 独立，自主 | The state or fact of being independent.  Freedom from control, influence, support, or the like; self-governance. |例句：The country declared its independence from Britain in 1776. 这个国家在1776年宣布脱离英国独立。

例句：After graduating from college, she gained her financial independence. 大学毕业后，她获得了经济上的独立。


近义词：freedom, autonomy, self-reliance, liberty | 反义词：dependence, reliance, subjection
