ostracism: /ˈɒstrəsɪzəm/ | /ˈɑːstrəˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 排斥；放逐；冷落 | The act of excluding someone from a society or group.  It often involves deliberate isolation and shunning.  |例句：His outspoken views led to his ostracism from the community.  他的直言不讳导致了他被社区排斥。

例句：The new student faced ostracism because of her different background. 新生因为不同的背景而面临排斥。


近义词：exclusion, shunning, isolation, banishment, boycott

反义词：inclusion, acceptance, welcome


词性：名词
