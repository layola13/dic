likelihood:/ˈlaɪklihʊd/ | n. |可能性，可能|The likelihood of success is high.  成功的可能性很高。| probability, chance | impossibility | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The likelihood of rain is high today. = 今天下雨的可能性很高。
What is the likelihood of him succeeding? = 他成功的可能性有多大？


一般过去时:
The likelihood of a recession was underestimated. = 对经济衰退的可能性估计不足。
What was the likelihood of winning the lottery? = 中彩票的可能性有多大？


现在完成时:
The likelihood of a cure has increased. = 治愈的可能性增加了。
What has been the likelihood of finding a job in this market? = 在这个市场上找到工作的可能性有多大？


过去完成时:
The likelihood of success had diminished. = 成功的可能性已经减小了。
What had been the likelihood of rain before the storm started? = 暴风雨开始前下雨的可能性有多大？


将来时:
The likelihood of war will increase if negotiations fail. = 如果谈判失败，战争的可能性将会增加。
What will be the likelihood of a good harvest this year? = 今年获得好收成的可能性有多大？


虚拟语气:
If the likelihood of success were higher, we would invest more. = 如果成功的可能性更高，我们会投入更多。
I wish the likelihood of a peaceful resolution were greater. = 我希望和平解决的可能性更大。


被动语态:
The likelihood of infection was not properly assessed. = 感染的可能性没有得到正确的评估。
The likelihood of further complications is being closely monitored. = 进一步并发症的可能性正在密切监测中。


疑问句:
What is the likelihood of a change in policy? = 政策改变的可能性有多大？
What's the likelihood of her agreeing to the proposal? = 她同意这项提议的可能性有多大？


否定句:
There is little likelihood of a quick solution. = 快速解决的可能性很小。
The likelihood of a positive outcome is not guaranteed. = 积极结果的可能性并非有保证。


PHRASE->
