goldilocks-planet:/ˈɡoʊldɪˌlɑːks ˈplænɪt/| n. |适居行星，金发姑娘行星|A planet that is neither too hot nor too cold, and potentially habitable, like the "Goldilocks" zone in the fairy tale.  It refers to a planet within the habitable zone of a star, where liquid water could exist on the surface.| Example: Scientists are searching for goldilocks planets in other solar systems. 科学家们正在寻找其他太阳系中的适居行星。|近义词：habitable planet, Earth-like planet|反义词：uninhabitable planet, barren planet


USAGE->
This term is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, traditional verb tenses (present, past, future, etc.) do not apply.  However, we can demonstrate its usage in different sentence structures:

Declarative Sentence:
Kepler-186f is considered a potential goldilocks planet.  开普勒-186f被认为是一颗潜在的适居行星。

Interrogative Sentence:
Is Proxima Centauri b a goldilocks planet?  比邻星b是一颗适居行星吗？
