dekabrist:/ˈdɛkəbrɪst/| n. |十二月党人；(植物)一品红|A member of a group of Russian aristocratic army officers who staged an unsuccessful revolt against Tsar Nicholas I in December 1825.  The name derives from the month of the uprising (December, in Russian, is Декабрь, dekabr').  The Decembrists advocated liberal reforms and played a significant role in the development of Russian revolutionary thought.|The Decembrist revolt, though ultimately unsuccessful, had a lasting impact on Russian political thought.  |近义词：rebel, revolutionary|反义词：loyalist, monarchist|名词
