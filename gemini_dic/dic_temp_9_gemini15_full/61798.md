emphatic:/ɛmˈfætɪk/| adj.|强调的；重读的；强烈的|Expressing something forcibly and clearly; giving emphasis.|例句：She gave an emphatic denial of the charges. 她断然否认了这些指控。|近义词：stressful, forceful, strong|反义词:weak, understated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks emphatically about his beliefs. = 他坚定地谈论着自己的信仰。
Her writing style is emphatically clear and concise. = 她的写作风格非常清晰简洁。


一般过去时:
She emphatically stated her position on the matter. = 她坚定地阐述了她对这件事的立场。
He spoke emphatically about the importance of education. = 他着重强调了教育的重要性。


现在完成时:
She has emphatically rejected all offers of help. = 她已经断然拒绝了所有帮助。
He has emphatically proven his point. = 他已经充分证明了他的观点。


过去完成时:
They had emphatically warned him of the dangers. = 他们已经郑重地警告过他危险。
She had emphatically stated her intentions before leaving. = 离开之前，她已经明确地表达了她的意图。


将来时:
He will emphatically defend his actions. = 他将坚定地捍卫自己的行为。
She will emphatically disagree with the proposal. = 她将强烈反对该提案。


虚拟语气:
If he were to speak emphatically, everyone would listen. = 如果他说话坚定有力，每个人都会倾听。
I wish she would speak more emphatically. = 我希望她说话能更坚定一些。


被动语态:
His point was emphatically made during the presentation. = 他的观点在演示中得到了有力的阐述。
The importance of the issue was emphatically stressed by the speaker. = 演讲者着重强调了该问题的重要性。


疑问句:
Was his response emphatic enough? = 他的回应足够有力吗？
Is this an emphatic enough statement of the problem? = 这是对问题的足够有力的陈述吗？


否定句:
He did not speak emphatically enough to convince the audience. = 他说话不够有力，无法说服观众。
Her answer was not emphatic enough to satisfy everyone. = 她的回答不够有力，无法让所有人满意。
