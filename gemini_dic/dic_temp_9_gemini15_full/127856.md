neo:/ˈniːoʊ/| prefix | 新的；新生的；类似的；后来的 |  From the Greek word "neos," meaning "new" or "young." It's a prefix used to indicate something new, recent, or related to a later stage of development.  |例句：Neolithic refers to the New Stone Age. 新石器时代指的是新石器时代。| 近义词：new, modern, recent | 反义词：old, ancient, archaic |
