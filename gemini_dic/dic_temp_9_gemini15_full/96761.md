ineptitudes:/ɪˈnep.tɪ.tʃuːdz/| n.|愚蠢的行为；笨拙；无能|Foolish or incompetent actions or behavior.  The author's ineptitudes in handling the plot weakened the novel.|近义词：foolishness, incompetence, clumsiness, inabilities|反义词：skill, competence, expertise, ability
