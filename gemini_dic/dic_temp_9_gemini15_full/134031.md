nonhygienic:/ˌnɒnhaɪˈdʒiːnɪk/ | adj. | 不卫生的，不洁的 | Not clean enough to prevent the spread of disease.  This refers to conditions or practices that are likely to cause or spread disease.  | Example: The restaurant was deemed nonhygienic due to the unclean kitchen and improper food handling. 这家餐馆由于厨房不干净和食物处理不当而被认为不卫生。  | 近义词：unsanitary, unhygienic, dirty, filthy | 反义词：hygienic, clean, sanitary |
