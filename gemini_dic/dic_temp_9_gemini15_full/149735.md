palatability:/ˌpæləˈtæbɪləti/| n. |适口性；美味；可口性 |The degree to which something is pleasant or acceptable to the taste or mind.  It refers to how agreeable something is to eat or drink, or how acceptable an idea or suggestion is. |例句：The palatability of the new food product was tested extensively before its launch. 新食品的适口性在上市前进行了广泛的测试。

The high palatability of the dessert led to its quick sellout.  甜点的美味导致它很快售罄。|近义词：acceptability, agreeableness, pleasantness |反义词：unacceptability, unpleasantness, distastefulness |名词
