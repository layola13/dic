lenticula: /lenˈtɪkjʊlə/ | n. |小透镜；小凸透镜；[植]小扁豆状体 | A small lens, especially a small convex lens.  The term is also used in botany to describe small, lens-shaped structures. |例句：The camera's tiny lenticula focused the light perfectly. 这台相机的微型小透镜完美地聚焦了光线。

The microscope's lenticula magnified the specimen hundreds of times. 显微镜的小透镜将样本放大了数百倍。


近义词：lenslet (smaller lens),  convex lens (if convex) | 反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑凹透镜concave lens作为相对概念 | 词性：名词


词源:  From Latin *lenticula, diminutive of lens, "lentil," referring to the lentil's lens-like shape.  The botanical use reflects this origin, as the small structures are often lens-shaped.  The optical use extends this analogy to describe small lenses.
