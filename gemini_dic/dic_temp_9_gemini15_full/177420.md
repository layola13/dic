reballoted:/ˌriːˈbælətid/ | v. | 重新投票 | To vote again on something.  This often happens when the initial vote is inconclusive or deemed invalid. | Example: The motion was reballoted after a dispute over the counting of the initial votes.  重新投票表决该动议，因为对最初的投票计数存在争议。The council decided to reballot on the controversial building project.  市议会决定就备受争议的建筑项目重新投票。|近义词：revote, re-vote | 反义词：final vote, conclusive vote | 动词
