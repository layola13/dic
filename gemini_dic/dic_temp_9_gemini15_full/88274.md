hint's: /hɪnts/ | n. | 暗示；提示；线索 |  a suggestion or piece of advice intended to be subtle or indirect.  |例句：He gave me a hint about the surprise party. 他给了我关于惊喜派对的暗示。  |近义词：clue, suggestion, implication | 反义词：revelation, disclosure | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher gives hints to help students understand. = 老师给出提示来帮助学生理解。
He often hints at his dissatisfaction. = 他经常暗示他对现状的不满。


一般过去时:
She hinted at the problem during the meeting. = 她在会议期间暗示了这个问题。
He gave me a hint about the answer. = 他给了我关于答案的提示。


现在完成时:
She has hinted at her plans for retirement. = 她已经暗示了她退休的计划。
He has given me several hints about the solution. = 他给了我几个关于解决方案的提示。


过去完成时:
I realized later that he had hinted at my mistake earlier. = 我后来才意识到他之前已经暗示了我的错误。
They had hinted at the possibility of a merger before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，他们已经暗示了合并的可能性。


将来时:
She will hint at her decision in her upcoming speech. = 她将在即将发表的演讲中暗示她的决定。
He will give you hints as you work through the puzzle. = 他会在你解谜的过程中给你提示。


虚拟语气:
If she had hinted at the danger, we might have been more careful. = 如果她暗示了危险，我们可能会更小心。
I wish he had hinted at the problem sooner. = 我希望他早点暗示这个问题。


被动语态:
The solution was hinted at in the previous chapter. = 解决方案在上一章中有所暗示。
The answer was hinted at but never explicitly stated. = 答案有所暗示，但从未明确说明。


疑问句:
Was he hinting at something? = 他是在暗示什么吗？
What are you hinting at? = 你在暗示什么？


否定句:
He didn't hint at his true feelings. = 他没有暗示他真实的想法。
She didn't give any hints about the test. = 她没有给出关于考试的任何提示。
