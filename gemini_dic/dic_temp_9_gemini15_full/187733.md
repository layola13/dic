rooted:/ˈruːtɪd/| adj.|生根的；根深蒂固的；固定的|Having roots; firmly established; ingrained.|The tree is deeply rooted in the ground. 这棵树深深地扎根于地下。|近义词：established, ingrained, fixed|反义词：superficial, shallow, unfixed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The plant is rooted in the soil.  = 植物扎根于土壤中。
His beliefs are rooted in his childhood experiences. = 他的信仰根植于他的童年经历。


一般过去时:
The tree was deeply rooted before the storm. = 这棵树在暴风雨来临之前已经深深扎根了。
Her fear was rooted in a past trauma. = 她的恐惧源于过去的创伤。


现在完成时:
The problem has been rooted in the system for years. = 这个问题多年来一直根植于这个系统中。
His success has been rooted in hard work and dedication. = 他的成功源于勤奋和奉献。


过去完成时:
The tradition had been deeply rooted in the community for generations. = 这个传统在社区中世代相传，根深蒂固。
The prejudice had been rooted in societal norms for centuries. = 这种偏见在社会规范中根深蒂固了几个世纪。


将来时:
The new policy will be rooted in fairness and equality. = 新政策将以公平与平等为基础。
The company's future will be rooted in innovation and technology. = 公司的未来将植根于创新和技术。


虚拟语气:
If the problem were rooted in the software, we could easily fix it. = 如果问题根源在于软件，我们可以很容易地修复它。
I wish this prejudice weren't so deeply rooted in our society. = 我希望这种偏见不会在我们的社会中如此根深蒂固。


被动语态:
The tree was rooted in the fertile soil. = 这棵树扎根在肥沃的土壤中。
The idea was deeply rooted in the minds of the people. = 这个想法深深地植根于人们的心中。


疑问句:
Is the problem rooted in poor management? = 问题是根源于管理不善吗？
Are your fears rooted in past experiences? = 你的恐惧是源于过去的经历吗？


否定句:
The plant is not rooted properly. = 植物没有好好地扎根。
His argument was not rooted in facts. = 他的论点并非基于事实。


PHRASE->

be rooted in = 根植于；源于
deeply rooted = 根深蒂固的
take root = 生根；扎根
root out = 根除；消灭
root and branch = 完全地；彻底地
