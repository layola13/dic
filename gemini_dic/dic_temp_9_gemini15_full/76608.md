gallivant:/ˈɡælɪvænt/| v.|四处游荡；游乐；寻欢作乐 |to travel around from place to place, especially in search of pleasure or amusement.|例句：They gallivanted around Europe for three months.  他们游荡欧洲三个月。|例句： After finishing his work, he gallivanted around town with his friends. 完成工作后，他和朋友们在城里四处游乐。|近义词：roam, wander, travel, frolic |反义词：stay home, remain stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They gallivant around the world. = 他们周游世界。
He gallivants around town every weekend. = 他每个周末都在城里四处游荡。


一般过去时:
We gallivanted across Europe last summer. = 我们去年夏天在欧洲四处游荡。
She gallivanted around the city with her friends. = 她和朋友们在城里四处游乐。


现在完成时:
They have gallivanted across several continents. = 他们已经游历了几个大洲。
She has gallivanted all over the country this year. = 她今年已经在全国各地游玩了。


过去完成时:
They had gallivanted through many countries before settling down. = 他们在定居之前已经游历了许多国家。
He had gallivanted through Europe before he found his wife. = 他在找到妻子之前已经游历了欧洲。


将来时:
We will gallivant around Asia next year. = 我们明年将在亚洲四处游荡。
They will gallivant across South America for a month. = 他们将在南美洲游玩一个月。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would gallivant around the world. = 如果我有时间，我会周游世界。
I wish I could gallivant around Europe this summer. = 我希望今年夏天能去欧洲四处游荡。


被动语态:
(The verb "gallivant" is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies active movement and enjoyment.)


疑问句:
Do they often gallivant around? = 他们经常四处游荡吗？
Where did they gallivant last summer? = 他们去年夏天去哪里游荡了？


否定句:
They don't gallivant around much anymore. = 他们不再经常四处游荡了。
He didn't gallivant around last weekend. = 他上周末没有四处游荡。


PHRASE->
gallivant around =四处游荡
gallivant about =四处闲逛
gallivant off = 突然离开；跑掉
gallivant with = 和...一起游玩
gallivant through = 穿越；游历
