foamflower:/ˈfoʊmˌflaʊər/ | n. |泡沫花，铃兰花 |A plant of the genus *Tiarella*, having delicate, feathery, white or pinkish flowers.  These plants are known for their delicate, airy flower clusters that resemble foam.  They are popular in shade gardens. |例句：The foamflower added a delicate touch to the woodland garden. 泡沫花为林地花园增添了精致的一笔。|例句：Several varieties of foamflower are available at the local nursery. 当地苗圃出售几种泡沫花品种。|近义词：Tiarella (botanical name) |反义词：无
