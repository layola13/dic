quasi-tyrannical: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ taɪˈrænɪkəl/ | adj. | 类似暴君的；近乎专制的 |  Approaching or resembling tyranny; characterized by excessive control or oppression, but not to the full extent of tyranny.  It suggests a degree of authoritarianism or dominance that falls short of outright tyranny but still exhibits many of its characteristics. |


例句：

The company's quasi-tyrannical management style led to high employee turnover.  = 公司近乎专制的管理风格导致员工流失率很高。

His quasi-tyrannical reign over the department ended when he was finally transferred. = 他对部门近乎专制的统治在他最终被调走时结束了。


近义词：autocratic, authoritarian, domineering, oppressive, despotic


反义词：democratic, liberal, egalitarian, lenient, permissive


词性：形容词
