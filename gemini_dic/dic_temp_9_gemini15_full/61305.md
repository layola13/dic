embarked:/ɪmˈbɑːrkt/ | v. | 上船；着手；开始 | To go aboard a ship or aircraft; to begin a journey; to start doing something new or challenging. | 例句：The passengers embarked on the cruise ship. = 乘客登上了游轮。  | 例句：She embarked on a new career in writing. = 她开始从事写作的新职业。 | 近义词：boarded, commenced, started, launched | 反义词：disembarked, ended, finished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embarks on a new project every year. = 他每年都开始一个新项目。
They embark on a journey to the mountains. = 他们开始了一次去山里的旅行。

一般过去时:
We embarked on the ship at dawn. = 我们在黎明时登上了船。
He embarked on a new business venture last month. = 他上个月开始了一项新的商业冒险。

现在完成时:
She has embarked on a challenging academic program. = 她已经开始了一个具有挑战性的学术项目。
They have embarked on a long and difficult journey. = 他们已经开始了一段漫长而艰难的旅程。

过去完成时:
Before the war, he had embarked on a promising career as a lawyer. = 在战争爆发前，他开始了作为律师的有前途的职业生涯。
They had embarked on their journey before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经开始了他们的旅程。

将来时:
We will embark on our trip to Europe next summer. = 我们将在明年夏天开始我们的欧洲之旅。
She will embark on a new research project in the fall. = 她将在秋季开始一个新的研究项目。

虚拟语气:
If I had enough money, I would embark on a world tour. = 如果我有足够的钱，我会开始环球旅行。
I wish I could embark on a career in music. = 我真希望我能从事音乐事业。

被动语态:
The passengers were embarked onto the lifeboats. = 乘客们被送上了救生艇。(注意：此处的被动语态略显不自然，通常用主动语态表达更佳)
The project was embarked upon with great enthusiasm. = 这项项目以极大的热情开始了。


疑问句:
When will they embark on their voyage? = 他们什么时候开始他们的航行？
Has he embarked on his new writing project yet? = 他开始他的新写作项目了吗？

否定句:
He did not embark on the journey because of the bad weather. = 由于天气不好，他没有开始旅程。
They haven't embarked on their ambitious plan yet. = 他们还没有开始他们雄心勃勃的计划。
