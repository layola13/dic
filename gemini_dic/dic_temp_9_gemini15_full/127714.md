neglection:/nɪˈɡlɛkʃən/| n. |忽视；疏忽；忽略 |The act of neglecting or being neglected; a state of being disregarded or overlooked.  It often implies a failure to give proper attention or care to something or someone. |例句：The neglection of safety regulations led to the accident. 忽视安全条例导致了事故。

例句：His neglection of his studies resulted in poor grades. 他对学习的忽视导致了成绩差。


近义词： disregard, oversight, omission, neglect, carelessness, inattention

反义词： attention, care, diligence, consideration


词性：名词
