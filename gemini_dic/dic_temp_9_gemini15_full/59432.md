earthquake:/ˈɜːθkweɪk/|/ˈɝθkweɪk/| n. |地震 |A sudden and violent shaking of the ground, sometimes causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action. |The earthquake caused widespread damage to buildings and infrastructure.  地震对建筑物和基础设施造成了广泛的破坏。| tremor, temblor, seismic activity | calm, stillness | noun
