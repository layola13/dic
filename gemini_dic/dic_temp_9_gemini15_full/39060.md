conformational:/ˌkɒnfɜːˈmeɪʃənəl/| /ˌkɑːnfɚˈmeɪʃənəl/ | adj. |构象的；构型的|Relating to or denoting the spatial arrangement of atoms in a molecule.  It refers to the different shapes a molecule can adopt without breaking any bonds. These different shapes are called conformers or conformations. |例句：The conformational changes in the protein are crucial for its function. 蛋白的构象变化对其功能至关重要。

The study of conformational isomers is important in organic chemistry. 构象异构体的研究在有机化学中很重要。

近义词：configurational, steric (in some contexts) | 反义词：None directly, but the opposite concept would be a fixed, rigid structure. |  词性：形容词
