footprints:/ˈfʊtˌprɪnts/ | n. |足迹；踪迹；影响 |Marks left by a foot or shoe; evidence or remains of something that has passed or existed.  |例句：The footprints in the sand showed where the dog had run. 沙子上的足迹显示了狗跑过的地方。

例句：The explorer's footprints were the only evidence of his journey through the wilderness. 探险家的足迹是他穿越荒野之旅的唯一证据。


近义词：tracks, traces, marks, impressions |反义词：none  |词性：名词
