pills:/pɪlz/ | n. |药丸，药片 |Small, solid, often round, medicinal substances to be swallowed.  Pills contain medicine to treat various illnesses or conditions. |He took two pills for his headache. 他服了两片药治头痛。  She swallowed the pills with a glass of water. 她用一杯水吞服了药片。| tablets, capsules, medication | antidote, cure (in some contexts) | 名词
