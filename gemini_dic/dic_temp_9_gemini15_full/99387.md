interdictions:/ˌɪntərˈdɪkʃənz/| n. |禁令；禁止；阻挠|The act of prohibiting or forbidding something; an order or decree that prohibits something.  It can also refer to the act of obstructing or hindering. |例句：The government issued interdictions against the import of certain goods.  政府发布了禁止进口某些商品的禁令。

例句：Numerous interdictions were put in place to prevent the spread of the disease.  为了防止疾病蔓延，实施了许多禁令。

近义词：prohibitions, bans, restrictions, embargoes, impediments

反义词：permissions, approvals, authorizations
