remedying:/ˈrɛmədiɪŋ/| v.|补救；纠正；治疗 |The act of correcting or improving something that is wrong or damaged.  It implies taking steps to alleviate a problem or improve a situation. |例句：The government is focusing on remedying the economic crisis. 政府正专注于解决经济危机。

例句：The doctor is remedying the patient's illness with medication. 医生正在用药物治疗病人的疾病。


近义词：correcting, rectifying, repairing, fixing, treating, alleviating

反义词：worsening, exacerbating, damaging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are remedying the situation as quickly as possible. = 他们正在尽可能快地补救局面。
He remedies his mistakes diligently. = 他勤奋地纠正自己的错误。


一般过去时:
She remedied the problem with a simple solution. = 她用一个简单的办法解决了问题。
We remedied the leak in the roof. = 我们修好了屋顶的漏水。


现在完成时:
They have remedied the damage caused by the storm. = 他们已经修复了暴风雨造成的损害。
The company has remedied the software bug. = 公司已经修复了软件错误。


过去完成时:
He had remedied the situation before anyone noticed. = 他在任何人注意到之前就已经解决了问题。
She had remedied the faulty wiring before the fire started. = 在火灾发生之前，她已经修复了有故障的线路。


将来时:
They will remedy the situation immediately. = 他们会立即解决这个问题。
We will remedy the defect in the product. = 我们将修复产品中的缺陷。


虚拟语气:
If they had acted sooner, they could have remedied the situation more easily. = 如果他们早点采取行动，他们就能更容易地解决这个问题。
I wish I had remedied the situation earlier. = 我希望我早点解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The problem is being remedied by the engineers. = 这个问题正在由工程师们解决。
The situation was remedied quickly and efficiently. = 情况得到了迅速而有效的解决。


疑问句:

否定句:
