halberds:/ˈhɑːlbərdz/ | n. |戟；长柄武器 | A halberd is a type of polearm, a long-handled weapon with a blade or axe head at one end and often a hook or spike at the other.  They were commonly used by infantry soldiers in the Middle Ages and the Renaissance.  | Example: The medieval soldier brandished his halberd menacingly. = 中世纪士兵凶狠地挥舞着他的戟。

The museum displayed a collection of ancient halberds. = 博物馆展出了一批古代的戟。
