noncondensible:/ˌnɒnkənˈdensəbl/|/ˌnɑːnkənˈdensəbl/| adj. |不凝结的；不能凝结的 |Not capable of being condensed into a liquid.  These gases remain in the gaseous state even under high pressure and low temperature.  |Example:  The noncondensible gases were vented from the system. 不凝结的气体从系统中排出。 The presence of noncondensible gases reduced the efficiency of the refrigeration cycle. 不凝结气体的存在降低了制冷循环的效率。|近义词： incondensable, noncondensable gases|反义词： condensable
