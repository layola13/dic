percolators:/ˈpɜːrkəleɪtərz/ | n. | 渗滤器；咖啡壶 | A percolator is a coffee maker that uses a process of boiling water to extract the flavor from coffee grounds.  It typically involves a pot with a filter basket and a tube to circulate the water. | Example:  She brewed coffee in her old-fashioned percolator. 她用她那老式的渗滤器煮咖啡。

Example: The aroma of freshly brewed coffee from the percolator filled the kitchen.  渗滤器里新鲜冲泡的咖啡香气充满了厨房。


近义词：coffee maker, drip coffee maker | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)
