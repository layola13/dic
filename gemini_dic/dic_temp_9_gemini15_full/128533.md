It can also refer to the continuous flow of experience or events. |例句：The nextness of the event filled her with excitement.  (事件的紧迫性让她兴奋不已。)  The painting captures the nextness of the moment. (这幅画捕捉到了那一刻的紧迫感。) |近义词：immediacy, proximity, succession, futurity |反义词：remoteness, distance, pastness
