dancette:/dænˈsɛt/| n.|小舞步，轻快舞步|A dancette is a short, light, quick dance step or movement, often used in ballet or other forms of dance.  It refers to a graceful, delicate, and often playful step.  |Example: The ballerina executed a series of delicate dancettes across the stage.  芭蕾舞演员在舞台上轻盈地跳了一系列的小舞步。|近义词：step, skip, hop|反义词：leap, jump, bound|名词
