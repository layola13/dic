scarecrows:/ˈskɛrkroʊz/| n.|稻草人|A scarecrow is a figure made to look like a human, typically made of straw or other materials, and placed in a field to frighten birds away from crops.|例句：The scarecrow stood guard in the cornfield. 稻草人守护在玉米地里。
例句：Farmers often use scarecrows to protect their crops from birds.  农民经常用稻草人保护庄稼免受鸟害。|近义词：bird scarer, effigy|反义词：None|名词
