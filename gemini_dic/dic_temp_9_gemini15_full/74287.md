fract:/frækt/ | v. | 破碎；裂开；使破裂 | To break or cause to break into pieces or fragments.  | The vase fractured when it fell to the floor.  花瓶掉到地上摔碎了。| The impact fractured his skull.  撞击使他的头骨骨折。| break, shatter, crack | mend, repair, whole


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ice fractures under pressure. 冰在压力下断裂。
The earthquake fractures the ground. 地震使地面断裂。

一般过去时:
The glass fractured when I dropped it. 我把玻璃杯掉在地上摔碎了。
The bone fractured during the accident. 骨头在事故中骨折了。

现在完成时:
The vase has fractured into many pieces. 花瓶已经碎成许多碎片。
The building has fractured due to the earthquake. 建筑物由于地震而开裂。

过去完成时:
The foundation had fractured before the building collapsed. 建筑物倒塌之前地基就已经开裂了。
The branch had fractured under the weight of the snow. 树枝在雪的重量下已经断裂了。

将来时:
The glass will fracture if you drop it. 如果你把玻璃杯掉在地上，它会碎的。
The dam might fracture under the pressure of the water. 水坝可能会承受不住水的压力而断裂。
