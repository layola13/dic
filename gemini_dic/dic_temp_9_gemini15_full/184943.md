retariff:/ˌriːˈtærɪf/| v.|重新制定关税；对……重新征税 |To impose new tariffs on (goods); to revise tariffs.| The government decided to retariff imported steel to protect domestic industries. 政府决定对进口钢材重新征税以保护国内产业。|reimpose tariffs, readjust tariffs|remove tariffs, abolish tariffs|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retariff imported goods annually. = 他们每年对进口商品重新征税。
The government retariffs certain products based on market conditions. = 政府根据市场情况对某些产品重新征税。


一般过去时:
The country retariffed its agricultural products last year. = 该国去年对其农产品重新征税。
They retariffed the imported cars to increase revenue. = 他们对进口汽车重新征税以增加收入。


现在完成时:
The administration has retariffed several items since taking office. = 自上任以来，政府已经对几项商品重新征税。
The country has retariffed its exports to improve its trade balance. = 该国已经对其出口商品重新征税以改善贸易平衡。


过去完成时:
Before the trade agreement, they had retariffed many goods. = 在贸易协定之前，他们已经对许多商品重新征税。
The company had retariffed its products before the economic crisis hit. = 在经济危机爆发之前，该公司已经对其产品重新征税。


将来时:
The government will retariff imported textiles next month. = 政府下个月将对进口纺织品重新征税。
They will retariff the goods if the negotiations fail. = 如果谈判失败，他们将对商品重新征税。


虚拟语气:
If they retariffed the goods, it would impact the consumers. = 如果他们对商品重新征税，将会影响消费者。
I wish the government hadn't retariffed those essential goods. = 我希望政府没有对那些必需品重新征税。


被动语态:
Imported cars were retariffed last year. = 进口汽车去年被重新征税了。
The tariffs on agricultural products are being retariffed currently. = 目前正在对农产品的关税进行重新制定。


疑问句:
Will the government retariff the steel imports? = 政府会对钢铁进口重新征税吗？
Have they retariffed the electronic components? = 他们是否已经对电子元件重新征税了？


否定句:
They did not retariff the goods as planned. = 他们没有按照计划对商品重新征税。
The government will not retariff the oil imports this year. = 政府今年不会对石油进口重新征税。
