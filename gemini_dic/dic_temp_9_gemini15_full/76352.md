gadders:/ˈɡædər z/| n. |闲逛的人；四处游荡的人|People who habitually go about from place to place, often for leisure or socializing.  They are often characterized by their aimless wandering and socializing.|The gadders spent the afternoon strolling through the park, chatting with friends and enjoying the sunshine.|近义词：wanderers, roamers, idlers, strollers|反义词：homebodies, recluses
