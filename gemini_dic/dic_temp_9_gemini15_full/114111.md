magnum opus:/ˈmæɡnəm ˈoʊpəs/| n.|杰作，代表作|A magnum opus is a great work of art, literature, or music, especially one that is considered to be the most important work of an artist or writer's career.  It is typically a substantial and significant achievement.|例句：His latest novel is considered to be his magnum opus.|近义词：masterpiece, chef-d'œuvre|反义词：flop, failure.
