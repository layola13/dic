layered: /ˈleɪərd/ | adj. |分层的，层状的 |Having several layers.  Something that is layered has several parts that are placed one on top of another. |例句：The cake was layered with chocolate and cream. 这蛋糕分层涂抹了巧克力和奶油。

例句：The geologist studied the layered rocks. 地质学家研究了分层的岩石。


近义词：stratified, multi-layered, tiered |反义词：uniform, single-layered


词源:  The word "layered" is derived from the noun "layer," which ultimately comes from the Old French word "laie," meaning "a layer of earth or stone."  The suffix "-ed" transforms the noun into an adjective, describing something possessing or characterized by layers.
