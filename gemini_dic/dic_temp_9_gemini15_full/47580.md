debasement:/dɪˈbeɪsmənt/| n.|贬低；贬值；堕落；降低身份|The act of debasing something, especially lowering its value, quality, or moral character.  It can refer to the reduction in the value of currency, the lowering of moral standards, or the degradation of someone's status or dignity.|例句：The debasement of the currency led to inflation. 通货贬值导致通货膨胀。

The debasement of political discourse is worrying many citizens.  政治言论的堕落让许多公民担忧。|近义词：degradation, depreciation, deterioration, corruption|反义词：elevation, enhancement, improvement, upliftment
