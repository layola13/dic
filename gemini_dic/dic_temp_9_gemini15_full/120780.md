misapprehensiveness:/ˌmɪsˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. | 误解；疑虑；猜疑 | A feeling or state of being apprehensive about something being misunderstood or misinterpreted; the state of being wrongly understood or interpreted.  It suggests a worry or anxiety about potential miscommunication or a lack of clarity. | 例句：His misapprehensiveness about the future led him to make hasty decisions.  他的对未来的疑虑导致他做出了草率的决定。

Her misapprehensiveness concerning the project's feasibility caused unnecessary delays. 她对项目可行性的疑虑造成了不必要的延误。


近义词：apprehension, anxiety, suspicion, doubt, uncertainty | 反义词：certainty, confidence, understanding, clarity |  词性：名词
