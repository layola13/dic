foreheads:/ˈfɔːrhedz/| n.|前额，额头|The part of the face above the eyes and below the hairline.  It is often used to describe the area of the head between the eyebrows and the hairline.|例句：He wiped the sweat from his forehead. 他擦去了额头上的汗水。|例句：She had a high forehead. 她额头很高。|近义词：brow|反义词：chin, jaw
