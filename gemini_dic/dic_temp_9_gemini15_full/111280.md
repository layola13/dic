littering:/ˈlɪtərɪŋ/| v.|乱扔垃圾，抛撒 |The act of throwing or dropping litter or rubbish in a public place.  Littering is a serious offense that can result in fines. |例句：Littering is against the law. 乱扔垃圾是违法的。|例句：Please don't litter on the streets. 请不要在街上乱扔垃圾。|近义词：strewn, scattering, discarding |反义词：cleaning, tidying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He litters the park every day. 他每天都在公园里乱扔垃圾。
They litter the streets with trash. 他们把垃圾扔得到处都是。

一般过去时:
She littered the beach with plastic bottles. 她把塑料瓶扔得到处都是海滩上。
We littered the campsite with food wrappers. 我们把食品包装纸乱扔在营地里。

现在完成时:
He has littered the neighborhood for years. 他多年来一直在附近乱扔垃圾。
They have littered the park with candy wrappers. 他们把糖纸乱扔在公园里。

过去完成时:
Before the cleanup, they had littered the area extensively. 清理之前，他们已经把这个区域弄得很脏了。
She had littered the streets before the city implemented the new laws. 在城市实施新法律之前，她已经在街上乱扔垃圾了。

将来时:
I will not litter in the park. 我不会在公园里乱扔垃圾。
They will litter the beach if we don't pick up our trash. 如果我们不捡垃圾，他们会在海滩上乱扔垃圾。

虚拟语气:
If he littered again, he would get a fine. 如果他再次乱扔垃圾，他将被罚款。
I wish they hadn't littered the beautiful park. 我真希望他们没有把美丽的公园弄脏。

被动语态:
The park was littered with trash. 公园里到处都是垃圾。
The streets are being littered with cigarette butts. 街上到处都是烟头。

疑问句:
Is he littering again? 他又在乱扔垃圾了吗？

否定句:
