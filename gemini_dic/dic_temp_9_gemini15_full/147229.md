overmeddles:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdəlz/| v.|过分干涉；多管闲事|To interfere excessively in the affairs of others.|例句：He overmeddles in everyone's business. 他事事都过分干涉。|例句：Don't overmeddle in matters that don't concern you. 不要干涉与你无关的事情。|近义词：interfere, meddle, butt in|反义词：mind one's own business

He overmeddles in his neighbor's affairs. = 他干涉他邻居的私事。
She often overmeddles in her children's lives. = 她经常干涉孩子的生活。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:
