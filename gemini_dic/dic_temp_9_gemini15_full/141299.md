open-mindedly:/ˈoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdli/ | adv. | 开阔地，虚心地，坦率地 |  Approaching situations or ideas with a willingness to consider different perspectives and possibilities, without prejudice or preconceived notions.  |  She listened open-mindedly to his criticisms. 她虚心听取了他的批评。 He approached the problem open-mindedly, considering all options. 他开阔地看待这个问题，考虑了所有方案。 |  tolerantly, impartially, receptively |  narrow-mindedly, dogmatically, rigidly
