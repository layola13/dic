fishwife:/ˈfɪʃwaɪf/| n. |鱼贩妇女；泼妇|A fishwife is a woman who sells fish, often implying she is loud, aggressive, and vulgar.  The term is now primarily used as a derogatory term for a shrewish or quarrelsome woman.|例句：She behaved like a right fishwife at the market, shouting at everyone. 她像个泼妇一样在市场上大喊大叫。|近义词：shrew, termagant, virago|反义词：lady, gentlewoman
