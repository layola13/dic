dabbler:/ˈdæblər/| n. |涉猎者；业余爱好者；浅尝辄止者 |A dabbler is someone who takes up an activity or subject without serious intention or commitment.  They explore it superficially rather than deeply. |例句：He's a dabbler in photography, only taking pictures occasionally. 他是个业余摄影爱好者，只是偶尔拍拍照。

例句：She's a dabbler in many fields, but doesn't excel in any.  她涉猎很多领域，但在任何领域都不擅长。

近义词：amateur, dilettante, superficialist

反义词：expert, professional, specialist

词性：名词
