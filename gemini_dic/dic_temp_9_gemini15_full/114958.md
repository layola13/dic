manaus:/məˈnaʊs/| n. |马瑙斯|Manaus is the capital city of the Brazilian state of Amazonas. It is located on the Rio Negro, near its confluence with the Amazon River.  The city is known for its role in the rubber boom of the late 19th and early 20th centuries and its current status as a major center for trade and transportation in the Amazon region. It's a bustling city with a mix of modern and historical architecture, and serves as a gateway to the Amazon rainforest.|例句：Manaus is a vibrant city in the heart of the Amazon.|近义词：Amazon (as a regional reference)|反义词：无
