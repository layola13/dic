food:/fuːd/| n.|食物，食品|Something that provides nourishment;  the substance that plants, animals, and humans consume to maintain life and growth.  It can be processed or unprocessed, and encompasses a vast range of edible substances.|例句：The food was delicious.  这食物真美味。|例句：We need to buy food for the week. 我们需要买一周的食物。|近义词：nourishment, sustenance, victuals|反义词：poison, toxin
