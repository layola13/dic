moth:/mɒθ/ /mɑːθ/| n. |飞蛾；蛀虫|A moth is a nocturnal flying insect, typically with nocturnal habits and attracted to light.  Moths are closely related to butterflies.  The term can also refer to the larvae of certain moths, which are destructive to clothes and other fabrics.|例句：The moth flew towards the lamp. 这只飞蛾飞向灯。|例句：The moths have eaten holes in my sweater. 飞蛾在我的毛衣上咬了洞。|近义词：butterfly (in some contexts), insect|反义词：N/A
