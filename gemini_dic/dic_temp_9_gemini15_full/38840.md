confarreation:/ˌkɒnfæriˈeɪʃən/ | n. |古罗马宗教婚礼 |The ancient Roman religious ceremony of marriage, involving the sharing of a sacred bread.  This was a highly significant ritual, symbolizing the union of the couple and their integration into Roman society.  Confarreation was considered a legal and religious bond stronger than other forms of marriage. | Example: Confarreation was the most sacred form of Roman marriage, reserved for patricians and emphasizing the importance of religious and social unity.  古罗马的联姻仪式，一种通过分享圣饼来完成的婚姻，这是非常重要的仪式，象征着夫妻的结合以及他们融入罗马社会。联姻被认为是一种比其他形式的婚姻更牢固的法律和宗教纽带。|近义词:marriage, wedding, nuptials |反义词:divorce, annulment |名词
