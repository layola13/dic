denotive: /ˈdiːnoʊtɪv/ | adj. | 指称的；表示意义的 | Denotative refers to the literal or dictionary definition of a word,  as opposed to its connotative meaning (the feelings or ideas associated with the word).  It focuses on the objective meaning, devoid of emotional or subjective interpretations. | Example: The denotative meaning of "home" is a place of residence.  However, its connotative meaning can be warmth, comfort, and security. |近义词: literal, explicit | 反义词: connotative, figurative | 词性: adjective
