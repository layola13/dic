defluxion:/dɪˈflʌkʃən/| n. |(医学)向下流动；(体液的)流注；(肿胀的)消退 |The downward flow of a fluid, especially pus or other morbid matter.  It often refers to the discharge of morbid humors from a superior to an inferior part of the body.  |例句：The physician observed a defluxion of pus from the infected wound.  医生观察到感染伤口有脓液流出。

The defluxion of the swelling relieved the pressure on the nerve. 肿胀消退减轻了对神经的压迫。|近义词：discharge, flow, effusion |反义词：influx, accumulation, congestion |名词
