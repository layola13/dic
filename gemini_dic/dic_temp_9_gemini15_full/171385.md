puck:/pʌk/| n.|冰球；顽皮鬼；小精灵|A puck is a flat, vulcanized rubber disc used in ice hockey.  It can also refer to a mischievous sprite or goblin in folklore.|例句：The hockey player skillfully shot the puck into the net.  冰球运动员熟练地将冰球射入球网。|例句：Shakespeare's Puck is a mischievous sprite from A Midsummer Night's Dream.  莎士比亚笔下的Puck是《仲夏夜之梦》中一个顽皮的小精灵。|近义词：disc, washer (in the hockey sense); imp, sprite, goblin (in the folklore sense)|反义词：N/A|名词
