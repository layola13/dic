non-retirement:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtaɪərmənt/|/ˌnɑːn rɪˈtaɪərmənt/| n. |非退休；继续工作；工作生涯|The state or period of not being retired; continued employment after the typical retirement age.  It often refers to the active lifestyle and continued engagement in work or other pursuits after the traditional retirement age.|Example:  He embraced non-retirement, taking on a part-time consulting role. 他欣然接受了非退休生活，担任了一份兼职咨询工作。|Example: Many people are choosing non-retirement and extending their working lives. 许多人选择不退休，延长他们的工作寿命。|近义词：continued employment, post-retirement work, extended working life|反义词：retirement
