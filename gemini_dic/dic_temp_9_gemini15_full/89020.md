holly:/ˈhɒli/ /ˈhɑːli/| n. |冬青树，冬青属植物|An evergreen shrub or small tree with spiky leaves and bright red berries, often used as a Christmas decoration.  |The holly berries are poisonous if eaten.|近义词：evergreen, holm oak|反义词：无
