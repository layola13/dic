fattiest:/ˈfætiːɪst/| adj.|最肥的；最胖的；含脂肪最多的 |Most fat; containing the most fat.  This word is the superlative form of "fat". It describes something or someone possessing the greatest amount of fat.|例句：That's the fattiest steak I've ever seen. = 那是我看过最肥的牛排。

The pig is the fattiest animal on the farm. = 猪是农场上最肥的动物。|近义词：fattest, most corpulent, most obese |反义词：leanest, thinnest
