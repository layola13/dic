short-circuit:/ʃɔːrtˈsɜːkɪt/|/ˈʃɔːrtˌsɜːrkɪt/| n. v.|短路；中断；使…短路|The act or instance of an electric current taking a shorter, less-resistive path than intended, usually causing excessive heating and malfunction.  It can also refer to a situation where a process is bypassed or interrupted prematurely.|例句：The power went out because of a short circuit. = 由于短路，电源断了。
The negotiations short-circuited when the two sides couldn't agree on a price. = 当双方就价格无法达成一致时，谈判中断。|近义词：bypass, shortcut, interrupt|反义词：completion, continuity


USAGE->

一般现在时:
A short circuit occurs when there is a unintended connection between two points of different electric potential. = 当两个不同电位的点之间存在意外连接时，就会发生短路。
The system short-circuits frequently due to poor design. =  由于设计不良，系统经常短路。


一般过去时:
The old wiring short-circuited, causing a fire. = 旧线路短路，引发了火灾。
The project short-circuited due to lack of funding. =  由于资金短缺，项目夭折了。


现在完成时:
The computer has short-circuited twice this week. = 这台电脑本周已经短路了两次。
The negotiations have short-circuited, leading to a deadlock. = 谈判破裂，导致僵局。


过去完成时:
The lights had short-circuited before the storm hit. =  暴风雨来临之前，灯就已经短路了。
The deal had short-circuited because of a disagreement over terms. =  由于对条款存在分歧，交易已经失败了。


将来时:
The overloaded circuit will short-circuit if we don't fix it. = 如果我们不修理，超载的电路将会短路。
If we don't address the issues, the peace talks will short-circuit. = 如果我们不解决这些问题，和平谈判将会破裂。


虚拟语气:
If the wire had not been damaged, the circuit wouldn't have short-circuited. = 如果电线没有损坏，电路就不会短路。
If we had anticipated the disagreement, we could have prevented the negotiations from short-circuiting. = 如果我们预料到分歧，我们就能防止谈判破裂。


被动语态:
The appliance was short-circuited by a surge of power. =  电器因电源浪涌而短路。
The process was short-circuited by unexpected delays. =  该过程因意外延误而中断。


疑问句:
Did the power outage result from a short circuit? =  停电是由于短路造成的吗？
Has the project short-circuited due to unforeseen circumstances? = 项目由于不可预见的情况而中断了吗？


否定句:
The circuit didn't short-circuit, despite the heavy load. =  尽管负载很重，电路却没有短路。
The negotiations didn't short-circuit; they reached an agreement. = 谈判没有破裂；他们达成了协议。
