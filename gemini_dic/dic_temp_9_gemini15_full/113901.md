madrigalesque:/ˌmædrɪɡəˈlesk/| adj. |麦德利格风格的，具有麦德利格特点的 |Resembling or characteristic of a madrigal; having the qualities of a madrigal, such as sweetness, grace, and intricacy.  The term often describes music, literature, or art that evokes the spirit and style of madrigals. |The painting had a madrigalesque quality, with its delicate colors and intricate details. 这幅画具有麦德利格风格，色彩细腻，细节精巧。The novel's prose was surprisingly madrigalesque, full of lyrical beauty and playful wordplay. 这部小说的散文出人意料地具有麦德利格风格，充满了抒情的美感和俏皮的文字游戏。|近义词：lyrical, melodious, ornate, elegant |反义词：crude, harsh, simple, plain
