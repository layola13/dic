dock:/dɒk/| /dɑːk/| n./v.|码头；船坞；（使）停靠|A dock is a structure alongside which ships can load and unload.  It can also refer to a place where a vehicle is repaired or serviced. As a verb, it means to bring a ship or boat into a dock, or to connect or disconnect something.|例句：The ship docked at the port this morning.  这艘船今天早上停靠在港口。
The mechanic docked the car to check the engine. 技师把车停进车坞检查发动机。|近义词：pier, wharf, harbor, berth|反义词：undock, depart
