deoxidate:/ˌdiːˈɒksɪdeɪt/| vt.|脱氧，除氧|To remove oxygen from a substance.|例句：The process deoxidates the metal, making it more malleable.  这个过程使金属脱氧，使其更易延展。例句：The blood was deoxidated before being transfused. 血液在输血前已脱氧。|近义词：reduce, deoxygenate|反义词：oxidize, oxygenate|动词
