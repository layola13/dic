nonprofits:/ˈnɒnˌprɒfɪts/| n. |非营利组织|Organizations that operate for purposes other than generating profit, often focusing on social or environmental causes. They typically rely on donations, grants, and fundraising to support their activities.  |Example: Many nonprofits are dedicated to helping the homeless. 许多非营利组织致力于帮助无家可归者。|近义词：charitable organizations, NGOs (non-governmental organizations), charities |反义词：for-profit organizations, businesses, corporations|名词
