cottages:/ˈkɒtɪdʒɪz/| n. |小屋，村舍 |A cottage is a small house, typically one in the countryside.  Cottages are often charming and quaint, and may be made of stone, wood, or brick. |例句：They spent their honeymoon in a charming cottage by the sea. 他们在海边一间迷人的小屋度蜜月。|例句：Many cottages in the village are centuries old. 村里许多小屋已有数百年历史。|近义词：cabin, hut, chalet |反义词：mansion, palace


USAGE->

They rent out cottages during the summer. = 他们在夏天出租小屋。
The cottages are beautifully decorated. = 小屋装饰精美。

一般过去时:
