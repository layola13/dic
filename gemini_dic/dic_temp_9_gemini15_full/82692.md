guey:/ɡeɪ/| n.|家伙，哥们儿（俚语）|A slang term, primarily used in Latino communities, often used as a term of endearment or to address a friend.  It can also be used in a derogatory way depending on tone and context.|例句：Hey guey, what's up? = 嘿，哥们儿，怎么了？  例句：That guey is always causing trouble. = 那家伙总是惹麻烦。|近义词：dude, man, bro, pal|反义词：无|名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily used in informal settings and doesn't readily lend itself to grammatical tense changes in the way verbs do.  Its use is largely confined to present tense.


PHRASE->
There aren't common set phrases using "guey" in the same way there are with words like "what". The word's usage is context-dependent and more about tone and relationship between speakers.
