practically:/ˈpræktɪkli/ | adv. |实际上，几乎，差不多 | In a practical way; almost; virtually.  |例句：Practically everyone agreed with the proposal.  几乎每个人都同意这项提案。

例句：It's practically impossible to finish the project in a week. 完成这个项目一周内几乎是不可能的。


近义词：virtually, almost, nearly, substantially | 反义词：theoretically, ideally, impossibly


词源:  Derived from "practical," ultimately from the Latin "practica," meaning "practice" or "doing."  The adverbial suffix "-ly" was added to form "practically."

USAGE->

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
