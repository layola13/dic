dreamiest: /ˈdriːmiːɪst/ | adj. | 最梦幻的，最梦一般的 | Most dreamlike; most suggestive of dreams.  It is the superlative form of "dreamy".  It implies an extreme degree of the qualities associated with dreams – often involving beauty,  unreality, or a sense of longing. |  The dreamiest sunset I've ever seen painted the sky in vibrant hues.  = 我见过的最梦幻的日落用鲜艳的色彩描绘了天空。

The cafe had the dreamiest atmosphere, filled with soft lighting and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee. = 咖啡馆有着最梦幻的氛围，充满了柔和的灯光和新鲜冲泡咖啡的香气。


近义词: most enchanting, most idyllic, most magical, most fantastical | 反义词: most realistic, most mundane, most ordinary
