overtorturing: /ˌoʊvərˈtɔːrtʃərɪŋ/ | v. |过度折磨；过度拷问 |  The act of torturing excessively or beyond what is necessary or justifiable. It implies a cruelty and prolonged nature of the torture.  This word is not commonly used and often sounds overly dramatic or literary. | Example: The prisoner accused the guards of overtorturing him to extract a confession. = 囚犯指控狱警为了逼他认罪而对他进行了过度折磨。  | 近义词：excessive torture, brutalizing, tormenting,  | 反义词：leniency, humane treatment |  动词
