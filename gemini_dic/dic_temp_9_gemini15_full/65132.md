ethicist:/ˈɛθɪsɪst/| n. |伦理学家，道德学家 |A person who specializes in ethics.  They study moral principles and values, and often apply these principles to real-world problems.  |例句：The ethicist argued that the company's actions were morally reprehensible.  伦理学家认为该公司的行为在道德上令人谴责。

例句：Many ethicists are involved in debates about artificial intelligence and its implications.  许多伦理学家都参与了关于人工智能及其影响的辩论。


近义词：moral philosopher, moralist


反义词：None directly opposite, but someone who consistently ignores ethical considerations could be considered an antonym in a contextual sense.


词性：名词
