satinet:/ˌsætɪˈnet/| n. |细棉布，细斜纹布 |A lightweight fabric, usually cotton, with a smooth, lustrous surface. It's often used for linings and inexpensive dresses.  |例句：The dress was lined with a soft satinet. 这件裙子内衬是柔软的细棉布。

例句：She chose a satinet fabric for the curtains because of its sheen. 她选择细斜纹布做窗帘是因为它的光泽。


近义词：cambric, muslin, poplin |反义词：None specifically, but heavier fabrics like denim or tweed are opposites in weight and texture.
