esteemer: /ɪˈstiːmər/ | n. | 尊重者，敬重者 | One who esteems; one who holds in high regard.  |  He is an esteemer of fine art and classical music. 他是一位对美术和古典音乐推崇备至的人。 | 近义词：admirer, enthusiast, devotee | 反义词：despiser, critic, detractor | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher esteems her students' hard work. = 老师很尊重学生们的努力。
He esteems honesty above all else. = 他最看重诚实。


一般过去时:
She esteemed her grandmother's wisdom. = 她很尊重祖母的智慧。
They esteemed his contributions to the project. = 他们很看重他对项目的贡献。


现在完成时:
I have always esteemed your opinion. = 我一直很尊重你的意见。
We have esteemed their efforts to improve the community. = 我们一直很看重他们为改善社区所做的努力。


过去完成时:
She had esteemed his character before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，她一直很尊重他的品格。
They had esteemed his judgment until they saw the evidence. = 直到他们看到证据之前，他们一直很尊重他的判断。


将来时:
I will always esteem your friendship. = 我将永远珍惜我们的友谊。
They will esteem the artist's new collection. = 他们将欣赏这位艺术家的新作品系列。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would esteem their advice. = 如果我是你，我会重视他们的建议。
I wish I had esteemed his warning sooner. = 我真希望我早点重视他的警告。


被动语态:
His work is esteemed by many experts. = 他的作品受到许多专家的推崇。
Her kindness is esteemed by everyone who knows her. = 认识她的人都非常敬重她的善良。


疑问句:
Do you esteem their achievements? = 你尊重他们的成就吗？
Does he esteem the value of education? = 他重视教育的价值吗？


否定句:
I do not esteem his methods. = 我不赞同他的方法。
They did not esteem his opinion in the matter. = 他们在这件事上并不重视他的意见。


PHRASE->
hold in esteem = 尊重
have high esteem for = 对...高度评价
esteem someone highly = 高度评价某人
show esteem for = 表示对...的敬重
in high esteem = 受人尊敬
