elimination:/ɪˌlɪmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |消除；排除；淘汰 | The act of removing or getting rid of something.  This can refer to removing something unwanted, or a process of removing options to arrive at a solution.  |Example: The elimination of poverty is a major goal. 消除贫困是一个主要目标。


Example: The elimination of the suspect from the investigation was a mistake. 从调查中排除嫌疑犯是一个错误。


近义词：removal, exclusion, eradication, deletion | 反义词：inclusion, addition, retention
