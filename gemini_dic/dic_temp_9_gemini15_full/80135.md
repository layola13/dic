goby:/ˈɡoʊbi/ | n. |虾虎鱼 | A small fish that lives on the bottom of the sea or in rivers.  Many species are known for their symbiotic relationships with other organisms, such as pistol shrimps. | Example:  The tide pool teemed with gobies and anemones. 潮池里挤满了虾虎鱼和海葵。  |近义词:  small fish, bottom-dweller |反义词:  whale, shark |名词
