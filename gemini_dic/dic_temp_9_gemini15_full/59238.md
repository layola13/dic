e-bike:/ˈiːbaɪk/| n.|电动自行车|An electric bicycle, also known as an e-bike, is a bicycle equipped with an electric motor which assists propulsion.  E-bikes offer pedal assist, meaning the motor only engages when the rider is pedaling, or throttle-only operation, where the motor powers the bike without pedaling.|例句：I commute to work on my e-bike every day. 我每天都骑电动自行车上班。|例句：E-bikes are becoming increasingly popular as a form of sustainable transportation. 电动自行车作为一种可持续的交通工具正变得越来越流行。|近义词：electric bicycle, power bike|反义词：bicycle (non-electric)
