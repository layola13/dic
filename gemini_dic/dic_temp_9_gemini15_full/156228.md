phthisicky:/ˈfɪzɪki/| adj.|痨病的，消瘦的，虚弱的|Characterized by or suggestive of phthisis (consumption, tuberculosis); wasted or emaciated.  The word evokes a sense of fragility and illness associated with tuberculosis.|The phthisicky patient coughed constantly, a chilling sound that echoed through the ward.  这位肺结核病人不停地咳嗽，那种令人不寒而栗的声音在病房里回荡。|近义词：emaciated, wasted, consumptive, sickly|反义词：healthy, robust, strong
