pulverization: /ˌpʌlvərɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 粉碎；研磨成粉；彻底破坏 | The process of reducing something to powder or dust;  the act of completely destroying something.  | 例句：The pulverization of the rock was achieved using a powerful jackhammer.  岩石的粉碎是用一台强大的凿岩机完成的。

The pulverization of the opposition party's campaign left them with little hope of victory. 反对党的竞选活动被彻底摧毁，使他们几乎没有获胜的希望。| 近义词：comminution, grinding, crushing, disintegration | 反义词：aggregation, consolidation, construction
