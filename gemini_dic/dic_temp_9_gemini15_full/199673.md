sinusoid:/ˈsaɪnəsɔɪd/ | n. |正弦曲线，正弦波| A curve representing a sine wave; a smooth wave-like curve.  A sinusoid is a mathematical curve that describes a smooth periodic oscillation. It's characterized by its wave-like shape, which repeats itself regularly over time or space.  This shape is fundamental in many areas of science and engineering, representing phenomena like sound waves, alternating current, and simple harmonic motion. | Example: The electrocardiogram showed a normal sinusoid rhythm. 心电图显示正常的窦性心律。 The sound wave was modeled as a sinusoid. 声波被建模为正弦波。|近义词：sine wave, sinusoidal curve |反义词：None (it describes a shape, not a concept with a direct opposite)|
