close-fitting:/ˌkloʊsˈfɪtɪŋ/| adj. | 贴身的，紧身的 |  Close-fitting describes something that fits the body closely. It emphasizes a snug and tight fit, often suggesting a form-fitting garment. | Example:  She wore a close-fitting dress to the party. 她穿着一件贴身连衣裙去参加派对。  | 近义词：tight-fitting, snug, form-fitting | 反义词：loose-fitting, baggy, oversized |  形容词
