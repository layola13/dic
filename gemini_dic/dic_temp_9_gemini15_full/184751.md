resumeing:/rɪˈzuːmɪŋ/ | v. |继续；重新开始；总结；概述 | Continuing or recommencing an activity; summarizing or outlining. |例句：He resumed reading after a brief interruption. 他短暂中断后继续阅读。 |例句：The meeting resumed after a coffee break. 会议在咖啡休息后继续。|近义词：continuing, recommencing, restarting, summarizing |反义词：stopping, ceasing, interrupting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resuming his studies. = 他正在继续他的学业。
She is resuming her work after maternity leave. = 她产假后重新开始工作。

一般过去时:
He resumed his work after lunch. = 他午饭后继续工作。
She resumed her seat after answering the question. = 她回答完问题后重新坐下。

现在完成时:
They have resumed their negotiations. = 他们已经恢复了谈判。
He has resumed his training after his injury. = 他在受伤后恢复了训练。

过去完成时:
They had resumed their journey before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临前已经继续他们的旅程。
She had resumed her writing after a long break. = 她在长时间休息后恢复了写作。

将来时:
He will resume his position next month. = 他下个月将恢复他的职位。
She will resume her duties tomorrow. = 她明天将恢复她的职责。

虚拟语气:
If he resumed his studies, he would graduate sooner. = 如果他继续他的学业，他就能早点毕业。
I wish she resumed her painting. = 我希望她能继续她的绘画。

被动语态:
The meeting was resumed after a short delay. = 会议在短暂延迟后恢复了。
The project was resumed after funding was secured. = 项目在资金到位后恢复了。

疑问句:
When will he resume his job? = 他什么时候恢复工作？
Has she resumed her exercise routine? = 她恢复她的运动习惯了吗？

否定句:
He did not resume his work until the next day. = 他直到第二天才恢复工作。
She has not yet resumed her travel plans. = 她还没有恢复她的旅行计划。
