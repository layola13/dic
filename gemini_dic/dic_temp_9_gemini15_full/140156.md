offendedness:/əˈfɛndɪdnəs/| n. |冒犯；生气；不悦|The feeling of being offended; resentment at having been insulted or injured.  It often involves a sense of injustice or hurt pride.|例句：His blatant disregard for her feelings caused considerable offendedness.  他的公然无视她的感受引起了相当的不悦。

例句：The offendedness she felt lingered long after the apology.  她所感受到的冒犯感在道歉很久之后仍然挥之不去。


近义词：resentment, indignation, umbrage, displeasure, hurt feelings

反义词：pleasure, satisfaction, contentment


词性:名词
