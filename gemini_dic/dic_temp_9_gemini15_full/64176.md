equations:/ɪˈkweɪʒənz/ | n. | 方程式，等式 | Mathematical statements that assert the equality of two expressions.  Equations are used to represent relationships between variables and constants.  They are fundamental to many areas of mathematics and science. | Example: The equation x + 2 = 5 has a solution of x = 3.  这个方程式x + 2 = 5 的解是x = 3。 |近义词： formulas, expressions | 反义词：inequalities
