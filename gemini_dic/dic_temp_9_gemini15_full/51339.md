detecting:/dɪˈtɛktɪŋ/| v. |探测；发现；查明|The act of discovering something hidden or concealed, often through the use of special equipment or techniques. It implies a process of searching and identifying something that is not immediately obvious.|例句：The police are detecting a rise in crime rates.  警方正在侦测犯罪率上升。|例句：The sensor is detecting a leak in the pipe. 传感器检测到管道漏水。|近义词：discovering, finding, identifying, locating|反义词：missing, overlooking, ignoring|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is detecting a problem. = 系统正在检测到一个问题。
She detects a hint of sarcasm in his voice. = 她察觉到他语气中有一丝讽刺。


一般过去时:
The doctor detected a heart murmur. = 医生发现了一个心脏杂音。
They detected a flaw in the design. = 他们发现设计中存在缺陷。


现在完成时:
Scientists have detected a new planet. = 科学家们已经探测到一颗新的行星。
We have detected no errors in the program. = 我们没有检测到程序中的任何错误。


过去完成时:
They had detected the signal before the interference began. = 干扰开始之前，他们就已经探测到信号了。
The researchers had detected a pattern in the data. = 研究人员已经发现了数据中的模式。


将来时:

We will detect the problem early on. = 我们将尽早发现问题。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
