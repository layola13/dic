depopulated:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjuleɪtɪd/| adj.|人口减少的，荒无人烟的| emptied of people; lacking inhabitants.  The once-bustling town is now depopulated.  |例句：The village has become depopulated due to the drought. 这个村庄由于干旱而人口减少了。

例句：After the war, the city was largely depopulated. 战争之后，这座城市人口大量减少。


近义词：deserted, uninhabited, sparsely populated, empty

反义词：populated, densely populated, thriving


词性：形容词
