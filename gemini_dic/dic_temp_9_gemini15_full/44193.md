croaking:/ˈkroʊkɪŋ/| /ˈkrəʊkɪŋ/| adj., n., v.|呱呱叫声；嘶哑的声音；（声音）沙哑的|The sound of a frog or toad, or a similar harsh, low sound.|例句：The frogs were croaking loudly in the pond.  青蛙在池塘里大声呱呱叫。

The old woman's voice was a croaking whisper. 老妇人的声音像嘶哑的低语。


近义词：grunting, rasping, quacking| 反义词：melodious, clear


词性:  adj., n., v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The frogs are croaking in the night.  青蛙在晚上呱呱叫。
He croaks every time he tries to sing. 他每次唱歌都声音沙哑。


一般过去时:
The frogs croaked all night long. 青蛙整夜呱呱叫。
He croaked out a reply. 他沙哑地回答。


现在完成时:
The frogs have been croaking since sunset. 日落后青蛙一直在呱呱叫。
He has croaked all day due to a sore throat. 他因为喉咙痛整天声音都沙哑。


过去完成时:
The frogs had croaked for hours before I went to sleep.  在我睡觉前青蛙已经叫了好几个小时了。
He had croaked so much that he was hoarse. 他叫得太多，嗓子都哑了。


将来时:
The frogs will croak even louder tomorrow night. 明晚青蛙会叫得更大声。
He will probably croak again if he keeps singing. 如果他继续唱歌，他可能还会再次声音沙哑。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't smoke so much, he wouldn't croak so much. 如果他不抽那么多烟，就不会那么沙哑了。
I wish I hadn't croaked during my presentation. 我希望我在演示时没有声音沙哑。


被动语态:
His voice was croaked by a bad cold. 他的声音被重感冒弄哑了。
The sound was croaked out by the old gramophone.  旧留声机发出的声音沙哑。


疑问句:
Are the frogs croaking again? 青蛙又在叫了吗？
Is your voice still croaking? 你的声音还沙哑吗？


否定句:
The frogs weren't croaking last night.  昨夜青蛙没有叫。
My voice isn't croaking today. 我的声音今天不沙哑。
