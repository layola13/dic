impenetrable:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbl/ | adj. | 不可渗透的；难以理解的；不可克服的 |  Impossible to penetrate or pass through; incapable of being understood; insuperable. | 例句：The fortress was impenetrable.  = 这座堡垒坚不可摧。

The professor's lecture was completely impenetrable to the students. = 教授的讲座学生们完全听不懂。


近义词：impervious, unyielding, incomprehensible, inscrutable | 反义词：permeable, transparent, comprehensible, understandable


词源:  From Old French *impenetrable, from Latin impenetrabilis, from in- "not" + penetrabilis "penetrable," from penetrare "to penetrate."
