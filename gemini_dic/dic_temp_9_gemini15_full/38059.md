composing:/kəmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ | v. |创作；作曲；构成；使镇定 |to create or produce something, especially music, writing, or a work of art| 例句：She's composing a symphony. = 她正在创作一首交响乐。

She's composing a letter to her friend. = 她正在给朋友写信。


近义词：creating, writing, forming, constructing | 反义词：decomposing, destroying

词源:  源自古法语 *composer  "to put together, arrange," 来自拉丁语 *componere "to put together,"  由 *com- "together" + *ponere "to put"  构成。
