overshake:/ˈoʊvərʃeɪk/| vt.|过度摇晃；剧烈摇动|To shake something too much or violently.|例句：Don't overshake the cocktail, or it will become frothy. 不要过度摇晃鸡尾酒，否则会变得泡沫状。|例句：The earthquake overshook the entire city. 地震剧烈震动了整个城市。|近义词：shake violently, agitate excessively|反义词：under shake (not a common word)|动词
