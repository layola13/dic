oompahed:/ˈʊmpɑːd/| v.|发出翁帕的声音，模仿翁帕人的声音|The word "oompahed" is a verb formed from the onomatopoeia "oompa,"  associated with the Oompa Loompas from Roald Dahl's *Charlie and the Chocolate Factory*.  It describes the act of making the characteristic singing or chanting sound of the Oompa Loompas.  The "-ed" suffix indicates past tense.|例句：The children oompahed along to the song. 孩子们随着歌曲发出翁帕的声音。The actor oompahed convincingly.  演员模仿翁帕人的声音很逼真。|近义词：chanted, hummed, sang|反义词：shouted, screamed.
