driftweed:/ˈdrɪftwiːd/| n. |漂浮物，海藻 |Seaweed or other vegetation that has been detached from its roots and is floating on the water.  Driftweed often accumulates on beaches. |例句：The beach was littered with driftweed after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上布满了漂浮物。|例句：The children collected colorful shells and pieces of driftweed during their beach walk. 孩子们在海滩散步时收集了五颜六色的贝壳和漂浮物。|近义词：seaweed, flotsam, jetsam |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with anchored vegetation.
