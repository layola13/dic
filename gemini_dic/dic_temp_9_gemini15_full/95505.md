incline-planes:/ˈɪnklaɪn pleɪnz/| n.|斜面，倾斜平面|A plane surface that makes an angle with the horizontal; a ramp.  It is used to move heavy objects up or down with less effort.|例句：We used an incline-plane to move the heavy piano into the house. 我们用斜面把沉重的钢琴搬进了房子。|例句：The steep incline-plane made it difficult to climb.  陡峭的斜面使得攀登很困难。|近义词：ramp, slope, inclined plane|反义词：vertical plane, horizontal plane
