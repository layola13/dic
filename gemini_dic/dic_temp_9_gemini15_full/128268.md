neuropathic:/ˌnʊərəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. |神经病的，神经痛的 | Relating to or caused by disease of the nerves.  Neuropathic pain is caused by damage or dysfunction in the nervous system.  |例句：She suffers from neuropathic pain in her legs. 她腿部患有神经性疼痛。|例句：The doctor diagnosed his condition as neuropathic. 医生诊断他的病情为神经病变。|近义词：neurological, neuralgic |反义词：non-neuropathic
