noncosmopolitan:/ˌnɑːnˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/| adj. |非世界性的；地方性的；不普遍的 |Not cosmopolitan; provincial; lacking in worldly experience or sophistication.  The term suggests a lack of exposure to diverse cultures and perspectives, often implying a narrow or limited worldview. |例句：His views on international relations were rather noncosmopolitan, reflecting his limited exposure to global affairs.他的国际关系观点相当非世界性的，反映了他对全球事务的有限了解。

例句：The small town's noncosmopolitan atmosphere was a stark contrast to the vibrant multiculturalism of the capital city. 这个小镇非世界性的氛围与首都充满活力的多元文化形成了鲜明对比。


近义词：provincial, parochial, insular, local, limited


反义词：cosmopolitan, international, worldly, global, universal


词性：形容词
