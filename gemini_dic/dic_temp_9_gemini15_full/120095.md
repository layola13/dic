mimeograph:/ˈmaɪmioʊɡræf/| n. |油印机；油印品|A mimeograph is a machine that produces copies of documents using a stencil and ink.  It was a common method of duplication before the advent of photocopying.|Example: The teacher used a mimeograph to make copies of the worksheet for her students.老师用油印机为学生复印了工作表。|近义词：duplicator, copier|反义词：None
