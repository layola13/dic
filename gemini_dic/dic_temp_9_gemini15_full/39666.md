conservatories:/kənˈsɜːrvətɔːriz/| n. | 温室；音乐学院；(尤指英国的)私立学校 | A conservatory is a building, often made of glass, used for growing plants, especially delicate ones, that need protection from bad weather. It can also refer to a specialized school, particularly for music or the performing arts, often private and providing advanced training. In Britain, it can also refer to certain types of private schools. | 例句：The conservatory was filled with exotic orchids.  温室里充满了奇异的兰花。

The Royal Conservatory of Music is highly regarded. 皇家音乐学院享有盛誉。


近义词：greenhouse, hothouse, music school, academy | 反义词：open field, public school | 名词
