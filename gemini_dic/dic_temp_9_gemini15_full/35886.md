cocomposer:/koʊˈkɑːmpoʊzər/| n. |共同作曲者，合作作曲家 |A cocomposer is a person who composes music collaboratively with one or more other people.  They share the creative process and contribute equally to the final composition. |Example:  The two musicians worked as cocomposers on the film's score.  两位音乐家合作创作了这部电影的配乐。|近义词：collaborator, joint composer |反义词：sole composer, individual composer
