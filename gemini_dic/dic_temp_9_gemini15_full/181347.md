reinvasion:/ˌriːɪnˈveɪʒən/ | n. | 再入侵，重新侵略 | The act of invading again; a second invasion.  It implies a previous invasion has already occurred. | Example: The fear of a reinvasion hung over the country after the war.  战后，再次入侵的恐惧笼罩着这个国家。


The reinvasion of the territory was met with fierce resistance.  对该领土的再次入侵遭到了强烈的抵抗。|近义词：reoccupation, reconquest |反义词：withdrawal, liberation
