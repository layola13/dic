reemigrating:/ˌriːˈeɪməɡreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再移民；重新移民 | The act of migrating back to a country one has previously emigrated from.  It involves returning to one's former home country after having lived in another country for a period. | Example: After spending twenty years in Canada, she is reemigrating to her native Ireland.  = 在加拿大生活了二十年后，她要重新移民回她的祖国爱尔兰。  |近义词: returning, remigrating | 反义词: emigrating, immigrating | 动词
