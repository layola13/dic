nemertes:/nəˈmɜːrtiːz/| n. |纽形动物| ribbon worm; a long, flat, soft-bodied worm of the phylum Nemertea, many of which are predatory.  They are characterized by a long proboscis used for capturing prey. | Example: The nemertean emerged from the sand, its proboscis extended.  纽形动物从沙中钻出，它的吻伸了出来。
