eldorado:/ˌɛl dəˈrɑːdoʊ/| n. |黄金之国；理想乡；充满财富的地方 |A mythical place of great wealth, usually associated with gold.  The name is often applied to places believed to be rich in gold or other valuable resources.| 例句：The conquistadors searched for El Dorado for years, believing it to be a land overflowing with gold.  征服者们多年来一直在寻找黄金国，相信那里遍地都是黄金。

例句：Many people see success as their personal El Dorado, a place where all their dreams come true. 许多人把成功视为他们个人的黄金国，一个他们所有梦想成真的地方。|近义词：paradise, utopia, Shangri-La |反义词：desolation, wasteland
