pseudo-Russians: /ˈsuːdoʊˈrʌʃənz/ | n. |假俄罗斯人；冒充俄罗斯人的人 |  Those who falsely claim Russian identity or affiliation, often for political or personal gain.  This term often refers to individuals or groups engaging in disinformation campaigns, cyberattacks, or other malicious activities while pretending to be associated with Russia.  The term carries a strong negative connotation. | Example:  The investigation revealed that the so-called "pseudo-Russians" behind the cyberattack were actually based in a different country. = 调查显示，实施网络攻击的所谓“假俄罗斯人”实际上来自另一个国家。  | 近义词:  imposters, deceivers, fraudsters | 反义词: genuine Russians, authentic Russians | 名词
