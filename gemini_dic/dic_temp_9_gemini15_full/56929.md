dotingness: /ˈdoʊtɪŋnəs/ | n. |溺爱；过度溺爱 |The excessive fondness or love of a parent for a child.  It often implies an overindulgent and potentially unhealthy level of affection.  This can lead to the child becoming spoiled or lacking independence. |例句：Her dotingness on her grandson bordered on obsession.  她的溺爱孙子简直到了痴迷的地步。


例句：The child's dotingness was evident in his constant clinging to his mother.  孩子的溺爱在他不断依偎着母亲的行为中显而易见。


近义词：overindulgence, pampering, spoiling, excessive affection


反义词：neglect, indifference, harshness, strictness
