conservatorial:/ˌkɒnsɜːvəˈtɔːriəl/| adj.| 保守的；保护性的；馆长的 | Relating to or characteristic of a conservator or conservation;  showing a tendency to preserve traditional values and customs.  |例句：The conservatorial approach to the restoration prioritized preserving the original materials.  =  修复工作采取了保守的做法，优先考虑保留原始材料。

The museum's conservatorial staff meticulously cleaned and preserved the artifacts. = 博物馆的馆长工作人员一丝不苟地清洁和保存文物。


近义词：preservationist, protectionist, traditionalist | 反义词：radical, revolutionary, avant-garde
