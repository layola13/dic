dreams: /driːmz/ | n. |梦，梦想，愿望 |  Dreams are a series of thoughts, images, and sensations occurring in a person's mind during sleep. They can be vivid and emotional, or vague and fleeting.  | Example: I had a strange dream last night. 我昨晚做了一个奇怪的梦。  I have dreams of becoming a doctor. 我梦想成为一名医生。|近义词：visions, aspirations, fantasies | 反义词：reality, actuality


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dreams of a better future. 他梦想拥有一个更美好的未来。
She dreams every night. 她每晚都做梦。

一般过去时:
I dreamt of flying last night. 我昨晚梦见自己在飞。
They dreamed of a peaceful world.  他们梦想一个和平的世界。

现在完成时:
I have dreamed of this moment for years. 我多年来一直梦想着这一刻。
She has dreamt of traveling the world. 她一直梦想着环游世界。

过去完成时:
I had dreamt of success before I started my business. 在创业之前，我就梦想着成功。
He had dreamed of owning a house since he was a child. 他从小就梦想拥有一个房子。

将来时:
I will dream of you tonight. 今晚我会梦见你。
She will dream big dreams. 她将怀揣伟大的梦想。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could fly, like I dreamt I did last night. 我希望我能像昨晚梦里一样飞翔。
If I were rich, I would fulfill all my dreams. 如果我有钱，我会实现我所有的梦想。


被动语态:
(Rarely used passively, as dreams are typically experienced actively.)  The future is often dreamt of. 未来经常被人们梦想。
His dreams were interpreted by a shaman. 他的梦被萨满解释了。


疑问句:
What did you dream about last night? 你昨晚梦到什么了？
Do you often dream? 你经常做梦吗？


否定句:
I didn't dream at all last night. 我昨晚一点都没做梦。
She doesn't dream very often. 她不经常做梦。
