non-credibility:/ˌnɒnˌkredəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可信；不可靠性 | The lack of credibility; the state of not being believable or trustworthy.  It refers to a situation or statement that is not considered believable or reliable.  | Example: The non-credibility of the witness's testimony led to the dismissal of the case.  证人的证词不可信导致案件被驳回。  The politician's repeated lies contributed to his growing non-credibility.  这位政客的反复谎言导致了他的声誉越来越不可信。 | 近义词：unreliability, untrustworthiness, implausibility | 反义词：credibility, trustworthiness, reliability | 名词
