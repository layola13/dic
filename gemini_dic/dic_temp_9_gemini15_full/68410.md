falsetto: /fælˈsɛtoʊ/ | n. | 假声，假音 | A voice register characterized by a high pitch produced by vibrating the vocal cords in a thin, tense manner.  It's typically higher than the normal speaking voice. | 例句：He sang the high notes in falsetto. 他用假声唱高音。


例句：The tenor used falsetto to reach the highest notes of the aria. 男高音用假声唱出了咏叹调的最高音。


近义词：high voice, head voice, artificial voice


反义词：chest voice, baritone, bass


词性：名词
