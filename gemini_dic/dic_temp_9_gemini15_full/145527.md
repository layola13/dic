overcome:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌm/ | vt. |克服，战胜，解决 | to succeed in dealing with or controlling (a problem or difficulty) |例句：She overcame many obstacles to achieve her goals. 她克服了许多障碍才实现了自己的目标。

例句：He finally overcame his fear of public speaking. 他最终克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。


近义词：conquer, defeat, master, surmount | 反义词：succumb, yield, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She overcomes challenges with grace. = 她优雅地克服挑战。
He overcomes his fear of heights every day. = 他每天都克服对高处的恐惧。


一般过去时:
They overcame the difficulties and finished the project. = 他们克服了困难，完成了项目。
She overcame her shyness and spoke to the audience. = 她克服了害羞，对观众讲话。


现在完成时:
We have overcome many obstacles on our journey. = 在我们的旅程中，我们克服了许多障碍。
She has overcome her addiction to drugs. = 她已经克服了对毒品的瘾。


过去完成时:
They had overcome many setbacks before achieving success. = 他们在取得成功之前克服了许多挫折。
He had overcome his initial reluctance before agreeing to help. = 他在同意帮忙之前克服了最初的犹豫。


将来时:
We will overcome this crisis together. = 我们将一起克服这场危机。
She will overcome her fear of spiders eventually. = 她最终会克服对蜘蛛的恐惧。


虚拟语气:
If only I could overcome my procrastination! = 但愿我能克服我的拖延症！
I wish I could overcome my fear of failure. = 我希望我能克服我害怕失败的心理。


被动语态:
The problem was overcome by a team of experts. = 这个问题被一个专家团队解决了。
The challenges were overcome through hard work and dedication. = 通过努力工作和奉献精神克服了这些挑战。


疑问句:
Can we overcome this obstacle? = 我们能克服这个障碍吗？
How did she overcome her depression? = 她是如何克服她的抑郁症的？


否定句:
He didn't overcome his fear of water. = 他没有克服他对水的恐惧。
They couldn't overcome the technical difficulties. = 他们无法克服技术难题。


PHRASE->
overcome with emotion = 感动得热泪盈眶
overcome by grief = 悲痛欲绝
overcome obstacles = 克服障碍
overcome difficulties = 克服困难
overcome a challenge = 克服挑战

词源:
over- (表示“过度，超过”) + come (表示“来，到达”)，字面意思是“压倒，征服”，引申为“克服”。
