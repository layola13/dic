foraminiferous:/ˌfɔːræmɪˈnɪfərəs/| adj.|有孔虫的；含孔虫的|Containing or characterized by foraminifera.  Foraminifera are single-celled organisms with shells. The term describes rocks, sediments, or other materials that contain these shells.|The foraminiferous limestone is a valuable source of information about past environments.  有孔虫石灰岩是了解过去环境的重要信息来源。|近义词：foraminiferal|反义词：无
