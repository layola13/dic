ethyne:/ˈɛθaɪn/| n. |乙炔|Ethyne is the simplest alkyne, an unsaturated hydrocarbon with a triple bond between two carbon atoms.  It is a colorless, odorless gas at room temperature and pressure, but commercially available ethyne often has a distinctive garlic-like odor due to impurities. It is highly flammable and is used extensively in welding and cutting torches due to its high combustion temperature.| Example:  Ethyne, also known as acetylene, is used in oxy-acetylene welding. 乙炔，也称为乙炔，用于氧乙炔焊接。| 近义词:acetylene|反义词:无|名词
