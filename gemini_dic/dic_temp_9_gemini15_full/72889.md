followable: /ˈfɒləʊəbəl/ | adj. | 可遵循的，易于理解的 |  Easily understood and able to be followed or obeyed.  It implies clarity and simplicity, making something easy to comprehend and act upon. | Example:  The instructions were clear and followable.  说明清晰易懂。|近义词：understandable, comprehensible, manageable, tractable | 反义词：unclear, incomprehensible, confusing, intractable
