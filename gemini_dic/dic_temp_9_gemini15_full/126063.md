muster:/ˈmʌstər/ | v., n. |集合；召集；鼓起（勇气）；点名；聚集；总数 |To gather together (people, especially soldiers); to summon up (courage, energy, etc.); a gathering or collection, especially of soldiers for inspection; the assembled body of people or things. |例句：The general mustered his troops for a final assault.将军集合他的军队进行最后攻击。

例句：She mustered the courage to speak to her boss. 她鼓起勇气和她老板说话。


近义词：assemble, gather, collect, rally, summon | 反义词：disperse, scatter, dissipate | 词性:动词，名词


USAGE->

The sergeant regularly musters the troops. = 中士定期集合军队。
She musters her strength for the task ahead. = 她为即将到来的任务积攒力量。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
