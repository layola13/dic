inbreathe:/ˈɪnˌbriːð/| vt.|吸气，吸入|To draw air or other substances into the lungs.  |例句：Inhale deeply and then slowly inbreathe the fragrant air. 深吸一口气，然后慢慢地吸入芬芳的空气。|例句：The patient inbreathed the oxygen through a mask. 病人通过面罩吸入氧气。|近义词：inhale, inspire|反义词：exhale, breathe out|动词
