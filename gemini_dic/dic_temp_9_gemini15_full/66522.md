exhaustedly: /ɪɡˈzɔːstɪdli/ | adv. | 筋疲力尽地，疲惫不堪地 | Exhaustedly describes the manner in which something is done when someone is extremely tired.  It indicates a state of physical or mental exhaustion. |  She slumped into the chair, exhaustedly sighing. 她无力地倒在椅子上，疲惫地叹了口气。  He spoke exhaustedly, his voice barely a whisper. 他疲惫地说着话，声音几乎低不可闻。 | tiredly, wearily, weakly | energetically, vigorously | 副词
