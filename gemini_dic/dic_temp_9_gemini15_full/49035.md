degrees:/dɪˈɡriːz/ | n. | 度数；学位；等级 |  Degrees refer to units of measurement for angles, temperature, or other quantities.  They can also refer to an academic rank conferred by a college or university or a level or grade of something. |例句：The temperature is 25 degrees Celsius.  温度是摄氏25度。

例句：She has a master's degree in engineering. 她拥有工程硕士学位。|近义词：level, rank, grade,  qualification |反义词：radian (for angles)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water boils at 100 degrees. = 水在100度沸腾。
This thermometer measures degrees Fahrenheit. = 这个温度计测量华氏度。


一般过去时:
The temperature dropped several degrees overnight. = 温度一夜之间下降了几度。
He received his law degree last year. = 他去年获得了法律学位。


现在完成时:
The temperature has risen several degrees. = 温度已经上升了几度。
She has earned two degrees from prestigious universities. = 她已经从著名大学获得了两个学位。


过去完成时:
The temperature had already dropped ten degrees before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，温度已经下降了十度。
He had completed his undergraduate degree before starting his master's program. = 他在开始硕士课程之前已经完成了本科课程。


将来时:
The temperature will likely rise several degrees tomorrow. = 明天的气温可能会上升几度。
She will receive her degree next month. = 她下个月将获得学位。


虚拟语气:
If the temperature were higher, we could go swimming. = 如果温度更高，我们就能去游泳。
I wish I had a degree in computer science. = 我希望我有计算机科学学位。


被动语态:
The temperature is measured in degrees Celsius. = 温度以摄氏度来衡量。
His degree was awarded with honors. = 他的学位以优异成绩获得。


疑问句:
What is the temperature in degrees Fahrenheit? = 华氏温度是多少？
What degree are you studying for? = 你正在攻读什么学位？


否定句:
The temperature is not below zero degrees. = 温度不低于零度。
He doesn't have a college degree. = 他没有大学学位。
