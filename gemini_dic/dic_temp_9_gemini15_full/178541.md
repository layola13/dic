reconvicts:/ˌriːkənˈvɪkts/ | v. | 再定罪，再次判罪 | To convict (someone) again for the same or a similar crime.  |例句：He was reconvicted on the same charges after appealing his original sentence. 他在对原判提出上诉后，因同样的罪名再次被定罪。

例句：The serial killer was reconvicted after new evidence emerged.  连环杀手在出现新的证据后再次被定罪。


近义词：reconvict, retry, condemn again | 反义词：acquit, exonerate | 词性:动词
