mayhap:/ˈmeɪhæp/ | adv. |或许，也许 |Perhaps, possibly; used to express uncertainty or possibility.  It is an archaic or literary word, rarely used in modern conversation. |例句：Mayhap we shall meet again someday.  = 也许我们将来某一天还会再见。

例句：Mayhap the rain will stop soon. = 也许雨很快就会停了。


近义词：perhaps, maybe, possibly, perchance

反义词：certainly, definitely, undoubtedly


词性:副词
