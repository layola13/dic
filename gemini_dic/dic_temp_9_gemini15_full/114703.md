mallards:/ˈmælɑːrdz/| n. |绿头鸭（复数）|A mallard is a type of dabbling duck, the most common and widespread of all ducks.  They are known for their distinctive features, especially the male's iridescent green head.  Mallards are found in a wide variety of habitats, including marshes, ponds, lakes, and rivers.|  Example:  We saw a flock of mallards swimming on the lake. 我们看到一群绿头鸭在湖里游泳。|近义词：ducks|反义词：无
