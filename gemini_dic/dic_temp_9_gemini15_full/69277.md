feathers:/ˈfɛðərz/ | n. |羽毛；翎毛|The outer covering of a bird's body, used for flight, insulation, and display.  Feathers are light, flexible, and often brightly colored. They are made of keratin, the same protein that makes up human hair and nails. |例句：The bird fluffed its feathers to keep warm.  这只鸟蓬松起羽毛以保暖。

例句：The peacock's feathers shimmered in the sunlight. 孔雀的羽毛在阳光下闪闪发光。

近义词：plumage

反义词：scales (of a reptile), fur (of a mammal)

词性：名词
