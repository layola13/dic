roadway:/ˈroʊdˌweɪ/| n. |道路，路面|The part of a road used by vehicles; the traveled portion of a road.  It often refers to the paved or improved surface. |例句：The roadway was slick with ice.  路面结了冰很滑。|例句：They are widening the roadway to reduce congestion. 他们正在拓宽路面以缓解交通拥堵。|近义词：road, street, highway, thoroughfare |反义词：sidewalk, footpath
