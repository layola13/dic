platings:/ˈpleɪtɪŋz/| n. |镀层；电镀；金属板；菜肴摆盘|The process of applying a metallic coating to a surface, or the resulting coating itself. It can also refer to the arrangement of food on a plate.|例句：The platings on the jewelry were tarnished.  珠宝上的镀层已经失去光泽了。


例句：The chef carefully considered the platings for the wedding banquet.  厨师仔细考虑了婚礼宴会上的菜肴摆盘。


近义词：coatings, plating,  metalwork (for the metal aspect)


反义词：unplated, bare (lack of plating)


词性: 名词
