maidkin:/ˈmeɪdkɪn/| n.|侍女；小女佣|A young maid or female servant.  The word is chiefly found in older texts and fantasy literature.|例句：The maidkin scurried to answer the bell. 小女佣赶紧去开门。
The old castle was served by several maidkins. 老城堡里有好几个小女佣服侍。|近义词：maidservant, handmaiden|反义词：master, lord.
