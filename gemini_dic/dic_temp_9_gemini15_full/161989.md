pratingly:/ˈpreɪtɪŋli/| adv.|喋喋不休地；夸夸其谈地；饶舌地 |In a manner characterized by excessive or empty talk; garrulously.  |例句：He prated pratingly about his achievements, ignoring everyone else's contributions. 他喋喋不休地谈论着自己的成就，无视其他人的贡献。

例句：She prated pratingly of her travels,  unaware of her listeners' boredom. 她夸夸其谈地讲述自己的旅行经历，全然不觉听者已经厌烦了。

近义词： garrulously, loquaciously, volubly, incessantly

反义词： silently, taciturnly, quietly


词源:  Derived from the verb "prate," meaning to talk excessively or idly.  The suffix "-ingly" transforms the verb into an adverb, modifying the manner of the action.  The root of "prate" is uncertain, but possibly related to Old French words suggesting chattering or babbling.
