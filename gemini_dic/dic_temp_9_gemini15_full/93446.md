illumined:/ɪˈluːmɪnd/| adj. |照亮的；启发的；受启蒙的|  To shine light on; to enlighten or inspire.  The word suggests a more profound and transformative effect than simply being lit up. It implies a spiritual or intellectual awakening.|例句：The cathedral was illumined by stained glass. 大教堂被彩色玻璃照亮了。 The philosopher's words illumined my understanding of the subject. 哲学家的言辞启迪了我对这个主题的理解。|近义词：lighted, lit, enlightened, inspired|反义词：darkened, obscured, unenlightened
