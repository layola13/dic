cryptology:/krɪpˈtɒlədʒi/|/kɹɪpˈtɑːlədʒi/| n. |密码学|The study of secure communication techniques that allow only the sender and intended recipient to understand the message. It encompasses both cryptography (the creation of secure communication methods) and cryptanalysis (the breaking of those methods).| Example: Cryptology plays a crucial role in protecting sensitive data in online transactions. 密码学在保护在线交易中的敏感数据方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：codebreaking, cryptography, cryptanalysis|反义词：plaintext, open communication
