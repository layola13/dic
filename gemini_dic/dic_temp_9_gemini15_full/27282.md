calumnious: /kəˈlʌmniəs/ | adj. | 诽谤的；中伤的 |  Characterized by, or containing, calumny; slanderous; defamatory.  It implies a false and malicious accusation intended to harm someone's reputation. |


例句：

He spread calumnious rumors about his rivals to damage their reputations. = 他散布关于竞争对手的诽谤性谣言来损害他们的声誉。

The article contained several calumnious statements that were completely unfounded. = 这篇文章包含几条完全没有根据的诽谤性言论。


近义词：slanderous, defamatory, libelous, malicious, scandalous, vituperative


反义词：laudatory, complimentary, flattering, truthful


词性: 形容词
