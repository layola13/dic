centigrade:/ˈsentɪɡreɪd/| adj.|摄氏的；百分度的|Relating to or denoting a scale of temperature in which the freezing point of water is at 0° and the boiling point is at 100° under standard atmospheric pressure.|The water boiled at 100 degrees centigrade. 水在摄氏100度沸腾。|Celsius, Fahrenheit|Fahrenheit|adj.
