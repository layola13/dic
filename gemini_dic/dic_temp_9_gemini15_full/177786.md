receipt: /rɪˈsiːt/ | n. | 收据，收执，收到 | A written acknowledgement that something has been received, especially one given to a person paying money.  |  例句：I need a receipt for the purchase. 我需要这张购买的收据。

例句：Can I have a receipt, please? 请给我一张收据好吗？


近义词：acknowledgment, confirmation, proof

反义词：无


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop gives a receipt for every purchase. = 商店每次购买都给收据。
He always keeps receipts for his expenses. = 他总是保留他费用的收据。


一般过去时:
I received the receipt yesterday. = 我昨天收到了收据。
She lost the receipt for the airline ticket. = 她丢失了机票收据。


现在完成时:
I have kept all the receipts for my tax return. = 我已经保存了我所有报税用的收据。
She has not received the receipt yet. = 她还没有收到收据。


过去完成时:
I had already submitted the receipt before the deadline. = 我在截止日期之前已经提交了收据。
They had lost the receipts before realizing they needed them. = 他们在意识到需要收据之前就已经丢失了它们。


将来时:
I will give you the receipt when you pay. = 你付款时我会给你收据。
He will receive the receipt by email. = 他将通过电子邮件收到收据。


虚拟语气:
If I had the receipt, I could get a refund. = 如果我有收据，我就可以退款。
I wish I hadn't lost the receipt. = 我真希望我没有弄丢收据。


被动语态:
Receipts are often requested for tax purposes. = 收据通常用于税务目的。
The receipt was emailed to me. = 收据通过电子邮件发送给了我。


疑问句:
Did you get a receipt? = 你拿到收据了吗？
Where is the receipt? = 收据在哪里？


否定句:
I didn't receive a receipt. = 我没有收到收据。
He doesn't need a receipt for this small purchase. = 这件小东西购买他不需要收据。



PHRASE->
tax receipt: 税务收据
sales receipt: 销售收据
electronic receipt: 电子收据
gift receipt: 礼品收据
duplicate receipt: 复制收据
