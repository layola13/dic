seismometric:/ˌsaɪzˈmɒmɪtrɪk/ | adj. |地震测量的；地震仪的 | Relating to the measurement of seismic waves or the instruments used for this purpose.  |The seismometric data revealed a series of minor tremors preceding the earthquake. 地震测量数据显示，地震前发生了一系列轻微震动。 |近义词：seismic, seismological |反义词：无
