pyrazin:/ˈpɪrəzɪn/| n. |吡嗪|A heterocyclic aromatic organic compound with the formula C₄H₄N₂. It is a colourless crystalline solid with a faint, characteristic odour.  It is a building block in many other heterocyclic compounds and is found in some food and tobacco smoke.|Pyrazin is a key component in the flavor of roasted coffee and toasted nuts.|近义词：None (unique chemical structure)|反义词：None (no direct opposite)|名词
