fowls:/faʊlz/| n.|家禽；禽类|Domesticated birds kept for their meat or eggs.  The word often refers to chickens, ducks, geese, and turkeys collectively. |例句：We raise several kinds of fowls on our farm. 我们在农场饲养几种家禽。|例句：The farmer sells his fowls at the market. 这位农民在市场上出售他的家禽。|近义词：poultry, birds, birds, chickens|反义词：wild birds
