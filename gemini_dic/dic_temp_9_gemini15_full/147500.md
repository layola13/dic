overpackage:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒ/ | v. |过度包装|To package something in more packaging than is necessary or appropriate.  This often involves using excessive amounts of material or unnecessarily large containers.  It's a concern for environmental reasons due to waste generation and resource depletion.|例句：The company was criticized for overpackaging its products. 公司因其产品过度包装而受到批评。

The retailer decided to reduce overpackaging to cut costs and improve its environmental image. 零售商决定减少过度包装以降低成本并改善其环保形象。|近义词：overwrap, excessively package|反义词：minimally package, eco-package|动词
