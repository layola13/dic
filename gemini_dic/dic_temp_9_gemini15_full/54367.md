dishpan:/ˈdɪʃpæn/| n. |洗碗盆，洗碗盘|A basin, usually metal, used for washing dishes.|例句：She filled the dishpan with hot, soapy water. 她用热水和肥皂水灌满了洗碗盆。|例句：The old dishpan was rusty and worn out. 旧的洗碗盆生锈了，也磨损了。|近义词：basin, tub |反义词：无
