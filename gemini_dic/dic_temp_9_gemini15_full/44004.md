criminalizing:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/| v.|使犯罪化；定为犯罪|To make something illegal.  The act of declaring something a crime.|The government is considering criminalizing the use of certain pesticides. 政府正在考虑将某些杀虫剂的使用定为犯罪。|近义词：outlawing, prohibiting, banning|反义词：legalizing, permitting, allowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are criminalizing downloading copyrighted music. = 他们正在将下载受版权保护的音乐定为犯罪。
The government criminalizes drug possession. = 政府将持有毒品定为犯罪。


一般过去时:
They criminalized gambling in the 19th century. = 他们在19世纪将赌博定为犯罪。
The country criminalized same-sex marriage. = 该国将同性婚姻定为犯罪。


现在完成时:
Many countries have criminalized hate speech. = 许多国家已经将仇恨言论定为犯罪。
Society has criminalized certain behaviours over time. = 社会随着时间的推移将某些行为定为犯罪。


过去完成时:
They had criminalized the protest before it even began. = 他们在抗议活动甚至开始之前就已将其定为犯罪。
The authorities had criminalized the possession of weapons. = 当局已将持有武器定为犯罪。


将来时:
They will criminalize the use of AI in warfare. = 他们将把在战争中使用人工智能定为犯罪。
The new law will criminalize unauthorized data collection. = 新法律将未经授权的数据收集定为犯罪。


虚拟语气:
If they criminalized that act, there would be fewer incidents. = 如果他们将该行为定为犯罪，事件就会减少。
I wish they would criminalize dangerous driving. = 我希望他们能将危险驾驶定为犯罪。


被动语态:
The possession of that substance was criminalized. = 持有那种物质被定为犯罪。
The sale of drugs is being criminalized in many parts of the world. = 在世界许多地方，贩卖毒品正在被定为犯罪。


疑问句:
Will they criminalize this type of behavior? = 他们会将这种行为定为犯罪吗？
Is the government planning to criminalize online harassment? = 政府是否计划将网络骚扰定为犯罪？


否定句:
They did not criminalize the peaceful protest. = 他们没有将和平抗议定为犯罪。
The act was not criminalized until years later. = 该行为直到数年后才被定为犯罪。


PHRASE->
criminalizing behavior = 使行为犯罪化
criminalizing speech = 使言论犯罪化
criminalizing poverty = 使贫困犯罪化
criminalizing dissent = 使异议犯罪化
criminalizing addiction = 使成瘾犯罪化
