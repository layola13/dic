loyalty:/ˈlɔɪəlti/| n.|忠诚；忠实；忠心|Faithfulness to a person, cause, or belief; devotion to one's country, a sovereign, or a cause.|例句：Her loyalty to her friends is unwavering.  她的朋友们对她忠诚不二。|例句：The soldiers showed great loyalty to their commander.  士兵们对他们的指挥官表现出极大的忠诚。|近义词：fidelity, allegiance, devotion, faithfulness|反义词：treachery, betrayal, disloyalty
