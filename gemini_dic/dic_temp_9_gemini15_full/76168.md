fusing:/ˈfjuːzɪŋ/ | v. |熔合；融合；结合|The act of melting two or more things together, usually by heat, to form a single mass or substance.  It can also refer to the merging or blending of different things, ideas, or elements.|例句：The glass artist is fusing colored glass pieces to create a beautiful mosaic. 玻璃艺术家正在熔合彩色玻璃碎片以创造美丽的马赛克。

例句：The two companies are fusing their operations to become a larger, more powerful entity. 两家公司正在合并业务，以成为一个更大、更强大的实体。|近义词：melting, blending, merging, combining, uniting|反义词：separating, dividing, splitting
