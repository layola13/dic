chrissake:/ˈkrɪsˌeɪk/| int.|该死，天哪(粗俗)|An exclamation of frustration, annoyance, or disbelief, often used as a milder substitute for a stronger curse word. It is considered informal and even mildly offensive in some contexts.  The origin is uncertain but likely involves a corruption of "Christ's sake."|例句：For chrissake, just clean your room!  该死，快去打扫你的房间！

例句：

What in chrissake are you doing?  你到底在搞什么鬼？


近义词：goodness gracious, heavens, hell, damn


反义词：无


词性：感叹词
