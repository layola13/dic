pious:/ˈpaɪəs/| adj.|虔诚的，信奉宗教的，虚伪虔诚的|Showing or having a deep devotion to religion; devout.  Often implies excessive or hypocritical religious observance.|例句：She was a pious woman who attended church every Sunday. 她是一位虔诚的妇女，每周日都去教堂。

例句：His pious pronouncements masked a deep-seated cynicism. 他虔诚的声明掩盖了根深蒂固的愤世嫉俗。


近义词：devout, religious, godly, holy, reverent|反义词：irreverent, impious, ungodly, profane


词源:  From Old French *piete,* from Latin *pietas,* meaning "dutifulness, piety," ultimately from *pius,* meaning "dutiful, pious."  The Latin root also gives us words like "paternal" (relating to a father) as *pietas* originally referred to dutiful conduct towards one's parents and ancestors.
