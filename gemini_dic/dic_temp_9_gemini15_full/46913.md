darkness:/ˈdɑːknəs/ /ˈdɑːrknes/ | n. |黑暗，阴暗；无知；邪恶 |The absence of light; a state or place where there is little or no light.  It can also refer to ignorance, evil, or a lack of understanding. |例句：The darkness frightened the child. 黑暗吓坏了孩子。

The darkness of the room was oppressive. 房间的黑暗令人压抑。


近义词：blackness, shadow, obscurity, gloom |反义词：light, brightness, illumination
