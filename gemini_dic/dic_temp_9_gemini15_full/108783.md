leasing:/ˈliːsɪŋ/| n. |租赁；出租；租赁协议|The act of granting the use of something for a specific period of time in exchange for payment.  It often involves real estate, vehicles, or equipment.|例句：The company offers leasing options for its new cars. 这家公司为其新车提供租赁选择。|例句：Our lease on the apartment expires next month. 我们公寓的租约下个月到期。|近义词：renting, hiring, letting|反义词：buying, owning


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He leases a car every year. = 他每年租一辆车。
The landlord leases apartments to tenants. = 房东把公寓出租给租客。

一般过去时:
They leased the land for farming. = 他们租赁了这块土地用于耕种。
She leased a small office space downtown. = 她租用了市中心的一间小办公室。

现在完成时:
We have leased this property for five years. = 我们已经租赁了这处房产五年了。
The company has leased several new computers. = 公司已经租赁了几台新电脑。

过去完成时:
They had leased the equipment before the project started. = 项目开始前，他们已经租赁了设备。
She had leased the apartment for a year before moving out. = 她搬出去之前已经租了一年的公寓。


将来时:
We will lease a bigger office next year. = 我们明年将租赁一个更大的办公室。
He will lease a car from that dealership. = 他将从那家经销商处租赁一辆汽车。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would lease a luxury car. = 如果我有更多钱，我会租一辆豪华车。
I wish I could lease a beachfront property. = 我真希望我能租一套海滨房产。

被动语态:
The car was leased by a young businessman. = 这辆车被一位年轻的商人租赁了。
The apartment is being leased to a new tenant. = 这套公寓正在出租给一位新租客。

疑问句:
Does he lease his car or buy it? = 他是租赁还是购买他的车？
Are you leasing or buying the equipment? = 你是租赁还是购买设备？

否定句:
They did not lease the building. = 他们没有租赁那座大楼。
She doesn't want to lease a car anymore. = 她不想再租车了。


PHRASE->
lease agreement = 租赁协议
lease option = 租赁选择
lease term = 租赁期限
lease payment = 租赁付款
break a lease = 违反租赁合同
