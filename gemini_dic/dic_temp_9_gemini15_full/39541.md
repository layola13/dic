consanguined:/kənˈsæŋɡwɪnd/| adj.| 血亲的；同族的；近亲结婚的 | Related by blood; of the same ancestry; having a common ancestor.  The term often implies a close blood relationship, frequently used in discussions of incest or close family ties. | 例句：The consanguined marriage resulted in a higher risk of genetic disorders. 近亲结婚导致遗传疾病的风险更高。

The consanguined families shared a long history and traditions.  这些血亲家族共享着悠久的历史和传统。


近义词：related, kindred, blood-related, affiliated


反义词：unrelated, unrelated by blood
