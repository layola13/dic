regurgitation:/ˌriːɡɜːrˈdʒɪteɪʃən/| n.|反刍；倒吐；重述；(思想、观点等的)反复陈述|The act of bringing swallowed food back up into the mouth.  It can also refer to the repetition of something already heard or read, often without understanding.|例句：The bird regurgitated the undigested seeds.  这只鸟吐出了未消化的种子。

例句：His speech was nothing more than a regurgitation of old ideas.  他的演讲不过是旧思想的重复。|近义词：repetition, reiteration, vomiting|反义词：originality, innovation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regurgitates his food sometimes. = 他有时会反刍食物。
She regurgitates information from textbooks without understanding. = 她不理解地从课本里重复信息。

一般过去时:
The baby regurgitated milk after feeding. = 婴儿喂奶后吐奶了。
He regurgitated the lecture he'd heard earlier that day. = 他重复了他那天早些时候听到的讲座内容。

现在完成时:
The owl has regurgitated a pellet containing bones and fur. = 猫头鹰已经吐出了一个含有骨头和毛发的食团。
She has regurgitated the same arguments repeatedly in the debate. = 在辩论中，她反复重复着同样的论点。

过去完成时:
The patient had regurgitated blood before arriving at the hospital. = 病人到达医院前曾吐过血。
He had regurgitated the plot of the novel without adding any original thought.  = 他重复了小说的情节，没有任何原创的想法。

将来时:
The bird will likely regurgitate food to feed its young. = 这只鸟可能会吐出食物喂养它的幼鸟。
He will probably regurgitate the same tired clichés in his next speech. = 他可能会在他的下一次演讲中重复同样的陈词滥调。

虚拟语气:
If he ate too quickly, he might regurgitate his dinner. = 如果他吃得太快，他可能会吐出晚餐。
If she had understood the material better, she wouldn't have regurgitated it so poorly. = 如果她更好地理解了材料，她就不会如此糟糕地重复它了。

被动语态:
The food was regurgitated by the bird. = 食物被鸟吐了出来。
The information was regurgitated by the student without comprehension. = 学生不理解地重复了信息。

疑问句:
Did the baby regurgitate its milk? = 婴儿吐奶了吗？
Is he merely regurgitating information or actually understanding it? = 他只是在重复信息还是真的理解了？

否定句:
The cat did not regurgitate its hairball. = 猫没有吐出毛球。
He didn't regurgitate the facts; he actually analyzed them. = 他并没有简单地重复事实；他实际上分析了它们。
