call the shots:/kɔːl ðə ʃɒts/ | v. | 发号施令；掌控全局；说了算 | To be the person who makes the important decisions and is in control.  |  The general called the shots during the battle.  将军在战斗中发号施令。|近义词：be in charge,  take charge, dictate, control, rule |反义词：obey, follow, submit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He calls the shots in this company. = 他在这家公司说了算。
She calls the shots on all major decisions. = 所有重大决定都由她说了算。


一般过去时:
The CEO called the shots before the merger. = 首席执行官在并购前说了算。
My father always called the shots in our family. = 我父亲总是掌控着我们家的所有事。


现在完成时:
He has called the shots for many years. = 他多年来一直掌控全局。
She has called the shots since the founder retired. = 自从创始人退休后，她一直掌控大局。


过去完成时:
They had called the shots before the crisis hit. = 在危机爆发前，他们一直掌控全局。
Before the project started, he had called the shots. = 项目开始前，他一直说了算。


将来时:
She will call the shots in the new department. = 她将在这个新部门说了算。
He will call the shots at the upcoming meeting. = 他将在即将举行的会议上掌控全局。


虚拟语气:
If he called the shots, things would be different. = 如果他说了算，情况就会不一样。
I wish I called the shots in this situation. = 我希望在这种情况下我能掌控全局。


被动语态:
The decisions were called by the project manager. = 决定是由项目经理拍板的。(被动语态用法较少见)
The shots were usually called by the team leader. = 决定通常由团队领导来做。(被动语态用法较少见)


疑问句:
Who calls the shots around here? = 这里谁说了算？
Does she call the shots in this project? = 这个项目是她说了算吗？


否定句:
He doesn't call the shots anymore. = 他不再说了算。
She doesn't call the shots in that department. = 她不在那个部门说了算。


PHRASE->
call the shots in a meeting = 在会议上做决定
call the shots on a project = 掌控一个项目
call the shots in the family = 在家里说了算
call the shots in the government = 在政府中掌权
call the shots in a negotiation = 在谈判中掌握主动
