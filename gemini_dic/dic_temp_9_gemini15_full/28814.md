carotin:/ˈkærəˌtɪn/| n. |胡萝卜素|A pigment found in plants and some animals, responsible for the orange, yellow, and red colors in many fruits and vegetables.  Carotins are important precursors to vitamin A.  |例句：Carrots are a rich source of carotene. 胡萝卜是胡萝卜素的丰富来源。|例句：Beta-carotene is a type of carotin. β-胡萝卜素是一种胡萝卜素。|近义词：carotenoid |反义词：无|名词
