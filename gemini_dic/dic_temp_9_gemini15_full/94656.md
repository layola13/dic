impressions:/ɪmˈprɛʃənz/ | n. |印象，感觉；效果；冲击 |The overall impression is one of elegance and sophistication.  整体印象是优雅和精致。|近义词：feelings, sensations, effects |反义词：misconceptions, delusions


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advertisement leaves a strong impression on the audience. = 广告给观众留下了深刻的印象。
His words make a lasting impression. = 他的话给人留下了深刻的印象。


一般过去时:
The meeting made a positive impression on me. = 会议给我留下了积极的印象。
The painting left a deep impression on the visitors. = 这幅画给参观者留下了深刻的印象。


现在完成时:
The movie has made a lasting impression on my mind. = 这部电影在我的脑海中留下了持久的印象。
His performance has left a strong impression on the judges. = 他的表演给评委留下了深刻的印象。


过去完成时:
I had already formed a strong impression of him before meeting him. = 在见到他之前，我已经对他有了深刻的印象。
By the end of the presentation, the audience had received a clear impression of the product. = 演示结束时，观众已经对产品有了清晰的印象。


将来时:
This new product will make a great impression on the market. = 这款新产品将在市场上产生巨大影响。
The campaign will leave a lasting impression on voters. = 这场竞选活动将给选民留下深刻的印象。


虚拟语气:
I wish my speech had made a better impression on the audience. = 我希望我的演讲能给观众留下更好的印象。
If I had known better, I would have made a different impression. = 如果我更了解情况，我会留下不同的印象。


被动语态:
A good impression was made on the interviewer. = 给面试官留下了好印象。
A lasting impression is made by the beauty of the scenery. = 风景的美丽留下了持久的印象。


疑问句:
What impression did the speaker make on you? = 演讲者给你留下了什么印象？
What is your first impression of this city? = 你对这座城市的最初印象是什么？


否定句:
The movie didn't make much of an impression on me. = 这部电影并没有给我留下什么印象。
He failed to make a good impression on his boss. = 他没能给老板留下好印象。


PHRASE->
first impressions = 第一印象
make an impression = 留下印象
create an impression = 创造印象
give an impression = 给人印象
lasting impression = 持久的印象
