overingenuity: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌdʒɛnuɪti/ | n. | 过于精巧；过于巧妙；矫揉造作 | Excessive cleverness or ingenuity; often implying artificiality or lack of naturalness.  It suggests a level of cleverness that is unnecessary or even counterproductive. | The architect's overingenuity led to a structurally unsound design.  建筑师的过度巧妙设计导致结构不稳定。 The novel's plot suffered from overingenuity; the twists and turns felt forced and unnatural. 这部小说的情节过于巧妙，情节的曲折变化显得牵强且不自然。 | 近义词：artificiality, contrivance, affectation | 反义词：simplicity, naturalness, straightforwardness
