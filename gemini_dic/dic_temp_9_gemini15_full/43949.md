cribble:/ˈkrɪbəl/| v.| 随意涂写，乱写；(尤指儿童) 潦草地写；|to write or draw carelessly and untidily, especially as a child|例句：The child cribbled all over the wallpaper.  孩子在墙纸上乱涂乱画。|例句：He cribbled a note to his mother. 他潦草地给母亲写了张便条。|近义词：scribble, scrawl, doodle |反义词：write neatly, compose carefully
