pounces:/paʊnsɪz/| v.|猛扑，突然袭击|To spring or swoop suddenly upon something or someone.|The cat pounced on the mouse.  猫猛扑向老鼠。|The eagle pounced from the sky. 老鹰从空中猛扑下来。|leap, spring, swoop|retreat, withdraw, back off|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat pounces on the mouse. = 猫猛扑向老鼠。
He pounces on every opportunity. 他抓住每一个机会。

一般过去时:
The tiger pounced on its prey. = 老虎猛扑向猎物。
She pounced on the mistake in his report. 她发现了报告中的错误。

现在完成时:
The cat has pounced on several mice this week. = 这周猫已经抓了好几只老鼠。
He has pounced on the chance to travel abroad. 他抓住了出国旅行的机会。


过去完成时:
The bird had pounced before I could react. = 我还没反应过来，鸟就已经猛扑下来了。
They had pounced on the idea before we could discuss it. 他们抢先一步提出了这个想法。


将来时:

She will pounce on any opportunity to advance her career. 她会抓住任何机会来提升她的事业。


虚拟语气:
