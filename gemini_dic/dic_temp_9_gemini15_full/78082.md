geoduck:/ˈɡiːdʌk/ | n. |象拔蚌|A large, edible saltwater clam, *Panopea generosa*, found along the west coast of North America.  It is known for its exceptionally long siphon. |例句：The geoduck is a popular delicacy in some Asian cuisines. 象拔蚌在一些亚洲菜系中是一种受欢迎的美味佳肴。|例句：We went clam digging and found a huge geoduck. 我们去挖蛤蜊，发现了一个巨大的象拔蚌。|近义词：clam (general term)|反义词：无
