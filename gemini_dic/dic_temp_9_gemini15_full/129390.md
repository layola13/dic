nomenclatural:/ˌnoʊmɛnˈkleɪtʃərəl/ | adj. | 命名法的；命名学的 | Relating to nomenclature, the system of names used in a particular field, especially in biology or chemistry.  It often refers to the rules and conventions for naming things, especially in taxonomy. | The nomenclatural rules for naming new species are complex and require careful adherence.  命名新物种的命名规则复杂，需要仔细遵守。 | 近义词：taxonomic, classificatory | 反义词：无
