phoronomics:/ˌfɔːrəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. | 措辞经济学；词语经济学 | The study of the economic principles and practices related to the use of language, particularly in areas like advertising, marketing, and communication.  It examines how language choices impact consumer behavior, brand perception, and overall economic outcomes.  | Example:  The company's marketing team employed advanced phoronomics to craft slogans that resonated with the target demographic and boosted sales. 公司的营销团队运用先进的措辞经济学来设计与目标群体产生共鸣并提高销量的标语。|近义词：linguistic economics, communication economics | 反义词：N/A
