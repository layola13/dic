over-seeded:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdəd/ | v. | 过度播种；补播 | To sow or plant seeds in excess or to plant seeds on top of an existing lawn to improve its density or quality.  | Example: The farmer over-seeded the field to ensure a bountiful harvest.  农民过度播种田地以确保丰收。  The gardener over-seeded the lawn to thicken it up. 园丁在草坪上补播以使其变厚。 |近义词：reseed, topdress | 反义词：underseed (播种不足)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They over-seed their lawn every autumn. = 他们每年秋天都在草坪上补播。
We over-seed the pasture to improve grazing. = 我们在牧场上补播以改善放牧条件。

一般过去时:
The farmer over-seeded the field last spring. = 农民去年春天在田里过度播种。
She over-seeded the bare patches in her garden. = 她在她花园里光秃秃的地方补播。

现在完成时:
They have over-seeded the field twice this year. = 今年他们已经在这个田地里补播了两次。
I have over-seeded my lawn to improve its density. = 我已经在我的草坪上补播以提高其密度。

过去完成时:
They had over-seeded the lawn before the first frost. = 他们在第一次霜冻之前已经补播了草坪。
The gardener had over-seeded the area before the heavy rain. = 园丁在大雨之前已经在该区域补播。

将来时:
We will over-seed the pasture next month. = 我们下个月将要补播牧场。
They will over-seed the lawn in the fall. = 他们将在秋季补播草坪。

虚拟语气:
If I had over-seeded earlier, the lawn would look better now. = 如果我早点补播，草坪现在看起来会更好。
I wish I had over-seeded the lawn last year. = 我真希望去年在草坪上补播了。

被动语态:
The field was over-seeded by the farmer. = 田地是由农民过度播种的。

疑问句:

否定句:
