mafiosi:/ˌmæfiˈoʊzi/| n. |黑手党成员（复数）|The plural of mafioso, referring to multiple members of the Mafia.  Mafiosi are often involved in organized crime, such as extortion, drug trafficking, and murder. They are known for their secretive nature and powerful influence.|The mafiosi controlled the city's port through intimidation and violence.|近义词：gangsters, mobsters, racketeers|反义词：law-abiding citizens, honest people|名词
