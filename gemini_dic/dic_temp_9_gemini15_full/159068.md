pneumothorax:/njuːməˈθɔːræks/| n. |气胸|A pneumothorax is a collapsed lung.  This occurs when air leaks into the space between your lung and chest wall.  This air pushes on the outside of your lung and causes it to collapse.  |例句：The accident caused a pneumothorax, requiring immediate medical attention. 事故导致气胸，需要立即就医。

例句：He was diagnosed with a spontaneous pneumothorax and needed surgery. 他被诊断出患有自发性气胸，需要手术。


近义词：collapsed lung, lung collapse |反义词：inflated lung, healthy lung |词性:n.
