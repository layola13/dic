half-drunken:/ˈhæfˈdrʌŋkən/| adj.|半醉的，微醺的|Partially intoxicated; somewhat drunk.  It describes a state between being completely sober and being completely drunk.|例句：He was half-drunken and stumbled as he walked. 他半醉着，走路时摇摇晃晃。|例句：The half-drunken revelers sang loudly.  那些半醉的狂欢者大声唱歌。|近义词：tipsy, slightly drunk, inebriated (less common)|反义词：sober, completely drunk


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is half-drunken. = 他有点醉。
She is half-drunken and singing. = 她有点醉，并且在唱歌。


一般过去时:

被动语态:
