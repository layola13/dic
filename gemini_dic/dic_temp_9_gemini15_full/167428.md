prevalent:/ˈprɛvələnt/| adj.|普遍的；盛行的；流行的|Existing generally or widely; common.|The prevalent belief is that the earth is round. 普遍的信念是地球是圆的。|近义词：common, widespread, general, rife|反义词：rare, uncommon, scarce, infrequent|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flu is prevalent in the winter. = 流感在冬天很常见。
This opinion is prevalent among young people. = 这种观点在年轻人中很流行。

一般过去时:
That disease was prevalent in the 19th century. = 那种疾病在19世纪很盛行。
Such beliefs were prevalent in ancient times. = 这种信仰在古代很普遍。

现在完成时:
This practice has been prevalent for many years. = 这种做法已经盛行多年了。
That style of music has been prevalent throughout the decade. = 那种音乐风格在十年间一直很流行。

过去完成时:
Before the invention of antibiotics, this illness had been prevalent for centuries. = 在抗生素发明之前，这种疾病已经盛行了几个世纪。
That philosophy had been prevalent in many European countries. = 这种哲学在许多欧洲国家曾很盛行。

将来时:
Experts predict that this technology will be prevalent in the near future. = 专家预测这项技术在不久的将来将会很普遍。
It is expected that this trend will be prevalent in the coming years. = 预计这种趋势在未来几年将很流行。


虚拟语气:
If this trend were prevalent, things would be very different. = 如果这种趋势很普遍，情况就会大不一样。
If that belief had been more prevalent, the outcome might have been different. = 如果那种信念更普遍，结果可能就会不同。

被动语态:
It is reported that this virus is prevalent in several regions. = 据报道，这种病毒在几个地区很流行。
The view that the earth is flat was once widely prevalent.  = 认为地球是平的这种观点曾经广泛流行。

疑问句:
Is this disease prevalent in your area? = 这种疾病在你们地区流行吗？
Are those views prevalent amongst your peers? = 那些观点在你的同龄人中流行吗？

否定句:
That type of car is not prevalent in this country. = 那种类型的汽车在这个国家并不常见。
These ideas are not prevalent in modern society. = 这些想法在现代社会并不流行。


PHRASE->
be prevalent among  在…中流行
prevalent in  在…中普遍存在
most prevalent 最普遍的
widely prevalent 广泛流行的
currently prevalent 目前流行的
