christen:/ˈkrɪsən/| vt.|给…施洗；命名；为…举行洗礼仪式|To perform a Christian baptism on (a person); to give a name to (a person, place, or thing), especially in a ceremony.|例句：They christened their daughter Mary. 他们为女儿取名玛丽。|例句：The ship was christened the 'Queen Mary'. 这艘船被命名为“玛丽皇后号”。|近义词：baptize, name, dedicate|反义词：unname


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They christen their child every Sunday. = 他们每周日都为他们的孩子施洗。
She christens her new car "The Beast". = 她为她的新车命名为“野兽”。


一般过去时:
The priest christened the baby. = 牧师为婴儿施洗。
My parents christened me John. = 我的父母给我取名为约翰。


现在完成时:
They have christened their new yacht. = 他们已经为他们的新游艇命名了。
She has christened her new novel "Winter's Embrace." = 她已经为她的新小说命名为“冬日的拥抱”。


过去完成时:
They had christened the ship before the launch ceremony. = 他们在下水仪式之前已经为船命名了。
The parents had christened the baby before the party. = 父母在派对之前已经为婴儿施洗了。


将来时:
We will christen our new house next month. = 我们下个月将为我们的新房子举行命名仪式。
They will christen the baby in the church. = 他们将在教堂为婴儿施洗。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would christen the boat "Ocean Dream". = 如果我是你，我会把船命名为“海洋梦”。
I wish they had christened the baby a more traditional name. = 我希望他们给婴儿取个更传统的名字。


被动语态:
The baby was christened last Sunday. = 婴儿上周日受洗了。
The new building was christened "The Innovation Center". = 新建筑被命名为“创新中心”。


疑问句:
When was the ship christened? = 这艘船是什么时候命名的？
Will the baby be christened in a church or at home? = 婴儿会在教堂受洗还是在家受洗？


否定句:
They did not christen their child until he was three years old. = 直到他三岁，他们才为他施洗。
She didn't christen her new painting yet. = 她还没有为她的新画命名。


PHRASE->
christen a ship = 为船命名
christen a child = 为孩子施洗
christen a new product = 为新产品举行发布仪式
christen a building = 为建筑物举行落成典礼
christen a project = 为项目启动仪式
