percolating: /ˈpɜːrkəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 渗透；慢慢扩散；酝酿；逐渐形成 |  The process of liquid slowly passing through a porous substance; to spread gradually; to brew (coffee); to develop slowly. |

例句：

The news of the scandal is slowly percolating through the community.  = 丑闻的消息正在慢慢地传遍整个社区。

Ideas were percolating in his mind as he walked through the park. = 他在公园散步时，脑海中不断涌现着各种想法。


近义词：infuse, seep, filter, spread, brew

反义词：stagnate, halt, stop
