mesocephalon:/ˌmɛsoʊˈsɛfəlɒn/| n.|中脑|The mesencephalon, also known as the midbrain, is a portion of the brainstem that is located between the diencephalon and the pons.|Example: The mesencephalon plays a crucial role in visual and auditory processing. 中脑在视觉和听觉处理中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：midbrain|反义词：无
