misemploys:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪz/ | vt. | 误用；滥用；错用 | To use wrongly or inefficiently.  | 例句：He misemployed his talents by working as a simple clerk. 他把自己的才能用错了地方，去做了一名普通的职员。


例句：The company misemployed its resources on a failed project.  这家公司把资源错用在一个失败的项目上。


近义词：misuse, misapply, waste


反义词：utilize, employ effectively, optimize


词性：动词

词源:
The word "misemploys" is formed from the prefix "mis-" (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") and the verb "employs" (meaning "to make use of").  Therefore, it literally means "to use wrongly" or "to use incorrectly."
