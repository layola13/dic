misspelling:/ˌmɪsˈspɛlɪŋ/ | n. | 拼写错误 | The incorrect spelling of a word.  Misspellings are common, especially in online writing.  | 例句：There are several misspellings in this essay. 这篇文章里有好几个拼写错误。  | 例句：The numerous misspellings in his letter showed his carelessness. 他信中大量的拼写错误显示了他的粗心。 | 近义词：error, mistake, blunder | 反义词：correct spelling
