haematoblast:/ˈhiːmætəʊblæst/| n. |造血干细胞，血细胞母细胞|A haematoblast is a hematopoietic stem cell, a type of cell in the bone marrow that can develop into various types of blood cells.  It's a precursor cell capable of differentiating into red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets.|例句：The study examined the role of haematoblasts in the development of leukemia. 这项研究考察了造血干细胞在白血病发展中的作用。|近义词：hematopoietic stem cell, hemocytoblast|反义词：无
