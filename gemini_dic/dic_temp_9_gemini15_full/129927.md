non-linear:/ˌnɒnˈlɪniər/| adj.|非线性的|Not forming a straight line; characterized by or exhibiting nonlinearity.|The relationship between the input and output is not proportional.  For example, in a nonlinear system, doubling the input does not necessarily double the output.|

The graph shows a non-linear relationship between temperature and pressure.  这条曲线图显示了温度和压力之间的非线性关系。

The non-linear equation is difficult to solve analytically.  这个非线性方程很难用解析方法求解。


近义词: curvilinear, curved, convoluted

反义词: linear
