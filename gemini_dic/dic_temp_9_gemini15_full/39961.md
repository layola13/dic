construe:/kənˈstruː/| vt.|解释，理解，翻译|To interpret or understand something in a particular way.|The poem can be construed in many different ways. 这首诗可以有多种不同的解读方式。|interpret, understand, explain|misinterpret, misunderstand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He construes the evidence to support his theory. 他解释证据以支持他的理论。
How do you construe this ambiguous statement? 你如何理解这个模棱两可的陈述？


一般过去时:
She construed his silence as agreement. 她把他的沉默理解为同意。
The judge construed the law differently than the defense attorney. 法官对法律的解释与辩护律师不同。


现在完成时:
Scholars have construed this passage in various ways over the centuries.几个世纪以来，学者们对这段话作出了各种解释。
We have construed their actions as a sign of hostility. 我们把他们的行动理解为敌意的迹象。


过去完成时:
They had construed the message to mean something entirely different. 他们之前已经将信息解释为完全不同的意思。
By then, I had construed her actions as a deliberate attempt to sabotage the project. 到那时，我已经把她的行为理解为蓄意破坏该项目的企图。


将来时:
He will construe the situation to his advantage. 他将利用这种情况来为自己谋利。
Future generations will likely construe our actions differently than we do. 后代可能会对我们的行为有不同的解读。


虚拟语气:
If I were to construe this email correctly, it means they've accepted our proposal. 如果我正确理解这封邮件，这意味着他们已经接受了我们的提案。
I wish I could construe the meaning of this ancient inscription.我希望我能解读这块古代铭文的含义。


被动语态:
His words were construed as a threat. 他的话被理解为威胁。
The passage can be construed in a number of ways. 这段话可以有多种解释。


疑问句:
How do you construe this action? 你如何解释这个行为？
How might historians construe this event in the future? 历史学家将来可能会如何解读这一事件？


否定句:
Don't construe my silence as agreement. 不要把我的沉默理解为同意。
I cannot construe what he's trying to say. 我无法理解他想说什么。


PHRASE->
construe something as something: 将某事理解为某事
construe something to mean something: 将某事理解为某种意思
construe something in a particular way: 以某种特定方式解释某事
construe something liberally/strictly: 宽松/严格地解释某事
construe a text/passage: 解释文本/段落
