/prɪˈzɛnt/ | v. n. adj. | 呈现；礼物；现在；出席 | to show, give, or offer something to somebody; something given to somebody as a gift; the present time; being present at a place or event |例句：He presented his findings to the committee. 他向委员会提交了他的调查结果。  He received a lovely present for his birthday. 他收到了一个可爱的生日礼物。|近义词：offer, gift, current, existing |反义词：past, future, absent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He presents his ideas clearly. 他清晰地表达了他的想法。
She presents the news every evening. 她每晚播报新闻。

一般过去时:
They presented a cheque to the charity. 他们向慈善机构捐赠了一张支票。
He presented a convincing argument. 他提出了一个令人信服的论点。

现在完成时:
She has presented many papers at conferences. 她已在许多会议上发表了论文。
He has presented his case to the judge. 他已向法官陈述了他的案情。

过去完成时:
They had presented their proposal before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前提交了他们的提案。
She had presented her research findings at several universities. 她已经在几所大学展示了她的研究成果。

将来时:
He will present his work at the exhibition. 他将在展览会上展示他的作品。
She will present the awards at the ceremony. 她将在仪式上颁发奖项。

虚拟语气:
If he presented more evidence, the jury would be convinced. 如果他提供更多证据，陪审团就会相信。
I wish he had presented his arguments more clearly. 我希望他更清晰地陈述他的论点。

被动语态:
The award was presented to the winner. 奖项颁发给了获胜者。
His findings were presented at the conference. 他的调查结果在会议上提交。

疑问句:
Will you present your project tomorrow? 你明天会展示你的项目吗？
When will the results be presented? 结果将在何时公布？

否定句:
He did not present any evidence. 他没有提供任何证据。
She did not present her case effectively. 她没有有效地陈述她的案情。
