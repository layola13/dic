forefingers:/ˈfɔːrfɪŋɡərz/| n. |食指|The forefingers are the fingers next to the thumbs. They are often used to point.  |例句：She pointed with her forefinger. 她用食指指着。|例句：He used his forefinger to trace the lines on the map. 他用食指沿着地图上的线条描绘。|近义词：index finger|反义词:无
