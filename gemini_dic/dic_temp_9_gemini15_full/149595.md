pails:/peɪlz/| n.|桶，提桶|A pail is a bucket, typically made of metal or plastic, used for carrying liquids or other materials.|例句：He carried a pail of water to the plants. 他提着一桶水去浇花。
例句：The children played happily in the sand, using pails and shovels. 孩子们快乐地在沙滩上玩耍，用着桶和铲子。|近义词：bucket, container|反义词：无
