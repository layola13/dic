phoenix:/ˈfiːnɪks/| n.|凤凰；浴火重生|A phoenix is a mythical bird that cyclically regenerates or is otherwise born again.  Associated with the sun, it is often depicted as cyclically dying in a burst of fire, only to be immediately reborn from the ashes.  The phoenix also symbolizes immortality, rebirth, and resurrection.|例句：The phoenix, a symbol of rebirth, rose from the ashes. 凤凰，作为重生的象征，从灰烬中升起。|例句：The company, after bankruptcy, is like a phoenix rising from the ashes. 这家公司在破产后，如同凤凰涅槃般浴火重生。|近义词：rebirth, resurrection, regeneration|反义词：death, extinction
