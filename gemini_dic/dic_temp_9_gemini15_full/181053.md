reincorporate:/ˌriːɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/| vt.|重新合并；使恢复法人资格|To combine or unite again; to restore to corporate status.|The company was reincorporated in Delaware after its merger.|近义词：reintegrate, reunite, reconstitute|反义词：discorporate, dissolve
