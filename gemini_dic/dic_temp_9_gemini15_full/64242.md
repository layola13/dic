equipage:/ˈɛkwɪpɪdʒ/| n.|装备，行李，马具|Equipment, especially for a journey or expedition; the harness and trappings of a horse.  |例句：The expedition was well equipaged for a long journey in the wilderness. 探险队装备精良，足以应付在荒野中的长途跋涉。|例句：The horse's equipage was beautifully crafted. 这匹马的马具制作精美。|近义词：equipment, gear, outfit, harness|反义词：lack of equipment, unpreparedness|名词
