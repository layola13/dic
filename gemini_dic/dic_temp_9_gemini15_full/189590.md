salicylol:/ˌsælɪˈsaɪləl/| n.|水杨醛|Salicylol, also known as salicylaldehyde, is an organic compound with the formula C₇H₆O₂.  It's a colorless to pale yellow liquid with a strong odor. It's used in the synthesis of other chemicals, particularly pharmaceuticals and perfumes.  |Example: Salicylol is a key intermediate in the production of aspirin.|近义词: salicylaldehyde|反义词: N/A
