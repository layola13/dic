consensus:/kənˈsɛnsəs/| n. |共识，一致意见 | a general agreement among a group of people | After much debate, they finally reached a consensus on the issue.  经过长时间的辩论，他们最终就这个问题达成了一致意见。| agreement, accord, unanimity | dissent, disagreement, discord | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team reaches a consensus on the project plan. = 团队就项目计划达成了一致意见。
A consensus exists regarding the best course of action. = 关于最佳行动方案，存在着共识。


一般过去时:
The committee reached a consensus last night. = 委员会昨晚达成了一致意见。
A consensus emerged from the lengthy discussions. = 在冗长的讨论之后，达成了一致意见。


现在完成时:
They have reached a consensus after weeks of negotiation. = 经过数周的谈判，他们已经达成了一致意见。
A consensus has formed regarding the new policy. = 关于这项新政策，已经形成了共识。


过去完成时:
By the end of the meeting, a consensus had been reached. = 到会议结束时，已经达成了一致意见。
Before the vote, a consensus had already developed among the members. = 在投票之前，成员之间已经形成了共识。


将来时:
We hope to reach a consensus by the end of the week. = 我们希望在本周末达成共识。
A consensus will likely emerge after further discussion. = 进一步讨论后，可能会达成共识。


虚拟语气:
If we could only reach a consensus, we could move forward. = 如果我们能够达成共识，我们就能继续前进。
I wish we could reach a consensus on this matter. = 我希望我们能够就此事达成共识。


被动语态:
A consensus was reached on the proposed budget. = 就拟议的预算达成了一致意见。
A consensus is being sought on the best way to proceed. = 正在寻求关于最佳行动方案的共识。


疑问句:
Has a consensus been reached yet? = 已经达成共识了吗？
Is there a consensus on this topic? = 关于这个话题有没有共识？


否定句:
They did not reach a consensus on the matter. = 他们在这个问题上没有达成共识。
There was no consensus among the participants. = 与会者之间没有达成共识。


PHRASE->
reach a consensus = 达成共识
build a consensus = 建立共识
broad consensus = 广泛共识
general consensus =  普遍共识
lack of consensus = 缺乏共识
