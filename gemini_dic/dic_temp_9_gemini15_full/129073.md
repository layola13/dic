nitroglycerine:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈɡlɪsərɪn/| n. |硝化甘油|A pale yellow, oily, explosive liquid made by nitrating glycerol. It is used in the manufacture of explosives and as a medicine to treat angina. |例句：Nitroglycerine is a powerful explosive. 硝化甘油是一种强力炸药。|例句：Doctors sometimes prescribe nitroglycerine to patients with angina.  医生有时会为心绞痛患者开硝化甘油。|近义词：glyceryl trinitrate|反义词:|无
