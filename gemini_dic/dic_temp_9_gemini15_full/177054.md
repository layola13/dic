reanoint:/ˌriːəˈnɔɪnt/| vt.|重新涂油；再次圣膏；重新委任|To anoint again; to consecrate or invest anew with an office or dignity.|例句：The priest will reanoint the altar before the mass.  祭司将在弥撒前重新涂抹祭坛。

例句：The newly elected president was reanointed with a renewed sense of responsibility.  新当选的总统带着更新的责任感重新就职。


近义词：consecrate, invest, inaugurate


反义词：desecrate, un-invest, dismiss


词性：动词
