porphyric:/pɔːˈfɪrɪk/| adj.|紫红色的；斑岩的；紫苏病的|Relating to or characteristic of porphyry, a volcanic rock with large crystals embedded in a fine-grained matrix.  Also used to describe a condition characterized by purple discoloration of the skin, often associated with porphyria.|The porphyric texture of the rock was evident under the microscope.  = 在显微镜下，岩石的斑状结构很明显。The patient presented with porphyric skin lesions. = 病人出现紫红色的皮肤损害。|igneous, volcanic|pale, normal|形容词
