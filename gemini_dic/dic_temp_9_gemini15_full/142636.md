ostlers:/ˈɒstləz/| n.|马夫；马厩管理员|A person who looks after horses at an inn or stable.|The ostlers were busy grooming the horses before the guests arrived.|近义词：groom, stablehand|反义词：无

一般现在时:
The ostler cleans the stables every morning. = 马夫每天早上都打扫马厩。
The ostlers care for the horses diligently. = 马夫们勤勉地照顾马匹。


一般过去时:
The ostler fed the horses before nightfall. = 马夫在夜幕降临前喂了马。
The ostlers saddled the horses for the journey. = 马夫们为旅程为马匹备鞍。


现在完成时:
The ostler has mucked out the stables. = 马夫已经清理了马厩。
The ostlers have prepared the horses for the race. = 马夫们已经为比赛准备好了马匹。


过去完成时:
