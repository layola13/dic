foresee:/fɔːˈsiː/ | vt. | 预见；预知 | To see or know beforehand; anticipate.  |例句：I foresee a bright future for this company. 我预见这家公司会有一个光明的未来。|例句：We failed to foresee the consequences of our actions. 我们没有预见到我们行为的后果。|近义词：predict, anticipate, foretell |反义词：overlook, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I foresee a successful outcome. = 我预见一个成功的结局。
She foresees potential problems. = 她预见到潜在的问题。

一般过去时:
They foresaw the economic downturn. = 他们预见了经济衰退。
He foresaw the storm approaching. = 他预见到了暴风雨即将来临。

现在完成时:
We have foreseen many challenges in this project. = 我们在这个项目中已经预见了许多挑战。
She has foreseen this complication for weeks. = 她几周前就预见到了这个复杂的情况。

过去完成时:
I had foreseen the difficulty, so I prepared accordingly. = 我预见到困难，所以做了相应的准备。
They had foreseen the need for extra staff. = 他们预见到需要额外的员工。

将来时:
I will foresee the risks involved. = 我将会预见到其中涉及的风险。
The analysts will foresee a market crash. = 分析师们将会预见到市场崩盘。

虚拟语气:
If I had foreseen the problem, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预见了这个问题，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had foreseen the consequences. = 我真希望我预见到了后果。

被动语态:
The potential risks were foreseen by the management. = 潜在的风险已被管理层预见。
The consequences were not foreseen by anyone. = 后果没有人预见到。

疑问句:
Do you foresee any difficulties? = 你预见到任何困难吗？
Can you foresee the future of technology? = 你能预见技术的未来吗？

否定句:
I did not foresee the sudden change in weather. = 我没有预见到天气的突然变化。
They failed to foresee the negative impacts. = 他们没能预见到负面影响。
