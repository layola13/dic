finger-tip: /ˈfɪŋɡərˌtɪp/ | n. |指尖；触手可及|The end of a finger.  Something that is at one's fingertips is easily accessible or obtainable.|The artist's skill was at his fingertips.  He could paint anything with incredible ease.|近义词：finger end|反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The information is at my fingertips. = 信息触手可及。
Her skills are always at her fingertips. = 她的技能总是运用自如。


一般过去时:
The answer was at my fingertips. = 答案当时唾手可得。
The solution was at his fingertips. = 解决方法当时他立刻想到了。


现在完成时:
The knowledge has been at my fingertips for years. =  多年来，这些知识一直掌握在我的手中。
I have had this information at my fingertips since last week. = 从上周开始，我就掌握了这个信息。


过去完成时:

All the necessary details had been at her fingertips before the presentation. = 演示之前所有必要的细节都在她掌握之中。


将来时:
The report will be at my fingertips by tomorrow. = 明天报告就能拿到手了。

疑问句:
