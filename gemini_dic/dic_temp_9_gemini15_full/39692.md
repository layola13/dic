consideration:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |考虑，体谅，因素，报酬 |The act of thinking carefully about something.  It can also refer to thoughtful care for someone's feelings or needs, or a factor to be considered in making a decision.  It can also mean payment or a bribe. |例句：The project requires careful consideration of all the potential risks.  这个项目需要仔细考虑所有潜在的风险。

例句：He showed great consideration for the feelings of others. 他非常体谅别人的感受。

近义词：thought, deliberation, reflection, regard, payment

反义词：neglect, disregard, inconsiderateness


词源:  From Old French *consideracioun, from Latin consideratio(n-), from considerare "to consider," from com- "together" + sidus "star" (originally referring to observing the stars for guidance). The modern meaning evolved gradually.

用法说明:


名词用法:

单数形式:  a consideration, one consideration, much consideration

复数形式: considerations

介词搭配: under consideration (正在考虑中), for consideration (供考虑), in consideration of (考虑到……, 鉴于……), out of consideration for (出于对……的考虑)


例句 (名词用法):


1.  The company's decision was made after careful consideration of all the facts. 公司在仔细考虑所有事实后做出了决定。

2.  Cost is an important consideration when planning a large project.  在规划大型项目时，成本是一个重要的考虑因素。

3.  In consideration of his age, he was given a lighter sentence. 考虑到他的年龄，他被判了较轻的刑罚。

4.  The deal fell through because of a lack of consideration from both sides. 这笔交易因为双方缺乏考虑而告吹。


动词用法: (consider)


一般现在时:
I consider this matter very important. 我认为这件事非常重要。
They consider their options before making a decision. 他们会在做决定之前考虑他们的选择。

一般过去时:
She considered her options carefully before making a decision. 她仔细权衡了自己的选择之后才做出了决定。
He considered the proposal thoroughly. 他仔细考虑了这个提案。

现在完成时:
I have considered all the possibilities. 我已经考虑了所有可能性。
They have considered the impact on the environment. 他们已经考虑了对环境的影响。

过去完成时:
I had considered quitting my job before I received the promotion. 在我升职之前，我曾考虑过辞职。
She had considered many options before choosing this one. 在选择这个方案之前，她已经考虑过许多方案。

将来时:
I will consider your suggestion. 我会考虑你的建议。
We will consider the matter further. 我们会进一步考虑这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would consider the consequences carefully. 如果我是你，我会仔细考虑后果。
I wish I had considered the risks more carefully. 我希望我当时能更仔细地考虑风险。

被动语态:
The proposal is being considered by the board. 该提案正在由董事会考虑。
Your application will be considered shortly. 你的申请很快就会被考虑。

疑问句:
Do you consider this a good idea? 你认为这是一个好主意吗？
What factors did you consider when making your decision? 你在做决定时考虑了哪些因素？

否定句:
I don't consider myself an expert. 我不认为自己是个专家。
They didn't consider the long-term effects. 他们没有考虑长远的影响。
