mcmurdo:/ˈmʌkmɜːrdoʊ/| n. |麦克默多|McMurdo is a research station located on Ross Island, Antarctica. It is the largest research station in Antarctica and serves as a hub for scientific research and logistical support for other Antarctic research stations. The name is derived from the nearby McMurdo Sound.|The McMurdo Station is a vital base for Antarctic research.|例句：McMurdo Station is the largest research station in Antarctica. 麦克默多站是南极洲最大的研究站。 The scientists at McMurdo Station are conducting important research on climate change. 麦克默多站的科学家们正在进行关于气候变化的重要研究。|近义词：Antarctic research station|反义词：无
