interstreaked:/ˌɪntərˈstriːkt/| adj.| 交织的；杂色的 |  Marked by streaks of different colors or substances; interwoven or intermingled.  |例句：The marble countertop was beautifully interstreaked with shades of grey and white. 大理石台面漂亮地交织着灰色和白色的色调。

The sunset sky was interstreaked with fiery orange and deep purple.  日落的天空交织着火红的橙色和深紫色。|近义词：striped, variegated, mottled, streaked |反义词：uniform, plain, solid
