contemporary: /kənˈtɛmpəreri/ | adj. | 当代的；同时代的；现代的 | Belonging to the same period of time; existing, occurring, or living at the same time.  |例句：Contemporary art often challenges traditional notions of beauty. 当代艺术常常挑战传统的审美观念。
例句：He is a contemporary of Shakespeare. 他是莎士比亚的同代人。|近义词：modern, current, present-day, simultaneous |反义词：ancient, archaic, obsolete, past
