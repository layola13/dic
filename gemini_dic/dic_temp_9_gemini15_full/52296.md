dichroscopic:/ˌdaɪkrəˈskɒpɪk/| adj.|二向色性的；双色性的|Exhibiting dichroism; appearing in two different colors when viewed in polarized light from different directions.  The property of appearing in two different colors when viewed with polarized light from different directions is called dichroism. Dichroscopic materials are commonly used in polarizing filters.|例句：The dichroscopic crystal showed a deep blue in one orientation and a pale yellow in another.  这种二向色性晶体在一个方向上显示深蓝色，而在另一个方向上显示浅黄色。


例句：Some gemstones exhibit dichroism, appearing in different colors depending on the angle of observation. 一些宝石表现出二向色性，呈现不同的颜色，这取决于观察的角度。


近义词：bichromatic, pleochroic|反义词：monochromatic
