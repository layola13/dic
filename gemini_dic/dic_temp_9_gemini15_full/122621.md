modesty:/ˈmɑːdəsti/ /ˈmɒdəsti/| n.|谦逊；适度；端庄；朴素|The quality of being modest; the avoidance of vanity; humility.  It can also refer to the quality of being unassuming in behavior or appearance.|例句：Her modesty prevented her from boasting about her achievements.  她的谦逊使她无法夸耀自己的成就。

例句：The simple design of the house reflects a certain modesty.  房子的简洁设计体现出一种朴素。


近义词：humility, humbleness, unassumingness, diffidence, reserve|反义词：vanity, arrogance, boastfulness, ostentation, pride|词性：名词
