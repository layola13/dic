careerwomen: /kəˈrɪrwʊmən/ | n. |职业女性| Women who have careers, typically in professional or managerial positions.  The term often emphasizes their commitment to their work and professional achievements. | Example:  Many careerwomen struggle to balance work and family life. 许多职业女性都在努力平衡工作和家庭生活。  |近义词: working women, professional women | 反义词: housewives (in a contrasting sense)
