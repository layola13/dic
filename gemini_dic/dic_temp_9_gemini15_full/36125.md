coessential:/ˌkoʊɪˈsɛnʃəl/| adj.| 本质的，必要的，同等的 | Essentially necessary or fundamental;  inseparably connected.  |例句：Faith and reason are coessential to a complete understanding of the world. 信仰和理性对于完整理解世界是同等重要的。

例句：The artist's skill and creativity are coessential to the success of the artwork. 艺术家的技巧和创造力对于艺术品的成功是必要的。


近义词：essential, fundamental, indispensable, integral


反义词：unnecessary, incidental, superfluous


词性：形容词
