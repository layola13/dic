pressurizes:/ˈprɛʃəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |施加压力，使…承受压力 | To put pressure on someone or something; to force someone or something to do something.  |例句：The company pressurizes its employees to meet unrealistic deadlines. 公司强迫员工在不切实际的期限内完成工作。
例句：The government is pressurizing the opposition party to accept the new laws. 政府正向反对党施压，要求其接受新法律。 |近义词：coerce, compel, force, influence |反义词：support, encourage, assist |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The manager pressurizes his team to work overtime. = 经理强迫他的团队加班。
They constantly pressurize me to make a decision. = 他们不断地向我施压让我做决定。

一般过去时:
The deadline pressurised her to work faster. = 截止日期迫使她加快工作速度。
He pressurised his friend into lending him money. = 他强迫他的朋友借给他钱。


现在完成时:
The campaign has pressurised the government to act. = 这场运动已经向政府施压促使其采取行动。
She has been pressurised into accepting a job she doesn't want. = 她被迫接受了一份她不想要的工作。


过去完成时:
The threat of war had pressurised the nations into negotiation. = 战争的威胁迫使各国进行谈判。
They had pressurised him into confessing before the trial. = 他们在审判前就迫使他认罪了。


将来时:
The impending storm will pressurize the building's structure. = 即将来临的风暴将给建筑物的结构带来压力。
They will pressurize us to sign the contract. = 他们会向我们施压让我们签合同。


虚拟语气:
If they pressurised him harder, he might confess. = 如果他们对他施加更大的压力，他可能会认罪。
I wish they wouldn't pressurize me so much. = 我希望他们不要给我这么大的压力。


被动语态:
The employees felt pressurised to work overtime. = 员工感到被迫加班。
The negotiations were pressurised by the threat of a strike. = 罢工的威胁给谈判带来了压力。


疑问句:
Does the management pressurize employees to work late? = 管理层是否会强迫员工加班？
Are they pressurizing you to buy the product? = 他们是否正在向你施压让你购买产品？


否定句:
The company doesn't pressurize its employees unnecessarily. = 公司不会不必要地向员工施压。
He didn't pressurize her to make a decision. = 他没有强迫她做决定。


PHRASE->

feel pressurised = 感到有压力
