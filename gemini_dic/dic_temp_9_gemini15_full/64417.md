/ˌɜːɡənəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj. |符合人体工程学的；符合人机工程学的 | Relating to the design and arrangement of workplaces, products, and systems so that they are suitable for people to use without causing discomfort or injury.  Ergonomics studies the interaction between humans and their work environment. | Example: The office chairs are ergonomically designed to support the back and promote good posture.  办公室的椅子设计符合人体工程学，以支撑背部并促进良好姿势。  The new keyboard is ergonomically superior to the old one. 新键盘在人体工程学方面优于旧键盘。 | 近义词:human-factors, anthropometric | 反义词:unergonomic, ill-designed
