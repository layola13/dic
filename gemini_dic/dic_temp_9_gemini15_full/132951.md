nonemissions: /ˌnɑːnɪˈmɪʃənz/ | n. | 无排放；零排放 | The absence of emissions, especially of pollutants into the atmosphere.  This term often refers to vehicles, power plants, or industrial processes that produce zero or negligible emissions. | Example: The company is investing heavily in nonemissions technologies.  这家公司正在大力投资无排放技术。

The development of electric vehicles is driving the growth of nonemissions transportation.电动汽车的研发正在推动无排放交通工具的发展。 | 近义词：zero emissions, emission-free | 反义词：high emissions, polluting emissions | 名词
