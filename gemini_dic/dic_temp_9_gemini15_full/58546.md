dugout:/ˈdʌɡaʊt/| n.| dugout, dugout shelter |简易掩体；（棒球场的）教练席；|A dugout is a simple shelter, often dug into the ground.  It can also refer to the shelter used by baseball coaches and players. |The soldiers took shelter in the dugout during the bombing.  士兵们在轰炸期间躲进了掩体。 The manager emerged from the dugout to argue with the umpire. 经理从教练席出来与裁判争论。|shelter, trench, foxhole| (no direct antonym)
