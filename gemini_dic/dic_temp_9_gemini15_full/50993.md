desires:/dɪˈzaɪərz/| n.|愿望，欲望|a strong feeling of wanting to have something or wishing for something to happen|例句：He desires wealth and power. 他渴望财富和权力。

例句：Her desires are simple: a loving family and a good job. 她的愿望很简单：一个充满爱的家庭和一份好工作。


近义词：wishes, longings, yearnings, cravings|反义词：dislikes, aversions


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He desires a peaceful life. = 他渴望平静的生活。
She desires to travel the world. = 她渴望环游世界。


一般过去时:
He desired a promotion. = 他渴望升职。
She desired a quiet evening at home. = 她渴望在家里度过一个安静的夜晚。


现在完成时:
He has desired this for many years. = 他多年来一直渴望这个。
She has desired to meet her idol. = 她一直渴望见到她的偶像。


过去完成时:
He had desired a new car before the accident. = 事故发生前，他一直渴望拥有一辆新车。
She had desired to become a doctor since childhood. = 她从小就渴望成为一名医生。


将来时:
He will desire more responsibility in the future. = 他将来会渴望承担更多责任。
She will desire a change of scenery. = 她会渴望换个环境。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could fulfill all my desires. = 我希望我能实现我所有的愿望。
If I had known his desires, I would have helped him. = 如果我知道他的愿望，我就会帮助他。


被动语态:
His desires were not met. = 他的愿望没有得到满足。
Her desires are often overlooked. = 她的愿望常常被忽视。


疑问句:
What are your desires? = 你的愿望是什么？
What does he desire most? = 他最渴望什么？


否定句:
He doesn't desire fame or fortune. = 他不渴望名利。
She doesn't desire to be involved. = 她不想参与其中。
