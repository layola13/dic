demoed:/dɛˈmoʊd/ | v. | 演示；展示；试用 | To demonstrate or show the capabilities of something, often a product or software.  |例句：The company demoed their new software at the conference.  公司在会议上演示了他们的新软件。

例句：We demoed the car before we bought it. 我们在买车之前试驾了这辆车。

近义词：demonstrated, showcased, presented, exhibited | 反义词：concealed, hidden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They demo the new product every day. = 他们每天演示新产品。
The company demos its latest software to potential clients. = 公司向潜在客户演示其最新的软件。

一般过去时:
We demoed the software to the clients yesterday. = 我们昨天向客户演示了该软件。
The team demoed the prototype at the investor meeting. = 团队在投资者会议上演示了原型。

现在完成时:
They have demoed the product at several trade shows. = 他们在几个贸易展销会上演示了该产品。
We have demoed the new features to our beta testers. = 我们已经向我们的测试用户演示了新功能。

过去完成时:
They had already demoed the system before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前就已经演示过该系统了。
The developers had demoed the app to the marketing team. = 开发人员已经向营销团队演示了该应用程序。

将来时:
We will demo the updated version next week. = 我们下周将演示更新版本。
The company will demo its new technology at the upcoming expo. = 公司将在即将举行的博览会上演示其新技术。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have demoed the full range of features. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们将演示全套功能。
I wish we had demoed the product earlier. = 我希望我们早点演示这个产品。

被动语态:
