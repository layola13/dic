lao:/laʊ/| adj.| 老的；老练的；资深的 | Old; experienced; senior.  |例句：He's a lao hand at this job. 他是这工作的熟手。 |例句：She's a lao teacher with many years of experience. 她是一位经验丰富的资深教师。|近义词：old, veteran, seasoned |反义词：young, new, inexperienced


USAGE->

He is lao. = 他老了。

否定句:
