reputes:/rɪˈpjuːts/| n.|名誉，名声|The general opinion of someone or something.  It often refers to a good reputation, but can also be used for a bad one.|例句：The company has a good reputation for quality. 这家公司以其高质量而享有盛誉。

例句：His reputation was ruined by the scandal.  丑闻毁了他的名声。

近义词：fame, renown, standing, prestige

反义词：disrepute, infamy, ill-repute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His work reputably earns him praise. = 他的作品因其可靠性而获得赞扬。
The restaurant enjoys a good reputation among locals. = 这家餐馆在当地人中享有盛誉。

一般过去时:
The politician's reputation was damaged by the scandal. = 这名政治家的名声因丑闻而受损。
The artist's work was initially poorly reputed, but later gained recognition. = 这名艺术家的作品最初评价不佳，但后来获得了认可。


现在完成时:
The company has built a strong reputation over many years. = 这家公司多年来建立了良好的声誉。
His actions have seriously damaged his reputation. = 他的行为严重损害了他的名誉。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal, he had enjoyed an impeccable reputation. = 在丑闻之前，他享有无可挑剔的声誉。
The company had already built a poor reputation before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经建立了不良的声誉。


将来时:
This new product will hopefully enhance our reputation. = 这款新产品有望提升我们的声誉。
Their actions will likely damage their reputation in the long run. = 从长远来看，他们的行为可能会损害他们的声誉。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't lied, he would have maintained his reputation. = 如果他没有撒谎，他就能保持自己的名誉。
I wish my reputation wasn't tarnished by those rumors. = 我希望我的名誉不会因为那些谣言而受损。


被动语态:
His reputation was unfairly damaged by the media. = 他的名誉被媒体不公平地损害了。
The company's reputation is being carefully managed by its PR team. = 该公司的声誉正由其公关团队精心管理。


疑问句:
What is the company's reputation? = 这家公司的声誉如何？
Does this product have a good reputation? = 这个产品口碑好吗？


否定句:
The product does not have a good reputation. = 该产品没有良好的声誉。
The company's reputation is not what it used to be. = 这家公司的声誉不如以前了。


PHRASE->
have a good reputation = 享有盛誉
damage one's reputation = 损害某人的名誉
ruin one's reputation = 毁掉某人的名誉
build a reputation = 建立声誉
enhance one's reputation = 提升某人的名声
