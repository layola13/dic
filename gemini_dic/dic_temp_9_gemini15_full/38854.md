confeder:/kənˈfɛdər/| n. |同盟者；联盟；邦联|A confederacy is a group of people, countries, or organizations that are joined together in an alliance or federation.  It often implies a loose association, rather than a fully unified entity.  The term can also refer to a person who is a member of such a group.|例句：The states formed a confederation to defend against foreign invasion.  各州结成联盟以抵御外来侵略。


例句：The rebellious groups formed a loose confederation against the central government.  反抗组织结成了一个松散的联盟来对抗中央政府。


近义词：alliance, federation, league, coalition|反义词：enemy, opponent, adversary|名词
