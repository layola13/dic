cicada:/sɪˈkɑːdə/| n. |蝉|A cicada is a large insect with transparent wings and a loud, high-pitched buzz.  They are known for their long life cycles, with some species spending years underground as nymphs before emerging as adults. |例句：The cicadas were buzzing loudly in the trees.  树上的蝉鸣声很大。|  例句：The emergence of the periodical cicadas is a spectacular natural event. 定期蝉的出现是一场壮观的自然事件。|近义词：locust (in some contexts), insect|反义词：无
