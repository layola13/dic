knitchet:/ˈnɪtʃɪt/| n. |钩针编织；钩针编织品|A knitchet is a type of needlework that uses a hook to create fabric from yarn or thread.  It is similar to crochet, but often involves different techniques and patterns.|She made a beautiful knitchet blanket for her baby.|crochet,  needlework| weaving (反义词较为宽泛，因为knitchet是编织的一种方式，而weaving是编织的总称)|名词
