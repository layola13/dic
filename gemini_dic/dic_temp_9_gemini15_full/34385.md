classifiers:/ˈklæsɪˌfaɪərz/| n. |分类器，分类法|A classifier is a system or algorithm used to categorize or classify data, objects, or information into predefined categories or groups.  It's a fundamental concept in machine learning, data analysis, and many other fields. |Example:  The image classifier correctly identified the object as a cat.  图像分类器正确地将物体识别为猫。 The new software uses a sophisticated classifier to sort emails into spam and not spam folders. 新软件使用复杂的分类器将电子邮件分类到垃圾邮件和非垃圾邮件文件夹中。|近义词：categorizer, sorter, identifier, type|反义词：unifier (in some contexts)|名词
