siderography:/ˌsaɪdəˈrɑːɡrəfi/| n. |钢版印刷术；金属版画|The process of printing from steel plates.  It is a type of intaglio printing where the image is incised into the plate.  The process is similar to other intaglio methods like etching and engraving, but uses steel plates instead of copper or other materials.|Example:  The museum showcased a stunning collection of prints created using siderography. = 博物馆展示了一套令人惊叹的采用钢版印刷术制作的版画。The artist mastered the technique of siderography to create detailed landscapes. =这位艺术家掌握了钢版印刷术，创作出精细的风景画。|近义词：steel plate printing, intaglio printing|反义词：relief printing
