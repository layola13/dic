rugosity:/ruːˈɡɒsəti/| n. |粗糙度；凹凸不平；褶皱 |The unevenness or roughness of a surface.  It refers to the degree to which a surface deviates from being perfectly smooth.  This can be caused by various factors, including texture, imperfections, and irregularities. |例句：The rugosity of the rock surface made it difficult to climb.  岩石表面的粗糙使攀爬变得困难。

The high rugosity of the seabed affected the sonar readings. 海底的高粗糙度影响了声纳读数。|近义词：roughness, unevenness, irregularity, texture |反义词：smoothness, smoothness, evenness
