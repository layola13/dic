honor:/ˈɑːnər/ /ˈɑːnɚ/| n. vt.|荣誉，尊敬，光荣 |to show great respect for someone or something|例句：He received many honors for his work. 他因其工作获得了许多荣誉。|近义词：respect, esteem, reverence |反义词：dishonor, shame, disgrace | 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He honors his parents. = 他尊敬他的父母。
She honors her commitments. = 她信守承诺。


一般过去时:
They honored the agreement. = 他们遵守了协议。
We honored our guests with a feast. = 我们用盛宴款待了我们的客人。


现在完成时:
She has honored her promise. = 她已经信守了诺言。
He has been honored with numerous awards. = 他获得了许多奖项的殊荣。


过去完成时:
They had honored the treaty before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前，他们已经履行了条约。
She had honored her father's memory by establishing a scholarship. = 她通过设立奖学金来纪念她的父亲。


将来时:
We will honor our guests with a special dinner. = 我们将用特别的晚餐款待我们的客人。
They will honor their commitments to the project. = 他们将信守对项目的承诺。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would honor my word. = 如果我是你，我会信守我的诺言。
I wish I had honored my parents' advice. = 我真希望我听了父母的劝告。


被动语态:
The soldier was honored for his bravery. = 这名士兵因其勇敢而受到表彰。
The agreement was honored by both parties. = 双方都遵守了协议。


疑问句:
Will they honor the contract? = 他们会遵守合同吗？
Does he honor his commitments? = 他信守承诺吗？


否定句:
He did not honor his promise. = 他没有信守诺言。
They dishonored the agreement. = 他们违反了协议。


PHRASE->
in honor of = 为了纪念...；向...表示敬意
honor roll = 光荣榜
a matter of honor = 荣誉问题
on my honor = 我以人格担保
honor system = 诚信制度

词源:
From Old French *honour*, from Latin *honor*, "honor, respect, reverence".  The word's journey reflects the consistent importance placed upon respect and dignity across cultures and time.
