extends:/ɪkˈstɛndz/| v.|扩展，延伸，延长|To stretch out in length or area; to make something longer or larger; to continue something over a longer period of time.  |例句：The road extends for miles. 这条路延伸数英里。|例句：We've extended the deadline for submissions. 我们延长了提交截止日期。|近义词：prolong, lengthen, expand, amplify|反义词：shorten, contract, reduce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extends his hand in greeting. = 他伸出手打招呼。
The park extends to the river. = 公园延伸到河边。


一般过去时:
The company extended its operations to Asia. = 这家公司将其业务扩展到亚洲。
She extended her stay in London by a week. = 她将她在伦敦的逗留时间延长了一周。


现在完成时:
They have extended the warranty on the product. = 他们已经延长了产品的保修期。
We have extended our services to new customers. = 我们已经将我们的服务扩展到新的客户。


过去完成时:
They had extended the deadline before we submitted the report. = 在我们提交报告之前，他们已经延长了截止日期。
The bridge had extended across the river for decades. = 这座桥已经横跨河流几十年了。


将来时:
The government will extend the tax break for another year. = 政府将把税收减免延长一年。
We will extend our help to those in need. = 我们将向有需要的人提供帮助。


虚拟语气:
If they extended the deadline, we could finish the project. = 如果他们延长截止日期，我们就能完成项目。
I wish they extended the holiday. = 我希望他们延长假期。


被动语态:
The contract was extended for another year. = 合同延长了一年。
The deadline has been extended. = 截止日期已被延长。


疑问句:
Does the park extend to the beach? = 公园延伸到海滩吗？
Will you extend your kindness to others? = 你会对其他人施以你的善意吗？


否定句:
The road doesn't extend beyond the village. = 这条路没有延伸到村庄以外。
They did not extend their invitation to me. = 他们没有邀请我。


PHRASE->
extend an invitation = 发出邀请

extend one's stay = 延长逗留
extend a warm welcome = 热烈欢迎
extend one's condolences = 表示慰问
