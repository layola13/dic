potshard:/ˈpɒtʃɑːrd/| n. |陶片，瓦片 |A fragment of a broken pot or earthenware vessel.  Potshards are often found at archaeological sites and can provide valuable information about past cultures. |Example: The archaeologists carefully collected the potshards, hoping to reconstruct the ancient vessels.  考古学家小心翼翼地收集陶片，希望能重建古代的器皿。  |近义词：shard, fragment, piece |反义词：whole pot, intact vessel |名词
