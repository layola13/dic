colourfully:/ˈkʌləfli/| adj.|色彩鲜艳地；丰富多彩地|Describes something that is full of bright colors or a variety of colors, often in a lively or attractive way. It can also refer to something expressed in a vivid and imaginative way.|例句：The birds sang colourfully in the vibrant garden.  五彩缤纷的鸟儿在充满生机的花园里歌唱。

The artist painted the scene colourfully, using a wide range of hues.  艺术家用各种各样的色调，色彩鲜艳地描绘了这幅场景。|近义词：vividly, brightly, richly, picturesquely|反义词：monotonously, dully, blandly
