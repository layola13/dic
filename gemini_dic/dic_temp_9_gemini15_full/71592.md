flasks:/flæskz/ | n. |烧瓶；细颈瓶 | A flask is a container, typically made of glass or plastic, with a narrow neck and a round body, used for holding liquids.  |例句：The chemist carefully poured the liquid into the flask. 化学家小心地将液体倒入烧瓶中。

例句：She used a flask to carry her water on her hike. 她在徒步旅行时用烧瓶装水。


近义词：bottle, vial, container |反义词：None specifically, but depending on context, words implying large containers could be considered antonyms. |词性：n.
