salesmen:/ˈseɪlzmen/| n.|推销员，销售人员|A salesman is a person whose job is to sell goods or services.|例句：The salesman showed me several different models of cars.  这位推销员给我展示了几款不同的汽车。|例句：He's a very successful salesman, earning a high commission. 他是一位非常成功的推销员，赚取了很高的佣金。|近义词：sales representative, seller, vendor|反义词：customer, buyer


USAGE->

The salesman sells cars. = 这位推销员卖汽车。
Our salesmen often visit clients. = 我们的销售人员经常拜访客户。
