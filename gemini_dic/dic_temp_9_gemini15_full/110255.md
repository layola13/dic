lifestyle:/ˈlaɪfstaɪl/ | n. |生活方式 |The way a person or group of people live, including their habits, attitudes, and values.  It encompasses various aspects of daily life, such as work, leisure, diet, and social interactions. |例句：He adopted a healthier lifestyle after his doctor's warning. 他在医生的警告后过上了更健康的生活方式。|例句：Her lifestyle is quite different from mine. 她的生活方式与我的大相径庭。|近义词：way of life, living, mode of living |反义词：N/A |名词
