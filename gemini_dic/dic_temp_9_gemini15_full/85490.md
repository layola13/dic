hastate:/ˈheɪsteɪt/| adj.|戟形叶的；矛形的|Having the shape of a halberd or spearhead; shaped like a halberd, with two pointed basal lobes diverging at an acute angle from a central lobe.  The term is used primarily in botany to describe the shape of leaves.|The arrowhead plant has hastate leaves.|近义词：spear-shaped, lanceolate (but lanceolate is typically narrower and more pointed)|反义词：cordate (heart-shaped), ovate (egg-shaped)
