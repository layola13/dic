dash-down: /dæʃ daʊn/ | v. |猛冲；冲下；匆匆写下 |To move or fall suddenly and forcefully; to write something quickly and carelessly.  |例句：He dashed down the stairs. 他冲下楼梯。  The journalist dashed down the notes.  记者匆匆记下笔记。|近义词：hurtle, rush, scrawl |反义词：ascend, climb, slowly write |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dashes down the street every morning. = 他每天早上都在街上飞奔。
She dashes down her ideas as they come to her. = 她想到什么就匆匆记下什么。

一般过去时:
The children dashed down the hill. = 孩子们冲下了山。
He dashed down a quick note before leaving. = 他离开前匆匆记下了一张便条。

现在完成时:
She has dashed down several chapters of her novel. = 她已经匆匆写下了小说中的几章。
The rain has dashed down all afternoon. = 下午一直在下倾盆大雨。(这里dash down指倾盆而下)

过去完成时:
They had dashed down the stairs before the alarm sounded. = 警报响起前他们已经冲下了楼梯。
She had dashed down a quick email before the meeting. = 会议前她匆匆发了一封邮件。

将来时:
He will dash down to the store to buy milk. = 他将冲到商店去买牛奶。
She will dash down the report before the deadline. = 她将在截止日期前匆匆完成报告。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dash down a longer letter. = 如果我有更多时间，我会写一封更长的信。
I wish I could dash down to the beach. = 我真希望我能跑到海滩去。

被动语态:
The message was dashed down on a scrap of paper. = 这条消息匆匆写在了一张纸片上。
The notes were dashed down too quickly to be legible. = 笔记记得太快，无法辨认。

疑问句:
Did he dash down the stairs? = 他是冲下楼梯的吗？
Will she dash down the report on time? = 她会按时完成报告吗？

否定句:
He didn't dash down the street; he walked calmly. = 他没有在街上飞奔；他平静地走着。
She didn't dash down the notes; she wrote them carefully. = 她没有匆匆记下笔记；她认真地写下了它们。
