clones:/kloʊnz/| n. v.|克隆，复制品；克隆，复制|A clone is a genetically identical copy of an organism or cell.  Clones can be created naturally or artificially.  The verb "to clone" means to make a genetic copy.|例句：Scientists have successfully cloned a sheep. 科学家们成功地克隆了一只羊。

The cloned plant showed identical characteristics to the original. 克隆植物显示出与原植物相同的特性。|近义词：copy, duplicate, replica|反义词：original, unique
