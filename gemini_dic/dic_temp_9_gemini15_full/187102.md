riviere:/riˈvjɛər/| n. |河，溪流；项链| A rivière is a type of jewelry, typically a necklace, consisting of a continuous line of gemstones or precious stones set close together, often without visible metal between them.  It can also refer to a small river or stream. |Example: She wore a stunning diamond rivière necklace to the gala. 她戴了一条令人惊艳的钻石项链参加盛会。|近义词：necklace, stream, brook |反义词：mountain, upland
