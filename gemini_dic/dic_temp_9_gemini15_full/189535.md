salaries:/ˈsæləriːz/ | n. | 工资（复数） | Salaries are paid monthly. 工资每月支付。|近义词：wages, pay, remuneration | 反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company pays its employees fair salaries. = 公司支付员工合理的工资。

The salaries of executives are often very high. = 高管的薪水通常非常高。


一般过去时:
Last year, my salary was much lower. = 去年我的工资低得多。

They received their salaries on time last month. = 他们上个月按时领到了工资。


现在完成时:
She has received her salary for this month. = 她已经收到了这个月的工资。

They have not yet increased salaries this year. = 他们今年还没有加薪。

By the end of the year, I had received all my salaries. = 到年底，我已经收到了所有的工资。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the salaries were higher, more people would apply. = 如果工资更高，更多的人会申请。

I wish my salary were higher. = 我希望我的工资更高。


被动语态:

The salaries are paid directly into our bank accounts. = 工资直接支付到我们的银行账户。


疑问句:
