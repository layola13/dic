patted:/ˈpætd/| v.|轻拍；抚摸|To gently stroke or tap (someone or something) with the hand or a flat object.|例句：She patted the dog's head. 她轻轻拍了拍狗的头。|例句：He patted his pocket to check for his keys. 他拍了拍口袋检查钥匙。|近义词：stroked, tapped,caressed|反义词：punched,slapped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pats the dog every day. = 他每天都拍拍狗。
She pats her child on the back. = 她轻拍孩子的背。


一般过去时:
I patted my friend on the shoulder. = 我拍了拍朋友的肩膀。
He patted the horse gently. = 他轻轻地拍了拍马。


现在完成时:
She has patted the cat many times. = 她已经摸了好多次猫了。
He has patted his pocket, but his keys are still missing. = 他拍了拍口袋，但钥匙仍然不见了。


过去完成时:
She had patted the dog before leaving the house. = 离开家之前，她已经摸过狗了。
He had patted his pockets several times before realizing his wallet was missing. = 他在意识到钱包不见了之前已经拍了好几次口袋了。


将来时:
I will pat the dog when I see him. = 我看到狗的时候会拍拍它。
She will pat her child's head to comfort him. = 她会拍拍孩子的头安慰他。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pat the dog. = 如果我是你，我会拍拍狗。
I wish I had patted the cat before it ran away. = 我真希望猫跑掉之前我摸摸它。


被动语态:
The dog was patted by the child. = 狗被孩子摸了一下。
The horse was patted gently by the rider. = 马被骑手轻轻地拍了拍。


疑问句:
Did you pat the dog? = 你摸狗了吗？
Have you ever patted a lion? = 你曾经摸过狮子吗？


否定句:
I did not pat the dog. = 我没有摸狗。
She didn't pat the child's head. = 她没有拍孩子的头。


PHRASE->
pat someone on the back = 赞扬某人
pat down = 搜身
pat someone on the shoulder = 轻拍某人的肩膀
give sb a pat on the head = 轻拍某人的头
pat oneself on the back = 自我表扬
