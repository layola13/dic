piperidine:/paɪˈpɛrɪdiːn/| n. |哌啶|A heterocyclic organic compound with the formula (CH2)5NH. It is a colorless liquid with a pungent, pepper-like odor. Piperidine is a secondary amine and a building block in organic synthesis. It is also found in some natural products. |Piperidine is a common building block in the synthesis of pharmaceuticals and other organic compounds. |近义词：hexahydropyridine|反义词：无
