justifiers:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪərz/| n. |辩护者；理由；证明者|Those who provide reasons or evidence to support a claim or action.  Justifications; reasons or evidence offered in support of a claim. |例句：The lawyer presented compelling justifiers for his client's innocence.  律师为其当事人的清白提供了令人信服的理由。

例句：The government's justifiers for the new policy were unconvincing. 政府为这项新政策提出的理由令人难以信服。

近义词：excusers, apologists, advocates, reasons, arguments
