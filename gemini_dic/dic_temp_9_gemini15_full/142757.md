otters:/ˈɒtəz/ | n. |水獭 |A small, semi-aquatic mammal with a long, streamlined body, short legs, webbed feet, and thick, waterproof fur. Otters are known for their playful behavior and their ability to hunt fish and other aquatic creatures. |例句：We saw a family of otters playing in the river. 我们看到一群水獭在河里玩耍。

例句：The otter skillfully caught a fish with its paws. 水獭熟练地用爪子抓到了一条鱼。

近义词：none (no perfect synonym, but terms like "mustelids" or "semi-aquatic mammals" could be considered related)

反义词：none
