homicidally:/hɒmɪˈsaɪdəli/ | adj. | 凶杀地；具有杀人之心的 |  In a manner suggesting or capable of homicide; murderously.  The word describes an action or state of mind characterized by an intent or potential for killing. | 例句：He stared at her homicidally, his eyes blazing with rage. 他凶狠地盯着她，眼睛里燃烧着怒火。


He looked at the weapon homicidally. 他凶狠地望着那件武器。


近义词：murderously, lethally, killingly, deathly | 反义词：lovingly, kindly, gently
