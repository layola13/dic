coold:/kuːld/| adj.|凉的，酷的，冷静的|Cool means pleasantly low in temperature.  It can also mean fashionable, impressive, or calm.|例句：The water in the pool was cool and refreshing. 泳池里的水凉爽宜人。 The new car is really cool. 这辆新车真酷。|近义词：chilly, refreshing, stylish, trendy, calm, composed|反义词：hot, warm, unfashionable, boring, agitated, flustered

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The air conditioner keeps the room cool. = 空调使房间保持凉爽。
This new style is cool. = 这种新风格很酷。

一般过去时:
The lemonade was cool and sweet. = 柠檬水又凉又甜。
He remained cool under pressure. = 他在压力下保持冷静。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
