dove:/dʌv/| n. |鸽子；和平象征|A dove is a small, gentle bird, often seen as a symbol of peace.|例句：The dove flew peacefully into the sky.  鸽子平静地飞向天空。

例句：A white dove was released at the wedding ceremony. 婚礼上放飞了一只白鸽。


近义词：pigeon (though pigeons are generally larger and less symbolic)|反义词：hawk (a bird of prey, representing war)|词性：名词
