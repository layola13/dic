originated:/ˈɒrɪdʒɪneɪtɪd/ | /ˈɔːrɪdʒɪneɪtɪd/ | v. |起源于；发源于；产生 |To begin to exist or develop; to come into being.  |The tradition originated in ancient Rome.  这个传统起源于古罗马。|  The fire originated in the kitchen. 火灾起源于厨房。| started, began, stemmed from | ended, ceased | 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem originates in poor management. = 问题源于管理不善。
This river originates in the mountains. = 这条河发源于山区。

一般过去时:
The idea originated with him. = 这个主意是他想出来的。
The custom originated centuries ago. = 这个习俗起源于几个世纪以前。


现在完成时:
Where has this rumor originated? = 这个谣言是从哪里传出来的？
Many of our traditions have originated from other cultures. = 我们的许多传统都起源于其他文化。


过去完成时:
It had originated in a small village. = 它起源于一个小村庄。
By the time I arrived, the argument had already originated. = 我到达的时候，争论已经开始了。


将来时:
The project will originate in the research department. = 该项目将由研究部门启动。
Where will the next big trend originate? = 下一个重大趋势将从哪里产生？


虚拟语气:
If the conflict had originated earlier, the situation would be worse. = 如果冲突更早开始，情况会更糟。
I wish the misunderstanding had not originated from a simple mistake. = 我希望误解不是源于一个简单的错误。


被动语态:
The rumour was originated by a rival company. = 这个谣言是由竞争对手公司散布的。
This style of painting is believed to have originated in the Renaissance.  这种绘画风格被认为起源于文艺复兴时期。


疑问句:
Where did this idea originate? = 这个想法起源于哪里？
When did the company originate? = 这家公司是什么时候成立的？


否定句:
The story didn't originate in this country. = 这个故事并非起源于这个国家。
The problem did not originate with the software. = 问题并非源于软件。
