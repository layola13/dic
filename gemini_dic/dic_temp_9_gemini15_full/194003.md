self-expressions:/ˌsɛlfɪkˈsprɛʃənz/| n.|自我表达，个人表达|The ways in which individuals communicate their thoughts, feelings, and ideas to others.  This can encompass a wide range of behaviors and actions, from verbal communication to artistic expression. |例句：Her paintings are powerful self-expressions of her inner turmoil.她的画作是她内心骚动的有力自我表达。

例句：He found his self-expressions through music and writing. 他通过音乐和写作找到了自己的表达方式。


近义词：self-revelation, expression, communication, articulation|反义词：repression, suppression, silence
