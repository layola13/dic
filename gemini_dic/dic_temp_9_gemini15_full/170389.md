provoke:/prəˈvoʊk/|/ˈprəʊvəʊk/| vt. |激怒；挑衅；引起；导致|To stimulate or incite to action or feeling; to call forth (a reaction, feeling, etc.).  To annoy, anger, or irritate someone. |例句：His aggressive behavior provoked a fight.  他的侵略性行为挑起了一场争斗。

例句：The comedian's jokes provoked laughter from the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话引来观众的阵阵笑声。


近义词：irritate, anger, incite, instigate, stimulate, trigger


反义词：soothe, calm, appease, pacify


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He provokes his sister constantly. = 他不断地激怒他姐姐。
The news provokes strong reactions. = 这则新闻引发了强烈的反应。


一般过去时:
The argument provoked a lot of anger. = 那场争论激起了很多怒火。
His words provoked a heated debate. = 他说的话引发了一场激烈的辩论。


现在完成时:
His actions have provoked widespread criticism. = 他的行为引起了广泛的批评。
The incident has provoked a public outcry. = 这起事件引发了公众的强烈抗议。


过去完成时:
The announcement had provoked much speculation. = 此公告已引起诸多猜测。
They had provoked the conflict by their actions. = 他们因其行为而引发了冲突。


将来时:
This decision will provoke a lot of controversy. = 这个决定将会引发很多争议。
I think his speech will provoke a strong response. = 我认为他的演讲会引发强烈的回应。


虚拟语气:
If he provoked her again, she would leave. = 如果他再次激怒她，她就会离开。
I wish he hadn't provoked that argument. = 我真希望他没有挑起那场争论。


被动语态:
The riot was provoked by a small group of extremists. = 暴动是由一小撮极端分子挑起的。
He felt provoked by their constant insults. = 他感到被他们不断的侮辱激怒了。


疑问句:
What provoked the fight? = 什么引发了这场争斗？
Did his comments provoke any reaction? = 他的评论引起了什么反应吗？


否定句:
His words did not provoke a response. = 他的话没有引起任何回应。
She tried to avoid provoking him. = 她试图避免激怒他。



PHRASE->
provoke a reaction = 引发反应
provoke a response = 引发回应
provoke a debate = 引发辩论
provoke an attack = 挑起攻击
provoke conflict = 挑起冲突
