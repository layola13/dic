fetters:/ˈfetərz/| n. |脚镣；束缚；障碍 |Chains or shackles used to restrain the feet.  Figuratively, it refers to anything that restricts freedom or progress. |例句：The prisoner was bound by fetters.  犯人被脚镣束缚着。

例句：The strict rules felt like fetters on her creativity. 严格的规定让她感觉创作受到了束缚。

近义词：shackles, bonds, restraints, constraints

反义词：freedom, liberty, emancipation
