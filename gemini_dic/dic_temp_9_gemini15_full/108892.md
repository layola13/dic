lecturette:/ˌlɛktʃʊˈrɛt/| n.|小型讲座，简短演讲|A short lecture or talk on a particular subject.|例句：The professor gave a brief lecturette on the history of the Roman Empire. 教授做了一个关于罗马帝国历史的简短讲座。|近义词：short talk, brief presentation, address|反义词：long lecture, dissertation
