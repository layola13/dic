pedipalpous:/ˌpɛdɪˈpælpəs/ | adj. | 具触肢的；有触肢的 | Having pedipalps.  Pedipalps are appendages near the mouth in arachnids, similar to legs but often modified for functions like sensing, capturing prey, or mating. | Example:  The scorpion's pedipalpous appendages are highly modified for capturing and subduing prey.  这只蝎子的触肢非常适合捕捉和制服猎物。 | 近义词：arachnid (in the context of possessing pedipalps) | 反义词：无  | 词性：形容词
