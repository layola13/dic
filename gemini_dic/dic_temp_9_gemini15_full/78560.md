ghoul:/ɡuːl/| n.|食尸鬼，恶鬼|A ghoul is a supernatural being, often depicted as a malevolent spirit or demon that feeds on corpses or human flesh.  They are often associated with graveyards and are typically portrayed as frightening and repulsive.|例句：The villagers whispered stories of ghouls haunting the old graveyard. 村民们低声讲述着食尸鬼出没于古老墓地的故事。
The horror movie featured a ghoul that snatched bodies from their graves. 这部恐怖电影中出现了一个从坟墓中抢夺尸体的食尸鬼。|近义词：monster, fiend, specter, demon|反义词：angel, saint
