civilizing: /ˈsɪvəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 使文明的；使开化的；教化的 |  Making or tending to make civilized; refining or improving the manners, morals, and culture of.  | 例句：The civilizing influence of education is undeniable. 教育的教化影响是不可否认的。 | 例句：The process of civilizing the wilderness was long and arduous.  使荒野文明化的过程漫长而艰巨。 | 近义词：culturing, refining, educating, enlightening | 反义词：barbarizing, brutalizing, dehumanizing | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is implementing policies aimed at civilizing the society. = 政府正在实施旨在使社会文明化的政策。
This book is civilizing young readers through its moral stories. = 这本书通过它的道德故事来教化年轻的读者。


一般过去时:
The Roman Empire played a significant role in civilizing Europe. = 罗马帝国在使欧洲文明化方面发挥了重要作用。
The missionaries attempted to civilize the indigenous population. = 传教士试图使当地居民文明化。


现在完成时:
Education has civilized many aspects of human behaviour. = 教育已经使人类行为的许多方面变得文明。
Globalization has both civilized and brutalized the world. = 全球化既使世界文明化也使世界野蛮化。


过去完成时:
They had civilized the area before the settlers arrived. = 在定居者到来之前，他们已经使该地区文明化了。
The ancient Greeks had already civilized a large part of the Mediterranean. = 古希腊人已经使地中海地区的大部分地区文明化了。


将来时:
Technological advancements will further civilize our daily lives. = 技术进步将进一步使我们的日常生活文明化。
We hope this program will civilize the attitudes of the youth. = 我们希望这个项目能使青少年的态度文明化。


虚拟语气:
If only we could civilize the hearts of men. = 如果我们能使人们的心灵文明就好了。
It would be wonderful if we could civilize the entire world. = 如果我们能使全世界文明化就好了。


被动语态:
The country was slowly civilized by the introduction of new ideas. = 通过引入新的思想，这个国家慢慢地文明化了。
The region is being civilized through infrastructure development. = 通过基础设施建设，该地区正在文明化。


疑问句:
How can we civilize the Internet? = 我们如何才能使互联网文明化？
What measures are being taken to civilize the political discourse? = 正在采取什么措施来使政治论述文明化？


否定句:
The war did not civilize, but brutalized the population. = 战争并没有使人民文明化，而是使他们野蛮化。
Their actions did not civilize the community but increased conflict. = 他们的行为并没有使社区文明化，反而加剧了冲突。
