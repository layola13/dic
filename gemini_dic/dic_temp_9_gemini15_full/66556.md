exhibition:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. |展览，展览会 |A public display of objects of art or other interest.  |例句：The museum is hosting an exhibition of Impressionist paintings.  博物馆正在举办一次印象派绘画展览。

例句：There will be an exhibition of modern art next month.下个月将举办一个现代艺术展览。


近义词：show, display, exposition, fair | 反义词：concealment, hiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum holds many exhibitions every year. = 博物馆每年举办许多展览。
This exhibition showcases local artists' work. = 这个展览展示了当地艺术家的作品。


一般过去时:
The exhibition attracted thousands of visitors. =  展览吸引了成千上万的游客。
We visited an interesting exhibition last weekend. = 我们上周末参观了一个有趣的展览。


现在完成时:
The gallery has held several successful exhibitions this year. = 画廊今年举办了几次成功的展览。
They have just finished setting up the exhibition. = 他们刚刚完成了展览的布展。


过去完成时:
The exhibition had already opened when we arrived. = 我们到达时展览已经开始了。
Before the exhibition opened, they had spent months preparing. = 在展览开幕之前，他们花了几个月的时间准备。


将来时:
The new exhibition will open next week. = 新展览将于下周开幕。
They will be exhibiting their latest collection at the fair. = 他们将在展览会上展出他们最新的收藏。


虚拟语气:
If the exhibition were free, more people would attend. = 如果展览免费，会有更多人参加。
I wish the exhibition were longer. = 我希望展览时间更长一些。


被动语态:
The paintings were carefully arranged for the exhibition. = 画作被仔细地布置在展览中。
The exhibition is being sponsored by a local business. = 展览由一家当地企业赞助。


疑问句:
What time does the exhibition close? = 展览几点结束？
Where is the exhibition being held? = 展览在哪里举办？


否定句:
The exhibition didn't receive many reviews. = 展览没有收到很多评论。
I haven't been to that exhibition yet. = 我还没有去过那个展览。
