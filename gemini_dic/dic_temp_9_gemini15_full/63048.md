enriches:/ɪnˈrɪtʃɪz/ | vt. |使富裕；使丰富；充实 | To improve or enhance something by adding valuable qualities or components.  |例句：Travel enriches the mind. 旅行丰富了思想。  |例句：The new program enriches the learning experience for students. 新的课程丰富了学生的学习体验。|近义词：enhance, improve, augment |反义词：impoverish, deplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The experience enriches her life. = 这段经历丰富了她的生活。
This book enriches my understanding of history. = 这本书丰富了我对历史的理解。

一般过去时:
The artist's work enriched the cultural scene. = 艺术家的作品丰富了文化景象。
The program enriched the lives of many participants. = 该项目丰富了许多参与者的生活。

现在完成时:
Reading has enriched my vocabulary. = 阅读丰富了我的词汇量。
Traveling abroad has enriched my perspective. = 出国旅行拓宽了我的视野。

过去完成时:
Years of study had enriched his knowledge. = 多年的学习丰富了他的知识。
The experience had enriched her understanding of different cultures. = 这段经历丰富了她对不同文化的理解。

将来时:
This course will enrich your skills. = 这门课程将提高你的技能。
The new software will enrich the user experience. = 新软件将改善用户体验。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would enrich my knowledge of art. = 如果我有更多时间，我会充实我的艺术知识。
I wish I could enrich the lives of others. = 我希望我能丰富他人的生活。

被动语态:
His life was enriched by his travels. = 他的生活因旅行而丰富。
Our community is enriched by the diversity of its people. = 我们社区因其人民的多样性而丰富多彩。

疑问句:
How can we enrich our community? = 我们如何才能丰富我们的社区？
What experiences enrich your life the most? = 什么样的经历最能丰富你的生活？

否定句:
The lack of funding did not enrich the program. = 缺乏资金并没有使该项目更丰富。
The poor quality of the materials did not enrich the students' learning. = 材料质量差并没有丰富学生的学习。
