quadrature:/ˈkwɑːdrətʃər/ | n. | 求积, 正交, 方形 | The process of finding a square with the same area as a given figure;  also refers to a phase difference of 90 degrees between two waves or signals. It can also refer to the act of squaring a number. |例句：The problem of squaring the circle is a classic example of quadrature.  求圆的面积是一个求积的经典例子。
