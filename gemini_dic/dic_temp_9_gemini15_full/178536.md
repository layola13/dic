reconveyance:/ˌriːkənˈveɪəns/| n. | 再转让；再转让契约 | The act of conveying or transferring property back to a previous owner or other party.  It often involves reversing a previous conveyance.  This is commonly seen in real estate transactions where property ownership is transferred back to the original grantor. | Example: The reconveyance of the property to the original owner was finalized after the loan was repaid.  再转让该房产给原业主的手续在贷款偿还后完成。 |近义词：retransfer, reversion |反义词：conveyance
