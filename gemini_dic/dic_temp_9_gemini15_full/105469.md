killdeer:/ˈkɪldɪr/| n.|美洲鸻|A medium-sized shorebird with a distinctive, repetitive, and high-pitched call that sounds like its name.  It is known for its cryptic nesting behavior and its aggressive defense of its nest and young. |例句：The killdeer's nest was well-camouflaged amongst the pebbles. 美洲鸻的巢穴很好地伪装在鹅卵石之间。

例句：We saw a killdeer frantically running back and forth, trying to distract us from its nest. 我们看到一只美洲鸻疯狂地跑来跑去，试图分散我们的注意力，让我们远离它的巢穴。


近义词：plover (a more general term for similar birds)|反义词：无
