overcontrol:/ˌoʊvər kənˈtroʊl/ | n. |过度控制；控制过度|The excessive or inappropriate control of something or someone.  It often leads to negative consequences.  Overcontrol can manifest in various ways, including micromanagement, authoritarianism, and excessive regulation.|例句：His overcontrol of his employees led to high turnover.他的过度控制员工导致了高离职率。

例句：The overcontrol of the economy stifled innovation. 对经济的过度控制扼杀了创新。


近义词：excessive control, micromanagement, authoritarianism, repression


反义词：lack of control, laissez-faire, freedom, autonomy


词性:名词
