oncologist:/ɒŋˈkɒlədʒɪst/| n. |肿瘤学家|A doctor who specializes in the treatment of cancer.|My mother is seeing an oncologist for her breast cancer treatment.  我的母亲正在找肿瘤科医生治疗她的乳腺癌。|hematologist, radiotherapist|surgeon (in some contexts)|
