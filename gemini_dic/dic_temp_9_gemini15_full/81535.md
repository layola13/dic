gravitative: /ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 万有引力的；有引力的；有吸引力的 | Relating to or caused by gravitation.  The word implies a force of attraction, similar to gravity, but can be used metaphorically. |例句：The gravitative pull of the moon affects the tides. 月球的引力影响着潮汐。

例句：His gravitative personality drew people to him. 他有吸引力的个性吸引了人们。


近义词：attractive, compelling, alluring | 反义词：repulsive, repellent


词源:  Derived from "gravitation," which comes from the Latin "gravitas" (weight, heaviness).  The suffix "-ative" forms adjectives.
