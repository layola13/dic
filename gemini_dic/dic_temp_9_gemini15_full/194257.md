self-mutilations:/ˌsɛlf ˈmjuːtɪleɪʃənz/ | n. | 自残行为 | Acts of self-harm, typically involving the deliberate infliction of injury upon oneself.  This can range from minor cuts and scratches to more serious acts of violence.  The motivations behind self-mutilation are complex and varied, often stemming from underlying mental health issues such as depression, anxiety, or trauma. | Example: The therapist helped the patient understand the root causes of their self-mutilations.  治疗师帮助病人理解他们自残行为的根本原因。


The study revealed a correlation between childhood abuse and adult self-mutilations.  这项研究揭示了童年虐待与成年自残行为之间的关联。|近义词：self-harm, self-injury,  body mutilation|反义词：self-care, self-preservation|名词
