deregulated:/ˌdiːˈreɡjʊleɪtɪd/| adj. |放松管制的；解除管制的| Freed from government regulation or control; lacking regulation.  The term implies a previous state of regulation. |例句：The deregulated airline industry has led to lower fares for consumers.  放松管制的航空业导致消费者票价降低。
The deregulated financial markets contributed to the 2008 crisis.  放松管制的金融市场导致了2008年的危机。|近义词：unregulated, uncontrolled, liberalized |反义词：regulated, controlled, restricted

The government deregulates the industry. = 政府放松对该行业的管制。
This policy deregulates the market. = 这一政策放松了市场管制。
