o'clock:/əˈklɒk/| n.|几点钟|表示几点整的时间；通常与数字连用表示具体时间。|It's three o'clock.  现在是三点钟。|  It's five o'clock sharp. 现在是五点整。|近义词：on the hour|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It's three o'clock now. = 现在是三点。
What time is it? It's ten o'clock. = 现在几点了？现在是十点。


一般过去时:
The meeting started at two o'clock. = 会议在两点开始。
We arrived at the station at five o'clock. = 我们五点到达车站。


现在完成时:
The film has already started; it's past two o'clock. = 电影已经开始了；现在已经过了两点。
They have been waiting since eleven o'clock. = 他们从十一点就开始等了。


过去完成时:
By two o'clock, we had finished our work. = 到两点钟，我们已经完成了工作。
She had left before ten o'clock. = 她十点之前就离开了。


将来时:
The train will arrive at three o'clock tomorrow. = 火车明天三点到达。
We will meet at eight o'clock tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上八点见面。


虚拟语气:
If it were five o'clock, we would leave. = 如果是五点钟，我们就走了。
I wish it were already twelve o'clock. = 我希望已经是十二点了。


被动语态:
The announcement was made at noon o'clock. = 公告在中午十二点宣布。
The meeting was scheduled for two o'clock. = 会议安排在两点举行。


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
Is it three o'clock yet? = 现在三点了吗？


否定句:
It's not yet three o'clock. = 现在还没到三点。
The train didn't arrive until after five o'clock. = 火车直到五点后才到达。


PHRASE->
half past two o'clock = 两点半
a quarter past three o'clock = 三点一刻
a quarter to four o'clock = 差一刻四点
five o'clock shadow = 五点钟胡须 (指一天没刮的胡须)
around/about two o'clock = 大约两点钟
