keeled:/kiːld/| adj.|龙骨状的；带龙骨的；|Having a keel or keel-like structure.  A keel is a structural element, often along the bottom of a boat or ship, providing stability. The word can also describe something with a similar shape.|例句：The boat had a deep keeled hull, giving it excellent stability in rough seas. 这艘船有一个很深的龙骨船体，使其在波涛汹涌的海面上具有极好的稳定性。

The bird's breastbone was strongly keeled, indicating powerful flight muscles.  这只鸟的胸骨非常突出，表明它有强大的飞行肌肉。|近义词：ridged, carinated|反义词：flat, smooth
