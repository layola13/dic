semibiologically: /ˌsemiˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adj. | 半生物学地；部分生物学地 |  Partially or to a certain extent biologically; relating to or characteristic of something that is partly biological in nature.  This word combines "semi-" (meaning half or partly) with "biologically" (meaning relating to biology). It describes something that has some biological aspects but also non-biological components or influences.


例句：
The experiment investigated the semibiologically engineered material's response to stress.  = 实验研究了半生物工程材料对压力的反应。

The system's semibiologically derived components were crucial for its functionality. = 系统的半生物衍生部件对其功能至关重要。


近义词：partially biological, biomimetic (if mimicking biological processes), partially organic


反义词：completely abiological, entirely artificial, purely synthetic


词性：形容词
