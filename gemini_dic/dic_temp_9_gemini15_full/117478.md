medlar:/ˈmɛdlər/| n.|欧楂，木梨|The medlar is a small tree that produces a fruit of the same name.  The fruit is usually eaten after it has become overripe and slightly rotten, developing a sweet and slightly acidic flavor. |The medlar tree is native to southwest Asia and Europe. |近义词：None (unique fruit)|反义词：None
