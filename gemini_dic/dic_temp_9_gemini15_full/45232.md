culpability:/ˌkʌlpəˈbɪləti/| n. | 可责性；有罪性；咎责 | The state of being culpable; blameworthiness.  It refers to the degree to which someone is responsible for a wrongdoing.  |例句：The court considered the culpability of the defendant in the accident. 法院审理了被告在事故中的责任。

例句：His culpability in the matter is undeniable. 他在这件事中的责任是不可否认的。


近义词：guilt, blameworthiness, responsibility, fault

反义词：innocence, blamelessness
