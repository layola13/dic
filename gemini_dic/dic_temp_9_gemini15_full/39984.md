consulate:/ˈkɒnsjʊleɪt/ /ˈkɑːnsəleɪt/| n. |领事馆；领事职位 |A consulate is a diplomatic mission established by a country in another country to represent its interests and provide services to its citizens.  It is typically headed by a consul. |例句：The American consulate in Shanghai provides assistance to American citizens in China.  上海的美国领事馆为在中国的美籍公民提供帮助。


例句：He applied for a visa at the British consulate. 他在英国领事馆申请签证。


近义词：embassy (but generally smaller and less powerful than an embassy)


反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider terms implying lack of diplomatic presence.


词性:名词
