interchange: /ˈɪntərtʃeɪndʒ/ | n. v. | 交换；互换；立交桥；交通枢纽 |

英文解析：As a noun, it refers to an act of exchanging or substituting one thing for another, or a place where roads or transportation lines cross or connect. As a verb, it means to exchange or substitute one thing for another.

例句：

名词例句1：The interchange of ideas between the two cultures was enriching.  两个文化之间的思想交流是丰富多彩的。

名词例句2：The highway interchange was heavily congested during rush hour.  高速公路立交桥在高峰时段交通拥堵严重。


动词例句1：We interchanged addresses before we parted.  我们分别前交换了地址。

动词例句2：The two teams interchanged players during the game.  两队在比赛中互换了球员。


近义词：exchange, swap, intersection, junction

反义词：retention, keeping (for noun); maintain, retain (for verb)

词性：noun, verb
