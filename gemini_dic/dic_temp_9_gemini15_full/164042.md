predischarged:/ˌpriːdɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒd/| adj.| 预先出院的，提前出院的 |  Describes a patient who is released from a hospital or other healthcare facility earlier than originally planned or expected.  This often implies a shorter than usual hospital stay. | Example:  The patient was predischarged due to a lack of available beds.  = 由于床位不足，病人提前出院了。


The patient, predischarged against medical advice, later experienced complications. =  这位病人违反医嘱提前出院，后来出现了并发症。


近义词：early discharged, prematurely released


反义词：delayed discharge, prolonged hospitalization


词性：形容词
