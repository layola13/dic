misreferences:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərənsɪz/ | n. | 误引；错误的参考；错误的引用 | Incorrect or misleading references; citations that are inaccurate, out of context, or otherwise misrepresent the original source.  Misreferences can occur due to errors in transcription, misinterpretation of the source material, or intentional manipulation. | Example: The article contained several misreferences, citing sources that did not support the claims made.  这篇文章包含几处误引，引用的资料并不支持文中提出的论点。 | 近义词：misquotations, miscitations, inaccuracies, errors | 反义词：accurate references, correct citations | 名词
