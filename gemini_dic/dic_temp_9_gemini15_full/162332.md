preacherly: /ˈpriːtʃərlɪ/ | adj. | 牧师似的；说教的；冗长乏味的 | Having the characteristics of a preacher; excessively moralistic or didactic; tediously long and moralizing.  | The preacherly tone of his speech bored the congregation.  他的演讲充满说教意味，让会众感到厌烦。 | sanctimonious, didactic, moralistic | irreverent, playful, informal
