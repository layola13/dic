noncontinuously:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪnjuəsli/ | adv. |非连续地，间歇地 |  Not continuously; intermittently.  It describes something that does not happen or exist without interruption. | Example: The machine operated noncontinuously, pausing every few minutes.  机器间歇性地运转，每隔几分钟停顿一次。 The data was collected noncontinuously over a period of several months. 数据在几个月的时间里断断续续地收集。 | 近义词：intermittently, sporadically, discontinuously | 反义词：continuously, constantly, uninterruptedly | 副词
