pothecary:/ˈpɒθɪkəri/ /ˈpɑːθɪkɛri/| n. |药剂师，药商|A person who prepared and sold medicines and drugs.  The term is now largely archaic, having been replaced by "pharmacist" or "chemist".|The apothecary carefully measured the ingredients for the potion.  药剂师仔细地测量药水的成分。| pharmacist, chemist |  — |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The apothecary prepares medicines daily. = 药剂师每天都配制药物。
He sells herbal remedies at his shop. = 他在他的店里出售草药疗法。


一般过去时:
The apothecary weighed the herbs precisely. = 药剂师精确地称量了草药。
She mixed the concoction according to the recipe. = 她按照配方混合了混合物。


现在完成时:
The apothecary has compounded many different medicines. = 药剂师已经配制了许多不同的药物。
He has served the community for over twenty years. = 他为社区服务了二十多年。


过去完成时:
The apothecary had already closed the shop when I arrived. = 我到达时药剂师已经关店了。
She had prepared the prescription before the patient came. = 病人来之前，她已经准备好处方了。


将来时:
The apothecary will open a new branch next month. = 药剂师下个月将开设新分店。
He will fill the prescription promptly. = 他将迅速配药。
