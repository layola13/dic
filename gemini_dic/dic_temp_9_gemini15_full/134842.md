nonmobility: /ˌnɒnmoʊˈbɪləti/ | n. |不动性，不活动性 |The inability to move or be moved.  Lack of mobility.  This term often refers to a state of being immobile, either temporarily or permanently, and can apply to people, objects, or systems. |例句：The patient's nonmobility was a major concern after the surgery.  手术后病人的不动性是主要的担忧。  The nonmobility of the heavy machinery required a crane for relocation. 重型机械的不动性需要起重机才能搬运。|近义词：immobility, stillness, fixity |反义词：mobility, movement, activity
