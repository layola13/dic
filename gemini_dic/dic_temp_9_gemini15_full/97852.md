inlaying:/ɪnˈleɪɪŋ/| n. v.|镶嵌；嵌入|The act or process of setting pieces of one material into the surface of another.  It can refer to the process itself or the resulting decoration. |例句：The table top featured beautiful inlaying of mother-of-pearl.  这张桌子桌面有美丽的珍珠母贝镶嵌。

The craftsman carefully executed the inlaying, ensuring a seamless finish.  工匠仔细地进行了镶嵌，确保了无缝的表面处理。|近义词：embedding, setting, insetting|反义词：extracting, removing
