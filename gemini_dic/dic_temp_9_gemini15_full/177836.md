recentralizing: /ˌriːˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |重新集中；重新中央集权化 | The act of bringing functions, powers, or resources back to a central location or authority.  It implies a reversal of a previous decentralization process. |例句：The company is recentralizing its operations after years of decentralization. 公司在经历了多年的分散化之后，正在重新集中运营。

The government is recentralizing control over the healthcare system. 政府正在重新集中对医疗系统的控制。


近义词：centralizing, concentrating, consolidating | 反义词：decentralizing, dispersing


词性：动词
