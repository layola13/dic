mulewort: /ˈmjuːlwɜːrt/ | n. | 驴蹄草 | A common name for plants of the genus *Borago*, particularly *Borago officinalis*, known for their star-shaped blue flowers and hairy leaves.  The name likely derives from the shape of the leaves, resembling a mule's foot. | Example:  The gardener carefully tended to her patch of mulewort, admiring its vibrant blue blooms.  = 园丁仔细照料她的一块驴蹄草，欣赏着它鲜艳的蓝色花朵。


The leaves of the mulewort were used traditionally for medicinal purposes. = 驴蹄草的叶子传统上用于药用目的。
