rubbling:/ˈrʌblɪŋ/| n.|碎石；瓦砾堆；碎石声|The sound or action of breaking something into small pieces, typically stones or rocks, resulting in a pile of rubble.  It can also refer to the rubble itself.|例句：The earthquake left behind a scene of rubbling and devastation. 地震过后留下了一片瓦砾和破坏的景象。


The constant rubbling of the construction site was a nuisance. 建筑工地持续不断的碎石声令人讨厌。|近义词：crumbling, shattering, debris|反义词：intact, whole, solid|名词
