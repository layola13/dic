chorals:/ˈkɔːrəlz/ | n. |合唱队；合唱曲；合唱音乐 |  A choral work is a musical composition written to be performed by a choir.  Chorals can range from simple hymns to complex orchestral works.  The term often refers to a piece of music written for a choir, particularly a church choir. | Example: The church choir sang beautiful chorals during the service.  教堂唱诗班在仪式上演唱了优美的合唱曲。 The concert featured several impressive chorals by Bach. 音乐会演奏了几首巴赫令人印象深刻的合唱作品。 | 近义词：choruses, anthems | 反义词：solo, solo performance
