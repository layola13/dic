microzyme:/ˈmaɪkroʊzaɪm/| n. |微酶 |A microzyme is a very small enzyme, often artificially produced.  They are used in various applications, such as in detergents or in targeted drug delivery.  | Example: Researchers are developing new microzymes for use in cleaning products.  研究人员正在开发用于清洁产品的新型微酶。 |近义词：nanoenzyme (纳米酶), mini-enzyme (微型酶) |反义词:macroenzyme (巨型酶)
