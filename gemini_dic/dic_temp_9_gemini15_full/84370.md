haling:/ˈheɪlɪŋ/| v.|呼喊；拖；拉；猛吸|The act of shouting or calling out loudly; to pull or drag something forcefully; to inhale deeply and forcefully.|例句：He was hailing a taxi. 他在叫出租车。

例句：The strong wind was hailing the boat across the lake. 大风把船拖过湖面。


近义词：shouting, yelling, pulling, dragging, inhaling|反义词：whispering, pushing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hails a cab every morning. = 他每天早上都叫出租车。
She hails from a small village. = 她来自一个小村庄。


一般过去时:
The fisherman hailed the passing ship. = 渔夫向经过的船只打招呼。
They hailed the arrival of spring. = 他们欢呼春天的到来。


现在完成时:
He has hailed a taxi and is waiting for it. = 他已经叫了一辆出租车，正在等待。
She has hailed from that town for many years. = 她在那座小镇住了很多年。


过去完成时:
He had hailed a taxi before the rain started. = 下雨之前他已经叫了一辆出租车。
They had hailed the new policy before it was implemented. = 他们在新政策实施之前就对其表示了欢迎。


将来时:
He will hail a taxi when he arrives at the airport. = 他到达机场后会叫一辆出租车。
They will hail the new leader. = 他们将欢迎新领导。


虚拟语气:
If he hailed a taxi, he would have arrived on time. = 如果他叫了出租车，他就会准时到达。
I wish I had hailed a cab earlier. = 我真希望我早点叫辆出租车。


被动语态:
A taxi was hailed by the passenger. = 一辆出租车被乘客叫住了。
The new law was hailed as a great success. = 新法被誉为巨大的成功。


疑问句:
Did you hail a taxi? = 你叫了出租车吗？
Where did she hail from? = 她来自哪里？


否定句:
He didn't hail a taxi; he walked. = 他没有叫出租车；他是走着去的。
She doesn't hail from this country. = 她不是这个国家的人。
