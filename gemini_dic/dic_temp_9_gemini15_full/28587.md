careful:/ˈkerfəl/| adj.|小心的，谨慎的，仔细的|Showing care and attention; avoiding risks.|例句：Be careful crossing the road.  过马路要小心。|例句：She carefully examined the painting. 她仔细检查了这幅画。|近义词：cautious, attentive, heedful, meticulous|反义词：careless, reckless, negligent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is careful with his money. = 他对钱很小心。
She is careful about what she eats. = 她对吃的东西很讲究。

一般过去时:
He was careful not to make a mistake. = 他小心谨慎，避免犯错。
She was careful in her choice of words. = 她说话很谨慎。

现在完成时:
They have been careful to avoid any controversy. = 他们一直小心谨慎，避免任何争议。
She has always been careful with her health. = 她一直很注意自己的健康。

过去完成时:
He had been careful to hide his feelings. = 他之前一直小心地隐藏着自己的感情。
They had been careful in their planning. = 他们的计划非常周密。

将来时:
I will be careful when I drive. = 我开车的时候会小心。
She will be careful not to offend anyone. = 她会小心，避免冒犯任何人。

虚拟语气:
If I were careful, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我小心点，就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had been more careful with my money. = 我真希望我之前对钱更小心点。

被动语态:
The work must be done carefully. = 这项工作必须仔细完成。
The instructions should be followed carefully. = 应该仔细遵循这些指示。

疑问句:
Are you careful when you handle those chemicals? = 你处理这些化学品的时候小心吗？
Was he careful enough to avoid the accident? = 他足够小心以避免事故吗？

否定句:
He wasn't careful enough. = 他不够小心。
She wasn't careful with her belongings. = 她没有保管好自己的东西。


PHRASE->
be careful of = 注意…
take careful consideration of = 仔细考虑…
look carefully = 仔细看
be careful with = 小心…
a careful study = 仔细的研究
