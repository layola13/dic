fibros:/ˈfaɪbrəʊz/| adj.|纤维的，纤维状的|Fibrous refers to something composed of or resembling fibers; characterized by or containing fibers.|例句：The fibrous tissue of the ligament was damaged.  韧带的纤维组织受损了。|例句：He felt a fibrous texture under his fingers. 他感觉手指下是一种纤维状的质地。|近义词：stringy, filamentous|反义词：smooth, non-fibrous


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and therefore doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It doesn't have tenses or moods.  Its usage is descriptive.
