gustable:/ˈɡʌstəbəl/| adj.| 可口的，美味的 |Relating to or capable of being tasted; palatable.  The word suggests a pleasant or agreeable taste.  It is less common than synonyms like "delicious" or "tasty."|例句：The chef prepared a gustable feast for the king.  厨师为国王准备了一顿美味的盛宴。 The gustable soup warmed me from the inside out. 这碗美味的汤从里到外温暖了我。|近义词：delicious, tasty, palatable, savory |反义词：disgusting, unpleasant, unpalatable, tasteless
