iconologies:/ˌaɪkənˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. |图像学研究；符号学研究 |The study of images and their meanings, often focusing on the cultural and social contexts in which they are created and interpreted. It examines how images function as signs and symbols within a specific society or historical period.  | Example:  Her dissertation focused on the iconoligies of Renaissance painting.  她的论文侧重于文艺复兴时期绘画的图像学研究。 |近义词：iconography, semiotics |反义词：无
