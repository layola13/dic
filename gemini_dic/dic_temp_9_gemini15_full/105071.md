kerbstones:/ˈkɜːrbstoʊnz/| n. |路边石；（英式英语）人行道缘石|Curbstones are stones that form the edge of a curb or sidewalk.  They are typically made of durable materials like concrete or granite and serve to define the boundary between the roadway and pedestrian areas.|例句：The kerbstones were worn smooth from years of traffic.  路边石被多年的车流磨得光滑。|The kerbstones were newly installed and gleamed in the sunshine. 新安装的路边石在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：curbstones, curbs, kerbs|反义词：无
