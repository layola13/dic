correlatable:/ˈkɔːrəleɪtəbl/| adj.| 可关联的，可相互关联的 | Able to be correlated; showing a correlation.  This adjective describes things that can be shown to have a relationship or connection, often statistical. | Example: The researchers found that smoking and lung cancer are highly correlatable. 研究人员发现吸烟和肺癌高度相关。 The data sets proved correlatable, indicating a strong relationship between variables. 数据集证明是可关联的，表明变量之间存在很强的关系。|近义词：related, connected, associated, linked|反义词：unrelated, unconnected, independent
