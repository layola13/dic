nonsedentariness:/ˌnɒnˈsɛdəntɛrɪnɪs/| n. |非定居性，流动性|The state or quality of not being sedentary; a way of life characterized by movement and change of location, often associated with nomadic or migratory lifestyles.  It contrasts with sedentariness, the state of being settled in one place.|Example: The study explored the impact of nonsedentariness on the social structures of early human populations.  这项研究探讨了非定居性对早期人类社会结构的影响。|近义词：mobility, nomadism, migration, itinerancy|反义词：sedentariness, settlement, stability
