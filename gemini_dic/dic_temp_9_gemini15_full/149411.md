packer:/ˈpækər/| n. v.|打包者；包装工；包装机|A person or machine that packs things.|例句：He works as a packer at the factory. 他在工厂做包装工。|近义词：wrapper, bundler|反义词：unpacker


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs his suitcase every morning. = 他每天早上都打包行李。
The machine packs the boxes automatically. = 这台机器自动打包箱子。

一般过去时:
She packed her bags before the trip. = 她旅行前打包了行李。
They packed the boxes carefully. = 他们仔细地打包箱子。

现在完成时:
I have packed all my belongings. = 我已经打包好了所有我的东西。
He has packed the parcels for shipping. = 他已经打包好包裹准备邮寄了。

过去完成时:
They had packed their lunch before they left. = 他们离开前已经打包好午餐了。
She had packed the gifts before the party. = 聚会前她已经打包好礼物了。

将来时:
We will pack our things tomorrow. = 我们明天会打包东西。
He will pack the order for delivery. = 他会打包订单准备送货。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack more efficiently. = 如果我有更多时间，我会打包得更高效。
I wish I had packed a heavier coat. = 我真希望我打包了一件更厚的外套。

被动语态:
The luggage was packed by the porter. = 行李是由搬运工打包的。
The boxes are being packed for shipment. = 箱子正在打包准备发货。

疑问句:
Have you packed your camera? = 你打包好相机了吗？
Is the package packed securely? = 包裹打包牢固吗？

否定句:
I didn't pack my swimsuit. = 我没有打包我的泳衣。
He hasn't packed his lunch yet. = 他还没打包午餐。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "packer" comes from the verb "pack," which has Old English roots.  The Old Norse word *pakka ("bundle, package") is the likely source, entering English in the 14th century.  The suffix "-er" is an agent suffix, indicating a person or thing that performs the action of the verb.
