reinvestigates:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪts/| v.|重新调查，复查|To investigate something again, often with more thoroughness or from a different perspective.  The police reinvestigated the case after new evidence emerged.|近义词：re-examine, review, re-evaluate|反义词：conclude, finish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee reinvestigates the budget annually. = 委员会每年重新审查预算。
The detective reinvestigates cold cases on a regular basis. = 侦探定期重新调查悬案。

一般过去时:
They reinvestigated the accident after new witnesses came forward. = 在新的证人出现后，他们重新调查了这起事故。
The court reinvestigated the evidence presented by the defense. = 法院重新审查了被告提供的证据。

现在完成时:
The authorities have reinvestigated the crime scene multiple times. = 当局已经多次重新调查了犯罪现场。
The journalist has reinvestigated the historical event, revealing new information. = 记者重新调查了这一历史事件，揭示了新的信息。

过去完成时:
The prosecutor had reinvestigated the case before presenting it to the grand jury. = 检察官在大陪审团面前提交案件之前已经重新调查了此案。
The team had reinvestigated the data before publishing their findings. = 团队在发表研究结果之前已经重新调查了数据。

将来时:
The government will reinvestigate the allegations next month. = 政府下个月将重新调查这些指控。
The company will reinvestigate the matter after the internal audit. = 公司将在内部审计之后重新调查此事。

虚拟语气:
If they had reinvestigated the evidence earlier, they might have found the truth sooner. = 如果他们早点重新调查证据，他们可能会更快地找到真相。
I wish they would reinvestigate the case; I believe there's been a miscarriage of justice. = 我希望他们能重新调查此案；我相信这是一个司法错误。

被动语态:
The case was reinvestigated by the special task force. = 此案由特遣部队重新调查。
The matter is being reinvestigated by a panel of experts. = 此事正在由专家组重新调查。

疑问句:
Will the police reinvestigate the unsolved murder? = 警方会重新调查这起未破的谋杀案吗？
Has the committee reinvestigated the controversial policy? = 委员会是否重新调查了这一有争议的政策？

否定句:
The police did not reinvestigate the case due to lack of new evidence. = 由于缺乏新的证据，警方没有重新调查此案。
They will not reinvestigate the matter unless further information is provided. = 除非提供进一步的信息，否则他们不会重新调查此事。
