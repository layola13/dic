palatable:/ˈpælətəbl/| adj.|美味的，可口的，令人愉快的|Pleasing to the taste; agreeable to the palate.  Something palatable is acceptable or enjoyable. It can refer to food or to something less tangible, such as an idea or a suggestion.|例句：The chef prepared a palatable meal that delighted all the guests.  厨师准备了一顿美味佳肴，让所有客人都很高兴。

The proposed solution, while not perfect, was at least palatable to all parties involved.  虽然提出的解决方案并不完美，但至少对所有相关方来说都是可以接受的。|近义词：agreeable, delicious, enjoyable, acceptable|反义词：disagreeable, unpleasant, distasteful, repulsive
