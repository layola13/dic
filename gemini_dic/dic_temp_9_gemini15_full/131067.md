nonbarbiturate:/ˌnɒnˈbɑːrbɪtʃʊreɪt/| adj. |非巴比妥类药物的|Not containing or relating to barbiturates.  These are a class of sedative-hypnotic drugs.  The term often refers to medications used for similar purposes (e.g., sleep aids, anxiety relief) but with different chemical structures. |例句：Many nonbarbiturate hypnotics are now available for treating insomnia. 许多非巴比妥类催眠药现在可用于治疗失眠。
The doctor prescribed a nonbarbiturate sleeping pill. 医生开了非巴比妥类的安眠药。

近义词：non-barbiturate, barbituric acid-free

反义词：barbiturate
