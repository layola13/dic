headstart:/ˈhɛdˌstɑːrt/ | n. | 领先，优势，先机 | An advantage at the beginning of something, especially a race or competition.  A person or thing with a head start is ahead of others from the beginning. |例句：He got a head start in the race by beginning before the others. 他在比赛中抢先开始，领先于其他人。


He had a head start in life, thanks to his wealthy parents.  由于父母富有，他在生活中获得了先机。| 近义词：advantage, edge, lead, jump | 反义词：disadvantage, handicap
