nonpolemic:/ˌnɒnpoʊˈlɛmɪk/| adj. |非争论的；非争议性的 |Not controversial or argumentative; avoiding controversy.  It suggests a calm, objective, and unbiased approach to a topic. |Example:  The author presented a nonpolemic account of the historical events.  作者对历史事件进行了非争议性的描述。|近义词：uncontroversial, impartial, objective, dispassionate |反义词：polemical, controversial, contentious, argumentative
