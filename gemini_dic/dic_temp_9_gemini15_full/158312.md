playhouse:/ˈpleɪhaʊs/| n. |玩具屋；儿童剧场；戏院|A small house, typically one built by children for play, or a small theatre for children's plays.  |例句：The children built a playhouse in the garden.孩子们在花园里建了一个玩具屋。

例句：The local theatre group put on a play at the playhouse. 当地剧团在儿童剧场演出了一部戏。|近义词：dollhouse, children's theatre |反义词：adult theatre, opera house
