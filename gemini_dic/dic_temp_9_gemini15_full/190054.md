sandpit:/ˈsændpɪt/| n. |沙坑；沙池| A pit or container filled with sand, typically used as a play area for children.  Sandpits are often found in playgrounds or backyards. |例句：The children were happily playing in the sandpit, building sandcastles. 孩子们快乐地在沙坑里玩耍，堆沙堡。

例句：My son spent hours in the sandpit this afternoon, digging and pouring sand. 我儿子今天下午在沙坑里玩了好几个小时，挖沙和倒沙。
