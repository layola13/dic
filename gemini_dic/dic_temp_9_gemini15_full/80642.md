gophered:/ˈɡoʊfərd/ | adj. |被土拨鼠挖洞的；充满土拨鼠洞的|  Describes something that has been dug up or damaged by gophers, or filled with gopher holes.  It implies a disruption or damage to the land or something within it caused by gopher activity.|例句：The garden was completely gophered;  the lawn was riddled with holes. 花园完全被土拨鼠挖得不成样子；草坪上布满了洞。 The farmer's field was gophered beyond repair. 农民的田地被土拨鼠挖得无法修复。|近义词：damaged, undermined, burrowed, riddled|反义词：intact, undamaged, smooth|形容词
