cannots: /ˈkænɑːts/ | n. | 不能，不可能的事 |  The word "cannots" is the plural form of "cannot," which itself is a contraction of "can not." It refers to things that are impossible or prohibited.  It's less common than using "cannot" in a general sense.  | Example: The list of cannots was extensive, outlining everything from smoking to using cell phones.  =  不能做的事情清单很长，列出了从吸烟到使用手机等各种事情。 | 近义词：impossibilities, prohibitions | 反义词：possibilities, permissions | 名词
