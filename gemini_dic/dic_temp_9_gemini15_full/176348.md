re-execution:/ˌriːˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃən/| n. |重新执行，再次执行 |The act of executing a program or process again.  This often happens after an error, to correct the mistake, or in a testing environment to verify results. |例句：The re-execution of the code revealed a previously undetected bug. = 代码的重新执行揭示了一个以前未检测到的错误。

例句：After the system crash, the re-execution of the backup process restored the data. = 系统崩溃后，备份过程的重新执行恢复了数据。


近义词：rerun, repetition, replay


反义词：first execution, initial run


词性：名词
