frontalized:/ˈfrʌntəlaɪzd/| adj.|额叶化的；额叶相关的|  Describes something that has been modified or interpreted in a way that emphasizes or is characteristic of the frontal lobes of the brain.  This often relates to cognitive functions like planning, decision-making, and social behavior.  The term is primarily used in neuroscience and related fields.|例句：The study examined the frontalized aspects of language processing. 这项研究考察了语言处理的额叶方面。 The patient exhibited frontalized behavior after the brain injury.  脑损伤后，病人表现出额叶化的行为。|近义词：forebrain-related, prefrontal-related|反义词： N/A
