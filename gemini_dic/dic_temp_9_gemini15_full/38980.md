confines:/kənˈfaɪnz/| n.|界限，范围，限制|The confines of the prison were inescapable. |近义词：limits, boundaries, restrictions|反义词：freedom, openness


英文解析：The word "confines" refers to the limits or boundaries of something, often in a physical or metaphorical sense. It implies a sense of restriction or enclosure.


例句：

The confines of the small apartment felt claustrophobic.  = 小公寓的局限空间让人感到压抑。

He pushed the boundaries of what is considered acceptable, exceeding the confines of his authority. = 他突破了被认为可以接受的界限，超越了他的权限。


词源：

From Old French *confins, from Latin confinis "bordering, neighboring," from com- "together" + finis "end, boundary."

过去完成时:
