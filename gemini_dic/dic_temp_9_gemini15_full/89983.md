horseworm: /ˈhɔːrswɜːrm/ | n. |马胃蝇幼虫|A parasitic larva of a botfly (family Gasterophilidae) that infests the stomach of horses.  They are also known as bots. |例句：The veterinarian found several horseworms in the horse's stomach during the examination.  兽医在检查中发现马胃里有好几条马胃蝇幼虫。

The presence of numerous horseworms can severely impact a horse's health.  大量的马胃蝇幼虫会严重影响马的健康。|近义词：bot, gastrophile larva |反义词：无
