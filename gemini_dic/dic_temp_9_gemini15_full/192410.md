scroll: /skrəʊl/ | /skroʊl/ | n., v. | 卷轴；滚动；缓慢移动 | A scroll is a roll of parchment or paper, typically containing writing or pictures;  to scroll means to move something smoothly along a surface or to move something on a screen by turning a wheel or using a similar device. | 近义词：roll, scroll down,  | 反义词：stop, remain stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrolls through his phone. = 他在浏览手机。
She scrolls the document. = 她正在看文件。


一般过去时:
I scrolled down the page to find the information. = 我向下滚动页面以查找信息。
They scrolled through the ancient scrolls. = 他们翻阅了古老的卷轴。


现在完成时:
I have scrolled through countless websites today. = 我今天浏览了无数的网站。
She has scrolled past that picture many times. = 她已经多次看到那张照片了。


过去完成时:
He had scrolled to the bottom of the page before he realized he'd missed something. = 他滚动到页面底部才意识到自己错过了一些东西。
They had scrolled through all the documents before making a decision. = 他们看完所有文件后才做出决定。


将来时:
I will scroll down to check the comments. = 我将向下滚动查看评论。
She will scroll through the pictures again later. = 她稍后会再次浏览这些照片。


虚拟语气:
If I had scrolled up, I would have seen the important information. = 如果我向上滚动，我就会看到重要的信息。
I wish I hadn't scrolled past that article. = 我真希望我没有错过那篇文章。


被动语态:
The document is scrolled upwards automatically. = 文件自动向上滚动。
The image was scrolled across the screen. = 图片在屏幕上滚动。


疑问句:
Can you scroll down a little bit? = 你能稍微向下滚动一下吗？
Did you scroll to the end of the page? = 你滚动到页面底部了吗？


否定句:
He didn't scroll down far enough. = 他没有向下滚动足够远。
She couldn't scroll because the website was slow. = 由于网站速度慢，她无法滚动。


PHRASE->
scroll down = 向下滚动
scroll up = 向上滚动
scroll through = 浏览
scroll bar = 滚动条
scroll wheel = 滚轮
