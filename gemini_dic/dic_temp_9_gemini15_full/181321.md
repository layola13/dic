reintervenes:/ˌriːɪntərˈviːnz/ | v. |再次干预；重新介入 | To intervene again; to become involved again in a situation to influence the course of events. |例句：The government reintervened in the economic crisis to prevent a collapse. 政府再次干预经济危机以防止崩溃。

例句：After a period of calm, the army reintervened in the political dispute. 在一段平静时期之后，军队再次介入政治争端。

近义词：intercede, mediate, interfere

反义词：withdraw, abstain, ignore

词性:动词
