self-suspicions:/ˌself səˈspɪʃənz/| n.|自我怀疑，对自己不信任|Self-suspicions are feelings of distrust or lack of confidence in oneself.  They can manifest as negative self-talk, insecurity, or a tendency to second-guess one's own abilities and judgments. |例句：Her self-suspicions prevented her from applying for the promotion, even though she was highly qualified.  她的自我怀疑阻止了她申请晋升，即使她非常合格。

例句：His self-suspicions stemmed from a difficult childhood and a lack of positive reinforcement. 他的自我怀疑源于艰难的童年和缺乏积极的强化。


近义词：self-doubt, lack of self-confidence, insecurity, mistrust of oneself|反义词：self-assurance, self-belief, confidence, self-trust|词性:noun
