dome-like:/ˈdoʊmˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 圆顶状的，像圆顶的 | Resembling a dome in shape or form.  It describes something that has the characteristic curve and shape of a dome. |例句：The building had a dome-like roof. 这座建筑有一个圆顶状的屋顶。

例句：The cloud formation was dome-like and ominous.  云层形成圆顶状，预兆不祥。


近义词：cupola-shaped, rounded, vaulted

反义词：flat, angular, pointed
