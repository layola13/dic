dittoes:/ˈdɪtoʊz/| n.|同上，相同之处|The same as what has been said before; also used as a way of agreeing with what someone else has said.|例句：I'll have the same as him, dittos.|近义词：same, likewise, also|反义词：different, unlike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The same applies to me; dittos. = 这也适用于我；同上。
He agrees with my opinion, dittos. = 他同意我的意见，同上。


一般过去时:
She said the same thing, dittoes. = 她说了同样的话，同上。
They had the same reaction, dittoes. = 他们有同样的反应，同上。


现在完成时:
We have reached the same conclusion, dittoes. = 我们得出了同样的结论，同上。
They have expressed the same sentiment, dittoes. = 他们表达了同样的情感，同上。


过去完成时:
They had already decided on the same course of action, dittoes. = 他们之前已经决定了相同的行动方案，同上。
She had already stated her position, dittoes. = 她之前已经表明了她的立场，同上。


将来时:
We will come to the same conclusion, dittoes. = 我们将得出相同的结论，同上。
They will likely choose the same option, dittoes. = 他们很可能会选择相同的选项，同上。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would say the same, dittoes. = 如果他在的话，他会这么说，同上。
If she had known, she would have done likewise, dittoes. = 如果她知道的话，她也会这么做，同上。


被动语态:
The same result was obtained, dittoes. = 得到了同样的结果，同上。
The same decision was made by both parties, dittoes. = 双方做出了相同的决定，同上。


疑问句:

Will their choice be the same as ours, dittoes? = 他们的选择会和我们的一样吗，同上？


否定句:
Her opinion isn't the same, not dittoes. = 她的意见不一样，并非如此。
His actions were not the same, not dittoes. = 他的行为不一样，并非如此。
