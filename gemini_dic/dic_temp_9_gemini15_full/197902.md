shirking:/ˈʃɜːrkɪŋ/| v.| 逃避；偷懒；推卸 |  The act of avoiding or neglecting a duty or responsibility.  |例句：He was accused of shirking his duties. 他被指控逃避责任。|例句：She's been shirking her work lately. 她最近一直在偷懒。|近义词：evading, avoiding, neglecting, dodging |反义词：facing, confronting, fulfilling, undertaking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shirks his responsibilities. = 他逃避责任。
They shirk their work. = 他们逃避工作。


一般过去时:
He shirked his duties last week. = 他上周逃避了他的职责。
She shirked her responsibilities to her family. = 她逃避了她对家庭的责任。


现在完成时:
He has shirked his responsibilities for years. = 他多年来一直逃避责任。
She has shirked her homework all semester. = 她整个学期都没做作业。


过去完成时:
He had shirked his duties before the incident. = 事件发生前，他一直逃避责任。
They had shirked their responsibilities, leading to the company's failure. = 他们逃避责任，导致公司倒闭。


将来时:
He will shirk his responsibilities again. = 他还会再次逃避责任。
She will likely shirk her duties if she's not properly supervised. = 如果没有适当的监督，她很可能会逃避职责。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so lazy, he wouldn't shirk his work. = 如果他不那么懒惰，他就不会逃避工作。
I wish he wouldn't shirk his responsibilities. = 我希望他不逃避责任。


被动语态:
His duties were shirked by him. = 他的职责被他逃避了。(This passive voice is less common)
The task was shirked by the team. = 这个任务被团队成员逃避了。(This passive voice is less common)


疑问句:
Is he shirking his responsibilities? = 他是否在逃避责任？
Are they shirking their duties? = 他们是否在逃避职责？


否定句:
He does not shirk his responsibilities. = 他没有逃避责任。
She did not shirk her duties. = 她没有逃避职责。


PHRASE->
shirk one's duties = 逃避责任
shirk one's responsibilities = 逃避责任
shirk work = 逃避工作
shirk one's obligations = 逃避义务
shirk no effort = 不遗余力 (This is an exception, showing the opposite meaning)

词源:
The word "shirk" ultimately derives from the Old Norse word "skrýrka," meaning "to hide," "to conceal," or "to shelter."  Over time, its meaning evolved to encompass the sense of avoiding one's duties or responsibilities.
