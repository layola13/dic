lactometer:/lækˈtɒmɪtər/| n. |乳稠计，乳汁比重计 |A lactometer is an instrument used to measure the density of milk.  It helps determine the purity and quality of milk by measuring the amount of water and fat present.  |例句：The farmer used a lactometer to check the quality of his cow's milk.  这位农民用乳稠计来检查他奶牛的牛奶质量。

The dairy technician calibrated the lactometer before testing the milk samples.  乳制品技师在测试牛奶样本之前校准了乳稠计。|近义词：hydrometer (比重计)|反义词：None|名词
