parturitive:/pɑːrˈtʃuːrətɪv/| adj.|分娩的；生产的；临产的|Relating to childbirth or parturition; productive or tending to produce.  The word often describes the period or process of giving birth, or the ability to give birth.|例句：The parturitive process can be painful but ultimately rewarding. 分娩过程可能很痛苦，但最终是值得的。

例句：She experienced intense parturitive pains. 她经历了剧烈的分娩阵痛。


近义词：childbearing, obstetric, natal,  giving birth

反义词：postpartum, postnatal (referring to the period *after* childbirth)
