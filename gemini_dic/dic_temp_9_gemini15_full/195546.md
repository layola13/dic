semiphilosophic: /ˌsɛmiˌfɪləˈsɒfɪk/ | adj. | 半哲学的，略带哲学意味的 | Partially philosophical; having some characteristics of philosophy but not fully or systematically so.  It suggests an approach to a topic that incorporates philosophical ideas but without the rigor or depth of a fully philosophical treatment. |例句：His approach to art criticism was semiphilosophic, blending aesthetic judgments with reflections on the human condition.  他的艺术评论方法略带哲学意味，将审美判断与对人类状况的思考融合在一起。


His semiphilosophic musings on the nature of reality often drifted into fanciful speculation. 他对现实本质的半哲学思考经常飘向奇特的推测。 |近义词：metaphysical (in part), speculative, reflective |反义词： purely practical, purely scientific
