himselve:/hɪmzɛlf/| pron.|他自己，他本人|himself is a reflexive pronoun, meaning it refers back to the subject of the sentence. It is used to indicate that the subject performs the action on itself.  |He hurt himself playing football. 他踢足球时伤到了自己。
He cooked himself a delicious meal. 他给自己做了一顿美味的饭菜。|himself, oneself|itself, themselves|pronoun

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He praises himself every day. = 他每天都赞扬自己。
She prepares herself for the exam. = 她为考试做准备。


一般过去时:
He blamed himself for the accident. = 他为这次事故责备自己。
She dressed herself quickly. = 她迅速地穿好了衣服。


现在完成时:
He has hurt himself badly. = 他伤得很重。
She has taught herself to play the piano. = 她自学了钢琴。


过去完成时:
He had already prepared himself for the worst. = 他已经做好了最坏的打算。
She had dressed herself before he arrived. = 他到达之前，她已经穿好衣服了。


将来时:
He will reward himself after finishing the project. = 他完成项目后会奖励自己。
She will present herself confidently at the interview. = 她将在面试中自信地展现自己。


虚拟语气:
If he were to blame himself, he would feel better. = 如果他责备自己，他会感觉好些。
I wish she had prepared herself more thoroughly. = 我希望她能更充分地做好准备。


被动语态:
(Himself is rarely used in the passive voice, as it's reflexive.)  The problem solved itself.  问题自己解决了。
The door opened itself. 门自己开了。


疑问句:
Did he hurt himself? = 他伤到自己了吗？
Does she blame herself for this? = 她为此责备自己吗？


否定句:
He didn't hurt himself. = 他没有伤到自己。
She didn't prepare herself adequately. = 她没有充分准备。

PHRASE->
by himself = 独自地
for himself = 为他自己
to himself = 自言自语
in himself = 本身
of himself = 自动地
