lavage:/ləˈvɑːʒ/| n. v.|灌洗；冲洗；洗净|The process of washing out a cavity or wound with a liquid.  It is often used in medical contexts to clean wounds, body cavities, or organs.|例句：The doctor performed a gastric lavage to remove the poison. 医生进行了胃灌洗以清除毒药。  The wound required a thorough lavage to remove debris. 伤口需要彻底冲洗以清除碎屑。|近义词：irrigation, flushing, washing|反义词：contamination, infection|名词，动词
