reposeful:/rɪˈpoʊsfəl/| adj. |宁静的；安详的；平静的 |Characterized by or suggesting repose; tranquil; peaceful.  It implies a state of calm and quiet, often associated with a sense of serenity and restfulness. |例句：The reposeful atmosphere of the library was conducive to studying. 图书馆宁静的气氛有利于学习。

例句：Her reposeful expression hinted at a deep inner peace. 她安详的表情暗示着内心的平静。


近义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene, restful

反义词：restless, agitated, turbulent, frantic, chaotic

词性：形容词
