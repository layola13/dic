quarter's:/ˈkwɔːrtərz/ | n. | 四分之一；季度；一刻钟；地区 |  The possessive form of "quarter," indicating ownership or belonging. It refers to a period of three months, a specific location, or a fourth part of something. |例句：This year's third quarter's profits were higher than expected. 今年第三季度的利润高于预期。


The company's headquarters are in the city's financial quarter. 这家公司的总部位于该市金融区。


近义词：fourth, trimester, section | 反义词：whole, entirety
