rampacious:/ræmˈpeɪʃəs/| adj.|贪婪的；凶猛的；强暴的 | excessively greedy or grasping; aggressively greedy.  Characterized by unrestrained violence or aggression. |例句：His rampacious appetite led him to eat everything on the table. 他的贪婪食欲导致他吃光了桌子上的所有东西。


例句：The rampacious general led his troops on a brutal campaign.  这位贪婪的将军带领他的军队进行了一场残酷的战役。


近义词：voracious, rapacious, predatory, ravenous


反义词：generous, benevolent, merciful, gentle


词源：源自拉丁语 "rapere" (to seize, snatch, carry off by force),  体现了强取豪夺的意味，与 "rapacious" (贪婪的，掠夺的)  词根相近，但更强调蛮横和暴力。
