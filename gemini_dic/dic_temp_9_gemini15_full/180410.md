regal:/ˈriːɡəl/| adj.|帝王的；宏伟的；庄严的 | majestic, royal, grand, imposing;  fitting for a monarch or other person of high status; characterized by magnificence, splendor, or grandeur. |例句：The regal palace overlooked the city.  这宏伟的宫殿俯瞰着城市。

例句：She carried herself with a regal air. 她举止庄重。


近义词：royal, majestic, imperial, kingly, queenly, princely, grand, imposing, stately

反义词：humble, modest, mean, common, ordinary


词源：源自古法语 *regal,  最终源自拉丁语 *regalis,  意思是"属于国王的"，源自rex, regina  (国王，女王)。
