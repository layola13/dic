daughterly:/ˈdɔːtəli/| adj.|女儿般的，像女儿一样的|Characterized by the qualities or behavior expected of a daughter; showing filial affection and obedience.  It suggests traits like gentleness, kindness, and respect.|例句：Her daughterly devotion to her aging parents was admirable.  她对年迈父母的孝顺令人钦佩。


例句：She showed a daughterly concern for her father's health. 她表现出对父亲健康的女儿般的关切。


近义词：filial, dutiful, obedient


反义词：disobedient, rebellious, undutiful


词性:形容词
