fourdrinier:/ˌfɔːrdrɪˈnjeɪr/| n. |福尔德里尼尔造纸机；（造纸机的）网｜A Fourdrinier machine is a type of paper-making machine that uses a moving wire mesh to form a continuous sheet of paper.  It's named after the brothers Henry and Sealy Fourdrinier, who were instrumental in its development and popularization.｜The invention of the Fourdrinier machine revolutionized paper production.  福尔德里尼尔造纸机的发明彻底改变了造纸业。｜近义词：paper machine, papermaking machine｜反义词：无
