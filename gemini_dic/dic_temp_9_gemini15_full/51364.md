deteriorate:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/| v.|恶化，变坏|To become worse|The situation has deteriorated rapidly.局势迅速恶化。The weather is deteriorating. 天气正在恶化。|worsen, decline, degenerate|improve, enhance, ameliorate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is deteriorating steadily. = 天气正在稳步恶化。
His health deteriorates gradually. = 他的健康状况逐渐恶化。

一般过去时:
The building deteriorated over the years. = 这栋建筑物多年来逐渐破败。
Her condition deteriorated rapidly after the surgery. = 她手术后情况迅速恶化。

现在完成时:
The situation has deteriorated significantly since the accident. = 事故发生以来，情况已经恶化了很多。
His mental state has deteriorated over the past few months. = 他过去几个月的心理状态一直在恶化。

过去完成时:
The bridge had deteriorated before the engineers discovered the problem. = 工程师发现问题之前，桥梁就已经破损了。
Their relationship had deteriorated long before they separated. = 他们的关系早在他们分开之前就已经恶化了。

将来时:
The economy is expected to deteriorate further next year. = 预计明年经济将进一步恶化。
If we don't act now, the environment will continue to deteriorate. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，环境将继续恶化。


虚拟语气:
If the situation continues to deteriorate, we will have to take drastic measures. = 如果情况继续恶化，我们将不得不采取严厉措施。
I wish the quality of the product hadn't deteriorated so much. = 我真希望产品的质量没有恶化这么多。


被动语态:
The building was allowed to deteriorate for years. = 这栋建筑物被允许年久失修。
The roads have been allowed to deteriorate because of lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，道路一直处于失修状态。

疑问句:
Has the patient's condition deteriorated? = 病人的病情恶化了吗？
Is the quality of the air deteriorating in this city? = 这个城市的空气质量是否正在恶化？

否定句:
The quality of the food hasn't deteriorated. = 食物质量没有恶化。
His health didn't deteriorate significantly. = 他的健康状况没有显著恶化。


PHRASE->
rapidly deteriorate  迅速恶化

significantly deteriorate  显著恶化
gradually deteriorate 逐渐恶化
noticeably deteriorate 明显恶化
