pianist:/ˈpiænɪst/| n. |钢琴家|A person who plays the piano, especially professionally.  A pianist is skilled in playing the piano and often performs for an audience or teaches piano lessons. |He is a renowned pianist known for his virtuosic performances. 他是一位以精湛演奏而闻名的钢琴家。|musician, player|non-musician.
