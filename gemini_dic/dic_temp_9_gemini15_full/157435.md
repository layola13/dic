pirati:/pɪˈrɑːti/| n. |海盗；盗版者|  Pirates are criminals who attack ships at sea to steal their cargo. 海盗是袭击海上船只以抢夺货物罪犯。|近义词：buccaneer, sea robber, privateer |反义词：sailor, merchant, law-abider|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pirates attack ships. = 海盗袭击船只。
Pirates steal valuable goods. = 海盗偷窃贵重货物。

一般过去时:
The pirates attacked a merchant ship yesterday. = 海盗昨天袭击了一艘商船。
They plundered the ship and took all the treasure. = 他们洗劫了船只并带走了所有财宝。

现在完成时:
The pirates have plundered many ships. = 海盗已经洗劫了许多船只。
They have hidden their treasure on a deserted island. = 他们把宝藏藏在一个荒岛上。

过去完成时:
The pirates had already left when the navy arrived. = 海军到达时，海盗已经离开了。
They had buried the treasure before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经埋藏了宝藏。

将来时:
The pirates will attack again soon. = 海盗很快会再次袭击。
They will be hunted by the navy. = 他们将被海军追捕。

虚拟语气:
If the pirates had not been so greedy, they might have escaped. = 如果海盗不那么贪婪，他们可能就逃脱了。
I wish the pirates hadn't stolen our cargo. = 我真希望海盗没有偷走我们的货物。

被动语态:
Many ships were attacked by pirates. = 许多船只遭到海盗袭击。
The treasure was buried on a secret island. = 宝藏被埋在一个秘密岛屿上。

疑问句:
Were the pirates caught? = 海盗被抓住了吗？
How many ships did the pirates attack? = 海盗袭击了多少艘船？

否定句:
The pirates did not escape. = 海盗没有逃脱。
They did not find the treasure. = 他们没有找到宝藏。
