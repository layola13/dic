dinkier:/ˈdɪŋkiər/| adj.|更小巧的，更精致的|Smaller and more delicate or dainty.  The word implies a slightly informal or even playful diminutiveness.|例句：Her dinkier earrings were more elegant than her larger, bolder ones.  她的更小巧的耳环比她那些更大更大胆的耳环更优雅。
例句：The dinkier version of the car was surprisingly spacious. 这款更小巧的汽车内部空间出奇地宽敞。|近义词：smaller, tinier, daintier, more delicate|反义词：larger, bigger, bulkier, coarser
