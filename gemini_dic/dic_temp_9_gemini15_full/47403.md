deadish:/ˈdedɪʃ/| adj.|有点死的；几乎死的；非常疲惫的|Slightly dead; nearly dead; extremely tired.  The plant looked deadish, but I watered it and it revived.  这植物看起来快要死了，但我浇了水，它又活过来了。|近义词：dying, lifeless, exhausted,  |反义词：alive, lively, energetic

USAGE->

He feels deadish after a long day's work. = 他一天工作下来感觉很疲惫。
The flower looks deadish, but it might still be alive. = 这朵花看起来快死了，但可能还活着。
