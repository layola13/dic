glissandi:/ɡlɪˈsænˌdi/| n.|滑音(复数)|A gliding from one pitch to another without distinct steps; a smooth transition between musical notes.|例句：The pianist played a series of beautiful glissandi.  钢琴家演奏了一系列优美的滑音。

The violinist's glissandi added a dramatic effect to the piece. 小提琴手的滑音为乐曲增添了戏剧性的效果。


近义词：slide, portamento|反义词：staccato
