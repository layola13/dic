over-powerful: /ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərfl/ | adj. | 过于强大的，权力过大的 | Excessively powerful; wielding excessive power or influence.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the power is misused or harmful. |例句：The over-powerful CEO ignored the concerns of his employees.  过于强大的首席执行官无视了员工的担忧。

The over-powerful government suppressed dissent.  权力过大的政府压制了异议。|近义词：tyrannical, domineering, authoritarian, overwhelming, excessive |反义词：powerless, weak, ineffective, insignificant
