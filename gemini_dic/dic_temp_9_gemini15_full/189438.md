sailings:/ˈseɪlɪŋz/ | n. | 航行；启航；航次 |  The plural form of "sailing," referring to instances of a ship or boat setting out on a journey or the act of traveling by sea. It can also refer to a scheduled departure of a vessel. |例句：The ship's sailings are scheduled for every Tuesday. 这艘船的航次安排在每周二。

The upcoming sailings to the island are fully booked.  去岛上的下次航次已全部预订。


近义词：voyages, departures, trips (by sea) | 反义词：arrivals, landings


词性：名词

词源:
From the noun "sailing," meaning the act of traveling by sea under sail. The addition of "-ings" creates a plural noun, indicating multiple instances of sailing or scheduled departures.  The word ultimately derives from the Old English "seglian," meaning "to sail."
