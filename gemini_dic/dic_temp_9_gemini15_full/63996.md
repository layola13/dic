epistolatory: /ɪˌpɪˈstɒlətəri/ | adj. | 书信的；信件体的 | Relating to or characteristic of letter writing.  | The epistolatory style of the novel gave readers an intimate perspective on the characters' lives. 这部小说的书信体风格让读者对人物的生活有了更深入的了解。 | 近义词：letter-writing, correspondence-based | 反义词：face-to-face, verbal


ETYMOLOGY->
The word "epistolatory" comes from the Late Latin word *epistolatorius*, which is derived from the Latin word *epistola* meaning "letter".  The suffix "-atory" indicates relating to or characteristic of.
