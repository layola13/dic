pantographer:/pænˈtɒɡrɑːfər/| n. |万花筒，复写器 |A pantograph is a mechanical device used to copy drawings or designs, enlarging or reducing them in the process. It consists of a parallelogram-shaped linkage with a stylus that traces the original drawing and a pen that simultaneously reproduces it on a different scale.  |The architect used a pantograph to enlarge the blueprint. 建筑师用万花筒放大蓝图。|  |  | noun
