misbehaved:/ˌmɪs.bɪˈheɪvd/| adj. | 行为不端的；举止不好的 | behaving badly or in a way that is not acceptable | The children misbehaved during the school trip.  孩子们在学校郊游期间行为不端。|近义词：naughty, unruly, disobedient | 反义词：well-behaved, obedient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children misbehave in class. = 孩子们在课堂上行为不端。
He often misbehaves when he's tired. = 他累了的时候经常行为不端。


一般过去时:
She misbehaved at the party last night. = 她昨晚在派对上行为不端。
They misbehaved and got sent to the principal's office. = 他们行为不端，被送到了校长办公室。


现在完成时:
He has misbehaved repeatedly this semester. = 这个学期他已经多次行为不端了。
The dog has misbehaved and chewed up my shoes. = 狗行为不端，把我的鞋子咬坏了。


过去完成时:
She had misbehaved so many times that her parents were very upset. = 她已经多次行为不端，以至于她的父母非常生气。
They had misbehaved earlier, so they were grounded. = 他们之前行为不端，所以被禁足了。


将来时:
I think he will misbehave again if he doesn't get enough sleep. = 我认为如果他不睡够觉，他会再次行为不端。
If you don't discipline him, he'll misbehave at the wedding. = 如果你不约束他，他会在婚礼上行为不端。


虚拟语气:
If he had not misbehaved, he wouldn't have been punished. = 如果他没有行为不端，他就不会受到惩罚。
I wish the children hadn't misbehaved at the restaurant. = 我希望孩子们没有在餐馆行为不端。


被动语态:
His misbehavior was reported to the teacher. = 他的不当行为被报告给了老师。
The misbehavior of the students was dealt with swiftly. = 学生的不当行为得到了迅速处理。


疑问句:
Did the children misbehave today? = 孩子们今天行为不端了吗？
Has he ever misbehaved so badly before? = 他以前有没有行为如此恶劣？


否定句:
The children did not misbehave during the play. = 孩子们在演出期间没有行为不端。
He hasn't misbehaved all week. = 他整个星期都没有行为不端。
