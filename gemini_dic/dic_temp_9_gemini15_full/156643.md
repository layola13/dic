picture:/ˈpɪktʃər/| n. |图片，照片，景象，描写|A picture is worth a thousand words. 一张图片胜过千言万语。  The painting was a beautiful picture of a landscape. 这幅画是美丽的风景画。 |image, illustration, representation |abstract, reality


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The picture hangs on the wall. = 图片挂在墙上。
I see a beautiful picture. = 我看到一张美丽的图片。


一般过去时:
He took a picture of the mountain. = 他拍了一张山的照片。
She painted a picture of her family. = 她画了一幅她家人的画像。


现在完成时:
I have taken many pictures of my cat. = 我已经拍了很多我猫的照片。
She has painted several pictures this year. = 她今年已经画了几幅画。


过去完成时:
I had already seen the picture before you showed it to me. = 在你给我看之前我已经看过这张照片了。
They had finished the picture before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了这幅画。


将来时:
I will take a picture of you tomorrow. = 我明天会给你拍照。
She will paint a picture of the sunset. = 她将画一幅日落图。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better camera, I would take a better picture. = 如果我有一台更好的相机，我会拍出更好的照片。
I wish I could paint a picture like that. = 我希望我能画出那样一幅画。


被动语态:
The picture was taken by a professional photographer. = 这张照片是由专业摄影师拍摄的。
The picture is being displayed in the gallery. = 这张照片正在画廊展出。


疑问句:
Is this picture yours? = 这张照片是你的吗？
What is in the picture? = 图片里有什么？


否定句:
I don't like that picture. = 我不喜欢那张照片。
He didn't take a picture. = 他没有拍照。


PHRASE->

picture yourself = 想象一下你
in the picture = 在照片中；参与其中
a picture of health = 健康的象征
a clear picture = 清晰的画面
a bigger picture = 更大的局面
