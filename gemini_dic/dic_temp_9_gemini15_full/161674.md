pourparler:/ˈpʊrpɑːrleɪ/ | n. |初步会谈；非正式谈判 | preliminary discussions; informal negotiations;  |例句：The pourparlers between the two countries have broken down.  两国之间的初步会谈破裂了。

例句：Before the formal negotiations, they engaged in some pourparlers to establish common ground. 在正式谈判之前，他们进行了一些初步会谈以建立共同立场。


近义词：negotiations, discussions, talks, consultations | 反义词：confrontation, conflict, war


词源: 法语  *pour parler*  "to speak," from *pour* "for" + *parler* "to speak."
