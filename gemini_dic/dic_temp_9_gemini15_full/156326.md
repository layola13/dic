physician:/fɪˈzɪʃən/| n. |医生，内科医生|A physician is a medical doctor who is licensed to practice medicine.  Physicians diagnose and treat illnesses and injuries. They may specialize in a particular area of medicine, such as cardiology or oncology. |例句：My physician recommended regular exercise and a healthy diet. 我的医生建议我经常锻炼和健康饮食。  The physician examined the patient carefully before making a diagnosis. 医生仔细检查了病人后才做出诊断。|近义词：doctor, medical doctor, M.D.|反义词：patient
