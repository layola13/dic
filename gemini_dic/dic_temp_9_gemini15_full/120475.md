minstral: /ˈmɪnstrəl/ | n. |吟游诗人，乐师| A medieval musician, singer, and storyteller, often traveling from place to place and entertaining people with songs, poems, and musical performances.  They often played various instruments and were skilled in juggling, acrobatics, and other forms of entertainment. |The minstrels entertained the king and his court with songs and dances.  吟游诗人们用歌声和舞蹈招待国王及其朝臣。|近义词：musician, troubadour, ballad singer|反义词：N/A
