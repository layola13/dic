contributive: /kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/ | adj. | 有贡献的；促成的 | Contributing to a result or effect; helpful.  | 例句：His contributive efforts were essential to the project's success.  他的贡献对项目的成功至关重要。

His contributive role in the team is undeniable. 他在团队中的贡献是不可否认的。


近义词：helpful, aiding, assisting, supportive, beneficial | 反义词：harmful, detrimental, obstructive, unhelpful, detrimental


词源:  Derived from the verb "contribute," ultimately from the Latin "contribuere," meaning "to contribute," which is formed from "con-" (together) and "tribuere" (to give, assign).
