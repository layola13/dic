indiscretion:/ˌɪndɪsˈkriːʃən/| n. |轻率；不谨慎；失言；秘密泄露|Lack of discretion; imprudence in speech or action.  An indiscretion is a careless or unwise action, often one that reveals something private or embarrassing.  It implies a lack of judgment or caution. |例句：His indiscretion in revealing company secrets cost him his job. 他泄露公司秘密的轻率行为让他丢了工作。


例句：Her indiscretion with a married man led to a scandal. 她与已婚男子的不当行为导致了一场丑闻。


近义词：imprudence, recklessness, folly, impropriety, indiscreetness


反义词：prudence, discretion, caution, circumspection


词性：名词
