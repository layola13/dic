sediment:/ˈsɛdəmənt/| n.|沉淀物，沉积物|Matter that settles to the bottom of a liquid; the solid material that is deposited by water, wind, or glaciers.  It is often composed of sand, silt, and clay, but can also include organic matter and other materials.|例句：The sediment at the bottom of the lake was thick with decaying leaves.  湖底的沉淀物里堆满了腐烂的树叶。

例句：The river carries a lot of sediment downstream. 河流向下游携带大量沉积物。


近义词：deposits, dregs, lees, silt

反义词：supernatant


词性：名词
