legitim:/ləˈdʒɪtɪm/| adj.|合法的，正当的，合理的|Legitimate means conforming to the law or to rules.  It suggests that something is genuine, authentic, and rightfully accepted.  The word can apply to various contexts, from actions and claims to children and governments.|例句：The government's actions were deemed legitimate by the court. = 法院认为政府的行为是合法的。

例句：His claim to the throne is legitimate. = 他对王位的继承是合法的。|近义词：lawful, legal, valid, rightful, genuine|反义词：illegitimate, unlawful, illegal, invalid, spurious|词性：形容词
