crural:/ˈkruərəl/| adj.|小腿的；腿的|Of or relating to the leg, especially the lower leg (shin).  The term often refers to the anatomical region between the knee and the ankle. |例句：The crural muscles are essential for walking and running. 小腿肌肉对于行走和跑步至关重要。

例句：He suffered a severe crural injury in the accident. 他在事故中遭受了严重的腿部（小腿）损伤。

近义词：leg, tibial

反义词：None directly opposite, but words describing other body parts could be considered contrasting (e.g., brachial, femoral)


词源: From Latin *cruralis, from crus "leg".
