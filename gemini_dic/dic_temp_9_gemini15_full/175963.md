rats:/ræts/| n.|老鼠|A small rodent, typically with a long tail, small ears, and pointed snout. Rats are known for their intelligence and ability to adapt to various environments.|例句：The rats scurried away when the light came on.  老鼠看到灯亮了就逃跑了。|例句：We need to get rid of the rats in the basement. 我们需要清除地下室的老鼠。|近义词：mice, rodents|反义词：无
