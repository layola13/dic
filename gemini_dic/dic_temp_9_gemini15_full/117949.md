membraniform:/ˈmembreɪnɪfɔːrm/| adj.|膜状的，膜形的|Resembling a membrane in form or structure.  It refers to something that is thin, flexible, and often somewhat translucent, similar to a biological membrane.|The membraniform wings of the insect were delicate and easily damaged.  昆虫的膜状翅膀很精致，很容易损坏。|膜状的，薄膜状的|  non-membraniform
