PHRASE->
There is no common phrase directly using "consonantism".  The term is primarily used in academic linguistic contexts.  However, related phrases might include:

* **Consonant clusters:**  Groups of consonants occurring together.
* **Consonant inventory:** The complete set of consonants in a language.
* **Consonant assimilation:**  The process where one consonant sound becomes more like a neighboring consonant.
* **Consonant harmony:** A phonological process where consonants in a word share certain features.
* **Place of articulation:** The point in the vocal tract where a consonant is produced.
