caulescent: /kɔːˈlesənt/ | adj. | 有茎的；具茎的 | Having a stem or stems;  possessing a stem.  The term usually refers to plants that have an above-ground stem, differentiating them from stemless plants. | Example:  The caulescent plant displayed vibrant flowers atop its sturdy stem. 这株有茎的植物在其坚实的茎上展现出鲜艳的花朵。  The caulescent sunflower towered over the surrounding vegetation.  这株有茎的向日葵高耸于周围的植被之上。| 近义词: stemmed, stalked | 反义词: acaulescent
