nonviolence:/ˈnɒnˌvaɪələns/| n. |非暴力；和平主义|The absence or rejection of violence as a means of resolving conflict or achieving social or political goals.  It emphasizes peaceful and non-aggressive methods of conflict resolution and social change.  This philosophy often involves civil disobedience, protests, and other forms of peaceful resistance.|例句：Gandhi's philosophy of nonviolence inspired many civil rights movements. 甘地的非暴力哲学启发了許多民權運動。|例句：The protesters advocated for nonviolence, even in the face of police brutality. 即使面對警方的暴行，抗議者仍然主張非暴力。|近义词：pacifism, peacefulness, non-aggression|反义词：violence, aggression, brutality|名词
