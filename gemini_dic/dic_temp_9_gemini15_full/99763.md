intermediary:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieri/| n. |中间人，媒介，调停者 |A person or thing that acts as a medium between two others.  An intermediary facilitates communication or negotiation between parties who cannot or do not wish to communicate directly. |例句：The lawyer acted as an intermediary between the two disputing parties.  律师充当了两个争端方的中间人。

例句：The real estate agent served as an intermediary in the sale of the house. 房地产经纪人在房屋买卖中充当了中间人。


近义词：mediator, go-between, negotiator, agent, intermediary, middleman


反义词：direct participant, principal


词性:名词
