crowing:/ˈkraʊɪŋ/| n. v.|啼叫；得意洋洋；高傲自大|The act of a cock or rooster making a loud, characteristic sound;  a boastful or triumphant expression of pride or self-importance.|例句：The rooster's crowing woke me up at dawn.  公鸡的啼叫在黎明时分把我吵醒了。

The athlete's crowing about his victory was rather irritating.  运动员对他胜利的夸耀相当令人恼火。|近义词：cock-a-doodle-doo, boasting, bragging|反义词：silence, humility, modesty|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rooster crows every morning. = 公鸡每天早上都啼叫。
He crows about his achievements constantly. = 他不断地夸耀他的成就。

一般过去时:
The rooster crowed at sunrise. = 公鸡在日出时啼叫。
She crowed with delight after winning the race. = 赢得比赛后，她得意地欢呼。

现在完成时:
The rooster has crowed several times this morning. = 公鸡今天早上已经啼叫了好几次。
He has crowed about his success to everyone he knows. = 他已经向他认识的每个人夸耀了他的成功。

过去完成时:
The rooster had crowed before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前公鸡已经啼叫了。
She had crowed about her promotion before she even received the official offer. = 她甚至在收到正式录取通知书之前就已经对她的晋升大肆宣扬了。

将来时:
The rooster will crow again soon. = 公鸡很快就会再次啼叫。
He will crow about this for weeks. = 他会为此夸耀几周。

虚拟语气:
If the rooster crowed at midnight, I would be very surprised. = 如果公鸡在午夜啼叫，我会非常惊讶。
If he had crowed less, people would have liked him more.  如果他少吹嘘一些，人们会更喜欢他。

被动语态:
His victory was crowed about by the media. = 他的胜利被媒体大肆宣传。
(Note:  "Crowing" is less commonly used passively.)


疑问句:
Does the rooster crow every day? = 公鸡每天都啼叫吗？
Does he often crow about his accomplishments? = 他经常夸耀他的成就吗？

否定句:
The rooster didn't crow this morning. = 公鸡今天早上没有啼叫。
He didn't crow about his win; he was very humble. = 他没有夸耀他的胜利；他非常谦虚。


PHRASE->

early crowing = 过早的得意
to crow over = 对…得意洋洋
