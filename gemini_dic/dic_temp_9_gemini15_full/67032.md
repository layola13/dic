explains:/ɪkˈspleɪnz/ | v. | 解释，说明 | To make something clear or easy to understand by describing or giving information about it.  | He explains the rules clearly. 他清晰地解释了规则。  | clarifies, elucidates, interprets | confuses, obscures | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He explains the problem to me every day. = 他每天都向我解释这个问题。
She explains the concept in detail. = 她详细解释了这个概念。


一般过去时:
The teacher explained the lesson yesterday. = 老师昨天解释了这节课。
He explained his absence to his boss. = 他向老板解释了他的缺席原因。


现在完成时:
She has explained the situation to her parents. = 她已经向父母解释了情况。
They have explained the rules several times. = 他们已经解释过好几次规则了。


过去完成时:
He had explained the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经解释了计划。
She had explained the process thoroughly before we started. = 在我们开始之前，她已经彻底解释了这个过程。


将来时:
He will explain the new software next week. = 他下周将解释新软件。
The professor will explain the theory in the next lecture. = 教授将在下一节课解释这个理论。


虚拟语气:
If he explained it more clearly, I would understand. = 如果他解释得更清楚些，我就能理解了。
I wish he had explained the instructions better. = 我希望他把说明解释得更好一些。


被动语态:
The rules were explained to the students. = 规则向学生们解释了。
The process will be explained in the manual. = 这个过程将在手册中解释。


疑问句:
Can you explain this to me? = 你能向我解释一下这个吗？
How will you explain your absence? = 你将如何解释你的缺席？


否定句:
He didn't explain the reason for his delay. = 他没有解释他延误的原因。
She couldn't explain the strange phenomenon. = 她无法解释这种奇怪的现象。


PHRASE->
explain away = 搪塞，解释掉
explain oneself = 作出解释
explain to = 向...解释
explain in detail = 详细解释
cannot explain = 无法解释
