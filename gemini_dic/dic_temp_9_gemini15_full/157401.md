pipsqueak:/ˈpɪpskwik/| n. |小人物；无足轻重的人；小不点|A pipsqueak is a small, insignificant person, often implying a lack of power or importance.  The term is often used dismissively or contemptuously.|例句：He's just a pipsqueak trying to make a name for himself. 他只是一个想出名的小人物。

例句：Don't listen to that pipsqueak; he doesn't know what he's talking about.  别听那个小不点瞎说，他不知道自己在说什么。


近义词：lightweight, shrimp, runt, insignificant person, nobody|反义词：big shot, heavyweight, VIP, important person|词性:名词

词源:
The word "pipsqueak" is of uncertain origin, but likely a combination of "pip" (a small seed or sound) and "squeak" (a high-pitched, thin sound).  The combination suggests something small and insignificant making a weak or feeble sound.
