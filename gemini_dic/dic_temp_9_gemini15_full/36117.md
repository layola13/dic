coercive: /kəʊˈɜːrsɪv/ | adj. | 强制的；高压的；胁迫的 |  relating to or using force or threats to make someone do something.  | Example: The coercive tactics of the regime led to widespread protests.  这个政权的强制手段导致了大范围的抗议。 | 近义词：compulsory, forceful, oppressive, dictatorial | 反义词：voluntary, persuasive, gentle, lenient |
