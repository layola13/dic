nonpatented:/ˌnɒnˈpætntɪd/ | adj. | 未获专利的；非专利产品 | Not protected by a patent.  This refers to an invention, process, or design that is not legally protected by a patent, allowing others to use or produce it without restriction. |  例句：The company uses a nonpatented process for manufacturing its widgets. 这家公司使用一种未获专利的工艺来制造其部件。

例句：This design is nonpatented, so anyone can copy it. 这个设计未获专利，所以任何人都可以复制它。


近义词：unpatented, unprotected, public domain


反义词：patented, protected
