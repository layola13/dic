decarbonized:/ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənʌɪzd/| adj.|脱碳的；已脱碳的|Reduced or removed carbon dioxide or carbon emissions.|The city aims to become a decarbonized society by 2050.  这座城市的目标是到2050年成为一个脱碳社会。|The decarbonized power grid relies heavily on renewable energy.  脱碳的电网严重依赖可再生能源。|近义词：carbon-neutral, low-carbon|反义词：carbon-intensive

词源:
The word "decarbonized" is formed from the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to reverse"), the combining form "carbon" (referring to carbon), and the suffix "-ized" (indicating a process of making something).  Therefore, it literally means "having had carbon removed or reduced".
