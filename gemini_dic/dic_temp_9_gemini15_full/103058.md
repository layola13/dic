jemmying: /ˈdʒɛmiɪŋ/ | v. |撬动；撬开；强行进入|The act of using a jemmy (a type of crowbar) to force something open or apart.  It often implies a forceful or illicit entry. |例句：The burglar gained entry by jemmying the back door.  窃贼撬开了后门进入。

例句：They were accused of jemmying the safe to steal the money. 他们被指控撬开保险箱偷钱。


近义词：prizing, forcing, breaking into, levering


反义词：closing, securing, locking


词性:动词
