/kaɪm/ | n. |炼金术；化学（旧用法）|Alchemy; chemistry (archaic).  The word refers to the medieval pseudoscience of alchemy, which aimed to transmute base metals into gold and discover the elixir of life.  In modern usage, it's largely obsolete, replaced by "chemistry". |例句：In the Middle Ages, chymistry was a popular pursuit. = 在中世纪，炼金术是一种流行的追求。|近义词：alchemy, chemistry (modern)|反义词：None directly applicable.
