oxygenators:/ˈɑːksɪdʒəneɪtərz/| n. | 氧合器；充氧器 | A device or system that increases the oxygen content of a substance, typically blood.  | Example:  The heart-lung machine includes an oxygenator that provides oxygen to the patient's blood during surgery. 心肺机包括一个在手术期间为病人血液提供氧气的氧合器。 |近义词：oxygenator, aerator |反义词：deoxygenator
