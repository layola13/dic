palamedeae:/ˌpæləˈmiːdiːiː/| n. | पालमेडीई (印度的一种鸟类)|A taxonomic tribe of birds in the family Anatidae,  belonging to the subfamily Anatinae (dabbling ducks).  The tribe includes several genera of ducks, often characterized by specific plumage patterns and behavioral traits.  The exact classification within the tribe can be subject to ongoing taxonomic revisions.|The Palamedeae tribe includes ducks like the shelducks and other closely related species.|近义词：Anatinae (broader term)|反义词：None directly applicable.  Could be contrasted with other duck tribes within Anatinae.
