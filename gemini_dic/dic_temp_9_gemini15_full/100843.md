intropression:/ˌɪntrəˈprɛʃən/| n. |内省；反省；自省|The act of looking inward to examine one's own thoughts, feelings, and motivations.  It involves self-reflection and introspection to understand one's inner world. |Example:  Through intropression, she came to understand the root of her anxiety. 通过内省，她明白了自己焦虑的根源。|近义词：introspection, self-reflection, self-examination |反义词：extrospection
