multilateralness:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlˌnəs/| n. |多边性；多边主义|The inherent complexity of multilateralness makes achieving consensus difficult. 多边性的固有复杂性使得达成共识变得困难。|近义词：polycentrism, multilateralism|反义词：unilateralism, bilateralism


英文解析:The state or quality of involving or being conducted by more than two parties or nations.  It refers to the involvement of multiple entities in a process, agreement, or relationship.  It often implies collaboration and cooperation among these entities.


例句:

The agreement reflects a commitment to multilateralness in addressing global challenges.  该协议体现了在应对全球挑战中对多边主义的承诺。

Promoting multilateralness is crucial for fostering international cooperation.  促进多边主义对于促进国际合作至关重要。
