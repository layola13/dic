parable:/ˈpærəbl/| n. |比喻；寓言 |A parable is a simple story used to illustrate a moral or spiritual lesson.  It often uses everyday situations and characters to convey a deeper meaning.  Parables are common in religious texts and literature. |Example: The parable of the Good Samaritan teaches us the importance of compassion.  这个好撒玛利亚人的比喻教导我们慈悲为怀的重要性。|近义词：allegory, fable, anecdote |反义词：None specifically.
