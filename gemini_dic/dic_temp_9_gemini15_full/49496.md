demagnetize:/ˌdiːˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/ | vt. |消磁，去磁化 | To remove the magnetism from (something).  The process involves reducing the magnetic field strength to zero.  This can be achieved through various methods, including heating, applying an alternating magnetic field, or physical impact. | 例句：The technician demagnetized the hard drive to erase the data. 技术员消磁了硬盘以擦除数据。

例句：We need to demagnetize the speaker before we can repair it. 我们需要在修理扬声器之前先消磁。


近义词：degauss,  dismagnetize


反义词：magnetize
