imprinters:/ɪmˈprɪntərz/| n.|压印机；印记者|A machine or device that makes impressions or prints.  Someone or something that leaves a lasting impression.|例句：The factory uses high-speed imprinters to mark the products. 工厂使用高速压印机来标记产品。

The artist's early works left a lasting imprint on the art world; the imprinters of those styles are still visible today.  这位艺术家的早期作品给艺术界留下了持久的印记；那些风格的印记至今仍可见。|近义词：stamper, marker, embosser|反义词：eraser, remover
