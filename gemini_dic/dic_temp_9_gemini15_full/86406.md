hedonists:/ˈhiːdənɪsts/| n. |享乐主义者|People who believe that pleasure is the most important thing in life and who pursue pleasure as a goal. They prioritize the pursuit of pleasure and sensual gratification above other values such as morality, duty, or productivity.  |The ancient Greek philosophers were some hedonists who believed that maximizing pleasure and minimizing pain were the ultimate goals.|近义词：pleasure-seeker, sensualist, epicurean|反义词：ascetic, puritan, stoic
