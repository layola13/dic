pseudosubtly: /ˈsuːdəʊˈsʌbtli/ | adj. |似是而非地微妙地；假装微妙地 | Apparently subtle but actually not so; deceptively subtle.  It suggests an attempt to appear subtle or nuanced, but ultimately failing to achieve genuine subtlety.  The word combines "pseudo" (false) and "subtle" (delicate, understated).| The artist's pseudosubtle use of color was ultimately distracting, not enhancing.|近义词：affectedly subtle, deceptively delicate, subtly contrived|反义词：genuinely subtle, transparent, obvious
