hauls:/hɔːlz/| n. vt.|大量货物；拖；拉；用力拖运|to transport a large quantity of something, especially goods, typically by vehicle.|例句：The truck hauls goods across the country. 这辆卡车在全国各地运输货物。|例句：He hauled the heavy box up the stairs. 他用力把沉重的箱子拖上楼梯。|近义词：transport, carry, drag, lug|反义词：push(反向动作), leave(不搬运)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The truck hauls cargo daily. = 这辆卡车每天都运输货物。
She hauls her luggage to the airport. = 她把行李拖到机场。


一般过去时:
They hauled the boat ashore. = 他们把船拖到岸上。
He hauled the net in. = 他把网拉了上来。


现在完成时:
We have hauled all the debris away. = 我们已经把所有的碎屑都搬走了。
The company has hauled millions of tons of goods this year. = 今年该公司已运输了数百万吨的货物。


过去完成时:
They had hauled the equipment before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把设备搬运好了。
She had hauled several heavy bags before feeling tired. = 她搬运了好几个沉重的包之后才感到疲倦。


将来时:
We will haul the timber tomorrow. = 我们明天将运输木材。
He will haul the furniture to his new apartment. = 他将把家具搬到他的新公寓。


虚拟语气:
If I had a truck, I would haul the materials myself. = 如果我有辆卡车，我会自己运输材料。
If he had more strength, he could haul that heavy rock. = 如果他更有力气，他就能搬动那块巨石。


被动语态:
The goods were hauled by train. = 货物是通过火车运输的。
The heavy equipment was hauled to the construction site. = 重型设备被运送到建筑工地。


疑问句:
How much cargo does the ship haul? = 这艘船运输多少货物？
What will be hauled tomorrow? = 明天要运输什么？


否定句:
They did not haul the furniture themselves. = 他们没有自己搬运家具。
The farmer didn't haul his crops to the market. = 农民没有把他的庄稼运到市场。


PHRASE->
haul in: 收获；逮捕
haul away: 把…运走
haul up: 提升；上拉；审讯
haul off: 猛拉；突然离开
haul out: 拖出；取出
