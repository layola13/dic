cashews:/ˈkæʃuːz/ | n. |腰果|Kidney-shaped seeds of a tropical tree, eaten as a snack or used in cooking.  Cashews are known for their creamy texture and slightly sweet flavor. They are often roasted and salted before consumption. |例句：I love to eat a handful of cashews while watching TV. = 我喜欢一边看电视一边吃一把腰果。

例句：She used cashew nuts in her stir-fry for added flavor and texture. = 她用腰果炒菜，以增加风味和口感。


近义词：macadamia nuts, peanuts (though botanically different) | 反义词：无


词性：名词
