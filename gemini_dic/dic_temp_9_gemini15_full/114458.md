makeweights: /ˈmeɪkˌweɪts/ | n. |配重物，平衡物 |Objects used to add weight to something to balance it or to make it stable.  Makeweights are often used in various contexts, from industrial machinery to scientific experiments. For example, they might be added to a washing machine to counteract vibration or to a scale to ensure accuracy. |例句：The engineer added makeweights to the machine to reduce vibration. 工程师在机器上添加了配重物以减少振动。

The chemist used makeweights to balance the scales. 化学家用配重物来平衡天平。
