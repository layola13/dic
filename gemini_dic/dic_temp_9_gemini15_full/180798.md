rehabilitation:/ˌriːˌhæbɪlɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |康复；恢复；改造 | The process of restoring someone to health or normal life through training and therapy after imprisonment, addiction, or illness.  It aims to help individuals regain their physical, mental, and social functioning. |例句：The rehabilitation center provides physical therapy and occupational therapy to help patients regain their independence.  康复中心提供物理治疗和职业治疗以帮助病人恢复独立生活能力。


例句：After his surgery, he underwent a long period of rehabilitation to regain his strength.  手术后，他进行了长时间的康复治疗以恢复体力。


近义词：recovery, restoration, reconditioning, remediation


反义词：deterioration, decline, incapacitation


词性:名词
