penetrativities:/ˌpɛnɪtrəˈtɪvɪtiz/| n.|渗透性；穿透力 |The ability to penetrate or be penetrated; the degree to which something can penetrate.|例句：The penetrativities of different materials vary widely.  不同材料的渗透性差异很大。


例句：The research focused on the penetrativities of various substances through biological membranes. 研究重点是各种物质透过生物膜的渗透性。


近义词：permeability, transmissivity, penetrability


反义词：impermeability, impenetrability


词性:名词


词源:  Derived from the word "penetrative," which comes from the Latin word "penetratīvus," meaning "able to penetrate."  The suffix "-ities" denotes a plural of qualities or properties.
