ethically:/ˈeθɪkəli/ | adv. | 道德上地，伦理上地 |  In a way that conforms to accepted moral standards; morally.  It describes actions or behaviors that are considered right or proper according to ethical principles. | 例句：The company is committed to acting ethically in all its business dealings.  这家公司致力于在其所有商业交易中都以道德的方式行事。

例句： We must always treat each other ethically. 我们必须始终以道德的方式对待彼此。


近义词：morally, virtuously, righteously, justly | 反义词：immorally, unethically, unjustly, wickedly

词性: 副词
