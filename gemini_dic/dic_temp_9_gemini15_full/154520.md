perpetuality:/ˌpɜːrpɛtʃuˈæləti/| n. |永久性，永恒；永久存在|The state or quality of being perpetual; lasting forever.  It refers to something that continues indefinitely without end.  The concept often relates to things that are not subject to change or decay.|例句：The treaty ensured the perpetuity of peace between the two nations.  这项条约确保了两国之间永久的和平。

The perpetuity of the universe is a question that has puzzled philosophers for centuries. 宇宙的永恒是一个困扰哲学家几个世纪的问题。|近义词：permanence, eternity, immortality|反义词：transitoriness, ephemerality, temporariness
