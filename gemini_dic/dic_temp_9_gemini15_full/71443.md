flambé:/flɑːmˈbeɪ/| vt.| flambéed |  flambéing |  浇酒点燃（食物）|To flambé food is to add liquor to it and then set it alight, usually as a finishing touch. This technique adds a distinctive flavour and visual appeal to dishes.| The chef flambéed the bananas with rum.  厨师用朗姆酒将香蕉 flambé 了。|  sear, ignite | extinguish
