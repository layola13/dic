nullified:/ˈnʌlɪfaɪd/| v.| 使无效；取消；废除 |To make something legally or officially null and void; to render ineffective or useless. |例句：The contract was nullified by the court. 法院宣布合同无效。

The judge nullified the agreement between the parties.  法官宣布双方之间的协议无效。


近义词：invalidate, annul, abolish, cancel, void |反义词：validate, confirm, ratify

一般现在时:
The court nullifies many contracts each year. = 法院每年使许多合同无效。
He nullifies his opponent's arguments with facts. = 他用事实驳斥了对手的论点。

一般过去时:
The rain nullified our plans for a picnic. = 雨水使我们的野餐计划泡汤了。
The high winds nullified the effect of the pesticide. = 强风抵消了杀虫剂的效果。

现在完成时:
The new law has nullified the previous regulations. = 新法律使之前的规章失效。
The evidence presented has nullified the defendant's alibi. = 出示的证据推翻了被告的不在场证明。

过去完成时:
The prior agreement had been nullified by the subsequent contract. = 先前的协议已被后续合同所取代（使无效）。
They had nullified their own efforts by making a crucial mistake. = 他们因犯了一个关键性错误而使自己的努力付诸东流。

将来时:
The new policy will nullify many existing permits. = 新政策将使许多现有的许可证失效。
The upcoming storm will likely nullify our chances of a successful launch. = 即将来临的风暴很可能会使我们成功发射的机会化为泡影。


虚拟语气:
If the treaty were nullified, it would lead to international conflict. = 如果条约被废除，将导致国际冲突。
I wish the judge had nullified the unfair sentence. = 我希望法官已经推翻了那个不公平的判决。

被动语态:
The agreement was nullified because of a technicality. = 由于一个技术性问题，协议被宣布无效。
His efforts were nullified by unforeseen circumstances. = 他的努力由于不可预见的情况而付诸东流。

疑问句:
Will the new law nullify the existing tax code? = 新法律会使现有的税法失效吗？
Does this clause nullify the previous agreement? = 这条款是否使之前的协议无效？

否定句:
The court did not nullify the contract. = 法院没有宣布合同无效。
The evidence did not nullify his claims. = 证据没有推翻他的说法。


PHRASE->

词源:
From  Latin *nullificare, from nullus "nothing" + facere "to make".
