grapeshot:/ˈɡreɪpʃɒt/| n. |葡萄弹,霰弹 |A type of ammunition consisting of a number of small iron balls or other projectiles enclosed in a canvas bag and fired from a cannon.  It was designed to inflict widespread damage and casualties among massed ranks of enemy soldiers. |The grapeshot blast scattered the enemy troops.|近义词：cannister shot, chain shot|反义词：single projectile
