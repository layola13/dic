hanger:/ˈhæŋər/| n. |衣架；绞架；飞机的起落架|A hanger is a device, typically a piece of wire or wood shaped like a hook, used to hang clothes.  It can also refer to a gallows or the landing gear of an aircraft.|例句：I hung my coat on the hanger.我把外套挂在衣架上。

例句：The prisoner was hanged on the hanger. 犯人被绞死在绞刑架上。


近义词： clothes peg, hook, rack |反义词：None directly applicable.


词源:  The word "hanger" comes from the Old English word "hangere," meaning "one who hangs," which itself derives from the verb "hang."  Its evolution reflects its primary function of supporting something that is hung.
