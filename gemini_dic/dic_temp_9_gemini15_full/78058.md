geocaching:/ˈdʒioʊˌkæʃɪŋ/| n. | 地理寻宝，GPS寻宝 | The activity of using a GPS device to find hidden containers (caches) based on their coordinates.  Geocaching combines the thrill of treasure hunting with the technology of GPS navigation. |  例句：My friends and I went geocaching in the park last weekend.  我们上周末在公园里玩地理寻宝游戏。

例句：She uses a smartphone app to help her geocaching. 她用智能手机应用程序来帮助她进行地理寻宝。


近义词：treasure hunting, GPS treasure hunting | 反义词：None specifically.  | 词性：名词
