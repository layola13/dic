/ɡrɑːmz/ | n. | 克(重量单位) | grams are a unit of mass in the metric system.  One gram is equal to one thousandth of a kilogram. | Example: This bag of sugar weighs 500 grams.  这包糖重500克。

Example:  The recipe calls for 20 grams of flour. 这个食谱需要20克面粉。


近义词: gramme


反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以根据语境用kilogram等表示更大的重量单位)


词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The package weighs 100 grams. = 这个包裹重100克。
These earrings weigh only 2 grams. = 这些耳环只有2克重。


一般过去时:
The gold bar weighed 1 kilogram. = 金条重1公斤。
The letter weighed only 5 grams. = 这封信只有5克重。


现在完成时:
I have weighed the ingredients. = 我已经称量了配料。
She has used 100 grams of butter. = 她已经用了100克黄油。


过去完成时:
He had already weighed the flour before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经称量好面粉了。
They had used up all the grams of sugar. = 他们已经用完了所有的糖。


将来时:
I will weigh the vegetables before I cook them. = 我会在烹饪蔬菜之前称量它们。
We will need 250 grams of meat for the recipe. = 这个食谱需要250克肉。


虚拟语气:
If I had more grams of salt, the soup would taste better. = 如果我有更多的盐，汤的味道会更好。
I wish I had weighed the package before I mailed it. = 我希望我在邮寄包裹之前称过重。


被动语态:
The goods were weighed before shipment. = 货物在运输前已过秤。
The flour is being weighed by the baker. = 面粉正在被面包师称量。


疑问句:
How many grams does this weigh? = 这个重多少克？
How many grams of sugar did you use? = 你用了多少克糖？


否定句:
The package doesn't weigh more than 100 grams. = 这个包裹不超过100克。
This bag of rice doesn't weigh 5 kilograms. = 这袋米不重5公斤。
