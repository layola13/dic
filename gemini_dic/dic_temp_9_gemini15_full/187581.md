romanticizes:/rəʊˈmæntaɪsaɪz/| vt.|浪漫化；理想化；美化|To represent or think about something in a romantic or idealized way, often ignoring the less pleasant aspects.|例句：The film romanticizes the lives of pirates, showing them as adventurous heroes instead of brutal criminals. 这部电影浪漫化了海盗的生活，将他们描绘成冒险英雄而不是残酷的罪犯。|例句：He romanticizes his childhood, forgetting the hardships his family endured. 他把童年浪漫化了，忘记了家人所忍受的苦难。|近义词：idealize, idealise, glamorize, glorify|反义词：realistic, downplay, trivialize

一般现在时:
He romanticizes his past relationships. = 他把过去的恋情浪漫化了。
She romanticizes the idea of living in Paris. = 她对住在巴黎的想法进行了浪漫化处理。
