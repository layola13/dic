It implies an excessive desire for attention and admiration, often accompanied by a lack of substance.  The possessive 's indicates possession or belonging.|例句：Her peacockishness at the awards ceremony was embarrassing.  (她在颁奖典礼上的炫耀行为令人尴尬。)  His peacockishness ultimately overshadowed his actual talent.
