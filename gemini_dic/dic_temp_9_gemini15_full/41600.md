corpus:/ˈkɔːrpəs/| /ˈkɔːrpʌs/ | n. |语料库；语料；文本集|A body of text, speech, or other data used to analyze language use.  It can be a collection of written texts, spoken recordings, or even images.|例句：The researchers used a large corpus of newspaper articles to study changes in language use over time. 研究人员使用大量的报纸文章语料库来研究语言使用随时间的变化。|例句：This corpus of historical letters provides valuable insights into the social life of the period. 这批历史信件语料库为了解当时的社会生活提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：collection, body, compilation |反义词：none
