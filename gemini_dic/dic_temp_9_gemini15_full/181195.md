reinjuring: /ˌriːˈɪndʒərɪŋ/ | v. | 再受伤；再次受伤 | The act of injuring something or someone again after a previous injury.  This word describes the act of suffering an injury to a previously injured area or body part. |  He was reinjured his knee during the game. 他在比赛中再次伤到了膝盖。 The runner reinjured his hamstring in the final race.  这位跑步者在决赛中再次拉伤了腿筋。 | injuring again, re-injuring | healing, recovering  | 动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinjures his ankle frequently. = 他经常扭伤脚踝。
She reinjures her back lifting heavy boxes. = 她搬重箱子时再次伤了腰。

一般过去时:
He reinjured his shoulder playing tennis. = 他打网球时再次伤了肩膀。
She reinjured her knee during the marathon. = 她在马拉松比赛中再次伤了膝盖。

现在完成时:
He has reinjured his wrist several times this year. = 今年他已经几次扭伤了手腕。
She has reinjured her ankle, delaying her recovery. = 她再次扭伤了脚踝，延误了康复。

过去完成时:
He had reinjured his back before the competition even started. = 比赛甚至还没开始，他就已经再次伤了腰。
She had reinjured her leg, so she couldn't participate. = 她再次伤了腿，所以不能参加。

将来时:
He will reinjure his knee if he doesn't take it easy. = 如果他不轻松点，他将会再次伤到膝盖。
She will likely reinjure her shoulder if she resumes training too soon. = 如果她太早恢复训练，她很可能会再次伤到肩膀。

虚拟语气:
If he had warmed up properly, he wouldn't have reinjured his hamstring. = 如果他好好热身了，他就不会再次拉伤腿筋了。
