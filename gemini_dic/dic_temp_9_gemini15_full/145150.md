overassess:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɛs/| vt.|过高评估，高估|To assess something at too high a value or rate.  This often implies an inaccurate or unfair judgment.|例句：The committee overassessed the risks involved in the project. 委员会高估了该项目中的风险。
The appraiser overassessed the value of the house by 20%. 评估师将房子的价值高估了20%。|近义词：overestimate, overvalue, exaggerate|反义词：underestimate, undervalue
