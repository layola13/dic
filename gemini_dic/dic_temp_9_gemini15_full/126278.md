mygale:/maɪˈɡeɪl/| n. |大蜘蛛；捕鸟蛛|A mygale is a large, hairy spider, often belonging to the Theraphosidae family (tarantulas). They are known for their size and sometimes venomous bite, though most species are not dangerous to humans.  |Example:  The entomologist carefully observed the mygale in its terrarium.  昆虫学家仔细观察了它笼子里的捕鸟蛛。|近义词：tarantula, giant spider|反义词：small spider, insect
