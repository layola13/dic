predistinguished: /ˌpriːdɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/ | adj. | 预先著名的；卓越的；杰出的 |  Already distinguished; famous or celebrated before a particular time or event.  The word combines the prefix "pre-" (before) with "distinguished," indicating prior recognition or eminence. |


例句：

He gave a predistinguished lecture on the subject. 他就这个主题做了一个著名的演讲。

The predistinguished scientist received numerous awards for his groundbreaking research.  这位卓越的科学家因其突破性的研究获得了无数奖项。


近义词：eminent, renowned, illustrious, celebrated


反义词：obscure, unknown, insignificant


词性：形容词
