mescaline:/ˈmɛskəliːn/| n. |麦司卡林|A psychedelic alkaloid found in the peyote cactus, known for its hallucinogenic effects.  It is used in some Native American religious ceremonies and is a Schedule I controlled substance in many countries.|例句：Mescaline is a powerful hallucinogen that can alter perception and mood. 麦司卡林是一种强大的致幻剂，可以改变感知和情绪。|例句：The use of mescaline in religious ceremonies is a complex and controversial topic. 在宗教仪式中使用麦司卡林是一个复杂且有争议的话题。|近义词：psychedelic, hallucinogen|反义词：sobering agent (a substance that reduces or counteracts the effects of a hallucinogen)|词性:名词
