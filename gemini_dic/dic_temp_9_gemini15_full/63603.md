envying: /ˈɛnviɪŋ/ | v. | 羡慕；嫉妒 |  Feeling or showing envy.  The act of envying. |例句：I was envying her beautiful new car.  我羡慕她的漂亮新车。

She couldn't help envying her sister's success. 她禁不住羡慕她姐姐的成功。


近义词：coveting, jealousing | 反义词：admiring (in a non-envious way)


词源:

The word "envying" comes from the Old French word "envire," meaning "to surround, encompass," and ultimately from the Latin word "invidere," meaning "to look at with ill will, to envy."  The sense shifted from simply looking at with ill will to the feeling of envy itself.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I envy your talent. = 我羡慕你的才能。
They envy his wealth. = 他们羡慕他的财富。

一般过去时:
I envied her confidence yesterday. = 昨天我羡慕她的自信。
He envied his friend's promotion. = 他嫉妒他朋友的升迁。

现在完成时:
I have envied her success for years. = 多年来我一直羡慕她的成功。
She has envied his travels since childhood. = 从小她就一直羡慕他的旅行。

过去完成时:
I had envied his position before I got my own promotion. = 在我得到升迁之前，我一直羡慕他的职位。
She had envied his skills before she mastered them herself. = 在她自己掌握这些技能之前，她一直羡慕他的技能。

将来时:
I will envy her when she wins the award. = 当她获奖时，我会羡慕她。
They will envy his new house next year. = 明年他们会羡慕他的新房子。

虚拟语气:

I wish I didn't envy his lifestyle. = 我希望我不羡慕他的生活方式。

被动语态:
