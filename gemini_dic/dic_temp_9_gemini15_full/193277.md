seekers:/ˈsiːkərz/| n.|寻求者，追求者|People who are searching for something, such as a job, truth, or spiritual enlightenment.  They are actively looking for something specific.|例句：Job seekers are encouraged to apply online. 求职者鼓励在线申请。


例句：Spiritual seekers often embark on journeys of self-discovery. 寻求精神解脱的人经常踏上自我发现的旅程。

近义词：searchers, pursuers, hunters, questors

反义词：finders (in the sense of having already found what they sought)
