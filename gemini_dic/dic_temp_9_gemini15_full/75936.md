fungite:/ˈfʌnɡaɪt/| n. |真菌；真菌病|A fungite is any member of the kingdom Fungi, a large group of eukaryotic organisms that includes yeasts, molds, and mushrooms. They are heterotrophic, meaning they obtain their nutrients by absorbing organic matter from their environment.  Many fungi are important decomposers, playing a crucial role in nutrient cycling. Others are pathogens, causing diseases in plants and animals.  The study of fungi is called mycology. | Example:  The farmer's crop suffered from a devastating fungite.  农民的庄稼遭受了毁灭性的真菌病害。|近义词：fungus (单数形式)|反义词：无
