odiously:/ˈoʊdiəsli/| adj. |令人厌恶地；可憎地；讨厌地 | Extremely unpleasant; hateful.  It describes something or someone that is highly offensive or disagreeable.  |例句：He behaved odiously towards his colleagues, spreading malicious rumors and undermining their work. 他对同事的行为令人厌恶，四处散布恶意谣言，破坏他们的工作。

例句：The smell in the room was odiously foul. 房间里的气味令人作呕。


近义词：hatefully, abominably, detestably, repulsively, disgustingly |反义词：pleasantly, agreeably, charmingly, delightfully


词源:  Derived from the adjective "odious," ultimately from the Latin word "ōdī," meaning "I hate."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb.
