sandblasting:/ˈsændˌblæstɪŋ/| n. |喷砂；砂喷 |The process of cleaning or etching a surface by blowing sand against it at high velocity.|例句：The old paint was removed by sandblasting. 旧油漆是用喷砂去除的。
The statue was cleaned using sandblasting techniques.  雕像使用了喷砂技术进行清洁。|近义词：abrasive blasting, grit blasting|反义词：polishing, smoothing
