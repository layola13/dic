holland:/ˈhɒlənd/ /ˈhɑːlənd/| n. |荷兰；荷兰人；荷兰语|The Netherlands, a low-lying country in northwestern Europe, famous for its windmills, tulips, and canals.  It is often referred to as Holland, although this is technically only a region within the Netherlands. The term "Holland" is often used colloquially to refer to the entire country. |例句：We spent a week cycling through the beautiful countryside of Holland. = 我们花了一周时间在荷兰美丽的乡村骑自行车。

例句：Many Dutch people are proud of their heritage and culture.  = 许多荷兰人都为他们的传统和文化感到自豪。


近义词：The Netherlands (formal), Dutch (referring to people or language) |反义词：无|词性:n.
