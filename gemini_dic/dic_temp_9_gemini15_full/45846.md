cut-a-deal:/kʌt ə diːl/| v. |达成协议，做交易 |to reach an agreement, especially in business or politics, often one that benefits both sides.  This phrase implies a quick, sometimes informal, agreement.|例句：They cut a deal to share the profits. 他们达成协议分享利润。

例句：The two companies cut a deal that allowed them to avoid a costly lawsuit.  两家公司达成协议，避免了一场代价高昂的诉讼。


近义词：make a deal, strike a bargain, negotiate a settlement, reach an agreement

反义词：fail to agree, reach an impasse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cut a deal every week. = 他们每周都达成协议。
We cut a deal with the supplier. = 我们和供应商达成了协议。


一般过去时:
They cut a deal last month. = 他们上个月达成了协议。
She cut a deal with the police. = 她和警方达成了协议。


现在完成时:
They have cut a deal with a new partner. = 他们已经和一位新伙伴达成了协议。
We have cut many deals this year. = 今年我们已经达成了许多协议。


过去完成时:
They had cut a deal before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经达成了协议。
She had cut a similar deal before. = 她以前也达成过类似的协议。


将来时:
They will cut a deal next week. = 他们下周将达成协议。
We will cut a deal if the conditions are right. = 如果条件合适，我们将达成协议。


虚拟语气:
If they had cut a better deal, they would be richer. = 如果他们达成更好的协议，他们会更富有。
I wish I had cut a better deal on that car. = 我真希望我当初买那辆车时能谈个更好的价钱。


被动语态:
A deal was cut between the two parties. = 双方达成了协议。
The deal was cut quickly and efficiently. = 协议达成迅速而有效率。


疑问句:
Did they cut a deal? = 他们达成协议了吗？
Have they cut a deal yet? = 他们达成协议了吗？


否定句:
They didn't cut a deal. = 他们没有达成协议。
We couldn't cut a deal with them. = 我们无法与他们达成协议。


PHRASE->
cut a sweet deal = 达成一项非常有利的交易
cut a rough deal = 达成一项不公平的交易
cut a backroom deal = 秘密达成协议
cut a corner to cut a deal = 为了达成协议而走捷径
cut a deal with the devil = 与魔鬼做交易 (比喻为为了达成目标不择手段)
