forestage:/ˈfɔːrˌsteɪdʒ/| n. |前台；舞台前部 |The part of a stage that is closest to the audience.  It is often used to refer to the area of a stage where actors make their entrances and exits, and where they interact with the audience. |例句：The actors took their bows on the forestage.  演员们在前台谢幕。

例句：The lighting designer focused on the forestage to highlight the main characters.  灯光设计师将灯光集中在前台，以突出主要人物。


近义词：proscenium, apron


反义词：backstage


词性：名词
