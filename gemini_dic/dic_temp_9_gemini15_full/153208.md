pelopium:/pɛˈloʊpiəm/| n. |镥(旧名，现已废弃)|Pelopium is an obsolete name for the chemical element niobium.  It was mistakenly identified as a separate element in the 19th century. The name is derived from the Peloponnese peninsula in Greece.  The element is now exclusively referred to as niobium (Nb).|The early chemists believed pelopium to be a distinct element, but further research revealed it was actually niobium.|近义词:niobium (Nb)|反义词:None|名词
