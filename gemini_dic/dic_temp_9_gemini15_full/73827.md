formication:/ˌfɔːrmɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |蚁走感，爬行感|The sensation of insects crawling on or under the skin.  This is often a symptom of neurological disorders, drug withdrawal, or other medical conditions.|例句：The patient reported experiencing formication, a feeling of ants crawling on her skin.  病人报告说有蚁走感，感觉像蚂蚁在她皮肤上爬行。

例句：She suffered from formication during her alcohol withdrawal. 她戒酒期间患有蚁走感。


近义词：paresthesia (感觉异常), crawling sensation (爬行感)|反义词：Numbness (麻木)|词性：名词
