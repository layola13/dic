self-congratulation:/ˌsɛlf kənˌɡrætʃʊˈleɪʃən/| n. |自我祝贺；沾沾自喜|The act of praising or congratulating oneself; excessive pride in one's achievements.  It often implies an excessive or unwarranted sense of self-satisfaction.|例句：Her self-congratulation on winning the award was a little embarrassing for everyone else. 她对自己赢得奖项的自我祝贺让其他人有点尴尬。

例句：His self-congratulation after a minor success was tiresome. 他在取得小小的成功后沾沾自喜，令人厌烦。


近义词：self-praise, complacency, pride, smugness|反义词：self-deprecation, humility, modesty|词性:名词
