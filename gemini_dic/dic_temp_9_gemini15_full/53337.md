disadorn: /dɪsæˈdɔːrn/ | vt. |除去…的装饰；使…失去光彩| To remove the adornment from; to deprive of ornament; to divest of beauty or attractiveness. |例句：The vandals disadorned the statue by scratching its surface.  破坏者通过划伤雕像表面来破坏它的装饰。

例句：The simple dress disadorned her usual glamorous appearance.  这件简单的衣服让她通常光彩照人的外表黯然失色。


近义词：deface, despoil, divest, strip, mar


反义词：adorn, decorate, embellish


词性：动词
