manifestly:/ˈmænɪfɛstli/| adv. |明显地，显然地 |Clearly and obviously; in a way that is easily seen or understood.  |The results manifestly demonstrate the effectiveness of the new treatment.  结果明显地证明了新疗法的有效性。|clearly, evidently, obviously, plainly |obscurely, subtly, vaguely |副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It manifestly shows the truth. = 它明显地显示了真相。
The problem is manifestly unfair. = 这个问题显然是不公平的。


一般过去时:
The evidence manifestly proved his guilt. = 证据显然证明了他的罪行。
It manifestly failed to meet expectations. = 它显然没有达到预期。


现在完成时:
The results have manifestly exceeded our projections. = 结果显然已经超过了我们的预期。
His incompetence has manifestly damaged the project. = 他的无能显然损害了这个项目。


过去完成时:
It had manifestly become clear that he was lying. = 很明显他已经在说谎。
The danger had manifestly increased. = 危险显然增加了。


将来时:
The benefits will manifestly outweigh the costs. = 好处将明显超过成本。
The consequences will manifestly be severe. = 后果显然将非常严重。


虚拟语气:
If it were manifestly true, we would act differently. = 如果这是显而易见的真相，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish it were manifestly clear to everyone. = 我希望每个人都明白这一点。


被动语态:
The truth was manifestly ignored. = 真相显然被忽视了。
The flaws were manifestly overlooked. = 缺陷显然被忽略了。


疑问句:
Isn't it manifestly obvious? = 这不是很明显吗？
Is the solution not manifestly clear? = 解决方法难道不明显吗？


否定句:
It is not manifestly clear what the next step is. = 下一步是什么还不明显。
The reasons were not manifestly apparent. = 原因并不明显。


PHRASE->
manifestly clear:  显而易见
manifestly evident:  非常明显
manifestly unfair:  显然不公平
manifestly impossible:  显然不可能
manifestly untrue:  显然不真实
