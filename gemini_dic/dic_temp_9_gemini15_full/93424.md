illume:/ɪˈluːm/| vt.|照亮；阐明；使辉煌|To illuminate; to brighten; to enlighten.|例句：The moon illumed the night sky. 月亮照亮了夜空。|例句：His speech illumed the complex issue. 他的演讲阐明了这个复杂的问题。|近义词：light up, illuminate, brighten|反义词：darken, obscure
