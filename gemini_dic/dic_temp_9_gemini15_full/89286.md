homodynamic:/ˌhoʊmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. | 同动力学的；同向的 |  Relating to or characterized by homodynamics;  having the same dynamic properties.  This term is primarily used in specialized fields like physics and engineering, often referring to systems with similar energy or force distributions.  It lacks a widely used common definition. | Example: The researchers observed homodynamic behavior in the two fluid systems.  研究人员观察到这两个流体系统具有同动力学行为。 |近义词: isodynamic (in some contexts) | 反义词: heterodynamic
