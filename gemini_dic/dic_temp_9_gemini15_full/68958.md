fatherlessness:/ˈfɑːðərləsnəs/| n. |缺乏父爱；没有父亲；父爱的缺失|The absence of a father figure in a child's life.  This can have significant negative impacts on a child's development and well-being, leading to various social, emotional, and behavioral problems.  It is a complex issue with multifaceted causes and consequences.|The study explored the effects of fatherlessness on adolescent boys. 这项研究探讨了缺乏父爱对青春期男孩的影响。 The long-term consequences of fatherlessness are often devastating.  缺乏父爱的长期后果通常是毁灭性的。|paternal deprivation, lack of a father figure|parental presence, fatherhood|名词
