retaping:/ˌriːˈteɪpɪŋ/| n.|重新录音；重新粘贴|The act of taping something again, either audio or video recording or physically attaching something with tape.|例句：The director ordered a retaping of the scene due to a technical error.  导演因为技术错误下令重拍该场景。|例句：The loose hem needed retaping to secure it.  松开的衣边需要重新粘贴才能固定。|近义词：re-recording, re-attaching|反义词：removal, detaching|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band is retaping their new album. = 乐队正在重新录制他们的新专辑。
He retapes the edges of the carpet regularly. = 他定期重新粘贴地毯的边缘。


一般过去时:
We retaped the interview yesterday after the power outage. = 停电后，我们昨天重新录制了采访。
She retaped the poster after it fell off the wall. = 海报从墙上掉下来后，她重新粘贴了它。


现在完成时:
They have retaped the entire movie sequence. = 他们已经重新录制了整段电影片段。
I have retaped all the loose wires. = 我已经重新粘贴了所有松动的电线。


过去完成时:
They had retaped the audio before submitting it to the editor. = 他们在提交给编辑之前已经重新录制了音频。
She had retaped the broken vase before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，她已经重新粘贴了破裂的花瓶。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had known the recording was faulty, I would have retaped it. = 如果我知道录音有故障，我就会重新录制它。
If we had more time, we would retape the entire scene. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新拍摄整个场景。


被动语态:

The loose button was retaped onto the shirt. = 松动的纽扣被重新缝在了衬衫上。

否定句:

She didn't retape the package, so it came undone. = 她没有重新粘贴包裹，所以它散开了。
