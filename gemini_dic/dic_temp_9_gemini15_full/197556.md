shellproof: /ˈʃɛlpruːf/ | adj. | 防弹的，防炮弹的 |  Resistant to shelling or bombardment; able to withstand shelling.  This word combines "shell" (a projectile fired from a cannon or other weapon) and "proof" (resistant to).  | Example: The shellproof bunker protected the soldiers from enemy fire.  防弹掩体保护士兵免受敌方炮火袭击。 | 近义词：bombproof, bulletproof, impenetrable | 反义词：vulnerable, exposed
