reunion:/ˌriːˈjuːnjən/| n. |团聚，重聚 |The act of coming together again after a period of separation; a gathering of people who have not seen each other for a long time.  |例句：The family reunion was a joyous occasion. 家庭团聚是一个快乐的时刻。

例句：Our college reunion is next month. 我们大学的同学聚会下个月举行。


近义词：reassembly, get-together, gathering |反义词：separation, parting


词源:  From Old French *reunion, from the verb réunir "to reunite," from re- "again" + unir "to unite."  Ultimately derived from Latin *re- "again" + unire "to unite."
