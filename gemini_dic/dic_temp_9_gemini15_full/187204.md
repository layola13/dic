roastery:/ˈroʊstəri/| n. |烘焙坊，咖啡烘焙厂|A place where coffee beans are roasted.  A roastery can range from a small-scale operation to a large-scale commercial facility.|例句：The aroma of freshly roasted coffee beans filled the air in the roastery.  烘焙好的咖啡豆的香气充满了烘焙坊的空气。

例句：Many small-batch roasters are popping up all over the city. 许多小型咖啡烘焙厂正在这座城市遍地开花。


近义词：coffee roaster, roasting plant|反义词：None.

词性：名词
