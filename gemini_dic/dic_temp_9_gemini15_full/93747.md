immatureness:/ˌɪmætʃʊrˈnɛs/ | n. |不成熟；幼稚；未成熟 |The state or quality of being immature; lack of maturity.  It often refers to a lack of emotional, intellectual, or social development appropriate for one's age or stage of life.  |例句：His immatureness was evident in his impulsive decisions.  他的幼稚在他的冲动决定中显而易见。

His immatureness prevented him from handling the situation responsibly. 他的不成熟使他无法负责任地处理这种情况。


近义词：childishness, naiveté, puerility, juvenility |反义词：maturity, adulthood, sophistication
