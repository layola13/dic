nontribal:/ˌnɑːnˈtraɪbəl/ | adj. | 非部落的；非宗族的 | Not relating to or characteristic of a tribe or tribes; not tribal.  This adjective describes things or people that are not associated with a particular tribe or the characteristics of tribal life. It often implies a broader, more inclusive, or less localized identity or structure. | Example: The nontribal communities in the region have their own unique traditions.  这个地区非部落社群有其独特的传统。 The nontribal government supported the development of  infrastructure in rural areas. 非部落政府支持农村地区的基础设施建设。 | 近义词: non-tribal, non-clan, general, universal | 反义词: tribal, clannish, localized
