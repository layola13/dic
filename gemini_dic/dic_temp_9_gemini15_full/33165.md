chromatype:/ˈkroʊməˌtaɪp/ | n. | 色素型，肤色类型 | A chromatype is a classification system for human skin based on its color and response to sunlight.  It's often used in the context of skincare and cosmetics to determine appropriate products and sun protection strategies.  | Example:  Determining your chromatype is crucial for choosing the right foundation shade and sun protection.  确定你的色素型对于选择合适的粉底色号和防晒霜至关重要。 | 近义词：skin type, complexion, skin tone | 反义词：无
