reamer:/ˈriːmər/| n. |铰孔器，扩孔器|A reamer is a tool used to enlarge or finish a hole that has already been drilled.  It's typically cylindrical with cutting edges or teeth that remove small amounts of material to achieve a precise diameter and smooth surface. |例句：The machinist used a reamer to enlarge the hole to the exact size. 技工用铰孔器将孔扩大到精确尺寸。

例句：We needed a reamer to smooth the rough edges of the drilled hole. 我们需要一个铰孔器来磨平钻孔的粗糙边缘。


近义词：reaming tool, boring tool, expanding tool |反义词：None (Antonyms are not readily applicable)|词性:Noun
