forth:/fɔːrθ/| adv.|向前，向外，出来，公开地|Forward in space or time; outward; into view or notice; into existence or prominence.|例句：He stepped forth to greet me. 他走上前来迎接我。|例句：The truth will come forth eventually. 真相最终会大白。|近义词：forward, onward, outward|反义词：backward, inward, back


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun comes forth each morning. = 太阳每天早晨升起。
She puts forth her best effort. = 她尽了最大努力。


一般过去时:
The soldiers marched forth bravely. = 士兵们勇敢地前进。
He put forth a new theory. = 他提出了一种新理论。


现在完成时:
The truth has at last come forth. = 真相终于大白了。
She has put forth a lot of effort. = 她付出了很多努力。


过去完成时:
After the storm had passed, the sun came forth. = 暴风雨过后，太阳出来了。
Before the meeting, he had put forth his proposal. = 会议之前，他提出了他的建议。


将来时:
The results will soon come forth. = 结果很快就会出来。
He will put forth his plan tomorrow. = 他明天将提出他的计划。


虚拟语气:
If he were to come forth with the truth, it would be better. = 如果他肯说出真相，那就好了。
If she had put forth more effort, she might have succeeded. = 如果她当时更努力一些，也许就成功了。


被动语态:
The new product was put forth last week. = 新产品上周发布了。
The information was brought forth at the meeting. = 信息在会议上被公布。


疑问句:
When will the results come forth? = 结果什么时候出来？
What ideas will he put forth? = 他会提出什么想法？


否定句:
The truth did not come forth. = 真相没有大白。
He did not put forth any effort. = 他没有付出任何努力。


PHRASE->
come forth = 出现，出来
set forth = 阐明，出发
forthwith = 立即，马上
put forth = 提出，发表
from this day forth = 从今天起
