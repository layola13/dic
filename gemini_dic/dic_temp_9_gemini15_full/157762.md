placebos:/pləˈsiːboʊz/| n.|安慰剂，无效疗法|A placebo is a substance that has no therapeutic effect, often used in medical research to compare the effectiveness of a treatment against a control group.|例句：The patients in the control group received a placebo instead of the new drug. 对照组的病人接受的是安慰剂而不是新药。

例句：The study showed that the placebo effect was significant. 研究表明安慰剂效应显著。|近义词：sham, dummy|反义词：active treatment, cure
