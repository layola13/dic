gassed-up:/ɡæstˈʌp/| adj.|  兴奋的；充满能量的；(车辆)加满油的 |  Excited, energetic, or full of enthusiasm;  (of a vehicle) having its fuel tank filled. |例句：The team was gassed-up and ready for the game.  球队兴奋起来，准备比赛了。|例句：Before the long road trip, we made sure the car was gassed up. 长途旅行前，我们确保汽车加满了油。|近义词：energized, hyped, pumped, fueled |反义词：exhausted, drained, lethargic
