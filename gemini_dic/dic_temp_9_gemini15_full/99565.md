interim:/ˈɪntərɪm/| adj., n. |临时的；过渡时期；中间时期 |Temporary; a period of time between two events; an intermediate stage. |例句：The interim government will be in power until the elections.  临时政府将在选举前执政。 |例句：He held the interim position until a permanent replacement was found. 他担任临时职位，直到找到永久的替代者。 |近义词：temporary, provisional, transitional |反义词：permanent, final


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The interim report is satisfactory. = 临时报告令人满意。
This is an interim solution. = 这是一个临时的解决方案。


一般过去时:
The interim government collapsed last week. = 临时政府上周垮台了。
They appointed an interim manager. = 他们任命了一名临时经理。

过去完成时:
