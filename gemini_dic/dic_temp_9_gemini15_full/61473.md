embrace:/ɪmˈbreɪs/ | vt. |拥抱；欣然接受；包含；围绕| to hold someone tightly in your arms, usually to show affection; to accept something enthusiastically; to include something as part of something else; to surround or enclose something | Examples:  She embraced her son tightly. 她紧紧地抱着她的儿子。 He embraced the opportunity to travel. 他欣然接受了旅行的机会。|近义词：hug, clasp, accept, include |反义词：reject, refuse, shun


USAGE->

She embraces every challenge. = 她迎接每一个挑战。
