USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs. Therefore, tense changes are not applicable.  The usage depends on the context and what kind of sac is being described.

This word is not typically used in common phrases.  Its usage is primarily in scientific or medical contexts.  Phrases would be dependent on the specific type of sac.  For example, "lacrimal sac" (泪囊), "air sac" (气囊).
