reflexly:/rɪˈflɛksli/ | adv. | 反射地，本能地 |  Acting or done without conscious thought; instinctively.  | 例句：He reflexly ducked as the ball came flying towards him. 他本能地躲开了飞向他的球。  | 例句：She reflexly grabbed her purse when she heard a noise.  听到声音，她本能地抓起了钱包。| 近义词：instinctively, automatically, involuntarily | 反义词：deliberately, consciously
