nondiplomacy: /ˌnɒnˈdɪpləməsi/ | n. | 非外交；不通过外交途径处理 | The deliberate avoidance of diplomatic channels in international relations.  It often involves unconventional or clandestine methods.  | 例句：The country's reliance on nondiplomacy in its foreign policy led to international tensions.  这个国家在其外交政策中对非外交手段的依赖导致了国际紧张局势。  | 例句： The use of nondiplomacy, such as cyberattacks, is increasingly common in international conflicts. 使用非外交手段，例如网络攻击，在国际冲突中越来越普遍。| 近义词：back-channel diplomacy, covert action,  under-the-table dealings | 反义词：diplomacy, negotiation, open dialogue |  名词
