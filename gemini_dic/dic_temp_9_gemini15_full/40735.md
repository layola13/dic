conveyance:/kənˈveɪəns/| n.|运输工具；运送；转让|The act of conveying something from one place to another;  a vehicle, especially for carrying passengers or goods; the legal transfer of property or right.  |例句：The conveyance of the goods was delayed by the storm. 货物的运输由于暴风雨而延误了。
例句：The old bus is a rather unreliable means of conveyance. 这辆旧公共汽车是一种相当不可靠的交通工具。|近义词：transport, transfer, vehicle, conveyance|反义词：retention, withholding


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company conveys goods to customers daily. = 公司每天向顾客运送货物。
This bus conveys passengers to the city center. = 这辆公共汽车把乘客送到市中心。

一般过去时:
They conveyed the message quickly and efficiently. = 他们迅速有效地传达了信息。
The documents were conveyed to the lawyer by special courier. = 文件由特快专递送交律师。

现在完成时:
He has conveyed his feelings to his partner. = 他已经向他的伴侣表达了他的感受。
The artist has conveyed a sense of movement in her painting. = 艺术家在她的画作中传达了一种动感。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, she had conveyed her concerns to the manager. = 会议之前，她已经向经理表达了她的担忧。
They had conveyed the news to all the employees before the official announcement. = 在正式宣布之前，他们已经将消息传达给了所有员工。

将来时:
The company will convey the packages using drones. = 公司将使用无人机运送包裹。
We will convey our gratitude in a letter. = 我们将在信中表达我们的感激之情。

虚拟语气:
If only I had conveyed my thoughts more clearly. = 要是我能更清楚地表达我的想法就好了。
I wish he had conveyed the information sooner. = 我希望他早点传达信息。

被动语态:
The message was conveyed through various channels. = 这条信息通过各种渠道传达。
The package is being conveyed to its destination. = 包裹正在被运送到目的地。

疑问句:
How will the goods be conveyed? = 货物将如何运输？
How can I best convey my appreciation? = 我该如何最好地表达我的感激之情？

否定句:
The driver couldn't convey the passengers safely due to the heavy rain. = 由于大雨，司机无法安全地运送乘客。
He failed to convey the seriousness of the situation. = 他未能传达情况的严重性。
