reapplying:/ˌriːəˈplaɪɪŋ/| v. |重新申请，再次申请|The act of applying again for something, such as a job, a loan, or a permit.  It implies a previous application that was unsuccessful or needs renewal.|例句：He is reapplying for the scholarship after being rejected last year. 他在去年被拒之后正在重新申请奖学金。


例句：She is reapplying for her driver's license after it expired. 她的驾照过期后，她正在重新申请。


近义词：reapplying for,renewing,resubmitting


反义词：withdrawing,canceling


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reapplies for the job every year. = 他每年都重新申请这份工作。
They are reapplying for the grant. = 他们正在重新申请拨款。


一般过去时:
She reapplied for the position last week. = 她上周重新申请了这个职位。
He reapplied for his passport after it was lost. = 他的护照丢了之后，他又重新申请了一本。


现在完成时:
I have reapplied for the visa three times. = 我已经三次重新申请签证了。
They have reapplied for funding since their initial proposal was rejected. = 自从他们的初始提案被拒绝以来，他们已经重新申请了资金。


过去完成时:
He had reapplied for the loan before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经重新申请了贷款。
She had reapplied for the job several times before she finally got it. = 她在她最终得到这份工作之前已经好几次重新申请过了。


将来时:
I will reapply for the scholarship next semester. = 下学期我将重新申请奖学金。
They will reapply for the permit if their first application is denied. = 如果他们的第一次申请被拒绝，他们将重新申请许可证。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reapply immediately. = 如果我是你，我会立即重新申请。
I wish I had reapplied sooner. = 我真希望我早点重新申请。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
