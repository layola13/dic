gimmer:/ˈɡɪmə(r)/| n.|母羊(尤指一岁或两岁的母羊)|A female sheep, especially one that is one or two years old.|The farmer carefully checked each gimmer in the flock.  这位农民仔细检查了羊群中的每一只母羊。| ewe, lamb| ram|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer shears the gimmers every spring. = 农民每年春天都剪羊毛。
This gimmer produces a lot of milk. = 这只母羊产奶量很大。

一般过去时:
The shepherd counted the gimmers yesterday. = 牧羊人昨天数了母羊。
