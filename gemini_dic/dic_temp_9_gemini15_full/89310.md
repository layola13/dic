homogamous:/həˈmɒɡəməs/ | adj. | 同配的；自花授粉的；同型交配的 |  Relating to or characterized by homogamy;  the ability of a plant or animal to self-fertilize or to mate with individuals of the same genotype.  This term is frequently used in biology, particularly in botany and zoology, to describe reproductive systems. | Example: Many plant species are homogamous, meaning they can self-pollinate.  许多植物物种是同配的，这意味着它们可以自花授粉。

Many insect species exhibit homogamous mating systems. 许多昆虫物种表现出同型交配系统。


近义词: self-fertilizing, autogamous | 反义词: heterogamous, cross-pollinating
