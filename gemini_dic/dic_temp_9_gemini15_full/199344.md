similiter:/ˈsɪmɪlɪtər/| n. |比喻；相似之处|A similiter is a figure of speech that uses comparison to illustrate a point, often using words like "like" or "as". It is a type of analogy that emphasizes the similarity between two things.|例句：The poet used a similiter to compare the moon to a silver coin.  诗人用比喻将月亮比作一枚银币。|近义词：analogy, comparison, metaphor, simile|反义词：dissimilarity, contrast
