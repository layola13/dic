faucets:/ˈfɔːsɪts/ | n. | 水龙头；龙头 | A faucet is a device for controlling the flow of liquid, typically water, from a pipe.  It usually consists of a valve and a spout. | Example:  Turn on the faucet to fill the sink. 打开水龙头注满水槽。 |近义词：tap, spigot | 反义词：None
