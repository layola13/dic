nonpursuance: /ˌnɒnpərˈsjuːəns/ | n. | 未追究；未执行；放弃 | The act or instance of not pursuing something; the failure or omission to pursue something.  This term often implies a deliberate choice not to follow through on something, rather than a simple inability. | Example: The nonpursuance of the legal action surprised everyone. 未采取法律行动令所有人惊讶。  The company's nonpursuance of new markets led to decreased sales. 公司未开拓新市场导致销售额下降。 | 近义词: neglect, omission, forbearance, abandonment, relinquishment | 反义词: pursuit, prosecution, enforcement
