misplace:/mɪsˈpleɪs/ | vt. | 放错地方；遗失 | To put something in the wrong place and forget where you put it.  This often implies a temporary loss, with the expectation that the item can be found again. | 例句：I've misplaced my keys; I can't find them anywhere. 我把钥匙放错地方了，到处都找不到。

I misplaced my glasses on the bus. 我把眼镜落在公交车上了。


近义词：mislay, lose (but lose often implies a more permanent loss) | 反义词：find, locate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often misplace my phone. = 我经常把手机放错地方。
He misplaces his tools frequently. = 他经常把工具放错地方。


一般过去时:
She misplaced her wallet yesterday. = 她昨天把钱包放错地方了。
They misplaced the important document. = 他们把重要的文件放错地方了。


现在完成时:
I have misplaced my passport several times. = 我已经好几次把护照放错地方了。
We have misplaced the contract; can you help us find it? = 我们把合同放错地方了，你能帮我们找到吗？


过去完成时:
She realized she had misplaced her keys before leaving the house. = 她出门前才意识到自己把钥匙放错地方了。
He remembered that he had misplaced his notes the night before.  = 他记得前一天晚上把笔记放错地方了。


将来时:
I think I will misplace my book again. = 我想我还会再次把书放错地方。
She worries that she will misplace her tickets for the concert.  = 她担心她会把演唱会的票放错地方。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misplaced my notes, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我没有把笔记放错地方，我就能通过考试了。
I wish I hadn't misplaced my camera; I missed some great shots. = 我真希望我没有把相机放错地方；我错过了很多很棒的照片。


被动语态:
The report was misplaced by someone in the office. = 报告被办公室的某人放错地方了。
The documents were unfortunately misplaced. = 文件不幸被放错地方了。


疑问句:
Have you ever misplaced something important? = 你曾经把重要的东西放错地方吗？
Did you misplace the letter I gave you? = 你把给你的信放错地方了吗？


否定句:
I didn't misplace my keys; I left them on the table. = 我没有把钥匙放错地方；我把它们放在桌上了。
He didn't misplace the files; they were stolen. = 他没有把文件放错地方；它们被偷了。


PHRASE->
misplace confidence in = 对...过分自信
misplace one's trust in = 对...给予错误的信任
misplace one's affection on = 把爱意错放在...
misplace an item = 把东西放错地方
misplace one's anger on = 把怒气发错对象


词源:
The word "misplace" is formed from the prefix "mis-" (meaning wrongly or badly) and the verb "place" (to put something in a particular position).  Therefore, it literally means "to place wrongly".
