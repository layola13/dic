seel:/siːl/| vt.|密封；盖章；封锁；确定|to close something tightly so that air, liquid, etc. cannot enter or leave; to close an opening with a lid, cover, etc.|例句：He sealed the envelope and put a stamp on it. 他封好了信封并贴上了邮票。|例句：The police sealed off the area after the explosion. 爆炸后警方封锁了该区域。|近义词：close, shut, fasten|反义词：open, unseal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seals the letters every day. = 他每天都封信。
She seals the jars with lids. = 她用盖子封住罐子。

一般过去时:
They sealed the documents yesterday. = 他们昨天封存了文件。
The company sealed the deal. = 公司达成了协议。


现在完成时:
He has sealed all the envelopes. = 他已经封好了所有信封。
She has sealed her fate. = 她已经决定了自己的命运。


过去完成时:
They had sealed the room before the inspection. = 他们检查前已经封好了房间。
The workers had sealed the cracks in the wall. = 工人们已经封好了墙上的裂缝。


将来时:
We will seal the package tomorrow. = 我们明天将封好包裹。
They will seal the agreement soon. = 他们很快就会签署协议。


虚拟语气:
If I sealed the bottle tightly, the liquid wouldn't spill. = 如果我把瓶子封紧，液体就不会洒出来。
I wish I had sealed the documents earlier. = 我真希望我早点把文件封存起来。


被动语态:
The documents were sealed by the court. = 文件由法院封存。
The area was sealed off by the police. = 该区域被警方封锁。


疑问句:
Did you seal the package? = 你封好包裹了吗？
Has she sealed the letter yet? = 她封好信了吗？


否定句:
He didn't seal the box properly. = 他没有把箱子封好。
She hasn't sealed the envelope. = 她还没封信封。
