clean:/kliːn/| adj., v.|干净的；清洁；打扫|Clean means free from dirt, marks, or impurities.  It can also refer to the act of making something free from dirt.|例句：The house is clean and tidy. 这房子干净整洁。  She cleaned the kitchen after dinner. 晚饭后她打扫了厨房。|近义词：pure, spotless, tidy, neat|反义词：dirty, filthy, soiled, messy|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cleans his room every day. = 他每天都打扫他的房间。
She cleans the dishes after each meal. = 她每顿饭后都洗碗碟。


一般过去时:
I cleaned the car yesterday. = 我昨天洗了车。
They cleaned the entire house last weekend. = 他们上周末打扫了整个房子。


现在完成时:
I have cleaned the bathroom. = 我已经打扫了浴室。
She has cleaned the windows twice this week. = 她本周已经擦过两次窗户了。


过去完成时:
I had cleaned the house before they arrived. = 他们到达之前我已经打扫了房子。
She had cleaned the kitchen before she started cooking. = 她开始做饭之前已经打扫了厨房。


将来时:
I will clean the garage tomorrow. = 我明天将打扫车库。
We will clean the apartment before the party. = 聚会前我们将打扫公寓。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would clean the attic. = 如果我有更多时间，我会打扫阁楼。
I wish I had cleaned the car earlier. = 我真希望我早点洗车。


被动语态:
The room was cleaned yesterday. = 房间昨天被打扫了。
The windows are being cleaned now. = 窗户现在正在被擦拭。


疑问句:
Is the house clean? = 房子干净吗？
Have you cleaned the dishes yet? = 你洗碗了吗？


否定句:
The room is not clean. = 房间不干净。
I didn't clean my room today. = 我今天没有打扫我的房间。


PHRASE->
clean up = 打扫；清理
clean out = 清理干净；彻底清除
clean shaven = 胡子刮得很干净的
make a clean breast of = 坦白交代
clean slate = 新局面；白手起家
