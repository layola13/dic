preoccupants:/ˌpriːˈɑːkjʊpənts/| n. | 全神贯注的人；心事重重的人；占据者 | People who are deeply engrossed in thought or preoccupied with something; those who occupy a place or space.  |例句：The preoccupants of the meeting were clearly focused on the agenda.  会议上全神贯注的人显然都集中在议程上。

例句：The preoccupants of the abandoned building were finally evicted. 废弃建筑物的占据者最终被驱逐了。


近义词：absorbers, engrossers, inhabitants, occupants


反义词：distracted people, unconcerned people
