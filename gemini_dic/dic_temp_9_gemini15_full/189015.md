sabbat:/ˈsæbæt/| n.|安息日；休假；|The seventh day of the week observed as a day of rest by Jews and some Christians.  It is also used to refer to a period of rest or vacation.|例句：The Jewish sabbath begins at sundown on Friday.  犹太人的安息日从星期五日落开始。

例句：She took a sabbatical to travel around the world. 她休了一个学术假去环游世界。|近义词：rest, holiday, vacation|反义词：workday, workday
