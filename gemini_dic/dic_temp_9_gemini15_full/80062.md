goalless:/ˈɡoʊlleɪs/ | adj. | 无进球的，没有目标的 | Without any goals being scored; lacking purpose or ambition. | 例句：The football match ended in a goalless draw.  足球比赛以0比0平局告终。

The project feels goalless; we need to define clearer objectives. 这个项目感觉毫无目标；我们需要定义更明确的目标。


近义词： scoreless, aimless, purposeless, directionless | 反义词：high-scoring, purposeful, ambitious | 词性：形容词
