phalansterianism:/ˌfælənˈstɪəriənɪzəm/| n. |空想社会主义；法朗士特制；集体公社制度 | A socialistic system advocating communal living and the abolition of private property, often characterized by large, self-sufficient communities.  It is named after Charles Fourier's proposed model community, the phalanstery. | The utopian community embraced phalansterianism, believing it would foster equality and efficiency.  这个乌托邦式的社区采纳了法朗士特制，相信它会促进平等和效率。 | communalism, utopian socialism | individualism, capitalism | 名词
