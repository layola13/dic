restoked:/riːˈstɒkt/ | v. | 重新储备；重新供应；重新激起；重新点燃 | To replenish or refill a supply of something; to rekindle or reignite something. |例句：The store was restocked with new merchandise. = 商店重新补充了新的商品。
The campfire was restocked with wood. = 营火重新添了柴火。 |近义词：replenish, refill, resupply, reignite |反义词：deplete, exhaust


USAGE->
