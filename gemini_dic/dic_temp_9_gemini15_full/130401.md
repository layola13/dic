non-visible:/ˌnɒnˈvɪzəbl/ | adj. |不可见的，无形的 | Not able to be seen.  | Example: The bacteria are non-visible to the naked eye.  细菌肉眼不可见。  The effects of climate change are sometimes non-visible in the short term. 气候变化的影响有时在短期内是不可见的。|近义词：invisible, unseen, imperceptible |反义词：visible, apparent, obvious

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The writing is non-visible under normal light. = 在正常光线下字迹不可见。
The details are non-visible without magnification. = 没有放大镜细节不可见。

一般过去时:
The damage was non-visible at first. = 起初损坏是不可见的。
The signature remained non-visible even after cleaning. = 清洁后签名仍然不可见。

现在完成时:
The effects have remained non-visible until now. = 到目前为止，影响仍然是不可见的。
The problem has been non-visible for many years. = 这个问题多年来一直未被察觉。

过去完成时:
The defect had been non-visible prior to the inspection. = 在检查之前，缺陷是不可见的。
The cracks had remained non-visible until the earthquake. = 直到地震，裂缝才变得可见。

将来时:
The results will be non-visible without further analysis. = 没有进一步的分析，结果将是不可见的。
The changes will remain non-visible for some time. = 这些变化在一段时间内将保持不可见。

虚拟语气:
If the object were non-visible, we wouldn't be able to detect it. = 如果物体不可见，我们就无法探测到它。
I wish the flaws were non-visible to the customer. = 我希望客户看不到这些缺陷。

被动语态:
The problem is considered non-visible by many experts. = 许多专家认为这个问题是不可见的。

疑问句:

否定句:
The damage is not non-visible; it's clearly apparent. = 损坏并非不可见；它非常明显。
