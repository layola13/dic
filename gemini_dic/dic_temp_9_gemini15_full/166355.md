preremove:/ˌpriːrɪˈmuːv/| v.|预先移除，事先去除|To remove something beforehand or in advance.|The technician preremoved the old hard drive before installing the new one. 技师在安装新硬盘之前预先拆除了旧硬盘。|近义词：pre-remove, remove beforehand,  anticipate removal|反义词：post-remove, add, install


USAGE->

They preremove the faulty parts before shipment. 他们在发货前预先移除有故障的部件。
We preremove obstacles to ensure smooth operation. 我们预先移除障碍以确保顺利运作。

She preremoved the weeds from her garden. 她预先从花园里除去了杂草。
