peculiarities:/pjʊˌkjulˈjærətiz/| n.|奇特性；怪癖；独特之处 |The peculiarities of his behavior puzzled everyone.  他的行为怪异，让每个人都感到困惑。|近义词：quirks, oddities, eccentricities |反义词：normalities, regularities


英文解析：Peculiarities refer to strange or unusual characteristics, habits, or features that are different from what is considered normal or typical.  They can be either positive or negative, depending on the context.

例句：

The old house had many peculiarities, such as creaky floors and crooked walls.  这座老房子有很多奇特之处，例如吱吱作响的地板和歪斜的墙壁。

His speech was filled with peculiarities of grammar and pronunciation. 他的演讲中充满了语法和发音上的怪异之处。


近义词：quirks, oddities, eccentricities, idiosyncrasies, singularities


反义词：normalities, regularities, commonalities, typicalities


词源：源自于中古英语peculiarite，意为“特殊财产”，来自拉丁语peculiaris，意思是“属于个人的，特殊的”，由peculium（个人财产）派生而来。
