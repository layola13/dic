jiffy:/ˈdʒɪfi/| n.|瞬间，片刻|A very short period of time.  It often implies a vaguely defined short duration.|例句：I'll be back in a jiffy. 我一会儿就回来。
例句：Just give me a jiffy to finish this.  给我一点时间完成这个。|近义词：moment, second, instant|反义词：eternity, age


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It takes a jiffy to heat up the milk. = 加热牛奶只需要一会儿。
A jiffy is a short period of time. = 一瞬间是一段很短的时间。


一般过去时:
The task was completed in a jiffy. = 任务很快就完成了。
He finished the puzzle in a jiffy. = 他很快完成了拼图。


现在完成时:

We have finished the work in a jiffy. = 我们已经很快完成了工作。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
