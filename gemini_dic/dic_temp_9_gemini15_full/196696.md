severing: /ˈsɛvərɪŋ/ | v. | 切断；断绝；分离 | To cut or separate something, especially forcibly or completely.  |例句：The storm severed the power lines.  暴风雨切断了电力线。

例句：The accident severed his relationship with his family.  事故使他与家人断绝了关系。


近义词：cutting, breaking, separating, disconnecting, dividing


反义词：joining, uniting, connecting, reuniting


词源:  From Old French *severer, from Latin severāre "to separate, sever, put apart," from severus "serious, strict."

He severs the rope with a sharp knife. = 他用一把锋利的刀切断了绳子。
The company severs ties with its problematic supplier. = 公司与有问题的供应商断绝了关系。


一般过去时:
