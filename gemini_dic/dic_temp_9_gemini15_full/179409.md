/ˌredˈtæpɪzəm/| n. |官僚主义；繁文缛节 |The excessive formality and routine required by bureaucratic organizations.  It often involves unnecessary paperwork, delays, and lack of efficiency.  Red tape can be frustrating and impede progress. |例句：The application process was bogged down in red tape.  申请流程被繁文缛节拖延了。|例句：Excessive red tape discouraged many small businesses from applying for the grant.  过多的繁文缛节劝退了许多小型企业申请拨款。|近义词：bureaucracy, officialism, formality |反义词：efficiency, streamlining, simplicity |名词
