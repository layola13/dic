campaign:/kæmˈpeɪn/| n. v.|战役；运动；竞选活动|A series of actions intended to achieve a particular result, especially a political or social one.  A military operation. |例句：The presidential campaign is heating up.总统竞选活动正在升温。

The charity launched a campaign to raise money for the victims of the earthquake.  慈善机构发起了一场运动，为地震灾民筹款。

近义词：movement, crusade, drive, election|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They campaign for better schools. = 他们在为更好的学校而奔走。
He campaigns against animal cruelty. = 他反对虐待动物的活动。


一般过去时:
She campaigned for mayor last year. = 她去年竞选市长。
They campaigned vigorously for social justice. = 他们为社会正义而积极奔走。


现在完成时:
We have campaigned for this cause for many years. = 我们为这个事业奔走多年了。
She has campaigned extensively on environmental issues. = 她对环境问题进行了广泛的宣传。


过去完成时:
They had campaigned tirelessly before the election. = 他们在大选前不知疲倦地奔走。
He had campaigned for years before finally winning the seat. = 他奋斗多年后终于赢得了席位。


将来时:
They will campaign for a new hospital. = 他们将为新建医院而奔走。
She will campaign fiercely against the new law. = 她将强烈反对这项新法律。


虚拟语气:
If he campaigned harder, he might win. = 如果他更努力竞选，他可能会赢。
I wish they had campaigned more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地开展竞选活动。


被动语态:
The issue was campaigned for by many groups. = 这个问题得到了许多团体的支持。
The candidate was widely campaigned against. = 候选人受到了广泛的反对。


疑问句:
Are they campaigning for re-election? = 他们在竞选连任吗？
What issues are they campaigning on? = 他们在为哪些问题奔走？


否定句:
He didn't campaign for that position. = 他没有竞选那个职位。
They are not campaigning for change. = 他们没有为变革而奔走。
