organizational:/ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənəl/ | adj. |组织的；组织上的| Relating to or involving an organization or its structure.  This adjective describes something connected to how a group or company is structured and functions. | The organizational chart clearly showed the reporting structure. 组织结构图清楚地显示了汇报关系。  The organizational culture is very important for employee morale. 组织文化对员工士气非常重要。| structural, systematic,  | disorganized, chaotic | 形容词
