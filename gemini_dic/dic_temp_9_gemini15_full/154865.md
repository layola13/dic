英文解析:

Perversities refers to instances of deviating from what is considered morally right, proper, or expected. It suggests a deliberate or inherent inclination towards wrongdoing, wickedness, or unnatural behavior.  The plural form emphasizes the multiplicity or recurrence of such actions or characteristics.
