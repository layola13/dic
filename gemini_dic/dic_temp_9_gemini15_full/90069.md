hosted:/ˈhoʊstɪd/| v. |主办；主持；寄存；提供 |To act as host for an event, website, or program.  To provide accommodation or a place to stay. |例句：The university hosted a conference on climate change.  大学主办了一场关于气候变化的会议。

The company hosted a website for its new product.  这家公司为其新产品建立了一个网站。|近义词：organized, accommodated, provided |反义词：attended, participated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They host a weekly podcast. = 他们每周主持一个播客。
The server hosts multiple websites. = 这台服务器托管多个网站。


一般过去时:
She hosted a dinner party last night. = 她昨晚举办了一场晚宴。
The hotel hosted many tourists during the peak season. = 在旅游旺季，这家酒店接待了许多游客。


现在完成时:
We have hosted several successful events this year. = 今年我们已经成功举办了几场活动。
They have hosted the website for over five years. = 他们已经托管了该网站五年多了。


过去完成时:
The company had hosted a similar event the previous year. = 这家公司在去年举办过类似的活动。
Before the meeting, they had hosted a welcome reception. = 会议之前，他们举办了欢迎酒会。


将来时:
We will host a fundraising gala next month. = 我们下个月将举办一个筹款晚会。
The platform will host millions of users in the future. = 未来这个平台将容纳数百万用户。


虚拟语气:
If they hosted a better event, more people would attend. = 如果他们举办更好的活动，会有更多人参加。
I wish we had hosted the party at a larger venue. = 我希望我们把派对办在一个更大的场地。


被动语态:
The conference was hosted by the university. = 这场会议由大学主办。
The website is hosted on a reliable server. = 该网站托管在可靠的服务器上。


疑问句:
Who hosted the party? = 谁主办的派对？
Where is the website hosted? = 网站托管在哪里？


否定句:
They didn't host the event this year. = 他们今年没有主办这个活动。
The server was not hosting the website properly. = 服务器没有正常托管网站。
