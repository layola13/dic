outstreaming: /ˌaʊtˈstriːmɪŋ/ | v. |涌出，流出；溢出 | The act of flowing or streaming out;  a continuous outward flow.  It often implies a large quantity or forceful movement. |例句：Lava outstreamed from the volcano.  熔岩从火山中涌出。

The river outstreamed into the ocean.  河流奔腾入海。|近义词：efflux, outflow, pouring out, gushing |反义词：instreaming, influx

The crowd outstreams from the stadium after the game.  比赛结束后，人群从体育场涌出。

疑问句:
