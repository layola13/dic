diacritic:/ˈdaɪəkrɪtɪk/| n. |附加符号，变音符号|A diacritic is a mark added to a letter to indicate a difference in pronunciation or meaning.  Diacritics are used in many languages to show different sounds or tones. For example, the acute accent (´) in café changes the pronunciation of the final "e".|The acute accent (´) in "café" is a diacritic that distinguishes it from "cafe".|近义词：accent mark, diacritical mark|反义词：无
