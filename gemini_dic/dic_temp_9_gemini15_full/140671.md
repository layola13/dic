omens:/ˈoʊmənz/ | n. | 预兆，征兆 | Signs or events that are believed to foretell the future, often of something bad.  Omens can be interpreted in various ways depending on cultural beliefs and context. |例句：The dark clouds were considered an omen of an approaching storm.  乌云被认为是暴风雨来临的预兆。

The raven's croak was seen as an ominous omen.  乌鸦的叫声被视为不祥的预兆。


近义词：portents, signs, forebodings, predictions | 反义词：good signs, assurances
