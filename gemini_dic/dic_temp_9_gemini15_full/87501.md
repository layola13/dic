heterochronic:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɒnɪk/| adj.|异时性的；不同时间的|Relating to or characterized by heterochrony, a developmental phenomenon where the timing of developmental events differs between organisms or lineages.  It describes changes in the timing or rate of developmental events, leading to changes in the size, shape, or other characteristics of an organism.  This can involve shifts in the onset, offset, or duration of developmental processes. |Example:  The accelerated development of reproductive organs in some species is an example of heterochronic change.  异时性变化的一个例子是一些物种中生殖器官的加速发育。|近义词：developmental timing alteration, ontogenetic shift|反义词：isochronic
