词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or too much. (过度)
* **prosper-**: from Latin *prosperare* "to succeed, fare well," from *prosperus* "successful, fortunate," from *prospere* "successfully, favorably." (繁荣)
* **-ousness**: suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality. (……的状态)

因此，overprosperousness字面意思就是“过度繁荣的状态”。
