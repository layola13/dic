madelaines:/ˌmædˈleɪnz/| n.|马德琳蛋糕|Small, shell-shaped sponge cakes, typically made with butter, eggs, and flour, often featuring a slightly crisp exterior and a soft, tender interior.  They are named after the French writer, Marcel Proust's character, Madeleine. |例句：The café served delicious homemade madelaines with afternoon tea. 这家咖啡馆供应美味的自制马德琳蛋糕和下午茶。

例句：She baked a batch of madelaines for her friend's birthday. 她为朋友的生日烤了一批马德琳蛋糕。

近义词：petite fours (a broader category of small cakes), sponge cakes


反义词：无


词性:名词
