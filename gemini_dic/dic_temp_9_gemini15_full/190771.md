saviors:/ˈseɪvjərz/| n.|救星；救世主|People or things that save someone or something from a dangerous or difficult situation.  |例句：The firefighters were true saviors, rescuing the family from the burning building. 消防员是真正的救星，他们把这个家庭从燃烧的建筑物中救了出来。

例句：She considered her doctor her savior after a long illness.  在她久病之后，她认为她的医生是她的救星。


近义词：rescuers, deliverers, liberators|反义词：destroyers, tormentors, oppressors|词性:名词
