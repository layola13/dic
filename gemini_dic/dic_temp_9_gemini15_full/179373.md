redressers: /rɪˈdrɛsərz/ | n. | 纠正者；赔偿者；修补者 | People or things that redress something, typically a wrong or grievance.  They may be individuals, organizations, or even processes that aim to correct an injustice, repair damage, or provide compensation. |例句：The redressers of grievances were finally able to resolve the long-standing dispute between the two families.  纠正冤屈的人最终能够解决这两个家庭之间长期存在的纠纷。

The company hired a team of redressers to fix the damage caused by the faulty product. 公司雇佣了一组赔偿者来修复有缺陷产品造成的损坏。

|近义词：rectifiers, compensators, repairers, amenders |反义词：wrongdoers, perpetrators, offenders |名词
