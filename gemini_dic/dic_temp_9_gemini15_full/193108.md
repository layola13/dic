secularises:/ˈsɛkjʊləraɪz/ | vt. | 使世俗化；使非宗教化 | To remove (something) from religious influence or control; to make worldly or temporal.  | The government secularised the education system, removing religious instruction from schools.  政府使教育系统世俗化，取消了学校的宗教教育。|  desecrate, profane |  sacralize, sanctify | 动词
