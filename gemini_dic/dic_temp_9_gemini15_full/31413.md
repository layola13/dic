chargrilling:/tʃɑːrˈɡrɪlɪŋ/| v.|炭火烧烤|The process of cooking food over hot coals, often resulting in a charred or slightly burnt surface.  This cooking method imparts a smoky flavor.|例句：We're chargrilling some steaks for dinner tonight. 今晚我们用炭火烤牛排吃。|例句：The restaurant is known for its expertly chargrilled seafood. 这家餐馆以其精湛的炭烤海鲜而闻名。|近义词：grilling, barbecuing|反义词：boiling, steaming
