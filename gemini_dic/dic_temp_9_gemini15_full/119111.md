metronome:/ˈmɛtrənəʊm/| n. |节拍器|A device used to mark time in music, typically by producing a regular ticking sound at a pre-set speed.|例句：The musician used a metronome to keep a steady tempo.  这位音乐家使用节拍器来保持稳定的节奏。|The composer set the metronome to 120 beats per minute. 作曲家将节拍器设定为每分钟120拍。|近义词：timer, beat keeper|反义词:None
