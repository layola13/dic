picture:/ˈpɪktʃər/| n. |图片，照片，景象 |A picture is worth a thousand words. 一张图片胜过千言万语。|image, photograph, illustration|abstract, unclear.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I see a picture of a cat. = 我看到一张猫的图片。
The picture shows a beautiful landscape. = 这张图片展现了一幅美丽的风景。

一般过去时:
She drew a picture of her family. = 她画了一幅她家人的画像。
He took a picture of the sunset. = 他拍了一张日落的照片。

现在完成时:
I have taken many pictures of this place. = 我已经拍了很多这个地方的照片。
They have painted a picture of their dream house. = 他们已经画了一幅他们梦想之家的画。

过去完成时:
She had already seen the picture before I showed it to her. =在我给她看之前，她已经看过那张照片了。
They had finished the picture before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了这幅画。

将来时:
I will take a picture of you tomorrow. = 我明天会给你拍照。
We will paint a picture of the city at night. = 我们将画一幅夜晚城市的画。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better camera, I would take a better picture. = 如果我有一台更好的相机，我会拍出更好的照片。
I wish I had taken a picture of that bird. = 我真希望我拍下了那只鸟的照片。

被动语态:
The picture was taken by a professional photographer. = 这张照片是由一位专业摄影师拍摄的。
The painting was completed last year. = 这幅画是去年完成的。

疑问句:
What is in the picture? = 图片里有什么？
Do you like this picture? = 你喜欢这张照片吗？

否定句:
I don't have a picture of him. = 我没有他的照片。
The picture doesn't show much detail. = 这张照片没有显示很多细节。


PHRASE->
picture perfect = 十全十美
mental picture = 脑海中的画面
word picture = 生动的描写
a picture of health = 非常健康
in the picture = 参与其中
