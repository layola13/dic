epizootic:/ˌɛpɪzuːˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 动物传染病的；动物瘟疫的 | Relating to or denoting a disease that is prevalent among animals in a particular region or during a particular period.  | 例句：The epizootic outbreak of avian influenza caused significant losses to poultry farmers.  动物流感疫情给家禽养殖户造成了重大损失。  | 近义词：pandemic (in animals), widespread, prevalent | 反义词：endemic (in animals), sporadic
