chart-topper:/ˈtʃɑːrtˌtɒpər/|/ˈtʃɑːrtˌtɑːpər/| n. |排行榜冠军；最受欢迎的人或事物 |A chart-topper is a song, album, or other item that reaches the top of a music chart or other ranking system.  It indicates immense popularity and success. |Example:  Her latest single is a chart-topper, already at number one for three weeks.  她的最新单曲是排行榜冠军，已经连续三周排名第一。

Example: The band's new album is a chart-topper, selling millions of copies worldwide.  该乐队的最新专辑是排行榜冠军，全球销量达数百万张。|近义词：bestseller, hit, top seller |反义词：flop, failure
