hamletize:/ˈhæmlɪtaɪz/| vt.|使犹豫不决；使优柔寡断；使悲观；|To behave in an indecisive or hesitant manner, like Hamlet; to overthink and delay action.|The professor hamletized over which research project to pursue next. 教授在犹豫不决接下来要进行哪个研究项目。|近义词：waver, vacillate, procrastinate|反义词：decide, act decisively
