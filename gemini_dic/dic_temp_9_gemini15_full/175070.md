radiophone:/ˈreɪdioʊfoʊn/| n. |无线电话；无线电电话|A radiophone is a device that allows communication over long distances using radio waves. It is essentially a combination of a radio transmitter and receiver.  Early radiophones were often bulky and required significant power. Modern radiophones, such as cell phones, are much smaller and more portable.| Example:  The invention of the radiophone revolutionized communication.  无线电话的发明彻底改变了通讯方式。 |近义词：wireless telephone, mobile phone|反义词：wired telephone, landline
