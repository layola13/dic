impressionistic:/ˌɪmˈprɛʃənɪstɪk/| adj. |印象派的；印象主义的；印象式的|Characterized by a style of painting that emphasizes the subjective impression of the moment and the use of light and color to create an effect rather than to represent objects accurately.  It suggests a feeling or atmosphere rather than precise detail.  |例句：The painting has an impressionistic quality, capturing the fleeting light of the sunset. 这幅画具有印象派风格，捕捉到了夕阳转瞬即逝的光线。

例句：His description of the scene was highly impressionistic, focusing on the overall mood rather than specific details. 他对场景的描述非常印象派，侧重于整体气氛而不是具体的细节。


近义词：suggestive, evocative, atmospheric, suggestive, evocative


反义词：realistic, representational, detailed, precise


词性:形容词
