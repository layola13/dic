outmuscling: /ˌaʊtˈmʌs(ə)lɪŋ/ | v. |胜过；以实力压倒|  To defeat or surpass (someone or something) by being stronger or more powerful.  |例句：The startup outmuscled its competitors by securing key partnerships. 这家初创公司通过获得关键合作伙伴关系而击败了竞争对手。


例句：The larger company easily outmuscled the smaller one in the merger negotiations.  更大的公司在并购谈判中轻松地击败了较小的公司。


近义词：outpower, overpower, overwhelm, overshadow


反义词：be outmatched, be overpowered, be defeated


词性：动词
