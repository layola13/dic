nemophilist:/ˈnɛməˌfɪlɪst/| n. |恋花癖者，爱花者| A person who loves flowers, especially wood anemones.  This term is relatively rare and often used in a playful or literary context. It doesn't carry the strong negative connotations that some other "-philia" words might. | He is a true nemophilist, spending hours tending his garden of wildflowers. 他是一个真正的恋花癖者，花数小时照料他的野花花园。 |  flower lover,  floraphile |  (无明确反义词，可考虑： apathetic to flowers)  | 名词
