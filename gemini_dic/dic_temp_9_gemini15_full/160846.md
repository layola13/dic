possibility:/ˌpɒsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌpɑːsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可能性；可能的事|The possibility of success is high.成功的可能性很高。There is a possibility that it might rain.可能下雨。|近义词：chance, probability, likelihood |反义词：impossibility, certainty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The possibility of rain exists. = 下雨的可能性存在。
What are the possibilities for tonight? = 今晚有什么可能性？


一般过去时:
The possibility of a strike existed last week. = 上周存在罢工的可能性。
What were the possibilities he considered? = 他考虑过哪些可能性？


现在完成时:
Many possibilities have emerged since the meeting. = 会议以来出现了许多可能性。
What possibilities have you explored so far? = 到目前为止你探索了哪些可能性？


过去完成时:
The possibility of escape had vanished. = 逃跑的可能性已经消失了。
What possibilities had they considered before making the decision? = 他们在做决定之前考虑过哪些可能性？


将来时:
There will be many possibilities open to you after graduation. = 毕业后你将会有许多可能性。
What possibilities will we face next year? = 明年我们将面临哪些可能性？


虚拟语气:
If there were a possibility, I would take it. = 如果有可能性，我会抓住它。
I wish there was a possibility of reconciliation. = 我希望有和解的可能性。


被动语态:
The possibility of failure was discussed. = 失败的可能性被讨论了。
All possibilities are being explored. = 所有可能性都在被探索。


疑问句:
Is there any possibility of a refund? = 有任何退款的可能性吗？
What are the possibilities? = 可能性有哪些？


否定句:
There is no possibility of that happening. = 那件事没有发生的可能性。
He denied the possibility of any wrongdoing. = 他否认有任何不当行为的可能性。


PHRASE->
a strong possibility = 很大的可能性
a remote possibility = 很小的可能性
the possibility of... = ...的可能性
within the realm of possibility = 在可能的范围内
beyond the possibility of doubt = 毫无疑问


词源:
possibility源自于拉丁语"possibilitas",  由"possibilis" (possible的词根，意为"可能的")演变而来。  "-itas" 是拉丁语后缀，表示"性质"或"状态"。
