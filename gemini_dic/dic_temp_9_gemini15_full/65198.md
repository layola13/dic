ethylenes:/ˈɛθɪliːnz/| n.|乙烯（复数）|Ethylenes are unsaturated hydrocarbons with the formula C₂H₄. They are the simplest alkenes and are widely used in the chemical industry.  |例句：The production of ethylenes is a crucial process in the petrochemical industry. 乙烯的生产是石化工业中的一个关键过程。  |例句：Various polymers are synthesized from ethylenes, contributing to the production of plastics. 各种聚合物都是由乙烯合成的，这有助于塑料的生产。|近义词：ethene (单数)|反义词：无|名词
