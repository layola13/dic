overinclined: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnd/ | adj. | 倾向过度的；过于偏向的 |  Having an excessive inclination or tendency; excessively disposed or prone to something.  | Example: He was overinclined to believe gossip, often leading him into trouble. 他过于轻信流言蜚语，经常因此惹上麻烦。 | 近义词: predisposed, biased, partial, prone | 反义词: impartial, unbiased, objective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overinclined to criticize others. = 他倾向于过度批评别人。
She is overinclined to worry about things. = 她倾向于过度担心事情。


一般过去时:
He was overinclined to trust strangers. = 他过去容易过度相信陌生人。
She was overinclined to spend money. = 她过去喜欢过度花钱。


现在完成时:
He has been overinclined to procrastinate on his work. = 他一直以来都过度拖延工作。
She has been overinclined to indulge in sweets. = 她一直以来都过度沉溺于甜食。


过去完成时:
He had been overinclined to drink before the accident. = 事故发生前，他一直过度饮酒。
She had been overinclined to rely on her parents. = 她之前一直过度依赖她的父母。


将来时:
He will be overinclined to argue if you disagree with him. = 如果你不同意他，他会倾向于争论。
She will be overinclined to take risks in her new job. = 在她的新工作中，她会倾向于冒险。


虚拟语气:
If he were not overinclined to anger, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么容易生气，他就会有更多朋友。
If she were not overinclined to pessimism, she would be happier. = 如果她不那么悲观，她会更快乐。


被动语态:
His judgment was overinclined toward leniency. = 他的判断过于偏向宽大处理。
The decision was overinclined in favor of the defendant. = 该决定过于偏向被告。


疑问句:
Is he overinclined to take shortcuts? = 他是否倾向于走捷径？
Is she overinclined to trust promises easily? = 她是否容易轻信承诺？


否定句:
He is not overinclined to complain. = 他并不倾向于抱怨。
She is not overinclined to follow the rules blindly. = 她并不倾向于盲目遵守规则。
