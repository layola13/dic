invertebrates:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrəts/ | n. | 无脊椎动物 | Animals lacking a backbone or spinal column. They represent the vast majority of animal species, encompassing a wide range of phyla, including insects, crustaceans, mollusks, arachnids, and many others.  | Example:  Insects, spiders, and worms are all examples of invertebrates.  无脊椎动物的例子包括昆虫、蜘蛛和蠕虫。

|  Many invertebrates play crucial roles in their ecosystems, such as pollination and decomposition.许多无脊椎动物在其生态系统中扮演着至关重要的角色，例如授粉和分解。 | 近义词：invertebrata (less common) | 反义词：vertebrates | 名词
