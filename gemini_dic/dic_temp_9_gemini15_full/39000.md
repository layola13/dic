confirmer:/kənˈfɜːrmər/| n. |确认者；证实者|A confirmer is a person or thing that confirms something.  It can also refer to a program or system that confirms actions or data.|Example: The lab results served as the confirmer of his diagnosis.  实验室结果证实了他的诊断。|近义词：verifier, validator, authenticator|反义词：denier, refuter|名词
