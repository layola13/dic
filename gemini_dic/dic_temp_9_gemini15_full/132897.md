nonelectrification:/ˌnɒnɪˌlɛktrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |非电气化 |The lack or absence of electrification; the state of not being electrified.  It refers to a situation where electricity is not used or available, or a process of removing or reversing electrification. | Example: The remote village suffered from nonelectrification, making life difficult for its inhabitants.  偏远村庄缺乏电力供应，使村民生活艰难。|近义词：de-electrification, lack of electricity | 反义词：electrification
