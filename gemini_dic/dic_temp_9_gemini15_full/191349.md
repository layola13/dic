scepticism:/ˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/| n. |怀疑主义；怀疑态度|The questioning of the validity of claims or beliefs.|例句：His scepticism about the new project was well-founded.  他的怀疑新项目的理由充分。|例句：The report was met with considerable scepticism. 这份报告受到了相当大的质疑。|近义词：doubt, mistrust, suspicion, incredulity|反义词：belief, faith, credulity|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows scepticism towards new ideas. = 他对新想法持怀疑态度。
Her scepticism is evident in her questioning. = 她质疑的态度很明显。


一般过去时:
His scepticism prevented him from investing. = 他的怀疑阻止了他投资。
Their scepticism about the claims was understandable. = 他们对这些说法表示怀疑是可以理解的。


现在完成时:
My scepticism has grown after seeing the evidence. = 在看到证据后，我的怀疑越来越大了。
Their scepticism has been justified by recent events. = 最近的事件证明了他们的怀疑是正确的。


过去完成时:
His scepticism had already been established before the presentation. = 在演示之前，他已经抱有怀疑态度。
Their scepticism had grown over time due to repeated failures. = 由于屡屡失败，他们的怀疑越来越大。


将来时:
His scepticism will likely persist until more evidence is presented. = 在提供更多证据之前，他的怀疑很可能会持续下去。
Their scepticism will be tested by the upcoming results. = 即将公布的结果将检验他们的怀疑。


虚拟语气:
If he had less scepticism, he might have invested. = 如果他不那么怀疑，他可能会投资。
I wish I didn't have so much scepticism about their motives. = 我希望我不那么怀疑他们的动机。


被动语态:
The proposal was met with widespread scepticism. = 这个提案遭到了广泛的怀疑。
His ideas were dismissed due to scepticism from the board. = 由于董事会的怀疑，他的想法被否决了。


疑问句:
Is there any basis for your scepticism? = 你的怀疑有什么依据吗？
What is the source of your scepticism? = 你怀疑的根源是什么？


否定句:
He didn't show any scepticism towards the plan. = 他对这个计划没有任何怀疑。
She accepted the explanation without scepticism. = 她毫不怀疑地接受了这个解释。
