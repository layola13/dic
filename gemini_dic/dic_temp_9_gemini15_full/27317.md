calypsoing:/kæˈlɪpsoʊɪŋ/| v. |卡里普索舞曲演奏；演唱卡里普索歌曲 |The act of performing or singing calypso music.  Calypso is a style of music and dance from Trinidad and Tobago, characterized by its lively rhythms and often satirical lyrics. Calypsoing can refer to both the musical performance and the act of singing calypso songs.|例句：They were calypsoing on the beach, their voices blending with the sound of the waves.他们在海滩上演奏卡里普索音乐，他们的歌声与海浪声融为一体。  He spent the evening calypsoing with his friends. 他和朋友们一起度过了一个夜晚，演奏卡里普索音乐。|近义词：singing, performing, playing (calypso music)|反义词：N/A
