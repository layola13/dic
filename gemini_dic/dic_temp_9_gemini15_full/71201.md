fistulate:/ˈfɪstjuleɪt/| v.|形成廔管；发生廔管|To form or create a fistula (an abnormal connection between two organs or vessels).  The term often refers to the development of an abnormal passage, typically due to infection or injury.|例句：The infection fistulated through the tissue, creating an opening to the skin. 感染穿透组织，在皮肤上形成一个开口。The surgeon successfully closed the fistulating wound.  外科医生成功地缝合了这个廔管伤口。|近义词：canalize, perforate|反义词：heal, close
