capstans:/ˈkæpstənz/| n.|卷扬机，绞盘|A capstan is a vertical rotating drum used for hoisting or hauling heavy loads by winding a rope or cable around it.  It's often found on ships and in construction.|例句：The sailors used the capstan to raise the anchor. 水手们用绞盘起锚。

例句：The construction crew employed a capstan to lift the heavy beams. 建筑队用绞盘吊起沉重的梁木。|近义词：winch, windlass|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying ease of lifting could be considered antonyms in context.
