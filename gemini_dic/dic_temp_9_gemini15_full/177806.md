receives:/rɪˈsiːvz/ | vt. |接收，收到，获得 | To take something that is given or offered to you.  |例句：He receives many letters every day. 他每天收到很多信。 |例句：The company receives a lot of applications for the job. 这家公司收到了很多求职申请。|近义词：gets, obtains, accepts |反义词：sends, gives, transmits |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She receives many emails each day. = 她每天收到很多邮件。
He receives a salary every month. = 他每个月领取薪水。

一般过去时:
I received a package yesterday. = 我昨天收到一个包裹。
They received good news about their application. = 他们收到了关于他们申请的好消息。

现在完成时:
She has received numerous awards for her work. = 她因她的工作获得了无数奖项。
He has received several calls about the issue. = 他已经接到几个关于这个问题的电话。

过去完成时:
I had received the letter before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经收到了信。
They had received the information before making the decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经收到了信息。

将来时:
She will receive her bonus next week. = 她下周将收到她的奖金。
He will receive an answer by tomorrow. = 他明天将收到答复。

被动语态:
The package was received yesterday. = 包裹昨天收到了。
The award was received with great appreciation. = 这个奖项受到了高度赞扬。

疑问句:
Does she receive many calls? = 她收到很多电话吗？
Did he receive your email? = 他收到你的邮件了吗？


否定句:
He does not receive enough support. = 他没有得到足够的支持。
She did not receive an invitation to the party. = 她没有收到参加聚会的邀请。
