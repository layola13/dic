reprogrammable: /ˌriːˈprəʊɡræməbl/ | adj. | 可重新编程的 | Capable of being reprogrammed; able to have its program or instructions altered or replaced.  This term often refers to hardware or software that can be modified after its initial creation. |


例句：

This reprogrammable microcontroller allows for flexible control of the robotic arm.  这个可重新编程的微控制器允许灵活控制机械臂。

The company's new software is fully reprogrammable, offering users extensive customization options.  这家公司的新软件完全可重新编程，为用户提供了广泛的定制选项。


近义词：reconfigurable, modifiable, adaptable


反义词：fixed, immutable, unchangeable


词性: adjective
