redraft's:/ˌriːˈdræft/| n. |重新起草；修改稿|A revised version of something written.|The editor asked for a redraft of the article.  编辑要求重新起草这篇文章。| revision, rewrite, amendment | original draft |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The writer redrafts the essay. = 作家正在修改论文。
He redrafts his speech every night. = 他每天晚上都修改他的演讲稿。

一般过去时:
She redrafted the contract last night. = 她昨晚修改了合同。
They redrafted the proposal after the meeting. = 会议后他们修改了提案。

现在完成时:
I have redrafted the report three times. = 我已经修改了报告三次。
She has redrafted her application several times. = 她已经修改了她的申请好几次。

过去完成时:
He had redrafted the letter before sending it. = 他在寄出信之前已经修改了信。
They had redrafted the plan before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经修改了计划。

将来时:
I will redraft the document tomorrow. = 我明天将修改这份文件。
We will redraft the agreement next week. = 我们下周将修改协议。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
