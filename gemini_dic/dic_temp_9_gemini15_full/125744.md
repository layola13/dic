muppet:/ˈmʌpɪt/| n. |木偶；笨蛋；傀儡|A muppet is a type of puppet, often made of foam rubber, with large, expressive features.  The term is also used informally to refer to a foolish or incompetent person. |例句：The children were delighted by the muppets in the show.孩子们对演出中的木偶很高兴。

例句：He's such a muppet; he spilled coffee all over the new carpet. 他真是个笨蛋，把咖啡洒得到处都是。|近义词：puppet, dummy, fool, idiot |反义词：genius, mastermind, clever person
