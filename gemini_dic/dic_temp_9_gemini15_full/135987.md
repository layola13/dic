nonrailroads:/ˌnɑːnˈreɪlroʊdz/ | n. |非铁路运输 |  Refers to all forms of transportation that are not railroads, including roads, airways, waterways, and pipelines. This term is often used in the context of transportation planning, logistics, and infrastructure development.  | Example: The government is investing heavily in nonrailroad infrastructure to improve overall transportation efficiency.  政府正在大力投资非铁路基础设施以提高整体交通效率。 | 近义词：alternative transportation, other modes of transport | 反义词：railroads, rail transport
