hegelian:/hɛˈɡeɪliən/| adj.|黑格尔的；黑格尔主义的；|Relating to the philosophy of Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel, characterized by its emphasis on dialectical reasoning, historical progress, and the idea of Geist (spirit or mind) as the driving force of history.|例句：Hegel's ideas had a profound influence on subsequent thinkers, giving rise to Hegelian interpretations of history and society. 黑格尔的思想深刻地影响了后来的思想家，导致了对历史和社会的黑格尔式解释。  His analysis of the Hegelian dialectic was insightful and illuminating. 他对黑格尔辩证法的分析富有洞察力且发人深省。|近义词：idealistic, dialectical|反义词：materialistic, empirical|
