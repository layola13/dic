migrants:/ˈmaɪɡrənts/| n. |移民，移居者 |People who move from one place to another, especially to a new country, for a new life or to find work.  |Example: Millions of migrants crossed the border last year. 数百万移民去年越过边境。 |近义词：immigrants, settlers, expatriates |反义词：natives, locals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Migrants often face many challenges in their new homes. = 移民在他们的新家常常面临许多挑战。
Many migrants work hard to build a better life. = 许多移民努力工作以创造更好的生活。


一般过去时:
The migrants arrived in the city last week. = 移民上周到达这座城市。
Many migrants left their home countries due to war. = 许多移民因为战争而离开他们的祖国。


现在完成时:
The government has provided support to many migrants. = 政府已向许多移民提供了支持。
Millions of migrants have sought refuge in other countries. = 数百万移民在其他国家寻求庇护。


过去完成时:
The migrants had already settled in before the winter arrived. = 移民在冬天到来之前已经定居下来了。
Before the journey, the migrants had prepared for many months. = 在旅程之前，移民们已经准备了几个月。


将来时:
More migrants will likely arrive next year. = 明年很可能会来更多移民。
The government will provide assistance to arriving migrants. = 政府将为到达的移民提供援助。


虚拟语气:
If I were a migrant, I would try to learn the local language. = 如果我是个移民，我会尝试学习当地的语言。
I wish the migrants had more opportunities. = 我希望移民有更多机会。


被动语态:
Migrants are often exploited by unscrupulous employers. = 移民经常被不择手段的雇主剥削。
Support services are being provided to the new migrants. = 正在为新移民提供支持服务。


疑问句:
How many migrants are there in this country? = 这个国家有多少移民？
Where do the migrants usually settle? = 移民通常定居在哪里？


否定句:
The migrants did not receive the help they needed. = 移民没有得到他们需要的帮助。
He wasn't a migrant; he was born here. = 他不是移民；他在这里出生。
