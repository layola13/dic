requalifying:/ˌriːˈkwɑːləfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 重新取得资格；重新认证 | The process of regaining or re-establishing qualifications or certifications. This often involves meeting updated requirements or demonstrating continued competency.  | Example: He is requalifying for his pilot's license after a long break. 他在长时间中断后正在重新取得飞行执照的资格。  | 近义词：recertifying, re-qualifying | 反义词：disqualifying
