revelation: /ˌrevəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |启示；揭露；展现 | The sudden revelation of the truth shocked everyone.  真相的突然揭露震惊了所有人。|近义词：disclosure, unveiling, exposure |反义词：concealment, secrecy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist's research reveals a groundbreaking discovery. = 科学家的研究揭示了一个突破性的发现。
This book reveals the hidden history of the city. = 这本书揭示了这座城市的隐藏历史。


一般过去时:
The journalist's article revealed a major scandal. = 记者的文章揭露了一场重大丑闻。
The meeting revealed a deep division within the team. = 会议揭示了团队内部的严重分歧。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have revealed a link between diet and disease. = 最近的研究揭示了饮食与疾病之间的联系。
The investigation has revealed new evidence in the case. = 调查已经揭示了此案中的新证据。


过去完成时:
After years of investigation, the police had finally revealed the truth about the crime. = 多年调查后，警方终于揭露了这起犯罪的真相。
The detective had revealed the killer's identity at the end of the movie. = 侦探在电影结尾揭露了杀手的身份。


将来时:
The upcoming report will reveal the company's financial problems. = 即将来临的报告将揭示公司的财务问题。
The next chapter will reveal the hero's secret identity. = 下一章将揭示英雄的真实身份。


虚拟语气:
If he had revealed the truth earlier, things might have been different. = 如果他早点说出真相，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish she had revealed her feelings to me sooner. = 我希望她早点向我吐露她的感情。


被动语态:
The truth was revealed during the trial. = 真相在审判中被揭露。
The secret was revealed by an anonymous source. = 秘密被一个匿名消息来源透露。


疑问句:
What did the investigation reveal? = 调查揭示了什么？
What will the upcoming election reveal about the public's mood? = 即将来临的选举将揭示公众情绪的什么？


否定句:
The report did not reveal any new information. = 报告没有透露任何新信息。
He refused to reveal his sources. = 他拒绝透露他的消息来源。


PHRASE->
