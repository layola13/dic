electress:/ɪˈlɛktrɪs/ | n. |女选帝侯；女统治者 | A woman who holds the title or position of an elector, especially in the Holy Roman Empire.  The term often refers to a female ruler of a principality or electorate. | Example:  The Electress of Saxony played a significant role in the political affairs of her time.  萨克森女选帝侯在其时代发挥了重要的政治作用。  |近义词: princess, sovereign, ruler |反义词: subject, commoner
