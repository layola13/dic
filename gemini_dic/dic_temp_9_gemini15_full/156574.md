picking:/ˈpɪkɪŋ/| n. v.|采摘；挑选；扒窃|The act of selecting or gathering something, especially fruit or flowers;  the act of stealing something.|例句：The picking of apples is a favorite autumn activity. 秋天采摘苹果是一项最受欢迎的活动。

例句：He was arrested for picking pockets. 他因扒窃被捕。|近义词：selecting, choosing, gathering, pilfering|反义词：planting, sowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is picking flowers in the garden. = 她在花园里采摘鲜花。
He picks up his children from school every day. = 他每天都去学校接孩子。

一般过去时:
They picked berries in the forest yesterday. = 他们昨天在森林里采摘浆果。
I picked a beautiful dress for the party. = 我为派对挑选了一件漂亮的裙子。

现在完成时:
He has picked all the ripe tomatoes. = 他已经摘完了所有成熟的西红柿。
She has been picking at her food all evening. = 她整个晚上都在挑食。

过去完成时:
They had picked the fruit before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经摘完了水果。
We had picked our seats before the concert began. = 音乐会开始之前，我们已经选好了座位。

将来时:
I will be picking you up at 7 o'clock. = 我七点钟来接你。
They will pick the winners tomorrow. = 他们明天将选出优胜者。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pick a different path. = 如果我有更多时间，我会选择不同的道路。
I wish I had picked a better hotel. = 我真希望我选了一家更好的酒店。

被动语态:
The apples are picked by hand. = 苹果是手工采摘的。
The winner will be picked at random. = 优胜者将被随机抽取。

疑问句:
Are you picking up the kids today? = 你今天去接孩子吗？
What are you picking on me for? = 你为什么老是针对我？

否定句:
He didn't pick up the phone. = 他没有接电话。
I didn't pick that color. = 我没有选择那种颜色。


PHRASE->
pick up: 捡起，搭载，学会
pick out: 挑选，辨认出
pick on: 欺负，挑剔
pick at: 挑剔，少量地吃
pick over: 仔细检查，挑拣
