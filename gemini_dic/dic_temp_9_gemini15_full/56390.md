dole-out:/doʊlˈaʊt/ | vt. | 分配；施舍；少量地给予 | To distribute something, especially in small amounts or reluctantly.  |例句：The government doled out emergency aid to the flood victims. 政府向水灾受害者分发了紧急援助。
The teacher doled out the candy one by one to the children.老师一个一个地把糖果分给孩子们。|近义词：distribute, allot, dispense, hand out |反义词：hoard, keep, withhold
