introverting: /ˌɪntrəˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 内向化；使内向 |  The process of becoming more introverted; exhibiting or characterized by introversion.  It describes the action of someone becoming more withdrawn and focused on their internal world.  | 例句：He's been introverting lately, spending most of his time alone. 他最近变得很内向，大部分时间都独自一人。  She noted a gradual introverting of her personality after the trauma. 她注意到自己在创伤后性格逐渐内向。 | 近义词： withdrawing, turning inward | 反义词：extroverting, outgoing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is introverting. = 他正在变得内向。
She introverts after a stressful day. = 压力大的一天后，她变得内向。


一般过去时:
He introverted after the argument. = 争吵后，他变得内向。
She introverted significantly following the loss of her job. = 在失去工作后，她变得非常内向。


现在完成时:
He has introverted since the move. = 自从搬家以来，他变得内向。
She has been introverting more frequently recently. = 最近她越来越频繁地变得内向。


过去完成时:
He had introverted before I even met him. = 我认识他之前，他就已经很内向了。
She had introverted for months before seeking help. = 在寻求帮助之前，她已经内向了好几个月。


将来时:
He will introvert further if he continues to isolate himself. = 如果他继续自我隔离，他会变得更内向。
She will likely introvert after the intense period of work. = 在这段紧张的工作之后，她可能会变得内向。


虚拟语气:
If he were to introvert any further, it would be concerning. = 如果他变得更内向，那就令人担忧了。
I wish she wouldn't introvert so much after setbacks. = 我希望她在遇到挫折后不要那么内向。


被动语态:
His behavior is being interpreted as him introverting. = 他的行为被解释为他在变得内向。
The situation was seen as having introverted him completely. = 这种情况被认为使他完全内向。


疑问句:
Is he introverting? = 他正在变得内向吗？
Is she introverting due to the pressure? = 她是因为压力而变得内向吗？


否定句:
He isn't introverting; he's just tired. = 他不是变得内向，他只是累了。
She didn't introvert after the party; she was actually quite sociable. = 派对后她并没有变得内向；事实上她相当合群。

词源:  The verb "introverting" is derived from the noun "introvert," which itself comes from the Latin prefix "intro-" (meaning "inward" or "within") and the verb "vertere" (meaning "to turn").  Thus, it literally means "turning inward."
