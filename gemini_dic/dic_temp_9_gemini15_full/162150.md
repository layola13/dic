pre-Greek:/ˌpriːˈɡriːk/| adj.| 前希腊的；希腊化之前的 |  relating to the period of time before the Classical Greek civilization.  This term often refers to the civilizations and cultures that existed in the Aegean region before the rise of the Mycenaean civilization or the influence of Classical Greece.  It encompasses various cultures and periods, and is sometimes used loosely. | Example: Pre-Greek pottery has been found in the region.  前希腊时期的陶器已在该地区被发现。| 近义词：antecedent, prior, earlier | 反义词：post-Greek, Hellenic
