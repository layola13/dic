/ˈɒlədʒiz/ | /ɑːˈlɑːdʒiz/ | n. |学科；学说；论 |The suffix "-ologies" is used to form plural nouns referring to various branches of study or systems of beliefs. It's derived from the Greek word "-logia," meaning "study of" or "science of."  Many words ending in "-ology" are included,  representing different fields of knowledge. |Examples:  Many different ologies are taught at the university.  (大学教授许多不同的学科。) The professor's lectures covered various ologies related to the environment. (教授的讲座涵盖了与环境相关的各种学科。) |近义词: disciplines, branches of knowledge, fields of study |反义词: N/A
