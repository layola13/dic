paisa:/ˈpaɪsə/| n.|派萨（印度货币单位）|A paisa is a former monetary unit of India and other South Asian countries.  It was a subunit of the rupee, with 100 paise equaling one rupee. While no longer in circulation in India (replaced by the paise), the term remains in use in some contexts. |The shopkeeper gave me back five paise in change.  店主找给我五派萨的零钱。|rupee, cent|dollar, pound|名词
