parents:/ˈpeərənts/| n.|父母，双亲|Parents are the people who raised you.  父母是你抚养你的人。|近义词：father and mother, mom and dad, guardians|反义词：children, offspring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My parents work hard. = 我的父母工作努力。
My parents live in the countryside. = 我的父母住在农村。


一般过去时:
My parents went to the park yesterday. = 我的父母昨天去了公园。
My parents helped me with my homework. = 我的父母帮我做了家庭作业。


现在完成时:
My parents have traveled to many countries. = 我的父母去过很多国家。
My parents have supported me throughout my education. = 我的父母一直支持我的学业。


过去完成时:
My parents had already left when I arrived. = 我到达时，我的父母已经离开了。
My parents had prepared dinner before we got home. = 我们回家之前，我的父母已经准备好晚餐了。


将来时:
My parents will visit us next month. = 我的父母下个月会来看我们。
My parents will retire next year. = 我的父母明年将退休。


虚拟语气:
If my parents were here, they would be so proud. = 如果我的父母在这里，他们会非常自豪。
I wish my parents were younger. = 我希望我的父母年轻一些。


被动语态:
My parents were invited to the school event. = 我的父母被邀请参加学校活动。
Their concerns were addressed by the parents. = 他们的担忧得到了父母的回应。


疑问句:
Do your parents know about this? = 你父母知道这件事吗？
Where do your parents live? = 你父母住在哪里？


否定句:
My parents don't approve of my decision. = 我的父母不赞成我的决定。
My parents didn't attend the meeting. = 我的父母没有参加会议。
