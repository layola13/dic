discourages:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒɪz/| vt.|使气馁；劝阻；阻止|To deprive someone of courage or confidence; to dissuade someone from doing something.|例句：The bad weather discouraged us from going for a hike.  恶劣的天气使我们放弃了远足。
例句：His parents discouraged him from pursuing a career in music. 他的父母劝他不要从事音乐事业。|近义词：depress, deter, dissuade|反义词：encourage, inspire, motivate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain discourages people from going out. = 雨让人们不想出门。
His attitude discourages his teammates. = 他的态度让他的队友们感到气馁。

一般过去时:
The teacher's harsh words discouraged the student. = 老师严厉的话语让学生感到气馁。
The economic downturn discouraged many from investing. = 经济低迷使许多人不敢投资。

现在完成时:
His repeated failures have discouraged him. = 他屡屡失败让他灰心丧气。
The lack of support has discouraged many volunteers. = 缺乏支持已经让许多志愿者感到气馁。

过去完成时:
Previous setbacks had discouraged him from trying again. = 之前的挫折让他不敢再尝试。
The negative feedback had discouraged her from submitting her work. = 负面反馈让她不敢提交作品。

将来时:
The rising cost of living will discourage many from buying a house. = 生活成本的上升会让许多人打消买房的念头。
This policy will discourage further investment in the sector. =这项政策将会阻止对该行业的进一步投资。

虚拟语气:
If the weather hadn't been so bad, it wouldn't have discouraged us. = 如果天气不是那么糟糕，它就不会让我们气馁。
I wish my parents hadn't discouraged me from studying art. = 我希望我父母没有劝我不要学习艺术。

被动语态:
He was discouraged by his failure. = 他因为失败而灰心。
Many potential investors were discouraged by the market volatility. = 许多潜在投资者都被市场的波动吓退了。

疑问句:
Does this weather discourage you from going out? = 这样的天气会让你不想出门吗？
What discourages you from pursuing your dreams? = 什么让你打消追求梦想的念头？

否定句:
Nothing can discourage him from achieving his goals. = 没有任何事情能让他放弃实现目标。
The company did not discourage employees from taking risks. = 公司并没有阻止员工冒险。


PHRASE->
discourage someone from doing something = 劝阻某人做某事
be discouraged by something = 因某事而气馁
feel discouraged = 感到气馁
discourage further development = 阻止进一步发展
discourage unhealthy habits = 阻止不健康的习惯
