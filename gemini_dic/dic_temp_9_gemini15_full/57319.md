downtowner:/ˈdaʊntaʊnər/| n. |城里人，市中心居民|A person who lives in or frequents the downtown area of a city.  Downtowner often implies a certain lifestyle or character associated with the city center, perhaps someone who is sophisticated, busy, or involved in urban life. |Example:  He's a true downtowner; he knows all the best hidden restaurants and bars.|近义词：city dweller, urbanite|反义词：suburbanite, rural resident|名词
