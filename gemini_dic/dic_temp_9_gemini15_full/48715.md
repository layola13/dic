deficit:/ˈdɛfɪsɪt/| n.|赤字；不足；缺陷 | A deficiency or lack of something.  It often refers to a shortfall in funds, but can also describe a lack of something else, like a skill deficit. | The company reported a large budget deficit last year.  这家公司去年报告了巨额预算赤字。 | The country is facing a growing trade deficit. 这个国家正面临不断增长的贸易逆差。| surplus, shortfall, deficiency | surplus, abundance, excess | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The country faces a large trade deficit. = 这个国家面临着巨大的贸易逆差。
A skills deficit hinders economic growth. = 技能短缺阻碍了经济增长。


一般过去时:
The government reported a budget deficit last fiscal year. = 政府报告上个财政年度的预算赤字。
We experienced a significant learning deficit in math. = 我们在数学方面经历了严重的学习不足。


现在完成时:
The company has accumulated a substantial deficit over the past decade. = 公司在过去十年中累积了巨额赤字。
They have overcome a significant skills deficit through training. = 他们通过培训克服了严重的技能不足。


过去完成时:
Before the intervention, the school had a large achievement deficit. = 在干预之前，学校存在很大的成就差距。
The company had already incurred a considerable deficit before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经产生了相当大的赤字。


将来时:
The project is projected to have a small deficit next year. = 该项目预计明年将出现少量赤字。
Unless we act now, we will face a huge energy deficit in the future. = 除非我们现在采取行动，否则未来我们将面临巨大的能源短缺。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't intervened, the deficit would have been much larger. = 如果政府不干预，赤字将会更大。
If we had a sufficient budget, we wouldn't have this deficit. = 如果我们有足够的预算，就不会有这个赤字。


被动语态:
The deficit was caused by unexpected expenses. = 赤字是由意外支出造成的。
The skills deficit is being addressed through government funding. = 技能短缺正在通过政府资金来解决。


疑问句:
What is the current budget deficit? = 目前的预算赤字是多少？
Is there a significant learning deficit in this area? = 这个领域是否存在严重的学习不足？


否定句:
The company did not report a deficit this year. = 公司今年没有报告赤字。
There is no significant deficit in our resources. = 我们的资源没有明显的不足。


PHRASE->

budget deficit: 预算赤字
trade deficit: 贸易逆差
learning deficit: 学习障碍，学习不足
skills deficit: 技能差距，技能短缺
attention deficit: 注意力缺陷
