freestyler:/ˈfriːstaɪlər/| n. |自由式表演者，自由发挥者 | A person who performs freestyle, especially in sports, music, or other creative fields.  They improvise and create spontaneously, rather than following a pre-determined plan or routine. | Example:  The freestyler amazed the crowd with his incredible skateboarding tricks.  自由式表演者以其令人难以置信的滑板技巧让观众惊叹不已。 |近义词: improviser, innovator, artist |反义词: conformist, traditionalist
