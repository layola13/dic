matriculations:/ˌmætrɪkjuˈleɪʃənz/| n. |入学登记；注册；入学 |The act of matriculating; the process of being enrolled in a college or university.  It often refers to the formal ceremony or process of enrolling. |例句：The university held a matriculation ceremony for the new students.  大学为新生举行了入学典礼。|例句：Matriculations were down this year due to the economic downturn.  由于经济衰退，今年入学人数下降了。|近义词：enrollment, registration, admission |反义词：graduation, expulsion
