retestifying: /ˌriːˈtɛstɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 重新作证；再次作证 | The act of giving testimony again, often in a court of law, after having previously testified.  This might be due to new evidence, inconsistencies in earlier testimony, or the need for clarification. | Example:  The witness was called back for retestifying after new evidence emerged. =  新的证据出现后，证人被召回重新作证。


The defendant's lawyer requested retestifying after discovering new evidence. =  被告的律师在发现新证据后请求重新作证。


近义词:  re-testifying, giving further testimony, repeating testimony


反义词:  withdrawing testimony, refusing to testify


词性: 动词
