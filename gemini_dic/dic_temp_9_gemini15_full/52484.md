dietician: /ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. | 营养学家，饮食学家 | A dietician is a healthcare professional who is trained to assess, diagnose, and treat nutritional problems. They provide guidance on food and nutrition to individuals and groups. |  My dietician helped me create a meal plan to lose weight. 我的营养师帮我制定了一个减肥食谱。  She consulted a dietician to manage her diabetes. 她咨询了一位营养师来控制她的糖尿病。 | nutritionist, dietary consultant |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with someone promoting unhealthy eating habits)
