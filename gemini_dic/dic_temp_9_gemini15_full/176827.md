readvertisement:/ˌriːˈædvərtaɪzmənt/| n. | 再广告，重新刊登广告 | A readvertisement is the act of advertising something again, typically after an initial advertisement failed to achieve its purpose.  It often involves re-publishing or re-broadcasting the original advertisement, sometimes with minor modifications. | Example: Due to overwhelming response, we've had to issue a readvertisement for the position.  由于反应热烈，我们不得不重新发布该职位的广告。 |近义词:re-advertisement, renewed advertisement|反义词:withdrawal of advertisement|词性:noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company readvertises the job opening every week. = 公司每周都重新发布招聘启事。
He readvertises his car for sale online. = 他在网上再次发布了他的汽车出售广告。


一般过去时:
They readvertised the product after the initial campaign failed. = 首次宣传活动失败后，他们重新为产品做广告。
She readvertised the lost dog on social media. = 她在社交媒体上再次发布了寻狗启事。


现在完成时:
The company has readvertised the position three times this month. = 这个月公司已经三次重新发布了这个职位。
They have readvertised their services on several platforms. = 他们在多个平台上重新发布了他们的服务广告。


过去完成时:
They had readvertised the concert before the tickets sold out. = 门票售罄前，他们已经重新发布了音乐会的广告。
The store had readvertised the sale items after the initial promotion ended. = 首次促销结束后，商店重新发布了促销商品的广告。


将来时:
We will readvertise the property next week if we don't find a buyer. = 如果我们找不到买家，我们下周将重新发布房产广告。
They will readvertise the job opening on a different website. = 他们将在不同的网站上重新发布招聘启事。


虚拟语气:
If we had readvertised sooner, we might have had more applicants. = 如果我们早点重新发布广告，我们可能会有更多申请者。
I wish we had readvertised the event in a different way. = 我希望我们能以不同的方式重新发布活动广告。


被动语态:
The position was readvertised due to a lack of qualified candidates. = 由于缺乏合格的候选人，该职位被重新发布。
The product is being readvertised with a new marketing strategy. = 该产品正在采用新的营销策略进行重新宣传。


疑问句:

Has the product been readvertised yet? = 产品已经重新发布广告了吗？


否定句:
They did not readvertise the product after the first campaign. = 第一次宣传活动后，他们没有重新为产品做广告。
The company will not readvertise the position unless necessary. = 除非必要，否则公司不会重新发布该职位。
