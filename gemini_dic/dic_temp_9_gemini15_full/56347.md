dogtrick:/ˈdɒɡtrɪk/|/n.|狗戏法，小把戏|A dog trick is a feat or skill performed by a dog, often for entertainment.  It can range from simple commands like "sit" and "stay" to more complex actions. |例句：The dog performed several impressive dog tricks, including jumping through hoops and fetching specific items. 这只狗表演了几项令人印象深刻的狗戏法，包括跳圈和取回特定物品。

例句：Learning new dog tricks can be a fun and rewarding activity for both the dog and its owner. 学习新的狗戏法对狗和它的主人来说都是一项有趣且有益的活动。|近义词：dog act, stunt, feat|反义词：serious skill, genuine ability
