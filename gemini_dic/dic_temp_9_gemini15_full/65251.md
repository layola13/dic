etymologicon:/ˌɛtɪməˈlɒdʒɪkɒn/ | n. | 词源词典；词源学辞典 | A book that provides etymologies of words.  It's a comprehensive dictionary that traces the origins and historical development of words. | Example:  The *etymologicon* showed the word "nice" originally meant "foolish."  |近义词：etymological dictionary, word origin dictionary |反义词：无
