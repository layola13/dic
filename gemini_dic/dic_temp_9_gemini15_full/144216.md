outskating:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪtɪŋ/| v.|胜过(在滑冰方面)|To surpass someone in skating skill or speed.  It implies a significant difference in ability.|例句：The young skater outskated his opponent, winning the race easily. 年轻的滑冰者轻松地超过了他的对手，赢得了比赛。

例句：The professional outskated all the amateurs in the competition.  职业选手在比赛中胜过了所有业余选手。


近义词：outperform, outstrip, surpass, exceed


反义词：be outskated by


词性:动词
