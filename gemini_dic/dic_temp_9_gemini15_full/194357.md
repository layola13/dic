self-postponements:/self-pəʊstˈpəʊnmənts/| n. |自我拖延，一再推迟 |The act of repeatedly delaying or postponing tasks or activities due to one's own volition. This often stems from procrastination, avoidance, or a lack of self-discipline.  |例句：Her self-postponements led to a significant backlog of unfinished work.  她的自我拖延导致大量工作积压。


Her constant self-postponements prevented her from achieving her goals. 她不断地自我拖延，阻止了她实现目标。|近义词：procrastination, delay, deferral |反义词：promptness, punctuality, diligence |名词
