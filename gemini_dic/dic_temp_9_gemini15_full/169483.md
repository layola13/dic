propagandistically:/ˌprɒpəˈɡændɪstɪkli/| adv.|宣传地；鼓吹地|In a manner characteristic of propaganda;  using methods typical of propaganda.|例句：The government propagandistically presented its policies as beneficial to all citizens.  政府以宣传的方式将政策描绘成对所有公民都有利。 The news was propagandistically slanted to favor the ruling party. 新闻带有宣传色彩，偏向执政党。|近义词：propagandizingly,  aggressively (in the context of promoting an idea),  biasedly|反义词：objectively, truthfully, impartially
