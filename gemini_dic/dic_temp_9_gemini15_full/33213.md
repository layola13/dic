chronicled:/ˈkrɒnɪkld/| /ˈkrɑːnɪkld/| v.|编年史般记录；详细记载|To record (events) in a chronological order; to give a detailed account of.|例句：The historian chronicled the rise and fall of the Roman Empire.  历史学家详细记述了罗马帝国的兴衰。|例句：The journalist chronicled the events of the war.  记者详细报道了战争的事件。|近义词：recorded, documented, narrated, described|反义词：forgot, ignored, neglected|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chronicles the daily events in his journal. = 他在他的日记中记录每天的事件。
She chronicles the history of her family. = 她记录了她家族的历史。

一般过去时:
The author chronicled the lives of several prominent figures. = 作者记述了几位杰出人物的生活。
They chronicled the progress of the scientific discovery. = 他们记录了科学发现的进展。

现在完成时:
Historians have chronicled the impact of the war for generations. = 历史学家们几代人以来一直在记录战争的影响。
She has chronicled her journey across the country in her blog. = 她已在博客中记录了她穿越全国的旅程。

过去完成时:
Before the war broke out, they had chronicled the peaceful life of the village. = 在战争爆发之前，他们已经记录了村庄的和平生活。
He had chronicled the events leading up to the revolution for years. = 多年来，他一直在记录导致革命的事件。

将来时:
She will chronicle the development of the new technology in her upcoming book. = 她将在即将出版的书中记录这项新技术的开发。
They will chronicle the expedition to the Amazon rainforest. = 他们将记录亚马逊雨林探险的经过。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would chronicle the history of my town. = 如果我有时间，我会记录下我镇的历史。
I wish I could chronicle the lives of the people I met. = 我希望我能记录下我遇到的人们的生活。

被动语态:
The events of the revolution were chronicled by many historians. = 革命的事件被许多历史学家记录下来。
The discovery was chronicled in detail in the scientific journal. = 这一发现被科学期刊详细记录下来。

疑问句:
Has the author chronicled all the important events? = 作者是否记录了所有重要的事件？
Will they chronicle the impact of climate change on the environment? = 他们会记录气候变化对环境的影响吗？

否定句:
He did not chronicle the details of the meeting. = 他没有记录会议的细节。
They have not yet chronicled the full story of the disaster. = 他们还没有完整地记录这场灾难的故事。
