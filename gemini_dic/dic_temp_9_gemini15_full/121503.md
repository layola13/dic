mislearns:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrnz/ | v. | 学错，误学 | To learn incorrectly or to misunderstand.  | He mislearns the grammar rules. 他语法规则学错了。|  learn incorrectly, misunderstand | learn correctly, understand | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mislearns new vocabulary every day. 他每天都把新词汇学错。
She mislearns the instructions and makes a mistake. 她误解了说明，犯了个错误。


一般过去时:
I mislearned the song lyrics. 我把歌的歌词记错了。
They mislearned the history of the event. 他们误解了事件的历史。


现在完成时:
He has mislearned several key concepts in the course. 他已经把这门课里好几个关键概念都学错了。
She has mislearned the pronunciation of many words. 她已经把很多单词的发音都读错了。


过去完成时:
I realized I had mislearned the formula before the exam. 我考试前才意识到自己把公式记错了。
They discovered they had mislearned the procedure after the failure. 失败后他们才发现自己把程序搞错了。


将来时:
I fear he will mislearn the crucial information. 我担心他会把关键信息学错。
She believes she will mislearn the language if she doesn't practice enough. 她相信如果练习不够，她就会把这门语言学错。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I wouldn't have mislearned the material. 如果我学习更努力，我就不会把材料学错了。
I wish I hadn't mislearned those facts years ago. 我真希望我几年前没有把那些事实弄错。


被动语态:
The information was mislearned by many students. 很多学生都把信息学错了。
The lesson was mislearned because of the confusing instructions. 因为说明混乱，这节课被学错了。


疑问句:
Did you mislearn anything in your last class? 你在上一节课上学错什么了吗？
Have they mislearned the techniques required for the experiment? 他们是否误解了实验所需的技巧？


否定句:
He didn't mislearn the formula; he simply forgot it. 他没有把公式学错，他只是忘了。
She didn't mislearn the recipe; she just followed it incorrectly. 她没有把菜谱学错，她只是操作不正确。
