individualize: /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使个性化；使个别化 | To make or become individual or distinct; to adapt to the needs of an individual.  | 例句：The teacher tried to individualize the curriculum to meet the needs of each student. 老师试图个性化课程以满足每个学生的需要。 | 近义词：personalize, customize, tailor | 反义词：standardize, generalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school individualizes its teaching methods. = 学校个性化教学方法。
They individualize their marketing strategies. = 他们个性化他们的营销策略。

一般过去时:
The company individualized its products last year. = 公司去年对其产品进行了个性化设计。
She individualized her approach to each patient. = 她对每个病人的治疗方法都进行了个性化调整。

现在完成时:
We have individualized our learning plans. = 我们已经个性化了我们的学习计划。
The hospital has individualized treatment plans for all patients. = 医院已经为所有病人制定了个性化治疗方案。

过去完成时:
They had individualized the training program before the workshop. = 他们在研讨会之前已经将培训计划个性化了。
The designer had individualized the dress before the fashion show. = 设计师在时装秀之前已经将服装个性化了。

将来时:
We will individualize the software for our clients. = 我们将为客户个性化软件。
The company will individualize the product offerings based on customer feedback. = 公司将根据客户反馈个性化产品供应。

虚拟语气:
If we individualized the service, more customers would be satisfied. = 如果我们个性化服务，更多的客户将会满意。
I wish they had individualized the lesson plans. = 我希望他们当时已经个性化了教学计划。

被动语态:
The lessons are individualized to suit the students' needs. = 课程被个性化以适应学生的需求。
The software was individualized for each user. = 软件为每个用户都进行了个性化设置。

疑问句:
Does the company individualize its marketing campaigns? = 公司是否个性化其营销活动？
Will the program individualize the learning experience? = 该程序会个性化学习体验吗？

否定句:
The factory doesn't individualize its products. = 工厂没有对产品进行个性化处理。
They didn't individualize the training materials for each group. = 他们没有为每个小组个性化培训材料。


PHRASE->
individualize learning = 个性化学习
individualize instruction = 个性化教学
individualize treatment = 个性化治疗
individualize marketing = 个性化营销
individualize education = 个性化教育


词源:
individualize 源自 individual (个体) + -ize (动词后缀，表示“使……化”)。  individual 的词源追溯到拉丁语 individuum，意思是“不可分割的个体”。
