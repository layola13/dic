glyptodon:/ˈɡlɪptodɒn/| n. |雕齿兽|An extinct genus of large, heavily armored mammals that lived during the Pleistocene epoch.  They were related to armadillos and sloths.  They were characterized by their large, bony shell, which provided protection from predators.|Example:  The glyptodon's massive shell was its primary defense against saber-toothed cats.  雕齿兽巨大的甲壳是它抵御剑齿虎的主要防御手段。|近义词：armadillo (in terms of relationship, not size or features)|反义词：None (extinct animal)|名词
