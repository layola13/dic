complementing: /ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntɪŋ/ | /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 补充；赞美；使完善 |  To enhance or complete something else; to praise or express admiration. |

例句：
Her red shoes complemented her dress perfectly. = 她的红鞋子完美地衬托了她的裙子。

The flowers complemented the beauty of the garden. =  鲜花使花园更加美丽。


近义词：enhancing, supplementing, praising, admiring

反义词：contrasting, detracting from, criticizing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is complementing her on her cooking skills. = 他正在赞美她的厨艺。
The colors in the painting complement each other. = 画中的颜色互相衬托。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

疑问句:

否定句:

His actions did not complement his words. = 他的行为与他的话语并不相符。
