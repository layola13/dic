extremis:/ɪkˈstriːmɪs/| n.|极端；危急关头|The state of extreme danger or urgency; the utmost point.  It often refers to a situation of great peril or emergency.|例句：The country was in extremis after the devastating earthquake.  这个国家在地震后处于危急关头。|例句：He fought for his life in extremis.|他在危急关头为自己的生命而战。|近义词：emergency, crisis, extremity, brink|反义词：calm, safety, security|名词
