expends:/ɪkˈspɛndz/ | vt. |花费；消耗；用尽 | To use up (time, money, energy, etc.).  |例句：She expends a great deal of energy on her work. 她把大量的精力都花在了工作上。
例句：The company expends significant resources on research and development. 这家公司在研发上投入了大量资源。|近义词：spend, consume, use up |反义词：save, conserve, accumulate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expends his energy wisely. = 他明智地运用他的精力。
They expend considerable funds on advertising. = 他们在广告上花费了大量的资金。


一般过去时:
She expended all her savings on a new car. = 她把所有积蓄都花在新车上。
The project expended more time than anticipated. = 这个项目花费的时间比预期的要长。


现在完成时:
They have expended all their effort on this project. = 他们已经在这个项目上付出了全部努力。
We have expended a significant amount of money this month. = 这个月我们已经花费了一大笔钱。


过去完成时:
They had expended all their resources before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经用尽了所有资源。
She had expended much effort trying to persuade him. = 她已经费了很大的劲试图说服他。


将来时:
He will expend more effort in his studies next semester. = 下学期他将在学习上付出更多努力。
The company will expend a large sum on the new marketing campaign. = 公司将在新的营销活动上投入巨资。


虚拟语气:
If he expended more time on his studies, he would get better grades. = 如果他花更多时间学习，他的成绩会更好。
I wish I had expended more energy on that project. = 我真希望我当时在那个项目上多花些精力。


被动语态:
A large sum of money was expended on the new building. = 大笔资金花在了新建大楼上。
Significant resources are being expended on renewable energy research. = 大量资源正在投入可再生能源研究。


疑问句:
How much money did they expend on the trip? = 他们这次旅行花了多少钱？
What resources will be expended on the new project? = 新项目将投入哪些资源？


否定句:
He did not expend much effort on the task. = 他没有在这个任务上花太多力气。
The company did not expend any money on unnecessary things. = 公司没有在不必要的事情上花钱。


PHRASE->
