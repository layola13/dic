non-hydraulic: /ˌnɒn haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/ | adj. | 非液压的 | Not using hydraulic power.  This adjective describes systems, mechanisms, or processes that do not utilize liquid pressure to generate force or motion.  | Example:  The non-hydraulic braking system relies on mechanical leverage.  这个非液压刹车系统依靠机械杠杆。 The company is developing a non-hydraulic steering system for its new line of vehicles.  这家公司正在为其新系列车辆开发非液压转向系统。 | 近义词: mechanical, manual | 反义词: hydraulic
