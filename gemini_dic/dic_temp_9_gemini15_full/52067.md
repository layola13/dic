dialogue: /ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ /ˈdaɪəlɔːɡ/ | n. | 对话，对话体；脚本 | A conversation between two or more people.  It can also refer to the written text of a conversation in a play, film, or novel. | Example: The dialogue in the movie was witty and engaging. 这部电影中的对话机智而引人入胜。  The play's dialogue was realistic and believable.  这出戏的对话真实可信。 |近义词：conversation, exchange, chat, discussion |反义词：monologue, soliloquy | 名词


USAGE->

The characters engage in lively dialogue. = 角色们进行着热烈的对话。
We have a dialogue about the project every week. = 我们每周就项目进行一次对话。
