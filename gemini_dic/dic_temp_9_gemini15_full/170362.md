provisionals:/prəˈvɪʒənəlz/ | adj. |临时的；暂时的；备用的 |Temporary or for the time being; intended to be replaced later.  |The provisional government collapsed after only a few weeks. 临时政府只存在了几个星期就垮台了。  The provisional results showed a close race.  初步结果显示选情胶着。|permanent, temporary, interim | permanent, final |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These are provisional arrangements. = 这些是暂时的安排。
The provisional government is in power. = 临时政府当政。

一般过去时:
They announced provisional figures. = 他们公布了初步数字。
The committee adopted provisional measures. = 委员会采取了临时措施。

现在完成时:
They have released provisional results. = 他们已经发布了初步结果。
We have made provisional bookings for the trip. = 我们已经为这次旅行做了预订。

过去完成时:
They had implemented provisional solutions before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前实施临时解决方案。
The company had already made provisional plans. = 公司已经制定了初步计划。

将来时:
We will announce provisional findings next week. = 我们下周将公布初步调查结果。
The school will implement provisional schedules until the new term. = 学校将在新学期开始前实施临时课程表。

虚拟语气:
If we had provisional data, the analysis would be easier. = 如果我们有初步数据，分析就会更容易。
I wish we had provisional access to the system. = 我希望我们可以暂时访问该系统。

被动语态:
Provisional measures were taken to control the outbreak. = 采取了临时措施来控制疫情。
The provisional results were released yesterday. = 初步结果昨天公布了。

疑问句:
Are these provisional arrangements? = 这些是暂时的安排吗？
Were the figures provisional? = 这些数字是初步的吗？

否定句:
These are not provisional arrangements; they are permanent. = 这些不是暂时的安排；它们是永久的。
The final results are not yet available, only the provisional ones. = 最终结果尚未公布，只有初步结果。
