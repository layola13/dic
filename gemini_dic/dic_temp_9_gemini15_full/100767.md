intravenous:/ˌɪntrəˈveɪnəs/| adj.|静脉内的，静脉注射的|relating to or occurring within a vein or veins.  The term is often shortened to IV. |例句：The patient received intravenous fluids.  病人接受了静脉输液。|例句：The medication was administered intravenously.  药物是静脉注射的。|近义词：venous, intravascular|反义词：oral, intramuscular
