chops:/tʃɑːps/ | n. | 排骨，肉块；砍，剁 | Thick slices of meat, especially from a rib; to cut into pieces with a sharp instrument. |例句：He ordered a plate of pork chops. 他点了一盘猪排。

例句：The butcher chopped the meat into small pieces.  屠夫把肉剁成小块。

近义词：cuts, pieces, slices | 反义词：whole, uncut

词源:  From Middle English *choppe, from Old French *chope, from Vulgar Latin *coppa ("a blow, cut"), a diminutive of Latin *cappa ("a blow, cutting").
