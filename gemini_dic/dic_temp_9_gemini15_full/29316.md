castes:/ˈkæsts/| n.|种姓制度；等级制度|A social system in which people are divided into different classes, especially by birth, and in which social mobility is very limited.  The term is most commonly associated with the Hindu social system in India, but similar systems have existed in other parts of the world.|例句：The caste system in India has historically determined social status and occupation.  印度的种姓制度历来决定着社会地位和职业。

The rigid caste system prevented social mobility.  僵化的种姓制度阻碍了社会流动。|近义词：classes, hierarchies, strata|反义词：egalitarianism, equality
