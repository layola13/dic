prestudying:/ˌpriːˈstʌdiɪŋ/ | v. | 预习，预先学习 |  The act of studying something beforehand.  It implies preparation before a formal learning session or event. | Example:  I'm prestudying the chapters for tomorrow's lecture. = 我正在预习明天讲座的内容。

I'm prestudying the material for the upcoming exam. = 我正在预习即将到来的考试材料。


近义词: reviewing, rehearsing, preparing


反义词: reviewing (in the sense of studying after the event), neglecting

词性:动词
