highroad:/ˈhaɪroʊd/| n. |主要道路；坦途；光明大道|The main road, especially one that is well-maintained and easy to travel. It can also refer to a morally upright or straightforward course of action. |例句：We took the highroad through the mountains. 我们选择了翻山越岭的主要道路。|例句：He chose the highroad of honesty, even though it was difficult. 即使很难，他还是选择了诚实的坦途。|近义词：main road, highway, direct route, straight path|反义词：byway, detour, back road, underhanded approach|词性:名词
