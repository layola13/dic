resewing:/ˌriːˈsoʊɪŋ/| v.|重新缝制，缝补|The act of sewing something again, often to repair or mend it.  It implies a previous sewing process.|例句：I spent the afternoon resewing the buttons on my coat. 我花了整个下午重新缝补外套上的纽扣。

例句：The tailor is resewing the rip in my favorite dress.裁缝正在缝补我最喜欢的裙子上的裂口。


近义词：mending, repairing, patching|反义词：ripping, tearing, unsewing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She resews her own clothes. = 她自己缝补衣服。
He resews the torn hem of his trousers. = 他缝补他裤子撕裂的裤脚。

一般过去时:
I resewed the button last night. = 我昨晚重新缝上了纽扣。
She resewed the rip in the fabric. = 她缝补了布料上的裂口。

现在完成时:
I have resewed all the loose seams. = 我已经缝好了所有松动的缝线。
She has resewed the lining of her jacket. = 她已经缝好了夹克的里衬。

过去完成时:
He had resewed the tear before he gave the shirt to the charity. = 他在把衬衫捐给慈善机构之前已经缝好了撕裂的地方。
They had resewed the curtain before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经缝好了窗帘。


将来时:
I will re-sew the button tomorrow. = 我明天会重新缝上纽扣。
She will re-sew the dress before the wedding. = 婚礼前她会重新缝制这件裙子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-sew the entire garment. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新缝制整件衣服。
I wish I had resewed the hem before I wore the dress. = 我真希望在我穿这件裙子之前就把裙脚缝好了。


被动语态:
The torn sleeve was resewn by the seamstress. = 撕破的袖子被裁缝缝好了。
The damaged cushion cover will be resewn. = 损坏的靠垫套将会被重新缝制。


疑问句:
Have you resewn the rip in your trousers yet? = 你已经缝好裤子上的裂口了吗？
Will she be resewing the dress for the party? = 她会为派对重新缝制这件裙子吗？


否定句:
I didn't re-sew the button, it's still loose. = 我没有重新缝纽扣，它仍然很松。
She hasn't resewn the dress yet. = 她还没重新缝制那件裙子。
