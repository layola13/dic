词源:
The word "emotioned" is derived from the noun "emotion," ultimately from the Old French word "emocioun" and the Latin word "emotio," meaning "a stirring up, excitement." The suffix "-ed" transforms the noun into an adjective, describing something characterized by or expressing emotion.
