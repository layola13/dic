grippers:/ˈɡrɪpərz/| n.|抓具；夹持器；抓爪|A gripper is a device used to grasp and hold objects, often used in robotics and manufacturing.  They can be mechanical, pneumatic, or vacuum-based.  |例句：The robot's grippers carefully picked up the delicate components. 机器人的抓具小心地拾起了精密的部件。

The factory uses advanced grippers to increase efficiency in the assembly line. 工厂使用先进的抓具来提高装配线的效率。|近义词：clamps, tongs, holders|反义词：releasers
