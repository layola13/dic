outpunched:/ˌaʊtˈpʌntʃt/| v.|击垮，以猛烈打击战胜 |  To defeat or surpass someone in a fight or competition by delivering stronger or more effective punches.  |例句：In the boxing match, the underdog outpunched the champion and won.  在拳击比赛中，弱者以猛烈的拳击击败了冠军并赢得了比赛。

例句：He completely outpunched his opponent, leaving him dazed and confused. 他彻底击垮了他的对手，让他晕头转向。


近义词：defeat, overcome, surpass, outfight, overpower

反义词：be defeated by, be outmatched by

词性：动词
