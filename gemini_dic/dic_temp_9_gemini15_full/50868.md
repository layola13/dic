desensitised: /ˌdiːˈsɛnsaɪzd/ | adj. | 脱敏的；感觉迟钝的 | Made less sensitive or responsive;  having reduced sensitivity to something. | 例句：After years of exposure to violence, he became desensitised to it. 多年接触暴力后，他对暴力麻木了。  |  例句：The constant bombardment of negative news can desensitise people to suffering.  持续不断的负面新闻轰炸可能会让人们对苦难麻木。 | 近义词：numb, indifferent, unresponsive, callous | 反义词：sensitive, responsive, empathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is desensitised to criticism. = 他对批评麻木不仁。
She is desensitised to the violence on TV. = 她对电视上的暴力麻木不仁。

一般过去时:
He became desensitised after witnessing the accident. = 他目睹事故后变得麻木不仁。
The constant exposure to violence desensitised her to the suffering of others. = 持续接触暴力让她对别人的痛苦麻木不仁。

现在完成时:
They have become desensitised to the harsh realities of war. = 他们已经对战争的残酷现实麻木不仁。
She has desensitised herself to the negativity online. = 她已经让自己对网上的负面信息麻木不仁。

过去完成时:
He had desensitised himself to the pain by the time the surgery started. = 手术开始时，他已经让自己对疼痛麻木不仁。
They had become desensitised to the constant noise of the city. = 他们已经对城市里不断的噪音麻木不仁了。

将来时:
Constant exposure to violent media will desensitise him to real-life violence. = 持续接触暴力媒体会让他对现实生活中的暴力麻木不仁。
She fears she will desensitise herself to the beauty of nature if she continues to work such long hours. = 她担心如果继续长时间工作，她会对自然之美麻木不仁。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't witnessed so much violence, he wouldn't be so desensitised. = 如果他没有目睹那么多暴力，他就不会如此麻木不仁。
I wish I wasn't so desensitised to the suffering of others. = 我希望自己不要对别人的痛苦如此麻木不仁。

被动语态:
The audience was desensitised by the graphic images. = 观众被血腥的图像弄得麻木不仁。
He felt he was being desensitised by the constant negativity in the news. = 他觉得新闻中持续不断的负面信息让他麻木不仁。

疑问句:
Is he desensitised to the pain? = 他对疼痛麻木不仁吗？
Are you desensitised to the violence in video games? = 你对电子游戏中的暴力麻木不仁吗？

否定句:
He is not desensitised to the plight of the homeless. = 他并没有对无家可归者的困境麻木不仁。
She was not desensitised to the beauty around her. = 她并没有对周围的美景麻木不仁。
