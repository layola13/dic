ramekin:/ræmɪkɪn/| n. |小烤碗，小瓷碗 |A small, shallow, ovenproof dish, typically used for baking individual portions of food.  |例句：She baked individual ramekins of chocolate mousse. 她烤了几个巧克力慕斯的杯子蛋糕。|例句：The restaurant served its crème brûlée in elegant ramekins. 这家餐厅用精致的小烤碗盛着焦糖布丁。|近义词：custard cup, oven-safe dish |反义词：large bowl, casserole dish
