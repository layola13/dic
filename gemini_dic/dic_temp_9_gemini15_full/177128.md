reapproaches:/ˌriːəˈproʊtʃɪz/ | v. | 再次接近；重新探讨 | To approach again; to reconsider or discuss something again.  |例句：The committee reapproached the issue of funding after new data emerged.  委员会在出现新的数据后重新讨论了资金问题。

例句：He reapproached the problem from a different angle, finally finding a solution. 他从不同的角度重新审视这个问题，最终找到了解决方法。


近义词：revisit, reconsider, reassess | 反义词：avoid, ignore, neglect | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reapproaches its marketing strategy every year. = 公司每年都会重新调整其营销策略。
He reapproaches the task with renewed determination. = 他以新的决心再次着手这项任务。


一般过去时:
They reapproached the negotiations after a brief recess. = 他们短暂休会后重新开始谈判。
She reapproached her teacher to ask for clarification. = 她再次找到老师寻求澄清。


现在完成时:
The team has reapproached the design several times. = 团队已经多次重新设计。
He has reapproached the subject in his latest book. = 他在他的最新著作中再次探讨了这个话题。


过去完成时:
They had reapproached the issue before the final decision was made. = 在做出最终决定之前，他们已经重新讨论过这个问题。
She had reapproached her boss several times before he finally listened. = 在老板最终听取她的意见之前，她已经多次找过老板了。


将来时:
We will reapproach the problem with a fresh perspective. = 我们将以新的视角重新审视这个问题。
They will reapproach the client next week. = 他们下周将再次联系客户。


虚拟语气:
If they reapproached the situation calmly, they might find a solution. = 如果他们平静地重新面对这种情况，他们可能会找到解决办法。
I wish he had reapproached the matter with more diplomacy. = 我希望他当初能更外交地处理这件事。


被动语态:
The proposal was reapproached by the board after further review. = 在进一步审查之后，该提案被董事会重新审议。
The problem is being reapproached using a different methodology. = 这个问题正在使用不同的方法重新处理。


疑问句:

否定句:
