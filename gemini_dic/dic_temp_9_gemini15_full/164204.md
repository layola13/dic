predivided:/ˌpriːdɪˈvaɪdəd/| adj.|预先分好的，预先划分的|Already divided or separated before a particular process or event.  This adjective describes something that has been divided or portioned beforehand.|例句：The class was predivided into smaller groups for the activity.  班级为了这次活动事先被分成更小的组。

例句：The cake was predivided into twelve slices for easy serving. 蛋糕被预先切成十二块，方便分发。


近义词：partitioned, pre-allocated, pre-sectioned
反义词：undivided, whole, unpartitioned


词性:形容词
