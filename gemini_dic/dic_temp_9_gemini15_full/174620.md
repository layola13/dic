quitter:/ˈkwɪtər/| n.|放弃者，轻易放弃的人|A quitter is someone who gives up easily when faced with difficulties or challenges. They lack perseverance and determination.|He's a quitter; he gave up on the project after only a week. 他是个放弃者；他只做了一周就放弃了那个项目。|persisters, perseverers|achiever, fighter


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He quits his job every year. 他每年都辞职。
She quits smoking easily. 她很容易戒烟。


一般过去时:
He quit his job last month. 他上个月辞职了。
She quit smoking a year ago. 她一年前戒烟了。


现在完成时:
He has quit smoking twice. 他戒烟两次了。
She has quit her job and started a business. 她已经辞职创业了。


过去完成时:
He had quit his job before he met her. 他在她之前就辞职了。
She had quit smoking before her pregnancy. 她怀孕前就戒烟了。


将来时:
He will quit his job next month. 他下个月要辞职。
She will quit smoking next year. 她明年将戒烟。


虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would quit my job. 如果我是他，我会辞职。
I wish I had quit smoking sooner. 我希望我早点戒烟。


被动语态:
His job was quit by him.  他的工作被他辞掉了。(This is not a natural-sounding sentence; the active voice is preferred.)
The smoking was quit by her. 她戒了烟。(This is also unnatural; the active voice is preferred.)


疑问句:
Did he quit his job? 他辞职了吗？
Will she quit smoking? 她会戒烟吗？


否定句:
He didn't quit his job. 他没有辞职。
She didn't quit smoking. 她没有戒烟。



PHRASE->
quit smoking 戒烟
quit drinking 戒酒
quit your job 辞职
give it a quit 放弃
don't be a quitter 不要轻易放弃
