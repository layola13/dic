post-adolescent: /ˌpoʊstˌædəˈlɛsənt/ | adj. |青春期后的；青年期的|

英文解析:  Referring to the period of life immediately following adolescence, typically characterized by continued development and transition into adulthood.  This period is not strictly defined and can vary depending on cultural and individual factors.  It often encompasses the late teens and early twenties.

例句:
