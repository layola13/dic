cybercriminal:/ˈsaɪbərkrɪmɪnəl/ | n. |网络罪犯 | A person who commits crimes using computers and the internet.  Cybercriminals engage in a wide range of illegal activities, from hacking and data theft to fraud and online harassment. | Example: The cybercriminal was caught and sentenced to five years in prison.  网络罪犯被抓获并被判处五年监禁。 |近义词: hacker, online criminal, digital criminal |反义词:cybersecurity professional, law enforcement officer
