catamount:/ˈkætəmaʊnt/| n. |美洲狮，山猫|A catamount is a large wild cat, typically a cougar or mountain lion.  The term is primarily used in North America and is often considered a regional or archaic name.|Example:  The hikers saw a catamount near the trail.  徒步旅行者在小径附近看到一只美洲狮。|近义词：cougar, mountain lion, puma|反义词：None (the antonym would describe a small cat)|名词
