ichthyologist:/ˌɪkθiˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |鱼类学家|A scientist who studies fish.  Ichthyologists study the biology, ecology, and behavior of fish. They may work in museums, universities, government agencies, or aquariums. |例句：Dr. Lee is a renowned ichthyologist specializing in deep-sea fish. 李博士是一位专门研究深海鱼类的著名鱼类学家。

例句：The ichthyologist presented her findings on the migration patterns of salmon.  这位鱼类学家介绍了她关于鲑鱼迁徙模式的研究结果。


近义词：fish biologist, ichthyologer


反义词：无


词性：名词
