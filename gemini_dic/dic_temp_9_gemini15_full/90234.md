house-hunting:/ˈhaʊsˌhʌntɪŋ/| n. |房屋寻觅；购房找房|The act of looking for a house to buy or rent.  It often involves viewing multiple properties, comparing prices and features, and negotiating with sellers or landlords. |例句：House-hunting can be a stressful but exciting process. 购房找房既紧张又刺激。|例句：They've been house-hunting for months without finding anything suitable. 他们找房子找了几个月，还没找到合适的。|近义词：home-seeking, property searching|反义词：N/A|名词
