goldsmiths:/ˈɡoʊldsmiθs/| n. |金匠；金银匠 | A goldsmith is a craftsperson who makes and sells objects from gold, silver, and other precious metals.  They often create jewelry, tableware, and other decorative items. | The goldsmith crafted a beautiful necklace for the queen.  这位金匠为女王制作了一条美丽的项链。| jeweler, silversmith | blacksmith  |  n.
