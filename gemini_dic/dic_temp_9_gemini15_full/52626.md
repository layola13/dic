digged-out: /ˈdɪɡd aʊt/ | adj. |  挖出来的；筋疲力尽的；憔悴的 |  Describes something that has been excavated or unearthed, or a person who appears exhausted and worn out.  | 例句：The dig team unearthed a digged-out ancient city.  挖掘队发现了一座被挖掘出来的古城。

The long journey left her feeling completely digged-out. 漫长的旅程让她感到筋疲力尽。| 近义词：exhausted, worn-out, unearthed, excavated | 反义词：refreshed, energetic, revitalized | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The archaeologist digs out artifacts. 考古学家挖掘文物。
They often dig out old photos. 他们经常翻出旧照片。


一般过去时:
He dug out his old baseball glove. 他找出了他的旧棒球手套。
She dug out the information from the archives. 她从档案中挖掘出信息。


现在完成时:
We have dug out all the potatoes from the garden. 我们已经把花园里所有的土豆都挖出来了。
They have dug out a fascinating history of the town. 他们已经挖掘出了这个小镇引人入胜的历史。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, she had dug out all the necessary data. 在演示文稿之前，她已经找到了所有必要的数据。
They had dug out a new strategy after several failed attempts. 经过几次失败的尝试后，他们制定了一个新的策略。


将来时:
We will dig out the buried treasure tomorrow. 我们明天将挖掘出埋藏的宝藏。
They will dig out the truth behind the mystery. 他们将挖掘出谜团背后的真相。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dig out the old letters. 如果我有更多的时间，我会把旧信挖出来。
I wish I could dig out the answer to this question. 我希望我能找到这个问题的答案。


被动语态:
The ancient ruins were dug out by the archaeologists. 古代遗址由考古学家挖掘出来。
The truth will eventually be dug out. 真相最终会被挖掘出来。


疑问句:
Have they dug out the evidence yet? 他们已经挖掘出证据了吗？
What did you dig out from the attic? 你从阁楼上挖出了什么？


否定句:
They didn't dig out the truth. 他们没有挖掘出真相。
I couldn't dig out the information I needed. 我找不到我需要的信息。
