montage's:/mɒnˈtɑːʒz/| n.|蒙太奇（的）|A montage is a technique in filmmaking, photography, or other visual arts where a series of images or shots are quickly put together to create a composite effect. It can be used to condense time, show a sequence of events, or create a particular mood or atmosphere.  The plural form "montages" refers to multiple instances of this technique.|例句：The film's powerful montage of war scenes left a lasting impression. 这部电影中战争场面的有力蒙太奇留下了持久的印象。


The opening credits featured a striking montage of cityscapes. 片头字幕以引人注目的城市景观蒙太奇为特色。|近义词：collage, medley, sequence|反义词：single shot, static image|名词
