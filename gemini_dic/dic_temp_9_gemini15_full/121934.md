misshipped:/mɪsˈʃɪpt/| adj.|错运的，寄错的|The word "misshipped" is a relatively new term, formed by combining "miss" (meaning to fail to hit or achieve something) and "shipped" (the past participle of "ship," meaning to transport goods). It describes goods or items that have been incorrectly shipped, either to the wrong address, or in the wrong way.|例句：My order was misshipped and arrived at the wrong address. 我的订单寄错了地址。The package was misshipped; it should have gone via express mail but went by standard post.包裹寄错了；它本应该走快递，却走了平邮。|近义词：misdirected, wrongly shipped, incorrectly sent|反义词：correctly shipped, properly delivered|
