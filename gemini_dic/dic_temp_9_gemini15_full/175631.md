rankless:/ˈræŋkləs/ | adj. | 无等级的；无级别的；无地位的 | Lacking rank or status; without a hierarchical position.  The organization is deliberately rankless, aiming for a more egalitarian structure.  | 近义词：unranked, untitled, classless | 反义词：ranked, high-ranking, hierarchical |

例句：

The commune operated on a rankless system, where everyone was considered equal.  = 公社实行无等级制度，人人平等。

He preferred the rankless informality of the small workshop to the rigid hierarchy of the large corporation. = 他更喜欢小型工作室的无等级随意性，而不是大型公司的严格等级制度。
