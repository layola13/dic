muzarabic:/ˌmjuːzəˈræbɪk/| adj.|摩萨拉伯的；穆萨拉比风格的|Of or relating to the Visigothic-Arab culture that flourished in the Iberian Peninsula after the Muslim conquest in the 8th century.  The term often refers to the language, literature, or art produced during this period.  It particularly describes the Romance languages and artistic styles that developed under Moorish influence.|The Muzarabic Rite is a liturgical rite used by some Mozarabs.|摩萨拉伯礼仪是部分摩萨拉伯人使用的礼仪。|近义词：Moorish (in some contexts)|反义词：无
