recited:/rɪˈsaɪtɪd/ | v. | 背诵；朗诵；叙述 | To repeat aloud from memory, especially a poem or passage.  |例句：She recited the poem flawlessly. 她完美地背诵了这首诗。|例句：The witness recited the events of that night.目击者叙述了那天晚上发生的事情。|近义词：repeated, recounted, declaimed |反义词：forgot, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recites poetry every evening. = 他每天晚上都背诵诗歌。
She recites the alphabet to her toddler. = 她给幼儿背诵字母表。

一般过去时:
The student recited the Gettysburg Address. = 学生背诵了葛底斯堡演说。
He recited a funny story at the party. = 他在派对上讲了一个有趣的故事。

现在完成时:
She has recited that poem many times. = 她已经背诵过那首诗很多次了。
He has recited the whole book from memory. = 他已经凭记忆背诵了整本书。

过去完成时:
They had recited the script before the performance. = 他们在演出前已经背诵过剧本了。
She had recited the pledge of allegiance many times before. = 她以前多次宣誓效忠。

将来时:
The child will recite a poem at the school assembly. = 孩子将在学校集会上朗诵一首诗。
He will recite his speech tomorrow. = 他明天将发表他的演讲。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have recited the passage perfectly. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能完美地背诵这段话了。
I wish I could recite Shakespeare as well as he can. = 我希望我能像他一样流利地背诵莎士比亚的作品。

被动语态:
The poem was recited by the young girl. = 这首诗是由小女孩朗诵的。
The speech was recited with great passion. = 这篇演讲充满激情地被朗诵了。

疑问句:
Did you recite the poem in class? = 你在课堂上背诵了这首诗吗？
Will she recite the lines from the play? = 她会背诵戏剧中的台词吗？

否定句:
He didn't recite the whole poem. = 他没有背诵整首诗。
She didn't recite the answer correctly. = 她没有正确地背诵答案。
