osmogene:/ˈɒzmədʒiːn/| n. |渗透基因，诱导渗透压变化的基因|A gene that induces changes in osmotic pressure;  a gene whose expression is directly or indirectly affected by changes in osmotic pressure.  Osmogenes are crucial for organism survival under osmotic stress. |Example: Studies on osmogenes in halophilic archaea reveal mechanisms for adaptation to hypersaline environments.  渗透基因在嗜盐古菌中的研究揭示了适应高盐环境的机制。|近义词：osmoregulatory gene (渗透调节基因)|反义词：N/A|名词
