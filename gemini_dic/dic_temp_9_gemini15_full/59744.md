ecological:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|生态的；生态学的|Relating to or concerned with the relation of living organisms to one another and to their physical surroundings.|例句：The ecological impact of the oil spill was devastating.  石油泄漏对生态环境造成了毁灭性的影响。
例句：We need to adopt more ecological practices in agriculture. 我们需要在农业中采取更环保的做法。|近义词：environmental, biological|反义词：artificial, unnatural
