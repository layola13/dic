flickering: /ˈflɪkərɪŋ/ | adj. | 闪烁的；摇曳的；忽明忽暗的 |  Characterized by intermittent light; wavering or unsteady.  It describes something that shines or burns with an unsteady light, often due to a weak or intermittent power source. It can also describe something that is unsteady or wavering in its appearance or existence. |

例句：

The candlelight cast flickering shadows on the wall.  烛光在墙上投下闪烁的阴影。

The flickering screen made it difficult to watch the movie. 闪烁的屏幕使得看电影很困难。


近义词：wavering, unsteady, intermittent, intermittent, fluctuating, shimmering


反义词：steady, constant, stable, unwavering


词性：形容词
