circumspection:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈspekʃən/ | n. | 谨慎；周到；考虑周全 | Careful consideration of all circumstances before acting; caution.  It involves thinking carefully about the possible consequences of one's actions before making a decision. | Example:  The general proceeded with circumspection, knowing a hasty move could cost many lives.  将军谨慎行事，知道轻率的行动可能会付出许多生命的代价。 |近义词：caution, prudence, wariness, deliberation | 反义词：recklessness, impulsiveness, carelessness |
