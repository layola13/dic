half-dazed:/ˌhɑːfˈdeɪzd/| adj.|半昏迷的，迷迷糊糊的|Partially dazed or confused; in a state between being fully awake and fully asleep.  It implies a state of mental confusion or disorientation, often resulting from shock, fatigue, or a blow to the head.|例句：After the car crash, she was half-dazed and couldn't remember what happened.  车祸后，她迷迷糊糊的，记不起发生了什么事。|例句：The loud noise left him half-dazed and disoriented.  巨大的噪音使他感到迷迷糊糊且迷失方向。|近义词：disoriented, confused, dazed, bewildered, stunned|反义词：alert, aware, clear-headed|形容词
