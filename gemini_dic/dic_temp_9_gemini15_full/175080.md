radiotelegraphy:/ˌreɪdioʊtəˈlɛɡrəfi/| n. |无线电报；无线电通信|The transmission of messages by means of radio waves.  It is a form of wireless communication that predates radio broadcasting and modern telecommunications.  Radiotelegraphy uses Morse code to transmit messages. | Example:  Early maritime communication relied heavily on radiotelegraphy. = 早期的海上通信严重依赖无线电报。

The invention of radiotelegraphy revolutionized long-distance communication. = 无线电报的发明彻底改变了长途通信。|近义词：wireless telegraphy, radio communication |反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable)|名词
