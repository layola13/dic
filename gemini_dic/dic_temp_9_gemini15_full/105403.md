kidf:/kɪdf/| n.|孩子；小孩|A young person, especially one who is not yet old enough to be considered an adult.|例句：The kids are playing in the park.孩子们在公园里玩耍。|近义词：child, youngster, minor|反义词：adult, grown-up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The kid plays in the park every day. = 这个孩子每天都在公园里玩耍。
The kids often go to school by bus. = 孩子们经常乘公共汽车去上学。


一般过去时:
The kid played with his toys yesterday. = 孩子昨天玩了他的玩具。
The kids went to the zoo last weekend. = 孩子们上个周末去了动物园。


现在完成时:
The kid has finished his homework. = 孩子已经完成了他的家庭作业。
The kids have been to the beach many times. = 孩子们去过海滩很多次了。


过去完成时:
The kid had already eaten before his parents came home. = 孩子在他父母回家之前已经吃过了。
The kids had finished their project before the deadline. = 孩子们在截止日期之前完成了他们的项目。


将来时:
The kid will go to school next year. = 孩子明年将去上学。
The kids will visit their grandparents this summer. = 孩子们今年夏天将去看望他们的祖父母。


虚拟语气:
If the kid were older, he could go to the movies alone. = 如果孩子再大一点，他就可以独自去看电影了。
I wish the kids were more helpful around the house. = 我希望孩子们在家里能更乐于助人。


被动语态:
The kid was praised by his teacher. = 孩子受到了老师的表扬。
The toys were cleaned by the kids. = 玩具被孩子们打扫干净了。


疑问句:
Is the kid happy? = 孩子开心吗？
Are the kids ready to go? = 孩子们准备好出发了吗？


否定句:
The kid doesn't like vegetables. = 孩子不喜欢蔬菜。
The kids didn't go to the party. = 孩子们没有去参加聚会。
