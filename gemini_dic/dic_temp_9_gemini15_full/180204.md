reforwarded:/ˌriːˈfɔːrwəːdɪd/| v. |再次转发，重新转发|The act of forwarding something again, typically an email or message.  It implies that the item was already forwarded once before.|例句：I reforwarded the email to my manager. = 我再次将邮件转发给了我的经理。

例句：He reforwarded the important document to the relevant department. = 他将重要文件再次转发给了相关部门。


近义词：resubmitted, retransmitted|反义词：deleted, withdrawn|词性:动词
