overgrazing:/ˈoʊvərˌɡreɪzɪŋ/ | n. |过度放牧 |The practice of grazing too many animals for too long on a certain area of land, resulting in damage to the vegetation and soil.  Overgrazing can lead to desertification and loss of biodiversity. | Example: Overgrazing in the region has led to soil erosion and the loss of valuable pastureland.  过度放牧导致该地区土壤侵蚀和宝贵牧场的流失。|近义词：overstocking,  desertification (as a result)|反义词：sustainable grazing,  rotational grazing
