humbugged:/ˈhʌmbʌɡd/ | v. |欺骗，愚弄 | To deceive or trick someone.  |  He humbugged me into buying a useless gadget. 他骗我买了一个无用的玩意儿。  | 近义词：deceived, tricked, cheated, hoodwinked | 反义词：helped, assisted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They humbug their customers with false promises. = 他们用虚假承诺欺骗顾客。
He humbugs his friends into believing his lies. = 他骗他的朋友相信他的谎言。


一般过去时:
The salesman humbugged me into buying an expensive car. = 销售员骗我买了一辆昂贵的汽车。
She humbugged her parents about where she was going. = 她骗了她的父母关于她要去哪里。


现在完成时:
He has humbugged his way to the top of the company. = 他通过欺骗手段爬到了公司的高层。
They have humbugged us for years. = 他们欺骗了我们多年。


过去完成时:
I realized that I had been humbugged after I paid for the product. = 付款后我才意识到自己被骗了。
She discovered that she had been humbugged by her so-called friend. = 她发现自己被所谓的的朋友欺骗了。


将来时:
He will humbug you if you're not careful. = 如果你不小心，他会欺骗你。
They will humbug the investors with their false claims. = 他们会用虚假说法欺骗投资者。


虚拟语气:
If he had not humbugged me, I would have been rich. = 如果他没有欺骗我，我就富有。
I wish I hadn't been humbugged by that salesman. = 我真希望我没有被那个销售员骗了。


被动语态:
Many people have been humbugged by online scams. = 许多人被网络诈骗所欺骗。
He was humbugged into investing his life savings. = 他被骗光了毕生的积蓄。


疑问句:
Were you humbugged by the con artist? = 你被那个骗子骗了吗？
Has anyone been humbugged by this company before? = 之前有谁被这家公司骗过吗？


否定句:
I wasn't humbugged; I knew what I was doing. = 我没有被骗；我知道我在做什么。
They haven't humbugged me yet. = 他们还没骗我。



PHRASE->
be humbugged: 被骗
get humbugged: 被愚弄
humbug someone into doing something: 骗某人做某事
humbug someone out of something: 骗取某人的某物
a humbug: 骗子 (也可指虚伪的人或事)
