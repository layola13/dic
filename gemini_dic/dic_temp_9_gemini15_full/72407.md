fluently:/ˈfluːəntli/ | adv. |流利地，流畅地 |  speaking or writing easily and smoothly.  It describes the manner in which something is done, particularly speaking or writing, indicating ease and smoothness. |She speaks English fluently. 她英语说得流利。|  smoothly, effortlessly, readily | haltingly, hesitantly, stumblingly | 副词
