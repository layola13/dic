roaringest:/ˈrɔːrɪŋɪst/| adj.|最喧闹的，最轰鸣的 |  Superlative form of "roaring," indicating the highest degree of roaring or loudness. It describes something that is extremely loud, boisterous, or turbulent.  |例句：The roaringest applause followed his incredible performance.  他精彩的表演赢得了最热烈的掌声。
The roaringest engine I've ever heard was on that old truck. 我听过的最响的发动机是那辆旧卡车上的。|近义词：loudest, noisiest, most boisterous |反义词：quietest, stillest
