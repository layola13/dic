mustache:/məˈstæʃ/| n. |胡子(唇上之须)|A mustache is the hair growing on the upper lip of a man's face.  It can be styled in various ways, from neatly trimmed to long and flowing. |He has a magnificent handlebar mustache.|近义词：moustache|反义词： beardless


USAGE->

He has a thick mustache. = 他留着浓密的胡子。
My brother grows a mustache. = 我弟弟留起了胡子。


一般过去时:
He shaved off his mustache yesterday. = 他昨天刮掉了胡子。
