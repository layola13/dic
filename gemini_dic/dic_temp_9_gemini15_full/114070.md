magneto:/mæɡˈniːtoʊ/| n. |磁电机，磁发电机|A magneto is a type of electrical generator that uses permanent magnets to create an electric current.  It's often used in older vehicles and small engines to provide ignition. |例句：The old lawnmower's magneto needed replacing. 这台旧割草机的磁电机需要更换。|例句：The aircraft used a magneto for its ignition system. 这架飞机使用磁电机作为其点火系统。|近义词：generator, alternator (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but terms describing devices that don't use magnets for electricity generation could be considered antonyms in a broad sense. |名词
