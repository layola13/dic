poets:/ˈpoʊɪts/|/ˈpəʊɪts/| n. |诗人 |A poet is a person who writes poetry.  Poets use language in a creative and expressive way to evoke emotions, tell stories, or explore ideas.  They may experiment with different forms, styles, and techniques to achieve their artistic vision.|Example:  Shakespeare is considered one of the greatest poets in the English language.莎士比亚被认为是英语中最伟大的诗人之一。


The Romantic poets of the 19th century revolutionized the way poetry was written.  19世纪的浪漫主义诗人彻底改变了诗歌的写作方式。|近义词：verse-writers, lyricists, rhymers |反义词：prosaist
