rued:/ruːd/| v.|后悔，悔恨|To feel regret or remorse about (something that one has done or allowed to happen).|He rued the day he had ever met her. 他后悔那天见到她。|regret, repent|rejoice, celebrate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rue my hasty decision. = 我后悔我草率的决定。
She rues her past mistakes. = 她后悔她过去的错误。


一般过去时:
He rued his missed opportunity. = 他后悔错过了机会。
They rued their silence. = 他们后悔他们的沉默。


现在完成时:
I have rued this choice for years. = 多年来我一直后悔这个选择。
She has rued her impulsiveness. = 她一直后悔自己的冲动。


过去完成时:
He had rued his actions before he confessed. = 他在坦白之前就已经后悔自己的所作所为。
They had rued their decision before the consequences unfolded. = 在后果显现之前，他们就已经后悔他们的决定了。


将来时:
I will rue this day if it doesn't go well. = 如果事情进展不顺利，我会后悔这一天的。
She will rue her inaction later. = 她以后会后悔她的不作为。


虚拟语气:
I would rue the day if I failed. = 如果我失败了，我会后悔这一天的。
If I had known better, I wouldn't have done it; I would rue my actions. = 如果我知道得更好，我不会那样做的；我会后悔我的行为。


被动语态:
His actions were rued by many. = 许多人后悔他的行为。
The missed chance was rued by the team. = 球队后悔错过了机会。


疑问句:
Do you rue your decision? = 你后悔你的决定吗？
Does she rue her choices? = 她后悔她的选择吗？


否定句:
I don't rue my decision. = 我不后悔我的决定。
She doesn't rue her actions. = 她不后悔她的行为。


PHRASE->
rue the day = 后悔某一天
ruefully regret = 悲痛地后悔
deeply rue = 深深后悔
bitterly rue = 痛苦地后悔
secretly rue = 暗自后悔
