glutinous:/ˈɡluːtɪnəs/| adj.|粘性的；胶粘的；糯米的 | Sticky; adhesive; resembling glue; pertaining to or made from glutinous rice.  The word refers to a substance's stickiness or the quality of being glue-like.  It can also describe rice that is particularly sticky when cooked. |例句：The glutinous rice cake was so delicious. 这糯米糕真好吃。

例句：The glue was very glutinous and difficult to remove. 这个胶水非常粘稠，很难去除。


近义词：sticky, adhesive, viscous, gummy, pasty

反义词：dry, crisp, brittle


词性：形容词
