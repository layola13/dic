repute:/ˈrɛpjʊt/ | n. | 名声，名誉 |  The general opinion of someone or something.  It refers to the estimation in which someone or something is held, especially by the public. |例句：He has a reputation for honesty. 他以诚实著称。

例句：The company enjoys a good repute among its customers. 这家公司在客户中享有良好的声誉。

近义词：fame, renown, reputation, standing

反义词：disrepute, infamy, ill-repute


词性：名词
