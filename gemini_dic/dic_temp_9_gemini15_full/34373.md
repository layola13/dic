classiest:/ˈklæsɪəst/| adj.|最优雅的，最上品的|Most elegant or refined.  It is the superlative form of "classy."|例句：Her classiest outfit was a simple black dress. = 她最优雅的服装是一件简单的黑色连衣裙。
例句：The classiest restaurant in town is known for its exquisite cuisine. = 城里最上品的餐厅以其精致的菜肴而闻名。|近义词：most elegant, most stylish, most sophisticated|反义词：tackiest, cheapest, most vulgar
