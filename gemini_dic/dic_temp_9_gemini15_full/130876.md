nonarguments:/ˌnɒnˈɑːɡjumənts/ | n. |非论证，非论辩 |Things that are not arguments; statements or expressions that lack the structure or characteristics of a logical argument.  They may be assertions, opinions, or irrelevant statements. | Example:  His responses were mostly nonarguments, filled with emotional outbursts rather than reasoned counterpoints. 他回应多半是非论证，充满情绪爆发而非理性的反驳。|近义词：irrelevancies, non sequiturs, red herrings |反义词：arguments, reasoning, proofs |
