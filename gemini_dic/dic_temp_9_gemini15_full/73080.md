footstalk:/ˈfʊtˌstɔːk/| n. |叶柄；菌柄|A slender stalk that supports a plant structure, such as a leaf or flower, or a fungus cap.|The sunflower's footstalk was thick and strong.|近义词：petiole, peduncle|反义词：无
