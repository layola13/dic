re-performing:/ˌriːpərˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| v.|再次表演；重新演出|The act of performing something again, often with improvements or changes.  It implies a previous performance and a subsequent repetition, possibly with modifications.|例句：The band is re-performing their hit song at the festival. 乐队将在音乐节上再次演奏他们的热门歌曲。

例句：The actress is re-performing the role after a long break.  这位女演员在长时间休息后再次扮演这个角色。


近义词：repeating, replaying, re-enacting|反义词：premiering, debuting |词性:动词
