contaminating: /kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 污染；弄脏 | 使……受污染；弄脏；感染 | The factory was contaminating the river with its waste.  这家工厂的废物正在污染河流。 | 近义词：polluting, polluting, infecting | 反义词：purifying, cleansing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory contaminates the river. = 工厂正在污染河流。
He is contaminating the food with bacteria. = 他正在用细菌污染食物。


一般过去时:
The oil spill contaminated the ocean. =漏油污染了海洋。
They contaminated the water supply. = 他们污染了供水系统。


现在完成时:
The soil has been contaminated by the chemicals. =土壤已经被化学物质污染了。
We have contaminated the air with pollution. = 我们已经用污染污染了空气。


过去完成时:
The river had already been contaminated before they arrived. =在他们到达之前，河流已经被污染了。
The experiment had contaminated the sample. =实验已经污染了样本。


将来时:
The new factory will contaminate the air. = 新工厂将污染空气。
They will contaminate the water supply if they are not careful. =如果他们不小心，他们将会污染供水系统。


虚拟语气:

I wish they hadn't contaminated the well. =我希望他们没有污染这口井。


被动语态:
The water was contaminated by the sewage. =水被污水污染了。
The food is being contaminated by harmful bacteria. =食物正在被有害细菌污染。


疑问句:

否定句:
The factory doesn't contaminate the air anymore. = 工厂不再污染空气了。

词源:
contaminating comes from the Latin word "contaminare", which means "to pollute" or "to defile".  The prefix "con-" means "together," and the root "-tamin-" is related to the word "taint," suggesting a sense of corruption or impurity.
