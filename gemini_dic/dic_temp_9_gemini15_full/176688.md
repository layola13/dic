reacquisitions:/ˌriːækwɪˈzɪʃənz/ | n. | 再收购；重新获得 | The process of reacquiring something previously owned or lost, or the items reacquired.  This often refers to a company buying back its own stock or acquiring a previously divested asset. | Example: The company announced a series of reacquisitions to strengthen its market position. 公司宣布了一系列再收购以增强其市场地位。

The company's reacquisitions were a key factor in its turnaround.  公司的再收购是其扭转局势的关键因素。|近义词：repurchase, reacquisition, recovery |反义词：divestiture, sale, disposal |名词
