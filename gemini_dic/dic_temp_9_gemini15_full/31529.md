charted:/ˈtʃɑːtɪd/ | v. adj. | 绘制…的图表；规划；记录；有详细记录的 | to make a map or chart of (an area of land or sea); to plan or record in detail  | 例句：The explorers charted the unknown coastline. 探险家们绘制了未知海岸线的图表。

The company charted a course for growth.  公司规划了一条增长之路。


近义词：mapped, plotted, planned, recorded | 反义词：uncharted, unplanned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chart the progress of the project every week. = 他们每周都会绘制项目的进度图表。
We chart our expenses carefully. = 我们仔细记录我们的开支。


一般过去时:
The sailors charted the new island. = 水手们绘制了新岛屿的地图。
She charted her family history. = 她记录了她家族的历史。


现在完成时:
They have charted a new course for the company. = 他们已经为公司规划了一条新的路线。
Scientists have charted the spread of the disease. = 科学家们已经绘制了这种疾病的传播图。


过去完成时:
They had charted the course before setting sail. = 他们在启航前已经规划好了航线。
The historians had charted the development of the city for centuries. = 历史学家们已经记录了这座城市几个世纪的发展历程。


将来时:
We will chart the stars tonight. = 我们今晚将绘制星图。
They will chart the progress of their research. = 他们将记录他们研究的进展。


虚拟语气:
If they had charted a different course, they might have succeeded. = 如果他们选择了不同的路线，他们也许会成功。
I wish I had charted my investments more carefully. = 我希望我当初更仔细地规划我的投资。


被动语态:
The area was charted by the explorers in 1849. = 这片区域在1849年被探险家们绘制了地图。
The progress of the project is charted weekly. = 项目的进度每周都会被记录。


疑问句:
Have you charted the progress of your studies? = 你绘制了你的学习进度图表吗？
Will they chart the new territory? = 他们会绘制新的领土地图吗？


否定句:
They did not chart the unknown waters. = 他们没有绘制未知水域的地图。
She hasn't charted her financial goals yet. = 她还没有规划她的财务目标。


PHRASE->
charted course:  规划好的路线
charted territory: 已知领域
charted waters:  熟悉的水域
charted area: 已绘制地图的区域
charted flight: 包机


词源:
From Old French *chartier, from charte "chart, map".  Ultimately from Latin charta "papyrus, paper".
