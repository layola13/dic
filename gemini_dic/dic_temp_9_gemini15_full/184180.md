The word "relates" does not exist in standard English.  It's likely a misspelling.  If you meant "relates",  here's the analysis:

relates:/rɪˈleɪts/ | v. | 叙述；关联；有关；认同 | To tell or describe something; to show or find a connection between things; to have a connection or relationship with someone or something; to understand and empathize with someone's feelings or experiences. | Examples: He relates the story of his childhood. 他讲述了他童年的故事。 The two ideas relate to each other. 这两个想法是相关的。 I can relate to your feelings. 我能理解你的感受。 |近义词：connects, associates, links, corresponds, empathizes |反义词：disconnects, separates, isolates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relates his experiences in the army. = 他讲述他在军队里的经历。
She relates the events of the day to her diary. = 她把当天发生的事情记在日记里。

一般过去时:
He related the story to the police. = 他向警方讲述了这个故事。
She related her childhood memories to her therapist. = 她向她的治疗师讲述了她童年的回忆。

现在完成时:
He has related his story many times. = 他已经多次讲述了他的故事。
She has related the findings of her research in a report. = 她在一份报告中叙述了她的研究结果。

过去完成时:
He had related the incident before the meeting. = 他在会议之前讲述了这件事。
She had related her experiences to her friends before arriving. = 她到达之前已经向她的朋友们讲述了她的经历。

将来时:
He will relate his experiences to the students. = 他将向学生们讲述他的经历。
She will relate the key findings in her presentation. = 她将在她的演示文稿中讲述关键发现。

虚拟语气:
If he related his story honestly, people would believe him. = 如果他诚实地讲述他的故事，人们就会相信他。
I wish she had related the details more clearly. = 我希望她能更清楚地讲述细节。

被动语态:
The story was related to the audience with great enthusiasm. = 这个故事以极大的热情讲述给了观众。
The findings have been related in a detailed report. = 研究结果已在一份详细的报告中进行了说明。

疑问句:
How does he relate to his colleagues? = 他如何与同事相处？
Does this theory relate to the current situation? = 这一理论与当前形势有关吗？

否定句:
He didn't relate the full story. = 他没有把整个故事讲完。
The two concepts don't relate to each other. = 这两个概念没有关联。


PHRASE->
relate to (someone/something): 与…相关；理解…
relate to (a story/event): 讲述…；叙述…
be related to: 与…有关；与…有亲戚关系
relate a story: 讲述一个故事
in relation to: 关于；至于


注意事项:

音标为英式发音。美式发音也类似，只是重音位置可能略有不同。
