filamentous:/ˌfɪləˈmɛntəs/ | adj. |丝状的，细丝状的 | Consisting of or resembling filaments; threadlike.  The term refers to structures that are long, thin, and thread-like.  It is often used to describe biological structures, such as algae or fungi, but can also be applied to other materials. | Example:  The bacteria formed a filamentous colony.  细菌形成了丝状菌落。 The artist used filamentous materials to create a unique texture.  艺术家使用丝状材料创造了一种独特的纹理。|近义词：threadlike, thread-shaped, fibrous |反义词：massive, bulky, chunky
