/moˈtʃila/ | n. | 背包，书包 | A mochila is a bag, typically made of fabric or leather, used to carry belongings on one's back. It often has straps that go over the shoulders.  |  I packed my books and lunch in my mochila. 我把书和午餐都装进了我的背包。  She bought a new mochila for school. 她买了一个新的书包上学。 | knapsack, rucksack, backpack | suitcase, handbag | 名词
