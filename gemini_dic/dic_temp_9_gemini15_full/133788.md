nongenetic: /ˌnɑːnˌdʒɛˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非遗传的 | Not relating to or caused by genes.  This adjective describes traits, characteristics, or diseases that are not passed down from parents to offspring through genetic material.  | Example:  The researcher attributed the observed differences to nongenetic factors, such as diet and environment.  研究人员将观察到的差异归因于非遗传因素，例如饮食和环境。


Example: The study showed that the observed phenotype was due to nongenetic factors. 这项研究表明，观察到的表型是由于非遗传因素造成的。


近义词: acquired, environmental, nonhereditary | 反义词: genetic, hereditary, inherited
