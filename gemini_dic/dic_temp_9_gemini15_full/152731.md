peasants:/ˈpɛzənts/| n.|农民，乡下人|People who work on the land, especially those with low social status.|The peasants worked hard in the fields from sunrise to sunset. 农民们从日出到日落都在田里辛勤劳作。|farmers, rural people, agricultural workers|aristocrats, gentry, nobility|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The peasants cultivate their land. = 农民们耕种他们的土地。
Many peasants work long hours. = 许多农民工作时间很长。

一般过去时:
The peasants harvested their crops last week. = 农民们上周收割了他们的庄稼。
Peasants lived a hard life in the past. = 过去农民们生活很艰苦。

现在完成时:
The peasants have tilled the soil for generations. = 农民世世代代耕耘这片土地。
Many peasants have migrated to the cities. = 许多农民已经迁移到城市。

过去完成时:
The peasants had already planted the seeds before the rain came. = 雨来之前农民们已经播种了。
The peasants had suffered greatly before the revolution. = 革命前农民们遭受了巨大的痛苦。

将来时:
The peasants will plant rice next season. = 农民们下个季度将种植水稻。
The peasants will likely face challenges due to climate change. = 农民们可能会面临气候变化带来的挑战。

虚拟语气:
If the peasants had more land, they would produce more food. = 如果农民们有更多的土地，他们就能生产更多的食物。
I wish the peasants had better living conditions. = 我希望农民们有更好的生活条件。

被动语态:
The land was cultivated by the peasants. = 这片土地是由农民们耕种的。

疑问句:
