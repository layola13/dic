crockeries:/ˈkrɒkəriːz/| n. |陶器，瓦器|Pottery, earthenware, or china, especially a collection of such items.  The term often refers to tableware like plates, cups, and bowls.  |例句：She inherited a beautiful collection of crockeries from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一套漂亮的陶器。

例句：The antique shop sold a wide variety of crockeries, including teacups and dinner plates. 古董店出售各种各样的陶器，包括茶杯和餐盘。


近义词：pottery, china, earthenware, tableware


反义词：无


词性：名词
