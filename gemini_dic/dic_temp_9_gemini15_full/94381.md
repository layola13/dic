impleach:/ɪmˈpliːtʃ/| vt.|控告；弹劾；牵连；含蓄地表达|To formally accuse someone of wrongdoing, especially in a court of law or a formal setting.  It often carries a sense of serious accusation and involves a formal process.|例句：The senator was impleached for corruption. 参议员因腐败而被弹劾。

例句：The evidence impleached him in the crime.  证据将他牵连到这起犯罪中。


近义词：accuse, indict, impeach, charge|反义词：exonerate, acquit|动词
