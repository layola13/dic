limitation:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |限制，局限性 |The main limitation of this method is its cost.  这个方法的主要限制是它的成本。|近义词：restriction, constraint, boundary |反义词：abundance, freedom


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The budget limitations prevent us from expanding. = 预算限制阻止我们扩张。
This software has several limitations. = 这个软件有几个限制。

一般过去时:
Time limitations prevented us from finishing the project. = 时间限制阻止我们完成项目。
The limitations of the old system were apparent. = 旧系统的局限性很明显。


现在完成时:
The limitations of this approach have become clear. = 这种方法的局限性已经变得很清楚。
We have overcome many limitations in our research. = 我们在研究中克服了许多限制。


过去完成时:
We hadn't realised the limitations of the plan until it was too late. = 直到为时已晚，我们才意识到计划的局限性。
They had underestimated the limitations of the technology. = 他们低估了这项技术的局限性。


将来时:
Future limitations in funding will affect our progress. = 未来资金的限制将影响我们的进展。
We will address the limitations of the current system. = 我们将解决当前系统的局限性。


虚拟语气:
If there were no limitations, we could achieve more. = 如果没有限制，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish there were fewer limitations on our creativity. = 我希望我们创造力的限制更少。


被动语态:
The project was hampered by financial limitations. = 该项目受到了资金限制的阻碍。
Our progress is limited by technological limitations. = 我们的进展受到技术限制的限制。


疑问句:
What are the limitations of this design? = 这个设计的局限性是什么？

否定句:
