grandad:/ˈɡrændæd/| n. |爷爷；外公|A grandfather; a father's or mother's father.  The term is informal and often used affectionately.|例句：My grandad used to tell me stories about his childhood.  我的爷爷过去常常给我讲他童年时代的故事。

例句：I spent the summer with my grandad at his seaside cottage. 我在海边的爷爷小屋和他一起度过了夏天。


近义词：grandfather, grandpa, poppy (informal)


反义词：grandchild


词性：名词


USAGE->

My grandad lives in the countryside. = 我的爷爷住在乡下。
Grandad often visits us. = 爷爷经常来看我们。
