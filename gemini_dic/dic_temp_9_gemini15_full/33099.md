christening: /ˈkrɪsənɪŋ/ | n. | 洗礼，命名仪式 | The act of baptizing someone, especially an infant, into the Christian faith; a ceremony marking the giving of a name to a child.  |例句：The christening was a joyous occasion for the whole family. 洗礼对全家来说是一个快乐的场合。

例句：They had a christening ceremony for their baby daughter last Sunday.  他们上周日为他们的女儿举行了洗礼仪式。


近义词：baptism, dedication


反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider words suggesting lack of religious ceremony.
