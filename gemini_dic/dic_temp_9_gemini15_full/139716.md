ocelots:/ˈɒsəlɒts/ | n. |美洲豹;  |A medium-sized wild cat of the Americas, with a spotted or striped coat. Ocelots are known for their distinctive markings and nocturnal habits. They are skilled hunters, preying on small mammals, birds, and reptiles.  |例句：The ocelot is a beautiful and elusive creature of the rainforest. 美洲豹是热带雨林中美丽而难以捉摸的生物。


The ocelots were spotted near the river.  在河边发现了美洲豹。|近义词：leopard, jaguar, cat |反义词：无
