quizzer:/ˈkwɪzər/| n.|出题者；测验者；提问者|A person who asks questions, especially in a quiz or test.  Someone who frequently asks questions to test knowledge or understanding.|例句：The quizzer asked a series of difficult questions about history. 出题者问了一系列关于历史的难题。|例句：She's a tough quizzer; she always finds the gaps in your knowledge. 她是个严格的提问者；她总能找到你知识中的漏洞。|近义词：examiner, questioner, interrogator|反义词：examinee, contestant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quizzes the students every week. 他每周都考学生。
The teacher quizzes the class on new vocabulary. 老师考查班级的新词汇。


一般过去时:
She quizzed him on the details of the meeting. 她就会议的细节盘问了他。
The professor quizzed the students on the chapter they had read. 教授就学生们读过的章节考问了学生们。


现在完成时:
He has quizzed all the participants. 他已经考问了所有参与者。
They have quizzed the witnesses several times. 他们已经多次盘问了证人。


过去完成时:
She had quizzed the students before the test. 她在考试前考问了学生们。
They had quizzed the suspect thoroughly before releasing him. 他们在释放嫌疑人之前彻底盘问了他。


将来时:
He will quiz the students on the material next week. 他下周将就这些材料考问学生们。
The instructor will quiz the students before the final exam. 讲师将在期末考试前考查学生们。


虚拟语气:
If I were the quizzer, I would ask tougher questions. 如果我是出题者，我会问更难的问题。
I wish I had quizzed her more thoroughly on the subject. 我真希望我当时更彻底地盘问了她关于这个话题。


被动语态:
The students were quizzed on their knowledge of grammar. 学生们被考查了他们的语法知识。
The suspect was quizzed by the police for hours. 嫌疑人被警察盘问了几个小时。


疑问句:
Will you quiz us on the reading assignment? 你会考查我们阅读作业吗？
Who will be quizzing the contestants tonight? 今晚谁将考查参赛者？


否定句:
He didn't quiz the students on the material. 他没有就这些材料考问学生们。
She doesn't usually quiz her students so frequently. 她通常不会这么频繁地考查她的学生们。
