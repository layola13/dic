entermise:/ˈɛntəpraɪz/ | n. |企业；事业；进取心 |A project or undertaking, especially one that is difficult or ambitious; a business or company.  |例句：She showed great enterprise in starting her own business. 她创业展现了极大的进取心。|例句：The new enterprise requires significant investment. 这项新事业需要大量的投资。|近义词： undertaking, venture, project, business |反义词：inaction, lethargy


USAGE->
