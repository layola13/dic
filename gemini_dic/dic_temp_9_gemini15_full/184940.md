retargeted:/ˌriːˈtɑːrɡɪtɪd/ | adj. | 重新定位的，重新定向的 |  Having been targeted again;  subject to renewed targeting, especially in advertising.  This often refers to showing advertisements to people who have previously interacted with a company's website or products. | 例句：The retargeted ads showed me the same shoes I'd looked at last week. = 重新定位的广告向我展示了我上周看过的同一双鞋子。

The company used retargeted email campaigns to re-engage past customers. = 公司使用重新定位的电子邮件活动来重新吸引过去的客户。| 近义词：re-engaged, re-focused | 反义词：untargeted, ignored


USAGE->
