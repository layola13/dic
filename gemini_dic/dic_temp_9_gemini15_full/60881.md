elementality: /ˌɛləmənˈtælɪti/ | n. | 元素性；本质；元素属性 | The inherent nature or essence of something, especially in relation to its elemental or fundamental components.  It often refers to the control or manipulation of the classical elements (earth, air, fire, water) in fantasy settings or fictional universes. | 例句：The elementality of the spell was clearly fire, evidenced by the flames engulfing the target.  这个法术的元素属性显然是火，从吞噬目标的火焰可以看出。

The game's magic system is based on the elementality of different spells.  这个游戏的魔法系统基于不同法术的元素属性。


近义词：essence, nature, quality, substance | 反义词：complexity, sophistication
