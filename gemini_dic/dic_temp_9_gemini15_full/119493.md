micropayment:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌpeɪmənt/| n. |微支付，小额支付 |A micropayment is a financial transaction involving a very small sum of money, typically less than a dollar.  It's often used for digital goods or services. | Example:  Many websites use micropayments to charge for access to individual articles or pieces of content.  许多网站使用微支付来收取访问单个文章或内容片段的费用。|近义词：small payment, tiny payment |反义词：large payment, substantial payment
