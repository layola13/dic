protagonisms:/ˈproʊtæɡənɪzəm/| n. |主角地位；主角行为；主角作用|The quality or state of being a protagonist; the leading or central role in a situation or event.  It often refers to the actions and characteristics of a protagonist, influencing the plot's direction.|例句：The novel explores the protagonisms of a young woman navigating a patriarchal society. 这部小说探讨了一个年轻女子在父权社会中所扮演的主角地位。|例句：His protagonisms in the conflict led to a decisive victory. 他在冲突中的主角作用导致了决定性的胜利。|近义词：leadership, dominance, prominence|反义词：passivity, submissiveness, insignificance|名词
