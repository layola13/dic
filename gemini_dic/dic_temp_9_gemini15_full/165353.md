prejudge:/ˌpriːˈdʒʌdʒ/| vt.|预先判断;  prejudgment|对…预先作出判断|To form an opinion or make a judgment about (someone or something) before having enough information to do so fairly.|例句：It's unfair to prejudge a person based on their appearance.  基于外表来预先判断一个人是不公平的。


例句：Don't prejudge the situation until you have all the facts. 在你掌握所有事实之前，不要对情况妄下判断。


近义词：predetermine, prejudicate, anticipate


反义词：postjudge, fairly judge


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prejudges every situation. 他总是对每种情况预先下判断。
She prejudges people based on their background. 她根据人们的背景对他们进行预先判断。


一般过去时:
They prejudged the outcome of the election. 他们预先判断了选举的结果。
He prejudged her abilities before she even started the project. 他在她甚至还没有开始项目之前就预先判断了她的能力。


现在完成时:
I have prejudged many things in my life. 我一生中预先判断过许多事情。
She has prejudged him based on a single incident. 她根据一起孤立事件对他做出了预先判断。


过去完成时:
They had prejudged the defendant before the trial began. 在审判开始之前，他们就已经对被告做出了预先判断。
We had prejudged the difficulty of the task before we started. 在我们开始之前，就已经预先判断了任务的难度。


将来时:
I will not prejudge the results of the experiment. 我不会预先判断实验的结果。
They will prejudge the candidate's chances of winning. 他们会预先判断候选人获胜的几率。


虚拟语气:
If I had not prejudged the situation, I would have reacted differently. 如果我没有预先判断情况，我会采取不同的反应。
If I were to prejudge the competition, I would say they are the favorites. 如果我要预先判断比赛，我会说他们是热门。


被动语态:
The case was prejudged by the media. 这个案子被媒体预先判断了。
The new policy was prejudged by the public as ineffective. 新政策被公众预先判断为无效。


疑问句:
Did you prejudge the interviewee's qualifications? 你是否预先判断了面试者的资格？
Have they prejudged the impact of the new law? 他们是否预先判断了新法律的影响？


否定句:
I did not prejudge the situation. 我没有预先判断情况。
She did not prejudge his character based on rumors. 她没有根据谣言预先判断他的性格。
