dysodile:/ˈdɪsoʊdaɪl/| n. |褐煤，沥青煤 |A type of brown coal or lignite that is rich in bitumen and often has a fetid odor. It is a combustible sedimentary rock and a variety of brown coal.  |例句：Dysodile is a relatively uncommon type of coal. 褐煤是一种相对不常见的煤炭。  |近义词：lignite, brown coal |反义词：anthracite (hard coal) |名词
