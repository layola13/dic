frit:/frɪt/| n.|弗里特；熔块；釉料|Frit refers to a partially fused mixture of powdered glass and other materials, used in making glazes for ceramics and enamels for metal.  It's also a verb meaning to cook something until it's browned or fried. |例句：The ceramic artist carefully sifted the frit before mixing it with the glaze. 陶瓷艺术家在将弗里特与釉料混合之前仔细地筛选了它。The chef fritted the onions until they were golden brown. 厨师将洋葱煎至金棕色。|近义词：fritted, glaze, enamel|反义词：uncooked, raw
