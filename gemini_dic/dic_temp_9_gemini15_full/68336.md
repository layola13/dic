fall on deaf ears:/fɔːl ɒn def ɪəz/ | phrasal verb | 充耳不闻；不被理会；不被采纳 |  To be ignored or disregarded.  When something is said or done, but the person or people it is directed to do not react, listen, or take any notice.  |例句：My warnings about the dangers of the project fell on deaf ears. 我的关于项目危险性的警告被忽视了。

My pleas for help fell on deaf ears. 我的求助请求无人理会。


近义词：be ignored, be disregarded, go unheeded,  be met with indifference


反义词：be heeded, be listened to, be taken seriously


词性: phrasal verb
