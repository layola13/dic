deducted:/dɪˈdʌktɪd/| v. |扣除，减去 |to take away an amount or part from a total |The tax was deducted from my salary. 税款已从我的工资中扣除。  My insurance company deducted the cost of repairs from my settlement.我的保险公司从我的赔偿金中扣除了维修费用。| subtracted, subtracted from |added| 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company deducts taxes from my paycheck. = 公司从我的工资中扣除税款。
They deduct a small fee for each transaction. = 他们每次交易收取少量手续费。

一般过去时:
They deducted the cost of shipping from the total. = 他们从总额中扣除了运费。
The bank deducted the overdraft fee from my account. = 银行从我的账户中扣除了透支费用。

现在完成时:
They have deducted the expenses from the profit. = 他们已经从利润中扣除了支出。
The government has deducted the tax from my income. = 政府已经从我的收入中扣除了税款。

过去完成时:
After the deductions, they had less money than expected. = 扣除后，他们比预期的钱少。
The company had already deducted the bonuses before the announcement. = 公司在宣布之前就已经扣除了奖金。

将来时:
They will deduct the service charge from your bill. = 他们会从你的账单中扣除服务费。
The system will automatically deduct the membership fee. = 系统将自动扣除会员费。

虚拟语气:
If they deducted the full amount, I would have no money left. = 如果他们扣除全部金额，我就一分钱都没剩下了。
I wish they had not deducted so much from my pay. = 我希望他们不要从我的工资中扣除那么多。

被动语态:
The tax was deducted from my paycheck. = 税款已从我的工资中扣除。
The expenses were deducted from the budget. = 支出已从预算中扣除。

疑问句:

Have the taxes been deducted yet? = 税款已经扣除了吗？

否定句:
They did not deduct the discount from the price. = 他们没有从价格中扣除折扣。
The bank did not deduct any additional fees. = 银行没有收取任何额外费用。
