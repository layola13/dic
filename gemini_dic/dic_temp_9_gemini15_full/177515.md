reblooms:/ˌriːˈbluːmz/| v.|再次开花；重新繁荣|To bloom again; to flourish anew after a period of decline or dormancy.  |例句：The rose bushes rebloomed after the late spring rains.  玫瑰花丛在春末雨后再次盛开。

例句：The economy rebloomed after a period of recession. 经济在经历衰退后再次繁荣。

近义词：reflourish, revive, regenerate |反义词：wither, fade, decline|动词
