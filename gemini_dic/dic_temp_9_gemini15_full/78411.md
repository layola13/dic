get-it-off:/ˌɡɛtɪˈɒf/| phrasal verb|去掉，脱掉，摆脱|This phrase is informal and means to remove something quickly or forcefully, or to rid oneself of something undesirable. It can refer to physical objects or abstract problems.|例句：He got the mud off his shoes. 他把鞋上的泥巴弄掉了。

例句：She needs to get that worry off her chest. 她需要把那份担忧说出来。


近义词：remove, take off, get rid of, eliminate|反义词：put on, keep, retain|词性:phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They get the stickers off their car every week. = 他们每周都会把车上的贴纸撕掉。
He gets his worries off his chest by talking to his friend. = 他通过和朋友聊天来排解烦恼。

一般过去时:
She got the stain off the shirt. = 她把衬衫上的污渍弄掉了。
We got the project off our backs finally. = 我们终于完成了那个项目。

现在完成时:
I have gotten the mud off my boots. = 我已经把靴子上的泥巴弄干净了。
They have gotten the problem off their hands. = 他们已经解决了这个问题。

过去完成时:
He had gotten the bandage off before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，他已经把绷带拆掉了。
She had gotten the weight off before her vacation. = 在度假之前，她已经减掉了体重。

将来时:
I will get the price off the final bill. = 我会从最终账单中扣除这笔费用。
He will get the old wallpaper off the wall tomorrow. = 他明天会把墙上的旧壁纸撕掉。

虚拟语气:
If I had a tool, I would get the nail off easily. = 如果我有工具，我会很容易把钉子拔掉。
I wish I could get this feeling off my mind. = 我希望我能摆脱这种感觉。

被动语态:
The sticker was easily gotten off the box. = 贴纸很容易从盒子上撕下来。
The problem will be gotten off our hands soon. = 这个问题很快就会解决。

疑问句:
Can you get the blood off this shirt? = 你能把这件衬衫上的血迹弄掉吗？
How can I get this annoying song off my head? = 我怎样才能把这首烦人的歌从脑子里赶出去？

否定句:
I couldn't get the lid off the jar. = 我打不开罐子的盖子。
They haven't gotten the old paint off the woodwork yet. = 他们还没有把木制品上的旧油漆清除干净。
