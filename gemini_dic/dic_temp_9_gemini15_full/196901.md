shader:/ˈʃeɪdər/| n. |着色器，渲染器|A shader is a small program that runs on a graphics processing unit (GPU) to determine the color of each pixel in an image.  It's used extensively in computer graphics, video games, and other visual applications to create realistic and visually appealing effects.  Shaders manipulate the light, color, texture, and other visual properties of objects to produce the final rendered image.|Example: The game uses advanced shaders to create realistic lighting and shadows.  这个游戏使用高级着色器来创建逼真的灯光和阴影。|近义词：pixel shader, vertex shader, fragment shader|反义词：None
