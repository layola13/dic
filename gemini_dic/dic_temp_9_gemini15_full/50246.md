dependency:/dɪˈpɛndənsi/| n. |依赖，依靠；从属；附属物 |The state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else.  It can refer to a thing or a person. |例句：His dependency on drugs led to his downfall.  他的药物依赖导致了他的垮台。|例句：The country's economic dependency on tourism is a concern.  该国对旅游业的经济依赖令人担忧。|近义词：reliance, dependence, subjection |反义词：independence, autonomy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project's success depends on your contribution. = 项目的成功取决于你的贡献。
My happiness depends on my family's well-being. = 我的幸福取决于家人的安康。

一般过去时:
The old woman depended on her daughter for support. = 老妇人依靠女儿的支持生活。
The company's success depended on a strong marketing strategy. = 公司的成功取决于强大的营销策略。

现在完成时:
We have depended on this supplier for years. = 我们多年来一直依赖这家供应商。
She has developed a dependency on painkillers. = 她对止痛药产生了依赖。

过去完成时:
He had depended on his savings before finding a job. = 在找到工作之前，他一直依靠自己的积蓄。
The business had depended heavily on foreign investment. = 这家企业曾严重依赖外国投资。

将来时:
They will depend on the weather for their harvest. = 他们的收成将取决于天气。
The success of the mission will depend on careful planning. = 任务的成功取决于周密的计划。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't have this dependency, I would be much happier. = 如果我没有这种依赖，我会快乐得多。
I wish I didn't have such a strong dependency on caffeine. = 我真希望我不那么依赖咖啡因。

被动语态:
The outcome is depended on by many factors. = 结果取决于许多因素。
The final decision will be depended on the board's vote. = 最终决定将取决于董事会的投票。

疑问句:
Does your happiness depend on external factors? = 你的幸福取决于外部因素吗？
Does the system depend on a stable internet connection? = 该系统依赖稳定的互联网连接吗？

否定句:
The outcome does not depend on chance. = 结果不取决于偶然。
The project's success doesn't depend on a single person. = 项目的成功不依赖于一个人。
