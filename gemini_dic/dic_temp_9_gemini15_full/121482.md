mislactation:/ˌmɪsˌlækˈteɪʃən/| n. |乳汁分泌异常|The production of milk outside of normal lactation, such as in males or non-lactating females.| 例句：Mislactation can occur due to hormonal imbalances or certain medications. 乳汁分泌异常可能是由于荷尔蒙失衡或某些药物引起的。


例句：The doctor suspected a pituitary tumor as the cause of her mislactation. 医生怀疑她的乳汁分泌异常是由垂体肿瘤引起的。


近义词：galactorrhea (医学术语)


反义词：normal lactation


词性:名词
