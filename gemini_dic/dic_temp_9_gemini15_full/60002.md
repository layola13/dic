editorializer:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzər/ | n. | 专栏作家；喜欢发表社论的人 |A person who writes editorials or frequently expresses opinions in a way similar to editorials.  They often present their views forcefully and sometimes with bias. |例句：He's become a prolific editorializer on the issue of climate change, regularly contributing op-eds to various newspapers. 他已经成为气候变化问题上的多产专栏作家，定期向各种报纸投稿。|近义词：commentator, columnist, pundit |反义词：listener, reader (in the sense of passively receiving information)
