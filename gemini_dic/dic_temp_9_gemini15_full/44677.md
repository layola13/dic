crucifixion:/ˌkruːsɪˈfɪkʃən/| n. |钉死在十字架上；受难；酷刑|The act of fastening someone to a cross and leaving them to die.  It is a method of capital punishment and a significant event in Christian theology, representing the death of Jesus Christ. |例句：The crucifixion of Jesus is a central event in Christianity.  耶稣的受难是基督教的核心事件。

例句：His painting depicted the brutal crucifixion scene in vivid detail.他的画作以生动的细节描绘了残酷的钉十字架的场景。


近义词：impalement, execution, martyrdom

反义词：resurrection, salvation, liberation
