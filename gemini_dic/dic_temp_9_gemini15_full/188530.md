rugged:/ˈrʌɡɪd/| adj.|崎岖的；粗糙的；坚强的；耐用的；| strong and rough; not smooth or neat; hardy; durable; |例句：The terrain was rugged and difficult to navigate.  地形崎岖难行。

例句：He has a ruggedly handsome face. 他有一张粗犷英俊的脸。

近义词：rough, uneven, tough, sturdy, robust

反义词：smooth, even, delicate, fragile, weak


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The path is rugged. = 这条路很崎岖。
The fabric feels rugged. = 这布料摸起来很粗糙。

一般过去时:
The journey was rugged. = 这段旅程很艰辛。

现在完成时:
