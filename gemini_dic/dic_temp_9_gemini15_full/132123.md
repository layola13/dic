noncryptically:/ˌnɒnkrɪˈptɪkli/ | adv. | 不隐晦地；直截了当地 | In a way that is clear and straightforward, without being cryptic or ambiguous.  | 例句：He explained the instructions noncryptically, leaving no room for misinterpretation. 他直截了当地解释了说明，没有留下任何误解的余地。


例句：She answered the question noncryptically, stating the facts plainly. 她直截了当地回答了这个问题，清楚地陈述了事实。


近义词：clearly, plainly, explicitly, frankly, openly, directly


反义词：cryptically, ambiguously, obscurely, vaguely


词性：副词


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **cryptic**: adjective meaning "having a meaning that is mysterious or obscure"  (from Greek *kryptós* meaning "hidden")
* **-ally**: adverbial suffix


用法说明：

`noncryptically` is used to describe the manner in which something is said, written, or done. It emphasizes the absence of ambiguity or hidden meaning.  It's often used in contrast to situations where something is deliberately unclear or mysterious.

The word is relatively uncommon in everyday speech but is useful in more formal writing or when precision of communication is crucial.
