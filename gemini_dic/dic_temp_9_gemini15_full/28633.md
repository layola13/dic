cargo:/ˈkɑːrɡoʊ/| n. |货物，行李|Goods or merchandise carried on a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  The cargo ship is carrying tons of grain. 这艘货船运载着数吨谷物。|近义词：freight, load, shipment |反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ship carries a large cargo. = 这艘船运载大量的货物。
They transport cargo daily. = 他们每天运输货物。


一般过去时:
The truck carried a heavy cargo. = 卡车运载了重货物。
We unloaded the cargo yesterday. = 我们昨天卸了货。


现在完成时:
They have shipped the cargo. = 他们已经运送了货物。
The company has handled many cargos this year. = 今年该公司已经处理了许多货物。


过去完成时:
The plane had already unloaded its cargo. = 飞机已经卸下了它的货物。
They had transported the cargo before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经运送了货物。


将来时:
We will ship the cargo tomorrow. = 我们明天将运送货物。
The train will be carrying a valuable cargo. = 火车将运载宝贵的货物。


虚拟语气:
If the cargo were lighter, transportation would be easier. = 如果货物更轻，运输就会更容易。
I wish the cargo had arrived earlier. = 我希望货物早点到达。


被动语态:
The cargo was damaged during transit. = 货物在运输途中受损。
The cargo is being unloaded at the port. = 货物正在港口卸货。


疑问句:
What type of cargo is being shipped? = 正在运输什么类型的货物？
Where is the cargo destined for? = 货物运往何处？


否定句:
The ship did not carry any dangerous cargo. = 这艘船没有运载任何危险货物。
The cargo was not delivered on time. = 货物没有按时送达。
