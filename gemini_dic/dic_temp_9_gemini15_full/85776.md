hayfork:/ˈheɪfɔːrk/| n. |干草叉|A long-handled tool with two or more prongs, used for pitching or handling hay.|例句：The farmer used a hayfork to load the hay onto the wagon.  农民用干草叉把干草装上马车。
例句：He skillfully tossed the hay with the hayfork. 他熟练地用干草叉抛掷干草。|近义词：pitchfork|反义词:None.
