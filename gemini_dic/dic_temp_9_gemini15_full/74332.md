fragilities:/fræˈdʒɪlətiz/| n.|脆弱性；弱点；易碎性|The plural form of "fragility," referring to the state or quality of being fragile, easily broken or damaged, or weak and vulnerable.  It can refer to physical objects, systems, or people. |例句：The fragilities of the old bridge were evident in its crumbling structure.  这座古老桥梁的脆弱性在其崩塌的结构中显而易见。

例句：The company's financial fragilities were exposed during the recession.  公司在经济衰退期间暴露出财务上的弱点。


近义词：weaknesses, vulnerabilities, defects, shortcomings, frailties


反义词：strengths, robustnesses, resiliences


词性: 名词
