pro-Darwin: /ˌproʊˈdɑːrwɪn/ | adj. | 支持达尔文的；达尔文主义的 |  Supporting or in accordance with the theories of Charles Darwin, particularly his theory of evolution by natural selection.  |  例句：Many scientists hold pro-Darwin views on the origin of species. 许多科学家持有支持达尔文物种起源论的观点。

例句：He wrote a pro-Darwin essay arguing for the validity of natural selection. 他写了一篇支持达尔文的文章，论证自然选择的有效性。


近义词：Darwinian, evolutionist, selectionist | 反义词：anti-Darwinian, creationist,  anti-evolutionist | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms in common usage.  Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable.  The usage is always descriptive.
