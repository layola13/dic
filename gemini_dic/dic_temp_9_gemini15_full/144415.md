outsuck:/ˈaʊtˌsʌk/| v.| 差劲，糟糕透顶 | To be extremely bad or unpleasant; to be far inferior to something else.  This is a slang term and should be used cautiously. | 例句：That band totally outsucked last night. = 那个乐队昨晚演出糟透了。  The new software outsucks the old version. = 新软件比旧版本差远了。|近义词：suck, stink, be awful, be terrible |反义词: excel, rock, be amazing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That restaurant outsucks all the others in town. = 那家餐馆比镇上所有其他餐馆都差。
This plan outsucks the previous one. = 这个计划比之前的计划差劲。

一般过去时:
The movie outsucked my expectations. = 这部电影远不如我预期的好。
His performance outsucked everyone else's. = 他的表现比其他任何人都差。

现在完成时:
The weather has outsucked all predictions. = 天气比所有预测都要糟糕。
This project has outsucked all our previous attempts. = 这个项目比我们之前的任何尝试都要糟糕。

过去完成时:
The previous design had outsucked all other options. = 先前的设计比其他所有方案都要差。
The original plan had outsucked what was eventually implemented. = 最初的计划比最终实施的计划差。

将来时:
I think this concert will outsuck the last one. = 我认为这场音乐会将比上一场更糟。
The new system will likely outsuck the current one. = 新系统很可能不如目前的系统好。

虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't gone, it wouldn't have outsucked so much. = 要是我没去就好了，就不会那么糟糕了。
I wish the food hadn't outsucked our expectations. = 我希望食物没有辜负我们的期望。

被动语态:
The performance was outsucked by the previous one. = 表演不如之前的。
The product was outsucked by its competitors. = 该产品不如竞争对手的产品。

疑问句:
Does this new phone outsuck the old one? = 这款新手机比旧手机差吗？
Does the quality of this film outsuck the book? = 这部电影的质量比原著小说差吗？

否定句:
The game didn't outsuck my expectations; it was quite good. = 游戏并没有辜负我的期望，它相当不错。
The service didn't outsuck the food; both were terrible. = 服务和食物一样糟糕。
