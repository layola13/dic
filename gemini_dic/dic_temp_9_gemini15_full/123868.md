morassy:/ˈmɔːrəsi/| adj.|沼泽的；泥泞的；混乱的 |  Descriptive of something resembling a morass; soft, wet, and muddy ground; figuratively, something confusing or difficult to navigate.  | 例句：The morassy ground made walking difficult. = 沼泽地使得行走困难。

The morassy political situation made it difficult to predict the outcome of the election. = 混乱的政治局势使得难以预测选举结果。


近义词：marshy, swampy, boggy, muddy, miry, quagmire (n.)

反义词：dry, firm, solid, clear, straightforward

词性:形容词
