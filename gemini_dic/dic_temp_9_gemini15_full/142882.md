out-of-state:/ˌaʊt əvˈsteɪt/ | adj. |州外的；外州的 |  Referring to something or someone that is located outside of the state in question.  This term is primarily used in the United States. | Example: He received an out-of-state tuition waiver. 他获得了一项州外学费减免。  |近义词：non-resident, from another state | 反义词：in-state
