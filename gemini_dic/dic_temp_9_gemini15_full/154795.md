perturb:/pərˈtɜːrb/ | vt. |扰乱；使不安；使焦虑 | to disturb or make someone anxious | The news perturbed him greatly.  这个消息使他非常不安。  The unexpected noise perturbed the quiet atmosphere.  突如其来的噪音扰乱了宁静的气氛。| disturb, unsettle, agitate | calm, soothe, tranquilize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music perturbs the neighbours. = 吵闹的音乐扰乱了邻居们。
He perturbs easily. = 他很容易焦虑不安。


一般过去时:
The bad news perturbed her deeply. = 这个坏消息让她非常不安。
The sudden storm perturbed their travel plans. = 突如其来的暴风雨打乱了他们的旅行计划。


现在完成时:
Nothing has perturbed him all day. = 一整天都没什么事让他不安。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
