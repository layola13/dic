This term is primarily used in music and poetics to describe a lack of pleasing or harmonious sounds.  It refers to the discordant or jarring effect created by the juxtaposition of sounds that do not blend well together. |Example: The composer deliberately introduced nonconsonances to create a sense of unease and tension in the piece.  作曲家故意引入不和谐音，以在乐曲中营造不安和紧张感。|近义词：dissonance, discord, cacophony |反义词：consonance, harmony
