decarbonizers:/ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənaɪzərz/| n. |脱碳剂，减碳者|People or things that reduce or remove carbon dioxide from the atmosphere or industrial processes.  They are crucial in mitigating climate change. |Example:  Scientists are developing new decarbonizers to capture carbon emissions from power plants.  科学家们正在研发新的脱碳剂来捕捉发电厂的碳排放。

The development of effective decarbonizers is a key priority for many governments and corporations.  研发出有效的脱碳剂是许多政府和公司的一个关键优先事项。|近义词：carbon absorbers, carbon sequesters|反义词：carbon emitters, carbon producers|名词
