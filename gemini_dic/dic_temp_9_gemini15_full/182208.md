remonstrate:/rɪˈmɒnstreɪt/|/rɪˈmɑːnstreɪt/| vt.|抗议；规劝；反驳|To protest or argue against something; to reason earnestly with someone against something.|The workers remonstrated against the unfair treatment. 工人们抗议不公平待遇。|He remonstrated with his son about his bad behavior. 他规劝儿子不要有不良行为。|protest, object, expostulate|acquiesce, comply|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They remonstrate against the new policy. = 他们抗议这项新政策。
He remonstrates with his friend about his drinking habit. = 他规劝朋友改掉酗酒的习惯。

一般过去时:
The citizens remonstrated against the government's decision. = 公民们抗议政府的决定。
She remonstrated with her daughter about her choice of friends. = 她规劝女儿注意选择朋友。

现在完成时:
They have remonstrated repeatedly, but to no avail. = 他们一再抗议，但没有效果。
He has remonstrated with her many times about her spending habits. = 他多次规劝她改掉乱花钱的习惯。

过去完成时:
They had remonstrated before, but their concerns were ignored. = 他们以前就抗议过，但他们的担忧被忽视了。
She had remonstrated with him countless times about his carelessness. = 她无数次规劝他不要粗心大意。

将来时:
The students will remonstrate if the tuition fees are increased. = 如果学费上涨，学生们将会抗议。
He will remonstrate with his boss about the unfair workload. = 他将会向老板抗议不公平的工作量。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would remonstrate with him about his attitude. = 如果我是你，我会规劝他改掉他的态度。
I wish I had remonstrated sooner; perhaps things would be different. = 我真希望我早点抗议，也许情况会不一样。

被动语态:
The new law was remonstrated against by many people. = 这项新法律受到了许多人的抗议。
His bad habits were often remonstrated with, but to little effect. = 他经常被规劝改掉坏习惯，但效果甚微。

疑问句:
Did they remonstrate against the proposal? = 他们是否抗议了这项提案？
Will you remonstrate with him about his behavior? = 你会规劝他改掉他的行为吗？

否定句:
They did not remonstrate against the injustice. = 他们没有抗议这种不公正的行为。
He didn't remonstrate with her about her decision. = 他没有规劝她改变她的决定。
