pronouns:/ˈproʊnaʊnz/ | n. | 代词 | Pronouns are words that replace nouns or noun phrases. They are used to avoid repetition and make sentences more concise and natural.  They can refer to people, places, things, or ideas.  Different types of pronouns exist, including personal pronouns, possessive pronouns, reflexive pronouns, relative pronouns, interrogative pronouns, demonstrative pronouns, and indefinite pronouns. | Example: He went to the store.  (He replaces a noun like "John" or "the man"). She loves her new car. (She and her refer to a person and her possession).|近义词:substitutes|反义词:nouns


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a doctor. = 他是一名医生。
They are playing football. = 他们正在踢足球。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had more time. = 我希望我有更多时间。


被动语态:

否定句:
