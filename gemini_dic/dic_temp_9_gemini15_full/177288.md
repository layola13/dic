reassist:/ˌriːəˈsɪst/| vt.|再次帮助；重新协助|To assist again or anew; to help again.|例句：The rescue team had to reassist the injured climber after the initial aid proved insufficient.  救援队在初步援助证明不足后不得不再次帮助受伤的登山者。

例句：The teacher decided to reassist the struggling student with the difficult math problem. 老师决定再次帮助那个在数学难题上挣扎的学生。


近义词：re-aid, help again, support again|反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede|词性:动词
