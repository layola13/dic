mopers:/ˈmoʊpərz/| n.|闷闷不乐的人，消沉的人|People who are habitually gloomy or depressed.  They tend to be inactive and withdrawn. |例句：The mopers sat in the corner, avoiding eye contact. 闷闷不乐的人坐在角落里，避免眼神接触。
The constant rain seemed to turn everyone into mopers.  持续的雨似乎让每个人都变得郁郁寡欢。|近义词：dejected people, downcast individuals, glum people |反义词：cheerful people, upbeat individuals, jovial people
