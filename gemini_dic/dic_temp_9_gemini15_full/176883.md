reaggravates:/ˌriːˈæɡrəveɪts/| vt. |使恶化；使加剧；重新激怒|To make (a problem, bad situation, etc.) worse; to worsen or intensify.|The rain reaggravated the already flooded streets.  雨水使本已泛滥的街道更加糟糕。|His constant criticism reaggravates her anxieties. 他不断的批评加剧了她的焦虑。|worsen, exacerbate, intensify|improve, alleviate, soothe|动词


USAGE->

He constantly reaggravates the situation with his impulsive actions. 他不断地用冲动的行为使情况恶化。
