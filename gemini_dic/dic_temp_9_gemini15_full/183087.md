repopulates:/ˌriːˈpɒpjuleɪt/|/ˈriːpɑːpjuleɪt/| vt.|重新填充；使…重新住满；重新繁衍|To repopulate means to populate again; to re-establish a population in a place.  It often implies that the population was previously reduced or eliminated.|例句：The government is planning to repopulate the abandoned villages. 政府计划重新居住被遗弃的村庄。

The area was repopulated with native species after the fire.  火灾后，该地区重新被本地物种繁衍生息。|近义词：replenish, restock, repopulate, reseed|反义词：depopulate, evacuate, empty
