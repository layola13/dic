outblown: /ˌaʊtˈbloʊn/ | adj. | 被吹掉的；被吹散的；过时的 |  Something that is outblown has been blown away or dispersed by the wind, or is outdated and no longer relevant.  | The outblown leaves littered the street.  被吹落的树叶散落在街道上。The theory is now completely outblown. 现在这个理论已经完全过时了。 |  dispersed, scattered, obsolete |  intact, current, relevant | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind outblows the leaves. = 风把树叶吹走了。
The old ideas are outblown. = 旧观念已经过时了。

一般过去时:
The storm outblew all the candles. = 暴风雨吹灭了所有的蜡烛。

过去完成时:
