joggling:/ˈdʒɑːɡlɪŋ/ | n. | 杂耍；艰难地应付；|The act of juggling multiple tasks or responsibilities at the same time, often implying difficulty or stress.|例句：Juggling work and family responsibilities can be exhausting.  努力兼顾工作和家庭责任可能会让人筋疲力尽。|例句：He was juggling three balls in the air. 他正在空中抛接三个球。|近义词：juggling, balancing, managing |反义词： neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->

He joggles several projects at once. = 他同时进行着好几个项目。
She joggles her responsibilities effectively. = 她有效地处理着她的责任。

一般过去时:
He joggled the balls expertly. = 他熟练地抛接球。
