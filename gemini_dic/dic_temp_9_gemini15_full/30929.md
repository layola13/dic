chairing:/ˈtʃeərɪŋ/| v.|主持；担任主席 |The act of presiding over a meeting or organization.  |例句：She is chairing the meeting. 她正在主持会议。  |例句：He is chairing the committee. 他担任委员会主席。|近义词：presiding, leading, heading |反义词：participating (as a member, not the chair)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is chairing the committee meeting. 他正在主持委员会会议。
She chairs the board of directors. 她担任董事会主席。


一般过去时:
The professor chaired the debate. 教授主持了这场辩论。
She chaired the meeting last week. 她上周主持了会议。


现在完成时:
He has chaired numerous conferences this year. 他今年已经主持了许多会议。
She has chaired the organization for five years. 她已经担任该组织主席五年了。


过去完成时:
He had chaired the committee before she joined.  在她加入之前，他担任过该委员会主席。
They had chaired the event successfully in the past. 他们过去成功地主持过这个活动。


将来时:
Professor Smith will be chairing the upcoming symposium. 史密斯教授将主持即将举行的研讨会。
She will chair the next board meeting. 她将主持下一次董事会会议。


虚拟语气:
If she were chairing the meeting, things would be more organized. 如果她主持会议，事情会更有条理。
I wish I were chairing that committee; it sounds interesting. 我希望我能够主持那个委员会；听起来很有趣。


被动语态:
The meeting was chaired by the department head. 会议由系主任主持。
The event will be chaired by a renowned expert.  活动将由一位知名专家主持。


疑问句:
Who is chairing the conference? 谁主持这次会议？
Is he chairing the meeting tomorrow? 他明天主持会议吗？


否定句:
He is not chairing the meeting today. 他今天没有主持会议。
She did not chair the committee last year. 她去年没有担任该委员会主席。


PHRASE->
chairing a meeting 主持会议
chairing a committee 担任委员会主席
chairing a session 主持一个会议
chairing a board 主持董事会
chairing an event 主持一个活动
