misinterpreter:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrpətər/| n.|误解者，曲解者|A person who misinterprets something. Someone who understands or explains something incorrectly. |例句：The misinterpreter of the contract led to a costly dispute.  合同的误解者导致了一场代价高昂的纠纷。|近义词：wrong interpreter, misreader|反义词：accurate interpreter, correct explainer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misinterprets my intentions. 他误解了我的意图。
She misinterprets the data regularly. 她经常误解数据。


一般过去时:
He misinterpreted the map and got lost. 他误读了地图，迷路了。
They misinterpreted the instructions and failed the task. 他们误解了指示，任务失败了。


现在完成时:
I have misinterpreted your words. 我误解了你的话。
She has misinterpreted the poem's meaning. 她误解了这首诗的意思。


过去完成时:
He had misinterpreted the signal before the accident. 事故发生前他已经误解了信号。
They had misinterpreted the clues, leading to a dead end in the investigation. 他们误解了线索，导致调查陷入僵局。


将来时:
He will misinterpret the information again. 他还会再次误解信息。
They will misinterpret the situation if we don't clarify it. 如果我们不澄清，他们会误解情况。


虚拟语气:
If he had not misinterpreted the message, the problem would have been avoided. 如果他没有误解信息，问题本来是可以避免的。
I wish she hadn't misinterpreted my silence as rejection. 我真希望她不要把我的沉默误解为拒绝。


被动语态:
His words were misinterpreted by many. 他的话被很多人误解了。
The data was misinterpreted by the research team. 数据被研究团队误解了。


疑问句:
Did he misinterpret the question? 他是否误解了这个问题？
Could she have misinterpreted the instructions? 她有可能误解了指示吗？


否定句:
He did not misinterpret the instructions. 他没有误解指示。
She didn't misinterpret the meaning of the gesture. 她没有误解那个手势的意思。
