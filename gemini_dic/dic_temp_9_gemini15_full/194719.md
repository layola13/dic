self-worthiness:/ˌsɛlfˈwɜːrðinəs/| n. |自我价值感，自尊，自我肯定|A feeling of being worthy of respect and having value.  It encompasses a positive self-image, confidence in one's abilities, and a belief in one's right to happiness and success.  It is crucial for mental health and well-being.|例句：Developing a strong sense of self-worthiness is essential for a fulfilling life.  建立强大的自我价值感对于过上充实的生活至关重要。

例句：Her self-worthiness grew as she achieved her goals. 随着她实现目标，她的自我价值感也增强了。|近义词：self-esteem, self-respect, self-regard |反义词：self-loathing, low self-esteem, worthlessness |词性：名词
