merges:/mɜːrdʒɪz/ | v. | 合并；融合 | To combine or blend together.  |例句：The two companies merged to form a larger corporation. 两家公司合并组成了一家更大的公司。

The river merges with the ocean.  河流与海洋汇合。|近义词：combine, unite, consolidate, amalgamate |反义词：separate, divide, split


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river merges with the sea. = 河流与大海汇合。
Two companies often merge to increase market share. = 两家公司经常合并以增加市场份额。

一般过去时:
They merged their businesses last year. = 他们去年合并了他们的企业。
The two streams merged into a larger river. = 两条小溪汇成一条大河。

现在完成时:
Several small companies have merged into a large conglomerate. = 几家小公司已经合并成一个大型企业集团。
The two teams have merged their ideas into a single proposal. = 这两个团队已将他们的想法合并到一个提案中。

过去完成时:
By the time the merger was announced, the two companies had already merged their marketing departments. = 在合并宣布之前，两家公司已经合并了他们的市场营销部门。
Before the acquisition, the smaller firm had already merged with another company. = 在收购之前，这家较小的公司已经与另一家公司合并。

将来时:
The two companies will merge next quarter. = 这两家公司将在下一季度合并。
We expect that the two departments will merge in the near future. = 我们预计这两个部门将在不久的将来合并。

虚拟语气:
If the two companies merged, they would have a larger market share. = 如果这两家公司合并，它们将拥有更大的市场份额。
It would be beneficial if the two departments merged their resources. = 如果两个部门合并资源，将是有益的。

被动语态:
The two companies were merged last year. = 这两家公司去年合并了。
The different ideas were merged into a cohesive plan. = 不同的想法被融合成一个连贯的计划。

疑问句:
Will the two teams merge their efforts? = 两个团队会合并他们的努力吗？
Do you think these two companies will merge? = 你认为这两家公司会合并吗？

否定句:
The two companies did not merge. = 这两家公司没有合并。
The merger negotiations did not lead to a successful merge. = 合并谈判没有导致成功的合并。
