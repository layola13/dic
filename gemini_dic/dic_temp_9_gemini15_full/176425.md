re-initiate:/ˌriːɪˈnɪʃieɪt/| vt.|重新开始；再次启动|To begin or start something again after it has been stopped or interrupted.|例句：The company decided to re-initiate the project after a period of inactivity. 公司决定在一段时间的停顿后重新启动该项目。|例句：After the power outage, we had to re-initiate the computer system. 停电后，我们不得不重新启动计算机系统。|近义词：restart, recommence, resume|反义词：terminate, end, cease|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-initiate the negotiations every year. = 他们每年都重新开始谈判。
We re-initiate the process after each failure. = 我们每次失败后都会重新启动流程。

一般过去时:
The government re-initiated the economic reform program. = 政府重新启动了经济改革方案。
She re-initiated contact with her old friend. = 她重新与老朋友取得联系。

现在完成时:
They have re-initiated the search for the missing child. = 他们已经重新开始寻找失踪的孩子。
We have re-initiated the project with a new team. = 我们已经用一个新的团队重新启动了这个项目。

过去完成时:
They had re-initiated the plan before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经重新启动了计划。
She had re-initiated the conversation after a long pause. = 在长时间的停顿后，她重新开始了谈话。

将来时:

We will re-initiate the program after reviewing the results. = 我们将在审查结果后重新启动该程序。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
