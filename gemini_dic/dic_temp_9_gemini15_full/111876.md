logoff:/ˈlɒɡɒf/ | vt. | 注销，登出 | To end a session on a computer system or network.  | Example:  Remember to log off before leaving your computer.  记住离开电脑前要注销。 |近义词：sign out, disconnect, shut down | 反义词：log on, log in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He logs off his computer every night. = 他每天晚上都注销电脑。
She logs off the network after work. = 她下班后注销网络。


一般过去时:
I logged off the system after finishing my work. = 我完成工作后注销了系统。
They logged off their accounts before leaving the office. = 他们离开办公室前注销了他们的账户。


现在完成时:
He has logged off his computer already. = 他已经注销了他的电脑。
She has logged off the server for the night. = 她已经注销了服务器过夜。


过去完成时:
I had logged off before the power outage. = 断电之前我已经注销了。
They had logged off all the computers before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经注销了所有电脑。


将来时:
I will log off when I finish this report. = 我完成这份报告后就会注销。
She will log off the system at 5 pm. = 她下午5点将注销系统。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would log off immediately. = 如果我是你，我会立即注销。
I wish I had logged off earlier. = 我希望我早点注销。


被动语态:
The system was logged off automatically. = 系统自动注销了。
All accounts will be logged off at midnight. = 所有账户将在午夜注销。


疑问句:
Have you logged off yet? = 你注销了吗？
Did he log off the server? = 他注销了服务器吗？


否定句:
I didn't log off my computer last night. = 我昨晚没有注销我的电脑。
She hasn't logged off the network yet. = 她还没注销网络。


PHRASE->
log off the system = 注销系统
log off the network = 注销网络
log off the computer = 注销电脑
automatically log off = 自动注销
log off and on again = 注销并重新登录
