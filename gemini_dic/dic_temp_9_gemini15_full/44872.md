cry-babied:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbid/| v.|哭哭啼啼地抱怨；无理取闹|To behave in a childish and overly emotional way, complaining excessively and whining.  It implies an excessive display of emotion and a lack of maturity in handling problems.|例句：He cry-babied about the unfairness of the test after failing it. 他考试不及格后，哭哭啼啼地抱怨考试不公平。

例句：Stop cry-babying and deal with the problem like an adult. 别像个孩子一样哭哭啼啼的了，像个成年人一样解决问题吧。


近义词：whined, complained, sulked,  fuss,  fretted


反义词：remained calm, accepted responsibility,  faced the challenge


词性:动词
