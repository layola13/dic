psychoanalysis:/ˌsaɪkoʊəˈnæləsɪs/ | n. |精神分析|The study of the unconscious mind and its influence on behavior and experience, especially through techniques such as dream interpretation and free association.  It aims to treat mental disorders by investigating the interaction of conscious and unconscious elements in the mind and bringing repressed fears and conflicts into the conscious mind by techniques such as dream interpretation and free association.|例句：His depression was treated through psychoanalysis.  他的抑郁症通过精神分析治疗。|近义词：psychotherapy, psychodynamic therapy |反义词：N/A
