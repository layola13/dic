hang-ups:/ˈhæŋˌʌps/| n.|难以克服的障碍；心理障碍；挂断电话；|A hang-up is something that prevents someone from doing something or achieving something, often a psychological problem or inhibition. It can also refer to the act of disconnecting a phone call.|例句：He has a real hang-up about commitment. 他对承诺有很大的心理障碍。

例句：She hung up the phone in a huff. 她生气地挂断了电话。|近义词：inhibition, obsession, complex, psychological block|反义词：ease, comfort, confidence


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He has many hang-ups about his appearance. 他对自己的外表有很多困扰。
Her biggest hang-up is her fear of public speaking. 她最大的心理障碍是对公开演讲的恐惧。


一般过去时:
He had a hang-up about sharing his feelings. 他过去对分享自己的感受有顾虑。
She hung up the phone abruptly after the argument. 争吵后她突然挂断了电话。


现在完成时:
I've overcome many of my hang-ups over the years. 这些年来我已经克服了许多心理障碍。
He has hung up the phone several times today. 他今天已经挂断电话好几次了。


过去完成时:
She had already hung up before he could apologize.  在他道歉之前，她就已经挂断了电话。
He had several hang-ups about his job that he needed to address. 他对自己的工作有一些困扰需要解决。


将来时:
I will hang up the phone if you continue to be rude. 如果你继续粗鲁，我就挂断电话。
She will likely have fewer hang-ups after therapy.  治疗后，她可能会减少一些心理障碍。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have so many hang-ups, I'd be more successful. 如果我没有这么多心理障碍，我会更成功。
If she hadn't hung up so quickly, they could have resolved the issue. 如果她没有这么快挂断电话，他们就能解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The call was hung up on him unexpectedly.  电话意外地被他挂断了。
His hang-ups were subtly addressed during therapy. 他的心理障碍在治疗期间被巧妙地解决了。


疑问句:
Do you have any hang-ups about this project? 你对这个项目有什么顾虑吗？
Why did she hang up the phone so quickly? 她为什么这么快就挂断了电话？


否定句:
He doesn't have any hang-ups about expressing his opinions. 他对表达自己的意见没有任何顾虑。
She didn't hang up the phone; it must have been a bad connection.  她没有挂断电话；一定是线路不好。
