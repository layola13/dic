munchausenism:/ˈmʌŋˌhɔːzənɪzəm/| n. | 孟乔森综合征；编造谎言以博取同情 | A psychiatric disorder characterized by the fabrication of illnesses or symptoms, often involving self-harm, to gain attention or sympathy.  This is also sometimes referred to as factitious disorder. | Example:  Her constant complaints about various ailments, despite numerous negative medical tests, led doctors to suspect Munchausen syndrome.  她的各种疾病的不断抱怨，尽管进行了多次阴性医学检查，导致医生怀疑她患有孟乔森综合征。 |近义词:factitious disorder, malingering (though malingering implies a conscious attempt to avoid responsibility or gain something, while Munchausen's is more about attention-seeking)|反义词:honesty, truthfulness |名词
