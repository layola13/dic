houseboating:/ˈhaʊsbəʊtɪŋ/| n. |水上房屋生活；乘船屋旅行|The activity of living on a houseboat or travelling on a houseboat.  It encompasses the lifestyle, the travel, and the experience of living afloat. |Example: Houseboating on the canals of France is a popular vacation activity.  法国运河上的水上房屋生活是一种受欢迎的度假活动。|近义词：canal boating, river boating|反义词：land-based living
