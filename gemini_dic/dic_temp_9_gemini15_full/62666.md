engagingly:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 吸引人地；迷人地；引人入胜地 |  In a way that is charming and interesting, captivating the attention and interest of others. It suggests a captivating and delightful manner. | Example: He spoke engagingly about his travels, captivating the audience. 他饶有兴致地讲述了他的旅行经历，吸引了听众的注意力。 | 近义词：charmingly, captivatingly, fascinatingly, compellingly | 反义词：dully, boringly, unimpressively
