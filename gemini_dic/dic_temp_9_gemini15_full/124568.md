mourned:/mɔːnd/| v. |哀悼，悼念 |to feel or express great sadness, especially because of someone's death |例句：The nation mourned the death of its beloved leader.  全国人民都为他们敬爱的领导人的逝世而哀悼。

例句：She mourned the loss of her childhood home. 她为失去童年时的家而悲伤。


近义词：grieved, lamented, bewailed |反义词：celebrated, rejoiced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mourns his late wife every day. 他每天都思念已故的妻子。
They mourn the loss of their innocence. 他们为失去纯真而悲伤。


一般过去时:
We mourned the passing of our friend. 我们为朋友的去世而哀悼。
The family mourned privately after the funeral. 葬礼后，家人私下悼念。


现在完成时:
She has mourned the loss of her pet for weeks. 她已经为失去宠物哀悼了好几周了。
They have mourned the destruction of their village. 他们一直为村庄的破坏而悲痛。


过去完成时:
He had mourned the loss of his father for many years before he died. 他在他去世前多年一直为父亲的去世而哀悼。
The community had mourned the victims of the accident for months. 社区几个月来一直在悼念事故的受害者。


将来时:
They will mourn the passing of the Queen. 他们将为女王的逝世而哀悼。
The country will mourn the loss of its heroes.  国家将为失去英雄而哀悼。


虚拟语气:
If I had known he was going to die, I would have mourned him properly. 如果我知道他要死了，我会好好地为他哀悼。
I wish I hadn't mourned his death so publicly. 我真希望我没有那么公开地哀悼他的死。


被动语态:
The fallen soldiers were mourned by the entire nation. 全体国民都在悼念阵亡的士兵。
His death was mourned by his friends and family. 他的死受到了朋友和家人的悼念。


疑问句:

否定句:
He did not mourn the death of his enemy. 他没有为敌人的死而悲伤。
She didn't mourn the end of their relationship. 她并没有为他们关系的结束而悲伤。
