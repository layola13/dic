instill:/ɪnˈstɪl/| vt.|灌输；注入；逐渐养成|To gradually put an idea or feeling into someone's mind, especially over a period of time.|例句：They tried to instill in their children a love of nature. 他们试图在孩子们心中灌输热爱大自然的感情。|例句：The teacher instilled a sense of discipline in her students.老师在她的学生中灌输了纪律感。|近义词：infuse, implant, inculcate|反义词：eradicate, remove, suppress|动词
