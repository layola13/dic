puffballs: /ˈpʌfˌbɔːlz/ | n. |马勃菌；灰包菌 |  A puffball is a type of fungus, typically round and white, that releases its spores in a puff of dust when squeezed or touched. They are edible when young and firm, but become inedible as they mature.  | Example: We found several giant puffballs growing in the meadow. 我们在草地上发现了几个巨大的马勃菌。  |近义词:fungus, mushroom (when young and edible) |反义词: N/A
