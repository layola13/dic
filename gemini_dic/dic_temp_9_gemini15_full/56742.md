doorpost: /ˈdɔːrpost/ | n. |门柱，门框|A vertical post forming one side of a doorway.  It is usually a structural part of a building, supporting the lintel (horizontal beam) above the door.|例句：The old doorpost was rotting at the base.  老门柱底部正在腐烂。

The paint was peeling from the doorpost. 门柱上的油漆正在剥落。|近义词：door jamb, post|反义词：None
