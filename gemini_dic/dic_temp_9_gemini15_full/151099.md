paraselene:/ˌpærəˈsiːliːn/| n. |幻月，月亮的伴影|A paraselene is a bright spot of light that sometimes appears on either side of the moon, caused by the refraction of moonlight through ice crystals in the atmosphere.  It is a type of halo.  Paraselenae are often seen in pairs, flanking the moon. |Example:  Last night, we saw a beautiful paraselene beside the full moon. 昨晚，我们在满月旁边看到美丽的幻月。|近义词：mock moon, lunar halo|反义词：无
