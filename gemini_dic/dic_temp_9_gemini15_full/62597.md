enfeloned: /ɪnˈfɛlənd/ | adj. | 犯下重罪的；被判重罪的 |  Made subject to a felony; convicted of a felony.  The term implies a past action resulting in a serious criminal charge.  It is less common than "felonious" but carries a similar meaning with emphasis on the legal consequence of the crime. | 例句：He was enfelonded for embezzlement. = 他因贪污而被判重罪。  The enfelonded man faced a lengthy prison sentence. = 犯下重罪的那个人面临着漫长的监禁。 | 近义词：felonious, guilty of a felony, convicted | 反义词：innocent, acquitted
