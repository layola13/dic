marquisship:/ˈmɑːkwɪʃɪp/| n. |侯爵爵位；侯爵地位|The state or rank of a marquess.  It refers to the title, dignity, and privileges associated with being a marquess.  Marquess is a British noble title, ranking below a duke and above an earl.|例句：His marquisship was inherited from his father. 他的侯爵爵位是继承自他父亲的。

例句：The responsibilities of his marquisship were considerable.  他侯爵的地位责任重大。


近义词： marquisate (less common)


反义词：commoner (lack of noble title)


词性：noun
