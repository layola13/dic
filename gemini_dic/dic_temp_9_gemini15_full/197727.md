shiftingly:/ˈʃɪftɪŋli/| adv.|变化不定地，摇摆不定地，闪烁不定地|Moving or changing in an unsteady or unpredictable way; flickering or wavering.|The lights flickered shiftingly in the storm.  暴风雨中灯光闪烁不定。The wind shifted shiftingly, making it hard to sail. 风向变化不定，难以航行。|unsteadily, erratically, vacillatingly|steadily, consistently, firmly|副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs or change form to indicate tense.  It describes the manner in which an action occurs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.

例句：
The shadows shiftingly danced on the wall. 影子在墙上摇曳不定地舞蹈。
His gaze shifted shiftingly between her and the door. 他的目光在她和门之间闪烁不定地移动。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases specifically using "shiftingly."  Its usage is generally within a sentence describing the manner of movement or change.
