genets:/ˈdʒɛnɪts/ | n. |非洲靈貓 |A genet is a small, slender carnivorous mammal belonging to the family Viverridae.  They are known for their spotted or ringed coats, long tails, and nocturnal habits.  Genets are found in Africa, Europe, and Asia. |例句：The genet silently stalked its prey through the undergrowth.  非洲靈貓悄無聲息地在灌木叢中獵捕獵物。

例句： Several species of genets inhabit various regions of Africa.  幾種靈貓棲息在非洲的不同地區。
