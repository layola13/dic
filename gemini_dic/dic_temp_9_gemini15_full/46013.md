cybernetics:/ˌsaɪbərˈnɛtɪks/| n. |控制论；信息论|The science of communication and control theory, dealing with the structure and function of information processing systems, particularly in machines and living organisms.  It explores how systems process information, regulate themselves, and adapt to change. | Example:  Cybernetics is a multidisciplinary field that draws upon engineering, mathematics, biology, and computer science. 控制论是一个多学科领域，它借鉴了工程学、数学、生物学和计算机科学。


Example: The principles of cybernetics are applied in robotics to create machines that can learn and adapt. 控制论的原理应用于机器人技术，以创造能够学习和适应的机器。


近义词:control theory, systems theory, information theory
