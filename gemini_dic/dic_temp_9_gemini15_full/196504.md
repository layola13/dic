servicewoman:/ˈsɜːrvɪswʊmən/| n.|女军人，女服务员|A woman who serves in the armed forces or performs a service.  This word can refer to women in the military or those who work in a service-oriented job.|例句：She is a servicewoman in the Navy. = 她是海军的一名女军人。|例句：The servicewoman at the restaurant was very helpful. = 餐厅的女服务员非常乐于助人。|近义词：female soldier, military woman, waitress (in the context of service-oriented jobs)|反义词：serviceman|名词
