glenoid:/ˈɡlɛnɔɪd/| n. |关节盂，臼窝|The glenoid cavity is the shallow, pear-shaped articular surface of the scapula that receives the head of the humerus to form the shoulder joint.  It's a crucial part of the shoulder's structure, allowing for a wide range of motion. |The shallowness of the glenoid cavity contributes to the shoulder's mobility but also its instability. | 近义词：socket, articular surface | 反义词：N/A
