choosiness:/ˈtʃuːzinəs/| n. |挑剔；过分讲究；吹毛求疵|The quality of being too picky or demanding;  excessively careful in choosing.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting an unwillingness to accept anything less than perfect or a tendency to find fault.|例句：Her choosiness made it difficult to find a suitable partner. 她的挑剔让她很难找到合适的伴侣。

例句：His choosiness about food was legendary. 他对食物的挑剔是出了名的。


近义词：fastidiousness, fussiness, particularity, persnicketiness|反义词：uncriticalness, acceptance, tolerance|词性:名词
