pulpitish:/ˈpʌlpɪtɪʃ/| adj.|讲坛似的；牧师式的；说教的；冗长乏味的|Having the manner or style of a sermon; preachy and tedious.|例句：His pulpitic speech was long and boring. 他的讲道式演讲冗长乏味。

例句：The professor's pulpitish tone alienated many students. 教授的说教语气让许多学生反感。


近义词：preachy, didactic, sermonizing, moralistic|反义词：unpretentious, informal, casual|词性：形容词
