contrasting: /kənˈtræstɪŋ/ | adj. | 对比的；形成对比的 | Showing a difference between things; highlighting differences. |  例句：The contrasting colors of the painting were striking.  这幅画中对比鲜明的色彩非常醒目。

例句：The essay presented contrasting viewpoints on the issue. 这篇文章提出了关于这个问题的对比观点。


近义词：different, opposite, contrasting, disparate


反义词：similar, alike, identical, uniform


词性：形容词
