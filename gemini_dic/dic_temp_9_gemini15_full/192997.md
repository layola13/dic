second-guessing: /ˈsɛkənd ˈɡɛsɪŋ/ | n. |事后质疑；反复琢磨；猜疑|The act of questioning a decision or action that has already been made. It often implies doubt and uncertainty about the wisdom of the past choice.  |例句：Her constant second-guessing of her decisions prevented her from moving forward.  她的不断质疑自己的决定让她无法前进。


例句：The coach regretted second-guessing his starting lineup after the team's loss. 教练在球队输球后后悔自己对首发阵容的反复琢磨。|近义词：retrospection, re-evaluation, questioning, doubting|反义词：confidence, certainty, acceptance|词性：名词
