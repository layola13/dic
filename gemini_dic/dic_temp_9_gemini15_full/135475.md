nonpharmaceutical: /ˌnɒnˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkəl/ | adj. |非药物的；非药品的 | Not relating to or involving pharmaceuticals or pharmacy.  It describes measures or interventions that don't involve medication. |

例句：

Nonpharmaceutical interventions, such as handwashing and social distancing, are crucial in controlling the spread of infectious diseases.  = 非药物干预措施，例如洗手和保持社交距离，对于控制传染病的传播至关重要。


The government recommended a range of nonpharmaceutical approaches to mitigate the impact of the pandemic. = 政府建议采取一系列非药物方法来减轻疫情的影响。


近义词：non-medicinal, non-drug, alternative

反义词：pharmaceutical, medicinal, drug-based


词性：形容词
