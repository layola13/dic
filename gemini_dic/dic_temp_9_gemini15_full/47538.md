deathlike:/ˈdɛθlaɪk/| adj.| 死一般的；极其苍白的；极度冷漠的 | Resembling death; extremely pale; extremely cold or indifferent. |例句：His face was deathlike in its pallor.  他的脸苍白得像死人一样。

例句：The atmosphere in the room was deathlike; no one spoke.  房间里的气氛死气沉沉的；没有人说话。

近义词： cadaverous, ghostly, pallid, lifeless, apathetic

反义词： lively, vibrant, animated, enthusiastic, warm
