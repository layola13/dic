enemy:/ˈɛnəmi/ | n. |敌人，仇敌 | A person who is actively opposed or hostile to someone or something.  |The two countries have been enemies for centuries.  这两个国家几个世纪以来一直是敌人。| foe, opponent, adversary | friend, ally | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is my enemy. = 他是我的敌人。
They are enemies of the state. = 他们是国家的敌人。

一般过去时:
He was my enemy in the past. = 他过去是我的敌人。
She was his enemy for many years. = 她是他的敌人很多年了。

现在完成时:
He has been my enemy since childhood. = 他从孩提时代起就是我的敌人。
They have been enemies for a long time. = 他们是很长时间的敌人。

过去完成时:
He had been my enemy before we reconciled. = 在我们和解之前，他一直是我的敌人。
They had been enemies for years before the peace treaty. = 在和平条约签订之前，他们已经做了多年的敌人。


将来时:
He will remain my enemy. = 他将依然是我的敌人。
They will always be enemies. = 他们将永远是敌人。

虚拟语气:
If he were my enemy, I would be careful. = 如果他是我的敌人，我会小心谨慎。
If they were our enemies, we would be in trouble. = 如果他们是我们的敌人，我们会陷入困境。


被动语态:
He was considered an enemy by many. = 他被许多人视为敌人。
They were treated as enemies by the regime. = 他们被政权视为敌人。

疑问句:
Is he your enemy? = 他是你的敌人吗？
Are they our enemies? = 他们是我们的敌人吗？

否定句:
He is not my enemy. = 他不是我的敌人。
They are not my enemies. = 他们不是我的敌人。
