lowlander:/ˈloʊlændər/| n. |低地居民；低地人|A lowlander is an inhabitant of a lowland area, especially one considered less sophisticated or cultured than inhabitants of highlands.|例句：The lowlanders from the coastal plains often traded with the highlanders from the mountainous regions.  低地沿海平原的居民经常与山区的高地居民进行贸易。|近义词：plainsman, plains dweller|反义词：highlander
