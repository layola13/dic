premere:/ˈprɛmər/|v.|压,按,压迫|To press; to exert pressure on; to urge or insist on something.|例句：He pressed the button to start the machine. 他按下按钮启动机器。|例句：She pressed him for an answer. 她逼他回答。|近义词：push, squeeze, urge, compel|反义词：release, loosen, yield

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He presses the grapes to make wine. = 他压榨葡萄酿酒。
They press their demands on the government. = 他们向政府施压，要求满足他们的要求。

一般过去时:
She pressed the doorbell. = 她按了门铃。
He pressed the iron down on the shirt. = 他把熨斗压在衬衫上。


现在完成时:
I have pressed all the laundry. = 我已经把所有的衣服都熨烫过了。(此处press指熨烫)
They have pressed for a quick resolution. = 他们一直在争取快速解决问题。


过去完成时:
She had pressed the button before the alarm sounded. = 警报响起之前，她已经按下了按钮。
They had pressed the issue for weeks before getting a response. = 他们在得到回应之前已经就此问题施压了数周。


将来时:
He will press the charges against the thief. = 他将对窃贼提起诉讼。(此处press指控告)
They will press for higher wages. = 他们将争取更高的工资。


虚拟语气:
If I pressed harder, I might have succeeded. = 如果我再用力一点，也许就能成功了。
I wish I had pressed him for more details. = 我真希望我当时能逼问他更多细节。


被动语态:
The grapes were pressed to extract the juice. = 葡萄被压榨以提取果汁。
The issue is being pressed by the media. = 媒体正在关注这个问题。


疑问句:
Are you pressing the brake pedal hard enough? = 你踩刹车踏板够用力了吗？
Were they pressing for a change in policy? = 他们当时是否正在争取改变政策？


否定句:
He didn't press the issue. = 他没有坚持这个问题。
She didn't press the button hard enough. = 她没有用力按按钮。


PHRASE->
press conference:新闻发布会
press charges:提起诉讼
press release:新闻稿
press down:压下
under pressure:在压力下
