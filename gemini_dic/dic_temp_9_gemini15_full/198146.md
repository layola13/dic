shopman:/ˈʃɒpmæn/ /ˈʃɑːpmæn/| n. |店主，店员 |A shopman is a man who works in a shop, typically selling goods to customers.  He may be the owner or an employee.|例句：The shopman helped me find the right size shoes. 店员帮我找到了合适的尺码的鞋子。 The shopman was very friendly and helpful.  店主非常友好和乐于助人。|近义词：shopkeeper, salesman, retailer |反义词：customer, buyer
