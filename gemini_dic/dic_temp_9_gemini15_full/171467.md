puffiness:/ˈpʌfɪnɪs/| n.|浮肿，肿胀|A swollen appearance of the body, typically in the face or around the eyes, often caused by fluid retention.  It can be a symptom of various conditions, including allergies, infections, or kidney problems.|例句：The puffiness under her eyes suggested a lack of sleep.  她眼睛下面的浮肿暗示她睡眠不足。|例句：His face showed signs of puffiness after the allergic reaction. 过敏反应后，他的脸上出现了浮肿的迹象。|近义词：swelling, edema, bloating |反义词：slimness, leanness
