noncomparable: /ˌnɒnkəmˈpɛərəbl/ | adj. | 不可比拟的，无法比较的 |  Incapable of being compared;  having no common standard of measurement or evaluation.  The differences are so profound that the two things are noncomparable. |  例句：The two paintings are noncomparable in terms of artistic merit. 这两幅画在艺术价值上无法比较。 The complexity of the two tasks makes them noncomparable. 这两项任务的复杂性使它们无法比较。 | 近义词：incomparable, unequal, unlike | 反义词：comparable, similar
