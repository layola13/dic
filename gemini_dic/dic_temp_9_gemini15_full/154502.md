perpenensity:/prəˈpɛnsɪti/| n.|倾向，癖性，嗜好|a natural inclination or tendency|He has a propensity for getting into trouble. 他有惹麻烦的倾向。|近义词：inclination, tendency, proneness|反义词：aversion, dislike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His propensity for risk-taking is well-known. = 他喜欢冒险是出了名的。
She shows a propensity for hard work. = 她展现出努力工作的倾向。


一般过去时:

Her propensity for kindness was evident in her actions. = 她的善良倾向在她的一举一动中都显而易见。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
His propensity for gambling had already cost him a fortune before he sought help. = 在他寻求帮助之前，他嗜赌的倾向已经让他损失了一大笔钱。
Her propensity for secrecy had prevented her from forming close relationships. = 她喜欢保密的倾向阻碍了她与人建立密切关系。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
