irrecoverably:/ˌɪrɪˈkʌvərəbli/| adj. |不可挽回地；无法恢复地 |  in a way that cannot be recovered or rectified.  |The damage to the painting was irrecoverably lost.|近义词：irreversibly, irretrievably |反义词：recoverably


例句：

The data was irrecoverably lost after the hard drive crashed.  硬盘崩溃后，数据不可挽回地丢失了。

His reputation was irrecoverably damaged by the scandal.  他的名誉因丑闻而受到了不可挽回的损害。


词源:

ir- (否定前缀) + recover (恢复) + -ably (副词后缀)


用法说明:

Irrecoverably is an adverb that modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb. It emphasizes the permanent and irreversible nature of a situation or action.  It suggests that something is beyond repair or restoration.
