poncho:/ˈpɒn(t)ʃoʊ/ | n. | 蓬чо，斗篷 | A poncho is a type of cloak, typically made of a single piece of fabric with a hole in the center for the head.  It's often worn as outerwear for warmth or protection from the elements. | Example: She wore a colorful poncho to stay warm during the chilly evening. 她穿着一件色彩鲜艳的蓬чо来抵御寒冷的夜晚。  |近义词：cape, cloak, shawl | 反义词：None  |名词
