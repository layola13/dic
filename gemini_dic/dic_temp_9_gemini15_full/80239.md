goes:/ɡoʊz/| /ɡəʊz/ | v. | 去；走；进行；运转；适合 | The verb "go" in the third-person singular simple present tense. It indicates movement, progress, or operation. | 例句：The river goes to the sea. 这条河通向大海。| 例句：The clock goes at night.  钟表晚上走时。 |近义词：travel, proceed, move, function |反义词：stay, remain, stop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes to school every day. = 他每天去上学。
She goes to the gym three times a week. = 她一周去健身房三次。

一般过去时:
We went to the park yesterday. = 我们昨天去了公园。
They went to the movies last night. = 他们昨晚去看电影了。

现在完成时:
I have gone to that restaurant many times. = 我去过那家餐馆很多次了。
She has gone to the market already. = 她已经去市场了。

过去完成时:
Before I left, he had already gone home. = 我离开之前，他已经回家了。
They had gone to bed before the storm began. = 暴风雨开始之前，他们已经睡觉了。

将来时:
I will go to London next year. = 我明年要去伦敦。
They are going to go to the beach tomorrow. = 他们明天要去海滩。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would go to the concert. = 如果我有时间，我会去看音乐会的。
I wish I could go to Paris. = 我希望我能去巴黎。

被动语态:
(The verb "go" is rarely used in the passive voice, as it is typically intransitive.  A more suitable example would use a different verb.)
The plan was put into action.  = 计划被付诸实施了。
The project was completed on time. = 项目按时完成了。


疑问句:
Where does he go every day? = 他每天去哪里？
Does she go to work by bus? = 她乘公共汽车上班吗？

否定句:
He doesn't go to work on weekends. = 他周末不上班。
They didn't go to the party. = 他们没有去参加派对。


PHRASE->
go ahead = 开始；继续
go away = 走开；离开
go back = 返回
go on = 继续；发生
go out = 出去；熄灭
