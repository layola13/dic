quickish:/ˈkwɪkɪʃ/| adj. |有点快的；相当快的 |Somewhat quick; fairly quick.  The adjective "quickish" is an informal word that implies a degree of speed that is more than just average but not necessarily extremely fast. It suggests a moderate level of quickness. |例句：He finished the race in quickish time. 他以相当快的速度完成了比赛。

The meeting was quickish, lasting only about an hour. 会议开得比较快，只持续了一个小时左右。|近义词：rapid, speedy, brisk, fast |反义词：slow, sluggish, leisurely |词性：形容词
