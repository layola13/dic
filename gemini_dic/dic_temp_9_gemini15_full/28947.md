carry:/ˈkæri/ |/ˈkæri/| vt. n.|携带，搬运；支撑；承担；意味；具有；传播|To support and move something from one place to another; to bear the weight of; to sustain; to convey or transmit; to imply or suggest.|例句：He carried the heavy box upstairs. 他把沉重的箱子搬上了楼。|例句：The bridge carries a lot of traffic. 这座桥承载着大量的交通流量。|近义词：transport, bear, convey, support |反义词：leave, abandon, drop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I carry my bag to school every day. = 我每天都背着书包去上学。
She carries herself with grace. = 她举止优雅。


一般过去时:
He carried the groceries into the kitchen. = 他把杂货搬进了厨房。
They carried out the plan successfully. = 他们成功地执行了计划。


现在完成时:
I have carried this heavy suitcase all day. = 我整天都提着这个沉重的行李箱。
She has carried on bravely despite her difficulties. = 尽管遇到困难，她仍然勇敢地坚持下去。


过去完成时:
He had carried the message before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经传达了这个信息。
They had carried out many experiments before they got the result. = 在得到结果之前，他们已经进行了许多实验。


将来时:
I will carry the luggage for you. = 我会帮你提行李。
They will carry on with their work tomorrow. = 他们明天将继续他们的工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I could carry that heavy box. = 如果我更强壮些，我就能搬动那个沉重的箱子。
I wish I could carry on a conversation in Mandarin. = 我希望我能用普通话进行对话。


被动语态:
The injured man was carried to safety. = 受伤的男子被抬到了安全的地方。
The news was carried by all the major newspapers. = 所有主要报纸都报道了这条新闻。


疑问句:
Can you carry this for me? = 你能帮我拿这个吗？
Does this bridge carry heavy vehicles? = 这座桥能承载重型车辆吗？


否定句:
I cannot carry that weight. = 我无法承受那个重量。
He didn't carry out the instructions properly. = 他没有正确执行指示。


PHRASE->
carry on =继续
carry out = 执行，完成
carry through = 贯彻到底
carry forward = 继续进行；发扬光大
carry off = 成功地完成；夺取（奖项）
