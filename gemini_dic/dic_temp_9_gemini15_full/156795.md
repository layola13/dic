pig:/pɪɡ/| n.|猪，猪肉|A pig is a domesticated farm animal kept for its meat.  It's also a common symbol of greed or dirtiness. |例句：We raise pigs on our farm. 我们在农场养猪。|例句：He's such a pig; he ate everything on the table. 他真是个贪婪鬼，把桌上的东西都吃光了。|近义词：hog, swine|反义词：None directly, but connotations could be contrasted with words like "cleanliness" or "generosity".|名词
