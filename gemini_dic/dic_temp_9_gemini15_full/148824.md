overvigorous:/ˌoʊvərˈvɪɡərəs/ | adj. | 过于强烈的；过于用力的；过于精力充沛的 | Excessively forceful or energetic; too vigorous.  | The overvigorous application of fertilizer damaged the plants.  过于强烈的施肥损坏了植物。He displayed an overvigorous enthusiasm that bordered on mania. 他表现出一种近乎狂热的过度热情。|近义词：excessive, strenuous, intense, forceful | 反义词：lackadaisical, languid, feeble, weak
