prediscounted:/ˌpriːdɪˈskaʊntɪd/| adj.|预先打折的，预先减价的|Already discounted before the official sale or promotion.  This term implies a discount applied before the advertised price reduction, often indicating a deeper discount or a special offer for a select group.|例句：The prediscounted price made the item even more attractive. 预先打折的价格使该商品更具吸引力。

例句：They offered a prediscounted rate for early bird registrations. 他们为提前注册的人提供预先打折的费率。


近义词：discounted, reduced, marked down

反义词：full-priced, unreduced, original price


词性:形容词
