commigrate:/ˈkɒmɪɡreɪt/ | /ˈkɑːmɪɡreɪt/ | v. |一起迁移；共同移居|To migrate together or in a group; to move in large numbers from one region to another.|例句：Many birds commigrate to warmer climates during the winter.  许多鸟类在冬天一起迁移到气候温暖的地方。|例句：The entire family decided to commigrate to the countryside. 全家决定一起迁移到农村。|近义词：migrate, move, relocate|反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Birds commigrate to warmer regions every year. = 鸟类每年迁徙到温暖的地区。
Large groups of animals commigrate in search of food. = 大群动物为了寻找食物而迁徙。

一般过去时:
The family commigrated to Canada last year. = 这家人去年移居加拿大。
Many people commigrated to the city during the industrial revolution. = 工业革命期间，许多人移居城市。

现在完成时:
They have commigrated to the coast and settled down. = 他们已经移居到海岸并定居下来。
Millions of people have commigrated to urban areas. = 数百万的人口已经迁徙到城市地区。

过去完成时:
Before the war, many families had commigrated to safer areas. = 在战争爆发之前，许多家庭已经迁移到更安全的地方。
The settlers had commigrated to the new land decades ago. = 定居者几十年前就迁移到了这片新土地。

将来时:
We will commigrate to a new country next year. = 我们明年将移居到一个新的国家。
They plan to commigrate to the mountains when they retire. = 他们计划退休后移居山区。

虚拟语气:
If they had more money, they would commigrate to a warmer climate. = 如果他们有更多钱，他们就会迁徙到气候更温暖的地方。
I wish we could commigrate to a quieter place. = 我希望我们能搬到一个更安静的地方。

被动语态:
The population was commigrated to the new city. = 人口被迁移到了新的城市。(This usage is less common and often sounds unnatural.)
Few people were commigrated against their will. = 很少有人被迫迁移。(This usage is less common and often sounds unnatural.)

疑问句:
Will the herd commigrate this year? = 兽群今年会迁徙吗？
Did your family commigrate to this town long ago? = 你家很久以前就迁到这个镇了吗？


否定句:
The birds did not commigrate this year due to the warm weather. = 由于天气温暖，鸟类今年没有迁徙。
They chose not to commigrate, preferring to stay in their hometown. = 他们选择不迁移，宁愿留在他们的家乡。
