cofinance:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnæns/ | v. | 共同资助，联名资助 | To provide funding for a project or venture jointly with another party or parties.  This often involves sharing the financial risk and responsibility. | 例句：Several organizations decided to cofinance the new community center.  几个组织决定共同资助新的社区中心。

The government and private investors agreed to cofinance the infrastructure project. 政府和私人投资者同意共同资助基础设施项目。


近义词：jointly fund, co-fund,  collaboratively finance

反义词：solely fund, individually finance

词性：动词
