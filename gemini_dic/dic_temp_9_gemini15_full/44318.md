cropland:/ˈkrɒplænd/|/ˈkrɑːplænd/| n. |耕地，农田 |Land used for growing crops.  Cropland is essential for food production and plays a vital role in the global food system. |Example: The farmer carefully tended his cropland, ensuring a bountiful harvest.  这位农民仔细耕种他的农田，确保获得丰收。  The expansion of cropland has led to deforestation in many parts of the world.  耕地的扩张导致世界许多地区森林遭到砍伐。|近义词：farmland, cultivated land |反义词：wilderness, wasteland
