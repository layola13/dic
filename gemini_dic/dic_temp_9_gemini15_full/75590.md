fuel:/ˈfjuːəl/| n. vt.|燃料，刺激物|  a material that releases energy when it burns, used to provide power for engines, heating etc.|例句：The car runs on unleaded fuel. 这辆车使用无铅汽油。

例句：The fire was fueled by strong winds. 大火被强风助长。|近义词：gas, energy, power|反义词:None

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine uses gasoline as fuel. = 这台发动机使用汽油作为燃料。
This new policy fuels economic growth. = 这项新政策刺激了经济增长。


一般过去时:

词源:
The word "fuel" comes from the Old French word "fuele," meaning "fuelwood."  This in turn likely comes from the Vulgar Latin *focāle, a diminutive of focus (hearth, fire).  The meaning has expanded over time from solely firewood to encompass all materials used to produce energy.
