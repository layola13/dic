rejacketed:/ˌriːˈdʒækɪtɪd/ | adj. | 再穿外套的；重新装套的 | Having been jacketed again; having had a new jacket put on.  This word typically refers to something that has had a protective outer layer added or replaced.  For example, a rejacketed book might have a new cover, or a rejacketed cable might have a new protective sheath. | 例句：The old building was rejacketed with modern insulation.  旧建筑重新加装了现代隔热层。

The rejacketed wire is much more durable.  重新装套的电线耐用得多。 | 近义词：recoated,  re-covered | 反义词：unjacketed, bare
