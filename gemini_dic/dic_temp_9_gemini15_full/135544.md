nonpneumatic: /ˌnɒnˌnjuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非气动的；非充气的 | Not using or relating to compressed air.  This adjective is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with "pneumatic" (relating to air or gas).  It describes something that doesn't operate or function using compressed air.


例句：

The nonpneumatic tires on my bicycle are much more durable. = 我的自行车上的非充气轮胎耐用得多。

This nonpneumatic drill requires manual operation. = 这款非气动钻需要手动操作。


近义词：airless, non-inflated


反义词：pneumatic, air-powered


词性：形容词
