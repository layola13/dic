encomia:/ɛnˈkoʊmiə/| n.|赞美诗；颂词；赞扬|A formal expression of praise, especially a speech or written work.  It often involves elaborate or heightened language. | Example: The poet wrote encomia to celebrate the king's victory.  这位诗人写了颂词来庆祝国王的胜利。|近义词：praise, eulogy, panegyric, tribute|反义词：criticism, condemnation, denunciation|名词

词源:
From Late Latin *encomium, from Greek ἔγκωμιον (enkōmion), from ἐγκωμιάζειν (enkomiazein) "to praise," from ἐν (en) "in" + κῶμος (kōmos) "revel, merrymaking."  The original sense implied praise sung at a celebratory gathering.
