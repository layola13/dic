lessen:/ˈlɛsən/| vt.|减少；减轻；降低|To make or become less; to diminish in amount, degree, or intensity.|例句：The teacher lessened the workload for the students. 老师减少了学生的作业量。|例句：The rain lessened in the afternoon. 下午雨小了。|近义词：reduce, decrease, diminish|反义词：increase, augment, enlarge|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain lessens in the afternoon. = 下午雨小了。
He lessens his workload every weekend. = 他每个周末都减少工作量。

一般过去时:
The teacher lessened the workload for the students. = 老师减少了学生的作业量。
The storm lessened its intensity as it moved inland. = 暴风雨向内陆移动时强度减弱了。

现在完成时:
The medication has lessened my pain. = 药物减轻了我的疼痛。
The drought has lessened the water supply. = 干旱减少了水供应。

过去完成时:
The rain had lessened before they started their hike. = 他们开始徒步旅行之前雨已经小了。
He had lessened his expectations after several setbacks. = 在几次挫折之后，他降低了期望。

将来时:
The doctor hopes the treatment will lessen the swelling. = 医生希望治疗能减轻肿胀。
They believe the new policy will lessen traffic congestion. = 他们相信新政策将减少交通拥堵。

虚拟语气:
If the government lessened taxes, the economy might improve. = 如果政府降低税收，经济可能会改善。
I wish the noise would lessen. = 我希望噪音能小一些。

被动语态:
The workload was lessened for the students. = 学生的作业量减少了。
The impact of the storm was lessened by the emergency services. = 紧急服务部门减轻了风暴的影响。

疑问句:
Will this medicine lessen the pain? = 这药能减轻疼痛吗？
Does this plan lessen the risk? = 这个计划能降低风险吗？

否定句:
The new rules didn't lessen the confusion. = 新规并没有减少混乱。
The efforts to control pollution haven't lessened the air quality problems. = 控制污染的努力并没有改善空气质量问题。
