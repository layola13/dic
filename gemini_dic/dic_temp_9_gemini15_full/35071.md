closeup:/ˈkloʊsʌp/| n. adj.|特写镜头，近距离；近距离的|A closeup is a photograph or a shot in a film or television program that shows something at close range.  A closeup shot can be used to highlight details or emotions.|例句：The director wanted a closeup of the actor's face to show his fear.  导演想要一个演员面部的特写镜头来展现他的恐惧。|例句：The closeup revealed a tiny scratch on the surface. 近距离观察显示表面有细微划痕。|近义词：close-up, detail shot, close range|反义词：long shot, wide shot|名词，形容词
