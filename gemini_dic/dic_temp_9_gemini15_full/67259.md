expurgations:/ˌɛkspərˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 删节；净化；清除 | The act of removing objectionable or offensive passages from a text or other work.  It often refers to the removal of material considered obscene, blasphemous, or politically dangerous. | Example: The expurgations made to the book were extensive, removing almost all references to sex and violence.  = 这本书的删节非常广泛，几乎删去了所有关于性和暴力的内容。  |近义词: deletions, omissions, redactions, bowdlerizations | 反义词: additions, insertions, inclusions | 名词
