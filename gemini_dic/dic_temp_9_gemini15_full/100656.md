intone:/ɪnˈtoʊn/| vt.|吟诵；以某种语气说；|to pronounce or utter with a particular tone or intonation.  It implies a certain feeling or attitude is conveyed through the tone of voice.|例句：He intoned the poem with great feeling. 他饱含感情地吟诵这首诗。|例句：She intoned her disapproval. 她语气中表达了她的不满。|近义词：chant, recite, utter, pronounce|反义词：mumble, whisper


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intone the prayer every morning. = 他每天早上吟诵祈祷文。
She intone her lines with perfect clarity. = 她清晰地朗诵台词。

一般过去时:
The priest intoned the blessing. = 牧师吟诵了祝福。
