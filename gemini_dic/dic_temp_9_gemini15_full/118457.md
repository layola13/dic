mesencephalon:/ˌmɛzɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/| n. |中脑|The mesencephalon, also known as the midbrain, is a small but important part of the brain located between the forebrain and the hindbrain.  It plays a crucial role in several functions, including vision, hearing, motor control, sleep/wake cycles, alertness, and temperature regulation.  It contains structures such as the superior and inferior colliculi, and the substantia nigra. |The mesencephalon is a crucial part of the brain involved in visual and auditory processing.|近义词：midbrain |反义词：无
