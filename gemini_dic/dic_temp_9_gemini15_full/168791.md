produce:/prəˈduːs/|/ˈprodjuːs/| n. vt.| 生产；产品；农产品 | To create, make, or grow something.  It can refer to manufacturing goods, growing crops, or creating artistic works. |例句：The factory produces cars.  这家工厂生产汽车。
The farmer produces a variety of fruits and vegetables. 这个农民生产各种水果和蔬菜。


近义词：manufacture, create, generate, yield, farm  | 反义词：consume, destroy, waste | 词性：n. vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They produce high-quality furniture. = 他们生产高质量的家具。
The farm produces organic vegetables. = 这个农场生产有机蔬菜。

一般过去时:
The company produced a new line of clothing last year. = 公司去年推出了一系列新服装。
She produced a stunning painting for the exhibition. = 她为展览创作了一幅令人惊叹的画作。

现在完成时:
We have produced over 1000 units this month. = 我们这个月已经生产了1000多件产品。
The artist has produced several acclaimed sculptures. =这位艺术家创作了几件广受好评的雕塑作品。

过去完成时:
They had produced all the parts before the assembly line started. =装配线启动前，他们已经生产了所有零件。
The writer had produced three novels before her breakthrough. =这位作家在取得突破之前已经创作了三部小说。

将来时:
We will produce more electric cars in the future. = 我们将来会生产更多的电动汽车。
The company will produce a new smartphone next year. =该公司明年将生产一款新的智能手机。

虚拟语气:
If they produced more, they would make more profit. =如果他们生产更多，他们就能赚更多钱。
I wish I had produced a better presentation. =我希望我做了一个更好的演示。

被动语态:
The report was produced by the research team. =这份报告是由研究小组编写的。
The goods are produced in China. =这些商品是在中国生产的。

疑问句:
What does the factory produce? =这家工厂生产什么？
Does the company produce any sustainable products? =这家公司生产任何可持续产品吗？

否定句:
The factory doesn't produce toys anymore. =这家工厂不再生产玩具了。
They didn't produce enough goods to meet demand. =他们生产的商品不足以满足需求。
