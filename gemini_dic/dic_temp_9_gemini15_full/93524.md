imagery:/ˈɪmɪdʒəri/| n. |意象；图像；意境 |The creation and use of images, especially in literature.  It refers to the descriptive or figurative language used to create vivid mental pictures and sensory experiences. Imagery can appeal to any of the five senses (sight, sound, touch, taste, smell), or a combination of them, to evoke emotions and create a more impactful experience for the reader or listener. |例句：The poet's imagery vividly evoked the sights and sounds of the bustling marketplace.  这位诗人的意象生动地再现了喧嚣市场中的景象和声音。

例句：The novel uses powerful imagery to depict the horrors of war. 这部小说运用强烈的意象来描绘战争的恐怖。


近义词：figurative language, visualization, mental images, description | 反义词：abstraction, vagueness
