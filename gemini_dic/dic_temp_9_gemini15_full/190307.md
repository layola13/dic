sarcenet:/ˈsɑːrsənɛt/ | n. | 绸缎；沙丽绸 | A fine, lightweight, plain-woven silk fabric with a smooth, lustrous surface.  It is often used for clothing, linings, and accessories. | Example: The dress was made of shimmering sarcenet.  这件裙子是用闪亮的沙丽绸做的。  The lining of the coat was a delicate sarcenet. 这件外套的里衬是一种精致的沙丽绸。| 近义词：silk, taffeta, satin | 反义词：burlap, canvas
