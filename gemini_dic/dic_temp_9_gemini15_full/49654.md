demiquaver:/ˈdɛmɪˌkweɪvər/| n. |十六分音符 |A demiquaver is a note in music that is equal to one sixteenth of a whole note (or semibreve). It is half the length of a quaver (eighth note).  |Example: The fast passage contained many demiquavers, making it challenging to play.  这段快速的乐句包含许多十六分音符，演奏起来很有挑战性。|近义词：sixteenth note |反义词：semibreve, whole note
