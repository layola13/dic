epiphytal:/ˌɛpɪˈfaɪtəl/| adj.|附生的；气生的|Growing upon another plant, but not parasitic.  Epiphytic plants derive moisture and nutrients from the air, rain, and debris that collects around them, rather than from the plant they are attached to.|例句：Many orchids are epiphytal, growing on trees in rainforests.  许多兰花是附生的，生长在雨林的树木上。|例句：The epiphytal ferns draped the branches, creating a lush canopy. 附生的蕨类植物垂挂在枝条上，形成茂密的树冠。|近义词：air plant, lithophytic (if growing on rock)|反义词：terrestrial, parasitic
