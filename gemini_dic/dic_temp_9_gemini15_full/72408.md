fluentness:/ˈfluːəntnəs/| n. |流利，流畅；熟练 |The ability to speak or write a language easily and smoothly, or the quality of being able to do something easily and smoothly.  It refers to the smoothness and ease with which someone speaks or writes a language, or performs a task. |例句：Her fluentness in French impressed everyone.  她的法语流利程度给每个人留下了深刻印象。

例句：The pianist displayed remarkable fluentness in his performance.  这位钢琴家在演奏中展现了非凡的流畅性。


近义词：fluency, smoothness, ease, facility


反义词：hesitation, stammering, difficulty, awkwardness


词性:名词
