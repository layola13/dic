lovelify:/ˈlʌvɪfaɪ/| vt.|使可爱；使美丽；使令人喜爱|To make something or someone lovely or beautiful; to enhance the attractiveness or charm of something.|例句：She lovelified her garden with colorful flowers. 她用五颜六色的花朵装饰了她的花园，使它变得美丽动人。|例句：He tried to lovelify his presentation with engaging visuals. 他试图通过引人入胜的视觉效果来使他的演示文稿更具吸引力。|近义词：beautify, embellish, adorn|反义词：uglify, deface, mar|动词
