revokers:/rɪˈvoʊkərz/| n.|撤销者；废除者|Those who revoke or cancel something.  People or entities with the power to nullify or invalidate something.|例句：The revokers of the contract were the company's board of directors.  合同的撤销者是公司的董事会。


例句：The government revokers announced the cancellation of the outdated law.  政府撤销者宣布废除过时的法律。


近义词：cancelers, annulers, repealers


反义词：approvers, ratifiers, enforcers


词性:名词
