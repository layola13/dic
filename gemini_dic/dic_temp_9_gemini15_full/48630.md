defendable: /dɪˈfɛndəbəl/ | adj. | 可辩护的；可防御的；站得住脚的 | Justifiable; able to be defended against attack or criticism.  |例句：His actions, while questionable, were ultimately defendable in court.  他的行为虽然令人质疑，但最终在法庭上是可以辩护的。

His argument was completely defendable, supported by irrefutable evidence. 他的论点完全站得住脚，有无可辩驳的证据支持。


近义词：justifiable, defensible, supportable, tenable, valid | 反义词： indefensible, unjustifiable, untenable
