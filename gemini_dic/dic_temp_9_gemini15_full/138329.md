noteworthy:/ˈnoʊtwɜːrði/ | adj. |值得注意的；显著的；重要的|  Something noteworthy is important enough to deserve attention or notice. It is significant or remarkable. |例句：Her contribution to the project was noteworthy.  她的项目贡献值得注意。

例句：The museum houses many noteworthy artifacts. 这个博物馆收藏了许多值得注意的文物。


近义词：remarkable, significant, important, outstanding, striking, considerable

反义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial, commonplace, ordinary


词源：note (注意) + worthy (值得的)  The word combines "note," meaning to observe or pay attention to, with "worthy," meaning deserving or valuable.  This indicates something deserving of being noted or observed.
