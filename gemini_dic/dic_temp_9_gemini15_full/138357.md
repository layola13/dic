notification:/ˌnoʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |通知，通告，告知 |An announcement, warning, or other piece of information.  It can be a formal or informal message conveying news or information.|例句：I received a notification that my order had shipped. = 我收到通知说我的订单已经发货了。

例句：The company sent out a notification about the upcoming changes. = 公司发布了关于即将发生变化的通知。


近义词：announcement, alert, warning, advice, information |反义词：secrecy, concealment


USAGE->
