furlough:/ˈfɜːrloʊ/ (US) /ˈfɜːləʊ/ (UK)| n. v.|休假；准假；暂时解雇|A period of time when someone is temporarily allowed to leave their job, usually with pay.  It can also refer to the act of granting such a leave.|例句：The company announced a two-week furlough for all employees due to the economic downturn. 公司宣布由于经济低迷，所有员工将休假两周。|例句：The soldiers were furloughed for a month to visit their families.  士兵们被准假一个月去看望家人。|近义词：leave, vacation, layoff (for the temporary dismissal aspect)|反义词：reinstatement (for the opposite of temporary dismissal)|名词，动词
