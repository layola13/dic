protoplasmatic:/ˌproʊtoʊˈplæzmætɪk/ | adj. | 原生质的；细胞质的 | Relating to or characteristic of protoplasm.  The term refers to the living substance of a cell, excluding the cell wall and other non-living components.  It encompasses the cytoplasm and the nucleus. |

例句：

The protoplasmatic membrane regulates the passage of substances into and out of the cell. = 原生质膜调节物质进出细胞。

Scientists studied the protoplasmatic streaming within the plant cell. = 科学家研究了植物细胞内的原生质流动。


近义词：cellular, cytoplasmic


反义词：non-living, extracellular


词性：形容词
