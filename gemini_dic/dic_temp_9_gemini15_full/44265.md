croma:/ˈkroʊmə/| n.| 色彩；色度；饱和度 | chroma refers to the purity or intensity of a color.  It's the degree to which a color is free from white, gray, or black. A high chroma color is vibrant and saturated, while a low chroma color is dull or muted. | Example: The painting's vibrant chroma made it stand out. 这幅画鲜艳的色彩使它脱颖而出。 |近义词：saturation, intensity, purity |反义词：desaturation, dullness
