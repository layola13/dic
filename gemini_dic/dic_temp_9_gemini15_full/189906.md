samplery:/ˈsæmpləri/| n. |样本集，样品陈列室，纺织品样本|A collection of samples, especially of fabrics or textiles, shown to potential buyers.  It can also refer to a room or place where such samples are displayed.|例句：The designer showed us a beautiful samplery of silk fabrics. 设计师给我们展示了一套精美的丝绸织物样本集。
The furniture store had a large samplery in the showroom. 家具店在展厅里摆放了一个大型样品陈列室。|近义词：collection, assortment, display|反义词：无
