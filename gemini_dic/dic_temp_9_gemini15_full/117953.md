Memes often spread through the internet and social media, taking many forms, including images, videos, and text. |Example: The "Drakeposting" meme is a popular example of an image macro that expresses a decision between two options.  这个“德雷克表情包”迷因是一个流行的图片模因的例子，它表达了在两个选项之间的选择。|近义词：trend, fad, catchphrase |反义词：uniqueness, originality
