crania:/ˈkreɪniə/| n.|颅骨；头盖骨|The plural of cranium, referring to the skull, especially the part enclosing the brain.  Crania are studied in anatomy and paleontology to understand brain size, shape, and evolutionary history. |例句：The anthropologist examined the crania found at the archaeological site. 人类学家检查了考古遗址中发现的头盖骨。 |例句：  Researchers compared the crania of different hominin species to trace evolutionary relationships. 研究人员比较了不同人族物种的头盖骨以追踪进化关系。|近义词：skulls|反义词：None specifically contrasting|名词
