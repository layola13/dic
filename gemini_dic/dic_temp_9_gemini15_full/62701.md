engineering: /ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪr.ɪŋ/ | n. | 工程学；工程；设计；建造 | The practical application of science and mathematics to achieve a specific objective, often involving the design, construction, or maintenance of structures, machines, or systems. | 例句：Civil engineering is crucial for building infrastructure. 土木工程对于建设基础设施至关重要。

例句：She's studying mechanical engineering at university. 她正在大学学习机械工程。


近义词：construction, design, technology | 反义词：destruction, demolition
