nonincriminating: /ˌnɒnɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不构成犯罪的；无罪的；无损于名誉的 | Not tending to incriminate or expose one to criminal charges; not suggesting guilt.  |例句：The witness's testimony was entirely nonincriminating.  证人的证词完全没有构成犯罪。

The police investigation uncovered some nonincriminating evidence. 警方调查发现了一些无罪的证据。

近义词：exculpatory, innocent, harmless, non-incriminatory

反义词：incriminating, inculpatory, damning, compromising


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  (否定前缀)
* **incriminating**:  present participle of "incriminate", meaning "to accuse of a crime; to implicate". (来自动词incriminate，意为“控告犯罪；牵连”)
* **-ing**:  suffix forming present participles and adjectives. (构成现在分词和形容词的后缀)

因此，"nonincriminating" 字面意思是“不是使人被控告犯罪的”。


USAGE->
(由于nonincriminating是形容词，没有动词变化，以下列举其在不同句式中的用法)

肯定句:
His alibi was completely nonincriminating. 他的不在场证明完全没有问题。
The documents provided were entirely nonincriminating. 提供的文件完全没有问题。


否定句:
The evidence was not nonincriminating; it strongly suggested his guilt.  证据并非无罪的；它强烈暗示了他的罪行。
Her statement was far from nonincriminating; it directly implicated her in the crime.  她的陈述远非无罪的；它直接将她牵扯到犯罪中。


疑问句:
Is the information nonincriminating?  这些信息是无罪的吗？
Was his testimony truly nonincriminating? 他的证词真的无罪吗？
