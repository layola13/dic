negligibly:/ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbli/ | adv. | 可以忽略不计地，微不足道地 |  It means to such a small extent as to be insignificant or unimportant.  It describes something that is so small or unimportant that it can be disregarded. | The difference in price is negligibly small. 价格差异微乎其微。|  insignificantly, minimally, trivially | significantly, substantially, considerably | 副词
