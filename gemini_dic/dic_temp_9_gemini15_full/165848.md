preoccurs:/ˌpriːəˈkɜːr/| v.|预先发生，先于…发生 |To happen or exist before something else.  It implies a causal or temporal relationship where one event precedes and potentially influences another.|例句：The symptoms preoccurred the diagnosis.  症状先于诊断出现。|例句：The earthquake preoccurred the tsunami. 地震先于海啸发生。|近义词： precede, antecede, forerun|反义词： follow, succeed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The symptoms preoccur the diagnosis. = 症状先于诊断出现。
The earthquake preoccurs the tsunami. = 地震先于海啸发生。


一般过去时:
The symptoms preoccurred the diagnosis. = 症状先于诊断出现。
The earthquake preoccurred the tsunami. = 地震先于海啸发生。


现在完成时:
The symptoms have preoccurred the diagnosis in many cases. = 在许多案例中，症状先于诊断出现。
The warning signs have preoccurred the disaster. = 预警信号先于灾难出现。


过去完成时:
The symptoms had preoccurred the doctor's visit. = 症状在医生来访之前就已经出现了。
The initial tremors had preoccurred the main earthquake. = 主要地震之前发生了初始震动。


将来时:
The rain will preoccur the picnic. = 雨将会在下雨之前。
The preparations will preoccur the event. = 准备工作将在活动之前完成。


虚拟语气:
If the warning signs had preoccurred, the disaster could have been avoided. = 如果预警信号提前出现，灾难本来是可以避免的。
I wish the symptoms had preoccurred the severe illness. = 我希望症状在重病出现之前就已经显现。


被动语态:
The diagnosis was preoccurred by the symptoms. = 诊断先于症状出现。（此用法略显不自然，应避免）
The event was preoccurred by extensive planning. = 活动之前进行了大量的计划。（此用法略显不自然，应避免）


疑问句:
Did the symptoms preoccur the diagnosis? = 症状是否先于诊断出现？
Did the earthquake preoccur the tsunami? = 地震是否先于海啸发生？


否定句:
The symptoms did not preoccur the diagnosis. = 症状没有先于诊断出现。
The rain did not preoccur the picnic. = 雨没有在下雨之前。



PHRASE->
preoccur with: 与...同时发生(虽然词典中没有这个词组, 但根据其含义可以理解为这个意思)
preoccur to:  发生在...之前 (虽然词典中没有这个词组, 但根据其含义可以理解为这个意思)
preoccur and: 先于...并...
preoccur by: 先于... (表示时间上的先后)
preoccur in: 在...之前发生
