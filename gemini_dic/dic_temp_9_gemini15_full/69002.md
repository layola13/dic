fatness:/ˈfæt.nəs/| n. |肥胖；肥沃；丰盛|The state or quality of being fat; obesity; corpulence.  It can also refer to richness or abundance, especially of soil or a harvest.|例句：His fatness was a cause for concern for his doctor. 他的肥胖令他的医生担忧。

例句：The fatness of the land resulted in a bountiful harvest. 土地的肥沃带来了丰收。


近义词：obesity, corpulence, plumpness, richness, abundance

反义词：thinness, leanness, scarcity
