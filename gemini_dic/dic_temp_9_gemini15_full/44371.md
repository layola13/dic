cross-country: /ˈkrɒs ˈkʌntri/ | adj. n. |越野的；越野赛跑|

英文解析:  Relating to or denoting a race or journey across country, typically over rough ground.  As a noun, it refers to the race or journey itself, or a style of skiing.

例句:

He participated in a cross-country race. 他参加了一场越野赛跑。

The cross-country skiing trip was exhausting but rewarding.  这次越野滑雪旅行很累，但也很有收获。


近义词: overland, transcontinental (for journeys), off-road (for vehicles)

反义词: on-road (for vehicles),  inland (depending on context)

词性: adjective, noun


词源:

The word is a compound of "cross" (meaning "across") and "country" (meaning "land" or "territory").  The meaning evolved naturally from the literal sense of traveling across a country to the more specific sporting sense of a race across varied terrain.
