overtightening: /ˌoʊvərˈtaɪtənɪŋ/ | n. | 过度拧紧；过度收紧 | The act of tightening something too much, often resulting in damage or malfunction.  | Example: Overtightening the bolts caused the engine to seize. 过度拧紧螺栓导致发动机卡住。 | 近义词：over-torquing, excessive tightening | 反义词：loosening, undertightening
