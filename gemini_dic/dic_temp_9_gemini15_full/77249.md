gastronome:/ˈɡæstrənɒm/|/ˈɡæstrəˌnoʊm/| n. |美食家，烹饪专家 |A gastronome is a person who is knowledgeable about and enthusiastic about good food and drink. They appreciate the finer points of cuisine, often traveling to experience different culinary traditions.  |He is a renowned gastronome, known for his discerning palate and extensive knowledge of wines.|近义词：gourmet, epicure, gourmand |反义词：glutton (in the sense of excessive eating without appreciation)
