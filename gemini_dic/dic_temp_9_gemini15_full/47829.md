decategorizing: /ˌdiːˈkætəɡəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |取消分类；去除类别；使非范畴化 |The process of removing something from a category or classification system.  It often implies a challenge to established categories or a blurring of boundaries between categories. |例句：The essay decategorizes traditional gender roles, arguing for a more fluid understanding of identity.  这篇论文取消了传统的性别角色的分类，主张对身份认同有更灵活的理解。

例句：Decategorizing the patient's symptoms allowed the doctor to consider a wider range of diagnoses.  取消对病人症状的分类使医生能够考虑更广泛的诊断。


近义词：uncategorizing, reclassifying (in a negative sense), de-classifying


反义词：categorizing, classifying


词性：动词
