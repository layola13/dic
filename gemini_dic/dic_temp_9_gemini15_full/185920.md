revolver:/rɪˈvɒlvər/ | n. |左轮手枪|A revolver is a handgun that has a revolving cylinder containing multiple cartridges.  It is typically loaded by rotating the cylinder to align each chamber with the barrel. |例句：The outlaw drew his revolver and fired.  歹徒拔出左轮手枪并开火。|例句：The detective found a silver revolver at the crime scene.  侦探在犯罪现场发现了一把银色左轮手枪。|近义词：pistol, handgun |反义词：N/A
