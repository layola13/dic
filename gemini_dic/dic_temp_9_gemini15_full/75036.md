friezes:/ˈfriːzɪz/| n.| 弗里兹；饰带；带状装饰 | A frieze is a broad horizontal band of sculpted or painted decoration, especially on a wall near the ceiling.  It often depicts scenes or patterns.  | The museum featured a stunning frieze depicting scenes from ancient mythology.  博物馆展出了一幅描绘古代神话场景的令人惊叹的带状装饰。 | decoration, molding, ornamentation | plain, undecorated | 名词
