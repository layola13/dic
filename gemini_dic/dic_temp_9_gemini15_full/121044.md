misconducts:/ˈmɪskɑːndʌkts/ | n. |行为不端；不当行为 | Improper behavior, especially by someone in a position of authority.  |Example: The doctor's misconduct led to the loss of his license. 这名医生的行为不端导致他失去了行医执照。

The teacher's misconduct was reported to the principal. 教师的不当行为被报告给了校长。 |近义词：misbehavior, wrongdoing, impropriety |反义词：good conduct, proper behavior
