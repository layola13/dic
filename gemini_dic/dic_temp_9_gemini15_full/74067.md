fossilification:/ˌfɒsɪlɪˈfeɪʃən/| /ˌfɑːsəlɪˈfeɪʃən/| n. |化石化；石化；僵化|The process by which organic matter is converted into a fossil.  This includes the replacement of organic material with minerals, resulting in the preservation of the organism's structure.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe the process of something becoming rigid or unchanging. |例句：The fossilification of the dinosaur bones took millions of years. 恐龙骨骼的石化过程花费了数百万年。

The rapid fossilification of the political system prevented any meaningful reform. 政治体制的迅速僵化阻碍了任何有意义的改革。|近义词：petrification, mineralization|反义词：decomposition, disintegration
