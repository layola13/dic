pomelos:/ˈpɒmɛloʊz/|/ˈpɑːmeloʊz/| n. |柚子|A large citrus fruit with a thick rind and a pale yellow or pinkish flesh, similar to a grapefruit but larger and sweeter.|Example:  I bought some pomelos at the Asian market. 我在亚洲超市买了一些柚子。  The pomelo salad was refreshing.  柚子沙拉很清爽。|近义词：grapefruit, shaddock|反义词：无
