pastoralism:/ˈpæstərəlɪzəm/| n. |牧业；游牧生活；牧歌体；田园诗意|The practice of pastoral farming, especially with livestock.  It often involves the seasonal movement of livestock between different pastures.  The term also encompasses the cultural and social aspects of life associated with such practices.|例句：The study explores the impact of climate change on pastoralism in the region.  这项研究探讨了气候变化对该地区牧业的影响。

例句：The novel depicts a romanticized view of pastoralism, ignoring the hardships of nomadic life. 这部小说描绘了牧业生活浪漫化的景象，忽略了游牧生活的艰辛。


近义词：nomadism, sheep farming, cattle ranching|反义词：sedentary lifestyle, industrial agriculture|词性:名词
