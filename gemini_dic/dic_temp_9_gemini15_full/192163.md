scranky:/ˈskræŋki/| adj.| 脾气暴躁的；易怒的；古怪的；不稳定的|  Describes someone who is irritable, grumpy, or bad-tempered.  It can also suggest someone eccentric or unreliable. |例句：He's been rather scranky all morning. 他整个早上都脾气暴躁。

例句：The old engine was scranky and prone to breakdowns. 这台旧发动机脾气古怪，容易坏。


近义词：grumpy, irritable, crotchety, cantankerous, ill-tempered, churlish, fractious


反义词：good-natured, amiable, cheerful, pleasant, easygoing


词性：形容词
