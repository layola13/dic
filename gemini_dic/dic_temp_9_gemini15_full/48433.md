deductible:/dɪˈdʌktɪbl/| adj. n.| 可扣除的；免赔额 |  An amount of money that you must pay yourself before your insurance company will pay anything.  It's the portion of a loss that is not covered by insurance. |例句：My health insurance has a $1,000 deductible. = 我的健康保险有1000美元的免赔额。

The deductible on my car insurance is $500. = 我的汽车保险免赔额是500美元。|近义词：allowable, permissible |反义词：unallowable, non-deductible|名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The deductible is high this year. = 今年的免赔额很高。
What is the deductible for this policy? = 这份保单的免赔额是多少？
