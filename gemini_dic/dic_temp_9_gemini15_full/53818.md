disconnectedness:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪdnɪs/ | n. | 脱节；疏离感；缺乏联系 | The feeling of being isolated or separated from others, or from a sense of purpose or belonging.  It can refer to a lack of connection between people, ideas, or systems. |例句：The pandemic exacerbated feelings of disconnectedness among many people. 大流行加剧了许多人的疏离感。

例句：His disconnectedness from reality was alarming. 他与现实脱节令人担忧。


近义词：isolation, alienation, separation, detachment, remoteness

反义词：connection, togetherness, unity, integration, relatedness


词性：名词
