dissocialize:/ˌdɪˈsoʊʃəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使脱离社会；使疏远社会；使不适应社会 | To deprive of social interaction or skills; to make someone less sociable or more withdrawn.  |例句：Years of solitary confinement dissocialized the prisoner.  =多年的单独监禁使囚犯与社会脱节。

例句：His dissocialized behavior made it difficult for him to maintain friendships. = 他反社会的行为使他难以维持友谊。


近义词：alienate, isolate, estrange, withdraw


反义词：socialize, integrate, connect


词性：动词
