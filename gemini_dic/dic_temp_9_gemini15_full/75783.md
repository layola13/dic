fumage:/ˈfjuːmɑːʒ/| n.|熏制；烟熏味；烟熏画|The process of smoking or curing something, especially food or art.  It can also refer to the smoky smell or appearance resulting from this process.|例句：The fumage created a unique flavor in the cheese.  烟熏使奶酪产生了一种独特的风味。|例句：The artist used fumage to create a dramatic effect in the painting.艺术家用烟熏法在绘画中创造出戏剧性的效果。|近义词：smoking, smoking process,燻製|反义词：没有直接的反义词，但可以考虑fresh, unsmoked等词语来表达对立含义。
