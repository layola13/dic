extractions:/ɪkˈstrækʃənz/ | n. | 提取；摘录；拔出 (尤指牙齿) |The process of removing something, especially a substance or object, from something else.  This often involves a degree of force or skill.  It can refer to the act itself or the result of the act. |例句：The extraction of oil from the earth is a complex process. 从地球上开采石油是一个复杂的过程。

The dentist performed a difficult tooth extraction. 牙医进行了艰难的拔牙手术。


近义词：removal, extraction, severance, detachment|反义词：insertion, implantation
