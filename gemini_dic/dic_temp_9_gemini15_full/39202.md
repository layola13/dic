congers:/ˈkɒŋɡəz/ | n. |康吉鳗；海鳗 | A conger is a type of eel, typically large and found in the sea.  They are known for their long, snake-like bodies and powerful jaws. |Example: The fisherman caught a huge conger eel off the coast.  渔民在海岸边钓到了一条巨大的康吉鳗。  The conger was so large, it took three men to lift it. 这条康吉鳗很大，需要三个人才能抬起来。|近义词：eel (general term) |反义词：None specifically, but small fish could be considered an antonym in terms of size.
