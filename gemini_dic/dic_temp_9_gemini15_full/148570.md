overtakes:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪks/| vt.|超过，赶上，压倒|To go past something or someone by being faster or better.  It can also mean to become more important or successful than something else. |例句：The blue car overtook the red car.  蓝色的车超过了红色的车。

The popularity of smartphones has overtaken that of traditional cameras. 智能手机的普及程度已经超过了传统相机的普及程度。|近义词： surpass, exceed, outstrip |反义词：lag behind, fall behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overtakes other cars on the highway. 他在高速公路上超车。
She often overtakes her colleagues in sales. 她经常在销售额上超过她的同事。


一般过去时:
The cyclist overtook the runner.  骑自行车的人超过了跑步的人。
Our company overtook our competitor in market share. 我们公司在市场份额上超过了我们的竞争对手。


现在完成时:
He has overtaken me in the race. 他在比赛中超过我了。
The new technology has overtaken the old one. 新技术已经取代了旧技术。


过去完成时:
She had overtaken him by the time they reached the finish line. 他们到达终点线时，她已经超过了他。
The storm had overtaken the ship before help arrived. 在救援到来之前，暴风雨已经袭击了那艘船。


将来时:
I think she will overtake him soon. 我认为她很快就会超过他。
Electric cars are expected to overtake gasoline cars in sales within the next decade. 预计电动汽车在未来十年内将在销量上超过汽油车。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have overtaken the leader. 如果他训练更努力，他也许就能超过领先者。
If only I had worked harder, I could have overtaken my competitors.  如果我当时更努力工作的话，我就能超过我的竞争对手了。


被动语态:
He was overtaken by a faster runner. 他被一个跑得更快的人超过了。
The company was overtaken by a larger competitor. 这家公司被一家更大的竞争对手收购了。


疑问句:
Will you overtake me? 你会超过我吗？
Has he been overtaken by the other team? 他被另一支队伍超过了吗？


否定句:
He didn't overtake the other car. 他没有超过另一辆车。
She couldn't overtake him in the race. 她没能在那场比赛中超过他。


PHRASE->
overtake an event: 赶上某事件
overtake someone's position: 取代某人的职位
overtake the market: 占据市场
overtake a crisis: 度过危机
overtake a country: 占领一个国家
