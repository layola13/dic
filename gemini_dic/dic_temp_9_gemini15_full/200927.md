slips:/slɪps/| n./v.|滑倒；疏忽；纸条；鞋垫|to slide unintentionally, or to make a small mistake |The banana peel caused him to slip and fall. 香蕉皮导致他滑倒摔倒了。My memory slipped, and I forgot her name. 我的记忆力下降了，我忘了她的名字。|slide, stumble, blunder|stand firm, remember perfectly|n., v.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He often slips on the ice. 他经常在冰上滑倒。
She sometimes slips up in her work. 她有时工作中会犯错。

一般过去时:
I slipped on the wet floor yesterday. 我昨天在湿滑的地板上滑倒了。
He slipped and broke his arm. 他滑倒了，摔断了胳膊。


现在完成时:
She has slipped several times this winter.  她今年冬天已经滑倒好几次了。
He has slipped in his studies lately. 他最近学习懈怠了。


过去完成时:
I had slipped on the ice before I realized it. 我在意识到之前就已经滑倒在冰上了。
She had slipped the note into his pocket before he noticed.  在她被发现之前，她已经把纸条塞进了他的口袋。


将来时:
I think I will slip on this icy pavement. 我想我会在这条结冰的人行道上滑倒。
He will slip up if he's not careful. 如果他不小心，他会犯错的。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't slipped, I wouldn't have fallen. 如果我没有滑倒，我就不会摔倒了。
I wish I hadn't slipped and told him the secret. 我真希望我没有说漏嘴告诉他这个秘密。


被动语态:
The note was slipped under the door. 纸条被塞到了门下。
The information was slipped to the press. 信息被泄露给了媒体。


疑问句:
Did you slip and fall? 你滑倒摔倒了吗？
Has he slipped up in his work again? 他工作中又犯错了吗？


否定句:
I didn't slip on the ice. 我没有在冰上滑倒。
She didn't slip up during the presentation. 她在演示过程中没有犯错。


PHRASE->

slip away:溜走；悄悄离开
slip of the tongue: 口误
slip one's mind: 忘记
slip into: 悄悄穿上；不知不觉陷入
let slip: 泄露；失手
