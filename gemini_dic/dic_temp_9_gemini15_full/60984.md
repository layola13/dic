elide:/ɪˈlaɪd/| vt.|省略，略去，删除|To omit or suppress (something, typically a vowel or syllable) in pronunciation or writing.  To leave out or suppress; to pass over without mention.|例句：The poet elides the final syllable of the word.  诗人省略了单词的最后一个音节。
例句：He elides the difficult parts of the story. 他省略了故事中难以理解的部分。|近义词：omit, suppress, leave out, skip|反义词：include, retain, emphasize
