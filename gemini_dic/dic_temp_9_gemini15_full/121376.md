mish-mash:/ˈmɪʃmæʃ/| n.|混杂，大杂烩，混乱无序|A mishmash is a confused mixture of different things. It suggests a lack of organization or coherence.  The term often implies a haphazard or unappealing combination.|例句：The artist's latest work is a mishmash of styles and techniques. = 艺术家的最新作品是各种风格和技巧的大杂烩。

The room was a mishmash of furniture and belongings. = 房间里是家具和物品的混乱堆积。|近义词：hodgepodge, jumble, medley, mixture|反义词：order, organization, coherence
