preserves:/prɪˈzɜːrvz/ | n. v. |果酱；蜜饯；保护区；保存；维护|

英文解析:As a noun, preserves refers to food, such as fruit, that has been preserved, typically in sugar.  As a verb, it means to protect something from harm or decay, or to keep something in its original state.

例句:
(Noun): The shop sells a wide variety of fruit preserves, including strawberry jam and apricot marmalade. = 这家商店出售各种各样的果酱，包括草莓酱和杏子酱。

(Noun):  The national park is a nature preserve, protecting endangered species. = 国家公园是一个自然保护区，保护着濒危物种。

(Verb): We need to preserve our natural resources for future generations. = 我们需要为子孙后代保护我们的自然资源。

(Verb):  The painting has been remarkably well preserved. = 这幅画保存得非常好。


近义词: (Noun) jams, jellies, conserves; (Verb) protect, conserve, maintain, safeguard

反义词: (Verb) destroy, ruin, damage, waste


词性: Noun, Verb
