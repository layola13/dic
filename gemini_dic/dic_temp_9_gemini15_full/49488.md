delved:/dɛlvd/| v.|深入研究；探究；钻研|To search deeply and thoroughly into a subject, typically in order to discover information.|例句：Historians have delved into the archives to find information about the past. 历史学家们深入研究了档案以寻找有关过去的信息。
例句：She delved into her memories to try and remember the details. 她深入回忆试图记起细节。|近义词：investigate, explore, probe, examine|反义词：skim, glance, overlook

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He delves into the mysteries of the universe. = 他深入研究宇宙的奥秘。
She delves into ancient history. = 她钻研古代史。

一般过去时:
The detectives delved into the case. = 侦探们深入调查了这个案件。
He delved into the old books. = 他深入研究了那些旧书。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

She had delved into many books before she wrote her novel. = 在写小说之前，她已经研读过许多书籍。

虚拟语气:
