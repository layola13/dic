escorted:/ɛˈskɔːrtɪd/ | v. | 护送，陪同 | To accompany or protect someone, especially to ensure their safety or provide them with guidance.  |例句：The police escorted the witness to the courthouse. 警方护送证人到法院。|例句：She was escorted to her seat by a member of the staff. 工作人员陪她到座位。|近义词：accompanied, guided, conducted, chaperoned |反义词：abandoned, deserted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The security guard escorts visitors to their cars. = 保安护送访客到他们的车旁。
He escorts her to the door every night. = 他每晚都护送她到门口。

一般过去时:
The police escorted the suspect to the station. = 警察护送嫌疑犯到警局。
My father escorted me to my first dance. = 我父亲护送我去参加我的第一次舞会。

现在完成时:
The army has escorted several humanitarian aid convoys. = 军队已经护送了几支人道主义援助车队。
She has escorted countless VIPs throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经护送了无数的贵宾。

过去完成时:
They had escorted the president to the airport before the incident occurred. = 事件发生前，他们已将总统护送至机场。
The guards had escorted the prisoner to his cell before the riot started. = 暴动开始前，警卫已将囚犯护送至其牢房。


将来时:
The soldiers will escort the refugees to safety. = 士兵们将护送难民到安全地带。
I will escort you to your hotel. = 我将护送你去你的酒店。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would escort you home. = 如果我有车，我会送你回家。
I wish I could have escorted her to the party. = 我真希望我能送她去参加派对。

被动语态:
The VIP was escorted by a detail of security officers. = 贵宾由一队保安人员护送。
The tourists were escorted to their hotel safely. = 游客们被安全地护送到了他们的酒店。

疑问句:
Will someone escort the children to the bus stop? = 会有人护送孩子们到公共汽车站吗？
Who escorted the witness to the court? = 谁护送证人去法院？

否定句:
He didn't escort her home last night. = 他昨晚没有送她回家。
The police did not escort the suspect to the precinct. = 警察没有将嫌疑犯护送至派出所。
