overdebating: /ˌoʊvərˈdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度争论；喋喋不休地争论 |  To debate or discuss something excessively or to a point of being unproductive or tiresome.  It implies that the discussion has gone on for too long and has become unproductive or even harmful. |例句：They were overdebating the minor details and lost sight of the bigger picture. = 他们对细枝末节进行了过度争论，而忽略了大局。

They overdebated the issue until everyone was exhausted. = 他们对这个问题争论了太久，以至于每个人都筋疲力尽了。| 近义词：overdiscussing,  arguing excessively,  belaboring,  prolonging a debate | 反义词：concisely discussing, briefly debating, reaching a consensus quickly | 动词
