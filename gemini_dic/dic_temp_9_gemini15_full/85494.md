hasteful: /ˈheɪstfəl/ | adj. | 轻率的；匆忙的；仓促的 | characterized by excessive speed or urgency; done too quickly and carelessly. | 例句：His hasty decision led to regrettable consequences.  他的轻率决定导致了令人遗憾的后果。

例句：The hasty construction of the building resulted in structural problems. 这座建筑的仓促施工导致了结构问题。


近义词：hurried, rushed, precipitate, reckless, impetuous | 反义词：deliberate, careful, cautious, slow, leisurely


词源:  Derived from "haste" (meaning speed or urgency), plus the suffix "-ful" (meaning "full of").  The word's history reflects the long-standing association between speed and carelessness.
