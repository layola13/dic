choke-down: /tʃoʊk daʊn/ | vt. |强压；强行吞咽；抑制；压制 | To force oneself to swallow something with difficulty, or to suppress or restrain something.  |例句：He choked down his anger. 他强压下怒火。 He had to choke down the lump in his throat. 他不得不强忍住哽咽。|近义词：suppress, restrain, stifle, repress |反义词：express, unleash, release |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chokes down his food quickly. = 他很快地吞咽食物。
She chokes down her emotions. = 她强忍着情绪。


一般过去时:
He choked down the bitter medicine. = 他强忍着喝下了苦药。
She choked down her tears. = 她强忍着眼泪。


现在完成时:
He has choked down his pride to apologize. = 他为了道歉而强压下了自尊心。
She has choked down her anger many times. = 她多次强忍着怒火。


过去完成时:
He had choked down his fear before speaking. = 他在说话之前已经强压下了恐惧。
She had choked down her disappointment before announcing the news. = 在宣布这个消息之前，她已经强压下了失望的心情。

虚拟语气:
