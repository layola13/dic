counterfeits:/ˈkaʊntərfɪts/ | n. | 伪造品；假货 |  Counterfeits are illegal copies of something valuable, like money or designer goods.  They are made to deceive people into thinking they are genuine. | The police seized a large quantity of counterfeit banknotes.警方查获了大量假钞。  He was selling counterfeit watches. 他在卖假表。| forgeries, fakes, imitations | genuine, authentic | 名词
