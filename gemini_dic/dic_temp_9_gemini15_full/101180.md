invigoration:/ɪnˌvɪɡəˈreɪʃən/| n. |精神焕发；精力充沛；复兴；振兴|The act of invigorating or the state of being invigorated; renewed energy or strength.|例句：The invigorating mountain air filled him with renewed energy.  清新的山间空气使他精力充沛。

例句：The company's new marketing campaign brought about an invigorating boost to sales. 公司新的营销活动使销售额得到了令人振奋的提升。


近义词：rejuvenation, revitalization, refreshment, stimulation|反义词：debilitation, exhaustion, depletion|词性:名词
