emblematic:/ˌɛmbləˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 象征性的；代表性的 |  Serving as a symbol of a particular quality or concept; representative.  | The lion is emblematic of courage.  狮子象征着勇气。 The Eiffel Tower is emblematic of Paris.  埃菲尔铁塔是巴黎的象征。 | symbolic, representative, typical | atypical, unrepresentative

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
