genetics:/dʒəˈnɛtɪks/ | n. |遗传学|The study of heredity and variation in living organisms.  It examines how traits are passed from parents to offspring, the mechanisms involved in inheritance, and the causes of genetic variation. |Genetics plays a crucial role in understanding diseases and developing new treatments. 遗传学在理解疾病和开发新疗法方面起着至关重要的作用。|genomics, heredity |environmental factors|名词
