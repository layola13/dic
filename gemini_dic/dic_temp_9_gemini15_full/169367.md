pronaos: /ˈproʊneɪɒs/ | n. |柱廊，前厅 |The entrance hall or porch of a classical temple, typically enclosed by columns.  It is the space between the outer columns and the inner wall of the temple. |The pronaos of the Parthenon was a majestic space, adorned with sculptures.  帕特农神庙的前厅是一个宏伟的空间，装饰着雕塑。| vestibule, portico | naos (the inner sanctuary) |名词
