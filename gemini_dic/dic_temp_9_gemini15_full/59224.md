dystrophias:/daɪˈstrɒfiːz/ | n. |营养不良；肌营养不良症 | A group of diseases characterized by progressive degeneration of muscles and other tissues.  These diseases often lead to weakness, muscle wasting, and other complications depending on the specific type. | Example:  Muscular dystrophies are a group of inherited diseases that cause progressive muscle weakness and loss of muscle tissue. = 肌营养不良症是一组遗传性疾病，会导致进行性肌肉无力和肌肉组织丢失。  There are various types of dystrophies affecting different parts of the body. = 各种营养不良会影响身体的不同部位。 | 近义词：myopathies, degenerations | 反义词：hypertrophy
