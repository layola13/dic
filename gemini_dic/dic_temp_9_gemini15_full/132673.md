nondispensable:/ˌnɒndɪˈspɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可或缺的，必不可少的 |  Essential; absolutely necessary.  Something that is nondispensable is so important that it cannot be done without. |


例句：

Water is nondispensable for human survival. = 水对于人类生存是不可或缺的。

His expertise is nondispensable to the project's success. = 他的专业知识对项目的成功至关重要。


近义词：essential, indispensable, crucial, vital, critical, necessary

反义词：unnecessary, dispensable, optional, superfluous


词性：形容词
