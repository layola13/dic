focimeter:/foʊˈsɪmɪtər/| n. |焦距计|A focimeter is an instrument used to measure the focal length of a lens or optical system.  It's commonly used in ophthalmology to determine the refractive power of an eye or lens.  |例句：The optometrist used a focimeter to determine the correct prescription for my glasses.验光师用焦距计确定我的眼镜的正确处方。

The technician carefully calibrated the focimeter before testing the lens. 技术人员在测试镜头之前仔细校准了焦距计。|近义词：lensometer, refractor|反义词：无|词性:noun
