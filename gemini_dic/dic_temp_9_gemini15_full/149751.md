palatalizes:/ˈpælətəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |软腭化；使成颚音| To change the pronunciation of a consonant by raising the tongue towards the hard palate.  This often results in a shift in the sound of the consonant. |The speaker palatalizes the 'k' sound in the word 'key'. 发音者在单词“key”（钥匙）中将“k”音软腭化。|近义词：soften (in the context of sounds), modify (in the context of pronunciation)|反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of processes that *de-palatalize* sounds.
