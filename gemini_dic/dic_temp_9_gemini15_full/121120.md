misdeclare:/ˌmɪs.dɪˈkleər/ | vt. | 错报；误报；谎报 | To declare incorrectly or falsely.  This typically involves providing inaccurate or misleading information, often intentionally. | 例句：He misdeclared his income to avoid paying higher taxes. 他为了避免支付更高的税款而谎报了自己的收入。

例句：The company misdeclared the amount of hazardous waste it produced. 这家公司谎报了其产生的危险废物数量。


近义词：understate, misrepresent, falsify, misreport


反义词：accurately declare, truthfully report


词性：动词
