gonophore:/ˈɡɒnəfɔːr/|/ˈɡɑːnəfɔːr/| n. |生殖體, 胞芽|A gonophore is a structure in certain hydrozoans (a class of aquatic animals) that produces reproductive polyps, either medusae (the free-swimming form) or gametes (sex cells).  It is essentially a specialized bud that develops into reproductive structures.| Example: The gonophores of this hydrozoan species are particularly large and easily visible.|近义词: reproductive bud, medusa bud |反义词:N/A
