platefuls:/ˈpleɪtfuls/| n.|满盘的量；一盘的量|A plateful is the amount of something that fills a plate.  It often refers to food.|例句：She served three platefuls of pasta. 她盛了三盘意大利面。

例句：The children ate two platefuls of cookies. 孩子们吃了两盘饼干。


近义词：servings, portions


反义词：None directly applicable.  The opposite would be a very small amount, not a specific word.


词性:名词
