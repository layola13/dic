kerf:/kɜːrf/ | n. | 槽，缝，刻痕 | A cut or notch made in a surface, especially one made by a saw.  It often refers to the narrow cut made by a saw blade. | Example: The kerf of the saw blade was very narrow.  锯条的缝隙非常窄。

The kerf in the wood was surprisingly deep. 木头上的槽出乎意料的深。| 近义词：slot, groove, slit, cut | 反义词：weld, join, fuse
