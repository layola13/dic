narky:/ˈnɑːrki/| adj.|脾气暴躁的；易怒的；爱抱怨的|  Irritable, bad-tempered, and complaining.  The word often implies a slightly unpleasant or spiteful personality.|例句：He's been really narky all day. 他今天一整天都脾气暴躁。

例句：She's got a narky disposition and is always complaining. 她性情暴躁，总是抱怨。


近义词：irritable, grumpy, bad-tempered, crabby, surly, grumpy, crotchety


反义词：cheerful, good-natured, pleasant, amiable


词性：形容词
