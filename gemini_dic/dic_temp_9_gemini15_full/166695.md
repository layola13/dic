preshape:/ˈpriːʃeɪp/| vt.| 预先成形；预先塑造 | To give a preliminary shape or form to something; to mold or fashion something in advance.  |例句：The clay was preshaped before being placed on the potter's wheel.  黏土在放在陶轮上之前就已经预先成形了。

例句：The metal was preshaped using a die before final forging. 金属在最终锻造之前使用模具预先成形。


近义词：preform, mold, shape, fashion, form


反义词：deform, reshape, unshape


词性:动词
