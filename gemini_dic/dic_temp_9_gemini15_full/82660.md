It can also refer to informal or non-standard expressions in language. |例句：The rebels employed various guerrillaisms to outwit the government forces.  游击队采用各种游击战策略来智胜政府军。

例句：His speech was full of colloquialisms and guerrillaisms, making it difficult for outsiders to understand. 他的演讲充满了口语化表达和非标准用法，让外人难以理解。
