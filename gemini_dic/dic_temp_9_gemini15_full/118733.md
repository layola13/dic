metagraphy:/mɛˈtæɡrəfi/ | n. |元叙事学，元图像学 |The study of the meta-narrative structures and conventions of a particular genre or medium, especially in relation to its representation of reality.  It explores how narratives about narratives shape our understanding of the world.  |例句：Her dissertation delves into the metagraphy of superhero comics, analyzing how they construct and deconstruct their own mythologies.  她的论文深入探讨了超级英雄漫画的元叙事学，分析了它们如何构建和解构自身的神话。|近义词：metanarrative analysis, narrative theory |反义词： (lacks a direct antonym, but could be contrasted with) literal interpretation, surface-level analysis
