intention:/ɪnˈtɛnʃən/| n. |意图；目的；打算 |The intention is to finish the project by the end of the month. |计划在本月底前完成这个项目。|purpose, aim, goal |indifference, apathy |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His intention is to travel the world. = 他的意图是环游世界。
What is your intention in doing this? = 你这么做的目的是什么？


一般过去时:
Her intention was to help, but she made things worse. = 她的本意是帮忙，但她却把事情弄得更糟了。
What was your intention when you said that? = 你当时说那句话的意图是什么？


现在完成时:
His intention has been to support his family. = 他一直以来的意图都是养家糊口。
Have you changed your intention? = 你改变主意了吗？


过去完成时:
My intention had been to go to the party, but I got sick. = 我原本打算去参加聚会，但我病倒了。
They had no intention of harming anyone. = 他们无意伤害任何人。


将来时:
I intend to visit my parents next week. = 我打算下周去看望父母。
What do you intend to do about it? = 你打算怎么办？


虚拟语气:
I would have gone if I had had the intention. = 如果我有这个打算的话，我就去了。
If he had the intention, he would have helped. = 如果他有这个意图，他会帮忙的。


被动语态:
The intention was misunderstood. = 这个意图被误解了。
It was his intention to deceive us. = 他本意是想欺骗我们。


疑问句:
What is your intention? = 你的意图是什么？
Do you have any intention of resigning? = 你有辞职的打算吗？


否定句:
I have no intention of apologizing. = 我没有道歉的意思。
They had no intention of causing trouble. = 他们无意制造麻烦。


PHRASE->
with the intention of  = 打算...
have every intention of = 打算...
good intentions = 好意
with malice aforethought = 蓄意
beyond intention = 非故意


词源:
intention comes from the Old French "intencion,"  itself derived from the Latin "intendere," meaning "to stretch out," "to aim," or "to direct."  The sense of purpose and aim evolved from the literal meaning of stretching or directing something towards a goal.
