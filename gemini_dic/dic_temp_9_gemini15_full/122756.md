moisturises:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪzɪz/ | v. | 使湿润；滋润 | To make (something) moist or damp; to apply a moisturizing substance to (skin, hair, etc.).  | Example:  She moisturises her face every morning. 她每天早上都会给脸部保湿。 The cream moisturises dry skin effectively.  这款面霜能有效滋润干燥的皮肤。 |近义词: hydrate, condition, rehydrate | 反义词: dry out, dehydrate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She moisturises her face every day. = 她每天都给脸部保湿。
The lotion moisturises my skin effectively. = 这款乳液有效滋润我的皮肤。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:
