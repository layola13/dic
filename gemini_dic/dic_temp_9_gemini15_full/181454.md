rejigger:/rɪˈdʒɪɡər/| vt.|重新调整；修改；篡改|To reorganize or readjust something; to alter or modify something.|例句：We need to rejigger the schedule to accommodate the new client. 我们需要重新调整日程安排以适应新客户。

例句：The marketing team decided to rejigger their campaign after the poor initial results.  市场团队在最初结果不佳后决定修改他们的宣传活动。


近义词：rearrange, reorganize, revamp, revise, modify|反义词：leave alone, maintain, preserve|动词
