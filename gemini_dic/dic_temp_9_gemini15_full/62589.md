enfeebled:/ɪnˈfiːbld/ | adj. |虚弱的，衰弱的 | Weakened; deprived of strength.  The illness left him enfeebled and unable to work.  | The prolonged illness left him enfeebled.  他长期生病，身体虚弱。 His enfeebled state prevented him from attending the ceremony.  他虚弱的状态使他无法参加仪式。 | weakened, debilitated, frail, feeble | strong, robust, vigorous, healthy
