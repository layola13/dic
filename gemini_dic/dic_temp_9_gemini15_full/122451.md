mobilehome:/ˈmoʊbaɪlˌhoʊm/ | n. |活动房屋，移动房屋 | A prefabricated structure designed for mobile living, typically towed to a designated site.  It is often used as permanent or semi-permanent housing. |例句：They live in a mobile home on the outskirts of town. 他们住在城郊的一辆活动房屋里。  |例句：Many retirees choose to live in mobile home parks for their affordability and community.  许多退休人员选择住在移动房屋公园，因为那里价格实惠，而且有社区。|近义词：trailer, manufactured home, mobile house |反义词：brick-and-mortar house, permanent dwelling |名词
