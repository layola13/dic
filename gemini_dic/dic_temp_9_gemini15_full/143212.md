outcrops:/ˈaʊtkrɒps/| n. |露头；出露地层|Exposed parts of a rock formation that appear at the surface of the Earth. Outcrops are important for geologists to study the Earth's structure and history.|The mountain's rocky outcrops offered stunning views.  这座山的岩石露头提供了令人惊叹的景色。|The geologist carefully examined the outcrops along the riverbank. 地质学家仔细检查了河岸边的露头。|exposures, afflorments | buried, concealed |
