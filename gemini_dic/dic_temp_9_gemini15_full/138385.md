notorious:/noʊˈtɔːriəs/| adj. |臭名昭著的，声名狼藉的 | Widely known for being bad or evil.  The adjective "notorious" describes someone or something that is famous or well-known for a negative reason, often involving wickedness, scandal, or infamy. |例句：The city is notorious for its high crime rate. 这个城市以其高犯罪率而臭名昭著。|例句：He is notorious for his bad temper. 他以坏脾气而臭名昭著。|近义词：infamous, scandalous, disreputable |反义词：renowned, respected, esteemed
