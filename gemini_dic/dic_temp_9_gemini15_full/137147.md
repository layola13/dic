nonsubstitution: /ˌnɒnsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | n. |非替换；不替代 |The act or instance of not substituting something.  It implies a deliberate choice to avoid replacement or exchange.  This term is often used in linguistics, chemistry, and other fields where substitution is a common operation.|例句：The nonsubstitution of the original ingredient resulted in a noticeably different flavor.  (原食材的未被替代导致味道明显不同。)  The study examined the effects of nonsubstitution on the reaction. (该研究考察了不替代对反应的影响。) |近义词：omission, avoidance, refusal to substitute |反义词：substitution, replacement
