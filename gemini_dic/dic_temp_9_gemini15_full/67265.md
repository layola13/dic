expurge:/ɪkˈspɜːrdʒ/| vt.|清除；净化；剔除|To remove or eliminate something unwanted, especially something considered objectionable or harmful.  This often involves a thorough and systematic process.|例句：The government expurgated all references to the dictator from the history books.  政府从历史书中删除了所有关于独裁者的记载。

例句：The editor expurgated the offensive language from the manuscript. 编辑从稿件中删除了攻击性语言。


近义词：purge, delete, remove, eliminate, censor, expunge


反义词：add, include, insert, retain


词性：动词
