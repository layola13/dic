This word is grammatically incorrect and should not be used.  The correct word is "equipment," which is an uncountable noun and therefore does not have different tense conjugations.  Examples using "equipment" are shown below.


一般现在时:
The company needs new equipment. = 公司需要新设备。
We use specialized equipment for this task. = 我们这项任务使用专用设备。


一般过去时:
They bought new equipment last year. = 他们去年买了新设备。
The team lacked essential equipment. = 该团队缺乏必要的设备。


现在完成时:
The factory has received its new equipment. = 工厂已经收到了它的新设备。
We have upgraded our equipment recently. = 我们最近升级了我们的设备。


过去完成时:
They had already installed the equipment before the deadline. = 他们已经在截止日期前安装好了设备。
The engineers had prepared all the necessary equipment. = 工程师们已经准备好了所有必要的设备。


将来时:
They will buy new equipment next month. = 他们下个月将购买新设备。
We will need more equipment for the project. = 我们这个项目将需要更多设备。


虚拟语气:
If we had the right equipment, we could finish the job faster. = 如果我们有合适的设备，我们就能更快地完成工作。
I wish we had better equipment. = 我希望我们有更好的设备。


被动语态:

疑问句:
What kind of equipment do you need? = 你需要什么类型的设备？

否定句:
