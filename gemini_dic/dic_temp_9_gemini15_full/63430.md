entreat:/ɪnˈtriːt/| vt.|恳求；哀求；乞求|To ask someone earnestly or beg for something.|例句：I entreat you to reconsider your decision. 我恳求你重新考虑你的决定。

例句：She entreated him to help her. 她恳求他帮助她。


近义词：beg, implore, beseech, plead|反义词：command, order, demand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He entreats his father for forgiveness. 他恳求父亲原谅他。
She entreats her friends for help. 她恳求朋友们帮助她。


一般过去时:
He entreated his father for forgiveness. 他恳求父亲原谅他。
She entreated her friends for help. 她恳求朋友们帮助她。


现在完成时:
She has entreated him repeatedly for help. 她反复恳求他帮忙。
He has entreated his boss for a raise. 他一再恳求老板加薪。


过去完成时:
He had entreated his father many times before he finally relented. 他在父亲最终让步之前已经多次恳求他了。
She had entreated him to leave, but he refused. 她曾恳求他离开，但他拒绝了。


将来时:
I will entreat him to reconsider. 我将恳求他重新考虑。
They will entreat the court for mercy. 他们将恳求法院宽大处理。


虚拟语气:
I would entreat you to reconsider, if I were you. 如果我是你，我会恳求你重新考虑。
If I had known, I would have entreated him to stay. 如果我知道的话，我就会恳求他留下。


被动语态:
He was entreated by his sister to help. 他被姐姐恳求帮忙。
The judge was entreated to show leniency. 法官被恳求宽大处理。


疑问句:
Will you entreat him on my behalf? 你会代表我恳求他吗？
Did she entreat you to help her? 她恳求你帮助她了吗？


否定句:
He did not entreat her for money. 他没有恳求她给钱。
She didn't entreat them to change their minds. 她没有恳求他们改变主意。


PHRASE->
entreat earnestly  恳切地恳求
entreat humbly  谦卑地恳求
entreat fervently  热切地恳求
entreat incessantly  不断地恳求
entreat for mercy 恳求宽恕


词源:
Old French *entraiter (to treat, handle), from Latin *inter- + tractare (to treat, handle).  The sense shifted from general treatment to earnest pleading.
