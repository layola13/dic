localizing:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使本地化；使适应当地情况 | The process of adapting a product, service, or content to a specific local market. This includes translating text, adapting images and videos, and considering cultural nuances.  | Example:  The company is localizing its software for the Japanese market.  该公司正在将其软件本地化以适应日本市场。

Example: We need to localize the website for better user experience in different regions.我们需要将网站本地化，以提升不同地区用户的体验。|近义词：adapting, customizing | 反义词：globalizing, standardizing
