crackdown:/ˈkrækdaʊn/| n. v.|镇压；严厉打击|A crackdown is a series of actions taken to suppress something considered undesirable, such as crime or dissent.  It often involves increased enforcement of laws and regulations.|例句：The government launched a crackdown on drug trafficking.  政府对贩毒进行了严厉打击。|例句：There was a police crackdown on speeding drivers.警方对超速驾驶者进行了严厉打击。|近义词：suppression, repression, clampdown |反义词：leniency, tolerance|名词，动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police crack down on illegal parking. = 警方严厉打击非法停车。
The government cracks down on corruption. = 政府严厉打击腐败。

一般过去时:
The authorities cracked down on protesters. = 当局镇压了抗议者。
They cracked down on illegal gambling. = 他们打击了非法赌博。

现在完成时:
The city has cracked down on noise pollution. = 该城市已经严厉打击噪音污染。
The school has cracked down on cheating. = 学校已经严厉打击作弊行为。

过去完成时:
The police had cracked down on the gang before the major crime occurred. = 在重大犯罪发生之前，警方已经对该团伙进行了打击。
The company had cracked down on employee misconduct before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公司已经对员工的不当行为进行了打击。

将来时:
The government will crack down on tax evasion. = 政府将严厉打击逃税行为。
They will crack down on underage drinking. = 他们将打击未成年人饮酒。

虚拟语气:
If the government cracked down harder on crime, the streets would be safer. = 如果政府更严厉地打击犯罪，街道会更安全。
I wish they would crack down on the noise from the construction site. = 我希望他们能制止建筑工地的噪音。

被动语态:
Illegal activities are being cracked down on by the authorities. = 当局正在严厉打击非法活动。
The protests were cracked down on brutally. = 抗议活动受到了残酷的镇压。

疑问句:
Will the government crack down on these illegal businesses? = 政府会打击这些非法企业吗？
Is the police cracking down on speeding? = 警方正在打击超速行为吗？

否定句:
The government did not crack down on the protesters. = 政府没有镇压抗议者。
They haven't cracked down on the problem yet. = 他们还没有解决这个问题。
