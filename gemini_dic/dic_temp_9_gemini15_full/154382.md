permanents: /ˈpɜːrmənənts/| n.|永久性事物；永久波；正式成员|Something that is permanent lasts or continues for a very long time or forever.  It can also refer to a type of hairstyle.|The new additions to the staff are all permanents. 新员工都是正式员工。

The museum's collection includes many permanent exhibits.  博物馆的收藏包括许多永久性展品。


近义词：lasting, enduring, fixed, stable|反义词：temporary, transient, ephemeral, fleeting
