sinhalese:/ˌsɪŋhəˈliːz/ | adj. n. |僧伽罗语；僧伽罗人；僧伽罗的 | Of or relating to Sinhalese people, their language, or their culture.  | The Sinhalese language is an Indo-Aryan language. |近义词:  Ceylonese (less preferred now)|反义词: None


英文解析:
Sinhalese refers to the people of Sri Lanka who trace their ancestry to the island's earliest inhabitants.  It also describes their language, which is an Indo-Aryan language, and their culture.  While "Ceylonese" was once used interchangeably,  "Sinhalese" is now preferred to avoid ambiguity and political sensitivity surrounding the island's diverse populations.
