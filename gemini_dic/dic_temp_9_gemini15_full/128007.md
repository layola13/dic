nephritic:/nɪˈfrɪtɪk/| adj. |肾炎的；肾脏的| Relating to or affected by nephritis (inflammation of the kidney).  The term often implies a serious condition. |例句：The patient presented with nephritic syndrome, characterized by hematuria and edema.  病人出现肾炎综合征，其特征是血尿和水肿。

例句：He suffered from nephritic pain in his lower back. 他遭受着下背部的肾脏疼痛。


近义词：renal (referring to the kidneys, but less specifically implying disease) |反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting kidney health could be considered antonyms in context.


词性：形容词
