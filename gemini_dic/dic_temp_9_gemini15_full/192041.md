英文解析:
As a noun, "scotch" can refer to Scotch whisky (a type of whisky from Scotland), a person from Scotland, or a type of adhesive tape. As a verb, it means to deliberately damage the surface of something by scratching it, to suppress or put an end to something, or to stick something using adhesive tape.

He scotched the rumour by releasing a statement. 他发表声明平息了谣言。
