enablement: /ɪˈneɪblmənt/ | n. | 授权；使能够；授权机制 | The process of empowering individuals or groups to achieve their full potential.  It often involves providing resources, training, and support. | Example: The company's employee enablement program focuses on providing training and development opportunities.  公司的员工赋能计划侧重于提供培训和发展机会。| 近义词：empowerment, capacity building, facilitation | 反义词：disability, incapacitation
