disoxygenate:/dɪsˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/ | vt. |脱氧；除氧| To remove oxygen from (a substance or environment).  The process involves reducing the amount of dissolved oxygen present.  This can be achieved through various methods, such as chemical reactions, physical processes (like boiling), or biological means.|例句：The experiment aimed to disoxygenate the water sample before testing.  实验旨在测试前去除水样中的氧气。

The process of disoxygenating the blood can lead to tissue damage.  血液脱氧的过程会导致组织损伤。


近义词：deoxygenate, deprive of oxygen |反义词：oxygenate
