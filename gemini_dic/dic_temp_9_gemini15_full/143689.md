outleap:/ˌaʊtˈliːp/| v.|跳出，超越，超过|To jump or spring out; to surpass or exceed.|例句：The rabbit outleaped the fox. 兔子跳过了狐狸。 The company's profits outleaped expectations. 公司的利润超出了预期。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, transcend|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outleaps the fence. = 他跳过了栅栏。
The athlete outleaps his rivals in the long jump. = 运动员在跳远比赛中超过了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The deer outleaped the hunter. = 鹿跳过了猎人。
She outleaped all expectations with her performance. = 她以她的表现超越了所有人的期望。


现在完成时:
The runner has outleaped all his previous records. = 赛跑者已经超越了他之前的所有纪录。
He has outleaped his own personal best. = 他已经超越了他自己的最佳成绩。


过去完成时:
The horse had outleaped the obstacle before anyone realised. = 马在任何人意识到之前就已经越过了障碍。
By the time I arrived, the child had outleaped his fear. = 当我到达时，孩子已经克服了他的恐惧。


将来时:
The kangaroo will outleap the fence easily. = 袋鼠很容易就能跳过围栏。
I believe she will outleap the competition in the upcoming race. = 我相信她会在即将到来的比赛中超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
