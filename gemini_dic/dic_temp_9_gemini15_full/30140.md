ceiling: /ˈsiːlɪŋ/ | n. | 天花板；上限；最高限度 | The upper interior surface of a room.  The limit of something. | 例句：The ceiling was painted a soft blue.  天花板漆成了浅蓝色。

例句：The company reached its sales ceiling.  这家公司达到了销售额上限。


近义词：overhead, top, limit, maximum | 反义词：floor, bottom, minimum


词源:  From Old French *ceil, ciel (“sky, heaven”), from Latin caelum (“sky, heaven”). The original sense referred to the "sky" or "heaven" above, which then evolved to mean the overhead surface of a room.
