correspondence:/ˌkɒrəˈspɒndəns/ /ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndəns/| n.| 通信，信件；一致，符合 | The act of communicating by exchanging letters;  agreement or similarity. | Example:  I maintain a regular correspondence with my pen pal in France. = 我定期与我在法国的笔友通信。  There is a close correspondence between the two maps. = 这两张地图非常相似。 |近义词：communication, agreement, conformity |反义词：disagreement, discrepancy, difference


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company maintains a regular correspondence with its clients. = 公司定期与客户保持联系。
Our findings correspond with previous research. = 我们的发现与之前的研究相符。


一般过去时:
They exchanged a lengthy correspondence before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前交换了大量的信件。
The two accounts corresponded in most details. = 这两个账户的大部分细节都是一致的。


现在完成时:
We have received no correspondence regarding your application. = 我们没有收到关于您申请的任何信件。
Their explanations have corresponded perfectly with the facts. = 他们的解释与事实完全吻合。


过去完成时:
They had already exchanged several letters before meeting in person. = 他们在亲自见面之前已经交换了几封信。
The results had corresponded to their predictions. = 结果与他们的预测相符。


将来时:

The data will likely correspond to the model's predictions. = 数据很可能与模型的预测相符。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
