overmodernize:/ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdənaɪz/ | vt. | 过度现代化 | To modernize to an excessive degree, often resulting in the loss of valuable traditional elements or creating impractical or inefficient systems.  |例句：They overmodernized the old town, destroying its historical charm.  他们过度现代化了老城，破坏了它的历史魅力。
They overmodernized the factory, leading to increased costs and decreased efficiency. 他们过度现代化了工厂，导致成本增加和效率降低。| 近义词：overhaul, revamp, reconstruct (with negative connotations)| 反义词：preserve, conserve, maintain traditional aspects | 动词
