sandpapers:/ˈsændˌpeɪpərz/ | n. | 砂纸 |  Sandpapers are abrasive papers used for smoothing surfaces. They are made of sandpaper glued to a backing material.  | Example: He used fine-grit sandpapers to smooth the wood before painting. 他用细砂纸打磨木材，然后才上漆。 | 近义词：emery paper, abrasive paper | 反义词：None  |
