reforest:/ˌriːˈfɒrɪst/| vt.|重新造林；使复绿|To plant trees on land that has been deforested.  The act of replanting trees in a previously forested area. |例句：The government is planning to reforest the area after the devastating wildfire. 政府计划在毁灭性野火之后对该地区进行重新造林。

例句：They are working hard to reforest the hillsides that were stripped bare by logging. 他们正在努力对被伐木剥光山坡进行重新造林。


近义词：afforest, replant, regenerate


反义词：deforest


词性:动词
