luigi:/ˈluːiːdʒi/| n. |路易吉|A common Italian masculine given name, meaning "famous in war". It is a variation of Louis. The name is ultimately derived from the Germanic name Chlodovic, composed of the elements *chlod* ("famous") and *wig* ("war").  This name is famously associated with the video game character Luigi from the Mario franchise.|Luigi is a popular name in Italy and other countries.|My brother's name is Luigi.|近义词:Louis, Luis|反义词: N/A|名词
