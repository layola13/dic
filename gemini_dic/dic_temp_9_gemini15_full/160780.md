positioned:/pəˈzɪʃənd/ | v. | 定位；安置；摆放 | To place someone or something in a particular position.  | The furniture was positioned strategically around the room. 家具被有策略地摆放在房间周围。| place, locate, situate | mispositioned, misplaced | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He positions the camera carefully. 他仔细地摆放摄像机。
The company positions itself as a leader in innovation.  这家公司把自己定位为创新的领导者。
