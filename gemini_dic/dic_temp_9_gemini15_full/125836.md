murrey:/ˈmʌri/| adj.|暗红色，红褐色|A dark reddish-brown color.  The word is now largely archaic or used in specialized contexts, such as describing certain types of plums or wine.|The walls were painted a deep murrey.  墙被漆成深红褐色。 The murrey wine was rich and full-bodied. 这红褐色葡萄酒醇厚浓郁。|近义词：maroon, crimson, russet|反义词：pale, light
