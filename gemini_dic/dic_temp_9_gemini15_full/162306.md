preaccounting: /ˌpriːəˈkaʊntɪŋ/ | n. | 预备会计；前期会计 | Preliminary accounting work, typically involving the initial recording and classification of financial transactions before formal accounting processes begin.  It often involves tasks such as gathering source documents, verifying data accuracy, and preparing data for entry into the accounting system. | Example: The preaccounting team worked diligently to prepare the financial data for the upcoming audit. 预备会计团队努力为即将到来的审计准备财务数据。 | 近义词: preliminary accounting, initial accounting | 反义词: post-accounting, final accounting
