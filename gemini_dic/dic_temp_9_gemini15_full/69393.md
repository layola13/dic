feeders:/ˈfiːdərz/| n.|喂食器；饲养者；进料器|A feeder is a device or person that provides food.  It can refer to a device for feeding animals, a person who feeds animals, or a device that feeds material into a machine.|例句：The birds flocked to the feeders in the garden. 花园里的鸟儿成群结队地飞向喂食器。

例句：The farmer is a dedicated feeder of his livestock.  这位农民是位尽职尽责的牲畜饲养员。|近义词：provider, supplier, dispenser|反义词：consumer, recipient
