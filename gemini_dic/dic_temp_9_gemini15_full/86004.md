headtire:/ˈhɛdˌtaɪər/| n.|头饰，头巾|A headtire is an ornamental covering for the head, often worn by women.  It can range from simple scarves to elaborate hats and headdresses.|Example: She wore a beautiful headtire to the wedding.|近义词：headdress, head covering, hat|反义词：None specifically, but "bare head" could be considered an antonym.
