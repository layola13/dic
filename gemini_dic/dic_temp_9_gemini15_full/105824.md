kit:/kɪt/| n.|成套工具；工具箱；成套用品|A set of tools or materials needed for a specific purpose.  |例句：He bought a fishing kit. 他买了一套钓鱼工具。|例句：The science kit contained everything we needed for the experiment. 科学工具箱里包含了我们实验所需的一切。|近义词：set, outfit, collection|反义词：无


USAGE->

The kit includes all the necessary parts. = 这套工具包含所有必要的零件。
This kit is suitable for beginners. = 这套工具适合初学者。
