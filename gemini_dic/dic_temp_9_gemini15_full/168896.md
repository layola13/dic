professional:/prəˈfɛʃənəl/| adj., n.|专业的；职业的；职业人员| Relating to or connected with a profession.|He is a professional musician. 他是一位职业音乐家。|amateur, skilled|unprofessional, unskilled|形容词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a professional basketball player. = 他是一位职业篮球运动员。
She works as a professional translator. = 她从事专业翻译工作。


一般过去时:
He was a professional photographer before he retired. = 他退休前是一位职业摄影师。
She was a professional dancer in her youth. = 她年轻时是一位职业舞蹈演员。


现在完成时:
She has been a professional writer for ten years. = 她已经是一位职业作家十年了。
He has worked as a professional consultant for many years. = 他多年来一直担任专业顾问。


过去完成时:
He had been a professional athlete before his injury. = 他受伤前是一位职业运动员。
She had already established herself as a professional artist before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，她已经确立了自己作为专业艺术家的地位。


将来时:
He will be a professional pilot next year. = 他明年将成为一名职业飞行员。
She will become a professional singer after graduation. = 她毕业后将成为一名职业歌手。


虚拟语气:
If I were a professional chef, I would open my own restaurant. = 如果我是一位职业厨师，我会开一家自己的餐厅。
I wish I were a professional musician. = 我希望我是一位职业音乐家。


被动语态:
He was hired as a professional consultant. = 他被聘为专业顾问。
The project was managed by a professional team. = 该项目由一个专业的团队管理。


疑问句:
Are you a professional artist? = 你是职业艺术家吗？
Is he a professional athlete? = 他是职业运动员吗？


否定句:
He is not a professional musician; he just plays for fun. = 他不是职业音乐家；他只是为了好玩才演奏。
She is not a professional writer; she only writes as a hobby. = 她不是职业作家；她只是把写作当作爱好。
