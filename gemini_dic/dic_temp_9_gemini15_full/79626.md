glossaries:/ˈɡlɒsəˌriːz/ | n. |词汇表；术语表 | A glossary is an alphabetical list of terms in a particular domain of knowledge with their definitions.  It is often included at the end of a book or document to help readers understand specialized vocabulary. | Example: The scientific paper included a glossary of technical terms at the end.  这篇科学论文在结尾处包含了一个技术术语表。|近义词：lexicon, terminology, index of terms |反义词：N/A
