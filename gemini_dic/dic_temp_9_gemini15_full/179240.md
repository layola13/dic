redispute:/ˌriːdɪˈspjuːt/| vt.|再争论，重新辩论|To dispute again; to contest or argue about something again.|例句：The two sides redisputed the terms of the contract after a week of silence.  双方在沉默一周后再次就合同条款进行争论。|例句：The judge refused to redispute the already settled case.  法官拒绝重新审理已经结案的案件。|近义词：reargue, contest, debate, challenge|反义词：agree, accept, concede|动词
