meetup:/ˈmiːtʌp/| n. |聚会，会面|A meetup is an informal gathering of people, often organized around a shared interest or activity.  It's typically arranged online and then takes place in person.| 例句：There's a meetup for photography enthusiasts every Friday evening.  周五晚上有个摄影爱好者的聚会。| 例句： We arranged a meetup at the coffee shop to discuss the project. 我们在咖啡店安排了一次会面来讨论项目。|近义词：gathering, meeting, assembly, rendezvous|反义词：separation, parting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The group has a meetup every week. = 这个小组每周都有聚会。
We schedule a meetup online. = 我们在网上安排聚会。


一般过去时:
They had a meetup at the park yesterday. = 他们昨天在公园聚会了。
I attended a meetup last night. = 我昨晚参加了一个聚会。


现在完成时:
We have had many successful meetups this year. = 今年我们举办了很多成功的聚会。
They have already planned their next meetup. = 他们已经计划好了下一次聚会。


过去完成时:

They had finished their meetup before dinner. = 他们晚饭前已经结束了聚会。
