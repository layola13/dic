faradize:/ˈfærədaɪz/| vt.|电疗；用电流治疗|To apply a mild electric current to (a part of the body) for therapeutic purposes.  This term is now largely obsolete, replaced by more modern electrotherapy techniques.|例句：The doctor decided to faradize the patient's muscles to relieve the pain. 医生决定用电疗法治疗病人的肌肉以缓解疼痛。  The patient underwent faradization to stimulate nerve function. 病人接受了电疗以刺激神经功能。|近义词：electrify (in a therapeutic context), electrotherapy|反义词：None specifically, but  "to leave untreated" could be considered an antonym in the context of medical treatment.
