predoom:/priːˈduːm/| vt.|预先注定;预先判决|To predetermine or foreordain, especially to something bad or unfortunate.|例句：The prophecy seemed to predoom the hero to a tragic end.  预言似乎预先注定英雄以悲剧收场。|例句：His reckless actions seemed to predoom his failure. 他的鲁莽行为似乎预先注定了他会失败。|近义词：foreordain, preordain, doom, destine|反义词：bless, save
