nights:/naɪts/| n.|夜晚，黑夜|The plural of "night".  Refers to multiple instances of the period of darkness between sunset and sunrise.  It can also refer to a period of time, often associated with darkness or obscurity.|例句：The summer nights were warm and pleasant. 夏夜温暖宜人。|例句：He spent many nights working on his project. 他花了许多夜晚在他的项目上工作。|近义词：evenings, dark hours|反义词：days
