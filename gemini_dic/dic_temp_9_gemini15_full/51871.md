dextrousness: /ˈdekstrəsnəs/ | n. |灵巧；熟练；敏捷 | Skill and dexterity in the use of hands or body; the ability to perform tasks skillfully and efficiently.  |例句：The surgeon's dextrousness was evident in the precision of the operation.  外科医生的灵巧在手术的精确性中显而易见。

例句：Her dextrousness with a needle allowed her to create intricate embroidery. 她用针的灵巧让她能够创作出复杂的刺绣。


近义词：skill, agility, dexterity, adeptness, proficiency


反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptitude


词源：源自拉丁语dexter，意思是“右边的，灵巧的”。  This reflects the historical association of right-handedness with skill and ability.  这反映了历史上将右手与技能和能力联系起来的情况。
