proemployment: /ˌproʊɪmˈplɔɪmənt/ | adj. | 支持就业的；促进就业的 |  Relating to or supporting policies and practices that encourage employment and job creation.  It emphasizes the positive aspects of employment and aims to increase job opportunities. | 例句：The government launched a proemployment initiative to reduce unemployment. = 政府启动了一项支持就业的倡议以降低失业率。

The proemployment policies of the administration have led to a significant decrease in unemployment rates. = 政府的促进就业政策导致失业率大幅下降。


近义词：job-creating, employment-boosting, employment-oriented


反义词：anti-employment, job-destroying


词性：形容词
