declares:/dɪˈklɛərz/| vt.|宣布；声明；断言|To state something clearly and formally.  |例句：The government declared a state of emergency. 政府宣布进入紧急状态。|例句：She declared her love for him. 她向他表白了爱意。|近义词：announce, proclaim, assert, affirm|反义词：conceal, hide, deny, suppress|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The judge declares the defendant guilty. = 法官宣判被告有罪。
She declares her intention to run for office. = 她宣布她打算竞选公职。

一般过去时:
He declared war on his rival. = 他向他的对手宣战。
The company declared a dividend. = 公司宣布派息。

现在完成时:
They have declared their support for the project. = 他们已经宣布支持该项目。
She has declared her candidacy for mayor. = 她已经宣布竞选市长。

过去完成时:
He had declared his independence before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前，他已经宣布独立。
The committee had declared the results invalid. = 委员会已经宣布结果无效。

将来时:
The president will declare a new policy next week. = 总统下周将宣布一项新政策。
They will declare their intentions at the meeting.  = 他们将在会议上宣布他们的意图。

虚拟语气:
If he declared his love, she would be thrilled. = 如果他表白，她会非常激动。
I wish he would declare his intentions clearly. = 我希望他能清楚地表明他的意图。

被动语态:
The winner was declared by the judges. = 获胜者由评委宣布。
The new law has been declared unconstitutional. = 新法律已被宣布违宪。

疑问句:
Will the government declare a national holiday? = 政府会宣布全国假日吗？
Has the company declared bankruptcy? = 公司已经宣布破产了吗？

否定句:
He didn't declare his income correctly. = 他没有正确申报他的收入。
She refused to declare her allegiance to the new regime. = 她拒绝宣誓效忠新政权。
