holdback:/ˈhoʊldˌbæk/| n. vt.|压制；隐瞒；阻止；保留；迟缓|to prevent something from happening or developing; to restrain or control something; to keep something secret; to delay something|例句：The government is holding back information about the accident. 政府隐瞒了关于事故的信息。
The strong wind held back the boat. 强风阻碍了船只前进。|近义词：suppress, restrain, conceal, withhold, delay|反义词：release, disclose, accelerate, promote


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He holds back his emotions. 他压抑自己的情绪。
They hold back information from the press. 他们向媒体隐瞒信息。

一般过去时:
She held back tears during the ceremony. 她在仪式上忍住了眼泪。
The company held back the release of the new product. 公司推迟了新产品的发布。

现在完成时:
He has held back his anger for too long. 他压抑怒火太久了。
The project has been held back due to funding issues.  由于资金问题，项目进展受阻。

过去完成时:
She had held back the truth from her family for years. 她多年来对家人隐瞒了真相。
The rain had held back the start of the game. 雨推迟了比赛的开始。

将来时:
They will hold back the announcement until next week. 他们会把公告推迟到下周。
I will hold back nothing from you. 我不会对你有所隐瞒。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't held back, he would have won the race. 如果他不压制自己，他就能赢得比赛。
I wish I hadn't held back my feelings. 我真希望我没有压抑自己的感情。

被动语态:
The release of the report was held back. 报告的发布被推迟了。
His promotion was held back due to his poor performance. 由于表现不佳，他的晋升被搁置了。

疑问句:
Are you holding back anything from me? 你对我有所隐瞒吗？
Why did they hold back the evidence? 他们为什么隐瞒证据？

否定句:
He didn't hold back his opinions during the meeting. 他在会议上没有隐瞒自己的意见。
She didn't hold back from expressing her anger. 她毫不犹豫地表达了自己的愤怒。
