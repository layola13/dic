resculpturing:/ˌriːˈskʌlptʃərɪŋ/| v. n.|重塑；再塑；重新塑造|The act of reshaping or remodeling something, especially a sculpture or a physical feature.  It implies a significant alteration or refinement of the original form.|例句：The artist is resculpturing the clay figure, adding finer details to the face.  艺术家正在重塑粘土雕像，为脸部添加更精细的细节。

例句：The plastic surgeon performed resculpturing on the patient's nose to improve its shape. 整形外科医生对病人的鼻子进行了重塑手术，以改善其形状。


近义词：remodeling, reshaping, reforming, remaking|反义词：destroying, demolishing|动词
