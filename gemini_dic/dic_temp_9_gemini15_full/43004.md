cowardish:/ˈkaʊərdɪʃ/| adj.|懦弱的，胆小的|Lacking courage; timid.|The cowardly soldier deserted his post.|近义词：timid, pusillanimous, craven|反义词：brave, courageous, valiant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cowardly in the face of danger. = 他面对危险时很懦弱。
She acts cowardly when confronted with problems. = 她遇到问题时表现得很胆小。

一般过去时:
He was cowardly when he refused to help. = 他拒绝帮忙的时候很懦弱。
They acted cowardly during the crisis. = 他们在危机期间表现得很胆小。

现在完成时:
He has always been cowardly throughout his life. = 他一生都胆小如鼠。
She has shown cowardly behavior repeatedly. = 她反复表现出懦弱的行为。

过去完成时:
He had been cowardly before, but this time he was brave. = 他以前曾胆小过，但这次他很勇敢。
They had acted cowardly in the past, leading to regret. = 他们过去的行为懦弱，导致后悔。

将来时:
He will probably be cowardly again in a similar situation. = 在类似的情况下，他可能会再次胆小。
She will likely act cowardly if faced with real danger. = 如果面临真正的危险，她可能会表现得很胆小。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't so cowardly, he would have stood up for himself. = 如果他不那么懦弱，他就会为自己辩护。
I wish he weren't so cowardly and would face his fears. = 我希望他不那么懦弱，能够面对恐惧。

被动语态:
His actions were seen as cowardly by many. = 许多人认为他的行为很懦弱。
His behavior is considered cowardly and unacceptable. = 他的行为被认为是懦弱和不可接受的。

疑问句:
Is it cowardly to run away from a fight? = 逃离战斗是懦弱的吗？
Was his refusal to help cowardly? = 他拒绝帮助是懦弱的吗？

否定句:
He was not cowardly; he was simply cautious. = 他并不懦弱；他只是谨慎。
Her actions were not cowardly; they were strategic. = 她的行为并不懦弱；它们是有策略的。
