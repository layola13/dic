rededication: /ˌriːˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |重新奉献；再献身；重新致力于 | The act of dedicating something again; renewing a commitment or devotion.  It often implies a renewed or strengthened commitment after a period of lapse or decline. |例句：The rededication of the church involved a complete renovation and a celebratory service.  教堂的重新奉献仪式包括一次彻底的翻修和一场庆祝活动。

例句：Her rededication to her studies resulted in significantly improved grades. 她重新致力于学业，成绩有了显著提高。


近义词：recommitment, recommitment, renewal, reaffirmation |反义词：abandonment, neglect, disregard |词性：名词
