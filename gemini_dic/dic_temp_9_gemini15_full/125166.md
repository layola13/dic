multibillions:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪljənz/| n.|数十亿；几百亿|The term "multibillions" refers to a very large number, typically in the range of several billion or many billions. It emphasizes the scale and magnitude of a quantity, usually concerning money, but it can also apply to other countable things.|例句：The company's annual revenue is in the multibillions. 这家公司的年收入达数十亿美元。

例句：The project cost multibillions of dollars.  这个项目耗资数十亿美元。


近义词：billions, vast sums, enormous amounts

反义词：millions (relatively smaller), thousands (significantly smaller)

词性：名词
