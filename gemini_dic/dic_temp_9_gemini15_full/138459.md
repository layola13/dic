novel's:/ˈnɑːvəlz/ | n. | 小说的；这部小说的 |  The possessive form of "novel," indicating belonging or relationship to a novel.  It's used to show that something belongs to or is associated with a specific novel. | Example: The novel's plot was intricate and captivating. 这部小说的情节错综复杂，引人入胜。  |近义词: the novel's, of the novel |反义词: N/A | 名词


USAGE->

This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It only shows possession.  Therefore, tense forms are not applicable.  The usage is always in a possessive context.


Example 1:  The novel's ending surprised everyone.  这部小说的结尾让每个人都大吃一惊。

Example 2: I enjoyed the novel's detailed descriptions of the characters. 我喜欢这部小说对人物的细致描写。


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases specifically using "novel's" as a base.  The usage is always within a sentence describing a novel's attribute.


Example 1: The novel's main theme is love.  这部小说的主要主题是爱情。

Example 2:  I particularly liked the novel's use of symbolism. 我尤其喜欢这部小说中象征主义的运用。
