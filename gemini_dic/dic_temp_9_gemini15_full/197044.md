shalm:/ʃɑːlm/| n.|沙龙；沙伦（人名）|A shalm is a type of social gathering, often held in a private home or club, where people can meet and socialize.  It can also be a personal name.|例句：We had a delightful shalm at Mary's house last night. 昨晚我们在玛丽家举行了一个愉快的沙龙。|近义词：gathering, soiree, party |反义词：None.
