dissector:/dɪˈsɛktər/| n.|解剖者；分析器；分解器| A dissector is a person or thing that dissects something.  This can refer to a person who performs dissections (like a surgeon or biologist), or a tool or program that breaks down data or information into smaller parts for analysis. |例句：The surgeon was a skilled dissector.  外科医生是一位熟练的解剖者。

The network dissector analyzed the packet stream. 网络分析器分析了数据包流。|近义词：analyzer, parser, debugger|反义词：integrator, synthesizer
