cast-aside:/kæst əˈsaɪd/| v. |抛弃；丢弃；摒弃 |To throw or put aside; to discard or reject.  It implies a deliberate act of getting rid of something that is no longer wanted or needed.  |例句：He cast aside his old coat and put on a new one. 他扔掉旧外套，穿上了一件新的。

例句：She cast aside her doubts and embraced the opportunity. 她抛开疑虑，抓住机会。


近义词：discard, abandon, reject, throw away, dispense with

反义词：keep, cherish, retain, embrace, preserve

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cast aside their differences to work together. = 他们抛开分歧一起工作。
He casts aside his worries every evening. = 他每天晚上都抛开烦恼。


一般过去时:
She cast aside her old toys after getting new ones. = 她拿到新玩具后就把旧玩具扔掉了。
We cast aside our prejudices and listened to his story. = 我们抛开偏见，听了他的故事。


现在完成时:
They have cast aside their old habits. = 他们已经改掉了旧习惯。
She has cast aside her fears and stepped into the unknown. = 她抛开恐惧，迈向了未知。


过去完成时:
He had cast aside his ambitions before he retired. = 他退休前已经放弃了自己的雄心壮志。
They had cast aside their initial reservations and accepted the proposal. = 他们已经抛开最初的顾虑，接受了这个提议。


将来时:
We will cast aside our differences and unite against our common enemy. = 我们将抛开分歧，团结起来对抗共同的敌人。
She will cast aside her old life and start anew. = 她将抛弃过去的生活，重新开始。


虚拟语气:
If I were to cast aside my pride, I could apologize. = 如果我能放下自尊，我就能道歉。
I wish I could cast aside these worries. = 我真希望我能摆脱这些烦恼。


被动语态:
The old traditions were cast aside in favor of modernity. = 为了现代化，旧传统被抛弃了。
His ideas were cast aside without proper consideration. = 他的想法未经认真考虑就被抛弃了。


疑问句:
Will he cast aside his old beliefs? = 他会抛弃他的旧信仰吗？
Why did they cast aside such a valuable opportunity? = 他们为什么放弃这么好的机会？


否定句:
He did not cast aside his loyalty to his friends. = 他没有抛弃对朋友的忠诚。
She would not cast aside her principles for any amount of money. = 她不会为了任何金钱而放弃自己的原则。
