scalebeam:/ˈskeɪlbiːm/| n.|秤杆，天平梁|A scalebeam is the horizontal beam of a balance scale used to weigh objects. It is typically a rigid bar with a fulcrum in the center, and pans are suspended from either end.|例句：The scalebeam was perfectly balanced, indicating the weights were equal.|近义词：balance beam, weighing beam|反义词：无
