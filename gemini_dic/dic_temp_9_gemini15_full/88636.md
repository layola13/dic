hmu:/ˈeɪtʃɛmˈjuː/|/v.| |联系我；给我发消息|Hit me up is a slang phrase used primarily in online communication and text messaging. It's a casual way of saying "contact me" or "message me."  The "hit" implies a quick, informal contact.|Example:  Hmu if you want to hang out later.  = 如果你晚上想出去玩，联系我。|近义词：contact me, message me, text me, get in touch|反义词：ignore me, avoid me


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hmus me every day. = 他每天都联系我。
They hmu when they need help. = 他们需要帮助时就联系我。

一般过去时:
She hmued me last night. = 她昨晚联系我了。
He hmued me about the party. = 他联系我关于派对的事。


现在完成时:
I haven't hmued him yet. = 我还没联系他。
They have hmued me several times. = 他们已经联系我几次了。


过去完成时:
I had hmued her before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经联系过她了。
He had hmued them many times before they responded. = 他在他们回应之前已经联系他们很多次了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
