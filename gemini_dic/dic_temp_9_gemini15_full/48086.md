declassifications:/ˌdiːklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |解密；解除保密 |The act of removing the classification of something, making it publicly available.  This often refers to government documents or information previously kept secret for national security or other reasons. |例句：The recent declassifications of government files revealed shocking truths about the war. 最近政府文件的解密揭露了关于战争的令人震惊的事实。

例句：The declassifications were met with both excitement and concern from the public.  解密受到了公众的兴奋和担忧。


近义词：disclosure, revelation, release

反义词：classification, secrecy, concealment

词性：名词
