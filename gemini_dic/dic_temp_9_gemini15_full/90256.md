housebuilder:/ˈhaʊsˌbɪldər/| n. |房屋建筑商，建房者 |A housebuilder is a company or person that constructs houses.  They are involved in all aspects of the building process, from planning and design to construction and sale. |Example:  Many large housebuilders are currently facing challenges due to rising material costs. 许多大型房屋建筑商目前正面临着材料成本上涨的挑战。|近义词：home builder, developer |反义词：home buyer, homeowner
