property:/ˈprɒpəti/ /ˈprɑːpərti/| n. |财产；性质；特性；所有权|A property is something that is owned by someone.  It can refer to physical things like land or buildings, or intangible things like intellectual property.  The word also describes a characteristic or quality of something.|例句：He owns several properties in the city. 他在城里拥有几处房产。|例句：The most important property of this material is its strength.  这种材料最重要的特性是它的强度。|近义词：possession, ownership, characteristic, attribute |反义词：liability
