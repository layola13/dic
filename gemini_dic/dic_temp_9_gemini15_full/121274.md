misfall:/ˈmɪsfɔːl/| v.| 失败；流产；堕胎 | To fail to happen as intended; to end unsuccessfully; (of a pregnancy) to end in a miscarriage. |例句：His plans to climb the mountain misfell due to bad weather.  他的登山计划因恶劣天气而失败。|例句：Sadly, her pregnancy misfell in the early stages.  令人悲伤的是，她的怀孕在早期流产了。|近义词：fail, miscarry (in the context of pregnancy), abort (in the context of pregnancy)|反义词：succeed, thrive
