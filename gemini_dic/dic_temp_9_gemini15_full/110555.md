limewater:/ˈlaɪmˌwɔːtər/ | n. |石灰水|A clear, alkaline solution formed by dissolving calcium hydroxide (slaked lime) in water.  It is used in various applications, including testing for carbon dioxide and treating acidic conditions.| 例句：We used limewater to test for the presence of carbon dioxide in the exhaled breath. 我们用石灰水来检测呼出气体中二氧化碳的存在。
The gardener added limewater to the soil to neutralize its acidity. 园丁往土壤中加入石灰水以中和其酸性。|近义词：calcium hydroxide solution |反义词：acidic solution |词性：名词
