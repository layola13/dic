perplexedness:/pərˈplekstnəs/| n. |困惑；茫然；费解|The state of being perplexed; confusion; bewilderment.  It describes a feeling of uncertainty, confusion, or bewilderment caused by something difficult to understand.|例句：The complexity of the instructions caused a great deal of perplexedness among the students.  复杂的指令让学生们感到非常困惑。

例句：Her perplexedness was evident in her furrowed brow. 她困惑的表情显而易见。


近义词：confusion, bewilderment, bafflement, mystification, uncertainty

反义词：clarity, understanding, comprehension, certainty


词性：名词
