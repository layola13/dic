revend:/rɪˈvɛnd/| vt.|报复；复仇；为…报仇|To inflict revenge or punishment on (someone or something); to avenge.|例句：He revenged his father's death by killing the murderer. 他杀死了凶手来报复他父亲的死。|例句：The army revenged the loss of their comrades.  军队为失去的战友报了仇。|近义词：avenge, retaliate, redress|反义词：forgive, pardon|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revenges his wrongs. = 他报复他的冤屈。
She revenges herself on her enemies. = 她向敌人报仇。


一般过去时:
He revenged the insult. = 他报复了侮辱。
They revenged the defeat. = 他们报复了失败。


现在完成时:
He has revenged himself on his betrayer. = 他已经向背叛他的人报仇了。
She has revenged the death of her brother. = 她已经为她哥哥的死报仇了。


过去完成时:
He had revenged himself before he died. = 他死前已经报仇了。
She had revenged the injustice done to her family. = 她已经为对她家庭的冤屈报仇了。


将来时:
He will revenge the wrongs done to him. = 他将报复加诸于他的不公。
She will revenge her father's murder. = 她将为她父亲的谋杀报仇。


虚拟语气:
If he were stronger, he would revenge himself. = 如果他更强壮，他会报仇。
I wish I could revenge my friend's death. = 我希望我能为我朋友的死报仇。


被动语态:
His death was revenged by his son. = 他的死被他儿子报了仇。
The insult was revenged swiftly and decisively. = 侮辱得到了迅速而果断的报复。


疑问句:
Will he revenge the injustice? = 他会报复不公吗？
How will she revenge herself? = 她将如何报复自己？


否定句:
He did not revenge the insult. = 他没有报复侮辱。
She will not revenge her father's death. = 她不会为她父亲的死报仇。


PHRASE->
revenge oneself on = 向…报仇
