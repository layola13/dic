descriptions:/dɪˈskrɪpʃənz/ | n. |描述，说明 | Descriptions provide details about something.  Descriptions can be written or spoken. | Example:  The descriptions in the travel brochure were enticing.  旅行手册中的描述很诱人。 The police gave a detailed description of the suspect. 警方对嫌疑人做了详细的描述。 |近义词：accounts, narrations, portrayals |反义词：omissions, concealments |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The brochure includes descriptions of the local wildlife. = 这本手册包含当地野生动物的描述。
His descriptions of the accident were unclear. = 他对事故的描述不清楚。

一般过去时:
She gave a vivid description of her childhood. = 她对童年做了生动的描述。
The witness's descriptions were inconsistent. = 目击者的描述前后矛盾。

现在完成时:
Have you finished writing the descriptions for the product catalogue? = 你写完产品目录的描述了吗？
She has given several descriptions of the event, but none are completely accurate. = 她已经对这件事做了好几次描述，但没有一个是完全准确的。

过去完成时:
By the time the police arrived, the witness had already given several descriptions of the thief. = 等警察到达时，目击者已经对小偷做了好几次描述。
The book had already included descriptions of various mythical creatures. = 这本书已经包含了各种神话生物的描述。

将来时:
The architect will provide descriptions of the building's design. = 建筑师将提供建筑物设计的描述。
We will send descriptions of our products to potential customers. = 我们会把我们产品的描述发送给潜在客户。

虚拟语气:
If I had better descriptions, I could sell more products. = 如果我有更好的描述，我就能卖出更多产品。
I wish my descriptions were more concise. = 我希望我的描述更简洁。

被动语态:
The paintings were described in great detail in the art book. = 艺术书中对这些画作做了非常详细的描述。
The suspect's appearance was described by multiple witnesses. = 多名目击者描述了嫌疑人的外貌。

疑问句:
What descriptions are included in the report? = 报告中包含哪些描述？
Could you provide more detailed descriptions of the process? = 你能提供更详细的流程描述吗？

否定句:
The article didn't include descriptions of the historical context. = 这篇文章没有包含历史背景的描述。
He gave no description of what he had seen. = 他没有描述他所看到的东西。
