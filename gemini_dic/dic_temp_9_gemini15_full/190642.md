sauce:/sɔːs/| n.|酱汁，调味汁|A sauce is a liquid, cream, or semi-solid preparation that is used to add flavor, moisture, or richness to a dish.  It can be made from a variety of ingredients, including fruits, vegetables, herbs, spices, and liquids.|例句：This pasta dish needs a creamy tomato sauce. 这道意面需要奶油番茄酱。|例句：The chef created a unique sauce using exotic fruits.  这位厨师用异域水果调制了一种独特的酱汁。|近义词：dressing, gravy, relish, condiment|反义词：dryness
