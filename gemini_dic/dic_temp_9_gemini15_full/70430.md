figment:/ˈfɪɡmənt/| n. |虚构的事物；幻想；臆造 |A figment is something that is invented or imagined and is not real.  It refers to something that exists only in the mind and has no basis in reality. |例句：His fears were just figments of his imagination. 他的恐惧只是他想象出来的东西。

例句：That story is a pure figment; it never happened.  那个故事纯属虚构；它从未发生过。


近义词：invention, fabrication, fantasy, illusion, delusion

反义词：reality, fact, truth


词性：名词
