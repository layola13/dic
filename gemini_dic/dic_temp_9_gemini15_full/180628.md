pregnancy: /ˈprɛɡnənsi/ | n. | 怀孕，妊娠 | The state of being pregnant; the period from conception to birth.  | Example:  Her pregnancy was announced to the family.  她的怀孕消息告诉了家人。 |近义词：gestation, gravidity | 反义词：non-pregnancy
