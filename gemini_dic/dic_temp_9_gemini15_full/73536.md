foresightless:/ˈfɔːrsaɪtləs/| adj.|缺乏远见的；目光短浅的；没有先见之明的|Lacking foresight; not considering the future consequences of actions.|His foresightless decision to invest all his money in a risky venture cost him dearly.  他缺乏远见的决定，把所有钱都投资在一个高风险的项目上，让他付出了惨痛的代价。|The company's foresightless management led to its eventual bankruptcy. 公司缺乏远见的管理最终导致了其破产。|farsighted, prescient|shortsighted, imprudent, myopic|形容词
