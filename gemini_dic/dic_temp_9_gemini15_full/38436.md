concerting:/kənˈsɜːrtɪŋ/| adj.|令人担忧的；令人不安的；协调的|causing worry or anxiety;  harmonious or in agreement|The concerning news about the rising inflation rates caused widespread anxiety.  令人担忧的通货膨胀率上涨的消息引起了广泛的焦虑。|近义词：worrying, disturbing, unsettling, alarming, harmonious, coordinated|反义词：reassuring, comforting, calming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is concerning. = 情况令人担忧。
This new policy is concerning many people. = 这项新政策让许多人感到担忧。

一般过去时:
The doctor's report was concerning. = 医生的报告令人担忧。
His behavior yesterday was concerning to everyone. = 他昨天的行为让每个人都感到担忧。

现在完成时:
Recent developments have been concerning. = 最近的事态发展令人担忧。
Everything she has said has been concerning. = 她所说的一切都令人担忧。

过去完成时:
I had been concerning myself with this issue for weeks.  = 我几周来一直为此问题担忧。
The company's financial situation had become increasingly concerning. = 公司的财务状况变得越来越令人担忧。

将来时:
The economic forecast is concerning.  = 经济预测令人担忧。
The potential consequences will be concerning to us all. =  潜在的后果将让我们所有人担忧。

虚拟语气:
If the situation were less concerning, I would relax. = 如果情况不那么令人担忧，我会放松一下。
I wish this news weren't so concerning. = 我希望这个消息不会这么令人担忧。

被动语态:
The problem is being concerningly addressed by the government. = 政府正在令人担忧地处理这个问题。(This example demonstrates a slightly awkward but grammatically correct usage.)
The matter was concerningly overlooked. = 这件事令人担忧地被忽视了。(This example demonstrates a slightly awkward but grammatically correct usage.)


疑问句:
Is the situation concerning you? = 情况让你担忧吗？
Are the recent statistics concerning? = 最近的统计数据令人担忧吗？

否定句:
The news wasn't concerning. = 这个消息并不令人担忧。
The results were not concerning at all. = 结果一点也不令人担忧。
