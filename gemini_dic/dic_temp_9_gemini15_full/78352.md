gestaltism:/dʒɛˈʃtɑːltɪzəm/| n. |格式塔心理学；格式塔主义|The theory and practice of Gestalt psychology, emphasizing holistic perception and the organization of experience into meaningful wholes.  Gestaltism posits that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.  It studies how we perceive and organize sensory information into meaningful patterns.  |Example:  His art demonstrates a clear understanding of gestaltism, with elements carefully arranged to create a unified and impactful whole.  他的艺术作品清晰地展现了对格式塔心理学的理解，其构成元素经过精心安排，创造出一个统一而有冲击力的整体。|近义词：holism, configurationism|反义词：elementalism, reductionism
