interoperably:/ˌɪntərˈɒpərəbli/ | adv. | 可互操作地 |  In a way that allows different systems or components to work together.  It emphasizes the ability of different systems to exchange information and use each other's resources seamlessly. | Example:  The two databases were designed to work interoperably, allowing for easy data sharing.  两个数据库的设计目的是为了实现互操作性，从而方便数据共享。| 近义词:  seamlessly, compatibly | 反义词: incompatibly, separately
