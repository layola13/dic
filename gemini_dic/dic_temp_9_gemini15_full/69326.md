fecks:/feks/| int.|  哎呀;该死;真是的 | An exclamation of frustration, annoyance, or surprise, often used as a mild swear word.  It's a variation of "f**k," but significantly less offensive. | Example: "Fecks! I dropped my phone!"  哎呀！我的手机掉了！| 近义词: darn, shoot, gosh,  | 反义词: N/A |  感叹词


USAGE->
This word is primarily used as an interjection and doesn't have grammatical tenses like verbs do.  Therefore, traditional tense classifications don't apply.


PHRASE->
Fecks sake!:  哎呀，我的天！/ 真是的！
For fecks sake!: 为了老天爷的份上！/看在老天的份上！
What the fecks?: 搞什么鬼？/怎么回事？

注意事项:
The word "fecks" is informal and should be avoided in formal writing or situations.  Its usage is largely confined to casual conversation.
