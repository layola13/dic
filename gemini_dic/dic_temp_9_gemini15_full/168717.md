proctoring:/ˈprɑːktərɪŋ/ | n. |监考；监督；代理 |The act of supervising or overseeing an exam or other activity to ensure fairness and prevent cheating.  |例句：The proctoring of the exam was rigorous to prevent cheating.  监考非常严格以防止作弊。|例句：Online proctoring systems use AI to detect cheating during remote exams. 在线监考系统利用人工智能来检测远程考试中的作弊行为。|近义词：supervising, monitoring, overseeing |反义词：unsupervised, unattended |名词
