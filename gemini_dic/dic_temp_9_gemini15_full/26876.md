cake:/keɪk/ | n. |蛋糕，饼|A sweet baked food made from flour, sugar, eggs, and other ingredients.  Cakes can be decorated in various ways and come in many flavors. |Example:  She baked a delicious chocolate cake for my birthday. 她为我的生日烤了一个美味的巧克力蛋糕。

|Example: The bakery sells a wide variety of cakes. 这家面包店出售各种各样的蛋糕。|近义词：pastry, dessert |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He bakes a cake every Sunday. = 他每周日都烤蛋糕。
The cake tastes delicious. = 这个蛋糕味道很好。


一般过去时:
She baked a birthday cake yesterday. = 她昨天烤了一个生日蛋糕。
