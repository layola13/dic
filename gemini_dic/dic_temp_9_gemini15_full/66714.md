exoplanet:/ˈɛksoʊˌplænɪt/| n. |系外行星|A planet that orbits a star other than the Sun.|例句：Scientists have discovered many exoplanets in recent years. 近年来，科学家们发现了许多系外行星。  The Kepler space telescope has been instrumental in finding thousands of exoplanets. 开普勒太空望远镜在发现数千颗系外行星方面发挥了重要作用。|近义词：extrasolar planet|反义词:planet (in our solar system)
