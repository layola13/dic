hit the sack:/hɪt ðə sæk/| phrasal verb|睡觉，上床睡觉|To "hit the sack" is an informal idiom meaning to go to bed.  It implies a desire for sleep and often suggests tiredness.|I'm exhausted; I think I'll hit the sack.|近义词：go to bed, turn in, retire|反义词：wake up, get up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hits the sack early every night. = 他每天晚上很早就睡了。
She hits the sack after watching TV. = 她看完电视后就睡觉了。

一般过去时:
I hit the sack as soon as I got home. = 我一到家就睡了。
They hit the sack late last night. = 他们昨晚睡得很晚。

现在完成时:
I have hit the sack early every night this week. = 这周我每天晚上都睡得很早。
She has hit the sack before midnight every night. = 她每天晚上都在午夜之前睡觉。

过去完成时:
He had hit the sack before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前他已经睡了。
They had hit the sack before the phone rang. = 电话响之前他们已经睡了。

将来时:
I will hit the sack as soon as I finish this work. = 我完成这项工作后就去睡觉。
We will hit the sack after the movie. = 电影结束后我们就要睡觉了。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would hit the sack right now. = 如果我是你，我现在就睡觉。
I wish I could hit the sack, but I have too much work to do. = 我真希望我能睡觉，但是我还有很多工作要做。

被动语态: (This phrase is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
When do you usually hit the sack? = 你通常几点睡觉？
Are you going to hit the sack soon? = 你很快就要睡觉了吗？

否定句:
I didn't hit the sack until after 3 a.m. = 我直到凌晨三点以后才睡。
She didn't hit the sack because she was too excited. = 她因为太兴奋而没有睡觉。
