overmourned: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnd/ | adj. |过度哀悼的，哀悼过度的 | Excessively grieved or mourned.  It implies a grief that is excessive or unhealthy, often prolonged beyond what is considered normal or appropriate. |  She overmourned her lost pet,  failing to move on with her life. 她过度哀悼她失去的宠物，无法继续生活。The family felt they had overmourned the death and needed to find a way to heal.  家人觉得他们对死者的哀悼过度，需要找到一种疗伤的方法。|近义词：excessively grieved,  deeply mourned, unduly sorrowful | 反义词：undermourned, unconcerned, indifferent
