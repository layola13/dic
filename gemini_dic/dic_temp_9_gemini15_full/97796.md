injurer:/ɪnˈdʒʊərər/ | n. |伤害者，致伤者 |A person who causes injury to another.  |Example: The injurer was found guilty of reckless driving.  伤害者被判犯有鲁莽驾驶罪。|近义词：offender, wrongdoer, perpetrator |反义词：victim, helper |名词
