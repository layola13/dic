self-paid:/ˌsɛlfˈpeɪd/| adj.|自费的，自己支付的|Self-paid refers to something that is paid for by oneself, without external funding or assistance.  It emphasizes personal financial responsibility. |例句：He undertook self-paid travel to Europe. 他自费去了欧洲旅行。  |例句：The program offers self-paid options for those who don't qualify for financial aid. 该项目为不符合经济援助条件的人提供自费选择。|近义词：self-funded, privately funded |反义词：subsidized, sponsored, funded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The course is self-paid. = 这门课程是自费的。
He self-pays for his medical bills. = 他自己支付医疗费用。


一般过去时:
She self-paid for her education. = 她自费完成了学业。
They self-paid for their wedding. = 他们自费举办了婚礼。


现在完成时:
I have self-paid for all my trips this year. = 今年我所有的旅行都是自费的。
She has self-paid for her children's tuition. = 她已经自费支付了孩子的学费。


过去完成时:
He had self-paid for his previous trips before receiving a grant. = 在获得资助之前，他已经自费支付了他之前的旅行费用。
They had self-paid for the renovation before the bank loan was approved. = 在银行贷款获得批准之前，他们已经自费完成了装修。


将来时:
I will self-pay for my master's degree. = 我将自费攻读硕士学位。
She will self-pay for her daughter's college education. = 她将自费支付女儿的大学教育费用。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would self-pay for a new car. = 如果我有更多钱，我会自费买一辆新车。
I wish I could self-pay for my dream vacation. = 我希望我能自费去我的梦想度假。


被动语态:
The trip was self-paid. = 这趟旅行是自费的。（很少用被动语态）
The tuition fees cannot be self-paid by all students. =  并非所有学生都能自费支付学费。（强调能力）


疑问句:
Is the conference self-paid? = 会议是自费的吗？
Are the participants self-paying for their travel expenses? = 参加者是自费支付他们的差旅费吗？


否定句:
The project is not self-paid; it's funded by a grant. = 这个项目不是自费的；它是由赠款资助的。
His medical treatment was not self-paid; his insurance covered it. = 他的医疗费用不是自费的；他的保险支付了费用。
