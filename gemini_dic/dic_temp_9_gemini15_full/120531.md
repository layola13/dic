miosis:/maɪˈoʊsɪs/| n. |瞳孔缩小|The constriction of the pupil of the eye.  This is a normal physiological response to bright light or near vision, and it can also be caused by certain medications or neurological conditions. |Example:  The ophthalmologist noted the patient's miosis after administering the eye drops. 眼科医生注意到病人点眼药水后瞳孔缩小了。|近义词：pupillary constriction|反义词：mydriasis
