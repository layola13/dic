pimento:/pɪˈmɛntoʊ/| n. |红甜椒，皮曼多辣椒|A pimento is a mild-flavored red pepper, typically used in cooking.  It is also known as a sweet pepper. Pimentos are often used to stuff olives, in salads, and as a garnish.| Example: The chef added sliced pimentos to the pasta dish for a touch of sweetness and color.  厨师在意大利面中加入了切片的皮曼多辣椒，以增添一丝甜味和色彩。|近义词：sweet pepper, bell pepper|反义词：hot pepper, chili pepper
