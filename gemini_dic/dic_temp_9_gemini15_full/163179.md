precompensated: /ˌpriːˈkɒmpənseɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先补偿的，预先赔偿的 |  Already compensated in advance; having received prior payment or adjustment for a potential loss or expense.  |  The precompensated damages covered the anticipated losses from the storm.  预先赔偿的损失涵盖了风暴预期的损失。 The project was precompensated for potential delays. 项目已经预先补偿了潜在的延误。 |  prepaid, advanced | undercompensated | adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company precompensates its employees for travel expenses. = 公司预先支付员工差旅费。
This system precompensates for potential signal loss. = 这个系统预先补偿潜在的信号丢失。

一般过去时:
The contractor was precompensated for the extra work. = 承包商因额外工作获得了预先补偿。
The insurance company precompensated the homeowner for the damaged property. = 保险公司预先赔偿了房主受损的财产。


现在完成时:
The client has precompensated the consultant for the project. = 客户已预付顾问项目费用。
They have precompensated for the risk of inflation. = 他们已经预先补偿了通货膨胀的风险。

过去完成时:

We had precompensated for any potential supply chain disruptions. = 我们已经预先补偿了任何潜在的供应链中断。

虚拟语气:
