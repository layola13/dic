expiry:/ɪkˈspaɪəri/ | n. | 期满，截止日期 | The expiry date of the milk is tomorrow.  牛奶的有效期是明天。|  The expiry of the visa means he has to leave the country. 签证过期意味着他必须离开这个国家。|  expiration, termination, end | renewal, extension | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The warranty expires next month. = 保修期下个月到期。
My passport expires in two years. = 我的护照两年后过期。


一般过去时:
The offer expired last week. = 上周优惠活动结束了。
The contract expired on December 31st. = 合同于12月31日到期。


现在完成时:
My membership has expired. = 我的会员资格已经过期了。
The deadline has already expired. = 截止日期已经过了。


过去完成时:
The insurance had expired before the accident. = 事故发生前保险已经过期了。
The license had expired a month ago. = 执照一个月前就过期了。


将来时:
The contract will expire next year. = 合同将于明年到期。
Your visa will expire soon. = 你的签证很快就要过期了。


虚拟语气:
If the contract had not expired, we could have renewed it. = 如果合同没有过期，我们可以续签。
I wish my driver's license hadn't expired. = 我真希望我的驾照没有过期。


被动语态:
The product was declared expired. = 该产品被宣布过期。
The agreement will be deemed expired. = 该协议将被视为过期。


疑问句:
When does this medication expire? = 这种药什么时候过期？
Has the warranty on my car expired? = 我的汽车保修期过期了吗？


否定句:
The product is not expired yet. = 该产品尚未过期。
My subscription hasn't expired. = 我的订阅还没有过期。



PHRASE->
expiry date:  有效期
date of expiry: 到期日
before expiry: 在过期之前
after expiry: 过期后
near expiry: 快要过期
