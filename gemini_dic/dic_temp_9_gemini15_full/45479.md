curbsides:/ˈkɜːrbˌsaɪdz/| n.|路边；路旁|The noun "curbsides" refers to the sides of a curb, typically the area immediately adjacent to the curb of a road or street.  It often implies the area where pedestrians walk or where vehicles briefly stop.|例句：Many vendors set up their stalls on the curbsides during the festival.  节日期间，许多小贩在路边摆摊。

The homeless often sleep on the curbsides at night.  无家可归者经常在夜里睡在路边。|近义词：roadside, streetside, kerbside (British English)|反义词：midstreet, center(of the road)|名词
