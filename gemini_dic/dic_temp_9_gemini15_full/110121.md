lid:/lɪd/| n.|盖子，眼睑|A lid is a cover for a container, or the upper eyelid.  It can also refer to something that acts as a cover or a restriction.|例句：The lid of the jar was stuck.  罐子的盖子卡住了。

例句：He closed his eyelids. 他闭上了眼睛。

|近义词：cover, top, cap|反义词：bottom, base|名词
