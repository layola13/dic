fly-past:/ˈflaɪ pɑːst/| n. |飞越；飞行表演|A fly-past is a ceremonial flight of aircraft, usually in formation, often performed as a tribute or display.  It involves aircraft flying in a predetermined path past a reviewing stand or point of observation.|例句：The Red Arrows put on a spectacular fly-past for the royal wedding. 红箭飞行表演队为皇室婚礼进行了一场壮观的飞越表演。

例句：A fly-past of fighter jets marked the end of the airshow.  战斗机的飞越表演标志着航展的结束。|近义词：air display, aerial demonstration|反义词：N/A
