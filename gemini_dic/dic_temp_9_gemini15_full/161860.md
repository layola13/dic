praecocial: /prɪˈkoʊʃəl/ | adj. |早熟的；早成性的 |  Refers to animals that are relatively mature and independent at birth or hatching, requiring little parental care.  These animals are often born with open eyes, fur or down, and the ability to move around and feed themselves relatively soon after birth. |  Example:  Many bird species, such as chickens and ducks, are praecocial.  许多鸟类，如鸡和鸭，都是早熟的。  |近义词：precocious, advanced, mature |反义词：altricial, helpless, dependent |
