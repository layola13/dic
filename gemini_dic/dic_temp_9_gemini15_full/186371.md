rhythm:/ˈrɪðəm/| n.|节奏，韵律|A strong, regular, repeated pattern of sounds or movements.|The rhythm of the music was infectious.  这音乐的节奏极具感染力。|The rhythmic beat of the drum kept us dancing. 鼓的规律节奏让我们不停地跳舞。|近义词：beat, cadence, tempo, meter|反义词：disorder, chaos


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music has a strong rhythm. = 这音乐节奏强烈。
She feels the rhythm of the waves. = 她感受着海浪的节奏。

一般过去时:
The dancers followed the rhythm perfectly. = 舞蹈演员完美地跟随节奏。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
