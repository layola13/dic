merman:/ˈmɜːrmæn/ | n. | 美人鱼（男）| A mythical creature, typically depicted as a man from the waist up and a fish from the waist down.  Mermen are often associated with the sea and possess magical abilities in folklore. | Example:  The legend speaks of a merman who guarded the sunken treasure.  传说中有一位美人鱼守护着沉没的宝藏。  |近义词：sea-man (less common, implies a man who is a sailor)|反义词：mermaid
