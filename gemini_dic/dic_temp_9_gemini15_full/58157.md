droughts:/draʊts/| n. |干旱；缺乏|A drought is a prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.  Droughts can have devastating effects on agriculture, ecosystems, and human populations.|例句：The severe drought caused widespread crop failure. 严重的干旱导致了大范围的农作物歉收。|例句：Years of drought have turned the land into a desert. 多年的干旱使这片土地变成了沙漠。|近义词：aridness, dryness|反义词：deluge, downpour|名词
