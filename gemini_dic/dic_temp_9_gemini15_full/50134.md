denudation:/ˌdiːnjuˈdeɪʃən/| n. |剥蚀；裸露；(土地的)侵蚀|The process of stripping away the surface layer of something, especially the removal of soil or rock by natural processes such as erosion or weathering.|The denudation of the hillsides was caused by heavy rains.  山坡的裸露是由大雨造成的。|The continued denudation of the rainforest is a serious environmental concern. 热带雨林持续的剥蚀是一个严重的环保问题。|erosion, weathering, stripping|afforestation, reforestation, accretion
