recreancy:/ˈrɛkrənsi/| n.|背叛；失职；不忠|The act of failing in one's duty or allegiance; disloyalty; treachery.  It often implies a desertion or abandonment of a cause, principle, or commitment. |例句：His recreancy in the face of danger cost his comrades dearly.  他的临阵脱逃让他的战友们付出了惨痛的代价。

例句：The general's recreancy led to the army's defeat. 将军的临阵脱逃导致了军队的失败。

近义词：treachery, betrayal, disloyalty, defection, desertion

反义词：loyalty, faithfulness, fidelity, allegiance, devotion

词性:名词
