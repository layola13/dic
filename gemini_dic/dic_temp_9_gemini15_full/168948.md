profile's: /ˈproʊfaɪlz/ | n. | 个人简介，个人资料 |  A brief description of someone or something.  It often includes key information and characteristics.  The possessive form indicates that the profile belongs to someone or something. | 例句：  The company's profile's been updated on their website. 公司的简介已更新在他们的网站上。


The candidate's profile's quite impressive. 这位候选人的个人资料非常令人印象深刻。|近义词：bio, résumé, summary, description | 反义词：None (it's not a word that readily has antonyms) |  名词
