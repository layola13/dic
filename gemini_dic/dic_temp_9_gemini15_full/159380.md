poking:/ˈpoʊkɪŋ/|/ˈpəʊkɪŋ/| v. |戳，捅，拨弄 |To push or prod with a pointed object; to stir or disturb gently.  |例句：He was poking the fire with a stick. 他用棍子拨弄着火。

例句：She poked him in the ribs to get his attention. 她戳了戳他的肋骨以引起他的注意。


近义词：prodding, jabbing, nudging |反义词：pulling, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pokes the bear with a stick. = 他用棍子戳熊。
She pokes her food with her fork. = 她用叉子拨弄她的食物。

一般过去时:
He poked the fire to make it burn brighter. = 他戳火堆让它烧得更旺。
She poked her friend playfully. = 她顽皮地戳了戳她的朋友。

现在完成时:
I have poked the cake with a toothpick to check if it's done. = 我用牙签戳了蛋糕检查是否烤好了。
He has been poking around in my things again. = 他又在翻我的东西了。

过去完成时:
He had poked the sleeping dog, and it woke up and barked. = 他戳了睡着的狗，它醒了并叫了起来。
She had poked at the food several times before finally eating it. = 在最终吃掉食物前，她已经多次拨弄它了。

将来时:
I will poke the cake to see if it is baked through. = 我将戳蛋糕看看是否烤透了。
He will poke the fire later to keep it burning. = 他稍后会拨弄火堆以保持燃烧。


虚拟语气:
If I poked the bear, it would probably attack me. = 如果我戳熊，它可能会攻击我。
I wish I hadn't poked him; now he's angry. = 我真希望我没有戳他；现在他生气了。

被动语态:
The fire was poked by the old man. = 火堆被老人拨弄了。
The sleeping dog was poked, and it woke up. = 睡着的狗被戳了，它醒了。

疑问句:
Are you poking fun at me? = 你在取笑我吗？
Was he poking the bear just now? = 他刚才在戳熊吗？

否定句:
Don't poke the sleeping bear! = 不要戳睡着的熊！
I didn't poke him; he tripped and fell. = 我没有戳他；他是自己绊倒摔倒的。
