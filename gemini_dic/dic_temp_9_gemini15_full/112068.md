longhorn:/ˈlɒŋhɔːrn/| /ˈlɔːŋhɔːrn/| n. |长角牛；长角的 |A longhorn is a breed of cattle known for their very long horns.  The term can also refer to anything with exceptionally long horns. |例句：Texas longhorns are famous for their impressive horns.  德州长角牛以其令人印象深刻的角而闻名。

The ranch raised a herd of magnificent longhorns.  这个牧场饲养着一群壮观的角牛。


近义词：long-horned cattle (更正式) |反义词：shorthorn (短角牛)


词性：名词


词源：源于long (长的) + horn (角)，描述了这种牛最显著的特征——长角。  The term's origin is straightforwardly descriptive of the animal's characteristically long horns.
