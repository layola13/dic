pathetically:/pəˈθɛtɪkli/| adj.|可怜地，悲惨地，可悲地|In a pathetic manner; pitifully; miserably.  It describes something that evokes pity or sadness because it is so inadequate, ineffective, or unfortunate. |例句：He pathetically tried to explain, but no one believed him. 他可怜地试图解释，但没有人相信他。
例句：The team played pathetically, losing by a huge margin. 这支球队打得非常糟糕，输得很惨。|近义词：miserably, pitifully, sadly, deplorably|反义词：gloriously, triumphantly, wonderfully|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pathetically begs for money. = 他可怜兮兮地乞讨钱财。
The old dog pathetically whimpers. = 老狗可怜地呜咽着。


一般过去时:
She pathetically recounted her misfortunes. = 她可怜巴巴地叙述了她的不幸遭遇。
The lost child pathetically cried for his mother. = 迷路的孩子可怜地哭着喊妈妈。


现在完成时:
He has pathetically failed to meet his goals. = 他可怜地未能实现他的目标。
She has pathetically tried to hide her grief. = 她可怜地试图掩饰她的悲伤。


过去完成时:
They had pathetically underestimated the challenge. = 他们可怜地低估了挑战的难度。
He had pathetically hoped for a better outcome. = 他曾经可怜地希望有更好的结果。


将来时:
He will pathetically plead for mercy. = 他将会可怜地恳求宽恕。
She will pathetically watch her dreams crumble. = 她将会可怜地看着她的梦想破灭。


虚拟语气:
If he had tried harder, he wouldn't have performed so pathetically. = 如果他更努力一些，他就不会表现得如此糟糕。
I wish he hadn't behaved so pathetically. = 我真希望他不要表现得如此可怜。


被动语态:
His attempts were pathetically dismissed. = 他的尝试被可怜地驳回了。
His efforts were pathetically disregarded. = 他的努力被可怜地忽视了。


疑问句:
Wasn't his performance pathetically weak? = 他的表现是不是可怜地弱？
Did he act pathetically to gain sympathy?  = 他是否可怜兮兮地表现以博取同情？


否定句:
His performance wasn't pathetically bad; it was simply average. = 他的表现并非糟糕透顶；只是中等水平。
She didn't act pathetically; she was simply exhausted. = 她并不是可怜兮兮的；只是精疲力尽了。


PHRASE->
pathetically weak = 可怜地虚弱
pathetically inadequate = 可怜地不足
pathetically small = 可怜地小
pathetically slow = 可怜地慢
pathetically obvious = 可怜地明显


词源:
源自 pathetic (adj.)  + -ally (adv. suffix)
pathetic 来自于希腊语 pathos “suffering, feeling, experience,”  最终来源于古希腊语的 πάθος (pathos)。
