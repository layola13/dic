danite:/ˈdeɪnaɪt/| n. |达尼人；达尼石|A member of a biblical tribe descended from Dan, one of the twelve sons of Jacob. Also, a mineral consisting of a hydrated silicate of zinc. |例句：The Danites were known for their military prowess. 达尼人以其军事实力而闻名。 The geologist identified the sample as danite. 地质学家将样品鉴定为达尼石。 |近义词：Israelite (for the tribal context), zinc silicate (for the mineral context)|反义词：无|名词
