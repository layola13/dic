mis-administration:/ˌmɪsædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/| n. | 误管理；行政失误； maladministration |The mismanagement or improper handling of affairs, especially in government or a business. It suggests a lack of competence, efficiency, or ethical conduct.  |例句：The mis-administration of the funds led to a significant budget deficit.  资金的误管理导致了严重的预算赤字。

The company's mis-administration resulted in numerous lawsuits.  公司的管理不善导致了无数的诉讼。


近义词：maladministration, mismanagement, bad governance, incompetence |反义词：good governance, efficient administration, competent management |词性:noun
