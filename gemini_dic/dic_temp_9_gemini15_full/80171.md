godfather:/ˈɡɑːdfɑːðər/ | n. |教父；教父（电影名）|A godfather is a man who is the sponsor at a baptism.  In the context of organized crime, it refers to a powerful and influential leader. The term also refers to the famous 1972 film. |例句：He was the godfather of his niece. 他是他侄女的教父。

例句：The Godfather is a classic crime film. 《教父》是一部经典的犯罪电影。


近义词：sponsor, patron, protector (in some contexts) | 反义词：godmother (female equivalent) | 词性: 名词
