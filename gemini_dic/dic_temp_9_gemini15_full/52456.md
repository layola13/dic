diencephalon:/ˌdaɪ.ənˈsɛf.əl.ɒn/| n. |间脑|The diencephalon is a part of the brain located between the cerebrum and the brainstem. It includes structures such as the thalamus, hypothalamus, and epithalamus, which play crucial roles in various bodily functions, including sensory processing, hormone regulation, and autonomic control.  | The diencephalon is a critical relay station for sensory information. |近义词:interbrain|反义词:无
