pituitary:/pɪˈtjuːɪtəri/ | n. | 垂体；脑垂体 | The pituitary gland is a small, pea-sized gland located at the base of the brain. It plays a crucial role in regulating various bodily functions through the production and secretion of hormones.  | Example:  The pituitary gland controls growth and many other important bodily functions. 垂体控制生长和其他许多重要的身体机能。
