In a broader sense, it can refer to any comb-like instrument used for similar purposes. | Example: The spider used its calamistrum to carefully comb the silk threads into a neat web.  蜘蛛用它的梳状器仔细地梳理丝线成一个整齐的网。|近义词：comb, brush (in a broader sense)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be a tool that tangles or messes fibers. |名词
