reexposure: /ˌriːɪkˈspoʊʒər/ | n. | 再暴露，再次接触 | The act or instance of being exposed again to something, especially something harmful or unpleasant.  It often refers to a repeated exposure to a stimulus, substance, or situation after an initial exposure. |

例句：

The reexposure to the allergen triggered a severe allergic reaction. =  再次接触过敏原引发了严重的过敏反应。

His reexposure to the traumatic event led to a relapse of his PTSD. = 他再次经历创伤事件导致他的创伤后应激障碍复发。


近义词：repeated exposure, renewed exposure, second exposure

反义词：protection, shielding, avoidance


词性：名词
