marketing:/ˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/| n. |市场营销，市场推广|The process of planning and executing the conception, pricing, promotion, and distribution of ideas, goods, and services to create exchanges that satisfy individual and organizational objectives.  市场营销是计划和执行构思、定价、推广和分销理念、商品和服务以创造满足个人和组织目标的交换的过程。|Our marketing campaign was a huge success.  我们的营销活动非常成功。|advertising, promotion, sales |anti-marketing (though this is more of a concept than a direct antonym)|名词
