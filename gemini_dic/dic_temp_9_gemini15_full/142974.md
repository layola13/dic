outbake:/ˌaʊtˈbeɪk/| v.|胜过…的烘焙技术|To surpass someone in baking skills or the quality of baked goods.|例句：He outbaked all the other contestants in the baking competition. 他在烘焙比赛中胜过了所有其他选手。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass|反义词：underbake


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outbakes everyone in the family. = 他烘焙技术胜过家里的每一个人。
She outbakes her competitors with her innovative recipes. = 她凭借创新的食谱胜过她的竞争对手。

一般过去时:
He outbaked his sister in last year's pie contest. = 去年派对比赛中，他烘焙技术胜过他姐姐。
The experienced baker outbaked the newcomer with ease. = 经验丰富的烘焙师轻松地胜过了新手。

现在完成时:
She has outbaked all her previous attempts at making sourdough bread. = 她制作酸面包的技术已经超越了她之前的每一次尝试。
He has outbaked every other contestant in the show so far. = 到目前为止，他在节目中烘焙技术已经胜过其他所有选手。

过去完成时:
By the time the competition ended, she had outbaked every other participant. = 当比赛结束时，她已经胜过其他所有参赛者。
He had outbaked even the most experienced judges in the previous round. = 在上一轮比赛中，他甚至胜过了最有经验的评委。

将来时:
She will outbake her rival in the upcoming baking championship. = 她将在即将到来的烘焙锦标赛中胜过她的对手。
He believes he will outbake the reigning champion. = 他相信他会胜过卫冕冠军。

虚拟语气:
If he practiced more, he would outbake everyone. = 如果他多练习，他就能胜过所有人。
I wish I could outbake her exquisite cakes. = 我希望我能胜过她精美的蛋糕。

被动语态:
No one can be outbaked by her. = 没有人能在烘焙技术上胜过她。(被动语态较少用在此处)
He was outbaked by a surprising underdog. = 他被一个令人惊讶的黑马击败了。(被动语态较少用在此处)

疑问句:
Can he outbake the renowned pastry chef? = 他能胜过著名的糕点厨师吗？
Will she outbake the judges with her innovative techniques? = 她能用创新的技术胜过评委们吗？

否定句:
He couldn't outbake her sophisticated techniques. = 他无法超越她复杂的技巧。
She didn't outbake the champion, but she came close. = 她没有胜过冠军，但她很接近。
