estrange:/ɪˈstreɪndʒ/| vt.|使疏远；使格格不入|To make (someone) feel isolated or alienated.|His behavior estranged him from his family. 他的行为使他和家人疏远了。|近义词：alienate, isolate, separate|反义词：unite, reconcile, connect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He estranges himself from his friends. = 他疏远了他的朋友们。
The new policy estranges many citizens from the government. = 新政策使许多公民与政府疏远。

一般过去时:
The argument estranged the two brothers. = 那场争吵使两兄弟疏远了。
Her actions estranged her from her former colleagues. = 她的行为使她与以前的同事疏远了。

现在完成时:
Years of conflict have estranged the two families. = 多年的冲突使这两个家庭疏远了。
His constant criticism has estranged his wife. = 他不断的批评使他妻子疏远了他。

过去完成时:
They had estranged themselves before the final fallout. = 在最终决裂之前，他们就已经疏远了。
The couple had estranged for years before the divorce. = 这对夫妇在离婚前已经疏远多年了。

将来时:
This decision will estrange many voters. = 这个决定将会使许多选民疏远。
Continued silence will estrange the two friends. = 持续的沉默将会使这两个朋友疏远。

虚拟语气:
If he continued this behavior, he would estrange his family. = 如果他继续这种行为，他会使他的家人疏远他。
I wish I hadn't done that; it estranged my best friend. = 我真希望我没有那样做；那疏远了我最好的朋友。


被动语态:
He was estranged from his family after the incident. = 事件发生后，他和家人疏远了。
She felt estranged from the community after moving. = 她搬家后感到与社区格格不入。


疑问句:
Has his behavior estranged his family? = 他的行为是否使他的家人疏远了他？
What actions have estranged them? = 什么行为使他们疏远了？

否定句:
His actions did not estrange his friends. = 他的行为并没有使他的朋友们疏远。
The family tried not to be estranged after the dispute. = 家庭在争吵后试图避免疏远。



PHRASE->

feel estranged = 感到疏远
estranged couple = 疏远的夫妇
