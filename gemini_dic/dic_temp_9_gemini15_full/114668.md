malicious: /məˈlɪʃəs/ | adj. | 恶意的；怀有恶意的；蓄意的 |

英文解析：Intended to do harm; deliberately harmful.  Showing malice; malevolent.


例句：

He spread malicious rumors about his colleague.  他散布了关于他同事的恶意谣言。

The malicious software damaged the computer's operating system.  恶意软件损坏了计算机的操作系统。


近义词：malevolent, spiteful, spiteful, vindictive, wicked, evil, malign, ill-intentioned


反义词：benevolent, kind, friendly, well-intentioned, harmless


词性：形容词
