pre-Doric:/ˌpriːˈdɔːrɪk/| adj.| 前多立克的；多立克式之前的 | Relating to the period or style of architecture that preceded the Doric order in ancient Greece.  This style is characterized by features that are simpler and less developed than the later Doric order, often showing influences from earlier architectural traditions.  | Example:  The pre-Doric structures found on the island reveal a fascinating evolution of building techniques.  前多立克时期的建筑遗迹表明岛上的建筑技术有着迷人的发展历程。| 近义词：proto-Doric, pre-classical| 反义词：Doric, classical
