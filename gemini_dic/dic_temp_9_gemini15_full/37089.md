combinatorially:/ˌkɒmbɪnəˈtɔːriəli/| adv. |组合地；组合上地|In a way that relates to or uses combinatorics (the branch of mathematics dealing with combinations and permutations).  It describes processes or analyses that consider all possible combinations of elements within a set.|The problem was solved combinatorially by examining all possible arrangements of the components.  这个问题通过检查所有可能的组件排列组合来解决。|  systematically, exhaustively|randomly, haphazardly|副词
