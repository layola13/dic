daylighting:/ˈdeɪˌlaɪtɪŋ/| n. |采光；日光照明|The use of natural light to illuminate the interior of a building. Daylighting reduces the need for artificial lighting, saving energy and improving occupant well-being. |例句：The architect incorporated clever daylighting strategies into the design. 建筑师在设计中融入了巧妙的采光策略。

例句：The building's large windows maximize daylighting. 这座建筑的大窗户最大限度地利用了日光照明。


近义词：natural lighting, sun lighting

反义词：artificial lighting
