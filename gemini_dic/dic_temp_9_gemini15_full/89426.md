Their apartment, though small, felt incredibly homy thanks to the carefully chosen decorations. 他们的公寓虽然小，但由于精心挑选的装饰，感觉非常温馨舒适。|近义词：cozy, comfortable, snug, inviting, welcoming |反义词：cold, stark, uninviting, austere, uncomfortable
