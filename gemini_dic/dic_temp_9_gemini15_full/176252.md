re-coined:/ˌriːˈkɔɪnd/| vt.|重新铸造；重新创造(词语)；重新使用(词语)|To coin a word or phrase again, often with a slightly altered meaning or in a new context.  It can also refer to the act of reusing an existing word or phrase to give it new currency or relevance.|例句：The term "blog" was re-coined in the late 1990s to describe online diaries.|近义词：recast, reformulate, redefine|反义词：coin (for the first time)
