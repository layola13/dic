mucilage:/ˈmjuːsɪlɪdʒ/| n. |粘液；胶质；粘胶|A sticky, viscous substance, typically of plant origin.  It's often used as an adhesive or a binding agent.|例句：The plant produces a mucilage that helps it retain moisture.  这种植物产生一种有助于它保持水分的粘液。

例句：The stamp stuck to the envelope thanks to the mucilage on its back.  邮票由于背面的粘液而粘在信封上。


近义词：glue, gum, slime, adhesive|反义词：dryness|词性:名词


Etymology:  From Late Latin *mucilago, from mucus "mucus, slime".
