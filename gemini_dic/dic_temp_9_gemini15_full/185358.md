retrocession:/ˌretrəˈseʃən/ | n. |领土归还；倒退；逆行 | The return of territory or rights to a previous possessor.  It can also refer to a return to a previous state or condition, often implying a decline or worsening. | Example: The retrocession of the territory to its original owners was a landmark event. 领土归还给原主人是一件具有里程碑意义的事件。The retrocession of democratic values was a worrying trend. 民主价值观的倒退是一个令人担忧的趋势。 | 近义词：cession, reversion, surrender, relapse | 反义词：acquisition, advance, progression
