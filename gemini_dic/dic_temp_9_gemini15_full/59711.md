eclectic:/ɛˈklɛktɪk/ | adj. | 折衷的；兼容并蓄的；选择而成的 |  Drawing from diverse sources or styles.  An eclectic approach combines different methods or ideas. |


例句：

Her taste in music is eclectic, ranging from classical to hip-hop.  她的音乐品味很杂，从古典音乐到嘻哈音乐都有。

The museum boasts an eclectic collection of artifacts from around the world. 这个博物馆拥有一个包罗万象的文物收藏，来自世界各地。


近义词：mixed, diverse, varied, heterogeneous

反义词：uniform, homogeneous, consistent


词源：

The word "eclectic" comes from the Greek word "eklektikos," meaning "selective,"  "choosing," or "picking and choosing."  It ultimately derives from "eklegein," meaning "to select" or "to pick out."


用法说明:


一般现在时:
His style is eclectic. = 他的风格是折衷的。
The restaurant offers an eclectic menu. = 这家餐厅提供一份兼容并蓄的菜单。


一般过去时:
Her last project was eclectic in its approach. = 她上一个项目的做法是折衷的。
The artist's early work was less eclectic than his later pieces. = 艺术家早期的作品不如他后来的作品那么折衷。


现在完成时:
They have developed an eclectic collection of stamps. = 他们已经收集到了一套包罗万象的邮票。
She has always maintained an eclectic wardrobe. = 她一直保持着一种不拘一格的衣着风格。


过去完成时:
He had already established an eclectic reputation before his death. = 他死前已经树立了不拘一格的名声。
The company had pursued an eclectic marketing strategy. = 公司之前采取了一种折衷的市场营销策略。


将来时:
She will create an eclectic garden design. = 她将设计一个风格多样的花园。
The exhibition will showcase eclectic works of art. = 展览将展示各种风格的艺术作品。


虚拟语气:
If he were more eclectic, he would be more successful. = 如果他更兼容并蓄一些，他会更成功。
I wish my taste in art were more eclectic. = 我希望我的艺术品味更广泛一些。


被动语态:
An eclectic mix of styles was chosen for the building's design. = 建筑设计选择了多种风格的混合。
An eclectic approach is being adopted by the government. = 政府正在采取折衷的方法。


疑问句:

Is the museum's collection eclectic? = 这个博物馆的藏品是兼容并蓄的吗？


否定句:
His taste in music is not eclectic; he only listens to classical music. = 他对音乐的品味并不广泛；他只听古典音乐。
