cross-promote:/krɒs prəˈmoʊt/ | vt. |交叉推广，互推|To advertise or promote two or more products or services together, often in a way that benefits all of them.  This strategy leverages the existing audience of each product to reach new customers.|例句：The company cross-promoted its new energy drink with its existing line of sports apparel. 这家公司将其新的能量饮料与其现有的运动服装系列交叉推广。


例句：We will cross-promote our new movie with a popular TV show. 我们将把我们的新电影与一个受欢迎的电视节目交叉推广。


近义词：co-promote, synergistically promote | 反义词：isolate, segregate | 词性:Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cross-promote their products on social media. = 他们在社交媒体上交叉推广他们的产品。
The two companies cross-promote each other's services. = 这两家公司互相推广彼此的服务。


一般过去时:
The marketing team cross-promoted the new album with the tour. = 市场营销团队将新专辑与巡演交叉推广。
Last year, they cross-promoted their products through a joint campaign. = 去年，他们通过联合活动交叉推广了他们的产品。


现在完成时:
They have cross-promoted their products successfully for years. = 多年来，他们成功地交叉推广了他们的产品。
The brand has cross-promoted its products with several influencers. = 该品牌已与几位网红交叉推广其产品。


过去完成时:
By the time the campaign ended, they had cross-promoted their brands extensively. = 到活动结束时，他们已经广泛地交叉推广了他们的品牌。
Before the launch, they had cross-promoted the product through various channels. = 在发布之前，他们已经通过各种渠道交叉推广了该产品。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
