liftable: /ˈlɪftəbəl/ | adj. | 可举起的，可抬起的 | Able to be lifted.  Something that is liftable is light enough or manageable enough to be lifted. | 例句：The box was surprisingly liftable despite its size.  这个箱子虽然很大，但出乎意料地轻便易举。

The small child found the toy liftable.  这个小孩发现玩具是可以举起的。


近义词：movable, portable, manageable | 反义词：unliftable, immovable, heavy
