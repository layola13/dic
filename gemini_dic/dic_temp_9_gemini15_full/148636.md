overthoughtful: /ˌoʊvərˈθɔːtfʊl/ | adj. | 过度考虑的，想得过多的 |

英文解析：  Excessively thoughtful; given to excessive worrying or consideration of possibilities.  Suggests a level of thoughtfulness that is detrimental, leading to anxiety or inaction.

例句：

He became overthoughtful about his presentation,  losing sleep and ultimately delivering a subpar performance. = 他对自己的演示过度考虑，失眠了，最终表现不佳。

Her overthoughtful nature prevented her from taking risks and pursuing her dreams. = 她过度考虑的性格使她无法冒险追求梦想。


近义词：  overwrought, anxious, apprehensive, preoccupied, worried, solicitous


反义词：  carefree, unconcerned, nonchalant, relaxed


词性：形容词
