pre-record:/ˌpriːˈrɛkɔːrd/| v.|预先录制|To record something beforehand, typically audio or video, for later use or broadcast.|例句：We pre-recorded the interview so we could edit it later. 我们预先录制了采访，以便之后可以编辑。|例句：The band pre-recorded their performance for the television special. 乐队为电视特别节目预先录制了他们的演出。|近义词：record in advance, prerecord|反义词：live record, record on the spot


USAGE->

We pre-record the announcements for the next day. = 我们预先录制第二天的通知。
