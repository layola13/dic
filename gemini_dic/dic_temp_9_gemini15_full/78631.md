gibibit:/ˈdʒɪbɪbɪt/| n. |吉比比特（信息单位）|A gibibit (GiB) is a unit of information or computer storage.  It is equal to 2<sup>30</sup> bits, or 1,073,741,824 bits.  The term "gibibit" is used to distinguish it from the gigabyte, which is based on the decimal system (10<sup>9</sup> bytes).|The hard drive has a capacity of 10 gibibits.|近义词：None (strictly defined)|反义词：None (no direct opposite)|名词
