pseudostome:/ˈsuːdəʊstoʊm/| n. |假口, 假口器|A pseudostome is a false or rudimentary mouth, especially in certain larval stages of some invertebrates.  It's a structure that resembles a mouth but doesn't function as one in the same way a true mouth does.  It may play a role in attachment or feeding in a limited capacity.  |Example: The larva of this species develops a pseudostome that facilitates attachment to the host plant.  这个物种的幼虫发育出一个假口，有助于附着在寄主植物上。|近义词：rudimentary mouth, false mouth |反义词：true mouth
