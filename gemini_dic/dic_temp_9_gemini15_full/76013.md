furculum: /ˈfɜːrkjʊləm/ | n. |叉骨；锁骨| The furculum, also known as the wishbone, is a forked bone found in birds formed by the fusion of the two clavicles (collarbones). It acts as a spring, storing energy during flight and assisting in wing beats.  | Example: The child excitedly pulled apart the wishbone (furculum) hoping for good luck. | 近义词：wishbone | 反义词：无
