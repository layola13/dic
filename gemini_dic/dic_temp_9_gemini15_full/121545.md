mismated:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不相配的；不协调的；婚姻不幸福的 | Ill-suited; not well matched.  Describes a couple whose personalities or interests clash, or a situation where things are poorly combined. |例句：Their personalities are mismated; they constantly argue. 他们的性格不相配；他们总是吵架。

例句：The mismated furniture looked out of place in the elegant room.  不相配的家具在优雅的房间里显得格格不入。


近义词：incompatible, ill-matched, ill-suited, discordant


反义词：compatible, well-matched, harmonious, congruous


词性：形容词
