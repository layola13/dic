parochian:/pəˈroʊkiən/| n. |教区居民；教友|A member of a parish, especially a regular attendee at a particular church.  |例句：The priest greeted each parishioner warmly as they entered the church. 牧师热情地向每位教区居民问好，当他们进入教堂时。|例句：Many parishioners volunteered their time to help with the church renovations. 许多教区居民自愿贡献时间来帮助教堂的翻修。|近义词：churchgoer, congregant |反义词：non-parishioner
