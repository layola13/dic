precontesting:/ˌpriːkənˈtɛstɪŋ/| n.|预先测试；预先检验|The act of testing something before it is officially launched or used, often to identify and fix potential problems.  This involves running trials or simulations to evaluate performance, identify flaws, and ensure readiness.|例句：The company conducted extensive precontesting of the software before its release to the public. = 公司在向公众发布软件之前进行了广泛的预先测试。

例句：Precontesting the bridge design revealed a critical weakness in its structural integrity. = 预先测试桥梁设计揭示了其结构完整性中的一个关键弱点。


近义词：pretesting, pilot testing, trial run, dry run|反义词：post-testing, final testing|词性：名词
