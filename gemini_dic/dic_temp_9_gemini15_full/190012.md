sandblasts:/ˈsændˌblæsts/| vt.|喷砂；用砂子喷射|To clean or etch a surface by forcing sand against it with compressed air.  This technique is commonly used to clean or prepare surfaces for painting or other treatments, or to create textured effects.|例句：They sandblasted the brick wall to remove the graffiti.  他们用喷砂法清除砖墙上的涂鸦。

例句：The artist sandblasted the glass to create a frosted effect. 艺术家用喷砂法在玻璃上制作磨砂效果。


近义词：abrasion blasting, grit blasting|反义词：polishing, smoothing|词性：动词
