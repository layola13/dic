proletarian:/proʊlɪˈtɛəriən/ | n. adj. |无产阶级成员；无产阶级的 | A member of the working class, especially one who is manual or unskilled.  The term is often used in Marxist contexts to describe those who own little to no property and must sell their labor to survive. |例句：Marxist theory focuses on the struggle between the bourgeoisie and the proletariat.  马克思主义理论关注资产阶级和无产阶级之间的斗争。

The proletariat are often exploited by the capitalist system. 无产阶级经常被资本主义制度剥削。


近义词：worker, laborer, working class | 反义词：bourgeoisie, capitalist, elite


词性:名词，形容词
