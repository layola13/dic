finial:/ˈfɪniəl/| n.|尖顶; 顶饰; 冠饰|An ornamental ending, usually curved or pointed, placed on top of a structure or object, such as a gable, spire, or piece of furniture.|The church spire was topped with a decorative finial. 教堂尖顶顶部装饰着一个精美的尖顶。 The bedpost had a beautiful carved finial. 床柱上有一个美丽的雕刻顶饰。|近义词：crest, topknot, acroterion|反义词：base, bottom
