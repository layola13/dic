flyaway:/ˈflaɪəweɪ/| adj.|飞走的；飘逸的；难以捉摸的|Easily blown or moved by the wind; tending to fly off or away.  Describes something light and airy, often hair or clothing.|例句：Her flyaway hair was a constant source of frustration.  她的飘逸的头发总是让她很苦恼。
例句：The flyaway strands of her hair danced in the breeze. 她飘逸的发丝在微风中飞舞。|近义词：unruly, stray, wispy|反义词：tamed, controlled, anchored|词性：形容词
