noninterchangeable: /ˌnɑːnɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒəbəl/ | adj. | 不可互换的；不能互换的 |  Not capable of being exchanged or substituted for one another.  These components are noninterchangeable; you must use the exact part specified in the instructions. |近义词：indistinguishable, inseparable, irreplaceable | 反义词：interchangeable, substitutable


例句：

The screws in this engine are noninterchangeable; using the wrong one could cause serious damage.  这个引擎里的螺丝是不可互换的；使用错误的螺丝可能会造成严重的损坏。

These two chemicals are noninterchangeable and must be kept strictly separate. 这两种化学物质是不可互换的，必须严格分开存放。


词源：

* **non-:**  prefix meaning "not"
* **inter-:** prefix meaning "between" or "among"
* **changeable:**  adjective meaning "capable of being changed"
