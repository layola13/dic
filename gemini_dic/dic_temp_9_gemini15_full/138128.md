north-westwards:/ˌnɔːθˈwɛstwədz/| adv. |向西北方向；往西北 |Adverb indicating direction towards the northwest.  It combines the directions "north" and "west" and adds "-wards" to indicate movement. |Example: The ship sailed north-westwards towards the open sea.  船向西北驶向公海。|近义词：northwesterly, to the northwest |反义词：south-eastwards, to the southeast
