overimports:/ˈoʊvərɪmpɔːrts/| n.|过度进口；进口过剩|The phenomenon of importing more goods than necessary, often leading to trade imbalances and economic consequences.|例句：The country's overimports of consumer goods contributed to its widening trade deficit.  这个国家的消费品过度进口导致其贸易逆差扩大。|例句：The recent spike in overimports has put pressure on the domestic industries. 最近进口过剩的激增给国内产业带来了压力。|近义词：excess imports, surplus imports|反义词：underimports, import restrictions|名词
