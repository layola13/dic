glassed:/ɡlæst/| adj.|玻璃状的；装有玻璃的|covered or fitted with glass; resembling glass in appearance or texture|The glassed-in porch offered a beautiful view.  玻璃封闭的阳台提供了美丽的景色。 The dessert had a beautifully glassed surface. 甜点表面像玻璃一样光滑漂亮。|近义词：glazed, glassy|反义词：unglazed, unpolished|
