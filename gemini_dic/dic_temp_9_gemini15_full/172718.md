The ducks are quacking loudly. = 鸭子大声嘎嘎叫。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

If he hadn't been quacking so much, people might have believed him. = 如果他不那么夸夸其谈，人们可能会相信他。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action performed by the subject.)  这个动词很少用于被动语态，因为它描述的是主语执行的动作。
