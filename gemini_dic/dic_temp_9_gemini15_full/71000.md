firework:/ˈfaɪərwɜːrk/| n.|烟火，爆竹|An explosive device that produces a visual display of light and color when ignited.  Fireworks are often used for celebrations and entertainment.|例句：The fireworks display was spectacular.  烟火表演非常壮观。|例句：We watched the fireworks light up the night sky. 我们看着烟花照亮夜空。|近义词：pyrotechnics|反义词：None
