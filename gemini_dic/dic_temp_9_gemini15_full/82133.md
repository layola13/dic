gromet:/ˈɡrɑːmɪt/ | n. |金属环；金属眼；衬圈 | A grommet is a small metal or plastic ring used to reinforce a hole and protect a rope, cable, or other material from chafing.  It is often used in fabrics, such as sails or awnings, to prevent tearing. | Example:  The sailmaker used grommets to strengthen the holes for the ropes.  帆匠用金属环加固绳索的孔洞。|近义词：eyelet, washer |反义词：无
