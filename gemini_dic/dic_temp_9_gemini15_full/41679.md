correspondent:/ˌkɒrəˈspɒndənt/ /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/| n. |通讯员；记者；对应物 | A correspondent is a person who regularly sends reports to a newspaper or magazine, or a television or radio station.  A correspondent can also refer to something that matches or is similar to something else. | 例句：She is a foreign correspondent for the New York Times. 她是一位《纽约时报》的外国记者。  The two maps are correspondents; they show the same area.  这两张地图是对应的；它们显示的是同一个区域。 | 近义词：reporter, journalist, writer, equivalent | 反义词：N/A | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The correspondent files his report every day. = 通讯员每天提交他的报道。
Our correspondent in London sends regular updates. = 我们在伦敦的记者定期发送更新。


一般过去时:
The correspondent interviewed the president yesterday. = 通讯员昨天采访了总统。
The newspaper correspondent reported on the earthquake. = 报纸记者报道了地震。


现在完成时:
The correspondent has filed several articles this week. = 通讯员本周已经提交了几篇文章。
She has been a foreign correspondent for many years. = 她做了多年的外国记者。


过去完成时:
The correspondent had already submitted his report before the deadline. = 通讯员在截止日期之前已经提交了他的报告。
The correspondent had interviewed several witnesses before writing the article. = 通讯员在撰写文章之前已经采访了几位证人。


将来时:
The correspondent will travel to the war zone next week. = 通讯员下周将前往战区。
The correspondent will file a report on the upcoming election. = 通讯员将提交一份关于即将到来的选举的报告。


虚拟语气:
If I were a correspondent, I would travel the world. = 如果我是一名记者，我会周游世界。
I wish I were a correspondent covering the Olympics. = 我希望我是一名报道奥运会的记者。


被动语态:
The report was filed by the correspondent on time. = 报告由通讯员按时提交。
The interview was conducted by the correspondent professionally. = 采访是由通讯员专业地进行的。


疑问句:
Is the correspondent available for an interview? = 通讯员有空接受采访吗？
Who is the correspondent for this article? = 这篇文章的通讯员是谁？


否定句:
The correspondent did not submit his report on time. = 通讯员没有按时提交他的报告。
She is not a correspondent for that newspaper. = 她不是那家报纸的记者。
