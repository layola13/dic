halloysite:/ˈhælɔɪsaɪt/| n. |埃洛石|A clay mineral consisting of hydrated aluminosilicate.  It is a tubular or layered structure, and is often found in altered volcanic rocks and hydrothermal deposits.  It is used in various industrial applications, including in ceramics, paper coatings, and as a filler in plastics.|例句：Halloysite nanotubes are being investigated for their potential in drug delivery. 埃洛石纳米管正在被研究其在药物递送方面的潜力。|近义词：kaolinite (similar clay mineral)|反义词：无|名词
