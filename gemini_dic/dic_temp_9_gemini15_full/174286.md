questions:/ˈkwɛstʃənz/| n.|问题，疑问|Questions are essential for learning.|问题对于学习至关重要。|近义词：queries, inquiries, issues|反义词：answers, solutions|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your questions? = 你有什么问题？
What questions do you have about the project? = 你对这个项目有什么问题？


一般过去时:
What were his questions? = 他有什么问题？
What questions did she ask yesterday? = 她昨天问了什么问题？


现在完成时:
What questions have you asked? = 你问了什么问题？
What questions have they raised so far? = 到目前为止他们提出了什么问题？


过去完成时:
What questions had you asked before the meeting? = 会议前你问了什么问题？
What questions had he considered before answering? = 他在回答之前考虑过什么问题？


将来时:
What questions will you ask tomorrow? = 你明天会问什么问题？
What questions will they address in the next meeting? = 他们将在下一次会议上讨论什么问题？


虚拟语气:
If I knew the answers, I wouldn't have so many questions. = 如果我知道答案，我就不会有这么多问题了。
I wish I had fewer questions. = 我希望我少一些问题。


被动语态:
What questions were asked at the meeting? = 会议上问了什么问题？
What questions are being considered by the committee? = 委员会正在考虑什么问题？


疑问句:
What questions do you have? = 你有什么问题？
What questions concern you most? = 什么问题最让你担忧？


否定句:
I have no questions. = 我没有问题。
She didn't raise any questions. = 她没有提出任何问题。


PHRASE->
a matter of questions = 一个有疑问的问题
a series of questions = 一连串的问题
raise questions = 提出问题
answer questions = 回答问题
open questions = 未解决的问题
