replete:/rɪˈpliːt/| adj.|充满的；装满的；丰富的|Full or abundantly supplied with something.  The book is replete with fascinating details. 这本书充满了引人入胜的细节。|近义词：full, abundant, brimming, crammed|反义词：empty, devoid, lacking
