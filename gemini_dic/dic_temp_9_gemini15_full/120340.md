minimalize: /ˈmɪnɪməlaɪz/ | vt. | 最小化；使减到最少 | To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree.  | 例句：We need to minimalize the risk of infection. 我们需要将感染风险降到最低。  | 例句：The company is trying to minimalize its environmental impact. 这家公司正在努力将对环境的影响降到最低。 | 近义词：reduce, lessen, minimize, curtail | 反义词：maximize, amplify, increase | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They minimalize their expenses. = 他们尽量减少开支。
We minimalize the use of plastic. = 我们尽量减少塑料的使用。

一般过去时:
He minimalized the damage. = 他将损失降到了最低。
She minimalized her role in the project. = 她尽量淡化自己在项目中的角色。

现在完成时:
They have minimalized their carbon footprint. = 他们已经将碳足迹降到了最低。
We have minimalized the disruption to services. = 我们已经将对服务的干扰降到了最低。

过去完成时:
They had minimalized the risk before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经将风险降到了最低。
She had minimalized her involvement in the scandal. = 她已经尽量淡化自己与丑闻的牵连。

将来时:
We will minimalize the impact of the changes. = 我们将把这些变化的影响降到最低。
They will minimalize their losses in the coming quarter. = 他们将在下一个季度尽量减少损失。

虚拟语气:
If we minimalized our waste, we would help the environment. = 如果我们减少浪费，就能保护环境。
I wish they had minimalized the noise during the meeting. = 我真希望他们在会议期间能把噪音降到最低。

被动语态:
The risks were minimalized by the careful planning. = 通过周密的计划，风险被降到了最低。
The impact on the environment should be minimalized. = 对环境的影响应该降到最低。

疑问句:
How can we minimalize the cost? = 我们怎样才能把成本降到最低？
What steps can be taken to minimalize the damage? = 可以采取什么措施来将损失降到最低？

否定句:
They did not minimalize the importance of the issue. = 他们没有低估这个问题的重要性。
We cannot minimalize the challenges we face. = 我们不能低估我们面临的挑战。


PHRASE->
minimalize the impact = 将影响降到最低
minimalize risk = 最小化风险
minimalize cost = 降低成本
minimalize effort = 减少努力
minimalize damage = 减少损失

词源:
minimalize 来自于 minimal (最小的) + -ize (动词后缀，表示“使…成为”)。 minimal  源于拉丁语 minimus (最小的)。
