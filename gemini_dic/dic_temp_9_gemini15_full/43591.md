creamer:/ˈkriːmər/ | n. | 奶油；奶精；咖啡伴侣 | A substance used to add creaminess to coffee or tea, often made from dairy products or plant-based alternatives.  | Example: I prefer my coffee with creamer. 我喜欢加奶精的咖啡。 |近义词：milk, coffee whitener | 反义词：black coffee (无奶精的咖啡)

词源:
The word "creamer" comes from the word "cream," referring to the creamy texture and appearance of the substance.  The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or instrument, thus indicating something that produces or adds creaminess.
