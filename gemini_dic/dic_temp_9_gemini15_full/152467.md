pay-per-view:/ˌpeɪ pər ˈvjuː/| n. |付费观看，按次付费观看|A system in which television programs or events are broadcast for a fee paid by the viewer.|例句：Many boxing matches are only available via pay-per-view. 许多拳击比赛只能通过付费观看的方式收看。|近义词：subscription television, premium television|反义词：free-to-air television
