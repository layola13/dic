reinfested:/ˌriːɪnˈfestɪd/ | v. | 再受侵扰；再次感染 | To infest again; to become infested again.  |  例句：The house was reinfested with rats after the initial extermination.  房子在第一次灭鼠后又被老鼠侵扰了。


例句：The farmer found his crops reinfested with locusts after the initial spraying. 农民发现他的庄稼在第一次喷洒后又被蝗虫侵扰了。


近义词：repopulate (with pests), reinvade (with pests)


反义词：eradicate, exterminate, eliminate


词性:动词
