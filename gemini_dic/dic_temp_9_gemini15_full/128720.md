niftier:/ˈnɪftiər/| adj. |更漂亮的；更灵巧的；更时髦的 |More stylish, clever, or effective.  It is the comparative form of "nifty."|例句：His new apartment is niftier than his old one.  他的新公寓比他的旧公寓更漂亮。  His solution to the problem was niftier than mine. 他解决问题的办法比我的更巧妙。|近义词：neater, slicker, more stylish, more efficient |反义词：clumsier, less stylish, less efficient
