mil:/mɪl/ | n. |毫升，千分之一升| A milliliter (mL) is a metric unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a liter.  It's commonly used to measure small amounts of liquids. |Example: The recipe calls for 250 mil of milk. 这个食谱需要250毫升牛奶。|近义词：milliliter|反义词：None
