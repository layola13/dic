scope:/skoʊp/ | n. |范围，视野，机会 |The scope of the project is vast.  这个项目的范围很大。|近义词：range, extent, reach |反义词：limitation, constraint


The scope of this project includes market research, product development and sales.  这个项目的范围包括市场调研、产品开发和销售。

The telescope expanded our scope of vision.  望远镜扩展了我们的视野。


词源:
Middle English: from Old French scope ‘survey’, from Old Norse skop ‘aim, target’.


用法说明：

名词：

1. 范围，界限：指某事物的范围、大小或程度。
The scope of the investigation is limited to the past three years.  调查的范围仅限于过去三年。
The scope of her knowledge is impressive. 她的知识面令人印象深刻。


2. 视野，眼界：指一个人能看到或理解的范围。
His scope of experience was limited. 他的经验范围有限。
The vast scope of the desert amazed us.  广阔的沙漠景色让我们惊叹。


3. 机会，余地：指做某事的机会或可能性。
There is little scope for improvement.  改进的余地很小。
This job offers scope for creativity. 这份工作为创造力提供了空间。


例句：

The scope of the problem is far greater than we initially thought.  问题的范围远比我们最初想象的要大得多。

The artist's work explores the scope of human emotions. 艺术家的作品探索了人类情感的广度。

The meeting's scope will include discussions on budget and staffing. 会议的范围将包括关于预算和人员配置的讨论。


近义词：

range, extent, reach, ambit, compass


反义词：

limitation, constraint, restriction
