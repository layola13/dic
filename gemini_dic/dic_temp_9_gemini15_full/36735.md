collop:/ˈkɒlɒp/ | n. |肉片，小块肉 |A small slice or piece of meat, especially of meat for frying or stewing.  |例句：She fried the collops until golden brown. 她把肉片煎至金黄色。|例句：The stew contained tender collops of beef. 这道炖菜包含嫩牛肉片。|近义词：cutlet, slice, rasher |反义词：whole, uncut
