reminiscer: /ˌremɪˈnɪsər/ | n. |回忆者，缅怀往事的人 | A person who reminisces; one who recalls past experiences.  |例句：He's a great reminiscer, always ready with a story from his youth. 他是一位很棒的回忆者，总是乐于讲述他年轻时的故事。|近义词：recaller, rememberer, recollecter |反义词：forgetter (无直接反义词，反义概念为遗忘者)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reminisces about his childhood often. = 他经常回忆起他的童年。
She reminisces with her friends about old times. = 她和朋友们一起回忆往昔。

一般过去时:
He reminisced about his travels in Europe. = 他回忆起他在欧洲的旅行。
They reminisced about their college days. = 他们回忆起他们的大学时光。

现在完成时:
She has reminisced about that trip many times. = 她已经多次回忆起那次旅行。
We have reminisced about our shared experiences. = 我们已经回忆起我们共同的经历。

过去完成时:
He had reminisced about his grandfather before he passed away. = 他在爷爷去世前回忆起了他的爷爷。
They had reminisced about the party before the new guests arrived. = 新客人到达之前，他们已经回忆起了派对。


将来时:
She will reminisce about her wedding anniversary. = 她将回忆起她的结婚纪念日。
We will reminisce about this trip for years to come. = 未来几年我们将回忆起这次旅行。

虚拟语气:
If I could reminisce with my childhood friends, I would. = 如果我能和我的童年朋友们一起回忆往昔，我会的。
I wish I could reminisce about those happy days again. = 我希望我能再次回忆起那些快乐的日子。

被动语态:
His life story was reminisced by many people. = 许多人回忆起他的生活故事。
The old photos were reminisced over by the family. = 这家人反复回忆着旧照片。


疑问句:
Do you often reminisce about the past? = 你经常回忆过去吗？
Does she reminisce about her school days? = 她回忆起她的学生时代吗？

否定句:
He didn't reminisce about his difficult times. = 他没有回忆起他艰难的时光。
They didn't reminisce at all during the reunion. = 他们在聚会上根本没有回忆往事。


PHRASE->
reminisce about = 回忆…
reminisce with = 和…一起回忆
fondly reminisce = 深情地回忆
happily reminisce = 开心地回忆
bitterly reminisce = 苦涩地回忆
