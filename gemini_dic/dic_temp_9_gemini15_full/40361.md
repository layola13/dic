contractive:/kənˈtræktɪv/| adj.| 缩略的；收缩的；压缩的 |  Relating to or characterized by contraction; tending to contract.  It often describes words, phrases, or processes that are shortened or reduced in form. |例句：The contractive form of "cannot" is "can't".  "不能" 的缩略形式是 "can't"。|  例句：The muscle showed a strong contractive force. 这块肌肉显示出强大的收缩力。|近义词：abbreviated, shortened, condensed, compressed |反义词：expanded, extended, prolonged, full
