pinacone:/ˈpaɪnəkəʊn/| n. | pinacol, 频哪醇 |A type of organic compound containing two hydroxyl (-OH) groups attached to adjacent carbon atoms.  Pinacones are often formed by the pinacol coupling reaction.  They are typically used as intermediates in organic synthesis. |Example: The pinacol rearrangement is a useful reaction in organic chemistry for converting a pinacone to a carbonyl compound.  频哪醇重排是有机化学中将频哪醇转化为羰基化合物的有用反应。|近义词:diol (a more general term)|反义词:None specifically, but anti-pinacones are structurally distinct. |名词
