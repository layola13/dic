medivacked:/ˈmɛdɪvækt/| v.|医疗后送|To evacuate (someone) by medical transport, typically by helicopter or airplane.|例句：The injured soldier was medivacked to a field hospital. 受伤的士兵被医疗后送到了野战医院。 The civilian casualties were quickly medivacked from the disaster zone.  平民伤亡人员迅速被从灾区医疗后送。|近义词：evacuate, airlift, transport|反义词：leave behind
