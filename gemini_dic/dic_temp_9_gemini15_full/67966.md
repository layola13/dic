facilitators:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪtərz/| n. | 促进者；协调者；辅导员 | People or things that make something easier or more likely to happen.  They often act as intermediaries or guides, helping individuals or groups achieve a common goal. | Example: The workshop facilitators guided the participants through a series of exercises.  研讨会的促进者引导参与者进行了一系列练习。

The teachers acted as facilitators, helping students to learn independently.  老师们担任促进者，帮助学生独立学习。|近义词：helpers, assistants, mediators, guides |反义词：obstructors, hinderers,impediments
