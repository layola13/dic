pro-rata:/ˌproʊˈreɪtə/| adj.|按比例的，成比例的|Proportionate; in proportion.  It refers to a calculation that is made in proportion to something else, often used in situations where a payment or amount needs to be divided fairly.|The insurance premium will be pro-rata for the remaining months of the policy. 保险费将按比例计算剩余月份的保费。|proportional, proportionate|inversely proportional


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The rent is calculated pro-rata. = 房租是按比例计算的。
We pay our taxes pro-rata. = 我们按比例缴税。


一般过去时:
The refund was calculated pro-rata. = 退款是按比例计算的。
They paid the fees pro-rata for the service. = 他们按比例支付了服务的费用。


现在完成时:
We have already paid the pro-rata share of the costs. = 我们已经支付了按比例分摊的成本。
They have received their pro-rata share of the profits. = 他们已经收到了他们应得的按比例利润。


过去完成时:
They had already paid their pro-rata share before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经支付了他们应得的按比例份额。
The company had calculated the pro-rata amount before the announcement. = 公司在宣布之前已经计算出了按比例的金额。


将来时:
The final payment will be pro-rata. = 最终付款将按比例计算。
We will receive our pro-rata share of the dividends next month. = 我们下个月将收到我们应得的按比例股息。


虚拟语气:
If the project were cancelled, we would receive a pro-rata refund. = 如果项目取消，我们将获得按比例退款。
If the work were incomplete, payment would be pro-rata. = 如果工作未完成，付款将按比例计算。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
