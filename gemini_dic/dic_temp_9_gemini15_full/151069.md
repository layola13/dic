paraplegia:/ˈpærəpliːdʒiə/| n. |截瘫；下半身瘫痪|Paraplegia is the loss of motor and sensory function in the lower half of the body.|例句：The accident left him with paraplegia.  事故导致他下半身瘫痪。|例句：She has been living with paraplegia for many years and has adapted well. 她多年来一直生活在截瘫的状况下，并且适应得很好。|近义词：paralysis (of the lower body), spinal cord injury|反义词：full mobility, complete function
