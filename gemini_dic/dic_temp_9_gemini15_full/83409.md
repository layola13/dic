haberdash:/ˈhæbərdæʃ/| n. |男装用品；缝纫用品|The word "haberdashery" refers to men's clothing and haberdashery items, such as buttons, thread, and needles.  It is now more commonly used to refer to a shop selling such goods.  The term originates from the Old French word "haberdassier," meaning a seller of small wares.|The haberdashery sold a wide range of buttons and threads.|近义词：mercery, notions|反义词：无
