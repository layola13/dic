diker:/ˈdaɪkər/| n.|筑堤者；挖土机；（南非语）农场主|A diker is a person who builds dikes, or a machine used for digging.  In South African English, it can also refer to a farmer.|例句：The dikers worked tirelessly to reinforce the seawall before the storm.  筑堤工人风雨无阻地加固海堤。

The diker efficiently excavated the trench for the pipeline.  挖土机高效地挖掘了管道沟槽。

近义词：dike builder, excavator, (SA) farmer|反义词：None specifically applicable.
