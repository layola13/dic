refight:/ˌriːˈfaɪt/| vt.|重战，再战|To fight again; to renew a fight or battle.|例句：The two armies refought the battle several times.  两军数次重演这场战役。

例句：The boxer decided to refight the match after the controversial decision. 拳击手决定在有争议的判决后重赛。


近义词：rebattle, renew, recommence


反义词：cease, end, surrender


词性：动词
