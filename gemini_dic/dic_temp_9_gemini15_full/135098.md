nonobservation:/ˌnɒnˌɒbzəˈveɪʃən/| n. |未观察到；未被观察；未观察到的现象|The lack of observation or recording of something;  the failure to observe something.  It can refer to the absence of a phenomenon, or the failure to detect a phenomenon that is present. |例句：The nonobservation of the comet was puzzling to astronomers. 天文学家对未观测到彗星感到困惑。
