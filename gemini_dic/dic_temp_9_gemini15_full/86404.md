hedonistic:/ˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. |享乐主义的，追求感官享乐的 |  Relating to or characterized by hedonism; pleasure-seeking.  It emphasizes the pursuit of pleasure and avoidance of pain as the ultimate goal of life. | The hedonistic lifestyle of the wealthy often contrasts sharply with the struggles of the poor.  享乐主义的富人生活方式往往与穷人的挣扎形成鲜明对比。  |  pleasure-seeking, self-indulgent | ascetic, austere, puritanical
