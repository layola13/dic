nyctalopia:/ˌnɪktəˈloʊpiə/| n. |夜盲症|Night blindness;  reduced visual acuity in low light conditions.  This is a condition where the eyes have difficulty adjusting to darkness, making it hard to see at night or in dimly lit areas.|Example:  He suffers from nyctalopia and struggles to drive at night. 他患有夜盲症，晚上开车很困难。|近义词：night blindness|反义词：photopia (normal vision in bright light)
