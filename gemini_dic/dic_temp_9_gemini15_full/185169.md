retook:/ˌriːˈtʊk/| v.|重新占用；重新夺回；重考|To take something again, especially after having lost or given it up.|例句：The army retook the city after a fierce battle.  军队经过激烈的战斗后重新夺回了这座城市。
例句：She retook the exam and got a much better grade. 她重考了，取得了更好的成绩。|近义词：reclaim, recapture, retake|反义词：lose, surrender, relinquish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retakes the exam every year. 他每年都重考。
They retake the lost territories. 他们收复失地。


一般过去时:
She retook the photographs. 她重拍了照片。
The general retook the fort. 将军收复了要塞。


现在完成时:
They have retaken control of the situation. 他们已经重新掌控了局面。
She has retaken the driving test three times. 她已经三次重考驾照了。


过去完成时:
He had retaken the course before he graduated. 他在毕业前已经重修过这门课了。
They had retaken the city before the enemy reinforcements arrived. 在敌人增援到达之前，他们已经收复了这座城市。


将来时:
We will retake the test next week. 我们下周将重考。
The army will retake the lost province. 军队将收复失落的省份。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have retaken the exam with confidence. 如果我学习更努力，我会更有信心重考。
If he were to retake the role, he would do it differently. 如果他要重新扮演这个角色，他会用不同的方式来演。


被动语态:
The city was retaken by the rebels. 这座城市被叛军收复了。
The exam will be retaken next month. 考试将在下个月重考。


疑问句:
Did you retake the exam? 你重考了吗？
Will they retake the position? 他们会夺回阵地吗？


否定句:
He did not retake the exam. 他没有重考。
They could not retake the lost ground. 他们无法收复失地。
