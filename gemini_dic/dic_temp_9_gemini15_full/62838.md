enigmatology:/ˌɛnɪɡməˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |谜语学，谜题研究 |The study of riddles and puzzles, especially those involving wordplay and cryptic clues.  It encompasses the creation, analysis, and solving of enigmas. |例句：He's an expert in enigmatology, able to decipher even the most complex riddles. 他是谜语学专家，能够解读最复杂的谜语。

例句：Her passion for enigmatology led her to write a book on the history of riddles. 她对谜语学的热情促使她写了一本关于谜语历史的书。


近义词：puzzology, cryptology (if focusing on coded messages) |反义词：None directly applicable, as it's a field of study.

词源:  From "enigma" (a puzzling or inexplicable occurrence or situation) + "-ology" (the study of).  "Enigma" ultimately derives from the Greek word αἴνιγμα (ainigma), meaning "riddle" or "obscure utterance".
