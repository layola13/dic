joist:/dʒɔɪst/| n.|托梁，小梁|A horizontal structural member used to support a ceiling or floor.  Joists are typically made of wood or steel and are spaced evenly apart.|The joists in the attic were old and needed replacing.|近义词：beam, girder|反义词:None
