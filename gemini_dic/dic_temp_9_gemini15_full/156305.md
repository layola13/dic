/ˈfaɪzə/| n. |椎实螺属(一种淡水螺)|Physa is a genus of small, air-breathing freshwater snails, aquatic pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Physidae.  They are characterized by their sinistral (left-handed) shells, meaning the shell spirals counterclockwise when viewed from the apex.  They are often found in lakes, ponds, and slow-moving streams. |例句：The pond was teeming with Physa snails, their shells glistening in the sunlight. 池塘里挤满了椎实螺，它们的贝壳在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：暂无直接近义词，可根据语境使用其他淡水螺类名称|反义词：暂无|名词
