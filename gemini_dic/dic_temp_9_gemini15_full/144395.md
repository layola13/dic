outstrikes:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪks/| vt.|超过，胜过|To surpass or exceed in performance, extent, or amount.|例句：Our sales outstripped those of our competitors last year. 我们的销售额去年超过了竞争对手。|例句：His ambition outstripped his abilities. 他的野心超过了他的能力。|近义词：surpass, exceed, excel, outdo|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's profits outstrip those of its rivals. = 该公司的利润超过了其竞争对手。
His determination outstrips his fear. = 他的决心战胜了他的恐惧。


一般过去时:
The athlete outstripped all his opponents in the race. = 运动员在比赛中超过了所有对手。
Her success outstripped her expectations. = 她的成功超出了她的预期。


现在完成时:
Technology has outstripped our ability to control it. = 技术已经超过了我们控制它的能力。
The demand for housing has outstripped the supply. = 住房需求已经超过了供应。


过去完成时:
The army had outstripped the enemy's forces before reaching the city. = 军队到达城市之前已经超过了敌军。
The project had outstripped its initial budget. = 项目已经超过了最初的预算。


将来时:
We expect our production to outstrip the current market demand. = 我们预计我们的产量将超过目前的市场需求。
The growth of the city will outstrip that of the surrounding area. = 城市的增长将超过周边地区。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we might outstrip our competitors. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们可能会超过竞争对手。
If only I had outstripped my fears, I would have achieved more. = 如果我当时战胜了我的恐惧，我会取得更大的成就。


被动语态:
The company's performance was outstripped by its rivals. = 该公司的业绩被竞争对手超越。
His efforts were outstripped by unforeseen circumstances. = 他的努力被不可预见的情况所超越。


疑问句:
Does our innovation outstrip that of our competitors? = 我们的创新是否超过了竞争对手？
Will the demand for skilled workers outstrip the supply? = 熟练工人的需求是否会超过供应？


否定句:
Our progress didn't outstrip the expectations. = 我们的进展没有超过预期。
His achievements didn't outstrip those of his peers. = 他的成就没有超过同龄人。


PHRASE->

outstrip the demand = 超过需求
outstrip the previous record = 超过之前的记录
