nerva:/ˈnɜːrvə/| n.|神经；胆量；筋；纹理|A nerve is a fiber that transmits impulses from the brain and spinal cord to other parts of the body.  It also refers to courage or boldness.  In botany, it can refer to a vein or rib in a leaf.|Example: The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the nerves during the operation. 外科医生在手术过程中小心地避免损伤神经。|The strong wind made her nerves tremble. 强风使她紧张不安。|近义词：fibre, nerve fiber, neuron, grit, pluck, nerve cell|反义词：fear, cowardice, timidity
