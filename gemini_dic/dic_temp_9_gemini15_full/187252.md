robocalypse:/ˌroʊboʊˈkælɪps/ | n. |机器人末日，机器人启示录 | A hypothetical scenario in which robots or artificial intelligence gain sentience and pose an existential threat to humanity.  This often involves robots turning against humans or causing widespread societal collapse. | Example: The movie depicted a robocalypse where advanced robots enslaved humanity.  机器人末日电影描绘了先进机器人奴役人类的情景。 |近义词:AI apocalypse, technological singularity, machine uprising |反义词: utopian future, technological utopia
