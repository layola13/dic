nonwriters:/ˈnɒnˌraɪtərz/| n. |非作家；不会写作的人|People who do not write professionally or regularly.  They may be capable of writing, but it's not their primary occupation or skill.| Example:  Many nonwriters can still express themselves effectively through other mediums. 许多非作家仍然可以通过其他媒介有效地表达自己。|近义词：non-writers, nonwriting people|反义词：writers, authors
