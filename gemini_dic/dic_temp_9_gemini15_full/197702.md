shielding:/ˈʃiːldɪŋ/| n. v.|屏蔽；保护；防护|The act of protecting something or someone from harm or danger.  It can refer to a physical barrier or a protective measure.|例句：The soldiers used shielding to protect themselves from enemy fire. 士兵们用掩护来保护自己免受敌人的炮火。

例句：The company implemented new shielding to protect its data from hackers. 公司实施了新的防护措施来保护其数据免受黑客攻击。


近义词：protection, defense, safeguard, security|反义词：exposure, vulnerability

词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The metal shields the equipment from radiation. 金属屏蔽设备免受辐射。
He shields his eyes from the sun. 他用手遮挡阳光。


一般过去时:
They shielded the children from the storm. 他们保护孩子们免受暴风雨的侵袭。
The wall shielded the house from the wind. 这堵墙保护房子免受风吹。


现在完成时:
The government has shielded the economy from the worst effects of the crisis. 政府已使经济免受危机最严重的影响。
We have shielded our data from unauthorized access. 我们已经保护了我们的数据免受未经授权的访问。


过去完成时:
They had shielded the witness from the press before the trial. 审判前，他们已将证人与媒体隔离开来。
She had shielded her heart from further heartbreak. 她已经保护好自己的心，以免再次心碎。


将来时:
We will shield the project from any potential threats. 我们将保护项目免受任何潜在威胁。
They will shield the astronauts from harmful radiation. 他们将保护宇航员免受有害辐射。


虚拟语气:
If we had shielded the crops, they would not have been damaged. 如果我们保护了庄稼，它们就不会受损。
I wish I had shielded myself from his deception. 我真希望当初能保护自己不受他的欺骗。


被动语态:
The equipment is shielded from electromagnetic interference. 设备受到保护，免受电磁干扰。
The witness was shielded from the media. 证人受到媒体的保护。


疑问句:
What can we do to shield our data? 我们能做些什么来保护我们的数据？
How can we effectively shield the children? 我们如何有效地保护孩子们？


否定句:
The building did not shield us from the noise. 这座建筑物没有保护我们免受噪音的侵扰。
They failed to shield the information from leaks. 他们未能保护信息不被泄露。
