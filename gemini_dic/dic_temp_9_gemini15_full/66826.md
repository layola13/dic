expansivity: /ˌɛkspænˈsɪvɪti/ | n. |膨胀性；扩张性；广阔；健谈|

英文解析:The quality of being expansive; the tendency to expand or become larger.  It can refer to physical expansion,  a broadness of scope or mind, or even excessive talkativeness.

例句：

The expansivity of the desert landscape was breathtaking.  沙漠景观的广阔令人叹为观止。

His expansivity in the meeting made it difficult to stay focused on the agenda. 他在会议上的健谈使得很难集中精力在议程上。


近义词：expansion, breadth, vastness, volubility, loquacity

反义词：contraction, constriction, narrowness, brevity, taciturnity


词源:  From the Latin *expansivus*, from *expandere* "to spread out," from *ex-* "out" + *pandere* "to stretch, spread."
