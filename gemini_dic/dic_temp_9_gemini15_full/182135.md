remittances:/rɪˈmɪtənsɪz/| n.|汇款；汇兑；汇款额 |The sending of money, especially by mail, to a person in another place.  Remittances are a crucial source of income for many families in developing countries.|例句：International remittances support the economies of many developing nations. 国际汇款支撑着许多发展中国家的经济。

例句：My family relies heavily on remittances from my brother working abroad. 我的家人严重依赖我哥哥在国外工作的汇款。


近义词：transfers, payments, money orders

反义词：receipts (in the sense of receiving money)

词性:名词
