lindens:/ˈlɪndənz/| n.|椴树（复数）|Linden trees are known for their heart-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers.  They are often planted as ornamentals.|例句：The park was shaded by majestic lindens. 公园里矗立着雄伟的椴树。|例句：Many types of bees are attracted to the nectar of lindens. 许多种类的蜜蜂都被椴树的花蜜所吸引。|近义词：lime trees (less formal)|反义词：无
