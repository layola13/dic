fermatas:/fərˈmɑːtəz/| n. |持续音符，延长音|A fermata is a musical notation indicating that a note should be held longer than its usual value.  It is typically shown as a small arc placed above or below the note. The exact duration of the fermata is left to the performer's discretion, depending on the musical context.|Example: The singer held a long fermata on the final note, creating a dramatic effect.  演唱者在最后一个音符上保持了一个长长的持续音，营造出戏剧性的效果。|近义词：hold,sustain|反义词： staccato
