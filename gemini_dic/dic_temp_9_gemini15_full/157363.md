piperonal:/ˌpaɪpəˈroʊnəl/| n. |胡椒醛|A colorless crystalline compound with a strong, sweet, and slightly spicy odor, similar to heliotrope. It is used in perfumes and flavorings.  |Example:  Piperonal is a common ingredient in many perfumes due to its pleasant aroma. 胡椒醛因其令人愉悦的香气而成为许多香水中的常见成分。|近义词：heliotropin|反义词：无|名词
