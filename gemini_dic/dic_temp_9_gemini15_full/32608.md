chirper:/ˈtʃɜːrpər/ | n. | 啁啾者；叽叽喳喳的人；(尤指)快乐的人 | A chirper is a person who chirps, often implying a cheerful and lively disposition.  It can also refer to a bird or insect that chirps. | Example: The little girl was such a chirper, always singing and laughing.  那个小女孩真是个快乐的人，总是唱歌和大笑。  |近义词: chatterbox, twitterer, merrymaker | 反义词: grump, pessimist | 名词
