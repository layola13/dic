eviscerates: /ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪts/ | vt. |掏空内脏；使失去活力；彻底摧毁 | To remove the internal organs of (a body); to remove the essential or vital parts of something; to severely criticize or undermine something. |

例句：

The surgeon eviscerated the animal to study its internal organs. = 外科医生解剖了动物以研究其内脏器官。

The critic's review completely eviscerated the author's new novel. = 批评家的评论彻底摧毁了作者的新小说。


近义词： disembowel, gut, destroy, demolish, annihilate, devastate,  critique severely


反义词：  preserve, protect, strengthen, support, uphold
