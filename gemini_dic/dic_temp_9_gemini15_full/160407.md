popped-out: /pɑpt aʊt/ | adj. v. | 突然出现；凸出；脱落 |  Describes something that suddenly appeared or became visible, or something that protrudes or has come loose.  It can be used as an adjective describing the state of something or as a verb describing an action. |

例句：

The rabbit popped out from behind the bush.  兔子从灌木丛后面突然窜了出来。

A button popped out of my shirt. 我的衬衫上掉了一个扣子。


近义词：appeared, emerged, protruded, came out, jumped out

反义词：disappeared, receded, sunk in


USAGE->

The button pops out frequently. 扣子经常会脱落。
Something always pops out when I least expect it. 我总是在最意想不到的时候冒出一些想法。

一般过去时:
The cork popped out of the bottle. 瓶塞从瓶子里弹了出来。
He popped out to get some milk. 他出去买牛奶了。

现在完成时:
Several teeth have popped out since the accident. 事故以来，好几颗牙都掉了。
My fillings have popped out in the past. 我以前补牙的填充物也掉过。

过去完成时:
The picture had popped out of its frame before I noticed it.  在我注意到之前，照片已经从相框里掉了出来。
Before I knew it, the idea had popped out of my head. 我还没反应过来，这个想法就从我的脑海中蹦出来了。


将来时:
I think this button will pop out soon. 我觉得这颗扣子很快就会掉了。
The news will pop out soon, I guess. 我猜这个消息很快就会传出来。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
