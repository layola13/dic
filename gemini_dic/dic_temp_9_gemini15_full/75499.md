fry-pans:/ˈfraɪpænz/| n. |煎锅，平底锅|A frypan is a shallow, broad pan used for frying food.  It typically has a long handle and is made of metal, such as steel or aluminum.|例句：I need a larger frypan to cook this much pasta. 我需要一个更大的煎锅来煮这么多意面。|例句：She seasoned the chicken before placing it in the hot frypan.  她在把鸡肉放进热的煎锅之前先给它调味了。|近义词：frying pan, skillet|反义词:pot, kettle
