collards:/ˈkɑːlədz/| n. |羽衣甘蓝|A type of dark leafy green vegetable related to cabbage and kale, known for its slightly bitter flavor and tough leaves.  Collard greens are a staple in Southern US cuisine. |例句：My grandmother's collard greens recipe is legendary.  我祖母的羽衣甘蓝食谱非常有名。

例句：We had collard greens with smoked ham for dinner. 晚饭我们吃了烟熏火腿配羽衣甘蓝。|近义词：kale, cabbage |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其他蔬菜对比)

词源:
The word "collards" comes from the Old French word "coulard," which is a variant of "chou," meaning "cabbage."  This reflects its close botanical relationship to cabbage.
