out-glows:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊz/| v.|超越的光芒，超过的光辉| to shine or glow more brightly than something else; to surpass in brilliance or splendor. |例句：The moon out-glowed the stars that night.  那晚月亮的光芒超过了星星。

The diamond out-glowed all the other jewels in the crown.  这颗钻石的光芒超过了皇冠上的其他所有珠宝。|近义词：outshine, outshine, surpass, exceed|反义词：be dimmed by, be eclipsed by
