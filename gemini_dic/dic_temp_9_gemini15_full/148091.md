overscrupulous:/ˌoʊvərˈskruːpjʊləs/| adj.|过分谨慎的；过于小心谨慎的| excessively careful; painstaking to a fault.  It implies an excessive concern with details or moral principles, often to the point of being impractical or hindering progress. |例句：His overscrupulous attention to detail slowed down the project considerably. 他对细节的过度关注大大延缓了项目进度。

例句：She was overscrupulous in checking her work, leading to missed deadlines. 她过度仔细地检查她的工作，导致错过最后期限。


近义词：meticulous, painstaking, fastidious, scrupulous (but less extreme)

反义词：careless, negligent, reckless, lax


词性：形容词
