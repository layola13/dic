santon:/ˈsæntən/| n.|圣童，圣像；小雕像|A santon is a small, typically hand-crafted figurine, often depicting a religious figure or a traditional character, especially those associated with the Provençal Christmas crèche.|The artisan carefully painted each santon, giving each a unique personality.|近义词：figurine, statuette, figure|反义词：巨像，大型雕塑
