realizes:/ˈriːəlaɪzɪz/| v. |意识到，实现，了解|To understand a situation, fact, or feeling fully; to achieve or fulfill something.|He finally realized his mistake. 他最终意识到了自己的错误。  She realized her ambition to become a doctor. 她实现了成为医生的抱负。| understands, comprehends, achieves, accomplishes | ignores, overlooks, neglects, fails |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He realizes his potential. 他意识到自己的潜力。
She realizes the importance of education. 她意识到教育的重要性。

一般过去时:
I realized I was wrong. 我意识到我错了。
They realized their dream of traveling the world. 他们实现了环游世界的梦想。

现在完成时:
He has realized his mistake and apologized. 他已经意识到了自己的错误并道歉了。
She has realized how much she loves her family. 她已经意识到自己有多爱她的家人。

过去完成时:
I had realized my error before he pointed it out.  在他指出之前，我已经意识到了我的错误。
They had realized the danger before it was too late. 在为时已晚之前，他们已经意识到了危险。

将来时:
He will realize his dream one day. 他总有一天会实现他的梦想。
She will realize the consequences of her actions. 她将会意识到自己行为的后果。

虚拟语气:
If only I had realized sooner! 要是我早点意识到就好了！
I wish I had realized the importance of saving money. 我真希望我早点意识到储蓄的重要性。

被动语态:
His potential was realized by his mentor. 他的潜力得到了他导师的认可。
The importance of the project was not fully realized until later. 直到后来，这个项目的重要性才被充分认识到。

疑问句:
Do you realize how late it is? 你意识到现在已经很晚了么？
Does she realize the gravity of the situation? 她意识到局势的严重性了吗？

否定句:
He didn't realize the depth of his feelings. 他没有意识到自己感情的深度。
She didn't realize she had left her keys at home. 她没意识到把钥匙落在家里了。
