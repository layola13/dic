misclaim:/mɪsˈkleɪm/| v.|错误声称，误称|To make a false or inaccurate statement or claim.  To wrongly claim something as one's own or as belonging to oneself.|例句：He misclaimed ownership of the intellectual property. 他错误地声称拥有该知识产权。  The company misclaimed the effectiveness of its new product. 该公司错误地夸大了其新产品的功效。|近义词：misrepresent, misstate, falsify|反义词：accurately claim, truthfully assert|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misclaims his qualifications. = 他虚报自己的资历。
They misclaim the benefits of the product. = 他们夸大产品的益处。


一般过去时:
She misclaimed the amount of money she had earned. = 她谎报了她赚的钱数。
The politician misclaimed his support from the public. = 这位政治家谎称获得了公众的支持。


现在完成时:
He has misclaimed his experience in the field. = 他一直以来都虚报他在该领域的经验。
The company has misclaimed its market share for years. = 这家公司多年来一直虚报其市场份额。


过去完成时:
They had misclaimed their intentions before the negotiations. = 谈判前他们就已隐瞒了他们的意图。
She had misclaimed her relationship with the suspect. = 她之前就谎报了她与嫌疑人的关系。


将来时:
He will misclaim his involvement in the project. = 他将会否认自己参与了这个项目。(隐含的misclaim,  并非直接否认)
They will misclaim their financial status to get the loan. = 他们将虚报自己的财务状况以获得贷款。


虚拟语气:
If he misclaimed his age, he wouldn't have gotten the job. = 如果他谎报年龄，他就不会得到这份工作。
I wish they hadn't misclaimed their expertise. = 我真希望他们没有夸大他们的专业知识。


被动语态:
The facts were misclaimed by the media. = 事实被媒体歪曲了。
His qualifications were misclaimed on his resume. = 他的简历上虚报了他的资历。


疑问句:
Did he misclaim his identity? = 他是否谎报了自己的身份？
Were the figures misclaimed intentionally? = 这些数字是故意谎报的吗？


否定句:
He did not misclaim his intentions. = 他没有隐瞒他的意图。
They did not misclaim the product's features. = 他们没有夸大产品的特性。
