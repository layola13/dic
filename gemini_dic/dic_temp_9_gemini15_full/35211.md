cluckier:/ˈklʌkiər/| adj.|咯咯叫的；絮絮叨叨的；爱唠叨的|More inclined to cluck; showing more of a tendency to make clucking sounds or to be fussy or talkative.|The hen became cluckier as she watched her chicks scatter.|近义词：fussier, chattier, more garrulous|反义词：quiet, silent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The hen clucks constantly. = 母鸡不停地咯咯叫。
He is cluckier than his brother. = 他比他弟弟更爱唠叨。


一般过去时:
The hen clucked loudly after laying an egg. = 母鸡下蛋后大声咯咯叫。
She was cluckier yesterday than usual. = 她昨天比平时更爱唠叨。


现在完成时:
The hen has clucked all morning. = 母鸡整个上午都在咯咯叫。
He has become much cluckier since his retirement. = 他退休后变得更爱唠叨了。


过去完成时:
The hen had clucked before I even entered the coop. = 我还没进鸡舍，母鸡就已经咯咯叫了。
She had been cluckier than I'd ever seen her before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，她比我见过的任何时候都更爱唠叨。


将来时:
