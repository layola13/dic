murk:/mɜːrk/| n. adj.|黑暗，阴霾；模糊不清的 |Darkness, especially in the form of mist, fog, or smoke; something that is unclear or obscure. |例句：The fog hung heavy, shrouding the town in a thick murk.  浓雾弥漫，笼罩着小镇，一片阴霾。 |The details of the agreement remained shrouded in murk.  协议的细节仍然模糊不清。|近义词：darkness, gloom, obscurity, haze |反义词：clarity, light, brightness

词源:
The word "murk" ultimately derives from Old English *morc, meaning "darkness," "gloom," or "mist."  This Old English word is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
