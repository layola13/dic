comedian:/kəˈmiːdiən/ | n. |喜剧演员 | A comedian is a person who makes people laugh, typically by telling jokes or acting in a funny way. Comedians work in various settings, including stand-up comedy clubs, theaters, television, and film. Their performances often involve observational humor, satire, storytelling, and physical comedy.  | Example:  Jim Carrey is a famous comedian known for his slapstick style. 吉姆·凯瑞是一位以滑稽风格闻名的著名喜剧演员。

Example: The comedian's jokes were so funny that the audience roared with laughter.  这位喜剧演员的笑话太好笑了，观众都笑得前仰后合。|近义词：humorist, comic, funny person |反义词：tragedian
