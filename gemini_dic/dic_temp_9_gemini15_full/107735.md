lap:/læp/| n. v.|一圈；膝盖；拍打；重叠|A single circuit or revolution, as of a race track; the space between the upper and lower thighs of a seated person; to strike or pat gently; to overlap. |例句：He completed the lap in under 60 seconds. 他在不到60秒内完成了一圈。She sat on her lap. 她坐在她的膝盖上。The cat gently lapped up the milk. 猫轻轻地舔着牛奶。The shingles lapped over each other.  瓦片互相重叠。|近义词：round, lap over, pat|反义词：unlap, separate
