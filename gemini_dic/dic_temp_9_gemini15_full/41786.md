corsair:/kɔːˈsɛər/| n.|海盗；私掠船；剽悍的人 | A corsair is a pirate, especially one operating in the Mediterranean Sea.  The term often implies a degree of private authorization or at least a lack of explicit condemnation from a sovereign power, distinguishing them from simple pirates.  |例句：The corsair ship attacked the merchant vessel. 这艘海盗船袭击了商船。|例句：He was a daring corsair, known for his bravery and ruthlessness. 他是一个勇敢无畏、冷酷无情的海盗。|近义词：pirate, privateer, buccaneer |反义词：merchant, civilian |名词
