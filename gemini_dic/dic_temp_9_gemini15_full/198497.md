shrine:/ʃraɪn/| n.|圣地；神殿；圣坛|A shrine is a place that is considered sacred, often dedicated to a particular deity, person, or event.  It can be a building, a place in nature, or even an object.|例句：Many pilgrims visit the shrine to pray.  许多朝圣者前往圣地祈祷。|例句：A small shrine was built to commemorate the fallen soldiers.  一个小型圣坛被建造来纪念阵亡的士兵。|近义词：temple, sanctuary, altar|反义词：profane place, unholy ground
