indomptable: /ɪnˈdɒmptəbl/ | adj. | 不可征服的；不屈不挠的 |  Unconquerable; refusing to yield or be defeated.  | 例句：Her indomitable spirit helped her overcome many challenges.  她的不屈不挠的精神帮助她克服了许多挑战。

例句：The indomitable army fought bravely against overwhelming odds.  这支不可征服的军队英勇地对抗着压倒性的优势。


近义词：unyielding, unbending, invincible, resolute, tenacious

反义词：yielding, submissive, conquerable, weak, irresolute


词源:  From Old French *indomptable, from *in- "not" + dompter "to tame," from Latin domitare "to tame, subdue."
