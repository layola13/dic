deniable:/dɪˈnaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可否认的；不可靠的 |  capable of being denied; that can be refuted or disproved.  It suggests something is questionable or not easily proven. | The evidence against him was deniable, so he was acquitted.  他被指控的证据是可以否认的，所以他被无罪释放了。  His alibi was easily deniable. 他的不在场证明很容易被否认。 | undeniable, disputable, questionable | undeniable, irrefutable, provable
