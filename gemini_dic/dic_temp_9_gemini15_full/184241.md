resolve-on:/rɪˈzɑːlv ɒn/|v.|下定决心；决定|To make a firm decision to do something.  It implies a strong determination and commitment.|例句：She resolved on a career in medicine. 她下定决心从事医学事业。|例句：He resolved on climbing Mount Everest. 他下定决心攀登珠穆朗玛峰。|近义词：decide on, determine to, be resolved to|反义词：hesitate, waver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She resolves on a new strategy every year. = 她每年都制定新的策略。
They resolve on a course of action after careful consideration. = 他们在仔细考虑之后决定采取行动。


一般过去时:
He resolved on leaving his job last month. = 他上个月下定决心辞职。
She resolved on a trip to Europe after graduation. = 她毕业后决定去欧洲旅行。


现在完成时:
They have resolved on a plan to increase sales. = 他们已经决定了一个增加销售额的计划。
We have resolved on supporting the local charity. = 我们已经决定支持当地慈善机构。


过去完成时:
He had resolved on quitting smoking before his doctor's appointment. = 在去看医生之前，他已经下定决心戒烟。
She had resolved on pursuing her dreams before the setback. = 在遭遇挫折之前，她已经下定决心追求自己的梦想。


将来时:
They will resolve on a solution by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末之前找到解决办法。
She will resolve on her future plans after the conference. = 会议结束后，她将决定未来的计划。


虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would resolve on a different approach. = 如果我是她，我会采取不同的方法。
I wish I had resolved on studying harder earlier. = 我真希望我早点下定决心努力学习。


被动语态:
A decision was resolved on after a long debate. = 经过长时间的辩论后，做出了决定。(注意：被动语态用词略有不同，更常用被动形式的"a decision was made" )
A new policy will be resolved on next month. = 下个月将决定一项新政策。(同样，被动语态用词略有不同，更常用被动形式的"a new policy will be adopted")


疑问句:
What have you resolved on for your next project? = 你下一个项目决定做什么？
What will they resolve on concerning the budget? = 他们将如何决定预算？


否定句:
He did not resolve on any drastic measures. = 他没有决定采取任何严厉措施。
They haven't resolved on a date for the meeting yet. = 他们还没有确定会议日期。


PHRASE->
resolve on a course of action = 决定采取行动
resolve on a plan = 制定计划
resolve on a goal = 制定目标
resolve on a solution = 找到解决办法
resolve on a strategy = 制定策略
