homographs:/ˈhɒməɡræfs/ /ˈhɑːməɡræfs/ | n. |同形异义词 |Words that are spelled the same but have different meanings and may or may not be pronounced the same.  Homographs arise from different etymological origins or from a shift in meaning over time. | Examples:  "bank" (river bank) and "bank" (financial institution); "bat" (flying mammal) and "bat" (baseball bat) |近义词:  words with the same spelling but different meanings|反义词:  N/A
