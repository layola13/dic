recontesting:/ˌriːkənˈtɛstɪŋ/| v. |重新质疑；再次竞争；重新测试 |The act of contesting something again;  challenging a decision or result a second time;  repeating a test or examination. |例句：The losing candidate is recontesting the election results. 失败的候选人正在重新质疑选举结果。|例句：The scientist is recontesting the experiment to verify the findings. 科学家正在重复实验以验证结果。|近义词：rechallenging, appealing, retesting |反义词：accepting, conceding
