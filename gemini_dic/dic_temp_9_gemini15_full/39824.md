conspiracy: /kənˈspɪrəsi/ | n. |阴谋；密谋 | A secret plan by a group of people to do something unlawful or harmful.  |例句：The police uncovered a conspiracy to overthrow the government.  警方揭露了一个推翻政府的阴谋。

例句：They were accused of being involved in a conspiracy to commit fraud. 他们被指控参与了诈骗阴谋。

近义词：plot, scheme, intrigue, cabal | 反义词：openness, transparency
