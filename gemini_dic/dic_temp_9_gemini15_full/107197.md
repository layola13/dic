lagrangian:/ləˈɡreɪndʒɪən/| n. |拉格朗日量，拉格朗日函数|A Lagrangian is a function in classical mechanics that describes the dynamics of a system. It is the difference between the kinetic energy and the potential energy of the system.  The Lagrangian is used in the Euler-Lagrange equations to find the equations of motion for the system.|Example:  The Lagrangian of a simple pendulum is given by L = T - V, where T is the kinetic energy and V is the potential energy.  拉格朗日量是描述系统动力学的经典力学函数，是系统动能与势能之差。拉格朗日量用于欧拉-拉格朗日方程中，以求得系统的运动方程。 例如：简单摆的拉格朗日量由L = T - V给出，其中T是动能，V是势能。|近义词：Hamiltonian (in some contexts)|反义词：无
