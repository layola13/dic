reconstitutes:/ˌriːˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ | vt. | 使恢复；使重建；重新组成；复原 | To restore something to its former state or condition; to rebuild or reconstruct.  |例句：The dehydrated soup reconstitutes quickly in hot water. 热水很快就能把脱水的汤复原。

The company is trying to reconstitute its image after the scandal. 公司正试图在丑闻后重建其形象。


近义词：restore, rebuild, reconstruct, revive | 反义词：destroy, demolish, dismantle | 词性：动词
