retransmissions:/ˌriːtrænzˈmɪʃənz/| n. |重传，再发送|The process of sending a message or data again, typically because the initial transmission failed.  Retransmissions are crucial in ensuring reliable communication over unreliable channels, such as wireless networks. |例句：Due to network congestion, multiple retransmissions were required to successfully send the file. = 由于网络拥塞，需要多次重传才能成功发送文件。

The system automatically initiated retransmissions after detecting packet loss. = 系统在检测到数据包丢失后自动启动了重传。

|近义词：resendings, repetitions|反义词：initial transmissions|名词
