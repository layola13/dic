psycho-aesthetics: /ˌsaɪkoʊ.ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ | n. | 心理美学 | The study of the psychological responses to aesthetic experiences, including the perception, cognition, and emotion involved in appreciating art and beauty.  It explores the interplay between the mind and the experience of beauty.  | The psycho-aesthetic impact of the sculpture was profound, leaving viewers deeply moved and contemplating its meaning.  |  aesthetic psychology, art psychology |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with purely formalist approaches to aesthetics) | 名词
