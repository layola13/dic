oxide:/ˈɒksaɪd/ /ˈɑːksaɪd/| n. |氧化物|A compound of oxygen with another element or group, especially a binary compound.  Oxides are formed when oxygen reacts with other elements or compounds.  Many oxides are important in various industrial processes and natural phenomena.|例句：Iron oxide is a common component of rust.  氧化铁是锈蚀的常见成分。|例句：Silicon dioxide is the main component of sand. 二氧化硅是沙子的主要成分。|近义词：compound (in the context of chemical combination)|反义词：无|名词
