leavers:/ˈliːvərz/| n.| 离职者，毕业生 | People who are leaving a job, school, or other institution.  | Example:  The company is struggling to retain its best leavers. 这家公司正在努力留住最优秀的离职员工。 The leavers' ceremony was a very emotional occasion. 毕业典礼是一个非常感人的场合。|近义词：graduates (for school leavers),  departures, those leaving|反义词：new recruits, newcomers, joiners
