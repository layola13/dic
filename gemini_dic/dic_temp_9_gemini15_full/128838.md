nightwards:/ˈnaɪtwərdz/| adv.|向夜间；往夜里；朝夜晚|Moving or directed toward the night.  It suggests a movement or progression towards nightfall or the nighttime hours.|例句：The birds flew nightwards as the sun dipped below the horizon.  鸟儿在太阳落山时飞向夜空。

例句：The shadows lengthened, stretching nightwards across the valley.  影子拉长了，在山谷中延伸到夜晚。


近义词：towards night,  into the night


反义词：daywards


词性:副词
