predetentions:/ˌpriːdɪˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. | 预先拘留；预防性拘留 |  The act of detaining someone before a formal arrest or trial, often based on suspicion of future wrongdoing. This is usually done to prevent harm or escape.  |例句：The police made several predetentions in the lead-up to the protest. = 在抗议活动开始前，警方进行了多次预防性拘留。

例句：His predetention was deemed unlawful by the court. = 法院认为他的预防性拘留是非法的。| 近义词：preventive detention, preemptive arrest | 反义词：release, acquittal | 名词
