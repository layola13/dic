self-soothed:/ˌselfˈsuːðd/| adj.| 自我安慰的；自我抚慰的 |  Describes someone who has calmed or comforted themselves, typically after experiencing distress or anxiety.  It implies an action taken independently to alleviate negative feelings. | Example: After the scary movie, the child self-soothed by cuddling her teddy bear.  孩子看完恐怖电影后，抱着泰迪熊自我安慰。|近义词：self-comforted, calmed (oneself), reassured (oneself) |反义词：distressed, agitated, anxious |词性:形容词
