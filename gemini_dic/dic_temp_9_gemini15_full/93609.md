imbedded:/ɪmˈbedɪd/ | v. adj. | 嵌入的；植入的 | To fix (something) firmly and deeply in a surrounding mass.  |例句：The shrapnel was embedded in his leg.  弹片嵌入了他的腿里。|例句：The journalist embedded himself with the army unit.  记者随军报道。|近义词：insert, implant, embed | 反义词：extract, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jewel is embedded in the crown. = 这颗宝石镶嵌在王冠上。
He embeds himself in the local community. = 他融入当地社区。


一般过去时:
She embedded the code into the program. = 她将代码嵌入程序中。
They embedded themselves with the rebel group. = 他们潜伏在叛军组织中。


现在完成时:
The chip has been embedded in his brain. = 芯片已被植入他的大脑。
She has embedded herself in the company culture.  = 她已经融入公司文化。


过去完成时:
The spy had embedded himself in the organization for years. = 这个间谍已潜伏在这个组织多年。
The journalist had embedded herself with the troops before the battle. = 记者在战斗前已随军行动。


将来时:
They will embed the sensor in the device. = 他们将把传感器嵌入设备中。
She will embed herself in the research project. = 她将全身心投入研究项目。


虚拟语气:
If they embedded the software correctly, it would work. = 如果他们正确地嵌入软件，它就能工作。
I wish I had embedded the image properly. = 我真希望我正确地嵌入了图像。


被动语态:
The data was embedded into the file. = 数据被嵌入文件。
The reporter was embedded with the troops. = 记者随军报道。


疑问句:
Is the microchip embedded in the product? = 微芯片是否嵌入产品中？
Was the camera embedded in the wall? = 摄像机是嵌入墙里的吗？


否定句:
The message was not embedded in the audio file. = 信息没有嵌入音频文件中。
He didn't embed the image properly. = 他没有正确地嵌入图像。


PHRASE->
embedded system  = 嵌入式系统

embedded software = 嵌入式软件
deeply embedded = 深度嵌入
fully embedded = 完全嵌入
