caught-up: /kɔːt ʌp/ | adj. v. | 赶上的；忙碌的；陷入困境的 |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "caught up" describes someone who is very busy or involved in something.  As a verb, it means to reach the same level or point as someone or something else; to become involved in something unexpectedly; or to bring someone up to speed on something.

例句:

I'm so caught up with work at the moment. = 我现在工作太忙了。

She finally caught up with her classmates after missing a month of school. = 她缺课一个月后终于赶上了同学。


近义词:
busy, involved, engrossed, overtaken, up to date


反义词:
idle, free, behind, uninvolved


词性:
Adjective, Verb


USAGE->

将来时:
