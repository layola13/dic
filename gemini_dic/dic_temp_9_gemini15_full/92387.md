hypocritic:/ˌhɪpəˈkrɪtɪk/| adj.|伪善的；虚伪的|Characterized by hypocrisy; insincere; pretending to have virtues, beliefs, or principles that one does not actually possess.|例句：His hypocritical pronouncements on morality contrasted sharply with his private life.  他的关于道德的伪善声明与其私生活形成了鲜明对比。

例句：It was hypocritical of her to criticize his gambling when she herself played the lottery every week. 她批评他的赌博行为却自己每周都买彩票，这是虚伪的。


近义词：insincere, sanctimonious, dishonest, two-faced|反义词：sincere, genuine, honest, truthful
