green-lighted:/ˌɡriːnˈlaɪtɪd/| adj. |绿灯放行；批准；获准|Given official approval; authorized to proceed.|The project was green-lighted by the board of directors.  董事会批准了该项目。|近义词：approved, authorized, sanctioned|反义词：rejected, denied, vetoed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is green-lighted. = 项目已获批准。
They green-light new ideas frequently. = 他们经常批准新的想法。


一般过去时:
The proposal was green-lighted last week. = 该提案上周获得了批准。
The film was green-lighted after several revisions. = 电影经过多次修改后获得了批准。


现在完成时:
The initiative has been green-lighted. = 该倡议已获批准。
Many projects have been green-lighted this year. = 今年许多项目都已获批准。


过去完成时:
The deal had been green-lighted before the meeting. = 在会议之前，这笔交易就已经获得了批准。
They had green-lighted the budget before the crisis hit. = 在危机发生之前，他们已经批准了预算。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
