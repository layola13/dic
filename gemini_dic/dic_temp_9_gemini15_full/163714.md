predefaults:/ˌpriːdɪˈfɔːlts/ | n. | 预设值，默认设置 | Predefaults refer to settings or values that are automatically assigned or selected by a system or application unless the user explicitly changes them.  These are often used to provide a convenient starting point or a reasonable default behavior. | Example:  The predefaults for the printer were set to black and white printing.  打印机的预设值为黑白打印。|近义词：default settings, default values, initial settings |反义词：custom settings, user-defined settings| 名词
