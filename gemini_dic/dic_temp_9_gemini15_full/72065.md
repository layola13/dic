flits:/flɪts/| v.|飞掠；快速移动；一闪而过 |To move swiftly and lightly; to dart or flit.  The butterfly flits from flower to flower.  |例句：The hummingbird flits from blossom to blossom.  蜂鸟在花丛中飞来飞去。
The idea flits through my mind. 这个想法一闪而过我的脑海。|近义词：dart, flicker, flash, skim |反义词：lumber, trudge, creep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird flits among the trees.  鸟儿在树林中飞来飞去。
He flits between his two jobs. 他在他两份工作之间奔波。

一般过去时:
The shadow flitted across the wall. 影子快速掠过墙壁。
She flitted from room to room, tidying up. 她快速地从一个房间跑到另一个房间，打扫卫生。

现在完成时:
The butterfly has flitted away. 蝴蝶飞走了。
He has flitted in and out of my life for years. 多年来，他一直断断续续地出现在我的生活中。

过去完成时:
The thought had flitted through her mind before she spoke.  在她说话之前，这个想法已经在她脑海中闪过。
Before I knew it, the day had flitted by.  不知不觉中，一天就过去了。

将来时:
The dragonfly will flit across the pond.  蜻蜓将会飞过池塘。
I think she will flit between several different projects. 我认为她会在几个不同的项目之间来回切换。

虚拟语气:
If the bird flitted closer, I could see it better. 如果鸟飞近一点，我就能看得更清楚了。
I wish the time would flit by faster. 我希望时间能过得更快一点。


被动语态:
The light was flitted across the ceiling. 灯光在房顶上快速移动。 (This usage is less common)
The image was flitted across the screen. 图像快速闪过屏幕。(This usage is less common)


疑问句:
Does the hummingbird flit quickly? 蜂鸟飞得快吗？
Did the shadow flit past you? 影子从你身边掠过吗？

否定句:
The butterfly didn't flit near the flowers. 蝴蝶没有飞到花朵附近。
He didn't flit around aimlessly; he had a purpose. 他不是漫无目的地四处乱窜；他是有目的的。
