shutters:/ˈʃʌtərz/ | n. | 百叶窗；快门 |  Protective coverings for windows, typically made of wood or metal slats; in photography, a device that controls the amount of light reaching the film or sensor.  | Example: The old house had beautiful wooden shutters. 这栋老房子有漂亮的木制百叶窗。  The photographer adjusted the camera shutters to get the perfect exposure. 摄影师调整了相机快门以获得完美的曝光。 |近义词：blinds, shades | 反义词：open windows
