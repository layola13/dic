flavoring:/ˈfleɪvəriŋ/| n.|调味品，调味料|A substance used to add flavor to food or drink.  It can be natural or artificial.|例句：Vanilla flavoring is often added to ice cream. 香草调味料常被添加到冰淇淋中。

例句：The chef used a special blend of flavorings to create a unique dish.  厨师使用了独特的调味料混合物来制作一道独特的菜肴。


近义词：seasoning, spice, condiment|反义词：无
