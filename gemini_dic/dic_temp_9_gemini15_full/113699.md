macrame:/ˈmækrəmeɪ/| n.|马克兰工艺，编结工艺|A form of textile-making using knotting rather than weaving or knitting.  Macrame is often used to create decorative items such as wall hangings, plant holders, and jewelry.|例句：She learned macrame from her grandmother and now makes beautiful wall hangings. 她从祖母那里学会了编织工艺，现在制作精美的挂毯。|近义词：knotting, crocheting, knitting|反义词: weaving (in the sense of a contrasting textile technique)
