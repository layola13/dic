fender-benders:/ˈfɛndərˌbɛndərz/| n. |轻微的交通事故|A fender bender is a minor car accident, typically involving a collision between the fenders of two vehicles, resulting in little to no serious damage.  It usually involves only superficial damage to the vehicles and no or minimal injuries. | Example:  We had a fender bender in the parking lot this morning, just a little scratch on the bumper.  今天早上我们在停车场发生了一起轻微的交通事故，只是保险杠上有一点划痕。


There was a fender bender at the intersection, but luckily no one was hurt. 路口发生了一起轻微的交通事故，但幸运的是没有人受伤。|近义词：minor accident, scrape, bump |反义词：major accident, crash, collision |
