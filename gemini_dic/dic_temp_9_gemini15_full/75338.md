frostiness:/ˈfrɒstɪnɪs/| n.|冷淡；严寒；霜冻|The quality of being frosty; coldness of manner; a feeling of coldness or unfriendliness.|例句：There was a frostiness in his voice that suggested displeasure.  他的声音里带着一丝冷淡，暗示着不满。

例句：The early frostiness damaged the crops.  早霜损坏了庄稼。|近义词：coldness, chilliness, iciness, frigidity|反义词：warmth, cordiality, friendliness
