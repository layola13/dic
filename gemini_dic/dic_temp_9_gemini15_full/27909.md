canter:/ˈkæntər/|/ˈkɑːntər/| n. v.|小跑；缓步跑|A canter is a relatively slow and easy gait of a horse, faster than a walk but slower than a gallop.  It is a comfortable pace for both horse and rider.|例句：The horse cantered gracefully across the field. 这匹马优雅地小跑穿过田野。|例句：We enjoyed a leisurely canter along the beach. 我们沿着海滩享受了一次悠闲的小跑。|近义词：lope, trot, amble|反义词：gallop, sprint|动词，名词


USAGE->

He canters his horse every morning. = 他每天早上都骑马慢跑。
The horses canter across the field. = 马儿们在田野里慢跑。
