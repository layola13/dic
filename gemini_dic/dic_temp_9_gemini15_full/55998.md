do-a-number-on:/duː ə ˈnʌmbər ɒn/| phrasal verb|狠狠地对待；严重破坏；欺骗；戏弄|To severely affect or damage something or someone; to deceive or trick someone; to playfully tease or make fun of someone.  |例句：The storm did a number on the garden.  暴风雨把花园糟蹋得很厉害。

例句：He tried to do a number on me, but I saw through his lies. 他试图欺骗我，但我识破了他的谎言。


近义词：destroy, damage, ruin, deceive, trick, fool, tease,  play a trick on


反义词：help, assist, protect, support, be honest with


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain does a number on the crops every year.  每年的暴雨都严重损害庄稼。
He often does a number on his little brother by playing pranks. 他经常恶作剧来戏弄他的小弟弟。


一般过去时:
The accident did a number on his car. 事故严重损坏了他的车。
She did a number on the presentation, making it completely unintelligible. 她把演示文稿弄得一塌糊涂，完全让人无法理解。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

They had done a number on the evidence, making it hard to prosecute. 他们篡改了证据，使得起诉变得困难。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

If I hadn't studied hard, the exam would have done a number on my grade. 如果我不努力学习，考试就会严重影响我的成绩。


被动语态:

否定句:
