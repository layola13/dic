recognitor:/ˈrɛkəɡnaɪtər/| n. |识别器，识别装置|A recognitor is a device or system that identifies or recognizes something, such as a person's face, a voice, or a pattern.|Example: The security system uses facial recognitor to grant access.  安全系统使用面部识别器来授予访问权限。|近义词：identifier, detector, verifier|反义词：obfuscator
