capulin:/kæpjuˈlin/| n. |卡普林（一种浆果）|A small, dark-colored, sweet cherry-like fruit of a Mexican shrub (Prunus capuli).  It is also known as capulín cherry. The fruit is commonly used in jams, jellies, and other desserts.|Example: We made a delicious pie filled with fresh capulin. 我们做了一个美味的馅饼，里面装满了新鲜的卡普林。  The capulin trees bloomed profusely this year. 今年卡普林树开满了花。|近义词：cherry, berry|反义词：无
