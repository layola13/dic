receder:/rɪˈsiːd/| vi.| 后退；减弱；撤回；(头发)后移 | To move back or away from a previous position; to become less intense or noticeable; to withdraw or retreat; (of a hairline) to recede from the forehead. |例句：The floodwaters finally receded. 洪水最终退去。|例句：His hairline is beginning to recede. 他的发际线开始后移。|近义词：retreat, withdraw, abate, diminish |反义词：advance, progress, increase, grow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tide recedes twice a day. = 潮汐一天退两次。
His influence is receding. = 他的影响力正在减弱。

一般过去时:
The pain receded after he took the medicine. = 他服药后疼痛减轻了。
The army receded from the border. = 军队从边境撤军了。

现在完成时:
The floodwaters have receded, leaving behind a trail of destruction. = 洪水已经退去，留下一片破坏的景象。
The symptoms have receded, but I still feel weak. = 症状已经减轻，但我仍然感到虚弱。

过去完成时:
The glacier had receded significantly by the time we arrived. = 我们到达时，冰川已经明显后退了。
The threat had receded after the negotiations. = 谈判后威胁消退了。

将来时:
The storm is expected to recede by tomorrow. = 预计风暴将于明天减弱。
The government will likely recede from its hardline stance. = 政府可能会放弃其强硬立场。

虚拟语气:
If the ice caps receded further, sea levels would rise dramatically. = 如果冰盖进一步融化，海平面将急剧上升。
I wish my worries would recede. = 我希望我的担忧能够消退。

被动语态:
The problem cannot be receded from. = 这个问题无法回避。
The decision was receded from after much debate. =经过多次辩论后，该决定被撤回。


疑问句:
Is the pain receding? = 疼痛正在减轻吗？
Will the hairline recede further? = 发际线还会继续后移吗？


否定句:
The floodwaters didn't recede for days. = 洪水持续多日未退。
The symptoms haven't receded despite treatment. = 尽管进行了治疗，症状仍未减轻。


PHRASE->
receding hairline = 后退的发际线
receding glacier = 后退的冰川
receding gums = 牙龈萎缩
receding memory = 记忆衰退
receding shoreline = 后退的海岸线

词源:
receder 源于古法语 *recedir，来自拉丁语 recedere，由 re- (向后) + cedere (走，让步) 构成。
