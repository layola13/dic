quitted:/kwɪtɪd/| v.|停止；离开；辞职；放弃|To stop doing something; to leave a job or place; to give up.|例句：He quitted his job after a heated argument with his boss. 他和老板发生激烈争吵后辞职了。|例句：She quitted smoking after her doctor's warning.  医生警告后，她戒了烟。|近义词：stopped, resigned, abandoned, relinquished|反义词：continued, remained, persisted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quits his job every few months. 他每隔几个月就辞职。
She quits smoking every year, but always starts again. 她每年都戒烟，但总是又开始。


一般过去时:
He quitted his job last week. 他上周辞职了。
She quitted smoking cold turkey. 她突然戒了烟。


现在完成时:
He has quitted smoking for five years. 他戒烟五年了。
She has quitted her job and started her own business. 她已经辞职并开始自己创业了。


过去完成时:
He had quitted his job before he moved to the countryside. 他搬到乡下之前已经辞职了。
She had quitted smoking several times before she finally succeeded.  在她最终成功之前，她已经戒烟好几次了。


将来时:
He will quit his job next month. 他下个月将辞职。
She will quit smoking once she feels ready. 她准备好后就会戒烟。


虚拟语气:
If he quitted his job, he would have more free time. 如果他辞职，他就会有更多空闲时间。
If she had quitted smoking earlier, she would be healthier now. 如果她早点戒烟，现在就会更健康。


被动语态:
His job was quitted by him last year. 他的工作去年被他辞掉了。(This is unnatural and not commonly used.)
Smoking should be quitted by everyone for better health.  为了健康，每个人都应该戒烟。(This is more acceptable in this context)


疑问句:
Did he quit his job? 他辞职了吗？
Has she quitted smoking yet? 她戒烟了吗？


否定句:
He didn't quit his job. 他没有辞职。
She hasn't quitted smoking yet. 她还没戒烟。


PHRASE->
quit cold turkey: 突然戒掉（毒品、坏习惯等）
quit smoking: 戒烟
quit drinking: 戒酒
quit a job: 辞职
quit one's bad habits: 改掉坏习惯
