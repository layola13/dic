cenobite:/ˈsiːnəbaɪt/| n.|苦行僧；禁欲主义者；（尤指）克莱尔·克莱门特的《地狱神探》中的角色|A cenobite is a member of a religious order living a life of extreme asceticism, often involving self-mortification.  The term has also gained popularity through Clive Barker's "Hellraiser" series, where cenobites are supernatural beings who inflict pain and pleasure. |例句：In some ancient religious orders, cenobites lived in communal settings and practiced extreme self-denial.  在一些古老的宗教组织中，苦行僧们过着集体生活，并且实行极端的自我克制。

The cenobites in "Hellraiser" are far from the traditional religious connotations of the word.   《地狱神探》中的苦行僧与该词的传统宗教含义相去甚远。|近义词：ascetic, hermit, recluse|反义词：hedonist, libertine|名词
