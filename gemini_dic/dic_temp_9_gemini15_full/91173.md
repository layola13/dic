hydrant:/ˈhaɪdrənt/| n.|消防栓|A hydrant is a vertical pipe with a nozzle, usually located on a street, that provides water for firefighting.  It is typically connected to a municipal water supply.|Example: The firefighter quickly connected the hose to the hydrant. 消防员迅速将水管连接到消防栓上。|近义词：fireplug|反义词：无

The hydrant provides water for emergencies. = 消防栓提供紧急用水。
Firefighters often use hydrants during fires. = 消防员经常在火灾中使用消防栓。
