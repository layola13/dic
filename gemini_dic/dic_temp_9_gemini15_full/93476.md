illustrated:/ˈɪləstreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有插图的；图解的；例证的 | Containing illustrations; explained or made clear by examples.  | The illustrated book was a joy to read. 这本图画书读起来很愉快。 |近义词：pictured, exemplified, demonstrated | 反义词：unillustrated, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book is illustrated with beautiful photographs. = 这本书用美丽的图片进行了插图说明。
This article illustrates the point perfectly. = 这篇文章完美地阐明了这一点。


一般过去时:
The artist illustrated the children's storybook. = 艺术家为儿童故事书配图。
He illustrated his argument with several examples. = 他用几个例子来说明了他的论点。


现在完成时:
The author has illustrated many books for children. = 作者已经为孩子们绘制过许多图书插图。
She has illustrated her theory with compelling evidence. = 她用令人信服的证据证明了她的理论。


过去完成时:
The manuscript had been illustrated before it was published. = 手稿在出版前就已经配图了。
He had illustrated his point convincingly before the debate began. = 在辩论开始之前，他已经令人信服地阐明了他的观点。


将来时:
The new textbook will be illustrated in full color. = 新教科书将以全彩印刷。
She will illustrate her presentation with charts and graphs. = 她将用图表来说明她的演示。


虚拟语气:
If the book were illustrated, it would sell better. = 如果这本书有插图，它会卖得更好。
I wish my report were illustrated with more data. = 我希望我的报告能用更多的数据来说明。


被动语态:
The story was illustrated by a famous artist. = 这个故事是由一位著名的艺术家绘制插图的。
The concept is illustrated by the following example. = 下面的例子说明了这个概念。


疑问句:
Is the magazine illustrated? = 这本杂志有插图吗？
How is this concept illustrated in the text? = 文本中是如何说明这个概念的？


否定句:
The article wasn't illustrated. = 这篇文章没有插图。
He didn't illustrate his argument clearly enough. = 他没有足够清楚地阐明他的论点。
