hyperacuity:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkjuːɪti/| n. |超敏锐视觉；超常视力|The ability to perceive extremely fine visual details, exceeding the normal limits of visual acuity.  This often involves the ability to detect small differences in the spatial arrangement of visual stimuli.  Hyperacuity tasks include vernier acuity, stereoacuity, and contour interaction.|例句：Research into hyperacuity has revealed surprising capabilities of the human visual system. 研究超敏锐视觉揭示了人类视觉系统的惊人能力。

例句：The experiment measured participants' hyperacuity using a high-resolution display. 实验使用高分辨率显示器测量参与者的超敏锐视觉。


近义词：superacuity,  exceptional visual acuity|反义词：poor vision, low visual acuity|词性：名词
