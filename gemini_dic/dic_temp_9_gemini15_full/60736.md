electrocoat:/ɪˈlɛktrəʊkoʊt/ | vt. | 电泳涂装，电沉积涂层 | To apply a coating to a metal object by means of electrophoresis. This process involves immersing the object in a bath containing charged paint particles, and applying an electric current to deposit the paint evenly onto the surface.  | Example: The car body was electrocoated for corrosion protection. 汽车车身进行了电泳涂装以防腐蚀。 | 近义词:electrophoretically deposit, paint electrophoretically | 反义词: N/A
