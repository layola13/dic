idiotize:/ˈɪdiətaɪz/ | vt. |使愚蠢；使变傻；使白痴化 | To make someone or something stupid or foolish; to render someone or something idiotic.  |例句：The constant barrage of misinformation idiotized the population.  持续不断的错误信息使民众变得愚蠢。

例句：His actions idiotized the entire project. 他的行为使整个项目变得愚蠢。

近义词：befuddle, stupefy,  obfuscate,  dumbfound

反义词：enlighten, illuminate, clarify,  educate

词性:动词
