distinguish-by:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃbaɪ/| vt.|根据…区别；以…为区分标准|To recognize or treat (someone or something) as different from others; to identify or perceive the difference between.|The twins are difficult to distinguish by appearance alone.  这对双胞胎仅凭外表很难区分。|The birds can be distinguished by their songs.  这些鸟可以根据它们的歌声来区分。| differentiate, discriminate, separate|confuse, blend, merge|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We distinguish this product by its high quality. = 我们以其高质量来区分这款产品。
They distinguish the two theories by their different assumptions. = 他们根据不同的假设来区分这两个理论。

一般过去时:
Scientists distinguished the two species by their genetic markers. = 科学家们根据它们的基因标记区分了这两个物种。
She distinguished herself from the others by her hard work. = 她通过努力工作使自己与众不同。

现在完成时:
Researchers have distinguished several subtypes of the virus by their effects on the host. = 研究人员根据病毒对宿主的不同影响，已经区分了几种病毒亚型。
We have distinguished our company by its commitment to customer service. = 我们公司以其对客户服务的承诺而著称。

过去完成时:
By the time the investigation ended, they had distinguished the perpetrator from the witnesses by their alibis. = 在调查结束时，他们已经根据不在场证明将作案者与证人区分开来。
Before the experiment, they had distinguished the control group from the experimental group by random assignment. = 在实验之前，他们通过随机分配将对照组和实验组区分开来。

将来时:
We will distinguish the genuine product from the counterfeit by its unique serial number. = 我们将根据其独特的序列号来区分真伪产品。
Future studies will distinguish the effects of this drug by analyzing various patient demographics. = 未来的研究将通过分析不同的患者人口统计数据来区分该药物的作用。

虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could distinguish the two hypotheses more effectively. = 如果我们有更多数据，我们就能更有效地区分这两种假设。
I wish I could distinguish the subtle differences in their accents. = 我希望我能区分他们口音的细微差别。

被动语态:
The two paintings were distinguished by their use of color. = 这两幅画因其色彩运用而有所区别。
The winning team was distinguished by its superior strategy. = 获胜的球队以其优越的策略而著称。

疑问句:
How do you distinguish between fact and opinion? = 你如何区分事实和观点？
What characteristics distinguish this species from others? = 什么特征将这个物种与其他物种区分开来？

否定句:
I can't distinguish the different shades of blue. = 我无法区分不同的蓝色阴影。
They failed to distinguish the cause from the effect. = 他们未能区分原因和结果。


PHRASE->

distinguish from = 将…与…区分开
distinguish A from B = 将A与B区分开
easily distinguishable = 易于区分的
