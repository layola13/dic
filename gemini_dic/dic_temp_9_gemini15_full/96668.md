industriously:/ɪnˈdʌstriəsli/ | adv. |勤勉地；勤奋地；努力地 |Diligent and hardworking in a way that shows dedication and effort.  The adverb "industriously" describes an action performed with consistent effort and diligence. It emphasizes the persistent and focused nature of the activity. |例句：She industriously worked on her project until late into the night. 她勤奋地工作到深夜。

She industriously researched the topic, gathering information from various sources. 她勤奋地研究了这个话题，从各种来源收集信息。|近义词：diligently, assiduously, diligently, painstakingly |反义词：lazily, idly, sluggishly
