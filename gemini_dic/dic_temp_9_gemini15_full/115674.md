margaritas:/ˌmɑːrɡəˈriːtəz/| n. |玛格丽塔酒 |A margarita is a popular cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur (typically Cointreau or Triple Sec).  It's usually served with salt on the rim of the glass.  |例句：I ordered two margaritas at the bar. 我在酒吧点了两杯玛格丽塔酒。  She prefers her margaritas on the rocks. 她喜欢她的玛格丽塔酒加冰块。|近义词：cocktail, mixed drink |反义词：无
