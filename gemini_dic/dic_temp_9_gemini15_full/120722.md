misallocating: /ˌmɪsˈæləˌkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 错配；分配错误；误用 |  To allocate incorrectly or inefficiently; to use something for the wrong purpose. | 例句：The company is misallocating resources, leading to decreased efficiency. 公司资源配置不当，导致效率下降。
The government's misallocating of funds resulted in several failed projects. 政府资金分配不当导致多个项目失败。 | 近义词：misapply, misdirect, misuse | 反义词：allocate properly, optimize
