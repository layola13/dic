outjumping:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmpɪŋ/| v.|跳得更远；超越|The act of jumping farther than someone or something else.  It implies surpassing someone in a competition or achievement involving jumping.|例句：The kangaroo outjumped the dog. 袋鼠跳得比狗远。

例句：He outjumped all his competitors in the long jump. 他在跳远比赛中跳得比所有竞争对手都远。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outdistancing

反义词：being outjumped, underperforming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outjumps his opponents regularly. 他经常跳得比对手远。
The athlete outjumps the previous record holder.  这位运动员跳得比之前的纪录保持者远。


一般过去时:
The horse outjumped the fence. 马跳过了栅栏。
She outjumped her personal best in the high jump. 她跳高成绩超过了个人最好成绩。


现在完成时:
He has outjumped all expectations. 他已经超出了所有人的预期。
The team has outjumped their rivals this season. 这个赛季，这支队伍的跳跃能力超过了他们的对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the competition, she had outjumped everyone else. 比赛结束时，她已经跳过了其他人。
They had outjumped their previous best score before the final jump. 在最后一次跳跃之前，他们已经超过了之前的最好成绩。


将来时:
