colorize:/ˈkʌlərɑɪz/ | vt. |着色；使…彩色|To add color to (something, especially a photograph or video) , often digitally.|例句：He colorized the old black and white film. 他给那部黑白老电影着色了。
例句：The software can colorize old photos. 这个软件可以给旧照片着色。|近义词：dye, tint, paint|反义词：decolorize, bleach
