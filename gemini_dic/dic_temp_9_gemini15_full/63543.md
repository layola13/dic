enveloper:/ɪnˈvɛləpər/| n.|信封;包裹者；覆盖物|A person or thing that envelops or encloses something.  An enveloper is something that wraps or surrounds something else, often to protect or conceal it.  It can refer to a literal envelope for letters, or a metaphorical enveloper, such as a cloud of smoke.|例句：The thick fog acted as an enveloper, obscuring the city from view.  浓雾就像一层覆盖物，遮蔽了城市景色。 The postman delivered the enveloper containing important documents. 邮递员送来了装着重要文件的信封。|近义词：wrapper, container, covering, sheath|反义词：unveiler, exposer
