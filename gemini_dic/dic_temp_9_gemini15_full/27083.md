calibration:/ˌkælɪˈbreɪʃən/| n. |校准，标定 |The process of adjusting or checking the accuracy of an instrument or device by comparing its readings to a known standard.  Calibration ensures that measurements are accurate and reliable. |例句：The technician performed a calibration on the scale to ensure accurate weight measurements. 技师对秤进行了校准，以确保重量测量的准确性。


The scientist calibrated the equipment before conducting the experiment. 科学家在进行实验之前校准了设备。|近义词：adjustment, standardization, verification |反义词：miscalibration, inaccuracy
