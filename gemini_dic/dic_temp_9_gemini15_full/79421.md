glissandos:/ɡlɪˈsændoʊz/ | n. | 滑音 (复数) | A gliding or sliding movement, especially one made by a finger across the strings of a stringed instrument to produce a continuous change in pitch.  | Example: The violinist's glissandos were breathtaking.  小提琴家演奏的滑音令人叹为观止。 | 近义词:slide, sweep | 反义词:  staccato (a detached, separate note)
