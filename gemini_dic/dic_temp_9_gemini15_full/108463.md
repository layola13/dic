lay-bys:/ˈleɪbaɪz/| n.|路边停车带；临时停靠处|A lay-by is a wide area at the side of a road where drivers can stop their vehicles, often for a rest or to allow other vehicles to pass.|例句：We pulled into a lay-by to have a picnic. 我们把车停在一个路边停车带野餐。
例句：The lay-by was full of cars. 路边停车带停满了汽车。|近义词：pull-off, roadside stop|反义词：无
