pardo:/ˈpɑːrdoʊ/| adj.|斑驳的，杂色的；（尤指）浅褐色的| mottled, dappled; especially light brownish|The pardo horse had a beautiful coat. 这匹浅褐色斑驳的马有着美丽的毛色。|近义词：mottled, dappled, piebald|反义词：uniform, plain
