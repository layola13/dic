polygon:/ˈpɒlɪɡɒn/ /ˈpɑːlɪɡɑːn/| n. |多边形|A polygon is a plane figure with at least three straight sides and angles.  It is a closed shape with multiple sides.  The sides can be of different lengths, and the angles can be of different sizes.|例句：A triangle is a polygon with three sides. 三角形是一个有三条边的多边形。
A hexagon is a polygon with six sides. 六边形是一个有六条边的多边形。|近义词：multigon|反义词：circle, curve
