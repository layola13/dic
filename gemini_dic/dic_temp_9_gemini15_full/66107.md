excellence:/ˈɛksələns/| n. |卓越，优秀，杰出|Excellence is a high standard of quality.  卓越是高质量的标准。|The excellence of her work is undeniable. 她作品的优秀是不可否认的。|superiority, perfection |mediocrity, inferiority |名词
