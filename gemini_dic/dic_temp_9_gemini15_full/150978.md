parametric:/ˌpærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 参数的；参量的；用参数表示的 | Relating to or involving parameters;  characterized by the use of parameters.  A parametric equation uses parameters to define a curve or surface.  Parametric studies vary one or more parameters to see the effect on the outcome. |

例句：

The parametric model accurately predicted the system's behavior.  参数模型准确地预测了系统的行为。

We used a parametric design to generate various shapes. 我们使用参数化设计来生成各种形状。


近义词：variable, adjustable, controlled


反义词：fixed, constant, invariable


词性：形容词
