frisson:/ˈfrɪsɒn/|/ˈfrɪsən/| n. |一阵激动；一阵兴奋；寒意；战栗|A sudden feeling of excitement and pleasure; a thrill.  It can also refer to a sudden feeling of fear or horror, or a slight shiver. |例句：A frisson of excitement ran through the audience.  观众中涌起一阵兴奋。

例句：A frisson of fear gripped her as she entered the dark alley. 她走进黑暗的小巷时，一阵恐惧感抓住她。|近义词：thrill, shiver, shudder, tremor|反义词：calmness, indifference, apathy|词性：名词
