infecting: /ɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 感染的；传染的 |  infecting refers to the process of causing infection or the state of being infected.  It can be used as a verb (present participle of "infect") or an adjective describing something that is capable of causing infection. |例句：The doctor is treating a patient with an infecting disease. 医生正在治疗一位患有传染病的病人。

The bacteria are infecting the wound. 细菌正在感染伤口。


近义词：contagious, contaminating, spreading | 反义词：sterile, clean, disinfecting
