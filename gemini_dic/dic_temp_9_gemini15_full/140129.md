off-the-cuff:/ˌɒf ðə kʌf/ /ˌɑːf ðə kʌf/| adj.|即兴的；未经准备的；脱口而出的|Impromptu, without preparation or forethought.  It suggests a casual and spontaneous manner of speaking or acting.|例句：He gave an off-the-cuff remark about the situation. 他对局势发表了即兴的评论。

例句：Her off-the-cuff answer surprised everyone.  她脱口而出的回答让每个人都感到惊讶。

近义词：improvised, impromptu, spontaneous, extemporaneous

反义词：rehearsed, planned, prepared, deliberate
