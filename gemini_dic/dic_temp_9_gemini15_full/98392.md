insolvency: /ɪnˈsɒlvənsi/ | n. |无力偿债，破产 | The state of being insolvent; inability to pay one's debts.  |例句：The company's insolvency led to the loss of many jobs. 公司无力偿债导致许多人失业。 |近义词：bankruptcy, default, failure |反义词：solvency
