concurred:/kənˈkɜːrd/| v.|同意，一致；同时发生|To agree or be in agreement; to happen at the same time. |例句：The committee concurred on the proposal.  委员会同意了这项提案。|例句：The two events concurred in time.  这两件事同时发生。|近义词：agree, coincide, correspond|反义词：disagree, differ, conflict|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jury concurs with the verdict. = 陪审团同意这项判决。
They concur on most issues. = 他们在大多数问题上意见一致。


一般过去时:
The scientists concurred on their findings. = 科学家们对他们的发现意见一致。
The meeting concluded after they concurred on a plan. = 在他们就计划达成一致后，会议结束了。


现在完成时:
We have concurred on a solution to the problem. = 我们已经就解决这个问题达成一致。
The board has concurred with the CEO's decision. = 董事会已经同意了首席执行官的决定。


过去完成时:
They had concurred on the strategy before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就策略达成了一致。
Once they had concurred, the project moved forward. = 一旦他们达成一致，项目就开始推进。


将来时:
We will concur on the budget after further review. = 我们将在进一步审查后就预算达成一致。
I believe they will concur with our assessment. = 我相信他们会同意我们的评估。


虚拟语气:
If they concurred, the project would proceed smoothly. = 如果他们同意，项目就会顺利进行。
I wish they would concur with our proposal. = 我希望他们能同意我们的提案。


被动语态:
The proposal was concurred with by the majority. = 大多数人同意了该提案。
The decision was concurred in by all members. = 所有成员都同意了该决定。


疑问句:
Did the judges concur on the sentence? = 法官们对判决意见一致吗？
Does everyone concur with this conclusion? = 每个人都同意这个结论吗？


否定句:
The two experts did not concur on the diagnosis. = 这两位专家在诊断上意见不一。
The committee did not concur with the recommendation. = 委员会不同意这项建议。


PHRASE->
concur with = 同意
concur in = 同意（意见、计划等）
concur on = 就……达成一致
to concur that = 同意……
generally concur = 大致同意
