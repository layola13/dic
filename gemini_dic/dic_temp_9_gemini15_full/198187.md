short-haired:/ʃɔːrtˈheəd/| adj. | 短发;短毛的 | Having short hair.  This adjective describes someone or something with short hair or fur. | 例句：She has a short-haired cat. = 她有一只短毛猫。  The short-haired boy ran quickly. = 那个短发男孩跑得很快。 | 近义词：cropped, bobbed | 反义词：long-haired,  shaggy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has short hair. = 他留着短发。
The dog is short-haired. = 这只狗是短毛的。


一般过去时:
She had short-haired before. = 她以前留着短发。
The cat was short-haired. = 那只猫是短毛的。


现在完成时:
He has always had short hair. = 他一直留着短发。
She has kept her hair short-haired for years. = 她多年来一直保持短发。


过去完成时:
He had had short hair since childhood. = 他从小就留着短发。
The dog had been short-haired its whole life. = 这只狗一生都是短毛的。


将来时:
She will keep her hair short-haired. = 她将保持短发。
He will have short hair next year. = 他明年将留短发。


虚拟语气:
If he were short-haired, he would look better. = 如果他留短发，他会更好看。
I wish my dog was short-haired. = 我希望我的狗是短毛的。


被动语态:
His hair was cut short. = 他的头发被剪短了。(This implies a result of short hair, not a description of the inherent trait)
The cat's fur was kept short-haired. = 猫的毛被修剪得很短。(This implies a maintained state of short hair.)


疑问句:
Is he short-haired? = 他是短发吗？
Is the dog short-haired or long-haired? = 这只狗是短毛的还是长毛的？


否定句:
He is not short-haired; he has long hair. = 他不是短发，他留着长发。
The cat is not short-haired; its fur is quite long. = 这只猫不是短毛的，它的毛很长。
