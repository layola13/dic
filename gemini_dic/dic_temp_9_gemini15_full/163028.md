preclassic: /ˌpriːˈklæsɪk/ | adj. | 前古典的；前经典时期的 |  Referring to the period in Mesoamerican cultures preceding the Classic period, typically characterized by the development of early agriculture, village life, and rudimentary forms of social organization.  It lacks the monumental architecture and sophisticated writing systems of the Classic period. | Example:  The preclassic Olmec civilization laid the groundwork for later Mesoamerican cultures.  前古典时期的奥尔梅克文明为后来的中美洲文化奠定了基础。 | 近义词：prototypical, antecedent, preliminary | 反义词：classic, mature
