cosplaying:/ˈkɒzˌpleɪɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːzˌpleɪɪŋ/ | v. |角色扮演，扮演动漫人物 |The act of dressing up as a character from a fictional work, such as a comic book, video game, or anime, often involving elaborate costumes and makeup.  |例句：She's been cosplaying as Sailor Moon for years. 她多年来一直扮演水手月亮。

例句：Many people enjoy cosplaying at comic conventions.  许多人在动漫展上喜欢角色扮演。


近义词：role-playing, character portrayal, impersonation |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be not engaging in such activities. |词性：动词
