franks:/fræŋks/| n.|法兰克，法郎，香肠|Franks refers to several things:  the ancient Franks, a Germanic people; the French Franc, a former currency; and frankfurters, a type of sausage.  The meaning depends heavily on context.|例句：The Franks were a powerful Germanic tribe.  法兰克人是一个强大的日耳曼部落。

The museum displayed ancient Frankish coins.  博物馆展出了古代法兰克硬币。

I bought some delicious franks for the barbecue. 我买了些美味的香肠来烧烤。
