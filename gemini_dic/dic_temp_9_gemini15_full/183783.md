rescued:/ˈreskjuːd/| v.|营救；援救；拯救|To save someone or something from a dangerous or harmful situation.|例句：The firefighters rescued the cat from the burning building. 消防员从燃烧的建筑物中救出了猫。|例句：The stranded hikers were rescued by a helicopter. 被困的徒步旅行者被直升机救了。|近义词：saved, liberated, delivered|反义词：abandoned, deserted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lifeguard rescues swimmers every summer. = 救生员每年夏天都救助游泳者。
She rescues stray animals and finds them homes. = 她救助流浪动物并为它们找到家。

一般过去时:
He rescued a kitten from a tree. = 他从树上救下一只小猫。
They rescued the passengers from the sinking ship. = 他们从沉船中救出了乘客。

现在完成时:
The charity has rescued hundreds of dogs from puppy mills. = 该慈善机构已从幼犬养殖场救出了数百只狗。
She has rescued several people from burning buildings. = 她已经从燃烧的建筑物中救出了几个人。

过去完成时:
The police had rescued the hostages before the terrorists could harm them. = 在恐怖分子伤害人质之前，警方已经解救了他们。
The firefighters had rescued everyone from the building before it collapsed. = 大楼倒塌之前，消防员已经将所有人救了出来。

将来时:
The coast guard will rescue the sailors stranded at sea. = 海岸警卫队将营救被困在海上的水手。
We will rescue the project from failure. = 我们将拯救这个即将失败的项目。

虚拟语气:
If only I had been there, I could have rescued him. = 要是我当时在那儿就好了，我就能救他了。
If the police hadn't arrived in time, we wouldn't have been rescued. = 如果警察没有及时赶到，我们就不会获救了。

被动语态:
The child was rescued from the well. = 孩子从井里被救了出来。
Many people were rescued after the earthquake. = 地震后许多人获救了。

疑问句:
Were the miners rescued? = 矿工们获救了吗？
Will the lost dog be rescued? = 迷路的狗会被救回来吗？

否定句:
They couldn't rescue the trapped miners. = 他们无法营救被困的矿工。
The firefighters were unable to rescue everyone from the fire. = 消防员未能将所有人从火中救出。
