reglazed:/ˌriːˈɡleɪzd/| v. |重新上釉；重新施釉 |To apply a new glaze to (something, especially pottery or tiles).  This typically involves cleaning the existing surface and then applying a fresh layer of glaze. |例句：The cracked tiles were reglazed to restore their shine.  重新上釉的裂开的瓷砖恢复了光泽。

The antique vase was reglazed by a skilled artisan.  一个熟练的工匠为古董花瓶重新上了釉。


近义词：re-enameled, refinished


反义词：unglazed


词性：动词
