prerevision:/ˌpriːrɪˈvɪʒən/| n. |预修订；预先修改|The act of revising something before it is finalized or published.  It involves making changes and improvements to a draft or plan before the final version is created.  This process often involves checking for errors, inconsistencies, and areas for improvement. |例句：The prerevision of the manuscript took several weeks.  = 手稿的预修订花了几个星期。

The prerevision stage involved extensive feedback from colleagues. = 预修订阶段包括来自同事的大量反馈。|近义词：preliminary revision, pre-edit, draft review |反义词：final revision, post-publication edit |名词
