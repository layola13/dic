exercises:/ˈɛksərˌsaɪzɪz/| n.|练习，习题|Exercises are activities designed to improve skills or fitness.|例句：She does daily exercises to stay fit. 她每天锻炼以保持健康。|近义词：drills, practices, workouts|反义词：rest, idleness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We do exercises every day. = 我们每天做练习。
He exercises regularly. = 他定期锻炼。

一般过去时:
I did my exercises last night. = 我昨晚做了练习。
They exercised for an hour. = 他们锻炼了一个小时。

现在完成时:
She has finished her exercises. = 她已经完成了她的练习。
They have exercised regularly this year. = 他们今年定期锻炼。

过去完成时:
I had completed all my exercises before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前完成了所有的练习。
They had exercised strenuously before the competition. = 比赛前他们进行了剧烈的锻炼。

将来时:
We will do more exercises tomorrow. = 我们明天会做更多练习。
He will exercise in the park later. = 他稍后会在公园锻炼。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would do more exercises. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做更多练习。
I wish I had exercised more regularly. = 我希望我更经常地锻炼。

被动语态:
The exercises are marked by the teacher. = 练习由老师批改。
These exercises are designed to improve vocabulary. = 这些练习旨在提高词汇量。

疑问句:
Do you do daily exercises? = 你每天做练习吗？
What kind of exercises do you like? = 你喜欢什么类型的练习？

否定句:
I don't do many exercises. = 我不做很多练习。
He didn't exercise yesterday. = 他昨天没有锻炼。
