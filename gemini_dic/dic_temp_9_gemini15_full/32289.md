chiefs:/tʃiːfs/| n.|酋长，首领；领导；(一群人的)头头 |The word "chiefs" refers to the leaders of a tribe, group, or organization. It can also refer to the most important or skilled people in a particular field.  |例句：The tribal chiefs met to discuss the peace treaty. 部落酋长们会面讨论和平条约。

The police chiefs from several cities met to coordinate their strategies.  几个城市的警察局长们会面协调策略。|近义词：leaders, heads, bosses |反义词：followers, subordinates
