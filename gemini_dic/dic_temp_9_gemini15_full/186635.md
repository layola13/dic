riflemen:/ˈraɪflmən/ | n. |步枪手，步枪兵 | A soldier who is trained to use a rifle.  | Example: The riflemen advanced cautiously through the jungle. 步枪兵小心翼翼地穿过丛林。  The experienced riflemen provided accurate fire support. 经验丰富的步枪兵提供了精确的火力支援。|近义词：rifleman, marksman, sharpshooter |反义词：None specifically.  |名词
