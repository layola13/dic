词源:
The word "overlaxly" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") with the adverb "laxly" (meaning "in a loose or careless manner").  "Laxly" itself derives from the adjective "lax," ultimately from Latin "laxus" meaning "loose" or "slack."
