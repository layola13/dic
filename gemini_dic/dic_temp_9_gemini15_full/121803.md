misreferring:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrɪŋ/| v. |误指，错指，引用错误 |To refer to something incorrectly or inappropriately. This involves a mistake in identifying or associating something with a particular thing, person, or idea.  |例句：He was misreferring to the data in his presentation, leading to a misunderstanding. 他在演示文稿中错误地引用了数据，导致了误解。

例句：The article misreferred to the scientist's findings, misrepresenting his actual conclusions. 这篇文章错误地引用了这位科学家的研究成果，歪曲了他的实际结论。


近义词：misinterpret, misquote, misattribute, mistake, confuse

反义词：refer correctly, cite accurately
