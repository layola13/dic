presifted:/ˈpriːsɪftɪd/| adj.|预先筛过的，预先筛选的|Already sifted before use.  This term usually refers to flour that has been sifted before being sold or used in a recipe.  |例句：The recipe calls for two cups of presifted flour.  这个食谱需要两杯预先筛过的面粉。


例句：Using presifted flour can make baking easier and more consistent. 使用预先筛过的面粉可以使烘焙更容易和更一致。


近义词：sieved, screened, strained|反义词：unsifted
