hydrozoa:/haɪˈdroʊzoʊə/| n. |水螅纲动物|A class of mostly marine invertebrates in the phylum Cnidaria, characterized by a polyp form and often a medusa form in their life cycle.  They are often colonial and include hydroids, fire corals, and siphonophores.|Example:  Many species of hydrozoa exhibit both polyp and medusa stages in their life cycle. 许多水螅纲动物在其生命周期中都表现出息肉和水母两个阶段。|近义词：cnidarian (更广泛的分类)|反义词：无|
