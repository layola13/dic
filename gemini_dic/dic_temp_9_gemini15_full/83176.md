gutturalize:/ˈɡʌtərəlaɪz/| vt.|使成喉音的；用喉音说|To pronounce or utter with a guttural sound; to give a guttural quality to (speech or a sound).|例句：He gutturalized his words, making them sound harsh and throaty. 他用喉音说出他的话，使它们听起来粗糙而沙哑。He tried to gutturalize his pronunciation to sound more authentic. 他试图用喉音发音，以听起来更真实。|近义词：vocalize, pronounce, articulate|反义词：whisper, murmur

词源:
From guttural + -ize.  "Guttural" comes from the Latin *gutturalis, from guttur "throat".  The suffix "-ize" is of Greek origin, indicating "to make" or "to cause to be".  Therefore, "gutturalize" means "to make guttural" or "to cause to be uttered with a guttural sound".
