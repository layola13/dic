merinos:/mɛˈriːnoʊz/| n. |美利奴羊毛；美利奴羊|Merinos refers to a breed of sheep known for its fine, soft wool, or the wool itself.  The wool is highly prized for its softness, warmth, and drape.  It is often used in high-quality clothing.|例句：The sweater is made of 100% merino wool. 这件毛衣是100%美利奴羊毛制成的。

例句：She bought a luxurious merino wool blanket. 她买了一条豪华的美利奴羊毛毯。


近义词：fine wool,  sheep's wool|反义词：coarse wool,  rough wool|名词
