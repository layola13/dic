effeminates:/ɪˈfɛmɪneɪts/| v.|使女性化；使柔弱|To make or become womanly or unmanly in appearance or behavior.  |例句：He effeminates his behavior to fit in with the group. 他为了融入群体而使自己的行为女性化。

例句：The way he dresses effeminates his strong physique. 他穿衣打扮的方式弱化了他强壮的体格。


近义词：feminize, unman


反义词：masculinize, embolden


词性：动词
