foresaw:/fɔːrˈsɔː/| /fɔːrˈsɔː/| vt.|预见；预料|To have knowledge of (something) before it happens.|The old woman foresaw the coming storm and took precautions. 老妇人预见到了即将到来的暴风雨并采取了预防措施。|She foresaw the difficulties of the project. 她预见到了这个项目的困难。|predict, anticipate, foresee|fail to foresee, overlook|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foresees potential problems. = 他预见到潜在的问题。
I foresee a bright future for the company. = 我预见公司拥有光明的未来。


一般过去时:
They foresaw the market crash. = 他们预见了市场崩盘。
We foresaw the need for more staff. = 我们预见了对更多员工的需求。


现在完成时:
I have foreseen this situation many times before. = 我以前多次预见到这种情况。
She has foreseen the consequences of her actions. = 她已经预见到了自己行为的后果。


过去完成时:
They had foreseen the risks involved. = 他们已经预见到了所涉及的风险。
We had foreseen the possibility of a delay. = 我们已经预见了延误的可能性。


将来时:
They will foresee the challenges ahead. = 他们将预见到未来的挑战。
I will foresee any potential issues before they arise. = 我会在问题出现之前预见到任何潜在的问题。


虚拟语气:
If I had foreseen the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预见到了后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had foreseen the storm. = 我真希望我预见到了暴风雨。


被动语态:
The difficulties were foreseen by the project manager. = 项目经理预见到了这些困难。
The potential risks were foreseen by the experts. = 专家们预见到了潜在的风险。


疑问句:
Did you foresee any problems? = 你预见到任何问题了吗？
Could they have foreseen the outcome? = 他们能预见到结果吗？


否定句:
They did not foresee the difficulties. = 他们没有预见到困难。
I did not foresee the negative impact. = 我没有预见到负面影响。


PHRASE->

foresee trouble 预见麻烦
