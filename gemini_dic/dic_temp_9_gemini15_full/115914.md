marooned:/məˈruːnd/| vt. adj.|使…孤立；被遗弃的； | to leave (someone) alone and isolated, especially in a place that is difficult or impossible to leave.  |例句：The sailors were marooned on a desert island after their ship sank. 水手们在船沉没后被困在一个荒岛上。The stranded hikers felt marooned in the wilderness.  被困的徒步旅行者们感到被困在荒野中，孤立无援。|近义词：stranded, isolated, abandoned, deserted |反义词：rescued, saved, assisted


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tide maroons the boats on the sand. = 潮水将船只搁浅在沙滩上。
He often feels marooned in his big house. = 他经常在他那大房子里感到孤独。


一般过去时:
The storm marooned the climbers on the mountain. = 暴风雨使登山者被困在山上。
They were marooned by the unexpected flood. = 他们被突如其来的洪水困住了。


现在完成时:
The ship has been marooned for weeks. = 这艘船已经被困数周了。
We have been marooned here since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，我们一直被困在这里。


过去完成时:
They had been marooned for days before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，他们已经被困了几天了。
The explorers had been marooned in the jungle for months. = 探险家们在丛林中被困了几个月。


将来时:
The rising tide will maroon the small boats. = 上涨的潮水将使小船搁浅。
If the storm continues, we will be marooned. = 如果暴风雨持续，我们将被困住。


虚拟语气:
If the plane had crashed, they would have been marooned. = 如果飞机坠毁，他们就会被困住了。
I wish we hadn't been marooned here. = 我真希望我们没有被困在这里。


被动语态:
The sailors were marooned by the storm. = 水手们被暴风雨困住了。
The passengers were marooned on the island. = 乘客们被困在了岛上。


疑问句:
Were the passengers marooned? = 乘客们被困住了吗？
Have they been marooned by the flood? = 他们被洪水困住了吗？


否定句:
The lifeboat prevented them from being marooned. = 救生艇阻止了他们被困住。
They were not marooned; they chose to stay. = 他们没有被困住；他们选择留下。
