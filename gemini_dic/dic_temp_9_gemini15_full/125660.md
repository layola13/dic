mumper:/ˈmʌmpər/| n. |乞丐，流浪汉|A mumper is a beggar, especially one who is habitually begging.  The word often implies a persistent and perhaps somewhat sly or dishonest beggar.|例句：The mumper sat on the corner, holding out his hat for coins.  这个乞丐坐在街角，举着帽子要钱。

The old mumper was known for his elaborate stories to garner sympathy.  这个老乞丐以他编造的精彩故事来博取同情而闻名。|近义词： beggar, tramp, vagrant|反义词：millionaire, philanthropist
