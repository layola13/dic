detectives:/dɪˈtɛktɪvz/| n. |侦探们，侦探（复数）|The plural form of "detective," referring to more than one person who investigates crimes.  Detectives are skilled in gathering evidence, interviewing witnesses, and solving mysteries. |例句：The detectives interviewed several witnesses. 侦探们采访了几位证人。

例句：The detectives are working tirelessly to solve the case. 侦探们正夜以继日地努力破案。

近义词：investigators, investigators, sleuths
反义词：无
