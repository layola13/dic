schleppers:/ˈʃlɛpərz/| n. |搬运工；苦力；（尤指）大包小包的人|A schlep is a long, tiring journey, often involving carrying heavy things.  The word "schleppers" refers to people who do this kind of work, especially those who carry many things at once. It often carries a connotation of being overworked or burdened.|例句：The construction workers were true schleppers, carrying heavy beams all day. 这些建筑工人真是苦力，整天搬运沉重的梁木。
The movers were real schleppers, lugging furniture up three flights of stairs.  搬家公司的人真是大包小包，把家具搬上三层楼。|近义词：carriers, porters, haulers, luggers |反义词：idler, loafer
