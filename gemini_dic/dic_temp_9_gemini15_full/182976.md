replated:/riːˈpleɪtɪd/| adj.| 再镀的；重新镀层的 |  Describes something that has been plated again, usually a metal surface that has undergone a second plating process. This typically improves the appearance, durability, or other properties of the original plating. |例句：The replated silverware gleamed brilliantly.  再镀的银器闪闪发光。

The replated engine parts were as good as new.  重新镀层的引擎部件如同新的一样。


近义词：re-coated, refinished

反义词：unplated, bare


词性：形容词
