mixtures:/ˈmɪkstʃərz/| n. |混合物，混合体|A mixture is a substance comprising two or more components not chemically bonded.  A mixture is a physical combination of multiple substances whose identities are retained when mixed in solutions, suspensions and colloids.|例句：The painter mixed various pigments to create a unique mixture of colors.  画家混合了各种颜料，创造出独特的颜色混合物。


The chef created a delicious mixture of spices for the curry. 厨师为咖喱创造了一种美味的香料混合物。|近义词：blend, combination, composite|反义词：pure substance, element
