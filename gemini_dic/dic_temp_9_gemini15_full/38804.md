conducts:/ˈkɒndʌkts/ /ˈkɑːndʌkts/| vt. n.|引导；进行；传导；指挥|To lead, guide, or escort someone or something. To organize and carry out. To transmit (heat, electricity, etc.). To behave in a specified way.  |例句：The orchestra conductor conducts the symphony.  乐队指挥指挥交响乐。The experiment was conducted under strict conditions. 实验在严格的条件下进行。Copper conducts electricity well. 铜导电性良好。He conducts himself with great dignity. 他举止非常端庄。|近义词：manage, direct, lead, carry out, transmit|反义词：insulates, obstructs, hinders|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist conducts experiments daily. = 科学家每天都进行实验。
The teacher conducts the class with enthusiasm. = 老师充满热情地进行课堂教学。
