loathes:/loʊðz/| vt.|厌恶；憎恨；极不喜欢|To feel intense dislike or disgust for (something).|He loathes violence and injustice. 他厌恶暴力和不公正。|近义词：abhor, detest, despise, hate|反义词：adore, love, cherish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loathes public speaking. = 他讨厌公开演讲。
She loathes the smell of fish. = 她讨厌鱼腥味。

一般过去时:
I loathed my job. = 我讨厌我的工作。
He loathed the way she treated him. = 他讨厌她对待他的方式。

现在完成时:
I have loathed spinach my whole life. = 我一辈子都讨厌菠菜。
She has loathed him since their argument. = 从他们争吵以来，她就一直讨厌他。

过去完成时:
I had loathed the idea from the start. = 我从一开始就讨厌这个主意。
She had loathed him long before they met. = 在他们相遇之前很久，她就讨厌他了。

将来时:
I will loathe this assignment until it’s finished. = 在完成这个任务之前，我都会讨厌它。
He will loathe her even more if she does that. = 如果她那样做，他会更讨厌她。

虚拟语气:
I would loathe to have to do that again. = 我不愿意不得不再次那样做。
If I were him, I would loathe the situation. = 如果我是他，我会讨厌这种情况。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His actions were loathed by many. = 他的行为被许多人所厌恶。
The proposal was loathed by the committee. = 委员会讨厌这个提案。

疑问句:
Do you loathe that kind of music? = 你讨厌那种音乐吗？
Does she loathe cats? = 她讨厌猫吗？


否定句:
I don't loathe him, I just dislike him. = 我并不讨厌他，我只是不喜欢他。
She doesn't loathe all vegetables, just broccoli. = 她并非讨厌所有蔬菜，只是讨厌西兰花。


PHRASE->
loathes the thought of = 想到…就厌恶
loathes the very idea of = 想到…就极其厌恶
loathes the sight of = 看到…就厌恶
loathes being = 讨厌成为…
loathes to admit = 不愿承认
