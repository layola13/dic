pneumonitic:/ˌnjuːməˈnɪtɪk/| adj.|肺炎的；肺部的炎症|Relating to or characteristic of pneumonia.  The term often describes conditions or symptoms resembling pneumonia, even if not caused by a typical pneumonia-inducing pathogen.|例句：The patient presented with pneumonitic symptoms, including cough and shortness of breath.  这位病人出现了肺炎症状，包括咳嗽和呼吸短促。

The X-ray showed pneumonitic infiltrates in both lungs. X光显示两肺均有肺炎浸润。|近义词：pulmonary, inflammatory|反义词：healthy, non-inflammatory
