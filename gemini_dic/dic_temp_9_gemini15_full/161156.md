postfrontal: /ˌpoʊstˈfrʌntəl/ | adj. |额叶后的，额后部的| Relating to the area of the brain behind the frontal lobe.  This area is involved in various cognitive functions, including attention, working memory, and executive functions.  |例句：Studies have shown a correlation between damage to the postfrontal cortex and deficits in executive function.  研究表明，额叶后皮质受损与执行功能缺陷之间存在相关性。|近义词：retrofrontal (less common)|反义词：prefrontal|无
