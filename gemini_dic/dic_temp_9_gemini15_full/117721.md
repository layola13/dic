melancholize:/ˈmɛləŋkəlaɪz/| vt.|使忧郁；使悲伤|To make or become melancholic; to cause sadness or depression.|例句：The dreary weather melancholized her.  阴沉的天气使她忧郁。  The news melancholized the entire family. 这个消息使全家人都悲伤。|近义词：sadden, depress, dispirit|反义词：cheer, exhilarate, gladden|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The grey skies melancholize many people. = 灰色的天空使许多人忧郁。
Rainy days melancholize her. = 雨天使她忧郁。


一般过去时:
The loss of her pet melancholized her deeply. = 失去宠物让她非常悲伤。
The bad news melancholized him for weeks. = 那个坏消息让他悲伤了好几周。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has melancholized the whole town. = 持续的雨水使整个城镇都变得忧郁。
This ongoing situation has melancholized me. = 持续的现状让我感到忧郁。


过去完成时:
He had been melancholized by the news before he even heard it fully. = 在他完全听到消息之前，他已经为此感到悲伤了。
She had melancholized for days before finally breaking down. = 在她最终崩溃之前，她已经悲伤了好几天了。


将来时:
The upcoming winter will melancholize many people. = 即将来临的冬天将会使许多人感到忧郁。
That event will melancholize him greatly. = 那件事将会让他非常悲伤。


虚拟语气:
If only the sun would shine, it wouldn't melancholize me so. = 如果阳光能够照耀，它就不会让我如此忧郁。
If she had not lost her job, she would not have melancholized. = 如果她没有失业，她就不会感到忧郁。


被动语态:
He was melancholized by the tragic story. = 他被这个悲剧故事弄得忧郁了。
Many are melancholized by the current state of affairs. = 许多人被目前的状况弄得忧郁了。


疑问句:
Does the music melancholize you? = 这音乐让你感到忧郁吗？
Does this situation melancholize you? = 这种情况让你感到忧郁吗？


否定句:
The bright sunshine did not melancholize her. = 明媚的阳光并没有让她感到忧郁。
The happy occasion did not melancholize anyone. = 快乐的场合并没有让任何人感到忧郁。
