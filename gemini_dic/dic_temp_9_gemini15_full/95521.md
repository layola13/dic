included:/ɪnˈkluːdɪd/| adj.|包括的，包含的|Something that is included is part of a larger group or set.  It is already present or contained within something else. |The price includes breakfast. 价格包含早餐。
The tour included a visit to the museum. 旅行包括参观博物馆。|近义词：contained, comprised, encompassed|反义词：excluded, omitted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The package included a free gift.  包裹里包含一份免费礼物。
The price included tax. 价格包含税费。


一般过去时:
The invitation included a map. 邀请函包含一张地图。
The report included all the necessary data. 报告包含所有必要的数据。


现在完成时:
The total cost has included all expenses.  总成本已包含所有费用。
Everything has been included in the budget. 一切都已包含在预算中。


过去完成时:
The final draft had included all the suggested changes.  最终稿已包含所有建议的更改。
She had included a personal note in the package. 她已在包裹中附上了一张私人便条。


将来时:
The next update will include new features. 下次更新将包含新功能。
The package will include a tracking number. 包裹将包含一个跟踪号码。


虚拟语气:
If the price included shipping, I would order it. 如果价格包含运费，我会订购它。
I wish the meeting had included a Q&A session. 我希望会议包含一个问答环节。


被动语态:
Breakfast is included in the room rate. 早餐包含在房费中。
A detailed explanation was included in the manual. 手册中包含了详细的解释。


疑问句:
Is tax included in the price? 价格包含税费吗？

否定句:
