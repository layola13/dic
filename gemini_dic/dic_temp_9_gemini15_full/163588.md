It implies a preceding event that anticipates or foreshadows a later event.  The word often carries a sense of being an indication or precursor.|The precurrent symptoms of the disease included fatigue and muscle aches.|近义词：preceding, antecedent, prior, preliminary|反义词：subsequent, following, succeeding
