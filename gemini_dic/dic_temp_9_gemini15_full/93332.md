ill-treats:/ɪlˈtriːts/ | v. |虐待；粗暴对待 | To treat someone cruelly or badly.  |例句：He ill-treats his animals. 他虐待他的动物。|例句：The prisoners were ill-treated by their captors. 囚犯受到了俘虏的虐待。|近义词：abuse, mistreat, maltreat, persecute |反义词：cherish, nurture, care for
