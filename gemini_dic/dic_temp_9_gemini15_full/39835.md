conspirer:/kənˈspɪərər/| n.|阴谋者，共谋者|A conspirer is a person who takes part in a conspiracy.  They secretly plan with others to do something illegal or harmful. |例句：The conspirers were finally caught and brought to justice. 阴谋者最终被抓获并绳之以法。
例句：Several conspirers were implicated in the assassination attempt.  好几名共谋者牵涉到暗杀未遂事件中。|近义词：plotter, conspirator, accomplice|反义词：innocent, bystander


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He conspires against his rivals. 他密谋反对他的竞争对手。
They conspire to overthrow the government. 他们密谋推翻政府。

一般过去时:
The nobles conspired against the king.  贵族们密谋反对国王。
She conspired to steal the company's secrets. 她密谋窃取公司机密。

现在完成时:
They have conspired to defraud the investors. 他们已经密谋欺诈投资者。
He has conspired with others to commit this crime. 他已经与其他人密谋犯下此罪。

过去完成时:
They had conspired for months before the event. 他们在事件发生前已经密谋了几个月。
The police discovered that they had conspired to hide evidence. 警方发现他们已经密谋隐藏证据。

将来时:
They will conspire to ruin his reputation. 他们将会密谋毁坏他的名声。
He will conspire with his friends to cheat on the exam. 他将与朋友们密谋考试作弊。

虚拟语气:
If they had not conspired, the plan would have failed. 如果他们没有密谋，计划就会失败。
I wish they hadn't conspired to harm me. 我真希望他们没有密谋伤害我。

被动语态:
The plot was discovered because the conspirers were betrayed.  阴谋被发现是因为阴谋者被出卖了。
The evidence suggests that he was conspired against. 证据表明他受到了阴谋陷害。

疑问句:
Who conspired against him? 谁密谋反对他？
Did they conspire to commit murder? 他们是否密谋犯下谋杀罪？

否定句:

They denied that they conspired to overthrow the government. 他们否认他们密谋推翻政府。
