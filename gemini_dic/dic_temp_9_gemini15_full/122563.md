moderation:/ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃən/| n. |节制；温和；缓和；中庸之道 |The practice of avoiding excesses or extremes, especially in one's behavior or the pursuit of pleasures. It involves self-control and balance.  |例句：Moderation in all things is key to a healthy lifestyle.  节制是健康生活方式的关键。  |例句：He showed great moderation in his response to the provocation. 他对挑衅的回应表现出极大的克制。|近义词：temperance, restraint, self-control |反义词：excess, extravagance, immoderation
