hunan:/huːˈnæn/| n. |湖南省|Hunan is a province in central-south China, known for its spicy cuisine and rich culture.  It is bordered by several other provinces, including Hubei, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, and Jiangxi.|例句：I'm planning a trip to Hunan to experience its famous spicy food. 我计划去湖南体验它著名的辣味食物。|近义词：province (省份)|反义词：无|名词
