literate:/ˈlɪtərət/| adj.|有文化的；识字的；精通文学的|Able to read and write; possessing knowledge or skill in a particular area, especially literature.|例句：He is a literate man who enjoys reading classic novels. 他是一位喜欢阅读经典小说有文化的人。|例句：The program aims to improve literacy rates in rural areas. 该项目旨在提高农村地区的识字率。|近义词：educated, learned, cultured, well-read|反义词：illiterate, uneducated, unlettered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is literate in both English and French. = 他精通英语和法语。
She is literate and can read complex texts. = 她有文化并且能阅读复杂的文本。


一般过去时:
He was literate by the age of five. = 他五岁时就识字了。
They were literate enough to understand the instructions. = 他们识字程度足够理解这些说明。


现在完成时:
She has become literate in her later years. = 她晚年才识字。
He has remained literate throughout his life. = 他一生都保持着文化素养。


过去完成时:
He had been literate for many years before he started writing novels. = 在他开始写小说之前，他已经识字很多年了。
They had become literate before the war started. = 战争开始之前，他们已经识字了。


将来时:
He will be literate by the end of the year. = 到年底他就能识字了。
The program hopes that all children will be literate by the time they leave primary school. = 该项目希望所有孩子在小学毕业时都能识字。


虚拟语气:
If I were literate, I could read this book. = 如果我识字，我就能读这本书了。
I wish I were literate so I could understand this document. = 我希望我识字，这样我就能理解这份文件了。


被动语态:
Many people are being made literate through adult education programs. = 通过成人教育项目，许多人正在被扫盲。
Literacy is being promoted through various initiatives. = 通过各种举措正在推广识字。


疑问句:
Is he literate? = 他识字吗？
Are all the students literate in their native language? = 所有学生都能读写母语吗？


否定句:
He is not literate. = 他不识字。
Many children in that region are not literate. = 在那个地区，许多孩子不识字。
