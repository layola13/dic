delicatessens:/ˌdɛlɪkəˈtɛsns/ | n. |熟食店；美味佳肴 |A delicatessen is a shop that sells prepared foods such as cheeses, salads, and cooked meats.  It often also sells imported or specialty foods. |例句：I bought some delicious cheeses at the delicatessen. 我在熟食店买了一些美味的奶酪。  The delicatessen offered a wide array of gourmet sandwiches. 这家熟食店提供各种各样的美食三明治。|近义词：gourmet shop, specialty food store |反义词：grocery store (in the sense of lacking specialty items) |名词
