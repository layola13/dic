pseudo-australian: /ˌsjuːdoʊɔːˈstreɪliən/ | adj. | 假澳大利亚的；冒充澳大利亚人的 |  Appearing or claiming to be Australian, but not genuinely so; often used to describe something that imitates or parodies Australian culture, style, or characteristics.  It can imply a superficial or inauthentic connection to Australia. | 例句：The movie's attempt at an "Aussie" accent was rather pseudo-australian. 这部电影试图模仿澳大利亚口音，但却显得非常做作。 | 近义词：faux-Australian, mock-Australian, affectedly Australian | 反义词：authentically Australian, genuinely Australian |  词性:形容词
