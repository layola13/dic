quits:/kwɪts/| v.|停止，放弃，辞职|To stop doing something; to leave a job.|例句：He quits smoking. 他戒烟了。|例句：She quits her job. 她辞职了。|近义词：stop, cease, resign|反义词：continue, persist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quits his job every year. 他每年都辞职。
She quits smoking after many attempts. 她经过多次尝试后戒烟了。

一般过去时:
He quit his job last week. 他上周辞职了。
She quit smoking cold turkey. 她突然戒烟了。

现在完成时:
He has quit smoking for five years. 他戒烟五年了。
She has quit her job and started her own business. 她辞职了，自己创业了。

过去完成时:
He had quit his job before he went traveling. 他旅行前已经辞职了。
She had quit smoking before she became pregnant. 她怀孕前已经戒烟了。

将来时:
He will quit his job next month. 他下个月将辞职。
She will quit smoking when she is ready. 她准备好后就会戒烟。

虚拟语气:
If he quit his job, he would have more time for his family. 如果他辞职了，他就会有更多时间陪伴家人。
I wish I had quit smoking years ago. 我真希望几年前就戒烟了。

被动语态:  (Quits is rarely used passively)  This section is inapplicable.
The decision to quit was made by the team.  (This uses a related noun) 团队做出了辞职的决定。
His job was terminated.  (This uses a related concept) 他的工作被解雇了。


疑问句:
Will he quit his job? 他会辞职吗？
Did she quit smoking? 她戒烟了吗？

否定句:
He didn't quit his job. 他没有辞职。
She didn't quit smoking easily. 她没有轻易戒烟。


PHRASE->
quit smoking:戒烟
quit drinking:戒酒
quit a job:辞职
throw in the towel/quit:放弃
quit cold turkey:突然戒掉
