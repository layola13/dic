linked:/lɪŋkt/| adj.|连接的，相关的，联系的|connected or associated; forming a chain or series.|The linked articles provided a comprehensive overview of the topic.  这些相关的文章提供了该主题的全面概述。|The two cities are linked by a new highway. 这两座城市由一条新的高速公路连接。|connected, related, associated|disconnected, unrelated, separate|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The events are linked together. = 这些事件相互关联。
The two concepts are linked in my mind. = 这两个概念在我的脑海中联系在一起。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
The websites have been linked for years. = 这些网站已经连接多年了。
Many studies have linked smoking to cancer. = 许多研究将吸烟与癌症联系起来。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
