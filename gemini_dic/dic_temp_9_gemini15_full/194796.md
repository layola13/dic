sem:/sɛm/| n.|种子；精子；含义；意义；|The basic unit of meaning in a language.|例句：The semantic meaning of the word is different from its pronunciation.这个词的语义含义与其发音不同。|近义词：seed, germ, signification, meaning|反义词：null, void

This word has multiple meanings. = 这个词有多个意思。
