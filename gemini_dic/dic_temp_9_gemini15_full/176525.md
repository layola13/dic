re-regulate:/ˌriːˈreɡjuleɪt/| vt.|重新规范，重新调整|To regulate something again or differently; to impose new regulations on something.|The government decided to re-regulate the telecommunications industry to promote competition.|近义词：reorganize, readjust, revise|反义词：deregulate, liberalize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-regulate the market every year. = 他们每年都会重新规范市场。
The company re-regulates its production processes regularly. = 公司定期重新调整其生产流程。


一般过去时:
The government re-regulated the banking sector after the crisis. = 危机过后，政府重新规范了银行业。
They re-regulated the traffic flow to ease congestion. = 他们重新调整了交通流量以缓解拥堵。


现在完成时:
The authority has re-regulated the emission standards. = 当局已经重新规范了排放标准。
The school has re-regulated its disciplinary procedures. = 学校已经重新调整了其纪律程序。


过去完成时:
They had re-regulated the industry before the new law was passed. = 新法通过之前，他们已经重新规范了该行业。
The company had re-regulated its internal policies before the merger. = 合并之前，该公司已经重新调整了其内部政策。


将来时:
They will re-regulate the energy market next year. = 他们明年将重新规范能源市场。
The city will re-regulate parking to improve traffic flow. = 城市将重新规范停车以改善交通流量。


虚拟语气:
If the government re-regulated the industry, it might be more competitive. = 如果政府重新规范该行业，它可能会更有竞争力。
I wish they had re-regulated the system sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新调整这个系统。


被动语态:
The industry was re-regulated by the government last year. = 该行业去年被政府重新规范了。
The traffic flow is being re-regulated to reduce accidents. = 交通流量正在被重新调整以减少事故。


疑问句:
Will the government re-regulate the healthcare system? = 政府会重新规范医疗系统吗？
Has the company re-regulated its pricing strategy? = 公司是否重新调整了其定价策略？


否定句:
The government did not re-regulate the market this year. = 政府今年没有重新规范市场。
The company has not yet re-regulated its safety procedures. = 公司尚未重新调整其安全程序。
