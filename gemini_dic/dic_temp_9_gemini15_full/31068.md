chamois:/ʃæˈmiː/| n. |沙皮革；羚羊；擦皮|A chamois is a type of soft leather traditionally made from the skin of a chamois, a type of antelope. It's also used to refer to the antelope itself, and now often refers to synthetic cleaning cloths made to mimic the texture of genuine chamois leather.|例句：He polished the car with a chamois. 他用擦皮擦拭汽车。|例句：The mountain chamois are agile climbers.  山羚羊是敏捷的攀登者。|近义词： buckskin, suede, shammy (for the leather)|反义词：无|
