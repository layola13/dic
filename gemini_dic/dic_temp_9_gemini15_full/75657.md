fukushima:/ˌfuːˈʃɪmɑː/| n. |福岛；福岛核事故|The word "Fukushima" refers to Fukushima Prefecture, a prefecture in Japan, and is often used in reference to the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear disaster.|例句：The Fukushima disaster had a profound impact on the world. 福岛灾难对世界产生了深远的影响。|近义词：Japan (in the context of the nuclear disaster)|反义词：无|名词
