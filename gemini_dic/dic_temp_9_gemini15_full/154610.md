persicaria:/ˌpɜːrsɪˈkeəriə/| n. |蓼属植物|A genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae, commonly known as knotweeds or smartweeds.  They are typically herbaceous plants with small flowers arranged in spikes or racemes. Many species are widespread weeds, while others are cultivated as ornamentals. |Example:  Many species of Persicaria are found in damp environments.  许多蓼属植物生长在潮湿的环境中。|近义词：knotweed, smartweed|反义词：无
