convocational:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃənəl/| adj. |召集的；会议的；集会的| Relating to or of the nature of a convocation; assembled or called together.  It often refers to a formal assembly or meeting, particularly in an academic or religious context. |例句：The convocational address was delivered by the university chancellor. 大学校长发表了开学典礼致辞。

The convocational ceremony marked the beginning of the academic year.  开学典礼标志着学年的开始。


近义词：assembly, congregational, convening


反义词：dispersed, scattered, individual


词性:形容词
