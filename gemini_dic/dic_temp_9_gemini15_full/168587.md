processioning: /prəˈsɛʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 列队行进；游行；队伍的组织 | The act of forming or moving in a procession; the process of organizing or conducting a procession.  |例句：The processioning of the graduating class was a proud moment for the school.  毕业班的游行是学校引以为豪的时刻。

例句：The meticulous processioning of the royal family through the streets captivated the crowd. 皇家家族在街道上井然有序的游行吸引了人群。


近义词：parading, marching, moving in a line, conducting, organizing


反义词：dispersing, scattering, breaking up (referring to a procession)
