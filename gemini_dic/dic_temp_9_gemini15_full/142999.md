outbeams:/ˌaʊtˈbiːmz/| v. |胜过；超过；比…更明亮|To surpass or exceed in brightness or brilliance.  It often implies a significant difference in intensity.|例句：The sun outbeams the moon in brightness. 太阳比月亮亮得多。

The lighthouse outbeams all other lights on the coast.  灯塔的光芒超过海岸上所有其他的灯光。


近义词：outshines, surpasses, exceeds, overshadows|反义词：is outshone by, is surpassed by, is eclipsed by|词性:动词
