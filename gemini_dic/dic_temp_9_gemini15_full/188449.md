rudeness:/ˈruːdnəs/| n.|粗鲁，无礼|The quality or state of being rude; lack of good manners or consideration for others.  It often involves a deliberate lack of respect or disregard for others' feelings.|例句：His rudeness shocked everyone in the room.  他的粗鲁震惊了房间里的每个人。|例句：The rudeness of the waiter ruined our dinner. 服务员的粗鲁态度毁了我们的晚餐。|近义词：discourtesy, impoliteness, incivility, impertinence,insolence|反义词：politeness, courtesy, consideration, respect
