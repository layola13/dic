self-intensifying: /ˌsɛlf ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我强化的，自我增强的 |  Describes a process or system where the output of the system reinforces the input, leading to an increase in the intensity or magnitude of the process over time. This is often a positive feedback loop.  | Example:  The self-intensifying nature of the wildfire made it difficult to contain.  这场野火的自我强化特性使其难以控制。 | 近义词: self-reinforcing, accelerating, escalating | 反义词: self-limiting, decelerating, dampening |  形容词
