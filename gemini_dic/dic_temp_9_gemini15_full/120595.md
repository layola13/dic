mirrorless:/ˈmɪrərləs/| adj.| 无反光镜的 | Lacking a mirror reflex system; typically referring to a type of camera.  A mirrorless camera uses an electronic viewfinder (EVF) instead of an optical viewfinder with a mirror. | Example: My new mirrorless camera takes stunning photos.  我的新无反光镜相机拍出的照片令人惊叹。|近义词：mirrorless camera (无反光镜相机)|反义词：reflex camera (反光相机)
