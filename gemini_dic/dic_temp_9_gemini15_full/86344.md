/hɛkt/ | n. |百公顷|A unit of area equal to 100 hectares (or 10,000 square meters).  It's primarily used in the context of land measurement, particularly in agriculture and forestry.  The word itself is rarely used in everyday conversation;  hectare is more common.|Example: The farm covers a vast area of several hects.|近义词: hectare (more common)|反义词: None directly applicable.  The antonym would relate to a smaller unit of area measurement.
