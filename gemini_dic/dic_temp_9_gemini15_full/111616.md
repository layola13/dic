locales:/ləˈkeɪlz/| n.|语言环境；地区设置；语言区域|The plural form of "locale," referring to specific geographical or cultural regions, often defined by language, customs, and conventions.  It's frequently used in software development to specify how data is formatted and displayed based on the user's location.|Example: The application supports multiple locales, including English (US), English (UK), and Spanish (Spain).  该应用程序支持多种语言环境，包括美国英语、英国英语和西班牙语（西班牙）。|近义词：settings, regions, areas, localities|反义词：global (in a broad sense)|名词
