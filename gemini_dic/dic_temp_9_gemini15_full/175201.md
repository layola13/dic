ragtimes:/ˈræɡˌtaɪmz/| n. |拉格泰姆音乐 |A style of music that was popular in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, characterized by a syncopated rhythm and a distinctive piano style.  It's a precursor to jazz. |Example:  The pianist played a lively ragtime tune.  钢琴家演奏了一首欢快的拉格泰姆乐曲。|近义词：syncopated music, early jazz |反义词：classical music (in contrast to its rhythmic style)
