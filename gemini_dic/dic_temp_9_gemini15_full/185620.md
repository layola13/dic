reventilating: /ˌriːˈvɛntɪleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再次通风；重新通风 |  The process of ventilating again, often implying a previous ventilation that was insufficient or flawed.  It suggests a renewed or improved airflow. | 例句：The building requires reventilating after the fire damaged the ventilation system. = 大楼需要在火灾损坏通风系统后重新通风。

The mine shafts need reventilating to remove accumulated gases. = 矿井需要重新通风以排出积聚的气体。


近义词：re-airing,  re-circulating (air),  refreshening (air)

反义词：suffocating, stagnating (air)

词源:

The word "reventilating" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") to the verb "ventilating," which comes from the Latin word "ventilare," meaning "to fan" or "to blow upon."  Therefore, "reventilating" literally means "to ventilate again."
