promontory:/ˈprɑːmənˌtɔːri/| n. |海角；岬；突出物 | A promontory is a point of high land that juts out into a body of water.  It is often a dramatic and striking feature of a coastline. | Example: The lighthouse stood proudly on the rocky promontory, a sentinel against the stormy sea.  灯塔骄傲地矗立在岩石岬角上，像守护神一样抵御着暴风雨的海浪。|近义词：headland, cape, point |反义词：inlet, bay
