egrette:/ˈɛɡrɪt/| n. |白鹭；饰羽|A small, elegant white heron, often with long plumes.  Egret refers to several species of wading birds in the heron family, known for their beautiful white plumage, especially during breeding season. They are often seen in wetlands and marshes. |例句：The egret gracefully landed in the shallow water.  白鹭优雅地降落在浅水中。

The egret's long plumes were a striking feature.  白鹭的长羽是其引人注目的特征。|近义词：heron, white heron |反义词:None
