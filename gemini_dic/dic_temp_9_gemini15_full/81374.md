graptolite:/ˈɡræptəlaɪt/| n. |笔石|Extinct colonial marine animals that lived in the Paleozoic Era, characterized by branching or feathery skeletons made of chitinous material.  They are important index fossils used in dating Paleozoic rocks.|例句：The discovery of graptolites in the rock formation helped geologists date the strata to the Ordovician period.  发现岩石构造中的笔石帮助地质学家将地层年代确定为奥陶纪。|近义词：fossil (in the context of index fossils)|反义词：N/A|名词
