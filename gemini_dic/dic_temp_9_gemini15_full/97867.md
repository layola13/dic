intimacy: /ˈɪntɪməsi/ | n. | 亲密；隐私；私密关系 | The state or feeling of being intimately acquainted with someone or something; close familiarity or friendship; a close, personal, and affectionate relationship.  | 例句：Their intimacy grew over the years.  他们的亲密关系随着时间的推移而增长。

例句：The intimacy of the small village was appealing. 小村庄的亲密氛围很有吸引力。


近义词：closeness, familiarity, nearness, bond, connection

反义词：distance, estrangement, alienation, remoteness
