maxilliform:/ˈmæksɪlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 上颌形的；具有上颌骨形状的 | Resembling or characteristic of a maxilla (the upper jawbone).  The term is often used in zoology and comparative anatomy to describe structures that are shaped like the maxilla. | Example: The maxilliform process of the insect's head articulated with the mandibles.  昆虫头部上颌形的突起与下颚相连。|近义词:maxillary, jaw-like |反义词:mandibular
