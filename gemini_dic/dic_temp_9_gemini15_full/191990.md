scorge:/skɔːdʒ/| n. |灾祸；祸害；鞭打；惩罚| A scourge is something that causes great suffering or distress. It can refer to a person, event, or thing that inflicts widespread misery.  The word also historically referred to a whip used for punishment. |例句：The plague was a terrible scourge that devastated entire populations. 瘟疫是一种可怕的灾祸，摧毁了整个人口。 The drought is a scourge on the farmers in this region. 干旱是这个地区农民的一场灾难。|近义词：affliction, plague, calamity, disaster, pestilence |反义词：blessing, boon, advantage
