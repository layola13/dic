narrow-minded:/ˈnærəʊˈmaɪndɪd/| adj. | 思想狭隘的；心胸狭窄的 | Having or showing a limited outlook or understanding; intolerant of different views.  |  He's so narrow-minded that he refuses to consider any other point of view. 他思想如此狭隘，拒绝考虑任何其他观点。|近义词：bigoted, intolerant, prejudiced, parochial |反义词：open-minded, broad-minded, tolerant


USAGE->

They are narrow-minded and unwilling to compromise. = 他们思想狭隘，不愿妥协。
