masthead:/ˈmæstˌhed/| n.|刊首标题;船桅顶;（船的）船首标志|The masthead is the title of a publication, usually found at the beginning of the publication. It may also refer to the top of a mast on a ship or a ship's figurehead.  |例句：The magazine's masthead proudly displayed its founding date.  该杂志的刊首标题骄傲地展示了它的创刊日期。

The ship's masthead light guided sailors through the stormy night.  船上的船桅顶灯引导水手度过暴风雨的夜晚。|近义词：title page, heading, caption|反义词：None
