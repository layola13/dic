humbler: /ˈhʌmblər/ | adj. | 谦逊的；卑微的 | More humble; showing modesty or lowliness.  It suggests a greater degree of humility than "humble". | 例句：He offered a humbler apology than expected. 他比预期更谦逊地道歉了。

例句：Her humbler beginnings made her appreciate her success even more. 她卑微的出身让她更加珍惜自己的成功。


近义词：modest, meek, unassuming, self-effacing | 反义词：arrogant, haughty, proud, boastful | 词性：形容词
