investigation:/ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |调查，研究|The act of investigating or inquiring into something systematically; a systematic inquiry into a matter.  It involves collecting information, examining evidence, and drawing conclusions.|例句：The police are conducting an investigation into the murder. 警方正在对这起谋杀案进行调查。|例句：The scientific investigation yielded surprising results. 科学研究产生了令人惊讶的结果。|近义词：inquiry, examination, scrutiny, probe|反义词：conclusion, resolution
