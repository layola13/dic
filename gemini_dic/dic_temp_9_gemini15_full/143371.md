outfeasts:/ˌaʊtˈfiːsts/| v.|超过…的盛宴；胜过…的宴会；|to surpass in feasting; to exceed in lavish entertainment.|例句：The king's feast outfeasted all others in the kingdom. 国王的宴会胜过王国里其他的所有宴会。The lavish party outfeasted any previous celebration.  这场奢华的派对超过了以往的任何庆祝活动。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo|反义词：underdo, underserve|动词
