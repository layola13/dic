dismisses:/dɪsˈmɪsɪz/ | v. | 解雇；驳回；不理会；解散 | To refuse to consider or accept something; to end the employment of someone; to formally end a meeting or gathering. | 例句：The judge dismissed the case. = 法官驳回了此案。  | 例句：He was dismissed from his job for misconduct. = 他因行为不端而被解雇。 |近义词：reject, fire, discharge,  dismiss, adjourn | 反义词：accept, retain, consider, employ


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dismisses my concerns every time. = 他每次都忽略我的担忧。
The teacher dismisses the class at 3 pm. = 老师下午3点下课。

一般过去时:
The jury dismissed the charges. = 陪审团驳回了指控。
She dismissed her assistant last week. = 她上周解雇了她的助理。

现在完成时:
The court has dismissed the lawsuit. = 法院已经驳回了诉讼。
He has dismissed three employees this year. = 他今年已经解雇了三名员工。

过去完成时:
The judge had dismissed the previous case before hearing this one. = 法官在审理此案之前已驳回了之前的案件。
They had dismissed the meeting before the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前已经解散了会议。

将来时:
The board will dismiss the proposal. = 董事会将驳回该提案。
The principal will dismiss the students early due to the snowstorm. = 校长由于暴风雪将会提前让学生放学。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the judge would not dismiss the case. = 如果证据更充分，法官就不会驳回此案。
I wish the committee would dismiss this pointless debate. = 我希望委员会能够结束这场毫无意义的辩论。

被动语态:
The case was dismissed due to lack of evidence. = 由于证据不足，此案被驳回。
The employees were dismissed without warning. = 员工们被解雇，事先没有任何警告。

疑问句:
Will the court dismiss the charges? = 法院会驳回指控吗？
Why did the manager dismiss him? = 为什么经理解雇了他？

否定句:
The judge did not dismiss the case. = 法官没有驳回此案。
The company did not dismiss any employees. = 公司没有解雇任何员工。
