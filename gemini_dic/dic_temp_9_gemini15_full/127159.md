natural-historian:/ˈnætʃrəl hɪsˈtɔːriən/| n. |自然历史学家|A person who studies natural history, encompassing the observation and description of organisms, their behaviors, and their relationships with their environment.  Natural historians may specialize in specific areas such as botany, zoology, geology, or paleontology, but their work often involves a broad interdisciplinary approach.|Example:  David Attenborough is a renowned natural historian whose documentaries have captivated audiences worldwide.  大卫·爱登堡是一位著名的自然历史学家，他的纪录片迷住了全世界的观众。|近义词：naturalist, field biologist, environmentalist|反义词：N/A
