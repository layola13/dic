fitter:/ˈfɪtər/| n. |安装工；健身教练；使…更合适的人或事物|A fitter is a person who installs or repairs machinery or equipment.  A fitter can also be someone who improves physical fitness. |例句：He works as a fitter in a factory. 他在一家工厂当安装工。|例句：She's a personal fitter helping clients achieve their fitness goals. 她是一位私人健身教练，帮助客户实现他们的健身目标。|近义词：installer, mechanic, trainer, coach|反义词：deteriorator (in the context of fitness)
