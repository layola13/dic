mur:/mɜːr/| n.| 喃喃低语，低沉的声音 | A low, indistinct sound; a murmur.  |例句：The crowd murmured their approval.  人群低声表示赞同。|例句：A murmur of conversation filled the room.  房间里充满了低声的谈话声。|近义词：whisper, mutter, rumble|反义词：shout, roar


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crowd murmurs their approval. = 人群低声表示赞同。
He murmurs something under his breath. = 他低声咕哝着什么。


一般过去时:
The audience murmured their discontent. = 观众低声表达了他们的不满。
She murmured a prayer before sleeping. = 她睡觉前低声祈祷。


现在完成时:
The wind has murmured through the trees all night. = 风整夜都在树林中低语。
He has murmured his intentions to no one. = 他从未向任何人透露过他的意图。


过去完成时:
The river had murmured its secrets for centuries. = 这条河几个世纪以来一直低语着它的秘密。
She had murmured apologies before leaving. = 离开之前，她低声说了声抱歉。


将来时:
The waves will murmur against the shore. = 海浪将拍打着海岸发出低语声。
He will murmur sweet nothings in her ear. = 他会在她耳边轻声细语。


虚拟语气:
If he murmured a single word, she would hear it. = 如果他低声说出一个字，她都会听到。
I wish he would murmur his feelings to me. = 我希望他能向我倾诉他的感受。


被动语态:
His name was murmured with respect. = 他的名字被人们尊敬地低声提起。
The secret was murmured among the townspeople. = 这个秘密在镇上的人们中间被低声传说着。


疑问句:
What was he murmuring to himself? = 他自言自语说了什么？
Did you hear what she was murmuring? = 你听到她低声说了什么吗？


否定句:
He did not murmur a word. = 他一句话也没说。
She did not murmur in her sleep. = 她睡觉时没有低声说话。


PHRASE->
a murmur of approval = 一片赞许声
a murmur of discontent = 一片不满声
a low murmur = 低低的murmur
a hushed murmur = 压低声音的murmur
a barely audible murmur = 几乎听不见的murmur
