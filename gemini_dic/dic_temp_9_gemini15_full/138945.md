nutting:/ˈnʌtɪŋ/ | v. | 收集坚果；寻找乐趣 | The act of gathering nuts, often implying a leisurely or recreational activity.  It can also refer to a fruitless or unproductive search. |例句：We spent the afternoon nutting in the woods. 我们在树林里度过了一下午采集坚果。

例句：He went nutting, but found nothing worthwhile. 他去寻找乐趣，但一无所获。

近义词：gathering, foraging, searching

反义词：harvesting (if referring to a productive search)
