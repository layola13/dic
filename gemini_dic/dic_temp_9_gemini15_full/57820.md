drenchingly:/ˈdrɛntʃɪŋli/| adv.|湿透地，非常潮湿地|Extremely wet; soaked.  The adjective "drenching" describes something that causes someone or something to become thoroughly soaked with liquid, usually water.  The adverbial form intensifies this meaning.|例句：The rain was drenchingly cold and relentless.  这场雨又冷又下个不停，让人湿透了。The hiker was drenchingly wet after crossing the stream.  徒步旅行者过了小溪后，浑身湿透了。|近义词：soakingly, thoroughly wet, saturatedly|反义词：dryly, aridly
