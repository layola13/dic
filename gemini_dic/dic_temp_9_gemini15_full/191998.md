scorify:/ˈskɔːrɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使成渣;烧成熔渣;使焦化|To convert (a substance, especially a mineral) into a slag or scoria by heating.  The term often refers to the process of heating a material to a high temperature until it becomes porous and brittle.| The blacksmith scorified the iron ore to remove impurities.  铁匠将铁矿石熔渣化以去除杂质。|The furnace scorified the waste metal.  熔炉将废金属熔渣化。|近义词：vitrify, fuse, calcine |反义词：refine, purify
