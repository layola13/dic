jock:/dʒɑːk/| n.|运动员；粗鲁的人；|A jock is typically a male athlete, often associated with a particular sport or team, and sometimes stereotyped as being overly focused on athletics, sometimes to the detriment of academics or social skills.  The term can also be used derisively to refer to someone considered arrogant or insensitive due to their athletic prowess.|例句：He's a star jock on the football team. 他是足球队的一名明星运动员。|例句：Some people consider him a jock and therefore unintelligent. 有些人认为他是个只知道运动的傻瓜。|近义词：athlete, sportsman, player |反义词：nerd, bookworm
