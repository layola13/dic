downsampling:/ˈdaʊnˌsæmplɪŋ/| v.|下采样，降采样|The process of reducing the number of samples in a signal or image. This is often done to reduce the size of a data set, improve processing speed, or reduce noise.|例句：We downsampled the high-resolution image to create a thumbnail. 我们对高分辨率图像进行下采样以创建缩略图。
例句：The audio signal was downsampled from 44.1 kHz to 22.05 kHz. 音频信号从 44.1 kHz 下采样到 22.05 kHz。|近义词：subsampling, decimation|反义词：upsampling, interpolation|动词
