lymphs:/lɪmfz/| n.|淋巴液，淋巴细胞|A lymph is a colorless fluid containing white blood cells, that bathes the tissues and drains through the lymphatic system into the bloodstream.  Lymphs are an essential part of the immune system.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's lymph nodes to check for signs of infection.  医生检查了病人的淋巴结，以检查是否有感染迹象。  The analysis of the lymph revealed the presence of cancerous cells. 淋巴分析显示存在癌细胞。|近义词：lymphatic fluid|反义词：blood (in the context of body fluids)|名词
