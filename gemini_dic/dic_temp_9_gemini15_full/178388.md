reconjure:/ˌriːˈkɒndʒər/|/ˌriːˈkɑːndʒər/ | vt. |重新变戏法般地变出；重新召唤；使再现 |To conjure again; to summon or evoke again; to recreate or revive something in the mind or imagination. |例句：The magician attempted to reconjure the vanished rabbit.  魔术师试图再次变出消失的兔子。

例句：She tried to reconjure the feeling of happiness she had experienced as a child. 她试图重温儿时感受到的快乐。


近义词：recreate, revive, evoke, summon, reimagine | 反义词：destroy, obliterate, forget, dispel


词性:动词
