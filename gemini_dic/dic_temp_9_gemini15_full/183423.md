reproval: /rɪˈpruːvəl/ | n. | 责备；谴责；非难 |  The act of reproving; rebuke; censure.  It expresses disapproval or criticism of someone's actions or behavior. |例句：His reproval stung more than any physical blow. 他的责备比任何体罚都更刺痛。

例句：The teacher's reproval silenced the noisy class. 老师的责备使喧闹的课堂安静下来。


近义词：rebuke, reprimand, censure, condemnation, blame, criticism | 反义词：praise, commendation, approval
