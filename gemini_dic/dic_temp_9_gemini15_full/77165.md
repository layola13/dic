gaslighting: /ˈɡæsˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. |煤气灯操纵；精神控制；心理操控 | The act of manipulating someone by psychologically controlling them, often making them question their own sanity and perception of reality.  This is often done subtly and insidiously. |例句：He was accused of gaslighting his wife, making her doubt her own memory and judgment. 他被指控操纵他妻子，让她怀疑自己的记忆力和判断力。

例句：The constant criticism and denial were a form of gaslighting designed to undermine her confidence.  持续不断的批评和否认是一种旨在破坏她自信心的煤气灯操纵。


近义词：manipulation, mind control, psychological abuse, brainwashing, deception

反义词：empowerment, validation, support, honesty, truthfulness

词性: 名词
