detects:/dɪˈtɛkts/ | vt. | 检测到；发现；查明 | To discover or notice something, typically something that is not easily detectable.  | 例句：The smoke alarm detected smoke and sounded the alarm.  = 烟雾报警器探测到烟雾并发出警报。

The doctor detected a problem with her heart. = 医生发现了她心脏的问题。


近义词：discover, find, identify, recognize, perceive | 反义词：miss, overlook, ignore |  动词
