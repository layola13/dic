photolithographic: /ˌfoʊtoʊˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 光刻的；光致平版印刷的 | Relating to or denoting a photographic process used in microfabrication to create patterns on a substrate, typically silicon wafers for integrated circuits.  This involves using light-sensitive materials and masks to transfer a pattern onto the substrate. | Example: The photolithographic process is crucial in the manufacturing of microchips. 光刻工艺对于微芯片的制造至关重要。 |近义词：lithographic, photographic (in this context) | 反义词：None directly applicable.
