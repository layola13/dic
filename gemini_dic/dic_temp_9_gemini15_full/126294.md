myochrome:/ˈmaɪoʊkroʊm/ | n. |肌色素；肌红蛋白|A pigment found in muscle tissue that is responsible for its color.  It is related to hemoglobin and myoglobin.  Myochrome contributes to the color of meat. |The myochrome in the beef gave it a deep red hue.  这牛肉中的肌色素使它呈现深红色。|近义词：muscle pigment, heme protein |反义词：无
