ripsaw:/ˈrɪpsɔː/| n. |粗齿锯，拉锯| A ripsaw is a type of saw with large, widely spaced teeth designed for ripping (cutting along the grain) of wood.  It is typically used for rough cuts and is less precise than a crosscut saw.|例句：He used a ripsaw to cut the lumber into planks. 他用拉锯把木材锯成木板。  The carpenter needed a ripsaw for the job. 木匠需要一把拉锯来完成这项工作。|近义词：hand saw, wood saw|反义词：crosscut saw
