downshift:/ˈdaʊnʃɪft/| vt. n.|减速换挡；降档；减速；放慢速度|To shift a vehicle's gears into a lower gear, typically to reduce speed or increase engine power for climbing a hill.|例句：He downshifted as he approached the curve. 他在接近弯道时减速换挡。|例句：The downshift gave the car more power to climb the hill. 降档使汽车获得更多动力爬坡。|近义词：reduce speed, decelerate, shift down|反义词：upshift, accelerate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He downshifts when he needs more power. = 他需要更多动力时会降档。
She downshifts before turning. = 她转弯前会降档。


一般过去时:
The driver downshifted before entering the tunnel. = 司机在进入隧道前降了档。
He downshifted quickly to avoid a collision. = 他快速降档以避免碰撞。

The cyclist has downshifted several times on this steep incline. = 这位骑车人在这段陡峭的斜坡上已经降档好几次了。
