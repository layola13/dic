hautboyist: /ˈhɔːtbɔɪɪst/ | n. | 簧管乐器演奏者（尤指双簧管演奏者） | A hautboyist is a musician who plays the hautboy, an older name for the oboe.  The term is now largely archaic, with "oboist" being the preferred term. | Example: The concert featured a renowned hautboyist known for his expressive playing.  这场音乐会特邀了一位以演奏富有表现力著称的簧管乐器演奏家。 | 近义词: oboist | 反义词:  (无直接反义词)
