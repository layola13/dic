chip:/tʃɪp/ | n. vt. |碎片；芯片；炸薯条；削；凿；| a small piece of something, especially something that has broken off something larger; an integrated circuit; a small, thin slice of potato fried until crisp. |例句：He chipped the paint off the wall. 他把墙上的油漆刮掉了。 |近义词：fragment, flake, microchip |反义词：whole, intact
