colleague:/ˈkɒliːɡ/ /ˈkɑːliːɡ/| n. |同事，同僚 |A colleague is a person you work with, especially in a profession.  |例句：I have a great working relationship with all my colleagues.我和我的所有同事都有良好的工作关系。

例句：She discussed the project with her colleagues before submitting it.  她在提交项目之前与同事们讨论了该项目。


近义词：co-worker, fellow worker, associate |反义词：N/A


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My colleague helps me with my work. = 我的同事帮助我工作。
She often collaborates with her colleagues on projects. = 她经常与同事合作完成项目。


一般过去时:
My colleague helped me with my presentation yesterday. = 我的同事昨天帮我做了演示。
They discussed the proposal with their colleagues last week. = 他们上周与同事们讨论了这个提案。


现在完成时:
I have worked with this colleague for five years. = 我和这位同事共事五年了。
She has collaborated with many colleagues on international projects. = 她与许多同事合作过国际项目。


过去完成时:
I had already spoken to my colleague before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经和我的同事谈过了。
They had completed the report before their colleagues arrived. = 他们在同事到达之前已经完成了报告。


将来时:
My colleague will assist me with the upcoming conference. = 我的同事将在即将到来的会议上协助我。
We will be meeting with our colleagues next week to discuss the budget. = 我们下周将与同事们会面讨论预算。


虚拟语气:
If I had a more helpful colleague, I would finish this project sooner. = 如果我有一个更有帮助的同事，我会更快地完成这个项目。
I wish I had a colleague who could speak Mandarin. = 我希望我有一个会说普通话的同事。


被动语态:
The project was completed by a team of colleagues. = 这个项目是由一个同事团队完成的。
The decision was made after consulting with colleagues. = 在咨询了同事之后做出了这个决定。


疑问句:
Do you get along well with your colleagues? = 你和你的同事相处得好吗？
Have you introduced your new colleague to the team? = 你把你新来的同事介绍给团队了吗？


否定句:
I don't always agree with my colleagues' opinions. = 我并不总是同意我同事的意见。
She didn't consult her colleagues before making the decision. = 她做决定前没有征求同事的意见。


PHRASE->
a close colleague = 亲密的同事
a trusted colleague = 可信赖的同事
a former colleague = 前同事
work with colleagues = 与同事一起工作
consult with colleagues = 与同事协商
