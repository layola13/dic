knap:/næp/| vt. n.|敲打；猛击；背包；(尤指用石头)敲碎|To strike or hit something forcefully, especially with a stone; a rucksack or backpack.|例句：He knapped the stone to shape it. 他敲打石头以塑造它。  He carried all his equipment in a knap. 他把所有装备都放在背包里。|近义词：strike, hit, thump, backpack, rucksack|反义词：polish, smooth
