effects:/ɪˈfɛkts/ | n. |结果，影响，特效|The effects of the medicine were immediate.  药物的效果是立即见效的。 The special effects in the movie were amazing. 电影中的特效令人惊叹。|近义词：results, consequences, impacts, repercussions|反义词：causes (in some contexts)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine has many effects. = 这药有很多作用。
The sun's effects on the skin are well known. = 太阳对皮肤的影响是众所周知的。


一般过去时:
The effects of the storm were devastating. = 暴风雨的影响是毁灭性的。
The special effects in the old movie were quite dated. = 那部老电影的特效相当过时了。


现在完成时:
The long-term effects of pollution have yet to be fully understood. = 污染的长期影响尚未完全被理解。
The effects of his actions have been far-reaching. = 他行为的影响深远。


过去完成时:
The effects of the drought had already been felt by the farmers. = 农民们已经感受到了干旱的影响。
The cumulative effects of years of neglect were evident. = 多年忽视的累积影响是显而易见的。


将来时:
The effects of climate change will be felt worldwide. = 气候变化的影响将在全世界感受到。
We will see the effects of this decision in the coming years. = 我们将在未来几年看到这个决定的影响。


虚拟语气:
If the effects were less severe, we would have acted differently. = 如果影响不那么严重，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish the side effects of the medication were less severe. = 我希望药物的副作用不那么严重。


被动语态:
The effects of the earthquake were felt across the region. = 地震的影响在整个地区都能感受到。
The positive effects are being studied by scientists. = 科学家们正在研究积极的影响。


疑问句:
What are the effects of this new policy? = 这项新政策的影响是什么？
What side effects are associated with this medication? = 服用这种药有什么副作用？


否定句:
The treatment had no noticeable effects. = 该治疗没有明显效果。
The experiment showed no negative effects. = 实验没有显示负面影响。


PHRASE->
side effects: 副作用
special effects: 特效
sound effects: 音效
cause and effect: 因果关系
take effect: 生效
