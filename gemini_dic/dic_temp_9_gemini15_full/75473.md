frustrate:/frʌˈstreɪt/| vt. adj. n.|使沮丧；使受挫；阻挠；令人沮丧的事|to prevent someone from achieving a goal; to cause someone to feel anger or annoyance because they are unable to do something|例句：The constant delays frustrated the team.  持续的延误使团队感到沮丧。

The complex instructions frustrated me. 复杂的说明让我感到沮丧。|近义词：discourage, thwart, hinder, irritate, annoy|反义词：encourage, help, assist, satisfy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The traffic frustrates me every morning. = 早上的交通让我很沮丧。
His constant complaining frustrates his wife. = 他不断的抱怨让他妻子很沮丧。


一般过去时:
The exam frustrated me because I didn't study enough. = 考试让我很沮丧，因为我没有好好学习。
The rain frustrated our plans for a picnic. =  雨水打乱了我们野餐的计划。


现在完成时:
The project has frustrated us for months. = 这个项目已经让我们沮丧了好几个月了。
She has been frustrated by her lack of progress. = 她因为缺乏进步而感到沮丧。


过去完成时:
I had been frustrated by his behavior before the argument. = 在争吵之前，我就已经被他的行为激怒了。
They had already been frustrated by numerous setbacks. = 他们已经被无数的挫折打击过。


将来时:
The new regulations will frustrate small businesses. = 新的规章制度将阻碍小型企业的发展。
I think the delay will frustrate many people. = 我认为拖延会让很多人感到沮丧。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have this deadline, I wouldn't be so frustrated. = 如果我没有这个最后期限，我就不会这么沮丧。
I wish I hadn't been so frustrated by the outcome. = 我希望我没有因为结果而如此沮丧。


被动语态:
He was frustrated by the lack of support. = 他因为缺乏支持而感到沮丧。
The team was frustrated by repeated failures. =  团队因屡次失败而感到沮丧。


疑问句:
Are you frustrated by your job? = 你对你的工作感到沮丧吗？
Does this situation frustrate you? = 这种情况让你沮丧吗？


否定句:
I wasn't frustrated by the criticism. = 我并没有因为批评而感到沮丧。
He wasn't frustrated by the failure. = 他并没有因为失败而感到沮丧。


PHRASE->
feel frustrated = 感到沮丧
become frustrated = 变得沮丧
extremely frustrated = 极其沮丧
deeply frustrated = 深感沮丧
utterly frustrated = 完全沮丧
