relegate:/ˈrɛləɡeɪt/| vt.|降级；贬低；委派；把…归类于|to assign to a lower position, rank, or class; to send or consign to an inferior position or place; to transfer (a matter, task, or responsibility) to someone else|例句：He was relegated to a minor role in the company. 他被贬低到公司的一个次要角色。

例句：The problem was relegated to a committee for further discussion. 这个问题被交给一个委员会进一步讨论。


近义词：demote, downgrade, assign, delegate, consign|反义词：promote, elevate, upgrade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They relegate him to a lower position. = 他们把他降职。
The company relegates tasks to different departments. = 公司将任务委派给不同的部门。

一般过去时:
They relegated him to a minor role. = 他们把他降职到一个次要的角色。
The court relegated the case to a lower court. = 法院将此案移交下级法院审理。

现在完成时:
They have relegated him to a less important project. = 他们已经把他调到一个不太重要的项目上了。
The committee has relegated the issue to a subcommittee. = 委员会已将该问题提交给小组委员会。

过去完成时:
They had relegated him before the new manager arrived. = 新经理来之前，他们就已经把他降职了。
The board had relegated the decision to the CEO. = 董事会之前已将决定权交给首席执行官。

将来时:
They will relegate him to a less demanding job. = 他们将会把他调到一个要求较低的工作岗位。
The government will relegate the responsibility to the local authorities. = 政府将把责任下放到地方政府。

虚拟语气:
If they were to relegate him, he would resign. = 如果他们要降他的职，他会辞职。
I wish they hadn't relegated him so quickly. = 我真希望他们没有这么快就把他降职。

被动语态:
He was relegated to a less prestigious position. = 他被降到了一个不太体面的职位。
The matter was relegated to a lower priority. = 这件事被列为较低的优先级。

疑问句:
Will he be relegated to a lower division? = 他会被降到较低的级别吗？
Was the task relegated to the correct team? = 任务是否委派给了正确的团队？

否定句:
They did not relegate him despite his mistakes. = 尽管他犯了错，他们也没有降他的职。
The project was not relegated to the inexperienced team. = 这个项目并没有委派给缺乏经验的团队。
