gentlewoman: /ˈdʒɛntlˌwʊmən/ | n. | 女绅士；淑女 | A woman of gentle birth, refined manners, and often high social standing.  A polite and respectful term for a woman. |例句：She behaved like a true gentlewoman throughout the ordeal. 她在那场磨难中表现得像个真正的淑女。

例句：The gentlewoman offered her seat to the elderly man.  这位女士把座位让给了那位老人。


近义词：lady, dame (formal), refined woman | 反义词：vulgarian, hoyden, slattern


词源：源自古英语 *gentil and *wifman,  “gentle” meaning of noble birth or refined manners, and "woman" meaning a female adult human being.  The word evolved through Middle English gentilwoman.
