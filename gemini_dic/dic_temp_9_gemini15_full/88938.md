holdout: /ˈhoʊldaʊt/ | n., v. | 拒绝合作的人；坚持抵抗的人；顽固分子；坚持；抵制；不合作 |

英文解析: A person or group that refuses to cooperate or give in;  the act of refusing to cooperate or give in.  It can refer to a person, group, or even a thing (like a piece of data) that remains separate from a larger group or process.

例句:

The last holdout finally agreed to the terms of the settlement. = 最后一个顽固分子最终同意了和解条款。

Several holdouts refused to participate in the strike. =  一些坚持抵抗的人拒绝参加罢工。


近义词: resister, objector, dissenter, defector, obstructionist

反义词: collaborator, conformist, accomplice


词性: Noun, Verb
