essoin:/ˈɛsɔɪn/ | n. | 藉口；托词；(旧)法庭延期理由 | A reason given for non-appearance, especially in a court of law; an excuse.  It often refers to a valid excuse, particularly those relating to illness or other unavoidable circumstances. In older legal contexts, it specifically referred to a plea for postponement of a court case due to a legitimate reason. | The defendant offered an essoin of illness to explain his absence.  被告以生病为由推迟出庭。| excuse, pretext, plea |  (无明确反义词，但可以根据语境用反义词如：lie, fabrication) | 名词
