lazuli:/ˈlæzjuːli/ | n. |天青石，青金石 |A deep blue metamorphic rock, valued as a gemstone and pigment.  It is composed primarily of lazurite, a complex aluminosilicate mineral. |The lapis lazuli pendant was a stunning piece of jewelry.|近义词：azure, sapphire|反义词：无


USAGE->
(由于lazuli本身是名词，没有动词的用法，以下例句展示的是lazuli在不同句法结构中的使用)

一般现在时:
That necklace is made of lapis lazuli. = 那条项链是用青金石做的。
The artist uses lapis lazuli pigment in his paintings. = 这位艺术家在他的绘画中使用青金石颜料。


一般过去时:
Ancient Egyptians used lapis lazuli for jewelry and decoration. = 古埃及人使用青金石制作珠宝和装饰品。
The museum acquired a large piece of lapis lazuli. = 博物馆获得了一大块青金石。


现在完成时:
Scientists have studied the chemical composition of lapis lazuli for centuries. = 科学家们已经研究了几个世纪的青金石的化学成分。
They have found many examples of lapis lazuli artifacts. = 他们发现了许多青金石文物的例子。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery of ultramarine, artists had used ground lapis lazuli for blue pigment. = 在发现群青之前，艺术家们曾使用研磨的青金石作为蓝色颜料。
The ancient civilization had already mastered the art of carving lapis lazuli. = 古代文明已经掌握了雕刻青金石的艺术。


将来时:
Researchers will continue to analyze the properties of lapis lazuli. = 研究人员将继续分析青金石的特性。
The exhibition will feature several pieces of exquisite lapis lazuli jewelry. = 展览将展出几件精美的青金石珠宝。


被动语态:
Lapis lazuli was highly valued in ancient civilizations. = 青金石在古代文明中非常珍贵。
The pigment was extracted from finely ground lapis lazuli. = 颜料是从细磨的青金石中提取的。


疑问句:
Is this pendant made of lapis lazuli? = 这枚吊坠是用青金石做的吗？
What is the chemical formula of lapis lazuli? = 青金石的化学式是什么？


否定句:
That stone is not lapis lazuli; it's sodalite. = 那块石头不是青金石；那是蓝方石。
The painting doesn't use lapis lazuli; it uses synthetic ultramarine. = 这幅画没有使用青金石；它使用的是合成群青。
