desubsidization: /ˌdiːˈsʌbsɪdaɪzeɪʃən/ | n. | 停止补贴；取消补贴 | The process of removing or reducing subsidies.  It refers to the act of withdrawing government financial support for a particular industry, product, or service. | Example: The desubsidization of the agricultural sector led to higher food prices.  取消农业部门的补贴导致食品价格上涨。 | 近义词：withdrawal of subsidy, removal of support, ending of financial aid | 反义词：subsidization, granting of subsidy
