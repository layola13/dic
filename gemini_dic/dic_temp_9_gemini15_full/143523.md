outgrossed:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊst/| v.|超过票房收入 |to earn more money than another film at the box office.  This is a relatively new word, formed by combining "out-" (meaning "to surpass" or "exceed") and "grossed" (the past participle of "gross," meaning to earn a certain amount of money).|例句：The new superhero movie outgrossed all expectations, earning over $1 billion worldwide.  这部新的超级英雄电影票房收入远超预期，全球票房超过10亿美元。

The independent film surprisingly outgrossed the big-budget Hollywood production. 这部独立电影出人意料地超过了大制作好莱坞电影的票房收入。


近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed, eclipse
反义词：underperform, fall short of
