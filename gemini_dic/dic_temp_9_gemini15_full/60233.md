effusion:/ɪˈfjuːʒən/| n. |涌出；流出；倾诉；（感情的）流露 |The outward flow of a substance, especially a liquid; a pouring forth.  Also refers to an unrestrained expression of emotion. |例句：The effusion of blood from the wound was alarming.  伤口大量出血令人担忧。

例句：Her effusion of gratitude was heartfelt.  她发自内心的感激之情溢于言表。


近义词：outpouring, overflow, gush, outburst |反义词：restraint, repression, suppression |词性: 名词
