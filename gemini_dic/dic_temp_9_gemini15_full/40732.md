convexo: /kənˈvɛksoʊ/ | adj. | 凸的，凸面的 | Convex describes a surface that curves outward, like the outside of a sphere or a dome.  It is the opposite of concave. | 例句：The lens of the telescope was convexo-concave. 望远镜的镜头是凸凹的。


The earth is approximately a convexo sphere. 地球近似于一个凸球体。

近义词：protruded, bulging, protuberant | 反义词：concave, indented


词源:  From Latin *convexo, from *con- + *vexō "to carry, bring, lift". The prefix con- suggests intensifying the action, and  *vexō implies raising or lifting, creating the image of a surface being pushed outward.
