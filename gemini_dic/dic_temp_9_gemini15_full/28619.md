/keərˈteɪkən/| adj.| 被照看的；被保管的 | cared for and protected; looked after.  This word describes something or someone that is being looked after or protected. It implies a temporary or ongoing responsibility for the well-being of something or someone. |例句：The abandoned kitten was carefully caretaken by the animal shelter.  被遗弃的小猫受到了动物收容所的精心照料。

The museum's collection is meticulously caretaken. 博物馆的藏品得到了细致的保管。|近义词：guarded, protected, looked after, supervised |反义词： neglected, abandoned, ignored

词源:
The word "caretaken" is derived from the verb "caretake," which combines "care" (meaning concern or attention) and "take" (meaning to assume responsibility or possession).  The suffix "-en" transforms the verb into an adjective, indicating a state or condition of being cared for.
