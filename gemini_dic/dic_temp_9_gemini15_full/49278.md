deliberative: /dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 深思熟虑的；审议的；有讨论性质的 | characterized by or given to careful and slow consideration; relating to or characterized by deliberation | The committee held a deliberative session to discuss the proposal.  委员会举行了一次审议会议来讨论这项提案。  The judge's deliberative approach to the case ensured fairness.  法官审慎处理此案确保了公平性。 | thoughtful, reflective, considered | impulsive, hasty, rash | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The council is deliberative in its approach to policy. = 理事会对政策采取深思熟虑的方式。
He is deliberative when making important decisions. = 他做重要决定时很慎重。


一般过去时:
The jury was deliberative before reaching a verdict. = 陪审团在做出裁决之前进行了仔细考虑。
She was deliberative in her response to the accusation. = 她对指控的回应非常谨慎。


现在完成时:
The committee has been deliberative throughout the process. = 委员会在整个过程中一直都非常慎重。
The government has been deliberative on this matter for months. = 政府已经就此问题深思熟虑了好几个月。


过去完成时:
They had been deliberative for hours before announcing their decision. = 他们在宣布决定之前已经仔细考虑了几个小时。
The senators had been deliberative on the bill prior to the vote. = 参议员们在投票前对法案进行了仔细讨论。


将来时:
The board will be deliberative in its consideration of the merger. = 董事会将慎重考虑合并事宜。
The court will be deliberative before issuing its ruling. = 法院在发布裁决前将进行仔细考虑。


虚拟语气:
If the committee were more deliberative, the outcome might be different. = 如果委员会更慎重一些，结果可能会不同。
I wish the council had been more deliberative in its decision-making. = 我希望理事会在决策时更慎重一些。


被动语态:
The matter was deliberated upon extensively by the council. = 理事会对这件事进行了广泛的讨论。
The proposal was deliberated before being approved. = 该提案在获得批准之前经过了仔细讨论。


疑问句:
Was the decision truly deliberative? = 这个决定真的是经过深思熟虑的吗？
Is the process sufficiently deliberative? = 这个过程足够慎重吗？


否定句:
The council was not deliberative in its actions. = 理事会的行动并不慎重。
His response was not deliberative; he acted impulsively. = 他的回应并不慎重；他是冲动行事。

词源:
From Latin *dēliberātivus, from dēliberāre "to consider, weigh," from dē- "down, completely" + librāre "to weigh."
