monition:/məˈnɪʃən/ | n. |警告；劝告；训诫 | A warning or reminder, especially one given with an air of authority.  It often implies a serious or solemn warning, carrying the weight of potential consequences. |例句：The judge's monition to the defendant was clear and firm.  法官对被告的警告清晰而坚定。

例句：Her mother's monition about the dangers of speeding saved her from a serious accident. 她母亲关于超速危险的警告使她避免了一场严重的事故。


近义词：warning, caution, admonition, reprimand, advice |反义词：encouragement, praise, commendation |词性：名词
