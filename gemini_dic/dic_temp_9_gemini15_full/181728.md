reliberates:/ˌriːˈlɪbəreɪts/| vt.|重新解放；再次释放|To set free again; to liberate a second time.  The word is a relatively rare and newly coined term, not found in standard dictionaries. It combines "re-" (again) and "liberates" (sets free).|例句：The prisoner was initially captured, then reliberated after a successful appeal. 囚犯最初被捕，后来在成功上诉后被再次释放。


The army reliberated the city after the enemy's retreat.  军队在敌人撤退后再次解放了这座城市。


近义词：re-emancipate, release anew, free again|反义词：imprison, enslave, capture|动词
