grasps:/ɡræspz/| vt. n.|抓住；理解；掌握；|To take hold of something firmly with one's hand or hands.  To understand something completely. |例句：She grasped the rope tightly. 她紧紧抓住绳子。

He grasped the meaning of the poem. 他理解了这首诗的意思。|近义词：seize, grip, comprehend, understand|反义词：release, let go, misunderstand, misinterpret|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grasps the concept easily. = 他很容易理解这个概念。
She grasps the opportunity. = 她抓住了机会。

一般过去时:
I grasped his hand firmly. = 我紧紧握住他的手。
He grasped the situation quickly. = 他迅速掌握了情况。

现在完成时:
She has grasped the main points of the lecture. = 她已经掌握了讲座的主要内容。
I have grasped the opportunity to learn more. = 我抓住了学习更多知识的机会。

过去完成时:
He had grasped the importance of the meeting before it started. = 会议开始前他已经意识到了会议的重要性。
They had grasped the situation after several days of investigation. = 经过几天的调查，他们已经掌握了情况。

将来时:
I will grasp every chance to improve myself. = 我将抓住每一个机会提升自己。
She will grasp the opportunity to travel abroad. = 她将抓住出国旅行的机会。

虚拟语气:
If I grasped the concept, I could explain it. = 如果我理解了这个概念，我就能解释它。
I wish I had grasped the opportunity earlier. = 我真希望我早点抓住那个机会。

被动语态:
The opportunity was grasped by her. = 她抓住了机会。(被动语态较少用)
The concept was easily grasped by the students. = 学生们很容易理解这个概念。(被动语态较少用)

疑问句:
Do you grasp the meaning? = 你理解意思了吗？
Does he grasp the situation? = 他掌握情况了吗？

否定句:
I don't grasp the significance of this. = 我不明白这其中的意义。
He didn't grasp the opportunity. = 他没有抓住机会。


PHRASE->

lose one's grasp on: = 失去对…的控制
