reafforest:/ˌriːˈfɒrɪst/| vt.|重新造林；使重新长满树木|To plant trees on land that has been deforested.  The act of replanting trees in a previously forested area. |例句：The government is planning to reafforest the mountain slopes. 政府计划对山坡进行重新造林。

例句：They are working hard to reafforest the area after the devastating wildfire. 他们在努力重建毁于大火的地区的森林。


近义词：replant, reforest, afforest

反义词：deforest

词性:动词
