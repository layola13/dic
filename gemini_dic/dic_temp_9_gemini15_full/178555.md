recopying:/ˌriːˈkɒpiɪŋ/ | v. | 重新抄写；复制 | The act of copying something again.  It implies a previous act of copying, or a need for a more accurate or complete copy. | Example:  The student was asked to recopy his essay because of numerous errors.  学生因为有很多错误被要求重新抄写他的论文。 |近义词：copying, duplicating, reproducing | 反义词：deleting, erasing


USAGE->

He recopies his notes every evening. = 他每天晚上都重新抄写笔记。
She recopies the document for better clarity. = 她为了更好的清晰度重新复制了文件。


一般过去时:
