incurable:/ɪnˈkjʊərəbl/| adj.|不治之症的，无法治愈的|incapable of being cured; hopeless.  The disease is incurable.  |The cancer was declared incurable.  = 癌症被宣布为不治之症。| curable, treatable | curable, treatable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This disease is incurable. = 这种病是不治之症。
His condition is incurable at this stage. = 在这个阶段，他的病情是无法治愈的。


一般过去时:
Doctors said the disease was incurable. = 医生说这种病是无法治愈的。
The illness was deemed incurable by the specialists. = 专家认为这种疾病是无法治愈的。


现在完成时:
The damage has proven incurable. = 造成的损害已被证明是无法治愈的。
Sadly, her condition has remained incurable despite the treatments. = 令人悲伤的是，尽管接受了治疗，她的病情仍然无法治愈。


过去完成时:
They had discovered that the illness was incurable. = 他们已经发现这种疾病是无法治愈的。
Before the diagnosis, they hadn't realized the injury was incurable. = 在诊断之前，他们没有意识到伤势是无法治愈的。


将来时:
This new treatment might make the disease less incurable. = 这种新疗法可能会使这种疾病不那么难以治愈。
Scientists hope to find a cure, making the disease no longer incurable. = 科学家们希望找到一种治愈方法，使这种疾病不再是不治之症。


虚拟语气:
If this were an incurable disease, we'd have to consider palliative care. = 如果这是一种不治之症，我们就必须考虑姑息治疗。
I wish this condition weren't incurable. = 我希望这种情况不是不治之症。


被动语态:
The disease was declared incurable by the medical team. = 医疗团队宣布该疾病无法治愈。
It was determined that the damage was incurable. = 确定损害是无法治愈的。


疑问句:
Is this condition truly incurable? = 这种情况真的无法治愈吗？
Are there any incurable diseases that can be managed effectively? = 有哪些不治之症能够得到有效的控制？


否定句:
The disease is not incurable; there are treatments available. = 这病并非不治之症；有治疗方法。
This injury is not necessarily incurable; further tests are needed. = 这伤并不一定无法治愈；需要进一步检查。
