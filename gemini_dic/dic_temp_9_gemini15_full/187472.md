roll-up:/ˈroʊl ʌp/ | /ˈrəʊl ʌp/| n., v.|卷起，卷筒，卷状物 | A roll-up is something rolled up, often a poster or banner rolled into a cylinder for easy storage and transport. As a verb, it means to roll something into a cylindrical shape. |  例句：The artist carefully rolled up the painting.  艺术家小心地卷起了画作。

例句：We need to roll up the carpet before we leave. 我们离开之前需要卷起地毯。


近义词：roll, wrap, coil | 反义词：unroll, unfold


词性：Noun, Verb


USAGE->

He rolls up the map every day. 他每天都卷起地图。
They roll up their sleeves and start working. 他们卷起袖子开始工作。
