plonks:/plɒŋks/| vt.|砰地放下；笨拙地放下|To drop or put down something heavily or clumsily.  The verb often implies a lack of grace or care.|例句：He plonked the heavy box on the floor. 他重重地把沉重的箱子放在地板上。|例句：She plonked herself down on the sofa. 她重重地坐到沙发上。|近义词：drop, dump, thud, fling|反义词：place carefully, gently put down|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plonks his bag on the table. = 他把包砰地放在桌子上。
She plonks the dishes in the sink. = 她把盘子砰地扔进水槽里。


一般过去时:
He plonked the book down angrily. = 他生气地把书砰地扔下。
She plonked herself onto the chair. = 她重重地坐在了椅子上。


现在完成时:
He has plonked his keys on the counter. = 他把钥匙砰地放在柜台上。
She has plonked the letter on the desk. = 她把信砰地放在桌子上。


过去完成时:
He had plonked the suitcase on the floor before he sat down. = 他在坐下之前把行李箱砰地放在地板上。
She had plonked the food on the table before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，她已经把食物砰地放在桌上了。

虚拟语气:
If he had more care, he wouldn't plonk things down so carelessly. = 如果他更小心一点，他就不会那么随便地把东西砰地放下。
I wish he wouldn't plonk his dirty shoes on the carpet. = 我希望他不要把脏鞋子砰地扔在地毯上。

疑问句:

否定句:

She didn't plonk the toys on the floor. = 她没有把玩具砰地扔在地上。


PHRASE->
