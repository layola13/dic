gunfire:/ˈɡʌnfaɪər/| n. |枪声，炮火|The sound of guns firing; the act of firing guns.  It often implies a rapid or continuous discharge of firearms, suggesting a chaotic or violent situation.|例句：The gunfire intensified as the soldiers advanced.  枪声随着士兵的推进而加剧。

例句：We heard sporadic gunfire throughout the night.  我们整夜都听到零星的枪声。


近义词：shots, shooting, cannonade, barrage

反义词：silence, peace, quiet


词性:名词
