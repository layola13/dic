pisciculture:/ˈpɪsɪkʌltʃər/| n. |养鱼业，养鱼学|The practice of breeding, raising, and harvesting fish.  It encompasses various techniques and approaches to fish farming, from extensive pond culture to intensive recirculating aquaculture systems.  |例句：Pisciculture is a growing industry, providing a significant source of protein for many communities. 养鱼业是一个增长中的产业，为许多社区提供重要的蛋白质来源。

例句：Modern pisciculture techniques have significantly increased the efficiency of fish production. 现代养鱼技术大大提高了鱼类生产效率。


近义词：fish farming, aquaculture
