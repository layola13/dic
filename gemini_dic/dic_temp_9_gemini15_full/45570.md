curliest:/ˈkɜːrliɪst/| adj.| 最卷曲的，最弯曲的 | Most curled; having the tightest curls.  It is the superlative form of "curly".|例句：Her hair was the curliest I had ever seen.  她的头发是我见过的最卷的。

例句：The curliest vines climbed the wall. 最卷曲的藤蔓攀上了墙。


近义词：crispest, most coiled


反义词：straightest, smoothest


词性:形容词
