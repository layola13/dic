naftali:/næfˈtɑːli/| n. |纳夫塔利（男子名）|A male given name of Hebrew origin.  It is a biblical name meaning "my wrestling" or "my struggle". The name is associated with the patriarch Jacob's son, Naphtali, who was known for his quickness and agility.|例句：Naftali is a popular name in Israel. 纳夫塔利是以色列一个流行的名字。|例句：My friend's son's name is Naftali. 我朋友的儿子名叫纳夫塔利。|近义词：Naphthali (variant spelling)|反义词：无|名词
