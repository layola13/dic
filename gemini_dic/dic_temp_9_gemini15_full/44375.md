cross-culturally:/ˌkrɒsˈkʌltʃərəli/|/ˌkrɔːsˈkʌltʃərəli/| adv.|跨文化地，跨文化地|Across different cultures; in a way that takes into account or compares different cultures.|Example: The research was conducted cross-culturally to ensure its global applicability.  这项研究是在跨文化背景下进行的，以确保其全球适用性。|近义词：interculturally, internationally|反义词： monoculturally
