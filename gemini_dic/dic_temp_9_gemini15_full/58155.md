droughtiness:/ˈdraʊtɪnɪs/| n.|干旱；干涸；枯竭|The state or quality of being drought; dryness; aridity.  It refers to the condition of extreme dryness, especially prolonged lack of rain.  The word often implies the negative consequences of this dryness, such as the withering of plants and the scarcity of water.|例句：The droughtiness of the land led to crop failure.  这片土地的干旱导致了农作物歉收。 The extreme droughtiness of the region forced many families to migrate.  该地区极度干旱迫使许多家庭迁移。|近义词：aridity, dryness, aridness|反义词：wetness, humidity, moisture|名词
