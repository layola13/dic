earnings-related: /ˈɜːrnɪŋz rɪˈleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 与收入相关的 | Relating to or based on earnings.  This adjective describes something connected to or determined by the amount of money someone earns.  | Example:  The pension plan is earnings-related, meaning your payments will depend on your salary.  养老金计划与收入挂钩，这意味着你的付款将取决于你的薪水。  |近义词: income-based, salary-related | 反义词: unrelated to earnings, fixed
