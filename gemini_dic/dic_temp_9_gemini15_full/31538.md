chartering: /ˈtʃɑːrtərɪŋ/ | v. |包租；特许；发给特许状 |The act of hiring or renting a vehicle, aircraft, or vessel for a specific period.  It also refers to granting a charter or license. |例句：We are chartering a yacht for our anniversary. 我们包租了一艘游艇庆祝结婚纪念日。

例句：The company was chartered to provide financial services. 这家公司获得特许经营金融服务。


近义词：renting, leasing, hiring

反义词：owning, purchasing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They charter a bus every year for the school trip. = 他们每年都为学校旅行包一辆巴士。
The company charters planes to transport goods. = 这家公司包机运输货物。


一般过去时:
We chartered a boat and sailed around the island. = 我们租了一艘船，环岛航行。
The king chartered a new city. = 国王批准建立一座新城市。


现在完成时:
They have chartered several yachts this summer. = 他们今年夏天已经租了几艘游艇。
The government has chartered many new businesses this year. = 政府今年批准了许多新企业。


过去完成时:
They had chartered a private jet before the emergency. = 紧急情况发生前，他们已经包了一架私人飞机。
The town had chartered its own water supply before the drought. = 在旱灾之前，小镇已经特许了自己的供水系统。


将来时:
We will charter a helicopter to get a better view. = 我们将包租一架直升机以获得更好的视野。
The organization will charter a new branch in the city. = 该组织将在该市设立一个新的分公司。


虚拟语气:
If we chartered a bigger boat, we could invite more guests. = 如果我们租一艘更大的船，我们就可以邀请更多客人。
I wish we had chartered a car instead of relying on public transport. = 我真希望我们租了一辆车而不是依赖公共交通。


被动语态:
The plane was chartered by a large corporation. = 这架飞机是由一家大公司包租的。
The new university was chartered in 1998. = 这所新大学成立于1998年。


疑问句:
Have you ever chartered a private jet? = 你曾经包租过私人飞机吗？
Is the company chartered to operate in this country? = 这家公司有资格在这个国家运营吗？


否定句:
We did not charter a car; we took a taxi. = 我们没有租车；我们打的。
The project was not chartered by the committee. = 该项目未获得委员会批准。

词源:
From Old French *charter, from Latin chartula ("small paper"), diminutive of charta ("paper, papyrus").  The evolution reflects the historical use of written charters as formal documents granting privileges or rights.
