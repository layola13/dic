magnetises:/ˈmæɡnətaɪzɪz/| v.| 使磁化；吸引；迷住 | To cause (something) to become magnetic; to attract or fascinate strongly. |例句：The Earth magnetises a compass needle. 地球使指南针磁化。|例句：His charisma magnetises the audience. 他的魅力吸引了观众。|近义词：attract, fascinate, charm |反义词：repulse, repel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The magnet magnetises the iron filings. = 磁铁使铁屑磁化。
His personality magnetises people to him. = 他的个性吸引人们靠近他。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
