side-step:/ˈsaɪdˌstɛp/ | v. | 侧身避开；回避；闪避 | To move to the side to avoid something; to evade or avoid something, especially a difficult issue or question. |例句：He side-stepped the question about his past. 他回避了关于他过去的问题。 |例句：She side-stepped the puddle skillfully. 她熟练地避开了水坑。 |近义词：evade, avoid, dodge, circumvent |反义词：confront, face, encounter |
