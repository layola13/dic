nonreactor:/ˈnɒnriˈæktə(r)/| n. |非反应堆；非反应器|A nonreactor is anything that is not a reactor, especially a nuclear reactor.  It can refer to a component or system in a nuclear power plant that does not participate in the nuclear chain reaction, or it can refer to a facility or device that does not utilize nuclear fission or fusion.  |Example: The study focused on the nonreactor components of the power plant.|近义词：non-nuclear device, non-fission system |反义词：reactor
