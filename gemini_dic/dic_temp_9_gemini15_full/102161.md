isthmian:/ɪzˈmiːən/| adj.|地峡的；狭窄的； | Relating to an isthmus; narrow.  The word derives from the Greek word "isthmos," meaning "neck" or "narrow strip of land." |例句：The Isthmian Games were ancient Greek games held near the Isthmus of Corinth.  =  古代的伊斯米亚运动会是古代希腊在哥林多地峡附近举行的运动会。

The Isthmian Canal project was ambitious but ultimately unsuccessful. = 伊斯米亚运河项目雄心勃勃，但最终失败了。

近义词：narrow, constricted, confined |反义词：wide, broad, expansive
