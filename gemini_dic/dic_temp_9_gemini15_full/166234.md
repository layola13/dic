prequoting:/ˈpriːˌkwəʊtɪŋ/| v.|预先报价，预先估价 |The act of providing a price estimate before a formal quote is given.  It often involves preliminary discussions and assessments to determine the scope of work and potential costs.  |例句：The contractor is prequoting the project to give us a rough idea of the cost. 承包商正在对项目进行预先报价，以便让我们大致了解成本。


例句：We received several prequotings before choosing a vendor. 在选择供应商之前，我们收到了几份预先报价。


近义词：preliminary estimate, advance quotation, rough estimate


反义词：final quotation, confirmed price


词性：动词
