recategorize:/ˌriːˈkætəɡəraɪz/| vt.|重新分类，重新归类 |To classify or arrange something again in a different way.|例句：We need to recategorize our products to better target our customers. 我们需要重新分类我们的产品以更好地定位我们的客户。
例句：The librarian recategorized the books according to the Dewey Decimal System. 图书管理员根据杜威十进制系统重新分类书籍。|近义词：reorganize, reclassify, re-group |反义词：categorize (in the original way)
