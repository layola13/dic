sifts:/sɪfts/| v.|筛，筛选，查阅|to separate finer particles from coarser ones by passing through a sieve or screen; to examine or sort carefully|例句：She sifted the flour before adding it to the cake batter. 她把面粉过筛后才加入蛋糕糊中。
例句：The police carefully sifted through the evidence. 警方仔细地审查了证据。|近义词：screen, filter, examine|反义词：mix, combine
