come-what-may:/ˌkʌmˈhwɑːtmeɪ/| adv. |无论如何；不管发生什么事|Regardless of what may happen; no matter what.  This phrase expresses determination and resilience in the face of uncertainty. It suggests a commitment to a course of action despite potential difficulties or setbacks.|例句：I will go ahead with the project, come what may.  无论如何，我都会继续进行这个项目。

例句：We'll finish this job, come what may. 不管发生什么事，我们都会完成这项工作。


近义词：no matter what, whatever happens, regardless


反义词：if things go wrong, depending on circumstances


词性: 副词
