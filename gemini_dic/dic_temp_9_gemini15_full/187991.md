roturier:/ʁɔ.ty.ʁje/| n.|平民；庶民；乡下人|A roturier is a commoner, a person who is not of noble birth.  The term is primarily used in the context of historical feudal societies, particularly in France.|例句：In feudal France, the roturiers were subject to the authority of the nobles.  在封建法国，平民受贵族统治。|例句：The lives of roturiers were often harsh and difficult compared to those of the aristocracy. 平民的生活与贵族相比通常是艰难困苦的。|近义词：commoner, peasant, plebeian|反义词：noble, aristocrat, gentry
