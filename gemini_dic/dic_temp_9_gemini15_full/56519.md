domiciled:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪld/ /ˈdɑːməˌsaɪld/| adj.|  居住的，住所设在…的 | Having one's permanent home in a particular place.  |例句：He is domiciled in London. 他住在伦敦。|例句：The company is domiciled in Delaware. 这家公司注册地在特拉华州。|近义词：resident, established, based |反义词：transient, itinerant


USAGE->

He is domiciled in Paris. = 他住在巴黎。
The company is domiciled in the UK. = 这家公司注册地在英国。
