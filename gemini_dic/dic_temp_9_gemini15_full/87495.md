heterocercal:/ˌhɛtəroʊˈsɜːrkl/| adj.|歪尾形的；不对称尾形的|Having a tail that is asymmetrical, with the upper lobe larger than the lower.  This is a common characteristic of many sharks and some extinct fishes.  The asymmetry aids in lift and thrust during swimming.|例句：Sharks possess a heterocercal tail, providing them with powerful propulsion. 鲨鱼拥有歪尾形的尾巴，这为它们提供了强大的推进力。

The heterocercal tail of the extinct lobe-finned fish is a key feature in understanding its evolutionary relationship to tetrapods.  已灭绝的叶鳍鱼的歪尾形尾巴是理解其与四足动物进化关系的关键特征。|近义词：asymmetrical, lopsided|反义词：homocercal|词性：形容词

Etymology:

The word "heterocercal" comes from the Greek words:

* **hetero-** (ἕτερος, heteros): meaning "other," "different," or "unlike."
* **cerc-** (κερκός, kerkos): meaning "tail."

Therefore, "heterocercal" literally means "having a different tail" or "having an asymmetrical tail."
