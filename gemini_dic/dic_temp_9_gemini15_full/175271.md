raincoat:/ˈreɪnˌkoʊt/| n. |雨衣|A waterproof coat worn to protect against rain.|例句：I wore my raincoat because it was raining heavily. 我因为雨下得很大所以穿了雨衣。
例句：She needs a new raincoat; hers is old and leaky. 她需要一件新的雨衣；她原来的又旧又漏。|近义词：mackintosh, waterproof,  |反义词:None.
