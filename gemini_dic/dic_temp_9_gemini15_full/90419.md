/eɪtʃ piː/ | n. | 马力；英制马力 | Horsepower; a unit of power equal to 550 foot-pounds per second.  It is used to measure the power output of engines and motors. | The car has a 200 hp engine. 这辆车有一个200马力的引擎。|  kilowatt, watt |  low power | noun
