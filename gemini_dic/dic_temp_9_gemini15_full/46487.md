daintily:/ˈdeɪntɪli/ | adv. | 优雅地；精致地；小巧地 | In a delicate or refined way; gracefully.  | She daintily picked at her salad. 她优雅地拨弄着她的沙拉。| elegantly, delicately, gracefully | coarsely, roughly, clumsily | 副词
