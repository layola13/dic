regionality: /ˌriːdʒəˈnæləti/ | n. |区域性，地区性 |The quality or fact of being regional; relating to or characteristic of a particular region.  It often refers to variations in culture, language, or other characteristics across different geographical areas. |例句：The regionality of dialects is evident in the different pronunciations of the same word.  地区的方言差异在同一个词的不同发音中很明显。


The strong regionality of the cuisine reflects the diverse agricultural products of the area.  当地菜肴的强烈区域性反映了该地区多样化的农产品。|近义词：locality, provincialism, localism |反义词：universality, globalism

词源:
The word "regionality" is derived from the word "region," which comes from the Latin word "regio," meaning "district" or "tract of land."  The suffix "-ality" is added to create a noun meaning "the quality or state of being regional."
