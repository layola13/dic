misquoted:/ˌmɪsˈkwoʊtɪd/| adj.| 引用错误的；被误引的 |  Something that is misquoted has been quoted incorrectly, either accidentally or deliberately.  It refers to a statement or passage that is reproduced inaccurately. | Example: The article misquoted the senator's statement on immigration. 这篇文章错误地引用了参议员关于移民的声明。  |近义词：misrepresented, paraphrased incorrectly, wrongly cited | 反义词：accurately quoted, correctly cited |
