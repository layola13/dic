emotivity:/ˌiːməʊˈtɪvɪti/| n. | 情感性；易动感情；情感表达|The capacity to experience and express emotions; the quality of being emotive.  It often refers to the degree to which a word, phrase, or text evokes emotion in the reader or listener.  |例句：The poem was full of emotivity, leaving the reader deeply moved. 这首诗充满了情感，使读者深受感动。

The author's emotivity was evident in the passionate tone of the novel. 作者的情感在小说热情的基调中显而易见。|近义词：emotionality, expressiveness, feeling, sentimentality |反义词：apathy, indifference, stoicism
