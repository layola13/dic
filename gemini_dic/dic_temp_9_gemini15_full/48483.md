deep-water:/ˈdiːpˌwɔːtər/| adj.|深水的；深海的 |Relating to or occurring in deep water, typically at depths exceeding 200 meters.  The term often implies significant depth and associated characteristics like high pressure, low light, and unique ecosystems. |例句：Deep-water fishing is a challenging but rewarding occupation. 深海捕鱼是一项充满挑战但又令人满意的职业。

例句：The submarine explored the mysteries of the deep-water trenches. 潜艇探索了深海海沟的奥秘。

近义词：abyssal, oceanic, profound

反义词：shallow-water, coastal, superficial

词性:形容词
