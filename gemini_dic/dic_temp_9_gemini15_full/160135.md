英文解析：Pompeii was an ancient Roman city near modern Naples, Italy.  It was famously destroyed and buried by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD.  The city's remarkably well-preserved ruins offer a unique glimpse into Roman life.  The eruption preserved buildings, artifacts, and even the bodies of victims, providing invaluable historical information.


例句：

The eruption of Mount Vesuvius completely buried Pompeii.  维苏威火山爆发彻底掩埋了庞贝城。

Tourists from all over the world visit the ruins of Pompeii every year.  每年都有来自世界各地的游客参观庞贝古城的遗址。


词源：The name Pompeii is believed to be of Oscan origin, a language spoken in ancient Italy before Latin became dominant.  The exact meaning is uncertain, with suggestions ranging from "five" to a reference to a particular tribe or settlement.  The city's name has remained largely unchanged throughout history.
