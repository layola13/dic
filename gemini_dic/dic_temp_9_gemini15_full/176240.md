re-centre:/ˌriːˈsɛntər/| vt.|重新定位；重新以…为中心；使重新集中精力|To reposition something or someone at the center or to refocus one's attention or efforts on a central point.|例句：The company is re-centering its marketing strategy on the younger demographic. 公司正在将其营销策略重新定位在年轻人群体上。

例句：After the crisis, the government decided to re-centre its efforts on economic recovery. 危机过后，政府决定将工作重点重新放在经济复苏上。

近义词：refocus, reposition, realign|反义词：decentralize, disperse

词性：动词
