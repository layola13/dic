rationals:/ˈræʃənəlz/| n. |有理数；理性的人|Rational numbers are numbers that can be expressed as a fraction p/q, where p and q are integers and q is not zero.  有理数是可以表示为分数p/q的数，其中p和q是整数，q不为零。|例句：The set of rational numbers includes all integers and fractions. 有理数集包括所有整数和分数。|近义词：reasonables, intellecturals|反义词： irrationals
