decadence:/ˈdɛkədəns/| n.|颓废；衰落；堕落|The state of being decadent; moral or cultural decline.|The decadence of the Roman Empire led to its downfall. 罗马帝国的衰败导致了它的灭亡。|decline, corruption, degeneration|progress, improvement, prosperity|名词
