deutoplastic:/ˌdjuːtəʊˈplæstɪk/ | adj. |  兼具延展性和可塑性的；双重可塑的 |  Describing a material or substance that exhibits both ductility (ability to be drawn into wires) and plasticity (ability to be molded or shaped).  It implies a higher degree of malleability than simply plastic or ductile alone. |  The deutoplastic alloy showed excellent formability in both drawing and stamping processes.  这种双重可塑合金在拉拔和冲压过程中表现出优异的成形性。| 近义词:  malleable, ductile, plastic | 反义词: brittle, rigid
