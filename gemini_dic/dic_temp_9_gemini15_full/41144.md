copublishes:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃɪz/| vt.|合作出版|To publish jointly; to publish something in collaboration with another publisher or entity.  This implies a shared effort in the production and distribution of a publication.|例句：Oxford University Press copublishes several journals with other academic presses. 牛津大学出版社与其他学术出版社合作出版了几种期刊。|例句：The two companies copublished a groundbreaking book on climate change. 这两家公司合作出版了一本关于气候变化的开创性著作。|近义词：jointly publish, collaborate to publish|反义词：solely publish, individually publish|动词
