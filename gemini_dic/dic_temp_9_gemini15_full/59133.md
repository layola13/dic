dynamiter:/ˈdaɪnəmaɪtər/| n. |爆破者；使用炸药的人|A dynamiter is a person who uses dynamite to blast rocks, demolish buildings, or engage in other explosive activities.  Dynamiters require specialized training and licensing due to the inherent dangers of handling explosives.|例句：The experienced dynamiter carefully placed the charges before initiating the blast.  经验丰富的爆破工人在引爆炸药前小心地放置了炸药。

The careless dynamiter caused an unexpected explosion.  粗心的爆破工导致了一次意外爆炸。|近义词：blaster, demolitionist, saboteur (if used for malicious purposes)|反义词：builder (in the context of construction)|词性:名词
