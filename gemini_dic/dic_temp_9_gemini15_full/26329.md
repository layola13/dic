buzz-cut:/ˈbʌz kʌt/| n. |平头，寸头 |A buzz cut is a very short haircut, where the hair is cut very close to the scalp using electric clippers.  It's a style characterized by its uniformity and lack of layering. |Example: He got a buzz cut to beat the summer heat. 他理了个寸头来对抗夏日的酷暑。|近义词：crew cut, butch cut |反义词：long hair, shaggy hair
