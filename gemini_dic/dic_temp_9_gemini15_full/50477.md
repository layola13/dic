depressing:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋ/| adj.|令人沮丧的，使人抑郁的|causing feelings of sadness and hopelessness;  making someone feel unhappy and without hope.|The depressing news made her cry.  令人沮丧的消息让她哭了。 The gray weather was depressing.  阴沉的天气令人沮丧。|sad, gloomy, disheartening, discouraging|uplifting, cheerful, encouraging, inspiring|形容词
