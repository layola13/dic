overtopple:/ˌoʊvərˈtɑːpəl/ | vt. |推翻；使倾覆；使垮台 |To cause (something, especially a government or system) to fall or be overthrown.  |例句：The dictator was overtoppled in a military coup. 独裁者在军事政变中被推翻了。 |例句：The high winds overtoppled several trees. 大风刮倒了几棵树。|近义词：overthrow, overturn, topple, subvert |反义词：establish, support, uphold |动词
