revealed: /rɪˈviːld/ | vt. |揭露；展现；显示| To make (something) known that was previously secret or unknown.  |例句：The investigation revealed a shocking truth.  调查揭露了一个令人震惊的真相。|例句：The artist revealed his masterpiece to the public.  艺术家向公众展示了他的杰作。|近义词：disclosed, unveiled, uncovered, exposed |反义词：concealed, hidden, obscured |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist reveals his findings to the world. = 科学家向全世界公布了他的发现。
She reveals her secrets to her best friend. = 她向她最好的朋友吐露了她的秘密。


一般过去时:
He revealed the truth about the accident. = 他揭露了事故的真相。
The letter revealed a long-lost connection. = 这封信揭示了一个失散已久的联系。


现在完成时:
She has revealed all her cards. = 她已经摊牌了。
The investigation has revealed a network of corruption. = 调查已经揭露了一个腐败网络。


过去完成时:
They had revealed their plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就透露了他们的计划。
It had been revealed that he was a spy. = 已经有人揭露他是间谍了。


将来时:
The company will reveal its new product next month. = 公司将于下个月发布其新产品。
He will reveal the secret at the right time. = 他会在适当的时候揭露这个秘密。


虚拟语气:
If he revealed the truth, he would be in danger. = 如果他揭露真相，他会很危险。
I wish he had revealed the truth earlier. = 我希望他早点揭露真相。


被动语态:
The truth was revealed to the public. = 真相被公诸于世。
The secret was revealed by an anonymous source. = 这个秘密是由一个匿名消息来源透露的。


疑问句:
When will the results be revealed? = 结果什么时候公布？
What will the investigation reveal? = 调查会揭露什么？


否定句:
He did not reveal his identity. = 他没有透露他的身份。
The company has not yet revealed its plans. = 公司尚未公布其计划。


PHRASE->
