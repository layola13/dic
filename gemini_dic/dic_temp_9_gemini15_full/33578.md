cilician:/sɪˈlɪʃən/| adj.|西里西亚的；西里西亚人的|Of or relating to Cilicia, a historical region in southern Asia Minor (modern-day southern Turkey).  The term can refer to the people, language, or culture of Cilicia.|例句：The Cilician Gates were a strategically important pass. 西里西亚门是一个具有战略意义的山口。|近义词：south Anatolian (in a geographical context)|反义词：None specifically.
