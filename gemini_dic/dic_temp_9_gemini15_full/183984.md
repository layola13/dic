reshingling: /ˌriːˈʃɪŋɡlɪŋ/ | v. | 重新盖瓦，重新铺盖 | The act of replacing old shingles on a roof.  This involves removing the old shingles and installing new ones. | The house needs reshingling; the roof is leaking.  这房子需要重新盖瓦；屋顶漏水了。 | reroofing, retiling |  (no direct antonym, but) leaving the roof unrepaired.
