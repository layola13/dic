maladroitly:/ˌmæləˈdrɔɪtli/| adv.|笨拙地，不灵巧地|in a clumsy or awkward way; without skill or dexterity.|例句：He maladroitly dropped the vase, shattering it on the floor. 他笨手笨脚地把花瓶掉了，摔碎在地上。

例句：She maladroitly attempted to fix the computer, making the problem worse. 她笨拙地试图修理电脑，结果把问题弄得更糟了。


近义词：awkwardly, clumsily, ineptly, ungracefully


反义词：skillfully, deftly, adroitly, gracefully


词性: 副词
