/ˈhæpən/| v.|发生，出现|To take place; to occur.  It often describes events that are unplanned or unexpected.|The accident happened on a rainy Tuesday.  事故发生在一个雨天的星期二。|  A strange thing happened on the way to the forum. 在去论坛的路上发生了一件奇怪的事。|occur, take place, transpire|cease, stop.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What happens next? = 接下来的事情会发生什么？
Something unexpected always happens to him.  一些意想不到的事情总是发生在他身上。


一般过去时:
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？
A terrible accident happened last night. 昨晚发生了一起可怕的事故。


现在完成时:
What has happened to him? = 他发生了什么事？
Nothing much has happened since I saw you last. 自从上次见到你以来，没有什么大事发生。


过去完成时:
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？
The fire had already happened when the firefighters arrived. 当消防员到达时，火灾已经发生了。


将来时:
What will happen tomorrow? = 明天会发生什么？
I don't know what will happen in the future. 我不知道将来会发生什么。


虚拟语气:
If that happened, I would be very surprised. = 如果那样的事情发生了，我会非常惊讶。
I wish this hadn't happened. 我希望这件事没有发生。


被动语态:
The accident was reported to have happened at noon. 事故据报道发生在中午。
It happened that he was chosen.  他被选中了。（强调偶然性）


疑问句:
What happened? = 发生了什么？
How did that happen? = 那是怎么发生的？


否定句:
Nothing happened. = 什么事也没发生。
That didn't happen. 那没有发生。


PHRASE->
what happened? = 发生什么事了？
as it happened = 碰巧
it so happened that = 碰巧的是
happen to = 碰巧
what if...? = 如果...将会怎样？
