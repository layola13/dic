grosser:/ˈɡroʊsər/| n. |杂货商，批发商|A grosser is someone who buys and sells goods in large quantities, often wholesale. They are typically involved in the distribution of food and other goods to retailers.|Example: The local grocer supplied fresh produce to all the neighborhood stores.  本地杂货商向所有邻里商店供应新鲜农产品。|近义词：wholesaler, merchant, dealer|反义词：retailer (in some contexts)|名词

词源:
The word "grocer" comes from the Old French word "grossier," meaning "wholesale dealer," which in turn is derived from the Old French word "gros," meaning "large" or "great."  This reflects the historical role of grocers as dealers in large quantities of goods.
