Many common fish species are malacopterygious. | Example:  Many commercially important fish are malacopterygious species.  许多重要的商业鱼类都是软鳍鱼类。  The malacopterygious fishes of the lake are diverse and abundant. 湖中软鳍鱼种类繁多，数量丰富。|近义词：soft-finned |反义词：acanthopterygian (硬鳍的)
