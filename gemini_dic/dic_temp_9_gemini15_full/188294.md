rub-down:/ˈrʌbdaʊn/| n. v.|按摩，擦拭|A rub-down is a vigorous rubbing of the body, often with oil or lotion, to relax muscles or improve circulation.  It can also refer to the act of wiping or cleaning something vigorously.|例句：After the marathon, I treated myself to a relaxing rub-down.  马拉松之后，我给自己做了个放松的按摩。

例句：He gave the car a good rub-down to remove the dust. 他用力擦拭汽车以去除灰尘。|近义词：massage,  scrub, wipe|反义词：neglect, ignore|词性：n. v.
