outback:/ˈaʊtˌbæk/| n.|澳大利亚内陆地区；偏远地区|The remote and often arid inland regions of Australia, characterized by sparsely populated areas and vast distances.  It often refers to areas beyond the settled coastal regions.|例句：We took a trip to the outback and saw kangaroos and emus. 我们去内陆旅行，看到了袋鼠和鸸鹋。
例句：Life in the outback is challenging due to the harsh climate.  内陆地区的生活由于恶劣的气候而充满挑战。|近义词：bush, backcountry, wilderness|反义词：city, metropolis, urban area
