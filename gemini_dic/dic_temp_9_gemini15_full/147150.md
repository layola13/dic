overlording: /ˌoʊvərˈlɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 霸权统治；过度统治 | The act of exercising excessive control or authority, often in a tyrannical or oppressive manner.  It implies a domineering influence over a large area or group of people. |例句：The overlording of the empire led to widespread resentment and rebellion.  帝国的过度统治导致了广泛的怨恨和叛乱。

The relentless overlording of the corporation stifled innovation and creativity among its employees. 公司无情的过度统治扼杀了员工的创新和创造力。| 近义词：domination, tyranny, oppression, subjugation | 反义词：liberation, emancipation, freedom
