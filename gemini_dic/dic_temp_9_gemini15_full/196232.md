serape:/səˈreɪpi/ | n. |沙丽(一种墨西哥披肩)| A serape is a type of woolen blanket or shawl, typically brightly colored and patterned, worn especially in Mexico.  It's often woven with intricate designs and traditionally used as both clothing and a practical item for warmth. | The woman draped a vibrant serape around her shoulders to ward off the evening chill.  这位女士披上色彩鲜艳的沙丽来抵御夜晚的寒意。| shawl, poncho |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
