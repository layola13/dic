prebendary:/ˈpribəndəri/| n. |教区牧师，享有教区收入者|A prebendary is a member of the clergy who holds a prebend, which is a stipend or benefice endowed with land or other property, providing an income.  Historically, prebends were often associated with cathedral chapters.
