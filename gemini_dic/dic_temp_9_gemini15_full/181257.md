reinstates:/ˌriːɪnˈsteɪt/| vt.|恢复职位；恢复；使复职|To restore someone or something to their former position or state.|The company reinstated him after the investigation cleared him. 公司在调查证明他清白后恢复了他的职位。|近义词：restore, reinstate, reestablish|反义词：remove, dismiss, discharge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinstates the old policy. 他恢复了旧政策。
The court reinstates her driving license. 法院恢复了她的驾照。


一般过去时:
They reinstated the employee after the strike. 罢工后，他们恢复了该员工的职位。
The judge reinstated the verdict. 法官恢复了判决。


现在完成时:
The government has reinstated the tax. 政府已经恢复了这项税收。
She has reinstated her membership in the club. 她已经恢复了她在俱乐部的会员资格。


过去完成时:
They had reinstated the program before the budget cuts. 预算削减之前，他们已经恢复了该项目。
The company had reinstated his position before he appealed.  在他上诉之前，公司已经恢复了他的职位。


将来时:
The committee will reinstate the rule next week. 委员会下周将恢复这项规定。
He will reinstate the order immediately. 他将立即恢复秩序。


虚拟语气:
If they reinstated the law, crime would increase. 如果他们恢复这项法律，犯罪就会增加。
I wish they would reinstate the old system. 我希望他们能恢复旧制度。


被动语态:
The old system was reinstated last year. 旧制度去年被恢复了。
His driving license was reinstated after the appeal.  他的驾照在上诉后被恢复了。


疑问句:
Will they reinstate the funding? 他们会恢复资金吗？
Has the judge reinstated the verdict?  法官是否恢复了判决？


否定句:
They did not reinstate the employee. 他们没有恢复该员工的职位。
The school did not reinstate the program. 学校没有恢复该项目。
