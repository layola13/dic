reoiling:/ˌriːˈɔɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 再加油；重新加油 | The act of applying oil again, typically to machinery or wood.  | Example: The reoiling of the engine restored its smooth operation.  重新给发动机加油使它恢复了平稳运行。 | 近义词：re-lubrication, oiling anew | 反义词：de-oiling, drying


USAGE->

The mechanic is reoiling the machine. = 机械师正在给机器重新加油。
She reoils her wooden furniture annually. = 她每年都会给她的木制家具重新上油。

一般过去时:
They reoiled the car engine after the race. = 比赛结束后，他们给汽车发动机重新加了油。
He reoiled the squeaky hinges. = 他给吱吱作响的门铰重新上了油。
