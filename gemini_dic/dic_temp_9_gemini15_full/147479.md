overoptimists:/ˌoʊvərˈɒptɪmɪsts/| n. |过度乐观者|People who are excessively optimistic; those whose optimism is unrealistic or unfounded.  They tend to overestimate the likelihood of positive outcomes and underestimate the possibility of negative ones.|The overoptimists in the investment group lost a significant portion of their savings.|近义词：unrealistic optimists, Pollyannas |反义词：pessimists, realists
