informers:/ɪnˈfɔːrmərz/ | n. |告密者，线人 | A person who gives information to the authorities, especially about a crime.  |例句：The police relied heavily on informers to gather intelligence about the gang.  警方严重依赖线人来收集有关该团伙的情报。

例句：Several informers came forward with evidence against the corrupt officials.  几位告密者站出来提供证据指控腐败官员。


近义词：whistle-blowers, stool pigeons, snitches | 反义词：无
