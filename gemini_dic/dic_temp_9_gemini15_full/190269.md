saprophytic:/ˌsæprəˈfɪtɪk/ | adj. |腐生的；腐物寄生性的 |  Obtaining nourishment from dead organic matter.  Saprophytic organisms, such as fungi and some bacteria, play a crucial role in the decomposition of organic materials in ecosystems. | 例句：Many fungi are saprophytic, breaking down dead plants and animals.  许多真菌是腐生的，分解动植物的尸体。

Many bacteria are saprophytic, contributing to the nutrient cycle. 许多细菌是腐生的，有助于养分循环。| 近义词：saprobiotic, decomposing | 反义词：parasitic, autotrophic
