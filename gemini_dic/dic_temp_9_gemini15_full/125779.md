murenoid:/ˈmjʊərənɔɪd/| adj.|类菌丝体的；菌丝状的|Resembling a fungus in structure or growth habit, characterized by filamentous or thread-like structures.  The term is often used in biology and microbiology to describe structures that are similar to fungal hyphae but may not be of fungal origin.|例句：The bacterium exhibited a murenoid cell wall structure.  这种细菌表现出类菌丝体的细胞壁结构。|例句：The researcher observed murenoid filaments under the microscope. 研究人员在显微镜下观察到类菌丝体丝状体。|近义词：filamentous, mycelial, hypha-like|反义词：amorphous, non-filamentous
