fortified:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪd/|/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪd/| adj.| 加固的；设防的；强化了的 | Strengthened; made more secure or effective.  Fortified foods have added vitamins or minerals. | The castle walls were fortified against attack.  城堡的城墙经过加固以抵御攻击。| The fortified wine has a higher alcohol content.  这种强化葡萄酒的酒精含量更高。|  reinforced, strengthened | undefended, weakened, vulnerable |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fortified the walls of his castle. = 他加固了他城堡的城墙。
She fortifies her cereal with extra vitamins. = 她用额外的维生素强化她的麦片。

一般过去时:
They fortified the position with extra troops. = 他们用额外的军队加强了阵地。
The Romans fortified many towns along the border. = 罗马人在边境沿线加固了许多城镇。

现在完成时:
The government has fortified the national defense system. = 政府已经加强了国防系统。
She has fortified herself with a large cup of coffee. = 她喝了一大杯咖啡来提神。

过去完成时:
They had fortified the bridge before the enemy arrived. = 敌人到达之前，他们已经加固了桥梁。
The villagers had fortified their homes against the approaching storm. = 村民们已经加固了他们的房屋以抵御即将到来的风暴。

将来时:
We will fortify the building against earthquakes. = 我们将加固建筑物以抵御地震。
The army will fortify the strategic positions. = 军队将加固战略要地。

虚拟语气:
If they had fortified the city walls, the enemy would not have broken through. = 如果他们加固了城墙，敌人就不会突破了。
I wish I had fortified myself against the cold. = 我真希望我当时已经做好防寒准备了。

被动语态:
The city was fortified by the king's army. = 这个城市被国王的军队加固了。
The food is fortified with essential nutrients. = 这种食物添加了必需的营养素。

疑问句:
Were the walls fortified? = 城墙加固了吗？
Is the drink fortified with alcohol? = 这种饮料添加了酒精吗？

否定句:
They did not fortify the building properly. = 他们没有正确地加固建筑物。
The soldiers didn't fortify the bridge. = 士兵们没有加固桥梁。
