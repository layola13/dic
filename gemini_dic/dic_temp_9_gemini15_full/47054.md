/ˈdeɪtə/ | n. | 数据 | Data refers to collections of facts, figures, or other information that can be processed or analyzed.  It is often used in the plural form. | The research team analyzed the data to draw conclusions.  研究团队分析了数据以得出结论。  | information, statistics, figures |  (无明确反义词，可以根据语境用lack of information等表达) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data shows a clear trend. = 数据显示了一个清晰的趋势。
This data supports our hypothesis. = 这些数据支持我们的假设。

一般过去时:
The data revealed a surprising result. = 数据揭示了一个令人惊讶的结果。
The scientists collected and analyzed the data. = 科学家们收集并分析了数据。

现在完成时:
We have collected all the necessary data. = 我们已经收集了所有必要的数据。
They have analyzed the data and presented their findings. = 他们已经分析了数据并展示了他们的发现。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, we had processed all the data. = 在演示之前，我们已经处理了所有数据。
The team had reviewed the data multiple times. = 团队多次审查了数据。

将来时:
We will collect the data next week. = 我们下周将收集数据。
The results will be based on the data obtained. = 结果将基于获得的数据。

虚拟语气:
If we had better data, the results would be more accurate. = 如果我们有更好的数据，结果会更准确。
I wish we had more data to work with. = 我希望我们有更多的数据可以处理。

被动语态:
The data was analyzed using statistical software. = 数据使用统计软件进行分析。
The results are being affected by inaccurate data. = 结果正受到不准确数据的影响。

疑问句:
What data do we have available? = 我们有什么可用数据？
Where can we find the relevant data? = 我们在哪里可以找到相关数据？

否定句:
The data does not support the theory. = 数据不支持该理论。
We don't have enough data to make a conclusion. = 我们没有足够的数据来下结论。
