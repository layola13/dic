cunningest:/ˈkʌnɪŋɪst/| adj.|最狡猾的，最精明的|Most skillful and clever, especially in a deceitful way.  It is the superlative form of "cunning".|例句：The cunningest fox in the forest outwitted the hunters. 森林里最狡猾的狐狸智胜了猎人。

例句：He employed the cunningest of tactics to win the game. 他使用了最狡猾的策略赢得比赛。


近义词：most shrewd, most sly, most crafty, most astute|反义词：most naive, most ingenuous, most straightforward
