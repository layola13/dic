greet:/ɡriːt/| /ɡriːt/ | vt. |问候；迎接 |To say hello to someone; to welcome someone.  |例句：She greeted me warmly at the door. 她热情地在门口迎接我。|例句：He greeted his guests with a smile. 他笑着迎接他的客人。|近义词：welcome, salute, hail |反义词：ignore, snub


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I greet my friends every morning. = 我每天早上都向我的朋友们问好。
She greets her customers with a smile. = 她面带微笑地迎接顾客。

一般过去时:
He greeted me at the airport. = 他在机场迎接我。
They greeted their parents with gifts. = 他们用礼物迎接他们的父母。

现在完成时:
I have greeted all the guests. = 我已经问候了所有的客人。
She has greeted the new employee. = 她已经问候了新员工。

过去完成时:
They had greeted the speaker before the presentation began. = 演讲开始前，他们已经向演讲者问候过了。
I had already greeted him when he arrived. = 当他到达时，我已经向他问候过了。

将来时:
I will greet him when he arrives. = 当他到达时，我会向他问候。
She will greet the audience before her speech. = 她将在演讲前向观众致意。

虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would greet him politely. = 如果我看到他，我会礼貌地向他问候。
I wish I had greeted her earlier. = 我真希望我早点向她问候。

被动语态:
The guests were greeted warmly. = 客人们受到了热烈的欢迎。
The new employee was greeted by the manager. = 新员工受到了经理的迎接。

疑问句:
Did you greet your teacher? = 你向你的老师问好了吗？
How did you greet your family? = 你是怎么向你的家人问候的？

否定句:
I didn't greet him. = 我没有向他问候。
She didn't greet her boss this morning. = 今天早上她没有向她的老板问候。
