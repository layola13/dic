phlegm: /ˈflem/ | n. | 痰，粘液 | Thick, viscous secretion from the mucous membranes of the respiratory tract.  It is often expelled by coughing. | Example: He coughed up a large amount of phlegm. 他咳出一大口痰。|近义词：mucus, sputum |反义词：dryness
