charbroiled:/ˈtʃɑːrbroʊld/| adj.|炭烤的，烧焦的| Cooked over hot coals until the surface is charred.  Charbroiling involves cooking food directly over hot coals or a very hot grill, resulting in a distinctive smoky flavor and charred exterior.|例句：The charbroiled steak was delicious.  这块炭烤牛排真好吃。 The charbroiled chicken was slightly burnt. 这只炭烤鸡有点烧焦了。|近义词：grilled, barbecued, broiled|反义词：boiled, steamed
