journeymen:/ˈdʒɜːrnɪmən/| n.|熟练工人，技工|A journeyman is a skilled worker who has completed an apprenticeship and is now fully qualified to work independently.  They are typically employed by others, rather than being self-employed.|He's a journeyman carpenter, skilled in all aspects of the trade.|近义词：craftsman, artisan, tradesman|反义词：apprentice, novice
