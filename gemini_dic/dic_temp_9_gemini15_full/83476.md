hack-down:/hækdɑːn/| vt.|砍倒；破坏；使…崩溃|To cut down (a tree, etc.) with an axe or similar tool; to forcefully disrupt or destroy something; to cause something (such as a system or program) to fail.|The lumberjacks hacked down the ancient redwood trees.  伐木工人砍倒了古老的红杉树。They hacked down the website with a denial-of-service attack. 他们用拒绝服务攻击破坏了网站。|cut down, fell, demolish, disable|repair, build, restore, enable|动词
