inquorate: /ɪnˈkwɔːrət/ | adj. | 无权力的；未达到法定人数的；无效的 | Lacking the authority or power to act;  not having the required quorum.  The term often refers to a meeting or assembly that lacks the minimum number of members needed to conduct valid business.  | 例句：The meeting was inquorate because not enough board members were present.  = 由于出席的董事会成员不足，会议无法召开。The decision was declared inquorate due to procedural irregularities. = 由于程序上的不规范，该决定被宣布无效。| 近义词：invalid, unauthorized, powerless, ineffective | 反义词：valid, authorized, empowered, effective |
