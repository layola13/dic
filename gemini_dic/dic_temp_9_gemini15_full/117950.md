membranoid:/ˈmɛmbrənɔɪd/ | adj. | 膜状的，似膜的 | Resembling a membrane in structure or function.  The term often describes structures that are thin, sheet-like, and may have a selectively permeable nature, but are not true biological membranes. | Example: The membranoid structure of the cell wall provided some protection against dehydration.  膜状的细胞壁结构提供了一些防止脱水的保护。|近义词：membrane-like, film-like | 反义词：thick, solid
