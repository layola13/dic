pathognomy:/pæθˈɒɡnəmi/ | n. | 特征病理学；(疾病的)特异症状 | The study of the characteristic symptoms of a disease.  It refers to a sign or symptom that is so specific to a particular disease that its presence virtually confirms the diagnosis. | Example: A high fever and characteristic rash can be pathognomonic of measles.  高烧和特征性皮疹可能是麻疹的特征性症状。|近义词：diagnostic sign, specific symptom |反义词：nonspecific symptom
