laurite:/ˈlɔːraɪt/| n. |硫化钌矿|A rare mineral consisting of ruthenium sulfide (RuS2). It is a source of ruthenium, a platinum group metal.  Laurite is typically found in sulfide ores associated with platinum group metals. Its crystals are usually small and cubic in shape. |例句：Laurite is an important ore of ruthenium. 硫化钌矿是钌的重要矿石。
