hash-out: /ˈhæʃ aʊt/ | vt. | 详细讨论；解决；消除；详尽解释 | To discuss something thoroughly in order to reach a solution or understanding; to resolve or eliminate something; to explain something in detail. |例句：We need to hash out the details of the contract before signing. 我们需要在签合同之前详细讨论合同细节。

They hashed out their differences and reached an agreement. 他们解决了分歧并达成了一致。


近义词：resolve, iron out, work out, clarify, elucidate | 反义词：ignore, neglect, gloss over
