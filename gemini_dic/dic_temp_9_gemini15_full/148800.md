overvaluing: /ˌoʊvərˈvæljuɪŋ/ | v. | 过高评价；高估 | To place too high a value on something; to overestimate the worth or importance of something.  |  例句：He's overvaluing his own abilities. 他过高估计了自己的能力。


例句：The company is overvaluing its assets.  这家公司高估了自己的资产。


近义词：overestimating, exaggerating, inflating, overpricing


反义词：undervaluing, underestimating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overvalue their contributions to the project. = 他们高估了他们对项目的贡献。
We overvalue the importance of material possessions. = 我们过高估计了物质财富的重要性。


一般过去时:
The investors overvalued the startup's potential. = 投资者高估了这家初创公司的潜力。
She overvalued his loyalty and trust him too much. = 她高估了他的忠诚，过于信任他。


现在完成时:
The market has overvalued tech stocks for years. = 多年来，市场一直高估了科技股的价值。
He has overvalued his skills in interviews, leading to disappointment. = 他在面试中高估了自己的技能，导致失望。


过去完成时:
They had overvalued the property before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经高估了该房产的价值。
The board had overvalued the company's prospects, leading to a poor investment decision. = 董事会高估了公司的前景，导致投资决策失误。


将来时:
Analysts predict that the market will overvalue this new cryptocurrency. = 分析师预测，市场将高估这种新的加密货币。
They fear the government will overvalue the national currency. = 他们担心政府会高估本国货币。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overvalued my abilities, I would have applied for the job. = 如果我没有高估自己的能力，我就会申请这份工作。
If they hadn't overvalued the risk, they would have invested. = 如果他们没有高估风险，他们就会投资。


被动语态:
The antique was overvalued by the auction house. = 这件古董被拍卖行高估了。
The company's stock has been overvalued by the market for some time. = 这家公司的股票已经被市场高估了一段时间。


疑问句:
Do you think the appraisers overvalued the artwork? = 你认为评估师是否高估了这件艺术品的价值？
Are we overvaluing the importance of these metrics? = 我们是否过高估计了这些指标的重要性？


否定句:
They did not overvalue the risks involved in the venture. = 他们没有高估这项风险投资所涉及的风险。
The team didn't overvalue its chances of winning the competition. = 该团队没有高估自己赢得比赛的机会。
