grapes:/ɡreɪps/| n. |葡萄|Grapes are a type of fruit that grow in bunches on vines.  They come in various colors, including green, red, and purple, and have a sweet taste. They are often eaten fresh, used in winemaking, or made into juice.|例句：We bought a bunch of grapes at the farmer's market. 我们在农贸市场买了一串葡萄。

例句：The wine is made from locally grown grapes. 这葡萄酒是用当地种植的葡萄酿造的。|近义词：berries|反义词：无
