manga:/ˈmæŋɡə/ | n. | 漫画 | Japanese comics, typically published in black and white, often serialized in magazines.  Manga encompasses a wide range of genres, including action, adventure, romance, comedy, horror, and science fiction.  | 例句：I love reading manga; my favorite is One Piece. 我喜欢看漫画，我最喜欢的是海贼王。| 近义词：comics, graphic novel | 反义词：None
