midsummer:/ˈmɪdsʌmər/| n. |仲夏，夏至 |Midsummer refers to the middle of summer, often specifically associated with the summer solstice (around June 21st in the Northern Hemisphere). It's a time traditionally associated with festivals and celebrations in many cultures.|例句：We celebrated Midsummer with a bonfire on the beach. 我们在海滩上燃起了篝火庆祝仲夏节。|近义词：midsummer's day, summer solstice|反义词：midwinter
