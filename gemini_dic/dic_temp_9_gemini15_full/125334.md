multilateralist: /ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪst/ | n. | 多边主义者；多边论者 | A person who supports or advocates for multilateralism (the principle of cooperation among multiple countries).  A multilateralist believes in international cooperation and agreements to solve global problems.  |  The foreign minister is a strong multilateralist, advocating for international cooperation on climate change.  这位外交部长是坚定的多边主义者，主张在气候变化问题上开展国际合作。 |  internationalist, globalist | isolationist, unilateralist | 名词
