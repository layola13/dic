一般现在时:
The lightning flashes brightly.  闪电明亮地闪烁着。
The sun flashes through the clouds. 太阳透过云层闪耀。

一般过去时:
The camera flashed brightly. 相机闪光灯一闪一闪的。
A brilliant idea flashed into my mind. 一个绝妙的主意突然出现在我的脑海里。

现在完成时:
The firefly has flashed many times tonight. 萤火虫今晚已经闪了几次了。
The news has flashed across the world. 这则新闻已经传遍了全世界。


过去完成时:
The light had flashed before the accident. 事故发生前灯闪了一下。
He had flashed his ID before entering the building. 他在进入大楼前亮出了他的身份证。

将来时:
The lights will flash when the train arrives. 火车到达时，灯光将会闪烁。
The news will flash on all channels.  这条新闻将在所有频道上发布。

虚拟语气:
If the light flashed, I would have seen it. 如果灯闪了，我会看到的。
I wish the warning light had flashed sooner. 我希望警告灯能早点闪烁。

被动语态:
The pictures were flashed onto the screen. 图片被快速地投射到屏幕上。
The news was flashed to the public.  新闻被迅速发布给公众。

否定句:
