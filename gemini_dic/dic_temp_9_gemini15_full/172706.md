/kwɑːr/ | n. |二维码，快速响应码 | A QR code (quick response code) is a type of matrix barcode (or two-dimensional barcode) readable by dedicated QR barcode readers and camera phones.  It's used to provide easy access to information via a smartphone. | Example:  Scan the QR code to access the Wi-Fi password. 扫描二维码以访问WiFi密码。 |近义词：matrix barcode, 2D barcode |反义词：无
