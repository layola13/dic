largish:/ˈlɑːrdʒɪʃ/ | adj. |  ziemlich groß, ziemlich umfangreich | Somewhat large; fairly large.  The word implies a size that is larger than average but not exceptionally so. It suggests a degree of approximation or estimation rather than precise measurement. |例句：He has a largish house in the suburbs. 他在郊区有一栋相当大的房子。

例句：She bought a largish bag to carry all her shopping. 她买了一个相当大的包来装她所有的购物品。


近义词： sizable, fairly large, reasonably large, considerable


反义词： tiny, small, minute


词性：形容词
