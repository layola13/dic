coachmen:/ˈkoʊtʃmən/ | n. |马车夫|A coachman is a person whose job is to drive a coach (a type of horse-drawn carriage).  They were a common sight in many parts of the world before the advent of automobiles.  Their skills included handling horses, navigating roads, and caring for the coach itself.|The coachman expertly guided the horses through the crowded streets.  马车夫熟练地驾驭着马匹穿过拥挤的街道。|charioteer, driver|pedestrian, passenger|名词
