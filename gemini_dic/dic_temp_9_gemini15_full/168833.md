proelectrification: /ˌproʊɪˌlɛktrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |  支持电气化；促进电气化 | The advocacy or support for increased use of electricity.  It encompasses the expansion of electrical infrastructure and the transition to electric-powered systems. | 例句：The government's proelectrification policies aim to reduce carbon emissions. = 政府的支持电气化政策旨在减少碳排放。

The growing awareness of climate change has fueled a global push for proelectrification initiatives. =  对气候变化日益增长的认识推动了全球对支持电气化倡议的努力。


近义词：electrification promotion,  electrical expansion | 反义词：anti-electrification,  de-electrification | 词性：名词
