polyurethanes:/ˌpɒliˈjʊərɪθeɪnz/ | n. |聚氨酯 |A family of synthetic polymers characterized by the presence of urethane groups (-NH-COO-) in their structure. They are known for their versatility and are used in a wide range of applications. | Example: Polyurethanes are used in the production of foams, coatings, and elastomers. 聚氨酯用于生产泡沫、涂料和弹性体。|近义词:polymers, plastics |反义词:N/A
