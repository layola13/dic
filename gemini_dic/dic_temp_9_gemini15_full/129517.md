non-attendance: /ˌnɒn əˈtɛndəns/ | n. |缺席，不到场 |The absence of someone from a place or event where their presence is expected.  It often implies a failure to attend, rather than a simple absence due to unavoidable circumstances. |例句：His non-attendance at the meeting raised concerns.  他的缺席引起了担忧。 |例句：The teacher noted the student's repeated non-attendance. 老师注意到学生屡次缺席。|近义词：absence, non-participation |反义词：attendance, presence |名词
