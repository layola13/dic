insomnia:/ɪnˈsɒmniə/ | n. |失眠症，失眠|The inability to sleep.  A sleep disorder characterized by difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or both, leading to poor quality sleep.  This can result in daytime tiredness and impairment of functioning.|例句：She suffers from chronic insomnia and often feels exhausted during the day. 她患有慢性失眠症，白天经常感到疲惫不堪。

例句：His insomnia is made worse by stress and anxiety. 他的失眠症因压力和焦虑而加重。

近义词：sleeplessness, sleep disorder

反义词：sound sleep
