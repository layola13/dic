overclaimed:/ˌoʊvərˈkleɪmd/| v. | 夸大索赔；过分要求 | To claim more than is true or justified; to exaggerate a claim.  |例句：The company overclaimed the effectiveness of its new product, leading to customer disappointment.  该公司夸大了其新产品的功效，导致客户失望。

例句：He overclaimed his contributions to the project, taking credit for work done by others. 他夸大了自己对项目的贡献，把别人的功劳据为己有。


近义词：exaggerate, inflate, overstate, embellish | 反义词：understate, downplay


词源：over- (prefix indicating excess) + claim (verb meaning to state as a fact)
