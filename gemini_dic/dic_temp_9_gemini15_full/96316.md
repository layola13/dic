indigestibles: /ˌɪnˈdɪdʒɛstəblz/ | n. |难以消化的食物；难以理解的事物 |  Things that cannot be easily digested or understood.  |The steak was indigestible, leaving me feeling bloated and uncomfortable.  这块牛排很难消化，让我感到腹胀和不舒服。| digestible | digestible |

The instructions are indigestible; I can't understand them. = 说明书难以理解，我看不懂。

词源:
From "in-" (not) + "digestible" (able to be digested).  "Digestible" comes from the Latin "digerere" (to digest), which in turn comes from "dis-" (apart) + "gerere" (to carry).  Therefore, indigestible literally means "not able to be carried apart" (in the context of breaking down food).
