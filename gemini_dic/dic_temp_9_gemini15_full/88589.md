hitman:/ˈhɪtmæn/ | n. |杀手，暗杀者 | A person who is paid to kill someone.  Hitmen are often involved in organized crime. |例句：The hitman was hired by a rival gang to eliminate their leader.  这个杀手是被一个竞争帮派雇来消灭他们的头目的。

例句：Police are investigating the murder, suspecting a professional hitman was involved. 警方正在调查这起谋杀案，怀疑牵涉到一名职业杀手。


近义词：assassin, murderer, contract killer, gun for hire | 反义词：victim, target
