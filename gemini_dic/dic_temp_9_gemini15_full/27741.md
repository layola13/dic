canister:/ˈkænɪstər/| n. |金属罐；容器|A canister is a small container, typically cylindrical and made of metal, used for storing or carrying something.  It often has a lid or other closure to keep its contents secure. Examples include canisters for storing coffee, tea, or other dry goods. |例句：She opened the canister and poured the coffee grounds into the filter. 她打开金属罐，把咖啡粉倒进滤器里。|例句：The canister of pepper spray was used to stop the attacker.  胡椒喷雾罐被用来阻止袭击者。|近义词：container, jar, tin, cylinder|反义词: None specifically, but words implying openness or lack of containment could be considered antonyms depending on context.
