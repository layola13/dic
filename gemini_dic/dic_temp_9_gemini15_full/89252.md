/ˈhoʊmɪnɪnz/ | n. | 人类（包括现代人和已灭绝的近亲） |  Hominins are members of the taxonomic tribe Hominini, which includes all species of bipedal primates more closely related to humans than to chimpanzees.  They are characterized by bipedalism, large brains relative to body size, and the use of tools. | The discovery of  *Australopithecus afarensis* fossils provided strong evidence for early hominin bipedalism.  =  发现南方古猿阿法种的化石为早期人类的双足行走提供了强有力的证据。

The study of hominin evolution helps us understand our own origins and the diversity of human ancestors. = 人类进化研究帮助我们了解自身的起源和人类祖先的多样性。


近义词: hominids (更广泛的分类) | 反义词:  无直接反义词，可考虑与其他灵长类动物对比
