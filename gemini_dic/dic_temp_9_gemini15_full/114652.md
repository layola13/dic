malgracious:/mælˈɡreɪʃəs/| adj.|不友好的；粗鲁的；不乐意的；吝啬的|Unkindly or ungracious in manner or behavior; discourteous.  It suggests a deliberate lack of grace or politeness, often implying ill will or resentment. |例句：He gave a malgracious nod of acknowledgement. 他不情愿地点点头表示承认。

例句：Her malgracious response left me feeling hurt and ignored.  她粗鲁的回应让我感到受伤和被忽视。


近义词：ungracious, discourteous, surly, churlish, unfriendly, rude, unpleasant


反义词：gracious, courteous, friendly, kind, pleasant, amiable


词源：源于古法语 *malgracieux，由mal-（坏的，不好的）+ gracieux（优雅的，有礼貌的）构成。
