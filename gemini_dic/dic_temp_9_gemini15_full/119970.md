millepede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/| n.|千足虫，马陆|A myriapod with a long segmented body and numerous legs.  Many species are harmless, feeding on decaying plant matter. Some secrete irritating or toxic substances as a defense mechanism.|例句：The children shrieked when they saw the millepede crawling across the path.  孩子们看到千足虫在小路上爬行时尖叫起来。
例句：The gardener carefully removed the millepede from the flowerbed. 园丁小心翼翼地把千足虫从花坛里移走了。|近义词：centipede, millipede (millipede is more common)|反义词：无
