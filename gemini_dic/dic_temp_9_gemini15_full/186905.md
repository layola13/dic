riotry:/ˈraɪətri/ | n. |暴乱，骚乱 |Violent and disruptive behavior by a crowd of people.  Riots often involve vandalism, looting, and other criminal acts.  They are usually a response to social or political unrest. |Example: The city was gripped by rioting after the controversial election results were announced.  城市在有争议的选举结果公布后陷入暴乱。 The police struggled to control the rioters.  警察努力控制暴徒。|近义词：turmoil, upheaval, disturbance, commotion |反义词：peace, tranquility, order, calm |名词
