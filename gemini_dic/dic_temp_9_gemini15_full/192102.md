scouter:/ˈskaʊtər/| n. |侦察员；球探；童子军成员|A scouter is a person who scouts, especially a member of the Scouting movement or someone who searches for and recruits talented people for a sports team or organization.  |例句：He was a skilled scouter, always finding the best young players. 他是一位熟练的球探，总是能找到最优秀的年轻球员。

例句：The scout leader organized a camping trip for the troop. 童子军领队为队伍组织了一次露营旅行。


近义词：scout, explorer, recruiter |反义词：None specifically applicable. |词性:Noun
