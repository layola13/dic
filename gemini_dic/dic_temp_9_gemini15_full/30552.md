cephalothorax:/ˈsɛfələˌθɔːræks/| n. |头胸部|The fused head and thorax of certain arthropods, such as crustaceans and arachnids.  It contains the brain, eyes, mouthparts, and legs.|例句：The crab's cephalothorax is covered in a hard shell.  螃蟹的头胸部覆盖着坚硬的外壳。

The spider's cephalothorax is the front section of its body. 蜘蛛的头胸部是其身体的前部。|近义词：prosoma (in arachnids)|反义词：abdomen (in arthropods with a cephalothorax)|词性:名词
