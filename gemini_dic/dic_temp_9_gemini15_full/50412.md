depot:/ˈdiːpoʊ/| n. |仓库；车库；车站；兵站|A depot is a place where goods are stored, especially before they are sent out to shops or customers.  It can also refer to a transportation hub, such as a bus depot or train depot.  In a military context, it's a supply base.|例句：The company's main depot is located in the industrial park. 这家公司的主要仓库位于工业园区。|例句：We'll be catching the bus from the central depot. 我们将在中央车站乘车。|近义词：warehouse, station, terminal, storehouse|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept might be "delivery point" or "point of sale".|
