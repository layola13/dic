sanguine:/ˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.|乐观的；充满希望的；红润的|Of or relating to blood; blood-red.  Characterized by or exhibiting optimism and confidence. |例句：She remained sanguine about the outcome of the election. 她对选举的结果仍然乐观。|例句：His sanguine complexion suggested good health. 他红润的脸色暗示着健康。|近义词：optimistic, hopeful, confident, rosy |反义词：pessimistic, gloomy, despondent, cynical


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is sanguine about the future. = 他对未来充满希望。
They are sanguine about their chances of success. = 他们对成功的几率充满信心。


一般过去时:

He was sanguine that he would find a solution. = 他乐观地认为他会找到解决办法。
