ennoblers:/ɪˈnəʊblərz/| n.|使高尚的人；提升者；改良者|Those who or that which ennobles; something that elevates or improves.|The arts are ennoblers of the human spirit. 艺术是提升人类精神的东西。  |近义词：elevators, improvers, refiners|反义词：debasers, degraders, corruptors|名词
