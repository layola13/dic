ice-box:/ˈaɪsbɒks/| n. |冰盒；冰箱 |A container, typically insulated, used for keeping things cold, especially food and drinks.  In the past, it often referred to a simple insulated box with ice, while now it commonly means a refrigerator. |例句：We packed the sandwiches in an ice-box for the picnic. 我们把三明治装进冰盒里野餐。

例句：The ice-box kept the milk fresh. 冰盒使牛奶保持新鲜。


近义词：refrigerator, cooler, ice chest |反义词：oven, heater
