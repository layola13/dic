misconnected:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛktɪd/ | adj. | 连接错误的，连接失败的 | Improperly or incorrectly connected.  Describes a situation where a connection, whether physical or abstract, has not been established correctly or has been disrupted. | Example: The phone line was misconnected, so I couldn't receive calls. 电话线路连接错误，所以我无法接听电话。 The two ideas were misconnected in his argument, leading to a flawed conclusion. 他论证中的两个观点连接错误，导致结论有缺陷。 |近义词:faulty, flawed, incorrect, improperly connected |反义词:correctly connected, properly connected, well-connected
