nonresilience: /ˌnɒnˌrɪˈzɪliəns/ | adj. |  非弹性；缺乏恢复力；不易复原的 | Lacking resilience; inability to recover readily from illness, adversity, or change.  | The company's nonresilience to the market crash led to its bankruptcy.  这家公司对市场崩盘缺乏恢复力，导致其破产。 | resilient, resistant | fragile, vulnerable
