nonmutinously: /ˌnɒnˈmjuːtɪnəsli/ | adv. |  不叛逆地；服从地 |  In a way that does not involve or suggest mutiny or rebellion.  It describes an action or behavior that is obedient and compliant, lacking any sign of defiance or insubordination. | 例句：The soldiers followed orders nonmutinously. = 士兵们服从命令，没有叛逆。The crew worked nonmutinously throughout the storm. = 船员们在风暴中服从地工作。| 近义词：obediently, submissively, compliantly, docilely | 反义词：rebelliously, mutinously, defiantly
