in no case:/ɪn noʊ keɪs/| adj. phrase|无论如何都不，决不|This phrase emphasizes a strong negation, meaning something will absolutely not happen under any circumstances.  It is used to express an absolute prohibition or impossibility.|例句：In no case should you leave the children unattended.  无论如何你都不能让孩子们无人看管。|例句：In no case will I accept a bribe. 我决不会接受贿赂。|近义词：under no circumstances, on no account, never, absolutely not|反义词：in all cases, always, invariably
