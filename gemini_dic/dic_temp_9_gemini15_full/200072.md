sketchy:/ˈskɛtʃi/ | adj. | 粗略的；不完整的；可疑的；不牢靠的 |  Suggests something is not complete, reliable, or trustworthy; possibly illegal or immoral. It can refer to a drawing, plan, or a person's behavior or character. | 例句：The police found the evidence sketchy and inconclusive. 警方发现证据粗略且不足以定案。

例句：His explanation of the events was rather sketchy. 他对事件的解释相当粗略。


近义词：suspicious, dubious, unreliable, questionable, incomplete, vague, flimsy, dodgy


反义词：reliable, trustworthy, certain, definite, complete, thorough


词性：形容词
