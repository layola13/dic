prointervention:/ˌproʊˌɪntərˈvɛnʃən/| n. | 支持干预；主张干预 |  The act of favoring or supporting intervention, especially in the affairs of other countries or in a particular situation.  It implies a positive stance towards taking action to influence or resolve a problem. | Example:  His prointervention stance on the Syrian conflict led to heated debates. 他的支持叙利亚冲突干预的立场引发了激烈的辩论。|近义词：interventionism, advocacy for intervention | 反义词：noninterventionism, opposition to intervention | 名词
