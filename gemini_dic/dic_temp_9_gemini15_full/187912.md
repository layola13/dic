rotary:/ˈroʊtəri/| adj. n.|旋转的；旋转的装置|Relating to or characterized by rotation.  A rotary device is one that rotates. |例句：The rotary engine is known for its smooth operation.  旋转发动机以其平稳的运行而闻名。The museum features a fascinating rotary display of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了一个令人着迷的古代文物的旋转展示。|近义词：revolving, rotating, circular |反义词：stationary, fixed
