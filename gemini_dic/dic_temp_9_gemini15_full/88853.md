hoi:/hɔɪ/| int.|嘿，你好|A casual greeting, similar to "hi".  It's informal and often used among friends or people who know each other well. |例句：Hoi!  Long time no see! 嘿！好久不见了！|近义词：hi, hey, hello |反义词: N/A


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't have grammatical tense variations like verbs.  It's used as a standalone greeting.

PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "hoi".  Its usage is very limited and straightforward as a simple greeting.
