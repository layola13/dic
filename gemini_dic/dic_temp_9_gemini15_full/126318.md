myopathia:/ˌmaɪəˈpæθiə/| n. |肌病|A disease of muscle tissue.  It encompasses a wide range of disorders that affect the structure and function of skeletal, smooth, or cardiac muscle. Symptoms vary depending on the specific type of myopathy but often include muscle weakness, pain, and fatigue.|例句：Duchenne muscular dystrophy is a type of myopathia characterized by progressive muscle degeneration.|近义词：muscular dystrophy, myositis|反义词：muscular health
