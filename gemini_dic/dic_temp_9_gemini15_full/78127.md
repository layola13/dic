geomembrane:/ˈdʒioʊˌmɛmbreɪn/| n. |土工膜|A geomembrane is a synthetic membrane liner used with many different applications in civil engineering projects.  It is typically made of high-density polyethylene (HDPE), polyvinyl chloride (PVC), or other similar materials and is designed to be impermeable to liquids and gases. It's used to create barriers in applications such as landfill liners, canals, and reservoirs.|例句：The landfill was lined with a thick geomembrane to prevent leakage.  垃圾填埋场内衬有一层厚厚的土工膜以防止渗漏。|近义词：liner, membrane, barrier|反义词:permeable material
