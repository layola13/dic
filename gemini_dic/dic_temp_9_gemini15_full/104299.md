justifier:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪər/| n. |辩护者；证明者；理由；借口 |A justifier is someone or something that justifies or provides a reason or excuse for something.  It can also refer to a device or system used to justify text. |例句：He acted as the justifier for the company's actions. 他充当了公司行为的辩护者。 The report serves as a justifier for further investment. 这份报告为进一步投资提供了理由。|近义词：defender, vindicator, apologist, rationale, excuse |反义词：critic, accuser, condemnation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The lawyer justifies his client's actions. = 律师为其当事人的行为辩护。
This evidence justifies a thorough investigation. = 这些证据证明需要进行彻底调查。


一般过去时:
He justified his lateness by saying he missed the bus. = 他解释说自己错过了公共汽车，以此为迟到辩解。
The government justified the war on the grounds of national security. = 政府以国家安全为由为战争辩护。


现在完成时:
She has justified her decision with compelling arguments. = 她用令人信服的论据证明了自己的决定。
They have justified their investment in the project. = 他们为在该项目上的投资提供了理由。


过去完成时:
He had justified his actions to his superiors before the meeting. = 在会议之前，他已经向上司解释了他的行为。
The company had justified its layoffs due to economic downturn. = 公司由于经济衰退而为裁员提供了理由。


将来时:
She will justify her claims with concrete evidence. = 她将用具体的证据来证明自己的说法。
The report will justify the need for additional funding. = 这份报告将证明需要额外的资金。


虚拟语气:
If he could justify his actions, he wouldn't be in trouble. = 如果他能为自己的行为辩护，他就不会有麻烦了。
I wish I could justify spending so much money. = 我希望我能证明自己花这么多钱是合理的。


被动语态:
His actions were justified by the court. = 他的行为得到了法院的辩护。
The decision was justified on economic grounds. = 这一决定在经济方面是合理的。


疑问句:
How can you justify your behavior? = 你怎么能为你的行为辩护？
Does this data justify a change in strategy? = 这些数据是否证明需要改变策略？


否定句:
He could not justify his absence from work. = 他无法为旷工辩解。
The evidence does not justify further investigation. = 证据不足以证明需要进一步调查。


PHRASE->

justify oneself = 为自己辩护
justify the means = 为方法辩护
justify the end = 为目的辩护
fully justify = 完全证明
to justify a claim = 证明一个说法
