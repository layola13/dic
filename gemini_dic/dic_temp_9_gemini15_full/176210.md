re-become:/ˌriːbɪˈkʌm/| v.|重新成为，恢复为|To become again; to return to a former state or condition.|The caterpillar will re-become a butterfly. 毛毛虫将再次变成蝴蝶。|近义词：revert, return to, regain|反义词：cease to be, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-becomes a member of the club every year. 他每年都重新成为俱乐部会员。
They re-become friends after a long quarrel.  他们经过长时间的争吵后又重新成为朋友。


一般过去时:
She re-became interested in painting after a long break.  她休息很久后又对绘画重新产生了兴趣。
The country re-became independent after many years of occupation.  这个国家在被占领多年后重新独立。


现在完成时:
He has re-become a believer in the power of prayer. 他已经重新相信祈祷的力量。
She has re-become a vegetarian after years of eating meat. 她吃肉很多年后又重新成为素食者。


过去完成时:
Before the war, he had re-become a successful businessman.  战争爆发前，他已重新成为一名成功的商人。
By the time I met her again, she had re-become the confident woman I remembered.  当我再次遇到她时，她已经重新成为我记忆中那个自信的女人。


将来时:
They will re-become allies if the situation changes. 如果情况改变，他们将重新成为盟友。
He believes he will re-become a champion someday. 他相信他总有一天会重新成为冠军。


虚拟语气:
If only he would re-become the kind man he once was.  但愿他能重新成为以前那个善良的人。
I wish she would re-become involved in community service. 我希望她能重新参与社区服务。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies an active process of change.)  这个动词很少用于被动语态，因为它暗示了一个主动的改变过程。


疑问句:
Will he re-become the CEO of the company? 他会重新成为公司首席执行官吗？
Did she re-become a teacher after raising her children? 她抚养孩子后重新当老师了吗？


否定句:
He did not re-become a member of the team. 他没有重新成为团队成员。
She will not re-become friends with him. 她不会再和他做朋友。
