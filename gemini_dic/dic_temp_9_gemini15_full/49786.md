demonism:/ˈdiːmənɪzəm/| n.|恶魔崇拜；恶魔论|The belief in or worship of demons.  It often involves the invocation of demonic powers for various purposes, sometimes for good, but more often for evil.  The practice can range from individual rituals to organized cults. |例句：The medieval church fiercely condemned demonism and witchcraft. 中世纪教会强烈谴责恶魔崇拜和巫术。

例句：Accusations of demonism were often used to persecute religious and political opponents.  对恶魔崇拜的指控常常被用来迫害宗教和政治对手。


近义词：demonic worship, devil worship, satanism |反义词：angelism, piety, holiness
