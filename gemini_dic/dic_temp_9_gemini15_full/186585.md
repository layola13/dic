ridiculers:/rɪˈdɪkjʊlərz/| n. |嘲笑者，讥讽者 |Those who ridicule or make fun of others.  They often lack empathy and understanding.  |例句：The bullies were relentless ridiculers, targeting anyone different.  霸凌者是无情的嘲笑者，他们以任何与众不同的人为目标。

例句：The internet is full of anonymous ridiculers hiding behind fake profiles. 网络上充满了躲在假账号后面的匿名嘲笑者。


近义词：mocker, scoffer, jeerer, taunter

反义词：supporter, admirer, praiser
