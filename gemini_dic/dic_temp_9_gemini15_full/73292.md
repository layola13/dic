forebrains:/ˈfɔːrˌbreɪnz/ | n. |前脑|The forebrain is the anterior part of the brain, containing the cerebrum, thalamus, and hypothalamus.  It's responsible for higher-level cognitive functions, such as thinking, learning, and memory. | Example:  The forebrain is the largest part of the human brain. = 人类大脑的前脑是最大的部分。

The damage to his forebrain resulted in significant cognitive impairment. = 他前脑的损伤导致了严重的认知障碍。|近义词：cerebrum (部分重叠)|反义词：hindbrain, midbrain |名词
