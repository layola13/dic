gru:/ɡruː/| n.|咕噜声，牢骚，脾气暴躁的人|a low, guttural sound, often expressing displeasure or annoyance; a grumpy person|例句：The old man grumbled about the noise.  老人生气地抱怨噪音。
例句：I heard a gruff gru from the bear. 我听到熊发出粗哑的咕噜声。|近义词：grumble, growl, mutter|反义词：whisper, murmur
