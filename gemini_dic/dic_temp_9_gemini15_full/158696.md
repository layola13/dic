plotted:/ˈplɒtɪd/| v. |策划的；密谋的；绘制的|To plan or devise secretly, especially to do something wrong or illegal; to mark on a map or chart; to represent graphically.  |例句：They plotted to overthrow the government.他们密谋推翻政府。|例句：The architect plotted the building's layout on graph paper. 建筑师在绘图纸上绘制了建筑物的平面图。|近义词：planned, schemed, conspired, charted, graphed |反义词：unplanned, improvised
