indivertible: /ˌɪndɪˈvɜːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可改变的，不可避免的 | Incapable of being changed or avoided; inevitable. |


英文解析:  Something that is indivertible is fixed and cannot be altered or prevented from happening. It suggests a sense of inevitability and finality.


例句:

The decline of the Roman Empire was an indivertible process. = 罗马帝国的衰落是一个不可避免的过程。

The judge's decision was indivertible; there was no appeal. = 法官的决定是不可改变的；没有上诉的余地。


近义词: unavoidable, inevitable, inescapable, immutable, certain


反义词: avoidable, preventable, reversible, changeable


词性: adjective
