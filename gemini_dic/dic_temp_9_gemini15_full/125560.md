multitasker:/ˈmʌltiˌtæskər/| n. |多任务处理者；能够同时处理多项任务的人 |A multitasker is someone who can effectively handle multiple tasks or activities simultaneously.  They possess the ability to switch between different tasks quickly and efficiently without significant loss of productivity or accuracy.  This often involves prioritizing, organizing, and managing time effectively. |Example: She's a great multitasker; she manages a household, runs a business, and still finds time for her hobbies.  她是一个很棒的多任务处理者；她管理着一个家，经营着一家企业，仍然有时间从事她的爱好。|近义词: juggler,  multiprocessor|反义词: single-tasker
