categories:/ˈkætɪɡɔːriz/| n. |类别，范畴|Categories help us organize information and make it easier to understand.|例句：We need to categorize these files by date.|近义词：classes, groups, types|反义词：randomness, chaos|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This store categorizes its products by brand. = 这家商店按品牌对产品进行分类。
The librarian categorizes books by subject. = 图书管理员按主题对书籍进行分类。

一般过去时:
She categorized the emails into work and personal. = 她将电子邮件分类为工作和个人邮件。
They categorized the responses based on age group. = 他们根据年龄组对回复进行了分类。

现在完成时:
We have categorized all the data for the report. = 我们已经为报告对所有数据进行了分类。
She has categorized her collection of stamps by country. = 她已按国家对她的邮票收藏进行了分类。

过去完成时:
He had categorized the information before starting the presentation. = 他在开始演示文稿之前已经对信息进行了分类。
They had categorized the artifacts before the museum opening. = 博物馆开馆前，他们已经对文物进行了分类。

将来时:
I will categorize the photos after I finish editing them. = 我编辑完照片后再对它们进行分类。
We will categorize the survey results by gender. = 我们将按性别对调查结果进行分类。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would categorize all my old photos. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把我所有的旧照片都分类。
I wish I had categorized my notes before the exam. = 我希望我在考试前已经把笔记分类了。

被动语态:
The items are categorized according to their weight. = 这些物品按重量进行分类。
The data was categorized using a sophisticated algorithm. = 数据使用复杂的算法进行分类。

疑问句:
How are these products categorized? = 这些产品是如何分类的？
How did you categorize the information? = 你是如何对信息进行分类的？

否定句:
They did not categorize the data properly. = 他们没有正确地对数据进行分类。
I haven't categorized my books yet. = 我还没有整理我的书。
