iridectomy:/ˌaɪrɪˈdɛktəmi/| n. |虹膜切除术|Surgical removal of a portion of the iris. This procedure is often performed to treat glaucoma or to improve vision.  |Example: The ophthalmologist recommended an iridectomy to lower the intraocular pressure.|近义词：iris resection|反义词：无
