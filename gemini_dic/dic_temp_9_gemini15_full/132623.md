nondisarmaments:/ˌnɒnˌdɪˈzɑːrməmənts/| n. |非裁军；不裁军|The absence or rejection of disarmament; a policy or state of not reducing or eliminating weapons.  This term often implies a continuation of an existing arms buildup or a refusal to engage in disarmament talks.|例句：The country's nondisarmaments policy led to increased international tension.  这个国家的非裁军政策导致国际紧张局势加剧。

例句：Their stance of nondisarmaments was met with criticism from many nations. 他们的不裁军立场受到了许多国家的批评。

近义词：nonproliferation, arms buildup, militarization

反义词：disarmament, demilitarization, arms control

词性:名词
