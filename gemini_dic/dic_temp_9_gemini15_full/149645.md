paintiness:/ˈpeɪntinəs/| n. |绘画感，油画感，色彩感|The quality of resembling a painting; the appearance or effect of being painted.  It can refer to the vividness of colors, the texture of the surface, or the overall artistic impression.  |例句：The paintiness of the sunset was breathtaking.  日落的绘画感令人叹为观止。

The paintiness of the landscape was remarkable, capturing the essence of the scene. 这幅风景画的绘画感非凡，捕捉到了场景的精髓。|近义词：vividness, pictorialness, artistry |反义词：dullness, drabness, monochromaticity
