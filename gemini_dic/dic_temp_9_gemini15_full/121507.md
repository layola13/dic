mislight:/ˌmɪsˈlaɪt/ | v. | 误导，照亮不当 | To give a wrong or misleading impression; to illuminate inadequately or incorrectly. |例句：The misleading advertisement mislighted consumers about the product's capabilities.  这则误导性广告误导了消费者对产品性能的认知。|例句：The dim lighting mislighted the stage, making it difficult to see the actors.  昏暗的灯光照亮舞台不当，使得演员难以看清。|近义词：misinform, mislead, deceive |反义词：enlighten, clarify, illuminate correctly
