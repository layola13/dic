reenthroning:/ˌriːˈθroʊnɪŋ/| v.|重新加冕；复位|The act of putting someone back on a throne; restoring someone to power or a position of authority.|例句：The reenthroning of the king marked the end of the civil war.  重新加冕国王标志着内战的结束。
例句：The reenthroning ceremony was a lavish affair, attended by dignitaries from across the land.  重新加冕仪式盛大隆重，全国各地的显要人士都出席了。|近义词：reinstatement, restoration, reinstallation|反义词：dethronement, deposition, removal
