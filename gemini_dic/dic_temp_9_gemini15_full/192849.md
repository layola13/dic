searing:/ˈsɪərɪŋ/| adj. |灼热的；严厉的；强烈的|extremely hot; intensely critical or harsh; causing a burning sensation|The searing heat of the desert sun made travel difficult.  沙漠炙热的阳光使得旅行变得困难。|The critic's searing review devastated the young author. 批评家严厉的评论摧毁了年轻作者。|scorching, burning, blistering, intense|mild, gentle, lukewarm|形容词

一般过去时:
The fire seared the wood. 火烧焦了木头。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
The sun had seared the ground before the rain came.  雨下之前，太阳已经把地面烤焦了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
