cursing:/ˈkɜːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 咒骂；诅咒；辱骂 | The act of using offensive or abusive language.  It can involve swearing, blasphemy, or other forms of verbal abuse. | Example:  The cursing was so loud it disturbed the entire neighborhood.  咒骂声很大，扰乱了整个街区。  |近义词：swearing, blasphemy, imprecation, malediction | 反义词：blessing, praise

He curses frequently. = 他经常咒骂。
