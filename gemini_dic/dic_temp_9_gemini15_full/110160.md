liegedom: /ˈliːdʒdəm/ | n. | 臣民身份；效忠；领地 | The territory or people owing allegiance to a lord or sovereign.  It refers to the state of being a liegeman, the rights and duties associated with that status, and sometimes the territory ruled by a liege lord.  The term is largely archaic. | Example:  The king demanded unwavering loyalty from all within his liegedom.  国王要求他所有领地内的臣民都对其绝对忠诚。 | 近义词：allegiance, fealty, domain, realm | 反义词：rebellion, independence
