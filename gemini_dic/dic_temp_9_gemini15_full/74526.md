fraternises:/ˈfrætənaɪz/| v.|兄弟般相处；友善交往；|To associate or be friendly with, especially in a familiar or informal way.  It often implies a close, often overly familiar relationship, sometimes to the point of inappropriateness, especially if it involves members of opposing groups or sides.|例句：The soldiers fraternized with the enemy. 士兵们与敌人友好相处。

例句：The opposing teams fraternized after the game, sharing stories and laughter. 比赛结束后，对垒的队伍友好相处，分享故事和笑声。


近义词：associate, socialize, mingle, hobnob

反义词：estrange, alienate, isolate, shun

词性：动词
