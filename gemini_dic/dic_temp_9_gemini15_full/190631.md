The word is often used in the context of truthfulness, honesty, and integrity.  |例句：Satya is a core value in many cultures. 真理是许多文化中的核心价值观。|例句：The witness's satya was questioned by the defense attorney. 证人的真实性受到了辩护律师的质疑。|近义词：truth, verity, honesty, integrity|反义词：falsehood, lie, deception, dishonesty|名词
