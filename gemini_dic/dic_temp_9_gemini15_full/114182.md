mahratta:/məˈrætə/| n. |马拉地人；马拉地语；马拉地帝国|A member of a people inhabiting western India, speaking Marathi.  Historically, the Marathas were a powerful group who established a large empire in India. The term can also refer to their language or their empire.|The Maratha Empire was a significant force in Indian history. = 马拉地帝国是印度历史上的一支重要力量。|The Maratha people are known for their rich culture and history. = 马拉地人民以其丰富的文化和历史而闻名。|近义词：Maharashtrian (referring to the people)|反义词：无
