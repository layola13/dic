consumer:/kənˈsuːmər/ | n. |消费者，用户 |A person who buys goods or services for personal use.  |Example: The new marketing campaign is aimed at attracting younger consumers.  新的营销活动旨在吸引年轻消费者。|近义词：buyer, purchaser, client |反义词：producer, manufacturer |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consumer buys groceries every week. = 消费者每周都买杂货。
Consumers demand higher quality products. = 消费者要求更高质量的产品。

一般过去时:
The consumer purchased a new television. = 消费者购买了一台新电视。
Consumers rejected the inferior product. = 消费者拒绝了劣质产品。

现在完成时:
The consumer has bought several items online. = 消费者已经在网上购买了几件商品。
Consumers have expressed dissatisfaction with the service. = 消费者对服务表示不满。

过去完成时:
The consumer had already spent his budget before the sale. = 消费者在促销之前就已经花完了预算。
Consumers had complained about the poor quality before the recall. = 在召回之前，消费者就已经抱怨了产品质量差的问题。

将来时:
The consumer will purchase a new car next month. = 消费者下个月将购买一辆新车。
Consumers will likely boycott the company's products. = 消费者可能会抵制该公司的产品。

虚拟语气:
If the consumer were richer, he would buy a bigger house. = 如果消费者更有钱，他会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish the consumer had read the reviews before buying. = 我希望消费者在购买前阅读了评论。

被动语态:
The product was well-received by the consumer. = 该产品受到了消费者的好评。
The consumer's needs are being addressed by the company. = 该公司正在满足消费者的需求。

疑问句:
Who is the target consumer for this product? = 这个产品的目标消费者是谁？
What are the consumer's rights in this situation? = 在这种情况下，消费者的权利是什么？

否定句:
The consumer did not complain about the product. = 消费者没有抱怨该产品。
Consumers are not satisfied with the current prices. = 消费者对目前的价格不满意。
