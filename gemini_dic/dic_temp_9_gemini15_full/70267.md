fidalgo:/fɪˈdælɡoʊ/| n.| 菲达尔戈（葡萄牙贵族称号）| A member of the Portuguese nobility, especially one of lower rank.  The term originally referred to sons of nobles who did not inherit titles but retained some privileges. | Example:  He claimed descent from a long line of fidalgos. 他声称自己是世袭菲达尔戈的后代。|近义词：nobleman, hidalgo (Spanish equivalent)|反义词：commoner, peasant|名词
