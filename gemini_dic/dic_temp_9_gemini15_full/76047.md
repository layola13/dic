furloughed:/ˈfɜːrloʊd/ | v. | 休假；强制休假 | To grant leave of absence to (an employee), especially temporarily and without pay.  | The company furloughed its employees due to the economic downturn. 公司由于经济衰退而让员工休假。  | lay off, suspend |  reinstate, employ |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company furloughs employees during slow seasons. = 公司在淡季会让员工休假。
He regularly furloughs his staff for a week in summer. = 他定期在夏天让员工休假一周。


一般过去时:
The government furloughed thousands of workers. = 政府让数千名工人休假。
They furloughed the entire team last month. = 他们上个月让整个团队休假。


现在完成时:
The factory has furloughed its workforce several times this year. = 该工厂今年已经几次让员工休假。
They have furloughed staff to cut costs. = 他们已经让员工休假以削减成本。


过去完成时:
The company had furloughed its staff before the announcement. = 公司在宣布之前就已经让员工休假了。
Before the pandemic, they had never furloughed any employees. = 在疫情爆发之前，他们从未让任何员工休假。


将来时:
The company will furlough employees if sales continue to decline. = 如果销售额继续下降，公司将让员工休假。
They will likely furlough some workers next quarter. = 他们很可能在下个季度让一些工人休假。


虚拟语气:
If the project failed, they would have furloughed the team. = 如果项目失败，他们就会让团队休假。
I wish they hadn't furloughed me; I really needed the money. = 我真希望他们没有让我休假；我真的很需要钱。


被动语态:
Many workers were furloughed during the recession. = 许多工人在经济衰退期间被强制休假。
Hundreds of employees have been furloughed this year. = 今年已经有数百名员工被强制休假。


疑问句:
Were any employees furloughed during the restructuring? = 在重组期间是否有任何员工被强制休假？
Will the employees be furloughed again next year? = 明年员工还会被强制休假吗？


否定句:
The company did not furlough any employees last year. = 公司去年没有让任何员工休假。
They have not furloughed anyone in this department yet. = 他们还没有让这个部门的任何人休假。
