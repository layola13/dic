illusory: /ɪˈluːzəri/ | adj. | 虚幻的；错觉的；不真实的 | seeming real or true, but actually not so  |The oasis proved to be illusory.  | deceptive, fanciful, imaginary | real, actual, genuine


例句：

The shimmering heat haze created an illusory lake in the desert.  沙漠中闪闪发光的热浪创造了一个虚幻的湖泊。

His promises of wealth turned out to be illusory.  他关于财富的承诺最终证明是虚假的。


近义词：deceptive, fanciful, imaginary, unreal, fictitious, phantasmal

反义词：real, actual, genuine, authentic, factual, tangible
