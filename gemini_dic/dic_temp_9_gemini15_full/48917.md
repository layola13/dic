defrosted:/dɪˈfrɒstɪd/| adj. |解冻的；除霜的| Having been thawed or freed from frost.  The defrosted meat is ready to cook. 解冻的肉可以烹饪了。|近义词：thawed, unfrozen |反义词：frozen, icy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The food defrosts slowly in the refrigerator. = 食物在冰箱里慢慢解冻。
He defrosts the freezer regularly. = 他定期除冰箱的霜。


一般过去时:
I defrosted the chicken last night. = 我昨晚把鸡肉解冻了。
She defrosted the vegetables before cooking them. = 她在烹饪蔬菜之前把它们解冻了。


现在完成时:
We have defrosted the turkey for tomorrow's dinner. = 我们已经把火鸡解冻了，准备明天的晚餐。
They have defrosted the pipes after the cold snap. = 严寒过后，他们已经把管道除霜了。


过去完成时:
The cake had defrosted completely by the time we arrived. = 我们到达时，蛋糕已经完全解冻了。
He had defrosted the car before driving it. = 他在开车之前已经把车上的霜除掉了。


将来时:
I will defrost the strawberries before making the smoothie. = 我将在制作冰沙之前解冻草莓。
She will defrost the freezer compartment tomorrow. = 她明天将除掉冰箱冷冻室的霜。


虚拟语气:
If I had defrosted the meat earlier, dinner would have been ready sooner. = 如果我早点把肉解冻，晚餐就能早点准备好。
I wish I had defrosted the car before leaving for work. = 我真希望在上班前就把车上的霜除掉。


被动语态:
The meat was defrosted overnight. = 肉在夜间解冻了。
The freezer was defrosted by the technician. = 冰箱由技术人员除霜。


疑问句:

否定句:

He didn't defrost the car before driving it. = 他开车前没有除掉车上的霜。
