flat-mate:/ˈflætmeɪt/| n. |室友，同住者 |A person who shares a flat (apartment) with another person or people.  |例句：I'm looking for a flat-mate to share the rent and bills. 我正在寻找一位室友来分摊房租和账单。|例句：My flat-mate is very tidy and organised. 我的室友非常整洁和有条理。|近义词：roommate, housemate |反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
My flatmate cooks dinner every Tuesday. = 我的室友每周二做晚饭。
He cleans the bathroom every week. = 他每周打扫浴室。
