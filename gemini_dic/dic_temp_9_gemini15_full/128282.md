neuroprosthetics:/ˌnʊroʊprɒsˈθɛtɪks/| n. |神经修复学；神经假体|The study and application of prosthetic devices that interact with the nervous system to restore lost function.  Neuroprosthetics aims to replace or enhance lost sensory or motor functions by directly interfacing with the brain, spinal cord, or peripheral nerves.|例句：Researchers are making significant advancements in neuroprosthetics, developing devices that allow paralyzed individuals to control robotic limbs.|近义词：neural prosthetics, brain-computer interface|反义词：N/A
