ideogram:/ˈaɪdiəɡræm/| n. |表意文字，语符|A symbol representing an idea or object; a character in a writing system in which symbols represent ideas or concepts rather than sounds.|Example:  Chinese characters are ideograms, representing words or concepts visually.|近义词：logogram, pictogram|反义词：alphabet, phonetic script
