seclusively:/sɪˈkluːsɪvli/| adv. |独家地；排外地；隐居地 |Exclusively means solely or entirely. It implies a restriction or limitation, suggesting that something is limited to a specific group or purpose, or happens in only one way.  It can also suggest a sense of privacy or seclusion. |例句：The information was released exclusively to the press. 信息只发布给了媒体。

The research was conducted exclusively by our team. 这项研究完全由我们团队进行。


近义词：solely, only, entirely, solely, privately

反义词：inclusively, publicly, openly

词性:副词
