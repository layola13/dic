eremitage:/ˈɛrəmɪtɪdʒ/| n. |隐居处；隐修院；（尤指）冬宫博物馆|A hermitage is a secluded dwelling, especially one occupied by a hermit or a religious recluse.  The Hermitage Museum in St. Petersburg is a famous example, though not literally a hermit's dwelling.  |例句：The hermit lived a solitary life in his remote hermitage.  隐士在他偏远的隐居处过着孤独的生活。

The Hermitage Museum is a world-renowned art museum. 冬宫博物馆是世界著名的艺术博物馆。|近义词：retreat, seclusion, monastery |反义词：metropolis, public place
