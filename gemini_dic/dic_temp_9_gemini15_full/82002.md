griots:/ˈɡriːoʊz/| n.|格里奥（西非民间艺人）|A griot is a West African storyteller, musician, singer, poet, and praise singer, who acts as a keeper of history and tradition.  They often accompany their storytelling with musical instruments like the kora or djembe.  Their role is essential in preserving and transmitting oral history, cultural values, and genealogical information within their communities.|The griots of Mali are renowned for their musical talents and historical knowledge. |近义词：storyteller, bard, minstrel |反义词：无
