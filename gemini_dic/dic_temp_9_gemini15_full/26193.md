由于“buts”本身不是一个独立存在的单词，而是名词“but”的复数形式，因此无法按照你提供的格式进行详细解析。  “but”的解析如下：


but:/bʌt/ | conj. | 但是，然而 |  连接两个意义上相对立的句子或分句，表示转折或对比。|例句：I wanted to go, but I was too tired. 我想去的，但是我太累了。|近义词：however, yet, nevertheless | 反义词：and, therefore


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I like apples, but I hate oranges. = 我喜欢苹果，但是我讨厌橘子。
He works hard, but he doesn't earn much. = 他工作努力，但他赚的不多。

一般过去时:
She tried to call, but her phone was dead. = 她试图打电话，但是她的手机没电了。
They wanted to leave early, but the meeting ran late. = 他们想早点离开，但是会议开晚了。

现在完成时:
I have finished my work, but I still feel tired. = 我已经完成了我的工作，但是我仍然感到疲倦。
She has studied hard, but she hasn't passed the exam yet. = 她学习很努力，但是她还没通过考试。

过去完成时:
I had already eaten, but I was still hungry. = 我已经吃过了，但是我还饿。
They had planned a trip, but it was cancelled due to the weather. = 他们已经计划了一次旅行，但是由于天气原因取消了。

将来时:
I will go to the party, but I won't stay late. = 我会去参加聚会，但我不会待到很晚。
He will try his best, but he can't guarantee success. = 他会尽力而为，但他不能保证成功。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, but I don't. = 如果我有更多的时间就好了，但是我没有。
I wish I could help, but my hands are tied. = 我真希望我能帮忙，但是我无能为力。

被动语态:
The work was completed, but it wasn't perfect. = 工作完成了，但是并不完美。
The meeting was postponed, but a new date hasn't been set yet. = 会议被推迟了，但是还没有确定新的日期。

疑问句:
Do you want to go out, but what do you think? = 你想出去吗，但是你怎么想呢？
Is it raining, but should we stay inside? = 下雨了吗，那我们是不是应该待在里面？

否定句:
I don't like her, but I have to work with her. = 我不喜欢她，但我不得不和她一起工作。
He didn't want to go, but he had no choice. = 他不想去，但他别无选择。


PHRASE->

nothing but = 只有；只不过是
but for =要不是
all but =几乎；差不多
cannot but = 不得不
but also =而且


词源:

The word "but" has a long and complex history in the English language. Its origins can be traced back to Old English "buton," meaning "outside," "except," or "unless."  Over time, its meaning evolved to encompass its current primary usage as a conjunction indicating contrast or exception.
