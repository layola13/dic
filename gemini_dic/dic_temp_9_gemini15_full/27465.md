campaigned:/kæmˈpeɪnd/ | v. | 参加竞选；领导运动；开展活动 | To work actively to achieve a particular goal, typically a political or social one.  |例句：He campaigned tirelessly for the presidency. 他为总统职位不懈地奔走。|例句：The organization campaigned against animal cruelty. 该组织开展了反对虐待动物的活动。|近义词：agitated, promoted, advocated |反义词：opposed, resisted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They campaign for better working conditions. = 他们为改善工作条件而奔走。
She campaigns against poverty. = 她积极参与反贫困运动。


一般过去时:
The candidate campaigned extensively across the state. = 候选人在全州范围内进行了广泛的竞选活动。
We campaigned for a new school. = 我们为争取新学校而努力。


现在完成时:
They have campaigned for years to protect the environment. = 他们多年来一直致力于保护环境。
She has campaigned tirelessly for social justice. = 她为社会正义不懈地努力。


过去完成时:
After he had campaigned for months, he finally won the election. = 在竞选了几个月后，他终于赢得了选举。
They had campaigned vigorously before the referendum. = 他们在那次公投之前进行了积极的宣传。


将来时:
They will campaign for a new hospital next year. = 他们明年将为争取新医院而努力。
She will campaign for the nomination. = 她将参加提名竞选。


虚拟语气:
If he had campaigned harder, he might have won. = 如果他竞选更努力的话，他可能会赢。
I wish she had campaigned more effectively. = 我希望她能更有效地进行竞选。


被动语态:
The issue was campaigned for by many groups. = 许多团体都为这个问题奔走呼吁。
The election was campaigned on promises of lower taxes. = 这场选举以降低税收的承诺为竞选纲领。


疑问句:
Did they campaign for that candidate? = 他们为那位候选人奔走呼吁了吗？
What are they campaigning for? = 他们在为争取什么？


否定句:
He did not campaign for the position. = 他没有为这个职位奔走。
She didn't campaign against the policy. = 她没有反对这项政策。



PHRASE->
campaign for: 为…而奔走
campaign against: 反对…
election campaign: 选举运动
marketing campaign: 市场营销活动
public awareness campaign: 公众意识宣传活动
