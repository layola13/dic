fortifies:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪz/| vt.|加强；巩固；筑防御工事|To strengthen or reinforce something, making it more resistant to attack or damage.  It can refer to physical structures, defenses, or even abstract concepts like one's resolve.|例句：The army fortifies the border to prevent invasion.  军队加强边境防御以防止入侵。

例句：  She fortifies herself with a cup of coffee before tackling the difficult task. 她喝一杯咖啡来提神，然后开始处理困难的任务。


近义词：strengthen, reinforce, bolster, secure

反义词：weaken, undermine, sabotage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fortifies his position with new evidence. 他用新的证据巩固自己的立场。
The company fortifies its products with vitamins.  这家公司用维生素强化其产品。


一般过去时:
They fortified the castle walls against attack. 他们加固了城堡城墙以抵御攻击。
The doctor fortified him with a vitamin injection. 医生给他注射维生素来增强体质。


现在完成时:
She has fortified her immune system with healthy eating. 她通过健康饮食增强了免疫系统。
The government has fortified the national defense. 政府加强了国防力量。


过去完成时:
They had fortified the city before the enemy arrived. 他们在敌人到来之前就已加固了城市。
He had fortified his argument with compelling statistics. 他用令人信服的统计数据巩固了他的论点。


将来时:
They will fortify the bridge before the heavy traffic starts.  他们在交通繁忙之前将加固桥梁。
We will fortify our position in the market. 我们将在市场上巩固我们的地位。


虚拟语气:
If they fortified the defenses, the city would be safer. 如果他们加强防御工事，城市就会更安全。
I wish I had fortified myself against the cold. 我真希望我当时已经做好防寒准备。


被动语态:
The city walls were fortified against siege. 城墙被加固以抵御围攻。
His position is being fortified by new allies. 他的立场正在得到新盟友的巩固。


疑问句:
How will they fortify the crumbling infrastructure? 他们将如何加固正在崩塌的基础设施？
Have they fortified the bridge against the flood?  他们是否加固了桥梁以抵御洪水？


否定句:
They did not fortify the castle adequately. 他们没有充分加固城堡。
She did not fortify her argument with sufficient evidence. 她没有用足够的证据来支持她的论点。
