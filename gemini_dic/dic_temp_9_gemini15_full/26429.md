BYOD:/ˈbaɪ.oʊd/| n. |自带设备|Bring Your Own Device;  a policy that allows employees to use their personal mobile devices (smartphones, tablets, laptops) for work purposes.| 例句：Our company has a BYOD policy, so employees can use their own laptops. = 我们公司有自带设备政策，所以员工可以使用自己的笔记本电脑。

Our company is considering implementing a BYOD policy to reduce IT costs. = 我们公司正在考虑实施自带设备政策以降低IT成本。


近义词：employee-owned device policy|反义词：COPE (Corporate-Owned, Personally Enabled)


词性:名词
