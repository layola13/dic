例句：He showed his savviest side during the crisis, making tough but necessary decisions. 他在危机中展现了他最精明的一面，做出了艰难但必要的决定。|近义词：most shrewd, most astute, most skillful, most adept |反义词：most naive, most inexperienced, most inept
