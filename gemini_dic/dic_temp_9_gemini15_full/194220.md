self-lubrication:/ˌsɛlfˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |自润滑|The ability of a machine or component to lubricate itself, usually through the incorporation of self-lubricating materials.|例句：The bearings used in this high-speed motor feature self-lubrication for reduced maintenance.  这种高速电机使用的轴承具有自润滑功能，以减少维护。|近义词：self-oiling, automatic lubrication|反义词：requiring external lubrication
