frize:/fraɪz/| n. v. | 卷曲的头发；粗呢绒；装饰；使卷曲；使起皱 |

英文解析:
Noun: A short, thick, and often curly or wavy hair. Also, a type of heavy, durable fabric with a napped surface.  As a verb, it means to curl or cause to curl.  It can also refer to the decorative ornamentation of a surface, often in a repetitive pattern.


例句:
The sheep had a thick frieze of wool. = 这只羊长着厚厚的卷毛。

The coat was made of a heavy, warm frieze. = 这件外套是用厚实的保暖粗呢绒做的。


近义词:
Noun: curl, fleece, nap, weave (for the fabric);  ornamentation, decoration (for the decorative sense)
Verb: curl, crimp, wrinkle


反义词:
Noun: smooth (for hair and fabric); plain (for fabric)
Verb: straighten, smooth


词性:
Noun, Verb
