hagiographer:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfər/| n. |圣徒传记作者，颂扬者|A hagiographer is a person who writes hagiographies, which are biographies of saints or other holy figures, often emphasizing their virtues and miracles and downplaying or omitting any flaws.  They often present a highly idealized and positive portrayal of their subject. | The hagiographer meticulously detailed the saint's life, highlighting his numerous acts of charity.  圣徒传记作者一丝不苟地详述了圣徒的一生，突显了他无数的慈善行为。| biographer, panegyrist | detractor, critic | 名词
