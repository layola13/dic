nth:/nθ/| adj.| 第n个；第n位的 |The nth term in a sequence is the term in the nth position.  数列中的第n项是第n个位置上的项。| 近义词：ordinal | 反义词：first, second, third (具体序号)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is the nth time I've told you. = 这是我第n次告诉你。
The nth element in the array is zero. = 数组中的第n个元素是零。

一般过去时:
He finished the nth task on the list. = 他完成了列表中的第n项任务。
That was the nth time she called me today. = 那是她今天第n次打电话给我。

现在完成时:
I have completed the nth chapter of the book. = 我已经完成了这本书的第n章。
This is the nth time I've seen this movie. = 这是我第n次看这部电影。

过去完成时:
They had already finished the nth project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经完成了第n个项目。
By then, she had already made the nth attempt. = 到那时，她已经进行了第n次尝试。

将来时:
I will finish the nth exercise tomorrow. = 我明天将完成第n个练习。
He will be working on the nth project next week. = 他下周将从事第n个项目。

虚拟语气:
If this were the nth time, I would be more prepared. = 如果这是第n次，我会更有准备。
I wish this wasn't the nth time this happened. = 我希望这不是第n次发生这种情况。

被动语态:
The nth position was filled by a new employee. = 第n个职位由一位新员工担任。
The nth problem is being addressed by the team. = 团队正在处理第n个问题。

疑问句:
Which is the nth element? = 哪一个是第n个元素？
Is this the nth iteration of the loop? = 这是循环的第n次迭代吗？

否定句:
This is not the nth time. = 这不是第n次。
It wasn't the nth attempt. = 这不是第n次尝试。


PHRASE->
nth degree  =  极高的程度; 最大程度
the nth power =  n次方
to the nth degree =  达到极点；达到最大程度
nth root = n次方根
beyond the nth degree = 超过n次方
