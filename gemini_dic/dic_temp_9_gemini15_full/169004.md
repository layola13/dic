profs:/prɒfs/| n.|教授们|Professors, a shortened form of "professors".  It's a plural noun referring to a group of professors.  |The profs are meeting to discuss the curriculum.|近义词: professors, teachers, lecturers|反义词:students


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The profs teach various subjects. = 教授们教授各种科目。
The profs are holding office hours today. = 教授们今天正在举行办公时间。


一般过去时:
The profs graded the exams last week. = 教授们上周批改了考试试卷。
The profs attended the conference. = 教授们参加了会议。


现在完成时:
The profs have published many research papers. = 教授们已经发表了许多研究论文。
The profs have already finished grading. = 教授们已经批改完了。


过去完成时:
The profs had finished their lectures before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，教授们已经完成了他们的讲座。
The students had submitted their papers before the profs announced the deadline. = 在教授们宣布截止日期之前，学生们已经提交了他们的论文。


将来时:
The profs will be teaching a new course next semester. = 下学期教授们将教授一门新课程。
The profs will attend the upcoming seminar. = 教授们将参加即将举行的研讨会。


虚拟语气:
If the profs were here, they would answer your questions. = 如果教授们在这里，他们会回答你的问题。
I wish the profs had given us more time for the project. = 我希望教授们给我们更多的时间来完成这个项目。


被动语态:
The research papers were reviewed by the profs. = 研究论文是由教授们评审的。
The new curriculum is being implemented by the profs. = 新的课程正在由教授们实施。


疑问句:
Are the profs available for consultation? = 教授们有空咨询吗？
When will the profs announce the exam schedule? = 教授们什么时候会公布考试时间表？


否定句:
The profs didn't attend the meeting. = 教授们没有参加会议。
The profs haven't graded the papers yet. = 教授们还没有批改论文。
