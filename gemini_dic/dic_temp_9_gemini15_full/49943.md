dendroclimatologist:/ˌdɛndrəʊˌklaɪməˈtɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 古气候学家 | A scientist who studies past climates using tree rings.  Dendroclimatology is the science of determining past climates from tree rings.  | Example: The dendroclimatologist presented her findings on the medieval warm period.  古气候学家展示了她关于中世纪温暖期的研究结果。 | 近义词: paleoclimatologist | 反义词:  N/A
