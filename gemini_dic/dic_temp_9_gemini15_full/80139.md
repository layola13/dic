god:/ɡɑːd/| n. |神，上帝|A supreme being, deity, or spirit worshipped by people.  God is often considered the creator and ruler of the universe.  The concept of God varies significantly across different religions and belief systems. |例句：Many people believe in God. 很多人相信上帝。|例句：The priest prayed to God for guidance. 牧师向上帝祈祷寻求指引。|近义词：deity, divinity, supreme being|反义词：atheism, non-belief
