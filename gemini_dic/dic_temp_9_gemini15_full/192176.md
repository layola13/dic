scrapers:/ˈskreɪpərz/ | n. |刮刀；刮削工具；(网络)信息收集器 |A scraper is a tool used for scraping or removing material from a surface.  It can be made of various materials, such as metal, plastic, or wood, and comes in many different shapes and sizes depending on its intended use.  In the context of web scraping, a scraper is a program that automatically extracts data from websites. |例句：The painter used a scraper to remove the old paint from the wall. 画家用刮刀去除墙上的旧油漆。

The chef used a vegetable scraper to peel the carrots. 厨师用刮菜器削胡萝卜皮。
