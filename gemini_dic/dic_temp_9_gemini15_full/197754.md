shillingless:/ˈʃɪlɪŋləs/| adj.|无先令的；贫困的 | Without shillings; penniless; poor.  The word implies a lack of money, particularly in the context of the historical British shilling. |例句：After losing his job, he found himself shillingless and desperate.  在他失业后，他发现自己身无分文，走投无路。

例句：The shillingless beggar sat on the street corner, hoping for alms.  这个身无分文的乞丐坐在街角，希望能得到施舍。


近义词：penniless, impecunious, broke, destitute


反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
