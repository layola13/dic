interfederations:/ˌɪntərˌfedəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |联邦间的联系，联邦联盟|The term "interfederations" refers to relationships, interactions, or agreements between different federations or federated entities.  It implies a level of cooperation or competition between these groups. The word is less common than "inter-federation" and often used in specialized contexts like political science or international relations.|例句：The study examined the successes and failures of interfederations in managing shared resources.|近义词：inter-federation, federation alliances, cross-federation relationships|反义词：isolation, separation, independence|名词
