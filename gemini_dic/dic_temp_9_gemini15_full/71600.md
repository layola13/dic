flat-out:/ˌflætˈaʊt/| adj. adv.|彻底的；完全的；全力以赴地；坦率地|Completely; utterly; with maximum effort; frankly and directly.|例句：He denied the allegations flat-out. 他断然否认了这些指控。|例句：They worked flat-out to meet the deadline. 他们全力以赴以赶上截止日期。|近义词：completely, totally, utterly, frankly, directly|反义词：partially, incompletely, indirectly, subtly
