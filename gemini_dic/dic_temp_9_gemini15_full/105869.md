kiting:/ˈkaɪtɪŋ/| n. v.|风筝；放风筝；(金融)空头支票；|The act of flying a kite;  the practice of writing checks on insufficient funds.|例句：The children were excitedly flying their kites in the park. 孩子们在公园里兴奋地放风筝。 He was arrested for kiting checks. 他因开空头支票而被捕。|近义词：fly a kite, kite flying, check kiting|反义词：saving, depositing
