reoccurring:/ˌriːəˈkɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. |反复出现的，再发的| Happening again many times; recurring.|The reoccurring theme of betrayal in the novel was unsettling.  这本小说中反复出现的背叛主题令人不安。|His reoccurring nightmares left him exhausted. 他反复出现的噩梦让他筋疲力尽。|recurring,repeated|infrequent, occasional


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The problem reoccurs frequently. = 这个问题频繁地反复出现。
This pattern reoccurs throughout history. = 这种模式贯穿整个历史反复出现。

一般过去时:
The pain reoccurred after a few days. = 几天后疼痛又复发了。
The symptoms reoccurred last week. = 症状上周又出现了。


现在完成时:
The error has reoccurred several times. = 这个错误已经反复出现好几次了。
The problem has reoccurred since the last update. = 自上次更新以来，这个问题又反复出现了。


过去完成时:
The headache had reoccurred before she took the medicine. = 她吃药之前头痛又复发了。
The issue had reoccurred several times before they found a solution. = 这个问题在他们找到解决方案之前已经反复出现过好几次了。


将来时:
The doctor thinks the condition will reoccur. = 医生认为这种情况会再次发生。
I fear the problem will reoccur next year. = 我担心这个问题明年还会再次发生。


虚拟语气:
If the symptoms reoccurred, I would see a doctor immediately. = 如果症状再次出现，我会立即去看医生。
I wish the problem wouldn't reoccur. = 我希望这个问题不会再次发生。


被动语态:
The error was reoccurring throughout the program. =  这个错误在程序中反复出现。
The problem is reoccurring in several areas. = 这个问题在几个领域反复出现。


疑问句:
Will the problem reoccur? = 这个问题还会再次发生吗？
Does the pain reoccur often? = 疼痛经常复发吗？


否定句:
The problem didn't reoccur. = 问题没有再次发生。
The symptoms haven't reoccurred since the treatment. = 自从治疗以来，症状就没有再次出现。
