paulin:/ˈpɔːlɪn/| n. |防水油布；篷布|A heavy-duty waterproof cloth, typically made of canvas or similar material, used as a covering for loads, structures, or other items.  It often provides protection from rain, sun, or other elements.  |例句：The farmer used a paulin to protect his hay from the rain. 农民用防水油布保护他的干草免受雨淋。

例句：We secured the boat with a large paulin during the storm. 我们在暴风雨期间用一块大防水油布固定了船只。


近义词：tarpaulin, canvas cover, sheet


反义词：无


词性:名词
