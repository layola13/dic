junked:/dʒʌŋkt/| v. adj. |丢弃的；报废的；|to discard something as useless or worthless;  to get rid of something unwanted.|例句：The old car was junked because it was beyond repair.  这辆旧车因为无法修理而被报废了。|近义词：scrapped, discarded, disposed of |反义词：repaired, restored, salvaged |动词，形容词


USAGE->

They junk old appliances regularly. = 他们定期丢弃旧家电。
He junks his old clothes every year. = 他每年都丢弃旧衣服。

She junked her broken washing machine. = 她丢弃了她坏掉的洗衣机。
