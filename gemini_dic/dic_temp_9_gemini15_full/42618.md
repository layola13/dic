counterreformer:/ˈkaʊntərɪˈfɔːrmər/| n. |反宗教改革者，反对宗教改革者|A person who opposed the Protestant Reformation.  They often advocated for maintaining or restoring traditional Catholic practices and beliefs.|The Counter-Reformation saw many counterreformers rise to prominence, defending the Catholic Church against Protestant challenges.  反宗教改革时期涌现出许多著名的反宗教改革者，他们捍卫天主教对抗新教的挑战。|近义词：reactionary, opponent of the Reformation|反义词：reformer
