mine:/maɪn/| pron.|我的|Possessive pronoun indicating ownership or belonging.  It is used to show that something belongs to the speaker. |This is mine.  这是我的。|yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs|others'|pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This book is mine. = 这本书是我的。
That car is mine. = 那辆车是我的。

一般过去时:
That house was mine. = 那房子是我的。（过去拥有）
The toy was mine. =  那个玩具是我的。（过去拥有）

现在完成时:
The land has been mine for years. = 这块地是我的好多年了。
This idea has always been mine. = 这个主意一直是我的。

过去完成时:
The painting had been mine, but I sold it. = 那幅画曾经是我的，但我把它卖掉了。
The decision had already been mine before the meeting. = 在会议之前，决定就已经是我的了。


将来时:
The property will be mine soon. = 这处房产很快就会是我的。
That prize will surely be mine. = 那份奖品肯定是我的。


虚拟语气:
If it were mine, I would sell it. = 如果那是我的，我会把它卖掉。
I wish this success were mine. = 我希望这次成功是我的。


被动语态:
The decision was found to be mine. = 决定被发现是我的。
The mistake was proven to be mine. = 错误被证明是我的。


疑问句:
Is this pen mine? = 这支笔是我的吗？
Is that seat mine? = 那个座位是我的吗？


否定句:
That isn't mine. = 那不是我的。
This car is not mine. = 这辆车不是我的。


PHRASE->
all mine = 全是我的
of mine = 我的...之一 (e.g., a friend of mine)
to mine = 到我的（地方）
mine of information = 信息宝库
minefield = 雷区 (metaphorically, a difficult or dangerous situation)
