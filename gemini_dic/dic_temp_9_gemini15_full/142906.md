out-quarter:/ˌaʊtˈkwɔːrtər/| adj. |超出季度，季度外的|  Describes something that occurs outside of a specific quarter (typically a financial quarter, but can refer to other periods divided into quarters).  It often implies something unexpected or outside the normal timeframe.|例句：The project's completion was out-quarter, causing budget issues. 项目的完成超出了季度，导致预算问题。The out-quarter sales figures surprised everyone. 季度外的销售数据让所有人都大吃一惊。|近义词：extra-quarterly, beyond the quarter, outside the quarter|反义词：within the quarter, in-quarter|
