rechecking:/ˌriːˈtʃekɪŋ/| n.|复查，再检查|The act of checking something again.  It implies a previous check has already been performed, and this is a second verification.|例句：The rechecking of the data revealed a significant error.  复查数据发现了一个重大错误。

例句：After the initial inspection, a thorough rechecking was conducted. 初步检查后，进行了彻底的复查。


近义词：review, verification, double-check, inspection

反义词：acceptance (without further check)


词性：名词
