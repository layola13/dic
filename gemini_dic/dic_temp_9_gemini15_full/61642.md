emirate:/ˈɛmɪrət/ | n. |酋长国，埃米尔国 | A state ruled by an emir (a Muslim prince or ruler).  An emirate is a territory ruled by an emir, often a hereditary monarch.  Emirates are commonly found in the Arabian Peninsula. |例句：The United Arab Emirates is a federation of seven emirates.  = 阿拉伯联合酋长国是由七个酋长国组成的联邦。

The seven emirates of the UAE are independent states. = 阿联酋的七个酋长国是独立的国家。|近义词：kingdom, principality, sultanate |反义词：republic, democracy |词性:名词
