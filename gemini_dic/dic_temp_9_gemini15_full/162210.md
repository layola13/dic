prepositions:/ˌprepəˈzɪʃənz/ | n. |介词|Prepositions are words that show the relationship between a noun or pronoun and another word in the sentence. They indicate things like time, place, manner, or direction.  They often precede the noun or pronoun they modify.| Examples:  The book is *on* the table. (place)  She arrived *after* lunch. (time) He walked *with* his friend. (manner/companionship) They went *to* the store. (direction)|近义词:None (as a class of words)|反义词:None (as a class of words)|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is under the table. = 猫在桌子底下。
The book is on the shelf. = 书在书架上。


一般过去时:
The dog was in the garden. = 狗在花园里。
We walked through the park. = 我们穿过公园散步。


现在完成时:
She has been in London. = 她去过伦敦。
They have lived in this house for ten years. = 他们在这栋房子里住了十年。


过去完成时:
He had already left before I arrived. = 我到的时候他已经离开了。
They had lived in Paris before moving to New York. = 他们搬到纽约之前住在巴黎。


将来时:
She will be in Paris next week. = 她下周会在巴黎。
We will meet at the cafe tomorrow. = 我们明天将在咖啡馆见面。


虚拟语气:
If I were in your shoes, I would do differently. = 如果我是你，我会做得不一样。
If she had known, she would have helped. = 如果她知道的话，她会帮忙的。


被动语态:
The letter was sent to the wrong address. = 这封信寄错了地址。
The problem was discussed at the meeting. = 这个问题在会议上进行了讨论。


疑问句:
What is the matter with you? = 你怎么了？
Where are you going? = 你要去哪里？


否定句:
The car is not in the garage. = 车不在车库里。
He did not go to the party. = 他没有去参加聚会。


PHRASE->
according to = 根据
because of = 因为
in addition to = 除…之外
instead of = 代替
in front of = 在…前面
