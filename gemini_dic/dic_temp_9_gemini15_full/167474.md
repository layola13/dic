preventional:/prɪˈvɛnʃənəl/| adj.| 预防性的，预防的 | Relating to or concerned with prevention.  The word emphasizes the act of preventing something from happening. |例句：The government implemented preventional measures to combat the spread of the virus.  政府实施了预防措施来对抗病毒的传播。|例句：Preventional healthcare focuses on keeping people healthy rather than treating illnesses. 预防性医疗保健侧重于保持人们健康，而不是治疗疾病。|近义词：prophylactic, preventive, precautionary |反义词：curative, remedial
