proagitation:/ˌproʊˌæʒɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |煽动；鼓动；激动|The act of proagitation involves stirring up or inciting feelings or actions, often in a negative or harmful way.  It implies a deliberate attempt to influence others, often to cause unrest or conflict. |例句：The proagitation of racial hatred led to widespread violence. 煽动种族仇恨导致了广泛的暴力。|例句：The government accused the opposition party of proagitation against the new law. 政府指责反对党煽动反对新法律。|近义词：incitement, agitation, instigation, fomentation |反义词：pacification, appeasement, conciliation|名词
