malnutrition:/ˌmælˌnjuːˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |营养不良 |Lack of proper nutrition, caused by not having enough to eat, not eating enough of the right things, or being unable to use the food that one does eat.  It can lead to various health problems.  |例句：Malnutrition is a serious problem in many developing countries. 营养不良是许多发展中国家面临的严重问题。

例句：The children suffered from severe malnutrition after the famine. 饥荒过后，孩子们遭受了严重的营养不良。


近义词：undernourishment, starvation (in severe cases) |反义词：good nutrition, well-nourished


词性：名词
