pimenta:/pɪˈmɛntə/| n. |皮曼塔，牙买加胡椒|Pimenta is a genus of flowering plants in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.  The most well-known species is *Pimenta dioica*, also known as allspice, Jamaica pepper, or pimento.  The dried unripe berries of this plant are used as a spice. |The recipe calls for pimento peppers and other spices.|近义词：allspice, Jamaica pepper|反义词：无
