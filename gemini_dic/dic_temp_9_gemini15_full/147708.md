overpreoccupying: /ˌoʊvərˈpriːɒkjʊpaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度担忧的；过度专注的 | excessively preoccupied; overly focused on something to the point of distraction or detriment.  | The overpreoccupying nature of his work led to burnout.  他的工作过度让他筋疲力尽。|近义词: overly concerned, excessively focused, obsessed | 反义词: unconcerned, indifferent, detached
