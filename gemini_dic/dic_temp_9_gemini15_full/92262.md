hyperventilated: /ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪtɪd/ | v. |过度换气|

英文解析:To breathe too quickly and deeply, often causing dizziness or other symptoms.  This is often a response to stress, anxiety, or panic.

例句:
She hyperventilated after receiving the bad news. = 她收到坏消息后过度换气。

He hyperventilated during the intense workout. = 他在剧烈运动时过度换气。


近义词: overbreathe, breathe rapidly

反义词:  underbreathe (less common), breathe slowly and shallowly
