prodistribution:/ˌproʊˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 产品分销，产品配送 | The process of distributing products to consumers or businesses.  It encompasses all the activities involved in getting a product from the producer to the end user.  This includes warehousing, transportation, and marketing strategies. | Example: The company's efficient prodistribution network ensured timely delivery of goods to retailers across the country.  公司高效的产品分销网络确保了货物及时送达全国各地的零售商。 | 近义词：distribution, delivery, dissemination | 反义词：centralization, concentration | 名词
