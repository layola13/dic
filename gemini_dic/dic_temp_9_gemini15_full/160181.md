ponderal:/ˈpɒndərəl/| adj. |重量的；体重的；物质的|Relating to weight or mass.  The term is often used in the context of body weight or the weight of substances.|例句：The study focused on the ponderal index, a measure of body weight relative to height.  这项研究的重点是体重指数，它是衡量身高体重比的指标。|近义词：weight-related, mass-related |反义词：weightless, massless
