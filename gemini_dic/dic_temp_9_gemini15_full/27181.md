calligraphist:/kəˈlɪɡrəfɪst/| n. |书法家，书写家 | A calligraphist is an artist who practices calligraphy, the art of beautiful handwriting.  They are skilled in the use of various writing tools and styles to create aesthetically pleasing lettering. | The calligraphist carefully crafted each character, ensuring perfect balance and flow.  书法家仔细地创作每一个字，确保完美的平衡和流畅。| artist, scribe, penman |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
