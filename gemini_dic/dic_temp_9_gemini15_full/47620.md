debentures:/dɪˈbentʃərz/ | n. |债券；公司债券 |  A debenture is a type of debt instrument, similar to a bond, issued by a company to raise capital.  Unlike bonds, debentures are usually unsecured, meaning they are not backed by any specific asset.  They represent a loan made to the company by an investor, and the company promises to repay the principal amount along with interest at a specified rate and time. | Example: The company issued debentures to finance its expansion project.  这家公司发行债券为其扩张项目融资。 The investors bought company debentures hoping for a high return. 投资者购买公司债券，希望能获得高回报。 | 近义词：bond, loan, note | 反义词：equity, ownership
