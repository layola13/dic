entertained:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnd/ | vt. | 使快乐；款待；招待； 娱乐 | To provide entertainment for someone; to keep someone amused or occupied.  |例句：The clown entertained the children at the party. 小丑在派对上逗孩子们开心。

The comedian entertained the audience with his jokes.  喜剧演员用他的笑话逗乐了观众。| amuse, divert, please | bored, displeased |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He entertains his guests with stories. 他用故事招待他的客人。
She entertains the children with magic tricks. 她用魔术逗孩子们开心。

一般过去时:
We entertained our friends last night. 我们昨晚招待了朋友。
The singer entertained the crowd with her beautiful voice. 歌手用她优美的歌声娱乐了人群。

现在完成时:
They have entertained many visitors this year. 他们今年招待了许多访客。
She has entertained us with her singing for years. 多年来，她一直用她的歌声娱乐我们。

过去完成时:
We had entertained many guests before the party started. 在派对开始之前，我们已经招待了许多客人。
He had entertained the idea of moving to the countryside. 他曾考虑过搬到乡下。


将来时:
We will entertain our relatives during the holidays. 我们将在假期期间招待我们的亲戚。
The band will entertain the guests at the wedding. 乐队将在婚礼上为宾客们演奏。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would entertain my friends more often. 如果我有更多钱，我会更频繁地招待我的朋友。
I wish I could entertain my guests better. 我希望我能更好地招待我的客人。

被动语态:
The guests were entertained by the magician. 客人们被魔术师逗乐了。
The audience was entertained by the performance. 观众被演出所吸引。

疑问句:
Did you entertain the children well? 你把孩子们招待得好吗？
How will you entertain the guests tomorrow? 你明天将如何招待客人？

否定句:

He didn't entertain the idea of quitting his job. 他没有考虑过辞职。
