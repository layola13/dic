/ˌɒnəˈmætɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌɑːnəˈmætɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |拟声词学家|A person who studies onomatopoeia.  An onomatologist researches the creation, use, and effects of words that imitate sounds. They might analyze how different languages represent the same sounds, explore the cultural significance of onomatopoeia, or investigate its role in literature and music.|The onomatologist presented a fascinating lecture on the evolution of animal sounds in language. |  |  |n.
