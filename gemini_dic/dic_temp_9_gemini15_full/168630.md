procoelian:/proʊˈsiːliən/ | adj. |前凹的；前关节突凹的|  Relating to or characterized by having the anterior articular surface of a vertebra concave.  This term is primarily used in zoology and paleontology to describe the type of articulation between vertebrae in certain animals, particularly amphibians and reptiles.  A procoelian vertebra has a concave anterior (front) surface and a convex posterior (rear) surface.|

例句：The fossil remains revealed procoelian vertebrae, characteristic of early amphibians. = 化石遗骸显示出前凹椎骨，这是早期两栖动物的特征。

例句：Many extinct reptiles possessed procoelian vertebrae, contributing to their unique skeletal structure. = 许多已灭绝的爬行动物拥有前凹椎骨，这有助于它们独特的骨骼结构。


近义词：opisthocoelous (反义词), amphicoelous (不同类型)

反义词：opisthocoelous (后凹的)


词性：形容词
