over-laying: /ˌoʊvərˈleɪɪŋ/ | v. |覆盖；叠加；压盖 |  To place or lie over something else; to superimpose.  |例句：The new road overlies the old Roman road. 新路覆盖在古罗马道路之上。

例句：The artist overlaid the watercolor with a thin layer of acrylic. 艺术家在水彩画上覆盖了一层薄薄的丙烯颜料。


近义词：superimpose, cover, overlay,  superstrate


反义词：uncover, reveal, expose


词性：动词
