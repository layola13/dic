nonviviparous:/ˌnɒnvaɪˈvɪpərəs/ | adj. |非胎生的| Not giving birth to live young; laying eggs.  This term describes animals that reproduce by laying eggs, as opposed to giving birth to live young. The prefix "non-" indicates the negation of "viviparous," which means giving birth to live young.|例句：Most reptiles are nonviviparous, laying eggs instead of giving birth to live young.  大多数爬行动物都是卵生的，而不是胎生的。|近义词：oviparous, egg-laying |反义词：viviparous
