grebes:/ɡriːbz/| n.|鸊鷉|A grebe is a water bird with a long neck, a pointed bill, and lobed feet. They are excellent swimmers and divers, and are often seen on lakes and ponds.|例句：The grebes were diving for fish in the clear water. 鸊鷉们在清澈的水中潜水捕鱼。|例句：We saw a pair of grebes building a nest on the lake. 我们看到一对鸊鷉在湖上筑巢。|近义词：diver (潜水鸟)|反义词：无
