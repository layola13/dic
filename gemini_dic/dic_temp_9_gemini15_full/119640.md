middle-of-the-roadism: /ˌmɪdəl əv ðə ˈroʊdɪzəm/ | n. | 中庸主义；折中主义 |

英文解析:The policy or practice of avoiding extreme views or actions, typically by taking a moderate approach.  It often implies a lack of strong conviction or commitment to any particular ideology or cause.

例句:

His middle-of-the-roadism on economic policy alienated both the left and the right.  他的经济政策中庸之道疏远了左右两派。

The party's platform reflects a deliberate strategy of middle-of-the-roadism to appeal to a wider range of voters.  该党的纲领反映出一种刻意采取中庸之道的策略，以吸引更广泛的选民。


近义词: moderation, centrism, compromise, pragmatism


反义词: extremism, radicalism, dogmatism


词性: noun
