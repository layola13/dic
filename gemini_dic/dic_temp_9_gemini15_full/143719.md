outlining: /ˈaʊtlaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 概述；描绘轮廓；列提纲 | To make a summary of the main points of something.  To draw the outline of something. | 例句：He outlined his plan to the board. 他向董事会概述了他的计划。  She carefully outlined the shape of the vase before painting it.  她在绘画花瓶之前仔细勾勒出它的形状。| 近义词：summarizing, sketching, summarizing, schematizing | 反义词：detailing, elaborating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outlines his arguments clearly. = 他清晰地概述了他的论点。
She outlines the main points of the lecture. = 她概述了讲座的主要内容。

一般过去时:
The professor outlined the key concepts of the course. = 教授概述了课程的关键概念。
I outlined my travel plans to my family. = 我向家人概述了我的旅行计划。

现在完成时:
She has outlined the project proposal for the clients. = 她已经为客户概述了项目提案。
They have outlined the steps necessary for success. = 他们已经概述了成功所需的步骤。

过去完成时:
He had outlined his research findings before the presentation. = 他在演示之前已经概述了他的研究结果。
They had outlined the budget before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经概述了预算。

将来时:
She will outline the new marketing strategy tomorrow. = 她明天将概述新的营销策略。
He will outline the process step by step. = 他将逐步概述这个过程。

虚拟语气:
If I outlined my ideas more clearly, they would understand better. = 如果我更清晰地概述我的想法，他们会更好地理解。
I wish I had outlined my presentation more effectively. = 我真希望我更有效地概述了我的演示文稿。

被动语态:
The main points of the speech were outlined in the introduction. = 演讲的主要内容在引言中已概述。
The project plan will be outlined in detail next week. = 项目计划将在下周详细概述。

疑问句:
How will you outline your research paper? = 你将如何概述你的研究论文？
Did he outline the risks involved? = 他是否概述了所涉及的风险？

否定句:
He didn't outline his reasons for leaving. = 他没有概述他离开的原因。
She didn't outline the details of the contract. = 她没有概述合同的细节。
