misjudging:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒɪŋ/| v.|误判，错估|To make a wrong judgment about someone or something; to misunderstand.|例句：I was misjudging the situation. 我误判了形势。  He was misjudging her intentions. 他误解了她的意图。|近义词：misinterpret, miscalculate, underestimate|反义词：understand, correctly assess


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misjudges people easily. = 他很容易误判别人。
She misjudges the market trends regularly. = 她经常误判市场趋势。


一般过去时:
I misjudged his character. = 我误判了他的性格。
They misjudged the difficulty of the task. = 他们误判了任务的难度。


现在完成时:
We have misjudged the impact of the change. = 我们误判了变化的影响。
She has misjudged him repeatedly. = 她反复误判了他。


过去完成时:
He had misjudged the distance and run out of gas. = 他误判了距离，结果汽油用完了。
They had misjudged the severity of the storm. = 他们误判了风暴的严重性。


将来时:
I fear I will misjudge the situation again. = 我担心我会再次误判形势。
They will likely misjudge the consequences of their actions. = 他们很可能会误判其行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If I had not misjudged him, I wouldn’t have lost so much money. = 如果我没有误判他，我就不会损失这么多钱。
I wish I hadn't misjudged her abilities. = 我真希望我没有误判她的能力。


被动语态:
The situation was misjudged by the experts. = 专家们误判了形势。
His intentions were misjudged by his colleagues. = 他的意图被同事们误解了。


疑问句:
Did I misjudge the situation? = 我是不是误判了形势？
Could they have misjudged the risks involved? = 他们是否可能误判了所涉及的风险？


否定句:
I didn't misjudge his capabilities. = 我没有误判他的能力。
They didn't misjudge the importance of the meeting. = 他们没有误判会议的重要性。
