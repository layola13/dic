immoderation:/ˌɪmɒdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |放纵；过度；无节制 |Lack of moderation; excessive indulgence.  It often refers to excessive indulgence in something, especially food, drink, or pleasure. |例句：His immoderation in eating led to health problems.他的饮食无节制导致了健康问题。

例句：Her immoderation with spending resulted in significant debt. 她的过度消费导致了巨额债务。


近义词：excess, extravagance, intemperance, overindulgence


反义词：moderation, temperance, restraint, self-control


词源：
* **im-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **moder-**:  root derived from Latin "moderātus," meaning "moderate," "tempered," or "regulated."
* **-ation**: suffix forming nouns of action or process.
