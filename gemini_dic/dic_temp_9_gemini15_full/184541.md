restiness:/ˈrɛstɪnəs/| adj. |焦躁不安；坐立不安|A feeling of unease, impatience, or restlessness.  It often implies a desire for activity or change.|例句：The long wait increased his restiness. 漫长的等待加剧了他的焦躁不安。|例句：Her restiness was evident in her constant fidgeting. 她坐立不安，这从她不停地动来动去就能看出来。|近义词：unease, impatience, fidgetiness, restlessness, anxiety|反义词：calmness, tranquility, peacefulness
