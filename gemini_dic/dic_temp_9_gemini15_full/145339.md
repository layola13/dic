overbudget: /ˌoʊvərˈbʌdʒɪt/ | adj. | 超支的，预算超支的 | exceeding the planned or allowed budget.  | The project went overbudget by $10,000.  这个项目超支了一万美元。 | exceeding, in excess of | underbudget


USAGE->

The project is overbudget. = 项目超支了。
We are overbudget this month. = 这个月我们超支了。

一般过去时:
