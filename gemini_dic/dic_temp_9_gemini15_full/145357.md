overburden:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdn/| vt. n.|使负担过重；过重的负担 |To place too great a burden on someone or something; an excessive burden.|例句：The company is overburdened with debt. 公司负债累累。|例句：The overburden of paperwork is slowing down the project. 过多的文书工作正在拖慢项目进度。|近义词：overload, overwhelm, tax|反义词：underburden, relieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy workload overburdens the employees. =沉重的工作量使员工不堪重负。
This system overburdens the network. =这个系统使网络不堪重负。
