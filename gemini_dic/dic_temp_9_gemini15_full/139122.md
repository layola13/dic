obeisance:/oˈbeɪsns/| n. |敬礼；尊敬；顺从 |The act of obedience or respect; a gesture of deference.  It often involves bowing or prostrating oneself. |例句：He made a deep obeisance to the king. 他向国王深深鞠躬。  The courtiers paid obeisance to the queen. 朝臣们向王后行礼。|近义词：homage, reverence, deference, submission |反义词：defiance, disrespect, insubordination |名词
