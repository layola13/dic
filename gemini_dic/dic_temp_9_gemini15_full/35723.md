coccolite:/ˈkɒkəlaɪt/| n. |颗石|A small, discoidal grain of calcite, typically found in pelagic sediments.| Coccoliths are tiny calcite plates found in the shells of coccolithophores, a type of single-celled algae.  These coccoliths accumulate on the seafloor to form coccolith ooze, a type of pelagic sediment. |  The ocean floor is covered with layers of coccolith ooze. 海底覆盖着几层颗石软泥。|  nannofossil, calcite grain |  (no direct antonym)
