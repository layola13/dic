howled:/haʊld/ | v. | 嚎叫；怒吼；大声喊叫 | To make a long, loud, mournful cry, like a wolf or dog.  It can also describe the sound of wind or other things. | 例句：The wolves howled at the moon.  狼对着月亮嚎叫。

The wind howled fiercely through the trees. 风在树林里猛烈地呼啸。

近义词：roared, wailed, yelled, screamed | 反义词：whispered, murmured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog howls every night. = 这条狗每晚都嚎叫。
He howls with laughter. = 他放声大笑。

一般过去时:
The baby howled when his mother left. = 婴儿在母亲离开时嚎啕大哭。
The wind howled throughout the night. = 风整夜呼啸。

现在完成时:
The dog has howled all night. = 这条狗嚎叫了一整夜。
She has howled in frustration many times. = 她多次因沮丧而大声哭喊。

过去完成时:
The wolf had howled before the hunter arrived. = 在猎人到来之前，狼已经嚎叫了。
They had howled their protests for hours. = 他们已经抗议了几个小时。

将来时:
The wolves will howl when they hear the prey. = 当狼听到猎物时，它们将会嚎叫。
The storm will howl all night. = 暴风雨会整夜呼啸。

虚拟语气:
If the wind howled, we would stay inside. = 如果风呼啸，我们就待在里面。
I wish the dog wouldn't howl so much. = 我希望狗不要叫得这么厉害。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His complaints were howled down by the crowd. = 他的抱怨被人群淹没了。（这里howl down表示喝倒彩）
The protests were howled down by the authorities. = 抗议活动被当局压制了。（这里howl down表示喝倒彩）


疑问句:
Does the dog howl often? = 这条狗经常嚎叫吗？
Why did the children howl? = 孩子们为什么嚎叫？

否定句:
The dog didn't howl last night. = 这条狗昨晚没有叫。
The wind didn't howl as fiercely as expected. = 风并没有像预期的那样猛烈地呼啸。


PHRASE->
howl with laughter = 大笑
howl in pain = 痛苦地嚎叫
howl for help = 呼救
howl at the moon = 对着月亮嚎叫
howl of protest = 抗议声


词源:
源自古英语 *hūlian，意思是“哭泣，嚎叫”。与荷兰语 huilen 和德语 heulen 相关。
