modifies:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪz/ /ˈmɑːdəfaɪz/| v.|修改；更改；修饰|To change the form, nature, or qualities of something; to alter.|The artist modifies his paintings before exhibiting them.  艺术家在展览前修改了他的画作。


The editor modifies the manuscript to improve clarity. 编辑修改了手稿以提高清晰度。


近义词：alter, change, adjust, amend, revise|反义词：maintain, preserve, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He modifies his diet to lose weight. 他修改他的饮食以减肥。
She modifies her approach to each client. 她根据每个客户的情况调整自己的方法。


一般过去时:
They modified the design after receiving feedback. 他们在收到反馈后修改了设计。
He modified his statement to be more accurate. 他修改了他的陈述使其更准确。


现在完成时:
She has modified her resume several times. 她已经修改过几次简历了。
They have modified the software to fix the bugs. 他们已经修改了软件来修复错误。


过去完成时:
He had modified the engine before the race. 他在比赛前已经修改了发动机。
They had modified their plans due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，他们已经修改了他们的计划。


将来时:
We will modify the contract to reflect the new terms. 我们将修改合同以反映新的条款。
She will modify her strategy based on the market trend. 她将根据市场趋势修改她的策略。


虚拟语气:
If he modified his behavior, he would have more friends. 如果他改变他的行为，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish she had modified her tone earlier. 我希望她早点修改语气。


被动语态:
The software was modified by the developers. 软件由开发人员修改。
The plans were modified to accommodate the new information. 计划被修改以适应新的信息。


疑问句:
Will you modify the report before submitting it? 你会在提交报告之前修改报告吗？
Have they modified the system yet? 他们已经修改系统了吗？


否定句:
He did not modify his opinion despite the evidence. 尽管有证据，他也没有修改他的意见。
