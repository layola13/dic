marketeers:/ˌmɑːrkiˈtɪərz/| n. |市场营销人员，营销专家 |People involved in the marketing of goods or services.  They develop and implement strategies to promote and sell products or services. |例句：The marketeers launched a new advertising campaign to boost sales. 市场营销人员发起了一场新的广告宣传活动以提高销量。

例句：Experienced marketeers know how to target their audience effectively. 经验丰富的市场营销人员知道如何有效地定位目标受众。


近义词：marketers, advertising professionals, sales representatives, promotion managers| 反义词：无


词性：名词
