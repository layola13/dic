sheepherder: /ˈʃiːpˌhɜːrdər/ | n. | 牧羊人 | A person who herds and tends sheep.  | Example: The sheepherder skillfully guided his flock across the mountain pass.  牧羊人熟练地引导他的羊群越过山口。 | 近义词：shepherd, stockman | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who works with other animals or in a different occupation.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sheepherder watches over his flock daily. = 牧羊人每天照看他的羊群。
He herds his sheep to new pastures. = 他把羊群赶到新的牧场。


一般过去时:
The sheepherder drove the sheep to the valley. = 牧羊人把羊群赶到了山谷。
He carefully counted the sheep yesterday. = 他昨天仔细地数了羊。


现在完成时:
The sheepherder has herded sheep for twenty years. =这位牧羊人养羊二十年了。
He has lost some sheep in the storm. = 他在暴风雨中丢失了一些羊。


过去完成时:
The sheepherder had already moved the flock before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，牧羊人已经把羊群转移了。
He had never seen such a large flock before. = 他以前从未见过这么大的羊群。


将来时:
The sheepherder will move the flock to higher ground tomorrow. = 牧羊人明天将把羊群移到更高的地势。
He will shear the sheep next week. = 他下周将剪羊毛。


虚拟语气:
If I were a sheepherder, I would live a peaceful life. = 如果我是一个牧羊人，我会过着平静的生活。
I wish I could be a sheepherder and live in the mountains. = 我希望我能成为一名牧羊人，住在山上。


被动语态:
The sheep were herded by the experienced sheepherder. = 羊群由经验丰富的牧羊人看管。
The sheep are being moved to a new pasture. = 羊群正在被转移到新的牧场。


疑问句:
Is the sheepherder still with his flock? = 牧羊人还在他的羊群身边吗？
Where does the sheepherder live? = 牧羊人住在哪里？


否定句:
The sheepherder didn't see any wolves today. = 牧羊人今天没有看到任何狼。
He wasn't able to find all his sheep. = 他没能找到所有的羊。
