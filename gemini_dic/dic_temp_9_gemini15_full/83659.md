haemosiderosis:/ˌhiːməʊsaɪdəˈroʊsɪs/| n. |含铁血黄素沉着症|A condition in which hemosiderin (a brown pigment containing iron) is deposited in tissues and organs.  This is often a result of bleeding into tissues, such as in pulmonary hemosiderosis (bleeding into the lungs).|Example:  Pulmonary hemosiderosis can cause shortness of breath and coughing up blood.|近义词：iron deposition, siderosis|反义词：无
