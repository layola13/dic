It often implies a sense of pride or satisfaction in one's achievement or ability.|例句：His exuperation was evident in his boastful manner. 他的优越感在他自夸的态度中显而易见。  The team felt a sense of exuperation after their victory.  球队在获胜后感到一种优越感。|近义词：superiority, triumph, elation, exaltation|反义词：inferiority, defeat, dejection, humiliation|名词
