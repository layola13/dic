mismesh:/ˈmɪʃmɛʃ/| n.|混杂；混乱；杂乱无章|A mismesh is a confused or disordered mixture of things. It implies a lack of organization or coherence.  The term often suggests a chaotic or tangled state.|例句：The artist's studio was a complete mismesh of paint tubes, brushes, and canvases.  这位艺术家的工作室里画笔、颜料管和画布混杂在一起，一片混乱。|近义词：jumble, tangle, muddle, mess, chaos|反义词：order, organization, arrangement|名词
