rehardening:/ˌriːˈhɑːrdənɪŋ/ | n. | 再硬化，二次硬化 | The process of hardening a material again after it has been softened or weakened.  This often involves a heat treatment process.  Rehardening is crucial in restoring the strength and durability of materials that have undergone wear or damage. | Example: The rehardening of the steel tools extended their lifespan significantly.  再硬化钢制工具显著延长了其使用寿命。 |近义词：retempering, re-hardening | 反义词：softening, annealing |
