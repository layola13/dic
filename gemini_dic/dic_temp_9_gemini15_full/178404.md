reconnoitering: /rɪˈkɑːnəˌtɜːrɪŋ/ | v. |侦察，勘察 | To explore (a region) in order to gain information about the enemy or the terrain.  |例句：The soldiers were reconnoitering the enemy's position before the attack.  士兵们在攻击前正在侦察敌人的位置。

例句：The team spent a week reconnoitering the area for suitable locations to build their new camp.  团队花了一周时间勘察该地区，寻找合适的营地位置。


近义词：scouting, surveying, exploring, investigating | 反义词：retreating, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reconnoitering the area. = 他们在侦察该地区。
We reconnoiter the terrain before each mission. = 我们在每次任务前都会侦察地形。


一般过去时:
The scouts reconnoitered the enemy's defenses. = 侦察兵侦察了敌人的防御工事。
They reconnoitered the route carefully before proceeding. = 他们在继续前进之前仔细侦察了路线。


现在完成时:
The army has reconnoitered the border region. = 军队已经侦察了边境地区。
They have reconnoitered the area multiple times. = 他们已经多次侦察了该地区。


过去完成时:
They had reconnoitered the area before the main force arrived. = 主要部队到达之前，他们已经侦察了该地区。
The general had reconnoitered the battlefield personally before the battle. = 将军在战斗前亲自侦察了战场。


将来时:
We will reconnoiter the enemy's position tomorrow. = 我们明天将侦察敌人的位置。
The team will reconnoiter the route before the expedition. = 团队将在探险之前侦察路线。


虚拟语气:
If we had reconnoitered more thoroughly, we would have avoided the ambush. = 如果我们更彻底地侦察过，我们就不会遭到伏击。
I wish we had reconnoitered the terrain more carefully. = 我真希望我们更仔细地侦察过地形。


被动语态:
The area was being reconnoitered by the enemy. = 该地区正在被敌人侦察。
The terrain was reconnoitered before the construction began. = 在施工开始之前，地形已经被侦察过了。


疑问句:
Have they reconnoitered the enemy's positions yet? = 他们已经侦察敌人的阵地了吗？
Were the scouts reconnoitering the area at night? = 侦察兵在夜间侦察该地区吗？


否定句:
We did not reconnoiter the area properly. = 我们没有正确地侦察该地区。
They failed to reconnoiter the enemy's defenses adequately. = 他们未能充分侦察敌人的防御工事。

词源:
From French *reconnoître* (to recognize), from Latin *re-cognoscere* (to know again).  The "-ing" form is used as a gerund (verbal noun).
