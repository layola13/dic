peacenik:/ˈpiːsənɪk/| n. |和平主义者|A person who advocates peace, especially one who actively protests against war or violence.|例句：Many peaceniks demonstrated against the war. 许多和平主义者示威反对战争。|近义词：pacifist, peace activist|反义词：warmonger, hawk
