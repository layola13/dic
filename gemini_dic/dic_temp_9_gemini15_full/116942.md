mazedly:/ˈmeɪzɪdli/| adv.| 迷茫地；困惑地 | in a dazed or confused manner; bewilderedly. |例句：He looked mazedly around the unfamiliar room. 他茫然地环顾着陌生的房间。

例句：She wandered mazedly through the crowded streets. 她茫然地走在拥挤的街道上。


近义词：bewilderedly, confusedly, disorientedly

反义词：clearly, confidently, assuredly
