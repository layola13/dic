relabelled:/ˌriːˈleɪbəld/| v. |重新贴标签；重新命名 |To give a new label to something.  This often implies changing the name, description, or categorization of something. |例句：The company relabelled its products to appeal to a younger market. 公司重新为其产品贴标签以吸引更年轻的市场。
The old files were relabelled and reorganized. 旧文件被重新贴上标签并进行了重新整理。|近义词：relabeled, renamed, reclassified |反义词：originally labelled, kept the original label
