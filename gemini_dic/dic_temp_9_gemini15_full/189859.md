sambur:/ˈsæmbər/| n.|萨巴鲁鹿，赤鹿|A large deer (Rusa unicolor) of southern Asia, having a reddish-brown coat.  It is also known as the sambar deer.|例句：The hunter spotted a magnificent sambur in the dense jungle.  猎人看见一头雄伟的萨巴鲁鹿在茂密的丛林里。

例句：We saw a herd of sambur grazing peacefully near the river. 我们看到一群萨巴鲁鹿在河边平静地吃草。|近义词：deer, rusa|反义词：无|词性：名词
