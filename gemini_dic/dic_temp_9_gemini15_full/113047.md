lumme:/ˈlʌmi/| int.|哎呀，我的天哪！|An exclamation expressing surprise, annoyance, or disbelief, similar to "Goodness!" or "Oh my!"  It is primarily used in informal settings and is considered somewhat vulgar in formal contexts. The etymology is uncertain, possibly a variant of "lummy," itself of uncertain origin.|例句： Lumme, look at the size of that fish!  哎呀，看看那条鱼有多大！  Lumme, I've lost my keys again! 哎呀，我又把钥匙弄丢了！|近义词：Goodness!, Oh my!, Wow!, Crikey!, Blimey!|反义词:None specifically.|感叹词
