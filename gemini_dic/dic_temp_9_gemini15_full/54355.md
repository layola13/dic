dishonor:/dɪsˈɑːnər/| n., vt.| 耻辱；丢脸；不履行；拒绝接受| The act of dishonoring someone or something; a shameful act; a lack of honor; to refuse to accept or fulfill; to bring shame or disgrace upon.|例句：The soldier faced dishonor for deserting his post.  士兵因擅离职守而蒙受耻辱。


例句：He dishonored his family by his actions. 他因自己的行为而使他的家庭蒙羞。|近义词：shame, disgrace, disrespect, insult, violate|反义词：honor, respect, uphold
