scourers:/ˈskaʊərz/ | n. |擦洗器，清洁剂 |A scourer is a tool or substance used for cleaning, typically made of abrasive material.  They are often used for scrubbing pots and pans. |Example:  She used a steel scourer to clean the burnt pan. 她用钢丝球擦洗了烧焦的平底锅。|近义词：cleaners, scrubbers, scouring pads |反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would relate to the action of not cleaning.
