shovellike:/ˈʃʌvəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像铲子的；铲状的 | Resembling a shovel in shape or function.  The word combines "shovel" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance. |例句：The large, shovellike leaves of the plant helped it collect rainwater.  这植物又大又像铲子的叶子帮助它收集雨水。

The tool had a shovellike scoop at the end for moving dirt.  这个工具末端有一个像铲子一样的铲斗用来搬运泥土。


近义词：spade-shaped, scoop-shaped, trowel-like | 反义词：N/A


词源:

* **shovel:**  From Middle English *schofel*, from Old English *scofl*, probably of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *schop* and German *Schaufel*.  The ultimate origin is uncertain, but possibly related to words meaning "to push" or "to scoop."
* **-like:** This suffix is common in English and derives from Old English *līċ*, meaning "body," "form," or "likeness."  It creates adjectives indicating resemblance.
