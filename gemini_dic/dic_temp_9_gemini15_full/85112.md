hardening:/ˈhɑːrdənɪŋ/| n. |硬化；变硬；强化|The process of becoming hard or firm;  the act of making something hard or firm.  It can refer to physical materials or abstract concepts.|例句：The hardening of the concrete took several days. 混凝土的硬化花了几天时间。
例句：The company's hardening of its stance on layoffs surprised many employees. 公司在裁员问题上态度强硬，让许多员工感到意外。|近义词：solidification, strengthening, toughening |反义词：softening, weakening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The metal is hardening. 金属正在硬化。
The government is hardening its position on immigration. 政府正在收紧其移民政策。

一般过去时:
The clay hardened overnight. 粘土一夜之间变硬了。
The negotiations hardened into an impasse. 谈判陷入了僵局。

现在完成时:
The paint has hardened. 油漆已经干了。
The relationship has hardened over time. 这段关系随着时间的推移而变得紧张。

过去完成时:
The concrete had hardened before the rain started.  下雨之前混凝土已经硬化了。
The negotiations had hardened to the point of no return. 谈判已经僵化到无法挽回的地步。

将来时:
The steel will harden as it cools. 钢冷却后会变硬。
The government will harden its stance on crime. 政府将对犯罪采取强硬立场。

虚拟语气:
If the mixture hardened quickly, we could proceed to the next step. 如果混合物快速凝固，我们就可以进行下一步。
I wish the cement had hardened faster. 我希望水泥凝固得更快一些。

被动语态:
The steel was hardened by quenching. 钢通过淬火而硬化。
The policy was hardened in response to public pressure. 由于公众压力，政策变得更加强硬。

疑问句:
Is the concrete hardening properly? 混凝土是否正在正确地硬化？
Has the company hardened its position on the merger? 公司是否已经对并购案采取了强硬立场？

否定句:
The wood didn't harden as expected. 木头没有像预期的那样变硬。
The government hasn't hardened its stance on the issue. 政府尚未对这个问题采取强硬立场。
