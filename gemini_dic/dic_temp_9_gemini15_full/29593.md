catechize:/ˈkætɪkaɪz/| vt.|教导（尤指宗教教义）；质问；盘问|to instruct systematically, especially in religious doctrine; to question closely; to interrogate|例句：The priest catechized the children on the tenets of the faith.  牧师向孩子们讲解信仰教义。

例句：The police catechized the suspect for hours.  警方盘问了嫌疑犯数小时。

近义词：instruct, teach, question, interrogate|反义词：ignore, neglect|词性：动词

He catechizes the students every Tuesday. 他每周二都会给学生们上课。
She catechizes her children on Bible stories. 她向孩子们讲解圣经故事。
