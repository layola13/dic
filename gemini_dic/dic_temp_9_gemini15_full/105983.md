knack:/næk/| n.|诀窍；熟练技术；天赋|a special skill or talent that somebody has|He has a knack for languages. 他有语言天赋。|近义词：talent, skill, aptitude|反义词：inability, incompetence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a knack for solving puzzles. = 他有解谜的天赋。
She has a knack for making people feel comfortable. = 她有一种能让别人感到舒适的诀窍。

一般过去时:
He had a knack for remembering faces. = 他过去很擅长记住面孔。
She had a knack for making delicious cakes. = 她过去很擅长做美味的蛋糕。

现在完成时:
He has developed a knack for public speaking. = 他已经培养出了在公众面前演讲的技巧。
She has acquired a knack for playing the piano. = 她已经掌握了弹钢琴的技巧。

过去完成时:
He had already developed a knack for painting before he went to art school. = 他在上艺术学校之前就已经培养出了绘画的技巧。
She had already acquired a knack for writing before she started her blog. = 她开始写博客之前就已经掌握了写作的技巧。

将来时:
He will have a knack for coding by the end of the course. = 到课程结束时，他将掌握编程的技巧。
She will develop a knack for negotiation over time. = 她将随着时间的推移培养出谈判的技巧。

虚拟语气:
If he had a knack for cooking, he would open a restaurant. = 如果他有烹饪的天赋，他就会开一家餐馆。
If she had a knack for drawing, she would be an artist. = 如果她有绘画的天赋，她就会成为一名艺术家。

被动语态:
His knack for problem-solving was admired by everyone. = 他解决问题的能力受到了所有人的赞赏。(Knack本身难以用被动语态表达，此句改为对knack的体现被动)
Her knack for organization was utilized effectively by the team. = 团队有效地利用了她组织能力。(Knack本身难以用被动语态表达，此句改为对knack的体现被动)

疑问句:
Does he have a knack for fixing cars? = 他有修理汽车的技巧吗？
Does she have a knack for writing poetry? = 她有写诗的天赋吗？

否定句:
He doesn't have a knack for mathematics. = 他没有数学天赋。
She doesn't have a knack for public speaking. = 她不擅长在公众面前演讲。


PHRASE->
a knack for:  对…有天赋/诀窍
have a knack for: 擅长…
the knack of: …的诀窍
lose one's knack for: 失去…的技巧
pick up a knack for:  学会…的技巧
