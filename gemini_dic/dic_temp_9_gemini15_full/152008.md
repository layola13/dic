patched:/pæʧt/ | v. adj. n. | 修补；修补过的；补丁 | To mend or repair something by covering a hole or tear.  |例句：He patched the hole in his jeans. 他缝补了他牛仔裤上的洞。 The old road was patched with new asphalt. 那条旧路用新的沥青修补了。|近义词：mend, repair, fix | 反义词：destroy, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He patches his old clothes regularly. = 他定期缝补旧衣服。
She patches the cracks in the wall. = 她修补墙壁上的裂缝。

一般过去时:
The mechanic patched the tire. = 机械师修补了轮胎。
They patched the roof after the storm. = 暴风雨后他们修补了屋顶。

现在完成时:
She has patched up the argument with her brother. = 她已经和哥哥和解了。
He has patched the hole in the boat. = 他已经修补了船上的洞。

过去完成时:
They had patched the road before the race. = 比赛前他们已经修补了道路。
She had patched the quilt before giving it away. = 她送出这床棉被之前已经缝补好了。

将来时:
I will patch the broken window tomorrow. = 我明天将修补破窗户。
He will patch the software bug soon. = 他很快就会修补软件漏洞。

虚拟语气:
If I had a needle and thread, I would patch the tear. = 如果我有针线，我会缝补这个破洞。
I wish I had patched the roof sooner. = 我真希望我早点修补屋顶。

被动语态:
The hole in the road was patched last week. = 路上的洞上周被修补了。
The software will be patched next month. = 软件将在下个月修补。

疑问句:
Have you patched the leak yet? = 你已经修补好泄漏了吗？
Will the developers patch the game soon? = 开发人员会很快修补游戏吗？

否定句:
I haven't patched the hole in my shoe yet. = 我还没修补我鞋上的洞。
They didn't patch the roof before the rain started. = 雨开始下之前他们没有修补屋顶。


PHRASE->

software patch: 软件补丁
