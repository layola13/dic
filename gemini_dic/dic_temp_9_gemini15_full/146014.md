overdraft:/ˈoʊvədræft/ |/ˈəʊvədrɑːft/| n. |透支；透支额度|An overdraft is the amount of money you have withdrawn from your bank account that exceeds the available balance.  It's essentially borrowing money from the bank. |例句：I accidentally went into overdraft this month because I forgot to pay my rent.  这个月我不小心透支了，因为我忘了付房租。

例句：The bank charged a hefty fee for my overdraft.  银行因我的透支收取了高额费用。


近义词：deficit, overdrawn account, negative balance |反义词：credit, surplus, positive balance |词性:名词
