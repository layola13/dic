nondidactic:/ˌnɒnˌdɪˈdæktɪk/ | adj. |非说教的，非教诲的 | Not intended to teach or instruct; not didactic.  This word emphasizes the absence of a deliberate attempt to impart moral lessons or information in a preachy manner.  The focus is on entertainment, exploration, or other purposes rather than explicit instruction. |例句：The film, while entertaining, offers a nondidactic exploration of complex themes.这部电影虽然引人入胜，但却以非说教的方式探讨了复杂主题。


The novel presented a nondidactic account of the characters' lives, allowing readers to draw their own conclusions. 这部小说对人物的生活进行了非说教的描述，让读者自己得出结论。


近义词：unpedagogical, non-instructional, informal, entertaining


反义词：didactic, instructive, preachy, moralistic

词性：形容词
