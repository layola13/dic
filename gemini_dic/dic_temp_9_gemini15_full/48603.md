defecting:/dɪˈfɛktɪŋ/ | v. |叛逃；背叛；放弃；有缺陷 | Abandoning one's allegiance or principles; leaving a group, country, or cause to join the opposing side; having flaws or imperfections.  |例句：Many soldiers were defecting to the enemy. 许多士兵叛逃到敌方。

The building materials were found to be defecting. 建筑材料被发现有缺陷。|近义词：desertion, defection, betrayal |反义词：loyalty, allegiance, fidelity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is defecting from the party. = 他正在脱离该政党。
She is defecting to the opposition. = 她正在叛逃到反对派。


一般过去时:
He defected from the army last year. = 他去年从军队叛逃。
Many citizens defected to the neighboring country. = 许多公民叛逃到邻国。


现在完成时:
Several members have defected from the group. = 好几个成员已经脱离了该团体。
He has defected to the rival company. = 他已经叛逃到竞争对手公司。


过去完成时:
The spy had defected before the mission began. = 间谍在任务开始前就已经叛逃了。
They had defected to the enemy long before the battle. = 他们在战斗很久之前就已经叛逃到敌方了。


将来时:
He will defect to the opposition party soon. = 他很快就会叛逃到反对党。
More workers are expected to defect to the competitor. = 预计会有更多工人叛逃到竞争对手那里。


虚拟语气:
If he defected, the consequences would be severe. = 如果他叛逃，后果将不堪设想。
I wish he hadn't defected from our cause. = 我真希望他没有背叛我们的事业。


被动语态:
He was suspected of being defecting. = 他被怀疑正在叛逃。
The plan was defected by the opposition. = 该计划被反对派破坏了(引申含义，计划有缺陷)。


疑问句:
Is he defecting to the enemy? = 他是否叛逃到敌方？
Are many people defecting from the organization? = 许多人是否正在脱离该组织？


否定句:
He did not defect to the enemy. = 他没有叛逃到敌方。
She did not defect from her beliefs. = 她没有背叛她的信仰。


PHRASE->
defect to = 叛逃到
defect from = 脱离；叛离
defect in =  …方面的缺陷
be defecting =  正在叛逃
mass defecting = 大规模叛逃
