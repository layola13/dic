mycoderma:/maɪkoʊˈdɜːrmə/| n. |霉菌皮肤病；皮肤霉菌病 |A fungal infection of the skin.  Mycoderma refers to a genus of fungi that can cause skin infections.  The term is often used more broadly to refer to any fungal skin infection. | Example: The doctor diagnosed her rash as a type of mycoderma. 医生诊断她的皮疹为一种霉菌性皮肤病。|近义词:dermatomycosis, tinea, ringworm |反义词:None specifically, but conditions indicating healthy skin could be considered opposites. |名词
