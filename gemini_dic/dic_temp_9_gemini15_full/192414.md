scrollers:/ˈskroʊlər z/| n.|滚动者，卷轴播放器|A person or thing that scrolls.  It can refer to a person who scrolls through content online or a device that displays scrolling content.|例句：The website's many scrollers were impressed by the smooth animation.  网站的众多滚动浏览者对流畅的动画印象深刻。|近义词：scroller, browser, reader|反义词：N/A


USAGE->

The scroller effortlessly navigates the webpage. = 滚动者轻松地浏览网页。
He scrolls through his newsfeed every morning. = 他每天早上都会浏览他的新闻推送。
