manipulate:/məˈnɪpjuleɪt/ | vt. |操纵；控制；操作；巧妙地处理 | To control or influence (a person or situation) cleverly or unscrupulously.  |例句：The puppet master skillfully manipulated the puppets.  木偶大师熟练地操纵着木偶。

例句：He manipulated the evidence to support his theory. 他操纵证据以支持他的理论。


近义词：control, manage, influence, handle, exploit | 反义词：leave alone, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manipulates the market. = 他操纵市场。
She manipulates the data to fit her narrative. = 她操纵数据以符合她的叙述。

一般过去时:
They manipulated the votes. = 他们操纵了投票。
The magician manipulated the cards with amazing dexterity. = 魔术师以惊人的技巧操纵着扑克牌。

现在完成时:
He has manipulated the system for years. = 他多年来一直在操纵这个系统。
She has manipulated her way to the top. = 她通过操纵手段爬到了顶峰。

过去完成时:
They had manipulated the results before the announcement. = 他们早在宣布结果之前就已经操纵了结果。
The con artist had manipulated the victim into giving him money. = 骗子已经操纵受害者让他给了他钱。

将来时:
They will manipulate the public opinion. = 他们将会操纵公众舆论。
He will manipulate the controls to land the plane. = 他将操纵控制杆来降落飞机。

虚拟语气:
If he manipulated the data, the results would be different. = 如果他操纵数据，结果就会不同。
I wish I hadn't manipulated the situation. = 我真希望我没有操纵这种情况。

被动语态:
The market was manipulated by speculators. = 市场被投机者操纵了。
The data was manipulated to support a false conclusion. = 数据被操纵以支持错误的结论。

疑问句:
Did he manipulate the evidence? = 他操纵证据了吗？
Is she manipulating the situation to her advantage? = 她是否在操纵局势以使其对自己有利？

否定句:
He didn't manipulate the results. = 他没有操纵结果。
She doesn't manipulate people for her own gain. = 她不会为了自己的利益而操纵他人。


PHRASE->
manipulate data 数据处理
manipulate the market 操纵市场
manipulate the stock prices 操纵股价
manipulate public opinion 操纵舆论
manipulate the controls 控制操作


词源:
源自拉丁语 manus (手) + -pulare (处理,操作)  最终演变为 manipulate,  表示用手操作或控制。
