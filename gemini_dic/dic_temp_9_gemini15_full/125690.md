It can also refer to the process of making something worldly or global in scope. |例句：The mundification of the city involved a massive cleanup campaign. = 城市的净化工程包括大规模的清洁运动。  The mundification of the internet has led to its widespread use globally. = 互联网的世界化导致了其在全球的广泛使用。 |近义词：purification, cleansing, sanitization, globalization |反义词：pollution, contamination, soiling
