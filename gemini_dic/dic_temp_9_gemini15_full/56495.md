domesman:/ˈdoʊmzˌmæn/ | n. | 男管家；家庭男管家 | A male servant who manages a household.  The term is largely archaic and rarely used in modern English. It suggests a higher level of responsibility and authority than a simple male servant. | Example:  The wealthy family employed a domesman to oversee their large estate.  这个富有的家庭雇佣了一位男管家来管理他们的庄园。 | 近义词: butler, manservant, house steward | 反义词: mistress, housekeeper
