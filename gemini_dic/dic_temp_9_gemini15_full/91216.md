hydrobilirubin:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈbɪlɪruːbɪn/| n. |胆绿素|A green bile pigment produced by the reduction of bilirubin.  It is an intermediate product in the breakdown of heme and is less toxic than bilirubin.|The presence of hydrobilirubin in the feces indicates normal bile flow.|近义词：biliverdin (though not a direct synonym, it's closely related)|反义词：无
