dutiable:/ˈdjuːtiəbl/ | adj. |应税的；需纳税的 |Subject to a tax or duty.  Goods that are dutiable must have customs duties paid on them before they can be legally imported or sold. |例句：Imported alcohol is dutiable in many countries. = 许多国家的进口酒类是应税的。

Imported cigarettes are dutiable goods. = 进口香烟是应税商品。


近义词：taxable, subject to duty | 反义词：duty-free, tax-exempt


词源：duty (n.) + -able (suffix)  "capable of being," "worthy of," or "liable to."  The word essentially means "capable of being taxed."
