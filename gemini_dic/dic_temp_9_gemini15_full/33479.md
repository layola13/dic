chylous:/ˈkaɪləs/| adj.|乳糜的；含乳糜的|Containing or resembling chyle (a milky fluid containing emulsified fats).  It refers to something related to or containing chyle, the milky fluid absorbed from the intestine into the lymphatic system.|Example: The patient presented with a chylous ascites, indicating a leak in the lymphatic system.  病人出现乳糜腹水，表明淋巴系统有渗漏。|近义词：lymphatic|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to something lacking lymphatic fluid.
