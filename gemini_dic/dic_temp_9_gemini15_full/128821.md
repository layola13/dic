nightmare:/ˈnaɪtmer/| n. |噩梦，可怕的经历 |A nightmare is a frightening dream.  It can also refer to a very unpleasant or difficult experience. |例句：I had a nightmare last night about being chased by a monster.  昨晚我做了一个噩梦，梦见被怪物追赶。

例句：The project quickly turned into a nightmare.  这个项目很快变成了一个噩梦。|近义词：bad dream, terror, ordeal |反义词：pleasant dream, delightful experience |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often has nightmares. = 他经常做噩梦。
This situation is a nightmare for him. = 这种情况对他来说是个噩梦。

一般过去时:
She had a terrible nightmare last night. = 她昨晚做了一个可怕的噩梦。
