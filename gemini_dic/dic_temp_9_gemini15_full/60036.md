educatedness:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪdnɪs/| n. |受教育程度；知识；教养|The level of education or knowledge that someone has. It refers to the state or quality of being educated, encompassing knowledge, skills, understanding, and refinement acquired through education and experience.|例句：His educatedness was evident in his eloquent speech.  他的演讲体现了他的渊博学识。

例句：The high level of educatedness in the community contributed to its economic success. 社区的较高受教育程度促进了其经济的成功。


近义词：learning, scholarship, culture, refinement

反义词：ignorance, illiteracy, uncouthness
