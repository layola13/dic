precompared:/ˌpriːkəmˈpɛərd/| adj.|预先比较过的；事先比较过的|Precompared data refers to data that has been compared before a certain point in time or process.  It implies a prior comparison or analysis has already taken place.|例句：The precompared results showed a significant difference between the two groups. 预先比较的结果显示两组之间存在显著差异。The report utilized precompared figures from the previous year's study. 报告使用了前一年研究中预先比较的数据。|近义词：preanalyzed, pre-examined, previously compared|反义词：uncompared, unexamined
