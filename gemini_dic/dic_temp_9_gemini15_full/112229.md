loosen:/ˈluːsən/ | vt. |放松；松开；使松弛 | To make or become less tight or firm.  |例句：He loosened his tie. 他松了松领带。|例句：The screws had loosened. 螺丝松了。|近义词：relax, slacken, release |反义词：tighten, firm up, strengthen |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loosens his grip on the rope. = 他放松了对绳子的抓握。
She loosens her hair after work. = 她下班后把头发放下来。


一般过去时:
The gardener loosened the soil before planting. = 园丁在种植前松了松土。
I loosened my belt because I was too full. = 我因为吃得太饱而解开了皮带。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has loosened the ground. = 持续的雨水使地面松软了。
They have loosened their restrictions on travel. = 他们已经放宽了旅行限制。


过去完成时:
He had loosened his tie before the presentation. = 他在演讲前已经松了松领带。
She had loosened the screws before she tried to remove the panel. = 她试图拆卸面板之前已经松开了螺丝。


将来时:
The heat will loosen the paint. = 热量会使油漆脱落。
I will loosen my grip on the situation. = 我将放松对局势的控制。


虚拟语气:
If he loosened his grip, he might fall. = 如果他放松抓握，他可能会掉下去。
If she had loosened the soil earlier, the plants might have grown better. = 如果她早点松土，植物可能长得更好。


被动语态:
The soil was loosened by the gardener. = 土地被园丁松动了。
The regulations were loosened last year. = 规定去年放宽了。


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't loosen his grip on the steering wheel. = 他没有放松对方向盘的控制。
She didn't loosen her purse strings. = 她没有花钱大手大脚。
