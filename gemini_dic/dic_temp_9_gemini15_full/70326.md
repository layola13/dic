field-worker:/ˈfiːldˌwɜːrkər/ | n. |田间工作人员，外勤人员 |A field worker is someone who works outdoors, often in agriculture or related fields, performing tasks such as planting, harvesting, or surveying.  They may also work in social work or other fields requiring on-site investigation or interaction. |Example:  The field workers spent the day harvesting the wheat crop. 田间工作人员花了一整天收割小麦。  The social worker, a dedicated field worker, visited families in need.  这位社会工作者，一位尽职尽责的外勤人员，探访了需要帮助的家庭。|近义词：fieldhand, operative, researcher (depending on context) |反义词：office worker, desk worker
