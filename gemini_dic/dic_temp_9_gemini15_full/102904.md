词源:
The word "jawbreaker" is a compound word.  "Jaw" refers to the lower part of the face, and "breaker" signifies something that breaks or is hard to break.  The name aptly describes the candy's hardness and the effort required to chew it.  The figurative use developed from the literal, extending the meaning to anything difficult to comprehend or overcome.
