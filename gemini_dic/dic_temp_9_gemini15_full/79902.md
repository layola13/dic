gnawed:/nɔːd/| v.|啃咬；折磨；使苦恼|To bite or chew on (something) persistently.|The dog gnawed on the bone.  这只狗啃着骨头。|The problem gnawed at his conscience.  这个问题折磨着他的良心。|chew, nibble, bite|soothe, comfort|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gnaws on his pen when he's thinking. 他思考时会啃咬他的钢笔。
The rats gnaw through the walls at night.  老鼠晚上啃咬墙壁。


一般过去时:
She gnawed nervously at her fingernails. 她紧张地啃咬指甲。
The mice gnawed through the packaging. 老鼠啃咬穿了包装。


现在完成时:
The dog has gnawed through the furniture leg. 狗已经啃咬坏了家具的腿。
The worry has gnawed at her for weeks.  这担忧折磨了她几周。


过去完成时:
He had gnawed his pencil down to a stub. 他把铅笔啃咬成了短短的一截。
The rats had gnawed holes in the floorboards. 老鼠已经在地板上啃咬出洞了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had known the dog would gnaw the furniture, I wouldn't have left him alone. 如果我知道狗会啃咬家具，我不会把他单独留下的。
I wish the mice hadn't gnawed through the wires. 我真希望老鼠没有啃咬断电线。


被动语态:
The bone was gnawed clean. 骨头被啃得干干净净。

疑问句:

否定句:
