sassily:/ˈsæsɪli/| adv.| 傲慢无礼地；轻浮地；放肆地 | In a sassy manner; impudently or impertinently.  |例句：She sassily retorted, "Mind your own business!" 她傲慢无礼地回击道：“管好你自己的事！”

例句：He sassily flipped his hair and walked away. 他轻浮地甩了甩头发，然后走开了。


近义词：cheekily, impudently, pertly, brazenly

反义词：submissively, meekly, respectfully, timidly


词性：副词
