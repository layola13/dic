entrochite:/ˈɛntrəkaɪt/| n. |海百合茎的化石碎片|A small, disc-like fossil fragment of a crinoid stem.  Entrochites are common fossils found in sedimentary rocks and are often referred to as "St. Cuthbert's beads".|The limestone contained numerous entrochites, indicating a past marine environment rich in crinoids.|近义词：crinoid fragment, fossil disc|反义词：无
