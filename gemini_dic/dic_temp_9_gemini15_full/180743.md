reguerdon:/ˈrɛɡərdən/| n. |报酬，奖赏 | A recompense; a reward.  The word is largely archaic. It suggests a reward given for a service rendered, often implying a sense of gratitude or generosity beyond mere payment. | Example:  The king bestowed a generous guerdon upon his loyal knight. 国王慷慨地赏赐了他的忠诚骑士。|近义词：reward, recompense, compensation |反义词：punishment, penalty |名词
