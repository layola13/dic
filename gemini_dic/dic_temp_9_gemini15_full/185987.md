rewatered:/ˌriːˈwɔːtərd/| v. |再次浇水；重新灌溉|To water again; to supply with water anew.|例句：The gardener rewatered the thirsty plants after the hot day. 园丁在炎热的一天过后再次浇灌了口渴的植物。
The farmer rewatered the parched fields following the drought. 农民在干旱过后重新灌溉了干旱的田地。|近义词：rehydrate, irrigate |反义词：dehydrate, dry out |动词
