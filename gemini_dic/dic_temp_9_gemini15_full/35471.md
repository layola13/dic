coachful: /ˈkoʊtʃfʊl/ | n. | 一车厢的人；满载乘客的车辆 | A coachful of people is a group of people that can fill a coach (bus).  It implies a large number of people.  The term emphasizes the capacity of a vehicle rather than a precise number. |  There was a coachful of excited children heading to the zoo.  =  有一车厢兴奋的孩子正前往动物园。


There were two coachfuls of tourists waiting for the train. = 有两车厢的游客在等火车。| 近义词：busload, carload | 反义词：无
