intravenous:/ˌɪntrəˈveɪnəs/ | adj. | 静脉内的，静脉注射的 | Relating to or occurring within a vein or veins.  | Example: The patient received an intravenous injection. 病人接受了静脉注射。 |近义词：intravenal |反义词：oral, subcutaneous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication is intravenously administered. = 药物是静脉注射给药的。
This solution is intravenously infused. = 这种溶液是静脉输液的。


一般过去时:
The doctor intravenously injected the medicine. = 医生静脉注射了药物。
The nurse intravenously administered fluids. = 护士静脉输液。


现在完成时:
The patient has received intravenous antibiotics. = 病人已经接受了静脉注射抗生素。
She has had several intravenous infusions this week. = 她本周已经进行了几次静脉输液。


过去完成时:
They had already received intravenous fluids before the surgery. = 他们在手术前已经接受了静脉输液。
The patient had been given intravenous medication prior to the examination. = 病人在检查前已接受静脉药物治疗。


将来时:
The surgeon will administer the anesthetic intravenously. = 外科医生将静脉注射麻醉剂。
The patient will receive intravenous fluids post-operation. = 病人术后将接受静脉输液。


虚拟语气:
If the patient were allergic, we wouldn't administer the drug intravenously. = 如果病人过敏，我们就不会静脉注射药物。
I wish I had known the risks of intravenous injection before. = 我希望我以前就知道静脉注射的风险。


被动语态:
The medication was intravenously injected into the vein. = 药物被静脉注射到静脉中。
The fluids are being intravenously administered to the patient. = 正在为病人静脉输液。


疑问句:
Is the medication administered intravenously? = 药物是静脉注射的吗？
Was the injection given intravenously? = 注射是静脉注射的吗？


否定句:
The medicine is not administered intravenously. = 药物不是静脉注射的。
The drug should not be given intravenously. = 该药物不应静脉注射。
