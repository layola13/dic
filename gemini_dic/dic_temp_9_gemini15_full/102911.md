jawing:/ˈdʒɔːɪŋ/ | v. |喋喋不休地谈话；唠叨；争吵 |To talk continuously and usually in a complaining or irritating way; to quarrel or argue noisily. |例句：They were jawing away about the game all evening.  他们整个晚上都在喋喋不休地谈论比赛。

例句：The couple were jawing at each other across the table. 那对夫妇在餐桌对面大声争吵。


近义词：chatting, gossiping, arguing, bickering, squabbling |反义词：being silent, being quiet


词性:动词
