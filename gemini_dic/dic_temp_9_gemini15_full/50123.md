dentitions:/denˈtɪʃənz/| n. |牙齿状况，齿列|The arrangement or condition of the teeth in a person or animal.  It refers to the number, kind, and arrangement of teeth, often considered in relation to the animal's age and diet.  |例句：The dentist examined the child's dentition for any signs of decay.  牙医检查了孩子的牙齿状况，是否有龋齿迹象。

The study of dentitions in different species provides valuable insights into their evolutionary history and dietary habits.  对不同物种牙齿状况的研究，为了解它们的进化史和饮食习惯提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：teeth,  dental arrangement |反义词：无
