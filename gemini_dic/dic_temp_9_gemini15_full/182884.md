repercuss:/ˌriːpərˈkʌs/| v.| 反响，影响，冲击 | To have a significant indirect effect on something or someone.  | The decision to raise taxes will repercuss throughout the economy.  提高税收的决定将对整个经济产生影响。| reverberate, echo, impact |  (近义词缺乏精确的反义词，因为“反响”本身是中性词，其影响可能是积极或消极的)|动词

His actions repercuss on his family's reputation. = 他的行为影响到他家人的声誉。
