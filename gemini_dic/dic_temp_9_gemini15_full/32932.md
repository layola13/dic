chop-up: /tʃɒp ʌp/ | vt. | 剁碎；砍碎；切碎 | To cut something into small pieces.  |例句：He chopped up the vegetables for the stew. 他把蔬菜切碎做炖菜。

例句：She chopped up the onions and garlic before adding them to the pan.  她把洋葱和大蒜切碎后才放入锅中。


近义词：mince, dice, hack, fragment

反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent, e.g., leave whole)

词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chops up the carrots every day. = 他每天都切碎胡萝卜。
They chop up the wood for the fireplace. = 他们把木头砍碎放到壁炉里。


一般过去时:
She chopped up the herbs for the salad. = 她把香草切碎做沙拉。
The butcher chopped up the meat into small pieces. = 屠夫把肉切成小块。


现在完成时:
I have chopped up all the vegetables for the soup. = 我已经把所有蔬菜都切碎准备做汤了。
He has chopped up the old furniture. = 他已经把旧家具拆了。


过去完成时:
She had chopped up the ingredients before she started cooking. = 她开始做饭之前就已经把配料切碎了。
They had chopped up the tree before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经把树砍倒了。


将来时:
I will chop up the potatoes for the roast. = 我会把土豆切碎准备烤。
She will chop up the fruit for the pie. = 她会把水果切碎做馅饼。


虚拟语气:
If I had a sharp knife, I would chop up the vegetables more easily. = 如果我有一把锋利的刀，我会更容易地切碎蔬菜。
I wish I had chopped up the onions earlier; my eyes are burning! = 我真希望早点把洋葱切碎；我的眼睛都辣了！


被动语态:
The vegetables were chopped up by the chef. = 蔬菜是由厨师切碎的。

疑问句:

否定句:
