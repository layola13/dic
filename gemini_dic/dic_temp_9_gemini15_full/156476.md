piazzas:/piˈɑːzəz/| n. |广场（复数）|A piazza is an open public space, typically a square or plaza, in a town or city, often surrounded by buildings.|例句：The tourists gathered in the piazzas of Rome.  游客们聚集在罗马的广场上。|例句：Many Italian piazzas host outdoor markets and festivals. 许多意大利广场举办户外市场和节日。|近义词：plaza, square, public square|反义词：private space, interior
