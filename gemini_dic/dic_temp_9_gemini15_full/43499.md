/ˈkreɪtɑːnz/| n. |克拉通|A craton is a large, stable block of the Earth's crust that has remained relatively undisturbed for a long period of geological time.  It forms the ancient, stable core of a continent. Cratons are typically composed of Precambrian igneous and metamorphic rocks, and are often covered by a relatively thin layer of sedimentary rock.|  The North American craton is one of the largest and oldest cratons on Earth.  北美克拉通是地球上最大和最古老的克拉通之一。| The Siberian craton is another example of a stable continental block.  西伯利亚克拉通是另一个稳定的大陆块的例子。 | shield, platform | unstable crust, mobile belt | 名词
