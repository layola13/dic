self-assignments:/ˌsɛlf əˈsaɪnmənts/| n.|自我布置的任务，自主安排的任务 |Tasks or projects that someone assigns to themselves.  It implies a proactive approach to work or learning, often without external direction or pressure. |例句：She took on several self-assignments to improve her skills. 她承担了几项自我布置的任务来提高自己的技能。

例句：He completed all his self-assignments before the deadline. 他在截止日期前完成了所有自己布置的任务。


近义词：self-imposed tasks, personal projects, independent studies, voluntary undertakings


反义词：assigned tasks (tasks assigned by others), mandatory assignments


词性:名词
