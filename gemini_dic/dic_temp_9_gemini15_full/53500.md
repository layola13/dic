disassemblies:/ˌdɪs.əˈsɛm.bliːz/| n. |拆卸；分解；反汇编|The process of taking something apart, often into its component parts.  This can refer to physical objects or computer programs.  |例句：The technicians performed several disassemblies of the engine to identify the problem. 技师们对发动机进行了多次拆卸以找出问题。

The disassemblies of the software revealed a security vulnerability. 该软件的反汇编揭示了一个安全漏洞。|近义词：dismantlings, breakdowns, deconstructions |反义词：assemblies, constructions, compilations |名词
