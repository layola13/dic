follow:/ˈfɒləʊ/ /ˈfɑːloʊ/| vt. vi. n.|跟随，遵循，听从|To go or come after; to come or be next after; to pursue; to conform to; to result as a consequence.|例句：Please follow me. 请跟着我。

例句：The children followed their teacher into the classroom. 孩子们跟着老师走进教室。


近义词：pursue, chase, obey, adhere to|反义词：lead, precede, disobey, ignore|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I follow the news closely. = 我密切关注新闻。
He follows his passion for art. = 他追逐他对艺术的热情。


一般过去时:
She followed the directions carefully. = 她仔细地按照指示做了。
They followed the river downstream. = 他们沿着河顺流而下。


现在完成时:
I have followed this case for months. = 我关注这个案子已经好几个月了。
She has followed her dreams and achieved success. = 她追逐梦想并取得了成功。


过去完成时:
He had followed the trail for hours before he found the lost hiker. = 他沿着小路走了几个小时才找到迷路的徒步旅行者。
They had followed the instructions, but still failed. = 他们按照指示做了，但仍然失败了。


将来时:
I will follow your advice. = 我会听从你的建议。
We will follow the map to find the treasure. = 我们将按照地图找到宝藏。


虚拟语气:
If I followed my heart, I would travel the world. = 如果我跟随自己的内心，我会环游世界。
I wish I had followed his advice earlier. = 我真希望我早点听从他的建议。


被动语态:
The rules must be followed strictly. = 必须严格遵守规则。
His instructions were followed carefully. = 他的指示被仔细地执行了。


疑问句:
Do you follow my instructions? = 你能理解我的指示吗？
Will you follow me to the meeting? = 你会跟我一起去开会吗？


否定句:
I don't follow your logic. = 我不明白你的逻辑。
They didn't follow the rules. = 他们没有遵守规则。
