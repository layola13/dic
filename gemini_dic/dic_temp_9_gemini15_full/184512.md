restaurate:/ˈrɛstərɑːt/| vt.|修复，重建；恢复(健康)|To restore something to its former state, especially to repair or renovate a building or work of art.|例句：The old church was painstakingly restaurated. 这座古老的教堂经过精心修复。|例句：The doctor helped to restaurate his health. 医生帮助他恢复了健康。|近义词：restore, renovate, repair|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin
