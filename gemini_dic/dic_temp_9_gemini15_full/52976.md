dining:/ˈdaɪnɪŋ/ | n. |进餐；用餐；餐厅 |The act of eating a meal, or the place where one eats.|例句：We enjoyed a lovely dining experience at the restaurant. 我们在餐厅享用了一顿美味的晚餐。|例句：The hotel has a sophisticated dining room. 这家酒店有一间精致的餐厅。|近义词：eating, supping, feeding|反义词：fasting, starving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We dine at seven o'clock every night. = 我们每天晚上七点吃饭。
They dine out twice a week. = 他们每周外出就餐两次。

一般过去时:
We dined at a fancy restaurant last night. = 我们昨晚在一家高档餐厅吃饭。
They dined on lobster and champagne. = 他们吃了龙虾和香槟。

现在完成时:
We have dined at that restaurant many times. = 我们在那家餐厅吃过很多次饭了。
They have dined with the President. = 他们与总统共进晚餐。

过去完成时:
We had dined before the show began. = 演出开始前我们已经吃过饭了。
They had dined and were ready for the party. = 他们已经吃过饭了，准备好了参加聚会。

将来时:
We will dine at home tonight. = 我们今晚在家吃饭。
They will dine with their friends tomorrow. = 他们明天将和朋友们一起吃饭。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would dine at a more elegant restaurant. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会去一家更优雅的餐厅用餐。
I wish we could dine under the stars tonight. = 我希望我们今晚能在星空下用餐。

被动语态:
Dinner is being served. = 晚餐正在供应。
The guests were dined and wined. = 客人们受到了盛情款待（吃喝玩乐）。

疑问句:
Where will we dine tonight? = 我们今晚在哪里吃饭？
What time is dinner being served? = 晚餐几点供应？

否定句:
We did not dine out last night. = 我们昨晚没有外出就餐。
They will not dine with us tomorrow. = 他们明天不会和我们一起吃饭。
