jugger:/ˈdʒʌɡər/| n.|大壶；(尤指)盛水的陶器|A jugger is a large jug, typically one made of earthenware and used for holding water or other liquids.  It is often characterized by its size and handle.|例句：The gardener filled the jugger with water to water the thirsty plants. 园丁用大壶装满水浇灌口渴的植物。|近义词：pitcher, jar, container|反义词:None.
