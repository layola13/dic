reflowing:/ˌriːˈfloʊɪŋ/| v.|重新排版，重新流动|To rearrange the layout of text or images, typically to fit a different space or format.  It also refers to the movement of a liquid or gas in a new direction or pattern.|例句：The software automatically reflows the text when you resize the window.  软件会在你调整窗口大小的时候自动重新排版文本。

The printer reflowed the ink after the nozzle clogged. 打印机喷嘴堵塞后重新输送墨水。|近义词：reformat, rearrange, reshape|反义词：fix, solidify
