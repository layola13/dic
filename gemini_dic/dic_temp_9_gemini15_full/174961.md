radiators:/ˈreɪdieɪtərz/ | n. |散热器，暖气片 |A radiator is a device used to heat a room or building by radiating heat. It typically consists of a metal unit filled with water or other fluids that are heated and then radiate warmth into the surrounding space.  |Example:  The radiators in the old house were inefficient and needed replacing. 老房子里的暖气片效率低下，需要更换。The car's radiator needs to be checked regularly to prevent overheating. 汽车的散热器需要定期检查以防止过热。|近义词：heater, heat emitter |反义词：air conditioner, cooler |名词
