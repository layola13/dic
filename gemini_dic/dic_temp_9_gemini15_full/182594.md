reoffending:/ˌriːˈɒfɛndɪŋ/ | n. |再次犯罪；再次违法 | The act of committing a crime after having previously been convicted of a crime.  It is a major concern for criminal justice systems worldwide. |例句：The high rate of reoffending is a serious problem.  再次犯罪的高比率是一个严重的问题。

例句：Reducing reoffending should be a priority for the government.  降低再次犯罪率应该成为政府的优先事项。


近义词：recidivism, repeat offending | 反义词：law-abiding behavior, rehabilitation
