intoxicatingly:/ɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. |令人陶醉地，使人兴奋地 |  In a way that intoxicates or excites.  It describes something that has a captivating or thrilling effect, often through its sensory appeal or stimulating nature. |例句：The intoxicatingly beautiful sunset painted the sky with vibrant hues.  令人陶醉的美丽日落用鲜艳的色彩描绘了天空。

The music was intoxicatingly rhythmic, making everyone want to dance.  音乐节奏令人陶醉，使每个人都想跳舞。

| 近义词：enthralling, captivating, mesmerizing, heady, stimulating | 反义词：boring, dull, uninteresting, tedious | 词性：副词
