cyclostome:/ˈsaɪkləstoʊm/ | n. | 圆口类动物；无颌类；七鳃鳗 | A cyclostome is a jawless vertebrate belonging to the class Cyclostomata, characterized by a circular mouth and a cartilaginous skeleton.  They are often parasitic, attaching themselves to other fish to feed. | Example: Lampreys and hagfish are examples of cyclostomes.  七鳃鳗和盲鳗是圆口类动物的例子。 | 近义词: agnath, jawless fish | 反义词: gnathostome (jawed vertebrate)
