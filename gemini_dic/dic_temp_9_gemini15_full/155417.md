/piːeɪtʃˈdiː/|abbr.|博士学位|Doctor of Philosophy; a postgraduate academic degree awarded by universities; typically involves original research and a dissertation.|例句：She earned her PhD in astrophysics. 她获得了天体物理学博士学位。|近义词：doctorate|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pursuing a PhD in linguistics. = 他正在攻读语言学博士学位。
She holds a PhD from Harvard University. = 她拥有哈佛大学的博士学位。


一般过去时:
He completed his PhD last year. = 他去年完成了博士学位论文。
She received her PhD in 2020. = 她于2020年获得了博士学位。


现在完成时:
He has already finished his PhD research. = 他已经完成了博士研究。
She has held a PhD for over five years. = 她拥有博士学位已超过五年。


过去完成时:
He had completed his coursework before starting his dissertation. = 他在开始撰写论文之前已经完成了课程学习。
She had earned her PhD before she took the professorship. = 她获得博士学位后才担任教授。


将来时:
He will defend his PhD dissertation next month. = 他下个月将答辩他的博士论文。
She will likely complete her PhD in the spring. = 她很可能在春天完成她的博士学位。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would pursue a PhD. = 如果我有时间，我会去攻读博士学位。
I wish I had the resources to pursue a PhD. = 我希望我有资源去攻读博士学位。


被动语态:
The PhD program is highly competitive. = 博士项目竞争非常激烈。
His PhD thesis was published in a prestigious journal. = 他的博士论文发表在一本著名的期刊上。


疑问句:
Does he have a PhD? = 他有博士学位吗？
What is her PhD topic? = 她的博士论文题目是什么？


否定句:
He does not yet have a PhD. = 他还没有获得博士学位。
She did not pursue a PhD after her master's degree. = 她硕士毕业后没有继续攻读博士学位。
