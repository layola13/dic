enshrine:/ɪnˈʃraɪn/| vt.|奉为神圣；珍藏；铭记|To cherish or preserve something as sacred or valuable.  |例句：The museum enshrines many historical artifacts.  博物馆珍藏着许多历史文物。

例句：Her achievements are enshrined in the history books.  她的成就被载入史册。


近义词：treasure, cherish, preserve, consecrate|反义词：desecrate, profane, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They enshrine their traditions. = 他们珍视他们的传统。
We enshrine our values in the constitution. = 我们在宪法中铭记我们的价值观。

一般过去时:
The king enshrined the new law. = 国王颁布了新法律（并使之神圣）。
They enshrined the treaty in a stone tablet. = 他们将条约刻在石碑上（以示珍藏）。

现在完成时:
The museum has enshrined many works of art. = 该博物馆已珍藏了许多艺术作品。
Society has enshrined the principle of equality. = 社会已经将平等的原则奉为圭臬。

过去完成时:
They had enshrined the hero's memory in a statue. = 他们已经用雕像来纪念这位英雄。
The people had enshrined the ancient customs for generations. = 人们世世代代珍藏着古老的习俗。

将来时:
The new constitution will enshrine the right to free speech. = 新宪法将保障言论自由的权利。
They will enshrine the agreement in a formal document. = 他们将在正式文件中载明该协议。

虚拟语气:
If they enshrined these values, society would be better. = 如果他们珍视这些价值观，社会将会更好。
I wish they had enshrined the agreement in a legally binding document. = 我希望他们当时已将协议载入具有法律约束力的文件中。

被动语态:
The principles of justice are enshrined in the law. = 正义原则已载入法律。
The agreement was enshrined in a treaty. = 该协议已载入条约。

疑问句:
Does the constitution enshrine freedom of religion? = 宪法是否保障宗教自由？
What values does this organization enshrine? = 这个组织珍视什么价值观？

否定句:
The company doesn't enshrine ethical principles in its practices. = 这家公司在其业务实践中没有体现道德原则。
They did not enshrine the agreement. = 他们没有将协议铭记（或正式记录）。
