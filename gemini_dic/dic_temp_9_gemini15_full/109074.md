legated:/ˈlɛɡeɪtɪd/ | vt. | 授权；委任；遗赠 | To entrust (a task or responsibility) to another; to bequeath (property) by will. | 例句：The ambassador was legated to negotiate the treaty. 大使被委派谈判条约。 The old woman legated her estate to her nephew. 老妇人将她的遗产遗赠给她的侄子。 | 近义词：entrusted, delegated, bequeathed | 反义词：retained, kept

一般现在时:
He legates his responsibilities to his assistant. 他把责任委托给他的助手。
The company legates the project to a new team. 公司将项目委托给一个新的团队。
