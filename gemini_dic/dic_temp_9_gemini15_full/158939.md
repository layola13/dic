pluralizing: /ˈplʊərəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使复数化；变成复数 | The process of making a word or group of words plural.  It involves adding suffixes or changing the word's form to indicate more than one. | Example:  Pluralizing "cat" results in "cats".  Pluralizing "child" results in "children". | 近义词: making plural, pluralization | 反义词: singularizing
