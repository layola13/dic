precollection: /ˌpriːkəˈlɛkʃən/ | n. | 预备收藏；先行收藏 | A precollection is a smaller, preview collection of items released before the main collection, often showcasing upcoming trends or styles. It serves as a teaser for the larger collection to come.  | Example:  The designer released a precollection of summer dresses in April, giving customers a sneak peek at the full collection launching in June. = 设计师在四月发布了一系列夏季连衣裙的预备收藏，让顾客抢先预览六月推出的完整系列。  |近义词: preview, advance showing,  sneak peek | 反义词: main collection, final collection | 名词
