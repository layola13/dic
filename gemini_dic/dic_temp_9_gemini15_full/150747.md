paraconid:/ˈpærəˌkɒnɪd/| n. |副锥|A small cusp on the medial side of the lower premolars and molars of some mammals, particularly primates. It's a rudimentary cusp, often less developed than other cusps. |The paraconid is a prominent feature in some early primate teeth.  副锥是某些早期灵长类动物牙齿中的一个显著特征。|近义词：protoconid (有时可互换使用，但位置不同)|反义词：无
