dutches:/ˈdʌtʃɪz/| n.|荷兰女公爵；（尤指）荷兰妻子|The word "dutchess" refers to a woman holding the rank of duchess, typically the wife of a duke.  It's less commonly used than "duchess" and often carries a slightly archaic or informal connotation.|Example:  She was a Dutchess of considerable wealth and influence. 她是一位拥有相当财富和影响力的荷兰女公爵。|近义词：duchess|反义词：无
