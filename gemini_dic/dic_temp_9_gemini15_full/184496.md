restarts:/ˌriːˈstɑːrts/ | vt. |重新开始，重启 | To begin something again after it has stopped.  |例句：The computer restarts automatically after a power failure. 电脑在断电后会自动重启。

例句：After a brief pause, the meeting restarts.  短暂暂停后，会议重新开始。


近义词：reboots, recommences, resumes | 反义词：ends, stops, ceases | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system restarts every night. = 系统每晚重启。
He restarts his car after it stalls. = 他的车熄火后，他重新启动。


一般过去时:
The computer restarted after the crash. = 电脑在崩溃后重新启动了。
She restarted the project after a long delay. = 经过长时间的延迟后，她重新启动了项目。


现在完成时:
The machine has restarted several times today. = 这台机器今天已经重启了好几次。
We have restarted negotiations after a long break. = 我们在长时间休会后重新开始了谈判。


过去完成时:
The program had restarted before I could save my work. = 在我能够保存我的工作之前，程序已经重新启动了。
They had restarted the engine several times before it finally started. = 他们在发动机最终启动之前尝试重启了好几次。


将来时:
The server will restart automatically at midnight. = 服务器将在午夜自动重启。
I will restart my computer after I finish this task. = 完成这项任务后，我将重新启动我的电脑。


虚拟语气:
If the computer froze, I would restart it. = 如果电脑死机了，我会重启它。
It would be best if we restarted the discussion from the beginning. = 最好我们从头开始重新讨论。


被动语态:
The software is restarted by the system administrator. = 软件由系统管理员重新启动。
The meeting was restarted after a short break. = 会议在短暂休息后重新开始。


疑问句:
Does the computer restart automatically? = 电脑会自动重启吗？
Should we restart the experiment? = 我们应该重新开始实验吗？


否定句:
The engine didn't restart. = 发动机没有重新启动。
The system won't restart unless you press the button. = 除非你按下按钮，否则系统不会重启。
