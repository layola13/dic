popular-etymologies:/ˌpɑːpjələrˌɛtɪˈmɑːlədʒiz/ | n. |通俗词源学|The study of word origins based on popular beliefs and assumptions, often inaccurate or fanciful, rather than rigorous scholarly research.  Popular etymologies are often based on perceived similarities in sound or meaning, rather than actual historical linguistic development.  They can be entertaining but should not be relied upon for accurate information about word origins.|例句：Many popular etymologies for the word "nice" are incorrect, tracing its origins to unrelated words.|近义词：folk etymology, false etymology |反义词：scholarly etymology, scientific etymology
