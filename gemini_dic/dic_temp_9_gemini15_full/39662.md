conservatoires:/kənˈsɜːvətɔːrz/| n. |音乐学院；艺术学校|A conservatoire is an institution providing training in the performing arts, especially music, dance, and drama.  It often implies a high level of professional training.| Many aspiring musicians attend conservatoires to hone their skills. 许多有抱负的音乐家去音乐学院磨练他们的技能。|music school, academy, institute|  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑与其功能相反的机构，如：general school, vocational school)|名词
