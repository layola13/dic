defusing:/diˈfjuːzɪŋ/| v.|化解；平息；拆除引信|To make something less dangerous or tense; to remove the fuse from a bomb or other explosive device.|例句：The negotiator worked hard to defuse the tense situation between the two warring factions.  调解员努力化解两个交战派系之间的紧张局势。

例句：The bomb squad carefully defused the explosive device before it could detonate.  拆弹小组小心地拆除了爆炸装置的引信，防止其爆炸。

近义词：neutralize, alleviate, calm, pacify

反义词：aggravate, escalate, inflame, exacerbate

词性:动词
