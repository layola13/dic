lamming:/ˈlæmɪŋ/| n. | 羔羊肉；小羊羔的时期 |  The act of a ewe giving birth to a lamb or the period of time when lambs are young.  It can also refer to the meat of a young sheep. | 例句：The lambing season is a busy time for shepherds.  产羔季节是牧羊人忙碌的时期。

The lambing was successful this year, with all the ewes producing healthy lambs.今年的产羔很成功，所有的母羊都产下了健康的羔羊。


近义词：lambing season (产羔季节), lambing time (产羔期)
反义词：无
词性：名词
