词源:

The word "reusables" is derived from the verb "reuse," meaning to use something again.  "Reuse" itself comes from the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "use." The suffix "-able" transforms the verb into an adjective, meaning "capable of being reused," and the addition of "-s" makes it a plural noun.
