refugees:/ˌrefjuˈdʒiːz/| n.|难民|People who have been forced to leave their country or home, because of war, persecution, or natural disaster.  They seek safety and protection in another place.|例句：Millions of refugees fled the war-torn country. 数百万难民逃离了饱受战争蹂躏的国家。|例句：The refugee camp is overcrowded and lacking in essential supplies. 难民营人满为患，缺乏必需的物资。|近义词：asylum seekers, displaced persons, exiles|反义词：settlers, immigrants (in a voluntary context)

将来时:
