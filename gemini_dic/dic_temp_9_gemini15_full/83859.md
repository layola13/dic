haled:/heɪld/ | vt. | 吸入；呼气；大声喊叫 | To breathe in; to inhale; to shout or call out loudly.  |例句：He inhaled deeply, filling his lungs with the crisp mountain air. 他深深地吸了一口气，肺部充满了清新的山间空气。

例句：The protesters hailed their leader with cheers. 抗议者们高呼欢呼，向他们的领导人致敬。|近义词：inhale, breathe in, shout, yell |反义词：exhale, breathe out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hails a taxi. = 他叫了一辆出租车。
She hails from a small town. = 她来自一个小镇。


一般过去时:
He hailed a cab. = 他拦了一辆出租车。
They hailed the arrival of spring. = 他们庆祝春天的到来。


现在完成时:
He has hailed a cab several times today. = 他今天已经好几次拦出租车了。
She has hailed from this region for generations. = 她家几代人都来自这个地区。


过去完成时:
He had hailed a taxi before the rain started. = 下雨之前他已经叫了一辆出租车。
They had hailed the new policy as a success. = 他们曾将这项新政策视为成功。


将来时:
He will hail a taxi when he gets to the airport. = 他到机场后会叫一辆出租车。
They will hail the new leader with enthusiasm. = 他们将热情地欢迎新领导人。


虚拟语气:
If I needed a taxi, I would hail one. = 如果我需要出租车，我会叫一辆。
I wish I had hailed a cab earlier. = 我真希望我早点拦一辆出租车。


被动语态:
The new leader was hailed as a hero. = 新领导人被誉为英雄。
The arrival of the ship was hailed with joy. = 船的到来受到了热烈的欢迎。


疑问句:
Did he hail a taxi? = 他叫了出租车吗？
Where do you hail from? = 你是哪里人？


否定句:
He didn't hail a taxi. = 他没有叫出租车。
She doesn't hail from this country. = 她不是这个国家的人。


PHRASE->
hail a taxi = 拦出租车
hail a storm = 暴风雨来临
hail someone as something = 赞扬某人为……
hail from = 来自
hail down = 下冰雹


词源:
源自古英语 *halan，意为“拉，拖，召唤”。  This Old English root is related to words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings, ultimately deriving from Proto-Germanic *halaną.  The sense of "call out" and "acclaim" developed later.
