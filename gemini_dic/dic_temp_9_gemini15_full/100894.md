intuition:/ˌɪntuˈɪʃən/ | n. |直觉，第六感 | the ability to understand something immediately, without the need for conscious reasoning.  It is a feeling of knowing something without knowing how you know it. |例句：I had an intuition that something was wrong. 我有一种直觉，觉得有什么不对劲。

例句：My intuition told me to avoid that meeting. 我的直觉告诉我应该避免参加那个会议。


近义词：instinct, hunch, feeling, gut feeling | 反义词：reason, logic, analysis | 词性：名词
