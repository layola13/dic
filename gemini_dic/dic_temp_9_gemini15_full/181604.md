relative's: /ˈrɛlətɪvz/ | n. | 亲戚的；亲属的 |  The possessive form of "relative," indicating something belonging to or related to a relative.  It is used to describe the possessions, characteristics, or relationships of a relative. | Example: I'm going to my relative's house for dinner. 我要去我亲戚家吃晚饭。 | 近义词: kinsman's, family member's | 反义词: unrelated person's | 名词
