flakelike:/ˈfleɪkˌlaɪk/| adj.|像薄片的；片状的；鳞片状的|Resembling a flake or flakes; having a flaky texture or appearance.  The word combines "flake" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The snow fell in a flakelike manner, creating a beautiful, soft blanket on the ground.  雪花像薄片一样飘落，在地面上形成了一层美丽柔软的毯子。The paint had a flakelike finish, peeling off in small, thin pieces.油漆具有片状的表面，脱落成薄薄的小片。|近义词：scaly, flaky, layered, sheetlike|反义词：smooth, solid, uniform
