sagas:/ˈsɑːɡəz/ | n. |传奇故事；英雄史诗|Long prose narratives, typically of heroic exploits, often from Scandinavian or Icelandic folklore.  They are characterized by their epic scope and often involve mythical or legendary figures and events.|The Icelandic sagas are renowned for their detailed descriptions of life in medieval Iceland.|近义词：legends, epics, myths|反义词：brief story, anecdote
