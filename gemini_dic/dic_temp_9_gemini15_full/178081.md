recliner: /rɪˈklaɪnər/ | n. | 躺椅；斜倚椅 | A recliner is a chair that can be tilted back to a reclining position, often with a footrest that extends.  It's designed for comfort and relaxation. | Example:  He relaxed in his favorite recliner after a long day at work. 他在辛苦工作一天后，放松地坐在他最喜欢的躺椅上。  She bought a new recliner with a built-in massage function. 她买了一张带内置按摩功能的新躺椅。| 近义词：armchair, lounger | 反义词：standing chair (a chair that doesn't recline)
