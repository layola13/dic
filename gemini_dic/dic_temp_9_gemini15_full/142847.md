out-from-under:/ˌaʊt frəm ˈʌndər/ | adv. | 从…下面出来；摆脱；脱离 |  Describes movement from a position underneath or a state of oppression or concealment. It implies a complete emergence or escape. | 例句：The cat emerged out from under the bed.  猫从床底下出来了。

The company finally broke out from under its massive debt. 这家公司终于摆脱了巨额债务。


近义词：from under, out of, away from | 反义词：into, under, beneath | 词性：副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emerges out from under the table every morning. 他每天早上都从桌子底下出来。
The plant grows out from under the soil. 植物从土壤中生长出来。

一般过去时:
She pulled the blanket out from under the child. 她把毯子从孩子身下拉出来。
The secret finally came out from under wraps. 秘密最终被揭露。

现在完成时:
The truth has finally come out from under the surface. 真相终于浮出水面。
We have broken out from under the control of the dictator. 我们已经摆脱了独裁者的控制。

过去完成时:
Before the police arrived, the thief had escaped out from under the house. 在警察到达之前，小偷已经从房子底下逃跑了。
The bird had flown out from under the bush. 鸟儿已经从灌木丛下飞走了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
