favela:/fəˈvɛlə/| n.|贫民窟|A favela is a Brazilian shantytown, typically characterized by informal housing, poverty, and a lack of basic services.  Favela residents often lack access to clean water, sanitation, and formal education.|Example:  Rio de Janeiro is known for its vibrant culture and its many favelas.|近义词：shantytown, slum|反义词：affluent suburb, upscale neighborhood
