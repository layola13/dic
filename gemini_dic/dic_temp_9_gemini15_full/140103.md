off-load:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/| vt. n.|卸载，卸货，转移负担|To remove a load from; unload.  To transfer (a task, responsibility, etc.) to another person or system.|例句：The truck was used to off-load the goods at the warehouse. 这辆卡车被用来在仓库卸货。

The manager decided to off-load some of his work to his assistant. 经理决定把一些工作转移给他的助手。

近义词：unload, transfer, delegate|反义词：load, burden, shoulder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He offloads the packages every morning. 他每天早上卸载包裹。
The company offloads its data to a cloud server. 公司将数据卸载到云服务器。

一般过去时:
They offloaded the container yesterday. 他们昨天卸下了集装箱。
She offloaded her worries onto her friend. 她向朋友倾诉了她的烦恼。


现在完成时:
We have offloaded all the cargo. 我们已经卸完了所有货物。
He has offloaded his responsibilities to his team. 他已将责任分担给他的团队。

过去完成时:
They had offloaded the supplies before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经卸下了物资。
She had offloaded her emotional baggage before starting therapy.  她开始治疗之前已经卸下了她的情感包袱。

将来时:
We will offload the equipment tomorrow. 我们明天将卸载设备。
The project manager will offload some tasks to the interns. 项目经理将把一些任务分派给实习生。


虚拟语气:
If he offloaded his stress, he would feel better. 如果他卸下压力，他会感觉好些。
I wish I could offload my responsibilities more easily. 我希望我能更容易地卸下我的责任。

被动语态:
The goods were offloaded quickly and efficiently. 货物快速有效地被卸下。
The workload is offloaded to the support team. 工作量被分担给支持团队。

疑问句:
When will the containers be offloaded? 集装箱什么时候卸载？
How do you offload your stress? 你如何释放压力？

否定句:
They didn't offload the truck properly. 他们没有正确地卸货。
He couldn't offload his emotional burden. 他无法卸下他的情感负担。
