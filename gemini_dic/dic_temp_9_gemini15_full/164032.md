predisasters:/ˌpriːdɪˈzæstərz/ | n. | 灾难前兆；灾难预警 |  Events or circumstances that precede and indicate the likelihood of a disaster.  The term emphasizes the predictive nature of certain signs and allows for proactive measures. | Example:  The increased seismic activity was a clear predisaster warning sign.  地震活动的加剧是灾难预警的明显迹象。  | 近义词：precursors, warnings, omens, harbingers | 反义词：aftermath, consequences, results
