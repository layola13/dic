evacuation:/ˌɛvjʊˈeɪʃən/ | n. |疏散；撤离 |The act of evacuating or the state of being evacuated; the removal of people from a place of danger to a safer place.  |例句：The evacuation of the city was completed before the hurricane hit.  城市在飓风来临之前完成了疏散。|近义词：removal, withdrawal, exodus |反义词：arrival, ingress
