microphotography: /ˌmaɪkroʊfoˈtɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 显微摄影术 | The technique of photographing microscopic objects or structures, using a microscope to magnify the image before it is recorded.  It involves specialized equipment and techniques to capture high-resolution images of extremely small subjects.  | 例句：Microphotography is essential in fields like biology and materials science. 显微摄影术在生物学和材料科学等领域至关重要。

例句：The researchers used microphotography to study the cellular structure of the plant. 研究人员使用显微摄影术来研究植物的细胞结构。


近义词：microscopy (显微镜检查), photomicrography (显微摄影)


反义词：macrophotography (微距摄影)


词性: 名词
