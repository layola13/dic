jarosite:/ˈdʒærəsaɪt/| n. |黄钾铁矾|A hydrous potassium iron sulfate mineral, typically yellow or brownish-yellow in color. It is often found in acidic environments, such as those formed by the oxidation of sulfide minerals.  |The jarosite found in this mine is unusually pure. 这座矿里发现的黄钾铁矾异常纯净。|近义词: natrojarosite (钠黄钾铁矾)|反义词:  (no direct antonym, but minerals formed under alkaline conditions could be considered opposite)|名词
