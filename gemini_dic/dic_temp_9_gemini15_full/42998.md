cowalker:/ˈkoʊˌwɜːrkər/ | n. |同事|A person who works with you, especially in the same place or at the same time.  A coworker is someone you share a workplace with. |Example: I get along well with most of my coworkers. 我和大多数同事相处融洽。|近义词:colleague, teammate|反义词:supervisor, manager


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I work closely with my coworkers on this project. = 我在这个项目中与我的同事密切合作。
My coworkers help each other when needed. = 我的同事在需要的时候互相帮助。


一般过去时:
My coworkers celebrated my birthday last week. = 我的同事上周为我庆祝生日。
I discussed the project with my coworkers yesterday. = 我昨天与我的同事讨论了这个项目。


现在完成时:
I have worked with my coworkers for five years. = 我和我的同事一起工作了五年。
My coworkers have completed the project ahead of schedule. = 我的同事提前完成了项目。


过去完成时:
I had already informed my coworkers before the meeting started. = 会议开始之前我已经通知了我的同事。
My coworkers had finished their tasks before the deadline. = 我的同事在截止日期之前完成了他们的任务。


将来时:
I will meet with my coworkers tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上将与我的同事会面。
My coworkers will present the findings next week. = 我的同事下周将展示研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would help my coworkers more. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更多地帮助我的同事。
I wish my coworkers were more cooperative. = 我希望我的同事更合作一些。


被动语态:
The project was completed by my coworkers on time. = 这个项目按时由我的同事完成。
The problem was discussed by my coworkers and a solution was found. = 这个问题由我的同事们讨论，并找到了解决方案。


疑问句:
Do you get along well with your coworkers? = 你和你的同事相处得好吗？

否定句:

My coworkers didn't attend the meeting yesterday. = 我的同事昨天没有参加会议。
