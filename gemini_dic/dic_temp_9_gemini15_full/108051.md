late:/leɪt/| adj.|迟的；晚的；最新的；已故的|  Late means after the expected, usual, or proper time. It can also refer to something recent or modern, or to someone who has died.|例句：He was late for the meeting. 他开会迟到了。

The news is late. 消息是刚收到的。|近义词：tardy, delayed, overdue, deceased|反义词：early, punctual, timely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is late for work every day. = 他每天上班都迟到。
The train is late again. = 火车又晚点了。


一般过去时:
I was late for the appointment. = 我约会迟到了。
She arrived late at the party. = 她晚到了聚会。


现在完成时:
He has been late three times this week. = 他本周已经迟到三次了。
The project is late, but we're catching up. = 项目进度落后了，但我们正在赶上。


过去完成时:
I had been late several times, so my boss was angry. = 我已经迟到过好几次了，所以我的老板很生气。
We realized the report was already late when we started working on it. = 当我们开始工作时，我们意识到报告已经迟交了。


将来时:
The flight will be late due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，航班将延误。
I think I will be late for dinner tonight. = 我想我今晚会晚饭吃晚。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been late, I would have caught the bus. = 如果我没有迟到，我就赶上公共汽车了。
I wish I hadn't been late for the interview. = 我真希望我没有面试迟到。


被动语态:
The delivery was late because of the storm. = 由于暴风雨，货物送晚了。
The meeting was declared late to start. = 会议宣布迟到开始。


疑问句:
Is he late? = 他迟到了吗？
Are you late for class? = 你上课迟到了吗？


否定句:
He is not late. = 他没有迟到。
The train is not late today. = 火车今天没有晚点。



PHRASE->
of late: 最近
late at night: 深夜
too late: 太迟了
better late than never: 迟到总比不到好
as of late: 近来
