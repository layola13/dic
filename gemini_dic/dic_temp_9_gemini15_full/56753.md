doorstops:/ˈdɔːrstɑːps/| n.|门档，门楔|A doorstop is a wedge-shaped object placed at the bottom of a door to keep it open.  They can be made of various materials, such as rubber, wood, or metal.  They are commonly used to prevent doors from closing. |例句：I need a doorstop to keep the door open while I paint.|近义词：door wedge, door holder|反义词:None.
