divorcer:/dɪˈvɔːrsər/ | n. |离婚者，办理离婚手续的人 | A person who initiates or is involved in a divorce.  | Example: He was a divorcer who quickly moved on after the separation. 他是一个离婚者，分手后很快就开始了新的生活。 | 近义词：divorcee, separator | 反义词：spouse, partner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The divorcer files for divorce. = 离婚者提出离婚申请。
He divorces his wife. = 他和他妻子离婚了。

一般过去时:
The divorcer initiated the legal proceedings. = 离婚者启动了法律程序。
She divorced him last year. = 她去年和他离婚了。

现在完成时:
The divorcer has finalized the divorce. = 离婚者已经完成了离婚手续。
They have been divorced for five years. = 他们离婚五年了。

过去完成时:
The divorcer had already consulted a lawyer. = 离婚者已经咨询过律师了。
She had been unhappy in the marriage for years before she divorced him. = 在她和他离婚之前，她多年来婚姻一直不幸福。

将来时:
The divorcer will attend court next week. = 离婚者下周将出庭。
They will divorce soon. = 他们很快就会离婚。

虚拟语气:
If he were a divorcer, he would understand the process better. = 如果他是离婚者，他会更好地理解这个过程。
I wish I hadn't become a divorcer. = 我真希望我没有成为离婚者。

被动语态:
The divorce was initiated by the divorcer. = 离婚是由离婚者提出的。
The property was divided after the divorcer and spouse reached an agreement. = 在离婚者和配偶达成协议后，财产被分割了。

疑问句:
Is he a divorcer? = 他是离婚者吗？
Was she the divorcer in the case? = 在这个案件中，她是离婚者吗？

否定句:
He is not a divorcer. = 他不是离婚者。
She was not the divorcer; it was her husband. = 她不是离婚者，是她丈夫。
