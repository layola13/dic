interwrapping: /ˌɪntərˈræpɪŋ/ | adj. | 交织的，互相缠绕的 |  Describes something that is interwoven or intertwined;  things are wrapped around or among each other. | 例句：The interwrapping vines formed a dense, green wall.  交织的藤蔓形成了一堵茂密的绿色墙。

The interwrapping threads created a complex and beautiful tapestry. 交织的线创造了一幅复杂而美丽的挂毯。


近义词：entwined, interwoven, intertwined, interlaced, tangled


反义词：separate, unentangled, disentangled


词性：形容词
