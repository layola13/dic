propagate:/ˈprɒpəɡeɪt/ /ˈprɑːpəɡeɪt/| vt. vi. n.|传播；繁殖；增殖|To spread or cause to spread widely; to multiply or increase in number.|例句：The fire quickly propagated through the dry grass. 火迅速蔓延到干草丛中。The plants propagate by seeds. 这些植物靠种子繁殖。|近义词：spread, disseminate, multiply, reproduce|反义词：suppress, extinguish, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company propagates its brand through social media. = 公司通过社交媒体传播其品牌。
The bacteria propagate rapidly in warm conditions. = 细菌在温暖的环境中迅速繁殖。

一般过去时:
The rumor propagated quickly throughout the town. = 谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。
The farmer propagated new varieties of apples. = 农民繁殖了新品种的苹果。

现在完成时:
The idea has propagated widely across the globe. = 这个想法已经在全球广泛传播。
The plant has propagated successfully through cuttings. = 这种植物通过扦插成功繁殖了。

过去完成时:
The disease had propagated extensively before the outbreak was discovered. = 在发现疫情爆发之前，这种疾病已经广泛传播了。
The family had propagated their business for generations. = 这个家族世世代代传承发展他们的生意。

将来时:
The government will propagate its new policies through the media. = 政府将通过媒体宣传其新政策。
We will propagate the seeds in the spring. = 我们将在春天播种。

虚拟语气:
If they propagated their message effectively, they would gain more support. = 如果他们有效地传播信息，他们将获得更多支持。
I wish the plants had propagated more successfully. = 我希望这些植物繁殖得更成功一些。

被动语态:
The news was propagated by the mainstream media. = 这条新闻是由主流媒体传播的。
New varieties of rice are being propagated by agricultural scientists. = 农业科学家们正在培育新的水稻品种。

疑问句:
How does this plant propagate? = 这种植物是如何繁殖的？
How is the information propagated? = 信息是如何传播的？

否定句:
The rumor did not propagate widely. = 谣言并没有广泛传播。
The seeds failed to propagate in the poor soil. = 种子在贫瘠的土壤中未能繁殖。
