dashedly:/ˈdæʃɪdli/| adv.|仓促地；鲁莽地；大胆地；不顾一切地|In a hasty or reckless manner; with great energy or boldness.  It implies a lack of careful consideration or planning.|例句：He dashedly crossed the street, nearly getting hit by a car. 他仓促地穿过马路，差点被车撞到。

例句：She dashedly declared her love, surprising everyone. 她大胆地宣称自己的爱意，让每个人都感到惊讶。

近义词：recklessly, hastily, rashly, impetuously, wildly

反义词：cautiously, carefully, deliberately, thoughtfully

词性:副词
