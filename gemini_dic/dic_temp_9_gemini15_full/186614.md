riff:/rɪf/| n. v.|反复的乐句，即兴演奏；（口语）挖苦，嘲弄|A short, repeated musical phrase;  a repeated or recurring element in a conversation or situation; (informal) to tease or make fun of someone.  |例句：The guitarist played a catchy riff. 吉他手弹奏了一段朗朗上口的反复乐句。 The comedian riffed on current events. 喜剧演员对时事进行了调侃。|近义词：refrain, repeat,  (v.) tease, mock |反义词：monotone (n.),  serious (v.) |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He riffs on the same chord progression. = 他反复弹奏同样的和弦进行。
The band often riffs during their live shows. = 乐队经常在现场演出中即兴演奏。

一般过去时:
She riffed a funny story about her cat. = 她讲了一个关于她猫的滑稽故事。
The musicians riffed on a blues scale. = 音乐家们即兴演奏布鲁斯音阶。

现在完成时:
They have riffed on that joke many times. = 他们已经多次重复过那个笑话了。
The comedian has riffed on politics throughout his career. = 这名喜剧演员在他的整个职业生涯中都在调侃政治。

过去完成时:
He had riffed on that topic before, so he moved on. = 他以前就谈论过那个话题，所以他转移了话题。
The band had riffed extensively before settling on their final arrangement. = 乐队在确定最终编排之前进行了大量的即兴演奏。

将来时:
She will riff on the theme of love in her next stand-up routine. = 她将在她的下一个单口喜剧表演中以爱情为主题即兴发挥。
The jazz musicians will riff spontaneously throughout the performance. = 爵士乐音乐家将在整个演出中自发地即兴演奏。

虚拟语气:
If he riffed less, the performance would be more focused. = 如果他少一些即兴发挥，演出就会更有重点。
I wish he hadn't riffed on my personal life. = 我真希望他没有调侃我的私生活。

被动语态:
The song was built around a catchy riff. = 这首歌是围绕着一个朗朗上口的反复乐句构建的。
His jokes were often riffed on by other comedians. = 他的笑话经常被其他喜剧演员模仿和改编。

疑问句:
Will he riff on the same material again? = 他会再次重复同样的内容吗？
What will they riff about tonight? = 他们今晚会即兴发挥什么？

否定句:
He didn't riff on anything controversial. = 他没有调侃任何有争议的事情。
The guitarist didn't riff during the slow ballad. = 吉他手在演奏缓慢的歌谣时没有即兴演奏。


PHRASE->
riff on something: 对…进行即兴发挥/调侃
a killer riff:  极好的反复乐句
a catchy riff:  朗朗上口的反复乐句
riffing on a theme:  围绕一个主题进行即兴发挥
to riff something out:  即兴演奏出…


词源:  The word's origin is uncertain.  It may be related to earlier slang terms for repeated phrases or possibly onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of repeated musical notes.
