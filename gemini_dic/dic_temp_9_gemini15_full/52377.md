dictums:/ˈdɪktəmz/ | n. |格言；断言；权威性说法 |  A dictum is a formal statement of principle, opinion, or belief, often made by a person in authority. It can also refer to a saying or proverb that expresses a general truth or piece of advice.  | The judge's dictum was clear: justice would be served.  法官的断言很明确：正义将会得到伸张。 | maxims, proverbs, pronouncements, aphorisms |  (无明显反义词，取决于语境，例如：contradiction, refutation) | 名词
