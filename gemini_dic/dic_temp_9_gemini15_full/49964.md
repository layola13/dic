denied:/dɪˈnaɪd/| vt.|拒绝，否认 |To declare untrue; refuse to grant or allow.  |例句：The bank denied his loan application. = 银行拒绝了他的贷款申请。
The witness denied seeing the suspect. = 证人否认见过嫌疑人。|近义词：refuse, reject, repudiate |反义词：grant, allow, admit, concede |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He denies the accusations. = 他否认这些指控。
They deny access to the building. = 他们拒绝进入该建筑物。

一般过去时:
She denied everything the police said. = 她否认了警方所说的一切。
The judge denied the motion. = 法官驳回了动议。

现在完成时:
They have denied any involvement in the crime. = 他们否认参与了犯罪。
He has repeatedly denied the allegations. = 他一再否认这些指控。

过去完成时:
He had denied knowing her before the trial. = 在审判之前，他否认认识她。
They had denied the request previously. = 他们之前已经拒绝了这个请求。

将来时:
The government will likely deny the request for funding. = 政府可能会拒绝拨款请求。
She will deny all responsibility for the accident. = 她将否认对事故的全部责任。

虚拟语气:
If he denied the truth, I wouldn't believe him. = 如果他否认真相，我不会相信他。
I wish he hadn't denied my request. = 我真希望他没有拒绝我的请求。

被动语态:
His request was denied. = 他的请求被拒绝了。
Access to the information was denied. = 访问信息被拒绝了。

疑问句:
Did he deny the charges? = 他是否否认了指控？
Will they deny the evidence? = 他们会否认证据吗？

否定句:
He didn't deny that he was there. = 他没有否认他在那里。
She couldn't deny the truth of the matter. = 她无法否认事情的真相。


PHRASE->
