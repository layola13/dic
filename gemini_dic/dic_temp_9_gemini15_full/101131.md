investigatingly: /ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 探究地；调查似地 | In a manner suggesting investigation or inquiry.  It implies a careful and detailed examination, often with suspicion or curiosity. | 例句：He looked at the evidence investigatingly. 他审慎地查看了证据。

He examined the scene investigatingly, noting every detail. 他调查似地检查了现场，注意每一个细节。| 近义词：inquisitively, searchingly, inquiringly | 反义词：casually, carelessly, superficially


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It doesn't have tenses.  It simply modifies a verb to show the manner in which an action is performed.

例句1: She looked at the strange package investigatingly. 她审视地看了看那个奇怪的包裹。
例句2: The detective investigated the crime scene investigatingly, searching for clues.  侦探调查似地勘察了犯罪现场，寻找线索。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "investigatingly".  Its usage is primarily as a standalone adverb modifying verbs of investigation or observation.


词源:

The word "investigatingly" is derived from the verb "investigate," which comes from the Latin word "investigare," meaning "to track down; to search out."  The suffix "-ingly" is an adverbial suffix, transforming the present participle "investigating" into an adverb.
