crossbreed:/ˈkrɒsbriːd/| n. v.|杂交品种；混血儿；杂交|A crossbreed is an animal or plant that is the offspring of parents of different breeds or varieties.  It can also refer to a person of mixed racial or ethnic heritage. |例句：The farmer bred a crossbreed of cow for its increased milk production. 这个农民培育了一种杂交奶牛以提高产奶量。

The new dog is a crossbreed of a Labrador and a Golden Retriever. 这只新狗是拉布拉多犬和金毛猎犬的杂交品种。

近义词：hybrid, mongrel, mixed breed|反义词：purebred
