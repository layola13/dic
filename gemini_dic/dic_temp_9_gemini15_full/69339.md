fedary:/ˈfɛdəri/| n.|同伙，伙伴，盟友|A fedary is a confederate or accomplice, often implying a shared secret or undertaking.  The term suggests a close and often conspiratorial relationship.  It carries a slightly archaic or literary tone.|例句：The king discovered his fedary's plot to overthrow him. 国王发现了他的同伙密谋推翻他的阴谋。
