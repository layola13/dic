intercomparing:/ˌɪntər kəmˈpɛərɪŋ/ | v. |相互比较；互相对比 | The act of comparing two or more things to each other.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of the comparison, suggesting a back-and-forth process of evaluation. | Example: Researchers are intercomparing the results from different experiments to identify consistent patterns. 研究人员正在相互比较不同实验的结果以识别一致的模式。|近义词: comparing, contrasting, correlating | 反义词: separating, isolating
