demobilizes: /ˌdiːˈməʊbɪlaɪz/ | v. | 解散；复员；使…停止活动 | To disband or discharge (soldiers, etc.) from military service; to stop the activities of (something).  | The army demobilized after the war ended.  军队在战争结束后复员了。| The company demobilized its marketing team after the product launch. 公司在产品发布后解散了其营销团队。| mobilize, activate |  immobilize, activate
