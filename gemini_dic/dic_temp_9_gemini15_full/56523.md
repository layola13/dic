domiciliary:/ˌdɒmɪˈsɪlɪəri/| adj.|住所的；家访的；家中的|Relating to or happening at someone's home.  The adjective refers to something connected with one's home or residence.|The domiciliary visit was necessary to assess the patient's home environment.  家访是评估病人家庭环境所必需的。|domestic, home, residential|nondomiciliary
