pseudo-russian:/ˈsuːdəʊˈrʌʃən/| adj. |伪俄罗斯的，假冒俄罗斯的 | seemingly or deceptively Russian;  often used to describe something that imitates or attempts to emulate Russian style or culture, but lacks authenticity.  It can be applied to various aspects, including art, music, literature, architecture, and even political ideologies. | Example: The film's pseudo-Russian setting felt unconvincing due to its numerous historical inaccuracies.  这部电影的伪俄罗斯背景因其众多历史错误而显得缺乏说服力。|近义词: ersatz, imitation, sham, fake|反义词: genuine, authentic, real
