half-pipe:/ˈhæfpaɪp/| n. |U型池, 半管(滑板、滑雪等运动场地)|A half-pipe is a ramp with a curved, U-shaped surface used in skateboarding, snowboarding, and other extreme sports.  It is typically made of wood, concrete, or metal. Riders gain speed by dropping into the pipe and then ride back and forth along the curved walls, performing tricks in the air. |The skater landed a perfect 900 in the half-pipe.  滑板运动员在U型池中完成了完美的900度旋转。

The snowboarder soared high above the half-pipe. 单板滑雪运动员在U型池上方高高跃起。|近义词：U-shaped ramp, vert ramp|反义词：flat surface
