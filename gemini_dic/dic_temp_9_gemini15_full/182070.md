remeshed:/riːˈmɛʃt/| v. |重新网格化，重新划分网格|To create a new mesh for (a 3D model or similar) by altering the existing mesh's structure.  This often involves changing the polygon count, adding or removing vertices and edges, or smoothing the surface.| 例句：The 3D artist remeshed the model to improve its performance in the game engine.  三维艺术家重新划分了模型的网格，以提高其在游戏引擎中的性能。


The model needed remeshing before texturing.  模型在纹理化之前需要重新网格化。|近义词：remodel, refine, restructure |反义词：coarsen (in the context of mesh density)
