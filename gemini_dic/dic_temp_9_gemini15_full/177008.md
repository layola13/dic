amendment:/əˈmɛndmənt/ | n. | 修正案；修改；改进 | A change or correction made to a document, law, or other text.  | The amendment to the constitution guarantees equal rights.  宪法修正案保障平等权利。| alteration, modification, revision |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境用original version, unmodified form等) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The committee is considering several amendments to the bill.  委员会正在考虑对该法案的几项修正案。
This amendment improves the clarity of the document.  这项修正案提高了文件的清晰度。


一般过去时:
The parliament passed an amendment to the tax law.  议会通过了一项税法修正案。
They made several amendments to the original plan.  他们对最初的计划做了一些修改。


现在完成时:
Congress has passed many amendments since its inception. 国会自成立以来已经通过了许多修正案。
She has made several amendments to her manuscript. 她已经对她的手稿做了几次修改。


过去完成时:
Before the final vote, they had already made several amendments to the proposal.  在最终投票之前，他们已经对提案做了几次修改。
The committee had made significant amendments before presenting the report.  委员会在提交报告之前已经做了重大修改。


将来时:
The government will propose an amendment to the education bill next month.  政府将于下月提出对教育法案的修正案。
We will need to make several amendments to the contract before signing.  在签署合同之前，我们需要对合同做几处修改。


虚拟语气:
If the amendment were passed, it would significantly impact the economy. 如果这项修正案获得通过，它将对经济产生重大影响。
I wish the amendment had been approved earlier. 我希望这项修正案早些获得批准。


被动语态:
The amendment was debated for hours before being approved.  这项修正案在获得批准之前经过了数小时的辩论。
The document will be amended to reflect the latest changes.  该文件将被修改以反映最新的变化。


疑问句:
Are there any further amendments to the proposal?  对该提案还有其他修改吗？
What amendments were made to the original document?  最初的文件做了哪些修改？


否定句:
No amendments were made to the final version of the report.  报告的最终版本没有任何修改。
The court rejected the proposed amendment. 法院否决了提议的修正案。
