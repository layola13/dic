pre-cleared:/ˌpriːˈklɪərd/| adj.|预先清除的；预先批准的 | Already cleared or approved in advance.  This term often implies a process has been completed before a specific event or action. |例句：The pre-cleared documents expedited the process.  预先清除的文件加快了流程。

The shipment arrived with pre-cleared customs documentation.  货物抵达时带有预先报关的单据。|近义词：approved, authorized, vetted |反义词：unapproved, unauthorized, uncleared


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The documents are pre-cleared. = 文件已经预先清除/批准。
He pre-clears all his travel arrangements. = 他预先处理好所有旅行安排。


一般过去时:
They pre-cleared the site before construction. = 他们在施工前预先清理了场地。
She pre-cleared the application for the permit. = 她预先审批了许可证申请。


现在完成时:
We have pre-cleared all the necessary materials. = 我们已经预先清理了所有必要的材料。
He has pre-cleared the project with the manager. = 他已经与经理预先批准了该项目。


过去完成时:
They had pre-cleared the path before the hikers arrived. = 远足者到达前，他们已预先清理了道路。
She had pre-cleared the funds for the project. = 她已为该项目预先拨款。


将来时:
We will pre-clear the area for the event. = 我们将预先清理活动区域。
They will pre-clear the application next week. = 他们下周将预先审批申请。


虚拟语气:
If they had pre-cleared the documents, the delay would have been avoided. = 如果他们预先处理了文件，就可以避免延误。
I wish I had pre-cleared my travel visa. = 我真希望我预先办理了旅游签证。


被动语态:
The shipment was pre-cleared by customs. = 货物已由海关预先清关。
The application will be pre-cleared by the committee. = 申请将由委员会预先审批。


疑问句:
Were the documents pre-cleared? = 文件是否预先清除/批准？
Have the permits been pre-cleared? = 许可证是否已预先批准？


否定句:
The documents were not pre-cleared. = 文件未经预先清除/批准。
The application has not been pre-cleared yet. = 申请尚未预先批准。
