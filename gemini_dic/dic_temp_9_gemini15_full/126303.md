myographic:/ˌmaɪəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|肌电图的；肌动描记的 | Relating to or using myography, the process of recording the electrical activity of muscles.  Myographic techniques are used in various medical and research settings to study muscle function and diagnose neuromuscular disorders. | Example: The myographic data revealed significant muscle fatigue after prolonged exercise. 肌电图数据显示长时间运动后肌肉疲劳显著。|近义词：electromyographic|反义词：N/A
