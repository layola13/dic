emissaries:/ˈɛmɪsɛriːz/| n.|使节；特使；代表|Representatives sent on a special mission, usually diplomatic.  They often carry messages or negotiate on behalf of a government or organization.|The president sent emissaries to negotiate a peace treaty.|近义词：envoys, delegates, messengers|反义词：None specifically, but  "ordinary citizens" could be considered an antonym in the context of special missions.
