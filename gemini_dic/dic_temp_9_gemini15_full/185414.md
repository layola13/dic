retrovert:/ˈrɛtroʊvɜːrt/ | v. |使倒退；使逆转；使反向|To turn or move backward; to revert.  The term often implies a return to a previous state or condition, sometimes with a sense of regression.|例句：The company retroverted to its old methods after the new strategy failed. 公司在新战略失败后又回到了旧方法。


The economic downturn retroverted the growth of the country.经济衰退使国家的增长倒退了。|近义词：revert, regress, reverse|反义词：progress, advance, evolve|
