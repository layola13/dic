gruffed:/ɡrʌft/| v.|粗暴地说话；粗鲁地对待；抱怨；发牢骚|To speak roughly or rudely; to treat roughly; to grumble or complain.|例句：He gruffed at the waiter for the slow service. 他因为服务慢而粗暴地对待服务员。|例句：She gruffed about the unfair treatment she received. 她抱怨自己受到不公平的待遇。|近义词：snapped, grumbled, snarled, complained|反义词：complimented, praised, appeased


USAGE->

She gruffs about the workload. = 她抱怨工作量太大。
