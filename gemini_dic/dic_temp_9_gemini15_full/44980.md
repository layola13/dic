crystallising: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 结晶；明确化；使具体化 | The process of a substance forming crystals.  To make something clear or definite.  To give a definite form to an idea or feeling. |例句：The sugar is crystallising in the syrup. 糖在糖浆中结晶。 The ideas are crystallising into a plan. 这些想法正在形成一个计划。|近义词：solidifying, clarifying, defining |反义词：dissolving, blurring, obscuring |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sugar is crystallising slowly. = 糖正在慢慢结晶。
He is crystallising his thoughts on the matter. = 他正在明确他对这件事的看法。


一般过去时:
The solution crystallised overnight. = 溶液一夜之间结晶了。
She crystallised her feelings into a powerful poem. = 她将自己的情感凝练成一首充满力量的诗歌。


现在完成时:
The scientists have crystallised a new protein structure. = 科学家们已经结晶了一种新的蛋白质结构。
My understanding of the problem has crystallised. = 我对这个问题的理解已经清晰了。


过去完成时:
The team had crystallised their strategy before the presentation. = 团队在演示之前已经确定了他们的策略。
He had crystallised his ideas into a well-structured argument. = 他已经将自己的想法提炼成一个结构严谨的论点。


将来时:

We will crystallise our vision for the future in a detailed business plan. = 我们将在详细的商业计划中明确我们对未来的愿景。


虚拟语气:

否定句:
