词源:
The word "chiffoniere" comes from the French word "chiffon," meaning "rag" or "shred."  Originally, a chiffoniere was a small chest used to store rags or other bits of fabric. Over time, its usage evolved to include the storage of other items, and its design became increasingly sophisticated.
