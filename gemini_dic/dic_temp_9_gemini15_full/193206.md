seducer:/sɪˈduːsər/| n. |诱惑者，引诱者 | A person who seduces someone, especially someone who is considered innocent or naive.  Someone who uses charm or persuasion to persuade someone to do something. | He was a charming but ultimately dangerous seducer. 他是一个有魅力但最终很危险的诱惑者。 |近义词：tempter, enticer, charmer |反义词：rejecter, abstainer | 名词
