could-care-less:/kʊdˈkɛərlɛs/| adj.|毫不在乎的，漠不关心的|The phrase "couldn't care less" expresses extreme indifference or lack of concern.  It's ironic, as the literal meaning is the opposite. The speaker is actually saying they care so little that they couldn't care any less. |例句：I could care less about what he thinks. = 我一点也不在乎他怎么想。
He could care less about the mess he made. = 他对造成的烂摊子毫不在乎。|近义词：indifferent, unconcerned, apathetic |反义词：concerned, caring, interested
