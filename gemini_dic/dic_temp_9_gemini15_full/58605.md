dullhead:/ˈdʌlhed/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A dullhead is a stupid or unintelligent person.  It is an informal and somewhat insulting term.|例句：He's such a dullhead; he can't even follow simple instructions. 他真是个笨蛋，连简单的指示都听不懂。|例句：Don't be such a dullhead!  Think before you speak!  别那么傻！说话前先动动脑子！|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, simpleton|反义词：genius, intellectual, bright spark
