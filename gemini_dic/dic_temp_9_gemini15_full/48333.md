decriminalises:/diːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/| v.|使非罪化；使合法化 | To remove the criminal status of something.  The act of making something that was previously a crime no longer a crime. | 例句：Many countries have decriminalised the possession of small amounts of marijuana. = 许多国家已经将持有少量大麻非罪化。  The government is considering decriminalising assisted suicide. = 政府正在考虑使辅助自杀合法化。| 近义词：legalise, legitimate | 反义词：criminalise
