gusted:/ˈɡʌstɪd/| adj.|厌恶的，反感的|Feeling strong dislike or disgust.  The smell gusted him.|The foul odor gusted him.  The sight of the blood gusted him.|近义词：disgusted, revolted, sickened|反义词：pleased, delighted

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gusted by the sight of insects. = 他对昆虫的景象感到厌恶。
The smell gusted her. = 这个气味让她感到恶心。

I was gusted by the violence in the movie. = 我对电影中的暴力感到反感。
