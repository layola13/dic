pulmometer:/pʌlˈmɒmɪtər/ | n. |肺活量计|A pulmometer is a device used to measure the vital capacity of the lungs.  It measures the amount of air a person can exhale after taking the deepest possible breath. |The doctor used a pulmometer to assess the patient's lung function. 医生用肺活量计评估患者的肺功能。| spirometer |  (no direct antonym, but terms indicating poor lung function could be considered contextual opposites)|名词
