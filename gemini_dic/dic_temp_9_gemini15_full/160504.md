pore:/pɔːr/| /pɔːr/| n. v.|毛孔；细孔；仔细阅读；沉思|To examine something very carefully; a tiny opening, especially in the skin; to read or study something attentively.|The pores on her face were clogged with dirt.  她的脸上毛孔堵塞了污垢。

He pored over the map, trying to find his way. 他仔细研究地图，试图找到方向。


|近义词：opening, hole, scrutinize, study|反义词：glance, skim|名词，动词
