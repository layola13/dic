goldilocks:/ˈɡoʊldɪˌlɑːks/|/ˈɡəʊldɪˌlɒks/| n. |金发姑娘|The name of the heroine in a popular children's fairy tale, "Goldilocks and the Three Bears."  The name suggests a character with golden hair and a personality that is "just right"—not too hot, not too cold, but just right.  This is reflected in the story where she tries three bowls of porridge, three chairs, and three beds, choosing the one that is "just right" for her.|Example:  The story of Goldilocks and the Three Bears is a classic children's tale.|近义词：None (proper noun)|反义词：None (proper noun)|名词
