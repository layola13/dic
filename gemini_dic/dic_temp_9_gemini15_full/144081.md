outrushes:/ˈaʊtrʌʃɪz/| n. |涌出；奔涌而出 |The rapid flow or rush of something out.  It often describes a sudden and forceful movement of a large quantity of something. |例句：A wave of outrushes from the stadium after the game ended.  比赛结束后，人群从体育场涌出。
The outrushes of water from the dam were spectacular. 水坝泄洪的景象十分壮观。|近义词：outflow, rush, surge, torrent |反义词：inflow, influx


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water outrushes from the broken pipe. = 水从破裂的管道中涌出。
Lava outrushes from the volcano. = 岩浆从火山喷涌而出。

一般过去时:
The crowd outrushed the stadium after the concert. = 音乐会结束后，人群涌出体育场。

过去完成时:
