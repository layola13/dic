noninclusively:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkluːsɪvli/ | adv. | 不包含地；排斥性地 |  The term "noninclusively" means not including; in a way that excludes something or someone. It emphasizes that something is left out or not considered part of a whole.  | The survey results were presented noninclusively, omitting data from the control group.  调查结果没有包含对照组的数据，以排斥的方式呈现。|inclusively, comprehensively |exclusively, restrictively| 副词
