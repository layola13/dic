loss-making: /ˈlɒsˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 亏损的，赔钱的 |  Describes a business, activity, or project that is losing money.  It emphasizes the ongoing nature of the financial losses. |

例句：The company has been loss-making for the past three years. = 这家公司过去三年一直处于亏损状态。

The loss-making project was finally shut down. = 那个亏损的项目最终被关闭了。


近义词：unprofitable, unprofitable, money-losing, deficit-making

反义词：profitable, lucrative, gainful
