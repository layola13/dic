It aims to build hype and increase sales or improve chances of a successful promotion.  | Example: The company launched a prepromotion campaign featuring sneak peeks of the new product to build excitement before the official release.  公司发起了一场预先促销活动，展示新产品的抢先看，以在正式发布前营造兴奋感。| 近义词:pre-launch promotion, advance publicity, early bird promotion | 反义词:post-promotion, delayed launch | 名词
