muslinet:/ˈmʌzlɪnɛt/| n. |薄棉布；细棉布|A lightweight, plain-woven cotton fabric, often sheer and translucent.  It's known for its softness and drape.  |The dress was made of delicate muslinet, perfect for a summer evening.|近义词：batiste, voile, gauze|反义词：denim, canvas
