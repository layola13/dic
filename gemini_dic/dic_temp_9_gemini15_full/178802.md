recurrency:/rɪˈkɜːrənsi/| n. |复发；再现；经常发生|The recurrence of the problem forced them to rethink their strategy. 问题的反复出现迫使他们重新考虑策略。|近义词：repetition, reappearance, recurrence|反义词：uniqueness, singularity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The recurrence of this error is frustrating. = 这个错误的反复出现令人沮丧。
Recurrence of the symptoms is common after surgery. = 手术后症状复发很常见。

一般过去时:
A recurrence of the illness hospitalized him again. = 病情的复发使他再次住院。
The recurrence of the drought was devastating for farmers. = 干旱的再次发生对农民来说是毁灭性的。

现在完成时:
There have been several recurrences of the disease this year. = 今年这种疾病已经复发了数次。
We have seen a recurrence of the same problem in different departments. = 我们在不同的部门看到了同样的问题再次出现。

过去完成时:
Before the recent outbreak, there had been no recurrence of the virus for decades. = 在最近的爆发之前，这种病毒几十年来都没有复发。
By then, he had experienced several recurrences of his migraine headaches. = 到那时，他已经经历了几次偏头痛复发。

将来时:
Doctors predict a recurrence of the infection is likely. = 医生预测感染很可能复发。
The forecast indicates a recurrence of heavy rainfall next week. = 预报显示下周将再次出现强降雨。

虚拟语气:
If there were a recurrence of the earthquake, the city would be severely damaged. = 如果地震再次发生，这座城市将会遭受严重的破坏。
I wish there were no recurrence of this violence. = 我希望这种暴力不会再次发生。

被动语态:
The recurrence of the issue was discussed at length. = 该问题的再次出现被详细讨论。
The possibility of recurrence was carefully assessed by the doctors. = 医生仔细评估了复发的可能性。

疑问句:
Is there any chance of recurrence? = 有复发的可能性吗？
What are the risk factors for recurrence? = 复发的危险因素有哪些？

否定句:
There was no recurrence of the symptoms after treatment. = 治疗后症状没有复发。
The study showed no significant recurrence of the problem. = 研究表明该问题没有显著复发。
