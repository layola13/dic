/mɔːk/| v.|伤感地哭泣；哭诉；悲叹|To cry weakly or feebly; to whine or complain in a childish or affected manner.  |例句：The child mawkily cried for his mother.  孩子伤心地哭着喊妈妈。|例句：She mawkily complained about her unfair treatment. 她哭哭啼啼地抱怨自己受到不公平待遇。|近义词：whimper, whine, snivel|反义词：laugh, rejoice

She mawks about her problems all day. = 她整天哭哭啼啼地抱怨她的问题。
