obstructions:/əbˈstrʌkʃənz/| n.|障碍物；阻碍；妨碍|Something that blocks or obstructs.  It can refer to physical objects, but also to abstract things that hinder progress or movement.|例句：The fallen tree caused a major obstruction on the road. 倒下的树木造成了道路上的主要障碍。

例句：Bureaucracy is a major obstruction to efficient government. 官僚主义是政府效率的主要障碍。|近义词：impediments, hindrances, obstacles, barriers|反义词：clearances, openings, pathways
