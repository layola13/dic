school-leaver:/ˈskuːlˌliːvər/| n. |毕业生，离开学校的人|A school-leaver is a young person who has recently finished their schooling and is now looking for work or further education.  They are typically in their late teens or early twenties.|Many school-leavers choose to go to university.|近义词：graduate (if they have a degree),  recent graduate|反义词：schoolchild, pupil
