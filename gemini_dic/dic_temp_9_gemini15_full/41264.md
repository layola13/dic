cord:/kɔːrd/| n. /kɔːd/|绳索，线，电线|A cord is a piece of strong string or thin rope.  It can also refer to a group of wires or fibers bound together.|The climber secured the rope to the cord.|绳索，电线，线|近义词：rope, string, cable|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lamp is powered by a long cord. = 这盏灯由一根长电线供电。
He ties the package with a cord. = 他用绳子捆绑包裹。

一般过去时:
She pulled the cord to open the blinds. = 她拉绳子打开百叶窗。
The electrician replaced the damaged cord. = 电工更换了损坏的电线。

现在完成时:
They have installed a new electrical cord. = 他们已经安装了一根新的电线。
She has cut the cord connecting the two machines. = 她剪断了连接两台机器的电线。

过去完成时:
The machine had already malfunctioned before they checked the cord. = 在他们检查电线之前，机器就已经出现故障了。
He had tied the parcel with a strong cord before he mailed it. = 他在邮寄包裹之前用结实的绳子捆绑好了。

将来时:
We will need a longer cord for the new television. = 我们需要一根更长的电线来连接新的电视机。
He will replace the frayed cord tomorrow. = 他明天会更换磨损的绳子。


虚拟语气:
If I had a longer cord, I could reach the outlet. = 如果我有一根更长的电线，我就能够到插座。
I wish I had a stronger cord to tie this heavy box. = 我希望我有一根更结实的绳子来捆绑这个沉重的箱子。

被动语态:
The cord was pulled by the child. = 电线被孩子拉动了。
The new cord was installed by the technician. = 新的电线由技师安装。

疑问句:
Is this cord long enough? = 这根电线够长吗？
What kind of cord do you need? = 你需要什么类型的电线？

否定句:
The cord isn't long enough to reach the socket. = 这根电线不够长，够不到插座。
He didn't have a cord to tie up the luggage. = 他没有绳子捆绑行李。
