rappage:/ræˈpæʒ/| n.|  一阵猛烈的敲打；连续的敲击声 |  A series of rapid, forceful blows or knocks.  It can refer to a continuous and intense striking or hitting. |The rappage on the door startled the dog. 门上的猛烈敲打吓到了狗。 The constant rappage of the rain against the window kept me awake. 雨水持续不断地拍打窗户的声音让我睡不着。| 近义词：pounding, hammering, battering | 反义词：gentle tapping, soft knock
