rocketing:/ˈrɒkɪtɪŋ/ /ˈrɑːkɪtɪŋ/| v.|火箭般上升；迅速增长|The verb "rocketing" describes a rapid and dramatic increase or rise, often suggesting a sudden and significant upward trajectory, similar to the movement of a rocket.  It implies a fast and intense growth. |例句：House prices are rocketing in the city.  城市房价正在飙升。

The company's profits are rocketing this year.  公司今年的利润正在猛增。

|近义词：soaring, surging, skyrocketing, booming|反义词：plummeting, declining, falling, dropping|动词
