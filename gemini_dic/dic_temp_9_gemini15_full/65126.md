ethicalism:/ˈɛθɪkəlɪzəm/ | n. |伦理主义；伦理学说 |The philosophical study of ethics and moral principles; a system of ethical principles.  It often emphasizes the importance of moral rules and principles in guiding human behavior and social life.  Ethicalism can take various forms, depending on the specific moral principles it advocates.  |Example:  His actions are guided by a strict ethicalism that prioritizes honesty and fairness above all else. = 他的行为遵循着一种严格的伦理主义，将诚实和公平置于一切之上。


The rise of ethicalism in business has led to increased corporate social responsibility initiatives. = 商业中伦理主义的兴起导致了企业社会责任倡议的增加。|近义词：moralism, deontology, utilitarianism |反义词：amorality, nihilism |名词
