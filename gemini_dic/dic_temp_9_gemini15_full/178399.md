reconned:/ˌriːkənˈed/| v.|侦察，勘察|To reconnoiter; to make a reconnaissance of (a region, etc.).  It implies a careful and detailed examination to gain information.|例句：The soldiers reconned the enemy's position before the attack.  士兵们在进攻前侦察了敌人的阵地。
例句：The team reconned the area for potential hazards.  小组勘察了该区域是否存在潜在危险。|近义词：scout, explore, survey|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They recon the area regularly. = 他们定期侦察该地区。
We recon the terrain before each mission. = 我们每次任务前都会侦察地形。

一般过去时:
The scouts reconned the enemy's defenses. = 侦察兵侦察了敌人的防御工事。
They reconned the route carefully before proceeding. = 他们在继续前进之前仔细侦察了路线。

现在完成时:
We have reconned the entire valley. = 我们已经侦察了整个山谷。
They have reconned the area several times. = 他们已经多次侦察了该地区。

过去完成时:
The troops had reconned the bridge before crossing. = 部队在过桥之前已经侦察过桥梁了。
The team had reconned the route, ensuring its safety. = 小组已经侦察过路线，确保其安全。

将来时:
We will recon the area tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将侦察该地区。
They will recon the target before the strike. = 他们在袭击之前将侦察目标。

虚拟语气:
If we had reconned the area, we would have avoided the ambush. = 如果我们侦察了该地区，我们就不会遭遇伏击。
I wish we had reconned the route more thoroughly. = 我真希望我们更彻底地侦察过路线。

被动语态:
The area was reconned by the scouts. = 该地区由侦察兵进行了侦察。
The route was reconned before the convoy moved. = 车队出发前对路线进行了侦察。

疑问句:
Has the team reconned the building yet? = 小组已经侦察过那座建筑了吗？
Did they recon the area before the attack? = 他们在进攻之前侦察过该地区吗？

否定句:
They did not recon the enemy's position. = 他们没有侦察敌人的阵地。
We haven't reconned the area thoroughly enough. = 我们对该地区的侦察还不够彻底。
