contusion:/kənˈtʃuːʒən/| n. |挫伤，瘀伤 |A contusion is an injury to the body caused by a blow or impact, resulting in bleeding within the tissues but without a break in the skin.  It is characterized by swelling, discoloration (bruising), and pain. |例句：The fall resulted in a painful contusion on his leg.  摔倒导致他腿上出现一个疼痛的挫伤。  He suffered a severe contusion to his chest after the car accident. 车祸后，他的胸部受到了严重的挫伤。|近义词：bruise, hematoma |反义词：incision, laceration |名词
