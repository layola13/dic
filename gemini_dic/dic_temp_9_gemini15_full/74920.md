frets:/fretz/| n. v.| 忧虑；琴弦；（使）烦恼； |英文解析：A fret can refer to a feeling of worry or anxiety, or to one of the ridges on the fingerboard of a stringed instrument.  As a verb, it means to worry or to make someone worry. |例句：He frets about his upcoming exam. 他为即将到来的考试而担忧。 The guitarist carefully fretted the high notes. 吉他手仔细地按压高音琴弦。|近义词：worries, anxieties, concerns, chafes (for the ridges)|反义词：calmness, peace, tranquility|词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->

He frets about his job. = 他为他的工作而担忧。
She frets over her children. = 她为她的孩子们而担忧。


一般过去时:
I fretted about the presentation all day. = 我整天都在为演示而担忧。
They fretted over the lost dog. = 他们为丢失的狗而担忧。


现在完成时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I didn't fret about everything. = 我希望我不必为所有事情担忧。


被动语态:

She was fretted by the constant noise. = 她被持续的噪音弄得心烦意乱。


疑问句:

否定句:

She doesn't fret easily. = 她不容易担忧。
