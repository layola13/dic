guestrooms:/ˈɡɛstrʊmz/| n. |客房，宾客房间 |Guestrooms are rooms in a hotel, motel, or other lodging establishment that are rented out to guests.  They typically contain a bed, bathroom, and other amenities.|The hotel offers a variety of guestrooms, from standard rooms to suites.|近义词：rooms, accommodations, lodgings|反义词：private rooms (in a different context)
