midwinter:/ˈmɪdˌwɪntər/| n. |隆冬，仲冬 |The coldest part of winter, typically around the time of the winter solstice.  It often refers to a period of several weeks around the solstice.|例句：The shortest day of the year falls in midwinter.  一年中最短的一天落在隆冬时节。

例句：We experienced a severe blizzard in midwinter.  我们在隆冬时节遭遇了一场严重的暴风雪。|近义词：depth of winter, heart of winter |反义词：midsummer
