overextracting: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræktɪŋ/ | v. |过度开采，过度提取 | The act of extracting something to an excessive degree, often resulting in depletion or damage.  It implies taking more than is sustainable or responsible. |例句：Overextracting groundwater can lead to land subsidence and water shortages.  过度开采地下水会导致地表沉降和缺水。

The continuous overextracting of timber from the rainforest is causing irreversible damage to the ecosystem.  持续过度砍伐雨林木材正在对生态系统造成不可逆转的破坏。|近义词：overexploiting, depleting, exhausting, overusing |反义词：conserving, preserving, protecting, replenishing |动词

词源:
The word "overextracting" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) and the verb "extracting" (meaning to remove or obtain something).  "Extract" comes from the Latin word "extractus," the past participle of "ex" (out) and "trahere" (to draw or pull).
