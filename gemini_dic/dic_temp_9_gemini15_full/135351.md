nonperceptibility:/ˌnɒnpɜːsɛptɪˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可感知性，难以察觉 | The state or quality of being imperceptible; inability to be perceived by the senses or mind.  This refers to something that is extremely difficult or impossible to detect or sense.  | Example: The nonperceptibility of the changes in temperature made it difficult to notice the gradual shift in climate.  不可感知的温度变化使得很难注意到气候的逐渐转变。|近义词：imperceptibility, undetectability, inscrutability |反义词：perceptibility, detectability
