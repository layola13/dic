proglottis:/prɒˈɡlɒtɪs/| n. |节片，体节|A proglottis is one of the segments that make up the body of a tapeworm.  Each proglottis contains a complete set of reproductive organs. |例句：The tapeworm was segmented into numerous proglottides, each capable of reproduction.  这条绦虫分成许多节片，每一节都能繁殖。
|例句：Microscopic examination revealed numerous mature proglottids in the stool sample.  显微镜检查显示粪便样本中有很多成熟的节片。|近义词：segment, section|反义词：无|
