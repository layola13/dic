chucked:/tʃʌkt/ | vt. |抛，扔，丢弃；突然说出；解雇 | to throw something carelessly or casually; to suddenly say something; to dismiss someone from a job. |例句：He chucked the ball to his friend. 他把球扔给了他的朋友。 |例句：She chucked a few ideas into the meeting. 她在会议上随意提了一些想法。|近义词：threw, tossed, flung, dismissed |反义词：caught, retained, kept, hired


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chucks the ball every day. 他每天都扔球。
She chucks her old clothes away regularly. 她定期扔掉旧衣服。


一般过去时:
He chucked the rubbish in the bin. 他把垃圾扔进了垃圾桶。
They chucked the project because of budget issues. 因为预算问题，他们放弃了这个项目。


现在完成时:
I have chucked all my old books. 我已经把所有旧书都扔掉了。
She has chucked her job and started a new business. 她辞掉了工作，开始了一项新业务。


过去完成时:
He had chucked the letter before he realized it was important. 他在意识到信很重要之前就把信扔掉了。
They had chucked the idea before the meeting even began.  会议还没开始，他们就已经放弃了那个主意。


将来时:
I will chuck this broken chair away tomorrow. 我明天要把这把坏椅子扔掉。
She will chuck her old car once she buys a new one. 她买了一辆新车后就会把旧车扔掉。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would chuck this project. 如果我有更多时间，我会放弃这个项目。
I wish I had chucked that job sooner. 我真希望我早点辞掉那份工作。


被动语态:
The old furniture was chucked out last week. 旧家具上周被扔掉了。
The employee was chucked out for misconduct. 那名员工因行为不端被解雇。


疑问句:
Did he chuck the ball away? 他把球扔掉了吗？
Have they chucked the plan yet? 他们已经放弃计划了吗？


否定句:
He didn't chuck the letter. 他没有扔掉信。
They haven't chucked the idea yet. 他们还没有放弃那个主意。


PHRASE->
chuck something away: 扔掉某物
chuck it in: 辞职
chuck a wobbly: 发脾气
chuck someone out: 把某人赶出去
chuck something down: 狼吞虎咽
