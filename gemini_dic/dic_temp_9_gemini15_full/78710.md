gigantism:/ˈdʒaɪɡəntɪzəm/| n. |巨人症，巨大症 |A condition characterized by excessive growth of the body or its parts, often caused by excessive secretion of growth hormone.| 例句：His gigantism made him stand out from the crowd. 他的巨人症使他从人群中脱颖而出。  The doctor diagnosed him with gigantism after observing his abnormal height. 医生观察到他的身高异常后，诊断他患有巨人症。|近义词：hypertrophy, macroglossia (if referring to a specific body part)|反义词：dwarfism
