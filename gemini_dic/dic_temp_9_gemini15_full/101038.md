invectives:/ɪnˈvektɪvz/| n. |辱骂；恶言；痛斥 | Abusive or venomous speech; a vehement denunciation or criticism.  |例句：He unleashed a torrent of invectives against his political opponents. 他对他的政敌发泄了一连串的辱骂。|例句：The speaker's invectives were so harsh that many people walked out. 演讲者的辱骂太激烈了，以至于许多人都离开了。|近义词：abuse, diatribe, vituperation, tirade |反义词：praise, commendation, flattery |名词
