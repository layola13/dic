quasi-deliberate:/ˈkwɑːzi dɪˈlɪbərət/ | adj. |似是而非的，貌似故意的，有几分故意的 | Seemingly deliberate but perhaps not fully intentional; showing some degree of intentionality but lacking complete premeditation or careful consideration.  It implies an action that appears intentional but might be the result of carelessness, impulsiveness, or unconscious motivations. |例句：His quasi-deliberate insults were more hurtful than outright aggression.  他似是而非的侮辱比直接的攻击更伤人。

The accidental collision seemed quasi-deliberate given the driver's history of reckless driving.  考虑到司机有鲁莽驾驶的历史，这次意外碰撞看起来像是故意的。|近义词：unintentional, seeming deliberate, seemingly planned,  calculated (in a less strong sense)|反义词：deliberate, intentional, premeditated, accidental, unplanned
