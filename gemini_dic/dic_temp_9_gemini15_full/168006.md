prisoner:/ˈprɪzənər/| n. |囚犯，犯人 |A prisoner is a person who is kept in prison as a punishment for committing a crime, or while waiting to be tried in court.  |例句：The prisoner was sentenced to ten years in prison.  这个囚犯被判处十年监禁。

例句：Several prisoners escaped from the jail last night.  昨晚有几个囚犯越狱了。


近义词：captive, inmate, convict |反义词：jailer, warden, guard |词性：名词
