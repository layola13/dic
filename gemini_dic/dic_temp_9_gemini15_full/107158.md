laffer:/ˈlæfər/| n. |拉弗尔曲线；拉弗曲线|The Laffer curve is a representation of the relationship between tax rates and government revenue.  It suggests that there's an optimal tax rate that maximizes revenue, and that raising taxes beyond that point will actually decrease revenue because it discourages economic activity.|The Laffer curve shows that raising taxes too high can actually reduce government revenue.|近义词：tax revenue curve|反义词：无
