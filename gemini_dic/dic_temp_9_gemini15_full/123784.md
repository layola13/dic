mooting:/ˈmuːtɪŋ/| n. |辩论；讨论；模拟法庭辩论|The act of holding a moot court or engaging in a moot point discussion;  a simulated court proceeding used for educational purposes. |例句：The law students participated in a lively mooting session. 法学院学生参加了一场热烈的模拟法庭辩论。|近义词：debate, discussion, argument|反义词：decision, resolution


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are mooting the case in the courtroom. = 他们在法庭上模拟辩论这个案件。
The committee is mooting several new proposals. = 委员会正在讨论几个新的提案。


一般过去时:
We mooted the idea at the last meeting. = 我们在上一次会议上讨论了这个想法。
The lawyers mooted various legal strategies. = 律师们讨论了各种各样的法律策略。


现在完成时:
They have mooted this issue many times. = 他们已经多次讨论过这个问题了。
The team has mooted several possible solutions. = 团队已经提出了几个可能的解决方案。


过去完成时:
They had mooted the plan before the final decision was made. = 在最终决定做出之前，他们已经讨论过这个计划了。
The committee had mooted the proposal several times before rejecting it. = 委员会在否决提案之前已经讨论过几次了。


将来时:
We will be mooting the contract terms tomorrow. = 我们明天将要讨论合同条款。
The judges will moot the case next week. = 法官们下周将对这个案件进行模拟审判。


虚拟语气:
If we mooted the issue earlier, we might have avoided this problem. = 如果我们早点讨论这个问题，我们也许就能避免这个问题了。
I wish we had mooted the potential risks before starting the project. = 我希望我们在开始项目之前就讨论过潜在的风险。


被动语态:
The case was mooted by the students in the moot court. = 这个案子在模拟法庭由学生们进行了模拟审判。
Several solutions were mooted at the meeting. = 会议上提出了几个解决方案。


疑问句:
Are they mooting the new policy? = 他们正在讨论新的政策吗？
Was the contract mooted before signing? = 合同签署前是否进行了讨论？


否定句:
They didn't moot the possibility of failure. = 他们没有讨论失败的可能性。
We haven't mooted that option yet. = 我们还没有讨论那个选择。


PHRASE->

moot question 有争议的问题
to moot a question 提出一个问题
