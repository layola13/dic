contradicting:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktɪŋ/| adj.|相互矛盾的；抵触的 |  Describing something that opposes or is inconsistent with something else.  It often refers to statements, actions, or evidence that clash. |例句：Her account of the events was contradicting the witness's testimony. 她的事件描述与证人的证词相矛盾。

例句：The two studies presented contradicting findings on the effectiveness of the new drug. 这两项研究对这种新药的有效性提出了相互矛盾的发现。


近义词：inconsistent, conflicting, opposing, contrary | 反义词：consistent, agreeing, harmonious | 词性：形容词
