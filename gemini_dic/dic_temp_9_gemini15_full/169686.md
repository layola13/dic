proposition's: /ˌprɑːpəˈzɪʃənz/ | n. | 主张的；命题的 |  The possessive form of "proposition," referring to something belonging to a proposition.  It indicates ownership or relationship.  It is primarily used in grammatical contexts. | Example: The proposition's validity was questioned by the committee.  该命题的有效性受到了委员会的质疑。 | 近义词: claim's, assertion's, statement's | 反义词: N/A | 名词


USAGE->
This word is not used independently in various tenses like "What" because it's a possessive form.  Its usage depends entirely on the context of the proposition it modifies.  Examples illustrating its use in different sentence structures are shown below:

Simple Present:
The proposition's main point is clear. = 该命题的要点很清楚。
This proposition's impact is significant. = 这个命题的影响是重大的。


Simple Past:
The proposition's weakness was evident. = 该命题的弱点很明显。
Yesterday, the proposition's flaws were discussed. = 昨天讨论了该命题的缺陷。


Present Perfect:
The proposition's influence has been far-reaching. = 该命题的影响深远。
We have seen the proposition's success. = 我们已经看到了该命题的成功。


Past Perfect:
The proposition's implications had already been considered. = 该命题的含义已经被考虑过了。
Before the debate, the proposition's merits had been debated. = 在辩论之前，该命题的优点已经被争论过了。


Future Simple:
The proposition's acceptance will be crucial. = 该命题的接受将至关重要。
The proposition's effects will be felt soon. = 该命题的影响很快就会显现。


Conditional:
If the proposition's premise is true, then...= 如果该命题的前提是正确的，那么……
The proposition's success would depend on several factors. = 该命题的成功将取决于几个因素。


Passive Voice:
The proposition's details were carefully examined. = 该命题的细节被仔细检查了。
The proposition's significance is widely acknowledged. = 该命题的重要性得到了广泛认可。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "proposition's" as it's a possessive form and its use is highly contextual. The phrases would relate to the nature of the proposition itself, not the possessive form.  Examples below use "proposition" instead:

The core proposition of the argument. = 论证的核心命题。
A controversial proposition. = 一个有争议的命题。
To put forward a proposition. = 提出一个命题。
A sound proposition. = 一个合理的命题。
A weak proposition. = 一个薄弱的命题。
