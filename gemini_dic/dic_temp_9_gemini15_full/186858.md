ringmasters:/ˈrɪŋˌmɑːstərz/| n.| 马戏团的总管；领导者 | A ringmaster is the master of ceremonies at a circus, responsible for introducing acts and controlling the overall flow of the performance. They are skilled at commanding attention and interacting with the audience and performers.  | The ringmaster cracked his whip and announced the next act. 马戏团的总管甩了甩鞭子，宣布下一个节目。 The ringmaster kept the show running smoothly. 总管让演出顺利进行。|  master of ceremonies, impresario |  follower, subordinate | 名词
