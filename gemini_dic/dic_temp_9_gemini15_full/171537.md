pulicene: /ˈpjuːlɪsiːn/ | n. |倍半萜烯 | A sesquiterpene hydrocarbon found in certain plants, especially those of the genus *Pulicaria*. It has a characteristic odor and is sometimes used in perfumery.  | Example:  The essential oil of *Pulicaria dysenterica* contains a significant amount of pulicene.  倍半枯萎病植物精油含有大量的倍半萜烯。 |  近义词：sesquiterpene (更广泛的类别) | 反义词：无  |  名词
