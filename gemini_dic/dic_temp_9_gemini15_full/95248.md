incandescence:/ˌɪn.kænˈdɛs.əns/ | n. |白热，炽热；发光 |The emission of light by a body as a result of its high temperature.  It is the glowing of an object due to high temperature. Examples include the glowing of a lightbulb filament or the sun. |The incandescence of the sun is what makes it visible from Earth. 太阳的白热使它从地球上可见。 The blacksmith's forge glowed with a fierce incandescence. 铁匠的熔炉发出强烈的白热光。 |luminescence, radiance, glow |darkness, dullness |名词
