preinfering:/ˌpriːɪnˈfɜːrɪŋ/| v.|预先推断，预先判断|The act of making a judgment or prediction before having all the necessary information.  It often involves making assumptions based on limited evidence or prior knowledge.|例句：Preinfering the customer's needs based on their browsing history can improve marketing effectiveness.  基于客户的浏览历史预先推断他们的需求可以提高营销效率。

例句：The detective's preinfering of the suspect's guilt proved to be inaccurate.  侦探对嫌疑人有罪的预先判断被证明是不准确的。|近义词：predicting, anticipating, foreseeing, presuming|反义词： verifying, confirming
