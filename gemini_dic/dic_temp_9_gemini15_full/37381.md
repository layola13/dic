commentated:/ˈkɒmənˌteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 加了评论的；带有解说的 | Provided with a commentary; explained or interpreted.  | Example:  The commentator provided a detailed, commented version of the game. 解说员提供了比赛的详细解说版本。 |近义词：described, explained, annotated | 反义词：uncommented, unexplained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commentates on the news every evening. = 他每晚都评论新闻。
The book is well-commentated with insightful notes. = 这本书有很好的注释，充满了深刻的见解。


一般过去时:
She commentated on the political situation. = 她评论了政治局势。
They commentated the match live on television. = 他们在电视上对比赛进行了现场解说。


现在完成时:
He has commentated on many important events. = 他已经对许多重要事件进行了评论。
The professor has commentated extensively on this topic. = 教授对这个话题做了广泛的评论。


过去完成时:
The journalist had commentated on the scandal before the evidence emerged. = 在证据出现之前，记者就已经对这起丑闻进行了评论。
The historian had commentated extensively on the historical significance of the event.  = 历史学家已经广泛地评论了该事件的历史意义。


将来时:
She will commentate on the upcoming election. = 她将对即将到来的选举发表评论。
They will commentate the Olympic games live. = 他们将对奥运会进行现场解说。


虚拟语气:
If he commentated on the issue, it would create a lot of controversy. = 如果他评论这个问题，将会引起很大的争议。
I wish he had commentated more objectively on the situation. = 我希望他对形势的评论更客观一些。


被动语态:
The game was commentated by a well-known sportscaster. = 比赛由一位著名的体育播音员进行解说。
The article was heavily commentated upon in the media. = 这篇文章在媒体上受到了大量的评论。


疑问句:
Will he commentate on the debate tonight? = 他今晚会评论这场辩论吗？
Has anyone commentated on this before? = 之前有人评论过这个吗？


否定句:
He did not commentate on the controversial topic. = 他没有评论这个有争议的话题。
The event was not commentated live. = 这场活动没有现场解说。


PHRASE->
commentate on = 对...进行评论
commentate live = 现场解说
commentate extensively = 广泛评论
commentate objectively = 客观评论
offer commentary = 提供评论
