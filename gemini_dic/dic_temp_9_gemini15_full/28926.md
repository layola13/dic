carries:/ˈkæriz/ | vt. |携带；运送；支撑；意味；传播| To transport or convey something from one place to another; to support or bear the weight of something; to have as a characteristic or implication; to transmit or spread something.|例句：The river carries a lot of sediment. 这条河携带大量泥沙。|例句：The bridge carries a heavy load. 这座桥承受着巨大的重量。|近义词：conveys, transports, bears, supports |反义词：drops, abandons, relinquishes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carries a heavy backpack to school every day. 他每天背着沉重的书包去上学。
The company carries a wide range of products. 这家公司经营各种各样的产品。


一般过去时:
She carried the baby gently in her arms. 她轻轻地抱着婴儿。
The wind carried the seeds far away. 风把种子吹得很远。


现在完成时:

They have carried out extensive research on the topic. 他们已经对这个话题进行了广泛的研究。


过去完成时:

将来时:
