outquaffed:/ˌaʊtˈkwɑːft/| vt.|  胜过（尤指在喝酒方面）| To drink more alcohol than someone else.  This word implies a competitive drinking contest.|例句：He outquaffed his opponent in the drinking contest. 他在喝酒比赛中胜过了他的对手。
例句：  After a long night, she proudly announced that she had outquaffed everyone at the pub. 长夜过后，她自豪地宣布她在酒吧里把所有人都喝倒了。|近义词：outdrink, surpass (in drinking)|反义词：be outdrunk, lose (a drinking contest)|动词
