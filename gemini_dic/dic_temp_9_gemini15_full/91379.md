hydrosulphurous: /ˌhaɪdroʊsʌlˈfjʊərəs/ | adj. | 次硫酸的 | Relating to or denoting hydrosulfurous acid or its salts.  This adjective describes compounds containing the hydrosulfite ion (S2O42−).  It's often used in the context of reducing agents in chemical processes. | Example: Hydrosulphurous acid is a strong reducing agent. 次硫酸是一种强还原剂。The hydrosulphurous bleach is used to remove stains from fabrics. 次硫酸漂白剂用于去除织物上的污渍。| 近义词: thiosulfurous (less common)| 反义词: N/A
