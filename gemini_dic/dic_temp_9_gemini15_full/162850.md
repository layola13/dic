precation:/prɪˈkɔːʃən/ | n. |预防措施，警惕 | a measure taken in advance to prevent something from happening |  The doctor recommended taking precautions against the flu.  医生建议采取预防流感的措施。 |近义词：precaution, safeguard, preventative measure | 反义词：negligence, carelessness


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He takes precautions against fire. 他采取防火措施。
We take precautions to avoid accidents. 我们采取预防措施以避免事故。


一般过去时:
She took precautions to protect her valuables. 她采取措施保护她的贵重物品。
They took precautions before the hurricane. 他们在飓风来临之前采取了预防措施。


现在完成时:
We have taken all necessary precautions. 我们已经采取了所有必要的预防措施。
They have taken precautions to ensure safety. 他们已采取预防措施以确保安全。


过去完成时:
I had taken precautions against theft. 我已采取防盗措施。

将来时:
We will take precautions to prevent this from happening again. 我们将采取预防措施以防止这种情况再次发生。
They will take precautions against potential risks. 他们会采取措施防范潜在风险。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken more precautions, the accident wouldn't have happened. 如果我采取更多预防措施，事故就不会发生了。
I wish I had taken precautions against the rain. 我真希望我采取了防雨措施。


被动语态:

Precautions are being taken to prevent the spread of the disease. 正在采取预防措施以防止疾病蔓延。


疑问句:

否定句:
