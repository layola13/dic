remoistens:/ˌriːˈmɔɪstən/| v. |使再次湿润；使重新湿润|To make (something) moist again.  The verb "remoisten" implies a prior state of dryness followed by the restoration of moisture.|例句：The gardener remoistened the soil before planting the seeds. 园丁在播种前再次润湿了土壤。

例句：She remoistened the cake with a little syrup to keep it from drying out. 她用一点糖浆使蛋糕再次湿润，以防止它变干。


近义词：rehydrate, dampen, moisten |反义词：dry, dehydrate

词性:动词
