dethronize:/ˌdiːˈθroʊn/| vt.|废黜；使…下台|To remove from a position of power or authority.|例句：The revolution dethroned the king.  革命推翻了国王。|例句：The scandal dethroned the CEO.  丑闻导致首席执行官下台。|近义词：depose, oust, displace|反义词：enthrone, install


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dethrone corrupt leaders. = 他们废黜腐败的领导人。
The people dethrone the tyrant. = 人民推翻暴君。


一般过去时:
The rebels dethroned the emperor. = 反叛者废黜了皇帝。
The citizens dethroned the dictator. = 公民们推翻了独裁者。


现在完成时:
The people have dethroned the unjust king. = 人民已经废黜了不公正的国王。
They have dethroned the oppressive regime. = 他们已经推翻了压迫性政权。


过去完成时:
The revolution had dethroned the monarchy before the new government was formed. = 在新政府成立之前，革命已经推翻了君主制。
The people had dethroned the cruel ruler before peace returned. = 在和平回归之前，人民已经推翻了残酷的统治者。


将来时:
The opposition will dethrone the current president. = 反对派将推翻现任总统。
The people will dethrone the corrupt government. = 人民将推翻腐败的政府。


虚拟语气:
If the people had united, they would have dethroned the dictator. = 如果人民团结起来，他们就会推翻独裁者。
I wish they would dethrone the incompetent leader. = 我希望他们能罢黜无能的领导人。


被动语态:
The king was dethroned by the revolutionaries. = 国王被革命者废黜了。
The CEO was dethroned by the board of directors. = 首席执行官被董事会罢免了。


疑问句:
Will the opposition party dethrone the incumbent? = 反对党会推翻现任者吗？
Can the people dethrone the authoritarian regime? = 人民能够推翻专制政权吗？


否定句:
The rebels did not dethrone the king. = 反叛者没有废黜国王。
They failed to dethrone the dictator. = 他们未能推翻独裁者。
