endogenetic:/ˌɛn.doʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |内生的；内源性的 | Originating or produced from within a system, organism, or cell.  It refers to processes or factors that arise from within something rather than from external sources.  | Example: The researchers studied the endogenetic factors contributing to the disease. 研究人员研究了导致这种疾病的内源性因素。


Example:  The endogenetic processes of the earth create mountains and volcanoes. 地球的内生过程形成了山脉和火山。

近义词：intrinsic, internal, inherent

反义词：exogenous, extrinsic, external
