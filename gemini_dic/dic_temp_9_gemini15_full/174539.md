quintile:/ˈkwɪntaɪl/| n. |五分位数，五等分之一|A quintile is one of five equal groups into which a population can be divided according to the distribution of a particular variable.  For example, in terms of income, the top quintile represents the 20% of the population with the highest income.|Example: The top quintile of earners in the country receive 50% of the total income.  这个国家收入最高的五分之一的人口获得了总收入的50%。|近义词：percentile, fifth|反义词：N/A
