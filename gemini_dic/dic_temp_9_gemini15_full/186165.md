rheochord:/ˈriːoʊkɔːrd/| n.|变阻器，电流调节器|A rheochord is a variable resistor used to control the flow of electric current.  It's often used in older electrical equipment and laboratory settings.  The resistance can be adjusted smoothly to vary the current.|Example: The technician adjusted the rheochord to fine-tune the current flowing through the circuit. 技师调整了变阻器以微调流过电路的电流。|近义词：variable resistor, potentiometer|反义词：fixed resistor
