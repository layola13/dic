prevene:/prɪˈvɛn/|v.|预防，阻止|To prevent something from happening means to stop it from happening.|The doctor prescribed medication to prevent the spread of the infection.医生开了药以预防感染的蔓延。|近义词：avoid, hinder, avert |反义词：cause, promote, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They prevent accidents by enforcing safety rules. = 他们通过执行安全规则来预防事故。
We prevent diseases through vaccination. = 我们通过接种疫苗来预防疾病。


一般过去时:
The police prevented a riot from breaking out. = 警察阻止了一场暴乱的爆发。
He prevented me from leaving. = 他阻止我离开。


现在完成时:
She has prevented many problems by being careful. = 她通过小心谨慎避免了许多问题。
They have prevented the spread of the virus through strict quarantine measures. = 他们通过严格的检疫措施阻止了病毒的传播。


过去完成时:
He had prevented a similar incident from happening before. = 他之前已经阻止过类似事件的发生。
The safety regulations had prevented numerous accidents. = 安全条例已经预防了许多事故。


将来时:
This new policy will prevent future financial crises. = 这项新政策将防止未来的金融危机。
We hope this medicine will prevent the disease from returning. = 我们希望这种药能防止疾病复发。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have prevented the disaster. = 如果我们早点行动，就能阻止这场灾难。
I wish I had prevented him from making that mistake. = 我真希望我能阻止他犯那个错误。


被动语态:
The spread of the disease was prevented by swift action. = 疾病的传播被迅速的行动阻止了。
Accidents are prevented by following safety procedures. = 通过遵循安全规程来预防事故。


疑问句:
What can we do to prevent climate change? = 我们能做些什么来防止气候变化？
How can we prevent this from happening again? = 我们如何才能防止这种情况再次发生？


否定句:
They failed to prevent the fire from spreading. = 他们未能阻止火势蔓延。
His efforts did not prevent the company from going bankrupt. = 他的努力并没有阻止公司破产。


PHRASE->
prevent from = 阻止...
prevent against = 预防...
prevent the spread of = 防止...的传播
take preventive measures = 采取预防措施
preventive medicine = 预防医学
