calorie:/ˈkæləri/| n. |卡路里，卡|A unit of energy, equal to the amount of heat required to raise the temperature of one gram of water by one degree Celsius.  It is used to measure the energy content of food.|例句：This candy bar contains 200 calories. 这块糖果棒含有200卡路里。|例句：She tries to burn off excess calories by exercising. 她试图通过运动燃烧掉多余的卡路里。|近义词：kilocalorie|反义词：无
