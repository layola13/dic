lymphography:/lɪmfˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |淋巴造影术 |The process of taking x-ray pictures of the lymphatic system after a contrast medium has been injected.  It is used to detect and diagnose various lymphatic system diseases. |Example:  Lymphography revealed the presence of cancerous cells in his lymph nodes. 淋巴造影显示他的淋巴结中有癌细胞。  |近义词：lymphangiography |反义词：无
