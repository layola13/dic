loon:/luːn/ | n. |潜鸟；傻瓜；疯子 |A loon is a type of diving bird with a distinctive cry.  It can also refer to a foolish or crazy person. |例句：The loon's call echoed across the lake.  潜鸟的叫声在湖面上回荡。|例句：He's a loon; don't trust him. 他是个疯子；别相信他。|近义词：diver (bird), fool, idiot, lunatic |反义词：sage, wise person
