seeker:/ˈsiːkər/ | n. | 寻求者，探索者 | A person who is searching for something, especially a job or information.  | Example: He is a job seeker looking for a position in finance. 他是一个正在寻找金融职位的工作申请者。 |近义词：searcher, hunter, pursuer | 反义词：finder (in the context of already having found something)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The seeker searches for answers. = 寻求者寻找答案。
She seeks a better life. = 她寻求更好的生活。

一般过去时:
The seeker searched for clues. = 寻求者寻找线索。
He sought refuge in the mountains. = 他在山中寻求庇护。

现在完成时:
The seeker has searched many places. = 寻求者已经搜索过许多地方。
She has sought help from many sources. = 她已经从许多来源寻求帮助。

过去完成时:
The seeker had searched all night. = 寻求者已经搜索了一整夜。
He had sought advice before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经寻求过建议。

将来时:
The seeker will search the archives. = 寻求者将搜索档案。
She will seek justice for her family. = 她将为家人伸张正义。

虚拟语气:
If I were a seeker, I would persevere. = 如果我是寻求者，我会坚持下去。
I wish I had sought help sooner. = 我希望我早点寻求帮助。

被动语态:
The truth is being sought by the seeker. = 真相正在被寻求者寻找。
Justice was sought by the victims. = 受害者寻求正义。

疑问句:
Is he a truth seeker? = 他是一个追求真理的人吗？
What are you seeking? = 你在寻找什么？

否定句:
The seeker did not find what he was looking for. = 寻求者没有找到他想要的东西。
She doesn't seek fame or fortune. = 她不追求名利。
