He gave a quasi-heartily thumbs-up to the project. 他敷衍地竖起了大拇指表示赞同这个项目。


近义词：halfheartedly, unenthusiastically, perfunctorily, lukewarmly, insincerely

词性:形容词


词源:

* **quasi-**:  From Latin *quasi*, meaning "as if," "as it were," or "almost." This prefix indicates something that resembles or approximates something else but isn't truly that thing.

* **heartily**: From "heart" + "-ily," an adverbial suffix.  "Heartily" means with warmth, enthusiasm, and sincerity.
