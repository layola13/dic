gunflint:/ˈɡʌnflɪnt/| n.|火药枪的燧石|A hard, dark-colored flint used to strike sparks to ignite gunpowder in a firearm.  It's a crucial component in early firearms, creating the spark that ignited the gunpowder charge.|The old musket was carefully cleaned, its gunflint gleaming in the candlelight.  古老的火枪被仔细地擦拭干净，它的燧石在烛光下闪闪发光。|  The gunsmith replaced the worn-out gunflint.  枪匠更换了磨损的燧石。|flint, firestone|  (no direct antonym,  as it describes a specific type of flint)|名词
