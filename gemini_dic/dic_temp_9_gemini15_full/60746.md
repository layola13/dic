electrodeposition:/ɪˌlɛkˈtroʊdɛpəˈzɪʃən/| n. |电沉积|The process of depositing a metal or other substance onto a surface from an electrolyte solution using an electric current.  It's a widely used technique in various industries for coating, plating, and refining materials.| Example:  Electrodeposition is used to create protective coatings on metal parts. 电沉积用于在金属部件上创建保护涂层。  The company uses electrodeposition to create highly conductive copper films.  该公司使用电沉积来制造高导电性铜膜。| electroplating, electrowinning |  (no direct antonym, but opposite processes might include chemical deposition or mechanical coating)| 名词
