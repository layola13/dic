eurocentricity:/ˌjʊərəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/| n. |欧洲中心主义|The tendency to interpret the world primarily from a European perspective, often neglecting or undervaluing other cultures and perspectives.  It's a worldview that places Europe and European culture at the center of history and global affairs, often implicitly judging other cultures by European standards. |The book critiques the eurocentricity of traditional historical narratives. 这本书批判了传统历史叙事中的欧洲中心主义。|ethnocentrism, occidentalism|multiculturalism, global perspective|名词
