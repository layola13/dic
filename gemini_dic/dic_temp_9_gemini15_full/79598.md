glorification:/ˌɡlɔːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. | 赞美；颂扬；美化 | The act of glorifying or the state of being glorified; the attribution of glory or honor to someone or something.  It often implies exaggeration or excessive praise. |例句：The glorification of violence in video games is a concern for many parents.  电子游戏中对暴力的颂扬让许多父母担忧。

The glorification of war heroes often overlooks the human cost of conflict.  对战争英雄的赞美常常忽视了冲突中的人力代价。


近义词：exaltation, praise, commendation, aggrandizement, glorifying

反义词：denigration, condemnation, criticism, belittling, vilification


词源：

From Middle English *glorificacioun, from Old French glorificacion, from Late Latin glorificatio, from glorificare "to glorify," from gloria "glory" + -ficare (suffix forming verbs with the sense "to make").  The ultimate origin is from the Latin *gloriā, from *gloria, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to the Greek κλέος (kléos, "fame").
