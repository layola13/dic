grumpily:/ˈɡrʌmpɪli/| adv.|脾气暴躁地，不高兴地|In a grumpy manner; sullenly.  It describes an action performed in a bad-tempered or surly way.|例句：He grumpily refused to help. 他脾气暴躁地拒绝帮忙。
He answered grumpily, barely looking up. 他不高兴地回答，几乎没有抬头。|近义词：sullenly, sulkily, crabbedly, testily|反义词：cheerfully, happily, pleasantly

被动语态: (This adverb doesn't typically take a passive voice)  The task was completed, but grumpily so. 任务完成了，但完成得很不情愿。

疑问句: (This adverb doesn't form questions directly)  Did he answer grumpily? 他回答得脾气暴躁吗？

否定句:
He didn't grumpily answer the question; he answered politely. 他并没有不高兴地回答这个问题；他回答得很礼貌。
She didn't grumpily refuse; she simply said no. 她并没有不高兴地拒绝；她只是简单地说不。
