nasalizable: /ˈneɪzəlɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可鼻音化的 | Capable of being nasalized;  able to be pronounced with resonance in the nasal cavity.  This refers to sounds or vowels that can be modified by lowering the velum (soft palate) to allow air to flow through the nasal passages during their production. | The vowel in "man" is nasalizable, while the vowel in "mat" is not in most dialects of English.  The /æ/ sound in "cat" is not inherently nasalizable but can be in some languages or by speakers affected by certain speech disorders. |近义词: nasal, resonating | 反义词: oral
