dehumanize: /ˌdiːˈhjuːmənaɪz/ | vt. | 使没有人性；使失去人性；贬低人的价值 | To deprive of human qualities or attributes; to treat or regard as less than human.  |例句：The factory conditions dehumanized the workers. 工厂条件使工人失去了人性。  |例句：The propaganda campaign aimed to dehumanize the enemy. 宣传活动旨在贬低敌人的价值。|近义词：degrade, brutalize, depersonalize |反义词：humanize, empathize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dehumanize their opponents through hateful rhetoric. 他们通过仇恨言论来贬低对手。
The system dehumanizes individuals by reducing them to numbers. 这个系统通过将个人简化为数字来剥夺他们的人性。


一般过去时:
The regime dehumanized its citizens through systematic oppression. 该政权通过系统的压迫剥夺了其公民的人性。

过去完成时:
