skiver:/ˈskɪvər/| n. |逃学者，逃课者，偷懒的人| A person who avoids work or duty, especially by feigning illness.  |例句：He was a notorious skiver, always finding excuses to avoid his responsibilities. 他是个臭名昭著的逃学者，总是找借口逃避责任。

例句：The teacher suspected several students were skivers after noticing the unusually low attendance. 老师注意到出勤率异常低，怀疑有好几个学生在逃课。


近义词：idler, shirker, malingerer, slacker|反义词：hard worker, diligent worker|词性:noun
