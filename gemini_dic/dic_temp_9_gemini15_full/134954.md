nonnarrative: /ˌnɒnˈnærətɪv/ | adj. |非叙事的，非记叙的 | Not relating to or characterized by a narrative; lacking a story or plot.  It often describes texts or other forms of communication that don't tell a story in a traditional sense. |例句：The essay was nonnarrative, focusing instead on abstract concepts. 这篇文章是非叙事的，而是关注抽象概念。 |近义词：descriptive, expository, analytical |反义词：narrative, storytelling
