riffler:/ˈrɪflər/| n. |搓纹机；压花机；(木材) 刨花机|A riffler is a machine used to create a textured surface, often on wood or metal, by pressing or cutting.  It can also refer to a person who operates such a machine.|例句：The carpenter used a riffler to create a decorative pattern on the wooden panel.  木匠用搓纹机在木板上制作装饰图案。 The factory uses industrial riflers for creating textured surfaces on metal sheets. 工厂使用工业搓纹机在金属板上制作纹理表面。|近义词：embosser, texturizer |反义词：smoother
