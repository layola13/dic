non-submission:/nɒn.səbˈmɪʃən/| n. |未提交；未提交的事物|The failure to submit something that is required or expected.  This can refer to documents, assignments, applications, or other items.  The context is crucial in understanding the exact meaning.|例句：The professor penalized students for non-submission of their essays. 教授因为学生未提交论文而惩罚了他们。

The late non-submission of the project proposal resulted in a delay. 项目建议书的延迟未提交导致了延期。|近义词：failure to submit, omission, default|反义词：submission, compliance
