convoker:/kənˈvoʊkər/| n. |召集者，召唤者 |A convoker is a person or thing that summons or calls together others.  It often refers to a character in games who has the ability to summon creatures or allies. |Example:  The powerful mage acted as a convoker, summoning forth elemental spirits to aid in the battle.  强大的法师充当了召唤者的角色，召唤出元素精灵来帮助战斗。|近义词：summoner, invoker, caller|反义词：disperser, scatterer|名词
