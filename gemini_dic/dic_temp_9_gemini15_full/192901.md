seats:/siːts/ | n. | 座位，席位 | Seats are available in the front row. 前排还有座位。The number of seats in the auditorium is limited. 礼堂的座位数量有限。|近义词：chairs, places, positions |反义词：standing room


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The theatre has many seats. = 剧院有很多座位。
These seats offer a good view of the stage. = 这些座位能很好地观看舞台。

一般过去时:
We took our seats before the concert began. = 音乐会开始前我们入座了。
He reserved two seats for the show. = 他为演出预订了两张票。

现在完成时:
They have occupied all the seats. = 他们已经占了所有的座位。
She has already booked her seats for the flight. = 她已经预订好了她的机票座位。

过去完成时:
We had already taken our seats when the play started. = 话剧开始时我们已经就座了。
They had reserved the best seats in the house. = 他们预订了最好的座位。

将来时:
We will have our seats by the window. = 我们将坐在靠窗的座位。
They will need more seats for the extra guests. = 他们需要为额外的客人准备更多座位。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would buy better seats. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买更好的座位。
I wish I had booked my seats earlier. = 我希望我早点预订座位。

被动语态:
The seats were quickly filled. = 座位很快就被坐满了。
The seats are being cleaned. = 座位正在被清洁。

疑问句:

How many seats are there in the bus? = 公共汽车上有多少个座位？

否定句:

He didn't get the seats he wanted. = 他没有得到他想要的座位。
