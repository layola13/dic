shuns:/ʃʌnz/| v.|躲避；避免；摒弃|To avoid or reject (something considered undesirable or unpleasant).|He shuns the limelight. 他躲避公众的注意。|近义词：avoids, evades, eschews|反义词：embraces, welcomes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuns social gatherings. = 他避免参加社交聚会。
She shuns unhealthy foods. = 她避免吃不健康的食物。

一般过去时:
He shunned responsibility. = 他逃避责任。
They shunned the dangerous path. = 他们避开了危险的道路。

现在完成时:
He has shunned his family for years. = 他多年来一直躲避他的家人。
She has shunned all contact with him. = 她一直避免和他联系。

过去完成时:
He had shunned his friends before the incident. = 在事件发生之前，他已经疏远了他的朋友们。
They had shunned the offer because of the risk. = 他们因为风险而拒绝了这个提议。

将来时:
He will shun any further involvement. = 他将避免进一步的参与。
She will shun the temptation. = 她将避免诱惑。

虚拟语气:
If he knew the consequences, he would have shunned it. = 如果他知道后果，他就会避免它。
I wish I had shunned that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时避开了那个机会。

被动语态:
His advice was shunned by many. = 他的建议被许多人所忽视。
The proposal was shunned due to its high cost. = 由于成本高昂，该提案被否决了。

疑问句:
Does he shun responsibility? = 他逃避责任吗？
Why do they shun that place? = 为什么他们避开那个地方？

否定句:
He doesn't shun hard work. = 他不逃避努力工作。
She doesn't shun challenges. = 她不回避挑战。
