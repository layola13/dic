patronizers:/ˈpeɪtrənaɪzərz/| n.|施舍者；以恩人自居者；傲慢的顾客|People who act superior or condescending towards others, often while providing assistance or support.  They treat others as if they are inferior or less knowledgeable.|例句：Her patronizing tone made me uncomfortable.  她的居高临下的语气让我感到不舒服。


例句：He offered help in a patronizing manner, implying I was incapable of managing on my own. 他以一种施舍的姿态提供帮助，暗示我无法独立处理。


近义词：condescenders, protectors, benefactors


反义词：equals, peers, respectors


词性: 名词
