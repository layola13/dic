rimey:/ˈraɪmi/| adj.|多霜的，潮湿的，有霜的|Covered with or resembling rime; frosty.  The word evokes a sense of dampness and coldness associated with frost or ice crystals.  |例句：The rime-covered branches looked beautiful in the morning sun.  早晨阳光下，覆盖着霜的树枝看起来很漂亮。

The fields were rime-covered after the cold night. 冷夜过后，田野覆盖着霜。


近义词：frosty, icy, frosty, damp,  chilly|反义词：dry, warm, arid
