motorwagen:/ˈmoʊtərˌwæɡən/|/ˈməʊtəˌwæɡən/| n. |机动车，汽车(早期) |A motorwagen is an early form of automobile, typically powered by an internal combustion engine.  It is a less common term now, largely replaced by "automobile" or "car".|例句：The inventor proudly showed off his new motorwagen.  发明家自豪地展示了他的新机动车。

例句：Early motorwagens were often unreliable and prone to breakdowns. 早期的机动车常常不可靠，容易发生故障。


近义词：automobile, car, auto


反义词：无


词性：名词
