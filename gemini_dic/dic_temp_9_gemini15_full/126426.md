mystic: /ˈmɪstɪk/ | adj., n. |神秘的；神秘主义者；神秘事物 |

英文解析:  Relating to or characteristic of mystics or mysticism; mysterious, magical, or supernatural.  As a noun, it refers to a person who seeks direct communion with God or ultimate reality through prayer, contemplation, and self-discipline.

例句:

The mystic experience left her feeling profoundly changed.  神秘的经历让她感觉发生了深刻的变化。

He was a mystic who lived a life of seclusion and meditation. 他是一位过着隐居和冥想生活的神秘主义者。


近义词: enigmatic, occult, arcane, supernatural, spiritualist

反义词: mundane, ordinary, commonplace, rational, clear


词性: adjective, noun
