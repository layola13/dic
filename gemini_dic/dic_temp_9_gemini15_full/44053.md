cringeworthy: /ˈkrɪndʒˌwɜːrði/ | adj. | 令人尴尬的，令人不适的 |  Something cringeworthy causes an involuntary shrinking or flinching, typically from embarrassment or awkwardness. It describes something so awkward, embarrassing, or tasteless that it makes you want to cringe. |


例句：

His attempt at a joke was cringeworthy.  他的笑话很尴尬。

That movie was so cringeworthy, I had to turn it off. 那部电影太尴尬了，我不得不关掉它。


近义词：embarrassing, awkward, uncomfortable, shameful, distasteful

反义词：enjoyable, pleasant, comfortable, delightful
