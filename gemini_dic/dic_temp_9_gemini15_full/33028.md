chorused:/ˈkɔːrəst/| v.|齐声说，合唱|To say or shout something at the same time; to sing in a chorus.|例句：The crowd chorused their approval.  人群齐声表示赞同。
The choir chorused a beautiful hymn. 唱诗班合唱了一首优美的圣歌。|近义词：chant, sing together, shout in unison|反义词：whisper individually, speak separately|动词
