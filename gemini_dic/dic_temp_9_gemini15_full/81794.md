grenadier:/ˌɡrænəˈdɪər/| n. |榴弹兵，掷弹兵|A soldier who throws grenades.  Grenadiers were originally soldiers who specialized in throwing grenades in battle.  They were often elite troops, known for their size and strength.|例句：The grenadier regiment charged across the field, throwing grenades with deadly accuracy. = 榴弹兵团冲过田野，以致命的精准度投掷手榴弹。

The grenadiers formed a strong defensive line. = 掷弹兵们组成了一条坚固的防线。|近义词：soldier, infantryman |反义词：civilian
