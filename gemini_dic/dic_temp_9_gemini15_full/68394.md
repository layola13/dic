falls:/fɔːlz/ /fɔːlz/| n. vi.|瀑布；跌落；下降；（疾病的）发作|The word "falls" can function as a noun or a verb. As a noun, it typically refers to a cascading body of water, such as a waterfall.  As a verb, it describes the action of something dropping or descending. It can also refer to the onset of an illness or a decline in something.|例句：Niagara Falls is a spectacular natural wonder. 尼亚加拉大瀑布是一个壮丽的自然奇观。|The leaves fell from the trees in autumn. 秋天树叶从树上落下。|cascades, cataracts, drops|rises, ascents, climbs|n.,vi.

一般现在时:
Water falls from the sky. = 水从天上落下。
The price of oil falls. = 油价下跌。


一般过去时:
He fell down the stairs. = 他从楼梯上摔下来了。
The Roman Empire fell. = 罗马帝国灭亡了。


现在完成时:
The leaves have fallen. = 叶子已经落了。
The temperature has fallen significantly. = 气温已经大幅下降。


过去完成时:
The river had fallen before the rain came. = 雨来之前河水已经下降了。
She had fallen ill before the trip. = 旅行之前她就病倒了。


将来时:
The snow will fall tonight. = 今晚会下雪。
The stock prices will fall tomorrow. = 明天的股价将会下跌。


虚拟语气:
If it fell, it would make a loud noise. = 如果它掉下来，会发出很大的声响。
I wish the rain would fall soon. = 我希望雨快点下下来。


被动语态:
The tree was felled by the storm. (Note: "felled" is the past participle of "fell") = 这棵树被暴风雨刮倒了。
The plan was fallen through. = 计划失败了。


疑问句:
Did he fall? = 他摔倒了吗？
When will the rain fall? = 雨什么时候下？


否定句:
The rain did not fall yesterday. = 昨天没有下雨。
The apple didn't fall far from the tree. = 苹果没有离树远。
