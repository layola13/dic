footer:/ˈfʊtər/ | n. |页脚，脚注|A footer is a section of text at the bottom of a page, often containing information such as page numbers, dates, or website addresses.  It's a common element in printed documents and web pages.| Example: The footer of the website displayed the copyright information.  网站页脚显示了版权信息。|近义词：foot note, bottom note|反义词：header, heading
