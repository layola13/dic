inspected:/ɪnˈspɛktɪd/| v.|检查；视察；检阅|To examine something carefully to check its condition or quality.|例句：The engineer inspected the bridge for damage.工程师检查了桥梁是否有损坏。|例句：The teacher inspected the students' homework.老师检查了学生的作业。|近义词：examined, surveyed, reviewed|反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inspector inspects the building regularly. = 检查员定期检查大楼。
She inspects the quality of the products before shipping. = 她发货前检查产品的质量。


一般过去时:
The mechanic inspected the car engine. = 机械师检查了汽车发动机。
They inspected the house carefully before buying it. = 他们买房前仔细检查了房子。


现在完成时:
The team has inspected all the equipment. = 团队已经检查了所有设备。
She has inspected the documents thoroughly. = 她已经彻底检查了文件。


过去完成时:
The police had inspected the scene before the paramedics arrived. = 验尸官到达前，警察已经检查了现场。
He had inspected the goods twice before deciding to buy. = 他在决定购买之前检查了两次货物。


将来时:
The inspector will inspect the factory next week. = 检查员下周将检查工厂。
We will inspect the results of the experiment tomorrow. = 我们明天将检查实验结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had inspected the car more carefully, I would have noticed the problem. = 如果我更仔细地检查汽车，我就会注意到这个问题。
I wish I had inspected the house before signing the contract. = 我真希望在签合同之前检查过房子。


被动语态:
The building is inspected annually. = 这座建筑每年都会被检查。
The products are inspected for defects before sale. = 产品在销售前都会被检查是否有缺陷。


疑问句:
Has the building been inspected yet? = 这座建筑检查过了吗？
Will the goods be inspected before shipment? = 货物在装运前会被检查吗？


否定句:
They didn't inspect the equipment properly. = 他们没有正确检查设备。
The manager didn't inspect the report. = 经理没有检查报告。
