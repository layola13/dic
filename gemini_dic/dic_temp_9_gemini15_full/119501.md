microphoning:/ˈmaɪkrəfoʊnɪŋ/| n. |麦克风录音；使用麦克风 |The act of recording sound using a microphone.  It can also refer to the process of amplifying sound with a microphone. |例句：The band's microphoning was excellent, capturing every nuance of their performance. 乐队的麦克风录音效果极佳，捕捉到了他们表演的每一个细微之处。

例句：The poor microphoning during the speech made it difficult to understand the speaker. 演讲过程中麦克风效果差，使得听众难以理解演讲者。|近义词：recording, amplification |反义词：silencing
