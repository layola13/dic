equipping:/ɪˈkwɪpɪŋ/| v. |装备；配备；使有能力|The act of providing someone or something with the necessary equipment, tools, or resources.  It can also refer to the process of gaining the skills or knowledge needed for a particular task or situation.|例句：The army is equipping its soldiers with new weapons.  军队正在为士兵们配备新武器。

例句：He's equipping himself with the skills necessary for a career in finance. 他正在学习从事金融行业所需的技能。


近义词：furnishing, supplying, providing, preparing


反义词：disarming, stripping, disabling


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are equipping the laboratory with new instruments. = 他们正在为实验室配备新的仪器。
The company is equipping its employees with the latest software. = 公司正在为员工配备最新的软件。


一般过去时:
The soldiers equipped themselves for battle. = 士兵们为战斗做好了准备。
She equipped her backpack with essential supplies. = 她为背包准备了必需的用品。


现在完成时:
They have equipped the hospital with state-of-the-art technology. = 他们已经为医院配备了最先进的技术。
We have equipped ourselves with the knowledge to tackle this challenge. = 我们已经掌握了应对这一挑战的知识。


过去完成时:
The team had equipped the research facility before the experiment began. = 团队在实验开始前已经为研究设施做好了准备。
She had equipped her car with winter tires before the snowstorm. = 在暴风雪来临之前，她已经为汽车配备了冬季轮胎。


将来时:
They will equip the new school with interactive whiteboards. = 他们将为新学校配备互动式白板。
The company will equip its workforce with new training programs. = 公司将为员工配备新的培训项目。


虚拟语气:
If they equipped the factory with better machinery, production would increase. = 如果他们为工厂配备更好的机器，产量就会增加。
I wish I had equipped myself better for that interview. = 我真希望我当时为面试做了更好的准备。


被动语态:
The students were equipped with textbooks and notebooks. = 学生们配备了教科书和笔记本。
The building is being equipped with fire alarms. = 该建筑正在安装火灾警报器。


疑问句:
Are they equipping the classrooms with computers? = 他们正在为教室配备电脑吗？
Is the new factory being equipped with robots? = 新工厂正在配备机器人吗？


否定句:
They did not equip the expedition with enough supplies. = 他们没有为探险队配备足够的物资。
The school was not equipped to handle the influx of new students. = 学校没有能力应付大量涌入的新生。


PHRASE->
equip oneself with=  使自己具备...

poorly equipped= 设备简陋的
