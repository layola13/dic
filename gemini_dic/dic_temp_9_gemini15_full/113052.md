lumpectomy:/ˈlʌmpɛktoʊmi/| n. |肿块切除术|The surgical removal of a lump or tumor, typically from the breast.  This procedure is often used in the treatment of breast cancer, removing only the cancerous tissue and leaving surrounding healthy tissue intact. |例句：The doctor recommended a lumpectomy to remove the suspicious lump in her breast. 医生建议她做肿块切除术以去除乳房中可疑的肿块。

例句：After a successful lumpectomy, she underwent radiation therapy.  肿块切除术成功后，她接受了放射治疗。


近义词：tumor excision, local excision|反义词：mastectomy
