micrococcus:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈkɒkəs/| n. |微球菌属|A genus of spherical bacteria, typically found in pairs or clusters, often non-pathogenic but some species can cause opportunistic infections.  Micrococci are Gram-positive, aerobic or facultatively anaerobic, and generally catalase-positive.|Example:  Several species of *Micrococcus* are commonly found on human skin.  几种微球菌常见于人体皮肤上。|近义词: Staphylococcus (but distinct genus)|反义词: N/A (no direct antonym)|名词
