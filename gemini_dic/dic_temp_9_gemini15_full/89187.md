homepage:/ˈhoʊmpeɪdʒ/ | n. |主页，首页 |The homepage is the first page a user sees when they visit a website. 主页是用户访问网站时看到的第一个页面。|website, landing page, front page |  | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The homepage displays our company's mission statement. = 主页显示了我们公司的使命宣言。
Our homepage features a slideshow of our products. = 我们的主页特色是产品幻灯片展示。


一般过去时:
The homepage looked different last year. = 主页去年看起来不一样。
They redesigned the homepage last month. = 他们上个月重新设计了主页。


现在完成时:
They have updated the homepage several times this year. = 他们今年已经更新了主页几次。
We have added a new contact form to the homepage. = 我们在主页上添加了一个新的联系表单。


过去完成时:
Before the redesign, the homepage had been cluttered and confusing. = 在重新设计之前，主页杂乱无章且令人困惑。
They had already updated the homepage before the marketing campaign launched. = 在营销活动启动之前，他们已经更新了主页。


将来时:
We will launch a new homepage next week. = 我们下周将推出一个新的主页。
The homepage will be more user-friendly after the update. = 更新后，主页将更方便用户使用。


虚拟语气:
If the homepage were more attractive, we would attract more customers. = 如果主页更吸引人，我们会吸引更多客户。
I wish the homepage loaded faster. = 我希望主页加载速度更快。


被动语态:
The homepage is being redesigned by a professional team. = 主页正在由一个专业团队重新设计。
The homepage was updated yesterday. = 主页昨天更新了。

否定句:
