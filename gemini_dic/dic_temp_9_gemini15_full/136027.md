nonrealization: /ˌnɒnˌriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 未实现；未实现化 | The failure to achieve or accomplish something.  It often refers to the lack of manifestation or actualization of something expected or intended.  | Example: The nonrealization of his dreams left him feeling deeply disappointed.  他的梦想未能实现让他深感失望。 |近义词：failure, nonfulfillment, unrealization | 反义词：realization, achievement, fulfillment |

词源:  The word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "realization" (the act of realizing or making real).  "Realization" itself comes from the verb "realize," which derives from the Old French "realiser" and ultimately from the Latin "realis" (real).
