decimalize:/ˈdesɪməlaɪz/ | vt. | 将…改用十进制；实行十进制 | To convert (a system of weights, measures, or currency) to a decimal system.  This involves expressing values using base-10.  The process often aims for simplification and standardization. | Example: The government decided to decimalize the currency to make calculations easier. 政府决定将货币改用十进制以简化计算。 |近义词：convert to decimal,  express in decimal form | 反义词：None  | 动词
