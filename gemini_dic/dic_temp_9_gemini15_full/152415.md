pavon:/ˈpeɪvɒn/| n. |雄孔雀；炫耀；夸耀|A pavon is a peacock, specifically a male peacock, known for its iridescent plumage.  The word can also refer to something showy or boastful.  |例句：The pavon strutted proudly, displaying its magnificent tail.  雄孔雀骄傲地昂首阔步，展示着它华丽的尾巴。

The pavon of his achievements was rather tiresome. 他对成就的夸耀相当令人厌烦。|近义词：peacock, showoff, braggart|反义词：humble, modest|名词
