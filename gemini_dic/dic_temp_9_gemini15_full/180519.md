regerminate:/ˌriːˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/| v.| 再发芽；重新萌芽 |To germinate again; to sprout anew.  The term implies a previous germination event followed by a period of dormancy or interruption, and subsequent renewed growth.| Example: The seeds regerminated after a period of unexpected rainfall.  种子在意外降雨后再次发芽。 The plant, after a harsh winter, regerminated in the spring.  这种植物在严冬之后在春天重新发芽。|近义词：resprout, resprout, re-sprout|反义词：die, wither
