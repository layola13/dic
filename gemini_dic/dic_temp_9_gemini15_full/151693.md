partly:/ˈpɑːrtli/ | /ˈpɑːrtli/ | adv. | 部分地；在某种程度上 |  Partially; to some extent.  It indicates that something is only true or applies to a certain degree, not completely. | 例句：The project was partly funded by the government. = 这个项目部分由政府资助。

例句：The success was partly due to her hard work. = 成功部分归功于她的努力工作。


近义词：partially, somewhat, in part, to some extent | 反义词：completely, entirely, wholly, fully | 词性:副词
