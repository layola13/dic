glancer:/ˈɡlænsər/| n. |匆匆一瞥，闪光 | A quick look.  |例句：He took a quick glance at the newspaper. 他匆匆瞥了一眼报纸。|近义词：glance, peep, peek |反义词：stare, gaze |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glances at his watch. = 他看了一眼手表。
She glances around the room. = 她环顾了一下房间。


一般过去时:
He glanced at the menu. = 他瞥了一眼菜单。
She glanced nervously at the door. = 她紧张地看了一眼门。


现在完成时:
He has glanced at the email, but hasn't read it yet. = 他瞥了一眼邮件，但还没读。
She has glanced through the report several times. = 她已经看过几次报告了。


过去完成时:
He had glanced at the clock before leaving. = 他离开前看了一眼钟。
She had glanced at the letter several times before opening it. = 在打开信之前，她已经瞥过几次了。


将来时:
He will glance at the map before starting his journey. = 他出发前会看一下地图。
She will glance over the document again tomorrow. = 她明天会再浏览一下文件。


虚拟语气:
If he glanced at the instructions, he wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他看了一眼说明书，他就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish she had glanced at my notes before the presentation. = 我希望她在演示文稿之前看过我的笔记。


被动语态:
The picture was glanced at by many people. = 那张照片被很多人匆匆瞥了一眼。
The document is being glanced at by the manager. = 经理正在浏览那份文件。


疑问句:
Did he glance at the letter? = 他看了一眼那封信吗？
Has she glanced at the report yet? = 她看过报告了吗？


否定句:
He didn't glance at the price tag. = 他没有看价签。
She didn't glance at him during the meeting. = 会议期间她没有看他。


PHRASE->
a fleeting glance: 匆匆一瞥
a furtive glance: 偷偷的一瞥
a sideways glance: 斜眼看
at a glance: 一眼就能看出
to glance over something: 匆匆浏览某物
