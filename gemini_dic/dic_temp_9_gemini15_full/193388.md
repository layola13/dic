seines:/seɪnz/| n.|拖网；(复数)|渔网，特指一种大而长的渔网|A seine is a large fishing net that hangs vertically in the water.  The net is drawn in to trap fish.|例句：The fishermen cast their seines into the deep water. 渔民们把他们的拖网撒向深水。

例句：They hauled in the seines, filled with glistening fish. 他们收起了拖网，网里装满了闪闪发光的鱼。


近义词：dragnet, trawl net|反义词：None|词性:名词
