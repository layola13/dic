computer:/kəmˈpjuːtər/| n. |计算机，电脑 |An electronic device for storing and processing data, typically in binary form, according to instructions given to it in a variable program.  Computers are used for a wide range of tasks, from simple calculations to complex simulations. |This computer is very powerful and can run many programs simultaneously. 这台电脑非常强大，可以同时运行多个程序。

My new computer has a large hard drive. 我的新电脑有一个很大的硬盘。
