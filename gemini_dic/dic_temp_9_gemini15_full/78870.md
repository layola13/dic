gingkos:/ˈɡɪŋkoʊz/| n.|银杏树（复数）|Ginkgos are deciduous trees native to China.  They are known for their fan-shaped leaves and distinctive fruit.|例句：The gingkos in the park turned a beautiful golden yellow in autumn. 公园里的银杏树在秋天变成了美丽的金色。|例句：Many gingkos were planted along the streets to beautify the city.为了美化城市，许多银杏树被种在了街道两旁。|近义词：ginkgo trees|反义词:无
