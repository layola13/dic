immodest:/ɪˈmɑːdɪst/| adj.| 不谦虚的；不适度的；无耻的 |  Lacking modesty; excessively bold or forward.  It can refer to behavior, dress, or claims. |例句：Her immodest dress drew unwanted attention.她的穿着暴露，引来了不必要的注意。

例句：His immodest claims about his achievements were quickly refuted. 他关于自己成就的大胆说法很快就被驳斥了。


近义词：shameless, brazen, indecent, immodest, unblushing, forward, presumptuous, vain

反义词：modest, humble, demure, reserved, unassuming


词源:

* **im-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **modest**:  from Latin *modestus*, meaning "moderate," "temperate," or "self-controlled."  This ultimately derives from the Latin *modus*, meaning "measure" or "manner."
