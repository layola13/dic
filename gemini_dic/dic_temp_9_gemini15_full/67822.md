eyewitness: /ˈaɪwɪtnəs/ | n. | 目击证人 | A person who has seen something happen and can describe it to other people.  | 例句：The police interviewed several eyewitnesses to the accident.  警察采访了几位事故目击证人。

The eyewitness testimony was crucial to the case. 目击证词对案件至关重要。 | 近义词：observer, spectator, witness | 反义词：N/A
