gyri:/ˈdʒaɪri/| n.|脑回，回旋|Elevated ridges on the surface of the brain, separated by sulci (grooves).  They are crucial for brain function, involved in higher cognitive processes.|Example: The gyri and sulci of the cerebral cortex give the brain its characteristic wrinkled appearance.  脑皮质的脑回和脑沟赋予大脑其特征性的皱纹外观。|近义词：convolution, gyrus (singular)|反义词：sulcus (singular), sulci (plural)
