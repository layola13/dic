reverberatory: /rɪˈvɜːrbəreɪtəri/ | adj. | 反射的；回响的；回荡的 | Relating to or causing reverberation; characterized by or producing reverberation.  It describes something that reflects sound or light, creating echoes or prolonging the effect. | The reverberatory furnace reflected the heat, making the room intensely hot.  回响炉反射热量，使房间非常热。| resonant, echoing, resounding | muted, silent |
