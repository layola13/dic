/ˌɪntərˌdɪˌnɑːmɪneɪʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |  超教派主义；跨宗派合作 | The belief in and practice of cooperation among different Christian denominations.  It emphasizes unity despite doctrinal differences.  Interdenominationalism often involves joint projects, shared resources, and collaborative efforts to address common goals. | The interdenominational church hosted a community Thanksgiving service.  = 这间超教派教堂举办了一场社区感恩节仪式。 The growing interdenominationalism in the region reflects a shared commitment to social justice. = 该地区日益增长的超教派合作反映了对社会正义的共同承诺。 | denominationalism, ecumenism | denominationalism, sectarianism
