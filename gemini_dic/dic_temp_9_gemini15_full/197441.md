sheepberry: /ˈʃiːpˌbɛri/ | n. |羊莓，山茱萸属植物的浆果 | A small, usually dark-colored berry produced by various plants, especially those in the Viburnum genus.  They are often eaten by sheep, hence the name. |例句：The sheep were grazing peacefully, enjoying the abundance of sheepberries in the field.  羊群在田野里平静地吃草，享受着丰富的羊莓。


The children collected a handful of juicy sheepberries for a snack. 孩子们收集了一把多汁的羊莓作为零食。


近义词：viburnum berry, cranberry (depending on the species) |反义词：无


词源:The name "sheepberry" is a descriptive name, combining "sheep" (referring to the animals that often consume the berries) and "berry" (referring to the fruit's type). The etymology is straightforward and reflects the common observation of sheep feeding on these berries.
