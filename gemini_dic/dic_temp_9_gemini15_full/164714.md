prefabbing:/ˈpriːfæbɪŋ/| n. v.|预制；预制构件的制作|The process of manufacturing components of a structure, such as buildings or other large-scale projects, off-site in a factory or workshop before transporting them to the final location for assembly.  This method often reduces construction time and costs. |例句：The rapid prefabbing of housing units allowed for the quick resettlement of the disaster victims.  快速预制房屋单元使得灾民能够迅速安置。

The company specializes in prefabbing modular classrooms for schools. 这家公司专门为学校预制模块化教室。|近义词：prefabrication, preassembly|反义词：on-site construction, traditional construction
