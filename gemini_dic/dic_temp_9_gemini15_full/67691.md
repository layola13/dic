exuded:/ɪɡˈzuːd/| vt.|渗出；散发；流露|To discharge or emit gradually; ooze.  It also implies a slow, often unconscious process of releasing something.|例句：The flower exuded a sweet fragrance. 这朵花散发着甜美的香味。

例句：His confidence exuded from him as he walked into the room. 他走进房间时，自信从他身上散发出来。


近义词：emit, ooze, secrete, radiate, emanate

反义词：absorb, retain, withhold

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tree exudes sap. = 这棵树渗出树液。
He exudes confidence. = 他散发着自信。


一般过去时:
The wound exuded pus. = 伤口流出脓液。
She exuded happiness. = 她流露出快乐。


现在完成时:
The plant has exuded a sticky substance. = 植物已经渗出一种粘性物质。
He has exuded charm all evening. = 他整个晚上都散发着魅力。


过去完成时:
The jar had exuded its contents. = 罐子已经渗出了里面的东西。
Before the interview, she had exuded nervousness. = 面试前，她表现得很紧张。


将来时:
The volcano will exude lava. = 火山将喷出熔岩。
He will exude charisma on stage. = 他将在舞台上散发魅力。


虚拟语气:
If the wound exuded more pus, we would need to treat it differently. = 如果伤口流出更多脓液，我们就需要采取不同的治疗方法。
If he exuded more confidence, he would get the job. = 如果他更有自信，他就能得到这份工作。


被动语态:
A sweet scent was exuded by the roses. = 玫瑰花散发着甜美的香味。
Confidence was exuded by the successful entrepreneur. = 成功的企业家散发着自信。


疑问句:
Does this plant exude any toxins? = 这棵植物会散发任何毒素吗？
What emotions did he exude during the speech? = 他在演讲中流露出了什么情绪？


否定句:
The wound did not exude any blood. = 伤口没有流血。
He didn't exude any hostility towards his rivals. = 他对他的对手没有表现出任何敌意。


PHRASE->
exude confidence: 散发自信
exude charm: 散发魅力
exude warmth: 散发温暖
exude energy: 散发活力
exude tension: 散发紧张


词源:
From Latin *exūdāre, from ex- "out" + sūdare "to sweat".  The original meaning related to sweating or oozing a liquid.  The modern usage has broadened to include intangible qualities.
