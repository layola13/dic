lackadaisicalness:/ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəlnəs/| n. |倦怠；懒散；无精打采|A state of lacking enthusiasm and energy; listlessness; indolence.  It describes a general feeling of apathy and lack of motivation.|The lackadaisicalness of the team was evident in their poor performance.  这支队伍的倦怠感在他们糟糕的表现中显而易见。


Their lackadaisicalness towards their studies resulted in poor grades.  他们对学习的倦怠导致了糟糕的成绩。


近义词：apathy, lethargy, indolence, lassitude, listlessness|反义词：energy, enthusiasm, diligence, vigor, zeal
