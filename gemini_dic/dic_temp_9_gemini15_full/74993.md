fried:/fraɪd/ | adj. |油炸的，油煎的 | Cooked in hot fat or oil.  |例句：I had fried chicken for dinner. 我晚饭吃了炸鸡。|例句：The potatoes were fried until golden brown. 土豆炸至金黄。|近义词：deep-fried, pan-fried, sautéed |反义词：boiled, baked, roasted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cook fries the potatoes every morning. = 厨师每天早上都炸土豆。
We fry the eggs in butter. = 我们用黄油煎鸡蛋。

一般过去时:
She fried the fish until it was crispy. = 她把鱼炸至酥脆。
He fried an egg for breakfast. = 他煎了个鸡蛋当早餐。

现在完成时:
I have fried several batches of onion rings today. = 我今天已经炸了好几批洋葱圈了。
They have fried the chicken until it's golden brown. = 他们已经把鸡肉炸至金黄。

过去完成时:
She had fried the potatoes before the guests arrived. = 客人来之前，她已经把土豆炸好了。
We had fried the fish before we started the grill. = 我们在开始烧烤之前就已经把鱼炸好了。

将来时:
I will fry some fish for dinner tonight. = 今晚我将炸一些鱼做晚餐。
We will fry the spring rolls until they are crispy. = 我们会把春卷炸至酥脆。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would fry some dumplings. = 如果我有更多时间，我会炸些饺子。
I wish I had fried the chicken longer. = 我真希望我把鸡肉炸久一点。

被动语态:
The fish was fried to perfection. = 这鱼炸得完美无缺。
The potatoes are being fried right now. = 土豆正在油炸中。

疑问句:

否定句:
