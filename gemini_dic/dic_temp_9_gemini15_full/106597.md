kudos:/ˈkuːdoʊz/| n.|荣誉；赞扬；功劳|Praise and admiration given for an achievement.|The team received kudos for their outstanding performance.|近义词：praise, acclaim, commendation|反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The athlete receives kudos for his victory. = 运动员因获胜而受到赞扬。
Her work deserves much kudos. = 她的作品值得高度赞扬。


一般过去时:
The artist received kudos for her innovative painting. = 艺术家因其创新的绘画而获得赞扬。
They gave kudos to the volunteers for their hard work. = 他们对志愿者们的辛勤工作表示赞扬。


现在完成时:
She has earned kudos for her tireless efforts. = 她因不懈的努力赢得了赞扬。
He has received kudos from many colleagues. = 他已获得许多同事的赞扬。


过去完成时:
The team had already received kudos for their earlier achievements. = 该团队因其早期的成就已获得赞扬。
By the end of the project, they had earned significant kudos. = 到项目结束时，他们已经赢得了很大的赞誉。


将来时:
She will receive kudos for her presentation. = 她将因她的演讲而获得赞扬。
They will surely receive kudos for their bravery. = 他们一定会因为他们的勇敢而获得赞扬。


虚拟语气:
If she had finished the project on time, she would have received more kudos. = 如果她按时完成项目，她会得到更多赞扬。
I wish I had received more kudos for my efforts. = 我希望我的努力能得到更多赞扬。


被动语态:
Kudos were given to the team for their hard work. = 团队因辛勤工作而获得赞扬。
The award was given as a token of kudos. = 该奖项是作为赞扬的象征而颁发的。


疑问句:
Will you give kudos to your team? = 你会赞扬你的团队吗？
Did they receive kudos for their efforts? = 他们因他们的努力而获得赞扬了吗？


否定句:
He did not receive the kudos he deserved. = 他没有得到他应得的赞扬。
The project didn't earn much kudos. = 该项目没有赢得多少赞扬。
