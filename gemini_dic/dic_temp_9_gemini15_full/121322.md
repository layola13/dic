misframed:/ˈmɪsfreɪmd/| adj.|曲解的，被歪曲的，被误读的 | To present something in a false or misleading way; to wrongly interpret or represent something.  |例句：The article misframed the scientist's findings, leading to public misunderstanding. 这篇文章曲解了科学家的研究结果，导致公众误解。

The media's coverage misframed the protest as a violent riot, ignoring the peaceful demonstrators. 媒体的报道歪曲了抗议活动，将其描绘成暴力骚乱，而忽略了和平示威者。|近义词：distorted, misinterpreted, misrepresented, skewed |反义词：accurately framed, truthfully represented, correctly interpreted |形容词
