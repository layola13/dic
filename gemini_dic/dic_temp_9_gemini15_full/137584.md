nontravelling: /ˌnɒnˈtrævəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不旅行的；非旅行的 | Not traveling; not involved in travel.  This word describes something or someone that is not currently engaged in travel or related activities. | 例句：The nontravelling salesman worked from his home office.  = 不经常出差的销售员在家办公。

例句：  During the nontravelling season, the hotel offered discounted rates. = 在旅游淡季，酒店提供折扣价。


近义词：stationary, sedentary, immobile

反义词：traveling, mobile, itinerant

词性：形容词
