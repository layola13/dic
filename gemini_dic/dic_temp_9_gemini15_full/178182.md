recoloring:/ˌriːˈkʌləriŋ/| v.|重新着色，重新染色|The act of coloring something again, often to change its appearance or to correct a previous coloring job.  It can refer to both the process and the result.|例句：The artist spent hours recoloring the old painting to restore its vibrancy.  艺术家花了数小时重新着色这幅旧画，以恢复其活力。
The recoloring of the map made the geographical features clearer. 地图重新着色使地理特征更清晰。|近义词：redyeing, repainting, recoloring,  restoring|反义词：fading, bleaching
