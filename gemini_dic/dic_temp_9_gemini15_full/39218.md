conglomerating:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |  使成团；联合；合并 | The process of combining different things into a single, often large mass or organization.  It implies a lack of uniformity in the elements being combined. | Example: The company is conglomerating several smaller businesses to expand its market share. 公司正在合并几家小型企业以扩大其市场份额。 | 近义词：amalgamating, consolidating, uniting | 反义词：separating, disintegrating, dividing
