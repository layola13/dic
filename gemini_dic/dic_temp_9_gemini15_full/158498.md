plenipotentiary:/ˌplɛnɪpəˈtɛnʃəri/| adj., n.|全权的；全权代表|A plenipotentiary is a person who is fully authorized to act on behalf of another, especially in diplomatic matters.  A plenipotentiary treaty is one that is made by a person with full power. |例句：The ambassador acted as a plenipotentiary, signing the treaty without further consultation.  这位大使以全权代表的身份行事，在未经进一步协商的情况下签署了条约。

The conference was attended by plenipotentiaries from many countries.  许多国家的全权代表出席了会议。|近义词：authorized representative, envoy, delegate|反义词：limited representative
