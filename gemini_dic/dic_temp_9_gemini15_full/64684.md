escaped:/ɪˈskeɪpt/| v.|逃脱，避免，泄露| to get free from something, especially a place where you have been kept or confined; to avoid something unpleasant or dangerous |例句：The prisoner escaped from jail.  囚犯从监狱逃脱了。|例句：We escaped the storm by taking shelter in a cave. 我们躲在山洞里躲过了暴风雨。|近义词：fled, got away, evaded|反义词：captured, apprehended, trapped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He escapes from prison every night in his dreams. 他每晚都在梦中越狱。
The water escapes from the leaking pipe. 水从漏水的管道里流出来。

一般过去时:
The bird escaped from its cage. 那只鸟从笼子里飞走了。
They escaped unharmed from the burning building. 他们毫发无损地从燃烧的建筑物中逃了出来。

现在完成时:
He has escaped punishment for his crime. 他逃脱了对他的罪行的惩罚。
The secret has escaped the attention of the public. 这个秘密没有引起公众的注意。

过去完成时:
The thief had escaped before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。
She had escaped the danger just in time. 她及时逃脱了危险。

将来时:
The hostages will escape soon, we hope. 我们希望人质很快就能逃脱。
He will escape serious injury, luckily. 幸运的是，他会避免重伤。

虚拟语气:
If only I could escape this dreadful situation!  要是我能逃脱这种可怕的境地就好了！
I wish I could escape the pressure of work. 我希望我能摆脱工作压力。

被动语态:
The prisoner was escaped by the guards. 囚犯被狱警放跑了。（被动语态，强调被放跑）
The problem was escaped by him. 他逃避了这个问题。（被动语态，强调问题被逃避）

疑问句:
Did the criminal escape? 罪犯逃脱了吗？
How did he escape from the prison? 他是怎么从监狱逃脱的？


否定句:
He didn't escape the consequences of his actions. 他没有逃避他行为的后果。
The murderer didn't escape capture. 凶手没有被捕。


PHRASE->
escape from  逃离...
escape to 逃往...
escape notice  未被注意到
narrow escape 死里逃生
a lucky escape 幸运逃脱
