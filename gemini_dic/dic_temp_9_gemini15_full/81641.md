greedy:/ˈɡriːdi/| adj.|贪婪的，贪心的|Having or showing a strong desire for more than one needs or deserves.|The greedy king hoarded all the gold for himself.  贪婪的国王把所有的金子都据为己有。|He is greedy for power and wealth. 他贪恋权力和财富。|generous, avaricious|generous, unselfish, content|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is greedy for money. = 他贪财。
They are always greedy for more. = 他们总是贪得无厌。


一般过去时:
She was greedy when she was a child. = 她小时候很贪吃。
