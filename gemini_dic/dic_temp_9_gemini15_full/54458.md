disinsure:/ˌdɪsɪnˈʃʊr/ | vt. |解除保险；停止保险 | To cancel or terminate an insurance policy.  | 例句：The company decided to disinsure the high-risk properties. 公司决定取消高风险房产的保险。 | 例句：He disinsured his car after selling it. 他卖掉车后解除了汽车保险。| 近义词：cancel, terminate, void | 反义词：insure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disinsures high-risk properties. = 公司取消高风险房产的保险。
They disinsure their vehicles after paying off the loan. = 他们还清贷款后取消车辆保险。

一般过去时:
He disinsured his life insurance policy last year. = 他去年解除了人寿保险。
She disinsured her car after the accident. = 事故后她解除了汽车保险。
