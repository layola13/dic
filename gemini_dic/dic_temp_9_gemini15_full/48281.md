decoyed:/dɪˈkɔɪd/| v.| 诱骗；诱捕；欺骗 | To lure or entice someone or something into a trap or other dangerous situation by means of deception.  | 例句：The hunters decoyed the ducks into a net. 猎人用诱饵把鸭子诱捕到网里。

The poacher decoyed the deer into a clearing with salt. 偷猎者用盐诱使鹿进入空地。


近义词：lure, entice, trap, ensnare, beguile

反义词：warn, protect
