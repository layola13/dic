monosperm:/ˈmɒnəʊspɜːm/| n.|单精子|A monosperm is a seed or ovule that develops from a single fertilized egg cell. It contrasts with a polysperm, which develops from multiple fertilized egg cells.  The term is primarily used in botanical contexts.| Example:  The research focused on the development of monosperms in certain plant species.  研究集中在某些植物物种中单精子的发育上。|近义词: single-fertilized ovule|反义词: polysperm|名词
