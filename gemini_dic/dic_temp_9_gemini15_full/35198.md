clubfooted:/ˈklʌbˌfʊtɪd/| adj.| 弓足的，内翻足的 | Having a deformed foot, typically one in which the foot is twisted inward.  The condition is often present at birth.  |例句：The child was born clubfooted and required corrective surgery. 这个孩子生下来就是弓足，需要矫正手术。

例句：He walked with a noticeable limp due to his clubfooted condition. 他因为弓足而走路明显跛脚。


近义词：talipes equinovarus (medical term)


反义词：normal footed
