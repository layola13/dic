sensated:/ˈsɛnseɪtɪd/| adj.|感觉到的；感知到的|Experienced or perceived through the senses.  It implies a strong and often vivid sensory experience.|例句：The sensated heat from the fire was intense.  火带来的灼热感非常强烈。

例句：The artist aimed to create a sensated experience for the viewer, engaging all five senses.  艺术家旨在为观众创造一种感官体验，调动所有五种感官。


近义词：perceived, felt, sensed, experienced

反义词：unfelt, unperceived, unnoticed

词性：形容词
