riveter:/ˈrɪvɪtər/| n. v.|铆工；铆接机；铆钉机|A person whose job is to fasten metal plates together using rivets; a tool for fastening metal plates together using rivets.|例句：He worked as a riveter in a shipyard. 他在船厂当铆工。|例句：The riveter malfunctioned during the construction. 铆接机在施工过程中发生故障。|近义词：fastener, joiner|反义词：disassembler, separator

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The riveter fastens the metal plates. = 铆接机固定金属板。
He rivets the pieces together. = 他用铆钉把部件固定在一起。


一般过去时:
The workers riveted the panels yesterday. = 工人们昨天铆接了面板。
She used a hand riveter to fix the broken chair. = 她用手动铆接机修理了坏掉的椅子。


现在完成时:
They have riveted all the parts. = 他们已经铆接了所有部件。
The mechanic has repaired the riveter. = 技工已经修理好了铆接机。


过去完成时:
The bridge had been riveted before the inspection. = 大桥在检查之前就已经铆接好了。
They had finished riveting the fuselage before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经完成了机身的铆接工作。


将来时:
