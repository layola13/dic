downtrod:/ˈdaʊnˌtrɒd/ | adj. | 被践踏的；被压抑的；沮丧的 | downtrodden describes someone who is treated unfairly and cruelly and is often poor and powerless.  It emphasizes the feeling of being oppressed and suppressed. |例句：The downtrodden people finally rose up against their oppressors.  受压迫的人民最终反抗了他们的压迫者。
The downtrodden farmer worked tirelessly in the fields.  那个被压迫的农民在田里不知疲倦地工作着。 |近义词：oppressed, suppressed, subjugated, crushed |反义词：privileged, empowered, liberated
