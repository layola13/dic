outswimming: /ˌaʊtˈswɪmɪŋ/ | v. |  超越（游泳）; 游泳速度超过 |  The act of swimming faster or further than someone else.  It implies a competition or comparison of swimming abilities. | 例句：He achieved victory by outswimming all his competitors. 他通过超越所有竞争对手的游泳速度获得了胜利。

例句：The young athlete outswam his older brother in the race. 年轻的运动员在比赛中游泳速度超过了他的哥哥。


近义词：outpacing, surpassing, exceeding (in swimming) | 反义词：being outswum | 词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outswims everyone in the pool. = 他在泳池里游泳速度超过所有人。
She outswims her training partner consistently. = 她始终比她的训练伙伴游泳速度快。

一般过去时:
He outswam his opponent in the final lap. = 他在最后一圈超过了他的对手。
She outswam all the other competitors. = 她超过了所有其他竞争者。

现在完成时:
He has outswam me in every race this year. = 今年的每次比赛他都比我游得快。
She has outswam all expectations. = 她超出了所有人的预期。

过去完成时:
He had outswam the current before reaching the shore. = 他到达岸边之前已经克服了水流。
She had outswam her previous best time. = 她已经超过了她之前的最好成绩。

将来时:
He will outswim his rivals in the upcoming competition. = 他将在即将到来的比赛中超越他的对手。
She will outswim the competition if she trains hard. = 如果她刻苦训练，她将战胜对手。

虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outswim anyone. = 如果他训练更努力，他就能超过任何人。
I wish I could outswim the strong current. = 我希望我能克服强大的水流。

被动语态:
He was outswam by the younger competitor. = 他被更年轻的竞争对手超过了。
She was outswam in the final race. = 她在决赛中被超过了。

疑问句:
Did he outswim his brother? = 他游泳速度超过他哥哥了吗？
Can she outswim the champion? = 她能超过冠军吗？

否定句:
He didn't outswim the other swimmers. = 他没有超过其他游泳者。
She couldn't outswim the strong tide. = 她无法克服强劲的潮水。
