fellow-feelings:/ˈfɛloʊˌfiːlɪŋz/| n. | 同情；同感；感情；相互了解 |A feeling of sympathy and understanding for others; shared feelings.  It emphasizes mutual understanding and shared emotions, often in the face of hardship or shared experience.|例句：The fellow-feelings between the two friends were evident in their supportive words.  两个朋友之间的同情之情在他们互相支持的话语中显而易见。

例句：Their shared experiences fostered a deep sense of fellow-feeling. 他们共同的经历培养了深厚的同感。|近义词：sympathy, empathy, compassion, solidarity |反义词：indifference, apathy, animosity
