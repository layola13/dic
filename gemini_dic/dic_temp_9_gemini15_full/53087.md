diphtherias:/dɪfˈθɪəriəz/| n.|白喉（复数）|Diphtherias refers to multiple instances or cases of diphtheria, a serious bacterial infection that typically affects the nose and throat.  It is characterized by the formation of a thick, grayish membrane in the throat, potentially leading to breathing difficulties and other severe complications. |例句：Several cases of diphtherias were reported in the remote village, prompting a public health emergency.  好几个白喉病例在偏远村庄被报道，引发了公共卫生紧急事件。|近义词：diphtheria (singular)|反义词：无
