The word is a relatively rare and somewhat archaic term. It emphasizes the negative aspect of complexion. | Example: The long illness left her with a dreadful discomplexion.  长期生病使她的脸色非常差。 | 近义词: poor complexion, sallowness, pallor, discoloration | 反义词: healthy complexion, radiant complexion | 名词
