cantingly:/ˈkæntɪŋli/| adv.|虚伪地，假惺惺地，伪善地 |In a hypocritical or sanctimonious manner; affectedly pious.  |例句：He spoke cantingly about his devotion to charity, but his actions told a different story. 他假惺惺地谈论他对慈善事业的奉献，但他的行为却说明了另一番情况。


例句：She cantingly professed her love for animals, yet she wore a fur coat. 她假惺惺地宣称自己热爱动物，却穿着一件皮大衣。


近义词：hypocritically, sanctimoniously, insincerely, affectedly


反义词：honestly, sincerely, genuinely, truthfully


词源: 源自"cant" (n.)  "伪善的话语；黑话"，最终源于拉丁语“cantare”（唱歌），指说教式的、空洞的言语。 "-ingly" 是副词后缀。
