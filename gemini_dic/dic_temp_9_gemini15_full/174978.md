radicalizes:/ˈrædɪkəlaɪzɪz/| v.|使激进化；使极端化 |To cause (someone) to adopt radical or extreme political or social views.|例句：The internet can radicalize young people, exposing them to extremist ideologies.  互联网会使年轻人激进化，使他们接触极端主义思想。|例句：His experiences radicalized him, turning him into a fierce advocate for social justice.  他的经历使他激进化，使他成为社会正义的坚定拥护者。|近义词：extremize, incite, inflame |反义词：moderate, calm, pacify


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The internet radicalizes many young people. = 互联网使许多年轻人激进化。
He radicalizes his followers with his inflammatory speeches. = 他用煽动性的演讲使他的追随者激进化。


一般过去时:
The event radicalized the community. = 那件事使社区激进化了。
His imprisonment radicalized his political views. = 他的监禁使他的政治观点激进化了。


现在完成时:
The movement has radicalized a significant portion of the population. = 这个运动已经使很大一部分人口激进化了。
The leader's rhetoric has radicalized his followers over the years. = 多年来，领导人的言辞使他的追随者激进化了。


过去完成时:
The years of oppression had radicalized the people before the revolution. = 在革命爆发之前，多年的压迫已经使人民激进化了。
They had radicalized their beliefs before the protests started. = 在抗议活动开始之前，他们已经使自己的信仰激进化了。


将来时:
This policy will radicalize the opposition. = 这项政策将使反对派激进化。
The new leader's speeches will likely radicalize the youth. = 新领导人的讲话很可能会使年轻人激进化。


虚拟语气:
If the government continues its policies, it will radicalize the entire nation. = 如果政府继续执行其政策，它将使整个国家激进化。
I wish they hadn't radicalized the youth with their hate speech. = 我真希望他们没有用仇恨言论使年轻人激进化。

疑问句:

否定句:
