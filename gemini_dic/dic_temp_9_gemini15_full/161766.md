powertrains:/ˈpaʊərtreɪnz/ | n. |动力传动系统 | A powertrain is the group of components that deliver power from an engine to the wheels of a vehicle.  It typically includes the engine, transmission, driveshaft, axles, and differential.  | Example:  The new car boasts a highly efficient powertrain. 这辆新车拥有一个高效的动力传动系统。 The hybrid powertrain combines a gasoline engine and an electric motor. 混合动力传动系统结合了汽油发动机和电动机。 |近义词：drivetrain, transmission system |反义词：无
