peraeopod:/ˈpɛriəpɑːd/| n. |步足，胸足|A peraeopod is a thoracic appendage of a crustacean, typically used for walking or swimming.  It's one of the several pairs of legs attached to the thorax (chest region).|例句：The amphipod's peraeopods propelled it swiftly through the water.  = 这只端足类的步足使它迅速地穿过水面。

The shrimp uses its peraeopods to grasp food. = 虾用它的步足抓住食物。|近义词：leg, appendage|反义词: N/A
