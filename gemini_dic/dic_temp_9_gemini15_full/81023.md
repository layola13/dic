graduals:/ˈɡrædʒuəlz/ | adj. |逐渐的，逐步的 | Gradually increasing in intensity or amount.  Something that happens gradually happens slowly and in stages. |例句：The pain gradually subsided. 疼痛逐渐减轻了。

例句：The sun gradually rose over the horizon. 太阳逐渐升起在水平线上。


近义词：progressive, incremental, step-by-step, slow, steady | 反义词：sudden, abrupt, immediate, rapid


词源：源自于拉丁语 "gradus"，意思是“步骤”或“等级”。  "-al" 是形容词后缀。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature gradually rises during the day. = 白天温度逐渐升高。
The plant gradually grows taller. = 植物逐渐长高。


一般过去时:
The music gradually faded away. = 音乐逐渐消逝。
Her anxiety gradually decreased. = 她的焦虑逐渐减少。


现在完成时:
The condition has gradually improved. = 情况逐渐好转。
My understanding of the subject has gradually deepened. = 我对这个主题的理解逐渐加深。


过去完成时:
The tension had gradually built up before the argument. = 在争吵之前，紧张气氛已经逐渐积累。
His confidence had gradually eroded over time. = 他的信心随着时间的推移逐渐被消磨。


将来时:
The problem will gradually disappear. = 问题将会逐渐消失。
The symptoms will gradually lessen. = 症状会逐渐减轻。


虚拟语气:
If the situation improved gradually, we would be less worried. = 如果情况逐渐好转，我们会不那么担心。
I wish my skills improved gradually and consistently. = 我希望我的技能能够逐渐而且稳定地提高。


被动语态:
The changes were gradually implemented. = 这些变化逐渐被实施。
The project is being gradually completed. = 项目正在逐渐完成。


疑问句:
Does the intensity gradually increase? = 强度是否逐渐增加？
Is the plan to gradually implement the new rules? = 计划是逐步实施新规吗？


否定句:
The symptoms did not gradually disappear. = 症状并没有逐渐消失。
The situation did not gradually improve. = 情况并没有逐渐好转。


PHRASE->
