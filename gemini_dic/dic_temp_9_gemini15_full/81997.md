grint:/ɡrɪnt/| v.|磨碎；压碎；咬牙切齿|To grind or crush into small pieces; to clench one's teeth together, usually in anger or frustration.|例句：He grinted the peppercorns before adding them to the stew. 他在把胡椒粒加到炖菜里之前先把它们磨碎了。|例句：She grinted her teeth in pain. 她疼得牙关紧咬。|近义词：grind, crush, pulverize|反义词：pulverize
