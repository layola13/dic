shophouse:/ˈʃɒphaʊs/| n. |店屋；商住两用房屋 |A shophouse is a type of building, common in Southeast Asia, that combines residential space with commercial space on the ground floor.  These buildings often feature a long, narrow design and are typically found in rows. |例句：Many historic shophouses in Georgetown have been beautifully restored. 乔治城许多历史悠久的店屋都得到了美丽的修复。
Many shophouses in the old town are now trendy cafes and boutiques. 老城区许多店屋现在都是时尚的咖啡馆和精品店。|近义词：row house, terrace house |反义词：detached house, freestanding house
