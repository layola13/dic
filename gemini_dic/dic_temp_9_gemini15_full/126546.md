nada:/ˈnɑːdə/|/ˈnɑːdɑː/| n. |什么也没有；零；毫无价值的东西 |Nothing at all; zero; something of no value or importance.  |例句：I asked him for help, but he gave me nada.  我向他求助，但他什么也没给。
例句：The search yielded nada. 搜索一无所获。|近义词：nothing, zilch, zero |反义词：something, everything, plenty
