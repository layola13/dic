comparison:/kəmˈpærɪsən/ | n. |比较，对照|The act of comparing two or more things; a consideration or estimate of similarities or dissimilarities.  It involves identifying similarities and differences between items to reach a conclusion or judgment.|例句：The comparison of the two paintings revealed subtle differences in brushstroke.|近义词：contrast, similitude, parallel|反义词：difference, dissimilarity


例句：
A careful comparison of the two proposals is necessary before making a decision.  在做决定之前，必须仔细比较这两个提案。

The teacher made a comparison between the two poems to highlight their contrasting styles. 老师比较了两首诗歌，以突出它们不同的风格。
