kibed:/kaɪbd/| adj.|冻裂的，发炎的，皲裂的 |Chapped or cracked, especially from cold or exposure.  The term often refers to cracked skin on the heels or hands. |例句：My heels are kibed from walking in the snow. 我的脚后跟因为在雪地里行走而冻裂了。|例句：The constant washing had left her hands kibed and sore.  不断地洗手使她的手冻裂且疼痛。|近义词：chapped, cracked, fissured |反义词：smooth, supple
