look-out: /ˈlʊkˌaʊt/ | n. |瞭望台；警戒；注意 | A place providing a view, often for warning of danger; a warning of danger; careful attention. |例句：The ship's lookout spotted an iceberg.  这艘船的瞭望员发现了一座冰山。|例句：There's a lookout for thieves in the area.  该地区正在注意防范盗贼。|近义词：watchtower, warning, observation |反义词：negligence, carelessness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lookout watches the sea. = 瞭望员注视着大海。
He keeps a lookout for cars. = 他留神看着是否有车。

一般过去时:
The lookout saw a ship. = 瞭望员看到了一艘船。
They kept a sharp lookout yesterday. = 他们昨天非常警惕地注意着周围。

The lookout has spotted several ships. = 瞭望员已经发现了多艘船。

将来时:
