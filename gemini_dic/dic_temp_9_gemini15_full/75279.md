frontages:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒɪz/| n. |正面，临街面，门面|The outward appearance or aspect of something, especially a building's facade facing a street or open space.  It often refers to the length of a property's boundary facing a street or road. |例句：The shop has a wide frontage, attracting many customers. 这家商店门面宽敞，吸引了很多顾客。|例句：The total frontage of the building is 100 feet. 这栋建筑物的总门面长度为100英尺。|近义词：facade, front, exterior |反义词：rear, back
