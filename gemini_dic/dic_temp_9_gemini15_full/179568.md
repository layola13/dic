reembarking:/ˌriːɪmˈbɑːrkɪŋ/| v. |重新登船；重新开始；再次从事|The act of getting back on a ship or plane, or figuratively, restarting an activity or project.  |例句：After a brief stop, the passengers were reembarking on the cruise ship.  重新短暂的停留后，乘客们重新登上了游轮。

例句：He's reembarking on his writing career after a long break. 他在长时间的休息后重新开始了他的写作生涯。|近义词：restart, recommence, resume |反义词：disembark, abandon, cease
