sculp:/skʌlpt/| vt.|雕刻，塑造|To carve or shape (a material, especially stone or wood) into a work of art.|The artist sculpted a beautiful bird from a piece of wood.  艺术家用一块木头雕刻了一只美丽的鸟。|carve, mold, shape, fashion|destroy, raze|动词
