co-ownerships:/ˌkoʊˈoʊnərʃɪps/| n. |共有权；联名所有权 |The legal right of two or more individuals to jointly own a property or asset.  This arrangement often involves shared responsibilities and benefits.  |例句：The brothers entered into a co-ownership of the family farm. 兄弟俩对家族农场进行了共有权登记。

例句：The condominium building allows for various forms of co-ownership, including timeshares and fractional ownership.  这座公寓楼允许各种形式的共有权，包括分时度假和部分所有权。


近义词：joint ownership, shared ownership, common ownership

反义词：sole ownership, individual ownership

词性:名词
