noncontributions:/ˌnɒnkɒntribˈjuːʃənz/| n. |非贡献；未作贡献 |Things that do not contribute or add value. It can also refer to a lack of contribution. |例句：His noncontributions to the project led to its delay. 他的项目中毫无贡献导致了项目的延误。
The board members discussed the team's noncontributions to the company's overall success. 董事会成员讨论了团队对公司整体成功的毫无贡献。|近义词：lack of contribution, uselessness, inactivity |反义词：contributions, achievements, successes |名词
