force-fed:/fɔːrsfed/| v. |强行喂食，强迫接受|To force someone to eat or drink more than they want.  This often implies a negative connotation, suggesting coercion or even cruelty. |Example: The prisoners were force-fed by their captors. 囚犯们被他们的绑架者强行喂食。
The child was force-fed until he vomited.  孩子被强行喂食直到呕吐。|近义词：overfeed, cram, stuff|反义词：starve, underfeed|
