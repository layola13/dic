dart:/dɑːrt/| /dɑːrt/| n. vi. vt.|飞镖；突然移动；猛冲；|a small pointed object thrown as a weapon or for sport; a sudden quick movement; to move suddenly and quickly; to throw a dart|例句：He threw a dart at the board. 他向靶子上扔了一支飞镖。
He darted across the road. 他迅速穿过马路。


近义词：javelin, spear, fling, bolt, shoot|反义词：meander, stroll, saunter, creep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child darts around the playground. = 孩子在操场上跑来跑去。
He darts across the street. = 他飞快地穿过街道。

一般过去时:
The bird darted into the bushes. = 鸟儿窜进了灌木丛。
She darted a glance at her watch. = 她快速看了一眼手表。

现在完成时:
The rabbit has darted into its burrow. = 兔子已经窜进了它的地洞。
He has darted between the cars several times. = 他已经好几次在车流中穿梭了。

过去完成时:
The thief had darted away before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。
She had darted a look at the letter before putting it down. = 她放下信之前快速地看了一眼。

将来时:
The squirrel will dart up the tree if it sees danger. = 如果松鼠看到危险，它就会窜上树。
He will dart out into the street without looking. = 他会不看路就冲到街上。

虚拟语气:
If I were to dart across the street, I would risk being hit by a car. = 如果我冲过马路，就有可能被车撞到。
I wish I had not darted out into traffic. = 我真希望我没有冲进车流。

被动语态:
The dart was thrown with great force. = 飞镖是用很大的力扔出去的。
The ball was darted across the court. = 球被快速地传过球场。

疑问句:
Did the cat dart under the fence? = 猫是不是窜到篱笆底下了？
Will the runner dart past the other competitors? = 跑步者会超过其他竞争对手吗？

否定句:
The bird did not dart away when it saw me. = 鸟看到我时并没有飞走。
He did not dart his eyes around the room. = 他并没有环顾房间。


PHRASE->
dart a glance  = 快速地看一眼
dart in and out  = 忽隐忽现
dart about  = 到处乱窜
dart forward  = 猛冲向前
dart a look  = 快速地瞥一眼
