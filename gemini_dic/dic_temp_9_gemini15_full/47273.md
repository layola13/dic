de-automated:/ˌdiːˈɔːtəmeɪtɪd/| adj.|去自动化的；手动化的|The term "de-automated" describes a process or system that has had its automation removed or reduced.  It implies a shift from automated processes to manual or less automated ones.  This often occurs due to cost considerations, reliability issues with the automation, or a need for greater human control.|例句：The factory underwent de-automation, resulting in increased labor costs.  工厂进行了去自动化改造，导致劳动力成本增加。|近义词：manual, non-automated, mechanized|反义词：automated, automatic
