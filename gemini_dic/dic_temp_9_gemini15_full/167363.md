prettifier:/ˈprɛtɪfaɪər/ | n. | 美化者；使漂亮的人或物 | A prettifier is a person or thing that makes something more attractive or beautiful.  It can refer to a person who applies makeup or does hair styling, or a tool or software that enhances the visual appeal of something.  | Example:  She used a photo prettifier app to enhance her vacation pictures. 她用了一个照片美化应用程序来增强她的度假照片。 |近义词：beautifier, enhancer, improver |反义词：marrer, defacer, spoiler
