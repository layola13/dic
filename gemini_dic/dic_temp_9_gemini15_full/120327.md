minihome:/ˈmɪnihoʊm/| n.|小型住宅，微型住宅|A small, often prefabricated, house;  a compact home designed for smaller living spaces or specific needs.  It can refer to tiny houses, modular homes, or other types of dwellings characterized by their reduced size.|例句：They decided to build a minihome on their small plot of land.他们决定在他们的小块土地上建造一栋小型住宅。|例句：The minihome movement is gaining popularity among people seeking a simpler lifestyle. 小型住宅运动在追求更简单生活方式的人群中越来越受欢迎。|近义词：tiny house, compact home, small house|反义词：mansion, large house
