compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ /ˈkɑːmpənseɪt/ | vt.  | 补偿；赔偿；抵消 | To make up for something bad or undesirable; to counteract or balance.  | The company will compensate employees for their travel expenses. 公司将补偿员工的差旅费。  |reimburse, recompense, make up for |  deprive,  underpay |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compensates his employees fairly. = 他公平地补偿他的员工。
The insurance company compensates for damages. = 保险公司赔偿损失。

一般过去时:
They compensated the victims for their losses. = 他们赔偿了受害者的损失。
The government compensated the farmers for their damaged crops. = 政府赔偿了农民受损的庄稼。

现在完成时:
The company has compensated me for the delay. = 公司已经赔偿我因延误造成的损失。
We have compensated for the lost time by working overtime. = 我们通过加班来弥补失去的时间。

过去完成时:
They had compensated the workers before the strike. = 他们在罢工前已经补偿了工人们。
She had compensated for her lack of experience with hard work. = 她用努力工作弥补了经验不足。


将来时:
The company will compensate the employees for their extra work. = 公司将补偿员工的额外工作。
They will compensate for the lost opportunity. = 他们将弥补失去的机会。


虚拟语气:
If the company compensated us fairly, we wouldn't be complaining. = 如果公司公平地补偿我们，我们就不会抱怨了。
I wish they had compensated me for my trouble. = 我希望他们能补偿我为此付出的麻烦。


被动语态:
The workers were compensated for their injuries. = 工人们因受伤而得到了赔偿。
The damages will be compensated by the insurance company. = 损失将由保险公司赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company compensate us for the damage? = 公司会赔偿我们的损失吗？
How will you compensate for your mistakes? = 你将如何弥补你的错误？


否定句:
The company did not compensate the employees for their overtime. = 公司没有补偿员工的加班费。
They failed to compensate for the loss of sales. = 他们未能弥补销售损失。


PHRASE->
compensate for = 补偿…
compensate somebody for something = 赔偿某人…
fully compensate = 全额赔偿
overcompensate = 过度补偿
compensate oneself = 自我补偿


词源:
From Late Latin *compensare, from Latin compensare "to weigh against," from com- "together" + pensare "to weigh."  The sense development is from "weighing against" to "making up for."
