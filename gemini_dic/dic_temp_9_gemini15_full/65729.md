eventuating: /ɪˈvɛntʃueɪt/ | v. |最终发生；结果是 | To happen or result as a final consequence.  |例句：His efforts eventuated in success.  他的努力最终获得了成功。
The negotiations eventuated in a compromise.  谈判最终达成妥协。|近义词：result, culminate, conclude, end |反义词：begin, start, commence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His hard work usually eventuates in success. = 他努力工作通常最终导致成功。
The current situation rarely eventuates in a positive outcome. = 目前的情况很少最终导致积极的结果。


一般过去时:
The project eventuated in a significant breakthrough. = 这个项目最终取得了重大突破。
Their disagreements eventuated in a bitter separation. = 他们的分歧最终导致了痛苦的分离。


现在完成时:
All our efforts have eventuated in a successful product launch. = 我们所有的努力最终导致了产品成功发布。
The investigation has eventuated in several arrests. = 调查最终导致了几起逮捕。


过去完成时:
It had eventuated that the plan was flawed from the start. = 最终发现，这个计划从一开始就存在缺陷。
They realized that their actions had eventuated in unintended consequences. = 他们意识到他们的行为最终导致了意料之外的后果。


将来时:
We hope that this initiative will eventuate in improved relations. = 我们希望这项倡议将最终改善关系。
This research is expected to eventuate in a new treatment. = 预计这项研究将最终研发出一种新的治疗方法。


虚拟语气:
If we had planned better, the outcome might not have eventuated as it did. = 如果我们计划得更好，结果可能不会像现在这样。
I wish the negotiations had eventuated in a more favorable agreement. = 我希望谈判能达成更有利的协议。


被动语态:
A compromise was eventuated after lengthy negotiations. = 在漫长的谈判之后，最终达成了妥协。（此处被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）
Success was eventuated through perseverance and hard work. = 通过坚持和努力，最终取得了成功。（此处被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）


疑问句:
Will this plan eventuate in the desired results? = 这个计划最终会达到预期的效果吗？
What will eventuate from this conflict? = 这场冲突最终会产生什么结果？


否定句:
The experiment did not eventuate in the expected results. = 实验并没有产生预期的结果。
His efforts did not eventuate in a promotion. = 他的努力并没有最终获得晋升。



PHRASE->
