reattributes:/ˌriːˈætrɪbjuːts/| vt.|重新归属，重新分配|To attribute (something) again or differently; to assign a different attribute to.|The company reattributed the sales decline to a weakened economy, not poor management. 公司将销售额下降归因于经济疲软，而不是管理不善。|reassign, reallocate|retain, keep|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reattribute the problem to a software bug. = 他们将问题重新归因于软件错误。
The company reattributes resources to the new project. = 公司将资源重新分配给新项目。

一般过去时:
The historians reattributed the painting to a different artist. = 历史学家将这幅画重新归于另一位艺术家。
The researchers reattributed the cause of the disease. = 研究人员重新确定了该疾病的病因。

现在完成时:
The team has reattributed the responsibility to a new member. = 团队已将责任重新分配给一位新成员。
The government has reattributed funds to disaster relief. = 政府已将资金重新分配用于救灾。

过去完成时:
They had reattributed the authorship before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，他们已经重新认定了作者。
The committee had reattributed the blame to the previous administration. = 委员会已将责任归咎于前任政府。

将来时:

The board will reattribute the budget for next year. = 董事会将重新分配明年的预算。

虚拟语气:
If they had reattributed the funds earlier, the project would have succeeded. = 如果他们早些时候重新分配资金，项目就会成功。
I wish they had reattributed the responsibility more fairly. = 我希望他们能更公平地重新分配责任。

被动语态:

否定句:
