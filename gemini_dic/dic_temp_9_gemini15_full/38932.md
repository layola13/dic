confidante:/ˌkɒnfɪˈdɑːnt/| n. |知己，密友 | A confidante is a close and trusted friend to whom one can confide secrets and personal matters.  A confidante offers unwavering support, empathy, and discretion. | She confided her deepest fears to her confidante. 她向她的知己吐露了她内心最深处的恐惧。| close friend, trusted friend, bosom friend | enemy, stranger
