scarce:/skɛrs/ | adj. | 稀少的；缺乏的；不足的 |  Something that is scarce is not readily available; it is in short supply.  It often implies a lack of something essential or desirable. |


例句：

Fresh water is scarce in many parts of the world. = 在世界许多地区，淡水资源匮乏。

Job opportunities are scarce in the current economic climate. = 在当前的经济环境下，就业机会稀少。


近义词：rare, infrequent, uncommon, limited, deficient


反义词：abundant, plentiful, copious, ample


词性: 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Food is scarce in the drought-stricken region. = 旱区食物匮乏。
Resources are scarce for this project. = 这个项目资源匮乏。


一般过去时:
Supplies were scarce during the war. = 战争期间物资匮乏。
Fuel was scarce last winter. = 去年冬天燃料短缺。


现在完成时:
Oil has become scarce in recent years. = 近年来石油变得稀缺。
Opportunities have become scarce since the recession. = 自经济衰退以来，机会变得越来越少。


过去完成时:
The explorers discovered that food had become scarce. = 探险家们发现食物已经短缺。
They realized that water had become scarce before reaching the summit. = 他们到达山顶之前才意识到水已经短缺了。


将来时:
Water will become scarce if we don't conserve it. = 如果我们不节约用水，水资源将会匮乏。
Essential materials will be scarce in the coming months. = 未来几个月必需品将会短缺。


虚拟语气:
If resources were not scarce, we could do more. = 如果资源不匮乏，我们就能做更多的事情。
If oil were not scarce, gas prices would be lower. = 如果石油不稀缺，汽油价格就会更低。


被动语态:
The opportunity was deemed scarce. = 这个机会被认为是稀少的。
The resource is considered scarce by experts. = 专家认为这种资源稀缺。


疑问句:
Is clean water scarce in your area? = 你们地区清洁用水短缺吗？
Are skilled workers scarce in your industry? = 贵行业熟练工人短缺吗？


否定句:
Food is not scarce in this region. = 这个地区食物并不匮乏。
Money is not scarce for that company. = 那家公司并不缺钱。


PHRASE->
scarce resources = 稀缺资源
scarce commodity = 稀缺商品
become scarce = 变得稀少
make scarce = 使稀少
scarcity of resources = 资源短缺
