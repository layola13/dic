precorrespondences:/ˌpriːkɔːrəˈspɒndənsɪz/| n. |前期通信，预先通信 |  Communications or exchanges that take place before a main event or series of events.  It often refers to preliminary communications, emails, or letters exchanged before a formal meeting, negotiation, or project begins. | Example: The precorrespondences between the two companies laid the groundwork for a successful merger.  = 两家公司之间的前期通信为成功并购奠定了基础。|近义词： preliminary communications, advance communications, prior correspondence |反义词：post-correspondence, subsequent communications|名词
