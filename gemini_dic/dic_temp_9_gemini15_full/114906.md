mana:/ˈmænə/| n.|魔力，法力；精力；精神力量|Magical power or energy, especially in myths and fiction; also, energy or strength.|Example: The wizard drew upon his mana to cast a powerful spell.  巫师运用他的魔力施放了一个强大的咒语。|近义词：power, energy, force|反义词：weakness, impotence|名词
