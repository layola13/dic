mycology:/maɪˈkɒlədʒi/| n. |真菌学|The scientific study of fungi, including their genetic and biochemical properties, their taxonomy and their use to humans.  Mycology encompasses the study of the fungal kingdom, which includes yeasts, molds, and mushrooms.|例句：He is pursuing a career in mycology, specializing in the identification of poisonous mushrooms. 他正在从事真菌学研究，专门研究有毒蘑菇的鉴定。|例句：Mycology is a vast field, constantly expanding our understanding of these diverse organisms. 真菌学是一个广阔的领域，不断扩展我们对这些多样化生物的理解。|近义词：fungal biology, fungal science |反义词: N/A
