by-passers:/ˈbaɪˌpæsərz/ | n. |路过的人，旁观者 | People who are passing by a particular place.  | Example: The by-passers stopped to watch the accident. 路过的人停下来观看事故。  The by-passers showed no interest in the street performer. 路过的人对街头表演者不感兴趣。|近义词：passersby, onlookers |反义词：participants, involved parties
