predrove:/ˌpriːˈdroʊv/| v.|预先驾驶；提前驾驶|To drive beforehand or in advance.  This word is a relatively rare back-formation from "driven." It's not commonly used and often sounds awkward.|例句：He predrove the route to check for road closures. 他提前驾驶路线以检查道路封闭情况。


例句：They predrove the race track to familiarize themselves with the course. 他们提前驾驶赛道以熟悉赛道。


近义词：pre-run, scout, reconnoiter|反义词：None directly applicable.  The opposite would be to drive *after* or *without* prior inspection.|词性:动词
