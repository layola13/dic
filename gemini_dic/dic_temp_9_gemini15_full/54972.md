disproportional: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənəl/ | adj. |不成比例的，不相称的 |  Out of proportion; not corresponding in size, degree, or amount.  It refers to a situation where one part is significantly larger or smaller than another part in relation to the whole. |

例句：

The punishment was disproportional to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。

The company's response to the crisis was disproportional to the scale of the problem. 公司对危机的回应与问题的规模不成比例。


近义词：unbalanced, uneven, disproportionate, incommensurate

反义词：proportional, balanced, commensurate


词性：形容词
