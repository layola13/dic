indebt:/ɪnˈdɛt/ | adj. |负债的，欠债的 | owing money; in a state of debt.  |例句：He is deeply indebt to his parents for their support. 他深深地感激父母的资助。(因为父母的资助而负债)  He's indebt to the bank for a large loan. 他欠银行一大笔贷款。|近义词：in arrears, owing, obligated |反义词：solvent, debt-free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is deeply indebt to his family. = 他深深地欠着家人的情。
The company is indebt to its creditors. = 这家公司欠着债权人的债。

一般过去时:
They were indebt after buying the house. = 他们买房后负债了。
The farmer was indebt to the bank for the loan. = 农民因为贷款而欠着银行的债。

现在完成时:
She has been indebt since losing her job. = 自从失业以来，她一直负债。
We have been indebt for many years. = 我们负债很多年了。

过去完成时:
He had been indebt for years before he finally declared bankruptcy. = 他在最终宣告破产之前已经负债多年了。
The business had been indebt long before the recession. = 在经济衰退之前，这家企业就长期负债了。

将来时:
They will be indebt if they buy that car. = 如果他们买那辆车，他们将会负债。
The project will leave us indebt if we don't secure more funding. = 如果我们不能获得更多资金，这个项目将使我们负债。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't taken out that loan, he wouldn't be indebt now. = 如果他没有贷款，他现在就不会负债了。
I wish I wasn't indebt. = 我希望我不欠债。

被动语态:
The company is rarely seen as being indebt. = 这家公司很少被认为是负债的。 (This is less common usage.  Indebt is less frequently used in the passive voice)

疑问句:
Is he indebt? = 他欠债吗？
Are they indebt to anyone? = 他们欠任何人的债吗？

否定句:
He is not indebt. = 他不欠债。
The company is not indebt to any outside lenders. = 这家公司不欠任何外部贷款人的债。


PHRASE->
deeply indebt = 深陷债务
heavily indebt = 负债累累
become indebt = 负债
go indebt = 负债
remain indebt = 继续负债

词源:
The word "indebt" comes from the Old French word "endetter," meaning "to put in debt."  The prefix "in-" in this case indicates a state of being.
