rotator:/ˈroʊteɪtər/| n. |旋转器，旋转体，旋转肌| A device or body that rotates;  a muscle that causes rotation.  A rotator can refer to various mechanical parts that perform rotation, or to muscles in the body that cause rotation of a limb. |例句：The engine's rotator malfunctioned, causing it to stall. 发动机的旋转器发生故障，导致其熄火。|例句：The rotator cuff muscles help stabilize the shoulder joint.  肩袖肌有助于稳定肩关节。|近义词：rotor, revolving device, swivel, rotator cuff (for muscle)|反义词：stationary device, fixed part
