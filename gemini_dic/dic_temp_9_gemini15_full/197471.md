sheerer:/ˈʃɪərər/| n.|稀薄者，精纺工|A sheerer is someone who shears sheep or other animals, removing their wool or fleece.  It can also refer to someone who makes or sells sheer fabrics.|例句：The sheerer skillfully worked his shears, removing the fleece in one swift motion.  精纺工熟练地使用剪刀，迅速地剪下羊毛。The sheerer sold his fine woolen cloth to the tailor. 精纺工把他的精细羊毛布卖给了裁缝。|近义词：shearer, clipper|反义词:|无
