reoccurring:/ˌriːəˈkɜːrɪŋ/| adj.|反复出现的，再发的|happening again and again; recurring.|The reoccurring theme in his novels is the struggle for identity. 他小说中反复出现的主题是身份认同的斗争。|近义词：repeated, recurrent, recurring, iterative|反义词：unique, one-time


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem is reoccurring frequently. = 这个问题经常反复出现。
This pattern reoccurs throughout the data. = 这种模式在整个数据中反复出现。


一般过去时:
The error reoccurred after the system reboot. = 系统重启后错误再次出现。
The same issue reoccurred last week. = 上周又出现了同样的问题。


现在完成时:
The symptoms have reoccurred several times this month. = 这个月这些症状已经反复出现好几次了。
The problem has reoccurred despite our efforts to fix it. = 尽管我们努力修复，问题还是反复出现。


过去完成时:
The pain had reoccurred before she sought medical attention. = 在她寻求医疗救助之前，疼痛已经反复出现过。
The system crash had reoccurred several times before they found the cause. = 在他们找到原因之前，系统崩溃已经反复出现过几次了。


将来时:
The doctor suspects the symptoms will reoccur. = 医生怀疑症状会再次出现。
I fear the problem will reoccur in the future. = 我担心这个问题将来还会再次出现。


虚拟语气:
If the problem were to reoccur, we would need to take immediate action. = 如果问题再次出现，我们需要立即采取行动。
I wish the headaches wouldn't reoccur. = 我希望头痛不会再次出现。


被动语态:
The issue was reoccurring frequently in the early development phase. = 在早期开发阶段，这个问题经常反复出现。
The error is being reoccurring due to a software bug. = 由于软件错误，错误正在反复出现。


疑问句:
Is this a reoccurring problem? = 这是个反复出现的问题吗？
Will the pain reoccur after the surgery? = 手术后疼痛还会再次出现吗？


否定句:
The problem is not reoccurring. = 问题没有反复出现。
The symptoms did not reoccur after the treatment. = 治疗后症状没有再次出现。

reoccurring error: 反复出现的错误
