enemas:/ˈɛnɪməz/| n.|灌肠；灌肠剂|The act of introducing fluid into the rectum and colon via the anus, typically to relieve constipation or cleanse the bowel before a medical procedure.  Enemas can also be used to administer medication.|例句：The doctor recommended an enema to relieve his constipation. 医生建议他灌肠以缓解便秘。|例句：The patient received a cleansing enema before the colonoscopy. 病人在结肠镜检查前接受了清洁灌肠。|近义词：colonic irrigation, rectal lavage|反义词：N/A
