noninflation:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfleɪʃən/| n. |非通货膨胀；物价稳定|The absence of inflation; a period or situation in which prices are not rising.  This contrasts with inflation, which refers to a general increase in prices. Noninflation can imply price stability or even a slight deflation.|Example:  The central bank aims for noninflation to maintain economic stability.  中央银行的目标是保持物价稳定以维持经济稳定。


The long-term goal is noninflation. 长期目标是物价稳定。|近义词：price stability, stable prices |反义词：inflation, deflation (depending on context)
