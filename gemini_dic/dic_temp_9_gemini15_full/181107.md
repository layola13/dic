reindulgence:/ˌriːɪnˈdʌldʒəns/| n.|再次沉溺；再次放纵|The act of indulging again in something, especially something pleasurable but potentially harmful.  It implies a previous instance of indulgence and a return to that behavior. |例句：After a period of abstinence, her reindulgence in chocolate was swift and complete.  在她戒掉一段时间后，她很快就又开始沉溺于巧克力了。

例句：His reindulgence in gambling cost him his savings. 他再次沉迷赌博，输掉了所有的积蓄。


近义词：relapse, repetition of indulgence, renewed indulgence

反义词：abstinence, self-control, restraint


词性:名词
