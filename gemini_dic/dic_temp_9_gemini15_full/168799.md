producers:/prəˈduːsərz/| n.|生产者；制片人；生产商|People or things that create or produce something.  This can refer to organisms in an ecosystem, individuals in the film industry, or businesses involved in manufacturing.|例句：Plants are the primary producers in most ecosystems.  植物是大多数生态系统中的主要生产者。

例句：Steven Spielberg is a famous film producer. 史蒂文·斯皮尔伯格是一位著名的电影制片人。|近义词：manufacturers, creators, generators|反义词：consumers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces cars. = 工厂生产汽车。
She produces excellent work. = 她创作出优秀的作品。

一般过去时:
They produced a great film last year. = 他们去年制作了一部很棒的电影。
The company produced thousands of units. = 公司生产了数千个单位。

现在完成时:
We have produced three reports this month. = 我们这个月已经制作了三份报告。
The team has produced significant results. = 团队已经取得了显著的成果。

过去完成时:
They had produced all the parts before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经生产出了所有零件。
She had produced a compelling argument. = 她已经提出了一个令人信服的论点。

将来时:
We will produce more next year. = 我们明年将生产更多。
The artist will produce a new painting. = 这位艺术家将创作一幅新画。

虚拟语气:
If they produced more, they would make more profit. = 如果他们生产更多，他们将获得更多利润。
I wish I had produced a better presentation. = 我希望我做了更好的演示。

被动语态:
The cars are produced in Germany. = 这些汽车是在德国生产的。
The report was produced by a team of experts. = 这份报告是由一个专家团队制作的。

疑问句:
Who produces this software? = 谁制作这个软件？
What company produces these goods? = 哪家公司生产这些商品？

否定句:
They don't produce enough goods to meet demand. = 他们生产的商品不足以满足需求。
The factory didn't produce anything yesterday. = 工厂昨天什么也没生产。
