collisionless: /kəˈlɪʒən.ləs/ | adj. | 无碰撞的；无冲突的 |  Lacking collisions; not involving collisions.  Describes a system or process where particles or objects do not interact through collisions. This term is often used in physics, particularly in plasma physics and astrophysics. |

例句：

The simulation assumed a collisionless plasma. = 模拟假设了一个无碰撞等离子体。

In a collisionless shock, the dissipation is due to wave-particle interactions rather than particle collisions. = 在无碰撞激波中，耗散是由于波粒相互作用而不是粒子碰撞造成的。


近义词：collision-free


反义词：collisional


词性：形容词
