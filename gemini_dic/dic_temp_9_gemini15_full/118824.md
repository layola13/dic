metanephros:/ˌmɛtəˈnɛfrɒs/| n. |后肾|The metanephros is the permanent kidney that develops in mammals and other amniotes.  It develops from the ureteric bud and the metanephrogenic blastema.  The metanephros is responsible for filtering waste products from the blood and producing urine. |The metanephros is the final stage in kidney development.  |近义词：permanent kidney|反义词：pronephros, mesonephros
