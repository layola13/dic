ironmaster:/ˈaɪərnˌmɑːstər/| n. |举重高手；大力士；铸铁厂主|A person who is exceptionally strong, often skilled in weightlifting;  also, historically, the owner or operator of an iron foundry or forge.|例句：He's an ironmaster in the gym, capable of lifting incredible weights. 他是健身房里的举重高手，能够举起难以置信的重量。|例句：The ironmaster's forge produced tools and weapons for the entire region. 铸铁厂主打造的铁器供应整个区域。|近义词：weightlifter, strongman, blacksmith (in historical context)|反义词：weakling, frail
