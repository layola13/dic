calamint:/ˈkæləmɪnt/| n. |香薄荷，香蜂草|A genus of aromatic herbs of the mint family, characterized by square stems, opposite leaves, and small, usually purplish flowers.  Calamint is often used in herbal medicine and as a culinary herb.|例句：The gardener carefully cultivated the calamint in her herb garden. 园丁仔细地在她家的药草园里种植香薄荷。|例句：We added a sprig of calamint to the lamb stew for a refreshing flavor. 我们在羊肉炖菜里加了一枝香薄荷，增添清爽的味道。|近义词：mint, balm|反义词：无|名词
