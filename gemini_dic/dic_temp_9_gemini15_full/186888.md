rinser:/ˈrɪnsər/| n. |冲洗器；漂洗器|A device or person that rinses something.  It can refer to a machine used for rinsing things like dishes or clothes, or a person who rinses something.|Example:  The automatic dish rinser made quick work of cleaning the plates. 自动洗碗机很快地洗干净了盘子。 The hair rinser left my hair feeling squeaky clean.  护发素冲洗后，我的头发感觉非常干净。|近义词：washer, cleaner |反义词：soiler, dirtier |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rinses his mouth after every meal. 他每餐后都漱口。
She rinses the dishes before putting them in the dishwasher. 她把盘子冲洗干净后再放进洗碗机。


一般过去时:
I rinsed the salad greens before adding the dressing. 我在加调料之前冲洗了沙拉蔬菜。
He rinsed his hands thoroughly after working in the garden. 他在花园里工作后彻底地洗了手。


现在完成时:
She has rinsed the paintbrushes and put them away. 她已经冲洗了画笔并收了起来。
They have rinsed the clothes and hung them out to dry. 他们已经冲洗了衣服，并把它们晾起来晒干。


过去完成时:
He had rinsed the car before waxing it. 他在打蜡之前已经冲洗了汽车。
She had rinsed the vegetables before chopping them.  她在切菜之前已经洗过蔬菜了。


将来时:
I will rinse the glasses before we have our guests arrive. 客人来之前我会冲洗杯子。
They will rinse the laundry and then put it in the dryer. 他们会把衣服冲洗干净，然后放进烘干机。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better rinser, I would rinse my clothes more often. 如果我有一个更好的冲洗器，我会更频繁地冲洗衣服。
I wish I had rinsed the paint off my hands sooner. 我真希望我早点把油漆从手上洗掉。


被动语态:
The dishes were rinsed thoroughly by the machine. 盘子被机器彻底冲洗干净了。
The car is being rinsed at the car wash. 汽车正在洗车场冲洗。


疑问句:
Did you rinse the vegetables before cooking them? 你在做菜之前有没有洗蔬菜？
Have you rinsed your hair after using conditioner? 你用护发素后洗头了吗？


否定句:
He didn't rinse the cup properly, so it was still dirty. 他没有好好冲洗杯子，所以杯子仍然很脏。
She didn't rinse the clothes before putting them in the dryer. 她没有在把衣服放进烘干机之前冲洗。
