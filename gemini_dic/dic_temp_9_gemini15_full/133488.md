nonfeminists:/ˌnɒnˈfɛmɪnɪsts/ | n. |非女权主义者 |Those who oppose or do not support feminist principles and movements.  They may hold diverse views on gender roles and equality, often disagreeing with core feminist tenets. |Example:  Some nonfeminists argue that feminist movements have gone too far.  一些非女权主义者认为女权运动走得太远了。|近义词：anti-feminists, gender traditionalists |反义词：feminists
