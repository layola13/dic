inactivate:/ɪˈnæktɪveɪt/ | vt. |使无效；使不活跃；使钝化 | To make something inactive or ineffective.  |例句：The drug inactivates the virus.  这种药物使病毒失效。|例句：Heat can inactivate enzymes. 热可以使酶失活。|近义词：deactivate, disable, neutralize |反义词：activate, energize
