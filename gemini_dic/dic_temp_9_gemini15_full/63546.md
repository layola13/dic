enveloping: /ɛnˈvɛləpɪŋ/ | adj. | 包围的；笼罩的；覆盖的 | Surrounding completely; encompassing.  | The enveloping darkness made it difficult to see.  包围的黑暗使得难以看清。| encompassing, surrounding, covering | uncovered, exposed | 形容词
