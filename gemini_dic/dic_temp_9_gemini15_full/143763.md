outmaster:/ˌaʊtˈmæstər/| v. |胜过，超越，技高一筹 |To surpass or excel; to be superior to someone in skill or ability. |He outmastered his opponent in the chess match. 他在象棋比赛中胜过了对手。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, outperform |反义词：be outmatched, be inferior to

She outmasters her colleagues in problem-solving skills. = 她在解决问题的能力方面胜过她的同事。
