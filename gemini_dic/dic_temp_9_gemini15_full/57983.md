drive-wall:  /ˈdraɪv wɔːl/ | n. |隔音墙，隔音屏障 | A drive-wall is a wall, typically made of concrete or other sound-dampening materials, designed to reduce or eliminate noise transmission between different areas.  They are commonly used in construction projects to mitigate noise pollution from roads, railways, or industrial facilities. | Example:  The new drive-wall significantly reduced the noise from the highway.  新的隔音墙大大降低了高速公路的噪音。|近义词：noise barrier, sound barrier, acoustic barrier | 反义词：sound amplifier, noise enhancer |
