interbelligerent:/ˌɪntərbeˈlɪdʒərənt/| adj.|交战的，处于战争状态的|Belonging to or characteristic of nations or groups engaged in war with each other.  The term often implies a state of ongoing conflict, rather than a single battle.|例句：The interbelligerent nations signed a truce. = 交战国签署了停战协议。

The interbelligerent countries struggled to find a lasting peace. = 交战的国家努力寻找持久的和平。|近义词： warring, belligerent, hostile|反义词：peaceful, amicable
