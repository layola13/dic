landscapers:/ˈlændˌskeɪpərz/| n.|园景设计师；园林工人|People who design, install, and maintain landscapes.  They work with plants, hardscapes (like patios and walkways), and other elements to create aesthetically pleasing and functional outdoor spaces.|例句：The landscapers are planting new shrubs in the front garden. 园林工人正在前花园种植新的灌木。|例句：Our landscapers redesigned our backyard into a beautiful oasis. 我们的园林设计师将我们的后院改造成美丽的绿洲。|近义词：gardeners, landscape architects, groundskeepers|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与破坏景观相关的人或事物)|名词
