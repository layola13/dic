nonsolicitously:/ˌnɒn səˈlɪsɪtəsli/ | adv. |未经请求地；主动地 |  Done or given without being asked for; unsolicited.  It implies an unwelcome or intrusive nature, often suggesting a lack of consideration for the recipient's wishes. | Example: He nonsolicitously offered his opinion on the matter. 他未经请求地就此事发表了自己的意见。 | 近义词: unsolicitedly, unasked, gratuitously | 反义词: solicitously, at someone's request


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  Instead, we'll show how it's used in different sentence structures.

Simple Sentence:
He nonsolicitously handed her a bouquet of flowers.  = 他主动地递给她一束花。

Complex Sentence:
Although she hadn't asked for advice, she received nonsolicitous comments about her appearance. = 尽管她没有寻求建议，但她收到了关于她外貌的主动评论。
