erythrolitmin:/ˌɛrɪθroʊˈlɪtmɪn/| n. | 赤藓苔藓素 | A red dye obtained from certain lichens, used as an acid-base indicator. It changes color depending on the pH of a solution, turning red in acidic solutions and blue in alkaline solutions. | Erythrolitmin is a useful indicator in titrations.  赤藓苔藓素在滴定中是一种有用的指示剂。|  litmus, azolitmin |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
