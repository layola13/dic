party:/ˈpɑːrti/ /ˈpɑːrɪ/| n. v.|聚会；党；派系；一群人 |a social gathering of people, often for a specific purpose or celebration; a political organization; a group of people sharing a common interest or characteristic |例句：We're having a party to celebrate my birthday. 我们将举办一个派对来庆祝我的生日。  They belong to the same political party. 他们属于同一个政党。|近义词：celebration, gathering, group, faction |反义词：solitude, isolation
