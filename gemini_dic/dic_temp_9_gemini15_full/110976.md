liquidating:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪŋ/| v.| 清算；变现；消灭 |  The process of winding up a business or organization by selling its assets and paying off its debts.  It can also refer to the act of eliminating something, often something undesirable. | 例句：The company is liquidating its assets to pay off its debts.  这家公司正在变卖资产以偿还债务。

The bank is liquidating the failing business. 银行正在清算这家即将倒闭的企业。


近义词：winding up, closing down, selling off, eliminating, destroying

反义词：establishing, building up, accumulating, preserving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is liquidating its inventory. = 公司正在清算其库存。
They are liquidating their assets to pay off their debts. = 他们正在变卖资产以偿还债务。


一般过去时:
The business liquidated its assets last year. = 该企业去年清算了其资产。
The government liquidated the rebel group. = 政府消灭了叛乱组织。


现在完成时:
The company has liquidated all its debts. = 公司已经清偿了所有债务。
They have liquidated their holdings in the stock market. = 他们已经清算了他们在股市的持股。


过去完成时:
The company had liquidated its assets before filing for bankruptcy. = 该公司在申请破产前已清算其资产。
They had liquidated their investment before the market crash. = 他们在市场崩盘前已经清算了他们的投资。


将来时:
The company will liquidate its remaining assets next month. = 公司将于下个月清算其剩余资产。
The government will liquidate the terrorist organization. = 政府将消灭恐怖组织。


虚拟语气:
If the company were to liquidate, many people would lose their jobs. = 如果公司要清算，许多人将会失业。
I wish they had liquidated their assets sooner. = 我希望他们早点清算资产。


被动语态:
The assets were liquidated quickly and efficiently. = 资产被迅速而有效地清算。
The rebel group was liquidated by the army. = 叛乱组织被军队消灭了。


疑问句:
Is the company liquidating? = 公司正在清算吗？
Will they be liquidating their business? = 他们会清算他们的生意吗？


否定句:
The company is not liquidating at this time. = 公司目前没有进行清算。
They did not liquidate their assets. = 他们没有清算他们的资产。


PHRASE->
liquidate assets = 清算资产
liquidate debt = 清偿债务
liquidate a company = 清算一家公司
liquidate a position = 清仓
liquidate holdings = 清算持股

词源:
From Latin *liquidāre "to make liquid," from liquidus "liquid."  The sense of "to convert into cash" developed in the 18th century, and the sense of "to destroy" in the 19th.
