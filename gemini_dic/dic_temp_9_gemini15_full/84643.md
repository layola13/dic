hand-to-hand:/ˌhænd tu ˈhænd/| adj.|肉搏战的，近距离的，直接的|Close combat; directly; without intermediary.|例句：The soldiers engaged in fierce hand-to-hand combat.  士兵们进行了激烈的肉搏战。|例句：The information was passed on hand-to-hand. 信息是直接传递的。|近义词：close-quarters, direct, personal|反义词：distant, remote, indirect
