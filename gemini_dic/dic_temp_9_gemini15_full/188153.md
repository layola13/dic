rout:/raʊt/| n., v.|溃败；彻底击败；驱逐；（使）仓皇逃窜|

英文解析:
Noun: A disorderly retreat of defeated troops or a mob; a complete defeat or overthrow.  As a verb, it means to defeat utterly; to drive out or expel; to cause to flee in disorder.

例句:
The army suffered a disastrous rout after the enemy's surprise attack.  敌军突袭后，我军溃不成军。

The rebels were routed by government forces.  政府军彻底击溃了叛军。


近义词:
defeat, overthrow, rout, drubbing, thrashing, massacre, slaughter, annihilation, decimation

反义词:
victory, triumph, win, success


词性:
Noun, Verb
