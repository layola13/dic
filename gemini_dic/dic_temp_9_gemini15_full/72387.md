fluctuant:/ˈflʌktʃuənt/| adj.|波动的；变化的；起伏不定的|Changing or varying irregularly; unsteady.|The fluctuating exchange rate made budgeting difficult.  波动汇率使得预算编制困难。The fluctuating price of oil affects the economy. 石油价格的波动影响着经济。|steady, constant, stable|stable, constant, steady


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The price fluctuates constantly. = 价格不断波动。
The market fluctuates throughout the day. = 市场全天都在波动。

一般过去时:
The temperature fluctuated wildly yesterday. = 昨天的温度剧烈波动。
The stock price fluctuated throughout the trading session. = 股票价格在整个交易时段内波动。

现在完成时:
The value of the currency has fluctuated significantly this year. = 今年货币价值已大幅波动。
The company's profits have fluctuated since the merger. = 自合并以来，公司的利润一直在波动。

过去完成时:
The river level had fluctuated dramatically before the flood. = 洪水来临之前，河流水位已剧烈波动。
The patient's blood pressure had fluctuated for days before the emergency. = 病人在紧急情况发生前的几天里血压一直波动。

将来时:
The weather is expected to fluctuate throughout the week. = 预计本周天气将波动。
The price of gold is likely to fluctuate in the coming months. = 黄金价格在未来几个月可能会波动。

虚拟语气:
If the economy fluctuated less, businesses would plan more effectively. = 如果经济波动较小，企业就能更有效地规划。
I wish the exchange rate didn't fluctuate so much. = 我希望汇率不要波动这么大。

被动语态:
The results were affected by the fluctuating data.  结果受到数据波动的影响。
The market is influenced by the fluctuating demand. 市场受需求波动的影响。

疑问句:
Does the temperature fluctuate frequently in this region? = 这个地区的气温经常波动吗？
Does the stock market always fluctuate? = 股市总是波动吗？

否定句:
The quality of the product doesn't fluctuate. = 产品质量没有波动。
The signal strength didn't fluctuate during the call. = 通话期间信号强度没有波动。
