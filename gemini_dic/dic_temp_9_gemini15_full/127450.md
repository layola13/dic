nebulation:/ˌnɛbjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |星云化；雾化；混浊；模糊|The process of forming or becoming a nebula;  the act of converting a substance into a fine mist or spray; a hazy or unclear state.  It can refer to the astronomical phenomenon of nebula formation, the medical process of atomizing a liquid, or a metaphorical state of confusion. |例句：The nebulation of the liquid medicine made it easier to inhale. 液态药物的雾化使其更容易吸入。

The nebulation of the distant galaxy was stunning. 遥远星系的星云化景象令人惊叹。|近义词：atomization, vaporization,  haze, fog, obscurity |反义词：clarity, lucidity, transparency
