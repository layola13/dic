flavourings:/ˈfleɪvərɪŋz/ | n. |调味料，香料 | Substances added to food or drink to give them a particular flavour.  They can be natural or artificial. | Example: The chef added various flavourings to enhance the taste of the dish.  厨师添加各种调味料来增强菜肴的味道。  The vanilla flavourings in the ice cream were artificial. 冰淇淋中的香草调味料是人工合成的。|近义词：seasonings, spices, additives |反义词：None (反义词不适用)|名词

词源:
The word "flavouring" comes from the noun "flavour," which ultimately derives from the Old French "flavor," meaning "taste, smell."  The suffix "-ing" indicates a gerund or noun of action.
