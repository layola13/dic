/ˈɛldərz/| n. |长老；长辈；前辈|Elders are respected members of a community, often holding positions of authority and wisdom.  Elders often provide guidance and support to younger generations. |The village elders met to discuss the upcoming harvest.  村里的长老们聚在一起讨论即将到来的收成。|senior, veteran, ancestor |junior, youngster
