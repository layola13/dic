They are typically opposed to radical or extremist ideologies and methods. | Example: The government's policies were supported by a coalition of nonradicals from across the political spectrum.  政府的政策得到了来自政治领域各方的温和派联盟的支持。|近义词：moderates, centrists, conservatives (in some contexts)| 反义词：radicals, extremists, revolutionaries | 名词
