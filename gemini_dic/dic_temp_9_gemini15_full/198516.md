shrive:/ʃraɪv/| vt.|赦免；忏悔；坦白；彻底搜查|To grant absolution to (someone) after confession of sins; to confess one's sins.  The word also means to search thoroughly. |例句：The priest shrived the penitent after hearing his confession.  = 牧师在听取忏悔者忏悔后赦免了他。|例句：The police shrived the house looking for the stolen jewels. = 警察彻底搜查了房子寻找被盗的珠宝。|近义词：absolve, forgive, confess, search|反义词：condemn, punish
