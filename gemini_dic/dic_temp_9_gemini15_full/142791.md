ouncer: /ˈaʊnsər/ | n. |盎司(重量单位)|An ouncer is a unit of weight equal to 1/16 of a pound (approximately 28.35 grams).  It's primarily used in the United States and some other countries, particularly for precious metals and some food items. |The gold bar weighed one ounce. 这块金条重一盎司。 He bought a one-ounce bag of coffee. 他买了一袋一盎司的咖啡。| gram, pound | kilogram | noun
