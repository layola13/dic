例句：The deli is famous for its delicious pastrami. 这家熟食店以其美味的熏牛肉而闻名。|近义词：corned beef,  smoked beef|反义词：无

词源:
The word "pastrami" is derived from the Romanian word "pastramă," which refers to a similar cured and smoked meat.  The word entered the English language via the Yiddish language of Eastern European Jewish communities, who brought the preparation method with them to America.
