re-silver:/ˌriːˈsɪlvər/| vt.|镀银，再镀银|To coat (something) again with silver.  This typically involves a process of removing existing silver plating (if present) and then applying a new layer. |例句：The antique candlestick was re-silvered to restore its original luster.  这支古董烛台重新镀银以恢复其原来的光泽。

The jeweler re-silvered the old locket, giving it new life. 珠宝匠重新为旧锁匙镀银，赋予它新的生命。|近义词：plate, resurface|反义词：strip (of silver), remove plating|动词
