reendorse:/ˌriːɪnˈdɔːrs/ | vt. |再次认可；重新背书；再次批准 | To endorse again; to give one's support or approval to something again.  |例句：The board decided to reendorse the CEO after a period of uncertainty. 董事会在经历一段不确定时期后决定再次支持首席执行官。

例句：The committee reendorsed the proposal after making some minor revisions. 委员会在进行了一些小的修改后再次批准了该提案。


近义词： reaffirm, ratify, endorse again, approve again | 反义词：reject, disapprove, revoke


词性：动词
