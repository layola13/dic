cavalcades:/ˌkævəlˈkeɪdz/| n.|骑兵队；盛大的行列；车队|A cavalcade is a procession or a series of things of the same type following one after the other.  It often implies a grand or impressive display, particularly of mounted figures. |Example: A cavalcade of knights rode through the town.  骑士队伍穿过城镇。 The film opens with a stunning cavalcade of images. 电影以一系列令人惊叹的画面开场。|procession, parade, cortege|disorder, chaos|名词
