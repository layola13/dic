remonetized: /ˌriːˈmʌnɪtaɪzd/ | v. | 重新货币化 | To make something that was not previously monetized, or to re-establish the monetization of something that had been demonetized.  This typically refers to reintroducing a system of payment or financial value to something, often digital content or services. | 例句：The video platform remonetized the channel after reviewing its content. = 在审查其内容后，视频平台重新将该频道货币化。


The platform decided to remonetize the user's videos after resolving the copyright issues. = 在解决版权问题后，平台决定重新将用户的视频货币化。| 近义词：re-monetize, revalue | 反义词：demonetize
