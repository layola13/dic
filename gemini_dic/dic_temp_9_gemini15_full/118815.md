metamorphoser: /ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəʊzər/ | n. | 变形者；蜕变者 | A person or thing that undergoes metamorphosis; an agent of metamorphosis.  | The caterpillar is a metamorphoser, transforming into a butterfly.  毛毛虫是变形者，它会变成蝴蝶。| transformer, changer, mutator |  (no direct antonym, but words implying lack of change could be considered opposites)| 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The caterpillar metamorphoses into a butterfly. = 毛毛虫蜕变成蝴蝶。
This machine metamorphoses raw materials into finished products. = 这台机器将原材料加工成成品。


一般过去时:
The tadpole metamorphosed into a frog. = 蝌蚪变成了青蛙。
The artist metamorphosed the ordinary into the extraordinary. = 艺术家将平凡的事物变成了非凡的事物。


现在完成时:
The larva has metamorphosed into a beautiful insect. = 幼虫已经蜕变成美丽的昆虫。
The city has metamorphosed significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这座城市发生了巨大的变化。


过去完成时:
The pupa had already metamorphosed before we arrived. =在我们到达之前，蛹已经蜕变了。
The company had metamorphosed completely by the time the new CEO took over. = 新任CEO接手时，公司已经彻底改头换面了。


将来时:
The egg will metamorphose into a larva. = 卵将会变成幼虫。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
