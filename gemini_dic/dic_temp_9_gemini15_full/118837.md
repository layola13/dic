metaphoric:/ˌmɛtəˈfɒrɪk/| adj.|比喻的，隐喻的|Figurative; expressing one thing in terms of another.  It involves an implied comparison, unlike a simile which uses "like" or "as".|The poem used metaphoric language to describe the speaker's grief. 这首诗用比喻的语言来描述说话者的悲伤。|figurative, symbolic, allegorical|literal, realistic|形容词
