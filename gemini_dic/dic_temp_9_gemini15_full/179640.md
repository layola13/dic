reendows:/ˌriːɪnˈdaʊz/ | vt. |重新赋予；再次授予 |To endow again; to provide with new or renewed gifts, qualities, or powers.  |The university reendowed the scholarship program after receiving a large donation. 大学在收到一笔巨额捐款后，重新为奖学金项目拨款。|近义词：reinstate, replenish, refresh |反义词：deprive, divest

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The foundation reendows the arts program annually. = 基金会每年都为艺术项目重新拨款。
He reendows his employees with new skills through training. = 他通过培训使他的员工掌握新的技能。


一般过去时:
The government reendowed the museum with historical artifacts. = 政府为博物馆重新提供了历史文物。
She reendowed her life with purpose after overcoming hardship. = 在克服困难之后，她的人生目标又焕发了生机。


现在完成时:
They have reendowed the church with a new organ. = 他们已经为教堂配备了一架新的管风琴。
The company has reendowed its employees with greater responsibilities. = 公司已经赋予其员工更大的责任。


过去完成时:
Before the renovation, they had reendowed the building with modern amenities. = 在翻新之前，他们已经为这座建筑配备了现代化设施。
Having reendowed himself with knowledge, he felt confident to face the challenge. = 在重新获得知识后，他充满信心去面对挑战。


将来时:
The philanthropist will reendow the hospital with state-of-the-art equipment. = 慈善家将为医院配备最先进的设备。
They will reendow the project with sufficient funding next year. = 他们明年将为该项目提供足够的资金。


虚拟语气:
If they reendowed the school with better resources, the students would perform better. = 如果他们为学校提供更好的资源，学生们的表现将会更好。
I wish they had reendowed the library with more books. = 我希望他们已经为图书馆增添了更多的书籍。


被动语态:
