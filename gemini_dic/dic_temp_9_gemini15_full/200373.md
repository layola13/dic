skyish:/ˈskaɪɪʃ/| adj.|天蓝色的，略带天色的|Slightly resembling the sky in color; bluish.  The word suggests a pale or light blue, not a deep or intense blue. It often implies a subtle or faint resemblance to the sky's color.|例句：The artist used skyish blues and whites in her painting.  艺术家在她的画中使用了天蓝色的蓝色和白色。


The skyish hue of the distant hills was beautiful. 远山略带天蓝色的色调很美。|近义词：bluish, pale blue, light blue|反义词：dark, deep, intense
