descanter:/dɪˈskæntər/| n. |酒瓶，醒酒器|A container, typically of glass, with a wide body and a narrow neck, used for decanting wine.  It's designed to allow sediment to settle while pouring.|例句：He carefully poured the wine into the decanter. 他小心地将酒倒入醒酒器中。|例句：The decanter sat proudly on the dining table.  醒酒器自豪地摆放在餐桌上。|近义词：carafe, bottle|反义词：N/A
