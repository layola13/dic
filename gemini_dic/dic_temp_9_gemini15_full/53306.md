disability:/ˌdɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |残疾；无能；无力 | A physical, mental, cognitive, or developmental condition that impairs, interferes with, or limits a person's ability to engage in certain tasks or actions.  It can range from mild to severe and affect various aspects of a person's life. |  He has a disability that limits his mobility. 他有残疾，行动不便。
