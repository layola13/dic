citizen-arrests: /ˈsɪtɪzən əˈrɛsts/ | n. | 公民逮捕 | The act of a private citizen detaining a person suspected of committing a crime, pending the arrival of law enforcement.  This right varies by jurisdiction and usually requires probable cause that a crime has been committed. |


例句：

The shop owner made a citizen's arrest of the thief who stole a necklace. = 商店老板对偷项链的小偷进行了公民逮捕。

A bystander performed a citizen arrest on the man assaulting a woman in the park. = 一位旁观者对在公园里殴打一名妇女的男子进行了公民逮捕。


近义词：detainment, apprehension


反义词：release, liberation


词性：名词
