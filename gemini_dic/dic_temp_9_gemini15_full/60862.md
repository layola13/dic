elegans:/ˈɛlɪɡəns/| adj.|优雅的；精致的；高雅的|Elegant means graceful and stylish in appearance or manner. It suggests refinement and sophistication.  The word often describes clothing, design, or behavior.|例句：She wore an elegant gown to the ball. 她穿着一件优雅的礼服去参加舞会。|例句：The restaurant had an elegant atmosphere. 这家餐厅有着优雅的氛围。|近义词：graceful, refined, sophisticated, stylish|反义词：gauche, clumsy, crude, unrefined|
