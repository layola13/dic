future:/ˈfjuːtʃər/| n. |未来，将来|The future is uncertain. 未来是不确定的。|近义词：tomorrow, hereafter |反义词：past, present


英文解析:  Future refers to the time that is to come; the period of time after the present. It can also refer to something that will or may happen or exist in the future.  It can be used as a noun, adjective, or even an adverb in some contexts.


例句:

What are your plans for the future? 你对未来有什么计划？

We must prepare for the future. 我们必须为未来做准备。



词性: noun
