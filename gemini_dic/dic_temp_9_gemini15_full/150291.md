panelling: /ˈpænəlɪŋ/ | n. |嵌板；镶板；壁板 | The decorative panelling in the dining room was exquisite. 餐厅里装饰性的嵌板非常精美。|近义词：paneling, wainscoting |反义词：None

This room features beautiful wood panelling. = 这个房间有美丽的木质嵌板。

词源:
From "panel" + "-ing".  "Panel" itself comes from Old French *pannel, meaning "a piece of cloth," ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to Latin pannus ("cloth"). The "-ing" suffix indicates the action or result of the process.
