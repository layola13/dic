replace:/rɪˈpleɪs/| vt.|替换，取代|to take the place of (someone or something)|The old building will be replaced by a new shopping mall. 旧建筑将被一座新的购物中心取代。|substitute, exchange|keep, retain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replace the old tires with new ones. = 他们用新轮胎替换旧轮胎。
He replaces the broken window pane. = 他更换了破损的窗格。


一般过去时:
She replaced the damaged book with a new one. = 她用一本新的书替换了损坏的那本。
The mechanic replaced the engine. = 技工更换了发动机。


现在完成时:
They have replaced all the faulty components. = 他们已经更换了所有有故障的部件。
We have replaced the old system with a more efficient one. = 我们已经用更高效的系统取代了旧系统。


过去完成时:
He had replaced the battery before the journey. = 出发前他已经换了电池。
She had replaced the broken vase before her guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经换掉了破碎的花瓶。


将来时:
They will replace the damaged roof tiles next week. = 他们下周将更换损坏的屋顶瓦片。
We will replace the outdated software soon. = 我们很快就会更换过时的软件。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would replace my old car. = 如果我有钱，我会换掉我的旧车。
I wish I could replace this broken chair. = 我希望我能换掉这把坏椅子。


被动语态:
The broken parts will be replaced tomorrow. = 破损的零件明天将被更换。
The old equipment is being replaced with new technology. = 旧设备正在被新技术取代。


疑问句:
Will they replace the damaged equipment? = 他们会更换损坏的设备吗？
Can you replace the missing document? = 你能替换丢失的文件吗？


否定句:
They didn't replace the broken lightbulb. = 他们没有更换坏掉的灯泡。
He wouldn't replace the old furniture. = 他不愿意更换旧家具。


PHRASE->
replace with = 用…替换
replace A with B = 用B替换A
in place of = 代替
instead of = 代替
to the replacement of = 取代
