sided:/ˈsaɪdɪd/| adj.| 面的；有…边的；偏袒的 | Having or involving a specified number of sides.  This word is usually used in compound words to describe the number of sides something has. |例句：The building is three-sided. 这座建筑物有三面。  The polygon is many-sided. 这个多边形有很多边。|近义词：faced, many-faceted |反义词：unbiased, impartial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The coin is two-sided. = 这枚硬币有两面。
The argument is one-sided. = 争论是一面倒的。

一般过去时:
The debate was one-sided. = 辩论是一面倒的。
The box was three-sided. = 箱子有三面。

现在完成时:
The project has become one-sided. = 项目变得一面倒了。
The discussion has been two-sided and fair. = 讨论是双向且公平的。


过去完成时:
The decision had already been one-sided before the meeting. = 会议之前决定就已经是一面倒的了。
The game had been one-sided until the last minute. = 直到最后一刻比赛才一边倒。


将来时:
The presentation will be two-sided, showing both pros and cons. = 演示将是双面的，展示优缺点。
The negotiations will likely be one-sided. = 谈判很可能是一面倒的。


虚拟语气:
If the issue were two-sided, we could find a compromise. = 如果这个问题是双面的，我们可以找到一个妥协方案。
I wish the debate were less one-sided. = 我希望这场辩论不那么一面倒。


被动语态:
The issue was presented in a one-sided manner. = 这个问题以片面的方式呈现。
The argument was perceived as two-sided by the audience. = 观众认为这场争论是双面的。


疑问句:
Is the contract one-sided? = 合同是一面倒的吗？
Are the arguments two-sided and balanced? = 论点是双面的且平衡的吗？


否定句:
The approach wasn't one-sided; it considered all perspectives. = 这种方法并非片面的；它考虑了所有观点。
The painting isn't two-sided; it's a single panel. = 这幅画不是双面的；它是一个单面板。


PHRASE->
one-sided = 片面的，一边倒的
two-sided = 双面的，两面的
three-sided = 三面的
many-sided = 多面的，多方面的
multi-sided = 多面的，多方面的
