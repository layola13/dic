photocontrol: /ˈfoʊtoʊkənˈtroʊl/ | n. | 光控，光调节 | The control of a process or system by the use of light.  This often involves using light-sensitive devices to regulate the operation of something. Examples include light-activated switches, automatic exposure control in cameras, and automated street lighting. | Example: The greenhouse uses a sophisticated photocontrol system to regulate the amount of light plants receive. 温室使用一套复杂的灯光控制系统来调节植物接收的光量。|近义词：light control, photosensitivity |反义词：manual control
