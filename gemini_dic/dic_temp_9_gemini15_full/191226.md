scars:/skɑːrz/| n.|伤疤，创伤，痕迹|Marks left on the skin after an injury has healed;  a lasting effect of a painful experience. |例句：The accident left him with several scars on his face.  事故在他的脸上留下几道疤痕。|例句：The war left deep scars on the nation's psyche.  战争给国家的心理留下了深深的创伤。|近义词：marks, blemishes, wounds, trauma|反义词：smooth skin, unblemished skin, flawless skin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has many scars on his arms. = 他胳膊上有很多伤疤。
The event leaves a scar on her memory. = 这件事在她记忆中留下阴影。

一般过去时:
The surgery left a small scar. = 手术留下了一道小疤痕。
The argument left a scar on their relationship. = 那次争吵给他们的关系留下了裂痕。

现在完成时:
She has overcome the scars of her past. = 她已经克服了过去的创伤。
The community has begun to heal from the scars of the disaster. = 社区已经开始从灾难的创伤中恢复过来。

过去完成时:
By the time he was 20, he had acquired many scars from his adventurous life. = 到他20岁的时候，他那充满冒险的生活已经给他留下了许多伤疤。
Before the therapy, she had carried the emotional scars of abuse for years. = 在治疗之前，她多年来一直背负着虐待带来的情感创伤。

将来时:
These wounds will eventually become scars. = 这些伤口最终会变成疤痕。
Time will eventually heal these emotional scars. = 时间最终会治愈这些情感创伤。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't fallen, I wouldn't have this scar. = 如果我没有摔倒，我就不会有这道疤痕。
If she had received proper support, she might not carry these emotional scars. = 如果她得到适当的支持，她可能就不会背负这些情感创伤。

被动语态:
His face was scarred in the accident. = 他的脸在事故中留下了疤痕。
His reputation was scarred by the scandal. = 他的名誉被丑闻玷污了。

疑问句:
Does this scar hurt? = 这道疤痕疼吗？
Do these emotional scars affect your daily life? = 这些情感创伤会影响你的日常生活吗？

否定句:
He doesn't have any visible scars. = 他没有任何明显的伤疤。
The experience didn't leave any lasting emotional scars. = 那次经历没有留下任何持久的情感创伤。
