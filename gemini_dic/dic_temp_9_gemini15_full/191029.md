scampers:/ˈskæmpərz/| v.|轻快地跑，蹦蹦跳跳地跑|To run or move quickly and lightly.|The children scampered across the playground. 孩子们在操场上蹦蹦跳跳地跑。|The rabbit scampered into the bushes. 兔子蹦蹦跳跳地跑进了灌木丛。|dash,  hurry,  rush|walk slowly, crawl|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scampers across the field every morning. 他每天早上都在田野里蹦蹦跳跳地跑。
The squirrels scamper up the trees. 松鼠们快速地爬上树。

一般过去时:
The dog scampered after the ball. 狗追着球蹦蹦跳跳地跑。
She scampered down the stairs. 她轻快地跑下楼梯。

现在完成时:
The mouse has scampered away. 老鼠已经逃跑了。
He has scampered up the mountain. 他已经跑上山了。


过去完成时:
The bird had scampered away before I could catch it.  在我能够抓住它之前，鸟已经跑掉了。
They had scampered to safety before the storm hit.暴风雨来临之前，他们已经跑到安全的地方了。

将来时:
The children will scamper through the park. 孩子们将在公园里蹦蹦跳跳地跑。
She will scamper to answer the phone. 她会跑过去接电话。

虚拟语气:
If I were a child, I would scamper all day long. 如果我是一个孩子，我会整天蹦蹦跳跳地跑。
I wish I could scamper like a deer. 我希望我能像鹿一样快速奔跑。

被动语态: (被动语态较少用在此词上)
The ball was scampered after by the dog. 球被狗追着跑。(不太自然)
The path was scampered along by the tourists.游客们在小路上快速跑过。(不太自然)


疑问句:
Does the kitten scamper around the house? 小猫是否在房子里跑来跑去？
Will the children scamper across the street? 孩子们会跑过马路吗？

否定句:
The cat didn't scamper away. 猫没有跑掉。
He didn't scamper to answer the door. 他没有跑过去开门。
