colourism:/ˈkʌləˌrɪzəm/| n. |肤色歧视；重色轻肤|The prejudice or discrimination based on the social stratification of skin color, typically favoring lighter skin tones over darker ones.|  例句：Colourism is a pervasive issue in many societies, leading to inequality and discrimination. 肤色歧视在许多社会中是一个普遍存在的问题，导致不平等和歧视。


例句：The company's hiring practices were criticized for evidence of colourism. 该公司的招聘做法因存在肤色歧视的证据而受到批评。


近义词：skin-color bias, racial prejudice, discrimination


反义词：colour-blindness, equality, fairness


词性:名词
