sketching:/ˈskɛtʃɪŋ/ | v. |素描，速写，勾勒 |To make a rough drawing of something.  It often involves quick, loose lines and doesn't aim for perfect detail. |She was sketching the landscape from the window. 她正在从窗户上速写风景。 |drawing, outlining, drafting|painting, sculpting|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am sketching a portrait of my friend. = 我正在为我的朋友画肖像。
He sketches landscapes every weekend. = 他每个周末都速写风景。

一般过去时:
She sketched a quick design for the new building. = 她为新建筑快速勾勒了一个设计。
They sketched out the plan during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间草拟了计划。

现在完成时:
I have sketched several designs for the project. = 我已经为这个项目画了几张设计图。
She has sketched the main characters for her new novel. = 她已经为她的新小说勾勒了主要人物。

过去完成时:
He had sketched the outline before he started painting. = 他在开始绘画之前已经勾勒出了轮廓。
They had sketched the basic idea before presenting it to the client. = 他们在向客户展示之前已经勾画了基本思路。

将来时:
I will sketch the scene from memory. = 我将凭记忆速写这个场景。
We will be sketching in the park tomorrow. = 我们明天将在公园里写生。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sketch more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地画速写。
I wish I could sketch as well as he does. = 我希望我的绘画技术和他一样好。

被动语态:
The scene was sketched beautifully by the artist. = 这个场景被艺术家精美地勾勒出来了。
The plan was sketched out in detail during the meeting. = 计划在会议期间被详细地勾勒出来了。

疑问句:
Are you sketching anything interesting? = 你在画什么有趣的东西吗？
What are you sketching? = 你在画什么？

否定句:
I cannot sketch very well. = 我不太擅长画画。
He didn't sketch the details. = 他没有画细节。
