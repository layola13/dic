moorpan:/ˈmʊrpæn/| n.|沼泽地，泥炭沼泽|A moorpan is a hard, impervious layer in the soil profile of some moorland soils, typically composed of iron and organic matter.  It restricts drainage and can lead to waterlogging.|The moorpan prevented the water from draining properly, leading to waterlogged conditions.|近义词：hardpan, iron pan|反义词: permeable layer
