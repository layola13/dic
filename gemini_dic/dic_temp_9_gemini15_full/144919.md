over-talks:/ˈoʊvərˈtɔːks/ | v. |喋喋不休；说得过多 |To talk excessively or for too long; to speak more than is necessary or desirable.  |例句：He over-talks at meetings, making it difficult for others to contribute. 他在会议上喋喋不休，使其他人难以发表意见。

例句：She over-talked about her vacation, boring everyone in the room. 她对她的假期喋喋不休，让房间里的每个人都感到厌烦。


近义词：babble, chatter, gabble, prattle, ramble


反义词：be silent, be brief, be concise


词性:动词
