meshed: /mɛʃt/ | adj. | 交织的；纠缠的；网状的 |  Intertwined or interconnected; resembling a mesh or net.  | 例句：The wires were meshed together. 电线交织在一起。

The threads of the fabric were finely meshed. 这块布的线织得很细密。


近义词：interwoven, tangled, interconnected, networked | 反义词：separated, disentangled, unconnected


词源:  From Middle English *meschen, from Old French *mescier, from Late Latin *mitiscare "to mix," from Latin *miscere "to mix."  The original sense related to mixing or mingling, evolving to its current connotations of interconnectedness and network-like structures.
