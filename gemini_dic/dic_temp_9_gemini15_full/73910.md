forstall:/fɔːˈstɔːl/| vt.|阻止；预先阻止；抢先；囤积|To prevent something from happening by taking action in advance.|The government took measures to forstall a crisis. 政府采取措施预防危机。|近义词：prevent, avert, anticipate, preempt|反义词：facilitate, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forstalls problems before they arise. 他在问题出现之前就阻止了它们。
They forstall the inevitable by delaying the announcement. 他们通过延迟公告来阻止不可避免的事情发生。


一般过去时:
The quick action forestalled a potential disaster. 快速行动阻止了一场潜在的灾难。
She forestalled his anger by apologizing immediately. 她立刻道歉以避免他的愤怒。


现在完成时:
We have forestalled many difficulties through careful planning. 通过仔细的计划，我们已经避免了许多困难。
The company has forestalled a lawsuit by settling out of court. 公司通过庭外和解避免了一场诉讼。


过去完成时:

They had forestalled the spread of the disease by implementing strict quarantine measures. 他们通过实施严格的检疫措施阻止了疾病的蔓延。

虚拟语气:
