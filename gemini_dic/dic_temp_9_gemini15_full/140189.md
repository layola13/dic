offhandish:/ˌɒfˈhændɪʃ/| adj. |唐突的；鲁莽的；不经意的；粗鲁的 |Discourteous or rude in a casual or thoughtless way.  Showing a lack of consideration or forethought. |例句：His offhandish remarks offended several guests. 他不经意的言论冒犯了几位客人。

例句：Her offhandish manner made it clear she wasn't interested in our project. 她漫不经心的态度表明她对我们的项目不感兴趣。


近义词：abrupt, brusque, curt, blunt, discourteous, rude, impolite, thoughtless, inconsiderate

反义词：considerate, polite, courteous, thoughtful, respectful


词源:  The word "offhandish" is derived from "offhand," which means impromptu or without prior thought. The suffix "-ish" adds a slightly derogatory or less intense connotation.  Essentially, it implies a degree of rudeness or thoughtlessness that falls short of being fully deliberate or malicious.
