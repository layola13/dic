hylopathist:/ˌhaɪloʊˈpæθɪst/| n. |森林病理学家|A hylopathist is a specialist in the diseases of trees and forests. They study the causes, effects, and prevention of diseases affecting trees and forest ecosystems.|例句：The hylopathist examined the diseased trees to determine the cause of the blight.  森林病理学家检查了患病的树木以确定枯萎病的原因。|近义词：forest pathologist, arboriculturist|反义词：无
