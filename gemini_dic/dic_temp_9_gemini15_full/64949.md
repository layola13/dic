esters:/ˈɛstərz/| n.|酯类|Esters are organic compounds that are derived from an acid (organic or inorganic) in which at least one –OH (hydroxyl) group is replaced by an –O–alkyl (alkoxy) group. They are commonly found in many fruits and flowers, contributing to their characteristic scents and flavors.  Esters are also used extensively in various industrial applications, such as solvents, plasticizers, and synthetic fragrances.|例句：Many fruits contain esters, which give them their pleasant aromas. 许多水果含有酯类，赋予它们宜人的香气。|例句：The synthesis of esters involves a reaction between a carboxylic acid and an alcohol. 酯的合成涉及羧酸和醇之间的反应。|近义词：organic compound|反义词：无
