eggplants: /ˈɛɡplænts/ | n. |茄子 |A large, dark-purple fruit that is eaten as a vegetable.  It has a smooth, glossy skin and a soft, spongy flesh.  Eggplants are used in many cuisines around the world. |例句：I love to eat eggplants in stir-fries.  我喜欢在炒菜里吃茄子。

例句：She used eggplants to make baba ghanoush. 她用茄子做了巴巴甘努什。


近义词：aubergine (British English)


反义词：无


词性：名词
