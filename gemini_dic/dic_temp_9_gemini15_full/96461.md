individualisms: /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəm/ | n. | 个人主义；个人英雄主义 | The belief that the individual should be free to act as they choose, without unnecessary interference from society or the state.  It emphasizes individual rights, freedoms, and self-reliance.  This can manifest in various ways, from political ideologies to artistic expressions. |


例句：

The rise of individualism in the 19th century led to significant social and political changes.  19世纪个人主义的兴起导致了重大的社会和政治变革。

Her paintings reflect a strong sense of individualism, showcasing unique perspectives and techniques. 她的画作体现了强烈的个人主义，展现了独特的视角和技巧。


近义词：egoism, self-reliance, autonomy


反义词：collectivism, conformity, socialism


词性：名词
