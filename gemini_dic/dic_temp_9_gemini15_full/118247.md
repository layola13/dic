merch:/mɜːrtʃ/| n. |商品，货物| merchandise; goods for sale, especially in large quantities.  |The band sold a lot of merch at their concert. 乐队在演唱会上卖了很多商品。|goods, wares, products|services, intangibles| n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store sells a lot of merch. = 这家商店卖很多商品。
We stock a wide range of merch. = 我们库存各种各样的商品。

一般过去时:
They bought some merch at the festival. = 他们在节日上买了一些商品。
The company launched new merch last month. = 该公司上个月推出了新的商品。

现在完成时:
I have already bought all the merch I wanted. = 我已经买了我想要的所有商品。
She has collected a lot of band merch over the years. = 多年来她收集了很多乐队的商品。

过去完成时:
He had already sold out of merch before the concert ended. = 演唱会结束之前，他已经卖光了商品。
They had ordered more merch before the demand increased. = 在需求增加之前，他们已经订购了更多商品。

将来时:
We will sell our new merch online. = 我们将在网上销售我们的新商品。
They will announce the new merch next week. = 他们下周将宣布新的商品。

虚拟语气:
If we had more merch, we could sell it at a lower price. = 如果我们有更多商品，我们可以以更低的价格出售。
I wish I had bought that limited edition merch. = 我真希望我买了那件限量版商品。

被动语态:
The merch was displayed beautifully in the store. = 商品在商店里被漂亮地展示出来。
New merch is being prepared for the upcoming tour. = 正在为即将到来的巡演准备新的商品。

疑问句:
What kind of merch do you sell? = 你卖什么商品？
Where can I buy the band's merch? = 我在哪里可以买到乐队的商品？

否定句:
They don't sell that kind of merch anymore. = 他们不再卖那种商品了。
We didn't have enough merch to meet the demand. = 我们没有足够的商品来满足需求。
