krystal:/ˈkrɪstəl/| n.|水晶；结晶体；水晶玻璃制品|A clear and colorless crystalline substance, typically quartz.  It is often used in jewelry and other decorative items. Also refers to something clear and transparent like crystal glass.|例句：The chandelier was made of sparkling crystal.  这盏枝形吊灯是用闪闪发光的水晶制成的。

例句：The scientist examined the crystal structure under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下检查晶体结构。|近义词：quartz, gemstone, glass|反义词：opaque, cloudy, dull|名词
