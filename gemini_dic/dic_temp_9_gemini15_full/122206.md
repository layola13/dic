misusing:/ˌmɪsˈjuːzɪŋ/| v. |滥用；误用；错用| To use something incorrectly, inappropriately, or for a wrong purpose.  |例句：He was accused of misusing company funds. 他被指控挪用公司资金。|例句：She was misusing her position of power. 她滥用职权。|近义词：abusing, misapplying, mishandling |反义词：using correctly, employing properly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are misusing the software. = 他们正在滥用软件。
He misuses his influence. = 他滥用他的影响力。

一般过去时:
She misused her authority. = 她滥用职权。
They misused the equipment. = 他们误用了设备。

现在完成时:
We have misused the resources. = 我们滥用了资源。
They have been misusing the system for months. = 几个月来他们一直在滥用系统。

过去完成时:
He had misused the funds before the audit. = 审计之前，他已经挪用了资金。
The company had been misusing its patents for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在滥用其专利。

将来时:
They will misuse the opportunity. = 他们将会错失良机。(这里misuse 指的是“错失”，并非完全符合滥用定义，但可以作为例句)
She will continue misusing the tools. = 她将继续误用工具。


虚拟语气:
If he misused the data, he would be fired. = 如果他滥用数据，他就会被解雇。
I wish they hadn't misused the information. = 我真希望他们没有滥用信息。

被动语态:
The funds were misused by the manager. = 资金被经理挪用了。
The system is being misused by unauthorized users. = 该系统正被未经授权的用户滥用。

疑问句:
Are they misusing the equipment? = 他们是否在误用设备？
How are you misusing the software? = 你是如何滥用软件的？

否定句:
He didn't misuse the information. = 他没有滥用信息。
They were not misusing the resources. = 他们没有滥用资源。


PHRASE->
