furthermore:/ˈfɜːðərmɔːr/|/ˈfɜːðərˌmɔːr/| adv.|此外；而且；再者|In addition; besides; moreover.  It is used to introduce additional information or arguments to support a previous statement.|例句：The house is beautiful; furthermore, it's in a great location. 这房子很漂亮；此外，地理位置也很好。

例句：He is intelligent and, furthermore, he is very hardworking. 他很聪明，而且非常努力。


近义词：also, besides, moreover, in addition, additionally|反义词：however, nevertheless, nonetheless, on the other hand|词性：副词
