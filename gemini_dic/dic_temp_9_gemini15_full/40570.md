contuse:/kənˈtuːz/| vt. |挫伤；打伤；使瘀伤 |To injure (a part of the body) by a blow or pressure without breaking the skin.  |The fall contused his shoulder.  摔倒挫伤了他的肩膀。| bruise, injure |heal, mend|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contuses his knee playing football. = 他踢足球时挫伤了膝盖。
The boxer contuses his opponent's ribs. = 拳击手打伤了对手的肋骨。


一般过去时:
She contused her ankle during the hike. = 她在徒步旅行中挫伤了脚踝。
The accident contused his face. = 那次事故使他的脸受伤了。
