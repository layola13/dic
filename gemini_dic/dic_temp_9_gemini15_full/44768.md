crump:/krʌmp/| v.|嘎吱作响；压碎；倒塌|To make a short, sharp, crushing sound; to crush or crumble something.|例句：The old wooden floor crumped under his weight.  古老的木地板在他体重下嘎吱作响。|例句：The biscuit crumped easily in my hand.  饼干在我的手中很容易压碎。|近义词：crunch, crack, crumble|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to the opposite action, like building or strengthening.|
