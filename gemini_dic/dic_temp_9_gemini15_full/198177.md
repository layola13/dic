short:/ʃɔːrt/ /ʃɔːrt/| adj.| 短的，矮的；简洁的；短期的 |Short describes something that has little length or duration. It can refer to physical objects, time periods, or even written or spoken communication.|例句：The movie was short and sweet. 这部电影短小精悍。

The athlete is short for his age.  这位运动员相比同龄人个子矮。|近义词：brief, concise, succinct |反义词：long, tall, lengthy


USAGE->

The road is short. = 这条路很短。
The speech is short. = 这篇演讲很简短。

一般过去时:
