prongs:/prɒŋz/| n.|尖头；齿；叉；(尤指)三叉戟的尖头|The pointed ends of a fork, or similar object.  It often refers to three or more points, especially those that are sharp or pointed.|例句：The trident has three sharp prongs.  这把三叉戟有三个尖头。

例句：The pitchfork had several strong prongs. 这把干草叉有好几个结实的叉齿。


近义词：points, tines, spikes, teeth|反义词：blunt ends, rounded ends|词性:名词
