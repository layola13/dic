inopercular: /ˌɪnɒpərˈkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. |  非鳃盖的 |  Relating to or situated outside the operculum (the gill cover of a fish).  This term is primarily used in zoology and ichthyology to describe anatomical structures or locations not associated with the operculum. | Example: The inopercular bone is located behind the operculum.  这条鱼的非鳃盖骨位于鳃盖之后。| 近义词: extraopercular | 反义词: opercular
