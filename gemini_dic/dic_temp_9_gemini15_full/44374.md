cross-cultural:/ˌkrɒsˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj. |跨文化的；不同文化的 | Relating to or involving different cultures.  It refers to interactions, comparisons, and relationships between people from different cultural backgrounds.  | Example: The company offers cross-cultural training to its employees. 这家公司为其员工提供跨文化培训。|近义词：intercultural, multinational |反义词：monocultural
