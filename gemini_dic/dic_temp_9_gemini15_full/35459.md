coach-load: /ˈkoʊtʃˌloʊd/ | n. |  满载的客车；一车人 | A coach-load refers to the number of people that a coach (bus) can carry, often used to describe a large group of people traveling together.  |  Example: A coach-load of tourists arrived at the museum. 一车游客到达了博物馆。 | 近义词：busload, group, party | 反义词：a few, handful
