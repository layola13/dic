outearns:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrnz/| vt.|赚得比…多；比…挣钱多|To earn more money than someone else.  This verb is formed by combining the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent") with the verb "earns."|例句：He outearns his wife by a considerable margin. 他比他妻子赚得多得多。

例句：She consistently outearns her colleagues in the sales department. 她在销售部门一直比她的同事赚得多。


近义词：out-earn, surpass, exceed,  top


反义词：under-earn, earn less than


词性：动词
