fisherwomen:/ˈfɪʃərˌwɪmɪn/| n.|女渔民|Women who fish for a living or as a hobby.  Fisherwomen often work in challenging conditions, using various techniques and tools to catch fish.|例句：The fisherwomen returned to port with their day's catch.  那些女渔民带着一天的渔获回到了港口。|例句：Many fisherwomen in this coastal village have been fishing for generations.  这个沿海村庄的许多女渔民世世代代都在捕鱼。|近义词：fishwives (more informal)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with office workers, farmers etc.|名词
