overmanages:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒɪz/| v.|过度管理，管理过度|The verb "overmanages" describes a situation where someone excessively controls or interferes in the affairs of others or a project, often to the detriment of efficiency and morale. It implies too much management and intervention.|例句：The project failed because the manager overmanages every aspect, stifling creativity and innovation.  项目失败是因为经理过度管理各个方面，扼杀了创造力和创新。


The CEO overmanages the marketing team, leading to burnout and decreased productivity.  首席执行官过度管理营销团队，导致倦怠和生产力下降。|近义词：micromanages, excessively controls, overcontrols|反义词：undermanages, delegates effectively, trusts employees


USAGE->

He overmanages his employees. = 他过度管理他的员工。
She overmanages every detail of the event. = 她过度管理活动的每一个细节。


一般过去时:

They overmanaged the budget, resulting in cost overruns. = 他们过度管理预算，导致成本超支。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

词源:

The word "overmanages" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") with the verb "manages" (from "manage," meaning to control or direct).  Therefore, it literally means "to manage excessively."
