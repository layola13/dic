multiarticulated: /ˌmʌltiˈɑːrtɪkjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 多关节的，多节的 | Having many joints or articulations.  This term is used to describe structures, mechanisms, or systems composed of numerous interconnected parts, allowing for complex movement and flexibility. | Example: The robot's multiarticulated arm allowed it to reach into tight spaces.  这个机器人的多关节手臂使它能够到达狭小的空间。|近义词: articulated, jointed, segmented | 反义词: single-jointed, monolithic
