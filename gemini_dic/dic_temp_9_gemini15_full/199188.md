silicule:/ˈsɪlɪkjuːl/| n. |短角果|A silicule is a type of fruit, a short, broad siliqua (a dry dehiscent fruit that splits along two sutures), usually less than twice as long as broad.  It's characteristic of certain plants in the Brassicaceae (mustard) family.|The shepherd's purse plant produces silicules.|近义词：silique (but longer)|反义词：None specifically, but  "long silique" could be considered an antonym.
