multi-circuited:/ˌmʌltiˈsɜːkɪtɪd/ | adj. |多电路的，多回路的 | Having or involving multiple circuits.  This term often refers to electronic systems or networks with more than one path for electrical current to flow.  | Example: The multi-circuited system ensures redundancy and high availability.  这个多电路系统确保了冗余和高可用性。 |近义词: multipath, redundant |反义词: single-circuited
