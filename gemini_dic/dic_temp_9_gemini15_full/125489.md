multipurpose:/ˌmʌltiˈpɜːrpəs/| adj.|多用途的，多功能的|Having or serving many purposes.  This word combines "multi-" (meaning many) and "purpose" (meaning aim or intention).|例句：This multipurpose tool is perfect for camping.  这个多用途工具非常适合野营。

例句：She bought a multipurpose cleaner that can be used on various surfaces. 她买了一种多用途清洁剂，可以用在各种表面。


近义词：versatile, all-purpose, multifunctional


反义词：single-purpose, specialized


词性:形容词
