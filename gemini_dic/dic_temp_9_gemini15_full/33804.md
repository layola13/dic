circumferentor:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfɛrəntɔːr/| n. |经纬仪；测角仪|A circumferentor is a surveying instrument used to measure horizontal angles.  It's a simpler, older version of a theodolite.  It consists of a magnetic compass combined with a sighting device. |The surveyor used a circumferentor to accurately measure the angles of the property.  测量员用经纬仪精确测量了该物业的角度。|theodolite, compass |(no direct antonym)|名词
