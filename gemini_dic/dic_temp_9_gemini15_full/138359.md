notified:/ˈnoʊtɪfaɪd/ | v. | 通知，告知 | To inform someone of something officially or formally.  |例句：I was notified of the change in schedule. 我被告知了日程的变更。|  例句：The company notified its customers of the product recall. 公司通知其客户召回产品。|近义词：informed, advised, alerted |反义词：concealed, hidden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school notifies parents of important events. 学校通知家长重要事件。
He notifies his boss of his arrival. 他通知老板他已到达。


一般过去时:
She notified me of the accident. 她通知我发生了事故。
They notified the authorities immediately. 他们立即通知了当局。


现在完成时:
I have notified them of my decision. 我已经通知他们我的决定。
Have you notified the bank of your change of address? 你已经通知银行你的地址变更了吗？


过去完成时:
He had notified the police before the incident occurred.  事故发生前他已经通知了警方。
They had notified the tenants of the repairs. 他们已经通知房客维修事宜。


将来时:
I will notify you as soon as possible. 我会尽快通知你。
The company will notify customers of any delays. 公司将通知客户任何延误。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would notify the insurance company immediately. 如果我是你，我会立即通知保险公司。
I wish I had notified him earlier. 我希望我早点通知他。


被动语态:
The customers were notified of the price increase. 客户被告知价格上涨。
He was notified that his application was approved. 他被告知他的申请获批了。


疑问句:
Have you been notified of the meeting? 你被告知会议了吗？
When will we be notified of the results? 我们什么时候会被告知结果？


否定句:
I haven't been notified yet. 我还没有收到通知。
They didn't notify the press about the investigation. 他们没有将调查结果通知媒体。


PHRASE->
be notified of: 被告知…
notify sb of sth: 通知某人某事
notify sb that: 通知某人…
receive notification: 收到通知
give notification:发出通知
