grader:/ˈɡreɪdər/| n. |评分者；分级器；分级机|A person or thing that grades or assigns grades.  This can refer to a person who assesses and assigns grades to students' work, or a machine that sorts or separates items based on size or quality.|例句：The teacher is a diligent grader, carefully reviewing each student's paper.  老师是一位勤奋的评分者，仔细检查每位学生的试卷。

例句：The factory uses a sophisticated grader to sort the oranges by size. 工厂使用精密的分级机按大小分拣橘子。
