carpetway:/ˈkɑːrpɪtweɪ/ | n. |地毯路，地毯通道 | A covered walkway, often found in airports or other large buildings, that is covered with carpeting.  It provides a comfortable and often quieter way to travel between areas. | Example: The airport's carpetway connected the terminal to the gates.  机场的地毯通道连接着航站楼和登机口。|近义词：conveyor, walkway, passage |反义词：open air path
