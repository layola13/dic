leap-in-the-dark:/ˌliːpɪnðədɑːrk/| n. |孤注一掷；冒险行动；盲目尝试|An action taken without knowing the consequences or outcome.  It implies a significant risk and uncertainty.|例句：Investing all his savings in the startup was a leap in the dark.  把所有的积蓄都投资到这家初创公司是一次孤注一掷。

例句：He took a leap in the dark by quitting his job without another lined up. 他冒险辞去了工作，而没有找到另一份工作。


近义词：gamble, risk, venture, plunge


反义词：calculated move, sure thing, safe bet


词性:名词
