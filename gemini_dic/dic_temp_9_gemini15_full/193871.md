self-disgrace: /ˌsɛlf dɪsˈɡreɪs/ | n. | 自辱；自我贬低 | The act of bringing shame or dishonor upon oneself through one's own actions or behavior.  It implies a deliberate or careless act that results in loss of respect or reputation. |例句：His public outburst was a shocking act of self-disgrace.  他的公开爆发是令人震惊的自我羞辱行为。

His constant lying led to his eventual self-disgrace. 他持续的谎言最终导致了他的自我贬低。


近义词：humiliation, shame, disgrace, dishonor, degradation

反义词：honor, glory, pride, self-respect
