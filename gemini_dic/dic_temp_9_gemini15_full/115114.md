manges:/mændʒɪz/ | v. | 管理；处理；吃 | To manage something means to control or be in charge of it, often successfully.  To manage also means to eat something. |  例句：He manages a large team of software engineers. 他管理着一个庞大的软件工程师团队。


例句：She manages to eat a whole pizza by herself. 她自己吃掉了一整个披萨。


近义词：administer, supervise, direct, govern, consume, eat


反义词：neglect, abandon, ignore, starve


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manages the project effectively. = 他有效地管理着这个项目。
She manages to finish her work on time. = 她设法按时完成了工作。


一般过去时:
They managed the company for many years. = 他们管理这家公司很多年了。
He managed to escape the burning building. = 他设法从燃烧的建筑物中逃了出来。


现在完成时:
She has managed the department since 2010. = 自2010年以来，她一直管理着这个部门。
He has managed to learn three languages. = 他已经学会了三种语言。


过去完成时:
They had managed the crisis before it escalated. = 他们在危机升级之前已经控制住了局面。
She had managed to save enough money for a down payment. = 她已经攒够了首付的钱。


将来时:
He will manage the new branch office. = 他将管理新的分公司。
She will manage to get there before dark. = 她将设法在天黑前赶到那里。


虚拟语气:
If he managed the team better, they would be more productive. = 如果他更好地管理团队，他们的生产力就会更高。
I wish I had managed to attend the conference. = 我真希望我能参加这次会议。


被动语态:
The company is managed by a skilled CEO. = 这家公司由一位经验丰富的首席执行官管理。
The project was managed efficiently. = 这个项目得到了有效的管理。


疑问句:
Who manages the marketing department? = 谁管理市场部？
Does he manage to solve the problem? = 他解决这个问题了吗？


否定句:
He doesn't manage the team very well. = 他管理团队不太好。
She didn't manage to contact him. = 她没有联系到他。


PHRASE->
manage to do something = 设法做某事
manage without something = 没有…也能应付
manage one's time = 管理时间
manage expectations = 管理期望
manage a smile = 强作欢颜
