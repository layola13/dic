preemployed: /ˌpriːɪmˈplɔɪd/ | adj. | 预先雇佣的；事先雇用的 |  Previously employed; hired before a specific time or event.  This term often implies that the individual was employed before a certain point, such as starting a new job or project.  It doesn't necessarily imply a long-term employment history. |


例句：

The preemployed consultants brought valuable experience to the project. = 预先雇佣的顾问们为项目带来了宝贵的经验。

She was preemployed by the company before the merger. = 在合并之前，她已经被公司雇佣了。


近义词：previously employed, beforehand hired, already employed


反义词：newly hired, freshly employed, newly recruited


词性: adjective
