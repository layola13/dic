memorability: /ˌmɛməˈræbɪləti/ | n. |令人难忘的特性；记忆度|The quality of being memorable; the extent to which something is likely to be remembered.  It refers to the degree to which an experience, event, or piece of information is likely to be retained in memory. |例句：The memorability of the film was enhanced by its stunning visuals. 这部电影令人难忘的特性因其令人惊叹的视觉效果而增强。

The memorability of the campaign was crucial to its success.  这次活动的记忆度对其成功至关重要。|近义词：rememberableness, retention |反义词：forgettability, oblivion |名词
