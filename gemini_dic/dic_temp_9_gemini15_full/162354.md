preacknowledge:/ˌpriːækˈnɒlɪdʒ/| vt.|预先承认；预先告知|To acknowledge something before it happens or before it is officially known.  This word is rarely used and often considered overly formal or unnecessarily complex.|例句：The company preacknowledged potential supply chain disruptions in their quarterly report. 公司在其季度报告中预先承认了潜在的供应链中断。|近义词：anticipate, foresee, preempt|反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook|动词
