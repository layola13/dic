desertization: /ˌdɛzərtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |荒漠化；沙漠化 | The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.  This involves the loss of topsoil, vegetation, and overall productivity of the land.  | Example: Desertization is a major environmental problem affecting many parts of the world.  荒漠化是影响世界许多地区的一个主要环境问题。 The rapid desertization of the Sahel region is a serious concern.  萨赫勒地区迅速的沙漠化令人担忧。|近义词：desertification |反义词：afforestation, reforestation
