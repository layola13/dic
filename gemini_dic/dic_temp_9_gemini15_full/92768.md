ichthyosauria:/ɪkθiːəˈsɔːriə/| n. |鱼龙类|An extinct order of marine reptiles that lived during the Mesozoic Era, characterized by their streamlined bodies, flippers, and long snouts.  They were highly adapted for aquatic life and were top predators in their environment. |Example: Ichthyosauria flourished in the oceans of the Mesozoic Era.  鱼龙类在中生代的海洋中繁盛。  The fossil record shows a wide diversity within Ichthyosauria.  化石记录显示鱼龙类内部存在着广泛的多样性。|近义词：marine reptile (广义)|反义词：N/A
