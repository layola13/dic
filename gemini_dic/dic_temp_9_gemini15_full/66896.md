expedite:/ˈɛkspədaɪt/| vt.|加快；促进；便利|To make (a process or task) happen sooner or more quickly.|The company expedited the shipping process to ensure the customer received their order on time. 公司加快了运输流程，以确保客户及时收到订单。|accelerate, hasten, facilitate|delay, hinder, obstruct|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They expedite the delivery process. = 他们加快了送货流程。
We expedite the application process. = 我们加快了申请流程。

一般过去时:
The manager expedited the project. = 经理加快了项目进度。
She expedited the visa application. = 她加快了签证申请。

现在完成时:
They have expedited the shipment. = 他们已经加快了货运速度。
We have expedited the approval process. = 我们已经加快了审批流程。

过去完成时:
He had expedited the report before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经完成了报告。
They had expedited the repairs. = 他们已经加快了维修速度。

将来时:
We will expedite the order. = 我们将加快订单处理速度。
They will expedite the response. = 他们将加快回复速度。

虚拟语气:
If we expedited the process, we would finish earlier. = 如果我们加快流程，就能更早完成。
I wish they had expedited the delivery. = 我希望他们能加快送货速度。

被动语态:
The process was expedited by the new software. = 新软件加快了流程。
The request was expedited by the manager. = 经理加快了请求处理速度。

疑问句:
Can we expedite this procedure? = 我们能加快这个程序吗？
How can we expedite the workflow? = 我们如何加快工作流程？

否定句:
They did not expedite the process. = 他们没有加快流程。
We cannot expedite the application without more information. = 没有更多信息，我们无法加快申请速度。


PHRASE->
expedite the process = 加快流程
expedite delivery = 加快递送

expedite matters = 加快事情处理


词源:
From Latin *expeditare "to free from entanglement," from ex- "out" + ped-, stem of pes "foot."  The sense evolved to "to free from delay," then "to hasten."
