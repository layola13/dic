knur:/nɜːr/| n.|旋纹, 滚花|A knur is a pattern of small, regularly spaced indentations or projections on a surface, typically a cylindrical one, designed to improve grip or provide a decorative effect.  Knurling is the process of creating this pattern.|例句：The handle of the tool had a knurled surface for a better grip.  这个工具的手柄表面有滚花，以获得更好的抓握力。The decorative knurling on the pen added a touch of elegance. 笔上的装饰性滚花增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：grooving, milling, embossing|反义词：smooth, polished|名词
