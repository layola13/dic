gnaw:/nɔː/| vt.|啃，咬，折磨|To bite or chew on something persistently, often in a way that is destructive or irritating.  It can also refer to a persistent worry or anxiety that troubles someone.|例句：The dog gnawed on the bone.  这只狗啃着骨头。The problem gnawed at his conscience.  这个问题一直折磨着他的良心。|近义词：chew, bite, nibble, erode|反义词：heal, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rat gnaws on the cheese. = 老鼠啃着奶酪。
He constantly gnaws his fingernails. = 他不停地啃指甲。

一般过去时:
The dog gnawed the rope until it broke. = 狗啃断了绳子。
She gnawed on her lip nervously. = 她紧张地咬着嘴唇。

现在完成时:
The mice have gnawed through the wires. = 老鼠啃断了电线。
He has gnawed away at the problem for weeks. = 他已经啃了几个星期的这个问题了。

过去完成时:
The termites had gnawed through the wooden beams before we noticed. = 在我们发现之前，白蚁已经蛀空了木梁。
She had gnawed her nails to the quick before the interview. = 面试前她把指甲都啃掉了。

将来时:
The mice will gnaw through the packaging if we leave it here. = 如果我们把它放在这里，老鼠会啃坏包装的。
He will gnaw at the issue until he finds a solution. = 他会一直纠结这个问题直到找到解决方法。


虚拟语气:
If I were a mouse, I would gnaw on this cheese. = 如果我是一只老鼠，我会啃这个奶酪。
I wish I hadn't gnawed my nails so much during the exam. = 我真希望考试期间不要啃那么多指甲。

被动语态:
The wood was gnawed by the beetles. = 木头被甲虫啃食了。
The problem is being gnawed at by many experts. = 许多专家都在苦思这个问题。

疑问句:
Is the dog gnawing on the furniture again? = 狗又在啃家具了吗？
What is gnawing at your conscience? = 什么事在折磨你的良心？

否定句:
The dog didn't gnaw the shoes. = 狗没有啃鞋子。
The news did not gnaw at her as much as she expected. = 消息并没有像她预料的那样折磨她。


PHRASE->
gnaw at (something):  啃咬；折磨
gnaw away at (something):  逐渐地破坏；慢慢地折磨
gnaw on (something):  啃咬…
gnaw through (something):  啃穿…
gnaw one's lip:  咬嘴唇
