ionize:/ˈaɪənaɪz/ | vt. | 电离；使离子化 | To convert (an atom, molecule, or substance) into ions, typically by removing one or more electrons.  | Example:  The high-energy radiation ionized the air molecules. 高能辐射使空气分子电离。
The strong acid ionized completely in water. 强酸在水中完全电离。|近义词：ionise (BrE)|反义词：neutralize
