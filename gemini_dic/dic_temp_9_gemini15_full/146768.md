overhyped:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪpt/ | adj. | 过度宣传的，炒作过度的 | Excessively publicized or promoted;  having been given more attention or praise than is deserved.  | 例句：The new phone is overhyped; its features are not as impressive as the marketing suggests. 这款新手机被过度宣传了；它的功能不如市场营销宣传的那样令人印象深刻。

例句：The concert was completely overhyped; the performance was disappointing.  这场音乐会完全被炒作过头了；演出令人失望。


近义词：overblown, overrated, hyped-up, exaggerated

反义词：understated, underrated, downplayed


USAGE->

The film is overhyped. = 这部电影被过度宣传了。
That product is overhyped. = 那个产品被过度宣传了。
