rains:/reɪnz/| n. v.|雨；降雨|The word "rains" can function as a noun referring to rainfall or as the third-person singular present tense of the verb "to rain," indicating that it is raining.  |例句：The rains have been heavy this year.  今年雨水很多。|例句：It rains a lot in Seattle. 西雅图经常下雨。|近义词：showers, downpour, precipitation|反义词：drought, sunshine|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It rains a lot in this region. = 这个地区雨水很多。
The sun shines, and the rain falls. = 太阳照耀，雨水落下。

一般过去时:
It rained heavily yesterday. = 昨天雨下得很大。
The rain stopped after noon. = 中午以后雨停了。

现在完成时:
It has rained all day. =  今天下了一整天的雨。
It has rained incessantly for weeks. = 好几个星期一直在不停地下雨。

过去完成时:
It had rained all night, so the ground was very wet. =  下了一整夜的雨，所以地面非常湿润。
Before we left, it had rained heavily. = 我们离开之前，下了一场大雨。

将来时:
It will rain tomorrow. = 明天会下雨。
They predict it will rain heavily next week. = 他们预测下周会下大雨。

虚拟语气:
If it rained tomorrow, we would cancel the picnic. = 如果明天下雨，我们将取消野餐。
I wish it rained; the garden needs watering. = 我希望下雨；花园需要浇水。

被动语态:
(The verb "rain" is rarely used in the passive voice,  as it doesn't typically have a direct object that can be acted upon.)


疑问句:
Is it raining outside? = 外面在下雨吗？
Does it often rain here? = 这里经常下雨吗？

否定句:
It didn't rain yesterday. = 昨天没有下雨。
It doesn't rain often in the desert. = 沙漠地区不常下雨。
