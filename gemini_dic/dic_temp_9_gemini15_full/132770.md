nondriveable:/ˌnɒnˈdraɪvəbəl/ | adj. | 不可驾驶的，无法行驶的 |  Not able to be driven on; unsuitable for driving. This typically refers to roads or terrains that are impassable by vehicles due to their condition. | Example: The road became nondriveable after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，这条路变得无法通行。 The mountainous terrain was nondriveable for our car.  我们的车无法在这山区地带行驶。|近义词：impassable, inaccessible, unpassable | 反义词：drivable, passable
