It often implies a state of contentment and peace, achieved through spiritual practice or a shift in perspective.  This is not necessarily negative; in some philosophies, it's considered a desirable state.|例句：He found desirelessness in his quiet life of meditation. 他在宁静的冥想生活中找到了无欲无求的状态。|例句：The desirelessness of the monk was evident in his serene demeanor.  僧侣的淡泊名利从他平静的态度中显而易见。|近义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern, detachment|反义词：greed, ambition, avarice, covetousness|名词
