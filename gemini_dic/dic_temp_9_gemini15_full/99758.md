intermediacy:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəsi/| n. |中间性；媒介作用；中间状态|The state of being intermediate; the condition of being between two extremes or stages.  It can also refer to the role of an intermediary in facilitating communication or interaction between parties. |例句：The painting demonstrates a fascinating intermediacy between realism and abstraction. 这幅画展现了现实主义和抽象主义之间引人入胜的中间性。  The lawyer acted as an intermediacy, negotiating between the two disputing parties. 律师充当中间人，在两个争执方之间进行谈判。|近义词：mediation, intervention, interposition|反义词：extremity, directness
