cowpens:/ˈkaʊpɛnz/| n. |考彭斯战役；考彭斯（地名）|Cowpens refers to both a historical battle and a place name.  As a place name, it is a town in South Carolina, USA. As a historical term, it refers to the Battle of Cowpens, a significant American Revolutionary War battle fought on January 17, 1781.|The Battle of Cowpens was a decisive victory for the Continental Army.  The town of Cowpens is located in Spartanburg County.|近义词: (as a battle) Battle of Cowpens,  (as a place) Spartanburg County town|反义词: None.
