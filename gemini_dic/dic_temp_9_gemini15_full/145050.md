overagitation: /ˌoʊvərˌæʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |过度激动，过度兴奋|The state of being excessively agitated or excited.  It often implies a negative consequence, such as anxiety or distress.|例句：Her overagitation prevented her from sleeping.  她的过度兴奋让她无法入睡。

例句：The overagitation of the crowd led to a riot.  人群的过度激动导致了暴动。


近义词：hyperactivity, overstimulation,  excessive excitement, agitation, frenzy


反义词：calmness, tranquility, peace, composure


词性: 名词
