cadets:/kəˈdɛts/ | n. |学员，候补军官 | Young people, typically teenagers or young adults, undergoing military training.  Cadets are often students at a military academy or similar institution.  They are learning military skills and discipline, and are not yet fully commissioned officers. | Example:  The cadets marched in perfect formation.  学员们步伐一致地行进。

The naval cadets spent the summer at sea. 海军学员们在海上度过了夏天。| 近义词：trainees, students, recruits | 反义词：veterans, officers
