postmedieval:/ˌpoʊstˌmiːdiˈiːvəl/ | adj. | 后中世纪的 | Relating to the period of history following the Middle Ages, typically from the 15th to the 18th centuries.  This period is characterized by significant changes in politics, culture, and society, marking a transition from the medieval era to the modern era. | Example: Postmedieval literature often reflects the humanist ideals of the Renaissance.  后中世纪文学往往反映了文艺复兴的人文主义理想。The postmedieval period saw significant advancements in navigation and exploration. 后中世纪时期航海和探险取得了重大进展。| 近义词：late medieval, Renaissance, early modern | 反义词：medieval
