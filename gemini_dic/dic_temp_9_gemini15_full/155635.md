phishes:/ˈfɪʃɪz/| n. |网络钓鱼行为(复数)|The act of attempting to acquire sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details (and sometimes indirectly, money), often for malicious reasons, by disguising as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication.|例句：Many phishes are sent via email, appearing as legitimate messages from banks or other organizations. 许多网络钓鱼邮件伪装成来自银行或其他组织的合法邮件发送。|例句：The company implemented new security measures to protect against phishes and other cyberattacks.  该公司实施了新的安全措施来防范网络钓鱼和其他网络攻击。|近义词：fishing expeditions, scams, hoaxes|反义词：legitimate communication, secure transaction


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Phishing scams target vulnerable users. = 网络钓鱼骗局针对易受攻击的用户。
He phishes for personal information online. = 他在网上钓鱼获取个人信息。


一般过去时:
They phished my account last week. = 他们上周盗取了我的账户。
The hackers phished sensitive data from the server. = 黑客从服务器上窃取了敏感数据。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
