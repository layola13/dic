ports:/pɔːrts/| n.|港口，港湾；(计算机)端口|A port is a town or city with a harbor, where ships can load and unload goods.  It can also refer to a place where ships can enter and leave a body of water, or a computer's point of connection.|例句：The ship docked at the busy port of Shanghai. 这艘船停靠在繁忙的上海港。|例句：My computer has multiple USB ports. 我的电脑有多个USB接口。|近义词：harbor, haven, terminal|反义词：inland, interior|名词
