exp:/ɛksp/| n. v.|经验，表达；解释，阐述|experience, explanation, expression |例句：He has a lot of experience in teaching. 他有丰富的教学经验。 |近义词：experience, practice, clarify |反义词：inexperience, ignorance, obscurity|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I explain the problem to him. = 我向他解释了这个问题。
She expresses her feelings through painting. = 她通过绘画表达自己的感情。

一般过去时:
He explained the rules clearly. = 他清楚地解释了规则。
They expressed their concerns to the manager. = 他们向经理表达了他们的担忧。

现在完成时:
I have explained this many times. = 我已经解释过很多次了。
She has expressed her dissatisfaction with the product. = 她已经表达了她对产品的 不满意。

过去完成时:
I had explained the situation before he arrived. =在他到达之前我已经解释过情况了。
They had expressed their opinions before the vote. = 他们在投票前已经表达了他们的意见。

将来时:
I will explain it to you later. = 我稍后会向你解释。
She will express her gratitude in a letter. = 她将在信中表达她的感激之情。

虚拟语气:
If I had explained it better, he might have understood. = 如果我解释得更好，他可能就明白了。
I wish I had expressed my feelings more clearly. = 我希望我当时能更清楚地表达我的感受。

被动语态:
The problem was explained in detail. = 这个问题得到了详细的解释。
Her concerns were expressed to the committee. = 她的担忧已向委员会表达。

疑问句:
How did you explain that? = 你是怎么解释的呢？
What did she express in her speech? = 她在演讲中表达了什么？

否定句:
I didn't explain myself well. = 我没有解释清楚。
She didn't express her feelings openly. = 她没有公开表达自己的感情。



PHRASE->
explain oneself = 自我解释
explain away = 搪塞，解释掉
explain in detail = 详细解释
to explain to sb. = 向某人解释
explain the reason = 解释原因
