refracts:/rɪˈfrækts/| vt.| 折射；弯曲|To bend or change the direction of a ray of light, sound, or heat wave as it passes from one medium to another.|例句：The prism refracts white light into a spectrum of colors.  棱镜将白光折射成彩色光谱。|例句：The lens refracts the light, focusing it on the retina. 透镜折射光线，将其聚焦在视网膜上。|近义词：bend, deflect, diffract|反义词：reflect (in some contexts)|动词
