czar:/zɑːr/| n.|沙皇，独裁者，首脑|A ruler with absolute power, especially the former emperor of Russia.|例句：The czar ruled Russia with an iron fist. 沙皇铁腕统治俄罗斯。|近义词：emperor, autocrat, dictator, potentate|反义词：subject, democrat


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The czar rules the country. = 沙皇统治这个国家。
He is the czar of the company. = 他是这家公司的领导。


一般过去时:
The czar ruled with absolute power. = 沙皇以绝对权力统治。
The last czar was overthrown in 1917. = 1917年，最后一位沙皇被推翻。


现在完成时:
The czar has ruled for many years. = 沙皇已经统治多年了。
The people have suffered under the czar's rule. = 人民在沙皇的统治下遭受了苦难。


过去完成时:
The revolution had begun before the czar abdicated. = 在沙皇退位之前，革命已经开始了。
The people had endured years of oppression under the czar. = 在沙皇统治下，人民忍受了多年的压迫。


将来时:
The czar will continue to rule. = 沙皇将继续统治。
What will happen if the czar dies? = 如果沙皇死了会发生什么？


虚拟语气:
If the czar were a benevolent ruler, the people would be happier. = 如果沙皇是一位仁慈的统治者，人民会更幸福。
I wish the czar had been more compassionate. = 我希望沙皇能更富有同情心。


被动语态:
The country was ruled by the czar. = 这个国家由沙皇统治。
The czar's decisions were often questioned. = 沙皇的决定经常受到质疑。


疑问句:
Who is the czar of this land? = 这片土地的沙皇是谁？
Was the czar a good leader? = 沙皇是一位好领导吗？


否定句:
The czar did not listen to the people’s pleas. = 沙皇没有听取人民的请求。
He was not a popular czar. = 他不是一位受欢迎的沙皇。


PHRASE->
czar of (something):  某方面的权威
media czar: 媒体大亨
fashion czar: 时尚教父
technology czar: 科技巨头
business czar: 商业巨头
