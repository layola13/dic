excommunicating: /ˌɛksˈkɒmjʊneɪtɪŋ/ | v. |逐出教籍；开除教籍 | The act of formally excluding someone from a religious community.  This is the present participle of the verb "excommunicate". It describes the ongoing action of excluding someone. | Example: The church was excommunicating members who violated its strict code of conduct. 教会正在逐出违反其严格行为准则的成员。 | 近义词: ousting, banishing, expelling | 反义词: accepting, welcoming, including | 动词
