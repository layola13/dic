nonexplosively: /ˌnɒnɪkˈspləʊsɪvli/ | adv. | 非爆炸性地；不爆炸地 |  Describes an action or process that occurs without an explosion.  It emphasizes the absence of explosive force or a sudden, violent release of energy. | Example: The demolition was carried out nonexplosively, minimizing damage to surrounding structures.  = 拆除工作是非爆炸性进行的，最大限度地减少了对周边建筑的损害。| 近义词：gently, calmly, quietly, peacefully | 反义词：explosively, violently, suddenly
