overmix:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪks/ | vt. |过度混合；过度搅拌|To mix or stir something for too long, resulting in a less desirable texture or outcome.  This is often applied to baking, where overmixing can develop the gluten in flour too much, resulting in a tough product.  It can also apply to other contexts where excessive mixing negatively impacts the final result.|例句：Don't overmix the cake batter; it will become tough. 不要过度搅拌蛋糕糊；它会变得很硬。

例句：Overmixing the paint led to a grainy texture. 过度搅拌颜料导致粗糙的纹理。


近义词：overbeat, overblend | 反义词：undermix
