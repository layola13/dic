confirmor:/kənˈfɜːrmər/| n. |确认人，保证人 | A person who confirms something, especially a guarantor or surety.  A confirmor plays a crucial role in trade finance, guaranteeing the buyer's payment to the seller. |例句：The bank acted as a confirmor for the letter of credit, ensuring payment to the supplier. 银行作为信用证的确认人，确保向供应商付款。|例句：The confirmor's guarantee provided added security for the transaction. 确认人的担保为交易提供了额外的安全保障。|近义词：guarantor, surety, insurer |反义词：denier, disbeliever
