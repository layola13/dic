overarticulate: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪt/ | v. | 过分清晰地发音；过于强调地表达 | To pronounce words or syllables with excessive precision or clarity, often to the point of sounding affected or unnatural.  It can also refer to expressing oneself with excessive detail or emphasis. |


例句：

He overarticulated his words, making him sound pompous. = 他过分清晰地发音，听起来很傲慢。

She overarticulated her concerns, losing the audience's attention. = 她过于强调地表达她的担忧，失去了听众的注意力。


近义词：affect, exaggerate, emphasize excessively, overemphasize

反义词：mumble, slur, understate
