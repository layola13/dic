nonautomatic:/ˌnɒnˌɔːtəˈmætɪk/| adj.|非自动的，手工的|Not operating automatically; requiring manual operation.  The process is nonautomatic and requires human intervention at several stages.|近义词：manual, hand-operated|反义词：automatic


英文解析:The word "nonautomatic" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "automatic".  It describes something that does not function automatically, but instead requires human intervention or manual operation.


例句:

The old washing machine was nonautomatic, requiring you to manually adjust the timer and water level. = 那台旧洗衣机是非自动的，需要你手动调节定时器和水位。

This system is nonautomatic; data entry must be done manually. = 这个系统是非自动的；数据录入必须手动完成。
