conjurers:/kənˈdʒʊərərz/| n. |魔术师，变戏法的人|People who perform magic tricks, illusions, or other feats of deception. They often use sleight of hand, misdirection, and other techniques to create a sense of wonder and amazement.|The conjurers amazed the audience with their incredible illusions.  魔术师们以他们令人难以置信的幻象让观众惊叹不已。|illusionists, magicians, sorcerers|debunker, realist|名词
