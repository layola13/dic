name-caller: /ˈneɪmˌkɔːlər/ | n. |辱骂者，叫嚣者 | A person who insults or attacks others by using offensive or insulting names.  | Example:  He's a known name-caller, always trying to belittle others with hurtful words. 他是个出了名的辱骂者，总是试图用伤人的话贬低别人。 | 近义词：insulter, abuser, bully | 反义词：complimenter, encourager | 名词
