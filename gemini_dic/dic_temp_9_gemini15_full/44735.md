crumb:/krʌm/| n. |面包屑，少量，碎片 |A small piece of bread or cake remaining after a meal.  |例句：The children were picking up the crumbs from the floor.孩子们正在捡地上的面包屑。  |例句：He only gave me a crumb of information. 他只给了我一点点信息。|近义词：fragment, bit, speck |反义词：abundance, plenty |名词
