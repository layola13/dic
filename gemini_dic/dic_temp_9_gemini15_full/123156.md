monkeyed:/ˈmʌŋkiːd/ | v. | 胡乱摆弄；篡改；捣乱 | To interfere with or tamper with something in a clumsy or mischievous way.  It often implies a lack of skill or understanding. | 例句：He monkeyed with the engine and broke it. 他胡乱摆弄引擎把它弄坏了。  They monkeyed with the computer settings, causing a system crash. 他们乱改电脑设置，导致系统崩溃。|近义词：tamper with, fiddle with, mess with | 反义词：repair, fix, improve


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He monkeys with the radio every day. = 他每天都胡乱摆弄收音机。
She monkeys with her hair before every date. = 她每次约会前都乱弄头发。
