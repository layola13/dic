hastens:/ˈheɪsnz/| v.|赶紧；催促；加速|To move or act quickly; to urge someone to hurry; to cause something to happen sooner.|例句：He hastened his steps to catch the bus. 他加快脚步赶公共汽车。 He hastened to add that he didn't mean to offend anyone. 他急忙补充说他并非有意冒犯任何人。|近义词：hurry, accelerate, expedite|反义词：delay, slow down, procrastinate
