conjugating:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːndʒʊɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 活用；结合；使结合 |  The process of changing the form of a verb to show tense, person, number, mood, or voice.  This involves adding prefixes or suffixes, or changing the stem of the verb. |

Example 1:  He is conjugating the verb "to be" in the present tense. = 他正在将动词“to be” 活用到现在时态。

Example 2:  Learning to conjugate verbs is a crucial part of mastering a new language. = 学习动词的活用是掌握一门外语的关键部分。


近义词：inflecting, varying


反义词：None directly opposite, but  "leaving unchanged" could be considered a contrasting concept.


词性：动词
