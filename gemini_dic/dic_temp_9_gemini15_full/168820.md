productiveness:/ˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvnəs/ /ˌprɑːdʌkˈtɪvnəs/| n. |生产力；生产效率；多产；结果丰富|The ability to produce goods or services efficiently and effectively.  It refers to the output or results achieved in relation to the input or resources used. High productivity is often associated with efficiency, effectiveness, and innovation.|例句：The company's productiveness increased significantly after implementing new technologies. 这家公司在实施新技术后，生产力显著提高。

例句：Their high level of productiveness resulted in record-breaking profits.  他们高水平的生产力带来了创纪录的利润。


近义词：efficiency, output, yield, fruitfulness, fertility

反义词：inefficiency, unproductiveness, sterility, barrenness

词性: noun
