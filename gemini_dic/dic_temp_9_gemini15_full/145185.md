overawareness:/ˌoʊvərəˈwɛərnəs/| n. |过度警觉，过度敏感|Excessively heightened awareness, often leading to anxiety and distress.  It's a state of being overly conscious of one's surroundings, thoughts, and feelings, often to a point of discomfort or impairment.  |例句：Her overawareness of her own flaws prevented her from enjoying the party. 她对自身缺陷的过度敏感让她无法享受派对。


例句：His overawareness of potential dangers made him constantly jumpy. 他对潜在危险的过度警觉让他总是很紧张。


近义词：hypervigilance, hypersensitivity, anxiety, heightened awareness |反义词：obliviousness, unawareness, complacency


词性:名词
