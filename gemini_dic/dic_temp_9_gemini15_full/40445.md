contrastive:/kənˈtræstɪv/| adj.| 对比的；对照的；有对比的 | Relating to or involving contrast; highlighting differences.  It emphasizes the differences between things. | The contrastive analysis of the two languages revealed significant differences in their grammatical structures.  对比分析这两种语言揭示了它们语法结构上的显著差异。| comparative, differential | similar, identical | 形容词
