raining:/ˈreɪnɪŋ/| v. |正在下雨；下着雨 |The present participle of the verb "to rain".  It describes the action of rain falling. |例句：It's raining cats and dogs. = 现在下着倾盆大雨。

例句：It was raining heavily when I left home. = 我离开家的时候雨下得很大。


近义词：pouring, downpouring, showering | 反义词：sunny, clear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is raining heavily. = 雨下得很大。
It rains a lot in this region. = 这个地区雨水很多。


一般过去时:
It rained all day yesterday. = 昨天一整天都在下雨。
It rained cats and dogs last night. = 昨晚下着倾盆大雨。


现在完成时:
It has been raining since morning. = 从早上开始就一直在下雨。
It has rained a lot this week. = 这周雨水很多。


过去完成时:
It had been raining for hours before the sun came out. = 太阳出来之前已经下了好几个小时的雨了。
It had rained heavily, flooding the streets. = 雨下得很大，街道都淹了。


将来时:
It will rain tomorrow. = 明天会下雨。
It is going to rain later. = 后来会下雨。


虚拟语气:
If it were raining, we would stay inside. = 如果下雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it were raining so I could stay home. = 我希望下雨，这样我就可以待在家里了。


被动语态:
(The verb "rain" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is it raining outside? = 外面在下雨吗？
Was it raining when you arrived? = 你到达的时候在下雨吗？


否定句:
It isn't raining now. = 现在不下雨。
It didn't rain yesterday. = 昨天没有下雨。


PHRASE->
raining cats and dogs = 倾盆大雨
raining fire = 狂风暴雨
raining money = 赚钱容易
make it rain = 挥金如土
a rainy day = 雨天；困境


词源:

The word "raining" comes from the Old English word "rēnian," meaning "to rain."  It is the present participle of the verb "to rain," formed by adding "-ing."
