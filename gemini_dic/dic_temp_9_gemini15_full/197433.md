sheening:/ˈʃiːnɪŋ/| n. adj.|光泽；闪光；有光泽的|A smooth, glossy surface that reflects light.  It can refer to the act of shining or the quality of being shiny.|例句：The sheening of the polished wood was remarkable.  抛光木材的光泽非常显著。

The varnish gave the furniture a beautiful sheening.  清漆使家具呈现出美丽的亮泽。|近义词：gloss, luster, shine, gleam|反义词：dullness, matte, flat
