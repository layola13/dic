shacks:/ʃæks/| n.|棚屋，简陋小屋|A shack is a small, roughly built hut or cabin.  It is typically made of cheap or readily available materials and is often temporary or in poor condition.|例句：The family lived in a dilapidated shack on the edge of town. 这家人住在城郊一座破旧的棚屋里。|例句：He built a small shack to store his tools. 他建了一个小棚屋来存放工具。|近义词：hut, shanty, cabin, shed|反义词：mansion, palace
