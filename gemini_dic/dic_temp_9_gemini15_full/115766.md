marinates:/ˈmærɪneɪts/| vt.|腌制；浸渍|To soak food in a marinade, typically a mixture of oil, vinegar, herbs, and spices, to flavor and tenderize it.|例句：I marinated the chicken in a lemon and herb marinade for several hours before grilling it. 我在烤鸡肉之前用柠檬和香草腌料腌制了几个小时。|例句：The chef marinated the steak in a red wine reduction overnight.  厨师将牛排在红酒汁中腌制了一整夜。|近义词：soak, pickle, infuse|反义词：dry out
