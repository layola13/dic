flails:/fleɪlz/| n., v.|挥舞；挣扎；徒劳；| 英文解析：A flail is a tool used for threshing grain, consisting of a handle and a stick with a heavy weight at the end. As a verb, it means to wave or swing wildly; to thrash about wildly.  | 例句：The farmer flailed the wheat with his flail.  = 农民用他的连枷打麦子。

The drowning man flailed wildly in the water. = 溺水的人在水中拼命挣扎。| 近义词：thrash, swing, flounder, struggle | 反义词：control, manage, succeed
