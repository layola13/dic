fetched:/fetʃt/| vt.|取来，带来；牵来；(尤指从远处)取回|to go and bring or get something |He fetched the doctor. 他去请医生来了。|She fetched a pail of water from the well. 她从井里打来一桶水。|bring, retrieve, obtain|leave, abandon, drop|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fetches the newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上都取报纸。
She fetches her children from school. = 她从学校接她的孩子。

一般过去时:
I fetched a glass of water for him. = 我给他拿了一杯水。
He fetched the ball from the garden. = 他从花园里把球拿来了。

现在完成时:
I have fetched the mail. = 我已经取了邮件。
She has fetched the children from school. = 她已经从学校接孩子了。

过去完成时:
I had fetched the tools before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经把工具拿来了。
She had fetched the car before the rain started. = 下雨之前她已经把车开来了。


将来时:
I will fetch you a drink. = 我会给你拿一杯饮料。
He will fetch the children from school tomorrow. = 他明天将从学校接孩子。

虚拟语气:
If I needed something, I would fetch it myself. = 如果我需要什么东西，我会自己去取。
I wish I had fetched an umbrella before I left. = 我真希望我离开前带了把伞。


被动语态:
The book was fetched from the library. = 这本书是从图书馆取来的。
The children were fetched from school by their parents. = 孩子们被父母从学校接走了。


疑问句:
Did he fetch the milk? = 他取牛奶了吗？
Will you fetch me a towel? = 你会帮我拿一条毛巾吗？


否定句:
He didn't fetch the parcel. = 他没有取包裹。
I won't fetch the children today. = 我今天不会去接孩子们。


PHRASE->

fetch a high price:  卖高价
fetch a sigh:  叹气
fetch and carry:  跑腿
fetch up:  呕吐；最终到达
go fetch: 去取来
