crossovers:/ˈkrɒsˌoʊvərz/ | n. |跨界，跨界车，交叉，混合 |A crossover is something that combines elements of different things, or an instance of moving from one activity, subject, or style to another.  It can refer to vehicles that blend features of different vehicle types (like SUVs and cars), or to instances of combining genres in art, music, or other fields. |例句：The new car is a crossover between an SUV and a sedan. 这辆新车是SUV和轿车的混合车型。

The movie is a crossover hit, appealing to both action and romance fans. 这部电影是一部跨界热门电影，吸引了动作片和爱情片迷。|近义词：hybrid, blend, combination, intermingling |反义词：separation, division, segregation
