mortifying:/ˈmɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj.| 令人羞愧的；令人尴尬的 | Extremely embarrassing and humiliating.  |例句：It was mortifying to trip and fall in front of everyone.  = 在大家面前绊倒真是太丢人了。

例句：Spilling coffee all over my boss was a mortifying experience. = 把咖啡洒在我的老板身上是一次令人羞愧的经历。


近义词：humiliating, embarrassing, shameful, degrading

反义词：pleasing, flattering, complimentary
