seismogram:/ˈsaɪzməɡræm/| n. |地震图，地震记录|A seismogram is a visual record produced by a seismograph of the ground motion during an earthquake or other seismic event.  It shows the amplitude and timing of seismic waves. |The seismogram clearly showed the arrival of the P-waves before the S-waves. 地震图清楚地显示了P波在S波之前到达。|  |  |noun
