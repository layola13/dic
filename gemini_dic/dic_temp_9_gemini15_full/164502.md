preerect:/ˌpriːɪˈrekt/| adj.| 挺胸的，昂首挺胸的，趾高气扬的 |  Preerect describes someone who stands or walks with their chest held high, often suggesting arrogance or self-importance.  It implies a deliberate and somewhat showy posture. | 例句：He walked with a preerect gait, his chin held high. 他昂首挺胸地走着，下巴高高地扬起。
