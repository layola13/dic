onlan:/ˈɑːnˌlæn/| adj.|在线的，网络上的|Online refers to something that is connected to a computer network, especially the internet.  It describes activities, services, or resources accessible via the internet.|例句：The online store offers a wide variety of products.  这家网上商店提供各种各样的产品。|例句：I'm taking an online course in Spanish. 我正在上网课学习西班牙语。|近义词：digital, virtual, web-based|反义词：offline, disconnected
