penetrates:/ˈpɛnɪtreɪts/ | v. | 穿透；渗透；深入了解 | To force a way into or through (something).  To affect or influence deeply. |例句：The sunlight penetrates the leaves.  阳光穿透树叶。

The knife penetrated his skin.  刀子刺穿了他的皮肤。


近义词：pierce, permeate, infiltrate, pervade | 反义词：emerges, exits, leaves


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain penetrates the soil. 雨水渗透土壤。
The aroma penetrates the air. 香气弥漫在空气中。


一般过去时:
The bullet penetrated the wall. 子弹穿透了墙壁。
His words penetrated her defenses. 他的话语击破了她的防线。


现在完成时:
The idea has penetrated the public consciousness. 这个想法已经深入人心。
The roots have penetrated deep into the earth. 根部已深入地下。


过去完成时:
The rain had penetrated the tent before we arrived. 我们到达之前，雨水已经渗透了帐篷。
The message had penetrated his thick skull.  信息已经渗透进他那迟钝的头脑里。


将来时:
The missile will penetrate the enemy's defenses. 导弹将穿透敌人的防线。
The truth will eventually penetrate. 真相最终会大白。


虚拟语气:
If the knife had penetrated deeper, it would have been fatal. 如果刀子刺得更深，就会致命。
If the message penetrated their minds, they might change their ways. 如果信息能打动他们的思想，他们可能会改变他们的方式。


被动语态:
The wall was penetrated by the bullet. 墙壁被子弹穿透了。
The secret was eventually penetrated by the police. 这个秘密最终被警方破获了。


疑问句:
Does the sunlight penetrate the clouds? 阳光能穿透云层吗？
Does your argument penetrate the issue’s core? 你的论点能触及问题的核心吗？


否定句:
The water didn't penetrate the waterproof fabric. 水没有渗透防水织物。
His words didn't penetrate the thick fog of their prejudice. 他的话语没有穿透他们偏见的浓雾。


PHRASE->
penetrate the market  渗透市场
penetrate the mystery  揭开谜团
penetrate the truth  洞悉真相
penetrate the enemy lines  突破敌军防线
penetrate the secrets  探究秘密

词源:
From Old French *penetrer, from Latin penetrāre "to enter, penetrate," from penitus "inward, to the depths," from PIE *pen- "inward, inside."
