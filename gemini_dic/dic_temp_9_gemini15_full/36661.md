collet:/ˈkɒlɪt/| n.|夹头；颈圈；托；收集物|A collet is a small, typically cylindrical, device used to hold or grip something, often in a rotating machine.  It's often used in jewelry making to hold gemstones. |例句：The jeweler used a collet to secure the diamond in the setting. 珠宝商用夹头将钻石固定在座上。

例句：The machine's collet was worn and needed replacing.  机器的夹头磨损了，需要更换。


近义词：chuck, holder, grip|反义词：release, loosen


词源：From Middle French *colet, diminutive of col "neck," from Vulgar Latin *collu "neck."  The meaning shifted from "neck" to "collar" and then to the technical sense of a gripping device.
