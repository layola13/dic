glyptodont:/ˈɡlɪptodɒnt/| n. |雕齿兽|An extinct genus of large, heavily armored mammals that lived during the Pleistocene epoch.  They were characterized by their bony shell, which resembled that of a turtle, and their strong, powerful legs. |Example:  The glyptodont fossils found in Argentina provided valuable insights into the Pleistocene megafauna.  阿根廷发现的雕齿兽化石为了解更新世巨型动物群提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：armadillo (less armored)|反义词：None
