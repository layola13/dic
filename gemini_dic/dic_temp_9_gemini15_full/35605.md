coast:/koʊst/| n.|海岸，海滨|The land bordering the sea or ocean; the shore.|例句：We spent the day relaxing on the coast. 我们在海边度过了一天。|例句：The rugged coastline was breathtaking.  崎岖的海岸线令人叹为观止。|近义词：shore, seaside, beachfront|反义词：inland
