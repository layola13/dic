fencing:/ˈfɛnsɪŋ/| n. v.|击剑；围栏；防护；|The art and sport of fighting with swords.  Also refers to the construction of fences or barriers.|例句：He's a skilled fencer and won many competitions. 他是一位技艺高超的击剑运动员，赢得了许多比赛。

例句：They are building a fence around their property. 他们在他们的房产周围建围栏。|近义词：sword fighting,  fencing in (v.),  enclosure (n.)|反义词：opening,  breach (n.)|名词
