horseshoers:/ˈhɔːrsˌʃuːərz/| n.| 马蹄铁匠 | A person who makes and fits horseshoes.  Horseshoers are skilled craftsmen who work with horses' hooves to ensure their health and comfort. They need strong knowledge of equine anatomy and farrier techniques. | 例句：The horseshoer carefully shaped the horseshoe to fit the horse's hoof.  马蹄铁匠仔细地将马蹄铁塑造成适合马蹄的形状。

The local horseshoer is known for his meticulous work. 当地马蹄铁匠以其细致的工作而闻名。|近义词：farrier, blacksmith (in some contexts)|反义词：无
