deadpan:/ˈdedpæn/| adj.|面无表情的；一本正经地开玩笑的|Having or showing no emotion; impassive.  Often used to describe someone delivering a joke or a shocking statement with a completely straight face.|例句：He delivered the bad news with a deadpan expression. 他面无表情地宣布了这个坏消息。

例句：She told the joke with a deadpan delivery, making it even funnier. 她一本正经地讲笑话，这让笑话更有趣了。

近义词：impassive, expressionless, unemotional, stoic

反义词：expressive, animated, emotional
