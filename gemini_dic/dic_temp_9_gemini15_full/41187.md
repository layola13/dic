copyrighted:/ˈkɒpiraɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːpiraɪtɪd/ | adj. | 受版权保护的 | Protected by copyright; having the exclusive legal right to reproduce, publish, sell, or distribute a creative work.  | Example: The song is copyrighted, so you can't use it without permission. 这首歌受版权保护，未经许可你不能使用它。 | 近义词：patent, protected | 反义词：public domain, unlicensed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This book is copyrighted. = 这本书受版权保护。
That software is copyrighted material. = 那个软件是受版权保护的材料。


一般过去时:
The photograph was copyrighted in 1990. = 这张照片在1990年获得了版权保护。
His novel was copyrighted last year. = 他的小说去年获得了版权保护。


现在完成时:
The design has been copyrighted for many years. = 这项设计已经受版权保护多年了。
They have copyrighted their latest invention. = 他们已经为他们最新的发明申请了版权。


过去完成时:
The music had been copyrighted before they released the album. = 在他们发行专辑之前，音乐就已经获得了版权保护。
She had copyrighted the painting before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，她已经为这幅画申请了版权。


将来时:
The company will copyright the new software next month. = 公司下个月将为新的软件申请版权。
He will copyright his book before publishing it. = 他将在出版前为他的书申请版权。


虚拟语气:
If the artwork were copyrighted, we couldn't use it. = 如果这件艺术品受版权保护，我们就不能使用它。
I wish my invention were copyrighted. = 我希望我的发明受版权保护。


被动语态:
The image was copyrighted by the photographer. = 这张图片受摄影师的版权保护。
The song is being copyrighted currently. = 这首歌目前正在申请版权。


疑问句:
Is the music copyrighted? = 这首音乐受版权保护吗？
Are these photos copyrighted? = 这些照片受版权保护吗？


否定句:
The article is not copyrighted. = 这篇文章不受版权保护。
The images are not yet copyrighted. = 这些图片还没有获得版权保护。


PHRASE->
