demotivational: /ˌdiːˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 令人泄气的；消极的；使人失去动力的 |  Causing a loss of motivation or enthusiasm; discouraging.  |


英文解析:The word "demotivational" describes something that takes away someone's motivation or enthusiasm. It suggests a negative influence that discourages effort or ambition.


例句:

The demotivational speech left the team feeling disheartened. = 令人泄气的演讲使团队感到沮丧。

His demotivational management style led to high employee turnover. = 他消极的管理风格导致员工高流失率。


近义词: discouraging, dispiriting, disheartening, discouraging, uninspiring


反义词: motivational, inspiring, encouraging, uplifting


词性: adjective
