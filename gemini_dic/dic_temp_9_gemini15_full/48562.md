defangs: /dɪˈfæŋz/ | vt. | 去毒牙；消除危险性 | To remove the fangs from (a venomous snake or other creature).  Figuratively, to remove the dangerous aspects of something. | 例句：The snake charmer deftly defanged the cobra.  = 驯蛇人熟练地去掉了眼镜蛇的毒牙。

The government attempted to defang the new regulations. = 政府试图消除新规定的危险性。


近义词：disarm, neutralize, mitigate, render harmless | 反义词：empower, enable, intensify
