caramelizes:/ˈkærəməlaɪzɪz/| v. |焦糖化；糖化|To heat sugar until it melts and turns brown, often used in cooking to create a sweet and rich flavor.  The process involves the breakdown of sucrose into simpler sugars and the Maillard reaction, creating complex flavors and colors. |例句：The onions caramelized beautifully in the pan.  洋葱在锅里焦糖化得很好。The chef caramelized the sugar to create a rich glaze. 厨师焦糖化了糖来制作浓郁的糖衣。|近义词：brown, candy, sugarize|反义词：N/A
