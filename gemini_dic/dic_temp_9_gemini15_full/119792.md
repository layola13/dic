migrates:/ˈmaɪɡreɪts/| v.|迁徙；移居；迁移|To move from one region or habitat to another, especially regularly according to the seasons.|例句：Many birds migrate south for the winter.  许多鸟类冬天会飞往南方。|例句：The family migrated to the city in search of better opportunities.  这家人为了寻找更好的机会而移居城市。|近义词：emigrate, relocate, move|反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Birds migrate south for the winter every year. = 鸟类每年冬天都迁徙到南方。
The company migrates its data to the cloud. = 公司将其数据迁移到云端。

一般过去时:
The family migrated to Australia in 2005. = 这家人在2005年移居澳大利亚。
Swallows migrated south before the first frost. = 燕子在第一次霜冻前就飞往南方了。

现在完成时:
We have migrated all our files to the new server. = 我们已经将所有文件迁移到新服务器。
Many people have migrated to urban areas in search of work. = 许多人为了寻找工作而移居城市地区。

过去完成时:
They had migrated to Canada before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前他们已经移居加拿大。
The birds had migrated south before the heavy snow fell. = 大雪落下之前，鸟儿们已经飞往南方了。


将来时:
We will migrate to a new office building next year. = 我们明年将迁入新的办公楼。
The whales will migrate to warmer waters in the spring. = 鲸鱼将在春天迁徙到温暖的水域。

虚拟语气:
If the company migrated its data, it would save money. = 如果公司迁移数据，它就能节省资金。
I wish I could migrate to a warmer climate. = 我希望我能移居到气候温暖的地方。

被动语态:
The data is migrated to the new server every night. = 数据每晚都会迁移到新服务器。
The population was migrated forcefully during the war. = 战争期间人口被迫迁移。

疑问句:
Do these birds migrate every year? = 这些鸟每年都迁徙吗？
When will the company migrate its systems? = 公司什么时候迁移其系统？

否定句:
The birds did not migrate this year. = 鸟类今年没有迁徙。
The company has not yet migrated to the new platform. = 公司尚未迁移到新的平台。
