re-measured:/ˌriːˈmɛʒərd/| v. |重新测量|To measure something again, often to correct a previous measurement or to obtain a more accurate result.|例句：The surveyor re-measured the land after discovering an error in the initial survey. 测量员在发现初始测量有误后重新测量了土地。
例句：The scientist re-measured the sample to ensure accuracy. 科学家重新测量了样本以确保准确性。|近义词：remeasure, re-evaluate|反义词：measure (initially)
