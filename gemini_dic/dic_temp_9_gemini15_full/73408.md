forejudgment:/ˈfɔːrˌdʒʌdʒmənt/ | n. |预先判断；偏见；先入为主 |Preconceived judgment or opinion, often unfair or biased.  It implies a judgment formed before having sufficient evidence or consideration. |例句：His forejudgment of the situation led him to make a costly mistake.  他的先入为主的判断导致他犯了一个代价高昂的错误。

例句：The judge's forejudgment of the defendant's guilt was evident in his questioning. 法官对被告有罪的预先判断在他的提问中显而易见。


近义词：prejudgment, bias, prejudice, predisposition


反义词：fair judgment, impartiality, objectivity


词性：名词
