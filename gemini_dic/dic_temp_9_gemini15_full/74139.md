foundationless:/faʊnˈdeɪʃən.ləs/ | adj. | 无基础的；缺乏根据的；不稳固的 | Lacking a foundation or basis; groundless; unsubstantiated.  The argument was foundationless and easily refuted.  | 近义词：baseless, groundless, unsubstantiated, unsupported | 反义词：well-founded, grounded, substantial


例句：

His claims were foundationless and lacked any supporting evidence.  他的说法毫无根据，缺乏任何证据支持。

The building, constructed on foundationless ground, collapsed during the earthquake.  这栋建筑建在不稳固的地基上，在地震中倒塌了。


词源：

The word "foundationless" is formed by combining the noun "foundation" with the suffix "-less," which indicates the absence or lack of something.  "Foundation" derives from the Old French word "fondation," ultimately from the Latin "fundamentum," meaning "foundation, base."  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin.


用法说明:
