refamiliarized:/ˌriːfəˈmɪljəriˌaɪzd/| v. |使再次熟悉；使重新熟悉 |To make someone or something familiar again; to reacquaint.  The process involves renewing or restoring a previous level of familiarity. |例句：After a long break, she refamiliarized herself with the software.  = 长期休息后，她重新熟悉了这个软件。

例句：The museum curator refamiliarized himself with the collection before the exhibition. = 博物馆馆长在展览前重新熟悉了馆藏。


近义词：reacquaint, reaccustom, familiarize


反义词：estrange, alienate


词性：动词
