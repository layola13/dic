leathery:/ˈlɛðəri/| adj.|皮革般的，坚韧的，干枯的|Resembling leather in texture or appearance; tough and hard; dry and withered.|The old book had a leathery cover. 这本旧书的封皮像皮革一样。 The sunbaked earth felt leathery underfoot. 晒干的土地踩上去感觉像皮革一样坚硬。|tough, hard, stiff, dry|soft, supple, moist|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old shoes feel leathery. = 这双旧鞋摸起来像皮革一样。
This leather jacket is very leathery. = 这件皮夹克很硬实。


一般过去时:
His hands felt leathery after years of hard work. = 他多年的辛劳使他的双手变得粗糙坚硬。
The fruit was leathery and inedible. = 水果干枯得像皮革一样，不能吃了。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
