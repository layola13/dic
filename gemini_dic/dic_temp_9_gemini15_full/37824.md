compense:/kɒmˈpɛns/ /ˈkɑːmpɛnseɪt/| vt. |补偿，赔偿；抵消|To make up for something bad or undesirable; to counterbalance or offset.|The company will compensate employees for their travel expenses. 公司将补偿员工的差旅费。|近义词：reimburse, repay, offset|反义词：deprive,shortchange


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company compensates its employees for overtime work. = 公司补偿员工加班费。
He compensates for his lack of experience with hard work. = 他用努力工作来弥补经验不足。


一般过去时:
They compensated the victims for their losses. = 他们赔偿了受害者的损失。
The government compensated the farmers for the damaged crops. = 政府赔偿农民受损的庄稼。


现在完成时:
The insurance company has compensated me for the damage to my car. = 保险公司已经赔偿了我汽车的损失。
She has compensated for her initial mistakes. = 她已经弥补了她最初的错误。


过去完成时:
They had compensated the workers before the strike. = 他们在大罢工前已经补偿了工人们。
The company had compensated the investors for their losses before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公司已经补偿了投资者的损失。


将来时:
The government will compensate the residents for the disruption caused by the construction. = 政府将补偿因施工造成的居民不便。
The company will compensate its employees for the holiday work. = 公司将补偿员工节假日的工作。


虚拟语气:
If the company compensated us fairly, we wouldn't have sued. = 如果公司公平地赔偿我们，我们就不会起诉了。
I wish they had compensated me for the inconvenience. = 我希望他们已经赔偿了我由此带来的不便。


被动语态:
The victims were compensated for their injuries. = 受害者因受伤得到了赔偿。
The employees will be compensated for their extra hours. = 员工将获得加班费补偿。


疑问句:
Will the company compensate us for the delay? = 公司会赔偿我们因为延误造成的损失吗？
How will the damage be compensated? = 如何赔偿损失？


否定句:
The company did not compensate the workers for their lost wages. = 公司没有补偿工人们损失的工资。
They refused to compensate for the damage they caused. = 他们拒绝赔偿他们造成的损失。


PHRASE->
compensate for = 弥补
compensate somebody for something = 赔偿某人某物
fully compensate = 全额赔偿
adequately compensate = 足额赔偿
compensate losses = 补偿损失


词源:
From Latin *compensare, from com- "together" + pensare "to weigh".  The original sense was to weigh together, to balance.  The modern meaning developed from the idea of balancing out a loss or disadvantage.
