enteropathy:/ˌɛntərˈɑːpəθi/ | n. |肠病|A disease of the intestines.  Enteropathy encompasses a wide range of conditions affecting the structure and function of the intestines, leading to various symptoms depending on the underlying cause and severity.  These can include malabsorption, diarrhea, abdominal pain, and weight loss.  The term is often used to describe chronic or recurring intestinal disorders. |The patient presented with chronic diarrhea, suggestive of an underlying enteropathy.  患者出现慢性腹泻，提示存在潜在的肠病。| Inflammatory bowel disease, celiac disease |Healthy intestines|名词
