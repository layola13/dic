conquering:/ˈkɒŋkərɪŋ/|/ˈkɑːŋkərɪŋ/| adj. |征服的；胜利的|Describes the act of overcoming or subduing something, often through force or effort.  It can refer to a physical conquest (like a battle) or a metaphorical one (like conquering a fear).|例句：The conquering army marched triumphantly into the city. 凯旋的军队胜利地进入城市。

The feeling of conquering a difficult challenge was exhilarating. 战胜困难挑战的感觉令人兴奋。


近义词：victorious, triumphant, winning, overcoming


反义词：defeated, surrendering, yielding


词性：形容词 (participle of conquer)
