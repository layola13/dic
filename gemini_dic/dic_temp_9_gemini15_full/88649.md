hoarded:/ˈhɔːrdɪd/| v. |囤积；积聚|To gather and store away large quantities of something, especially in a secretive or greedy way.  It implies excessive accumulation often to the point of being wasteful or harmful.|例句：The squirrels hoarded nuts for the winter.  松鼠为冬天囤积坚果。

例句：He hoarded his money, never spending a penny more than necessary. 他囤积他的钱，从不花比必要多的一分钱。


近义词：amassed, accumulated, stockpiled, cached

反义词：dispersed, distributed, squandered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hoard food in case of emergency. = 他们囤积食物以备不时之需。
He hoards old newspapers in his attic. = 他在阁楼里囤积旧报纸。


一般过去时:
The dragon hoarded gold in its cave. = 龙在它的洞穴里囤积黄金。
She hoarded her memories, refusing to share them. = 她珍藏着她的回忆，拒绝与人分享。


现在完成时:
They have hoarded enough supplies to last the winter. = 他们已经囤积了足够的物资以度过冬天。
He has hoarded so much junk that he can barely move in his house. = 他囤积了那么多垃圾，以至于他几乎无法在房子里移动。


过去完成时:
The farmer had hoarded all his harvest before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，农民已经囤积了他所有的收成。
She had hoarded her feelings for years, until she finally broke down. = 她压抑了自己的感情多年，直到她最终崩溃。


将来时:
They will hoard water if the drought continues. = 如果干旱持续下去，他们将囤积水。
He will hoard his knowledge, unwilling to share his expertise. = 他将隐瞒他的知识，不愿意分享他的专业知识。


虚拟语气:
If they had not hoarded so much food, they wouldn't have had enough to last the winter. = 如果他们没有囤积那么多食物，他们就不会有足够的粮食度过冬天。
I wish I hadn't hoarded all those old clothes; I could have donated them. = 我真希望我没有囤积所有那些旧衣服；我可以把它们捐出去。


被动语态:
Large quantities of grain were hoarded during the war. = 在战争期间，大量的粮食被囤积起来。
His secrets were carefully hoarded from the public. = 他的秘密被小心地隐瞒起来，不让公众知道。


疑问句:
Why are they hoarding all that sugar? = 他们为什么囤积那么多糖？
Are they hoarding supplies in anticipation of a natural disaster? = 他们是否为了应对自然灾害而囤积物资？


否定句:
They did not hoard any money, instead they invested wisely. = 他们没有囤积任何钱，而是明智地进行了投资。
She did not hoard her emotions; she expressed them openly. = 她没有压抑自己的情绪；她公开表达了自己的情绪。
