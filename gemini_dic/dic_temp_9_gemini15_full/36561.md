collapsed:/kəˈlæpst/ | adj. | 倒塌的；崩溃的；压缩的 |  Describes something that has fallen down or broken down, or has been reduced in size or complexity.  |  The building collapsed after the earthquake. 地震后，那栋建筑物倒塌了。 The software collapsed under the heavy load. 软件在重负载下崩溃了. |  broken, fallen, compressed | standing, erect, expanded |形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bridge is collapsed. = 桥梁倒塌了。
The economy is collapsed. = 经济崩溃了。

一般过去时:
The tent collapsed in the storm. = 帐篷在暴风雨中倒塌了。
The negotiations collapsed without agreement. = 谈判破裂，没有达成协议。


现在完成时:
The stock market has collapsed. = 股市已经崩盘了。
The system has collapsed due to a virus. = 系统由于病毒而崩溃了。


过去完成时:
The dam had collapsed before the rescue teams arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，大坝已经垮塌了。
The talks had collapsed before they could reach a compromise. = 在他们能达成妥协之前，会谈就已经破裂了。


将来时:
The old house will collapse soon. = 这座老房子很快就会倒塌。
If the pressure continues, the system will collapse. = 如果压力持续下去，系统将会崩溃。


虚拟语气:
If the support beams were removed, the structure would collapse. = 如果支撑梁被移除，结构就会倒塌。
I wish the deal hadn't collapsed. = 我真希望这笔交易没有失败。


被动语态:
The bridge was collapsed by the flood. = 桥梁被洪水冲垮了。(注意：此用法较少见，更常用主动语态)
The building was collapsed by the explosion. = 建筑物被爆炸摧毁了。(注意：此用法较少见，更常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Has the roof collapsed? = 屋顶塌了吗？
Did the negotiations collapse? = 谈判破裂了吗？


否定句:
The building hasn't collapsed yet. = 建筑物还没有倒塌。
The deal didn't collapse. = 这笔交易没有失败。


PHRASE->

collapsed star: 坍缩星
