prematurity:/ˌpriːmətʃʊəriti/| n. |早产；过早； преждевременность |The state of being born or happening before the expected or usual time.  It often refers to babies born before 37 weeks of gestation. |例句：The baby's prematurity caused some health concerns.  婴儿的早产引起了一些健康问题。|例句：The prematurity of his death shocked everyone. 他过早的去世震惊了所有人。|近义词：prematuration, early arrival,  hastiness|反义词：full-term, maturity, lateness |名词
