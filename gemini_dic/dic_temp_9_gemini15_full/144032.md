outrhymes:/ˌaʊtˈraɪmz/| n. |押韵，押尾韵|Words or lines that rhyme perfectly at the end.  Outrhymes are often used in poetry and songwriting to create a sense of rhythm and musicality.|例句：The poem's outrhymes were particularly effective in conveying its emotional impact. 这首诗的押尾韵在表达其情感冲击方面特别有效。|近义词：rhymes, end rhymes|反义词：None specifically;  the opposite concept would be lack of rhyme.
