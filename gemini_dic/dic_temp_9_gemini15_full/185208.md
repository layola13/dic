retrack:/riːˈtræk/ | vt. |重新追踪；重走（路线）；重新录制（录音）|To follow a track again; to go back over a path or route.  It can also refer to re-recording audio or video.|例句：We had to retrack the hiking trail because we got lost. 我们迷路了，不得不重新走回登山小路。

例句：The singer had to retrack the song because of a mistake.  歌手因为一个错误不得不重新录制这首歌。|近义词： retrace, re-trace, follow again |反义词： forge ahead, proceed
