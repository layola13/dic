overclassification:/ˌoʊvərˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |过度分类，分类过度|The act of classifying something into too many categories or subcategories, often leading to unnecessary complexity or confusion.  It can also refer to the state of being excessively categorized. |例句：The overclassification of documents hindered efficient retrieval of information.  过度分类的文件阻碍了信息的有效检索。

例句：The library's overclassification system made it difficult for patrons to find the books they needed. 图书馆的过度分类系统使得读者难以找到他们需要的书籍。


近义词：overcategorization, excessive categorization, over-indexing


反义词：underclassification, simplification


词性: noun
