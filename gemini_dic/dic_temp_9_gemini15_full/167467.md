preventer:/prɪˈvɛntər/| n. |预防者；防止器；预防物|Something or someone that prevents something from happening.|The seatbelt is a preventer of injuries in car accidents.  安全带是防止车祸受伤的装置。|  The new medication acts as a preventer of further infection.  这种新药可以防止进一步感染。| inhibitor, deterrent, safeguard | accelerator, promoter | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The device prevents accidents. = 该装置防止事故发生。
This medicine prevents infection. = 这药预防感染。


一般过去时:
He prevented the accident. = 他阻止了事故发生。
The policy prevented inflation. = 该政策阻止了通货膨胀。


现在完成时:
She has prevented many problems. = 她已经避免了许多问题。
They have prevented the spread of the disease. = 他们已经阻止了疾病的蔓延。


过去完成时:
He had prevented a disaster. = 他已经避免了一场灾难。
They had prevented the leak of information. = 他们已经阻止了信息的泄露。


将来时:
We will prevent future errors. = 我们将防止未来的错误。
The new system will prevent fraud. = 新系统将防止欺诈。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have prevented the crisis. = 如果我们早点行动，就能避免这场危机。
I wish I had prevented the argument. = 我真希望我能阻止这场争论。


被动语态:
Accidents are prevented by careful driving. = 小心驾驶可以防止事故发生。
The disease was prevented by vaccination. = 通过接种疫苗预防了这种疾病。


疑问句:
What can we do to prevent this? = 我们能做些什么来防止这种情况？
How can we prevent future failures? = 我们如何才能防止未来的失败？


否定句:
We cannot prevent all accidents. = 我们不能防止所有的事故。
He failed to prevent the catastrophe. = 他未能阻止这场灾难。


PHRASE->
prevent from: 防止…
prevent...from happening: 防止…发生
prevent the spread of: 阻止…的蔓延
take preventive measures: 采取预防措施
a fire preventer:  防火器
