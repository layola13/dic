look-up-to:/ˈlʊk ʌp tuː/ | v. |敬仰；仰慕；尊敬 |To admire and respect someone.  This phrase implies a sense of admiration and aspiration; the person being looked up to is often seen as a role model. |例句：Many young athletes look up to famous sports stars. 许多年轻运动员都敬仰著名的体育明星。

例句：I've always looked up to my grandfather for his wisdom and kindness. 我一直很敬仰我的祖父，因为他智慧和善良。


近义词：admire, respect, revere, esteem | 反义词：despise, scorn, disdain | 词性:动词
