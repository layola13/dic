butyryl:/ˈbjuːtɪrɪl/ | adj. | 丁酰基的；丁酰的 | Relating to or denoting the butyryl group (C3H7CO−), a radical derived from butyric acid.  | Butyrylcholinesterase is an enzyme that breaks down acetylcholine. 丁酰胆碱酯酶是一种分解乙酰胆碱的酶。 | 近义词: butanoic, n-butyryl | 反义词: N/A
