pteridology:/ˌtɛrɪˈdɒlədʒi/| n. |蕨类植物学|The scientific study of ferns and other pteridophytes (seedless vascular plants).  It encompasses their taxonomy, morphology, physiology, ecology, evolution, and geographic distribution. |Example:  Her research focuses on the pteridology of the Amazon rainforest.  她的研究集中在亚马逊雨林的蕨类植物学上。|近义词：fern science, pteridophyte botany|反义词：None.
