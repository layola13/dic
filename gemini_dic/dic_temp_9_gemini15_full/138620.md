nucleated:/ˈnjuːklɪeɪtɪd/| adj.|有核的；含核的|Having a nucleus or nuclei.  The term is commonly used to describe cells, atoms, or other structures containing a central core or nucleus.|例句：Most human cells are nucleated. = 大多数人体细胞是有核的。

例句：The nucleated red blood cells of amphibians and birds are different from those of mammals.=两栖动物和鸟类的有核红细胞与哺乳动物的不同。


近义词：nuclear (in some contexts)


反义词：anucleate, enucleated
