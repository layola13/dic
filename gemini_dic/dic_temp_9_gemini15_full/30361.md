centennials:/ˌsɛnˈtɛniəl/| n. |百年纪念；百年庆典；一百周年纪念|A centennial is the 100th anniversary of an event or the founding of something.  It often involves celebrations and commemorations.|Example: The city is planning a grand centennial celebration to mark its 100th anniversary.|近义词：centenary, anniversary|反义词：无
