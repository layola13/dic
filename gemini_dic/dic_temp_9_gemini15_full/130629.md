nonadministratively: /ˌnɒnədˈmɪnɪstrətɪvli/ | adv. | 非行政性地，非管理性地 |  Not in an administrative or managerial capacity; without administrative involvement or oversight.  | Example: The decision was made nonadministratively, bypassing the usual channels.  这个决定是非行政性地做出的，绕过了通常的渠道。 | 近义词：unofficially, informally, independently | 反义词：administratively, officially


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.  Therefore, tense and voice are applied to the verb it modifies, not to the adverb itself.
