renotarizes:/ˌriːˈnoʊtəraɪzɪz/| v.|重新公证；再次公证|To notarize again or anew.  The process involves verifying the authenticity of a document or signature a second time by a notary public.|例句：The lawyer had to renotarize the document after a clerical error was discovered. = 律师必须在发现文书错误后重新公证该文件。


The document needed renotarizing because the original notary's stamp was illegible. =  由于原公证员的印章无法辨认，文件需要重新公证。


近义词：recertify, revalidate|反义词：invalidate, decertify|动词
