fuselike:/ˈfjuːzlaɪk/| adj. |像保险丝的；易熔的 |Resembling a fuse in appearance or function.  Often used to describe something that is thin, easily broken, or prone to melting or burning out under stress. |例句：The thin, fuselike wire snapped under the pressure.  那根细细的、像保险丝一样的电线在压力下断了。 The old building's structure was fuselike, vulnerable to even minor tremors. 那座老建筑的结构像保险丝一样，即使是轻微的震动也容易损坏。|近义词：fragile, brittle, flammable, delicate |反义词：strong, sturdy, robust, durable
