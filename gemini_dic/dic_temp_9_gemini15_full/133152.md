nonethically:/ˌnɒnˈeθɪkəli/ | adv. | 不道德地，违反道德地 | acting in a manner that is not ethical; morally wrong.  | The company was accused of acting nonethically in its dealings with suppliers.  这家公司被指控在与供应商的交易中行为不道德。 | ethically | morally |


USAGE->

They often make nonethical decisions. = 他们经常做出不道德的决定。


一般过去时:

She acted nonethically by leaking confidential information. = 她泄露机密信息的行为是不道德的。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
