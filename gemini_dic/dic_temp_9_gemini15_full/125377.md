multimillionaires:/ˌmʌltiˈmɪljəneərz/| n. |数百万富翁|People who have assets worth several million dollars or more. They are often entrepreneurs, investors, or inheritors of significant wealth.|例句：Many multimillionaires donate a large portion of their wealth to charity. 许多百万富翁将大部分财富捐献给慈善机构。|例句：Becoming a multimillionaire requires hard work, smart investments, and often a bit of luck. 成为百万富翁需要努力工作、聪明的投资，以及一些运气。|近义词：millionaires, tycoons, magnates|反义词：paupers, indigents
