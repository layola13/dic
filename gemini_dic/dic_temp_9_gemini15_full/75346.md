frostweed:/ˈfrɔːstwiːd/| n. |霜草，冰霜草|A plant that shows ice crystals on its stems in freezing weather.  Frostweed is a common name for several plants, most notably those in the genus *Verbesina*, which exude water that freezes into ice ribbons on their stems in cold weather. This phenomenon is called "frost heaving." |例句：The frostweed's stems were adorned with intricate ice sculptures created by the escaping moisture. 霜草的茎上装饰着由逸出的水分形成的精致冰雕。

The frostweed plants are most often seen in the southeastern United States, displaying their icy formations during cold snaps.  霜草植物最常见于美国东南部，在寒流期间展示其冰冷的形态。|近义词：ice plant (in some contexts), winter plant |反义词：None specifically, but words implying warmth or absence of frost could be considered antonyms contextually.
