knapbottle:/ˈnæpbɒtl/| n. |小酒瓶，酒壶|A small bottle, especially one for carrying liquor.  The term is now largely archaic or dialectal, referring to a small, often portable bottle used for carrying alcoholic beverages.  It evokes a sense of rustic or old-fashioned imagery.|例句：He pulled a knapbottle from his pocket and took a swig. 他从口袋里掏出一个酒瓶，喝了一口。|例句：The old shepherd always carried a knapbottle with him on his long walks across the moor.这位老牧羊人在穿越荒野的长途跋涉中总是随身携带一个酒瓶。|近义词：flask, hip flask, pocket flask |反义词：large bottle, cask
