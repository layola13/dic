frenetically:/frɛˈnɛtɪkli/| adv.|狂乱地；疯狂地；极度兴奋地 |  In a frantic, uncontrolled, or highly agitated manner.  Characterized by uncontrolled excitement or wild activity. |例句：He worked frenetically to finish the project before the deadline. 他疯狂地工作以在截止日期前完成项目。

例句：The crowd surged frenetically toward the stage.  人群狂热地涌向舞台。


近义词：frantically, wildly, frantically, feverishly, hysterically

反义词：calmly, placidly, serenely, quietly, peacefully

词源：源自法语 *frénétique*，最终源自希腊语 *phrenitis* (phren- "diaphragm, mind" + -itis "inflammation")，指大脑发炎，即狂躁症。  因此，frenetically 描述的是一种类似于精神错乱的极度兴奋或活动状态。
