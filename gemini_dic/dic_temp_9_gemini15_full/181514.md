rekeyboarding:/ˌriːˈkiːbɔːrdɪŋ/| v.|重新键入，重新打字|The act of typing something again, often to correct errors or to transfer data from one format to another.|例句：The document needed rekeyboarding due to numerous typos.  这份文件由于有很多错字需要重新打字。|例句：After the system crash, we had to perform a complete rekeyboarding of the database. 系统崩溃后，我们不得不重新输入整个数据库。|近义词：retyping, re-entering|反义词：None
