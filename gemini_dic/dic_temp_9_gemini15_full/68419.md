falsification:/ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|伪造；篡改；歪曲|The act of falsifying something; the state of being falsified.  It can refer to the deliberate alteration or misrepresentation of information, evidence, or documents. |例句：The falsification of documents led to his arrest.  伪造文件导致了他的逮捕。|例句：The scientist was accused of data falsification in his research.  这位科学家被指控在其研究中篡改数据。|近义词：forgery, fabrication, adulteration, misrepresentation|反义词：verification, authentication, truth, accuracy|
