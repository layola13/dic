non-forest:/ˈnɑːnˈfɔːrɪst/| adj. |非森林的，非林地的|Not relating to or characteristic of forests; outside or beyond a forest.  The term often describes areas of land that are not covered by trees or wooded areas, encompassing various land uses and ecosystems.  |例句：The non-forest areas are primarily grasslands and agricultural fields. = 非森林地区主要是草原和农田。

The non-forest land was converted for urban development. = 非林地被改造成城市发展用地。|近义词：non-wooded, treeless, open|反义词：forested, wooded
