majored:/ˈmeɪdʒərd/| v.|主修，专攻|To study a particular subject as the main focus of one's studies, especially at a college or university.|例句：She majored in English literature at Oxford. 她牛津大学主修英国文学。|例句：He majored in physics and went on to a successful career in engineering. 他主修物理，后来在工程领域取得了成功。|近义词：specialized in, concentrated on|反义词：minored in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He majors in computer science. = 他主修计算机科学。
She majors in history and French. = 她主修历史和法语。

一般过去时:
I majored in biology during my undergraduate years. = 我本科期间主修生物学。
He majored in economics at Harvard. = 他在哈佛大学主修经济学。

现在完成时:
She has majored in psychology and now works as a therapist. = 她主修心理学，现在是一名治疗师。
They have majored in different fields but work together on this project. = 他们主修不同的领域，但在这个项目上合作。

过去完成时:
Before applying for the job, he had majored in mechanical engineering. = 在申请这份工作之前，他主修机械工程。
Having majored in marketing, she easily secured a position in a large firm. = 因为主修市场营销，她很容易就获得了一家大公司的工作。

将来时:
I will major in medicine at university. = 我将在大学主修医学。
She will major in law, following in her father's footsteps. = 她将主修法律，跟随她父亲的脚步。

虚拟语气:
If I had majored in business, I might have a different career now. = 如果我主修商业，我现在可能会有不同的职业。
I wish I had majored in something more practical. = 我希望我主修一些更实际的东西。

被动语态:
This subject is majored in by many students. = 许多学生都主修这门科目。(This sentence is less common in active voice)
The course was majored in by a large number of students. = 这门课程被许多学生主修。(This sentence is less common in active voice)

疑问句:
What did you major in at university? = 你大学主修什么？
What will she major in next year? = 她明年将主修什么？

否定句:
He did not major in engineering; he studied art history. = 他没有主修工程；他学习艺术史。
She didn't major in anything related to science. = 她没有主修任何与科学相关的专业。


PHRASE->
major in = 主修
major subject = 主修科目
major field = 主要领域
double major = 双学位
minor in = 副修
