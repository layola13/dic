deleting:/dɪˈliːtɪŋ/| v. |删除；清除|The act of removing something, usually data or text, from a system or document.  It implies a purposeful action of erasure.|例句：I'm deleting all the unnecessary files from my computer. 我正在从我的电脑中删除所有不必要的文件。

例句：She is deleting the offensive comments from her social media post.  她在删除她社交媒体帖子上的冒犯性评论。


近义词：erasing, removing, expunging

反义词：adding, inserting, creating

词性：动名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am deleting the email. = 我正在删除邮件。
He is deleting the photos. = 他正在删除照片。

一般过去时:
I deleted the file yesterday. = 我昨天删除了文件。
She deleted her account. = 她删除了她的账户。

现在完成时:
I have deleted all the spam emails. = 我已经删除了所有垃圾邮件。
He has deleted the message. = 他已经删除了信息。

过去完成时:
I had deleted the document before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经删除了文件。
She had deleted the photos before I saw them. = 我看到之前，她已经删除了照片。

将来时:
I will delete the file later. = 我稍后会删除文件。
He will delete the account tomorrow. = 他明天会删除账户。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would delete that comment. = 如果我是你，我会删除那条评论。
I wish I had deleted that email. = 我真希望我删除了那封邮件。

被动语态:
The file was deleted by mistake. = 文件被误删了。
The account has been deleted. = 账户已经被删除了。

疑问句:
Are you deleting that file? = 你要删除那个文件吗？
Have you deleted the photos yet? = 你已经删除照片了吗？

否定句:
I didn't delete the file. = 我没有删除文件。
She hasn't deleted the message yet. = 她还没有删除信息。


PHRASE->
delete a file = 删除文件
delete a message = 删除信息
delete a user = 删除用户
