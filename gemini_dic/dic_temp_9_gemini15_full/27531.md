campylospermous:/ˌkæmpɪˈloʊspɜːrməs/| adj.|弯胚的|Having a curved embryo.  The term refers to seeds whose embryos are curved or bent, rather than straight.| Example:  The campylospermous seeds of this plant species are easily distinguishable from those of related species.  这种植物的弯胚种子很容易与相关物种区分开来。|近义词：curved-embryo (informal)|反义词：orthospermous
