precious:/ˈprɛʃəs/| adj.|珍贵的，宝贵的，贵重的|extremely valuable or important; cherished|例句：This necklace is a precious family heirloom. 这条项链是珍贵的传家宝。
例句：Time is precious, so don't waste it. 时间宝贵，别浪费它。|近义词：valuable, dear, priceless, treasured|反义词：worthless, valueless, cheap


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This ring is precious to me. = 这枚戒指对我来说很珍贵。
These memories are precious. = 这些回忆很珍贵。

一般过去时:
The painting was precious to her grandmother. = 这幅画对她祖母来说很珍贵。
He considered his friendship with her precious. = 他认为与她的友谊弥足珍贵。

现在完成时:
I have always considered my family precious. = 我一直认为我的家人很珍贵。
We have learned that time is precious. = 我们已经明白时间是宝贵的。

过去完成时:
She had already realized that the opportunity was precious. = 她已经意识到这个机会很宝贵。
They had treasured that moment as precious. = 他们珍视那一刻，视之为珍宝。

将来时:
This experience will be precious to me in the future. = 这段经历将来对我来说将弥足珍贵。
These skills will prove precious in your career. = 这些技能在你的职业生涯中将非常宝贵。

虚拟语气:
If I had known how precious the moment was, I would have savored it more. = 如果我知道那一刻有多珍贵，我会更加珍惜它。
I wish I had appreciated how precious my time with my grandfather was. = 我希望我当时能欣赏与祖父相处时光的珍贵。

被动语态:
The vase was considered precious by the museum. = 这个花瓶被博物馆视为珍品。
The diamond was described as a precious stone. = 这颗钻石被描述为一颗珍贵的宝石。

疑问句:
Is this jewel precious? = 这颗珠宝珍贵吗？
Are these stamps precious to you? = 这些邮票对你来说珍贵吗？

否定句:
That information isn't precious. = 那信息并不珍贵。
His words weren't precious enough to be remembered. = 他的话不足以让人记住。
