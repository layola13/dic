paperback:/ˈpeɪpəbæk/| n. |平装本，简装本 |A paperback is a book that has a soft cover, as opposed to a hardback or hardcover book.  They are generally less expensive than hardback books. |I bought a paperback copy of the novel at the bookstore. 我在书店买了一本该小说的平装本。  She prefers reading paperbacks because they are lighter and easier to carry. 她更喜欢读平装书，因为它们更轻便，更容易携带。|hardback, softcover |hardcover
