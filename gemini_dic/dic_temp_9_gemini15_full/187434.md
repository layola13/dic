roiling:/ˈrɔɪlɪŋ/ | adj. v. |翻滚的；动荡的；扰乱的 |  agitated; turbulent; churning; to make (a liquid) churn or bubble; to disturb or make agitated |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "roiling" describes something in a state of turbulent motion, often involving liquids or emotions.  It suggests a chaotic and restless energy.  As a verb, it means to make something churn or bubble, or to disturb and agitate something.


例句:
The roiling sea threatened to capsize the small boat. = 翻滚的海浪威胁着要使小船倾覆。

The roiling emotions of the crowd were palpable. = 人群中翻腾的情绪是显而易见的。


近义词:
turbulent, churning, bubbling, agitated, restless, stormy, tumultuous


反义词:
calm, peaceful, tranquil, still, serene


词性:
adjective, verb
