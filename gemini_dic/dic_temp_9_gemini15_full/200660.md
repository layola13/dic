sledding:/ˈslɛdɪŋ/| n. |雪橇，雪橇运动|The act of riding on a sled, typically down a snowy hill.  It's a popular winter activity for children and adults alike.|例句：The children enjoyed sledding down the snowy hill.孩子们喜欢在雪坡上滑雪橇。

例句：We went sledding last weekend and had a blast! 我们上周末去滑雪橇，玩得很开心！


近义词： tobogganing, sleighing |反义词：None directly applicable.  The opposite would be activities done in warmer weather. |词性：名词
