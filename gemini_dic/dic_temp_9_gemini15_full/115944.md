marrer:/ˈmærər/| vt.|毁坏；糟蹋；破坏；弄糟|To spoil or ruin something completely.|例句：He marred the painting with a clumsy brushstroke. 他用笨拙的笔触毁坏了这幅画。|近义词：spoil, ruin, damage, impair|反义词：improve, enhance, mend, repair


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He marrs his chances with his arrogance. = 他因为傲慢而毁了自己的机会。
The constant noise marrs the beauty of the scenery. = 持续的噪音破坏了风景的美感。


一般过去时:
The storm marred the picnic. = 暴风雨破坏了野餐。
His careless words marred their relationship. = 他粗心的话语破坏了他们的关系。


现在完成时:
The scandal has marred his reputation. = 这场丑闻毁了他的名誉。
Years of neglect have marred the building's appearance. = 多年的忽视使这座建筑物的外观破败不堪。


过去完成时:
The previous owner had marred the house with numerous additions. = 前任房主在房屋上增建了许多东西，从而破坏了房屋的美观。
The argument had marred their previously harmonious relationship. = 那场争吵破坏了他们之前和谐的关系。


将来时:
The upcoming election may mar the peace of the country. = 即将来临的选举可能会破坏国家的和平。
Further delays will mar the project's timeline. = 进一步的延误将破坏项目的进度安排。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been so careless, he wouldn't have marred the project. = 如果他不那么粗心，他就不会破坏这个项目了。
I wish he hadn't marred the painting with his signature. = 我真希望他没有在画上签字。


被动语态:
The reputation of the company was marred by the recent scandal. = 公司的声誉因最近的丑闻而受损。
The plans were marred by unforeseen circumstances. = 计划因不可预见的情况而受阻。


疑问句:
Did the rain mar the outdoor concert? = 雨是否破坏了户外音乐会？
Will the new regulations mar the company's efficiency? = 新规章制度是否会影响公司的效率？


否定句:
The new policy did not mar the company's progress. = 新政策并没有影响公司的发展。
The slight scratch did not mar the beauty of the antique vase. = 轻微的划痕并没有影响古董花瓶的美观。


PHRASE->
marred by = 被…破坏的
marred with = 混杂着…的;带有…瑕疵的
severely marred = 严重损坏的
irrevocably marred = 不可挽回地破坏的
deeply marred = 深受损害的
