nailer:/ˈneɪlər/| n.|钉子工，高手，能手|A nailer is a person skilled in using nails, or a person who is exceptionally skilled at a particular task.|例句：He's a nailer with a hammer. 他是个用锤子很熟练的人。 He's a real nailer at basketball. 他篮球打得真棒。|近义词：expert, master, pro|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner


USAGE->

He nails the boards to the wall. = 他把木板钉在墙上。
She nails down the details of the contract. = 她敲定了合同的细节。

一般过去时:
