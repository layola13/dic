emphasis:/ˈɛmfəsɪs/ | n. |强调，重点 |The emphasis is on quality, not quantity.  重点在于质量，而不是数量。|近义词：stress, accent, highlight |反义词：neglect, disregard |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The emphasis is on customer satisfaction. = 重点是客户满意度。
His emphasis lies in environmental protection. = 他强调的是环境保护。


一般过去时:
The emphasis was on teamwork. = 重点是团队合作。
Her emphasis placed on detail was crucial. = 她对细节的强调至关重要。


现在完成时:
The emphasis has been on efficiency. = 一直以来都强调效率。
There has been an emphasis on renewable energy. = 一直以来都强调可再生能源。


过去完成时:
The emphasis had been on cost reduction before the project started. = 项目开始前，重点是降低成本。
They had placed emphasis on safety procedures. = 他们之前一直强调安全规程。


将来时:
The emphasis will be on innovation. = 重点将放在创新上。
The government will place emphasis on education reform. = 政府将重视教育改革。


虚拟语气:
If the emphasis were on prevention, we would have fewer problems. = 如果重点放在预防上，我们将会有更少的问题。
I wish the emphasis were on long-term sustainability. = 我希望重点是长期的可持续性。


被动语态:
Emphasis was placed on the importance of education. = 重视教育的重要性。
Emphasis is being placed on sustainable development. = 正在重视可持续发展。


疑问句:
What is the emphasis of your presentation? = 你演讲的重点是什么？
Where is the emphasis in this sentence? = 这句话的重点在哪里？


否定句:
There was no emphasis on the details. = 没有强调细节。
He didn't place any emphasis on the deadlines. = 他并没有强调截止日期。


PHRASE->
place emphasis on = 强调

put emphasis on = 强调
lay emphasis on = 强调
with emphasis = 着重地


词源:
From Old French *emphase, from Latin emphasis, from Greek ἔμφασις (émphasis), from ἐμφαίνειν (emphaínein) "to show clearly," from ἐν- (en-) "in" + φαίνειν (phaínein) "to show."
