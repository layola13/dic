demission:/dɪˈmɪʃən/| n. |辞职；辞去职务；放弃；让位|The act of resigning from a position or office; the relinquishing of a right, claim, or office.  It often implies a formal or official resignation.|例句：The CEO's demission from the company shocked everyone.  首席执行官辞职的消息震惊了所有人。

例句：His demission of his claim to the throne paved the way for his brother's ascension. 他放弃王位继承权为其兄弟的登基铺平了道路。


近义词：resignation, relinquishment, abdication, retirement|反义词：assumption of office, appointment, accession


词性：名词
