knickknackery:/ˈnɪkˌnækəri/| n. |小摆设；小玩意儿；琐碎物品 | A collection of small, decorative objects, often considered to be of little value.  It often implies a cluttered or disorganized collection. | The attic was filled with knickknackery, from old photos to chipped teacups.  阁楼里堆满了小玩意儿，从旧照片到破损的茶杯应有尽有。|trinkets, gewgaws, bits and pieces, odds and ends|orderliness, tidiness|名词
