conversational:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəl/ /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 会话的；谈话的；非正式的 | Relating to or suitable for conversation; informal in style.  |例句：The conversational tone of the letter made it feel more personal. 这封信的口语化语气使其感觉更个人化。

例句：Her conversational style made the lecture engaging for the audience. 她轻松的谈话风格使讲座对听众更具吸引力。


近义词：informal, colloquial, chatty, familiar | 反义词：formal, stiff, official


词源：来自conversation（谈话）+ -al（形容词后缀）,  conversation 源自中古英语 conversacioun，源自古法语 conversacion，源自拉丁语 conversatio,  conversationem (noun of action from converseri to converse), from con- "together" + versari "to turn, move about," from vertere "to turn."
