It often involves detailed descriptions of algal morphology, reproductive structures, and habitats.  Phycography is a subfield of phycology (the study of algae).|例句：The phycography of the lake revealed a diverse community of algae.  湖泊的藻类植物志揭示了一个多样化的藻类群落。|近义词：algology, phycology|反义词：N/A
