moisturizer:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪzər/ | n. | 保湿霜，润肤霜 | A cosmetic cream or lotion used to moisturize the skin.  It helps to retain skin moisture and prevent dryness. |  She applies moisturizer every morning and night. 她每天早晚都涂抹保湿霜。  I need a good moisturizer for my dry skin. 我需要一款适合我干性皮肤的保湿霜。 | lotion, cream, balm | drying agent (反义词并非完全对立，而是指具有相反作用的物质) | 名词
