diagonals:/daɪˈæɡənəlz/| n. |对角线，斜线|Lines connecting non-adjacent vertices of a polygon or polyhedron.  Diagonal lines are lines that run from one corner to another, not along a side. |例句：The diagonals of a square are equal in length. 正方形的对角线长度相等。

The diagonals of a rectangle bisect each other.  矩形的对角线互相平分。


近义词：slant, oblique |反义词：parallel, perpendicular |词性:名词
