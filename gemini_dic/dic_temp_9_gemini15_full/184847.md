resynchronized:/ˌriːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzd/| v. |重新同步，使重新同步 |To bring back into synchronization; to restore to a synchronized state.  The process involves adjusting timing or phases to achieve agreement or consistency.|例句：The engineers resynchronized the clocks across the network. 工程师们重新同步了网络上的时钟。

The software automatically resynchronized the data after the power outage. 软件在断电后自动重新同步了数据。


近义词：realign, harmonize, coordinate | 反义词：desynchronize, misalign
