inhabitable: /ɪnˈhæbɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可居住的，适于居住的 | Able to be lived in; suitable for habitation.  | The island is habitable, with plenty of fresh water and fertile land.  这个岛屿适宜居住，拥有充足的淡水和肥沃的土地。  The desert is largely uninhabitable due to extreme temperatures. 由于极端气温，沙漠大部分地区不宜居住。 | uninhabitable, livable, habitable  | uninhabitable


USAGE->

The planet is habitable. = 这个星球适宜居住。
This house is habitable. = 这房子适宜居住。

现在完成时:

Scientists have found a habitable planet. = 科学家们发现了一颗适宜居住的星球。
