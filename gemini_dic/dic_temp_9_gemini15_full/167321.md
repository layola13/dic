pretranslate:/ˌpriːˈtrænzleɪt/ | vt. |预翻译，预先翻译|To translate something before it is officially needed or used, often as a preparation for a later, more formal translation.|例句：We pretranslate all documents before sending them to the client for final review. 我们在将所有文件发送给客户进行最终审查之前会先进行预翻译。|近义词：translate in advance, prepare a translation|反义词：post-translate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pretranslate the documents every week. = 他们每周都预先翻译文件。
She pretranslates subtitles for many films. = 她为许多电影预先翻译字幕。


一般过去时:
He pretranslated the speech before the conference. = 他在会议之前预先翻译了演讲稿。
We pretranslated the manual last month. = 我们上个月预先翻译了手册。


现在完成时:
They have pretranslated all the necessary materials. = 他们已经预先翻译了所有必要的材料。
She has pretranslated the website content. = 她已经预先翻译了网站内容。


过去完成时:
