demonolatry:/ˌdiːməˈnɒlətri/ | n. |恶魔崇拜 |The worship of demons.  Demonolatry is a practice involving the invocation and worship of demons, often for personal gain or power. It is a form of occultism that is distinct from satanism, though there can be overlap.  |Example:  The historical records show evidence of demonolatry in various ancient cultures.  历史记录显示在各种古代文化中都有恶魔崇拜的证据。|近义词:demon worship, devil worship |反义词:angelolatry, theolatry
