crore:/krɔːr/| n. |千万；一千万|A crore is a unit of ten million (10,000,000) used primarily in South Asia, particularly in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.  It's a convenient way to express large numbers. |The company's annual revenue is over two crore rupees. 这家公司的年收入超过两千万卢比。|million, ten million|thousand, hundred|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The population of the city is one crore. = 这个城市的人口是一千万。
He earns a crore rupees annually. = 他每年赚一千万卢比。


一般过去时:
