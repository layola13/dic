re-seeding:/ˌriːˈsiːdɪŋ/ | v. | 再播种；重新播种 | The act of planting seeds again, often after a previous planting has failed or to improve the existing growth.  This involves preparing the soil, scattering new seeds, and providing suitable conditions for germination and growth. | Example:  The farmer is re-seeding the field after the drought.  农民在干旱之后正在重新播种田地。

The lawn needs re-seeding in several bare patches.  草坪在几个光秃秃的地方需要重新播种。


近义词：replanting, sowing again, resewing (in the context of sowing seeds)
反义词：harvesting, reaping
