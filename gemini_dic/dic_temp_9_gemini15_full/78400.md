get-down:/ɡɛtˈdaʊn/ | v. | 使沮丧；使灰心；使降低；写下；开始认真工作；(非正式)跳舞 | To make someone feel unhappy or depressed; to reduce something in amount, level, or intensity; to write something down; to start working seriously; (informal) to dance. | 例句：The bad news really got me down.  坏消息真让我沮丧。
The government is trying to get inflation down. 政府正在努力降低通货膨胀。

近义词：depress, discourage, lower, reduce, write, commence, dance
反义词：cheer up, encourage, elevate, raise,  ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bad news gets me down. = 坏消息让我沮丧。
He gets his work down quickly. = 他很快就把工作做完了。

一般过去时:
That movie got me down. = 那部电影让我沮丧。
She got all the important information down. = 她把所有重要的信息都记了下来。

现在完成时:
This situation has really gotten me down. = 这种状况真的让我很沮丧。
I have gotten all the notes down. = 我已经把所有的笔记都记下来了。

过去完成时:
I had already gotten my notes down before the lecture started. = 讲座开始之前我已经把笔记记好了。
The team had gotten their project down to a manageable size. = 团队已经把他们的项目规模缩减到可控的程度。


将来时:
This news will get me down. = 这个消息会让我沮丧。
I will get all the details down. = 我会把所有细节都记下来。


虚拟语气:
If I knew it would get me down, I wouldn't watch it. = 如果我知道它会让我沮丧，我就不会看了。
If only I had gotten the address down. = 但愿我当时记下了地址。


被动语态:
The price was got down by 10%. = 价格降低了10%。
The details need to be got down accurately. = 细节需要准确地记录下来。


疑问句:
Does this problem get you down? = 这个问题让你沮丧吗？
Did you get the recipe down correctly? = 你把菜谱记正确了吗？


否定句:
The news didn't get me down at all. = 这个消息一点也没让我沮丧。
I didn't get his phone number down. = 我没有记下他的电话号码。


PHRASE->
get down to business = 开始认真工作
get down on your knees = 跪下
get down with = 与...相处融洽；开始做
get down and dirty = 认真处理；不择手段
get someone down = 使某人沮丧
