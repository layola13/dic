expatiation:/ˌɛkspˌeɪʃɪˈeɪʃən/| n. |详述；详细解释；发挥；详论 |The act of expounding or explaining in detail; a detailed explanation or discussion.  |例句：His expatiation on the subject bored the audience.  他的冗长解释让听众感到厌烦。 |例句：The professor's expatiation clarified the complex theory. 教授的详尽解释阐明了复杂的理论。|近义词：elaboration, explication, elucidation, discourse |反义词：summary, brevity, condensation |名词
