oilfield:/ˈɔɪlfiːld/ | n. |油田 | An area of land where oil is extracted from the ground.  Oilfields are typically characterized by drilling rigs, pipelines, and other infrastructure related to petroleum production. | Example: The North Sea oilfield is a major source of oil for Europe.  北海油田是欧洲主要的石油来源。  The discovery of a new oilfield boosted the country's economy. 新油田的发现促进了该国的经济发展。|近义词：oil deposit, petroleum field |反义词：N/A |名词
