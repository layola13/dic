overbaiting: /ˌoʊvərˈbeɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 过度诱饵；使用过量诱饵 | The act of using more bait than is necessary or advisable, often in fishing or trapping.  This can be counterproductive, as it can scare away the target species or attract unwanted ones. |例句：Overbaiting the hook resulted in a tangled mess and no fish.  过度使用鱼饵导致鱼钩缠结，一条鱼也没钓到。  |近义词：overloading, excess baiting |反义词：underbaiting

词源:
The word "overbaiting" is a compound word formed from "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and "baiting" (the act of using bait).  The suffix "-ing" forms the gerund or present participle, indicating the action or process.
