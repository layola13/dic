词源:

The word "cinnamoned" is derived from the word "cinnamon," ultimately from the Greek word "kinnamomon," which itself may have originated from a Dravidian language.  The "-ed" suffix indicates the past participle form of the verb "to cinnamon," meaning to add cinnamon to something.
