detestate:/dɪˈtɛsteɪt/| vt.|痛恨，厌恶|To feel intense dislike or disgust for.  The word implies a strong feeling of aversion, often bordering on hatred.|例句：I detestate hypocrisy. 我痛恨虚伪。

例句：She detests the smell of cigarette smoke. 她讨厌香烟味。


近义词：abhor, loathe, abominate, despise

反义词：adore, love, cherish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I detestate his arrogance. = 我痛恨他的傲慢。
She detests loud noises. = 她讨厌吵闹的声音。


一般过去时:
He detested the food they served. = 他讨厌他们提供的食物。
I detested her behaviour. = 我讨厌她的行为。


现在完成时:
I have detested spinach my whole life. = 我一辈子都讨厌菠菜。
She has detested that man since they met. = 从他们见面起她就讨厌那个男人。


过去完成时:
He had detested her lies before he discovered the truth. = 在他发现真相之前，他就讨厌她的谎言了。
I had detested the job long before I quit. = 在我辞职很久以前，我就讨厌这份工作了。


将来时:
I will detest this weather forever. = 我将永远讨厌这种天气。
She will detest him more and more. = 她会越来越讨厌他。


虚拟语气:
If I knew he was such a liar, I would detest him. = 如果我知道他是这样的大骗子，我就会讨厌他。
I wish I had not detested him so much. = 我真希望我以前没有那么讨厌他。


被动语态:
His actions are detested by many. = 他的行为受到许多人的厌恶。
His ideas are detested by the public. = 他的想法受到公众的厌恶。


疑问句:
Do you detest this music? = 你讨厌这音乐吗？
Does he detest working long hours? = 他讨厌长时间工作吗？


否定句:
I don't detest all politicians. = 我并非讨厌所有政客。
She doesn't detest him as much as she pretends. = 她并没有像她假装的那样讨厌他。


PHRASE->
There are not many common phrasal verbs with "detestate".  The word itself is fairly strong and rarely needs modification.  Instead, consider phrases that modify the *object* of detestation:  to detestate someone's behavior, to detestate the very thought of something, to detestate with a passion, to detestate something profoundly, to detestate something utterly.
