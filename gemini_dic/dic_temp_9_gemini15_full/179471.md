redwing:/ˈrɛdˌwɪŋ/| n.|红翅黑鹂；红翼鞋|A redwing is a type of bird, specifically the red-winged blackbird (Agelaius phoeniceus), known for the distinctive red and yellow shoulder patches on the males.  It can also refer to a type of shoe or boot.|例句：The male redwing’s vibrant red epaulets are easily visible in the marsh. 雄性红翅黑鹂鲜艳的红色肩章在沼泽地里很容易看到。


The redwings were singing their cheerful song in the reeds. 红翅黑鹂在芦苇丛中唱着欢乐的歌。|近义词：blackbird (general term)|反义词: N/A|名词
