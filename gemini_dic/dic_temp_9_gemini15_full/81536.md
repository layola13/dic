gravities:/ˈɡrævətiz/| n. |重力；严重性；庄严；重要性 |The force that attracts a body towards the center of the earth, or towards any other physical body having mass.|例句：The apple fell to the ground due to gravity. 苹果由于重力落到地上。

例句：The gravity of the situation wasn't immediately apparent.  局势的严重性并非立即显而易见。


近义词：seriousness, importance, weight


反义词：lightness, insignificance
