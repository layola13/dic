rifleman:/ˈrɪflmən/| n. |步枪手，射手|A rifleman is a soldier trained to use a rifle.  They are typically infantry soldiers skilled in marksmanship and infantry tactics.|例句：The rifleman took careful aim before firing.  步枪手在射击前仔细瞄准。|例句：He was a highly skilled rifleman, known for his accuracy. 他是一位技艺精湛的步枪手，以其精准著称。|近义词：shooter, marksman, sharpshooter|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with roles like artilleryman or support staff.|名词
