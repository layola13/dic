reindeers:/ˈreɪnˌdɪrz/| n.|驯鹿|The plural of reindeer.  Reindeer are large deer with antlers found in Arctic and subarctic regions. They are known for their ability to withstand extreme cold and their role in Santa Claus's sleigh. |例句：Santa Claus's reindeer pulled his sleigh across the night sky. 圣诞老人的驯鹿拉着他的雪橇飞越夜空。

例句：Many reindeer migrate long distances each year in search of food. 许多驯鹿每年迁徙很长的距离寻找食物。


近义词：caribou (in North America)  |反义词：无


词性：名词(复数)
