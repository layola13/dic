inattentively: /ˌɪnəˈtɛntɪvli/ | adv. | 不专心致志地；粗心大意地 |  Lacking attention; carelessly.  |例句：He listened inattentively to the lecture, frequently checking his phone. 他心不在焉地听讲座，经常查看手机。


例句：She drove inattentively and nearly caused an accident. 她开车心不在焉，差点造成事故。


近义词：carelessly, heedlessly, negligently, thoughtlessly


反义词：attentively, carefully, thoughtfully


词性：副词
