The shape and size of the gonys are important characteristics used in bird identification.  | Example: The sharp gonys of the falcon allowed it to efficiently tear its prey.  这只猎鹰锋利的下颌骨能够有效地撕裂猎物。 | 近义词：lower mandible, lower jaw | 反义词：maxilla (upper mandible)
