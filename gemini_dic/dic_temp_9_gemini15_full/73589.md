foretell:/fɔːˈtɛl/| vt.|预言，预示|To say what will happen in the future.|The fortune teller foretold a great misfortune.|predict, prophesy, augur|—|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foretells the future using tarot cards. = 他用塔罗牌预言未来。
She foretells the outcome of the election. = 她预言选举的结果。


一般过去时:
The oracle foretold the king's death. = 预言家预言了国王的死亡。
The prophet foretold a time of great prosperity. = 先知预言了一个繁荣昌盛的时代。


现在完成时:
The ancient texts have foretold this event for centuries. = 古代文本几个世纪以来一直预言这一事件。
No one has foretold such a dramatic shift in the market. = 没有人预言市场会有如此剧烈的变化。


过去完成时:
They had foretold the storm's arrival hours before it hit. = 他们在暴风雨来临前的几个小时就预言了它的到来。
The seers had foretold the king's downfall years ago. = 预言家几年前就预言了国王的垮台。


将来时:
Scientists will foretell the path of the hurricane. = 科学家们将预言飓风的路径。
Experts will foretell the economic trends for the next year. = 专家们将预测明年的经济趋势。


虚拟语气:
If the soothsayer had foretold the earthquake, many lives could have been saved. = 如果预言家预言了地震，许多生命本来可以挽救。
I wish I had foretold the market crash. = 我真希望我预言到了市场崩盘。


被动语态:
The coming of the comet was foretold by astronomers. = 彗星的到来被天文学家预言了。
The disaster was foretold, but no one heeded the warning. = 灾难已经被预言了，但是没有人理会警告。


疑问句:
Can anyone foretell the future with certainty? = 有谁能肯定地预言未来吗？
Did the ancient scriptures foretell the rise of this empire? = 古代经文中是否预言了这个帝国的兴起？


否定句:
No one could foretell the devastating consequences. = 没有人能预言灾难性的后果。
The experts failed to foretell the economic downturn. = 专家们未能预言经济衰退。

foretell doom and gloom = 预言厄运
foretell the coming of = 预言...的到来
