epipubic:/ˌɛpɪˈpjuːbɪk/| adj.|耻骨上方的，耻骨上部的|Situated above the pubic bone or symphysis pubis.  The term often refers to bones or structures in the pelvic region.|例句：The epipubic bones are found in some mammals, but not in humans.  耻骨上方的骨骼存在于某些哺乳动物中，但人类没有。|例句：The epipubic symphysis is a cartilaginous joint connecting the epipubic bones.耻骨联合是连接耻骨上方的软骨关节。|近义词：supra-pubic|反义词：infra-pubic|


词源:

* **epi-**:  From Greek *epí*, meaning "upon," "on," or "above."
* **pubic**: From Latin *pubēs*, meaning "adult," referring to the area covered by pubic hair.
