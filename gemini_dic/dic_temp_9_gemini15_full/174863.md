rack:/ræˈk/| n. vt.|架子，搁架；折磨；榨取|A rack is a framework, typically with bars or shelves, used for holding or storing things.|The library has many racks filled with books. 图书馆有很多装满书的书架。 He was racked with guilt. 他饱受内疚的折磨。|近义词：shelf, stand, torment,  extort|反义词：comfort, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He racks his brain trying to solve the puzzle. = 他绞尽脑汁试图解开这个谜题。
The store racks its merchandise neatly. = 这家商店整齐地摆放商品。


一般过去时:
They racked their brains all night trying to find a solution. = 他们整夜绞尽脑汁试图找到解决方案。
The company racked up huge debts. = 公司积累了巨额债务。


现在完成时:
She has racked up many awards for her achievements. = 她因自己的成就获得了许多奖项。
The team has racked up a series of victories. = 该团队取得了一系列胜利。


过去完成时:
They had racked up significant losses before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经积累了巨大的损失。
He had racked his brain for hours trying to remember the password. = 他绞尽脑汁想了几个小时，试图记住密码。


将来时:
They will rack up more points in the next game. = 他们将在下一场比赛中获得更多积分。
She will rack up a lot of frequent flyer miles this year. = 她今年将累积大量的飞行常客里程。


虚拟语气:
If I had known better, I wouldn't have racked myself with worry. = 如果我早知道，我就不会让自己如此担心。
If they racked their brains harder, they might find the answer. = 如果他们更努力地动脑筋，他们可能会找到答案。


被动语态:
The books were racked neatly on the shelves. = 书本整齐地摆放在书架上。
The server was racked with viruses.  = 服务器感染了病毒。（这里指服务器系统受到病毒攻击）


疑问句:
Where do you rack your bikes? = 你把自行车放在哪里？
How many items are racked in the warehouse? = 仓库里堆放了多少物品？


否定句:
He didn't rack his brains over the minor problem. = 他没有为这个小问题绞尽脑汁。
They didn't rack up much debt this month. = 他们这个月没有积累太多债务。
