peripherals:/pəˈrɪfərəlz/| n. |外围设备，周边设备 | External devices that are connected to a computer or other central processing unit.  These devices expand the functionality of the central system.  Examples include printers, keyboards, mice, and monitors. | The peripherals of the computer system were upgraded to improve performance.  电脑系统的外围设备进行了升级以提高性能。
