The word "onuses" does not exist in standard English.  It's likely a misspelling or a non-standard variation.  The intended word is probably "onus".

onus: /ˈoʊnəs/ | n. |责任，负担 |The responsibility or duty to do something.  The onus is on you to prove your innocence. |近义词：burden, responsibility, obligation |反义词：privilege, right
