preequips:/priːˈɪkwɪps/ | vt. |预先装备；预先配备|To equip or furnish beforehand or in advance.|The company preequips its delivery trucks with GPS tracking devices.  这家公司为其送货卡车预先配备了GPS跟踪设备。|The army preequipped the soldiers with winter clothing before deploying them to the arctic.  军队在将士兵部署到北极之前，为他们预先配备了冬装。| equip, furnish | unequip
