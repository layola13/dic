secludedly:/səˈkluːdɪdli/ | adv. | 隐居地；僻静地 | In a secluded and isolated manner; privately.  It describes the state of being hidden away or sheltered from view. | 例句：They lived secludedly in a remote mountain village. 他们隐居在一个偏僻的山村里。  They secludedly celebrated their anniversary. 他们私下庆祝了结婚周年纪念日。 | 近义词：privately, secretly, solitarily, remotely | 反义词：publicly, openly, conspicuously
