pop-corns: /ˈpɒpˌkɔːrnz/ | n. |爆米花|Popcorn is a snack made from kernels of corn that puff up when heated.  It is often seasoned with salt, butter, or other flavorings. |例句：I bought a large bucket of popcorn at the cinema. 我在电影院买了一大桶爆米花。|例句：The popcorn was so salty, it made me thirsty. 这爆米花太咸了，让我口渴。|近义词：corn, snacks|反义词：无
