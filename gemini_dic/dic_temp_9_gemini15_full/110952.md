lipsynching:/ˈlɪpsɪŋkɪŋ/| v.|对嘴型演唱，假唱|The act of moving one's lips in synchronization with a pre-recorded vocal track, creating the illusion of singing live.  It's often used in performances where the singer's voice is not live, but pre-recorded. |例句：The pop star was caught lipsynching during her live performance.  这位流行歌星在现场演出中被发现假唱。

例句：The actress was skilled at lipsynching, making it seem like she was actually singing.  这位女演员擅长对嘴型演唱，让人感觉她好像真的在唱歌。


近义词：miming, mouthing the words|反义词：singing live
