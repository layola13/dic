comical:/ˈkɒmɪkl/ /ˈkɑːmɪkl/| adj.|滑稽的，好笑的|causing laughter because of being absurd or unusual.  It suggests something is funny in a lighthearted, often slightly silly way.  |例句：His comical walk made everyone laugh.  他滑稽的步态逗笑了所有人。

例句：The clown's comical antics entertained the children. 小丑滑稽的表演逗乐了孩子们。


近义词：funny, humorous, amusing, ludicrous, farcical|反义词：serious, solemn, grave, tragic


词源:  From Middle English *comical, from Latin *comicus "of or relating to comedy," from Greek *kōmikos, from *kōmos "revel, merrymaking."  The suffix "-al" is an adjectival suffix.
