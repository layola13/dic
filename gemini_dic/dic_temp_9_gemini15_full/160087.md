pomading:/pəˈmeɪdɪŋ/| n.|搽油膏；(头发)用发油梳理|The act of applying pomade to the hair to style it.  Pomading is a common practice for men who want to achieve a slick, groomed look.|例句：He spent a long time pomading his hair before the event. 他在活动前花了很长时间用发油梳理头发。|近义词：hair styling, grooming|反义词：uncombed, unkempt
