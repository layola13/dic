godfathering:/ˈɡɑːdfɑːðəriŋ/ | n. | 教父式指导；提携；资助 | The act of mentoring, supporting, and guiding someone, often in a business or professional context, similar to the role of a godfather.  It implies a significant level of influence and assistance. | Example:  The company's godfathering of the young entrepreneur helped launch her successful career. 这家公司对这位年轻企业家的提携帮助她开始了成功的职业生涯。 | 近义词：mentoring, sponsoring, supporting, guiding | 反义词：hindering, obstructing, neglecting
