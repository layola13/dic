cabriole:/ˈkæbriˌoʊl/| n. |轻快活泼的动作；轻快活泼的步伐；（家具）弯腿；|A quick, lively movement or step;  a curved leg on a piece of furniture.|例句：The dancer executed a graceful cabriole across the stage. 舞者在舞台上优雅地跳了一个轻快活泼的动作。  The antique chair was notable for its intricately carved cabrioles. 这把古董椅子的精雕细琢的弯腿令人注目。|近义词：leap, spring, jump, curlicue|反义词：stumble, plod, straight leg|名词
