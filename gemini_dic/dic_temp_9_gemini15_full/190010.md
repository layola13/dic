sandblasters:/ˈsændˌblæstərz/| n. |喷砂机；喷砂工人（复数）|A sandblaster is a tool or machine used to clean or etch surfaces by propelling a stream of sand or other abrasive material at high velocity. It can also refer to the people who operate these machines.|例句：The workers used sandblasters to clean the rust off the metal beams. 工人们用喷砂机清理金属梁上的锈迹。 The sandblasters carefully prepared the surface for painting. 喷砂工人们仔细地准备了绘画的表面。|近义词：abrasive blaster, shot blaster|反义词:  (无直接反义词，但与其作用相反的是：抛光机，涂层机等)|名词
