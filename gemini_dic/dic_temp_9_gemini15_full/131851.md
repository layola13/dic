nonconstricted: /ˌnɒn kənˈstrɪktɪd/ | adj. | 未受约束的，自由的 | Not constricted; free from constraint or restriction.  The term describes a state or condition lacking constriction, tightness, or limitation. It implies openness, freedom of movement, or lack of pressure. |

例句：

The nonconstricted blood vessels allowed for efficient circulation.  | 未受约束的血管允许有效的血液循环。

The artist enjoyed the nonconstricted space of the large studio. | 艺术家享受着大型工作室里不受约束的空间。


近义词：free, unrestricted, open, unconfined, unconstrained

反义词：constricted, restricted, confined, constrained, cramped

词性:形容词
