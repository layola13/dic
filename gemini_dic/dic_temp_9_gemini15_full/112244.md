looted:/ˈluːtɪd/| v. |洗劫；抢劫；掠夺|To steal goods from a place, especially during a war or riot.  |例句：The rebels looted the town, stealing food and supplies.  叛军洗劫了城镇，偷走了食物和补给品。

例句：After the earthquake, many homes were looted by opportunistic thieves. 地震过后，许多房屋被投机取巧的小偷洗劫一空。


近义词：plundered, pillaged, ransacked, robbed

反义词：protected, defended, guarded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They loot shops every night. = 他们每晚都抢劫商店。
He regularly loots the local market. = 他经常洗劫当地的市场。


一般过去时:
