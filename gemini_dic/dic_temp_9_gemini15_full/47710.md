decachord:/ˈdɛkəkɔːrd/| n. |十弦琴；十音阶|A decachord is a musical instrument with ten strings or a musical scale with ten notes.  The term is often used in reference to ancient Greek instruments. |Example: The ancient Greek decachord was a significant instrument in their musical tradition.  古代希腊十弦琴在其音乐传统中是一种重要的乐器。|近义词：ten-stringed instrument|反义词：无
