resighting:/ˌriːˈsaɪtɪŋ/| n.|再看见；重新定位；再次目击|The act of seeing something again, especially after a period of time.  It can also refer to the act of relocating something or someone. |例句：The resighting of the missing bird thrilled the ornithologists.  再次发现失踪的鸟类让鸟类学家们兴奋不已。

例句：The company's resighting of its warehouse improved efficiency. 公司仓库的重新选址提高了效率。


近义词：rediscovery, relocation, reappearance, sighting (again)
反义词：loss, disappearance
