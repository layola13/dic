dimmed-out: /dɪmdˈaʊt/ | adj. | 昏暗的，暗淡的，模糊不清的 |  Describes something that is less bright, less distinct, or less clear than usual, often implying a deliberate reduction in brightness or visibility.  | 例句：The lights were dimmed-out for the movie. 电影放映时灯光被调暗了。 The photograph was slightly dimmed-out, making the details hard to see. 照片有些暗淡，细节难以辨认。 | 近义词：faded, muted, subdued, obscure | 反义词：bright, vivid, clear, distinct |
