jeweler:/ˈdʒuːələr/| n. |珠宝商，珠宝匠|A jeweler is a person who makes, repairs, or sells jewelry. Jewelers often work with precious metals and gemstones.|例句：My grandmother took her broken necklace to a jeweler for repair. 我祖母把她的断项链拿到珠宝店修理。  The jeweler crafted a stunning diamond ring. 珠宝匠制作了一个令人惊叹的钻石戒指。|近义词：jewellery maker, goldsmith|反义词：无
