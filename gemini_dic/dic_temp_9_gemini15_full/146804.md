overimitating: /ˌoʊvərɪmɪˈteɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度模仿 |  To imitate excessively or to an inappropriate degree; to copy or mimic something too closely.  This often results in a lack of originality or a distortion of the original. |


例句：

He was overimitating his favorite musician, losing his own unique style in the process.  = 他过度模仿他最喜欢的音乐家，在这个过程中失去了自己独特的风格。

The student's essay showed signs of overimitating the source material, leading to accusations of plagiarism. = 学生的论文显示出过度模仿来源材料的迹象，导致了剽窃的指控。


近义词：aping, mimicking excessively, copying slavishly, plagiarizing (in certain contexts)


反义词：being original, innovating, creating independently


词性：动词
