facsimilize:/fækˈsɪmɪlaɪz/ | vt. |制作传真；复制；摹仿 | To make a facsimile of; reproduce.  The process involves transmitting a document or image electronically. |例句：We facsimilize all important documents before sending them by mail. 我们在邮寄所有重要文件之前都会制作传真件。

例句：The artist attempted to facsimilize the ancient painting. 艺术家试图复制这幅古代绘画。


近义词：copy, reproduce, duplicate, replicate | 反义词：destroy, obliterate
