foreclose:/ˌfɔːrˈkloʊz/| vt. |取消抵押品赎回权；强行拍卖；(尤指银行)停止贷款；阻止(某事物)的发生 | To legally take possession of a property because the owner has not repaid a loan.  |例句：The bank foreclosed on his house because he failed to make his mortgage payments.  银行因他未能偿还抵押贷款而将其房屋强行拍卖。|例句：They were able to foreclose on the development of a dangerous technology. 他们能够阻止一项危险技术的开发。|近义词：repossess, seize, shut down |反义词：finance, grant, allow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank forecloses on properties with delinquent mortgages. = 银行对拖欠抵押贷款的房产进行强行拍卖。
He forecloses any possibility of reconciliation. = 他阻止了任何和解的可能性。

一般过去时:
The lender foreclosed on the property last year. = 贷款方去年取消了该房产的抵押品赎回权。
She tried to foreclose the argument before it escalated. = 她试图在争论升级之前阻止它。

现在完成时:
The bank has foreclosed on several properties this month. = 本月银行已经对几处房产进行了强行拍卖。
They have foreclosed all avenues of escape. = 他们已经堵死了所有逃生之路。


过去完成时:
The homeowner had already foreclosed on the idea of selling his house. = 房主之前就已经放弃了出售房屋的想法。(此处foreclose 的含义略微不同，指放弃)
The government had foreclosed any chance of negotiation. = 政府已经堵死了任何谈判的机会。

将来时:
The bank will foreclose on the property if the payments are not made. = 如果不付款，银行将取消该房产的抵押品赎回权。
He will foreclose any attempts at interference. = 他将阻止任何干涉企图。

虚拟语气:
If the payments were not made, the bank would foreclose. = 如果不付款，银行就会取消抵押品赎回权。
If he had acted sooner, he could have foreclosed the crisis. = 如果他早点采取行动，他就能阻止这场危机。

被动语态:
The property was foreclosed on due to unpaid taxes. = 由于未缴税款，该房产被强行拍卖。
The possibility of a peaceful solution was foreclosed by their actions. = 他们的一举一动堵死了和平解决的可能性。

疑问句:
Will the bank foreclose if I miss a payment? = 如果我错过付款，银行会取消抵押品赎回权吗？
Can we foreclose the risk of failure? = 我们能阻止失败的风险吗？

否定句:
The bank did not foreclose on the property. = 银行没有取消该房产的抵押品赎回权。
They did not foreclose the possibility of a compromise. = 他们没有排除妥协的可能性。
