shelterage:/ˈʃɛltərɪdʒ/ | n. | 庇护；遮蔽处；住所 | Shelterage is the provision of shelter or protection from the elements or danger. It can refer to the act of providing shelter, the shelter itself, or the state of being sheltered.  | Example:  The charity provided emergency shelterage to the homeless during the storm.  慈善机构在暴风雨期间为无家可归者提供了紧急住所。 |近义词： refuge, protection, accommodation,  housing | 反义词：exposure, vulnerability
