preschedule:/ˈpriːˌskɛdʒuːl/ | vt. | 预先安排，提前安排 | To schedule something in advance.  |例句：We prescheduled the meeting for next Monday. 我们把会议预先安排在了下周一。|例句：The company prescheduled all the training sessions for the new employees. 公司为新员工预先安排了所有培训课程。|近义词：plan, arrange, schedule in advance |反义词：postpone, reschedule
