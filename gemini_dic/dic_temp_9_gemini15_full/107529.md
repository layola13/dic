landflood:/ˈlændflʌd/ | n. | 泛滥, 洪水 | A landflood is a flood that occurs on land, often caused by heavy rainfall or the bursting of a dam.  It is a large and destructive inundation of water over land. |例句：The heavy rains caused a devastating landflood that destroyed homes and businesses.  这场大雨引发了破坏性的洪水，摧毁了房屋和企业。


The landflood inundated the entire village, leaving residents stranded. 这场洪水淹没了整个村庄，使居民被困。|近义词：deluge, inundation, flood |反义词：drought |词性:名词
