intercepter:/ˌɪntərˈsɛptər/| n. |拦截器，截取器|A device or system that intercepts and stops something, such as a missile or a radio signal.  It can also refer to a person who intercepts something.|The missile defense system uses interceptors to destroy incoming missiles.  导弹防御系统使用拦截器摧毁来袭导弹。|近义词：interceptor, receiver, blocker |反义词：transmitter, sender


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The interceptor intercepts enemy aircraft. = 拦截器拦截敌机。
The system intercepts all incoming calls. = 系统拦截所有来电。


一般过去时:
The pilot intercepted the enemy bomber. = 飞行员拦截了敌人的轰炸机。
The police intercepted a drug shipment. = 警方拦截了一批毒品。


现在完成时:
The government has intercepted several terrorist plots. = 政府已经拦截了几起恐怖袭击阴谋。
The team has intercepted numerous emails. = 团队已经拦截了许多电子邮件。


过去完成时:
The military had intercepted the message before the enemy could act. = 军方在敌人采取行动之前已经截获了信息。
The detective had intercepted the suspect before he could escape. = 侦探在他逃跑之前已经拦截了嫌疑人。


将来时:
The new radar system will intercept any incoming missiles. = 新的雷达系统将拦截任何来袭导弹。
The authorities will intercept the illegal package. = 当局将拦截非法包裹。


虚拟语气:
If we had an interceptor, we could stop the attack. = 如果我们有拦截器，我们就能阻止袭击。
I wish we could intercept their communications. = 我希望我们能够拦截他们的通讯。


被动语态:
The message was intercepted by the intelligence agency. = 信息被情报机构截获。
The shipment was intercepted at the border. = 货物在边境被拦截。


疑问句:
Did the system intercept the message? = 系统拦截了信息吗？
Was the package intercepted? = 包裹被拦截了吗？


否定句:
The system did not intercept the signal. = 系统没有拦截信号。
The police could not intercept the fleeing suspect. = 警方无法拦截逃跑的嫌疑犯。


PHRASE->
