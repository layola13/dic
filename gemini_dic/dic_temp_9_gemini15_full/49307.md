delight:/dɪˈlaɪt/ | n., v. | 快乐；使高兴 |  great pleasure; to give great pleasure to | 近义词：joy, pleasure, happiness | 反义词：sadness, displeasure, misery


英文解析：
As a noun, delight refers to a feeling of great pleasure or happiness.  As a verb, it means to give someone great pleasure or happiness; to greatly please or satisfy.

例句(Noun):
The children expressed their delight at receiving the gifts. = 孩子们收到礼物后表达了他们的喜悦。

The concert was a pure delight. = 音乐会简直太棒了。


例句(Verb):
The beautiful scenery delighted us. = 美丽的景色让我们感到高兴。

The news delighted her parents. = 这个消息让她父母很高兴。
