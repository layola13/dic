caviar:/ˈkæviɑːr/|/ˈkævɪɑːr/| n. |鱼子酱|Salted roe of sturgeon, especially of the species Acipenser huso, used as a delicacy.  It is typically served as an appetizer or as a garnish.  Different types of caviar exist, depending on the species of sturgeon and its origin.|The restaurant is famous for its exquisite caviar service.|近义词：roe, fish eggs|反义词：None
