seahorses:/ˈsiːhɔːrsɪz/| n. |海马|Seahorses are small marine fish with a horse-like head and a prehensile tail. They are known for their unique mating habits, where the male carries the eggs in a pouch until they hatch.|例句：The aquarium has a beautiful display of seahorses.  水族馆有一个美丽的观赏海马的展览。|例句：We saw several seahorses while snorkeling in the coral reef.我们在珊瑚礁浮潜时看到几只海马。|近义词：sea dragon|反义词：无
