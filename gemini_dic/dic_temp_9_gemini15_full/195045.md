semiaxis:/ˈsemiæksɪs/| n.|半轴|A semiaxis is half the length of an axis, particularly in an ellipse or ellipsoid.  It refers to either the major semiaxis (half the longest diameter) or the minor semiaxis (half the shortest diameter).|例句：The semiaxes of the ellipse are 5 and 3.  椭圆的半轴是5和3。

The major semiaxis of the Earth's orbit is approximately 149.6 million kilometers. 地球轨道的长半轴大约是1.496亿公里。|近义词：half-axis|反义词：axis
