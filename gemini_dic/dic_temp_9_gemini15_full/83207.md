guzzled:/ˈɡʌzəl/| vt.| 狂饮；狼吞虎咽地吃 | to drink large quantities of liquid quickly  |例句：He guzzled down a pint of beer in one go. 他一口气喝掉了一品脱啤酒。 |例句：She guzzled the whole pizza in under ten minutes.  她在十分钟内狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整个比萨饼。|近义词：gulp, quaff, swig |反义词：sip, savour
