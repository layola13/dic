mezzo:/ˈmɛtsoʊ/ | adj. | 中等的；中音的 |  Meaning:  It means "half" or "middle" in Italian.  As an adjective, it's used to describe something that is medium-sized, intermediate in quality, or a middle vocal range. | Example: mezzo-soprano (a female voice between soprano and alto).  The room was of mezzo size, not too big, nor too small. | 近义词：medium, average, middling | 反义词：extreme, large, small
