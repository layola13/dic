roa:/ˈroʊ.ə/|/ˈrəʊ.ə/ |abbr.| 资产回报率 |Return on assets; a financial ratio that indicates how profitable a company is relative to its total assets.| 例句：The company's ROA improved significantly this year. = 今年公司的资产回报率显著提高。  The low ROA suggests the company is not efficiently utilizing its assets. = 低资产回报率表明该公司没有有效利用其资产。|近义词：return on investment (ROI)|反义词：无直接反义词，但低ROA可以与低盈利能力相关联。|abbr.
