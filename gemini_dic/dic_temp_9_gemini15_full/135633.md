nonpragmatic: /ˌnɒnpræɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. |非务实的；不切实际的 | Not concerned with practical considerations; idealistic.  Focusing on theory or abstract concepts rather than practical applications or consequences. |例句：His nonpragmatic approach to problem-solving often led to inefficient solutions.  他的非务实的问题解决方法经常导致效率低下的解决方案。

例句：The nonpragmatic policies of the government led to economic instability. 政府不切实际的政策导致了经济不稳定。


近义词：unrealistic, idealistic, impractical, theoretical, utopian

反义词：pragmatic, practical, realistic, down-to-earth
