epiphytic:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪk/ | adj. | 附生的；附生植物的 | Growing upon another plant, but not parasitic. Epiphytic plants, such as orchids and bromeliads, derive moisture and nutrients from the air, rain, and debris accumulating around them.  They do not draw sustenance from the host plant. |  Many orchids are epiphytic, clinging to tree branches for support. 许多兰花是附生的，依附在树枝上以获得支撑。  The epiphytic ferns thrived in the humid rainforest canopy.  附生蕨类植物在潮湿的热带雨林树冠中茁壮成长。 | parasitic, lithophytic | terrestrial (反义词并非完全对立，而是指生长方式不同)
