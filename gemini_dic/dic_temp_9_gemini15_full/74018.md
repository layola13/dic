forward-looking: /ˈfɔːrwərdˈlʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 前瞻性的，着眼于未来的 |  Focusing on or concerned with the future and its potential;  prospective. | 例句：The company has a forward-looking strategy aimed at long-term growth. 这家公司有一个着眼于长期发展的远见卓识的战略。


例句：Their forward-looking approach to technology has given them a competitive edge. 他们对技术的超前眼光使他们获得了竞争优势。


近义词：prospective, visionary, future-oriented, progressive


反义词：retrospective, backward-looking, short-sighted


词性：形容词
