/ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ | n. | 美学；审美观 | The branch of philosophy dealing with the nature of beauty, art, taste, and the creation or appreciation of beauty.  It encompasses the philosophical inquiry into the nature of art, beauty, and taste.  | Example:  The professor delivered a lecture on the aesthetics of Renaissance painting.  教授做了一个关于文艺复兴绘画美学的讲座。

|  Example:  She has a highly developed sense of aesthetics. 她有高度发达的审美观。 |近义词:beauty, art appreciation, taste | 反义词:ugliness, vulgarity |  n.
