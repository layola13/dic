词源:
The word "microwave" is a combination of "micro-" (meaning "small") and "wave" (referring to electromagnetic waves).  It describes the short wavelengths of electromagnetic radiation used in microwave ovens.  The term emerged in the mid-20th century with the development of microwave oven technology.
