quet: /ket/ | adj. | 安静的；宁静的 | quiet; still; tranquil | The library was quet, allowing for focused study. 图书馆很安静，可以集中学习。| 近义词：silent, peaceful, calm | 反义词：noisy, loud, boisterous

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The streets are quiet at night. = 夜晚的街道很安静。
The library is usually quiet. = 图书馆通常很安静。

一般过去时:
The room was quiet after everyone left. = 大家离开后房间很安静。
The baby slept quietly last night. = 婴儿昨晚睡得很安静。

现在完成时:
It has been quiet since the storm passed. = 暴风雨过去后一直很安静。
The town has remained quiet despite the recent events. = 尽管最近发生了一些事情，但小镇仍然保持平静。

过去完成时:

Everything had been quiet until the unexpected phone call. = 直到接到意外的电话之前，一切都平静无声。

将来时:
The streets will be quiet tomorrow. = 明天的街道将会很安静。
I hope the concert will be quiet enough to sleep through. = 我希望音乐会足够安静，我可以睡个好觉。

虚拟语气:
If it were quiet, I could concentrate better. = 如果安静的话，我可以更集中注意力。
I wish the neighborhood were quieter. = 我希望这个社区更安静一些。

被动语态:
The room was kept quiet for the sleeping baby. = 为了让婴儿睡觉，房间保持安静。
The library is kept quiet to facilitate studying. = 图书馆保持安静以方便学习。

疑问句:

否定句:
