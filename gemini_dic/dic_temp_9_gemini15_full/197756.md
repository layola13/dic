词源:
The word "shilly-shally" is of uncertain origin, but it's likely an onomatopoeic word, meaning it imitates the sound of indecision or wavering.  Some theories suggest it might be related to earlier words suggesting weakness or foolishness.  Its current usage solidified in the 17th century.
