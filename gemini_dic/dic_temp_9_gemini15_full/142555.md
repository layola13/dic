ostentation:/ˌɒsteˈnteɪʃən/| /ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃən/ | n. |炫耀；夸示；卖弄；铺张浪费 |The act of ostentatiously displaying wealth, knowledge, etc.; showy or pretentious display.  |例句：Her ostentation was evident in her extravagant jewelry and clothing.  她的炫耀在她的奢华珠宝和服装中显而易见。|例句：The ostentation of the new CEO's office was a clear indication of his arrogance. 新任CEO办公室的铺张浪费清楚地表明了他的傲慢。|近义词：showiness, flamboyance, pretentiousness, display, extravagance |反义词：modesty, humility, simplicity, reserve |名词
