cultivability: /ˌkʌltɪˈveɪbɪlɪti/ | n. | 可耕性，可培养性 | The ability of land to be cultivated or of something to be cultivated.  The suitability of land for cultivation; the state of being cultivable. | 例句：The cultivability of the soil is crucial for successful farming.  土壤的可耕性对于成功的农业至关重要。


The high cultivability of this new strain of rice makes it ideal for large-scale production.  这种新型水稻的高可培养性使其成为大规模生产的理想选择。


近义词：arability, cultivableness | 反义词：uncultivability, sterility


词性：名词
