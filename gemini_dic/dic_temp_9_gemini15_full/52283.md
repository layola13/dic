dichotomy:/daɪˈkɑːtəmi/ | n. | 二分法；对立；分裂 | A division or contrast between two things that are or are represented as being opposed or entirely different.  It often implies an artificial or forced division. | Example: The dichotomy between good and evil is a recurring theme in literature.  二善恶之间的对立是文学中反复出现的主题。  The dichotomy between theory and practice is often difficult to bridge. 理论与实践之间的差异往往难以弥合。 |近义词：division, split, contrast, duality |反义词：unity, synthesis, harmony
