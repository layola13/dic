oleoptene:/ˈoʊliəpˌtin/| n. |油树脂,挥发油|A volatile, oily substance found in plants, especially in the essential oils of certain flowers and fruits. It is often responsible for the characteristic aroma and flavor of the plant.  Oleoptene is generally considered to be the terpene fraction of an essential oil.|Example: The oleoptene in lavender oil gives it its distinctive floral scent.|近义词: essential oil component, terpene|反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与其化学组成成分相对的物质)|名词
