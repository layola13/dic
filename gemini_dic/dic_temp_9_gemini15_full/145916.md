overdilating:/ˌoʊvərˈdaɪleɪtɪŋ/| v. |过度扩张，膨胀过度|The act of excessively dilating or expanding something beyond its normal or desirable limits.  This often refers to medical contexts, such as excessively widening a blood vessel or a pupil.|例句：The surgeon warned against overdilating the pupil during the procedure, as it could damage the eye.  外科医生警告说，在手术过程中过度扩张瞳孔可能会损伤眼睛。The excessive irrigation led to overdilating the wound, hindering healing. 过度冲洗导致伤口过度扩张，阻碍愈合。|近义词：overexpand, overextend, distend excessively |反义词：constrict, contract, narrow
