relationally:/rɪˈleɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 关系上地；关联地 | In a relational manner; concerning relationships.  This adverb describes something that pertains to or is characterized by relationships.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness or interdependence of elements. | Example:  The data was organized relationally, making it easier to understand the connections between different variables. 数据以关系方式组织，从而更容易理解不同变量之间的联系。  |近义词：interrelationally, interconnectedly | 反义词：independently, separately | 副词
