luminant:/ˈluːmɪnənt/| adj. |发光的；明亮的|Emitting or reflecting light; shining.  The luminant screen illuminated the dark room.  发光的屏幕照亮了黑暗的房间。|近义词：bright, luminous, radiant, shining |反义词：dark, dim, obscure |形容词

词源:
Luminant derives from the Latin word "lumen," meaning "light."  The suffix "-ant" indicates something that performs the action or possesses the quality of the root word.  Therefore, luminant means "something that gives light."
