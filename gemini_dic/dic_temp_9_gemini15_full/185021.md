retexture:/ˌriːˈtekstʃər/| vt.|重新赋予纹理；改变纹理；重新纹理化|To give a new texture to something, especially a surface or material.  It often implies a digital process, like changing the surface detail of a 3D model or image.|例句：The artist retextured the digital sculpture, adding more intricate details to the surface.  艺术家重新调整了数字雕塑的纹理，在表面添加了更多复杂的细节。

The game developers retextured the environment to make it look more realistic. 游戏开发者重新调整了环境纹理，使其看起来更逼真。|近义词：remodel, revamp, refine|反义词：smooth (in the context of removing texture)
