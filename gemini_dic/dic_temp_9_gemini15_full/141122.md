ontologists:/ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 本体论者，本体学家 |  Experts in ontology, the branch of metaphysics dealing with the nature of being, existence, or reality, as well as the categories of being and their relations.  They study concepts and categories, and how they relate to each other and to the real world.  | Example:  The ontologists debated the nature of time. 本体论者们就时间的本质进行了辩论。  The ontologists are developing a new knowledge representation system. 本体论学家们正在开发一种新的知识表示系统。 | 近义词: metaphysicians, semanticists | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，因为本体论本身不是一个具有对立概念的领域) | 名词
