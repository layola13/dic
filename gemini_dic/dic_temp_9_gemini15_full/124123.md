mortgaged:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒd/| adj. |抵押的；用作抵押的|Having been pledged as security for a loan.  A mortgaged property is one that has been used as collateral to secure a loan.  |例句：The house was mortgaged to the bank. 这房子已抵押给银行。|例句：He mortgaged his future to pay off his debts. 他抵押了他的未来以偿还债务。|近义词：pledged, encumbered, hypothecated |反义词：unencumbered, free and clear


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The house is mortgaged. = 这房子抵押了。
They mortgage their property every year. = 他们每年都将他们的房产抵押。


一般过去时:
He mortgaged his car to get a loan. = 他抵押了他的汽车来获得贷款。
We mortgaged our house to pay for our son's education. = 我们抵押了房子来支付儿子的教育费用。


现在完成时:
They have mortgaged their home several times. = 他们已经抵押了他们的房子好几次了。
She has mortgaged her assets to start a business. = 她已经抵押了她的资产来创业。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, they had mortgaged all their property. = 危机爆发前，他们已经抵押了所有的房产。
He had mortgaged his business before the bankruptcy. = 破产前他已经抵押了他的生意。


将来时:
They will mortgage their land to build a new factory. = 他们将抵押他们的土地来建造新工厂。
We will likely mortgage our house to fund our trip. = 我们可能会抵押房子来资助我们的旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I needed money, I would mortgage my car. = 如果我需要钱，我会抵押我的车。
I wish I hadn't mortgaged my business last year. = 我真希望去年我没有抵押我的生意。


被动语态:
The property was mortgaged to secure the loan. = 该房产被抵押以担保贷款。
His assets were mortgaged to pay the debts. = 他的资产被抵押以偿还债务。


疑问句:
Is your house mortgaged? = 你的房子抵押了吗？
Have they mortgaged their business yet? = 他们已经抵押他们的生意了吗？


否定句:
The car is not mortgaged. = 这辆车没有抵押。
They did not mortgage their house. = 他们没有抵押他们的房子。

词性: Adjective (形容词)
