colligates:/ˈkɒlɪɡeɪts/ | n. | 搭配词，共现词 | Words that frequently occur together in a corpus of text.  Colligates are studied in corpus linguistics to understand patterns of word usage and collocation. They are often semantically related but do not necessarily form fixed phrases.  | Example:  The words "heavy rain" are colligates because they frequently appear together.  | 近义词： collocates, associated words | 反义词：无
