go-along:/ˌɡoʊ əˈlɒŋ/ | adj. | 顺从的；随和的；苟同的 |  Agreeable; compliant; willing to go along with others' plans or ideas.  It emphasizes a passive acceptance rather than enthusiastic agreement. |  He's a go-along person, always willing to do what others suggest. 他是一个随和的人，总是愿意做别人建议的事情。  She had a go-along attitude towards the new rules. 她对新规采取了顺从的态度。|近义词：compliant, agreeable, acquiescent |反义词：obstinate, defiant, resistant |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a go-along guy. = 他是一个随和的家伙。
They are go-along with the plan. = 他们同意这个计划。


一般过去时:
She was go-along with his suggestion. = 她同意了他的建议。
They were go-along about the decision. = 他们对这个决定表示顺从。


现在完成时:
I have been go-along with their ideas. = 我一直同意他们的想法。
We have been go-along with the changes. = 我们一直顺从这些改变。


过去完成时:
He had been go-along with everything until then. = 到那时为止，他一直都顺从。
They had been go-along with the pressure. = 他们一直屈服于压力。


将来时:
She will be go-along with the new policy. = 她将会顺从新政策。
They will be go-along with our proposal. = 他们将会同意我们的提议。


虚拟语气:
If he were more go-along, things would be easier. = 如果他更随和一些，事情就会更容易。
I wish she were more go-along with my plans. = 我希望她能更同意我的计划。


被动语态:
His suggestions were go-along with by the team. = 他的建议被团队接受了。 (Note: This usage is less common and might sound unnatural.  A more natural phrasing would be "The team agreed with his suggestions.")
The changes were go-along with reluctantly. = 这些改变是不情愿地被接受的。(Note: Similar to the above, a more natural phrase would be preferred)


疑问句:
Is he a go-along person? = 他是一个随和的人吗？
Are they go-along with the project? = 他们同意这个项目吗？


否定句:
He is not a go-along person at all. = 他一点也不随和。
They were not go-along with the idea. = 他们不同意这个主意。


PHRASE->
go-along with the crowd = 随大流
go-along get-along =  (informal)  相处融洽；一团和气
go-along with the flow =  顺其自然
go-along to get along =  (informal)  为了相处融洽而勉强同意
go-along for the ride =  跟着一起参与；搭便车
