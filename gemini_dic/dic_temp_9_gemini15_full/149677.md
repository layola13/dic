paiute:/ˈpaɪjuːt/|/ˈpaɪjuːt/| n. |派尤特人；派尤特语|A Native American people of the Great Basin, primarily in Nevada and parts of Oregon, California, and Utah.  Their language is also called Paiute.|The Paiute people have a rich history and culture, deeply connected to the land.|近义词：Shoshone (related group)|反义词：无
