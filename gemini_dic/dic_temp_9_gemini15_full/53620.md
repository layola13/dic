discede:/dɪˈsiːd/| v.|离开，退出，去世|To depart; to withdraw; to cease to exist; to die. The word often implies a formal or ceremonious departure, or a departure from a position or office.|例句：The ambassador decided to discede from the diplomatic corps. 大使决定退出外交部门。

例句：The old king disceded peacefully after a long reign. 老国王在长期统治后平静地去世了。


近义词：depart, leave, withdraw, retire, exit, decease|反义词：arrive, enter, join, begin


词性：动词
