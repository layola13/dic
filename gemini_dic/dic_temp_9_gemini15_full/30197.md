celiac sprue:/ˈsiːliæk spruː/| n.|腹腔疾病，乳糜泻|A chronic autoimmune disorder that damages the small intestine and interferes with the absorption of nutrients. It is triggered by gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye.|例句：She was diagnosed with celiac sprue after experiencing persistent digestive problems. 她因持续的消化问题被诊断出患有乳糜泻。|近义词：gluten-sensitive enteropathy, nontropical sprue|反义词：无
