dissembling: /dɪˈsɛmbəlɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 伪装；掩饰；虚伪的 | Concealing one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.  | 例句：He was dissembling his true intentions. 他在掩饰他真正的意图。


例句：Her dissembling smile hid her true anger. 她虚伪的笑容掩盖了她真正的愤怒。


近义词：disguising, feigning, simulating, pretending, masking


反义词：being honest, being truthful, being frank, being candid


词性: Verb (present participle), Adjective
