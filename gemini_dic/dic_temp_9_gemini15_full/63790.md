epicycloidal:/ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɔɪdəl/| adj.|外摆线的；外旋轮线的|  Describing a curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle rolling around the outside of another circle.  It's a type of cycloid. | The gears in the machine used epicycloidal teeth for smoother operation. 这台机器的齿轮使用了外摆线齿，以获得更平稳的运行。| 近义词：cycloidal (更广义)| 反义词：hypocycloidal (内摆线的)
