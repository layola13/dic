semifiguratively:/ˌsemiˈfɪɡjʊrətɪvli/| adv.|半比喻地，部分比喻地|  Describes language or expression that uses figurative language (like metaphors or similes) to some extent, but not completely. It suggests a blend of literal and figurative meaning.  |例句：He described the situation semifiguratively, using a few metaphors to illustrate his point but mostly sticking to factual details. 他半比喻地描述了情况，用了一些隐喻来说明他的观点，但主要还是坚持事实细节。|近义词：partially figuratively, somewhat metaphorically|反义词：literally, factually|副词
