exacerbates:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪts/| vt.|加剧；恶化；使加重|To make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse.|例句：The drought exacerbated the existing food shortage.  干旱加剧了现有的粮食短缺。

例句：His anger was exacerbated by the delay. 他的愤怒因延误而加剧。


近义词：aggravate, worsen, intensify, inflame|反义词：alleviate, mitigate, improve, ease|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stress exacerbates her health problems. = 压力加剧了她的健康问题。
This policy exacerbates the inequality in society. =这项政策加剧了社会的不平等。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain exacerbated the flooding. = 大雨加剧了洪水。
The argument exacerbated their already strained relationship. = 争吵加剧了他们本来就紧张的关系。


现在完成时:
Years of conflict have exacerbated tensions in the region. = 多年的冲突加剧了该地区的紧张局势。
The drought has exacerbated the water crisis. = 干旱加剧了水危机。


过去完成时:
The previous government's policies had exacerbated the economic problems. = 前政府的政策加剧了经济问题。
Years of neglect had exacerbated the building's structural damage. = 多年的忽视加剧了建筑物的结构损坏。


将来时:
Further delays will exacerbate the situation. = 进一步的延误将加剧局势。
The predicted heatwave will exacerbate the wildfires. = 预计的热浪将加剧野火。


虚拟语气:
If we don't address the issue, it will only exacerbate the problem. = 如果我们不解决这个问题，只会使问题更加严重。
I wish the government would take action before the situation exacerbates further. = 我希望政府在情况进一步恶化之前采取行动。


被动语态:
The situation was exacerbated by the recent events. = 近期事件加剧了局势。
The problem is exacerbated by a lack of funding. = 缺乏资金加剧了这个问题。


疑问句:
Does smoking exacerbate lung disease? = 吸烟是否会加剧肺病？
Will this decision exacerbate the existing tensions? = 这个决定会加剧现有的紧张局势吗？


否定句:
The new policy does not exacerbate the existing problems. = 新政策并没有加剧现有问题。
The medication did not exacerbate his allergies. = 这种药物并没有加剧他的过敏症。
