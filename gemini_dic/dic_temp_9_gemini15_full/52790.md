dilettantish:/ˌdɪləˈtæntiʃ/| adj.|业余的；肤浅的；不熟练的 | Showing a superficial interest in an art, a branch of knowledge, etc.  Lacking serious interest or commitment.  |例句：His dilettantish approach to painting resulted in a series of unfinished canvases. 他的绘画态度业余，结果是一系列未完成的画布。

His knowledge of art is rather dilettantish. 他对艺术的了解相当肤浅。


近义词：amateurish, superficial, shallow, unsophisticated |反义词：professional, expert, skilled, masterful

词性:形容词
