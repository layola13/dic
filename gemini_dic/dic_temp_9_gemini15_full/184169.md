reskill:/riːˈskɪl/| vt.|重新培训，再培训|To train (someone) again to do a different job or task.  The process involves acquiring new skills or upgrading existing ones to meet the demands of a changing job market or personal goals.|例句：The company is reskilling its workforce to meet the demands of the new technology. 公司正在对员工进行再培训以满足新技术的需要。

例句：Many workers are reskilling themselves to adapt to the changing job market. 许多工人正在重新培训自己以适应不断变化的就业市场。


近义词：retrain, re-educate, upskill|反义词：deskilling
