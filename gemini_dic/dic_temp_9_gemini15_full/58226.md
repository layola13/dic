druggy's:/ˈdrʌɡiz/| n.|瘾君子，吸毒者|A druggy is a person who uses illegal drugs, especially habitually and to excess.  The term is often used derogatorily.|例句：He became a druggy after losing his job. 他失业后变成了瘾君子。|例句：The police arrested several druggy's near the park. 警方在公园附近逮捕了几名瘾君子。|近义词：junkie, addict, user |反义词:N/A
