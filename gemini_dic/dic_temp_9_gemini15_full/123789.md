moov:/muːv/ | v. |移动，搬动；感动；迁移；行动|to change position; to go from one place to another; to cause something to change position; to affect someone emotionally; to  take action|例句：The car moved slowly up the hill.  这辆车慢慢地驶上了山坡。


例句：The story moved me to tears.  这个故事让我感动得流泪。

近义词：shift, relocate, transfer, stir, touch

反义词：stay, remain, halt, stop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The Earth moves around the sun. = 地球绕着太阳转。
He moves very quickly. = 他行动非常迅速。


一般过去时:
She moved to a new apartment last month. = 她上个月搬到了新公寓。
The soldiers moved forward. = 士兵们向前推进。


现在完成时:
We have moved three times in the last five years. = 在过去的五年里，我们搬了三次家。
The government has moved to address the issue. = 政府已采取行动解决这个问题。


过去完成时:
They had moved out before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经搬走了。
The company had moved its headquarters to London. = 公司已将总部迁至伦敦。


将来时:
I will move to a bigger house next year. = 我明年将搬到一栋更大的房子。
The meeting will move to a larger room. = 会议将移至较大的房间。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would move to a better neighborhood. = 如果我有更多钱，我会搬到更好的社区。
I wish I could move to the countryside. = 我希望我能搬到乡下。


被动语态:
The furniture was moved into the new house. = 家具被搬进了新房子。
The meeting was moved to a later date. = 会议被推迟到以后。


疑问句:
Will you move your car, please? = 请你把车挪一下好吗？
Does this button move the cursor? = 这个按钮能移动光标吗？


否定句:
The car didn't move. = 车没动。
He refused to move. = 他拒绝移动。



PHRASE->

move out =搬出
move around =四处走动
