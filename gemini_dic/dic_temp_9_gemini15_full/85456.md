hashtag:/ˈhæʃtæɡ/ | n. |标签，主题标签 | A word or phrase preceded by a hash symbol (#), used on social media to identify messages on a specific topic.  | Example:  The hashtag #MeToo helped raise awareness about sexual harassment.  这个#MeToo标签帮助提高了对性骚扰的认识。 |近义词：tag, keyword |反义词：N/A
