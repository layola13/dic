effectiveness:/ɪˈfektɪvnəs/ | n. |有效性，效力 |The effectiveness of the new drug is being tested.  新药的有效性正在测试中。| efficiency, efficacy, potency | ineffectiveness | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The effectiveness of this method is questionable. = 这种方法的有效性值得怀疑。
His teaching effectiveness is undeniable. = 他教学的有效性是不容置疑的。


一般过去时:
The effectiveness of the campaign was surprisingly high. = 这场运动的有效性出奇地高。
We evaluated the effectiveness of the previous strategy. = 我们评估了先前策略的有效性。


现在完成时:
Researchers have demonstrated the effectiveness of the treatment. = 研究人员已经证明了这种疗法的有效性。
The company has seen increased effectiveness since implementing the new system. = 自从实施新系统以来，该公司已经看到了效率的提高。


过去完成时:
They had already questioned the effectiveness of the old system before implementing the new one. = 他们在实施新系统之前就已经质疑旧系统的有效性了。
The doctors had realized the limited effectiveness of the medication before prescribing a different one. = 医生在开出不同的药之前就已经意识到这种药物的疗效有限。


将来时:
We will assess the effectiveness of the program next year. = 我们明年将评估该项目的有效性。
The study will determine the long-term effectiveness of the intervention. = 该研究将确定干预措施的长期有效性。


虚拟语气:
If the plan were more effective, we would have seen better results. = 如果计划更有效，我们将看到更好的结果。
It is essential that the plan's effectiveness be carefully considered. = 计划的有效性必须仔细考虑。


被动语态:
The effectiveness of the new policy is being evaluated. = 正在评估新政策的有效性。
The drug's effectiveness was proven in clinical trials. = 该药物的有效性已在临床试验中得到证实。


疑问句:
What is the effectiveness of this approach? = 这种方法的有效性如何？
Can you explain the effectiveness of your strategy? = 你能解释一下你策略的有效性吗？


否定句:
The treatment showed no effectiveness in relieving the symptoms. = 该治疗在缓解症状方面没有显示出任何效果。
The study revealed the ineffectiveness of the proposed solution. = 研究揭示了所提方案的无效性。
