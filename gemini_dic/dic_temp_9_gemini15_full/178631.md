recoveries: /rɪˈkʌvəriːz/ | n. | 康复；恢复；挽回；追回 | The plural form of "recovery," referring to multiple instances of regaining something lost or recovering from a setback.  It can refer to physical health, financial situations, stolen goods, or data. |


例句：

The doctor is pleased with the patient's rapid recoveries from the surgery.  医生对病人手术后迅速康复感到高兴。

Police made several recoveries of stolen artwork. 警方追回了多件被盗艺术品。


近义词：restorations, recuperations, retrievals, rebounds


反义词：losses, setbacks, deteriorations


词性：名词 (复数)
