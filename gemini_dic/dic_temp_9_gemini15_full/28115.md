capitalizing: /ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 大写；利用；获利 |  The act of writing or printing a letter or word in a capital letter, or the act of taking advantage of an opportunity to gain something.  | 例句：He was capitalizing on his opponent's mistakes. 他正在利用对手的错误获利。


例句：The computer automatically capitalizes the first letter of each sentence. 电脑自动将每个句子的首字母大写。


近义词：uppercase, exploit, profit from

反义词：lowercasing, neglecting

词性: 动词
