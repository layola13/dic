girls:/ɡɜːrlz/| n. |女孩们，姑娘们 |The word "girls" is the plural form of "girl," referring to female children or young women.  It can also be used informally to refer to women in general, especially among friends or in a playful context. |例句：The girls are playing in the park. 女孩们在公园里玩耍。

The girls went to a concert last night. 女孩们昨晚去看了演唱会。


近义词：young women, ladies (formal), maidens (archaic) |反义词：boys, men |词性:名词
