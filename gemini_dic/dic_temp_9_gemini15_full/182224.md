remorate:/ˈrɛməreɪt/| vt.| 回忆；追忆；细想 | To recall or think about (something) again, especially something from the past.  |例句：He sat quietly, remorating his childhood. 他静静地坐着，回忆着他的童年。  |例句：She spent hours remorating the events of that fateful day. 她花了几个小时回忆那命运攸关的一天发生的事情。|近义词：recollect, remember, recall, contemplate |反义词：forget, overlook
