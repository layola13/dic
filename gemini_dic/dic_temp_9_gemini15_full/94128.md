impeded:/ɪmˈpiːdɪd/| v.|妨碍；阻碍；阻止|To impede something means to slow its progress or make it difficult.  It often implies a physical obstruction, but can also refer to more abstract obstacles.|例句：The heavy snow impeded travel. 大雪阻碍了交通。|例句：His progress was impeded by a lack of funds.  他的进步由于缺乏资金而受阻。|近义词：hinder, obstruct, hamper, block|反义词：facilitate, assist, aid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy traffic impedes my commute. = 拥堵的交通阻碍了我的通勤。
This policy impedes economic growth. = 这一政策阻碍了经济增长。

一般过去时:
A fallen tree impeded the road. = 一棵倒下的树木阻碍了道路。
The rain impeded our hiking plans. = 雨水阻碍了我们的远足计划。

现在完成时:
Several accidents have impeded traffic on the highway. = 几起事故已经阻碍了高速公路上的交通。
Political instability has impeded the country's development. = 政治不稳定已经阻碍了国家的发展。

过去完成时:
The storm had impeded our journey, delaying our arrival. = 暴风雨已经阻碍了我们的旅程，延误了我们的到达。
Years of conflict had impeded the country's progress towards peace. = 多年的冲突已经阻碍了该国走向和平的进程。

将来时:
The expected snowstorm will impede travel tomorrow. = 预计的暴风雪将阻碍明天的出行。
Further budget cuts will impede the project's completion. = 进一步的预算削减将阻碍项目的完成。

虚拟语气:
If the road hadn't been impeded, we would have arrived earlier. = 如果道路没有被阻碍，我们本可以早点到达。
If the funding wasn't impeded, the research could have been completed sooner. = 如果资金没有被阻碍，研究本来可以早些完成。

被动语态:
Our progress was impeded by unexpected delays. = 我们的进度因意外延误而受阻。
The construction project was impeded by bad weather. = 建设项目因恶劣天气而受阻。

疑问句:
What impeded your progress on the project? = 什么阻碍了你在这个项目上的进展？
Did anything impede your travel plans? = 有什么东西阻碍了你的旅行计划吗？

否定句:
Nothing impeded their success. = 没有什么阻碍他们的成功。
The smooth road did not impede their journey. = 平坦的道路没有阻碍他们的旅程。
