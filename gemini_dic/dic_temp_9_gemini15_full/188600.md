rumbaed:/rʌmˈbɑːd/ | v. | 跳伦巴舞；以伦巴舞的节奏移动 | To dance the rumba; to move rhythmically in a manner suggestive of the rumba. | 例句：They rumbaed all night long.  他们整晚都在跳伦巴舞。

They rumbaed across the dance floor with effortless grace. 他们优雅地，毫不费力地在舞池中跳着伦巴舞。


近义词：danced, swayed, moved rhythmically | 反义词：stood still, remained motionless | 词性：动词
