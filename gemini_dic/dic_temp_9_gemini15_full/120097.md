mimers:/ˈmaɪmərz/| n.|哑剧演员；默剧演员|People who perform mime, a form of theatrical performance using gestures and body movements without speech.  Mimers use their bodies to tell stories and express emotions without speaking. |The mimers captivated the audience with their expressive movements.  哑剧演员们以他们富有表现力的动作迷住了观众。|actors, performers|speakers, vocalists
