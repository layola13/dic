majesty:/ˈmædʒɪsti/ | n. |庄严，威严；陛下 |The quality of being majestic; impressive greatness.  It often refers to the power and dignity of a sovereign.  |例句：The majesty of the mountains filled us with awe.  群山的雄伟壮丽让我们肃然起敬。

例句：Her Majesty the Queen will be attending the ceremony. 女王陛下将出席仪式。


近义词：grandeur, splendor, magnificence, dignity, sovereignty

反义词：meanness, insignificance, humility
