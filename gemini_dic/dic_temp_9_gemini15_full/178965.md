rededicated:/ˌriːˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd/| v.|重新奉献；重新致力于|To dedicate (something) again; to commit oneself anew to a cause, purpose, or task.  It implies a renewed commitment or a return to a previous dedication after a period of time or change.|例句：The president rededicated himself to fighting poverty after his reelection. 总统在连任后重新致力于打击贫困。

例句：The community rededicated the park after extensive renovations. 社区在进行大规模翻修后重新启用(奉献)了这个公园。


近义词：redevoted, recommitted, renewed, reinstated


反义词：abandoned, neglected, forsaken


词性:动词
