manifestational: /ˌmænɪfɛsˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 表现的；显示的；显现的 | Relating to or characterized by manifestation; showing or revealing something.  The word describes something that makes something else evident or apparent. It emphasizes the process of becoming visible or known. | 例句：The artist's work is a powerful manifestational of his inner turmoil.  艺术家的作品强烈地表现了他的内心骚动。

The patient's symptoms were a manifestational of the underlying disease. 病人的症状是潜在疾病的表现。


近义词：demonstrative, expressive, indicative, revealing | 反义词：latent, hidden, concealed, obscure | 词性:形容词
