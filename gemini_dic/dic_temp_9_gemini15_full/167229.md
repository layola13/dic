preterist:/ˈpriːtərɪst/| n. |预成论者；预成论的解释者|A person who interprets biblical prophecy as having been fulfilled in the past, especially in the events of the first century.|The preterist interpretation of the Book of Revelation sees its events as having been fulfilled in the Roman Empire's destruction and the early Church's persecution.|近义词：futurist (in contrast), historicist|反义词：futurist
