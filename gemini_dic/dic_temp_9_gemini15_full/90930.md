hurdy-gurdies:/ˈhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːdiz/ | n. |手摇风琴；手摇提琴 |A hurdy-gurdy is a stringed instrument played by turning a crank that rotates a wheel which rubs against the strings to produce sound.  It is also sometimes called a vielle. |例句：The street musician played a lively tune on his hurdy-gurdy. 街头艺人用他的手摇风琴演奏了一首欢快的曲子。

The children were mesmerized by the sounds of the hurdy-gurdies. 孩子们被手摇风琴的声音迷住了。|近义词：vielle, wheel fiddle |反义词：无
