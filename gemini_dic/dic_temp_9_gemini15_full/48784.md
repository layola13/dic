deflect:/dɪˈflɛkt/ | vt. | 使偏斜；使转向；避开；转移（注意力） | To cause (something, especially a blow, missile, or course) to change direction; to turn aside or away from a straight course. | 例句：The shield deflected the blow.  盾牌挡住了那一击。

The driver deflected the car to avoid hitting a pedestrian. 司机转动方向盘以避免撞到行人。


近义词：divert, deviate, avert, ward off | 反义词：direct,straighten


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He deflects criticism with humor. 他用幽默来化解批评。
The mirror deflects light.  镜子反射光线。


一般过去时:
She deflected the question skillfully. 她巧妙地避开了这个问题。
The goalkeeper deflected the ball. 守门员挡住了球。


现在完成时:
The government has deflected accusations of corruption. 政府已经避开了腐败的指控。
The new policy has deflected public anger. 新政策已经转移了公众的愤怒。


过去完成时:
He had deflected several attempts to interview him. 他已经避开了几次采访他的尝试。
The company had deflected previous criticism by releasing a statement. 公司之前通过发布声明来化解批评。


将来时:
They will deflect the missile with a laser beam. 他们将用激光束拦截导弹。
The new strategy will deflect any negative publicity. 新策略将化解任何负面宣传。


虚拟语气:
If he had deflected the blow, he might not have been injured. 如果他挡住了那一击，他可能就不会受伤了。
I wish I had deflected her questions more skillfully. 我希望我当时能更巧妙地避开她的问题。


被动语态:
The attack was deflected by the defensive system.  攻击被防御系统化解了。
The ball was deflected off the post. 球被球门柱挡了回来。


疑问句:
How can we deflect the negative press? 我们如何才能化解负面新闻？

否定句:
He did not deflect the blame. 他没有推卸责任。
The armor could not deflect all the arrows.  盔甲无法挡住所有的箭。


PHRASE->

词源:

From Middle French *deflecter, from Latin deflectere "to bend aside," from de- "away" + flectere "to bend."
