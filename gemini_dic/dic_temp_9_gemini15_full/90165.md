hotkey:/ˈhɑːtkiː/| n.|热键，快捷键|A hotkey is a combination of keys that you press to perform a specific action or command in a computer program or operating system.  It provides a quicker way to access functions than using a mouse or navigating menus.|Example: Ctrl+C is the hotkey for copy in most applications.  Ctrl+C 是大多数应用程序中复制的热键。|近义词：shortcut, keyboard shortcut|反义词：N/A
