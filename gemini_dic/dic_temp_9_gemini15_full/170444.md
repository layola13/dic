prude:/pruːd/| n. adj.| 拘谨的人；过分拘谨的；|A prude is a person who is excessively concerned with propriety or modesty.  They often disapprove of things considered sexually suggestive or immoral.  |She's such a prude; she even blushes at the mention of romance. 她太拘谨了，一提到浪漫就脸红。  |prudish, modest, prim,  |immodest, libertine, licentious|名词，形容词


USAGE->

He is a prude. = 他是个拘谨的人。
She acts like a prude. = 她表现得像个拘谨的人。

He was a prude in his youth. = 他年轻时是个拘谨的人。
