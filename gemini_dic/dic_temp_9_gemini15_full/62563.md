energising: /ˈɛnərdʒaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人精力充沛的；使人振奋的 |  Giving energy to; invigorating.  It describes something that makes you feel more energetic and enthusiastic. |

例句：
The energising music got everyone dancing.  =  这令人振奋的音乐让每个人都跳起舞来。

The energising speech inspired the team to work harder. =  这令人振奋的演讲激励团队更加努力工作。


近义词： invigorating, stimulating, uplifting, inspiring, motivating

反义词：  debilitating, draining, tiring, exhausting, depressing


词性:  形容词
