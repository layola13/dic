outplodding:/ˌaʊtˈplɒdɪŋ/| adj.| 缓慢而乏味地前进的；超过…缓慢地前进|  Exceeding in slowness and tediousness of progress; surpassing in slow plodding. |例句：The outplodding journey across the desert tested their endurance.  沙漠中缓慢而乏味的旅程考验了他们的耐力。

例句：His outplodding pace irritated the rest of the hiking group. 他缓慢而乏味的步伐惹恼了其他徒步旅行者。


近义词：tedious, slow, laborious, plodding

反义词：swift, rapid, brisk, speedy
