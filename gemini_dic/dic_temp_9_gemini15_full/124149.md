mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/| n. v.|榫眼；榫孔；嵌合；|A mortise is a hole or recess in a piece of wood, stone, or other material, into which a tenon (a projecting piece of wood or other material) is inserted to form a joint.|The carpenter carefully cut the mortise in the wooden beam.|近义词：hole, recess, socket|反义词：tenon
