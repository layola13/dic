modernised: /ˈmɒdənaɪzd/ /ˈmɑːdərnɑɪzd/ | v. | 使现代化；现代化改造 | To make something modern; to adapt something to modern conditions, methods, or ideas. |  例句：The factory has been modernised with new equipment. 工厂已经用新设备进行了现代化改造。

例句：They modernised the curriculum to better meet the needs of students. 他们对课程进行了现代化改革，以更好地满足学生的需要。


近义词：updated, renovated, revamped, improved, advanced

反义词：antiquated, outdated, old-fashioned


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They modernise their teaching methods regularly. = 他们定期更新教学方法。
The company modernises its products to stay competitive. = 公司对其产品进行现代化改造以保持竞争力。


一般过去时:
The government modernised the infrastructure. = 政府对基础设施进行了现代化改造。
They modernised the railway system last year. = 他们去年对铁路系统进行了现代化改造。


现在完成时:
They have modernised the hospital's facilities. = 他们已经对医院的设施进行了现代化改造。
The city has modernised its public transportation system. = 这个城市已经对其公共交通系统进行了现代化改造。


过去完成时:
They had modernised the building before the conference. = 他们在会议之前已经对建筑进行了现代化改造。
The company had modernised its processes before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经对其流程进行了现代化改造。


将来时:
They will modernise the airport next year. = 他们明年将对机场进行现代化改造。
The company will modernise its software in the near future. = 该公司将在不久的将来对其软件进行现代化改造。


虚拟语气:
If they modernised the system, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们对系统进行现代化改造，它将更有效率。
I wish they had modernised the equipment earlier. = 我希望他们早点对设备进行现代化改造。


被动语态:
The system was modernised last month. = 该系统上个月进行了现代化改造。
The factory is being modernised at the moment. = 工厂目前正在进行现代化改造。


疑问句:
Has the company modernised its website yet? = 公司是否已经对其网站进行了现代化改造？
Will they modernise the school? = 他们会对学校进行现代化改造吗？


否定句:
They didn't modernise the equipment. = 他们没有对设备进行现代化改造。
The building hasn't been modernised yet. = 这座建筑还没有进行现代化改造。


PHRASE->
modernise a city = 使城市现代化
modernise a factory = 使工厂现代化
modernise infrastructure = 使基础设施现代化
modernise the curriculum = 使课程现代化
modernise the economy = 使经济现代化

词源:
From "modern" + "-ise" (suffix forming verbs).  "Modern" ultimately derives from Latin *modernus, meaning "of the present time".
