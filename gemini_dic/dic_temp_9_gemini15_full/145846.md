/ˌoʊvərdiˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/| n. |过度指责；谴责过度；过分批评 |Excessive and often unfair criticism or condemnation.  The overdenunciation of the politician damaged his reputation.  |例句：The overdenunciation of the protestors by the media fueled further unrest.  媒体对抗议者的过度指责加剧了进一步的动荡。

The overdenunciation of his actions led to his dismissal from the company. 对其行为的过度谴责导致他被公司解雇。|近义词：condemnation, denouncement, criticism, censure, reproof|反义词：praise, commendation, approval
