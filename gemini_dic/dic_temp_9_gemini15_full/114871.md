mammonist:/ˈmæmənɪst/| n. |拜金主义者，唯利是图者 | A person who is excessively materialistic and values wealth above all else.  They prioritize material possessions and financial gain over ethical considerations, spiritual values, or personal relationships. |例句：He was a mammonist, always chasing after the next big deal, regardless of the cost. 他是个拜金主义者，总是追逐下一个大交易，不管代价如何。

He is considered a mammonist for his relentless pursuit of wealth and disregard for the welfare of his employees. 他因不顾员工福利而一心追求财富而被认为是一个拜金主义者。|近义词：materialist, money-grubber, plutocrat |反义词：altruist, philanthropist, ascetic
