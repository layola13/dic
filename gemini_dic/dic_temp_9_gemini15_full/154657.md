personalises: /ˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使个性化；定制 | To make something suitable for or appropriate to a particular person or their tastes.  | 例句：The website personalises the newsfeed based on your interests. = 网站根据您的兴趣个性化新闻推送。  | 例句：She personalises each gift to make it extra special. = 她个性化定制每一份礼物，使其更加特别。 | 近义词：customise, tailor, individualise | 反义词：generalise, standardise | 动词


USAGE->

The company personalises its marketing materials. = 公司将其营销材料个性化。
He personalises his work emails to each client. = 他将他的工作邮件个性化到每个客户。
