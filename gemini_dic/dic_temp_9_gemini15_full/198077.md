shojo:/ˈʃoʊdʒoʊ/| n.|少女漫画，少女|Japanese term referring to a genre of manga (Japanese comics) and anime (Japanese animation) targeted at a young female audience. Typically features romantic storylines, beautiful art, and characters with idealized appearances.|例句：She enjoys reading shojo manga about romance and school life. 她喜欢阅读关于恋爱和学校生活的少女漫画。|近义词：girls' manga, young adult manga|反义词：shonen (少年漫画)|名词
