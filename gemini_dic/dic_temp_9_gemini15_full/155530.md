The term often carries connotations of romanticism and idealism, and can refer to individuals who actively supported Greece during times of struggle, such as the Greek War of Independence. |  Lord Byron was a famous philhellene who fought and died for Greek independence.  拜伦勋爵是一位著名的亲希腊者，他为希腊独立而战并牺牲。|近义词：Greekophile, Hellenophile | 反义词：Philoturk (in historical context) | 名词
