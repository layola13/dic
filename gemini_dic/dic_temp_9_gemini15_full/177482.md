rebidding:/ˌriːˈbɪdɪŋ/| n.|重新投标；再次竞标|The act of submitting a new bid after an initial bid has been rejected or withdrawn.  It often happens in auctions or tender processes where the initial bids are deemed unsatisfactory.|例句：The company is considering rebidding on the construction project after their initial proposal was rejected. 公司正在考虑重新竞标该建筑项目，因为他们的初步提案被拒绝了。

例句：Due to unforeseen circumstances, the contractor decided to rebidding on the government contract. 由于不可预见的情况，承包商决定重新竞标政府合同。


近义词：re-tendering, resubmitting a bid, submitting a revised bid|反义词：withdrawing a bid|词性：名词
