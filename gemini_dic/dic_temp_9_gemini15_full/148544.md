oversweeten:/ˌoʊvərˈswiːtn/| vt.|加糖过多；使过于甜腻|To add too much sugar to something, making it excessively sweet.|The baker oversweetened the cake, making it inedible.|近义词：over-sugar, excessively sweeten|反义词：undersweeten


USAGE->

She oversweetens the tea for her guests. = 她为客人准备的茶加糖加得太多了。
