noncommunication: /ˌnɒnkəmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |  非沟通；沟通不良；缺乏沟通 | The failure to exchange information or understanding effectively.  It can refer to a complete lack of communication or a communication that is ineffective or misunderstood.  |


例句：

The breakdown in communication between the two departments led to noncommunication and ultimately, project failure. = 两个部门之间沟通的 breakdown 导致了沟通不良，最终导致项目失败。

His silence constituted a form of noncommunication, leaving her feeling ignored and frustrated. = 他的沉默构成了一种沟通不良的形式，让她感到被忽视和沮丧。


近义词：miscommunication, lack of communication, communication breakdown,  silence


反义词：communication, interaction, dialogue


词性：名词
