obfuscates:/ˈɒbsfəkeɪts/| v.|使模糊；使费解；使困惑|To make something unclear, confusing, or difficult to understand.  The politician obfuscated the issue with complex jargon. |近义词：obscure, confuse, bewilder, shroud|反义词：clarify, elucidate, enlighten|动词

He obfuscates his intentions with vague statements. = 他用含糊不清的言辞掩盖了他的意图。
