fictionalizer:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzər/| n. |虚构者；虚构化者|One who or that which fictionalizes; something that transforms something into a fictional account or representation.  It can refer to a person, a process, or a device that creates fiction. |The author acted as a fictionalizer, transforming real-life events into a compelling novel.  |近义词：novelist, storyteller, fabulist |反义词：documentarian, chronicler
