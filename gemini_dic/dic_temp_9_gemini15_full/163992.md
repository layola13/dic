predilection:/ˌpreːdɪˈlɛkʃən/| n. |偏爱，嗜好 |a strong liking for something or someone |She has a predilection for wearing bright colors. 她偏爱穿鲜艳的衣服。|近义词：preference, fondness, penchant |反义词：aversion, dislike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a predilection for spicy food. = 他偏爱辛辣食物。
My sister shows a predilection for classical music. = 我妹妹偏爱古典音乐。

一般过去时:
She had a predilection for romantic novels. = 她过去偏爱浪漫小说。
He displayed a predilection for solitude. = 他过去表现出对独处的偏爱。

现在完成时:
I have developed a predilection for hiking in the mountains. = 我已经养成了喜欢在山里远足的习惯。
They have shown a predilection for sustainable living. = 他们已经表现出对可持续生活的偏爱。

过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had a predilection for fast cars. = 事故发生前，他偏爱跑车。
She had always had a predilection for art, even as a child. = 她从小就一直偏爱艺术。

将来时:
He will likely develop a predilection for gardening as he gets older. = 他年纪大了可能会养成喜欢园艺的习惯。
I believe she will maintain her predilection for cats. = 我相信她会继续保持她对猫的喜爱。

虚拟语气:
If I had a predilection for anything, it would be chocolate. = 如果我有什么偏爱的话，那就是巧克力。
I wish I didn't have such a strong predilection for sweets. = 我真希望自己对甜食没有那么大的偏爱。

被动语态:
His predilection for gambling was widely known. = 他嗜赌成性是众所周知的。
Her predilection for vintage clothes is often commented on. = 她对复古服装的偏爱经常被人谈论。

疑问句:
What is your predilection in terms of music? = 你在音乐方面有什么偏爱？
Does he have a predilection for a particular type of wine? = 他对某种类型的葡萄酒有偏爱吗？

否定句:
He has no predilection for violence. = 他不喜欢暴力。
She showed no predilection for attending social gatherings. = 她对参加社交聚会没有表现出任何偏爱。


PHRASE->
a predilection for = 对……的偏爱
have a predilection for = 偏爱……
show a predilection for = 表现出对……的偏爱
a marked predilection for = 对……明显的偏爱
develop a predilection for = 养成对……的偏爱

词源:
From Middle French *prédilection, from Latin praedilectionem (nominative praedilectio), from praediligere "to prefer," from prae- "before" + diligere "to choose, love."  The word essentially means "a choosing before" or "a prior choosing."
