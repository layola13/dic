fluting:/ˈfluːtɪŋ/| n. v.|凹槽；槽纹；笛声；吹笛|The act of making flutes or grooves; a series of parallel grooves or channels.  It can refer to the decorative grooves on a column or other architectural element, the sound of flutes, or the act of playing the flute.|例句：The elegant fluting on the Greek columns was a testament to the skill of the artisans.  希腊柱子上优雅的凹槽证明了工匠的技艺。

The musician's skillful fluting filled the hall with beautiful melodies. 音乐家娴熟的笛声充满了大厅。|近义词：channeling, grooving, scoring|反义词：smoothness, plainness|名词，动词
