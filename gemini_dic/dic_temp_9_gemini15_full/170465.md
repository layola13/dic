pruinous:/ˈpruːɪnəs/| adj.|霜冻的，覆盖着霜的；霉烂的，腐败的|Covered with or resembling frost;  decayed or rotten.  The word evokes a sense of decay and deterioration, often associated with something that is withered or spoiled.|例句：The pruinous leaves crunched underfoot.  落叶在脚下发出嘎吱声。

The pruinous fruit was unfit for consumption.  腐烂的水果不宜食用。|近义词：frosty, rime-covered, moldy, decaying, rotten|反义词：fresh, pristine, sound, unspoiled
