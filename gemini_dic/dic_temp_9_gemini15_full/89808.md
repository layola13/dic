hornbeam:/ˈhɔːrnbiːm/| n. |角树，鹅耳枥|A hornbeam is a tough, strong hardwood tree with smooth, grey bark and leaves that resemble those of a beech tree.  It is known for its dense, strong wood and its ability to be shaped into hedges.|The hornbeam hedge provided a beautiful, impenetrable barrier around the garden. 角树树篱为花园提供了一个美丽而难以逾越的屏障。| beech,  hazel |  (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific type of tree)|名词
