sgml:/ˈɛsˌdʒiːˌɛmˈɛl/| n. |标准通用标记语言|Standard Generalized Markup Language; a system for annotating text in a way that is independent of the application that will ultimately use the text.  It is the basis for HTML and XML.|SGML is a powerful tool for creating and managing large amounts of structured data.|近义词：markup language, XML, HTML|反义词：None
