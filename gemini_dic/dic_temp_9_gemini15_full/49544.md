demasculinizing: /ˌdiːmæsˈkjʊlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使去男性化；使女性化 | The process of reducing or eliminating masculine characteristics or traits.  This can refer to social roles, behaviors, or physical attributes.  It can be a conscious or unconscious process, and often carries implications of challenging traditional gender norms. | Example:  The demasculinizing effects of modern society are debated by many sociologists.  现代社会去男性化的影响受到了许多社会学家的争论。 | 近义词：feminizing, effeminizing | 反义词：masculinizing
