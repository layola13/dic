eclecticist:/ɪˈklɛktɪsɪst/| n. |兼容并蓄者；折衷主义者 |A person who adopts eclectic methods or principles. An eclectic is someone who selects what seems best of various styles, sources, or systems.  |Example:  The museum's collection was the work of an eclectic curator, showcasing diverse styles from different eras.  博物馆的收藏是一位兼容并蓄的馆长的心血，展出了不同时代的多样化风格。|近义词： syncretist, integrator,  universalist |反义词： purist, traditionalist, dogmatist
