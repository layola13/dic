re-reform:/ˌriːˈfɔːrm/| v.|重新改革；再次改革|To reform again; to make further changes to something that has already been reformed.  The word implies a second or subsequent attempt at improvement or restructuring.|例句：The government announced a re-reform of the education system to address persistent inequalities.  政府宣布对教育体系进行重新改革，以解决持续存在的不平等问题。

例句：After the initial reforms proved insufficient, the company underwent a re-reform of its management structure. 首次改革证明不足后，公司对其管理结构进行了重新改革。|近义词：revise,amend,restructure|反义词：maintain,preserve,conserve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-reform the policy every year. = 他们每年都重新改革这项政策。
The committee re-reforms its procedures regularly. = 委员会定期重新改革其程序。

一般过去时:
The city re-reformed its public transportation system last year. = 该市去年重新改革了其公共交通系统。
They re-reformed the tax laws after much public debate. = 经过大量的公开辩论后，他们重新改革了税法。

现在完成时:
The company has re-reformed its marketing strategy several times. = 公司已经多次重新改革了其营销策略。
The government has re-reformed the healthcare system to improve access. = 政府已经重新改革了医疗系统以改善医疗服务的可及性。

过去完成时:
They had re-reformed the curriculum before the new academic year began. = 新学年开始前，他们已经重新改革了课程。
The organization had re-reformed its internal structure to become more efficient. = 该组织已经重新改革了其内部结构以提高效率。

将来时:
The school will re-reform its disciplinary policy next semester. = 学校将在下学期重新改革其纪律政策。
They will re-reform the voting system to ensure fairness. = 他们将重新改革投票系统以确保公平性。

虚拟语气:
If they re-reformed the law, it would be more effective. = 如果他们重新改革法律，它将会更有效。
I wish they had re-reformed the outdated regulations sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新改革那些过时的规章制度。

被动语态:
The regulations were re-reformed by the governing body. = 规章制度由管理机构重新改革。
The entire system is being re-reformed to meet new standards. = 整个系统正在被重新改革以满足新的标准。

疑问句:
Will the government re-reform the social security system? = 政府会重新改革社会保障体系吗？
Have they re-reformed the environmental protection laws yet? = 他们已经重新改革了环境保护法了吗？

否定句:
They did not re-reform the outdated policies. = 他们没有重新改革那些过时的政策。
The company has not re-reformed its hiring practices. = 公司尚未重新改革其招聘措施。
