pixies:/ˈpɪksiz/| n.|小精灵，小仙女|Mythological creatures of folklore, typically depicted as small, winged humanoids with magical powers. They are often mischievous but sometimes helpful.|例句：The children believed in pixies and fairies living in the woods.孩子们相信树林里住着小精灵和仙女。|例句：Many stories tell of pixies playing tricks on travelers.许多故事讲述小精灵捉弄旅行者的趣事。|近义词：fairies, sprites, elves|反义词: giants, trolls
