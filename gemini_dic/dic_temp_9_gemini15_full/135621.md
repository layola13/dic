nonpracticableness: /ˌnɒnˌpræktɪˈkæbəlɪnəs/ | n. |不可行性，不切实际| The impossibility or impracticality of something.  It refers to the state or quality of being not feasible or workable.  The concept encompasses various reasons for failure, including lack of resources, technological limitations, legal constraints, or inherent flaws in the plan itself. |例句：The sheer nonpracticableness of the plan led to its abandonment.  这项计划完全不可行，最终被放弃了。

The nonpracticableness of building a bridge across such a wide river was evident to everyone. 建造一座跨越如此宽阔河流的桥梁的不可行性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。


近义词：impracticability, infeasibility, unfeasibility, unrealism

反义词：feasibility, practicality, workability


词性: 名词
