preexpeditions:/ˌpriːˌɛkspəˈdɪʃənz/| n. |前期考察；预备探险|Preparatory expeditions or explorations conducted before a main expedition.  These might involve reconnaissance, resource assessment, or route planning.|例句：The preexpeditions to the Antarctic were crucial for the success of the main mission.前期南极考察对主要任务的成功至关重要。

The preexpeditions mapped the terrain and identified potential hazards.前期考察绘制了地形图并确定了潜在的危险。|近义词：reconnaissance, surveys, preliminary explorations |反义词：postexpeditions, aftermath
