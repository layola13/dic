remastication:/ˌriːmæsˈteɪʃən/| n.|再咀嚼；反复咀嚼|The act of chewing something again after it has already been swallowed.  It is often used in the context of ruminant animals, but can also refer to humans chewing their food again. |例句：The cow engaged in thorough remastication of the cud. 这头牛彻底地反刍了。
The chef tasted the sauce, then, after some consideration, performed a remastication. 厨师尝了尝酱汁，然后经过一番考虑后，又重新咀嚼了一下。|近义词：rechewing, chewing again|反义词：swallowing without chewing
