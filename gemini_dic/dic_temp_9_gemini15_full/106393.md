kookaburra:/ˌkʊkəˈbʌrə/| n. |笑翠鸟|A kookaburra is a type of kingfisher bird, known for its distinctive, loud, laughing call.  They are native to Australia and New Guinea. |Example:  We heard the kookaburras laughing in the early morning.  一大早我们听到笑翠鸟的叫声。|近义词: kingfisher (a broader term)|反义词: N/A
