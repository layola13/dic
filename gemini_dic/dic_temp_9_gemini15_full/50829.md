descriptor:/dɪˈskrɪptər/ | n. |描述符，描述词 |A word or phrase that describes something.  It can refer to a word used to describe a person, object, or event, or a technical term used in programming or databases to define data. |例句： "Tall" is a good descriptor for a giraffe.  “高大”是用来形容长颈鹿的一个很好的描述词。

例句： The database uses "age" and "occupation" as descriptors.  数据库使用“年龄”和“职业”作为描述符。


近义词：description, qualifier, attribute, term, characteristic | 反义词：None (antonyms depend on the specific descriptor used) | 词性: Noun

词源:
The word "descriptor" comes from the verb "describe," ultimately deriving from the Late Latin word *describere*, which means "to write down," "to delineate," or "to depict."  The suffix "-or" denotes an agent or instrument, thus creating "descriptor" to mean "something that describes."
