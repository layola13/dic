dines:/daɪnz/| v.|进餐，吃饭|To eat dinner.|例句：He dines elegantly every night. 他每晚都优雅地用餐。  He dines alone most evenings. 他大多数晚上独自用餐。|近义词：eats, suppers, feeds|反义词：fasts, starves


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dines at seven o'clock every night. = 他每晚七点吃饭。
They dine at a fancy restaurant every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都在一家高级餐厅吃饭。


一般过去时:
We dined at a Michelin-starred restaurant last night. = 我们昨晚在米其林星级餐厅用餐。
She dined with her family on her birthday. = 她在生日那天与家人共进晚餐。


现在完成时:
They have dined at that restaurant several times. = 他们在那家餐厅吃过几次饭了。
I have never dined in such a luxurious setting before. = 我以前从未在如此奢华的环境中用餐过。


过去完成时:
He had dined before the play began. = 在戏剧开始之前他已经吃过饭了。
We had already dined when they arrived. = 他们到达时我们已经吃过饭了。


将来时:
We will dine at the new Italian restaurant tonight. = 我们今晚将在新开的意大利餐厅用餐。
They will dine out to celebrate their anniversary. = 他们将外出就餐以庆祝他们的周年纪念日。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would dine at that famous restaurant. = 如果我有时间，我会在那家著名的餐厅用餐。
I wish I could dine with you tonight. = 我希望今晚能和你一起吃饭。


被动语态:
Dinner is usually dined at 7pm in this household. = 在这个家庭里，晚餐通常在晚上7点吃。
The meal was dined upon with great gusto. = 这顿饭吃得很香。


疑问句:
Where do you dine tonight? = 你今晚在哪里吃饭？
When will you dine with your friends? = 你什么时候和你朋友一起吃饭？


否定句:
I didn't dine out last night. = 我昨晚没有出去吃饭。
They don't dine at home very often. = 他们不经常在家吃饭。


PHRASE->
dine out = 外出吃饭
dine in = 在家吃饭
dine with = 与...共进晚餐
dine formally = 正式用餐
have a dinner = 吃一顿饭
