legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/ | adj. | 合法的；正规的；合理的 |  Legitimate means conforming to the law or to rules. It implies something is genuine, authentic, and valid.  It can also describe something that is reasonable or justifiable. |

例句：

The police investigated the legitimacy of the claim.  |警方调查了该说法是否合法。

His claim to the throne is legitimate. |他继承王位的权利是合法的。


近义词： lawful, legal, valid, genuine, authentic, reasonable, justifiable

反义词： illegal, unlawful, illegitimate, invalid, spurious, false, unreasonable, unjustified
