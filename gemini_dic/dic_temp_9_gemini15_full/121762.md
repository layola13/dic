misquotation:/ˌmɪskwoʊˈteɪʃən/| n. | 误引用；曲解 | An inaccurate or misleading quotation of someone's words.  It can involve changing the meaning of the original statement. | Example:  The article contained a blatant misquotation of the senator's speech. 这篇文章对参议员的讲话进行了公然的误引用。|近义词：misrepresentation, distortion, paraphrase (in a misleading way) |反义词：accurate quotation, faithful representation
