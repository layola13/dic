pinxit:/ˈpɪŋksɪt/| v.| 他画了；他画的 |  A third-person singular perfect indicative form of the Latin verb *pingere*, meaning "to paint". It is often used as an inscription on paintings, indicating the artist's authorship.  | Example:  The painting bore the inscription "Raphael pinxit." = 这幅画上刻着“拉斐尔作”。 | 近义词: painted, drew | 反义词: None directly applicable.  The opposite would be something like "did not paint".
