hyperlipidaemic: /ˌhaɪpərˌlɪpɪˈdiːmɪk/ | adj. | 高脂血症的；脂血症的 | Having or characterized by abnormally high concentrations of lipids in the blood.  This condition is a major risk factor for cardiovascular disease. |

例句：

He was diagnosed with hyperlipidaemic cardiomyopathy. = 他被诊断患有高脂血症性心肌病。

The doctor prescribed medication to control her hyperlipidaemic condition. = 医生开了药来控制她的高脂血症。


近义词：dyslipidemic


反义词：normolipidemic


词性：形容词


词源：

* **hyper-**:  from Greek ὑπέρ (hupér), meaning "over," "above," "beyond."
* **lipid-**: from Greek λίπος (lípos), meaning "fat."
* **-aemic**: from Greek -αιμία (-aimia), meaning "blood condition."  This suffix is often used to denote a condition related to the blood.
