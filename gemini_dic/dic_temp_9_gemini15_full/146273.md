/ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfəlˌnəs/ | n. | 过分忠诚；过度忠实 | Excessive loyalty or faithfulness; often to a fault.  It implies a degree of blind adherence that may be detrimental.  |  His overfaithfulness to a failing company cost him his savings.  他的过度忠诚毁掉了他的积蓄，因为他死心塌地地跟着一家即将倒闭的公司。 |  Devotedness, fidelity, allegiance |  Disloyalty, treachery, betrayal | 名词
