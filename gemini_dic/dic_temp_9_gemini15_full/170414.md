prowlers:/ˈpraʊlərz/| n.|潜行者，夜间游荡者|A prowler is someone who moves around stealthily, especially at night, often with the intention of committing a crime or causing harm.  It can also refer to an animal that hunts at night.|例句：The police warned residents to be cautious of prowlers in the neighborhood.  警察警告居民要小心街区的潜行者。

例句：  A pack of prowlers was spotted near the riverbank. 一群潜行者在河边被发现。


近义词：stalker, wanderer, trespasser, loiterer, predator

反义词：guard, protector, sentinel


词性：名词
