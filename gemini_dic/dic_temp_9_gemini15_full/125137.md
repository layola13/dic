This adjective describes something that can operate or be used in several different positions or locations, or a job that requires working in multiple locations.|例句：The company is looking for a multi-position engineer who can work on different projects at various sites. 这家公司正在寻找一位能够在不同地点从事不同项目的全能工程师。

The new multi-position camera allows for a wider range of shots. 这款新型多角度相机允许拍摄更广阔的范围。|近义词：versatile, adaptable, flexible|反义词：fixed, stationary, single-position|
