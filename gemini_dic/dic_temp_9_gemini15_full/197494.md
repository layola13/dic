sheikdoms:/ˈʃeɪkdəm/| n. |酋长国；谢赫统治的地区|A sheikdom is a territory ruled by a sheikh.  Sheikdoms are often associated with Arab countries in the Middle East and are typically characterized by a strong centralized leadership under a hereditary ruler.  |例句：The United Arab Emirates is a federation of seven sheikdoms.  阿拉伯联合酋长国是由七个酋长国组成的联邦。

The seven sheikdoms of the UAE each maintain a degree of autonomy.  阿联酋的七个酋长国各自保持一定程度的自治权。|近义词：principality, emirate|反义词：republic, democracy|
