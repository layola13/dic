relations:/rɪˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |关系；联系；关联 | The word "relations" refers to the way in which two or more people, things, or concepts are connected, or the state of being connected.  It can encompass a wide range of connections, from familial ties to international diplomacy. | Example:  The relations between the two countries are currently strained.  两个国家之间的关系目前很紧张。  |The relationship between siblings can be complex. 兄弟姐妹之间的关系可能很复杂。| kinship, connection, association, linkage | discord, conflict, antagonism | 名词

一般现在时:
My relations with my neighbors are good. = 我和邻居的关系很好。
Their relations with the company are improving. = 他们与公司的关系正在改善。

一般过去时:
International relations were tense during the Cold War. = 冷战期间国际关系紧张。

现在完成时:
Relations between the two countries have improved significantly. = 两国关系已显著改善。
We have had a strong relationship with our clients for many years. = 多年来，我们与客户保持着良好的关系。

过去完成时:

将来时:

The ambassador predicted a strengthening of relations in the coming months. = 大使预测未来几个月关系将加强。
