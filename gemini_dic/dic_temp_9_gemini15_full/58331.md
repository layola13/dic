drysalter:/ˈdraɪˌsɔːltər/| n. |干货商；杂货商|A drysalter is a merchant who deals in dried goods, such as spices, salt, and other non-perishable food items.  They were historically important in supplying communities with essential provisions.|The drysalter sold a wide variety of spices and dried fruits.|近义词：grocer, provisioner|反义词：无
