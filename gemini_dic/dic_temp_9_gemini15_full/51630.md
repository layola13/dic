develops:/dɪˈvɛləps/ | v. |发展；开发；养成；显现 |To grow or improve; to create or produce something new; to become apparent or noticeable. |例句：The company is developing a new product. 公司正在开发一款新产品。|例句：His skills developed rapidly. 他的技能迅速提高。|近义词：progresses, advances, expands, evolves |反义词：declines, deteriorates, regresses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The business develops new strategies every year. = 这家公司每年都会制定新的战略。
She develops her photographic skills by practicing regularly. = 她通过定期练习来提高摄影技巧。


一般过去时:
The city developed rapidly after the war. = 战后这座城市迅速发展。
He developed a strong interest in music during his childhood. = 他在童年时期对音乐产生了浓厚的兴趣。


现在完成时:
The company has developed several successful products. = 这家公司已经开发出几款成功的产品。
She has developed a reputation for her hard work. = 她因努力工作而赢得了声誉。


过去完成时:
They had developed a plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经制定了一个计划。
The scientists had developed a new vaccine before the pandemic hit. = 科学家们在疫情爆发前已经研制出一种新疫苗。


将来时:
The technology will develop further in the next decade. = 这项技术在未来十年将得到进一步发展。
They will develop a new marketing campaign next month. = 他们下个月将开展一项新的营销活动。


虚拟语气:
If the project developed as planned, we would be successful. = 如果项目按计划进行，我们会成功。
I wish I had developed better study habits earlier. = 我真希望我早点养成更好的学习习惯。


被动语态:
The software is being developed by a team of experts. = 该软件正在由一个专家团队开发。
New methods were developed to improve efficiency. = 开发了新的方法来提高效率。


疑问句:
How does the plot develop in the novel? = 这部小说情节是如何发展的？
What problems did they develop during the project? = 他们在项目期间遇到了什么问题？


否定句:
The film didn't develop the characters well enough. = 这部电影没有很好地刻画人物。
The company failed to develop a successful marketing strategy. = 公司未能制定成功的营销策略。
