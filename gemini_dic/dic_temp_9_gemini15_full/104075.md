jumbos:/ˈdʒʌmboʊz/| n.|大型飞机；大象；特大型事物|A jumbo is something that is unusually large.  It often refers to large airplanes, elephants, or other exceptionally large things.|例句：We flew on a jumbo jet to Australia. 我们乘坐大型喷气式飞机飞往澳大利亚。

例句：The circus featured a magnificent jumbo. 马戏团里有一头雄伟的大象。


近义词：giant, colossal, enormous, huge|反义词：tiny, miniature, small


词源:  The word "jumbo" originated from the name of a large African elephant, Jumbo, who was owned by P.T. Barnum's circus in the 19th century.  Jumbo's fame and size led to the word's adoption as a descriptor for things of exceptionally large size.
