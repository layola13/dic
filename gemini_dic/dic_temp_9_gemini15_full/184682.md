restuffing: /ˌriːˈstʌfɪŋ/ | v. | 重新填塞；再填充 | To fill something again, especially with the same material.  |例句：He spent the afternoon restuffing the old armchair. 他花了整个下午重新填塞旧扶手椅。

例句：The chef restuffed the turkey with a delicious herb mixture.  厨师用美味的香草混合物重新填塞火鸡。


近义词：refill, repack, replenish | 反义词：empty, evacuate

词性：动词
