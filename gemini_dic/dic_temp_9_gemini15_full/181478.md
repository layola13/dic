rejourn:/rɪˈdʒɜːrn/| v.|休会；延期；暂停|To adjourn is to formally bring a meeting, session, or proceedings to a temporary end, with the intention of resuming later.|The judge adjourned the court until the following morning. 法官将法庭休庭到第二天早上。|The committee adjourned to discuss the matter further in private. 委员会休会，以便私下进一步讨论此事。|近义词：suspend, postpone, defer|反义词：resume, reconvene


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The court adjourns at 5 p.m. every day. = 法院每天下午5点休庭。
The committee adjourns its meetings when the agenda is finished. = 委员会在议程结束后休会。


一般过去时:
The meeting adjourned after three hours of debate. = 会议经过三个小时的辩论后休会了。
The judge adjourned the trial until next week. = 法官将审判延期到下周。


现在完成时:
The council has adjourned until further notice. = 理事会已休会，直到另行通知。
They have adjourned the meeting several times due to lack of quorum. = 由于缺乏法定人数，他们已经几次休会。


过去完成时:
The committee had adjourned before the important news arrived. = 重要消息到达之前，委员会已经休会了。
After the witness had given his testimony, the court adjourned for lunch. = 证人作证后，法庭休庭吃午饭。


将来时:
The session will adjourn after the final vote. = 最后投票后，会议将休会。
The meeting will adjourn when the speaker finishes. = 演讲者结束后，会议将休会。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, we would adjourn the meeting outdoors. = 如果天气更好，我们会把会议改在户外举行。
I wish the meeting had adjourned earlier. = 我希望会议早点结束。


被动语态:
The meeting was adjourned due to a power outage. = 由于停电，会议被休会了。
The session was adjourned abruptly because of the disturbance. = 会议由于骚乱而突然休会了。


疑问句:
When will the meeting adjourn? = 会议什么时候休会？
Has the court adjourned for the day? = 法院今天休庭了吗？


否定句:
The meeting did not adjourn until late at night. = 会议直到深夜才休会。
The judge refused to adjourn the hearing. = 法官拒绝休庭。


PHRASE->
adjourn a meeting = 休会
adjourn sine die = 永远休会
adjourn until further notice = 延期到另行通知
adjourn the court = 休庭
adjourn the proceedings = 暂停程序
