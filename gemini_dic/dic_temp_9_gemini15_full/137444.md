nontherapeutical: /ˌnɒnˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkəl/ | adj. | 非治疗性的 | Not relating to or intended for the treatment of disease or injury.  This adjective describes something that does not have a therapeutic purpose or effect. It indicates the absence of healing or curative properties. |

例句：
The side effects of the medication were nontherapeutical, causing further discomfort. = 药物的副作用是非治疗性的，导致了进一步的不适。

The study focused on nontherapeutical uses of the compound. = 这项研究的重点是非治疗性用途的化合物。


近义词：curative, remedial, therapeutic (反义词)


反义词：therapeutic, curative, remedial


词性:形容词
