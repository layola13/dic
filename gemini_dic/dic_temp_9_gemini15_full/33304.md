chuck-out:/tʃʌkˈaʊt/| vt.|抛出；赶走；丢弃 | To throw something away forcefully or carelessly; to forcefully eject someone from a place. |例句：He chucked the old newspapers out. 他用力扔掉了旧报纸。They chucked him out of the pub. 他们把他从酒吧赶了出去。|近义词：throw out, eject, expel, discard |反义词：keep, retain, welcome


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chucks the rubbish out every day. = 他每天都把垃圾扔出去。
They chuck unwanted items out regularly. = 他们定期扔掉不需要的东西。

一般过去时:
She chucked the broken vase out. = 她把破花瓶扔掉了。
The bouncer chucked the unruly patron out. = 保安把那个不守规矩的顾客赶了出去。

现在完成时:
We have chucked out all the old clothes. = 我们已经把所有旧衣服都扔掉了。
They have chucked him out of the party. = 他们已经把他从派对上赶了出去。

过去完成时:
He had chucked out all the evidence before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经把所有证据都扔掉了。
They had chucked her out of the club before I got there. = 在我到达之前，他们已经把她从俱乐部赶了出去。

将来时:
I will chuck this broken chair out tomorrow. = 我明天要把这把坏椅子扔掉。
They will chuck him out if he continues to misbehave. = 如果他继续不规矩，他们会把他赶出去。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it was broken, I would have chucked it out earlier. = 如果我知道它坏了，我就会早点把它扔掉。
If he behaved himself, they wouldn't have chucked him out. = 如果他规矩点，他们就不会把他赶出去了。

被动语态:
The old furniture was chucked out. = 旧家具被扔掉了。
He was chucked out of the meeting for being disruptive. = 他因为扰乱秩序而被赶出了会议。

疑问句:
Did they chuck the letter out? = 他们把信扔掉了吗？
Will they chuck me out if I'm late? = 如果我迟到了，他们会把我赶出去吗？

否定句:
I didn't chuck the box out. = 我没有把箱子扔掉。
They didn't chuck him out of the game. = 他们没有把他赶出比赛。
