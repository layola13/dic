croupy:/ˈkruːpi/| adj.|  类似喉炎的；声音嘶哑的；|  Having a cough or voice like that of a person with croup; characterized by a harsh, barking cough.  |例句：The child's croupy cough kept everyone awake.  孩子的喉炎样咳嗽使每个人都睡不着觉。|例句：His voice was croupy and raspy. 他的声音嘶哑沙哑。|近义词：hoarse, raspy, harsh |反义词：clear, smooth
