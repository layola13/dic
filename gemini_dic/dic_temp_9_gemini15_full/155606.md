philosophises:/fɪˈlɒsəfaɪzɪz/| v. | 哲学思考；思辨；空谈 | To engage in philosophical thought or discussion; to reason in a philosophical manner; sometimes implying excessive or unproductive speculation. | He philosophises about the meaning of life. 他在思考生命的意义。  She philosophises endlessly about the nature of reality. 她没完没了地空谈现实的本质。 | ponders, speculates, contemplates | acts, does | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He philosophises about ethics every day. = 他每天都在思考伦理道德。
She philosophises on the nature of existence. = 她思考存在的本质。


一般过去时:
He philosophised about the meaning of life for hours. = 他花了几个小时思考生命的意义。
She philosophised on the nature of time. = 她思考时间的本质。


现在完成时:
He has philosophised extensively on the subject of free will. = 他对自由意志这个主题进行了广泛的哲学思考。
She has philosophised about the existence of God. = 她思考过上帝的存在。


过去完成时:
He had philosophised for years before writing his magnum opus. = 他在写他的巨著之前已经思考了很多年。
She had philosophised extensively on the topic before the debate. = 在辩论之前，她对这个话题进行了广泛的思考。


将来时:
He will philosophise on the implications of artificial intelligence. = 他将思考人工智能的影响。
She will philosophise about the future of humanity. = 她将思考人类的未来。


虚拟语气:
If he philosophised more, he might understand better. = 如果他多思考一些，他可能会更好地理解。
If she had philosophised more deeply, she might have reached a different conclusion. = 如果她更深入地思考，她可能会得出不同的结论。


被动语态:
The topic was philosophised upon by many great thinkers. = 许多伟大的思想家都对这个主题进行了哲学思考。
The problem was philosophised about, but no solution was found. = 这个问题被反复思考，但没有找到解决方案。

否定句:
