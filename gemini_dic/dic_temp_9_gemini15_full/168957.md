profit:/ˈprɒfɪt/ /ˈprɑːfɪt/| n. v.|利润，盈利；获益，得益|The financial gain resulting from a transaction; advantage or benefit.|例句：The company reported a large profit this year.  今年公司盈利丰厚。|例句：She profited greatly from her experience. 她从她的经验中获益良多。|近义词：gain, earnings, benefit, advantage|反义词：loss, deficit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company profits from this new product. = 这家公司从这个新产品中获利。
We profit from our mistakes. = 我们从错误中吸取教训。

一般过去时:
They profited handsomely from the deal. = 他们从这笔交易中获得了巨额利润。
The farmers profited from the good harvest. = 农民们从丰收中获益。

现在完成时:
We have profited significantly from this investment. = 我们从这项投资中获得了可观的收益。
The company has profited immensely from its innovative approach. = 公司因其创新方法而获得了巨大的利润。

过去完成时:
They had profited considerably before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经获得了相当大的利润。
The investors had profited from the rising stock prices. = 投资者们已经从不断上涨的股价中获利。

将来时:
We will profit from the new marketing strategy. = 我们将从新的营销策略中获益。
The company expects to profit substantially from the expansion. = 公司预计将从扩张中获得可观的利润。

虚拟语气:
If we invested wisely, we would profit greatly. = 如果我们明智地投资，我们将获益良多。
I wish I had profited more from that opportunity. = 我真希望我从那个机会中获得更多利益。

被动语态:
The shareholders were profited by the successful merger. = 股东们从成功的合并中获益。
The community will be profited by the new infrastructure project. = 新的基础设施项目将使社区受益。

疑问句:
How much profit did the business make last year? = 去年这家企业赚了多少利润？
Will this venture profit the company? = 这项投资会给公司带来利润吗？

否定句:
The project didn't profit the company as much as expected. = 该项目并没有像预期的那样给公司带来多少利润。
The company did not profit from the new product launch. = 公司并没有从新产品的发布中获利。
