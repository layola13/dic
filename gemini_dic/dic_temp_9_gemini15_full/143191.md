outcompeting: /ˌaʊt kəmˈpiːtɪŋ/ | v. |胜过，竞争中击败 |  To surpass or defeat in competition.  It describes the act of being more successful than others in a competitive environment.  The word implies a struggle for dominance or superiority. |

例句：

Their innovative marketing strategy allowed them to outcompete their rivals. = 他们创新的营销策略使他们能够战胜竞争对手。

Smaller companies often struggle to outcompete larger corporations. = 小型公司通常难以与大型公司竞争。


近义词：outshine, outstrip, surpass, exceed, overshadow

反义词：underperform, lose to, be defeated by

词性: 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new product consistently outcompetes its predecessors in the market. = 新产品在市场上始终胜过其前辈产品。
Our team outcompetes other teams in terms of efficiency. = 我们的团队在效率方面胜过其他团队。


一般过去时:
Last year, our company outcompeted all its competitors in sales. = 去年，我们公司在销售额上击败了所有竞争对手。
The athlete outcompeted his rivals to win the gold medal. = 运动员战胜对手赢得金牌。


现在完成时:
Our company has outcompeted its rivals in the market for the last five years. = 我们公司在过去五年中一直胜过其竞争对手。
The innovative technology has outcompeted traditional methods. = 创新技术已经胜过传统方法。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, our team had outcompeted the other teams in terms of quality. = 在项目结束时，我们的团队在质量上已经胜过其他团队。
The company had outcompeted its rivals before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经击败了其竞争对手。


将来时:
We believe our new strategy will outcompete the competition in the long run. = 我们相信我们的新战略从长远来看将胜过竞争对手。
The improved design is expected to outcompete existing models. = 改进后的设计有望胜过现有型号。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more in research, we might have outcompeted our rivals. = 如果我们对研究投资更多，我们可能会胜过竞争对手。
If they had a better marketing strategy, they could have outcompeted their competitors. = 如果他们有更好的营销策略，他们本可以胜过竞争对手。


被动语态:
The smaller company was outcompeted by the larger corporation. = 小型公司被大型公司击败。
Their product was outcompeted in the market due to its high price. = 他们的产品由于价格过高而在市场上被击败。


疑问句:
Will our new product outcompete the existing ones in the market? = 我们的新产品会在市场上胜过现有产品吗？
Did their marketing campaign successfully outcompete the competition? = 他们的营销活动成功地击败了竞争对手吗？


否定句:
Our efforts to outcompete the competition were unsuccessful. = 我们战胜竞争的努力失败了。
They failed to outcompete their rivals in the market share. = 他们未能战胜竞争对手的市场份额。


PHRASE->
outcompete the market = 称霸市场
outcompete rivals = 击败竞争对手
outcompete competitors = 胜过竞争者
outcompete the industry = 统治行业
outcompete others = 胜过其他人
