extols:/ɪkˈstoʊlz/| vt.|赞扬；颂扬|To praise enthusiastically; to glorify.|The critic extolled the novel's innovative style. 批评家盛赞这部小说的创新风格。|近义词：praise, laud, commend, celebrate|反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extols the virtues of hard work. = 他赞扬努力工作的优点。
She extols the benefits of regular exercise. = 她赞扬定期锻炼的好处。

一般过去时:
The speaker extolled the achievements of the company. = 演讲者赞扬了公司的成就。
The article extolled the beauty of the natural landscape. = 文章赞美了自然景观的美丽。

现在完成时:
They have extolled the importance of education for years. = 他们多年来一直赞扬教育的重要性。
She has extolled the benefits of meditation in her recent book. = 她在最近的书中赞扬了冥想的好处。

过去完成时:
He had extolled the virtues of patience before the difficult task. = 在面临艰巨的任务之前，他曾赞扬过耐心的美德。
They had extolled the bravery of the firefighters after the blaze. = 大火之后，他们曾赞扬消防员的勇敢。

将来时:
The mayor will extol the city's new initiatives at the conference. = 市长将在会议上赞扬城市的新举措。
She will extol the merits of her new product in her presentation. = 她将在演示中赞扬她新产品的优点。

虚拟语气:
If I were to write a review, I would extol the film's stunning visuals. = 如果我要写一篇影评，我会赞扬这部电影令人惊叹的视觉效果。
I wish I could extol the virtues of this product more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地赞扬这个产品的优点。

被动语态:
The benefits of exercise are extolled by many health professionals. = 许多健康专业人士都赞扬运动的好处。
His achievements were extolled in numerous articles. = 他的成就被许多文章所赞扬。

疑问句:
Does the author extol the virtues of democracy in his latest book? = 作者在他的最新著作中是否赞扬了民主的优点？
Will the speech extol the benefits of international cooperation? = 演讲会赞扬国际合作的好处吗？

否定句:
The critic did not extol the film's plot, finding it predictable. = 批评家并没有赞扬这部电影的情节，认为它很可预测。
He did not extol the virtues of laziness, instead emphasizing diligence. = 他并没有赞扬懒惰的优点，而是强调勤奋。
