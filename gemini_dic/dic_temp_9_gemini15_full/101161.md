invidiously:/ɪnˈvɪdiəsli/| adj.| 恶意地；暗中地；阴险地 |  Characterized by envy, malice, or ill will, often in a subtle or hidden way.  It suggests a deliberate attempt to cause harm or damage, often through indirect means. | The company invidiously targeted its competitor's marketing campaign. 这家公司恶意地针对其竞争对手的营销活动。|近义词：maliciously, spitefully,  enviously, sneakily |反义词：kindly, generously, openly, fairly

She invidiously undermines her coworkers' efforts. 她暗中破坏同事的努力。

虚拟语气:
