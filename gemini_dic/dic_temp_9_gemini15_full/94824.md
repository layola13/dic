impurely:/ɪmˈpjʊrli/ | adv. | 不纯地；不洁地；不完全地 | In an impure manner; not completely or perfectly.  The experiment was conducted impurely, leading to unreliable results.  | 近义词：uncleanly, imperfectly, incompletely | 反义词：purely, cleanly, completely


例句：

The water was impurely sourced, resulting in a contamination outbreak. = 水源不纯，导致了污染爆发。

He argued impurely, leaving out crucial evidence. = 他论证不充分，遗漏了关键证据。
