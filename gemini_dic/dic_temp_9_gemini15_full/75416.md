frugality:/fruːˈɡæləti/| n. |节俭；俭省；朴素 |The quality of being economical with money or food; thriftiness.  Frugality is often associated with avoiding waste and unnecessary spending. |例句：Her frugality allowed her to save enough money for a down payment on a house.  她的节俭让她攒够了足够的钱付房子的首付。

例句：His frugality was evident in his simple lifestyle. 他的俭朴生活方式体现了他的节俭。

近义词：thrift, economy, sparingness, parsimony

反义词：extravagance, profligacy, wastefulness

词源:  From Middle English *frugalite, from Old French frugalité, from frugal "thrifty," from Latin frugi "fruitful, productive, good," from frux, frugis "fruit, produce, enjoyment."
