nonbeneficial:/ˌnɒnˌbenɪˈfɪʃəl/ | adj. |无益的，有害的 | Not providing any benefit; harmful or disadvantageous.  | Example:  The side effects of the medication were nonbeneficial and even detrimental to the patient's health.  这种药物的副作用弊大于利，甚至对病人的健康有害。 |近义词：harmful, detrimental, disadvantageous, unhelpful |反义词：beneficial, advantageous, helpful, useful
