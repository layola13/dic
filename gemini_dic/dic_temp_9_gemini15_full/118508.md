mesobiology:/ˌmɛzoʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/| n. |介观生物学|The study of biological processes at the mesoscale, which is the intermediate level between the microscopic and macroscopic scales.  It bridges the gap between molecular biology and macroscopic observations, focusing on phenomena arising from the collective behavior of many molecules or cells.  |例句：Mesobiology research is advancing our understanding of complex biological systems.  介观生物学研究正在增进我们对复杂生物系统的理解。|近义词：systems biology, integrative biology |反义词：microscopy (as a contrasting level of study)
