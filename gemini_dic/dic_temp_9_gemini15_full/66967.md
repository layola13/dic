experimentation:/ˌɛkspɛrɪmənˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 实验；试验；尝试 | The process of conducting experiments; the act of trying out new things or methods.  It involves testing hypotheses and observing results. | Example: The scientist's experimentation led to a breakthrough discovery.  这位科学家的实验导致了一项突破性发现。

Example:  The company's experimentation with new marketing strategies proved successful. 这家公司尝试新的营销策略证明是成功的。|近义词：testing, trial, research |反义词：theory, speculation
