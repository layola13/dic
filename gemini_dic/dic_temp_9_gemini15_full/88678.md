hoaxing: /ˈhoʊksɪŋ/ | v. | 欺骗；开玩笑；恶作剧 |  The act of deceiving someone, typically by playing a prank or spreading false information.  | 例句：He was hoaxing the public with his fake news stories. 他在用假新闻欺骗公众。  | 例句：The children were hoaxing their teacher by pretending to be sick. 孩子们假装生病来戏弄他们的老师。 | 近义词：tricking, deceiving, kidding, playing a prank on | 反义词：telling the truth, being honest | 动词
