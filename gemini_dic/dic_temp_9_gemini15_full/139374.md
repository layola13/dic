obsequience: /əˈsiːkwɪəns/ | n. | 顺从；奉承；谄媚 |  Obsequience is excessive willingness to serve or please others.  It often involves flattery and a lack of independent thought.  It suggests a fawning or servile attitude. | Example:  His obsequience to the dictator was sickening.  他的对独裁者的顺从令人作呕。

The manager’s obsequience to the CEO was evident in every meeting. 经理对CEO的顺从在每次会议上都显而易见。|近义词：subservience, servility, fawning, flattery |反义词：defiance, insubordination, independence
