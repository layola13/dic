cybernetically:/ˌsaɪbərˈnɛtɪkli/| adv.|控制论地，电子控制地|In a manner characteristic of or relating to cybernetics;  using or involving cybernetics.|例句：The factory is cybernetically controlled, meaning its processes are automated and regulated by computer systems.  工厂是电子控制的，这意味着它的流程是自动化的，并由计算机系统进行调节。 The experiment was conducted cybernetically, with precise data collection and feedback loops. 实验是通过电子控制进行的，具有精确的数据收集和反馈回路。|近义词：automatically, mechanically, electronically|反义词：manually, organically
