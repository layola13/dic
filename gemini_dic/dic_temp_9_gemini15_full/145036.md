overage:/ˈoʊvərɪdʒ/ | n. | 超量；过量；超额；剩余 | Excess quantity; an amount exceeding what is usual, expected, or allowed.  | Example: There was a significant overage in the inventory.  库存有大量的剩余。

The bank charged an overage fee for exceeding the withdrawal limit.  银行因超过取款限额而收取超额费用。|近义词：surplus, excess, extra | 反义词：shortage, deficiency
