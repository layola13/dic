compound:/ˈkɒmpaʊnd/ /ˈkɑːmpaʊnd/| n., adj., v.|化合物；复合的；混合的；合成；混合|

英文解析:As a noun, a compound refers to something formed by combining two or more parts or elements.  As an adjective, it describes something composed of two or more parts or elements. As a verb, it means to combine or mix together.  The specific meaning depends on the context.

例句:

(Noun):
Salt is a compound of sodium and chlorine.  = 盐是钠和氯的化合物。

Water is a compound substance. = 水是一种化合物。


(Adjective):
The building had a compound design. = 这座建筑物设计复杂。

He gave a compound answer. = 他给出了一个复杂的答案。


(Verb):
The chemist compounded the medicine. = 药剂师配制了这种药。

They compounded their problems by arguing. = 他们通过争吵加剧了他们的问题。


近义词:
(Noun): mixture, combination, blend
(Adjective): complex, composite, combined
(Verb): mix, blend, combine


反义词:
(Adjective): simple, single, unmixed
(Verb): separate, divide, decompose


词性: noun, adjective, verb
