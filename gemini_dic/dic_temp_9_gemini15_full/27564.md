canaling:/kəˈneɪlɪŋ/| n.|运河化；开凿运河；|The process of creating canals or channels.  It often involves the construction of artificial waterways for navigation, irrigation, or drainage. |例句：The canalizing of the river improved transportation.  河道的运河化改善了运输。|例句：The project involved extensive canalizing to create a navigable waterway. 这个项目涉及大规模的开凿运河以创造一条可通航的水道。|近义词：channelization, canalization|反义词：damming, blocking|名词
