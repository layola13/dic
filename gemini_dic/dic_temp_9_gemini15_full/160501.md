porcupines: /ˈpɔːrkjupaɪnz/ | n. | 豪猪 | A rodent covered in sharp spines or quills.  Porcupines are known for their defensive mechanism of raising their quills when threatened. They are relatively solitary animals and are found in various habitats across the globe. |  The porcupines in the zoo were surprisingly active. 动物园里的豪猪出人意料地活跃。  The farmer found his crops damaged by porcupines.  农民发现他的庄稼被豪猪破坏了。| hedgehogs, quill pigs |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "smooth-skinned rodent") | 名词
