schoolboy:/ˈskuːlˌbɔɪ/| n. |小学生，男学生 |A schoolboy is a boy who attends school.  The term typically refers to a boy in primary or secondary school, not a university student.  |Example: The schoolboy eagerly awaited the school holidays.  这位小学生迫不及待地等待着学校假期。

The schoolboy tripped and fell in the playground. 这个小学生在操场上绊倒了。|近义词：pupil, student (in certain contexts)|反义词：schoolgirl
