intercarpal:/ˌɪntərˈkɑːrpəl/| adj.|腕骨间的|Relating to the bones of the wrist (carpus); situated between the carpal bones.  The term describes structures or movements that occur between the individual bones within the wrist joint.|例句：Intercarpal ligaments help stabilize the wrist joint. 腕骨间的韧带有助于稳定腕关节。|例句：The radiologist identified an intercarpal fracture. 放射科医生发现了一处腕骨间的骨折。|近义词：carpal, intracarpal|反义词：extracarpal
