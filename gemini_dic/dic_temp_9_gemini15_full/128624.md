nickelian:/nɪˈkiːliən/| adj. |镍的；含镍的；像镍的|  Relating to or containing nickel.  The term describes materials or substances that possess nickel properties or have nickel as a component. |例句：The alloy possesses a nickelian sheen. 这合金具有镍一样的光泽。

例句：The nickelian content of the steel was carefully controlled.  钢材中的镍含量得到了严格控制。


近义词：nickel-containing, nickel-based


反义词：nickel-free


词性：形容词
