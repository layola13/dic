preidea:/ˌpriːaɪˈdiːə/| n.|预想，初步构想|A preidea is a preliminary or initial idea, a concept that is still in its early stages of development and may not be fully formed or refined. It's a seed of an idea before it's fully developed.|例句：The preidea for the novel involved a detective searching for a missing heir.|近义词：concept, notion, conception, inkling|反义词：final product, finished work
