psychosis:/saɪˈkoʊsɪs/| n. |精神病，精神失常|A severe mental disorder in which thought and emotions are so impaired that contact is lost with external reality.  It is characterized by symptoms such as delusions, hallucinations, disorganized speech, and grossly disorganized or abnormal motor behavior.|例句：His psychosis manifested as auditory hallucinations and paranoid delusions. 他的精神病表现为幻听和偏执妄想。

例句：The medication helped to alleviate the symptoms of his psychosis.  药物帮助减轻了他的精神病症状。


近义词：insanity, madness, mental illness, schizophrenia |反义词：sanity, normalcy, mental health|词性:名词
