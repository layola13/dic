decarburize:/ˌdiːkɑːrˈbʌraɪz/ | vt. | 脱碳；除碳 | To remove carbon from (a substance, especially iron or steel).  |例句：The steel was decarburized to improve its ductility.  钢材经过脱碳处理以提高其延展性。

例句：The process decarburizes the iron, making it more malleable. 这个过程使铁脱碳，使其更易锻造。


近义词： decarbonize


反义词： carburize


词性：动词
