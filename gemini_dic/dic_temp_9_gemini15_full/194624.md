self-therapies:/ˈsɛlf ˈθɛrəpiz/| n. |自我疗法|Methods and techniques individuals use to manage their mental and emotional well-being independently, without professional help.  These can include practices like meditation, journaling, exercise, and cognitive behavioral techniques. |Example:  She found solace in self-therapies like yoga and mindfulness meditation to cope with stress. 她通过瑜伽和正念冥想等自我疗法来缓解压力。|近义词：self-help, self-care, personal growth strategies|反义词：professional therapy, psychotherapy
