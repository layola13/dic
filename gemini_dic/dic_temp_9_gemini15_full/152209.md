patrimony:/ˈpætrɪməni/| n. |遗产；祖传财产；继承权；父权；传统|Property inherited from one's ancestors; a heritage; the rights, privileges, etc., transmitted from father to son; a system of patrilineal descent.|例句：The family's patrimony included a vast estate and a collection of priceless antiques.  这个家族的遗产包括一个广阔的庄园和一系列无价的古董。


例句：His artistic patrimony is evident in his paintings, which clearly show the influence of his father and grandfather.  他的艺术遗产在他的绘画中显而易见，这些绘画清楚地展现了他父亲和祖父的影响。


近义词：inheritance, legacy, heritage, bequest|反义词：disinheritance, forfeiture
