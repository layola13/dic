devolvement:/dɪˈvɒlvmənt/ | n. |权力下放；移交；衰退；退化 | The transfer or delegation of power to a lower level, especially from the central government to local or regional administrations.  It can also refer to a decline or deterioration in something. |例句：The devolvement of power to regional governments has led to increased autonomy. 权力下放给地方政府导致了更大的自治权。

The devolvement of responsibility for education to individual schools has been met with mixed reactions.  将教育责任下放给各个学校的做法褒贬不一。


近义词：delegation, transfer, devolution, decentralization | 反义词：centralization, concentration
