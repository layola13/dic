difference:/ˈdɪfərəns/| n.|差异，不同，分歧|The difference between the two plans is significant. 这两个计划之间的差异很大。|近义词：distinction, disparity, divergence|反义词：similarity, resemblance, likeness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The difference between A and B is obvious. = A和B之间的区别是显而易见的。
What is the difference in price? = 价格差是多少？


一般过去时:
The difference was not immediately apparent. = 差异并非立即显现。
There was a significant difference in their opinions. = 他们的意见存在显著差异。


现在完成时:
The scientists have found a difference in the samples. = 科学家们在样本中发现了差异。
What differences have you noticed? = 你注意到了哪些不同之处？


过去完成时:
By then, the difference had become clear. = 到那时，差异已经变得清晰。
I had not noticed the difference before. = 我以前没有注意到这种差异。


将来时:
There will be a difference in the results. = 结果会有所不同。
What difference will it make? = 这会有何不同？


虚拟语气:
If there were no difference, it wouldn't matter. = 如果没有区别，那就不重要了。
I wish there were less difference between rich and poor. = 我希望贫富差距能小一些。


被动语态:
The difference was highlighted in the report. = 报告中突出了差异。
The differences are being analyzed by the team. = 团队正在分析这些差异。


疑问句:
What is the difference? = 区别是什么？
Is there any difference? = 有什么区别吗？


否定句:
There is no difference between them. = 他们之间没有区别。
I can't see any difference. = 我看不出有什么区别。
