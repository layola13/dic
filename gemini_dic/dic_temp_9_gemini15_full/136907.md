nonsolidarity:/ˌnɒnsɒlɪˈdærɪti/| n. |非团结；缺乏团结；分裂|Lack of solidarity; a state of disunity or division among a group of people who should be united.  It often implies a failure to support or stand together in a common cause. |The nonsolidarity among the union members led to the failure of the strike. 工会成员之间的缺乏团结导致罢工失败。  The increasing nonsolidarity within the political party threatened its ability to govern effectively.  该政党内部日益增长的分裂威胁到其有效执政的能力。|solidarity, unity, cohesion|discord, disunity, fragmentation|名词
