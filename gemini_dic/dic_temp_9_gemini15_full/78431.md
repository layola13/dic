get-to:/ɡɛt tuː/ | v. | 到达；开始；着手；有机会 | To arrive at a place or situation; to begin to do something; to have the opportunity to do something. | 例句：We finally got to the beach after a long drive.  我们经过长时间的驾车后终于到达了海滩。
We got to talking about old times. 我们开始聊起往事。


例句：I got to meet the author at the book signing. 我有机会在签售会上见到作者。


近义词：reach, arrive at, begin, start, have the chance to | 反义词：leave, depart from, finish, end, miss the chance to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get to work early every day. = 我每天都早早去上班。
They get to see their grandparents every week. = 他们每周都能去看望他们的祖父母。

一般过去时:
We got to the airport on time. = 我们准时到达了机场。
He got to know her during the project. = 他在项目期间认识了她。

现在完成时:
I've gotten to know a lot of people through my work. = 我通过工作认识了很多的人。
She's finally gotten to travel to Europe. = 她终于有机会去欧洲旅行了。

过去完成时:
We had gotten to the theater before the show started. = 我们在演出开始前就到达了剧院。
They had gotten to understand the problem before proposing a solution. = 他们在提出解决方案之前已经理解了这个问题。

将来时:
We will get to the meeting as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快赶到会议。
She will get to experience a new culture in her travels. = 她将在旅行中体验一种新的文化。

虚拟语气:
If I got to choose, I would go to Paris. = 如果我能选择，我会去巴黎。
I wish I got to see the Northern Lights. = 我希望我能看到北极光。

被动语态:
The message got to be delivered late. = 消息被延迟送达了。
The problem got to be addressed immediately. = 这个问题必须立即得到解决。

疑问句:
Do you get to use the new software? = 你能使用新的软件吗？
When did you get to know him? = 你是什么时候认识他的？

否定句:
I didn't get to finish my work today. = 我今天没完成我的工作。
They didn't get to attend the conference. = 他们没有参加会议。


PHRASE->
get to know = 认识；了解
get to work = 开始工作
get to the point = 谈重点
get to like = 开始喜欢
get to the bottom of = 找出...的真相
