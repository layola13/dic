prerecitals:/ˌpriːrɪˈsaɪtəlz/| n. |预演，预告演出 |Preliminary performances or rehearsals, often given before a main performance to test or refine the production.  These are typically smaller-scale events than the main performance, serving as a dress rehearsal or a chance to get feedback. |Example: The orchestra held several prerecitals before their highly anticipated concert. 乐队在他们备受期待的音乐会之前举行了几次预演。|近义词：rehearsals, previews, dress rehearsals |反义词：final performance, premiere
