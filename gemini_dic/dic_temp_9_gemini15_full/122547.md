modelize: /ˈmɒdəlaɪz/ | /ˈmɑːdəlaɪz/ | vt. | 建模，模拟 | To create a model of something, typically a system or process, to represent it in a simplified or abstract form.  This often involves using computer software or mathematical equations. | 例句：The engineers modelized the bridge's behavior under stress. =工程师模拟了桥梁在压力下的行为。

例句：Scientists modelized the spread of the virus to predict future outbreaks. = 科学家模拟了病毒的传播以预测未来的疫情爆发。


近义词：model, simulate, represent, emulate

反义词：deconstruct, analyze (in the sense of breaking down rather than simplifying)

词性：动词
