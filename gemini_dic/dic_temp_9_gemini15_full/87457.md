hesperidin:/ˈhɛspərɪdɪn/| n. |橙皮苷；维生素P|A flavanone glycoside found in citrus fruits, particularly in the peel and pulp.  It's known for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties and is sometimes used as a dietary supplement.| Example: Hesperidin is a bioactive compound found abundantly in citrus fruits. 橙皮苷是一种在柑橘类水果中大量存在的生物活性化合物。|近义词：bioflavonoid|反义词：无

词源:
From the Greek word "Hesperides," referring to the mythical garden of the Hesperides, where golden apples grew.  The citrus fruits, rich in hesperidin, were associated with this legendary garden.  The "-idin" suffix indicates a glycoside.
