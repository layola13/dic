kinglet:/ˈkɪŋlɛt/| n. |小型王鹟；小国王|A small, active songbird of the family Regulidae, characterized by a distinctive crown patch.  Kinglets are known for their bright colours and energetic foraging behaviour. |Example:  The tiny kinglet flitted among the branches, searching for insects.  这只小小的王鹟在树枝间飞舞，寻找昆虫。|近义词：small bird, wren|反义词：eagle, large bird
