intangibles: /ɪnˈtænʒəbəlz/ | n. | 无形资产；难以捉摸的事物 |  Things that cannot be physically touched or easily measured, often relating to value or qualities.  Intangibles are often crucial to a company's success, but are difficult to quantify on a balance sheet. | 例句：Company culture and brand reputation are valuable intangibles. 公司文化和品牌声誉是宝贵的无形资产。

The strong team spirit contributed significantly to the company's success; it was a crucial intangible. 强大的团队精神极大地促进了公司的成功；这是一个至关重要的无形资产。


近义词：assets, qualities, attributes, goodwill, intellectual property | 反义词：tangibles, material assets
