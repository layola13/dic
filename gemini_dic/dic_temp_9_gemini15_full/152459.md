pay-back:/ˈpeɪbæk/ | n. v. | 报答；报复；偿还 |

英文解析:As a noun, payback refers to an act of revenge or retaliation, or the repayment of a debt or favor. As a verb, it means to retaliate, repay, or get even with someone.

例句 (Noun):
The payback for his betrayal was swift and brutal. = 他背叛的报应迅速而残酷。

The payback on the investment was substantial. = 这项投资的回报相当可观。


例句 (Verb):
I will payback the money I owe you next week. = 我下周会还你欠你的钱。

He vowed to payback his enemies for their treachery. = 他发誓要报复敌人的背叛。


近义词: (Noun) retribution, revenge, repayment, recompense; (Verb) retaliate, repay, avenge, recompense

反义词: (Noun) forgiveness, pardon; (Verb) forgive, pardon


词性: Noun, Verb
