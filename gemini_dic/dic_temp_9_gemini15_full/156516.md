picea:/ˈpiːsiə/| n. |云杉属；云杉|A genus of evergreen coniferous trees belonging to the pine family (Pinaceae), characterized by their conical shape, needle-like leaves, and woody cones.  They are widely distributed across the Northern Hemisphere. |Example:  The forest was dominated by towering picea trees. 这片森林主要由高耸的云杉树构成。|近义词：spruce (a specific type of picea)|反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑阔叶树(broadleaf tree)作为对比|名词
