shaming:/ˈʃeɪmɪŋ/| adj. n. | 羞辱；使蒙羞的；可耻的 | causing someone to feel ashamed and humiliated;  the act of causing someone to feel shame and humiliation. |例句：The public shaming of the politician damaged his reputation. 公开羞辱这位政治家损害了他的名誉。  The shaming tactics used by the company were unethical. 公司使用的羞辱策略是不道德的。|近义词：humiliation, disgrace, embarrassment, ignominy |反义词：honor, praise, glorification |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher shames the student for cheating. = 老师因作弊而羞辱学生。
This action shames our family. = 这件事使我们家蒙羞。


一般过去时:
She shamed him in front of everyone. = 她当着所有人的面羞辱了他。
The scandal shamed the entire organization.  = 这场丑闻使整个组织蒙羞。


现在完成时:
They have shamed themselves with their actions. = 他们的行为使他们自己蒙羞。
His behavior has shamed his family. = 他的行为使他的家人蒙羞。


过去完成时:
He had shamed his parents by his irresponsible behavior. = 他不负责任的行为使他的父母蒙羞。
She had shamed herself before she apologized. = 在道歉之前，她已经使自己蒙羞了。


将来时:
This will shame us all if we don't act. = 如果我们不采取行动，这将使我们所有人蒙羞。
Their actions will shame the company. = 他们的行为将使公司蒙羞。


虚拟语气:
If I did that, I would shame myself. = 如果我那样做了，我会使自己蒙羞。
I wish I hadn't shamed my friends. = 我真希望我没有羞辱我的朋友们。


被动语态:
He was shamed by his own mistakes. = 他因自己的错误而蒙羞。
The company was shamed by the media report. = 公司因媒体报道而蒙羞。


疑问句:
Does this action shame you? = 这个行为让你感到羞愧吗？
Will this decision shame the company? = 这个决定会让公司蒙羞吗？


否定句:
He didn't shame himself. = 他没有使自己蒙羞。
I wouldn't shame my family like that. = 我不会那样让我家蒙羞。


PHRASE->
public shaming 公开羞辱

body shaming 羞辱身材
cyber shaming 网络羞辱
victim shaming 受害者羞辱
