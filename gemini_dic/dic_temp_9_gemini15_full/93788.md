immensurate: /ɪˈmɛnʃərət/ | adj. |无法衡量的；不相称的；巨大的 |  incapable of being measured; disproportionate; vast.  | The benefits of the program are immeasurable.  该项目的益处是无法衡量的。|  immeasurable, boundless, infinite | measurable, limited, finite | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The task is immense and immeasurable. = 这项任务巨大且无法衡量。
His contribution to the project is immeasurable. = 他对项目的贡献是无法衡量的。


一般过去时:
The damage caused by the storm was immeasurable. = 暴风雨造成的损害是无法估量的。
Her kindness was immeasurable. = 她的善良是无法衡量的。


现在完成时:
The impact of her work has been immeasurable. = 她工作的冲击力是无法估量的。
His influence on the community has been immeasurable. = 他对社区的影响是无法估量的。


过去完成时:
The damage to the city had been immeasurable after the earthquake. = 地震过后，城市受到的破坏是无法估量的。
The losses incurred by the company had been immeasurable. = 公司遭受的损失是无法估量的。


将来时:
The potential benefits of this technology will be immeasurable. = 这项技术的潜在好处将是无法估量的。
The consequences of this decision will be immeasurable. = 这个决定的后果将是无法估量的。


虚拟语气:
If the benefits were immeasurable, we would continue the project. = 如果好处是无法估量的，我们将继续该项目。
I wish the support for my research were immeasurable. = 我希望对我的研究的支持是无限的。


被动语态:

The impact of the new policy is being described as immeasurable. = 新政策的影响被描述为无法估量的。


疑问句:
Is the effect of this medication immeasurable? = 这种药物的效果无法衡量吗？

否定句:
The task, while difficult, was not immeasurable. = 这项任务虽然困难，但并非无法衡量。

PHRASE->

词源:
The word "immensurate" comes from the Latin prefix "im-" (meaning "not") and "mensurare" (meaning "to measure").  Therefore, it literally means "not measurable".
