overrank:/ˌoʊvərˈræŋk/ | vt. |等级高于；职位高于；胜过|To surpass in rank or position; to exceed in importance or influence.|The general overranked the colonel.  将军的军衔高于上校。|近义词：outshine, outstrip, surpass |反义词：subordinate, be inferior to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overranks his colleagues in terms of experience. = 就经验而言，他的职位高于他的同事。
The new policy overranks all previous regulations. = 新政策凌驾于所有先前的规章制度之上。


一般过去时:
She overranked her opponent in the chess tournament. = 在国际象棋锦标赛中，她胜过了对手。
The CEO overranked the marketing manager's proposal. = 首席执行官否决了市场经理的提案。


现在完成时:
His contributions have overranked all others in the project. = 他对项目的贡献已经超过了所有其他人。
The company's new product has overranked its competitors in sales. = 公司的新产品在销售额上已经超过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the war, his experience had overranked everyone else's. = 到战争结束时，他的经验已经超过了其他所有人。
The evidence presented had overranked the initial assumptions. = 提出的证据已经超过了最初的假设。


将来时:
The upcoming launch will overrank all previous product releases. = 即将来临的发布将超过所有之前的产品发布。
His qualifications will overrank those of the other applicants. = 他的资历将超过其他申请人的资历。


虚拟语气:
If he had more experience, he would overrank his peers. = 如果他有更多经验，他就会超过他的同龄人。
If the project were successful, it would overrank all others in innovation. = 如果项目成功，它将在创新方面胜过所有其他项目。


被动语态:
His achievements were overranked by the media. = 他的成就被媒体夸大了。(Note:  While grammatically correct,  "overranked" in passive voice is less common and sounds slightly unnatural.  A better phrasing would likely be used.)
The proposal was dismissed because it was overranked by the budget constraints. = 该提案被驳回，因为它被预算限制所超越。


疑问句:
Does his seniority overrank the team leader's authority? = 他的资历是否超过了团队领导的权威？
Does the new software overrank the existing systems? = 新软件是否优于现有系统？


否定句:
His performance did not overrank his colleagues'. = 他的表现并没有超过他的同事。
The proposal did not overrank the other submissions. = 该提案并没有超过其他提交的提案。
