counter-motion:/ˈkaʊntərˈmoʊʃən/| n. |反动议，反对动议 |A counter-motion is a motion made in response to another motion, typically to oppose or amend it.  It's a formal proposal offered in a meeting or legal proceeding to challenge the original motion. |Example:  The board member proposed a counter-motion to the initial budget proposal, suggesting a more conservative spending plan.  董事会成员对最初的预算提案提出了反动议，建议制定更保守的支出计划。|近义词：counterproposal, opposition motion, amendment |反义词：original motion, supporting motion
