/ˈpɛtrəl/| n. |海燕，海燕属鸟类 |A petrel is a small to medium-sized seabird belonging to the family Procellariidae.  They are known for their graceful flight and ability to glide over the ocean for long periods. |Example: The storm petrel skimmed the waves, its tiny feet barely touching the water.  暴风雨中的海燕掠过波浪，它的小脚几乎不触及水面。|近义词：seabird |反义词：无
