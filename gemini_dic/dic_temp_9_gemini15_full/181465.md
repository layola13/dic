rejoicing:/rɪˈdʒɔɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 庆祝；欢庆；喜悦 | The act of expressing great joy; celebration; a feeling of great happiness. |例句：There was much rejoicing at the news of the victory.  胜利的消息传来，人们一片欢腾。|例句：The city was filled with rejoicing after the team won the championship. 球队赢得冠军后，城市里充满了欢庆的气氛。|近义词：celebration, jubilation, merriment |反义词：mourning, sorrow, grief | 名词
