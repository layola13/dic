friederich:/ˈfriːdərɪk/| Proper Noun|弗里德里希|A German given name, equivalent to the English "Frederick". It is derived from the Old High German name "Fridurih," composed of the elements "fridu" meaning "peace" and "rihhi" meaning "powerful" or "ruler".  The name has a long and distinguished history, associated with numerous rulers and notable figures throughout German history. |例句：Friedrich Nietzsche was a highly influential German philosopher.  弗里德里希·尼采是一位极具影响力的德国哲学家。|近义词：Frederick, Friedrich Wilhelm|反义词：N/A
