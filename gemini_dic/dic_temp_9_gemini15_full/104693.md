/kəˈzæk/| n. |哥萨克人；哥萨克服装| A member of any of various Cossack peoples of southern Russia and Ukraine, traditionally skilled horsemen and warriors.  Also, a type of long, close-fitting, typically sleeveless jacket. |例句：The Cossacks were renowned for their horsemanship and military prowess. 哥萨克人以其骑术和军事实力而闻名。

例句：She wore a stylish kasack, a type of jacket that was fashionable during that era. 她穿着一件时尚的哥萨克服装，这是一种在那个时代流行的夹克。

近义词： hussar (similar military context), jacket (general clothing)
