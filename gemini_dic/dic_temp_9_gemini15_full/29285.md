cast-off:/kæstˈɔːf/| adj., n., v.|抛弃的；废弃物；抛弃；解雇|discarded; something discarded; to discard; to dismiss|The cast-off clothes were donated to charity.  = 那些被抛弃的衣服捐给了慈善机构。|The old fishing net was a cast-off from a larger vessel. = 那张旧渔网是从一艘更大的船上抛弃下来的。|abandoned, discarded, rejected|treasured, kept, valued|adj., n., v.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He casts off his old shoes every year. = 他每年都扔掉旧鞋子。
She casts off her worries when she's with her friends. = 和朋友在一起时，她会抛开烦恼。


一般过去时:
The captain cast off the mooring lines. = 船长解开了系泊缆绳。
They cast off the old furniture. = 他们扔掉了旧家具。


现在完成时:
He has cast off his old habits. = 他已经改掉了旧习惯。
She has cast off the shackles of her past. = 她已经摆脱了过去的束缚。


过去完成时:
The ship had cast off before the storm hit. = 船在暴风雨来临之前就已经起航了。
They had cast off their responsibilities. = 他们已经摆脱了他们的责任。


将来时:
We will cast off the ropes at dawn. = 我们将在黎明解开绳索。
She will cast off her inhibitions at the party. = 她将在派对上放下拘束。


虚拟语气:
If he cast off his pride, he might find happiness. = 如果他放下骄傲，他可能会找到幸福。
I wish she had cast off her fears earlier. = 我希望她早点放下恐惧。


被动语态:
The old building was cast off by the company. = 这座旧建筑被公司抛弃了。
The old traditions were cast off in favor of new ones. = 旧传统被抛弃，取而代之的是新的传统。


疑问句:
Will they cast off the anchor soon? = 他们很快就会起锚吗？
Has she cast off her engagement ring? = 她摘下订婚戒指了吗？


否定句:
He did not cast off his responsibilities. = 他没有逃避责任。
She will not cast off her friends. = 她不会抛弃她的朋友们。
