overcivilly:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪvɪli/| adj. |过分客气地，过于文雅地 |Excessively polite or refined, often to the point of being insincere or affected.  It suggests an artificiality or unnaturalness in the politeness.|例句：He greeted her overcivilly, making her uncomfortable. 他过分客气地向她打招呼，让她感到不舒服。


例句：She spoke in an overcivil tone, which felt disingenuous. 她说话的语气过于文雅，让人感觉不真诚。


近义词：excessively polite, overly courteous, affectedly polite, insincerely polite


反义词：rude, impolite, blunt, discourteous


词性：形容词
