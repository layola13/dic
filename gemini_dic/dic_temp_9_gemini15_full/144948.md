overabstemiously: /ˌoʊvəræbˈstiːmiəsli/ | adv. | 过分节制地；极其克制地 | Excessively abstinent or temperate; to an extreme degree of self-denial. |  He lived overabstemiously, neglecting his health in his pursuit of ascetic ideals. 他过分节制，为了追求禁欲的理想而忽视了自己的健康。| 近义词：extremely temperately, excessively abstinently | 反义词：debauchedly, intemperately

词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "excessively" or "too much"
* **abstemious**:  adjective meaning "sparing or moderate in eating and drinking; temperate."  This in turn comes from Latin *abstemius* meaning "abstaining."  This is derived from *abs* "away" + *temetum* "wine."  The original sense was abstaining from wine.
