overhearers:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪrərz/| n. |偷听者，无意听到者 |People who accidentally or intentionally listen to a private conversation without the speakers' knowledge or consent.  They may be present nearby or using electronic devices to listen in. |例句：The overhearers exchanged knowing glances.  偷听者交换了心照不宣的眼神。

例句：The disgruntled employees were worried about being overheard by the overhearers. 不满的员工担心被偷听者听到。


近义词：eavesdroppers, listeners, bystanders (if unintentional)


反义词：speakers, participants (of the conversation)

词性: 名词
