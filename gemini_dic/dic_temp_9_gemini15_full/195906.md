sensibilities: /ˌsɛnsəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 敏感性；感觉；情趣 |  The capacity to feel, understand, and appreciate subtle differences or nuances; refined or delicate feelings.  It often refers to a person's emotional, moral, or aesthetic sensitivities.  |


例句：

Her artistic sensibilities were evident in her carefully chosen decor. = 她精挑细选的装饰体现了她高雅的艺术鉴赏力。

He lacked the sensibilities to understand the gravity of the situation. = 他缺乏理解形势严重性的敏感性。


近义词：feelings, sensitivities, perceptions, susceptibilities


反义词：insensitivity, callousness, apathy


词性：名词
