conjunctivitis:/ˌkɒndʒʌŋktɪˈvaɪtɪs/|/ˌkɑːndʒʌŋktɪˈvaɪtɪs/| n. |结膜炎|Inflammation of the conjunctiva, the membrane that lines the inside of the eyelids and covers the sclera (white part) of the eye.  It can be caused by bacterial or viral infections, allergies, or irritants. Symptoms include redness, itching, burning, and discharge. |例句：He suffered from conjunctivitis after swimming in a polluted lake. 他在污染的湖里游泳后患上了结膜炎。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with allergic conjunctivitis. 医生诊断她患有过敏性结膜炎。


近义词：pinkeye (informal)


反义词：None (It's a disease, not a state that has a direct opposite.)


词性：名词
