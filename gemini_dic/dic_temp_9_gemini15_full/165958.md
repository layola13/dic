preparator:/ˌprɛpəˈreɪtər/ | n. | 制备者；标本制作人 | A person who prepares specimens for display in a museum or other institution.  They often work in museums and handle artifacts, fossils, and other collections. | The museum hired a skilled preparator to restore the ancient artifacts.  博物馆聘请了一位技艺精湛的标本制作人来修复古代文物。 | curator, technician, conservator |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用如 destroyer) | 名词
