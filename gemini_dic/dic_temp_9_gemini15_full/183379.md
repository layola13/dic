reprogrammer:/ˌriːˈprəʊɡræmər/ | n. |重新编程者，程序再编写者 | A person who rewrites or modifies existing computer programs.  |Example: The company hired a reprogrammer to update their outdated software.  这家公司雇佣了一位程序再编写者来更新他们过时的软件。 |近义词：programmer, coder, software developer |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who only uses existing software.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewrites the code regularly. = 他定期重写代码。
She reprograms the robot's movements. = 她重新编程机器人的动作。


一般过去时:
They reprogrammed the old system last year. = 他们去年重新编程了旧系统。
The engineer rewrote the faulty script. = 工程师重写了有故障的脚本。


现在完成时:
We have reprogrammed the software several times. = 我们已经多次重新编程了该软件。

过去完成时:

The team had re-written the code before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期前已经重写了代码。


将来时:
