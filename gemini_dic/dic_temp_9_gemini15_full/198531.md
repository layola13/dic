shroud:/ʃraʊd/| n. v.|裹尸布；遮蔽物；掩盖；遮蔽|A shroud is a piece of cloth used to wrap a dead body for burial.  It can also refer to something that covers or obscures something else.|例句：The body was found shrouded in a white sheet. 尸体被裹在一张白单里。

The fog shrouded the mountain in mystery.  雾气笼罩着山峦，使之显得神秘莫测。|近义词：sheet, veil, cover, conceal|反义词：reveal, uncover, expose|名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The shroud covers the coffin. = 裹尸布盖着棺材。
The fog shrouds the valley. = 雾笼罩着山谷。

一般过去时:
They shrouded the body in a white cloth. = 他们用白布裹尸。
The darkness shrouded the city. = 黑暗笼罩着城市。

现在完成时:
The mystery has been shrouded in secrecy for years. = 这个谜团多年来一直被秘而不宣。
The evidence has been shrouded in doubt. = 证据一直存在疑问。

过去完成时:
The truth had been shrouded in mystery until the discovery. = 直到发现之前，真相一直是个谜。
The ancient city had long been shrouded by jungle. = 古城早已被丛林掩盖。

将来时:
The ceremony will shroud the event in solemnity. = 典礼将使这一事件庄严肃穆。
The upcoming election will shroud the country in uncertainty. = 即将来临的选举将使全国笼罩在不确定性中。


虚拟语气:
If the truth were not shrouded in secrecy, we would know more. = 如果真相没有被秘而不宣，我们会知道更多。
I wish my future were not shrouded in uncertainty. = 我希望我的未来不会充满不确定性。

被动语态:
The body was shrouded in a simple linen cloth. = 尸体被简单的亚麻布裹着。
The mountain peak was shrouded in mist. = 山峰被雾气笼罩。

疑问句:
Is the mountain shrouded in cloud today? = 山今天被云雾笼罩了吗？
Is the truth shrouded in mystery? = 真相被掩盖了吗？

否定句:
The truth was not shrouded in mystery. = 真相并没有被掩盖。
The evidence was not shrouded in doubt. = 证据没有疑问。
