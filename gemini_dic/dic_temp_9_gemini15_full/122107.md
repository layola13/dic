mistle:/ˈmɪs(ə)l/| n.|槲寄生|A parasitic plant with white berries, often hung in houses at Christmas.  It is traditionally associated with kissing under the mistletoe.|例句：We hung some mistletoe above the doorway for the holiday season. 我们在门口上方挂了一些槲寄生来庆祝节日。|例句：The mistletoe's white berries are a festive symbol. 槲寄生的白色浆果是节日的一个象征。|近义词：parasite|反义词：None.
