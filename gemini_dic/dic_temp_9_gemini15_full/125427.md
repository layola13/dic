multiphotons: /ˌmʌltiˈfoʊtɑːnz/ | n. |多光子 |  The absorption or emission of more than one photon simultaneously in a physical process.  This phenomenon is fundamental to multiphoton microscopy and other nonlinear optical techniques. | Example:  Multiphoton excitation microscopy allows for high-resolution 3D imaging of biological samples. = 多光子激发显微镜允许对生物样品进行高分辨率的三维成像。  |近义词: multiple photons | 反义词: single photon
