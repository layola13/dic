misborn: /ˈmɪsbɔːrn/ | adj. | 出生有缺陷的，畸形的 |  Born with a physical defect or deformity.  The term often implies a condition present at birth that affects the individual's health or well-being.  It is not typically used to describe minor birthmarks or imperfections. | 例句：The misborn foal was unable to walk properly.  这匹出生有缺陷的小马驹无法正常行走。


The child was misborn with a severe heart defect. 这个孩子出生时患有严重的先天性心脏病。


近义词：deformed, malformed, defective, imperfect | 反义词：healthy, normal, perfectly formed |  词性: adjective
