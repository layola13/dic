journaled:/ˈdʒɜːrnəld/| v. |记录，写日记|To record something in a journal or diary; to keep a journal.|例句：I journaled my thoughts and feelings every day. 我每天都记录我的想法和感受。|近义词：recorded, logged, documented|反义词：ignored, neglected|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She journals her daily experiences. = 她记录她的日常经历。
He journals his thoughts and feelings. = 他记录他的想法和感受。

一般过去时:
I journaled my trip to Europe. = 我记录了我的欧洲之旅。
They journaled their progress on the project. = 他们记录了项目进展。

现在完成时:
She has journaled regularly for five years. = 她定期写日记五年了。
He has journaled his entire life story. = 他已经记录了他一生的故事。

过去完成时:
I had journaled about the incident before the meeting. = 我在会议前已经记录了关于这次事件的事情。
They had journaled extensively before publishing their findings. = 他们在发表研究结果之前做了大量的记录。

将来时:
I will journal my experiences in Africa. = 我将记录我在非洲的经历。
She will journal the entire conference. = 她将记录整个会议。

虚拟语气:
If I had journaled more frequently, I would have a better record. = 如果我更频繁地写日记，我的记录会更好。
I wish I had journaled my childhood memories. = 我希望我曾经记录过我的童年记忆。

被动语态:
The events were journaled meticulously. = 这些事件被一丝不苟地记录下来。
The meeting was journaled by the secretary. = 会议由秘书记录。

疑问句:
Did you journal your thoughts after the interview? = 面试后你记录你的想法了吗？
Has she journaled her feelings about the situation? = 她记录了她对这种情况的感受了吗？

否定句:
I didn't journal yesterday. = 我昨天没有写日记。
He doesn't journal often. = 他不经常写日记。

PHRASE->
journal daily = 每天写日记
journal regularly = 定期写日记
journal meticulously = 细致地记录
journal honestly = 诚实地记录
journal privately = 私下记录
