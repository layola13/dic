give the lie to: /ɡɪv ðə laɪ tuː/ | v. | 证明…是假的；揭露…的虚伪；驳斥 | To prove something to be false or untrue; to expose the falsity or hypocrisy of something. |


例句：

His confident pronouncements gave the lie to his actual nervousness.  他的自信言论掩盖了他内心的紧张。

The recent economic figures give the lie to the government's claims of prosperity. 最近的经济数据驳斥了政府关于繁荣的声明。


近义词：contradict, refute, disprove, belie

反义词：confirm, corroborate, verify

词性：动词 (verb)
