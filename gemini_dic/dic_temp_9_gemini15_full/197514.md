shelve:/ʃɛlv/| vt.|搁置；把…放在架子上|to put something on a shelf or shelves|例句：I shelved the books carefully.我把书小心地放在书架上。|例句：We decided to shelve the project for now.我们决定暂时搁置这个项目。|近义词：put away, store, postpone|反义词：retrieve, resume


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shelves his books every night. = 他每天晚上都把书放回书架上。
She shelves the new inventory. = 她把新到的存货放到货架上。

一般过去时:
They shelved the old furniture. = 他们把旧家具搁置起来。
He shelved the project until next year. = 他把项目搁置到明年。

现在完成时:
She has shelved all her old toys. = 她已经把所有旧玩具都收起来了。
We have shelved that idea for the time being. = 我们暂时搁置了那个想法。

过去完成时:
They had shelved the proposal before the meeting. = 会议之前他们已经搁置了提议。
She had shelved her career ambitions to raise her children. = 她为了抚养孩子而搁置了她的职业抱负。

将来时:
They will shelve the plan if the funding is insufficient. = 如果资金不足，他们将搁置该计划。
He will shelve the books after he finishes reading them. = 他看完书后会把书放回书架上。

虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I would shelve all my books. = 如果我有更多空间，我会把所有书都放在书架上。
I wish I could shelve my worries. = 我希望我能把我的烦恼抛诸脑后。

被动语态:
The books were shelved neatly. = 书被整齐地放在书架上。
The project was shelved indefinitely. = 该项目被无限期搁置。

疑问句:
Where do you shelve your coats? = 你把外套放在哪里？
Have they shelved the controversial bill? = 他们是否搁置了那项有争议的法案？

否定句:
He didn't shelve the documents. = 他没有把文件归档。
They won't shelve their plans easily. = 他们不会轻易放弃他们的计划。


PHRASE->
shelve a plan = 搁置计划
shelve a project = 搁置项目
shelve a decision = 搁置决定
shelve a proposal = 搁置提议
shelve an idea = 搁置一个主意
