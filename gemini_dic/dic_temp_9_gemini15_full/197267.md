sharpen:/ˈʃɑːrpən/ | vt. | 磨尖；使敏捷；使清晰 | to make something sharper or more effective | He sharpened his knife before carving the meat. 他在切肉之前磨了刀。 The experience sharpened her mind.  这段经历使她头脑更加敏捷。|近义词：hone, refine, enhance | 反义词：blunt, dull


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sharpens his pencils every day. = 他每天都磨他的铅笔。
She sharpens her skills through practice. = 她通过练习来磨练自己的技能。

一般过去时:
I sharpened my axe before chopping wood. = 我在砍柴之前磨了我的斧头。
The teacher sharpened the students' focus on the topic. = 老师使学生们更专注于这个话题。

现在完成时:
He has sharpened his knife for the carving competition. = 他已经为雕刻比赛磨好了刀。
She has sharpened her presentation skills significantly. = 她已经显著地提高了她的演讲技巧。

过去完成时:
I had sharpened my pencils before the exam. = 我在考试前已经磨好了铅笔。

将来时:
I will sharpen my skills in coding next year. = 我明年将提高我的编码技能。

虚拟语气:
