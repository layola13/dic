canvasers:/kænˈvæsərz/| n.|挨家挨户推销的人；游说者|People who go from door to door to try to sell something or to get support for a cause.|The canvasers collected signatures for the petition.  挨家挨户推销的人收集请愿书的签名。|The political party sent out its canvasers to talk to voters.  政党派出了游说者去和选民交谈。|solicitors, canvassers, campaigners|---


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The canvasers canvass the neighborhood every week. = 这些挨家挨户推销的人每周都会挨家挨户地推销。
They canvas for votes before the election. = 他们在选举前拉选票。


一般过去时:
The canvasers canvassed the entire town. = 这些挨家挨户推销的人走遍了整个城镇。
They canvassed for donations for the charity. = 他们为慈善机构募捐。


现在完成时:
The team has canvassed most of the city. = 团队已经走访了这座城市的大部分地区。
They have canvassed extensively for the new product. = 他们为新产品进行了广泛的宣传。


过去完成时:
Before the election, they had canvassed every household. = 在选举之前，他们已经走访了每家每户。
They had canvassed the area before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经勘察过该地区。


将来时:
The company will canvas the market for new ideas. = 公司将调查市场以寻找新想法。

虚拟语气:
If they canvassed more effectively, they would get more support. = 如果他们更有效地游说，他们将获得更多支持。
