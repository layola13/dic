invoice:/ˈɪnvɔɪs/| n. |发票；账单 |A commercial document issued by a seller to a buyer, indicating the products, quantities, and agreed prices.  It serves as a request for payment. |例句：Please send me the invoice for the goods I ordered. 请寄给我我订购货物的发票。|例句：The invoice shows a total of $1,000. 发票显示总计1000美元。|近义词：bill, statement, account |反义词：receipt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invoices clients weekly. = 他每周给客户开具发票。
The company invoices customers for services rendered. = 公司向客户开具已提供服务的账单。

一般过去时:
She invoiced the client yesterday. = 她昨天给客户开了发票。
They invoiced the wrong amount for the project. = 他们为该项目开具了错误的金额发票。

现在完成时:
I have invoiced all the clients this month. = 我这个月已经给所有客户开了发票。
She has not yet invoiced him for the repairs. = 她还没有为他修理工作开具发票。

过去完成时:
We had invoiced them before the deadline. = 我们在截止日期前已经给他们开了发票。
They had already invoiced the client for the previous month's work. = 他们已经为上个月的工作向客户开具了发票。


将来时:
I will invoice you next week. = 我下周会给你开票。
They will invoice the customer upon completion of the project. = 项目完成后，他们将向客户开具发票。

虚拟语气:
If I had invoiced him earlier, I would have received the payment by now. = 如果我早点给他开票，我现在就收到付款了。
It would be better if we invoiced them separately. = 如果我们分别给他们开票会更好。

被动语态:
The client was invoiced for the damages. = 客户被开具了损坏赔偿的发票。
Invoices are generated automatically by the system. = 发票由系统自动生成。

疑问句:
Have you invoiced the customer yet? = 你已经给客户开票了吗？
When will the invoice be sent? = 发票什么时候发送？

否定句:
I haven't invoiced him yet. = 我还没给他开票。
They didn't invoice us for the extra work. = 他们没有因为额外工作向我们开票。


PHRASE->
raise an invoice = 开具发票
issue an invoice = 开具发票
send an invoice = 发送发票
pay an invoice = 支付发票
process an invoice = 处理发票

词源:
The word "invoice" comes from the Old French word "envoier," meaning "to send."  Over time, it evolved to refer to the act of sending a bill or statement of account.
