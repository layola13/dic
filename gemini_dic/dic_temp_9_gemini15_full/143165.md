outchiding:/aʊtˈtʃaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 斥责的；责骂的 |  Describing the act of scolding or reprimanding someone severely.  This word is rare and largely obsolete, found primarily in older literature. | Example:  The outchiding of the unruly children echoed through the hall.  = 不守规矩的孩子们受到了严厉的斥责，他们的声音回荡在走廊里。| 近义词：rebuking, scolding, reprimanding, upbraiding | 反义词：praising, commending, applauding | 形容词
