cariopsis:/kæriˈɑːpsɪs/| n. |颖果；谷粒|A caryopsis is a type of simple dry fruit in which the seed coat is fused to the ovary wall.  It is characteristic of grasses (Poaceae) and is commonly known as a grain.  The term is often used interchangeably with "grain," although technically, "grain" can refer to other types of dry fruits.  |Example: Wheat, rice, and corn all produce caryopses. 小麦、水稻和玉米都产生颖果。|近义词：grain, kernel|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with fruits having separate seed coats and ovary walls.|名词
