fungologist:/ˌfʌnˈdʒɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |真菌学家|A scientist who studies fungi (mushrooms, molds, yeasts, etc.).  They research the classification, genetics, biochemistry, and ecology of fungi. |例句：The renowned fungologist, Dr. Smith, discovered a new species of bioluminescent mushroom.  著名的真菌学家史密斯博士发现了一种新的生物发光蘑菇。

例句：Many fungologists are collaborating on a project to map the fungal diversity of the Amazon rainforest. 许多真菌学家正在合作开展一个项目，绘制亚马逊雨林真菌多样性的地图。


近义词：mycologist (although mycologist is a more general term encompassing the study of all fungi, while fungologist might sometimes be used more specifically for those who focus on macroscopic fungi like mushrooms) |反义词：无
