predictivity: /ˌprɛdɪkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |预测能力；可预测性 |The ability to predict future outcomes or events.  It refers to the extent to which a model, system, or method can accurately forecast future occurrences. This can be based on past data, current trends, or other relevant information. |例句：The model showed high predictivity in forecasting next quarter's sales.  该模型在预测下季度销售额方面显示出很高的预测能力。

The study examined the predictivity of various economic indicators. 这项研究考察了各种经济指标的预测能力。


近义词：forecasting ability, predictive power, predictability | 反义词：unpredictability, randomness

词性：名词
