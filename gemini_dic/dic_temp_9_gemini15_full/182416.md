renewable: /rɪˈnuːəbl/ | adj. | 可再生的；可更新的 | Able to be replaced by natural processes; capable of being renewed.  Renewable resources are those that replenish naturally over a relatively short period of time.  | 例句：Solar and wind power are renewable energy sources.  太阳能和风能是可再生能源。

例句：We need to invest more in renewable technologies to combat climate change. 我们需要在可再生技术上投入更多资金以应对气候变化。


近义词：sustainable, replaceable, replenishable | 反义词：nonrenewable, exhaustible, irreplaceable


词源:  The word "renewable" comes from the verb "renew," meaning "to make new or fresh again."  "Renew" itself originates from Old French *renouveler,  from re- ("again") + *nouveler ("to make new"), ultimately from Latin novellus ("new"). The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
