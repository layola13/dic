disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/| vt.|不赞成；反对|To have or express an unfavorable opinion of; to disagree with or object to.|例句：My parents disapproved of my boyfriend.  我的父母不赞成我的男朋友。

例句：The committee disapproved the proposal. 委员会否决了该提案。

近义词：object to, oppose, condemn, disfavor

反义词：approve, endorse, sanction, support

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disapproves of smoking. 他不赞成吸烟。
She disapproves of my lifestyle. 她不赞成我的生活方式。

一般过去时:
They disapproved of her decision. 他们不赞成她的决定。
The teacher disapproved of the student's behavior. 老师不赞成学生的行径。

现在完成时:
My parents have disapproved of my choice of career since I was young.  我的父母从小就不赞成我的职业选择。
The council has disapproved of the building plans. 理事会否决了建筑计划。

过去完成时:
He had disapproved of the project from the start. 他从一开始就不赞成这个项目。
She had disapproved of his actions before he even told her.在他告诉她之前，她就已经不赞成他的所作所为了。

将来时:
I think they will disapprove of my marriage. 我认为他们不会赞成我的婚姻。
The board will disapprove of any changes to the current policy. 董事会将否决对现行政策的任何更改。

虚拟语气:
If he knew, he would disapprove. 如果他知道，他会反对。
I wish they didn't disapprove of my relationship. 我希望他们不要反对我的恋情。

被动语态:
His ideas were disapproved of by the board. 他的想法遭到董事会的反对。
The plan was disapproved of by the majority of the members.  该计划遭到大多数成员的反对。

疑问句:
Does she disapprove of our plans? 她不赞成我们的计划吗？
Do you disapprove of his methods? 你不赞成他的方法吗？

否定句:
I don't disapprove of his actions. 我不反对他的行为。
They didn't disapprove of the new policy.  他们不反对这项新政策。
