overdocumenting: /ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjʊˌmɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 过度记录；文件过多 | The act of providing excessive documentation, often to the point of being unhelpful or counterproductive.  It can lead to wasted time and resources, and can make it difficult to find the essential information. |  Example: Overdocumenting the project led to confusion rather than clarity.  项目过度记录导致混乱而非清晰。 The team was criticized for overdocumenting every minor detail. 团队因过度记录每一个细微之处而受到批评。| 近义词：overdetailing, excessive documentation | 反义词：underdocumenting, insufficient documentation
