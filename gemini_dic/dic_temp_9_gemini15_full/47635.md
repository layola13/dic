debilities:/dɪˈbɪlətiz/| n.|虚弱；衰弱；弱点 |Weaknesses or impairments, especially physical or mental ones.  Debilities can range from minor inconveniences to severe limitations affecting daily life.  |例句：The old man suffered from several debilitating debilites.  这位老人患有几种使人衰弱的疾病。

The prolonged illness left him with several debilites. 长期疾病使他留下了一些虚弱。


近义词：weaknesses, impairments, infirmities, disabilities |反义词：strengths, abilities, capabilities |词性：名词


词源:  From Middle English *debilite, from Old French *debilité, from Latin *debilitās, from *debilis ("weak").  The suffix "-ities" indicates a plural noun form representing a state or quality.
