levulose:/ˈlɛvjuˌloʊs/| n. |果糖；左旋糖|Fructose, a simple ketonic monosaccharide found in many plants, especially fruits. It is also known as fruit sugar and is a type of reducing sugar.| Example:  Honey contains a significant amount of levulose.  蜂蜜含有大量的果糖。|近义词：fructose, fruit sugar |反义词：glucose (in some contexts, as they are different types of sugars)
