reachers:/ˈriːtʃərz/| n. |到达者; 伸手的人; 探测器|People or things that reach or extend to a particular place or point.  It is a plural noun, derived from the verb "reach".|例句：The reachers of the summit were exhausted but triumphant. 山顶的到达者筋疲力尽，但凯旋而归。 The robotic reachers extended into the narrow crevice. 机械臂伸入狭窄的裂缝中。|近义词：extenders, explorers, probes|反义词：N/A
