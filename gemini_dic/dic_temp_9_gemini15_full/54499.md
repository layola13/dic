disinvestments:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛstmənts/ | n. | 撤资；减持投资 | The act of selling or liquidating investments;  a reduction in investment.  It often refers to a deliberate strategy to reduce holdings in a particular asset class or company. | 例句：The company announced significant disinvestments in the energy sector. = 公司宣布大幅减少对能源行业的投资。


The government's policy of disinvestments led to a decline in the stock market. = 政府的撤资政策导致股市下跌。


近义词：divestment, withdrawal of investment, liquidation of assets | 反义词：investment, capital injection
