disconnection:/dɪs.kəˈnek.ʃən/| n.|断开连接；脱节；疏远；中断|The state or an instance of being disconnected; a lack of connection or continuity.  It can refer to physical disconnections (like a power outage), mental disconnections (like a feeling of detachment), or social disconnections (like a breakdown in relationships).|例句：The sudden disconnection of the internet caused a disruption to our work. 互联网突然断开导致我们的工作中断。

例句：There's a growing disconnection between the government and the people. 政府和人民之间越来越脱节。


近义词：separation, severance, detachment, disruption, break, interruption|反义词：connection, linkage, continuity, unity, integration|词性:n.
