heredity:/hɪˈrɛdɪti/| n. |遗传；遗传性 |The transmission of genetic characteristics from parents to offspring.  It encompasses the passing down of traits, both physical and behavioral. |例句：Heredity plays a significant role in determining a person's height. 遗传在决定一个人的身高方面起着重要作用。

例句：The study of heredity helps us understand how characteristics are passed from one generation to the next.  遗传学研究帮助我们了解特征是如何一代一代遗传的。


近义词：inheritance, heritage, transmission |反义词：acquired, learned
