noncorruptive: /ˌnɒnkəˈrʌptɪv/ | adj. | 不腐败的，正直的 | Not corrupt; honest and ethical.  The adjective describes something or someone that is free from corruption or the tendency to corrupt.  It implies integrity, probity, and a commitment to ethical principles. |


例句：

The noncorruptive judge ruled fairly, despite immense pressure. =  这位正直的法官尽管面临巨大压力，仍然公正地判决。

The company prides itself on its noncorruptive business practices. = 这家公司以其正直的商业行为而自豪。


近义词：honest, upright, incorruptible, ethical, virtuous, just


反义词：corrupt, dishonest, unethical, immoral, crooked


词性：形容词
