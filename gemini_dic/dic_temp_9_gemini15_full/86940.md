himself:/hɪmˈsɛlf/ | pron. |他自己；他本人 | himself refers to a male person as the object of a verb or preposition, indicating that the action is performed by that person on himself.  It is a reflexive pronoun. | He hurt himself playing football. 他踢足球时伤了自己。| He completed the project all by himself. 他独自一人完成了这个项目。| oneself, himself (反义词较少，可考虑用 others, other people 等来表达与“他自己”相对的概念)| pronoun
