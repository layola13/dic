dynamometer:/daɪˈnæməˌmiːtər/ | n. |测力计，测功器 | An instrument for measuring force, torque, or power.  It's used in various applications, from measuring the force of a muscle contraction to testing the power output of an engine. | Example: The engineers used a dynamometer to test the engine's horsepower.  工程师们用测功器来测试发动机的马力。|近义词：force gauge, power meter |反义词：N/A
