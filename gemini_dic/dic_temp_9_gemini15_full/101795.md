irrigated:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | v. | 灌溉；冲洗 | To supply water to (land) artificially.  |例句：The farmer irrigated his fields with a sprinkler system.  农民用喷灌系统灌溉他的田地。|例句：The doctor irrigated the wound to clean it. 医生冲洗伤口以清洁它。|近义词：watered, flooded |反义词：drained, desiccated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They irrigate their crops regularly. = 他们定期灌溉庄稼。
The farmer irrigates his land using a canal system. = 农民用运河系统灌溉他的土地。

一般过去时:
The farmers irrigated their fields last week. = 农民上周灌溉了他们的田地。
We irrigated the garden after a long dry spell. = 经历了长时间的干旱后，我们灌溉了花园。

现在完成时:
They have irrigated the rice paddies twice this month. = 这个月他们已经灌溉了两次稻田。
The government has irrigated vast areas of desert land. = 政府已经灌溉了大片的沙漠土地。

过去完成时:
They had irrigated the fields before the drought hit. = 干旱来临之前，他们已经灌溉了田地。
The crops had been irrigated thoroughly before the harvest. = 收获前，庄稼已经得到了充分的灌溉。

将来时:
We will irrigate the lawn tomorrow. = 我们明天将要浇灌草坪。
The new irrigation system will irrigate the entire valley. = 新的灌溉系统将灌溉整个山谷。

虚拟语气:
If we had irrigated the crops earlier, they wouldn't have withered. = 如果我们早点灌溉庄稼，它们就不会枯萎了。
It is important that the fields be irrigated regularly. = 定期灌溉田地很重要。

被动语态:
The fields were irrigated using a sophisticated system. = 田地是用一套复杂的系统灌溉的。
The land is being irrigated by a new pumping station. = 这块土地正在由一个新的抽水站灌溉。

疑问句:

否定句:
