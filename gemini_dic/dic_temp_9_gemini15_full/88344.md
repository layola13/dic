hippogriff:/ˈhɪpəɡrɪf/| n. |狮鹫兽| A mythical creature with the body of a horse and the head and wings of an eagle.  Hippogriffs are often depicted as proud and majestic creatures. They are known for their intelligence and loyalty, but can also be dangerous if provoked. |Example:  The wizard rode a magnificent hippogriff across the valley.  骑着雄伟的狮鹫兽飞越山谷的巫师。|近义词: griffin (but griffins have a lion's body)|反义词:  N/A
