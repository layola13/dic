retool:/ˌriːˈtuːl/| vt.|改造，重新装备，改进|To change or modify something, especially a machine, process, or system, to make it more efficient or suitable for a new purpose.  |例句：The factory is being retooled to produce a new line of products. 工厂正在改造成产新产品线。|例句：The company decided to retool its marketing strategy after a series of unsuccessful campaigns. 公司在经历一系列失败的营销活动后决定调整其营销策略。|近义词：refurbish, revamp, modernize, overhaul|反义词：deteriorate, worsen|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company retools its production line every year. = 公司每年都会改造其生产线。
They retool their marketing strategy regularly. = 他们定期调整他们的营销策略。

一般过去时:
The factory retooled its equipment last year. = 工厂去年改造了它的设备。
We retooled the software to improve its functionality. = 我们重新设计了软件以提高其功能。

现在完成时:
The company has retooled its entire operation. = 公司已经对其整个运营进行了改造。
They have retooled their approach to customer service. = 他们已经调整了他们的客户服务方法。

过去完成时:
They had retooled the machinery before the new product launch. = 他们在新产品发布前已经改造了机器。
The team had retooled its presentation before the final meeting. = 团队在最后一次会议之前已经重新制作了他们的演示文稿。

将来时:
The plant will retool its production process next quarter. = 该工厂将在下个季度改造其生产流程。
We will retool the website to improve user experience. = 我们将重新设计网站以改善用户体验。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would retool the entire factory. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会改造整个工厂。
It would be beneficial if they retooled their management style. = 如果他们调整他们的管理风格，将会很有益。

被动语态:
The software is being retooled to fix the bugs. = 软件正在被重新设计以修复错误。
The production line was retooled to increase efficiency. = 生产线被改造以提高效率。

疑问句:
Will the company retool its outdated equipment? = 公司会改造其过时的设备吗？
Has the software been retooled since the last update? = 自上次更新以来，软件是否已被重新设计？

否定句:
The company did not retool its factory this year. = 公司今年没有改造其工厂。
They haven't retooled their marketing materials yet. = 他们还没有重新设计他们的营销材料。
