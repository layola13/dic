gratitude:/ˈɡrætɪtuːd/ | n. |感激之情，感谢；感恩 |The feeling of being grateful and showing appreciation.  It involves recognizing and appreciating the good things in one's life, often stemming from the actions of others. |例句：I expressed my gratitude for their help. 我表达了对他们帮助的感激之情。

例句：She felt a deep sense of gratitude towards her family. 她对家人怀有深深的感激之情。

近义词：thankfulness, appreciation, gratefulness

反义词：ingratitude
