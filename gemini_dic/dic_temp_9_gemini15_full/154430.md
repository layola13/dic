permittee:/pərˌmɪˈtiː/| n. |许可证持有者，被许可人|A person or entity granted permission to do something, often under a permit or license.  The permittee is responsible for adhering to the terms and conditions of the permit.|例句：The permittee must follow all regulations outlined in the permit.  被许可人必须遵守许可证中规定的所有规章制度。

The permittee was fined for violating the terms of their permit.  该被许可人因违反许可证条款而被处以罚款。|近义词：licensee, permit holder|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "violator" or "offender".|名词
