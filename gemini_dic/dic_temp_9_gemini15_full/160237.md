pontifical:/pɒnˈtɪfɪkəl/| adj.|教皇的；自以为是的；傲慢的；权威的|Of or relating to a pope or the papacy; relating to or characteristic of a pope or the papacy.  It can also mean pompous or arrogant, suggesting an unwarranted display of authority.|例句：The pontifical ceremony was attended by dignitaries from around the world. 教皇仪式吸引了来自世界各地的显要人物。

The pontifical pronouncements of the professor irritated some students. 教授自以为是的言论激怒了一些学生。|近义词：papal, ecclesiastical, authoritative, dogmatic, arrogant, pompous|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming
