cliffier:/ˈklɪfiər/| adj.|更陡峭的，更峭壁的|More steep and rocky;  having more cliffs.  It describes something that is more characterized by cliffs than something else.|The mountain range became increasingly cliffier as we ascended.|steeper, rockier|flatter, gentler


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The path is cliffier than I expected. = 这条路比我想象的更陡峭。
The coastline is cliffier in this area. = 这个区域的海岸线更陡峭。


一般过去时:
The trail became cliffier as we climbed higher. = 当我们爬得越高，小路就变得越陡峭。
The landscape was cliffier than we anticipated. = 风景比我们预期的更陡峭。
