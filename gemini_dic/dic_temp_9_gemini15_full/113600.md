macerater:/ˈmæsəreɪtər/| n. |捣碎机，浸软器 |A macerator is a device that breaks down solid waste into a slurry for easier disposal, typically used in plumbing systems to handle waste from toilets or sinks.  It uses rotating blades or other mechanisms to grind and pulverize the waste. |例句：The macerator in the bathroom is making a strange noise.  浴室里的捣碎机发出奇怪的声音。

The kitchen sink is fitted with a macerator to handle food waste. 厨房水槽安装了捣碎机来处理食物垃圾。|近义词：disintegrator, pulper |反义词：aggregator
