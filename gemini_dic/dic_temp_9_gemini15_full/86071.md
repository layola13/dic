hearingless: /ˈhɪərɪŋləs/ | adj. | 听不见的，聋的 | Lacking the sense of hearing; deaf.  |  The hearingless child communicated through sign language.  听不见的孩子通过手语交流。| deaf, stone-deaf | hearing, able to hear


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It describes a state of being.  Therefore, tense and voice are not applicable in the same way as with verbs.  Examples will show its use in different sentence structures.


Simple Present Tense:
The hearingless man uses a hearing aid.  聋人使用助听器。
She is hearingless and relies on lip reading. 她听不见，依靠读唇。


Simple Past Tense:
He was hearingless from birth. 他天生聋。
The doctor confirmed that the child was hearingless. 医生证实孩子听不见。


Present Perfect Tense:
She has been hearingless since childhood. 她从小就听不见了。
They have always been understanding of his hearingless condition. 他们一直都很理解他的听力障碍。


Future Tense:
He will likely remain hearingless. 他很可能仍然听不见。
The new technology may help those who are hearingless. 新技术可以帮助听不见的人。


Conditional Sentences:
If he were hearingless, he would use sign language. 如果他听不见，他会用手语。
She would be better off if she had been diagnosed as hearingless earlier. 如果她能早点被诊断为听不见，情况会更好。


Passive Voice:
The hearingless community is often overlooked. 听不见的群体常常被忽视。
His hearingless condition was not discovered until later. 他的听力障碍直到后来才被发现。


Interrogative Sentences:
Is he hearingless? 他聋吗？
Are there any supports available for the hearingless?  有没有为听不见的人提供支持？


Negative Sentences:
He is not hearingless; he just has a hearing impairment. 他不是聋的；他只是听力受损。
She is not completely hearingless, but her hearing is severely impaired. 她不是完全听不见，但她的听力严重受损。


PHRASE->
There are not common phrases directly using "hearingless" as it is an adjective describing a state.  Instead, phrases would describe the condition or experiences of hearingless people.  Here are some examples using related terms:


hearing impaired: 听力受损
hard of hearing:  听力障碍
loss of hearing: 听力丧失
deaf community: 聋人社群
sign language interpreter: 手语翻译
