plod:/plɒd/ /plɑːd/| v.| 沉重地走；（缓慢而费力地）工作；|To walk heavily and slowly; to work slowly and steadily but without inspiration.|He plodded through the mud. 他在泥泞中艰难地行走。|She plodded away at her homework. 她埋头苦做家庭作业。| trudge, lumber, slog | sprint, dash | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plods along the road every day. = 他每天都在路上缓慢地行走。
They plod through their work. = 他们埋头苦干。


一般过去时:
She plodded home in the rain. = 她冒雨慢慢地走回家。
He plodded through the lengthy report. = 他艰难地完成了冗长的报告。


现在完成时:
I have plodded through this project for weeks. = 我已经在这个项目上辛勤工作了几周。
They have plodded along, making slow but steady progress. = 他们一直在缓慢但稳定地进步。


过去完成时:
She had plodded for hours before she reached the summit. = 她走了好几个小时才到达山顶。
He had plodded through numerous obstacles before achieving success. = 在获得成功之前，他克服了许多障碍。


将来时:
I will plod on, regardless of the difficulties. = 不管遇到什么困难，我都会继续努力。
They will plod through the paperwork tomorrow. = 他们明天将处理文书工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be so difficult, I would not have plodded on. = 如果我知道这么困难，我就不会坚持下去了。
I wish I hadn't plodded through that boring book. = 我真希望我没有读那本无聊的书。


被动语态:
The work was plodded through by the team. = 这项工作由团队成员缓慢地完成了。
The long journey was plodded through in the cold. = 漫长的旅程在寒冷中缓慢地完成了。


疑问句:
Does he always plod along like that? = 他总是这样缓慢地走吗？
Will they plod through the entire report today? = 他们今天会完成整个报告吗？


否定句:
He didn't plod through the assignment; he finished it quickly. = 他没有缓慢地完成作业；他很快就完成了。
They won't plod along indefinitely; they need a better strategy. = 他们不会无限期地缓慢前进；他们需要一个更好的策略。
