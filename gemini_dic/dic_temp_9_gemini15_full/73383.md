forehead:/ˈfɔːrhed/| n. |前额，额头 |The forehead is the part of the face above the eyes and below the hairline.  It is often considered a prominent feature of the face. |例句：He wiped the sweat from his forehead. 他擦去了额头上的汗水。

例句：She had a high forehead. 她额头很高。


近义词：brow


反义词：无


词性：名词
