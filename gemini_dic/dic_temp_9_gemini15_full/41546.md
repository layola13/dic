coronium:/kəˈroʊniəm/| n. |假想元素，日冕物质|A hypothetical element that was once believed to exist in the sun's corona.  Scientists in the late 19th and early 20th centuries thought they had detected spectral lines indicating a new element in the sun's corona, which they named coronium. However, later research showed these lines were due to known elements under unusual conditions. |Example:  The discovery of coronium was a significant event in early astrophysics, although it was later shown to be an erroneous conclusion.  =  日冕物质的发现是早期天体物理学中的一个重要事件，尽管后来证明这是一个错误的结论。 |近义词：N/A|反义词：N/A |名词
