reigniting: /ˌriːˈaɪtnaɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新点燃；使复燃；重新激起 | To ignite again; to rekindle; to reawaken or revive interest or activity. |

例句：
The news reignited the debate about gun control. = 这则新闻重新燃起了关于枪支管制的争论。

The success of their latest album reignited their fans' enthusiasm. = 他们最新专辑的成功重新激起了歌迷的热情。


近义词：rekindle, re-ignite, revive, renew, revitalize

反义词：extinguish, quench, suppress
