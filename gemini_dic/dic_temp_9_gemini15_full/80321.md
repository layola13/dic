golden:/ˈɡoʊldən/| adj.|金色的，金制的，宝贵的，美好的 |  Of the color of gold; shining like gold.  Possessing or suggesting great value or excellence.|例句：The sunset painted the sky in golden hues.  日落给天空涂上了金色的色彩。

The golden rule is to treat others as you want to be treated.  黄金法则就是己所不欲，勿施于人。


近义词：gold, gilded, precious, valuable, excellent

反义词：dull, worthless, base, poor
