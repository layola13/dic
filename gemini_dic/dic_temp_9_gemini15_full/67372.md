extensor:/ɪkˈstensər/| n.|伸肌，伸展肌|A muscle that extends or straightens a limb or body part.  It increases the angle between the bones of a joint.|例句：The extensor muscles in your leg help you straighten your knee.  你腿部的伸肌帮助你伸直膝盖。

例句：The extensor carpi radialis longus is a muscle in the forearm.  桡侧腕长伸肌是前臂的一块肌肉。


近义词：extender,straightener|反义词：flexor
