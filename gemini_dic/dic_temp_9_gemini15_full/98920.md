intentionalities:/ɪnˌtɛnʃəˈnælɪtiz/ | n. |意图性；意向性 |The plural of "intentionality," referring to the property of mental states of being directed at or about something.  It often involves the study of consciousness and how our minds engage with the world. | Example: The artist's intentionalities were evident in the meticulous detail of the painting.  艺术家的意图性在绘画的细致细节中显而易见。 The multiple intentionalities behind the political speech were hard to decipher.  政治演讲背后的多重意图很难解读。|近义词：purposes, aims, goals, objectives |反义词：accidentality, randomness, chance |名词
