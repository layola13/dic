ptolemy:/ˈtɒləmi/ /ˈtɑːləmi/| n. |托勒密；托勒密的著作|  A Greek astronomer, mathematician, and geographer of the 2nd century CE, whose geocentric model of the universe was widely accepted for centuries.  His major work, the Almagest, summarized and expanded upon the astronomical knowledge of his time.  The word also refers to his writings and system of astronomy.| Example: Ptolemy's geocentric model placed the Earth at the center of the universe. 托勒密的地球中心说将地球置于宇宙中心。  The influence of Ptolemy on astronomy was immense. 托勒密对天文学的影响是巨大的。|近义词：astronomer, geographer, mathematician |反义词：无
