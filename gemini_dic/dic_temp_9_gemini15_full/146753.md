/ˌoʊvərˈhʌmblnəsɪz/| n. |过分谦逊；过度卑微|Excessive humility; an exaggerated display of modesty.  It often suggests a false or affected humility, rather than genuine modesty. The word implies an excess to the point of being almost self-deprecating or embarrassing. |The overhumbleness of his apologies made me uncomfortable.  他的过度谦卑的道歉让我感到不舒服。|近义词: excessive modesty, false humility, self-deprecation|反义词: arrogance, boastfulness, conceit|名词
