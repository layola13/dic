febricitate:/ˌfebriːˈsɪteɪt/| vt.|发烧；使发烧|To cause to have a fever; to become feverish.|The illness febricitated him for days.  疾病使他发烧了好几天。 The high fever febricitated the patient. 高烧使病人发热。|近义词：fever, overheat|反义词：cool down, alleviate
