cleading:/ˈkliːdɪŋ/| adj.|引导的；领先的；主要的|Leading refers to being at the head or forefront of something, guiding or directing, or being most important or prominent.  It can describe a person, an idea, or a thing.|例句：The leading candidate in the election is expected to win.  选举中领先的候选人预计会获胜。

例句：This company is a leading manufacturer of computer chips. 这家公司是电脑芯片的主要制造商。

近义词：principal, foremost, chief, primary, dominant

反义词：trailing, lagging, last, minor, insignificant
