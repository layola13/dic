pellicle:/ˈpɛlɪkəl/| n.|薄膜；表皮|A thin skin or membrane.  It often refers to a very thin, delicate film on a surface, such as the film on the surface of a liquid or the outer layer of an organism.|例句：A pellicle formed on the surface of the liquid. 液体表面形成了一层薄膜。|例句：The fruit had a thin, delicate pellicle. 水果上有一层薄而细致的表皮。|近义词：film, membrane, skin|反义词：thick layer, bulk
