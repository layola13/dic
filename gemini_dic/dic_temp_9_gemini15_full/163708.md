prededuction:/ˌpriːdɪˈdʌkʃən/| n.|预扣；预先扣除|The act of deducting something before it is due or before the usual time.  It often refers to a deduction from income or payment before it is received. |Example: The company implemented a prededuction of taxes from employee salaries. 公司实行从员工工资中预扣税款。|近义词：prepayment, advance deduction|反义词：postdeduction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company prededucts taxes from employee salaries. = 公司从员工工资中预扣税款。
He prededucts his expenses before submitting his report. = 他在提交报告之前预先扣除他的费用。


一般过去时:
The employer prededucted the insurance premiums last month. = 上个月雇主预扣了保险费。
They prededucted the cost of materials from the final payment. = 他们从最终付款中预扣了材料成本。


现在完成时:
The government has prededucted a significant portion of the tax already. = 政府已经预扣了一大部分税款。
We have prededucted the transportation fees from the total cost. = 我们已经从总成本中预扣了交通费。


过去完成时:
The accountant had prededucted all the applicable expenses before preparing the financial statement. = 会计在准备财务报表之前已经预扣了所有适用的费用。
They had prededucted the service charge before calculating the final bill. = 他们计算最终账单之前已经预扣了服务费。


将来时:
The company will prededuct the employee contributions to the pension plan. = 公司将预扣员工对养老金计划的缴款。
They will prededuct the cost of shipping from the invoice amount. = 他们将从发票金额中预扣运输成本。


虚拟语气:
If they prededucted the fees, the final cost would be lower. = 如果他们预扣费用，最终成本将会降低。
I wish they had prededucted the taxes earlier. = 我希望他们早点预扣税款。


被动语态:
Taxes are prededucted from the employee's paycheck. = 税款从员工的工资中预扣。
The shipping fee was prededucted from the payment. = 运费已从付款中预扣。


疑问句:
Is the tax prededucted from the gross income? = 税款是从总收入中预扣的吗？
Will the membership fee be prededucted? = 会员费将会预扣吗？


否定句:
They do not prededuct the VAT from the invoice. = 他们不会从发票中预扣增值税。
The bank did not prededuct any fees from the transaction. = 银行没有从交易中预扣任何费用。
