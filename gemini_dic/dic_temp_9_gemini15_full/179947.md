references:/ˈrɛfrənsɪz/| n. |参考文献；参考书目；证明人；提及；|英文解析:Something that provides information or evidence about something else.  A reference can be a book, article, or other source of information, or a person who can vouch for someone's character or abilities.|例句：The essay includes numerous references to classical literature. 这篇文章包含许多关于古典文学的参考文献。

The professor will provide references for further reading. 教授会提供进一步阅读的参考文献。
