locomotives:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪvz/| n. |机车；火车头|A locomotive is a powered rail vehicle used for hauling trains.  It is typically found at the front of a train and provides the motive power for its movement.  Locomotives can be powered by various means, including steam, diesel, or electricity.|例句：The powerful locomotive pulled the long freight train across the plains. 这台强大的机车拉着长长的货运列车穿过平原。

The old steam locomotive was a museum piece. 这台古老的蒸汽机车成了博物馆的展品。|近义词：engine, train engine|反义词：None specifically, but words implying lack of movement or power could be considered antonyms in context.  |词性 n.
