extemporizing:/ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 即兴创作；临时发挥 |  To speak or perform without preparation or notes; to improvise.  | He was extemporizing on the topic of climate change, weaving together various points without a pre-written script. 他在即兴演讲气候变化的主题，没有预先写好的稿子，将各种观点交织在一起。 | improvising, ad-libbing |  preplanning, scripting |
