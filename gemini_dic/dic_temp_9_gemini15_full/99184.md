/ˌɪntərˌsɜːrkjuˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |循环流通；相互循环 |The study of intercirculations focuses on the complex interactions and flows of various elements within a system.  This could refer to the circulation of blood, air, water, information, or even ideas. For example, the global intercirculation of capital is a key factor in the world economy.  |例句：The research paper details the intercirculations of nutrients within the ecosystem.  = 这篇研究论文详细阐述了生态系统中养分的循环流通。 The model simulates the intercirculations of air masses across the globe. = 该模型模拟了全球范围内气团的相互循环。|近义词：circulation, flow, exchange, interplay |反义词：stagnation, standstill, isolation
