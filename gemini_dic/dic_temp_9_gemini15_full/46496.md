dairies:/ˈdɛəriːz/| n. |乳制品；日记；农场|Plural of dairy.  Dairies refer to places where milk and milk products are processed and/or sold, or to personal journals. |例句：Many dairies produce cheese, yogurt, and butter.  许多乳制品厂生产奶酪、酸奶和黄油。|例句：She kept a daily diary of her travels. 她每天都写日记记录她的旅行。|近义词：farms (for production), journals (for personal writings)|反义词：N/A
