performer:/pərˈfɔːrmər/| n. |表演者，演出者 |A person who performs, especially in a show or play.  Someone who gives a performance. |例句：The performer captivated the audience with their amazing skills.  这位表演者以其精湛的技艺迷住了观众。|例句：She is a talented performer known for her expressive singing. 她是一位以富有表现力的歌声而闻名的才华横溢的表演者。|近义词：artist, actor, actress, musician, entertainer |反义词：audience, spectator
