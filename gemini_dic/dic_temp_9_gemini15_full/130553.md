nonacquiescence:/ˌnɒnækwɪˈɛs(ə)ns/| n. |不顺从；不同意；不服从|The refusal to accept or comply with something.  It implies a deliberate and active rejection, rather than passive resistance. |例句：Their nonacquiescence to the new rules led to a heated debate. 他们对新规的不服从导致了一场激烈的辩论。
Their nonacquiescence in the matter was quite surprising. 他们在此事上的不顺从令人相当惊讶。|近义词：dissent, defiance, disobedience, rejection, opposition |反义词：acquiescence, compliance, obedience, acceptance
