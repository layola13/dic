initiate:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ | vt.  | 开始；发起；介绍；使初步了解 | to cause something to begin | The company initiated a new marketing campaign.  这家公司发起了一场新的营销活动。| begin, commence, start, launch | terminate, end, conclude | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He initiates new projects every year. = 他每年都启动新的项目。
She initiates conversations with strangers easily. = 她很容易就能和陌生人搭讪。


一般过去时:
They initiated the peace talks last month. = 他们上个月启动了和谈。
The teacher initiated a discussion on the topic. = 老师就这个话题展开了讨论。


现在完成时:
We have initiated several changes in the company. = 我们已经在公司启动了几项变革。
She has initiated contact with the potential client. = 她已经与潜在客户取得了联系。


过去完成时:
They had initiated the process before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们就已经启动了这个流程。
He had initiated the conversation, but she didn't respond. = 他已经开始对话了，但她没有回应。


将来时:
We will initiate the project next week. = 我们下周将启动该项目。
The government will initiate new environmental policies. = 政府将启动新的环保政策。


虚拟语气:
If he initiated the project, it would be successful. = 如果他启动这个项目，它将会成功。
I wish he had initiated the conversation sooner. = 我希望他早点开始对话。


被动语态:
The program was initiated by the government. = 该项目是由政府启动的。
The changes were initiated to improve efficiency. = 这些改变是为了提高效率而启动的。


疑问句:
Who initiated the conflict? = 谁发起了冲突？
When will they initiate the next phase? = 他们什么时候启动下一阶段？


否定句:
He did not initiate any action. = 他没有采取任何行动。
They haven't initiated the plan yet. = 他们还没有启动计划。


PHRASE->
initiate a conversation: 开始对话
initiate a lawsuit: 提起诉讼
initiate a project: 启动项目
initiate contact: 建立联系
initiate a discussion: 开始讨论


词源:
源自拉丁语initiāre，意为“开始，进入”。  initiāre本身来自initium，意思是“开始，开端”。
