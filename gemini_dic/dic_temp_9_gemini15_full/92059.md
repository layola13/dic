hyperproduction:/ˌhaɪpərprəˈdʌkʃən/| n. |过度生产；产量过剩|The excessive production of something, often beyond what is needed or can be consumed.  This can lead to waste, price drops, and economic instability.  |例句：The factory's hyperproduction of widgets led to a significant drop in their price. 工厂对小部件的过度生产导致其价格大幅下降。|例句：The government's policies led to hyperproduction in the agricultural sector, resulting in food waste. 政府的政策导致农业部门产量过剩，造成粮食浪费。|近义词：overproduction, surplus production, excessive production|反义词：underproduction, scarcity, shortage|名词
