implicating:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v.|牵连；暗示；表明；含蓄地表达|To suggest or indirectly express something. To show someone to be involved in a crime or wrongdoing.|例句：The evidence implicates him in the robbery.  证据表明他参与了抢劫案。

例句：His silence implicated his guilt. 他的沉默暗示了他的罪行。


近义词：involving, suggesting, implying, indicating|反义词：exonerating, clearing, absolving|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence implicates the suspect in the crime. = 证据牵连嫌疑犯参与了犯罪。
This statement implicates a larger conspiracy. = 这份声明暗示了一个更大的阴谋。


一般过去时:
The witness implicated the defendant in the fraud. = 证人牵连被告参与了欺诈。
The letter implicated the politician in a scandal. = 这封信将这位政治家牵涉到了一场丑闻中。


现在完成时:
The investigation has implicated several high-ranking officials. = 调查已经牵涉到几位高级官员。
The media has implicated the company in unethical practices. = 媒体已经将该公司牵涉到不道德行为中。


过去完成时:
The police had implicated the driver in the hit-and-run accident. = 警方已经将司机牵涉到这起肇事逃逸事故中。
It had been implicated that the CEO knew about the illegal activities. =  人们已经暗示首席执行官知道这些非法活动。


将来时:
The report will implicate more people in the corruption scandal. = 这份报告将牵涉更多的人到腐败丑闻中。
The upcoming trial will likely implicate several key figures. =  即将到来的审判可能会牵涉到几个关键人物。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence implicated him, he would be arrested. = 如果证据牵连他，他就会被逮捕。
I wish the investigation hadn't implicated my friend. = 我希望调查没有牵涉到我的朋友。


被动语态:
He was implicated in the murder. = 他被牵涉到谋杀案中。
The company was implicated in a price-fixing scheme. = 这家公司被牵涉到一个价格操纵计划中。


疑问句:
Does this evidence implicate anyone else? = 这证据是否牵涉到其他人？
What exactly does this statement implicate? = 这份声明到底暗示了什么？


否定句:
The investigation did not implicate the CEO. = 调查没有牵涉到首席执行官。
The report failed to implicate the mayor in the wrongdoing. = 报告未能将市长牵涉到不当行为中。


PHRASE->

directly implicated = 直接牵涉
