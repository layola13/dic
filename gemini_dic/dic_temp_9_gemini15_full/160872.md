post-Carboniferous:/ˌpoʊstˌkɑːrbənɪfərəs/| adj.|石炭纪以后的，后石炭纪的|Post-Carboniferous refers to the geological period following the Carboniferous period, encompassing the Permian, Triassic, Jurassic, Cretaceous, and subsequent periods.  It marks a significant shift in Earth's geology, climate, and biological life. |The post-Carboniferous era saw the rise of reptiles and the diversification of plant life.|近义词：Permian, Triassic, Jurassic, etc. (depending on the specific period)|反义词：Pre-Carboniferous|
