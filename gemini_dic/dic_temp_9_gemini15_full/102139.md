/ˈɪzreɪˌlaɪt/| n. |以色列人；以色列民族|A member of the ancient Hebrew people,  descendants of Jacob (also called Israel) and their descendants.  The term is used both historically and religiously to refer to the people of ancient Israel and their modern descendants.|The Israelites journeyed through the desert for forty years. 以色列人在沙漠中跋涉了四十年。|Jew, Hebrew|Gentile (反义词，指非以色列人)|名词
