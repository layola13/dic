myriopoda:/ˌmɪriˈɒpədə/| n. |多足类动物|Myriopoda is a subphylum of arthropods that includes millipedes, centipedes, and other similar creatures characterized by their numerous legs. They are primarily terrestrial invertebrates found in diverse habitats.  |Example:  The myriopod scuttled across the forest floor.  多足类动物在森林地面上快速爬行。|近义词:  arthropods (更广义)|反义词:  N/A
