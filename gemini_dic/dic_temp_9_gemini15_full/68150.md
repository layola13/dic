failest:/ˈfeɪlɪst/| v.|失败（第二人称单数现在时）|The second-person singular present tense form of the verb "to fail," indicating that you are failing or will fail.  It is an archaic or poetic form rarely used in modern English.|例句：Thou failest in thy duty. = 你没有尽到你的职责。(Thou is an archaic form of "you")
He failest to impress. = 他没能给人留下深刻印象。(Archaic usage)|近义词：falter, stumble,  underperform|反义词：succeed, triumph, excel

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I fail my exam. = 我考试不及格。
You fail to understand. = 你没有理解。


一般过去时:
I failed my driving test. = 我驾照考试不及格。
She failed to meet her deadline. = 她没有按时完成期限。


现在完成时:
I have failed many times. = 我失败过很多次。
He has failed to convince me. = 他没能说服我。


过去完成时:
I had failed to notice the sign. = 我没有注意到那个标志。
They had failed to prepare adequately. = 他们没有充分准备。


将来时:
I will fail if I don't study. = 如果我不学习，我会不及格。
She will fail to reach her goal. = 她将无法实现她的目标。


虚拟语气:
If I failed, I would try again. = 如果我失败了，我会再试一次。
I wish I hadn't failed. = 我真希望我没有失败。


被动语态:
The project was failed. = 项目失败了。 (Note: This usage is less common and often replaced with "The project failed.")
The attempt was failed. = 尝试失败了。(Note: This usage is less common and often replaced with "The attempt failed.")


疑问句:
Will I fail? = 我会失败吗？
Did you fail the test? = 你考试不及格了吗？


否定句:
I did not fail. = 我没有失败。
She will not fail. = 她不会失败。


PHRASE->
fail to:未能
fail in:在...方面失败
fail at: 在...方面失败
fail safe: 安全失效
fail-proof:万无一失的
