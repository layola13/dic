prediminution:/ˌpriːˌdɪmɪˈnuːʃən/| n. | 预先减少；先期缩减 | The act or process of diminishing something beforehand; a reduction in size, amount, or value that occurs before something else.  It often implies a planned or deliberate reduction, rather than a random or accidental one. | Example: The prediminution of the army's supplies led to shortages during the campaign. 预先减少军队的物资供应导致了战役期间的短缺。 | 近义词：reduction, decrease, lessening, diminution, abatement | 反义词：augmentation, increase, enhancement | 名词
