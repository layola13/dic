Emolliences refers to substances that soften or soothe the skin, often used in cosmetics and skincare products.  They work by creating a protective barrier on the skin's surface, preventing moisture loss and reducing irritation.  The term can also refer to the act of softening or soothing something, particularly in a figurative sense.
