mysticete:/ˈmɪstɪsiːt/| n. |齿鲸|A mysticete is a baleen whale, meaning it has baleen plates in its mouth instead of teeth, which it uses to filter feed on krill and other small organisms.  They are characterized by their lack of teeth and the presence of baleen plates.|例句：Humpback whales are a type of mysticete known for their acrobatic displays.|近义词：baleen whale|反义词：odontocete
