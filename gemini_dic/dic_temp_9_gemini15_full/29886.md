caustics:/ˈkɔːstɪks/| adj. n.|腐蚀性的；苛刻的；(复数)苛性物质|adj. capable of burning, destroying, or eating away by chemical action; severely critical or sarcastic. n. a caustic substance, especially an alkali. |例句：The acid is a caustic substance that can burn skin.  这种酸是一种腐蚀性物质，会灼伤皮肤。

例句：Her caustic remarks stung him deeply. 她尖刻的言辞深深刺痛了他。

近义词：corrosive, abrasive, biting, scathing|反义词：mild, gentle, soothing


词源:  From Late Latin *causticus, from Greek kaustikos "burning," from kaiein "to burn."
