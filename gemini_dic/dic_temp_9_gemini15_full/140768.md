omnivory:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːri/ /ˈɑːmnɪvɔːri/| n. |杂食性；杂食动物|The practice or characteristic of eating both plants and animals.  Omnivory is a common dietary strategy among many animal species, allowing them to adapt to varying food availability. |例句：The omnivory of bears allows them to thrive in diverse habitats. 熊的杂食性使它们能够在不同的栖息地繁荣发展。

例句：The study examined the impact of omnivory on ecosystem stability. 这项研究考察了杂食性对生态系统稳定性的影响。


近义词：generalist feeding, polyphagy|反义词：herbivory, carnivory


词源:  From omni- (all) + -vory (eating).  The suffix "-vory" is derived from the Latin word *vorare*, meaning "to devour" or "to eat".
