look-forward-to:/ˈlʊkˈfɔːwədtuː/ | phrasal verb | 期待，盼望 | This phrasal verb expresses anticipation and excitement for a future event.  It is followed by a noun or a gerund (verb + -ing). | 例句：I'm looking forward to the weekend. = 我期待着周末。

I look forward to hearing from you soon. = 我期待很快收到你的来信。


近义词：anticipate, expect, await | 反义词：dread, fear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I look forward to your reply. = 我期待你的回复。
We look forward to seeing you at the party. = 我们期待在派对上见到你。


一般过去时:
She looked forward to her vacation. = 她期待着她的假期。
They looked forward to the new year. = 他们期待着新年。


现在完成时:
I have been looking forward to this day for months. = 我已经期待这一天几个月了。
We have looked forward to this opportunity for a long time. = 我们已经期待这个机会很久了。


过去完成时:
I had looked forward to the concert all week. = 我盼望这场音乐会已经整整一周了。
They had looked forward to the trip for years. = 他们期盼这次旅行已经好几年了。


将来时:
I will look forward to your visit. = 我将期待你的来访。
We will be looking forward to the results. = 我们将期待着结果。


虚拟语气:
I would look forward to a vacation if I had more money. = 如果我有更多钱，我会期待一个假期。
She would have looked forward to the wedding if she had been invited. = 如果她被邀请的话，她会期待婚礼的。


被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Are you looking forward to the holidays? = 你期待假期吗？
Is he looking forward to his retirement? = 他期待退休吗？


否定句:
I'm not looking forward to the exam. = 我不期待考试。
They weren't looking forward to the long journey. = 他们不期待漫长的旅程。



PHRASE->
look forward to the future = 期待未来

look forward to seeing you again = 期待再次见到你
look forward to working with you = 期待与你合作
look forward to a better tomorrow = 期待更美好的明天
