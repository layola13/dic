manhead:/ˈmænhed/| n.|男子气概；男人；人头|The term "manhead" is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It refers to the qualities associated with a man, especially courage and strength.  It can also refer to a man's head, literally or figuratively.  The word is largely obsolete, replaced by more common synonyms.|例句：The legend speaks of a warrior of immense manhead. 传说中讲述了一个拥有巨大男子气概的战士。 The painting depicted the manhead of a king. 这幅画描绘了一个国王的头颅。|近义词：manliness, manhood, courage, bravery, head|反义词：cowardice, weakness, effeminacy|名词
