funds:/fʌndz/| n.|资金，基金|Money or capital that is available for use.|The company needs to secure more funds before it can expand.  这家公司需要在扩张之前获得更多资金。

The project is short of funds. 这个项目资金短缺。|money, capital, resources|debt, liabilities|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company has sufficient funds for the project. = 这家公司有足够的资金来进行这个项目。
We need to find more funds to cover the expenses. = 我们需要找到更多资金来支付费用。


一般过去时:
They received significant funds from investors last year. = 他们去年从投资者那里获得了大量资金。
The government allocated funds to the disaster relief effort. = 政府为救灾工作拨款。


现在完成时:
The organization has raised substantial funds for its charitable work. = 该组织为其慈善工作筹集了大量资金。
We have already secured the funds needed for the first phase of the project. = 我们已经为项目的第一个阶段获得了所需的资金。


过去完成时:
They had already exhausted their funds before the project was completed. = 在项目完成之前，他们就已经用光了资金。
The company had secured sufficient funds before starting the construction. = 公司在开始建设之前已经获得了足够的资金。


将来时:
We will seek additional funds to complete the research. = 我们将寻求额外资金来完成研究。
The project will be funded by a grant from the foundation. = 该项目将由基金会的资助。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funds, we could expand our business. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们就可以扩大我们的业务。
I wish we had sufficient funds to hire more staff. = 我希望我们有足够的资金来雇佣更多员工。


被动语态:
The project was funded by a private investor. = 该项目由一位私人投资者资助。
Funds were allocated to different departments based on their needs. = 基金根据不同部门的需求分配。


疑问句:
Where will you get the funds for the new equipment? = 你将从哪里获得新设备的资金？
Do we have enough funds to cover the unexpected costs? = 我们有足够的资金来支付意外费用吗？


否定句:
The company lacks the funds to invest in new technology. = 这家公司缺乏投资新技术的资金。
They did not receive the funds they had requested. = 他们没有收到他们申请的资金。
