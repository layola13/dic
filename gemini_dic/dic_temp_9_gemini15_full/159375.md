pokiest:/ˈpoʊkiɪst/| adj.|最慢的，最迟钝的，最笨拙的|The superlative form of "poky," meaning slow, sluggish, or awkward.  It describes something or someone that is exceptionally slow or clumsy.|例句：The pokiest snail won the race. 最慢的蜗牛赢得了比赛。 The pokiest student finished the test last.  最慢的学生最后完成了测试。|近义词：slowest, laziest, most sluggish, most cumbersome|反义词：fastest, quickest, most agile
