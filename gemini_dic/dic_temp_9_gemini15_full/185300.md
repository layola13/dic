retreats:/rɪˈtriːts/| n.|撤退；静修；隐退；避难所|Places of peace and quiet, often in nature, designed for relaxation, reflection, or spiritual renewal. They can be simple cabins or luxurious resorts.|例句：Many people find peace and quiet in mountain retreats. 许多人在山区静修中找到了平静与安宁。|例句：The army was forced to retreat after suffering heavy losses.  军队在遭受重大损失后被迫撤退。|近义词：withdrawal, refuge, sanctuary, hideaway|反义词：advance, attack, assault


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The yoga retreat starts next week. = 瑜伽静修营下周开始。
He regularly retreats to his cabin in the woods. = 他定期退回到他在树林里的木屋。


一般过去时:
The soldiers retreated after the battle. = 战后士兵们撤退了。
She retreated to her room to escape the noise. = 她退回到房间里以躲避噪音。


现在完成时:
The army has retreated from the city. = 军队已从城市撤退。
They have retreated to their respective positions. = 他们已经退回到各自的位置。


过去完成时:
The enemy had retreated before our arrival. = 我们到达之前敌人已经撤退了。
She had retreated into herself after the tragedy. = 悲剧发生后，她变得沉默寡言。


将来时:
We will retreat to the mountains for the weekend. = 我们周末将撤退到山里。
The general will retreat his troops to a safer location. = 将军将把他的部队撤退到一个更安全的地方。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would retreat from the argument. = 如果我是你，我会避免这场争论。
I wish I could retreat to a quiet island. = 我真希望我能退隐到一个安静的岛屿。


被动语态:
The troops were retreated to the base. = 部队被撤退到基地。
The plan was retreated due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，计划被取消了。


疑问句:
Will the army retreat? = 军队会撤退吗？
Where will they retreat to? = 他们会撤退到哪里？


否定句:
The soldiers did not retreat. = 士兵们没有撤退。
He refused to retreat from his position. = 他拒绝放弃自己的立场。
