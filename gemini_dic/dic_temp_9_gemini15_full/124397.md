motoneuron:/ˈmoʊtəʊˌnʊrɒn/ /ˈmoʊtərˈnʊrɑːn/| n. |运动神经元|A nerve cell forming part of a pathway along which impulses pass from the brain or spinal cord to a muscle or gland, resulting in muscular contraction or glandular secretion.|例句：Motoneurons transmit signals from the central nervous system to muscles, causing them to contract. 运动神经元将信号从中枢神经系统传递到肌肉，导致肌肉收缩。|例句：Damage to motoneurons can lead to muscle weakness or paralysis. 运动神经元受损会导致肌肉无力或瘫痪。|近义词：motor neuron, efferent neuron |反义词：sensory neuron
