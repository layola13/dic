konite:/ˈkɒnaɪt/| n. |柯奈石|A variety of opal that is usually dark-colored and contains inclusions of other minerals.  It is prized for its unique appearance and is often used in jewelry.|例句：The necklace featured a striking konite pendant, its dark hues flecked with shimmering inclusions.  这条项链饰有一枚引人注目的柯奈石吊坠，其深色调点缀着闪闪发光的内含物。|近义词：opal (general term)|反义词：None specifically|名词
