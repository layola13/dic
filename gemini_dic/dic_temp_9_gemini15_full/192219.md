The term emphasizes the difficulty of removing the weed due to its tenacity or prickly nature. | Example:  The farmer struggled to clear the field of scratchweed before planting his crops.  = 农民在播种前努力清除地里的杂草。 | 近义词：weed, unwanted plant, pest plant | 反义词：cultivated plant, desirable plant | 名词
