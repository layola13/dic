determination:/dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |决心；毅力；果断；确定；测定|the quality of being determined; firmness of purpose; resoluteness.  It also refers to the act of finding something out exactly; establishing something definitely.|例句：Her determination to succeed was admirable.  她的成功决心令人钦佩。

例句：The scientist's determination of the chemical composition was precise. 科学家对化学成分的测定是精确的。|近义词：resolve, perseverance, tenacity, decision, resolution |反义词：indecision, irresolution, weakness, doubt
