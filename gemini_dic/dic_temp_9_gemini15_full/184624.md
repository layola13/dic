restrapping:/riːˈstræpɪŋ/ | n. |重新捆扎；重新系紧；重新束缚|The act of restraining or tying something again;  the process of reapplying straps or other restraints.  It can also refer to figurative restraints or limitations.|例句：The restrapping of the cargo was necessary after the storm.  暴风雨过后，重新捆扎货物是必要的。

例句：The restrapping of his emotions prevented him from fully expressing his grief.  他抑制住自己的情绪，无法完全表达自己的悲伤。


近义词：restraining, refastening, resecuring, rebinding


反义词：unstrapping, loosening, releasing


词性:名词
