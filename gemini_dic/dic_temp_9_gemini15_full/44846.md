crustier:/ˈkrʌstiər/| adj.|更硬的；更粗糙的；更不友好的|More hard or rough;  less friendly or approachable.  The adjective "crustier" is the comparative form of "crusty," meaning having a hard, rough surface or a gruff, unfriendly manner.|例句：The bread is crustier today than yesterday. =今天的面包比昨天更硬。The old sailor had a crustier personality after years at sea. =这位老水手在海上待了多年后，性格变得更粗鲁了。|近义词：rougher, tougher, harder, gruffer |反义词：smoother, softer, gentler, friendlier
