outrebounds:/ˌaʊtˈraʊndz/| n. |篮板球（尤指进攻篮板球）|Offensive rebounds, also known as outrebounds, are rebounds gained by the offensive team after a missed shot.  They represent a second chance to score. |The team secured several key outrebounds in the final minutes.  球队在最后几分钟抢到了几个关键的进攻篮板。  |rebounds, offensive rebounds |  | n.
