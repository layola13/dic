confuted:/kənˈfjuːtɪd/ | adj. | 反驳的；驳斥的 | refuted or proved wrong;  disproved.  |  例句：His theory was confuted by new evidence. 他的理论被新的证据驳斥了。|近义词：refuted, disproved, rebutted | 反义词：confirmed, verified, substantiated | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence confutes his claims. = 证据驳斥了他的说法。
His arguments are confuted by facts. = 事实驳斥了他的论点。


一般过去时:
The scientist confuted the prevailing theory. = 科学家驳斥了流行的理论。
The judge confuted the defendant's alibi. = 法官驳斥了被告的不在场证明。


现在完成时:
New research has confuted earlier findings. = 新的研究已经驳斥了早期的发现。
Scientists have confuted the hypothesis. = 科学家们已经驳斥了这个假设。


过去完成时:
They had confuted the opposing arguments before the debate. = 在辩论之前，他们已经驳斥了对方的论点。
The lawyer had confuted the witness's testimony. = 律师已经驳斥了证人的证词。


将来时:
Further evidence will confute his conclusions. = 进一步的证据将驳斥他的结论。
The new data will likely confute the existing model. = 新数据可能会驳斥现有的模型。


虚拟语气:
If new evidence were found, it would confute the theory. = 如果找到新的证据，它将驳斥该理论。
I wish the facts would confute his misleading statements. = 我希望事实能驳斥他误导性的陈述。


被动语态:
His arguments were confuted by the opposing team. = 他的论点被对方团队驳斥了。
The theory was confuted by subsequent research. = 该理论被随后的研究驳斥了。


疑问句:
Was his argument confuted effectively? = 他的论点是否被有效地驳斥了？
Can this hypothesis be confuted by experiment? = 这个假设能通过实验来驳斥吗？


否定句:
The study did not confute the original hypothesis. = 这项研究并没有驳斥最初的假设。
His explanation was not confuted by any evidence. = 他的解释没有任何证据驳斥。
