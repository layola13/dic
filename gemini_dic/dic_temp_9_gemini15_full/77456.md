gay-marriage:/ɡeɪˈmærɪdʒ/ | n. | 同性婚姻 | The legal or social union between two people of the same sex.  This term encompasses various legal and social aspects, including the rights and responsibilities afforded to same-sex couples, and the ongoing debates and legal battles surrounding its acceptance and recognition globally. | Example:  The legalization of gay marriage was a landmark achievement for LGBTQ+ rights. 同性婚姻的合法化是LGBTQ+权利的一个里程碑式的成就。  The debate over gay marriage continues to be a divisive issue in many countries. 关于同性婚姻的辩论在许多国家仍然是一个有争议的问题。|近义词：same-sex marriage, same-sex union |反义词：heterosexual marriage, opposite-sex marriage
