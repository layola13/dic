flotilla:/fəˈtɪlə/| n. |小舰队，小船队 |A flotilla is a small fleet of ships or boats, often sailing together for a particular purpose.  |例句：A flotilla of fishing boats was heading out to sea. 一支渔船小舰队正驶向大海。

例句：The yacht club organized a flotilla to celebrate the town's anniversary.  游艇俱乐部组织了一支小船队来庆祝小镇的周年纪念。


近义词：fleet, squadron, armada


反义词：None (Antonyms are not readily applicable to this noun.  It's more about scale than opposition.)


词性:Noun
