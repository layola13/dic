outstudying: /ˌaʊtˈstʌdiɪŋ/ | v. |  超过……在学习上；比……学习更好 |  To surpass someone in academic achievement or study habits.  It implies a competitive context where one person outperforms another in their studies. | 例句：He outstudied all his classmates and got the highest score. 他在学习上超过了所有同学，取得了最高分。  She consistently outstudied her brother in their preparation for the exam. 在考试准备中，她一直比她哥哥学习得更好。| 近义词：outshine, outstrip, excel, surpass | 反义词：underperform, lag behind | 动词
