half-sightedly: /ˌhɑːfˈsaɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 半盲地；视力差地；目光短浅地 |  Acting or seeing with impaired vision; lacking foresight or long-range perspective.  | He looked half-sightedly at the approaching car. 他半盲地望着驶近的汽车。  She planned half-sightedly, only considering the immediate consequences. 她目光短浅地计划，只考虑了直接的后果。| short-sightedly, myopically | far-sightedly
