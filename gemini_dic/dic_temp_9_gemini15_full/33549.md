cigar:/sɪˈɡɑːr/| n. |雪茄|A cigar is a roll of finely cut, cured tobacco leaves, typically wrapped in a leaf of a different type.  It is smoked for pleasure. |He lit a large, expensive cigar. 他点燃了一支又大又贵的雪茄。|cigarette, cheroot|pipe, cigarette (less strong)|名词
