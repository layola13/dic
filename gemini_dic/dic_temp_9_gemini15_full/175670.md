ranunculaceous:/ˌrænʌŋkjuˈleɪʃəs/| adj. |毛茛科的；类似毛茛的| Belonging to or characteristic of the Ranunculaceae, a family of plants including buttercups, anemones, and delphiniums.  The term often describes the flower structure, which is typically radially symmetrical with numerous stamens and pistils. | Example:  Many ranunculaceous plants are poisonous.  许多毛茛科植物是有毒的。 The ranunculaceous flower had multiple petals and numerous stamens.  这朵毛茛科的花有许多花瓣和雄蕊。|近义词：buttercup-like (if referring to appearance)|反义词：无
