cross-pollinated:/ˌkrɒsˈpɒləneɪtɪd/| adj.|异花授粉的；杂交的 |  Describes plants whose flowers have been fertilized by pollen from a different plant of the same species, resulting in genetic diversity.  | Example: The farmer ensured a bountiful harvest by planting cross-pollinated varieties of apples.  农民通过种植异花授粉的苹果品种确保了丰收。 |近义词：hybridized, crossbred | 反义词：self-pollinated
