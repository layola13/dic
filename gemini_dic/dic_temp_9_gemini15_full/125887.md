muscovado:/ˌmʌskəˈvɑːdoʊ/| n. |粗糖，赤砂糖|Dark brown, unrefined cane sugar with a strong molasses flavor.  It is produced by evaporating sugarcane juice and contains a higher proportion of molasses than refined sugar.  |例句：The muscovado sugar added a rich, caramel-like flavor to the cake.|近义词：unrefined sugar, panela, jaggery|反义词：refined sugar, white sugar|名词
