compartition:/ˌkɒmpɑːˈtɪʃən/| n. |隔间；划分；区隔|The act of dividing something into compartments or sections; the state of being divided into compartments.  It often refers to the division of a space or resource into separate parts. |例句：The  compartition of the office space improved efficiency.  办公室空间的隔间提高了效率。
The careful compartition of the land ensured each family had a fair share.  土地的仔细划分确保每个家庭都有公平的份额。|近义词：division, partition, subdivision, compartmentalization |反义词：unification, integration, consolidation
