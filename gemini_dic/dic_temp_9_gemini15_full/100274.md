intersecant:/ɪntərˈsɛkənt/| adj.|相交的，截线的 | A line or plane that intersects another.  A line that cuts across another line or surface. |例句：The two lines are intersecant at point A. 这两条线在A点相交。 The transversal is an intersecant line. 横截线是一条相交线。|近义词：intersecting, crossing, cutting |反义词：parallel, disjoint
