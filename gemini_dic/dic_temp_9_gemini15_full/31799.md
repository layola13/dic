checkmarks:/ˈtʃɛkˌmɑːrks/ | n. |复数勾号，对号 |Marks used to indicate agreement, correctness, or completion.  They are often used in lists, forms, or tests to show that something has been reviewed or approved.|例句：Please put checkmarks next to the items you want to order. 请在您想订购的商品旁边打勾。|例句：The teacher used checkmarks to grade the students' assignments.老师用勾号来批改学生的作业。|近义词：ticks,  ✓ |反义词：crosses, Xs


USAGE->
This word is a noun and does not conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense variations do not apply.  The usage revolves around its function as a plural noun.


PHRASE->
place a checkmark = 打勾
put a checkmark next to = 在...旁边打勾
a list of checkmarks = 一系列勾号
multiple checkmarks = 多个勾号
checkmark all the boxes = 全部打勾
