megalosaurus:/ˌmɛɡəˈlɔːsərəs/| n. |巨型蜥蜴，巨齿龙|A genus of large carnivorous theropod dinosaurs from the Middle Jurassic period.  Megalosaurus was one of the first dinosaurs to be scientifically described.| Example:  The discovery of Megalosaurus bones revolutionized our understanding of prehistoric life. 巨齿龙骨骼的发现彻底改变了我们对史前生命的理解。|近义词：theropod (general term)|反义词：N/A
