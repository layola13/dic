leasee:/lɛˈsiː/ | n. | 承租人，承租方 | A person who leases property from a lessor;  the tenant.  | Example: The leasee failed to pay rent on time.  承租人没有按时付租金。 |近义词：tenant, renter | 反义词：lessor, landlord


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leasee pays rent monthly. = 承租人每月支付租金。
The leasee maintains the property in good condition. = 承租人保持房产状况良好。


一般过去时:
The leasee signed the lease last week. = 承租人上周签署了租赁合同。
The leasee occupied the apartment for five years. = 承租人住了这套公寓五年。


现在完成时:
The leasee has paid all the rent. = 承租人已经付清了所有租金。
The leasee has lived here since 2020. = 承租人自2020年以来一直住在这里。


过去完成时:
The leasee had already moved out before the inspection. = 检查之前，承租人已经搬走了。
The leasee had complained about the noise several times. = 承租人曾多次抱怨噪音问题。


将来时:
The leasee will renew the lease next year. = 承租人明年将续签租赁合同。
The leasee will vacate the premises by the end of the month. = 承租人将在月底腾空房屋。


虚拟语气:
If the leasee were responsible, this wouldn't have happened. = 如果承租人负责任，这件事就不会发生了。
I wish the leasee had informed me earlier. = 我希望承租人早点告诉我。


被动语态:
The lease was signed by the leasee. = 租赁合同由承租人签署。

疑问句:

否定句:

The leasee is not responsible for major repairs. = 承租人无需负责重大维修。
