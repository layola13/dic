outtravelled:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəld/| v.|旅行得比…远；超过…的旅行范围|To travel farther or more extensively than.  It implies surpassing someone or something in the distance or extent of travel.|例句：He outtravelled his father, exploring continents his father never even dreamed of. 他比他父亲走得更远，探索了他父亲从未梦想过的几个大陆。


例句：The expedition outtravelled all previous attempts to reach the summit.  这次探险比以往所有到达顶峰的尝试都走得更远。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip


反义词：undertravel, lag behind

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outtravels everyone in his family. = 他比他家里的每个人都走得远。
She outtravels her colleagues on business trips. = 她在商务旅行中比她的同事走得更远。

一般过去时:
They outtravelled their rivals in the race to the North Pole. = 他们在前往北极的竞赛中超过了他们的对手。
We outtravelled our original plan by a significant margin. = 我们超出了原计划的行程范围很多。

现在完成时:
He has outtravelled most people his age. = 他比大多数同龄人都走得远。
She has outtravelled her expectations for this year's travels.  = 她今年的旅行超过了她的预期。

过去完成时:
By the time they reached the finish line, they had outtravelled all other competitors. = 当他们到达终点线时，他们已经超过了所有其他竞争对手。
Before the trip ended, we had outtravelled our initial estimates. = 在旅行结束之前，我们已经超出了最初的估计。


将来时:
They will outtravel their previous record this summer. = 他们今年夏天将超过他们之前的记录。
We will outtravel the scope of the current project. = 我们将超出当前项目的范围。


虚拟语气:
If he had a better car, he would outtravel his friends. = 如果他有一辆更好的车，他会比他的朋友们走得更远。
If she had more time, she would outtravel the entire country. = 如果她有更多时间，她将游遍整个国家。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
