nondelivery: /ˌnɒn dɪˈlɪvəri/ | n. | 未送达，未交付 | The failure to deliver something as promised or expected.  This term often refers to goods or services, but can also apply to information or messages. |

例句：

The customer complained about the nondelivery of his order. = 客户抱怨他的订单未送达。

The nondelivery of the crucial documents delayed the project. = 关键文件的未送达延误了项目。


近义词：failure to deliver, undelivery, non-arrival, default (in delivery)

反义词：delivery, arrival


词性：名词
