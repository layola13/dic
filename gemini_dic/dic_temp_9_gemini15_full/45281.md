culturable:/ˈkʌltʃərəbl/ | adj. | 可培养的；可耕作的 | Capable of being cultivated or grown; able to be grown in a culture.  This refers to microorganisms that can be grown in a laboratory setting, or to land suitable for farming. | Example:  The soil is culturable and suitable for growing crops. 这块土壤可以耕种，适合种植庄稼。 The bacteria proved culturable in the lab.  这种细菌在实验室中被证明是可以培养的。|近义词：cultivable, growable | 反义词：uncultivable, sterile
