immaculate:/ɪˈmækjʊlət/| adj.|纯洁的；完美的；无瑕疵的|Free from flaws or defects; perfectly clean.  The immaculate white dress was a stunning sight.  |近义词：spotless, pristine, flawless, perfect|反义词：dirty, stained, flawed, imperfect

例句：

The museum's collection of ancient artifacts was immaculate.  博物馆收藏的古代文物保存完好无损。

Her performance was immaculate; not a single mistake.  她的表演完美无瑕，没有一个错误。
