schmo:/ʃmoʊ/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋，家伙|A schmo is a foolish or inept person; a clumsy or awkward person.  It's often used informally and can be considered slightly offensive. |He's such a schmo; he fell for that obvious scam. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信了那个明显的骗局。|近义词：fool, dope, klutz, idiot |反义词：genius, smart, clever
