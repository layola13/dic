godchildren:/ˈɡɑːdˌtʃɪldrən/ (US) /ˈɡɒdˌtʃɪld(r)ən/ (UK)| n. |教子，教女；受洗教子 | A godchild is a child who has been named as a godson or goddaughter in a religious ceremony, typically a christening or baptism.  The godparents are expected to take a spiritual interest in the child’s upbringing. |例句：She is the godmother of my two godchildren. 她是我两个教子的教母。

例句：He is a proud godfather to his godchildren. 他以身为教父而自豪。


近义词：spiritual child, sponsored child | 反义词：N/A
