pan-fried:/pæn fraɪd/| adj.|煎的；平底锅煎的| Cooked in a frying pan with a small amount of fat or oil.  This term describes a cooking method where food is cooked in a pan over heat with a minimal amount of oil or fat, resulting in a browned, crispy exterior and a tender interior. |例句：I had some delicious pan-fried dumplings for lunch. 我午餐吃了美味的煎饺子。

例句：The pan-fried scallops were perfectly cooked.  这道煎扇贝做得恰到好处。|近义词：sautéed, stir-fried, fried|反义词：boiled, steamed, baked|词性：形容词
