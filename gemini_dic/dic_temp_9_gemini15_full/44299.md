crooked:/ˈkrʊkɪd/ | adj. | 弯曲的；不正直的；欺诈的 |  crooked means bent or twisted out of shape; not straight or aligned. It can also refer to someone who is dishonest or morally corrupt. | 例句：The crooked road led to a secluded village.  弯曲的道路通向一个僻静的村庄。

The crooked politician was eventually caught and jailed.  那个不诚实的政客最终被抓住并入狱。

| 近义词：bent, curved, twisted, dishonest, corrupt | 反义词：straight, honest, upright, truthful |  adj.
