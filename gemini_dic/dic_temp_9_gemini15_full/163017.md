precipitation:/prɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |降水，沉淀|The process of precipitating a substance from a solution; the deposit of moisture from the atmosphere in the form of rain, snow, hail, or sleet.  Precipitation includes all forms of water that fall from the atmosphere to the earth's surface.  |例句：The heavy precipitation caused flooding in the lowlands.  这场大雨导致低地发生洪涝。|例句：The scientist studied the precipitation of silver chloride from the solution. 科学家研究了氯化银从溶液中析出的过程。|近义词：rainfall, downfall, sedimentation |反义词：evaporation, sublimation |名词
