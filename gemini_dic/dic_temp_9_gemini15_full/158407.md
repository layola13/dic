pleasurably:/ˈplɛʒərəbli/| adj. |令人愉快地；愉快地 |Pleasurably describes something done or experienced in a pleasant and enjoyable way. It emphasizes the feeling of satisfaction and delight associated with the action or experience. |例句：She smiled pleasurably as she watched the children play. 她看着孩子们玩耍，愉快地笑了。|例句：He spent his retirement years pleasurably, traveling and pursuing his hobbies. 他愉快地度过了退休时光，四处旅行并从事他的爱好。|近义词：delightfully, enjoyably, agreeably |反义词：unpleasantly, disagreeably, painfully
