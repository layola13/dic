bycatch:/ˈbaɪkætʃ/| n. |兼捕，误捕 |Unintentional catch of nontarget species while fishing.  This includes marine mammals, seabirds, turtles, and fish that are not the intended catch. |例句：The shrimp trawlers' bycatch included a large number of dolphins.  虾拖网渔船的兼捕中包括大量的海豚。

例句：Reducing bycatch is a major goal of sustainable fishing practices. 减少兼捕是可持续渔业实践的主要目标。


近义词：incidental catch, unintended catch |反义词：target catch, intended catch


词性：名词
