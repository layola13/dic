proof:/pruːf/| n. |证据，证明；校样；考验；酒精度 |Evidence or argument that serves to establish a fact or the truth of something; a trial or test of something's capabilities, quality, or effectiveness; in printing, a preliminary version of a publication to be checked for errors; the alcoholic content of a drink. |例句：The police have enough proof to convict him.  警方有足够的证据将他定罪。

例句：The new software is undergoing rigorous proof before release. 新软件在发布前正在进行严格的测试。


近义词：evidence, verification, demonstration, test, trial |反义词：disproof, refutation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence proves his guilt. = 证据证明了他的罪行。
This experiment proves the theory. = 这个实验证明了这个理论。


一般过去时:
The lawyer presented proof of the defendant's innocence. = 律师提供了被告无罪的证据。
The test proved the effectiveness of the medicine. = 测试证明了这种药的有效性。


现在完成时:
Scientists have proved the existence of black holes. = 科学家们已经证明了黑洞的存在。
They have proved their loyalty to the company. = 他们已经证明了他们对公司的忠诚。


过去完成时:
They had proved the theorem before the conference. = 他们在会议之前已经证明了这个定理。
She had already proved her ability to manage a team. = 她已经证明了她管理团队的能力。


将来时:
The results of the study will prove the hypothesis. = 研究结果将证明该假设。
We will prove our innocence. = 我们会证明我们的清白。


虚拟语气:
If they had more proof, they would win the case. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会赢得官司。
If only I had proof of his wrongdoing! = 但愿我有他做错事的证据！


被动语态:
His guilt was proved beyond reasonable doubt. = 他的罪行已无可辩驳地被证明。
The theory has been proved time and time again. = 这个理论已经被反复证明。


疑问句:
What proof do you have to support your claim? = 你有什么证据来支持你的说法？
Does this evidence prove anything? = 这证据证明了什么吗？


否定句:
They could not prove his involvement in the crime. = 他们无法证明他参与了犯罪。
There is no proof that he is guilty. = 没有证据证明他有罪。


PHRASE->
