conder:/ˈkɒndə(r)/| n. |秃鹰，神鹰|A large, mainly New World bird of prey with a black or dark brown plumage and a bare head.  They are known for their scavenging habits.|例句：The condor soared high above the mountains.  秃鹰在群山之上翱翔。|例句：The Andean condor is a magnificent bird.  安第斯秃鹰是一种雄伟的鸟类。|近义词：vulture, eagle|反义词：None|名词
