featherstitch:/ˈfɛðərˌstɪtʃ/| n. |羽毛针法，羽毛绣 |A decorative stitch resembling a feather, used in embroidery and sewing.  It involves a series of slanted stitches radiating from a central line, creating a feather-like appearance. |例句：She used a featherstitch to embellish the neckline of the dress. 她用羽毛针法装饰了裙子的领口。|例句：The intricate featherstitch on the quilt showcased the quilter's skill.  这床被子上的精美羽毛针法展现了缝纫者的技艺。|近义词：feather stitch, chain stitch (in some applications)|反义词：无
