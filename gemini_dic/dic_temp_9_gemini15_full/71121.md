fishhook:/ˈfɪʃhʊk/ | n. |鱼钩 | A hook used for catching fish.  It typically has a sharp point and a barb to prevent the fish from escaping. |例句：He baited the fishhook with a worm. 他用虫子做鱼饵。

例句：The fisherman cast his line, hoping to snag a fish on the fishhook. 渔夫抛出鱼线，希望能用鱼钩钩住一条鱼。


近义词：hook, snare


反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性：Noun
