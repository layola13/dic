flaxy:/ˈflæksi/ | adj. |亚麻色的，柔软的，有光泽的 |  Descriptive of something resembling flax in color or texture;  often refers to hair that is light brown or golden, soft, and somewhat shiny.  |例句：Her flaxen hair shone in the sunlight.  她的亚麻色头发在阳光下闪闪发光。


例句：The flaxen field was ready for harvest.  亚麻田已经准备好收割了。


近义词：blond, tow-headed, golden


反义词：dark, black, brunette

词性:形容词
