例句：The reenjoyment of his favorite novel after many years was a delightful experience.  多年后再次阅读他最喜欢的书籍是一次令人愉悦的体验。


近义词：rediscovery, renewed enjoyment, relish, repetition of pleasure | 反义词：disappointment, displeasure, misery
