recontract:/ˌriːˈkɒntrækt/ | vt. |重新签订合同；再订合同 | To enter into a new contract, often replacing or modifying a previous one.  It implies a renewal or renegotiation of an existing agreement. |例句：We had to recontract with the supplier after the initial agreement expired. 我们在最初的协议到期后不得不与供应商重新签订合同。

例句：The company decided to recontract its insurance policy with a different provider. 公司决定与不同的提供商重新签订保险合同。


近义词：renew, renegotiate, revise | 反义词：terminate, cancel | 词性：动词


USAGE->

The company recontracts with its suppliers regularly. = 公司定期与供应商重新签订合同。

过去完成时:
