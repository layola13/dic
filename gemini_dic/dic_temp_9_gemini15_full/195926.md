sensitising:/ˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪŋ/| v. |致敏；使敏感；使易感|  To make (someone or something) more sensitive or responsive to something.  The process involves making something more susceptible to a particular stimulus or reaction.  This often applies to biological or chemical contexts,  but can be used more broadly. |例句：Exposure to certain chemicals can sensitise the skin.  接触某些化学物质会使皮肤过敏。

The prolonged use of this medication can sensitise the patient to sunlight. 长期使用这种药物会使病人对阳光过敏。|近义词：desensitize的反义词,  sensitize, make sensitive,  condition|反义词：desensitize, dull,  deaden|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The doctor is sensitising the patient to potential allergens. = 医生正在让病人对潜在的过敏原致敏。
This training program sensitises employees to diversity issues. = 这项培训计划使员工对多元化问题更加敏感。


一般过去时:
The experiment sensitised the mice to light. = 实验使老鼠对光线敏感。
The teacher sensitised her students to the plight of the refugees. = 老师使她的学生们对难民的困境更加敏感。


现在完成时:
The campaign has sensitised the public to the importance of environmental protection. = 这场运动已经提高了公众对环境保护重要性的认识。
Repeated exposure to loud noises has sensitised my hearing. = 反复暴露在巨大的噪音中使我的听力变得敏感。


过去完成时:
They had sensitised the film to low light conditions before the shoot. = 他们在拍摄前已经将胶卷对弱光条件进行了处理以提高感光度。
The artist had sensitised the clay to the humidity before sculpting. = 艺术家在雕刻前已经让泥土对湿度敏感。


将来时:
The new marketing strategy will sensitise consumers to the brand's values. = 新的营销策略将使消费者对品牌的价值观更加敏感。
We will sensitise the equipment to detect even the slightest variations. = 我们将使设备对最细微的变化都敏感。


虚拟语气:
If we sensitised the public more effectively, we could prevent many accidents. = 如果我们更有效地提高公众意识，就能预防许多事故。
I wish they had sensitised me to the risks involved earlier. = 我希望他们早些让我了解所涉及的风险。


被动语态:
The population was sensitised to the dangers of smoking through public health campaigns. = 通过公共卫生宣传活动，人们对吸烟的危害有了认识。
The photographic plates were sensitised with special chemicals. = 感光板用特殊的化学物质进行了处理。


疑问句:
How can we sensitise people to the needs of the homeless? = 我们怎样才能使人们对无家可归者的需求更加敏感？
Was the public sufficiently sensitised to the issue before the vote? = 在投票前，公众对这个问题的认识是否足够？


否定句:
The campaign failed to sensitise the public to the importance of voting. = 这场运动未能提高公众对投票重要性的认识。
The drug did not sensitise the patient to the allergen. = 该药物没有使病人对过敏原过敏。
