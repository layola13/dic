detectors:/dɪˈtɛktərz/ | n. |探测器，检测器 |A device or system designed to detect the presence or absence of something, often used in scientific, medical, or security contexts.  |例句：Smoke detectors are essential for home safety.  烟雾探测器对家庭安全至关重要。

例句：The airport uses advanced detectors to screen passengers' luggage. 机场使用先进的探测器来检查乘客的行李。

近义词：sensors, monitors, receivers | 反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent) | 词性：Noun

词源:
The word "detector" is derived from the verb "detect," which comes from the Latin word "detegere," meaning "to uncover" or "to reveal."  The suffix "-or" indicates the agent or instrument of the action.
