/dʒæt/| n.| 掷，投掷；急冲；短暂的经历 |A jat is a quick throw or toss, or a short, sudden rush or burst of something. It can also refer to a brief experience or encounter.|例句：He made a jat of the ball. 他快速地投掷了球。 He had a jat of inspiration. 他灵光一闪。|近义词：throw, hurl, fling, dash, spurt |反义词：slow, gradual, prolonged
