non-contagious: /ˌnɒn kənˈteɪdʒəs/ | adj. |非传染性的；不会蔓延的 | Not capable of being transmitted from one person or organism to another.  It refers to diseases or conditions that cannot spread from one individual to another through direct or indirect contact. | 例句：The doctor confirmed that the illness was non-contagious, relieving the family's worries. 医生确认这种疾病是非传染性的，这减轻了家人的担忧。

The rash is non-contagious, so there's no need to worry about spreading it.  疹子是非传染性的，所以不用担心会传染。


近义词：non-communicable, non-infectious | 反义词：contagious, infectious, communicable | 词性：形容词
