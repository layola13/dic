oversentimentalizes:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪmənˈtælˌaɪzɪz/| vt. |使过于感伤；过度赋予情感|To exaggerate or distort something by making it excessively sentimental.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting an unhealthy or unrealistic level of sentimentality.| The movie oversentimentalizes the characters' grief, making it feel contrived and unrealistic.  这部电影对人物的悲伤进行了过度渲染，使其显得做作而不真实。|近义词：sentimentalize, romanticize, overdramatize|反义词：de-sentimentalize, objectify, harden|动词
