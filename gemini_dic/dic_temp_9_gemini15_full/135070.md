This term is commonly used in biology and medicine to describe cells that do not contain a nucleus, such as mature red blood cells in mammals. |Example: Mature mammalian red blood cells are nonnucleated.  哺乳动物成熟的红细胞是无核的。|近义词：anucleate, enucleated |反义词：nucleated
