exsudation:/ˌɛksjuːˈdeɪʃən/| n. |渗出；分泌；溢出 |The oozing or leaking of a substance from a plant or animal tissue, often due to injury or disease.  It involves the passage of fluid through a membrane or tissue.  | Example: The exsudation of sap from the wounded tree attracted insects. 树木受伤部位渗出的树液吸引了昆虫。The exsudation of pus from the wound indicated an infection. 伤口流出的脓液表明感染了。|近义词：ooze, seepage, exudation, discharge |反义词：absorption, retention |名词
