mercery:/ˈmɜːrsəri/| n. |杂货店；纺织品商店；商品；货物|A mercery was a shop selling small wares, textiles, and haberdashery.  The term is now largely archaic, replaced by more specific terms like "haberdashery" or "dry goods store".  Mercery can also refer to the goods themselves.|例句：In the 17th century, a thriving mercery supplied the town with fabrics and notions.  十七世纪，一家繁荣的杂货店为城镇提供布料和日用品。

The mercery of the market stall was quite extensive. 市场摊位的商品相当丰富。|近义词：haberdashery, dry goods store, shop, store|反义词：N/A
