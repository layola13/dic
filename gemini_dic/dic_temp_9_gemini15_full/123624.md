monthly:/ˈmʌnθli/ | adj. | 每月的，按月的 | Occurring or done once a month; calculated or payable monthly.  |例句：We have a monthly meeting. 我们每月举行一次会议。|例句：She receives a monthly salary. 她每月领取薪水。|近义词：periodic, regular |反义词：daily, weekly, yearly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The magazine is published monthly. = 这本杂志每月出版一次。
We have a monthly team meeting. = 我们每月举行一次团队会议。

一般过去时:
They paid their rent monthly. = 他们每月支付房租。
She received a monthly allowance. = 她每月收到津贴。

现在完成时:
We have paid our subscription monthly for five years. = 我们已经每月支付订阅费五年了。
She has worked here monthly for the last two years. = 她过去两年每月都在这里工作。


过去完成时:
They had paid their monthly bills before the end of the month. = 他们在一个月结束前已经付清了月费。
She had received her monthly salary before the deadline. = 截止日期前她已经收到了月薪。


将来时:
We will pay the bill monthly. = 我们将每月支付账单。
They will receive a monthly bonus. = 他们将获得月度奖金。


虚拟语气:
If we had a monthly budget, we would be able to save more. = 如果我们有每月预算，我们将能够节省更多。
I wish I had a monthly income. = 我希望我有月收入。


被动语态:
The rent is paid monthly. = 房租是每月支付的。
The bills are paid monthly by automatic transfer. = 账单通过自动转账每月支付。


疑问句:
Is the rent paid monthly? = 房租是每月支付的吗？
Do you have a monthly payment plan? = 你有每月付款计划吗？


否定句:
The payment is not monthly. = 付款不是每月的。
The report is not issued monthly. = 报告不是每月发布的。
