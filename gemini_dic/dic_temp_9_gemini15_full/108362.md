lavishingly:/ˈlævɪʃɪŋli/| adv. | 非常大方地；极其奢华地 |  Describes something done in an extravagant and generous manner, often involving a large amount of something.  | The party was lavishly decorated with flowers and balloons.  派对上装饰着鲜花和气球，非常奢华。  | generously, extravagantly, sumptuously, profusely | sparingly, frugally, modestly, minimally | 副词


USAGE->

They lavishly praise their employees' achievements. = 他们对员工的成就给予了极高的赞扬。
She lavishly applies sunscreen before going to the beach. = 她去海滩前豪迈地涂抹防晒霜。
