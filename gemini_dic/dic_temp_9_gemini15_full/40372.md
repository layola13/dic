contradicted:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktɪd/ | v. | 反驳；与…矛盾 | To express the opposite of (a statement, person, etc.); to be in conflict with.  | Example: His statement contradicted the evidence presented in court. (他的陈述与法庭上提出的证据相矛盾。)  His alibi was contradicted by several witnesses. (他的不在场证明被几位证人驳斥了。) |近义词：refuted, denied, opposed | 反义词：confirmed, corroborated, supported | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His actions contradict his words. = 他的行为与他的言辞相矛盾。
The two reports contradict each other. = 这两份报告互相矛盾。

一般过去时:
She contradicted everything he said. = 她反驳了他所说的一切。
The witness contradicted his previous testimony. = 证人与他之前的证词相矛盾。

现在完成时:
The new evidence has contradicted the original findings. = 新证据与最初的调查结果相矛盾。
Recent research has contradicted earlier theories. = 最近的研究与早期的理论相矛盾。

过去完成时:
The lawyer argued that the witness had contradicted himself. = 律师辩称证人自相矛盾。
It became clear that his account had contradicted the facts. = 很明显，他的说法与事实相矛盾。


将来时:
His testimony will contradict the defendant's claim. = 他的证词将与被告的说法相矛盾。
The results of the experiment will likely contradict the hypothesis. = 实验结果很可能会与假设相矛盾。


虚拟语气:
If he had not contradicted the expert, the trial would have gone more smoothly. = 如果他不反驳专家的话，审判就会进行得更顺利。
If only he hadn't contradicted himself! = 但愿他没有自相矛盾！

被动语态:
His statement was contradicted by the prosecution. = 他的陈述被检方驳斥了。
The theory has been contradicted by numerous studies. = 这一理论已被许多研究驳斥。


疑问句:
Does his testimony contradict the facts? = 他的证词与事实相矛盾吗？
Does the evidence contradict the defendant's alibi? = 证据是否与被告的不正当理由相矛盾？

否定句:
The two stories did not contradict each other. = 这两个故事并不互相矛盾。
His account did not contradict the known facts. = 他的说法与已知事实并不矛盾。


PHRASE->
contradict oneself = 自相矛盾
contradict a statement = 反驳一项声明
contradict the evidence = 与证据相矛盾
contradict the findings = 与调查结果相矛盾
contradict each other = 互相矛盾
