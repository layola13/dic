internment:/ɪnˈtɜːnmənt/ | n. |拘留；收容；监禁 |The confinement or detention of a person, typically for political reasons.  Internment often involves the restriction of movement and liberty, and is usually applied to a group of people rather than individuals. |例句：The internment of Japanese Americans during World War II is a controversial chapter in American history.  二战期间对日裔美国人的拘留是美国历史上一个有争议的篇章。

例句：Many political dissidents faced internment under the authoritarian regime.  许多政治异议人士在专制政权下面临监禁。


近义词：detention, confinement, imprisonment, incarceration |反义词：release, liberation, freedom
