non-verbalized:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəlaɪzd/| adj.|未用言语表达的，非语言化的|Not expressed in words; communicated through nonverbal cues such as body language, facial expressions, or tone of voice.  |Example:  His non-verbalized anger was evident in his clenched fists.  他的未用言语表达的愤怒从他紧握的拳头中显而易见。


Example: The child's non-verbalized distress was clear from his tears. 孩子未用言语表达的痛苦从他的眼泪中显而易见。


近义词：unuttered, unspoken, implicit, unexpressed


反义词：verbalized, expressed, articulated


词性：形容词
