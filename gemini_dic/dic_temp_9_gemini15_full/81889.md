grievousnesses:/ˈɡriːvəsnəsiz/ | n. | 委屈；痛苦；冤情 | Grievances are complaints or expressions of unhappiness about something that you feel is unfair.  The word often implies a sense of injustice or suffering.  It usually refers to a collection or accumulation of complaints. | Example: The workers voiced their grievances about low wages and poor working conditions. 工人们表达了他们对低工资和恶劣工作条件的不满。The company failed to address the employees' numerous grievances, leading to a strike. 公司未能解决员工的诸多不满，导致了罢工。|近义词：complaints, grievances, resentments, hurts |反义词：satisfactions, joys, pleasures |名词
