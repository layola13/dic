egocentrism:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/| n. |自我中心主义，利己主义 |The tendency to consider oneself the center of the universe; a preoccupation with oneself and one's own affairs.  It often involves a lack of consideration for the feelings or needs of others. |例句：His egocentrism made it difficult to maintain friendships. 他的自我中心使他难以维持友谊。
例句：The egocentrism of the CEO led to a decline in company morale.  首席执行官的自我中心导致公司士气下降。|近义词：selfishness, narcissism, self-absorption, self-centeredness |反义词：altruism, selflessness, empathy
