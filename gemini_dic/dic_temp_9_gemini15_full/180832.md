rehashes:/ˌriːˈhæʃɪz/| n. v.|  重新处理；炒冷饭；重复讲述|To rehash something means to use it again in a slightly different form, often without adding anything new or interesting.  It can refer to presenting old ideas as new, or repeating stories or information without significant alteration. |例句：The movie is just a rehash of old clichés. 这部电影只是对老套俗语的重新包装。  He kept rehashing the same old story. 他不停地重复着同一个老故事。|近义词：recycle, reiterate, repeat, revamp|反义词：innovate, create, originate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rehashes old news stories for his blog. = 他为他的博客重新包装旧新闻故事。
They rehash the same arguments every meeting. = 他们每次会议都重复同样的论点。


一般过去时:
She rehashed her presentation for the new audience. = 她为新的听众重新整理了她的演示文稿。
The band rehashed their old hits for the concert. = 乐队为演唱会重新演绎了他们的老歌。


现在完成时:

They have rehashed the same ideas in every proposal. = 他们在每一个提案中都重复了同样的想法。


过去完成时:
They had rehashed the plan several times before finally approving it. = 他们在最终批准计划之前已经修改过几次了。
She had rehashed the data multiple times to ensure accuracy. = 她已经多次重新整理数据以确保准确性。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
