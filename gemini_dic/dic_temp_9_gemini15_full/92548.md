/hɜːrs/ | n. |灵车；殡葬马车|A hearse is a vehicle used to transport a deceased person to their burial or cremation.  It is typically a black, elongated car designed for the purpose.|例句：The hearse followed the funeral procession slowly. 灵车缓缓地跟在送葬队伍后面。|The hearse arrived at the cemetery. 灵车到达了墓地。|近义词：funeral car,  death carriage|反义词：无
