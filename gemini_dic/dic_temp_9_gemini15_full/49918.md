denationalises: /ˌdiːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使非国有化；取消国有化 | To remove something from national ownership or control; to privatize.  | The government denationalised the railways, transferring them to private companies.  政府将铁路私有化，将其转交给私营公司。 | privatize, deregulate | nationalize, socialize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government denationalises several industries each year. = 政府每年将几个行业非国有化。
They denationalise the airline to improve efficiency. = 他们将航空公司非国有化以提高效率。

一般过去时:
The country denationalised its telecommunications industry in the 1990s. = 该国在20世纪90年代将其电信行业非国有化。
The previous administration denationalised the energy sector. = 前一届政府将能源部门非国有化。

现在完成时:
The state has denationalised most of its major industries. = 国家已经将其大部分主要产业非国有化。
Since the reforms, the government has denationalised many formerly state-owned enterprises. = 自改革以来，政府已经将许多以前国有的企业非国有化。


过去完成时:
By 1990, the government had denationalised most of its key industries. = 到1990年，政府已将其大部分关键产业非国有化。
Before the economic crisis, the country had denationalised its banking system. = 在经济危机爆发之前，该国已经将其银行体系非国有化。


将来时:
The government will denationalise the postal service next year. = 政府明年将对邮政服务进行非国有化。
They plan to denationalise the water supply company in the near future. = 他们计划在不久的将来对供水公司进行非国有化。


虚拟语气:
If the government denationalised the healthcare system, it could lead to increased costs. = 如果政府将医疗系统非国有化，可能会导致成本增加。
It would be better if they had not denationalised the railway network. = 如果他们没有将铁路网非国有化就好了。


被动语态:
Many state-owned enterprises were denationalised during the economic reforms. = 在经济改革期间，许多国有企业被非国有化。
The process of denationalisation is being carefully managed by the government. = 非国有化过程正在由政府谨慎管理。


疑问句:

否定句:

The plan to denationalise the health service was rejected. = 将医疗服务非国有化的计划被否决了。
