restructured:/ˌriːˈstrʌktʃərd/| adj. | 重组的；改组的；调整结构的 |  Describes something that has been reorganized or rearranged, often to improve efficiency or effectiveness.  It implies a significant change in the structure or organization of something. | Example: The company's restructured management team is more efficient.  这家公司重组后的管理团队效率更高了。 The restructured sentence is much clearer.  改写后的句子清晰多了。|近义词：reorganized, revamped, reorganized, overhauled |反义词：disorganized, chaotic, unchanged |形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company regularly restructured its departments. = 公司定期重组部门。
This process is restructured every year. = 这个流程每年都会进行重组。


一般过去时:
They restructured the entire organization last year. = 他们去年重组了整个组织。
The government restructured the education system. = 政府重组了教育体系。


现在完成时:
The company has restructured its operations to improve profitability. = 公司已经重组了运营以提高盈利能力。
We have restructured the project timeline. = 我们已经调整了项目的进度安排。


过去完成时:
They had restructured the department before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经重组了部门。
The team had restructured its strategy before the presentation. = 团队在演示之前已经调整了策略。


将来时:
They will restructure the department next quarter. = 他们下个季度将重组该部门。
The company will restructure its debt. = 公司将重组债务。


虚拟语气:
If they restructured the company, it might be more successful. = 如果他们重组公司，它可能会更成功。
I wish they had restructured the team sooner. = 我希望他们早点重组团队。


被动语态:
The department was restructured last month. = 该部门上个月进行了重组。
The company's finances were restructured to avoid bankruptcy. = 公司的财务状况进行了重组以避免破产。


疑问句:
Was the team restructured after the layoffs? = 在裁员后团队是否进行了重组？
Has the curriculum been restructured for the new academic year? = 新学年课程是否进行了调整？


否定句:
The company did not restructure its operations. = 公司没有重组其运营。
The project was not restructured due to time constraints. = 由于时间限制，项目没有进行重组。
