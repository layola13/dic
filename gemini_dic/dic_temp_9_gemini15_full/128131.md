nether:/ˈnɛðər/| adj.|下界的，阴间的；低的，下层的|belonging to a lower region; situated below or beneath something else.  The word often evokes a sense of darkness, gloom, and the underworld.|例句：The nether regions of the city were filled with crime and poverty. 城市的下层地区充满了犯罪和贫困。|例句：He descended into the nether world of despair. 他堕入了绝望的深渊。|近义词：lower, under, infernal, subterranean|反义词：upper, higher, celestial
