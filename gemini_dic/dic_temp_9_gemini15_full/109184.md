legitimizes:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪz/| vt.|使合法化；使正当化 | To give legitimacy to something; to make something lawful or acceptable.  |The government's actions legitimized the use of force. 政府的行为使武力使用合法化了。|The new law legitimizes same-sex marriage. 新法律使同性婚姻合法化。| justifies, validates, authorizes | criminalizes, invalidates, condemns |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy legitimizes the use of drones. = 新政策使无人机的使用合法化。
This process legitimizes the company's actions. = 这个过程使公司的行为合法化。


一般过去时:
The court decision legitimized their claim to the property. = 法院的判决使他们对该财产的主张合法化。
His success legitimized his methods. = 他的成功使他的方法正当化。


现在完成时:
Recent events have legitimized the need for stricter security measures. = 最近发生的事件使加强安全措施的必要性合法化了。
The movement has legitimized alternative medicine practices. = 这场运动使替代医学实践合法化了。


过去完成时:
The previous government had legitimized the practice through years of inaction. = 前政府多年来无所作为，使这种做法合法化了。
They had legitimized their power through a carefully orchestrated campaign. = 他们通过精心策划的活动使他们的权力合法化了。


将来时:
The upcoming legislation will legitimize online gambling. = 即将出台的立法将使在线赌博合法化。
This new evidence will legitimize our suspicions. = 这项新证据将使我们的怀疑合理化。


虚拟语气:
If they passed the bill, it would legitimize their authority. = 如果他们通过了法案，那将使他们的权威合法化。
If the evidence were stronger, it would legitimize their claim. = 如果证据更充分，那将使他们的说法合法化。


被动语态:
The use of force was legitimized by the international community. = 武力的使用得到了国际社会的认可。
Their actions were legitimized by the recent developments. = 他们的行为因最近的事态发展而变得合法。


疑问句:
Does this action legitimize the use of violence? = 这种行为是否使使用暴力合法化？
What legitimizes their claim to the throne? = 什么使他们对王位的要求合法化？


否定句:
The lack of evidence did not legitimize the accusations. = 缺乏证据并没有使这些指控合法化。
The judge's ruling did not legitimize the illegal activity. = 法官的裁决并没有使非法活动合法化。
