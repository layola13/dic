mellows:/ˈmɛloʊz/| v.|变得柔和；变得成熟；变醇厚|To become softer, smoother, or more gentle; to ripen or mature, especially of fruit or wine; to become less intense or harsh.|The music mellowed as the evening progressed. 音乐随着夜晚的进行变得柔和了。|The wine mellowed beautifully in the cellar. 这酒在地窖里陈酿得极好。|近义词：soften, mature, ripen|反义词：harden, intensify, sharpen


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun mellows the fruit.  阳光使水果成熟。
His anger slowly mellows. 他的怒气慢慢消退了。


一般过去时:
The wine mellowed over time.  酒随着时间的推移变得醇厚。
The harsh edges of his personality mellowed with age. 他性格中粗糙的一面随着年龄增长而变得温和了。


现在完成时:
The years have mellowed her.  岁月使她变得温柔了。
The fruit has mellowed nicely. 水果已经成熟得很好。


过去完成时:
The wine had mellowed considerably before we tasted it. 我们品尝这酒之前，它已经陈酿得相当醇厚了。
He had mellowed significantly since our last meeting. 自从我们上次见面以来，他已经成熟了很多。


将来时:
This wine will mellow with age. 这酒会随着时间的推移变得醇厚。
I think he will mellow as he gets older. 我认为他年纪大了会变得温和。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were warmer, the fruit would mellow faster. 如果天气更暖和，水果就会成熟得更快。
I wish my temper would mellow with age. 我希望我的脾气能随着年龄的增长而变得温和。


被动语态:
The fruit is mellowed by the sun. 水果被阳光晒熟了。
His opinions are mellowed by experience. 他的观点因经验而变得温和。


疑问句:
Does the cheese mellow with age? 奶酪会随着时间推移变得醇厚吗？
Has his attitude mellowed at all? 他的态度有所缓和吗？


否定句:
The fruit didn't mellow properly this year. 今年的水果没有成熟好。
His personality hasn't mellowed much.  他的个性并没有变得温和多少。
