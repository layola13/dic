houseleek:/ˈhaʊsliːk/| n. |瓦松，长生草|A succulent plant of the genus Sempervivum, typically having rosettes of fleshy leaves and star-shaped flowers.  Houseleeks are known for their ability to thrive in harsh conditions, such as on roofs and walls. |Example:  The houseleek clung tenaciously to the old stone wall.  瓦松顽强地依附在古老的石墙上。

The houseleek is a popular choice for rock gardens. 长生草是岩石花园的热门选择。|近义词：sempervivum, succulent |反义词：无
