flat-footed:/ˌflætˈfʊtɪd/| adj.|扁平足的；笨拙的；措手不及的 |Having flat feet; lacking grace or dexterity; unprepared or caught off guard. |例句：He was flat-footed and clumsy on the dance floor. 他在舞池里笨手笨脚的。  |例句：The company was completely flat-footed by the sudden economic downturn. 公司对突如其来的经济衰退完全措手不及。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, unprepared, surprised |反义词：graceful, nimble, prepared, ready
