lineage:/ˈlɪniɪdʒ/| n. |血统；家系；世系；血脉；传承|The line of descent traced continuously from an ancestor.  It refers to a sequence of ancestors and descendants of a person, animal, or plant, or to the succession of rulers or members of a family.|例句：He boasted about his noble lineage. 他夸耀他显赫的家世。

例句：Tracing her lineage back to the founding fathers proved difficult. 追溯她的家系到开国元勋被证明很困难。

近义词：ancestry, descent, pedigree, extraction|反义词：None directly opposite, but terms suggesting lack of connection or unknown origins could be considered contrasting.  |词性:名词
