reconfining:/ˌriːkənˈfaɪnɪŋ/| v.|重新限制；再次限制 |The act of confining again; subjecting to confinement a second time.  It implies a previous confinement has been lifted or ended, and is now being reinstated. |例句：After a brief period of freedom, the prisoners were reconfined.  再次获得短暂自由后，囚犯们再次被监禁。

The government is reconfining the city due to a surge in COVID-19 cases. 由于新冠肺炎病例激增，政府正在对该城市重新实行封锁。|近义词：reimprison, re-restrict, reconstrain |反义词：release, liberate, free
