contributable:/kənˈtrɪbjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 可归因的，可归咎的 | Attributable; able to be assigned or credited to a specific cause or source.  | The accident was contributable to driver error.  这次事故可归咎于驾驶员的错误。 | attributable, ascribable |  unattributable, inexplicable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The damage is contributable to the storm. = 损坏可归因于风暴。
His success is contributable to hard work and dedication. = 他的成功归功于努力工作和奉献精神。

一般过去时:
The problem was contributable to a lack of communication. = 这个问题可归因于沟通不畅。
The decline in sales was contributable to the economic downturn. = 销售额下降可归因于经济衰退。

现在完成时:
The improvements have been contributable to new management strategies. = 这些改进归功于新的管理策略。
Many achievements have been contributable to the team's collaborative efforts. = 许多成就都归功于团队的协作努力。

过去完成时:

The failure had been contributable to a series of poor decisions. = 失败是由一系列错误的决定造成的。

将来时:
The increase in profits will be contributable to the new marketing campaign. = 利润的增长将归功于新的营销活动。
The project's success will be contributable to the team's hard work. = 项目的成功将归功于团队的辛勤工作。

虚拟语气:
If the accident were contributable to mechanical failure, the manufacturer would be liable. = 如果事故可归因于机械故障，制造商将承担责任。
If the disease were contributable to a virus, a vaccine could be developed. = 如果这种疾病可归因于病毒，就可以研制疫苗。

被动语态:

否定句:

The failure was not contributable to a lack of effort. = 失败并非由于缺乏努力。
