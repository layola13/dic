It's typically represented by the symbol /ə/ in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA).  The exact pronunciation of a schwa can vary slightly depending on the surrounding sounds and the speaker's accent. |Example: The schwa sound is heard in the unstressed syllable of the word "about" (/əˈbaʊt/).
