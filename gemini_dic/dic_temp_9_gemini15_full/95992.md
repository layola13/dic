incurably:/ɪnˈkjʊərəbli/ | adj. | 不可治愈地，无法医治地 | Incurably means incapable of being cured. It describes a condition or illness that cannot be healed or improved through medical treatment.  | The disease is incurable.  = 这病无法治愈。

His illness is incurably progressive. = 他的疾病不可治愈地发展。


近义词：irremediably, hopelessly, irrecoverably | 反义词：curably, treatable
