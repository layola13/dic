retentiveness: /rɪˈtɛntɪvnəs/ | n. | 保持力；记忆力；记忆力强 | The ability to remember things.  This includes the capacity to retain information, experiences, or skills over time.  It can refer to both short-term and long-term memory. | 例句：His remarkable retentiveness allowed him to recall details from decades ago.  他的惊人记忆力使他能够回忆起几十年前的细节。

His excellent retentiveness for names and faces was a valuable asset in his job. 他极强的记住姓名和面孔的能力在他工作中是一笔宝贵的财富。


近义词：memory, recall, memorization, retention | 反义词：forgetfulness, amnesia
