USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, grammatical tenses, voice, and mood are not applicable.  Usage focuses on context and correct application within sentences.

Example 1: We drove ten kilometers to the beach. 我们开车开了十公里到海滩。
Example 2: The distance between the two cities is approximately 500 kilometers.  两座城市之间的距离大约是500公里。
