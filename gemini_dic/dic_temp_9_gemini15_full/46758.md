dandys:/ˈdændiz/| n.|花花公子；讲究派头的人；漂亮东西|A dandy is a man who pays excessive attention to his clothes and appearance.  The term often implies a degree of superficiality and affectation.|例句：He was a dandy, always impeccably dressed and meticulously groomed. 他是个花花公子，总是衣着讲究，打扮一丝不苟。|例句：The antique shop was full of  dandys, each more exquisite than the last. 古董店里摆满了漂亮的东西，一件比一件精致。|近义词：fop, beau, exquisite|反义词：sloven, slob|名词
