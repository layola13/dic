go-ahead:/ˈɡoʊ əˈhɛd/ | n., int. |许可，同意；继续 |  Approval or permission to proceed;  an indication to start or continue. |例句：We got the go-ahead to start the project. = 我们得到了启动项目的许可。

例句：The teacher gave him the go-ahead to leave early. = 老师准许他早退。


近义词：permission, approval, consent, okay, all clear | 反义词：prohibition, denial, refusal, veto


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We usually get the go-ahead before starting a new project. = 我们通常在开始新项目之前获得批准。
The boss gives the go-ahead to most proposals. = 老板批准大多数提案。


一般过去时:
