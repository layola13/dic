court-martials:/ˈkɔːrtˈmɑːrʃəl/| n. v.|军事法庭审判；军事法庭|A court-martial is a military court that tries members of the armed forces for offenses against military law.  It can be a noun referring to the court itself or a verb referring to the act of trying someone in such a court.|例句：The soldier was court-martialed for desertion.  这名士兵因逃兵被军事法庭审判。 |例句：The court-martial found him guilty. 军事法庭判定他有罪。|近义词：military trial, military court|反义词：civilian trial
