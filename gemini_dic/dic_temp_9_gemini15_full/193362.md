segregational:/ˌseɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/| adj.|隔离的；种族隔离的| Relating to or characterized by segregation; promoting or enforcing segregation.  The word describes something connected to the practice of separating people based on their race, ethnicity, religion, or other characteristics.|例句：The city's history is marred by its segregational housing policies. 这个城市的歷史因其隔离的住房政策而蒙上阴影。
例句：Segregational practices were once common in many parts of the United States. 种族隔离的做法曾经在美国许多地区很普遍。|近义词：separatist, discriminatory, exclusionary|反义词：integrative, inclusive, unified
