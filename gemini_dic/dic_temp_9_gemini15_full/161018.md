postcardiac: /ˌpoʊstˈkɑːrdiæk/ | adj. |术后心脏的；心脏手术后的| Postcardiac refers to the period or condition following a cardiac event, typically a heart surgery or a serious heart attack. It describes the time after the event when the patient is recovering and undergoing post-operative care or rehabilitation.  | Example: Postcardiac rehabilitation is crucial for patients to regain their strength and improve their heart function.  术后心脏康复对于患者恢复体力和改善心脏功能至关重要。|近义词: post-operative (in the context of heart surgery), postoperative cardiac |反义词: precardiac (before a cardiac event)
