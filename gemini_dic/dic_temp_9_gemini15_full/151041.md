paraphrase:/ˈpærəfreɪz/| vt. n.|释义，改写；解释|to express the meaning of (something written or spoken) using different words, especially to achieve greater clarity.|例句：Can you paraphrase this sentence for me?  你能帮我改写一下这句话吗？|近义词：rephrase, restate, summarize|反义词：quote, repeat verbatim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paraphrases the article every day. = 他每天都释义这篇文章。
She paraphrases difficult texts for her students. = 她为学生们释义难懂的课文。


一般过去时:
The student paraphrased the poem beautifully. = 这位学生精彩地改写了这首诗。
I paraphrased the professor's lecture notes. = 我对教授的讲课笔记做了释义。


现在完成时:
She has paraphrased several chapters of the book. = 她已经改写了这本书的几章。
He has paraphrased the main points of the argument. = 他已经对论点的要点做了释义。


过去完成时:
They had paraphrased the key findings before submitting the report. = 他们在提交报告之前已经对主要发现做了释义。
She had paraphrased the original text several times. = 她已经多次改写了原文。


将来时:
I will paraphrase this complex sentence for you. = 我会帮你改写这个复杂的句子。
He will paraphrase the speech for the audience. = 他会为听众们释义演讲。


虚拟语气:
If I understood the passage better, I could paraphrase it easily. = 如果我更好地理解了这篇文章，我就能轻松地改写它。
I wish I could paraphrase this legal document effectively. = 我希望我能有效地改写这份法律文件。


被动语态:
The poem was paraphrased by several students. = 这首诗被几个学生改写了。
The author's ideas were paraphrased in the review. = 作者的观点在评论中被改写了。


疑问句:
How would you paraphrase this complex idea? = 你会如何改写这个复杂的想法？
Can you paraphrase that sentence in simpler terms? = 你能用更简单的词语改写那个句子吗？


否定句:
He did not paraphrase the quote correctly. = 他没有正确地改写引语。
I couldn't paraphrase the technical jargon. = 我无法改写这些技术术语。

PHRASE->

paraphrase in one's own words = 用自己的话来解释
accurate paraphrase = 准确的释义
close paraphrase = 接近原文的释义
free paraphrase = 自由释义
