renunciant:/rɪˈnʌnsiənt/| n. |放弃者；弃权者；脱俗者|A person who has renounced something, typically worldly possessions or ambitions.  A renunciant is someone who has given up material possessions and worldly attachments in pursuit of spiritual enlightenment or a higher purpose. They often live a simple life, dedicating themselves to religious or philosophical ideals.|Example:  He lived as a renunciant, giving away all his wealth to the poor. 他过着放弃尘世享乐的生活，把所有财富都捐给了穷人。|近义词：ascetic, hermit, recluse |反义词：materialist, hedonist, worldling |名词
