rechallenging:/ˌriːˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 再挑战的；重新挑战的 |  Describes something that presents a challenge again, or something that is challenging in a renewed or repeated way.  It implies a previous challenge has already been addressed, and now a similar or related challenge has reappeared. |例句：The team faced a rechallenging situation when the market shifted unexpectedly.  团队在市场意外变化时面临着重新挑战的局面。


The company's rechallenging its previous marketing strategy. 公司正在重新挑战其之前的营销策略。| 近义词：re-examining, retesting, reconsidering | 反义词：unchallenging, easy, simple |  词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is rechallenging our assumptions. = 这个项目正在重新挑战我们的假设。
This new data is rechallenging our previous conclusions. = 这些新的数据正在重新挑战我们之前的结论。

一般过去时:
The unexpected results rechallenged the team's hypothesis. = 意想不到的结果重新挑战了团队的假设。
The crisis rechallenged the government's economic policies. = 危机重新挑战了政府的经济政策。

现在完成时:
The recent events have rechallenged the stability of the region. = 最近发生的事件已经重新挑战了该地区的稳定性。
Repeated failures have rechallenged our confidence in the project. = 重复的失败已经重新挑战了我们对项目的信心。

过去完成时:
They had rechallenged the rules before the meeting even started. = 他们在会议甚至开始之前就已经重新挑战了规则。
The new evidence had rechallenged the existing theory. = 新的证据已经重新挑战了现有的理论。

将来时:
The upcoming election will rechallenge the incumbent's authority. = 即将到来的选举将重新挑战现任者的权威。
Technological advancements will rechallenge the traditional business models. = 技术进步将重新挑战传统的商业模式。

虚拟语气:
If we were to proceed, it would rechallenge our resource allocation. = 如果我们继续进行，这将重新挑战我们的资源分配。
I wish the new evidence hadn't rechallenged our initial findings. = 我希望新的证据没有重新挑战我们的初步发现。

被动语态:
The company's strategy was rechallenged by the economic downturn. = 公司的战略被经济低迷重新挑战了。
Our assumptions are being rechallenged by the latest research. = 我们的假设正在被最新的研究重新挑战。

疑问句:
Does this new information rechallenge our understanding of the problem? = 这些新信息是否重新挑战了我们对这个问题的理解？
Will this new technology rechallenge the existing industry standards? = 这项新技术会重新挑战现有的行业标准吗？

否定句:
The new evidence did not rechallenge the established theory. = 新证据并没有重新挑战既定的理论。
The company's marketing campaign did not rechallenge the market leader's dominance. = 公司的营销活动并没有重新挑战市场领导者的主导地位。
