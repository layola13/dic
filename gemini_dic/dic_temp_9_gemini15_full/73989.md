fortresses:/ˈfɔːtrəsɪz/| n.|要塞；堡垒；坚固的防御工事|A fortress is a strong building or place that is built to be defended against attack.  It is typically a large structure with strong walls and other defensive features.  The term often implies a place of significant military or strategic importance.|例句：The medieval city was surrounded by imposing fortresses.  中世纪城市被雄伟的要塞包围着。

例句：The army established a series of fortresses along the border.  军队沿着边境建立了一系列要塞。


近义词：stronghold, citadel, rampart, fortification

反义词：vulnerability, weakness

词性：名词
