remortgages:/ˌriːˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɪz/| v.|再次抵押，二次抵押|To remortgage is to take out a new mortgage on a property that is already mortgaged, often to access equity or to secure a better interest rate.|例句：They remortgaged their house to consolidate their debts. 他们再次抵押房屋以整合债务。

例句：We remortgaged to get a lower interest rate on our loan. 我们再次抵押以获得更低的贷款利率。

近义词： refinance, re-mortgage

反义词：None

词性: Verb (动词)
