papad:/pəˈpɑːd/|/ˈpæpɑːd/| n. |薄饼，油炸薄饼 |A papad is a thin, crispy flatbread, often made from lentils, rice, or other pulses. It is a popular snack or accompaniment to meals in many parts of India and South Asia.  | Example:  I love to eat papadums with my curry. 我喜欢吃咖喱配薄饼。 She served papads as a starter. 她上薄饼作为开胃菜。 |近义词：cracker, crispbread |反义词：None  |名词
