counterproject:/ˈkaʊntɚprɑːdʒɛkt/ | n. | 反计划；对抗性项目 | A project designed to counteract or oppose another project.  It often involves a competing initiative or a response to a negative action. | Example: The company launched a counterproject to address the competitor's new product line.  这家公司启动了一个反计划来应对竞争对手的新产品线。|近义词：countermeasure, opposition project, rival project | 反义词：supportive project, collaborative project | 名词
