kulfi:/ˈkʊlfiː/| n.|库尔菲（印度甜点）|A kulfi is a traditional Indian frozen dessert similar to ice cream, but denser and often flavored with nuts, cardamom, or other spices.  It is typically made with milk, sugar, and flavorings, and sometimes contains nuts or dried fruits.| 例句：I enjoyed the mango kulfi after dinner.  晚饭后我享用了一份芒果库尔菲。|近义词：ice cream, gelato|反义词：无
