non-contradiction:/ˌnɒnˌkɒntrəˈdɪkʃən/| n. |非矛盾；一致性|The principle of non-contradiction states that something cannot be both true and false at the same time and in the same respect.  非矛盾律指出，同一事物在同一时间和同一方面不可能既是真又是假。|例句：The two statements are in non-contradiction; they support each other. 这两个陈述并不矛盾；它们互相支持。

例句：The theory's internal non-contradiction is a key element of its validity.  该理论的内在一致性是其有效性的关键因素。

近义词：consistency, agreement, accord |反义词：contradiction, inconsistency, conflict |词性:noun
