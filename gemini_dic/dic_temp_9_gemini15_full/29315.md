casters:/ˈkæstərz/| n.|轮子，脚轮|Small wheels attached to the bottom of a piece of furniture or equipment, allowing it to be moved easily.|例句：The casters on the filing cabinet made it easy to move.  档案柜上的脚轮使它很容易移动。

例句：We need to replace the worn-out casters on the shopping cart. 我们需要更换购物车上磨损的脚轮。


近义词：wheels, rollers|反义词：None|词性:noun
