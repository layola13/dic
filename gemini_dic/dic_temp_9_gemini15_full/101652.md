irregular:/ɪˈreɡjələr/ | adj. | 不规则的，无规律的 | Not conforming to a rule or standard; not regular or uniform.  The word describes something that deviates from the usual or expected pattern. | Example:  The irregular verb "go" doesn't follow the standard conjugation pattern.  不规则动词“go”不遵循标准的词形变化模式。|近义词: abnormal, atypical, erratic, uneven |反义词: regular, normal, standard, consistent
