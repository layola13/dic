quadrennium:/kwɑːdˈrɛniəm/| n. |四年期；四年周期|A period of four years.  It is often used to refer to a four-year period in office, such as a presidential term or an Olympic cycle. |The quadrennium between the two Olympics saw significant advancements in the sport.|近义词：four-year period, tetrad|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with shorter or longer periods.
