creative:/krɪˈeɪtɪv/| adj.|创造性的；有创意的；富有创造力的|Having the ability or power to create. Showing imagination or skill in creating something new.  |例句：She's a very creative writer. 她是一位很有创造力的作家。

例句：His creative designs won him many awards. 他的创意设计为他赢得了许多奖项。


近义词：inventive, imaginative, innovative, original|反义词：unimaginative, uninspired, conventional


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is creative in his approach to problem-solving. = 他在解决问题的方法上很有创造力。
The team is creative and always comes up with new ideas. = 这个团队很有创造力，总是能想出新主意。


一般过去时:
She was creative in her use of color and texture. = 她对色彩和肌理的运用很有创意。
The artist was very creative when he painted that masterpiece. = 那位艺术家在创作那幅杰作时非常有创造力。


现在完成时:
They have been creative in their marketing campaigns. = 他们的市场营销活动一直很有创意。
She has creatively solved many complex problems. = 她已经创造性地解决了許多复杂的问题。


过去完成时:
He had been creative in his earlier works, but he seemed to have lost his touch. = 他早期的作品很有创意，但似乎已经江郎才尽了。
The company had been creatively bankrupt before the new CEO arrived. = 在新任CEO到来之前，公司在创意上已经破产了。


将来时:
They will be creative in designing the new product line. = 他们将在设计新的产品线时富有创意。
We will creatively address the challenges facing our community. = 我们将以富有创造性的方式解决我们社区面临的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If I were more creative, I could design a better website. = 如果我更有创造力，我就能设计一个更好的网站。
I wish I were as creative as Leonardo da Vinci. = 我真希望自己像达芬奇一样有创造力。


被动语态:
The design was creatively conceived and executed. = 这个设计构思巧妙，执行到位。
The problem was creatively solved by the team. = 这个问题由团队创造性地解决了。


疑问句:
Is your approach to this problem creative? = 你解决这个问题的方法有创意吗？
Are you feeling creative today? = 你今天感觉有创意吗？


否定句:
His work isn't very creative. = 他的作品不是很有创意。
The presentation was not creative at all. = 这个演示一点也不具有创意。
