gauntlet:/ˈɡɔːntlɪt/| n.|  挑战；手套；金属护手| A gauntlet is a challenge or an ordeal.  It can also refer to a type of glove, especially a medieval armored glove, or a series of challenges or obstacles. |例句：He threw down the gauntlet and challenged his rival to a duel. 他扔下手套，向他的对手挑战决斗。

He had to run the gauntlet of reporters. 他不得不面对记者的盘问。

近义词：challenge, ordeal, glove |反义词：acceptance, surrender
