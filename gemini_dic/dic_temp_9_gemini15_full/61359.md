embezzle:/ɪmˈbezəl/| vt.| 挪用；盗用；侵吞 | To embezzle means to steal money that you have been trusted to look after, usually while working for an organization or company.  | The accountant was found guilty of embezzling thousands of dollars from the company. 会计被发现犯有侵吞公司数千美元的罪行。 | 近义词：misappropriate, defraud, pilfer, steal | 反义词：contribute, donate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He embezzles funds from the charity. 他挪用慈善机构的资金。
The company regularly audits its accounts to prevent embezzlement. 公司定期审计账目以防止挪用公款。


一般过去时:
She embezzled a large sum of money from her employer. 她挪用了雇主一大笔钱。
The CEO embezzled millions before fleeing the country. 首席执行官携巨款潜逃。


现在完成时:
They have embezzled millions of dollars over the past decade. 过去十年他们挪用了数百万美元。
The investigation revealed that he had embezzled company funds for years. 调查显示，他多年来一直在挪用公司资金。


过去完成时:
The auditors discovered that he had embezzled the money before the audit began. 审计人员发现，他在审计开始之前就挪用了这笔钱。
Before the scandal broke, she had embezzled a significant portion of the company's assets. 在丑闻爆发之前，她已经挪用了公司相当一部分资产。


将来时:
He will embezzle more money if he isn't caught. 如果他不被抓住，他会挪用更多钱。
The authorities believe he will attempt to embezzle funds again. 当局认为他会再次企图挪用资金。


虚拟语气:
If he had not embezzled the money, he wouldn't be in jail. 如果他没有挪用这笔钱，他就不会坐牢。
I wish he hadn't embezzled the funds from the orphanage. 我真希望他没有挪用孤儿院的资金。


被动语态:
Millions of dollars were embezzled from the bank. 数百万美元被从银行挪用。
The money was embezzled by a trusted employee. 这笔钱是被一位值得信赖的员工挪用的。


疑问句:
Did he embezzle the money? 他挪用了这笔钱吗？
Was the money embezzled by a single person or a group? 这笔钱是被一个人还是一群人挪用的？


否定句:
He did not embezzle the company's funds. 他没有挪用公司的资金。
She denied embezzling the money. 她否认挪用了这笔钱。
