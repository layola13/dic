minsters:/ˈmɪnɪstərz/| n. |部长们，牧师们 |Ministers are government officials responsible for specific departments.  |例句：The ministers met to discuss the new policy. 部长们会面讨论新政策。
例句：Several ministers resigned in protest.  几位部长辞职以示抗议。|近义词：officials, pastors, clergymen |反义词：None directly applicable.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ministers meet every Tuesday. = 部长们每周二开会。
The ministers oversee their respective departments. = 部长们负责各自的部门。


一般过去时:
The ministers met yesterday to discuss the budget. = 部长们昨天开会讨论预算。
The ministers approved the new law last week. = 部长们上周批准了新法律。


现在完成时:

虚拟语气:
