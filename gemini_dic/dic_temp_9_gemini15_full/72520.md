flushboard:/ˈflʌʃbɔːrd/ | n. | 护墙板；冲洗板 | A flushboard is a flat piece of material, often wood or plastic, used to cover a surface, typically flush with the surrounding area.  It's used in various applications, from creating a smooth wall finish to constructing temporary dams. | Example: The carpenter installed flushboards to create a seamless wall surface.  木匠安装了护墙板以创造一个无缝的墙面。  The engineers used flushboards to temporarily dam the river. 工程师们用护墙板临时堵住河流。 | 近义词：panel, board, covering | 反义词：opening, gap
