harvesters:/ˈhɑːrvɪstərz/ | n. |收割机；收获者 |Those who or that which harvest.  Machines or people who gather crops. |例句：The farmers used powerful harvesters to gather the wheat.  农民们用强大的收割机来收割小麦。

The new harvesters are more efficient than the old ones. 新的收割机比旧的效率更高。|近义词： reapers, gatherers |反义词：planters (in the context of planting crops)
