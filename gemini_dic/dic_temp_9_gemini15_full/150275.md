panegyric:/pænəˈdʒɪrɪk/| n. |颂词；赞歌；赞美；|A formal expression of praise, typically one delivered publicly; a eulogy.  It's often elaborate and enthusiastic in tone.|The professor delivered a panegyric on the achievements of the Nobel laureate. 教授发表了一篇颂词，赞扬诺贝尔奖获得者的成就。| eulogy, encomium, tribute, laudation |criticism, condemnation, denunciation |名词
