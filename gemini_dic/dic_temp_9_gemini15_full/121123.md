misdeclaring:/ˌmɪs.dɪˈklɛərɪŋ/ | v. | 错报；错误声明 | To state something incorrectly or falsely, often deliberately.  This involves giving a wrong or misleading account of something. |例句：The company was accused of misdeclaring its profits.  公司被指控虚报利润。

例句：He misdeclared his income to avoid paying higher taxes. 他谎报收入以避免支付更高的税款。


近义词：misrepresenting, falsifying, misreporting, distorting


反义词：declaring truthfully, accurately reporting, stating correctly


词性：动词
