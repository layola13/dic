pong:/pɒŋ/| n. |乒乓球；砰的一声|The sound of a ball hitting something, especially in games like ping-pong.  It can also refer to the game itself. |例句：I heard a loud pong as the ball hit the wall.  我听到球击中墙壁时发出一声巨响。|例句： We played a game of pong after dinner. 晚饭后我们玩了一局乒乓球。|近义词：ping, thud, whack|反义词：silence
