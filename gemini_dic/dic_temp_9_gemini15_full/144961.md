/ˌoʊvərəˈbjuːzɪŋ/ | v. |过度使用，滥用 |The act of using something excessively or improperly, often to the point of harm or damage.  It implies a degree of misuse beyond simple overuse. |例句：Overabusing prescription drugs can lead to serious health consequences.  过度服用处方药会导致严重的健康后果。

例句：The company was accused of overabusing its market power. 这家公司被指控滥用其市场支配地位。

反义词：conserving, preserving, protecting, underusing
