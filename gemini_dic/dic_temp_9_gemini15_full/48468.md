deen:/diːn/| n.|教长；院长；主任；（伊斯兰教）宗教领袖|The word "deen" is primarily used in Islam to refer to the Islamic faith or way of life.  It encompasses all aspects of life, including religious beliefs, practices, and social interactions, guided by the Quran and Sunnah (prophetic traditions). It can also be used more generally to refer to a religious faith or system of belief, though its most common usage is within an Islamic context. |例句：He is a respected dean of the faculty of arts. 他是文学院一位受人尊敬的院长。|近义词：rector, head, leader, faith|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms implying secularism or irreligion.
