cogitating:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪtɪŋ/ | v. |沉思，思考 | To think deeply and carefully; to ponder.  | Example:  He sat cogitating on the problem for hours. 他坐了几个小时思考这个问题。|近义词：pondering, meditating, contemplating, reflecting | 反义词：ignoring, disregarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cogitating on his next move. = 他正在思考他的下一步行动。
She cogitates deeply before making decisions. = 她做决定前都会深思熟虑。

一般过去时:
I cogitated over the implications of the news. = 我仔细考虑了这个消息的含义。
They cogitated for hours, but reached no conclusion. = 他们思考了几个小时，但没有得出结论。

现在完成时:
He has been cogitating on the matter for days. = 他几天来一直在思考这件事。
She has cogitated long and hard about her future. = 她对自己的未来进行了长时间的认真思考。

过去完成时:
I had cogitated for weeks before finally deciding. = 在最终决定之前，我已经思考了好几个星期。
They had cogitated extensively before presenting their proposal. = 他们在提交提案之前进行了广泛的思考。

将来时:
I will be cogitating on this proposal all weekend. = 我整个周末都会思考这个提案。
She will cogitate carefully before responding. = 她会在回应之前仔细思考。

虚拟语气:
If I had cogitated more carefully, I would have made a better decision. = 如果我当时考虑得更仔细一些，我就会做出更好的决定。
I wish I had cogitated more before speaking. = 我希望我在说话之前考虑得更多。

被动语态:
The problem was cogitated upon by the committee. = 委员会对这个问题进行了仔细考虑。
The matter is being cogitated over by the experts. = 专家们正在仔细考虑这件事。

疑问句:
Are you cogitating on anything important? = 你在思考什么重要的事情吗？
What are they cogitating about at the moment? = 他们现在正在思考什么？

否定句:
He wasn't cogitating at all; he was just daydreaming. = 他根本没有在思考；他只是在做白日梦。
She didn't cogitate on the consequences of her actions. = 她没有考虑自己行为的后果。
