lankness: /ˈlæŋknəs/ | n. |空虚；缺乏；单调乏味 | A state or quality of being empty, vacant, or lacking in substance or interest.  It often implies a feeling of emptiness or hollowness, both physically and emotionally. |


例句：

The lankness of the landscape was unsettling.  =  这片景观的空旷令人不安。

The lankness of her explanation left many questions unanswered. = 她解释的空洞使得许多问题没有得到解答。


近义词：emptiness, hollowness, barrenness, void, vacuity

反义词：fullness, richness, abundance, repletion


词性：名词
