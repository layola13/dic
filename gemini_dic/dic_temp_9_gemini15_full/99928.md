internet:/ˈɪntərˌnɛt/| n. |互联网，因特网 |A global system of interconnected computer networks that use the standard Internet protocol suite (TCP/IP) to serve billions of users worldwide.  It is a network of networks that consists of millions of private, public, academic, business, and government networks of local to global scope, linked by a broad array of electronic, wireless, and optical networking technologies.|例句：I checked the news on the internet this morning. 我今天早上在网上查看了新闻。
例句：The internet has revolutionized communication. 互联网彻底改变了通讯方式。|近义词：web, online world, cyberspace |反义词：offline, disconnected
