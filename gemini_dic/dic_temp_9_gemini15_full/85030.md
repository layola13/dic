harbinger:/ˈhɑːrbɪndʒər/| n. |先兆，预兆；先驱|A harbinger is a person or thing that announces or signals the approach of someone or something else.|The dark clouds were a harbinger of the storm.  乌云是暴风雨的先兆。|The robin is a harbinger of spring. 知更鸟是春天的先兆。|forerunner, precursor, omen, portent|反义词较少，可考虑用表示相反含义的词语，例如：consequence (结果), aftermath (后果)|名词
