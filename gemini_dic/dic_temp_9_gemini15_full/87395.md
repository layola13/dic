herrerasaurus:/ˌhɛrəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |赫氏水龍獸|An extinct genus of early archosaur from the Late Triassic period (Carnian stage) of Argentina. It's considered one of the earliest known dinosaurs, known for its relatively small size, bipedal locomotion, and sharp teeth, suggesting a carnivorous diet.  |The Herrerasaurus was a fearsome predator for its time.|近义词：Staurikosaurus (similarly early dinosaur)|反义词：None (no direct antonym, as it refers to a specific extinct species)|名词
