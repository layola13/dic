butterbump:/ˈbʌtəbʌmp/| n.|田凫（一种水鸟）|A butterbump is a common name for the water rail (Rallus aquaticus), a small, secretive marsh bird.  It's known for its distinctive, booming call. |Example: The butterbump's call echoed across the marsh. 田凫的叫声在沼泽地回荡。|近义词：water rail|反义词：无
