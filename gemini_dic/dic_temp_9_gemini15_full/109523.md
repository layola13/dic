lesioned: /ˈliʒənd/ | adj. | 受损的；患病的 |  Damaged or injured; affected by a lesion (an area of tissue damaged by injury or disease).  | 例句：The lesioned area of the brain showed significant atrophy.  = 受损的大脑区域显示出明显的萎缩。

The patient presented with a lesioned nerve in his left arm. = 病人左臂出现神经受损的情况。


近义词：injured, damaged, impaired, diseased, affected

反义词：healthy, undamaged, intact, sound


词性：形容词
