imminent:/ˈɪmɪnənt/| adj.|即将发生的；迫近的；逼近的|  About to happen; likely to occur at any moment.  The word suggests a sense of urgency and nearness. |例句：The storm is imminent; we should seek shelter immediately.  暴风雨即将来临；我们应该立即寻求庇护。

例句：With the imminent deadline, she worked late into the night.  由于迫在眉睫的截止日期，她工作到深夜。


近义词： impending, approaching, forthcoming, threatening, near, close at hand

反义词： distant, remote, far off, unlikely


词性：形容词
