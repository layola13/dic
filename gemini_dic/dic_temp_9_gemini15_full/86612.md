heliotroper:/ˈhiːliəʊtroʊpər/|/ˈhɛliəʊtroʊpər/| n. |向日葵；日光追踪器；天芥菜属植物|A heliotroper is a plant that turns its flowers or leaves to follow the sun throughout the day.  It can also refer to a device or system that tracks the sun.  The term derives from the Greek words "helios" (sun) and "tropos" (turn).|The sunflower is a well-known heliotroper.|近义词：sun-follower|反义词：无
