monolatry:/ˈmɒnələtri/| n. |一神崇拜；单一神灵崇拜 |The worship of a single god, in contrast to polytheism.  Monolatry differs from monotheism in that while monolatrists worship only one god, they acknowledge the existence of other gods. |Example:  Some scholars argue that ancient Israelite religion was initially a form of monolatry before evolving into strict monotheism.  一些学者认为，古代以色列的宗教最初是一种一神崇拜，后来才发展成为严格的一神教。|近义词：henotheism |反义词：polytheism
