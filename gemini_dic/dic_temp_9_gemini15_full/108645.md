leafage:/ˈliːfɪdʒ/| n.| 叶子；叶丛；枝叶 |The mass of leaves of a plant or collection of plants.  It refers to the overall abundance or appearance of leaves. |例句：The leafage of the maple tree turned vibrant red in autumn.  秋天的枫叶变得鲜红。

The dense leafage blocked the sunlight.  茂密的树叶遮挡了阳光。


近义词：foliage, leaves


反义词：barrenness (缺乏植被)


词性：名词

词源:  From Middle English *leefage, from Old French fueillage ("foliage"), from feuille ("leaf").  Ultimately from Latin folium ("leaf").
