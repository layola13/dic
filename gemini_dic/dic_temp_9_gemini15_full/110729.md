lineup:/ˈlaɪnʌp/| n. |阵容；一排；行列；（嫌疑犯的）列队；（为识别而）排队 |A lineup is a group of people or things arranged in a row.  It can refer to a group of performers, suspects in a police investigation, or items arranged for display or selection. |例句：The concert featured a stellar lineup of musicians.  这场音乐会汇聚了一批杰出的音乐家。

The police showed the witness a lineup of suspects.  警方给目击者看了一排嫌疑犯。


近义词：array, arrangement, formation, roster


反义词：disarray, disorder


词性：名词
