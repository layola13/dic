nonfuels:/ˈnɑːnˌfjuːəlz/ | n. |非燃料 |Substances or energy sources that are not used as fuel.  These materials might be used in other industrial processes or have other applications, but they are not burned for energy. |Example:  The company is exploring the use of nonfuels in its manufacturing process. 这家公司正在探索在其制造过程中使用非燃料。|近义词:non-combustibles, alternative resources |反义词:fuels
