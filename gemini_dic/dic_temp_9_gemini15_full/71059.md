first-time:/ˌfɜːrstˈtaɪm/ | adj. |第一次的，首次的 |  Describes something happening or being done for the very first time.  |例句：This is my first-time trying this recipe. 这是我第一次尝试这个食谱。
例句：It was a first-time experience for all of us. 对我们所有人来说都是第一次的经历。|近义词：initial, maiden, inaugural | 反义词：repeated, subsequent, veteran | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The first-time user gets a free trial. = 首次用户可获得免费试用。
This is a first-time experience for me. = 这对我来说是第一次的体验。


一般过去时:
It was my first-time skiing. = 那是我第一次滑雪。
He had a first-time encounter with the wild animals. = 他第一次与野生动物相遇。


现在完成时:
This is the first time I have ever seen snow. = 这是我第一次看到雪。
She has never had a first-time failure in her career. = 她职业生涯中从未有过第一次失败。


过去完成时:
It was the first time I had ever been to Europe. = 那是我第一次去欧洲。
By then, he had already had his first-time business success. = 到那时，他已经取得了他的第一次事业成功。


将来时:
This will be my first-time attending a conference. = 这将是我第一次参加会议。
Next year, she will have her first-time solo exhibition. = 明年，她将举办她的第一次个人展览。


虚拟语气:
If it were my first-time, I would be nervous. = 如果这是我的第一次，我会很紧张。
I wish it were my first-time so I could experience the excitement again. = 我希望这是我的第一次，这样我就能再次体验兴奋了。


被动语态:
The first-time users were given a detailed introduction. = 首次用户得到了详细的介绍。
The new product was unveiled for the first time at the conference. = 新产品在会议上首次亮相。


疑问句:
Is this your first-time here? = 这是你第一次来这里吗？
Was that his first-time flying? = 那是不是他第一次飞行？


否定句:
It wasn't my first-time doing that. = 那不是我第一次做那样的事。
This is not her first-time encountering this problem. = 这不是她第一次遇到这个问题。
