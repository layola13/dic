homestays:/ˈhoʊmˌsteɪz/ | n. |寄宿家庭；家庭寄宿 |A homestay is an arrangement where a visitor stays in a local family's home, rather than a hotel or other commercial accommodation.  It offers a more immersive cultural experience. | Example:  She booked a homestay in a rural village to experience authentic local life. = 她预订了一个乡村的寄宿家庭，以体验真实的当地生活。

My family offered homestays to international students during summer. = 我家在暑假期间为国际学生提供寄宿家庭服务。

近义词：home stay, family stay | 反义词：hotel stay, commercial accommodation
