fountainlife:/ˈfaʊntənlaɪf/| n. |喷泉般的生活；充沛的生命力|A metaphorical term referring to a life full of abundance, joy, and vitality, like a continuously flowing fountain.  It often implies a life of continuous renewal and positive energy.|Example:  She lived a fountainlife, always brimming with enthusiasm and new projects. 她过着喷泉般的生活，总是充满热情和新的项目。|近义词：abundant life, vibrant life, flourishing life|反义词：stagnant life, bleak life, dreary life
