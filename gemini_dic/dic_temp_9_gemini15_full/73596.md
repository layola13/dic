forethought:/ˈfɔːrθɔːt/| n. |预见；深思熟虑；先见之明 |Careful consideration of what will happen in the future and planning for it.  It involves anticipating potential problems and opportunities and making decisions accordingly. |例句：The project's success was due in large part to the forethought of its planners.  该项目的成功很大程度上归功于其策划者的深思熟虑。

例句：With more forethought, we could have avoided this disaster.  如果事先多加考虑，我们就能避免这场灾难。


近义词：premeditation, planning, consideration, foresight, prevision

反义词：impulsiveness, recklessness, carelessness, shortsightedness
