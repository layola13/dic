feminism:/ˈfɛmɪnɪzəm/| n. |女权主义；女性主义|The advocacy of women's rights on the grounds of political, social, and economic equality to men.  Feminism encompasses a range of social theories, political movements, and moral philosophies.  It is a complex and diverse field, with various schools of thought and approaches.  |例句：The feminist movement has made significant strides in achieving gender equality. 女权运动在实现性别平等方面取得了重大进展。|例句：Her commitment to feminism is evident in her work advocating for women's rights. 她对女权主义的承诺在她为争取妇女权利的工作中显而易见。|近义词：women's liberation, gender equality, women's rights movement |反义词：sexism, misogyny, patriarchy |名词
