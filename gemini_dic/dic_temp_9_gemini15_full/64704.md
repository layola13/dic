It can also refer to a military defensive work consisting of a steep slope or scarp. |例句：The hikers carefully descended the steep escarpment. 登山者小心翼翼地下了陡峭的悬崖。  The ancient city was protected by a high escarpment.  这座古城受到高耸的悬崖的保护。|近义词：cliff, precipice, bluff, slope |反义词：plain, level ground |名词
