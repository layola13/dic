prelunch: /ˈpriːlʌntʃ/ | adj. |午餐前的；午饭之前的| Relating to the period of time before lunch.  It is typically used to describe activities, events, or feelings that occur before lunch.|例句：We had a prelunch meeting to discuss the project. 我们在午餐前开会讨论了项目。

例句：The prelunch atmosphere was tense due to the impending deadline. 由于迫在眉睫的最后期限，午餐前的氛围很紧张。


近义词：anteprandial, before-lunch


反义词：postlunch, after-lunch


词性：形容词
