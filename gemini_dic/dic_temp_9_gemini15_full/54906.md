disposable: /dɪˈspoʊzəbəl/ | adj. | 可丢弃的；一次性的 |  Something that is disposable is designed to be thrown away after use.  It is often made of inexpensive materials and is not intended for reuse. |

例句：
Disposable diapers are convenient for parents. = 一次性尿布对父母来说很方便。

Disposable cameras are easy to use and inexpensive. = 相机易于使用且价格便宜。


近义词：throwaway, single-use, expendable

反义词：reusable, durable, permanent

词性：形容词
