emmetropia:/ˌɛmɪˈtroʊpiə/| n. |正视，正常视力|The state of vision in which a faraway object is clearly seen without the aid of corrective lenses.  This is due to the eye's refractive power being perfectly balanced to focus light onto the retina.  |例句：She enjoys emmetropia and doesn't need glasses. 她拥有正常的视力，不需要戴眼镜。
例句：The optometrist confirmed his emmetropia after a thorough eye exam. 眼科医生在彻底的眼科检查后证实了他的视力正常。|近义词：normal vision, clear vision |反义词：ametropia, myopia, hyperopia
