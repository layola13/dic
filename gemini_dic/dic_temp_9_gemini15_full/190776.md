savor:/ˈseɪvər/| vt. n.|品味；尽情享受；回味|To taste or smell something with great pleasure; to enjoy something to the full.|例句：I savored every bite of the delicious cake. 我细细品味了美味蛋糕的每一口。|例句：She savored the moment of victory. 她尽情享受胜利的时刻。|近义词：enjoy, relish, appreciate|反义词：reject, despise|动词，名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He savors every sip of his coffee. = 他细细品味每一口咖啡。
They savor the quiet moments together. = 他们享受在一起的宁静时光。

一般过去时:
I savored the aroma of freshly baked bread. = 我回味着新鲜烘焙面包的香味。
She savored the memory of her childhood. = 她回味着童年时光的回忆。

现在完成时:
We have savored many wonderful meals in this restaurant. = 我们在这家餐厅享用过许多美味佳肴。
He has savored the fruits of his labor. = 他已经享受了他劳动成果。

过去完成时:
They had savored the peace and quiet before the storm broke. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们享受着平静与安宁。
She had savored the victory for a long time before sharing the news. = 在分享这个消息之前，她已经久久回味着胜利的喜悦。

We will savor the wine after dinner. = 晚饭后我们将细细品味这瓶酒。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would savor this beautiful sunset. = 如果我有更多时间，我会好好欣赏这美丽的日落。
I wish I could savor the taste of that delicious food again. = 我希望我能再次品尝那美味佳肴的味道。

被动语态:
