goodo:/ˈɡʊdoʊ/| interj.|好极了，很好|An informal expression of approval or agreement, similar to "good" or "okay".  It is often used in a slightly playful or enthusiastic way.|例句： "Goodo, let's do it!" = "好极了，咱们做吧！" "Goodo, I'll see you later." = "很好，待会儿见。" |近义词：good, okay, great, alright|反义词:bad, terrible, awful


USAGE->
This word is an interjection, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what". It's primarily used as an exclamation.  There's no tense to conjugate.

There are no common phrases specifically using "goodo".  Its usage is largely standalone.
