flexing:/ˈflɛksɪŋ/| v.|弯曲；炫耀；运用；发挥；|The act of bending or being bent, or the act of showing off or using something to advantage.|例句：He was flexing his biceps. 他正在炫耀他的二头肌。
例句：The company is flexing its muscles in the market. 公司正在市场上展现实力。|近义词：bending, showing off, displaying, exercising|反义词：straightening, hiding, concealing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flexes his muscles every day. = 他每天都锻炼肌肉。
She flexes her creative skills in her artwork. = 她在她作品中展现她的创造力。

一般过去时:
He flexed his muscles to impress her. = 他炫耀肌肉以给她留下深刻印象。
The company flexed its financial power to acquire the competitor. = 公司运用其强大的财务实力收购了竞争对手。

现在完成时:
She has flexed her political influence to get the bill passed. = 她运用她的政治影响力促使法案通过。
He has flexed his muscles so much that they are sore. = 他过度锻炼肌肉导致肌肉酸痛。

过去完成时:
They had flexed their muscles in previous negotiations. = 他们在之前的谈判中已经展现了实力。
She had flexed her creative talents in several successful projects. = 她已经在几个成功的项目中展现了她的创造才能。


将来时:

The company will flex its marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. = 公司将调整其营销策略以覆盖更广泛的受众。


虚拟语气:
If he flexed his muscles more often, he would be stronger. = 如果他经常锻炼肌肉，他会更强壮。
If the company had flexed its marketing muscles earlier, they might have avoided the decline. = 如果公司早点运用其营销实力，他们或许可以避免衰退。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
