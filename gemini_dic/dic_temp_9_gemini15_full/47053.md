datary:/ˈdeɪtəri/ | n. |日期簿；日记簿；档案室 | A datary is a record keeper, or a place where records are kept, especially historical or official records.  It can refer to a person's personal diary or a formal archive. |例句：The monastery's datary held centuries of invaluable historical documents.  修道院的档案室保存着几个世纪以来极其宝贵的历史文献。

The papal datary was responsible for issuing papal documents. 教皇档案室负责发布教皇文件。|近义词：archive, registry, record office, diary |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to concepts like "forgetfulness" or "disorganization". |名词
