proffering:/ˈprɒfərɪŋ/ | n. | 提供；提出；贡献 | The act of offering something.  It implies a willingness to give or share something, often in a generous or helpful way. |例句：The company's proffering of new benefits attracted many job applicants. 公司提供新的福利吸引了许多求职者。

例句：His proffering of assistance was greatly appreciated. 他主动提供帮助，深受感激。


近义词：offering, presentation, tendering | 反义词：withholding, refusing, denying | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is proffering new benefits. = 公司正在提供新的福利。
He proffers his services freely. = 他免费提供他的服务。


一般过去时:
She proffered a helpful suggestion. = 她提出了一个有帮助的建议。
They proffered their apologies. = 他们表达了歉意。


现在完成时:
The government has proffered little help to the victims. = 政府几乎没有向受害者提供帮助。
He has proffered his resignation. = 他已提出辞职。


过去完成时:
They had proffered their assistance before the disaster struck. = 在灾难发生之前，他们已经提供了帮助。
She had proffered several solutions, but none were satisfactory. = 她已经提出了几种解决方案，但没有一个令人满意。


将来时:
The company will proffer a new product line next year. = 公司明年将推出新的产品线。
He will proffer an explanation for his actions. = 他将解释他的行为。


虚拟语气:
If they proffered a reasonable compromise, we would accept. = 如果他们提出合理的妥协，我们会接受。
I wish they had proffered more support. = 我希望他们能提供更多支持。


被动语态:
New benefits were proffered to the employees. = 新的福利提供给了员工。
A solution was proffered by the committee. = 委员会提出了一项解决方案。


疑问句:
What assistance are they proffering? = 他们在提供什么帮助？
What solutions are being proffered to address this problem? = 正在提出什么解决方案来解决这个问题？


否定句:
The company did not proffer any explanation. = 公司没有给出任何解释。
He refused to proffer any help. = 他拒绝提供任何帮助。
