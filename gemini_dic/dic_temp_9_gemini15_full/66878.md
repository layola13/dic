expectoration:/ˌɛkspɛkˈtɔːreɪʃən/ | n. | 咳痰；痰 | The act of expelling mucus or phlegm from the lungs by coughing.  Expectoration can be a symptom of various respiratory illnesses. | Example: The doctor examined the patient's expectoration to diagnose the illness.  医生检查了病人的痰以诊断疾病。

The nurse noted the color and consistency of the patient's expectoration. 护士记录了病人痰的颜色和稠度。


近义词：sputum, phlegm | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词

词源:
From Latin *ex- "out" + pectus "breast, chest".  The word literally means "to cough out from the chest".
