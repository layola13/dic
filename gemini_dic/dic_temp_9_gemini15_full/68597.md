fantasised:/ˈfæntəsaɪzd/| v.|幻想；梦想；凭空想象|To have imagined or daydreamed about something.  It implies creating a mental image of something not real or not yet existing.|例句：She fantasised about winning the lottery. 她幻想中了彩票。

例句：He fantasised about a life of adventure. 他幻想过一种冒险的生活。


近义词：dreamed, imagined, daydreamed, envisioned

反义词：experienced, realised


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I fantasise about travelling the world. = 我幻想环游世界。
She fantasises about becoming a famous singer. = 她幻想成为一名著名的歌手。


一般过去时:
He fantasised about his childhood home. = 他幻想起了他的童年家园。
They fantasised about a life free from worry. = 他们幻想过一种无忧无虑的生活。


现在完成时:
I have fantasised about this moment for years. = 我多年来一直幻想这一刻。
She has fantasised about living in Paris. = 她一直幻想住在巴黎。


过去完成时:
He had fantasised about quitting his job for months. = 他已经幻想了几个月要辞职了。
They had fantasised about owning a yacht before they won the lottery. = 在他们中彩票之前，他们就幻想过拥有游艇。


将来时:
I will fantasise about my future success. = 我将会幻想我未来的成功。
She will fantasise about her wedding day. = 她将会幻想她的婚礼。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would fantasise more. = 如果我有时间，我会更多地幻想。
I wish I could fantasise more freely. = 我希望我能更自由地幻想。


被动语态:
(The passive voice is less common with this verb,  but it's possible in specific contexts):  His adventures were fantasised by many. = 许多人幻想过他的冒险经历。


疑问句:
What do you fantasise about? = 你在幻想什么？
Does she often fantasise about her future? = 她经常幻想她的未来吗？


否定句:
I don't fantasise very often. = 我不经常幻想。
He didn't fantasise about being rich. = 他不幻想自己富有。


PHRASE->
fantasise about = 幻想……
fantasise wildly = 疯狂地幻想
fantasise incessantly = 不停地幻想
live in a fantasy = 生活在幻想中
fantasise of = 幻想…… (less common than "fantasise about")
