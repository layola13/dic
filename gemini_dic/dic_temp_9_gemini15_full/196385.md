sermonizers:/ˈsɜːrmənˌaɪzərz/| n. |说教者；喜欢说教的人 |People who deliver sermons or who habitually moralize or preach.  They often offer unsolicited advice or opinions, sometimes in a tedious or tiresome manner. |例句：The sermonizers in the town square were constantly criticizing the youth's behavior. 镇广场上的说教者们不断批评青年的行为。

例句：Some find the constant sermonizers on social media exhausting. 社交媒体上那些不断说教的人让一些人感到精疲力尽。


近义词：preachers, moralizers, lecturers, pedagogues, didacts


反义词：listeners,  attentive audience (反义词更侧重于相反的行为或态度)


词性：名词
