fumigator:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪtər/| n.|熏蒸者；消毒者；灭虫器|A person or thing that fumigates.  A fumigator is someone or something that uses fumigants to kill pests or disinfect an area.  It can refer to a person who performs fumigation or a machine used for the process.|例句：The fumigator treated the house for termites.  熏蒸者对房屋进行了白蚁防治。

The fumigator sprayed the insecticide throughout the building.  灭虫器在整个建筑物内喷洒杀虫剂。|近义词： disinfector, exterminator|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to introduce pests or contamination.
