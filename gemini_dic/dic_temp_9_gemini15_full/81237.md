grandstander:/ˈɡrændˌstændər/| n.|哗众取宠者，卖弄风骚者|A person who behaves in a showy way in order to attract attention and admiration, often without genuine skill or merit.  They often act in an ostentatious manner to impress others. |例句：He's such a grandstander; he only does things to impress others. 他是个十足的哗众取宠者，他做任何事都只是为了给他人留下印象。

例句：The politician's speech was nothing but a grandstanding performance aimed at winning votes.  这位政治家的演讲不过是为了赢得选票而进行的哗众取宠的表演。


近义词：show-off, attention-seeker, poseur, exhibitionist|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|名词
