must-see:/ˌmʌstˈsiː/| adj.|必看的，不容错过的|An adjective used to describe something that is highly recommended and should not be missed.  It implies something is of exceptional quality or importance.|例句：The Grand Canyon is a must-see destination.  大峡谷是必游景点。

例句：That new museum exhibit is a must-see for art lovers.  对于艺术爱好者来说，那个新的博物馆展览是必看的。


近义词：essential, indispensable, unmissable, highly recommended

反义词：unnecessary, avoidable, optional


词性:形容词
