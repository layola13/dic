horsemanship:/ˈhɔːsmənʃɪp/ | n. |马术；骑术；骑马技巧 |The skill and art of riding and managing horses.  It encompasses various aspects, including riding techniques, horse care, and understanding equine behavior. |例句：His horsemanship is truly remarkable; he handles the horse with such grace and precision.  他的马术真是非凡；他驾驭马匹如此优雅精准。

His horsemanship improved significantly after taking lessons from a renowned instructor.  在他从一位著名的教练那里学习之后，他的马术有了显著提高。
