devise:/dɪˈvaɪz/| vt.|设计；发明；想出|To plan or invent something, typically a device or system.|They devised a plan to escape.|近义词：invent, create, contrive, formulate|反义词：destroy, demolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engineers devise new solutions to complex problems. =工程师们设计出解决复杂问题的方案。
She devises innovative marketing strategies. = 她设计出创新的营销策略。

一般过去时:
They devised a clever strategy to win the game. = 他们设计了一个巧妙的策略来赢得比赛。
The team devised a new system for managing data. = 团队设计了一个新的数据管理系统。

现在完成时:
Scientists have devised new methods for treating the disease. = 科学家们已经设计出了治疗这种疾病的新方法。
She has devised a plan to improve her fitness. = 她已经制定了一个计划来改善她的健康状况。

过去完成时:
They had devised a plan to escape before the guards arrived. = 在警卫到达之前，他们已经制定了一个逃跑计划。
She had devised a solution to the problem before the meeting.  = 会议之前，她已经想出了解决问题的办法。

将来时:
We will devise a new marketing campaign next month. = 我们下个月将制定一项新的营销活动。
They will devise a way to overcome the obstacles. = 他们将想出一个克服障碍的方法。

虚拟语气:
If they had devised a better plan, they might have won. = 如果他们制定了一个更好的计划，他们可能就赢了。
I wish I had devised a more efficient solution. = 我希望我当时想出了一个更有效的解决方案。

被动语态:
A new system was devised to improve efficiency. = 设计了一个新的系统来提高效率。
A new strategy is being devised to address the issue. = 正在制定一项新的策略来解决这个问题。

疑问句:
What method did they devise to solve the problem? = 他们设计了什么方法来解决这个问题？
Have they devised a plan yet? = 他们已经制定计划了吗？

否定句:
They did not devise a suitable plan. = 他们没有制定一个合适的计划。
We haven't devised a solution to this problem yet. = 我们还没有找到解决这个问题的办法。


PHRASE->
