reconcentrates:/ˌriːˈkɒnsənˌtreɪts/| v.|重新集中；重新浓缩|To concentrate again; to become concentrated again.  The verb implies a previous state of dispersion or dilution, followed by a renewed process of focusing or thickening.|例句：The company reconcentrated its efforts on its core product line. 公司重新将精力集中在其核心产品线上。

例句：After the initial panic subsided, the rescue teams reconcentrated their efforts on finding survivors.  最初的恐慌平息后，救援队重新集中精力寻找幸存者。


近义词：refocus, concentrate, consolidate|反义词：disperse, diffuse, dilute|动词
