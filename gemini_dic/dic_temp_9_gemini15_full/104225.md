juridical:/dʒʊˈrɪdɪkəl/| adj.|法律的；司法上的；|Relating to the law or the administration of justice.  It often refers to the legal aspects of something, rather than the practical or social ones.|例句：The juridical implications of the decision were far-reaching.  这个决定的法律影响深远。

例句：He has a strong background in juridical matters. 他在法律事务方面有很强的背景。


近义词：legal, judicial, forensic


反义词：illegal, extrajudicial


词性：形容词
