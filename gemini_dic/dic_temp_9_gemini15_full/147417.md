overnourishes: /ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃɪz/ | vt. | 过度喂养；使营养过剩 | To feed or nourish excessively.  |  例句：The farmer overnourished his prize-winning pig, leading to health problems.  = 农民过度喂养了他获奖的猪，导致健康问题。

例句：  Overfeeding a baby can overnourish it and lead to obesity. = 过度喂养婴儿会导致营养过剩并导致肥胖。


近义词：overfeed, glut, surfeit


反义词：underfeed, starve, malnourish


词性：动词
