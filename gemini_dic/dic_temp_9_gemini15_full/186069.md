rewrought:/ˌriːˈrɔːt/| v. |重新锻造；改写；重新制作 |To work or shape (metal, etc.) again; to make over or reshape; to rewrite or revise.  |例句：The blacksmith rewrought the broken sword.  铁匠重新锻造了那把断剑。

The author rewrought the novel, adding new chapters and characters. 作者改写了这部小说，增加了新的章节和人物。

|近义词：remake, refashion, revise, reshape |反义词：destroy, ruin, demolish |动词
