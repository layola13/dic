counter-step:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛp/ | n. |反制措施，对策 | A counter-step is an action taken in response to an opponent's move, often to negate or mitigate its effects.  It implies a strategic response rather than a simple reaction. |例句：The company took counter-steps to protect its market share after the competitor launched a new product.  这家公司在竞争对手推出新产品后采取了反制措施来保护其市场份额。


The government implemented counter-steps to prevent the spread of the virus. 政府实施了反制措施以防止病毒蔓延。|近义词：countermeasure, response, riposte, retort |反义词：support, assistance, collaboration
