headstrongly: /ˈhɛdstrɒŋli/ | adv. | 固执地；倔强地；顽固地 |  Acting in a determined and stubborn way, often despite opposition or reason.  |例句：He headstrongly refused to listen to our advice. 他固执地拒绝听取我们的建议。
She headstrongly pursued her career despite setbacks. 她不顾挫折，顽强地追求自己的事业。|近义词：obstinately, stubbornly, doggedly |反义词：yieldingly, submissively, compliantly
