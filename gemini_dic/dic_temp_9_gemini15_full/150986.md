paramnesic:/ˌpærəmˈniːzɪk/ | adj. |记忆错乱的，记忆偏差的 | Relating to or denoting a disorder of memory characterized by distortions or illusions of memory, rather than complete loss of memory.  It involves errors in remembering rather than forgetting.  | Example: The patient exhibited paramnesic symptoms, including déjà vu and jamais vu.  患者表现出记忆错乱的症状，包括似曾相识和似不曾识。 | 近义词: mnemic, mnemonic (in the context of memory distortion) | 反义词: amnesic (referring to memory loss) |  词性:形容词
