decrepitation:/ˌdekrɛpɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |爆裂声；爆裂；（矿物）爆裂|The crackling sound produced by the rapid heating of a substance, typically due to the release of trapped moisture or gases.  It often refers to the popping sound heard when heating certain salts or minerals.  |例句：The decrepitation of the salt crystals was quite loud. 盐晶体的爆裂声很大。
The decrepitation of the rock sample indicated the presence of trapped water. 岩石样品的爆裂表明存在被困的水分。|近义词：cracking, popping, snapping|反义词：silence, stillness|名词
