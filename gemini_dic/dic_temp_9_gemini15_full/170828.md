pseudofaithfully:/ˈsuːdoʊˈfeɪθfəli/| adv.|似真地，貌似忠诚地 |The word "pseudofaithfully" combines "pseudo" (meaning false or pretended) and "faithfully" (meaning loyally or truthfully). It describes an action or behavior that appears to be faithful or loyal but is actually not.  It implies a deceptive imitation of faithfulness. |例句：He served the company pseudofaithfully, secretly plotting his own rise to power. 他表面上忠诚地为公司服务，暗地里却密谋着自己上位。 |近义词：insincerely, deceptively, disloyally |反义词：faithfully, loyally, truthfully |副词
