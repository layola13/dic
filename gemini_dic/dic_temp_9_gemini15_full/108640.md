leaf:/liːf/| n.|叶子|A flattened structure of a higher plant, typically green and blade-like, that is attached to a stem or branch.  Leaves are the primary sites of photosynthesis.|The leaves on the oak tree turned brown in autumn.  秋天的橡树叶子变黄了。|The leaves of the maple tree are bright red.枫树的叶子是鲜红色的。|petal, foliage|trunk, stem|noun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The leaves fall in autumn. = 秋天树叶落下。
The tree has green leaves. = 这棵树有绿色的叶子。


一般过去时:
The leaves fell from the trees. = 叶子从树上掉下来了。
I raked the fallen leaves yesterday. = 我昨天耙了落叶。


现在完成时:
The leaves have changed color. = 叶子变色了。
The wind has blown all the leaves away. = 风把所有的叶子都吹走了。


过去完成时:
The leaves had already fallen before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，叶子已经落光了。
The gardener had collected all the fallen leaves. = 园丁已经收集了所有的落叶。


将来时:
The leaves will turn yellow next month. = 叶子下个月会变黄。
The leaves will fall soon. = 叶子很快就会落下。


虚拟语气:
If the leaves were green, the tree would be healthier. = 如果叶子是绿色的，这棵树会更健康。
I wish the leaves would stay on the trees longer. = 我希望叶子能在树上待久一点。


被动语态:
The leaves are blown by the wind. = 叶子被风吹动。
The leaves were raked by the gardener. = 叶子被园丁耙了。


疑问句:
Are the leaves changing color yet? = 叶子变色了吗？
How many leaves are on that tree? = 那棵树上有多少片叶子？


否定句:
The leaves are not green anymore. = 叶子不再是绿色的了。
The tree doesn't have many leaves this year. = 这棵树今年叶子不多。
