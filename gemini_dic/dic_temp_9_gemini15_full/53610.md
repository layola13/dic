discarded:/dɪsˈkɑːrdɪd/ | adj. | 丢弃的；抛弃的 |  Something that has been discarded is no longer wanted or needed and has been thrown away or rejected.  | Example: The discarded furniture sat on the curb.  丢弃的家具放在路边。  |近义词：abandoned, rejected, thrown away, cast off | 反义词：kept, treasured, valued | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory discards a lot of waste. = 工厂丢弃很多废料。
They discard old equipment regularly. = 他们定期丢弃旧设备。


一般过去时:
He discarded his old shoes. = 他扔掉了他的旧鞋子。
She discarded the broken vase. = 她丢弃了那个破花瓶。


现在完成时:
They have discarded all the broken parts. = 他们已经丢弃了所有损坏的零件。
We have discarded outdated information. = 我们已经丢弃了过时的信息。


过去完成时:
He had discarded the evidence before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经销毁了证据。
She had discarded her old habits. = 她已经改掉了她的旧习惯。


将来时:
They will discard the old computers next week. = 他们下周将丢弃旧电脑。
We will discard this plan if it doesn't work. = 如果这个计划行不通，我们将放弃它。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would discard that idea. = 如果我是你，我会放弃那个想法。
I wish I had discarded that old dress sooner. = 我真希望我早点扔掉那件旧衣服。


被动语态:
The old toys were discarded. = 旧玩具被丢弃了。
The proposal was discarded after careful consideration. = 经过仔细考虑后，该提案被否决了。


疑问句:
Were the letters discarded? = 信件被丢弃了吗？
Will the broken machine be discarded? = 那台坏机器会被丢弃吗？


否定句:
They didn't discard the valuable documents. = 他们没有丢弃那些宝贵的文档。
He did not discard his old friend's letter. = 他没有丢弃他老朋友的信。



PHRASE->
