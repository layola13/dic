repayable:/rɪˈpeɪəbl/ | adj. | 可偿还的，可付清的 | Capable of being repaid; able to be paid back.  |例句：The loan is repayable over five years. 这笔贷款可在五年内偿还。|例句：The debt is repayable on demand. 这笔债务可以随时偿还。|近义词：reimbursable, refundable |反义词：unpayable, irredeemable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loan is repayable in installments. = 这笔贷款可以分期偿还。
This debt is repayable within a year. = 这笔债务可在一年内偿还。

一般过去时:
The loan was repayable within a specified timeframe. = 这笔贷款在规定的时间内是可以偿还的。
The debt was repayable on demand, but we chose to pay it early. = 这笔债务可以随时偿还，但我们选择提前偿还。

现在完成时:
The loan has been repayable since last month. = 这笔贷款从上个月起就可以偿还了。
The debt has been fully repayable for some time now. = 这笔债务已经可以完全偿还一段时间了。

过去完成时:
The loan had been repayable for several months before he decided to pay it off. = 在他决定还清贷款之前，这笔贷款已经可以偿还几个月了。
The debt had been repayable before the economic downturn, but the situation has changed. = 在经济衰退之前，这笔债务是可以偿还的，但情况已经改变了。

将来时:
The loan will be repayable next year. = 这笔贷款明年可以偿还。
The debt will be fully repayable by the end of the fiscal year. = 到财政年度结束时，这笔债务将可以完全偿还。

虚拟语气:
If the loan were repayable immediately, I would do it. = 如果这笔贷款可以立即偿还，我会这么做。
I wish the debt were repayable sooner. = 我希望这笔债务能够更快地偿还。

被动语态:
The loan is considered repayable by many. = 这笔贷款被许多人认为是可以偿还的。
The debt was declared repayable by the court. = 这笔债务被法院宣布可以偿还。

疑问句:
Is the loan repayable? = 这笔贷款可以偿还吗？
Is the debt repayable in full? = 这笔债务可以全部偿还吗？

否定句:
The loan is not repayable yet. = 这笔贷款还不能偿还。
The debt is not currently repayable due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，这笔债务目前无法偿还。


PHRASE->
repayable loan 可偿还贷款
repayable debt 可偿还债务
easily repayable 容易偿还的
fully repayable 全额可偿还的
partially repayable 部分可偿还的


词源:
re- (prefix indicating again or back) + payable (from pay + -able, meaning capable of being paid)  表示“可以再次支付”或“可以被支付回来”。
