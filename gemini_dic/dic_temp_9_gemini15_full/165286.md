preintercedes:/ˌpriːɪntərˈsiːdz/| v.| 预先调解；预先介入 | To intervene or mediate beforehand; to take action in advance to prevent or resolve a conflict or problem.  | 例句：The mediator preinterceded in the dispute, preventing a violent confrontation. 调解员预先介入争端，避免了暴力冲突。

例句：The UN preinterceded to avert a potential humanitarian crisis.联合国预先介入以避免潜在的人道主义危机。


近义词：intervene, mediate, interpose, anticipate


反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard
