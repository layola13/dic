etiologies:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 病因学；病因；原因 | The study of the causes or origins of diseases.  It encompasses the investigation of all factors that contribute to the development of a particular disease or condition, including genetic predisposition, environmental factors, and lifestyle choices. | Example: Researchers are exploring the etiologies of Alzheimer's disease to develop effective treatments.  研究人员正在探索阿尔茨海默病的病因以开发有效的治疗方法。 |近义词：causations, origins, causes | 反义词：effects, consequences, results
