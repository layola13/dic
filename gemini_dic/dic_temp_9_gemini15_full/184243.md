resolved:/rɪˈzɒlvd/ | adj. v. |解决了的；已决定的；坚决的；下定决心的 | Determined; settled; firm in purpose.  The problem has been resolved. The committee resolved to take action. |The dispute was finally resolved.  The committee resolved to support the proposal.|近义词：settled, determined, decided |反义词： unresolved, unsettled, undecided


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resolves to study harder. = 他决心更加努力学习。
She resolves conflicts peacefully. = 她和平地解决冲突。


一般过去时:
They resolved the issue quickly. = 他们迅速解决了这个问题。
He resolved to quit smoking. = 他下定决心戒烟。


现在完成时:
We have resolved the technical difficulties. = 我们已经解决了技术难题。
She has resolved to pursue her dreams. = 她已经下定决心追求她的梦想。


过去完成时:
They had resolved the problem before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经解决了问题。
She had resolved to leave her job months ago. = 她几个月前就下定决心离开工作。


将来时:
We will resolve the matter soon. = 我们很快就会解决这个问题。
He will resolve to be more patient. = 他将会下定决心更加耐心。


虚拟语气:
If only we had resolved the conflict earlier. = 如果我们早点解决冲突就好了。
I wish I had resolved to study harder. = 我真希望我当时下定决心更加努力学习。


被动语态:
The issue was resolved amicably. = 这个问题得到了友好解决。
The matter will be resolved by the committee. = 这个问题将由委员会解决。


疑问句:
Has the problem been resolved? = 问题解决了吗？
Have they resolved to support the project? = 他们下定决心支持这个项目了吗？


否定句:
The conflict was not resolved. = 冲突没有解决。
He did not resolve to go. = 他没有下定决心去。


PHRASE->
