hitherto:/ˈhɪðə(r)tuː/| adv. |迄今为止，到目前为止 |Up to this time or point; until now.  |例句：Hitherto, the company has had little success in the international market. 迄今为止，该公司在国际市场上几乎没有取得成功。|例句：The treatment had, hitherto, been unsuccessful.  治疗到目前为止一直没有成功。|近义词：until now, so far, up to now, thus far |反义词：from now on, henceforth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works hard hitherto. 他迄今为止工作努力。
We study English hitherto. 我们迄今为止学习英语。


一般过去时:
They lived in London hitherto. 他们迄今为止住在伦敦。
She played the piano hitherto. 她迄今为止弹钢琴。


现在完成时:
He has worked hard hitherto. 他迄今为止努力工作了。
She has lived in Paris hitherto. 她迄今为止住在巴黎。


过去完成时:
They had worked hard hitherto. 他们那时为止努力工作了。
She had studied English hitherto. 她那时为止学习英语了。


将来时:
He will work hard hitherto. 他将来也会努力工作。
They will live in London hitherto. 他们将来还住在伦敦。


虚拟语气:
If he had worked hard hitherto, he would have succeeded. 如果他迄今为止努力工作，他本可以成功。
If she had studied harder hitherto, she would have passed the exam. 如果她迄今为止学习更努力，她就能通过考试。


被动语态:
The problem has hitherto been ignored.  这个问题迄今为止一直被忽视。
The matter has hitherto been unresolved. 这件事迄今为止一直未解决。


疑问句:
Has the project been completed hitherto? 项目迄今为止完成了吗？
Have you ever been to Paris hitherto? 你迄今为止去过巴黎吗？


否定句:
The plan has not hitherto been implemented.  该计划迄今为止尚未实施。
He has not hitherto expressed his opinion. 他迄今为止尚未表达他的意见。
