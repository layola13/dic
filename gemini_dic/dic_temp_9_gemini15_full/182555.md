reobject:/ˌriːˈɒbdʒekt/| vt.|重新反对，再提出异议|To object again or anew; to raise an objection again.|例句：The committee re-objected to the proposed budget cuts.  委员会再次反对拟议的预算削减。|例句：He re-objected to the judge's ruling, citing new evidence. 他以新的证据为由，再次对法官的裁决提出异议。|近义词：protest, oppose, remonstrate|反义词：accept, approve, concur|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reobject to the new policy. = 他们重新反对这项新政策。
He reobjects every time the topic is raised. = 每当这个话题被提起，他都会重新反对。

一般过去时:
The board reobjected to the merger proposal. = 董事会再次反对合并提案。
She reobjected after hearing the new information. = 在听到新信息后，她再次提出异议。

现在完成时:
They have reobjected several times. = 他们已经多次重新反对了。
He has reobjected to the plan since its inception. = 自从计划开始以来，他就一直反对。

过去完成时:
They had reobjected before the final vote. = 在最终投票之前，他们已经再次提出异议了。
She had reobjected numerous times before they finally listened. = 在他们最终听取意见之前，她已经多次提出异议。

将来时:
They will reobject if the changes are not made. = 如果不做出改变，他们将会再次反对。
He will reobject at the next meeting. = 他将在下次会议上再次提出异议。

虚拟语气:
If they knew the consequences, they would not reobject. = 如果他们知道后果，他们就不会再次反对。
I wish they would not reobject to my suggestions. = 我希望他们不要反对我的建议。

被动语态:
The proposal was reobjected to by several members. = 该提案遭到几名成员的再次反对。
His arguments were reobjected to by the opposing counsel. = 他的论点遭到对方律师的再次反驳。

疑问句:
Will they reobject to the decision? = 他们会再次反对这个决定吗？
Did he reobject to the judge's ruling? = 他对法官的裁决再次提出异议了吗？

否定句:
They did not reobject to the final decision. = 他们没有对最终决定再次提出异议。
He did not reobject after considering the new evidence. = 在考虑了新的证据后，他没有再次提出异议。


PHRASE->
reobject to a plan = 反对一个计划
reobject vehemently = 强烈反对
reobject on principle = 原则上反对
reobject repeatedly = 反复反对
reobject strenuously = 竭力反对
