butyl:/ˈbjuːtəl/| n. adj.|丁基，丁基的|Butyl is a four-carbon alkyl group derived from butane. It's commonly used in various chemical compounds.  |Example: Butyl rubber is used in many tire applications. 丁基橡胶用于许多轮胎应用。|近义词：n-butyl, sec-butyl, tert-butyl, isobutyl|反义词：无
