intangible: /ɪnˈtændʒəbəl/ | adj. | 无形的；难以捉摸的；难以描述的 |  Not able to be touched or grasped; not having physical presence.  It refers to things that exist but cannot be physically perceived. |

例句：
Goodwill is an intangible asset of a company. = 商誉是公司的一项无形资产。

The benefits of a good education are intangible. = 良好教育的好处是无形的。


近义词：impalpable, incorporeal, immaterial, abstract


反义词：tangible, concrete, material


词性：形容词
