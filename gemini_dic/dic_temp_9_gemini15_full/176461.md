re-motivate:/ˌriːˈmoʊtɪveɪt/| vt.|重新激励，再次激发动力|To motivate someone again after their motivation has decreased or been lost.  It implies a renewed sense of purpose or enthusiasm.|例句：The team needed to be re-motivated after their recent loss.  团队在最近的失利后需要重新激励。|例句：The manager's inspiring speech re-motivated the employees to achieve their sales targets. 经理鼓舞人心的演讲再次激励员工实现销售目标。|近义词：reinforce, inspire, encourage, revitalize |反义词：discourage, demotivate, deject
