rubiest:/ˈruːbiɪst/| adj.|红宝石色的，像红宝石的|Of or relating to rubies; resembling a ruby in color.  The word describes something having the deep, rich red color of a ruby.|例句：Her dress was a rubiest red.  她的裙子是红宝石般的红色。

例句：The sunset painted the sky in rubiest hues.  日落给天空涂上了红宝石般的色彩。


近义词：ruby-red, crimson, scarlet

反义词：pale, light, dull


词性:形容词
