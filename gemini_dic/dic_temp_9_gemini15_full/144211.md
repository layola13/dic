outsized:/ˈaʊtsaɪzd/| adj.|过大的，巨大的，不成比例的|exceeding the usual or expected size; disproportionately large.|例句：The company's outsized profits surprised everyone. 公司的巨额利润让所有人都大吃一惊。|近义词：enormous, immense, huge, gigantic, disproportionate|反义词：tiny, minuscule, insignificant


USAGE->

The project has an outsized impact. = 这个项目的影响巨大。
His influence is outsized in this field. = 他在这个领域的影响力很大。
