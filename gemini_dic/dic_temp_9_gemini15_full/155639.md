phlebitic:/flɛˈbɪtɪk/| adj.|静脉炎的；患静脉炎的| relating to or affected by phlebitis (inflammation of a vein).  Phlebitic conditions can cause pain, swelling, and redness along the affected vein.|The patient presented with phlebitic symptoms in her leg, including swelling and redness.|近义词：venous, inflammatory|反义词：healthy, uninflamed
