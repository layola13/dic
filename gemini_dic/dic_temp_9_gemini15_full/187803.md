rosarian:/ˌroʊzˈeəriən/ | n. |玫瑰种植者；玫瑰专家 | A rosarian is a person who cultivates, studies, or exhibits roses.  They are often passionate about roses and possess extensive knowledge about various rose varieties, their cultivation, and care. |例句：The renowned rosarian gave a lecture on hybrid tea roses.  著名的玫瑰种植者就杂交茶香玫瑰做了一个讲座。

The local rosarian's garden is a breathtaking display of colors and fragrances. 当地玫瑰种植者的花园是色彩和香味令人叹为观止的展示。|近义词：rose grower, rose enthusiast, rose expert |反义词：无
