self-representation:/ˌsɛlfˌrɛprɪzenˈteɪʃən/| n. |自我呈现，自我表达，自我描述 |The act of presenting or describing oneself, often in a particular way to achieve a specific goal. This can involve verbal communication, nonverbal cues, or written accounts. It's a process of shaping how others perceive you.  | Example: Her self-representation in the interview was highly polished and professional.  她的面试自我介绍非常精致专业。  |近义词：self-portrayal, self-portrayal, self-description |反义词：misrepresentation, false portrayal |名词
