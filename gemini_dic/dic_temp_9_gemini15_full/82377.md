groynes:/ɡrɔɪnz/ | n. | 格罗因，防波堤 | Artificial structures built perpendicular to a shoreline to trap sediment and reduce erosion.  They are typically made of rock, concrete, or wood. | Example: The groynes along the coast helped to prevent beach erosion.  海岸上的格罗因有助于防止海滩侵蚀。|近义词：breakwater, jetty, seawall | 反义词：None specifically, but structures that *accelerate* erosion could be considered antonymous in function. | 名词
