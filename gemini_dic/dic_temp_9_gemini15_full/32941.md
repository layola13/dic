chopper:/ˈtʃɒpə(r)/|/ˈtʃɑːpər/| n. v.| 砍伐者；直升机；切碎机 | A chopper is a person or thing that chops.  It can refer to a helicopter, a tool for chopping, or someone who chops. |例句：The wood chopper worked tirelessly all day.  =  那个伐木工整天不知疲倦地工作。  |例句：The police arrived in a helicopter chopper. = 警察乘坐直升机到达。|近义词：helicopter, cleaver, mincer |反义词： assembler, joiner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chops wood every morning. = 他每天早上砍柴。
The machine chops vegetables efficiently. = 这台机器高效地切碎蔬菜。

一般过去时:
They chopped down the old tree. = 他们砍倒了那棵老树。
She chopped the onions finely. = 她把洋葱切得很细。

现在完成时:
He has chopped all the wood for the winter. = 他已经砍好了整个冬天的柴火。
She has chopped the vegetables for the stew. = 她已经把炖菜的蔬菜切好了。

过去完成时:
They had chopped the vegetables before the chef arrived. = 在厨师到达之前，他们已经把蔬菜切好了。
He had chopped down several trees before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经砍倒了几棵树。

将来时:
They will chop down the diseased tree tomorrow. = 他们明天将砍倒那棵病树。
She will chop the vegetables for the salad later.  = 她稍后会把沙拉的蔬菜切好。

虚拟语气:
If I had a chopper, I would chop the wood more quickly. = 如果我有一把斧子，我会更快地砍柴。
I wish I had chopped the vegetables earlier. = 我希望我早点把蔬菜切好。

被动语态:
The wood was chopped by the lumberjack. = 木材被伐木工人砍伐了。
The vegetables are chopped by machine. = 蔬菜由机器切碎。

疑问句:
Who chopped down the tree? = 谁砍倒了那棵树？
How finely are the vegetables chopped? = 蔬菜切得有多细？

否定句:
He didn't chop the wood today. = 他今天没有砍柴。
She hasn't chopped the onions yet. = 她还没切洋葱。
