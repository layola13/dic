majestic:/məˈdʒɛstɪk/| adj. |雄伟的；庄严的；壮丽的 |Inspiring awe or admiration because of its grandeur, power, or beauty.  It suggests something impressive and commanding, often associated with royalty or nature. |例句：The majestic mountains loomed over the valley.  雄伟的山峰耸立在山谷之上。

The majestic lion surveyed its kingdom.  雄狮俯瞰着它的领地。


近义词：grand, imposing, magnificent, splendid, sublime |反义词：humble, insignificant, paltry, mean, petty
