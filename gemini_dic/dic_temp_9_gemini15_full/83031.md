gunwale:/ˈɡʌnwəl/| n.|船舷，舷边|The upper edge of a vessel's side.  It is the topmost structural member of a ship's side. |例句：The sailor gripped the gunwale as the boat pitched violently. 水手紧紧抓住船舷，因为船剧烈地摇晃。|例句：He leaned over the gunwale to watch the fish swim below. 他俯身探过船舷观看下面的鱼群。|近义词：rail, side, edge|反义词:keel
