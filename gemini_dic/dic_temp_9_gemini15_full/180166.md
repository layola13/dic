reformation:/ˌrɛfərˈmeɪʃən/| n.|宗教改革；改良；改造|The process of reforming or being reformed.  It often refers to the 16th-century religious movement that split the Western Church into Protestant and Catholic factions.|The Reformation led to significant changes in European society.  宗教改革导致了欧洲社会的重大变化。|improvement, reform, renewal|stagnation, corruption|名词
