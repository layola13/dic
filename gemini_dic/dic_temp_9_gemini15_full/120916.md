miscasualty:/ˌmɪsˈkæʒuəlti/| n.|误伤，意外伤亡|A miscasualty is an unintended or accidental injury or death, often in a military or emergency context.  It refers to someone who is harmed or killed as a result of an error or unforeseen circumstance.  It implies that the harm was not intentional.|例句：The bombing resulted in several miscasualties among civilians.  这次轰炸导致几名平民误伤。

The report detailed the number of miscasualties during the rescue operation. 报告详细说明了救援行动中的误伤人数。|近义词：accident, unintended consequence, collateral damage |反义词：intentional casualty, deliberate harm|名词
