hoolock:/ˈhuːlɒk/| n. |胡禄猴|A hoolock gibbon is a type of gibbon found in Northeast India, Bangladesh, Myanmar, and southwestern China.  They are known for their distinctive black and white fur coloration and loud calls.| Example: The hoolock gibbon’s call echoed through the dense forest. 胡禄猴的叫声在茂密的森林中回荡。|近义词: gibbon (泛指长臂猿)|反义词:  无
