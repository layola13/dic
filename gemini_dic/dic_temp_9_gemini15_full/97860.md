inlighten: /ɪnˈlaɪtn/ | vt. |启发；教导；使了解 | To enlighten someone is to give them more knowledge or understanding of something.  It implies removing ignorance or misunderstanding. |

例句：

The professor's lecture enlightened the students on the complexities of quantum physics. = 教授的讲座使学生们了解了量子物理的复杂性。

The experience enlightened her to the realities of poverty. = 这段经历让她了解了贫困的现实。


近义词：educate, inform, instruct, illuminate, clarify

反义词：confuse, bewilder, obfuscate


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher enlightens her students every day. = 老师每天都启发她的学生。
He enlightens me on the subject. = 他在这方面启发了我。


一般过去时:
The seminar enlightened me about sustainable living. = 研讨会让我了解了可持续生活。
She enlightened him on the dangers of smoking. = 她向他说明了吸烟的危害。


现在完成时:
This book has enlightened me on many things. = 这本书让我了解了很多事情。
Reading that article has enlightened my perspective. = 阅读那篇文章使我的观点开阔了。


过去完成时:
He had already been enlightened about the truth before the meeting. = 会议之前他已经了解了真相。
They had been enlightened by the wise words of their elder. = 他们受到了长者智慧话语的启迪。


将来时:
I hope this explanation will enlighten you. = 我希望这个解释能让你明白。
This new research will hopefully enlighten us on the origins of the universe. = 这项新的研究有望让我们了解宇宙的起源。


虚拟语气:
If only he would enlighten us about his plans. = 但愿他能把他的计划告诉我们。
I wish she would enlighten me on the subject. = 我希望她能在这方面启发我。


被动语态:
The students were enlightened by the professor's lecture. = 学生们受到了教授讲座的启发。
The audience was enlightened by the speaker's wisdom. = 听众受到了演讲者智慧的启迪。


疑问句:

否定句:
The explanation didn't enlighten me at all. = 这个解释一点都没让我明白。
His words did not enlighten the situation. = 他的话并没有让情况明朗。
