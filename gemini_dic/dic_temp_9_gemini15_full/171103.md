psychedelically:/ˌsaɪkəˈdɛlɪkli/| adv.|迷幻地，引起幻觉地|In a way that relates to or resembles psychedelic drugs or their effects; causing hallucinations or altered perceptions.|例句：The music was psychedelically intense, warping the listener's perception of reality.  音乐具有迷幻的强度，扭曲了听者对现实的感知。

例句：The artwork was psychedelically vibrant, a swirling mix of colors and shapes.  这幅艺术作品色彩鲜艳，具有迷幻效果，颜色和形状交织在一起。


近义词：hallucinogenically, hallucinatory, dreamily, trippily


反义词： soberly, realistically, clearly


词性:副词
