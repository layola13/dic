crabbier:/ˈkræbiər/| adj.|更坏脾气的，更暴躁的 |More irritable or grumpy than usual.  The adjective "crabby" describes someone who is irritable and bad-tempered. "Crabier" is the comparative form, indicating a greater degree of crabbiness. |例句：He was already crabby this morning, but after the traffic jam, he was even crabbier.  他今天早上已经很暴躁了，但堵车之后，他变得更暴躁了。  The older he gets, the crabbier he seems to become. 他年纪越大，似乎脾气越坏。|近义词：grumpier, grumpier, more irritable, more grumpy, more surly |反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant, good-natured
