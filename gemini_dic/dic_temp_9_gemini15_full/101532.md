ironbark:/ˈaɪənˌbɑːrk/ | n. | 赤铁木, 硬铁木 | A type of eucalyptus tree native to Australia, known for its extremely hard and durable wood.  The name refers to the extremely hard bark of the tree. | Example:  The fence posts were made of ironbark, known for its exceptional strength and resistance to rot.  铁栅栏柱是用赤铁木做的，这种木材以其非凡的强度和抗腐烂性而闻名。 |近义词:  tough wood, hard wood | 反义词: softwood
