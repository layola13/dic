legacy:/ˈlɛɡəsi/ | n. |遗产；留下的东西；传统 |  Something that is left behind by someone who has died, such as property, money, or a reputation; something that happened in the past that affects the present. |例句：He left a legacy of over a million dollars to his children. 他留给孩子们超过一百万美元的遗产。

例句：The company's legacy of innovation continues to this day. 这家公司的创新传统一直延续至今。

近义词：inheritance, bequest, heritage, tradition

反义词：nothing, nil
