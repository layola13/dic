misconceive:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːv/ | vt. |误解，误认为 | To have a wrong idea or understanding of something. |例句：Many people misconceive the role of government in the economy. 很多人误解了政府在经济中的作用。

例句：He completely misconceived my intentions. 他完全误解了我的意图。


近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, mistake | 反义词：understand, comprehend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misconceives the meaning of the poem. 他误解了这首诗的意思。
They misconceive the purpose of this meeting. 他们误解了这次会议的目的。


一般过去时:
I misconceived the instructions and did the wrong thing. 我误解了指示，做错了事。
