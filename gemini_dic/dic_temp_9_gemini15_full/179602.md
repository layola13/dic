reemphasizes:/ˌriˈɛmfəsaɪz/| vt.|再次强调，重新强调|To emphasize something again; to stress something once more.|例句：The teacher reemphasized the importance of studying hard.老师再次强调了努力学习的重要性。|例句：The report reemphasizes the need for stricter regulations.这份报告再次强调了需要更严格的法规。|近义词：reiterate, restate, underscore|反义词：downplay, understate|动词
