inclement:/ˈɪnklɪmənt/| adj.|不利的；风雨交加的；严酷的|Unfavorable, stormy, or severe weather; also used to describe someone's harsh or unkind disposition.  |例句：We had to postpone the picnic because of the inclement weather.  我们因为恶劣的天气不得不推迟野餐。

例句：The professor's inclement attitude toward his students made them reluctant to ask questions. 教授对学生的态度严厉，使得他们不愿提问。|近义词：stormy, harsh, severe, unkind, unpleasant|反义词：clement, mild, gentle, kind, favorable
