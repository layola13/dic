hastening:/ˈheɪsnɪŋ/| v.|加快；促进；赶紧|To cause something to happen sooner or to move more quickly.  It can also refer to the act of hurrying.|例句：The bad weather hastened the end of the outdoor concert.  恶劣天气使户外音乐会提前结束。

例句：He hastened his steps to catch the bus. 他加快脚步去赶公共汽车。


近义词：accelerate, expedite, quicken, speed up|反义词：delay, retard, slow down, procrastinate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hastens his pace every morning. 他每天早上都加快步伐。
They hasten to complete the project before the deadline. 他们赶在截止日期前完成项目。

一般过去时:
She hastened to help the injured man. 她赶紧去帮助受伤的人。
The news hastened his decision to leave. 这个消息促使他决定离开。

现在完成时:
The rain has hastened the growth of the plants. 雨水促进了植物的生长。
We have hastened our preparations for the trip. 我们加快了旅行的准备工作。

过去完成时:
They had hastened their departure because of the storm. 因为暴风雨，他们提前离开了。
She had hastened to warn her friends about the danger. 她赶紧警告她的朋友们注意危险。

将来时:
He will hasten to finish his work. 他将赶紧完成他的工作。
They will hasten the process to meet the demand. 他们将加快流程以满足需求。

虚拟语气:
If he had hastened, he would have caught the train. 如果他加快速度，他就能赶上火车了。
I wish I had hastened to tell him the truth. 我真希望我早点告诉他真相。

被动语态:
The project was hastened by the urgent need.  这个项目由于紧急需要而被加快了。
The decision was hastened by the pressure from the board. 这个决定受到了董事会的压力而被加快了。

疑问句:
Will you hasten your reply? 你会加快回复吗？
What hastened the process? 是什么加快了这个过程？

否定句:
He did not hasten his steps. 他没有加快脚步。
They did not hasten to inform us. 他们没有赶紧通知我们。
