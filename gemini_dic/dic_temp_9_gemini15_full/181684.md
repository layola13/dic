relentlessly:/rɪˈlɛntlɪsli/| adv. |不屈不挠地；持续不断地；无情地 | persistently and continuously; without giving up or showing mercy. |例句：The rain fell relentlessly for days.  雨连续下了好几天。

例句：He relentlessly pursued his goals, despite setbacks. 他不屈不挠地追求他的目标，尽管遭遇挫折。


近义词：unyieldingly, persistently, incessantly, continuously, relentlessly, remorselessly

反义词：mercifully, compassionately, intermittently
