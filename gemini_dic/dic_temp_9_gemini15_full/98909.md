intensive:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 密集的；集中的；加强的 |  Characterized by intensity or concentration; thorough; strenuous.  It describes something that is done with great effort or concentration, or something that is highly concentrated or focused. |

例句：
The farmer used intensive farming methods to maximize his yield. = 这位农民采用集约化耕作方法以最大限度地提高产量。

The course is intensive, requiring long hours of study. = 这门课程是强化课程，需要长时间学习。


近义词：concentrated, focused, thorough, strenuous, rigorous


反义词：extensive, superficial, lax, mild


词性：形容词
