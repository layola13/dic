galvanization: /ˌɡæl və naɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 镀锌；激励；兴奋 | The process of coating iron or steel with zinc to protect it from rust.  It can also refer to the act of stimulating or exciting someone. |  例句：The galvanization of the iron fence prevented rust.  镀锌铁栅栏防止了生锈。 The speaker's words caused a galvanization of the audience. 演讲者的言辞激起了听众的热情。 | 近义词：zincing, stimulation, excitement | 反义词：corrosion, apathy, depression | 名词
