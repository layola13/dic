half-confessed:/ˈhæf kənˈfɛst/ | adj. | 半承认的；含糊承认的 | Partially admitted or acknowledged; vaguely confessed.  It implies a reluctance to fully admit something. |例句：He gave a half-confessed apology, mumbling something about being sorry. 他含糊地道歉了一下，嘟囔着说抱歉。

例句：Her half-confessed guilt was evident in her downcast eyes. 她低垂的眼睛里流露出她半承认的罪恶感。


近义词：partially admitted, vaguely acknowledged, implicitly confessed, hinted at

反义词：fully confessed, openly admitted, unequivocally acknowledged


词性:形容词
