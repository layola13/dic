rewearing:/ˌriːˈwɛrɪŋ/| v. |重新穿着；再次穿着|The act of wearing something again.  It implies a previous wearing of the garment. |例句：Rewearing my favorite sweater felt comfortable and cozy.  重新穿上我最喜欢的毛衣感觉舒适又温暖。

例句：The concept of rewearing clothes is gaining popularity as a sustainable fashion choice.  再次穿着衣服的概念作为一种可持续的时尚选择越来越受欢迎。


近义词：wearing again, putting on again |反义词：discarding, throwing away |词性:动词
