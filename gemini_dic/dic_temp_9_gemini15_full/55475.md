distract:/dɪˈstrækt/| vt. |使分心，转移注意力|To draw someone's attention away from something.|The noise distracted me from my work.  噪音分散了我的注意力。|The beautiful scenery distracted her from her worries. 美丽的景色让她忘记了烦恼。|focus, divert |concentrate, engage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music distracts me. = 吵闹的音乐让我分心。
This task distracts her from her studies. = 这项任务让她无法专心学习。


一般过去时:
The phone call distracted me from my work. = 电话打断了我的工作。
The sudden noise distracted the driver. = 突然的噪音分散了司机的注意力。


现在完成时:
The constant interruptions have distracted me all day. = 一天到晚的打断让我一直分心。
The news has distracted many people from their daily routines. = 这个消息让许多人分心于日常事务。


过去完成时:
The argument had distracted him from the important decision. = 那场争论让他无法做出重要的决定。
The loud music had distracted her from the movie. = 吵闹的音乐让她无法专心看电影。


将来时:
The party will distract him from his studies. = 派对会让他无法专心学习。
The upcoming holiday will distract many people from work. = 即将来临的假期会让许多人分心于工作。


虚拟语气:
If the noise hadn't distracted me, I would have finished the work on time. = 如果噪音没有让我分心，我就能按时完成工作了。
If only the TV hadn't distracted him, he would have passed the exam. = 如果电视没有让他分心，他就能通过考试了。


被动语态:
He was distracted by the beautiful view. = 他被美丽的景色吸引住了。
She was distracted by the children's noise. = 她被孩子们的吵闹声分散了注意力。


疑问句:
What distracted you from your reading? = 什么让你分心于阅读？
What is distracting you from your work? = 什么让你分心于工作？


否定句:
The quiet environment didn't distract her from her studies. = 安静的环境并没有让她分心于学习。
Nothing could distract him from his goal. = 没有任何事情能让他偏离目标。


PHRASE->
distract from = 从…转移注意力
distract someone's attention = 转移某人的注意力
be distracted by = 被…分散注意力
highly distracted = 极度分心
easily distracted = 容易分心
