deleteriousness:/ˌdɛləˈtɪəriəsnəs/| n.|有害性，危害性|The deleteriousness of smoking is well documented. 吸烟的危害性已被充分证明。|近义词：harmfulness, nocivity, detriment|反义词：benefit, advantage, harmlessness|名词


例句：

The deleteriousness of the pesticide on the environment is a major concern.  杀虫剂对环境的危害性是人们主要关注的问题。

Scientists are studying the long-term deleteriousness of air pollution on human health. 科学家们正在研究长期空气污染对人类健康的危害性。
