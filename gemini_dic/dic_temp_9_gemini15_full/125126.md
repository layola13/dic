multi-ethnic:/ˌmʌltiˈeθnɪk/ | adj. | 多民族的；多种族的 | Relating to or consisting of several ethnic groups.  This term describes a society or group that comprises people from diverse ethnic backgrounds.  | 例句：The school boasts a vibrant and multi-ethnic student body. 这所学校拥有充满活力且多元民族的学生群体。

例句：Many large cities are characterized by their multi-ethnic populations. 许多大城市的特点是其多元民族的人口。


近义词：multicultural, diverse, heterogeneous

反义词：monolithic, homogeneous, uniform


词性:形容词
