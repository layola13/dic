reforger:/riːˈfɔːrdʒər/ | v. | 重铸；改造；重新锻造 | To reshape or restructure something, especially to improve it.  | The company is reforging its image after a series of scandals. 公司在一系列丑闻后正在重塑其形象。 | reshape, reform, reconstruct | destroy, ruin, dismantle | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reforges his strategy every year. = 他每年都会重塑他的战略。
The blacksmith reforges the broken sword. = 铁匠正在重铸那把断剑。


一般过去时:
They reforged the alliance after a period of conflict. = 冲突一段时间后，他们重修了联盟。
The artist reforged the sculpture from scrap metal. = 艺术家用废金属重铸了雕塑。


现在完成时:
She has reforged her career after a long break. = 她长时间休息后重塑了自己的职业生涯。
The government has reforged its relationship with the neighboring country. = 政府已经重修了与邻国的关系。


过去完成时:
He had reforged his identity before he moved to a new city. = 他在搬到新城市之前已经重塑了自己的身份。
The company had reforged its supply chains before the crisis hit. = 在危机来临之前，公司已经重塑了其供应链。


将来时:
They will reforge their commitment to sustainability. = 他们将重申他们对可持续发展的承诺。(这里用reforge指重新承诺，类似重塑承诺)
The team will reforge its winning strategy for the upcoming tournament. = 团队将为即将到来的锦标赛重塑其制胜策略。


虚拟语气:
If they reforged their alliance, they could achieve more. = 如果他们重修联盟，他们就能取得更多成就。
I wish he had reforged his relationship with his family. = 我希望他与家人重修旧好。(这里reforge指修复关系)


被动语态:
The agreement was reforged after many discussions. = 经过多次讨论后，协议得到了重修。
The old bridge is being reforged with modern materials. = 老桥正在用现代材料进行重铸。


疑问句:
Will they reforge the broken parts of the machine? = 他们会重铸机器的损坏部件吗？
Is the company reforging its marketing strategy? = 公司正在重塑其营销策略吗？


否定句:
He did not reforge his old habits. = 他没有改掉他的旧习惯。
The sculptor did not reforge the statue. = 雕塑家没有重铸雕像。
