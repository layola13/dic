flow-on:/ˈfloʊ ɔːn/|/ˈfləʊ ɒn/| adj. n. v.|后续的，间接的；后续影响，连锁反应；继续，延续|Resulting from or caused by something else; a knock-on effect.|The flow-on effects of the policy change were significant.|近义词：consequential, subsequent, knock-on|反义词：immediate, direct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The policy change has flow-on effects to the whole industry. = 政策改变对整个行业产生后续影响。
Increased investment flows on to job creation. = 投资增加会带来就业岗位的增加。


一般过去时:
The initial success flowed on to further achievements. = 最初的成功导致了进一步的成就。
The ripple effect flowed on through the community. = 连锁反应在社区蔓延开来。


现在完成时:
The impact of the decision has flowed on to many aspects of our lives. = 该决定的影响已经波及到我们生活的许多方面。
The benefits have flowed on to the next generation. = 好处已经延续到了下一代。


过去完成时:
The consequences had flowed on from the previous year's drought. = 后果源于前一年的干旱。
The project's failure had flowed on to several related initiatives. = 项目的失败影响了几个相关的计划。


将来时:
The investment will flow on to economic growth. = 投资将带动经济增长。
The reforms will flow on to improved efficiency. = 改革将带来效率的提高。


虚拟语气:
If the project succeeded, the benefits would flow on to the entire community. = 如果项目成功，好处将惠及整个社区。
I wish the positive effects would flow on for many years. = 我希望积极的影响能持续很多年。


被动语态:
The benefits were flowed on to the employees. = 好处已经惠及员工。
The consequences are being flowed on to future generations. = 后果正在波及后代。


疑问句:
Will the benefits flow on to everyone? = 好处会惠及所有人吗？
How will the changes flow on to other departments? = 这些变化将如何影响其他部门？


否定句:
The positive effects did not flow on as expected. = 积极的影响没有像预期的那样延续。
The changes did not flow on to the next phase of the project. = 这些变化没有延续到项目的下一阶段。


PHRASE->
flow-on effect = 连锁反应
flow-on benefits = 附带好处
flow-on costs = 间接成本
flow-on consequences = 后果
flow-on impact = 间接影响
