predetached:/ˌpriːdɪˈtætʃt/| adj.|预先分离的；预先拆卸的 |Predetached refers to something that has been detached or separated beforehand, usually in preparation for something else.  It implies a planned or intentional separation before a particular event or process.|例句：The predetached components made assembly of the machine much faster. 预先分离的部件使机器组装速度快得多。The predetached wings were easily attached to the aircraft body. 预先拆卸的机翼很容易安装到飞机机身上。|近义词：pre-removed, separated beforehand, pre-separated |反义词：attached, connected, joined
