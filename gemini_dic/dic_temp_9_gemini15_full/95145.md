inappropriately:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriətli/ | adv. | 不恰当地，不适当地 |  Acting in a way that is not suitable or proper for a particular situation or place.  It implies a lack of good judgment or sensitivity. |

例句：
He inappropriately touched her arm during the meeting. = 他在会议期间不恰当地碰了她的胳膊。

She inappropriately used company funds for personal expenses. = 她不恰当地将公司资金用于个人开支。


近义词：improperly, unsuitably, unfittingly, unbecomingly, indecorously

反义词：appropriately, suitably, properly, fittingly


词性: 副词
