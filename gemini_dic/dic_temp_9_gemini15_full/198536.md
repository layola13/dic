shrouding: /ˈʃraʊdɪŋ/ | v. | 遮蔽；掩盖；笼罩 |  The act of covering or enveloping something, often to conceal or protect it.  It implies a sense of mystery or obscurity. |


例句：

A thick fog was shrouding the valley.  浓雾笼罩着山谷。

The mystery surrounding her disappearance is still shrouding the town.  围绕着她失踪的谜团仍然笼罩着这个小镇。


近义词：covering, concealing, masking, veiling, obscuring


反义词：revealing, uncovering, exposing, disclosing


词性: 动词
