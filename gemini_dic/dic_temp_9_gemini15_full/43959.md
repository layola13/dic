crick:/krɪk/ | n. | 嘎吱声；（颈部或背部的）剧痛；（板球）得分；| A sharp, cracking sound; a sudden, sharp pain in the neck or back; (cricket) a score. |例句：I heard a crick in the floorboards. 我听到地板发出嘎吱声。  |例句： I have a crick in my neck from sleeping wrong. 我睡姿不对，脖子很僵硬。|近义词：crack, snap, twinge, ache |反义词：silence, smoothness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The floorboards often crick. = 地板经常发出嘎吱声。
A crick usually accompanies a bad posture. = 不良姿势通常伴随肌肉酸痛。

一般过去时:
The old door cricked as I opened it. = 我开门的时候，旧门嘎吱作响。
My neck cricked after I slept on the lumpy mattress. = 我睡在凹凸不平的床垫上后，脖子僵硬了。

现在完成时:
The floorboards have cricked all night. = 地板整夜都在嘎吱作响。
My back has cricked since I lifted that heavy box. = 自从我搬起那个沉重的箱子后，我的背就一直酸痛。

过去完成时:
The door had cricked several times before it finally broke. = 门在最终坏掉之前已经嘎吱作响好几次了。
My neck had cricked badly before I went to the doctor. = 在我去看医生之前，我的脖子已经非常僵硬了。

将来时:
The old house will crick and groan in the wind. = 老房子会在风中嘎吱作响并发出呻吟声。
I think my back will crick if I continue to sit like this. = 如果我继续这样坐着，我想我的背会僵硬的。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't slept wrong, my neck wouldn't crick so badly. = 如果我没有睡姿不对，我的脖子就不会这么僵硬。
If I hadn't lifted that heavy box, my back wouldn't crick. = 如果我没有搬起那个沉重的箱子，我的背就不会酸痛。

被动语态:
The door was cricked open. = 门被嘎吱地打开。
His neck was cricked from sleeping in an awkward position. = 他的脖子因为睡姿不舒服而僵硬了。

疑问句:
Does your neck crick often? = 你的脖子经常僵硬吗？
Does the floor crick when you walk across it? = 你走过地板的时候，地板会嘎吱作响吗？

否定句:
The door didn't crick at all. = 门一点声音也没有发出。
My neck didn't crick today. = 我的脖子今天没有僵硬。
