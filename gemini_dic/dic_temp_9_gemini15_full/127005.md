nasals:/ˈneɪzəlz/ | n. |鼻音，鼻辅音|Sounds produced with air flowing through the nose.  Nasals are a type of consonant sound.  Examples in English include /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/ (as in "sing").|The English language utilizes three nasal consonants: /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/.|近义词： nasal consonants |反义词：oral consonants
