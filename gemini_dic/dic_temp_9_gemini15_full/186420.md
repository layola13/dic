ribbing:/ˈrɪbɪŋ/| n.| 罗纹；肋骨状物；戏弄；嘲笑 |The act of teasing or making fun of someone.  It can also refer to a fabric texture or a structural element. |例句：The sweater has a nice ribbing at the cuffs. 这件毛衣袖口有漂亮的罗纹。

例句：He gave her a good-natured ribbing about her new haircut. 他善意地取笑她新剪的发型。


近义词：teasing, joking, jeering, raillery (戏谑),  knitting (指织物纹理)

反义词：praising, complimenting, flattery


词源:  From Middle English *ribbyng, from ribbe "rib," from Old English ribb.  The sense of teasing developed later, likely from the image of poking or prodding like ribs.
