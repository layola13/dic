redly:/ˈredli/| adv.| 红色地；红润地；血红地 |  In a red manner; with redness.  The word describes something done in a red way or with a red appearance. It can refer to color, blush, or blood. |例句：Her cheeks burned redly.  她的脸颊红彤彤的。

例句：The sunset painted the sky redly.  日落把天空染成一片红色。


近义词：ruddy, bloodily, crimsonly


反义词：palely, faintly


词性:副词
