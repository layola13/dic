redebited:/ˌriːˈdɛbɪtɪd/| v. |重新借记，再次借记 |To debit an account again, typically after a previous debit was reversed or unsuccessful.  This usually refers to financial transactions. |Example: My account was redebited after the initial payment was rejected. 我的账户在初始付款被拒绝后被重新借记。The bank redebited my account for the insufficient funds. 银行因余额不足而重新借记了我的账户。|近义词：recharged, rebilled |反义词：credited
