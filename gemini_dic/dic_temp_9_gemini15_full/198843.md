sideration:/ˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/| n. |考虑，体谅，周到|The act of considering something carefully; thoughtful consideration.|例句：The decision requires careful consideration of all the facts.  这个决定需要仔细考虑所有的事实。|例句：She showed great consideration for the feelings of others. 她非常体谅别人的感受。|近义词：consideration, deliberation, reflection, thoughtfulness|反义词：neglect, disregard, inconsiderateness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives careful consideration to every detail. 他对每一个细节都仔细考虑。
They are giving consideration to your proposal. 他们正在考虑你的提案。


一般过去时:
She gave considerable consideration to the problem. 她对这个问题给予了相当的考虑。
The committee gave much consideration to the matter. 委员会对这件事给予了充分的考虑。


现在完成时:
They have given careful consideration to the matter. 他们已经仔细考虑了这件事。
We have given much consideration to your request. 我们已经认真考虑了你的请求。


过去完成时:
She had given much consideration to the offer before rejecting it.  她在拒绝这个提议之前已经认真考虑过了。
They had given little consideration to the consequences.  他们在做出决定之前很少考虑后果。


将来时:
We will give your suggestion careful consideration. 我们会仔细考虑你的建议。
The board will give consideration to the new policy. 理事会将考虑这项新政策。


虚拟语气:
If they had given more consideration to the details, the project would have been more successful. 如果他们对细节给予更多考虑，项目会更成功。
I wish they had given more consideration to my feelings. 我希望他们能多考虑我的感受。


被动语态:
Your application is being given serious consideration. 你的申请正在被认真考虑。
The proposal was given little consideration by the board.  董事会对该提案几乎没有考虑。


疑问句:
Has consideration been given to the environmental impact?  是否考虑了对环境的影响？
What consideration did they give to the safety aspects? 他们对安全方面考虑了什么？


否定句:
They gave no consideration to my objections. 他们没有理会我的反对意见。
The plan received little consideration from the management. 这个计划没有得到管理层的重视。
