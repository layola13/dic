overtinting:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪntɪŋ/| n. |过度着色，颜色过深|The act of applying too much tint or color to something, resulting in an overly saturated or unnatural appearance.  It's often used in photography, graphic design, and makeup to describe an image or object with excessive color saturation.|Example: The overtinting of the photograph made the sky look unrealistically vibrant.  过度着色照片使天空看起来不真实地鲜艳。 The overtinting of her lipstick made her look clownish.  她口红的颜色太深让她看起来像小丑。|近义词：oversaturation, overcoloring|反义词：undercoloring, desaturation|名词
