karstenite:/ˈkɑːrstənˌaɪt/| n. |碳酸钙矿物|A rare mineral consisting of calcium carbonate (CaCO3), a polymorph of calcite. It is characterized by its orthorhombic crystal system and is found in some hydrothermal veins and low-temperature sedimentary environments.|Example: Karstenite, a less common form of calcium carbonate, was discovered in this geological formation.  卡斯滕石，一种较少见的碳酸钙形式，在这种地质构造中被发现。|近义词: calcite (though different crystal structure)|反义词: N/A
