caused:/kɔːzd/ | v. | 导致；引起 | To bring about or be the reason for (something, typically something bad).  |  The storm caused widespread flooding.  暴风雨导致了大范围的洪水。 The accident caused significant damage to the car. 事故对汽车造成了严重的损坏。 | resulted, triggered, initiated, provoked | prevented, stopped, avoided | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain causes the plants to grow. 雨水促使植物生长。
His actions cause problems for everyone. 他的行为给每个人都带来了麻烦。

一般过去时:
The fire caused extensive damage to the building. 火灾对建筑物造成了巨大的损失。
Her carelessness caused the accident. 她的粗心导致了事故。

现在完成时:
The drought has caused widespread famine. 干旱已造成大范围的饥荒。
His decisions have caused irreparable harm. 他的决定造成了不可挽回的伤害。

过去完成时:
The heavy rain had caused the river to overflow. 大雨导致河水泛滥。
The delay had caused him to miss the train. 延误导致他错过了火车。

将来时:
The new policy will cause some disruption. 新政策将造成一些混乱。
The storm will cause power outages. 暴风雨将导致停电。

虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have caused so many mistakes. 如果他学习更努力，他就不会犯这么多错误。
I wish I hadn't caused you so much trouble. 我真希望我没有给你带来这么多麻烦。

被动语态:
The damage was caused by the earthquake. 损害是由地震造成的。
The problem was caused by a software glitch. 问题是由软件故障引起的。

疑问句:
What caused the explosion? 什么导致了爆炸？
What caused the delay in the project? 什么导致了项目的延误？

否定句:
The rain didn't cause any damage. 雨水没有造成任何损失。
His actions didn't cause any problems. 他的行为没有造成任何问题。


PHRASE->
cause and effect 因果关系
cause for concern 令人担忧的原因
the cause of death 死因
contributing cause 促成原因
root cause 根源


词源:
The word "caused" is the past tense of the verb "cause," ultimately deriving from the Old French word "cause" and further back to the Latin word "causa," meaning "cause" or "reason."
