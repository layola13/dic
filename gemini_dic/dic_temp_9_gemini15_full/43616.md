creasing:/ˈkriːsɪŋ/ | n. v. |起褶，折痕；弄皱 |The action of making creases or folds in something; a wrinkle or fold.  |例句：The fabric shows signs of creasing after being packed for so long.  长时间打包后，布料上出现了褶皱。

The constant creasing of the paper made it difficult to read.  纸张不断地被折叠使得它难以阅读。


近义词：wrinkling, folding, crumpling |反义词：smoothing, ironing |词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
