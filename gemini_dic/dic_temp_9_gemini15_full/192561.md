sculler:/ˈskʌlə(r)/| n. |单桨艇运动员；划桨手；|A person who rows a scull.  A sculler is skilled in the technique of rowing a boat using two oars. |例句：He is a skilled sculler, winning many races. 他是一位技术娴熟的单桨艇运动员，赢得了许多比赛。

例句：The sculler pulled powerfully on the oars.  划桨手用力地划桨。


近义词：rower, oarsman |反义词：None
