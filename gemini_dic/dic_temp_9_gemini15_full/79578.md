gloomless:/ˈɡluːmləs/| adj.|无忧无虑的；快乐的；晴朗的|Free from gloom; cheerful; bright.  The word implies a state of being free from sadness, depression, or darkness, both literally and figuratively.|例句：The gloomless morning promised a pleasant day.  =  晴朗的早晨预示着美好的一天。

The gloomless atmosphere in the office was a welcome change. = 办公室里无忧无虑的气氛令人耳目一新。

近义词：cheerful, bright, sunny, happy, lighthearted |反义词：gloomy, dark, depressing, morose, somber

词性:形容词
