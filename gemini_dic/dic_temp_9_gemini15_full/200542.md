slashers: /ˈslæʃərz/ | n. | 砍杀者；（电影中的）凶杀狂 |  People who slash or stab others, often in a violent and uncontrolled manner.  The term is also frequently used to refer to the protagonists or antagonists of slasher films, a subgenre of horror films characterized by graphic violence and the killing of multiple victims. | Example: The police are hunting for the slashers responsible for the recent attacks.  警方正在追捕近期袭击事件中的凶手。

The term "slashers" derives from the verb "to slash," meaning to cut with a sweeping stroke.  The usage in relation to horror films is a relatively recent development, coinciding with the rise in popularity of the slasher film genre.


近义词: killers, murderers, butchers, maniacs
反义词: rescuers, saviors, protectors
