outdazzles: /ˌaʊtˈdæzəlz/ | vt. | 使…黯然失色；比…更耀眼 | To surpass in brilliance or splendor; to outshine.  |The diamond necklace outdazzled all the other jewelry. 这条钻石项链使其他所有珠宝都黯然失色。 |近义词：outshine, eclipse, surpass |反义词：be outshone by, be eclipsed by


USAGE->

The fireworks outdazzle the city lights.  烟火比城市灯光更耀眼。
Her beauty outdazzles everyone else in the room. 她的美貌使房间里其他人黯然失色。


一般过去时:
His performance outdazzled the audience. 他的表演让观众惊艳。
