perfects:/pərˈfɛkts/ | v. | 使完美；改进；完成 | To make perfect; to improve to a state of completeness or excellence; to bring to completion. |例句：He perfected his technique over years of practice. 他通过多年的练习完善了自己的技巧。


例句：She perfected the recipe after many attempts. 她经过多次尝试才完善了食谱。


近义词：improve, refine, complete | 反义词：ruin, spoil, mar | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perfects his skills daily. = 他每天都在完善他的技能。
They perfect their designs before launching. = 他们在发布之前完善他们的设计。

一般过去时:
She perfected the painting last night. = 她昨晚完成了这幅画。
He perfected his speech before the presentation. = 他在演讲前完善了他的演讲稿。

现在完成时:
They have perfected the technology. = 他们已经完善了这项技术。
She has perfected her baking skills. = 她已经完善了她的烘焙技术。

过去完成时:
He had perfected the method before he started the project. = 他在开始项目之前就已经完善了方法。
They had perfected their strategy before the game. = 比赛前他们已经完善了他们的策略。

将来时:
We will perfect the software by next month. = 我们将在下个月完善这个软件。
She will perfect her performance before the concert. = 演唱会之前她会完善她的表演。

虚拟语气:
