freethinker:/ˈfriːˌθɪŋkər/| n. |自由思想家，独立思考者 |A freethinker is someone who forms their own opinions and beliefs, rather than accepting those of a particular religion, political party, or other group.  They question traditional doctrines and authorities. | Example:  He was a freethinker who challenged the church's authority. 他是一位挑战教会权威的自由思想家。  She is known as a freethinker for her unconventional views on politics. 她因其对政治的非传统观点而被称为自由思想家。|近义词：independent thinker, maverick, nonconformist |反义词：conformist, traditionalist, dogmatist
