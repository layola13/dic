glycogenic:/ˌɡlaɪkəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|糖原生成的；生糖的| Relating to or promoting the formation of glycogen. Glycogenic substances are those that can be converted into glycogen in the body.  |Example:  The liver plays a crucial role in glycogenic processes.|近义词：glycogenetic|反义词：glycogenolytic
