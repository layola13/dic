prediscriminates:/ˌpriːdɪˈskrɪmɪneɪts/ | v. |预先歧视，预先区别对待 |To treat someone differently from others in a way that is unfair before having enough information to make a fair judgment. This often involves making assumptions or stereotypes about a person based on their group affiliation.  |例句：The company was accused of prediscriminating against applicants from certain ethnic backgrounds during the initial screening process.  公司被指控在初步筛选过程中歧视来自某些种族背景的申请人。

例句：The study showed that prediscriminating against individuals with disabilities often leads to a reduction in employment opportunities. 研究表明，歧视残疾人往往会导致就业机会减少。


近义词：prejudge, prejudicially discriminate, stereotype | 反义词：treat fairly, be unbiased
