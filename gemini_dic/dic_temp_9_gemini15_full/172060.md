purled:/pɜːld/| v. | 编织（针织物）, 使起卷曲 | To form (a fabric) by knitting or crocheting in a particular way, typically using a purl stitch.  It can also refer to the resulting fabric.  |例句：She purled the yarn to create a textured scarf. 她用反针编织毛线，做了一条纹理丰富的围巾。
The sweater was beautifully purled. 这件毛衣编织得非常漂亮。|近义词：knitted, crocheted |反义词：knit (in the context of contrasting knit and purl stitches)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She purls a complicated pattern. = 她正在编织复杂的图案。
He purls every other row. = 他每隔一行织反针。


一般过去时:
I purled the entire sweater yesterday. = 我昨天织完了整件毛衣。
She purled a beautiful hat for her daughter. = 她为女儿织了一顶漂亮的帽子。


现在完成时:
I have purled three scarves this month. = 这个月我已经织了三条围巾。
She has purled for many years. = 她已经织了很多年了。


过去完成时:
I had purled half the sweater before I ran out of yarn. = 我用完毛线之前已经织了一半的毛衣。
They had purled for hours before taking a break. = 他们休息之前已经织了好几个小时了。


将来时:
I will purl a blanket for the baby. = 我将为婴儿织一条毯子。
She will purl a new design next week. = 她下周将尝试新的编织图案。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would purl a quilt. = 如果我有更多时间，我会织一床被子。
I wish I could purl as well as my grandmother. = 我希望我的织造技术像我祖母一样好。


被动语态:
The sweater was purled by hand. = 这件毛衣是手工编织的。
The scarf is being purled using a special stitch. = 这条围巾正在用一种特殊的针法编织。


疑问句:
Do you purl or knit? = 你织正针还是反针？
Have you ever purled anything before? = 你以前编织过东西吗？


否定句:
He doesn't purl; he knits. = 他不织反针，他织正针。
She didn't purl the entire pattern correctly. = 她没有完全按照图案正确地织反针。


PHRASE->
purl stitch = 反针
purl one = 反针一针
purl two together = 两针反针一起织
purl three together = 三针反针一起织
garter stitch (which uses only purl stitches on one side and knit stitches on the other) = 罗纹针法 (在一面只用反针，另一面只用正针)
