impregnate:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪt/ | vt. |使怀孕；使充满；使渗透 | to make (a woman or female animal) pregnant; to fill something completely with a particular substance or quality | 例句：The farmer impregnated his cows with superior semen. 农场主用优良的精液使他的母牛受孕。  The soil was impregnated with chemicals. 土壤中充满了化学物质。 | 近义词：fertilize, saturate, permeate | 反义词：empty, deplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impregnates the soil with fertilizer. 他用肥料使土壤肥沃。
The artist impregnates her work with deep meaning.  这位艺术家赋予她的作品深刻的意义。


一般过去时:
The rain impregnated the ground with moisture. 雨水使地面湿润。
The artist impregnated his canvas with vibrant colors. 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩填满了他的画布。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has impregnated the earth with water.  持续的雨水使大地饱含水分。
Years of hard work have impregnated him with wisdom. 多年的努力使他充满了智慧。


过去完成时:
They had impregnated the fabric with a fire-retardant solution. 他们已经用阻燃剂处理了织物。
She had impregnated the cake batter with vanilla extract. 她已经用香草精浸渍了蛋糕糊。


将来时:
The company will impregnate the wood with a preservative. 公司将用防腐剂处理木材。
He will impregnate the canvas with oil paint. 他将用油彩涂满画布。


虚拟语气:
If he impregnated the soil properly, the crops would thrive. 如果他正确地施肥，庄稼就会茁壮成长。
If she had impregnated the fabric earlier, it would have been more effective. 如果她早点处理织物，效果会更好。


被动语态:
The wood was impregnated with a special resin. 木材经过特殊的树脂处理。
The fabric was impregnated with a water-repellent coating. 面料涂上了防水涂层。


疑问句:
Was the concrete impregnated with steel fibers? 混凝土中是否掺入了钢纤维？
Has the sponge been impregnated with cleaning solution? 海绵是否浸透了清洁液？


否定句:
The artist did not impregnate the canvas with enough paint. 艺术家没有在画布上涂足够的颜料。
They did not impregnate the seeds before planting. 他们播种前没有浸种。


PHRASE->
impregnate with:  用...浸渍
be impregnated with: 充满...
impregnated concrete: 渗透混凝土
impregnated wood: 浸渍木材
impregnated fabric: 浸渍织物

词源:
From Latin *impraegnāre, from in- "in, into" + praegnāre "to make pregnant," from praegnans "pregnant," from prae- "before" + gnāre "to bear, bring forth."  The sense shifted to "to fill thoroughly" in the 17th century.
