noosed:/nuːzd/| adj. v.|套索的；用套索套住的|Having been caught in or trapped by a noose.  A noose is a loop of rope or cord, especially one used for hanging someone.|The horse was noosed and gently led away. = 马被套索套住，然后轻轻地牵走了。The criminal was found noosed in his cell. = 罪犯被发现死在牢房里，脖子上套着套索。|近义词：snared, trapped, entangled|反义词：freed, released
