caverns:/ˈkævərnz/| n.|洞穴；岩洞 |Large underground chambers, typically in a system of interconnected passages.|例句：The explorers mapped the extensive caverns. 探险家们绘制了这些广阔洞穴的地图。

例句：We discovered beautiful stalactites hanging from the roof of the caverns. 我们发现美丽的钟乳石悬挂在洞穴的顶部。


近义词：caves, grottos, hollows|反义词：open space, surface


词源:  From Old French *caverne, from Latin *caverna ("cave, hollow"), from cavus ("hollow").
