rooked:/ˈruːkt/ | adj. | 欺骗的；狡猾的；不老实的 |  Characterized by dishonesty or trickery; deceitful.  The word often implies a clever or cunning deception, rather than outright villainy. | 例句：He felt he'd been rooked in the deal. 他觉得在这笔交易中被骗了。  The crooked salesman rooked many customers with his false promises.  那个不诚实的推销员用虚假承诺欺骗了许多顾客。 | 近义词：cheated, defrauded, swindled, tricked | 反义词：honest, upright, truthful, straightforward
