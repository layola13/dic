egalitarians:/ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriənz/| n. | 平等主义者 |Adherents of egalitarianism, believing in or advocating for social equality.  Egalitarians strive for a society where all individuals have equal rights, opportunities, and social status, regardless of their background or characteristics.  They often champion policies aimed at reducing inequality and promoting fairness.|  Many egalitarians support progressive taxation to redistribute wealth and provide social safety nets. |近义词：equalitarians, social reformers |反义词：elitists,authoritarians |名词
