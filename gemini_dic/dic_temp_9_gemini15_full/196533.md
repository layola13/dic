servus:/ˈsɜːrvəs/| n.|你好，奴隶，仆人|A Latin word meaning "slave" or "servant",  it's also used as a greeting meaning "hello" or "goodbye".  |例句：Servus! (Hello!)  Servus, domine. (Hello, master.)|近义词：hello, goodbye, slave, servant|反义词：master, lord


USAGE->
This word doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" because it is a noun, and in its usage as a greeting, it functions similarly to interjections.  Therefore, standard tense conjugation examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Servus! =  你好！/ 再见！
Servus et salve! =  你好！/ 再见！（more formal）
Vale servus! =  再见！（more formal, implying farewell to a servant）
