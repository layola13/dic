formerly:/ˈfɔːrməli/| adv.|以前，先前，从前|Previously; in the past.|例句：He formerly worked for a bank. 他以前在银行工作。|例句：The building was formerly a school. 这座建筑以前是一所学校。|近义词：previously, earlier, before, once|反义词：now, currently, presently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He formerly lives in London. = 他以前住在伦敦。(This sentence is grammatically correct but unnatural.  "Formerly" is usually used with past tense.)
They formerly attend this church. = 他们以前参加这个教会的活动。(This sentence is grammatically correct but unnatural.  "Formerly" is usually used with past tense.)

过去完成时:

将来时:
He will formerly be a famous writer. (Incorrect.  "Formerly" doesn't work with future tense.)
