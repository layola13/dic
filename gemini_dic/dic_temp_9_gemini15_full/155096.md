petrolatum: /ˌpɛtrəˈleɪtəm/ | n. |凡士林，石油脂| A jelly-like semi-solid mixture of hydrocarbons obtained from petroleum. It is used as a lubricant, protectant, and occlusive in various applications, including cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and industrial products.  It is also known as petroleum jelly. | 例句：The nurse applied petrolatum to the patient's wound to prevent infection. 护士在病人的伤口上涂抹凡士林以防止感染。
