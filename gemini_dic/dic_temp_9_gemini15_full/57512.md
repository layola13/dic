dragonfly: /ˈdræɡənˌflaɪ/ | n. | 蜻蜓 | A slender flying insect with two pairs of large, transparent wings, long slender body, and large eyes.  Dragonflies are predatory insects, feeding on other insects like mosquitoes.  | Example: A dragonfly zipped past my head.  一只蜻蜓从我头上飞过。


Example:  I watched a dragonfly hover over the pond. 我看着一只蜻蜓在池塘上方盘旋。


近义词:  damselfly (豆娘，与蜻蜓相似但体型更小，飞行更弱)


反义词:  None (没有明显的反义词)


词性: Noun (名词)
