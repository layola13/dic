metopic:/mɪˈtɒpɪk/| adj. |额缝的；额部联合的|Relating to the metopic suture, the line of junction between the two halves of the frontal bone in the skull.  The metopic suture is typically present in infants and usually fuses during childhood.  A persistent metopic suture is a condition where the suture remains unfused into adulthood. |例句：The radiograph revealed a persistent metopic suture.  X光片显示额缝持续存在。

The presence of a metopic suture is a normal developmental feature in newborns. 额缝的存在是新生儿正常发育特征。|近义词：frontal (in the context of the skull)|反义词：None specifically, but terms describing a fused frontal bone could be considered antonyms.
