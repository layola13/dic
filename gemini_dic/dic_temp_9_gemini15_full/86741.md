help:/hɛlp/| v. n.|帮助，援助|to give assistance to someone or something; to make it easier for someone to do something.|例句：Can you help me with this problem? 你能帮我解决这个问题吗？|例句：He helped his friend move house. 他帮助他的朋友搬家。|近义词：assist, aid, support|反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I help my neighbor with gardening. = 我帮助我的邻居做园艺。
She helps her children with their homework. = 她帮助她的孩子们做作业。

一般过去时:
He helped me fix my car. = 他帮我修车。
They helped the victims of the earthquake. = 他们帮助了地震的受害者。

现在完成时:
I have helped many people in need. = 我已经帮助过许多需要帮助的人。
She has helped raise a lot of money for charity. = 她已经为慈善机构筹集了很多钱。

过去完成时:
I had helped him before he asked me. = 在他问我之前我已经帮过他了。
She had helped her sister prepare the party. = 在她之前已经帮她姐姐准备派对。

将来时:
I will help you with your project. = 我会帮你做你的项目。
They will help us move the furniture. = 他们会帮我们搬家具。

虚拟语气:
If he helped me, I would finish on time. = 如果他帮我，我会按时完成。
I wish she had helped me earlier. = 我真希望她早点帮我。

被动语态:
I was helped by a kind stranger. = 我得到了一位好心陌生人的帮助。
The victims were helped by many volunteers. = 受害者得到了许多志愿者的帮助。

疑问句:
Can you help me? = 你能帮我吗？
Will you help us tomorrow? = 你明天会帮我们吗？

否定句:
I cannot help you with that. = 我帮不了你那个。
He didn't help me at all. = 他根本没帮我。


PHRASE->
help oneself to = 自便
help out = 帮助；援助
can't help but = 不禁
help with = 帮助…
with the help of = 在…的帮助下
