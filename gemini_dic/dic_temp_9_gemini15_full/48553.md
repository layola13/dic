defamatory: /dɪˈfæmətɔːri/ | adj. |诽谤的；损害名誉的 |  Damaging someone's reputation through false statements.  This term refers to statements that are both false and harmful to a person's reputation. It implies intentionality, though not necessarily malice.  |例句：He filed a lawsuit against the newspaper for publishing defamatory articles about him. 他就报纸刊登关于他的诽谤文章向其提起诉讼。

The statement made was defamatory and caused significant damage to her career.  那份声明是诽谤性的，并对其事业造成了重大损害。|近义词：libelous, slanderous, calumnious, injurious |反义词：flattering, complimentary, laudatory
