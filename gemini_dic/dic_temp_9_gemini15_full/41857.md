coryza:/kəˈraɪzə/| n. |普通感冒，鼻塞|A coryza is a common cold, characterized by inflammation of the mucous membrane of the nose.  It's often accompanied by sneezing, runny nose, and congestion.|Example:  She suffered from a severe coryza and had to stay home from work. 她患了重感冒，不得不请假在家休息。|近义词：common cold, rhinitis|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms implying absence of illness, such as health or wellness.|名词
