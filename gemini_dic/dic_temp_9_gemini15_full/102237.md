itemizer:/ˈaɪtəmaɪzər/| n. |项目列举者；明细表；分项列举器|A person or thing that itemizes; a detailed list.  An itemizer is often used in accounting or financial contexts to create a list of individual items with their corresponding values.|例句：The accountant used an itemizer to prepare the detailed financial report. 会计用项目列举器准备详细的财务报告。The itemizer showed each expense separately. 明细表分别列出了每一笔支出。|近义词：lister, detailler, cataloger|反义词：summarizer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software itemizes all the expenses. = 该软件列举所有支出。
He itemizes his income and expenses every month. = 他每月都详细列出他的收支。

一般过去时:
She itemized her deductions on her tax return. = 她在她纳税申报单上逐条列明了她的扣除额。
The auditor itemized all the discrepancies found in the accounts. = 审计员逐条列出了在账目中发现的所有差异。

过去完成时:
