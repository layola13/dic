killjoys:/ˈkɪlˌdʒɔɪz/| n.|扫兴的人，讨人厌的人|People who spoil the fun of others; those who deliberately dampen enthusiasm or enjoyment.  |例句：Don't be such a killjoy! Let's have some fun.  别那么扫兴！让我们玩得开心点。  |例句：The killjoys at the party refused to dance. 派对上的那些扫兴的人拒绝跳舞。|近义词：spoilsport, party pooper, wet blanket|反义词：life of the party, enthusiast
