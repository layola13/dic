noncolors:/ˈnɑːnˌkʌlərz/ | n. |非颜色；非彩色 |Noncolors are colors that are not considered to be true colors, often referring to shades of gray, white, black, or beige. They are often used in design and art to create a neutral or understated aesthetic.  |Example: The artist used a palette of noncolors to create a sense of calm and tranquility in the painting.  画家使用非彩色调色板在绘画中营造宁静感。|近义词：neutrals, achromatics |反义词：colors, chromatics |名词
