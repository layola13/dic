sharing: /ˈʃɛərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 分享，共享 | The act of giving someone a portion of something you possess; the state of being shared.  | 例句：Sharing is caring. = 分享是一种关爱。

例句：We're sharing a pizza. = 我们在分享一个披萨。


近义词：distribution, contribution, participation | 反义词：hoarding, keeping to oneself


词性: 名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shares his toys with his friends. = 他和朋友们分享他的玩具。
They share a flat. = 他们合住一套公寓。


一般过去时:
She shared her experiences with us. = 她与我们分享了她的经历。
We shared a taxi to the airport. = 我们合乘一辆出租车去机场。


现在完成时:
I have shared my ideas with the team. = 我已经与团队分享了我的想法。
They have shared a lot of happy memories together. = 他们一起分享了很多美好的回忆。


过去完成时:
They had shared their concerns before the meeting. = 他们在会议前分享了他们的担忧。
She had shared her secret with her best friend. = 她已经和她最好的朋友分享了她的秘密。


将来时:
We will share the profits equally. = 我们将平均分配利润。
He will share his knowledge with the students. = 他将与学生分享他的知识。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would share my story. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会分享我的故事。
I wish I could share your happiness. = 我希望我能分享你的快乐。


被动语态:
The profits were shared equally among the partners. = 利润在合伙人之间平均分配。
The information was shared with all employees. = 信息与所有员工共享。


疑问句:
Will you share your food with me? = 你愿意和我分享你的食物吗？
Do they share the same opinion? = 他们有相同的意见吗？


否定句:
He doesn't share my views on politics. = 他不同意我对政治的看法。
She didn't share her concerns with anyone. = 她没有与任何人分享她的担忧。


PHRASE->
share a meal: 共进餐食
share a secret: 分享秘密
share the cost: 分担费用
share a ride: 合乘
share an opinion: 分享观点
