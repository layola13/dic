hypopituitary: /ˌhaɪpoʊpɪˈtjuːɪtɛri/ | adj. | 垂体功能减退的；腺垂体功能不全的 |  Relating to or characterized by deficient activity of the pituitary gland.  It describes a condition where the pituitary gland doesn't produce enough hormones.
