hogskin:/ˈhɒɡskɪn/| n.|猪皮；猪皮革|Pigskin is the skin of a hog, typically used for making leather.  It is known for its durability and is often used in the production of leather goods such as footballs, saddles, and book bindings.|例句：The old football was made of genuine hogskin. 这只旧足球是用真猪皮做的。|例句：The craftsman carefully worked the hogskin to create a beautiful leather bag. 工匠仔细地加工猪皮，制作了一个漂亮的皮包。|近义词：pigskin, leather|反义词：无
