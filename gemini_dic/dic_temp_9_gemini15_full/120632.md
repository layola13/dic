mis-encoding:/ˌmɪsˈɛnkoʊdɪŋ/| n.|错误编码，误编码|The process of encoding data incorrectly, leading to data corruption or inability to interpret the data correctly.  This can occur due to software bugs, hardware malfunctions, or incorrect data entry. |例句：The mis-encoding of the file resulted in data loss.  文件错误编码导致数据丢失。|近义词：malencoding, incorrect encoding, faulty encoding|反义词：correct encoding, proper encoding|名词
