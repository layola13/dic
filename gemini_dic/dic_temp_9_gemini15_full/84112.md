half-learnedly:/ˌhæfˈlɜːrnɪdli/ | adv. | 半生不熟地；不熟练地；一知半解地 |  In a way that shows incomplete or superficial knowledge or skill. It suggests a lack of mastery or proficiency in a subject or skill. |例句：He spoke English half-learnedly, making many grammatical errors. 他英语说得半生不熟，犯了很多语法错误。

He played the piano half-learnedly, stumbling over several notes. 他弹钢琴弹得半生不熟，好几个音符都弹错了。


近义词：ineptly, clumsily, awkwardly, imperfectly, superficially | 反义词： proficiently, expertly, skillfully, masterfully


词性:副词
