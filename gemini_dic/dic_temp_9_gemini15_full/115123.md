mangle:/ˈmæŋɡəl/| vt. n.|毁坏；弄糟；严重损坏；使变形|To mutilate, disfigure, or damage severely; to spoil or ruin.  The word often implies a violent or chaotic destruction.|例句：The car was mangled in the accident.  这辆车在事故中严重损坏。

例句：The report was so badly mangled that it was useless. 这份报告被弄得乱七八糟，毫无用处。


近义词：mutilate, damage, ruin, spoil, deform, butcher, crush


反义词：repair, restore, improve, mend


词性:动词
