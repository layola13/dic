linocutting:/ˈlɪnəʊkʌtɪŋ/ | n. | 林版雕刻术 | The process of creating prints by carving a design into a linoleum block and then inking and printing it.  Linocutting is a type of relief printing. | Example:  She took a linocutting class and produced several beautiful prints. 她参加了一个林版雕刻班，制作了几幅精美的版画。 | 近义词:  relief printing, printmaking | 反义词:  N/A
