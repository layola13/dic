postscripts:/ˈpoʊstˌskrɪpts/| n. |附注；后记|A postscript is an additional note added to a letter or document after it has been written.  It's often used to add a thought or piece of information that was forgotten or came to mind after the main text was completed. |Example: I added a postscript to my letter to clarify a point. 我在信中添加了一个附注以阐明一个观点。|近义词：addendum, afterthought, codicil |反义词：prelude, preface
