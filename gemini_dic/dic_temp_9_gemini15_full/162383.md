preaction:/ˈpriːækʃən/| n. |预先行动；预防措施|An action taken in advance to prevent something from happening or to prepare for something.  It often involves anticipating a problem and taking steps to mitigate its potential impact.|例句：The preaction fire suppression system was activated.  = 预先启动了灭火系统。|例句：Regular maintenance is a form of preaction against equipment failure. = 定期维护是预防设备故障的一种预先行动。|近义词：prevention, precaution, anticipation|反义词：reaction, response
