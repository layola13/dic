enfleurage:/ˌɑ̃nfluˈrɑːʒ/| n. | 浸提法（一种萃取香精油的方法）|A process of extracting fragrance from flowers by absorbing their scent in a fat or oil.  It's a traditional method of perfume production.  The fragrant materials are layered on a bed of fat, and the scent is gradually absorbed over time.|Example: Enfleurage is a time-consuming but gentle method for extracting delicate flower fragrances.|近义词:extraction, maceration|反义词:None.
