hibernates:/ˈhaɪbərneɪts/| v.|冬眠；蛰伏；暂停活动|To spend the winter in a dormant state, characterized by slowed metabolism and reduced body temperature.  Many animals hibernate during the winter months to conserve energy.|例句：Bears hibernate in caves during the winter.  熊在冬天在洞穴里冬眠。

例句：The software hibernates to save energy when not in use. 软件在不用时休眠以节省能源。|近义词：dormant, inactive, sleeps|反义词：active, awake
