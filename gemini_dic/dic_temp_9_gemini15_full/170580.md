pseudo-catholic:/ˌsuːdoʊˈkæθəlɪk/| adj.|假天主教的；冒充天主教徒的|Apparently Catholic but lacking genuine Catholic belief or practice.  It describes something that superficially resembles Catholicism but lacks its essential tenets.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting insincerity or a lack of genuine faith.|例句：His pseudo-Catholic piety was evident in his actions, which contradicted his professed beliefs. 他伪善的天主教虔诚在他与自己信仰相悖的行为中显而易见。|例句：The movement presented a pseudo-Catholic facade to attract followers, but its theology was radically different.  这个运动披着天主教的外衣以吸引追随者，但其神学却大相径庭。|近义词：sham, imitation, counterfeit, spurious |反义词：genuine, authentic, true, orthodox
