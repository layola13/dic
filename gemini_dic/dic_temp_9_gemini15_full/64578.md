erst:/ɜːrst/| adv.| прежде, прежде всего, раньше, ранее | formerly, previously, at first, in the first place |例句：Erst später erkannte er seinen Fehler. = He only realized his mistake later.  |例句：Erst nach langem Überlegen entschied er sich. = Only after long consideration did he decide. |近义词：formerly, previously, before, earlier |反义词：later, afterwards, subsequently
