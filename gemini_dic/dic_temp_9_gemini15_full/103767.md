journalism:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlɪzəm/ | n. |新闻业；新闻工作；新闻学 |The practice of collecting, assessing, creating, and presenting news and information. It includes a range of activities, from reporting and interviewing to editing and publishing.  | Example:  She pursued a career in journalism after graduating from college. 她大学毕业后从事新闻工作。  |近义词：reporting, news reporting, news media |反义词：N/A
