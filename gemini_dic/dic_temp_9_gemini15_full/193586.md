self-approbations:/ˌsɛlf æprəˈbeɪʃənz/| n.|自赞；自我表扬|The act of praising oneself; self-congratulation.  It often implies excessive or undeserved praise.|例句：His self-approbations were tiresome to listen to.  他的自夸令人厌烦。


例句：Her constant self-approbations revealed a deep insecurity. 她不断地自我表扬暴露了她内心深处的不安全感。


近义词：self-praise, self-congratulation, vanity, conceit


反义词：self-criticism, humility, modesty


词性：名词
