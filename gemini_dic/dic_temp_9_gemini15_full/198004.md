shockable:/ˈʃɑːkəbəl/| adj.| 可电击的，适合电击的 | Capable of being successfully treated with a defibrillator.  This term refers to a patient whose heart rhythm is such that defibrillation is likely to be effective. | Example: The patient was deemed shockable and received immediate defibrillation.  病人被认为适合电击，并立即接受了除颤。

The paramedics determined the victim was shockable and administered a defibrillator shock. 急救人员确定受害者适合电击，并施行了除颤。|近义词：defibrillatable |反义词：nonshockable
