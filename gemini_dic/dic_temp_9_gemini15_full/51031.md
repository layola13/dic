desmid:/ˈdɛzmɪd/| n. |衣藻|A desmid is a single-celled green alga belonging to the order Desmidiales, characterized by its unique cell structure, often with symmetrical halves joined by an isthmus. They are found in freshwater habitats, particularly in acidic bogs and clean, nutrient-poor waters.|例句：The microscopic desmids revealed a surprisingly diverse community in the bog.  显微镜下的衣藻揭示了沼泽中一个惊人多样化的群落。|近义词：green alga (广义)|反义词：None|词性：名词
