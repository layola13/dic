sizing:/ˈsaɪzɪŋ/| n. |尺寸确定；大小调整；施胶；上浆|The process of determining the size or dimensions of something; the act of adjusting something to the correct size; applying a sizing agent to a material to improve its properties.|例句：The sizing of the window frames is crucial for a perfect fit.  窗户框架的尺寸确定对于完美贴合至关重要。|例句：The tailor did some careful sizing before cutting the fabric.裁缝在剪裁布料之前进行了一些仔细的尺寸调整。|近义词：dimensioning, measuring, scaling|反义词：N/A|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
