interposit:/ˌɪntərˈpɒzɪt/| /ˌɪntərˈpoʊzɪt/ | vt.|插入；介入；使…居于两者之间|To place (something) between two or more things; to intervene or intercede.  The word implies a deliberate act of placing something in between, often to mediate or separate.|例句：The mediator attempted to interposit himself between the warring factions. 调解员试图介入交战的各派之间。|例句：She interposited a screen to block the harsh sunlight. 她放了一块屏风来挡住刺眼的阳光。|近义词：intervene, intercede, insert, introduce|反义词：remove, withdraw, extract|动词
