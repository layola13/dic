flesh-out:/fleʃ aʊt/| vt.|充实，详细阐述|to add more details or information to something, making it more complete or comprehensive. |例句：We need to flesh out the plan with more specific details.我们需要用更具体的细节来充实这个计划。 |例句：He fleshed out his argument with several examples. 他用几个例子充实了他的论点。|近义词：elaborate, expand, develop, amplify|反义词：abbreviate, condense, summarize|动词


USAGE->

He fleshes out his ideas in his writing. = 他在写作中充实了他的想法。
She fleshes out the story with vivid descriptions. = 她用生动的描述充实了这个故事。


一般过去时:
