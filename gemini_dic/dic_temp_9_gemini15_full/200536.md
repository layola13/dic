slapstick: /ˈslæpstɪk/ | n. |滑稽剧，闹剧 | A type of comedy characterized by physical humor, exaggerated movements, and often silly or violent actions.  It relies on visual gags and pratfalls rather than witty dialogue or sophisticated plotting. | Example: The movie was a slapstick comedy filled with pratfalls and pie-throwing.  这部电影是一部充满滑稽动作和扔馅饼的闹剧喜剧。  | 近义词: farce, buffoonery, clowning | 反义词: sophisticated comedy, drama
