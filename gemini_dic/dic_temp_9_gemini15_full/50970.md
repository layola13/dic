desilverization:/ˌdiːsɪlvəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |脱银；除银|The process of removing silver from something, such as lead or other metals.  This often involves chemical or electrochemical methods.  For example, in the refining of lead, desilverization is a crucial step to obtain high-purity lead.|The refinery uses a Parkes process for desilverization of the lead bullion.  炼油厂采用帕克斯法去除铅锭中的银。|近义词：silver removal, refining|反义词：silver plating, silver addition
