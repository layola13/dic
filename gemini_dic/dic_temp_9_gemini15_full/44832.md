crustaceans:/krʌˈsteɪʃənz/ | n. |甲壳类动物 |Crustaceans are a large group of arthropods that includes crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles. They are characterized by their hard exoskeletons, segmented bodies, and jointed appendages.  |例句：Many crustaceans are delicious seafood. 许多甲壳类动物都是美味的海鲜。|例句：The ocean is home to a vast array of crustaceans. 海洋是种类繁多的甲壳类动物的家园。|近义词：shellfish, arthropods |反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑vertebrates脊椎动物作为对比)|名词
