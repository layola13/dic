presbyopy:/ˌprezbiˈoʊpiə/| n. |老年性远视,老花眼 |A gradual loss of your eyes' ability to focus on nearby objects, occurring as you age.|As I've gotten older, I've developed presbyopia and need reading glasses.  随着年龄增长，我得了老花眼，需要戴老花镜。|近义词：farsightedness, hyperopia|反义词：myopia
