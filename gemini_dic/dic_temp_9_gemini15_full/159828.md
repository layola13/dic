polygynian: /pɒlɪˈdʒɪniən/ | adj. |一夫多妻制的；具有多雌现象的 | Relating to or characterized by polygyny, a system of marriage in which a man has more than one wife.  The term can also be applied to animal species where one male mates with multiple females. | Example:  The anthropologist studied a polygynian society in rural Africa. 人类学家研究了非洲农村的一个一夫多妻制社会。  The species exhibits a strong polygynian mating system.  该物种表现出强烈的一夫多妻制交配系统。 | 近义词: polygynous | 反义词: monogamous
