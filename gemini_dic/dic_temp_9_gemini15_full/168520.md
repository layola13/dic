proboycott:/ˈproʊˌbɔɪkɑːt/ | n. | 支持抵制；赞成抵制 |  A proboycott is the act of publicly supporting a boycott. It involves actively promoting or advocating for a boycott of a particular product, company, or country. This support can take various forms, including participating in the boycott, spreading awareness about the boycott's goals, or donating to organizations that are organizing the boycott.  | Example: The proboycott movement against the company gained significant traction after the release of the damning report.  支持该公司抵制运动在该谴责报告发布后获得了很大的发展。| 近义词：support of boycott, advocacy for boycott | 反义词：opposition to boycott, support of the targeted entity | 名词
