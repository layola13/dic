henpeck:/ˈhenˌpek/| vt.|唠叨；数落；经常责骂（丈夫）|To repeatedly scold or nag (someone, especially one's husband).  The word implies a nagging and controlling behavior, often from a wife towards her husband.|例句：His wife henpecked him constantly about his messy habits.  他妻子不断地唠叨他邋遢的习惯。
例句：He was henpecked into agreeing to go to the opera. 他被唠叨得不得不答应去看歌剧。|近义词：nag, scold, chide, berate, browbeat |反义词：indulge, pamper,  spoil|动词
