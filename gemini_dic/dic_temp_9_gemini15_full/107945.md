/ˈlærɪŋɡətoʊm/| n. |喉切刀|A laryngotome is a surgical instrument used to incise or cut the larynx (voice box). It is typically used in laryngeal surgeries to remove tumors, polyps, or other growths, or to perform a tracheotomy.  |Example: The surgeon used a laryngotome to carefully excise the cancerous growth from the patient's larynx.  外科医生用喉切刀小心地切除了患者喉咙里的癌性肿瘤。|近义词:laryngeal knife, surgical knife|反义词:N/A
