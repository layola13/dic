language:/ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ |/ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/| n. |语言|A system of communication used by a particular country or community.|English is a global language.  英语是一种全球语言。|近义词：tongue, speech, idiom|反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What language do you speak? = 你说哪种语言？
Which language is spoken in Brazil? = 巴西说哪种语言？


一般过去时:
What language did they speak in the past? = 他们过去说哪种语言？
What language did she learn in school? = 她在学校学了什么语言？


现在完成时:
What languages have you learned? = 你学过哪些语言？
How many languages has she mastered? = 她掌握了几种语言？


过去完成时:
What language had she studied before moving abroad? = 在她搬到国外之前学过什么语言？
What language had they spoken before switching to English? = 他们在改说英语之前说过什么语言？


将来时:
What language will you learn next? = 你接下来要学什么语言？

虚拟语气:
If I spoke another language, I would travel more. = 如果我会说另一种语言，我会旅游更多。
I wish I knew more languages. = 我希望我会说更多语言。


被动语态:
What language is spoken in this region? = 这个地区说哪种语言？

疑问句:

What language are you writing in? = 你用什么语言写？


否定句:
