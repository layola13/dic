cheer-up:/tʃɪər ʌp/| v. |使振作起来；高兴起来 |To encourage someone who is sad or depressed to feel happier.  |例句：Cheer up! Things will get better. 振作起来！情况会好转的。

例句：He tried to cheer up his friend after she lost her job. 他试图在他朋友失业后使她振作起来。


近义词：brighten up, liven up, encourage, console |反义词：depress, discourage, sadden

词性:动词
