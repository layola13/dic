To approve, support, or sanction. |例句：The bank endorsed the check. 银行在支票上签了字。|例句： I endorse your efforts to improve the community. 我支持你改善社区的努力。|近义词：approve, support, sanction, confirm, ratify |反义词：reject, oppose, condemn, disapprove |动词
