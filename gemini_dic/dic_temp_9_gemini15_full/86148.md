heartland:/ˈhɑːrtlænd/|/ˈhɑːrtˌlænd/| n. |中心地带；核心地区；内地|The central region of a country or continent; especially, a region considered to be the most typical or representative of a nation.  It often refers to an area perceived as the cultural or political center of a nation, embodying its values and traditions.|例句：The American heartland is often associated with farming and traditional values. 美国的心脏地带通常与农业和传统价值观联系在一起。|例句：The Midwest is considered the heartland of the United States. 中西部被认为是美国的心脏地带。|近义词：core, center, interior, hinterland|反义词：periphery, outskirts, fringe
