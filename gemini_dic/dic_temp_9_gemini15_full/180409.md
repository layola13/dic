regains:/riˈɡeɪnz/| vt.|重新获得，恢复|To get back something that was lost or taken away.|例句：He regained consciousness after the accident. 他在事故后恢复了意识。|例句：The team regained its winning streak.  球队重新赢得了连胜。|近义词：recover, retrieve, repossess|反义词：lose, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regains his composure quickly. = 他很快恢复了镇静。
She regains her strength gradually. = 她逐渐恢复了体力。


一般过去时:
The country regained its independence. = 这个国家重新获得了独立。
He regained his sight after the surgery. = 手术后他恢复了视力。


现在完成时:
She has regained her confidence after the setback. = 挫折后她已经恢复了自信。
He has regained his position in the company. = 他已经重新获得了在公司的地位。


过去完成时:
They had regained control of the situation before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他们已经重新控制了局面。
She had regained her balance before falling again. = 她差点摔倒，但又恢复了平衡。


将来时:
He will regain his health soon. = 他很快就会恢复健康。
The economy will regain its momentum next year. = 经济明年将恢复增长势头。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have regained his title. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他就能夺回冠军头衔。
I wish I could regain my youth. = 我真希望我能恢复青春。


被动语态:
The lost treasure was never regained. = 丢失的宝藏再也没有找回。
Control of the city was regained by the government forces. = 城市的控制权被政府军夺回。


疑问句:
Will she ever regain her memory? = 她还能恢复记忆吗？
Can he regain his fitness before the competition? = 他能在比赛前恢复体能吗？


否定句:
He did not regain consciousness until hours later. = 他直到几个小时后才恢复意识。
She has not yet regained her full mobility. = 她尚未完全恢复行动能力。
