enharden:/ɪnˈhɑːrdn/| vt.|使硬化；使坚强；使坚定|To make something hard or harder; to make someone or something stronger or more determined.|例句：The sun hardened the clay.  太阳使粘土变硬了。|例句：The experience hardened her resolve.  这次经历使她更加坚定。|近义词：harden, toughen, strengthen|反义词：soften, weaken

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun hardens the clay. = 太阳使粘土变硬。
He hardens his resolve daily. = 他每天都在坚定自己的决心。

一般过去时:
The cold weather hardened the ground. = 寒冷的天气使地面变硬了。
The crisis hardened their opinions. = 危机使他们的意见更加坚定。

现在完成时:
Years of hardship have hardened her heart. = 多年的苦难使她变得冷漠无情。
The constant pressure has hardened his resolve. =  持续的压力使他更加坚定。

过去完成时:
The long journey had hardened the leather of his boots. = 漫长的旅程使他的靴子皮革变硬了。
She had hardened her stance before the debate. = 在辩论之前，她已经坚定了自己的立场。

将来时:
The heat will harden the cement. = 热量会使水泥变硬。
The coming challenges will harden their spirit. = 未来挑战将会磨练他们的精神。

虚拟语气:
If we hardened our hearts, we would never help. = 如果我们铁石心肠，就不会帮助别人。
I wish I had hardened my resolve sooner. = 我希望我早点下定决心。

被动语态:
The clay is hardened by the sun. = 粘土被太阳晒硬了。
His resolve was hardened by the difficulties. = 他的决心因困难而更加坚定。

疑问句:
Does the cold weather harden the roads? = 寒冷的天气会使道路变硬吗？
Will this experience harden his character? = 这次经历会使他的性格变得坚强吗？

否定句:
The heat didn't harden the mixture enough. = 热量不足以使混合物充分变硬。
He didn't harden his heart to their pleas. = 他没有对他们的请求无动于衷。
