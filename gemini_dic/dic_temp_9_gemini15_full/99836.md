intermittence:/ɪntərˈmɪtəns/| n. |间歇；断续；中断 |The state or fact of being intermittent; occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous.  |例句：The rain fell with intermittence throughout the day.  = 雨水一整天断断续续地下着。


例句：The intermittence of the power supply caused problems for the factory. = 供电中断给工厂带来了问题。


近义词：interruption, discontinuance, discontinuity, fluctuation


反义词：continuity, constancy, permanence


词性：名词
