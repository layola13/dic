paramours:/ˈpærəmɔːrz/| n.|情妇，情人|A paramour is a lover, especially one in a secret or illicit relationship.  The term typically implies a romantic relationship outside of marriage or a committed relationship.|例句：He had a secret paramour for many years. 他多年来一直有一个秘密情人。

例句：Their clandestine meetings with their paramours were finally discovered. 他们与情人的秘密会面最终被发现了。
