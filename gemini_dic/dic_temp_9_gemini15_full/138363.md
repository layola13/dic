notify:/ˈnoʊtɪfaɪ/| vt. |通知，告知|To inform someone officially or formally of something.|The company notified its customers of the price increase. 公司通知其客户价格上涨。|inform, advise, alert|ignore, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They notify customers of changes via email. = 他们通过电子邮件通知客户更改。
The bank notifies you of any suspicious activity. = 银行会将任何可疑活动通知您。


一般过去时:
She notified the police about the accident. = 她向警方报告了事故。
He notified his boss of his resignation. = 他通知老板他辞职了。


现在完成时:
I have notified them of the delay. = 我已经通知他们延误了。
Have you notified your insurance company? = 你已经通知你的保险公司了吗？


过去完成时:
They had notified the authorities before the incident. = 他们在事件发生前已通知了当局。
She had already notified him of the meeting. = 她已经通知他开会了。


将来时:
I will notify you as soon as I have the results. = 我一有结果就会通知你。
We will notify all participants of the schedule change. = 我们将通知所有参与者时间安排的更改。


被动语态:
Customers will be notified of any delays. = 客户将被告知任何延误。
He was notified of the cancellation. = 他被告知取消了。


疑问句:
Have you been notified of the changes? = 你是否已经被告知这些变化？
Will the participants be notified in advance? = 参与者会提前被告知吗？


否定句:
They did not notify me of the problem. = 他们没有将这个问题通知我。
I haven't been notified yet. = 我还没有被通知。


PHRASE->
notify someone of something = 通知某人某事
notify someone by phone/email = 通过电话/电子邮件通知某人
be notified of something = 被告知某事
give notice = 发出通知
official notification = 正式通知
