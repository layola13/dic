hay:/heɪ/| n. |干草，草料 |Dried grass, cut and cured for use as fodder for livestock.  |The farmer gathered the hay for his cows.  农民收集干草喂他的奶牛。|straw, fodder|  fresh grass, green grass|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer cuts hay every summer. = 农民每年夏天都割草。
There is plenty of hay in the barn. = 谷仓里有大量的干草。

一般过去时:
The horses ate all the hay last night. = 马昨晚把所有的干草都吃光了。
They baled the hay yesterday. = 他们昨天打包了干草。

现在完成时:
We have harvested all the hay this year. = 今年我们已经收割了所有的干草。
The farmer has stored the hay for winter. = 农民已经储存了过冬的干草。

过去完成时:
They had already gathered the hay before the rain started. = 在下雨之前他们已经收集好了干草。
The horses had eaten most of the hay by the time I arrived. = 当我到达时，马已经吃掉了大部分的干草。

将来时:
We will cut the hay next week. = 我们下周将割草。
The farmer will bale the hay tomorrow. = 农民明天将打包干草。

虚拟语气:
If we had more hay, the animals would be better fed. = 如果我们有更多的干草，动物们就能得到更好的喂养。
I wish we had enough hay to last the winter. = 我希望我们有足够的干草撑过冬天。

被动语态:
The hay was cut by the farmer. = 干草是由农民割的。
The hay is being baled now. = 干草正在被打包。

疑问句:
Is there enough hay for the winter? = 冬天有足够的干草吗？
Where is the hay stored? = 干草储存在哪里？

否定句:
There isn't enough hay for all the animals. = 没有足够的干草喂养所有动物。
They didn't have time to bale the hay. = 他们没有时间打包干草。
