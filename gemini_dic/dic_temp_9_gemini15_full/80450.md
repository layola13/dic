gonged:/ɡɒŋd/ | adj. | 被锣声打断的；被突然中止的 |  Describes something that has been abruptly stopped or interrupted, often by a loud noise like a gong.  | The meeting was gonged due to a fire alarm.  会议由于火警而被突然中止。 | interrupted, stopped, halted | continued, prolonged |  adj.
