mortaring: /ˈmɔːrtərɪŋ/ | v. | 砌砖；抹灰；用砂浆填缝 |  The act of applying mortar to bricks or stones, typically in construction.  | 例句：The builders were busy mortaring the bricks together.  = 建设工人正忙着砌砖。

The workers spent the afternoon mortaring the cracks in the old wall. = 工人们花了一下午的时间用砂浆填补旧墙上的裂缝。


近义词：pointing, cementing, plastering | 反义词：demolishing, destroying | 词性：动词
