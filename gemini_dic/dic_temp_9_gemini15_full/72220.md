floret:/ˈflɒrɛt/| n. |小花，小筒状花|A floret is a small individual flower, especially one of those composing a flower head in plants of the daisy family.  It is typically tubular or ligulate in shape, and contributes to the overall appearance of the composite flower. |Example: The sunflower head is composed of numerous tiny florets. 向日葵花头由许多细小的花朵组成。|近义词：floretlet, small flower|反义词：None
