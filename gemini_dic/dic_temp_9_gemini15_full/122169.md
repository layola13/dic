mistune:/mɪsˈtjuːn/| v.|  使走调；使不和谐；扰乱；使不愉快 | to cause something to be out of tune or harmony; to disturb or upset someone or something. |例句：The pianist mistuned the instrument.  钢琴家把乐器调得不准了。|例句：The constant noise mistuned her peaceful mood.  持续的噪音扰乱了她平静的心情。|近义词：disarrange, upset, disharmony |反义词：harmonize, tune, soothe
