legacy's:/ˈlɛɡəsiːz/| n.|遗产；遗留物；传统|The word "legacy's" is the possessive form of the noun "legacy," indicating that something belongs to or is associated with a legacy.  A legacy refers to something that is handed down from the past, such as a gift of money or property, a tradition, or a reputation. It can be tangible or intangible. |例句：The company's legacy is one of innovation and quality. 这家公司的遗产是创新和质量。

The museum houses a rich legacy of art and artifacts.  博物馆收藏着丰富的艺术品和文物遗产。|近义词：inheritance, heritage, tradition, bequest|反义词：novelty, innovation (in the sense of something completely new, not inherited)|词性:noun (possessive)
