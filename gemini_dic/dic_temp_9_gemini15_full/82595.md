guardians:/ˈɡɑːrdiənz/ | n. |监护人，守护者 |  A guardian is a person who protects or cares for someone or something.  Guardians can be legal, responsible for a child's well-being, or metaphorical, such as a guardian angel. | 例句：The guardians of the museum carefully preserved the ancient artifacts.  博物馆的守护者小心翼翼地保存着古代文物。

The court appointed her guardians for the orphaned children. 法院任命她为这些孤儿的监护人。


近义词：protector, custodian, keeper, ward, advocate | 反义词：neglecter, abuser
