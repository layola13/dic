postattack: /ˈpoʊstəˈtæk/ | adj. | 袭击后；攻击后 |  Post-attack refers to the period of time, activities, or conditions that occur after an attack, whether it be a physical attack, a cyberattack, or a metaphorical attack.  It often implies the aftermath and consequences of the attack. | Example: Post-attack analysis revealed vulnerabilities in the system. 袭击后分析揭示了系统中的漏洞。 | Near synonyms: post-incident, after-attack, subsequent to the attack | Antonyms: pre-attack, before the attack | adjective
