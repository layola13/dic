sallet: /ˈsælɪt/ | n. |色拉，沙拉，凉拌菜 | A cold dish of various mixtures of raw or cooked vegetables, fruits, or other ingredients, usually served with a dressing.  | Example: I made a delicious summer sallet with tomatoes, cucumbers, and feta cheese. 我做了一份美味的夏季沙拉，里面有西红柿、黄瓜和菲达奶酪。 |近义词：salad |反义词：无
