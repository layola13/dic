expectorations:/ˌɛkspɛkˈtɔːreɪʃənz/| n. |痰, 咳出的东西 |The expectorations were examined for signs of infection.  咳出的痰液被检查是否有感染迹象。|sputum, phlegm|  (无明显反义词, 可以用absence of expectoration表示没有痰)|名词

词源:
From expectorate + -ion.  Expectorate comes from Late Latin *expectorare, from Latin ex- "out" + pectus "breast, chest".  Thus, the word literally means "to bring something from the chest".
