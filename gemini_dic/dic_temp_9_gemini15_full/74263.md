foxtrot:/ˈfɒkstrɒt/ /ˈfɑːkstrɑːt/| n. |狐步舞|A ballroom dance characterized by a slow, gliding movement.  It's a smooth, flowing dance with a moderate tempo. |The couple danced a graceful foxtrot.  这对夫妇跳了一支优雅的狐步舞。| waltz, tango |  |  noun
