noninterpretational: /ˌnɒnɪnˌtɜːprɪˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非解释性的；不需解释的 |  Lacking interpretation or the need for interpretation; not requiring explanation or analysis.  The term often implies a direct, literal, or self-evident meaning. |


例句：

The noninterpretational nature of the data made analysis straightforward.  数据本身的非解释性使得分析变得简单明了。

The instructions were noninterpretational; just follow the steps. 指令非常直接，无需解释；只需按照步骤操作即可。


近义词：literal, unambiguous, straightforward, self-explanatory, explicit


反义词：ambiguous, cryptic, obscure, enigmatic, interpretative


词性：形容词
