duckweeds:/ˈdʌkˌwiːdz/| n.|浮萍；鸭萍|Small, free-floating aquatic plants that form a mat on the surface of still or slow-moving water.  They are simple plants, lacking true roots, stems, and leaves. Duckweeds are used as food for livestock and are also being investigated for their potential in biofuel production and wastewater treatment.|例句：Duckweeds covered the entire surface of the pond.  浮萍覆盖了整个池塘的表面。
例句：Researchers are studying duckweeds for their potential in biofuel production. 研究人员正在研究浮萍在生物燃料生产中的潜力。|近义词：water lentils|反义词：无
