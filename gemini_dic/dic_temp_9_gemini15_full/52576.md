diffusing:/dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ/ | v. | 扩散；传播；缓和；使模糊 | To spread or cause to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people.  |例句：The aroma of coffee diffused throughout the room. 咖啡的香味弥漫在整个房间。
The news quickly diffused across the country.  这个消息迅速传遍全国。|近义词：spreading, dispersing, disseminating, diluting |反义词：concentrating, focusing, intensifying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The perfume diffuses beautifully. = 香水散发得很美妙。
He diffuses information to his team regularly. = 他定期向团队传播信息。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
The rumor has diffused widely across the internet. = 谣言已经在互联网上广泛传播。
The government has diffused the crisis effectively. = 政府有效地化解了危机。

过去完成时:
