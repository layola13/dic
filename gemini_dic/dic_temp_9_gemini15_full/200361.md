skydived:/ˈskaɪdaɪvd/| v.|跳伞|To jump from an aircraft with a parachute.  It implies the successful completion of a skydive.|例句：He skydived from 10,000 feet. 他从一万英尺高空跳伞。

例句：She had never skydived before, but she felt exhilarated afterward. 她以前从未跳过伞，但事后感到兴奋。


近义词：parachuted


反义词：None (the antonym would describe the opposite action, such as staying on the ground)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I skydive every weekend. = 我每个周末都跳伞。
They skydive for fun. = 他们跳伞是为了娱乐。


一般过去时:
I skydived last summer. = 我去年夏天跳过伞。
He skydived from a plane. = 他从飞机上跳伞。


现在完成时:
I have skydived three times. = 我跳过三次伞。
She has never skydived before. = 她以前从未跳过伞。


过去完成时:
I had skydived before, so I wasn't nervous. = 我以前跳过伞，所以我不紧张。
They had skydived many times before that jump. = 在那次跳伞之前，他们已经跳过很多次了。


将来时:
I will skydive next month. = 我下个月将跳伞。
We will skydive together. = 我们将一起跳伞。


虚拟语气:
If I had the courage, I would skydive. = 如果我有勇气，我会跳伞。
I wish I could skydive. = 我真希望我能跳伞。


被动语态:
The parachute was skydived from the plane.  (While grammatically correct, this usage is unusual and unnatural.) = 降落伞是从飞机上投下的。(虽然语法上正确，但这种用法不常见也不自然。)
Many skydives are conducted every day. = 每天进行许多跳伞活动。


疑问句:
Have you ever skydived? = 你跳过伞吗？
Will you skydive with me? = 你会和我一起跳伞吗？


否定句:
I didn't skydive yesterday. = 我昨天没有跳伞。
She hasn't skydived yet. = 她还没跳伞。
