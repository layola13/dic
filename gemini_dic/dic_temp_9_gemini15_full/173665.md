quasi-lawful:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈlɔːfʊl/| adj.|准法的，似法的|Seemingly or apparently lawful, but not completely or strictly so.  It suggests a situation that possesses some, but not all, the characteristics of legality.|The company's actions, while technically quasi-lawful, were ethically questionable.  这家公司的行为虽然在技术上是准法的，但在道德上却值得怀疑。|近义词：semi-legal, arguably legal, borderline legal|反义词：illegal, unlawful
