graveyard:/ˈɡreɪvjɑːrd/| n. |墓地，坟场 | A graveyard is a consecrated ground for the burial of the dead.  It often contains tombstones or markers identifying the deceased. |例句：The old graveyard was overgrown with weeds.  古老的墓地杂草丛生。

例句：We visited my grandmother's grave in the graveyard. 我们去墓地看望了祖母的墓。


近义词：cemetery, burial ground, burying ground |反义词：None.
