self-boasting:/ˈsɛlfˈboʊstɪŋ/| n. |自吹自擂，自夸 |The act of boasting about oneself; excessive self-praise.  It often implies a lack of modesty or humility. |例句：His self-boasting about his achievements annoyed everyone.  他的自吹自擂成就惹恼了所有人。  His constant self-boasting made him unpopular. 他不断的自夸使他不得人心。|近义词：self-promotion, bragging, swaggering, conceit, vainglory |反义词：humility, modesty, self-deprecation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He self-boasts about his intelligence. = 他自夸他的聪明才智。
She often self-boasts of her success. = 她经常自夸她的成功。


一般过去时:
He self-boasted about his new car. = 他自夸他的新车。
She self-boasted during the interview. = 她在面试中自吹自擂。


现在完成时:
He has self-boasted about that project for weeks. = 他已经自吹自擂那个项目好几个星期了。
She has self-boasted incessantly since winning the award. = 自从获奖以来，她就不停地自吹自擂。


过去完成时:
He had self-boasted to everyone before the results were announced. = 结果公布之前，他已经向所有人自吹自擂了。
She had self-boasted so much that nobody believed her. = 她吹嘘得太多了，以至于没有人相信她。


将来时:
He will self-boast again at the next meeting. = 他下次会议还会再次自吹自擂。
She will probably self-boast about her trip. = 她可能会自夸她的旅行。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't self-boasted so much, people might have believed him. = 如果他不那么自吹自擂，人们可能会相信他。
I wish she wouldn't self-boast all the time. = 我希望她不要一直自吹自擂。


被动语态:
His achievements were self-boasted about extensively. = 他的成就被广泛地吹嘘。
Her success was self-boasted by her throughout the year. = 她在这一年中不断地自夸她的成功。


疑问句:
Does he often self-boast? = 他经常自吹自擂吗？

否定句:
He didn't self-boast at the conference. = 他在会议上没有自吹自擂。
She doesn't usually self-boast about her work. = 她通常不会自夸她的工作。
