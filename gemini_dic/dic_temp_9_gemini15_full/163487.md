preconventions:/ˌpriːkənˈvɛnʃənz/| n. |预备会议；预先约定|  Meetings or agreements made in advance of a main convention or event.  Preconventions often serve to plan the agenda, logistics, and other details for the larger event.| Example: The preconventions for the international summit were held in Geneva.|近义词： preliminary meetings, preparatory sessions|反义词：post-convention meetings
