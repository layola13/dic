relearnt:/ˌriːˈlɜːnt/| v.|重新学习，再学|To learn something again, often after forgetting it or having previously failed to master it.  It implies a renewed effort to acquire knowledge or skills.| I relearnt the song after forgetting the lyrics. 我重新学习了这首歌，因为忘记了歌词。|He relearnt his multiplication tables. 他重新学习了乘法口诀。|learn again, review, revise|forget, unlearn|动词
