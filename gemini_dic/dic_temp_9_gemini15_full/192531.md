scrutoire:/skruːˈtwɑːr/| n. |写字台；（带抽屉的）秘书桌 | A writing desk, typically with drawers.  A piece of furniture designed for writing, often with drawers and compartments for storing stationery and documents. |例句：The antique scrutoire was a beautiful piece of furniture, perfect for showcasing her collection of vintage pens. 这张古董写字台是一件美丽的家具，非常适合展示她收藏的复古钢笔。

例句：He carefully placed the important documents inside the scrutoire's many drawers. 他小心翼翼地将重要文件放在写字台的许多抽屉里。


词源:  The word "scrutoire" is derived from the French word "escritoire," meaning "writing desk."  This ultimately comes from the Old French word "escritoir," from the Latin "scribere," meaning "to write."


近义词：writing desk, secrétaire, desk


反义词：无
