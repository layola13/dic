gunk:/ɡʌŋk/| n. |脏东西，污垢，黏糊糊的东西 |Thick, sticky, unpleasant substance.  It often refers to something dirty, messy, or unpleasant to the touch.  The term is informal.|例句：There was a lot of gunk in the bottom of the engine. = 发动机底部有很多脏东西。

例句：I need a solvent to clean this gunk off my shoes. = 我需要溶剂来清洗我鞋子上的污垢。


近义词：muck, grime, sludge, goo, filth |反义词：cleanliness, purity |词性:名词
