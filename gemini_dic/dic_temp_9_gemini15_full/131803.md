nonconsecutiveness:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛkjʊtɪvnəs/ | n. |非连续性，不连续性 |The lack of consecutive order or sequence; discontinuity.  This refers to a situation where things are not in a continuous or logical order.  It often implies a lack of connection or coherence between elements. |例句：The nonconsecutiveness of the events made it difficult to understand the story.  事件的不连续性使得难以理解这个故事。

The nonconsecutiveness of the numbered pages in this book is quite distracting. 这本书页码的不连续性非常令人分心。|近义词：discontinuity, fragmentation, disconnectedness, interruption |反义词：continuity, consecutiveness, coherence
