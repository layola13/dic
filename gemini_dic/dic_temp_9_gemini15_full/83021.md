gunslingers:/ˈɡʌnslɪŋɡərz/| n. |枪手；快枪手|A gunslinger is a person who is skilled in using guns, especially one who is quick to draw and shoot.  They are often depicted as outlaws or lawmen in the American Old West.|例句：The gunslinger quickly drew his pistol and fired.  这位枪手迅速拔出手枪并开火了。

The legendary gunslinger faced off against his rival in a dusty saloon.  传奇枪手在一个尘土飞扬的酒馆里与他的对手对峙。|近义词：gunman, pistolero, sharp-shooter |反义词：pacifist
