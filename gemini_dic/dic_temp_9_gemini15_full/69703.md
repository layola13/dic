fennel: /ˈfɛnəl/ | n. | 茴香 | Fennel is a plant with aromatic leaves and seeds, used as a herb and vegetable.  It has a slightly licorice-like flavor. | Example:  I added some fennel seeds to the bread for extra flavor. 我在面包里加了一些茴香籽来增加风味。 | 近义词：anise (茴芹), dill (莳萝) | 反义词：无

词源:
The word "fennel" comes from Old English *fenol, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fanjulō, related to Latin *faenum (hay) and Greek φάνος (phanos, "shining," "bright").  The connection to hay is likely due to the plant's appearance.
