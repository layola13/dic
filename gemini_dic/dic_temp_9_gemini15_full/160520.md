poring:/ˈpɔːrɪŋ/| v.| 专心致志地阅读；仔细研究 | To read or study something very carefully and attentively for a long time. | 例句：She spent hours poring over old maps. 她花了几个小时仔细研究旧地图。 | 例句：He was poring over his notes before the exam. 他在考试前仔细研读笔记。 | 近义词： studying, scrutinizing, examining | 反义词： skimming, glancing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pores over his textbooks every night. = 他每晚都仔细研读课本。
She pores over the financial reports. = 她仔细研读财务报告。

一般过去时:
I pored over the document for hours. = 我花了几个小时仔细研读这份文件。
They pored over the blueprints before starting construction. = 他们动工前仔细研读了蓝图。

现在完成时:
She has been poring over the manuscript for weeks. = 她已经研读这份手稿好几个星期了。
He has pored over the data, trying to find a pattern. = 他一直在仔细研究数据，试图找到规律。

过去完成时:
They had pored over the evidence before reaching a conclusion. = 他们在得出结论之前已经仔细研究了证据。
She had pored over the recipe before attempting to bake the cake. = 在尝试烘焙蛋糕之前，她已经仔细研读了食谱。

将来时:
I will pore over the contract before signing it. = 我会在签署合同之前仔细研读它。
We will pore over the results of the experiment tomorrow. = 我们明天将仔细研究实验结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pore over those ancient texts. = 如果我有更多时间，我会仔细研读那些古代文献。
I wish I could pore over this book without interruption. = 我希望我能不受干扰地仔细研读这本书。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The ancient texts were pored over by scholars for years. = 这些古代文献被学者们仔细研究多年。


疑问句:
Are you poring over that book again? = 你又在仔细研读那本书吗？
Was he poring over the documents all night? = 他整晚都在仔细研读文件吗？


否定句:
He didn't pore over the instructions, and that's why he made a mistake. = 他没有仔细研读说明，这就是他犯错的原因。
She didn't pore over the details, so she missed some important information. = 她没有仔细研读细节，所以错过了一些重要信息。
