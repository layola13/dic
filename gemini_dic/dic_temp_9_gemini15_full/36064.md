coediting: /ˌkoʊˈɛdɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 共同编辑；合作编辑 | The act of two or more people editing a document or work together.  It often implies a collaborative and shared responsibility for the final product. | Example:  They are coediting a new textbook on linguistics. 他们正在合作编辑一本新的语言学教科书。 |近义词：collaborative editing, joint editing | 反义词：solo editing, independent editing
