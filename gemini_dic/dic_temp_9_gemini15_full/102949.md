jazzing:/ˈdʒæzɪŋ/| v.|爵士乐演奏；随意地演奏；兴高采烈地做某事|Improvising and playing jazz music; doing something in a lively and enthusiastic manner.|例句：They were jazzing away on their instruments.  他们在兴高采烈地演奏乐器。

例句：He's been jazzing around the house all day. 他一整天都在家里兴高采烈地忙活着。|近义词：improvising, playing, enlivening|反义词：being sluggish, being monotonous.
