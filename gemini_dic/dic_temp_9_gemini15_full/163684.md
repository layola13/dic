/ˌpriːdɪˈsaɪsɪvli/ | adv. | 预先决定地；果断地 |  Acting decisively in advance; showing firm determination before the event.  The word combines "pre-" (before) and "decisively" (in a decisive manner). It implies a proactive and resolute approach to making decisions. | The general decisively planned the preemptive strike, ensuring a swift victory.  将军预先果断地计划了先发制人的打击，确保了迅速的胜利。


近义词: proactively, resolutely, firmly, decisively in advance


反义词: hesitantly, indecisively, tentatively, vacillatingly


词性: 副词
