petites:/pəˈtiːts/| n.|小号的；小件的；小巧玲珑的|Small things, often referring to clothing or food.  The word is the plural of "petite."|例句：She prefers to wear petite-sized dresses. 她喜欢穿小号的连衣裙。

例句：The bakery offers a selection of petite pastries. 这家面包店提供各种精致的小糕点。

近义词：small, tiny, miniature, dainty

反义词：large, big, oversized, huge

词性：名词
