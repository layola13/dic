epithelial:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl/| adj.|上皮的；上皮细胞的|relating to or consisting of epithelium. Epithelial tissue covers the body's surface, lines its internal cavities and forms glands.|例句：The epithelial cells in the lungs are constantly exposed to air pollutants. 肺部上皮细胞不断暴露于空气污染物中。

例句：The damage to the epithelial lining of the stomach caused severe ulcers. 胃上皮组织的损伤导致严重的溃疡。


近义词：epithelioid (similar in appearance to epithelium)|反义词：无
