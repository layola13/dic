enthrill:/ɪnˈθrɪl/ | vt. |使激动；使兴奋；使陶醉|To fill (someone) with a thrilling feeling of excitement and pleasure.|The concert enthralled the audience. 音乐会使观众激动不已。|近义词：excite, thrill, exhilarate |反义词：bore, disappoint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roller coaster enthralls many people.  过山车使许多人兴奋不已。
This new technology enthralls the engineers. 这项新技术让工程师们兴奋不已。


一般过去时:
The magician's trick enthralled the children. 魔术师的戏法让孩子们着迷。
The breathtaking view enthralled us.  令人叹为观止的景色让我们着迷。


现在完成时:
The novel has enthralled readers for generations. 这部小说几代人都为之着迷。
The performance has enthralled the critics. 这场演出让评论家们赞叹不已。


过去完成时:
She had been enthralled by the story before she finished reading it. 她读完故事之前就被故事迷住了。
The audience had been enthralled by the singer's voice.  观众已经被歌手的歌声所倾倒。


将来时:
The new film will enthrall audiences worldwide.  这部新电影将让全球观众着迷。
I am sure the upcoming concert will enthrall everyone. 我确信即将到来的音乐会将让所有人着迷。


虚拟语气:
If only the movie had enthralled me as much as the book did!  要是电影像书一样让我着迷就好了！
I wish the play had enthralled me more. 我希望这出戏能让我更着迷。


被动语态:
The audience was enthralled by the performance.  观众被演出迷住了。
We were enthralled by the beauty of the sunset. 我们被日落的美丽所陶醉。


疑问句:
Will the new game enthrall players?  这款新游戏会让玩家着迷吗？
Does the story enthrall you? 这个故事让你着迷吗？


否定句:
The movie didn't enthrall me. 这部电影并没有让我着迷。
The lecture failed to enthrall the students.  讲座未能使学生们感兴趣。
