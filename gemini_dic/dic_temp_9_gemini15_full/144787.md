over-filters:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪltərz/ | v. |过度过滤；滤除过多 | To excessively remove or suppress information, data, or content, often resulting in the loss of important details or nuance.  | Example: The social media platform over-filters user comments, leading to a lack of authentic conversation. 社交媒体平台过度过滤用户评论，导致缺乏真实的对话。 |近义词：censor, suppress, sanitize |反义词：unfilter, allow
