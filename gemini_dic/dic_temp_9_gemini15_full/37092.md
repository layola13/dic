combined:/kəmˈbaɪnd/| adj.|组合的，联合的|Joined or merged together.  Combined efforts are often more effective than individual efforts.|例句：The combined weight of the boxes was too much for one person to lift. = 箱子的总重量一个人搬不动。|例句：The two companies announced a combined operation. = 两家公司宣布合并运营。|近义词：united, joined, merged, integrated|反义词：separated, divided, distinct|形容词
