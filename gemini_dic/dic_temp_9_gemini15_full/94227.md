imperialize:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlaɪz/ | vt. | 帝国统治；使帝国化；实行帝國主義 | To establish control over a country or territory and its people, often through military force or political influence, and exploit its resources.  This often involves imposing the dominant culture and systems of government. |  The Roman Empire imperialized much of Europe. 罗马帝国统治了欧洲大部分地区。

The British imperialized India for centuries. 英国对印度进行了长达数世纪的殖民统治。


近义词：colonize, dominate, subjugate, conquer

反义词：liberate, emancipate, free
