ornery:/ˈɔːrnəri/| adj.|脾气坏的；倔强的；难对付的 |  Difficult to manage or control; bad-tempered; stubborn.  |例句：He's an ornery old man who never listens to anyone. 他是个脾气坏的老头，从不听别人的话。
例句：The ornery mule refused to budge. 那头倔强的骡子拒绝动弹。|近义词：stubborn, cantankerous, grumpy, surly, crotchety |反义词：docile, amiable, agreeable, pleasant
