decidence:/ˈdɛsɪdəns/| n.|衰落；下降；终止；衰退|The decline or falling away of something; cessation; decay.  It often refers to a gradual process of deterioration or decline, especially in power, influence, or health.|例句：The decidence of the Roman Empire was a long and complex process. 罗马帝国的衰落是一个漫长而复杂的过程。

例句：The decidence of his health was noticeable after his accident.  事故之后，他的健康状况明显恶化。


近义词：decline, decay, deterioration, downfall, degeneration|反义词：ascension, rise, growth, improvement|词性：名词


词源:  The word "decidence" originates from the Latin word "decidere," meaning "to fall down," "to fall off," or "to cease."  This reflects the core meaning of gradual decline or falling away.
