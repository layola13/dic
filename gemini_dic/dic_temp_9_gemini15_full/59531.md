eat-your-words:/iːt jɔːr wɜːrdz/ |v. phr.|食言；承认错误|To be forced to admit that something you said was wrong.|例句：He had to eat his words when the project was a success.  他不得不承认自己错了，因为项目最终成功了。|近义词：recant, retract, admit defeat|反义词：persist, stand by one's words|动词词组


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats his words when he is proven wrong. = 当他被证明错了的时候，他会承认错误。
They eat their words after the evidence is presented. = 证据呈现后，他们承认自己错了。


一般过去时:
She ate her words after seeing the results. = 在看到结果后，她承认自己错了。
He ate his words when the facts came to light. = 当事实真相大白后，他承认自己错了。


现在完成时:
He has eaten his words many times in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中多次承认自己错了。
They have eaten their words about the economic forecast. = 他们已经承认自己对经济预测的错误了。


过去完成时:
She had eaten her words before the meeting even started. = 会议甚至还没开始，她就承认自己错了。
They had eaten their words after the investigation concluded. = 调查结束后，他们承认自己错了。


将来时:
He will eat his words if the plan succeeds. = 如果计划成功，他会承认自己错了。
They will eat their words when the truth is revealed. = 当真相大白时，他们会承认自己错了。


虚拟语气:
If he were honest, he would eat his words. = 如果他诚实的话，他会承认自己错了。
I wish she would eat her words and apologize. = 我希望她能承认错误并道歉。


被动语态:
His words were eaten after the new evidence was presented. = 新证据提交后，他的话被证明是错的。
Their predictions were eaten after the market crashed. = 市场崩盘后，他们的预测被证明是错的。


疑问句:
Will he eat his words when he sees the evidence? = 当他看到证据时，他会承认错误吗？
Did she eat her words after the debate? = 辩论后她承认自己错了吗？


否定句:
He didn't eat his words, even after being proven wrong. = 即使被证明错了，他也没有承认错误。
She didn't eat her words; she stuck to her guns. = 她没有承认错误；她坚持己见。
