calaboose:/kæˈluːs/| n.| 拘留所，牢房 | A calaboose is a jail or lockup, typically a small and informal one.  It often implies a temporary holding cell, rather than a formal prison. | Example: The sheriff locked the suspect in the calaboose overnight. 警长把嫌疑犯关在拘留所过夜。 |近义词：jail, lockup, brig,  |反义词：freedom, liberty.
