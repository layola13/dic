many:/ˈmɛni/| adj. pron.|许多的，大量的|Many people attended the concert. 许多人参加了音乐会。|近义词：numerous, plentiful, a lot of |反义词：few, little


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are many birds in the sky. = 天空中有许多鸟。
Many students study hard. = 许多学生努力学习。


一般过去时:
Many people came to the party. = 许多人来参加了派对。
There were many cars on the road. = 路上有许多汽车。


现在完成时:
Many things have changed since then. = 从那时起，许多事情都变了。
Many people have visited this place. = 许多人参观过这个地方。


过去完成时:
Many problems had been solved before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，许多问题已经得到解决。
Many people had already left when I arrived. = 我到达的时候，许多人已经离开了。


将来时:
Many more people will come next year. = 明年将会有更多的人来。
There will be many challenges ahead. = 未来将面临许多挑战。


虚拟语气:
If there were many more opportunities, I would take them. = 如果有更多机会，我会抓住它们。
I wish there were many more hours in a day. = 我希望一天有更多小时。


被动语态:
Many questions were asked during the meeting. = 会议期间提出了许多问题。
Many mistakes were made in the report. = 报告中犯了许多错误。


疑问句:
How many apples are there? = 有多少个苹果？
How many people are coming to the party? = 有多少人来参加聚会？


否定句:
There aren't many opportunities available. = 没有多少机会可用。
Not many people understand this concept. = 没有多少人理解这个概念。


PHRASE->
many a = 许多（单数名词）
many times = 许多次
as many as = 多达
so many = 如此之多
too many = 太多
