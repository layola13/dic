highlighter:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪtər/ | n. |荧光笔，突出显示器 | A pen or marker that uses fluorescent ink to highlight text or images.  It makes the highlighted text or image easier to see and stand out. | Example: I used a highlighter to mark the important points in the textbook. 我用荧光笔标出了教科书中的重点。 |近义词：marker, marker pen |反义词：eraser
