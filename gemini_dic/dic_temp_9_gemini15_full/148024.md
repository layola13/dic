overroasting:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊstɪŋ/| n.|烤过头；过度烘烤|The act of cooking something, especially meat, for too long, resulting in dryness and potentially burning.  It refers to the process of exceeding the optimal cooking time, leading to undesirable textural and flavor changes.|例句：The turkey was ruined by overroasting; it was dry and tough.  烤火鸡烤过头了，又干又韧。|近义词：overcooking, burning, scorching|反义词：undercooking
