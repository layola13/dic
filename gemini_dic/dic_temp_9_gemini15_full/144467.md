/aʊtˈliːz/ | v. |转租；再次出租| To sublet or lease out property that one has already leased.  This typically involves a landlord leasing property to a tenant, and then that tenant subletting a portion or all of that property to another party.  The original lease agreement should be reviewed carefully to ensure subletting is permitted. |The landlord outleased a portion of the warehouse to a smaller business. = 房东将仓库的一部分转租给了一家小型企业。

The tenant outleased his apartment to a friend while he was traveling abroad. = 租客在他出国旅行期间将公寓转租给了他的一个朋友。


| 近义词：sublet, re-lease | 反义词：retain, keep | 动词
