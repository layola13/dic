naphthalic: /næfˈθælɪk/ | adj. | 萘酸的；萘二甲酸的 | Relating to or derived from naphthalene.  The term usually refers to naphthalic anhydride, a dicarboxylic acid anhydride derived from naphthalene. | Example: Naphthalic anhydride is used in the production of certain dyes and pigments.  萘酸酐用于生产某些染料和颜料。 | 近义词: naphthalenedicarboxylic (less common) | 反义词: N/A
