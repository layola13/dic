prequarantine:/ˌpriːˈkwɒrəntiːn/| adj.|隔离前的，检疫前的|Existing or occurring before a period of quarantine.  This word is a relatively new coinage, combining "pre-" (before) with "quarantine".|例句：Prequarantine travel restrictions were less stringent than those imposed during the pandemic. 隔离前的旅行限制比疫情期间实施的限制要宽松得多。
例句：The prequarantine period saw a surge in international travel. 隔离前时期国际旅行激增。|近义词：antecedent, prior|反义词：postquarantine
