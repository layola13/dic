outwork:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrk/| vt.|胜过，超过（在工作量或效率上）|to surpass or exceed in work done or efficiency|The new machine outworks the old one by a considerable margin. 新机器的效率比旧机器高出许多。|近义词：surpass, excel, outperform|反义词：underperform


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outworks his colleagues every day. 他每天的工作都超过他的同事。
They consistently outwork their competitors. 他们始终比竞争对手工作更努力。


一般过去时:
She outworked everyone in the team to complete the project on time. 她比团队里的每个人都更努力工作，从而按时完成了项目。
Last year, our sales team outworked the marketing team. 去年，我们的销售团队的工作成果超过了市场营销团队。


现在完成时:
He has outworked everyone on the team this year. 今年他比团队里的其他人工作都更努力。
The company has outworked its rivals in the market. 这家公司的工作成果已经超过了市场上的竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the time the deadline arrived, she had outworked all her rivals. 截止期限到来时，她已经超过了所有竞争对手。
They had outworked the competition and secured the contract. 他们已经超过了竞争对手，并获得了合同。


将来时:
She will outwork her opponents and win the competition. 她将会比对手更努力工作并赢得比赛。
We believe that we will outwork our competitors in the coming year. 我们相信在来年我们将超过我们的竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he outworked his colleagues, he would get a promotion. 如果他比他的同事工作更努力，他就会得到晋升。
I wish I had outworked harder in college. 我希望我大学时能更努力学习。


被动语态:
The old system was outworked by the new one. 旧系统被新系统超越了。
The team was outworked and lost the competition.  团队的工作效率不足，输掉了比赛。


疑问句:
Does he outwork everyone else in the office? 他是否比办公室里其他人都更努力工作？
Can our team outwork the opposing team? 我们的团队能否胜过对方团队？


否定句:
He didn't outwork his colleagues on that project.  在那个项目中，他并没有比他的同事更努力工作。
The company failed to outwork its competitors in the crucial market. 这家公司未能在这个关键市场上超越其竞争对手。


PHRASE->
outwork the competition  胜过竞争对手
outwork oneself  过度劳累
outwork the problem  通过努力解决问题 (较为口语化)
outwork the opposition  胜过反对派
outwork the others 超过其他人
