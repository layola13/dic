discovers:/dɪˈskʌvərz/ | vt. |发现；发觉；揭露 | to find something or someone unexpectedly or while searching for something else.  |例句：The police discovered a large quantity of drugs. 警方发现了一大批毒品。

例句：She discovered a hidden talent for painting. 她发现自己有绘画的天赋。


近义词：find, uncover, detect, unearth | 反义词：lose, conceal, hide | 词性:动词
