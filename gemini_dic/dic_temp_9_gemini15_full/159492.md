poling:/ˈpoʊlɪŋ/| n. v.|竿撑；探测；投票；|The act of propelling a boat or raft with a pole; the act of using a pole to sound the depth of water; the process of voting.|例句：The fishermen were poling their boats upstream. 渔民们正在用竿撑着船逆流而上。

例句：They used poling to survey the lake bed.  他们用探测的方法勘测湖底。|近义词：probing, sounding, voting|反义词：None specifically applicable.|词性:noun, verb
