shortfalls:/ˈʃɔːrtˌfɔːlz/|/ˈʃɔːrtfɔːlz/| n. |不足；缺点；亏空；差距|A shortfall is a deficiency or lack of something, typically money or resources. It represents the difference between what is expected or needed and what is actually available.  |例句：The company reported a significant shortfall in profits this quarter.  这家公司报告称本季度利润严重不足。

The project is facing a shortfall of skilled workers. 这个项目面临熟练工人短缺的问题。


近义词：deficiency, deficit, shortage, gap, lack


反义词：surplus, abundance, excess


词性：名词
