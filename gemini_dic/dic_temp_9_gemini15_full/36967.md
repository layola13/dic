colporteur:/ˌkɒlpɔːˈtɜːr/| n. |小贩；（尤指宗教书籍）的流动贩卖者 | A colporteur is a person who travels from place to place selling goods, especially religious books or pamphlets.  They often work for religious organizations and spread their message through the distribution of literature. |The colporteur walked from village to village, selling Bibles and hymnals.  这个小贩走村串户，兜售圣经和赞美诗歌。| peddler, hawker, vendor |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：stationary seller) |名词
