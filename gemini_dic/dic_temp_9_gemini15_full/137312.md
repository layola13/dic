nontactile: /ˌnɒnˈtæktaɪl/ | adj. |非触觉的，非接触的 | Not involving or perceived through touch.  It refers to things that do not stimulate the sense of touch. | Example:  The virtual reality experience provided a nontactile sensation of flying.  虚拟现实体验提供了非触觉的飞行感。


近义词: intangible, non-contact, touchless


反义词: tactile, tangible


词性: adjective
