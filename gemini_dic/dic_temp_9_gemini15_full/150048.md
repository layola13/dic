palpitate:/ˈpælpeɪt/| v.| 心跳加速；悸动；颤动 |To beat rapidly or irregularly, especially of the heart.  It can also refer to a trembling or quivering motion.|例句：My heart palpitated with fear. 我的心脏因恐惧而剧烈跳动。
My hands palpitated as I waited for the results. 我等待结果时，双手颤抖。

近义词：flutter, throb, tremble, vibrate |反义词：be still, be calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My heart palpitates when I see him. = 我看到他时心跳加速。
Her hands palpitate nervously before a performance. = 演出前，她双手紧张地颤抖。

一般过去时:
My heart palpitated wildly after the accident. = 事故后，我的心跳剧烈加速。
His voice palpitated with emotion. = 他的声音因激动而颤抖。

现在完成时:
My heart has palpitated all day due to the stress. = 由于压力，我整天心跳都加速。
She has palpitated with excitement since hearing the news. = 自从听到这个消息以来，她就一直激动地颤抖。

过去完成时:
My heart had palpitated all night before the big presentation. = 在重要的演示之前，我整夜心跳加速。
He had palpitated with anxiety before the interview. = 面试前，他焦虑地颤抖。

将来时:
I think my heart will palpitate when I meet her. = 我想见到她时，我的心跳会加速。
His hands will probably palpitate during the surgery. = 手术过程中，他的手可能会颤抖。

虚拟语气:
If I had known what was going to happen, my heart would have palpitated even more. = 如果我知道会发生什么事，我的心跳会更加剧烈。
I wish my heart wouldn't palpitate so much during exams. = 我希望考试期间我的心跳不会这么快。

被动语态:  (Palpitate is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a feeling or action of the subject, not something done to the subject.)

疑问句:
Does your heart palpitate often? = 你的心跳经常加速吗？
Is your hand palpitating? = 你的手在颤抖吗？

否定句:
My heart didn't palpitate during the speech. = 演讲时我的心跳并没有加速。
Her hands did not palpitate, surprisingly. = 令人惊讶的是，她的手没有颤抖。


PHRASE->
heart palpitation = 心悸
nervous palpitation = 紧张性心跳加速
palpitation of the heart = 心跳加速
severe palpitation = 剧烈心跳
palpitation anxiety = 焦虑性心悸


词源:
From Latin *palpītāre, frequentative of palpāre "to stroke, touch," from palpa "palm of the hand."  The sense development is from the physical act of touching to the feeling of a rapid beating or trembling.
