receivable:/rɪˈsiːvəbl/| n. |应收款，应收账款 |Money owed to a business by its customers for goods or services sold on credit.  Receivables are considered assets on a company's balance sheet.| 例句：The company's receivables increased significantly last quarter.  这家公司的应收账款上个季度大幅增加。

例句：We need to improve our collection of receivables to increase cash flow. 我们需要改进应收账款的回收以增加现金流。


近义词：accounts receivable, outstanding invoices, debts owed, claims

反义词：payable (应付款)

词性:名词
