perversely:/pərˈvɜːrsli/ | adv. | 倔强地；反常地；与预期相反地 | In a perverse or obstinate manner; contrary to what is expected or considered right or proper.  |例句：Perversely, the simplest solution was the one nobody considered.  倔强地，最简单的解决方法是没有人考虑到的那个。

例句：Perversely, the rain stopped just as we reached the shelter.  反常地，就在我们到达避难所时雨停了。

近义词：contrarily, stubbornly, ironically, oddly, strangely

反义词：expectedly, naturally, normally, rightly
