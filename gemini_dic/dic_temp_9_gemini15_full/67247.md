expunge:/ɪkˈspʌndʒ/| vt.|涂去，删除，清除|To remove completely; erase or delete.|The judge ordered that the defamatory remarks be expunged from the record.  法官下令将诽谤性言论从记录中删除。|近义词：erase, delete, obliterate, remove|反义词：add, insert, include


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They expunge sensitive information from the report. 他们从报告中删除敏感信息。
The government expunges names from the blacklist. 政府从黑名单中删除姓名。


一般过去时:
The editor expunged several paragraphs from the manuscript. 编辑从手稿中删除了几段话。
He expunged the offensive words from his speech. 他从他的演讲中删除了冒犯性的词语。


现在完成时:
The court has expunged his criminal record. 法院已经删除了他的犯罪记录。
She has expunged all traces of her past. 她已经抹去了她过去的所有痕迹。


过去完成时:
They had expunged the evidence before the police arrived. 他们在警察到达之前已经销毁了证据。
The dictator had expunged all mention of his opponents from history books. 独裁者已经从历史书中删除了所有关于反对者的记载。


将来时:
The company will expunge the faulty data from its database. 公司将从数据库中删除错误数据。
We will expunge the error from the next edition. 我们将在下一版中删除错误。


虚拟语气:
If they were to expunge the names, the report would be less controversial. 如果他们删除这些名字，报告就不会那么有争议了。
I wish they would expunge that embarrassing photo from the internet. 我希望他们能把那张尴尬的照片从互联网上删除。


被动语态:
The offensive passages were expunged from the book. 书中令人反感的段落被删除了。
His name will be expunged from the list. 他的名字将从名单中删除。


疑问句:
Will the court expunge his conviction? 法院会撤销他的定罪吗？
Have they expunged the offensive comments yet? 他们已经删除那些冒犯性的评论了吗？


否定句:
They did not expunge the information from the file. 他们没有从文件中删除该信息。
The judge refused to expunge the evidence. 法官拒绝删除证据。
