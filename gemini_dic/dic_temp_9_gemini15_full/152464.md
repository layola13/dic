pay-off:/ˈpeɪ ɒf/|/ˈpeɪ ɔːf/| n./vt.|回报；结果；贿赂；付清；得到好结果|The result or consequence of an action or process.|The final payment of a debt.  To bribe someone.|例句：The pay-off for all their hard work was a successful product launch.  他们所有努力的回报是产品成功发布。

The pay-off for investing in the stock market can be substantial. 投资股市的回报可能是巨大的。


The company offered him a large pay-off to keep quiet. 公司给他一大笔贿赂让他保持沉默。

He finally made the final pay-off on his mortgage. 他终于还清了抵押贷款。


近义词：reward, outcome, bribe, settlement, compensation|反义词：failure, loss, penalty
