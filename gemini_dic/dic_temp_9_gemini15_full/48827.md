defogging:/dɪˈfɒɡɪŋ/ | v. |去雾，消除雾气 | The process of removing fog or mist, or the act of clearing something of obscurity or confusion.  | Example:  The defogging system on the airplane's windshield improved visibility significantly.  飞机挡风玻璃上的除雾系统大大提高了能见度。


The defogging of the window revealed a breathtaking view.  窗户除雾后展现出一幅令人叹为观止的景色。|近义词：clearing, cleaning, dehazing |反义词：fogging, obscuring
