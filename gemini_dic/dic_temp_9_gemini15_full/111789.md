loftily:/ˈlɒftɪli/|ˈlɔːftɪli/| adj.| 高傲地；自高自大地；崇高地 |  In a lofty manner; haughtily.  It suggests a superior attitude, often implying disdain or condescension. |例句：She loftily refused to answer his questions. 她高傲地拒绝回答他的问题。

She spoke loftily about her achievements. 她自豪地谈论着自己的成就。


近义词：haughtily, disdainfully, condescendingly, arrogantly, superciliously

反义词：humbly, modestly, meekly

词性：形容词
