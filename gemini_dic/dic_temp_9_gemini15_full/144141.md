outsentry:/ˈaʊtsɛntri/| n.|哨兵，警戒人员|A person stationed at an advanced position to watch for and give warning of the approach of an enemy.  An outsentry is a soldier or guard positioned at the perimeter of a military base or area to provide early warning of any approaching threats.|例句：The outsentry spotted the enemy approaching from the woods.  哨兵发现敌人从树林里靠近。
The outsentry challenged the strangers before they could enter the camp.  哨兵在陌生人进入营地之前就拦住了他们。|近义词：sentinel, guard, lookout, picket|反义词：None specifically antonymous, but could be contrasted with terms like "interior guard" or "rear guard".|名词
