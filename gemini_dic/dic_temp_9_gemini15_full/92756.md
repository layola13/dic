ichthyomorphic:/ˌɪkθiːoʊˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj. |鱼形的，似鱼形的 |Resembling a fish in form or structure.  The term refers to the overall body plan and shape, often including features like a streamlined body, fins, and a caudal fin. |Example: The ichthyomorphic larva of the amphibian undergoes a dramatic metamorphosis.  两栖动物的鱼形幼虫会经历剧烈的变态。|近义词：pisciform|反义词：mammaliform
