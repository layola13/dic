reconstructor:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌktər/| n.|重建者，重构者|A person or thing that reconstructs something.  Someone or something that puts something back together again, often after damage or destruction.  It can also refer to a program or algorithm that reconstructs data or images.|例句：The archaeologist acted as a reconstructor, piecing together fragments of ancient pottery. 考古学家充当重建者，将古代陶器的碎片拼凑起来。

例句：The software acted as a reconstructor, restoring the damaged image file. 该软件充当重建器，恢复了损坏的图像文件。

近义词：rebuilder, restorer, reformer |反义词：destroyer, demolisher| 名词
