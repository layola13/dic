hance:/hæns/| v.|提高，增强，促进|To increase or further the progress, value, or effectiveness of something.|The new policy will enhance the company's image.  新的政策将提升公司的形象。|improve, boost, augment|reduce, diminish, decrease|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new marketing strategy enhances brand awareness. = 新的营销策略提高了品牌知名度。
Regular exercise enhances cardiovascular health. = 定期锻炼增强心血管健康。


一般过去时:
The training program enhanced the employees' skills. = 培训项目提高了员工的技能。
The good weather enhanced our enjoyment of the picnic. = 好天气增强了我们野餐的乐趣。


现在完成时:
The renovations have enhanced the property's value. = 装修提高了房产的价值。
Recent advancements have enhanced the capabilities of the software. = 最近的进步增强了软件的功能。


过去完成时:
They had enhanced the security measures before the event. = 他们在活动前加强了安全措施。
The company had enhanced its products before launching them. = 公司在推出产品之前对其进行了改进。


将来时:
The new technology will enhance productivity. = 新技术将提高生产力。
The upcoming marketing campaign will enhance the company's profile. = 即将来临的营销活动将提升公司的形象。


虚拟语气:
If we used better materials, it would enhance the product's durability. = 如果我们使用更好的材料，它将提高产品的耐用性。
I wish the lighting would enhance the atmosphere of the room. = 我希望灯光能增强房间的氛围。


被动语态:
The company's image was enhanced by the successful product launch. = 公司的形象因产品成功推出而得到提升。
The quality of life is enhanced by access to good healthcare. = 获得良好的医疗保健提高了生活质量。


疑问句:
Will this new software enhance our workflow? = 这款新软件会提高我们的工作效率吗？
How can we enhance the customer experience? = 我们如何才能提升客户体验？


否定句:
The poor management did not enhance the team's morale. = 糟糕的管理并没有提高团队的士气。
The changes did not enhance the performance of the system. = 这些变化并没有提高系统的性能。


PHRASE->

enhance the quality of = 提高…的质量
enhance the value of = 提高…的价值
enhance one's skills = 提高某人的技能
enhance learning experience = 提升学习体验
