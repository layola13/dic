downheartedness:/ˈdaʊnˈhɑːtɪdnəs/| n. |沮丧；灰心；失望 |A feeling of discouragement or depression.  It is a state of low spirits characterized by a loss of hope and enthusiasm.  |例句：Her downheartedness was evident in her slumped posture and lackluster eyes.  她的沮丧从她无精打采的姿势和黯淡无光的眼神中显而易见。

例句：The team's downheartedness after their crushing defeat was palpable.  球队惨败后的沮丧情绪非常明显。


近义词：despair, dejection, discouragement, despondency, gloominess


反义词：elation, cheerfulness, optimism, hopefulness


词性: 名词
