deselect:/ˌdiːˈsɛləkt/| vt.|取消选择，去除选择|To remove the selection from (something), typically in a computer interface.|例句：Please deselect the file before proceeding.  请在继续操作前取消选择该文件。|例句：I accidentally deselected the important paragraph. 我不小心取消选择了重要的段落。|近义词：unselect, remove, uncheck|反义词：select, choose

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deselects the wrong option. 他取消选择了错误的选项。
She deselects all the images. 她取消选择了所有图片。

一般过去时:
I deselected the item from the list. 我从列表中取消选择了该项目。
They deselected the candidate from the ballot. 他们从选票上取消了候选人的选择。
