overlegislating:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪŋ/| v. |过度立法；立法过多|The act of passing too many laws, often resulting in inefficiency and unintended consequences.  It implies that the sheer volume of laws hinders effective governance and may create confusion or conflict.|例句：The government is accused of overlegislating, creating a complex and confusing web of regulations.  政府被指控过度立法，制造了一个复杂而混乱的法规网络。|例句：Overlegislating can stifle economic growth and innovation. 过度立法可能会扼杀经济增长和创新。|近义词：overregulation, excessive legislation, legislative overload |反义词：underregulation, deregulation
