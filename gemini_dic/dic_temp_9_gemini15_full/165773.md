prenups:/ˈpriːnʌps/| n.|婚前协议|A prenuptial agreement is a legal contract signed by a couple before they get married.  It outlines how assets and property will be divided in the event of a divorce or the death of one spouse.  It's designed to protect each party's financial interests.|Example: They signed a prenuptial agreement before their lavish wedding. 他们在奢华的婚礼前签署了婚前协议。|近义词：prenuptial agreement|反义词：无
