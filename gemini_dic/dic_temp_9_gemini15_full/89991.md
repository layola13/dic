horsing:/ˈhɔːrsɪŋ/| n. v.|粗暴对待；骑马；喧闹；强行进行|The act of riding a horse; rough treatment; boisterous behavior; forcing something through.|例句：He was accused of horsing around in the office. 他被指控在办公室里胡闹。

例句：The new policy was horsed through parliament. 这项新政策被强行通过了议会。


近义词：bullying, roughhousing, pushing, shoving|反义词：gentle treatment, kindness, diplomacy


词性: 名词和动词
