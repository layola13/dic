multihearths:/ˌmʌltiˈhɑːrθs/| n. |多灶台，多炉灶|A multihearth is a cooking system with multiple hearths or fireplaces, often used in large-scale food preparation or in settings with high demand for cooking.  This term is less common in everyday language and more frequently used in archaeological or historical contexts describing ancient or traditional cooking methods.  |Example: The archaeological dig uncovered evidence of a large multihearth complex, suggesting a communal cooking system.  考古发掘发现了大型多灶台建筑群的证据，这表明存在一个公共烹饪系统。  |近义词：multiple fireplaces, multiple ovens |反义词：single hearth, single oven
