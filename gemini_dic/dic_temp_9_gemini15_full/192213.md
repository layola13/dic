scratchiness:/ˈskræʧɪnɪs/ | n. |沙哑；粗糙；刺耳；烦躁 | A harsh, grating quality of sound or texture.  It can refer to a sound (like a voice), a texture (like fabric), or a feeling (like irritation). |例句：The scratchiness of his voice indicated a sore throat.  他的声音沙哑，表明他嗓子疼了。

例句：The wool sweater had an unpleasant scratchiness against her skin. 这件羊毛衫摸起来很不舒服，很粗糙。


近义词：roughness, raspiness, harshness, irritation |反义词：smoothness, softness, gentleness


词源：源自于动词 scratch，意思是“刮擦”，加上后缀 -iness 表示“性质”或“状态”。  The word evolved from Old English *scræccian, meaning "to scratch".
