leasehold: /ˈliːsˌhoʊld/ | n. |租赁权；租赁土地；租赁财产 | The right to use and occupy a property for a specified period, as defined by a lease agreement.  The owner of the leasehold is not the owner of the land itself. |例句：Many apartments in the city are leasehold properties.  这座城市里的许多公寓都是租赁产权。

例句：The leasehold on the shop expires next year. 这家商店的租赁权将于明年到期。


近义词：tenancy, lease, rental | 反义词：freehold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds a leasehold on that apartment. = 他拥有那套公寓的租赁权。
The leasehold agreement specifies the terms of the rental. = 租赁协议规定了租赁条款。


一般过去时:
They purchased a leasehold last year. = 他们去年购买了一项租赁权。
The leasehold agreement was signed in 2020. = 租赁协议签署于2020年。


现在完成时:
She has held this leasehold for ten years. = 她已经拥有这项租赁权十年了。
They have renewed their leasehold several times. = 他们已经续签过几次租赁权了。


过去完成时:
Before buying the apartment, they had checked the leasehold details carefully. = 在购买公寓之前，他们仔细检查了租赁权的细节。
He had already relinquished his leasehold before the building was demolished. = 在大楼拆除之前，他已经放弃了他的租赁权。


将来时:
They will renew their leasehold next month. = 他们下个月将续签租赁权。
The leasehold will expire in five years. = 租赁权将在五年后到期。


虚拟语气:
If I had a leasehold, I would renovate the property. = 如果我有一项租赁权，我会翻新房产。
I wish I had a longer leasehold on this land. = 我希望在这块土地上拥有更长的租赁权。


被动语态:
The leasehold was transferred to a new owner. = 租赁权已转让给新的业主。
The leasehold terms are being renegotiated. = 租赁条款正在重新谈判。


疑问句:

否定句:
He doesn't own the freehold; he only has a leasehold. = 他不拥有永久产权；他只有租赁权。
