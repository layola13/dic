encashes:/ɪnˈkæʃɪz/| vt.|兑现；变现|To exchange a check, money order, etc., for cash.  To realize or convert something into cash.|例句：He encashed his paycheck at the bank. 他在银行兑现了他的工资支票。

He encashed his savings bonds to pay for his son's education. 他变卖了他的储蓄债券来支付儿子的教育费用。|近义词：cash, redeem, realize|反义词：invest, deposit


USAGE->

She encashes her savings bonds regularly. 她定期变卖储蓄债券。
