enchants:/ɪnˈtʃænts/ | vt. |使着迷；施魔法于 |To fill (someone) with great delight; to charm or fascinate.  To put a spell on; to bewitch. |例句：The magician enchanted the audience with his amazing tricks. 魔术师以其惊人的魔术技巧使观众着迷。

The beautiful scenery enchanted her.美丽的景色使她着迷。

近义词：charm, fascinate, bewitch, captivate | 反义词：disenchant, repel, bore


USAGE->

The music enchants the listeners. = 音乐使听众着迷。
The story enchants children. = 这个故事使孩子们着迷。
