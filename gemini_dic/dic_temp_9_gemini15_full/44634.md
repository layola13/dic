crownet:/ˈkraʊnɛt/| n. |小皇冠；头饰|A small crown, often decorative.  It can be a literal small crown or a metaphorical one, referring to something that resembles a crown in shape or symbolic meaning.|例句：She wore a tiny crownet of wildflowers in her hair. 她头上戴着一个小小的野花冠。|例句：The achievement was the crownet of his long career.  这项成就是他漫长职业生涯的顶峰。|近义词：coronet, diadem|反义词：None specifically, but words implying lack of royalty or achievement could be considered antonyms in a metaphorical context.
