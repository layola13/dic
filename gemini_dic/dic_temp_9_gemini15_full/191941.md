scoots:/skuːts/| v.|轻快地走，疾驰，飞奔|to move quickly and lightly, typically on a scooter or similar vehicle; to move swiftly and easily. |例句：He scooted across the room to answer the phone. 他飞快地跑到房间另一边去接电话。|例句：The children scooted down the hill on their bikes. 孩子们骑着自行车飞快地冲下山坡。|近义词：zip, dash, whiz, zoom|反义词：crawl, creep, meander


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scoots to work every day. = 他每天骑车去上班。
The dog scoots around the park. = 狗在公园里四处乱窜。

一般过去时:
She scooted out of the room. = 她迅速离开了房间。
They scooted home after school. = 放学后他们飞快地回家了。

现在完成时:
I have scooted around the city all day. = 我今天在城里到处转悠了一整天。
He has scooted past me twice already. = 他已经两次从我身边飞驰而过。

过去完成时:
She had scooted away before I could ask her a question. = 我还没来得及问她问题她就跑了。
They had scooted off before the rain started. = 下雨前他们就跑了。

将来时:
I will scoot to the store after work. = 我下班后会骑车去商店。
We will scoot down the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天要在海滩上飞奔。

虚拟语气:
If I had a scooter, I would scoot everywhere. = 如果我有辆滑板车，我就会到处骑。
I wish I could scoot to the meeting, but it's too far. = 我真希望我能骑车去开会，但是太远了。

被动语态: (Scoot is rarely used in the passive voice)
The scooter was scooted quickly down the street.(不自然, 很少用) =  滑板车被迅速地推下街道。（不自然，很少使用）


疑问句:
Does he scoot to school? = 他骑车去上学吗？
Will you scoot to the library with me? = 你会和我一起去图书馆吗？

否定句:
He doesn't scoot to work anymore. = 他不再骑车上班了。
They didn't scoot home fast enough to avoid the rain. = 他们回家不够快，没躲过雨。
