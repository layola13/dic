protopetroleum: /ˌproʊtoʊˈpɛtroʊliəm/ | n. | 原油；原始石油 |  A hypothetical precursor to petroleum, believed to have formed from organic matter under high pressure and temperature conditions.  It is not a commercially viable substance itself, but rather a stage in the formation of crude oil.  | The study focuses on the transformation of protopetroleum into commercially viable crude oil.  这项研究的重点是将原油转化为具有商业价值的原油。|  precursor to petroleum |  (no direct antonym, as it's a stage in a process) | 名词
