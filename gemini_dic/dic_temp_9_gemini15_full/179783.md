reeve:/riːv/| n. v.|管理员；村长；穿线；|A reeve is a historical title, originally referring to a local official responsible for the administration of a village or shire.  It can also refer to someone who threads something, such as yarn through a loom. |例句：The village reeve was responsible for collecting taxes.  村长负责收税。|例句：She expertly reeves the yarn through the loom. 她熟练地将纱线穿梭于织机。|近义词：bailiff, officer, warden, thread |反义词：subject (in the sense of being governed), untangle
