episodic:/ˌɛpɪˈsoʊdɪk/ | adj. | 插曲的；片段的；间歇性的 | Relating to or consisting of a series of separate events; occurring in separate episodes.  | Example:  The patient experienced episodic memory loss.  患者经历了间歇性记忆丧失。  The show is an episodic drama. 这部剧集是一部连续剧。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional | 反义词：continuous, constant, persistent

词源:
From Greek *episodicós, from *episode, from *epi- "upon" + *odos "way, journey."  The original sense was of a narrative digression or incident in a longer work, later applying to any separate incident or event.
