hinged:/hɪndʒd/| adj.|铰链的；以铰链连接的；取决于；以…为中心|hinged describes something that is attached with a hinge, or that depends on something else.|The door is hinged on the right.|近义词：attached, pivoted|反义词：fixed, stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The door is hinged to the frame. = 门铰接到框架上。
The success of the project is hinged on funding. = 项目的成功取决于资金。

一般过去时:
The old cabinet was hinged with brass hinges. = 旧橱柜是用黄铜合页铰接的。
His decision was hinged on his family's opinion. = 他的决定取决于他家人的意见。

现在完成时:
The gate has been hinged securely. = 门已经牢固地铰接好了。
The outcome has been hinged on the results of the election. = 结果取决于选举的结果。

过去完成时:
The picture had been hinged to the wall for years. = 这幅画已经挂在墙上好多年了。
Everything had been hinged on his making the right decision. = 一切都取决于他做出正确的决定。

将来时:
The new shed will be hinged with strong metal hinges. = 新的棚屋将用坚固的金属合页铰接。
The future of the company will be hinged on the new product launch. = 公司的未来将取决于新产品的发布。

虚拟语气:
If the door were hinged properly, it wouldn't stick. = 如果门铰接得当，就不会卡住了。
If the plan hinged on his cooperation, it would fail without it. = 如果计划取决于他的合作，没有他的合作就会失败。

被动语态:
The picture was hinged to the wall. = 图片被铰接到墙上。
The success of the mission was hinged on the timely arrival of reinforcements. = 任务的成功取决于及时的增援到来。

疑问句:
Is the lid hinged? = 盖子是铰接的吗？
Is the success of your business hinged on social media? = 你生意的成功取决于社交媒体吗？

否定句:
The window isn't hinged correctly. = 窗户铰接得不正确。
The argument wasn't hinged on any factual basis. = 争论没有任何事实依据。
