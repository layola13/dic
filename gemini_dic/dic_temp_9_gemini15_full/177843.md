receptacles:/rɪˈsɛptəklz/ | n. |容器，受纳器 |A receptacle is a container, especially one that is designed to hold something.  It can refer to anything from a simple bin to a complex piece of equipment.  The word emphasizes the function of holding or receiving something. | Examples:  The waste receptacles were overflowing. (垃圾桶溢出来了。)  The plant needs a large receptacle for its roots. (这种植物需要一个大的根部容器。) |近义词：container, vessel, holder, receiver |反义词：source, origin
