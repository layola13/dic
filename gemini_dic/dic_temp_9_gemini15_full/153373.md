penetrator:/ˈpɛnɪtreɪtər/| n. |穿透物；渗透者；入侵者|A penetrator is something that penetrates or pierces something else, or someone who penetrates or intrudes.  It can refer to a physical object, a weapon, or a metaphorical force. |例句：The bullet acted as a penetrator, piercing the target.  这颗子弹像穿透物一样，穿透了目标。

The spy acted as a penetrator, gaining access to the enemy's secrets. 这个间谍充当渗透者，获得了敌人的秘密。|近义词：infiltrator, intruder, projectile, perforator|反义词：protector, defender
