residencia: /ˌrɛzɪˈdɛnsiə/ | n. |住所，居住地，居住权 | A place of residence; the place where someone lives; the right to live somewhere. |例句：My residencia is in the countryside. 我的住所位于乡下。|近义词：residence, dwelling, abode |反义词：departure, absence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My family resides in a small town. = 我的家人住在一个小镇上。
Where does she reside? = 她住在哪里？


一般过去时:
He resided in London for many years. = 他在伦敦住了很多年。
They resided in that house before moving to the city. = 在搬到城市之前，他们住在那个房子里。


现在完成时:
We have resided here for ten years. = 我们在这里住了十年了。
She has resided in Paris since 2010. = 自从2010年以来，她一直住在巴黎。


过去完成时:
They had resided in Spain before they moved to France. = 在搬到法国之前，他们住在西班牙。
He had resided in that apartment for five years before it was sold. = 在那套公寓出售之前，他住了五年。


将来时:
We will reside in a new house next year. = 我们明年将住在一栋新房子里。
She will reside in New York for the summer. = 她将在纽约住一个夏天。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger house, I would reside in the mountains. = 如果我有一栋更大的房子，我就会住在山上。
I wish I could reside closer to my work. = 我希望我能住得离我的工作更近一些。


被动语态:
The building is resided in by many families. = 这栋楼住着很多家庭。（此用法较少见，被动语态通常用residence）
The apartment was resided in by students for years. = 这套公寓住了很多年的学生。（此用法较少见，被动语态通常用residence）


疑问句:
Where do you reside? = 你住在哪里？
Does he reside in the city or the suburbs? = 他住在城市还是郊区？


否定句:
I do not reside in that city anymore. = 我不再住在那座城市了。
They did not reside in the same house. = 他们不住在同一所房子里。
