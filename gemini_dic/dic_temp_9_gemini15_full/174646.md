quizzes:/ˈkwɪzɪz/| n. |测验；问答比赛|A series of questions, typically given to test knowledge or skill.  Quizzes can be formal assessments or informal games.|例句：The teacher gave us a pop quiz on Friday. = 周五老师给我们做了一次突击测验。

例句：We're having a trivia quiz night at the pub. = 我们将在酒吧举办一个琐事问答之夜。

近义词：tests, examinations, questionaries|反义词：answers, solutions|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She takes quizzes regularly to improve her knowledge. = 她定期参加测验以提高知识水平。
The students are taking quizzes every week. = 学生们每周都参加测验。


一般过去时:
He took a quiz yesterday and got a good score. = 他昨天参加了一次测验，取得了不错的成绩。
