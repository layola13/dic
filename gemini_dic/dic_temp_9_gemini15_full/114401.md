make-believes:/ˈmeɪk bɪˈliːvz/ | n. | 假装游戏；虚构的事物 | Pretend play; imaginary things or situations.  Children often engage in make-believe to explore their imaginations and create their own worlds.  |例句：The children were engrossed in their make-believe world, acting out a pirate adventure. 孩子们全神贯注于他们的假装游戏中，扮演着海盗冒险的故事。

例句：Her stories were full of make-believe creatures and fantastical lands. 她的故事充满了虚构的生物和奇幻的土地。

近义词：pretend play, fantasy, imagination |反义词：reality, truth
