unconscious:/ʌnˈkɒnʃəs/| adj.| 无意识的；昏迷的 |  Lacking awareness or consciousness; in a state of unconsciousness.  |例句：He was knocked unconscious in the accident. 他在事故中被打昏了。
例句：She slipped into an unconscious state. 她陷入昏迷状态。|近义词：insensible, unaware, comatose |反义词：conscious, aware


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is unconscious of his mistakes. 他没有意识到自己的错误。
The patient is unconscious and unresponsive. 病人处于昏迷状态，没有反应。

一般过去时:
She was unconscious for several hours after the surgery. 手术后她昏迷了几个小时。
He became unconscious after hitting his head. 他撞到头后失去了知觉。

现在完成时:
She has been unconscious since the accident. 自从事故发生后，她就一直处于昏迷状态。
They have remained unconscious despite our efforts.  尽管我们努力了，他们仍然处于昏迷状态。

过去完成时:
He had been unconscious for days before they found him.  他们在找到他之前，他已经昏迷了好几天了。
The victim had been unconscious for a considerable time. 受害者昏迷了相当长的时间。

将来时:
He will likely remain unconscious for several more days. 他很可能还会昏迷几天。
The doctors believe she will soon regain consciousness. 医生认为她很快就会恢复知觉。


虚拟语气:
If he were unconscious, we would call an ambulance. 如果他昏迷了，我们会叫救护车。
I wish I hadn't been unconscious during that important meeting.  我希望在那次重要的会议上我没有昏迷。

被动语态:
He was found unconscious in his apartment. 他被发现昏迷在自己的公寓里。
The patient was kept unconscious during the operation. 病人在手术期间被维持在昏迷状态。

疑问句:
Is the patient still unconscious? 病人还处于昏迷状态吗？
Was he unconscious when the police arrived? 警察到达时他昏迷了吗？

否定句:
She was not unconscious, merely very tired. 她并没有昏迷，只是非常疲倦。
He was not unconscious; he was merely pretending. 他并没有昏迷；他只是在装作昏迷。
