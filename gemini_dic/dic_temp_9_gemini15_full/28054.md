capelle:/kəˈpɛl/| n. |小教堂，圣堂|A small chapel, often associated with a larger church or cathedral.  It can also refer to a small, private place of worship.|例句：The family held a private service in the capelle before the main ceremony at the cathedral. 家庭在主教座堂举行主要仪式之前，在小教堂里举行了一场私人仪式。|近义词：chapel, oratory, shrine|反义词：cathedral, basilica
