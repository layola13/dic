counterworlds: /ˈkaʊntərwɜːldz/ | n. | 反宇宙，平行宇宙 | Parallel or alternate universes existing alongside our own.  The term often implies a contrast or opposition to our known reality.  It is a concept frequently explored in science fiction. | Example: The novel explores the concept of counterworlds, where the laws of physics are different.  反宇宙小说探讨了物理定律不同的反宇宙概念。  | Near synonyms: parallel universes, alternate realities, alternate dimensions | Antonyms:  reality (as opposed to fictional counterworlds), this world |  词性: 名词
