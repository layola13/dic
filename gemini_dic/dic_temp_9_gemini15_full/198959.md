sight-read:/ˈsaɪt riːd/ | vt. |视奏，看谱演奏 | To read and perform music from the written score without prior rehearsal or preparation.  |例句：She could sight-read the difficult sonata with ease. 她能轻松地视奏这首难度较高的奏鸣曲。
                                                                                                      He struggled to sight-read the complex orchestral part. 他努力地视奏复杂的管弦乐部分。|近义词：play at sight, read at sight |反义词：rehearse, practice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sight-reads Bach effortlessly. = 她轻松地视奏巴赫的作品。
The orchestra sight-reads new pieces every week. = 乐队每周视奏新的乐曲。


一般过去时:
He sight-read the entire concerto flawlessly. = 他完美地视奏了整首协奏曲。
The pianist sight-read the challenging piece during the audition. = 钢琴家在试听时视奏了这首极具挑战性的乐曲。

She has sight-read many challenging scores in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经视奏过许多具有挑战性的乐谱。
