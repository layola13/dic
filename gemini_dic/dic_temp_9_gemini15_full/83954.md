half-consciousness:/ˌhɑːfˈkɒnʃəsnəs/| /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/| adj. n.|半清醒状态，朦胧的意识|A state of being only partly conscious; a drowsy or semi-conscious state.  It can also refer to a lack of full awareness or understanding.|例句：He awoke from a half-consciousness, disoriented and confused. 他从半昏迷状态中醒来，感到迷茫和困惑。


例句：The patient remained in a state of half-consciousness for several hours after the accident. 事故发生后，病人处于半清醒状态几个小时。


近义词：semi-consciousness, drowsiness, stupor|反义词：full consciousness, alertness, wakefulness|形容词，名词
