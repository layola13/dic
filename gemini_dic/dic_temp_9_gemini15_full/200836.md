slightly:/ˈslaɪtli/| adj.|轻微地；稍微；有点儿|A little; to a small degree.  The adjective "slightly" modifies a verb, adjective, or adverb to indicate a small degree or extent.|例句：The soup is slightly salty. 这汤有点咸。|例句：She slightly altered the recipe. 她稍微改动了食谱。|近义词：somewhat, a little, barely, faintly|反义词：significantly, considerably, greatly, intensely

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slightly prefers tea to coffee. 他稍微更喜欢茶而不是咖啡。
The colors are slightly different. 颜色略有不同。

一般过去时:
She slightly underestimated the cost. 她稍微低估了成本。
The car slightly bumped into a wall. 车子轻轻撞到了墙上。

现在完成时:
The weather has slightly improved. 天气稍微好转了。
My opinion has slightly changed. 我的想法稍微改变了。

过去完成时:
I had slightly forgotten her name. 我有点忘记了她的名字。
They had slightly miscalculated the time. 他们稍微误算了时间。

将来时:
The price will slightly increase next month. 下个月价格会略微上涨。
The temperature will slightly drop tonight. 今晚温度会略微下降。

虚拟语气:
If the instructions were slightly clearer, I would have understood. 如果说明稍微清楚一点，我就明白了。
I wish the music were slightly softer. 我希望音乐稍微轻柔一些。

被动语态:
The plan was slightly altered. 计划稍作修改。

疑问句:
Is the fabric slightly damaged? 布料稍微受损了吗？
Are the colors slightly off? 颜色稍微有点偏差吗？

否定句:
The task wasn't slightly difficult; it was extremely hard. 任务并非稍微困难；而是极其困难。
The results weren't slightly different; they were completely opposite. 结果并非略有不同；而是完全相反。
