habited:/ˈhæbɪtɪd/ | adj. |居住的；有居住者的；有…习惯的 |Having inhabitants; lived in; possessing a particular habit or characteristic.  |例句：The island is a sparsely habited region.  这个岛是一个人口稀少的地区。  |例句：He was habited in a simple robe. 他穿着简单的长袍。|近义词：inhabited, occupied, settled |反义词：uninhabited, deserted, vacant

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The village is habited by friendly people. = 村庄住着友善的人们。
This area is habited by a variety of birds. = 这个地区栖息着各种各样的鸟类。

一般过去时:
The house was habited by a large family. = 这所房子住着一大家子人。
The cave was once habited by ancient humans. = 这个山洞曾经有人类居住。

现在完成时:
The island has been habited for centuries. = 这个岛屿已经有人居住数个世纪了。
The forest has been habited by wolves for generations. = 森林里世世代代都居住着狼。

过去完成时:
The building had been habited for decades before it was demolished. = 那栋建筑在拆除之前已经住了几十年了。
The land had been habited by indigenous peoples for thousands of years. = 这片土地几千年来一直居住着土著居民。

将来时:
The new apartment building will be habited next year. = 新建的公寓楼明年将有人居住。
This area will be habited by many new residents soon. = 这个地区很快就会有很多新居民居住。

虚拟语气:
If the house were habited, it would be warmer. = 如果房子有人居住，就会暖和一些。
I wish the old castle were habited again. = 我希望古老的城堡能再次有人居住。

被动语态:
The area is habited by many different species of animals. = 这个地区居住着许多不同种类的动物。(被动语态中，habited多用于被动态)
The house was habited by a single old man. = 这所房子住着一位独居老人。(被动语态中，habited多用于被动态)

疑问句:
Is this area habited? = 这个地区有人居住吗？
Is the old building still habited? = 那栋旧建筑还住着人吗？

否定句:
The island is not habited. = 这个岛屿无人居住。
The abandoned town was no longer habited. = 废弃的城镇不再有人居住。


PHRASE->
well-habited:居住良好的
sparsely habited:人口稀少的
densely habited:人口稠密的
poorly habited:居住条件差的
habit-habited: 有某种习惯的


词源:
源自habit (习惯,居住) + -ed (形容词后缀)，表示“有…习惯的”或“居住的”。  The sense of "inhabited" developed from the older meaning relating to habits or customs.
