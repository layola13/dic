presterilize:/ˌpriːˈstɛrəlaɪz/| vt.| 预先消毒；预先灭菌 | To sterilize beforehand or in advance.  This is often done to equipment or materials before they are used in a procedure requiring sterility. | Example: The surgical instruments were presterilized before the operation.  手术器械在手术前已预先消毒。 The lab technician presterilized the petri dishes to prevent contamination. 实验室技术员预先对培养皿进行了消毒以防止污染。 | 近义词：sterilize in advance, pre-treat (with sterilizing agent) | 反义词：contaminate, infect | 动词
