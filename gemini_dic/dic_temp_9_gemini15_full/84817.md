handymen:/ˈhændiˌmæn/| n.|修理工，技工|A handyman is a person skilled in many practical tasks, typically around the home, and able to undertake small repairs or maintenance jobs.|例句：We hired a handyman to fix the leaky faucet. 我们雇了一个修理工来修理漏水的龙头。  例句：The handyman was able to repair the broken fence quickly.  这位技工很快就修好了坏掉的栅栏。|近义词：repairman, maintenance worker, handy person|反义词：specialist


USAGE->

The handyman fixes leaky faucets. = 修理工修理漏水的龙头。
He regularly maintains the building's facilities. = 他定期维护大楼的设施。
