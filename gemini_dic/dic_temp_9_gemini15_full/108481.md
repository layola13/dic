lay to heart:/leɪ tu hɑːrt/| v. |铭记于心；认真对待|To take something seriously and remember it well.  It implies a deep and lasting impact.|例句：He laid her advice to heart and avoided the risky investment. 他铭记她的忠告，避免了那项风险投资。
例句：She laid her father's words to heart and strived for excellence. 她铭记父亲的话，努力追求卓越。|近义词：take to heart, treasure, cherish|反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lays his mother's words to heart. 他铭记母亲的话。
They lay their teacher's advice to heart. 他们铭记老师的建议。

一般过去时:
She laid her friend's warning to heart. 她铭记朋友的警告。
He laid his grandfather's lessons to heart. 他铭记祖父的教诲。

现在完成时:
I have laid your concerns to heart. 我已将你的担忧铭记于心。
We have laid their suggestions to heart. 我们已经认真考虑了他们的建议。


过去完成时:
She had laid her mentor's words to heart for years. 她多年来一直铭记着导师的话。
They had laid the old adage to heart throughout their lives. 他们一生都铭记着那句古老的格言。

将来时:
He will lay his doctor's advice to heart. 他将会铭记医生的建议。
She will lay the criticism to heart and improve her work. 她会认真对待批评并改进她的工作。

虚拟语气:
If I had laid his warnings to heart, I wouldn't be in this mess. 如果我当时认真对待他的警告，我就不会陷入这种困境。
If she had laid her teacher's advice to heart, she would have succeeded. 如果她当时认真对待老师的建议，她就会成功。

被动语态:
Her advice was laid to heart by many students. 许多学生都铭记了她的建议。
His warnings should be laid to heart by everyone. 每个人都应该认真对待他的警告。

疑问句:
Did he lay my suggestion to heart? 他是否认真考虑了我的建议？
Will they lay the judge's ruling to heart? 他们会认真对待法官的裁决吗？

否定句:
He didn't lay his father's advice to heart. 他没有铭记父亲的建议。
She didn't lay the warning to heart, and it cost her dearly. 她没有把警告放在心上，为此付出了沉重的代价。
