chevachie:/ʃɛvəˈʃiː/| n.|骑马远征；远征；骑兵队|A chevauchée is a military expedition, typically involving mounted knights, undertaken for the purpose of raiding and plundering enemy territory.  It often involved a long ride across the countryside,  and was a common tactic in medieval warfare.|Example: The chevauchée devastated the countryside, leaving villages burned and plundered.|近义词：raid, foray, expedition|反义词：retreat, defense
