home-free:/hoʊmˈfriː/| adj.|自由的；顺利完成的；摆脱困境的|Free from obstacles or difficulties; successfully completed.|例句：After overcoming numerous challenges, they were finally home free.  克服重重困难后，他们终于顺利完成了。

例句：With the project finished, we are home free. 项目完成后，我们终于轻松了。


近义词：safe, clear, free and clear, out of the woods|反义词：stuck, trapped, in trouble


词性：形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is home free after passing the exam. = 他通过考试后终于轻松了。
They are home free with the deal finalized. = 交易完成，他们终于解脱了。


一般过去时:
She was home free once she paid off her debts. = 她还清债务后终于解脱了。
We were home free after the presentation went smoothly. = 演讲顺利进行后，我们终于松了一口气。


现在完成时:
He has been home free since he graduated. = 他毕业后一直很轻松。
They have been home free since the crisis ended. = 危机结束后他们一直很顺利。


过去完成时:
She had been home free before the unexpected problem arose. = 在出现意外问题之前，她一直很顺利。
We had been home free until the last minute change. = 直到最后一刻的改变之前，我们一直很顺利。


将来时:
They will be home free once the construction is complete. = 一旦建设完成，他们就万事大吉了。
He will be home free after he submits his thesis. = 他提交论文后就轻松了。


虚拟语气:
If the weather had been better, we would have been home free. = 如果天气好一些，我们早就顺利完成了。
If he had finished his work earlier, he would be home free now. = 如果他早点完成工作，现在就轻松了。


被动语态:
The project was declared home free after a successful audit. = 项目在成功审计后被宣布顺利完成。
The team was finally declared home free after months of hard work. = 经过几个月的努力，团队终于顺利完成了。


疑问句:
Is the team home free now? = 团队现在顺利完成了吗？
Are we home free with this project? = 这个项目我们已经顺利完成了吗？


否定句:
They are not home free yet; there are still some challenges ahead. = 他们还没有完全摆脱困境；前方还有挑战。
The project isn't home free until all the reports are submitted. = 直到所有报告提交后，项目才算顺利完成。



PHRASE->
