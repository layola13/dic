hectoring: /ˈhɛktərɪŋ/ | adj. | 训斥的；盛气凌人的；霸道的 |  Acting in a bullying or bossy manner; domineering.  |例句：His hectoring tone made it difficult to have a rational conversation. 他盛气凌人的语气使理性对话变得困难。

例句：The teacher's hectoring manner intimidated the students. 老师盛气凌人的态度吓住了学生们。


近义词：bullying, bossy, domineering, overbearing, tyrannical, imperious

反义词：gentle, kind, considerate, mild-mannered, submissive


词源:  Derived from the name Hector, a character in Homer's Iliad known for his arrogance and aggressive behavior.  The word's meaning evolved from referring specifically to bullying like Hector to a more general sense of domineering and bossy behavior.
