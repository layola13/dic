buttered:/ˈbʌtərd/ | adj. | 涂黄油的；油腻的 | Covered with butter.  |例句：The toast was nicely buttered.  烤面包涂上了黄油。

例句：The pan was buttered before the eggs were added. 煎锅在加鸡蛋之前涂上了黄油。


近义词：oiled, greased | 反义词：unbuttered


词源:  Comes from the verb "butter," meaning to spread butter on something.  The suffix "-ed" indicates the past participle, functioning as an adjective in this case.  The word's origins trace back to Old English "butere," ultimately from a Proto-Germanic source.
