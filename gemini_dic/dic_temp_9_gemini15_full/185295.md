retreating:/rɪˈtriːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 后退的；撤退的；衰退的 |  Describes something moving back or withdrawing; also used to describe a decline or weakening. |例句：The army was forced into a hasty retreating.  军队被迫仓促撤退。

The economy is showing signs of retreating growth. 经济显示出增长放缓的迹象。


近义词：withdrawing, receding, declining, weakening | 反义词：advancing, progressing, strengthening, improving | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The enemy is retreating from our position. = 敌人正在从我们的阵地上撤退。
The tide is retreating. = 潮水正在退去。

一般过去时:
The soldiers retreated after the battle. = 战后士兵们撤退了。
The ice retreated as the weather warmed. = 天气变暖后冰雪消融了。

现在完成时:
The army has retreated to its base. = 军队已经退回到基地。
The symptoms have retreated since I started the medication. = 自从我开始服药以来，症状已经减轻了。

过去完成时:
The enemy had retreated before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，敌人已经撤退了。
The glacier had retreated significantly over the centuries. = 冰川在几个世纪以来已经显著退缩了。

将来时:
The army will retreat if they are outnumbered. = 如果他们人数处于劣势，军队将会撤退。
The floodwaters will retreat once the rain stops. = 雨停后洪水将会退去。

虚拟语气:
If the enemy retreated, we would win the war. = 如果敌人撤退，我们将赢得战争。
I wish the pain would retreat. = 我希望疼痛能够减轻。

被动语态:
The troops were retreated to safety. = 部队被撤回安全地带。（注意：此句被动语态略显不自然，更常用的表达是"The troops retreated to safety"）
The plan was retreated from due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，计划被取消了。（此句更常用"The plan was abandoned"）


疑问句:
Is the army retreating? = 军队在撤退吗？
Is the pain retreating? = 疼痛正在减轻吗？

否定句:
The army is not retreating. = 军队没有撤退。
The glacier is not retreating at this moment. = 冰川目前没有退缩。


PHRASE->
retreating glacier: 退缩的冰川
retreating army: 撤退的军队
retreating hairline: 后退的发际线
retreating economy: 衰退的经济
retreating gums: 牙龈萎缩


词源:  The word "retreating" comes from the Old French word "retreter," meaning "to withdraw" or "to go back."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "retrāhere,"  which means "to draw back."
