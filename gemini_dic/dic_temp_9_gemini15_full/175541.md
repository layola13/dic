ranchlike:/ˈræntʃlaɪk/| adj.|牧场似的，像牧场的|Resembling a ranch; having the characteristics of a ranch, especially in terms of style, setting, or atmosphere.  It often implies a spacious, rustic, and informal setting.|例句：The house had a ranchlike feel, with its sprawling layout and large windows. 这栋房子有一种牧场的感觉，因为它布局宽敞，窗户很大。|例句：They built a ranchlike home on their expansive property. 他们在他们广阔的土地上建了一栋像牧场的房子。|近义词：rural, rustic, sprawling, spacious|反义词：urban, cramped, compact, formal
