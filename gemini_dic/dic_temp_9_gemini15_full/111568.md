lobelin:/ˈloʊbəlin/| n. |洛贝林|Lobelin is an alkaloid found in plants of the genus *Lobelia*, particularly *Lobelia inflata*.  It acts as a stimulant and has been used in traditional medicine, though its use is now largely restricted due to safety concerns.
