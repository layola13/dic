dopamines: /ˈdoʊpəmiːnz/ | n. | 多巴胺 | Dopamines are neurotransmitters that play a crucial role in various brain functions, including reward, motivation, and movement.  They are involved in feelings of pleasure and satisfaction, and their dysfunction can contribute to various neurological and psychiatric disorders. | Example:  Increased dopamine levels are associated with feelings of euphoria.  增加的多巴胺水平与欣快感有关。  Low dopamine levels can lead to Parkinson's disease. 低多巴胺水平会导致帕金森氏症。 | 近义词: neurotransmitters (神经递质) | 反义词: N/A
