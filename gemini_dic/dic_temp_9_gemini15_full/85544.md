hatchet:/ˈhætʃɪt/| n.|小斧头，短柄斧|A hatchet is a small axe with a short handle, typically used for chopping wood or other similar tasks.  It's smaller and lighter than a full-sized axe.|例句：He used a hatchet to chop the wood for the campfire. 他用小斧头砍柴生火。

例句：The woodsman carried a hatchet on his belt.  樵夫腰间别着一把小斧头。


近义词：small axe, hand axe|反义词：None specifically.  Antonyms would describe the opposite action (e.g., assembling instead of chopping).|名词
