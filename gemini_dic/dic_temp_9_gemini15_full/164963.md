preguides:/ˌpriːˈɡaɪdz/ | n. | 预先指导，预先引导 |  Guidance or instructions given in advance.  Preguides help prepare someone for a future event or task.  | Example: The preguides for the hiking trip included a detailed map and safety instructions.  预先的徒步旅行指导包括详细的地图和安全指示。 | 近义词：advance instructions, preliminary guidance, prior briefing | 反义词：post-instructions, afterthoughts
