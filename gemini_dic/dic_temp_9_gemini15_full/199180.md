siliconing: /ˈsɪlɪkəʊnɪŋ/ | n. |硅化；涂硅；使用硅胶处理 | The process of applying silicone to a surface.  This can refer to various applications, including waterproofing, lubricating, or creating a sealant. |例句：The siliconing of the baking pan prevented sticking.  (涂抹硅胶到烤盘上防止粘黏。)

The siliconing of the roof protected it from leaks. (屋顶的硅化处理保护它免受漏水。)


近义词：siliconizing, sealing, waterproofing  | 反义词：None directly opposite, but actions undoing the effect of siliconing could be considered antonyms (e.g., removing silicone). | 词性：名词
