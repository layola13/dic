nationalize:/ˈnæʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. |国有化；使民族化 | To bring under the control of a nation; to make something national in character.  |例句：The government decided to nationalize the railway system. 政府决定将铁路系统国有化。 |例句：The company's products were nationalized, marketed as representing the best of the country's heritage. 该公司的产品被赋予民族特色，作为该国遗产最佳代表进行销售。|近义词：socialize, collectivize |反义词：privatize

一般现在时:
The government nationalizes key industries. = 政府将关键产业国有化。

一般过去时:
