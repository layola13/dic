invade:/ɪnˈveɪd/ | vt. |入侵；侵袭；侵犯；涌入|To enter a place in large numbers, especially in a way that is forceful or damaging.  It often implies aggression and a violation of boundaries.|例句：The army invaded the country.  军队入侵了这个国家。|例句：Weed invaded the garden.  杂草侵占了花园。|近义词：attack, conquer, overrun, encroach |反义词：withdraw, retreat, vacate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The barbarians invade the city every year. = 野蛮人每年都入侵这座城市。
The weeds invade my garden. = 杂草入侵我的花园。

一般过去时:
The Romans invaded Britain in 43 AD. = 罗马人在公元43年入侵了不列颠。
The enemy invaded our territory last night. = 敌人昨晚入侵了我们的领土。

现在完成时:
The insects have invaded the house. = 昆虫已经入侵了房子。
The tourists have invaded the small town. = 游客涌入了这个小镇。

过去完成时:
The country had been invaded several times before. = 这个国家之前已经被入侵过好几次了。
The disease had already invaded the entire village before the cure was found. = 在找到治疗方法之前，疾病已经侵袭了整个村庄。

将来时:
They will invade the island tomorrow. = 他们明天将入侵该岛屿。
We fear they will invade our privacy. = 我们担心他们会侵犯我们的隐私。

虚拟语气:
If the enemy invaded, we would fight back. = 如果敌人入侵，我们将反击。
I wish the pests hadn't invaded my crops. = 我真希望害虫没有侵害我的庄稼。

被动语态:
The country was invaded by the enemy. = 这个国家被敌人入侵了。
The city was invaded by a swarm of locusts. = 城市被一大群蝗虫侵袭了。

疑问句:
Did the Vikings invade England? = 维京人入侵过英格兰吗？
Will the virus invade our immune systems? = 病毒会入侵我们的免疫系统吗？

否定句:
The army did not invade the neighboring country. = 军队没有入侵邻国。
The aliens did not invade Earth. = 外星人没有入侵地球。


PHRASE->
invade one's privacy = 侵犯某人的隐私
invade the market = 进入市场
invade one's thoughts = 涌入某人的脑海
invade one's space = 侵犯某人的空间
invade a country = 入侵一个国家
