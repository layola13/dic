self-parodists:/ˈsɛlf ˈpærədɪsts/ | n. | 自我模仿者，自嘲者 | Those who create works that satirize or mock their own style or previous works.  They often use humor and irony to comment on their own image or artistic persona. |  Example: Many stand-up comedians are self-parodists, incorporating jokes about their own flaws and experiences into their routines.  许多单口喜剧演员都是自我模仿者，他们在表演中加入了关于自身缺陷和经历的笑话。| 近义词：satirists, caricaturists, humorists | 反义词：serious artists, earnest performers |  名词
