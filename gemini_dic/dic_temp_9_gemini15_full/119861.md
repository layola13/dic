milestone:/ˈmaɪlˌstoʊn/ | n. | 里程碑，重要事件 | A significant event or stage in the development of something.  | Example:  The launch of the new product was a major milestone for the company.  这家公司新产品的发布是一个重要的里程碑。

The successful completion of the project was a significant milestone.  项目的成功完成是一个重要的里程碑。|近义词：landmark, turning point, significant event |反义词：insignificant event, minor detail |名词
