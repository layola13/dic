fallback:/ˈfɔːlbæk/ /ˈfɔːlˌbæk/| n. v.| 后备方案；退路；退却；|n. A plan or course of action to be followed if the primary plan fails.  v. To retreat or withdraw. |例句：n. We need a fallback plan in case the main project fails. 我们需要一个后备计划，以防主要项目失败。 v. The soldiers fell back to their original position. 士兵们退回到了原来的阵地。|近义词：alternative, backup, retreat, recourse|反义词：advance, primary plan, main plan|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system has a fallback mechanism. = 系统有一个后备机制。
What is your fallback position? = 你的后备方案是什么？


一般过去时:
They fell back on their savings. = 他们依靠他们的储蓄。
The army fell back after the defeat. = 军队在失败后撤退了。


现在完成时:
We have fallen back on plan B. = 我们已经转向B计划了。
The company has fallen back on its previous strategy. = 公司已经退回到了之前的策略。


过去完成时:
They had fallen back on their previous arguments. = 他们已经退回到了之前的论点。
The negotiators had fallen back on a compromise. = 谈判代表们已经退回到了妥协方案。


将来时:
We will fall back on our reserves if necessary. = 必要时我们将动用我们的储备。
If the negotiations fail, we will fall back on litigation. = 如果谈判失败，我们将诉诸诉讼。


虚拟语气:
If the primary system fails, we would fall back to the secondary system. = 如果主要系统失效，我们将切换到辅助系统。
I wish we had a better fallback option. = 我希望我们有一个更好的后备方案。


被动语态:
The fallback plan was activated. = 后备计划被启动了。
The old system was fallen back on due to technical issues. = 由于技术问题，旧系统被启用。


疑问句:
Do we have a fallback plan? = 我们有后备计划吗？
What is our fallback position if this deal collapses? = 如果这笔交易失败，我们的后备方案是什么？


否定句:
We don't have a fallback position. = 我们没有后备方案。
The company did not fall back on its original plan. = 公司没有退回到原来的计划。
