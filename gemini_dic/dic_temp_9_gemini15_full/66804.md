expands:/ɪkˈspændz/ | v. |膨胀；扩张；扩展；详述|To increase in size, number, or extent.  It can also refer to elaborating on a topic or idea.|例句：The balloon expands when you blow air into it.  气球充气后会膨胀。

The company plans to expand its operations overseas. 公司计划将其业务扩展到海外。


近义词：enlarge, extend, broaden, swell, dilate | 反义词：contract, shrink, condense


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The universe expands constantly. 宇宙不断膨胀。
My knowledge expands with each new book I read. 我的知识随着我读的每一本书而增长。

一般过去时:
The metal expanded when heated. 金属受热膨胀。
The empire expanded its territory through conquest. 帝国通过征服扩张了领土。

现在完成时:
The city has expanded significantly in recent years. 近年来，这座城市已经显著扩张。
My understanding of the problem has expanded considerably. 我对这个问题的理解已经大大扩展。

过去完成时:
The balloon had expanded to its maximum size before it burst. 气球膨胀到最大尺寸才爆裂。
The company had expanded its product line before the merger. 公司在合并之前已经扩大了产品线。

将来时:
The population will expand rapidly in the coming decade. 未来十年人口将迅速增长。
We will expand our marketing efforts next year. 我们明年将扩大我们的营销力度。

虚拟语气:
If the economy expanded, unemployment would decrease. 如果经济扩张，失业率就会下降。
I wish my vocabulary expanded more quickly. 我希望我的词汇量能更快地增长。

被动语态:
The bridge was expanded to accommodate more traffic. 桥梁进行了拓宽以适应更大的交通流量。
The company's market share has been expanded through aggressive marketing. 通过积极的营销，公司的市场份额得到了扩大。

疑问句:
Does the universe expand infinitely? 宇宙是否无限膨胀？
Will the company expand into new markets? 公司会进军新市场吗？

否定句:
The metal did not expand as expected. 金属没有按预期膨胀。
The business did not expand into new territories. 这家公司没有扩张到新的地区。


PHRASE->
expand on: 详述，详细解释
expand into: 扩展到，发展到
expand upon: 进一步阐述
rapidly expand: 快速扩张
expand the scope: 扩大范围
