exorcising:/ˈɛksɔːrsɪzɪŋ/| v. |驱魔；去除；消除|The act of driving out evil spirits;  removing or getting rid of something unwanted or unpleasant.|例句：The priest was exorcising the demon from the possessed woman.  牧师正在为被附身的女子驱魔。

例句：She spent the afternoon exorcising the negative thoughts from her mind. 她花了一下午的时间去除脑海中的消极想法。

近义词：expelling, banishing, eliminating, removing | 反义词：summoning, inviting, embracing

She exorcises negativity through meditation. = 她通过冥想来消除负能量。

词源:

源自古希腊语 "exorcismus"，由 "ex-" (out) 和 "orkizein" (to adjure, to bind by oath) 组成，字面意思是“发誓驱逐”。  这反映了驱魔仪式中使用誓言和祈祷的传统。
