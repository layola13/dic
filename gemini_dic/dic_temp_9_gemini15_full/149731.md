palanquin:/pælənˈkiːn/| n. |轿子，肩舆|A palanquin is a covered litter, typically carried by two or more people, used for transporting a person.  It's a traditional mode of transportation in some parts of Asia.|例句：The bride arrived at the ceremony in a richly decorated palanquin. 新娘乘坐一顶装饰华丽的轿子到达仪式现场。
The dignitaries were carried in palanquins through the streets.  要员们乘坐轿子穿过街道。|近义词：litter, sedan chair|反义词：car, automobile, vehicle|名词
