deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/| adj., v.|故意的；深思熟虑的；从容不迫的 |  Intentional; carefully considered; slow and steady. |例句：His actions were deliberate. 他的行为是故意的。 The committee deliberated for hours before reaching a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前讨论了数小时。 |近义词：intentional, purposeful, premeditated, considered |反义词：accidental, unintentional, spontaneous, impulsive |形容词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deliberates carefully before making a decision. 他做决定前仔细考虑。
She deliberates on the matter every day. 她每天都在思考这件事。


一般过去时:
The jury deliberated for three days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了三天后才做出裁决。
They deliberated long and hard about the problem.  他们认真地长时间考虑了这个问题。


现在完成时:
The committee has deliberated on the proposal for weeks. 委员会已经考虑这个提案好几个星期了。
She has deliberated carefully before responding to his email. 她仔细考虑后才回复了他的邮件。


过去完成时:
They had deliberated for hours before the chairman made his announcement. 主席宣布之前，他们已经讨论了几个小时。
After they had deliberated, a conclusion was reached.  他们讨论之后，得出了一个结论。


将来时:
The council will deliberate on the new budget next week. 理事会下周将审议新的预算。
We will deliberate the matter further tomorrow. 我们明天将进一步讨论此事。


虚拟语气:
If they had deliberated more carefully, they might have avoided the mistake. 如果他们更仔细地考虑过，他们可能就能避免这个错误。
I wish they had deliberated more thoroughly before acting. 我希望他们在行动前能够更周全地考虑一下。


被动语态:
The matter was deliberated upon by the committee.  委员会对这件事进行了审议。
The proposal is being deliberated at the moment. 目前正在讨论该提案。


疑问句:
Will the council deliberate on this issue? 理事会将讨论这个问题吗？
Have they deliberated the implications of their decision? 他们考虑过他们决定的含义吗？


否定句:
He did not deliberate before acting. 他没有经过考虑就采取行动。
The committee did not deliberate sufficiently before voting. 委员会在投票前没有充分考虑。
