noninfringement:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt/ | n. |不侵权；无侵犯 |The state or condition of not infringing; the absence of infringement, especially of a copyright, patent, or trademark.  It refers to the act of not violating someone's rights or a law. |Example:  The company ensured the noninfringement of its competitor's patents before launching its new product.  该公司在推出新产品之前确保不侵犯其竞争对手的专利。|近义词：compliance, conformity, observance |反义词：infringement, violation
