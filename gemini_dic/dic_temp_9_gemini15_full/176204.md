re-attributes:/ˌriːˈætrɪbjuːts/| vt.|重新归属；重新分配|To assign or ascribe something again or differently.|例句：The committee re-attributed the authorship of the painting to a lesser-known artist. 委员会将这幅画的作者重新归属于一位鲜为人知的艺术家。|例句：The company re-attributed the project's failure to a lack of funding, not poor management. 公司将项目的失败重新归因于资金不足，而不是管理不善。|近义词：reassign, reallocate, reassign, redistribute|反义词：appropriate, claim, attribute|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-attributes the blame to his assistant. = 他把责任重新归咎于他的助手。
The company re-attributes resources to the new project. = 公司将资源重新分配给新的项目。


一般过去时:
She re-attributed the success to teamwork. = 她把成功重新归功于团队合作。
They re-attributed the delays to unforeseen circumstances. = 他们将延误重新归因于不可预见的情况。


现在完成时:
The professor has re-attributed the findings to a different theory. = 教授已将研究结果重新归因于另一种理论。
We have re-attributed the tasks to improve efficiency. = 我们已经重新分配任务以提高效率。


过去完成时:
They had re-attributed the responsibility before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经重新分配了责任。
She had re-attributed the cause after further investigation. = 经过进一步调查后，她已经重新确定了原因。


将来时:

The manager will re-attribute the roles based on performance. = 经理将根据绩效重新分配角色。


虚拟语气:
If they re-attributed the funds, the project might succeed. = 如果他们重新分配资金，项目也许会成功。
I wish he had re-attributed the credit to the team. = 我希望他把功劳重新归于团队。
