ethene:/ˈiːθiːn/| n. |乙烯|Ethene is a colorless, flammable gas with a faint sweet odor. It's the simplest alkene, a hydrocarbon with a carbon-carbon double bond.  It's a crucial building block in the petrochemical industry, used to produce a vast array of plastics, synthetic fibers, and other chemicals.|Example:  Ethene is used in the production of polyethylene. 乙烯用于生产聚乙烯。|  Example: The polymerization of ethene creates polyethylene. 乙烯的聚合反应生成聚乙烯。|近义词:ethylene|反义词:|无
