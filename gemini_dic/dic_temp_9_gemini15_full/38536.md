conclaves:/ˈkɒŋkleɪvz/| n.|秘密会议；秘密聚会|A conclave is a private or secret meeting, especially one of a group of people who have power or influence.  It often implies secrecy and importance.|例句：The cardinals held a conclave to elect a new pope.  枢机主教们举行秘密会议选举新教皇。|例句：The leaders met in a secret conclave to discuss the crisis. 领导人们在一个秘密会议上讨论危机。|近义词：meeting, assembly, conference, caucus|反义词：public meeting, open discussion
