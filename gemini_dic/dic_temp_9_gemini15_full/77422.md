gavial:/ˈɡeɪviəl/| n. |恒河鳄|A gavial is a crocodilian reptile with a long, slender snout, typically found in the rivers of the Indian subcontinent.  Gavials are characterized by their extremely narrow snouts, which are adapted for catching fish.
