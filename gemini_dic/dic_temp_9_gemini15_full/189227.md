sacristans:/ˈsækrɪstənz/| n.|司事；圣器保管员|A sacristan is a person who is responsible for the care of a church and its sacred objects.  They often assist the priest or clergy with liturgical duties.|The sacristan prepared the altar for the morning Mass.  司事为早上的弥撒准备了祭坛。|The sacristan meticulously cleaned and polished the sacred vessels. 司事一丝不苟地清洁和擦亮圣器。|churchwarden, verger|  (no direct antonym)|名词
