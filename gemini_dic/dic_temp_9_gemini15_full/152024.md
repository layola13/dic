patchouli: /pætʃˈuːli/ | n. |广藿香，广藿香油 | A strong-smelling, dark-brown essential oil obtained from the leaves of the patchouli plant, used in perfumes and incense.  It has a musky, earthy scent.  | Example: The perfume contained a prominent note of patchouli. 这款香水含有明显的广藿香成分。  The air was thick with the scent of patchouli and sandalwood. 空气中弥漫着浓烈的广藿香和檀香的味道。|近义词：musk, sandalwood (in terms of scent profile)|反义词： (lacks a direct antonym, but scents like citrus could be considered contrasting)|名词
