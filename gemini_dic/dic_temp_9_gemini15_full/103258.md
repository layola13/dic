jewelweed:/ˈdʒuːəlwiːd/| n. |凤仙花，水金凤|A jewelweed is a plant in the genus Impatiens, known for its vibrant flowers and seed pods that explode when touched.  They are often found in moist areas.|Example:  I saw a patch of jewelweed growing by the stream. 我在小溪边看到一片凤仙花。


The jewelweed's seed pods burst open when touched, scattering seeds.  凤仙花的种子荚一碰就爆开，散播种子。|近义词：touch-me-not (referring to the seed pod characteristic)|反义词：无|名词
