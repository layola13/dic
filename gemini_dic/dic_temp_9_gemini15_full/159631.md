pollutions:/pəˈluːʃənz/ | n. |污染物；污染现象 |The plural form of "pollution". It refers to the presence in or introduction into the environment of a substance which has harmful or poisonous effects.  |例句：The air pollutions in the city are severe.  城市的空气污染严重。  例句：  The river is suffering from various pollutions, including industrial waste and sewage. 这条河遭受着各种污染，包括工业废物和污水。|近义词：contaminations, contaminations, pollutions |反义词：cleanliness, purity


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense aspects are not applicable.  However, it can be used in various sentence structures:


Singular and Plural Use:
Pollution is a serious problem. 污染是一个严重的问题。
Pollutions from multiple sources are affecting the ecosystem. 来自多个来源的污染正在影响生态系统。


With Prepositions:
The effects of pollution on human health are well documented. 污染对人体健康的影响已被充分证明。
We need to reduce pollution in our oceans. 我们需要减少海洋污染。


In Different Sentence Structures:
Air pollution causes respiratory problems. 空气污染会导致呼吸系统疾病。
Water pollution is a major threat to marine life. 水污染是对海洋生物的主要威胁。
The government is addressing the issue of soil pollution. 政府正在解决土壤污染问题。


PHRASE->
