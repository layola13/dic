phytophysiology: /ˌfaɪtoʊˌfɪziˈɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 植物生理学 | The study of the functions and processes of plants.  It encompasses various aspects of plant life, from cellular processes to whole-plant responses to the environment. | Example:  Phytophysiology research is crucial for understanding plant adaptation to climate change.  植物生理学研究对于理解植物对气候变化的适应至关重要。|近义词：plant physiology | 反义词：无
