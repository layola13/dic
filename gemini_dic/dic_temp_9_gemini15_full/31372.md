charbroiling:/ˈtʃɑːrbroʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 炭火烧烤；炙烤 | The process of cooking food over hot coals or on a grill, resulting in a charred or seared surface.  | Example: The restaurant is known for its delicious charbroiling technique. 这家餐馆以其美味的炭火烧烤技术而闻名。 |近义词：grilling, barbecuing | 反义词：boiling, steaming
