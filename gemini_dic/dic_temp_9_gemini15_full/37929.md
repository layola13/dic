completeness:/kəmˈpliːtnəs/ | n. |完整性，完全性 | The state or quality of being complete; the extent to which something is complete.  It refers to the lack of missing parts or elements, representing a whole and undivided entity. | Example: The completeness of the data set allowed for a thorough analysis.  这个数据集的完整性允许进行彻底的分析。  The completeness of his explanation left no room for doubt. 他解释的完整性消除了任何疑问。|近义词：wholeness, totality, integrity, perfection |反义词：incompleteness, deficiency, imperfection, fragmentariness |名词
