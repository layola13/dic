forks:/fɔːrks/| n.|叉子；岔路；分叉处|A fork is a utensil with prongs used for eating.  It can also refer to a branching point, like a road fork.|例句：I need a fork to eat this pasta. 我需要一把叉子吃这意大利面。

例句：The road forks into two paths. 这条路分岔成两条路。|近义词：prong, branch, division|反义词:knife, spoon
