cellulite:/ˈsɛljuˌlaɪt/| n. |橘皮组织，脂肪团|A dimpled, lumpy appearance of the skin, often on the thighs, buttocks, and hips, caused by fat deposits beneath the skin's surface.  It's not a medical condition but a cosmetic concern.|Many women suffer from cellulite on their thighs.|近义词：adipose tissue (脂肪组织), dimpling (凹凸)|反义词：smooth skin (光滑的皮肤)
