haemorrhage:/ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/|/ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/| n. v.|出血，大出血|The loss of a large amount of blood from the body, either internally or externally.|The patient experienced a severe haemorrhage following the accident.  病人事故后经历了严重出血。|The wound started to haemorrhage profusely.  伤口开始大量出血。|bleeding, effusion|healing, clotting|名词和动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He haemorrhages frequently. = 他经常出血。
The wound haemorrhages slowly. = 伤口慢慢地出血。


一般过去时:
She haemorrhaged after the surgery. = 她手术后出血了。
The artery haemorrhaged profusely. = 动脉大量出血。


现在完成时:
He has haemorrhaged several times this year. = 他今年已经几次出血了。
The patient has haemorrhaged since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，病人一直在出血。


过去完成时:
The patient had haemorrhaged before the doctors arrived. = 在医生到达之前，病人已经出血了。
She had haemorrhaged extensively before the ambulance arrived. = 救护车到达之前，她已经大量出血了。


将来时:
He will haemorrhage if he doesn't get treatment. = 如果他不接受治疗，他会出血。
The doctor thinks she will haemorrhage again. = 医生认为她还会再次出血。


虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn’t haemorrhaged so much. = 我真希望我没有流那么多血。


被动语态:
The haemorrhage was stopped quickly. = 出血很快被止住了。
The patient was treated for a severe haemorrhage. = 病人接受了严重出血的治疗。


疑问句:
Is he haemorrhaging internally? = 他内出血了吗？
Did the patient haemorrhage during the operation? = 病人在手术过程中出血了吗？


否定句:

The surgery was successful, and she didn't haemorrhage. = 手术很成功，她没有出血。
