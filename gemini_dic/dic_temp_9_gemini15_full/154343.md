perked:/pɜːkt/| v.| 使振作；使活跃；使竖起 | To make someone or something more lively or cheerful; to raise or lift something. | 例句：The good news perked her up. 好消息使她振作起来。

The coffee perked me up in the morning. 早上的咖啡使我精神振奋。


近义词：livened, cheered, invigorated, stimulated | 反义词：depressed, saddened, deflated
