guidance:/ˈɡaɪdns/| n.|指导，引导，建议|The act of guiding or showing someone the way; advice or information given to someone to help them. |例句：He sought guidance from his mentor. 他向他的导师寻求指导。|例句：The teacher provided guidance to her students. 老师为她的学生提供了指导。|近义词：direction, instruction, advice, counsel|反义词：misguidance, hindrance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher provides guidance to her students. = 老师为她的学生提供指导。
This app offers helpful guidance on using public transportation. = 这个应用程序提供了关于使用公共交通的实用指导。


一般过去时:
She received guidance from a career counselor. = 她从职业顾问那里得到了指导。
The map gave us guidance through the unfamiliar city. = 地图为我们指引了穿越陌生城市的道路。


现在完成时:
He has received excellent guidance throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都得到了极好的指导。
The company has provided guidance to its employees on safety procedures. = 公司就安全程序向其员工提供了指导。


过去完成时:
I had already received guidance on this matter before the meeting. = 会议之前我已经就此事收到了指导。
They had sought guidance from experts before making the decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经向专家寻求了指导。


将来时:
She will seek guidance from a financial advisor. = 她将寻求一位理财顾问的指导。
The company will provide guidance on the new software next week. = 公司下周将提供关于新软件的指导。


虚拟语气:
If I had received better guidance, I would have made different choices. = 如果我得到更好的指导，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had had more guidance in my early career. = 我希望在我的职业生涯早期有更多的指导。


被动语态:
Guidance was given to the students on how to write essays. = 学生们收到了关于如何写论文的指导。
The project is being guided by a team of experienced professionals. = 该项目正由一支经验丰富的专业团队指导。


疑问句:
What kind of guidance do you need? = 你需要什么指导？
Can you give me some guidance on this problem? = 你能就这个问题给我一些指导吗？


否定句:
He didn't receive the guidance he needed. = 他没有得到他需要的指导。
The instructions lacked clear guidance. = 说明书缺乏清晰的指导。
