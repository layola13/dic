motorboat:/ˈmoʊtərbəʊt/|/ˈməʊtəbəʊt/| n. |摩托艇；快艇 |A motorboat is a boat propelled by a motor, typically an internal combustion engine.  They are often used for recreation and sport. |例句：We rented a motorboat and spent the afternoon exploring the lake. 我们租了一艘摩托艇，花了一下午的时间探索湖泊。|例句：The motorboat sped across the water, leaving a white wake. 摩托艇飞速穿过水面，留下白色的尾迹。|近义词：speedboat, powerboat |反义词：sailboat, rowboat
