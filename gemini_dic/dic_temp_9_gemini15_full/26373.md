by and large:/baɪ ænd lɑːdʒ/ | adj. | 大体上；总的来说 | Generally speaking; for the most part.  This phrase indicates that a statement is true in most cases, but not necessarily in every single instance. |例句：By and large, the project was a success, although there were a few minor setbacks.  大体上，这个项目是成功的，尽管有一些小挫折。
例句：By and large, people are happy with the new policy. 总的来说，人们对新政策感到满意。|近义词：generally, mostly, primarily, mainly |反义词：entirely, completely, totally, absolutely
