dissociated:/dɪˈsoʊʃieɪtɪd/| adj.|分离的；脱节的；解离的；分裂的|Separated or detached; feeling disconnected from oneself or one's surroundings; in chemistry, separated into constituent parts.|The trauma left him feeling dissociated from his emotions.  创伤让他感觉与自己的情绪脱节。|The experiment involved dissociating the chemical compounds. 实验包括分离化学化合物。|associated, connected|integrated, unified, whole


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The patient feels dissociated from reality. = 病人感觉与现实脱节。
His thoughts are dissociated from his actions. = 他的思想与行为脱节。


一般过去时:
She dissociated herself from the controversial group. = 她与那个有争议的团体撇清了关系。
After the accident, he dissociated from his memories. = 事故后，他失去了部分记忆。


现在完成时:
He has dissociated himself from the project. = 他已从该项目中抽身。
The symptoms have dissociated her from her normal life. = 这些症状让她无法过正常生活。


过去完成时:
She had dissociated herself from the company before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，她已经与该公司撇清了关系。
The researchers had dissociated the proteins before analysis. = 研究人员在分析之前已经分离了蛋白质。


将来时:
He will dissociate himself from the organization. = 他将与该组织脱钩。
The chemicals will dissociate in water. = 这些化学物质会在水中分解。


虚拟语气:
If I were to dissociate myself from the situation, I would feel better. = 如果我能从这种情况中抽身，我会感觉更好。
I wish I could dissociate my emotions from the situation. = 我希望我能将我的情绪与这种情况分开。


被动语态:
The chemical compound was dissociated into its components. = 化合物被分解成其组分。
He was dissociated from his family due to the conflict. = 由于冲突，他与家人疏远了。


疑问句:
Is he dissociated from reality? = 他与现实脱节了吗？
Are the two concepts dissociated in your mind? = 在你的脑海中这两个概念是分开的吗？


否定句:
The symptoms are not dissociated from the underlying disease.  = 这些症状并非与潜在疾病无关。
He was not dissociated from the decision-making process. = 他并没有被排除在决策过程之外。
