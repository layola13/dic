liberties:/ˈlɪbərtiz/| n.|自由；特权；放肆；冒犯|Freedoms of thought or action; privileges or rights.  It can also refer to informal or disrespectful behavior.|例句：They took liberties with the original story. 他们对原著故事做了改动。(改动可以是自由发挥，也可以指不尊重的改动)|近义词：freedoms, privileges, rights|反义词：restrictions, constraints, limitations|名词


例句：

They fought for their liberties.  他们为争取自由而战。

The soldiers took liberties with the local population.  士兵们对当地居民进行了侵犯/放肆的行为。


PHRASE->
take liberties with:  随意对待；擅自改动
civil liberties: 公民自由
personal liberties: 个人自由
poetic liberties:  诗歌创作中的艺术性发挥（允许的偏差）
at liberty:  自由的；有权的
