iman:/ˈɪmæn/| n.|伊玛目（伊斯兰教的宗教领袖）|An imam is a leader of prayer in a mosque.  Imams are responsible for leading congregational prayers, delivering sermons, and providing religious guidance to the Muslim community.|例句：The imam led the Friday prayers at the mosque. 伊玛目带领了清真寺的周五祈祷。|例句：He consulted the imam for advice on religious matters. 他就宗教问题向伊玛目请教。|近义词：leader, cleric, preacher|反义词：follower, congregant
