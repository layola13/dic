crash-out: /ˈkræʃ aʊt/ | v. | 突然睡着；崩溃；(尤指因疲劳或饮酒过度而) 昏睡过去 | To fall asleep suddenly and deeply, especially because of exhaustion or excessive alcohol consumption.  |例句：After a long day of work, I completely crashed out on the sofa.  = 长时间工作后，我完全在沙发上睡着了。
I crashed out after drinking too much wine. = 我喝了太多的酒后就睡着了。|近义词：pass out, collapse,conk out|反义词：stay awake, remain conscious | 动词


USAGE->

He crashes out every night after 10 pm. = 他每天晚上十点后都睡着了。
She often crashes out in front of the TV. = 她经常在电视机前睡着。

被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action done to oneself.)
