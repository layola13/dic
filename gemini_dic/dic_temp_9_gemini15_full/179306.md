redocket:/ˌriːˈdɒkɪt/| vt.|重新申请；重新提交|To submit or file something again, especially a legal document or application.|The lawyer had to redocket the case after discovering a crucial piece of evidence.|近义词：refile, resubmit|反义词：withdraw, dismiss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redocket the appeal every year. = 他们每年都重新提交上诉。
She redockets her application for a visa. = 她重新提交了签证申请。


一般过去时:
The court redocketed the case after the error was discovered. = 在发现错误后，法院重新立案。
He redocketed the documents with the updated information. = 他用更新的信息重新提交了文件。


现在完成时:
They have redocketed the case several times. = 他们已经多次重新提交了此案。
She has redocketed her application since the previous one was rejected. = 自从她之前的申请被拒绝后，她已经重新提交了申请。


过去完成时:
They had redocketed the appeal before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前重新提交了上诉。
She had redocketed the documents before leaving for the conference. = 出席会议前，她已重新提交了文件。


将来时:
We will redocket the case next week. = 我们下周将重新提交此案。
They will redocket the application after making the necessary changes. = 他们会在进行必要的修改后重新提交申请。
