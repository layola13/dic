deletion:/dɪˈliːʃən/ | n. | 删除；删节；缺失 | The act of deleting something; the removal of something.  It can refer to the removal of text, data, or other information, or to a missing part or gap in a sequence. | Example: The editor made several deletions to the manuscript. 编辑对稿件做了一些删节。  The deletion of that scene weakened the plot.  删除那个场景削弱了剧情。 |近义词：removal, omission, erasure | 反义词：addition, insertion


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The editor deletes unnecessary words. = 编辑删除不必要的词语。
He deletes his browsing history daily. = 他每天删除他的浏览记录。


一般过去时:
She deleted the email. = 她删除了邮件。
They deleted the entire file. = 他们删除了整个文件。


现在完成时:
I have deleted all the spam emails. = 我已经删除了所有垃圾邮件。
She has deleted the photos from her phone. = 她已经从手机中删除了照片。


过去完成时:
He had deleted the file before the crash. = 电脑崩溃之前他已经删除了文件。
They had deleted the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他们已经销毁了证据。


将来时:
I will delete this file later. = 我稍后会删除这个文件。
She will delete her account tomorrow. = 她明天将注销她的账户。


虚拟语气:
If I had deleted the file, I wouldn't have lost the data. = 如果我删除了文件，我就不会丢失数据了。
I wish I hadn't deleted those photos. = 我真希望我没有删除那些照片。


被动语态:
The file was deleted accidentally. = 文件被意外删除了。
The message was deleted by the system. = 消息被系统删除了。


疑问句:
Have you deleted the document? = 你删除了文件吗？
Was the data deleted? = 数据被删除了吗？


否定句:
I didn't delete the file. = 我没有删除文件。
She hasn't deleted the message yet. = 她还没删除消息。
