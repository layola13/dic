child-proofing: /ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːfɪŋ/ | n. |儿童防护；防止儿童伤害| The process of making a home or environment safe for children by removing or securing potential hazards.  It involves identifying and eliminating dangers that could cause injury or harm to a child. | Example:  We're doing some child-proofing before the baby arrives. 我们在宝宝到来之前正在进行一些儿童防护措施。|近义词：baby-proofing, child safety, hazard prevention |反义词：child endangerment
