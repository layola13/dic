come to grips with: /kʌm tuː ɡrɪps wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 掌握；应付；理解；接受现实 | To understand and deal with a difficult situation or problem.  |例句：It took him a long time to come to grips with his father's death. 他花了很长时间才接受父亲去世的事实。


例句：She's finally come to grips with the challenges of motherhood. 她终于应付得了为人母的挑战。


近义词：grasp, understand, confront, address, reconcile oneself to


反义词：ignore, avoid, evade, neglect


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They come to grips with the new software every day. = 他们每天都在努力掌握新软件。
We come to grips with the problem by brainstorming solutions. = 我们通过集思广益来解决这个问题。


一般过去时:
She came to grips with her fear of public speaking after years of practice. = 她经过多年的练习后终于克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。
He came to grips with the financial crisis by cutting expenses. = 他通过削减开支来应对金融危机。


现在完成时:
They have come to grips with the complexities of the project. = 他们已经掌握了这个项目的复杂性。
We have come to grips with the loss of our pet. = 我们已经接受了失去宠物的事实。


过去完成时:
By the time the deadline arrived, they had come to grips with the technical difficulties. = 到截止日期到来的时候，他们已经解决了技术难题。
She had come to grips with her grief before she could move on with her life. = 在她能够继续生活之前，她已经克服了悲伤。


将来时:
They will come to grips with the situation once they have all the facts. = 一旦掌握了所有事实，他们就会应付这种情况。
We will come to grips with the issue after further investigation. = 我们将在进一步调查后解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If she came to grips with her anxiety, she would be more confident. = 如果她能克服焦虑，她就会更有自信。
I wish he had come to grips with his addiction sooner. = 我希望他早点克服他的瘾。


被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Have they come to grips with the challenges yet? = 他们已经克服这些挑战了吗？
Will she be able to come to grips with the pressure? = 她能够承受压力吗？


否定句:
He hasn't come to grips with his responsibilities yet. = 他还没有承担起他的责任。
They didn't come to grips with the problem effectively. = 他们没有有效地解决这个问题。


PHRASE->

come to grips with a problem = 解决问题
