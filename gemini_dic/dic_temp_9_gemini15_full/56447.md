dolmens:/ˈdəʊlmənz/| n. |巨石墓；多尔门|A dolmen is a prehistoric structure consisting of a large flat stone supported by upright stones, forming a kind of chamber, often used as a tomb.  Dolmens are megalithic structures, meaning they are made of large stones.  They are found in many parts of the world and represent a significant aspect of prehistoric culture and burial practices.|Example: The ancient dolmens of Newgrange are a testament to the engineering skills of Neolithic people.  古代纽格兰奇的巨石墓证明了新石器时代人们的工程技术。|近义词：megalithic tomb, stone chamber|反义词：None
