cookhouses:/ˈkʊkˌhaʊzɪz/| n.|伙房；食堂|A cookhouse is a place where food is cooked and served, often in a communal setting such as a military base, a work camp, or a large institution.  It's typically a large kitchen facility designed to prepare meals for many people.|Example: The soldiers ate their meals in the army cookhouse.  士兵们在军队伙房吃饭。 The workers at the construction site relied on the cookhouse for their daily meals. 建筑工地上的工人依靠伙房提供他们的日常伙食。|近义词：kitchen, cafeteria, mess hall|反义词：private dining room
