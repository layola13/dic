sickish:/ˈsɪkɪʃ/| adj.|有点不舒服的，略感不适的，令人作呕的|Slightly ill; feeling slightly unwell; nauseating.  The adjective "sickish" suggests a mild or temporary state of illness, often accompanied by feelings of nausea or discomfort. It's not as severe as being truly sick.  |例句：I felt a bit sickish after eating that dodgy kebab.  我吃了那个不太新鲜的烤肉串后感觉有点恶心。

He looked pale and sickish. 他脸色苍白，看起来有点不舒服。|近义词：nauseous, queasy, slightly ill, unwell|反义词：healthy, well, robust
