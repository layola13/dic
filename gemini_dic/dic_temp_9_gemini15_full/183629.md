reran:/ˌriːˈræn/| v.|重播，重演|To run (a television program, etc.) again.|The movie was rerunning on cable last night.  昨晚有线电视重播了这部电影。|They decided to reran the experiment. 他们决定重做实验。|rebroadcast, replay|—|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reran the advertisement. = 他们重播了广告。
We reran the test. = 我们重新进行了测试。

一般过去时:
The station reraned the popular show. = 电台重播了那部受欢迎的节目。
The athlete reraned the race. = 运动员重新跑了比赛。

现在完成时:
They have reraned the documentary several times. = 他们已经重播了好几次纪录片了。
We have reraned the experiment with different parameters. = 我们已经用不同的参数重新进行了实验。


过去完成时:
They had reraned the game before the final decision. = 他们在做出最终决定之前已经重播了比赛。
She had reraned the entire presentation before showing it. = 她展示之前已经把整个演示文稿重新排练了一遍。

将来时:
They will reran the program next week. = 他们下周将重播该节目。
We will reran the simulation after correcting the error. = 我们纠正错误后将重新运行模拟。

I wish they had reraned the experiment. = 我希望他们重做实验。
