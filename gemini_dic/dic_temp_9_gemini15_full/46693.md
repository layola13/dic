damsels:/ˈdæmzəlz/| n.|少女；贵妇；（尤指）处于困境的少女|A young woman, especially a young unmarried woman of noble birth;  often used in a context of being in distress.|例句：The damsel was rescued from the burning building.  这位少女从燃烧的建筑物中获救了。|例句：In the fairy tale, a handsome prince rescued the damsel in distress. 在童话故事中，一位英俊的王子救出了陷入困境的少女。|近义词：maiden, young woman, lass|反义词：old woman, crone
