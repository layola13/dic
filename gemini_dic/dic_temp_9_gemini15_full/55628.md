ditation:/daɪˈteɪʃən/| n.| 听写；口述；默写|The act of dictating or writing from dictation.  It refers to the process of writing down words spoken by someone else.  This can be done for various purposes, such as taking notes in a meeting, recording a speech, or completing a dictation exercise.|例句：The teacher gave us a dictation on irregular verbs.老师让我们默写不规则动词。|例句：The lawyer dictated the letter to her secretary. 律师向她的秘书口述信件。|近义词：transcription, writing, recording|反义词：reading aloud, speaking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dictates his letters to his secretary. = 他向秘书口述信件。
She dictates the minutes of the meeting. = 她口述会议记录。


一般过去时:
