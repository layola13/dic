preconcealment: /ˌpriːkənˈsiːlmənt/ | n. | 预先隐藏；预先掩盖 | The act of concealing something beforehand; the state of being concealed beforehand.  It often implies a deliberate attempt to hide something before it is discovered or revealed. | The preconcealment of evidence hampered the investigation.  证据的预先隐藏妨碍了调查。 The artist's preconcealment of his true intentions was masterful. 艺术家巧妙地预先隐藏了他真正的意图。 | concealment, hiding, masking | revelation, disclosure | 名词
