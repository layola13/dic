permissionless:/pərˈmɪʃən ləs/| adj. |无需许可的，无须授权的 |Without requiring permission or authorization.  It describes systems, actions, or processes that operate without needing explicit approval or consent from a central authority or governing body.  |Example:  The permissionless nature of blockchain technology allows for decentralized and transparent transactions.  无需许可的区块链技术允许去中心化和透明的交易。|近义词：unlicensed, unauthorized, open |反义词：permissioned, authorized, regulated
