longbow:/ˈlɒŋˌboʊ/ /ˈlɔːŋˌboʊ/| n.|长弓|A longbow is a long, powerful bow used for hunting and warfare, typically made of wood and strung with a bowstring.  It was a particularly important weapon in medieval England.|Example: The English longbowmen were renowned for their skill and accuracy during the Hundred Years' War.  英格兰长弓手以其在百年战争中的技艺和精准而闻名。|近义词：bow (general term), archer's bow|反义词：crossbow (different type of bow)|名词
