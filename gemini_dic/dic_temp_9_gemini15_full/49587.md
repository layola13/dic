demerit's:/ˈdiːmɛrɪt/| n.|缺点；过失；劣迹| a fault or failing; a mark against someone's record.  The plural form indicates multiple shortcomings or flaws.|例句：His demerits far outweigh his merits. 他的缺点远大于优点。

例句：The student's demerits included lateness and poor work. 这个学生的缺点包括迟到和工作质量差。


近义词：shortcoming, fault, flaw, defect, blemish

反义词：merit, advantage, virtue, strength


词性：名词 (复数)
