pneumography:/nuːˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |肺描记法，肺图描记术 | The recording of respiratory movements by means of an instrument.  It is a technique used to measure and record the movements of the chest and abdomen during breathing.  | Example:  Pneumography is a useful tool in diagnosing respiratory disorders. = 肺描记法是诊断呼吸系统疾病的有用工具。 The pneumography results showed irregular breathing patterns. = 肺描记术结果显示呼吸模式不规则。| 近义词:respiratory monitoring, respiratory pattern analysis | 反义词: N/A
