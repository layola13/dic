explorability:/ˌɛksploʊrəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可探索性 | The degree to which a system or environment can be explored and understood.  It refers to the ease with which one can discover information, navigate, and understand the system's structure and functionality.  High explorability implies a system that is easy to learn and use intuitively. | Example: The game's high explorability encouraged players to discover hidden areas and secrets. 游戏的高可探索性鼓励玩家发现隐藏区域和秘密。 |近义词：discoverability, navigability, accessibility | 反义词：inaccessibility, opacity, impenetrability
