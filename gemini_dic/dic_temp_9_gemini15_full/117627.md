megasse:/məˈɡæs/| n.|甘蔗渣，蔗渣|The fibrous residue remaining after the extraction of juice from sugarcane stalks.  It is a byproduct of sugar production and can be used as fuel or in various industrial applications.|例句：The megasse from the sugar mill is used to generate electricity. 甘蔗糖厂的甘蔗渣被用来发电。|例句：Farmers often burn megasse in their fields, which leads to air pollution. 农民经常在田里焚烧甘蔗渣，这会导致空气污染。|近义词：bagasse|反义词：sugarcane juice|名词
