policed:/pəˈliːst/| adj. | 受管制的；被警察管辖的 |  Maintained order by police; subject to police control.  |例句：The streets were heavily policed during the demonstration.  示威游行期间，街道戒备森严。
例句：The area is well policed and considered safe.  这个地区治安良好，被认为很安全。|近义词：patrolled, guarded, controlled |反义词：uncontrolled, lawless, unregulated
