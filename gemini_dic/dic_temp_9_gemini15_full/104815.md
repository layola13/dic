kebobs:/kəˈbɒbz/| n. |烤肉串；肉串 |Grilled or roasted pieces of meat on a skewer.  Kebabs are a popular dish in many cultures, often featuring lamb, beef, chicken, or vegetables. |例句：We had delicious kebabs at the street food market. 我们在街头小吃市场吃了美味的烤肉串。

例句：The restaurant is famous for its flavorful chicken kebabs. 这家餐馆以其美味的鸡肉串而闻名。


近义词：shish kebab, skewer


反义词：无


词性：名词
