/ˈsɛksæŋɡəl/ | n. |六角形 | A six-sided polygon.  A sexangle is a plane figure with six sides and six angles.  It can be regular, meaning all its sides and angles are equal, or irregular, with varying side and angle lengths. | Example: A regular sexangle, also known as a hexagon, is often found in nature in the form of honeycomb cells.  一个正六边形，也被称为六边形，经常以蜂巢细胞的形式出现在自然界中。|近义词：hexagon |反义词：triangle, square, pentagon (depending on context)
