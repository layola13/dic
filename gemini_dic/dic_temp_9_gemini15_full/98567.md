institutes:/ˈɪnstɪtʃuːts/ | n. |协会；机构；学院；制度；制定；建立 |  An institute is an organization founded for a specific purpose, typically a scientific, educational, or professional one.  It can also refer to the act of establishing or introducing something, such as a system or law. |例句：The research institute is renowned for its groundbreaking work in cancer research.  这个研究所以其在癌症研究方面的开创性工作而闻名。

The government institutes new policies to improve the economy. 政府实施新的政策以改善经济。|近义词：organization, establishment, foundation, society, academy |反义词：destruction, abolishment

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The university institutes new programs every year. = 这所大学每年都开设新课程。
Many countries institute strict immigration policies. = 许多国家都制定了严格的移民政策。


一般过去时:
They instituted a new system of grading. = 他们实行了一种新的评分制度。
The company instituted a new policy on employee conduct. = 公司制定了关于员工行为的新政策。


现在完成时:
The government has instituted several reforms in the education system. = 政府已经在教育体系中实施了几项改革。
They have instituted new safety measures following the accident. = 事故发生后，他们实施了新的安全措施。


过去完成时:
Before the war, they had instituted a strict rationing system. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经实行了一种严格的配给制度。
The company had instituted a new dress code before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经实施了新的着装规范。


将来时:
The school will institute a new language program next semester. = 学校下学期将开设一个新的语言课程。
The government will institute stricter environmental regulations. = 政府将实施更严格的环境法规。


虚拟语气:
If they instituted a better system, the results would be better. = 如果他们实行一个更好的制度，结果将会更好。
I wish they had instituted these changes sooner. = 我希望他们早点实施这些改变。


被动语态:
New regulations were instituted by the government last year. = 新的法规是政府去年制定的。
A new system of taxation will be instituted next year. = 明年将实行新的税收制度。


疑问句:
What measures did the company institute to improve productivity? = 公司采取了什么措施来提高生产力？
Will the government institute any new laws to address climate change? = 政府会制定任何新的法律来应对气候变化吗？


否定句:
The company did not institute any changes to its hiring policy. = 公司没有对其招聘政策进行任何更改。
They did not institute the new rules until the following week. = 直到下周他们才实施新规。
