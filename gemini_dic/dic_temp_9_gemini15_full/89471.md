honeycombing:/ˈhʌnikəʊmiŋ/ | v. |蜂窝状结构；充满蜂窝状孔洞 |To form or fill with a network of interconnected cavities resembling a honeycomb.  The process often involves erosion, decay, or the creation of many small holes. |例句：The rock was honeycombed with caves.  这块岩石布满了蜂窝状的洞穴。The old tree trunk was honeycombed with insects.  这棵老树干被昆虫蛀蚀得千疮百孔。|近义词：perforate, riddle, pockmark |反义词：solidify, compact
