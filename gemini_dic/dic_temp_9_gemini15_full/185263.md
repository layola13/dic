retranslate:/ˌriːˈtrænzleɪt/ | vt. |重新翻译 |To translate something again, often from one language into another, or from one form of expression into another.| 例句：The document needs to be retranslated into English. 这份文件需要重新翻译成英文。  例句：The poem was retranslated with a focus on its imagery. 这首诗被重新翻译，重点放在意象上。 |近义词：re-render, reinterpret|反义词：translate (in the original sense)
