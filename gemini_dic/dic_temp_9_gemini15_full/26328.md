buzz-along:/bʌz əˈlɒŋ/| v. |嗡嗡地飞；快速前进；匆忙行动 |To move quickly and noisily, like a bee.  Often implies a sense of urgency or busyness. |例句：The bees were buzzing along in the garden. 蜜蜂在花园里嗡嗡地飞着。

The helicopter buzzed along towards the disaster site. 直升机嗡嗡地朝灾难现场飞去。

近义词：whizz, zoom, hurtle, speed |反义词：crawl, dawdle, meander
