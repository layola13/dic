lottos:/ˈlɒtoʊz/| n. |彩票（复数）|Lottos are games of chance where people buy tickets with numbers, and if their numbers match the winning numbers drawn, they win a prize.  The plural form of "lotto".|例句：He bought several lottos, hoping to win big. 他买了多张彩票，希望能赢得大奖。

例句：The lottos were sold out before noon. 彩票在中午之前就卖光了。


近义词：lottery tickets, raffle tickets | 反义词：无
