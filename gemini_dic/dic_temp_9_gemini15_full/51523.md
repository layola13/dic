detrituses:/dɪˈtraɪtəsɪz/| n. |碎屑；残渣；瓦砾 |Detritus is the product of the breakdown of organic matter. It is composed of various materials, including decaying plants and animals, minerals, and other inorganic substances.  Detritus plays a crucial role in many ecosystems, serving as a food source for various organisms.  The accumulation of detritus can form layers of sediment.|The forest floor was covered in a thick layer of detritus. 森林地面覆盖着一层厚厚的碎屑。The riverbed was filled with detritus from the recent flood. 河床充满了最近洪水带来的碎屑。|debris, rubble, sediment|  (近义词缺乏精确的反义词，可以考虑“整洁的物质”, "intact materials")|名词
