prosoma:/ˈproʊsoʊmə/| n. |前体,前体节|The prosoma is the anterior part of the body of arachnids, such as spiders, scorpions, and mites, which includes the cephalothorax. It bears the eyes, mouthparts, and legs.|例句：The spider's prosoma was covered in fine hairs.  这只蜘蛛的前体覆盖着细毛。|例句：Scientists studied the prosoma of various arachnid species to understand their evolutionary relationships. 科学家研究了各种蛛形纲动物的前体，以了解它们的进化关系。|近义词：cephalothorax|反义词：opisthosoma
