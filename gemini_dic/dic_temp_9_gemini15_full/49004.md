degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| n. |退化；降级；恶化 |The process of being reduced in rank or status; the deterioration of something into a lower or less desirable state.  It can refer to a decline in quality, value, or moral character. |例句：The degradation of the environment is a serious concern. 环境恶化是一个严重的问题。

例句：The degradation of his social status caused him much pain. 他社会地位的下降给他带来了很大的痛苦。


近义词： deterioration, decline, decay, degeneration, debasement |反义词： improvement, enhancement, elevation, advancement |词性：名词
