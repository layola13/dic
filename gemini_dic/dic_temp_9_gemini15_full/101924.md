islanded:/ˈaɪləndɪd/ | adj. |孤立的，与世隔绝的；像岛屿一样分散的 |  Surrounded by water or otherwise isolated; separated, like islands.  | 例句：The house felt islanded, far from the main road and any neighbors. 这房子感觉很孤立，远离主路和任何邻居。

The small community felt islanded after the bridge collapsed.  桥梁倒塌后，这个小社区感觉与世隔绝了。| 近义词：isolated, secluded, detached, remote | 反义词：connected, integrated, central
