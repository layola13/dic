pre-Linnaean: /ˌpriːlɪˈniːən/ | adj. | 林奈前的；林奈之前的 | Relating to the period of biological classification before the system of Carl Linnaeus (1707-1778).  This period was characterized by less rigorous and more inconsistent systems of naming and classifying organisms. | Example: Pre-Linnaean taxonomy relied heavily on observable characteristics and lacked a standardized binomial nomenclature. 林奈前的分类法严重依赖可观察的特征，并且缺乏标准化的二名法。 | 近义词:  pre-systematic | 反义词: Linnaean, post-Linnaean | 词性:形容词
