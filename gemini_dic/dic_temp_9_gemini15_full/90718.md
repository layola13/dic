humidities:/hjuːˈmɪdətiz/| n. |湿度(复数)|The state or quality of being humid; dampness.  It refers to different levels of moisture in the air.  The plural form indicates multiple instances or measurements of humidity.|例句：The humidities in the rainforest are extremely high.  热带雨林的湿度非常高。

The humidities varied significantly throughout the day.  一天中湿度变化很大。|近义词：dampness, moistness|反义词：dryness, aridity|
