knowingly:/ˈnoʊɪŋli/ | adv. |故意地；明知故犯地 |  Knowingly implies that someone is aware of the implications or consequences of their actions and chooses to proceed regardless. It suggests a deliberate act, often with negative connotations. | Example: He knowingly broke the law. 他明知故犯地违反了法律。


Example: She knowingly lied to the police. 她故意向警方撒谎。


近义词：intentionally, deliberately, purposely, consciously


反义词：unintentionally, accidentally, inadvertently, unconsciously


词性: 副词
