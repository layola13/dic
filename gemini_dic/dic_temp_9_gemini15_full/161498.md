potential:/pəˈtɛnʃəl/ | n. adj. |潜力；可能性；潜能；潜在的；可能的 |

英文解析：The word "potential" refers to the inherent capacity or ability of something to develop or achieve a certain result.  It can refer to a possibility that may or may not be realized. As an adjective, it describes something that has the capacity to develop or become something. As a noun, it refers to this capacity itself.


例句：

He has the potential to become a great musician.  = 他有潜力成为一名伟大的音乐家。

The potential benefits of this new technology are enormous. = 这项新技术的潜在效益是巨大的。


近义词：possibility, capacity, capability, promise, prospect


反义词：impossibility, inability, incapacity


词性：noun, adjective
