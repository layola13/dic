postnecrotic: /ˌpoʊstnɪˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 坏死后的，坏死之后的 | Relating to or denoting the stage following necrosis (death of tissue).  It often describes the changes and consequences that occur after tissue death.  The term is commonly used in medicine, particularly in the context of liver disease. | 例句：Postnecrotic cirrhosis is a serious complication of liver damage. 坏死后肝硬化是肝损伤的严重并发症。


The patient presented with postnecrotic scarring on the lung. 病人出现了肺部坏死后瘢痕。


近义词：post-mortem (in some contexts), sequelae (referring to consequences) | 反义词：prencrotic (not a commonly used term) | 词性：形容词
