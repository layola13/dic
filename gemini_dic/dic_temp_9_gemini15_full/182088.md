remind:/rɪˈmaɪnd/ | vt. |提醒，使想起 | To bring (something) back to someone's memory or attention; to cause someone to remember something.  | 例句：Please remind me to call my mother.  请提醒我给妈妈打电话。
例句：The smell of baking bread reminded her of her childhood. 烤面包的味道让她想起了童年。 |近义词：recall, remember, prompt |反义词：forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reminds me to take my medicine every day. 他每天提醒我吃药。
I remind my students of the deadline every week. 我每周都提醒学生截止日期。


一般过去时:
She reminded him of their anniversary. 她提醒他他们的周年纪念日。
The teacher reminded the students to study hard for the exam. 老师提醒学生们要努力学习考试。


现在完成时:
I have reminded him several times, but he still forgot. 我已经提醒他好几次了，但他还是忘了。
Have you reminded her about the meeting? 你提醒她开会了吗？


过去完成时:
I had reminded her before, but she still forgot. 我之前提醒过她，但她还是忘了。
He had reminded me earlier, so I didn’t miss the appointment. 他之前提醒过我，所以我没有错过约会。


将来时:
I will remind you when it's time to leave. 到时间离开时我会提醒你。
She will remind him about the project tomorrow. 她明天会提醒他关于这个项目的事。


虚拟语气:
I wish you would remind me more often. 我希望你能更经常地提醒我。
If only she had reminded me earlier! 要是她早点提醒我该多好！


被动语态:
He was reminded of his promise. 他被提醒了他的承诺。
We were reminded to submit our reports by Friday. 我们被提醒要在星期五之前提交报告。


疑问句:
Does this remind you of anything? 这让你想起什么了吗？
Will you remind me again later? 你稍后再提醒我一次好吗？


否定句:
I didn’t remind him because I thought he already knew. 我没有提醒他，因为我认为他已经知道了。
She didn’t remind me about the deadline. 她没有提醒我截止日期。


PHRASE->
remind sb of sth：使某人想起某事
remind sb to do sth：提醒某人做某事
a constant reminder：不断的提醒
a gentle reminder：委婉的提醒
a timely reminder：及时的提醒
