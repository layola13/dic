fadeless:/ˈfeɪdɪləs/| adj. |不褪色的；永不衰退的；永恒的 |Not losing freshness or intensity of color; enduring; unchanging.  The word implies a resistance to fading, whether it's the fading of color, the fading of memory, or the fading of influence. |例句：The fadeless beauty of the mountain scenery captivated us.  山景的永恒美丽迷住了我们。

例句：Her fadeless love for her family was a constant source of strength. 她对家人的永恒的爱是她力量的源泉。


近义词：everlasting, imperishable, unfading, permanent

反义词：fading, ephemeral, transient

词性：形容词
