pick on:/pɪk ˈɒn/ | vt. | 欺负，挑剔，吹毛求疵 | To repeatedly criticize, tease, or annoy someone.  This often implies a power imbalance, where the person "picking on" is stronger or more dominant. |例句：The older boys were picking on the smaller children.  较大的男孩们在欺负较小的孩子们。

例句：She's always picking on my grammar. 她总是挑剔我的语法。


近义词：tease, bully, harass, torment, criticize | 反义词：defend, protect, support | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pick on him every day. = 他们每天都欺负他。
She picks on my spelling mistakes. = 她总是挑剔我的拼写错误。


一般过去时:
The teacher picked on me in class. = 老师在课堂上批评了我。
They picked on him because he was different. = 他们欺负他，因为他与众不同。


现在完成时:
They have been picking on me all week. = 他们整个星期都在欺负我。
She has constantly picked on my work. = 她一直在挑剔我的工作。


过去完成时:
He had been picked on for years before he finally stood up for himself. = 在他最终为自己挺身而出之前，他已经受了好几年的欺负了。
The bully had picked on many students before. = 那个恶霸以前欺负过很多学生。


将来时:
I think they will pick on him again. = 我认为他们还会再欺负他。
She will probably pick on my presentation. = 她可能会挑剔我的演示文稿。


虚拟语气:
If they picked on me, I would tell the teacher. = 如果他们欺负我，我会告诉老师。
I wish they wouldn't pick on him so much. = 我希望他们不要再那么欺负他了。


被动语态:
He was picked on mercilessly. = 他被无情地欺负了。
My work is often picked on by my boss. = 我的工作经常被老板挑剔。


疑问句:
Are they picking on you? = 他们在欺负你吗？

否定句:

She didn't pick on me today. = 她今天没有欺负我。
