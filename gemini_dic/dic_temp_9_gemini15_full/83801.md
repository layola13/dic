hairs:/hɛrz/ | n. | 头发；毛发 |  The plural of "hair," referring to multiple strands of hair on a person's head, body, or an animal's body. It can also refer to fine filaments or thread-like structures. | 例句：Her hairs were long and flowing.  她的头发又长又飘逸。

The cat's hairs were matted with dirt. 这猫的毛脏兮兮的，纠结在一起。


近义词：strands (of hair), locks (of hair) | 反义词：baldness (lack of hair) | 词性：名词
