prevents:/prɪˈvɛnts/| vt.|阻止；防止|To stop something from happening or someone from doing something.|例句：The rain prevented us from going to the park.  雨阻止了我们去公园。|例句：Regulations prevent companies from polluting the river. 法规阻止公司污染河流。|近义词：stop, hinder, impede, obstruct|反义词：allow, permit, enable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prevents accidents by driving carefully. = 他小心驾驶以防止事故。
The law prevents discrimination based on race. = 法律禁止基于种族的歧视。


一般过去时:
The heavy snow prevented the flight from taking off. = 大雪阻止了飞机起飞。
A sudden illness prevented him from attending the meeting. = 突发疾病阻止了他参加会议。


现在完成时:
The new security system has prevented several break-ins. = 新的安全系统已经阻止了几次闯入。
She has prevented many problems through careful planning. = 她通过细致的计划避免了许多问题。


过去完成时:
A flat tire had prevented them from arriving on time. = 漏气的轮胎阻止了他们准时到达。
He had prevented a major crisis through his quick thinking. = 他凭借他的快速思维避免了一场重大危机。


将来时:
The new regulations will prevent further environmental damage. = 新的法规将防止进一步的环境破坏。
We hope the vaccine will prevent the spread of the disease. = 我们希望疫苗能够阻止疾病的传播。


虚拟语气:
If only I had prevented the argument, things would be different. = 如果我能阻止这场争论就好了，事情会不一样。
I wish I could prevent him from making that mistake. = 我希望我能阻止他犯那个错误。


被动语态:
The spread of the virus was prevented by strict quarantine measures. = 病毒的传播被严格的隔离措施阻止了。
Further damage to the building was prevented by the firefighters. = 消防员阻止了对建筑物的进一步破坏。


疑问句:
What can be done to prevent this from happening again? = 可以做些什么来防止这种情况再次发生？
What measures are in place to prevent fraud? = 有哪些措施可以防止欺诈？


否定句:
Nothing could prevent her from achieving her goals. = 没有任何事情能够阻止她实现她的目标。
He failed to prevent the accident from happening. = 他没能阻止事故的发生。


PHRASE->
prevent from = 阻止...
prevent...from... = 阻止...做...
prevent the spread of = 阻止...的传播
prevent accidents = 预防事故
prevent crime = 预防犯罪
