homiletic:/ˌhɒmɪˈlɛtɪk/| adj. |说教的；讲道的；劝诫的 | Relating to or characteristic of a homily; preachy.  The sermon was highly homiletic, filled with moralizing pronouncements.  | 近义词：didactic, preachy, moralistic | 反义词：unmoralistic, secular


The word "homiletic" is derived from the Greek word "homilia," meaning "sermon" or "conversation."  It ultimately stems from the verb "homilein," meaning "to converse," "to speak in public," or "to preach."
