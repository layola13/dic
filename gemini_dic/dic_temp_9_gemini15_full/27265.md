/kælk/ | n. | 仿译词；卡尔克；照译 | A calque is a word or phrase borrowed from another language by literal, word-for-word translation.  It often sounds unnatural in the borrowing language. |The word "skyscraper" is a calque from the German "Wolkenkratzer".|近义词：loan translation, literal translation |反义词：None specifically, but contrasts with naturalized loanwords. |名词
