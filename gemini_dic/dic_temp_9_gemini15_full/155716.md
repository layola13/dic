phoenixes:/ˈfiːnɪksɪz/| n. |凤凰（复数）|The plural of phoenix, a mythical bird that cyclically regenerates or is otherwise born again.  Associates with immortality, rebirth, and resurrection.|例句：Legends speak of phoenixes rising from ashes. 传说中凤凰浴火重生。|例句：Several beautiful phoenixes adorned the tapestry.  几只美丽的凤凰装饰着挂毯。|近义词： mythical birds, immortal birds |反义词：mortal creatures
