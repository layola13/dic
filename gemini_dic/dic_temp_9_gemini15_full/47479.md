deals:/diːlz/ | n., v. |交易；协议；处理；应付 |英文解析：As a noun, "deals" refers to an agreement or arrangement, often involving business or commerce.  As a verb, it means to buy, sell, or trade something; to distribute or give out something; or to handle or cope with a situation. |例句：He made a great deal on the car. 他以很划算的价格买了这辆车。(n.)

The company deals with customer complaints efficiently. 这家公司有效地处理客户投诉。(v.)


近义词：(n.) agreement, transaction, bargain, contract; (v.) handles, manages, copes with, distributes


反义词：(n.) disagreement, conflict; (v.) neglects, ignores


词性:名词，动词
