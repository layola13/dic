carbonized:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzd/ | adj. | 碳化的；变成炭的 |  Turned into carbon or charcoal; charred.  The process involves heating organic matter in the absence of oxygen, resulting in a black, brittle substance. | 例句：The wood was carbonized by the intense heat of the fire.  /  木材被大火烧成了炭。 The ancient artifacts were remarkably well-preserved, though partially carbonized. / 尽管部分碳化，古代文物保存得非常好。| 近义词：charred, charred, coked | 反义词：unburned, raw
