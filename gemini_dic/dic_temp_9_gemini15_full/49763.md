demolition:/ˌdɛməˈlɪʃən/| n. |拆除；破坏；摧毁 |The act or process of demolishing something, especially a building.  It often involves the systematic destruction of a structure. |例句：The demolition of the old factory made way for a new shopping mall. 老工厂的拆除为新的购物中心腾出了空间。

例句：The building was beyond repair, so demolition was the only option. 这栋建筑物无法修复，所以拆除是唯一的选择。


近义词：destruction, wrecking, razing |反义词：construction, building, erection |词性:名词
