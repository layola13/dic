groomer:/ˈɡruːmər/ | n. | 梳毛者；打扮者；性侵儿童者 | A person who grooms animals, especially horses, by brushing and cleaning their coats.  Also, a person who sexually abuses or exploits a child, often through manipulation and seduction. | Example:  The groomer meticulously brushed the horse's mane.  |近义词: (for animals)  cleaner,  (for children) abuser, predator | 反义词: (no direct antonym)
