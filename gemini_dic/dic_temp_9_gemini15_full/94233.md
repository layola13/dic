imperil:/ɪmˈpɛrəl/| vt.|危及；使陷入危险|To put someone or something in danger.|例句：The storm imperiled the small fishing boats.  暴风雨危及了小型渔船。|例句：His reckless driving imperiled not only himself but also others. 他鲁莽的驾驶不仅危及他自己，也危及他人。|近义词：endanger, jeopardize, threaten|反义词：protect, safeguard, secure|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The reckless driver imperils others on the road. = 鲁莽的司机危及路上的其他人。
This policy imperils the future of the company. = 这项政策危及公司的未来。


一般过去时:
The sudden storm imperiled the hikers. = 突如其来的暴风雨危及了徒步旅行者。
His careless actions imperiled the whole project. = 他粗心大意的行为危及了整个项目。


现在完成时:
The ongoing drought has imperiled the crops. = 持续的干旱已经危及了庄稼。
Their irresponsible behavior has imperiled the environment. = 他们不负责任的行为已经危及了环境。


过去完成时:
The faulty brakes had imperiled the passengers before the accident. = 事故发生前，有故障的刹车已经危及了乘客。
The company's poor management had imperiled its financial stability. = 公司糟糕的管理已经危及了它的财务稳定。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will imperil coastal communities. = 海平面上升将危及沿海社区。
The proposed changes will imperil the delicate ecosystem. = 提出的改变将危及脆弱的生态系统。


虚拟语气:
If we continue this way, we would imperil our health. = 如果我们继续这样下去，我们会危及我们的健康。
I wish the government hadn't implemented policies that imperil the economy.  我希望政府没有实施危及经济的政策。


被动语态:
The village was imperiled by the flood. = 村庄被洪水危及。
The project is imperiled by a lack of funding. = 项目因缺乏资金而受到威胁。


疑问句:
Does this action imperil our safety? = 这一行为是否危及我们的安全？
Does the current situation imperil the peace talks? = 目前的情况是否危及和平谈判？


否定句:
The new safety regulations will not imperil workers. = 新的安全条例不会危及工人。
The company's strong financial position means it is not imperiled by the recession. = 公司强大的财务状况意味着它不会受到经济衰退的威胁。
