fascinating: /ˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |迷人的，极有吸引力的 | Extremely interesting and attractive.  |例句：The museum's exhibits were fascinating. 博物馆的展品非常迷人。

例句：I found her story fascinating. 我发现她的故事非常迷人。


近义词： captivating, enthralling, intriguing, absorbing, compelling

反义词：boring, dull, uninteresting, tedious, monotonous

词源：源自动词fascinate，最终源于拉丁语 *fascinare，意为“施魔法，迷惑”。  The word's evolution reflects its connection to the power of something captivating to hold one's attention completely.
