extrapolator: /ɪkˈstræpəleɪtər/ | n. | 推断器，外推器 | A person or thing that extrapolates; a device or program that estimates values beyond the range of known data.  | Example: The scientist used a sophisticated extrapolator to predict future climate change based on current trends.  科学家使用复杂的推断器根据当前趋势预测未来的气候变化。|近义词：predictor, estimator, projector | 反义词：interpolator (in some contexts)
