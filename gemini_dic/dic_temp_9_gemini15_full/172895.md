quails:/kweɪlz/| n.|鹌鹑；胆怯|The word "quails" refers to the small, game birds known for their delicate meat and distinctive call.  It can also be used figuratively to mean to lose courage or become disheartened. |例句：We had roasted quails for dinner.  我们晚餐吃烤鹌鹑。  He quailed at the sight of the snake. 他看到蛇就胆怯了。|近义词：cowers, flinches, shrinks|反义词：braves, dares, faces
