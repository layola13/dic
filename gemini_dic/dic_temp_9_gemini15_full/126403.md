myself: /maɪˈsɛlf/ | pron. |我自己，我本人 | Myself is a reflexive pronoun, meaning it refers back to the subject of the sentence.  It is used to emphasize the subject's action or to indicate that the subject performed the action on themselves. |


例句：I hurt myself playing soccer. = 我踢足球时弄伤了自己。

例句：I made the cake myself. = 我自己做的蛋糕。


近义词：me (in some contexts)


反义词：无


词性：代词
