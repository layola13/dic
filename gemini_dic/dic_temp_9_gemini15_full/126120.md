mutes:/mjuːts/| n. v.| 哑巴；使沉默；消音 | A mute person is unable to speak.  A mute switch silences a device. |例句：He was born mute. 他生来就是哑巴。  The musician muted the strings. 音乐家把琴弦消音了。|近义词：silences, stills, deaf-mutes |反义词：vocal, articulate

She mutes the television commercials. 她把电视广告静音。
