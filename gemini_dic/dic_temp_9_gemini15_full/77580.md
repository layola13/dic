geckos:/ˈɡɛkoʊz/| n. |壁虎|Geckos are small lizards characterized by their adhesive toe pads, allowing them to climb walls and ceilings. They are found in warm climates worldwide and are known for their diverse appearances and behaviors.  |例句：I saw a gecko clinging to the wall in my kitchen. 我看见一只壁虎粘在我的厨房墙上。

例句：Many species of geckos are nocturnal and have excellent night vision. 许多种类的壁虎是夜行动物，并且拥有极佳的夜视能力。


近义词：lizard (更广义的术语)


反义词：无


词性:名词
