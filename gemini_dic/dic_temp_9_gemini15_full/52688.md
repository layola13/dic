dignifies:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪz/| vt.|使尊贵；使显得庄重；维护…的尊严|To make someone or something seem worthy of respect or honor.  It implies adding to the status or importance of something.|The new building dignifies the city skyline. 新建筑使城市天际线显得更加庄重。|The ceremony dignified the occasion.  仪式使这个场合显得庄严。|exalts, elevates, ennobles|degrades, dishonors, humiliates|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He dignifies his work with great care. 他认真地对待工作，使工作显得更有价值。
The artist dignifies her subjects with sensitive portrayals. 艺术家用细腻的描绘使她的作品更显尊贵。


一般过去时:
The award dignified her years of hard work.  这个奖项肯定了她多年的辛勤工作。
The king dignified the knight with a medal. 国王授予骑士一枚勋章，以此表彰他。


现在完成时:
His actions have dignified his family name. 他的行为维护了他家族的名誉。
Her achievements have dignified the field of science. 她的成就提升了科学领域的声望。


过去完成时:
The ancient rituals had dignified the culture for centuries.  古老的仪式几个世纪以来一直维护着这种文化。
They had dignified the fallen soldiers with a solemn memorial service. 他们为阵亡士兵举行了庄严的追悼会，以此表达敬意。


将来时:
The new park will dignify the neighborhood. 新公园将提升社区的形象。
The upcoming exhibition will dignify the artist's career.  即将举行的展览将提升这位艺术家的职业生涯。


虚拟语气:
If he behaved more responsibly, it would dignify his position. 如果他行为更负责任一些，那会提升他的地位。
I wish the media would dignify the issue with more serious reporting. 我希望媒体能以更严肃的报道来对待这个问题。


被动语态:
The event was dignified by the presence of royalty.  皇室成员的出席使活动增光添彩。
His work was dignified by its meticulous detail. 他的作品因其精细的细节而显得更加尊贵。


疑问句:
Does this action dignify our profession?  这个举动能提升我们行业的形象吗？
Will this decision dignify the company's image?  这个决定会提升公司的形象吗？


否定句:
His behavior did not dignify the occasion. 他的行为没有使这个场合显得庄重。
Their actions did not dignify the cause they claimed to support. 他们的行为并没有提升他们声称支持的事业。
