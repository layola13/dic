shortest: /ˈʃɔːrtɪst/ | adj. | 最短的 |  The shortest route is usually the busiest. 最短的路线通常是最繁忙的。|近义词：briefest, minimum | 反义词：longest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shortest way is always the best. = 最短的路总是最好的。
This is the shortest sentence I can write. = 这是我能写出的最短的句子。

一般过去时:
The shortest film I ever saw lasted only five minutes. = 我看过的最短的电影只持续了五分钟。
That was the shortest day of the year. = 那是一年中最短的一天。

现在完成时:
That has been the shortest vacation I've ever had. = 那是我度过的最短的假期。
This is the shortest route I have ever found. = 这是我找到的最短的路线。

过去完成时:
It was the shortest flight I had ever been on. = 那是我坐过的最短的航班。
Before this, the shortest race I had ever run was 5km. = 在此之前，我跑过的最短的比赛是5公里。

将来时:
The shortest route will be the quickest. = 最短的路线将是最快的。
This will be the shortest meeting we've had all year. = 这将是我们今年开过的最短的会议。

虚拟语气:
If there were a shortest way, I would take it. = 如果有最短的路，我会走。
I wish I knew the shortest way to get there. = 我希望我知道到达那里的最短路线。

被动语态:
The shortest distance was chosen for the new road. = 为新路选择了最短的距离。
The shortest path is always being sought after. = 人们总是寻找最短的路径。

疑问句:
Which is the shortest way to the station? = 去车站哪条路最近？
What is the shortest distance between two points? = 两点之间最短的距离是什么？

否定句:
This isn't the shortest route. = 这不是最短的路线。
The planned route is not the shortest one. = 计划中的路线不是最短的。
