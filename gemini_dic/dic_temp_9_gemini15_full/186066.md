rewritings:/ˌriːˈraɪtɪŋz/ | n. |改写；改写本；重写|The process of writing something again, often to improve it or to make it clearer or more concise.  It can also refer to the resulting text. |例句：The professor requested rewritings of the essays. 教授要求重写论文。

例句： Her rewritings of the historical novel were significantly improved. 她对这部历史小说的改写有了显著的改进。


近义词： revisions, redrafts, rewrites, emendations | 反义词： originals


词性：名词
