overacts:/ˈoʊvərækts/| v.|夸张地表演；反应过度|To exaggerate one's actions or reactions; to act in an excessively dramatic or emotional way.|例句：He overacts in every scene, making the film feel unrealistic. 他在每一个场景中都表演夸张，使电影感觉不真实。
例句：She overreacted to the minor criticism, bursting into tears. 她对小小的批评反应过度，哭了起来。|近义词：exaggerates, overemphasizes|反义词：underacts, underplays
