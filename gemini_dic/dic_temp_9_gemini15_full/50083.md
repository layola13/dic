/ˈdɛnti/ | n. |牙齿；齿状物 | A tooth, especially one of a set of human teeth.  The word often refers to a single tooth, but can also refer to teeth collectively in certain contexts.  It is also used to describe something shaped like a tooth or teeth. | Example: The dentist examined the patient's dente.  牙医检查了病人的牙齿。  The edge of the saw had a dente.  锯子的边缘有齿状物。| 近义词：tooth, cog,  | 反义词：toothless  | 名词
