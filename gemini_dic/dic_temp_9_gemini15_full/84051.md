half-finished:/ˌhɑːfˈfɪnɪʃt/| adj. |未完成的，半成品的 |Partially completed; not yet brought to a state of completion.  |例句：The project is half-finished, and we need more time to complete it.  这个项目只完成了一半，我们需要更多时间来完成它。

例句：He left the painting half-finished. 他把画作留作半成品。


近义词：incomplete, unfinished, partial, uncompleted
反义词：complete, finished, perfected
