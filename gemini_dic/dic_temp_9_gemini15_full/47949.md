decidedly:/dɪˈsaɪdədli/ | adv. |明确地；坚决地；无疑地 |Clearly and without doubt; definitely.  The adverb "decidedly" emphasizes the certainty or firmness of something. It suggests a strong conviction or a clear-cut decision. |例句：He decidedly refused the offer. 他断然拒绝了这个提议。

例句：The evidence decidedly points to his guilt.  证据确凿地表明他有罪。


近义词：definitely, certainly, unquestionably, absolutely, emphatically, plainly

反义词：uncertainly, doubtfully, hesitantly, vaguely


词性: 副词
