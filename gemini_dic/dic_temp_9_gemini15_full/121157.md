misdeveloping:/ˌmɪsdiˈvɛləpɪŋ/| v. |发展不良；发育不良；误导发展|The word "misdeveloping" is a verb formed by adding the prefix "mis-" to the verb "developing."  It implies a process of development that is flawed, incorrect, or leads to undesirable outcomes.  It suggests a deviation from a healthy or expected path of growth or progress.|例句：The company's misdeveloping strategy led to significant financial losses. 公司发展战略失误导致了巨大的经济损失。The child's misdeveloping bone structure required corrective surgery.  孩子的骨骼发育不良需要矫正手术。|近义词：underdeveloping, maldeveloping, hindering, stunting|反义词：developing, flourishing, thriving|动词
