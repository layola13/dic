pessimize:/ˈpɛsɪmaɪz/| vt.|使恶化；使悲观；使处于最坏状态|To make something worse; to make someone feel pessimistic; to reduce something to its worst possible state.|例句：The constant negativity pessimized the team's morale.  持续的消极情绪使团队士气低落。
例句：Don't pessimize the situation; let's find a solution. 不要使情况恶化；让我们找到一个解决方案。|近义词：worsen, deteriorate, depress|反义词：optimize, improve, enhance|动词
