manatees:/ˈmænətiːz/| n. |海牛|Large, slow-moving, aquatic mammals found in warm coastal waters and rivers.  They are herbivores and known for their gentle nature.  Manatees are also called sea cows.|例句：Manatees are gentle giants of the sea. 海牛是海洋中的温柔巨人。
例句：The manatees grazed peacefully on the seagrass. 海牛平静地吃着海草。|近义词：sea cow|反义词:None.
