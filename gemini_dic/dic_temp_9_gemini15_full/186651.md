rigadoon:/ˈrɪɡəduːn/| n. |里戈东舞|A lively, quick-paced dance in 6/8 time, typically performed by a couple. It originated in 17th-century France and became popular throughout Europe.  The dance is characterized by its intricate steps and elegant movements. |The dancers performed a graceful rigo-doon.  舞者跳了一支优雅的里戈东舞。|minuet, gavotte|none|
