nematocera:/nɛmətˈsɛrə/| n. |长角亚目昆虫|Nematocera is a suborder of insects in the order Diptera, characterized by long antennae.  They are typically small to medium-sized flies with slender bodies and long, many-segmented antennae.  Many species are important pollinators or play a role in nutrient cycling.  Some are also vectors of diseases.|Example:  Mosquitoes and crane flies are examples of nematocerans.  蚊子和蚊子是长角亚目昆虫的例子。|近义词: Diptera (order)|反义词: Brachycera (suborder of Diptera)|名词
