defectionist: /dɪˈfɛkʃənɪst/ | n. |变节者；叛逃者 |A person who defects or deserts a cause, country, or group.  Someone who abandons their allegiance or principles. |例句：The defectionist fled to the enemy lines, taking vital information with him.  这个叛逃者逃到了敌人的阵线，带走了重要的情报。

例句：  He was branded a defectionist after he revealed government secrets to the press. 他在向媒体泄露政府机密后被贴上了叛逃者的标签。


近义词：deserter, renegade, traitor, turncoat

反义词：loyalist, patriot


词性：名词
