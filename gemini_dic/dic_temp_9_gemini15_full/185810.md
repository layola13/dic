revisitations:/ˌriːvɪzɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 重访；重新审视；再访 | The act of revisiting something, whether a place, a topic, or a memory. It implies a return to something previously experienced or considered.  |例句：His revisitations to the old house brought back many childhood memories.  他的重访老房子勾起了许多童年的回忆。

例句：The professor's revisitations of the historical data revealed new insights. 教授对历史数据的重新审视揭示了新的见解。


近义词：revisits, reconsiderations, re-examinations | 反义词：first visits, initial considerations


词性: 名词
