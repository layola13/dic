non-benevolent:/ˌnɒnˈbenɪvələnt/| adj. |非善意的，恶意的|Not benevolent; unkind; malicious.  It describes something or someone lacking kindness, goodwill, or generosity; often implying actively harmful or malevolent intent.|The non-benevolent dictator ruled with an iron fist, suppressing dissent and harming his people.|近义词：malicious, malevolent, unkind, cruel, hostile |反义词：benevolent, kind, compassionate, charitable, benign
