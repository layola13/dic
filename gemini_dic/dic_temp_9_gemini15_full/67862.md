fabler:/ˈfeɪblər/| n. |说谎者；编造故事的人；寓言作者|A fabler is someone who tells fables or makes up stories, often untrue ones.  They may be skilled storytellers, but their stories lack truth. | Example:  He was known as a fabler, always spinning tall tales about his adventures. 他以说谎者闻名，总是编造关于他冒险经历的夸大其词的故事。 |近义词：liar, storyteller, mythmaker |反义词：truth-teller, fact-checker |名词
