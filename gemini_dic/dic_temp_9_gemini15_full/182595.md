reoffends:/ˌriːəˈfɛndz/ | v. |再次犯罪；重犯 |To commit a crime again after having been punished for a previous crime.  |例句：He was released from prison, but he reoffended within six months. 他获释出狱，但在六个月内再次犯罪。|例句：After serving his sentence, he reoffended and was sent back to jail. 服刑后，他再次犯罪，被送回监狱。|近义词：repeat, recommit |反义词：reforms, rehabilitates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reoffends frequently. = 他经常再次犯罪。
She reoffends whenever she is stressed. = 她一有压力就再次犯罪。

一般过去时:
He reoffended after only a few weeks. = 他仅仅几周后就再次犯罪了。
She reoffended and was sent back to prison. = 她再次犯罪，被送回监狱。

现在完成时:
He has reoffended several times since his release. = 自从他获释以来，他已经多次再次犯罪。
She has never reoffended after that incident. = 那件事之后，她再也没有犯过罪。

过去完成时:
He had reoffended before the police even knew about his release. = 警察甚至不知道他获释之前，他就已经再次犯罪了。
She had reoffended multiple times, leading to a harsher sentence this time. = 她已经多次再次犯罪，导致这次判决更严厉。

将来时:
He will likely reoffend if he doesn't get help. = 如果他不得到帮助，他很可能会再次犯罪。
She will probably reoffend unless she addresses her underlying issues. = 除非她解决她潜在的问题，否则她可能会再次犯罪。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't reoffended, he would be a free man today. = 如果他没有再次犯罪，他今天将是一个自由人。
I wish he hadn't reoffended; it breaks my heart. = 我真希望他没有再次犯罪；这让我心碎。

被动语态:
It is feared that he will be reoffending soon. = 人们担心他会很快再次犯罪。
The judge stated that he could have been reoffended if he had not received support. = 法官说，如果他没有得到支持，他可能会再次犯罪。


疑问句:
Has he reoffended since his last conviction? = 自从他上次定罪以来，他再次犯罪了吗？
Will she reoffend after completing her rehabilitation program? = 完成康复计划后，她还会再次犯罪吗？

否定句:
He did not reoffend after completing his community service. = 完成社区服务后，他没有再次犯罪。
She has not reoffended in over five years. = 她五年多来没有再次犯罪。
