concoction:/kənˈkɒkʃən/| n. |混合物；调合剂；调制饮料|A concoction is a mixture of various ingredients, often in unusual or unexpected combinations.  It often implies something created in a somewhat haphazard or experimental way, and can be either pleasant or unpleasant. |例句：The witch brewed a strange concoction in her cauldron. 女巫在她的大锅里熬制了一种奇怪的混合物。


例句：He created a delicious concoction of fruits and spices. 他调制了一种美味的水果和香料混合物。

近义词：mixture, blend, brew, combination

反义词：purity, simplicity


词源:  From Middle French *concoction, from Latin *concoctionem (nominative concoctio), from con- "together" + coquere "to cook".  The word's evolution reflects its original meaning of something cooked together.
