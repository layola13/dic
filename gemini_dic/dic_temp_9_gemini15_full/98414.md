insouciance:/ˌɪnsuːˈsiːɑːns/| n. |无忧无虑；漫不经心；漠不关心|A feeling or appearance of not worrying about anything; lighthearted unconcern.|例句：She accepted the news with an air of insouciance. 她以一种无忧无虑的神情接受了这个消息。

例句：His insouciance in the face of danger was both admirable and terrifying. 他面对危险的漫不经心既令人钦佩又令人恐惧。


近义词：carefreeness, nonchalance, unconcern, apathy


反义词：anxiety, worry, concern, apprehension


词性：名词


词源：源自法语单词“insouciance”，由“in-”（否定前缀）和“souci”（关心，担忧）组成，字面意思是“无忧无虑”。
