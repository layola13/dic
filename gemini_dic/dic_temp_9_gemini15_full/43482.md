crate:/kreɪt/ | n. | 箱子，板条箱 | A large strong box of wood, plastic, or metal, used for storing or transporting goods.  |  The oranges arrived in a wooden crate. 橘子装在木箱里运来了。 They shipped the equipment in a metal crate. 他们用金属箱子运送设备。 | box, container |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用open space等) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company uses crates to ship its products. = 公司用板条箱运送产品。
He often carries a crate of apples to the market. = 他经常提着一箱苹果去市场。


一般过去时:
They packed the fragile items carefully into crates. = 他们小心翼翼地将易碎物品装进箱子。
The workers unloaded the crates from the truck. = 工人们从卡车上卸下箱子。


现在完成时:
We have shipped several crates of goods this month. = 这个月我们已经运送了几箱货物。
She has already unpacked the crate of books. = 她已经打开书箱了。


过去完成时:
They had already prepared the crates before the delivery. = 送货前他们已经准备好箱子了。
The company had shipped all the crates before the storm. = 暴风雨来临前，公司已经运送了所有箱子。


将来时:
We will send the crates by express delivery. = 我们将通过快递发送箱子。
They will need more crates for the increased orders. = 他们需要更多箱子来满足增加的订单。


虚拟语气:
If I had more crates, I could ship everything today. = 如果我有更多箱子，我今天就能把所有东西都运走了。
I wish I had used stronger crates for the shipment. = 我真希望我用更结实的箱子来装运货物。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
