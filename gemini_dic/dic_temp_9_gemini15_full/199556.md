singled:/ˈsɪŋɡld/| vt.|挑选；单独；强调；|to choose one person or thing from a group; to pick out; to select; to point out as exceptional; to focus attention on.|例句：He singled out one student for praise. 他挑选出一名学生予以表扬。|例句：The report singled out several areas for improvement.  这份报告特别指出了几个需要改进的方面。|近义词：selected, picked, chosen, highlighted, emphasized|反义词：ignored, overlooked, neglected


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He singles out the best apples. = 他挑选出最好的苹果。
She singles out her favorite song. = 她挑出她最喜欢的歌。

一般过去时:
They singled him out for special treatment. = 他们单独对他进行特殊对待。
The teacher singled out John for his excellent work. = 老师表扬了约翰的优异表现。

现在完成时:
I have singled out three candidates for the job. = 我已经为这份工作挑选出了三名候选人。
She has singled out this painting as her masterpiece. = 她已经将这幅画选为她的杰作。

过去完成时:
They had singled out the problem before the meeting. = 他们已经在会议之前确定了这个问题。
He had singled out his best friend to help him. = 他已经挑选了他最好的朋友来帮助他。

将来时:
She will single out the most qualified applicant. = 她将挑选出最合格的申请人。
They will single out the key issues for discussion. = 他们将挑选出关键问题进行讨论。

虚拟语气:
If I were to single out one person, it would be him. = 如果要我挑选一个人，我会选他。
I wish I had singled out that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时抓住了那个机会。

被动语态:
He was singled out for criticism. = 他受到了批评。
This problem was singled out as the most important. = 这个问题被认为是最重要的。

疑问句:
Which candidate did they single out? = 他们挑选了哪位候选人？
What aspect of the project did she single out? = 她强调了项目的哪个方面？

否定句:
They didn't single out anyone for punishment. = 他们没有惩罚任何人。
The report didn't single out any specific companies. = 这份报告没有点名任何特定的公司。


PHRASE->

single out someone for criticism = 批评某人
single out someone for punishment = 惩罚某人
single out a problem = 挑出一个问题
