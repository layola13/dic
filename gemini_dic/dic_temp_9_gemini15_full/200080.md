skewed:/skjuːd/| adj.|歪斜的；偏斜的；有偏差的 |  Not straight or aligned; distorted or biased.  It implies a deviation from a norm or expectation, often in a way that is unfair or inaccurate. |例句：The survey results were skewed because the sample was not representative.  调查结果有偏差，因为样本不具有代表性。

The photograph was skewed due to the camera angle. 照片因相机角度而变形。|近义词：distorted, biased, slanted, lopsided, asymmetrical |反义词：straight, unbiased, balanced, symmetrical, fair
