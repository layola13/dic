exeunt:/ˈɛɡziːʌnt/| v.|他们退场；(演员)退下|This word is a Latin term, the third-person plural present active indicative form of the verb *exire*, meaning "to go out." In dramatic texts, it indicates that multiple characters are leaving the stage.  | Example: The villain and his henchmen exeunt, leaving the hero alone.  恶棍和他的爪牙退场了，留下英雄独自一人。|近义词：exit, depart, leave |反义词：enter, arrive |动词
