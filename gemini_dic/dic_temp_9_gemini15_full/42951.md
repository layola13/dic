coverages:/ˈkʌvərɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 覆盖范围；报道；保险范围 | The extent to which something is covered, dealt with, or included.  This can refer to physical coverage (like the area a blanket covers), news coverage (like a story's reporting on an event), or insurance coverage (like the types of events an insurance policy covers). | 例句：The map shows the coverage of the mobile phone network. 这张地图显示了移动电话网络的覆盖范围。  The news coverage of the election was extensive.  对这次选举的新闻报道非常广泛。 | 近义词：scope, extent, range, reporting | 反义词：lack, absence, omission
