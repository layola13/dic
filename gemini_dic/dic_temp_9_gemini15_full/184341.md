respects:/rɪˈspɛkts/ | n. | 方面；尊敬；问候 | The word "respects" refers to particular aspects or details of something, or to the honor and esteem shown to someone.  It can also be used as a plural form of "respect," indicating multiple aspects or instances of showing respect. |例句：In many respects, the two situations are similar.  在许多方面，这两种情况都很相似。
He paid his respects to the fallen soldiers. 他向阵亡将士致敬。|近义词：aspects, regards, considerations, esteem, homage |反义词：disrespect, scorn, contempt |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows his respects to his elders. = 他对长辈表示尊敬。
The report covers all aspects with respects to the project. = 报告涵盖了项目的所有方面。


一般过去时:
She paid her respects at the memorial service. = 她在追悼会上表达了敬意。
They showed their respects by attending the funeral. = 他们通过参加葬礼来表达他们的敬意。


现在完成时:
He has shown his respects to his teachers throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都对老师表示尊重。
She has paid her respects to the victims of the disaster. = 她向灾难的受害者表示了敬意。


过去完成时:
They had paid their respects before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前，他们已经表达了敬意。
He had shown his respects to his mentor many times. = 他曾多次向他的导师表示敬意。


将来时:
She will pay her respects at the grave. = 她将在坟墓前表达敬意。
We will show our respects to the national flag. = 我们将向国旗致敬。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would show my respects to the veterans. = 如果我是你，我会向退伍军人表示敬意。
I wish I had paid my respects earlier. = 我希望我早点表示敬意。


被动语态:
Respects were paid to the deceased. = 向死者表示了敬意。
All aspects were considered with respects to the design. = 在设计方面考虑了所有方面。


疑问句:
In what respects do these two paintings differ? = 这两幅画在哪些方面有所不同？
Did you pay your respects to the family? = 你向家人表示慰问了吗？


否定句:
He showed no respects to the law. = 他对法律毫无尊重。
They did not pay their respects to the fallen soldiers. = 他们没有向阵亡将士致敬。


PHRASE->
