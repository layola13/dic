roaster:/ˈroʊstər/| n. |烤肉架；烤箱；（家禽等的）烤制者；（尤指公鸡）名册；花名册| A roaster is an apparatus for roasting, typically meat or coffee beans. It can also refer to a person who roasts, or a list of names, especially of roosters.  |例句：The chef used a large roaster to cook the turkey.  厨师用一个大烤箱烤火鸡。

The farmer checked the roaster for the names of the roosters ready for sale. 农场主查看了待售公鸡的名册。|近义词：oven, grill, baker, register, list |反义词：boiler, freezer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He roasts coffee beans every morning. 他每天早上都烘焙咖啡豆。
She roasts a chicken for Sunday dinner. 她周日晚餐烤鸡。

一般过去时:
They roasted marshmallows around the campfire. 他们在篝火旁烤棉花糖。
He roasted a whole pig for the festival. 他为节日烤了一整头猪。

现在完成时:
I have roasted several turkeys this year. 今年我已经烤了几只火鸡了。
She has never roasted a duck before. 她以前从未烤过鸭子。

过去完成时:
They had roasted the vegetables before adding the sauce. 他们在加入酱汁之前已经烤好了蔬菜。
We had roasted the chicken for two hours before it was cooked through. 我们烤鸡两个小时后才烤熟。

将来时:
I will roast a leg of lamb for Christmas dinner. 我将为圣诞晚餐烤一条羊腿。
She will roast the nuts before adding them to the cake. 她将在把坚果加到蛋糕里之前烤一下。

虚拟语气:
If I had a roaster, I would roast a whole pig. 如果我有一个烤箱，我会烤一头猪。
I wish I had roasted that chicken more slowly. 我希望我烤那只鸡慢一点。

被动语态:
The coffee beans are roasted at high temperatures. 咖啡豆在高温下烘焙。
The turkey was roasted until golden brown. 火鸡烤至金棕色。

疑问句:
Do you roast your own coffee beans? 你自己烤咖啡豆吗？
How long did you roast the chicken for? 你烤鸡烤了多久？

否定句:
I don't roast meat very often. 我不经常烤肉。
He didn't roast the vegetables long enough. 他没有把蔬菜烤够时间。
