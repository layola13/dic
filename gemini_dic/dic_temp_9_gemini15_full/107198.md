lags:/læɡz/| v. n.|落后，滞后；延迟；迟缓|To move or develop slowly or less quickly than others.|The project is lagging behind schedule.  项目进度滞后。|The economy is lagging. 经济正在滞缓。|近义词：delay,retard,  fall behind|反义词：lead, advance, accelerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project lags behind the schedule. = 项目进度落后于计划。
He lags behind his classmates in math. = 他数学成绩落后于同班同学。

一般过去时:
The negotiations lagged for weeks. = 谈判拖延了几周。
The car lagged behind the others on the highway. = 这辆车在高速公路上落后于其他车辆。

现在完成时:
The team has lagged behind in the competition. = 该队在比赛中落后了。
The economy has lagged for several quarters. = 经济已经滞缓了几个季度。

过去完成时:
The runner had lagged behind the pack and lost the race. = 跑步者落后于大部队，输掉了比赛。
The company had lagged in adopting new technologies. = 公司在采用新技术方面落后了。

将来时:
The construction is expected to lag next year. = 预计明年建筑工程将滞后。
Production is likely to lag in the coming months. = 未来几个月产量可能会下降。

虚拟语气:
If the economy lagged further, we would face a recession. = 如果经济进一步滞后，我们将面临经济衰退。
I wish the project hadn't lagged so much. = 我希望项目没有这么滞后。

被动语态:
The progress of the project was lagged by unforeseen circumstances. = 项目的进展因不可预见的情况而滞后。
The development of the new drug has been lagged by regulatory hurdles. = 新药的研发因监管障碍而滞后。

疑问句:
Is the construction project lagging? = 建筑项目是否滞后？
Are the sales figures lagging this year? = 今年的销售数据是否滞后？

否定句:
The company did not lag in responding to the crisis. = 公司在应对危机时没有落后。
The team didn't lag behind their rivals. = 该队没有落后于他们的竞争对手。
