self-admittedly:/ˌsɛlf ədˈmɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 自认地，承认地 |  This adverb describes something acknowledged or confessed by the person involved. It emphasizes that the statement comes from the person's own admission, not from external sources. | 例句：He self-admittedly lacked the skills needed for the job.  他自认缺乏这份工作所需的技能。

例句：She self-admittedly made a mistake in her calculations. 她自认在计算中犯了一个错误。


近义词：frankly, openly, honestly, admittedly

反义词：falsely, deceptively, hypocritically


词性：副词
