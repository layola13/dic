refractive:/rɪˈfræktɪv/| adj.| 折射的，屈折的 | Relating to or causing refraction (the bending of a wave as it passes from one medium to another).  It describes the ability of a substance to bend light. | 例句：The refractive index of glass is higher than that of air. 玻璃的折射率高于空气。

例句：The lens's refractive power focuses the light onto the retina.  透镜的折射能力将光线聚焦到视网膜上。


近义词：refracting, bending, deflecting | 反义词：straight, direct | 词性：形容词
