data-warehousing: /ˈdeɪtə ˈwɛrˌhaʊzɪŋ/ | n. | 数据仓库 | The process of collecting and managing large amounts of data from various sources to provide business intelligence.  It involves extracting, transforming, and loading (ETL) data into a central repository for querying and analysis. | Example: Our company implemented a new data-warehousing system to improve our business decision-making.  我们的公司实施了一个新的数据仓库系统以改进我们的商业决策。  |近义词: data warehousing system, data mart, data lake |反义词: data scattering, data siloing |名词
