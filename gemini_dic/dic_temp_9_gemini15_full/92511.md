hypothesizing: /haɪˈpɒθəsaɪzɪŋ/ /haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 假设；假设；提出假设 |  The act of forming a hypothesis; the process of making a tentative explanation or assumption.  It involves formulating a testable statement based on observation and reasoning, which can then be investigated through further research or experimentation. |

例句：

Scientists are hypothesizing about the origins of the universe. = 科学家们正在假设宇宙的起源。

She is hypothesizing a link between diet and disease. = 她正在假设饮食和疾病之间的联系。


近义词：theorizing, postulating, conjecturing, speculating

反义词：proving, verifying, confirming


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They hypothesize a connection between stress and illness. = 他们假设压力和疾病之间存在联系。
Scientists hypothesize that climate change is affecting weather patterns. = 科学家们假设气候变化正在影响天气模式。

一般过去时:
The researchers hypothesized that the drug would be effective. = 研究人员假设该药物将是有效的。
He hypothesized a relationship between economic growth and social inequality. = 他假设经济增长与社会不平等之间存在关系。

现在完成时:
Scientists have hypothesized about the existence of dark matter. = 科学家们已经假设了暗物质的存在。
Researchers have hypothesized that certain genes increase the risk of cancer. = 研究人员已经假设某些基因会增加患癌症的风险。

过去完成时:
They had hypothesized that the experiment would fail before they even started. = 他们在实验开始之前就假设实验会失败。
Having hypothesized a correlation, they then set out to test it. = 在提出相关性假设之后，他们着手进行检验。

将来时:
We will hypothesize about the potential impact of the new policy. = 我们将假设这项新政策的潜在影响。
They will hypothesize different scenarios to prepare for the future. = 他们将假设不同的情景以应对未来。

虚拟语气:
If we were to hypothesize a different scenario, the outcome might be different. = 如果我们假设不同的情况，结果可能会不同。
I wish I had hypothesized the problem more carefully before starting. = 我希望在开始之前更仔细地假设这个问题。

被动语态:
A link between smoking and lung cancer was hypothesized decades ago. = 几十年前就有人假设吸烟和肺癌之间存在联系。
Several different theories have been hypothesized to explain the phenomenon. = 已经提出了几种不同的理论来解释这种现象。

疑问句:
What are you hypothesizing about the cause of the problem? = 你对这个问题的原因作出了什么假设？
What did the researchers hypothesize in their study? = 研究人员在他们的研究中假设了什么？


否定句:
They did not hypothesize a causal relationship between the two variables. = 他们没有假设这两个变量之间存在因果关系。
We cannot hypothesize about the future with certainty. = 我们不能肯定地假设未来。
