laburnic:/læˈbɜːrnɪk/| adj.|金链花属的；金链花色的|Of or relating to the genus Laburnum, a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae, known for their pendulous racemes of yellow flowers.  The term can also describe the color of these flowers, a bright, golden yellow.|例句：The laburnic blossoms brightened the spring garden.  金链花的花朵照亮了春季的花园。 The painting featured a rich, laburnic hue. 这幅画以丰富的金链花色调为特色。|近义词：golden yellow, daffodil yellow|反义词：dark, dull
