joints:/dʒɔɪnts/| n.|关节；接合处；接缝；(香烟的)一支；(俚语)监狱|A joint is a place where two or more bones meet in the body.  It allows movement and flexibility.  It can also refer to a connection between two things, like the joints in a pipe. In slang, it can refer to a prison or a marijuana cigarette.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's injured knee joint.医生检查了病人受伤的膝关节。

The carpenter carefully glued the joints of the wooden box.木匠仔细地粘合了木盒的接缝。

近义词：articulation, connection, juncture|反义词：separation, disconnection

词源:
The word "joint" comes from the Old French word "joinct," meaning "joined" or "joined together." This ultimately derives from the Latin word "junctus," the past participle of "jungere," meaning "to join."
