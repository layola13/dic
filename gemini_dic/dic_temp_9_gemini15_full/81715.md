greenkeeping:/ˈɡriːnkiːpɪŋ/| n. |绿地养护；高尔夫球场管理|The practice of maintaining turfgrass areas, particularly on golf courses, sports fields, and parks.  It involves a range of tasks including mowing, fertilizing, watering, pest and disease control, and general maintenance. |例句：The greenkeeping team worked tirelessly to prepare the course for the tournament. 绿地养护团队夜以继日地为锦标赛准备球场。

例句：Good greenkeeping is essential for a high-quality playing surface. 良好的绿地养护对于高质量的比赛场地至关重要。|近义词：turf management, groundskeeping|反义词：neglect, abandonment|名词
