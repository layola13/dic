dreamt:/drɛmt/| v.| 做梦；梦想；想象 | Past tense of "dream". It means to have a dream while sleeping, or to imagine something.  |例句：I dreamt I was flying. = 我梦见我在飞。
I dreamt of a better future. = 我梦想过一个更美好的未来。|近义词：dreamed, envisioned, imagined |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dream of becoming a doctor. = 我梦想成为一名医生。
He dreams of winning the lottery. = 他梦想赢得彩票。

一般过去时:
I dreamt of you last night. = 我昨晚梦见你了。
She dreamt she was on a beach. = 她梦见自己在一个海滩上。

现在完成时:
I have dreamt of this moment for years. = 我多年来一直梦想着这一刻。
He has dreamt of traveling the world. = 他一直梦想着环游世界。

过去完成时:
I had dreamt of going to Paris before I finally went. = 在我最终去巴黎之前，我一直梦想着去那里。
She had dreamt of becoming a singer since she was a child. = 从小她就梦想着成为一名歌手。

将来时:
I will dream of you tonight. = 今晚我会梦见你。
He will dream sweet dreams tonight. = 今晚他会做个好梦。

虚拟语气:
If I dreamt that I could fly, I would be very happy. = 如果我梦见自己会飞，我会非常高兴。
I wish I dreamt more often. = 我希望我更经常做梦。
