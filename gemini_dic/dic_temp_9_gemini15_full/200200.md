skinks:/skɪŋks/| n.|石龙子；(尤指) 蜥蜴|A skink is a type of lizard.  Many skinks are characterized by smooth, often shiny scales. They are found worldwide in various habitats. |例句：The children were fascinated by the brightly colored skinks basking in the sun. 孩子们被在阳光下晒太阳的色彩鲜艳的石龙子迷住了。|例句：We saw several skinks scurrying under the rocks. 我们看到几条石龙子在岩石下快速爬行。|近义词：lizard, reptile|反义词：无
