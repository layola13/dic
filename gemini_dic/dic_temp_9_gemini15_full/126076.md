mustily:/ˈmʌstɪli/ | adj. | 霉臭地；发霉地 | Having or smelling of a musty odor; damp and stale.  The old book smelled mustily. | The attic air was mustily damp. | 近义词：fusty, moldy, mildewy | 反义词：fresh, clean, sweet


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed or a state exists.  Therefore, grammatical examples focusing on tense are not applicable.

Example Sentences:

The old chest was opened, revealing clothes that smelled mustily.  = 打开旧箱子，露出了闻起来霉臭的衣服。

The basement felt mustily cold and damp. = 地下室感觉又冷又潮湿，带着霉味。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "mustily".  The word is primarily used as a descriptive adverb.
