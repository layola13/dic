pedaled:/ˈpɛdəld/| v.|踩踏（自行车踏板）；用力地移动|To operate a bicycle or similar vehicle by pushing the pedals.  It can also mean to move something using a similar pushing motion.|例句：He pedaled his bike up the steep hill. 他用力踩着自行车上了陡峭的山坡。

例句：She pedaled furiously to catch up with her friends. 她用力蹬自行车追赶她的朋友们。


近义词：cycled, rode (a bike)


反义词：None directly opposite, but  "coasted" implies the opposite action of not pedaling.


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pedals his bike to work every day. 他每天骑自行车上班。
She pedals slowly up the hill. 她慢慢地骑车上山。


一般过去时:
I pedaled as fast as I could. 我尽可能快地骑车。
They pedaled through the countryside. 他们骑车穿过乡下。


现在完成时:
He has pedaled over 100 miles this week.  他本周骑车超过100英里了。
She has pedaled to the top of the mountain. 她已经骑车到达山顶了。


过去完成时:
I had pedaled for hours before I stopped. 我骑了好几个小时才停下来。
They had pedaled a long distance before they took a break. 他们在休息之前骑了很长一段路。


将来时:
I will pedal to the store tomorrow. 我明天将骑车去商店。
They will pedal along the beach path. 他们将在海滩小路上骑车。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bike, I would pedal to the park. 如果我有一辆自行车，我会骑车去公园。
I wish I could pedal faster. 我希望我能骑得更快。


被动语态: (Pedal is rarely used passively)
The bicycle was pedaled rapidly down the hill. 自行车快速地被蹬下山坡。(This is unusual usage)


疑问句:
Did you pedal to school today? 你今天骑车去学校了吗？
Will they pedal to the concert? 他们会骑车去音乐会吗？


否定句:
I didn't pedal very far today. 我今天没骑多远。
They didn't pedal all the way home. 他们没有一路骑车回家。


PHRASE->
pedal to the metal: 全力以赴
pedal pusher: 骑自行车的人
pedal power: 脚踏动力
pedal stroke: 踩踏动作
pedal car: 脚踏车
