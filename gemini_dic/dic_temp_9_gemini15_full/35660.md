cobaltic:/koʊˈbɔːltɪk/| adj.|钴的；三价钴的 |Relating to or containing cobalt, especially in the trivalent state.  The term often refers to compounds where cobalt is in the +3 oxidation state.|例句：Cobaltic oxide is a dark brown powder. 三氧化二钴是一种深棕色的粉末。|例句：Cobaltic compounds are often used as catalysts. 钴化合物经常用作催化剂。|近义词：cobalt(III), trivalent cobalt|反义词：cobaltous(二价钴的)
