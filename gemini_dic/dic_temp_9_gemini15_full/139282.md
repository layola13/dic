obliterates:/əˈblɪtəreɪts/ | vt. | 抹去；涂掉；摧毁；消灭 | To destroy completely so that nothing remains; to erase or wipe out.  | The fire obliterated the entire forest. 火灾夷平了整片森林。The dictator tried to obliterate all evidence of his crimes. 独裁者试图抹去所有犯罪证据。 | destroy, annihilate, eradicate, wipe out | preserve, save, protect, conserve | 动词
