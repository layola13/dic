self-preoccupations:/ˌsɛlf ˌpriːˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃənz/| n. |自我专注；沉溺于自我；自我沉迷|Engrossment in one's own thoughts, feelings, or concerns, often to the exclusion of others or external realities.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting an unhealthy level of self-absorption.|例句：His self-preoccupations prevented him from seeing the needs of his family. 他的自我专注使他看不到家人的需求。|例句：Her self-preoccupations led to social isolation and depression. 她的自我专注导致了社会孤立和抑郁。|近义词：self-absorption, narcissism, egotism, self-centeredness |反义词：selflessness, altruism, empathy, consideration for others |名词
