regale:/rɪˈɡeɪl/| vt.|款待；使高兴；使快乐|To entertain or amuse someone with something interesting or enjoyable.|The storyteller regaled the children with tales of dragons and knights.  讲故事的人给孩子们讲了龙和骑士的故事，使他们很开心。|近义词：entertain, amuse, delight|反义词：bore, displease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regales his friends with funny stories. = 他用有趣的故事款待他的朋友们。
She regales her audience with her singing. = 她用歌声款待她的观众。

一般过去时:
The host regaled us with delicious food and wine. = 主人用美味的食物和酒款待了我们。
He regaled the children with a captivating story. = 他给孩子们讲了一个引人入胜的故事。

现在完成时:
She has regaled us with many interesting anecdotes over the years. = 多年来，她一直用许多有趣的小故事款待我们。
He has regaled his listeners with countless tales of adventure. = 他给听众讲了无数个冒险故事。

过去完成时:
They had regaled themselves with a hearty meal before setting off on their journey. = 他们在出发之前好好吃了一顿。
She had regaled her friends with her exciting travel experiences. = 她曾向朋友们讲述了她激动人心的旅行经历。

将来时:
The comedian will regale the audience with his hilarious jokes. = 喜剧演员将用他滑稽的玩笑逗乐观众。
She will regale her guests with stories of her childhood. = 她将用童年故事招待她的客人。


虚拟语气:
If I could sing, I would regale you with a beautiful song. = 如果我会唱歌，我会为你唱一首优美的歌。
I wish I could regale you with news of my success. = 我希望我能告诉你我成功的消息。


被动语态:
The guests were regaled with a sumptuous feast. = 客人们享用了丰盛的盛宴。
The audience was regaled with a spectacular performance. = 观众欣赏了一场精彩的演出。


疑问句:
Will the speaker regale us with any anecdotes? = 演讲者会给我们讲一些轶事吗？
How will she regale the children during the party? = 派对上她将如何款待孩子们？


否定句:
He didn't regale them with any news. = 他没有告诉他们任何消息。
She did not regale her listeners with boring details. = 她没有用无聊的细节来打发她的听众。
