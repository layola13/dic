canoes:/kəˈnuːz/ | n. |独木舟；轻舟 |  A canoe is a light narrow boat propelled by a paddle.  Canoes are typically made from wood, canvas, or other lightweight materials. They are often used for recreational paddling, fishing, or transportation in calm waters. | 例句：We rented canoes and spent the afternoon paddling on the lake. = 我们租了独木舟，在湖上划船度过了下午。

They used canoes to cross the river. = 他们用独木舟渡河。|近义词：kayaks, boats | 反义词：ships (in terms of size), cars (in terms of mode of transportation)
