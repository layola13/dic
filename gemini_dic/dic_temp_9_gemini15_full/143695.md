outlearned:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrnd/| v. |超过…在知识或技能上；胜过；比…学识渊博|To surpass someone in knowledge or skill.  It implies a significant difference in learning or expertise.|He was completely outlearned by his younger sister in the debate. 他在辩论中被比他年轻的妹妹完全胜过。|近义词：surpassed, excelled, outstripped |反义词：underlearned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outlearns his classmates in mathematics. 他在数学方面超过了他的同学们。
She outlearns her colleagues in the field of astrophysics.  她在天体物理学领域胜过她的同事们。


一般过去时:
He outlearned his opponent in the trivia contest.  他在知识竞赛中胜过了他的对手。
She outlearned all her competitors in the spelling bee.  她在拼字比赛中胜过了所有竞争对手。


现在完成时:
He has outlearned many of his professors throughout his academic career. 在他的学术生涯中，他已经超过了许多教授。
She has outlearned her initial expectations in learning the new language. 她已经超过了她最初学习新语言的预期。


过去完成时:
He had outlearned his brother by the time they reached university.  当他们到达大学时，他已经超过了他的兄弟。
She had outlearned her teacher on the subject of ancient history.  她在古代史方面已经超过了她的老师。


将来时:
He will outlearn his current limitations with dedicated practice.  他会通过刻苦练习来克服他目前的局限性。
She will outlearn her current knowledge base through further research. 她将通过进一步的研究来扩展她目前的知识储备。


虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he would have outlearned his rival. 如果他学习更努力，他就会超过他的对手。
If she had access to better resources, she would have outlearned her peers. 如果她有更好的资源，她就会超过她的同龄人。


被动语态: (此词较少用被动语态)
He was outlearned by a younger student. 他被一个更年轻的学生超过了。
The older generation was outlearned by the advancements in technology. 老一代人被技术的进步所超越。


疑问句:
Did he outlearn his tutor in logic? 他在逻辑方面超过了他的导师吗？
Has she outlearned her rivals in the competition?  她在比赛中超过了她的竞争对手吗？


否定句:
He did not outlearn his competitor in the chess match. 他在棋赛中没有超过他的对手。
She failed to outlearn her opponent in the debate. 她未能超越她在辩论中的对手。
