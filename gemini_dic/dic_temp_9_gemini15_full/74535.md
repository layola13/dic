fraternizer:/ˈfrætərnaɪzər/| n. v.|结兄弟，与…友好相处，士兵与敌人友好相处|One who fraternizes; one who is friendly with another.  To associate in a friendly way, especially with someone who is an enemy or is considered to be an outsider.|例句：The soldiers were court-martialed for fraternizing with the enemy.  士兵因与敌人友好相处而被军事法庭审判。 He fraternized with the locals, learning their customs and language. 他和当地人友好相处，学习他们的风俗习惯和语言。|近义词：associate, socialize, pal around with|反义词：estrange, alienate, isolate|动词
