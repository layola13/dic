judge's:/ˈdʒʌdʒɪz/| n.|法官的；裁判的|The possessive form of "judge," indicating belonging to or relating to a judge.  It's used to describe something owned by a judge or related to their work. |例句：The judge's gavel fell sharply. 法官的木槌重重落下。|例句：We waited for the judge's decision. 我们等待着法官的判决。|近义词：judicial, referee's, arbitrator's |反义词：defendant's, plaintiff's
