overviewing:/ˌoʊvərˈvjuːɪŋ/ | v. | 概述；综述；监视；俯瞰 | To provide a general summary or description of something; to supervise or oversee something; to look down upon from a high place. | Example:  He was overviewing the project's progress. 他正在概述项目的进展。  The general overviewed the troops from the hill. 将军从山上俯瞰军队。|近义词：summarizing, surveying, supervising, overseeing | 反义词：detailing, examining closely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overviewing the marketing plan. = 他正在概述营销计划。
She overviews the team's performance regularly. = 她定期审核团队的业绩。


一般过去时:
The manager overviewed the quarterly report. = 经理概述了季度报告。
They overviewed the construction site yesterday. = 他们昨天视察了建筑工地。


现在完成时:
He has overviewed all the data. = 他已经审核了所有数据。
She has overviewed the project for several months. = 她已经监视这个项目好几个月了。


过去完成时:
They had overviewed the situation before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经对情况进行了概述。
She had overviewed the report before submitting it. = 她提交报告之前已经审查过报告了。


将来时:
He will overview the new product launch. = 他将概述新产品的发布。
She will overview the team's progress next week. = 她下周将审核团队的进度。


虚拟语气:
If I had overviewed the contract carefully, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我仔细检查过合同，我就不会犯那个错误。
I wish I had overviewed the situation before investing. = 我希望在投资之前我已经对情况进行了评估。


被动语态:
The project was overviewed by the senior manager. = 该项目由高级经理进行了概述。
The progress is being overviewed by the project team. = 项目进度正在由项目团队进行审核。


疑问句:

否定句:
