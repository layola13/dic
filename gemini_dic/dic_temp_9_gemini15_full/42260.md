counter-argument:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrɡjumənt/|/ˈkaʊntə(r)ˌɑːɡjuːmənt/| n. |反驳，反论 |A counter-argument is a statement or argument that contradicts or opposes another argument.  It's used to challenge or refute a previously made point. |Example:  His argument that the policy was beneficial was met with a strong counter-argument about its negative impact on the environment. 他的论点认为这项政策是有益的，但遭到了关于其对环境负面影响的有力反驳。  |近义词：rebuttal, refutation, objection |反义词：support, endorsement, confirmation |名词
