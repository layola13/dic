skybox:/ˈskaɪbɑːks/ | n. |天空盒，天空箱|A cube-shaped texture applied to the background of a 3D scene to simulate a sky or environment.  It's a common technique in computer graphics and video games to create a sense of depth and immersion.  |例句：The game used a beautiful skybox to enhance the realism of the mountain landscape. 游戏使用了一个美丽的天空盒来增强山地景观的真实感。

The skybox in this flight simulator is breathtaking. 这个飞行模拟器中的天空盒令人叹为观止。|近义词：environment map, background texture|反义词：无
