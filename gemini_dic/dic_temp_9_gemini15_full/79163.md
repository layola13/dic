glamorised:/ˈɡlæməraɪzd/| v. |美化；使具有魅力；使迷人 |To make something seem more attractive, exciting, or glamorous than it really is.  |The film glamorised the life of a gangster. 这部电影美化了黑帮的生活。|近义词：romanticize, idealize, embellish |反义词：denigrate, disparage, uglify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They glamorise the lives of celebrities. = 他们美化了名人的生活。
The advertisement glamorises the product's features. = 广告美化了产品的特性。

一般过去时:
The media glamorised the war. = 媒体美化了这场战争。
He glamorised his past experiences. = 他美化了他过去的经历。

现在完成时:
The fashion industry has glamorised unrealistic body images. = 时尚行业已经美化了不切实际的体型形象。
She has glamorised her childhood memories. = 她已经美化了她童年的记忆。

过去完成时:

将来时:

They will glamorise their achievements in the report. = 他们将在报告中美化他们的成就。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
