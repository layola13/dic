calmest: /ˈkɑːməst/ | adj. | 最平静的，最镇定的 |  The superlative form of "calm," indicating the highest degree of calmness or tranquility.  It describes a state of being free from agitation or disturbance; peaceful and serene. |  例句：The calmest moment of the day was just before sunrise.  一天中最平静的时刻是日出前。


The calmest sea can become tumultuous in a storm.  最平静的海面在暴风雨中也会变得汹涌。

近义词：most peaceful, most tranquil, most serene, most composed

反义词：most agitated, most turbulent, most frantic, most stressed

词性: 形容词
