quasi-consequential:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/ | adj. |准结果的，似结果的|  Nearly or seemingly consequential; having the appearance or some of the characteristics of a consequence, but not a direct or full consequence.  It implies a partial or indirect causal link. | Example: The quasi-consequential damage to the ecosystem from the oil spill was still significant, even if not directly caused by the initial impact.  生态系统因漏油事件而造成的准结果性损害仍然是巨大的，即使它并非直接由最初的影响造成。|近义词:indirect, secondary, incidental |反义词:direct, primary, essential |词性:形容词
