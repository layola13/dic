loams:/loʊmz/| n.|壤土，砂壤土|A loam is a fertile soil consisting of a roughly equal mixture of sand, silt, and clay.|The farmer tested the loam to determine its suitability for planting.|近义词：clay loam, silty loam, sandy loam|反义词：暂无
