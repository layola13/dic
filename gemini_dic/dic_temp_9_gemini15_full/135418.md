It often refers to the deliberate ending of a system, practice, or tradition. | Example: The nonperpetuation of the monarchy was a controversial topic during the revolution.  革命期间，君主制的废除是一个有争议的话题。  The nonperpetuation of harmful stereotypes in media is crucial for social progress.  媒体中避免延续有害刻板印象对于社会进步至关重要。 | 近义词: discontinuation, termination, cessation, extinction | 反义词: perpetuation, continuation, preservation
