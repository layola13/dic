over-pollinating: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度授粉 | The act of pollinating plants to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences.  This can lead to reduced fruit set, smaller fruit size, or even plant damage. | Example: Over-pollinating the apple trees resulted in a smaller than expected yield of apples this year.  过度授粉苹果树导致今年苹果产量低于预期。 |近义词： excessive pollination, super-pollination | 反义词：under-pollination
