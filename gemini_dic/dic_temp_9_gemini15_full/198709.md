sibilation:/sɪbɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |咝音；齿擦音|The production of a hissing sound; the occurrence of this sound in speech or writing.  Sibilants are sounds produced by forcing air through a narrow channel in the mouth, creating a hissing or buzzing sound.  Examples include /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /tʃ/, and /dʒ/.|The poem's sibilation gave it a sinister tone. 这首诗的咝音使它有一种险恶的基调。The excessive sibilation in her speech was distracting. 她说话中过多的咝音令人分心。|sibilance, hissing| (无明确反义词，可考虑用absence of sibilance 或 unsibilated sounds)|名词
