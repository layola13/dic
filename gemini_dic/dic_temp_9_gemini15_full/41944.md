cosmopolitans:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənz/|/ˈkɑːzməˌpɑːlɪtənz/| n. |世界主义者；国际化大都市居民；见多识广的人|People who are at ease in many different countries and cultures;  inhabitants of a cosmopolitan city.  Cosmopolitans are often open-minded, adaptable, and interested in diverse perspectives.|例句：Many cosmopolitans choose to live in large, diverse cities. 许多世界主义者选择居住在大型多元化的城市。

例句：She's a true cosmopolitan, having lived in five different countries. 她是一位真正的世界主义者，曾在五个不同的国家生活过。

近义词：internationalist, global citizen, world traveler

反义词：provincial, insular, parochial
