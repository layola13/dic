megastore:/ˈmɛɡəˌstɔːr/| n. |大型超市，巨型商店 | A megastore is a very large retail store, typically selling a wide variety of goods.  |Example:  The new megastore opened last week and attracted thousands of shoppers.  新的巨型商店上周开业，吸引了数千名顾客。|近义词：superstore, hypermarket |反义词：small shop, boutique
