invasiveness:/ɪnˈveɪsɪvnəs/| n. |侵袭性；侵略性；入侵性 |The tendency of something to spread or intrude aggressively.  It often refers to the spread of plants, animals, or diseases into new areas, or the aggressive behavior of a species.  |例句：The invasiveness of the kudzu vine is a major problem in the southeastern United States.  美国东南部葛藤的入侵性是一个主要问题。

The invasiveness of the new software caused system crashes. 新软件的侵略性导致系统崩溃。

|近义词：aggressiveness, invasiveness, encroachment, intrusion |反义词：passiveness, harmlessness, non-intrusiveness |名词
