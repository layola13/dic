participated:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪd/| v. |参与，参加|To take part in something.|例句：Many students participated in the school play. 许多学生参加了学校的戏剧演出。
例句：She actively participated in the discussion. 她积极参与了讨论。|近义词：took part in, joined, involved|反义词：ignored, avoided, neglected|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He participates in many sports. = 他参加很多体育运动。
They participate in the community events. = 他们参与社区活动。

一般过去时:
I participated in the marathon last year. = 我去年参加了马拉松比赛。
We participated in a fundraising event. = 我们参加了一个筹款活动。

现在完成时:
She has participated in several international conferences. = 她已经参加了几次国际会议。
They have participated in volunteer work for years. = 他们多年来一直参与志愿者工作。

过去完成时:
He had participated in many similar projects before. = 他之前已经参与过许多类似的项目。
They had participated in the planning stage of the project. = 他们在项目的规划阶段就已经参与了。

将来时:
I will participate in the upcoming conference. = 我将参加即将举行的会议。
They will participate in the competition next month. = 他们下个月将参加比赛。

虚拟语气:
If I had participated, I might have won. = 如果我参加了，我可能会赢。
I wish I had participated in the debate. = 我真希望我参加了那场辩论。

被动语态:
Many people were participated in the survey. = 许多人参与了这项调查。(注意：participate本身很少用于被动语态，此句略显不自然，更常用的表达是“Many people took part in the survey”)
The project was participated in by many volunteers. = 许多志愿者参与了这个项目。(同样，此句也略显不自然，更常用的表达是“Many volunteers participated in the project”)


疑问句:
Did you participate in the meeting? = 你参加了会议吗？
Will he participate in the game? = 他会参加比赛吗？

否定句:
He didn't participate in the project. = 他没有参与这个项目。
She did not participate in the discussion. = 她没有参与讨论。


PHRASE->
participate in = 参与…
participate actively = 积极参与
fully participate = 全力参与
participate enthusiastically = 热情参与
participate willingly = 乐意参与
