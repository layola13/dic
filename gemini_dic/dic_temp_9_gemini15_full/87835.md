hidage:/ˈhaɪdɪdʒ/| n. |隐匿；隐藏；税收；|A tax levied in Anglo-Saxon England based on the number of hides of land a person owned.  A hide was a unit of land measurement, traditionally enough to support one family.  The term can also refer to the act of hiding or concealing something. |例句：The king imposed a heavy hidage to fund his wars. 国王征收重税来资助他的战争。|近义词：tax, levy, concealment, hiding |反义词：revelation, disclosure |名词
