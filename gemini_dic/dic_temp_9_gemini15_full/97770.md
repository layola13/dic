injecting:/ɪnˈdʒɛktɪŋ/ | v. |注射；注入；引入 | The act of forcing a liquid or gas into something.  It can refer to medical procedures, adding substances to systems, or introducing elements into a discussion. |例句：The doctor is injecting the patient with medicine. 医生正在给病人注射药物。

例句：The artist is injecting vibrant colors into her painting. 艺术家正在她的画作中注入鲜艳的色彩。


近义词：inserting, introducing, administering

反义词：extracting, withdrawing, removing


USAGE->

He is injecting the insulin daily. = 他每天注射胰岛素。
They are injecting new ideas into the project. = 他们正在向项目中注入新的想法。

一般过去时:
She injected the dye into the fabric. = 她将染料注入织物中。

过去完成时:

词源:
injecting 源自于动词 inject，其词根来自拉丁语的 *in- (进入) + *iacere (投掷，扔)。  因此，inject 本身的意思就是“扔进去”，引申为“注射，注入”。  "-ing" 是现在分词后缀，表示动作正在进行。
