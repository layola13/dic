overdeepens:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpənz/| v. |加深过度；过度挖掘|To deepen excessively; to excavate beyond a desired or safe limit.  This typically refers to geological processes or human activities like mining or dredging. |例句：The river overdeepens during periods of heavy rainfall. = 大雨期间，河流过度加深。 The prolonged mining operations overdeepened the shafts, creating a significant safety hazard. = 长期采矿作业过度挖掘了矿井，造成了重大的安全隐患。|近义词：exaggerate, deepen excessively |反义词：shallow, fill in
