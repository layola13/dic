recuperates:/rɪˈkuːpəreɪts/| v.|恢复；康复；重新获得|To regain or recover something lost or spent; to return to a normal state of health, strength, or spirits after illness, injury, or loss.|例句：He is slowly recuperating from his surgery. 他正在慢慢地从手术中恢复。

例句：The company is recuperating its losses from last year.  该公司正在弥补去年的损失。


近义词：recover, regain, rehabilitate, convalesce|反义词：deteriorate, worsen
