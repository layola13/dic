euphemistically:/ˌjuːfəˈmɪstɪkli/ | adv. |委婉地，含蓄地 |Using euphemisms to express something unpleasant or embarrassing in a more gentle way.  |He euphemistically referred to his dismissal as "a restructuring of the department." 他委婉地称他的解雇为“部门的重组”。| indirectly, delicately, tactfully | bluntly, frankly, directly | 副词
