palpably:/ˈpæl.pə.bli/| adj. |明显的，易觉察的 |easily perceptible; readily apparent; obvious.  The tension in the room was palpably thick.  The relief was palpably evident on her face. |近义词：obviously, evidently, clearly, perceptibly |反义词：imperceptibly, subtly, vaguely


USAGE->
