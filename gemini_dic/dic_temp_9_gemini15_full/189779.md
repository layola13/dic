salutation:/ˌsæljuˈteɪʃən/| n. |敬辞；问候；致意 |A salutation is a greeting, typically at the beginning of a letter or speech.  It shows politeness and respect. For example, "Dear Mr. Smith" is a formal salutation. |例句：The letter began with a formal salutation. 这封信以正式的敬辞开头。|例句：He offered a friendly salutation to his colleagues. 他向同事们友好的打招呼。|近义词：greeting, welcome, address |反义词：farewell, goodbye
