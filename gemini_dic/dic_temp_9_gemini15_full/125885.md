muscology:/ˌmʌsˈkɒlədʒi/ | n. |苔藓学|The scientific study of mosses and liverworts.  It encompasses their taxonomy, morphology, physiology, ecology, and evolutionary relationships. |例句：Muscology is a fascinating field that reveals the hidden diversity of bryophytes. 苔藓学是一个引人入胜的领域，它揭示了苔藓植物隐藏的多样性。|例句：Researchers in muscology are working to understand the role of mosses in carbon sequestration. 苔藓学的研究人员正在努力了解苔藓在碳封存中的作用。|近义词：bryology|反义词:  (无明确反义词)|名词
