internecine:/ˌɪntərˈniːsaɪn/| adj. | 内部互相残杀的；自相残杀的 |  destructive to both sides involved; mutually destructive.  It refers to conflict or struggle within a group, organization, or nation, resulting in damage or destruction to all parties involved. |  The internecine struggle within the company led to its eventual bankruptcy. 公司内部的激烈斗争导致了它最终的破产。 The internecine war between the two factions weakened both.  两个派系之间的内战削弱了双方。 |  internal, intra-group, fratricidal | peaceful, harmonious, unified
