/ˈpriːtʃɑːrt/| n. |预先绘制的图表；预先制定的图表|A prechart is a chart or graph that is created before data is collected, often to guide data collection or analysis.  It might outline expected relationships or serve as a template.|例句：The researchers created a prechart to visualize the anticipated results of their experiment.  研究人员创建了一个预先绘制的图表来直观地显示他们实验的预期结果。


例句：The marketing team used a prechart to plan the presentation of their sales data. 营销团队使用预先绘制的图表来规划销售数据的演示。


近义词：template, model, outline|反义词：postchart (此词并非标准英语词汇)|名词
