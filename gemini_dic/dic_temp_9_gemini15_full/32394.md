chillest:/ˈtʃɪləst/| adj.|最轻松的，最酷的，最令人放松的|The superlative form of "chill," meaning extremely relaxed, calm, and cool. It often implies a desirable state of being.  The word carries a connotation of casualness and coolness.|例句：This is the chillest cafe in town. 这家咖啡馆是城里最轻松的。

例句：That was the chillest movie I've seen all year. 那是今年我看过的最酷的电影。


近义词：most relaxed, coolest, calmest, most laid-back|反义词：most stressed, most anxious, most tense|词性:形容词
