owls:/aʊlz/ | n. |猫头鹰 |A nocturnal bird of prey with large forward-facing eyes, a hooked beak, and typically silent flight.| 例句：Owls are known for their excellent night vision. 猫头鹰以其极佳的夜视能力而闻名。

The owls hooted throughout the night.  猫头鹰整夜都在叫唤。|近义词：night bird, raptor |反义词：None specifically, but could consider diurnal birds (birds active during the day) as a contrasting group.
