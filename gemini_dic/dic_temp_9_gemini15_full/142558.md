ostentatiously:/ˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃəsli/ | adj. |炫耀地；夸示地；卖弄地 | Showily and pretentiously designed to impress; attracting attention.  |例句：He ostentatiously displayed his expensive watch. 他炫耀地展示了他的昂贵手表。

例句：She ostentatiously ignored him. 她故意无视他。


近义词：showily, conspicuously, flauntingly, demonstratively


反义词：modestly, humbly, unassumingly


词源:  From Middle French *ostentatoire, from Latin ostentare "to display, show off," from ostendere "to show, exhibit," from ob- "in front of" + tendere "to stretch."
