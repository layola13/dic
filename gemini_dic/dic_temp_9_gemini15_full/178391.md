reconnect:/ˌriːkəˈnɛkt/| vt.|重新连接，重新联系|To connect again; to establish communication or a relationship again.|例句：I need to reconnect my printer to the computer. 我需要把我的打印机重新连接到电脑上。

例句：After years of silence, they finally reconnected.  多年沉默之后，他们最终重新取得联系。

近义词：rejoin, reunite, link up | 反义词：disconnect, sever, separate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconnects his phone to the wifi every morning. = 他每天早上都会把手机重新连接到wifi。
She reconnects with her old friends on social media. = 她通过社交媒体与老朋友重新取得联系。

一般过去时:
They reconnected after a long separation. = 他们经过长时间的分离后重新取得联系。
He reconnected the wires and the machine started working again. = 他重新连接了电线，机器又开始工作了。

现在完成时:
I have reconnected with my childhood best friend. = 我已经和儿时的最好的朋友重新取得联系了。
She has reconnected with her passion for painting. = 她已经重新燃起了对绘画的热情。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had reconnected all the equipment. = 演示之前，他已经重新连接了所有设备。
After they had reconnected, they felt much closer. = 他们重新取得联系后，感觉亲密多了。

将来时:
I will reconnect with my college friends next year. = 我明年将与我的大学朋友们重新取得联系。
He will reconnect the power supply after the maintenance. = 维修后他会重新连接电源。

虚拟语气:
If I could reconnect with my past self, I would do things differently. = 如果我能与过去的自己重新连接，我会做不同的事情。
I wish I could reconnect with my family more often. = 我希望我能更频繁地与家人联系。

被动语态:
The network was reconnected by the technician. = 网络由技术人员重新连接。
The power supply will be reconnected at 6 pm. = 电源将在下午6点重新连接。

疑问句:
Can you reconnect the device? = 你能重新连接设备吗？
Have they reconnected yet? = 他们重新连接了吗？

否定句:
I couldn't reconnect to the server. = 我无法重新连接到服务器。
They haven't reconnected since the argument. = 争吵后他们一直没有重新取得联系。
