outstrives:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪvz/| vt.|胜过；超过；优于|To surpass or exceed in performance, ability, or achievement.|例句：His ambition outstrives his talent.  他的雄心壮志超过了他的才能。|例句：The company outstrives its competitors in innovation. 这家公司在创新方面胜过竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, excel, outdo|反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outstrives his classmates in academics. = 他在学业上胜过他的同学们。
The company consistently outstrives its competitors in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上始终胜过竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outstrived her rivals in the race. = 她在比赛中胜过了她的对手。
Last year, the sales team outstrived their targets. = 去年，销售团队超额完成了目标。

现在完成时:
Our team has outstrived all expectations this year. = 我们团队今年超出了所有预期。
The company has outstrived its competitors in technological advancements. = 这家公司在技术进步方面已经超越了竞争对手。

过去完成时:
By the time he finished, he had outstrived all his previous records. = 到他完成的时候，他已经超越了他之前的所有记录。
They had outstrived their initial goals by a significant margin. = 他们已经大大超过了最初的目标。

将来时:
He believes he will outstrive his current limitations. = 他相信他会克服目前的局限。
We expect the new product to outstrive its predecessors in the market. = 我们预计新产品将在市场上超越其前身。

虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he might outstrive his rivals. = 如果他更努力工作，他可能会胜过他的对手。
I wish I could outstrive my fear of public speaking. = 我希望我能克服我对公开演讲的恐惧。

被动语态:
His achievements were outstrived by his ambition. = 他的成就被他的雄心壮志所超越。
The older model was quickly outstrived by the newer technology. = 老款很快就被新技术超越了。

疑问句:
Does his talent outstrive his ambition? = 他的才能是否超过了他的雄心壮志？
Will our new strategy outstrive the competition? = 我们的新战略会胜过竞争对手吗？

否定句:
He did not outstrive his brother in the competition. = 他在比赛中没有胜过他的兄弟。
The project failed to outstrive its budget constraints. = 该项目未能超出其预算限制。
