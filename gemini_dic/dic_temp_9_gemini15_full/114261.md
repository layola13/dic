maiming:/ˈmeɪmɪŋ/| n. |使残废；重伤；严重损害 |The act of inflicting serious injury, especially to a limb or body part, resulting in permanent damage or disfigurement.  |The maiming of the soldiers was a terrible consequence of the war.  士兵的残疾是战争的可怕后果。  The accident resulted in the maiming of his left hand. 事故导致他的左手残废。|crippling, disabling, injuring, wounding |healing, restoring, repairing |名词
