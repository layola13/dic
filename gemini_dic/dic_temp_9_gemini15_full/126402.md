myself:/maɪˈsɛlf/ | pron. |我自己，我本人 | Myself is a reflexive pronoun, referring back to the subject of the sentence. It emphasizes the subject's action or state.  | I hurt myself playing football. 我踢足球时伤到了自己。|  himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves |  (no direct antonym)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I teach myself Spanish. = 我自学西班牙语。
I make myself a cup of tea. = 我给自己泡了一杯茶。


一般过去时:
I hurt myself yesterday. = 我昨天伤到自己了。
She dressed herself quickly. = 她迅速地穿好了衣服。


现在完成时:
I have taught myself to play the guitar. = 我自学了吉他。
He has convinced himself that he is right. = 他已经说服自己他是对的。


过去完成时:
I had already prepared myself for the worst. = 我已经做好了最坏的打算。
She had dressed herself before I arrived. = 我到的时候她已经穿好衣服了。


将来时:
I will teach myself to code next year. = 我明年将自学编程。
She will make herself a sandwich later. = 她一会儿会给自己做个三明治。


虚拟语气:
If I were myself, I would apologize. = 如果我是我自己，我会道歉的。
I wish I could trust myself more. = 我希望我能更信任自己。


被动语态:
(Myself is rarely used in passive voice.)  无法直接用被动语态


疑问句:
(Myself is rarely used in questions directly as the subject.)  无法直接作为主语用疑问句


否定句:
I didn't hurt myself. = 我没有伤到自己。
She didn't blame herself. = 她没有责怪自己。


PHRASE->
by myself = 独自地
to myself = 自言自语地
for myself = 为我自己
in myself = 就其本身而言
of myself = 自发地
