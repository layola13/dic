kanu:/ˈkɑːnuː/| n. |独木舟；轻舟|A canoe is a light narrow boat propelled by a paddle.  独木舟是一种用桨推进的轻便狭窄的小船。|例句：We paddled the canoe across the lake. 我们划着独木舟穿过湖泊。|近义词：kayak, boat|反义词：large ship


USAGE->  This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs do.  Therefore, the grammatical structures shown in the example are not applicable.  Instead, we will demonstrate different ways to use "canoe" in a sentence.
