fosterers:/ˈfɒstərərz/| n. |养育者；抚养者|People who foster children; those who provide a temporary home for children in need.  They offer care and support while working with social services to find a permanent solution for the child.| 例句：The fosterers provided a loving and stable home for the child.  养育者为孩子提供了一个充满爱和稳定的家。|  The fosterers worked closely with the social worker to ensure the child's well-being. 养育者与社工密切合作，以确保孩子的福祉。|  近义词： guardians, caregivers, adoptive parents | 反义词： birth parents (in the context of fostering)
