self-instructor:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈstrʌktər/| n. |自学者；自学教材|A person who teaches themselves a subject or skill, or a teaching material designed for self-learning.|例句：He is a self-instructor in guitar. 他是位自学吉他的自学者。

例句：This self-instructor guide will help you learn programming. 这本自学教材会帮助你学习编程。

近义词：autodidact, self-learner|反义词：teacher, tutor|名词
