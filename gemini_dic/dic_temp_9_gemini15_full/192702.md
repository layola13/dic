scyther:/ˈsaɪðər/| n. v.|镰刀，收割机；用镰刀割 | A scyther is a tool used for cutting grass or crops, typically with a long curved blade.  It can also refer to the act of cutting with a scyther. |例句：The farmer used a scyther to harvest the wheat.  农夫用镰刀收割小麦。

The gardener scythed the long grass. 园丁用镰刀割长草。|近义词：reaper, mower, cutter|反义词：planter (反义词较为宽泛，因为是相反的动作)|名词，动词
