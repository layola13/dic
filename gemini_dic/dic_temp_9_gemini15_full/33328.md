chuffing:/ˈtʃʌfɪŋ/| adj.| 冒烟的；兴奋的；得意洋洋的 |  Expressing or feeling great pleasure or satisfaction; smug.  Also, making a chuffing sound (like a train).|

例句：

He was chuffing about his promotion.  = 他对自己的晋升感到非常得意。

The old train chuffed along the track, emitting clouds of smoke. = 老火车沿着轨道 chug chug 的前进，冒出滚滚浓烟。


近义词：pleased, delighted, smug, puffing (making a chuffing sound)

反义词：disappointed, unhappy, displeased


词源:

The word "chuffing" originates from the sound a steam train makes,  a repetitive puffing sound. This onomatopoeic origin then extended to describe the feeling of satisfaction or smugness associated with achieving something,  mirroring the self-satisfied puffing of a satisfied engine.
