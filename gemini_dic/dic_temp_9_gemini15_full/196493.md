serviceabilities:/ˌsɜːrvɪs əˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 服务能力；服务性；可服务性 |  The capabilities of providing service; the range of services that can be offered.  This word is relatively rare and often used in technical contexts.  It refers to the inherent ability or potential of something to perform services. | 例句：The company's serviceabilities were severely hampered by the power outage. = 该公司的服务能力因停电而严重受损。 The new software significantly expanded the serviceabilities of our customer support team. = 新软件大大扩展了我们客户支持团队的服务能力。 | 近义词：service capabilities, service potential, service capacity | 反义词：service inabilities, lack of service
