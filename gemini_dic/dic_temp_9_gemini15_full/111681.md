locke:/lɒk/| n.|洛克（人名）|John Locke (1632-1704), a highly influential English philosopher.  His works, particularly *Two Treatises of Government* and *An Essay Concerning Human Understanding*, significantly impacted the development of political philosophy and epistemology.  He is considered one of the most important thinkers of the Enlightenment. |John Locke's ideas on natural rights profoundly influenced the American Revolution.  约翰·洛克关于自然权利的思想深刻影响了美国革命。|近义词:philosopher, thinker|反义词: N/A |名词
