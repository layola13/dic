good-looking:/ˈɡʊdˈlʊkɪŋ/ | adj. |好看的，英俊的，美丽的 |Attractive in appearance; pleasing to the eye.  This adjective is used to describe someone who is considered physically attractive.  It is a more informal and less intense term than "beautiful" or "handsome".|例句：He is a good-looking young man. 他是一个英俊的年轻人。

例句：She's a good-looking woman with a warm smile. 她是一位笑容温暖的漂亮女人。


近义词：attractive, handsome, beautiful, pretty, appealing

反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is good-looking. = 他长得好看。
She is good-looking in that dress. = 她穿那件衣服很好看。


一般过去时:
He was good-looking when he was young. = 他年轻的时候很英俊。
She was good-looking even without makeup. = 她即使不化妆也很好看。


现在完成时:
He has always been good-looking. = 他一直都很英俊。
She has remained good-looking throughout the years. = 她多年来一直保持着美丽。


过去完成时:
He had always been good-looking, even in his childhood. = 他从小就长得好看。
She had been good-looking, but age had started to show. = 她以前很漂亮，但是现在开始显老了。


将来时:
He will still be good-looking in ten years. = 十年后他仍然会很帅。
She will likely be good-looking even when she is older. = 她即使年纪大了也可能依然漂亮。


虚拟语气:
If he were good-looking, he would have more opportunities. = 如果他长得好看，他就会有更多机会。
If she were good-looking, she wouldn't have to work so hard. = 如果她漂亮的话，她就不用这么努力工作了。


被动语态:
His good looks were admired by many. = 他的英俊容貌受到许多人的赞赏。(This is less common as "good-looking" is rarely used passively)
Her beauty is often commented on; she's considered good-looking. = 人们经常评论她的美貌；她被认为是好看的。(This uses a related concept passively)


疑问句:
Is he good-looking? = 他长得好看吗？
Is she good-looking in that new outfit? = 她穿那套新衣服好看吗？


否定句:
He isn't good-looking. = 他不好看。
She wasn't good-looking in that dress. = 她穿那件衣服不好看。
