ivy:/ˈaɪvi/| n.|常春藤|A climbing plant with evergreen leaves, often growing on walls and trees.  It is known for its tenacious clinging and ability to cover surfaces.  |例句：Ivy covered the old stone walls, creating a picturesque scene. 常春藤覆盖着古老的石墙，构成了一幅美丽的景象。|例句：The ivy grew so vigorously it damaged the building's structure. 常春藤生长得如此茂盛，以至于损坏了建筑物的结构。|近义词：creeper, vine|反义词：无|名词
