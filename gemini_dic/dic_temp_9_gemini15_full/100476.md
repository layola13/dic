intervariation:/ˌɪntərˌveəriˈeɪʃən/| n. |变异，相互变化|The process or result of variations occurring between different things or individuals, often within a population or group.  It emphasizes the reciprocal or interacting nature of the variations.|例句：The study examined the intervariation of gene expression levels between different cell types. 这项研究考察了不同细胞类型之间基因表达水平的相互变化。

例句：  The intervariation in beak size among Darwin's finches allowed them to exploit different food sources. 达尔文雀喙部大小的相互变化使它们能够利用不同的食物来源。


近义词：variation, fluctuation, difference, modification


反义词：uniformity, consistency, sameness


词性: 名词
