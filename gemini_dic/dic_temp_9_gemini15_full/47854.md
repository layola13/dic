deceitful: /dɪˈsiːtfl/ | adj. | 欺骗的；奸诈的；虚伪的 | Characterized by deceit; dishonest; fraudulent.  It implies a deliberate intention to mislead or deceive. |  例句：He was deceitful in his dealings with his business partners. 他在与生意伙伴的交易中很奸诈。

例句：Her deceitful smile hid her true intentions. 她虚伪的笑容掩盖了她的真实意图。


近义词：dishonest, fraudulent, deceptive, treacherous, underhanded, misleading, duplicitous

反义词：honest, truthful, genuine, sincere, forthright


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is deceitful in his promises. = 他在承诺方面很欺骗。
She is deceitful about her age. = 她对年龄撒谎。


一般过去时:
He was deceitful in his business dealings. = 他在生意往来中很奸诈。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
