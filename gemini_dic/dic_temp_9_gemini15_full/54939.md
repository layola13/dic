dispossessing: /ˌdɪspəˈzɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 剥夺；驱逐；使失去财产 | The act of depriving someone of possession of something, especially property or land.  It implies a forceful or unjust taking away. |例句：The landlords are dispossessing tenants without proper notice. = 房东们正在未经适当通知的情况下驱逐租户。

例句：The government's policies are dispossessing many farmers of their land. = 政府的政策正在剥夺许多农民的土地。


近义词：depriving, ousting, evicting, disinheriting | 反义词：empowering, enriching, bestowing, granting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are dispossessing the farmers of their ancestral lands. = 他们正在剥夺农民祖传土地的所有权。
The company is dispossessing its employees of their benefits. = 公司正在剥夺员工的福利。


一般过去时:
The king dispossessed the nobles of their titles. = 国王剥夺了贵族的爵位。
The hurricane dispossessed many people of their homes. = 飓风使许多人失去了家园。


现在完成时:
The government has dispossessed countless families of their homes. = 政府已经使无数家庭失去家园。
The war has dispossessed many people of their belongings. = 战争使许多人失去了他们的财物。


过去完成时:
They had dispossessed him of his inheritance before he could even claim it. = 在他甚至能够索要遗产之前，他们就已经剥夺了他的继承权。
The invaders had dispossessed the native population of their land for centuries. = 侵略者几个世纪以来一直剥夺当地居民的土地。


将来时:
The new law will dispossess many homeowners of their properties. = 新法将使许多房主失去房产。
They will dispossess him of his power if he continues to act recklessly. = 如果他继续鲁莽行事，他们将剥夺他的权力。


虚拟语气:
If the landlord dispossessed us, we would have nowhere to go. = 如果房东驱逐我们，我们将无处可去。
I wish the government hadn't dispossessed those families of their homes. = 我真希望政府没有让那些家庭失去家园。


被动语态:
Many people were dispossessed of their land during the war. = 战争期间，许多人被剥夺了土地。
He was dispossessed of his rightful inheritance by his greedy uncle. = 他被贪婪的叔叔剥夺了他应得的遗产。


疑问句:
Were the villagers dispossessed of their land by force? = 村民的土地是被强行剥夺的吗？
Will they be dispossessed of their rights if they don't comply? = 如果他们不遵守，他们的权利会被剥夺吗？


否定句:
The court refused to dispossess the tenant from his apartment. = 法院拒绝驱逐该租户离开他的公寓。
They did not dispossess her of her property, despite her many debts. = 尽管她负债累累，他们也没有剥夺她的财产。
