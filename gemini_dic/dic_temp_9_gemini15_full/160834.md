possessiveness: /pəˈzɛsɪvnəs/ | n. |占有欲，独占欲 |The feeling of wanting to possess or control something or someone.  It can range from a healthy desire for security and connection to an unhealthy obsession that can be damaging to relationships.|例句：Her possessiveness towards her boyfriend made him feel suffocated.  她的男友感到窒息，因为她太爱占有他了。


例句：His possessiveness over his antique car was legendary. 他对他的古董车的占有欲是出了名的。


近义词：jealousy, protectiveness, possessiveness, greed |反义词：generosity, unselfishness, detachment
