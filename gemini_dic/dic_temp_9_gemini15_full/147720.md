overpresumptuous: /ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptʃuəs/ | adj. | 过分自以为是的，过于自信的 | Excessively presumptuous; arrogant and assuming.  It implies going beyond the bounds of what is appropriate or acceptable in terms of confidence or assumption.  | Example: His overpresumptuous behavior at the interview cost him the job.  他面试时过于自以为是的行为让他失去了这份工作。| 近义词: arrogant, presumptuous, impertinent, audacious | 反义词: humble, modest, diffident, unassuming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overpresumptuous in his dealings with clients. = 他在与客户打交道时过于自以为是。
She is overpresumptuous in her assessment of the situation. = 她对局势的评估过于自信。


一般过去时:
He was overpresumptuous in his demands. = 他提出的要求过于自以为是。
She was overpresumptuous in claiming victory before the results were announced. = 结果公布之前她就过于自信地宣称胜利了。


现在完成时:
He has been overpresumptuous in his actions throughout this project. = 在整个项目中，他的行为都过于自以为是。
She has been overpresumptuous in her expectations of a quick promotion. = 她对快速晋升的期望过于自信。


过去完成时:
He had been overpresumptuous in thinking he could finish the work alone. = 他曾过于自信地认为自己能独自完成这项工作。
She had been overpresumptuous in assuming that everyone would agree with her. = 她曾过于自信地认为每个人都会同意她的意见。


将来时:
He will be overpresumptuous in his approach to the negotiations. = 他在谈判中的做法将会过于自以为是。
She will be overpresumptuous if she expects to win easily. = 如果她期望轻松获胜，那将会过于自信。


虚拟语气:
If he were less overpresumptuous, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么自以为是，他就会有更多朋友。
If she hadn't been so overpresumptuous, she wouldn't have made such a mistake. = 如果她不那么自以为是，她就不会犯这样的错误。


被动语态:
His behavior was considered overpresumptuous by the committee. = 委员会认为他的行为过于自以为是。
Her claims were seen as overpresumptuous and were rejected. = 她的说法被认为过于自信，因此被拒绝了。


疑问句:
Was his request overpresumptuous? = 他的请求是否过于自以为是？
Isn't it overpresumptuous to assume you'll succeed without effort? = 不努力就假设你会成功，这难道不是过于自信吗？


否定句:
His actions were not overpresumptuous, just confident. = 他的行为并非过于自以为是，只是自信。
Her approach was not overpresumptuous; it was simply assertive. = 她的方法并非过于自以为是，只是比较果断。

词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "excessive" or "too much"
* **presumptuous**:  adjective meaning "assuming too much; arrogant"  Derived from the Latin *praesumere* ("to assume beforehand").
