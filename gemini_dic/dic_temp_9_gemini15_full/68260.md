faithlessnesses: /ˈfeɪθləsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 不忠行为；背叛行为；失信行为 | Plural of faithlessness;  instances or acts of being unfaithful or disloyal.  It refers to multiple occurrences of betrayal, dishonesty, or a breach of trust. |


例句：

His numerous faithlessnesses led to the collapse of his marriage. = 他多次的不忠行为导致了他的婚姻破裂。

The company's repeated faithlessnesses to its customers damaged its reputation. = 公司反复背叛客户的行为损害了其声誉。


近义词：treacheries, betrayals, infidelities, deceptions


反义词：loyalties, fidelities, faithfulnesses


词性：名词
