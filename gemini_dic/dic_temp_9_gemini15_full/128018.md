nepotism: /ˈnepəˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 裙带关系；任人唯亲 | The practice among those with power or influence of favoring relatives or friends, especially by giving them jobs.  | Example:  The CEO's nepotism was evident in the hiring of his unqualified son.  首席执行官的任人唯亲在其聘用不合格的儿子时显而易见。

The company's promotion policy is riddled with nepotism, leading to resentment among employees. 这家公司的晋升政策充满了裙带关系，导致员工不满。|近义词：favoritism, cronyism | 反义词：meritocracy, impartiality
