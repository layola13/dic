self-judgement:/ˈsɛlfˈdʒʌdʒmənt/| n. |自我评判，自我评价|The act of judging oneself; assessment of one's own actions, character, or abilities.  It can be positive (self-affirmation) or negative (self-criticism).|例句：Her self-judgement was harsh; she constantly criticized her own shortcomings.  她的自我评判很苛刻；她不断批评自己的缺点。|例句：Positive self-judgement can boost confidence and improve mental wellbeing. 正面的自我评判可以增强自信并改善心理健康。|近义词：self-assessment, self-evaluation, self-criticism (negative aspect), self-affirmation (positive aspect)|反义词：objective judgment, external evaluation|名词
