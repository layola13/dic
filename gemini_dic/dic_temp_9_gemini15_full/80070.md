goalscoring: /ˈɡoʊlˌskɔːrɪŋ/ | n. |进球得分；得分能力| The act of scoring goals in a sport, especially soccer or hockey.  It can also refer to the ability or skill of scoring goals. |例句：His goalscoring record is impressive.  他的进球纪录令人印象深刻。

例句：The team's goalscoring prowess was evident in their 5-0 victory.  该队的进球能力在他们5-0的胜利中显而易见。


近义词：scoring, goal-getting

反义词：goal prevention, conceding


词源: goal (目标，球门) + scoring (得分)  The word is a compound noun formed by combining "goal" and "scoring," reflecting the direct action of putting the ball into the goal.
