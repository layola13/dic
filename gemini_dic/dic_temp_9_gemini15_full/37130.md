come down with:/kʌm daʊn wɪð/| phrasal verb|患病，染上（疾病）|To become ill with a particular disease.  This phrase typically refers to a relatively minor illness.|I'm coming down with a cold. = 我感冒了。
I think I'm coming down with the flu. = 我觉得我得了流感。


近义词：catch, contract, develop, get


反义词：recover from, get over


词性：phrasal verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes down with a cold every winter. = 他每年冬天都感冒。
She comes down with allergies during spring. = 春天她就会过敏。


一般过去时:
I came down with the flu last week. = 我上星期得了流感。
He came down with a bad cough after the concert. = 音乐会后，他得了严重的咳嗽。


现在完成时:
She has come down with a stomach ache this morning. = 她今天早上肚子疼。
They have come down with chicken pox. = 他们得了水痘。


过去完成时:
Before the exam, he had come down with a fever. = 考试前，他发烧了。
She had come down with a headache before the meeting. = 会议前她就头痛了。


将来时:
I think I will come down with something if I don't get enough sleep. = 如果我不睡够觉，我想我会生病的。
If she keeps working so hard, she will come down with stress. = 如果她继续这么努力工作，她会压力过大的。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't gone out in the rain, I wouldn't have come down with a cold. = 如果我没有冒雨出去，就不会感冒了。
If she had stayed home, she wouldn't have come down with the flu. = 如果她待在家里，她就不会得流感了。


被动语态: (This phrase is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are you coming down with something? = 你是不是哪里不舒服？
Is he coming down with the measles? = 他是不是得了麻疹？


否定句:
I didn't come down with anything after the trip. = 旅行后我什么病都没得。
She didn't come down with the flu despite the cold weather. = 尽管天气寒冷，她也没得流感。


PHRASE->
come down with a cold = 感冒
come down with the flu = 得流感
come down with a fever = 发烧
come down with a cough = 咳嗽
come down with a headache = 头痛
