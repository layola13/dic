dosel: /ˈdoʊzəl/ | n. |睡帽；头巾| A dosel is a kind of headdress, often a hood or covering for the head,  especially one worn to bed.  It can also refer to a canopy or cover, particularly one over a bed or altar.|例句：She wore a silken dosel to bed. = 她睡觉时戴着丝绸睡帽。  The altar was adorned with a rich dosel. = 祭坛上装饰着华美的帷幔。|近义词：hood, head covering, canopy, veil|反义词：None specifically, but words implying uncovered head are antonymous in context.
