harbours:/ˈhɑːbərz/| n. |港口；避风港；藏匿处 |A place on the coast where ships can find shelter, especially one protected from the wind and waves.|The ship sailed into the harbour. 这艘船驶进了港口。|近义词：port, dock, haven|反义词：open sea


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city harbours a thriving arts scene.  = 这个城市拥有繁荣的艺术景象。
She harbours deep-seated resentment towards him. = 她对他怀有根深蒂固的怨恨。


一般过去时:
The pirate ship harboured in the hidden cove. = 海盗船停泊在隐蔽的海湾。
He harboured a secret hope for reconciliation. = 他怀有一个和解的秘密希望。


现在完成时:
The harbour has sheltered countless vessels over the years. = 多年来，港口已庇护了无数船只。
She has harboured doubts about his honesty. = 她一直怀疑他的诚实。


过去完成时:
They had harboured the fugitive for weeks before the police arrived. = 警察到来之前，他们已经藏匿了那个逃犯数周。
The country had harboured resentment against its former colonial power for generations. = 这个国家几代人都对它以前的殖民统治者怀有怨恨。


将来时:
The new harbour will harbour larger ships. = 新港口将容纳更大的船只。
He will harbour no ill feelings towards his rivals. = 他不会对他的对手怀有恶意。


虚拟语气:
If the harbour were larger, it could accommodate more ships. = 如果港口更大，它就能容纳更多船只。
I wish I hadn't harboured such negative thoughts. = 我真希望我没有怀有如此负面的想法。


被动语态:
The fugitive was harboured by a sympathetic family. = 那个逃犯得到一个同情他的家庭的庇护。
The harbour is being dredged to accommodate larger ships. = 港口正在进行疏浚以容纳更大的船只。


疑问句:
Does the harbour offer any protection from the storm? = 港口能提供任何抵御风暴的保护吗？
Does she harbour any secrets from her family? = 她对家人隐瞒了什么秘密吗？


否定句:

He harboured no malice towards his former friends. = 他对以前的朋友没有恶意。
