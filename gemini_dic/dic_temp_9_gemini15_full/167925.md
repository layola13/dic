print-out:/ˈprɪntˌaʊt/| n. |打印输出，打印件 |A print-out is a hard copy of a document produced by a printer.  It refers to the physical output generated from a digital document or data.| Example: I need a print-out of that report by tomorrow morning.  我明天早上需要这份报告的打印件。|近义词：hard copy, printout, output|反义词：digital copy, electronic file
