chosen: /ˈtʃoʊzn/ | adj. | 被选择的，当选的，精选的 |  Past participle of choose.  Something that has been selected or decided upon.  It implies a deliberate act of selection from a range of options. |


例句：

The chosen candidate will be announced next week. = 下周将宣布当选的候选人。

She carefully chose the perfect dress for the occasion. = 她仔细挑选了适合这次场合的完美礼服。


近义词：selected, elected, picked, preferred


反义词：rejected, refused, unwanted, overlooked


词性：形容词
