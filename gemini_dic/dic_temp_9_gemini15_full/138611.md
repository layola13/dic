nuclear:/ˈnjuːkliər/ | adj., n. | 核的；核子的；原子核的；中心的；关键的 | Relating to the nucleus of an atom, especially concerning the release of atomic energy.  The term can also refer to something central or crucial. |例句：Nuclear energy is a controversial source of power. 核能是一种有争议的能源。

例句：The nuclear family is composed of a mother, father, and their children.  核心家庭由父母和他们的孩子组成。


近义词：atomic, atomic energy(n.), central, crucial, pivotal | 反义词：peripheral, outer, insignificant
