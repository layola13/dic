juries:/ˈdʒʊəriːz/| n. |陪审团；评审团 |A jury is a group of people sworn to give a verdict in a legal case based on the evidence presented to them.  Juries are a cornerstone of the justice system in many countries, ensuring that legal decisions are made by a representative sample of the community.| Example: The jury deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict. 陪审团经过数小时的商议后才做出裁决。  The jury found the defendant guilty. 陪审团裁定被告有罪。| panels, tribunals |  --- | n.
