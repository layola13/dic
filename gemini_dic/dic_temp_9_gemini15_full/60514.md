elaborateness:/ɪˈlæbərətəs/| adj. | 精细；复杂；详尽 |  The elaborateness of the design is breathtaking.  (设计的精细令人叹为观止。)  The elaborateness of the explanation confused me. (解释的详尽让我困惑。) | 近义词：complexity, intricacy, detail, sophistication | 反义词：simplicity, plainness
