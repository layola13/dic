scuffling:/ˈskʌflɪŋ/| n.| 喧闹；争吵；拖着脚走；|The act of scuffling involves moving with short, quick steps, often making a shuffling sound. It can also refer to a disorderly fight or struggle.|例句：The children were scuffling in the playground.孩子们在操场上互相推搡。

例句：I heard a scuffling sound outside my window.我听到窗外传来一阵拖着脚走的声音。|近义词：scrambling, shuffling, struggling, fighting|反义词：walking calmly, gliding
