deconfliction:/ˌdiːkənˈflɪkʃən/| n. |冲突消除；矛盾解决；协调；|The process of identifying and resolving conflicts, especially between military operations or plans.  It aims to prevent unintended clashes or negative interactions between different entities or activities.|例句：The deconfliction process ensured that both military forces avoided accidental engagement.  冲突消除过程确保了双方军队避免了意外冲突。|近义词：reconciliation, coordination, resolution|反义词：escalation, conflict, aggravation|名词
