emptiest: /ˈɛmptɪəst/ | adj. | 最空的；最空虚的 |  The superlative form of "empty," indicating the greatest degree of emptiness.  It describes something that contains absolutely nothing or lacks any significant content, whether physical or emotional. |例句：The emptiest room in the house was the spare bedroom. =  房子里最空的一间是备用卧室。

The emptiest feeling was the profound loneliness after her move. =搬家后最空虚的感觉是深深的孤独。


近义词：most empty, voidest, vacuous


反义词：fullest, most crowded, most replete


词性：形容词
