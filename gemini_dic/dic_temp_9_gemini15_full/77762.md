genealogies:/ˌdʒiːniˈælədʒiz/ | n. | 家谱；系谱；宗谱 | Accounts of the descent of persons from their ancestors; family history.  | Example:  The genealogies of royal families are often complex and meticulously documented. 皇室家族的家谱通常复杂且记录详尽。

The study of genealogies revealed a surprising connection between the two families.  对家谱的研究揭示了这两个家族之间令人惊讶的联系。


近义词：family trees, lineages, pedigrees

反义词：None (no direct opposite)
