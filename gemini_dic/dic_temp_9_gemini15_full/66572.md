exhilarated: /ɪɡˈzɪləreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 兴高采烈的；精神焕发的 | Feeling or expressing great happiness and excitement.  |例句：She felt exhilarated after her successful presentation. 她成功演示后感到非常兴奋。

例句：The breathtaking view from the mountaintop left them exhilarated. 山顶令人惊叹的景色让他们感到兴高采烈。


近义词：thrilled, elated, delighted, invigorated, stimulated

反义词：depressed, dejected, disheartened, gloomy, despondent

词性：形容词
