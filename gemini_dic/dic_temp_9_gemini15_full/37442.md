commiserates:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/| v.|同情；怜悯；表示慰问|To express sympathy or feel pity for someone's misfortune.  It implies sharing the feeling of sorrow or distress.|例句：I commiserated with her on the loss of her pet. 我对她失去宠物表示慰问。

例句：He commiserated with his friend who had failed the exam. 他同情他的朋友考试不及格。


近义词：sympathize, empathize, console, condole|反义词：rejoice, celebrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commiserates with those who are suffering. 他同情那些受苦的人。
She commiserates with her friend about his breakup. 她同情她朋友的分手。


一般过去时:
They commiserated with the victims of the flood. 他们同情洪水中的受害者。
I commiserated with him after he lost his job. 他失业后我向他表示同情。


现在完成时:
She has commiserated with many people throughout her career.  她在职业生涯中曾向许多人表示过同情。
We have commiserated with each other over our shared misfortunes. 我们互相同情彼此共同的不幸。


过去完成时:
They had commiserated with her before she left for the hospital. 在她去医院之前，他们已经向她表示了同情。
He had commiserated with his colleague before the news broke. 在消息传出之前，他已经对他同事表示了同情。


将来时:
I will commiserate with him when I see him. 我见到他时会向他表示慰问。
She will commiserate with her friend over the difficult situation. 她会同情她朋友的困境。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would commiserate with him deeply. 如果我是他，我会深深地同情他。
I wish I could have commiserated with her sooner. 我希望我能早点向她表示慰问。


被动语态:
His misfortune was commiserated by many. 许多人同情他的不幸。
The victims were commiserated with by the whole community.  整个社区都同情受害者。


疑问句:
Does she commiserate with those less fortunate? 她同情那些不幸的人吗？
Did they commiserate with the grieving family? 他们是否同情那个悲伤的家庭？


否定句:
He didn't commiserate with his rival's loss. 他没有同情对手的失败。
She refused to commiserate with him about his problems. 她拒绝同情他的问题。


PHRASE->
commiserate with someone  与某人同情
commiserate over something  为某事表示同情
commiserate on something  对某事表示同情
express commiseration 表示同情
offer commiseration 提供慰问

词源:
From Middle French *commisérer, from Latin commiserāri "to pity, share pity," from com- "together" + miserāri "to feel pity," from miser "wretched."
