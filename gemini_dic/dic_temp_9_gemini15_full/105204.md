keying-in: /ˈkiːɪŋ ɪn/ | v. |键入，输入|The act of entering data into a computer or other electronic device using a keyboard.  It implies a process of carefully and deliberately inputting information, often one item at a time.|例句：I spent the afternoon keying in the sales figures. 我整个下午都在输入销售数据。

例句：She's keying in the addresses into the database.  她在将地址输入数据库。


近义词：typing, entering, inputting


反义词：outputting, extracting


词性: Verb
