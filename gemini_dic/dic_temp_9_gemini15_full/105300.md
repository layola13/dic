kibbles:/ˈkɪbəlz/| n.|狗粮，小块食物|Small pieces of food, especially dry dog or cat food.|例句：The dog eagerly gobbled up its kibbles.  这只狗贪婪地吃掉了它的狗粮。|例句：I need to buy more kibbles for my cat. 我需要为我的猫买更多的猫粮。|近义词：bits, pellets, chunks|反义词：whole food (as an antonym it's more of a contrasting concept)|名词
