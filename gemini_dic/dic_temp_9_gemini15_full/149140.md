oxidized:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzd/ /ˈɑːksɪdeɪtɪd/| adj. |氧化的；被氧化的|Combined with oxygen; having undergone oxidation.  The process of oxidation often involves a loss of electrons by one atom and a gain of electrons by another atom. This can lead to a change in color, smell, or other properties of the substance.|例句：The iron had oxidized and turned rusty.  铁已经氧化生锈了。

The apple slices oxidized quickly after being cut. 切开的苹果片很快氧化了。


近义词：rusted, corroded, tarnished|反义词：reduced
