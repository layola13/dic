hydrodynamometer:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/| n. |水动力测力计|A device used to measure the force or power of a fluid in motion, often used to test the performance of propellers, turbines, or pumps.|例句：The engineers used a hydrodynamometer to test the efficiency of the new propeller design. 工程师们用一台水动力测力计来测试新型螺旋桨设计的效率。

例句：The hydrodynamometer recorded a significant increase in power output after the modifications were made.  修改后，水动力测力计记录到功率输出显著增加。


近义词：fluid dynamometer, power meter


反义词：无


词性:名词
