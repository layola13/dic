outglows:/ˈaʊtɡloʊz/| v.|超越的光芒，胜过；发出比…更强烈的光|To shine or glow more brightly than something else; to surpass in brilliance or splendor.|The newly installed LED lights outglow the old incandescent bulbs. 新安装的LED灯比旧的白炽灯更亮。 The diamond's brilliance outglows all other jewels. 这颗钻石的光芒胜过所有其他宝石。|近义词：outshine, outshine, surpass|反义词：dim, fade


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun outglows the moon. = 太阳的光芒胜过月亮。
This star outglows all others in the constellation. = 这颗星的光芒胜过星座中的所有其他星星。


一般过去时:
The fireworks outglow the city lights. = 烟花的光芒胜过城市灯光。
Her beauty outglowed everyone else at the party. = 她的美丽胜过派对上其他所有人。


现在完成时:
The new technology has outglowed the old methods. = 新技术已经超越了旧方法。
His talent has outglowed his peers. = 他的才能已经超越了他的同龄人。


过去完成时:
The ancient civilization's achievements had outglowed all others of its time. = 古代文明的成就已经超越了当时的所有其他文明。
The performance had outglowed all expectations. = 表演已经超出了所有预期。


将来时:
The new stadium lights will outglow the old ones. = 新体育场灯将比旧灯更亮。
His success will outglow his past failures. = 他的成功将超越他过去的失败。


虚拟语气:
If the sun were to disappear, the moon would still outglow the stars. = 如果太阳消失了，月亮仍然会比星星更亮。
If he had worked harder, his success would have outglowed his rivals'. = 如果他更努力工作，他的成功就会超越他的竞争对手。


被动语态:
The old system was outglowed by the new one. = 旧系统被新系统超越了。
His work was outglowed by his colleague's. = 他的作品被同事的作品超越了。


疑问句:

Will her performance outglow the others? = 她的表演会胜过其他人吗？


否定句:
The small candle didn't outglow the bright lamp. = 小蜡烛的光芒没有胜过明亮的灯。
His efforts didn't outglow his opponent's. = 他的努力没有胜过他的对手。
