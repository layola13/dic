call it quits:/kɔːl ɪt kwɪts/| phrasal verb| 停止；放弃；结束；罢手 |To agree to end something, such as an argument, a relationship, or a project.  It implies mutual agreement to stop. |例句：After hours of arguing, they finally called it quits.  几小时的争论后，他们终于决定停止争吵。

例句：Let's call it quits for today; we can continue tomorrow. 今天就到此为止吧，我们明天继续。


近义词：give up, stop, cease, terminate, abandon


反义词：continue, persevere, persist


PHRASE->
call it a day:  收工；结束一天的工作
call it even:  扯平；两不相欠
call it quits on:  对…不再继续
call time on:  对…宣告结束
call the shots:  说了算；拍板决定
