empath:/ˈɛmpæθ/ | n. |移情者；有同理心的人 |A person who has the ability to understand and share the feelings of another.  Empaths are highly sensitive to the emotions of others and can often feel what others are feeling. | Example:  She's such an empath; she always knows how I'm feeling before I even say anything. 她是一个非常有同理心的人；在我开口之前她就总是知道我的感受。|近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person |反义词：sociopath, psychopath |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an empath. = 他是一个有同理心的人。
She empathizes with her patients. = 她对她的病人感同身受。


一般过去时:
He was an empath, always understanding others' feelings. = 他是一个有同理心的人，总是理解别人的感受。
She empathized with the grieving family. = 她同情那个悲伤的家庭。


现在完成时:
She has always been an empath. = 她一直以来都很有同理心。
He has empathized with many people throughout his life. = 他一生中同情过很多人。


过去完成时:
I had never met an empath before. = 我以前从未遇到过一个有同理心的人。
She had empathized with the situation before offering help. = 在提供帮助之前，她已经体会到了这种情况。


将来时:
He will be a great empath. = 他将成为一个伟大的移情者。
She will empathize with the challenges faced by refugees. = 她将体会到难民面临的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If I were an empath, I would understand her better. = 如果我有同理心，我会更了解她。
I wish I could empathize with his pain. = 我希望我能体会到他的痛苦。


被动语态:
His empathy was appreciated by everyone. = 大家都欣赏他的同理心。(被动语态在描述“empath”本身时较少使用)
The situation was empathized with by many. = 许多人都对这种情况感同身受。(被动语态更常用于描述“empathy”这个行为)


疑问句:
Is he an empath? = 他是一个有同理心的人吗？
Does she empathize with others easily? = 她很容易与他人产生共鸣吗？


否定句:
He is not an empath. = 他不是一个有同理心的人。
She didn't empathize with his situation. = 她对他的处境没有感同身受。


PHRASE->
high empathy  高度的同理心
emotional empathy 情绪上的同理心
cognitive empathy 认知上的同理心
lack of empathy  缺乏同理心
empathic response 同理心的反应
