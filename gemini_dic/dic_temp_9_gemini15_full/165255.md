preinsulate:/ˌpriːˈɪnsjuleɪt/| v.|预先隔热，预先保温|To insulate (something) in advance or before other work is done.|The builder decided to preinsulate the walls before installing the drywall.  建筑商决定在安装石膏板之前预先给墙壁隔热。|近义词：insulate in advance, pre-insulate|反义词：de-insulate

We preinsulate our homes to save energy. = 我们预先给房屋保温以节约能源。
