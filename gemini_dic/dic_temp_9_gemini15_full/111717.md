locomotion:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃən/| n. | 运动；移动；行动 | The act or power of moving from one place to another.  It refers to the movement of an organism or a part of an organism. |例句：The study of animal locomotion is fascinating. 动物运动学的研究令人着迷。

例句：Birds have mastered the art of locomotion in the air. 鸟类已经掌握了在空中运动的技巧。


近义词：movement, motion, transit, travel | 反义词：stillness, immobility, inertia | 词性：名词
