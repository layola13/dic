pursuer:/pərˈsuːər/| n. |追赶者；追求者；追捕者 |Someone who pursues someone or something.  A pursuer is someone who is chasing after something or someone, often with determination and persistence. This could be in a literal sense, like a police officer pursuing a criminal, or in a figurative sense, like someone pursuing their dreams. |例句：The police pursued the suspect for miles.  警察追捕嫌疑犯数英里。
The determined pursuer finally caught his prey. 那个执着的追赶者最终抓住了猎物。|近义词：chaser, follower, hunter |反义词：pursued (as in the person being pursued)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police officer pursues the thief. = 警察追捕小偷。
She pursues her dream of becoming a doctor. = 她追求成为一名医生的梦想。

一般过去时:
The hunter pursued the deer into the forest. = 猎人追赶鹿进入森林。
He pursued his studies diligently. = 他勤奋地学习。

现在完成时:
They have pursued this matter for years. = 他们已经追究此事多年了。
She has pursued her passion for painting throughout her life. = 她一生都在追求她对绘画的热情。

过去完成时:

He had pursued a career in finance before changing to teaching. = 他在转行教书之前曾在金融领域工作。

将来时:
We will pursue this issue further next week. = 我们下周将进一步讨论这个问题。
She will pursue her legal rights. = 她将维护自己的合法权益。

虚拟语气:
