overmast: /ˈoʊvərmæst/ | n. |超载的桅杆；（船只）桅杆过高|An overmast is a mast that is too tall or heavy for the ship it is on, potentially causing instability and danger.  It refers to a situation where the mast's height or weight exceeds the ship's structural capacity or the safe operational limits. This can result in reduced stability and increase the risk of capsizing. |The ship's overmast contributed to its capsizing during the storm. = 船的桅杆过高导致其在风暴中倾覆。 The excessive weight of the overmast made the vessel unstable. = 桅杆过重使得船舶不稳定。| topheavy, overloaded | well-balanced, stable | n.
