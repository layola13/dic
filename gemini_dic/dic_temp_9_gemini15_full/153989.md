perfidiously:/pərˈfɪdiəsli/| adv.|背信地；不忠地；奸诈地 | acting in a treacherous or deceitful way; in a way that shows a betrayal of trust.  |例句：He perfidiously betrayed his friends to the enemy. 他背叛朋友投向敌人。

例句：The general perfidiously abandoned his troops during the retreat.将军在撤退期间背信弃义地抛弃了他的部队。


近义词：treacherously, deceitfully, dishonestly, faithlessly, traitorously

反义词：faithfully, honestly, loyally, truthfully


词源：源自拉丁语 *perfidia （背叛，不忠），由 *per- (完全) + *fides (信任) 组成。  "-iously" 是副词后缀。
