estimation:/ˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/| n. |估计，估算，评价|The act of forming an opinion or judgment about something; a judgment or opinion about something.  It can also refer to the respect or admiration one has for someone or something.|例句：The estimation of the project's cost was inaccurate.  项目的成本估计不准确。

例句：Her estimation of the situation was remarkably accurate. 她对局势的估计非常准确。|近义词：calculation, appraisal, assessment, evaluation, judgment|反义词：precision (in the sense of exact measurement), fact (in the sense of confirmed truth)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My estimation of the damage is quite high. = 我对损失的估计相当高。
His estimation of the situation is based on his experience. = 他对局势的估计是基于他的经验。


一般过去时:
Their estimation of the task was too optimistic. = 他们对任务的估计过于乐观。
The initial estimation of the project’s timeline was wrong. = 项目时间表的初步估算是不正确的。


现在完成时:
We have completed the estimation of the project budget. = 我们已经完成了项目预算的估算。
She has revised her estimation of the costs several times. = 她已经多次修改了她对成本的估计。


过去完成时:
They had underestimated the complexity of the problem before starting. = 在开始之前，他们低估了问题的复杂性。
By the time the project ended, their estimation of the total costs had proved to be accurate. = 到项目结束时，他们对总成本的估计已被证明是准确的。


将来时:
We will finalize the estimation of the project by next week. = 我们将在下周完成该项目的估算。
They will provide a more accurate estimation after further investigation. = 他们将在进一步调查后提供更准确的估计。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better estimation of the time required, I would have planned better. = 如果我对所需时间的估计更好，我会计划得更好。
I wish I had a clearer estimation of the risks involved. = 我希望我对所涉及的风险有更清晰的估计。


被动语态:
The cost of the project was underestimated. = 项目成本被低估了。
The final estimation was presented to the board. = 最终估算结果已提交给董事会。


疑问句:
What is your estimation of the market share? = 你对市场份额的估计是多少？
What was the initial estimation of the repair costs? = 维修成本的初步估算是多少？


否定句:
My estimation of the time needed was incorrect. = 我对所需时间的估计是不正确的。
Their estimation of the risks did not take into account unforeseen circumstances. = 他们的风险评估没有考虑到不可预见的情况。
