headcase:/ˈhɛdkeɪs/| n. |疯子，神经病，古怪的人 |A headcase is someone who is considered to be mentally unstable or erratic in behavior.  They might be unpredictable, emotionally volatile, or have difficulty functioning in a normal social context. The term is often used informally and can be considered derogatory. |例句：He's a total headcase; you should avoid him. 他是个十足的疯子；你应该避开他。


He's acting like a headcase after the breakup. 分手后他行为举止像个疯子。|近义词：lunatic, maniac, psycho, crazy, nutcase |反义词：sane, rational, composed, level-headed
