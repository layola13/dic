planching: /ˈplæntʃɪŋ/ | n. |模板；地板；铺设地板 |The act of laying planks, or the planks laid.  It often refers to temporary flooring or a platform made of planks. |例句：The builders were busy with the planching before laying the final floor. 建筑工人正在铺设地板前的模板。

The planching supported the heavy equipment during construction.  施工期间，模板支撑着重型设备。|近义词：flooring, platforming, decking |反义词：removal of flooring
