migration:/maɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. | 迁移；迁徙；移居 | The movement of people or animals from one place to another, especially to find a new home or better living conditions.  | Example: The mass migration of people from rural areas to cities is a major social trend. 大量人口从农村地区迁移到城市是一种主要的社会趋势。  The annual migration of birds to warmer climates is a fascinating natural phenomenon. 鸟类每年迁徙到气候温暖的地方是一种令人着迷的自然现象。|近义词：emigration, immigration, relocation | 反义词：immigration (in the context of a specific location), settlement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds migrate south for the winter. = 鸟类冬天向南迁徙。
Many people migrate to find better job opportunities. = 许多人为了寻找更好的工作机会而迁移。


一般过去时:
Our family migrated to Canada in 1990. = 我们家在1990年移居加拿大。
The butterflies migrated across the ocean. = 蝴蝶飞越了海洋。


现在完成时:
Millions of people have migrated from Africa to Europe. = 数百万非洲人已经迁移到欧洲。
They have migrated to the coast for the summer. = 他们已经迁移到海边过夏天了。


过去完成时:
The tribe had migrated several times before settling in this valley. = 部落在定居在这个山谷之前已经迁移过几次了。
Before the war, they had migrated to the countryside. = 在战争爆发之前，他们已经迁移到农村了。


将来时:
We will migrate to a warmer climate when we retire. = 我们退休后将迁移到气候更温暖的地方。
The company plans to migrate its data to the cloud next year. = 公司计划明年将其数据迁移到云端。


虚拟语气:
If they migrated earlier, they would have found better housing. = 如果他们早点迁移，他们就能找到更好的住房。
I wish I had migrated sooner. = 我真希望我早点迁移。


被动语态:
The data was migrated to the new server. = 数据被迁移到新的服务器。
Many animals are migrated by environmental changes. = 许多动物的迁移是由环境变化引起的。


疑问句:
Why did they migrate to that region? = 为什么他们迁移到那个地区？
When do these birds migrate? = 这些鸟什么时候迁徙？


否定句:

They haven't migrated yet. = 他们还没有迁移。
