It often implies a degree of freedom within the confines.  | Example: The patient was placed under semiconfinement, allowed to walk in the hospital garden but not leave the grounds.  病人被限制在半禁闭状态，允许在医院花园散步，但不能离开医院范围。|近义词：partial restriction, limited confinement, restricted movement |反义词：full confinement, total imprisonment, unrestricted freedom |名词
