crocus:/ˈkroʊkəs/| n. |番红花，藏红花；番红花属植物|A crocus is a small plant with cup-shaped flowers, often purple, yellow, or white, that blooms in early spring.  It is also the name for the spice derived from its dried stigmas.|The crocuses bloomed early this year.今年番红花开得早。|The garden was ablaze with colorful crocuses. 花园里盛开着五彩缤纷的番红花。| daffodil, snowdrop |  |noun
