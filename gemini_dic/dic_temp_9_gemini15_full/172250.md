pusley: /ˈpʌsli/ | n. |马齿苋| A low-growing, succulent annual plant with small, reddish flowers and spoon-shaped leaves; also known as common purslane.  It is often considered a weed but is also edible. |例句：The garden was overrun with pusley. 花园里长满了马齿苋。|  例句：We used the pusley in a salad. 我们把马齿苋做成了沙拉。|近义词：purslane, portulaca|反义词:|
