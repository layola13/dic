plight:/plaɪt/| n.|困境，窘境，危险境地|a dangerous, difficult, or otherwise unfortunate situation.|The plight of the refugees is desperate. 难民的困境非常危急。|近义词：predicament, dilemma, trouble|反义词：advantage, blessing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Their plight worries me. = 他们的困境让我担心。
His current financial plight is severe. = 他目前的经济困境很严重。


一般过去时:
The plight of the homeless touched many hearts. = 无家可归者的困境触动了许多人的心。
Their plight during the war was unimaginable. = 他们在战争期间的困境难以想象。


现在完成时:
The organization has helped alleviate the plight of many families. = 该组织已经帮助减轻了许多家庭的困境。
We have witnessed the worsening plight of the poor in recent years. = 近年来，我们目睹了穷人的困境日益恶化。


过去完成时:
Before the aid arrived, their plight had worsened considerably. = 在援助到来之前，他们的困境已经大大恶化了。
By the time help reached them, their plight had become unbearable. = 当帮助到达他们身边时，他们的困境已经变得难以忍受了。


将来时:
Without intervention, their plight will only worsen. = 如果不采取干预措施，他们的困境只会恶化。
The rising sea levels will exacerbate the plight of coastal communities. = 海平面上升将加剧沿海社区的困境。


虚拟语气:
If only we could alleviate their plight! = 但愿我们能够减轻他们的困境！
I wish we could do more to improve their plight. = 我希望我们能够做更多的事情来改善他们的困境。


被动语态:
The plight of the victims was highlighted in the news report. = 受害者的困境在新闻报道中得到了强调。
The plight of the animals is being addressed by several organizations. = 几家组织正在解决动物的困境。


疑问句:
What is the nature of their plight? = 他们的困境是什么性质的？
What can be done to improve their plight? = 可以做些什么来改善他们的困境？


否定句:
Their plight is not being ignored. = 他们的困境并没有被忽视。
The situation is not as dire as some people think, their plight is manageable. = 局势并不像有些人认为的那样糟糕，他们的困境是可以控制的。
