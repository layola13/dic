outbranches: /ˈaʊtbrændʃɪz/ | n. | 分支机构，分公司 | The word "outbranches" refers to branches of an organization, company, or system that are located away from the main or central location.  It emphasizes the geographical separation and independent operation to some degree. | Example: The bank has numerous outbranches across the country.  这家银行在全国各地有许多分支机构。  The company's outbranches reported strong sales figures this quarter.  该公司各分支机构本季度销售业绩强劲。 | 近义词：branches, subsidiaries, affiliates | 反义词：headquarters, main office | 名词
