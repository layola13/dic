perfectible:/pərˈfɛktəbəl/| adj.| 可改进的；可完善的 | Capable of being made perfect or better; improvable.  This word emphasizes the potential for improvement, not that something is currently imperfect. |例句：The system is perfectible, and we should strive to improve it.  这个系统是可以改进的，我们应该努力改进它。

例句：Though seemingly perfect, his work is still perfectible. 尽管看起来很完美，他的作品仍然可以改进。


近义词：improvable, amendable, corrigible | 反义词：unimprovable, flawless, impeccable


词性：形容词
