retransferring:/ˌriːtrænsˈfɜːrɪŋ/| v.|重新转移；再次转让 |The act of transferring something again.  This involves the movement or assignment of something from one place, person, or entity to another, a second time.  It implies a prior transfer has already occurred.|例句：The company is retransferring the intellectual property rights to its original owner. 公司正在将知识产权重新转让给其原始所有者。

The bank is retransferring the funds to the correct account. 银行正在将资金重新转入正确的账户。|近义词：relocate,reassign,re-allocate|反义词：retain, keep
