chinampas:/tʃɪˈnæmpɑːz/| n.|人工浮岛（墨西哥）；水上花园|Rectangular areas of fertile land created on shallow lake beds in ancient Mexico, used for agriculture.  These floating islands were constructed using a framework of interwoven branches and reeds, covered with mud and vegetation.  They provided a sustainable and productive agricultural system.|The chinampas of Xochimilco are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.|近义词：floating gardens, artificial islands|反义词：dry land
