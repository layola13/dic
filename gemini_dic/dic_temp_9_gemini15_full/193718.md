self-conscious: /ˌsɛlfˈkɒnʃəs/ | adj. | 自觉的；害羞的；自我意识过强的 |

英文解析:  Aware of oneself as an object of observation; excessively concerned with one's appearance or behavior.  Feeling awkward or embarrassed because you are worried about what other people think about you.

例句:

He felt self-conscious in his new suit. 他穿着新西装感到很拘谨。

She became self-conscious when everyone started staring at her. 当每个人都盯着她看时，她变得很害羞。


近义词:  shy, bashful, embarrassed, awkward, self-aware, introspective

反义词:  confident, assured, self-possessed, unselfconscious
