fossilizations:/ˌfɒsɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 化石化；石化作用 | The process by which organic matter is converted into a fossil.  This includes the replacement of organic materials with minerals, resulting in the preservation of the original structure. |例句：The fossilizations of these ancient plants are remarkably well-preserved. 这些古代植物的化石保存得非常好。

例句：The study of fossilizations helps scientists understand past environments and ecosystems.  对化石化的研究帮助科学家了解过去的地球环境和生态系统。


近义词：petrification, mineralization | 反义词：decomposition, decay | 词性:名词
