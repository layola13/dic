love:/lʌv/| n./v.|爱，热爱；喜欢|  Love is a powerful emotion. 爱是一种强大的情感。  I love chocolate. 我喜欢巧克力。|近义词：affection, fondness, adoration |反义词：hate, loathe, detest|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I love my family. = 我爱我的家人。
She loves to dance. = 她喜欢跳舞。

一般过去时:
He loved her deeply. = 他深深地爱着她。
They loved living in the countryside. = 他们喜欢住在乡下。

现在完成时:
I have loved you since the first day we met. = 从我们第一次见面起我就爱上你了。
She has loved animals all her life. = 她一生都热爱动物。

过去完成时:
I had loved him for many years before he finally proposed. = 在他最终求婚之前，我已经爱了他很多年了。
She had loved painting since she was a child. = 从小她就热爱绘画。

将来时:
I will always love you. = 我将永远爱你。
They will love their new home. = 他们会喜欢他们的新家。

虚拟语气:
If I loved him, I would marry him. = 如果我爱他，我会嫁给他。
I wish I loved traveling more. = 我希望我更喜欢旅行。

被动语态:
Love is felt by everyone at some point in their lives.  = 每个人一生中都会感受到爱。
He was loved by many. = 他被很多人爱戴。

疑问句:
Do you love me? = 你爱我吗？
Does she love her job? = 她喜欢她的工作吗？

否定句:
I don't love him anymore. = 我不再爱他了。
She doesn't love spicy food. = 她不喜欢辛辣的食物。


PHRASE->
fall in love:坠入爱河
be in love:相爱
make love:做爱
love at first sight:一见钟情
for the love of:看在...的份上
