misplacements:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪsmənts/| n.|放错位置；错放；摆错地方 |The act of putting something in the wrong place; instances of something being in the wrong place.  |例句：The misplacements of the files caused a significant delay in the project. 文件的错放导致项目严重延误。

例句：Numerous misplacements of library books resulted in a need for a new inventory system. 图书馆书籍多次放错位置，导致需要一个新的库存系统。


近义词：errors, mistakes, mislocations, improper placements|反义词：correct placements, proper arrangements


词性:名词
