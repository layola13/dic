self-deprivations: /ˌsɛlfˌdɛprɪˈveɪʃənz/ | n. |自我剥夺；自我克制；自我禁欲 | The act of denying oneself something, often for moral, religious, or health reasons.  This usually involves abstaining from pleasures or necessities.  It can be a conscious choice or a consequence of circumstances. | 例句：His self-deprivations included fasting and abstaining from all forms of entertainment.  他的自我克制包括禁食和放弃所有形式的娱乐。


His years of self-deprivations finally led to a breakdown. 他多年的自我剥夺最终导致了崩溃。

| 近义词：self-denial, abstinence, self-discipline, asceticism | 反义词：indulgence, self-indulgence, gratification | 名词
