recoil:/ˈriːkɔɪl/| n., v.|反冲，畏缩，退却 | to spring back from impact or force; to shrink back in fear or disgust |例句：The gun recoiled violently after he fired it.  枪在他开火后猛烈后坐。 |例句：She recoiled in horror at the sight of the blood. 她看到血后吓得退缩了。|近义词：spring back, flinch, recoil from, shrink from |反义词：advance, approach, proceed


USAGE->

He recoils from the touch of strangers. = 他害怕陌生人的触碰而退缩。
