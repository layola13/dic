paleomagnetic:/ˌpeɪliˌoʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|古地磁的；古磁场的|Relating to or denoting the magnetism in rocks that was acquired when they were formed.  This magnetism provides information about the Earth's magnetic field in the past.|The paleomagnetic data revealed a significant shift in the Earth's magnetic poles millions of years ago. 古地磁数据揭示了数百万年前地球磁极的显著偏移。|近义词：paleomagnetically|反义词：None
