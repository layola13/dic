cementing:/sɪˈmɛntɪŋ/| v.|胶合；巩固；加强|The process of binding or uniting materials, typically using cement.  It can also refer to the act of strengthening or making something firm.|例句：The workers are cementing the bricks together. 工人们正在把砖块粘合在一起。

例句：He is cementing his position in the company. 他正在巩固他在公司的地位。|近义词：bonding, solidifying, strengthening|反义词：weakening, loosening, separating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are cementing the cracks in the wall. = 他们在粘合墙上的裂缝。
We cement our friendship with yearly trips. = 我们每年旅行一次来巩固我们的友谊。


一般过去时:
The builders cemented the foundation. = 建筑工人浇筑了地基。
She cemented her reputation as a top performer. = 她巩固了她作为顶级表演者的声誉。


现在完成时:

The rain has cemented the soil. = 雨水已经使土壤凝固。


过去完成时:

She had cemented her place in history. = 她已经确立了她在历史上的地位。


将来时:
