depravement:/dɪˈpreɪvmənt/ | n. |堕落；腐化；败坏 |The state of being depraved; wickedness; corruption.  Depravement refers to a state of moral corruption, decay, or perversion. The depravement of his character was revealed through his cruel actions.  他的残忍行为暴露了他性格的堕落。


近义词：corruption, degradation, debasement, decay, perversion |反义词：virtue, goodness, purity, righteousness, morality |词性：名词
