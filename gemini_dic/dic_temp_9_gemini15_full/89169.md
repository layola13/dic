homeopath:/hoʊˈmiːəpæθ/| n. |顺势疗法医师；顺势疗法者 |A homeopath is a practitioner of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine.  Homeopaths believe in treating like with like, using highly diluted substances to stimulate the body's healing response. | Example:  The homeopath prescribed a remedy made from highly diluted chamomile.  顺势疗法医师开了用高度稀释的甘菊制成的药。|近义词：alternative medicine practitioner, holistic practitioner |反义词：allopathic physician (conventional medicine doctor)
