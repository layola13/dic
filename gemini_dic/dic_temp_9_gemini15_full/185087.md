reticulation:/ˌrɛtɪkjuˈleɪʃən/| n.|网状结构；网络；网眼织物|The interweaving or interconnectedness of elements in a network-like structure.  It can refer to a physical network (like veins in a leaf) or a conceptual network (like a system of relationships).|例句：The reticulation of the river system created a fertile floodplain. 河流系统的网状结构创造了一个肥沃的洪泛平原。

例句：The intricate reticulation of nerves in the brain is crucial for its function.  大脑中复杂的神经网络对其功能至关重要。


近义词：network, mesh, interconnection, interlinking|反义词：disconnection, separation, isolation|词性：名词
