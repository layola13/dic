deport:/dɪˈpɔːrt/ | vt. |驱逐出境；放逐；举止；表现 | To expel (a foreigner) from a country; to banish; to carry oneself; to behave. |例句：The government decided to deport the illegal immigrants. 政府决定驱逐非法移民。

The criminal was deported back to his home country.  罪犯被遣返回他的祖国。


近义词：expel, banish, exile | 反义词：admit, welcome | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government deports illegal immigrants. = 政府驱逐非法移民。
He deports himself with great dignity. = 他举止非常端庄。


一般过去时:
They deported him for violating visa regulations. = 他们因为违反签证规定而将他驱逐出境。
She deported herself gracefully at the ceremony. = 她在仪式上举止优雅。


现在完成时:
The country has deported thousands of undocumented workers. = 这个国家已经驱逐了数千名无证工人。
He has always deported himself as a gentleman. = 他一直表现得像个绅士。


过去完成时:
They had deported him before the trial even began. = 审判甚至还没开始，他们就已经将他驱逐出境了。
She had deported herself impeccably throughout her career. = 在她的整个职业生涯中，她一直表现得无可挑剔。


将来时:
The court will deport him if he is found guilty. = 如果他被判有罪，法院将驱逐他出境。
He will deport himself appropriately at the meeting. = 他将在会议上表现得体。


虚拟语气:
If they had evidence, they would deport him immediately. = 如果他们有证据，他们会立即将他驱逐出境。
If he were to deport himself improperly, he would face consequences. = 如果他举止不当，他将面临后果。


被动语态:
He was deported for breaking the law. = 他因违法而被驱逐出境。
His behavior was considered to be well-deported. = 他的行为被认为是举止得体的。


疑问句:
Will they deport him? = 他们会驱逐他吗？
How does he deport himself in public? = 他在公共场合是如何举止的？


否定句:
They did not deport him because of lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，他们没有驱逐他。
He did not deport himself well at the interview. = 他在面试中表现不好。


PHRASE->
deport oneself well = 表现良好
deport oneself badly = 表现糟糕
deport oneself with dignity = 举止端庄
be deported from = 从...被驱逐出境
order someone's deportation = 下令驱逐某人


词源:
From Old French *deporter, from Latin deportare ("to carry away"), from de- ("away") + portare ("to carry").
