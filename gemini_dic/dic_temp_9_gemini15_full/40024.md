consumerism:/kənˈsjuːməˌrɪzəm/ | n. | 消费主义；重消费的倾向 | The excessive emphasis on acquiring goods and services.  It's characterized by the belief that happiness and success are achieved through material possessions and consumption. | Example:  The relentless pursuit of consumerism is damaging the environment.  消费主义的无情追求正在破坏环境。 The rise of social media has fueled consumerism among young people.  社交媒体的兴起加剧了年轻人之间的消费主义。|近义词：materialism, acquisitiveness, spendthriftness |反义词：frugality, minimalism, thrift
