comprehending:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ/ | v. |理解；领悟；包含| To understand fully; grasp the meaning of something.  It also refers to the act of including or encompassing something. |例句：I'm struggling to comprehend the complexity of the problem. 我很难理解这个问题的复杂性。

She comprehended the significance of the moment. 她理解了那一刻的重要性。


近义词：understanding, grasping, apprehending, encompassing | 反义词：misunderstanding, misinterpreting | 词性：动词
