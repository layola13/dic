inseparably: /ɪnˈsɛpərəbli/ | adv. | 不可分割地，密不可分地 |  Inseparably linked or connected; unable to be separated.  | 例句：The twins were inseparably attached since birth.  孪生姐妹从出生起就形影不离。

Their fates were inseparably bound. 他们的命运紧密相连。


近义词：indissolubly, inextricably, closely


反义词：separately, distinctly


词性：副词
