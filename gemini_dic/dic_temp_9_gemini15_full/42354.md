counteraggressions:/ˌkaʊntərəˈɡreʃənz/| n. |反侵略；反击；报复性侵略 | Acts of aggression in response to an initial aggression.  Counteraggressions are often characterized by a reciprocal nature, aiming to neutralize or retaliate against the original attack. They can range from military actions to diplomatic maneuvers.  |The country's counteraggressions were a direct response to the neighboring nation's invasion.  这个国家的反侵略行动是对邻国入侵的直接回应。|retaliation, reprisal, revenge |peace, appeasement, submission|名词
