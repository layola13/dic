gifting:/ˈɡɪftɪŋ/| n. |馈赠；赠送；天赋 |The act of giving gifts; the process of bestowing something.  It can also refer to a natural talent or aptitude. |例句：The gifting of presents was a highlight of the holiday season.  节日季里赠送礼物是亮点。

例句：Her musical gifting was evident from a young age. 她从小就显露出音乐天赋。|近义词：donation, bestowal, presentation, talent |反义词：receiving, taking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gifting his daughter a car for her birthday. = 他送给他女儿一辆车作为生日礼物。
They are gifting money to the charity. = 他们正在向慈善机构捐款。


一般过去时:
She gifted her friend a beautiful painting. = 她送给她朋友一幅美丽的画。
We gifted our parents a trip to Europe. = 我们送给我们父母一趟欧洲之旅。


现在完成时:
He has gifted his entire collection to the museum. = 他已将他的全部收藏捐赠给了博物馆。
They have gifted generously to various causes. = 他们慷慨地捐赠给了各种事业。


过去完成时:
She had gifted him a watch before he left for his trip. = 在他去旅行之前，她已经送给他一块手表了。
They had gifted the land to the city years ago. = 多年前他们已经把那块地捐给了城市。


将来时:
I will gift you a book for Christmas. = 圣诞节我将送你一本书。
We will be gifting scholarships to deserving students. = 我们将为优秀的学子提供奖学金。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would gift a million dollars to charity. = 如果我有钱，我会向慈善机构捐赠一百万美元。
I wish I could gift everyone a happy life. = 我希望我能给每个人幸福的生活。


被动语态:
The prize was gifted to the winner. = 奖品被授予了获胜者。
Many gifts were gifted during the celebration. = 在庆祝活动中送出了许多礼物。


疑问句:
What are you gifting your mother for her birthday? = 你送你妈妈什么生日礼物？
Will anything be gifted to the employees this year? = 今年员工会收到什么礼物吗？


否定句:
He didn't gift her anything for her birthday. = 他没有送她任何生日礼物。
They will not be gifting any more money to the project. = 他们不会再向该项目捐款了。
