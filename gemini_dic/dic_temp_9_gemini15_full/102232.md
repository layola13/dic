itemised:/ˈaɪtəmaɪzd/ | adj. | 项目分列的；逐条列明的 |  Detailed and listed separately; showing each item individually.  |The expenses were itemised on the receipt. 收据上详细列明了各项开支。  The report itemised all the company's assets. 报告逐条列明了公司所有的资产。 | detailed, specified, particularised |  general, summarized, unspecified | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The restaurant itemises all its charges on the bill. = 这家餐馆在账单上逐条列明所有费用。
They itemise every expense in the budget. = 他们在预算中逐项列出每一笔支出。


一般过去时:
She itemised her deductions on her tax return. = 她在纳税申报单上逐条列明了她的扣除额。
The accountant itemised the company's profits and losses. = 会计师逐条列明了公司的利润和亏损。


现在完成时:
He has itemised all the parts needed for the project. = 他已经列出了项目所需的所有零件。
The company has itemised the contributions from its donors. = 公司已经列出了捐赠者的捐款明细。


过去完成时:
She had itemised the ingredients before she started baking. = 在开始烘焙之前，她已经列出了配料清单。
They had itemised the costs associated with the trip. = 他们已经列出了与这次旅行相关的费用。


将来时:
We will itemise the charges on the invoice. = 我们将在发票上逐条列明各项费用。
They will itemise the results of the survey in their report. = 他们将在报告中逐条列明调查结果。


虚拟语气:
If I were to itemise every task, it would take hours. = 如果我要列出每一项任务，将需要数小时。
I wish I had itemised my expenses more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地列出了我的开支。


被动语态:
The expenses were itemised in the report. = 报告中列明了各项开支。
The ingredients should be itemised on the recipe card. = 配料应该在食谱卡上列明。


疑问句:
Are the expenses itemised on the invoice? = 发票上是否列明了各项开支？
Have you itemised all the potential risks? = 你是否列明了所有潜在的风险？


否定句:
The report did not itemise the individual contributions. = 该报告没有列明个人捐款。
The list did not itemise all the items purchased. = 该清单没有列明所有购买的商品。


PHRASE->
