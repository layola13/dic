gecko:/ˈɡɛkoʊ/ | n. |壁虎|A small lizard with adhesive toes that enable it to climb walls and ceilings.  They are known for their diverse colors and patterns, and their ability to detach their tails when threatened. |例句：I saw a gecko clinging to the wall. 我看到一只壁虎粘在墙上。

例句：The gecko's sticky toes allow it to climb smooth surfaces. 壁虎的粘性脚趾使它能够攀爬光滑的表面。


近义词：lizard (a more general term)


反义词：无


词性：名词
