chopine:/ʃəˈpiːn/ | n. |木底鞋，高跷鞋 | A type of high-heeled shoe, especially one with a very high platform sole, worn by women in the 15th and 16th centuries.  |Example:  The painting depicted a noblewoman wearing a richly embroidered gown and elaborate chopine.  这幅画描绘了一位贵妇穿着绣有精美图案的长袍和华丽的木底鞋。|近义词：platform shoe, pattens (though pattens are generally less elevated)|反义词：flat shoe, slipper
