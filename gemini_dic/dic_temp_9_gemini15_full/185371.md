retroflex:/ˈrɛtroʊflɛks/| adj.| 卷舌的；后仰的 | Relating to or denoting consonants articulated with the tongue curled back.  Retroflex consonants are produced by curling the tip of the tongue back towards the hard palate.  They are common in many languages of the Indian subcontinent and some other parts of the world. | Example:  The pronunciation of the "r" sound in many Indian languages is a retroflex consonant.  卷舌音在许多印度语言中很常见。|近义词：curled-back (in the context of tongue articulation)|反义词：apical (referring to sounds articulated with the tip of the tongue)
