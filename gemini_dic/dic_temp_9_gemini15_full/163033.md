preclassifies:/ˌpriːˈklæsɪfaɪz/ | v. |预先分类，预先归类 |To classify something before it is fully processed or analyzed.  This often involves using preliminary or incomplete information to make a rough categorization. |The system preclassifies emails based on subject lines before routing them to individual users. = 系统根据主题行预先对电子邮件进行分类，然后再将其路由到各个用户。 The researcher preclassifies the data into broad categories before conducting a more detailed analysis. = 研究人员在进行更详细的分析之前，将数据预先分类成大的类别。 |categorize, sort, group |unclassify (反义词不完全适用，因为"unclassify" 指的是取消分类，而非相反的分类方式) |动词
