demonised:/ˈdiːmənʌɪzd/| v.|妖魔化，丑化 |To represent (someone or something) as evil or wicked.  |例句：The media demonised the protesters, portraying them as violent thugs.  媒体妖魔化了抗议者，将他们描绘成暴力暴徒。

例句：The politician was demonised by his opponents for his controversial policies.  这位政治家因其有争议的政策而遭到对手的妖魔化。


近义词：vilify, denigrate, slander, malign, blacken


反义词：glorify, praise, extol, laud


词性:动词
