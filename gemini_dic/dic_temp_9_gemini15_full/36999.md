columbier:/ˈkʌləmbiər/| n.|鸽舍；鸽子养殖场|A structure or place where pigeons are kept.  It can refer to a dovecote, a pigeon house, or a place for breeding and raising pigeons.|例句：The old farmhouse had a charming columbier in the garden.  古老的农舍花园里有一个迷人的鸽舍。|例句：The farmer built a new columbier to house his growing flock of racing pigeons.  这位农民建造了一个新的鸽舍来容纳他不断增长的赛鸽群。|近义词：dovecote, pigeon house, aviary|反义词：N/A
