cross-referred:/ˌkrɒs rɪˈfɜːrd/| adj.|交叉引用的，相互参照的|The term is cross-referred to other relevant sections in the document.  这个术语在文档中交叉引用到其他相关部分。|近义词：interlinked, interconnected|反义词：unrelated, independent|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The information is cross-referred throughout the manual. = 信息在整个手册中交叉引用。
These two chapters are cross-referred to each other. = 这两章互相交叉引用。

一般过去时:
The study was cross-referred to previous research. = 这项研究与之前的研究进行了交叉引用。
The data was cross-referred to ensure accuracy. = 数据经过交叉引用以确保准确性。


现在完成时:

All relevant documents have been cross-referred in the index. = 所有相关文件都在索引中进行了交叉引用。
