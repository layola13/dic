outsailed:/ˌaʊtˈseɪld/| vt.|超过（在航行速度方面）|to sail faster than (another boat or person)|The smaller boat outsailed the larger one in the race. 小船在比赛中超过了大船。|近义词：outstripped, surpassed, exceeded|反义词：underperformed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outsails his rivals in every race. 他在每场比赛中都超过了他的对手。
The new yacht consistently outsails the older models. 新游艇始终比旧型号快。

一般过去时:
Our team outsailed the opposing team by a wide margin. 我们的团队以很大的优势超过了对方团队。
The experienced sailor outsailed the novice with ease. 经验丰富的海员轻松地超过了新手。

现在完成时:
They have outsailed all their competitors this season. 这个赛季他们已经超过了所有竞争对手。
She has consistently outsailed her brother in every sailing competition.  她在每次帆船比赛中都始终超过她的哥哥。

过去完成时:
By the time we reached the finish line, they had already outsailed us. 当我们到达终点线时，他们已经超过我们了。
Before the storm hit, the larger vessel had outsailed the smaller ones into safe harbor.  暴风雨来临之前，较大的船只已经驶入安全港口，超过了较小的船只。

将来时:
We predict that our new design will outsail all existing models. 我们预测我们的新设计将超过所有现有型号。
I believe that with this new sail, we will outsail the competition. 我相信有了这块新帆，我们将超过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If the wind had been stronger, we would have outsailed them. 如果风更大，我们就超过他们了。
If only we had a faster boat, we could have outsailed the others.  如果我们有一艘更快的船，我们就能超过其他人了。


被动语态:
They were outsailed by a smaller, faster boat. 他们被一艘更小、更快的船超过了。
The team was outsailed due to poor strategy.  由于策略不佳，该团队被超越了。

疑问句:
Did they outsail their opponents in the last regatta? 他们在上一届帆船比赛中超过了对手吗？
Will our boat outsail theirs in the upcoming race? 我们的小船会在即将到来的比赛中超过他们的吗？


否定句:
We did not outsail the leading boat. 我们没有超过领先的船。
The smaller boat failed to outsail its competitors.  小船未能超过其竞争对手。
