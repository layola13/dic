deslumming: /dɪˈslʌmɪŋ/ | v. |  贫民窟改造；清除贫民窟 | The process of improving or eliminating slums, often involving relocation of residents and redevelopment of the area.  It often implies a forceful or controversial approach. | Example: The government initiated a controversial deslumming project, displacing thousands of residents. = 政府启动了一个有争议的贫民窟改造项目，导致数千居民流离失所。  The city's deslumming efforts have led to improved sanitation and infrastructure. = 城市的贫民窟改造工作改善了卫生和基础设施。 | 近义词： slum clearance, urban renewal, redevelopment | 反义词： slumification
