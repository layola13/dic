entered:/ˈɛntərd/| v.|进入，加入，登记|To move into or come into a place.|He entered the room quietly. 他悄悄地走进了房间。|近义词：went in, came into, joined|反义词：exited, left


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enters the building every morning. 他每天早上都进入大楼。
She enters the competition every year. 她每年都参加比赛。

一般过去时:
They entered the contest last week. 他们上周参加了比赛。
I entered the house through the back door. 我从后门进了房子。

现在完成时:
She has entered three marathons this year. 她今年已经参加了三次马拉松比赛。
He has entered the university. 他已经进入了大学。

过去完成时:
Before the game started, the players had already entered the field.  比赛开始前，球员们已经进入了球场。
They had entered the building before the alarm sounded. 警报响起之前，他们已经进入了大楼。

将来时:
We will enter the race next month. 我们下个月将参加比赛。
She will enter the building from the main entrance. 她将从正门进入大楼。

虚拟语气:
If I had entered the lottery, I might have won. 如果我买了彩票，我可能就赢了。
I wish I had entered the competition. 我真希望我参加了比赛。

被动语态:
The names were entered into the database. 这些名字被输入数据库。
The application was entered on time. 申请书按时提交了。

疑问句:
Did he enter the room? 他进房间了吗？
Has she entered the competition yet? 她报名参加比赛了吗？

否定句:
He did not enter the race. 他没有参加比赛。
She hasn't entered the contest yet. 她还没有参加比赛。


PHRASE->

entered for: 报名参加
entered against:  记入（账目等）
