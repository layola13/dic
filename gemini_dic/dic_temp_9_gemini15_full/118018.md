memorizing: /ˈmeməraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |记忆；熟记 |The act of committing something to memory; learning something by heart.  | 例句：Memorizing vocabulary is crucial for language learning.  记忆词汇对于语言学习至关重要。

例句：She spent hours memorizing the lines for the play. 她花了数小时记忆戏剧台词。


近义词：learning by heart, committing to memory, rote learning

反义词：forgetting, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am memorizing the poem for tomorrow's recitation. 我正在记忆明天朗诵的诗歌。
He memorizes new vocabulary every day. 他每天都记住新的词汇。


一般过去时:
I memorized the entire speech last night. 我昨晚记住了整篇演讲。
She memorized the song lyrics before the karaoke night. 她在卡拉OK之夜之前记住了歌曲歌词。


现在完成时:
I have memorized all the capitals of the European countries. 我已经记住了所有欧洲国家的首都。
She has memorized the multiplication tables. 她已经记住了乘法表。


过去完成时:
I had memorized the script before the audition. 我在试镜前已经记住了剧本。
They had memorized the dance routine for weeks. 他们已经排练舞蹈几周了。


将来时:
I will memorize the entire book by the end of the year. 我将在年底前记住整本书。
She will memorize the speech before the presentation. 她将在演示文稿之前记住演讲稿。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would memorize the whole dictionary. 如果我有更多的时间，我会记住整个字典。
I wish I could memorize things more easily. 我希望我能更容易地记住事情。


被动语态:
The poem was memorized by all the students. 这首诗被所有学生背诵了。
The formula is easily memorized. 这个公式很容易记住。


疑问句:
Are you memorizing anything at the moment? 你现在正在记什么吗？
What are you trying to memorize? 你试图记住什么？


否定句:
I cannot memorize this long list. 我记不住这么长的清单。
He didn't memorize the instructions properly. 他没有正确记住说明。
