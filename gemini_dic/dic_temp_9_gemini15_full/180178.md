reformatted:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmætɪd/ | adj. |重新格式化的；重新排版的 |  Having been given a new format or layout.  This usually refers to text or data. | Example: The reformatted document was much easier to read.  重新格式化的文档更容易阅读。|近义词：restyled, rearranged |反义词：unformatted, original
