fritz's:/frɪtsɪz/| n.|弗里茨的（所有格）|The possessive form of the name Fritz, indicating ownership or belonging.|例句：That's Fritz's car.那是弗里茨的车。|例句：This is Fritz's idea.这是弗里茨的主意。|近义词：Fritz's belongings (弗里茨的物品)|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It shows possession and doesn't change based on tense.
