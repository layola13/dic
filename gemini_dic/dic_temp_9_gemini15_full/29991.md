cavings:/ˈkeɪvɪŋz/| n. |采矿塌方；矿山崩落；（尤指）采矿废料 | Loose material that falls from the roof or walls of a mine or tunnel; the process of this happening.  It often refers to the debris resulting from such collapses.  |例句：The mine was closed due to the dangerous cavings. 矿井因危险的塌方而关闭。

The recent cavings have made the tunnel impassable. 近期的矿山崩落使得隧道无法通行。|近义词：collapse, subsidence, downfall, debris |反义词：stability, solidity
