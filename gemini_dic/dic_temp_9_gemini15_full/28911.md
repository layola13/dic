carriaged:/ˈkærɪdʒd/ | adj. n. | （尤指货物）已装运的；运送；马车；(旧式)运货车厢 |  Describing something that has been carried, especially goods;  a conveyance, typically a horse-drawn carriage; an older term for a railway goods wagon. |例句：The carriaged goods arrived safely.  已装运的货物安全到达了。 The old carriage was beautifully restored. 这辆旧马车得到了精美的修复。|近义词：conveyed, transported, shipped, wagon |反义词：unloaded, unshipped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company carriages goods daily. = 公司每天都运送货物。
He carries his luggage carefully. = 他小心地搬运他的行李。


一般过去时:
They carried the piano upstairs. = 他们把钢琴搬上了楼。
The goods were carried by train. = 货物由火车运输。


现在完成时:
We have carried out the experiment successfully. = 我们已经成功地完成了实验。(carry out = 执行)
They have carried all the furniture into the new house. = 他们已经把所有家具搬进了新房子。

She had carried the heavy box all the way home. = 她把沉重的箱子一路扛回了家。


将来时:
