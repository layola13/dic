digests:/daɪˈdʒɛsts/ | n. v. |摘要；消化；领会|To condense (information) into a shorter, more concise form; to process food in the stomach and intestines.|例句：The editor digested the long article into a brief summary. 编辑将长篇文章浓缩成简短的摘要。  The body digests food to extract nutrients. 人体消化食物以提取营养物质。|近义词：summarize, abstract, absorb, assimilate|反义词：expand, elaborate|词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He digests information quickly. = 他快速消化信息。
She digests her food slowly. = 她慢慢消化食物。


一般过去时:
I digested the entire book in a week. = 我在一周内读完了整本书（理解了整本书的内容）。
The patient digested his meal poorly. = 病人消化不良。


现在完成时:
I have digested all the key points of the presentation. = 我已经消化了演示文稿的所有要点。
She has digested the news and is ready to react. = 她已经消化了这个消息，并准备做出反应。


过去完成时:
He had digested the evidence before reaching a conclusion. = 他在得出结论之前已经消化了证据。
They had digested the difficult concept before moving to the next chapter. = 他们在转向下一章之前已经理解了这个复杂的概念。


将来时:
I will digest this report tomorrow. = 我明天会消化这份报告。
She will digest the information before making a decision. = 她将在做出决定之前消化这些信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had digested the material earlier, I would have done better on the exam. = 如果我早点消化了资料，考试成绩会更好。
I wish I had digested the information more thoroughly. = 我希望我更彻底地理解了这些信息。


被动语态:
The food is digested in the stomach. = 食物在胃里消化。
The article was digested into a shorter version. = 这篇文章被浓缩成一个较短的版本。


疑问句:
How do you digest such a large amount of information? = 你是如何消化如此大量的信息的？
Does she digest food easily? = 她容易消化食物吗？


否定句:
I didn't digest the instructions properly. = 我没有正确理解说明。
He doesn't digest dairy products well. = 他不耐受乳制品。


PHRASE->
