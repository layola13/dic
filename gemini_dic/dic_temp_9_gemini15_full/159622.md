pollutants:/pəˈluːtənts/ | n. |污染物 |Substances that contaminate the environment, causing harm to ecosystems and human health.  They can be in the form of gases, liquids, or solids. Examples include industrial waste, vehicle emissions, and agricultural runoff. |例句：Air pollutants are a major cause of respiratory illnesses.  空气污染物是呼吸系统疾病的主要原因。

例句：The river was heavily polluted with industrial pollutants.  这条河受到了严重的工业污染物污染。


近义词：contaminants, impurities, toxins, poisons | 反义词：purifiers, cleansers


词性：名词
