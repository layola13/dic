hold:/hoʊld/ /həʊld/| v. n.|抓住，握住；保持；容纳；举行；拥有|to grasp, manage, contain, possess, or organize|例句：He held the baby gently. 他轻轻地抱着婴儿。

例句：The box can hold all my books. 这个箱子可以装下我所有的书。

近义词：grasp, grip, keep, contain, possess|反义词：release, let go, drop, abandon|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She holds the baby carefully. = 她小心地抱着婴儿。
He holds a senior position in the company. = 他在公司担任高级职位。

一般过去时:
I held the door open for her. = 我为她扶着门。
They held a meeting yesterday. = 他们昨天开会了。

现在完成时:
She has held that job for ten years. = 她已经做了那份工作十年了。
We have held onto our principles. = 我们坚持了我们的原则。

过去完成时:
He had held the record for five years before she broke it. = 在她打破纪录之前，他已经保持了五年纪录。
They had held a similar event last year. = 他们去年举办过类似的活动。


将来时:
I will hold the line until you get back. = 我会一直等着，直到你回来。
They will hold a conference next month. = 他们下个月将举行一次会议。

虚拟语气:
If I held the power, I would change things. = 如果我有权力，我会改变现状。
I wish I had held my tongue. = 我真希望当时我没有说话。

被动语态:
The meeting was held in the main hall. = 会议在大厅举行。
The election will be held next Tuesday. = 选举将于下周二举行。

疑问句:
Does she hold a driving license? = 她有驾照吗？
Will they hold a party this weekend? = 他们这个周末会举办派对吗？

否定句:
He didn't hold back his anger. = 他没有抑制住怒火。
The bridge couldn't hold the weight. = 这座桥承受不了重量。
