securitize:/sɪˈkjʊəraɪz/| vt.|证券化；将…转变为证券|To transform an asset (such as a loan or mortgage) into a marketable security.  This involves pooling similar assets, creating tradable securities backed by these assets, and selling them to investors.|例句：The bank securitized a portfolio of mortgages to raise capital.  银行将一系列抵押贷款证券化以筹集资金。|例句：They are securitizing their receivables to improve cash flow. 他们正在将其应收账款证券化以改善现金流。|近义词：financialize, monetize|反义词：de-securitize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company securitizes its assets regularly. = 这家公司定期将其资产证券化。
Banks securitize mortgages to reduce risk. = 银行将抵押贷款证券化以降低风险。

They securitized the loans last year. = 他们去年将贷款证券化了。

现在完成时:
We have securitized a significant portion of our portfolio. = 我们已经将大部分投资组合证券化了。

Investors had not fully understood the risks involved in securitized products before the 2008 financial crisis. = 在2008年金融危机之前，投资者并没有完全理解证券化产品中的风险。
