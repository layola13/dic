signature:/ˈsɪɡnətʃər/| n. |签名，署名；标志，特征 |A signature is a person's name written in their own handwriting, typically at the end of a document to authenticate it. It can also refer to a distinctive characteristic or feature.  |例句：Please sign your signature on the dotted line. 请在虚线上签你的名。

例句：The artist's signature style is easily recognizable.  这位艺术家的标志性风格很容易辨认。
