comanage:/koʊˈmænɪdʒ/ | v. |共同管理，协同管理 | To manage something jointly or collaboratively.  | Example: The two companies decided to comanage the project to leverage their respective strengths.  两个公司决定共同管理这个项目，以发挥各自的优势。 | 近义词: co-administer, jointly manage, collaborate on managing | 反义词: sole manage, independently manage


USAGE->

They comanage the marketing campaign. = 他们共同管理营销活动。
We comanage the family business. = 我们共同管理家族企业。
