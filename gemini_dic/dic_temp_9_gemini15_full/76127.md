furze:/fɜːrz/| n.|金雀花，荆棘|A prickly evergreen shrub of the genus Ulex, especially Ulex europaeus, with yellow flowers and spiny branches.  It is common in western Europe.|The furze bushes were ablaze with yellow flowers.|近义词：gorse, whin|反义词：无
