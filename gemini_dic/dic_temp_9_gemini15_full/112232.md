looseners:/ˈluːsənərz/ | n. | 松动器；放松剂 | Things that loosen or make something less tight.  They can be physical tools or chemical substances. For example, a bolt loosener is a tool, while a muscle relaxant is a chemical loosener. |例句：The mechanic used a special loosener to remove the rusted bolt. 机械师用了一种特殊的松动器来拧下生锈的螺栓。

The gardener applied a soil loosener to improve drainage. 园丁施用了土壤疏松剂以改善排水。|近义词：relaxants, softeners, looseners  |反义词：tighteners, binders, constrictors |名词
