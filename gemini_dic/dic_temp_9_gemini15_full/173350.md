quasi-devoted:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ dɪˈvoʊtɪd/ | adj. |  几乎献身的；似献身的；表面上忠诚的 |  Suggesting a devotion that is not entirely genuine or complete. It implies a level of commitment that falls short of true, unwavering devotion.  |例句：His quasi-devoted following consisted of fans who admired his work but were not necessarily passionate supporters.| 近义词：partially devoted, nominally devoted, superficially devoted | 反义词：totally devoted, fiercely devoted, wholeheartedly devoted
