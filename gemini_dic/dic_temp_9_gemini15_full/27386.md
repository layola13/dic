chameleon: /kəˈmiːliən/ | n. | 变色龙；善变的人 | A reptile that is able to change its skin color to blend in with its surroundings.  It is also used metaphorically to describe someone who is easily adaptable or changes their opinions frequently. | Example: The chameleon blended perfectly with the leaves.  变色龙完美地融入了树叶中。 The politician is a chameleon, changing his views to suit the current situation.  这位政客就像变色龙一样，改变他的观点以适应当前的情况。|近义词：lizard (in literal sense), opportunist (in metaphorical sense) |反义词：constant, unchanging

词源:
