fritter-away:/ˈfrɪtər əˈweɪ/| v.|浪费，挥霍|To waste time, money, or energy on unimportant things.|例句：He frittered away his inheritance on gambling. 他把继承的财产都花在了赌博上。|例句：Don't fritter away your time on trivial matters. 不要把时间浪费在琐碎的事情上。|近义词：squander, waste, dissipate|反义词：save, conserve, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fritters away his money on useless gadgets. = 他把钱浪费在无用的电子产品上。
She fritters away her time watching TV. = 她把时间浪费在看电视上。

一般过去时:
They frittered away the entire afternoon playing video games. = 他们整个下午都浪费在玩电子游戏上。
He frittered away his savings on a luxury car. = 他把积蓄挥霍在一辆豪华车上。

现在完成时:
I have frittered away too much time on social media. = 我在社交媒体上浪费了太多时间。
She has frittered away her chances of success. = 她已经浪费了她成功的机会。

过去完成时:
He realized he had frittered away his youth on meaningless pursuits. = 他意识到他把青春浪费在了毫无意义的事情上。
They regretted that they had frittered away their opportunity. = 他们后悔错过了机会。

将来时:
If he continues like this, he will fritter away all his money. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会把所有的钱都挥霍掉。
She fears that she will fritter away her potential. = 她担心她会浪费自己的潜力。

虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't frittered away my time in college. = 要是我大学时期没有浪费时间就好了。
I wish I hadn't frittered away my savings. = 我真希望我没有挥霍我的积蓄。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
His inheritance was frittered away on frivolous expenses. = 他的遗产被挥霍在无谓的开支上。
His opportunities were frittered away due to his laziness. = 由于他的懒惰，他的机会被浪费了。

疑问句:
Is he frittering away his life? = 他是在浪费他的生命吗？
Are you frittering away your potential? = 你在浪费你的潜力吗？

否定句:
He doesn't fritter away his time. = 他不浪费时间。
She doesn't fritter away her money. = 她不乱花钱。
