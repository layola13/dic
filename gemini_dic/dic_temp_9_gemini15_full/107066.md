lacy:/ˈleɪsi/| adj.| 花边的；精巧的；轻薄的 | Having a delicate pattern of interwoven loops; resembling lace.  |例句：The dress had a lacy trim. 这件裙子有花边的装饰。
The curtains were made of lacy fabric. 窗帘是用轻薄的花边织物做的。|近义词：delicate, intricate, delicate, filmy |反义词：coarse, rough, heavy
