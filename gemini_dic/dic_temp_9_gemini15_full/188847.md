russeting:/ˈrʌsɪtɪŋ/| n.|锈蚀；锈色；褐斑|The browning or reddish-brown discoloration of the skin of fruit, particularly apples and pears, caused by fungal infection, environmental stress, or genetic predisposition.|例句：The apples showed significant russeting after the late frost.  苹果在晚霜后出现了明显的锈斑。

例句：The russeting on the pears reduced their market value. 梨子上的锈斑降低了它们的市场价值。


近义词：browning, discoloration, spotting|反义词：smoothness, unblemished skin|词性:名词
