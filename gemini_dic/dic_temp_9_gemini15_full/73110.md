for dear life:/fɔːr dɪr laɪf/ | adv. | 拼命地，竭尽全力地 |  This phrase describes an action performed with intense effort and desperation, often to escape danger or avoid a negative outcome. It emphasizes the urgency and seriousness of the situation. | 例句：He clung to the rope for dear life. 他拼命抓住绳子。

He ran for dear life when he saw the bear. 他看见熊后拼命地跑。


近义词：desperately, frantically, with all one's might


反义词：casually, leisurely, nonchalantly

词性：副词
