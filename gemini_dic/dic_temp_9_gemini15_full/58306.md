dry-milks:/ˈdraɪmɪlks/ | n. |奶粉|Dried milk, usually in powder form, produced by removing water from milk. It is a convenient and long-lasting form of milk, often used in cooking and beverages. |Example:  I added dry-milks to my coffee this morning. 今天早上我在咖啡里加了奶粉。|近义词：powdered milk, instant milk|反义词：fresh milk, liquid milk
