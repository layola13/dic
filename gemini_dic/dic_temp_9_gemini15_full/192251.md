screechers:/ˈskriːtʃərz/| n.|尖声叫喊的人；发尖锐声音的东西 |People or things that make a loud, harsh, screeching noise.  |例句：The screechers in the audience drowned out the speaker.  观众席中的尖声叫喊声盖过了演讲者的声音。

The rusty hinges were screechers.生锈的合页发出尖锐的吱吱声。|近义词：shriekers, howlers, screamers |反义词：whisperers, mutters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds screech loudly every morning. = 鸟儿每天早上大声尖叫。
Those brakes screech terribly. = 那些刹车的声音非常刺耳。

一般过去时:
The tires screeched as the car turned the corner. = 汽车转弯时轮胎发出刺耳的尖叫声。
The children screeched with laughter. = 孩子们高兴地尖叫起来。

现在完成时:
The neighbors have screeched at us for playing loud music. = 邻居们因为我们播放大声的音乐而对我们大声尖叫。
The rusty swing set has been screeching all summer. = 生锈的秋千架整个夏天都在吱吱作响。

过去完成时:
The old car had screeching brakes long before it finally broke down. = 那辆旧车在最终抛锚之前很久，刹车就已经发出刺耳的尖叫声了。
The audience had been screeching with excitement before the concert even began. = 在音乐会开始之前，观众就已经兴奋地尖叫了。

将来时:
The engine will screech if you don't get it serviced. = 如果你不修理它，发动机就会发出尖叫声。
The protesters will screech their complaints outside the building tomorrow. = 抗议者们明天将在楼外大声喊叫他们的不满。

虚拟语气:
If the wheels screech, it means we need new brakes. = 如果车轮发出尖叫声，那就意味着我们需要更换刹车。
I wish those birds wouldn’t screech so loudly at dawn. = 我希望那些鸟儿不要在黎明时分叫得那么大声。

被动语态:
The song was screeching out from the radio. = 这首歌从收音机里尖声地播放出来。
The whole neighborhood was being kept awake by the screeching of the cats. = 猫的尖叫声吵得整个街区都睡不着觉。

疑问句:
Is that screeching sound coming from next door? = 那刺耳的声音是从隔壁传来的吗？
Why are the children screeching so loudly? = 为什么孩子们叫得这么大声？

否定句:
The car didn't screech when I braked. = 我刹车时，汽车没有发出刺耳的声音。
The audience didn't screech during the quiet parts of the performance. = 在表演的安静部分，观众没有尖叫。
