secretariats:/ˈsɛkrɪtəriəts/| n.|秘书处；秘书部门；秘书人员|The group of people who work as secretaries, or the office where they work.  It can also refer to the administrative staff of an organization.|例句：The United Nations secretariats are located in New York.|近义词：secretarial staff, administration, office|反义词：N/A
