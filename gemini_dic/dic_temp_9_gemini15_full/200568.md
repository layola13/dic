slatter:/ˈslætər/| v.| 随意乱扔，乱涂，溅污|To throw or spread something about carelessly or untidily; to splash or smear.|例句：He slattered paint all over the canvas. 他把颜料乱涂在画布上。|例句：The dog slattered mud all over the carpet. 狗把泥巴溅得到处都是。|近义词：fling, scatter, splash, smear|反义词：arrange, organize, clean


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slatters paint onto the canvas. = 他把颜料随意地涂在画布上。
She slatters food all over the table. = 她把食物弄得到处都是。

一般过去时:
He slattered the walls with mud. = 他把泥巴乱涂在墙上。
She slattered the cake with icing. = 她把蛋糕上涂满了糖霜。(此处slatter略带不均匀的意思)

现在完成时:
He has slattered paint all over his clothes. = 他把颜料弄到衣服上了。
She has slattered the floor with water. = 她把地板弄湿了。

过去完成时:
He had slattered the room with confetti before the party started. = 派对开始前，他已经把五彩纸屑撒得到处都是。
She had slattered the manuscript with corrections. = 她已经在稿件上乱涂改了。

将来时:
He will slatter the walls with posters. = 他将会把海报乱贴在墙上。
She will slatter the dessert with chocolate sauce. = 她将会把巧克力酱淋在甜点上。(此处slatter更偏向于随意地淋上)

虚拟语气:
If he slattered the paint carefully, it would look better. = 如果他仔细地涂抹颜料，效果会更好。
I wish she hadn't slattered the cake with icing. = 我真希望她没有把糖霜乱涂在蛋糕上。

被动语态:
The walls were slattered with graffiti. = 墙上被涂满了涂鸦。
The floor was slattered with broken glass. = 地板上散落着碎玻璃。

疑问句:
Did he slatter the paint on purpose? = 他是故意把颜料乱涂的吗？
Why did she slatter the food everywhere? = 她为什么把食物弄得到处都是？

否定句:
He didn't slatter the paint; he applied it carefully. = 他没有乱涂颜料；他是仔细地涂抹的。
She didn't slatter the sauce on the dessert; she drizzled it neatly. = 她没有把酱汁乱涂在甜点上；她是细细地淋上去的。
