rebuilt:/ˌriːˈbɪlt/| v. |重建，改造|To build something again after it has been damaged or destroyed.  It implies a complete or substantial reconstruction, not just minor repairs.|例句：The old factory has been rebuilt and is now a modern shopping center.  旧工厂已经被重建，现在是一个现代化的购物中心。

例句：They rebuilt the bridge after the flood.  洪水过后，他们重建了桥梁。


近义词：reconstruct, renovate, restore|反义词：destroy, demolish, raze|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rebuild houses after natural disasters. = 他们在自然灾害后重建房屋。
The company rebuilds its image after a scandal. = 公司在丑闻后重塑形象。

一般过去时:
We rebuilt the engine last week. = 我们上周重建了发动机。
She rebuilt her life after the divorce. = 她离婚后重建了她的生活。

现在完成时:
They have rebuilt the school since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，他们已经重建了学校。
The government has rebuilt many roads in the city. = 政府已经重建了城市中的许多道路。

过去完成时:
They had rebuilt the church before the anniversary. = 他们在周年纪念日之前已经重建了教堂。
She had rebuilt her confidence before the competition. = 在比赛之前，她已经重建了她的自信。

将来时:
We will rebuild the damaged houses next year. = 我们明年将重建受损的房屋。
The city will rebuild the park after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，城市将重建公园。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would rebuild the entire building. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们将重建整栋建筑。
I wish they had rebuilt the old theater. = 我希望他们已经重建了旧剧院。

被动语态:
The bridge is being rebuilt at the moment. = 桥梁正在重建中。
The house was rebuilt using sustainable materials. = 这座房子是用可持续材料重建的。

疑问句:
Will they rebuild the stadium? = 他们会重建体育馆吗？
Have they rebuilt the damaged roads yet? = 他们已经重建受损的道路了吗？

否定句:
They did not rebuild the old town. = 他们没有重建旧城镇。
We will not rebuild the factory in the same location. = 我们不会在同一地点重建工厂。
