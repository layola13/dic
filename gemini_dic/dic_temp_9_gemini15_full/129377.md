nomads:/ˈnoʊmædz/| n. |游牧民族，流浪者 |People who move from place to place instead of living in one place for a long time, typically with their herds or flocks.  Nomads often have a strong connection to their animals and the land they traverse. |例句：The Mongols were renowned nomads, traversing vast steppes with their herds.  蒙古人以游牧民族而闻名，他们带着牲畜穿越广阔的草原。

例句：Many nomadic tribes still maintain traditional ways of life in remote areas. 许多游牧部落仍在偏远地区保持着传统的生活方式。


近义词：wanderers, itinerants, migrants, nomads


反义词：settlers, residents
