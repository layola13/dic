comments:/ˈkɑːments/ /ˈkɒments/ | n. |评论，意见，注释 | remarks, observations, notes expressing an opinion  |例句：The comments section was filled with angry responses. 评论区充满了愤怒的回应。

例句：He added some helpful comments to my essay. 他在我的论文中添加了一些有益的评论。


近义词：remarks, observations, critiques | 反义词：silence, agreement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes insightful comments on current events. 他对时事发表富有见地的评论。
The teacher makes helpful comments on students' work.老师对学生的作业做了有益的评论。


一般过去时:
She made a few critical comments about the movie. 她对这部电影做了一些批评性的评论。
They made positive comments about the presentation.他们对这次演示做了积极的评价。


现在完成时:
They have made several comments on the proposal. 他们对这个提案已经做了好几条评论。
She has made no comments about the incident. 她对这起事件没有发表任何评论。


过去完成时:
He had made some harsh comments before realizing his mistake. 他在意识到自己的错误之前已经发表了一些尖刻的评论。
They had made no comments about the plan until the meeting. 在会议之前，他们对这个计划没有任何评论。


将来时:
I will make a comment on the article later. 我稍后会对这篇文章发表评论。
She will make a formal comment in response to the accusations. 她将对这些指控做出正式回应。


虚拟语气:
If I had known better, I wouldn't have made such a blunt comment. 如果我更了解情况，我就不会发表如此直率的评论。
I wish I hadn't made that insensitive comment. 我真希望我没有说过那句不敏感的话。


被动语态:
His comments were widely praised. 他的评论受到了广泛好评。
The article was filled with critical comments. 这篇文章充满了批评性的评论。


疑问句:
What are your comments on the matter? 你对这件事有什么看法？
Do you have any comments to make? 你有什么要评论的吗？


否定句:
I have no comments to offer. 我没有什么要说的。
She made no comments during the interview.  她在采访中没有发表任何评论。
