fertiliser:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzər/ | n. |肥料 |A substance added to soil to enhance the growth of plants by providing essential nutrients.  Fertilizers can be organic or inorganic, and their composition varies depending on the specific needs of the plants being cultivated. |The farmer applied fertiliser to the fields to increase crop yield.  农民给田地施肥以提高作物产量。|manure, compost, plant food |— |n.
