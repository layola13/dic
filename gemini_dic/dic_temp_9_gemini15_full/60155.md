effeminacy:/ɪˈfɛmɪnəsi/| n. |柔弱；女性化；娘娘腔 |The quality or state of being effeminate; unmanly behavior or appearance.  It often carries a negative connotation, suggesting weakness or a lack of masculinity.  |例句：His effeminacy was a source of mockery among his peers.  他的柔弱成为同龄人嘲笑的对象。

例句：The critics saw his delicate movements on stage as effeminacy. 批评家认为他在舞台上的轻柔动作是女性化的表现。


近义词：weakness, delicacy, femininity, unmanliness |反义词：manliness, masculinity, strength, virility |词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *effeminacie, from Old French effeminacie, from Latin effeminatus, past participle of effeminare "to make womanly," from ex- "out" + femināre "to make a woman," from femina "woman."
