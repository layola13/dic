outdriven:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪvən/| adj. | 被赶出去的；被驱逐的；被超越的 |  Driven out or surpassed.  |The outdriven refugees sought shelter in neighboring countries. 被赶走的难民在邻国寻求庇护。|  expelled, ousted, surpassed | welcomed, accepted |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company outdrives its competitors in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面超越了竞争对手。
This new technology outdrives the old one in efficiency. = 这项新技术在效率上超越了旧技术。


一般过去时:
The champion outdriven his opponent in the final race. = 冠军在决赛中超越了他的对手。
The invading army outdriven the local forces. = 入侵的军队击败了当地军队。


现在完成时:
She has outdriven all expectations in her career. = 她在她的事业中超越了所有人的期望。
The project has outdriven its initial budget. = 这个项目已经超出了最初的预算。


过去完成时:
By the end of the war, the rebels had outdriven the government troops. = 到战争结束时，叛军已经击败了政府军。
He had outdriven all his previous records. = 他已经打破了他之前的所有记录。


将来时:
We predict that electric cars will outdrive gasoline cars in the next decade. = 我们预测电动汽车将在未来十年超越汽油汽车。
They believe their new product will outdrive the competition. = 他们相信他们的新产品将超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outdriven his rival. = 如果他训练更努力，他可能会战胜他的对手。
If only I had outdriven my fear! = 要是我能克服我的恐惧就好了！


被动语态:
The old system was outdriven by the new one. = 旧系统被新系统取代了。
His claims were outdriven by the evidence. = 他的说法被证据驳斥了。


疑问句:
Was the old technology outdriven by the new one? = 旧技术是被新技术取代了吗？
Has the company been outdriven in the market? = 这家公司在市场上被超越了吗？


否定句:
The team wasn't outdriven by their opponents. = 这个团队没有被对手击败。
His arguments were not outdriven by the facts. = 他的论点没有被事实驳斥。


PHRASE->
outdriven by competition  被竞争对手超越
outdriven by events 被事件所超越
outdriven from power 被赶下台
outdriven by technology 被技术淘汰
outdriven by progress 被进步所淘汰
