hurls:/hɜːrlz/| vt.|猛扔，猛掷；大声喊叫|To throw something with great force.  It can also mean to utter something forcefully.|例句：He hurled a stone at the window. 他猛扔一块石头砸窗户。

例句：The angry man hurled insults at his opponent.  那个愤怒的男人向对手大声辱骂。


近义词：fling, throw, cast, toss, shout, yell

反义词：receive, catch, whisper


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hurls the ball to his teammate. = 他把球扔给他的队友。
She hurls insults at her critics. = 她向她的批评者大喊大叫。


一般过去时:
He hurled the javelin a great distance. = 他把标枪投掷得很远。
She hurled herself onto the sofa in exhaustion. = 她精疲力尽地把自己扔到沙发上。


现在完成时:
He has hurled himself into his work. = 他全身心地投入到工作中。
She has hurled many accusations at the company. = 她对该公司提出了许多指控。


过去完成时:
He had hurled the rock before anyone saw him. = 在任何人看到他之前，他已经扔出了石头。
She had hurled herself into the project with great enthusiasm. = 她曾以极大的热情投入到这个项目中。


将来时:
He will hurl the spear at the target. = 他将把长矛投向目标。
She will hurl herself into the fight for justice. = 她将投身正义的斗争。


虚拟语气:
If he hurled the ball harder, he might have won the game. = 如果他把球扔得更用力，他可能就赢了比赛。
I wish he hadn't hurled those insults at me. = 我真希望他不要对我大喊大叫。


被动语态:
The ball was hurled across the field. = 球被扔过了球场。
Insults were hurled at the politician during the speech. = 在演讲期间，政客遭到辱骂。


疑问句:
Did he hurl the object intentionally? = 他是故意扔那个东西的吗？
Why did she hurl such harsh words at him? = 她为什么对他说了这么严厉的话？


否定句:
He didn't hurl the stone; he dropped it. = 他没有扔石头；他是把它掉了。
She didn't hurl accusations without evidence. = 她没有没有证据就进行指控。


PHRASE->
hurl abuse at someone: 对某人破口大骂
hurl oneself at something: 奋不顾身地去做某事
hurl insults at someone: 对某人进行辱骂
hurl a challenge at someone: 向某人挑战
hurl oneself into something: 全身心投入某事
