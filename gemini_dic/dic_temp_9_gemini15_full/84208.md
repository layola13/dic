half-ripe:/ˌhɑːfˈraɪp/| adj.|半熟的；未完全成熟的|Partially ripe; not fully mature.  It describes fruits, vegetables, or other things that are not yet fully developed but are past the completely unripe stage.|例句：The mangoes are half-ripe; they need a few more days to ripen.  芒果半熟了；它们还需要几天才能成熟。

The pears are half-ripe and slightly tart. 梨子半熟，有点酸。


近义词：underripe, partially ripe, unripe (to a lesser extent)|反义词：ripe, fully ripe, overripe
