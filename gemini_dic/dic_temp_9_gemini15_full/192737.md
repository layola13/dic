seaboat:/ˈsiːboʊt/| n. |海船，小船 |A seaboat is a small boat used on the sea, often for fishing or pleasure.  It is typically smaller than a ship. |例句：The fishermen sailed out in their seaboat before dawn.  渔民们黎明前就乘着小船出海了。|例句：We rented a seaboat for a day of exploring the coast. 我们租了一艘小船去探索海岸一天。|近义词：boat, dinghy, skiff |反义词：ship, vessel (in terms of size)
