longhouses:/ˈlɒŋˌhaʊzɪz/ | n. |长屋 |A longhouse is a traditional dwelling, typically long and narrow, used by various indigenous cultures around the world.  They often house multiple families or clans under one roof.  The specific design and construction vary greatly depending on the culture and environment. |Example: The Iroquois longhouses were renowned for their communal living arrangements.  爱尔克人长屋以其集体生活方式而闻名。|近义词：communal dwelling, communal house |反义词：individual dwelling, detached house
