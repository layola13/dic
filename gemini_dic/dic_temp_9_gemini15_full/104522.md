kalinga:/kəˈlɪŋɡə/| n. |卡林加；古代印度王国|Kalinga refers to an ancient kingdom located in present-day Odisha, India.  It was known for its powerful rulers, its strategic location, and its role in the history of Buddhism. The Kalinga War, fought between the Mauryan Emperor Ashoka and the Kalinga kingdom, is particularly significant because it led to Ashoka's conversion to Buddhism. |The Kalinga War had a profound impact on Ashoka's reign. |近义词：Odisha (in a modern context)|反义词：无|名词
