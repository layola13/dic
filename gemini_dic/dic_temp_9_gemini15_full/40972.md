coopt:/koʊˈɒpt/ | vt. | 吸收；利用；篡夺；劫持 | To take (something, especially a resource or idea) for one's own use, often without authorization; to adopt (an idea or method) for one's own use.  | Example: The company coopted his idea without giving him credit. 公司未经他同意就利用了他的主意。  | Example:  The rebels coopted the government's communication system.  叛军劫持了政府的通讯系统。|近义词：adopt, appropriate, usurp, commandeer |反义词：reject, relinquish, abandon | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They coopt new members into their club. = 他们吸收新成员加入他们的俱乐部。
The company coopts innovative ideas from startups. = 公司吸收初创企业的创新理念。


一般过去时:
The dictator coopted the opposition leader. = 独裁者收买了反对派领导人。
They coopted the resources of the rival company. = 他们利用了竞争对手公司的资源。


现在完成时:
The government has coopted many of the opposition's policies. = 政府已经采纳了许多反对党的政策。
She has coopted elements of several different styles in her artwork. = 她在自己的艺术作品中融合了几种不同风格的元素。


过去完成时:
The rebels had coopted the city's communication network before the government could react. = 政府反应过来之前，叛军已经控制了城市的通讯网络。
They had coopted the technology before anyone else noticed its potential. = 在其他人注意到其潜力之前，他们就已经利用了这项技术。


将来时:
The corporation will coopt the smaller firm's technology. = 该公司将利用这家小型公司的技术。
They will coopt the best ideas from the brainstorming session. = 他们将采用头脑风暴会议中最好的想法。


虚拟语气:
If they coopted his expertise, the project would succeed. = 如果他们利用了他的专业知识，项目就会成功。
I wish they had not coopted my design without permission. = 我真希望他们没有未经允许就使用我的设计。


被动语态:
His ideas were coopted by the larger corporation. = 他的想法被大公司利用了。
The resources were coopted for the new project. = 资源被用于新的项目。


疑问句:
Were his ideas coopted without his consent? = 他的想法是否未经他同意就被利用了？
Will their technology be coopted by competitors? = 他们的技术会被竞争对手利用吗？


否定句:
They did not coopt his ideas, instead they gave him credit. = 他们没有利用他的想法，而是给了他应有的功劳。
The company refused to coopt the controversial strategy. = 公司拒绝采用有争议的战略。


PHRASE->
coopt into: 吸纳入...
coopt for:  为...而利用
coopt from:  从...中获取
coopt onto:  接纳...为成员
coopt upon:  利用，征用
