login's:/ˈlɒɡɪnz/ /ˈlɑːɡɪnz/| n. |登录（的）；登录信息|The plural of "login," referring to multiple logins or the process of logging in.|例句：The system requires multiple logins for enhanced security.  系统需要多次登录以增强安全性。
例句：He recorded all his logins in a secure password manager. 他在一个安全的密码管理器中记录了他所有的登录信息。|近义词：sign-ins, accesses|反义词：logouts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He logs in every morning. = 他每天早上登录。
They login to the system regularly. = 他们定期登录系统。

一般过去时:
I logged in yesterday. = 我昨天登录了。
She logged into her account last night. = 她昨晚登录了她的账户。

现在完成时:
I have logged in three times today. = 我今天已经登录三次了。
They have logged in since the update. = 他们自从更新后就登录了。

过去完成时:
I had logged in before the meeting started. = 会议开始前我已经登录了。
She had logged in many times before the error occurred. = 在错误发生之前，她已经登录过很多次了。

将来时:
I will log in tomorrow. = 我明天将登录。
He will log in after the system is updated. = 系统更新后，他将登录。

虚拟语气:
If I had logged in earlier, I would have seen the message. = 如果我早点登录，我就看到了那条消息。
It's time we logged in and started working. = 我们该登录开始工作了。

被动语态:
The account was logged into by an unauthorized user. = 该账户被未经授权的用户登录了。
All logins are monitored for security reasons. = 出于安全原因，所有登录都会被监控。

疑问句:
Have you logged in yet? = 你登录了吗？
When did he log in? = 他什么时候登录的？

否定句:
I haven't logged in yet. = 我还没有登录。
He didn't log in this morning. = 他今天早上没有登录。
