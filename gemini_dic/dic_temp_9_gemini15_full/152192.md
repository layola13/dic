patricianism:/pəˈtrɪʃənɪzəm/| n. |贵族统治；贵族气派；特权阶层；贵族习气 |The social and political system in which a small privileged class rules.  It often implies arrogance, aloofness, and a disdain for the common people.  |例句：The rise of the merchant class challenged the established patricianism of the city.  = 商人阶层的兴起挑战了这座城市既有的贵族统治。

例句：His patricianism was evident in his disdain for anything considered "common." = 他对任何被认为是“普通”的事物的轻蔑，显露出了他的贵族习气。


近义词：aristocracy, elitism, high society, upper-class dominance

反义词：egalitarianism, democracy, populism


词性：名词
