morgues:/mɔːrɡz/| n.|太平间，停尸房|A morgue is a place where dead bodies are kept, especially before they are buried or cremated.  It is often part of a hospital or a medical examiner's office. |例句：The police took the victim's body to the morgue for an autopsy. 警方将受害者的遗体送往太平间进行尸检。


The body was identified at the morgue.  遗体在太平间被辨认出来。|近义词：mortuary, funeral home|反义词:None.
