/kræft/| n.|工艺，手艺，技巧|Skill in a particular craft;  a job or activity that requires special skill|The potter showed great craft in shaping the clay. 制陶匠在塑造粘土时展现了精湛的技艺。  She learned the craft of weaving from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里学会了编织的手艺。|近义词：skill, art, trade|反义词：clumsiness, ineptitude|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He practices his craft every day. = 他每天练习他的手艺。
Her craft is renowned worldwide. = 她的手艺在世界各地享有盛名。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
