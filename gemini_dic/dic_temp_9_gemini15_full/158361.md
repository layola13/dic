plea-bargain:/ˈpliː bɑːrɡən/| n. |认罪协商；认罪减刑| An agreement in a criminal case where the defendant pleads guilty to a lesser charge (or fewer charges) in exchange for a lighter sentence. This avoids a trial and saves time and resources for the court.  | Example: The defendant entered into a plea bargain, pleading guilty to manslaughter instead of murder, receiving a reduced sentence.  认罪被告人与检方达成认罪协议，对误杀罪认罪而不是谋杀罪，从而获得减刑。|近义词：negotiated settlement, guilty plea|反义词：going to trial, full trial
