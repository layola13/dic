hyperpolarization:/ˌhaɪpərˌpoʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |超极化|The increase in the negativity of the membrane potential of a cell, making it more negative than the resting potential.|例句：Hyperpolarization of neurons can inhibit action potential generation.  神经元的超极化可以抑制动作电位的产生。


例句：The drug induced hyperpolarization in the nerve cells.  这种药物导致神经细胞超极化。
