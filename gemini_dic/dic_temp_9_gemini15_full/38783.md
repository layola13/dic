conductance:/kənˈdʌktəns/| n.|电导率，传导性|The ability of a material to conduct electricity or heat.  It is the reciprocal of resistance.|例句：The conductance of the wire is high. 这根导线的电导率很高。
例句：Good thermal conductance is essential for efficient heat dissipation. 良好的热传导性对于有效的散热至关重要。|近义词：conductivity, transmissibility|反义词：resistance, resistivity|名词
