freehand:/ˈfriːhænd/| adj.|徒手的；自由发挥的；未经设计的|Done without the aid of instruments or guidelines; improvised or impromptu.|例句：He made a freehand drawing of the landscape. 他徒手画了一幅风景画。|例句：She gave a freehand speech, without any notes. 她即兴演讲，没有任何笔记。|近义词：improvised, impromptu, extempore|反义词：planned, pre-meditated, designed
