civilization:/ˌsɪvəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |文明；文化；开化 |The state of being civilized or cultured; the process of becoming civilized; a particular stage of cultural or social development; a society or group showing a high level of cultural and technological development.  |例句：The ancient civilization of Egypt left behind many impressive monuments. 古埃及文明留下了许多令人印象深刻的纪念碑。

例句：The development of writing was a major step forward in human civilization.  文字的发展是人类文明向前迈出的重要一步。


近义词：culture, society, refinement, progress |反义词：barbarism, savagery, primitiveness


词源：源自于古法语 *civilisation*,  最终源自于拉丁语 *civilis* ("civic", "of citizens"),  *civilis* 的词根是 *civis* ("citizen").  因此，civilization 本质上指代的是公民社会的状态和发展。
