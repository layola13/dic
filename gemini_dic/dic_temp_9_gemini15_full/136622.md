nonsaturated:/ˌnɒnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. |不饱和的；未饱和的 | Not saturated; containing less than the maximum possible amount of a substance.  Unsaturated fats, for example, do not contain the maximum amount of hydrogen atoms that could be bonded to the carbon atoms in their structure. | Example:  Nonsaturated fats are considered healthier than saturated fats. 不饱和脂肪被认为比饱和脂肪更健康。  The nonsaturated solution could dissolve more solute. 未饱和溶液可以溶解更多溶质。 |近义词：unsaturated, undersaturated | 反义词：saturated, supersaturated
