herbalism:/ˈɜːrbəlɪzəm/| n. |草药学，草药疗法|The study and practice of using herbs for medicinal purposes.  Herbalism encompasses the cultivation, harvesting, preparation, and use of plants for therapeutic benefits. It often draws upon traditional knowledge and practices, but modern herbalism also incorporates scientific research.|例句：She is studying herbalism at a renowned school. 她正在一所著名的学校学习草药学。|例句：Many people turn to herbalism for alternative medicine. 许多人转向草药疗法寻求替代医学。|近义词：phytotherapy, herbology|反义词：allopathy (conventional medicine)
