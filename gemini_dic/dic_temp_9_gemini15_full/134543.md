nonliquefying: /ˌnɒnˈlɪkwɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 不液化的；不使液化的 | Not becoming or causing to become liquid.  This term is primarily used in scientific and technical contexts, particularly relating to materials science and geotechnical engineering.  It describes substances or processes that resist liquefaction. |

例句：

The nonliquefying soil remained stable during the earthquake. = 在地震期间，不液化的土壤保持稳定。

The nonliquefying nature of the concrete prevented foundation failure. = 混凝土不液化的特性防止了地基破坏。


近义词：solid, stable, unliquefied

反义词：liquefying, liquefiable


词性：形容词
