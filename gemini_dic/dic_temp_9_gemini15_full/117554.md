megachile:/ˈmɛɡəkaɪl/| n. |切叶蜂属|Megachile is a genus of bees in the family Megachilidae.  They are known for their habit of cutting leaf pieces to construct nests.  Different species use different materials, including leaves, resin, and flower petals. |例句：The female megachile carefully cut a perfect circle from the leaf.  雌性切叶蜂小心翼翼地从叶子上剪下一个完美的圆圈。

例句：Several species of megachile are important pollinators of various crops.  几种切叶蜂是多种作物的重要传粉者。|近义词：leafcutter bee (更通俗的称呼)|反义词：无|词性：名词
