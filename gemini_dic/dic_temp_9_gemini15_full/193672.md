self-cognizance:/ˌsɛlfˈkɑːɡnɪzəns/ | n. |自我认知，自我意识 | The awareness or understanding of one's own character, feelings, motives, and desires.  It involves introspection and reflection on one's own mental processes and experiences. | Example:  Developing a strong sense of self-cognizance is crucial for personal growth.  发展强烈的自我认知感对个人成长至关重要。

|Example: Through therapy, she gained a deeper self-cognizance and understood her own emotional patterns better. 通过治疗，她获得了更深层次的自我认知，并更好地理解了自己的情绪模式。|近义词：self-awareness, self-knowledge, introspection |反义词：self-deception, unawareness |名词
