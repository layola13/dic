shortly:/ˈʃɔːrtli/ | adv. |不久，很快；简短地，简洁地 |In a short time; soon.  Briefly; concisely.|例句：I'll be back shortly. 我很快就会回来。|例句：He spoke shortly and to the point. 他简短扼要地发言了。|近义词：soon, briefly, concisely |反义词：lengthily, extensively, at length

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks shortly and directly. = 他说话简短直接。
We usually finish our work shortly before noon. = 我们通常在中午之前不久完成工作。


一般过去时:
They finished the project shortly after the deadline. = 他们在大限之后不久完成了项目。
She spoke shortly but effectively. = 她说话简短但有效。


现在完成时:
We have shortly completed the first phase of the project. = 我们已经完成了项目的第一个阶段。
I have shortly explained the main points. = 我已经简要地解释了要点。


过去完成时:
They had shortly finished eating when the guests arrived. = 客人到达时，他们刚吃完饭不久。
She had shortly summarized the report before presenting it. = 她在陈述报告之前，已经简要地总结了报告。


将来时:
I will be there shortly. = 我一会儿就到。
The meeting will shortly commence. = 会议很快就要开始了。


虚拟语气:
If I had known you were coming, I would have prepared shortly. = 如果我知道你要来，我会简短地准备一下。
I wish he had spoken more shortly. = 我希望他说话更简短一些。


被动语态:
The matter will be dealt with shortly. = 这件事很快就会得到处理。
The report was summarized shortly for the board meeting. = 报告为董事会会议做了简短的总结。


疑问句:
Will you be finished shortly? = 你很快就会完成吗？
Could you explain that shortly? = 你能简要解释一下吗？


否定句:
The meeting did not last shortly; it went on for hours. = 会议持续时间不短；它进行了数小时。
He didn't explain the situation shortly; he went into great detail. = 他没有简要地解释情况；他详细地说明了。


PHRASE->
shortly after = 不久之后
shortly before = 不久之前
shortly thereafter = 此后不久
shortly thereafter = 之后不久
in short/shortly = 简而言之
