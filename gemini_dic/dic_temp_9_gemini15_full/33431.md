churchyard:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃjɑːrd/| n. |教堂墓地|A churchyard is the ground surrounding a church, often used as a burial ground.  It is typically consecrated ground. |The old churchyard was filled with ancient gravestones.  古老的教堂墓地里布满了古老的墓碑。
The family decided to bury their loved one in the quiet churchyard.  这个家庭决定把他们心爱的人埋葬在宁静的教堂墓地。|graveyard, cemetery| (几乎没有反义词，可以考虑用living space 或 non-sacred ground, 但意义相差较大)|名词
