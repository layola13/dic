dyspraxic:/dɪsˈpræksɪk/| adj.|发育性运动技能障碍的；患有运动技能障碍的 | Relating to or characteristic of dyspraxia, a neurological disorder affecting motor skills.  Dyspraxic individuals may have difficulty with coordination, fine motor skills, and planning movements. | Example: The child's dyspraxic tendencies made writing and drawing challenging.  这个孩子的发育性运动技能障碍使得书写和绘画具有挑战性。|近义词：clumsy, uncoordinated | 反义词：coordinated, dexterous
