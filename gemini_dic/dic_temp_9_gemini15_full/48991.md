deglazing:/diːˈɡleɪzɪŋ/| v.|去釉；脱釉；(烹调)上釉；|To remove the glaze from something, typically pottery or a cooking pan. In cooking, it refers to the process of adding liquid to a pan to loosen browned bits from the bottom, creating a flavorful sauce.|例句：The potter deglazed the ceramic piece to reveal the underlying clay. 陶工去除了陶瓷上的釉彩以露出底下的粘土。
The chef deglazed the pan with white wine to make a delicious sauce. 厨师用白葡萄酒给平底锅去釉，做成美味的酱汁。|近义词：remove glaze, disglaze|反义词：glazing
