crackheads: /ˈkrækˌhɛdz/ | n. | 瘾君子，吸毒成瘾者 | A crackhead is a derogatory term for someone addicted to crack cocaine.  The word combines "crack" (the drug) and "head" (referring to the user's mind or person). It is highly offensive and should be avoided.  | Example:  The police arrested several crackheads near the park.  警察在公园附近逮捕了几名瘾君子。  |近义词: addict, drug abuser, junkie (all highly offensive) |反义词: None (the word describes a negative condition) |
