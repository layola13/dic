defensively:/dɪˈfɛnsɪvli/ | adv. | 防御性地；戒备地；以防卫的姿态 | acting in a way that shows you feel you are being attacked or criticised | He responded defensively to the criticism. 他对批评作出了防御性的回应。 | aggressively, protectively | offensively, calmly | 副词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts defensively when questioned. = 当受到质问时，他表现得很防御。
She speaks defensively about her past mistakes. = 她谈到自己过去的错误时，态度很防御。


一般过去时:

She answered defensively when accused of lying. = 当她被指控说谎时，她以防御的姿态回答。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
