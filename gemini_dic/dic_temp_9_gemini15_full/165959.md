preparatorily:/prɪˈpærətɔːrɪli/| adv. |预备地，准备性地 |In a way that prepares for something.  It indicates that something is done in advance to get ready for a later event or action.|例句：He studied preparatorily for the exam. 他为考试做了准备。

例句：The team met preparatorily to discuss their strategy.  团队预先会面讨论策略。


近义词：in preparation, beforehand, in advance, preliminarily


反义词：haphazardly, carelessly, unpreparedly


词性：副词
