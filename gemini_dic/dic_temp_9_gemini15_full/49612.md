demi-monde:/ˈdɛmiˈmɒnd/| n. |半社会，交际花圈，上流社会边缘人物 |The demi-monde refers to a social group composed of wealthy but morally questionable individuals, often those on the fringes of high society, such as courtesans and their associates.  It suggests a world of luxury and intrigue, but also one marked by social exclusion and a lack of respectability. |The novel depicted the decadent lifestyle of the Parisian demi-monde. 这部小说描绘了巴黎半社会阶层颓废的生活方式。|近义词：high society (but with negative connotations), underworld, fringe society|反义词：high society (with positive connotations), respectable society
