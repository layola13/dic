mesosphere:/ˈmɛsəsfɪər/| n.|中间层|The mesosphere is the layer of the Earth's atmosphere above the stratosphere and below the thermosphere.  It is characterized by decreasing temperature with increasing altitude. |例句：Meteors burn up in the mesosphere.  流星在中间层燃烧殆尽。|例句：The mesosphere is a region of the atmosphere where temperatures decrease with altitude.|近义词：stratosphere, thermosphere|反义词：troposphere (in terms of proximity to the Earth's surface)
