caudex:/ˈkɔːdɛks/| n. |木本植物的根茎；树干基部膨大的部分|A caudex is a thickened, woody stem base, often found in desert plants. It serves as a water storage organ, allowing the plant to survive periods of drought.  |例句：The succulent plant has a large, prominent caudex.  这棵多肉植物有一个又大又突出的根茎。|例句：Many desert plants develop a caudex to survive harsh conditions.  许多沙漠植物进化出根茎来适应恶劣的环境。|近义词：bulb, tuber, rhizome |反义词：无
