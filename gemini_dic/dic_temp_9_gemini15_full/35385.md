co-operated:/koʊˈɒpəreɪtɪd/ | v. |合作，协作 | To work together for a common purpose.  |The two companies co-operated on the project.  两家公司在这个项目上进行了合作。| collaborated, cooperated, collaborated | competed, opposed |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They co-operate on most projects. = 他们在大多数项目上都合作。
We co-operate closely with our clients. = 我们与客户紧密合作。


一般过去时:
The team co-operated effectively to meet the deadline. = 团队有效地合作以完成最后期限。
They co-operated fully with the investigation. = 他们全力配合调查。


现在完成时:
We have co-operated with this charity for many years. = 我们与这家慈善机构合作多年了。
They have co-operated successfully on several occasions. = 他们多次成功合作。


过去完成时:
They had co-operated before on similar projects. = 他们之前曾在类似项目上合作过。
The groups had co-operated extensively prior to the merger. = 在合并之前，各集团进行了广泛的合作。


将来时:
We will co-operate fully with the authorities. = 我们将全力配合当局。
They will co-operate to achieve their shared goals. = 他们将合作以实现共同的目标。


虚拟语气:
If they co-operated, the project would be completed on time. = 如果他们合作，项目就能按时完成。
I wish they had co-operated more effectively. = 我希望他们合作更有效率。


被动语态:
The project was co-operated on by several teams. = 该项目由几个团队合作完成。(注意：被动语态用词略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The information was co-operated on by multiple sources. = 信息由多个来源合作提供。(注意：被动语态用词略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Did they co-operate with the police? = 他们配合警方了吗？
Will they co-operate on this difficult task? = 他们会合作完成这项艰巨的任务吗？


否定句:
They did not co-operate with the investigation. = 他们没有配合调查。
The two parties refused to co-operate. = 双方拒绝合作。


PHRASE->
co-operate with = 与...合作
co-operate fully = 全力配合
co-operate effectively = 有效合作
co-operate on a project = 合作一个项目
co-operate in achieving a goal = 合作实现目标
