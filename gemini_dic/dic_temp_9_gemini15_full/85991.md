headstands: /ˈhɛdˌstændz/ | n. | 倒立 |  A physical exercise or yoga pose in which one balances on their head and hands, with the body inverted.  | Example:  She practices headstands every morning to improve her balance. 她每天早上练习倒立以提高平衡能力。|近义词：inversion, head balance | 反义词：handstand (though this is a different pose)
