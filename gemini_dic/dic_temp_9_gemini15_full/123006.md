monarchize:/ˈmɒnəkəraɪz/| vt.|君主统治；使成为君主国|To govern or rule as a monarch; to establish a monarchy in (a country or territory).  The act of establishing or supporting a monarchy.|例句：The people tried to monarchize the country after the revolution.  革命后，人民试图使国家成为君主国。

例句：The ambitious general attempted to monarchize the republic.  野心勃勃的将军试图将共和国变成君主国。


近义词：reign, rule, govern,  king, queen

反义词：democratize, republicanize
