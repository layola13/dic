fettle:/ˈfɛtl/ | n. |状态，状况；健康状况 | The state of repair or working order of something;  a person's state of health or fitness. |例句：He's in good fettle. 他状态良好。 |例句：The car is in excellent fettle. 这辆车状况极佳。|近义词：condition, state, shape, trim |反义词：disrepair, poor condition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is in good fettle. = 这台机器状况良好。
He is always in excellent fettle. = 他总是状态极佳。


一般过去时:
The car was in poor fettle after the accident. = 事故后，这辆车状况很差。
She was not in good fettle after her illness. = 她生病后身体状况不佳。


现在完成时:
The engine has been in good fettle since the repair. = 自从修理后，发动机一直状况良好。
He has remained in excellent fettle despite his age. = 尽管上了年纪，他仍然保持着极佳的状态。


过去完成时:
The equipment had been in poor fettle for months before it finally broke down. = 设备在最终坏掉之前已经状况不佳好几个月了。
She had not been in good fettle for weeks leading up to the competition. = 在比赛前的几周里，她的身体状况一直不好。


将来时:
The project will be in good fettle by next month. = 到下个月为止，这个项目将会进展顺利。
He will be in top fettle for the marathon. = 他将以最佳状态参加马拉松比赛。


虚拟语气:
If the equipment were in better fettle, we could finish the job sooner. = 如果设备状况更好，我们就能更快地完成工作。
I wish I were in better fettle to go hiking. = 我希望我的身体状况更好，以便去远足。


被动语态:
The car was left in a sorry fettle after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，汽车状况很糟糕。
His health was brought into good fettle by the new treatment. = 新疗法使他的健康状况得到改善。


疑问句:
Is the machinery in good fettle? = 机器状况良好吗？
Is he in fettle enough to play the game? = 他的身体状况足以参加比赛吗？


否定句:
The house is not in good fettle. = 这房子状况不好。
He is not in the best fettle today. = 他今天状态不太好。


PHRASE->
in good fettle = 状态良好
in poor fettle = 状态不佳
out of fettle = 状况不佳；身体不适
get into fettle = 使…恢复健康；使…处于良好状态
to be in fine fettle = 处于极佳状态


词源:
The word "fettle" ultimately derives from the Old English word *fæteln, meaning "to fasten, to fit."  Over time, its meaning shifted to refer to the state of being fastened or fitted together, and then more broadly to the general condition or state of something.
