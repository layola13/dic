lurks:/lɜːrks/ | v. |潜伏；潜藏；暗藏| To exist unseen or unnoticed, or to wait in hiding, especially with harmful intentions.  |例句：Danger lurks around every corner. 危险潜伏在每一个角落。|例句：A killer lurks in the shadows.  一个杀手潜伏在阴影中。|近义词：hide, conceal, lie in wait,  |反义词：reveal, expose, show
