puritanic:/ˌpjʊərɪˈtænɪk/| adj.|清教徒式的；严厉禁欲的；过分拘谨的 |Characterized by or associated with the Puritans or Puritanism; austere, strict, and morally rigorous.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying excessive severity and a lack of tolerance for pleasure or enjoyment. |例句：His puritanic upbringing instilled in him a strong sense of morality.  他的清教徒式教养在他心中灌输了强烈的道德感。

例句：The puritanic atmosphere of the school discouraged any form of fun or relaxation. 这所学校清教徒式的氛围使任何形式的娱乐或放松都受到抑制。


近义词：austere, ascetic, strict, rigid, severe, moralistic

反义词：lax, permissive, indulgent, hedonistic, liberal
