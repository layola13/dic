nonappearance: /ˌnɒnəˈpɪərəns/ | n. |不出席；缺席；未出现 | The absence of someone or something expected to be present.  It often implies a failure to attend or appear, sometimes indicating negligence or disregard. |例句：His nonappearance at the meeting raised concerns.  他的缺席引起了担忧。

例句：The lawyer's nonappearance in court resulted in a default judgment. 律师未出庭导致缺席判决。


近义词：absence, default, nonattendance, failure to appear

反义词：presence, attendance, appearance


词源：

* **non-**:  Prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  Derived from Latin "non".
* **appearance**: From Old French "aparance", ultimately from Latin "apparere" meaning "to appear".
