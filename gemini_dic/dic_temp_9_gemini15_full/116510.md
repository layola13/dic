mateship:/ˈmeɪtʃɪp/| n. |友谊；伙伴关系；同志情谊|A close friendship or companionship, typically between men, characterized by loyalty, mutual respect, and a sense of shared experience.  It often implies a sense of equality and shared responsibility.|  The mateship between the two soldiers was evident in their unwavering support for each other.  两个士兵之间的友谊在他们对彼此坚定不移的支持中显而易见。| friendship, camaraderie, brotherhood| enmity, antagonism| 名词
