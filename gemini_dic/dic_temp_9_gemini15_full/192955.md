secedes:/səˈsiːdz/| v.|脱离，退出，分裂|To withdraw formally from membership of a federation or body, especially a political state.|The southern states seceded from the Union in 1861, leading to the Civil War.  南方各州于1861年脱离联邦，导致了内战。|The colony seceded from the empire.  殖民地脱离了帝国。|近义词：withdraw, separate, depart|反义词：join, unite, adhere
