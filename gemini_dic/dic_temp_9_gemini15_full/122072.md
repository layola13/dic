mistful: /ˈmɪstfʊl/ | adj. | 充满薄雾的；朦胧的；忧郁的；伤感怀旧的 |  Suggestive of mist; foggy; slightly hazy; characterized by or suggestive of melancholy or pensiveness.  |


例句：

The mistful morning shrouded the valley in a soft, ethereal light. = 薄雾弥漫的早晨笼罩着山谷，呈现出柔和、空灵的光线。

Her eyes held a mistful sadness, a longing for a time long past. = 她的眼神中充满了忧郁的悲伤，是对逝去年代的渴望。


近义词：foggy, hazy, misty, dreamy, melancholic, wistful


反义词：clear, bright, sunny, cheerful, joyful


词性: 形容词
