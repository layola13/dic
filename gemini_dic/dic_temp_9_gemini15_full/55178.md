dissensions:/dɪˈsenʃənz/ | n. |不和；意见分歧；争吵 | Discord or disagreement among people.  The dissensions within the party threatened to split it in two.  内部的意见分歧可能会导致该党分裂。|近义词：discord, strife, conflict, disagreement |反义词：harmony, accord, agreement, consensus |名词
