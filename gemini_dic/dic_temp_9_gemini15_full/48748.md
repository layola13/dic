definiteness:/ˈdɛfɪnɪtnəs/ | n. |确定性，明确性 |The quality of being definite; the state of being clearly expressed or understood; precision.  It refers to the grammatical feature in language that indicates whether a noun phrase refers to a specific or non-specific entity. |例句：The definiteness of the contract terms prevented any later disputes.  合同条款的明确性避免了以后的任何争议。

The lack of definiteness in his answer made me suspicious. 他回答的不确定性让我起了疑心。|近义词：certainty, precision, clarity, exactitude |反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, uncertainty, indefiniteness |名词
