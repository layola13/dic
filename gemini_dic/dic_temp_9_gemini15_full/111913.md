loiterer: /ˈlɔɪtərər/ | n. |闲逛者，游手好闲者 | A person who loiters; someone who spends time idly or without purpose. |例句：The police told the loiterers to move along.  警察让那些闲逛的人走开。|例句：He was arrested for being a loiterer near the bank. 他因在银行附近闲逛而被捕。|近义词：idler, vagrant, straggler, dawdler |反义词：worker, busybody
