misguide:/mɪsˈɡaɪd/| vt.| 误导，使误入歧途 | To lead someone in the wrong direction, either literally or figuratively.  |例句：The faulty map misguided us, and we ended up lost in the woods.  错误的地图误导了我们，我们最终迷失在树林里。|例句：His bad advice misguided her into making a poor investment. 他的坏建议误导了她，让她做了一笔糟糕的投资。|近义词：mislead, deceive, delude |反义词：guide, direct, enlighten |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misguides his followers with false promises. = 他用虚假的承诺误导他的追随者。
The signpost misguides tourists to the wrong path. = 路标误导游客走错了路。

一般过去时:
The unreliable information misguided the investigators. = 不可靠的信息误导了调查人员。
The misleading advertisement misguided many consumers. = 误导性的广告误导了许多消费者。

现在完成时:
The rumors have misguided public opinion. = 谣言已经误导了公众舆论。
His actions have misguided many young people. = 他的行为已经误导了许多年轻人。

过去完成时:
The faulty instructions had misguided the workers. = 有缺陷的指示已经误导了工人。
The outdated map had misguided the explorers.  过时的地图已经误导了探险家们。

将来时:
Poor planning will misguided the project. = 糟糕的计划将会误导这个项目。
The unclear instructions will misguided the participants. = 模糊的指示将会误导参与者。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been misguided, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我没有被误导，我就不会犯那个错误。
If she hadn't been misguided by her friends, she would have chosen a different career. = 如果她没有被朋友误导，她就会选择不同的职业。

被动语态:
Many people were misguided by the false propaganda. = 许多人被虚假宣传误导了。
The students were misguided by the teacher's incorrect explanation. = 学生们被老师的错误解释误导了。

疑问句:

Were the investors misguided by the company's financial reports? = 投资者是被公司的财务报告误导了吗？

否定句:
He did not misguided anyone intentionally. = 他并没有故意误导任何人。
The guide did not misguided the hikers. = 导游没有误导徒步旅行者。
