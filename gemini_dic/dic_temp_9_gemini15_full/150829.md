paraglided:/ˈpærəɡlaɪdɪd/| v. |滑翔伞飞行|Past tense and past participle of paraglide.  It describes the act of flying a paraglider. |例句：He paraglided over the mountains. 他在山间滑翔伞飞行。

例句：They paraglided from the top of the cliff. 他们从悬崖顶上滑翔伞飞行。


近义词：flew (a paraglider), soared


反义词：None directly related.  Antonyms would relate to the opposite of flying, such as walking or driving.

词性: Verb (Past tense and past participle)
