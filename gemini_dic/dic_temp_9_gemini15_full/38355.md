concentrating:/ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 专注，集中；浓缩 | To focus one's attention or efforts on a particular thing.  The act of making something denser or more concentrated. | 例句：He was concentrating on his work. 他正专注于他的工作。

She was concentrating the juice by boiling it. 她正在通过煮沸来浓缩果汁。


近义词： focusing, centering,  | 反义词：distracting, dispersing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am concentrating on my studies. = 我正在专注于我的学习。
She concentrates her energy on her career. = 她把精力集中在她的事业上。


一般过去时:
I concentrated on the lecture. = 我专心听讲座。
They concentrated their efforts on finding a solution. = 他们集中精力寻找解决办法。


现在完成时:
I have been concentrating on this project all week. = 我整个星期都一直专注于这个项目。
She has concentrated on improving her skills. = 她一直专注于提高自己的技能。


过去完成时:
I had concentrated on my studies before the exam. = 我在考试前一直专注于我的学习。
They had concentrated their resources before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前集中了他们的资源。


将来时:
I will concentrate on finishing this report tomorrow. = 我明天将集中精力完成这份报告。
She will concentrate on her presentation next week. = 她下周将专注于她的演示文稿。


虚拟语气:
If I concentrated harder, I would pass the exam. = 如果我更努力地集中注意力，我就能通过考试。
I wish I had concentrated more during the lesson. = 我希望我在课堂上更专注一些。


被动语态:
The resources were concentrated in the urban areas. = 资源集中在城市地区。
Attention should be concentrated on the main points. = 注意力应该集中在要点上。


疑问句:
Are you concentrating on what I'm saying? = 你专心听我说吗？
What are you concentrating on right now? = 你现在专注于什么？


否定句:
I couldn't concentrate on the meeting. = 我无法集中精力参加会议。
He didn't concentrate on his driving, causing an accident. = 他开车时没有集中注意力，导致了事故。
