importation:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 进口，输入 | The act of importing goods or services into a country.  It involves bringing goods from a foreign country into one's own country for sale or use.  This process is subject to various regulations and tariffs. | Example: The importation of cheap foreign goods has hurt domestic industries.  进口廉价外国商品损害了国内产业。 The recent increase in food importation is a concern for food security. 食品进口的近期增加令人担忧食品安全。|近义词：import, introduction | 反义词：exportation, exportation

The government regulates the importation of certain goods. = 政府规范某些商品的进口。
High tariffs discourage the importation of luxury items. = 高关税抑制奢侈品的进口。


一般过去时:
