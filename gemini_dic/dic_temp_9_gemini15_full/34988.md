clogs:/klɒɡz/| n. |木底鞋；阻塞；障碍物 |Wooden shoes; obstructions or blockages.  Clogs are typically made of wood and are worn to protect the feet from water and mud. They can also refer to something that blocks or obstructs. |例句：My shoes are clogs. = 我的鞋子是木底鞋。

The sink is clogged with hair. = 水槽被头发堵住了。|近义词：sabots, clogs, obstructions, impediments |反义词：clearances, openings


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drain clogs easily. = 下水道很容易堵塞。
These shoes clog my feet. = 这些鞋子让我的脚感觉很紧。

一般过去时:
The pipe clogged last night. = 管子昨晚堵住了。
The heavy rain clogged the streets. = 大雨堵塞了街道。

现在完成时:
The rain has clogged the drains. = 雨水已经堵塞了下水道。
The snow has clogged the roads. =  积雪已经堵塞了道路。

过去完成时:
The pipe had clogged before the plumber arrived. = 水管在水管工到达之前就已经堵住了。
The storm had clogged the highway before we left. = 我们出发前暴风雨已经堵塞了高速公路。

将来时:
The heavy snow will clog the roads. = 大雪将会堵塞道路。
If it rains heavily, the drains will clog. = 如果下大雨，下水道就会堵塞。

虚拟语气:
If the drain weren't clogged, the water would flow freely. = 如果下水道没有堵塞，水就会自由流动。
I wish the pipes weren't clogged. = 我希望管道没有堵塞。

被动语态:
The drain was clogged by leaves. = 下水道被树叶堵住了。
The streets were clogged by the traffic. = 街道被交通堵塞了。

疑问句:
Is the drain clogged? = 下水道堵了吗？
Are the roads clogged with snow? = 道路被雪堵住了吗？

否定句:
The drain isn't clogged. = 下水道没有堵塞。
The roads weren't clogged yesterday. = 道路昨天没有堵塞。
