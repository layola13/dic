overpraise:/ˈoʊvərpreɪz/| vt.|过分赞扬，吹捧|To praise excessively or to a degree that is not justified.|例句：Don't overpraise him; it will only spoil him. 不要过分赞扬他；那只会宠坏他。

例句：The critics overpraised the film, ignoring its flaws. 批评家们对这部电影赞誉有加，忽略了它的缺陷。


近义词：extol, laud, acclaim, eulogize|反义词：criticize, belittle, denigrate|动词

They overpraise their children. = 他们过分赞扬他们的孩子。
He overpraises his own work. = 他过分赞扬自己的作品。
