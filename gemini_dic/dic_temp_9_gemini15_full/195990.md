sentience:/ˈsɛnʃəns/| n. |感知能力；有感觉；意识|The capacity to feel, perceive, or experience subjectively.  It often implies consciousness and awareness, but the precise definition is debated.  The term is frequently used in discussions of animal rights, artificial intelligence, and philosophy of mind.|例句：Scientists are debating the sentience of animals, especially higher mammals. 科学家们正在争论动物，特别是高级哺乳动物的感知能力。

例句：The question of whether AI can achieve true sentience remains a topic of much discussion.  人工智能是否能够获得真正的感知能力仍然是一个讨论的热门话题。


近义词：consciousness, awareness, feeling, perception|反义词：insentience, unconsciousness, unawareness|词性：名词
