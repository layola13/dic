misediting:/ˌmɪsˈɛdɪtɪŋ/| n.|错误编辑，误编辑|The act of editing something incorrectly, resulting in errors or inaccuracies.  It can refer to mistakes made during the process of revising and correcting text, code, or other materials.|Example: The misediting of the manuscript led to several factual errors in the published book.  错误的稿件编辑导致出版书籍中出现几个事实错误。|近义词：incorrect editing, faulty editing, flawed editing|反义词：accurate editing, correct editing|名词
