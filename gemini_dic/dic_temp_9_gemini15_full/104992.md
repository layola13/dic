kenneled:/ˈkenld/| v.|  关进狗窝；寄养（宠物）| to place or keep (an animal, especially a dog) in a kennel | The dog was kenneled overnight while its owner was away.  这只狗主人不在时，一夜之间被关在狗窝里。The family kenneled their golden retriever while they went on vacation.  这家人度假时把他们的金毛猎犬寄养了。| boarded, housed | freed, released | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He kennels his dog every night. = 他每天晚上都把他的狗关进狗窝。
They kennel their pets during their business trips. = 他们出差期间把宠物寄养起来。

一般过去时:
She kenneled her dog while she was on vacation. = 她度假期间把狗寄养了。
We kenneled the puppies for the night. = 我们把小狗关了一夜。

现在完成时:
I have kenneled my dog several times. = 我已经把我的狗寄养过几次了。
They haven't kenneled their cat before. = 他们以前没有寄养过他们的猫。

过去完成时:
He had kenneled his dog before leaving for the conference. = 他在去参加会议之前已经把狗寄养了。
She had never kenneled a dog before that experience. = 在那次经历之前，她从未寄养过狗。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had a dog, I would kennel it when I travel. = 如果我有一条狗，我会在旅行时把它寄养。
I wish I could kennel my dog more often. = 我希望我能更经常地把我的狗寄养。
