extradited:/ˈekstrədaɪtɪd/ | v. |引渡，引渡犯人 |To surrender (a person accused or convicted of a crime) to the jurisdiction of another state or country.  |The fugitive was extradited from Mexico to the United States to face trial.  逃犯被从墨西哥引渡到美国受审。|deport, rendition |retain, keep |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government extradites criminals to other countries. = 政府将罪犯引渡到其他国家。
They usually extradite suspects quickly. = 他们通常会迅速引渡嫌疑人。


一般过去时:
The court ordered that he be extradited. = 法院下令将他引渡。
The criminal was extradited last week. = 罪犯上周被引渡。


现在完成时:
Several suspects have been extradited since the investigation began. = 自调查开始以来，已有几名嫌疑人被引渡。
The country has never extradited a citizen before. = 这个国家以前从未引渡过公民。


过去完成时:
The police had already extradited the suspect before the trial started. = 在审判开始之前，警方已经将嫌疑人引渡了。
It had been decided that he would be extradited. = 已经决定将他引渡。


将来时:
The government will extradite him as soon as possible. = 政府将尽快将他引渡。
They will likely extradite the suspect next month. = 他们可能下个月引渡嫌疑人。


虚拟语气:
If they had the evidence, they would extradite him immediately. = 如果他们有证据，他们会立即将他引渡。
I wish they would extradite the fugitive promptly. = 我希望他们能迅速引渡逃犯。


被动语态:
He was extradited to his home country. = 他被引渡回祖国。
The suspect is being extradited to stand trial. = 嫌疑人正在被引渡以接受审判。


疑问句:
Will he be extradited? = 他会被引渡吗？
Has the criminal been extradited yet? = 罪犯已经被引渡了吗？


否定句:
The government will not extradite him without sufficient evidence. = 没有足够的证据，政府不会将他引渡。
They didn't extradite the suspect because of a lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，他们没有引渡嫌疑人。
