pro-Soviet: /ˌproʊˈsoʊviɛt/ | adj. | 亲苏的，支持苏联的 |  Supporting or favoring the Soviet Union and its policies.  This term describes individuals, groups, or policies that aligned with or advocated for the Soviet Union, often during the Cold War era. |

例句：

The pro-Soviet government implemented strict economic controls.  亲苏政府实施了严格的经济管制。

Many intellectuals held pro-Soviet sympathies during the 1930s.  在20世纪30年代，许多知识分子对苏联抱有同情。


近义词：communist (in certain contexts), Soviet-aligned, pro-communist

反义词：anti-Soviet, pro-Western, pro-American


词性:形容词
