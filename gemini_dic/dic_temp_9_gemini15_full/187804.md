rosaries:/ˈroʊzəriːz/| n.|念珠；玫瑰经|A rosary is a string of beads, often 50 or more, used by some Christians, especially Catholics, to count prayers.  The beads are often used to recite the rosary prayers, a specific set of prayers focusing on the life of Jesus and Mary. |例句：She clutched her rosaries tightly in her hands during the prayer service.  她在祈祷仪式上紧紧地握着她的念珠。

例句：My grandmother always carried her rosaries with her, a symbol of her faith. 我祖母总是随身携带她的念珠，那是她信仰的象征。
