fingal:/ˈfɪŋɡəl/| n. |黑眼圈；瘀伤 |A fingal is a black eye or a bruise, especially one around the eye.  The term is informal and somewhat humorous.  It suggests a blow or injury that resulted in the discoloration.| He got a nasty fingal in the boxing match. = 他在拳击比赛中被打出了一个难看的黑眼圈。|  He woke up with a huge fingal under his eye. = 他醒来时眼睛下面有一个巨大的瘀伤。|近义词：bruise, black eye, shiner |反义词：None specifically, but implies the absence of injury.
