disinheritable: /ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtəbəl/ | adj. | 可被剥夺继承权的 | Capable of being disinherited;  able to be deprived of an inheritance.  This word describes a person or situation where the right to inherit property or title can be legally taken away. |

例句：

The will stated that under certain conditions, the son was disinheritable. = 遗嘱声明，在某些情况下，儿子可以被剥夺继承权。

His reckless behavior made him disinheritable in his father's eyes. = 他鲁莽的行为让他在他父亲眼中失去了继承权。


近义词：  deprivable, forfeitable


反义词： inheritable, unalienable


词性：形容词
