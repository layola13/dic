setdown:/ˈsɛtdaʊn/| vt. n.|放下；记下；使…下车；制定；搁置|To put or place (something) down; to write down; to dismiss or discharge (someone) from a vehicle; to establish or determine (something), such as rules or plans; to postpone or abandon.|例句：Please set down your bags. 请把你的包放下。 He set down his ideas in a letter. 他在一封信中写下了他的想法。The bus set him down at the corner. 公共汽车把他放在拐角处。They set down new rules for the competition. 他们为比赛制定了新的规则。They decided to set down the project for now. 他们决定暂时搁置这个项目。|近义词：put down, write down, establish, determine, postpone, abandon|反义词：pick up, lift, raise, continue, proceed


USAGE->
