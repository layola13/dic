refry:/riːˈfraɪ/| vt.|再次油炸|To fry (something) again.  This typically involves frying food that has already been cooked, often to make it crispier or to reheat it.|例句：I refried the potatoes to make them crispier. 我再次油炸土豆使它们更脆。

例句：She refried the leftover rice to make a fried rice dish. 她把剩下的米饭再次油炸做成炒饭。


近义词：refry, reheat, fry again


反义词：boil, steam


词性：动词
