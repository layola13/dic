fatalists:/ˈfeɪtəlɪsts/ | n. | 宿命论者，宿命主义者 | Believers in fatalism; people who believe that all events are predetermined and inevitable.  They accept whatever happens without resistance or struggle. | Example:  Many fatalists believe that their future is already written, and there's nothing they can do to change it. 许多宿命论者相信他们的未来已经注定，他们无法改变它。


Example: Some fatalists see hardship as an unavoidable part of life and don't actively try to avoid it. 一些宿命论者认为苦难是生活中不可避免的一部分，并且不会积极地试图避免它。


近义词：determinists (决定论者)


反义词：optimists (乐观主义者), free will believers (自由意志论者)


词性: 名词
