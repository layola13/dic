prerejecting:/ˌpriːriˈdʒektɪŋ/ | v. |预先拒绝；提前否决 |  The act of rejecting something before it has been fully considered or presented.  It implies a proactive and often preemptive dismissal. |例句：The committee prerejected the proposal without even reading it. 委员会甚至没有阅读就预先否决了该提案。
The company prerejected his application due to a lack of experience. 公司由于他缺乏经验而预先拒绝了他的申请。|近义词：preemptively rejecting, dismissing beforehand,  ruling out in advance|反义词：accepting, considering, approving |词性：动词
