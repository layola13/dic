proboscides:/ˈprɒbəsaɪdz/ | n. |长鼻；象鼻；喙 |  A proboscis is a long, flexible snout, especially that of an elephant or other animal.  It is often used for feeding, smelling, and grasping. | Example: The elephant used its proboscis to pick up the fruit.  大象用它的长鼻捡起水果。 The butterfly's proboscis reached deep into the flower. 蝴蝶的长喙伸入花朵深处。|近义词：trunk (elephant's), snout, beak, rostrum|反义词：None specifically, but could consider words implying a short nose or lack of a snout.|名词
