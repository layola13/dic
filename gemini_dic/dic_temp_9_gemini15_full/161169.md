posthemorrhagic: /ˌpoʊstˌhɛmərˈædʒɪk/ | adj. | 出血后的；出血引起的 | Relating to the period after a hemorrhage (severe bleeding).  It describes conditions or effects that occur following a hemorrhage. |  The patient experienced posthemorrhagic anemia after the surgery.  病人术后出现了出血性贫血。  |近义词：post-bleed, post-hemorrhage | 反义词：pre-hemorrhagic
