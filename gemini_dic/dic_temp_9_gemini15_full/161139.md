postexistent: /ˌpoʊstɪɡˈzɪstənt/ | adj. | 后续存在的；存在于…之后 | Existing or occurring after something else, especially after a significant event or period.  The term often implies a continuation or legacy of something that has ended. |


例句：

The postexistent effects of the war were devastating. = 战争的后遗症是毁灭性的。

The postexistent culture maintained some traditions from the previous era. = 后续的文化保留了一些前一个时代的传统。


近义词：subsequent, following, succeeding, leftover, residual


反义词：preceding, prior, antecedent, foregoing


词性: adjective
