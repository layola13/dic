discolourment:/dɪsˈkʌləmənt/| n. |变色；褪色；污点 |The alteration or loss of the natural color of something; a stain or blemish.  Discoloration refers to a change in color, often undesirable.  This change can be caused by various factors, including aging, exposure to the elements, or disease. |例句：The sun caused a significant discolourment of the fabric.  阳光导致织物严重褪色。

The old painting showed signs of discolourment due to age and moisture.  这幅旧画由于年代久远和潮湿而出现了褪色的迹象。|近义词：discoloration, staining, fading, discoloration|反义词：brightening, enhancement
