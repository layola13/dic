insurable:/ɪnˈʃʊərəbl/| adj.| 可保险的 | Capable of being insured;  suitable for insurance coverage.  Something is insurable if an insurance company is willing to accept the risk associated with it. |例句：The house is insurable, provided we install a modern alarm system. 这房子是可以投保的，只要我们安装一个现代化的报警系统。

例句：That risk is insurable through a specialized policy. 那风险可以通过专门的保单进行投保。


近义词：coverable, securable


反义词：uninsurable


词性：形容词
