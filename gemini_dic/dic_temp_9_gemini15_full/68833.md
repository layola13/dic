fascist:/ˈfæʃɪst/| adj. n. |法西斯的；法西斯主义者|

英文解析:Relating to or supporting fascism, a political ideology characterized by dictatorial power, forcible suppression of opposition, and strong regimentation of society and the economy.  A fascist is a supporter of fascism.

例句:
He is accused of being a fascist. 他被指控为法西斯分子。

The fascist regime suppressed all dissent. 法西斯政权压制了所有异见。


近义词:authoritarian, totalitarian, dictatorial, autocratic

反义词:democratic, liberal, libertarian


词性:形容词，名词


词源:From Italian *fascista*, from *fascio* ("bundle, group"), referring to the fasces, a symbol of authority in ancient Rome, adopted by Mussolini's movement.  The term evolved to describe the political ideology and its adherents.
