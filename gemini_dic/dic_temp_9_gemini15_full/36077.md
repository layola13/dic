coelacanthid:/ˈsiːləˌkænθɪd/| n. |腔棘鱼科的；腔棘鱼|A coelacanthid is a member of the Coelacanthiformes, an order of lobe-finned fishes.  They are known for their unique evolutionary history and are considered "living fossils."|The discovery of living coelacanths revolutionized our understanding of fish evolution.|近义词: lobe-finned fish|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性:n.
