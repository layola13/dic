USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't have tenses like verbs.  The usage is solely contextual.


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "hallo" in English.  "Hallo" is less common than "hello" in modern English.  The usage is largely regional or stylistic.
