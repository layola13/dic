premiered:/prɪˈmɪərd/| v.|首映，首次公演|To have its first public performance or showing.  The movie premiered last night to rave reviews.|近义词：debuted, launched, unveiled|反义词：concluded, ended


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The film premieres next week. = 这部电影下周首映。
The play premieres in London. = 这部剧在伦敦首映。


一般过去时:
The movie premiered last night. = 电影昨晚首映。
The play premiered to a full house. = 这部戏首映时座无虚席。


现在完成时:
The film has premiered in several countries. = 这部电影已经在几个国家首映了。
The band has premiered their new album. = 乐队已经首发了他们的新专辑。


过去完成时:
The director had premiered three films before this one. = 在这部电影之前，这位导演已经首映过三部电影了。
By the time the play premiered, the actors had rehearsed for months. = 到这部戏首映的时候，演员们已经排练了几个月了。


将来时:
The new musical will premiere next spring. = 新音乐剧将于明年春天首映。
The artist's work will premiere at the gallery next month. = 艺术家的作品将于下个月在画廊首映。


虚拟语气:
If the play had premiered earlier, it might have been a bigger success. = 如果这部戏早些首映，可能会更成功。
I wish the movie had premiered sooner. = 我希望电影早点首映。


被动语态:
The film was premiered at the Cannes Film Festival. = 这部电影在戛纳电影节上首映。
The new song was premiered on the radio this morning. = 这首新歌今天早上在广播里首播。


疑问句:
When did the movie premiere? = 电影什么时候首映的？
Where will the play premiere? = 这部戏将在哪里首映？


否定句:
The movie didn't premiere as expected. = 电影没有按预期首映。
The play hasn't premiered yet. = 这部戏还没首映。
