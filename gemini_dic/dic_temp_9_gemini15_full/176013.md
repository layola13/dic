ravage:/ˈrævɪdʒ/| vt. n.|破坏，毁坏；蹂躏|To damage something severely; to cause great destruction.|The storm ravaged the coastal towns.  暴风雨摧毁了沿海城镇。The war ravaged the countryside.战争蹂躏了农村。|destroy, devastate, ruin|repair, restore, rebuild|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The insects are ravaging the crops. = 昆虫正在毁坏庄稼。
The disease ravages the body. = 疾病正在摧残身体。


一般过去时:
The fire ravaged the forest. = 大火烧毁了森林。
The flood ravaged the village. = 洪水摧毁了村庄。


现在完成时:
The drought has ravaged the land. = 干旱已经摧毁了这片土地。
Years of war have ravaged the country. = 多年的战争已经摧毁了这个国家。


过去完成时:
The army had ravaged the city before retreating. = 军队在撤退之前已经摧毁了这座城市。
The disease had ravaged the population before the cure was found. = 在找到治愈方法之前，这种疾病已经摧残了人口。


将来时:
The hurricane will ravage the coast. = 飓风将摧毁海岸。
The locusts will ravage the fields. = 蝗虫将摧毁田地。


虚拟语气:
If the storm had hit, it would have ravaged the town. = 如果暴风雨袭击，它将摧毁小镇。
I wish the war hadn't ravaged our homeland. = 我真希望战争没有摧毁我们的家园。


被动语态:
The city was ravaged by the earthquake. = 这座城市被地震摧毁了。
The farmland was ravaged by the flood. = 农田被洪水摧毁了。


疑问句:
Has the storm ravaged the area? = 暴风雨是否摧毁了该地区？
What areas were ravaged by the fire? = 哪些地区被大火摧毁了？


否定句:
The army did not ravage the villages. = 军队没有摧毁村庄。
The disease did not ravage the entire population. = 这种疾病没有摧毁整个人口。


PHRASE->
ravage the countryside = 摧毁农村
ravage the economy = 破坏经济
ravage one's health = 损害某人的健康
ravage one's reputation = 毁坏某人的名誉
ravage a person's life = 毁掉某人的生活
