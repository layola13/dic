conservativism: /kənˈsɜːrvətɪvɪzəm/ | n. | 保守主义 |

英文解析:Conservatism is a political and social philosophy that promotes the maintenance of traditional institutions and values.  It emphasizes stability and order, often resisting rapid change and advocating for gradual reform.  Different forms of conservatism exist, with varying degrees of emphasis on tradition, individual liberty, and the role of the state.

例句:

His political views are firmly rooted in conservatism.  他的政治观点深深植根于保守主义。

The government's economic policy reflects a cautious approach to conservatism. 政府的经济政策反映了一种谨慎的保守主义方法。


近义词: traditionalism, traditional values, reactionism (more extreme), status quo

反义词: liberalism, progressivism, radicalism


词性: noun
