refurnish:/ˌriːˈfɜːrnɪʃ/ | vt. |重新装修；重新装备；补充家具|To furnish again; to supply with new furniture or equipment.  |例句：We decided to refurnish the living room with modern furniture. 我们决定用现代家具重新装修客厅。|例句：The company will refurnish the office with new computers. 公司将用新电脑重新装备办公室。|近义词：renovate, redecorate, refit |反义词：strip, deplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They refurnish their house every five years. = 他们每五年重新装修一次房子。
He refurnishes his office with new equipment. = 他用新设备重新装备他的办公室。

一般过去时:
She refurnished her apartment last summer. = 她去年夏天重新装修了她的公寓。
They refurnished the hotel with antique furniture. = 他们用古董家具重新装修了酒店。

现在完成时:
We have refurnished the kitchen twice this year. = 今年我们已经两次重新装修了厨房。
They have refurnished the library with new books. = 他们已经用新书重新装备了图书馆。

过去完成时:
They had refurnished the house before they moved in. = 他们在搬进去之前已经重新装修了房子。
She had refurnished the bedroom before her daughter's arrival. = 在她女儿到来之前，她已经重新装修了卧室。

将来时:
We will refurnish the bathroom next month. = 我们下个月将重新装修浴室。
He will refurnish his shop with new displays. = 他将用新的陈列品重新装修他的商店。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would refurnish the entire house. = 如果我们有更多的钱，我们会重新装修整个房子。
I wish we could refurnish the living room this year. = 我希望我们今年能重新装修客厅。

被动语态:
The office will be refurnished next week. = 办公室下周将被重新装修。
The hotel rooms are being refurnished at the moment. = 酒店房间目前正在被重新装修。

疑问句:
Will you refurnish the bedroom soon? = 你会很快重新装修卧室吗？
Are they going to refurnish the whole house? = 他们打算重新装修整个房子吗？

否定句:
We didn't refurnish the apartment last year. = 我们去年没有重新装修公寓。
They won't refurnish the office this year. = 他们今年不会重新装修办公室。
