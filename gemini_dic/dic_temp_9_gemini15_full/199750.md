sirenian:/saɪˈrɪniən/| n. |海牛 |A sirenian is any of a group of large, aquatic, plant-eating mammals.  They are characterized by their streamlined bodies, paddle-like flippers, and horizontal fluke-like tail.  Sirenians are closely related to elephants. |Example:  The manatee is a type of sirenian.  这条海牛是一种海牛。  The dugong is another well-known sirenian. 儒艮是另一种著名的海牛。|近义词:sea cow (informal)|反义词:None.
