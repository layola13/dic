orah:/ˈɔːrə/| n. |神谕；预言；祈祷|An orah is a divine utterance or revelation, often delivered through a prophet or oracle.  It can also refer to a fervent prayer or supplication.  The term often carries a sense of authority and profound meaning.|例句：The priest sought an orah from the gods to guide the king's decision.  祭司向神寻求神谕来指导国王的决定。|近义词：prophecy, prediction, revelation, prayer |反义词：silence, denial
