comprehensives:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪvz/|adj.|全面的，综合的|thorough, including many or all things; covering completely|The course offers a comprehensive overview of the subject. 这门课程对该科目进行了全面的概述。 |近义词：complete, thorough, exhaustive|反义词：partial, incomplete, superficial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This program offers a comprehensive solution. = 这个程序提供了一个全面的解决方案。
The report gives a comprehensive analysis of the market. = 这份报告对市场进行了全面的分析。

一般过去时:
The doctor conducted a comprehensive examination. = 医生进行了全面的检查。
She gave a comprehensive presentation on the topic. = 她就这个话题做了一个全面的演讲。

现在完成时:
We have completed a comprehensive review of the project. = 我们已经完成了对项目的全面审查。
They have implemented a comprehensive safety plan. = 他们已经实施了一个全面的安全计划。

过去完成时:

The team had prepared a comprehensive proposal for the new project. = 该团队已经为新项目准备了一个全面的提案。

将来时:
She will conduct a comprehensive assessment of the damage. = 她将对损失进行全面评估。
They will present a comprehensive report at the conference. = 他们将在会议上提交一份全面的报告。

虚拟语气:
If we had a comprehensive understanding, we would solve the problem easily. = 如果我们有全面的理解，我们将很容易解决这个问题。

被动语态:
