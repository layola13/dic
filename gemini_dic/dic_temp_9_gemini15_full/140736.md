omnipresence:/ˈɒmnɪˌprɛzəns/| /ˌɑːmnɪˈprɛzəns/ | n. |无所不在；遍在 |The state or quality of being everywhere at once.  It often refers to a deity or a powerful force. |例句：The omnipresence of God was a source of comfort to her.  上帝的无所不在带给她安慰。

例句：The omnipresence of technology in modern life is undeniable. 现代生活中科技的无所不在是不可否认的。


近义词：ubiquity, pervasiveness, universality |反义词：absence, scarcity, limitation |词性：名词
