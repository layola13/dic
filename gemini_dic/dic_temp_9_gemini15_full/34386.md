classifies:/ˈklæsɪfaɪz/| vt.|分类，归类|To arrange or organize into categories or classes according to shared characteristics.|The librarian classifies books by subject and author. 图书管理员按主题和作者对书籍进行分类。|categorizes, sorts, groups|unclassifies, mixes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He classifies the data into three groups. 他将数据分为三组。
The scientist classifies the species based on their DNA. 科学家根据它们的DNA对物种进行分类。


一般过去时:
She classified the documents according to their importance. 她根据重要性对文件进行了分类。
The teacher classified the students into different ability levels. 老师把学生分成了不同能力等级的组。


现在完成时:
They have classified all the fossils found so far. 他们已经对迄今为止发现的所有化石进行了分类。

过去完成时:
She had classified the evidence before presenting her findings. 在提交调查结果之前，她已经对证据进行了分类。
The team had classified the data by the time the deadline arrived.  截止日期到来时，团队已经对数据进行了分类。


将来时:
