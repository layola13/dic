haik:/haɪk/| n.|俳句|A short three-line poem, traditionally Japanese, having five syllables in the first line, seven in the second, and five in the third.  |例句：He wrote a haik about the cherry blossoms. 他写了一首关于樱花的俳句。 |近义词：tanka, poem |反义词：None.
