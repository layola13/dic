ranch:/ræntʃ/| n. |牧场；大农场|A large farm, especially in the western United States, where livestock are raised.  Ranches often consist of a large area of land, including grazing areas, barns, and other facilities needed to support the livestock. |例句：He inherited a cattle ranch in Montana. 他继承了蒙大拿州的一个养牛场。|例句：They spent their vacation working on a guest ranch. 他们度假期间在一个招待所牧场工作。|近义词：farm, estate, hacienda |反义词：city, urban area


USAGE->

The ranch raises cattle and sheep. = 这个牧场饲养牛羊。
He works on a large ranch. = 他在一个大型牧场工作。

一般过去时:

My grandfather owned a ranch. = 我祖父拥有一个牧场。
