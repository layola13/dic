newsdealers:/ˈnuːzˌdiːlərz/| n. |报刊零售商|A newsdealer is a person or business that sells newspapers and magazines, often from a small shop or stand.|Many newsdealers also sell other items such as candy and cigarettes.|近义词：newsagent, newspaper vendor|反义词：无
