divisible:/dɪˈvɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可分的，可除尽的 | Capable of being divided, especially without leaving a remainder.  | Example: 12 is divisible by 2, 3, 4, and 6. 12可以被2、3、4和6整除。  |近义词：dividable | 反义词：indivisible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The number 10 is divisible by 2. = 数字10可以被2整除。
This cake is divisible into eight slices. = 这个蛋糕可以分成八块。


一般过去时:
The problem was divisible into smaller parts. = 这个问题可以分解成更小的部分。
The land was divisible among the heirs. = 这块土地可以分配给继承人。


现在完成时:
The task has been divisible into manageable sections. = 任务已经被分解成可管理的部分。
The inheritance has been divisible among the family members. = 遗产已经被分配给家庭成员。


过去完成时:
The estate had been divisible before the dispute. = 在争议发生之前，遗产是可以分割的。
The work had been divisible into phases. = 工作已经被分成了几个阶段。


将来时:
The property will be divisible after the legal proceedings. = 在法律程序之后，房产将可以分割。
The project will be divisible into smaller, more manageable tasks. = 项目将被分解成更小、更容易管理的任务。


虚拟语气:
If the number were divisible by three, the answer would be different. = 如果这个数字能被3整除，答案就会不同。
If the land were divisible equally, there would be no conflict. = 如果土地可以平均分配，就不会有冲突了。


被动语态:
The profits are divisible among the partners. = 利润可以分配给合伙人。
The workload is divisible among the team members. = 工作量可以分配给团队成员。


疑问句:
Is this number divisible by five? = 这个数字能被5整除吗？
Is the pizza divisible into equal slices? = 比萨饼能分成大小相等的块吗？


否定句:
The task is not divisible into smaller subtasks. = 这项任务不能分解成更小的子任务。
That amount is not divisible by three. = 那笔钱不能被三整除。


PHRASE->
evenly divisible  均匀可分的
perfectly divisible  完全可分的
divisible by  可被...整除
mutually divisible  互相可分的
highly divisible  高度可分的
