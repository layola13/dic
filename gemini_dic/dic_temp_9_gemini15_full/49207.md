dele:/ˈdiːliː/| v.| 删除，划掉 |to remove or erase something, typically from a text or record.|例句：Please dele this paragraph from the report. 请从报告中删除这一段。|例句：The editor decided to dele several sentences to improve the flow. 编辑决定删除几句话以改善文章的流畅性。|近义词：remove, erase, delete, expunge|反义词：add, insert, include|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deletes his emails regularly. = 他定期删除电子邮件。
They delete unnecessary files. = 他们删除不必要的文件。

一般过去时:
She deleted the wrong file yesterday. = 她昨天删除了错误的文件。
I deleted the message by mistake. = 我误删了这条信息。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
