coalesce:/koʊəˈles/ | /kəʊəˈles/ | v. | 联合；合并；融合 | To come together and form one mass or whole.  | The small droplets coalesced to form a larger drop. 小水滴合并成了一大滴。The different groups coalesced into a single political party.不同的群体合并成一个政党。 | merge, combine, fuse, unite | separate, divide, disperse | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The clouds coalesce into a storm.  云汇聚成暴风雨。
The two companies coalesce their resources. 两家公司合并资源。

一般过去时:
The two rivers coalesced into one. 两条河汇合为一条。
The different factions coalesced into a single movement.  不同的派系合并成一个运动。

现在完成时:
The various ideas have coalesced into a comprehensive plan.  各种想法已经融合成一个全面的计划。
The small communities have coalesced into a larger city.  各个小社区已经合并成一个更大的城市。

过去完成时:
The individual components had coalesced to form a complex system. 各个组件已经融合形成一个复杂的系统。
The separate tribes had coalesced into a nation. 各个部落已经合并成一个国家。

将来时:
The different elements will coalesce into a cohesive whole. 不同的元素将融合成一个有凝聚力的整体。
The various opinions will coalesce into a consensus.  各种意见将融合成一个共识。

虚拟语气:
If the two groups coalesced, they would be stronger. 如果这两个群体合并，他们将更强大。
I wish the different ideas would coalesce into a single solution. 我希望不同的想法能融合成一个解决方案。

被动语态:
The ingredients were coalesced to form a smooth paste.  配料被混合成光滑的糊状物。
The various viewpoints were coalesced into a single report.  各种观点被融合到一份报告中。

疑问句:
Will the different factions coalesce? 不同的派系会合并吗？

否定句:
