peals:/piːlz/| n. |响亮的铃声；一阵阵（笑声、雷声等）|a loud ringing sound, as of bells; a series of sounds (as of laughter or thunder)|The church bells pealed merrily. 教堂的钟声欢快地鸣响。The audience pealed with laughter. 观众爆发出阵阵笑声。|近义词：rings, sounds, bursts|反义词：silence, hush


USAGE->

The bells peal every hour. = 钟声每小时鸣响。
The children peal with laughter. = 孩子们哈哈大笑。
