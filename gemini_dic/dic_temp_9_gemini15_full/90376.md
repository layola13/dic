howbeit:/ˈhaʊˈbiːɪt/| adv.|虽然，然而|Although; nevertheless; notwithstanding.  It is a somewhat archaic word, meaning "although it is true that" or "in spite of that". It is used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or qualifies a previous statement. |例句：The weather was terrible; howbeit, we still went for a walk. 天气很糟糕；然而，我们还是去散步了。|例句：He is a kind man; howbeit, he can be quite stubborn. 他是个善良的人；然而，他可能相当固执。|近义词：although, nevertheless, notwithstanding, however, yet |反义词：therefore, consequently, hence
