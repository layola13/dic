in-line-with: /ˌɪnˈlaɪn wɪð/ | prep. | 与…一致；符合…；按照… |  This preposition indicates agreement, conformity, or accordance with something.  It suggests a parallel or alignment. |

例句：

His actions were in line with company policy. = 他的行为符合公司政策。

The new design is in line with current trends. = 新设计符合当前趋势。


近义词：consistent with, in accordance with, conforming to, in agreement with

反义词：contrary to, at odds with, inconsistent with

词性：介词
