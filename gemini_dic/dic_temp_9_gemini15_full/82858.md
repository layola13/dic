guller:/ˈɡʌlər/| n.|骗子，欺诈者|A guller is a person who deceives or tricks others, often for personal gain.  They are skilled at manipulation and often use flattery or charm to achieve their goals. |例句：He was a guller, always looking for ways to take advantage of others. 他是个骗子，总是寻找各种方法利用他人。

例句：The guller skillfully convinced the old woman to give him her life savings.  那个骗子巧妙地骗取了老太太的毕生积蓄。


近义词：swindler, deceiver, cheat, fraud, con artist |反义词：honest person, truthful person|名词
