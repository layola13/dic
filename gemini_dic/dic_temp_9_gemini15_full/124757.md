USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, grammatical tenses and voice don't apply to "mRNA".  We can, however, use it in different sentence structures to express various ideas.

Example Sentences:

Declarative Sentence:  The researchers analyzed the mRNA extracted from the cells. = 研究人员分析了从细胞中提取的信使RNA。

Interrogative Sentence: Did the treatment affect the levels of mRNA in the liver? =  这种治疗是否影响了肝脏中信使RNA的水平？

Exclamatory Sentence:  The mRNA sequencing results were astounding! = 信使RNA测序结果令人震惊！
