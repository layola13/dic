overflows:/ˈoʊvərfloʊz/ | v. | 溢出；泛滥；充满；洋溢 | To flow over the brim or edge of something; to be so full that contents spill over.  It can also refer to an abundance or overwhelming amount of something. |例句：The river overflows its banks during the rainy season. 雨季河水泛滥成灾。
The box overflows with toys. 箱子里装满了玩具。

近义词：flood, inundate, brim, swell

反义词：underflow, subside


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river overflows its banks every spring. = 河水每年春天都会泛滥。
The mailbox overflows with junk mail. = 邮箱里塞满了垃圾邮件。

一般过去时:
The bathtub overflowed because I forgot to turn off the tap. = 因为我忘了关水龙头，浴缸溢出来了。
The stadium overflowed with excited fans. = 体育场挤满了兴奋的球迷。

现在完成时:
The river has overflowed three times this year. = 河水今年已经泛滥三次了。
My inbox has overflowed with emails since the conference. = 自从会议以来，我的收件箱里塞满了邮件。

过去完成时:
The basement had overflowed before the emergency services arrived. = 紧急服务人员到达之前，地下室就已经被水淹了。
The cup had overflowed, staining the tablecloth. = 杯子已经溢出来了，把桌布弄脏了。

将来时:
The lake will overflow if it continues to rain. = 如果继续下雨，湖水就会溢出来。
If the dam breaks, the reservoir will overflow. = 如果大坝决堤，水库就会溢出。

虚拟语气:
If the dam hadn't been reinforced, it would have overflowed. = 如果大坝没有加固，它就会决堤。
I wish my inbox hadn't overflowed with spam. = 我真希望我的收件箱里没有那么多垃圾邮件。

被动语态:
The streets were overflowed by the rising tide. = 街道被涨潮淹没了。
The server was overflowed by the unexpected traffic. = 服务器被意外的流量挤爆了。

疑问句:
Does the bathtub overflow easily? = 浴缸容易溢水吗？
Does the river overflow regularly? = 河水经常泛滥吗？

否定句:
The cup didn't overflow. = 杯子没有溢出来。
The system did not overflow, even under heavy load. = 即使在重载下，系统也没有溢出。


PHRASE->
overflow with = 充满……
overflowing with = 充满……(更强调)
overflow protection = 防溢保护
overflow error = 溢出错误
data overflow = 数据溢出


词源:
The word "overflow" is derived from the Old English words "ofer" (over) and "flowan" (to flow).  It combines the concepts of "over" and "flow," literally meaning to flow over a boundary or limit.
