nonhepatics: /ˌnɒnˈhɛpætɪks/ | adj. | 非肝脏的 | Not relating to the liver.  This term is used in medical and biological contexts to describe tissues, organs, or processes that are not associated with the liver. | Example: Nonhepatic causes of jaundice include certain blood disorders and bile duct obstructions.  非肝性黄疸的原因包括某些血液疾病和胆管阻塞。|近义词: extrahepatic | 反义词: hepatic
