justifies:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪz/| vt.|证明…是正当的；为…辩护；|To show or prove to be right or reasonable.|The end justifies the means.  目的可以证明手段的正当性。|vindicate, defend, support|condemn, refute, discredit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He justifies his actions. = 他为他的行为辩护。
The evidence justifies a thorough investigation. = 证据证明需要彻底调查。


一般过去时:
She justified her decision to quit her job. = 她为辞职的决定辩护。
The court justified the verdict. = 法院为判决辩护。


现在完成时:
His explanation has justified his absence. = 他的解释证明了他的缺席是合理的。
The success of the project has justified the investment. = 项目的成功证明了投资是合理的。


过去完成时:
They had justified their actions to the police. = 他们向警方解释了他们的行为。
The historical context had justified the controversial decision. = 历史背景证明了这一有争议的决定是合理的。


将来时:
We will justify our claims with evidence. = 我们将用证据证明我们的说法。
The results will justify the cost of the research. = 结果将证明研究的成本是合理的。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I could justify my position more fully. = 如果我有更多时间，我可以更充分地证明我的立场。
I wish I could justify my spending habits. = 我希望我能为我的消费习惯辩护。


被动语态:
His actions were justified by the circumstances. = 他的行为由于当时的状况而被认为是正当的。
The decision was justified by the need for security. =  出于安全需要，该决定被认为是合理的。


疑问句:
Does this action justify the means? = 这个行为能证明手段的正当性吗？
How can you justify such behavior? = 你怎么能为这种行为辩护？


否定句:
His excuse doesn't justify his lateness. = 他的借口并不能为他迟到辩护。
The outcome didn't justify the risk. = 结果并没有证明冒险是合理的。


PHRASE->
justify oneself = 为自己辩护
justify the expense = 为费用辩护
justify a claim = 证明某种说法是正确的
justify the means = 为手段辩护
fully justify = 完全证明


词源:
From Old French *iustifier, from Latin iustificare, from iustus ("just") + facere ("to make").  The word ultimately derives from the concept of making something just or righteous.
