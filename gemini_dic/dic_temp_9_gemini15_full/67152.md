exposes:/ɪkˈspoʊzɪz/ | vt. |揭露；使暴露；使遭受 | to reveal something bad or dishonest that was previously hidden.  |例句：The investigation exposed widespread corruption within the company.  调查揭露了公司内部普遍存在的腐败现象。 |例句：The reporter exposed the politician's lies. 记者揭露了这位政治家的谎言。|近义词：reveal, uncover, unveil |反义词：conceal, hide, mask |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The journalist exposes corruption in the government. = 记者揭露政府腐败。
Sunlight exposes the flaws in the material. = 阳光暴露出材料的缺陷。


一般过去时:
The experiment exposed the dangers of the chemical. = 实验揭示了这种化学品的危险性。
He exposed his plan to his friends. = 他向朋友们透露了他的计划。


现在完成时:
The media has exposed several scandals this year. = 媒体今年已经曝光了几起丑闻。
She has exposed herself to the risk of infection. = 她让自己面临感染的风险。


过去完成时:
The investigation had exposed the fraud before the trial. = 调查在审判前已经揭露了欺诈行为。
They had exposed the truth to the public. = 他们已经向公众公开了真相。


将来时:
The report will expose the company's unethical practices. = 这份报告将揭露该公司的违反道德的行为。
We will expose the painting to the sunlight to dry. = 我们要把画暴露在阳光下晾干。


虚拟语气:
If he exposed the truth, he would be in danger. = 如果他揭露真相，他会很危险。
I wish I had exposed the problem sooner. = 我真希望我早点发现这个问题。


被动语态:
The scandal was exposed by the media. = 这起丑闻被媒体曝光了。
The truth will be exposed eventually. = 真相最终会被揭露。


疑问句:
What will the investigation expose? = 调查会揭露什么？
Does this design expose the internal components? = 这种设计是否会暴露出内部组件？


否定句:
The company didn't expose its financial problems. = 公司没有公开其财务问题。
They did not expose the secret. = 他们没有泄露秘密。


PHRASE->
expose to:  使接触；使暴露于…
expose oneself to: 使自己暴露于…
expose the truth: 揭露真相
expose a conspiracy: 揭露阴谋
expose a fraud: 揭露诈骗


词源:
From Old French *exposer, from Latin exponere ("to set forth, explain"), from ex- ("out") + ponere ("to place").
