pocket: /ˈpɒkɪt/ /ˈpɑːkɪt/ | n. | 口袋；少量；隐秘处 | A small bag or pouch sewn into a garment or other object for carrying small items.  |例句：He put his hands in his pockets. 他把手插进口袋里。

例句：She has a pocket full of coins. 她的口袋里装满了硬币。


近义词：pouch, compartment | 反义词： N/A | 词性：名词


词源：源自古法语 *poce, poche,  最终来自拉丁语 *pōcca "bag, pouch"


用法说明:

一般现在时:
He keeps his keys in his pocket. = 他把钥匙放在口袋里。
She always carries a small pocket knife. = 她总是随身携带一把小刀。

一般过去时:
I found a coin in my pocket. = 我在口袋里发现了一枚硬币。
He reached into his pocket for his phone. = 他伸手进口袋拿手机。

现在完成时:
I've lost my wallet from my pocket. = 我的钱包从口袋里丢了。
She's had that lucky coin in her pocket all day. = 她一整天都把那枚幸运硬币放在口袋里。

过去完成时:
He had already emptied his pockets before the security check. = 安检前他已经把口袋掏空了。
She realized she had left her keys in her other pocket. = 她意识到她把钥匙留在另一个口袋里了。

将来时:
I will put my phone in my pocket. = 我要把手机放进口袋。
He will need to check his pockets for his ticket. = 他需要检查一下口袋是否有票。

虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger pocket, I would carry more things. = 如果我的口袋更大，我会带更多东西。
I wish I had a secret pocket in my jacket. = 我真希望我的夹克有个隐蔽口袋。

被动语态:
His pockets were searched by the police. = 他的口袋被警察搜查了。
The money was found in his pocket. = 钱在他的口袋里被发现了。

疑问句:
Do you have anything in your pockets? = 你的口袋里有什么东西吗？
What's in your pocket? = 你的口袋里有什么？

否定句:
He didn't have any money in his pockets. = 他的口袋里没有钱。
There was nothing in her pockets. = 她的口袋里什么也没有。
