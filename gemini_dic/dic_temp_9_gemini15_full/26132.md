bustiest:/ˈbɪzɪəst/ | adj. | 最繁忙的；最喧闹的 |  The superlative form of "busy," describing the highest degree of activity, noise, or fullness.  It implies a state of being extremely occupied or crowded. |例句：The bustiest time of day at the airport is always between 3 pm and 5 pm.  机场一天中最繁忙的时间总是下午3点到5点之间。
The city streets were bustiest during the holiday season.  节日期间，城市街道最热闹。|近义词：most active, most crowded, most hectic, most bustling |反义词：most quiet, most calm, most empty, most still |
