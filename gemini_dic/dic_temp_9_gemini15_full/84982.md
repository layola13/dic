This word is a misspelling of "happy".  The grammatical usage would be identical to "happy".  Therefore, examples using "happy" will be provided.

一般现在时:
I am happy today. = 我今天很快乐。
She is happy with her new job. = 她对她的新工作很满意。

一般过去时:
I was happy to see you. = 很高兴见到你。
We were happy with the results. = 我们对结果很满意。

现在完成时:
I have been happy since I met you. = 自从遇见你以来我一直很开心。
They have been happy together for many years. = 他们在一起快乐了很多年。

过去完成时:
I had been happy until I received the bad news. = 直到我收到坏消息之前我一直都很开心。
