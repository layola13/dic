platea:/ˈplætɪə/| n.|广场，平台，露天剧场|A platea is a level area, especially a raised, flat area of ground.  It can also refer to a stage in a theatre.  The term is often used in architectural and theatrical contexts.|例句：The actors performed on a raised platea in the center of the stage.  演员们在舞台中央的凸起平台上表演。|近义词：stage, platform, terrace, esplanade |反义词：pit, depression
