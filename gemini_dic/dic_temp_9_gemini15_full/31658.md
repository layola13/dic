chatterers:/ˈtʃætərərz/| n.|喋喋不休的人，唠叨的人 |People who talk continuously and rapidly, often about unimportant things.  They are characterized by their incessant and often frivolous conversation.|例句：The chatterers in the coffee shop were so loud I couldn't concentrate on my work. 咖啡馆里那些喋喋不休的人太吵了，我无法集中精力工作。
The group of chatterers filled the room with their animated discussion.  一群喋喋不休的人用他们热烈的讨论充满了房间。|近义词：gossips, babblers, chatterboxes|反义词：listeners, silent ones|名词
