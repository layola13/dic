mythology:/mɪˈθɒlədʒi/ | n. |神话学，神话体系 |The study of myths, especially in relation to their origins, development, and significance.  Mythology encompasses the myths of a particular culture or religion, often forming a coherent system of beliefs and narratives. |Example: Greek mythology is rich with stories of gods, heroes, and monsters. 希腊神话充满了关于神、英雄和怪兽的故事。|近义词：myths, legends, folklore |反义词：reality, fact
