procrastinate:/prəˈkræstɪneɪt/ | v. | 拖延；延误 | To delay or postpone something.  | 例句：I keep procrastinating on my essay. = 我一直拖延写我的论文。  | 例句：He procrastinated until the last minute to finish his project. = 他拖到最后一刻才完成他的项目。 | 近义词：delay, postpone, defer | 反义词：expedite, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He procrastinates on his homework every day. = 他每天都拖延做作业。
They procrastinate when faced with difficult tasks. = 他们遇到困难的任务时就会拖延。

一般过去时:
I procrastinated on that assignment and got a bad grade. = 我拖延了那项作业，结果得了低分。
She procrastinated all week and finally finished on Friday. = 她拖延了一周，最后在周五才完成。

现在完成时:
He has procrastinated so much that he's now behind schedule. = 他拖延了太多，现在已经落后于进度了。
She has procrastinated on her taxes for months. = 她已经拖欠税款好几个月了。

过去完成时:
I had procrastinated for so long that I had to rush to finish. = 我拖延了这么久，不得不赶快完成。
They had procrastinated all semester and failed the final exam. = 他们整个学期都在拖延，最后期末考试不及格。

将来时:
I will procrastinate no longer! I will start working now. = 我不会再拖延了！我现在就开始工作。
He will procrastinate until the deadline, as usual. = 他照例会拖到最后期限才开始。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't procrastinated, I would have finished the report on time. = 如果我没有拖延，我就能按时完成报告。
I wish I hadn't procrastinated on my studies. = 我真希望我没有拖延我的学习。

被动语态:
The project was procrastinated on for weeks. = 这个项目被拖延了好几个星期。
The decision was procrastinated until the next meeting. = 决定被推迟到下次会议。

疑问句:
Are you procrastinating again? = 你又在拖延了吗？
Why do you always procrastinate? = 你为什么总是拖延？

否定句:
I will not procrastinate any longer. = 我不会再拖延了。
She doesn't usually procrastinate. = 她通常不会拖延。


PHRASE->
procrastinate on = 拖延...
procrastinate about = 犹豫要不要做...
procrastinate with = 以...为借口拖延
put off procrastinating = 不要再拖延了
avoid procrastinating = 避免拖延
