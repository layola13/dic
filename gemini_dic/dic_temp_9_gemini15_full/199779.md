sis:/sɪs/| n. |姐妹，女儿|A female sibling.  It is often used as a shortened form of "sister".|例句：My sis is coming to visit next week.  我的妹妹下周要来拜访。
例句：I called my sis to tell her the good news. 我打电话给我妹妹告诉她好消息。|近义词：sister, sibling |反义词：brother, sibling (male)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
My sis helps me with my homework. = 我的妹妹帮我做作业。
She is my sis and my best friend. = 她是我妹妹也是我最好的朋友。


一般过去时:
My sis called me yesterday. = 我妹妹昨天打电话给我。
She was my sis's best friend. = 她是我妹妹最好的朋友。


现在完成时:
My sis has graduated from college. = 我妹妹大学毕业了。
I have known my sis since we were kids. = 从小我就认识我妹妹了。


过去完成时:
My sis had already left when I arrived. = 我到的时候，我妹妹已经离开了。

将来时:

If my sis were here, she would help me. = 如果我妹妹在这里，她会帮助我。
I wish my sis were here. = 我希望我妹妹在这里。


被动语态:
My sis was invited to the party. = 我妹妹被邀请参加派对。
Her achievements were recognized by everyone.  = 她的成就受到了所有人的认可。


疑问句:

否定句:
