cinched:/sɪntʃt/| v. adj.|系紧；束紧；巩固；（非正式）轻松获胜|To fasten or tighten something, especially with a cord or belt; to make something secure or firm; (informal) to win easily.|例句：She cinched her belt tighter. 她把腰带系紧了。  The deal was cinched after weeks of negotiations.经过几周的谈判，这笔交易终于敲定了。|近义词：fastened, tightened, secured, clinched|反义词：loosened, unfastened
