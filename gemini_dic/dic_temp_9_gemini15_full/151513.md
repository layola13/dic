parry:/ˈpæri/ | vt., n. | 挡开，躲避；招架 | To deflect or ward off (a blow, weapon, or attack).  To skillfully evade or avoid (a difficult question or situation). | 例句：He parried the blow with his sword. 他用剑挡开了那一击。  She skillfully parried the journalist's difficult questions. 她巧妙地避开了记者的刁难问题。 | 近义词：deflect, ward off, evade, avoid | 反义词：accept, confront


USAGE->

He parries every attack with ease. = 他轻松地挡开每一次攻击。
She parries questions about her private life. = 她避开了关于她私生活的问题。


一般过去时:
