sempster:/ˈsɛmpstər/| n. |缝纫工，裁缝|A sempster is a person, typically a woman, who sews or does needlework, especially professionally.  The term is now largely archaic, replaced by terms like seamstress or tailor. |She was a skilled sempster, known throughout the village for her exquisite embroidery.|近义词：seamstress, tailor|反义词：None specifically.
