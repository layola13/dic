protodeacon: /ˈproʊtoʊdiːkən/ | n. |首席执事；副主教 | A protodeacon is a high-ranking deacon, often second in rank only to the archdeacon or other chief deacon in a cathedral or other large church.  They typically assist the bishop or other chief cleric in liturgical services and hold significant administrative responsibilities. | Example: The protodeacon processed ahead of the bishop, carrying the processional cross. 首席执事走在主教前面，拿着游行十字架。 | 近义词: archdeacon, senior deacon | 反义词: junior deacon
