nonforgiving:/ˌnɒnfərˈɡɪvɪŋ/ | adj. | 不宽容的；不原谅的 | Unwilling to forgive; unforgiving.  It implies a lack of compassion and a tendency to hold onto resentment.  | 例句：He was a nonforgiving boss, quick to punish mistakes. 他是一个不宽容的老板，很快就惩罚错误。

The judge's sentence was nonforgiving, reflecting the severity of the crime.  法官的判决毫不留情，反映了罪行的严重性。

近义词：unforgiving, relentless, merciless, implacable

反义词：forgiving, merciful, compassionate, lenient

词性：形容词
