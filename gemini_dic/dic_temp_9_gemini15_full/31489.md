charmers:/ˈtʃɑːrmərz/ | n. |有魅力的人，迷人的人 |People who are charming and attractive. They possess qualities that are captivating and appealing to others.  |例句：The street performers were real charmers, captivating the audience with their skillful juggling and witty banter. 街头表演者们真是迷人，他们娴熟的杂耍和诙谐的玩笑吸引了观众。

例句：The puppies were such charmers, with their big, innocent eyes and playful antics.  这些小狗真可爱，它们有着又大又天真的眼睛和顽皮的滑稽动作。

近义词：allurers, fascinators, enchantresses, heartthrobs | 反义词：repulsives, bores, dullards | 词性:名词
