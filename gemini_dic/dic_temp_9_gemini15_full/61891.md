emptiness:/ˈɛmptɪnɪs/ | n. |空虚；空旷；虚无 |The state of being empty; the absence of something or someone.  It can refer to a physical space, a container, or a more abstract feeling of lack or void. |例句：The emptiness of the desert stretched before them.  沙漠的空旷景象展现在他们面前。

例句：The emptiness she felt after his death was profound. 他死后她感受到的空虚是深沉的。


近义词：void, vacuum, hollowness, vacancy, nothingness

反义词：fullness, abundance, repletion, plenty
