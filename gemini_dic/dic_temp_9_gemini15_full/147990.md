override:/ˈoʊvərˈaɪd/ | vt. |覆盖；推翻；优先于；不顾；凌驾于 |To overrule or supersede; to take precedence over; to ignore or disregard. |例句：The new law overrides the old one. 新法律取代了旧法律。

例句：The manager's decision overrides all other objections. 经理的决定压倒了所有其他的反对意见。


近义词：supersede, annul, preempt, negate |反义词：uphold, confirm, support |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system automatically overrides the default settings. = 系统自动覆盖默认设置。
He overrides his colleagues' opinions. = 他不顾同事们的意见。


一般过去时:
The judge overrode the jury's verdict. = 法官推翻了陪审团的裁决。
The emergency brake system overrode the accelerator. = 紧急刹车系统压过了油门。


现在完成时:
Recent events have overridden our original plans. = 最近发生的事件已经推翻了我们最初的计划。
The new regulations have overridden previous guidelines. = 新规章已经取代了以前的指导方针。


过去完成时:
The CEO had overridden the committee's recommendations before the meeting. = 首席执行官在会议前就否决了委员会的建议。
They had overridden the safety protocols, leading to the accident. = 他们无视安全规程，导致了事故。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the president overrides the bill, it will become law. = 如果总统否决该法案，它将成为法律。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
