membranology:/ˌmɛmˈbreɪnɒlədʒi/ | n. |膜学，膜生物学 |The study of membranes, their structure, function, and properties.  This field encompasses various aspects, including the chemical composition, physical properties, and biological roles of membranes in different organisms.  |Example:  Advances in membranology have led to new drug delivery systems. 膜学方面的进步带来了新的药物输送系统。|近义词：membrane biology, membrane science |反义词：无 |名词
