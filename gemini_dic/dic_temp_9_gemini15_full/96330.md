indignations:/ˌɪndɪɡˈneɪʃənz/| n. |愤慨；义愤；不满| a feeling of anger and annoyance caused by something unjust, unfair, or wrong.| Her indignation at the injustice was palpable.  她的愤怒和不满是显而易见的。|The accusations aroused considerable indignation among his supporters.  这些指控激起了他支持者们极大的愤怒。|近义词：anger, resentment, outrage, fury|反义词：acceptance, complacency, indifference|名词
