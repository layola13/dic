noncurative:/ˌnɒnˈkjʊərətɪv/| adj. |非治愈性的，不能治愈的 |Not able to cure a disease or condition.  It refers to treatments or therapies that alleviate symptoms or slow disease progression but do not eliminate the underlying cause. |Example:  The medication provided only noncurative palliative care, easing her pain but not curing the cancer.  = 这种药物只提供非治愈性的姑息治疗，减轻她的痛苦，但不能治愈癌症。|近义词： palliative, alleviative, symptomatic |反义词：curative, remedial
