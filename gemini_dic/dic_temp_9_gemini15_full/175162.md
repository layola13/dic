rager:/ˈreɪdʒər/| n.|狂怒的人；狂暴者；喧闹的人|A person who is angry and behaving in an uncontrolled way.  Someone who is wildly enthusiastic or excited.|He was a rager after losing the game. 他输掉比赛后怒不可遏。  The concert was a rager; everyone was jumping and singing.  这场音乐会太棒了；每个人都在又蹦又跳，又唱又叫。|近义词：brawler, rioter, fanatic |反义词：calm person, pacifist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child rages when he doesn't get his way. = 这个孩子不顺心就发脾气。
He rages against the injustice of the system. = 他对体制的不公正感到愤怒。


一般过去时:
She raged at her brother for breaking her toy. = 她因为弟弟弄坏了她的玩具而对他大发雷霆。
The storm raged throughout the night. = 暴风雨整夜肆虐。


现在完成时:
He has raged against the machine for years. = 他多年来一直反抗体制。
The fire has raged for three days. = 大火已经肆虐了三天。


过去完成时:
The volcano had raged for weeks before it finally calmed down. = 火山爆发了好几周才最终平息。
She had raged at him before realizing her mistake. = 她在他面前大发脾气，然后才意识到自己的错误。


将来时:
The wind will rage tomorrow. = 明天风会很大。
He will rage if he doesn't get his promotion. = 如果他不被提升，他会发怒。


虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, he would rage. = 如果他知道真相，他会勃然大怒。
I wish I hadn't raged at her; I feel terrible. = 我真希望我没有对她发脾气；我感觉很糟糕。


被动语态:
The city was raged by the storm. = 城市被暴风雨肆虐。
His reputation was raged against by the press. = 他的名声受到了媒体的攻击。


疑问句:
Does he often rage when things don't go his way? = 事情不如他的意时，他经常发脾气吗？
Will the storm rage all night? = 暴风雨会持续整夜吗？


否定句:
He didn't rage at her; he was surprisingly calm. = 他没有对她发火；他出奇地冷静。
The fire didn't rage out of control. = 大火没有失控。
