emblement:/ˈɛmblɪmənt/| n. |栽种物；附属物|  A plant or crop that is sown or planted.  It is considered personal property, even if growing on land owned by someone else. Emblements are typically harvested by the tenant farmer before the lease expires.| The farmer harvested his emblements before leaving the rented land.  农民在离开租赁土地之前收割了他的庄稼。| crops, produce | real property | 名词
