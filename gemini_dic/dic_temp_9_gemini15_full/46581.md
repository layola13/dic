damaged:/ˈdæmɪdʒd/ | adj. | 受损的；损坏的 |  Describes something that has been harmed or broken.  It implies impairment of function or value. | The car was damaged in the accident.  这辆车在事故中受损了。 The reputation of the company was severely damaged by the scandal. 这家公司的声誉因丑闻而严重受损。| broken, impaired, harmed, injured | undamaged, intact, perfect, sound |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm damages the crops every year.  暴风雨每年都会损害庄稼。
The overuse of the machine damages its components.  机器过度使用会损坏其部件。


一般过去时:
The earthquake damaged many buildings.  地震损坏了许多建筑物。
The heavy rain damaged the harvest.  大雨毁坏了收成。


现在完成时:
The flood has damaged several houses in the town.  洪水已经损坏了城镇中的几座房屋。
The constant exposure to sunlight has damaged her skin.  持续的阳光照射损害了她的皮肤。


过去完成时:
They discovered that the ship had been damaged before the voyage. 他们发现船在航行前就已经受损了。
The antique vase had been damaged beyond repair.  那个古董花瓶已经损坏到无法修复的地步了。


将来时:
The strong winds will damage the trees.  强风会损坏树木。
The construction project might damage the surrounding environment.  建筑项目可能会破坏周围环境。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have damaged the vase. 如果我更小心点，就不会损坏花瓶了。
I wish I hadn't damaged your phone. 我真希望我没有损坏你的手机。


被动语态:
The bridge was damaged by the flood.  桥梁被洪水冲坏了。
Many homes were damaged during the hurricane.  飓风期间许多房屋受损。


疑问句:
Was the car damaged badly?  这辆车损坏严重吗？
Were any buildings damaged in the fire?  火灾中是否有任何建筑物受损？


否定句:
The product was not damaged during shipping.  产品在运输过程中没有损坏。
My car wasn't damaged in the accident. 我的车在事故中没有损坏。
