regauging:/riːˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 重新测量；重新校准；重新评估 | To measure or assess something again.  This often involves recalibrating instruments or reassessing a situation. |例句：The engineer regauged the pipeline after the leak. 工程师在泄漏后重新测量了管道。

例句：We need to regauge the market's response to our new product. 我们需要重新评估市场对我们新产品的反应。


近义词：recalibrate, reassess, re-evaluate, remeasure | 反义词：None directly opposite, but words suggesting a lack of re-measurement could be considered opposites, such as: accept, ignore, maintain | 词性：动词
