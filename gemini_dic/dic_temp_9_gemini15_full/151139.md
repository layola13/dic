paratha:/pəˈrɑːθə/|/pəˈrɑːtɑː/| n. |印度烤饼|A paratha is a flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent, typically made from whole wheat flour and often stuffed with various fillings.  It is cooked on a griddle or pan. |例句：I had a delicious potato paratha for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个美味的土豆烤饼。|例句：She made several different kinds of parathas for the party. 她为聚会做了几种不同馅料的烤饼。|近义词：chapati, roti, flatbread|反义词：无
