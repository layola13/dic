intransgressible: /ˌɪntrænˈzɡrɛsəbl/ | adj. | 不可逾越的，不可违反的 | Impossible to violate or transgress;  incapable of being broken or disobeyed.  The rules were considered intransgressible. | The laws of physics are considered intransgressible.  The sacred oath was deemed intransgressible. | 近义词：unbreakable, inviolable, immutable, unchallengeable | 反义词：breakable, violable, mutable, challengeable
