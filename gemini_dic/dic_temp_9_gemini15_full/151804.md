passageway: /ˈpæsɪdʒweɪ/ | n. |通道，走廊，通路 | A passageway is a route or path, typically enclosed, that allows passage from one place to another. It can be a corridor, hallway, or any similar structure.  |例句：The passageway was dimly lit and smelled of damp earth. 这条通道光线昏暗，散发着潮湿泥土的气味。


The narrow passageway barely allowed two people to pass side by side.狭窄的通道几乎容不下两个人并排走过。|近义词：corridor, hallway, aisle, route, path |反义词：dead end, blockage, barrier |名词
