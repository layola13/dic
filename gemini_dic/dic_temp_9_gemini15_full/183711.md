resailing:/ˌriːˈseɪlɪŋ/| v. |再次出售；转售 |The act of selling something that has already been bought.  It often implies a profit motive,  and the item may or may not have been used. |Example:  Reselling concert tickets at inflated prices is illegal in many jurisdictions.  再次出售演唱会门票以高价在许多司法管辖区是非法的。|近义词：reselling, second-hand selling |反义词：buying, purchasing
