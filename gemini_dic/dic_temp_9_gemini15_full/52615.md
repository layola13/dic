digestion:/daɪˈdʒɛstʃən/| n. |消化；领悟|The process of breaking down food into a form that can be absorbed into the body.  This involves mechanical and chemical processes.|例句：Proper digestion is essential for good health. 良好的消化对健康至关重要。|例句：The digestion of complex carbohydrates takes time.  复杂碳水化合物的消化需要时间。|近义词：assimilation, absorption|反义词：maldigestion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He digests his food slowly. = 他消化食物很慢。
She digests information quickly. = 她理解信息很快。

一般过去时:
I digested the entire book in a week. = 我在一周内读完了整本书。
The patient digested his meal poorly. = 病人消化不良。

现在完成时:
I have digested all the important points in the lecture. = 我已经消化了讲座中的所有要点。
She has digested the news well. = 她已经很好地消化了这个消息。

过去完成时:
He had digested his lunch before the meeting. = 他在开会前已经吃完了午饭。
They had digested the information before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经消化了这些信息。


将来时:
