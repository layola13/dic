This often occurs in atmospheric processes or industrial applications.  | Example: The recondensations of water vapor in the atmosphere are crucial for the formation of clouds and precipitation.  再凝结的大气水汽对于云层和降水的形成至关重要。 | 近义词：recondensation, refreezing | 反义词：vaporization, sublimation | 名词
