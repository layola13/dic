pianissimo:/pjænɪˈsɪsimoʊ/| adj.|极弱的(音乐术语)|Extremely soft or quiet, especially in music.  It is the softest dynamic marking in music.|The pianist played the final notes pianissimo.|近义词：very soft, extremely quiet|反义词：fortissimo, forte


USAGE->
pianissimo is an adjective,  it doesn't have verb conjugations.  It describes the volume of music.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.  Instead, we can show its usage in different sentence structures.

Declarative Sentence:
The final movement was marked pianissimo. =  最后的乐章标记为极弱。

The orchestra played the passage pianissimo. = 乐队以极弱的音量演奏了这段乐句。


Interrogative Sentence:
Was the last note played pianissimo? =  最后一个音符是极弱演奏的吗？

Should we play this section pianissimo? = 我们应该以极弱的音量演奏这一段吗？


Exclamatory Sentence:
How pianissimo the music is! = 音乐多么轻柔！

Play it pianissimo! =  极弱的演奏！


PHRASE->
