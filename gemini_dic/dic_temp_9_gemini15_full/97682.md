inhuman:/ɪnˈhjuːmən/ | adj. | 不人道的；没有人性的；残酷的；非人的 | Lacking human qualities of kindness and compassion; cruel and brutal.  |例句：The inhuman conditions in the prison led to many deaths.  监狱中不人道的情况导致许多人死亡。
例句：His inhuman treatment of the animals was shocking. 他对动物的虐待令人震惊。 |近义词：barbaric, brutal, savage, cruel, heartless |反义词：humane, compassionate, merciful, kind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dictator's regime is inhuman. 独裁者的政权是不人道的。
His actions are inhuman and unacceptable. 他的行为是不人道的，不可接受的。

一般过去时:
The soldiers' actions during the war were inhuman. 战争期间士兵的行为是不人道的。
The treatment of prisoners was inhuman and illegal. 对待囚犯的方式是不人道的和非法的。

现在完成时:
The government has been inhuman in its response to the crisis. 政府应对危机的措施是不人道的。
They have shown inhuman cruelty towards their enemies. 他们对敌人表现出残忍的残酷。

过去完成时:
The people had suffered inhuman treatment for years.  人们多年来遭受着不人道的待遇。
It had become clear that the regime's policies were inhuman.  政权的政策是不人道的，这一点已经很清楚了。

将来时:
If the situation continues, more inhuman acts will follow. 如果情况继续下去，还会有更多不人道行为。
Their actions will likely be deemed inhuman by international standards.  他们的行为可能会被国际标准认定为不人道。

虚拟语气:
If only the world were not so inhuman! 但愿世界不是那么不人道！
I wish they had shown more humane, rather than inhuman, conduct. 我希望他们表现得更有人情味，而不是不人道。

被动语态:

The victims were treated inhumanely. 受害者受到了不人道的待遇。

疑问句:
Is this treatment inhuman? 这种待遇是不人道的吗？

否定句:
The response to the disaster was not inhuman; it was heroic. 对灾难的回应并非不人道，而是英勇的。
Their actions, while harsh, were not inhuman. 他们的行为虽然严厉，但并非不人道。

WORD ORIGIN->
The word "inhuman" is derived from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "human."  It literally means "not human," but in its common usage, it refers to a lack of human compassion and kindness, rather than a literal lack of human characteristics.
