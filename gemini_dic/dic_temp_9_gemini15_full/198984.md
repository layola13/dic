sightseeing:/ˈsaɪtsiːɪŋ/ | n. |观光；游览 |The act of visiting places of interest, typically as a tourist.  Sightseeing involves visiting and experiencing attractions in a new location. |例句：We spent the afternoon sightseeing in the old town. 我们在老城区度过了下午的观光时光。

例句：Our sightseeing tour included a visit to the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.  我们的观光之旅包括参观埃菲尔铁塔和卢浮宫博物馆。


近义词：tourism, excursion, tour, trip | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词
