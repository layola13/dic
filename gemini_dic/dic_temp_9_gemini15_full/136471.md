This term is often used in legal and technical contexts, particularly regarding certificates, licenses, or authorizations.|例句：The guarantee of nonrevocations is essential for the security of the system.  不可撤销的保证对于系统的安全至关重要。 The contract included a clause ensuring nonrevocations of the intellectual property rights. 合同中包含一项条款，确保知识产权不可撤销。|近义词：irrevocability, permanence, finality |反义词：revocability, cancellation, annulment |名词
