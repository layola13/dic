nonsupporting:/ˌnɒnsəˈpɔːtɪŋ/|/ˌnɑːnsəˈpɔːrtɪŋ/| adj. |不支持的；无帮助的|Not providing support or assistance.  This adjective describes something or someone that lacks support, either literally or figuratively.  It implies a lack of aid, backing, or reinforcement.|


例句：

His nonsupporting attitude towards the project led to its failure.  他的不支持态度导致了项目的失败。

The nonsupporting evidence made the case difficult to prove.  缺乏支持的证据使得此案难以证明。


近义词：unhelpful, unsupportive, unhelpful, antagonistic, contrary

反义词：supportive, helpful, assisting, aiding


词性：形容词
