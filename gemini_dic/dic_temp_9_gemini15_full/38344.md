conceives:/kənˈsiːvz/| vt.|构思，设想；怀孕；持有…想法|To form an idea, plan, or belief in the mind; to become pregnant.|例句：She conceived a plan to escape. 她构思了一个逃跑计划。
She conceived a child. 她怀孕了。|近义词：imagine, devise, invent, create|反义词：misconceive, misunderstand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceives innovative marketing strategies. 他构思创新的营销策略。
The artist conceives his works in vivid detail.  艺术家对他的作品有生动的构想。

一般过去时:
She conceived the idea during a long walk. 她在长时间散步时想到了这个主意。
They conceived a child last year. 他们去年生了个孩子。

现在完成时:
He has conceived several successful business ventures. 他已经构思出几个成功的商业项目。
The company has conceived a new product line. 公司已经构思出一条新的产品线。

过去完成时:
They had conceived the plan before the meeting. 他们在会议之前就已经构思好了计划。
She had conceived three children before she turned thirty.  她在三十岁之前已经生了三个孩子。

将来时:
He will conceive a masterpiece in the coming year. 他将在来年创作出一部杰作。
We will conceive a new approach to the problem. 我们将构思出解决这个问题的新方法。

虚拟语气:
If I had conceived that idea earlier, things would have been different. 如果我早点想到那个主意，情况就会不一样了。
I wish I had conceived a better solution. 我希望我能够想出一个更好的解决方案。

被动语态:
The project was conceived by a team of experts.  这个项目是由一个专家团队构思的。
A new marketing campaign has been conceived for the product launch. 为了产品发布，一个新的营销活动已经被构思出来。

疑问句:
How did she conceive such a brilliant idea? 她是怎么想到这么好的主意的？
Have they conceived a plan for the future? 他们有没有为未来制定计划？

否定句:
He didn't conceive the full implications of his actions. 他没有完全理解自己行为的后果。
They hadn't conceived of such a possibility. 他们从未想到过这种可能性。


PHRASE->
conceive of: 想象，设想
conceive in: 以...方式构思
conceive as: 把...看作
conceive with: 用...构思
to conceive a child: 怀孕
