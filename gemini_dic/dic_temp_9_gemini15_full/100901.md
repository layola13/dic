intuitionless:/ˌɪntuˈɪʃən.ləs/| adj.|缺乏直觉的，不凭直觉的|Lacking intuition; not guided by intuition.  The approach to the problem was purely logical and intuitionless.|例句：His analysis of the situation was completely intuitionless, relying solely on hard data. 他对局势的分析完全缺乏直觉，仅仅依靠硬数据。

例句：The robot's movements, though precise, were utterly intuitionless, lacking any sense of grace or fluidity. 机器人的动作虽然精确，但却完全缺乏直觉，没有任何优雅或流畅感。


近义词：unintuitive, instinctive, uninspired.

反义词：intuitive, insightful, instinctive.
