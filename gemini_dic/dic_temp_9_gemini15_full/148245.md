/ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪkəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 过度怀疑的倾向；过度怀疑的各种表现 | Excessive or unwarranted skepticism; instances or manifestations of excessive skepticism.  The word is extremely rare and largely theoretical, constructed by adding "-nesses" (a pluralizing suffix indicating instances or manifestations) to "overskeptical" (itself a combination of "over-" and "skeptical").  It suggests multiple instances of excessive doubt or disbelief. |  The professor's overskepticalnesses regarding the student's groundbreaking research were ultimately unfounded. 教授对学生突破性研究的过度怀疑最终被证明是毫无根据的。  His numerous overskepticalnesses hampered the team's progress. 他多次过度怀疑阻碍了团队的进展。 |  Excessive doubt, hypercriticism, incredulity |  Naiveté, credulity, gullibility | n.
