hancock:/ˈhænˌkɑːk/ | n. |签字；笔迹；手迹 |A person's signature, especially a prominent or distinctive one.  It can also refer to a person's handwriting or style of writing.|例句：The document bears the president's hancock. 这份文件上有总统的签字。

例句：His hancock is quite distinctive.他的签字很独特。


近义词：signature, autograph, scrawl

反义词：None directly opposite in meaning.  Words like "illegible" describe the opposite of a clear hancock.

词性：名词
