decarbonizing:/ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 脱碳；去碳化 | The process of reducing or eliminating carbon dioxide emissions.  It often refers to efforts to transition away from fossil fuels and towards renewable energy sources. | Example: Many countries are investing heavily in decarbonizing their economies. 许多国家正在大力投资其经济的脱碳。The company is committed to decarbonizing its operations by 2030.  该公司致力于到2030年实现运营脱碳。| 近义词：decarbonisation, decarbonization | 反义词：carbonizing, increasing carbon emissions |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is decarbonizing the energy sector. = 政府正在对能源行业进行脱碳。
We are decarbonizing our transportation system. = 我们正在对我们的交通系统进行脱碳。

一般过去时:
The factory decarbonized its production process last year. = 该工厂去年对其生产流程进行了脱碳。
They decarbonized their operations significantly. = 他们大幅度地减少了其运营的碳排放。

现在完成时:
The city has decarbonized its public transport. = 该城市已经对其公共交通进行了脱碳。
Scientists have successfully decarbonized a small-scale power plant. = 科学家们已经成功地对小型发电厂进行了脱碳。

过去完成时:
Before the new regulations, the company had not decarbonized its manufacturing. = 在新规出台之前，该公司尚未对其制造业进行脱碳。
The country had already decarbonized a significant portion of its economy by 2022. = 到2022年，该国已经对其经济的很大一部分进行了脱碳。

将来时:
The company will decarbonize its supply chain within the next decade. = 该公司将在未来十年内对其供应链进行脱碳。
We will decarbonize our homes through energy efficiency upgrades. = 我们将通过提高能源效率来对我们的房屋进行脱碳。

虚拟语气:
If we decarbonized our industries, we would mitigate climate change. = 如果我们对我们的工业进行脱碳，我们将减轻气候变化。
It is essential that we decarbonize our transportation system quickly. = 我们必须迅速对我们的交通系统进行脱碳。

被动语态:
The power grid is being decarbonized through renewable energy integration. = 电力网正在通过可再生能源整合进行脱碳。
The building's heating system is being decarbonized with a heat pump. = 该建筑的供暖系统正在通过热泵进行脱碳。

疑问句:
How can we effectively decarbonize the heavy industry sector? = 我们如何才能有效地对重工业部门进行脱碳？
Is the country committed to decarbonizing its agriculture? = 该国是否致力于对其农业进行脱碳？

否定句:
The company has not yet decarbonized its operations completely. = 该公司尚未完全对其运营进行脱碳。
They failed to decarbonize their manufacturing process sufficiently. = 他们未能充分对其制造流程进行脱碳。
