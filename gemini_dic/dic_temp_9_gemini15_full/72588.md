fly:/flaɪ/| n., v.|飞，飞行；苍蝇|To move through the air using wings; a small, winged insect.|例句：Birds fly in the sky. 鸟儿在天上飞翔。The fly landed on the food. 苍蝇落在食物上。|近义词：soar, soar, flit (v.); insect (n.)|反义词：crawl, walk (v.);  (n.无明确反义词)|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird flies high in the sky. = 鸟儿在高空中飞翔。
He flies to London every week. = 他每周都飞往伦敦。


一般过去时:
The plane flew over the mountains. = 飞机飞越了山脉。
She flew a kite in the park. = 她在公园里放风筝。


现在完成时:
I have flown in a helicopter before. = 我以前坐过直升机。
The birds have flown south for the winter. = 鸟儿们已经飞往南方过冬了。


过去完成时:
The plane had flown for hours before it landed. = 飞机飞行数小时后才降落。
They had flown to Paris before they went to Rome. = 他们在去罗马之前飞去了巴黎。


将来时:
We will fly to Hawaii next summer. = 我们明年夏天将飞往夏威夷。
He will fly a drone for the first time tomorrow. = 他明天将第一次操控无人机。


虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would fly. = 如果我有翅膀，我会飞。
I wish I could fly like a bird. = 我真希望我能像鸟一样飞翔。


被动语态:
The kite was flown by the child. = 风筝是被孩子放飞的。
The plane is flown by a skilled pilot. = 飞机由一名熟练的飞行员驾驶。


疑问句:
Does the bird fly south for the winter? = 这只鸟冬天会飞到南方去吗？
How long does the flight take? = 这趟飞行需要多长时间？


否定句:
The plane didn't fly because of the bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，飞机没有起飞。
I cannot fly a plane. = 我不会开飞机。
