parakeet:/ˈpærəkiːt/ | n. |鹦鹉，小型鹦鹉 | A small, often brightly colored parrot.  Parakeets are known for their ability to mimic sounds and their social nature.  Many species are popular as pets. |Example:  The children loved watching the vibrant green parakeet fly around the aviary. 孩子们喜欢看那只鲜艳的绿鹦鹉在鸟舍里飞来飞去。

The parakeet chirped happily in its cage.  这只鹦鹉快乐地叫着，在它的笼子里。|近义词：budgerigar, lovebird |反义词： (no direct antonym,  perhaps "large bird" could be considered a contrasting term) |名词
