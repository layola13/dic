enlists:/ɪnˈlɪsts/| vt.|登记；征募；获得…的帮助|To officially join the army, navy, air force, etc., or to persuade someone to join.|The army is enlisting new recruits.  军队正在招募新兵。|He enlisted the help of his friends. 他获得了朋友们的帮助。|recruits, registers, signs up| discharge, dismiss

USAGE->

一般现在时:
He enlists in the army every year. 他每年都报名参军。
She enlists the help of her colleagues regularly. 她经常寻求同事的帮助。


一般过去时:
He enlisted in the navy during the war. 他在战争期间加入了海军。
They enlisted the services of a professional consultant. 他们聘请了一位专业顾问的服务。


现在完成时:
He has enlisted in the marines for five years. 他已经参军5年了。
She has enlisted the support of several influential people. 她已经获得了几个有影响力的人的支持。


过去完成时:
He had enlisted before the war started.  战争爆发前他已经参军了。
They had enlisted the aid of experts before embarking on the project. 在开始这个项目之前，他们已经获得了专家的帮助。


将来时:
He will enlist in the air force next year. 他明年将加入空军。
She will enlist the help of her family during the move.  搬家期间她将寻求家人的帮助。


虚拟语气:
If he were younger, he would enlist in the army. 如果他年轻点，他会参军。
I wish I had enlisted in the navy when I was younger. 我真希望我年轻时就加入了海军。


被动语态:
Many young men were enlisted during the war. 战争期间许多年轻男子被征召入伍。
Her assistance was enlisted to complete the project on time.  为了按时完成项目，她的帮助被征用。


疑问句:
Will you enlist in the army? 你会参军吗？
Did they enlist the help of a lawyer?  他们寻求了律师的帮助吗？


否定句:
He did not enlist in the army. 他没有参军。
She did not enlist the help of anyone. 她没有寻求任何人的帮助。


PHRASE->

enlist in: 加入（军队等）
enlist the help of: 寻求…的帮助
enlist support for:  争取…的支持
enlist the services of: 聘用…的服务
enlist as:  以…的身份入伍
