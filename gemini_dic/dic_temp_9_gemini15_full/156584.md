picknick:/ˈpɪkˌnɪk/| n. |野餐；野餐会 |A picnic is a meal eaten outdoors, typically in a relaxed and informal setting.  It often involves bringing prepared food and drinks to a scenic location. |例句：We had a lovely picnic by the lake. 我们在湖边举行了一次愉快的野餐。|例句：They packed a picnic basket for their hike. 他们为徒步旅行准备了一个野餐篮。|近义词：outing, excursion |反义词：formal dinner, indoor meal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We have a picnic every Sunday. = 我们每周日都举行野餐。
They often pack sandwiches for their picnics. = 他们经常为野餐准备三明治。

I wish we could have a picnic tomorrow. = 我希望我们明天能举行野餐。
