nonflagellate: /ˌnɒnˈflædʒəleɪt/ | adj. | 无鞭毛的 | Not having a flagellum or flagella.  Describes organisms, particularly microorganisms, lacking the whip-like appendages used for locomotion. | Example:  Many amoebas are nonflagellate protists.  许多变形虫是无鞭毛的原生生物。 |近义词: aflagellate, nonmotile |反义词: flagellate
