copying:/ˈkɑːpiɪŋ/ /ˈkɒpiɪŋ/ | v. | 复制；抄袭；模仿 | The act of making a copy of something.  This can refer to reproducing something exactly, or imitating it.  It can also imply unauthorized reproduction, as in plagiarism. | 例句：He was copying the answers from his friend's paper. 他在抄他朋友试卷上的答案。
He's copying the artist's style. 他在模仿这位艺术家的风格。 | 近义词：duplicating, reproducing, imitating, plagiarizing | 反义词：creating, originating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He copies information from the internet. 他从互联网上复制信息。
She copies her sister's style. 她模仿她姐姐的风格。


一般过去时:
I copied the wrong address. 我抄错了地址。
They copied the design from a competitor. 他们抄袭了竞争对手的设计。


现在完成时:
I have copied all the necessary files. 我已经复制了所有必要的文档。
She has copied that painting several times. 她已经临摹过那幅画好几次了。


过去完成时:
He had copied the entire chapter before the exam. 他在考试前已经抄完了整章内容。
They had copied the data before the server crashed. 服务器崩溃前他们已经复制了数据。


将来时:
I will copy the document for you later. 我稍后会把文件复制给你。
She will copy the information to a flash drive. 她会把信息复制到闪存驱动器。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would copy the entire book. 如果我有时间，我会把整本书都抄下来。
I wish I had copied the notes before the lecture ended. 我真希望在讲座结束前我把笔记抄下来了。


被动语态:
The document was copied onto the server. 文件被复制到了服务器上。
The painting was copied by a famous artist. 这幅画是被一位著名艺术家临摹的。


疑问句:
Are you copying my work? 你在抄袭我的作品吗？
Did he copy the answers from the book? 他是从书上抄答案的吗？


否定句:
I did not copy his homework. 我没有抄袭他的作业。
She doesn't copy other people's work. 她不抄袭别人的作品。
