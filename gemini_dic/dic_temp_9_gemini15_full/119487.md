microorganism:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌɔːrɡənɪzəm/ | n. |微生物 |A microscopic organism, especially a bacterium, virus, or fungus.  微小的生物体，尤指细菌、病毒或真菌。| Example:  Microorganisms play a crucial role in decomposition. 微生物在分解中起着至关重要的作用。

The study of microorganisms is called microbiology. 微生物学是研究微生物的学科。|近义词：microbe, germ |反义词：macroscopic organism
