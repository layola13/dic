reverberating:/rɪˈvɜːrbəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 回荡的；持续的；引起共鸣的 |  Continuing to resonate or echo; reflecting or echoing sound.  The reverberating sound of the explosion shook the windows. 爆炸的回声震动了窗户。The reverberating effects of the scandal were felt for months.  丑闻的持续影响持续了数月。 | 近义词：resonating, echoing, resounding | 反义词：silent, muted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sound is reverberating through the valley. = 声音在山谷中回荡。
The news is reverberating across the nation. = 这个消息正在全国引起反响。


一般过去时:
The church bells reverberated across the town. = 教堂的钟声在小镇上回荡。
His words reverberated in my mind for days. = 他的话在我的脑海中回荡了好几天。


现在完成时:
The impact of the decision has reverberated throughout the company. =  这个决定的影响已经在公司中产生了持续的回响。
The artist's work has reverberated through generations. = 艺术家的作品影响了几代人。


过去完成时:
The echoes of the past had reverberated through his life. = 过去的回声在他的一生中持续回荡。
The effects of the war had already reverberated across the globe. = 战争的影响已经波及全球。


将来时:

The implications of the treaty will reverberate for years to come. = 这项条约的影响将在未来数年持续发酵。


虚拟语气:
If the news were true, it would reverberate around the world. = 如果这个消息是真的，它将在全世界引起反响。
I wish my voice could reverberate across the ocean to reach him. = 我希望我的声音能够传过大海到达他那里。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
