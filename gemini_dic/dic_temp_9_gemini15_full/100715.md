intralingual:/ˌɪntrəˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj.|语言内部的；语言内部的；|Relating to or occurring within a single language.  It refers to processes or phenomena that take place entirely within the boundaries of one language, without involving contact or comparison with other languages.|Example:  Intralingual errors are mistakes made within a single language, such as grammatical errors or misuse of vocabulary. 语言内部错误是在单一语言中犯的错误，例如语法错误或词汇误用。|近义词：intralanguage|反义词：interlingual
