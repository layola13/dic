gristing:/ˈɡrɪstɪŋ/| n.|磨碎；碾磨；谷物加工|The process of grinding grain into flour or meal.  It can also refer to the material being ground.|例句：The gristing of wheat is an essential step in bread making. 小麦的磨碎是制作面包的关键步骤。 The old mill was still used for gristing corn.  旧磨坊仍然用来磨玉米。|近义词：grinding, milling|反义词：暂无
