chutneys:/ˈtʃʌtniːz/| n.|酸辣酱；混合调味酱|Chutneys are a type of condiment, typically made from fruits, vegetables, vinegar, spices, and sugar, resulting in a sweet, sour, and spicy flavor profile. They are often served with Indian food.|例句：The restaurant served a delicious mango chutney with the curry. 这家餐厅用咖喱搭配了美味的芒果酸辣酱。|例句：She made a spicy tomato chutney for the party. 她为派对做了一款辣味番茄酸辣酱。|近义词：relishes, preserves, pickles|反义词：无
