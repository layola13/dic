finance:/ˈfaɪnæns/| n. v.|金融；财务；资金；为…提供资金|The management of money; the study of money and its uses.|例句：The company's finances are in a precarious state. 公司的财务状况岌岌可危。|例句：We need to finance the project somehow. 我们需要想办法为这个项目筹集资金。|近义词：funding, capital, economics|反义词：debt, deficit.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finances his education with loans. = 他通过贷款资助他的教育。
The company finances its operations through sales. = 公司通过销售来资助其运营。

一般过去时:
They financed the new building with a bank loan. = 他们通过银行贷款资助了新大楼的建设。
The government financed the research project. = 政府资助了该研究项目。

现在完成时:
We have financed several projects this year. = 今年我们已经资助了好几个项目。
She has financed her own business for five years. = 她自己经营了五年的生意。

过去完成时:
They had financed the expansion before the market crashed. = 市场崩盘之前，他们已经为扩张提供了资金。
The company had financed its growth through retained earnings. = 公司通过留存收益为其增长提供了资金。

将来时:
We will finance the project with a combination of grants and loans. = 我们将通过拨款和贷款的组合来为该项目提供资金。
The investors will finance the startup company. = 投资者将为这家初创公司提供资金。

虚拟语气:
If we had more capital, we could finance a larger project. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们可以资助更大的项目。
I wish we could finance the research without taking on debt. = 我希望我们能够在不承担债务的情况下为研究提供资金。

被动语态:
The project was financed by the government. = 该项目由政府资助。
The expansion was financed through a bond issue. = 扩张是通过发行债券来资助的。

疑问句:
How will you finance your trip? = 你将如何资助你的旅行？
Who financed the construction of the bridge? = 谁资助了这座桥的建设？

否定句:
They did not finance the film. = 他们没有为这部电影提供资金。
The company could not finance the new venture. = 公司无法为新的企业提供资金。
