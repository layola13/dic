morons:/ˈmɔːrɒnz/| n.|白痴，傻瓜|A moron is a stupid person.  The term is considered offensive and should be used cautiously. |例句：He called me a moron for making that mistake. 他因为我犯了那个错误而骂我是白痴。

例句：Don't be a moron; think before you act.  别傻了，行动前先想想。
