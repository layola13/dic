compendiums:/kəmˈpɛndiəm/| n. |纲要；概要；文摘集 |A compendium is a concise but thorough collection of information on a particular subject.  It is often a summary or abstract of a larger work or a collection of smaller works on a related topic. |例句：This compendium of poetry includes works from various eras and styles. 这本诗歌文摘集包括了各个时代和风格的作品。


例句：The professor gave us a compendium of notes on the upcoming exam. 教授给了我们一份关于即将到来的考试的笔记纲要。


近义词：summary, abstract, digest, synopsis


反义词：expansion, elaboration, detail


词性：名词
