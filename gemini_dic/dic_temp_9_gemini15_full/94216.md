imperforate:/ɪmˈpɔːrfəreɪt/| adj.|不通的；闭锁的；无孔的 | Not perforated or pierced; lacking an opening.  The term often refers to a congenital condition where a natural opening or passage is absent or blocked. | Example: The imperforate hymen prevented normal menstruation.  这层闭锁的处女膜阻止了正常的月经。  The imperforate anus required surgical intervention. 肛门闭锁需要手术干预。 |近义词：blocked, occluded, closed |反义词：perforated, open
