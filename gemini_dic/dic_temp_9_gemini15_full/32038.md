cherishment:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃmənt/ | n. |珍爱；珍惜；爱护 |The act of cherishing; the state of being cherished.  It implies a deep feeling of love, fondness, and protective care. |例句：Her cherishment of her family was evident in everything she did. 她对家人的珍爱在她所做的一切中都显而易见。

例句：The cherishment of old traditions is important to the community.  对古老传统的珍爱对这个社区很重要。


近义词：treasure, adoration, fondness, appreciation, regard

反义词：neglect, disregard, contempt, scorn


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cherishes his memories of childhood. = 他珍藏着他童年时期的回忆。
She cherishes her friendship with him. = 她珍视与他的友谊。

一般过去时:
They cherished their time together. = 他们珍惜他们在一起的时光。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
