semisatirically:/ˌsɛmiˈsætərikəli/| adv. | 半讽刺地；略带讽刺地 |  In a way that is partly satirical; with a hint of satire.  It implies a less direct or overt form of satire than "satirically".|例句：He spoke semisatirically about the politician's promises, hinting at their lack of realism. 他半讽刺地谈论了这位政客的承诺，暗示了它们的缺乏现实性。

例句：The article treated the subject semisatirically, using humor to subtly critique the situation. 这篇文章以半讽刺的方式处理这个主题，用幽默来巧妙地批判这种情况。


近义词：ironically, wryly, dryly


反义词：seriously, earnestly, sincerely


词性: 副词
