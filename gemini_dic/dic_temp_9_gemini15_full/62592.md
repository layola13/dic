enfeebler:/ɪnˈfiːblər/| n. |使衰弱者，使虚弱的事物 |A person or thing that weakens or debilitates.  It can refer to a person, a condition, or a process that causes weakness or debilitation. |The relentless heat was an enfeebler, sapping their energy.  持续的酷暑使他们衰弱。|近义词：debilitator, weakenor |反义词：strengthener, invigorator


USAGE->
