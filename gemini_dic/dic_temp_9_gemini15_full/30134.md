ceed:/siːd/ | 词根 |  继续，前进 | 来自拉丁语“cedere”，意思是“to go, to yield, to proceed”  |  ceed本身不是一个独立使用的单词，而是一个词根，出现在许多英语单词中，表示“前进”、“继续”或“让步”的意思。|  | |  词根


USAGE->
该词根不单独使用，以下列举包含该词根的单词的用法：

**Succeed:** /səkˈsiːd/ | v. | 成功；继承 | To achieve something that you have been trying to do or obtain. | He succeeded in his career. 他在事业上取得了成功。 |  He succeeded his father as CEO. 他继承了他父亲的CEO职位。| thrive, prosper | fail, falter | 动词


**Proceed:** /prəˈsiːd/ | v. | 继续；进行 | To continue doing something that has already been started. | Please proceed with your presentation. 请继续你的演讲。 | The meeting proceeded smoothly. 会议顺利进行。 | continue, advance | stop, cease | 动词


**Exceed:** /ɪkˈsiːd/ | v. | 超过；胜过 | To be greater than a particular number or amount. | The cost exceeded our budget. 成本超过了我们的预算。 |  His success exceeded all expectations. 他的成功超出了所有人的预期。 | surpass, outstrip | fall short of, underperform | 动词


**Precede:** /prɪˈsiːd/ | v. | 先于；在...之前 | To come before something or someone in time or order. | The introduction preceded the main speech. 介绍在主要演讲之前。 | The storm preceded the heavy rain. 暴风雨先于大雨来临。 | antecede, forerun | follow, succeed | 动词


**Accede:** /ækˈsiːd/ | v. | 同意；就职 | To agree to a request or demand. | The government acceded to the workers' demands. 政府同意了工人的要求。 | He acceded to the throne. 他即位了。 | consent, agree | refuse, reject | 动词


PHRASE->
包含ceed词根的单词短语用法随单词而异，无法统一列举。  例如：

* **Succeed in doing something:** 成功做某事
* **Proceed with caution:** 谨慎行事
* **Exceed the speed limit:** 超速
* **Precede the meeting with a prayer:** 会议前祈祷
* **Accede to power:** 上台执政
