electropneumatic: /ˌɪˌlɛktrəʊpnuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 电气气动的 |  Relating to or using a system that combines electrical and pneumatic power.  This type of system uses electrical signals to control pneumatic (compressed air) actuators or valves.  This combination allows for precise control and automation in various applications.


例句：

The factory uses an electropneumatic system to control its robotic arms. = 工厂使用电气气动系统来控制其机械臂。

The electropneumatic brake system provides superior stopping power and control. = 电气气动制动系统提供卓越的制动能力和控制。


近义词：electrohydraulic (电液的)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，只能用描述性的词语来表达相反的概念，例如：purely mechanical (纯机械的), purely electrical (纯电动的) )


词性：形容词
