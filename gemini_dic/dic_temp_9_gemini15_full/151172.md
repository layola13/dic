parceling:/ˈpɑːrsəlɪŋ/|/ˈpɑːrs(ə)lɪŋ/| n. v.|分割；分发；打包|The act of dividing something into parts or parcels; the process of distributing something in parcels.  It can also refer to the act of wrapping something into a parcel. |例句：The land was parcelled out among the heirs.  土地被分割给继承人。

The company specializes in parceling out its products to various retailers. 这家公司专门负责将其产品分发给各个零售商。|近义词：division, distribution, apportioning,packaging|反义词：unification, consolidation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They parcel out the land to farmers. = 他们把土地分给农民。
She parcels her time carefully between work and family. = 她仔细地分配自己的时间在工作和家庭之间。


一般过去时:
The government parceled out the relief supplies. = 政府分发了救济物资。
He parceled the gift carefully before sending it. = 他在寄送礼物之前仔细地包装好了它。


现在完成时:
They have parceled out all the available land. = 他们已经分配了所有可用的土地。
She has carefully parceled her time for the entire project. = 她已经仔细地为整个项目分配了时间。


过去完成时:
They had parceled out the inheritance before the lawyer arrived. = 在律师到来之前，他们已经分配了遗产。
She had already parceled the documents before leaving for the meeting. = 她离开去开会之前已经把文件整理好了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had parceled my time better for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更好地分配了时间。


被动语态:

The gifts were carefully parceled for safe delivery. = 礼品被仔细包装以确保安全送达。


疑问句:

否定句:
