overboiling: /ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪlɪŋ/ | n. |过度沸腾；煮过头| The act of boiling something for too long, resulting in a loss of quality or undesirable texture.  It often refers to food that has been cooked past its optimal point. |Example: The overboiling of the pasta resulted in a mushy texture.  过度沸腾的面条导致口感糊烂。 The overboiling of the potatoes made them taste unpleasant. 土豆煮过头了，尝起来不好吃。|近义词：overcooking, excessive boiling |反义词：undercooking
