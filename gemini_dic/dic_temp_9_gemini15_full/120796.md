misascribe:/ˌmɪs əˈskraɪb/| vt.|误归于；错认为…是…的；归咎于错误的人或事物|To attribute something to the wrong cause, person, or source.|例句：He misascribed the quote to Shakespeare, when it was actually written by Marlowe. 他错把这句名言归于莎士比亚，而它实际上是马洛写的。  例句：The historian misascribed the painting to a lesser-known artist. 这位历史学家将这幅画错误地归于一位鲜为人知的艺术家。|近义词：misattribute, misapply|反义词：ascribe correctly, attribute accurately|动词
