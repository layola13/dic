simultaneousnesses:/ˌsɪmlˈteɪniəsnisɪz/| n. |同时性；同时发生的事物|The plural form of "simultaneity," referring to multiple instances or aspects of things happening at the same time.  It emphasizes the multiplicity of concurrent events or actions.|例句：The simultaneousnesses of the city's sounds and movements overwhelmed her. 城市的声音和运动的同步性让她不知所措。
The simultaneousnesses of the project's various deadlines proved challenging to manage. 项目各种截止日期的同步性证明难以管理。|近义词：co-occurrences, concurrences|反义词：sequentiality, successiveness
