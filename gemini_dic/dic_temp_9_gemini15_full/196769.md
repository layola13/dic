sexless:/ˈsɛksləs/ | adj. | 无性别的；缺乏性吸引力的 | Lacking sexual desire or activity; not sexually attractive.  |例句：Their marriage has become sexless. 他们的婚姻已经变得毫无性趣。

例句：He felt sexless and unattractive after his illness.  他生病后感觉自己没有性吸引力，也不具魅力。


近义词：asexual, passionless, unromantic, frigid (for women)

反义词：passionate, sensual, erotic, sexually active
