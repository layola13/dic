polytechnics:/ˌpɒliˈtɛknɪks/ | n. | 理工学院；高等技术学院 | A polytechnic is an educational institution that specializes in technical and vocational education, often offering courses leading to diplomas or associate degrees in fields such as engineering, technology, and applied sciences.  Polytechnics typically emphasize practical skills and hands-on training alongside theoretical knowledge. | Many students choose polytechnics for their career-focused education.  许多学生选择理工学院接受以职业为导向的教育。| technical college, vocational school, institute of technology | liberal arts college (反义词不完全对应，侧重于对比其教育理念) | 名词
