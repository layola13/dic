chaplains:/ˈtʃæplɪnz/| n. |牧师；随军牧师|A chaplain is a member of the clergy, often associated with a particular institution (like a hospital, prison, military unit, or school), who provides religious guidance and support.  They offer spiritual comfort, counsel, and conduct religious services for those in their care. |例句：The hospital chaplain visited the patient daily to offer comfort and spiritual guidance. 医院的牧师每天探望病人，提供安慰和精神指导。

The military chaplain provided support to the troops during the difficult deployment.  随军牧师在部队艰难的部署期间为士兵们提供支持。


近义词：clergyman, priest, minister |反义词：N/A
