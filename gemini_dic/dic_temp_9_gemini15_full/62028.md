enchantment:/ɪnˈtʃɑːntmənt/ | n. |魅力；魔法；入迷 |The enchantment of the forest was undeniable.  森林的魅力是不可否认的。|近义词：charm, magic, fascination |反义词：repulsion, disenchantment

Her enchantment with the story is evident. = 她对故事的着迷是显而易见的。

一般过去时:
