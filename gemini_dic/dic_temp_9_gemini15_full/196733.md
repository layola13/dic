sews:/soʊz/| vt.|缝纫，缝合|To join or unite (pieces of fabric) with stitches using a needle and thread.  It also can refer to the act of creating something by sewing.|例句：She sews beautiful dresses. 她缝制漂亮的连衣裙。

She sews a button onto her shirt. 她把纽扣缝在衬衫上。

近义词：stitches, stitches together, mends, darns|反义词：rips, tears, unravels|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sews every day. = 她每天缝纫。
He sews clothes for a living. = 他靠缝衣服为生。

一般过去时:
My grandmother sewed my wedding dress. = 我祖母缝制了我的婚纱。
She sewed the rip in her jeans. = 她缝补了她牛仔裤上的裂口。

现在完成时:
I have sewed all the curtains for the living room. = 我已经缝好了客厅的所有窗帘。
She has sewed many quilts over the years. = 多年来她缝制了许多棉被。

过去完成时:
She had sewed the dress before the party. = 派对前她已经缝好了裙子。
They had sewed all the costumes before the play began. = 在戏剧开始之前，他们已经缝好了所有的戏服。

将来时:
I will sew a new dress for the wedding. = 我将为婚礼缝制一件新衣服。
She will sew the buttons back on tomorrow. = 她明天会把纽扣缝回去。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would sew a quilt. = 如果我有时间，我会缝制一床棉被。
I wish I could sew as well as my mother. = 我希望我能像我母亲一样擅长缝纫。

被动语态:
The dress was sewed by a professional seamstress. = 这件衣服是由专业的裁缝缝制的。
The buttons are being sewed onto the coat. = 纽扣正在缝到外套上。

疑问句:
Do you sew? = 你会缝纫吗？
Does she sew often? = 她经常缝纫吗？

否定句:
I don't sew very well. = 我缝纫不太好。
He didn't sew the button back on. = 他没有把纽扣缝回去。
