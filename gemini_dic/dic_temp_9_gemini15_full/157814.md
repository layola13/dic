placophora:/plækəˈfɔːrə/ | n. |多板纲动物 |  Placophora is a class of marine mollusks characterized by their eight overlapping dorsal plates, also known as valves, that protect their bodies. They are commonly known as chitons.  | Example:  The rocky intertidal zone is a habitat for many species of placophora.  多板纲动物在多岩石的潮间带栖息。 | 近义词: chiton (less formal) | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it refers to a taxonomic class)
