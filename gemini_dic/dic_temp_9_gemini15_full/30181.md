celesta:/səˈlɛstə/| n. |钢片琴|A musical instrument resembling a piano, but with steel bars instead of strings, which are struck by hammers.  The sound is ethereal and bell-like.|例句：The celesta's delicate melody filled the concert hall. 钢片琴的轻柔旋律充满了音乐厅。
例句：The composer used the celesta to create a magical atmosphere in the ballet. 作曲家在芭蕾舞剧中用钢片琴营造出梦幻般的氛围。|近义词：glockenspiel, carillon|反义词:无
