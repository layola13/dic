self-masteries:/ˌsɛlfˈmæstəriːz/| n. |自我控制能力；自我掌握能力 |The plural form of "self-mastery," referring to multiple instances or aspects of self-control and self-discipline. It implies a high level of skill and control over one's thoughts, emotions, and actions.  |例句：Her years of meditation practice resulted in remarkable self-masteries in emotional regulation and focus.  她的多年冥想练习使她在情绪调节和专注方面获得了非凡的自我控制能力。

|例句：The martial arts training emphasized the development of physical and mental self-masteries. 武术训练强调培养身心方面的自我控制能力。|近义词：self-control, self-discipline, self-regulation, mastery, command |反义词：lack of self-control, impulsiveness, self-indulgence |名词
