gingerbreads:/ˈdʒɪndʒərˌbredz/ | n. |姜饼(复数) |A sweet cookie or cake flavored with ginger and spices.  Gingerbread is often decorated and shaped into festive figures, especially during the Christmas season. |例句：The children decorated the gingerbread houses with colorful icing.孩子们用五彩缤纷的糖霜装饰姜饼屋。

例句：We bought a variety of gingerbread men at the Christmas market.我们在圣诞市场买了几种姜饼人。

近义词：gingerbread cookies, spiced cookies |反义词：无

词源:
The word "gingerbread" combines "ginger" (a spice) and "bread" (baked goods).  The use of ginger in baked goods dates back centuries. The specific term "gingerbread" emerged in the Middle Ages in Europe.
