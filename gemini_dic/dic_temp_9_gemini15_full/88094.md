The word "hight" does not exist in standard English.  There is no entry for it in major dictionaries.  It's likely a misspelling of "height".  Therefore, I will provide the analysis for "height".

height:/haɪt/ | n. | 高度，身高，海拔 | The height of the building is impressive. 这座建筑物的高度令人印象深刻。|近义词：elevation, altitude | 反义词：depth, low, shortness | 名词
