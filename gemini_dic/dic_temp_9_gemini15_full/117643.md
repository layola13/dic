megaton:/ˈmɛɡətɒn/| n. |百万吨(TNT当量)| A megaton is a unit of explosive power equal to one million tons of TNT.  It is often used to measure the power of nuclear weapons. |例句：The bomb had a yield of 20 megatons. 这枚炸弹的当量为20兆吨。

例句：The explosion was equivalent to several megatons of TNT.  这次爆炸相当于几百万吨TNT的威力。


近义词：kiloton (for smaller explosions)


反义词：N/A (no direct opposite, but smaller units like kiloton can be considered a relative opposite in terms of scale)


词性: 名词
