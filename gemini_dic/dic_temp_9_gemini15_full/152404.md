pavings:/ˈpeɪvɪŋz/| n. |铺路面；铺砌物| paved surfaces, such as roads, sidewalks, or driveways.  Paving refers to the material used for surfacing.  The plural "pavings" refers to multiple instances of paved surfaces or different types of paving materials.|例句：The new pavings in the town square are made of granite.  镇广场上的新铺路面是用花岗岩铺成的。

例句：Different types of pavings were used throughout the park. 公园里使用了不同类型的铺路材料。


近义词：pavements, surfaces, road surfaces|反义词：unpaved, rough ground


词源:源自动词pave (铺设)，加上-ing后缀构成名词，表示“铺设物”或“铺路面”。  The word "pave" itself comes from the Old French word "paver," meaning "to pave."
