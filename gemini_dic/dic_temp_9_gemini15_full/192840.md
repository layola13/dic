It often implies an intense or thorough search, driven by a specific need or purpose.  |例句：His searchingness was evident in his relentless pursuit of the truth. 他对真理的执着追求，显露出了他探寻的本质。

His eyes held a searchingness that made her uncomfortable. 他的眼神里有一种探寻的神情，让她感到不舒服。
