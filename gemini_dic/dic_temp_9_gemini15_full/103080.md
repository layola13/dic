/ˈdʒiːnəs/ | n. |属，类；种类；类型 | Genus refers to a taxonomic rank used in the biological classification of organisms, ranking above species and below family.  It represents a group of closely related species sharing common characteristics. |  The genus *Panthera* includes lions, tigers, leopards, and jaguars.  (豹属包括狮子、老虎、豹子和美洲豹。)  The genus *Homo* encompasses modern humans and their extinct ancestors. (人属包括现代人和他们的已灭绝祖先。) | species, kind, type | species (as a singular term), family |  n.
