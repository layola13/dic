precondition:/ˌpriːˈkɒndɪʃən/ /ˌpriːˈkɑːndɪʃən/ | n. |前提条件，先决条件 | A necessary condition that must be met before something else can happen or be done.  A precondition is something that must exist or be true before something else can happen.  |例句：  A precondition for starting the project is securing funding.  启动项目的前提条件是获得资金。

The successful completion of the training is a precondition for employment. 培训的成功完成是就业的先决条件。


|近义词： prerequisite, requirement, necessary condition |反义词： consequence, result |名词
