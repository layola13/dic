multitube:/ˈmʌltiˌtjuːb/| n. |多管的；多管系统 |A multitube system is a system that uses multiple tubes to perform a task, often in parallel.  It is commonly used in laboratory settings for various applications, such as blood testing or chemical reactions.  For example, a multitube centrifuge spins many samples simultaneously. |The lab technician used a multitube system to analyze the blood samples. = 实验室技术人员使用多管系统分析血液样本。  The new multitube reactor increased efficiency significantly. = 新的多管反应器大大提高了效率。|近义词：multichannel, multiple-tube |反义词：single-tube
