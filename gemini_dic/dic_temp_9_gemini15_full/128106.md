nesting:/ˈnestɪŋ/| n. v.| 筑巢；嵌套；套叠 |The act of building a nest, or the state of being nested.  It can also refer to the arrangement of items within each other, like Russian dolls.|例句：Birds are busy nesting in the spring.  鸟儿们在春天忙着筑巢。  The software uses a nesting structure for its data.  该软件使用嵌套结构存储其数据。|近义词：building (a nest), embedding,  enclosing|反义词：unpacking, dispersing

词源:
The word "nesting" derives from the Old English word "nest," meaning "bird's nest."  The "-ing" suffix indicates the act of doing something.  The extension of the meaning to include other forms of "nesting" (like data structures) is a later development based on the similarity of the structure.
