disinvest:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛst/| vt.|撤资；停止投资|To withdraw investment from (a company or project).  It often implies a deliberate decision to reduce or eliminate investment in something, sometimes for ethical or political reasons.|例句：The company decided to disinvest in the failing project.  公司决定停止对这个失败的项目投资。


例句：Several investors are disinvesting from fossil fuel companies.  一些投资者正在从化石燃料公司撤资。


近义词：divest, withdraw, pull out


反义词：invest


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government disinvests in failing industries. = 政府撤资于那些衰败的产业。
She disinvests her savings regularly to avoid risk. = 她定期撤回储蓄以规避风险。


一般过去时:
They disinvested from the stock market last year. = 他们去年从股市撤资了。
He disinvested in his failing business. = 他撤出了他亏损的生意。


现在完成时:
The fund has disinvested from several controversial projects. = 该基金已从几个有争议的项目撤资。
We have disinvested in that area of research. = 我们已经停止在那个研究领域投资。


过去完成时:
They had disinvested before the market crash. = 他们在市场崩盘前已经撤资了。
The company had disinvested its assets before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，该公司已经撤回了其资产。


将来时:
The investors will disinvest if the risk increases. = 如果风险增加，投资者将会撤资。
They will disinvest in the project next quarter. = 他们将在下个季度停止对该项目的投资。


虚拟语气:
If the economy worsened, they would disinvest immediately. = 如果经济恶化，他们会立即撤资。
I wish I had disinvested sooner. = 我真希望我早点撤资。


被动语态:
The company's assets were disinvested by the board. = 该公司的资产被董事会撤资了。
Significant funds have been disinvested from the project. = 大笔资金已经从该项目撤资。


疑问句:
Will the government disinvest in education? = 政府会削减教育经费吗？
Have they disinvested in renewable energy? = 他们是否已经停止对可再生能源的投资？


否定句:
The company did not disinvest in its research and development. = 公司并没有停止研发方面的投资。
They haven't disinvested despite the recent losses. = 尽管最近遭受损失，他们也没有撤资。


PHRASE->
disinvest in  = 撤资于...
disinvest from = 从...撤资
strategic disinvestment = 战略性撤资
partial disinvestment = 部分撤资
forced disinvestment = 被迫撤资
