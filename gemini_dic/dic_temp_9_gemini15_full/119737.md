midweek:/ˈmɪdwiːk/| adj.| 周中；工作日 |  Referring to the days in the middle of the week, typically Tuesday or Wednesday.  | Example: I have a meeting midweek.  我周中有一个会议。  | 近义词：weekday, mid-week | 反义词：weekend
