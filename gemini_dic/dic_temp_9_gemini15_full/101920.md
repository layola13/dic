islamize:/ˈɪslæmaɪz/| vt.|伊斯兰化；使符合伊斯兰教义|To make (a country, society, etc.) conform to the principles of Islam.  This can involve implementing Islamic law, promoting Islamic culture, or suppressing non-Islamic practices.|例句：The government attempted to islamize the education system by introducing religious studies into the curriculum.  政府试图通过在课程中引入宗教研究来使教育体系伊斯兰化。|例句：Some fear that the increasing islamization of the country will lead to restrictions on personal freedoms.  一些人担心该国伊斯兰化的加剧会导致个人自由受到限制。|近义词：Islamify|反义词：secularize
