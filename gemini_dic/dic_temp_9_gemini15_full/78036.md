genuflecting:/dʒɛnjuˈflɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 屈膝礼拜；跪拜 |  The act of bending the knee, especially in worship or reverence.  It often implies a respectful or humble bowing. | Example: The worshippers were genuflecting before the altar.  朝拜者在祭坛前屈膝礼拜。  | 近义词：kneeling, bowing, prostrating | 反义词：standing, rising
