microrecord:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌrɛkərd/| n. |缩微记录；缩微胶片|A microrecord is a photographic record of documents, typically reduced in size to save space and facilitate storage and retrieval.  Microrecords are often used for archiving large volumes of information. |例句：The library archives its historical documents on microrecords. 图书馆将历史文献保存在缩微胶片上。

例句：The company uses microrecords to store its vast collection of financial reports.  这家公司使用缩微记录来存储其大量的财务报表。


近义词：microfilm, microfiche|反义词：full-size record, original document|名词
