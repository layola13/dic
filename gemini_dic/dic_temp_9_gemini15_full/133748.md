nongalactic: /ˌnɒnɡəˈlæktɪk/ | adj. | 非银河系的 | Not belonging to or originating from the Milky Way galaxy.  The term describes objects, phenomena, or entities situated outside our galaxy. | Example:  The researchers detected a nongalactic source of radio waves. = 研究人员探测到一个非银河系来源的无线电波。


近义词: extragalactic

反义词: galactic


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so verb conjugations are not applicable.  The usage focuses on its descriptive function within a sentence.


Example Sentences:

Simple Present: That distant quasar is a nongalactic object. = 那个遥远的类星体是一个非银河系天体。
Simple Past: Astronomers previously identified the object as a nongalactic source of gamma rays. = 天文学家先前已将该天体确定为非银河系伽马射线源。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "nongalactic". Its usage is primarily within the context of astronomy and astrophysics.
