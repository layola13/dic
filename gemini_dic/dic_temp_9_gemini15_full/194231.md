self-manifestation: /ˌsɛlfˌmænɪfɛsˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 自我显化；自我实现 | The act or process of something becoming clearly visible or evident, especially through its own inherent qualities or actions; the process by which an individual's thoughts and beliefs create their reality.  | 例句：The artist's self-manifestation through her work was undeniable. 艺术家通过作品展现的自我显化是不可否认的。

Her self-manifestation as a successful entrepreneur took years of hard work and dedication. 她作为一名成功企业家的自我实现花费了多年的努力和奉献。


近义词：self-realization, actualization, materialization, embodiment | 反义词：latent, hidden, unrealized | 词性: noun
