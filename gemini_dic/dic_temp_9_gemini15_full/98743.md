insurrectionist: /ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪst/ | n. | 叛乱者，造反者 | A person who takes part in an insurrection.  Someone who engages in or supports armed rebellion against an established government or authority. | Example: The insurrectionists seized control of the capital building.  叛乱者占领了国会大厦。  The government accused the protestors of being insurrectionists.  政府指控抗议者是叛乱分子。|近义词：rebel, revolutionary, insurgent, mutineer |反义词：loyalist, supporter of the government |名词
