humidity:/hjuːˈmɪdəti/|/ˈhjuːmɪdɪti/| n. |湿度，潮湿|The amount of water vapor present in the air.  It is often expressed as a percentage of the maximum amount of water vapor the air can hold at a given temperature. High humidity can make the air feel heavy and sticky.|例句：The humidity is high today, making it feel very hot and muggy. 今天的湿度很高，让人感觉又热又闷。|例句：The humidity level in the greenhouse needs to be carefully monitored. 温室里的湿度水平需要仔细监测。|近义词：dampness, moistness|反义词：dryness, aridity|名词
