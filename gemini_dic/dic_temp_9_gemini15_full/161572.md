potterer:/ˈpɒtərər/| n. v.|陶工；笨手笨脚的人；慢吞吞地做事的人 |A potterer is someone who works with clay to make pots and other ceramic objects.  It can also refer to someone who does things slowly and inefficiently, or someone who fusses around with small tasks. |例句：He's a skilled potterer, creating beautiful pieces from simple clay. 他是一位技艺精湛的陶工，用简单的粘土创造出美丽的器物。

He's such a potterer; he takes ages to do anything. 他做事真慢吞吞的，做什么事情都要花很长时间。|近义词：craftsman, artisan, dawdler, dilly-daller |反义词：expert, professional, efficient, quick
