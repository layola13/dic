illustrating: /ˈɪləstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 说明；阐明；图解 | To clarify, explain, or make something clear, often with examples or visual aids.  | The professor illustrated his point with a relevant anecdote. 教授用一个相关的轶事来说明他的观点。 | clarifying, explaining, exemplifying | obscuring, confusing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He illustrates his lectures with slides. 他用幻灯片来说明他的讲座。
The book illustrates the importance of healthy eating. 这本书阐明了健康饮食的重要性。

一般过去时:
She illustrated her argument with statistics. 她用统计数据来说明她的论点。
The artist illustrated the children's book.  这位艺术家为儿童读物配图。

现在完成时:
The author has illustrated many books for children. 这作者已经为孩子们画了很多书的插图。
She has illustrated her point effectively with real-life examples. 她用现实生活中的例子有效地说明了她的观点。

过去完成时:
He had illustrated the concept before moving on to the next topic. 他在继续下一个话题之前已经阐明了这个概念。
They had illustrated the dangers of smoking in their presentation. 他们在演示中已经说明了吸烟的危害。

将来时:
She will illustrate her findings in the upcoming conference. 她将在即将召开的会议上阐述她的发现。
The company will illustrate its new product with a video. 公司将用视频来展示其新产品。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would illustrate the process in more detail. 如果我有更多时间，我会更详细地说明这个过程。
I wish I could illustrate the complexity of the problem more clearly. 我希望我能更清楚地说明这个问题的复杂性。

被动语态:
The point was illustrated with a diagram.  要点用图表说明了。
The book is illustrated by a well-known artist. 这本书是由一位著名的艺术家绘制插图的。

疑问句:
How will you illustrate your hypothesis? 你将如何说明你的假设？
Does the graph illustrate the data effectively? 这个图表有效地说明了数据吗？

否定句:
The speaker did not illustrate his points clearly enough. 演讲者没有足够清楚地说明他的观点。
The article failed to illustrate the benefits of the new policy. 这篇文章未能说明新政策的好处。


PHRASE->

well-illustrated = 图文并茂的
