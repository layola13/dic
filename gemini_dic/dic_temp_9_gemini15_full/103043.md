jelly:/ˈdʒɛli/ | n. |果冻，冻状物 |A sweet, soft, usually fruit-flavored dessert made with gelatin.  It can also refer to a viscous substance. |例句：I love strawberry jelly on my toast. 我喜欢在我的吐司上涂草莓果冻。

例句：The jellyfish left a trail of jelly in the water. 水母在水中留下了一条果冻状的痕迹。|近义词：jello, preserves,  gel |反义词：solid, firm
