crayfishes:/ˈkreɪˌfɪʃɪz/| n. |小龙虾（复数）|Crayfishes are freshwater crustaceans belonging to the family Astacidae. They are similar to lobsters but smaller.  |例句：The lake is teeming with crayfishes.  这个湖里充满了小龙虾。|例句：We caught several crayfishes during our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼旅行中抓到几只小龙虾。|近义词：crawfish, crawfishes, freshwater lobsters |反义词：None specifically.
