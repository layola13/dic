gravestones:/ˈɡreɪvstoʊnz/| n. |墓碑|A gravestone is a stone marker placed on a grave, usually inscribed with the name and dates of the deceased.|例句：The old gravestones were covered in moss.  古老的墓碑上覆盖着苔藓。|例句：We placed flowers on our grandmother's gravestone. 我们在祖母的墓碑上放了鲜花。|近义词：tombstone, headstone|反义词：无
