countertactics: /ˌkaʊntərˈtæktɪks/ | n. |反制战术，对策| Strategies or actions designed to oppose or counteract the tactics of an opponent.  It involves anticipating an opponent's moves and developing plans to neutralize their effectiveness. |例句：The general devised countertactics to the enemy's surprise attack.  将军制定了应对敌军突然袭击的反制战术。

例句：The company developed strong countertactics to deal with the competitor's aggressive marketing campaign. 公司制定了强有力的反制策略来应对竞争对手积极的营销活动。


近义词：countermeasures, countermoves, defensive strategies, responses


反义词：Offensive strategies, proactive strategies


词性：名词
