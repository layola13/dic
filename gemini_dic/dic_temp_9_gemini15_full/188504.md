ruffianous:/ˈrʌfɪənəs/| adj.|粗暴的；流氓的；恶棍的；粗鲁的 |  Characterized by the behavior or qualities of a ruffian; rough, violent, and lawless.  It suggests a more ingrained and habitual tendency towards violence and disregard for rules than simply being "rough" or "rowdy." |The ruffianous behavior of the gang led to their arrest.| 近义词：violent, brutal, aggressive, lawless, thuggish | 反义词：gentle, polite, civilized, law-abiding, courteous
