flak's:/flæk/| n.|防空炮火，强烈批评|Anti-aircraft fire; strong criticism.|例句：The plane took heavy flak from enemy fighters.  敌机遭到敌方战斗机的猛烈炮火攻击。|例句：The politician received a lot of flak for his controversial remarks.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论受到了很多批评。|近义词：barrage, criticism, attack|反义词：praise, approval


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plane takes flak from the enemy. = 飞机受到敌人的炮火攻击。
She gets flak for her ideas. = 她因为她的想法受到批评。

一般过去时:
The pilot took heavy flak during the mission. = 飞行员在任务中受到了猛烈的炮火攻击。
He took a lot of flak for his decision. = 他因为他的决定受到了很多批评。

现在完成时:
The city has taken significant flak from the storm. = 这个城市遭受了风暴的严重打击。
The company has taken considerable flak for its handling of the crisis. = 公司因其处理危机的措施而受到了相当大的批评。

过去完成时:
They had already taken flak for the delay before the meeting. = 会议之前，他们就已经因为延误受到了批评。
The general had taken intense flak for his military strategy. = 将军因为他的军事战略受到了强烈的批评。


将来时:
The new policy will likely take a lot of flak. = 新政策可能会招致很多批评。
The new building will probably take flak from environmental groups. = 这座新建筑可能会遭到环保组织的批评。

虚拟语气:
If he had anticipated the flak, he wouldn't have made that speech. = 如果他预料到会受到批评，他就不会发表那个演讲了。
I wish I hadn't made that comment; I've taken a lot of flak for it. = 我真希望我没有发表那番评论；因为那番评论我受到了很多批评。

被动语态:
The project was subjected to intense flak from the public. = 这个项目受到了公众的强烈批评。
The proposal was met with a barrage of flak. = 这个提案遭到了猛烈的批评。

疑问句:
Is the new policy going to take a lot of flak? = 新政策会招致很多批评吗？
Did he receive much flak for his actions? = 他因为他的行为受到了很多批评吗？

否定句:
The proposal didn't take much flak. = 这个提案没有受到多少批评。
He didn't expect to receive so much flak. = 他没想到会受到这么多的批评。
