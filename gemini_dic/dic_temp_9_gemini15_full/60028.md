edt:/ˈɛdɪt/|abbr.|编辑，编辑版|缩写词，指Eastern Daylight Time (东部夏令时)|例句：The article was sent to the editor for edt. 这篇文章被送去编辑进行编辑。|近义词：EST (Eastern Standard Time), revision |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor edits the manuscript carefully. = 编辑仔细地编辑稿件。
She edits the video before uploading it. = 她上传视频前会先编辑它。


一般过去时:
He edited the document last night. = 他昨晚编辑了这份文件。
They edited the film to remove inappropriate scenes. = 他们剪辑电影以去除不合适的场景。


现在完成时:
She has edited three chapters already. = 她已经编辑了三章。
He has edited the book for several months. = 他已经编辑这本书好几个月了。


过去完成时:
They had edited the article before submitting it. = 他们提交文章之前已经编辑过了。
She had edited the video twice before final approval. = 她最终批准之前已经编辑了两次视频。


将来时:
I will edit the photos tomorrow. = 我明天将编辑照片。
They will edit the footage after the interview. = 采访结束后他们会编辑素材。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would edit the video myself. = 如果我有时间，我会自己编辑视频。
I wish I had edited the report more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地编辑报告。


被动语态:
The manuscript was edited by a professional editor. = 这份手稿由专业编辑编辑。
The video is being edited right now. = 视频正在编辑中。


疑问句:
Have you edited the document yet? = 你编辑好文档了吗？
Who edited the film? = 谁编辑的这部电影？


否定句:
I did not edit the video. = 我没有编辑视频。
She hasn't edited the chapter yet. = 她还没编辑那一章。



PHRASE->
edit out = 删除
edit in = 加入
edit down = 删减
final edit = 最终编辑
rough edit = 初稿编辑
