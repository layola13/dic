non-contradictory: /ˌnɒnˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ | adj. | 不矛盾的，一致的 |  Free from contradiction; consistent.  The statements made were non-contradictory and supported each other.  | 近义词：consistent, compatible, coherent | 反义词：contradictory, inconsistent

例句：

His testimony was non-contradictory, strengthening the prosecution's case.  他的证词前后一致，加强了检方的案情。

The two theories, while different, are ultimately non-contradictory. 这两种理论虽然不同，但最终并不矛盾。


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **contra-**: prefix meaning "against," "opposite"
* **dict-**: root meaning "to say," "to speak"
* **-ory**: suffix forming adjectives


用法说明：


这个词主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某种说法、论述、证据或观点之间不存在矛盾。它强调的是一致性和逻辑的连贯性。


以下是一些例句，展现了不同语境下的用法：


1. **描述陈述:** The witness's account was completely non-contradictory. (证人的陈述完全前后一致。)

2. **描述理论:**  The two scientific theories are non-contradictory, despite their apparent differences. (这两个科学理论虽然表面上有所不同，但并不矛盾。)

3. **描述行为:**  Her actions were non-contradictory with her stated intentions. (她的行为与她所声明的意图并不矛盾。)

4. **描述证据:** The evidence presented was non-contradictory and compelling. (提交的证据前后一致且令人信服。)


需要注意的是，"non-contradictory"强调的是缺乏内在矛盾，而非与外部事实的吻合。一个非矛盾的论述，在逻辑上自洽，但其内容可能仍然是错误的。
