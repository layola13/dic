quietness:/ˈkwaɪətɪs/| n. |安静；宁静；平静 |The state or quality of being quiet; stillness; tranquility.  It refers to the absence of noise or disturbance. |例句：The quietness of the countryside was very relaxing.  乡村的宁静非常令人放松。

例句：The deep quietness of the forest was broken only by the chirping of crickets. 森林的幽静只被蟋蟀的鸣叫打破。


近义词：silence, stillness, peace, calm, tranquility

反义词：noise, clamor, uproar, turbulence, chaos

词性：名词
