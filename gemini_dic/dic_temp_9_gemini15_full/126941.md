narrators:/nəˈreɪtərz/ | n. |叙述者，讲述者 |Those who tell a story, whether in a book, film, or other medium.  They guide the audience through the narrative, often shaping the perspective and understanding of events. |例句：The novel has multiple narrators, each offering a unique viewpoint. 这部小说有多个叙述者，每个人都提供了独特的视角。

例句：The film's narrator is an omniscient presence, aware of all the characters' thoughts and feelings. 这部电影的叙述者是一个无所不知的存在，了解所有角色的想法和感受。


近义词：storytellers, speakers, recounters | 反义词：listeners, audience
