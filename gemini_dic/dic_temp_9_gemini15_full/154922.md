pessimism: /ˈpɛsɪmɪzəm/ | n. |悲观主义；悲观情绪 | The belief that bad things are more likely to happen than good things.  It is a general outlook characterized by negativity and a lack of hope. | 例句：His pessimism about the future of the company was infectious. 他对公司未来的悲观情绪具有传染性。

例句：Her pessimism prevented her from taking risks and pursuing her dreams. 她的悲观情绪使她不敢冒险，无法追求自己的梦想。


近义词：despair, gloom, cynicism, negativity | 反义词：optimism, hopefulness, cheerfulness


词性: 名词
