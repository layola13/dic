deism:/ˈdiːɪzəm/| n. |自然神论，顺其自然|Deism is a philosophical belief that attributes the existence of the universe to a supreme being, but denies supernatural revelation or intervention.  Deists believe God created the universe but does not intervene in its affairs. | Example:  Many Enlightenment thinkers embraced deism, emphasizing reason and observation over religious dogma.  许多启蒙思想家都信奉自然神论，强调理性与观察胜过宗教教条。|近义词：natural religion, rationalism |反义词：theism, atheism
