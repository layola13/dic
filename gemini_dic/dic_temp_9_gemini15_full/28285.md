capture:/ˈkæptʃər/ | n. vt. |捕获；俘获；夺取；(摄影)拍摄；(用文字或图像)记录；吸引；表达 | To take possession of something, especially by force; to record or represent something, especially in a photograph or other image; to attract and hold the attention of someone or something. |例句：The police captured the thief.  警察抓住了小偷。

The photographer captured a stunning sunset. 摄影师拍下了一幅令人惊叹的日落。


近义词：seize, catch, arrest, trap, grab, record, convey, fascinate, enthrall |反义词：release, liberate, free, miss, lose

词源:
The word "capture" comes from the Old French word "capturer," which itself is derived from the Latin word "capere," meaning "to take" or "to seize."  The evolution reflects the core meaning of taking possession or seizing something.
