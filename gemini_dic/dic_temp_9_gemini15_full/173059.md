quarterdeck:/ˈkwɔːtədɛk/ | n. | 艉楼；船尾上层甲板 | The quarterdeck is the highest stern section of a ship's upper deck, traditionally reserved for officers.  It is often a place of ceremony and command. | The captain stood on the quarterdeck, surveying the turbulent sea. 船长站在艉楼上，眺望汹涌的大海。 The admiral inspected the troops from the quarterdeck.  海军上将从艉楼上检阅部队。| stern, poop deck |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
