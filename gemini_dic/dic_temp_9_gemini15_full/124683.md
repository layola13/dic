moves:/muːvz/ | n. v. | 移动；举动；步骤；动作 |  The word "moves" can function as both a noun and a verb. As a noun, it refers to an action of changing position, a step in a process, or a piece of a game. As a verb, it means to change position or to cause something to change position. |

例句 (Noun):
The chess player's next move was crucial. = 棋手的下一步棋至关重要。

The dancer's moves were graceful and elegant. = 舞者的动作优雅而优美。


例句 (Verb):
He moved the chair closer to the table. = 他把椅子搬到桌子旁边。

The wind moved the leaves on the trees. = 风吹动了树叶。


近义词 (Noun): actions, steps, maneuvers, gestures, movements
近义词 (Verb): shifts, relocates, transfers, stirs, displaces


反义词 (Noun): stillness, inactivity, stagnation
反义词 (Verb): stays, remains, stops


词源:
From Old English *mōvian, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bewegen and German bewegen. The noun use developed from the verb.
