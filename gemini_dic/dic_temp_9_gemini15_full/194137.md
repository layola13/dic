self-inflicted: /ˌsɛlfɪnˈflɪktɪd/ | adj. | 自 inflicted 的；自己造成的；自己招致的 |  Describes an injury, problem, or situation that is caused by oneself.  It implies a degree of responsibility or culpability on the part of the person experiencing the negative consequence. |


例句：

His self-inflicted wounds required immediate medical attention.  他的自残伤口需要立即就医。

She blamed her current financial problems on her own self-inflicted mistakes. 她将目前的经济困境归咎于自己造成的错误。


近义词：self-caused, self-imposed, voluntary


反义词：accidental, involuntary, unintentional


词性：形容词
