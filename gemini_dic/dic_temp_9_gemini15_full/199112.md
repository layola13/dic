silage:/ˈsaɪlɪdʒ/| n.|青贮饲料|Preserved fodder, typically grass or other green crops, stored in airtight conditions to create an anaerobic environment that prevents spoilage and allows fermentation to preserve nutrients.  Silage is a crucial component of livestock feed, especially for cattle and sheep.|例句：The farmer carefully sealed the silo to ensure the proper fermentation of the silage.  这位农民仔细地密封了筒仓，以确保青贮饲料的适当发酵。

The quality of the silage depends heavily on the moisture content of the harvested crop. 青贮饲料的质量很大程度上取决于收获作物的含水量。|近义词：ensilage, fodder, forage|反义词：hay (dried fodder)
