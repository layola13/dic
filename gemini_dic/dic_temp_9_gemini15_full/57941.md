drinking:/ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ/| v.|饮酒；饮用；喝|The act of consuming a liquid, especially alcoholic beverages.|例句：He enjoys drinking beer after work. 他喜欢下班后喝啤酒。|例句：She was drinking water to stay hydrated. 她喝水以保持水分。|近义词：imbibing, quaffing, sipping|反义词：None specifically, but abstaining from drinking could be considered an antonym.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I drink coffee every morning. = 我每天早上喝咖啡。
They drink tea with their meals. = 他们用餐时喝茶。

一般过去时:
He drank too much wine last night. = 他昨晚喝了太多酒。
We drank champagne to celebrate. = 我们喝香槟庆祝。

现在完成时:
I have drunk all the milk. = 我已经喝完所有的牛奶了。
She has drunk three glasses of water. = 她已经喝了三杯水。

过去完成时:
He had drunk all the beer before we arrived. =在我们到达之前，他已经喝完所有的啤酒了。
They had drunk several cocktails before the party started. = 派对开始前，他们已经喝了几杯鸡尾酒。

将来时:
I will drink more water tomorrow. = 我明天会多喝水。
We will drink to your health. = 我们将为你健康干杯。

虚拟语气:
If I drank less coffee, I would sleep better. = 如果我少喝咖啡，我会睡得更好。
I wish I hadn't drunk so much wine. = 我真希望我没有喝那么多酒。

被动语态:
The water was drunk quickly. = 水很快就被喝完了。
The wine is being drunk at the party. = 酒正在派对上被喝掉。

疑问句:
Are you drinking anything? = 你喝什么东西吗？
What are they drinking? = 他们在喝什么？

否定句:
I don't drink alcohol. = 我不喝酒。
He didn't drink any coffee this morning. = 他今天早上没有喝咖啡。
