finalises:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使最终确定；完成；定稿 | To make something final or complete.  |例句：He finalised the report before submitting it. 他在提交报告之前完成了报告。 |例句：The committee finalised the plans for the new building. 委员会最终确定了新建筑的计划。 |近义词：complete, conclude, finish |反义词：begin, start, initiate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finalises the contract every month. 他每月最终确定合同。
She finalises her work before leaving the office. 她下班前完成工作。

一般过去时:
He finalised the deal yesterday. 他昨天完成了这笔交易。
They finalised the agreement last week. 他们上周达成了协议。

现在完成时:
She has finalised all her reports this week. 她本周已经完成了所有报告。
He has finalised the design for the new product. 他已经完成了新产品的最终设计。

过去完成时:
He had finalised the arrangements before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他已经完成了安排。
They had finalised the budget before the meeting. 会议前他们已经完成了预算。

将来时:
She will finalise the project by next Friday. 她将在下周五之前完成项目。
He will finalise the paperwork tomorrow. 他明天将完成文书工作。

虚拟语气:
If he finalised the report on time, he would get a bonus. 如果他按时完成报告，他将获得奖金。
I wish I had finalised my application earlier. 我希望我早点完成我的申请。

被动语态:
The contract was finalised last night. 合同昨晚最终确定了。
The details will be finalised soon. 细节很快就会确定。

疑问句:
Has she finalised the proposal yet? 她已经完成提案了吗？
When will the deal be finalised? 这笔交易什么时候才能最终确定？

否定句:
He didn't finalise the report on time. 他没有按时完成报告。
They haven't finalised the plans yet. 他们还没有最终确定计划。
