confident:/ˈkɒnfɪdənt/ /ˈkɑːnfɪdənt/ | adj. |自信的，确信的 |Having or showing confidence.  Feeling or showing a strong belief in one's own abilities or qualities. |例句：She's a confident speaker. 她是一位自信的演讲者。

例句：He felt confident about his chances of success. 他对成功的几率充满信心。


近义词：self-assured, sure, certain, positive, assured

反义词：insecure, doubtful, hesitant, uncertain, timid

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is confident in his abilities. = 他对自己的能力充满信心。
She is confident that she will succeed. = 她确信自己会成功。


一般过去时:
He was confident he would win the race. = 他自信他会赢得比赛。
She was confident of her decision. = 她对自己的决定很有把握。


现在完成时:
I have been confident throughout the process. = 我在整个过程中一直充满信心。
They have become more confident in their work. = 他们对自己的工作越来越有信心了。


过去完成时:
She had been confident that she would pass the exam, but she failed. = 她曾自信能通过考试，但她却失败了。
He had felt confident before the interview, but he got nervous. = 面试前他曾感到自信，但他却紧张起来。


将来时:
I will be confident when I'm better prepared. = 当我准备充分时，我会充满信心。
She will be confident in her presentation. = 她将在她的演示中充满信心。


虚拟语气:
If I were confident, I would take the risk. = 如果我有信心，我会冒险。
I wish I were more confident in my abilities. = 我希望我对自己的能力更有信心。


被动语态:  (Confident is rarely used in the passive voice)
His ability is considered confident by his colleagues. = 他的能力被同事们认为是自信的。（被动语态例子，但并非常用）
The project was completed with confident execution. = 项目以自信的执行完成。（被动语态例子，但并非常用）


疑问句:
Are you confident you can finish the project on time? = 你有信心按时完成项目吗？
Is he confident about his future? = 他对他的未来有信心吗？


否定句:
I am not confident about the outcome. = 我对结果没有信心。
She wasn't confident in her abilities. = 她对自己的能力没有信心。


PHRASE->
be confident in/about = 对……有信心
feel confident = 感到自信
look confident = 看起来自信
sound confident = 听起来自信
confidently  = 自信地
