rowan:/ˈroʊən/|n.|山楂树；山楂果|A rowan is a small tree with clusters of bright red berries.  The wood is hard and the berries are sometimes used to make jams and jellies.  It is also known as a mountain ash.|例句：The rowan trees were ablaze with red berries in the autumn. 秋天山楂树上挂满了火红的浆果。

The rowan tree is a symbol of protection in some cultures.  在一些文化中，山楂树是保护的象征。|近义词：mountain ash|反义词:|无
