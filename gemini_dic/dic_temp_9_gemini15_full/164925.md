pregnant:/ˈprɛɡnənt/| adj.|怀孕的，充满…的|Expecting a child; filled or saturated with something.|She is pregnant with her first child. 她怀了她的第一个孩子。The air was pregnant with tension.空气中充满了紧张气氛。|近义词：expectant, with child|反义词：barren, infertile


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She is pregnant. = 她怀孕了。
The atmosphere is pregnant with anticipation. = 空气中充满了期待。


一般过去时:
My wife was pregnant last year. = 我妻子去年怀孕了。
The room was pregnant with silence. = 房间里一片寂静。


现在完成时:
She has been pregnant for five months. = 她怀孕五个月了。
The situation has been pregnant with danger for weeks. = 这种情况已经危险了好几个星期了。


过去完成时:

The air had been pregnant with tension before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，空气中充满了紧张气氛。


将来时:
She will be pregnant next year. = 她明年会怀孕。
The political climate will be pregnant with possibilities after the election. = 选举后，政治气候将充满各种可能性。


虚拟语气:
If she were pregnant, she would be very happy. = 如果她怀孕了，她会非常高兴。
If the situation were pregnant with such danger, we should have been informed. = 如果情况如此危险，我们应该已经被告知了。


被动语态:
(The word "pregnant" is rarely used in the passive voice.)  这个词很少用被动语态。


疑问句:
Is she pregnant? = 她怀孕了吗？
Is the air pregnant with the scent of flowers? 空气中弥漫着花香吗？


否定句:
She is not pregnant. = 她没有怀孕。
The report was not pregnant with meaning. = 这份报告意义不明确。
