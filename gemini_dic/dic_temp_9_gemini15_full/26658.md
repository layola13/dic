cacophonous:/kæˈkɒfənəs/| adj.| 刺耳的；不和谐的；难听的 |  Characterized by a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds.  It describes a sound that is unpleasant to the ear because it is a mixture of many different and harsh sounds. |例句：The orchestra's cacophonous rehearsal was a source of annoyance to the neighbors.  乐队的刺耳排练让邻居们很烦躁。

The market was a cacophony of sounds, from vendors hawking their wares to the chatter of customers. 市场是一片嘈杂的声音，从小贩叫卖商品到顾客的闲聊，应有尽有。|近义词：discordant, jarring, harsh, raucous |反义词：harmonious, melodious, euphonious


词源:
From Medieval Latin *cacophonia, from Greek kakophōnía, from kakos "bad" + phōnē "sound, voice".
