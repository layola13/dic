/ˈhaɪərɪŋ/| v.|雇佣，提升|The act of employing someone or something; the process of raising something to a higher level or position.|The company is currently undertaking a major hiring drive. 这家公司目前正在进行大规模的招聘。  The higher management decided to promote him. 高层管理人员决定提升他。| employing, recruiting, engaging| firing, dismissing, lowering


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company is hiring new employees. = 公司正在招聘新员工。
He is constantly hiring new talent. = 他不断地招聘新人才。

一般过去时:
They hired a new marketing manager last month. = 他们上个月聘请了一位新的市场经理。
She was hired for her expertise in data analysis. = 她因其数据分析方面的专业知识而被聘用。

现在完成时:
We have hired three new engineers this year. = 今年我们已经聘请了三名新工程师。
The company has been hiring aggressively to expand its operations. = 公司一直在积极招聘以扩大业务。

过去完成时:
They had hired a consultant before starting the project. = 他们开始项目之前已经聘请了一位顾问。
She had already been hired by another firm before we contacted her. = 我们联系她之前，她已经被另一家公司聘用了。

将来时:
We will be hiring more staff next quarter. = 下个季度我们将招聘更多员工。
The company will hire additional workers to meet the increased demand. = 公司将雇佣更多工人以满足日益增长的需求。

虚拟语气:
If they hired more people, they would be more efficient. = 如果他们雇佣更多人，效率会更高。
I wish they had hired someone with more experience. = 我希望他们当初聘用一个更有经验的人。

被动语态:
A new marketing specialist was hired last week. = 上周聘请了一位新的市场专家。
Many candidates are being hired for the summer internship program. = 许多候选人正在被聘用参加暑期实习项目。

疑问句:
Are they hiring for this position? = 他们正在招聘这个职位吗？
How many people will be hired for this project? = 这个项目将招聘多少人？

否定句:
We are not hiring at the moment. = 我们目前没有招聘。
They did not hire anyone for the vacant position. = 他们没有为这个空缺职位招聘任何人。
