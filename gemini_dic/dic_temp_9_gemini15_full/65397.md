/ˌjuːfəˈmɪstɪkli/ | adv. |委婉地，含蓄地 |Using a euphemism to avoid being blunt or offensive.  It softens the impact of unpleasant or embarrassing words or topics. | He euphemistically referred to her dismissal as a "restructuring of the department." 他委婉地称她的解雇为“部门重组”。| indirectly, delicately, subtly | bluntly, frankly, directly |  副词
