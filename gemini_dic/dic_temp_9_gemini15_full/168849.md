词源:
The word is a combination of "pro-" (in favor of) and "euthanasia" (the act of intentionally ending a life to relieve pain and suffering).  Euthanasia itself comes from Greek roots: "eu" (good) and "thanatos" (death).  Therefore, the word literally translates to "in favor of a good death."
