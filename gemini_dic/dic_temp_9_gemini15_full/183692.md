rerouted:/ˌriːˈraʊtɪd/| v. |重新规划路线；改道|To change the route or course of (something, typically traffic or a journey).  It implies a deliberate change of plan, often due to an obstruction or unforeseen circumstance.|例句：The flight was rerouted due to bad weather.  航班因恶劣天气而改道。

例句：The traffic was rerouted around the accident scene. 交通绕过事故现场改道行驶。


近义词：diverted, redirected, rechanneled|反义词：maintained its original course


词源:re-(表示重复或再次)+route(路线)+-ed(过去分词后缀)
