codified:/ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪd/| adj.|编纂的；成文法的；法定的|Reduced to a systematic code or system; arranged and expressed in a systematic form.  Something that has been codified is put into a system or set of rules.|The laws of the country were codified centuries ago.  这个国家的法律几个世纪前就已经编纂成文了。|The rules of the game have been codified in a rulebook.  游戏的规则已经在规则手册中编纂成文了。|systematized, formalized, standardized|uncodified, unwritten, informal
