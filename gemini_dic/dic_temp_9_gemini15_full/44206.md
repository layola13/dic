crocetin:/ˈkroʊsɪtɪn/| n. | 番红素 | A red-orange carotenoid pigment found in saffron and other plants.  It's a diester of crocetin dialdehyde. Crocetin is known for its potential health benefits, including antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. | Example:  Studies have shown that crocetin, a component of saffron, possesses potent antioxidant properties.  研究表明，番红花中的成分番红素具有强大的抗氧化性能。|近义词：carotenoid, pigment |反义词：无
