passivizable:/ˈpæsɪvaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可被动的；可被动化的 | Capable of being made passive.  This term is primarily used in linguistics and refers to sentences or verbs that can be transformed into the passive voice.  It implies that the grammatical structure allows for the subject to become the object and the object to become the subject, highlighting the action performed upon it. |

The sentence "The dog chased the ball" is passivizable, becoming "The ball was chased by the dog."


The verb "to eat" is passivizable, as in "The apple was eaten."


近义词: passivatable


反义词:  unpassivizable (though this word is less commonly used)


词性: adjective
