sacalait:/ˈsækəleɪt/| n.|萨卡莱特（一种鱼）|A sacalait is a type of fish, specifically a small, freshwater fish, usually referring to the  shad,  especially the gizzard shad (Dorosoma cepedianum) in Louisiana French. It's also sometimes used more generally for other small, silvery fish.  |例句：The fishermen caught a bucket full of sacalait. 渔民们捕到了一桶萨卡莱特。|近义词：shad, silverfish (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A|名词
