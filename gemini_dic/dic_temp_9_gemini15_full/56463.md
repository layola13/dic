dolphinaria:/ˌdɒlfɪˈneəriə/| n. |海豚馆；海豚水族馆|A dolphinarium is a facility, usually a large aquarium or marine park, where dolphins are kept and displayed, often with shows or educational programs.  It's specifically designed for the keeping and exhibiting of dolphins. |Example: The children were thrilled by the dolphin show at the local dolphinarium. 孩子们对当地海豚馆的海豚表演感到兴奋。|近义词：aquarium, marine park |反义词：N/A
