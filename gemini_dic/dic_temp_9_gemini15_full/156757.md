piercingnesses:/ˈpɪərsɪŋnəsɪz/| n.|穿透性；尖锐性；刺痛感|The piercingnesses of the wind and rain made it hard to stay outside. 风雨的穿透力让人难以在户外待着。|近义词：sharpness, acuteness, intensity|反义词：dullness, bluntness


英文解析：Piercingnesses is the plural form of "piercingness," which refers to the quality of being piercing;  a sharp, intense, or penetrating quality.  It can describe a physical sensation (like a piercing cold wind), a sound (a piercing scream), or a more metaphorical sharpness (piercing insight).


例句：

The piercingnesses of the criticism deeply wounded her.  尖锐的批评深深地伤害了她。


The multiple piercingnesses of the interrogations left the suspect exhausted.  多轮尖锐的审问让嫌疑人筋疲力尽。


词源：

Derived from the adjective "piercing," which itself comes from the verb "pierce" (to penetrate or go through something).  The suffix "-ness" is added to create the noun form, indicating a state or quality.  The plural "-es" is added to indicate multiple instances of this quality.
