schemas:/ˈskiːməz/ | n. | 模式；结构；纲要 | A schema is a representation of a plan or theory in the form of an outline or model.  It's a mental framework that helps us organize and interpret information.  Schemas can be applied to various fields like databases, psychology, and computer science. | Example:  The database schema defines the tables and their relationships.  数据库模式定义了表及其关系。

The psychologist studied the patient's schemas to understand their behavior. 心理学家研究了病人的模式以理解他们的行为。 |近义词：model, framework, plan, template | 反义词：chaos, randomness
