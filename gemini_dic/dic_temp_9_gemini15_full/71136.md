fishmongers:/ˈfɪʃˌmʌŋɡərz/| n. |鱼贩；卖鱼的 |A fishmonger is a person who sells fish, typically in a market or shop.  They often deal with a variety of fresh and sometimes frozen seafood. |例句：The local fishmonger always has the freshest catches of the day. 当地鱼贩总是当天最新鲜的渔获。The fishmonger expertly filleted the salmon for the customer. 鱼贩熟练地为顾客将鲑鱼片成鱼片。|近义词：fish seller, fish dealer |反义词: N/A
