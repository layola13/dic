fills:/fɪlz/| v.|填充，充满，满足|to make something full; to occupy or use all of a space or container|例句：The room fills with laughter.  房间里充满了笑声。|例句：He fills the glass with water. 他用水把杯子装满。|近义词：complete, fulfill, saturate|反义词：empty, deplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The box fills with toys. = 箱子装满了玩具。
She fills the bathtub with water. = 她把浴缸装满了水。


一般过去时:
He filled the tank with gas. = 他把油箱加满了油。
They filled the forms with their personal information. = 他们用个人信息填写了表格。


现在完成时:
The pool has filled with rain water. = 游泳池已经积满了雨水。
I have filled all the requirements. = 我已经满足了所有要求。


过去完成时:
She had filled the jar with cookies before the party. = 派对前她已经把罐子装满了饼干。
They had filled out the application before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经填好了申请表。


将来时:
I will fill the bottle with juice. = 我将用果汁装满瓶子。
They will fill the order soon. = 他们很快就会完成订单。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would fill the application completely. = 如果我有更多时间，我会完整地填写申请表。
I wish I could fill this void in my life.  = 我希望我能填补我生命中的这个空虚。


被动语态:
The forms are filled by the applicants. = 表格由申请人填写。
The room is filled with beautiful flowers. = 房间里摆满了美丽的鲜花。


疑问句:
Does this bag fill your needs? = 这个包能满足你的需求吗？
What fills your days? = 你每天都做些什么？


否定句:
The tank doesn't fill completely. = 油箱没有完全加满。
He didn't fill out the application properly. = 他没有正确填写申请表。


PHRASE->
fill in = 填写；补充
fill out = 填写；长胖
fill up = 加满；装满
fill with = 装满…；充满…
be filled with = 充满…
