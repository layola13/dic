expatiatory: /ɪkˈspeɪʃəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 详尽的；解释性的；冗长的 |  Serving to expatiate or explain at length; tending to explain or elaborate.  Characterized by or given to copious explanation. |  例句：He gave an expatiatory lecture on the history of the region. 他就该地区的历史作了一场冗长的演讲。


例句：The professor's expatiatory remarks clarified the complex theory. 教授冗长的解释阐明了复杂的理论。


近义词：explanatory, elucidatory, elaborate, detailed, thorough | 反义词：brief, concise, succinct


词性:形容词
