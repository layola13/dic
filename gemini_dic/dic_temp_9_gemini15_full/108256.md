launchers:/ˈlɔːntʃərz/| n.|发射器；启动器；发起者|A launcher is a device or program used to launch or start something, such as a rocket, missile, application, or game.|例句：The rocket launchers were aimed at the enemy base.  火箭发射器瞄准了敌人的基地。|例句：The game launcher allows you to easily access all your installed games. 游戏启动器允许你轻松访问所有已安装的游戏。|近义词：starter, initiator, trigger|反义词：receiver, terminator
