polypi:/ˈpɒlɪpaɪ/| n. |息肉（复数）|Plural of polyp; small, usually benign growths found on mucous membranes.  They can occur in various parts of the body, such as the colon, nose, or uterus.  Some polyps can be precancerous and require medical attention.|Example: The colonoscopy revealed several polyps, which were removed during the procedure.  结肠镜检查显示有多个息肉，在手术过程中被切除了。

The doctor discovered several polyps in his nasal cavity. 医生在他的鼻腔中发现了几个息肉。|近义词：growth, tumor (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but the absence of polyps could be described as "polyp-free".|名词
