falcon: /ˈfɔːlkən/ /ˈfæl.kən/ | n. | 猎鹰；隼 | A falcon is a bird of prey typically having a long, pointed wing and a short, powerful beak. They are known for their speed and agility in hunting.  | Example: The falcon swooped down and caught a rabbit.  这只猎鹰俯冲下来抓到了一只兔子。 |近义词：hawk, kestrel | 反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑体型较大的猛禽如eagle作为对比) | 名词
