scrouge:/skruːʒ/| v.|吝啬鬼；守财奴；贪婪的人；|to behave in a mean and miserly way, especially in relation to money.|例句：He scrounged around for loose change. 他四处搜寻零钱。|近义词：miser, skinflint, cheapskate|反义词：generous, charitable, munificent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrounges for food every day. = 他每天都在寻找食物。
She scrounges money from her friends. = 她向朋友们要钱。

一般过去时:
He scrounged a cigarette from me. = 他向我要了一根香烟。
They scrounged around for a place to sleep. = 他们四处寻找睡觉的地方。

现在完成时:
He has scrounged enough money for a new car. = 他已经攒够了买新车的钱。
She has scrounged food from neighbours for weeks. = 她已经从邻居那里蹭吃蹭喝了好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
He had scrounged all the money he could before the trip. = 旅行前他已经尽可能地攒钱了。
They had scrounged a meal together before heading home. = 他们一起凑合吃了一顿饭才回家。

将来时:
He will scrounge some money from his parents. = 他将向父母要一些钱。
They will scrounge for information on the internet. = 他们将在互联网上搜集信息。

虚拟语气:
If he scrounged a bit more, he could buy the book. = 如果他再省一点，就能买到那本书了。
I wish I hadn't scrounged that money from him. = 我真希望我没有向他借那笔钱。

被动语态:
Money was scrounged from various sources. = 钱是从各种渠道筹集的。
A meal was scrounged up for the unexpected guests. = 为意外的客人凑合了一顿饭。

疑问句:
Did he scrounge money from you? = 他向你借钱了吗？
Where did they scrounge that old furniture? = 他们从哪里弄到那些旧家具的？

否定句:
He didn't scrounge anything from me. = 他没有向我索要任何东西。
They didn't scrounge a living from the land. = 他们并没有靠土地谋生。


PHRASE->
scrounge around = 搜寻；四处寻找
scrounge up = 凑集；勉强收集
scrounge for = 寻找；搜寻
scrounge off = 依赖；靠...生活
scrounge a living = 勉强糊口
