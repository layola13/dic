custode:/kʌˈstoʊdi/| n.|守护者；保管人；监护人|A custodian is a person who has the care and protection of something or someone.  It often refers to a person responsible for maintaining a building or property.|例句：The museum hired a custodian to protect its valuable artifacts.  博物馆雇佣了一名保管员来保护其珍贵的文物。
例句：The judge appointed a custodian for the orphaned child. 法官为孤儿指定了一名监护人。|近义词：guardian, keeper, protector, caretaker|反义词：neglecter, destroyer

The custodian cleans the school every day. = 保管员每天打扫学校。
