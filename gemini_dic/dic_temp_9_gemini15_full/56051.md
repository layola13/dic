docents:/ˈdoʊsents/| n.|讲解员， docent | A docent is a person who acts as a guide, especially in a museum or art gallery, providing information and explanations to visitors.  | Example: The museum docents were knowledgeable and enthusiastic, making the tour enjoyable.  博物馆的讲解员知识渊博且热情，使参观变得愉快。|近义词：guide, lecturer, interpreter |反义词：visitor, tourist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The docent guides the tour group. = 讲解员带领旅游团。
Our museum employs several docents. = 我们的博物馆雇佣了几位讲解员。


一般过去时:
The docent explained the painting's history. = 讲解员解释了这幅画的历史。
She was a docent at the art gallery last year. = 去年她曾在美术馆担任讲解员。
