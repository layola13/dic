martinetisms:/ˌmɑːrtɪˈnɛtɪzəm/| n. |马丁内特式作风；严厉的纪律；严格的规定|The strict adherence to rules and regulations, often to an excessive degree, characteristic of a martinet.  This term refers to a system or style of rule characterized by strict discipline and adherence to rules, often perceived as excessively rigid or inflexible.|例句：The company's martinetisms stifled creativity and innovation. 公司的严格规章制度扼杀了创造力和创新。|例句：His leadership style, characterized by martinetisms, alienated many employees. 他的领导风格，以严格的规章制度为特征，疏远了许多员工。|近义词：rigor, strictness, severity, authoritarianism|反义词：leniency, laxity, permissiveness|名词
