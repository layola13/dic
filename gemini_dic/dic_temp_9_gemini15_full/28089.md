capilliform:/kæˈpɪlɪfɔːrm/| adj. |毛发状的，毛细管状的 |Resembling a hair or fine thread; hairlike.  The term is often used to describe structures that are slender, threadlike, and often numerous. |Example: The delicate, capilliform roots of the plant spread through the soil.  植物纤细的毛发状根部遍布土壤。The astronomer observed capilliform filaments extending from the nebula. 天文学家观察到星云中延伸出的毛发状细丝。|近义词：hairlike, filamentous, threadlike |反义词：thick, coarse, robust
