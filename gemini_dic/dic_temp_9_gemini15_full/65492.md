europeanizing: /ˌjʊərəpiːənˈaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |使欧洲化；使具有欧洲特色 |The act of making something more European in character or style.  This can refer to cultural practices, political systems, or other aspects of life. |例句：The country is undergoing a rapid process of europeanizing its economy. 这个国家正在快速地使它的经济欧洲化。  The government's policies are aimed at europeanizing the education system. 政府的政策旨在使教育体系欧洲化。|近义词：westernizing, modernizing |反义词：indigenizing, traditionalizing
