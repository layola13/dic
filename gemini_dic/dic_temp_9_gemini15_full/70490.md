filanders:/ˈfɪlændərz/| n.|菲律宾鼯猴|A filander is a small, nocturnal, arboreal mammal belonging to the family Cynocephalidae, commonly known as the Philippine flying lemur or colugo.  They are not true lemurs, nor do they actually fly, but glide between trees using a patagium (a membrane of skin stretching between their limbs).|Example:  The researchers observed the filanders' gliding technique in the rainforest canopy.  研究人员观察了菲律宾鼯猴在雨林树冠层的滑翔技术。|近义词：colugo, flying lemur|反义词：无
