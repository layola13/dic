gerontology:/dʒɛrənˈtɒlədʒi/| n. |老年学，老年病学 |The scientific study of the biological, psychological, and social aspects of aging.  It encompasses the study of the aging process itself, as well as the diseases and problems associated with old age. |例句：She's studying gerontology to better understand the needs of the elderly. 她正在学习老年学，以便更好地了解老年人的需求。

例句：Gerontology research is crucial for developing effective interventions to improve the quality of life for older adults. 老年学研究对于开发有效的干预措施以提高老年人的生活质量至关重要。


近义词：geriatrics (but geriatrics focuses more on the medical aspects)


反义词：None directly;  the opposite would be the study of youth development or pediatrics.


词性:名词
