emulable:/ˈɛmjʊləbəl/ | adj. | 可效仿的；可模仿的；值得效仿的 |  Worthy of being imitated; capable of being imitated.  |  His leadership style is truly emulable. 他的领导风格确实值得效仿。  |  Imitable, commendable, exemplary |  Unremarkable, unexceptional |  adj.
