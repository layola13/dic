ethnocentrism:/ˌeθnoʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/| n. |民族中心主义；种族优越感 |The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own ethnic group or culture and the tendency to view other cultures through the lens of one's own, often leading to prejudice and discrimination.  |例句：His ethnocentrism prevented him from appreciating the beauty of other cultures.  他的民族中心主义使他无法欣赏其他文化的美丽。|例句：The ethnocentrism displayed in the media portrayal of the conflict was shocking. 媒体对冲突的报道中表现出的民族中心主义令人震惊。|近义词：bias, prejudice, parochialism, tribalism |反义词：cosmopolitanism, multiculturalism

词源:
Ethnocentrism is a blend of "ethno" (from Greek ἔθνος, ethnos, meaning "nation" or "people") and "centrism" (referring to a central or focal point).  Therefore, it literally means "placing one's own ethnic group at the center."
