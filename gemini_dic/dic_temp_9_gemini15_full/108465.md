lay down the law:/leɪ daʊn ðə lɔː/| phrasal verb|严厉地规定；强行执行；发号施令|To state rules or instructions firmly and forcefully, often in a way that is considered bossy or dictatorial.  This phrase implies a lack of negotiation or compromise. |例句：The new manager immediately started laying down the law about punctuality. 新经理一上任就开始严厉规定准时到岗。

例句：The teacher laid down the law about cheating on exams. 老师严厉警告考试作弊的后果。


近义词：dictate, enforce, impose, decree


反义词：negotiate, compromise, persuade


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lays down the law to his employees every Monday morning. 他每周一早上都会对员工发号施令。
She lays down the law about cleanliness in the house. 她对家里的清洁卫生规定很严格。


一般过去时:
The principal laid down the law after the school fight. 校长在学校打架事件后严厉处罚。
My father laid down the law about curfews when I was a teenager. 我十几岁的时候，我父亲对宵禁时间规定很严格。


现在完成时:
The boss has laid down the law about using company phones for personal calls. 老板已经严厉禁止使用公司电话打私人电话。
She has laid down the law regarding social media usage at work. 她已经制定了关于在工作中使用社交媒体的严格规定。


过去完成时:
He had laid down the law before the meeting even began. 会议还没开始，他就已经发号施令了。
They had laid down the law concerning safety procedures several times. 他们已经多次强调安全规程。


将来时:
The sergeant will lay down the law to the recruits tomorrow.  中士明天将对新兵发号施令。
She will lay down the law to her children about their behavior at the party. 她将在派对上对孩子们的不良行为严厉管教。


虚拟语气:
If he laid down the law more consistently, things would be better organized. 如果他更坚定地执行规定，事情就会安排得更好。
I wish she hadn't laid down the law so harshly. 我希望她不要那么严厉地发号施令。


被动语态:
The rules were laid down by the headmaster. 校长制定了这些规则。
The new regulations have been laid down by the government. 政府已经制定了新的规章制度。


疑问句:
Does he often lay down the law? 他经常发号施令吗？
Will she lay down the law about the dress code? 她会对服装规范发号施令吗？


否定句:
He didn't lay down the law this time. 这次他没有发号施令。
She doesn't usually lay down the law unnecessarily. 她通常不会无缘无故地发号施令。


PHRASE->
lay down the law to someone: 对某人发号施令
lay down the law about something: 对某事制定严格规定
lay down the law in a certain way: 以某种方式发号施令
lay down the law and order: 建立秩序
lay down the law without compromise: 不妥协地发号施令
