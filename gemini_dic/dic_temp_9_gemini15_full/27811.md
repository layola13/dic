cannons:/ˈkænənz/| n.|大炮，加农炮|Large, powerful guns, typically mounted on a carriage, used in warfare.  They were historically crucial in naval and land battles.|例句：The cannons roared as the ships engaged in battle.  大炮轰鸣，战舰交战。|例句：The army positioned its cannons strategically on the hill.  军队在山上战略性地部署了大炮。|近义词：guns, artillery|反义词：None specifically, but "small arms" could be considered an antonym in terms of scale.
