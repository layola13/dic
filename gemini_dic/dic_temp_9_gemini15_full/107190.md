lagoonal:/ləˈɡuːnəl/ | adj. |泻湖的；与泻湖有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of a lagoon.  A lagoon is a shallow body of water separated from a larger body of water by a narrow strip of land. | The lagoonal ecosystem is rich in biodiversity. 泻湖生态系统生物多样性丰富。|The water in the lagoonal area was calm and clear. 泻湖区域的水平静而清澈。| coastal, lacustrine | terrestrial
