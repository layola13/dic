gags:/ɡæɡz/| n. v.| gags(复数)|滑稽表演；玩笑；塞住；令人作呕的东西；|A gag is a joke or a funny story, often used in a performance.  It can also refer to something used to silence or restrain someone.  As a verb, it means to prevent someone from speaking or to choke someone.|例句：The comedian told a series of hilarious gags.  这位喜剧演员讲了一系列令人捧腹大笑的笑话。|例句：The kidnappers gagged the victim to stop them from screaming. 绑匪用东西堵住受害者的嘴以阻止他们尖叫。|近义词：joke, prank, stunt, gags|反义词：seriousness, truth, silence(as a noun), unmute (as a verb)
