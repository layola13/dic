setback:/ˈsɛtˌbæk/ | n. |挫折；倒退；障碍 | A setback is a delay or problem that prevents something from happening or progressing as planned.  It can refer to a sudden unexpected difficulty, a temporary defeat, or a reversal of progress. |例句：The project suffered a major setback due to unforeseen circumstances.  这个项目由于不可预见的情况而遭受了重大挫折。

The rain was a setback to our plans for a picnic.  下雨使我们的野餐计划受阻。

近义词：reversal, impediment, obstacle, hindrance, reverse, defeat

反义词：advancement, progress, success, triumph
