reprotesting:/ˌriːˈprəʊtɛstɪŋ/| v.|再抗议，再次抗议|The act of protesting again, often against the same issue or decision. It implies a continuation or renewal of previous protests.|例句：Despite the initial rejection, the community continued reprotesting the new highway route.  尽管最初遭到拒绝，但社区继续抗议新的高速公路路线。|近义词：remonstrating, objecting again, protesting anew|反义词：accepting, agreeing, approving
