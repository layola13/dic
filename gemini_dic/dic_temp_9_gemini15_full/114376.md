majuscule: /ˈmædʒəskjuːl/ | n. | 大写字母；大写 | A capital letter; an uppercase letter.  The term often refers to a letter in a larger size than lowercase letters, and is used in typography and other fields dealing with letterforms. | Example: The title of the book was printed in majuscules. 这本书的标题是用大写字母印刷的。| 近义词：capital letter, uppercase letter | 反义词：minuscule, lowercase letter
