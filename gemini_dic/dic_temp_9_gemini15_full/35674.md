cobblestone:/ˈkɒblˌstoʊn/ /ˈkɑːblˌstoʊn/| n.|鹅卵石；铺路石|A cobblestone is a relatively small, rounded stone, typically used to pave roads or paths.  Cobblestones are usually made of durable materials that can withstand heavy traffic and weathering.|The old town square was charming, with its uneven cobblestone streets.|近义词：paving stone, pebble, boulder|反义词：smooth pavement, asphalt
