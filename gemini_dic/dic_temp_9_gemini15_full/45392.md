cupboards: /ˈkʌbərdz/ | n. |碗橱，橱柜 | A cupboard is a type of cabinet or closet, typically used for storing food, dishes, or other household items.  It usually has doors and shelves. | Example: We keep our dishes in the kitchen cupboards. 我们把餐具放在厨房的碗橱里。  The old cupboards were filled with antique china. 旧碗橱里装着古董瓷器。 |近义词：cabinet, closet, pantry |反义词：open shelving
