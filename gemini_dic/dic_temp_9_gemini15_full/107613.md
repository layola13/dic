landward:/ˈlændwərd/| adj.|陆地的；向陆地的；|Moving or directed toward the land.  It describes something that is going in the direction of the land, typically from the sea.|例句：The ship sailed landward. 这艘船向陆地航行。


例句：The wind shifted landward, bringing the scent of pine trees. 风向陆地吹来，带来松树的香味。


近义词：inland, shoreward|反义词：seaward, outward


词性：形容词
