calamitousness:/kəˈlæmɪtəsnəs/ | n. |灾难性；不幸；灾难程度 |The calamitousness of the situation was undeniable.  情况的灾难性是不可否认的。|近义词：disaster, catastrophe, misfortune |反义词：good fortune, blessing, prosperity


英文解析：The noun "calamitousness" refers to the state or quality of being calamitous; characterized by calamity or disaster; involving great misfortune or distress.  It describes the extent or severity of a disastrous situation.


例句：

The calamitousness of the earthquake was evident in the widespread destruction. 地震的灾难性在广泛的破坏中显而易见。

The report highlighted the calamitousness of the environmental crisis. 报告强调了环境危机的灾难性。


词源:

Derived from the word "calamitous," which itself originates from the Latin word "calamitas," meaning "misfortune," "disaster," or "adversity."  The suffix "-ness" is added to create the noun form, indicating a state or quality.
