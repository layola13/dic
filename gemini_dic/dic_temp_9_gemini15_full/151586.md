parthenogenesis:/ˌpɑːrθɛnoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |孤雌生殖；单性生殖|The process by which an organism develops from an unfertilized egg.  This is a type of asexual reproduction. |Example: Parthenogenesis is common in some insect species. 孤雌生殖在某些昆虫物种中很常见。|近义词：asexual reproduction, virgin birth |反义词：sexual reproduction
