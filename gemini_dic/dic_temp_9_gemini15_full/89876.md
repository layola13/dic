horoscope:/ˈhɔːrəskoʊp/ | n. | 星相图，占星术预测 | A horoscope is a prediction of a person's future based on the positions of the stars and planets at the time of their birth.  | Example: I read my horoscope every morning to see what the day holds. 我每天早上都会看我的星座运势，看看这一天会发生什么。 |近义词：astrological prediction, prediction, forecast |反义词：N/A
