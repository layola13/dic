prescriptively:/prɪˈskrɪptɪvli/| adv.|规定性地；规范地|Adverb meaning according to prescription or rules; in a prescriptive manner.  It emphasizes adherence to established rules and standards, often in language or grammar.|例句：He spoke prescriptively about the correct use of the subjunctive mood. 他规范地讲解了虚拟语气的正确用法。
例句：The grammar book takes a prescriptive approach, dictating the “correct” way to write. 这本语法书采取规定性方法，规定了“正确”的写作方式。|近义词：authoritatively, dogmatically, formally|反义词：descriptively, descriptively
