contemptuous: /kənˈtɛmptʃuəs/ | adj. | 轻蔑的；鄙视的；不屑的 | Showing contempt; scornful.  It implies a feeling of superiority and disgust towards someone or something considered unworthy. |  例句：He gave her a contemptuous glance. 他给了她一个轻蔑的一瞥。


例句：Her contemptuous laughter stung him deeply.  她的轻蔑的笑声深深地刺痛了他。


近义词：scornful, disdainful, sneering, supercilious, contemptible


反义词：respectful, admiring, approving, reverent


词性：形容词
