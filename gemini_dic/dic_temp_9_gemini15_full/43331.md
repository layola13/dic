cramped:/kræmpt/| adj.|狭小的，拥挤的，局促的| feeling or appearing to be too small or restricted; confined.  The room was cramped and stuffy.  |The small apartment felt cramped.|近义词：confined, restricted, crowded, stuffy|反义词：spacious, roomy, vast


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The room feels cramped. = 这房间感觉很狭窄。
This apartment is cramped. = 这套公寓很狭窄。

The train was cramped. = 火车很拥挤。
