saxifrage:/ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ/| n. |虎耳草属植物；虎耳草|A saxifrage is any of various flowering plants of the genus Saxifraga, typically found in rocky or mountainous areas.  They are often characterized by their ability to grow in harsh conditions and their attractive flowers.|Example:  Many types of saxifrage thrive in alpine environments.  许多种虎耳草在高山环境中茁壮成长。|近义词：rockfoil (a common name for some saxifrages)|反义词：无|名词
