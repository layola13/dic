rigidifier: /rɪˈdʒɪdɪfaɪər/ | n. | 增强剂；硬化剂；定型剂 | A substance added to a material to increase its stiffness or rigidity.  It strengthens and firms the material, preventing it from bending or flexing easily. |  The manufacturer added a rigidifier to the plastic to make it more durable. 制造商在塑料中添加了增强剂以使其更耐用。 The new fabric treatment includes a rigidifier to maintain its shape. 新的织物处理包括一种定型剂以保持其形状。 | stiffener, hardener, strengthening agent | softener, flexibilizer |
