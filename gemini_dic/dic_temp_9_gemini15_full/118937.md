meteorites:/ˈmiːtiəraɪts/| n. |陨石|Pieces of rock or metal that have fallen to Earth from outer space.  Meteorites are remnants of asteroids, comets, or even planets that have broken apart.|例句：Scientists studied the meteorite to understand its composition and origin. 科学家研究了这块陨石以了解其成分和起源。

例句：The impact crater was formed by a large meteorite millions of years ago.  数百万年前，一个巨大的陨石形成了这个撞击坑。|近义词：meteor, asteroid (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
