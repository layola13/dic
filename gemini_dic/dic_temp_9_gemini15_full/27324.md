calyxes:/ˈkælɪsɪz/| n. |花萼复数|The plural of calyx, referring to the sepals of a flower.  Calyxes protect the developing flower bud and often contribute to the overall flower structure and appearance.|例句：The gardener carefully examined the calyxes of the roses, looking for signs of disease. 园丁仔细检查了玫瑰的花萼，寻找病害迹象。|The calyxes of these wildflowers are unusually vibrant. 这些野花的花萼异常鲜艳。|近义词：sepals (单数sepal)|反义词：无
