circumlocutions:/ˌsɜːrkəmloˈkjuːʃənz/| n. | 迂回说法；婉言；绕圈子说话 |The use of many words where fewer would do, especially in a deliberate attempt to be vague or evasive.  Circumlocutions are often used to avoid being direct or to make something sound more impressive or less offensive. |  He answered the question with a series of circumlocutions, avoiding a direct response. 他用一系列的迂回说法来回答这个问题，避免直接回应。  |periphrasis, evasions, indirectness |directness, frankness, brevity|名词
