gazin:/ˈɡeɪzɪŋ/| v.|凝视，注视|The act of looking intently and steadily at something.  Gazing implies a prolonged and often thoughtful look.|例句：She was gazing out the window at the falling snow. 她凝视着窗外飘落的雪花。
例句：He gazed at the painting, lost in thought. 他凝视着画作，陷入沉思。|近义词：staring, looking, contemplating|反义词：ignoring, overlooking|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gazes at the stars every night. = 他每天晚上都凝视着星星。
They are gazing out at the ocean. = 他们在凝视着大海。


一般过去时:
She gazed at her reflection in the mirror. = 她凝视着镜子里的倒影。

I have been gazing at this painting for an hour. = 我已经凝视着这幅画一个小时了。

过去完成时:
He had gazed at the photo for a long time before he spoke. = 他在说话之前已经凝视了这张照片很久了。
She had gazed out the window before realizing it was raining. = 她凝视窗外很久才意识到在下雨。
