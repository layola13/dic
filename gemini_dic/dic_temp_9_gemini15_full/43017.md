cowboy:/ˈkaʊbɔɪ/| n. |牛仔，牧童|A cowboy is a man who works on a ranch, typically herding cattle.  Cowboys are often associated with the American West and a particular lifestyle and culture.  They are skilled horsemen and are known for their independence and resilience.|例句：He dreamed of becoming a cowboy and riding the range. 他梦想成为一名牛仔，驰骋草原。|例句：The cowboy skillfully lassoed the runaway calf. 牛仔熟练地套住了逃跑的小牛。|近义词：rancher, cowhand, cattleman |反义词：city dweller, urbanite
