plait:/pleɪt/| vt. n.|编织；辫子；褶子|To braid or intertwine (strands of hair, etc.); a braid or fold.|She plaited her daughter's hair before school.  她上学前给女儿编了辫子。|He admired the intricate plaits in the rug. 他欣赏地毯上复杂的褶皱。|braid, weave, interweave|unplait, unravel
