nonrevolution: /ˌnɒnˌrevəˈluːʃən/ | n. |非革命；非革命性变革 |The absence of a revolution; a period or situation characterized by a lack of revolutionary change.  It refers to a state of affairs where significant societal, political, or technological upheaval is absent.  The term highlights the absence of radical transformation, often contrasted with periods of dramatic change. |Example: The country experienced a period of nonrevolution, with gradual reforms rather than sudden overthrows.  这个国家经历了一段非革命时期，采取的是渐进式改革而不是突然的推翻。|近义词：stability, gradualism, evolution, incrementalism |反义词：revolution, upheaval, radical change
