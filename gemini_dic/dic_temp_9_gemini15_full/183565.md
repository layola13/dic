requestioning: /ˌriːˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ/ | v. | 再次询问；反复提问 |  The act of asking again or repeatedly questioning something.  It implies a deeper investigation or a need for clarification beyond the initial response. | 例句：The police are requestioning the witnesses to gather more information.  警方正在再次询问证人以收集更多信息。


例句：The judge requestioned the defendant about his alibi.  法官再次询问了被告的不在场证明。


近义词：re-interrogating, re-examining, questioning again, cross-examining


反义词：accepting without question, believing implicitly
