disagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/| v.|不同意，不一致|To have or express a different opinion from someone; to be in conflict or opposition.|例句：I disagree with your assessment of the situation. 我不同意你对局势的评估。|例句：Their findings disagree with ours. 他们的发现与我们的不一致。|近义词：differ, dissent, object|反义词：agree, concur, assent|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I disagree with his political views. = 我不同意他的政治观点。
They disagree on the best course of action. = 他们对最佳行动方案意见不一。

一般过去时:
She disagreed with the judge's decision. = 她不同意法官的判决。
We disagreed about the price of the car. = 我们对汽车的价格意见不一。

现在完成时:
He has disagreed with his boss on several occasions. = 他曾多次与老板意见相左。
They have disagreed about this issue for years. = 他们就这个问题争论多年了。

过去完成时:
I had disagreed with him before, but I tried to understand his perspective. = 我以前就和他意见不合，但我试图理解他的观点。
They had disagreed on the plan, which led to its failure. = 他们对计划意见不一，导致计划失败。

将来时:
I think we will disagree on this matter. = 我认为在这个问题上我们会意见不合。
They will disagree if you suggest that change. = 如果你建议那样改变，他们会不同意的。

虚拟语气:
If I disagreed, I would have spoken up. = 如果我不同意，我会说出来的。
I wish they didn't disagree so much. = 我希望他们不要那么意见不合。

被动语态:
His ideas were disagreed with by many people. = 许多人不赞同他的想法。
The proposal was disagreed with by the committee. = 委员会不同意该提案。

疑问句:
Do you disagree with me? = 你不同意我的观点吗？
Does she disagree with the terms of the contract? = 她不同意合同条款吗？

否定句:
I don't disagree with you entirely. = 我并不完全不同意你。
They didn't disagree with the proposal. = 他们没有不同意该提案。


PHRASE->
disagree with someone = 与某人意见不合
disagree on something = 在某事上意见不合
strongly disagree = 强烈反对
fundamentally disagree = 从根本上不同意
disagree vehemently = 激烈反对
