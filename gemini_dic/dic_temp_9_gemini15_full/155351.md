pharmacologists:/ˌfɑːrməkˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |药理学家|Scientists who study the effects of drugs on living organisms. They research, develop, and test medications, investigating how drugs interact with the body and its systems.  |Pharmacologists are crucial in the development of new medicines. |近义词：pharmacists, medicinal chemists |反义词：无
