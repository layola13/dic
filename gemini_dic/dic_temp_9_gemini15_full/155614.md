philosophize:/fɪˈlɒsəfaɪz/ | v. | 哲学思考；空谈；高谈阔论 | To engage in philosophical speculation or discussion; to reason or think deeply about fundamental questions of existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language.  | Example:  He likes to philosophize about the meaning of life. 他喜欢对人生的意义进行哲学思考。

Example: She spent hours philosophizing about the nature of reality. 她花了几个小时思考现实的本质。


近义词：speculate, contemplate, ponder, theorize | 反义词：act, do
