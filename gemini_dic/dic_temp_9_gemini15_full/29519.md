catastrophic:/ˌkætəˈstrɒfɪk/ | adj. |灾难性的，毁灭性的 |extremely bad or harmful; causing suffering or disaster.  |The earthquake had catastrophic consequences for the region. 地震给该地区带来了灾难性的后果。  |devastating, disastrous, calamitous |beneficial, positive |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is catastrophic. = 情况很糟糕。
This policy has catastrophic effects. = 这项政策有灾难性的影响。


一般过去时:
The fire was catastrophic. = 那场火灾是灾难性的。
The consequences of his actions were catastrophic. = 他行为的后果是灾难性的。


现在完成时:
The failure has been catastrophic for the company. = 这次失败对公司来说是灾难性的。
The effects of the storm have been catastrophic. = 风暴的影响是灾难性的。


过去完成时:
The damage had been catastrophic before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，损害已经非常严重。
The situation had become catastrophic by the time help arrived. = 等到救援到来时，情况已经变得非常糟糕。


将来时:
The consequences will be catastrophic if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，后果将是灾难性的。
The failure of the dam could be catastrophic. = 水坝的垮塌可能造成灾难性的后果。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, the consequences could be catastrophic. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，后果可能是灾难性的。
It would be catastrophic if the volcano erupted. = 如果火山爆发，那将是灾难性的。


被动语态:
The city was left in a catastrophic state after the bombing. = 轰炸过后，这座城市处于一片灾难性的状态。
The environment has been catastrophically damaged by pollution. = 环境已经被污染严重破坏。


疑问句:
Could the consequences be catastrophic? = 后果会很严重吗？
Is this a catastrophic failure? = 这是灾难性的失败吗？


否定句:
The situation is not catastrophic yet. = 目前情况还不算灾难性。
The results were not catastrophic, but far from ideal. = 结果并非灾难性，但也远非理想。
