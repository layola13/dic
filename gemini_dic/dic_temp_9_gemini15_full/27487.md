campanulaceous:/ˌkæmpənjuˈleɪʃəs/| adj. |铃状的，钟状的|Having the shape of a bell;  referring to plants with bell-shaped flowers.  The term describes the shape of a flower, particularly the corolla, resembling a bell or cup.|The bluebells in the woods were campanulaceous, their delicate blooms swaying gently in the breeze.  铃兰花在树林里是钟状的，它们娇嫩的花朵在微风中轻轻摇曳。|bell-shaped, campanulate|tubular, cylindrical|形容词
