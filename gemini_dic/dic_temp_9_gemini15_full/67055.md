explicates:/ˈɛkspləkeɪts/| vt.|解释，阐明|To make (something) clear or easy to understand; explain.  |例句：The professor explicated the complex theory in a simple way. 教授以简单的方式解释了复杂的理论。
例句：He explicated his reasons for resigning. 他阐述了他辞职的原因。|近义词：explain, clarify, elucidate, interpret |反义词：obscure, confuse, mystify |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He explicates the poem's meaning. = 他解释了这首诗的意思。
She explicates her research findings clearly. = 她清晰地解释了她的研究结果。


一般过去时:
The teacher explicated the grammar rule. = 老师解释了语法规则。
He explicated the historical context of the event. = 他解释了事件的历史背景。


现在完成时:
She has explicated the difficult passage several times. = 她已经多次解释了那段难懂的章节。
He has explicated his position on the issue. = 他已经阐明了他对这个问题的立场。


过去完成时:
They had explicated the plan before the meeting. = 他们会议前已经解释了计划。
She had explicated the concept thoroughly in her previous lecture. = 在她之前的讲座中，她已经彻底解释了这个概念。


将来时:
He will explicate the data in his presentation. = 他将在他的演示文稿中解释这些数据。
She will explicate the new policy to the employees. = 她将向员工解释新政策。


虚拟语气:
If he explicated his ideas more clearly, people would understand him better. = 如果他更清晰地解释他的想法，人们会更好地理解他。
I wish he had explicated the instructions more carefully. = 我希望他更仔细地解释说明。


被动语态:
The theory was explicated in detail in the textbook. = 这本书详细解释了这个理论。
The complex problem was explicated by the expert. = 专家解释了这个复杂的问题。


疑问句:
How will you explicate this concept to the students? = 你将如何向学生解释这个概念？
Has the author explicated the symbolism in the novel? = 作者是否解释了小说中的象征意义？


否定句:
He did not explicate his reasoning adequately. = 他没有充分解释他的推理。
The speaker did not explicate the key points clearly enough. = 演讲者没有清晰地解释关键点。


PHRASE->
explicate the meaning of = 解释……的意思
explicate the reasons for = 解释……的原因
explicate in detail = 详细解释
explicate the significance of = 解释……的重要性
explicate the implications of = 解释……的含义


词源:
From Latin *explicare, from ex- + plicare "to fold," from PIE root *plek- "to weave, plait."  The sense evolution is from "unfold" to "make clear."
