slopping:/ˈslɒpɪŋ/| adj.| 邋遢的；污秽的；倾斜的；漫不经心的 |  sloping, messy, untidy, carelessly done.  Describes something that is messy, untidy, or carelessly done; also describes something that is tilted or leaning. |例句：The slopping paint on the canvas made the artwork look unfinished. 画布上凌乱的颜料使这幅作品看起来未完成。

The children’s room was in a slopping state after they played with their toys.孩子们玩完玩具后，他们的房间一片狼藉。


近义词：messy, untidy, sloppy, careless, tilted, inclined |反义词：neat, tidy, orderly, organized, upright, vertical |词性:adj.
