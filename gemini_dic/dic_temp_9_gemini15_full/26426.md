byname:/baɪˈneɪm/| adj.|按名称；名义上的|Used to describe something that is named or identified by a specific name.|The company is known byname for its innovative products. 这家公司以其创新产品而闻名。|近义词：nominally, nominally|反义词：anonymously, without a name
