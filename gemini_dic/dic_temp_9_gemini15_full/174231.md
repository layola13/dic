queried:/ˈkwɪəri/ | v. | 询问；质询；对…提出疑问 | To ask a question about something; to express doubt or uncertainty about something. | 例句：The police queried his alibi.  警察对他不在场的理由提出了质疑。

例句：I queried the accuracy of the figures. 我对这些数字的准确性提出了疑问。


近义词：questioned, inquired, asked, investigated | 反义词：answered, confirmed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He queries the information daily. 他每天都询问信息。
They query the results of the experiment. 他们对实验结果提出疑问。

一般过去时:
She queried the statement made by the witness. 她对证人做出的陈述提出了质疑。
The journalist queried the government's policy. 这名记者对政府的政策提出了质疑。

现在完成时:
He has queried the bill several times. 他已经多次对账单提出疑问。
They have queried the decision-making process. 他们已经对决策过程提出了质疑。

过去完成时:
She had queried the plan before it was implemented. 在计划实施之前，她已经对计划提出了质疑。
They had queried the data's reliability before drawing conclusions. 在得出结论之前，他们已经对数据的可靠性提出了质疑。

将来时:
He will query the results when they are released. 结果公布时，他会提出疑问。
They will query the new regulations soon. 他们很快就会对新规提出疑问。

虚拟语气:
If I queried him, he might tell me the truth. 如果我质问他，他可能会告诉我真相。
I wish I had queried the information earlier. 我希望我早点就提出疑问。

被动语态:
His alibi was queried by the police. 他的不在场证明受到了警方的质疑。
The proposed changes were queried by several members of the committee.  提议的变更受到了委员会中几位成员的质疑。

疑问句:
Did you query the unexpected results? 你对这些意外的结果提出疑问了吗？
Has anyone queried the data's integrity? 有人质疑过数据的完整性吗？

否定句:
He did not query the instructions. 他没有对指示提出疑问。
They did not query the findings of the report. 他们没有对报告的发现提出疑问。


PHRASE->
query about = 询问关于…
query into = 调查…
query with = 向…询问
raise a query = 提出疑问
frequently queried = 经常被询问的
