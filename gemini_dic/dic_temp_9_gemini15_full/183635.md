/ˌriːrɪˈkɔːrdɪd/ | adj. | 重新录制的；重新记录的 |  Having been recorded again.  This usually implies a previous recording existed and has been replaced or supplemented. | Example: The singer rerecorded the song to improve the sound quality. 歌手重新录制了这首歌以提高音质。  The band released a rerecorded version of their hit album.乐队发行了一张他们热门专辑的重新录制版本。|近义词：re-recorded, re-mastered | 反义词：original, first recording |  形容词
