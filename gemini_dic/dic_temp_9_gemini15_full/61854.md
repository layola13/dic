employment: /ɪmˈplɔɪmənt/ | n. |就业；雇佣；工作 | The state of having paid work.  The act of employing someone. |例句：He is seeking employment. 他正在找工作。

例句：The company offers excellent employment opportunities. 这家公司提供极好的就业机会。


近义词：occupation, job, work, hiring | 反义词：unemployment


词源：源自古法语 emploiement，由employer（雇佣）派生而来，employer本身则源自古法语 emploier，意为“使用；运用”。  最终追溯到拉丁语 implere，意为“填满；完成”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seeks employment. = 他正在找工作。
She has a good employment record. = 她有良好的工作记录。


一般过去时:
He found employment quickly. = 他很快找到了工作。
They lost their employment during the recession. = 他们在经济衰退期间失去了工作。


现在完成时:
She has had several employments since graduating. = 她毕业后做过几份工作。
He has been unemployed since losing his employment last year. = 自从去年失去工作以来，他一直失业。


过去完成时:
He had already secured employment before the interview. = 他在面试前已经找到了工作。
She had never held such a demanding employment before. = 她以前从未从事过如此要求高的工作。


将来时:
He will look for employment after graduation. = 他毕业后会找工作。
She will have a new employment next month. = 她下个月将会有新的工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had more skills, I would have better employment opportunities. = 如果我有更多技能，我将会有更好的就业机会。
I wish I had employment now. = 我希望我现在有工作。


被动语态:
Many people are employed in this industry. = 许多人在这行业就业。
He was employed by a large corporation. = 他受雇于一家大公司。（注意：被动语态更常用在“被雇佣”的含义上）


疑问句:
Do you have employment? = 你有工作吗？
What kind of employment are you looking for? = 你在寻找什么样的工作？


否定句:
He does not have employment. = 他没有工作。
She was not offered employment at the company. = 公司没有录用她。


PHRASE->
full employment =充分就业
gainful employment =有偿就业
employment opportunities =就业机会
employment law =劳动法
seeking employment = 寻找工作
