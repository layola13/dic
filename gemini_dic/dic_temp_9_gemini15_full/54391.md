disillusions: /ˌdɪsɪˈluʒənz/ | n. |幻想破灭；醒悟；失望 |The process of disillusioning someone; the state of being disillusioned.  The loss of illusions or ideals. |例句：His disillusionment with politics was complete after the scandal.  政治丑闻后，他对政治彻底失望了。

例句：The harsh realities of war brought about a profound disillusionment among the soldiers.  战争的残酷现实给士兵们带来了深刻的幻灭感。


近义词：disenchantment, disappointment, disenchantment


反义词：enlightenment, hope, optimism


词性：名词
