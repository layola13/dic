canal: /kəˈnæl/ | n. |运河；水道；管道 | An artificial waterway for navigation or irrigation.  | The Panama Canal connects the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. 巴拿马运河连接着大西洋和太平洋。  The irrigation canal brought much-needed water to the parched fields. 灌溉渠为干旱的田地带来了急需的水分。 | channel, waterway, ditch | (无明显反义词，可考虑land, road等作为对比) | 名词

The canal carries a lot of traffic. = 运河运载大量的交通工具。

将来时:
