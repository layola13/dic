groveled:/ˈɡrɑːvəl/| v.| 卑躬屈膝；匍匐；奴颜婢膝 | To crawl or creep on the ground; to behave in a servile manner; to act in a way that shows excessive humility. | 例句：He groveled before his boss, begging for forgiveness. 他在老板面前卑躬屈膝，乞求原谅。

He groveled at her feet, pleading for mercy. 他在她脚下匍匐，恳求怜悯。


近义词： cower, fawn, prostrate, abase, cringe | 反义词：stand tall, assert oneself, defy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He grovels to his superiors. = 他对上司卑躬屈膝。
She grovels for attention. = 她为了得到关注而卑躬屈膝。


一般过去时:
He groveled before the king. = 他在国王面前卑躬屈膝。
She groveled to get the job. = 她为了得到这份工作而卑躬屈膝。


现在完成时:
He has groveled to everyone he's ever met. = 他对他遇到的每一个人都卑躬屈膝。
She has never groveled in her life. = 她一生从未卑躬屈膝过。


过去完成时:
He had groveled before realizing his mistake. = 他在意识到自己的错误之前就已经卑躬屈膝了。
She had groveled for years before finally standing up for herself. = 她卑躬屈膝多年后才终于为自己挺身而出。


将来时:
He will grovel if he's caught. = 如果他被抓到，他会卑躬屈膝的。
She will not grovel, no matter what happens. = 无论发生什么，她都不会卑躬屈膝。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would not grovel. = 如果我是他，我不会卑躬屈膝。
I wish he wouldn't grovel so much. = 我希望他不必如此卑躬屈膝。


被动语态:  (Grovel is rarely used passively)
His apologies were considered groveling. = 他的道歉被认为是卑躬屈膝的。
His actions were interpreted as groveling. = 他的行为被解释为卑躬屈膝。


疑问句:
Does he often grovel to people? = 他经常对人卑躬屈膝吗？

否定句:
He didn't grovel; he stood his ground. = 他没有卑躬屈膝；他坚持自己的立场。
She refused to grovel even when threatened. = 即使受到威胁，她也拒绝卑躬屈膝。
