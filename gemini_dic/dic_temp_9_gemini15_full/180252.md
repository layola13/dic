refragate:/ˈrɛfræɡeɪt/| vt.| 折射；歪曲；曲解 |To refract (light, etc.) means to bend or change the direction of its path as it passes from one medium to another of different density.  It can also mean to distort or misrepresent something. |例句：The prism refracted the white light into a spectrum of colors.  棱镜将白光折射成各种颜色的光谱。
The media often refracts the truth to fit their agenda. 媒体经常歪曲事实以符合他们的议程。|近义词：bend, deflect, distort, misrepresent |反义词：reflect (in the sense of direct reflection), accurately represent
