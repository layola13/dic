paleophytology:/ˌpeɪliːoʊfaɪˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |古植物学 |The study of fossil plants.  It involves the identification, classification, and interpretation of plant remains from the geological past, providing insights into the evolution of plant life and past environments. |The paleophytological evidence suggests a significant change in flora during the Jurassic period. 古植物学证据表明侏罗纪时期植物区系发生了重大变化。|paleobotany|zoology|
