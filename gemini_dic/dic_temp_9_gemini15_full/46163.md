cynicism:/ˈsɪnɪsɪzəm/| n. |犬儒主义；愤世嫉俗；怀疑；冷嘲热讽|The belief that people are motivated purely by self-interest; distrust of human sincerity or integrity.|His cynicism about politics was evident in his sarcastic remarks. 他对政治的愤世嫉俗在他的讽刺言论中显而易见。| skepticism, distrust, pessimism |optimism, idealism, naivete|名词
