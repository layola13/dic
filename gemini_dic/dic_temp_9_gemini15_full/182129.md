remitment: /rɪˈmɪtmənt/ | n. | 汇款；汇付；委托；任务；职责 | The act of remitting something, typically money;  the state of being remitted; a sum of money remitted.  It can also refer to a commitment or obligation. |


例句：

The company's remitment of funds was delayed due to a banking error. = 由于银行错误，公司的资金汇款被延误了。

His remitment to the project was to ensure its timely completion. = 他对该项目的职责是确保其按时完成。


近义词：remittance, transmission, payment, obligation, commitment

反义词：retention, withholding


词性：名词


USAGE->

The company remitments funds regularly. = 公司定期汇款。
