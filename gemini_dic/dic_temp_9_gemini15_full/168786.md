prodrome:/ˈproʊdroʊm/| n. |先兆，前驱症状 |An early symptom or sign of a disease, indicating the onset of a more serious condition.  It is a symptom that appears before the characteristic symptoms of a disease. |Example:  Fatigue and headache can be prodromes of the flu.  疲劳和头痛可能是流感的先兆。|近义词：precursor, harbinger, forerunner |反义词：aftermath, consequence
