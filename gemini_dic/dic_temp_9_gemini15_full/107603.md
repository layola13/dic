landslips:/ˈlændslɪps/| n.|滑坡，山崩|The sudden movement of a mass of earth or rock down a slope.  Landslips are often caused by heavy rainfall or earthquakes.|例句：The heavy rain caused several landslips in the mountainous region.  大雨导致山区发生了几起滑坡。

例句：The landslip blocked the road, preventing vehicles from passing.  滑坡阻塞了道路，阻止车辆通行。


近义词：landslide, mudslide, rockslide|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

词源:
The word "landslip" is a compound word.  "Land" refers to the ground or earth, and "slip" refers to a sliding or gliding movement.  Therefore, "landslip" literally means a sliding of the land.
