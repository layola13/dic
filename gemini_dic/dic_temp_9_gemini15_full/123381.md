monomial:/ˈmɑːnoʊmiəl/| n. |单项式|A monomial is a polynomial with only one term.  It can be a number, a variable, or a product of a number and variables with non-negative integer exponents.  For example, 3x², 5, and -2xy are all monomials.|Example: The expression 7x³ is a monomial.  The expression 7x³ + 2x is not a monomial, it's a binomial. |近义词：term (in the context of polynomials)|反义词：polynomial (if considering polynomials with more than one term)
