nonmatrimonial:/ˌnɒnˌmætrɪˈmoʊniəl/| adj. |非婚的；婚外情的；非婚姻的 |Not relating to marriage; outside of marriage.  This adjective describes something that is not connected to or doesn't involve marriage. It often implies relationships or activities that take place outside the bounds of marriage. |例句：The couple had a nonmatrimonial relationship for many years before finally getting married. 这对夫妇在最终结婚之前多年保持着非婚关系。

The inheritance was subject to a nonmatrimonial agreement. 遗产受制于一项非婚姻协议。

近义词：extramarital, unmarried, unwed

反义词：marital, matrimonial
