mockest:/ˈmɒkɪst/| v.| 嘲笑；模仿；蔑视 |The archaic second-person singular present tense form of "mock."  It means to make fun of someone or something, often in a contemptuous or derisive way; to imitate in a derisive or contemptuous way; to treat with contempt. |例句：He mockest the poor man's plight. 他嘲笑穷人的困境。|近义词：deride, ridicule, scorn, jeer, taunt|反义词：praise, admire, respect
