originating:/ˈɒrɪdʒɪneɪtɪŋ/| /ˈɔːrɪdʒɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 起源的；发源的 |  Describing something that is the source or beginning of something else. It's typically used as a present participle, functioning as an adjective. |The originating country of this product is China. 这个产品的原产国是中国。
The originating cause of the fire remains unknown.  火灾的起因仍然不明。| originating from, stemming from, derived from |  terminating, ending |  形容词
