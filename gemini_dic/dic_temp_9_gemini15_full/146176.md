overexaggerating:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度夸张；言过其实 |  The act of exaggerating to an excessive degree;  making something seem more important, impressive, or significant than it really is.  It implies a deliberate and often noticeable inflation of the truth. | 例句：He was overexaggerating the difficulties he faced at work. 他夸大了他在工作中遇到的困难。  She was overexaggerating the size of the fish she caught. 她夸大了她钓到的鱼的大小。| 近义词：hyperbolizing, overstating, inflating, embellishing | 反义词：understating, downplaying, minimizing | 动词
