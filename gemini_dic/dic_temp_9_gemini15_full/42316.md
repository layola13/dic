counter-proliferation:/ˌkaʊntər prəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 反扩散 | The activities undertaken to prevent the spread of weapons of mass destruction (WMD) and their means of delivery.  It involves a range of measures, including diplomatic efforts, sanctions, and military actions. | Example: The international community is increasingly focused on counter-proliferation efforts to prevent nuclear weapons from falling into the wrong hands. 国际社会越来越关注反扩散工作，以防止核武器落入坏人之手。| 近义词：non-proliferation, arms control | 反义词：proliferation | 名词
