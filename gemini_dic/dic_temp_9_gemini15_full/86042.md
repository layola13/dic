healthiness:/ˈhelθɪnəs/| n. |健康状况，健康状态|The state of being healthy; the possession of good health.  It often implies a vigorous and robust state of well-being, going beyond the mere absence of disease.|例句：Regular exercise contributes to good healthiness.  规律的运动有助于良好的健康状态。

例句：The healthiness of the children is a priority for the school.  孩子们的健康是学校的首要任务。


近义词：well-being, fitness, robustness, vigor|反义词：illness, sickness, unhealthiness|词性：名词
