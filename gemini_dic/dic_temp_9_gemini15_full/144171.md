outshown: /ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/ | v. |胜过，超过，显出…的优越性|To surpass or exceed in excellence, importance, or influence; to make something appear superior by comparison. |例句：Her talent outshown all the others in the competition.  她的才能在比赛中胜过其他人。 |近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, overshadow|反义词：be outdone, be surpassed
