sampans:/ˈsæmpæn/| n.|舢板，小船|A sampan is a small, often flat-bottomed boat, traditionally used in Asia, especially in China, for fishing or transportation.  They are usually propelled by paddles or sails.|例句：The fishermen used sampans to navigate the narrow canals.  渔民们用舢板航行于狭窄的运河。

The children enjoyed riding in the colorful sampans. 孩子们喜欢乘坐色彩鲜艳的舢板。|近义词：boat, dinghy, skiff|反义词：large ship, ocean liner|名词
