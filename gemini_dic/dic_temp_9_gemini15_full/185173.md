retooling: /ˌriːˈtuːlɪŋ/ | n. | 重新装备；改造；改组 | The process of modifying or updating equipment, machinery, or systems to improve efficiency, productivity, or functionality.  It often involves replacing outdated or inefficient components or processes with newer, more advanced ones. | 例句：The factory underwent extensive retooling to increase production. 工厂进行了大规模的改造以提高产量。

The company's retooling initiative involved retraining its workforce.  公司的改造计划包括对员工进行再培训。


近义词：revamping, refurbishment, modernization, upgrading | 反义词：decommissioning, dismantling, scrapping | 词性：名词
