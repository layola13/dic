/ɪnˈfɪnɪtəm/| adv.|无限地；无止境地|To an unlimited extent; endlessly.|The list of possibilities goes on ad infinitum.  可能性清单无穷无尽。| endlessly, eternally, perpetually|finite, limited|副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and thus doesn't have tense variations like verbs.  It's used to describe actions or processes that continue without end.  Its usage is primarily in a descriptive capacity within a sentence.


例句1: The process repeats ad infinitum.  这个过程无限重复。
例句2: They argued ad infinitum about the details. 他们没完没了地争论细节。


PHRASE->
ad infinitum is the primary and only common phrase associated with this word.  It means "to infinity" or "without limit". There are no other common phrases using this term.
