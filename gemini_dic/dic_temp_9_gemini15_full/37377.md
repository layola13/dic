commentariat:/ˌkɒmənˈteəriæt/| n. |评论界，评论人士群体 | The group of people who write or broadcast comments on current affairs.  They often influence public opinion. |例句：The commentariat is largely divided on the issue of immigration. 评论界在移民问题上存在很大分歧。 |例句：The political commentariat has been quick to criticize the government's new policy. 政治评论界迅速批评了政府的新政策。|近义词：press, media, pundits, analysts |反义词：N/A
