proved:/pruːvd/| v.|证明；证实；结果是|To show something to be true by providing evidence.|The experiment proved his theory correct.  实验证明了他的理论是正确的。|The results proved disappointing. 结果令人失望。|disproved, refuted|unproven


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He proves his point with evidence. 他用证据证明了他的观点。
The data proves the hypothesis. 数据证明了这个假设。


一般过去时:
They proved their innocence. 他们证明了自己的清白。
The test proved positive. 测试结果呈阳性。


现在完成时:
Scientists have proved the existence of black holes. 科学家已经证明了黑洞的存在。
She has proved her ability to lead. 她已经证明了自己的领导能力。


过去完成时:
They had proved their loyalty before the trial. 他们在审判前已经证明了自己的忠诚。
It had already been proved that the theory was flawed. 该理论的缺陷已经被证明了。


将来时:
This new research will prove the link between diet and disease. 这项新的研究将证明饮食与疾病之间的联系。
Future tests will prove the efficacy of the drug. 未来的测试将证明这种药物的疗效。


虚拟语气:
If he proved his innocence, he would be freed. 如果他证明了自己的清白，他就会被释放。
If only I had proved my point earlier! 要是我早点证明我的观点就好了！


被动语态:
His innocence was proved in court. 他的清白在法庭上得到了证明。
The theory has been proved wrong. 这个理论已被证明是错误的。


疑问句:
Can you prove your claim? 你能证明你的说法吗？
How can we prove this hypothesis? 我们如何证明这个假设？


否定句:
They could not prove their alibi. 他们无法证明自己的不在场证明。
The defense failed to prove his guilt.  辩方未能证明他的罪行。


PHRASE->
