siesta:/siˈɛstə/| n.|午睡；午休|A siesta is a midday rest or nap, especially one taken in the hottest part of the day.|例句：I took a siesta after lunch to beat the afternoon heat. 我午饭后睡了个午觉来躲避下午的酷暑。
例句：The heat made a siesta almost unavoidable. 炎热的天气使得午睡几乎不可避免。|近义词：nap, rest, doze|反义词：insomnia, wakefulness|名词
