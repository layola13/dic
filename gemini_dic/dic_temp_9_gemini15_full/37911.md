complementation:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmeɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmeɪʃən/| n. |互补；补充；完成；补语|The act of completing or making perfect; the state of being complete or perfect;  a thing that completes or makes perfect something else;  in linguistics, a syntactic constituent that complements a verb, adjective, or preposition.|例句：The two theories offer a valuable complementation to each other. 这两种理论彼此提供了宝贵的补充。

例句：The successful completion of the project is a great complementation to his career.  项目的成功完成是对其职业生涯的极大补充。


近义词：supplement, addition, completion, fulfillment|反义词：deficiency, incompleteness, lack
