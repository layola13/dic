flaking:/ˈfleɪkɪŋ/| v. n.|剥落；片状物；|The process of something coming off in flakes or small pieces.  It can refer to paint, skin, or other materials. |例句：The paint on the wall is flaking. 墙上的油漆正在剥落。

The old wooden table showed significant flaking.  那张旧木桌严重剥落。

近义词：peeling, scaling, shedding|反义词：adhering, sticking
