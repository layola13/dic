father:/ˈfɑːðər/ /ˈfɑːðə(r)/| n.|父亲，爸爸|A father is a male parent.  The word can also refer to a male ancestor or a founder or originator of something.|例句：My father is a doctor.我的父亲是一位医生。

例句：He is a father to three children. 他是三个孩子的父亲。


近义词：dad, papa, sire, progenitor|反义词：mother, daughter, son


词源:  The word "father" has Proto-Germanic roots, tracing back to *fadar,  which itself likely derives from a Proto-Indo-European root *ph₂tḗr, meaning "father."  Cognates exist in many other Indo-European languages, reflecting its ancient origins.
