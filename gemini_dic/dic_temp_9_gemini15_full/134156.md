nonimpulsive:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpʌlsɪv/|/ˌnɑːnɪmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj. |非冲动的；沉着的；深思熟虑的 |Not acting or done impulsively; deliberate and thoughtful.  |He showed a nonimpulsive approach to the problem, carefully considering all options before making a decision. 他以一种非冲动的态度对待这个问题，在做决定之前仔细考虑了所有选择。|deliberate, thoughtful, premeditated |impulsive, rash, impetuous|形容词
