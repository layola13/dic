heftier:/ˈhɛftiər/| adj.|更重的；更大的；更强壮的|Larger, heavier, or more powerful.  The heftier package was harder to carry.  |例句：The heftier package was harder to carry.  那个更重的包裹更难搬动。
The heftier dog easily overpowered the smaller one. 那只更大的狗轻易地制服了较小的那一只。|近义词：larger, heavier, bigger, stronger, more substantial|反义词：lighter, smaller, weaker, less substantial|词性：形容词
