rectifier:/ˈrektɪfaɪər/| n. |整流器|A rectifier is an electrical device that converts alternating current (AC), which periodically reverses direction, into direct current (DC), which flows in only one direction.  Rectifiers are used in a wide variety of electronic devices, from power supplies to battery chargers.|Example: The power supply uses a rectifier to convert the AC mains voltage to the DC voltage required by the circuit.  这个电源使用整流器将交流电源电压转换为电路所需的直流电压。|近义词：converter, changer|反义词：inverter|名词
