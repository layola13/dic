single-handedly: /ˈsɪŋɡəlˌhændɪdli/ | adv. | 单独地；独自地 |  Alone; unaided; without assistance.  |例句：She single-handedly saved the company from bankruptcy. 她独自一人将公司从破产边缘拯救回来。 |近义词：alone, unaided, independently, solo | 反义词：collectively, jointly, collaboratively | 词性：副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He single-handedly manages the entire project. = 他独自一人管理整个项目。
She single-handedly runs the family business. = 她独自经营家族企业。


一般过去时:
He single-handedly won the chess tournament. = 他独自一人赢得了国际象棋锦标赛。
She single-handedly completed the challenging task. = 她独自完成了这项具有挑战性的任务。


现在完成时:
He has single-handedly built a successful empire. = 他独自一人建立了一个成功的商业帝国。
She has single-handedly raised three children. = 她独自一人抚养了三个孩子。


过去完成时:
He had single-handedly finished the report before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前独自完成了报告。
She had single-handedly prepared the entire meal. = 她独自一人准备了整顿饭菜。


将来时:
He will single-handedly lead the expedition to the Amazon. = 他将独自领导亚马逊探险队。
She will single-handedly organize the conference. = 她将独自组织这次会议。


虚拟语气:
If he were single-handedly responsible, he would apologize. = 如果他独自负责，他会道歉。
If she had been single-handedly in charge, things would have been different. = 如果她独自负责，情况就会有所不同。


被动语态:  (The adverb "single-handedly" is rarely used in the passive voice. The action it describes is inherently active.)  It's not grammatically incorrect, but sounds unnatural.  Here are examples showing the active voice as the natural expression:

The project was managed single-handedly by him.  = 他独自一人管理了这个项目。(Better phrasing would be the active voice example above)
The company was saved single-handedly by her. = 她独自一人拯救了公司。(Better phrasing would be the active voice example above)



疑问句:
Did he single-handedly accomplish this feat? = 他是独自一人完成这一壮举的吗？
Could she single-handedly handle such a large workload? = 她能独自处理如此大的工作量吗？


否定句:
He did not single-handedly complete the project; he had a team. = 他没有独自完成项目；他有一个团队。
She could not single-handedly manage all the responsibilities. = 她无法独自处理所有责任。
