endear:/ɪnˈdɪr/| vt.|使受喜爱；使可爱；使变得珍贵|To cause someone to be loved or liked; to make something precious or valuable.|例句：Her kindness endeared her to everyone.  她的善良使她深受大家的喜爱。|例句：The old house, with its creaky floors and quirky charm, endeared itself to its new owners. 这栋旧房子，带着吱吱作响的地板和古怪的魅力，让它的新主人爱上了它。|近义词：cherish, love, adore|反义词：alienate, estrange, repel|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endears himself to his colleagues with his humor. = 他用幽默使自己受到同事们的喜爱。
The beautiful scenery endears the place to tourists. = 美丽的景色使这个地方受到游客的喜爱。

一般过去时:
Her generosity endeared her to the community. = 她的慷慨使她受到社区的喜爱。
The old photo endeared itself to me because of its memories. = 这张旧照片因为承载着回忆而让我珍视。

现在完成时:
His dedication to his work has endeared him to his boss. = 他对工作的奉献精神使他受到老板的喜爱。
The antique furniture has endeared itself to collectors. = 这套古董家具深受收藏家的喜爱。

过去完成时:
The singer's performance had endeared her to the audience before the encore. = 在返场之前，这位歌手的表演已经赢得了观众的喜爱。
The old family recipes had endeared themselves to several generations. = 这些古老的家庭食谱已经传承了好几代人，并深受喜爱。

将来时:
I hope this gift will endear me to her. = 我希望这份礼物能让她喜欢我。
The new park will endear itself to the residents of the town. = 新公园将受到镇上居民的喜爱。

虚拟语气:
If he were more considerate, he would endear himself to more people. = 如果他更体贴一些，他就能赢得更多人的喜爱。
I wish that my actions had endeared me to them more. = 我希望我的行为能让他们更喜欢我。

被动语态:
He was endeared to his family by his kindness. = 他因为善良而受到家人的喜爱。
The painting was endeared to art lovers for its unique style. = 这幅画因其独特的风格而受到艺术爱好者的喜爱。

疑问句:
Does his charm endear him to people? = 他的魅力是否让他受到人们的喜爱？
Will the new policy endear itself to the public? = 新政策会受到公众的喜爱吗？

否定句:
His arrogance did not endear him to his superiors. = 他的傲慢并没有让他受到上司的喜爱。
The harsh regulations did not endear themselves to the citizens. = 这些苛刻的法规并没有受到市民的喜爱。
