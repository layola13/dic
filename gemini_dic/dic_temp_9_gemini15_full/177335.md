reattacking: /ˌriːəˈtækɪŋ/ | v. | 重新攻击，再次袭击 |  The act of attacking again; renewing an attack.  |例句：The army is reattacking the enemy's position.  军队正在重新攻击敌人的阵地。

例句：After a brief respite, the enemy forces began reattacking our lines.  短暂休整后，敌军开始再次攻击我们的防线。


近义词：counterattacking,  renewing the attack,  returning to the attack

反义词：retreating, withdrawing, ceasing fire


词性：动词
