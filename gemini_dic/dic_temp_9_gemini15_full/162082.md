pre-coloring: /ˌpriːˈkʌlərɪŋ/ | n. | 预先着色，预着色 | The act of coloring something before it is used or applied.  It often refers to coloring components of a larger project, such as coloring pages in a coloring book before assembling it.  | Example: The pre-coloring of the model kits saved time during assembly.  预先给模型套件着色节省了组装时间。 | 近义词: pre-painting, preliminary coloring | 反义词: post-coloring
