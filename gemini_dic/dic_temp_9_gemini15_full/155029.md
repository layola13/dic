petiolulate:/pɪˈtɪəljʊleɪt/ | adj. | 具短柄的；有叶柄的 | Having a petiole; provided with a petiole.  The term refers to a structure, typically a leaf or leaflet, that is attached to a stem by a short stalk called a petiole. |例句：The leaves of this plant are petiolulate, each attached to the stem by a small petiole. 这株植物的叶子都具有短柄，每个叶子都通过一个小的叶柄连接到茎上。The petiolulate leaflets of the acacia tree are easily identifiable. 金合欢树的小叶柄很容易辨认。|近义词：petiolate (more general term)|反义词：sessile (lacking a petiole)
