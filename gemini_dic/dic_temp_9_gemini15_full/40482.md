contributor: /kənˈtrɪbjʊtər/ | n. | 贡献者，投稿者 | A person who contributes something, especially money or help.  | Example: She is a major contributor to the charity. 她是一位主要的慈善捐款人。  |近义词：donor, giver, supporter | 反义词：recipient, receiver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contributes regularly to the local newspaper. = 他定期向当地报纸投稿。
She contributes to the success of the team. = 她为团队的成功做出了贡献。

一般过去时:
He contributed a significant amount of money to the project. = 他为该项目捐赠了一大笔钱。
She contributed several articles to the journal. = 她为该期刊贡献了几篇文章。

现在完成时:
They have contributed significantly to our understanding of the disease. = 他们对我们对这种疾病的理解做出了重大贡献。
She has contributed many hours of volunteer work to the hospital. = 她为医院贡献了许多小时的志愿者工作。

过去完成时:
Before the deadline, he had contributed all the necessary documents. = 在截止日期之前，他已经贡献了所有必要的文档。
They had contributed to the fund for several years. = 他们多年来一直为该基金捐款。


将来时:
He will contribute his expertise to the team. = 他将把他的专业知识贡献给团队。
She will contribute to the discussion tomorrow. = 她明天将参与讨论。


虚拟语气:
If he contributed more, we could finish the project earlier. = 如果他多贡献一些，我们就能更早完成项目。
I wish she had contributed more to the team effort. = 我希望她能为团队的努力做出更多贡献。


被动语态:
A large sum of money was contributed by anonymous donors. = 一大笔钱是由匿名捐赠者捐赠的。
Many valuable ideas were contributed to the meeting. = 会议上贡献了许多有价值的想法。


疑问句:
Who contributed the most to the project? = 谁对这个项目贡献最大？
How much did he contribute to the charity? = 他向慈善机构捐了多少钱？


否定句:
He did not contribute anything to the discussion. = 他没有对讨论做出任何贡献。
She didn't contribute to the team's success. = 她没有为团队的成功做出贡献。


PHRASE->
major contributor = 主要贡献者
significant contributor = 重要贡献者
key contributor = 关键贡献者
regular contributor = 定期投稿者
anonymous contributor = 匿名贡献者
