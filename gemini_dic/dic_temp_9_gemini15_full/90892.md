/hʌnt/ | v. | 狩猎；搜寻；追捕 | To chase and kill wild animals for food or sport; to search for something actively. | He hunts deer every autumn. 他每年秋天都打猎鹿。  He hunted for his lost keys. 他搜寻他丢失的钥匙。 | search, seek, pursue |  — |  verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hunts rabbits every weekend. = 他每个周末都去打兔子。
They hunt for treasure on the island. = 他们在岛上寻宝。


一般过去时:
She hunted for hours but found nothing. = 她搜寻了几个小时但什么也没找到。
The dogs hunted the fox through the forest. = 狗追着狐狸穿过森林。


现在完成时:
They have hunted in this forest for many years. = 他们在这片森林里打猎多年了。
I have hunted for a solution to this problem all day. = 我整天都在寻找这个问题的解决方法。


过去完成时:
They had hunted for hours before they finally found their prey. = 他们在最终找到猎物之前已经搜寻了几个小时。
He had hunted for the lost ring before he called the police. = 他在报警之前已经搜寻过丢失的戒指了。


将来时:
We will hunt for mushrooms in the woods tomorrow. = 我们明天将在树林里寻找蘑菇。
He will hunt lions in Africa next year. = 他明年将在非洲猎狮。


虚拟语气:
If I had a hunting license, I would hunt deer. = 如果我有狩猎执照，我会去打鹿。
I wish I could hunt with my father again. = 我希望我还能和爸爸一起打猎。


被动语态:
The deer were hunted to near extinction. = 鹿几乎被猎杀到灭绝。
The treasure was hunted for centuries. = 这笔宝藏被寻找了几个世纪。


疑问句:
Do you hunt? = 你打猎吗？
Where do they hunt? = 他们在哪里打猎？


否定句:
He doesn't hunt anymore. = 他不再打猎了。
They didn't hunt that day because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他们那天没有打猎。


PHRASE->
hunt down = 追捕；搜寻到
hunt for = 寻找
go hunting = 去打猎
on the hunt = 在寻找中
headhunting = 猎头
