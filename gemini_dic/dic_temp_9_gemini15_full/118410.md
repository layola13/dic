merrimake:/ˈmɛriˌmeɪk/| n. v.|欢闹，狂欢；寻欢作乐 | A merrymaking or revelry, often involving feasting, drinking, and dancing.  It can also refer to the act of engaging in such activities. |例句：The villagers held a merrimake to celebrate the harvest. 村民们举行了一次狂欢来庆祝丰收。

The children were full of merrimake after receiving their presents.孩子们收到礼物后非常开心。|近义词：revelry, festivity, celebration, carousal |反义词：mourning, solemnity, gloom
