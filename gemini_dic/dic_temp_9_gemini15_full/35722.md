coccobacterium:/ˌkɒkəʊbækˈtɪəriəm/| n. |球菌 |A spherical or oval bacterium.|Coccobacteria are a type of bacteria that are spherical or oval in shape. They are often found in pairs or clusters.  Some coccobacteria are harmless, while others can cause disease. |近义词：coccus (球菌), micrococcus (微球菌) |反义词：bacillus (杆菌), spirillum (螺旋菌)
