criers:/ˈkraɪərz/| n.|哭喊者，叫喊者|People who cry or shout loudly, often expressing grief, anger, or pain.  They may be individuals expressing personal emotions or part of a larger group demonstrating collective feelings.|例句：The criers in the market were trying to attract customers. 市场上的叫卖者试图吸引顾客。|例句：The criers of the fallen soldiers filled the air with sorrow.阵亡士兵的哭喊声充满了悲伤。|近义词：weepers, wailers, mourners, complainers|反义词：celebrators, rejoicers
