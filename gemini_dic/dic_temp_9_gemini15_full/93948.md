immunosuppressing: /ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 免疫抑制的 |  Suppressing the immune system.  The process or action of reducing or suppressing the activity of the immune system. | 例句：Immunosuppressing drugs are often used after organ transplants to prevent rejection. 免疫抑制药物常用于器官移植后以防止排斥反应。

Immunosuppressing treatments are crucial for autoimmune diseases. 免疫抑制治疗对自身免疫性疾病至关重要。


近义词：immunosuppressive, immunosuppressant (as a noun) | 反义词：immunostimulating


词源:

* **immuno-**:  from Latin *immunis*, meaning "exempt, free, safe".  This refers to the body's ability to be exempt from disease.

* **-suppress-**: from Latin *supprimere*, meaning "to press down, to subdue, to repress".

* **-ing**: present participle suffix, indicating an action or process.
