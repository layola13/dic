ridges: /ˈrɪdʒɪz/ | n. |山脊，脊状物，隆起部分 | A ridge is a long, narrow elevation of land, like a mountain range, or a raised line on a surface.  It can also refer to a line of raised or folded material. |例句：The hikers followed the mountain ridge. 登山者沿着山脊行走。

The roof had several ridges where the tiles overlapped. 屋顶瓦片重叠处有几道脊。

近义词：crest, peak, range, spine | 反义词：valley, trough, depression | 词性：名词
