skimpier:/ˈskɪmpiər/ | adj. | 更加不足的，更简陋的，更吝啬的 | Less generous or ample; meager; scanty.  It describes something that is smaller, less generous, or of poorer quality than expected or desired.  It implies a lack of sufficient quantity or quality. | 例句：The portions at the restaurant were skimpier than I expected. = 餐厅的分量比我预期的要少。


The new budget resulted in skimpier funding for education. = 新预算导致教育经费减少。


近义词：scanty, meager, insufficient, inadequate, paltry, stingy | 反义词：ample, generous, abundant, plentiful, lavish


词性:形容词
