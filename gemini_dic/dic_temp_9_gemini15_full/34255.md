clapper:/ˈklæpər/| n.|响板；敲击器；（钟等的）锤；絮叨的人|A clapper is the part of a bell, gong, or similar instrument that strikes the side to produce the sound.  It can also refer to a device used to make a clapping sound, or a person who talks incessantly.|例句：The church bell's clapper swung loudly in the wind. 教堂钟的锤在风中大声摇摆。

The child played with a pair of clappers, making a rhythmic clapping sound.  孩子玩着一对响板，发出有节奏的拍打声。|近义词：striker, beater, chatterbox|反义词：silent person
