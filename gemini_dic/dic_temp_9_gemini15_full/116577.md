matriarchal:/ˌmeɪtriˈɑːrkl/| adj. |母系的；女性族长的；| Relating to or characteristic of a matriarchy, a social system where the mother or eldest female is the head of the family or tribe and descent is traced through the female line.  |Example: In many matriarchal societies, women hold significant power and influence.  在许多母系社会中，妇女拥有重要的权力和影响力。  |近义词：female-dominated, gynocentric |反义词：patriarchal
