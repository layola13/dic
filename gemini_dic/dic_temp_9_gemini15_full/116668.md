mattock:/ˈmætək/| n.|鹤嘴锄；镐|A mattock is a tool with a long handle and two heads, one pointed and one bladed, used for digging, breaking up, and loosening hard ground.  It's similar to a pickaxe but often has a broader blade. |例句：The gardener used a mattock to break up the hard clay soil. 园丁用鹤嘴锄来翻动坚硬的粘土。

例句：He swung the mattock with powerful strokes, clearing a path through the dense undergrowth. 他用力挥舞着鹤嘴锄，在茂密的灌木丛中开辟出一条道路。


近义词：pickaxe, hoe|反义词：None specifically.|词性：noun
