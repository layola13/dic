nonimperialistically:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəlɪstɪkli/| adv.|非帝国主义地，不以帝国主义的方式|Acting or existing without the characteristics of imperialism; without seeking to dominate or control other nations or peoples.|例句：The country is attempting to develop its economy nonimperialistically, respecting the sovereignty of other nations.  这个国家正试图以非帝国主义的方式发展其经济，尊重其他国家的主权。|近义词：anti-imperialistically, democratically, independently|反义词：imperialistically, autocratically, dictatorially
