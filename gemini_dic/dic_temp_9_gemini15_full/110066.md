lichened:/ˈlaɪkənd/| adj.|长满地衣的；像地衣的|Covered with lichen.  The word describes a surface or object that has lichen growing on it, often implying age or exposure to the elements.|例句：The old stone wall was lichened and weathered.  古老的石墙长满了地衣，显得风化剥蚀。

例句：The lichened rocks showed the passage of time. 长满地衣的岩石显示了时间的流逝。


近义词：moss-covered, overgrown (with lichen)


反义词：bare, clean


词性：形容词
