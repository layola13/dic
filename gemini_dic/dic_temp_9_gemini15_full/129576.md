non-circular:/ˌnɒnˈsɜːkjʊlə(r)/| adj. |非循环的，非圆形的|Not circular; not forming a circle or a loop.  It describes something that does not have a circular shape or a cyclical process.  |例句：The argument is non-circular because it doesn't rely on its conclusion to support its premises.  这个论证是非循环的，因为它不依赖结论来支持前提。

例句：The design of the building is non-circular, featuring sharp angles and straight lines. 这座建筑的设计是非圆形的，具有尖锐的角和直线。


近义词：linear, straight, acyclic, non-cyclical

反义词：circular, cyclical, recurrent


词性: adjective
