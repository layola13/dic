They often advocate for traditional family structures and roles, emphasizing the father's dominance and control.  | Example:  The paterist argued for the restoration of traditional family values, emphasizing the father's role as the sole decision-maker.  父权制拥护者主张恢复传统的家庭价值观，强调父亲作为唯一决策者的角色。 | 近义词：traditionalist, patriarch, authoritarian | 反义词：feminist, egalitarian | 名词
