decipium:/dɪˈsɪpiəm/| v.|欺骗，迷惑|To deceive or mislead someone; to cause someone to believe something that is false.|例句：The magician deceived the audience with his skillful illusions. 魔术师用他娴熟的幻觉欺骗了观众。|例句：He was deceived by her charming facade. 他被她迷人的外表所迷惑。|近义词：delude, trick, beguile, mislead|反义词：enlighten, inform, reveal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deceives his friends. = 他欺骗他的朋友们。
She deceives her parents. = 她欺骗她的父母。


一般过去时:
The salesman deceived the customer. = 销售员欺骗了顾客。
He deceived me about his intentions. = 他欺骗了我关于他的意图。


现在完成时:
They have deceived us many times. = 他们已经欺骗了我们很多次。
She has been deceived by his promises. = 她一直被他的承诺所欺骗。


过去完成时:
I had been deceived before, so I was cautious. = 我以前被欺骗过，所以我非常谨慎。
They had deceived the authorities for years. = 他们多年来一直欺骗当局。


将来时:
He will deceive you if he gets the chance. = 如果他有机会，他会欺骗你。
They will deceive many people with this scam. = 他们会用这个骗局欺骗很多人。


虚拟语气:
If he deceived her, she would be heartbroken. = 如果他欺骗了她，她会心碎的。
I wish he hadn't deceived me. = 我希望他没有欺骗我。


被动语态:
The customers were deceived by the fraudulent advertisement. = 顾客们被虚假的广告欺骗了。
He was deceived into signing the contract. = 他被骗签了合同。


疑问句:
Was he deceived by her lies? = 他被她的谎言欺骗了吗？
Were the investors deceived? = 投资者们被欺骗了吗？


否定句:
He did not deceive anyone. = 他没有欺骗任何人。
She would never deceive her best friend. = 她永远不会欺骗她最好的朋友。
