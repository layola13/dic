discretely: /dɪˈskriːtli/ | adv. | 谨慎地；秘密地；小心地 | In a way that is not easily noticeable; subtly; quietly.  It implies a desire to avoid attention or observation. | 例句：She discretely slipped a note into his pocket. 她偷偷地把一张纸条塞进他的口袋。

例句：He discretely avoided mentioning the sensitive topic. 他谨慎地避免提及那个敏感话题。


近义词：secretly, subtly, quietly, cautiously, privately, unobtrusively


反义词：openly, obviously, publicly, conspicuously


词源:  The adverb "discretely" derives from the adjective "discrete," which comes from the Latin word "discretus," meaning "separated," "distinct," or "discernible."  The  "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective into an adverb, indicating manner or way.
