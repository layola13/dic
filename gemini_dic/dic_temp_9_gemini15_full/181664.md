releases:/rɪˈliːsɪz/| n. v.|释放；发行；发布|The act of setting something free;  the act of making something available to the public.|例句：The prisoner was finally released. 囚犯最终被释放了。|例句：The band released their new album last week. 乐队上周发行了他们的新专辑。|近义词：discharge, liberate, launch, issue|反义词：capture, restrain, withhold, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company releases new products every year. = 公司每年都会发布新产品。
He releases the bird from its cage. = 他把鸟从笼子里放出来。


一般过去时:
They released the hostages yesterday. = 他们昨天释放了人质。
The movie was released last month. = 这部电影上个月上映了。


现在完成时:
The government has released a statement on the situation. = 政府已经就此事发表声明。
She has released three albums so far. = 她迄今为止已经发行了三张专辑。


过去完成时:
The news had been released before the meeting started. = 新闻在会议开始之前就已经发布了。
He had already released the dog before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经把狗放走了。


将来时:
They will release the report next week. = 他们下周将发布报告。
The new game will be released in December. = 新游戏将于十二月发行。


虚拟语气:
If they released the information, it would cause chaos. = 如果他们发布这些信息，将会造成混乱。
I wish they had released the results sooner. = 我希望他们早点公布结果。


被动语态:
The prisoners were released after years in jail. = 囚犯们在狱中待了多年后被释放了。
The album was released to critical acclaim. = 专辑发行后受到好评。


疑问句:
When will the new software be released? = 新软件什么时候发布？
Has the company released a statement yet? = 公司已经发表声明了吗？


否定句:
The company did not release any further information. = 公司没有发布任何进一步的信息。
He hasn't released his new book yet. = 他还没有发行他的新书。


PHRASE->
release date 发行日期
press release 新闻稿
release from 释放自
