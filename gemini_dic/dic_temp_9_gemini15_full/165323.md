/ˌpriːɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先调查；初步调查 |  The act of investigating something before a full investigation is undertaken.  It involves preliminary inquiries and data gathering to determine the scope and nature of a more thorough investigation. | The police are preinvestigating the incident before launching a full-scale inquiry. 警方在展开全面调查之前正在对事件进行初步调查。 | investigating, examining, exploring |  not investigating, ignoring  | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are preinvestigating the case. = 他们正在对案件进行预先调查。
We preinvestigate all potential risks before launch. = 我们在启动之前会预先调查所有潜在风险。


一般过去时:
The team preinvestigated the complaint. = 该团队对投诉进行了初步调查。
She preinvestigated the market before making a decision. = 她做决定之前对市场进行了预先调查。


现在完成时:
They have preinvestigated several leads. = 他们已经预先调查了几条线索。
We have preinvestigated the problem and found some clues. = 我们已经对这个问题进行了初步调查，并发现了一些线索。


过去完成时:
The detective had preinvestigated the suspect's background. = 侦探已经预先调查了嫌疑人的背景。
They had preinvestigated the site before the excavation began. = 在挖掘开始之前，他们已经对现场进行了初步调查。


将来时:
They will preinvestigate the matter further. = 他们将进一步对此事进行初步调查。
We will preinvestigate the feasibility of the project. = 我们将对项目的可行性进行预先调查。


虚拟语气:
If they had preinvestigated the situation, they would have avoided the disaster. = 如果他们当时对情况进行了预先调查，他们就能避免这场灾难。
I wish we had preinvestigated the market trends. = 我希望我们当时已经对市场趋势进行了预先调查。


被动语态:
The incident was being preinvestigated by the authorities. = 该事件正在由当局进行初步调查。
The proposal is being preinvestigated by the committee. = 该提案正在由委员会进行预先调查。


疑问句:
Are they preinvestigating the cause of the accident? = 他们正在对事故原因进行初步调查吗？
Was the complaint preinvestigated before being dismissed? = 投诉在被驳回之前是否进行了初步调查？


否定句:
They did not preinvestigate the potential problems. = 他们没有预先调查潜在的问题。
The company did not preinvestigate the environmental impact. = 该公司没有预先调查环境影响。
