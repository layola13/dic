campered:/ˈkæmpərd/| v.|露营；宿营|To spend a period of time living in a tent or other temporary shelter, typically outdoors.|例句：We campered in the mountains last weekend.  我们上周末在山上露营。

例句：They campered by the lake, enjoying the scenery. 他们在湖边露营，欣赏风景。


近义词： camped, bivouacked


反义词： stayed indoors, housed


词性：动词
