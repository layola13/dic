exploration:/ˌɛkspləˈreɪʃən/ | n. |探险；探索；考察 |The act of exploring or investigating; a journey or expedition undertaken to explore an unknown area.  |例句：The exploration of space has led to many scientific discoveries. 太空探索带来了许多科学发现。|例句：The team's exploration of the Amazon rainforest yielded valuable data.  该团队对亚马逊雨林的考察获得了宝贵的数据。|近义词：investigation, research, survey, quest |反义词：exploitation, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists explore new frontiers in medicine. = 科学家们正在探索医学领域的新前沿。
We explore different possibilities. = 我们正在探索不同的可能性。

一般过去时:
Columbus explored the Americas. = 哥伦布探索了美洲。
The researchers explored the causes of the disease. = 研究人员探究了该疾病的起因。

现在完成时:
They have explored the caves extensively. = 他们已经广泛地探索了这些洞穴。
We have explored several options for the project. = 我们已经探索了该项目的几个方案。

过去完成时:
They had explored the possibility before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经探索过这种可能性。
After they had explored the area, they found a hidden cave. = 在他们探索了该地区之后，他们发现了一个隐藏的洞穴。

将来时:
We will explore the potential of renewable energy. = 我们将探索可再生能源的潜力。
The company plans to explore new markets next year. = 公司计划明年探索新的市场。

虚拟语气:
If we explored this option, we might find a solution. = 如果我们探索这个方案，我们可能会找到解决方案。
I wish I had explored more career options before. = 我希望我以前探索过更多的职业选择。

被动语态:
The Amazon rainforest is being explored by researchers. = 亚马逊雨林正在被研究人员探索。
The new technology is being explored for its potential applications. = 正在探索这项新技术的潜在应用。

疑问句:
What areas are they exploring? = 他们正在探索哪些领域？
Have you explored all the possibilities? = 你已经探索了所有可能性了吗？

否定句:
They did not explore the full potential of the plan. = 他们没有充分探索该计划的潜力。
We haven't explored that option yet. = 我们还没有探索那个方案。
