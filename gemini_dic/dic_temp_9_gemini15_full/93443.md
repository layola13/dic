illuminator: /ɪˈluːmɪneɪtər/ | n. | 照明器；阐明者 | A device that provides illumination, such as a lamp or spotlight; someone or something that clarifies or explains something. |例句：The museum installed a new illuminator to highlight the ancient artifacts.  博物馆安装了一个新的照明器来突出古代文物。  The professor's lecture served as an illuminator of complex theories. 教授的讲座阐明了复杂的理论。|近义词：lamp, light, explainer, elucidator |反义词：obscurer, darkener
