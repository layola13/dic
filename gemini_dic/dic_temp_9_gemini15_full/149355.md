pachyderms:/ˈpækɪdɜːrmz/| n. |厚皮动物；(尤指)象、犀牛、河马|Thick-skinned mammals, especially elephants, rhinoceroses, and hippopotamuses.  The term often evokes a sense of large size and ponderous movement. |例句：Elephants and rhinoceroses are examples of pachyderms.  象和犀牛是厚皮动物的例子。


例句：The zoo's pachyderm enclosure was a popular attraction. 动物园的厚皮动物围栏是一个热门景点。


近义词：thick-skinned mammals
