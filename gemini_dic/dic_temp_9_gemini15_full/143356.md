outfaced:/aʊtˈfeɪst/| vt.|使…相形见绌；战胜；勇敢地面对|To surpass or overcome (someone or something) in boldness, defiance, or strength.|例句：His courage outfaced the danger. 他的勇气战胜了危险。
例句：The small company outfaced its larger competitors.这家小公司战胜了它更大的竞争对手。|近义词：outbrave, overcome, defy|反义词：yield, succumb,  give in|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outfaces his opponents in every debate. = 他在每次辩论中都战胜了他的对手。
The new design outfaces the old one in terms of efficiency. = 新设计在效率方面胜过旧设计。


一般过去时:
She outfaced her fear and spoke to the crowd. = 她战胜了恐惧，对人群讲话。
The army outfaced the rebels and secured the city. = 军队战胜了叛军并保卫了城市。

虚拟语气:
