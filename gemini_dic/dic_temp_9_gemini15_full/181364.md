reinvestigations:/ˌriːɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 再调查；重新调查 | The act of investigating something again, often because of new evidence or inconsistencies in the original investigation.  It implies a more thorough or detailed examination than the initial inquiry. | Example: The police conducted reinvestigations into the cold case after new evidence emerged.  警方在出现新的证据后对悬案进行了重新调查。  |近义词：re-examinations, reviews, further inquiries | 反义词：conclusions, resolutions | 名词
