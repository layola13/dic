sergeant-majorish:/ˌsɑːrdʒəntˈmeɪdʒərɪʃ/|/ˈsɑːdʒəntˈmeɪdʒərɪʃ/| adj.|  像军士长的；专横的；盛气凌人的|Resembling a sergeant major in manner;  authoritarian and domineering.  The word evokes the stereotype of a strict, often officious, and bossy non-commissioned officer.|例句：His sergeant-majorish manner put everyone off. 他的军士长似的态度让每个人都反感。

例句：The sergeant-majorish tone of his voice irritated his colleagues. 他盛气凌人的语气激怒了他的同事。
