pro-unitarian:/ˌproʊ juːnɪˈtɛəriən/| adj.|支持一元论的；拥护一神论的 |Supporting or advocating Unitarianism.  The term signifies a position in favor of the Unitarian theological perspective, which emphasizes the oneness of God and rejects the doctrine of the Trinity. |Example:  His pro-unitarian views were evident in his sermons. = 他在布道中明显表达了他支持一神论的观点。  The pro-unitarian movement gained momentum in the 19th century. = 一神论运动在19世纪获得了发展势头。|近义词：unitarian, monotheistic |反义词：trinitarian, polytheistic
