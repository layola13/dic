motherless:/ˈmʌðərləs/| adj.|无母亲的，失去母亲的|Lacking a mother; deprived of a mother.|例句：She grew up motherless and had to take care of her younger siblings. 她从小失去母亲，不得不照顾她的弟弟妹妹。|近义词：orphaned, parentless|反义词：mothered

一般现在时:
The child is motherless. = 这个孩子没有母亲。
She feels motherless since her mother passed away. = 自从她母亲去世后，她就感到失去了母亲。


一般过去时:
