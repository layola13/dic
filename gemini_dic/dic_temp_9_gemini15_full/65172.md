ethnography:/ɛθˈnɑːɡrəfi/|/ˈeθnɒɡrəfi/| n. |人种志；民族志；民族学；人种学|The systematic study of people and cultures. Ethnographers immerse themselves in the lives of the people they study, observing their behaviors, participating in their activities, and conducting interviews to gain a deep understanding of their culture.  |例句：Her ethnography focuses on the social structures of a remote Amazonian tribe.她的民族志研究重点是亚马逊一个偏远部落的社会结构。

例句：The ethnography provides valuable insights into the daily lives and traditions of the community. 这篇民族志提供了对社群日常生活和传统的宝贵见解。


近义词：ethnological study, cultural study, anthropological study |反义词：N/A |词性：名词
