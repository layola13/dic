epitaphist:/ɪˈpɪtəfɪst/ | n. |墓志铭撰写者|A person who writes epitaphs.  An epitaphist crafts inscriptions for tombstones, often reflecting on the life and accomplishments of the deceased.  They strive to capture the essence of the individual in a concise and memorable way.|The famous poet was also a skilled epitaphist, composing moving tributes for his friends and family.|近义词：inscription writer, memorial writer |反义词：无
