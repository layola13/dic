rosinweed: /ˈrɒzɪnˌwiːd/ | n. |松香草，树脂草|A plant of the genus Silphium, having resinous sap.  Rosinweed are tall, sturdy plants with large, rough leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers. They are native to North America and are known for their ability to tolerate dry conditions. |Example: The prairie was ablaze with the golden flowers of the rosinweed.  草原上松香草金色的花朵盛开。

The rosinweed's deep taproot helps it survive drought. 松香草的深根系帮助它抵御干旱。|近义词：Silphium (genus name)|反义词：无|
