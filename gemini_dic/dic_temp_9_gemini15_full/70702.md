financed:/ˈfaɪnænst/| v.|资助，为…提供资金 | To provide funding for something.  |例句：The project was financed by a government grant.  这个项目是由政府拨款资助的。

例句：The company financed the new building with a loan. 公司通过贷款为新大楼提供资金。


近义词：funded, supported, bankrolled, capitalized

反义词：defunded, unpaid for


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They finance their research through grants. = 他们通过拨款资助他们的研究。
The government finances many social programs. = 政府资助许多社会项目。

一般过去时:
The company financed the expansion with borrowed money. = 公司用借来的钱资助了扩张。
They financed their education with student loans. = 他们用助学贷款资助了自己的教育。

现在完成时:
We have financed several projects this year. = 我们今年已经资助了好几个项目。
The foundation has financed countless charitable endeavors. = 该基金会已经资助了无数的慈善事业。

过去完成时:
They had financed the venture before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经为该企业提供了资金。
The investors had financed the startup for two years. = 投资者已经为这家初创公司注资两年了。

将来时:
They will finance the new factory next year. = 他们明年将为新工厂提供资金。
The company will finance its growth through increased sales. = 公司将通过增加销售额来为其增长提供资金。

虚拟语气:
If they financed the project, it would be successful. = 如果他们资助这个项目，它将会成功。
I wish they had financed my research proposal. = 我希望他们资助我的研究提案。

被动语态:
The project was financed by private investors. = 该项目由私人投资者资助。
The renovations were financed through a bond issue. = 整修工程通过发行债券获得资金。

疑问句:
How was the film financed? = 这部电影是如何融资的？
Who financed the construction of the bridge? = 谁资助了这座桥的建设？

否定句:
The government did not finance the controversial program. = 政府没有为这个有争议的项目提供资金。
They could not finance the expansion without additional funding. = 没有额外的资金，他们无法资助扩张。
