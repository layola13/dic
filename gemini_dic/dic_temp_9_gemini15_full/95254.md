incantation:/ˌɪŋkænˈteɪʃən/| n. |咒语，念咒|A incantation is a series of words said as a magic spell.  It's often used in rituals or ceremonies to invoke supernatural powers or effects.  |Example: The witch chanted a powerful incantation to summon the spirits.  巫婆念诵了一个强大的咒语来召唤灵魂。|近义词：spell, charm, conjuration |反义词：prayer(depending on context)
