electrocute:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌkjuːt/ | vt. |电死；触电致死 | To kill (a person or animal) by electric shock.  |例句：He was electrocuted by a faulty wire. 他被一条有故障的电线电死了。|例句：The stray dog was electrocuted after wandering onto a power line. 那只流浪狗在跑到电线上后被电死了。|近义词：execute (by electricity), kill by electricity |反义词：resuscitate, revive |动词

词源:
The word "electrocute" is a blend of "electro" (from "electricity") and "execute," reflecting its use as a method of execution.  It was coined in the late 19th century.
