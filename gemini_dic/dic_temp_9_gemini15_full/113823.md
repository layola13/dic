madden:/ˈmædən/| vt.|使恼怒；使发狂|to make someone very angry|The constant noise maddened him.  不断的噪音让他发狂。|His behavior maddened his parents.他的行为惹怒了他的父母。|irritate, infuriate, exasperate|calm, soothe, appease|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The traffic maddens me every morning. = 每天早上的交通让我抓狂。
Loud noises madden many people. = 嘈杂的声音让很多人抓狂。

一般过去时:
The delay maddened the passengers. = 延误让乘客们非常生气。
Her stubbornness maddened her teacher. = 她的固执让老师很生气。

现在完成时:
His constant complaining has maddened everyone. = 他不断的抱怨已经惹恼了所有人。
The heat has maddened the dogs. = 炎热的天气让狗们都发狂了。

过去完成时:
The long wait had maddened the audience. = 漫长的等待已经让观众们非常恼火。
I had been maddened by his lies. = 我被他那些谎言激怒了。

将来时:
The traffic will madden me if I'm late again. = 如果我又迟到了，交通会让我抓狂的。
This constant interruptions will madden me. = 这些不断的打扰会让我发疯的。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't do that; it would madden your boss. = 如果我是你，我不会那样做；那会惹怒你的老板。
I wish that noise wouldn't madden me so. = 我希望那种噪音不会让我这么生气。

被动语态:
He was maddened by the news. = 他被这个消息激怒了。
The crowd was maddened by the injustice. = 人群被这种不公正激怒了。

疑问句:
Does the heat madden you? = 炎热的天气会让你生气吗？
What maddens you about this situation? = 这种情况让你生气的是什么？

否定句:
The news didn't madden him. = 这个消息并没有让他生气。
The situation didn't madden her as much as she expected. = 情况并没有像她预期的那样让她生气。
