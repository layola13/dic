mastering:/ˈmɑːstərɪŋ/| v. |掌握；精通；控制|The act of gaining complete knowledge or skill in a particular subject or activity.  It implies a high level of proficiency and often involves dedicated effort and practice. |例句：She is mastering the art of pottery. 她正在掌握制陶的技艺。

例句：He's mastering a new programming language. 他正在掌握一门新的编程语言。


近义词：acquiring, learning, conquering, dominating,  subduing

反义词：failing, neglecting, ignoring, abandoning


词性: 动词
