reconstituent:/ˌriːkənˈstɪtʃuənt/| n. |重建者；再造物；成分；构成要素 |A reconstituent is something that rebuilds or restores something else, or a component part that makes up a whole. It can refer to a person who helps rebuild something, or a substance used to restore something to its original state.  |例句：The addition of water is a crucial step in the reconstitution of dried milk.  (水的添加是奶粉复原的关键步骤。)

The archaeologist acted as a reconstituent of the ancient civilization. (考古学家充当了古代文明的重建者。)

|近义词：component, constituent, ingredient, restorer |反义词：destroyer, decomposer |名词
