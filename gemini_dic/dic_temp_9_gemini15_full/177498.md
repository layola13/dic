rebleaching: /ˌriːˈbliːtʃɪŋ/ | n. | 再漂白；二次漂白 | The process of bleaching something again after it has already been bleached once.  This often refers to teeth whitening or fabric bleaching. | Example: The dentist recommended rebleaching my teeth after a few years.  牙医建议几年后再次漂白我的牙齿。


Example: After several washes, the fabric required rebleaching to restore its original whiteness. 经过几次清洗后，织物需要再次漂白才能恢复其原来的白色。


近义词:  secondary bleaching, re-whitening


反义词:  staining, darkening


词性: Noun (名词)
