innovated:/ˈɪnəveɪtɪd/| v.|创新，改革|To have introduced something new; to have made changes to something to improve it or make it more modern.|例句：The company innovated its product line to stay competitive.  公司对其产品线进行了创新以保持竞争力。The chef innovated a new dessert recipe.  这位厨师创新了一种新的甜点食谱。|近义词：invented, improved, modernized|反义词：stagnated, remained unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They innovate constantly to stay ahead. = 他们不断创新以保持领先地位。
The company innovates new technologies every year. = 公司每年都创新新技术。

一般过去时:
The engineer innovated a new design. = 工程师设计了一种新的设计。
They innovated their marketing strategy. = 他们创新了他们的营销策略。


现在完成时:
We have innovated significantly in recent years. = 近年来，我们在创新方面取得了显著进展。
The company has innovated many successful products. = 这家公司已经创新了许多成功的产品。

过去完成时:
They had innovated the system before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经对系统进行了创新。
She had innovated several processes before joining the team. = 她加入团队之前已经创新了几个流程。

将来时:
We will innovate more sustainable solutions. = 我们将创新更可持续的解决方案。
The firm will innovate new ways of communication. = 这家公司将创新新的沟通方式。


虚拟语气:
If they had innovated earlier, they would have been more successful. = 如果他们早些创新，他们会更成功。
I wish they had innovated a better solution. = 我希望他们能想出一个更好的解决方案。

被动语态:
The product was innovated by a young team. = 该产品是由一个年轻团队创新的。
New strategies are being innovated to increase efficiency. = 正在创新新的策略以提高效率。

疑问句:
Have they innovated their approach? = 他们是否改变了他们的方法？
What did they innovate to improve the process? = 为了改进流程，他们做了哪些创新？


否定句:
They did not innovate enough. = 他们的创新还不够。
The company hasn't innovated in years. = 这家公司多年来都没有创新。


PHRASE->
innovate upon = 在...基础上创新
innovate in = 在...方面创新
innovate with = 用...进行创新
innovate for = 为...而创新
innovate through = 通过...进行创新
