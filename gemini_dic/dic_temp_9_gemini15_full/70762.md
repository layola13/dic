fines:/faɪnz/| n.|罚款；罚金|Money paid as a penalty for an offense or crime.  Fines are imposed by courts or other authorities to punish wrongdoers and deter future offenses. |例句：He had to pay a fine for speeding. 他因超速而不得不支付罚款。|例句：The company was fined heavily for polluting the river. 这家公司因污染河流而被处以巨额罚款。|近义词：penalty, forfeit, mulct|反义词：reward, bonus|名词
