cantonalism:/kænˈtɔːnəlɪzəm/| n. |地方分权制；地方主义；邦国主义|The political system characterized by strong regional governments and a weak central government.  It often involves a degree of autonomy or self-governance for the cantons or regions.  This can lead to diverse political and economic systems across the country.|Example: Switzerland's success is often attributed to its effective cantonalism, balancing central authority with regional autonomy. 瑞士的成功往往归功于其有效的地方分权制，它平衡了中央权力与地方自治。|近义词：federalism, regionalism, decentralization|反义词：centralism, unification, centralization|名词
