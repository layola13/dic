cymballike:/ˈsɪmbəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像钹的；钹似的 | Resembling a cymbal in shape, sound, or appearance.  The word combines "cymbal" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance. |例句：The large, metallic discs hanging in the wind chime had a cymballike clang. 风铃里悬挂的大型金属圆盘发出类似钹的铿锵声。The rhythmic crashing of the waves sounded cymballike to her.  在她听来，海浪有节奏的拍打声听起来像钹声。|近义词：cymbal-sounding, gong-like |反义词: muted, soft
