slammed:/slæmd/ | vt. |猛摔；猛击；砰地关上；严厉批评| To forcefully throw or shut something; to criticize harshly. |例句：He slammed the door in anger. 他生气地砰地关上门。

He slammed his fist on the table. 他用力地把拳头砸在桌子上。


近义词： banged, crashed, slammed shut, criticized severely |反义词：gently closed, praised


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slams the door every time he leaves. 他每次离开都砰地关上门。
She slams her books on the desk. 她把书重重地摔在桌子上。


一般过去时:
I slammed the brakes when I saw the cat.  看到猫后我猛踩了刹车。
They slammed the lid shut. 他们砰地关上了盖子。


现在完成时:
He has slammed the door again. 他又砰地关上门了。
She has slammed the phone down in frustration. 她沮丧地把电话摔了。


过去完成时:
I had slammed the door before he even knocked.  在他敲门之前我就已经把门砰地关上了。
They had slammed the car door and ran. 他们砰地关上车门跑了。


将来时:
I will slam the door if you don't stop making noise. 如果你不停吵闹，我就把门砰地关上。
She will slam the book closed once she is finished. 她读完后会把书砰地关上。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't slam the door like that. 如果我是你，我不会那样砰地关上门。
I wish I hadn't slammed the door in his face. 我真希望我没有把门砰地关在他脸上。


被动语态:
The door was slammed shut. 门被砰地关上了。
The case was slammed by the critics. 这个案子受到了评论家的严厉批评。


疑问句:
Did he slam the door? 他把门砰地关上了吗？

否定句:
