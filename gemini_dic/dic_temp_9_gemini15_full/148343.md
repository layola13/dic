overspecialize: /ˌoʊvərˈspeʃəlˌaɪz/ | v. | 过度专业化，过于专门化 | To specialize to an excessive degree; to become excessively focused on a narrow area of expertise, often at the expense of broader knowledge or skills.  |例句：Don't overspecialize in college; take a variety of courses to broaden your horizons. 不要在大学里过度专业化；学习各种课程来拓宽你的视野。

He overspecialized in a niche market and struggled when the demand decreased. 他在一个利基市场过度专业化，当需求下降时就难以维持。


近义词：hyper-specialize, overfocus, overconcentrate | 反义词：diversify, generalize
