revolves:/rɪˈvɒlvz/ | v. |旋转；环绕；围绕…转动 | To move in a circle around a central point.  |例句：The Earth revolves around the sun. 地球绕着太阳转。

The moon revolves around the Earth. 月亮绕着地球转。


近义词：rotate, orbit, circle | 反义词：stand still
