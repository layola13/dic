oversmoking:/ˌoʊvərˈsmoʊkɪŋ/ | n. |过度吸烟|Excessive smoking; smoking more than is considered healthy or safe.  It often implies a detrimental effect on health. |Example: His oversmoking led to serious respiratory problems.他的过度吸烟导致了严重的呼吸系统疾病。|近义词：excessive smoking, heavy smoking |反义词：not smoking, abstaining from smoking
