hydrographer:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfər/| n. |水文地理学家|A hydrographer is a scientist who studies and maps the physical features of the oceans, seas, and other bodies of water, including their depths, currents, and tides.| The hydrographer meticulously charted the seabed, revealing previously unknown underwater canyons.  水文地理学家一丝不苟地绘制了海底地图，揭示了以前未知的海底峡谷。| oceanographer, cartographer |  geologist |
