shareowners: /ˈʃɛərˌoʊnərz/ | n. | 股东 |  People who own shares in a company.  They are the legal owners of the company, although their level of control may vary depending on the type of shares they hold and the company's structure.  |例句：The shareowners will vote on the proposed merger next month.  股东们将在下个月就拟议中的合并进行投票。

The company's shareowners are expecting a higher dividend this year. 该公司的股东们期望今年获得更高的股息。


近义词：stockholders, shareholders | 反义词：无 | 词性：名词
