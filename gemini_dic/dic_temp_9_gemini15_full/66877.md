expectorating:/ˈɛkspɛktoʊreɪtɪŋ/| v.| 咳出；吐痰 | The act of expectorating mucus or other matter from the lungs or respiratory tract.  Expectorating is often associated with respiratory illnesses or conditions. | 例句：He was expectorating phlegm into a tissue. 他正在往纸巾里吐痰。

例句：The doctor instructed the patient to expectorate into a container for analysis. 医生指示病人将痰吐到容器中进行化验。


近义词：coughing up, spitting out, ejecting

反义词：swallowing, inhaling
