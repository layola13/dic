laurentia:/ˌlɔːrɛnˈtʃiə/| n. |劳伦西亚古陆| A large continental landmass that existed during the Paleozoic Era, comprising parts of present-day North America, Greenland, and Europe.  It is considered a major component of the supercontinent Pangaea.|The formation of Laurentia was a crucial event in the Earth's geological history. 劳伦西亚的形成是地球地质史上的一件大事。|近义词:  (no exact synonyms,  related terms: paleocontinent, craton)|反义词: (no direct antonyms)|名词
