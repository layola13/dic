mini-industry:/ˈmɪniˌɪndəstri/| n. |小型产业，小行业|A mini-industry refers to a small-scale industry or sector of the economy, often characterized by a relatively small number of businesses and a specialized niche market.  It's typically less extensive and influential than a major industry but can still be economically significant within its specific area. |Example: The handcrafted jewelry market is a thriving mini-industry in the city.  这个城市的手工珠宝市场是一个蓬勃发展的微型产业。  The production of artisanal cheeses represents a significant mini-industry in the region.  手工奶酪的生产是该地区一个重要的微型产业。|近义词：niche market, small business sector, specialized industry|反义词：major industry, large-scale industry
