hygrology:/haɪˈdrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 水文学 | The scientific study of the properties, distribution, and effects of water on the Earth's surface, in the soil and underlying rocks, and in the atmosphere.  It encompasses the hydrological cycle, including precipitation, evaporation, transpiration, infiltration, runoff, and groundwater flow. | Example:  Hygrology is crucial for understanding and managing water resources.  水文学对于理解和管理水资源至关重要。|近义词：hydrology | 反义词：None
