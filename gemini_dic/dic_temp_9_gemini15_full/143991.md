outrate:/ˈaʊtˌreɪt/| vt.|胜过，超过，超过…的比率|to surpass or exceed in rate or amount|The company outrated its competitors in sales growth. 这家公司在销售增长方面超过了竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outpace|反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Our team outrates the opponent team in terms of scoring. = 我们的团队在得分方面胜过对方球队。
This machine outrates all others in efficiency. = 这台机器的效率超过所有其他机器。


一般过去时:
Last year, our sales outrated those of our competitors. = 去年，我们的销售额超过了竞争对手。
He outrated his previous best time in the race. = 他在比赛中超过了他之前的最好成绩。


现在完成时:
Our company has outrated its rivals in the market share. = 我们公司在市场份额方面已经超过了竞争对手。
She has outrated all expectations with her performance. = 她以自己的表现超过了所有人的期望。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, our team had outrated the initial projections. = 到项目结束时，我们的团队已经超过了最初的预测。
Before the final round, he had outrated all other participants. = 在最后一轮之前，他已经超过了所有其他参与者。


将来时:
We expect our production to outrate the demand next quarter. = 我们预计下个季度我们的产量将超过需求。
They predict that the inflation rate will outrate the wage growth. = 他们预测通货膨胀率将超过工资增长。


虚拟语气:
If we invested more, we would outrate our competitors in innovation. = 如果我们投资更多，我们将在创新方面胜过竞争对手。
I wish our performance had outrated the market average. = 我希望我们的业绩能超过市场平均水平。


被动语态:
The new strategy was expected to outrate the previous one in effectiveness. = 预计新战略的有效性将超过之前的战略。
Their efforts were outrated by unforeseen circumstances. = 他们的努力被不可预见的情况所超过。


疑问句:
Does our current strategy outrate the competitor's? = 我们目前的策略是否胜过竞争对手的？
Will this new technology outrate the existing one? = 这项新技术能否胜过现有的技术？


否定句:
Our sales didn't outrate the previous year's figures. = 我们的销售额没有超过去年的数字。
His attempt to outrate the record failed. = 他试图打破纪录的尝试失败了。


PHRASE->
outrate the competition = 超过竞争对手
outrate the market = 超过市场平均水平
outrate the expectations = 超过预期
outrate the previous record = 超过之前的记录
outrate the industry average = 超过行业平均水平
