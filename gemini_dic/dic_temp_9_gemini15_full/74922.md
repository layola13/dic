fretsaws:/ˈfrɛtsoːz/| n. |弓锯，弓形锯 |A fretsaw is a small saw with a thin, narrow blade that is held in a frame and used for cutting intricate shapes in wood or other materials.  It's often used for detailed work requiring precision and control.  | Example: He used a fretsaw to create the delicate filigree on the wooden box. 他用弓锯在木盒上雕刻出精细的镂空花纹。 |近义词：coping saw, jigsaw (though a jigsaw is typically powered)|反义词：axe, chainsaw (for their opposite nature of cutting)|名词
