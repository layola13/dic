edacious:/ɪˈdeɪʃəs/| adj.|贪吃的；狼吞虎咽的； voracious|英文解析:having a great desire to eat; very hungry; excessively eager.  Characterized by a voracious appetite. |例句:The edacious caterpillar devoured the entire leaf in minutes.  这条贪吃的毛毛虫几分钟内就吃掉了整片叶子。

The edacious bear consumed a large quantity of salmon.  这只贪吃的熊吃掉了大量的鲑鱼。


近义词:voracious, ravenous, gluttonous, insatiable|反义词: abstemious, temperate, moderate


词源:From Latin *edāx (“eating”), from edere (“to eat”).  The suffix -acious is an adjectival suffix meaning "having the nature of".
