fracturer: /ˈfrækʃərər/ | n. |骨折者，破裂者 | One who fractures something; one who suffers a fracture.  |例句：The surgeon was a skilled fracturer, adept at repairing complex bone breaks.  外科医生是一位熟练的骨折治疗专家，擅长修复复杂的骨折。  |近义词：bone breaker,  fracture repair specialist |反义词：healer, mender


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The surgeon fractures bones during the operation. = 外科医生在手术过程中会骨折。
He fractures the rules regularly. = 他经常违反规则。


一般过去时:
The accident fractured his leg. = 事故导致他腿骨折。
She fractured the vase accidentally. = 她不小心打碎了花瓶。


现在完成时:
The earthquake has fractured many buildings. = 地震已经使许多建筑物受损。

过去完成时:
