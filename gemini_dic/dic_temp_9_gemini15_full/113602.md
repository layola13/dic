macerates:/ˈmæsəreɪts/ | v. |浸软；捣烂；使软化 |To soften or disintegrate by steeping in liquid; to soften (tissue) by soaking.  |例句：The chef macerated the fruit in rum for several hours before making the cake.  厨师在制作蛋糕之前用朗姆酒浸泡水果数小时。

例句：The doctor macerated the tissue sample to facilitate microscopic examination. 医生将组织样本捣烂以方便显微镜检查。


近义词：soak, steep, soften, disintegrate | 反义词：harden, solidify
