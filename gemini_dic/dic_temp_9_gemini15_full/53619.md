disced:/dɪst/| v.|已耕作的；已耙过的|To have been ploughed or harrowed.  The field was disced before planting.|近义词：ploughed, harrowed|反义词：undisced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer discs the field every spring. = 农民每年春天都耕作田地。
They disc the land to prepare for planting. = 他们耕地以准备播种。

一般过去时:
He disced the field yesterday. = 他昨天耕了地。
The farmhands disced the entire plot before sunset. = 农场工人日落前耕完了整块地。

现在完成时:
They have disced the land several times this year. = 今年他们已经耕过地好几次了。
The field has been disced and is ready for seeding. = 田地已经耕好了，准备播种。

过去完成时:
The farmer had disced the field before the rain started. = 在下雨之前，农民已经耕好了地。
After they had disced the land, they planted the seeds. = 他们耕完地之后，就播种了。

将来时:
We will disc the field next week. = 我们下周将耕作田地。
The farmer will disc the land after the snow melts. = 雪融化后，农民将耕地。

虚拟语气:
If we had disced the field earlier, the crops would have grown better. = 如果我们早点耕地，庄稼就会长得更好。
I wish we had disced the land before the heavy rain. = 我真希望我们在暴雨前耕过地。

被动语态:
The field was disced by the farmer. = 田地被农民耕作了。
The land is being disced in preparation for planting. = 土地正在被耕作，以准备播种。

疑问句:
Has the field been disced yet? = 田地耕好了吗？
When will the land be disced? = 什么时候耕地？

否定句:
They didn't disc the field because it was too wet. = 因为太湿了，他们没有耕地。
The farmer hasn't disced the field yet this year. = 今年农民还没耕地。
