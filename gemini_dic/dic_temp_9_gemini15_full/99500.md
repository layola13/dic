interfluent:/ɪntərˈfluːənt/| adj.|相互流利的；流畅的，通顺的|Characterized by a smooth and easy flow between different languages or styles of expression.  It suggests a high degree of proficiency and ease in switching between languages or registers.  The word emphasizes the seamless transition and naturalness of the communication.|例句：Her interfluent command of both Spanish and English impressed the interviewers.  她的西班牙语和英语都非常流利，给面试官留下了深刻的印象。


例句：The speaker's interfluent presentation seamlessly blended academic jargon with everyday language. 演讲者的流畅演讲将学术术语和日常语言无缝地融合在一起。


近义词：fluent, articulate, eloquent, facile


反义词：halting, stumbling, incoherent, inarticulate


词性:形容词
