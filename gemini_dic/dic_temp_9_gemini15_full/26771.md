caesarian:/sɪˈzəriən/| n. adj.|剖腹产；凯撒的|A surgical operation for delivering a baby by cutting through the mother's abdomen and uterus.  The term derives from the Roman emperor Julius Caesar, although there is no reliable evidence that he was born by this method. |例句：She had a caesarean section because of complications during labor. 她因为分娩过程中出现并发症而进行了剖腹产。The baby was delivered via caesarean. 这个婴儿是通过剖腹产出生的。|近义词：cesarean section, abdominal delivery|反义词：vaginal delivery
