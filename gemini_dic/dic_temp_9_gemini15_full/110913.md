lipless:/ˈlɪpləs/| adj.|无嘴唇的，没有嘴唇的|Lacking lips.  The term can refer to a physical condition or be used figuratively.|例句：The newborn baby was born lipless, requiring immediate medical attention.  这个新生儿天生没有嘴唇，需要立即进行医疗救治。

例句：The cruel caricature depicted the politician as a lipless, sneering figure. 这幅刻薄的漫画把这位政治家描绘成一个没有嘴唇、冷笑的形象。


近义词：lip-less (same meaning)


反义词：full-lipped, having lips


词性:形容词
