dweebo:/ˈdwiːboʊ/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜，蠢货 |A dweeb is a socially awkward or inept person, often characterized by their nerdy or geeky interests.  The term is often used informally and can be considered mildly offensive. |例句：He's such a dweebo; he can't even parallel park. 他真是个笨蛋，连平行停车都不会。|例句：Don't be such a dweebo, just ask her out! 别那么傻，就去约她出去啊！|近义词：nerd, geek, dork, goofball, klutz |反义词：cool, popular, suave, sophisticated |名词
