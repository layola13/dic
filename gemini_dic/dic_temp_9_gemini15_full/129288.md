nodulous:/ˈnɒdjʊləs/ | adj. | 有结节的；块状的 | Having or characterized by small nodules or lumps.  The term refers to a surface texture marked by numerous small, rounded projections.  | Example: The surface of the potato was nodulous, indicating a disease.  马铃薯表面有很多结节，表明它患病了。 |近义词：nodular, tuberous, bumpy |反义词：smooth, even
