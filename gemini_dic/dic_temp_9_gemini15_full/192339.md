词源:
The word "scrimmage" comes from the Middle English word "scrimish," meaning a skirmish or fight.  This in turn likely derives from a combination of the Old French words "escrimer," meaning "to fence," and "escrime," meaning "fencing."  The original sense therefore related to a mock fight or contest of skill.  The American football usage is a later development.
