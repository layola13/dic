in respect that:/ɪn rɪˈspekt ðæt/| conj.|鉴于；由于；就…而言|This conjunction indicates the reason or cause for something.  It introduces a clause explaining the basis for a preceding statement. It is similar to "because," "since," or "inasmuch as," but often implies a more formal or reasoned explanation.|例句：The court ruled in his favor in respect that the evidence was insufficient. 法院判决对他有利，因为证据不足。|例句：In respect that he had no prior convictions, the judge showed leniency. 鉴于他之前没有犯罪记录，法官网开一面。|近义词：because, since, inasmuch as, considering that|反义词：despite, although, notwithstanding
