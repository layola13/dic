showdown:/ˈʃoʊdaʊn/| n. |摊牌；决战；最终对决 |A showdown is a final confrontation or decisive battle, often after a period of tension or dispute. It implies a direct and often intense conflict to resolve a matter. |例句：The two rivals finally had a showdown at the championship game.  两个竞争对手最终在冠军赛中进行了决战。

例句：After weeks of negotiations, the two companies reached a showdown.  经过几周的谈判，两家公司终于摊牌了。


近义词：confrontation, conflict, climax, final test,决战，对峙


反义词：compromise, negotiation, agreement, 和解，谈判


词性：名词
