profounder:/praʊˈfaʊndər/| adj.|更深刻的；更深奥的；更意味深长的 | More profound; showing greater depth of understanding or insight.  It implies a greater depth of meaning or feeling than "deep".|

例句：Her insights into human nature were profoundly affecting. = 她对人性的洞察深刻地打动了人们。

例句：The philosopher offered a profounder explanation of the universe. = 这位哲学家对宇宙作出了更深刻的解释。


近义词：deeper, more insightful, more penetrating

反义词：superficial, shallow, trivial

词性：形容词
