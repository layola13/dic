slavey:/ˈsleɪvi/| n.|奴隶，苦工|A slavey is a person who works very hard and is treated badly; often used derisively.|例句：She worked like a slavey all day long. 她整天像个苦力一样工作。|近义词：drudge, servant, slave|反义词：master, boss
