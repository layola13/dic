serrous: /ˈsɪəriəs/ | adj. | 严肃的；认真的；严重的 | serious; grave; solemn; weighty;  showing seriousness or importance;  demanding or requiring serious attention;  causing or likely to cause serious trouble or danger. | 例句：He has a serious look on his face. 他脸上带着严肃的表情。  The situation is far more serious than we initially thought.  情况比我们最初想的要严重得多。 | 近义词：grave, solemn, earnest, weighty | 反义词：frivolous, lighthearted, unserious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is serious about his work. = 他对他的工作很认真。
She is serious about her studies. = 她对她的学习很认真。

一般过去时:
He was serious when he said that. = 他说这话时很认真。
The situation was serious last night. = 情况在昨晚很严重。

现在完成时:
Things have become serious. = 情况已经变得严重了。
She has been serious about her future for a long time. = 她对她的未来已经认真考虑很久了。

过去完成时:
I had been serious about this project before he joined. = 在他加入之前，我就已经认真对待这个项目了。
The problem had become serious before we knew it. = 在我们意识到之前，问题已经变得严重了。

将来时:
The situation will become more serious. = 情况将会变得更严重。
Things will be serious if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会很严重。

虚拟语气:
If he were serious, he would have acted differently. = 如果他认真对待，他会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had been more serious in my studies. = 我希望我学习时能更认真一些。

被动语态:
The matter was taken seriously by the police. = 这件事被警方认真对待。
The situation is being taken seriously by the government. = 政府正在认真对待这种情况。

疑问句:
Is the situation serious? = 情况严重吗？
Are you serious about quitting your job? = 你认真考虑辞职吗？

否定句:
He wasn't serious about her. = 他对她并不认真。
The problem wasn't as serious as we thought. = 问题没有我们想象的那么严重。
