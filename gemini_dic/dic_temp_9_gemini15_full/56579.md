don't:/doʊnt/| v.| 不，别 | The contraction of "do not".  It is used to form the negative of verbs in the present simple tense. | Don't go! = 别去！ | 近义词：do not | 反义词:do


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I don't like broccoli. = 我不喜欢西兰花。
They don't understand the problem. = 他们不明白这个问题。

一般过去时:
He didn't come to the party. = 他没有来参加聚会。
We didn't see her yesterday. = 我们昨天没看见她。


现在完成时:
I haven't finished my work yet. = 我还没完成我的工作。
She hasn't called me back. = 她还没给我回电话。


过去完成时:
They hadn't arrived before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们还没到。
We hadn't eaten anything all day. = 我们一整天什么都没吃。


将来时:
I won't go to the concert. = 我不会去音乐会。
He won't be happy about this. = 他不会为这事高兴。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have to work, I would travel the world. = 如果我不必工作，我会环游世界。
I wish I didn't have so much work. = 我真希望我没有这么多工作。


被动语态:
The problem wasn't solved. = 问题没有解决。
The letter wasn't delivered. = 信没有寄到。


疑问句:
Don't you like pizza? = 你不喜欢披萨吗？
Doesn't she know the answer? = 她不知道答案吗？


否定句:
I don't believe it. = 我不相信。
He doesn't care. = 他不在乎。



PHRASE->
don't worry = 别担心
don't know = 不知道
don't mind = 不介意
don't forget = 别忘了
don't miss = 不要错过
