This can apply to physical objects or statements. |例句：The retractive claws of the cat allowed it to climb easily. 猫的可伸缩爪子让它很容易攀爬。

The company issued a retractive statement, correcting their earlier claims. 公司发布了一份更正声明，纠正了他们之前的声明。
