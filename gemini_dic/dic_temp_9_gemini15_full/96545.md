indoor:/ˌɪnˈdɔːr/| adj.|室内的，户内的|Existing or occurring within a building or other enclosed area.  It refers to activities, spaces, or things that are located inside a building.|例句：We played indoor games because it was raining. 我们因为下雨而在室内玩游戏。|例句：The indoor pool is open to the public. 室内游泳池对公众开放。|近义词：inside, interior, internal|反义词：outdoor, outside
