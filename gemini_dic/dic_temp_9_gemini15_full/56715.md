doomy:/ˈduːmi/| adj.|厄运的，不祥的，阴沉的|Descriptive of something that evokes a sense of doom, gloom, or impending disaster. It suggests a feeling of foreboding and negativity, often associated with dark and oppressive atmospheres.|例句：The doomy atmosphere of the abandoned factory unsettled me.  废弃工厂里阴森恐怖的气氛让我不安。

The doomy prophecy foretold a great catastrophe.  那则不祥的预言预示着一场巨大的灾难。|近义词：gloomy, ominous, foreboding, threatening, depressing|反义词：bright, cheerful, optimistic, hopeful, auspicious
