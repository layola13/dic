duking:/ˈduːkɪŋ/| v.|决斗；竞争；猛击；殴打 |To engage in a fight or competition, often implying a forceful or aggressive manner.  It can also refer to a forceful blow.|例句：They were duking it out in the boxing ring.他们在拳击台上激烈搏斗。

例句：The two companies are duking it out for market share. 这两家公司正在争夺市场份额。


近义词：battling, fighting, competing, wrestling, slugging

反义词：cooperating, collaborating, conceding


USAGE->
