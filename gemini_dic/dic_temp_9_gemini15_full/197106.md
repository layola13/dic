shammy:/ˈʃæmi/| n.|擦皮，鹿皮|A shammy is a soft, absorbent leather, originally made from chamois leather, now often made from synthetic materials, used for cleaning and polishing.  It's known for its ability to absorb large amounts of liquid. |例句：I used a shammy to dry the car after washing it.  我洗车后用擦皮擦干了车。
例句：The window cleaner used a shammy to leave the glass sparkling.  清洁工用擦皮擦拭玻璃，使其闪闪发光。|近义词：chamois, suede, microfiber cloth|反义词：None specifically, but words implying roughness or lack of absorbency could be considered opposites.|名词
