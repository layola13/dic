reprint:/ˌriːˈprɪnt/| v. n. |再版，重印|To print again; a new printing of a book, article, etc.|例句：The newspaper reprinted the article from the magazine.  报纸转载了杂志上的那篇文章。|例句：They released a reprint of his classic novel. 他们出版了他的经典小说再版。|近义词：reissue, reproduce|反义词：first edition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reprint the book annually. = 他们每年都重印这本书。
He reprints articles from old newspapers. = 他重印旧报纸上的文章。

一般过去时:
The publisher reprinted the book after it sold out. = 这本书卖光后，出版商又重印了一批。
She reprinted the essay for her portfolio. = 她为自己的作品集重印了这篇论文。

现在完成时:
They have reprinted the bestseller three times this year. = 今年他们已经三次重印了这本畅销书。
The magazine has reprinted the interview several times. = 这家杂志已经多次重印了这次采访。

过去完成时:
The company had reprinted the brochure before the conference. = 公司在会议之前已经重印了宣传册。
They had reprinted the book several times before realizing the error. = 他们在意识到错误之前已经重印了这本书好几次。

将来时:
We will reprint the flyer next week. = 我们下周将重新印刷传单。
The company will reprint the report if necessary. = 公司如有必要将重新印刷报告。

虚拟语气:
If we had more funds, we would reprint the magazine. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们将重印杂志。
I wish they had reprinted my article. = 我希望他们能重印我的文章。

被动语态:
The article was reprinted in several newspapers. = 这篇文章在几家报纸上被转载。
The book is being reprinted because of high demand. = 由于需求量大，这本书正在被重印。

疑问句:
Will the book be reprinted? = 这本书会再版吗？
Has the article been reprinted yet? = 这篇文章已经被重印了吗？

否定句:
They did not reprint the book this year. = 他们今年没有重印这本书。
The magazine will not reprint the controversial article. = 这本杂志不会重印这篇有争议的文章。
