marry:/ˈmæri/ | vt. & n. |结婚，嫁，娶 | To join in marriage; to give in marriage.  |例句：They married last year. 他们去年结婚了。|例句：She married a doctor. 她嫁给了一位医生。|近义词：wed, unite, espouse |反义词：divorce, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He marries her next month. = 他下个月和她结婚。
They marry in a church. = 他们在教堂结婚。

一般过去时:
They married in a small town. = 他们在小镇上结婚了。
She married him ten years ago. = 她十年前嫁给了他。

现在完成时:
They have married for five years. = 他们结婚五年了。
We have been married for 20 years. = 我们结婚20年了。

过去完成时:
They had married before I met them. = 我认识他们之前他们就已经结婚了。
She had already married when he proposed. = 他求婚时她已经结婚了。

将来时:
They will marry next spring. = 他们将在明年春天结婚。
He will marry her soon. = 他很快就要娶她了。

虚拟语气:
If I were to marry, I would marry someone kind. = 如果我要结婚，我会嫁给一个善良的人。
I wish I had married sooner. = 我真希望我早点结婚。

被动语态:
She was married last year. = 她去年结婚了。
The couple were married in a beautiful ceremony. = 这对夫妇在美丽的仪式上结婚了。

疑问句:
Are you married? = 你结婚了吗？
When did they get married? = 他们什么时候结婚的？

否定句:
They are not married. = 他们没有结婚。
She didn't marry him because she didn't love him. = 她没有嫁给他，因为她不爱他。


PHRASE->

marry off: 把...嫁出去
married life:已婚生活
marry into: 嫁入/娶入
