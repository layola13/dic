non-greasy:/ˌnɑːnˈɡriːzi/| adj.|不油腻的，不油滑的|Not greasy; not oily.  This describes something that doesn't have an oily or greasy texture or feel.  |例句：This lotion is non-greasy and absorbs quickly. 这款乳液不油腻，吸收很快。

例句：I prefer non-greasy sunscreens because they don't leave my skin feeling sticky. 我更喜欢不油腻的防晒霜，因为它们不会让我的皮肤感觉粘腻。


近义词：oil-free, non-oily, light


反义词：greasy, oily


词性：形容词
