equivocates:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪts/ | v. |含糊其辞；模棱两可地说；支吾其词 | To use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.  |例句：The politician equivocated when asked about his stance on the new tax bill.  这位政客在被问及他对新税收法案的立场时含糊其辞。

例句：He equivocated throughout the interview, never giving a straight answer. 他在整个采访中都含糊其辞，从未给出直接的答案。

近义词：prevaricate, hedge, obfuscate, tergiversate

反义词：clarify, elucidate, state clearly

词性:动词
