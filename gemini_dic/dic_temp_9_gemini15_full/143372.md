outfeat:/ˈaʊtfiːt/ | n. |装备，服装，全套装备 | An outfit is a set of clothes worn together, especially a particular style of clothing.  It can also refer to a complete set of equipment for a particular purpose. |例句：She wore a beautiful red outfit to the party. 她穿着一套美丽的红色衣服去参加派对。

例句：The climber had a complete outfit for scaling Mount Everest.  这位登山者有一套完整的装备用于攀登珠穆朗玛峰。

|近义词：gear, equipment, attire, apparel, ensemble |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to the context, such as "lack of equipment" or "plain clothing". |名词
