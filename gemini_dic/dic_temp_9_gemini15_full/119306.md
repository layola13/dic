microaneurysm: /ˌmaɪkroʊəˈnʊərɪzəm/| n. |微动脉瘤|A very small aneurysm, usually less than 100 micrometers in diameter, that occurs in the small blood vessels of the retina.  It's often a sign of diabetic retinopathy. |例句：The ophthalmologist detected several microaneurysms during the retinal examination. 眼科医生在视网膜检查中发现了几个微动脉瘤。|例句：The presence of numerous microaneurysms is a strong indicator of diabetic retinopathy.  许多微动脉瘤的存在是糖尿病性视网膜病变的有力指标。|近义词： retinal aneurysm (if in the retina) |反义词：None specifically, but the absence of microaneurysms would indicate a lack of the condition they signify.|名词
