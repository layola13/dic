launderer:/ˈlɔːndərər/| n. |洗衣工；洗衣店；洗钱者 |A person or business that washes and irons clothes.  Also, someone who engages in money laundering. |例句：The launderer returned my shirts perfectly pressed. 洗衣工把我的衬衫熨得整整齐齐地还给了我。|例句：He was arrested for being a launderer in a large-scale money laundering operation. 他因参与大规模洗钱活动而被捕。|近义词：washer, cleaner, money launderer |反义词：N/A
