reimmersed:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrsd/| v.|重新沉浸，再次沉浸|To immerse again; to submerge again.  It implies a previous immersion and a renewed experience of being surrounded or enveloped.|例句：After a brief respite, she reimmersed herself in her work.  (短暂休息后，她又全身心地投入工作。)

例句：He reimmersed his hands in the freezing water to retrieve the lost ring. (为了取回丢失的戒指，他又把手伸进了冰冷的水里。)


近义词：submerge again, immerse anew,  redesign, redo

反义词：emerge, extract, remove
