例句：

The professor overillustrated the concept, losing the students in a sea of unnecessary details.  教授对这个概念解释得过于冗长，学生们都听得云里雾里。

He overillustrated his point with so many examples that the main argument was lost. 他用太多例子来阐述他的观点，以至于主要的论点都模糊了。


近义词：overexplain, overelaborate, belabor, overdetail
