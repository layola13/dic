polypite:/ˈpɒlɪpaɪt/| n. |息肉，水螅体 |A polypite is an individual polyp in a colonial organism, such as a coral or hydroid.  It is a small, cylindrical animal with a mouth surrounded by tentacles.  Polypites are typically sessile, meaning they are attached to a substrate and do not move around.  They often live in colonies, with individual polypites connected to each other by a shared structure. |Example: The coral reef was composed of thousands of individual polypites. 这座珊瑚礁由数千个独立的息肉组成。|近义词：polyp (less specific)|反义词：N/A
