milksops:/ˈmɪlksɒps/| n.|娘娘腔，软弱的人|A milksop is a person, typically a man, who is considered to be weak, effeminate, or cowardly.  The term is often used derisively.  |例句：He's such a milksop; he's afraid of his own shadow. 他真是个娘娘腔，连自己的影子都怕。
He called his opponent a milksop for backing down from the challenge. 他称他的对手为懦夫，因为他退出了挑战。|近义词：wimp, weakling, sissy, coward|反义词：brave, courageous, strong, tough
