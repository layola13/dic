self-regulation: /ˌsɛlfˌreɡjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 自我调节；自我管理 | The ability to control one's own behavior, emotions, or thoughts.  It involves monitoring one's own actions and making adjustments as needed to achieve goals. |  例句：Effective self-regulation is crucial for academic success.  有效的自我调节对于学业成功至关重要。

例句：The child demonstrated impressive self-regulation by calming himself down after a tantrum. 这个孩子在发脾气后能够让自己平静下来，展现了令人印象深刻的自我调节能力。


近义词：self-control, self-discipline, self-governance, autonomy

反义词：impulsivity, lack of control, recklessness


词源:

* **self-**:  From Old English *self, *sylf, meaning "self, same."
* **regulation**: From Late Latin *regulationem,  from *regulare, meaning "to make straight, regulate," from *regula, meaning "rule, line."
