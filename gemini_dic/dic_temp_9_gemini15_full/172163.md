pursuance:/pərˈsuːəns/| n.|追求；实行；进行|The act of pursuing something; the continued effort to achieve something.|例句：The company is pursuing its expansion plans with vigor.  这家公司正积极地推进其扩张计划。|例句：In pursuance of its objectives, the organization launched a new initiative.  为了实现其目标，该组织启动了一项新的举措。|近义词： pursuit, prosecution, implementation |反义词： abandonment, neglect |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is in pursuance of its goals. = 公司正在追求其目标。
She is in pursuance of her dreams. = 她正在追求她的梦想。

一般过去时:
The project was completed in pursuance of the contract. = 该项目根据合同完成了。
They acted in pursuance of their beliefs. = 他们按照自己的信仰行事。

现在完成时:
They have been in pursuance of justice for years. = 他们多年来一直在追求正义。
The police have been in pursuance of the criminal. = 警方一直在追捕罪犯。

过去完成时:
The investigation had been in pursuance of a serious crime. = 调查是针对一起严重犯罪进行的。
He had acted in pursuance of his father's wishes. = 他按照父亲的意愿行事。

将来时:
They will be in pursuance of a new strategy next year. = 他们明年将采用新的战略。
The company will be in pursuance of higher profits. = 公司将追求更高的利润。

虚拟语气:
If they were in pursuance of their rights, they would have won the case. = 如果他们坚持自己的权利，他们就会赢得官司。
If she had been in pursuance of her studies, she would have graduated. = 如果她一直认真学习，她就会毕业了。

被动语态:
The plan was implemented in pursuance of the government's policy. = 该计划是根据政府的政策实施的。
The investigation is being conducted in pursuance of the law. = 调查正在依法进行。

疑问句:
Is the company in pursuance of ethical standards? = 公司是否在遵守道德标准？
Are they acting in pursuance of the rules? = 他们是否按照规则行事？

否定句:
The company is not in pursuance of its initial plan. = 公司没有按照最初的计划行事。
He was not acting in pursuance of the law. = 他不是依法行事。


PHRASE->
in pursuance of = 为了…；根据…；按照…
pursuance of justice = 对正义的追求
pursuance of happiness = 对幸福的追求
pursuance of knowledge = 对知识的追求
pursuance of a goal = 追求一个目标
