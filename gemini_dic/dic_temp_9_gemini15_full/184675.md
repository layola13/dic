restructuring: /ˌriːˈstrʌkʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 重组，改组，结构调整 | The process of reorganizing a company or system to improve efficiency or effectiveness.  It often involves changes to management, staffing, or operations. | 例句：The company is undergoing a major restructuring to cut costs. = 公司正在进行大规模重组以削减成本。

例句：The restructuring of the education system led to improved learning outcomes. = 教育体制的改革带来了更好的学习成果。


近义词：reorganization, reorganization, revamp, overhaul | 反义词：preservation, maintenance | 词性：名词
