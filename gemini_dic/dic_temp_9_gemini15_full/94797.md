impugns:/ɪmˈpjuːn/| vt.| 攻击；质疑；对…表示怀疑 | To attack the truth, honesty, or validity of (something); to challenge or question. |例句：He impugned the witness's credibility. 他质疑证人的可信度。|例句：The defense attorney impugned the integrity of the police investigation.  辩护律师质疑了警方调查的完整性。|近义词：challenge, question, dispute, assail, attack |反义词：confirm, support, endorse, validate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impugns the accuracy of the report. 他质疑报告的准确性。
They impugn her motives. 他们质疑她的动机。

一般过去时:
She impugned his honesty in the meeting. 她在会议上质疑了他的诚实。
The article impugned the government's policies. 这篇文章质疑了政府的政策。

现在完成时:
He has impugned the integrity of several witnesses. 他已经质疑了几位证人的正直。
They have impugned the validity of the contract. 他们已经质疑了合同的有效性。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had impugned the defendant's alibi before the jury. 律师在陪审团面前质疑了被告的不在场证明。
She had impugned the accuracy of his research. 她已经质疑了他研究的准确性。


将来时:
They will impugn the results of the election. 他们将质疑选举的结果。
He will impugn her character in court. 他将在法庭上质疑她的品格。


虚拟语气:
If he impugned my integrity, I would sue him. 如果他质疑我的正直，我会起诉他。
I wish he hadn't impugned her motives. 我真希望他没有质疑她的动机。


被动语态:
His statements were impugned by the opposition. 他的陈述受到了反对派的质疑。
The judge's decision was impugned by many legal experts. 许多法律专家的质疑法官的决定。


疑问句:
Does he impugn the findings of the study? 他是否质疑了这项研究的结果？
Did they impugn the witness's testimony? 他们是否质疑了证人的证词？


否定句:
He did not impugn her qualifications. 他没有质疑她的资格。
They did not impugn the accuracy of the data. 他们没有质疑数据的准确性。
