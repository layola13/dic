posterior:/pɒˈstɪəriə(r)/| adj.|后面的，后部的；后面的，后来的|Situated behind or at the rear of something.  The posterior part of the building was less well-maintained.|例句：The posterior chamber of the eye is filled with vitreous humor. 眼球的后房充满玻璃体液。

例句：He suffered from posterior hip pain. 他患有髋关节后部疼痛。


近义词：rear, hind, back, subsequent|反义词：anterior, front, prior


词源：From Latin *posterior (comparative of *post “behind”), from *pos “after, behind”.
