pancake:/ˈpænkeɪk/| n. |薄煎饼|A thin, flat cake made from batter and fried on a hot surface.  Pancakes are typically served with butter, syrup, or fruit.|例句：I made a stack of pancakes for breakfast. 我做了很多薄煎饼当早餐。|例句：The children loved the fluffy pancakes. 孩子们喜欢蓬松的薄煎饼。|近义词：crepe (法式薄饼), fritter|反义词：无
