circle: /ˈsɜːrkl/ | n. v. | 圆圈；循环；环绕 |

英文解析:
As a noun, a circle refers to a round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from a fixed center.  As a verb, it means to move in a circle around something or someone, or to draw a circle around something.  It can also imply a recurring pattern or sequence of events.

例句:
The children drew circles in the sand. 孩子们在沙滩上画圆圈。

He circled the building several times before entering. 他在进入大楼前绕着大楼转了几圈。


近义词:
round, ring, loop, cycle, orbit

反义词:
square, rectangle, straight line


词性:
Noun, Verb
