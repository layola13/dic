outbuilt:/ˈaʊtˌbɪlt/| v.|建成…的附属建筑|To build a structure that is an addition or extension to an existing building.  It often implies a secondary or less important structure compared to the main building.|例句：They outbuilt a small shed to store their gardening tools. 他们建了一个小型棚屋来存放园艺工具。|近义词：extend, add on, annex|反义词：demolish, raze
