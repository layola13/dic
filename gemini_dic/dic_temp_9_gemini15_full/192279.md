screenshot: /ˈskriːnʃɒt/ | n. |屏幕截图，屏幕快照 |A digital image of what is displayed on a computer screen.  A screenshot is a picture taken of a computer screen.| 例句：I took a screenshot of the error message. = 我截取了错误信息的屏幕截图。

例句：Can you send me a screenshot of that? = 你能给我发一个那个的屏幕截图吗？


近义词：screen capture, screen grab | 反义词：None
