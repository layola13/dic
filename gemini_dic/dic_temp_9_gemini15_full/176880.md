reagents: /riˈeɪdʒənts/ | n. |试剂，反应物 | Substances or compounds that are used in a chemical reaction to produce other substances.  Reagents are typically added to a system to cause a chemical reaction or to test for the presence of a particular substance. | Example: The experiment requires several reagents, including sulfuric acid and sodium hydroxide.  这个实验需要几种试剂，包括硫酸和氢氧化钠。|近义词：chemicals, reactants |反义词：products
