herb:/hɜːrb/| n. |草药，香草|A plant whose leaves, flowers, or stems are used to flavor food or for medicinal purposes.  |Example: Rosemary is a fragrant herb often used in cooking.|近义词：plant, spice, botanical|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She uses fresh herbs in her cooking. = 她烹饪时使用新鲜香草。
The garden grows many different herbs. = 花园里种植着许多不同的草药。

一般过去时:
He added some herbs to the soup. = 他在汤里加了一些香草。
