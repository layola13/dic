preservability: /ˌprɛzɜːrveɪˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可保存性，可保存度 | The ability of something to be preserved or kept from decay or deterioration.  The capacity for something to remain in its original state or condition over time. |


例句：

The preservability of the ancient artifacts was a major concern for the archaeologists.  古代文物的可保存性是考古学家主要关注的问题。

The high preservability of this food product makes it ideal for long journeys.  这种食品的高保存性使其成为长途旅行的理想选择。


近义词：durability,  keep-ability,  longevity


反义词：perishability,  decay,  decomposition


词性：名词
