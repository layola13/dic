contravenes:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːnz/| /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːnz/| vt.|违反；违背|To contravene something means to act in a way that is against or breaks a law, rule, agreement, or principle.|The company's actions contravene international law. 这家公司的行为违反了国际法。|His behaviour contravenes school regulations. 他的行为违反了学校规章制度。|近义词：violate, infringe, breach|反义词：obey, comply, conform|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The action contravenes the rules. =  这个行为违反了规则。
His behaviour contravenes company policy. = 他的行为违反了公司政策。


一般过去时:
The driver contravened the traffic laws. = 司机违反了交通法规。
Their actions contravened the agreement. = 他们的行为违反了协议。


现在完成时:
He has contravened the terms of his contract. = 他违反了他的合同条款。
The company has repeatedly contravened environmental regulations. = 这家公司一再违反环境法规。


过去完成时:
They had contravened the law before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们就已经违法了。
She realised she had contravened company policy. = 她意识到她违反了公司政策。


将来时:
This action will contravene the agreement. =  此举将违反协议。
If he continues this behaviour, he will contravene the law. = 如果他继续这种行为，他将违反法律。


虚拟语气:
If he were to contravene the rules, he would face consequences. = 如果他违反规则，他将面临后果。
I wish he hadn't contravened the agreement. = 我真希望他没有违反协议。


被动语态:
The law was contravened by several individuals. =  这项法律被几个人违反了。
The rules have been contravened repeatedly. = 这些规则被反复违反。


疑问句:
Does this action contravene any regulations? = 这个行为违反了任何规章吗？
Does his behaviour contravene the code of conduct? = 他的行为违反了行为准则吗？


否定句:
His actions did not contravene the law. = 他的行为没有违反法律。
The company claims its actions do not contravene any regulations. = 公司声称其行为没有违反任何规定。


PHRASE->
contravene the law = 违反法律
contravene an agreement = 违反协议
contravene the rules = 违反规则
contravene a regulation = 违反规章
contravene the terms = 违反条款


词源:
From Middle French *contravenir, from Latin contra- ("against") + venire ("to come").
