moniliales:/ˌmɒnɪliˈeɪliːz/| n. |串珠菌目|A taxonomic order of fungi characterized by the production of chains of spores (conidia) that resemble a string of beads.  These fungi are commonly found in various environments and can be saprophytic, parasitic, or pathogenic. They are often involved in plant diseases. |例句：The genus *Penicillium*, known for producing penicillin, belongs to the order Moniliales.  串珠菌属，即青霉素的生产菌属，属于串珠菌目。|近义词：Hyphomycetes (a broader term)|反义词：N/A|名词
