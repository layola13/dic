prededucted:/ˌpriːdɪˈdʌktɪd/| adj.|预先扣除的，预先减去的|Already deducted in advance; subtracted beforehand.  This term is often used in financial contexts, referring to amounts taken from a total before a final calculation.|例句：The tax was prededucted from my salary.  我的工资中已经预先扣除了税款。|例句：The agency prededucted the service fee from the total payment. 代理机构已从总付款中预先扣除了服务费。|近义词：prepaid, discounted, deducted beforehand|反义词：post-deducted, added, included


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company prededucts taxes from employee salaries. = 公司从员工工资中预扣税款。
This system prededucts shipping costs from the final price. = 这个系统从最终价格中预先扣除运费。
