词源:

The word "forefatherly" is a combination of "forefather" and the adjectival suffix "-ly".  "Forefather" itself comes from "fore" (meaning "before" or "in front of") and "father" (meaning "ancestor").  Thus, "forefatherly" literally means "like or relating to ancestors who came before".
