cenotaphic:/ˌsɛnəˈtæfɪk/| adj.|空冢的；纪念碑式的；|Relating to or resembling a cenotaph; commemorative but not containing the remains of the deceased.  It suggests a memorial nature without containing the actual body.|The cenotaphic monument stood as a solemn reminder of the fallen soldiers. 空冢纪念碑庄严地提醒着人们阵亡的士兵。|近义词：memorial, commemorative|反义词：actual, real
