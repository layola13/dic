overgrazes:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪz/ | vt. |过度放牧|To graze (animals) to the point of damaging the vegetation.  This involves allowing livestock to consume vegetation at a rate that exceeds its ability to regenerate, leading to soil erosion, desertification, and loss of biodiversity.|例句：The sheep overgrazed the pasture, leaving it barren and exposed to erosion.  绵羊过度放牧牧场，使其变得贫瘠，并暴露于侵蚀之下。

The cattle overgrazed the land, causing significant environmental damage. 牛群过度放牧这片土地，造成了严重的环保问题。|近义词：overstock, deplete|反义词：undergraze, conserve
