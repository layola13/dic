says:/sɛz/ | /sez/| vt. |说，表示；表明|The sign says "No Parking". 这个标志写着“禁止停车”。She says she'll be there by noon. 她说她中午之前到。|近义词：states, declares, mentions, remarks |反义词：remains silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He says he's tired. 他说他累了。
She says she understands.  她说她明白了。

一般过去时:
He said he was going home. 他说他要回家了。
She said she needed help. 她说她需要帮助。

现在完成时:
He has said that many times. 他已经说过很多次了。
She has said nothing about it. 她对此什么也没说。

过去完成时:
He had said he would be late. 他说过他会迟到。
She had said she didn't know. 她说过她不知道。

将来时:
He will say it again tomorrow. 他明天会再说一遍。
She will say what she thinks. 她会说出她的想法。

虚拟语气:
He said he would help if he could. 他说如果可以的话他会帮忙。
She said she wished she had more time. 她说她希望有更多的时间。

被动语态:
It is said that he is a genius. 据说他是个天才。
It was said that the meeting was a success. 据说会议很成功。

疑问句:
What does he say? 他说什么？
What did she say to you? 她对你说了什么？

否定句:
He doesn't say much. 他话不多。
She didn't say anything. 她什么也没说。


PHRASE->
says so = 这么说
that's what she says = 她这么说
as the saying goes = 常言道
it goes without saying = 不言而喻
let's just say = 这么说吧
