retooled:/ˌriːˈtuːld/| vt.|重新设计；改造；改进|To change, modify, or improve something, especially a machine, process, or system, by making significant alterations.|例句：The factory was retooled to produce a new line of products.  工厂经过重新设计以生产新的产品线。|例句：The company retooled its marketing strategy after a period of declining sales. 公司在销售额下降一段时间后调整了其营销策略。|近义词：revamp, renovate, overhaul, modernize|反义词：deteriorate, worsen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retool their software every year. = 他们每年都会重新设计他们的软件。
The company retools its manufacturing process regularly. = 公司定期改造其制造流程。


一般过去时:
The car manufacturer retooled its assembly line. = 汽车制造商对其装配线进行了改造。
We retooled the marketing campaign after the poor initial results. = 在最初结果不佳后，我们重新调整了营销活动。


现在完成时:
They have retooled the entire production line. = 他们已经改造了整条生产线。
The company has retooled its image to appeal to a younger audience. = 公司已经重塑了形象以吸引更年轻的受众。


过去完成时:
They had retooled the engine before the race. = 他们在比赛前已经改造了发动机。
The team had retooled their strategy before the final presentation. = 团队在最终演示前已经调整了他们的策略。


将来时:
We will retool the website next month. = 我们下个月将重新设计网站。
The factory will retool its operations to increase efficiency. = 工厂将调整其运营以提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would retool the entire system. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们将改造整个系统。
It would be wise to retool the product line if sales continue to decline. = 如果销售额继续下降，明智的做法是改造产品线。


被动语态:
The software is being retooled to improve performance. = 软件正在被重新设计以提高性能。
The factory's production line was retooled last year. = 工厂的生产线去年进行了改造。


疑问句:
Will they retool the product this year? = 他们今年会重新设计产品吗？
Have they retooled their marketing approach? = 他们是否调整了他们的营销方法？


否定句:
They did not retool the machine. = 他们没有改造那台机器。
We haven't retooled our processes yet. = 我们还没有调整我们的流程。


PHRASE->
retool for = 为...重新设计
retool oneself = 提升自我技能
retool the curriculum = 更新课程
retool the workforce = 提升员工技能
retool a factory = 改造工厂
