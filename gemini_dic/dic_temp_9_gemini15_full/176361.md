re-filtered:/ˌriːˈfɪltərd/| adj.|再次过滤的，重新过滤的|Having been filtered again.  This adjective describes something that has undergone a second or subsequent filtering process.|The re-filtered water was crystal clear.  再次过滤的水清澈见底。|近义词：refiltered, purified, strained|反义词：unfiltered, impure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The coffee is re-filtered every morning. = 咖啡每天早上都会重新过滤。
This water is re-filtered for better quality. = 这水再次过滤以获得更好的品质。

一般过去时:
The technician re-filtered the oil yesterday. = 技师昨天重新过滤了油。
They re-filtered the wine before bottling it. = 他们在装瓶之前重新过滤了葡萄酒。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
