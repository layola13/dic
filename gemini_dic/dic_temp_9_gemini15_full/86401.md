hedonism:/ˈhiːdənɪzəm/ | n. |享乐主义 |The pursuit of pleasure and avoidance of pain as the ultimate aim in life.  享乐是人生的最终目标，追求快乐，避免痛苦。|例句：His hedonism led him down a path of self-destruction.他的享乐主义导致了他走向自我毁灭的道路。|近义词：pleasure-seeking, sensualism, epicureanism |反义词：asceticism, puritanism, self-denial |名词
