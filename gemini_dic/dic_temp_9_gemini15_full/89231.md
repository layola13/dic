homicides:/ˈhɒmɪsaɪdz/|/ˈhɑːmɪsaɪdz/| n. |凶杀案（复数）|The intentional taking of a human life.  It refers to multiple instances of murder.  |例句：The city saw a spike in homicides last year.  去年该市凶杀案激增。|例句：Police are investigating a series of unsolved homicides. 警方正在调查一系列未破获的凶杀案。|近义词：murders, killings, manslaughters (depending on context)|反义词：births, saves
