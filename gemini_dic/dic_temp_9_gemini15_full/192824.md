searates:/ˈsiːreɪts/| n. |海运费，海运运费 |Sea rates refer to the cost of transporting goods by sea.  This includes charges for freight, handling, and other related services.  The specific costs depend on factors such as the distance, volume, type of goods, and market conditions.|The sea rates for shipping containers from China to the US have increased recently. = 最近从中国到美国的集装箱海运费上涨了。 The company is trying to negotiate lower sea rates with its shipping partners. = 该公司正试图与它的航运合作伙伴协商降低海运费。|freight rates, shipping charges|land transport costs|名词
