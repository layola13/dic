reprimes:/riːˈpraɪmz/| vt.|再次责备；再次批评|To reprimand or rebuke again.|The teacher reprimanded the student for talking during class, and then reprimed him for not completing his homework.|近义词：rebuke, reprove, reproach|反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reprimands his children frequently. = 他经常责备他的孩子们。
She reprimands her employees for lateness. = 她因迟到而责备她的员工。

一般过去时:
The principal reprimanded the student for his behavior. = 校长因他的行为而责备了学生。
My boss reprimanded me for missing the deadline. = 我的老板因为我错过了截止日期而责备了我。

现在完成时:
He has reprimanded the same employee three times this month. = 这个月他已经三次责备了同一个员工。
The teacher has reprimanded several students for disruptive behavior. = 老师已经因扰乱秩序的行为责备了好几个学生。

过去完成时:
She had reprimanded him several times before he finally understood. = 在他最终明白之前，她已经责备了他好几次了。
The manager had reprimanded the team before the presentation. = 经理在演示之前已经责备了团队。

将来时:
I will reprimand him if he does it again. = 如果他再这样做，我会责备他。
The supervisor will reprimand the employees for their negligence. = 主管将因他们的疏忽而责备员工。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reprimand him immediately. = 如果我是你，我会立即责备他。
I wish I had reprimanded him sooner. = 我希望我早点责备他。


被动语态:
He was reprimanded for his unprofessional conduct. = 他因其不专业的行为而受到责备。
The students were reprimanded for cheating on the exam. = 学生们因考试作弊而受到责备。


疑问句:
Have you ever been reprimanded by your boss? = 你有没有被老板责备过？
Will he be reprimanded for his actions? = 他会因为他的行为而受到责备吗？


否定句:
The teacher did not reprimand the student. = 老师没有责备学生。
I will not reprimand you this time, but don't do it again. = 我这次不会责备你，但不要再做了。


PHRASE->
reprimand severely = 严厉斥责
reprimand publicly = 公开谴责
reprimand formally = 正式谴责
reprimand repeatedly = 反复谴责
receive a reprimand = 受到谴责
