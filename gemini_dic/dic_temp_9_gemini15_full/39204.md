congested:/kənˈdʒɛstɪd/| adj. |拥挤的，堵塞的 |blocked or filled to excess; overcrowded.|The highway was congested with holiday traffic.  高速公路因假日交通而拥堵。|The city streets were congested during rush hour.  在高峰时段，城市街道非常拥挤。|uncongested, clear, open|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roads are congested during rush hour. = 在高峰时段，道路很拥挤。
This area is congested with tourists in summer. = 夏季这个地区游客拥挤。


一般过去时:
The airport was congested yesterday because of a storm. = 由于暴风雨，机场昨天很拥挤。
The streets were congested after the concert. = 音乐会后，街道很拥挤。


现在完成时:
The traffic has been congested all morning. = 交通堵塞了一上午。
The hospital has been congested with patients since the outbreak. = 自疫情爆发以来，医院一直人满为患。


过去完成时:
The road had been congested before the accident. 事故发生前，道路就已经拥堵了。
The server had been congested before the website crashed. 网站崩溃前，服务器就已经过载了。


将来时:
The roads will be congested tomorrow due to the marathon. = 明天由于马拉松比赛，道路将会拥挤。
The city center will be congested during the festival. = 节日期间市中心将会很拥挤。


虚拟语气:
If the roads weren't congested, I would have arrived earlier. = 如果道路不拥挤，我就会早到。
I wish the airport wasn't so congested. = 我希望机场不要那么拥挤。


被动语态:
The network was congested by the high volume of requests. = 网络因大量请求而拥塞。
The highway is often congested by heavy trucks. = 高速公路经常被重型卡车堵塞。


疑问句:
Is the network congested? = 网络拥塞了吗？
Are the streets congested today? = 街道今天拥挤吗？


否定句:
The roads are not congested this morning. = 今早道路不拥挤。
The system is not congested at the moment. = 系统目前没有拥塞。
