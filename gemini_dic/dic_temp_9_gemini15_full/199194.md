silique:/ˈsɪlɪk/| n. |角果|A silique is a dry dehiscent fruit that is characteristic of plants in the family Brassicaceae (mustard family). It is a pod-like structure that opens along two sutures (seams) to release seeds.  It is typically long and slender, with a noticeable difference in length between the valves (the sides of the pod) and the replum (the central partition).|The gardener carefully harvested the siliques from the mustard plants.|pod, capsule| berry, achene|名词
