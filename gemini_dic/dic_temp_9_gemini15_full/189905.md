samplers:/ˈsæmplərz/| n.|采样器；样本；样品；（尤指）刺绣样本；试听样品|A sampler is a small, representative sample of something, often used to test or demonstrate a larger product or collection.  It can refer to various contexts, from fabric swatches to audio previews. |例句：The company sent us a sampler of their new perfume. 公司寄给了我们他们新香水的一个试用装。|例句：She showed me a sampler of her needlework. 她给我看她的一些刺绣作品。|近义词：sample, specimen, example|反义词：whole, entirety
