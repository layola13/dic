overemphasise:/ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | v. |过分强调；夸大 |To give too much importance or attention to something.  |He overemphasized the negative aspects of the situation, causing unnecessary anxiety. 他过分强调了情况的负面方面，引起了不必要的焦虑。|emphasize, stress, highlight |downplay, understate |动词


USAGE->

They overemphasize the importance of grades. 他们过分强调了成绩的重要性。


一般过去时:
