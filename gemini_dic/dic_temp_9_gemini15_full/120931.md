It is often used with negative connotations, implying disapproval or prejudice. |例句：The laws prohibiting miscegenation were eventually overturned. 种族通婚禁令最终被推翻了。|近义词：racial intermarriage, interracial marriage, biraciality |反义词：racial segregation, racial purity
