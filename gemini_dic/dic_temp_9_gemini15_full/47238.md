dazzling: /ˈdæzəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 耀眼的；令人眼花缭乱的；极好的 | Extremely impressive, attractive, or exciting.  |


英文解析:  Describes something so bright or impressive that it temporarily affects your vision or overwhelms your senses with its brilliance or beauty. It can also refer to something exceptionally skillful or impressive.


例句:

The dazzling display of fireworks lit up the night sky. = 耀眼的烟花表演照亮了夜空。

Her dazzling performance on stage captivated the audience. = 她舞台上令人眼花缭乱的表演迷住了观众。


近义词: brilliant, radiant, glittering, stunning, spectacular, impressive


反义词: dull, dim, unimpressive, ordinary


词性: adjective
