heeding:/ˈhiːdɪŋ/ | v. | 注意，留心，理睬 | Paying attention to; taking notice of; giving consideration to.  |例句：Heeding the warnings, they stayed indoors during the storm.  注意警告，他们在暴风雨期间待在室内。|近义词：attending to, minding, regarding, considering | 反义词：ignoring, disregarding, neglecting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heeds his doctor's advice. = 他听从医生的建议。
She heeds the warnings about the storm. = 她注意到了关于暴风雨的警告。

一般过去时:
They heeded the call for help. = 他们听到了求救的呼声。
He heeded my advice and changed his plans. = 他听取了我的建议并改变了他的计划。

现在完成时:
She has heeded my warnings repeatedly. = 她一再听从我的警告。
Have you heeded the instructions carefully? = 你仔细阅读说明了吗？


过去完成时:
They had heeded the early warning signs, so they were prepared. = 他们注意到了早期的预警信号，所以他们做好了准备。
I realized I should have heeded his advice earlier. = 我意识到我应该早点听他的劝告。

将来时:
He will heed the advice of his mentor. = 他将听从导师的建议。
She will heed the warnings and take precautions. = 她会注意警告并采取预防措施。

虚拟语气:
If he had heeded the warnings, the accident wouldn't have happened. = 如果他听了警告，事故就不会发生了。
I wish he had heeded my advice. = 我希望他听了我的建议。

被动语态:
The warnings were heeded by the sailors. = 水手们注意到了警告。

疑问句:

否定句:
