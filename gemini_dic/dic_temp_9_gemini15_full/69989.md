fetch's:/fɛtʃz/| v. |取来；拿来；抓来；吸引；达到；卖得(某价)|To bring or go to get something and bring it back.|The dog fetched the stick.  狗叼回了棍子。|He fetched a bucket of water. 他提来一桶水。|bring, retrieve, get|leave, lose, miss|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fetches the newspaper every morning. 他每天早上都去取报纸。
She fetches her children from school. 她去学校接她的孩子。


一般过去时:
I fetched the mail from the mailbox yesterday. 昨天我去邮箱取了邮件。
He fetched a doctor immediately after the accident. 事故发生后，他立即请来了医生。


现在完成时:
She has fetched the medicine from the pharmacy. 她已经从药店取来了药。
He has fetched water from the well several times. 他已经从井里打过几次水了。


过去完成时:
I had fetched the books before the meeting started. 会议开始前我已经把书拿来了。
She had fetched her coat before leaving the house. 她离开房子前已经拿了她的外套。


将来时:
I will fetch you from the airport. 我会去机场接你。
He will fetch the car from the garage tomorrow. 他明天将把车从车库里取出来。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would fetch it myself. 如果我有更多的时间，我会自己去取。
I wish I had fetched the umbrella before leaving. 我真希望离开前把伞拿了。


被动语态:
The documents were fetched by the messenger. 文件是由信使取来的。
The information was fetched from the server. 信息是从服务器上获取的。


疑问句:
Did you fetch the dry cleaning? 你去取干洗衣服了吗？
Will he fetch the children from school today? 他今天会去学校接孩子们吗？


否定句:
I didn't fetch the package today. 我今天没有取包裹。
She couldn't fetch the heavy box. 她搬不动那个沉重的箱子。
