incensant: /ɪnˈsɛsənt/ | adj. | 不停的；不断的；连续不断的 |  Continuing without interruption; ceaseless. |  例句：The incessant rain made it impossible to go outside.  不停的雨使得无法出门。


例句：His incessant complaints were driving everyone crazy. 他不停的抱怨让每个人都快要疯了。


近义词：ceaseless, constant, continuous, uninterrupted | 反义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain is incessant. = 雨下个不停。
The machine runs incessantly. = 机器不停地运转。


一般过去时:
The noise was incessant. = 噪音不断。
The phone rang incessantly. = 电话不停地响。


现在完成时:
The rain has been incessant all day. = 雨整天都下个不停。
The construction has been incessant for months. = 建筑工程已经持续了好几个月。


过去完成时:
The buzzing had been incessant before the lights went out. = 灯灭之前，嗡嗡声一直持续不断。
The complaints had been incessant until the problem was solved. = 直到问题解决，抱怨才停止。


将来时:
The work will be incessant until the project is finished. = 直到项目完成，工作才会停止。
The noise will be incessant throughout the night. = 整个晚上都会有持续不断的噪音。


虚拟语气:
If the noise were incessant, I would move. = 如果噪音持续不断，我会搬家。
I wish the rain would stop; it's incessant. = 我希望雨能停，它下个不停。


被动语态:
The city was plagued by incessant traffic. = 这个城市饱受不停的交通困扰。
The meeting was disrupted by incessant interruptions. = 会议因不停的打断而中断。


疑问句:
Is the noise incessant? = 噪音是持续不断的吗？
Is the construction incessant? = 建筑工程是持续不断的吗？


否定句:
The rain is not incessant; it stops occasionally. = 雨不是持续不断的；它偶尔会停。
The noise was not incessant; there were periods of silence. = 噪音并非持续不断；期间也有寂静的时刻。


PHRASE->
incessant rain =  持续不断的雨
incessant noise =  持续不断的噪音
incessant chatter =  持续不断的闲聊
incessant activity =  持续不断的活动
incessant demands =  持续不断的需求

词源:
The word "incessant" comes from the Late Latin word "incessans," which is a combination of the prefix "in-" (not) and the verb "cessare" (to cease, to stop).  Therefore, it literally means "not ceasing."
