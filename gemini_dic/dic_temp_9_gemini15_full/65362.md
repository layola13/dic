eulogizes:/ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzɪz/| vt.|赞扬；颂扬；悼词|To speak or write in praise of someone or something, especially at a funeral.|例句：The speaker eulogized the deceased as a great humanitarian.  演讲者赞扬死者是一位伟大的慈善家。
The newspaper eulogized the achievements of the retiring CEO. 报纸赞扬了即将退休的首席执行官的成就。|近义词：praise, commend, celebrate, extol|反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce
