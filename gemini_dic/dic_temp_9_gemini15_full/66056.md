exasperatingly:/ɪɡˈzɑːspəreɪtɪŋli/| adv. |令人恼怒地，使人厌烦地 | in an exasperating manner; in a way that causes irritation or annoyance. |例句：He waited exasperatingly for the bus. 他令人恼火地等公共汽车。  例句：She sighed exasperatingly. 她恼怒地叹了口气。|近义词：irritatingly, frustratingly, annoyingly |反义词：pleasantly, calmly, soothingly
