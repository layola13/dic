cochairwomen:/ˈkoʊtʃɛrwɪmən/| n.|女联合主席；女共同主席|The term "cochairwomen" refers to two or more women who jointly hold the position of chair or chairperson of an organization, committee, or event.  It emphasizes the shared leadership role of the women involved.|例句：The conference was successfully co-chaired by two prominent co-chairwomen in the field of medicine. 这场会议由医学领域两位杰出的女联合主席成功主持。|近义词：co-chairs, joint chairs, chairwomen, chairpersons|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a single chairperson.
