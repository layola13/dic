isography:/aɪˈsɒɡrəfi/| n.|等高线图；等值线图 |A map showing lines of equal value, such as elevation, temperature, or pressure.  It visually represents the spatial distribution of a particular phenomenon. | Example: The isography clearly illustrated the steep incline of the mountain. 这张等高线图清晰地展示了这座山的陡峭斜坡。|近义词：contour map, isopleth map |反义词：无
