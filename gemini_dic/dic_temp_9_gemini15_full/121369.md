misguidedly:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪdədli/ | adv. | 误导地；错误地 |  Acting in a way that is based on wrong understanding or information; wrongly.  |例句：He misguidedly believed that he could win the lottery. 他错误地认为自己能中彩票。

例句：She misguidedly trusted him with her money. 她愚蠢地把钱托付给了他。


近义词：erroneously, wrongly, mistakenly, incorrectly

反义词：correctly, rightly, accurately, wisely


词性：副词
