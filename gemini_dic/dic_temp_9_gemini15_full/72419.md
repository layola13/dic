fluffiness:/ˈflʌfɪnəs/| n. |柔软蓬松；轻盈感|The quality or state of being fluffy; lightness and softness, often of texture.  It describes a soft, light, airy, and feathery texture.|The fluffiness of the freshly baked bread was delightful.  这刚烤好的面包蓬松柔软，令人愉快。The kitten's fluffiness made it look incredibly cute.  小猫毛茸茸的，看起来非常可爱。|softness, lightness, airiness|hardness, density|名词
