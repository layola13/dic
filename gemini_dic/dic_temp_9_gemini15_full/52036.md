dialectology:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɒlədʒi/ | n. |方言学|The scientific study of dialects; the study of the geographical distribution of language variations and their social and historical development.  It investigates the features that distinguish one dialect from another, including pronunciation, vocabulary, and grammar. | Example:  Dialectology is a fascinating field that helps us understand how languages change and diversify over time and space. 方言学是一个引人入胜的领域，它帮助我们理解语言如何随着时间和空间的变化而变化和多样化。|近义词:linguistic geography, dialectometry|反义词:standard language studies
