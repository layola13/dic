ornithologists:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |鸟类学家 |Scientists who study birds. Ornithologists research various aspects of bird life, including their behavior, physiology, ecology, and evolution.  | Many ornithologists are involved in bird conservation efforts. 许多鸟类学家参与了鸟类保护工作。| zoologists, avian biologists |  (无明确反义词) | 名词

词源:
ornithologist源于希腊语"ornis"（鸟）和"logos"（研究）。  ornith-是组合形式，表示“鸟类”。 -ologist是表示“学家”的后缀。
