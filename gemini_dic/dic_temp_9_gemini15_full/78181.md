geoscopy:/dʒiˈɒskəpi/| n. |地质学；地质勘探|The study of the earth's crust and its composition, structure, and history, often involving techniques like seismic surveys and drilling to infer subsurface conditions.  Geoscopy is a less common term, often replaced by more specific terms like geophysics or geological surveying.|例句：Geoscopy revealed a significant deposit of iron ore beneath the surface. 地质勘探揭示了地表下有大量的铁矿石沉积。|近义词：geophysics, geological surveying, earth science|反义词：N/A
