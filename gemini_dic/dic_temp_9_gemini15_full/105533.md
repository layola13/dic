kilowatts:/ˈkɪləˌwɒts/|/ˈkɪləˌwɑːts/| n. |千瓦|A kilowatt (kW) is a unit of power equal to 1,000 watts.  It's used to measure the rate at which energy is used or produced.  For example, a large appliance might consume several kilowatts of power.|The air conditioner uses 2 kilowatts of electricity.  空调使用2千瓦的电力。|megawatts, watts|milliwatts, microwatts|名词
