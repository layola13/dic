inexpertly:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɜːrtli/ | adj. | 不熟练地；笨拙地 |  done or performed without skill or expertise; clumsily.  | 例句：He inexpertly attempted to fix the broken appliance. 他笨拙地试图修理坏掉的电器。

例句：She inexpertly applied the makeup, smudging it across her face. 她笨拙地化了妆，把妆弄花了。


近义词：awkwardly, clumsily, unskillfully, amateurishly


反义词：expertly, skillfully, proficiently


词源：in- (否定前缀) + expert (熟练的，内行的) + -ly (副词后缀)  表示“非熟练地”，“笨拙地”。
