mullerian:/mʌˈlɛəriən/| adj.|缪勒氏的；副中肾管的|Relating to the Müllerian ducts, a pair of ducts in the embryo that develop into the fallopian tubes, uterus, and part of the vagina in females.  The term is often used in the context of developmental biology and reproductive anatomy.|Example:  The mullerian ducts are crucial for female reproductive organ development.  缪勒氏管对女性生殖器官的发育至关重要。|近义词：paramesonephric|反义词：wolffian
