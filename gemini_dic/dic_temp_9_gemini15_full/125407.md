multipage:/ˈmʌltipeɪdʒ/| adj.|多页的；跨页的|Consisting of or extending over multiple pages.  A multipage document requires more time to read.|例句：This report is a multipage document detailing the company's financial performance.这份报告是一份多页文件，详细说明了公司的财务业绩。

例句：The contract is a multipage agreement, so please read it carefully.这份合同是一份多页协议，所以请仔细阅读。


近义词：multiple-page, many-paged, extended


反义词：single-page, one-page


词性:形容词
