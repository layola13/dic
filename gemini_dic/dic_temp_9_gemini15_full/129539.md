non-bituminous:/ˌnɑːn bɪˈtʃuːmɪnəs/ | adj. | 非沥青质的 | Not containing or composed of bitumen.  This term typically refers to materials like coal or rock that lack the tarry, black, viscous hydrocarbon substance known as bitumen. | Example: The non-bituminous coal burned cleaner than the bituminous variety.  非沥青质煤燃烧起来比沥青质煤更清洁。|近义词: asphalt-free, non-asphaltic | 反义词: bituminous
