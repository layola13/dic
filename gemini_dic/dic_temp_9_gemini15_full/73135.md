foraging:/ˈfɒrɪdʒɪŋ/ /ˈfɔːrɪdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|觅食；搜索；搜寻|The act of searching for food or provisions.  It can refer to animals searching for food in their natural environment, or humans searching for food in a more deliberate way, such as gathering wild plants or hunting.|例句：The squirrels were foraging for nuts in the park.  松鼠们正在公园里觅食坚果。

例句：She spent the morning foraging for mushrooms in the woods. 她上午在树林里采蘑菇。|近义词：searching, hunting, gathering, scavenging|反义词：providing, supplying|
