fascicule:/ˈfæsɪkjuːl/| n. |小册子；部分；束|A fascicule is a small booklet or pamphlet, often part of a larger work. It can also refer to a bundle or cluster of something, such as nerve fibers.|例句：The professor distributed a fascicule summarizing the key concepts. 教授分发了总结关键概念的小册子。  The fascicules of the nerve were carefully examined under the microscope. 神经束在显微镜下被仔细检查。|近义词：booklet, pamphlet, brochure, leaflet|反义词：tome, volume (in the sense of a large book)
