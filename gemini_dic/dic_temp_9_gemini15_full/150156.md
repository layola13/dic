pan-orthodox:/ˌpænˈɔːrθədɒks/| adj.|泛正统的，全正统的 | Relating to or including all Orthodox churches or groups.  The term refers to a broad unity encompassing the diverse traditions within Orthodoxy, aiming for cooperation and understanding across jurisdictional lines. | Example:  The pan-orthodox council discussed issues of ecumenism and interfaith dialogue.  泛正统会议讨论了普世主义和宗教间对话的问题。|近义词： ecumenical, universal (in a religious context) |反义词： sectarian, divisive
