kimbo:/ˈkɪmboʊ/| adj.| 拳击手姿势的；两臂弯曲的 |  Describes a posture where the arms are bent at the elbows and held away from the body, often with the fists clenched, similar to a boxer's stance.  The origin is uncertain, but it's likely from a combination of the words "kink" (a twist or bend) and "bow" (to bend).|例句：He stood there kimbo, ready for a fight. 他两臂弯曲地站在那里，准备战斗。 He sat with his arms kimbo. 他两臂弯曲地坐着。|近义词：bent-armed, akimbo|反义词：straight-armed
