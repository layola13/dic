celebration:/ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃən/ | n. |庆祝，庆祝活动 |The act of celebrating; a festive gathering or commemoration.  It involves expressing joy, happiness, or commemorating a significant event. |例句：The town held a big celebration for the anniversary.  这个小镇为周年纪念举办了一场盛大的庆祝活动。

例句：We had a small celebration for my daughter's birthday. 我们为女儿的生日举办了一个小型的庆祝活动。


近义词：festivity, commemoration, observance, party

反义词：mourning, lament, grieving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team celebrates every victory.  = 这个团队庆祝每一次胜利。
She celebrates her birthday every year. = 她每年都庆祝她的生日。


一般过去时:
They celebrated their anniversary last week. = 他们上周庆祝了他们的周年纪念日。
We celebrated Christmas with family. = 我们和家人一起庆祝了圣诞节。


现在完成时:
We have celebrated many achievements this year. = 今年我们庆祝了许多成就。
They have celebrated their success with a grand party. = 他们以盛大的派对庆祝他们的成功。


过去完成时:
They had celebrated their graduation before the party. = 他们在派对之前已经庆祝了他们的毕业典礼。
We had celebrated the new year before we went to bed. = 我们在睡觉前已经庆祝了新年。


将来时:
We will celebrate our wedding anniversary next month. = 我们下个月将庆祝我们的结婚周年纪念日。
They will celebrate their son's birthday next week. = 他们下周将庆祝他们儿子的生日。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would celebrate wildly. = 如果我中了彩票，我会疯狂庆祝。
I wish we could celebrate our success together. = 我希望我们能够一起庆祝我们的成功。


被动语态:
The victory was celebrated throughout the nation. = 胜利在全国各地得到庆祝。
The anniversary will be celebrated with a grand parade. = 周年纪念日将以盛大的游行来庆祝。


疑问句:
How will you celebrate your birthday? = 你将如何庆祝你的生日？
What kind of celebration are you planning? = 你计划什么样的庆祝活动？


否定句:
We didn't celebrate Christmas this year. = 今年我们没有庆祝圣诞节。
They didn't celebrate their success properly. = 他们没有好好庆祝他们的成功。


PHRASE->
a birthday celebration = 生日庆祝
a wedding celebration = 婚礼庆祝
a victory celebration = 胜利庆祝
a holiday celebration = 节日庆祝
a graduation celebration = 毕业庆祝

词源:
From Old French *celebracion, from Latin celebratio(n-), from celebrare "to perform, accomplish, celebrate," from *celeber "famous, renowned".  The sense development is from "to make famous" to "to honor" to "to celebrate."
