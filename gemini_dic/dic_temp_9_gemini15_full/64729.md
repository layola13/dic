eschewer:/ɪˈskuːər/| v.|避免者；避开者|One who eschews something avoids or shuns it.|He is an eschewer of all things luxurious. 他是一个避免所有奢侈品的人。|近义词：avoider, shunners, abstainer|反义词：seeker, pursuer|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eschews all forms of violence. = 他避免一切形式的暴力。
She eschews gossip. = 她避免八卦。


一般过去时:
They eschewed the use of chemicals in their farming. = 他们在农业中避免使用化学品。
He eschewed a life of luxury. = 他避免了奢侈的生活。


现在完成时:
She has eschewed meat for many years. = 她多年来一直避免吃肉。
We have eschewed political involvement. = 我们一直避免卷入政治。


过去完成时:
He had eschewed the offer before it was formally made. = 在这个提议正式提出之前，他已经拒绝了。
They had eschewed all contact with the outside world. = 他们已经避免了与外界的任何接触。


将来时:
She will eschew any temptation to cheat. = 她将避免任何作弊的诱惑。
He will eschew risky investments. = 他将避免高风险投资。


虚拟语气:
If he were wiser, he would eschew such risky behavior. = 如果他更聪明些，他会避免这种冒险行为。
I wish I had eschewed that tempting dessert. = 我真希望我没有吃那个诱人的甜点。


被动语态:
Dangerous activities should be eschewed. = 应该避免危险的活动。
Such practices are generally eschewed by responsible companies. = 负责任的公司通常会避免这种做法。


疑问句:
Does he eschew all forms of social media? = 他避免使用所有形式的社交媒体吗？
Will they eschew the opportunity to travel abroad? = 他们会放弃出国旅行的机会吗？


否定句:
He doesn't eschew spicy food. = 他不吃辣的食物。
They didn't eschew the opportunity to make money. = 他们没有放弃赚钱的机会。
