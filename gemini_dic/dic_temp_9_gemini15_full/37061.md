combatants:/kəmˈbætənts/ | n. |战斗人员，交战者 |People, groups, or countries involved in fighting a war or battle.  |例句：The combatants agreed to a ceasefire.  战斗人员同意停火。|例句：Both combatants suffered heavy losses. 双方交战者都遭受了重大损失。|近义词：fighters, warriors, belligerents |反义词：civilians, non-combatants
