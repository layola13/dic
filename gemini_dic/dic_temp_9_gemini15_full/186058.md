rewritable:/ˌriːˈraɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可重写的，可改写的 | Capable of being written on again after being erased or deleted.  This term is commonly used in the context of computer storage media.  | Example:  This is a rewritable CD, so you can use it multiple times. 这张光盘是可重写的，所以你可以多次使用它。  | 近义词: erasable, reusable | 反义词: read-only, permanent


USAGE->

The program is rewritable. = 这个程序是可重写的。
This CD is rewritable. = 这张光盘是可重写的。

一般过去时:
The data was rewritable before the update. = 更新之前数据是可重写的。
The old system was rewritable, but the new one isn't. = 旧系统是可重写的，但新系统不是。

The information has been rewritable since the software upgrade. = 自软件升级以来，信息一直是可重写的。
