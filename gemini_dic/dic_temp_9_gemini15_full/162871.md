precedently:/ˈprɛsɪdɛntli/| adv.|以前地，先前地 | Previously; before; in the past.  The word implies a prior occurrence or example. |例句：Precedently, such a situation had never arisen. 以前从未发生过这种情况。  |例句：Precedently, the company had a strict no-smoking policy.  以前，公司有严格的禁烟政策。|近义词：previously, formerly, earlier, before, antecedently |反义词： subsequently, later, hereafter


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We precedently discussed this matter. = 我们以前讨论过这件事。
She precedently worked in that company. = 她以前在那家公司工作过。

一般过去时:
They precedently agreed to the terms. = 他们以前同意了这些条款。
He precedently held a similar position. = 他以前担任过类似的职位。

现在完成时:
We have precedently addressed this issue. = 我们以前已经处理过这个问题。
She has precedently expressed her concerns. = 她以前已经表达过她的担忧。

过去完成时:
They had precedently warned us about the dangers. = 他们以前已经警告过我们危险。
He had precedently applied for the job.  = 他以前已经申请过这份工作。

将来时:
We will precedently consider your proposal. = 我们将事先考虑你的提案。(This usage is less common and might sound slightly awkward.  It implies considering something based on past experience.)
They will precedently review the documents. = 他们会预先审查文件。(Similar to above, this usage is less common and might sound awkward.)


虚拟语气:
If we had precedently known this, we would have acted differently. = 如果我们以前知道这一点，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish we had precedently consulted the experts. = 我希望我们以前咨询过专家。


被动语态:
The matter was precedently discussed by the committee. = 这件事以前由委员会讨论过。
The issue had been precedently addressed by the previous administration. = 这个问题以前已被前任政府处理过。


疑问句:
Had they precedently considered the alternatives? = 他们以前是否考虑过其他方案？
Was the problem precedently identified? = 这个问题以前是否已经被发现？


否定句:
This situation has not precedently occurred. =这种情况以前从未发生过。
They did not precedently inform us of the changes. = 他们以前没有通知我们这些变化。
