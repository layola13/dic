comfortably:/ˈkʌmftəbli/ | adj. |舒适地，舒服地 |Feeling or expressing physical comfort and ease.  It describes a state of being relaxed, at ease, and free from discomfort. |例句：She settled comfortably into the armchair. 她舒服地坐在扶手椅上。|例句：The temperature was comfortably warm.  温度舒适宜人。|近义词：cozily, pleasantly, easily |反义词：uncomfortably, painfully, awkwardly
