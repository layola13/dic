fob:/fɒb/ /fɑːb/| n. v.|欺骗，骗取；(旧)小挂坠；(口)傻瓜；|英文解析：As a noun, "fob" can refer to a small pocket watch, a chain attached to a pocket watch, or a small ornament attached to a chain.  As a verb, it means to deceive or cheat someone, often by giving them something worthless or inferior in place of something valuable.  It can also be used informally as a derogatory term for a foolish or stupid person.|例句：He tried to fob off the faulty goods on me. 他试图把有缺陷的货物推销给我。

He wore a fob watch on his waistcoat. 他在背心里戴着一块怀表。


近义词：trick, deceive, cheat, dupe, swindle |反义词：honesty, truthfulness|词性:n. v.
