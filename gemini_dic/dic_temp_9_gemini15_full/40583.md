conus:/ˈkoʊnəs/| n.|圆锥体；锥；（某些软体动物的）壳锥|A cone-shaped structure or part.  The term often refers to anatomical structures resembling a cone.  In geology, it can refer to a volcanic cone. In zoology, it may refer to the conical shell of certain mollusks.|例句：The pine tree's shape resembles a conus. 这棵松树的形状像一个圆锥体。

例句：The volcano's conus was steep and imposing. 火山的锥体又陡峭又壮观。


近义词：cone, apex, peak|反义词：base, bottom
