kilning:/ˈkɪlɪŋ/| n. |烧制；焙烧；窑烧|The process of firing bricks, pottery, or other materials in a kiln.  Kilning is a crucial step in ceramic production.|例句：The kilning process takes several hours at high temperatures. 烧制过程需要几个小时的高温。

The successful kilning of the pottery depended on precise temperature control. 陶器的成功烧制取决于精确的温度控制。|近义词：firing, baking, calcining|反义词：cooling

一般现在时:
The potter kilns the clay pots every week. 陶工每周烧制陶罐。
They kiln the bricks at a high temperature. 他们高温烧制砖块。

一般过去时:
The kiln fired the bricks yesterday. 窑炉昨天烧制了砖块。
He kilned the pottery in the new kiln. 他在新窑里烧制陶器。

现在完成时:
We have kilned all the pottery for this season. 我们已经烧制了本季的所有陶器。
They have kilned the bricks for the new building. 他们已经烧制了新建筑的砖块。

过去完成时:
They had kilned the bricks before the rain started. 下雨前他们已经烧制好了砖块。
The artist had kilned the sculptures before the exhibition. 艺术家在展览前已经烧制好了雕塑。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
