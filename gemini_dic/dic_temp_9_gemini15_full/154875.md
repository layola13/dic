perverters:/pərˈvɜːrtərz/ | n. | 歪曲者；败坏者；堕落者 | Those who corrupt, distort, or pervert something, especially morals or principles.  They can also refer to individuals who engage in sexual perversion. | 例句：The perverters of justice will be punished.  正义的歪曲者将受到惩罚。

The perverters of truth spread misinformation. 歪曲真相的人传播虚假信息。

| 近义词：corrupters, depravers, defilers, deviants | 反义词：upholders, protectors, reformers |  无
