cross-infection:/ˈkrɒsɪnˈfɛkʃən/| n.|交叉感染，交互感染|The transmission of an infectious agent from one individual to another, often indirectly through a contaminated intermediary.|例句：Hospitals are always at risk of cross-infection. 医院总是面临交叉感染的风险。|例句：Strict hygiene protocols are essential to prevent cross-infection. 严格的卫生规程对于预防交叉感染至关重要。|近义词：secondary infection, contagious infection|反义词：sterile, aseptic
