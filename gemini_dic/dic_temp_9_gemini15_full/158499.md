plenish:/ˈplɛnɪʃ/| vt.|补充，再填满|To replenish something means to fill it up again.  It often refers to resources or supplies that have been used or depleted.|例句：We need to replenish our stock of coffee. 我们需要补充我们的咖啡库存。

例句：The farmer replenished the bird feeders with seeds.  农民用种子补充了鸟食器。


近义词：restock, refill, replenish, supplement, complete


反义词：deplete, exhaust, empty


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store replenishes its shelves daily. = 商店每天补充货架上的商品。
She replenishes her water bottle after her run. = 她跑步后补充了水壶里的水。


一般过去时:
He replenished the sugar bowl after breakfast. = 早餐后，他补充了糖罐里的糖。
They replenished the fuel tanks before the long drive. = 长途驾驶前，他们补充了油箱里的燃料。


现在完成时:
I have replenished my savings account this month. = 这个月我已经补充了我的储蓄账户。
She has replenished the first-aid kit with bandages. = 她已经用绷带补充了急救箱。


过去完成时:
He had replenished the ink cartridge before he started printing. = 他在开始打印之前已经补充了墨盒。
They had replenished their supplies before embarking on the expedition. = 他们在开始探险之前已经补充了他们的物资。


将来时:
We will replenish the inventory next week. = 我们下周将补充库存。
They will replenish the water supply after the drought. = 干旱过后，他们将补充水供应。


虚拟语气:
If they replenished the stock, we wouldn't be short. = 如果他们补充库存，我们就不会短缺。
I wish I had replenished my gas tank earlier. = 我希望我早点补充汽油。


被动语态:
The water tank was replenished overnight. = 水箱在夜间被补充满了。
The shelves are replenished every morning. = 货架每天早上都被补充商品。


疑问句:
Will they replenish the supplies soon? = 他们很快会补充物资吗？
Have you replenished the printer ink yet? = 你已经补充打印机墨水了吗？


否定句:
They didn't replenish the shelves in time. = 他们没有及时补充货架上的商品。
He hasn't replenished his coffee yet. = 他还没补充咖啡。


PHRASE->

replenish the energy = 补充能量
replenish the soil = 补充土壤
replenish the body = 补充身体所需
