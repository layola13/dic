freedom: /ˈfriːdəm/ | n. | 自由，自主 | The power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants without hindrances.  Freedom encompasses various aspects, including political freedom, economic freedom, and personal freedom.  | Example: The birds soared in freedom across the vast sky.  鸟儿在广阔的天空中自由飞翔。  She cherished her freedom to choose her own career path. 她珍视选择自己职业道路的自由。|近义词：liberty, independence, autonomy | 反义词：slavery, captivity, constraint |  名词

一般现在时:
He enjoys freedom of speech. = 他享受言论自由。
We value our freedom of thought. = 我们珍视我们的思想自由。


一般过去时:
They fought for freedom from oppression. = 他们为摆脱压迫而争取自由。
She gained her freedom after many years of imprisonment. = 她被监禁多年后获得了自由。


现在完成时:
They have achieved freedom from poverty. = 他们已经摆脱了贫困。
She has finally found freedom from her abusive relationship. = 她终于从虐待她的关系中解脱出来了。


过去完成时:
They had already tasted freedom before the revolution. = 革命前他们就已经尝到了自由的滋味。
She had long yearned for freedom before she escaped. = 在逃脱之前，她渴望自由很久了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had freedom, I would travel the world. = 如果我有自由，我会环游世界。
I wish I had the freedom to pursue my dreams. = 我希望我有自由去追求我的梦想。


被动语态:
Freedom was denied to them for many years. = 多年来，他们被剥夺了自由。
Their freedom is being threatened by the government. = 他们的自由正受到政府的威胁。


疑问句:

否定句:
