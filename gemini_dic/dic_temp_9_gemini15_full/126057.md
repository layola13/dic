mustard:/ˈmʌstərd/| n. |芥末，芥末酱 |A pungent condiment made from the seeds of a mustard plant.  It is commonly used as a seasoning for food.| 例句：I love to put mustard on my hot dogs. 我喜欢在我的热狗上放芥末。

例句：The sandwich was delicious with a little mustard.  三明治加一点芥末就很好吃了。


近义词：condiment, relish | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "mustard" comes from the Old French word "mostaerde," which itself derives from the Latin "mustum ardens," meaning "burning must."  "Mustum" refers to the unfermented grape juice, and "ardens" means "burning," referring to the pungent flavor of the condiment.
