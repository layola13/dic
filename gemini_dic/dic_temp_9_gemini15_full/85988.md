headstall: /ˈhedˌstɔːl/ | n. | 系马嚼子；马笼头 | A headstall is the part of a bridle that goes around a horse's head, usually consisting of a crownpiece, cheekpieces, and a throatlatch.  It is used to attach the reins and other parts of the bridle. | Example: The rancher adjusted the headstall on his horse before mounting.  牧场主在骑马之前调整了马的系马嚼子。  The broken headstall needed immediate repair.  坏掉的系马嚼子需要立即修理。 | 近义词：bridle (partially), halter | 反义词：None
