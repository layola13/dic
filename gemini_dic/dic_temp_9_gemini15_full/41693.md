corries:/ˈkɒriz/| n.|冰斗；岩斗|A corrie is a deep, bowl-shaped hollow on a mountainside, formed by glacial erosion.  It is typically characterized by steep sides and a lip at one end. Corries are often filled with a small lake or tarn.|The hikers admired the stunning corrie lake nestled high in the mountains.  登山者们欣赏了位于高山上的令人惊叹的冰斗湖。| cirque, cwm|valley, plain|名词
