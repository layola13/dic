cumberland:/ˈkʌmbərˌlænd/| n. |坎伯兰（地名）|Cumberland is a county in England and also a name for places in other countries.  It's derived from the Old English name "Cumbria," which itself has uncertain origins, possibly meaning "valley of the Cumber".  The name is associated with the Cumbrians, an ancient Brythonic people.  The county is known for its scenic beauty and rich history. |The city of Carlisle is the largest in Cumberland.|近义词:None (as a proper noun)|反义词:None (as a proper noun)|名词
