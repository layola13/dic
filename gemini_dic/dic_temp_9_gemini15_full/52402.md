diddler:/ˈdɪdlər/| n. |骗子，小偷，笨蛋|A diddler is someone who cheats or swindles others, often in small ways.  It can also be a derogatory term for a clumsy or foolish person.|例句：He's a diddler; he always tries to get something for nothing. 他是个骗子；他总是试图不劳而获。|例句：Don't be such a diddler; just finish the job. 不要这么笨手笨脚；赶紧完成工作。|近义词：swindler, cheat, trickster, knave|反义词：honest person, trustworthy person|名词
