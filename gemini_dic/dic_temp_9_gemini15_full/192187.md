scrappier:/ˈskræpiər/| adj. |更顽强的；更不屈不挠的；更简陋的；更破旧的 | More determined and aggressive;  showing more fight; less refined or polished; more dilapidated. |例句：The scrappier team managed to win the game.  = 更顽强的球队赢得了比赛。


The underdog team, though scrappier, lacked the experience of their opponents. =  尽管实力较弱的球队更顽强，但他们缺乏对手的经验。


近义词：tougher, grittier, feistier, more determined, more tenacious


反义词：less determined, weaker, more refined, more polished, more luxurious
