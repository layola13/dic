pottier:/ˈpɒtiə(r)/| n.|陶器工匠，制陶者|A potter is a craftsman who makes pottery.  Potters use clay to create various objects, from simple bowls to intricate sculptures.|例句：The pottier skillfully shaped the clay into a beautiful vase.  这位陶器工匠熟练地将粘土塑造成一个美丽的瓶子。  The ancient pottier's techniques are still studied today. 古代陶器工匠的技术至今仍在研究。|近义词：ceramist, artisan|反义词：无
