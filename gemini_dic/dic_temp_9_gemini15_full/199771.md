sirs:/sɜːrz/| n. |先生们|A polite and formal term of address used to refer to more than one man, especially in written communication or formal settings.  It is the plural form of "sir".| Examples: Sirs, we have received your letter. 先生们，我们已收到你们的来信。  The board of sirs will be discussing this matter soon. 先生们组成的董事会很快就会讨论此事。|近义词：gentlemen, messieurs|反义词：无
