aberration:/ˌæbəˈreɪʃən/| n.|异常；偏差；像差|A departure from what is normal, usual, or expected, typically one that is unwelcome.  It can refer to a deviation from a moral standard, a mental disorder, or an optical imperfection.|例句：His behavior was a serious aberration from the norm. 他的行为严重偏离了常态。

例句：The telescope's aberration caused blurry images. 望远镜的像差导致图像模糊。


近义词：deviation, anomaly, irregularity, abnormality, deflection

反义词：normality, regularity, conformity


词性:名词
