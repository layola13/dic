grandpa:/ˈɡrænpɑː/| n. |爷爷；外公|A grandfather; a father's or mother's father.  It is a term of endearment for one's paternal or maternal grandfather.|例句：My grandpa told me many stories about his childhood.  我的爷爷给我讲了许多关于他童年的故事。

例句：I spent the summer with my grandpa at his farm. 我在爷爷的农场度过了夏天。


近义词：grandfather, granddad, gramps|反义词：grandchild
