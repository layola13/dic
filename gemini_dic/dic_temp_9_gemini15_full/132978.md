nonencyclopedical: /ˌnɑːnɛnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪkəl/ | adj. | 非百科全书式的；非百科全书的 | Not characteristic of or suitable for an encyclopedia; lacking the comprehensiveness or objectivity of an encyclopedia.  It implies a lack of systematic or exhaustive coverage of a subject. |

例句：

His account of the war was highly personal and nonencyclopedical, focusing on his own experiences rather than the broader historical context.  他的战争叙述非常个人化，而且是非百科全书式的，侧重于他自己的经历而不是更广泛的历史背景。


The blog post offered a nonencyclopedical overview of the topic, highlighting only certain aspects.  这篇博文提供了对该主题的非百科全书式概述，只突出了某些方面。


近义词：unsystematic, incomplete, partial, biased, subjective

反义词：encyclopedic, comprehensive, exhaustive, objective, systematic


词源：

The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "encyclopedical," which is derived from "encyclopedia."  "Encyclopedia" itself comes from the Greek words "en-" (in, within), "kyklos" (circle), and "paideia" (education, instruction), originally referring to a complete circle of learning.
