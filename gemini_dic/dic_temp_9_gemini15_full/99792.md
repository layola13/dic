intermesh:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃ/| v. |使互相交错；使互相啮合|To fit or be fitted together in a complex or interlocking way.  |例句：The gears intermeshed smoothly. 齿轮平稳地啮合在一起。|例句：The threads of the two fabrics intermeshed beautifully.  两种织物的线交织在一起非常漂亮。|近义词：interweave, interlock, intertwine |反义词：separate, disconnect
