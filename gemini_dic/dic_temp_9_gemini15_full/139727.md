ochlocratical:/ˌɒkləˈkrætɪkəl/ | adj. | 乌合之众的；暴民统治的 | Relating to or characteristic of ochlocracy; governed or influenced by a mob.  The adjective describes a system or situation where the mob or the masses hold the power, often characterized by disorder, violence, and irrational decision-making. | Example: The ochlocratical regime quickly collapsed due to its internal contradictions.  这个乌合之众的政权由于其内部矛盾迅速崩溃。 | 近义词：mobocratic, populist (in a negative sense), demagogic | 反义词：aristocratic, oligarchic, democratic (in a well-functioning sense)
