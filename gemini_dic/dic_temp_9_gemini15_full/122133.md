mistransport:/ˌmɪsˈtrænspɔːrt/| v.|误运，运送错误|To transport something incorrectly or to the wrong place.|The package was mistransported to the wrong address.  包裹被误运到了错误的地址。|misplace, misdirect|transport correctly, deliver correctly|动词
