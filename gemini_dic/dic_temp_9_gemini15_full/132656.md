nondisguised:/ˌnɒnˈdɪsɡaɪzd/ | adj. | 不加掩饰的，坦率的 | Not disguised; open and honest.  |例句：He gave a nondisguised look of disgust. 他露出一副毫不掩饰的厌恶表情。
例句：Her nondisguised anger was evident to everyone. 她毫不掩饰的愤怒每个人都看得出来。|近义词：frank, open, undisguised, overt |反义词：hidden, concealed, disguised, covert |


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **disguise**: verb meaning "to conceal or obscure the identity or true nature of"

因此，nondisguised 字面意思是“没有伪装的”。
