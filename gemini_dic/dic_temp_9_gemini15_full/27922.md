cantharis:/kænˈθærɪs/| n. |芫菁；斑蝥|A genus of beetles, some species of which produce cantharidin, a blistering agent used in medicine (historically).  The name is also used for the blistering agent itself. |例句：Cantharides were once used as a counter-irritant in medicine. 芫菁曾被用作医学上的反刺激剂。


例句：The powdered cantharides were carefully measured for the preparation.  芫菁粉末被仔细测量用于制备。


近义词：blister beetle (指昆虫本身), Spanish fly (指昆虫及提取物)


反义词：无


词性：名词
