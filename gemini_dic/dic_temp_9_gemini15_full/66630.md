existed:/ɪɡˈzɪstɪd/| v.|存在，生存|The dinosaurs existed millions of years ago. 恐龙存在于数百万年前。|近义词：lived, occurred, subsisted|反义词：ceased, perished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Something exists. = 某物存在。
Life exists on Earth. = 地球上有生命。

一般过去时:
The dinosaurs existed millions of years ago. = 恐龙存在于数百万年前。
The problem existed before I arrived. = 我到达之前这个问题就存在了。

现在完成时:
The company has existed for over 50 years. = 这家公司已经存在了50多年。
This problem has existed since the beginning. = 自始至终这个问题就存在着。

过去完成时:
The evidence showed that the species had existed before the Ice Age. = 证据表明该物种在冰河时代之前就存在了。
Before the invention of the telephone, people had existed without such technology. = 在电话发明之前，人们没有这种技术也能生存。

将来时:
The project will exist in the future. = 该项目将在未来存在。
Hopefully, this peace will exist for many years to come. = 希望这种和平能够持续很多年。

虚拟语气:
If the universe hadn't existed, we wouldn't be here. = 如果宇宙不存在，我们就不会在这里。
I wish the problem hadn't existed. = 我希望这个问题不存在。

被动语态:
It is believed that life existed on Mars billions of years ago. = 人们相信数十亿年前火星上存在生命。
The theory is that the universe existed before time itself. = 该理论认为宇宙存在于时间本身之前。

疑问句:
Did the universe always exist? = 宇宙是否一直存在？
When did life first exist on Earth? = 地球上的生命是什么时候开始存在的？

否定句:
The rumours were false; it didn't exist. = 谣言是假的；它不存在。
That kind of animal no longer exists. = 那样的动物不再存在了。


PHRASE->
have existed = 存在过
come into existence = 产生；出现
in existence = 现存的；存在的
out of existence = 不复存在
bring into existence = 创造；使存在
