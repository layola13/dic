coaxing:/ˈkoʊksɪŋ/ | n. | 哄骗；劝诱；游说 | The act of persuading someone gently or patiently to do something.  It often implies a degree of flattery or manipulation. |例句：Her coaxing finally persuaded him to try the new dish.  她的哄骗最终说服他尝试新菜。

例句：The gentle coaxing of the child calmed her down.  温柔的哄劝使孩子平静下来。


近义词：persuasion, inducement, cajoling, wheedling | 反义词：coercion, forcing, compelling | 词性：名词
