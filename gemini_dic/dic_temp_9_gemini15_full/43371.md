craniologist:/ˌkreɪniˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |颅骨学家，头骨学家 |A craniologist is a scientist who studies crania (skulls), often to understand the evolution, development, or diseases of the human brain and skull.  They may also study the skulls of other animals. |例句：The craniologist meticulously examined the ancient skull, searching for clues about the individual's life and death.  颅骨学家仔细检查了古代的头骨，寻找关于该人生活和死亡的线索。


The craniologist presented her findings at the international conference.  这位颅骨学家在国际会议上展示了她的发现。|近义词：skull expert, anthropologist (in some contexts) |反义词：N/A
