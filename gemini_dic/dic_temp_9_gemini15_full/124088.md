morrows:/ˈmɔːroʊz/ | n. |明天，次日 |The word "morrow" refers to the day following the present day; the next day.  It is an archaic term, more commonly found in literature than in everyday speech. |例句：The morrow brought sunshine and a renewed sense of hope. 明天带来了阳光和重燃的希望。
例句：We shall discuss the matter further on the morrow. 我们明天将进一步讨论此事。|近义词：tomorrow, next day |反义词：yesterday, today
