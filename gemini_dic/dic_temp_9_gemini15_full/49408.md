deliveries:/dɪˈlɪvəriːz/| n.|递送；交付；分娩；演讲；发表|The act of delivering something, such as goods, services, or a speech.  It can also refer to the things that are delivered. |例句：The deliveries arrived on time.  送货准时到达了。

例句：She had a difficult delivery.  她分娩很艰难。


近义词：deliverance, conveyance, shipment, presentation|反义词：receipt, collection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes many deliveries every day. = 公司每天送很多货。
She delivers newspapers every morning. = 她每天早上送报纸。


一般过去时:
They delivered the package yesterday. = 他们昨天送了包裹。
The doctor delivered the baby safely. = 医生安全地接生了婴儿。


现在完成时:
We have made all the deliveries for today. = 我们今天所有的货都送完了。
She has delivered three speeches this week. = 她这周已经做了三次演讲。


过去完成时:
They had already made the deliveries before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经送完货了。
The midwife had delivered many babies before this one. = 这名助产士在此之前已经接生过很多婴儿。


将来时:
We will deliver the goods tomorrow. = 我们明天送货。
She will deliver a keynote speech at the conference. = 她将在会议上发表主题演讲。


虚拟语气:
If they delivered the package on time, we would be happy. = 如果他们准时送达包裹，我们会很高兴。
I wish she had delivered her speech with more confidence. = 我希望她能更有信心地发表演讲。


被动语态:
The packages were delivered this morning. = 包裹今天上午送到了。
The baby was safely delivered by the doctor. = 婴儿被医生安全地接生了。


疑问句:
When will the deliveries arrive? = 货物什么时候送达？
Has she delivered her presentation yet? = 她已经发表演讲了吗？


否定句:
They didn't deliver the order on time. = 他们没有按时送货。
She didn't deliver the bad news gently. = 她没有委婉地告诉坏消息。
