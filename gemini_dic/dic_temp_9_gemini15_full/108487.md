layaway:/ˈleɪəweɪ/| n. v.|分期付款；预购；赊购|A system of purchasing goods where the buyer makes a series of payments over time until the full price is paid, at which point the goods are delivered.  It's a form of retail financing.|例句：I put a new TV on layaway at the department store. 我在百货公司预购了一台新电视。  She's paying for her new furniture on layaway. 她在分期付款购买她的新家具。|近义词：installment plan, payment plan|反义词：cash purchase, immediate purchase|词性:noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lay away the merchandise until full payment is received. = 他们将商品存放起来，直到收到全款。
He lays away a small amount each week for a new bicycle. = 他每周存少量钱买一辆新自行车。


一般过去时:
She laid away a significant portion of her savings. = 她存了一大笔积蓄。
We laid away the winter coats last spring. = 我们去年春天把冬衣收了起来。


现在完成时:
They have laid away enough money for a down payment on a house. = 他们已经存够了买房的首付。
I have laid away several items in anticipation of the holidays. = 我已经预购了几样商品以备节日之需。


过去完成时:
They had laid away funds for their child's college education for years. = 他们多年来一直为孩子的大学教育存钱。
She had laid away her summer clothes before the autumn arrived. = 秋天来临之前，她已经把夏装收起来了。


将来时:
I will lay away some money each month for my vacation. = 我每个月都会存一些钱用于我的假期。
They will lay away the winter clothes in the basement next month. = 下个月他们将把冬衣存放在地下室。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would lay away a larger sum. = 如果我有更多钱，我会存更多钱。
I wish I had laid away more money for retirement. = 我希望我为退休存了更多的钱。


被动语态:
The goods were laid away until the final payment was made. = 货物被存放起来，直到付清最后一次款项。
A significant sum was laid away in a savings account. = 一大笔钱存入了储蓄账户。


疑问句:
Are you laying away anything special this year? = 你今年预购了什么特别的商品吗？
Where are you laying away your summer clothes? = 你把夏装放在哪里？


否定句:
They did not lay away any money for the unexpected expenses. = 他们没有为意外开支存钱。
I haven't laid away enough money for Christmas presents. = 我还没有存够买圣诞礼物的钱。


PHRASE->
put something on layaway = 将某物预购
