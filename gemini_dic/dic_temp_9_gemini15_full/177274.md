reassignment:/ˌriːəˈsaɪnmənt/| n. |重新分配；重新指派 |The act of assigning something or someone again, typically to a different task, role, or position.  It implies a previous assignment that is being changed. |例句：The reassignment of resources to the new project delayed its completion. 资源重新分配到新的项目上延迟了它的完成。
例句：Her reassignment to the London office was unexpected. 她被重新分配到伦敦办事处是意料之外的。|近义词：relocation, reallocation, redeployment |反义词：original assignment, initial allocation |名词
