lieutenant-commanders:/ˈlɛfˈtɛnənt kəˈmændərz/| n. |中校（复数）|A lieutenant-commander is a commissioned officer rank in many navies.  It is above a lieutenant and below a commander.  The specific responsibilities vary depending on the navy, but generally involve command of a smaller vessel or a significant role within a larger vessel's command structure.|The lieutenant-commanders were responsible for the successful navigation of the fleet.|近义词：commander (in some contexts)|反义词：ensign, seaman
