e-zines:/ˈiːziːnz/| n. |电子杂志|Electronic magazines or newsletters distributed online.  They often cover specific topics or niches and are typically free or subscription-based.  E-zines offer a blend of text, images, and sometimes audio or video content.|例句：I subscribe to several e-zines about sustainable living. 我订阅了几本关于可持续生活的电子杂志。

例句：Many companies use e-zines to market their products and services.许多公司使用电子杂志来销售他们的产品和服务。


近义词：online magazines, digital publications, webzines
反义词：print magazines, paper publications
词性：名词
