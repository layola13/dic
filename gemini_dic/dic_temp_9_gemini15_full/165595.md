prementioning: /ˌpriːmɛnʃənɪŋ/ | v. | 预先提及；提前提到 | The act of mentioning something beforehand.  It implies informing someone about something before it happens or is otherwise relevant.  This word is less common than its synonyms. | 例句：The prementioning of the meeting's agenda allowed attendees to prepare adequately.  预先提及会议议程使得与会者能够充分准备。


The prementioning of potential problems helped us avoid a crisis. 预先提及潜在问题帮助我们避免了一场危机。|近义词：mentioning beforehand, prior notification, advance notice, forewarning |反义词：concealing, withholding, keeping secret |动词
