post-mineral: /ˌpoʊstˈmɪnərəl/ | adj. | 后矿物时代的；矿物时代以后的 | Relating to the period after the peak of mineral extraction and use, characterized by resource scarcity, recycling, and sustainable practices.  | 例句：The post-mineral economy will require innovative solutions for material reuse and recycling. 后矿物时代经济需要在材料再利用和循环利用方面寻求创新解决方案。

例句：The transition to a post-mineral society necessitates a paradigm shift in our consumption habits. 向后矿物时代社会的过渡需要我们消费习惯的范式转变。


近义词：post-extraction, resource-constrained, sustainable


反义词：mineral-intensive, pre-industrial


词性: 形容词
