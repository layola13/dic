delightful:/dɪˈlaɪtfəl/| adj. |令人愉快的，令人高兴的，可爱的|Pleasing and enjoyable; causing delight.  It suggests something that gives great pleasure and satisfaction. |例句：The weather was delightful for a picnic.  天气非常适合野餐。

The children were delightful company. 孩子们是令人愉快的伙伴。


近义词：pleasant, enjoyable, charming, lovely, agreeable|反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, awful, terrible|词性：形容词
