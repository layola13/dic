septicidal:/ˌsɛptɪˈsaɪdəl/| adj.|杀菌的，杀灭病菌的|Relating to or causing the destruction of sepsis or septic organisms.  Septicidal agents kill bacteria, fungi, and other microorganisms that cause sepsis.| Example:  The surgeon used a septicidal solution to sterilize the wound.  外科医生用杀菌溶液消毒伤口。|近义词：bactericidal, germicidal, antimicrobial|反义词：aseptic
