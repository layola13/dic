manip:/ˈmænɪp/| vt. n.|操纵；控制；操作；处理|to control or influence someone or something, often in a clever or dishonest way; to skillfully handle or operate something.|例句：He manipulated the data to support his theory. 他操纵数据以支持他的理论。  例句：She skillfully manipulated the controls of the aircraft. 她熟练地操作飞机的控制装置。|近义词：control, handle, manage, influence|反义词：release, free, liberate|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manipulates the market. = 他操纵市场。
She manipulates the puppets skillfully. = 她熟练地操纵木偶。

一般过去时:
They manipulated the evidence. = 他们操纵了证据。
He manipulated the situation to his advantage. = 他操纵形势以利于自己。

现在完成时:
She has manipulated the figures to make them look better. = 她已经操纵了数据，使它们看起来更好。
He has manipulated his way to the top. = 他已经通过操纵手段爬到了顶峰。

过去完成时:
They had manipulated the votes before the results were announced. = 在结果公布之前，他们已经操纵了投票。
She had manipulated the system for years before getting caught. = 在被抓住之前，她已经操纵了这个系统多年。

将来时:
He will manipulate the public opinion. = 他将操纵舆论。
They will manipulate the stock prices. = 他们将操纵股价。

虚拟语气:
If he manipulated the evidence, he would be caught. = 如果他操纵证据，他会被抓住。
I wish I hadn't manipulated the situation. = 我真希望我没有操纵形势。

被动语态:
The data were manipulated to fit the hypothesis. = 数据被操纵以符合假设。
The market is being manipulated by large investors. = 市场正被大投资者操纵。

疑问句:
Did he manipulate the results? = 他操纵了结果吗？
Is she manipulating the evidence? = 她正在操纵证据吗？

否定句:
He didn't manipulate the system. = 他没有操纵系统。
They did not manipulate the data. = 他们没有操纵数据。
