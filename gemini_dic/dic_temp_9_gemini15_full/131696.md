noncondescendingly:/ˌnɒnkɒndɪˈsɛndɪŋli/| adv. |不屈尊俯就地，平等地，平易近人地 |Acting in a way that does not show superiority or patronizing behavior; treating others with respect and equality.  |例句：She explained the instructions noncondescendingly, ensuring everyone understood. 她不带任何居高临下的语气解释了说明，确保每个人都理解。


例句：He spoke to the children noncondescendingly, listening carefully to their concerns. 他平等地与孩子们交谈，认真倾听他们的担忧。


近义词：respectfully, humbly, politely, considerately


反义词：condescendingly, patronizingly, arrogantly, superciliously
