quasi-diplomatically: /ˈkwɑːzaɪˌdɪpləˈmætɪkli/ | adv. |  似外交般地；外交辞令般地 |  In a manner resembling diplomacy, but not fully or officially diplomatic.  It suggests a cautious and tactful approach, often used to avoid direct confrontation or offense, but without the full weight or authority of official diplomatic channels. | 例句：He handled the delicate situation quasi-diplomatically, avoiding direct accusations. 他以似外交般的方式处理了这个棘手的情况，避免了直接指责。


The government responded quasi-diplomatically to the international incident. 政府以外交辞令般的方式回应了这一国际事件。| 近义词：tactfully, subtly, indirectly, diplomatically (but less formally) | 反义词：bluntly, directly, aggressively, openly
