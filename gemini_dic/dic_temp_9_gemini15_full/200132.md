skilful:/ˈskɪlfəl/| adj.|熟练的，灵巧的|Having or showing great skill.  The surgeon is skilful in performing complex operations.|例句：He is a skilful craftsman. 他是一位技艺娴熟的工匠。

例句：Her skilful handling of the situation prevented a crisis. 她巧妙地处理了这种情况，避免了一场危机。


近义词： adept, proficient, expert, masterful, accomplished

反义词： clumsy, inept, unskilled, awkward, unskillful
