parlor:/ˈpɑːrlər/ | n. |客厅，接待室，店面 |A parlor is a room in a house, typically a sitting room or living room, especially one that is used for receiving guests.  It can also refer to a shop or business, especially one offering a specialized service.  |例句：We gathered in the parlor for tea and conversation. 我们在客厅里聚在一起喝茶聊天。

例句：The beauty parlor offered a wide range of services. 这家美容院提供各种各样的服务。


近义词：sitting room, living room, lounge, salon


反义词：bedroom, kitchen, bathroom


词性：名词
