gabbers:/ˈɡæbərz/| n. |喋喋不休的人；饶舌者 |A gabber is someone who talks a lot, often incessantly and about unimportant things.  They are known for their continuous chatter. |例句：The gabbers at the back of the bus were driving me crazy.  公交车后排那些喋喋不休的人快把我逼疯了。|近义词：chatterbox, blabbermouth, gossipmonger |反义词：silent person, quiet person
