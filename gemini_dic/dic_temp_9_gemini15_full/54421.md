disinfect:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkt/ | vt. | 消毒，杀菌 | To clean something using a chemical that kills bacteria and other harmful germs.  | 例句：We need to disinfect the kitchen surfaces after handling raw meat. 我们需要在处理生肉后对厨房表面进行消毒。 | 近义词：sterilize, sanitize | 反义词：contaminate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We disinfect the operating room daily. = 我们每天对手术室进行消毒。
She disinfects her hands before surgery. = 她在手术前消毒双手。


一般过去时:
The nurse disinfected the wound. = 护士对伤口进行了消毒。
They disinfected the entire house after the flood. = 洪水过后，他们对整座房子进行了消毒。


现在完成时:
The hospital has disinfected all the equipment. = 医院已经对所有设备进行了消毒。
We have disinfected the area thoroughly. = 我们已经彻底消毒了该区域。


过去完成时:
They had disinfected the instruments before the operation. = 他们在手术前已经对器械进行了消毒。
The team had disinfected the site, preparing for the investigation. = 团队已经对现场进行了消毒，准备进行调查。


将来时:
We will disinfect the pool tomorrow. = 我们明天将对游泳池进行消毒。
The staff will disinfect all the surfaces after the party. = 派对结束后，工作人员将对所有表面进行消毒。


虚拟语气:
If we had disinfected the tools properly, this wouldn't have happened. = 如果我们当时正确地对工具进行了消毒，就不会发生这种情况了。
It would be best if we disinfected the surfaces before painting. = 最好我们在粉刷之前对表面进行消毒。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Did you disinfect the counter? = 你消毒了柜台吗？

否定句:
