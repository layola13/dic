cockerel:/ˈkɒkərəl/| n.| 公鸡，小公鸡 |A young domestic cock; a young rooster.| 例句：The cockerel crowed at dawn.  = 公鸡在黎明时分啼叫。

The farmer proudly showed us his prize-winning cockerel. = 农场主自豪地向我们展示了他获奖的小公鸡。


近义词：rooster, cock, young cock| 反义词：hen, pullet
