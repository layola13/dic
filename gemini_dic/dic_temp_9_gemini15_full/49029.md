degreasers:/diːˈɡriːzərz/| n.|脱脂剂，去油剂|A degreaser is a cleaning agent used to remove grease, oil, and other oily substances from surfaces.  Degreasers are commonly used in industrial settings, kitchens, and workshops.|例句：We used a powerful degreaser to clean the engine. 我们用强力脱脂剂清洗发动机。

例句：The kitchen needs a good degreaser to remove all the stubborn grease. 厨房需要用好的脱脂剂来清除顽固的油污。|近义词：cleaner, solvent, detergent|反义词：N/A
