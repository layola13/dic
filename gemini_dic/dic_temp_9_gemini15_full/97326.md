informative:/ɪnˈfɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 富有教育意义的；提供信息的；有益的 | Giving or conveying information; enlightening.  | The documentary was highly informative, providing valuable insights into the subject. 这部纪录片信息量很大，提供了对主题的宝贵见解。 | instructive, enlightening, educational, revealing | uninformative, unhelpful, meaningless | 形容词
