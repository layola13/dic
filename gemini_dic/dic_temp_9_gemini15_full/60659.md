electives:/ɪˈlɛktɪvz/| n.|选修课，选修科目|Subjects that students can choose to study, usually from a range of options.  Electives allow students to tailor their education to their interests and career goals.|例句：I'm taking three electives this semester: photography, astronomy, and creative writing.  这个学期我选修了三门课：摄影、天文学和创意写作。

例句：She chose electives that would complement her major in engineering. 她选择了与她工程专业互补的选修课。


近义词：optional courses, optional subjects|反义词：required courses, compulsory subjects|名词
