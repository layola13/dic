pilgrimages:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒɪz/| n. |朝圣；朝圣之旅 | A pilgrimage is a journey to a place considered sacred for religious reasons.  It often involves a long and arduous trip, undertaken with a specific spiritual purpose. |例句：Many people undertake pilgrimages to Mecca.  许多人前往麦加朝圣。

例句：The Camino de Santiago is a famous pilgrimage route. 圣地亚哥朝圣之路是一条著名的朝圣路线。


近义词：journey, expedition, voyage |反义词：None directly applicable.  |词性：Noun (plural)


USAGE->

Pilgrimages often take many weeks. = 朝圣之旅通常需要数周时间。
