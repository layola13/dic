dementedly:/dɪˈmentɪdli/ | adv. | 疯狂地；痴呆地；精神错乱地 |  In a demented or insane manner; wildly or crazily.  The adverb describes an action performed in a way that suggests madness, severe mental illness, or a loss of control. | 例句：He laughed dementedly. 他疯狂地大笑。  She ran dementedly through the streets. 她疯狂地跑过街道。 | 近义词：madly, insanely, crazily, frantically | 反义词：calmly, rationally, sanely
