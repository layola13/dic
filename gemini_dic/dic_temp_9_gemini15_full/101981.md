isocheimenal: /ˌaɪsoʊˈkaɪmənəl/ | adj. | 等温线的；等冬线的 | Relating to or denoting lines on a map connecting points of equal mean winter temperature.  This term is primarily used in climatology and meteorology to describe lines connecting locations with the same average winter temperature.  The word combines "iso-" (equal) and "cheimenal" (relating to winter).

例句：

The isocheimenal lines on this map clearly show the temperature gradient across the region. 这张地图上的等温线清晰地显示了该地区的温度梯度。

The study analyzed the distribution of isocheimenal lines to understand the regional climate patterns. 这项研究分析了等温线的分布，以了解区域气候模式。


近义词：isothermal (in the context of temperature, but refers to all seasons, not just winter)

反义词：没有直接的反义词，因为这是一个描述特定等值线的形容词。  与其说有反义词，不如说可以用描述不同平均温度的等值线来对比。


词性:  adjective
