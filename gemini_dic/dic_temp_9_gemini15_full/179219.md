redismisses: /ˌriːdɪsˈmɪsɪz/ | v. |  再次解雇；再次驳回 | To dismiss again; to reject or refuse again.  | The judge redismissed the case due to lack of evidence.  法官因证据不足而再次驳回此案。| dismiss, reject, refuse | accept, approve | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redismisses the employee every year. = 他每年都再次解雇这位员工。
The committee redismisses the proposal. = 委员会再次否决了该提案。


一般过去时:
The court redismissed the charges against him. = 法院再次驳回了对他的指控。
She redismissed the idea after further consideration. = 她经过进一步考虑后再次放弃了这个想法。


现在完成时:
They have redismissed the appeal. = 他们已经再次驳回了上诉。
The company has redismissed several employees this month. = 这个月公司已经再次解雇了几名员工。


过去完成时:
