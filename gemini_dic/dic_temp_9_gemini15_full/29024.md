cartoned:/kɑːrˈtuːnd/ | adj. |装箱的；用纸盒包装的 | Packed in cartons.  |The apples were carefully cartoned for shipment. 苹果被仔细地装箱以便运输。| packaged, boxed | loose, unpackaged |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory cartons thousands of products every day. = 工厂每天装箱数千种产品。
He cartons his belongings before moving. = 他搬家前把他的东西装箱。


一般过去时:
They cartoned the books and shipped them. = 他们把书装箱并运走了。
She cartoned the fragile items with extra care. = 她非常小心地将易碎物品装箱。


现在完成时:
We have cartoned all the gifts for the party. = 我们已经把派对的所有礼物都装箱了。
The company has cartoned and dispatched the order. = 公司已经装箱并发送了订单。


过去完成时:
They had cartoned the merchandise before the truck arrived. = 卡车到达之前，他们已经把商品装箱了。
The workers had cartoned all the boxes before the end of the shift. = 在轮班结束之前，工人们已经把所有箱子都装箱了。


将来时:
We will carton the remaining items tomorrow. = 我们明天将包装剩余的物品。
They will carton the goods and ship them overseas. = 他们将把货物装箱并运往海外。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have cartoned everything properly. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们就会把所有东西都好好装箱。
I wish I had cartoned the breakables more carefully. = 我真希望我把易碎品装得更仔细些。


被动语态:
The goods were carefully cartoned for transport. = 货物被仔细地装箱以便运输。
The oranges are being cartoned for export. = 橘子正在被装箱以供出口。


疑问句:
Are the toys cartoned yet? = 玩具装箱了吗？
Have all the supplies been cartoned for the trip? = 所有的物资都为旅行装箱了吗？


否定句:
The books were not cartoned properly. = 书没有装箱好。
They didn't carton the glassware before shipping. = 他们在发货前没有把玻璃器皿装箱。
