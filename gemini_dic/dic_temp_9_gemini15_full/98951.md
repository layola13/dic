inter-coordinated: /ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 互相协调的，相互配合的 |  Describes a system or process where multiple components or entities work together in a synchronized and harmonious manner.  It emphasizes the mutual coordination and interdependence among the parts. | Example: The inter-coordinated efforts of the various departments ensured the project's success.  各个部门的协调努力确保了项目的成功。 | 近义词: coordinated, integrated, synchronized, harmonized | 反义词: uncoordinated, disjointed, disorganized | 词性: adjective
