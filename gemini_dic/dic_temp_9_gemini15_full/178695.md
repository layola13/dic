recrowning:/rɪˈkraʊnɪŋ/| v.|重新加冕；再次加冕 | The act of crowning someone again, or of replacing a crown.  This term often implies a restoration or a second crowning ceremony, possibly after a period of interruption or upheaval. | Example: The recrowning of the king after the civil war signified the restoration of the monarchy.  重新加冕国王标志着君主制的恢复。|近义词：re-coronation, reinstating|反义词：deposing, dethroning
