phytotomy:/faɪˈtɒtəmi/ | n. |植物解剖学|The study of the anatomy of plants.  It involves the detailed examination of plant structures, their organization, and their development. Phytotomy uses various techniques, including microscopy and dissection, to understand the internal structure and arrangement of plant tissues and organs. |Example:  Phytotomy is crucial for understanding plant physiology and evolution. 植物解剖学对于理解植物生理学和进化至关重要。|近义词：plant anatomy, plant morphology |反义词：无
