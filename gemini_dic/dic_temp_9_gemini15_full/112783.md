lubrications:/ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |润滑；润滑剂；润滑作用 |The act or process of lubricating; the state of being lubricated.  It refers to the application of a lubricant to reduce friction and wear between surfaces in relative motion. |例句：Regular lubrications are essential for the smooth operation of machinery. 定期润滑对于机器的平稳运行至关重要。

例句：The engine's poor performance was attributed to insufficient lubrications. 引擎性能不佳归因于润滑不足。


近义词：lubrication (单数形式), greasing, oiling |反义词：dryness, friction
