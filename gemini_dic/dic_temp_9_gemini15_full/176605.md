reaccepting: /ˌriːækˈsɛptɪŋ/ | v. | 重新接受，再次接受 |  The act of accepting something again after previously rejecting it or after a period of non-acceptance.  It implies a renewed or restored acceptance. |

例句：

The company is reaccepting applications after a temporary suspension. 公司在暂停一段时间后重新接受申请。

After a period of reflection, he is reaccepting his role in the project.  经过一段时间的反思后，他重新接受了他在项目中的角色。


近义词：readmitting, resuming, reinstating

反义词：rejecting, refusing, dismissing

词性：动词
