discumbency: /dɪˈskʌmbənsi/ | n. | 卧姿；躺卧；懒散 | The state of lying down; recumbency.  It can also imply laziness or indolence. | Example:  The cat enjoyed its discumbency in the sunbeam.  这只猫享受着在阳光下的卧姿。 The prolonged discumbency contributed to his muscle atrophy. 长时间的卧床导致了他的肌肉萎缩。| 近义词：recumbency, prostration, supination | 反义词：activity, alertness, uprightness |
