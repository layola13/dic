clouder:/ˈklaʊdər/| v.|使朦胧；使模糊；遮蔽|To make cloudy or obscure; to darken or dim.  It can also refer to gathering in clouds or groups.|例句：The smoke clouded the sky. 烟雾遮蔽了天空。  The memories clouded his judgment.  回忆使他的判断模糊不清。|近义词：obscure, darken, dim, obfuscate |反义词：clarify, illuminate, brighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smoke clouders the sky. = 烟雾使天空朦胧。
He clouders his intentions. = 他掩盖了他的意图。

一般过去时:
The storm clouded the view. = 暴风雨遮蔽了视线。
The dust clouded the air. = 灰尘使空气变得浑浊。

现在完成时:
Years have clouded his memory. = 岁月模糊了他的记忆。
The incident has clouded their relationship. = 那件事影响了他们之间的关系。

过去完成时:
The heavy fog had clouded the harbor. = 浓雾已经遮蔽了港口。
His anger had clouded his judgment. = 他的愤怒已经蒙蔽了他的判断。

将来时:
The approaching storm will cloud the sunset. = 即将来临的风暴将会遮蔽落日。
More information will cloud the issue further. = 更多信息会使问题更加复杂。

虚拟语气:
If the rain clouded the view, we wouldn't be able to see the mountain. = 如果雨水遮蔽了视野，我们就看不到山了。
If only the doubt hadn't clouded my judgment. = 但愿怀疑没有蒙蔽我的判断。

被动语态:
The sky was clouded by the smoke. = 天空被烟雾遮蔽了。
His mind was clouded by grief. = 他的思绪被悲伤所笼罩。

疑问句:
Is the picture clouded by the glare? = 图片是否因强光而模糊？
Was his vision clouded by the illness? = 他的视力是否因疾病而受损？


否定句:
The sky wasn't clouded that day. = 那天天空没有阴天。
The issue wasn't clouded by political considerations. = 这个问题没有受到政治因素的影响。
