outearn:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrn/| vt.|赚得比…多；收入超过|To earn more money than someone else.  |例句：He outearns his wife by a considerable margin. 他比他妻子赚得多得多。|例句：She consistently outearns her colleagues. 她一直比她的同事赚得多。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：under-earn, earn less than|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outearns his brother. = 他比他兄弟赚得多。
She outearns her colleagues. = 她比她的同事赚得多。

一般过去时:
He outearned his wife last year. = 去年他比他妻子赚得多。
They outearned their expectations. = 他们的收入超过了预期。

现在完成时:
She has outearned him for the past five years. = 过去五年里她一直比他赚得多。
He has consistently outearned his peers in his field. = 在他所在的领域，他一直比同龄人赚得多。

过去完成时:
By the age of 30, he had outearned most of his classmates. = 到30岁时，他已经比大多数同学赚得多。
She had outearned her target by the end of the quarter. = 到季度末，她已经超过了目标收入。

将来时:
They will outearn their parents in the future. = 将来他们会比父母赚得多。
He expects to outearn his current employer at his new job. = 他期望在新工作中比现在的老板赚得多。

虚拟语气:
If she worked harder, she might outearn her boss. = 如果她更努力工作，她可能会比老板赚得多。
I wish I could outearn my debts. = 我真希望我能赚得比我的债务多。

被动语态:
(Outearn is rarely used in the passive voice because it is a transitive verb describing the active earning of a subject.)  This sentence structure is unusual and generally avoided.

疑问句:
Does he outearn his wife? = 他比他妻子赚得多吗？
Do you think she will outearn him this year? = 你认为她今年会比他赚得多吗？

否定句:
He doesn't outearn his brother. = 他没有比他兄弟赚得多。
She didn't outearn her target last month. = 上个月她没有超过目标收入。


PHRASE->
outearn someone significantly = 显著地比某人赚得多
outearn the competition = 赚得比竞争对手多
consistently outearn = 一直赚得比…多
outearn one's expectations = 超出预期收入
outearn the market average = 超过市场平均收入
