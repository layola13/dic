driftingly: /ˈdrɪftɪŋli/ | adv. | 漫无目的地；漂流地；漂移地 |  Adverb describing movement or action in a slow, aimless, or uncontrolled way, like something drifting on a current.  It implies a lack of direction or purpose. | 例句：The boat driftedingly down the river.  小船顺着河流漫无目的地漂流。

The leaves driftedingly fell from the trees. 叶子漫无目的地从树上飘落。


近义词：aimlessly, listlessly, idly, haphazardly | 反义词：purposefully, deliberately, intentionally | 词性：副词
