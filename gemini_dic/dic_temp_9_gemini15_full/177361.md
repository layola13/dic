reattribute:/ˌriːəˈtrɪbjuːt/| vt.|重新归因，重新归属|To attribute something again or differently.  To assign a different cause, explanation, or source to something.|例句：Historians are reattributing the authorship of the play to a lesser-known writer.  历史学家们正将该剧的作者归于一位鲜为人知的作家。
例句：The company decided to reattribute the project's failure to poor management rather than insufficient funding. 公司决定将项目的失败归因于管理不善，而不是资金不足。|近义词：reassign, reassign, reallocate|反义词：attribute, ascribe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reattribute the painting to a different artist. = 他们将这幅画重新归于一位不同的艺术家。
We reattribute the problem to a software bug. = 我们将问题重新归因于软件错误。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
