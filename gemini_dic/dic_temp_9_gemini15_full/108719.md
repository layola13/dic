leanness:/ˈliːnnəs/| n. |瘦弱；精简；贫乏|The state or quality of being lean; thinness; meagerness.  It can also refer to the lack of something, such as fat, resources, or detail.|例句：His leanness was a result of his illness. 他的消瘦是疾病造成的。The leanness of the report made it difficult to understand. 报告过于简略，难以理解。|近义词：slimness, thinness, meagerness, sparseness|反义词：obesity, plumpness, corpulence, richness
