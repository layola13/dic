/dæl/| n.|山谷，溪谷|a low area of land between hills or mountains, often with a river running through it|例句：The village is nestled in a beautiful valley.  这个村庄坐落在一个美丽的山谷里。|近义词：dale, glen, ravine|反义词：mountain, peak, summit|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river flows through the valley. = 这条河流经山谷。
This valley produces excellent grapes. = 这个山谷盛产优质葡萄。

一般过去时:
We hiked through the valley yesterday. = 我们昨天徒步穿越了山谷。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
