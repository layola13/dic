cotransport:/ˈkoʊˌtrænspɔːrt/| n.|协同运输|The simultaneous transport of two different substances across a cell membrane, often involving one substance moving down its concentration gradient to power the movement of another substance against its gradient.  |Example: Sodium-glucose cotransport is a vital process in the absorption of glucose in the intestines. 钠-葡萄糖协同转运是肠道吸收葡萄糖的重要过程。|近义词：coupled transport, symport|反义词：antiport, uniport
