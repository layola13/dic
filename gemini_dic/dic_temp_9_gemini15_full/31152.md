chandler:/ˈtʃændlər/| n. |蜡烛制造商；（尤指）货运代理人 |A chandler is a person who makes or sells candles, or, more commonly today, a person or company that supplies goods to ships.  |Example: In the old days, a chandler would have been an essential part of any port town.  在过去，蜡烛制造商是任何港口城镇的重要组成部分。|近义词：candlemaker, supplier, provisioner |反义词：consumer, purchaser |名词
