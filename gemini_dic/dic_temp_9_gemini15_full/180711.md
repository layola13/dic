regrinding:/ˌriːˈɡraɪndɪŋ/| n.| 再磨，再研磨 | The process of grinding something again.  It often refers to sharpening tools or resurfacing materials. |例句：The regrinding of the cutting tools improved their efficiency.  再磨削切削工具提高了它们的效率。

The regrinding of the engine's cylinder head was necessary to restore its compression.  对发动机缸盖进行再研磨是恢复其压缩比所必需的。|近义词：re-sharpening, resurfacing |反义词：initial grinding, original grinding |名词
