handiron:/ˈhændaɪərn/| n. |熨斗|A heavy, usually metal, implement used for ironing clothes, typically heated on a fire.  It is a historical term and less common now.|Example: The laundress heated the handiron on the hearth before pressing the linens.  洗衣妇在炉膛上加热熨斗，然后熨烫亚麻布。|近义词：flatiron, smoothing iron|反义词：None specifically.
