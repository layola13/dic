mesoglea:/ˈmɛsoʊgliːə/| n. |中胶层|A non-cellular, gelatinous substance found between the two epithelial layers of a cnidarian (such as a jellyfish or sea anemone) or a sponge.  It provides structural support and allows for the diffusion of nutrients and waste products.| Example: The jellyfish's mesoglea is responsible for its characteristic bell shape and flexibility.  中胶层赋予水母其标志性的钟形和柔韧性。|近义词：mesoderm (in some contexts)|反义词：epithelium
