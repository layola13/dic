multinational:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/| adj. n.|跨国的；多国的；多民族的|Having operations or subsidiaries in several countries.  A multinational corporation operates in many countries.|例句：Many multinational companies have their headquarters in major cities. 许多跨国公司的总部设在主要城市。

例句：The multinational nature of the project required careful coordination between teams in different countries.  这个项目的跨国性质要求对不同国家的团队进行仔细协调。


近义词：international, transnational, global


反义词：domestic, national, local


词性：形容词，名词
