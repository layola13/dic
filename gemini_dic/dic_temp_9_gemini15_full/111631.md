localization:/ˌloʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | /ˌləʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |本地化；区域化；定位 |The adaptation of a product or content to a specific local market, considering cultural, linguistic, and technical factors.  This includes translating text, adapting images and videos, and modifying features to suit local preferences. |Example: The game's localization for the Japanese market included new voice acting and culturally relevant items.|近义词：adaptation, translation, internationalization |反义词：globalization, standardization
