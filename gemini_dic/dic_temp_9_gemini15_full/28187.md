/ˈkæpreɪt/| n. |癸酸酯；羊脂酸酯|Caprate refers to any ester of capric acid (decanoic acid), a saturated fatty acid with a ten-carbon chain.  These esters are commonly found in various natural oils and fats, and have applications in the food, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical industries.  |Example:  Coconut oil contains several caprates, contributing to its characteristic properties.  椰子油含有多种癸酸酯，赋予其独特的特性。|近义词:decanoate|反义词:  (no direct antonym)|名词
