lady: /ˈleɪdi/ | n. |女士，贵妇人 | A woman, especially one of high social standing.  A polite or respectful term of address for a woman. |例句：She is a lady of great charm and intelligence. 她是一位魅力和智慧兼备的女士。


例句：The elderly lady needed assistance crossing the street.  这位年迈的女士需要帮助过马路。


近义词：woman, gentlewoman, dame


反义词：gentleman (in the context of social standing)


词性: 名词
