lumberman:/ˈlʌmbərmæn/| n.|伐木工人|A lumberman is a person who cuts down trees for timber.  They work in forests, often using chainsaws and other heavy machinery.  Their work is physically demanding and often involves working outdoors in all types of weather.|Example: My grandfather was a lumberman in the Pacific Northwest.|近义词：logger, woodsman|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with office worker, etc.|名词
