pseudoedema:/ˌsuːdoʊˈiːdiːmə/ | n. | 假性水肿 | Apparent swelling due to factors other than fluid accumulation;  a condition mimicking edema but lacking the characteristic fluid retention.  It can be caused by various factors, including inflammation, lymphatic obstruction, and venous insufficiency, leading to tissue thickening and a swollen appearance. | Example:  The patient presented with pseudoedema of the legs, but further investigation revealed underlying venous insufficiency, not fluid retention.  |近义词:  apparent edema,  false edema | 反义词: true edema,  actual edema |
