deadlifts:/ˈdedlɪfts/| n. v.|硬拉|The act of lifting a weighted barbell from the floor to a standing position with a straight back.|例句：He performed several deadlifts with impressive form. 他以令人印象深刻的姿势进行了几次硬拉。|例句：Deadlifting is a great exercise for building strength in the posterior chain. 硬拉是增强后链力量的绝佳运动。|近义词：weightlifting, powerlifting|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I deadlift three times a week. = 我一周硬拉三次。
She deadlifts heavier weights than I do. = 她硬拉的重量比我重。

一般过去时:

I have deadlifted consistently for two years. = 我坚持硬拉两年了。
