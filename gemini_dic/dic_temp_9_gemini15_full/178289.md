reconcentration:/ˌriːkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/| n. |重新集中；再集中；集中营 |The act of concentrating again; the state of being concentrated again.  It can also refer to a policy of forcibly concentrating a population, often into camps.  |例句：The reconcentration of troops was crucial to winning the battle.  军队重新集结对赢得战斗至关重要。

The Spanish government's policy of reconcentration led to widespread suffering and death. 西班牙政府的重新集中政策导致了广泛的苦难和死亡。

|近义词：concentration, centralization, regrouping |反义词：dispersion, scattering, decentralization |名词
