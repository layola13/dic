chilled:/tʃɪld/| adj.|冰冷的；冷静的；轻松的|Cooled to a low temperature;  calm and relaxed;  informal: relaxed and calm.|例句：The chilled wine was perfect for a summer evening.  这冰镇的葡萄酒很适合夏夜享用。

例句：He remained chilled despite the pressure.  尽管压力很大，他仍然保持冷静。


近义词：cool, cold, relaxed, calm|反义词：hot, warm, agitated, stressed|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The beer is chilled. = 啤酒冰镇好了。
The water is chilled. = 水冰镇好了。


一般过去时:
He chilled the champagne. = 他把香槟冰镇了。
She chilled the soup. = 她把汤冰镇了。


现在完成时:
We have chilled the drinks. = 我们已经把饮料冰镇好了。
They have chilled the wine for the party. = 他们为派对准备好了冰镇葡萄酒。


过去完成时:
They had chilled the food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经把食物冰镇好了。
She had chilled the milk overnight. = 她把牛奶冰了一整夜。


将来时:
I will chill the white wine. = 我会把白葡萄酒冰镇一下。
We will chill the beer before the barbecue. = 烧烤前我们会把啤酒冰镇一下。


虚拟语气:
If I had chilled the wine earlier, it would be perfect now. = 如果我早点把酒冰镇好，现在就完美了。
I wish I had chilled the drinks before the guests arrived. = 我真希望在客人来之前把饮料冰镇好。


被动语态:
The drinks were chilled. = 饮料被冰镇了。
The wine is being chilled. = 葡萄酒正在冰镇中。


疑问句:

Are the drinks chilled enough? = 饮料冰镇得够了吗？


否定句:
