happy-clappy: /ˈhæpiˌklæpi/ | adj. | 虚假的快乐；过于乐观；轻率的 | Excessively cheerful and optimistic in a way that seems forced or insincere; superficially cheerful.  It often implies a lack of genuine feeling or understanding of a serious situation. | The happy-clappy attitude of the management was jarring given the company's financial troubles.  管理层的虚假乐观态度与公司面临的财务困境格格不入。|近义词：overly optimistic, superficially cheerful, relentlessly positive, Pollyannaish | 反义词：depressed, gloomy, pessimistic, realistic
