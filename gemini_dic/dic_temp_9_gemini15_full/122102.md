misting:/ˈmɪstɪŋ/| n. v.|轻雾；细雨；喷雾；(使)蒙蒙细雨；喷洒|The action or state of being misty; a fine spray or mist.|例句：The misting of the fields kept the crops hydrated. 田间的喷雾保持了农作物的湿润。The early morning misting gave way to sunshine.清晨的薄雾变成了阳光。|近义词：spraying, sprinkling, fogging|反义词：drenching, downpour


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gardener is misting the plants. = 园丁正在给植物喷雾。
It is misting outside. = 外面下着蒙蒙细雨。


一般过去时:
The farmer misted the crops yesterday. = 农民昨天给庄稼喷了雾。
It misted lightly all morning. = 整个上午都下着蒙蒙细雨。


现在完成时:
They have misted the greenhouse several times this week. = 他们本周已经多次给温室喷雾了。
It has been misting on and off all day. = 一整天都断断续续地下着蒙蒙细雨。


过去完成时:
The plants had been misted before the heatwave hit. = 在热浪来袭之前，植物已经喷过雾了。
It had misted earlier, but now the sun is shining. = 早些时候下过蒙蒙细雨，但现在阳光明媚。


将来时:
We will mist the orchids tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上会给兰花喷雾。
It will likely be misting when we leave. = 我们离开的时候可能会下蒙蒙细雨。


虚拟语气:
If it were misting, we would stay inside. = 如果下蒙蒙细雨，我们就待在里面。
I wish it were misting; the air is so dry. = 我希望下蒙蒙细雨；空气太干燥了。


被动语态:
The plants were misted regularly. = 植物定期喷雾。
The fields are being misted by the farmer. = 农田正在被农民喷雾。


疑问句:
Is it misting outside? = 外面下着蒙蒙细雨吗？
Are you misting the roses? = 你在给玫瑰花喷雾吗？


否定句:
It wasn't misting when I left. = 我离开的时候没有下蒙蒙细雨。
They didn't mist the vegetables. = 他们没有给蔬菜喷雾。
