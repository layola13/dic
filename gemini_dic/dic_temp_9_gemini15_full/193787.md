self-deluding: /ˌsɛlf dəˈluːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 自欺欺人的；自我欺骗的 |  Describes someone who deceives themselves, often by believing something that is untrue or unrealistic.  It implies a conscious or unconscious effort to avoid unpleasant realities. | Example: He was self-deluding himself into thinking he could win the lottery. 他自欺欺人地认为自己可以中彩票。

He lived a self-deluding existence, ignoring the mounting evidence against him. 他过着自欺欺人的生活，无视越来越多的证据反对自己。


近义词: self-deceptive, self-deceiving, deluded,  in denial


反义词: self-aware, realistic, clear-sighted


词性: 形容词
