christianization:/ˌkrɪs.tʃən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/| n. |基督教化；使基督教化|The process of converting or being converted to Christianity, or of spreading the influence of Christian beliefs and practices.  It can refer to the conversion of individuals, groups, or entire societies. |例句：The christianization of Europe took centuries.  欧洲的基督教化花了几个世纪。

例句：The forced christianization of indigenous populations led to the loss of many cultural traditions.  对土著居民的强制基督教化导致许多文化传统的丧失。


近义词：conversion, evangelization, proselytization

反义词：secularization, paganization
