plead:/pliːd/| vt. vi. n.|恳求；辩护；认罪|To plead is to make an earnest or emotional request for something.  It can also refer to making a formal statement in a court of law, either in defense or admitting guilt. As a noun, it refers to a formal statement made in court.|例句：He pleaded with the judge for mercy. 他恳求法官宽大处理。|例句：The lawyer pleaded his client's innocence. 律师为他的当事人辩护无罪。|近义词：beg, implore, argue, defend|反义词：accuse, condemn

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pleads guilty. = 他认罪。
She pleads for help. = 她请求帮助。


一般过去时:
He pleaded not guilty. = 他不认罪。
She pleaded with him to stay. = 她恳求他留下。


现在完成时:
He has pleaded his case effectively. = 他已有效地陈述了他的案情。
She has pleaded innocence since the beginning. = 从一开始她就声称无罪。


过去完成时:
He had pleaded with her many times before. = 他之前已经多次恳求她了。
She had pleaded guilty to a lesser charge. = 她已经对较轻的罪名认罪。


将来时:
He will plead insanity. = 他将以精神错乱为由进行抗辩。
She will plead for her life. = 她将为自己的生命而恳求。


虚拟语气:
If he pleaded guilty, he might receive a lighter sentence. = 如果他认罪，可能会得到较轻的判决。
I wish she had pleaded not guilty. = 我真希望她没认罪。


被动语态:
The case was pleaded before the judge. = 此案已在法官面前辩护。
His innocence was pleaded by his lawyer. = 他的无辜由他的律师辩护。


疑问句:
Will he plead guilty or not guilty? = 他会认罪还是不认罪？
What will she plead in court? = 她将在法庭上作何陈述？


否定句:
He did not plead guilty. = 他没有认罪。
She didn't plead for mercy. = 她没有请求宽恕。
