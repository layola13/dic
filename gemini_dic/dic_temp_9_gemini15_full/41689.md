corridor:/ˈkɔːrɪdɔːr/|/ˈkɔːrɪdɔːr/| n. |走廊，通道 |A corridor is a long passage in a building or train, from which rooms or compartments open.  It is typically enclosed by walls and has a roof. |例句：The long corridor was dimly lit. 这条长长的走廊灯光昏暗。

例句：We walked down the corridor to the conference room. 我们沿着走廊走到会议室。

近义词：passage, hallway, aisle |反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent, e.g.,  a large open space) |名词
