gyros:/ˈdʒaɪroʊz/| n. |希腊烤肉，肉卷|A gyro (plural gyros) is a Greek sandwich made with meat (usually lamb or pork) cooked on a vertical rotisserie, served in pita bread with vegetables and a sauce.  |Example:  I had a delicious gyro for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的希腊烤肉卷。
I ordered a gyro with extra tzatziki sauce. 我点了一个加了额外酸奶酱的希腊烤肉卷。|近义词：souvlaki (similar dish)|反义词：无|名词
