ice-skate:/ˈaɪsˌskeɪt/ | n. v. |冰鞋；滑冰 |A type of footwear with blades attached to the bottom, used for gliding on ice.  Also, the act of gliding on ice using ice skates. |例句：She bought a new pair of ice skates for her birthday. 她过生日买了一双新冰鞋。
