repelling:/rɪˈpɛlɪŋ/ | adj. |令人厌恶的，令人反感的；排斥的 | causing strong dislike or disgust;  pushing away or rejecting.  |The smell was utterly repelling.  这气味极其令人作呕。| repulsive, disgusting, loathsome, offensive | attractive, appealing, pleasant |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong magnet is repelling the other magnet. 强磁铁正在排斥另一块磁铁。
This idea repels most people.  这个主意让大多数人反感。


一般过去时:
The experiment repelled the invading insects. 实验排斥了入侵的昆虫。
The movie's plot repelled many viewers. 这部电影的情节让许多观众反感。


现在完成时:
His behavior has repelled many potential friends. 他的行为已经让许多潜在的朋友反感了。
The new policy has repelled foreign investment. 新政策已经排斥了外国投资。


过去完成时:
The stench had repelled them long before they reached the house.  臭味早在他们到达房子之前就让他们反感了。
He had repelled several attacks before succumbing to injury. 在屈服于伤势之前，他已经击退了几次攻击。


将来时:
This new chemical will repel insects effectively.  这种新型化学物质将有效地驱除昆虫。
I believe this proposal will repel many investors. 我认为这项提案将会让许多投资者反感。


虚拟语气:
If the surface were treated differently, it would repel water. 如果表面处理不同，它就会防水。
If only the smell hadn't repelled me, I might have enjoyed the party.  如果气味没有让我反感，我或许会喜欢这个派对。


被动语态:
The invaders were repelled by the soldiers. 入侵者被士兵击退了。
The proposal was repelled due to lack of support.  由于缺乏支持，该提案被否决了。


疑问句:
Does this material repel water?  这种材料防水吗？
Does his attitude repel you? 他的态度让你反感吗？


否定句:
This substance does not repel insects.  这种物质不能驱除昆虫。
His charm doesn't repel anyone. 他的魅力不会让任何人反感。



PHRASE->
repelling force  排斥力
repelling power  排斥力
repelling agent  驱避剂
self-repelling  自相排斥的
mutually repelling  相互排斥的

词源:  From Old French *repeler (to drive back), from Latin repellere (to drive back), from re- (back) + pellere (to push, drive).
