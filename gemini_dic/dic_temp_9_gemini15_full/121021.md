miscommunications:/ˌmɪskəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |误解，沟通不良 |  A failure to communicate effectively, resulting in misunderstanding.  Miscommunications can arise from various sources, including unclear language, cultural differences, or technological glitches. | Example: The project failed due to numerous miscommunications between the team members.  这个项目因为团队成员之间多次沟通不畅而失败了。|近义词： misunderstandings, misinterpretations, communication breakdowns | 反义词： clear communication, effective communication | 名词
