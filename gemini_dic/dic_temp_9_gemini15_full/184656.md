restringency: /rɪˈstrɪndʒənsi/ | n. | 紧缩；严格；严厉 | The act of making something stricter or more severe;  a state of severity or strictness, often in relation to financial or economic policy.  It implies a reduction or limitation, often enforced. |例句：The government implemented policies of fiscal restringency to combat inflation. 政府实施了财政紧缩政策以对抗通货膨胀。

The recent economic downturn led to increased restringency in government spending. 近期经济衰退导致政府支出更加紧缩。| 近义词：severity, austerity, rigor, stringency | 反义词：leniency, laxity, permissiveness |  词性: noun
