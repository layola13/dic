peers:/pɪərz/|/pɪə(r)z/| n. |同龄人，同伴；贵族；仔细看|People of the same age, status, or ability.  To look closely or scrutinizingly.|例句：He felt out of place among his wealthy peers. 他在富有的同龄人中感到格格不入。

例句：She peered into the darkness, trying to see what was there. 她仔细地凝视着黑暗，试图看清那里有什么。|近义词：equals, contemporaries, colleagues,  examine, scrutinize|反义词：superiors, inferiors, elders
