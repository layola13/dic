serves:/sɜːrvz/| vt. n.|服务；供应；适合；适用|To perform duties or provide a function for someone or something.  It can also mean to supply or provide something, or to be suitable or appropriate.|例句：The restaurant serves delicious food. 这家餐馆供应美味的食物。|例句：This experience serves as a valuable lesson.  这段经历是一个宝贵的教训。|近义词：provides, supplies, caters, fits, suits|反义词：hinders, obstructs, disserves


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This restaurant serves excellent seafood. = 这家餐厅供应美味的海鲜。
He serves as the company's CEO. = 他担任公司首席执行官。

一般过去时:
She served in the army for five years. = 她曾在军队服役五年。
The waiter served us our drinks quickly. = 服务员很快给我们上了饮料。

现在完成时:
They have served the community for many years. = 他们为社区服务多年了。
This policy has served its purpose well. = 这项政策已经很好地完成了它的目的。

过去完成时:
He had served breakfast before I woke up. = 我醒来之前他已经准备好了早餐。
She had served the company for twenty years before retiring. = 她退休前为公司服务了二十年。


将来时:
The new hotel will serve tourists from all over the world. = 新酒店将为来自世界各地的游客提供服务。
I will serve you a delicious meal. = 我将为你准备一顿美味的饭菜。


虚拟语气:
If the government served the people better, there would be less unrest. = 如果政府更好地为人民服务，就会有更少的动荡。
I wish this medicine served to cure my illness. = 我希望这种药能治好我的病。


被动语态:
The customers were served promptly. = 顾客得到了及时的服务。
The food is served on large platters. = 食物盛放在大盘子里端上来。


疑问句:
Does this restaurant serve vegetarian dishes? = 这家餐厅供应素菜吗？
What kind of drinks do they serve? = 他们供应什么饮料？


否定句:
The hotel does not serve alcohol. = 这家酒店不供应酒。
This excuse doesn't serve your purpose. = 这个借口对你的目的没有帮助。
