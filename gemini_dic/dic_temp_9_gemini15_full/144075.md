outrun:/ˌaʊtˈrʌn/| vt.|超过，跑赢，胜过|To run faster than and leave behind; to exceed or surpass.|例句：He outran all the other runners in the race. 他在比赛中超过了所有其他跑步者。

例句：The company's profits outran expectations this year.  今年公司的利润超出了预期。


近义词：outpace, outstrip, surpass, exceed|反义词：be outrun by, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outruns his opponents in every race. 他在每一场比赛中都超过了他的对手。
The demand outruns the supply. 需求超过供应。


一般过去时:
She outran the pursuing dog. 她跑赢了追赶她的狗。
The popularity of the product outran the company's predictions.  产品的受欢迎程度超过了公司的预测。


现在完成时:
They have outrun their rivals in the market. 他们在市场上超越了他们的竞争对手。
The project has outrun its budget.  项目超支了。


过去完成时:
He had outrun his pursuers before they could catch him. 在追捕者抓住他之前，他已经跑掉了。
The company had outrun its initial projections for growth. 公司已经超过了其最初的增长预测。


将来时:
We believe we will outrun our competitors in the long term. 我们相信从长远来看，我们将超越竞争对手。
She expects to outrun the deadline. 她期望赶在截止日期之前完成。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outrun his opponent. 如果他训练更刻苦，他可能会超过他的对手。
If the company had been more efficient, it would have outrun its expenses.如果公司更有效率，它就不会超支了。


被动语态:
He was outrun by the faster runner. 他被跑得更快的跑步者超过了。
The initial plan was outrun by unforeseen circumstances.最初的计划被不可预见的情况打乱了。


疑问句:
Can we outrun the storm? 我们能跑赢暴风雨吗？
Will the project outrun its budget? 项目会超支吗？


否定句:
They couldn't outrun the police. 他们没能甩掉警察。
The team didn't outrun their expectations.  团队没有超出预期。


PHRASE->
outrun the competition: 超越竞争对手
outrun expectations: 超出预期
outrun the deadline: 赶在截止日期前完成
outrun the supply: 超过供应
outrun one's resources: 资源耗尽
