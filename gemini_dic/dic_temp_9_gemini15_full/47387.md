deaden:/ˈdɛdn/| vt. adj.|使减弱；使迟钝；使麻木；减轻；使沉闷|to make something less strong or effective; to make someone or something less sensitive or responsive|例句：The anesthetic helped to deaden the pain. 麻醉剂帮助减轻了疼痛。 The heavy rain deadened the sound of the traffic. 大雨减弱了交通的声音。|近义词：dull, weaken, numb, muffle|反义词：sharpen, intensify, heighten, enliven


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drug deadens the pain. = 药物减轻疼痛。
He deadens the sound with thick curtains. = 他用厚窗帘来隔音。


一般过去时:
The heavy snow deadened the sound of the city. = 大雪降低了城市的声音。
The anesthetic deadened her feeling in her arm. = 麻醉剂使她的手臂麻木。


现在完成时:
The constant noise has deadened my hearing. = 持续的噪音已经损害了我的听力。
Years of hard work have deadened his enthusiasm. = 多年的辛勤工作已经消磨了他的热情。


过去完成时:
The rain had deadened the colors of the landscape. = 雨水已使景色的颜色暗淡。
The long journey had deadened their spirits. = 漫长的旅程已经使他们士气低落。


将来时:
The coming storm will deaden the radio signal. = 即将来临的风暴将使无线电信号减弱。
This medicine will deaden the nerve endings. = 这药会使神经末梢麻木。


虚拟语气:
If I drank less coffee, it might deaden the effects of my anxiety. = 如果我少喝咖啡，它可能会减轻我的焦虑。
If only I could deaden the throbbing in my head!  = 但愿我能减轻我头部的阵痛！


被动语态:
The pain was deadened by the medication. = 疼痛被药物减轻了。
His senses were deadened by the cold. = 他的感官被寒冷麻痹了。


疑问句:
Does this medicine deaden the pain quickly? = 这药能快速止痛吗？
Will the thick carpet deaden the noise from the upstairs apartment? = 厚地毯能隔绝楼上公寓的噪音吗？


否定句:
The painkiller didn't deaden the sharp pain. = 止痛药没有减轻剧烈的疼痛。
The insulation didn't deaden the sound completely. = 隔音材料并没有完全隔绝声音。
