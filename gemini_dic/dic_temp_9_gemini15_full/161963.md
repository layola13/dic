pranking:/ˈpræŋkɪŋ/| n. |恶作剧；开玩笑|The act of playing a prank or pranks; mischievous or playful trickery.|例句：The constant pranking in the office was getting old.  办公室里没完没了的恶作剧让人厌烦了。|例句：His pranking was harmless, but annoying. 他的恶作剧虽然无害，但也令人讨厌。|近义词：mischief, joke, trick, jest |反义词：seriousness, solemnity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pranking his sister. = 他正在捉弄他妹妹。
They are pranking each other constantly. = 他们不断地互相捉弄。

一般过去时:
He pranked his friend with a whoopie cushion. = 他用屁垫捉弄了他的朋友。
They pranked the teacher by moving her desk. = 他们把老师的桌子挪开来捉弄她。

现在完成时:
She has pranked him several times this week. = 本周她已经捉弄了他好几次了。
They have been pranking each other since childhood. = 从小他们就一直在互相捉弄。

过去完成时:
He had pranked her before, so she wasn't surprised. = 他以前就捉弄过她，所以她并不惊讶。
They had already pranked everyone in the office before lunch. = 午餐前他们已经捉弄了办公室里的所有人。

将来时:
I will prank him on April Fool's Day. = 我会在愚人节捉弄他。
They will be pranking their classmates tomorrow. = 他们明天将要捉弄他们的同学。

虚拟语气:
If I pranked him, he would be furious. = 如果我捉弄他，他会很生气。
I wish I hadn't pranked her; she's really upset. = 我真希望我没有捉弄她；她真的很沮丧。

被动语态:
He was pranked by his classmates. = 他被他的同学们捉弄了。
The teacher was pranked repeatedly. = 老师被反复捉弄。

疑问句:
Are you pranking me? = 你在捉弄我吗？
Who is being pranked here? = 这里谁被捉弄了？

否定句:
I didn't prank anyone today. = 我今天没有捉弄任何人。
He wasn't pranking; he was just joking. = 他不是在捉弄人；他只是在开玩笑。
