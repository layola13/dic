lovebirds:/ˈlʌvˌbɜːrdz/ | n. |爱情鸟，相亲相爱的伴侣 |Small parrots known for their strong pair bonds and affectionate behavior. They are often kept as pets.  |Example:  Many people buy lovebirds as pets because of their playful and affectionate nature.  许多人购买爱情鸟作为宠物，因为它们顽皮又深情。
