consolation:/ˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃən/| /ˌkɑːnsəˈleɪʃən/| n. |安慰；慰藉；令人安慰的事物 |Comfort received by a person after a loss or disappointment.  It can refer to the act of consoling or the comfort itself.  |例句：Her friends offered her consolation after she lost her job.  她的朋友在她失业后给了她安慰。

例句：The news that her family was safe was a great consolation.  她家人平安的消息对她来说是莫大的安慰。


近义词：comfort, solace, solace, sympathy, sympathy, support |反义词：agony, distress, misery, sorrow
