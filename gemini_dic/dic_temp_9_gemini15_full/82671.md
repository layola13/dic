guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmeɪt/ | n. v. | 粗略估计，大概估计 | An estimation made without sufficient data or precise calculation; a rough guess.  It combines "guess" and "estimate." | 例句：I guesstimated the cost of the project to be around $10,000. 我粗略估计该项目的成本约为 10,000 美元。

例句：The number of attendees was guesstimated at 500. 出席人数粗略估计为 500 人。


近义词：estimate, approximation, rough calculation, ballpark figure

反义词：precise calculation, exact measurement


词性：名词和动词
