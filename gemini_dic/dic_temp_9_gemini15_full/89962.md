horseshoed:/ˈhɔːrsʃuːd/| adj.|马蹄形的，像马蹄形的|Shaped like a horseshoe.  The word describes something having the characteristic U-shape of a horseshoe.|例句：The lake was horseshoed in shape.  这个湖是马蹄形的。|例句：The building had a horseshoed driveway. 这栋建筑有一个马蹄形的车道。|近义词：U-shaped, crescent-shaped|反义词：linear, straight
