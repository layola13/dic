dive-bombers:/ˈdaɪvˌbɒmbərz/ | n. |俯冲轰炸机 |  A dive bomber is a type of military aircraft designed to attack targets by diving steeply from a high altitude and releasing bombs at a close range.  This tactic maximizes the impact of the bombs. |Example:  The dive-bombers unleashed a devastating attack on the enemy's airfield.  俯冲轰炸机对敌人的机场发起了毁灭性的攻击。  The squadron of dive-bombers was crucial in winning the battle. 这支俯冲轰炸机中队对赢得战斗至关重要。|近义词：attack aircraft, bomber aircraft |反义词：reconnaissance aircraft (侦察机)
