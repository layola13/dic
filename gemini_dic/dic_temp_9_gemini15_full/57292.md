downside:/ˈdaʊnsaɪd/| n. |不利方面，缺点，负面影响 |The downside of this job is the long hours. 这份工作的缺点是工作时间长。|近义词：drawback, disadvantage, defect |反义词：advantage, upside


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The downside of this plan is its high cost. = 这个计划的不利之处是成本高。
What are the downsides of living in the city? = 住在城市里的缺点是什么？


一般过去时:
The downside of the project was the unexpected delays. = 这个项目的不利之处是意外的延误。
What were the downsides of their decision? = 他们的决定有哪些缺点？


现在完成时:
The downsides of our actions have become apparent. = 我们行动的不利影响已经显现。
What downsides have we encountered so far? = 到目前为止我们遇到了哪些不利方面？


过去完成时:
We hadn't foreseen the downsides of the deal before signing it. = 在签署协议之前，我们没有预料到交易的不利方面。
What downsides had they overlooked before making the investment? = 他们在投资之前忽视了哪些不利方面？


将来时:
The downsides of this policy will become clear in the future. = 这项政策的不利方面将在未来变得清晰。
What downsides will we face next year? = 明年我们将面临哪些不利方面？


虚拟语气:
If we had considered the downsides, we might have made a different decision. = 如果我们考虑了不利方面，我们可能会做出不同的决定。
I wish we had known about the downsides before we started. = 我希望我们在开始之前就知道这些不利方面。


被动语态:
The downsides of the new system were highlighted in the report. = 新系统的缺点在报告中被强调。
The downsides of this approach are being carefully considered. = 这种方法的不利方面正在被仔细考虑。


疑问句:
What are the downsides to this approach? = 这种方法有哪些缺点？
Are there any downsides to this technology? = 这种技术有什么缺点吗？


否定句:
There are no downsides to this opportunity. = 这个机会没有任何缺点。
They failed to consider the downsides of their plan. = 他们没有考虑到他们计划的缺点。


PHRASE->
the downside is... = 不利之处是...
on the downside = 不利的一面是...
weigh the downsides = 权衡缺点
consider the downsides = 考虑缺点
potential downsides = 潜在的缺点
