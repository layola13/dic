semi-nocturnal:/ˌsɛmiˈnɑːktɜːrnəl/ | adj. | 半夜行性的；夜间活动的 |  Active during the twilight hours (dusk and dawn) rather than at night.  Semi-nocturnal animals are most active during the periods of low light around sunrise and sunset. | Example:  Owls are nocturnal, while some bats are semi-nocturnal. 猫头鹰是夜行动物，而有些蝙蝠是半夜行动的。| 近义词：crepuscular | 反义词：diurnal

词源:
The word "semi-nocturnal" is a combination of "semi-" (meaning "half" or "partly") and "nocturnal" (meaning "of or relating to the night").  "Nocturnal" ultimately derives from the Latin word "nocturnus," meaning "nightly."
