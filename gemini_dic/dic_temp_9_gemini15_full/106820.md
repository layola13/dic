laborat: /ˈlæbəˌræt/ | n. |实验室 | A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching.  |例句：The scientists conducted their experiments in the laboratory. 科学家们在实验室里进行实验。|近义词：laboratory, lab |反义词：field (in contrast to a controlled environment)
