slaveholding: /ˈsleɪvˌhoʊldɪŋ/ | adj. | 奴隶主的；拥有奴隶的 |  Relating to or characteristic of the practice of owning slaves.  The term often refers to the system of slavery in the antebellum South of the United States. | Example:  The slaveholding South relied heavily on the forced labor of enslaved people for its agricultural economy.  奴隶制南方严重依赖被奴役人民的强迫劳动来维持其农业经济。  The debate over slaveholding was a major cause of the American Civil War. 关于奴隶制的辩论是美国内战的主要原因之一。| 近义词: pro-slavery, enslaving | 反义词: abolitionist, anti-slavery | 形容词
