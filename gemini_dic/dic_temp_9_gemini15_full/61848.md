employeer:/ɪmˈplɔɪər/ | n. |雇主，老板 | A person or organization that employs people.  |Example: My employer provides excellent health insurance. 我的雇主提供优良的健康保险。|近义词：boss, manager, supervisor |反义词：employee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The employer pays his employees on time. = 雇主准时支付员工工资。
My employer offers competitive benefits. = 我的雇主提供具有竞争力的福利。


一般过去时:
The employer hired ten new staff last month. = 雇主上个月聘用了十名新员工。
My former employer was very supportive. = 我以前的雇主非常支持我。


现在完成时:
The employer has already interviewed several candidates. = 雇主已经面试了几位候选人。

过去完成时:
