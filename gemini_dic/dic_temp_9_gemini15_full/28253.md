captains:/ˈkæptənz/| n.|船长；队长；首领|The word "captains" is the plural form of "captain," referring to a person who holds the highest rank in a team, ship, or organization.  It implies leadership, authority, and responsibility.|例句：The captains of the two teams shook hands before the game.  两队队长在比赛前握手。 The ship's captain steered the vessel through the storm. 船长引导船只度过暴风雨。|近义词：leaders, commanders, chiefs|反义词：followers, subordinates|名词
