patriotic:/ˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪk/ | adj. |爱国的，有爱国心的 | Showing or feeling great love and loyalty towards one's country.  |例句：He delivered a patriotic speech on Independence Day. 他在独立日发表了一篇爱国演讲。|例句：The crowd sang patriotic songs with great enthusiasm.  人群热情地演唱着爱国歌曲。|近义词：nationalistic, loyal, devoted |反义词：unpatriotic, disloyal, traitorous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is patriotic. = 他是爱国的。
She feels patriotic. = 她感到爱国。


一般过去时:
He was patriotic in his youth. = 他年轻时是爱国的。
They were patriotic citizens. = 他们是爱国的公民。


现在完成时:
He has always been patriotic. = 他一直都很爱国。
She has remained patriotic throughout her life. = 她一生都保持着爱国之心。


过去完成时:
He had been patriotic even before the war. = 甚至在战争爆发之前，他就已经很爱国了。
They had always been patriotic to their country. = 他们一直都对自己的国家忠贞爱国。


将来时:
He will continue to be patriotic. = 他将继续爱国。
She will remain patriotic in the face of adversity. = 面对逆境，她将依然保持爱国之心。


虚拟语气:
If he were more patriotic, he would support the national cause. = 如果他更爱国，他会支持国家事业。
I wish people were more patriotic. = 我希望人们更爱国。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
