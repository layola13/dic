libretto:/lɪˈbretəʊ/| n. | 歌剧或清唱剧的歌词；脚本 | The libretto is the text of an opera or oratorio, including the dialogue, arias, and recitatives.  It's the story told through song and spoken word. | Example: The libretto for "Carmen" is full of drama and passion.  卡门歌剧的歌词充满了戏剧性和激情。|近义词：text, script, words | 反义词：score (as it refers to the music)
