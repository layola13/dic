gargol:/ˈɡɑːrɡəl/| n.| 滴水嘴，排水兽，怪兽形排水管|A grotesque carved figure, typically a creature's head or a human face, projecting from a building's roof gutter or drainpipe to act as a waterspout.  It is often found in Gothic architecture. |例句：The old building was adorned with numerous gargoyles, each with a unique and terrifying expression.  这座古老的建筑装饰着许多滴水嘴，每一个都具有独特而可怕的表情。

The gargoyle on the cathedral spouted water during the heavy rain. 大教堂上的滴水嘴在暴雨中喷水。|近义词：waterspout, grotesque|反义词：none|名词
