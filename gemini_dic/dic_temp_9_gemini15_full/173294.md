quasi-convinced:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈvɪnst/| adj. |几乎确信，似信非信|Almost convinced; showing a degree of conviction but not completely certain.  It suggests a state of being partly persuaded but still harboring some doubt or hesitation.|例句：He was quasi-convinced that the evidence pointed to his brother's guilt, but a nagging uncertainty remained. 他几乎确信证据指向他兄弟有罪，但仍然存在挥之不去的不确定性。|例句：While the data was promising, she remained quasi-convinced, needing further confirmation before reaching a firm conclusion. 虽然数据很有希望，但她仍然似信非信，需要进一步证实才能得出最终结论。|近义词：partially convinced, almost persuaded, tentatively convinced|反义词： unconvinced, certain, completely persuaded
