ghost's: /ɡoʊsts/ | n. |鬼魂的；幽灵的 |  The possessive form of "ghost," indicating belonging to or associated with a ghost.  It describes something related to or characteristic of ghosts.  | 例句：I felt a ghost's touch on my shoulder. 我感觉我的肩膀上有鬼魂的触摸。  The ghost's wail echoed through the empty house.  鬼魂的哭嚎声回荡在空荡荡的房子里。 | 近义词：spectral, ethereal, supernatural | 反义词：corporeal, tangible, real.
