heaving:/ˈhiːvɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 举起；起伏；喘息；剧烈呕吐 |  To lift or carry something heavy with great effort; to rise and fall irregularly; to breathe heavily; to vomit violently. |例句：He was heaving the heavy box into the truck. 他正费力地把沉重的箱子搬上卡车。
The ocean was heaving in the storm.  暴风雨中海洋波涛汹涌。
近义词：lifting, hoisting, rising, surging, gasping, retching | 反义词：lowering, descending, calm, smooth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is heaving the sack of potatoes. = 他正在扛着那袋土豆。
The waves are heaving gently against the shore. = 海浪轻轻地拍打着海岸。

一般过去时:
She heaved a sigh of relief. = 她舒了一口气。
The ship heaved violently in the storm. = 船在暴风雨中剧烈摇晃。

现在完成时:
He has heaved all the furniture upstairs. = 他已经把所有的家具都搬到楼上了。
The ground has heaved after the earthquake. = 地震后地面隆起。

过去完成时:
They had heaved the anchor before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经抛锚了。
The old building had heaved and groaned under the weight of the snow. = 老房子在雪的重量下发出吱呀声并摇晃。

将来时:
He will heave the anchor at dawn. = 他将在黎明时抛锚。
The sea will heave and swell with the incoming tide. = 随着涨潮，大海将起伏涌动。

虚拟语气:
If he had heaved the stone harder, he might have broken the window. = 如果他用力抛石头，也许就能打破窗户。
I wish I hadn't heaved so much last night; I feel terrible. = 我真希望昨晚没吃那么多，我现在感觉糟透了。

被动语态:
The heavy box was heaved onto the truck. = 那只沉重的箱子被搬上了卡车。
The boat was heaved about mercilessly by the waves. = 小船被海浪无情地抛来抛去。

疑问句:
Is he heaving the anchor? = 他正在抛锚吗？
Are the waves heaving violently? = 海浪剧烈翻滚吗？

否定句:
He didn't heave the stone far enough. = 他没有把石头扔够远。
The ship didn't heave much in the calm sea. = 在平静的海面上，船没有怎么摇晃。


PHRASE->
heave a sigh:  舒了一口气

heave to: (船) 停航
heave up: 呕吐


词源:
From Old English *hebban, meaning "to lift, raise".  The modern sense of "to vomit" developed later.
