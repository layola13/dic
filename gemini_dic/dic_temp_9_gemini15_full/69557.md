fellowshiped:/ˈfɛloʊʃɪpt/| v.|授予奖学金；结社；联谊|To grant a fellowship to someone; to associate or unite in a fellowship.|例句：He was fellowshiped by the university for his outstanding research. 他因其杰出的研究而获得大学的奖学金。 He fellowshiped with his colleagues after work. 他下班后与同事联谊。|近义词：granted, associated, united|反义词：separated, isolated


USAGE->

The university fellowships many promising students. = 大学为许多有前途的学生提供奖学金。
They fellowship regularly to discuss their research. = 他们定期联谊讨论他们的研究。
