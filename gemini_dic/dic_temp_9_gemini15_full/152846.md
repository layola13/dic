pedagogical:/ˌpedəˈɡɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 教育学的；教学法的；教育的 | Relating to the method and practice of teaching.  It emphasizes the art, science, and principles of teaching. | The teacher employed various pedagogical methods to engage her students.  老师采用了多种教学方法来吸引她的学生。 | didactic, instructional, educative | unpedagogical
