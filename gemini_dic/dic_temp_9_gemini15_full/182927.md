rephrase:/ˌriːˈfreɪz/| vt.|改述；重新表达；解释|To express something again in different words, typically in order to make it clearer or more effective.|例句：Could you rephrase that question?  你能重新表达一下那个问题吗？

例句：The lawyer rephrased the witness's statement to make it more understandable for the jury. 律师改述了证人的证词，以便陪审团更容易理解。


近义词：paraphrase, restate, reformulate|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to leave something un-rephrased. |动词
