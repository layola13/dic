gloaters:/ˈɡləʊtərz/| n. |幸灾乐祸的人|A person who gloats.  Someone who takes pleasure in the misfortune of others. They often express their satisfaction openly and sometimes cruelly. |例句：The gloaters were quick to point out his mistakes. 幸灾乐祸的人很快指出他的错误。

例句：There were a few gloaters in the crowd after the team lost. 球队输球后，人群中有一些幸灾乐祸的人。


近义词：smugglers,  rejoicers (in others' misfortune),  malicious gossipers


反义词：sympathizers,  empathizers,  comforters


词性：名词
