reoccupying:/ˌriːˈɒkjʊpaɪɪŋ/ | v. |重新占领；重新占用 |  The act of occupying something again after a period of not being occupied.  This implies a previous occupation that was interrupted or ended, followed by a resumption of control or use. |例句：The army is reoccupying the lost territory.  军队正在重新占领失地。

The city is reoccupying the abandoned buildings to house refugees.  该市正在重新占用废弃建筑物来安置难民。

近义词：reclaiming, recovering, resuming, retaking | 反义词：vacating, abandoning, relinquishing | 词性：动词

一般现在时:
They are reoccupying the building after renovations. = 他们装修后正在重新占用这栋建筑。
We are reoccupying our old summer house this year. = 今年我们重新占用我们的旧夏令营。

一般过去时:
The soldiers reoccupied the city after a brief retreat. = 士兵们短暂撤退后重新占领了这座城市。
She reoccupied her childhood home after her parents passed away. = 她父母去世后重新住进了她童年时的家。

现在完成时:
The government has reoccupied the disputed islands. = 政府已经重新占领了有争议的岛屿。
They have reoccupied the land after years of legal battles. = 经过多年的法律诉讼后，他们重新占领了这块土地。

过去完成时:
The rebels had reoccupied the capital before the peace talks began. = 在和平谈判开始之前，叛军已经重新占领了首都。
Before the new tenants arrived, they had reoccupied the apartment. = 在新租户到来之前，他们已经重新占用了公寓。

将来时:
The company will reoccupy the office space next month. = 公司下个月将重新占用办公空间。
The family will reoccupy their house after the repairs are completed. = 修理完成后，这家人将重新入住他们的房子。

虚拟语气:
If the army hadn't retreated, they would have been reoccupying the territory now. = 如果军队没有撤退，他们现在就会重新占领这块领土了。
If they had enough money, they would be reoccupying their old farm. = 如果他们有足够的钱，他们就会重新占用他们的旧农场了。

被动语态:

否定句:
