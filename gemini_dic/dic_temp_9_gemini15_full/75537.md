USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't lend itself to verb conjugations in the same way as "to be" or "to do."  Therefore, typical verb tense examples are not applicable.  The usage focuses on its noun function in sentences.

Example 1:  He dismissed their promises as mere fubbery. 他认为他们的承诺只是虚假的诺言。
Example 2: The entire presentation was filled with fubbery and lacked substance.  整个陈述充满了废话且缺乏实质内容。
