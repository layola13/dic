deliberatively:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪvli/| adv.|故意地，慎重地，从容地|In a deliberate manner; intentionally.  It suggests careful consideration and a conscious choice before acting.|例句：He deliberately ignored her question. 他故意忽略了她的问题。
She spoke deliberately, choosing her words carefully. 她说话很慎重，仔细斟酌着词语。|近义词：intentionally, purposefully, consciously, thoughtfully|反义词：accidentally, unintentionally, carelessly, impulsively|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deliberately avoids eye contact. 他故意避免眼神接触。
She deliberately chooses her words. 她仔细斟酌自己的话语。

一般过去时:
He deliberately misled the police. 他故意误导了警察。
She deliberately broke the vase. 她故意打碎了花瓶。

现在完成时:
He has deliberately damaged his reputation. 他故意损坏了自己的名声。
She has deliberately avoided contact with him. 她一直故意避开和他联系。

过去完成时:
He had deliberately hidden the evidence. 他事先故意藏匿了证据。
She had deliberately planned her revenge. 她事先精心策划了她的报复。

将来时:
He will deliberately sabotage the project. 他将故意破坏这个项目。
She will deliberately ignore his calls. 她将故意不接他的电话。

虚拟语气:
If he had deliberately lied, he would be in trouble. 如果他故意撒谎，他就会有麻烦了。
If she had deliberately hurt him, she would apologize. 如果她故意伤害了他，她会道歉的。

被动语态:
The damage was deliberately inflicted. 损害是被蓄意造成的。
The meeting was deliberately scheduled late. 会议故意安排得很晚。

疑问句:
Did he deliberately exclude her from the invitation list? 他是不是故意把她排除在邀请名单之外？
Was the mistake deliberately made? 这个错误是故意犯的吗？

否定句:
He did not deliberately hurt her feelings. 他并非故意伤害她的感情。
She did not deliberately ignore his calls. 她并没有故意不接他的电话。
