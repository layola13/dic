lizard-like:/ˈlɪzərdlaɪk/| adj. |像蜥蜴的；蜥蜴似的|Resembling a lizard in appearance or behavior.  It suggests the qualities often associated with lizards, such as being scaly, cold-blooded, agile, or stealthy.|例句：The creature moved with a lizard-like grace.  那生物以蜥蜴般优雅的动作移动着。

例句：The reptile's skin had a lizard-like texture.  那爬行动物的皮肤有着像蜥蜴一样的纹理。


近义词：reptilian, saurian, scaled


反义词：mammalian, feathered, furry


词性:形容词
