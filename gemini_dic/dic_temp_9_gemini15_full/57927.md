drink:/drɪŋk/| /drɪŋk/| v. n.|喝，饮；饮料|To swallow liquid.|I drank a glass of water.  我喝了一杯水。 He had a drink before dinner. 他在晚餐前喝了一杯酒。|sip, gulp, quaff| abstain from, refuse|v. n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I drink water every day. = 我每天喝水。
She drinks tea in the morning. = 她早上喝茶。

一般过去时:
He drank a lot of beer last night. = 他昨晚喝了很多啤酒。
We drank champagne at the party. = 我们在派对上喝香槟。

现在完成时:
I have drunk three cups of coffee today. = 我今天已经喝了三杯咖啡。
She has drunk all the milk. = 她已经把牛奶都喝光了。

过去完成时:
They had drunk all the wine before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经把酒喝光了。
He had drunk too much before he drove. = 他开车前喝得太多了。

将来时:
I will drink orange juice tomorrow. = 我明天要喝橙汁。
She will drink water with her meal. = 她吃饭时会喝水。

虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would drink some water. = 如果我渴了，我会喝些水。
I wish I had drunk less wine last night. = 我真希望昨晚少喝点酒。

被动语态:
The wine was drunk quickly. = 酒很快就被喝光了。
The soda was drunk by the children. = 苏打水被孩子们喝掉了。

疑问句:
Do you drink coffee? = 你喝咖啡吗？
What are you drinking? = 你在喝什么？

否定句:
I don't drink alcohol. = 我不喝酒。
She didn't drink anything. = 她什么都没喝。


PHRASE->
drink up = 喝光
have a drink = 喝一杯
drink to = 为...干杯
soft drink = 软饮料
alcoholic drink = 酒精饮料
