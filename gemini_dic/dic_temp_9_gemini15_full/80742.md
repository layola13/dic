gosherd:/ˈɡɒʃərd/|  n.|  牧羊人，看护人 | A gosherd is a person who tends sheep or other livestock.  The word is primarily used in dialects of England, Scotland, and Ireland.  It's an older, less common term for a shepherd.| Example: The gosherd led his flock across the moor.  牧羊人带领他的羊群穿过荒野。|近义词：shepherd, herdsman |反义词: None specifically.  The antonym would depend on the context, e.g.,  someone who neglects animals.
