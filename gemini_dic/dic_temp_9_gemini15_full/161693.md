poverty:/ˈpɒvəti/ /ˈpɑːvərti/| n. |贫穷，贫困 |The state of being extremely poor.  It often involves a lack of basic necessities such as food, shelter, and clothing, and can also encompass social and economic disadvantages. |例句：Millions of people live in poverty around the world. 全球数百万人生活在贫困之中。|例句：Extreme poverty is a major global challenge. 极端贫困是全球面临的主要挑战。|近义词：destitution, indigence, penury |反义词：wealth, affluence, opulence |名词

词源:
The word "poverty" originates from the Old French word "povert," which in turn comes from the Latin word "paupertas."  "Paupertas" is derived from "pauper," meaning "poor person."  The evolution reflects the enduring and widespread nature of the concept of poverty across languages and cultures.
