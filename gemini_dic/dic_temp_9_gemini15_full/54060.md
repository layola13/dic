disdeify:/dɪsˈdiːɪfaɪ/| vt.|取消神格；贬低；诋毁 |To remove from a state of being deified; to deprive of divine honors; to degrade from divine rank or status; to belittle or denigrate.  |例句：The once-revered leader was quickly disdeified after the scandal broke.  = 丑闻爆发后，这位曾经受人尊敬的领导人很快就被贬低了。

例句：Their attempts to disdeify the national hero were met with strong public resistance. = 他们试图贬低民族英雄的企图遭到了公众的强烈抵制。

近义词：dethrone, degrade, denigrate, disparage, belittle

反义词：deify, exalt, glorify, idolize
