cesarean:/sɪˈzəriən/| n.|剖腹产，剖宫产|A surgical operation for delivering a baby by cutting through the mother's abdomen and uterus.|例句：She had a cesarean section because of complications during labor. 她因为分娩并发症而进行了剖腹产。

例句：The doctor recommended a cesarean delivery due to the baby's breech position. 由于婴儿臀位，医生建议剖腹产。


近义词：C-section, abdominal delivery|反义词：vaginal delivery
