nonfertility:/ˌnɒnfɜːˈtɪləti/ | n. |不育；生育力低下 |The inability to produce offspring; sterility.  This term often refers to the inability of a couple to conceive a child, encompassing both male and female factors. |Example:  The couple sought medical advice due to their nonfertility.  这对夫妇因不育而寻求医疗建议。|近义词：infertility, sterility, barrenness |反义词：fertility, fecundity |名词
