rerun:/ˌriːˈrʌn/| v. n. |重播；重演；再次发生|To run or perform again; to repeat a performance, broadcast, or event.|例句：The movie will be rerun next week. 这部电影下周将重播。|例句：The experiment was rerun with different parameters. 实验用不同的参数重新进行了。|近义词：repeat, replay, re-enact |反义词：premiere, debut


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rerun old sitcoms every night. = 他们每晚重播旧情景喜剧。
The network reruns classic films on weekends. = 该电视台周末重播经典电影。


一般过去时:
The news channel rerun the breaking news report. = 新闻频道重播了突发新闻报道。
