dampens:/ˈdæmpənz/ | vt. | 使潮湿；抑制；减弱 | to make something slightly wet; to make something less strong or intense | The rain dampened the bonfire. 雨淋湿了篝火。  The news dampened their spirits. 这个消息打击了他们的士气。 | lessens, weakens, diminishes, subdues | intensifies, enhances, strengthens, boosts | 动词
