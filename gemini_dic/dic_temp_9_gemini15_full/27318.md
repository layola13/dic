calypsonian:/ˌkælɪpˈsoʊniən/| n. |卡利普索歌手；卡利普索音乐家 | A performer or composer of calypso music.  Calypso is a style of music originating in Trinidad and Tobago, known for its witty and often satirical lyrics. Calypsonians often use their music to comment on social and political issues. | Example:  The calypsonian's performance was filled with sharp social commentary.  这位卡利普索歌手的表演充满了尖锐的社会评论。|近义词：calypso singer, calypso artist |反义词：无
