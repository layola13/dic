lozenger:/ˈlɒzɪndʒər/| n. |锭剂，薄荷糖|A small, hard candy, typically medicated, intended to be sucked slowly in the mouth.  It is often used to soothe a sore throat or cough.|例句：She sucked on a menthol lozenge to relieve her cough. 她含着一颗薄荷锭剂来缓解咳嗽。|例句：The doctor prescribed some cough lozenges. 医生开了些止咳锭剂。|近义词：pastille, troche|反义词:None
