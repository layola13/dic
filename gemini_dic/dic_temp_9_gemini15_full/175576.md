randomises:/ˈrændəmaɪzɪz/| v.|随机化，使随机 |To arrange or distribute (things) randomly; to make something random.|The computer program randomises the order of the questions in the test.  电脑程序将测试题的顺序随机化。|The lottery randomises the winning numbers. 彩票随机确定中奖号码。|systematises, shuffles |organises, orders|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The software randomises the data. = 软件随机化数据。
He randomises the participants in his experiments. = 他在他的实验中随机分配参与者。

一般过去时:
The teacher randomised the seating chart. = 老师随机安排了座位表。

过去完成时:
