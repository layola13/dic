fireplaces:/ˈfaɪərpleɪsɪz/ | n. |壁炉（复数）|A structure of brick, stone, or metal that is built into a wall to contain a fire.  It typically has a chimney to carry away smoke.|  例句：The old house had several beautiful fireplaces. 这栋老房子有好几个漂亮的壁炉。

例句：We gathered around the fireplace to stay warm. 我们围坐在壁炉旁取暖。


近义词：hearth (壁炉炉膛), firebox (炉膛)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词)


词性:名词
