lubrification:/ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |润滑；润滑作用 |The process of reducing friction between surfaces in contact, typically by using a lubricant.  Lubrification is essential for the smooth operation of machinery and to prevent wear and tear.|例句：Regular lubrication of the engine is crucial for its longevity. 定期润滑发动机对其寿命至关重要。

例句：The technician checked the lubrication system of the car. 技师检查了汽车的润滑系统。
