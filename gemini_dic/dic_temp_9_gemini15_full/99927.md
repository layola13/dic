internes:/ɪnˈtɜːrnz/| n.|实习生；实习医生|Interns are students or graduates who work in a company or organization for a fixed period to gain experience.|例句：Many medical interns work long hours. 许多实习医生工作时间很长。|近义词：trainee, resident|反义词：supervisor, employer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The interns work hard every day. = 实习生每天努力工作。
She interns at a law firm. = 她在一家律师事务所实习。


一般过去时:
He interned at Google last summer. = 他去年夏天在谷歌实习。
They interned at a hospital for three months. = 他们在医院实习了三个月。


现在完成时:
I have interned at several companies. = 我在几家公司实习过。
She has already completed her internship. = 她已经完成了她的实习。


过去完成时:
Before he started his job, he had interned at a bank. = 在他开始工作之前，他在一家银行实习过。
They had interned for a year before they were hired. = 他们在被录用之前实习了一年。


将来时:
I will intern at a tech company next year. = 我明年将在一家科技公司实习。
She will have completed her internship by June. = 到六月她将完成她的实习。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had interned at a bigger company. = 我希望我在一家更大的公司实习过。
