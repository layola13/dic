inspirative: /ɪnˈspaɪrətɪv/ | adj. | 鼓舞人心的，激励人心的 |  Giving inspiration; tending to inspire.  This adjective describes something that motivates, encourages, or stimulates creativity, thought, or action.  It evokes feelings of hope, enthusiasm, and a desire to achieve something. |

例句：

His inspirative speech moved the audience to tears.  = 他鼓舞人心的演讲使听众感动落泪。

The inspirative artwork filled the gallery with a sense of wonder. = 鼓舞人心的艺术作品使画廊充满了奇迹感。


近义词：motivational, inspirational, stimulating, encouraging, uplifting

反义词：discouraging, depressing, dispiriting, uninspiring


词性:形容词
