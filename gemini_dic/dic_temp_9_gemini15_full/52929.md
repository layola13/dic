din:/dɪn/| n.|喧嚣，嘈杂声|a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise.|The din of the city traffic was deafening. 城市交通的喧嚣震耳欲聋。|近义词：noise, clamor, racket|反义词：silence, quiet, hush|
