This refers to a lack of harmony or agreement between two or more things.  It can apply to various contexts, including software, personalities, ideas, or systems. | Example: The noncompatibleness of the two operating systems prevented the data transfer.  两个操作系统的不兼容性阻止了数据传输。  The noncompatibleness of their personalities led to frequent arguments. 他们性格的不兼容导致了频繁的争吵。 |近义词： incompatibility, conflict, discord, antagonism |反义词： compatibility, harmony, concord, agreement |名词
