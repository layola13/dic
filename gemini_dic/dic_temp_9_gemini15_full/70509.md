filed:/faɪld/ | v. |归档；提交；提出；磨平；(金属)锉平 | To put something in a file or system for keeping records.  |例句：I filed the documents in the cabinet. 我把文件归档在柜子里。

例句：The lawyer filed a lawsuit against the company. 律师对该公司提起诉讼。


近义词：submit, register, record, archive | 反义词：retrieve, unarchive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He files his taxes every year. = 他每年都报税。
She files her nails regularly. = 她定期修指甲。


一般过去时:
I filed a complaint yesterday. = 我昨天提交了一份投诉。
They filed the report last week. = 他们上周提交了报告。


现在完成时:
She has filed all the necessary paperwork. = 她已经提交了所有必要的文书工作。
He has filed three patents this year. = 他今年已经申请了三项专利。


过去完成时:
He had filed the report before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前提交了报告。
They had filed several complaints before this one. = 在这次投诉之前，他们已经提交了几次投诉。


将来时:
I will file the documents tomorrow. = 我明天将提交文件。
She will file a lawsuit next week. = 她下周将提起诉讼。


虚拟语气:
If I had filed the report on time, I wouldn't be in trouble. = 如果我按时提交报告，我就不会有麻烦了。
I wish I had filed my taxes earlier. = 我真希望我早点报税。


被动语态:
The documents were filed correctly. = 文件被正确归档。
The complaint was filed last month. = 投诉在上个月提交。


疑问句:
Have you filed your taxes yet? = 你报税了吗？
Did he file the application? = 他提交申请了吗？


否定句:
I haven't filed my taxes yet. = 我还没有报税。
He didn't file the report on time. = 他没有按时提交报告。


PHRASE->
filed away = 归档；被遗忘
filed under = 分类于…之下
filed for = 申请（破产、离婚等）
on file =存档的；备案的
cross-filed =交叉备案


词源:
From Old French *filer "to spin, to file," from Vulgar Latin *filire, from Latin filum "thread."  The sense development is from the action of spinning thread to the action of using a file (tool) to create fine lines and eventually to the filing of documents in an orderly manner.
