jilter:/ˈdʒɪltər/| vt.|抛弃；甩掉；拒绝；戏弄|To suddenly end a romantic relationship with someone.  To reject or abandon someone. |例句：He jilted his fiancée at the altar. 他在婚礼祭坛上抛弃了他的未婚妻。

例句：She jilted him for a richer man. 她为了一个更富有的男人抛弃了他。


近义词：dump, desert, abandon, forsake|反义词：marry, commit to, cherish
