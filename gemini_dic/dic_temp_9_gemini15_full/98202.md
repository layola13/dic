insecticides:/ɪnˈsɛktɪsaɪdz/| n.|杀虫剂|Chemical substances used to kill insects.|例句：Farmers often use insecticides to protect their crops from pests.  农民经常使用杀虫剂来保护他们的农作物免受害虫侵害。|例句：The overuse of insecticides can harm beneficial insects and pollute the environment.  杀虫剂的过度使用会危害益虫并污染环境。|近义词：pesticides, insect killers|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to methods promoting insect growth or preservation.
