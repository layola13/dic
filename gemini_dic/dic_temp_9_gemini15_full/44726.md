cruiser:/ˈkruːzər/| n.|巡洋舰；巡洋车；巡警车|A cruiser is a type of ship, car, or motorcycle designed for long-distance travel or patrol.  It can refer to a large warship, a comfortable car, or a police vehicle.|例句：The police cruiser sped to the scene of the accident. 警车迅速赶往事故现场。

例句：He bought a new cruiser motorcycle for his weekend trips. 他买了一辆新的巡洋摩托车用于周末旅行。|近义词：warship, patrol car, motorcycle|反义词： fighter (in the context of warships)
