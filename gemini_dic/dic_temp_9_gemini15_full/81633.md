greedier:/ˈɡriːdiər/| adj.|更贪婪的，更渴望的|more greedy; having or showing a strong desire for more than one needs or deserves.|The greedier the person, the less likely they are to be happy.|近义词：avaricious, covetous, rapacious|反义词：generous, altruistic
