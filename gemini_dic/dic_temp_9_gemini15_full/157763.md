placed:/pleɪst/| v. |放置，安置，摆放|To put something in a particular position.|The books were placed on the shelf. 书被放在书架上。|put, set, position|remove, take away, displace|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He places the flowers in a vase. 他把花插进花瓶里。
She places great emphasis on education. 她非常重视教育。


一般过去时:
I placed the order yesterday. 我昨天下了订单。
They placed the blame on him. 他们把责任归咎于他。


现在完成时:
She has placed all the furniture. 她已经把所有的家具都摆放好了。
He has placed a bet on the horse race. 他已在赛马上下注。


过去完成时:
They had placed the explosives before the building collapsed. 大楼倒塌前他们已经放置了炸药。
She had placed her trust in him, but he betrayed her. 她曾经信任他，但他背叛了她。


将来时:
I will place the call tomorrow morning. 我明天早上会打电话。
They will place the new statue in the town square. 他们将在城镇广场放置新的雕像。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would place my faith in her. 如果我是你，我会相信她。
I wish I had placed a larger order. 我真希望我下了更大的订单。


被动语态:
The vase was placed carefully on the table. 花瓶被小心地放在桌子上。
The blame was placed squarely on the shoulders of the manager. 责任完全落在了经理的肩上。


疑问句:
Where did you place your keys? 你把钥匙放在哪里了？
Where was the painting placed originally? 这幅画最初被放在哪里？


否定句:
I didn't place the order on time. 我没有及时下订单。
They didn't place any value on his opinion. 他们并不重视他的意见。


PHRASE->
placed an order: 下订单
placed a call: 打电话
well-placed: 位置合适的，有影响力的
highly placed: 位高权重的
out of place: 不得体，不相称
