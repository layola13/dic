crofts:/krɒfts/| n.|小农场；小块耕地|A croft is a small farm, typically in Scotland, cultivated by a single family.  It is often associated with self-sufficiency and a close relationship with the land.|The croft produced enough potatoes and vegetables to feed the family.|近义词：smallholding, farm, plot|反义词：large estate, plantation|名词
