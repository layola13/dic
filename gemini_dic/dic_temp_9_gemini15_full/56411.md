dolin:/ˈdoʊlɪn/| n. |洼地，岩溶漏斗|A doline is a closed depression found in karst landscapes, formed by the dissolution of soluble rocks like limestone. They are typically bowl-shaped or funnel-shaped and can vary greatly in size and depth.  |例句：The farmer's field contained several dolines, making cultivation difficult.  这个农民的田地里有好几个洼地，使得耕作很困难。
