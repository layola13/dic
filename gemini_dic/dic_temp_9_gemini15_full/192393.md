scritch:/skrɪtʃ/| v.|刮擦声；划拉声|To make a harsh, scraping sound, as of fingernails on a chalkboard.|例句：The cat scritched at the door. 猫在门上抓挠。|例句：The metal scratched against the stone, making a scritching sound. 金属摩擦石头，发出刮擦声。|近义词：scratch, scrape, scrape, scratch, rasp|反义词：smooth, silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat scritches at the door every morning. = 猫每天早上都在门上抓挠。
He scritches his pen across the paper. = 他用笔在纸上划拉。

一般过去时:
The dog scritched its back against the tree. = 狗在树上蹭背。
She scritched the chalk across the blackboard. = 她用粉笔在黑板上划。

现在完成时:
The kids have scritched the paint off the wall. = 孩子们把墙上的油漆刮掉了。
The mice have scritched holes in the floorboards. = 老鼠在木地板上刮出了洞。

过去完成时:
He had scritched his head before he noticed the solution. = 他挠了挠头才注意到解决方法。
They had scritched the message into the stone before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们把信息刻在了石头上。

将来时:
The wind will scritch against the windows all night. = 风会整夜刮擦窗户。
I think the dog will scritch at the door if we don't let him in. = 我想如果我们不让他进来，狗会抓门。

虚拟语气:
If he scritched the lottery ticket harder, he might win. = 如果他更用力地刮彩票，他可能会中奖。（此处用法略牵强，更自然的表达方式是“scratch”)
I wish the nails wouldn't scritch against the wall. = 我希望指甲不会刮到墙上。

被动语态:
The wall was scritched by the cat. = 墙被猫抓了。
The paint was scritched off the car. = 车漆被刮掉了。

疑问句:
Does the cat scritch the furniture often? = 猫经常抓家具吗？
Did the noise you heard scritch across the floor? = 你听到的声音是在地板上划过吗？

否定句:
The pen didn't scritch; it wrote smoothly. = 钢笔没有划，写得很流畅。
The dog didn't scritch the carpet; it just lay down. = 狗没有抓地毯，它只是躺下了。
