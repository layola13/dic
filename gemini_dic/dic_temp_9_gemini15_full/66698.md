exonerate:/ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪt/ | vt. | 宣告无罪；免除责任；为…开脱 | To clear (someone) from a charge of guilt or wrongdoing.  |例句：The court exonerated him of all charges. 法院宣告他所有指控不成立。

例句：The new evidence exonerated the suspect.  新的证据证明了嫌疑人的清白。


近义词：acquit, absolve, vindicate, clear | 反义词：convict, incriminate, condemn


词源：

From Late Latin *exonerāre, from Latin ex- (“out of, from”) + onus (“burden, load”).  The sense evolved from "to unload, to free from a burden" to "to free from blame or responsibility."
