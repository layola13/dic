carbonates:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪts/| n.|碳酸盐|Carbonates are salts of carbonic acid, formed by the reaction of carbonic acid with a base.|例句：Calcium carbonate is a common component of limestone.  碳酸钙是石灰岩的常见成分。|例句：Many minerals are carbonates, including calcite and dolomite. 许多矿物都是碳酸盐，包括方解石和白云石。|近义词：carbonic salts|反义词：无
