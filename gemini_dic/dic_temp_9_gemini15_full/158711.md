ploughgang:/ˈplaʊɡæŋ/| n.|耕作队，耕田小组|A group of people working together to plough a field.  It often refers to a team working in a cooperative or coordinated manner,  not necessarily just in agriculture.|例句：The ploughgang worked tirelessly from sunrise to sunset. 耕作队从日出到日落辛勤工作。

The entire village formed a ploughgang to get the harvest in before the storm.  整个村庄组成一个耕作队，以便在暴风雨来临之前收割庄稼。|近义词：team, crew, gang (in the sense of a working group)|反义词：individual workers, lone worker|名词
