shrub:/ʃrʌb/| n. |灌木，矮树|A shrub is a woody plant that is smaller than a tree and usually has multiple stems.  Shrubs are often used in landscaping for their attractive foliage and flowers. |The garden was filled with colorful shrubs. 花园里种满了色彩艳丽的灌木。  We planted a rose shrub near the fence. 我们在栅栏附近种了一棵玫瑰灌木。|bush, bushwood|tree|
