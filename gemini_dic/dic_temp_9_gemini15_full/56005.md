do-gooder:/ˈduːˌɡʊdər/ | n. |假仁假义的人，多管闲事的人 | A person who is excessively eager to do good deeds, often in a way that is annoying or ineffective to others.  They often interfere in matters that don't concern them. |例句：He's such a do-gooder, always trying to tell everyone what to do. 他真是个假仁假义的人，总是试图告诉每个人该做什么。

例句：She's a do-gooder who spends her weekends volunteering at the local soup kitchen, even though she barely has time for herself. 她是个假仁假义的人，周末都在当地的施粥所做志愿者，即使她几乎没有时间照顾自己。


近义词：meddler, busybody, reformer, philanthropist (sometimes, depending on context) | 反义词：apath, cynic

USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun,  and therefore doesn't conjugate like a verb.  The usage focuses on how the noun is used in different sentence structures.

一般现在时:
He is a do-gooder. = 他是个假仁假义的人。
The do-gooder volunteers at the animal shelter. = 那个假仁假义的人在动物收容所做志愿者。


一般过去时:
He was a do-gooder. = 他是个假仁假义的人。(过去时态)
The do-gooder helped the homeless. = 那个假仁假义的人帮助了无家可归的人。(过去时态)


现在完成时:
He has been a do-gooder all his life. = 他一辈子都是个假仁假义的人。
She has always been a do-gooder. = 她一直是个假仁假义的人。


过去完成时:
He had been a do-gooder before he became cynical. = 在他变得愤世嫉俗之前，他是个假仁假义的人。
She had been a do-gooder for many years. = 她做了很多年的假仁假义的事情。


将来时:
He will be a do-gooder, I'm afraid. = 我担心他会成为一个假仁假义的人。
She will continue to be a do-gooder. = 她将继续做一个假仁假义的人。


虚拟语气:
If he were a do-gooder, he would help. = 如果他是个假仁假义的人，他会帮忙的。
I wish he weren't such a do-gooder. = 我希望他不要这么假仁假义。

疑问句:

否定句:
