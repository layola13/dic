huguenot:/ˈhjuːɡənɒt/| n. |胡格诺派教徒；法国新教徒|A member of the Protestant Reformed Church of France in the 16th–18th centuries.  Huguenots were often persecuted for their religious beliefs.|The Huguenots faced intense persecution in France.  胡格诺派教徒在法国面临着残酷的迫害。|Protestant, Calvinist|Catholic, persecutor.|名词
