quarterbacking: /ˈkwɔːrtərˌbækiŋ/ | n. |四分卫的职责；指挥；领导 | The act of playing the quarterback position in American football, or of directing or managing something like a team or project.  It implies strategic thinking, decision-making, and leadership. |例句：His quarterbacking skills were crucial to the team's victory.  他的四分卫技巧对球队的胜利至关重要。

例句：The CEO's quarterbacking of the merger negotiations ensured a smooth transition.  首席执行官对并购谈判的指挥确保了顺利过渡。

近义词：directing, managing, leading, strategizing |反义词：following, obeying, passively participating
