detriment:/ˈdɛtrɪmənt/| n. |损害；损害物；不利因素 |Something that causes damage, injury, or loss; a disadvantage.  It often implies a significant negative impact. |His addiction was a detriment to his career. 他的瘾毁了他的事业。|近义词：harm, damage, injury, disadvantage, impairment |反义词：benefit, advantage, boon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pollution causes a detriment to the environment. = 污染对环境造成了损害。
His laziness is a detriment to his studies. = 他的懒惰妨碍了他的学习。


一般过去时:
The storm caused significant detriment to the coastal areas. = 暴风雨给沿海地区造成了巨大的破坏。
His poor decisions were a detriment to the company's success. = 他糟糕的决定损害了公司的成功。


现在完成时:
The drought has caused considerable detriment to the agricultural sector. = 干旱对农业部门造成了相当大的损害。
Years of neglect have been a detriment to the building's structure. = 多年的忽视损害了建筑物的结构。


过去完成时:
The previous management’s failures had already caused considerable detriment to the company's reputation. = 之前的管理失败已经对公司的声誉造成了相当大的损害。
The prolonged conflict had been a detriment to the nation's economy. = 长期的冲突损害了国家的经济。


将来时:
The proposed changes will be a detriment to the efficiency of the system. = 提出的变更将损害系统的效率。
Continued deforestation will be a detriment to the environment. = 持续的森林砍伐将损害环境。


虚拟语气:
If he continued down this path, it would be a detriment to his health. = 如果他继续走这条路，将会损害他的健康。
I wish the lack of funding hadn’t been such a detriment to the project. = 我真希望资金不足不会如此损害这个项目。


被动语态:
The project was hampered by the detriment caused by the unexpected storm. = 项目因突发暴风雨造成的损害而受阻。
The team's performance was negatively affected by the detriment of inadequate resources. = 球队的表现受到资源不足的负面影响。


疑问句:
What detriment did the fire cause to the building? = 火灾对建筑物造成了什么损害？
What is the potential detriment to public health? = 对公众健康的潜在损害是什么？


否定句:
The new policy didn't cause any detriment to the company's profitability. = 新政策并没有损害公司的盈利能力。
His actions did not bring any detriment to the team's overall performance. = 他的行为并没有损害团队的整体表现。


PHRASE->
to the detriment of:  对…有害
a detriment to: 对…有害
without detriment to: 不损害…
to one's detriment:  使某人受损
at someone's detriment: 以某人的损害为代价
