dollman:/ˈdɒlmən/| n. |矮小的人；玩偶人；（尤指）侏儒|A dollman is a small man, often used to describe a man of short stature, or a man who is perceived as small or insignificant.  It can also refer to a small figure, like a doll. The term often carries a slightly derogatory connotation.|例句：He was called a dollman by his bullies because of his small size.  他的小个子让欺凌他的人叫他玩偶人。|例句：The child played with his dollman collection all afternoon. 这个孩子一下午都在玩他的玩偶人收藏。|近义词：dwarf, midget, little man|反义词：giant, colossus
