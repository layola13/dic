eponym:/ˈɛpənɪm/| n. |以人名命名的词语；以人名命名的事物|A word or name derived from the name of a person (often a place or thing named after a person).  An eponym is a person after whom something is named.|The term "sandwich" is an eponym, named after the Earl of Sandwich.|近义词：namesake|反义词：无
