fade-out:/feɪd aʊt/| v.|逐渐消失；淡出；(声音、图像等)逐渐减弱|To gradually disappear or become less intense;  to decrease in volume or intensity gradually (of sound or image).|例句：The music faded out at the end of the movie. 电影结尾处音乐逐渐淡出。

The lights faded out slowly, leaving the audience in darkness. 灯光慢慢地暗淡下去，留下观众在黑暗中。|近义词：diminish, weaken, disappear, die away|反义词：fade in, intensify, brighten


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The music fades out slowly. = 音乐慢慢地淡出。
The image fades out gradually. = 图像逐渐淡出。

一般过去时:
The sound faded out abruptly. = 声音突然消失了。
The picture faded out before the credits rolled. = 片尾字幕出现前画面就淡出了。

现在完成时:
The pain has faded out gradually. = 疼痛逐渐消失了。
The color has faded out in the sun. = 颜色在阳光下褪去了。

过去完成时:
The memory had faded out over the years. = 这段记忆随着时间的流逝而逐渐淡忘了。
The noise had faded out before I could identify it. = 我还没辨认出声音，它就消失了。

将来时:
The echo will fade out eventually. = 回声最终会消失。
The feeling will fade out with time. = 这种感觉会随着时间而消退。

虚拟语气:
If the lights faded out completely, we would be in total darkness. = 如果灯光完全熄灭，我们将处于完全黑暗之中。
I wish the noise would fade out soon. = 我希望噪音很快消失。

被动语态:
The song was faded out at the end of the broadcast. = 这首歌在广播结束时被淡出。
The image is being faded out slowly. = 图像正在慢慢淡出。

疑问句:
Does the sound fade out gradually? = 声音是逐渐消失的吗？
Will the picture fade out at the end? = 画面会在结尾处淡出吗？


否定句:
The sound didn't fade out completely. = 声音没有完全消失。
The image didn't fade out quickly enough. = 图像没有足够快地淡出。


PHRASE->
fade out completely = 完全消失
fade out gradually = 逐渐消失
fade out slowly = 慢慢消失
fade out abruptly = 突然消失
fade out to black = 淡入黑色 (通常指画面)
