ok'd:/oʊkt/ | v. |批准，同意|Past participle of "okay," indicating approval or acceptance.  It's an informal contraction of "okayed."|The plan was ok'd by the committee.  委员会批准了该计划。|approved, sanctioned, authorized|rejected, disapproved, denied|动词


USAGE->

The boss oks my requests frequently. = 老板经常批准我的请求。
They usually ok new projects quickly. = 他们通常很快批准新的项目。

He ok'd the document yesterday. = 他昨天批准了文件。
