parches:/ˈpɑːrtʃɪz/|/ˈpɑːrtʃɪz/| vt. |使枯萎；使干透；使焦干|To make something dry out completely, often due to heat or lack of moisture.|The intense sun parched the land.  烈日把土地晒干了。|The drought parched the crops.  干旱使庄稼枯萎了。|近义词：dry out, desiccate, dehydrate |反义词：hydrate, moisten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun parches the fields every summer. = 每年夏天太阳都会晒干田地。
He parches the herbs before storing them. = 他在储存香草之前会先晒干它们。


一般过去时:
The intense heat parched the earth last week. = 上周酷暑炙烤大地。
The farmer parched the corn before milling it. = 农民在磨玉米之前先把它晒干了。


现在完成时:
The drought has parched the land for months. = 干旱已经持续数月，土地干涸了。
The prolonged heat has parched the plants in the garden. = 长时间的酷热已经晒干了花园里的植物。
