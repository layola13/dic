schematization: /ˌskiːmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 图示化；图解；模式化 | The process of representing something in a schematic or diagrammatic form; the result of this process.  It involves simplifying complex information into a more easily understandable visual representation. | Example: The schematization of the complex network helped to understand the flow of information.  图解复杂的网络有助于理解信息的流程。 The schematization of the process made it much easier to follow.  这个过程的图示化使之更容易理解。|近义词：diagrammatic representation, simplification, abstraction |反义词：detailed explanation, elaboration
