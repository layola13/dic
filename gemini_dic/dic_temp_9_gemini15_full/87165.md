herbous:/ˈhɜːrbəs/| adj.|草本的；似草的|Of or relating to herbs; having the characteristics of herbs.  The word implies a plant-like quality, often referring to texture, color, or general appearance rather than strict botanical classification.  |例句：The herbous landscape was dotted with wildflowers.  草本景观点缀着野花。

The herbous texture of the painting mimicked the feel of real grass. 这幅画的草本质感模仿了真草的感觉。


近义词：herbaceous, grassy, grassy-looking|反义词：woody, arboreal


词源:  Derived from the Old French word "herbos," meaning "grassy" or "covered with herbs," ultimately from the Latin word "herba," meaning "herb."
