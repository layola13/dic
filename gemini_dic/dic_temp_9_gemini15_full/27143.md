call a day:/kɔːl ə deɪ/| phrasal verb|收工；结束；停止|To decide to stop working on something for the day.|例句：We called a day at 5 pm because we were all exhausted.  我们下午5点收工了，因为大家都筋疲力尽了。|例句：After working for eight hours, they decided to call it a day. 工作了八个小时后，他们决定收工。|近义词：finish, quit, stop|反义词:continue, persevere


USAGE->
This phrasal verb does not conjugate in the same way as regular verbs.  It's treated as a single unit. The tense is reflected in the auxiliary verb "to call".

一般现在时:
We call a day when the work is finished. = 工作完成后我们就收工。
They call a day at 5 pm every day. = 他们每天下午5点收工。

一般过去时:
We called a day early yesterday. = 我们昨天提前收工了。
They called a day after completing the project. = 他们完成项目后就收工了。

现在完成时:
We have called a day on this project for today. = 我们今天已经完成了这个项目的收尾工作。
They have called a day on many occasions due to bad weather. = 由于天气不好，他们已经多次提前收工。

过去完成时:
We had called a day before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前我们就收工了。
They had called a day several times that week already. = 那周他们已经好几次提前收工了。

将来时:
We will call a day when we're done. = 我们完成的时候就收工。
They will call a day at 6 pm tomorrow. = 他们明天下午6点收工。

虚拟语气:
If we were finished, we would call a day. = 如果我们完成了，我们就收工。
I wish we could call a day early today. = 我希望我们今天能早点收工。

被动语态:
A day is not usually "called" passively.  This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice.


疑问句:
Will we call a day soon? = 我们很快就要收工了吗？
Should we call a day now? = 我们现在应该收工了吗？


否定句:
We didn't call a day until midnight. = 我们直到午夜才收工。
They won't call a day until the job is complete. = 工作完成之前他们不会收工。


PHRASE->
call a day on something:  结束某事
call it a day: 收工
call a day early: 提前收工
call a day off: 休假一天 (this is a slightly different meaning)
let's call it a day: 好了，收工吧
