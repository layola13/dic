marcescible:/mɑːˈsɛsəbl/ | adj. | 迟落叶的；不落叶的 |  Persisting on the branches after the leaves have died, remaining attached through the winter.  This term describes trees that retain their dead leaves throughout the winter. | Example:  The oak tree is marcescible, holding onto its brown leaves long after they have withered.  这棵橡树是迟落叶的，枯萎后很长时间还保留着它的棕色叶子。| 近义词：persistent, enduring | 反义词：deciduous
