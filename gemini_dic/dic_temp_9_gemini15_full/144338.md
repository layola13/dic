outstandingly:/ˌaʊtˈstændɪŋli/| adv.|杰出地，非常地，显著地| exceptionally or remarkably well.  It emphasizes the degree to which something is excellent or noticeable.|例句：He performed outstandingly well in the competition. 他在比赛中表现非常出色。|例句：The project was outstandingly successful. 这个项目取得了显著的成功。|近义词： remarkably, exceptionally, extraordinarily, significantly|反义词： poorly, inadequately, insignificantly
