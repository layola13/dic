overrode:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊd/|/ˈoʊvərroʊd/| vt.| 覆盖；推翻；压倒；凌驾于 | To override something means to overpower or supersede it, often implying a stronger force or authority taking precedence.  It can refer to physical actions, rules, or systems. |例句：The new regulations overrode the previous ones. 新规取代了旧规。

The chairman overrode the objections of the committee members. 主席否决了委员会成员的反对意见。


近义词：supersede, annul, negate, disregard, countermand |反义词：uphold, support, confirm, comply with |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overrides my decisions frequently. 他经常否决我的决定。
The system automatically overrides low-priority tasks. 系统会自动覆盖低优先级的任务。


一般过去时:
The judge overrode the jury's verdict.  法官推翻了陪审团的裁决。
She overrode her initial hesitation and spoke up. 她克服了最初的犹豫，大胆地说了出来。


现在完成时:
The new law has overrode many existing precedents. 新法律已经推翻了许多现有的先例。
They have overrode safety protocols in the name of efficiency. 他们为了效率而违反了安全规程。


过去完成时:
He had overrode his better judgment and trusted him. 他不顾自己较好的判断，相信了他。
The automatic system had overrode the manual controls. 自动系统已经覆盖了手动控制。


将来时:
The board will override the CEO's decision if necessary.  如果需要，董事会将推翻首席执行官的决定。
The software will automatically override any conflicting commands. 软件将自动覆盖任何冲突的命令。


虚拟语气:
If he had overrode the objections, the project would have succeeded. 如果他当时驳回了反对意见，项目就会成功。
I wish I hadn't overrode my instincts.  我希望我没有违背我的直觉。


被动语态:
His objections were overrode by the majority vote. 他的反对意见被多数票否决了。
The safety protocols were overrode, leading to a serious accident. 安全规程被违反，导致一起严重事故。


疑问句:
Did the president override the veto? 总统否决了否决权吗？（此处略显复杂，但可理解为总统是否推翻了先前的否决）
Will the court override the lower court's ruling? 法院会推翻下级法院的裁决吗？


否定句:
The manager did not override the employee's request.  经理没有否决员工的请求。
The system will not override the user's preferences. 系统不会覆盖用户的偏好。
