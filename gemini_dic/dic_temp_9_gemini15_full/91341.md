hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəpleɪn/| n. v.|水上飞机；水橇；(使)水橇；|A hydroplane is an aircraft that can take off and land on water.  It can also refer to a boat that skims across the water's surface at high speed. As a verb, it means to skim across water at high speed.|例句：The hydroplane soared gracefully over the lake. 水上飞机优雅地飞越湖面。The boat hydroplaned at high speed.  船以高速滑行在水上。|近义词：seaplane (n.), skim (v.)|反义词：submarine (n.)|名词，动词
