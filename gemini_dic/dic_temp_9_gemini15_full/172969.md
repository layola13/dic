quangos:/ˈkwæŋɡoʊz/| n. |准政府组织，半官方机构 |Quasi-autonomous non-governmental organizations;  bodies that are neither fully part of the government nor entirely independent, often funded by the government but operating with a degree of autonomy.  They frequently perform functions that would otherwise be undertaken by government departments. |The government has been criticized for its reliance on quangos to deliver public services.  政府因依赖准政府组织提供公共服务而受到批评。

The number of quangos has been reduced in recent years. 近年来，准政府组织的数量有所减少。|近义词：semi-governmental organization, public body, statutory body |反义词：government department, private company |名词
