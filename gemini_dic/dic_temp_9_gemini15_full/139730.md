ochrea:/ˈɒkriə/| n.|鞘状叶，叶鞘|A sheath, usually membranous or scarious, surrounding the stem at a node, especially characteristic of some plants in the Polygonaceae family (buckwheat family).  The ochrea is formed by the fusion of stipules. | Example: The  ochrea of the dock plant is quite prominent.  码头植物的鞘状叶非常明显。|近义词：sheath, stipular sheath|反义词：无
