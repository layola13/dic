elderwort:/ˈɛldərˌwɔːrt/| n. |接骨木属植物|Elderwort refers to any plant belonging to the genus Sambucus, commonly known as elderberries.  These plants are known for their clusters of small white or cream-colored flowers and dark purple or black berries.  They are used in various ways, including making jams, wines, and medicines.  However, some parts of the plant, particularly the leaves and stems, can be toxic if ingested.  |The elderwort flowers have a delicate fragrance.|  |近义词：elder, elderberry, Sambucus|反义词：无|名词
