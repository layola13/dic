octoate:/ˈɒktoʊeɪt/ | n. |辛酸酯，辛酸盐|An octoate is a salt or ester of octanoic acid (also known as caprylic acid).  Octoates are used in various applications, primarily as catalysts, stabilizers, and plasticizers.  They are often used in paints, inks, and plastics.|例句：Many metal octoates are used as catalysts in the polymerization of plastics. 许多金属辛酸盐被用作塑料聚合的催化剂。|例句：Zinc octoate is a common stabilizer in PVC formulations. 辛酸锌是PVC配方中常见的稳定剂。|近义词：caprylate|反义词：无
