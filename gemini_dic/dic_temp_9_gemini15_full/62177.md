encomber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/| vt.|妨碍；阻碍；拖累 |To impede or hinder the movement or progress of; to burden or weigh down.|例句：The heavy snow encombered our journey.  大雪阻碍了我们的旅程。  例句：Don't encomber yourself with unnecessary baggage. 不要带不必要的行李拖累自己。|近义词：hamper, hinder, obstruct, impede |反义词：aid, assist, facilitate
