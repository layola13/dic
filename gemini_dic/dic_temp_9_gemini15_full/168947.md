一般现在时:
His profile is very impressive. = 他的个人简介非常令人印象深刻。
The mountain's profile is clearly visible. = 山的轮廓清晰可见。

一般过去时:
She profiled her successful career in her autobiography. = 她在自传中介绍了她成功的职业生涯。
The artist carefully profiled the model's face. = 艺术家仔细地勾勒出模特的脸部轮廓。

现在完成时:
They have profiled several potential candidates for the job. = 他们已经对几个潜在的候选人进行了个人资料评估。
The media has extensively profiled the controversial politician. = 媒体对这位有争议的政治家进行了广泛的报道。

过去完成时:
The detectives had profiled the suspect before the arrest. = 侦探在逮捕之前已经对嫌疑人进行了画像。
The journalist had profiled the artist before his sudden death. = 记者在他突然去世之前已经对这位艺术家做过报道。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had profiled the market better, I would have made more money. = 如果我更好地分析了市场，我就会赚更多钱。
I wish I had profiled that artist years ago. = 我真希望几年前我就对那位艺术家做过报道。

被动语态:
The candidate was profiled in several major newspapers. = 这位候选人在几家主要报纸上都有报道。
The new product is being profiled in trade magazines. = 新产品正在行业杂志上进行介绍。

疑问句:
What is the profile of your target audience? = 你的目标受众的特征是什么？
Does this profile accurately reflect your experience? = 这个简介准确地反映了你的经验吗？

否定句:
The report did not profile the company's financial difficulties. = 这份报告没有介绍公司的财务困难。
The article failed to profile the key issues of the debate. = 这篇文章未能介绍辩论的关键问题。
