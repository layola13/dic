pinkiest:/ˈpɪŋkɪst/| adj.|最粉红色的；最娇嫩的|Most pink; most delicate.  It refers to the superlative degree of pink, indicating the highest degree of pinkness.|例句：Her cheeks were the pinkiest I've ever seen.  她的脸颊是我见过的最粉嫩的。
例句：The pinkiest flowers in the garden were the roses. 花园里最粉红的鲜花是玫瑰花。|近义词：bright pink, rosy, reddish-pink|反义词：palest,whitest
