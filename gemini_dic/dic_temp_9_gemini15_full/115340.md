manoeuvrability:/ˌmænʊvrəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可操作性；机动性；操纵性 | The ability to be easily maneuvered or controlled.  It refers to the ease with which something can be moved or directed. This could apply to vehicles, aircraft, ships, or even abstract concepts like a political situation. | The car's manoeuvrability was excellent, allowing for easy navigation through tight city streets. 这车的机动性非常好，方便在狭窄的城市街道上行驶。 The aircraft's manoeuvrability was crucial in avoiding the missile.  飞机的操纵性对于躲避导弹至关重要。| agility, maneuverability, flexibility | rigidity, inflexibility
