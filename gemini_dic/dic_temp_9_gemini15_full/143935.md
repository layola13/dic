outpushed:/ˌaʊtˈpʊʃt/| v.|超过，超越，挤出|To push further or with greater force than (someone or something else).  It implies surpassing someone or something in strength, influence, or achievement.|例句：The smaller company was outpushed by its larger competitor in the market.  这家较小的公司在市场上被更大的竞争对手超越了。


例句：The strong swimmer outpushed all the others to reach the finish line first.  这位强壮的游泳运动员超过所有其他人，第一个到达终点线。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, overpower


反义词：be outdone, be surpassed, be overtaken


词性：动词
