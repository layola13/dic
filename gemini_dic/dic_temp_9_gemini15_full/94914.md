in-hand:/ɪnˈhænd/| adj.|  手头的；现有的；在掌握中的|  Already possessed; available for immediate use.  |例句：We have plenty of in-hand resources to complete the project. 我们有充足的手头资源来完成这个项目。

例句：The company has several in-hand orders for new products. 公司有几笔现成的新品订单。

近义词：available, at hand, ready

反义词：unavailable, lacking, absent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is in-hand. = 项目正在进行中。
He has several in-hand projects. = 他有几个正在进行的项目。

一般过去时:
The situation was in-hand until the unexpected storm hit. = 在突如其来的暴风雨来临之前，情况都在掌握之中。
She had the situation well in-hand. = 她对局势掌控得很好。

现在完成时:
We have had this problem in hand for months. = 我们已经处理这个问题好几个月了。
They have kept the situation in hand despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，他们还是控制住了局面。

过去完成时:
The police had the situation in hand before the riot escalated. = 在暴乱升级之前，警方已经控制住了局面。
By the time the fire brigade arrived, the fire was already in hand. = 当消防队到达时，火势已经被控制住了。

将来时:
The situation will be in hand by the end of the week. = 到本周末，情况将会得到控制。
With the extra funding, we will have the project well in hand. =有了额外的资金，我们将能够很好地掌控这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If we had had more resources, the project would have been in hand sooner. = 如果我们有更多资源的话，项目就能更早地完成。
I wish the situation were in hand. = 我希望情况能够得到控制。

被动语态:
The situation was being kept in hand by the experienced team. = 经验丰富的团队正在控制局面。
The project is being brought in hand efficiently. = 项目正在高效地推进中。

疑问句:
Is the situation in hand? = 情况得到控制了吗？
Are the preparations in hand for the conference? = 会议的准备工作就绪了吗？

否定句:
The situation is not in hand. = 情况没有得到控制。
The project is not yet in hand. = 项目还没有开始。


PHRASE->
in hand with = 与…一起
take in hand = 处理；着手处理；接管
get in hand = 控制；掌握；处理
have in hand = 手头有；掌握；控制
out of hand = 无法控制；失控
