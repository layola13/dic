nonstickier: /ˌnɒnˈstɪkiər/ | adj. | 不太粘的，较不粘的 | Less sticky; comparatively less adhesive.  This word is the comparative form of "nonsticky".  It implies a degree of stickiness that is less than something else. | Example:  "The new paint is nonstickier than the old formula." = "新油漆比旧配方不太粘。" | 近义词: less sticky, less adhesive | 反义词: stickier, more adhesive |  形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "to be", "to have", etc.  The usage depends on the sentence structure.  Here are examples showing its usage in different sentence types:


肯定句:
The nonstickier surface made cleanup much easier. = 不太粘的表面使清理更容易。
This recipe results in a nonstickier dough. = 这个食谱做出来的面团不太粘。


否定句:
The surface wasn't nonstickier than I'd hoped. = 表面并没有像我希望的那样不太粘。
The mixture is not nonstickier even after adding more flour. = 即使加入更多的面粉，混合物也不太粘。


疑问句:
Is the new glue nonstickier than the old one? = 新胶水比旧胶水不太粘吗？
Is this nonstickier surface causing problems? = 这个不太粘的表面会导致问题吗？


比较句:
This is nonstickier than that. = 这个比那个不太粘。
The new material is far nonstickier than the previous version. = 新材料比以前的版本要不太粘得多。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonstickier".  The word itself is used to describe a comparative level of stickiness in various contexts.
