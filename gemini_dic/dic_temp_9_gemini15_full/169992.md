proteanism:/ˈproʊtiənɪzəm/ | n. |多变性；变化无常；变形能力 |The ability or tendency to change frequently or easily; versatility; mutability.  It often refers to the ability of a single organism to assume various forms or shapes. |例句：The proteanism of the artist's work is evident in his exploration of diverse styles and mediums.  这位艺术家的作品的多变性在他对各种风格和媒介的探索中显而易见。

例句：The chameleon's proteanism allows it to blend seamlessly into its environment. 变色龙的多变性使它能够完美地融入环境。


近义词：versatility, mutability, adaptability, polymorphism, changeability

反义词：constancy, immutability, stability, fixity


词性：名词
