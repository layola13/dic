collodiotype:/kəˈloʊdiəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 火棉胶照片 | A photographic process using a collodion-coated glass plate as the negative.  It produced high-quality images but was technically demanding.  | The collodiotype process was popular in the mid-19th century for its sharp detail and rich tones.  | ambrotype, ferrotype |  (no direct antonyms, as it refers to a specific process) | 名词
