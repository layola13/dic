decode:/diːˈkoʊd/ | /dɪˈkəʊd/ | vt. |解码；破译；理解；译解 | To convert (a coded message, piece of information, etc.) into understandable form.  |例句：The police tried to decode the kidnapper's message. 警察试图破译绑匪的信息。

例句：She struggled to decode the complex instructions. 她努力理解复杂的指令。


近义词： decipher, decrypt, interpret, translate | 反义词：encode, encrypt
