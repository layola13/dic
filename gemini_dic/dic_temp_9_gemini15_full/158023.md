plantations:/ˌplænˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |种植园；大农场 | A large farm, especially in a hot country, where crops such as cotton, coffee, or sugar are grown.  It often involves the use of  labor, historically including enslaved people. |例句：The old plantation house is now a museum. 这座旧种植园住宅现在是一座博物馆。

例句：Many plantations were established during the colonial era.  许多种植园是在殖民时期建立的。


近义词：estate, farm, ranch, estate (in the sense of a large landed property)

反义词：None directly opposite, but  "small farm" or "independent farm" could be considered contrasting concepts.
