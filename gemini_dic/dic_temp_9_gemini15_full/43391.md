cranker:/ˈkræŋkər/| n. adj. |脾气暴躁的人；曲柄；怪人；（发动机等的）启动装置| A cranker is a person who is irritable, grumpy, or bad-tempered.  It can also refer to a mechanical device, like a crank, used to start an engine or a type of person who holds unconventional or eccentric views.|例句：He's a real cranker; he complains about everything. 他是个脾气暴躁的人；他抱怨一切。  The old car needed a good cranker to start. 这辆旧车需要一个好的启动装置才能启动。|近义词：grump, curmudgeon, crank, eccentric |反义词：easygoing, amiable, pleasant
