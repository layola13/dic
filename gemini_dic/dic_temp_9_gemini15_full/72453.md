flumes:/fluːmz/ | n. |水槽；冲刷沟；矿井水槽 |A flume is a chute or trough, usually of wood or metal, used to convey water or other material, often in mining or irrigation.  It's also used to describe a natural channel carved by water. |例句：The miners used a flume to carry the ore down the mountain. 矿工们用水槽把矿石运下山。

The river carved a deep flume through the canyon.  河流在峡谷中冲刷出一个深沟。|近义词：channel, trough, conduit |反义词：dam, barrier
