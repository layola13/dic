fuzzle:/ˈfʌzəl/| v. n.|使混乱；弄糟；困惑；模糊不清；绒毛；蓬松的东西|To make something untidy or confusing; to become confused or unclear; a mass of soft, fluffy material.|例句：The cat fuzzled the yarn into a tangled mess.  猫把毛线弄得乱七八糟。

The instructions fuzzled me; I couldn't understand them.  说明把我搞糊涂了；我不明白。

近义词：confuse, muddle, tangle, bewilder|反义词：clarify, organize, straighten
