frescoist:/frɛˈskoʊɪst/| n. |壁画家|A person who paints frescoes.  Frescoes are paintings done on fresh, wet plaster.  The pigment is absorbed into the plaster, creating a durable and long-lasting artwork. |The frescoist meticulously applied the vibrant colors to the damp plaster.|近义词：muralist, painter|反义词：无
