hang-gliding:/ˈhæŋˌɡlaɪdɪŋ/| n. |滑翔伞运动|The activity of flying a hang glider.  It involves soaring through the air using a glider that is controlled by the pilot's body movements and weight shifts.|例句：He took up hang-gliding last year and loves the thrill of flight. 他去年开始玩滑翔伞运动，并且热爱飞行带来的刺激。

例句：Many people consider hang-gliding to be an extreme sport. 许多人认为滑翔伞运动是一项极限运动。


近义词：paragliding (动力滑翔伞运动，与hang gliding的区别在于动力滑翔伞有发动机)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与地面活动进行对比，如：walking, driving)


词性：名词
