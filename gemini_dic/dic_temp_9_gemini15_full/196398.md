/sɪrəʊˈθɛrəpi/ | n. | 血清疗法 | Serum therapy is a medical treatment that involves injecting serum containing antibodies into a patient to provide passive immunity against a specific disease.  It is used to treat or prevent infections caused by bacteria, viruses, or toxins. | Example:  Serotherapy was once widely used to treat diphtheria.  血清疗法曾经被广泛用于治疗白喉。  | 近义词: antiserum therapy, immunotherapy | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与疫苗接种等主动免疫方法对比) | 名词
