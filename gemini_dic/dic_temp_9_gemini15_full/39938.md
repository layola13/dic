constructed:/kənˈstrʌktɪd/| adj.| 建造的；构成的；人为的 | Built, formed, or made; not natural or spontaneous.  Something that is constructed is deliberately put together. |例句：The building is a beautifully constructed piece of architecture. 这座建筑物是一件建造精美的建筑杰作。

例句：His argument was cleverly constructed but ultimately flawed. 他的论点构思巧妙，但最终存在缺陷。


近义词：built, fabricated, manufactured, artificial, artificial, synthetic |反义词：natural, innate, inherent, spontaneous


词源:  From the past participle of the verb "construct," which comes from the Latin "construere," meaning "to build," "to construct," or "to pile up."  "Con-" is a prefix meaning "together," and "struere" means "to build" or "to arrange."
