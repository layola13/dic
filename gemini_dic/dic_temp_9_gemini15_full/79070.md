gizzard:/ˈɡɪzərd/| n. |砂囊；胃；磨胃|The gizzard is a muscular pouch in the digestive tract of birds, reptiles, and some other animals.  It contains small stones or grit that help grind up food.  |例句：The bird's gizzard was full of small stones.  这只鸟的砂囊里充满了小石头。

例句：The chicken's gizzard is a tough, muscular organ. 鸡的砂囊是一个坚韧的肌肉器官。


近义词：muscular pouch, stomach (in some contexts)

词源:
The word "gizzard" comes from the Old French word "giriere," which ultimately derives from the Vulgar Latin word "gigeria," meaning "entrails."  The word's evolution reflects its association with the internal organs of animals.
