keen:/kiːn/| adj.|热切的；敏锐的；强烈的；渴望的|Having or showing eagerness or enthusiasm.  Showing mental sharpness or quickness.|例句：She has a keen interest in art. 她对艺术有着浓厚的兴趣。
He has a keen eye for detail. 他观察细节很敏锐。|近义词：eager, enthusiastic, sharp, intense|反义词：indifferent, apathetic, dull, obtuse|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is keen to learn new things. = 他渴望学习新事物。
They are keen on playing football. = 他们热衷于踢足球。


一般过去时:
She was keen to help. = 她渴望帮忙。
He was keen on his studies. = 他学习很努力。


现在完成时:
I have been keen to visit Japan for years. = 我多年来一直渴望访问日本。
They have been keen supporters of the charity. = 他们一直是这个慈善机构的忠实支持者。


过去完成时:
We had been keen to finish the project before the deadline. = 我们渴望在截止日期前完成项目。
She had been keen on becoming a doctor since she was a child. = 她从小就渴望成为一名医生。


将来时:
He will be keen to get started on the new job. = 他将渴望开始新的工作。
They will be keen to hear the results. = 他们将渴望听到结果。


虚拟语气:
If I were keen enough, I would have tried harder. = 如果我足够渴望，我会更加努力。
I wish I were keen on learning a new language. = 我希望我渴望学习一门外语。


被动语态:
The opportunity was keenly sought after.  = 这个机会受到热烈追逐。
His suggestions were keenly debated. = 他的建议受到了热烈的讨论。


疑问句:
Are you keen on going to the party? = 你想去参加聚会吗？
Is he keen to help us? = 他渴望帮助我们吗？


否定句:
I'm not keen on spicy food. = 我不喜欢辛辣的食物。
He wasn't keen to go out in the rain. = 他不愿意冒雨出去。
