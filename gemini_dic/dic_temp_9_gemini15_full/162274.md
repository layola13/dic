preaccept:/ˌpriːəkˈsɛpt/| v.|预先接受，提前接受|To accept something before it is formally offered or before the usual time.  This word is rarely used and often considered informal or non-standard.|例句：The committee preaccepted his proposal, meaning they were likely to approve it formally later. 委员会预先接受了他的提案，这意味着他们很可能会在稍后正式批准它。


例句：They preaccepted the offer, subject to final contract negotiations.他们预先接受了报价，但须以最终合同谈判为准。


近义词：anticipate, foreshadow, preapprove


反义词：reject, refuse, decline


词性:动词
