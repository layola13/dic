fascinations:/ˌfæsɪˈneɪʃənz/| n.|迷恋；着迷；令人着迷的事物|The state or quality of being fascinated; something that fascinates.  It often refers to a strong interest or attraction.|例句：Her fascinations with ancient history led her to pursue archaeology.  她的古代史迷恋促使她从事考古学研究。

例句：The museum's exhibits held many fascinations for the children.博物馆的展品对孩子们很有吸引力。


近义词：enchantments, captivation, allure, charm|反义词：boredom, apathy, indifference|词性：名词
