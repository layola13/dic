plaques:/plæk/| n.|斑块；奖牌；铭牌|A plaque is a flat, usually decorative, plate or tablet, often made of metal, wood, or plastic, that is affixed to a wall or other surface.  It can be commemorative, informative, or purely decorative.|例句：The school unveiled a plaque to commemorate its 50th anniversary. 学校揭幕了一块牌匾以纪念其成立50周年。|例句：A plaque formed on his artery.  他的动脉上形成了斑块。|近义词：tablet, plate, medallion|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be a lack of a plaque.|名词
