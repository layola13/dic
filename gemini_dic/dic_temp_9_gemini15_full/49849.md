demoralizes:/dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪz/| vt.|使士气低落；使沮丧；使道德败坏|To lower someone's spirits; to discourage; to corrupt morally.|例句：The team's repeated losses demoralized the players.  球队接连失利使队员士气低落。

例句：The constant criticism demoralized her and made her question her abilities.  持续的批评使她沮丧，并让她怀疑自己的能力。


近义词：discourage, dispirit, depress, disheartens

反义词：encourage, inspire, uplift, hearten

词性:动词
