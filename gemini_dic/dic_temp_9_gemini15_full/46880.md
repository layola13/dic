darin:/ˈdɛərɪn/| adv.|在那里；在那方面；其中|In or into that place or thing.  It is an adverb derived from the Old English "þǣrin," meaning "in it" or "therein."|例句：The details are all therein. 细节都在里面。

The evidence was presented therein. 证据已在其中提出。|近义词：there, inside, within, amongst|反义词：thereout, outside
