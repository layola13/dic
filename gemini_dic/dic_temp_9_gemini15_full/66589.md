exhorts:/ɪɡˈzɔːrts/| vt.| 规劝；力劝；激励 | To urge or strongly advise (someone) to do something.  |例句：The doctor exhorted him to quit smoking. 医生力劝他戒烟。

例句：She exhorted her colleagues to work harder. 她规劝同事们更加努力工作。


近义词：urge, encourage, advise, implore, entreat

反义词：discourage, dissuade


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exhorts his students to study diligently. 他规劝他的学生勤奋学习。
She exhorts her children to be honest. 她告诫她的孩子们要诚实。


一般过去时:
The speaker exhorted the audience to take action. 演讲者规劝听众采取行动。
He exhorted his friend to seek help. 他规劝他的朋友寻求帮助。


现在完成时:
The teacher has exhorted the students to improve their grades. 老师已经规劝学生们提高他们的成绩。
She has exhorted him to reconsider his decision. 她已经规劝他重新考虑他的决定。


过去完成时:
He had exhorted them to leave before the storm hit. 他在暴风雨来临之前就劝他们离开。
She had exhorted him many times to be careful. 她曾多次劝告他要小心。


将来时:
The coach will exhort the team to play their best. 教练将会激励球队发挥最佳水平。
He will exhort his followers to remain faithful. 他将规劝他的追随者保持忠诚。


虚拟语气:
I wish he would exhort his son to study harder. 我希望他会规劝他的儿子更加努力学习。
If only she had exhorted him to be more cautious.  要是她当时规劝他更谨慎一些就好了。


被动语态:
The students were exhorted to participate in the competition. 学生们被规劝参加比赛。
He was exhorted to change his ways. 他被规劝改变他的行为方式。


疑问句:
Will he exhort them to change their minds? 他会规劝他们改变主意吗？
Did she exhort you to help her? 她规劝你帮助她了吗？


否定句:
He did not exhort them to violence. 他没有规劝他们使用暴力。
She didn't exhort him to give up. 她没有劝他放弃。



PHRASE->
exhort to action  呼吁采取行动
exhort to perseverance  规劝坚持不懈
exhort to obedience  规劝服从
exhort to repentance  规劝悔改
exhort to virtue  规劝行善


词源:
源自拉丁语 *exhortārī  (to encourage, urge)，由 *ex- (out, from) + *hortārī (to encourage)构成.
