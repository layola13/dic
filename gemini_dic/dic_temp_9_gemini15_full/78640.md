gibraltar: /dʒɪˈbrɔːltər/ | n. |直布罗陀| A British Overseas Territory located at the southern tip of the Iberian Peninsula. It is a peninsula strategically located at the entrance to the Mediterranean Sea, and comprises a small land area with a high population density.  The territory includes the town of Gibraltar and its surrounding areas.
