gondwana's:/ɡɒnˈdwɑːnəz/| n. |冈瓦纳古陆的；冈瓦纳大陆的| Gondwana's refers to something belonging to or relating to Gondwana, the supercontinent that existed from the Neoproterozoic (about 550 million years ago) until the Jurassic (about 180 million years ago).  It encompasses the geological features, flora, fauna, and history of this ancient landmass. | The unique flora of Gondwana's southern continents is evidence of their past connection. 冈瓦纳古陆南部大陆独特的植物群证明了它们过去的联系。|  Gondwanan, Gondwanaland's |  Laurasia's |  名词所有格
