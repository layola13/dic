excessed:/ɪkˈsɛst/| adj.|过量的，多余的|Having exceeded a limit or requirement; more than is needed or allowed.|The company excessed its production quota by 10%.  该公司超额完成了生产配额10%。|近义词：surplus, excessive, superfluous|反义词：deficient, insufficient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory excesses its production capacity. = 工厂生产能力过剩。
He excesses his authority. = 他滥用职权。


一般过去时:
The government excessed its spending last year. = 政府去年超支了。
The project excessed its budget. = 项目超支了。


现在完成时:
