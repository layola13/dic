pasteurizers:/ˈpæs.tʃə.raɪ.zɚz/| n.|巴氏杀菌器；巴氏消毒机|A pasteurizer is a machine used to pasteurize liquids, typically milk or juice, by heating them to a specific temperature for a set time to kill harmful bacteria.  |例句：The dairy uses a high-capacity pasteurizer to process thousands of gallons of milk daily.  这家乳制品厂使用一台高容量巴氏杀菌器每天处理数千加仑的牛奶。|例句：Modern pasteurizers are highly automated and efficient. 现代巴氏杀菌器高度自动化且效率高。|近义词： sterilizer (广义上的消毒器)|反义词：None|名词
