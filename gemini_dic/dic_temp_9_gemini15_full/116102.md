martyrologist:/ˌmɑːrtɪˈrɒlədʒɪst/| n. |殉道者传记作者；殉难者研究者|A person who studies or writes about martyrs.  A martyrologist compiles accounts of the lives and deaths of martyrs, often for religious reasons. |The martyrologist meticulously documented the suffering of early Christians.  殉道者传记作者一丝不苟地记录了早期基督徒的苦难。|hagiographer, biographer |(none readily available)|名词
