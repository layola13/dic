protectors:/prəˈtɛktərz/| n.|保护者，防护物|People or things that protect someone or something from harm or damage.  They can be physical objects, like a helmet, or people, like a bodyguard.|例句：The police are the protectors of the law. 警察是法律的保护者。

例句：He wore a face protector while working with chemicals. 他在处理化学品时戴着面罩。


近义词：guardians, defenders, shields, safeguards |反义词：destroyers, attackers, aggressors|名词
