drought:/draʊt/| n. |干旱，旱灾 |A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.  |The drought has devastated the crops. 这场干旱摧毁了庄稼。|  aridness, dryness |abundance, rainfall |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A severe drought is affecting the region. = 严重的干旱正在影响该地区。
The lack of rain causes frequent droughts. = 缺雨导致频繁的干旱。


一般过去时:
A terrible drought struck the country last year. = 去年一场可怕的旱灾袭击了这个国家。
The farmers suffered greatly during the drought. = 农民们在干旱期间遭受了巨大的损失。


现在完成时:
The region has experienced several droughts in recent years. = 近年来，该地区经历了几次干旱。
We have not seen such a severe drought in decades. = 我们几十年来都没有见过如此严重的干旱。


过去完成时:
By the time the rains came, the drought had lasted for two years. = 当雨水来临时，干旱已经持续了两年。
The crops had withered completely before the drought broke. = 在干旱结束之前，庄稼已经完全枯萎了。


将来时:
Scientists predict that more frequent droughts will occur in the future. = 科学家预测未来将发生更频繁的干旱。
The government will implement measures to mitigate the effects of the upcoming drought. = 政府将采取措施以减轻即将到来的干旱的影响。


虚拟语气:
If there had been more rain, the drought wouldn't have been so severe. = 如果雨水更多，干旱就不会那么严重。
I wish we had prepared better for the drought. = 我希望我们为干旱做更好的准备。


被动语态:
The area was severely affected by the drought. = 该地区受到干旱的严重影响。
Measures are being taken to alleviate the effects of the drought. = 正在采取措施减轻干旱的影响。


疑问句:
How long will this drought last? = 这场干旱将持续多久？
What are the causes of this drought? = 这场干旱的原因是什么？


否定句:
The drought did not affect the coastal regions as severely. = 干旱对沿海地区的影响没有那么严重。
There wasn't enough rain to prevent the drought. = 雨水不足以阻止干旱。
