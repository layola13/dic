reagent:/riˈeɪdʒənt/| n. |试剂，反应物 |A substance or compound that is added to a system to cause a chemical reaction, or is used in chemical analysis to detect, examine, or measure another substance.|The experiment requires a specific reagent to trigger the desired reaction.  这个实验需要一种特定的试剂来引发预期的反应。|catalyst, reactant|inhibitor, inactive substance|名词
