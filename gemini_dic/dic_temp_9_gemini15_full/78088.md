geoglyph:/ˈdʒiːoʊɡlɪf/ | n. | 地画，大地图形 | A geoglyph is a large design or symbol created on the ground, often using stones, earthworks, or other materials.  They are typically viewed from the air or from a distance. | Example: The Nazca Lines in Peru are famous examples of geoglyphs. 秘鲁的纳斯卡线条是地画的著名例子。 | 近义词：earthwork, ground drawing | 反义词：N/A
