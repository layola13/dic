prepositioned:/ˌpriːpəˈzɪʃənd/| adj.| 介词置位的；已加介词的 |  Placed before a noun or pronoun to show its relation to another word in the sentence. This term usually describes a word or phrase that already has a preposition attached to it.  | 例句：The prepositioned phrase "on the table" modifies the noun "book".  介词短语“在桌子上”修饰名词“书”。

The prepositioned adverb "in front" indicates the location of the car.  介词副词“前面”指出了汽车的位置。| 近义词：prepositional, modified  | 反义词：unmodified, unprepositioned |  adj.
