sententious:/senˈtɛnʃəs/| adj.| 说话冗长且教训意味的；简短精辟的；充满警句的 |  Given to moralizing; expressing much in few words; abounding in aphorisms.  | The professor's sententious remarks, though brief, were packed with wisdom. 教授简短的评论虽然简短，却充满了智慧。  His speech was filled with sententious pronouncements. 他的演讲充满了教训性的声明。 | pithy, concise, aphoristic | rambling, verbose, prolix |  adj.

一般过去时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he were less sententious, people would listen more. = 如果他不那么爱说教，人们就会多听一些。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
