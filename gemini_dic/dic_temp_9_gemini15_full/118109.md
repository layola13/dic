mennonite:/ˈmɛnənˌaɪt/| n. |门诺派教徒|A member of a Protestant Christian group that originated in the 16th century in Switzerland and the Netherlands. Mennonites are known for their pacifism, simple lifestyle, and commitment to community.  They often practice adult baptism and avoid military service. |The Mennonite community in Lancaster County is famous for its farming traditions.  门诺派教徒在兰开斯特郡的社区以其农业传统而闻名。| Amish, Anabaptist | Catholic, Baptist | 名词
