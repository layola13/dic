rushr:/rʌʃ/ | v. | 冲; 匆忙; 迅速移动 | To move or act with great speed or haste.  |例句：He rushed to catch the train. 他冲过去赶火车。|近义词：hurry, hasten, dash |反义词：dawdle, procrastinate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rushes to work every morning. = 他每天早上都匆忙去上班。
They rush through their meals. = 他们匆匆忙忙地吃饭。

一般过去时:
She rushed to the hospital after the accident. = 事故发生后，她急忙赶往医院。
We rushed to finish the project before the deadline. = 我们赶在截止日期前完成了项目。

现在完成时:
I have rushed through my work today. = 我今天匆忙完成了工作。
They have rushed into a decision without thinking it through. = 他们仓促做出了决定，没有仔细考虑。

过去完成时:
He had rushed to the airport, but the plane had already left. = 他匆忙赶到机场，但飞机已经起飞了。
She had rushed through her preparations, and consequently made several mistakes. = 她仓促准备，结果犯了好几个错误。

将来时:
I will rush to help him if he needs it. = 如果他需要帮助，我会赶紧去帮他。
They will rush to complete the task by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末之前赶紧完成任务。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I wouldn't rush. = 如果我有更多的时间，我就不会匆忙了。
I wish I hadn't rushed into that investment. = 我真希望我没有仓促进行那项投资。

被动语态:
The project was rushed through completion. = 项目仓促完成。
The decision was rushed and poorly considered. = 决定过于仓促，考虑不周。

疑问句:
Why are you rushing? = 你为什么这么匆忙？
Did you rush through your exam? = 你考试匆匆忙忙的吗？

否定句:
Don't rush; take your time. = 不要着急，慢慢来。
He didn't rush into the decision. = 他没有仓促做出决定。


PHRASE->
rush hour = 高峰时间

rush order = 急单
rush into = 仓促从事
rush out = 赶紧出版/发行
