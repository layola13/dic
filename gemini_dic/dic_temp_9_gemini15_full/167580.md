prewitness:/ˈpriːwɪtnəs/ | n. | 预先见证者，预先证人 | A person who witnesses an event before it officially begins or becomes public knowledge.  This term is not common and may be used in a legal or highly specific context. | Example:  The prewitness signed a sworn statement before the trial even commenced.  预先证人在审判开始之前就签署了一份宣誓声明。 | 近义词：early witness, advance observer | 反义词:  post-witness (a word not commonly used)
