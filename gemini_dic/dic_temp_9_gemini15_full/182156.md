remobilizes: /ˌriːˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ | vt. |重新动员；使恢复活力 | To mobilize again; to restore to activity or effectiveness. | 例句：The government remobilized the army to quell the rebellion. 政府重新动员军队以平息叛乱。

The company remobilized its sales team after a period of low performance. 公司在业绩低迷一段时间后重新动员了销售团队。


近义词：reactivate, revitalize, reinvigorate | 反义词：demobilize, deactivate, paralyze | 动词
