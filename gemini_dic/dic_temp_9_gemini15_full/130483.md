nonacademic:/ˌnɒnˌækəˈdɛmɪk/| adj. |非学术的；非学院派的|Not related to or connected with academics or academic institutions;  practical or professional rather than theoretical.  It implies a lack of formal academic training or focus. |例句：He pursued a nonacademic career in business after graduating. 他毕业后从事了一份非学术的商业职业。


例句：The nonacademic skills learned in sports are often valuable in the workplace. 在体育运动中学到的非学术技能在工作场所 often 很有价值。


近义词：practical, vocational, professional, non-scholarly


反义词：academic, scholarly, theoretical


词性：形容词
