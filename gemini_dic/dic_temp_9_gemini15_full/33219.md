chroniker:/ˈkrɒnɪkər/ | n. | 编年史作者；编年者 | A chroniker is a person who writes chronicles, which are historical accounts arranged in chronological order.  They meticulously record events as they unfold, often providing detailed descriptions and context. | Example: The court chroniker diligently documented every detail of the king's reign.  宫廷编年史作者一丝不苟地记录了国王统治的每一个细节。 |近义词：chronicler, annalist | 反义词：None specifically | 词性 n.
