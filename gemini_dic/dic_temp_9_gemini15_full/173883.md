quasi-reliably:/ˈkwɑːziˌraɪəbli/| adv.| 准可靠地，近乎可靠地 |  Describes something that is reliable to a certain extent, but not completely or consistently. It suggests a degree of uncertainty or inconsistency in its reliability.  | The system worked quasi-reliably, experiencing occasional outages.  这个系统运行得比较可靠，但偶尔会中断。 | reliably, dependably | unreliably, inconsistently | 副词
