isolux:/ˈaɪsoʊlʌks/| n. |等照度线|Isolux lines are lines on a map connecting points of equal illuminance.  They are used in lighting design to visualize the distribution of light. |例句：The isolux diagram showed that the lighting was insufficient in the corners of the room. 等照度线图显示房间角落的照明不足。|近义词：isophote |反义词：无
