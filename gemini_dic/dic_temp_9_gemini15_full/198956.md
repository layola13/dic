sighs:/saɪz/| int. n. v.|叹息，叹气|The act of sighing; a long, deep breath expressing sadness, relief, weariness, or the like.|例句：She sighed deeply, her shoulders slumping. 她深深地叹了口气，肩膀也垮了下来。
例句：A sigh escaped her lips. 一声叹息从她嘴唇溢出。|近义词：groan, gasp, murmur|反义词：laugh, cheer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He sighs frequently. = 他经常叹气。
She sighs with contentment. = 她满足地叹了口气。


一般过去时:
He sighed heavily after the news. = 听到这个消息后，他重重地叹了口气。
She sighed in relief when the plane landed. = 飞机着陆后，她如释重负地叹了口气。


现在完成时:
She has sighed all day. = 她整天都在叹气。
He has sighed with frustration many times. = 他多次沮丧地叹了口气。


过去完成时:
He had sighed before he spoke. = 他在说话之前叹了口气。
She had sighed with relief after finishing the exam. = 考试结束后，她如释重负地叹了口气。


将来时:
He will sigh when he hears the news. = 当他听到这个消息时，他会叹气。
She will sigh with exhaustion after the marathon. = 马拉松结束后，她会累得叹气。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth, I would have sighed with relief. = 如果我知道真相，我会如释重负地叹一口气。
I wish I could sigh and relax. = 我希望我能叹口气放松一下。


被动语态:
Sighs were heard throughout the room. = 整个房间都能听到叹息声。
A sigh was let out by the exhausted runner. = 精疲力尽的跑步者发出一声叹息。


疑问句:
Why are you sighing? = 你为什么叹气？

否定句:

She didn't sigh, but her face showed her disappointment. = 她没有叹气，但她的表情显示了她的失望。
