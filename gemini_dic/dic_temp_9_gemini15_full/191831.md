sclerema:/sklɪˈriːmə/| n. |硬肿症，皮肤硬化症 |A hardening and thickening of the subcutaneous tissues, particularly in infants.  This condition is characterized by firm, non-pitting edema and can be localized or generalized. It's often associated with other underlying medical conditions.|Example:  The infant was diagnosed with sclerema neonatorum, a serious condition requiring immediate medical attention.  婴儿被诊断患有新生儿硬肿症，这是一种需要立即就医的严重疾病。|近义词：scleroderma (but sclerema is specifically subcutaneous)|反义词：None specifically;  the antonym would describe the opposite condition, such as soft, pliable skin.|名词
