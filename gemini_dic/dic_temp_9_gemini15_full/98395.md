insoluble: /ɪnˈsɒljʊbl/ | adj. | 不溶解的；无法解决的 | incapable of being dissolved; incapable of being solved |

英文解析:  Insoluble refers to a substance that cannot be dissolved in a particular solvent.  It can also describe a problem or situation that is extremely difficult or impossible to solve.

例句:

Salt is soluble in water, but sand is insoluble.  = 盐溶于水，但沙子不溶于水。

The conflict between the two countries seems insoluble. = 两国之间的冲突似乎无法解决。


近义词: indissoluble, unsolvable, intractable

反义词: soluble, solvable


词性: adjective
