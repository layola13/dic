philologize:/fɪˈlɒlədʒaɪz/| vt.|  对…作语言学研究；从语言学的角度解释 |To study or explain something from a philological perspective; to treat (a subject) in a scholarly or learned manner, often with excessive detail or pedantry. |例句：He attempted to philologize the obscure passage, tracing the etymology of each word. 他试图对这段晦涩的文字进行语言学研究，追溯每个词的词源。

例句：The professor philologized the poem for an hour, analyzing its meter and vocabulary. 教授花了整整一个小时对这首诗进行语言学分析，分析了它的韵律和词汇。


近义词：analyze, interpret, explicate, elucidate

反义词：misinterpret, misunderstand


词性：动词
