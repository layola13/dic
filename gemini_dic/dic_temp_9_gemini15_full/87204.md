hereditably:/hɪˈrɛdɪtəbli/ | adv. | 遗传地；可遗传地 |  Hereditably means capable of being inherited genetically from one generation to the next. It describes traits or characteristics that are passed down through genes. |  The disease is hereditably transmitted from parent to offspring.  这种疾病是遗传自父母亲的。

The trait is hereditably determined.  这个性状是由遗传决定的。|  genetically, inheritable | non-heritable
