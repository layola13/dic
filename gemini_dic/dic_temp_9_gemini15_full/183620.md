reracked:/ˌriːˈrækt/| v.|重新整理；重新排列|To arrange (something, especially books or records) again on shelves or in racks.|The librarian reracked the returned books. 图书管理员重新整理了归还的书籍。|近义词：rearrange, reorganize, restock|反义词：disorganize, scatter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reracks the books after each library session. 他每次图书馆结束后都会重新整理书籍。
They rerack the inventory after the stocktake. 他们盘点后重新整理库存。


一般过去时:
She reracked all the CDs after cleaning them. 她清理完所有CD后重新整理了它们。
The staff reracked the merchandise after the sale.  销售结束后，员工们重新整理了商品。


现在完成时:
He has reracked the entire collection. 他已经重新整理了整个收藏。
