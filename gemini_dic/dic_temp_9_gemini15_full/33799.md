circumfer:/sɜːrˈkʌmfər/| n.|周长，圆周|The distance around a circle or any round object.  It is calculated using the formula 2πr, where r is the radius.|The circumference of the Earth is approximately 40,075 kilometers.|近义词：perimeter, girth|反义词：diameter
