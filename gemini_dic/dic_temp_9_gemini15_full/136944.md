nonspecialist:/ˈnɒnspɛʃəlɪst/ | adj. | 非专业人士的；外行的 | Not having special knowledge or skill in a particular area.  Someone who is not an expert in a particular field. | 例句：The explanation was simplified for the nonspecialist audience.  = 这份解释被简化以方便非专业人士理解。  The article provided a nonspecialist overview of quantum physics. = 这篇文章对量子物理学进行了外行式的概述。 | 近义词：lay, amateur, unprofessional | 反义词：specialist, expert, professional | 词性：形容词
