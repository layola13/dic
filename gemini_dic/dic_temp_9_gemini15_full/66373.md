exclusively: /ɪkˈskluːsɪvli/ | adv. | 仅仅；专门地；独家地 | Only; solely; to the exclusion of others.  |例句：This offer is exclusively for our subscribers.  这个优惠只针对我们的订阅用户。|例句：The information was revealed exclusively to the press.  该信息仅向媒体独家披露。|近义词：solely, only, entirely, uniquely |反义词：inclusively, generally, commonly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop exclusively sells organic products. = 这家商店只卖有机产品。
We exclusively use sustainable materials. = 我们只使用可持续材料。

一般过去时:
The event was exclusively for invited guests. = 此活动仅限受邀嘉宾参加。
They exclusively relied on traditional methods. = 他们完全依赖传统方法。

现在完成时:
We have exclusively focused on improving customer service. = 我们一直专注于改善客户服务。
The company has exclusively used renewable energy sources. = 该公司一直使用可再生能源。

过去完成时:
They had exclusively used that supplier before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们一直只使用那个供应商。
The artist had exclusively painted landscapes until recently. = 直到最近，这位艺术家一直只画风景画。

将来时:
The company will exclusively manufacture electric vehicles in the future. = 未来，该公司将只生产电动汽车。
We will exclusively promote our products online. = 我们将只在线推广我们的产品。

虚拟语气:
If the product were exclusively available online, sales would increase. = 如果该产品只在线销售，销量就会增加。
I wish this information were exclusively available to me. = 我希望这个信息只对我可用。

被动语态:
The event was exclusively catered for by a renowned chef. = 此活动由一位著名厨师专门承办。
The design was exclusively developed by our in-house team. = 该设计由我们内部团队专门开发。

疑问句:
Is this offer exclusively for new customers? = 这个优惠只针对新客户吗？
Are these products exclusively available in your store? = 这些产品只在你们商店有售吗？

否定句:
The restaurant does not exclusively serve seafood. = 这家餐馆不只供应海鲜。
The magazine does not exclusively feature celebrity interviews. = 这本杂志并非只刊登名人的采访。
