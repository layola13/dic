flywheel:/ˈflaɪˌwiːl/ | n. |飞轮，惯性轮 | A heavy wheel that stores energy when rotating and helps to regulate the speed of an engine or machine. It smooths out fluctuations in speed and power.  |The flywheel on the engine helps to maintain a consistent speed.  飞轮帮助发动机保持稳定的速度。|  rotor, momentum wheel |  (无明确反义词，可考虑  impetus reducer  作为语义上的反义) | 名词
