reeligibility:/ˌriːˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 再度符合资格；再次具备资格 | The state or quality of being eligible again; the right or capacity to be eligible again.  It often refers to the ability to run for office again after a term has ended, or to be considered for something after previously being ineligible. |  Example: The reeligibility of the senator after his scandal was questioned by many.  参议员在丑闻后的再次参选资格受到了许多人的质疑。 The company's reeligibility for the government contract hinged on its improved safety record. 公司再次获得政府合同的资格取决于其改进的安全记录。 | 近义词：re-qualification, renewed eligibility | 反义词：ineligibility, disqualification
