self-prepared:/ˌsɛlf prɪˈpɛərd/ | adj. | 自制的；自己准备的 |  Something that is self-prepared is made or done by oneself, without outside help or assistance. It emphasizes personal effort and lack of external contribution. | Example:  He brought a self-prepared lunch to the picnic. 他带了自制的午餐去野餐。

She gave a self-prepared presentation. 她做了一个自己准备的演示文稿。


近义词: homemade, home-prepared, self-made


反义词: commercially prepared, professionally prepared


词性:形容词
