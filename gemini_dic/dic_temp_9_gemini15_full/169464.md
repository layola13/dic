prop-up: /prɒp ʌp/ | /prɑːp ʌp/ | vt. |支撑；扶持；维持；支持 | to support or hold something up, preventing it from falling; to help someone or something continue to exist or function |

例句：
They propped up the roof with wooden beams. = 他们用木梁支撑屋顶。

He propped up the failing business with a large loan. = 他用一笔巨款支撑着快要倒闭的企业。


近义词：support, sustain, bolster, shore up

反义词：undermine, weaken, collapse, destroy

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They prop up the old table every day. = 他们每天都支撑着那张旧桌子。
We prop up the government's policies. = 我们支持政府的政策。

一般过去时:
He propped up the leaning fence. = 他支撑住了倾斜的栅栏。

现在完成时:
They have propped up the economy with stimulus packages. = 他们通过经济刺激计划支撑了经济。
We have propped up the sagging roof. = 我们已经支撑住了下垂的屋顶。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If we propped up the plant, it might survive. = 如果我们支撑住植物，它或许能活下来。
If they had propped up the business earlier, it might not have failed. = 如果他们早点支撑起这个企业，它可能就不会倒闭了。

被动语态:
