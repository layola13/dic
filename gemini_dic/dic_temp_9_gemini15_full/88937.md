holdings: /ˈhoʊldɪŋz/ | n. | 财产；资产；持有物 |  The things owned by someone or something; possessions.  |  例句：The company's holdings include several office buildings and a large plot of land.  这家公司的资产包括几栋办公楼和一大片土地。

例句：His investment holdings have increased significantly this year. 他今年的投资资产显著增长。


近义词：possessions, assets, property, investments | 反义词：liabilities, debts | 词性：名词


词源:  From "hold" (verb), meaning to possess or own,  plus the suffix "-ings," which forms nouns denoting things or actions.  The word evolved from Old English "haldan" (to hold).
