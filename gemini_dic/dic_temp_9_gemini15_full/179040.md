redemonstrate:/ˌriːˈdɛmənstreɪt/| vt.|再次演示；重新演示|To demonstrate something again; to show or explain something again.|例句：The teacher redemonstrated the experiment for the students who missed it. 老师为错过实验的学生再次演示了实验。

例句：The scientist redemonstrated his findings to the skeptical audience. 科学家向持怀疑态度的观众再次演示了他的发现。


近义词：re-enact, repeat, show again, illustrate again|反义词：conceal, hide, obscure|动词
