overages:/ˈoʊvərɪdʒɪz/| n. | 超支；超额；超过部分 |Amounts exceeding a specified limit or allowance.  It often refers to exceeding a pre-determined quantity, cost, or time. |例句：The project incurred significant overages due to unforeseen circumstances.  这个项目由于不可预见的情况而产生了大量的超支。

例句： We were charged extra for baggage overages. 我们因为行李超重而被额外收费。


近义词：excess, surplus, extra, overrun


反义词：underages, deficit, shortfall


词性:名词
