convict:/kənˈvɪkt/| v. n. | 证明…有罪；罪犯 | To declare someone guilty of a crime after a trial; a person found guilty of a crime. |例句：The jury convicted him of murder. 陪审团判定他犯有谋杀罪。
The convicted murderer was sentenced to life imprisonment. 那名被判有罪的杀人犯被判处无期徒刑。 |近义词： condemn, find guilty |反义词：acquit

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court convicts many criminals each year. = 法院每年判决许多罪犯。
He convicts the accused based on strong evidence. = 他根据确凿的证据判定被告有罪。


一般过去时:
The judge convicted the defendant of theft. = 法官判定被告犯有盗窃罪。
They convicted him despite his plea of innocence. = 尽管他认罪，他们还是判了他有罪。


现在完成时:
The court has convicted him of fraud. = 法院已判定他犯有欺诈罪。
She has been convicted twice for the same offense. = 她因同一罪行被判刑两次。


过去完成时:
By the time the trial ended, the jury had convicted him. = 审判结束时，陪审团已经判定他有罪。
The police had already convicted the suspect before the evidence was presented. = 在证据提交之前，警方已经认定嫌疑人有罪。


将来时:
The jury will likely convict him given the evidence. = 鉴于证据，陪审团很可能会判他罪。
They believe the court will convict the defendant. = 他们相信法院会判定被告有罪。


虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would convict him. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会判他罪。
I wish they would convict the real criminal. = 我希望他们能将真正的罪犯绳之以法。


被动语态:
He was convicted of tax evasion. = 他被判犯有逃税罪。
The suspect was convicted after a lengthy trial. = 经过漫长的审判后，嫌疑人被判有罪。


疑问句:
Will they convict the defendant? = 他们会判决被告吗？
Has he ever been convicted before? = 他以前被判罪过吗？


否定句:
The court did not convict him due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，法院没有判他罪。
They failed to convict the suspect. = 他们未能将嫌疑人绳之以法。



PHRASE->
convicted felon:  被判有罪的重罪犯
convicted criminal: 被判有罪的罪犯
convicted murderer: 被判有罪的杀人犯
convicted thief: 被判有罪的小偷
convicted of a crime: 因犯罪而被定罪


词源:
From Old French *convicte, past participle of convaincre "to convince," from Latin convincere "to overcome, convict," from com- "together" + vincere "to conquer."  The sense evolution is from "to overcome" to "to prove guilty" by convincing the court.
