outroom:/ˈaʊtruːm/ | n. | 外屋，外室 | An outroom is a room that is separate from the main house or building, often used for storage or other auxiliary purposes.  It is typically less refined or finished than the main rooms. | Example: The old tools were stored in the outroom.  旧工具都存放在外屋。 | 近义词：shed, annex, outhouse | 反义词：inner room, main room
