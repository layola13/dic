overspecify: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛsəfaɪ/ | vt. | 过度详细说明；过度规定 | To specify something in excessive detail or to a greater extent than is necessary or useful.  This often leads to unnecessary constraints or complexity. | 例句：The design specifications overspecified the material requirements, leading to increased costs. 设计规范对材料要求进行了过度详细说明，导致成本增加。

The project manager overspecified the timeline, making it unnecessarily difficult to meet deadlines. 项目经理对时间安排进行了过度规定，使得按时完成任务变得不必要地困难。| 近义词：overdefine, overdetermine, overdetail | 反义词：under specify, generalize | 词性:动词
