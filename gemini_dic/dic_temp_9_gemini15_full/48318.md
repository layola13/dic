decretion:/dɪˈkriːʃən/| n.|分泌物；决定；裁决|The process of secreting or separating a substance from the blood or other bodily fluid.  It can also refer to a decision or judgment, especially by an authority.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's nasal decretions. 医生检查了病人的鼻分泌物。|例句：The judge's decretion was final and binding. 法官的裁决是最终的且具有约束力。|近义词：secretion, excretion, judgment, ruling|反义词：absorption, retention|名词
