opposedness: /əˈpoʊzɪdnəs/ | n. | 对立性；相反；对抗 | The state of being opposed; antagonism; contrariety.  It refers to the quality of being in conflict or opposition.  This can manifest in ideas, opinions, actions, or things. |


例句：

The opposedness of their views made a compromise impossible.  他们的观点对立，使得妥协成为不可能。

The sheer opposedness of their personalities led to constant arguments.  他们性格上的强烈对立导致了不断的争吵。


近义词：opposition, antagonism, conflict, contrariety, contradiction


反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, conformity, accord


词性：名词


词源：源自动词oppose，加后缀-edness构成名词，表示“被反对的状态”或“反对的性质”。  Oppose本身来自拉丁语 *opponere，由ob-（表示“对抗”）和ponere（表示“放置”）构成，字面意思是“放置对抗”。


USAGE->  (This word is primarily a noun and does not conjugate like a verb.)  Therefore, tense and voice examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->

in opposedness to: 与...相反
sense of opposedness: 对立感
degree of opposedness: 对立程度
manifest opposedness: 表现出对立
the opposedness between:  两者之间的对立
