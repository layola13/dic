prisma:/ˈprɪzmə/| n.|棱镜；三棱镜；(观点等的)视角；|A prism is a transparent object, usually with triangular bases, that separates white light into its constituent colours.  It can also refer to a particular way of looking at or interpreting something. |例句：A prism separates white light into a spectrum of colours. 棱镜将白光分解成光谱。

例句：He viewed the situation through the prism of his own experiences. 他从自身经历的角度看待这种情况。

近义词：lens, perspective, viewpoint

反义词：N/A

词性:名词
