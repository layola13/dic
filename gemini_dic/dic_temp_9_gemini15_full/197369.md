sheared:/ʃɪərd/ | v. | 剪羊毛；剪；切断 | To cut the wool from (a sheep).  Also, to cut or break something with a sharp tool.  | Example: The farmer sheared his sheep in the spring.  农夫在春天剪了他的羊毛。

The shepherd sheared the flock. 牧羊人剪了羊群的毛。

| 近义词: clipped, shorn, trimmed | 反义词: grew, cultivated | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shears sheep every spring. 他每年春天都剪羊毛。
She shears her own hair. 她自己剪头发。

They sheared the wool from the sheep. 他们剪下羊身上的羊毛。
