conject:/kənˈdʒekt/| v.|推测，猜想|To form an opinion or idea without proof or sufficient evidence; to guess.|例句：I conjectured that the meeting would be cancelled. 我推测会议会被取消。

例句：Scientists conjectured about the origin of the universe. 科学家们推测宇宙的起源。


近义词：speculate, surmise, guess, hypothesize|反义词：know, confirm, verify|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conjectures that the economy will improve. = 他推测经济将会好转。
She conjectures the answer based on limited information. = 她根据有限的信息推测答案。


一般过去时:
They conjectured the cause of the accident. = 他们推测事故的原因。
The detective conjectured that the thief had escaped. = 侦探推测小偷已经逃跑了。


现在完成时:
Scientists have conjectured about the existence of dark matter. = 科学家们已经推测暗物质的存在。
We have conjectured that the project will be delayed. = 我们已经推测项目将会延期。


过去完成时:
They had conjectured that the experiment would fail. = 他们曾推测实验会失败。
I had conjectured that he would not show up. = 我曾推测他不会出现。


将来时:
They will conjecture about the future of artificial intelligence. = 他们将会推测人工智能的未来。
She will conjecture the outcome of the election. = 她将会推测选举的结果。


虚拟语气:
If I were to conjecture, I'd say the answer is B. = 如果我要推测的话，我会说答案是B。
I wish I could conjecture the winning lottery numbers. = 我希望我能推测出彩票中奖号码。


被动语态:
It was conjectured that the painting was a fake. = 人们推测这幅画是赝品。
The cause of the fire was conjectured to be arson. = 推测火灾原因是纵火。


疑问句:
What do you conjecture will happen next? = 你推测接下来会发生什么？
What did they conjecture about the missing documents? = 他们对失踪的文件做了什么推测？


否定句:
I don't conjecture that he is guilty. = 我不推测他是有罪的。
They didn't conjecture the true meaning of the message. = 他们没有推测出信息的真正含义。


PHRASE->
conjecture about = 推测关于…
conjecture that = 推测…
conjecture from = 从…推测
bold conjecture = 大胆的推测
educated conjecture = 有根据的推测
