词源:
The word "chimp" is a shortened form of "chimpanzee".  The word "chimpanzee" itself originates from the Kikongo word "kilu," meaning "man," and "n'Simba," meaning "leopard."  The combination refers to their human-like qualities and the leopard-like markings on their faces.
