harpooning:/hɑːˈpuːnɪŋ/ | n.  |鱼叉捕猎；用鱼叉刺；|The act of using a harpoon to catch a large fish or other sea animal.  |例句：The whalers were skilled in harpooning. 捕鲸者擅长使用鱼叉捕鲸。

例句：The documentary showed the dramatic harpooning of a whale.  纪录片展示了捕鲸的戏剧性场景。

近义词：spearing, lancing |反义词：releasing, freeing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harpoons fish every day. 他每天都用鱼叉捕鱼。
They harpoon whales in the icy waters. 他们在冰冷的海水中捕鲸。

一般过去时:
The fisherman harpooned a large tuna. 渔夫叉到了一条大金枪鱼。
They harpooned several whales during the expedition. 他们在探险期间叉到了几头鲸鱼。

现在完成时:
The crew has harpooned many whales this season. 船员们在这个季节已经捕获了许多鲸鱼。
He has never harpooned a marlin before. 他以前从未用鱼叉捕过枪鱼。

过去完成时:
The whalers had harpooned three whales before the storm hit.暴风雨来临之前，捕鲸者已经捕获了三头鲸鱼。
They had harpooned several fish before the boat capsized. 船倾覆之前，他们已经叉了几条鱼。

将来时:
They will harpoon more fish next week.他们下周将捕捞更多鱼。
The captain said they will harpoon the whale at dawn. 船长说他们将在黎明时分捕鲸。

虚拟语气:
If he had a harpoon, he would harpoon that shark. 如果他有一把鱼叉，他会叉那条鲨鱼。
I wish I had harpooned that big fish. 我真希望我叉到了那条大鱼。

被动语态:
The whale was harpooned and killed. 鲸鱼被叉死。
Many fish are harpooned in this area every year. 每年都有很多鱼在这个地区被叉到。

疑问句:
Did they harpoon the whale successfully? 他们成功地捕到鲸鱼了吗？

否定句:
