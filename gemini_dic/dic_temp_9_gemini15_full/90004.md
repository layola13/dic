horticulturist:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərɪst/| n. |园艺家，园艺师 |A horticulturist is a person who cultivates and studies plants, especially in gardens and orchards. They are experts in plant growth, propagation, and cultivation, often specializing in specific areas like fruit trees, vegetables, or ornamental plants.  |例句：The horticulturist carefully pruned the roses.  这位园艺师仔细修剪了玫瑰花。

例句：Our local horticulturist gave a talk on growing tomatoes.  我们当地的园艺师做了一个关于种植西红柿的报告。


近义词：gardener, plantsman, cultivator |反义词：None  |词性:名词
