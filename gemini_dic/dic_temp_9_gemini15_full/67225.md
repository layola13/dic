exploratory:/ɪkˈsplɔːrətɔːri/| adj.|探索性的，探测性的|relating to or involving exploration or investigation.|例句：The team conducted exploratory research into the causes of the disease.  团队对该疾病的病因进行了探索性研究。例句：The exploratory surgery revealed the extent of the damage. 探索性手术揭示了损伤的程度。|近义词：investigative, preliminary, initial|反义词：definitive, conclusive
