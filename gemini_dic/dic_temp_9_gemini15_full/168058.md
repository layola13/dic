privatively:/ˈpraɪvətɪvli/ | adv. | 仅就其负面方面而言；从反面；剥夺性地 |  Adverb indicating something is considered in terms of its absence or negation, rather than its presence or positive qualities. It suggests a definition or understanding based on what something *lacks* rather than what it *has*.  |  Blindness is defined privatively as the absence of sight.  失明被定义为缺乏视力的状态。|  negatively, inversely | positively, affirmatively | 副词
