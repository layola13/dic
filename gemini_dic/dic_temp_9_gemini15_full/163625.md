predating: /ˌpriːˈdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 早于；先于；比…早 | Existing or happening before something else.  |例句：The discovery predates the earliest known settlements in the region.  这项发现早于该地区最早的已知定居点。

例句：His interest in art predates his career in finance. 他对艺术的兴趣早于他在金融领域的职业生涯。


近义词：antedating, preceding, foregoing


反义词：postdating, succeeding, following


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence predates the current theories. = 证据早于目前的理论。
This tradition predates the founding of the nation. = 这个传统早于建国。


一般过去时:
The painting predated the artist's famous style. = 这幅画早于艺术家著名的风格。
The ruins predated the Roman Empire. = 这些废墟早于罗马帝国。


现在完成时:
Archaeologists have discovered artifacts predating the civilization. = 考古学家发现了早于该文明的文物。
Researchers have found evidence predating previous assumptions. = 研究人员发现了早于先前假设的证据。


过去完成时:
The letter had predated the actual event by several weeks. = 这封信比实际事件早了几周。
Scientists had found fossils predating the dinosaurs. = 科学家们发现了早于恐龙的化石。


将来时:
Future discoveries may predate current knowledge. = 未来的发现可能会早于目前的知识。
The new technology will predate existing systems. = 新技术将早于现有系统。


虚拟语气:
If the artifact predated the era, it would rewrite history. = 如果文物早于那个时代，它将改写历史。
I wish the documents predated the scandal. = 我希望这些文件早于丑闻。


被动语态:
The documents were predated by a more recent discovery. = 这些文件被一个较新的发现早于。
The theory is predated by several previous hypotheses. = 这个理论早于几个先前的假设。


疑问句:
Does the map predate the exploration? = 这张地图早于探险吗？
Does this technology predate the invention of the wheel? = 这项技术早于轮子的发明吗？


否定句:
The evidence does not predate the established timeline. = 证据并不早于既定的时间表。
The building does not predate the 19th century. = 这座建筑并不早于19世纪。


PHRASE->
predate something by years = 早于某事物数年

predate the discovery of = 早于…的发现
predate the establishment of = 早于…的建立
