dor:/dɔːr/| n.|门；门扉|A door is a hinged, sliding, or revolving barrier at an entrance to a building, room, or vehicle.|例句：He opened the door and stepped inside. 他打开门走了进去。|近义词：gate, entrance|反义词:window


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The door is open. = 门是开着的。
The doors creak when the wind blows. = 风吹的时候门会吱吱作响。


一般过去时:
The door slammed shut. = 门砰的一声关上了。
She closed the door quietly. = 她轻轻地关上门。


现在完成时:
I have painted the door red. = 我把门漆成了红色。
The door has been broken for weeks. = 门坏了几个星期了。


过去完成时:
They had locked the door before leaving. = 他们离开前锁上了门。
The door had been damaged in the storm. = 门在暴风雨中受损了。
