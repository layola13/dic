jagless:/ˈdʒæɡləs/| adj. |无锯齿的，平滑的|Free from irregularities or projections; smooth.  The term often refers to a surface or edge that lacks jaggedness or roughness.|例句：The polished stone was perfectly jagless.  这块抛光的石头完全没有锯齿。

The new blade is jagless and cuts cleanly. 这把新刀片没有锯齿，切得很干净。


近义词：smooth, even, seamless, uninterrupted


反义词：jagged, rough, uneven, serrated
