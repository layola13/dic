enraptured: /ɪnˈræpʃərd/ | adj. | 着迷的，被迷住的 | Filled with great delight or wonder; captivated.  |例句：She was enraptured by the beauty of the sunset. 她被日落的美丽迷住了。

例句：The audience was enraptured by the singer's performance. 观众被歌手的表演迷住了。


近义词：captivated, enchanted, charmed, fascinated, enthralled, mesmerized | 反义词：bored, uninterested, indifferent


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is enraptured by her singing. 他被她的歌声迷住了。
The child is enraptured by the story.  孩子被故事迷住了。

一般过去时:
She was enraptured by the scenery. 她被景色迷住了。
I was enraptured by his storytelling. 我被他讲的故事迷住了。

现在完成时:
We have been enraptured by the play. 我们一直被这出戏迷住了。
They have been enraptured by the beauty of nature. 他们一直被大自然的美丽迷住了。

过去完成时:
She had been enraptured by his charm before she met him. 在她遇见他之前，她就已被他的魅力迷住了。
The audience had been enraptured by the performance before the curtain fell. 在幕布落下之前，观众已经被演出迷住了。
