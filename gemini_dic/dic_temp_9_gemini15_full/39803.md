consortium:/kənˈsɔːrsiəm/| n. |财团；协会；联合体 |A consortium is an association of several companies, organizations, or individuals formed to undertake a joint project or venture.  It often involves sharing resources, expertise, and risks. |Example: Several universities formed a consortium to build a new research facility.  几个大学组成了一个财团来建造一个新的研究设施。|近义词：group, alliance, partnership |反义词：individual, sole proprietorship
