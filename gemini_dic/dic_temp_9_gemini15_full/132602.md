nondimensioned:/ˌnɒnˈdaɪmɛnʃənd/ | adj. | 无量纲的 |  Having no dimensions; expressed without units of measurement.  It refers to a quantity that is independent of any particular system of units.  For example, a ratio of two quantities with the same units is nondimensioned because the units cancel out.  |  The Reynolds number is a nondimensioned quantity used in fluid mechanics.  The aspect ratio of a wing is a nondimensioned parameter. | 近义词：dimensionless, unitless | 反义词：dimensioned
