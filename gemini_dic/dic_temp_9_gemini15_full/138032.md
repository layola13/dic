nopal:/ˈnoʊpɑːl/ | n. |仙人掌（尤指食用仙人掌）, 胭脂仙人掌|Prickly pear cactus, especially its edible pads.  The term often refers to the flat, paddle-like stems of the prickly pear cactus, which are used as a vegetable in Mexican and other cuisines. |例句：Nopal is a popular ingredient in Mexican cuisine.  仙人掌是墨西哥菜肴中的一种常用食材。|例句：We harvested the nopal pads for our salad. 我们收获了仙人掌叶片来做沙拉。|近义词：prickly pear, cactus pad, tuna (in some regions)|反义词：无
