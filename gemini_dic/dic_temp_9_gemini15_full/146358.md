overfixed:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪkst/| adj.|过度固定的；过于僵化的 |  Describes something that has been fixed or adjusted too much, resulting in rigidity or inflexibility. It implies an excessive or inappropriate degree of fixing, often leading to a negative outcome.  | Example: The overfixed schedule left no room for unexpected events.  过度固定的日程安排没有为意外事件留下任何空间。|近义词：rigid, inflexible, too tight, over-regulated|反义词：flexible, adaptable, loose, fluid|
