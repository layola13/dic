prelusory:/ˈpriːljuːsəri/| adj.|预备的；开端的；前奏的|Preliminary or introductory; serving as a prelude.  It suggests something that comes before the main event or action, often hinting at what is to come. |例句：His prelusory remarks hinted at the seriousness of the situation. 他开场白暗示了局势的严重性。

例句：The prelusory chapter sets the stage for the novel's main plot.  这开篇的章节为小说主线剧情奠定了基础。


近义词： preliminary, introductory, prefatory, introductory, anticipatory |反义词：concluding, final, ultimate |词性：形容词
