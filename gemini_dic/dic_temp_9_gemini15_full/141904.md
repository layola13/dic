ordering:/ˈɔːrdərɪŋ/| n.|排序，订购，命令|The act of arranging things in a particular order; the process of placing an order for goods or services; a command or instruction.|例句：The ordering of the books was alphabetical. 书籍的排列顺序是按字母顺序排列的。
例句：The ordering process was quick and easy. 订购过程快捷方便。

近义词：arrangement, sequence, command, instruction|反义词：disorder, chaos


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ordering a pizza. = 他正在点披萨。
They order supplies regularly. = 他们定期订购用品。

一般过去时:
She ordered a new dress. = 她订购了一件新裙子。
We ordered our food and waited. = 我们点了餐然后等待。

现在完成时:
I have ordered the tickets online. = 我已经在线订购了票。
They have ordered new equipment for the lab. = 他们已经为实验室订购了新设备。

过去完成时:
He had ordered the cake before the party started. = 派对开始前他已经订好了蛋糕。
She had ordered the flowers a week in advance. = 她在一周前就订好了鲜花。

将来时:
We will order a taxi later. = 我们稍后会叫一辆出租车。
They will order more books next month. = 他们下个月会订购更多书籍。

虚拟语气:
If I had ordered the tickets earlier, I would have gotten better seats. = 如果我早点订票，就能拿到更好的座位。
I wish I had ordered more food; I'm still hungry. = 我真希望我多点了些食物；我还饿着呢。


被动语态:
The books were ordered in advance. = 书籍是提前订购的。
The supplies are being ordered now. = 补给品正在订购中。


疑问句:
Have you ordered yet? = 你已经点餐了吗？
What did you order? = 你点了什么？


否定句:
I didn't order anything. = 我什么也没点。
They haven't ordered the cake yet. = 他们还没订蛋糕。


PHRASE->
customer ordering = 客户下单
online ordering = 在线订购
bulk ordering = 批量订购
order fulfillment = 订单履行
order management = 订单管理
