preinterview:/ˌpriːˈɪntərvjuː/| n. |面试前的准备；面试前的会面 | A preinterview is a meeting or conversation that takes place before a formal interview, often used to screen candidates or gather preliminary information.  | Example: The preinterview helped me relax before the main interview.  面试前的准备让我在正式面试前放松了很多。|近义词： preliminary meeting, screening interview |反义词：postinterview
