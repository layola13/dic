duvets:/ˈduːveɪz/| n.|羽绒被，羽绒制品|A duvet is a type of bedding consisting of a soft, flat bag filled with down, feathers, or synthetic filling, used as a covering on a bed.|例句：I bought a new duvet for the winter. 我买了一床新的羽绒被过冬。|例句：The duvet cover is made of 100% cotton. 羽绒被套是由100%棉制成的。|近义词：comforter, quilt|反义词：sheet(床单)
