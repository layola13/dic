rejeopardized:/ˌriːˈdʒɛpərdaɪzd/| v.|再次危及；重新使陷入危险 |To put something in danger again after it had been made safe or secure.  This word implies a previous period of safety or security that has been compromised a second time. |例句：The peace treaty was rejeopardized by the recent border clashes. 最近的边境冲突使和平条约再次陷入危险。

The fragile ceasefire was rejeopardized by the unexpected escalation of violence.  意想不到的暴力升级使脆弱的停火再次面临危险。|近义词：re-endanger, imperil again, put at risk again |反义词：secure, safeguard, protect
