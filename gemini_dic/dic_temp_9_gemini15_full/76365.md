gadgets:/ˈɡædʒɪts/| n. |小工具，装置 |A gadget is a small tool or device that has a particular use, often a clever or ingenious one.  It is usually a relatively simple device. |例句：He showed me his new gadgets for the kitchen. 他给我展示了他新的厨房小工具。

例句：My phone is my most important gadget. 我的手机是我最重要的工具。


近义词：device, tool, contrivance, gizmo |反义词：无
