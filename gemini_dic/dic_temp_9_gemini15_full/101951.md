/ˈɪzəm/| n. |主义，教条，习性，特色 |A distinctive practice, belief, or characteristic of a particular group, person, or period.  It often refers to a system of beliefs or principles. |Examples:  Capitalism is an economic system.  Many different -isms exist in the world. |近义词: doctrine, creed, tenet, principle |反义词: (No direct antonym, but depends on the specific -ism)
