evaporate:/ɪˈvæpəreɪt/| v.|蒸发；消失；逐渐减少 |To change from a liquid or solid state into a gas.  It can also refer to something gradually disappearing or fading away.|例句：The water evaporated in the sun.  阳光下水蒸发了。|例句：His anger evaporated quickly. 他的怒气很快就消散了。|近义词：vaporize, disappear, vanish |反义词：condense, materialize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water evaporates quickly in the heat. = 水在高温下很快蒸发。
He evaporates his frustrations through exercise. = 他通过运动来消除自己的挫败感。


一般过去时:
The puddle evaporated after the rain stopped. = 雨停后水坑蒸发了。
Her hopes evaporated when she didn't get the job. = 当她没有得到那份工作时，她的希望破灭了。


现在完成时:
Much of the lake has evaporated this summer. = 今年夏天湖泊的大部分水都蒸发了。
His confidence has evaporated since his defeat. = 自从失败后，他的信心消失了。


过去完成时:
The evidence had evaporated by the time the police arrived. = 等警察到达时，证据已经消失了。
All his savings had evaporated due to his gambling habit. = 由于他的赌博习惯，他所有的积蓄都消失了。


将来时:
The spilled milk will evaporate soon. = 洒出的牛奶很快就会蒸发。
His anger will evaporate once he calms down. = 他冷静下来后，怒气就会消散。


虚拟语气:
If the temperature were higher, the liquid would evaporate faster. = 如果温度更高，液体就会蒸发得更快。
If the snow had evaporated faster, we could have reached the summit. = 如果雪融化得更快，我们就能到达山顶了。


被动语态:
The water is evaporated by the heat. = 水被高温蒸发了。
His dreams were evaporated by the harsh realities of life. = 他的梦想被残酷的现实击碎了。


疑问句:
Does water evaporate faster in hot or cold weather? = 水在炎热或寒冷的天气中蒸发得更快吗？
Will the stain evaporate if I leave it in the sun? = 如果我把污渍放在阳光下，它会蒸发吗？


否定句:
The liquid didn't evaporate because it was too cold. = 液体没有蒸发，因为它太冷了。
His enthusiasm didn't evaporate despite the setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，他的热情并没有消退。
