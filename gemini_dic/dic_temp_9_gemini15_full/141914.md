ordinal:/ˈɔːrdɪnəl/| adj.|序数的，顺序的|An ordinal number is a number that indicates position or rank in a series, such as first, second, third, etc.  It describes the order of something in a sequence.|例句：First, second, and third are ordinal numbers.  第一、第二和第三是序数。|例句：She finished the race in fifth ordinal position. 她以第五名的成绩完成了比赛。|近义词：sequential, ranked|反义词：cardinal (基数的)
