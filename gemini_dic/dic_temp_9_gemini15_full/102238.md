itemizers: /ˈaɪtəmˌaɪzərz/ | n. | 项目列举者；分项列举者 | A person or thing that itemizes;  a system or process for creating an itemized list.  It often refers to a software or tool used to create detailed lists of items, often for accounting or inventory purposes. | Example: The accountant used specialized itemizers to prepare the detailed financial report.  会计师使用专业的项目列举器来准备详细的财务报告。|近义词：lister, cataloger, enumerator |反义词：summarizer (in the sense of combining rather than separating items)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software itemizes all expenses for the project. = 这个软件列出了项目的所有支出。
She itemizes her grocery purchases each week. = 她每周都列出她购买的杂货清单。


一般过去时:
He itemized the receipts before submitting his expense report. = 他在提交费用报告之前列出了收据。
The program itemized all the files in the directory. = 该程序列出了目录中的所有文件。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
