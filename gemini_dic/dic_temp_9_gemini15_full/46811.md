The word evokes a feeling of stuffiness and unpleasantness associated with dampness.|例句：The danky basement smelled of mildew.  潮湿的地下室散发着霉味。 The danky air hung heavy in the old castle.  阴冷的空气在古堡中沉重地压抑着。|近义词：damp, humid, muggy, clammy|反义词：dry, arid, sunny
