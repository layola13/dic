fashionless:/ˈfæʃənles/| adj.| 不时尚的，过时的 | Lacking fashion or style; unfashionable.  |The fashionless clothing made her look drab.|近义词：unfashionable, outdated, old-fashioned |反义词：fashionable, stylish, trendy

USAGE->

He is fashionless in his choice of clothes. = 他在服装选择上不时尚。
Her style is fashionless and quite dull. = 她的风格过时且乏味。
