flyleaf:/ˈflaɪliːf/| n.|衬页|A blank or printed leaf at the beginning or end of a book, often used for a title page, dedication, or other preliminary matter.  It is distinct from the main text pages.|例句：The flyleaf of the book contained a handwritten inscription. 这本书的衬页上有一段手写的题词。

例句：I found a pressed flower tucked inside the flyleaf. 我发现衬页里夹着一朵压花。


近义词：endpaper (有时可互换，但endpaper通常指书封和正文之间的纸页)


反义词：无


词性:名词
