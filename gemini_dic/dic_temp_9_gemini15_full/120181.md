minding:/ˈmaɪndɪŋ/| v.|介意，注意，照料|英文解析：The present participle of "mind". It can mean paying attention to something, taking care of something, or being bothered by something. |例句：I don't mind helping you. = 我不介意帮你。


He was minding his own business. = 他在管自己的事。


近义词：caring for, attending to, heeding, noticing, considering|反义词：ignoring, neglecting, disregarding


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am minding the children. = 我正在照看孩子们。
She is minding her own business. = 她正在管自己的事。

一般过去时:
I minded my own business. = 我管好自己的事。
He minded the shop while his boss was away. = 他老板不在的时候，他在照看商店。

现在完成时:
I have been minding my own business all day. = 我一整天都在管自己的事。
She has minded the store for ten years. = 她经营这家店十年了。

过去完成时:
I had minded the children for hours before my sister arrived. = 我姐姐来之前已经照看了孩子们几个小时了。
He had minded his own business before the incident happened. = 事件发生之前，他一直管好自己的事。

将来时:
I will be minding the store tomorrow. = 我明天将要照看商店。
She will mind the baby while we are out. = 我们出去的时候，她会照看婴儿。

虚拟语气:
If I minded my own business, I wouldn't be in this trouble. = 如果我管好自己的事，我就不会有这样的麻烦。
I wish I had minded my parents' advice. = 我真希望我听了父母的建议。

被动语态:
The children were minded by their grandmother. = 孩子们由他们的祖母照看。
The shop is minded by a very reliable person. = 这家店由一个非常可靠的人照看。

疑问句:
Are you minding the children? = 你在照看孩子吗？
Does he mind if I use his phone? = 我用他的电话他介意吗？

否定句:
I don't mind the noise. = 我不介意噪音。
She doesn't mind working late. = 她不介意加班。
