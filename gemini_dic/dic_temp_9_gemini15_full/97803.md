injuriously:/ɪnˈdʒʊəriəsli/ | adv. |有害地；伤心地；恶意地 |In a way that causes harm or damage; offensively.|The chemical is injuriously affecting the environment.  这种化学物质正在有害地影响环境。|He spoke injuriously about his former colleague. 他恶意地谈论他的前任同事。|harmfully, detrimentally, offensively|beneficially, helpfully, kindly
