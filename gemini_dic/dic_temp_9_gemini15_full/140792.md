on-a-cloud:/ˌɒnəˈklaʊd/| adj. |云上的；像在云端一样；幸福的，快乐的 |Describes something that is in, on, or resembling a cloud; often used metaphorically to describe a feeling of happiness, bliss, or detachment from reality. |例句：She felt on-a-cloud after winning the lottery.  她中了彩票后感觉像在云端一样。|近义词：blissful, elated, euphoric, dreamy |反义词：depressed, miserable, unhappy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels on-a-cloud today. = 他今天感觉像在云端一样。
The birds are on a cloud. = 鸟儿们在云上。


一般过去时:
She was on a cloud after her graduation. = 她毕业后感觉像在云端一样。
The airplane flew on a cloud. = 飞机飞在云层上。


现在完成时:
I have felt on-a-cloud ever since I met him. = 自从我遇到他以来，我一直感觉像在云端一样。
The clouds have moved on a cloud. = 云朵飘动在云层中。


过去完成时:
He had been on a cloud all day before the bad news arrived. = 在坏消息到来之前，他一整天都感觉像在云端一样。
The plane had already been on a cloud before the turbulence. = 在颠簸之前，飞机已经在云层中了。


将来时:
She will be on a cloud when she hears the good news. = 当她听到好消息时，她会感觉像在云端一样。
The weather forecast says the clouds will be on a higher cloud tomorrow. = 天气预报说明天的云层会更高。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would be on a cloud. = 如果我中了彩票，我会感觉像在云端一样。
I wish I were on a cloud right now. = 我希望我现在能像在云端一样。
