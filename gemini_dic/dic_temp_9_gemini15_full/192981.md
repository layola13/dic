seclusion:/sɪˈkluːʒən/| n. |隐居；与世隔绝；僻静处 |The state of being secluded or private;  a secluded place.  It implies a deliberate withdrawal from society or a busy environment, often for the sake of peace, quiet, or reflection. |例句：He sought seclusion in a remote mountain cabin. 他在偏僻的山间小屋寻求隐居。|例句：The garden offered a welcome seclusion from the noise of the city. 花园提供了一个远离城市喧嚣的静谧场所。|近义词：isolation, solitude, retirement, privacy |反义词：exposure, publicity, openness
