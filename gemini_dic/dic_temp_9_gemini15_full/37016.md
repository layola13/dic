columnarity:/ˌkɒljʊˈnærɪti/ | n. | 柱状性；列状性；垂直排列 | The property of being arranged in columns or having a columnar structure.  It often refers to data in databases or spreadsheets, where data is organized into vertical columns.  | Example: The database's high columnarity makes querying specific attributes very efficient. 数据库的高列状性使得查询特定属性非常高效。 |近义词：columnar arrangement, vertical alignment |反义词：random arrangement, scattered arrangement
