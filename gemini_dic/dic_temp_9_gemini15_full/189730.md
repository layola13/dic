salters:/ˈsɔːltərz/ | n. | 盐商；盐制造商；撒盐者 | A salter is someone who sells or produces salt, or someone who salts something (like food).  The word can also refer to a specific type of container used for salting. |例句：The salters controlled the price of salt for centuries.  盐商控制着盐的价格几个世纪。

例句：She worked as a salter in the local factory.她在当地工厂做盐工。


近义词：salt merchant, salt producer, salter (as a verb, meaning to add salt)

反义词：None directly opposite, but words suggesting a lack of salt might be considered contrastive.

词性：Noun
