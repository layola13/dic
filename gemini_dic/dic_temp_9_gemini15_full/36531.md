collaborateur:/kəˌlæbəˈrɑːtər/| n. |合作者，协作者 |A collaborator is a person who works jointly on an activity or project.  It often implies a shared effort towards a common goal. |例句：He was a key collaborator on the project, contributing significantly to its success. 他是该项目的主要合作者，为项目的成功做出了重大贡献。|例句：The artists collaborated on a new mural for the community center.  艺术家们合作创作了一幅社区中心的新壁画。|近义词：coworker, colleague, partner, associate |反义词：opponent, adversary, rival


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collaborates with several artists on his latest project. = 他与几位艺术家合作创作他的最新项目。
She collaborates closely with her team members. = 她与团队成员密切合作。


一般过去时:
They collaborated on a research paper last year. = 他们去年合作撰写了一篇研究论文。
He collaborated extensively with other scientists during his career. = 他在职业生涯中与其他科学家广泛合作。


现在完成时:
We have collaborated on many projects over the years. = 多年来，我们合作过许多项目。
She has collaborated with leading experts in the field. = 她与该领域的领先专家合作过。


过去完成时:
They had collaborated on several projects before starting this one. = 在开始这个项目之前，他们已经合作过几个项目了。
The company had collaborated with that firm for many years. = 那家公司已经与那家公司合作多年了。


将来时:
We will collaborate on a new marketing campaign next month. = 我们下个月将合作开展一个新的营销活动。
They will collaborate with international partners on this initiative. = 他们将与国际合作伙伴合作开展这项倡议。


虚拟语气:
If they collaborated more effectively, they would achieve better results. = 如果他们更有效地合作，他们将取得更好的成果。
I wish we had collaborated more closely on the last project. = 我希望我们在上一个项目中能更密切地合作。


被动语态:
The research paper was collaborated on by several experts. = 这篇研究论文是由几位专家合作完成的。
The project was successfully collaborated on by a diverse team. = 该项目由一个多元化的团队成功合作完成。


疑问句:
Will you collaborate with me on this task? = 你愿意在这个任务上与我合作吗？
Who did you collaborate with on that project? = 你在那个项目中与谁合作？


否定句:
He did not collaborate with his rivals. = 他没有与他的竞争对手合作。
They refused to collaborate with the opposing team. = 他们拒绝与对方团队合作。
