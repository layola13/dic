dialysis:/daɪˈælɪsɪs/| n.|透析；渗析|The process of separating substances in solution by means of their different rates of diffusion through a semipermeable membrane.  Dialysis is used to remove waste products from the blood of patients with kidney failure.|例句：He is undergoing dialysis three times a week. 他每周进行三次透析治疗。

例句：The scientist used dialysis to separate the proteins. 科学家用渗析法分离蛋白质。


近义词：filtration, separation|反义词：synthesis, combination|词性:名词
