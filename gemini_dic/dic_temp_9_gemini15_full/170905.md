pseudomedically:/ˌsuːdəʊˈmedɪkli/ | adv. |伪医学地，似是而非地医学地 |  Adverb describing something done in a way that imitates or falsely claims to be medical. It suggests a lack of genuine medical basis or scientific validity.  | Example: The treatment was pseudomedically administered, lacking any scientific evidence of effectiveness.  伪医学地施行了这种治疗，缺乏任何有效的科学证据。 |近义词：quackishly, charlatanically | 反义词：medically, scientifically
