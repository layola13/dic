repudiates:/rɪˈpjuːdieɪts/| vt.| 否认；拒绝；抛弃；与…断绝关系 | to refuse to accept, acknowledge, or be associated with something | The company repudiates any responsibility for the accident.  这家公司否认对事故负有任何责任。|He repudiated his earlier statement. 他否认了他先前的声明。|  accept, acknowledge, embrace | accept, acknowledge, endorse
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repudiates the accusations against him. 他否认针对他的指控。
She repudiates her past. 她否认她的过去。

一般过去时:
The government repudiated the treaty. 政府否决了条约。
He repudiated his debts. 他拒绝偿还债务。

现在完成时:
They have repudiated the agreement. 他们已经否决了协议。
She has repudiated her family. 她已经与她的家人断绝关系。

过去完成时:
He had repudiated his promise before the deadline. 他在最后期限前就已经违背了他的诺言。
They had repudiated their allegiance to the king. 他们早已背叛了对国王的效忠。

将来时:
She will repudiate the deal if the terms are not met. 如果条件不满足，她将否决这笔交易。
He will repudiate his inheritance. 他将放弃他的遗产。

虚拟语气:
If he repudiated the contract, he would face legal consequences. 如果他违反合同，他将面临法律后果。
I wish he had repudiated the offer earlier. 我希望他早点拒绝这个提议。

被动语态:
The contract was repudiated by the company. 这份合同被公司否决了。
His claims were repudiated by the court. 他的说法被法院驳回了。

疑问句:
Does he repudiate the charges? 他是否否认这些指控？
Will she repudiate her marriage? 她会否认她的婚姻吗？

否定句:
He does not repudiate his responsibilities. 他没有否认他的责任。
She did not repudiate her friend. 她没有抛弃她的朋友。
