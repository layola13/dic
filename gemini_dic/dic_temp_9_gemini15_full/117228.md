meatoscope: /ˈmiːtoʊskoʊp/ | n. | 肉类观察镜；肉类检验器 | A device used to examine meat for quality and defects.  It typically uses magnification and/or lighting to reveal internal structures and potential problems such as bruising, discoloration, or parasite infestation. | Example: The butcher used a meatoscope to inspect the beef for marbling and tenderness.  肉贩用肉类观察镜检查牛肉的大理石纹和嫩度。 | 近义词: meat inspector, quality control device | 反义词: N/A
