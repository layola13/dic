implausible:/ɪmˈplɔːzəbl/ | adj. |难以置信的，不可能的 |  unlikely to be true or to happen; not believable.  |The story is utterly implausible.  故事完全不可信。 | plausible, credible, believable | believable, credible, plausible, likely |
