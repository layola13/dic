coinventing: /ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 共同发明；合作发明 |  The act of two or more people inventing something together. It emphasizes the collaborative nature of the invention process. |例句：They are coinventing a new type of engine. = 他们正在共同发明一种新型发动机。

例句：The two scientists were coinventing a groundbreaking medical technology. = 这两位科学家正在合作发明一项具有突破性的医疗技术。


近义词：collaborating on an invention, jointly inventing, developing together


反义词：inventing independently, sole inventing


词性：动词
