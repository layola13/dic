perceptional: /pərˈsɛpʃənəl/ | adj. | 感知的，知觉的 | Relating to perception; involving or based on perception.  This adjective describes something connected to the process of perceiving or understanding something through the senses or mind. |  例句：His perceptional abilities were extraordinary, allowing him to notice subtle details others missed.  他的感知能力非凡，能注意到别人错过的细微之处。

例句：The painting evoked a strong perceptional response in the viewer. 这幅画在观看者心中激起了强烈的感知反应。


近义词：sensory, perceptive, cognitive

反义词：unperceptive, insensitive

词性：形容词
