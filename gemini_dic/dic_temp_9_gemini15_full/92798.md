icky:/ˈɪki/| adj.|令人作呕的，黏糊糊的，不舒服的|Something that is icky is unpleasant, sticky, or disgusting.  It often refers to something that is slightly unpleasant rather than truly horrifying. |例句：The slime was icky and made me shudder.  那黏液令人作呕，让我浑身发抖。

例句：That old food smells icky. 那剩饭闻起来令人作呕。


近义词：disgusting, unpleasant, yucky, gross, slimy, sticky|反义词：pleasant, delightful, appealing


词源:  The etymology of "icky" is uncertain, but it's likely an onomatopoeic word, meaning it imitates a sound or feeling.  It may be related to other words expressing disgust or unpleasantness, but a definitive origin is not established.  It gained popularity in the mid-20th century as informal slang.
