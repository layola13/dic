incorporate:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ | vt. |包含；吸收；合并；组成公司|To include something as part of a whole; to form a corporation.|The new design incorporates many user suggestions.  新的设计融入了许多用户的建议。|They decided to incorporate their two businesses. 他们决定合并他们的两家公司。|integrate, combine, consolidate|separate, divide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They incorporate new features into their software every year.  他们每年都在软件中加入新功能。
The company incorporates environmentally friendly practices into its operations. 公司在其运营中纳入环保措施。


一般过去时:
She incorporated several different styles into her painting. 她将几种不同的风格融入到她的绘画中。
The artist incorporated elements of surrealism into his work.  这位艺术家在他的作品中融入了超现实主义的元素。


现在完成时:
They have incorporated several new employees into the team.  他们已经将几名新员工纳入团队。
The company has incorporated sustainability into its core values. 公司已将可持续性纳入其核心价值观。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had incorporated a new accounting system.  在合并之前，他们已经采用了新的会计系统。
After he had incorporated all the feedback, he submitted his report. 在他吸收了所有反馈之后，他提交了他的报告。


将来时:
We will incorporate your feedback into the next version of the product. 我们将在产品的下一个版本中整合您的反馈。
The company will incorporate AI into its customer service. 公司将人工智能融入其客户服务中。


虚拟语气:
If we incorporated your suggestions, the project would be much better. 如果我们采纳你的建议，项目将会更好。
I wish they had incorporated more user-friendly features. 我希望他们能加入更多用户友好的功能。


被动语态:
New features are incorporated into the software regularly. 新功能定期添加到软件中。
Your suggestions will be incorporated into the final design. 您的建议将被纳入最终设计中。


疑问句:
Does the design incorporate any new technology?  该设计是否包含任何新技术？
Will they incorporate feedback from their customers?  他们会考虑客户的反馈吗？


否定句:
The final version did not incorporate all the proposed changes. 最终版本没有包含所有建议的更改。
The plan does not incorporate sufficient safety measures. 该计划没有纳入足够的安全措施。
