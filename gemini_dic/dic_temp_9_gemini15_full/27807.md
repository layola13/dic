canonical:/kəˈnɒnɪkəl/| adj.|规范的；标准的；权威的；经典的 | conforming to a general rule, standard, or principle;  authoritative or recognized as being the best or most typical example.  |例句：The canonical version of the text is the one published by the Oxford University Press.  （该文本的规范版本是牛津大学出版社出版的那个。）

例句：This novel is considered a canonical work of American literature. （这部小说被认为是美国文学的经典作品。）


近义词：standard, orthodox, authoritative, typical, classic

反义词：unconventional, unorthodox, atypical, nonstandard


词源：源自希腊语kanōn，意思是“规则”、“标准”或“直尺”。  This term was adopted into Latin and eventually into English.
