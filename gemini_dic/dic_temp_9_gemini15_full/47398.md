deadheaded:/ˈdedˌhedɪd/| adj. v.|枯萎的；(使)凋谢；终止；(使)停止；|  Describes something that is withered, faded, or no longer productive, often referring to flowers or plants.  It can also mean to remove the spent flower heads from a plant to encourage further growth or to stop the plant from producing seeds.  In a more figurative sense, it means to terminate or stop something, such as a project or a process. |例句：The roses were deadheaded to promote more blooms.  玫瑰花被摘除了枯萎的花朵以促进更多花朵的生长。

The project was deadheaded after funding was cut.  在资金被削减后，该项目被终止了。|近义词：withered, faded, terminated, stopped, ended |反义词：blooming, flourishing, thriving, active, ongoing
