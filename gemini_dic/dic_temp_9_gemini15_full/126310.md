myologic:/ˌmaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk/| adj.|肌的；肌肉的；肌理的|Relating to muscles or musculature.  The term often appears in medical and anatomical contexts.|例句：Myologic studies are crucial for understanding muscular diseases. 肌理学研究对于理解肌肉疾病至关重要。

例句：The myologic examination revealed significant atrophy in the patient's leg muscles. 肌理检查显示患者腿部肌肉萎缩严重。


近义词：muscular, myalgic (referring to muscle pain)


反义词：None directly opposite, but words like "non-muscular" or "skeletal" could be considered contrasting terms depending on context.

词性：形容词
