functionalism:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |功能主义；机能主义|A theory or approach that emphasizes the functions of things.  Functionalism in sociology, for example, focuses on the social structures and institutions and how they contribute to the stability and functioning of society. In architecture and design, functionalism prioritizes practicality and efficiency. |The functionalist approach in sociology explains social phenomena in terms of their contribution to the overall stability of society. |近义词：pragmatism, utilitarianism|反义词：formalism, idealism|名词
