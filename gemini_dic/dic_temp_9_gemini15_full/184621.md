restraints:/rɪˈstreɪnts/ | n. | 约束；限制；抑制；束缚 |  Something that restricts or limits a person's actions or freedom.  It can refer to physical restraints (like handcuffs) or emotional/behavioral restraints (like self-control). |

例句：

The police used restraints to subdue the suspect.  | 警方使用约束措施制服了嫌疑犯。

She showed remarkable restraint in the face of provocation. | 面对挑衅，她表现出非凡的克制力。


近义词：limitations, restrictions, constraints, controls, bonds


反义词：freedom, liberty, license


词性：名词
