satirize:/ˈsætəraɪz/| vt.|讽刺；挖苦|To use satire to expose or criticize someone or something.  The writer satirized the politician's hypocrisy in his latest column. |近义词：mock, lampoon, ridicule, parody |反义词：praise, flatter, commend
