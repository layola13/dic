It often implies a feeling of unreality or escapism.|例句：The old house felt like a dreamery, filled with whispers of forgotten stories. 这栋老房子感觉像个梦想之地，充满了被遗忘的故事的低语。|例句：Lost in her dreamery, she didn't hear the doorbell ring.  沉溺于幻想中，她没有听到门铃响。|近义词：fantasy, reverie, daydream|反义词：reality, harshness, practicality|名词
