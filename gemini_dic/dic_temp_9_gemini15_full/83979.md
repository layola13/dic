half-critically: /ˈhæf ˈkrɪtɪkəli/ | adv. | 半批判地，部分批判地 |  Describes an action or judgment that is partly critical but not entirely negative or dismissive. It suggests a balanced approach, acknowledging both positive and negative aspects. |例句：He reviewed the film half-critically, praising its visuals but criticizing its plot. 他半批判性地评论了这部电影，称赞了它的视觉效果，但批评了它的情节。 |近义词：ambivalently, cautiously, tentatively |反义词：uncritically, wholly critically
