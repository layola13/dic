rabbinism:/ˈræbɪnɪzəm/| n. |犹太教拉比主义；拉比教义|The body of laws, traditions, and interpretations developed and transmitted by rabbis.  It encompasses Jewish religious law (Halakha), ethics, customs, and practices, as well as philosophical and theological thought.  Rabbinism is distinct from earlier forms of Judaism and represents a significant development in Jewish religious life and thought.|例句：The study of rabbinism requires a deep understanding of Hebrew and Jewish history.  学习拉比主义需要对希伯来语和犹太历史有深入的了解。

The influence of rabbinism on Jewish life is undeniable. 拉比主义对犹太生活的影响是不可否认的。|近义词：rabbinical Judaism,  Jewish tradition|反义词：N/A
