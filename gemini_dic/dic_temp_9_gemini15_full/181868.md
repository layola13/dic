relubricated: /ˌriˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪd/ | v. | 再润滑；重新润滑 |  Past participle of relubricate. It means to lubricate something again, typically after it has become dry or worn.  | The machine was relubricated after a period of inactivity. 这台机器在一段时间的闲置后进行了重新润滑。 | lubricated, greased, oiled |  dry, unlubricated |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They relubricate the engine regularly. = 他们定期对发动机进行润滑。
We relubricate the machine every month. = 我们每月对机器进行一次润滑。


一般过去时:
The mechanic relubricated the car's engine. = 机械师对汽车的发动机进行了润滑。
She relubricated the bicycle chain before her ride. = 她在骑车前给自行车链条上了油。
