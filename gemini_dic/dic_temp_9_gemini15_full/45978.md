cyanite:/ˈsaɪənaɪt/| n. |蓝晶石|A metamorphic mineral consisting of aluminum silicate, typically blue but also found in green, black, and white varieties.  It is used as a gemstone and in some industrial applications.|例句：The jewelry featured a stunning cyanite pendant. 这件首饰上有一枚令人惊艳的蓝晶石吊坠。|例句：Cyanite's high-temperature stability makes it useful in certain ceramic applications. 蓝晶石的高温稳定性使其在某些陶瓷应用中非常有用。|近义词：kyanite (alternative spelling)|反义词: N/A
