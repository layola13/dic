judah:/ˈdʒuːdə/ | n. |犹大，犹大支派 | The name Judah refers to the fourth son of Jacob and Leah in the Bible, and also to the tribe descended from him and the kingdom that eventually bore his name.  It is also a common Jewish given name.  The kingdom of Judah was a major player in ancient Near Eastern history. | Example: Judah was one of the twelve tribes of Israel.  犹大是以色列十二支派之一。 The history of the Kingdom of Judah is fascinating and complex. 犹大王国的历史既迷人又复杂。|近义词：Jude (alternative spelling)|反义词：N/A
