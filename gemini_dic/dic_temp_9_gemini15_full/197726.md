shifting:/ˈʃɪftɪŋ/ | v. adj. n.|转移；移动；变化；轮班；转换；漂移 |  To move or cause to move from one place to another; to change gradually; a change in position, direction, or condition; a period of work |例句：The wind is shifting. 风向在变化。The company is shifting its focus to online sales. 公司正将重点转向在线销售。|近义词：moving, changing, altering, transferring |反义词：static, unchanging, fixed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind shifts constantly. = 风不断地变化。
He shifts his weight from one foot to the other. = 他把体重从一只脚换到另一只脚。


一般过去时:
The tectonic plates shifted violently. = 地壳板块剧烈移动。
She shifted the blame onto her colleague. = 她把责任推到了同事身上。


现在完成时:
The company has shifted its marketing strategy. = 公司已经改变了它的营销策略。
The sands have shifted dramatically over the centuries. = 数百年来，沙子发生了巨大的变化。


过去完成时:
The ground had shifted before the earthquake. = 地面在地震前就已经移动了。
They had shifted their priorities after the accident. = 事故发生后，他们改变了优先级。


将来时:
The company will shift its production to a new facility. = 公司将把生产转移到新的工厂。
The weather will shift to warmer conditions later this week. = 本周晚些时候天气将会转暖。


虚拟语气:
If the wind shifted, we could sail. = 如果风向改变，我们就能航行了。
I wish the focus of the discussion had shifted earlier. = 我希望讨论的重点早点转移。


被动语态:
The furniture was shifted to the new house. = 家具被搬到了新房子里。
The blame was shifted to the junior staff. = 责任被推到了初级员工身上。


疑问句:
Is the focus shifting? = 重点是否正在转移？
Has the wind shifted direction? = 风向改变了吗？


否定句:
The ground didn't shift during the storm. = 暴风雨期间地面没有移动。
The company hasn't shifted its position on the issue. = 公司在这个问题上的立场没有改变。


PHRASE->
shift gears = 换挡；改变策略
shift responsibility = 转嫁责任
shift focus = 转移焦点
night shift = 夜班
shift pattern = 轮班模式
