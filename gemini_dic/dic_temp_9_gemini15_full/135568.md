USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It modifies nouns.

* a nonpoliticking organization: 一个非政治组织

词源:

The word is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the noun "politicking" (engaging in politics).  "Politicking" itself comes from "politics," ultimately derived from the Greek word "politikos," meaning "of, for, or relating to citizens."
