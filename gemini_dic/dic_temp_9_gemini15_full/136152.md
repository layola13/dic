nonregenerating: /ˌnɒnˈrɪdʒənəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |非再生性的，不可再生的| Not able to regenerate or be regenerated.  This adjective describes something that cannot be renewed, restored, or replaced naturally or through any process.  It often applies to biological tissues or resources. |例句：The nerve cells in this part of the brain are nonregenerating.  这个大脑部位的神经细胞是非再生性的。

The damage to the spinal cord was nonregenerating, leading to permanent paralysis. 脊髓损伤是不可再生的，导致永久性瘫痪。


近义词：irreplaceable, unrenewable, irreparable |反义词：regenerative, renewable
