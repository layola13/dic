sceptic:/ˈskeptɪk/ | n. |怀疑论者，怀疑者 |A person inclined to question or doubt accepted opinions.  Someone who is skeptical. |例句：He's a sceptic about the government's claims. 他对政府的说法表示怀疑。|例句：Many sceptics remain unconvinced by the evidence. 许多怀疑论者仍然不相信证据。|近义词：doubter, questioner, cynic |反义词：believer, optimist | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a sceptic about climate change. = 他是一个怀疑气候变化的人。
The sceptic questions every claim. = 怀疑论者质疑每一个说法。


一般过去时:
He was a sceptic throughout his life. = 他一生都是个怀疑论者。
The sceptic questioned the validity of the study. = 怀疑论者质疑了该研究的有效性。


现在完成时:
She has been a sceptic since her youth. = 她从年轻时起就是一个怀疑论者。
The sceptic has voiced his concerns publicly. = 怀疑论者公开表达了他的担忧。


过去完成时:
He had been a sceptic before the evidence was presented. = 在证据出示之前，他一直是个怀疑论者。
The sceptic had already formed his opinion. = 怀疑论者已经形成了自己的观点。


将来时:
He will remain a sceptic. = 他将仍然是一个怀疑论者。
The sceptic will continue to ask questions. = 怀疑论者将继续提出问题。


虚拟语气:
If he were a sceptic, he would question the results. = 如果他是个怀疑论者，他会质疑结果。
I wish I were a sceptic, to have a clearer mind. = 我希望我是一个怀疑论者，能有一个更清晰的头脑。


被动语态:
His claims were doubted by the sceptic. = 他的说法受到怀疑论者的质疑。
The evidence was questioned by the sceptics. = 证据受到怀疑论者的质疑。


疑问句:
Is he a sceptic? = 他是怀疑论者吗？
Are there any sceptics among us? = 我们中间有怀疑论者吗？


否定句:
He is not a sceptic. = 他不是怀疑论者。
She wasn't a sceptic before the incident. = 事件发生前她不是怀疑论者。
