raddest: /ˈrædəst/ | adj. |最棒的，最酷的，最厉害的 |  The word "raddest" is a superlative form of "rad," a slang term meaning "excellent," "cool," or "awesome."  It emphasizes the highest degree of coolness or awesomeness.  It's primarily used in informal settings and among younger generations. |例句：That's the raddest skateboard I've ever seen!  = 那是我见过的最酷的滑板！

That concert was totally raddest! = 那场音乐会真是太棒了！
