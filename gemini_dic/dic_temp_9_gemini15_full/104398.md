kabukis:/kəˈbuːkiːz/| n. |歌舞伎剧目（复数）|Kabukis are traditional Japanese theatrical performances known for their elaborate costumes, stylized acting, and musical accompaniment.  The word refers to multiple plays or performances.  |例句：The theater company will be presenting several kabukis this season.  这家剧团本季将上演几出歌舞伎。

例句：Many tourists come to Japan specifically to see the kabukis.许多游客来日本就是为了看歌舞伎。


近义词：Japanese theatre performances, traditional Japanese dramas|反义词：无


词源：源自日语“歌舞伎”（Kabuki）。 “歌”（ka）意为歌，“舞”（bu）意为舞，“伎”（ki）意为技艺，合起来指一种结合了歌唱、舞蹈和表演艺术的戏剧形式。
