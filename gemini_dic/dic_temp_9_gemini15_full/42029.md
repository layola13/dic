costlier:/ˈkɒstliər/| /ˈkɔːstliər/ | adj. | 更昂贵的；代价更高的 | More expensive; having a higher cost.  |This diamond necklace is far costlier than I anticipated. 这条钻石项链比我预期的贵得多。|expensive, dear, pricey | cheaper, inexpensive


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new model is costlier than the old one. = 新型号比旧型号贵。
These shoes are costlier than I can afford. = 这些鞋比我能承受的贵。


一般过去时:
The tickets were costlier last year. = 去年票价更贵。
That painting was costlier than we expected. = 那幅画比我们预期的贵。


现在完成时:
Gas has become costlier this year. = 今年汽油变得更贵了。
Everything has gotten costlier since the war. = 自从战争以来，一切东西都变贵了。


过去完成时:
The house had already become costlier before we decided to buy. = 在我们决定购买之前，房子已经涨价了。
The materials had become costlier than originally estimated. = 材料的成本比最初估计的要高。


将来时:
Flights will be costlier during the peak season. = 在旅游旺季，航班会更贵。
Housing will likely be costlier in the city center. = 市中心的住房可能会更贵。


虚拟语气:
If the materials were costlier, the project would be delayed. = 如果材料更贵，项目就会被延误。
I wish the concert tickets weren't so costlier. = 我希望演唱会门票不要这么贵。


被动语态:
The product is considered costlier than its competitors. = 该产品被认为比竞争对手的产品更贵。
The project was made costlier by unexpected delays. = 由意外延误导致该项目成本增加。


疑问句:
Is this option costlier than that one? = 这个选项比那个选项贵吗？
Are the new prices costlier than before? = 新价格比以前贵吗？


否定句:
The car isn't costlier than I thought it would be. = 这辆车不像我想象的那么贵。
The renovations weren't costlier than we budgeted. = 装修的费用没有超出我们的预算。


PHRASE->
much costlier: 贵得多
considerably costlier:  贵得多
relatively costlier: 相对较贵
significantly costlier: 明显更贵
prohibitively costlier:  贵到买不起
