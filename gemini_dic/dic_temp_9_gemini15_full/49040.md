degustation:/ˌdeɡəˈsteɪʃən/| n. |品尝；品酒会 |A tasting, especially of wines.  A formal tasting of various foods or wines, often involving small portions.|The restaurant offered a delightful degustation menu featuring local ingredients. 这家餐厅提供了一份以当地食材为主的美味品尝菜单。| wine tasting, tasting menu |  nausea (in the context of an unpleasant tasting) | 名词
