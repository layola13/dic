rsvp'd: /ˌɑːrsviːˈpiːd/ | v. | 回复(RSVP) | The past tense and past participle of RSVP.  It indicates that someone has responded to a request for an RSVP. | 例句：I've RSVP'd to the wedding invitation. = 我已经回复了婚礼邀请。  She RSVP'd yes to the party. = 她回复说参加聚会。 | 近义词：responded, answered, confirmed | 反义词：declined, refused


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I RSVP to all invitations. = 我回复所有的邀请函。
They RSVP promptly. = 他们迅速地回复。


一般过去时:
I RSVP'd to the event last week. = 我上周回复了那个活动。
He RSVP'd  no to the dinner. = 他回复说不参加晚餐。


现在完成时:
I have RSVP'd to the conference. = 我已经回复了会议邀请。
She has RSVP'd to every event this month. = 她这个月已经回复了每一个活动的邀请。


过去完成时:
I had RSVP'd before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经回复了。
They had RSVP'd to the party before we even sent the invitations. = 在我们甚至发出邀请函之前，他们就已经回复了派对邀请。


将来时:
I will RSVP as soon as I know my schedule. = 我一知道我的行程安排就会回复。
We will RSVP by Friday. = 我们会在周五之前回复。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the details, I would RSVP immediately. = 如果我知道细节，我会立即回复。
I wish I had RSVP'd sooner. = 我希望我早点回复。


被动语态:
The invitations were RSVP'd to by most of the guests. = 大多数客人都回复了邀请函。
The event was poorly attended because many people didn't RSVP. = 活动参与度低，因为很多人没有回复。


疑问句:
Have you RSVP'd to John's party yet? = 你已经回复约翰的派对邀请了吗？
Did they RSVP to the wedding? = 他们回复婚礼邀请了吗？


否定句:
I didn't RSVP to the meeting. = 我没有回复会议邀请。
He hasn't RSVP'd yet. = 他还没回复。


PHRASE->
RSVP to an event = 回复一个活动的邀请
RSVP by a certain date = 在特定日期前回复
RSVP yes/no = 回复是/否
get an RSVP = 收到回复
send out RSVP requests = 发送回复请求
