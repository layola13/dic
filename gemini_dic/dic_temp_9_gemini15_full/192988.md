second:/ˈsekənd/ |/ˈsɛkənd/| n. adj. num. | 秒；第二；次等；其次的 |  A second is a unit of time equal to 1/60 of a minute.  It can also refer to the second in a series or order. As an adjective, it means coming after the first. As a numeral, it represents the number 2. | 例句：The race was decided in the last second.  比赛在最后一秒钟决出胜负。
