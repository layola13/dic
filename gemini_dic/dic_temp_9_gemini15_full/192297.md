screwed-up:/skruːd ʌp/ | adj. | 混乱的；糟糕的；搞砸了的；精神错乱的 |  Describes something that is badly done, disorganized, or in a state of disrepair; can also refer to someone who is mentally unstable or dysfunctional. |例句：The project is completely screwed up.  这个项目完全搞砸了。

例句：He's a screwed-up individual with a troubled past. 他是个过去经历坎坷，精神错乱的家伙。|近义词：messed up, messed-up, botched, chaotic, dysfunctional, deranged |反义词：organized, well-ordered, functional, sane
