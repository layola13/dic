equipedal: /ɪˈkwɪpɪdəl/ | adj. | 等足的；同足的 | Having or relating to feet of equal length or size.  The term is primarily used in zoology and related fields to describe animals with legs of equal length. | Example:  Many insects are equipedal, allowing for efficient movement across various terrains.  许多昆虫都是等足的，这使得它们能够在各种地形上有效地移动。|近义词: equilong (in terms of length)|反义词:inequalipedal (having legs of unequal length)
