noncrystallizing: /ˌnɒnˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 非晶化的，不结晶的 | Not forming crystals; amorphous.  The term describes a substance or process that does not solidify into a crystalline structure.  | Example: The noncrystallizing polymer remained in a viscous state even after cooling.  非晶态聚合物即使冷却后也保持粘性状态。 | 近义词：amorphous, glassy | 反义词：crystallizing, crystalline
