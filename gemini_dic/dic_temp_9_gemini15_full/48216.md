deconsecrate: /ˌdiːˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/ | /ˌdiːˈkɑːnsɪkreɪt/ | vt. | 撤圣；亵渎；取消…的圣洁 | To remove the consecration or sacred character from (something, especially a church or object). To profane or violate the sanctity of. |例句：The old church was deconsecrated and turned into a museum.  = 那座旧教堂被撤圣，变成了博物馆。
The vandals deconsecrated the ancient altar. = 那些破坏分子亵渎了古老的祭坛。|近义词：desecrate, profane, violate |反义词：consecrate, sanctify
