self-styled:/ˌselfˈstaɪld/| adj.|自称的，自封的|Describes someone who gives themselves a title or description without having been officially given it.  It often implies a lack of genuine entitlement to the title or description.|例句：He's a self-styled expert on ancient civilizations, but his knowledge is actually quite limited. 他自称是古代文明专家，但他的知识实际上相当有限。
例句：The self-styled revolutionary leader was eventually overthrown.  这位自封的革命领袖最终被推翻了。|近义词：self-proclaimed, self-designated|反义词：officially recognized, accredited
