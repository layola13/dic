contrarotation: /ˌkɒntrəʊroʊˈteɪʃən/| /ˌkɑːntrəʊroʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. |反向旋转；反旋|The simultaneous rotation of two parts in opposite directions.  This often occurs in biological systems and in certain types of machinery. For example, in some bird wings, the primary feathers rotate in one direction while the secondary feathers rotate in the opposite direction.  | Example: The contrarotation of the propellers provided increased stability to the aircraft.  反向旋转的螺旋桨为飞机提供了更高的稳定性。|近义词：opposite rotation, counter-rotation |反义词：co-rotation, corotation
