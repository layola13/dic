non-rational:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəl/| adj. |非理性的，不合理的|Not based on or guided by reason; illogical.  It implies a lack of logical thought processes or a disregard for reason. |例句：His decision to quit his job without another lined up was a non-rational act.  他的决定在没有找到另一份工作的情况下辞职是不理性的行为。

例句：Her fear of spiders, despite their harmlessness, was a non-rational response. 她对蜘蛛的恐惧，尽管它们无害，是一种非理性的反应。


近义词：irrational, illogical, unreasonable, absurd, senseless

反义词：rational, logical, reasonable, sensible
