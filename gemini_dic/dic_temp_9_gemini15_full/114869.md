mammonism:/ˈmæməˌnɪzəm/| n. |拜物教；唯利是图；贪财|The worship of wealth and material possessions; the belief that material wealth is the highest good.  It emphasizes the pursuit of riches and worldly success above all else, often to the detriment of spiritual or moral values.|例句：His mammonism was evident in his relentless pursuit of wealth, regardless of the ethical implications.他的拜物教在他不顾伦理后果地追求财富中显而易见。

例句：The company's actions showed a clear case of mammonism, prioritizing profit over employee well-being. 该公司的行为清楚地表明了拜物教，他们将利润置于员工福祉之上。|近义词：materialism, avarice, greed, cupidity|反义词：altruism, asceticism, charity, selflessness|名词
