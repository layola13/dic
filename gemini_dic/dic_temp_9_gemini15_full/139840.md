octopoda:/ɒkˈtɒpədə/| n. |八腕类，章鱼类|Octopoda is an order of marine cephalopod molluscs commonly known as octopuses.  They are characterized by eight arms, a soft body, and a remarkable intelligence.|Example:  Many species of octopuses belong to the order Octopoda.|近义词：octopus (although octopus refers to a single animal, while Octopoda refers to the order)|反义词：无
