empires:/ˈɛmpaɪərz/| n.|帝国，帝國|A large group of countries or regions ruled by a single person, government, or power.|例句：The Roman Empire was one of the most powerful empires in history. 罗马帝国是历史上最强大的帝国之一。
The British Empire once controlled a vast territory. 英国帝国曾经控制着广阔的领土。|近义词：kingdoms, realms, dominions|反义词：colonies, republics
