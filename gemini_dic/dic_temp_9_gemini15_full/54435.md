disinflation:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfleɪʃən/| n. |通货膨胀减缓，通货膨胀率下降|The slowing down of inflation; a decrease in the rate of inflation.|The government implemented policies aimed at disinflation to stabilize the economy. 政府实施旨在控制通货膨胀的政策以稳定经济。|近义词：deflation, slowing of inflation|反义词：inflation, hyperinflation
