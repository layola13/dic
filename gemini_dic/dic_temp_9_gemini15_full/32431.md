chime-in:/tʃaɪmˈɪn/| v.|插话，加入谈话|To interrupt a conversation to add one's opinion or information.|The children all chimed in with their ideas for the party. 孩子们都插话进来，提出他们对聚会的想法。|interrupt, interject, butt in|remain silent, keep quiet|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chimes in during every conversation. 他每次谈话都插话。
They chime in with their opinions frequently. 他们经常插话发表意见。


一般过去时:
She chimed in with a witty remark. 她插话说了句俏皮话。
He chimed in to correct the speaker's mistake. 他插话纠正说话者的错误。


现在完成时:
They have chimed in on the debate several times. 他们多次在辩论中插话。
She has chimed in with helpful suggestions. 她已经插话提出了有益的建议。


过去完成时:
Before I could finish, he had chimed in with a question.  在我还没说完之前，他已经插话问了一个问题。
They had chimed in with their concerns before the meeting ended.  在会议结束之前，他们已经插话表达了他们的担忧。


将来时:
I think he will chime in with his usual skepticism. 我想他会像往常一样，带着怀疑的态度插话。
She will chime in when she feels it's appropriate. 她会在她觉得合适的时候插话。


虚拟语气:
If he chimed in, the meeting would have been even longer. 如果他插话，会议就会更长。
I wish he hadn't chimed in so rudely. 我希望他不要那么粗鲁地插话。


被动语态:
His opinions were often chimed in with by others. 他的意见经常被别人附和。
The discussion was constantly being chimed in on.  讨论一直在不断地被插话打断。


疑问句:
Will he chime in during the presentation? 他会在演示过程中插话吗？
Did anyone chime in with a different solution? 有没有人插话提出不同的解决方案？


否定句:
He didn't chime in during the entire meeting. 他在整个会议期间都没有插话。
She chose not to chime in on the argument. 她选择不参与这场争论。


PHRASE->

chime in on =  参与讨论
