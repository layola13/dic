self-discoveries:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈskʌvəriːz/| n. |自我发现；自我认知 |The process of gaining a deeper understanding of oneself, one's strengths, weaknesses, values, and goals.  This often involves introspection, exploration, and experimentation.  It can be a gradual process spanning a lifetime or a series of significant realizations. |例句：Her travels led to many profound self-discoveries.  她的旅行让她有了许多深刻的自我发现。

例句：Through therapy, she made several important self-discoveries about her childhood. 通过治疗，她对自己的童年做了一些重要的自我发现。


近义词：self-awareness, self-knowledge, insights, epiphanies |反义词：self-deception, ignorance, unawareness |词性：名词
