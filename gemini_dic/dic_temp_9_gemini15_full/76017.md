furfur:/ˈfɜːrfɜːr/| n. |糠秕；麸皮；头屑 | Bran, the outer layer of a cereal grain, separated from the kernel during milling.  It is often used as animal feed or in some food products.  Also refers to dandruff. |例句：The miller separated the wheat into flour and furfur. 磨坊主将小麦分离成面粉和麸皮。 |例句：He suffered from excessive furfur on his scalp. 他头皮上长了很多头皮屑。 |近义词：bran, chaff, dandruff |反义词：flour (in the context of grain processing)
