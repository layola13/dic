fluvialist:/ˈfluːviəlɪst/ | n. |河流学家，河流专家| A fluvialist is a scientist who studies rivers, their processes, and their impact on the environment.  They investigate various aspects of river systems, including hydrology, geomorphology, ecology, and human interactions. | Example: The fluvialist presented her findings on the impact of dam construction on river ecosystems. 河流学家介绍了她关于水坝建设对河流生态系统影响的研究结果。 |近义词：river scientist, fluvial geomorphologist |反义词： (无明确反义词，可考虑与研究海洋或其他水体的专家的对比) |名词
