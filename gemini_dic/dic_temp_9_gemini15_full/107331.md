lambkill:/ˈlæmˌkɪl/| n.|羊羔杀;羊踯躅|A common name for several poisonous plants, especially *Kalmia angustifolia* and *Kalmia latifolia*, that can be fatal to sheep and other livestock if ingested.  The name derives from the plant's toxicity to lambs.  It is also known as sheep laurel or calico bush.|例句：The farmer lost several lambs to lambkill poisoning this spring.  今年春天，这位农民因羊踯躅中毒损失了几只羊羔。|近义词：sheep laurel, calico bush,  poison oak (in terms of toxicity)|反义词：无|名词
