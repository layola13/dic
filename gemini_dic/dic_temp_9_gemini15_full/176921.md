realising: /ˌriːəˈlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 意识到；实现；了解；完成 |  The act of becoming aware of something; the process of making something real or actual.  |  例句：He realised his mistake. 他意识到他的错误。


例句：I'm realising my dream of becoming a writer. 我正在实现我成为作家的梦想。


近义词：understanding, comprehending, achieving, accomplishing | 反义词：ignoring, overlooking, neglecting | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am realising my potential. = 我正在发挥我的潜力。
She is realising her ambitions. = 她正在实现她的抱负。


一般过去时:
He realised he was wrong. = 他意识到他错了。
They realised their mistake too late. = 他们太迟才意识到他们的错误。


现在完成时:
I have realised the importance of hard work. = 我已经意识到努力工作的重要性。
We have realised our goal. = 我们已经实现了我们的目标。


过去完成时:
She had realised her error before she submitted the report. = 她提交报告前已经意识到她的错误。
They had realised the danger before it was too late. = 他们意识到危险的时候还不算太晚。


将来时:
I will realise my dreams one day. = 我总有一天会实现我的梦想。
She will realise the consequences of her actions. = 她将会意识到她行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If I realised the risk, I would have done things differently. = 如果我意识到了风险，我会采取不同的做法。
I wish I had realised the truth sooner. = 我真希望我早点意识到真相。


被动语态:
The full potential of the software is yet to be realised. = 该软件的全部潜力尚未实现。
The project's goals were realised on time. = 项目的目标按时实现。


疑问句:
Do you realise the seriousness of the situation? = 你意识到情况的严重性吗？
Have you realised how much time has passed? = 你意识到过去了多少时间了吗？


否定句:
I didn't realise how late it was. = 我没有意识到已经这么晚了。
He didn't realise the impact of his words. = 他没有意识到他话语的影响。

PHRASE->
realising a dream = 实现梦想
realising the potential = 发挥潜力
realising the importance = 意识到重要性
realising an ambition = 实现抱负
realising the truth = 意识到真相
