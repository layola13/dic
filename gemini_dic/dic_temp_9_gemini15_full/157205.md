pink:/pɪŋk/| adj. |粉红色的；桃红色的|A pale red color.|例句：Her dress is a lovely shade of pink.  她的裙子是可爱的粉红色。|例句：The sunset painted the sky with streaks of pink and orange. 日落给天空涂上了粉红色和橙色的条纹。|近义词：rose, rosy, blush|反义词：black, dark


USAGE->
