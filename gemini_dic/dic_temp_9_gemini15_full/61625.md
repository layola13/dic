emigrating:/ˈɛməˌɡreɪtɪŋ/| v. | 移民；移居国外 | The act of leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another.  It implies a voluntary move, often for better opportunities or to escape difficult circumstances. |  例句：Many families are emigrating from their home country in search of better economic opportunities. 许多家庭正在从他们的祖国移居国外，以寻求更好的经济机会。

例句：He is emigrating to Australia next month. 他下个月要移民澳大利亚。


近义词： migrating, relocating, expatriating

反义词： immigrating, returning, repatriating

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are emigrating to Canada. = 他们正在移民加拿大。
She is emigrating next year. = 她明年要移民。


一般过去时:
My grandparents emigrated to the United States. = 我的祖父母移民到了美国。
He emigrated from Ireland in 1990. = 他在1990年从爱尔兰移民。


现在完成时:
They have emigrated to Australia. = 他们已经移民到澳大利亚了。
She has emigrated and started a new life. = 她已经移民并开始了新的生活。


过去完成时:
They had emigrated before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前他们已经移民了。
She had emigrated years before I met her. = 我遇见她之前，她已经移民多年了。


将来时:
We will emigrate to New Zealand next spring. = 我们明年春天将移民到新西兰。
They will emigrate as soon as they have saved enough money. = 他们攒够钱后就会移民。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would emigrate. = 如果我有钱，我会移民。
I wish I could emigrate to a warmer country. = 我希望我能移民到一个气候更温暖的国家。


被动语态:
Many people are being encouraged to emigrate. = 许多人被鼓励移民。
The family was forced to emigrate during the war. = 这个家庭在战争期间被迫移民。


疑问句:
Are they emigrating soon? = 他们很快就要移民了吗？
Will you emigrate to another country? = 你会移民到另一个国家吗？


否定句:
He is not emigrating yet. = 他还没移民。
They did not emigrate because of the pandemic. = 由于疫情，他们没有移民。


PHRASE->
emigrate from = 从...移民
emigrate to = 移民到...
emigrate because of = 因为...而移民
emigrate for = 为了...而移民
emigrate with = 和...一起移民
