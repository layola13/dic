词源:
The word "petrochemicals" is a compound word formed from "petro-" (from "petroleum") and "-chemicals".  "Petroleum" itself originates from Latin:  "petra" (rock) and "oleum" (oil), literally meaning "rock oil".  Thus, petrochemicals literally refers to chemicals derived from rock oil.
