cosmologists:/ˌkɒzmˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |宇宙学家 |Scientists who study cosmology, the origin and development of the universe.  They study the large-scale structure of the universe, its composition, and its evolution. |例句：Cosmologists are using powerful telescopes to observe distant galaxies and unravel the mysteries of the universe. 宇宙学家们正在使用强大的望远镜来观测遥远的星系，并解开宇宙的奥秘。|例句：Leading cosmologists disagree on the ultimate fate of the universe.  领先的宇宙学家们对宇宙的最终命运持有不同意见。|近义词：astrophysicists, astronomers |反义词：无
