pleurotoma:/ˌplʊərəˈtoʊmə/| n. |骨螺属；翼螺属|A genus of predatory sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the family Pleurotomidae.  Pleurotoma is an outdated term, and the genus has been divided into numerous other genera. |例句：The pleurotoma was a common sight in the shallow waters. 骨螺属在浅水区很常见。  |近义词：turrid (a more modern term for similar snails) |反义词：N/A
