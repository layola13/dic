funeral:/ˈfjuːnərəl/| n. |葬礼，丧葬|A funeral is a ceremony or service held to bury or cremate a deceased person.  It often involves religious rites, eulogies, and the gathering of mourners.|Example:  The funeral was a beautiful and moving tribute to his life. 葬礼是他人生的一个美丽而感人的致敬。 The funeral procession moved slowly through the streets. 葬礼队伍缓缓地穿过街道。|近义词：burial, obsequies|反义词：birth, celebration
