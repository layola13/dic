igloos:/ˈɪɡluːz/| n.|冰屋|A dome-shaped house built of blocks of hard-packed snow by the Inuit people of Arctic regions.  Igloos provide excellent insulation and protection from the harsh Arctic climate. |The Inuit built igloos to survive the harsh winters.  因纽特人建造冰屋以度过严酷的冬天。  Many tourists are fascinated by the traditional igloo structures. 许多游客对传统的冰屋结构很着迷。|hut, cabin|house (in a non-permanent sense), tent|名词
