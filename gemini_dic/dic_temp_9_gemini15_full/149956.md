pallor:/ˈpeɪlər/| n.|苍白，脸色苍白|A deficiency of color especially of the face; paleness.|The pallor of his face suggested illness. 他脸色苍白，这表明他病了。  Her face was marked by a deathly pallor. 她的脸上呈现出死一般的苍白。|paleness, wanness|ruddy, flushed
