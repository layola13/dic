hypothesize:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪz/ /haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪz/ | vt. |假设；提出假设 |To form a hypothesis; to propose an explanation based on limited evidence that can be tested.  |The scientist hypothesized that the new drug would cure the disease.  科学家假设这种新药能治愈这种疾病。| postulate, theorize, conjecture | refute, disprove | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists hypothesize about the origins of the universe. = 科学家们对宇宙的起源进行假设。
She hypothesizes that stress causes illness. = 她假设压力会导致疾病。


一般过去时:
They hypothesized that the results were due to chance. = 他们假设结果是由于偶然造成的。
The researchers hypothesized a link between diet and cancer. = 研究人员假设饮食与癌症之间存在关联。


现在完成时:
Researchers have hypothesized a connection between sleep deprivation and cognitive impairment. = 研究人员已经假设睡眠不足与认知障碍之间存在联系。
Scientists have hypothesized that dark matter makes up most of the universe. = 科学家们假设暗物质构成了宇宙的大部分。


过去完成时:
They had hypothesized that the experiment would fail before they even started. = 在他们甚至开始之前，他们就假设实验会失败。
The team had hypothesized a different outcome based on preliminary data. = 基于初步数据，团队曾假设过不同的结果。


将来时:
We will hypothesize about the impact of climate change. = 我们将假设气候变化的影响。
She will hypothesize that the new policy will be ineffective. = 她将假设新政策会无效。


虚拟语气:
If we were to hypothesize, we might suggest that the earth is flat. = 如果我们要假设的话，我们可能会认为地球是平的。
I wish I hadn't hypothesized so hastily. = 我真希望我没有如此匆忙地假设。


被动语态:
It was hypothesized that the cure would be found soon. = 人们假设很快就会找到治愈方法。
The connection between the two events was hypothesized to be coincidental. = 人们假设这两个事件之间的联系是巧合。


疑问句:
What do you hypothesize will happen next? = 你假设接下来会发生什么？
How do they hypothesize the data will be interpreted? = 他们如何假设数据将被解释？


否定句:
The researchers did not hypothesize a direct causal relationship. = 研究人员没有假设存在直接的因果关系。
We cannot hypothesize without sufficient evidence. = 没有足够的证据，我们不能假设。


PHRASE->
hypothesize about = 对…进行假设
hypothesize that = 假设…
hypothesize a connection = 假设一种联系
hypothesize the cause = 假设原因
formulate a hypothesis = 制定一个假设
