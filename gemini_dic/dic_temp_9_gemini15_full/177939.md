rechromatographed:/ˌriːˈkroʊməˌtɑːɡræft/| v. |再色谱分析的；再次进行色谱分离的 |To chromatograph (a substance) again. This is typically done to further purify a substance or to resolve components that were not fully separated in the initial chromatography.  |Example: The impure product was rechromatographed to obtain a higher purity compound.  再次进行色谱分离以获得更高纯度的化合物。|近义词：re-separated, purified further|反义词：N/A
