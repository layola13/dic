disapplication:/ˌdɪsˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |废止；取消适用；不适用|The act of declaring something inapplicable or void; the state of being inapplicable.  It often refers to the removal of the application of a law, rule, or provision to a particular case or situation. |例句：The court ordered the disapplication of the law in this specific instance. 法院命令在本例中废止该法律。 The disapplication of the regulation was deemed necessary due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，该规定的取消适用被认为是必要的。|近义词：invalidation, annulment, repeal, revocation |反义词：application, enforcement, implementation |名词
