outshrieked:/ˌaʊtˈʃriːkt/| v.|大声喊叫，尖叫|To shout or scream loudly and suddenly.  The word implies a sudden, forceful, and often emotional outburst.|例句：The crowd outshrieked their approval of the performance.  观众大声欢呼以示对演出的认可。

例句：She outshrieked her fear when she saw the snake. 她看到蛇时吓得大声尖叫。


近义词：yelled, screamed, shouted, cried out|反义词：whispered, murmured
