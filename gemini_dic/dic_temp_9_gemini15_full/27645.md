incandescent: /ˌɪn.kænˈdes.ənt/ | adj. | 白炽的；发光的；热烈的；激昂的 |  Shining brightly with heat; glowing.  Describes something that emits light as a result of being heated.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something intense or passionate. |


例句：

The incandescent bulb lit up the room. = 白炽灯泡照亮了房间。

His incandescent anger was frightening. = 他怒火中烧，令人害怕。


近义词：glowing, radiant, luminous, fervent, passionate

反义词：dull, dim, cold, apathetic, indifferent


词源:

From Latin *incandēscēre, from *in- + candēscēre "to become white-hot," from candēre "to be white, glow."
