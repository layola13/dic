coenoecium:/ˌsiːnoʊˈiːsiəm/| n. |群落，共同体（指有机体群落）|A coenoecium is a colony of organisms, particularly referring to a common living structure formed by colonial organisms, such as certain bryozoans or tunicates.  It represents a collective body formed by the fusion of individual zooids or organisms. |Example: The bryozoan formed a complex coenoecium, with numerous interconnected zooids.  苔藓虫形成了一个复杂的群落，许多个虫体相互连接。|近义词：colony, community|反义词：individual, isolate
