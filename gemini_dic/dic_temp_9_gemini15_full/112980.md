lullabying:/ˈlʌləbaɪɪŋ/| v. | 哼催眠曲；轻声细语地哄；轻柔地安抚 |The act of singing a lullaby or soothing someone to sleep with gentle sounds or movements.  It implies a gentle, rocking, and soothing action. |例句：The mother was lullabying her baby to sleep. 母亲正在哼着摇篮曲哄孩子睡觉。

例句：He lullabyed his worries away with a glass of wine. 他用一杯酒轻轻地抚平了忧虑。


近义词：soothing, comforting, rocking, humming, crooning |反义词：agitating, disturbing, jarring, exciting


词性:动词
