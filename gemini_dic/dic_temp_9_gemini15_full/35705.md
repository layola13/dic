cobwork:/ˈkɒbˌwɜːrk/ | n. | 编织的粗糙石墙；粗石砌筑 |Rough, uneven stonework, typically made with irregularly shaped stones and mortar.  It is often used in rustic or vernacular architecture.|The old cottage was built with charming cobwork walls. 这间老小屋是用迷人的粗石墙建造的。|近义词：rough stonework, rubblework |反义词：smooth stonework, ashlar
