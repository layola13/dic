ovaritis:/ˌoʊvəˈraɪtɪs/ | n. | 卵巢炎 | Inflammation of an ovary.  It can be caused by various factors, including infections, autoimmune disorders, and even the spread of infection from other pelvic organs. Symptoms can vary but may include pelvic pain, fever, and abnormal vaginal discharge.  Diagnosis typically involves a physical exam, imaging tests (like ultrasound), and sometimes blood tests.
