cromlech:/ˈkrɒmlɛk/ | n. |巨石阵；环状列石 |A cromlech is a prehistoric monument consisting of a circle of large stones, often with a capstone.  It is typically associated with Neolithic and Bronze Age cultures.  |例句：The cromlech at Stonehenge is a famous example of Neolithic architecture. 巨石阵的环状列石是新石器时代建筑的著名例子。  |近义词：stone circle, megalithic structure |反义词：N/A
