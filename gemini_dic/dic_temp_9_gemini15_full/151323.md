parochial:/pəˈroʊkiəl/| adj.|狭隘的；教区的；地方性的 | relating to a parish; having a limited or narrow outlook; confined or restricted | The newspaper's coverage was criticized for being too parochial, focusing only on local issues. 这家报纸的报道因过于地方化而受到批评，只关注当地问题。|近义词：provincial, narrow-minded, insular |反义词：cosmopolitan, broad-minded, universal
