revivifies:/rɪˈvaɪvɪfaɪz/| vt.|使复苏；使恢复活力；使再生|To restore to life or consciousness; to renew or refresh.|The rain revivified the wilting plants.  雨水使枯萎的植物恢复了生机。|The artist's new work revivified his career. 艺术家的新作使他的事业焕发了生机。|reanimate, revitalize, refresh|kill, destroy, extinguish|动词
