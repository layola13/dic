designs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/ | n. |设计；图案；计划；企图 |Designs refer to the process of creating something, often with aesthetic or functional considerations.  It can also refer to the plan or intention behind an action. |例句：The designs for the new building are impressive. 新建筑的设计令人印象深刻。
The interior designs are quite modern. 室内设计非常现代化。

近义词：plans, schemes, patterns, sketches, concepts |反义词：chaos, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He designs websites for a living. = 他以设计网站为生。
She designs beautiful dresses. = 她设计漂亮的衣服。


一般过去时:
They designed a new logo for the company. = 他们为公司设计了一个新的标志。
He designed and built his own house. = 他自己设计并建造了自己的房子。


现在完成时:
She has designed many successful products. = 她已经设计了许多成功的产品。
They have designed a new marketing campaign. = 他们已经设计了一个新的营销活动。


过去完成时:
The architect had designed the building before the construction began. = 建筑师在施工开始之前就设计好了建筑。
They had designed the game for several years before releasing it. = 他们在发行游戏之前已经设计了数年。


将来时:
We will design a new website next year. = 我们明年将设计一个新的网站。
The company will design a new product line. = 公司将设计一条新的产品线。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would design my own garden. = 如果我有更多时间，我会自己设计我的花园。
I wish I had designed the dress myself. = 我真希望我自己设计了那件衣服。


被动语态:
The website was designed by a professional team. = 该网站是由一个专业团队设计的。
The building was designed to be energy-efficient. = 这座建筑的设计是为了节能。


疑问句:
Who designed this beautiful dress? = 谁设计了这件漂亮的衣服？
What software do you use to design graphics? = 你用什么软件设计图形？


否定句:
He didn't design the logo; he only helped with the marketing. = 他没有设计标志；他只是帮助营销。
They didn't design the building to be so tall. = 他们设计这座建筑时并非打算这么高。
