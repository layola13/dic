fortifiers:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪərz/| n.|强化剂，增固剂|Substances added to food or drink to increase their nutritional value or to improve their texture or other qualities.|Examples:  Many breakfast cereals are fortified with vitamins and minerals. 许多早餐谷物都添加了维生素和矿物质。  The builder used fortifiers to strengthen the concrete. 建筑工人使用强化剂来增强混凝土强度。|近义词：additives, supplements|反义词：depleters, weakeners|名词
