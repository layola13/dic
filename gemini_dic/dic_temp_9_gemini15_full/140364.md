oil-painter: /ˈɔɪlˌpeɪntər/ | n. | 油画画家 | An artist who paints using oil paints.  Oil painters are known for their ability to create rich, luminous colors and textures.  Their work often involves layers of paint, allowing for depth and complexity. |  例句：He is a renowned oil-painter, famous for his landscapes. 他是一位著名的油画家，以其风景画而闻名。

例句：The museum showcased a collection of works by famous oil-painters from the Renaissance.  博物馆展出了文艺复兴时期著名油画家的一系列作品。


近义词：oil paint artist, painter (general term)


反义词：N/A (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with artists using other mediums like watercolorists or sculptors)


词性：名词
