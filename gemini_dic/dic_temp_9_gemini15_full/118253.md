merchandiser:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzər/|/ˈmɝːtʃəndaɪzɚ/| n. |商品策划人；推销员；经销商 |A merchandiser is a person who plans and executes the marketing of a product or range of products.  This includes activities such as product placement, pricing, promotion and sales.  They work to ensure that products are presented in the most appealing way to consumers, maximizing sales. |例句：The merchandiser carefully arranged the new clothing line to attract customers.  商品策划人仔细地安排新的服装系列以吸引顾客。

The experienced merchandiser developed a successful marketing campaign. 经验丰富的商品策划人制定了一个成功的营销活动。


近义词：retailer, marketer, salesperson, promoter |反义词：consumer, buyer
