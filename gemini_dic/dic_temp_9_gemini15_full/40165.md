contends:/kənˈtɛndz/ | vt. | 争论；主张；坚持；竞争 |  To argue or contend for something; to assert something as true; to strive or struggle in competition. | 例句：He contends that the evidence is insufficient. 他认为证据不足。  |  例句：Many companies contend for the same market share. 许多公司争夺相同的市场份额。 | assert, maintain, argue, claim | concede, admit, yield | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contends that the Earth is flat. = 他声称地球是平的。
She contends with her rivals for the top position. = 她与竞争对手争夺第一名。

一般过去时:
They contended that the treaty was unfair. = 他们认为该条约不公平。
He contended fiercely for his rights. = 他为自己的权利进行了激烈的争斗。

现在完成时:
Scientists have contended for years about the origin of the universe. = 科学家们多年来一直就宇宙的起源争论不休。
She has contended with many challenges throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她克服了许多挑战。

过去完成时:
He had contended that the results were flawed before the report was released. = 在报告发布之前，他就声称结果有缺陷。
They had contended for control of the company for many years. = 他们多年来一直争夺公司控制权。

将来时:
She will contend that her innocence will be proven. = 她将声称她的清白会被证明。
They will contend vigorously against any attempt to limit their freedom. = 他们将坚决反对任何限制他们自由的企图。

虚拟语气:
If he contended more forcefully, he might have won the debate. = 如果他更有力地争辩，他可能赢得了辩论。
I wish he wouldn't contend with me so much. = 我希望他不要总是跟我争论。

被动语态:
It was contended that the new policy was necessary. = 人们认为新政策是必要的。
The results of the study were contended by many experts. = 许多专家对研究结果提出了异议。

疑问句:
Does he contend that his actions were justified? = 他认为他的行为是正当的吗？
What points does she contend in her argument? = 她在论点中提出了哪些论点？

否定句:
He does not contend that he is perfect. = 他并不认为自己完美无缺。
They did not contend with the difficulties effectively. = 他们没有有效地应对困难。


PHRASE->
contend with = 与…斗争；应付
contend for = 为…而竞争；争取
contend against = 与…竞争；对抗
contend that = 声称；主张
contend over = 为…而争论
