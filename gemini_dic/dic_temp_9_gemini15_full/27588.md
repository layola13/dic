canaster:/kəˈnæstər/| n. |烟草罐；(盛烟草的)篮子|A canaster is a container, typically a basket or a jar, used for storing tobacco.  The term often refers to a specific type of loosely packed, dark, strong-flavored tobacco. |Example: The old canaster sat on the mantelpiece, filled with fragrant tobacco.  古老的烟草罐放在壁炉台上，里面装满了芬芳的烟草。


The term originally referred to a type of large basket used for carrying goods. The word's association with tobacco likely stems from its use in transporting and storing this commodity.

近义词：basket, container, jar
反义词：无
词性：名词
