delegates:/ˈdɛləɡeɪts/| n. |代表；委托人|A delegate is a person chosen or elected to represent others.  They often represent a group of people at a meeting or conference.  |例句：The delegates from various countries met to discuss climate change. 各国的代表会面讨论气候变化。

例句：Our company sent three delegates to the trade show. 我们公司派了三位代表去参加贸易展销会。


近义词：representatives, envoys, emissaries|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee delegates responsibilities to its members. = 委员会将责任委托给其成员。
She delegates tasks efficiently to her team. = 她有效地将任务委托给她的团队。


一般过去时:
The manager delegated the project to his assistant. = 经理将项目委托给了他的助手。
They delegated the responsibility to a specialized team. = 他们将责任委托给了一个专门的团队。


现在完成时:
The CEO has delegated authority to several managers. = 首席执行官已将权力委托给几位经理。
She has already delegated most of her work. = 她已经委托了大部分工作。


过去完成时:
He had delegated the task before leaving for the conference. = 他在去参加会议之前已经委托了这项任务。
They had delegated the decision-making process to a subcommittee. = 他们已将决策过程委托给了一个小组委员会。


将来时:
The board will delegate the decision to the executive committee. = 董事会将把决定权委托给执行委员会。
She will delegate more tasks to her team next week. = 她下周将更多任务委托给她的团队。


虚拟语气:
If the president delegated more responsibility, things would run more smoothly. = 如果总统下放更多权力，事情就会运行得更顺利。
I wish he had delegated the difficult task to someone else. = 我希望他把这个困难的任务委托给别人。


被动语态:
The task was delegated to the most experienced employee. = 任务被委托给了经验最丰富的员工。
Responsibilities are delegated fairly within the organization. = 责任在组织内部得到公平的分配。


疑问句:
Who will be delegated to lead the project? = 谁将被委托领导这个项目？
To whom were the tasks delegated? = 任务委托给了谁？


否定句:
The manager didn't delegate enough tasks to his team. = 经理没有给他的团队分配足够的任务。
She refused to delegate any of her responsibilities. = 她拒绝委托任何她的责任。
