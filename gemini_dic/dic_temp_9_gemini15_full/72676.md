foaminesses:/ˈfoʊminɪsiz/ | n. |泡沫性；起泡的性质 (复数) |The plural form of "foaminess," referring to multiple instances or types of foaminess.  It describes the quality or state of being foamy or having many bubbles. |例句：The different foaminesses of the various beers were noted by the judges.
