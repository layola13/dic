semiprotected:/ˌsɛmiprəˈtɛktɪd/| adj.|半受保护的，部分保护的|Partially protected; affording some but not complete protection.  The term is often used in contexts like Wikipedia editing, where it describes pages that can be edited by registered users but not by anonymous users. |例句：The article is semiprotected to prevent vandalism. 这篇文章受到半保护以防止破坏。


例句：This page is semiprotected; only registered users can edit it.  这个页面是半保护的；只有注册用户才能编辑它。

近义词：partially protected, restricted, guarded


反义词：unprotected, fully protected, open

词性：形容词
