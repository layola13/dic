iniquitously:/ɪˈnɪkwɪtəsli/| adj.|邪恶地；不公正地；不正当地|Wickedly; unjustly; wrongly.  The company acted iniquitously by exploiting its workers.  |近义词：wickedly, unjustly, wrongly, nefariously, villainously |反义词：righteously, justly, fairly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He iniquitously manipulates the system. 他不正当地操纵系统。
They iniquitously disregard the law. 他们公然无视法律。


一般过去时:
The dictator ruled iniquitously. 独裁者暴虐统治。
The judge iniquitously favored the wealthy defendant. 法官不正当地偏袒富有的被告。


现在完成时:
They have iniquitously amassed a fortune. 他们不正当地积累了一大笔财富。
The corporation has iniquitously polluted the river. 这家公司不正当地污染了河流。


过去完成时:
He had iniquitously embezzled funds for years. 他多年来一直不正当地挪用公款。
The government had iniquitously suppressed dissent for decades. 政府几十年来一直不正当地压制异见。


将来时:
They will iniquitously exploit the new resource. 他们将不正当地利用这种新的资源。

虚拟语气:
If they acted iniquitously, they would face consequences. 如果他们行为不正当，他们将面临后果。
I wish he hadn't acted so iniquitously. 我真希望他不要那样做不正当的事。


被动语态:
The workers were iniquitously treated. 工人们受到了不公正的待遇。
The law was iniquitously applied. 法律被不正当地适用。


疑问句:
Did they act iniquitously? 他们是否行为不正当？

否定句:
He did not act iniquitously. 他没有行为不正当。
The company did not iniquitously exploit its workers. 这家公司没有不正当地剥削其工人。

词源:
From Old French iniquité, from Latin iniquitas, from iniquus "unequal, unjust," from in- "not" + aequus "equal."  The suffix "-ously" is an adverbial suffix indicating manner.
