immunization:/ˌɪmjuːnɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |免疫接种；免疫化|The process of making someone immune to an infectious disease, typically by vaccination.|例句：Immunization is crucial for protecting children from preventable diseases.  免疫接种对于保护儿童免受可预防疾病至关重要。

例句：The doctor recommended a series of immunizations for the baby.  医生建议给婴儿接种一系列疫苗。


近义词：vaccination, inoculation|反义词：susceptibility
