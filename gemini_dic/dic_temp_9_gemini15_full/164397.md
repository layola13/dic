preenact:/priːˈækt/| v.|预演，预先演出|To rehearse or practice something beforehand, especially a play or other performance.|例句：The actors spent weeks preening and pre-enacting their roles before the opening night.  演员们在开幕之夜前花了数周时间打扮和预演他们的角色。
例句：The students pre-enacted the historical event for their history class. 学生们为他们的历史课预演了这个历史事件。|近义词：rehearse, practice, simulate|反义词：improvise, wing it|动词
