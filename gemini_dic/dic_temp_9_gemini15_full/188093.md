roundelay:/ˈraʊndəleɪ/ | n. |轮唱；回旋歌；回环诗 | A roundelay is a song or poem with a repeating refrain or chorus, often with a cyclical structure where the beginning and end are similar.  It is characterized by its musicality and repetitive nature.  The form often involves the repetition of lines and the use of rhyme. | Example: The birds sang a beautiful roundelay at dawn.  黎明时分，鸟儿唱起了优美的轮唱。|近义词：refrain, chorus, rondeau |反义词：monologue, solo

词源:
The word "roundelay" comes from the Old French "rondel," which itself comes from the Old French "ronde," meaning "round."  This reflects the cyclical and repetitive nature of the song form. The "-lay" suffix indicates a song or poem.
