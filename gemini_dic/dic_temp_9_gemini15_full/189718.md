saltatoria:/ˌsæl.təˈtɔːri.ə/| n.|跳跃动物；跳跃类|The term "saltatoria" refers to a group of animals characterized by their ability to jump.  It's often used in zoological classifications, particularly for insects.  The name derives from the Latin word "saltare," meaning "to jump."|例句：Many insects in the order Orthoptera are saltatoria, showcasing their remarkable jumping abilities.  许多直翅目的昆虫是跳跃类动物，展现了它们非凡的跳跃能力。|近义词：jumping animals, jumpers|反义词：creeping animals, crawlers
