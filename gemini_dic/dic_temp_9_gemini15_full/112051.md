longbows:/ˈlɒŋˌboʊz/ | n. |长弓 (复数) | Longbows are powerful weapons used for hunting and warfare, characterized by their length and the significant power they generate.  They were particularly prevalent in medieval warfare. | Example:  The English longbows were instrumental in winning the Battle of Agincourt.  英格兰的长弓在阿金库尔战役中发挥了重要作用。  The museum displayed a collection of ancient longbows. 博物馆展出了一批古代长弓。 | 近义词：long bow (单数)| 反义词：short bow, crossbow |  词性: 名词
