dotard:/ˈdɑːtɑːrd/| n.|昏聩老人；老糊涂；衰老的人|An old person, especially one who is weak or senile.|The dotard king was easily manipulated by his advisors.  这位昏聩的国王很容易被他的顾问们操纵。|He was a dotard, rambling incoherently about the past. 他是个老糊涂，语无伦次地唠叨着过去的事情。|senile, dodderer, old fool|youthful, vigorous, sharp-witted|名词
