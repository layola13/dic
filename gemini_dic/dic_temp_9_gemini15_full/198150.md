shoppes:/ʃɑːps/| n.|商店（复数）|The plural of "shop," referring to a collection of shops, often in a specific location or with a particular theme.  It often implies a more upscale or specialized collection of retail establishments.|例句：The new shoppes at the waterfront are attracting many tourists.  新的滨水商店吸引了许多游客。|近义词：stores, boutiques, outlets|反义词：None
