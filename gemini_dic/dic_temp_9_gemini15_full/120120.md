mimics:/ˈmɪmɪks/| v.|模仿，模拟|To imitate or copy someone's behaviour, speech, or appearance, often playfully or satirically.  It can also refer to the act of a machine or system imitating a human process.|例句：The comedian mimics famous politicians.  喜剧演员模仿著名的政治家。

The software mimics the way the human brain processes information.  这个软件模拟人脑处理信息的方式。|近义词：imitates, copies, impersonates, mocks|反义词：originates, creates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mimics his teacher's accent. = 他模仿他老师的口音。
The parrot mimics human speech. = 那只鹦鹉模仿人类的说话。

一般过去时:
She mimicked her friend's laugh. = 她模仿了她朋友的笑声。
