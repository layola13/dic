scanner:/ˈskænər/ | n. |扫描仪，扫描器 |A device that scans documents or images to create a digital copy.  It can also refer to a person or thing that scans. |例句：The office uses a high-speed scanner to digitize all incoming documents. 办公室使用高速扫描仪来将所有传入的文档数字化。  The airport security scanner detected a suspicious object in my bag. 机场安检扫描仪在我的包里检测到一个可疑物品。|近义词：imager, reader |反义词：printer
