nonstick: /ˈnɒnstɪk/ | adj. | 不粘的；不沾的 |  Not sticking; having a surface to which substances will not adhere.  | 例句：This pan has a nonstick coating, making it easy to cook eggs.  这个平底锅有不粘涂层，煎鸡蛋很容易。

例句：The nonstick surface of the frying pan prevents food from sticking.  煎锅的不粘表面防止食物粘住。


近义词：nonadhesive,  stick-resistant

反义词：sticky, adhesive
