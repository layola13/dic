repulsion:/rɪˈpʌlʒən/ | n. | 厌恶；反感；排斥力 |  A strong feeling of disgust or dislike.  The force that acts between bodies to push them apart. |例句：The idea of eating insects filled her with repulsion.  吃昆虫的想法让她感到厌恶。

The magnetic repulsion between the two poles prevented them from touching.  两极之间的磁力排斥阻止了它们接触。 |近义词：disgust, abhorrence, aversion, loathing |反义词：attraction, appeal, fondness
