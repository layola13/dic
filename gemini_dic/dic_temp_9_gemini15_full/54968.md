disproportionable: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃənəbl/ | adj. |不成比例的，不相称的 |  Not proportionate; out of balance or harmony.  It implies a significant and noticeable lack of balance or equality.  The word often suggests a degree of unfairness or inappropriateness. |


例句：

The punishment was disproportionable to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。

The resources allocated to the project were disproportionable to its importance.  分配给该项目的资源与其重要性不成比例。


近义词：unbalanced, unequal, disproportionate, incommensurate, incongruous

反义词：proportionate, balanced, equal, commensurate


词性：形容词
