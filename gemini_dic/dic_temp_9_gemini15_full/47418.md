deadlocked:/ˈdedˌlɒkt/ /ˈdedˌlɑːkt/| adj.|僵持的；无法解决的；|adj.  Unable to proceed because of disagreement; at a standstill.|The negotiations are deadlocked.  谈判陷入了僵局。|The committee is deadlocked on the issue.  委员会在这个问题上僵持不下。|近义词：stalemated, gridlocked, at an impasse|反义词：resolved, settled, agreed upon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The negotiations are deadlocked. = 谈判处于僵持状态。
The two sides remain deadlocked. = 双方仍然僵持不下。

一般过去时:
The talks deadlocked after several hours of discussion. = 会谈在经过数小时的讨论后陷入僵局。
The project was deadlocked due to funding issues. = 项目因资金问题而陷入僵局。

现在完成时:
The talks have been deadlocked for weeks. = 会谈已经僵持了几周了。
The situation has remained deadlocked since last month. = 自上个月以来，情况一直僵持不下。

过去完成时:
The project had been deadlocked before a new team was assigned. = 在新团队接手之前，项目就已经陷入僵局了。
They had been deadlocked for days before reaching a compromise. = 他们在达成妥协之前已经僵持了几天。

将来时:
The negotiations will likely be deadlocked for some time. = 谈判很可能会僵持一段时间。
The meeting is expected to end deadlocked. = 预计会议将以僵局告终。

虚拟语气:
If the parties were not so entrenched, they wouldn't be deadlocked. = 如果各方立场没有那么强硬，他们就不会僵持不下。
I wish the situation wasn't deadlocked. = 我希望情况不要僵持下去。

被动语态:
The issue was deadlocked by opposing viewpoints. = 由于对立的观点，问题陷入了僵局。
The agreement was deadlocked because of disagreements on key points. = 由于在关键点上存在分歧，协议陷入了僵局。

疑问句:
Is the committee deadlocked? = 委员会是否陷入了僵局？
Are the negotiations currently deadlocked? = 谈判目前是否处于僵局？

否定句:
The negotiations are not deadlocked; they're making progress. = 谈判并没有僵持，而是取得了进展。
The project isn't deadlocked; they found a solution. = 项目并没有陷入僵局，他们找到了解决方案。


PHRASE->
be deadlocked on = 在...问题上僵持
remain deadlocked = 保持僵局
deadlocked in disagreement = 在分歧中僵持
break the deadlock =打破僵局
a deadlocked situation = 一个僵局
