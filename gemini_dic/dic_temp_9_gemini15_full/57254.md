downloaded:/ˈdaʊnˈloʊdəd/ | v. |下载的（过去分词） |已下载；已完成下载 |例句：I downloaded the file successfully. 我成功下载了文件。 |例句：The software has been downloaded over a million times. 这个软件已经被下载超过一百万次。|近义词：obtained, received |反义词：uploaded


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I download files every day. = 我每天下载文件。
They download music from various platforms. = 他们从各种平台下载音乐。

一般过去时:
I downloaded a new game yesterday. = 我昨天下载了一个新的游戏。
She downloaded the document before the meeting. = 她在会议前下载了文件。

现在完成时:
I have downloaded all the necessary files. = 我已经下载了所有必要的文件。
They have downloaded the latest version of the software. = 他们已经下载了软件的最新版本。

过去完成时:
I had downloaded the movie before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经下载好了电影。
She had downloaded the presentation before the conference started. = 会议开始前她已经下载好了演示文稿。

将来时:
I will download the update later. = 我稍后会下载更新。
He will download the pictures tomorrow. = 他明天会下载图片。

虚拟语气:
If I had downloaded the software earlier, I could have finished the project. = 如果我早点下载软件，我就能完成项目了。
I wish I had downloaded the map before starting the hike. = 我真希望在开始徒步旅行前下载了地图。

被动语态:
The file was downloaded automatically. = 文件已自动下载。
The updates are being downloaded in the background. = 更新正在后台下载。

疑问句:
Have you downloaded the attachment? = 你下载附件了吗？
Did they download the report? = 他们下载报告了吗？

否定句:
I didn't download the video. = 我没有下载视频。
He hasn't downloaded the photos yet. = 他还没下载照片。
