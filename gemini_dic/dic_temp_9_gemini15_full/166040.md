preplacing: /ˌpriːˈpleɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 预先放置；提前安置 |  The act of placing something in advance or beforehand.  It implies preparation and anticipation. | 例句：Preplacing the emergency supplies ensured everyone was ready for the storm. = 预先放置应急物资确保每个人都为暴风雨做好了准备。

例句：The team preplaced the markers along the racecourse. = 团队预先将标记放置在赛道上。


近义词：prepositioning, advance placement, advance positioning


反义词：postplacing (not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning)


词性:动词
