radicalised: /ˈrædɪkəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使激进化；使极端化 | To influence (someone) to adopt radical or extremist views.  |  The young man was radicalised online by extremist propaganda.  这个年轻人受到了网络上极端宣传的煽动而激进化了。 |  indoctrinate,  extremise |  moderate,  pacify | 动词
