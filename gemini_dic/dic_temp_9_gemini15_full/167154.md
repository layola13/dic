pretabulates: /ˌpriːˈtæbjuleɪts/ | vt. | 预先制表；预先列表 | To prepare (tables of figures, etc.) in advance.  This word is rarely used and is essentially a combination of "pre-" and "tabulates". It implies creating tables before they are needed. |  The research team pretabulated the data to save time during the presentation.  研究团队预先制表了数据，以便在演示过程中节省时间。


近义词：precompute, precalculate, prepare beforehand


反义词：post-tabulate (not a common word)


词性：动词
