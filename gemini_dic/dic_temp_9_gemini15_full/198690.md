sialogogue:/ˈsaɪəl əɡɔːɡ/| n. |唾液分泌剂；催涎剂|A substance that promotes the secretion of saliva.  Sialogogues are often used in medicine to stimulate saliva production, which can be helpful in treating dry mouth or other conditions. |Example:  Lemon juice acts as a sialogogue, increasing saliva flow.  柠檬汁是一种唾液分泌剂，可以增加唾液分泌。|近义词：sialagogue, salivating agent |反义词：antisialagogue
