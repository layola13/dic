paroles:/pəˈrɒlz/ | n. |歌词；证词 |The lyrics of a song, especially those that tell a story or convey a message.  Paroles can also refer to the words spoken by a witness in a legal proceeding.|例句：The paroles of the song were deeply moving. 歌曲的歌词非常感人。|例句：The police carefully recorded the paroles of the witness. 警方仔细记录了证人的证词。|近义词：lyrics, testimony, statement |反义词：music (in the context of the contrast between words and music)
