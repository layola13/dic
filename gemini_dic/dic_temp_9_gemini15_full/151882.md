passivization:/ˌpæsɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |被动化；被动句转换|The process of transforming an active sentence into a passive sentence.  This involves changing the subject and object of the sentence and using a form of the verb "to be" plus the past participle.  Passivization is a common grammatical process found in many languages.|The active sentence "The dog chased the cat" can be passivized to "The cat was chased by the dog".|Nominalization,  grammaticalization|Activization|名词
