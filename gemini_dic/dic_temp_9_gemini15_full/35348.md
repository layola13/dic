co-exist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/| v.|共存，同时存在|To exist at the same time or in the same place.|例句：Many different cultures co-exist peacefully in this city.  这个城市里多种不同的文化和平共处。|例句：The two species can co-exist in the same habitat. 这两种物种可以在相同的栖息地共存。|近义词：cohabit, coexist, live together|反义词：conflict, exclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Humans and animals co-exist in many environments. = 人类和动物在许多环境中共存。
Different belief systems co-exist in a pluralistic society. = 在多元化的社会中，不同的信仰体系共存。


一般过去时:
Dinosaurs and mammals co-existed millions of years ago. = 恐龙和哺乳动物在数百万年前就共存了。
The two companies co-existed in the market until one merged with another. = 两家公司在市场上共存，直到其中一家与另一家合并。


现在完成时:
These two cultures have co-existed for centuries. = 这两种文化已经共存了几个世纪。
The different factions have co-existed peacefully since the treaty. = 自从签订条约以来，不同的派系一直和平共存。


过去完成时:
Before the war, the two nations had co-existed relatively peacefully. = 在战争爆发之前，这两个国家曾相对和平地共存。
It was discovered that the two species had co-existed in the area for thousands of years. = 人们发现这两个物种在该地区已经共存了数千年。


将来时:
Hopefully, humans and AI will co-exist peacefully in the future. = 希望将来人类和人工智能能够和平共存。
The project aims to ensure that the new system will co-exist with the existing one. = 该项目旨在确保新系统能够与现有系统共存。


虚拟语气:
If only we could find a way to make all species co-exist harmoniously. = 如果我们能找到一种方法让所有物种和谐共存就好了。
It is essential that different groups co-exist with mutual respect. = 不同群体之间必须互相尊重，才能和平共存。


被动语态:
The different ideas were allowed to co-exist in the open forum. = 在公开论坛上，不同的想法被允许共存。
The new policy is designed to ensure that all stakeholders can co-exist. = 新政策旨在确保所有利益相关者都能共存。


疑问句:
Can these two species co-exist in the same ecological niche? = 这两种物种能在同一个生态位共存吗？
How can competing businesses co-exist in a saturated market? = 在饱和的市场中，竞争企业如何共存？


否定句:
The two factions refused to co-exist peacefully. = 这两个派系拒绝和平共存。
The company's new product failed to co-exist successfully with its older models. = 该公司的新品未能与其旧型号产品成功共存。
