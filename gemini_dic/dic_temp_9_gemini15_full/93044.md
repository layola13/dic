idling:/ˈaɪdlɪŋ/| v.|空转；闲置；无所事事|The engine was idling smoothly.  发动机运转平稳。|近义词：coasting, loafing, dawdling|反义词：working, functioning, operating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine idles smoothly. = 发动机空转平稳。
He idles away his time playing video games. = 他玩电子游戏消磨时间。


一般过去时:
The car idled at the traffic light. = 车子在红绿灯处空转。
She idled away the afternoon reading a book. = 她下午读了一本书来消磨时间。


现在完成时:
The engine has been idling for too long. = 发动机空转时间太长了。
He has idled away most of his youth. = 他虚度了大部分青春。


过去完成时:
The engine had been idling before it stalled. = 发动机在熄火前一直在空转。
She had idled away the entire morning before starting her work. = 在开始工作之前，她整个上午都在闲逛。


将来时:
The car will idle while we wait. = 我们等待的时候，汽车将空转。
He will idle away the summer holiday. = 他将虚度暑假。


虚拟语气:
If the engine idled any longer, it would overheat. = 如果发动机再空转下去，它就会过热。
I wish I hadn't idled away so much time. = 我真希望我没有浪费那么多时间。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Is the engine idling? = 发动机在空转吗？

否定句:

He doesn't idle away his time; he's very productive. = 他没有虚度光阴；他非常高效。
