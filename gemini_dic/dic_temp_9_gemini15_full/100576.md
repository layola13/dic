interzones:/ˈɪntərzoʊnz/| n.|中间地带，过渡区，缓冲区|An area between two distinct zones or regions, often characterized by a blend of cultural or geographical features.|例句：The demilitarized zone served as an interzone between the warring nations.  非军事区作为交战国之间的缓冲区。|例句：The cultural interzone between the city and the countryside was evident in the architecture. 城市和乡村之间的文化过渡区在建筑风格中很明显。|近义词：borderland, transition zone, buffer zone|反义词：core area, center
