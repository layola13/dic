nondissenting: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈsɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 不反对的；赞成的 | Not dissenting; agreeing.  The nondissenting members of the committee approved the proposal.  | 近义词：approving, consenting, concurring | 反义词：dissenting, objecting


英文解析：The word "nondissenting" is formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the word "dissenting" (meaning expressing or characterized by difference of opinion; disagreeing). Therefore, "nondissenting" signifies a lack of dissent or disagreement; it indicates agreement or approval.


例句：

The nondissenting shareholders voted in favor of the merger.  = 不反对的股东投票赞成合并。

A nondissenting opinion is rarely voiced in this highly controlled environment. = 在这个高度管控的环境中，很少有人表达不同意见。


词源:

* **non-**:  A prefix meaning "not," "without," or "opposite of."  Derived from Latin.
* **dissenting**: From the verb "dissent," meaning to differ in opinion, which originates from the Old French word "dissentir" and ultimately from the Latin "dissentire," composed of "dis-" (apart) and "sentire" (to feel, think).
