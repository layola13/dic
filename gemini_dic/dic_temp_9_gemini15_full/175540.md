ranchless:/ˈræntʃləs/| adj.|无牧场的；非牧场的|Lacking ranches or ranch-like characteristics.  It typically describes a landscape or lifestyle devoid of the features associated with ranching.|The city offered a ranchless existence, far removed from the open plains and cattle drives.|近义词：farm-free, non-ranching|反义词：ranch-filled, ranching

The area is ranchless. = 这个地区没有牧场。
This lifestyle is ranchless. = 这种生活方式与牧场无关。

The land was ranchless before the settlers arrived. = 定居者到来之前，这片土地没有牧场。

虚拟语气:
