profiles:/ˈproʊfaɪlz/| n.|个人简介，轮廓，侧面图 |A brief biographical sketch of a person.  It can also refer to a side view of something.| 例句：He wrote a profile of the famous actress. 他写了一篇关于这位著名女演员的个人简介。| 例句：The artist sketched the profile of the mountain.  艺术家速写了山峰的轮廓。|近义词：bio, sketch, outline, silhouette |反义词：full-face view
