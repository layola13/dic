furnished:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃt/ | adj. | 已装修的；已配备家具的 |  Describes a property or room that is equipped with furniture.  It implies that the space is ready to be lived in without needing to add furniture. | The apartment is furnished, so you can move in immediately.  这套公寓已装修好，你可以立即搬进去。 | equipped, supplied, appointed | unfurnished, bare


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The apartment is furnished with modern furniture. = 这套公寓配备了现代化的家具。
This room is furnished simply but comfortably. = 这个房间装修简单但舒适。


一般过去时:
They furnished the house before they moved in. = 他们搬进去之前把房子装修好了。
The hotel room was furnished with a double bed and a small desk. = 酒店房间配备了一张双人床和一张小桌子。


现在完成时:
We have furnished the new office. = 我们已经装修了新办公室。
She has furnished her apartment beautifully. = 她把公寓装修得很漂亮。


过去完成时:
They had furnished the apartment before the tenants arrived. = 租客到达之前，他们已经装修好了公寓。
The landlord had furnished the house with antique furniture. = 房东用古董家具装饰了房子。


将来时:
We will furnish the new house next month. = 我们下个月将装修新房子。
They will have furnished the apartment by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末前装修好公寓。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would furnish the house in a more modern style. = 如果我有钱，我会把房子装修成更现代的风格。
If only I had furnished the apartment before the party! = 要是我在聚会之前就把公寓装修好了就好了！


被动语态:
The room was furnished with tasteful antiques. = 房间里摆放着雅致的古董家具。
The house is being furnished for the new owners. = 这房子正在为新房主装修。


疑问句:
Is the apartment furnished? = 这套公寓有装修吗？
Are the rooms furnished to a high standard? = 房间的装修标准高吗？


否定句:
The house is not furnished. = 这房子没有装修。
The apartment wasn't furnished when we moved in. = 我们搬进去的时候，公寓还没有装修。
