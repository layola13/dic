This often happens in programming contexts where a variable is declared more than once within a scope.|Example:  The redeclaration of the variable 'x' caused a compiler error.  重新声明变量'x'导致编译器错误。|近义词：restatement, reiteration|反义词：initial declaration, first declaration|名词
