shout-out:/ˈʃaʊt aʊt/| n.|公开赞扬；感谢；宣传|A shout-out is a brief public expression of thanks, praise, or acknowledgement, often used in media like social media, radio, or television.  It's a way to give someone or something recognition.| Example: "I want to give a shout-out to my amazing team for their hard work." 我想对我的优秀团队的辛勤工作表示感谢。|近义词：acknowledgment, commendation, tribute|反义词：criticism, condemnation|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I give a shout-out to my fans every week. = 我每周都会向我的粉丝们表达感谢。
She gives shout-outs to local businesses on her podcast. = 她在播客里向当地企业表示感谢。


一般过去时:
He gave a shout-out to his mentor during his acceptance speech. = 他在获奖感言中向他的导师表示感谢。
They gave a shout-out to the volunteers on social media. = 他们在社交媒体上向志愿者们表示感谢。


现在完成时:
She has given shout-outs to several charities this year. = 她今年已经向几家慈善机构表示感谢了。
We have given shout-outs to all our sponsors. = 我们已经向我们所有的赞助商表示感谢了。


过去完成时:
He had given a shout-out to his family before he started his performance. = 他在开始表演之前已经向家人表示感谢了。
They had given shout-outs to many people before the event concluded. = 活动结束前，他们已经向许多人表示感谢了。


将来时:
I will give a shout-out to my collaborators in the credits. = 我将在片尾感谢我的合作者们。
They will give a shout-out to the winning team during the ceremony. = 他们在仪式上将向获胜团队表示祝贺。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would give a shout-out to everyone who helped me. = 如果我中了彩票，我会感谢所有帮助过我的人。
I wish I could give a shout-out to every single person who supported me. = 我希望能够感谢每一个支持我的人。


被动语态:
The band was given a shout-out by the radio DJ. = 乐队得到了电台DJ的感谢。
Many local artists were given shout-outs during the festival. = 许多当地艺术家在节日庆典中得到了感谢。


疑问句:
Will you give a shout-out to my business on your Instagram? = 你会在你的Instagram上感谢我的企业吗？
Did he give a shout-out to his parents? = 他感谢了他的父母吗？


否定句:
I didn't give a shout-out to anyone. = 我没有感谢任何人。
She didn't give a shout-out to her sponsor. = 她没有感谢她的赞助商。


PHRASE->
give a shout-out to = 向...表示感谢
a shout-out for = 对...的感谢
a special shout-out to = 特别感谢...
send a shout-out = 传达感谢
deserve a shout-out = 值得感谢
