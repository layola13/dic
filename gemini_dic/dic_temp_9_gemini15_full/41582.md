corporealist: /kɔːrˈpɔːriəlɪst/ | adj. | 肉体的；物质的；唯物主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of the physical body; materialistic.  | The corporealist view emphasizes the importance of physical experience over spiritual or mental aspects.  | 近义词：materialistic, physicalist, bodily | 反义词：spiritualist, idealist
