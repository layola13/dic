inseparability: /ˌɪnˌsɛpərəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可分割性，不可分离性 | The state or quality of being inseparable; the inability to be separated.  It refers to a close and unbreakable connection or bond between two or more things. | Example: The inseparability of their friendship was evident to everyone.  他们的友谊的不可分割性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
