examines:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnz/ | vt. | 检查；仔细观察；审视 | To inspect or study something carefully. |例句：The doctor examines the patient carefully. 医生仔细检查了病人。
The scientist examines the data before drawing conclusions. 科学家在得出结论之前仔细检查了数据。|近义词：inspect, investigate, scrutinize | 反义词：ignore, overlook | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He examines the evidence every day. 他每天都检查证据。
She examines the painting with a magnifying glass. 她用放大镜仔细检查画作。

一般过去时:
The detective examined the crime scene thoroughly. 侦探彻底检查了犯罪现场。
They examined the contract before signing it. 他们在签署合同之前仔细检查了合同。

现在完成时:
The professor has examined hundreds of students' papers. 教授已经检查了数百份学生的论文。
She has examined the problem from all angles. 她已经从各个角度研究了这个问题。

过去完成时:
The police had examined the suspect's alibi before arresting him. 警方在逮捕嫌犯之前已经调查了他的不在场证明。
He had examined the map carefully before starting his journey. 他在开始旅程之前仔细检查了地图。

将来时:
The judge will examine the witness tomorrow. 法官明天将传唤证人作证。
They will examine the proposal in detail next week. 他们下周将详细审查该提案。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would examine the situation more carefully. 如果我是你，我会更仔细地检查情况。
I wish I had examined the data more thoroughly before submitting the report. 我希望在提交报告之前能更彻底地检查数据。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
