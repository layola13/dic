microgram:/ˈmaɪkroʊɡræm/ | n. |微克 |A microgram is a unit of mass equal to one millionth of a gram (10⁻⁶ g).  It's a very small unit of measurement often used in science and medicine to measure very small amounts of substances. |The doctor prescribed a microgram of medication.  医生开了微克剂量的药。

The sample contained only a few micrograms of the rare element. 样本中只包含少量微克的稀有元素。|近义词:mcg (abbreviation)|反义词:kilogram, gram, milligram


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable.  Examples will focus on usage in sentences.


PHRASE->
a microgram of  一个微克的...
several micrograms  数个微克
trace amounts of micrograms 微量微克
detect micrograms 检测微克
measure micrograms 测量微克


WORD ORIGIN->
The word "microgram" is a combination of "micro-" (meaning one millionth) and "-gram" (the basic unit of mass in the metric system).  The prefix "micro-" comes from the Greek word "mikrós" meaning "small".
