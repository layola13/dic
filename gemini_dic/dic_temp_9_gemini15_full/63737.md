epic:/ˈɛpɪk/| adj., n. |史诗的；英雄的；大型的；史诗；长篇叙事诗 |  Describing something impressively large, long, or great; a long narrative poem celebrating the feats of a hero. |例句：The battle was an epic struggle.  这场战斗是一场史诗般的斗争。

例句：The Lord of the Rings is an epic fantasy novel.  《指环王》是一部长篇奇幻小说。

近义词：grand, monumental, heroic, massive, extensive

反义词：brief, short, insignificant, minor, small

词性：adj., n.


词源:  The word "epic" comes from the Greek word "epos" (ἔπος), meaning "word," "speech," or "story,"  specifically a long narrative poem.  This Greek word ultimately derives from a Proto-Indo-European root *wek- meaning "to speak."
