/drm/ | /ˌdiː.ɑːrˈɛm/ | n. | 数字版权管理 | Digital Rights Management; a collection of technologies used by copyright holders to control access to digital content.  DRM technologies are used to prevent unauthorized copying, distribution, or use of copyrighted material.  Examples include encryption, watermarking, and access control. |  This music file is protected by DRM, so you can't copy it. 这首音乐文件受数字版权管理保护，所以你不能复制它。   This e-book uses DRM to prevent unauthorized sharing. 这本电子书使用DRM防止未经授权的分享。 |  copyright protection, access control | open access, free distribution | 名词
