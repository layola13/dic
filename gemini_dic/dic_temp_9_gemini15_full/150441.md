pantagruelism:/ˌpænˈtæɡruəlɪzəm/| n. | 狂饮；放纵；贪食；过度放纵|Excessive indulgence in eating, drinking, or sensual pleasures; riotous living.  The term derives from Rabelais's novel *Pantagruel*, whose protagonist is known for his gargantuan appetite and hedonistic lifestyle.|The party was a scene of pure pantagruelism; guests consumed vast quantities of food and drink and engaged in boisterous revelry.|近义词：debauchery, revelry, gluttony, excess|反义词：asceticism, temperance, abstinence
