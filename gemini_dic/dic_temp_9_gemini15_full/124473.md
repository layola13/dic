mouille:/mwiːj/| n.|湿润；潮湿；沾湿|A mouille is a wet or damp area, often referring to a small patch of wetness.  It can also refer to something that has been wetted or dampened.|例句：The rain created a small mouille on the otherwise dry path. 雨水在原本干燥的小路上形成了一小片潮湿的地方。|近义词：dampness, wetness, moisture|反义词：dryness
