shout-outs:/ˈʃaʊtˌaʊts/ | n. |大声喊叫；公开感谢；致谢 | A shout-out is a brief, public acknowledgment or expression of gratitude, often made in a casual or informal setting.  It's a way to give someone a quick mention, show appreciation, or promote something. | Example:  "I want to give a shout-out to my team for their hard work." = 我想向我的团队致谢，感谢他们的辛勤工作。

| Example: "The DJ gave a shout-out to his favorite rapper." = DJ 对他最喜欢的说唱歌手表达了感谢。|近义词：acknowledgment, appreciation, thanks, mention |反义词：criticism, condemnation, disregard |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I give shout-outs to my friends on social media. = 我在社交媒体上向我的朋友们表达感谢。
He regularly gives shout-outs to local businesses. = 他定期向当地企业表达感谢。


一般过去时:
She gave a shout-out to her family during her acceptance speech. = 她在获奖感言中向家人表达了感谢。
The band gave a shout-out to their fans at the concert. = 乐队在音乐会上向他们的粉丝们表达了感谢。


现在完成时:
He has given shout-outs to many charities over the years. = 多年来，他一直向许多慈善机构表达感谢。
She has given shout-outs to several influencers on her podcast. = 她在播客中向几位有影响力的人表达了感谢。


过去完成时:
Before the show, the comedian had given a shout-out to his mentor. = 演出前，这位喜剧演员向他的导师表达了感谢。
They had given shout-outs to their sponsors in previous videos. = 他们在之前的视频中向他们的赞助商表达了感谢。


将来时:
I will give a shout-out to my teacher in my graduation speech. = 我将在毕业典礼上向我的老师表达感谢。
She will give a shout-out to her collaborators in the upcoming press release. = 她将在即将发布的新闻稿中向她的合作者们表达感谢。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would give a shout-out to everyone who helped me. = 如果我中了彩票，我会向所有帮助过我的人表达感谢。
I wish I could give a shout-out to every single person who supported me. = 我希望能够向每一个支持我的人表达感谢。


被动语态:
Many small businesses are given shout-outs by popular YouTubers. = 许多小型企业受到受欢迎的YouTuber的感谢。
His contribution was given a shout-out by the project leader. = 项目负责人对他做出的贡献表达了感谢。


疑问句:
Will you give me a shout-out on your Instagram? = 你会在你的Instagram上向我表达感谢吗？
Did she give a shout-out to anyone during her speech? = 她在演讲中向任何人表达感谢了吗？


否定句:
I didn't give a shout-out to anyone because I forgot. = 我因为忘记了所以没有向任何人表达感谢。
The host didn't give a shout-out to the event sponsors. = 主持人没有向活动的赞助商表达感谢。
