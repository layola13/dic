coxing:/ˈkɒksɪŋ/| v.|掌舵；指导（尤指赛艇）；驱策|The act of steering a boat, especially a racing shell, or of directing a team, especially a rowing crew.|例句：He was an expert at coxing the boat through the rough waters. 他擅长在汹涌的水域中掌舵。|例句：The coxswain's coxing was crucial to their victory.舵手的指挥对他们的胜利至关重要。|近义词：steering, guiding, directing|反义词：following, obeying
