redecaying:/ˌriːdɪˈkeɪɪŋ/| adj.| 重新腐烂的；再次腐烂的 |  Describes something that is decaying again after a period of apparent stability or having been previously treated to prevent decay.  It implies a renewed or continued process of decomposition. | Example: The redecaying timbers of the old house required immediate attention.  老房子的木头再次腐烂，需要立即处理。 | 近义词：rotting, decomposing, putrefying | 反义词：preserving, conserving,  restoring
