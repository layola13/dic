controllers:/kənˈtroʊlər/| n. |控制器，控制者|A controller is a person or thing that controls something.  It can refer to a person who manages or directs something, or a device that regulates a system or machine.|Example: The air traffic controller guided the plane to safety.  空管引导飞机安全着陆。|Example:  The game controller was broken. 游戏控制器坏了。|近义词：manager, regulator, operator, governor|反义词：follower, subordinate
