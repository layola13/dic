nonconfidentiality:/ˌnɒnkɒnfɪdɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | n. |非机密性，不保密 |The state or quality of not being confidential; the lack of confidentiality.  This often refers to information that is not considered private or secret and can be openly shared. |例句：The nonconfidentiality of the meeting minutes meant everyone in the company knew the outcome.  会议纪要的非机密性意味着公司里的每个人都知道结果了。
