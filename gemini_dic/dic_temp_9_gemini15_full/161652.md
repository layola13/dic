pounders:/ˈpaʊndərz/| n.|磅重物；重击者；（尤指）重型武器|A pounder is something that weighs a pound or a person or thing that hits hard, especially a heavy weapon.|例句：He used a ten-pounder to crush the rock. 他用一个十磅重的锤子砸碎了石头。|近义词：hammer, hitter, batter|反义词：lightweight, featherweight

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine pounds the metal sheets. = 机器敲打金属板。
He pounds the nails into the wood. = 他把钉子钉进木头里。


一般过去时:
The blacksmith pounded the iron until it was red hot. = 铁匠反复捶打铁块直到它变红。
She pounded the table in anger. = 她生气地捶桌子。


现在完成时:
They have pounded the pavement for weeks looking for a job. = 他们已经找工作找了好几个星期了。
The rain has pounded the roof all night. = 雨整夜都在猛烈地敲打屋顶。


过去完成时:
He had pounded the concrete for hours before he took a break. = 他在休息之前已经敲打混凝土几个小时了。
The waves had pounded the shore all night. = 海浪整夜都在冲击着海岸。


将来时:

The storm will pound the coast with heavy rain and wind. = 暴风雨将伴随着大雨和强风袭击海岸。


虚拟语气:
