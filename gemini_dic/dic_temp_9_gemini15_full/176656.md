reachable: /ˈriːtʃəbl/ | adj. | 可到达的；可联系到的；可实现的 |

英文解析:Able to be reached or contacted; attainable or achievable.  It can refer to physical locations, people, or goals.

例句:
The island is reachable only by boat. = 这个岛屿只能乘船到达。

The doctor is reachable by phone after hours. =  医生下班后可以通过电话联系到。


近义词: accessible, attainable, achievable, available

反义词: inaccessible, unattainable, unreachable, unavailable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum is reachable by bus. = 博物馆可以乘公共汽车到达。
This information is reachable online. = 这些信息可以在网上获取。


一般过去时:
The summit was reachable only after many hours of climbing. = 只有在攀登数小时后才能到达山顶。
He was reachable by phone until late evening. = 他直到深夜都可以通过电话联系到。


现在完成时:
The goal has finally become reachable. = 目标终于可以实现了。
The support team has been reachable throughout the process. = 在整个过程中，支持团队一直可以联系到。


过去完成时:
The island had been reachable by boat before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，这个岛屿可以通过船只到达。
The manager had been reachable only by email prior to the meeting. = 会议之前，只能通过电子邮件联系到经理。


将来时:
The new village will be reachable by train next year. = 明年将可以通过火车到达这个新村庄。
The customer service team will be reachable 24/7. = 客服团队将全天候提供服务。


虚拟语气:
If the summit were reachable in one day, many more people would try to climb it. = 如果山顶一天内就能到达，更多的人会尝试攀登。
If the information were reachable more easily, more people would use it. = 如果信息更容易获取，更多的人会使用它。


被动语态:
The remote village is reachable only by helicopter. = 偏远的村庄只能通过直升机到达。
He was easily reachable by email. = 他很容易通过电子邮件联系到。


疑问句:
Is the help desk reachable at this time? = 现在可以联系到技术支持部门吗？
Is the information reachable through the company's website? = 可以通过公司网站获取信息吗？


否定句:
The house is not reachable by car. = 这栋房子无法开车到达。
He was not reachable by phone yesterday. = 昨天无法通过电话联系到他。
