hookworms:/ˈhʊkˌwɜːrmz/| n.|钩虫|Parasitic nematodes that attach to the intestinal wall of their host, often causing anemia and other health problems.  They are typically transmitted through contaminated soil.|例句：Hookworms are a common problem in areas with poor sanitation. 钩虫在卫生条件差的地区很常见。|例句：The doctor diagnosed the patient with a severe hookworm infection.医生诊断病人患有严重的钩虫感染。|近义词：parasites, nematodes|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与健康状态相关的词)|名词
