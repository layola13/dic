horseplayful: /ˈhɔːrspleɪfʊl/ | adj. |嬉戏的，顽皮的，爱闹的 | Playful in a boisterous or mischievous way, like a horse.  This word blends the noun "horseplay" with the adjective "playful," creating a more intense and perhaps slightly rougher connotation of playful behavior.  It suggests a level of energy and activity exceeding simple playfulness. | The children were horseplayful in the park, running and shouting.孩子们在公园里嬉戏打闹。The horseplayful puppy knocked over the lamp.这只爱闹的小狗打翻了灯。|近义词：mischievous, boisterous, rowdy, rambunctious |反义词：serious, subdued, quiet, calm
