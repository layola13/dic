cultivators:/ˈkʌltɪveɪtərz/| n. |耕作者；栽培者；培养者 |A cultivator is a person who cultivates land or plants.  They work the earth to grow crops or raise plants.  |例句：The cultivators worked tirelessly from sunrise to sunset.  耕作者们从日出到日落辛勤劳作。|例句：Experienced cultivators can identify the best soil for different crops.  经验丰富的耕作者能够辨别不同作物最合适的土壤。|近义词：farmers, growers, planters |反义词：consumers (in the context of agricultural production)
