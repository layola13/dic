expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/ | v. | 到期；失效；死亡；终止 | To come to an end; to cease to be valid; to die. |例句：My passport expires next month. 我的护照下个月到期。
The offer expires at midnight.  这个优惠活动在午夜截止。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My subscription expires next week. = 我的订阅下周到期。
The contract expires on December 31st. = 合同在12月31日到期。

一般过去时:
The milk expired yesterday. = 牛奶昨天过期了。
His driving license expired last year. = 他的驾照去年过期了。

现在完成时:
My visa has expired. = 我的签证已经过期了。
The warranty has expired on my new phone. = 我的新手机保修期已过。

过去完成时:
The medicine had expired before I could use it. = 我还没来得及用药就过期了。
The deadline had expired before he submitted his work. = 他提交作业之前截止日期就已经过了。

将来时:
The project will expire in six months. = 项目将在六个月后结束。
Your membership will expire at the end of the year. = 您的会员资格将在年底到期。

虚拟语气:
If the contract had expired, we would have renegotiated it. = 如果合同到期了，我们会重新谈判。
I wish my worries would expire soon. = 我希望我的担忧很快就能结束。

被动语态:
The agreement was expired by mutual consent. = 协议已由双方同意终止。
The product was expired and removed from the shelves. = 产品已过期并从货架上移除。

疑问句:
When does your driver's license expire? = 你的驾照什么时候到期？
Does this product expire quickly? = 这个产品容易过期吗？

否定句:
The warranty doesn't expire for another year. = 保修期还有一年才到期。
My membership hasn't expired yet. = 我的会员资格还没过期。


PHRASE->
expire a visa = 签证到期
expire a license = 执照过期
expire a contract = 合同到期
expire a patent = 专利过期
near expiration date = 临近过期日期


近义词：end, terminate, finish, cease, die
反义词：begin, start, renew, extend
词性:动词
