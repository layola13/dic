mettle:/ˈmɛtəl/ | n. | 勇气；精神；韧性；气质 | Courage, strength of character, and determination; the ability to cope with difficulties;  the quality of a person's character or the material something is made of. |例句：The soldiers showed their mettle in the face of danger.  士兵们在面对危险时展现了他们的勇气。

The marathon runner showed true mettle by finishing the race despite an injury.  这位马拉松运动员尽管受伤，却坚持完成了比赛，展现了真正的韧性。|近义词：courage, fortitude, spirit, grit, stamina |反义词：cowardice, timidity, weakness
