griller:/ˈɡrɪlər/ | n. |烧烤架；烧烤者；（尤指）街头小贩 | A griller is a person or device that grills food.  It can refer to a person who cooks food on a grill, or the grill itself, especially a portable one used by street vendors. |例句：He bought a new gas griller for his backyard. 他为他的后院买了一个新的燃气烧烤架。

例句：The street griller sold delicious kebabs. 街头烧烤摊卖美味的烤肉串。

近义词：grill (n.), barbecue (n.), broiler (n.) |反义词：None specifically.  |词性:名词
