overimmunize: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmjʊnaɪz/ | vt. |过度免疫；过度免疫接种| To provide excessive immunization to (a person or animal).  This can lead to undesirable side effects or a weakened immune response in the long term. | 例句：Overimmunizing a child can weaken their immune system and make them more susceptible to illness.  过度免疫接种可能会削弱孩子的免疫系统，使他们更容易生病。


例句：The doctor warned against overimmunizing the patient, suggesting a more tailored approach. 医生警告不要对病人进行过度免疫，建议采取更量身定制的方法。


近义词：overvaccinate, hyperimmunize
