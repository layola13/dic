Word Origin:

The word "interweavement" is formed from the prefix "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among"), the verb "weave" (meaning to interlace threads), and the suffix "-ment" (forming nouns of action or state).  The word's roots ultimately trace back to Proto-Germanic and Proto-Indo-European roots related to weaving and twisting.
