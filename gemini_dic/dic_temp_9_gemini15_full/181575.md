relatability: /ˌrɛləˈteɪbɪləti/ | n. | 可信度；相关性；易于产生共鸣 | The quality of being relatable; the extent to which something is easy to understand and relate to.  Relatability often stems from shared experiences, emotions, or perspectives.  It's a key element in effective communication and storytelling. |  The relatability of the character made the movie so successful. 这部电影的人物角色非常贴近生活，所以获得了巨大的成功。 The relatability of her struggles resonated with many viewers. 她奋斗经历的真实性引起了许多观众的共鸣。 |  identification, connection, empathy, sympathy |  unrelatedness, disconnection, alienation, indifference | noun
