portable:/ˈpɔːrtəbl/ | adj. | 便携式的，轻便的 | Easily carried or moved.  Something that is portable is designed to be easily moved or carried. |  例句：This laptop is very portable; I can take it anywhere. 这台笔记本电脑非常轻便；我可以随身携带。

例句：My new portable speaker delivers great sound. 我的新便携式音箱音质很棒。


近义词：movable, transportable, hand-held


反义词：stationary, fixed, immobile


词源：From Old French *portable, from Latin portabilis, from portare "to carry".
