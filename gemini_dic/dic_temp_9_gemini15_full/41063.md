corporation: /ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 公司，法人 | A large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity (legally a person) and recognized as such in law.  Corporations are typically owned by shareholders. | Example:  Many large corporations are headquartered in New York City.  许多大型公司都将总部设在纽约市。 | 近义词：company, firm, business, enterprise | 反义词：sole proprietorship, partnership
