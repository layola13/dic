praxeological:/ˌpræksiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.| 实践的；实践论的 | Relating to or characteristic of praxeology, the study of human action.  It emphasizes the subjective nature of human choices and the logical implications of those choices. | Praxeological analysis often focuses on the unintended consequences of actions. |近义词：practical, applied, action-oriented |反义词：theoretical, abstract, impractical
