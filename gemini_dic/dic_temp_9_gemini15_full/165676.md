premonopolizing: /ˌpriːməˈnɒpəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先垄断的；在垄断之前 |  Describing something that is done before a monopoly is established, often with the intention of creating or securing that monopoly.  | The premonopolizing tactics of the company involved buying up all available resources. = 这家公司在垄断之前采取的策略包括收购所有可用资源。  | monopolizing, preemptive | non-monopolizing |
