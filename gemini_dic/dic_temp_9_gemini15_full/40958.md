cooperative: /koʊˈɒpərətɪv/ | adj., n. |合作的；合作社；合作企业 |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "cooperative" describes something or someone that is willing to be helpful by working together with others. As a noun, it refers to a business organization owned and operated by its members, who share in the profits or benefits.

例句:
The students were very cooperative during the group project.  = 学生们在小组项目中非常合作。

The housing cooperative provides affordable homes for its members. = 这家住房合作社为其成员提供经济适用房。


近义词:
collaborative, joint, united, helpful, accommodating


反义词:
uncooperative, unhelpful, antagonistic, obstructive


词性:
adjective, noun
