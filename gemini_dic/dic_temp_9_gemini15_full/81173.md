grandest:/ˈɡrændɪst/| adj.|最伟大的；最壮丽的；最宏伟的|The superlative form of "grand," indicating the highest degree of grandeur, magnificence, or splendor.  It describes something that surpasses others in scale, importance, or impressiveness.|例句：The grandest castle in the kingdom stood proudly on the hill. 王国最宏伟的城堡骄傲地耸立在山上。

例句：His grandest achievement was the completion of the symphony. 他最伟大的成就是完成了这首交响乐。


近义词：most magnificent, most impressive, most splendid, most sublime

反义词：least impressive, most insignificant, most humble

词性:形容词
