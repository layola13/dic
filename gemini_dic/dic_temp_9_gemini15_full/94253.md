impermanence:/ɪmˈpɜːrmənəns/| n. |无常；短暂；变幻无常|The inherent lack of permanence; the quality or state of being impermanent.  It emphasizes the transient nature of things, highlighting that nothing lasts forever.  This concept is central to many philosophies, particularly Buddhism.|例句：The impermanence of life is a universal truth.  生命的无常是一个普遍的真理。

例句：He contemplated the impermanence of earthly possessions. 他沉思着尘世财产的短暂。


近义词：transitoriness, ephemerality, fleetingness, evanescence|反义词：permanence, eternity, perpetuity|词性:名词
