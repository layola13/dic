consolatorily:/kənˈsɒlətərili/ | adj. | 安慰地；慰藉地 | In a way that offers comfort or consolation.  It describes an action or manner that provides solace or relief from grief, distress, or disappointment. |例句：She spoke consolatorily to the grieving widow. 她安慰地对悲伤的寡妇说道。

例句：He patted her shoulder consolatorily. 他安慰地拍了拍她的肩膀。


近义词：comfortingly, soothingly, sympathetically

反义词：cruelly, harshly, unsympathetically


词源:  Derived from the word "consolation," which comes from the Latin "consolatio," meaning "comfort, solace." The suffix "-ly" transforms the noun into an adverb, indicating a manner or way of acting.
