puntello: /punˈtɛloʊ/ | n. |支撑物；小支柱；临时支撑物 | A small prop or support, typically of wood, used to hold something up temporarily.  |例句：The artist used a puntello to support the canvas while he painted.  艺术家在作画时用了一个支撑物来支撑画布。

The painter used a puntello to keep the wet paint from sagging. 画家用一个支撑物来防止湿颜料下垂。


近义词：support, prop, stay, brace | 反义词：weight, burden


词源：源于意大利语“puntello”，意为“小支柱”。  It ultimately derives from the Italian word *punta*, meaning "point" or "tip," referring to the pointed end of the support.
