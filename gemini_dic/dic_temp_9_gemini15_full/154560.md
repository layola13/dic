perquisition:/ˌpɜːkwɪˈzɪʃən/| n.|搜查；查抄；调查|The act of searching or examining something thoroughly, especially a house or other premises, typically by the police or other officials.  It often implies a forceful or intrusive search.|例句：The police conducted a perquisition of the suspect's apartment. =警方对嫌疑人的公寓进行了搜查。|例句：The judge authorized a perquisition of the company's records. =法官授权对该公司记录进行查阅。|近义词：search, seizure, investigation, inquiry, raid|反义词：release, relinquish
