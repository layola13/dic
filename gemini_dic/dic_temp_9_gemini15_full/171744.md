pumps:/pʌmps/| n. vt.|泵；抽水机；(鞋)高跟鞋；用力地抽吸；|A pump is a mechanical device used to move liquids, gases, or other fluids.  It can also refer to a type of shoe. As a verb, it means to move something forcefully.|例句：The farmer used a pump to water his crops. 农民用泵来灌溉庄稼。

The new pumps are really stylish. 这双新高跟鞋非常时尚。


近义词：machine, footwear,  (vt.) propel, force|反义词： (n.)  none; (vt.)  relax, soothe|词性:n. vt.
