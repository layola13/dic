postponements:/pəʊstˈpəʊnmənts/| n. |延期；推迟 |The act of postponing something to a later time.  Postponements are often necessary due to unforeseen circumstances or scheduling conflicts.|例句：Due to the storm, there were several postponements of the outdoor concerts. 由于暴风雨，户外音乐会延期了好几次。

例句：The numerous postponements frustrated the project team.  无数次的延期让项目团队感到沮丧。|近义词：deferrals, delays, adjournments |反义词：advancements, accelerations |名词
