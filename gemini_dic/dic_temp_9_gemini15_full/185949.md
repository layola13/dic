rew:/ruː/| v.|重写，改写|To write something again, often in a different or improved way.|例句：I had to rewrite the entire essay. 我不得不重写整篇文章。|例句：The author rewrote the ending to make it more satisfying. 作者改写了结局使其更令人满意。|近义词：redraft, revise, edit|反义词：write (initially)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewrites his poems frequently. = 他经常重写他的诗歌。
She rewrites her emails before sending them. = 她发送邮件前会重写邮件。


一般过去时:
I rewrote the report yesterday. = 我昨天重写了报告。
They rewrote the script after the first rehearsal. = 他们第一次排练后重写了剧本。


现在完成时:
He has rewritten the book three times. = 他已经重写了这本书三次。
She has rewritten her application multiple times. = 她已经多次重写了她的申请。


过去完成时:
I had rewritten the letter before she called. = 在她打电话之前，我已经重写了信。
They had rewritten the contract before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经重写了合同。


将来时:
I will rewrite the essay tomorrow. = 我明天将重写这篇文章。
She will rewrite the song with a new melody. = 她将用新的旋律重写这首歌。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rewrite the whole chapter. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重写整章。
I wish I had rewritten that email. = 我真希望我重写了那封邮件。


被动语态:
The document was rewritten by the editor. = 这份文件被编辑重写了。
The song has been rewritten several times. = 这首歌已经被重写了好几次。


疑问句:
Will you rewrite the proposal? = 你会重写提案吗？
Has he rewritten the article yet? = 他已经重写那篇文章了吗？


否定句:
I didn't rewrite the assignment. = 我没有重写作业。
She hasn't rewritten her essay yet. = 她还没有重写她的文章。


PHRASE->
rewrite history = 改写历史
