overfeed: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːd/ | vt. | 喂养过多；过度喂食 | To give (someone or something) too much food.  |例句：Don't overfeed the baby; it's not good for their health. 不要喂婴儿太多食物；这对他们的健康不好。

例句：The farmer overfed his cattle, leading to health problems.  这个农民喂牛太多，导致了健康问题。


近义词：overindulge, glut, cram


反义词：underfeed, starve


词性：动词
