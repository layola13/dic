hailstorms:/ˈheɪlstɔːrmz/| n. |冰雹风暴|A hailstorm is a storm with hail. Hail is precipitation in the form of balls or irregular lumps of ice, which is produced by intense thunderstorms.|The hailstorm caused significant damage to the crops. 冰雹风暴对庄稼造成了严重的破坏。  The children ran inside to escape the fierce hailstorm.孩子们跑到屋里躲避猛烈的冰雹风暴。|severe thunderstorm, ice storm|calm, sunshine|名词
