词源:
The word "recognitional" is derived from the verb "recognize," which comes from the Old French word "reconoistre," meaning "to recognize, acknowledge."  The suffix "-ional"  indicates something relating to or characterized by the action or process.  Therefore, "recognitional" signifies something connected to the act of recognizing.
