evolve:/ɪˈvɒlv/ /ɪˈvɑːlv/| v.|进化；发展；逐渐形成|To develop gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form.|The company has evolved into a major player in the industry. 这家公司已经发展成为该行业的主要参与者。|develop, progress, unfold|deteriorate, regress, decline|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Life evolves constantly. = 生命在不断进化。
The situation is evolving rapidly. = 情况正在迅速发展。


一般过去时:
The species evolved over millions of years. = 这个物种进化了数百万年。
His ideas evolved throughout his career. = 他的思想在他的职业生涯中不断发展。


现在完成时:
Humanity has evolved significantly. = 人类已经进化得非常显著。
The technology has evolved considerably since then. = 从那时起，这项技术已经有了很大的发展。


过去完成时:
The language had evolved from older forms. = 这种语言是从更古老的形式演变而来的。
Before the war, the country's political system had evolved slowly. = 在战争之前，这个国家的政治制度发展缓慢。


将来时:
The technology will continue to evolve. = 技术将继续发展。
We believe the market will evolve in a positive direction. = 我们相信市场将朝着积极的方向发展。


虚拟语气:
If the species had evolved differently, the world would be a different place. = 如果物种进化不同，世界将会是不同的样子。
I wish the situation had evolved more smoothly. = 我希望情况发展得更顺利一些。


被动语态:
The theory was evolved by Darwin. = 达尔文创立了这个理论。(被动语态，强调理论被创立)
New strategies are constantly being evolved to improve efficiency. = 不断发展新的策略以提高效率。


疑问句:
How did the universe evolve? = 宇宙是如何演化的？
What factors contributed to the evolution of this species? = 哪些因素促成了这个物种的进化？


否定句:
The plan did not evolve as expected. = 计划并未按预期发展。
The company hasn't evolved its marketing strategy recently. = 公司最近没有改进其营销策略。


PHRASE->
evolve from = 从...进化而来
evolve into = 进化成
evolve a plan = 制定一个计划
evolve a theory = 发展出一个理论
evolve a strategy = 制定策略
