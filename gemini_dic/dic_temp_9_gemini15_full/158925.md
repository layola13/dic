pluralist:/ˈplʊərəlɪst/| adj. n.|多元论者；多元主义的|Adjective:  Relating to or supporting pluralism; believing in or practicing the acceptance of many different viewpoints or systems of belief.  Noun: A person who believes in or practices pluralism.|例句：The university boasts a pluralist approach to education, embracing diverse perspectives and methodologies. 这所大学以多元化的教育方法为荣，包含各种不同的观点和方法。|近义词：inclusive, eclectic, tolerant|反义词：dogmatic, authoritarian, monolithic
