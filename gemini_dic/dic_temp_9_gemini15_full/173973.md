quasi-stationary:/ˈkwɑːziˌsteɪʃənəri/| adj.|准静态的；近似静止的|Approaching or being in a state of equilibrium; almost stationary, but with slow, gradual changes.|The population remained quasi-stationary for several years before experiencing a surge.|近义词：nearly stationary, almost static|反义词：dynamic, rapidly changing|
