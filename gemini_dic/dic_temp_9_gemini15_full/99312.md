intercreating:/ˌɪntərˈkriːeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 互相创造，共同创造 |  The term "intercreating" describes a process where two or more entities collaboratively create something new. It emphasizes the mutual and interactive nature of the creative process, highlighting the contributions of all participants.  This contrasts with a situation where one entity creates something independently. | Example: The artists were intercreating a mural, each contributing their unique style and vision.  艺术家们正在共同创作一幅壁画，每个人都贡献了他们独特的风格和视野。 | 近义词：co-creating, collaborating, synergizing | 反义词：creating independently, working in isolation
