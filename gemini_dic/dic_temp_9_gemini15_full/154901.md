pesar:/peˈsar/| v.| 称重；衡量；悲伤；惋惜 | to determine the weight of something; to consider something carefully; to feel sorrow or regret | 例句：The butcher weighed the meat carefully. (屠夫仔细地称了肉的重量。)  I weighed the pros and cons before making a decision. (我在做决定之前权衡了利弊。)


近义词：weigh, measure, assess, grieve, lament | 反义词：rejoice, celebrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He weighs the package every day. = 他每天称包裹的重量。
She weighs the options carefully before making a decision. = 她做决定前仔细权衡各种选择。


一般过去时:
They weighed the flour before baking the cake. = 他们烘焙蛋糕之前称了面粉的重量。
He weighed his words carefully before speaking. = 他说话前仔细斟酌了词语。


现在完成时:
I have weighed myself this morning. = 我今天早上称了体重。
She has weighed the consequences of her actions. = 她已经权衡了她行为的后果。


过去完成时:
He had weighed the evidence before reaching a verdict. = 他在作出判决之前已经权衡了证据。
She had weighed the pros and cons before accepting the offer. = 在接受这个提议之前，她已经权衡了利弊。


将来时:
They will weigh the goods at the port. = 他们将在港口称重货物。
I will weigh my options before applying for the job. = 我会在申请这份工作之前权衡我的选择。


虚拟语气:
If I weighed less, I could run faster. = 如果我的体重轻一些，我就能跑得更快。
I wish I had weighed the risks more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地权衡了风险。


被动语态:
The package was weighed at the post office. = 包裹在邮局称重了。
The evidence was carefully weighed by the judge. = 证据被法官仔细地权衡了。


疑问句:
How much does the package weigh? = 这个包裹有多重？
Have you weighed the consequences of your actions? = 你是否已经权衡了你行为的后果？


否定句:
He didn't weigh the ingredients accurately. = 他没有精确地称量配料。
She didn't weigh the risks involved. = 她没有权衡所涉及的风险。


PHRASE->
weigh in = 加入讨论；发表意见
weigh down = 使沮丧；压垮
weigh on = 使担忧；使烦恼
weigh up = 仔细衡量；权衡
to weigh one's words = 谨慎措辞


词源:
源自古英语 *wēgan，意为“to carry, move”。  其含义随着时间的推移演变为“to determine the weight of”。
