catchdrain: /ˈkætʃdreɪn/ | n. | 集水沟，排水沟 | A catchdrain is a ditch or channel designed to collect and carry away surface water, preventing flooding or erosion.  It's often found in landscaping, road construction, and other areas where water needs to be managed effectively. | Example: The catchdrain efficiently diverted rainwater away from the building's foundation.  集水沟有效地将雨水从建筑物的地基引流开来。  The new catchdrain system significantly reduced flooding in the low-lying area. 新建的集水沟系统大大减少了低洼地区的洪水。| 近义词：drain, ditch, gutter | 反义词： dam, reservoir | 名词
