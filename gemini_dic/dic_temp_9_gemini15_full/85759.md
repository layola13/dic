hawthorn: /ˈhɔːθɔːrn/ /ˈhɔːθɔrn/ | n. | 山楂树；山楂果 |  Hawthorn is a thorny shrub or small tree of the rose family, bearing white or pink flowers and small, usually red berries.  The berries are used in jams, jellies, and other food products. The wood is sometimes used for carving. | The hawthorn hedge was in full bloom.  山楂树篱笆盛开着。 |  The hawthorn berries are ripe now.  山楂果现在成熟了。| blackthorn, thornapple |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 non-thorny plants) | noun
