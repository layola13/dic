disqualify:/dɪsˈkwɑːlɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 取消…的资格；使…不合格 | To declare (someone) ineligible to compete or participate.  |例句：He was disqualified from the race for using illegal drugs. 他因为使用违禁药物而被取消比赛资格。|例句：The judge disqualified the evidence because it was obtained illegally. 因为证据是非法获得的，法官将其取消资格。|近义词：bar, debar, invalidate |反义词：qualify, enable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The referee disqualifies players who break the rules. = 裁判取消违反规则的球员的资格。
This evidence disqualifies the defendant's claim. = 这证据使被告的声明无效。

一般过去时:
The athlete was disqualified for doping. = 那位运动员因服用兴奋剂而被取消资格。
The judge disqualified the witness's testimony. = 法官认定证人的证词无效。

现在完成时:
They have disqualified him from future competitions. = 他们已经取消了他参加未来比赛的资格。
The court has disqualified the contract due to fraud. = 法院因欺诈行为而宣布合同无效。

过去完成时:
The team had been disqualified before the final match. = 该队在决赛前已被取消资格。
The evidence had already been disqualified by the time the trial started. = 审判开始时，证据已经被认定无效。

将来时:
The committee will disqualify any candidates with a criminal record. = 委员会将取消任何有犯罪记录的候选人的资格。
The court will likely disqualify the evidence presented. = 法院很可能会认定提交的证据无效。

虚拟语气:
If he had followed the rules, he wouldn't have been disqualified. = 如果他遵守规则，他就不会被取消资格。
If the evidence were admissible, it wouldn't have been disqualified. = 如果证据可采，它就不会被认定无效。

被动语态:
The runner was disqualified for tripping another competitor. = 那名跑步者因绊倒另一名竞争对手而被取消资格。
The results were disqualified due to procedural errors. = 由于程序错误，结果被认定无效。

疑问句:
Will he be disqualified for his actions? = 他会因为他的行为而被取消资格吗？
Was the evidence disqualified because of its unreliability? = 证据是因为不可靠而被认定无效的吗？

否定句:
The athlete was not disqualified despite the protests. = 尽管有抗议，运动员并没有被取消资格。
The judge did not disqualify the evidence, deeming it relevant. = 法官没有认定证据无效，认为它具有相关性。
