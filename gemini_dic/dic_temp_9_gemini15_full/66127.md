except-that:/ɪkˈsɛpt ðæt/| conj.|除...之外；只是...|This phrase introduces a clause that modifies or contrasts with a preceding statement, indicating an exception or qualification. It essentially means "with the exception of the fact that..." or "but for the fact that...".|例句：I agree with everything you said, except that I think the timing is off. 我同意你说的所有话，只是我认为时机不对。

例句：The plan is perfect except that it lacks funding. 这个计划很完美，只是缺乏资金。


近义词：but, however, save that, barring that|反义词：including, besides, in addition to|词性：连词
