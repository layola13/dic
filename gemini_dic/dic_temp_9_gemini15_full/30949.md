chairwomen:/ˈtʃɛrwʊmən/ | n. | 女主席；女会长 | A chairwoman is a woman who presides over a meeting or organization.  It's the feminine equivalent of chairman.  The term is gradually being replaced by chairperson or chair to be more gender-neutral. | The chairwoman opened the meeting with a brief welcome. 女主席以简短的欢迎辞开始了会议。 | chairperson, president, head | chairman, male leader | 名词
