godward:/ˈɡɒdwərd/| adj.|朝向神的；虔诚的|Moving or directed toward God; pious; devout.|例句：His godward gaze showed his deep faith. 他虔诚的目光展现了他坚定的信仰。

例句：The godward aspirations of the community were evident in their charitable work. 社区对神的向往在他们的慈善工作中显而易见。


近义词：divine, godly, religious, pious|反义词：ungodly, impious, irreligious
