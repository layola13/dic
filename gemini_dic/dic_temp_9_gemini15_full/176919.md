realised:/ˈriːəlaɪzd/ | v. | 意识到；实现；了解 | To understand clearly; to achieve or bring about (a goal, plan, etc.). |例句：I realised my mistake. 我意识到了我的错误。  |例句：They realised their ambition to climb Mount Everest. 他们实现了攀登珠穆朗玛峰的愿望。 |近义词：understood, achieved, accomplished, grasped |反义词：ignored, overlooked, missed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I realise the importance of this project. = 我意识到这个项目的重要性。
He realises his potential. = 他意识到了自己的潜力。

一般过去时:
I realised I was wrong. = 我意识到我错了。
She realised her dream of becoming a doctor. = 她实现了成为医生的梦想。

现在完成时:
I have realised the error in my calculations. = 我已经意识到了我计算中的错误。
They have realised significant profits this year. = 他们今年获得了可观的利润。

过去完成时:
I had realised my mistake before he pointed it out. = 他指出我的错误之前，我已经意识到了。
She had realised the danger before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，她已经意识到了危险。

将来时:
I will realise my full potential someday. = 我总有一天会充分发挥我的潜力。
He will realise his goals through hard work. = 他将通过努力工作实现他的目标。

虚拟语气:
If I realised the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我意识到了后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had realised the importance of education earlier. = 我真希望我早些意识到教育的重要性。

被动语态:
The potential of the new technology has not yet been fully realised. = 新技术的潜力尚未完全实现。
His ambition was realised through years of hard work. = 他的抱负是通过多年的努力实现的。

疑问句:
Do you realise how late it is? = 你意识到现在已经很晚了么？
Does he realise the seriousness of the situation? = 他意识到情况的严重性吗？

否定句:
I didn't realise it was so late. = 我没意识到这么晚了。
He didn't realise the impact of his words. = 他没有意识到自己话语的影响。


PHRASE->
realise a dream = 实现梦想
realise a profit = 实现利润
realise an ambition = 实现抱负
realise the importance of = 意识到...的重要性
realise one's potential = 发挥潜力
