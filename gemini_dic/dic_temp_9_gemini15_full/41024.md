cope:/koʊp/| v.|应对，处理，克服|To deal successfully with a difficult situation.|例句：She coped well with the pressure of the job. 她很好地应对了工作压力。|例句：He's finding it hard to cope with his wife's death. 他发现很难应对妻子的去世。|近义词：manage, handle, deal with|反义词：fail, succumb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He copes with stress effectively. = 他有效地应对压力。
They cope well under pressure. = 他们在压力下应对良好。


一般过去时:
She coped admirably with the crisis. = 她出色地应对了危机。
We coped with the problem by working together. = 我们通过合作解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
They have coped with many challenges in their lives. = 他们在生活中克服了许多挑战。
She has coped remarkably well since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，她应对得非常好。


过去完成时:
