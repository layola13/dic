cannabis:/ˈkænəbɪs/ | n. | 大麻 | A tall annual herb of the genus Cannabis, having opposite leaves and small flowers, the female plant of which yields hemp fiber and the resin from which marijuana and hashish are prepared.  Cannabis is used for various purposes, including the production of textiles, rope, paper, and medicine.  However, its recreational use is controversial and illegal in many places. | Example: The use of cannabis for medicinal purposes is becoming increasingly accepted in some countries.  大麻用于医疗用途在一些国家越来越被接受。|近义词: marijuana, hemp, weed |反义词: (无直接反义词，可根据语境选择如tobacco等) |名词
