discrown:/dɪsˈkraʊn/| vt.|废黜；夺取…的王位；除去…的冠冕|To remove a crown from; depose from a throne.  To remove something resembling a crown.|The king was discrowned after the rebellion.  叛乱后国王被废黜了。 He discrowned the statue of the fallen dictator. 他移除了那位垮台独裁者雕像上的皇冠。|dethrone, depose, unseat|crown, enthrone|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They discrown the king. = 他们废黜国王。
She discrows the champion. = 她剥夺了冠军的头衔。


一般过去时:
The rebels discrowned the tyrant. = 反叛者废黜了暴君。
The judges discrowned the winner due to doping. = 由于服用兴奋剂，裁判取消了获胜者的冠军头衔。


现在完成时:
The people have discrowned their former leader. = 人民已经废黜了他们之前的领导人。
The court has discrowned the unjustly crowned champion. = 法院已经取消了不公正加冕的冠军的头衔。


过去完成时:
They had discrowned the emperor before the revolution. = 他们早在革命之前就废黜了皇帝。
The committee had discrowned the previous winner before announcing the new one. = 委员会在宣布新的获胜者之前已经取消了上一届获胜者的头衔。


将来时:
The parliament will discrown the monarch. = 议会将废黜君主。
They will discrown the wrongfully crowned winner. = 他们将取消错误加冕的获胜者的头衔。


虚拟语气:
If they had evidence, they would discrown him. = 如果他们有证据，他们就会废黜他。
If the judges had known, they would have discrowned the athlete. = 如果裁判知道，他们就会取消那位运动员的头衔。


被动语态:
The king was discrowned by the rebels. = 国王被叛军废黜了。
The champion was discrowned because of his actions. = 冠军因为他的行为而被取消了头衔。


疑问句:
Was the king discrowned? = 国王被废黜了吗？
Will the winner be discrowned? = 获胜者会被取消头衔吗？


否定句:
They did not discrown the queen. = 他们没有废黜女王。
The committee did not discrown the athlete. = 委员会没有取消那位运动员的头衔。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "discrown".  The word itself is relatively rare.
