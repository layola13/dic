nongreener:/ˈnɒnˈɡriːnər/| adj. |非环保人士，不关心环保的人| A nongreener is someone who does not care about the environment or does not participate in environmentally friendly practices.  They might disregard recycling, overuse resources, or support environmentally damaging policies.|例句：He's a nongreener; he leaves the lights on all night and never recycles. 他是个不环保的人；他整夜开着灯，从不回收利用。|例句：Many consider those who drive gas-guzzling vehicles to be nongreeners. 许多人认为驾驶耗油汽车的人是不环保的人。|近义词：environmentally unfriendly person, anti-environmentalist |反义词：environmentalist, eco-warrior
