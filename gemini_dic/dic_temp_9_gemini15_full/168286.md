pro-Methodist: /ˌproʊˈmɛθədɪst/ | adj. | 支持卫理公会的；拥护卫理宗的 | Favoring or supporting Methodism.  | The pro-Methodist sentiment in the town was strong, leading to the construction of a new church.  | pro-religious, pro-Christian | anti-Methodist |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pro-Methodist. = 他支持卫理公会。
She supports pro-Methodist initiatives. = 她支持卫理公会的倡议。


一般过去时:
They were pro-Methodist in their beliefs. = 他们的信仰是支持卫理公会的。
He remained pro-Methodist throughout his life. = 他一生都支持卫理公会。


现在完成时:
The community has been largely pro-Methodist for generations. = 这个社区几代人都一直主要支持卫理公会。
She has always been pro-Methodist, actively participating in church activities. = 她一直支持卫理公会，积极参与教会活动。


过去完成时:
Before the schism, the congregation had been overwhelmingly pro-Methodist. = 在分裂之前，会众压倒性地支持卫理公会。
They had already been pro-Methodist before the revival. = 在复兴之前，他们就已经支持卫理公会了。


将来时:
He will remain pro-Methodist, regardless of the changes in the church. = 不管教会发生什么变化，他都会继续支持卫理公会。
The movement predicts that the region will become more pro-Methodist in the coming years. = 该运动预测该地区在未来几年将变得更加支持卫理公会。


虚拟语气:
If he were pro-Methodist, he would attend services regularly. = 如果他支持卫理公会，他会定期参加礼拜。
I wish my family were more pro-Methodist, so we could attend church together. = 我希望我的家人更支持卫理公会，这样我们就能一起去做礼拜。


被动语态:

否定句:

词源：
The word is a compound of "pro-" (in favor of) and "Methodist" (a follower of Methodism).  Methodism itself derives from the teachings and practices of John Wesley and his followers in the 18th century.
