role-playing:/ˈroʊlˌpleɪɪŋ/|/ˈrəʊlˌpleɪɪŋ/| n. |角色扮演；角色扮演游戏 |The activity of acting out a particular role, often in a game or therapeutic context.  It involves assuming a fictional persona and interacting with others within a defined setting and rules.  Role-playing can be used for entertainment, education, or therapy. |例句：The children enjoyed role-playing as pirates. 孩子们喜欢扮演海盗。

例句：She uses role-playing techniques in her therapy sessions.  她在治疗中使用角色扮演技巧。

近义词：acting, simulation, dramatization

反义词：reality, observation
