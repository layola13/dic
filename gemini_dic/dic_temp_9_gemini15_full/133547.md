nonfinding:/ˈnɒnfaɪndɪŋ/ | n. |未发现；未找到的结果；阴性结果 |  The absence of an expected finding; a negative result in a test or examination.  It often refers to the lack of evidence for something rather than an explicit statement that something is absent.  | Example: The nonfinding of cancerous cells in the biopsy was reassuring.  未在活检中发现癌细胞令人放心。  The study reported a nonfinding of a correlation between the two variables. 研究报告称这两个变量之间没有相关性。 |近义词：absence, lack, negative result | 反义词：finding, positive result | 名词
