hones:/həʊnz/| vt.|磨练；使诚实；精炼| to make something sharp or smooth by rubbing it against something hard; to make someone or something more honest or truthful | 例句：He honed his skills as a carpenter over many years. 他多年来磨练了他的木工技艺。|  例句：The experience honed her sense of responsibility. 这段经历磨练了她的责任感。|近义词：sharpen, refine, perfect|反义词：blunt, dull, corrupt|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hones his knives every week. = 他每周都磨刀。
She hones her writing skills daily. = 她每天磨练写作技巧。

一般过去时:
He honed his skills during his apprenticeship. = 他在学徒期间磨练了自己的技能。
She honed her presentation before the big meeting. = 她在大型会议前磨练了自己的演示文稿。

现在完成时:
He has honed his public speaking skills over the years. = 多年来，他磨练了自己的公开演讲技巧。
She has honed her culinary talents through practice. = 她通过练习磨练了自己的烹饪技巧。

过去完成时:
He had honed his argument before the debate. = 他在辩论前已磨练了自己的论点。
She had honed her skills for years before she started her business. = 在创业之前，她已经磨练了自己的技能多年。

将来时:
He will hone his skills further with practice. = 他将通过练习进一步磨练自己的技能。
She will hone her presentation before presenting it to the board. = 她将在向董事会演示之前磨练自己的演示文稿。

虚拟语气:
If he had honed his skills earlier, he would have succeeded sooner. = 如果他早点磨练自己的技能，他就会早点成功。
If she honed her skills more, she would be more competitive. = 如果她更多地磨练自己的技能，她将更有竞争力。

被动语态:
His skills were honed through years of experience. = 他的技能是通过多年的经验磨练出来的。
Her presentation was honed to perfection. = 她的演示文稿被磨练得尽善尽美。

疑问句:
How does he hone his skills? = 他如何磨练他的技能？
How will she hone her skills for the upcoming competition? = 她将如何为即将到来的比赛磨练技能？

否定句:
He didn't hone his skills enough for the competition. = 他的技能不足以参加比赛。
She hadn't honed her skills before the interview. = 面试前她没有磨练自己的技能。
