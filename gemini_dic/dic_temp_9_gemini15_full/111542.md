lobar:/ˈloʊbɑːr/| adj.|叶的；肺叶的；裂片的|Relating to a lobe of an organ, especially the lungs or brain.  This term is primarily used in medical contexts to describe structures or processes affecting a lobe of an organ.|例句：The patient presented with lobar pneumonia.  这位病人患有肺叶肺炎。|例句：The doctor suspected a lobar hemorrhage in the brain. 医生怀疑脑部发生叶状出血。|近义词：lobular, segmental|反义词：diffuse (in the context of disease spread)
