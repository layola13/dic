sceptics:/ˈskeptɪks/| n. |怀疑论者，持怀疑态度的人|People who are skeptical; those who question or doubt the truth of something.  They often demand evidence before accepting a claim or belief. |例句：Many sceptics remain unconvinced by the government's explanation. 许多怀疑论者仍然不相信政府的解释。|例句：The new theory was met with skepticism from sceptics in the scientific community.  新理论遭到了科学界怀疑论者的质疑。|近义词：doubters, questioners, critics, cynics|反义词：believers, supporters, enthusiasts
