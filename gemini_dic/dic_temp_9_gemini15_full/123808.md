mopped:/mɑːpt/| v.|擦拭；拖把拖；|to clean a surface by wiping it with a mop.|例句：I mopped the kitchen floor after dinner.  晚饭后我擦了厨房的地板。|例句：She mopped up the spilled milk. 她擦掉了洒出的牛奶。|近义词： wiped, cleaned, swabbed |反义词： dirtied, soiled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mops the floor every day. 他每天拖地。
She mops up spills quickly. 她很快擦掉洒出来的东西。

一般过去时:
I mopped the kitchen floor yesterday. 我昨天拖了厨房的地板。
He mopped up the spilled coffee. 他擦掉了洒出来的咖啡。

现在完成时:
I have mopped the floor twice today. 我今天已经拖过两次地了。
She has mopped up all the spills. 她已经擦掉了所有洒出来的东西。

过去完成时:
I had mopped the floor before the guests arrived. 客人来之前我已经拖过地了。
She had mopped up the mess before I got there. 我到那儿之前她已经清理了烂摊子。

将来时:
I will mop the floor after I finish cooking. 做完饭后我会拖地。
She will mop up any spills immediately. 她会立即擦掉任何洒出来的东西。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mop the floor. 如果我有更多时间，我会拖地。
I wish I had mopped the floor earlier. 我真希望我早点拖地。

被动语态:
The floor was mopped this morning. 今天早上地板被拖过了。
The spill was quickly mopped up. 洒出来的东西很快就被擦掉了。

疑问句:
Have you mopped the floor yet? 你拖地了吗？
Did she mop up the juice? 她擦掉果汁了吗？

否定句:
I didn't mop the floor today. 我今天没拖地。
She didn't mop up the water. 她没有擦掉水。
