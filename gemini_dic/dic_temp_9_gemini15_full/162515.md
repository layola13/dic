preaggressiveness: /ˌpriːəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs/ | n. | 先发制人的侵略性；预先的攻击性 |  A tendency to be aggressive before being provoked; proactive aggression.  It often involves initiating hostile actions or behaviors without immediate justification or provocation.  This can manifest in verbal attacks, physical threats, or other forms of intimidation. |


例句：

The preaggressiveness of the bully was evident in his constant taunting and threatening behavior.  = 那个恶霸的先发制人侵略性在他不断的嘲讽和威胁行为中显而易见。

His preaggressiveness stemmed from a deep-seated insecurity. = 他的先发制人侵略性源于根深蒂固的不安全感。


近义词：proactiveness (in aggression), hostility, belligerence, aggressiveness, antagonism


反义词：passivity, submissiveness, docility, amiability


词性: 名词
