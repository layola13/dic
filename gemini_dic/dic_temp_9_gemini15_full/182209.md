remonstrated:/rɪˈmɒnstreɪtɪd/ | v. | 规劝；抗议；强烈反对 | To reason with (someone) earnestly and seriously, especially in protest or reproof.  |例句：He remonstrated with his friend about the dangers of smoking. 他规劝他的朋友戒烟，因为吸烟很危险。

例句：The workers remonstrated against the unfair wage cuts. 工人们抗议不公平的工资削减。


近义词：protest, object, complain, expostulate, rebuke | 反义词：acquiesce, agree, concur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remonstrates with his children regularly. 他经常规劝他的孩子们。
She remonstrates against the government's policy. 她反对政府的政策。


一般过去时:
He remonstrated with his boss about the long working hours. 他向老板抗议长时间的工作。
They remonstrated against the new rules. 他们抗议新的规章制度。


现在完成时:
She has remonstrated with him several times about his behavior. 她已经多次规劝他改过自新。
They have remonstrated against the proposed development. 他们已经抗议了拟议中的开发项目。


过去完成时:
He had remonstrated with her before, but she had ignored him. 他之前曾劝说过她，但她不理他。
They had remonstrated against the decision, but to no avail. 他们曾抗议过这个决定，但没有用。


将来时:
She will remonstrate with him again tomorrow. 她明天会再次规劝他。
They will remonstrate against the injustice. 他们会抗议这种不公正。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would remonstrate with him. 如果我是你，我会规劝他。
I wish I had remonstrated with her sooner. 我希望我早点劝劝她。


被动语态:
His behavior was remonstrated against by many people. 许多人反对他的行为。
The unfair law was remonstrated against by the public. 公众抗议不公平的法律。


疑问句:
Did you remonstrate with him about his actions? 你有没有因为他的行为而规劝他？
Will they remonstrate against the new taxes? 他们会抗议新的税收吗？


否定句:
He did not remonstrate with her about the matter. 他没有因为这件事而规劝她。
They did not remonstrate against the proposal. 他们没有反对这项提议。



PHRASE->
