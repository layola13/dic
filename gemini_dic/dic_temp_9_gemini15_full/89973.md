horsewhipped:/ˈhɔːrsˌhwɪpt/ | v. |鞭打；严厉斥责 | To beat severely with a horsewhip.  The term often implies a harsh and public punishment, carrying a strong connotation of humiliation. |例句：He was horsewhipped in the town square for his crime. 他因犯罪在镇广场上被鞭打。  The politician was horsewhipped by the press for his scandalous behavior.  这位政客因其丑闻行为而受到媒体的严厉批评。|近义词：flogged, lashed, caned, castigated |反义词：praised, commended
