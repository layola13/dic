cruddle:/ˈkrʌdəl/| v.|拥抱，依偎|To cuddle means to hold close and affectionately, often to comfort or show affection.|例句：The mother cuddled her baby close. 妈妈紧紧地抱着她的婴儿。|例句：The couple cuddled on the sofa, watching a movie. 这对夫妇依偎在沙发上看电影。|近义词：hug, embrace, snuggle|反义词：push away, repel


USAGE->

They cuddle their pet dog every night. = 他们每天晚上都拥抱他们的宠物狗。
I cuddle my teddy bear before I sleep. = 我睡觉前会抱着我的泰迪熊。
