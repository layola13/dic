disinvigorate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/| vt.|使失去活力；使衰弱 |to deprive of vigor, strength, or energy; to weaken or enervate. |例句：The long, hot summer disinvigorated the entire town. 炎热的漫长夏季使整个城镇都失去了活力。

例句：The monotonous work disinvigorated him, leaving him feeling drained and listless. 单调的工作使他失去了活力，让他感到精疲力尽，无精打采。


近义词：debilitate, enervate, weaken, sap, exhaust


反义词：invigorate, energize, strengthen, revitalize


词性：动词
