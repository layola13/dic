phrenomagnetism: /ˌfrɛnoʊˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/ | n. |颅相磁力说|  A now-discredited pseudoscience that claimed to detect personality traits and mental abilities by measuring the bumps and indentations on a person's skull.  It combined the principles of phrenology (the study of skull shape and its relation to character) and mesmerism (animal magnetism).  | The doctor, a believer in phrenomagnetism, felt the bumps on his patient's head to diagnose his mental state. | 近义词: phrenology, mesmerism | 反义词: scientific psychology, neurology |
