reinstallations:/ˌriːˌɪnstəˈleɪʃənz/| n.|重新安装（复数）|The act of installing something again.  This typically refers to software, hardware, or applications. |例句：The system administrator oversaw the reinstallations of all the office computers after the virus attack. 系统管理员在病毒攻击后监督了所有办公室电脑的重新安装。

例句：The frequent reinstallations of the game became tedious for the user.  这款游戏的频繁重新安装让用户感到厌烦。


近义词：reinstallments (单复数形式), reinstalls (名词形式), re-installations


反义词：uninstallations, removals


词性:名词
