slabbing:/ˈslæbɪŋ/| n. |板材切割；平板铺设；大块状物体的形成|The process of cutting or shaping material into slabs, or the resulting slabs themselves.  It can refer to the action of cutting stone, wood, or concrete into slabs, or the process of laying slabs to create a surface. |例句：The quarry workers were busy with the slabbing of granite.  采石工人正忙着切割花岗岩板材。

The road was repaired using concrete slabbing.  这条路是用混凝土板铺修的。|近义词：slicing, paving,  laying|反义词：chiseling (in the context of detailed work), crumbling (in the context of destruction)
