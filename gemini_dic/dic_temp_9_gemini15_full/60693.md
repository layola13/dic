electrifiers:/ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪərz/| n. |电气化装置；使带电的装置| Devices or systems that electrify something, typically by converting other forms of energy into electrical energy or by applying an electric charge.  They can be used in various applications, from power grids to smaller-scale devices.|例句：The engineers installed new electrifiers to improve the efficiency of the power grid. 工程师们安装了新的电气化装置以提高电网的效率。

例句：The cattle prod is a type of electrifier used to control livestock. 电棍是一种用于控制牲畜的电气化装置。


近义词：energizers, chargers, energizing devices|反义词：de-energizers, dischargers|名词
