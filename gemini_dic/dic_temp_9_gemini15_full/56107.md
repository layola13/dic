doctors:/ˈdɒktərz/|/ˈdɑːktərz/| n. |医生们|The plural form of "doctor," referring to multiple medical practitioners.  Doctors diagnose and treat illnesses and injuries. |Example: The doctors are working hard to save the patient's life. 医生们正在努力抢救病人的生命。|  physicians, medical practitioners, healers |  patients, laymen| noun


USAGE->
