pimentos:/pɪˈmɛntoʊz/ | n. |红椒，甜椒|Pimentos are mild, sweet peppers, typically red, often used in cooking. They are a variety of Capsicum annum. | Example:  The stuffed pimentos were a delicious appetizer.  这些酿红椒是一道美味的开胃菜。|近义词：sweet peppers, bell peppers |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with hot peppers.
