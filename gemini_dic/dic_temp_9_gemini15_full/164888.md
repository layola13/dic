prefunding:/ˈpriːfʌndɪŋ/| n. |预先筹资；提前融资|The act of securing funds before they are actually needed, often to take advantage of favorable interest rates or to ensure availability when needed.|例句：The company engaged in prefunding to secure capital for its expansion project.  这家公司进行预先筹资以确保其扩张项目的资金。|例句：Prefunding the project allowed them to avoid potential delays caused by funding shortages. 预先为项目筹集资金使他们避免了因资金短缺而造成的潜在延误。|近义词：advance funding, upfront financing|反义词：post-funding, delayed financing|名词
