folksong:/ˈfoʊkˌsɒŋ/ /ˈfəʊkˌsɒŋ/ | n. |民歌，民谣 | A folksong is a song that originates from the common people of a region, often passed down through generations orally.  It typically reflects the everyday life, beliefs, and traditions of the culture it comes from.  Folksongs often have simple melodies and lyrics, easily remembered and sung by many. | Example: "The Ballad of John Henry" is a classic American folksong.  “约翰·亨利的故事”是一首经典的美国民歌。|近义词：ballad, traditional song | 反义词：art song, composed song
