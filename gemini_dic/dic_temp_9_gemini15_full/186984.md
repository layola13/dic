riskful: /ˈrɪskfəl/ | adj. | 危险的，冒险的 |  risky or dangerous; involving or exposing one to risk.  | 例句：It's riskful to invest all your money in one company. = 把所有钱都投资在一个公司里是很危险的。


例句：The riskful climb up the mountain left them exhausted but exhilarated. = 危险的山峰攀登使他们筋疲力尽，但也兴奋不已。


近义词：hazardous, perilous, dangerous, unsafe


反义词：safe, secure, harmless
