reignites:/ˌriːˈaɪtnaɪts/| vt.|重新点燃；使复燃；重新引起|To ignite again; to rekindle; to revive or renew with fresh energy or interest.|The debate reignited after the new evidence emerged.  新的证据出现后，这场辩论重新燃起。

The news reignited his hopes for a better future.  这个消息重新燃起了他对美好未来的希望。|ignite, rekindle, revive|extinguish, quell|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
The spark reignites the fire. = 火花重新点燃了火焰。
He reignites the discussion every meeting. = 他每次会议都会重新引发讨论。

过去完成时:
