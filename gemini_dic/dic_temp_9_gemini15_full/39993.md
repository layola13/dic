consultancies:/kənˈsʌltənsiz/| n. |咨询公司；咨询业务 | Businesses that provide expert advice and services to other organizations.  They often specialize in areas like management, finance, or technology. | Example: Many large companies rely on external consultancies for strategic planning. 许多大公司依靠外部咨询公司进行战略规划。|近义词：advisory firms, consulting firms |反义词：N/A
