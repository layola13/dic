freezingly:/ˈfriːzɪŋli/| adv. |极其寒冷地，冰冷地 |extremely cold; in a way that is extremely cold.  The word intensifies the adjective "freezing".|例句：It was freezingly cold outside; I had to wear three layers of clothing.  外面冷极了；我不得不穿三层衣服。

例句：The wind howled freezingly, making it difficult to stand still.  寒风呼啸，让人难以站稳。


近义词：bitterly, intensely, severely, terribly


反义词：warmly, mildly, pleasantly


词性: 副词
