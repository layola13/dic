footplate:/ˈfʊtplæt/| n.|踏板；底板；（蒸汽机等的）踏脚板|A footplate is a flat surface, often metal, that is used to support a foot or feet. In the context of steam locomotives, it refers to the platform where the driver stands and operates the controls.|例句：The driver stood on the footplate, controlling the steam locomotive.|近义词：platform, treadle, footrest|反义词: ceiling, top
