orbit's:/ˈɔːrbɪts/| n. |轨道；运行轨道|The path of an object as it revolves around another object, such as a planet around a star.|The Earth orbits the sun. 地球绕着太阳运行。|path, trajectory |—|  noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The moon orbits the Earth. = 月亮绕地球运行。
The planets orbit the sun. = 行星绕太阳运行。

一般过去时:
The satellite orbited the planet for several years. = 这颗卫星绕行星运行了好几年。
The spacecraft successfully orbited Mars. = 宇宙飞船成功地绕火星运行。

现在完成时:
The satellite has orbited the Earth for over a decade. = 这颗卫星绕地球运行已超过十年。
The probe has completed several orbits around Jupiter. = 探测器已经完成了绕木星的几次轨道运行。

过去完成时:
The astronaut had orbited the Earth several times before landing. = 宇航员着陆前已绕地球运行数次。
The comet had already completed its orbit before we could observe it. = 我们观察到之前，彗星已经完成了它的轨道运行。

将来时:
The new satellite will orbit the Earth at a high altitude. = 新卫星将在高空绕地球运行。
The spacecraft will orbit the moon before landing. = 宇宙飞船将在着陆前绕月球运行。

虚拟语气:
If the satellite orbited closer, we could get better images. = 如果卫星运行得更近，我们就能获得更好的图像。
I wish the planet orbited faster. = 我希望这颗行星运行得更快。

被动语态:
The planet is orbited by several moons. = 这颗行星被几颗卫星环绕。
The Earth is orbited by the moon. = 地球被月球环绕。

疑问句:
Does the comet orbit the sun? = 这颗彗星绕太阳运行吗？
How many times does the satellite orbit the Earth daily? = 卫星每天绕地球运行多少次？

否定句:
The asteroid does not orbit the sun. = 这颗小行星不绕太阳运行。
The planet does not orbit its star. = 这颗行星不绕它的恒星运行。
