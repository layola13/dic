entangler:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlər/| n.|纠缠者，缠绕物|Something or someone that entangles.  A person or thing that causes entanglement.|The thorny bushes were a real entangler for hikers.|近义词：snare, trap, impediment|反义词：disentangler, liberator|名词
