oversystematic: /ˌoʊvərˌsɪstəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 过度系统化的；过于系统化的 | Excessively systematic; characterized by an excessive or inappropriate degree of systematization.  It implies that the system or method is overly complex, rigid, or inefficient, often hindering rather than helping the process. | The project's oversystematic approach led to delays and frustration.  这个项目的过度系统化方法导致了延误和挫折。  The oversystematic filing system made it difficult to find anything. 这个过于系统化的归档系统使得很难找到任何东西。 |近义词：overorganized, overly methodical, excessively structured | 反义词：chaotic, disorganized, haphazard | 词性：形容词
