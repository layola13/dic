recuperative: /rɪˈkjuːpərətɪv/ | adj. |恢复健康的；有益于恢复健康的 |  Having the power to restore health or strength; promoting recovery.  | Example:  The recuperative powers of nature were evident in his improved health after a week in the mountains.  大自然的恢复能力在他山上待了一周后身体好转中显而易见。

Example:  She spent several weeks in a recuperative environment to regain her strength after surgery. 她在一个有益于恢复健康的的环境中度过了几周时间，以恢复手术后的体力。|近义词：restorative, healing, therapeutic |反义词：debilitating, weakening, harmful | 词性：形容词
