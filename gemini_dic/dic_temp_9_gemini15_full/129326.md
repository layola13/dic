noise:/nɔɪz/|n.|噪音，喧嚣，嘈杂声|Unwanted or unpleasant sound.|The construction work made a lot of noise.  施工造成了很大的噪音。|The children were making so much noise. 孩子们吵闹得很。|silence, din, clamor| quiet, silence, stillness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine makes a lot of noise. = 这台发动机发出很大的噪音。
There is always noise coming from their apartment. = 他们的公寓总是传来噪音。

一般过去时:
The party made a lot of noise last night. = 昨晚派对吵得很厉害。
I didn't hear the noise because I was asleep. = 我因为睡着了所以没听到噪音。

现在完成时:
They have made a lot of noise all day. = 他们整天都在吵闹。
The neighbors have complained about the noise several times. = 邻居们已经多次投诉噪音问题。

过去完成时:
The noise had stopped before I went to bed. = 我睡觉前噪音已经停止了。
They had made so much noise that everyone in the building could hear them. = 他们吵得那么厉害，楼里的人都听得见。

将来时:
The concert will probably make a lot of noise. = 音乐会可能会很吵。
I think the new machine will make less noise than the old one. = 我认为新机器比旧机器噪音小。

虚拟语气:
If the noise were less, I could concentrate better. = 如果噪音小一点，我就能更好地集中注意力。
I wish the traffic noise would stop. = 我希望交通噪音能停止。

被动语态:
The noise was caused by the construction work. = 噪音是由施工造成的。
The problem of noise pollution is being addressed by the city council. = 市议会正在解决噪音污染问题。

疑问句:
What's that noise? = 那是什么声音？
Can you hear that noise? = 你能听到那个噪音吗？

否定句:
There was no noise at all. = 一点声音都没有。
I couldn't hear any noise over the music. = 我听不见音乐声以外的任何噪音。
