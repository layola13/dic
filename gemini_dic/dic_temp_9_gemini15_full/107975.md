lash-out: /læʃ aʊt/ | v. | 猛烈抨击；突然发怒；挥打 | To suddenly express anger in a violent or aggressive way; to strike out violently. | 例句：He lashed out at his critics. 他猛烈抨击了他的批评者。  He lashed out at the waiter. 他朝服务员大发脾气。 | 近义词：attack, strike, assault, berate | 反义词：calm down, pacify, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lashes out at anyone who disagrees with him. = 他对任何与他意见相左的人都大发雷霆。
She lashes out verbally when she's stressed. = 她压力大的时候会口头攻击别人。


一般过去时:
He lashed out at his boss after the meeting. = 会后，他向老板发火了。
She lashed out at her friend for breaking her trust. = 她因为朋友背叛了她而对她大发脾气。


现在完成时:
He has lashed out at the media several times this year. = 今年他已经几次抨击媒体了。
She has never lashed out physically, only verbally. = 她从未有过肢体攻击，只有言语攻击。


过去完成时:
He had lashed out at his family before he calmed down. = 他在冷静下来之前曾对家人发过脾气。
She had lashed out at her colleagues before realizing her mistake. = 她意识到自己的错误之前，曾对同事们大发脾气。


将来时:
He will lash out if you provoke him. = 如果你激怒他，他会大发雷霆。
She will likely lash out if she doesn't get what she wants. = 如果她得不到想要的东西，她很可能会大发雷霆。


虚拟语气:
If he were less stressed, he wouldn't lash out so often. = 如果他不那么有压力，他就不会那么经常发脾气。
I wish he wouldn't lash out at me every time I disagree. = 我希望他每次不同意我的意见时，都不要对我发脾气。


被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
His anger was lashed out at the innocent bystander. = 他的怒火发泄在了无辜的路人身上。(此例句略显不自然)
The criticism was lashed out at the government's new policy. = 批评矛头指向政府的新政策。(更强调批评本身)


疑问句:
Why did he lash out at you? = 他为什么对你发火？
Will she lash out if we tell her the truth? = 如果我们告诉她真相，她会发脾气吗？


否定句:
He didn't lash out, he just walked away. = 他没有发脾气，只是走开了。
She didn't lash out even though she was angry. = 尽管她很生气，但她并没有发脾气。


PHRASE->
lash out at someone: 对某人猛烈抨击
lash out with: 用…猛击
lash out in anger: 愤怒地大发雷霆
lash out verbally: 言语攻击
lash out physically: 肢体攻击
