millerian:/mɪˈlɛəriən/| adj. |米勒氏的；穆勒氏管的| Relating to the Müllerian ducts, which are embryonic structures that develop into the female reproductive organs in females.  The term is often used in the context of developmental biology and reproductive medicine.| Example:  The researchers studied the development of the millerian ducts in the embryos. 研究人员研究了胚胎中米勒氏管的发育。|近义词：paramesonephric (as a synonym for Müllerian)|反义词：wolffian (referring to the Wolffian ducts, the male counterpart)|词性:形容词
