pdfs:/ˈpiːdiˈɛfs/| n. |PDF文件 (复数)| Portable Document Format files;  a collection of Portable Document Format files.  These are commonly used for sharing documents in a format that preserves formatting and is independent of the software used to create them.|例句：I downloaded all the pdfs from the website. 我从网站上下载了所有PDF文件。  I need to send you a few pdfs. 我需要给你发几个PDF文件。|近义词：documents, files|反义词：无
