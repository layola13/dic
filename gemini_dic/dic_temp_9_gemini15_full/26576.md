/kəˈbuːdəl/ | n. | 全体；全部人员；一整套 | The whole lot; the entire group or collection.  This word is often used informally to refer to a group of people or things considered as a single unit. | Example:  The whole caboodle of them went to the beach.  他们全体都去海滩了。  |近义词：whole lot, entire group, aggregate, assemblage | 反义词：part, fraction, piece
