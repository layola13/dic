intercession:/ˌɪntərˈsɛʃən/| n. |恳求；调解；代祷 |The act of interceding or pleading on behalf of another; intervention.  It can refer to pleading with someone in authority to obtain a favor or pardon, or to mediating a dispute. |例句：Her intercession saved him from punishment. 她的恳求救了他免受惩罚。|例句：The priest offered intercession for the sick. 牧师为病人祈祷。|近义词：mediation, intervention, plea, advocacy |反义词：opposition, obstruction
