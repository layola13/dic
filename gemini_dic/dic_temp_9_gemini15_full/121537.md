mismarking:/mɪsˈmɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 错误标记，误标 | The act of incorrectly marking something; a mistake in labeling or identifying something.  It can refer to incorrect labeling of goods, wrong answers on a test, or inaccurate identification in a system. | Example: The mismarking of the packages led to delays in shipping.  错误的包装标记导致了运输延误。 The student's mismarking of the answers resulted in a lower score. 学生答案标记错误导致分数降低。 |近义词：mislabeling,  incorrect marking,  erroneous marking | 反义词：correct marking, accurate labeling | 名词
