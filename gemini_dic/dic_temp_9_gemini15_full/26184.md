butler:/ˈbʌtlər/| n.|管家，男仆|A butler is a male servant in a wealthy household, responsible for managing the household staff and overseeing domestic affairs.  They often have extensive knowledge of etiquette and service. |例句：The butler served the guests with impeccable grace.  管家以无可挑剔的优雅为客人服务。

例句：The wealthy family employed a butler to manage their large estate.  那个富裕的家庭雇佣了一位管家来管理他们的庄园。


近义词：housekeeper (if female), manservant, valet


反义词：master, mistress (of the house)


词性:名词
