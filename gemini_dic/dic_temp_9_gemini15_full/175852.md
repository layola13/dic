rasterizer:/ˈræstəraɪzər/| n. |光栅化器，栅格化器 | A rasterizer is a component of a computer graphics system that converts vector graphics into raster graphics.  It takes geometric primitives (like lines, polygons, and curves) and transforms them into a pixel-based representation that can be displayed on a screen. | Example: The high-quality rasterizer produced a smooth, anti-aliased image.  高质量的光栅化器生成了平滑的、反锯齿的图像。 |近义词：rasterizer engine, rendering engine |反义词：vectorizer
