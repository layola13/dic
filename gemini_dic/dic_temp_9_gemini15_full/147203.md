overman:/ˈoʊvərmæn/| n.|超人，超越者，优等人|A person who transcends ordinary human limitations, often through exceptional strength, intelligence, or moral character.  The term is often associated with Nietzsche's philosophy.|例句：Nietzsche's concept of the overman represents a person who overcomes limitations and creates their own values. 尼采的超人概念代表一个克服局限并创造自己价值的人。|例句：He considered himself an overman, capable of achieving extraordinary feats. 他认为自己是一个超人，能够完成非凡的壮举。|近义词：superman, superior being, master race|反义词：underling, inferior, subhuman|名词
