landfills:/ˈlændfɪlz/| n.|垃圾填埋场|A landfill is a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial.  Landfills are designed to minimize environmental hazards, but they can still pose risks such as groundwater contamination and methane gas emissions.|Example: The city is planning to close the old landfill and build a new one.|近义词：dump, rubbish tip|反义词：recycling center
