serpentinely:/ˌsɜːrpənˈtaɪnli/ | adv. |蜿蜒地，曲折地，狡猾地 | Moving in a winding or twisting manner, like a serpent; characterized by cunning or deceit. |例句：The river flowed serpentinely through the valley.  这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。

The road serpentinely climbed the mountain. 这条路蜿蜒地爬上了山。


近义词：windingly, crookedly, deviously, craftily | 反义词：straightly, directly, honestly
