hypobranchial: /ˌhaɪpoʊˈbræŋkiəl/ | adj. | 舌下部的；位于鳃弓下的 | Relating to the region below the gills in animals, particularly in the context of anatomy and embryology.  The term often refers to structures or tissues located beneath the branchial arches, which are the skeletal supports of the gills in fish and some other aquatic animals. | The hypobranchial muscles in fish play a crucial role in respiration and feeding. = 鱼类的舌下肌在呼吸和摄食中起着至关重要的作用。


The hypobranchial region develops from the ventral part of the branchial arches during embryonic development. = 舌下区在胚胎发育过程中由鳃弓的腹侧部分发育而来。


近义词: subbranchial (less common)


反义词: epibranchial (above the gills)


词性: adjective
