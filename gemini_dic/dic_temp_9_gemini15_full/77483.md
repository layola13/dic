gazed:/ɡeɪzd/ | v. |凝视，注视 |To look steadily and intently, often with a fixed gaze.  |例句：She gazed at the painting, lost in thought. 她凝视着画，陷入沉思。|例句：He gazed out the window at the stormy sea. 他凝视着窗外暴风雨的海。|近义词：stare, contemplate, look, regard |反义词：glance, skim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gazes at the stars every night. = 他每晚都凝视着星星。
She gazes longingly at the ocean. = 她深情地凝视着大海。


一般过去时:
I gazed at the sunset and felt peaceful. = 我凝视着日落，感到平静。
They gazed in awe at the majestic mountain. = 他们敬畏地凝视着雄伟的山峰。


现在完成时:
He has gazed at that painting for hours. = 他已经凝视那幅画几个小时了。
She has gazed out the window all afternoon. = 她整个下午都凝视着窗外。


过去完成时:
I had gazed at the map for a long time before I found my way. = 我在找到路之前已经研究地图很长时间了。
They had gazed at the artwork for minutes before realizing it was a fake. = 他们在意识到那是一件赝品之前已经凝视这件艺术品几分钟了。


将来时:
I will gaze at the moon tonight. = 今晚我将凝望月亮。
She will gaze lovingly at her newborn baby. = 她将深情地凝视着她的新生儿。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would gaze at the stars for hours. = 如果我有更多时间，我会凝视星星几个小时。
I wish I could gaze at the beautiful scenery longer. = 我希望我能更长时间地欣赏美丽的风景。


被动语态:  (Gaze is rarely used in the passive voice)
The painting was gazed at by many visitors. = 那幅画被许多参观者凝视。  (This sounds unnatural)
The sunset was gazed upon with wonder. = 日落被人们惊奇地凝视着。(This is slightly more natural)


疑问句:
What are you gazing at? = 你在看什么？
Where did he gaze when he was lost in thought? = 他沉思时凝视着哪里？


否定句:
He did not gaze at the accident scene. = 他没有凝视事故现场。
She didn't gaze at him; she looked away. = 她没有凝视他；她把目光移开了。



PHRASE->
gaze intently = 专注地凝视
gaze longingly = 深情地凝视
gaze upon = 凝视
gaze blankly = 茫然地凝视
gaze steadily = 稳定地凝视


词源:
The word "gaze" ultimately derives from the Old French word "gazer" meaning "to look intently."  The Old French word itself likely comes from a Germanic root.
