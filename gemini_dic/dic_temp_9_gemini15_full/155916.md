photocopied:/ˈfoʊtoʊkɑːpiːd/ | adj. |影印的，复印的 | Made by photocopying.  The adjective describes something that has been created using a photocopier. | Example: I handed in a photocopied assignment.  我交了一份影印的作业。  |近义词：copied, duplicated | 反义词：original


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine photocopies documents quickly. = 这台机器快速复印文件。
He photocopies all his notes before class. = 他上课前复印所有笔记。

一般过去时:
She photocopied the report yesterday. = 她昨天复印了报告。
They photocopied the important documents. = 他们复印了重要的文件。

现在完成时:
I have photocopied all the necessary materials. = 我已经复印了所有必要的材料。
We have photocopied the contract for all parties involved. = 我们已经为所有相关方复印了合同。

过去完成时:
He had photocopied the article before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经复印了这篇文章。
She had photocopied the forms for the applicants. = 她已经为申请者复印了表格。

将来时:
I will photocopy the documents tomorrow. = 我明天将复印文件。
They will photocopy the blueprints for the construction project. = 他们将为建筑项目复印蓝图。

虚拟语气:
If I had a photocopier, I would photocopy the book. = 如果我有一台复印机，我会复印这本书。
I wish I had photocopied that document earlier. = 我真希望早点把那份文件复印了。

被动语态:
The documents were photocopied this morning. = 文件今天早上被复印了。
The letters will be photocopied and sent out. = 信件将被复印并寄出。

疑问句:
Have you photocopied the documents yet? = 你已经复印文件了吗？
Did they photocopy the blueprints for the project? = 他们为该项目复印蓝图了吗？

否定句:
I didn't photocopy the article. = 我没有复印这篇文章。
She hasn't photocopied the important papers. = 她还没有复印重要的文件。
