minorities:/maɪˈnɒrɪtiz/ | n. | 少数民族；少数派 | A group of people who are different from the larger group in a country or society, especially because of their race, religion, or origin.  They often face discrimination and prejudice. | Example:  Ethnic minorities in the United States have historically faced significant challenges. 美国少数民族历来面临着巨大的挑战。 The government is working to protect the rights of religious minorities. 政府正在努力保护宗教少数派的权利。|近义词：smaller groups, less powerful groups |反义词：majorities
