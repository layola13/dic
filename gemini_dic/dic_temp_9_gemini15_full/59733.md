eco-efficient:/ˌekoʊɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj. |生态高效的；环境友好的| Environmentally friendly and resource-efficient, aiming to minimize negative impacts on the environment while maximizing efficiency.  This concept encompasses various aspects, including energy conservation, waste reduction, and the use of sustainable materials.| 例句：The company's new eco-efficient production process reduces waste by 50%. 公司新的生态高效生产流程将废物减少了50%。

The new eco-efficient building uses solar panels and rainwater harvesting. 这座新的生态高效建筑使用太阳能电池板和雨水收集系统。


近义词：sustainable, environmentally friendly, green, resource-efficient | 反义词：environmentally damaging, unsustainable, wasteful


词源:  The word is a combination of "eco," a shortened form of "ecological" or "ecology," and "efficient," meaning producing a desired effect or result with a minimum of wasted time or effort.  The prefix "eco-" signifies a connection to the environment and its sustainability.
