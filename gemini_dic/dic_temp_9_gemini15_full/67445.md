extirpates:/ˈɛkstɜːrpeɪts/ | vt. |根除；消灭；连根拔起 | To remove or destroy completely; to eradicate.  The act of completely removing or destroying something, especially a plant or undesirable element. | 例句：The government is trying to extirpate corruption. 政府正试图根除腐败。
The disease was finally extirpated from the region.  这种疾病最终从该地区根除了。


近义词：eradicate, eliminate, root out, abolish, exterminate | 反义词：cultivate, foster, encourage, implant
