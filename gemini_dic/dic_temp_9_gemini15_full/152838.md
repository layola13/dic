pecunious:/pɪˈkjuːniəs/| adj.|有钱的，富有的|Having a lot of money; wealthy.|例句：He comes from a pecunious family and can afford the best education. 他来自一个富裕的家庭，可以负担最好的教育。
例句：Their pecunious lifestyle is quite enviable. 他们有钱的生活方式令人羡慕。|近义词：wealthy, affluent, rich, opulent|反义词：impecunious, poor, penniless|形容词

词源:
