premolar:/ˈpriːmoʊlər/| n. |前臼齿|A premolar is one of the teeth located between the canines and molars.  They are used for chewing and grinding food.  Humans typically have four premolars in each jaw.  |例句：The dentist examined my premolars for any signs of decay. 牙医检查了我的前臼齿是否有龋齿迹象。
The extraction of the damaged premolar was relatively painless.  拔除损坏的前臼齿手术相对无痛。|近义词：bicuspid (双尖牙)|反义词：incisor (门齿), canine (犬齿), molar (磨牙)
