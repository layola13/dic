coordinators:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtərz/ | n. | 协调员；协调者 |  A person who coordinates the activities of a group or organization.  Coordinators are responsible for ensuring that different parts of a project or task work together smoothly and efficiently. | 例句：The project coordinators ensured all teams met their deadlines. 项目协调员确保所有团队都按时完成工作。| 例句：The event coordinators handled all the logistics for the conference. 活动协调员负责会议的所有后勤工作。| 近义词：organizers, managers, directors | 反义词：disruptors, dividers
