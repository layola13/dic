half-republican:/ˌhæf rɪˈpʌblɪkən/| adj.| 半共和黨的；傾向於共和黨的 |  Describes someone who identifies with or leans towards the Republican Party but not fully or consistently.  This term often implies a degree of skepticism or disagreement with certain aspects of the party platform. | Example:  He's a half-republican, voting for some Republican candidates but also supporting some Democratic policies. 他是半个共和党人，既投票给一些共和党候选人，也支持一些民主党政策。|近义词：Republican-leaning,  centrist (if leaning towards Republicanism) | 反义词：Democrat, staunchly Democratic
