This term is often used in medicine to refer to the surgical removal of the cerebral cortex. | 例句：The decortication of the potatoes was a tedious task.  马铃薯去皮是一项乏味的工作。

The decortication of the brain is a serious surgical procedure. 大脑皮质切除术是一项严重的外科手术。
