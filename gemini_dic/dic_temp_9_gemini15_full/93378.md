illiberalism: /ɪˈlɪbərəlɪzəm/ | n. |非自由主义；限制自由的政治倾向 | The rejection of liberal principles, such as individual rights and freedoms, in favor of authoritarian or totalitarian rule.  It often involves suppressing dissent, limiting freedoms of speech and press, and restricting political participation. |  The rise of illiberalism in several countries is a cause for concern.  非自由主义在一些国家的兴起令人担忧。 The government's actions are a clear example of illiberalism. 政府的行为是明显的非自由主义的例子。| authoritarianism, totalitarianism, autocracy | liberalism, democracy | 名词
