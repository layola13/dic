haunchy:/hɔːntʃi/| adj.| 傲慢的；自大的；粗鲁的；笨拙的 | Having or showing an arrogant or supercilious manner; swaggering.  It can also imply clumsiness or awkwardness. |例句：He strutted down the street with a haunchy gait. 他迈着傲慢的步伐走在街上。

例句：Her haunchy attitude alienated many of her colleagues. 她傲慢的态度疏远了她许多同事。


近义词： arrogant, supercilious, haughty, imperious, pompous, swaggering

反义词：humble, modest, meek, unassuming


词性：形容词
