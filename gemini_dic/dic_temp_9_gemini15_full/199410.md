simplify:/ˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/| vt.|简化，使简易|To make something less complicated or easier to understand.|例句：We need to simplify the instructions so that everyone can understand them.我们需要简化说明，以便每个人都能理解。
例句：The teacher simplified the complex problem for the students.老师为学生简化了复杂的题目。|近义词：abridge, streamline, reduce|反义词：complicate, complexify


USAGE->

He simplifies complex equations for his students. 他为学生简化复杂的方程式。
They simplify their work processes to improve efficiency. 他们简化工作流程以提高效率。
