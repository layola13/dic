contender:/kənˈtɛndər/| n. |竞争者，角逐者 |A contender is someone who competes or is likely to compete for something.  |例句：He's a strong contender for the championship. 他是冠军的有力竞争者。|例句：Several contenders emerged from the primary election. 初选产生了几位竞争者。|近义词：competitor, rival, applicant |反义词：loser, non-competitor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a strong contender for the job. = 他是这份工作有力的竞争者。
She is a contender in the upcoming election. = 她是即将到来的选举中的竞争者。


一般过去时:
He was a contender in last year's race. = 他是去年比赛的竞争者。
She was a serious contender for the award. = 她是该奖项的有力竞争者。


现在完成时:
He has been a contender for many years. = 他多年来一直是竞争者。
She has emerged as a top contender in the field. = 她已成为该领域顶尖的竞争者。


过去完成时:
He had been a contender before he retired. = 他退休前一直是竞争者。
She had already been a contender before the scandal broke. = 丑闻爆发前她就已经是竞争者了。


将来时:
He will be a contender in the next Olympics. = 他将在下一届奥运会上成为竞争者。
She will likely be a contender for the position. = 她很可能会成为这个职位的竞争者。


虚拟语气:
If he were a contender, he would have a better chance. = 如果他是竞争者，他就会有更好的机会。
If she had been a contender, she might have won. = 如果她是竞争者，她可能就赢了。


被动语态:
He was considered a contender for the prize. = 他被认为是该奖项的竞争者。
She was seen as a serious contender by many. = 许多人把她视为一位强大的竞争者。


疑问句:
Is he a contender for the title? = 他是冠军的有力竞争者吗？
Are there any other contenders for the job? = 还有其他竞争者吗？


否定句:
He is not a contender for the presidency. = 他不是总统的竞争者。
She is no longer a contender in the tournament. = 她不再是锦标赛的竞争者了。


PHRASE->
a serious contender =  一位强有力的竞争者
a potential contender = 一位潜在的竞争者
a leading contender = 一位领先的竞争者
the main contender = 主要竞争者
a formidable contender = 一位强大的竞争者
