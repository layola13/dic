schematizer:/ˈskiːməˌtaɪzər/ | n. |  图示器；模式化者；概要制定者 | A schematizer is a person or thing that creates or uses schemata (plural of schema).  It can refer to a tool or process that simplifies complex information into a diagrammatic or symbolic representation.  It can also refer to someone who oversimplifies or stereotypes. | Example: The software acts as a schematizer, transforming raw data into easily understandable charts and graphs.  这个软件充当图示器，将原始数据转换成易于理解的图表。  | 近义词: diagrammatist, modeler, summarizer | 反义词: detailer, explicator |  n.
