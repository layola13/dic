sized:/saɪzd/| adj.| 特定尺寸的；大小合适的 | Having a particular size.  |例句：I need a sized shirt, not one that's too big or too small. 我需要一件合身的衬衫，而不是太大或太小的。|例句：The company offers products in various sized packages. 公司提供各种尺寸的包装产品。|近义词：dimensioned, measured, proportioned |反义词：unsized, shapeless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The box is sized perfectly for the gift. = 这个盒子的大小非常适合这个礼物。
The shoes are sized according to European standards. = 这些鞋子的尺码是按照欧洲标准设计的。


一般过去时:
The tailor sized the jacket for the customer. = 裁缝为顾客量体裁衣。
The artwork was sized down for the brochure. = 这幅作品为宣传册缩小了尺寸。


现在完成时:
The artist has sized the canvas for the painting. = 艺术家已经为绘画画布定好尺寸。
The company has sized its workforce for increased efficiency. = 公司已经调整了员工规模以提高效率。


过去完成时:
They had sized the room before ordering the furniture. = 他们在订购家具之前已经测量了房间的尺寸。
She had sized her portion of the cake before offering some to her friend. = 在给朋友一些蛋糕之前，她已经量好了自己那份的尺寸。


将来时:
We will size the land before beginning construction. = 我们将在开始建设之前测量土地面积。
The designer will size the model for the new dress. = 设计师将为新衣服给模特量尺寸。


虚拟语气:
If I had sized the ingredients correctly, the cake would have turned out better. = 如果我一开始就准确地测量了配料，蛋糕就会做得更好。
I wish I had sized the painting before framing it. = 我真希望我在装裱画之前就量过画的尺寸。


被动语态:
The paintings were sized to fit the gallery walls. = 画作的尺寸已调整以适应画廊的墙壁。
The photographs are being sized for the website. = 照片正在调整尺寸以适合网站。


疑问句:
Is the dress sized correctly? = 这件衣服尺寸正确吗？
Are the ingredients sized accurately? = 配料测量准确吗？


否定句:
The cake wasn't sized properly. = 蛋糕的尺寸不合适。
The shirt isn't sized to fit me. = 这件衬衫的尺寸不适合我。
