rotavating:/ˈroʊteɪtɪŋ/| v.|旋转的；轮换的|Rotating means turning around a central point or axis.  It can also refer to a system where tasks or responsibilities are shared among a group in a regular sequence.|例句：The Earth is constantly rotating on its axis. 地球不断地绕其轴自转。

The company uses a rotating schedule for weekend work. 公司采用轮班制安排周末工作。|近义词：revolving, spinning, cycling|反义词：stationary, still
