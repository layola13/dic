oversentimentalizing: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪmənˈtælˌaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度感伤化；矫揉造作 |  The act of making something excessively sentimental or emotionally charged, often to the point of being artificial or insincere.  It implies an exaggeration of emotion beyond what is natural or appropriate. |例句：He was oversentimentalizing the past, clinging to memories that were better left forgotten. 他对过去过于感伤，执着于那些最好忘记的回忆。

例句：Her oversentimentalizing of the pet's death made others uncomfortable. 她对宠物之死的过度感伤让其他人感到不舒服。


近义词：romanticizing, melodramatizing, sentimentalizing excessively, over-romanticizing


反义词：de-sentimentalizing, rationalizing, downplaying


词性：动词
