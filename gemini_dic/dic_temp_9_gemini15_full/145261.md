overboil:/ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪl/| vt.|煮过头，煮沸过度|To boil for too long, resulting in a loss of flavor, texture, or nutrients.  |例句：Don't overboil the pasta; it will become mushy.  别把意大利面煮过头了；它会变成糊状的。|例句：The potatoes were overboiled and tasted bland. 土豆煮过头了，尝起来很淡。|近义词：overcook, boil excessively|反义词：undercook
