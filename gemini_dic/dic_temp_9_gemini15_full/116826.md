maxillary:/ˈmæksɪləri/| adj.|上颌的，与上颌骨相关的|Relating to the maxilla, the upper jawbone.  The term often refers to structures or processes associated with the upper jaw, such as teeth, sinuses, or nerves.|例句：The maxillary sinus is one of the four paired paranasal sinuses.  上颌窦是四个成对的鼻旁窦之一。

例句：He suffered a fracture of his maxillary bone. 他上颌骨骨折了。


近义词：superior alveolar (referring specifically to the alveolar process of the maxilla)


反义词：mandibular (relating to the mandible, or lower jaw)


词性：形容词
