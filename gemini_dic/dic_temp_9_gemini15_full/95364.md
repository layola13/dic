incessable: /ɪnˈsɛsəbl/ | adj. | 不停的；连续不断的 | unceasing; continuous; without interruption. | The incessant noise from the construction site made it impossible to concentrate.  施工现场持续不断的噪音让人无法集中精力。|近义词：constant, continuous, unending, unrelenting | 反义词：intermittent, occasional, sporadic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine runs incessantly. = 机器不停地运转。
The rain falls incessantly. = 雨不停地下着。

一般过去时:
The phone rang incessantly last night. = 昨晚电话不停地响。
He complained incessantly about the weather. = 他不停地抱怨天气。

现在完成时:
The noise has been incessant all day. = 噪音整天都持续不断。
She has incessantly pursued her goals. = 她一直不懈地追求她的目标。

过去完成时:
The dog had barked incessantly before I finally fell asleep. = 我最终睡着之前，狗一直不停地叫。
They had incessantly worked on the project for weeks. = 他们几个星期以来一直不停地工作在这个项目上。

将来时:
The construction will be incessant for the next few months. = 未来几个月施工将持续不断。
The protests will likely be incessant until the issue is resolved. = 在问题解决之前，抗议活动可能会持续不断。

虚拟语气:
If the rain were incessant, we would have to postpone the picnic. = 如果雨不停地下，我们就得推迟野餐。
I wish the incessant noise would stop. = 我希望持续不断的噪音能停止。

被动语态:
The city was incessantly bombarded with advertisements. = 这个城市不断地受到广告的轰炸。
The problem has been incessantly ignored. = 这个问题一直被忽视。

疑问句:
Is the noise incessant? = 噪音是持续不断的吗？
Will the work be incessant? = 工作会持续不断吗？

否定句:
The work wasn't incessant; there were breaks. = 工作并非持续不断；期间有休息时间。
The music wasn't incessant; it stopped occasionally. = 音乐并非持续不断；它偶尔会停止。
