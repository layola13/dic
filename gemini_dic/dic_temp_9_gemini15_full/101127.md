investigate:/ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪt/| vt. |调查；研究；审查| to carefully examine the facts of a situation, crime, etc. in order to find out the truth  |例句：The police are investigating the murder. 警方正在调查这起谋杀案。

例句：We need to investigate the cause of the problem. 我们需要调查这个问题的原因。


近义词：examine, explore, inquire into, look into| 反义词：ignore, neglect |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police investigate the case carefully. = 警方仔细调查了这个案件。
Scientists investigate the properties of new materials. = 科学家们正在研究新材料的特性。


一般过去时:
Detectives investigated the crime scene thoroughly. = 侦探们彻底调查了犯罪现场。
The committee investigated the allegations of misconduct. = 委员会调查了不当行为的指控。


现在完成时:
They have investigated several possible causes. = 他们已经调查了几个可能的原因。
The authorities have investigated the incident and found no wrongdoing. = 当局已经调查了这一事件，没有发现任何不当行为。


过去完成时:
The investigators had investigated the suspect's alibi before the trial. = 调查人员在审判前已经调查了嫌疑人的不在场证明。
The team had investigated similar cases in the past. = 该团队过去曾调查过类似的案件。


将来时:
The team will investigate the accident thoroughly. = 该团队将彻底调查这起事故。
We will investigate all available options. = 我们将调查所有可行的方案。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would investigate the matter further. = 如果我是你，我会进一步调查此事。
It would be wise to investigate the situation before making a decision. = 在做出决定之前，明智的做法是调查一下情况。


被动语态:
The case is being investigated by the FBI. = 此案正在由联邦调查局调查。
The matter was thoroughly investigated by the committee. = 此事已由委员会彻底调查。


疑问句:
Are you investigating the matter fully? = 你在充分调查此事吗？
Will the authorities investigate this complaint? = 当局会调查这个投诉吗？


否定句:
The police did not investigate the incident properly. = 警方没有妥善调查这起事件。
We have not yet investigated all the possibilities. = 我们还没有调查所有可能性。
