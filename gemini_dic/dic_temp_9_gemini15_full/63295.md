enthusiasms:/ɪnˈθuːziæzəmz/| n.|热情；爱好；热衷的事物|Enthusiasm is a strong feeling of excitement and interest in something.  It often involves a passionate dedication and energy.|例句：Her enthusiasms include gardening and cooking.  她的爱好包括园艺和烹饪。

例句：His enthusiasms were infectious, inspiring others to join his projects.  他的热情具有感染力，激励他人加入他的项目。


近义词： passions, fervor, zeal, excitement|反义词：apathy, indifference, boredom|词性:名词
