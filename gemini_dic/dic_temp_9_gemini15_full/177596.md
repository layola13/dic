rebudgeted:/ˌriːˈbʌdʒɪtɪd/| v. |重新编制预算 |To revise a budget; to allocate funds again.  This implies a previous budget existed and is now being changed.  The changes might involve increasing, decreasing, or reallocating funds to different items. |例句：The project was rebudgeted after unexpected cost overruns. 项目因意外成本超支而重新编制预算。

The company rebudgeted to account for the rise in material costs. 公司为了应对材料成本上涨而重新编制预算。|近义词：revised, reallocated, amended |反义词：finalized, approved (in the context of a budget)
