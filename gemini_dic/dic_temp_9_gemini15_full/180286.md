refreshment:/rɪˈfreʃmənt/ | n. |点心；茶点；提神的东西 | Refreshments are light meals or drinks.  They are often served at meetings, events, or parties.  |例句：The company provided refreshments for the conference attendees. 公司为会议参与者提供了茶点。

例句：We stopped for refreshments during our long road trip.我们在长途旅行中停下来吃点心。


近义词：refreshments, snacks, treats, repast


反义词：None directly applicable, but antonyms could be applied to the *lack* of refreshments, such as deprivation, starvation.


词性：名词
