mantram:/ˈmæntrəm/| n. |真言；咒语；口头禅 |A mantra is a word, sound, or phrase that is repeated to aid concentration in meditation.  It can also refer to a statement or slogan repeated frequently. |例句：She repeated her mantra, "I am strong, I am capable," to calm her nerves. 她重复着她的真言：“我很强大，我很能干”，来平复她的神经。|例句：The company's marketing mantra is "quality first".  这家公司的营销口号是“质量第一”。|近义词：incantation, affirmation, slogan |反义词：silence
