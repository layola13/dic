runlet:/ˈrʌnlət/| n.|小溪，细流|A small stream or brook.  A runlet is a diminutive of run.  It suggests a small, often shallow and swiftly flowing stream.|例句：A tiny runlet trickled down the mountainside.  一条细小的溪流从山坡上涓涓流下。|例句：The children played in the shallow runlet.孩子们在浅浅的小溪里玩耍。|近义词：streamlet, rivulet, brook|反义词：river, torrent|名词
