phagedenic:/ˌfædʒɪˈdɛnɪk/| adj.|噬菌的；腐蚀性的| Relating to or characterized by phagedena, a rapidly spreading ulceration.  The term describes a type of ulceration characterized by rapid, destructive tissue breakdown.  It often involves bacterial infection and can be quite severe.|例句：The wound showed signs of phagedenic ulceration, requiring immediate medical attention.  伤口显示出噬菌性溃疡的迹象，需要立即就医。

The patient presented with a phagedenic lesion on their leg, indicating a severe infection. 病人腿上出现噬菌性病变，表明感染严重。|近义词：necrotic, ulcerative, destructive|反义词：healing, regenerative

词源:
From Greek *phagedaina (φαγαιδαίνη), from *phagein (φαγεῖν) "to eat".  The suffix "-enic" denotes relating to or causing.  Therefore, "phagedenic" literally means "relating to eating away" or "causing to eat away" tissue.
