self-vaunted:/selfˈvɔːntɪd/| adj.|自吹自擂的，自夸的|Self-vaunted refers to something or someone who boasts excessively about their own accomplishments or qualities.  It implies an excessive and often unwarranted pride. |例句：His self-vaunted expertise was quickly exposed as a sham.  他自吹自擂的专业知识很快就被揭露为骗局。


例句：The self-vaunted CEO's promises ultimately failed to materialize.  这位自吹自擂的CEO的承诺最终未能实现。|近义词：boastful, self-aggrandizing, arrogant, conceited|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性：形容词
