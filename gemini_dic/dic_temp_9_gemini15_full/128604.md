nicheing:/nɪtʃɪŋ/| v.| 细分市场化；寻找利基市场；专业化 | The process of finding and exploiting a niche market.  It involves identifying a specific, underserved segment of the market and tailoring products or services to meet their unique needs and preferences.  This can involve focusing on a particular demographic, geographic location, or product feature. | Example:  The company is nicheing its marketing efforts towards young professionals.  这家公司正在将其营销工作细分到年轻专业人士身上。| 近义词：specializing, focusing, targeting | 反义词：generalizing, broadening
