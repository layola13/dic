giga:/ˈɡɪɡə/| n. |吉伽，十亿|A prefix meaning "a billion" (10^9) or, in computing, often 2^30 (1,073,741,824). It is used to denote a billion units of something, especially in terms of data storage and network speed.|例句：A gigabit Ethernet connection offers speeds of up to one billion bits per second. 千兆以太网连接提供高达每秒十亿位的速度。  A gigahertz is one billion cycles per second. 吉赫兹是每秒十亿个周期。|近义词：billion (as a numerical prefix)|反义词：None (as a prefix)|名词
