dingies:/ˈdɪŋɡiz/| n.|小帆船，小艇|A small, open sailboat, often used for recreational purposes.  Dinghies are typically simple in design and easy to handle.|例句：We sailed our dinghy across the lake. 我们划着小帆船横渡了湖泊。
例句：The yacht had a small dinghy for getting ashore. 那艘游艇有一艘小艇用来上岸。|近义词：boat, rowboat, tender|反义词：yacht, ship
