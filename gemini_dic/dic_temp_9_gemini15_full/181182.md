reinitiate:/ˌriːɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ | vt. |重新开始；再次启动 | To start something again after it has stopped or been interrupted.  |例句：The company decided to reinitiate the project after a brief hiatus. 公司决定在短暂中断后重新启动该项目。|例句：After the power outage, we had to reinitiate the computer system. 停电后，我们不得不重新启动计算机系统。|近义词：restart, recommence, resume |反义词：terminate, end, cease |动词
