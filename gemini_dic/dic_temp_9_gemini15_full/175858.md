rat-arsed:/ˈrætˈɑːrsd/ | adj. | 烂醉的，喝醉的 | Extremely drunk; intoxicated.  The term is highly informal and considered vulgar. | 例句：He was rat-arsed after downing five pints. = 他喝了五品脱啤酒后烂醉如泥。


例句：She was completely rat-arsed at the party and couldn't remember getting home. = 她在派对上喝得酩酊大醉，记不得怎么回家的了。


近义词：smashed, pissed, blotto, plastered, drunk, inebriated


反义词：sober


词性:形容词
