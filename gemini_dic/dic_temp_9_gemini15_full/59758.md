economicus:/ˌɛkəˈnɒmɪkəs/|/n.|经济人，理性经济人|A hypothetical person who is perfectly rational and self-interested, making decisions to maximize their own utility.  This concept is used in economics to model human behavior. |Example: The model assumes all participants are *economicus*, maximizing their own profit.|近义词：rational actor, utility maximizer|反义词：altruist, irrational actor
