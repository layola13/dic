half-doors:/ˈhɑːfˌdɔːrz/|/ˈhæfˌdɔrz/| n. |半门；车门下半部 |Half-doors are doors that only cover the lower portion of an opening, often found in vehicles like Jeeps or convertibles. They provide some protection from the elements while allowing for easy entry and exit.|The Jeep Wrangler is known for its removable half-doors.|近义词：partial doors, stub doors|反义词：full doors
