handloom:/ˈhændluːm/ | n. | 手织机；手织布 | A loom operated by hand, as opposed to a power loom; also, cloth woven on such a loom.  | Example: The artisan skillfully worked the handloom, creating intricate patterns in the fabric.  这位工匠熟练地操作着手工织机，在织物上创造出复杂的图案。|近义词：hand-woven fabric, manually operated loom | 反义词：power loom
