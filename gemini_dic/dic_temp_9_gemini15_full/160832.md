possessive: /pəˈzɛsɪv/ | adj. | 所有格的；占有的；独占欲强的 | Showing or characterized by possessiveness.  Relating to or denoting grammatical possession. |  例句：He has a possessive attitude towards his girlfriend. 他对女朋友占有欲很强。


例句：The possessive pronoun "his" indicates ownership.  所有格代词“his”表示所有权。


近义词：owning, proprietary, jealous, covetous | 反义词：unselfish, generous, detached


词源:  From possessive (adjective) + -ive (suffix) deriving from Latin *possessīvus, from *possidēre "to sit in possession of"


用法说明:

作为形容词：

1. 指人的性格特点：表示对某人或某物有强烈的占有欲，不希望被分享或失去。

例句：Her possessive nature made it difficult for her to share her toys.  她占有欲很强，很难分享她的玩具。

例句：He exhibited possessive behavior, constantly checking her phone. 他表现出占有欲很强的行为，不断检查她的手机。


2. 指语法上的所有格：指表示所有关系的词或词组，例如名词所有格 ('s) 或形容词性物主代词 (my, your, his, her, its, our, their)。

例句：The dog's bone was buried in the garden. 狗的骨头埋在花园里。(名词所有格)

例句：This is my book. 这是我的书。(形容词性物主代词)


作为名词 (较少见):

指占有欲或拥有权的行为或状态。

例句：His possessiveness drove her away. 他的占有欲把她赶走了。

例句：The possessiveness shown by the older brother towards his younger sister is concerning. 哥哥对妹妹表现出的占有欲令人担忧。
