resilient: /rɪˈzɪliənt/ | adj. | 顽强的；有弹性的；适应力强的 | Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.  | 例句：The resilient economy quickly recovered from the recession.  经济复苏迅速地从衰退中恢复过来。


例句：She showed remarkable resilience in the face of adversity. 她面对逆境展现出非凡的韧性。


近义词：tough, strong, durable, adaptable | 反义词：fragile, weak, vulnerable


词源:  The word "resilient" originates from the Latin word "resilio," meaning "to leap back" or "to spring back."  This reflects the sense of bouncing back from adversity.
