corollifloral: /ˌkɒrɒˈlɪflɔːrəl/ | adj. | 冠状花(的)；轮状花冠的 | Relating to or characteristic of flowers with a corolla.  The term refers to flowers having petals united into a tube or cup. | The corollifloral arrangement of the petals in this flower makes it easily identifiable. 这朵花的冠状花排列使其易于识别。 |近义词：petalous (having petals), gamopetalous (having petals united) |反义词：apetalous (lacking petals), polypetalous (having separate petals)
