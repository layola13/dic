collaboration: /kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |合作，协作 |The act of working with someone or a group of people to achieve a common goal.  It involves sharing ideas, resources, and responsibilities. |例句：The success of the project was due to the excellent collaboration between the teams.  这个项目的成功归功于各团队之间的出色合作。

例句：The artist's latest work is a collaboration with a renowned architect.  这位艺术家的最新作品是与一位著名建筑师合作完成的。


近义词：cooperation, teamwork, joint effort |反义词：competition, antagonism


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collaborate on several projects each year. = 他们每年合作几个项目。
We collaborate closely with our partners. = 我们与合作伙伴紧密合作。


一般过去时:
The two companies collaborated on a new product. = 这两家公司合作开发了一款新产品。
They collaborated successfully on the research paper. = 他们成功地合作完成了这篇研究论文。


现在完成时:
We have collaborated with them on many occasions. = 我们已经和他们合作过很多次了。
They have collaborated extensively in the field of medicine. = 他们在医学领域进行了广泛的合作。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
If we collaborated more effectively, we would achieve better results. = 如果我们更有效地合作，我们将取得更好的成果。
I wish we had collaborated more closely on that project. = 我希望我们在那个项目上合作更紧密一些。


被动语态:
