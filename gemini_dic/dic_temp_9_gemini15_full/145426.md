overcharges:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒɪz/ | v. | 索要过高价钱；过度收费 | To charge (someone) more than is fair or legal.  |例句：The hotel overcharged me for the room.  酒店房间收费过高。

例句：The mechanic overcharged me for the repairs.  修理工向我收取了过高的修理费。


近义词：overbill,  gouge,  exorbitantly charge | 反义词：undercharge
