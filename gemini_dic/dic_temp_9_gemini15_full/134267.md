noninfluence: /ˌnɒnˈɪnflʊəns/ | n. | 无影响，不起作用 | The lack of influence; the state of not having influence.  This word is relatively rare and often used in specialized contexts, particularly in academic writing. It emphasizes the absence of any impact or effect. | Example:  The study showed a noninfluence of the new policy on consumer behavior. 这项研究表明新政策对消费者行为没有影响。 | 近义词: lack of influence, ineffectiveness, impotence | 反义词: influence, impact, effect | 名词
