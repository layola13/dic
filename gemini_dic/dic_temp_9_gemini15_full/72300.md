flounder:/ˈflaʊndər/| v. n.|比目鱼；挣扎；笨拙地行动；失败|to struggle or move clumsily; to fail or be unsuccessful.  |The fish floundered in the shallow water. 这条鱼在浅水中挣扎。 He floundered in his attempt to explain. 他试图解释，但失败了。|struggle, stumble, falter|succeed, thrive|动词，名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He flounders in his speech. 他说话结结巴巴。
The ship flounders in the storm. 船在暴风雨中挣扎。


一般过去时:
The project floundered due to lack of funding.  项目由于缺乏资金而失败。
She floundered in the deep snow. 她在厚厚的雪地里挣扎。


现在完成时:
The company has floundered since the CEO resigned. 自从首席执行官辞职以来，公司一直举步维艰。
He has floundered through life without a clear goal. 他在生活中没有明确的目标，一直苦苦挣扎。


过去完成时:
The negotiations had floundered before they even began. 谈判甚至还没开始就失败了。
The team had floundered for months before finding a solution.  这个团队在找到解决方案之前已经挣扎了几个月。


将来时:
The business will flounder without more investment.  如果没有更多的投资，这家企业将会失败。
I fear that he will flounder in his new job. 我担心他会在新工作中举步维艰。


虚拟语气:
If he had planned better, he wouldn't have floundered. 如果他计划得更好，就不会失败了。
I wish I hadn't floundered so badly in the interview. 我真希望我在面试中不要表现得那么糟糕。


被动语态:
The plan was floundered by unexpected events. 计划因意外事件而失败。
The argument was floundered by a lack of evidence.  由于缺乏证据，论证失败了。


疑问句:
Why did the negotiations flounder? 谈判为什么失败了？
Is the company floundering financially? 这家公司在经济上是否举步维艰？


否定句:
The company didn't flounder despite the economic downturn.  尽管经济低迷，公司并没有失败。
He didn't flounder on the stage; he delivered a confident speech. 他在舞台上没有表现得笨拙；他发表了一篇自信的演讲。


PHRASE->

flounder about:  笨拙地乱动

flounder in:  在……中挣扎
flounder on:  继续挣扎；勉强继续
flounder through:  艰难地完成
