goggles:/ˈɡɑːɡlz/| n.|护目镜，防护眼镜|Protective eyewear that covers the eyes and often the surrounding area, shielding them from impact, dust, chemicals, or other hazards.  They may be made of plastic, glass, or other materials. |例句：I need to wear goggles when I'm working with chemicals.  我使用化学药品时需要戴护目镜。

例句：The welder wore goggles to protect his eyes from the intense light. 焊接工戴着护目镜保护眼睛不受强光的伤害。


近义词：eye protection, safety glasses, spectacles (in some contexts) |反义词：None.

词性:Noun
