by-election:/ˈbaɪ ɪˈlɛkʃən/| n. | 补选 |A by-election is an election held to fill a political office that has become vacant between general elections.  This can be due to the death, resignation, or expulsion of the incumbent officeholder. |The by-election for the local council seat attracted a surprisingly large turnout.  这次地方议会席位的补选吸引了令人惊讶的大量选民。|  general election, special election |  general election (in the sense of a regularly scheduled election)|
