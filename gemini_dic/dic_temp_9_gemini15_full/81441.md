gratified:/ˈɡrædəfaɪd/ | adj. | 感到满足的；感到欣慰的 | Feeling pleasure or satisfaction; pleased.  |例句：I was gratified by the positive response to my proposal.  我对我提案的积极回应感到满意。

例句：She was gratified to see her hard work pay off. 她很欣慰地看到她的努力得到了回报。


近义词：pleased, satisfied, delighted, contented, fulfilled | 反义词：disappointed, dissatisfied, frustrated, unhappy


词性：形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gratified by the news. = 他对这个消息感到满意。
She is gratified with her achievements. = 她对自己的成就感到满意。

一般过去时:
He was gratified by her kindness. = 他对她善良的行为感到满意。
They were gratified to see their child succeed. = 他们看到他们的孩子成功感到很欣慰。

现在完成时:
I have been gratified by your support. = 我一直对你们的支持感到满意。
She has been gratified by the positive feedback. = 她一直对积极的反馈感到满意。

过去完成时:
He had been gratified to learn of his promotion. = 他得知自己升职后感到很欣慰。
We had been gratified by the outcome of the project. = 我们对项目的成果感到很满意。

将来时:
I will be gratified to see the final product. = 我将对最终产品感到满意。
They will be gratified by the results of the research. = 他们将对研究结果感到满意。

虚拟语气:
I would be gratified if you could attend. = 如果你能来，我会很高兴。
She would have been gratified if her son had followed her advice. = 如果她的儿子听了她的建议，她会很欣慰。

被动语态:
His efforts were gratified by the team's success. = 他的努力因团队的成功而得到了回报（感到满意）。
The audience was gratified by the performance. = 观众对演出感到满意。

疑问句:
Are you gratified with your progress? = 你对你的进步感到满意吗？
Is she gratified by the outcome? = 她对结果感到满意吗？

否定句:
He was not gratified by the results. = 他对结果并不满意。
I am not gratified with the current situation. = 我对目前的情况并不满意。


PHRASE->
be gratified to do sth  = 对做某事感到满意
feel gratified = 感到欣慰
deeply gratified = 深感欣慰
to one's gratification = 令某人欣慰的是
gratified by = 对...感到满意


词源:
The word "gratified" comes from the Latin word "gratia," meaning "grace, favor, gratitude."  It entered English through the Old French word "gratifier," meaning "to please, to satisfy."  The suffix "-fied" indicates a state of being.
