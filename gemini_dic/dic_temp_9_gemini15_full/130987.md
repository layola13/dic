nonatomic: /ˌnɑːnəˈtɒmɪk/ | adj. | 非原子性的 |  In Objective-C, `nonatomic` is an attribute used with properties to specify that access to the property will not be atomic.  This means that multiple threads can access and modify the property simultaneously, potentially leading to data races if not carefully managed.  The opposite is `atomic`, which guarantees thread safety but may impact performance. |


例句：

`@property (nonatomic, strong) NSString *name;` =  声明一个名为name的NSString类型的属性，该属性是非原子性的并且使用强引用。

`@property (nonatomic) int counter;` = 声明一个名为counter的整型属性，该属性是非原子性的。


近义词： non-atomic (less common in Objective-C context)

反义词： atomic

词性: adjective (形容词)
