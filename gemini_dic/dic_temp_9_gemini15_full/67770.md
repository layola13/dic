eyeing:/ˈaɪɪŋ/| v.|注视，打量，窥视|Looking at someone or something with a particular feeling or intention, often suggesting scrutiny, desire, or suspicion.|例句：The cat sat eyeing the bird. 猫坐着注视着那只鸟。  The businessman eyed his competitor suspiciously. 那位商人狐疑地打量着他的竞争对手。|近义词：watching, observing, scrutinizing, glancing|反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is eyeing the new car. = 他正在打量那辆新车。
She eyes the painting with admiration. = 她带着欣赏的目光注视着这幅画。


一般过去时:
They eyed each other cautiously. = 他们谨慎地互相打量着。
He eyed the menu, considering his options. = 他看着菜单，考虑着他的选择。


现在完成时:
She has been eyeing that house for months. = 她已经关注那栋房子好几个月了。
He has eyed several investment opportunities. = 他已经考察了好几个投资机会。


过去完成时:
I had been eyeing that job for a long time before applying. = 在申请之前，我已经关注那份工作很久了。
They had eyed the situation carefully before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前仔细观察了情况。


将来时:
She will be eyeing the competition closely. = 她将密切关注竞争对手。
He will eye the market for new opportunities. = 他将密切关注市场寻找新的机会。


虚拟语气:
If I eyed the situation differently, I might have acted differently. = 如果我当时对情况的看法不同，我的行为可能就会不一样。
I wish I hadn't eyed him so suspiciously. = 我真希望我没有那么狐疑地看他。


被动语态:  The painting was eyed with awe by the visitors. = 参观者们带着敬畏的目光注视着这幅画。(被动语态用法较少见)
The situation was being eyed with concern by the experts. = 专家们正担忧地关注着局势。(被动语态用法较少见)


疑问句:
Were they eyeing the prize? = 他们当时在盯着奖品吗？
Is she eyeing a promotion? = 她正在争取晋升吗？


否定句:
He didn't eye the stranger suspiciously. = 他没有狐疑地打量那个陌生人。
They didn't eye the opportunity. = 他们没有关注那个机会。
