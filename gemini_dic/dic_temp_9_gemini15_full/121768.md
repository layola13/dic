misquotes:/ˌmɪsˈkwoʊts/ | n. | 误引用；错误的引语 | An inaccurate or misleading quotation of someone's words.  | Example:  The article contained several misquotes of the politician's speech. 这篇文章包含了几处对这位政治家讲话的误引用。 |近义词：distortions, misrepresentations, paraphrases (inaccurate) | 反义词：accurate quotes, verbatim transcriptions | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newspaper often misquotes the sources. = 报纸经常误引用消息来源。
He frequently misquotes famous authors. = 他经常误引用著名作家的作品。


一般过去时:
The journalist misquoted the interviewee in the article. = 记者在文章中误引用了受访者的说法。
She misquoted the scripture during her sermon. = 她在布道中误引用了圣经。


现在完成时:
The blog has misquoted my statement several times. = 该博客已经多次曲解我的陈述。
They have misquoted the study's findings to support their argument. = 他们歪曲了研究结果来支持他们的论点。


过去完成时:
He realized he had misquoted the historical figure in his essay. = 他意识到他在论文中误引用了历史人物的话。
The speaker discovered she had misquoted the poet in her presentation. = 演讲者发现她在演讲中误引用了诗人的诗句。


将来时:

She worries that the media will misquote her comments. = 她担心媒体会曲解她的评论。


虚拟语气:
If I knew they would misquote me, I would not have spoken. = 如果我知道他们会曲解我的话，我就不应该说话了。
I wish the reporter hadn't misquoted my interview. = 我希望记者没有曲解我的采访。


被动语态:
His words were frequently misquoted in the media. = 他的话经常被媒体误引用。
The statement was misquoted by several online publications. = 这句话被几家在线出版物误引用了。


疑问句:
Were any of the quotes misquoted in the biography? = 传记中有没有任何引语被误引用？
Has the author misquoted any of the original sources? = 作者是否误引用了任何原始资料？


否定句:
The article did not misquote the professor's findings. = 这篇文章没有误引用教授的研究结果。
The reporter made sure not to misquote the witness's testimony. = 记者确保没有误引用证人的证词。
