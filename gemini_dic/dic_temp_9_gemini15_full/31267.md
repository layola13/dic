chapattis:/tʃəˈpætɪz/| n. |印度薄饼|Unleavened flatbreads made from whole wheat flour, common in South Asia.  They are typically cooked on a hot griddle or pan.|例句：For dinner, we had delicious chicken curry served with chapattis.  晚餐我们吃了美味的鸡肉咖喱配印度薄饼。|例句：She rolled out the dough thinly to make perfect chapattis. 她把面团擀得很薄，做出了完美的印度薄饼。|近义词：roti, flatbread|反义词：无
