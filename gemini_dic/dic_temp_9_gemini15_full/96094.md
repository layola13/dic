indefensible:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsɪbl/| adj. |不可辩护的，无法辩解的 |Unable to be defended or justified.  Something indefensible is so wrong or unreasonable that there is no way to defend it. |例句：His actions were completely indefensible.  他的行为完全不可辩护。

例句：The government's policy on immigration is indefensible. 政府的移民政策是不可辩护的。


近义词：unjustifiable, inexcusable, unpardonable, unwarranted


反义词：justifiable, defensible, excusable


词性：形容词
