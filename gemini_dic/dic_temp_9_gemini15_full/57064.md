doubts:/daʊts/| n.|怀疑，疑问|Uncertainty or lack of conviction; a feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction.|例句：He had doubts about the project's feasibility. 他对项目的可行性表示怀疑。|例句：My doubts were confirmed when I saw the results. 当我看到结果时，我的怀疑得到了证实。|近义词：suspicions, uncertainties, misgivings|反义词：certainties, convictions


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I doubt his honesty. = 我怀疑他的诚实。
She doubts her ability to succeed. = 她怀疑自己能否成功。

一般过去时:
I doubted his word. = 我不相信他的话。
They doubted the validity of the claim. = 他们怀疑该说法是否有效。

现在完成时:
I have doubted his motives for a long time. = 我很久以来就怀疑他的动机。
She has doubted her decision since she made it. = 自从她做出决定以来，她就一直对其感到怀疑。

过去完成时:
I had doubted his intentions before he revealed them. = 在他透露意图之前，我就已经怀疑他的意图了。
They had doubted the accuracy of the data before the re-analysis. = 在重新分析之前，他们就怀疑数据的准确性。

将来时:
I will doubt his story until I see proof. = 在我看到证据之前，我会怀疑他的故事。
She will doubt the outcome until the results are announced. = 在结果公布之前，她会怀疑结果。

虚拟语气:
I would doubt his competence if I hadn't seen his work. = 如果我没有看到他的作品，我会怀疑他的能力。
If I were you, I would doubt everything he says. = 如果我是你，我会怀疑他说的每一句话。

被动语态:
His honesty is doubted by many. = 许多人怀疑他的诚实。
The validity of the study was doubted by the scientific community. = 科学界对这项研究的有效性表示怀疑。

疑问句:
Do you have any doubts about this plan? = 你对这个计划有任何疑问吗？
Does she have doubts about her future? = 她对未来有什么疑问吗？

否定句:
I don't doubt his sincerity. = 我不怀疑他的真诚。
She doesn't doubt his capabilities. = 她不怀疑他的能力。


PHRASE->
have doubts about = 对…有疑问
raise doubts = 引起怀疑
cast doubt on = 对…表示怀疑
beyond a doubt = 毫无疑问地
in doubt = 令人怀疑的
