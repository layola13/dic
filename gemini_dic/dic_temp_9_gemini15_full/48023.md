decisionmaking: /dɪˈsɪʒənˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 决策；决策过程 | The process of making decisions, especially important ones that involve a series of steps or considerations.  It often involves identifying a problem, gathering information, evaluating options, and selecting a course of action. |  The company's decision-making process is notoriously slow. 公司的决策过程出了名的慢。

The board needs to improve its decision-making. 董事会需要改进其决策能力。|  decision-making process, strategic decision-making, effective decision-making, rational decision-making,  group decision-making | indecision, procrastination | noun
