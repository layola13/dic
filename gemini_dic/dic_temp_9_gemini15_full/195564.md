semipopularly: /ˌsɛmiˈpɑːpjələrli/ | adv. | 半普及地；比较流行地 |  Describes something that is popular to a certain extent, but not widely or universally so. It suggests a level of popularity that is somewhere between popular and niche. | Example: The band's music was semipopularly received, achieving modest chart success but not widespread fame.  乐队的音乐获得了中等程度的欢迎，取得了适度的排行榜成功，但没有获得广泛的知名度。| 近义词：moderately popular, fairly popular | 反义词：unpopular, obscure, niche
