radiochemist:/ˈreɪdiəʊˈkɛmɪst/| n. |放射化学家|A scientist who specializes in radiochemistry, the branch of chemistry dealing with radioactive substances.  Radiochemists study the chemical behavior of radioactive isotopes and their applications in various fields. |She is a renowned radiochemist who has made significant contributions to nuclear medicine. 她是一位著名的放射化学家，对核医学做出了重大贡献。|nuclear chemist, radiation chemist| (no direct antonym)|名词
