nose-out: /noʊz aʊt/ | adj. | 机头朝外的；突出；显眼 |  Describes something, typically a vehicle or its part, projecting or extending outwards, particularly the front part.  It can also describe something prominent or noticeable.  | 例句：The car was nose-out in the parking lot. 这辆车在停车场里机头朝外停着。 The new design has a nose-out feature. 新的设计有一个突出的特征。 | 近义词：protruding, projecting, prominent, conspicuous | 反义词：recessed, hidden, inconspicuous |  adj.
