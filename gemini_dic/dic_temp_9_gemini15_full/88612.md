hives:/haɪvz/| n.|蜂箱；荨麻疹；|A hive is a structure where bees live and store honey.  Hives can also refer to a skin rash characterized by raised, itchy welts.|例句：The bees were busy working inside their hive. 蜜蜂们在蜂箱里忙碌地工作着。

The allergic reaction caused a severe case of hives all over his body. 过敏反应导致他全身起了严重的荨麻疹。|近义词：beehive, apiary, urticaria|反义词：None specifically
