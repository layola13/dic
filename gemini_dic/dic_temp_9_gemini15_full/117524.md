meet:/miːt/|vt.&vi.|遇见，会面，满足|To come together, to encounter someone or something, or to fulfill a requirement or expectation.|例句：I met my friend at the cafe. 我在咖啡馆遇见了我的朋友。  I'm meeting the new boss tomorrow. 我明天要会见新老板。|近义词：encounter, encounter,join,  satisfy|反义词：part, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I meet my friends every week. = 我每周都见我的朋友们。
We meet at the library to study. = 我们在图书馆见面学习。

一般过去时:
I met him yesterday. = 我昨天遇见了他。
They met at a conference last year. = 他们去年在一个会议上相遇了。

现在完成时:
I have met her several times. = 我已经见过她好几次了。
We have met all the requirements. = 我们已经满足了所有要求。

过去完成时:
I had met him once before. = 我以前见过他一次。
They had met their quota by the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了他们的配额。

将来时:
I will meet you at the station. = 我将在车站与你会面。
We will meet to discuss the project. = 我们将开会讨论这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If I met him, I would ask for his help. = 如果我遇见他，我会请求他的帮助。
I wish I had met her earlier. = 我真希望我早点遇到她。

被动语态:
The deadline was met. = 最后期限被满足了。
The requirements were met. = 要求得到了满足。

疑问句:
Did you meet your goal? = 你达到你的目标了吗？
Have you met the new teacher? = 你见过新老师了吗？

否定句:
I didn't meet anyone interesting. = 我没遇到任何有趣的人。
We haven't met our sales target yet. = 我们还没有达到我们的销售目标。


PHRASE->
meet the needs  满足需求
meet the requirements 满足要求
meet the deadline 赶上最后期限
meet with sb 会见某人
meet one's demands 满足某人的要求
