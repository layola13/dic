mingling:/ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/| n. |混合；交往；融合|The act of mixing or blending together; social interaction or mixing with others.  |例句：The mingling of cultures created a vibrant atmosphere. 文化的融合创造了充满活力的氛围。|例句：There was a lot of mingling at the party. 派对上很多人在互相交流。|近义词：mixing, blending, intermingling, associating|反义词：separation, segregation, isolation|名词
