mapach:/ˈmɑːpætʃ/| n. |浣熊|A raccoon is a medium-sized mammal belonging to the family Procyonidae.  They are known for their masked faces, dexterous paws, and nocturnal habits.  They are omnivores with a diverse diet. |Example: The mapach raided the garbage cans last night.  昨晚浣熊袭击了垃圾桶。|近义词：raccoon|反义词：无
