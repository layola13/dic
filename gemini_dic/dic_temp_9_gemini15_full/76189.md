fusspots:/ˈfʌsˌpɒts/ | n. |  挑剔鬼，过分讲究的人 | A fusspot is a person who is excessively concerned with minor details and often complains about them. They are often seen as overly meticulous and fussy.  | He's such a fusspot about the arrangement of his books. 他对书的摆放过于讲究。  She's a bit of a fusspot when it comes to cleaning.  她在打扫卫生方面有点挑剔。 |  nitpicker, perfectionist, stickler | easygoing, nonchalant | 名词
