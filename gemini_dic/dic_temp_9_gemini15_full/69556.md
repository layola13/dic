fellowship:/ˈfɛloʊʃɪp/ | n. |伙伴关系；奖学金；协会；群体|

英文解析:Fellowship refers to a feeling of friendship and camaraderie, often within a group sharing common interests or goals. It can also refer to a financial award given to support a student's or researcher's studies or work, or to a group of people associated together for a particular purpose.

例句:
The two scientists enjoyed a close fellowship built on years of collaboration.  = 这两位科学家因多年的合作而建立了密切的伙伴关系。

He received a fellowship to study abroad. = 他获得了一笔奖学金去国外留学。


近义词: companionship, scholarship, grant, association, society, community

反义词: isolation, estrangement, antagonism
