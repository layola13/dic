open-hearted:/ˈoʊpənˈhɑːtɪd/ | adj. |坦诚的；慷慨的；宽宏大量的 | Open-hearted describes someone who is honest, generous, and kind, readily showing their feelings and intentions.  It implies a lack of deceit and a willingness to be vulnerable. | 例句：She gave an open-hearted speech about her struggles. 她发表了一篇坦诚地讲述她奋斗历程的演讲。  | 例句：He was open-hearted enough to admit his mistakes. 他坦诚地承认了自己的错误。 | 近义词：candid, frank, generous, magnanimous, benevolent | 反义词：close-fisted, secretive, deceitful, mean-spirited, ungenerous
