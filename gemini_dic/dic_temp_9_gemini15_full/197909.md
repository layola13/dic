shirt:/ʃɜːrt/|n.|衬衫|A garment worn on the upper body, typically having a collar, sleeves, and buttons down the front.|例句：He wore a white shirt and a dark tie. 他穿着一件白色衬衫和一条深色领带。|例句：She bought a new silk shirt for the party. 为了参加派对，她买了一件新的丝绸衬衫。|近义词：blouse, top|反义词：None|名词
