lamp:/læmp/| n.|灯，油灯|A device that produces light, typically using electricity or a flame.|例句：She switched on the lamp. 她打开了灯。|例句：The old lamp was made of brass. 这盏旧灯是用黄铜做的。|近义词：light, luminary|反义词：darkness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lamp illuminates the room. = 这盏灯照亮了房间。
The lamps shine brightly. = 灯光明亮地照耀着。


一般过去时:
The lamp shone brightly last night. = 昨晚灯很亮。
He switched off the lamp before going to bed. = 他睡觉前关掉了灯。


现在完成时:
