resultative:/rɪˈzʌltətɪv/| adj.|结果的；结果性的；效果的 |Describing a verb, adjective, or phrase that indicates the result or effect of an action.  It often involves a complement that describes the state achieved after the action.  |例句：He hammered the metal flat. (The metal being flat is the resultative complement.) 他把金属敲平了。(金属变平是结果补语。)

例句：She painted the door red. (The door being red is the resultative complement.) 她把门漆成了红色。(门变红是结果补语。)


近义词：consequential, effective, resultant |反义词：ineffective, fruitless, unproductive |词性：adj.
