encroachments:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃmənts/ | n. | 侵犯；蚕食；侵占 | Acts of encroaching or intruding; gradual advances or incursions.  Encroachments can refer to physical space, rights, or other areas.  |例句：The construction of the new highway led to encroachments on the neighboring farmland. 新高速公路的建设侵占了邻近的农田。

The government's new policies represent significant encroachments on individual liberties. 政府的新政策是对个人自由的重大侵犯。


近义词：intrusions, infringements, invasions, usurpations | 反义词：cessions, withdrawals, relinquishments | 词性：名词
