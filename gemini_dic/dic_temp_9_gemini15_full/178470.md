reconstructively:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌktɪvli/| adv.|重建地；复原地|In a way that involves reconstruction or rebuilding.  The process aims to understand and rebuild something, often after damage or destruction.  It implies a systematic and methodical approach to restoring something to its original state or a functional equivalent.|例句：The city was reconstructively rebuilt after the earthquake.  地震后，这座城市得到了重建。

The historical documents were reconstructively analyzed to understand the past events. 历史文献经过重建性分析以了解过去发生的事件。|近义词：restoratively, regeneratively|反义词：destructively, disruptively
