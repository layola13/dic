run-through: /ˈrʌnθruː/ | n. | 走查；排练；练习；概述 | A rehearsal or practice session, often for a performance or presentation; a quick review or summary.  |例句：The band did a run-through of the new song before the concert. 乐队在音乐会前对新歌进行了一次排练。

例句：Let's have a quick run-through of the presentation before we start. 我们在开始之前快速浏览一下演示文稿吧。


近义词：rehearsal, practice, review, overview | 反义词：final performance, finished product | 词性: noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band has a run-through every morning. = 乐队每天早上进行排练。
We do a run-through of the plan before implementation. = 我们在实施计划之前会先进行演练。

一般过去时:
The actors had a run-through yesterday afternoon. = 演员们昨天下午进行了一次排练。
They did a run-through of the script before the filming. = 他们在拍摄前对剧本进行了一次走查。

现在完成时:
They have completed several run-throughs of the play. = 他们已经完成了该剧的几次排练。
We have had a run-through of the presentation, and we are ready to go. = 我们已经对演示文稿进行了排练，准备好了。


过去完成时:
They had already had a run-through before the director arrived. = 在导演到达之前他们已经进行过一次排练了。
We had completed a run-through of the emergency procedures. = 我们已经完成了应急程序的演练。


将来时:
We will have a run-through of the project tomorrow. = 我们明天将对项目进行一次演练。
The orchestra will have a run-through before the concert. = 管弦乐团将在音乐会前进行一次彩排。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have another run-through. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会再进行一次排练。
I wish we had done a run-through before the presentation. = 我希望我们在演示之前进行过一次排练。


被动语态:
The presentation was given a run-through by the team. = 演示文稿由团队进行了走查。
The script was given a thorough run-through before filming began. = 剧本在开始拍摄前进行了彻底的走查。


疑问句:
Will we have a run-through before the performance? = 我们会在演出前进行彩排吗？
Have they had a run-through of the new material yet? = 他们已经对新材料进行过排练了吗？


否定句:
We didn't have time for a run-through this morning. = 我们今天早上没有时间进行排练。
They didn't do a run-through of the music before the performance. = 他们在演出前没有进行音乐排练。
