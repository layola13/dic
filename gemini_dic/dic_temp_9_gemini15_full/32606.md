chirp:/tʃɜːrp/| /tʃɝp/| n., v.|唧唧叫声；吱吱叫；发出啁啾声|A short, high-pitched sound made by birds or insects.|The crickets chirped all night.  蟋蟀整夜都在唧唧叫。|The bird chirped a cheerful tune.  鸟儿欢快地鸣叫。|twitter, tweet, peep|shout, roar|n., v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds chirp merrily in the morning.  鸟儿在清晨快乐地鸣叫。
The crickets chirp throughout the night. 蟋蟀整夜都在鸣叫。


一般过去时:
The sparrow chirped a sweet song yesterday.  昨天麻雀唱了一首甜美的歌。
The insects chirped loudly near the river.  昆虫在河边大声鸣叫。


现在完成时:
The birds have chirped all day long. 鸟儿整天都在鸣叫。
The cicadas have chirped incessantly this summer.  这个夏天蝉一直在不停地鸣叫。


过去完成时:
The birds had chirped before the sunrise.  日出前鸟儿就已经开始鸣叫了。
The crickets had chirped all through the previous night. 前一晚蟋蟀鸣叫了一整夜。


将来时:
The birds will chirp sweetly tomorrow morning. 明天早上鸟儿会甜美地鸣叫。
The crickets will chirp loudly after the rain. 雨后蟋蟀会大声鸣叫。


虚拟语气:
If the birds chirped louder, we would hear them. 如果鸟叫得更响亮，我们就能听到。
I wish the birds chirped more often. 我希望鸟儿能更经常地鸣叫。


被动语态:
A cheerful sound was chirped by the robin. 知更鸟发出欢快的鸣叫声。
A high-pitched sound was chirped by the insect.  昆虫发出高亢的叫声。


疑问句:
Did the birds chirp this morning?  今天早上鸟儿鸣叫了吗？
Do the crickets chirp at night? 蟋蟀晚上鸣叫吗？


否定句:
The birds didn't chirp this morning.  今天早上鸟儿没有鸣叫。
The insects didn't chirp loudly last night.  昨晚昆虫没有大声鸣叫。


PHRASE->
