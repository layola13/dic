punchiness:/ˈpʌntʃinɛs/ | n. | 简洁有力；活泼；有力 |  The quality of being concise and forceful; energetic and lively.  It suggests a strong impact or effect, often in a brief and memorable way. | 例句：The speech lacked punchiness; it needed more forceful delivery.  演讲缺乏力度；需要更有力的表达方式。

The article's punchiness made it engaging and easy to read. 这篇文章的简洁有力使其引人入胜且易于阅读。


近义词：forcefulness, vigor, energy, impact, brevity, conciseness | 反义词：weakness, dullness, vagueness, rambling, prolixity
