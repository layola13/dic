outtravelling: /ˌaʊtˈtrævəlɪŋ/ | v. | 超越旅行；旅行范围更广 |  This word is not a standard dictionary entry. It's a newly coined word or a very rare usage, formed by combining "out-" (meaning surpassing or exceeding) and "travelling" (the act of traveling). It implies exceeding someone else in the extent or scope of one's travels.  |  例句：He boasted about outtravelling his friends, having visited every continent. 他吹嘘自己比朋友们旅行的范围更广，因为去过七大洲。 | 近义词:  surpassing in travel, exceeding in exploration | 反义词:  limited travel, restricted exploration | 动词
