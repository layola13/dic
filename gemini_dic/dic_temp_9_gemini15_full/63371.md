entomophagous: /ˌɛntəˈmɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食虫的；吃昆虫的 |  Eating insects; feeding on insects.  | Example:  Many birds have entomophagous diets. 许多鸟类以昆虫为食。  Many species of spiders are entomophagous.  许多种类的蜘蛛是食虫的。 | 近义词: insectivorous | 反义词: carnivorous (if referring to a broader diet excluding insects)
