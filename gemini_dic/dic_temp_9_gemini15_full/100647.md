词源:
The word "intolerating" is derived from the verb "tolerate" (拉丁语 tolerare, from tolerāns, present participle of tolerāre "to bear, endure, suffer") + the prefix "in-" (meaning "not").  Therefore, "intolerating" means "not tolerating" or "unable to tolerate."
