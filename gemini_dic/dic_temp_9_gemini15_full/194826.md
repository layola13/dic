seme:/siːm/ | n. | 语义成分，语义单位 | A minimal unit of meaning in a language, often considered the smallest unit of meaning that can be isolated in a word or phrase.  Semes are abstract and can combine to create more complex meanings.  For example, the word "dog" might have semes such as "mammal," "canine," "domesticated," and "four-legged." | The word "bachelor" contains the semes "unmarried," "male," and "adult." | 近义词:  meaning element, semantic feature | 反义词:  N/A
