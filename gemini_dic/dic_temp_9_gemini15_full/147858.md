overrated:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd/| adj.|被高估的，名不副实的|Something that is overrated is considered to be better or more important than it actually is.  It often implies a level of hype or inflated expectation that doesn't match the reality.|例句：That restaurant is overrated; the food was just average. 那家餐馆被高估了；食物只是中等水平。|例句：I think his acting skills are overrated. 我认为他的演技被高估了。|近义词：overvalued, hyped, inflated, exaggerated|反义词：underrated, undervalued
