giveaway:/ˈɡɪvəweɪ/| n. v.|赠品；泄露；暴露；赠送；泄露秘密|

英文解析：A giveaway can be a free item given as a prize or promotion, or it can refer to something that unintentionally reveals a secret or truth. As a verb, it means to give something away for free or to unintentionally reveal a secret.


例句：

The company offered a free giveaway to attract customers.  这家公司提供免费赠品以吸引顾客。

His nervous laughter was a giveaway that he was lying.  他紧张的笑声暴露了他正在撒谎。


近义词：prize, present, handout,  betrayal, revelation


反义词：purchase, concealment, secret


词性：noun, verb
