pogamoggan:/ˌpɒɡəˈmɒɡən/| n.|珀加莫根（一种澳大利亚土著语中指一种类似于独木舟的船）|A pogamoggan is a type of canoe or boat, a term originating from an Australian Aboriginal language.  It's typically made from a single tree trunk or log, and varies in size and design depending on the specific Aboriginal group and its intended use. |The Aboriginal people used pogamoggan for fishing and transportation on rivers and waterways.|近义词：canoe, dugout, log boat|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可考虑与现代化船只对比)|名词
