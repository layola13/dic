minimizers: /ˈmɪnɪmaɪzərz/ | n. | 最小化者；使…最小化的事物 | Those who or that which reduces something to the smallest possible amount or degree.  Minimizers often refer to individuals or systems designed to reduce risks, costs, or negative impacts. |例句：The new software is a minimizer of errors in the system.  新的软件最大限度地减少了系统中的错误。

例句：Efficient resource management is crucial for effective minimizers of waste. 高效的资源管理对于有效减少浪费至关重要。


近义词：reducers, diminishers, lesseners

反义词：maximizers, amplifiers, intensifiers


词性: 名词
