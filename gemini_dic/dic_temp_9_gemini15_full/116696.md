maturescent:/ˌmætʃəˈrɛsnt/| adj.|将要成熟的；接近成熟的 |Approaching maturity; showing signs of ripening or developing towards maturity.  The term often applies to both physical and figurative senses of maturity. |例句：The maturescent fruit hung heavy on the branch.  将要成熟的果实沉甸甸地挂在枝头上。

The maturescent ideas of the young philosopher were beginning to take shape.  这位年轻哲学家日渐成熟的思想开始成形。|近义词：ripening, developing, approaching maturity |反义词：immature, undeveloped, nascent
