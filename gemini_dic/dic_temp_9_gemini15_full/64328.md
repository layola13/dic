eradication:/ɪˌrædəˈkeɪʃən/| n. |根除；消灭；扑灭 |The complete destruction of something, especially a disease, pest, or undesirable feature.  Eradication requires a concerted effort and often involves multiple strategies. |Example: The World Health Organization is committed to the eradication of polio.  世界卫生组织致力于消灭脊髓灰质炎。

The global eradication of smallpox is a significant achievement in public health.  天花的全球性根除是公共卫生领域的一项重大成就。|近义词：extermination, elimination, annihilation |反义词：propagation, proliferation, spread |名词
