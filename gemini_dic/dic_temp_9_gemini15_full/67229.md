expropriates:/ɪkˈsproʊprieɪts/ | vt. | 征用；没收；剥夺；驱逐 | To take possession of (property) for public use, typically with payment of compensation.  | The government expropriated the land to build a new highway. 政府征用这块土地修建一条新的高速公路。 | seize, confiscate, appropriate | return, restore, give back | 动词
