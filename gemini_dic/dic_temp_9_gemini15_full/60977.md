elicitation:/ɪˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |引出；诱导；探寻|The act of eliciting; the process of drawing out or obtaining information or a response.  It often involves skillful questioning or prompting. |例句：The researcher used careful elicitation techniques to gather data from the participants. 研究人员使用细致的引出技术从参与者那里收集数据。

例句：The teacher's elicitation of answers encouraged active participation from the students. 教师引出答案的行为鼓励学生积极参与。


近义词：extraction, procurement, solicitation, inducement|反义词：suppression, concealment, withholding|词性：名词
