dourly:/ˈdaʊərli/| adv.| 严厉地；阴沉地；愁眉苦脸地 |In a dour or sullen manner; gloomily.  It describes an action performed in a stern, gloomy, or sullen way. |例句：He looked dourly at the overflowing inbox. 他愁眉苦脸地看着塞满邮件的收件箱。

例句：She answered dourly, refusing to elaborate. 她阴沉地回答，拒绝详细解释。


近义词：sullenly, gloomily, grimly, severely, sternly

反义词：cheerfully, brightly, happily, merrily
