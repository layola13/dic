recuperation:/ˌriːkjuːpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |恢复；康复；休养 |The process of recovering from illness or injury; regaining health or strength.  |例句：After his surgery, he needed a period of recuperation.  手术后，他需要一段时间的恢复期。|例句：The recuperation of the wounded soldiers was slow but steady.  受伤士兵的康复缓慢但稳定。|近义词：recovery, convalescence, rehabilitation |反义词：deterioration, decline


USAGE->

She needs recuperation after her illness. = 她生病后需要休养。
His recuperation is progressing well. = 他的康复进展顺利。
