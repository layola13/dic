overweary: /ˌoʊvərˈwɪəri/ | adj. |极度疲惫的，精疲力尽的 | Extremely tired; exhausted. |例句：After the marathon, the runners were overweary. 马拉松结束后，跑步者们都精疲力尽了。  |近义词：exhausted, fatigued, worn out, spent |反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overweary after a long day's work. = 他一天工作下来非常疲惫。
They are overweary from their travels. = 他们旅途劳顿，非常疲惫。


一般过去时:
She was overweary after the strenuous hike. = 艰苦的徒步旅行后，她精疲力尽。
We were overweary and decided to rest early. = 我们精疲力尽，决定早点休息。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
He had been overweary for days before he finally collapsed. = 他已经疲惫了好几天，最后终于倒下了。
They had been overweary since the beginning of their journey. = 从旅程开始他们就一直非常疲惫。


将来时:

We will be overweary if we don't get enough rest. = 如果我们休息不够，我们会精疲力尽。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't worked so hard, I wouldn't be so overweary. = 如果我不那么努力工作，我就不会这么疲惫了。
I wish I weren't so overweary. = 我希望我不那么疲惫。


被动语态: (Overweary is rarely used passively)  This is because "overweary" describes a state of being, not an action performed upon someone.  A more natural way to express a passive idea would be: He was made overweary by the demands of his job. (他被工作要求弄得精疲力尽。)

She was not overweary despite the long walk. = 尽管走了很远的路，她也没有精疲力尽。
