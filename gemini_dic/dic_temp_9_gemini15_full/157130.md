pinching:/ˈpɪntʃɪŋ/| v.|捏，拧，偷窃，吝啬|To press something, especially someone's skin, between your finger and thumb.|例句：She pinched his cheek playfully. 她顽皮地捏了捏他的脸颊。|例句：He was pinching pennies to save for a new car. 他为了攒钱买新车而省吃俭用。|近义词：squeezing, stealing, scrimping|反义词：giving, sharing, generous|
