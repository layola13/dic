delegation:/ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |代表团；委派；授权 | A group of people sent to represent others; the act of assigning responsibility to another person or group; the assignment of a task or responsibility. |例句：The delegation from China arrived in Washington. 中国代表团抵达华盛顿。

The manager delegated the task to his assistant.  经理将任务委托给了他的助手。


近义词：representation, commission, assignment, entrustment | 反义词：retention, reservation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company sends a delegation to the conference every year. = 公司每年都派一个代表团参加会议。
She delegates many tasks to her team members. = 她把许多任务委托给她的团队成员。


一般过去时:
The delegation presented their proposals yesterday. = 代表团昨天提交了他们的提案。
He delegated the project to a junior employee. = 他把项目委托给了一名初级员工。


现在完成时:
The team has successfully completed its delegation of responsibilities. = 该团队已成功完成了其责任分工。
She has delegated all her urgent tasks. = 她已经委托了她所有紧急的任务。


过去完成时:
The committee had already delegated the power before the meeting. = 委员会在会议前已经授权了。
They had delegated the work before leaving for vacation. = 他们在休假前已经委托了工作。


将来时:
The president will delegate the authority to his vice president. = 总统将把权力委托给他的副总统。
We will send a strong delegation to the international forum. = 我们将派一个强大的代表团参加国际论坛。


虚拟语气:
If the manager delegated more tasks, the team would be more efficient. = 如果经理委托更多的任务，团队效率会更高。
I wish I had delegated that project earlier. = 我希望我早点把那个项目委托出去。


被动语态:
The work was delegated to the most experienced team member. = 工作被委托给了最有经验的团队成员。
The decision will be delegated to the board of directors. = 决定将委托给董事会。


疑问句:
Who will be delegated to lead the project? = 谁将被委派领导这个项目？
Has the task been delegated yet? = 任务已经委托出去了吗？


否定句:
He didn't delegate any tasks to his staff. = 他没有把任何任务委托给他的员工。
The company did not delegate the responsibility to the appropriate department. = 公司没有将责任委托给合适的部门。
