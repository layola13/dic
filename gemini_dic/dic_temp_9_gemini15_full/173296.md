quasi-cordially:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈkɔːrdiəli/ | adj. | 几乎热诚地；不冷不热地；似是而非地热情地 |  The word combines "quasi," meaning seemingly or apparently, with "cordially," meaning warmly and sincerely.  It describes an action or expression that only superficially resembles genuine warmth or friendliness.  There's a lack of genuine feeling behind the outward display. | He greeted me quasi-cordially, a smile barely touching his lips. 他几乎热情地向我打招呼，嘴角 barely 动了一下。 |近义词：lukewarmly, halfheartedly, insincerely | 反义词： wholeheartedly, sincerely, enthusiastically
