housebote:/ˈhaʊsbəʊt/| n.|房屋修缮费；（旧时）地主允许佃户免费砍伐木材用于房屋维修的权利| Allowance of timber from a lord's woods for the repair of a tenant's house.  This was a customary right in some parts of England. The term is now largely obsolete, referring to the right itself rather than the timber provided.|The tenant claimed housebote from the landlord to repair the roof. = 佃户向地主索要房屋修缮木材以修理屋顶。|近义词：timber allowance (in the context of feudal rights)|反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would be the *absence* of this right.
