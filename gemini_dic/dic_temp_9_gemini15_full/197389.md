sheathfish:/ˈʃiːθfɪʃ/| n. |鞘鱼，刀鱼 | A type of fish characterized by an elongated body and often a scaleless or smooth skin, sometimes with a laterally compressed body.  They are usually bottom-dwellers and nocturnal. Many species are important food sources. | Example:  The fisherman caught a large sheathfish near the riverbed.  渔夫在河床附近钓到一条大鞘鱼。
The aquarium showcased several species of sheathfish, highlighting their unique adaptations. 水族馆展示了几种鞘鱼，突出了它们独特的适应性。|近义词：eel (某些种类), catfish (某些种类) |反义词：N/A
