exited:/ɪɡˈzaɪtɪd/ | adj. |兴奋的，激动的 |Feeling or showing great enthusiasm and excitement.  |例句：The children were exited about the trip to the zoo. 孩子们对去动物园的旅行感到兴奋。

例句：She was exited to receive the award. 她很兴奋地获得了奖项。


近义词：thrilled, enthusiastic, exhilarated, energized |反义词：calm, apathetic, bored, indifferent |词性：形容词
