purported:/pərˈpɔːrtɪd/| adj.| 声称的；据称的；号称的 |  Claimed or alleged to be true, but not necessarily so.  It suggests a lack of definitive proof. | 例句：The purported author of the letter is unknown.  这封信的所谓作者是未知的。

The purported benefits of the new drug are yet to be scientifically proven.  这种新药的所谓益处还有待科学验证。


近义词：alleged, claimed, supposed, reputed


反义词：proven, verified, confirmed


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The purported reason is unclear. = 声称的原因尚不清楚。
He purportedly owns a yacht. = 他据称拥有一艘游艇。


一般过去时:
They purported to be experts. = 他们自称是专家。
The painting was purported to be a masterpiece. = 那幅画据说是杰作。


现在完成时:
The company has purported to improve its safety record. = 公司声称已经改善了其安全记录。
She has purportedly found a cure for cancer. = 她据称已经找到了癌症的治疗方法。


过去完成时:
He had purportedly stolen the money before fleeing. = 他据称在逃亡前偷走了钱。
It had purportedly been a successful project. = 它据称是一个成功的项目。


将来时:
They will purportedly announce their engagement soon. = 他们据称很快就会宣布订婚。
The government will purportedly increase taxes. = 政府据称会增税。


虚拟语气:
If the purported evidence were true, he would be guilty. = 如果所谓的证据属实，他就有罪了。
If it purportedly worked, we would have seen results by now. = 如果它真的有效，我们现在应该已经看到结果了。


被动语态:
The story is purported to be based on a true event. = 这个故事据说是基于真实事件。
The painting was purportedly created by a famous artist. = 这幅画据说是由一位著名艺术家创作的。


疑问句:
Is the purported discovery really significant? = 这个所谓的发现真的重要吗？
Are the purported benefits worth the risks? = 所谓的好处值得冒这个险吗？


否定句:
The purported evidence was not credible. = 所谓的证据不可信。
The purported witness's testimony was not convincing. = 所谓证人的证词并不可信。


PHRASE->
purportedly true = 据说是真的
purportedly based on = 据说是基于
purportedly written by = 据说是由...写的
purportedly discovered = 据说是发现的
purportedly stolen = 据说是被偷的


词源:
From the verb "portend," meaning "to signify," "to foreshadow," or "to indicate."  The prefix "pur-" adds an implication of falseness or uncertainty.  The word evolved through the past participle "purported" to its current adjectival usage.
