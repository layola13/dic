/kɒmˈpaʊnd vɜːrb/| phr.|复合动词|A compound verb is a verb phrase consisting of a main verb and one or more auxiliary verbs. These auxiliary verbs help to express grammatical functions such as tense, aspect, mood, and voice.|例句：She *has been working* hard lately. (完成进行时) 她最近一直在努力工作。 He *could have gone* to the party. (虚拟语气) 他本来可以去参加派对的。|近义词：verb phrase, multi-word verb|反义词：simple verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is singing. = 她正在唱歌。
They are playing football. = 他们在踢足球。


一般过去时:
He was studying. = 他正在学习。
We were eating dinner. = 我们正在吃晚饭。


现在完成时:
I have finished my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
She has been living here for five years. = 她在这里住了五年了。


过去完成时:
They had already left. = 他们已经离开了。
He had been waiting for an hour. = 他已经等了一个小时了。


将来时:
I will go to the park. = 我将去公园。
She will be studying tomorrow. = 她明天将学习。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would do it. = 如果我是你，我会这么做。
I wish I could fly. = 我希望我能飞。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
