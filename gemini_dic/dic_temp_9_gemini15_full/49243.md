deleterious:/ˌdɛləˈtɪəriəs/ | adj. | 有害的；有毒的 | causing harm or damage | The deleterious effects of pollution are well documented.  污染的有害影响已被充分记录。| harmful, detrimental, damaging, injurious | beneficial, advantageous, helpful, wholesome |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The chemical is deleterious to the environment. =这种化学物质对环境有害。
Smoking is deleterious to your health. =吸烟有害健康。


一般过去时:
The storm had deleterious effects on the crops. =暴风雨对庄稼产生了有害的影响。
The decision proved to be deleterious to the company's reputation. =这个决定证明对公司的声誉有害。


现在完成时:

Years of neglect have had a deleterious effect on the building's structure. =多年的忽视对建筑物的结构产生了有害的影响。


过去完成时:

Scientists had discovered the deleterious effects of the drug before it was released to the public. =科学家们在该药物公开发行之前就已经发现了它的有害作用。


将来时:
The new policy will have deleterious consequences for small businesses. =新政策将对小型企业产生有害的影响。
Continued pollution will have a deleterious effect on the ecosystem. =持续的污染将对生态系统产生有害的影响。


虚拟语气:
If we continue down this path, the consequences could be deleterious. =如果我们继续走这条路，后果可能会很糟糕。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
