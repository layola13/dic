manacles:/ˈmænəklz/| n.|手铐；束缚|Handcuffs or shackles used to restrain someone's hands.  Manacles can also refer to something that restricts freedom or progress. |例句：The police used manacles to restrain the suspect.  警察用手铐制服了嫌疑犯。

例句：The rigid rules felt like manacles on her creativity. 严格的规定让她感觉像是被束缚住了创造力。|近义词：handcuffs, shackles, restraints, bonds|反义词：freedom, liberty
