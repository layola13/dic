cross-pollinate:/krɒsˈpɒləneɪt/ | vt. |异花授粉；互相影响；互相借鉴 | To transfer pollen from the flower of one plant to the flower of another plant of the same species, resulting in cross-fertilization.  It can also refer to the exchange of ideas or influences between different groups or fields. |例句：Bees cross-pollinate flowers, ensuring genetic diversity. 蜜蜂为花朵进行异花授粉，确保基因多样性。

The researchers cross-pollinated their ideas to develop a new theory. 研究人员互相借鉴彼此的想法，从而发展出一个新的理论。|近义词：fertilize, hybridize, intermix |反义词：self-pollinate
