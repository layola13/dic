rejoices:/rɪˈdʒɔɪsɪz/| v.|欣喜，高兴|To feel or express great happiness or joy.|例句：She rejoices in her children's success. 她为孩子的成功感到高兴。|例句：They rejoiced at the good news. 他们为好消息而欢欣鼓舞。|近义词：exult, delight, celebrate|反义词：grieve, mourn, lament


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rejoices in his new job. = 他对他的新工作感到高兴。
She rejoices every time she sees her grandchild. = 她每次看到孙子都非常高兴。

一般过去时:
They rejoiced at the news of their victory. = 他们为胜利的消息而欢欣鼓舞。
We rejoiced when the rain finally stopped. = 雨终于停了，我们都很高兴。


现在完成时:
She has rejoiced at every success she has achieved. = 她为取得的每一个成功都感到高兴。
He has rejoiced since he met his soulmate. = 自从遇到他的灵魂伴侣以来，他一直很高兴。


过去完成时:
They had rejoiced at the news before the celebration began. = 庆祝活动开始前，他们就已经为这个消息而欢欣鼓舞了。
She had rejoiced many times before this special moment. = 在这一刻之前，她已经多次感到高兴了。


将来时:
We will rejoice when the project is finally completed. = 当项目最终完成时，我们将欢欣鼓舞。
They will rejoice at the birth of their first child. = 他们将会为第一个孩子的出生而欣喜若狂。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would rejoice greatly. = 如果我中了彩票，我会非常高兴。
I wish I could rejoice with them, but I'm too busy. = 我真希望我能和他们一起庆祝，但我太忙了。


被动语态:
The good news was rejoiced over by the entire community. = 整个社区都为这个好消息而欢欣鼓舞。（被动语态较为少用）
The victory was rejoiced at by the whole team. = 全队都为胜利而欢欣鼓舞。（被动语态较为少用）


疑问句:
Does he rejoice in his work? = 他对他的工作感到高兴吗？
Do they rejoice at the prospect of a holiday? = 他们对假期的前景感到高兴吗？


否定句:
He didn't rejoice at the news. = 他对这个消息并不高兴。
She doesn't rejoice in her work anymore. = 她对工作不再感到高兴了。


PHRASE->
rejoice in = 为...而高兴
rejoice at/over = 为...而欢欣鼓舞
rejoice with = 与...一起庆祝
rejoice one's heart = 使某人欣喜
rejoice and be glad = 欢欣喜悦


词源:
From Old French *rejoir, from Vulgar Latin *re-gaudrire, from Latin gaudere "to rejoice".  The prefix "re-" intensifying the meaning.
