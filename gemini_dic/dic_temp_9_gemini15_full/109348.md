leninites:/ˈlɛnɪnaɪts/ | n. |列宁主义思想；列宁主义者 |The term "leninites" refers to the ideologies, principles, and practices associated with Vladimir Lenin and his interpretation of Marxism, particularly his emphasis on a vanguard party and a revolutionary approach to achieving socialist goals.  It often implies a strict adherence to Lenin's specific policies and strategies.  The term is less frequently used than "Leninism" itself. |  The rise of leninites in the early 20th century drastically altered the political landscape of Russia and beyond. | 近义词:Leninism, Bolshevism | 反义词:Capitalism, anti-Leninism | 名词
