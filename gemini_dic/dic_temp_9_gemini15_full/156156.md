phototrophic:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈtrɒfɪk/| adj.|光养的，利用光的| Relating to or denoting organisms that obtain energy from light.  Phototrophic organisms use light as their primary energy source for growth and metabolism. They convert light energy into chemical energy through photosynthesis.|Example:  Cyanobacteria are a type of phototrophic bacteria.|近义词：photosynthetic|反义词：chemotrophic
