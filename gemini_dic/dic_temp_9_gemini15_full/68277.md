fakir:/ˈfeɪkər/| n.|苦行僧，托钵僧|A fakir is a Muslim or Hindu ascetic, typically a wandering beggar who practices extreme self-denial and often performs feats of apparent magic or hypnotism.|例句：The fakir sat cross-legged, his eyes closed in deep meditation.  那位苦行僧盘腿而坐，双眼紧闭，陷入深思。|例句：The fakir amazed the crowd with his seemingly impossible feats of strength.  这位苦行僧以他看似不可能完成的壮举让众人惊叹。|近义词：ascetic, hermit, holy man|反义词：hedonist, materialist|名词
