deducts:/dɪˈdʌkts/| vt.|扣除，减去，推断|To subtract or take away (an amount) from a total; to infer or conclude (something) from evidence.|例句：The company deducts taxes from my paycheck. = 公司从我的工资中扣除税款。|例句：From the evidence, we can deduct that he is guilty. = 从证据中，我们可以推断他是有罪的。|近义词：subtract, deduct from, infer, deduce|反义词：add, include


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store deducts a discount from the total price. = 商店从总价中扣除折扣。
He deducts important information from complex data. = 他从复杂数据中推断出重要信息。


一般过去时:
They deducted the cost of shipping from the final bill. = 他们从最终账单中扣除了运费。
The detective deducted the killer's motive from the crime scene. = 侦探从犯罪现场推断出凶手的动机。


现在完成时:
The bank has deducted the overdraft fee from my account. = 银行已从我的账户中扣除了透支费。
Scientists have deducted a new theory from the recent research. = 科学家们从最近的研究中推断出一个新的理论。


过去完成时:
They had deducted the expenses before calculating the profit. = 他们在计算利润之前已经扣除了费用。
The investigator had deducted the suspect's identity from the clues. = 调查员已经从线索中推断出嫌疑人的身份。


将来时:
The company will deduct the insurance premium from your salary next month. = 公司下个月将从您的工资中扣除保险费。
Researchers will deduct the relationship between these two variables from their study. = 研究人员将从他们的研究中推断出这两个变量之间的关系。


虚拟语气:
If they deducted the tax, we would have more money. = 如果他们扣除税款，我们将会有更多的钱。
If he had deducted the correct information, the result would be different. = 如果他推断出正确的信息，结果就会不同。


被动语态:
Taxes are deducted from the employee's salary. = 税款从员工的工资中扣除。
The conclusion was deducted from the data analysis. = 结论是从数据分析中推断出来的。


疑问句:
Is the sales tax deducted from the total cost? = 销售税是从总成本中扣除的吗？
Can we deduct any meaningful insights from this experiment? = 我们能从这个实验中推断出任何有意义的见解吗？


否定句:
The company does not deduct any additional fees. = 公司不收取任何额外费用。
The police could not deduct the perpetrator's identity from the limited evidence. = 警方无法从有限的证据中推断出犯罪者的身份。
