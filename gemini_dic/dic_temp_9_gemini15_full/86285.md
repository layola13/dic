heavy-handed: /ˌheviˈhændɪd/ | adj. |  笨拙的；粗暴的；强硬的；压制性的 |

英文解析:  Describes a manner or action that is forceful, clumsy, or insensitive;  excessively controlling or domineering. It suggests a lack of subtlety or finesse, often resulting in negative consequences.


例句:

His heavy-handed approach to discipline alienated his students.  他的严厉管教方式疏远了他的学生们。

The government's heavy-handed response to the protests sparked further unrest. 政府对抗议活动的强硬回应引发了进一步的骚乱。


近义词: forceful, brutal, clumsy, insensitive, domineering, overbearing, oppressive, authoritarian, dictatorial


反义词: gentle, subtle, delicate, tactful, considerate, lenient, permissive


词性:  形容词
