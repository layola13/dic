fluework:/ˈfluːwɜːrk/| n. |烟道工程；烟道系统|The intricate network of flues and chimneys in a building, often made of brick or stone, designed to carry away smoke and gases produced by fireplaces, furnaces, or other combustion devices.  It encompasses the design, construction, and maintenance of these systems.|例句：The old building's fluework needed extensive repairs to ensure safe and efficient smoke removal. 老建筑的烟道系统需要大规模维修以确保安全有效的排烟。 The architect specified complex fluework to accommodate multiple fireplaces. 建筑师指定了复杂的烟道系统来容纳多个壁炉。|近义词：chimney system, venting system|反义词：N/A
