chaffern:/ˈtʃæfən/| vt.|讨价还价，议价|To haggle or bargain over the price of something.|例句：He chaffered with the market vendor over the price of the rug. 他和市场小贩讨价还价地买地毯。|近义词：bargain, negotiate, haggle|反义词：accept, concede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chaffers with the shopkeeper over the price. = 他和店主讨价还价。
They often chaffer at the market. = 他们经常在市场上讨价还价。

一般过去时:
She chaffered for a lower price on the car. = 她为汽车讨价还价以获得更低的价格。
We chaffered with the seller until we reached an agreement. = 我们和卖家讨价还价直到达成协议。

现在完成时:
They have chaffered with several dealers. = 他们已经和几个经销商讨价还价了。
I have never chaffered over the price of a house before. = 我以前从未为房价讨价还价过。

过去完成时:
He had chaffered for hours before finally buying the painting. = 他讨价还价几个小时才最终买下这幅画。
They had chaffered extensively before agreeing on a price. = 在就价格达成一致之前，他们进行了广泛的讨价还价。

将来时:
I will chaffer with the antique dealer tomorrow. = 我明天将与古董商讨价还价。
She will chaffer until she gets the best possible price. = 她会讨价还价直到得到最好的价格。

虚拟语气:
If I were to chaffer, I would get a better price. = 如果我去讨价还价，我会得到更好的价格。
I wish I had chaffered more aggressively. = 我希望我当时更积极地讨价还价。

被动语态:
The price was chaffered down considerably. = 价格被大幅压低了。
The goods were chaffered over for a long time. =  这些商品被讨价还价了很久。


疑问句:
Do you usually chaffer when you shop? = 你通常在购物时会讨价还价吗？
Will you chaffer for a lower price? = 你会讨价还价以获得更低的价格吗？

否定句:
He didn't chaffer at all; he simply accepted the price. = 他根本没有讨价还价；他只是接受了价格。
She refused to chaffer with the salesman. = 她拒绝与销售员讨价还价。
