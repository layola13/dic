repacks:/ˌriːˈpæks/| vt.|重新包装，再包装|To repack something means to pack it again, often in a different way or container.  It implies a previous packing process.|例句：The company repacks the coffee beans into smaller bags for retail sale. 公司将咖啡豆重新包装成较小的袋子以便零售。

例句：We need to repack the suitcases before the flight. 我们需要在飞行前重新打包行李箱。


近义词：repackage, repackage, redistribute

反义词：unpack


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repacks the boxes every day. 他每天都重新包装箱子。
She repacks her suitcase after every trip. 每次旅行后她都会重新打包行李箱。

一般过去时:
They repacked the goods after the inspection. 检验后他们重新包装了货物。
I repacked my lunch after spilling my drink. 我洒了饮料后重新打包了午餐。

现在完成时:
He has repacked all the Christmas presents. 他已经重新包装了所有的圣诞礼物。
She has repacked her bags several times. 她已经几次重新打包了行李。

过去完成时:
They had repacked the boxes before the shipment. 他们发货前已经重新包装了箱子。
The worker had repacked the damaged items. 工人已经重新包装了损坏的物品。

将来时:
We will repack the products tomorrow. 我们明天将重新包装产品。
She will repack the gifts before sending them. 她会在寄送礼物之前重新包装它们。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would repack everything properly. 如果我有更多时间，我会把所有东西都好好重新包装。
I wish I had repacked my bag more carefully. 我真希望我当时更仔细地重新打包我的包。

被动语态:
The goods were repacked before being shipped. 货物在发货前被重新包装了。
The damaged items are being repacked now. 损坏的物品正在被重新包装。

疑问句:
Did he repack the boxes? 他重新包装了箱子吗？

否定句:
He didn't repack the suitcase. 他没有重新打包行李箱。
