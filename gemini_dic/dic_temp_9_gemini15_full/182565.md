reobservation:/ˌriːˌɒbzərˈveɪʃən/| n.|再观察，重新观察|The act of observing something again; a second observation.|例句：The reobservation of the comet confirmed its unusual trajectory.  再次观测彗星证实了其不寻常的轨迹。

例句：After the initial examination, a reobservation was deemed necessary. 初次检查后，认为有必要再次观察。


近义词：second observation, re-examination, review|反义词：initial observation, first glance|词性：名词
