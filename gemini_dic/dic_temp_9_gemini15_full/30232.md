cellphone:/ˈsɛlfoʊn/| n. |手机|A mobile phone; a portable telephone that can be used in many locations because it receives signals from a network of base stations.|例句：I lost my cellphone yesterday. 昨天我丢了手机。|例句：She answered the cellphone call immediately. 她立即接听了手机电话。|近义词：mobile phone, cell, mobile|反义词：landline
