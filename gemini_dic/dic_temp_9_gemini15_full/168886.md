profeminisms: /ˈproʊˌfɛmɪnɪzəmz/ | n. | 支持女权主义的言行 |  Actions and beliefs that support feminism.  Profeminism differs from feminism in that it doesn't necessarily imply a self-identification as a feminist, but rather an alignment with feminist goals and values.  It often refers to men who support feminism and women's rights. | Example: His profeminist stance was evident in his advocacy for equal pay.  他的支持女权主义的立场在他的倡导同工同酬中显而易见。| 近义词:  male feminism, feminist advocacy, support for women's rights | 反义词:  misogyny, sexism, antifeminism | 名词
