self-consecrations:/ˌsɛlf ˈkɒnsɪkreɪʃənz/| n.|自我奉献；自我祝圣|The act of dedicating oneself to a cause, purpose, or deity, often without formal religious ceremony.  It emphasizes a personal and internal commitment rather than an external ritual.|例句：Her life was a testament to self-consecration; she dedicated herself entirely to helping the poor.  她的生命是对自我奉献的证明；她完全致力于帮助穷人。

例句：His self-consecration to his art led to years of tireless work and eventual success. 他对艺术的自我奉献导致了多年的辛勤工作和最终的成功。


近义词：self-sacrifice, dedication, devotion, commitment|反义词：self-indulgence, selfishness, apathy
