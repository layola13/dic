intermarketing:/ˌɪntərˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/| n. |内部营销；内部市场营销|Internal marketing refers to the application of marketing principles and techniques within an organization to motivate and align employees with the company's overall marketing goals.  It aims to create a customer-centric culture within the company itself, treating employees as internal customers. |Example:  The company implemented an intermarketing strategy to boost employee morale and improve customer service.  这家公司实施了内部营销策略来提高员工士气和改善客户服务。|近义词：internal marketing, employee marketing |反义词：external marketing
