productive:/prəˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. |多产的；富有成效的；生产性的 |  Producing or achieving a significant amount of work or results.  It implies efficiency and effectiveness in achieving goals. |例句：She had a very productive meeting with her team, outlining the next steps of the project. 她和她的团队进行了一次非常富有成效的会议，概述了项目的下一步计划。

例句：The farmer had a productive harvest this year, yielding abundant crops. 今年农民获得了丰收，农作物产量丰富。


近义词：fruitful, effective, efficient, successful, yielding | 反义词：unproductive, inefficient, sterile, fruitless


词性：形容词
