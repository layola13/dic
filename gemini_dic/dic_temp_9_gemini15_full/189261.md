saddleless:/ˈsædəlləs/| adj.|无鞍的，不带鞍的|Without a saddle.  This adjective describes something lacking a saddle, typically referring to a horse or other riding animal, or to a type of riding or exercise that doesn't involve a saddle.|例句：The horse was ridden saddleless across the vast plains. 这匹马赤裸着脊背横越了广阔的平原。|例句：Saddleless riding requires significant skill and balance.  无鞍骑术需要相当的技巧和平衡能力。|近义词：unsaddled|反义词：saddled
