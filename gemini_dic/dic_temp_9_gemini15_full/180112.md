refluency:/rɪˈfluənsi/ | n. | 流利性，流畅性 | The ability to speak or write smoothly and easily.  It often refers to the ease and natural flow of speech, encompassing aspects like rhythm, intonation, and vocabulary choice.  It's the opposite of hesitancy or stuttering. | Example:  The student demonstrated impressive refluency in her presentation.  学生在她的演示中展现了令人印象深刻的流利性。 | 近义词：fluency, eloquence, fluency of speech | 反义词：hesitancy, stammering, stuttering | 名词
