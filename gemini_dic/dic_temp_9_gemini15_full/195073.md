The adjective "semicarved" describes something that has been carved, but not fully or completely. It suggests a work in progress or a piece that has undergone partial carving, leaving some areas untouched or less detailed. | Example: The semicarved wooden statue stood unfinished in the artist's studio.  这尊半雕刻的木雕静静地矗立在艺术家的工作室里，尚未完成。  |近义词: partially carved, incompletely carved, unfinished | 反义词: fully carved, completely carved, finished |
