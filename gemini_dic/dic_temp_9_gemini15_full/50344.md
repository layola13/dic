deplumation:/ˌdiːpluˈmeɪʃən/| n. |脱毛；拔毛；脱羽|The process of losing feathers, or the removal of feathers.  It can refer to natural molting or artificial removal. |Example:  The bird underwent a period of rapid deplumation before its next molt.  这只鸟在下次换羽前经历了一段快速的脱毛期。  The deplumation of the chicken was done to prepare it for market. 这只鸡的脱毛是为了准备上市。|近义词：plucking, moulting, shedding |反义词：feathering
