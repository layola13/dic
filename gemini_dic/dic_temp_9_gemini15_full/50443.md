deprecators:/dɪˈprɛkeɪtərz/ | n. |贬低者，轻视者 |Those who express disapproval or belittle something.  They often criticize or downplay the value or importance of a particular thing, idea, or person. |例句：The deprecators of the new policy argued it would be ineffective and costly.  贬低这项新政策的人认为它无效且成本高昂。  |例句：Online deprecators often hide behind anonymous profiles to avoid accountability. 网上的贬低者经常躲在匿名账户后面以逃避责任。 |近义词：critics, detractors, belittlers, disparagers |反义词：supporters, advocates, champions
