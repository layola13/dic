pedestrianising: /pəˈdɛstriənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  使行人专用；禁止车辆通行 |  The act of making an area accessible primarily to pedestrians by restricting or prohibiting vehicular traffic.  This often involves closing streets to cars, creating pedestrian walkways, and adding features like benches and plants to enhance the pedestrian experience. |The city council is pedestrianising the town centre to improve air quality and encourage more people to walk and cycle.  市议会正在将市中心改造成行人专用区，以改善空气质量并鼓励更多人步行和骑自行车。


They are pedestrianising the high street next year. 他们明年将把商业街改造成行人专用区。|近义词： pedestrianize, close to traffic, make car-free | 反义词： motorize, open to traffic
