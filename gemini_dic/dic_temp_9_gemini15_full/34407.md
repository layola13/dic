clatch:/klætʃ/| n.| 抓取；猛抓；（尤指鸟类的）一窝蛋；|a grasping or snatching; a clutch of eggs.  |The bird carefully tended its clatch of eggs.  这只鸟小心翼翼地照看着它的一窝蛋。|clutch, grasp, grip |release, let go |名词
