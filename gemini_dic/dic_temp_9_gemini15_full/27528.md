campus:/ˈkæmpəs/ | n. | 校园，大学（或学院）的范围 | A campus is the grounds and buildings of a university or college.  |例句：The university campus is beautiful in the autumn. 秋天的大学校园很美。|例句：She lives on campus. 她住在校园里。|近义词：grounds, college grounds |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The campus hosts many events. = 校园举办许多活动。
Students study on the campus. = 学生们在校园里学习。

一般过去时:
The campus held a graduation ceremony. = 校园举行了一场毕业典礼。
We walked around the campus yesterday. = 我们昨天在校园里散步。

现在完成时:
The university has expanded its campus. = 这所大学已经扩大了它的校园。
They have lived on campus for three years. = 他们已经在校园里住了三年了。

过去完成时:
The construction of the new campus had been completed. = 新校园的建设已经完成。
Before I graduated, I had spent five years on campus. = 在我毕业之前，我在校园里待了五年。

将来时:
The campus will host a concert next week. = 校园下周将举办一场音乐会。
We will visit the new campus tomorrow. = 我们明天将参观新的校园。

虚拟语气:
If the campus were larger, it could accommodate more students. = 如果校园更大一些，它就能容纳更多的学生。
I wish the campus had more green space. = 我希望校园有更多的绿地。

被动语态:
The campus is regularly maintained. = 校园定期维护。
The new buildings on campus are being constructed. = 校园里的新建筑正在建设中。

疑问句:
Is the campus open to the public? = 校园对公众开放吗？
Where is the main library on campus? = 校园主图书馆在哪里？

否定句:
The campus is not open late at night. = 校园晚上不开放。
He doesn't live on campus anymore. = 他不再住在校园里了。
