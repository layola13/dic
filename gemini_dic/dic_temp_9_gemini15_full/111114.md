literalizer:/ˈlɪtərəlaɪzər/ | n. | 文字化者；逐字翻译者 | A person or thing that converts something into a literal form, especially by translating or interpreting it word for word.  It can also refer to a software or tool that performs literal translation. | Example: The literalizer software translated the code directly, without any optimization. 文字化软件直接翻译代码，没有任何优化。 |近义词：translator, interpreter | 反义词：paraphraser, summarizer |  名词
