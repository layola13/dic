place-cards: /ˈpleɪsˌkɑːrdz/ | n. |座位卡，席位卡 | Small cards bearing the names of guests, placed at each setting at a formal dinner or other event to indicate where each person should sit.  | 例句：The place-cards were elegantly written in calligraphy. 座位卡是用优雅的书法写的。


例句：We carefully arranged the place-cards on the table before the guests arrived.  我们在客人到达之前仔细地将座位卡摆放在桌子上。


近义词：name cards, seating cards


反义词：无
