peculation:/ˌpɛkjuˈleɪʃən/| n.| 挪用公款；贪污；投机；推测 |The act of misappropriating money or funds entrusted to one's care.  It often involves embezzlement or fraud.  It can also refer to speculation or conjecture. |例句：The mayor was arrested for peculation of city funds. 市长因挪用公款而被捕。

例句：His business decisions were based on pure peculation, not sound market analysis. 他的商业决策是基于纯粹的投机，而不是合理的市场分析。

近义词：embezzlement, misappropriation, fraud, speculation, conjecture

反义词：honesty, integrity, probity
