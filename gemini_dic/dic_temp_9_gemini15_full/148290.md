oversocializing: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəlˌaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |过度社交；社交过度| Engaging in excessive social interaction, often to the point of exhaustion or detriment.  It implies a level of social activity that is beyond what is healthy or sustainable for the individual. | Example:  He's been oversocializing lately and is completely burnt out. 他最近社交过度，完全精疲力尽了。| Near Synonyms: overextending oneself socially, excessive socializing | Antonyms: social isolation, withdrawal, hermitage |  No specific noun or adjective form exists; the "-ing" form functions as a gerund (noun) or present participle (adjective/verb).
