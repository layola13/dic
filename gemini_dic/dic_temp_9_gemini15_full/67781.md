eyelid: /ˈaɪlɪd/ | n. | 眼睑 | A fold of skin that covers and protects the eye.  | Example: He blinked his eyelids rapidly. 他快速地眨了眨眼睛。 | 近义词:  eye-lid | 反义词: None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The eyelids protect the eyes from dust. = 眼睑保护眼睛免受灰尘的侵害。
She often touches her eyelids when she's nervous. = 她紧张的时候经常摸她的眼睑。

一般过去时:
His eyelids drooped with fatigue. = 他的眼睑因疲劳而下垂。
The doctor examined his swollen eyelids. = 医生检查了他肿胀的眼睑。


现在完成时:
I have felt a twitch in my eyelid all day. = 我一整天都感觉眼皮跳。
She hasn't had any problems with her eyelids recently. = 她最近眼睑没有任何问题。


过去完成时:
Her eyelids had become heavy before she fell asleep. = 她睡着之前眼皮已经很沉重了。
