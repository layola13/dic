non-automated:/ˌnɒnˈɔːtəmeɪtɪd/| adj.|非自动化的；手工的|Not done or operated automatically; manual.  It refers to processes or systems that require human intervention and are not controlled by machines or computers.|例句：The factory uses a non-automated system for quality control.  这家工厂使用非自动化的质量控制系统。
例句：This process is still largely non-automated, requiring significant manual labor. 这个过程在很大程度上仍然是非自动化的，需要大量的人工劳动。|近义词：manual, hand-operated,  unautomated|反义词：automated, automatic

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is non-automated. = 这个系统是非自动化的。
This task is non-automated and requires human input. = 这个任务是非自动化的，需要人工输入。


一般过去时:
The process was non-automated before the upgrade. = 升级之前，该流程是非自动化的。
Their workflow remained non-automated for many years. = 他们的工作流程多年来一直是非自动化的。


现在完成时:

We have successfully transitioned some non-automated tasks to automation.  我们已成功地将一些非自动化的任务转变为自动化。


过去完成时:
The factory had been non-automated until the recent investment in robotics. = 在最近投资机器人之前，这家工厂一直是非自动化的。
They had maintained a non-automated system for decades.  他们几十年来一直保持着非自动化的系统。


将来时:
The project will remain non-automated for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，该项目将保持非自动化的状态。
This department will likely still be non-automated next year.  明年这个部门可能仍然是非自动化的。


虚拟语气:
If the process were non-automated, it would be more labor-intensive. = 如果该流程是非自动化的，则会更费力。

被动语态:
