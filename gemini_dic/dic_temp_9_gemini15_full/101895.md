ish:/ɪʃ/ | adj. | …似的；有点儿…；大约… |  Suffix meaning "somewhat," "approximate," or "having the characteristics of." It's used to form adjectives and adverbs, indicating an approximation or resemblance.  |  The room is greenish-ish.  房间有点儿绿。 |  somewhat, slightly, approximately |  precise, exact |  形容词


例句：

He's kind of childish. 他有点孩子气。

Her hair is reddish-ish. 她的头发有点发红。


近义词：

somewhat, slightly, a bit, kind of, a little

反义词：

precisely, exactly
