hippie:/ˈhɪpi/| n. adj.|嬉皮士；嬉皮士的；|A hippie is a person, typically young, who rejects the established social norms and values, often embracing a countercultural lifestyle characterized by peace, love, and freedom.  They often wear unconventional clothing, listen to psychedelic rock music, and advocate for social and political change. |例句：The 1960s saw a rise in the hippie movement.  六十年代见证了嬉皮士运动的兴起。|例句：She adopted a hippie lifestyle, living in a commune and rejecting materialism. 她过上了嬉皮士的生活方式，住在公社里，拒绝物质主义。|近义词：bohemian, free spirit, flower child|反义词：conformist, traditionalist
