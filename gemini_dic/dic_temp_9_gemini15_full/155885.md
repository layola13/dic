photoallergic:/ˌfoʊtoʊælˈɜːrdʒɪk/| adj.|光敏性过敏的|  Relating to or caused by an allergic reaction to sunlight or ultraviolet (UV) light. This reaction typically occurs after exposure to certain substances, like perfumes, sunscreens, or medications, which act as photo sensitizers, making the skin more reactive to UV light.  Symptoms can include rash, itching, blisters, and swelling.|Example:  She experienced a photoallergic reaction after using a new sunscreen.|近义词：photosensitive, light-sensitive|反义词：phototolerant
