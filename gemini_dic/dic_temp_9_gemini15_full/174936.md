radially:/ˈreɪdiəli/| adv.|径向地，辐射状地|In a radial manner; extending in or along radii.  This describes something that moves or is arranged outwards from a central point, like spokes on a wheel.|例句：The spokes extend radially from the hub of the wheel.  轮辐从车轮中心向外辐射状延伸。

例句：Heat is distributed radially from the center of the sun. 热量从太阳中心向外辐射状分布。|近义词：radiantly, outwardly|反义词：centrally
