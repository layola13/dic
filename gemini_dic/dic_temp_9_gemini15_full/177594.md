rebud: /rɪˈbʌd/ | v. | 再发芽；重新萌芽 | To begin to grow again after being cut or damaged; to sprout anew. | 例句：The rose bush rebudded after the harsh winter.  玫瑰在严冬过后重新发芽了。| 例句：The tree rebudded vigorously after the pruning.  这棵树修剪后又长出了很多新芽。| 近义词：sprout, shoot, regenerate | 反义词：wither, die back
