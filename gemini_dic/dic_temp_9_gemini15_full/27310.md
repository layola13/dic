calculate: /ˈkælkjuleɪt/ | vt. | 计算，估算，预测 | To determine or ascertain by mathematical methods; compute.  | 例句：I need to calculate the total cost of the project. = 我需要计算项目的总成本。

例句：She calculated the area of the circle. = 她计算了圆形的面积。


近义词：compute, reckon, estimate, figure, determine | 反义词：None (Antonyms are context-dependent and would relate to the result of the calculation, not the act itself) | 词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He calculates the budget every month. = 他每月都计算预算。
They calculate the risk before making a decision. = 他们做决定前会计算风险。

一般过去时:
She calculated the distance incorrectly. = 她计算距离不正确。
We calculated the profit margin last week. = 我们上周计算了利润率。

现在完成时:
I have calculated the results of the experiment. = 我已经计算出了实验的结果。
They have calculated the trajectory of the rocket. = 他们已经计算出了火箭的轨迹。

过去完成时:
He had calculated the odds before placing the bet. = 他在下注之前已经计算过胜算。
She had calculated the amount of materials needed. = 她已经计算出所需的材料数量。

将来时:
We will calculate the final score after the game. = 我们比赛结束后会计算最终比分。
They will calculate the taxes owed next year. = 他们明年将计算应缴税款。

虚拟语气:

词源:
The word "calculate" ultimately derives from the Latin word "calculus," meaning "small stone" or "pebble."  In ancient times, pebbles were used as counters for counting and calculation.  The Latin word evolved into the Old French "calculer," and finally into the English "calculate."
