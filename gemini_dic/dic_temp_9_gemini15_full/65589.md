evagination:/ˌɛvæɡɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |外翻；外凸；反转；脱出|The outward turning or protrusion of an organ or part.  It often refers to a process in biology where a layer of tissue turns outward.  | Example: The evagination of the neural tube is a crucial step in the development of the nervous system.  神经管的外翻是神经系统发育的关键步骤。|protrusion, eversion, outpouching|invagination, intussusception|名词
