enthuses:/ɪnˈθuːzɪz/| v.|热情洋溢，热衷于|To fill with or show great enthusiasm; to inspire enthusiasm in.|例句：He enthused about his new project. 他对他的新项目充满热情。

例句：Her infectious enthusiasm enthused the entire team. 她极具感染力的热情鼓舞了整个团队。


近义词：excite, inspire, stimulate, fire up|反义词：discourage, depress, dampen

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enthuses about his work every day. 他每天都对他的工作充满热情。
She enthuses over the latest fashion trends. 她对最新的时尚潮流充满热情。


一般过去时:
He enthused about the concert last night. 他昨晚对这场音乐会赞不绝口。
She enthused to her friends about her trip. 她向朋友们兴高采烈地讲述她的旅行经历。


现在完成时:
He has enthused about this idea for months. 他几个月来一直对这个主意充满热情。
She has enthused the entire class with her presentation. 她用她的演示鼓舞了全班同学。


过去完成时:
He had enthused about the possibility before the meeting. 会议之前，他就对这种可能性充满了热情。
She had enthused her team before the competition. 比赛之前，她激励了她的团队。


将来时:
He will enthuse about his success to everyone. 他将会向所有人炫耀他的成功。
She will enthuse the audience with her performance. 她将以她的表演感染观众。


虚拟语气:
If he enthused more, he would be more successful. 如果他更有热情，他会更成功。
If she had enthused earlier, they might have finished sooner. 如果她早点热情参与，他们可能早点完成。


被动语态:
The audience was enthused by his speech. 观众被他讲话所鼓舞。
The team was enthused by her leadership. 团队被她的领导力所激励。


疑问句:
Does he enthuse about his job? 他对他的工作充满热情吗？

否定句:
He didn't enthuse about the new policy. 他对这项新政策并不热情。
She doesn't enthuse about going to the party. 她对去参加派对并不热情。


PHRASE->
enthuse about (something): 对...充满热情
enthuse over (something):  对...赞不绝口
enthuse someone: 激励某人
enthuse with (something): 用...来激励
enthuse to (someone): 向某人热情洋溢地表达
