ricebird: /ˈraɪsbɜːrd/ | n. | 稻米鸟，雀鸟的一种 | A small bird that feeds on rice and other grains, often found in rice paddies.  Many species are called ricebirds, often belonging to different genera and families.  The term is not taxonomically precise. |例句：Many farmers consider ricebirds a pest because they eat their rice crops.  许多农民认为稻米鸟是害虫，因为它们吃他们的稻米作物。

例句：We saw a flock of ricebirds feeding in the paddy field. 我们看到一群稻米鸟在稻田里觅食。


近义词： (depending on the specific species) weaverbird, sparrow, bunting  | 反义词： N/A | 词性: 名词
