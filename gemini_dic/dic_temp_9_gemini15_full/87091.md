her:/hɜːr/|pron.|她，她的|Her refers to a female person or animal previously mentioned.  It functions as a possessive pronoun indicating ownership or relationship.|That's her book.|近义词：hers|反义词：him, his


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She loves her cat. = 她爱她的猫。
She plays the piano. = 她弹钢琴。


一般过去时:
She visited her grandmother. = 她去看望了她的祖母。
She finished her work early. = 她早早完成了她的工作。


现在完成时:
She has finished her homework. = 她已经完成了她的家庭作业。
She has lost her keys. = 她丢了她的钥匙。


过去完成时:
She had already eaten before he arrived. = 他到达之前她已经吃过了。
She had finished her studies before she got married. = 结婚前她已经完成了学业。


将来时:
She will visit her friends next week. = 她下周将拜访她的朋友。
She will complete her project by Friday. = 她将在星期五前完成她的项目。


虚拟语气:
If she were here, she would help us. = 如果她在，她会帮助我们。
I wish she were more careful. = 我希望她更细心一点。


被动语态:
Her car was stolen. = 她的车被偷了。
Her name was mentioned in the report. = 报告中提到了她的名字。


疑问句:
Is this her bag? = 这是她的包吗？
Do you know her address? = 你知道她的地址吗？


否定句:
She doesn't like her job. = 她不喜欢她的工作。
She didn't go to the party. = 她没有去参加聚会。
