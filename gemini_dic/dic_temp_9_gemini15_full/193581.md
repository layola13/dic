self-appointments:/ˌsɛlf əˈpɔɪntmənts/| n. |自我任命；自己安排|Appointments made by oneself, without the authority or approval of others.  This often implies a lack of legitimacy or a presumption of authority.|例句：His self-appointments to various committees were met with skepticism.  他给自己安排了几个委员会的职位，受到了怀疑。

例句：The self-appointments of the board members were challenged in court. 董事会成员的自我任命在法庭上受到了质疑。


近义词：self-designation,  usurpation, arrogation|反义词：appointment by others, official appointment
