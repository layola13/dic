overpopulous: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑpjʊləs/ | adj. |人口过多的，过度拥挤的 |  Having a population that is too large for the available resources.  This adjective describes a place with an excessive number of people, often leading to overcrowding and strain on resources like food, water, and housing. | 例句：The city is overpopulous, leading to traffic congestion and housing shortages.  这座城市人口过多，导致交通拥堵和住房短缺。


The overpopulous island struggled to provide enough food for its inhabitants.  这个人口过多的岛屿难以提供足够的粮食供居民食用。| 近义词：overcrowded, densely populated, congested | 反义词：sparsely populated, underpopulated
