cash: /kæʃ/ | n. |现金，现款 |Ready money; money in the form of coins or banknotes.  |例句：I only have cash on me. = 我身上只有现金。|例句：The store doesn't accept credit cards; they only take cash. = 这家店不接受信用卡，只收现金。|近义词：money, ready money |反义词：credit, debt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have some cash. = 我有一些现金。
He needs cash for the taxi. = 他需要现金付出租车费。


一般过去时:
She paid cash for the groceries. = 她用现金买了杂货。
They cashed a check at the bank. = 他们在银行兑现了一张支票。


现在完成时:
I have cashed my paycheck. = 我已经兑现了我的工资支票。
We have saved some cash for a vacation. = 我们存了一些现金准备度假。


过去完成时:
They had already spent their cash before the trip. = 他们在旅行前就已经花光了现金。
She had cashed a large check earlier that day. = 她当天早些时候已经兑现了一张大额支票。


将来时:
I will get some cash from the ATM. = 我将从自动取款机取一些现金。
They will need cash to buy the tickets. = 他们需要现金购买门票。


虚拟语气:
If I had cash, I would buy that car. = 如果我有现金，我会买那辆车。
I wish I had enough cash to travel the world. = 我希望我有足够的现金环游世界。


被动语态:
The check was cashed. = 支票已兑现。
The money was paid in cash. = 钱是用现金支付的。


疑问句:
Do you have any cash? = 你有现金吗？
How much cash do you need? = 你需要多少钱？


否定句:
I don't have enough cash. = 我没有足够的现金。
They didn't pay in cash. = 他们没有用现金支付。


PHRASE->
cash on delivery (COD) = 货到付款
cash flow = 现金流
cash in = 兑现；套现
cash out = 提现
run out of cash = 用光现金


词源:
The word "cash" ultimately derives from the Old French word "casse," which meant "box" or "chest," specifically a strongbox used for storing money. This evolved through Middle English to its current meaning.
