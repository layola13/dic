escharotic:/ˌɛskəˈrɑːtɪk/| adj.|腐蚀性的；烧灼性的；|Causing a burning or corrosive effect, typically on living tissue.  It refers to substances or agents that cause the formation of an eschar (a dry, dark scab or crust formed over a burn or sore).|例句：The escharotic agent quickly cauterized the wound.  腐蚀剂迅速止住了伤口。

The strong acid had an escharotic effect on the skin. 强酸对皮肤有腐蚀作用。|近义词：caustic, corrosive, cauterizing|反义词：soothing, healing
