desynchronizes:/ˌdiːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪz/ | v. |使不同步；使失去同步 | To cause (something) to lose synchronization; to disrupt the synchronized operation of.  The term implies a previous state of synchronization that is now disrupted. |例句：The power outage desynchronized the factory's production line.  停电使工厂的生产线失去了同步。

例句：The conflicting schedules desynchronized the team's workflow.  相互冲突的时间表打乱了团队的工作流程。

近义词：disrupt, disorganize, derange, uncouple

反义词：synchronize, coordinate, align
