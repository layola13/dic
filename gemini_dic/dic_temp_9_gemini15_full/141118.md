ontologically:/ˌɒntəˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv. |本体论地，从本体论的角度| Relating to ontology, the branch of metaphysics dealing with the nature of being.  It describes something from the perspective of its existence and essence. |The philosopher analyzed the problem ontologically, examining the fundamental nature of existence itself.  哲学家从本体论的角度分析了这个问题，考察了存在本身的根本性质。|  metaphysically, philosophically |  (no direct antonym, but opposites would depend on the specific ontological claim being made)| 副词
