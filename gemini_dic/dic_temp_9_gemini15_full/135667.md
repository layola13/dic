nonprehensile: /ˌnɒnprɪˈhensəl/ | adj. | 非抓握的，不能抓握的 | Not capable of grasping or holding.  This adjective describes something that lacks the ability to grip or hold onto objects.  It's often used in the context of animal anatomy, particularly referring to limbs or appendages. | Example:  The sloth's nonprehensile tail cannot be used for climbing.  树懒的非抓握型尾巴不能用来攀爬。 | 近义词: ungripping, non-grasping | 反义词: prehensile
