goose-stepper: /ˈɡuːsˌstɛpər/ | n. |鹅步走的人，纳粹式正步走的人 | A person who performs the goose step, a military march characterized by stiff, high-kneed steps.  The term often carries a negative connotation, associated with militarism, fascism, and Nazi Germany. | Example: The goose-steppers marched rigidly down the street.  鹅步走的人笔直地走下街道。| 近义词:  marcher (in a goose step),  Nazi (in context),  militarist | 反义词:  pacifist
