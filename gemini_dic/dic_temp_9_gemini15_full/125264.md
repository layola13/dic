multiengine:/ˌmʌltiˈɛndʒɪn/| adj. |多引擎的；多发动机的|Having more than one engine.  Multiengine aircraft are commonly used for commercial flights because of their increased safety and power.|例句：The airline uses only multiengine aircraft for its long-haul flights. 这家航空公司只使用多引擎飞机进行长途飞行。 The new plane is a multiengine turboprop.  这架新飞机是多引擎涡轮螺旋桨飞机。|近义词：multiple-engine|反义词：single-engine
