cocreates:/ˌkoʊkrɪˈeɪts/| vt.|共同创造，协同创作|To create something together; to work collaboratively to bring something into existence.|例句：The artist and the community co-created a stunning mural that reflects their shared history.  艺术家和社区共同创作了一幅反映他们共同历史的令人惊叹的壁画。|例句：The team co-created a new marketing strategy that boosted sales significantly.团队共同制定了一个新的营销策略，显著提高了销量。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, create jointly|反义词：destroy, dismantle, undo|动词


USAGE->
