evasional:/ɪˈveɪʒənl/| adj.|逃避的；推诿的；闪烁其词的|Avoiding or escaping from something; intended to avoid something; characterized by evasion.  It suggests a deliberate attempt to avoid a direct answer or responsibility.|例句：His evasional answers aroused suspicion. 他闪烁其词的回答引起了怀疑。

例句：Her evasional tactics prevented us from getting a straight answer. 她逃避问题的策略让我们无法得到直接的答案。


近义词：evasive, elusive, equivocal, shifty, ambiguous

反义词：direct, frank, straightforward, forthright, candid
