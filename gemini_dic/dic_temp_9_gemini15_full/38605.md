concrete:/ˈkɒŋkriːt/ /ˈkɑːŋkriːt/| adj. n. |具体的；实在的；混凝土|

英文解析：As an adjective, "concrete" means specific, definite, and not abstract.  As a noun, it refers to the building material made from a mixture of cement, sand, and gravel.


例句：
adj. The police need concrete evidence to make an arrest.  警方需要确凿的证据才能逮捕。

adj.  He gave me some concrete suggestions on how to improve my work. 他给了我一些改进工作的具体建议。


n. The foundation of the house is made of concrete. 房子的地基是用混凝土做的。

n.  The workers poured the concrete for the new sidewalk. 工人们为新的走道浇筑了混凝土。


近义词：adj. specific, definite, tangible, particular, solid; n. cement


反义词：adj. abstract, vague, general, intangible


词性：adj., n.
