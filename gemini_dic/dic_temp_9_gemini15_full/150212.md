panda:/ˈpændə/| n. |熊猫|A large black and white bear-like mammal native to south-central China, characterized by its distinctive markings and its diet primarily consisting of bamboo.|Giant pandas are endangered and live in mountainous regions of China. |近义词：bear (in a general sense)|反义词：无
