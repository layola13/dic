peduncular:/pɪˈdʌŋkjələr/| adj.| 茎状的；柄状的；脚状的 | Relating to or resembling a peduncle; stalk-like.  The term often refers to anatomical structures resembling a stem or stalk. |例句：The peduncular attachment of the flower to the stem was quite sturdy.  花朵与茎的茎状连接非常牢固。  The surgeon carefully examined the peduncular region of the brain. 外科医生仔细检查了大脑的脚状区域。|近义词：stalked, stemmed |反义词：sessile (lacking a stalk) |形容词
