dot-com:/ˈdɑːt kɑːm/| n. | 网络公司，点子公司 | A company that conducts most of its business on the internet.  | Example:  Many dot-com companies thrived during the internet boom of the late 1990s.  许多网络公司在20世纪90年代末的互联网繁荣时期蓬勃发展。|近义词：internet company, online business |反义词：brick-and-mortar business

The dot-com company expands its online services. = 这家网络公司正在扩展其在线服务。
This dot-com is developing a new marketing strategy. = 这家点子公司正在制定一项新的营销策略。

一般过去时:
