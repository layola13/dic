couturier:/kuːtʊrˈjeɪ/|/kuːtʊrjɛː/| n. |高级女装设计师；服装设计师|A couturier is a person who designs, makes, and sells fashionable clothes, especially haute couture.  Couturiers are highly skilled and often work with exclusive clientele.|The renowned couturier presented his new collection at Paris Fashion Week.|近义词：fashion designer, dressmaker|反义词：None specifically.  Perhaps "mass producer" in a contrasting sense.
