centiloquy:/ˌsɛnˈtɪləkwi/| n. |细语；窃窃私语；低语|A quiet, private, or secret conversation or utterance.  It often implies a hushed or conspiratorial tone.|例句：The centiloquy in the corner booth suggested a clandestine meeting. 角落包厢里的窃窃私语暗示着一次秘密会面。|例句：Their centiloquy was overheard by a sharp-eared waitress.  他们窃窃私语被一个耳朵灵敏的服务员听到了。|近义词：whispering, murmuring, muttering, sotto voce|反义词： shouting, bellowing, clamor
