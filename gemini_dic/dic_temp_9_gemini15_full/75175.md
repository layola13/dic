friz:/frɪz/| v. n.|卷曲；使卷曲；卷发；(头发)卷曲|To curl or cause to curl, especially hair.  The verb refers to the action of making something curly, while the noun refers to the curly state itself. |例句：The wool frized easily. 这羊毛很容易卷曲。  Her hair frized in the humidity.  她的头发在潮湿的环境下卷曲了。|近义词：curl, crimp, kink|反义词：straighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wool frizes easily. = 这羊毛很容易卷曲。
My hair frizes in the humidity. = 我的头发在潮湿的环境下卷曲了。

一般过去时:
The wool frized easily in the dryer. = 这羊毛在烘干机里很容易卷曲。
Her hair frized after she washed it. = 她洗完头后，头发卷起来了。


现在完成时:

My hair has frized up in this damp weather. = 在这种潮湿的天气里，我的头发都卷起来了。


过去完成时:
