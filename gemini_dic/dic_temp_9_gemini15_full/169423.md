pronouncement:/prəˈnaʊnsmənt/| n. |宣布；声明；宣告 |A formal or authoritative statement; an announcement.  |例句：The president's pronouncement on the new policy was met with mixed reactions.总统关于新政策的声明受到了褒贬不一的反应。 |例句：The judge's pronouncement of the verdict ended the long trial.法官宣判结束了漫长的审判。|近义词：announcement, declaration, proclamation |反义词：withdrawal, retraction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government makes frequent pronouncements on economic policy. = 政府经常就经济政策发表声明。
His pronouncements on the matter are always clear and concise. = 他对此事的声明总是清晰简洁。


一般过去时:
The company made a surprising pronouncement about its future plans. = 公司对其未来计划做出了令人惊讶的声明。
She made a public pronouncement of her support for the candidate. = 她公开宣布支持这位候选人。


现在完成时:
The committee has made several pronouncements on the issue. = 委员会已经就此事发表了几项声明。
The scientist has made numerous pronouncements about the discovery. = 科学家已经就这一发现发表了许多声明。


过去完成时:
They had already made a pronouncement before the meeting began. = 他们在会议开始之前就已经发表了声明。
The judge had made his pronouncement before the lawyers could object. = 在律师们提出异议之前，法官已经做出了判决。


将来时:
The spokesperson will make a pronouncement on the situation later today. = 发言人将在今天晚些时候就此事发表声明。
The CEO will make a significant pronouncement regarding the merger. = 首席执行官将就并购事宜发表重要声明。


虚拟语气:
If the judge had made a different pronouncement, the outcome would have been different. = 如果法官做出不同的判决，结果就会不同。
I wish the government would make a clear pronouncement on the matter. = 我希望政府能就此问题发表明确的声明。


被动语态:
A statement was made, a pronouncement given. = 做出了声明，给出了宣告。
The policy was announced with a formal pronouncement. = 该政策以正式声明的方式宣布。


疑问句:
What was the content of the pronouncement? = 声明的内容是什么？
What pronouncement did the court make on the case? = 法院对该案件作出了什么判决？


否定句:
The government did not make any pronouncement on the issue. = 政府没有就此事发表任何声明。
There was no official pronouncement from the authorities. = 当局没有发布任何官方声明。
