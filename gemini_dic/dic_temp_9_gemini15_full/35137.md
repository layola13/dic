cloudxr:/ˈklaʊdˌɛksˈɑːr/| n.|云XR|Cloud XR refers to extended reality (XR) experiences delivered via cloud computing.  This allows for more powerful and immersive experiences than would be possible on local devices alone, as processing and rendering are handled remotely.  It also enables access to XR content on less powerful devices.|Example: Cloud XR is revolutionizing the gaming industry by enabling high-fidelity graphics on mobile devices.|近义词:cloud-based XR, remote rendering XR|反义词:local XR, on-device XR|名词
