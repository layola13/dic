rurigenous:/ruːˈrɪdʒənəs/| adj.|土著的；本地的；|Indigenous or native to a particular region or place. The word combines "ruri" (countryside, rural) and "-genous" (producing, originating).  It implies a connection to the land and a long history in a specific area.|例句：The rurigenous population of the island maintained their traditional way of life for centuries.  岛上的土著居民世世代代保持着他们的传统生活方式。|近义词：indigenous, native, aboriginal|反义词：alien, foreign, exotic
