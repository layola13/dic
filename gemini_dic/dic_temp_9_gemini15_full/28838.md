carpel:/ˈkɑːrpəl/ | n. | 心皮 | The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of the stigma, style, and ovary.  It produces ovules, which develop into seeds after fertilization.  A single carpel may form a simple pistil, or multiple carpels may fuse to form a compound pistil. | Example: The flower's carpel contained several ovules. 这朵花的心皮包含几个胚珠。  The carpels of this plant are fused together.  这种植物的心皮融合在一起。 | 近义词：pistil (although pistil can refer to a single or multiple fused carpels) | 反义词：stamen (male reproductive organ)
