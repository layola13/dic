playground's:/ˈpleɪɡraʊndz/| n. |操场的；游乐场的|The possessive form of "playground," indicating ownership or belonging.  It refers to something that belongs to or is associated with a playground. For example, the playground's equipment, the playground's rules.|例句：The playground's swings were rusty.  操场的秋千生锈了。|The playground's surface was covered in sand. 操场的地面覆盖着沙子。|近义词: play area's, recreation area's|反义词:  N/A
