It often implies resourcefulness and adaptability in the face of constraints.  The term is particularly associated with India, reflecting a culture of innovation under conditions of scarcity. |例句：They used a jugaad to fix the broken engine using only wire and tape.  他们用电线和胶带土办法修好了坏掉的引擎。
