ratoon:/ræˈtuːn/| n.| 甘蔗芽；嫩枝；再生枝 | A ratoon is a new shoot that grows from the base of a plant, especially a sugarcane plant, after the main stem has been harvested.  It's essentially a regrowth. | Example:  The farmer carefully nurtured the sugarcane ratoons to ensure a good second harvest. = 农民仔细培育甘蔗芽以确保获得良好的第二茬收成。|近义词：shoot, sprout, sucker|反义词：None specifically, but words like "mature plant" or "harvested stalk" could be considered opposites in context.|名词
