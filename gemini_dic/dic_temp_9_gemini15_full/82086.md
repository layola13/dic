groaned:/ɡroʊnd/ | /ɡrəʊnd/ | vt. vi. |呻吟；抱怨；发出咯吱声 |To make a deep, low sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or disapproval.  |例句：He groaned in pain after falling. 他摔倒后痛苦地呻吟着。  |例句：She groaned about the amount of work she had to do. 她抱怨自己有很多工作要做。|近义词：moan, whine, complain |反义词：exult, rejoice |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He groans every time he bends down. = 他每次弯腰都会呻吟。
She groans about the weather every day. = 她每天都抱怨天气。

一般过去时:
He groaned loudly when the doctor touched his wound. = 当医生触碰他的伤口时，他大声呻吟。
She groaned and turned over in bed. = 她呻吟一声，在床上翻了个身。

现在完成时:
He has groaned with exhaustion after the marathon. = 马拉松后，他累得直呻吟。
She has groaned about her boss all week. = 她整个星期都在抱怨她的老板。

过去完成时:
He had groaned with pain before the ambulance arrived. = 救护车到达之前，他已经痛苦地呻吟了。
She had groaned under the weight of her responsibilities. = 她一直在为自己的责任而苦不堪言。

将来时:
He will groan when he sees the bill. = 他看到账单时会呻吟的。
She will groan if she has to work overtime again. = 如果她不得不再次加班，她会抱怨的。

虚拟语气:
If he knew how much it would hurt, he wouldn't have groaned. = 如果他知道会这么痛，他就不会呻吟了。
I wish I hadn't groaned so loudly; everyone heard me. = 我真希望我没有大声呻吟；每个人都听到了。

被动语态:
A groan was heard from the next room. = 从隔壁房间传来一声呻吟。
His complaints were groaned about by his colleagues. = 他的抱怨受到了同事们的抱怨。

疑问句:
Did he groan in his sleep? = 他睡着时呻吟了吗？
Why did she groan when I mentioned the project? = 当我提到这个项目时，她为什么呻吟？

否定句:
He didn't groan, he just silently endured the pain. = 他没有呻吟，只是默默地忍受着疼痛。
She didn't groan at the extra work; she just got on with it. = 她并没有因为额外的工作而抱怨；她只是继续做。
