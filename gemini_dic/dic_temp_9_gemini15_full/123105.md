mongrelisms:/ˈmɒŋɡrəlɪzəm/| n. |混血儿语；混杂语；混种语言|Words or phrases considered nonstandard or of dubious origin, often resulting from a mixture of languages or dialects.  They are viewed negatively, implying a lack of purity or elegance. The term often carries a derogatory connotation.|The overuse of slang and colloquialisms in his writing was criticized as mere mongrelisms.|近义词：barbarisms, solecisms, vulgarisms|反义词：purity, elegance
