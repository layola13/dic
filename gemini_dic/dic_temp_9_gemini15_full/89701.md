hoovering:/ˈhuːvərɪŋ/| v.|吸尘，徘徊，犹豫不决|The act of using a vacuum cleaner to clean a surface; also, the act of lingering or hesitating.|例句：She spent the morning hoovering the carpets. 她花了整个上午吸地毯。|例句：He stood there, hoovering at the doorway. 他站在那里，在门口徘徊。|近义词：vacuuming, lingering, hesitating|反义词： rushing, hurrying

词源:
The verb "hoover" is derived from the brand name "Hoover," a well-known manufacturer of vacuum cleaners.  The brand name became so widely associated with the act of vacuuming that it became a genericized trademark, meaning the brand name is now used as the common name for the action.
