nonquality:/ˈnɒnkwɒləti/ | n. |非质量；劣质；次品|The lack of quality; something of inferior quality.  It refers to characteristics or aspects that detract from the overall quality or value of a product, service, or process.  This term is often used in the context of quality management and control.|例句：The high rate of nonquality in the manufacturing process resulted in significant losses.  生产过程中大量的次品导致了严重的损失。  The company implemented new quality control measures to reduce nonquality. 公司实施了新的质量控制措施以减少次品。|近义词：inferiority, defect, flaw, imperfection|反义词：quality, excellence, superiority|名词
