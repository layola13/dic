downtrend:/ˈdaʊnˌtrend/| n. |下降趋势，下跌趋势|A downtrend refers to a general direction of prices, values, or other data points moving consistently lower over time.  It suggests a negative trend or pattern of decline.|例句：The stock market experienced a sharp downtrend after the news broke.  股市在新闻发布后经历了急剧下跌。

例句：There is a clear downtrend in sales this quarter.  本季度销售额明显呈下降趋势。


近义词：decline, decrease, fall, drop, slump|反义词：uptrend, rise, increase, growth, upswing|词性：名词
