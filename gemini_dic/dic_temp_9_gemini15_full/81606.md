greatest:/ˈɡreɪtɪst/| adj.| 最伟大的，最大的；最好的 |  The word "greatest" is the superlative form of the adjective "great," indicating the highest degree of greatness, size, importance, or excellence. It signifies something surpassing all others in quality or magnitude. |例句：Shakespeare is considered the greatest writer in the English language.  莎士比亚被认为是英语中最伟大的作家。

This is the greatest victory of my career. 这是我职业生涯中最大的胜利。

|近义词：best, utmost, supreme, most significant |反义词：least, smallest, worst, insignificant |词性:形容词
