diluted:/daɪˈluːtɪd/| adj.|稀释的；冲淡的；减弱的|Weakened or made less effective by being mixed with something else.|The acid was diluted with water.  酸被水稀释了。The impact of the news was diluted by the constant stream of other information.  不断涌现的其他信息冲淡了这条新闻的影响。|concentrated, strong|undiluted, concentrated, strong


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The paint is diluted with water. = 颜料用水稀释。
This solution is diluted with alcohol. = 这种溶液用酒精稀释。

一般过去时:
The juice was diluted before serving. = 果汁在食用前被稀释了。
The teacher diluted the complex topic for the students. = 老师为学生们简化了复杂的主题。

现在完成时:
The company has diluted its market share. = 公司已经稀释了它的市场份额。
The government has diluted its environmental protection policies. = 政府已经削弱了它的环保政策。

过去完成时:
The color had been diluted before it was applied. = 颜色在使用前已经被稀释了。
The impact of the speech had been diluted by the preceding presentations. = 演讲的影响已经被之前的演讲削弱了。


将来时:
The chemist will dilute the solution tomorrow. = 化学家明天将稀释溶液。
They will dilute the paint before painting the wall. = 他们在粉刷墙壁前会稀释油漆。

虚拟语气:
If the solution were diluted, it would be safer. = 如果溶液被稀释，它会更安全。
I wish the strong coffee had been diluted a bit. = 我希望浓咖啡能稀释一点。

被动语态:
The solution was diluted by the technician. = 溶液被技术员稀释了。
The message was diluted by the poor translation. = 信息由于翻译不佳而被冲淡了。

疑问句:

否定句:

The impact of the announcement was not diluted by the media. = 公告的影响没有被媒体冲淡。
