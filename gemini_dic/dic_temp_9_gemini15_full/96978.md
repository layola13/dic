infantile: /ˈɪnfənˌtaɪl/ | adj. |幼稚的，孩子气的 |  Characterized by or suitable for an infant; childish; immature.  Showing a lack of maturity. |


例句：His infantile behavior at the meeting annoyed everyone.  他的会议上的幼稚行为惹恼了所有人。

例句：The novel's plot is rather infantile and predictable. 这部小说的情节相当幼稚和可预测。


近义词：childish, immature, puerile, juvenile


反义词：mature, adult, sophisticated


词源：From Late Latin *infantillis, from Latin infans "infant," from in- "not" + fari "to speak."  The word literally means "unable to speak," reflecting the inability of infants to articulate complex thoughts.
