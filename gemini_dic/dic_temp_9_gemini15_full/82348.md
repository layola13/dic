growbag:/ˈɡroʊbæɡ/| n.|育苗袋，种植袋|A growbag is a bag made of a fabric-like material, often a woven polypropylene, used for growing plants.  They are typically filled with a growing medium such as compost or coco coir and are designed to be convenient and efficient for cultivating plants, particularly vegetables and seedlings.| Example: I planted my tomatoes in growbags this year.今年我把西红柿种在了育苗袋里。|近义词：planting bag, plant bag|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与传统种植方法对比)|名词
