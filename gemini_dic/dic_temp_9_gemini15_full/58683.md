dump-on:/dʌmpˈɒn/ | vt. | 倾倒在…上；把…推卸给；责备；抱怨 | To unload something or someone onto; to criticize heavily; to shift responsibility onto someone else. | 例句：Don't dump all your problems on me.  别把你的所有问题都推卸给我。 He dumped on his assistant for the missed deadline. 他因为错过最后期限而责备他的助理。 | 近义词：unload, criticize, blame, reprimand | 反义词：support, help, praise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dumps his work on his colleagues. = 他把工作推卸给同事。
They dump all their garbage on the street. = 他们把垃圾都扔在街上。

一般过去时:
She dumped her feelings on her best friend. = 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的感受。
He dumped the blame on his partner. = 他把责任推卸给了他的搭档。

现在完成时:
They have dumped all the responsibility on the new manager. = 他们已经把所有责任都推卸给了新经理。
She has dumped her emotional baggage on me for weeks. = 她已经好几个星期把她的情绪包袱都倾泻到我身上了。

过去完成时:
He had dumped all the dirty dishes in the sink before leaving. = 他离开前把所有脏盘子都扔进了水槽。
They had dumped the project onto the junior team. = 他们已将项目推卸给了初级团队。

将来时:
She will dump her old clothes at the charity shop. = 她将会把旧衣服捐给慈善商店。
He will dump the entire problem on his supervisor. = 他将会把整个问题推卸给他的主管。

虚拟语气:
If he dumped all his worries on someone, he might feel better. = 如果他向某人倾诉他的烦恼，他可能会感觉好些。
I wish he wouldn't dump all his problems on me. = 我希望他不要把所有问题都推卸给我。

被动语态:
The extra work was dumped on the interns. = 额外的任务被推卸给了实习生。
The responsibility was unfairly dumped on the junior staff. = 责任被不公平地推卸给了初级员工。

疑问句:
Why are you dumping all your stress on me? = 你为什么把所有的压力都发泄在我身上？
Where did they dump all that waste? = 他们把所有的废物都扔到哪里去了？

否定句:
Don't dump your anxieties on others. = 不要把你的焦虑强加于他人。
He didn't dump his responsibilities. = 他没有推卸他的责任。
