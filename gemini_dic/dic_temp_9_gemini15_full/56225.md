does:/dʌz/| /dʌz/ | aux.v. |助动词；做；进行|Does is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "do."  It's used to form questions, negative sentences, and emphatic statements, primarily in the simple present tense.  It can also be used with other verb tenses to create more complex constructions.|

例句：
Does she like apples? = 她喜欢苹果吗？

He does not understand. = 他不明白。


近义词：performs, accomplishes, executes (in certain contexts) | 反义词：None (as an auxiliary verb)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Does he play the piano? = 他弹钢琴吗？

Does she work here? = 她在这里工作吗？


一般过去时: (Does is not used in the past tense; "did" is used instead)
Did he go to school? = 他去上学了吗？

Did she eat lunch? = 她吃午饭了吗？


现在完成时: (Does is not directly used;  "has" or "have" are used depending on the subject)
Has he finished his work? = 他完成他的工作了吗？

Have they seen the movie? = 他们看过这部电影了吗？


过去完成时: (Does is not directly used; "had" is used)
Had she completed her assignment? = 她完成了她的作业了吗？

Had they left before the storm? = 暴风雨来临之前他们离开了吗？


将来时: (Does is not directly used; "will" is used)
Will he come tomorrow? = 他明天会来吗？

Will she be here on time? = 她会准时到吗？


虚拟语气: (Does is not directly used; other auxiliary verbs are used depending on the context)
If he were here, it would be better. = 如果他在就好了。

I wish she would call me. = 我希望她能打电话给我。


被动语态: (Does is not directly used; "is," "are," "was," "were" are used with the past participle of the main verb)
The work is done. = 工作完成了。

The letter was written by him. = 这封信是他写的。


疑问句: (Does is used to form questions in the simple present tense)
Does it rain often here? = 这儿经常下雨吗？

Does he know the answer? = 他知道答案吗？


否定句: (Does is used with "not" to form negative sentences in the simple present tense)
He does not smoke. = 他不抽烟。

She does not like spicy food. = 她不喜欢辛辣食物。



PHRASE->
Does anyone know? = 有谁知道吗？
Does it matter? = 这重要吗？
Does that make sense? = 这说得通吗？
It does not matter. = 没关系。
Does this work? = 这个有用吗？
