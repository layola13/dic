maladministrator: /ˌmælædmɪnɪˈstreɪtər/ | n. | 拙劣的管理者；不称职的管理人员 | A person who manages something badly or inefficiently; an incompetent administrator.  | The maladministrator's decisions led to the company's downfall.  这个拙劣的管理者的决定导致了公司的倒闭。 | inept administrator, incompetent manager, bungler | effective administrator, competent manager | 名词
