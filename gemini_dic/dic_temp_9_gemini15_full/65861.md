evocator:/ˈɛvəkeɪtər/ | n. |唤起者； gợi lên người | One that evokes or calls forth something.  An evocator is someone or something that brings something to mind or into existence; it can be a person, an object, a piece of music, or even a smell.  | The old house was an evocator of happy childhood memories.  这座老房子唤起了快乐的童年回忆。| The painting served as an evocator of the artist's inner turmoil. 这幅画体现了艺术家内心的骚动。| conjurer, stimulator | inhibitor, suppressor | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma evokes memories of my grandmother's kitchen. = 这香味唤起了我对祖母厨房的回忆。
The musician's performance evokes strong emotions in the audience. = 音乐家的表演激起了观众强烈的情感。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
