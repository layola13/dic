shortlisted:/ˈʃɔːrtlɪstɪd/| adj.|入围的，被列入候选名单的|Selected from a larger group for further consideration.  The shortlisted candidates will be interviewed next week. 入围的候选人将在下周接受面试。|近义词：selected, nominated|反义词：rejected, eliminated|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company shortlisted five candidates for the position. = 公司为该职位选出了五位候选人。
Many talented musicians are shortlisted for the award. = 许多有才华的音乐家入围了该奖项。


一般过去时:
They shortlisted me for the job interview. = 他们选中我参加工作面试。
The judges shortlisted ten paintings for the final round. = 评委们为决赛选出了十幅画作。


现在完成时:
The committee has shortlisted three proposals for the project. = 委员会已经为该项目选出了三个提案。
They have shortlisted several applicants for the scholarship. = 他们已经为奖学金选出了几名申请者。


过去完成时:
The organizers had shortlisted the participants before the competition began. = 比赛开始前，组织者已经选出了参赛者。
By the deadline, they had shortlisted all the applicants. = 在截止日期前，他们已经筛选出了所有申请者。


将来时:
The panel will shortlist the best candidates by Friday. = 专家组将在周五之前选出最佳候选人。
They will shortlist the finalists for the competition next week. = 他们将在下周选出比赛的决赛选手。


虚拟语气:
If they had shortlisted me, I would have been thrilled. = 如果他们选中我，我会非常兴奋。
I wish they had shortlisted my application. = 我希望他们选中我的申请。


被动语态:
Five candidates were shortlisted for the final interview. = 五位候选人入围了最终面试。
Many applications were shortlisted for the next stage. = 许多申请被选入下一阶段。


疑问句:
Were you shortlisted for the role? = 你入围这个角色了吗？
Have they shortlisted the winners yet? = 他们已经选出获奖者了吗？


否定句:
I wasn't shortlisted for the job. = 我没有入围这份工作。
Unfortunately, my application wasn't shortlisted. = 不幸的是，我的申请没有入围。
