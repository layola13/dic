colluder:/kəˈluːdə(r)/| n. |共谋者，串通者 |A person who colludes with another or others to achieve a secret, usually illegal, purpose.  |例句：The investigators uncovered a network of colluders who rigged the bidding process.  调查人员发现了一个操纵投标过程的共谋者网络。
例句：He was accused of being a colluder in the fraudulent scheme. 他被指控是该欺诈计划的共谋者。|近义词：conspirator, accomplice, partner in crime|反义词：informant, whistleblower|名词
