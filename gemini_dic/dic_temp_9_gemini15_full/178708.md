recruited:/rɪˈkruːtɪd/| v. | 招募，征募 |  Past participle of "recruit". It describes the state of having been enlisted or hired, especially for military service or a particular job. | 例句：The company recruited 50 new graduates last year.  = 公司去年招募了50名应届毕业生。
The army recruited many young men during the war. = 战争期间，军队招募了许多年轻人。


近义词：enlisted, hired, engaged, employed


反义词：discharged, dismissed, fired


词性：动词(过去分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recruit new employees every year. = 他们每年都招募新员工。
The army recruits young people with strong physical fitness. = 军队招募身体素质强的年轻人。

一般过去时:
The company recruited 100 new staff members last quarter. = 公司上个季度招募了100名新员工。
They recruited volunteers to help with the disaster relief. = 他们招募志愿者来帮助救灾。

现在完成时:
They have recruited several talented engineers this year. = 他们今年已经招募了几位有才华的工程师。
The company has recruited over 500 employees since its establishment. = 自成立以来，该公司已招募了500多名员工。

过去完成时:
They had recruited all the necessary personnel before the project started. = 项目开始之前，他们已经招募了所有必要的人员。
The team had recruited its key players long before the season began. = 球队在赛季开始很久之前就已经招募了其关键球员。

将来时:
We will recruit more staff once the budget is approved. = 一旦预算获得批准，我们将招募更多员工。
The company plans to recruit extensively next year. = 公司计划明年大规模招聘。

虚拟语气:
If we had recruited more staff, we could have finished the project earlier. = 如果我们招募更多员工，我们就能更早完成项目。
If they recruited more experienced workers, the quality of the work would be better. = 如果他们招募更有经验的工人，工作质量就会更好。

被动语态:
Many new employees were recruited last month. = 上个月招募了许多新员工。
A large number of volunteers were recruited for the marathon. = 马拉松比赛招募了大量的志愿者。

疑问句:
How many people were recruited for the project? = 该项目招募了多少人？
Where did they recruit the new employees? = 他们在哪里招募的新员工？

否定句:
The company did not recruit any new staff members this year. = 公司今年没有招募任何新员工。
They haven't recruited enough people for the event yet. = 他们还没有为活动招募足够的人。
