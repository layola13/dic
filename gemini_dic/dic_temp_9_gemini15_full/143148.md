outcharged:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrdʒd/| v.| 超过收费；索价过高 | To charge more than is reasonable or fair.  This word is a relatively uncommon formation, combining "out-" (exceeding) with "charged" (to demand a price). |例句：The hotel outcharged us for the room service.  这家酒店在客房服务方面向我们收取了过高的费用。
The mechanic outcharged me for the repairs. 这个技工修理费收了我太多。|近义词：overcharge, gouge, fleece |反义词：undercharge
