misspent:/ˌmɪsˈspent/| adj. | 浪费的；虚度光阴的 |  Spent wrongly or unprofitably; wasted.  |例句：He regretted his misspent youth. 他后悔自己虚度了青春。

例句：Those were misspent years of his life. 那些是他生命中浪费的岁月。


近义词：wasted, squandered, thrown away | 反义词：well-spent, profitably spent, productively used | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His time is misspent on video games. = 他的时间浪费在电子游戏上了。
Their efforts are misspent on an impossible goal. = 他们的努力浪费在一个不可能实现的目标上。


一般过去时:
He realized he had misspent his inheritance. = 他意识到他挥霍了他的遗产。
She regretted the misspent years she had dedicated to a failing business. = 她后悔将多年时间投入到一个失败的企业中。


现在完成时:
She has misspent most of her savings. = 她已经挥霍了大部分积蓄。
They have misspent their energies on trivial matters. = 他们把精力浪费在琐碎的事情上了。


过去完成时:
He realized he had misspent his youth before he turned forty. = 他在四十岁之前才意识到自己虚度了青春。
She regretted that she had misspent her scholarship money. = 她后悔自己挥霍了奖学金。


将来时:
If he continues like this, he will misspend all his money. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会把所有的钱都挥霍掉。
They fear that they will misspend their opportunity. = 他们担心他们会错过机会。


虚拟语气:
If only he hadn't misspent his opportunities! = 要是他没有浪费机会就好了！
I wish I hadn't misspent my childhood. = 我真希望我没有虚度童年。


被动语态:
His talent was misspent on unimportant tasks. = 他的才能被浪费在不重要的任务上。
Their resources were misspent on a poorly planned project. = 他们的资源被浪费在一个计划糟糕的项目上。


疑问句:
Was his life misspent? = 他的一生是虚度了吗？
Were their efforts misspent? = 他们的努力是白费了吗？


否定句:
He didn't misspend his money; he invested it wisely. = 他没有挥霍他的钱；他明智地投资了。
She didn't misspend her time; she used it productively. = 她没有浪费时间；她有效地利用了它。


PHRASE->

词源:
mis- (错误地，坏地) + spent (花费，度过)
