gofer:/ˈɡoʊfər/ | n. |跑腿的人，杂役 |A gofer is someone whose job is to run errands and do small tasks for others, often in an office or studio setting.  They are typically low-level employees who assist higher-ups. |例句：He started as a lowly gofer, fetching coffee and making copies, but eventually worked his way up to become a senior partner. 他一开始只是个跑腿的，负责端咖啡和复印文件，但最终晋升为高级合伙人。|近义词：errand boy/girl, assistant, messenger |反义词：boss, manager, executive
