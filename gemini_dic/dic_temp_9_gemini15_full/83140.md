guts:/ɡʌts/| n.|勇气；胆量；内脏|The word "guts" refers to courage and determination, especially in difficult situations. It can also refer to the internal organs of an animal.  |例句：He showed great guts in facing the danger. 他面对危险表现出极大的勇气。  |近义词：courage, bravery, nerve, fortitude, spunk |反义词：cowardice, fear, timidity|名词
