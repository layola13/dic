It refers to actions or inactions that are not illegal or do not constitute a crime.  The plural form emphasizes the multiple occurrences of such non-criminal behavior. | Example:  The report highlighted the increase in noncriminalities among the youth after the implementation of the new community program.  这份报告强调了新社区计划实施后青少年非犯罪行为的增加。 |近义词: law-abiding behavior, non-offenses, innocent acts |反义词: crimes, offenses, illegalities |名词
