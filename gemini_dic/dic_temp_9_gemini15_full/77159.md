gask:/ɡæsk/ | n. |衬垫；垫圈；密封垫|A gasket is a flat, typically ring-shaped piece of material used to seal a joint between two surfaces.  It prevents leakage of liquids, gases, or pressure. |Example: The mechanic replaced the faulty head gasket in the car engine. 机械师更换了汽车发动机上故障的缸垫。|近义词：packing, seal, washer |反义词：opening, gap
