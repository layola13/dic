secularism:/ˈsɛkjələrɪzəm/ | n. | 世俗主义 | The principle of separation of the state from religious institutions and the belief that religion should not play a role in public life.  It emphasizes reason, ethics, and humanism over religious dogma.  | Example:  The rise of secularism in the West led to a decline in religious observance.  世俗主义在西方的兴起导致宗教信仰的下降。


Example: The government's policies reflect a commitment to strict secularism. 政府的政策反映了对严格世俗主义的承诺。 | 近义词：nonreligiousness, materialism, atheism (in some contexts) | 反义词：clericalism, theocracy, religious fundamentalism | 词性：名词
