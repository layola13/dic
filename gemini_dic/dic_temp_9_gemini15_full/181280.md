reinsulates: /ˌriːˈɪnsjʊleɪts/ | vt. | 重新隔热 | To insulate again; to apply insulation to something a second time.  |例句：The homeowner decided to reinsulate the attic after discovering insufficient insulation. 房主发现阁楼隔热不足，决定重新隔热。

例句：We need to reinsulate the pipes to prevent freezing during the winter. 我们需要重新给管道保温以防止冬天冻裂。


近义词：re-insulate, re-line (in the context of pipes or similar)


反义词：de-insulate, remove insulation


词性:动词
