overdrafting: /ˌoʊvərˈdræftɪŋ/ | v. |透支；超支| The act of withdrawing more money from a bank account than is available. This typically results in fees and potentially negative consequences.|例句：He was charged a hefty fee for overdrafting his account. 他因为透支账户而被收取了高额费用。


例句：The company is facing financial difficulties due to overdrafting its bank accounts repeatedly.  这家公司由于反复透支银行账户而面临财务困境。


近义词：exceeding, exceeding the limit, overdrawing


反义词：saving, depositing


词性：动词
