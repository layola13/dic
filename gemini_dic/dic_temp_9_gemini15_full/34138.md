claimants:/ˈkleɪmənts/| n.|索赔人，申请人|People who are making a claim, especially a claim for money.  |例句：The claimants are seeking compensation for their injuries. 索赔人正在寻求因其受伤而获得赔偿。|例句：Many claimants are waiting for their benefits payments. 许多申请人正在等待他们的福利金支付。|近义词：applicants, petitioners, demanders|反义词：payers, defendants


USAGE->

The claimants demand compensation. = 索赔人要求赔偿。
The claimants file their claims. = 索赔人提交他们的索赔申请。


一般过去时:
