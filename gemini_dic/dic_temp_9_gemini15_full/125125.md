multi-electrode:/ˌmʌltiˈɛləktrəʊd/| n. |多电极|A multi-electrode array is a device with multiple electrodes used for simultaneously recording or stimulating electrical activity from multiple points in a biological system or circuit.  It's commonly used in neuroscience, biomedical engineering, and other fields requiring high-density signal acquisition.|Example: Researchers used a multi-electrode array to record neural activity in the brain.  研究人员使用多电极阵列记录大脑中的神经活动。|近义词:multi-channel electrode array, microelectrode array|反义词:single-electrode|名词
