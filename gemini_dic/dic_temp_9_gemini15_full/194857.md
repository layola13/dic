semi-chemical:/ˈsɛmɪˈkɛmɪkəl/| adj.|半化学的；半化学浆的|Relating to or denoting a pulping process for wood that uses both chemical and mechanical means to break down the wood fibers.  It's a middle ground between purely mechanical pulping (groundwood) and purely chemical pulping (kraft or sulfite).|例句：Semi-chemical pulping is a cost-effective method for producing pulp for newsprint. 半化学制浆是生产新闻纸浆的经济有效方法。
例句：The mill uses a semi-chemical process to produce a pulp suitable for corrugating medium. 该厂采用半化学法生产适用于瓦楞纸板的纸浆。|近义词：part-chemical,mechanochemical|反义词:fully chemical, fully mechanical
