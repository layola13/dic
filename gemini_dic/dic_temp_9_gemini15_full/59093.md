dyeing:/ˈdaɪɪŋ/| v.|染色；着色|The act of coloring a fabric or other material using dyes.  It involves applying a dye to a substrate to change its color.|例句：She is dyeing her hair blonde. 她正在把头发染成金色。

例句：The fabric is being dyed a deep blue. 这块布料正在被染成深蓝色。


近义词：coloring, staining, painting|反义词：bleaching, fading|词性：动词
