英文解析: Suggesting or implying evil, misfortune, or danger.  Often used to describe something that is ominous or threatening.  It can also, less commonly, refer to something pertaining to the left side.


例句:
The sinistrous laughter echoed through the empty halls. = 凶险的笑声在空荡的大厅里回荡。

近义词: ominous, threatening, baleful, sinister, inauspicious

反义词: auspicious, propitious, favorable, benign


词源: From Latin *sinister, meaning "left," later acquiring the sense of "unlucky" or "evil" because the left side was traditionally associated with bad omens.
