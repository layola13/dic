retransplanted:/ˌriːˈtrænzˌplæntɪd/| v. |再移植，重新移植|To transplant again or anew.  The act of replanting something that has already been transplanted once. This often refers to plants, organs, or even tissues.|例句：The surgeon retransplanted the kidney after it showed signs of rejection.  外科医生在肾脏出现排斥迹象后又重新移植了它。

例句：The gardener retransplanted the seedlings after the heavy rain washed away some of the soil. 园丁在暴雨冲走了一些泥土后重新移植了幼苗。

近义词：replant, transplant again
反义词：remove, extract
