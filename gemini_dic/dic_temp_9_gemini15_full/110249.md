lifesaving: /ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/ | adj. | 救命的，挽救生命的 |  Able to save a person's life; crucial in preventing death.  | 例句：The quick actions of the lifeguard were lifesaving.  = 救生员迅速的行动挽救了生命。

The doctor's lifesaving surgery gave him a second chance. = 医生挽救生命的的手术给了他第二次机会。


近义词：life-preserving, crucial, vital, essential | 反义词：lethal, deadly, fatal | 词性: adjective
