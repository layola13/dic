ferre:/ˈfɛriː/|v.|运载，搬运|to carry or transport something.|The ants ferried food back to the nest.  蚂蚁把食物运回巢穴。|近义词：carry, convey, transport |反义词：unload, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ferries passengers across the river every day. 他每天摆渡乘客过河。
The company ferries goods between cities. 这家公司在城市之间运输货物。

一般过去时:
They ferried supplies to the disaster area. 他们把物资运送到灾区。
The ship ferried immigrants to a new land. 这艘船把移民运送到新的土地。

现在完成时:
The workers have ferried all the equipment to the construction site. 工人们已经把所有设备运送到施工现场。
She has ferried countless passengers during her career. 在她的职业生涯中，她已经运送了无数的乘客。

过去完成时:
The team had ferried the injured to the hospital before the ambulance arrived. 救护车到达之前，团队已经把伤员送到了医院。
Before the storm hit, they had ferried all the livestock to safety. 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把所有的牲畜转移到了安全地带。

将来时:
They will ferry the tourists to the island tomorrow. 他们明天将把游客送到岛上。
The new company will ferry goods across the country. 新公司将负责在全国范围内运输货物。

虚拟语气:
If I had a boat, I would ferry people across the lake. 如果我有一艘船，我会摆渡人们过湖。
I wish I could ferry my family to a safer place. 我希望我能把我的家人送到一个更安全的地方。

被动语态:
The goods were ferried by truck. 货物由卡车运输。
The passengers are ferried by boat. 乘客由船只运送。

疑问句:
How do they ferry the materials to the top of the mountain? 他们如何将材料运送到山顶？
Who ferried the message to the king? 谁把消息传给了国王？

否定句:
They did not ferry the refugees to safety. 他们没有把难民运送到安全的地方。
The company will not ferry goods during the strike. 罢工期间，公司不会运输货物。
