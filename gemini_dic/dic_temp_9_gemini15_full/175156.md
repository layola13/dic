rage:/reɪdʒ/| n., v.|狂怒，盛怒；猛烈；肆虐 | Intense anger; violent, uncontrolled behaviour; to feel or express intense anger; to continue with great force or violence. |例句：His rage was terrifying. 他的怒火令人恐惧。|例句：The storm raged all night. 暴风雨肆虐了一整夜。|近义词：fury, anger, wrath, tempest |反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|名词, 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rages at the slightest provocation. = 他一点就怒。
The fire rages uncontrollably. = 大火肆虐，无法控制。

一般过去时:
She raged at her brother for breaking her phone. = 她因为弟弟打破了她的手机而大发雷霆。
The wind raged throughout the night. = 狂风呼啸了一整夜。

现在完成时:
He has raged against injustice all his life. = 他一生都在反抗不公正。
The river has raged for days, causing widespread flooding. = 河水已经奔腾了几天，造成了大范围的洪水。

过去完成时:
She had raged at him before he even opened his mouth. = 他还没开口，她就对他大发雷霆了。
The storm had raged for hours before it finally subsided. = 暴风雨肆虐了好几个小时才最终平息。

将来时:
He will rage against the machine! = 他将反抗机器！
The fire is expected to rage for several more hours. = 预计大火还会持续燃烧几个小时。

虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, he would rage. = 如果他知道真相，他会大发雷霆。
I wish I hadn't raged at him like that. = 我真希望我没有那样对他大发雷霆。

被动语态:
The city was raged by the storm. = 城市被暴风雨肆虐。
His anger was raged against him by the injustice he suffered. = 他遭受的不公正激起了他的怒火。


疑问句:
Why are you raging so much? = 你为什么这么生气？
Is the fire still raging? = 火还在燃烧吗？

否定句:
He didn't rage at me; he was surprisingly calm. = 他没有对我发火；他出奇地平静。
The storm didn't rage as fiercely as predicted. = 暴风雨没有像预报的那样猛烈。


PHRASE->
a fit of rage: 一阵狂怒
to be in a rage: 勃然大怒
to fly into a rage: 勃然大怒
a rage against something: 对某事强烈不满
to work oneself into a rage: 让自己怒火中烧
