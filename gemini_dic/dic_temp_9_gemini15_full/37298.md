commandlets:/ˈkəˈmændləts/ | n. |命令程序, 命令小子 |  Commandlets are small, single-function commands in PowerShell. They are designed to be easily chained together to create more complex operations.  They are often used to manage and automate tasks within a system. | Example:  Get-ChildItem lists all files and folders in the current directory.  Set-Location changes the current directory. | 近义词:  PowerShell commands, scripts (in a broader sense) | 反义词:  None directly applicable.
