noncarbon: /ˌnɒnˈkɑːrbən/ | adj. |非碳的；不含碳的 | Not containing carbon.  This term is used to describe materials, processes, or technologies that do not utilize or rely on carbon.  It often appears in discussions about sustainable energy and environmental protection, as it highlights alternatives to carbon-based fuels and technologies.


例句：
Noncarbon energy sources are crucial for mitigating climate change. = 非碳能源对于减缓气候变化至关重要。

The company is developing noncarbon materials for construction. = 这家公司正在开发用于建筑的非碳材料。


近义词：carbon-free, carbonless


反义词：carbon-based, carbonaceous


词性:形容词
