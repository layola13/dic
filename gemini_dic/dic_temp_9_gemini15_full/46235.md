cystoplast:/ˈsɪstəˌplæst/| n.|膀胱成形术；膀胱成形术后产生的膀胱|A cystoplasty is a surgical procedure to repair or reconstruct the bladder.  It is often used to treat bladder problems caused by injury, disease, or birth defects.  The procedure involves creating a new bladder or augmenting the existing bladder using a section of intestine or other tissue.|The surgeon performed a cystoplasty to increase the bladder capacity. |近义词：bladder augmentation, bladder reconstruction|反义词：bladder removal, cystectomy
