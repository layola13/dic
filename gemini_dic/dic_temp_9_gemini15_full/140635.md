olympianism:/oʊˈlɪmpiənɪzəm/ | n. |奥林匹克主义 |The ideals and principles of the Olympic Movement, emphasizing fair play, sportsmanship, and the pursuit of excellence.  It encompasses the values of the Olympic Charter, promoting ethical conduct, respect for others, and the development of a healthy lifestyle through sport. |The IOC promotes Olympianism throughout the world. 国际奥委会向全世界推广奥林匹克主义。|近义词：sportsmanship, fair play |反义词：cheating, unsporting conduct
