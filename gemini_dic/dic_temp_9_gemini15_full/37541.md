commonnesses:/ˈkɑːmənəsɪz/ | n. |共同点，普遍性 |The commonnesses between the two cultures are surprisingly numerous. |近义词：similarities, parallels, universals |反义词：differences, distinctions, peculiarities |名词


英文解析:The plural form of "commonness," referring to the quality or state of being common; shared characteristics or features; widespread prevalence.  It highlights the shared aspects or similarities between things, people, or situations.


例句:

The commonnesses between the two cultures are surprisingly numerous.  两个文化之间的共同点多得令人惊讶。

Despite their differences, the commonnesses in their approaches to problem-solving were evident. 尽管他们有差异，但在解决问题的方法上，他们的共同点是显而易见的。
