lugubre:/ˈluːɡəbrə/| adj.|阴沉的；悲伤的；凄凉的|  Gloomy, dismal, and depressing; suggesting death or mourning.  The word evokes a feeling of sadness, darkness, and foreboding. It often describes settings, music, or events that are somber and melancholic.|例句：The lugubre atmosphere of the old house weighed heavily on us.  这栋老房子的阴沉气氛让我们感到沉重。

The lugubre melody of the funeral march added to our sorrow.  葬礼进行曲的阴沉旋律增加了我们的悲伤。|近义词：gloomy, dismal, somber, mournful, melancholy|反义词：cheerful, bright, joyous, merry, sunny|
