lightening:/ˈlaɪtnɪŋ/| n.|闪电|The sudden, bright flash of light produced by a discharge of atmospheric electricity.|例句：The tree was struck by lightning.  这棵树被闪电击中了。|近义词：flash, thunderbolt|反义词：darkness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Lightning strikes the ground frequently during thunderstorms. = 雷雨期间闪电经常击中地面。
Lightning illuminates the night sky. = 闪电照亮了夜空。


一般过去时:
A powerful lightning bolt struck the house. = 一道强大的闪电击中了房子。
Lightning illuminated the landscape briefly. = 闪电短暂地照亮了风景。


现在完成时:
Lightning has damaged several power lines. = 闪电损坏了多条电力线。
Lightning has never struck my house before. = 闪电以前从未击中过我的房子。


过去完成时:
The fire had been started by a lightning strike. = 大火是由闪电引起的。
Before the storm arrived, lightning had already begun flashing in the distance. = 在暴风雨来临之前，远处就已经开始出现闪电。
