make an appearance:/meɪk ən əˈpɪərəns/| v. phr.|露面；出现；现身|To appear or show oneself, especially in a public or formal context.  It implies a deliberate act of showing oneself, often for a specific purpose or occasion. |例句：The celebrity made a surprise appearance at the festival.  这位名人在节日上惊喜现身。

The singer made a brief appearance on the late-night show.  这位歌手在深夜脱口秀节目中短暂露面。


近义词：show up, appear, emerge, turn up|反义词：disappear, vanish, absent oneself


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band makes an appearance at every concert.乐队在每场音乐会上都会露面。
She makes an appearance at charity events regularly. 她定期参加慈善活动。


一般过去时:
The mayor made an appearance at the ribbon-cutting ceremony. 市长出席了剪彩仪式。
He made a surprise appearance at her birthday party. 他在她生日派对上惊喜现身。


现在完成时:
The author has made several appearances on television this year. 今年这位作者已在电视上露面好几次了。
She has made many appearances at conferences. 她参加过多次会议。


过去完成时:
Before the concert started, the lead singer had already made an appearance backstage.  音乐会开始前，主唱已经在后台露面了。
They had made several appearances before finally achieving stardom. 他们在最终成名之前已经多次露面了。


将来时:
The president will make an appearance at the summit. 总统将在峰会上露面。
The actor will make a guest appearance on the popular sitcom.  这位演员将在热门情景喜剧中客串演出。


虚拟语气:
If the star made an appearance, the event would be a huge success. 如果明星露面，活动将获得巨大成功。
I wish the author had made an appearance at the book signing. 我希望作者能在签售会上露面。


被动语态:
An appearance was made by the CEO at the annual meeting.  首席执行官在年度会议上露面了。(注意被动语态较少用在此短语)
A special guest appearance was scheduled, but it was cancelled.  原本安排了一个特别嘉宾的露面，但是取消了。(注意被动语态较少用在此短语)


疑问句:
Will the famous musician make an appearance tonight?  这位著名的音乐家今晚会露面吗？
Did the politician make an appearance at the debate?  这位政客参加了辩论吗？


否定句:
The star did not make an appearance at the premiere.  这位明星没有出席首映式。
Unfortunately, he didn't make an appearance at the meeting. 不幸的是，他没有参加会议。
