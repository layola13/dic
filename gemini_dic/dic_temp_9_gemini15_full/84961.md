haploidies:/ˈhæplɔɪdiːz/| n.|单倍体现象，单倍体性|The state of having only one set of chromosomes.  It is often associated with gametes (sperm and egg cells).  Multiple haploidies can occur in a single organism, leading to mosaicism.|例句：The study examined the frequency of haploidies in different plant species. 这项研究考察了不同植物物种中单倍体现象的频率。|近义词：monoploidy|反义词：diploidy, polyploidy
