premeasurements:/ˌpriːˈmɛʒərmənts/ | n. | 预先测量；初步测量 | Preliminary measurements taken before a project begins.  These measurements provide a baseline for subsequent, more precise measurements. | The premeasurements for the new building were completed last week.  上周完成了新建筑的预先测量工作。 | initial measurements, preliminary surveys |  final measurements | 名词
