mislays:/mɪsˈleɪz/| vt.|放错地方；丢失|To put something in a place where one cannot find it again; to lose (something) by placing it carelessly.|例句：I mislaid my keys again!  我又把钥匙放错地方了！|例句：He mislaid the important document. 他把重要的文件弄丢了。|近义词：misplace, lose|反义词：find, retrieve|动词
