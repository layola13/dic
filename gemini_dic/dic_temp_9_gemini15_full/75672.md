fulfil:/fʊlˈfɪl/ | /fʊlˈfɪl/ | vt. |履行；实现；满足 |To carry out or bring to completion (a promise, task, etc.); to satisfy (a need, desire, etc.). |例句：He fulfilled his promise to help us. 他履行了帮助我们的承诺。 |例句：The new job fulfilled her ambition. 这份新工作实现了她的抱负。|近义词：accomplish, achieve, complete, satisfy |反义词：fail, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fulfills his duties diligently. 他勤勤恳恳地履行职责。
She fulfills her potential. 她充分发挥了自己的潜能。

一般过去时:
They fulfilled their contract. 他们履行了合同。
He fulfilled his dream of becoming a doctor. 他实现了成为医生的梦想。

现在完成时:
She has fulfilled all her obligations. 她已经履行所有义务。
We have fulfilled our quota for the month. 我们已经完成了本月的配额。

过去完成时:
