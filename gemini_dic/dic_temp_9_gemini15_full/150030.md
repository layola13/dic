palpates:/ˈpælpeɪts/| v.|触诊，摸诊|To examine (a part of the body) by touch, especially to detect abnormalities.|例句：The doctor palpated her abdomen to check for any tenderness.医生触诊了她的腹部以检查是否有压痛。
例句：The nurse palpated the patient's pulse. 护士触诊了病人的脉搏。|近义词：feel, touch, probe|反义词：ignore, neglect
