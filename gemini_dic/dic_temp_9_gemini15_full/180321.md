refueling:/ˌriːˈfjuːəlɪŋ/ | n. |加油；补充燃料 | The act of supplying fuel to something, especially a vehicle or aircraft.  |例句：The plane needed refueling before its transatlantic flight. 这架飞机需要在跨大西洋飞行前加油。 |例句：The refueling process took approximately 30 minutes. 加油过程大约花了30分钟。|近义词：replenishing, refilling |反义词：depleting, emptying |名词
