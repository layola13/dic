sheed:/ʃiːd/| n.|脱落；（尤指）大量脱落|The shedding of leaves in autumn is a natural process. 秋天树叶脱落是自然规律。|近义词：dropping, falling, casting off|反义词：retention, clinging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tree sheds its leaves every autumn. = 这棵树每年秋天都会落叶。
He sheds his skin every few weeks. = 他几周就要蜕一次皮。

一般过去时:
The snake shed its skin last night. = 蛇昨晚蜕皮了。
The old building shed its roof during the storm. = 老房子在暴风雨中屋顶脱落了。

现在完成时:
The dog has shed its winter coat. = 这只狗已经脱掉了冬毛。
The factory has shed many jobs this year. = 今年这家工厂裁掉了许多员工。

过去完成时:
The plant had shed all its leaves before the frost. = 霜冻来临之前，植物已经落光了叶子。
The company had shed a significant portion of its workforce before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经裁减了一大部分员工。

将来时:
The oak tree will shed its acorns in the fall. = 这棵橡树将在秋天掉落橡子。
The company will likely shed more jobs next quarter. = 公司很可能在下个季度裁掉更多员工。


虚拟语气:
If the tree shed its leaves earlier, it would be more resistant to frost. = 如果树木早点落叶，它就会更抗冻。
If the company had not shed so many employees, it might not have gone bankrupt. = 如果公司没有裁掉这么多员工，它可能就不会破产。

被动语态:
Many jobs were shed during the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退期间，许多工作岗位被裁掉。
The old building's roof was shed by the storm. = 老房子的屋顶被暴风雨吹掉了。

疑问句:
Does your cat shed a lot? = 你的猫掉毛多吗？
Does this plant shed its leaves in winter? = 这种植物冬天落叶吗？

否定句:
The tree doesn't shed its leaves until late autumn. = 这棵树直到深秋才落叶。
The snake hasn't shed its skin yet. = 蛇还没蜕皮。
