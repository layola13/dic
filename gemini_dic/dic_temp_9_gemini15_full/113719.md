macroclimatic:/ˌmækroʊklaɪˈmætɪk/| adj.|宏观气候的，大尺度气候的|relating to the climate of a large region, as opposed to the microclimate of a small, localized area.  Macroclimatic factors include things like prevailing winds, overall temperature and precipitation patterns across a broad geographical area.| Example: The macroclimatic conditions of the region are characterized by hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters.  该地区的宏观气候条件的特点是夏季炎热干燥，冬季温和潮湿。|近义词：regional climatic, large-scale climatic|反义词：microclimatic
