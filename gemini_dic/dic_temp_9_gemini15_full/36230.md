cognisee:/ˌkɒɡnɪˈsiː/ | n. | 受益人，被认知者 | A person who receives or benefits from something, especially a trust or will.  It also refers to someone or something that is perceived or understood. | Example: The cognisee of the trust received a substantial inheritance.  受托人继承了一大笔遗产。The painting's cognisee was the artist's granddaughter. 这幅画的欣赏者是艺术家的孙女。|近义词：beneficiary, recipient, percipient | 反义词：donor, grantor
