ptarmigan:/ˈtɑːrmɪɡən/|/ˈtɑːrmɪɡæn/| n. |雷鸟|A ptarmigan is a bird of the grouse family, typically inhabiting mountainous or arctic regions.  They are known for their seasonal changes in plumage, often camouflaging them against snow in winter and rocks and vegetation in summer. |Example:  The ptarmigan blended seamlessly with the snowy landscape. 雷鸟与雪景融为一体。

The ptarmigan's plumage provides excellent camouflage. 雷鸟的羽毛提供了极好的伪装。|近义词：grouse (a broader term)|反义词：None directly applicable.
