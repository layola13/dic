cure:/kjʊr/| n., vt.|治愈；治疗；解决办法；良药|The act of making someone or something healthy again; a remedy for a disease or problem. |例句：The doctor found a cure for his illness. 医生找到了治疗他疾病的方法。|例句：There's no easy cure for poverty.  贫困没有简单的解决办法。|近义词： remedy, treatment, heal, solve|反义词：disease, illness, problem


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine cures many diseases. = 这药能治很多病。
This treatment cures the infection. = 这种疗法能治愈感染。


一般过去时:
The doctor cured his patient. = 医生治好了他的病人。
They cured the problem quickly. = 他们迅速解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
Scientists have cured many previously incurable diseases. = 科学家们已经治愈了许多以前无法治愈的疾病。
She has cured her addiction. = 她已经戒掉了她的瘾。


过去完成时:
They had cured the disease before the outbreak spread. = 在疫情蔓延之前，他们已经治愈了这种疾病。
The doctors had cured him of his fever. = 医生治好了他的发烧。


将来时:
This new drug will cure many cancers. = 这款新药将治愈许多癌症。
I hope they will cure this problem soon. = 我希望他们能尽快解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If we found a cure, we would celebrate. = 如果我们找到了治疗方法，我们会庆祝。
I wish I could cure my fear of heights. = 我希望我能克服我的恐高症。


被动语态:
The disease was cured by a new drug. = 这种疾病被一种新药治愈了。
The problem is being cured gradually. = 问题正在逐渐得到解决。


疑问句:
Can this medicine cure my cough? = 这药能治好我的咳嗽吗？
Will this solution cure the problem? = 这个方案能解决问题吗？


否定句:
This medicine doesn't cure all illnesses. = 这药不能治所有病。
They couldn't cure the problem immediately. = 他们不能立即解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
a cure for = ...的治疗方法
a miracle cure = 奇效药
a quick cure = 速效药
seek a cure = 寻求治疗
find a cure = 找到治疗方法
