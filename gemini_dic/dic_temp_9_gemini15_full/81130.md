gramophone:/ˈɡræməfoʊn/ | n. |留声机 | A gramophone is a device for reproducing sound from a record by means of a needle that vibrates in the groove of the record and transmits the vibrations to a diaphragm.  | Example: My grandfather had an old gramophone in his attic.  我的祖父在阁楼里有一台旧留声机。
