recourseful: /rɪˈsɔːrsfl/ | adj. | 足智多谋的；机智的；有资源的 | resourceful people are skilled at finding clever ways to overcome difficulties. They are adept at using whatever resources are available to them to achieve their goals.  |  例句：She was a resourceful woman, always finding a way to solve problems, even when faced with limited resources. 她是一位足智多谋的女人，总是能找到解决问题的方法，即使面临资源有限的情况。

例句：The resourceful engineer designed a solution using only scrap materials.这位足智多谋的工程师利用废料设计了一种解决方案。


近义词：inventive, ingenious, imaginative, creative, adept

反义词：unimaginative, inept, helpless, incompetent


词源:  The word "resourceful" is derived from the noun "resource" and the suffix "-ful."  "Resource" itself comes from the Old French word "ressource," meaning "a means of supplying a want or need."  The suffix "-ful" signifies "full of" or "characterized by." Therefore, "resourceful" literally means "full of resources" or "characterized by the ability to find and use resources effectively."
