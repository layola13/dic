impersonator:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənˌeɪtər/| n. |模仿者；冒名顶替者 |A person who impersonates another, often for fraudulent purposes.  An impersonator can mimic someone's voice, mannerisms, or even their entire identity. |例句：The police arrested the impersonator who had been using a stolen identity to defraud banks.  警方逮捕了冒用他人身份诈骗银行的冒名顶替者。

例句：The comedian was a skilled impersonator, perfectly capturing the president's voice and gestures. 这名喜剧演员是一位技艺精湛的模仿者，完美地捕捉到了总统的声音和姿态。


近义词：mimic, imitator, fraud, imposter

反义词：original, genuine
