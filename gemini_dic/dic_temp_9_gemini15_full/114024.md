It highlights the repeated display of a noble and forgiving character. |例句：His magnanimousnesses throughout the trial showed his true character. 他在整个审判过程中表现出的宽宏大量展现了他的真实性格。  The magnanimousnesses of the king towards his conquered enemies secured lasting peace. 国王对被征服敌人的宽宏大量确保了持久的和平。 |近义词：generosity, benevolence, forbearance, clemency |反义词：spitefulness, vindictiveness, cruelty, pettiness |名词
