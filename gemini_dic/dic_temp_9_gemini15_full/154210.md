peripetalous: /ˌpɛriˈpɛtələs/ | adj. | 周位花被的 |  Referring to floral parts (tepals or petals) that are attached to the perianth, or the outermost whorl of a flower.  It describes the arrangement of petals or tepals around the reproductive organs of a flower.  | Example: The peripetalous stamens of the lily are clearly visible. 百合的花药明显地着生于花被上。|近义词: epipetalous (if specifically referring to petals), peri-floral|反义词: hypogynous (if referring to floral parts attached below the ovary)
