herbaceous:/hɜːˈbeɪʃəs/| adj. |草本的；多年生草本植物的| Relating to or characteristic of herbs; having the nature of an herb; not woody.  Herbaceous plants typically die back to the ground each year. |例句：Many wildflowers are herbaceous plants. 许多野花都是草本植物。

例句：The garden was filled with herbaceous borders. 花园里种满了草本植物的花坛。


近义词：herby, grassy, leafy | 反义词：woody, ligneous, arborescent


词性:形容词
