condemns:/kənˈdemz/| vt.|谴责，判刑，注定失败|To express strong disapproval of; to pronounce judgment against; to sentence to punishment; to declare unfit for use.  |例句：The international community condemns the use of chemical weapons. 国际社会谴责使用化学武器。

例句：The judge condemned the criminal to life imprisonment. 法官判处罪犯无期徒刑。

近义词：criticize, denounce, censure, reprove |反义词：praise, commend, approve

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He condemns violence in all its forms. 他谴责一切形式的暴力。
The government condemns the actions of the protestors. 政府谴责抗议者的行为。


一般过去时:
The court condemned him to death. 法庭判他死刑。
She condemned his behavior as unacceptable. 她认为他的行为不可接受而予以谴责。


现在完成时:
The world has condemned the terrorist attack. 全世界都谴责了这次恐怖袭击。
The committee has condemned the report as biased. 委员会认定该报告有偏见而予以谴责。


过去完成时:
They had condemned the building as unsafe before the earthquake. 地震前他们就已认定该建筑物不安全而予以谴责。
The jury had condemned the defendant before the evidence was presented.  在证据出示之前，陪审团就已经判决被告有罪。


将来时:
The judge will condemn the guilty party. 法官将谴责罪犯。
The public will condemn his actions. 公众将谴责他的行为。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the jury would condemn him. 如果证据更充分，陪审团就会判他罪。
I wish the world would condemn this injustice. 我希望全世界谴责这种不公正行为。


被动语态:
His actions were condemned by many. 他的行为受到许多人的谴责。
The building was condemned as unsafe. 这栋建筑被认定为不安全。


疑问句:
Does the law condemn this type of behavior? 法律是否谴责这种行为？
Will the court condemn the accused? 法院会判决被告有罪吗？


否定句:
He did not condemn the actions of his friend. 他没有谴责朋友的行为。
The report did not condemn the company's practices. 该报告没有谴责公司的做法。


PHRASE->
condemn to death: 判处死刑
condemn someone to:  判处某人…
condemn something as:  将某事认定为…
self-condemnation: 自责
condemn oneself: 自我谴责
