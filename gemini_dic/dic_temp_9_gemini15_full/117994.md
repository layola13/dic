memorialized:/məˈmɔːriəlaɪzd/| v. |纪念，使不朽|To keep the memory of (someone or something) alive; to commemorate.|The fallen soldiers were memorialized in a moving ceremony.  阵亡士兵在一次感人的仪式上得到了纪念。|The museum memorializes the history of the city.  这个博物馆纪念了这座城市的历史。|commemorated, celebrated, honored|forgotten, ignored|动词
