nonlitigious:/ˌnɑːn lɪˈtɪdʒəs/| adj.|非诉讼的，不愿打官司的|Not inclined to engage in lawsuits; avoiding litigation.  This describes a person, company, or situation that prefers to settle disputes outside of court.|例句：The company has a nonlitigious approach to resolving customer complaints.  这家公司采取非诉讼的方式解决客户投诉。

例句：He prefers a nonlitigious solution to the contract dispute. 他更倾向于通过非诉讼方式解决合同纠纷。


近义词：amicable, conciliatory, peaceable, uncombative

反义词：litigious, quarrelsome, contentious


词源:  The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "litigious," which derives from the Latin word "litigare" meaning "to contend in court" or "to litigate."  Therefore, "nonlitigious" literally means "not engaging in litigation."
