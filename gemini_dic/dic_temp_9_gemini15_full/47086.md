daub:/dɔːb/| /dɑːb/| vt.& n.|涂抹；乱涂；污点；拙劣的绘画|To cover (a surface) with a thick layer of something, especially paint or mud; a rough or careless piece of painting; a smear or spot.|例句：He daubed the wall with paint. 他用油漆涂抹墙壁。

例句：The child daubed the paper with crayon. 孩子用蜡笔乱涂纸张。


近义词：smear, smudge, plaster, slap|反义词：refine, polish|词性:动词及名词
