semilustrousness:/ˌsɛmiˈlʌstrəsnəs/| n. |半光泽；半光亮|The state or quality of being semilustrous; having a moderate sheen or luster. * **lustrous**: An adjective meaning "shining; luminous."  This comes from the Old French *lustrous* and ultimately from the Latin *lūstrāre* ("to purify, brighten").
* **-ness**: A suffix forming nouns of state or quality.
