logons:/ˈlɒɡɒnz/| n.|登录（次数），登录过程|The plural of "logon," referring to multiple instances of logging in to a system or network.  It often refers to the number of successful login attempts.|例句：The system recorded over 1000 logons during peak hours. 系统在高峰时段记录了超过1000次登录。  The security team analyzed the logons to identify suspicious activity. 安全团队分析了登录记录以识别可疑活动。|近义词：logins, sign-ins, accesses|反义词：logoffs, sign-outs|名词
