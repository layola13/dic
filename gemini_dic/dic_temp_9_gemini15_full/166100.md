prepossessingly:/ˌpriːpəˈzesɪŋli/| adv.| 令人印象深刻地，迷人地，有魅力地 |  In a way that makes a strong and favorable impression; charmingly.  |例句：She entered the room prepossessingly, capturing everyone's attention. 她以令人印象深刻的方式走进房间，吸引了所有人的注意。

例句：He was prepossessingly handsome and confident. 他英俊自信，令人印象深刻。


近义词：charmingly, attractively, captivatingly, alluringly, winningly

反义词：unattractively, repulsively, unimpressively
