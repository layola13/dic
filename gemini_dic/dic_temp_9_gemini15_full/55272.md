dissipation:/ˌdɪsɪˈpeɪʃən/| n. |消散；浪费；挥霍；衰减|The gradual dissipation of the fog allowed us to see the landscape clearly.  雾逐渐消散，我们得以清晰地看到景色。 The dissipation of his energy after the marathon was evident. 马拉松过后，他的精力明显衰减了。|近义词：dispersion, scattering, waste, extravagance|反义词：conservation, preservation, accumulation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Heat dissipates quickly in the open air. = 热量在户外迅速消散。
The company's profits are dissipating due to poor management. = 由于管理不善，公司的利润正在减少。

一般过去时:
The storm dissipated quickly after sunset. = 日落后风暴迅速消散。
He dissipated his inheritance in a few years. = 他几年内就挥霍掉了他的遗产。

现在完成时:
The fog has dissipated, revealing a beautiful sunrise. = 雾已散去，露出了美丽的日出。
He has dissipated most of his savings on gambling. = 他把大部分积蓄都花在了赌博上。

过去完成时:
The tension had dissipated by the time the meeting ended. = 会议结束时，紧张气氛已经消散了。
He had dissipated his fortune before he turned thirty. = 他在三十岁之前就挥霍掉了他的财产。

将来时:
The clouds will dissipate by noon. = 到中午云彩就会消散。
He will likely dissipate his energy if he doesn't rest. = 如果他不休息，他很可能会耗尽精力。

虚拟语气:
If the clouds dissipated, we could see the stars. = 如果云散了，我们就能看到星星。
If he hadn’t dissipated his money, he would be rich now. = 如果他没有挥霍他的钱，他现在就会很富有。

被动语态:
The energy was dissipated by friction. = 能量因摩擦而损耗。
His fortune was dissipated through reckless spending. = 他的财产因挥霍无度而耗尽。

疑问句:
Has the pain finally dissipated? = 疼痛终于消散了吗？
How quickly will the medication dissipate the swelling? = 药物要多久才能消除肿胀？

否定句:
The pain did not dissipate easily. = 疼痛不容易消散。
He did not dissipate his inheritance; he invested it wisely. = 他没有挥霍他的遗产；他明智地投资了它。
