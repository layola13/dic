rationalizers: /ˈræʃənəlaɪzərz/ | n. | 找理由者；合理化者 | Those who rationalize their actions.  |  They are skilled rationalizers, always finding excuses for their behavior. 他们是很擅长找理由的人，总是为自己的行为找借口。 | apologists, justifiers |  critics, condemners | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The manager rationalizes the company's decisions. = 经理为公司的决定找理由。
They rationalize their bad habits. = 他们为自己的坏习惯找借口。

一般过去时:
He rationalized his mistakes after the project failed. = 项目失败后，他为自己的错误找借口。
She rationalized her spending by saying it was for investment. = 她声称那是为了投资，以此为自己的消费找理由。

现在完成时:
They have rationalized their actions to the board. = 他们已经向董事会解释了他们的行为。
He has rationalized his behavior for years. = 他多年来一直为自己的行为找借口。

过去完成时:
She had rationalized her decision before telling her parents. = 在告诉父母之前，她已经为自己做出的决定找好了理由。
They had rationalized their investment losses before reporting to their investors. = 在向投资者汇报之前，他们已经为投资损失找好了理由。

将来时:
He will rationalize his absence from the meeting. = 他将会为缺席会议找借口。
She will rationalize her lateness to her boss. = 她将会向老板解释自己迟到的原因。

虚拟语气:
If I were to fail, I would rationalize it as a learning experience. = 如果我失败了，我会把它合理化为一次学习经验。
I wish I hadn't rationalized my procrastination. = 我真希望我没有为自己的拖延找借口。

被动语态:
His actions were rationalized by his superiors. = 他的行为得到了上级的谅解。
The company's decision was rationalized in a press release. = 公司的决定在新闻稿中得到了解释。

疑问句:
How did they rationalize their unethical behavior? = 他们是如何为自己的不道德行为找借口的？
Why did she rationalize her decision to quit her job? = 她为什么为辞职的决定找借口？

否定句:
He didn't rationalize his actions; he took full responsibility. = 他没有为自己的行为找借口；他承担了全部责任。
She didn't rationalize her mistakes; she learned from them. = 她没有为自己的错误找借口；而是从中吸取教训。


PHRASE->
rationalize away:  解释掉…
rationalize one's fears:  消除恐惧
rationalize an action: 为行为找理由
rationalize a decision: 为决定找理由
rationalize the situation: 为情况找理由

词源:
From "rationalize," meaning to make rational or reasonable;  ultimately derived from "ratio" (reason) + "-alize" (to make).  The noun form "rationalizers" follows the standard pattern of adding "-er" to a verb to create an agent noun (one who performs the action).
