metaldehyde:/ˌmɛtəlˈdɪhaɪd/| n. |金属乙醛|A cyclic tetramer of acetaldehyde, a white crystalline solid used as a molluscicide (slug killer).  It is poisonous if ingested.| 例句：The garden was treated with metaldehyde to control the slug infestation. 花园里使用了金属乙醛来控制蛞蝓的侵害。

The farmer used metaldehyde pellets to protect his crops from slugs.  农民使用金属乙醛颗粒来保护他的庄稼免受蛞蝓的侵害。|近义词：molluscicide, slug killer|反义词：None specifically, but substances promoting slug growth could be considered antonyms in a broad sense.|名词
