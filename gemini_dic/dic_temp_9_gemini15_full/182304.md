remuneratory: /rɪˈmjuːnərətɔːri/ | adj. | 有报酬的；酬金的 | Relating to or constituting remuneration; rewarding.  |The remuneratory aspects of the job were a strong incentive. 这份工作的报酬方面是一个强大的激励因素。  |compensatory, rewarding | unremunerative, unpaid |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company offers a remuneratory package to attract talented employees. = 公司提供有吸引力的薪酬待遇来吸引优秀人才。
This position is highly remuneratory for experienced professionals. = 对于经验丰富的专业人士来说，这个职位报酬很高。


一般过去时:
The previous role was not very remuneratory. = 之前的职位报酬不高。
His efforts were remuneratory in the end. = 他的努力最终得到了回报。


现在完成时:
The project has been highly remuneratory for the team. = 这个项目给团队带来了丰厚的回报。
The investment has proven remuneratory. = 这项投资已被证明是盈利的。


过去完成时:
They had hoped the venture would be more remuneratory. = 他们曾希望这个企业能更有利可图。
The company had already received remuneratory payments before the project was even completed. = 在项目完成之前，公司就已经收到了报酬。


将来时:
The new contract will be more remuneratory. = 新合同的报酬会更高。
This investment is expected to be highly remuneratory in the future. = 这项投资预计将来会带来丰厚的回报。


虚拟语气:
If the job were more remuneratory, I would apply. = 如果这份工作报酬更高，我会申请。
I wish the new policy were more remuneratory for lower-level employees. = 我希望新政策能给低层员工带来更多报酬。


被动语态:
The work was adequately remunerated. = 这项工作得到了足够的报酬。
His contributions were not adequately remunerated. = 他的贡献没有得到足够的报酬。


疑问句:
Is the position sufficiently remuneratory? = 这个职位的报酬是否足够？
Are the benefits remuneratory enough to attract top talent? = 福利是否足够有吸引力以吸引顶尖人才？


否定句:
The work was not remuneratory enough to justify the effort. = 这项工作的报酬不足以弥补付出的努力。
The volunteer position is not remuneratory. = 志愿者职位没有报酬。

词源:
From Latin *remuneratorius, from remunerari "to reward, recompense," from re- "again" + munus "gift, service, duty."
