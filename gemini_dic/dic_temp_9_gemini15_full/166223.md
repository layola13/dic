prequel:/ˈpriːkwəl/| n. |前传|A prequel is a story that comes before a previously existing story, usually providing background information or context.  It often features the same characters or setting as the original work, but at an earlier point in time.  For example, a prequel to a film would be a movie that tells a story that happened before the events of the original film. |The movie is a prequel to the popular series. 这部电影是热门剧集的前传。

The book serves as a prequel, explaining the events that led to the main story. 这本书充当前传，解释了导致主要故事发生的事件。|近义词：precursor, forerunner |反义词：sequel
