semi-sterile:/ˈsɛmiˈstɛraɪl/| adj.|半无菌的，部分无菌的|Partially sterile; containing some, but not all, microorganisms.  This term is often used to describe environments or materials that have undergone a process to reduce the number of microorganisms, but not eliminate them completely.|例句：The operating room maintains a semi-sterile environment to minimize the risk of infection. 手术室保持半无菌环境以最大限度地降低感染风险。


The equipment was semi-sterile after the cleaning process. 清洁过程后，设备处于半无菌状态。|近义词：partially sterile, relatively clean|反义词：sterile, completely clean
