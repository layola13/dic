overfurnishes: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃɪz/ | v. |过度装饰，摆设过多 | To furnish excessively; to provide with more furniture than is needed or desirable.  |The apartment was overfurnished; it felt cramped and cluttered. 这套公寓摆设过多，感觉拥挤杂乱。|近义词：clutter, overcrowd, overload |反义词：underfurnish, sparsely furnish |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overfurnishes his house every year. = 他每年都把房子装饰得过于华丽。
They overfurnish their new apartment with antique furniture. = 他们用古董家具把新公寓装饰得过于华丽。

一般过去时:

We overfurnished the guest room, making it difficult to move around. = 我们把客房摆设得太过拥挤，以至于难以走动。

过去完成时:
