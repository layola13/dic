flesher:/ˈfleʃər/| n. |剥皮工；屠夫；肉贩|A flesher is a person who works with meat, typically by butchering animals or selling meat.  They may work in a slaughterhouse, butcher shop, or supermarket.|例句：The flesher skillfully cut the meat into steaks.  这个剥皮工熟练地将肉切成牛排。|例句：The local flesher is known for his high-quality beef. 当地肉贩以其优质牛肉而闻名。|近义词：butcher, meat cutter, butcherman|反义词：vegetarian (not a direct antonym, but represents a contrasting occupation)|名词
