corgi:/ˈkɔːrɡiː/ | n. |柯基犬 | A small breed of dog originating in Wales, characterized by its short legs and long body.  Corgis are known for their intelligence, loyalty, and playful nature. They are popular family pets. |Example: My neighbor owns a Pembroke Welsh corgi. 我的邻居养了一只彭布罗克威尔士柯基犬。|近义词：dog, canine |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑体型较大的犬种如Great Dane) | 名词
