expounded:/ɪkˈspaʊndɪd/| v.|详细解释，阐述|To explain or discuss something in detail.|The professor expounded on the theory of relativity.  教授详细解释了相对论。|explained, elaborated, elucidated|simplified, obscured|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expounds his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地阐述了他的想法。
She expounds on the benefits of exercise. = 她详细解释了锻炼的好处。


一般过去时:
The speaker expounded on the historical context. = 演讲者详细阐述了历史背景。
He expounded his views at the meeting. = 他在会议上阐述了他的观点。


现在完成时:
She has expounded this theory in several publications. = 她已经在几篇出版物中阐述了这一理论。
He has expounded upon the subject many times. = 他多次阐述过这个主题。


过去完成时:
They had expounded their reasons before the judge. = 他们在法官面前阐述了他们的理由。
She had expounded the concept thoroughly in her previous lecture. = 在她之前的演讲中，她已经彻底阐述了这个概念。


将来时:
He will expound on the new policy at the conference. = 他将在会议上详细解释这项新政策。
She will expound her findings in her upcoming presentation. = 她将在即将发表的演讲中阐述她的研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If he had expounded his ideas more clearly, people would have understood. = 如果他更清晰地阐述他的想法，人们就会理解。
I wish he had expounded on that point further. = 我希望他对那一点作进一步的阐述。


被动语态:
The theory was expounded in great detail. = 这一理论被非常详细地阐述了。
His ideas were expounded by several scholars. = 他的思想被几位学者详细阐述。


疑问句:
Will the professor expound on this topic further? = 教授会进一步阐述这个话题吗？
Has she expounded her reasons for leaving? = 她阐述了她离开的原因了吗？


否定句:
He did not expound on the controversial aspects of the issue. = 他没有阐述这个问题中有争议的方面。
She didn't expound her ideas sufficiently. = 她没有充分阐述她的想法。


PHRASE->
expound on = 详细解释
expound upon = 详细阐述

expound the meaning = 解释含义
