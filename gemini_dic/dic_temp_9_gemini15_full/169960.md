prosuffrage:/ˈproʊˌsʌfrɪdʒ/| n.|支持妇女参政权|Advocacy for women's suffrage.  The movement gained momentum in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.|例句：The prosuffrage movement achieved significant victories in many countries.|近义词：feminist, suffragette (as a noun)|反义词：antisuffrage


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense variations (present, past, future, etc.)  and active/passive voice don't apply.  The word itself expresses a stance.
