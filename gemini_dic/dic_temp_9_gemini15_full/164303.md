preeditorial: /ˌpriːˌedɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 出版前的；编辑前的 | Relating to the period or process before a piece of writing, especially a newspaper article or editorial, is published.  It describes something that happens or is done in preparation for the final publication. | The preeditorial meeting involved brainstorming ideas for the upcoming article.  = 出版前的会议涉及到为即将发表的文章集思广益。  The preeditorial revisions were extensive and time-consuming. = 出版前的修改非常广泛且耗时。 |  prepublication, preliminary | postpublication | adj.
