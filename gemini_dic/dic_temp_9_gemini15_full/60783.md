electromechanical:/ˌɪˌlɛktroʊməˈkænɪkl/| adj. |机电的；机电一体化的| Combining or relating to both electrical and mechanical systems or components.  The term describes systems, devices, or processes that integrate electrical and mechanical elements to perform a specific function. For example, a simple electromechanical system might involve an electric motor turning a mechanical gear. More complex examples include robots, automated manufacturing systems, and many types of sensors and actuators.|The new car boasts an advanced electromechanical braking system.|近义词：electro-mechanical|反义词：None (antonyms don't naturally exist for this descriptive term)
