lyrate:/ˈlaɪreɪt/| adj.|琴状的，羽状裂的|Having lobes or divisions that resemble a lyre;  having a lyre-shaped outline, especially of a leaf.  The lobes are usually progressively smaller towards the base.|The lyrate leaves of the dandelion are easily recognizable.|近义词：pinnate, lobed|反义词：entire, undivided
