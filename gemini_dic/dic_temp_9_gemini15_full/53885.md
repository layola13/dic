discordance:/dɪsˈkɔːrdəns/| n. |不一致；不协调；冲突 |A lack of agreement or harmony between things.  It can refer to a disagreement in opinions, a clash of sounds, or an inconsistency in data. |例句：There is a discordance between her words and her actions.  她的言行不一。|例句：The discordance in the musical piece created a jarring effect. 这首乐曲中的不和谐音产生了刺耳的效果。|近义词：discrepancy, inconsistency, conflict, dissonance |反义词：harmony, agreement, concordance, consonance
