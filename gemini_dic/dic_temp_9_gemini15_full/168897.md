professionalise: /prəˈfɛʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使专业化；使职业化 | To make something more professional or to cause something to become more like a profession.  This often involves formalizing processes, standardizing practices, and increasing expertise. |例句：The company is working to professionalise its image. 公司正在努力提升其专业形象。

例句：The government is attempting to professionalise the police force. 政府正在努力使警务部门更加专业化。


近义词：professionalize, formalize, standardize


反义词：amateurish, unprofessional


词性:动词

词源:
The word "professionalise" is derived from the word "professional," which ultimately comes from the Middle French word "professionel," meaning "of or relating to a profession."  The suffix "-ise" is a common verb-forming suffix in English, indicating the act of making something into or like something else.
