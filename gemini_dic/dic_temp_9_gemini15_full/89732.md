hoping:/ˈhoʊpɪŋ/| v.|希望，期望|expressing a wish or desire for something to happen.|例句：I'm hoping for good weather tomorrow. 我希望明天天气好。

例句：She's hoping to get a promotion. 她希望得到晋升。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am hoping for a better future. = 我希望有一个更好的未来。
We are hoping to see you soon. = 我们希望很快见到你。


一般过去时:
I was hoping to finish the project by Friday. = 我希望在星期五之前完成这个项目。
They were hoping for a miracle. = 他们希望出现奇迹。


现在完成时:
I have been hoping for this opportunity for years. = 我多年来一直期盼着这个机会。
She has been hoping to hear from him. = 她一直希望收到他的来信。


过去完成时:
I had hoped to see you earlier. = 我本希望早点见到你。
They had hoped to win the game. = 他们本希望赢得比赛。


将来时:
I will be hoping for a positive result. = 我将期待一个积极的结果。
We will be hoping that everything goes smoothly. = 我们将希望一切顺利进行。


虚拟语气:
I hoped that she would be happy. = 我希望她会快乐。(过去虚拟语气)
I wish I were hoping for more. = 我希望我能抱有更大的希望。(现在虚拟语气)


被动语态:
Nothing is being hoped for. = 没有什么希望了。(被动语态较为罕见)
It was hoped that the problem would be solved. = 人们曾希望这个问题能够得到解决。(被动语态较为罕见)


疑问句:
Are you hoping for a raise? = 你希望加薪吗？
What are you hoping to achieve? = 你希望取得什么成就？


否定句:
I am not hoping for any trouble. = 我不希望有任何麻烦。
They were not hoping for a negative response. = 他们不希望得到负面回应。


PHRASE->
hoping against hope = 绝望中的希望
hoping for the best = 期待最好的结果
hoping to see you soon = 希望很快见到你
hoping for a miracle = 期待奇迹
I am hoping you can help me. = 我希望你能帮助我。

近义词： wishing, desiring, expecting, anticipating

反义词： despairing, fearing, doubting
