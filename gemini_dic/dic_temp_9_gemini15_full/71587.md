flashpoint:/ˈflæʃpɔɪnt/ | n. | 燃点；导火线；冲突爆发点 | The point at which a flammable substance will ignite.  It can also refer to a situation that is likely to cause a sudden outburst of violence or anger. | 例句：The flashpoint of gasoline is relatively low.  汽油的燃点比较低。 | 例句：The border dispute was a flashpoint for the war. 边界争端是战争的导火索。 | 近义词：ignition point, trigger, breaking point | 反义词：calm, peace, stability | 名词
