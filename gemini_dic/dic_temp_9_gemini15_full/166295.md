prerectal: /ˌpriːˈrɛktəl/ | adj. | 直肠前的；直肠之前的 | Situated or occurring in front of the rectum.  This term is primarily used in anatomical descriptions. | Example: The prerectal space is an area of potential concern during pelvic surgery. 直肠前间隙是盆腔手术中一个可能引起关注的区域。| 近义词：anterior rectal, pre-rectal | 反义词：postrectal, retrorectal |  词性：形容词
