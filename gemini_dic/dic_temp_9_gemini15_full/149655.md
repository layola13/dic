pair:/peər/ | n. |一对，一双；夫妇，夫妻 | A pair of things is two things of the same kind that are used together.  A pair of people is two people who are married or in a romantic relationship. | Example: I bought a new pair of shoes. 我买了一双新鞋。  John and Mary are a lovely pair.  约翰和玛丽是一对可爱的夫妇。 |近义词：couple, duo, twosome |反义词：single, individual


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This pair of shoes fits me perfectly. = 这双鞋很合我的脚。
That pair of birds sings beautifully. = 那对鸟儿唱歌很美。

一般过去时:
I saw a pair of deer in the forest yesterday. = 我昨天在森林里看到一对鹿。
They were a happy pair before the argument. = 争吵之前他们是一对幸福的夫妇。


现在完成时:
I have lost one of the pair of gloves. = 我丢了一只手套。
We have been a pair for ten years. = 我们已经在一起十年了。

They will be a happy pair, I'm sure. = 我相信他们会成为一对幸福的夫妇。
