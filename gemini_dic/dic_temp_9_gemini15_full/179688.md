reenlistment:/ˌriːɪnˈlɪstmənt/| n. |重新入伍；再次应征|The act of re-enlisting in the military after a period of service.  It involves signing a new contract to serve again. Reenlistment often comes with benefits and incentives.|Example: His reenlistment was celebrated by his unit.|近义词：re-enrollment, reengagement|反义词：discharge, separation
