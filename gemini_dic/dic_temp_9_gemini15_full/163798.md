predemonstrating: /ˌpriːˈdɛmənstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先演示；提前展示 |  To demonstrate something beforehand; to give a preview or trial run of something before the main demonstration.  | The company is predemonstrating the new software to select clients before the official launch. = 公司在正式发布之前向部分客户预先演示了新软件。  They are predemonstrating the new product at a trade show. = 他们在贸易展上预先展示了新产品。 | showcasing, previewing, exhibiting |  | v.
