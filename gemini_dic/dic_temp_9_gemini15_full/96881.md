inexorably:/ɪnˈɛksərəbli/| adj. |不可避免地；必然地； relentless |The word "inexorably" describes something that cannot be stopped or changed, and will inevitably continue to happen. It suggests a relentless and unstoppable force.  |例句：The empire's power declined inexorably.  帝国的力量不可避免地衰落了。|例句：The disease inexorably progressed.  疾病无情地发展着。|近义词：unavoidably, inevitably, relentlessly, relentlessly, inescapably |反义词：avoidably, preventatively, stoppably


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river inexorably flows towards the sea. = 河水不可避免地流向大海。
The sun inexorably rises each morning. = 太阳每天早晨都不可避免地升起。

一般过去时:
The empire inexorably declined in the years following the war. = 战争后的几年里，帝国不可避免地衰落了。
The old man inexorably weakened as the illness progressed. = 随着疾病的发展，这位老人不可避免地虚弱了。

现在完成时:
The glacier has inexorably advanced down the mountain. = 冰川已经不可避免地沿着山坡向下移动了。
The price of housing has inexorably risen over the past decade. = 在过去的十年里，房价一直在不可避免地上涨。

过去完成时:
By the time they reached the summit, their supplies had inexorably dwindled. = 当他们到达山顶时，他们的补给已经不可避免地减少了。
The evidence had inexorably pointed to his guilt before the trial. = 在审判之前，证据已不可避免地指向了他的罪行。

将来时:
The technology will inexorably change the way we live and work. = 这项技术将不可避免地改变我们的生活和工作方式。
The effects of climate change will inexorably impact our planet. = 气候变化的影响将不可避免地影响我们的星球。

虚拟语气:
If we continued down this path, we would inexorably reach disaster. = 如果我们继续走这条路，我们将会不可避免地走向灾难。
I wish the decline of the old ways wouldn't inexorably continue.  我希望旧习俗的衰落不会不可避免地继续下去。

被动语态:
The city was inexorably transformed by the rapid industrialization. = 这个城市被快速的工业化不可避免地改变了。
The old traditions were inexorably eroded by the changing times. = 旧的传统被变化的时代不可避免地侵蚀了。

疑问句:
Will the situation inexorably worsen? = 局势会不可避免地恶化吗？
Is this trend likely to continue inexorably? = 这种趋势可能会不可避免地继续下去吗？

否定句:
The situation was not inexorably doomed; there was still hope. = 局势并非不可避免地注定失败；仍然有希望。
The project's failure wasn't inexorable; it could have been avoided. = 项目的失败并非不可避免的；本可以避免。
