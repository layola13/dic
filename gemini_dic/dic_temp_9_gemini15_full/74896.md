freshman:/ˈfrɛʃmən/| n. |新生，一年级学生 | A first-year student at a university or college.  |Example:  She's a freshman at Harvard. 她是个哈佛大学新生。 |近义词：first-year student, newbie |反义词：senior, graduate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a freshman this year. = 他今年是大一新生。
She is studying hard to be a top freshman. = 她正在努力学习以成为一名优秀的新生。


一般过去时:
He was a freshman last year. = 他去年是大一新生。
She was a diligent freshman. = 她是一个勤奋的新生。


现在完成时:
She has been a freshman for a semester. = 她已经做了一学期的新生。
He has already adapted to college life as a freshman. = 作为一名新生，他已经适应了大学生活。


过去完成时:
Before the exam, he had been a freshman for only a few months. = 考试前，他当新生才几个月。
By the end of the semester, she had already made many friends as a freshman. = 到学期末，她作为一名新生已经交到了很多朋友。


将来时:
He will be a sophomore next year. = 他明年将是大二学生。
She will graduate as an excellent freshman. = 她将以优秀新生的身份毕业。（虽然不太符合常理，但语法上成立）


虚拟语气:
If I were a freshman again, I would study harder. = 如果我再做一次新生，我会更努力学习。
I wish I were a freshman again, so I could experience college life again. = 我希望我还能再做一次新生，这样我就能再次体验大学生活了。


被动语态:
Freshmen are expected to attend orientation. = 新生应该参加迎新会。
The scholarship is awarded to promising freshmen. = 这项奖学金授予有前途的新生。


疑问句:
Is he a freshman? = 他是大一新生吗？
Are you a freshman in this college? = 你是这所大学的新生吗？


否定句:
He is not a freshman; he's a sophomore. = 他不是新生，他是二年级学生。
She wasn't a freshman last year. = 她去年不是新生。
