discontinuities:/ˌdɪskɒntɪˈnjuːɪtiːz/ | n. |不连续性；间断；中断 |  Points at which a function is not continuous; interruptions or breaks in a continuous sequence or process.  | 例句：The graph shows several discontinuities.  图表显示了几个不连续点。

The research revealed significant discontinuities in the data. 研究揭示了数据中明显的间断。


近义词：breaks, interruptions, gaps, disruptions, inconsistencies

反义词：continuity, consistency, uniformity


词性: 名词
