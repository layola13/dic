prosobranch:/ˈprɒsəʊbræŋkʃ/| n. |前鳃类动物|A prosobranch is a gastropod mollusk belonging to the subclass Prosobranchia, characterized by gills located in front of the heart.  They are a diverse group including many marine snails and limpets. |Example: Many prosobranchs exhibit a spiral shell. 许多前鳃类动物具有螺旋形贝壳。|近义词: gastropod (more general term)|反义词: opisthobranch (subclass with gills behind the heart)|名词
