dereliction:/ˌdɛrɪˈlɪkʃən/| n. | 玩忽职守；荒废；遗弃 | The neglect of a duty or responsibility.  It can also refer to the state of being abandoned or neglected. |例句：The dereliction of duty led to the company's failure. 玩忽职守导致公司倒闭。

The building fell into a state of dereliction. 这栋建筑物荒废了。|近义词：neglect, abandonment, omission, default |反义词：fulfillment, diligence, attention, maintenance |词性:名词
