ripener:/ˈraɪpənər/| n.|催熟剂，促熟剂|A substance or process that hastens the ripening of fruit.|The farmer used a chemical ripener to speed up the ripening of his tomatoes.|近义词：accelerator, maturer|反义词：inhibitor, retardant
