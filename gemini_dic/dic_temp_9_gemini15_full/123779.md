mootable:/ˈmuːtəbl/ | adj. | 可变的；易变的 |  Capable of being changed; changeable; mutable.  The term often implies a lack of firmness or stability, suggesting something is prone to alteration or fluctuation. |例句：The weather is mootable, so pack layers. 天气变化多端，所以要多带几件衣服。

The plans are mootable, depending on funding. 计划是可变的，取决于资金。


近义词：changeable, variable, mutable, fickle, unsteady | 反义词：immutable, unchanging, constant, stable, fixed | 词性：形容词
