odours:/ˈoʊdərz/| n.|气味，臭味|The plural of "odour".  Odours are smells, often implying unpleasant ones.|The odours emanating from the factory were unbearable.  工厂散发出的气味令人难以忍受。The strong odours of the spices filled the kitchen.  香料的强烈气味充满了厨房。|scents, smells, fragrances, aromas|fresh air|n.


USAGE->
This word is a noun, so it doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  The usage varies based on the sentence structure.


Example sentences demonstrating different sentence structures:

Declarative sentence:
The odours of the city were overwhelming. = 城市的气味令人难以招架。
There were strange odours coming from the basement. = 地下室传来奇怪的气味。


Interrogative sentence:
What odours are you detecting? = 你闻到了什么气味？
Are those unpleasant odours coming from the drain? = 那些难闻的气味是从下水道传来的吗？


Exclamatory sentence:
What terrible odours! = 多么可怕的气味！
Those sweet odours! = 那些甜蜜的气味！


PHRASE->
strong odours = 强烈的味道
foul odours = 恶臭
pleasant odours = 宜人的气味
unpleasant odours = 令人不快的味道
subtle odours = 微妙的气味
