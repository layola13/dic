organic:/ɔːrˈɡænɪk/ | adj. | 有机的；器官的；组织的；根本的 | Relating to or derived from living matter.  Characterized by or involving the use of natural substances; free from artificial chemicals. |例句：Organic farming methods avoid the use of pesticides. 有机耕作方法避免使用杀虫剂。
  
The farm produces organic vegetables. 这个农场生产有机蔬菜。


近义词：natural, biological, holistic, inherent | 反义词：synthetic, artificial, inorganic, chemical


词性：形容词
