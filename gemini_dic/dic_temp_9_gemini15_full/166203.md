prepurchased:/ˌpriːˈpɜːrtʃəst/| adj. |预先购买的，预购的 |Prepurchased means bought or acquired in advance.  It describes something that has been purchased before it is needed or before a specific time or event. |Example: The prepurchased tickets saved us a considerable amount of money at the gate. 预先购买的门票为我们在入口处节省了相当多的钱。

The prepurchased airline tickets were non-refundable. 预先购买的机票是不予退还的. |近义词： advance-purchased, pre-booked, beforehand-purchased |反义词：last-minute purchased, impromptu-purchased

词源:
The word "prepurchased" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the past participle of the verb "purchase" ("purchased").  Therefore, it literally means "bought before."
