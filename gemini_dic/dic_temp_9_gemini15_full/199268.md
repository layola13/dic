silver: /ˈsɪlvər/ | n. adj. | 银；银色的；银器 |

英文解析:Silver is a precious, shiny, grayish-white metal. It is highly valued for its beauty, malleability, and conductivity.  It's used in jewelry, silverware, electronics, and various industrial applications.  As an adjective, it describes the color or something made of silver.


例句:

The necklace is made of pure silver.  这条项链是用纯银制成的。

He polished the silver until it shone. 他把银器擦得闪闪发光。


近义词:  argent (formal),  pewter (a tin-lead alloy, often similar in appearance)


反义词:  gold (in the context of precious metals),  black (in the context of color)


词性:  noun, adjective
