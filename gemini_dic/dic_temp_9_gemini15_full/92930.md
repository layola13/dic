identifications:/ˌaɪdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |识别；鉴定；身份证明|The process of identifying something or someone.  This includes establishing the identity of a person, object, or substance. It often involves comparing characteristics to known examples.|例句：The police made several identifications based on witness testimony.  警方根据证人证词进行了多次身份识别。|例句：The identifications of the plant species were confirmed by a botanist. 植物物种的鉴定得到了植物学家的确认。|近义词：recognition, identification, verification, authentication|反义词：misidentification, anonymity
