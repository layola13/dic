precomments:/ˈpriːkɒments/ | n. |预评论，前期评论 | Comments made before a main event or discussion, often expressing opinions or expectations.  These comments might be posted online before a product launch, a movie release, or a public debate. | Example: The precomments on the new video game were overwhelmingly positive, creating high anticipation for its release.  = 新视频游戏的预评论压倒性地积极，为其发布创造了很高的期待。|近义词：advance comments, preliminary remarks, anticipatory opinions |反义词：post-comments, afterthoughts
