例句：My teacher gave us a challenging homework assignment. 我的老师给我们布置了一项具有挑战性的家庭作业。


近义词：assignment, exercises, tasks |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|词性:名词

词源:
The word "homework" is a compound word formed from "home" and "work."  "Home" refers to the place where one resides, and "work" refers to tasks or assignments. The term emerged in the late 19th and early 20th centuries as a formalization of the practice of assigning tasks to students to be completed outside of class.  Its etymology is straightforward, reflecting the simple combination of its constituent parts.
