servicewomen: /ˈsɜːrvɪswʊmən/ | n. |女军人，女兵| Female members of the armed forces.  The term encompasses women serving in all branches of the military, including the army, navy, air force, marines, and coast guard.  It emphasizes their role as active service members. |例句：Many servicewomen are now serving in combat roles. 许多女兵现在都在担任战斗角色。

例句：The number of servicewomen has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，女兵人数显著增加。


近义词：female soldiers, women in uniform, military women

反义词：servicemen (male counterparts)


词性: 名词
