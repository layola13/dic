quasi-opposed:/ˈkwɑːzi əˈpoʊzd/|/ˈkwɑːzaɪ əˈpoʊzd/| adj.| 准对立的，似是而非地对立的 |  Describes things that are seemingly or partially opposed, but not completely or directly antagonistic.  It suggests a relationship of partial or indirect opposition, often with some degree of overlap or ambiguity. | The two political parties, while quasi-opposed on economic policy, found common ground on environmental issues.  两个政党在经济政策上看似对立，但在环境问题上却找到了共同点。|  partially opposed, indirectly opposed, seemingly opposed | completely opposed, directly antagonistic, entirely different
