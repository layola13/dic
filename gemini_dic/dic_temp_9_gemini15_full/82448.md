grumbling:/ˈɡrʌmblɪŋ/| n. v.|抱怨；咕哝；低沉的隆隆声|The act of grumbling or complaining in a low, indistinct tone; a low, rumbling sound.|例句：The passengers grumbled about the delay.乘客们抱怨延误。  The distant thunder was a low grumbling sound.远处的雷声是低沉的隆隆声。|近义词：complaining, muttering, murmuring|反义词：praising, complimenting|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grumbles about his job every day. = 他每天都抱怨他的工作。
They are grumbling about the unfair treatment. = 他们正在抱怨不公平的待遇。

一般过去时:
She grumbled about the cold weather. = 她抱怨寒冷的天气。
He grumbled under his breath. = 他低声咕哝。

现在完成时:
I have grumbled to him many times. = 我已经和他抱怨过很多次了。
They have been grumbling for hours. = 他们已经抱怨了好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
He had grumbled about the food before he left. = 他离开之前就抱怨过食物。
She had been grumbling all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在抱怨。

将来时:
I will grumble if the service is bad. = 如果服务不好，我会抱怨的。
They will be grumbling if the meeting runs late. = 如果会议迟到，他们会抱怨的。


被动语态:
His complaints were often grumbled about by others. = 他的抱怨经常被别人诟病。
The decision was grumbled at by many. = 许多人对这个决定表示不满。


疑问句:
Is he grumbling again? = 他又在抱怨了吗？
Were they grumbling about the food? = 他们是在抱怨食物吗？


否定句:
He didn't grumble about the long wait. = 他没有抱怨漫长的等待。
They weren't grumbling during the performance. = 他们在演出期间没有抱怨。
