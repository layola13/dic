redistrainer:/ˌriːˈdɪstreɪnər/| n. |再训练者，再培训者 |A person who undergoes retraining or who trains others in a new skill or field.  This word is a relatively recent coinage, not found in standard dictionaries. It combines "re" (again), "dist" (likely a shortened form of "distribute," suggesting the allocation of training), and "trainer." The meaning is derived from the components. |Example: The company provided funds to support the redistributors and redistreiners involved in the workforce transition. 公司提供资金支持参与劳动力转型中的再分配者和再培训者。|近义词:retrainer, refresher, upskiller|反义词: N/A
