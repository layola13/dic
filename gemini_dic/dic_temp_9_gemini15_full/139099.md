obduct:/əbˈdʌkt/| vt.|绑架；拐骗；使偏离（正常位置）|to take (someone) away illegally by force or deception; to draw or pull away from a straight line or course|例句：The child was abducted from his school.  孩子从学校被绑架了。|例句：The surgeon had to abduct the patient's leg to set the fracture.  外科医生不得不使病人的腿外展以接上骨折。|近义词：kidnap, carry off, snatch|反义词：release, restore


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The police abduct criminals. = 警方绑架罪犯。(This example is grammatically correct but factually incorrect;  police do not abduct criminals.  It is used to illustrate the verb tense.)
They abduct witnesses to prevent testimony. = 他们绑架证人以阻止证词。


一般过去时:
The gang abducted the businessman. = 该团伙绑架了这位商人。
They abducted her from her home. = 他们从她家绑架了她。


现在完成时:
The kidnappers have abducted three children this year. = 绑匪今年已经绑架了三个孩子。
They have abducted him and are demanding a ransom. = 他们绑架了他并要求赎金。


过去完成时:
The police discovered that the suspect had abducted the victim weeks earlier. = 警方发现嫌疑犯几周前绑架了受害者。
It turned out they had abducted the wrong person. = 原来他们绑架错了人。


将来时:
They will abduct the witness tomorrow. = 他们明天将绑架证人。(This is a hypothetical example.)
The police fear they will abduct more people.  = 警方担心他们会绑架更多的人。


虚拟语气:
If they abducted the president, the country would be in chaos. = 如果他们绑架了总统，国家就会陷入混乱。
I wish they hadn't abducted my sister. = 我真希望他们没有绑架我姐姐。


被动语态:
The child was abducted from the playground. = 孩子在操场上被绑架了。
The witness was abducted and held for ransom. = 证人被绑架并被索要赎金。


疑问句:
Was the victim abducted? = 受害者被绑架了吗？
Who abducted the child? = 谁绑架了孩子？


否定句:
The police did not abduct the suspect. = 警方没有绑架嫌疑人。
He was not abducted; he ran away. = 他没有被绑架；他是逃跑的。



PHRASE->
forcefully abduct = 强行绑架
secretly abduct = 秘密绑架
illegally abduct = 非法绑架
to abduct someone from = 从...绑架某人
to abduct someone to = 绑架某人到...
