scarves:/skɑːrvz/| n.|围巾；头巾|A long piece of cloth worn around the neck or head for warmth or decoration.  |例句：She wore a beautiful silk scarf. 她戴着一条美丽的丝绸围巾。

例句：Many winter scarves are made of wool. 许多冬天的围巾是用羊毛制成的。


近义词：shawls, wraps|反义词：None. |词性:名词

词源:
The word "scarf" comes from the Old Norse word "scarfa," meaning "to cover."  It evolved through Middle English and Old French. The plural form "scarves" is a regular pluralization.
