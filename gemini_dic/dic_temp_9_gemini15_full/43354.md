cranch:/kræntʃ/ | vt.|嘎吱嘎吱地咬碎；压碎；|to crush or grind something noisily between the teeth|例句：He cranched the ice cubes between his teeth. 他嘎吱嘎吱地咬碎了冰块。|例句：The machine cranched the rocks into gravel. 机器把岩石压碎成碎石。|近义词：crunch, crush, grind|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crunches the apples. = 他嘎吱嘎吱地吃苹果。
She crunches the numbers. = 她仔细计算数字。


一般过去时:
He cranched the nuts. = 他嘎吱嘎吱地嚼碎坚果。
She cranched the data. = 她处理数据。

He has cranched all the carrots. = 他已经把所有胡萝卜都咬碎了。

过去完成时:

She had cranched the data before she started the analysis. = 在开始分析之前，她已经处理好数据了。


将来时:
