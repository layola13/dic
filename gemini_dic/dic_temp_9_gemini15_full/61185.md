eluding:/ɪˈluːdɪŋ/ | v. |躲避；逃避；使难以捉摸| to escape from or avoid (someone or something), especially by being clever or quick.  |例句：The thief eluded the police. 小偷躲避了警察。 |例句：The answer to the riddle eluded me. 我找不到谜语的答案。|近义词：evading, avoiding, escaping, dodging|反义词： confronting, facing, catching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police are eluding the suspect. = 警察正在追捕嫌疑犯。
He eludes his responsibilities. = 他逃避责任。


一般过去时:
The criminal eluded capture for years. = 这个罪犯潜逃多年。
She cleverly eluded her pursuers. = 她巧妙地躲过了追捕者。


现在完成时:
The truth has eluded us for too long. = 真相我们已经寻找太久了。
He has eluded capture despite the police's best efforts. = 尽管警方尽了最大努力，他还是逃脱了逮捕。


过去完成时:
The treasure had eluded explorers for centuries. = 宝藏几个世纪以来一直未被探险家发现。
He had eluded detection until now. = 直到此刻，他都一直未被发现。


将来时:
He will elude capture if he's careful. = 如果他小心谨慎，他会逃脱追捕。
The solution to the problem will likely elude us for some time. = 这个问题的解决方法可能在一段时间内难以找到。


虚拟语气:
If only I could elude this feeling of dread. = 但愿我能摆脱这种恐惧感。
I wish I could elude the consequences of my actions. = 我真希望我能逃避我行为的后果。


被动语态:
The criminal was eluded by the police. = 罪犯被警察追捕。
The solution to the puzzle was eluded by the students. = 学生们没有找到谜题的答案。


疑问句:
How did he elude the guards? = 他是如何躲过警卫的？
Can we elude this difficult conversation? = 我们能避免这场艰难的谈话吗？


否定句:
The suspect did not elude the police. = 嫌疑犯没有躲避警察。
We could not elude the inevitable. = 我们无法逃避不可避免的事情。
