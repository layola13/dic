semi-intellectuals:/ˈsɛmiɪnˌtɛləkʃuəl/| n. |半知识分子；自以为是的知识分子 |People who affect intellectualism but lack genuine depth of knowledge or understanding. They often display superficial knowledge and engage in intellectual posturing. |Example:  He considered himself a semi-intellectual, quoting obscure authors while demonstrating little true understanding of their work. 他自认为是半知识分子，引用晦涩的作家，却很少真正理解他们的作品。|近义词：intellectual pretender, dilettante, poseur |反义词：intellectual, scholar, expert
