dutiful:/ˈdjuːtɪfl/ | adj. | 尽职的；孝顺的；恭敬的 | Characterized by a strong sense of duty; obedient and respectful.  |例句：She was a dutiful daughter who always visited her parents. 她是一位孝顺的女儿，总是去看望她的父母。|例句：He was a dutiful employee who always completed his work on time. 他是一位尽职的员工，总是按时完成工作。|近义词：obedient, compliant, respectful, conscientious |反义词：disobedient, rebellious, negligent, irresponsible
