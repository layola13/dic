quasi-greatly: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈɡreɪtli/ | adv. | 准伟大地，近乎伟大地 |  This word is not a standard English word. It's a combination of "quasi" and "greatly," creating a non-standard adverb.  It implies something approaching greatness but falling short of it.  The meaning depends heavily on context. | 例句：His quasi-greatly improved performance was still not enough to win the competition.  他的表现虽然有了很大的提升，但仍然不足以赢得比赛。


近义词: almost greatly, nearly greatly, somewhat greatly, significantly


反义词: insignificantly, minimally, slightly


词性: adverb
