preappointment:/ˌpriːəˈpɔɪntmənt/| n. | 预约；事先约定 | A preappointment is an arrangement made in advance for an appointment. It refers to the process or act of scheduling something before the actual appointment time.  This often involves confirming details, such as date, time, and purpose. | Example:  The patient made a preappointment to see the doctor next week.  这位病人预约了下周去看医生。 |近义词：advance scheduling, prior arrangement |反义词：last-minute arrangement, impromptu meeting |名词
