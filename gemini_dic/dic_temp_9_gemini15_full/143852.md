outplayed:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪd/| v.|技高一筹，胜过，打败|To surpass or defeat someone in a game or competition, often by demonstrating superior skill or strategy.|例句：The underdog team outplayed the favorites and won the championship.  实力较弱的球队技高一筹，赢得了冠军。|例句：He completely outplayed his opponent in the chess match.  他在国际象棋比赛中完全击败了他的对手。|近义词：outmaneuvered, defeated, surpassed, outwitted |反义词：outplayed (被动语态时为反义), defeated (被动语态时为反义)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outplays his opponents every week. = 他每周都胜过他的对手。
She consistently outplays her teammates in practice. = 她在练习中总是胜过她的队友。

一般过去时:
The champion outplayed the challenger in the final round. = 冠军在决赛中击败了挑战者。
Our team outplayed their team by a large margin. = 我们队以很大的优势击败了他们队。

现在完成时:
He has outplayed every opponent he's faced this season. = 这个赛季他击败了他遇到的每一个对手。
She has outplayed her rivals throughout her career. = 在她的整个职业生涯中，她都胜过她的对手。

过去完成时:
By the end of the game, they had outplayed their rivals for two hours. = 到比赛结束时，他们已经胜过对手两个小时了。
The team had outplayed the opposition, and the victory was clear. = 球队已经胜过对手，胜利已成定局。

将来时:
I predict that she will outplay her opponent in the next match. = 我预测她将在下一场比赛中击败对手。
He believes he will outplay his rival and win the tournament. = 他相信他会击败他的对手并赢得比赛。

虚拟语气:
If he had played better, he might have outplayed his opponent. = 如果他打得更好，他可能就击败了他的对手。
I wish I could outplay the world champion. = 我希望我能击败世界冠军。

被动语态:
He was outplayed by a younger, more agile opponent. = 他被一位更年轻、更灵活的对手击败了。
The team was outplayed in the second half of the game. = 该队在下半场被击败了。

疑问句:
Did he outplay his opponent in the game? = 他在比赛中击败了他的对手吗？
Will she be able to outplay her rival in the final? = 她能够在决赛中击败她的对手吗？

否定句:
He didn't outplay his opponent; he was defeated. = 他没有击败他的对手；他被击败了。
She didn't outplay her rival, but she played a very good game. = 她没有击败她的对手，但她打了一场非常好的比赛。
