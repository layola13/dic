dendroidal: /dɛnˈdrɔɪdəl/ | adj. | 树状的，树枝状的 | Resembling a tree or tree branch in form or structure; branching like a tree.  The term often describes structures with a hierarchical, branching pattern, similar to the branching of a tree's limbs. | Example: The dendroidal molecule exhibited a complex, highly branched structure.  树状分子展现出复杂的高度分支结构。  The dendroidal polymer showed excellent properties due to its unique branching architecture.  这种树状聚合物由于其独特的分支结构而表现出优异的性能。 | 近义词: arborescent, treelike, ramified, branching | 反义词: linear, unbranched
