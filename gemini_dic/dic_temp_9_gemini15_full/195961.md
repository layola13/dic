sensuism: /ˈsɛnʃuɪzəm/ | n. |感觉主义；感官主义 |The philosophical doctrine that all knowledge is derived from sensory experience.  It emphasizes the role of the senses in acquiring knowledge and understanding the world. Sensuism often contrasts with rationalism, which prioritizes reason and intellect. | Example:  His art was a pure expression of sensuism, relying entirely on the viewer's sensory perception to convey meaning.  他的艺术是纯粹的感觉主义的表达，完全依靠观众的感官感知来传达意义。| 近义词：empiricism, sensualism | 反义词：rationalism, idealism | 名词
