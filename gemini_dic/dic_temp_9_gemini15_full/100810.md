intrinsic: /ɪnˈtrɪnzɪk/ | adj. | 内在的；本质的；固有的 | Belonging to or being a part of the essential nature of something; inherent.  |例句：The intrinsic value of a diamond is determined by its size and clarity.  钻石的内在价值取决于其大小和净度。

例句：The intrinsic motivation to learn is crucial for academic success.  学习的内在动力对学业成功至关重要。


近义词：inherent, innate, immanent, essential, fundamental | 反义词：extrinsic, external, superficial


词源:  From Middle English intrinsèque, from Old French intrinsèque, from Latin intrinsecus "from within," from intra- "within" + secus "near, beside."
