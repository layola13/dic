cushite:/ˈkjuːsaɪt/| n. |库施人；库施语|A member of a group of peoples inhabiting ancient Kush (in present-day Sudan and Eritrea).  The term can also refer to their language.  The etymology is ultimately from the name of the ancient kingdom of Kush.|The Cushite languages are a branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family.  Many ancient Egyptian texts mention Cushites.|例句：Archaeologists have discovered numerous artifacts related to the Cushite civilization. 考古学家发现了许多与库施文明相关的文物。

The ancient Cushite kingdom was known for its advanced irrigation systems. 古代库施王国以其先进的灌溉系统而闻名。|近义词：Nubian (in some contexts)|反义词：无
