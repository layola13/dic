rebounding:/rɪˈbaʊndɪŋ/| v. |反弹；恢复；回升|The act or process of bouncing back; recovery from a setback or difficulty;  a resurgence or renewed growth.|例句：The economy is showing signs of rebounding after the recent recession. 近期经济衰退后，经济显示出复苏的迹象。

例句：After a period of illness, she is slowly rebounding.  生病一段时间后，她正在慢慢恢复。


近义词：recovering, rallying, reviving, springing back|反义词：collapsing, declining, deteriorating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ball is rebounding off the wall. = 球从墙上反弹回来。
The company is rebounding from its losses. = 公司正在从损失中恢复过来。

一般过去时:
The price rebounded sharply after the initial drop. = 价格在最初下跌后迅速反弹。
The team rebounded from their defeat. = 球队从失败中恢复过来。

现在完成时:
The market has rebounded strongly this year. = 今年市场强劲反弹。
She has rebounded well from her injury. = 她从伤病中恢复得很好。

过去完成时:
The stock market had rebounded before the news broke. = 在消息传出之前，股市已经反弹。
He had rebounded from several setbacks before achieving success. = 在取得成功之前，他已经克服了多次挫折。

将来时:
The economy is expected to rebound next year. = 预计经济明年将反弹。
They believe the team will rebound next season. = 他们相信球队下个赛季将反弹。

虚拟语气:
If the economy rebounded quickly, we would see growth. = 如果经济迅速反弹，我们将看到增长。
I wish the team had rebounded sooner. = 我希望球队能早点恢复过来。

被动语态:
The situation is being rebounded from. = 局势正在得到扭转。
The damage was quickly rebounded from. = 损害很快得到了弥补。

疑问句:
Is the market rebounding? = 市场正在反弹吗？
Will the team rebound from this loss? = 球队会从这场失利中恢复过来吗？

否定句:
The economy is not rebounding as quickly as expected. = 经济并没有像预期的那样迅速反弹。
The sales figures did not rebound last quarter. = 上个季度的销售额没有回升。
