siccative:/ˈsɪkətɪv/| n.|干燥剂；促干剂|A substance that accelerates the drying of paints, inks, or oils.  It's often a metallic soap or a drier. |Example: The artist added a siccative to the oil paint to speed up the drying process.  艺术家在油画颜料中添加了干燥剂以加快干燥过程。|近义词：drier, dryer|反义词：None specifically, but substances that *slow* drying could be considered antonyms.
