inventorying:/ˈɪnvəntɔːriɪŋ/| v.|盘存；清点；编目|The act of creating or updating an inventory.  It involves systematically identifying, counting, and recording items. This process is crucial for managing assets, tracking stock levels, and ensuring accurate accounting. |例句：We are inventorying the warehouse before the year-end audit. 我们在年末审计前正在清点仓库。

例句：The museum is currently inventorying its collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆目前正在清点其古代文物收藏。


近义词：cataloging, listing, counting, stocktaking
