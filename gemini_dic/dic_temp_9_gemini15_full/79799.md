glycerole:/ˈɡlɪsərɒl/| n. |甘油；丙三醇|Glycerol, also called glycerine or glycerin, is a simple polyol compound. It is a colorless, odorless, viscous liquid that is sweet-tasting and non-toxic. It is widely used in food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic products as a humectant, solvent, and sweetener.|例句：Glycerol is a key component of many skincare products. 甘油是许多护肤品的重要成分。|例句：The experiment used glycerol as a solvent. 实验中使用甘油作为溶剂。|近义词：glycerine, glycerin|反义词：无
