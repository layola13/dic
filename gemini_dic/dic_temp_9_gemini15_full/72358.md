flowerpot: /ˈflaʊəpɒt/ /ˈflaʊərˌpɑːt/ | n. | 花盆 | A container, typically made of clay, plastic, or ceramic, used for growing and displaying flowers or plants.  | Example: She placed the delicate orchid in a beautiful terracotta flowerpot. 她把娇嫩的兰花放在一个漂亮的白陶花盆里。 |近义词：plant pot, planter |反义词：None
