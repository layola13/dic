conj:/kənˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ | conj. | 连词 | A word that connects clauses or sentences or words within a clause, such as "and," "but," "or," "nor," "for," "so," "yet."  It shows the relationship between the connected elements. |  例句：He is rich, but he is not happy. 他很有钱，但他并不快乐。 | 近义词：however, therefore, furthermore, moreover | 反义词: N/A

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
