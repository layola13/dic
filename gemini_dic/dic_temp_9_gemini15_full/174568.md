quips:/kwɪps/| n. v.|俏皮话，妙语；挖苦；快速地说|A quip is a witty and brief remark.  It can be humorous, sarcastic, or cynical.  The verb form means to say something witty or sarcastic quickly.|例句：He's known for his sharp quips and witty observations. 他以其尖刻的俏皮话和诙谐的见解而闻名。

She quipped that the meeting was a waste of time. 她俏皮地说会议是浪费时间。|近义词：witticism, joke, jest, remark|反义词：serious statement, lengthy explanation
