camouflaged:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd/| adj.|伪装的，隐蔽的| disguised or hidden by camouflage.  Camouflaged objects blend in with their surroundings to avoid detection.|例句：The soldier was camouflaged in the jungle.  士兵在丛林中伪装了起来。

例句：The chameleon camouflaged itself against the leaves.  变色龙把自己伪装成树叶的颜色。


近义词：concealed, disguised, hidden, masked

反义词：exposed, visible, obvious
