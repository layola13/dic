shipment:/ˈʃɪpmənt/| n.|装运；货物；运送|The act of shipping goods; the goods shipped.|例句：The shipment arrived on time. 这批货物准时到达了。|例句：We are expecting a large shipment of new products next week. 我们预计下周将有一大批新产品到货。|近义词：consignment, delivery, cargo|反义词：receipt (in the sense of receiving goods)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company ships many products every day. = 公司每天运送许多产品。
He usually checks the shipment details before it leaves. = 他通常在货物离开之前检查装运细节。

一般过去时:
They shipped the goods last week. = 他们上周运送了货物。
The shipment arrived late last night. = 货物昨晚深夜才到达。

现在完成时:
We have shipped all the orders. = 我们已经发货了所有订单。
They have received the shipment already. = 他们已经收到了货物。

过去完成时:
They had shipped the goods before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已发货。
The customer had already received the shipment when we called. = 当我们打电话时，客户已经收到了货物。

将来时:
We will ship the order tomorrow. = 我们明天发货。
The shipment will arrive next week. = 货物将于下周到达。

虚拟语气:
If we shipped the goods earlier, we would have avoided the delay. = 如果我们早点发货，就能避免延误。
I wish we had shipped the goods sooner. = 我真希望我们早点发货。

被动语态:
The goods were shipped yesterday. = 货物昨天被运送了。
The shipment is being processed now. = 货物正在处理中。

疑问句:
When will the shipment arrive? = 货物什么时候到达？
Has the shipment been sent yet? = 货物已经发出去了吗？

否定句:
We did not ship the order yet. = 我们还没发货。
The shipment has not arrived. = 货物还没到。


PHRASE->
a large shipment = 大量货物
a shipment of goods = 一批货物
delayed shipment = 延迟的货物
track a shipment = 追踪货物
shipment confirmation = 发货确认
