chevronel:/ʃɛvrənˈɛl/ | n. |人字形饰物；锯齿形图案|A chevronel is a heraldic charge, a shape in the form of a pair of chevrons joined at their points, forming a shape resembling a zigzag line or a series of inverted Vs.  It is often used in architecture, design, and heraldry. |The architect incorporated chevronels into the design of the building's facade. 建筑师将人字形饰物融入建筑物立面的设计中。|近义词：zigzag, chevron (单一人字形)|反义词：straight line, smooth surface|名词
