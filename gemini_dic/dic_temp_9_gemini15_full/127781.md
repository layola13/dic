neighborhoods:/ˈneɪbərhʊdz/| n. |街区；邻里；附近地区 | Neighborhoods are the smaller geographic areas within a city or town. They are often characterized by a sense of community and shared identity.  |The neighborhoods in this city are very diverse.  这个城市的街区非常多元化。|The new park improved the quality of life in the surrounding neighborhoods. 新公园提高了周边街区的居民生活水平。|districts, areas, communities | wilderness, rural areas
