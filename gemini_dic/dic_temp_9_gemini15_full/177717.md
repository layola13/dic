recapitulate:/rɪˈkæpɪtʃuleɪt/| vt.|概括；总结；重述|To summarize and state again the main points of.|The teacher recapitulated the main points of the lesson.老师概括了这节课的主要内容。|He recapitulated his argument. 他再次陈述了他的论点。|summarize, summarize, restate|elaborate, detail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recapitulates the key findings in his conclusion. 他在结论中概括了主要发现。
She recapitulates the story at the end of the chapter. 她在章节结尾处对故事进行了总结。

一般过去时:
The speaker recapitulated the previous points before moving on. 说话人在继续之前概括了之前的要点。
The lawyer recapitulated the witness's testimony. 律师概括了证人的证词。

现在完成时:
The author has recapitulated the historical events leading up to the war. 作者已经概括了导致战争的历史事件。
She has recapitulated the arguments in her final paper. 她在她的最终论文中总结了论点。

过去完成时:
He had recapitulated the events of the day before falling asleep. 他在睡着前回顾了一天发生的事情。
The professor had recapitulated the theory before beginning the experiment. 教授在开始实验之前回顾了理论。

将来时:
The presenter will recapitulate the main points at the end of the presentation. 演示者将在演示结束时概括要点。
She will recapitulate the research findings in her report. 她将在报告中总结研究结果。

虚拟语气:
If I were to recapitulate my life, I would focus on my family. 如果我要总结我的一生，我会关注我的家人。
I wish I could recapitulate the entire conversation. 我希望我能总结整段对话。

被动语态:
The key points were recapitulated by the speaker. 要点由演讲者进行了总结。
The professor's lecture was recapitulated in the student's notes. 教授的讲座在学生的笔记中得到了总结。

疑问句:
Can you recapitulate the instructions for me? 你能为我总结一下说明吗？
Will you recapitulate the arguments presented? 你会总结提出的论点吗？

否定句:
He did not recapitulate the details of the accident. 他没有详细叙述事故的细节。
She did not recapitulate her findings in the final report. 她没有在最终报告中总结她的发现。
