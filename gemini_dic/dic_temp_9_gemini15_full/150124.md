pamperos:/pæmˈpɛroʊz/| n. |潘帕斯风，南美大草原的强风|Strong, gusty winds that blow across the pampas of South America. These winds are often associated with cold fronts and can bring significant temperature drops and dust storms.|The pamperos swept across the pampas, causing havoc to the crops.|近义词：gale, blizzard, squall|反义词：calm, breeze
