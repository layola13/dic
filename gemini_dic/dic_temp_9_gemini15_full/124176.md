mosaic's:/moʊˈzeɪks/| n.|马赛克的；镶嵌画的|A mosaic is a picture or decoration made of small pieces of colored stone, glass, or tile.  It refers to something composed of diverse elements. The possessive form indicates belonging to or associated with a mosaic.|例句：The church floor was a beautiful mosaic of intricate designs.  教堂的地面是一个美丽的，图案复杂的马赛克。 The artist's style is a mosaic of influences from different cultures.  这位艺术家的风格是来自不同文化的各种影响的融合。|近义词：inlay, patchwork, composite|反义词：uniform, homogenous|名词
