physicist:/ˈfɪzɪsɪst/ | n. |物理学家 | A scientist who studies physics.  Physicists work in various fields, from theoretical physics to experimental physics, applying physical principles to understand the universe. | Example: Einstein was a renowned physicist who revolutionized our understanding of gravity. 爱因斯坦是一位著名的物理学家，他彻底改变了我们对引力的理解。 | 近义词：physical scientist, researcher (in physics) | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)
