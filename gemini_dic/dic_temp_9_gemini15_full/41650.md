correctress:/kəˈrektəs/ | n. |女校正员，女订正者 |A woman employed to correct proofs or manuscripts.  This word is now largely archaic or used in very specific contexts. It emphasizes the gender of the person performing the correction task. |Example: The meticulous correctress ensured the book was free of errors before publication.  女校正员确保书在出版前没有错误。|近义词：proofreader, editor (in a specific context)|反义词：None directly, but the opposite would be someone who introduces errors. |名词
