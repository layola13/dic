meaningfully:/ˈmiːnɪŋfli/| adv.| 有意义地；意味深长地 |In a way that is full of meaning or significance; meaningfully.  It implies purpose, intention, and impact. |He meaningfully tapped his finger on the table. 他意味深长地用手指敲了敲桌子。|significantly, purposefully, expressively|pointlessly, insignificantly, meaninglessly|副词
