retaliate:/rɪˈtælieɪt/ | vt. | 报复；反击 | To retaliate means to take revenge or act against someone who has harmed you.  It implies a deliberate and often forceful response to a previous act of aggression or injustice. |  例句：The country retaliated against the attack by launching its own missiles. 这个国家通过发射自己的导弹来报复袭击。

例句：He retaliated by refusing to cooperate. 他以拒绝合作来进行报复。


近义词：revenge, avenge, reprisal, counterattack | 反义词：forgive, pardon, absolve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retaliate against their enemies. = 他们报复他们的敌人。
We retaliate with equal force. = 我们以同样的力量反击。


一般过去时:
The victim retaliated against his attacker. = 受害者向袭击者报复。
She retaliated by filing a lawsuit. = 她通过提起诉讼来报复。


现在完成时:
He has retaliated for the insults he received. = 他已经报复了他收到的侮辱。
They have retaliated by imposing sanctions. = 他们通过实施制裁来进行报复。


过去完成时:
They had retaliated before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经进行了报复。
She had retaliated by spreading rumors. = 她通过散布谣言来进行报复。


将来时:
The government will retaliate if necessary. = 必要时，政府将进行报复。
He will retaliate against those who wronged him. = 他将报复那些冤枉他的人。


虚拟语气:
If he were to retaliate, it would only make things worse. = 如果他要报复，只会使事情变得更糟。
I wish I hadn't retaliated so aggressively. = 我真希望我没有那么强硬地报复。


被动语态:
The attack was retaliated against swiftly. = 袭击得到了迅速的报复。
Retaliatory measures are being considered. = 正在考虑报复措施。


疑问句:
Will the company retaliate against the competitor? = 公司会报复竞争对手吗？
How will they retaliate against the injustice? = 他们将如何报复这种不公正？


否定句:
He did not retaliate against his opponent. = 他没有报复他的对手。
The government chose not to retaliate. = 政府选择不报复。
