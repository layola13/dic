meal:/miːl/| n. |膳食，一顿饭 |A meal is an occasion when food is eaten, or the food eaten on such an occasion.  It can refer to breakfast, lunch, or dinner. |例句：We had a delicious meal at the restaurant. 我们在餐厅享用了一顿美味的餐点。|例句：The chef prepared a three-course meal for the guests.  厨师为客人准备了一套三道菜的饭菜。|近义词：repast, repast, feed |反义词:fast, starvation


USAGE->

What meal do you prefer? = 你更喜欢哪一顿饭？
