ity:/ˈɪti/ | suffix | 性质；状态；程度 |  A suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.  It is derived from the Latin suffix "-itas," which has the same function. |  The clarity of the explanation was impressive.  The city's prosperity is evident. | 近义词: -ness, -ship, -hood | 反义词: (depends on the adjective it's added to)


USAGE->
This is a suffix, not an independent word, so it doesn't have verb conjugations or tenses like "what".  The usage examples would be the words it creates.  For example, using "clarity":

一般现在时:
The clarity of the water is excellent. = 水的清澈度极佳。
His explanation shows great clarity. = 他的解释非常清晰。


一般过去时:
The clarity of the photo was amazing. = 照片的清晰度令人惊叹。
The professor's lecture lacked clarity. = 教授的讲座缺乏清晰度。


现在完成时:
The improvements have increased the clarity of the image. = 改进提高了图像的清晰度。
Recent studies have added clarity to the issue. = 最近的研究使这个问题更加清晰。


过去完成时:
The initial draft had lacked clarity, but the final version was much better. = 初稿缺乏清晰度，但最终版本好多了。
Before the revision, the manuscript had little clarity. = 修改前，手稿几乎没有清晰度。


将来时:
The new software will improve the clarity of the display. = 新软件将提高显示器的清晰度。
Further research will bring more clarity to the problem. = 进一步的研究将使这个问题更加清晰。


虚拟语气:
If the instructions were clearer, the task would be easier. = 如果说明更清晰，任务就会更容易。
I wish the report had more clarity. = 我希望这份报告更清晰。


被动语态:
The issue was discussed with great clarity. = 这个问题得到了非常清晰的讨论。
Clarity is ensured through careful editing. = 通过仔细编辑来确保清晰度。


疑问句:
Is there any clarity in their response? = 他们的回应是否清晰？
What is the clarity of the audio recording? = 音频录制的清晰度如何？


否定句:
The instructions lacked clarity. = 说明不够清晰。
Her explanation did not provide any clarity. = 她的解释没有提供任何清晰度。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases using "-ity" itself, as it's a suffix. Phrases would be built around words *containing* the suffix. Examples using words with "-ity":

*  "Superiority complex" (优越感)
*   "Community spirit" (社区精神)
*   "Simplicity itself" (简单本身)
*   "High quality" (高质量) (quality contains the suffix -ity in its origin)
*   "Extreme rarity" (极其罕见) (rarity contains the suffix -ity)
