lassie: /ˈlæsi/ | n. | 少女；小姑娘；（尤指）牧羊犬 | A young girl; a young female dog, especially a sheepdog.  |例句：The lassie was playing with her dog in the park.  这个小姑娘正在公园里和她的小狗玩耍。  |例句： That lassie is a clever sheepdog. 那只牧羊犬很聪明。 |近义词：girl, maiden, lass,  shepherdess (if referring to a sheepdog handler) |反义词：old woman, matron
