hypertrophy:/haɪˈpɜːtrəfi/| n.|肥大；增生|The increase in the size of an organ or tissue due to an increase in the size of its cells.  This is distinct from hyperplasia, which is an increase in the number of cells.|例句：Muscle hypertrophy is a common goal for weightlifters. 肌肥大是举重运动员的常见目标。

例句：The doctor diagnosed the patient with cardiac hypertrophy. 医生诊断病人患有心脏肥大。


近义词：enlargement, growth, swelling |反义词：atrophy, degeneration
