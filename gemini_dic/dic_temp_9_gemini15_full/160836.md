possessives:/pəˈzɛsɪvz/| n.|所有格，所有物|The term "possessives" refers to words or grammatical structures that indicate possession or ownership.  They show a relationship of belonging between one thing and another. This can be shown through possessive nouns (e.g., John's car), possessive pronouns (e.g., mine, yours, his), or possessive adjectives (e.g., my, your, his).|例句：That's John's book. 那是约翰的书。The dog wagged its tail. 那条狗摇着它的尾巴。|近义词：ownership, possession|反义词：N/A
