raids:/reɪdz/| n. v.|袭击；突袭；搜查|A sudden attack, typically a military one.  Also refers to a series of attacks or a sudden incursion.  As a verb, it means to make a sudden attack on a place. |例句：The enemy launched a series of raids on our border towns. 敌人对我们的边境城镇发动了一系列袭击。

The police raided the suspected drug dealer's house. 警方突袭了涉嫌贩毒者的住所。|近义词：attacks, assaults, incursions, forays|反义词：defenses, retreats|词性: n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army raids enemy territory regularly. = 军队定期袭击敌方领土。
He raids the fridge at night. = 他晚上经常偷吃冰箱里的东西。


一般过去时:
The Vikings raided the coast. = 维京人袭击了海岸。
She raided her piggy bank for money. = 她为了钱而洗劫了她的存钱罐。


现在完成时:
Terrorists have raided several buildings this year. = 恐怖分子今年已经袭击了几座建筑物。
They have raided the museum three times. = 他们已经三次洗劫了博物馆。


过去完成时:
The police had raided the building before the criminals escaped. = 罪犯逃脱之前，警方已经突袭了那座建筑物。
The thieves had raided the shop before the alarm went off. = 警报响起之前，小偷们已经洗劫了商店。


将来时:
The rebels will raid the city tomorrow. = 反叛者明天将袭击这座城市。
She will raid the shops for a new dress. = 她将去商店抢购一件新衣服。


虚拟语气:
If they raided the bank, they would be arrested. = 如果他们抢劫银行，他们会被逮捕。
I wish I hadn't raided the cookie jar. = 我真希望我没有偷吃饼干。


被动语态:
The town was raided by the enemy. = 该镇遭到敌人的袭击。
The suspect's house is being raided by the police. = 嫌疑人的房子正在被警方搜查。


疑问句:
Were the villages raided last night? = 村庄昨晚被袭击了吗？
Has the bank been raided recently? = 银行最近被抢劫了吗？


否定句:
The soldiers did not raid the enemy camp. = 士兵们没有袭击敌营。
They haven't raided the store yet. = 他们还没有洗劫商店。
