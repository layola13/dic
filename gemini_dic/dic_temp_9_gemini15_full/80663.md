gordia:/ˈɡɔːrdiə/| n.|戈尔迪之结；难以解决的问题|A Gordian knot is a complex problem that is extremely difficult or impossible to solve.  The term originates from the legend of Gordius, a Phrygian king, whose ox-cart yoke was tied with a knot so intricate that it was said only the person who could untie it would rule Asia. Alexander the Great famously solved the problem by cutting the knot with his sword.|例句：The negotiations became a Gordian knot, with neither side willing to compromise.  谈判变成了一个戈尔迪之结，双方都不愿妥协。|近义词：tangle, enigma, puzzle |反义词：simple solution, easy fix|名词
