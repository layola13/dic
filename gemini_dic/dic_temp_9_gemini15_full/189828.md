salvor:/ˈsælvər/| n. |救捞者；打捞者；（船舶或货物）救助者|A salvor is a person or company that salvages wrecked or abandoned ships or cargo.  They undertake the risky and often difficult task of recovering property from maritime disasters.  Their work involves considerable expertise and specialized equipment.|例句：The salvor successfully recovered a significant portion of the cargo from the sunken vessel.  救捞者成功地从沉船中打捞出大部分货物。

The salvor claimed a substantial reward for their efforts in rescuing the stranded yacht.  救助者因成功营救搁浅的游艇而索要巨额酬劳。|近义词：rescuer, recoverer|反义词：wrecker (in the sense of someone who deliberately destroys)|词性:名词
