scrummagers:/ˈskrʌmɪdʒərz/| n.|争抢者，奋力拼搏者|People who scrummage, especially in rugby.  They are characterized by their aggressive and determined efforts to gain possession or advantage.|The scrummagers fought fiercely for the ball.  争球的队员们为了球奋力拼搏。 The team's success depended heavily on the effectiveness of their scrummagers. 球队的成功很大程度上取决于他们争球队员的有效性。|近义词：scramblers, contenders, competitors|反义词：bystanders, spectators
