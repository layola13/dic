imitating:/ˈɪmɪteɪtɪŋ/| v.|模仿，效仿|The act of copying someone or something.|例句：The child was imitating his father's walk.  孩子正在模仿他父亲的走路姿势。|例句：She's imitating a famous actress's style. 她正在模仿一位著名女演员的风格。|近义词：copying, mimicking, simulating|反义词：originating, innovating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is imitating my accent. = 他正在模仿我的口音。
Birds are imitating each other's songs. = 鸟儿们正在互相模仿彼此的歌声。

一般过去时:
She imitated her teacher's style of teaching. = 她模仿了她老师的教学风格。
The monkey imitated the actions of the humans. = 猴子模仿了人类的动作。

现在完成时:
They have been imitating the latest trends in fashion. = 他们一直在模仿最新的时尚潮流。
The artist has imitated many famous paintings. =这位艺术家模仿了许多名画。

过去完成时:
He had imitated that artist's style for years. = 他多年来一直模仿那位艺术家的风格。
Before the performance, she had imitated the lead singer's voice countless times. = 演出之前，她无数次模仿过主唱的声音。

将来时:
She will imitate the famous singer's performance. = 她将模仿这位著名歌手的演出。
The students will imitate the teacher's pronunciation. = 学生们将模仿老师的发音。

虚拟语气:
If I could imitate his skill, I would be very happy. = 如果我能模仿他的技巧，我会很高兴。
I wish I could imitate his talent for painting. = 我希望我能模仿他绘画的天赋。

被动语态:
His style was imitated by many young artists. = 他的风格被许多年轻艺术家模仿。
The speech was being imitated by several students. = 几个学生正在模仿这个演讲。

疑问句:
Is he imitating anyone in particular? = 他是在模仿某个特定的人吗？
Are they imitating the style of the 1920s? = 他们是在模仿20年代的风格吗？

否定句:
She didn't imitate his bad habits. = 她没有模仿他的坏习惯。
They are not imitating anyone; they are being original. = 他们没有模仿任何人；他们是原创的。
