/ˈɪzɑːrd/| n. |蜥蜴|A lizard is a reptile that typically has scales, four legs, and a long tail.  Lizards are found in a wide variety of habitats around the world. |Example:  The chameleon is a type of lizard.  变色龙是一种蜥蜴。|近义词：reptile, saurian |反义词：无
