collocutor:/ˈkɒləkjuːtə(r)/|/ˈkɑːləˌkjuːtər/| n. |对话者；谈话者|A collocutor is a person who is involved in a conversation or dialogue.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of the interaction.  The term is often used in linguistics and communication studies.|Example:  The collocutors engaged in a lively debate about the merits of different philosophical approaches.  对话者就不同哲学方法的优缺点进行了热烈的辩论。  |近义词：conversationalist, interlocutor, speaker|反义词：listener (in a specific context)|名词
