shuttlecock:/ˈʃʌtəlˌkɒk/| n.| 羽毛球 |A shuttlecock is a projectile used in the game of badminton.  It consists of a small, rounded cork or plastic base with feathers or synthetic fabric attached.  The feathers are arranged in a symmetrical pattern to create a stable flight path. |例句：The shuttlecock soared high over the net.  羽毛球高高地飞过球网。
