depressingly:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋli/ | adv. |令人沮丧地，令人失望地 |  Describes something that causes sadness, disappointment, or a feeling of hopelessness. It modifies a verb, adjective, or entire clause, expressing the manner in which something happens or exists. |例句：The rain continued depressingly throughout the day.  = 雨整整一天令人沮丧地下个不停。

The results of the experiment were depressingly low. = 实验结果令人沮丧地低。

近义词：discouragingly, dishearteningly, bleakly, grimly | 反义词：encouragingly, upliftingly, joyfully, exhilaratingly
