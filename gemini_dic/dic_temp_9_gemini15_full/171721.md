pummelo:/ˈpʌmɛloʊ/| n. |柚子|A large citrus fruit, similar to a grapefruit but larger and sweeter, with a thick rind.  It is believed to be the ancestor of the grapefruit. |例句：The pummelo is a delicious tropical fruit with a slightly tart flavor. 柚子是一种美味的热带水果，味道略带酸涩。|例句： We bought a large, juicy pummelo at the farmers market.我们在农贸市场买了一个又大又多汁的柚子。|近义词：grapefruit, shaddock|反义词：N/A
