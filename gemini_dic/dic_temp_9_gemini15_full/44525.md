crossroad:/ˈkrɒsroʊd/| /ˈkrɔːsroʊd/ | n. |十字路口，转折点 |A crossroad is a place where two or more roads meet and cross each other.  It can also refer to a point in time when a crucial decision must be made, often signifying a turning point in someone's life. |例句：He stood at a crossroad, unsure which path to take. 他站在十字路口，不知道该走哪条路。

例句：The company is at a crossroads, needing to decide whether to expand or downsize. 这家公司正处于十字路口，需要决定是扩张还是缩减规模。


近义词：intersection, junction, turning point, critical juncture |反义词：straight path, single direction
