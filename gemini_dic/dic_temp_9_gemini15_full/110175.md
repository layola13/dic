liens:/ˈliːnz/| n.|留置权，债权|A right to keep possession of property belonging to another person until a debt owed by that person is discharged.|The bank placed a lien on the house until the loan was repaid.  银行对该房屋设置了留置权，直到贷款偿还。


The mechanic placed a lien on the car until the repair bill was paid. 修理工对汽车设置了留置权，直到修理费支付。|近义词：claim, charge, encumbrance|反义词：release, discharge|名词
