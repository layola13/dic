overimaginatively:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmædʒɪnətɪvli/ | adv. | 过度想象地，异想天开地 | Excessively or unrealistically imaginative.  It suggests an imagination that goes beyond what is reasonable or practical. | 例句：He overimaginatively described the monster as a ten-headed dragon breathing fire and ice. 他异想天开地描述那怪物是一条喷火吐冰的十头龙。


例句：She overimaginatively embellished her travel stories, adding fantastical elements that never actually happened. 她过度想象地润色她的旅行故事，添加了一些从未发生过的奇幻元素。


近义词：fancifully, whimsically, fantastically


反义词：realistically, practically, soberly


词性:副词
