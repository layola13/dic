monoculturist:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. |单一文化主义者；单一栽培者 | A person who advocates or practices monoculture, either in agriculture or in society.  A monoculturist in agriculture focuses on growing a single crop, while a monoculturist in society might promote a single dominant culture, often suppressing diversity. | Example:  The farmer is a monoculturist, planting only corn on his vast fields.  这个农民是个单一栽培者，他的广阔田地里只种玉米。  |近义词：cultivator (in agricultural context), cultural homogenizer (in societal context) |反义词：multiculturalist, biodiversity advocate
