redesignating:/ˌriːˈdɪzɪɡneɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新指定；重新命名；改变用途 | The act of assigning a new designation or purpose to something.  It involves changing the existing classification, name, or function. |例句：The company is redesignating some of its office space for a new project. 公司正在为一个新项目重新指定部分办公空间。

例句：The government is redesignating the area as a national park. 政府正在将该地区重新指定为国家公园。


近义词：relabeling, reclassifying, reassigning, renaming


反义词：maintaining, preserving (the original designation)


USAGE->

They are redesignating the funds for disaster relief. = 他们正在重新分配资金用于救灾。
