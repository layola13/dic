overflog:/ˈoʊvərflɒɡ/| vt.|过度使用；滥用；过度宣传|To use or promote something excessively or to the point of exhaustion or diminishing returns.  It implies a weariness or lack of effectiveness from overuse.  |例句：The company overflogged its new product, leading to decreased consumer interest. 公司过度宣传其新产品，导致消费者兴趣下降。
The comedian overflogged the same joke throughout his act.  这位喜剧演员在整场表演中过度重复同一个笑话。|近义词：exploit, overuse, overpromote, saturate |反义词：underuse, undersell
