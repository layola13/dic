deletes:/dɪˈliːts/| v. |删除，去除|To remove (something) from a text, file, or other record.|The editor deletes unnecessary words to make the article concise. 编辑删除不必要的词语使文章简洁。 He deleted the email before anyone could see it. 他在任何人看到之前删除了邮件。|近义词：erase, remove, expunge|反义词：add, insert, include|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deletes his browsing history every day. 他每天都删除他的浏览历史。
She deletes unwanted files regularly. 她定期删除不需要的文件。

一般过去时:
I deleted the wrong file by mistake. 我错误地删除了错误的文件。
They deleted all the messages in the group chat. 他们删除了群聊中的所有消息。

现在完成时:
He has deleted all the evidence. 他已经删除了所有证据。
She has deleted her social media accounts. 她已经删除了她的社交媒体账号。

过去完成时:
I realised I had deleted the important document after restarting my computer. 我重启电脑后才意识到自己删除了重要的文件。
They discovered they had deleted the wrong files after the project deadline.  他们在项目截止日期后才发现自己删除了错误的文件。

将来时:
I will delete these files later. 我稍后会删除这些文件。
She will delete the photos after backing them up. 她备份照片后会删除它们。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it was important, I wouldn't have deleted it. 如果我知道它很重要，我就不会删除它了。
I wish I hadn't deleted those pictures. 我真希望我没有删除那些照片。

被动语态:
The file was deleted by mistake. 文件被错误地删除了。
All the emails were deleted automatically. 所有邮件都被自动删除了。

疑问句:
Have you deleted the file? 你删除了文件吗？
Did he delete the message? 他删除了消息吗？

否定句:
I didn't delete the file. 我没有删除文件。
She hasn't deleted the photos yet. 她还没有删除照片。
