outbud:/ˈaʊtˌbʌd/| n. |嫩芽；新芽|A new bud that has emerged from a plant.  An outbud is a bud that develops on the outside of a plant stem, as opposed to an inbud which develops in the axil (the angle between a leaf and stem).|例句：The apple tree produced many outbuds this spring.  这棵苹果树今年春天长出了许多嫩芽。|The rose bush is full of promising outbuds. 这棵玫瑰丛充满了很有希望的嫩芽。|近义词：bud, sprout, shoot|反义词：无
