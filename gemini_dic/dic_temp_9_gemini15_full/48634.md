defendee: /dɪˈfɛndiː/ | n. | 被告，被辩护人 | The person against whom a legal action is brought.  A defendee is the person who is being sued or accused in a legal case.  | 例句：The defendee pleaded not guilty to the charges.  被告对指控表示不认罪。  | 例句：The defendee's lawyer presented a strong defense.  被告的律师提出了强有力的辩护。 | 近义词：defendant, respondent | 反义词：plaintiff, claimant | 名词
