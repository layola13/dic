rampaging:/ræmˈpeɪdʒɪŋ/| adj.| 横冲直撞的；暴怒的；肆虐的 | wildly violent or destructive; running or behaving in a wild and uncontrolled way. |例句：The rampaging bull charged through the field. 这头横冲直撞的公牛冲过田野。

例句：The rampaging fire destroyed several homes.  肆虐的大火烧毁了几座房屋。


近义词：raging, furious, violent, destructive, uncontrolled

反义词：calm, peaceful, controlled, orderly
