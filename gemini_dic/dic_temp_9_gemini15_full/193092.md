sectionize:/ˈsɛkʃənaɪz/ | vt. | 分节；划分章节 | To divide into sections or parts.  The author sectionized the novel into chapters.  |近义词：divide, subdivide, compartmentalize | 反义词：unify, integrate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sectionizes his research paper into different parts. = 他把他的研究论文分成不同的部分。
The teacher sectionizes the class into smaller groups for activities. = 老师把班级分成较小的组进行活动。

一般过去时:
She sectionized the document for easier reading. = 她将文件分段以便于阅读。
They sectionized the land into plots for building houses. = 他们将土地划分成地块用于建造房屋。

现在完成时:
We have sectionized the project into manageable tasks. = 我们已经将项目分解成易于管理的任务。
The editor has sectionized the manuscript into chapters and subchapters. = 编辑已将手稿分成章节和小节。

过去完成时:
They had sectionized the garden before planting the flowers. = 他们在种植花卉之前已经将花园划分好了。
She had sectionized the report according to the different topics. = 她已经根据不同的主题对报告进行了分段。

将来时:
I will sectionize the presentation into three main parts. = 我将把演示文稿分成三个主要部分。
They will sectionize the data for better analysis. = 他们将对数据进行分段以进行更好的分析。

虚拟语气:
If I were to write a book, I would sectionize it carefully. = 如果我要写一本书，我会仔细地把它分成章节。
It would be helpful if they sectionized the instructions more clearly. = 如果他们能更清晰地分段说明，那就更有帮助了。

被动语态:

The map is sectionized by region. = 地图按区域划分。

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not sectionize the essay effectively. = 他没有有效地对文章进行分段。
The document was not sectionized properly. = 文件没有被正确地分段。
