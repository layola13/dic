condolement:/kənˈdəʊlmənt/ | n. |慰问；吊唁 | An expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death.  |例句：Please accept my deepest condolement on the loss of your mother. 请接受我对您母亲去世的深切慰问。|例句：She offered her condolements to the bereaved family. 她向丧家表示慰问。|近义词：sympathy, compassion, commiseration |反义词：celebration, rejoicing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My deepest condolement is with you. = 我向你致以最深切的慰问。
We offer our condolements to the family. = 我们向这个家庭表示慰问。


一般过去时:
I offered my condolements to her after her father's death. = 她的父亲去世后，我向她表示慰问。
They expressed their condolements to the victim's family. = 他们向受害者家属表示慰问。


现在完成时:
I have expressed my condolements to them already. = 我已经向他们表达了慰问。
She has sent her condolements in a letter. = 她已在信中表达了慰问。


过去完成时:
I had already sent my condolements before the funeral. = 在葬礼之前，我已经表达了我的慰问。
They had expressed their condolements before the service began. = 在仪式开始之前，他们已经表达了慰问。


将来时:
I will send my condolements to the family tomorrow. = 我明天将向家人表示慰问。
We will offer our condolements at the memorial service. = 我们将在追悼会上表示慰问。


虚拟语气:
If I knew their address, I would send my condolements. = 如果我知道他们的地址，我会送上我的慰问。
I wish I could have expressed my condolements sooner. = 我希望我能早点表达我的慰问。


被动语态:
Condolements were offered by many people. = 许多人都表达了慰问。
Their condolements were deeply appreciated. = 他们的慰问深受感激。


疑问句:
Have you sent your condolements yet? = 你已经表达慰问了吗？
Did you offer your condolements to the family? = 你向家人表示慰问了吗？


否定句:

She didn't offer her condolements publicly. = 她没有公开表达她的慰问。
