round-robins:/ˈraʊnd ˈrɒbɪnz/ | n. |循环赛；循环赛制|A round-robin tournament or competition is one in which each participant plays every other participant once.  This ensures fairness and equal opportunity for all competitors. |Example: The tennis club organized a round-robin tournament for its members. 网球俱乐部为会员组织了一场循环赛。

The school held a round-robin debate competition. 学校举办了一场循环赛辩论赛。|近义词：tournament, competition, all-play-all |反义词：single-elimination tournament, knockout
