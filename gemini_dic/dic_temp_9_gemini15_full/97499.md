ingestion:/ɪnˈdʒɛstʃən/| n. |摄入；吞咽|The act of taking in something, especially food, drink, or drugs, into the body.|例句：The ingestion of alcohol can lead to liver damage. 摄入酒精会导致肝损伤。
例句：Accidental ingestion of poison requires immediate medical attention.意外摄入毒药需要立即就医。|近义词：consumption, swallowing, absorption|反义词：excretion, expulsion
