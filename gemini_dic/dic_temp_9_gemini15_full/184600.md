restowing:/riːˈstoʊɪŋ/| v.|重新装载，重新存放|The act of putting something back into its place or container, often after it has been moved or unloaded.  It implies a careful and organized process of rearrangement.|例句：The crew spent the afternoon restowing the cargo after the storm. 船员们花了整个下午重新装载暴风雨过后散落的货物。

例句：After the inventory, we need to restow the supplies in the warehouse. 盘点之后，我们需要重新存放仓库里的物资。


近义词：repacking, reloading, rearranging, restocking|反义词：unloading, removing, discarding|动词
