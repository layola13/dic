imbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/| vt.|吸收；饮用；接受（思想、观点等）|to drink (especially alcohol), to absorb or soak up, to receive and accept (information, ideas, etc.)|例句：He imbibed large quantities of beer. 他喝了很多啤酒。

例句：She imbibed the wisdom of her elders. 她汲取了长者的智慧。

近义词：drink, absorb, assimilate, soak up|反义词：reject, expel, refuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imbibes a glass of wine every evening. = 他每天晚上喝一杯葡萄酒。
She imbibes knowledge from various sources. = 她从各种来源汲取知识。

一般过去时:
They imbibed deeply of the local culture. = 他们深深地沉浸在当地文化中。
I imbibed a refreshing drink after the workout. = 锻炼后我喝了一杯清爽的饮料。

现在完成时:
He has imbibed too much alcohol tonight. = 他今晚喝了太多的酒。
She has imbibed the principles of non-violence. = 她已经吸收了非暴力的原则。

过去完成时:
They had imbibed several glasses of champagne before the speech. = 在演讲之前，他们已经喝了几杯香槟。
Before the presentation, she had imbibed all the relevant materials. = 在演示之前，她已经吸收了所有相关的资料。

将来时:
I will imbibe the beauty of the scenery tomorrow. = 我明天将陶醉于风景的美丽之中。
We will imbibe copious amounts of water after the hike. = 徒步旅行后，我们将大量饮水。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would imbibe less caffeine. = 如果我是你，我会少喝点咖啡因。
I wish I had imbibed more information before the meeting. = 我希望我在会议前能吸收更多信息。

被动语态:
The information was imbibed readily by the students. = 学生们很容易就吸收了这些信息。
The old building imbibed the dampness of the surrounding atmosphere. = 这座古老的建筑吸收了周围环境的潮湿。

疑问句:

Will they imbibe the new rules effectively? = 他们会有效地吸收新规则吗？

否定句:
He did not imbibe any alcohol last night. = 他昨晚没有喝酒。
She does not imbibe easily from lectures. = 她不容易从讲座中吸收知识。
