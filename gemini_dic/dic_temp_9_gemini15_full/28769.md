carnivorous:/kɑːrˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食肉的；肉食性的 | Relating to or characteristic of carnivores; feeding on flesh.  | 例句：Lions are carnivorous animals.  狮子是食肉动物。  |  例句：The plant has carnivorous tendencies, trapping insects in its sticky leaves. 这植物有食肉的倾向，用粘乎乎的叶子捕捉昆虫。 | 近义词：predatory, flesh-eating | 反义词：herbivorous, vegetarian

词源:
The word "carnivorous" comes from the Latin words "caro" (flesh) and "vorare" (to devour or eat).  Therefore, it literally means "flesh-eating".
