repealer:/rɪˈpiːlər/| n.|废除者；废除法案|A person or thing that repeals something, especially a law.  It can also refer to legislation that repeals a previous law.|The government introduced a repealer to overturn the unpopular tax.  政府推出了一项废除法案以推翻不受欢迎的税收。|近义词：repealing act, abrogator|反义词：enactor, legislator
