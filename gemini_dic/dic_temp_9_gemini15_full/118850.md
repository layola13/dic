metaphrastic:/ˌmɛtəˈfræstɪk/| adj.|  释义性翻译的；意译的 |  Relating to or characterized by metaphrase, a type of translation that prioritizes literal rendering over meaning and style.  It aims to convey the exact words of the source text, even if it results in an awkward or unnatural translation in the target language.  | Example:  The metaphrastic translation, while accurate word-for-word, lacked the fluency of the original text.  | 近义词：literal, verbatim | 反义词：paraphrastic, free
