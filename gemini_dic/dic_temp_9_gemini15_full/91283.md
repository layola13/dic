hydroguret:/ˈhaɪdrəɡjuːrɛt/ | n. |氢化物 | A hydroguret is a compound containing hydrogen and another element, typically a metal, where the hydrogen acts as a negatively charged ion (hydride).  It often refers to metal hydrides.  The term is less common than "hydride". | Example:  Lithium hydride is an example of a hydroguret.  锂氢化物就是一个氢化物的例子。 | 近义词：hydride | 反义词：N/A
