contrapuntist:/ˌkɒntrəˈpʌnɪst/| n. |复调作曲家；对位法专家|A contrapuntist is a composer or musician skilled in counterpoint, a compositional technique where two or more independent melodic lines are combined.  They understand and can create complex musical textures through the interplay of these melodies. |Example: Bach was a master contrapuntist, renowned for his intricate and beautiful counterpoint.  巴赫是一位复调大师，以其复杂而优美的对位法而闻名。|近义词：counterpoint composer, polyphonist |反义词：monophonist
