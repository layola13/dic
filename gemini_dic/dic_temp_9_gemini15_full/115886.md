marmalades:/ˈmɑːrməleɪdz/| n.|橘子酱，果酱|A marmalade is a preserve made from citrus fruits, typically oranges, lemons, or grapefruits, along with sugar and water. It usually contains pieces of the fruit peel.|例句：We had marmalade on toast for breakfast.  我们早餐吃了涂了橘子酱的面包。|例句：She makes her own marmalade every year. 她每年都自己做橘子酱。|近义词：jam, preserve, conserve|反义词:None
