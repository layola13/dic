remediate:/rɪˈmiːdieɪt/| vt.|修复；改进；补救；矫正|To correct or improve something that is wrong or deficient.|例句：The company is working to remediate the environmental damage caused by the spill.  公司正在努力修复泄漏造成的环境破坏。|例句：The teacher remediated the student's misunderstanding of the concept. 老师纠正了学生对这个概念的误解。|近义词：rectify, correct, repair, remedy|反义词：worsen, damage, deteriorate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government remediates contaminated sites. = 政府修复受污染的场地。
They remediate the problem quickly. = 他们迅速解决了这个问题。

一般过去时:
The team remediated the software bug. = 团队修复了软件错误。
We remediated the situation with a new plan. = 我们用一个新计划来补救局面。

现在完成时:
They have remediated the damage caused by the storm. = 他们已经修复了风暴造成的损害。
The company has remediated its environmental violations. = 公司已经纠正了其环境违规行为。

过去完成时:
They had remediated the leaks before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经修复了泄漏。
The engineers had remediated the structural problems before the building opened. = 工程师在建筑物开放之前已经修复了结构问题。

将来时:
The contractor will remediate the asbestos in the building. = 承包商将修复建筑物中的石棉。
We will remediate the situation as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快解决这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If we had remediated the issue earlier, the damage would have been less. = 如果我们早点解决这个问题，损害就会小一些。
It is essential that the school remediate the failing students. = 学校必须对不及格的学生进行补救教学至关重要。

被动语态:
The contaminated soil is being remediated. = 受污染的土壤正在被修复。
The problem will be remediated by the end of the week. = 这个问题将在周末前得到解决。

疑问句:
Will the damage be remediated? = 损害会被修复吗？
How will they remediate the situation? = 他们将如何解决这个问题？

否定句:
They did not remediate the problem effectively. = 他们没有有效地解决这个问题。
The company failed to remediate the environmental damage. = 公司未能修复环境破坏。
