lilt:/lɪlt/| n. v.|轻快活泼的曲调；轻快地走动；轻快地吟诵|A characteristic rise and fall of the voice in speaking; a light, springy gait; to move or proceed with a light, springy gait.|例句：Her voice had a pleasant lilt.  她的声音轻快悦耳。 The children lilted down the lane. 孩子们轻快地沿着小路走下去了。|近义词：trill, warble, carol, skip, bounce|反义词：drag, trudge, slump
