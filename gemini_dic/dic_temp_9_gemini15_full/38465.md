concetto:/konˈtʃɛtoʊ/| n. |概念；意念；构思|A concetto is a highly intellectualized and often artificial poetic conceit, typically characterized by ingenious and surprising imagery and metaphors, often based on wordplay or paradox. It's a concept or idea, often complex and abstract, that forms the basis of a work of art or literature.  It also refers to a musical idea, especially a theme or motif.|The concerto's central concetto was the contrast between light and darkness.  这首协奏曲的核心概念是光明与黑暗的对比。|concept, idea, notion|concrete, simple|名词
