orbiter:/ˈɔːrbɪtə(r)/| n. |轨道器，绕行器|A spacecraft designed to orbit a planet or other celestial body.  It typically carries scientific instruments to study the object it orbits. |例句：The Mars orbiter sent back stunning images of the Martian surface.  火星轨道器发回了火星表面的惊人图像。

例句：The Hubble Space Telescope is a type of orbiter that has revolutionized our understanding of the universe. 哈勃太空望远镜是一种轨道器，它彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。|近义词：satellite, spacecraft|反义词：lander (着陆器)|名词
