choicest: /ˈtʃɔɪsɪst/ | adj. | 最好的；最精选的；最上品的 |  The word "choicest" is the superlative form of the adjective "choice," meaning of the highest quality or most desirable. It implies a selection from among many options, with only the very best being chosen. It often describes things that are rare, exquisite, or of superior quality. | 例句：The chef used only the choicest ingredients in his famous dish.  厨师在他的名菜中只使用了最优质的食材。

The restaurant offers a selection of choicest wines. 这家餐厅提供精选的优质葡萄酒。|近义词：best, finest, prime, superior, excellent | 反义词：worst, poorest, inferior
