listable:/ˈlɪstəbəl/| adj.| 可列出的，可编入目录的 | Able to be listed or included in a list.  Something that can be easily organized into a list. |例句：The items on the menu were all listable. 菜单上的所有项目都是可以列出的。

例句：The students' names are listable alphabetically. 学生的名字可以按字母顺序排列。
