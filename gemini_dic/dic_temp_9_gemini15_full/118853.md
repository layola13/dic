It often implies something mysterious or beyond ordinary understanding. | Example:  The painting has a metaphysical quality, evoking a sense of mystery and the unknown. 这幅画有一种形而上学的气质，唤起了一种神秘和未知的感觉。 |近义词：transcendental, supernatural, abstract |反义词：physical, concrete, material
