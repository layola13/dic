realists: /ˈriːəlɪsts/ | n. |现实主义者，现实主义画家|People who accept the world as it is, without illusions or idealism; artists who depict subjects as they appear in reality. |例句：Many realists believe that utopian ideals are unrealistic.  许多现实主义者认为乌托邦式的理想是不现实的。|例句：The painting is a fine example of realist art. 这幅画是现实主义艺术的优秀范例。|近义词：pragmatists, realists, pragmatists |反义词：idealists, romantics
