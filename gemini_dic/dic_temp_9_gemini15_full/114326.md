maisonnettes:/ˌmeɪzənˈɛts/| n. |复式住宅，小公寓|A maisonette is a dwelling over two or more stories, typically forming part of a larger building, but having its own entrance and often a small garden.  It's essentially a smaller, less grand version of a townhouse. |例句：They live in a charming maisonette in the heart of the city.  他们住在市中心一间迷人的复式住宅里。|例句：The maisonette offered stunning views of the park. 这间复式住宅可以欣赏到公园的迷人景色。|近义词：duplex, apartment, flat|反义词：single-story dwelling, detached house|名词

词源:
