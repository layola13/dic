deskmate:/ˈdɛskmeɪt/| n. |同桌，课桌伙伴 |A deskmate is someone who sits at the same desk as you, usually in a classroom.  |Example: I've been deskmates with Sarah for the whole semester.  我的同桌莎拉已经整个学期了。|近义词： classmate, seatmate |反义词：None specifically.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My deskmate always helps me with my homework. = 我的同桌总是帮我做作业。
He is a kind deskmate and shares his stationery with me. = 他是一位友善的同桌，并与我分享文具。


一般过去时:
My deskmate lent me his pen yesterday. = 我的同桌昨天借给了我他的钢笔。
Last year, my deskmate and I worked on a project together. = 去年，我的同桌和我一起完成了一个项目。


现在完成时:
I have known my deskmate for three years. = 我认识我的同桌三年了。
My deskmate has helped me a lot this semester. = 这个学期我的同桌帮了我很多。


过去完成时:
Before the exam, my deskmate had already finished reviewing. = 考试前，我的同桌已经复习完了。
I had never met my deskmate before the first day of class. = 在开学第一天之前，我从未见过我的同桌。


将来时:
I will ask my deskmate for help with this problem. = 我会向我的同桌寻求帮助解决这个问题。
My deskmate will likely be in the same class next semester. = 下学期我的同桌很可能在同一个班。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better deskmate, I would be less stressed. = 如果我有一个更好的同桌，我会少一些压力。
I wish my deskmate were more organized. = 我希望我的同桌更整洁一些。


被动语态:
The desk was shared by two deskmates. = 这张课桌由两个同桌共用。
The problem was solved with the help of my deskmate. = 在我同桌的帮助下，这个问题得到了解决。


疑问句:
Who is your deskmate? = 你的同桌是谁？
Do you get along with your deskmate? = 你和你同桌相处得好吗？


否定句:
My deskmate didn't come to school today. = 我的同桌今天没来上学。
I don't know my new deskmate's name yet. = 我还不知道我新同桌的名字。


PHRASE->
be deskmates with sb. = 与某人为同桌

change deskmates = 换同桌
have a deskmate = 有一个同桌
become deskmates = 成为同桌
