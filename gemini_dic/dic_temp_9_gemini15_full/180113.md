refluent:/ˈrɛfluənt/| adj.|倒流的；回流的；逆流的|Flowing back; returning.  Refluent describes something that flows backward or returns to its source. This often applies to liquids, but can also be used metaphorically.|The refluent tide carried debris back out to sea.  退潮把残骸带回了大海。

The refluent current made swimming difficult. 逆流使游泳变得困难。|backflowing, returning, reverting|effluent, outgoing, advancing
