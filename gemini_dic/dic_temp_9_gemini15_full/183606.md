requital:/rɪˈkwaɪtəl/| n. |报答；报复；酬谢 |The act of making a return; repayment; reciprocation.  It can refer to a reward for a service or kindness, or to revenge or punishment for a wrong. |例句：His kindness received its requital in the form of a heartfelt thank you. 他的善举得到了衷心的感谢作为回报。

例句：The swift requital of his treachery was a warning to others.  对他背叛行为的迅速报复是对其他人的警告。


近义词：repayment, recompense, retaliation, revenge |反义词：neglect, disregard

词性：名词
