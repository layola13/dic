raspingly:/ˈræspɪŋli/| adv. | 刺耳地，沙哑地 |  Describes a sound that is harsh and grating, like the sound of a rasp.  It can also describe a voice that is rough and unpleasant. | The old man spoke raspingly, his voice barely a whisper. 老人的声音沙哑刺耳，几乎像耳语。 The rusty hinges raspingly protested as the door creaked open. 生锈的铰链发出刺耳的吱吱声，门吱呀一声开了。| gratingly, harshly, roughly | smoothly, softly |
