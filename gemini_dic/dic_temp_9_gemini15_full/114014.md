magmatically:/mæɡˈmætɪkli/| adv.|岩浆地质学地；岩浆作用地| Formed or produced by magmatic processes.  The term refers to geological processes related to magma (molten rock within the Earth).|例句：The islands were formed magmatically, through volcanic eruptions.  这些岛屿是通过火山喷发，以岩浆作用的方式形成的。|近义词：igneously, volcanically|反义词：sedimentarily, metamorphically
