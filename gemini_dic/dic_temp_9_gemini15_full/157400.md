pipsqueak:/ˈpɪpskwik/| n. |小人物，无足轻重的人，瘦小的人|A pipsqueak is a small, insignificant person, often a child or someone who is considered weak or unimportant.  The term often carries a slightly contemptuous or humorous tone. |例句：He's just a pipsqueak; don't pay him any attention. 他只不过是个小人物；别理他。

例句：Don't let that pipsqueak bully you. 别让那个小不点欺负你。


近义词：weakling, shrimp, runt, insignificant person, nobody

反义词：giant, heavyweight, big shot, important person

词性：名词
