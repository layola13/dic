overburn:/ˈoʊvərˈbɜːrn/| vt.|过烧；烧坏|To burn excessively or beyond a safe or intended point.  This often refers to burning something for too long, at too high a temperature, or with insufficient ventilation, leading to damage or destruction.  It can also refer to overworking or overtaxing something, causing damage.|例句：The cake was overburned in the oven.  烤箱里的蛋糕烤焦了。

The engine overburned due to lack of lubrication.  由于缺乏润滑，发动机烧坏了。|近义词：scorch, char, incinerate, overheat|反义词：undercook, underheat
