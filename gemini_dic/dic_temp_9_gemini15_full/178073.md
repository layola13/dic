reclimbs:/ˌriːˈklaɪmz/| v.|重新攀登，再爬|To climb again; to climb up again after having descended.|例句：After resting, he reclimbed the mountain. 休息后，他又重新攀登了那座山。
例句：The climber reclimbed the treacherous slope.攀登者再次攀登了险峻的山坡。|近义词：reascend, reascend|反义词：descend,  go down|动词
