reexported:/ˌriːˈɛkspɔːtɪd/ | adj. | 再出口的；转口输出的 |  Having been exported from one country and then exported again from another.  This refers to goods that have already been imported into a country and are then exported to a third country. | Example:  The re-exported goods were subject to different tariffs. 再出口商品需缴纳不同的关税。  |近义词: re-exported, transshipped | 反义词: imported, domestically produced | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reexports goods to many countries. = 这家公司向许多国家再出口商品。
Many countries reexport products from China. = 许多国家再出口中国产品。


一般过去时:
They reexported the textiles to Europe last year. = 他们去年将纺织品再出口到欧洲。
The government reexported the surplus grain to neighboring countries. = 政府将剩余的粮食再出口到邻国。


现在完成时:
We have reexported a significant quantity of goods this year. = 今年我们已经再出口了大量的货物。
Several firms have reexported the components to other Asian markets. = 几家公司已将这些零部件再出口到其他亚洲市场。


过去完成时:
Before the new regulations, they had reexported the goods without paying duty. = 在新规出台之前，他们未缴税就将货物再出口了。
The company had reexported most of its products before the trade war started. = 在贸易战开始之前，该公司已经再出口了大部分产品。


将来时:
They will reexport the products once they receive the necessary permits. = 他们一旦获得必要的许可证，就会再出口这些产品。
The trader will reexport the goods through a different port next time. = 商人下次将通过不同的港口再出口货物。


虚拟语气:
If they had reexported the goods earlier, they would have made more profit. = 如果他们早点再出口货物，就会赚更多钱。
If we reexported these products, we might increase our market share. = 如果我们再出口这些产品，可能会增加我们的市场份额。


被动语态:
The goods were reexported through a third country. = 货物通过第三国再出口。
A large quantity of tea was reexported to the UK last month. = 上个月大量茶叶再出口到英国。


疑问句:
Were the goods reexported legally? = 这些货物是否合法再出口？
Will the company be reexporting the products next quarter? = 公司下个季度会再出口这些产品吗？


否定句:
The company did not reexport the defective products. = 公司没有再出口有缺陷的产品。
They haven't reexported anything since the new tariffs were imposed. = 自从新的关税实施以来，他们什么也没有再出口。
