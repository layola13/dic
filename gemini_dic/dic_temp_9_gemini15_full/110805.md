linkmen: /ˈlɪŋkmæn/ | n. |联络员，联络人 | A person who acts as a connecting link between individuals or groups.  A linkman facilitates communication and coordination.  | 例句：The linkman kept us informed of the enemy's movements. 联络员不断地向我们通报敌人的动向。


例句：He served as a vital linkman between the two negotiating teams. 他担任两个谈判小组之间至关重要的联络员。


近义词：liaison, intermediary, contact, go-between


反义词：separator, divider


词性: 名词
