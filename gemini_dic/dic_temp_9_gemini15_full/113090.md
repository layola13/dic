lunation:/luːˈneɪʃən/| n. |月相变化周期；朔望月|A lunation is the period of time between one new moon and the next, approximately 29.5 days.  It is also known as a synodic month.  The term is often used in astronomy and astrology.|The lunar cycle is roughly a lunation.|近义词：synodic month|反义词：无
