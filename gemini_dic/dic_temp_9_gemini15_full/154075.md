pericarditis:/ˌpɛrɪkɑːrˈdaɪtɪs/| n. |心包炎|Inflammation of the pericardium, the sac-like membrane surrounding the heart.  Symptoms can include chest pain, shortness of breath, and fever.  It can be caused by infections, autoimmune diseases, or injury.| The patient was diagnosed with pericarditis after experiencing severe chest pain.|近义词：pericardial inflammation|反义词：无
