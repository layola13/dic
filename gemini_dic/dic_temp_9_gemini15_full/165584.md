prememoranda:/ˌpriːˌmeməˈrændə/ | n. |备忘录（复数）；预备备忘录 | Preliminary memoranda; notes or records made in preparation for a more formal document.  Prememoranda are often used to outline key points, gather information, or brainstorm ideas before drafting a final report, agreement, or other official document.  They are not usually intended for distribution outside of the drafting team. | Example: The team spent a week preparing prememoranda before drafting the final contract.  团队在起草最终合同之前花了一周时间准备备忘录。


近义词: preliminary notes, draft notes, preparatory memoranda, working papers

反义词: final document, finished report


词性:名词
