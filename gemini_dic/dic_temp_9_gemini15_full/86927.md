hemorrhaging:/ˈhɛməˌreɪdʒɪŋ/| v. | 大出血；大量流血；迅速丧失（支持、资金等）| The process of excessive bleeding.  It can refer to the escape of blood from a ruptured blood vessel, or figuratively, a rapid loss of something valuable, such as support or funds. |例句：The accident victim was hemorrhaging profusely.  这名意外事故受害者正在大量出血。

The company is hemorrhaging money due to poor management. 这家公司由于管理不善而正在迅速亏损。|近义词：bleeding, losing (in a figurative sense)|反义词：coagulation, stemming the flow
