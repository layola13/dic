quadrangularness:/kwɒdrˈæŋɡjʊlərnəs/ | n. |四方形；四边形；四角形 | The state or quality of being quadrangular; having four angles and four sides.  It often implies a certain regularity or symmetry, though not necessarily perfect geometrical precision.  | The quadrangularness of the building was striking, with its perfectly symmetrical facade. 建筑物的四方形外观非常引人注目，其正面完全对称。 The lack of quadrangularness in the design made the building look awkward.  设计缺乏四方形的特性，使建筑看起来很笨拙。 | 近义词：four-sidedness, squareness | 反义词：roundness, circularity
