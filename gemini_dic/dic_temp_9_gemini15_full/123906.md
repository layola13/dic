mordor:/ˈmɔːrdɔːr/| n. |魔多（地名）|The dark land of Mordor, in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy world of Middle-earth, is a region of fire, shadow, and evil, ruled by the Dark Lord Sauron. It is a symbol of darkness, evil, and despair.  |Example:  The armies of Mordor marched towards Gondor. 魔多的军队向刚铎进军。  The Ring of Power was forged in the fires of Mount Doom in Mordor.  权力之戒是在魔多末日火山之火中铸造的。|近义词：hell, abyss, underworld |反义词：paradise, heaven, utopia
