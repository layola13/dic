law-making:/ˈlɔːˌmeɪkɪŋ/| n.|立法；法律制定|The process of creating laws.  It involves various stages, from drafting legislation to its final enactment and implementation.  This process often includes public debate, review by legislative bodies, and executive approval.|例句：The law-making process in the United States is complex and often slow. 美国立法过程复杂且缓慢。|例句：Efficient law-making is crucial for a well-functioning society.  高效的立法对于一个运转良好的社会至关重要。|近义词：legislation, lawmaking, statute-making|反义词：lawbreaking, lawlessness|名词
