satanist:/ˈseɪtənɪst/| n. |撒旦崇拜者|A satanist is a person who worships Satan or practices Satanism.  Satanists believe in Satan as a powerful entity and often engage in rituals and practices associated with Satanic beliefs.  The term encompasses a wide range of beliefs and practices, from theistic Satanism, which involves the worship of Satan, to atheistic Satanism, which uses Satan as a symbol of rebellion and individualism.  The popular image of satanists is often distorted and sensationalized in media portrayals. |Example:  He was falsely accused of being a satanist. 他被错误地指控为撒旦崇拜者。

The accusations that he was a satanist were completely unfounded.  关于他是撒旦崇拜者的指控完全没有根据。|近义词：devil worshiper, Luciferian (in some contexts)|反义词：devotee of God, theist (in a religious context)
