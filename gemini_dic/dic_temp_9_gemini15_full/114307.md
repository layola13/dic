maintain:/meɪnˈteɪn/| vt. |保持；维持；维修；坚持；供养|To continue to have; to keep in existence, or not allow to become less; to keep in good condition by regularly making repairs; to assert or argue strongly in favour of something.|例句：We must maintain a positive attitude. 我们必须保持积极的态度。|例句：The building needs maintaining. 这座建筑需要维修。|近义词：preserve, uphold, sustain, support |反义词：neglect, abandon, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains a high standard of work. = 他保持着很高的工作标准。
They maintain a large garden. = 他们维护着一个大花园。

一般过去时:
The company maintained its profits despite the recession. = 尽管经济衰退，该公司仍保持了利润。
She maintained her innocence throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，她都坚持自己的清白。

现在完成时:
We have maintained this policy for many years. = 多年来我们一直坚持这项政策。
They have maintained the house in excellent condition. = 他们一直把房子维护得很好。

过去完成时:
He had maintained his composure even under pressure. = 即使在压力下，他也保持了镇静。
She had maintained a diary since she was a child. = 她从孩提时代就一直坚持写日记。

将来时:
The government will maintain its commitment to education. = 政府将继续履行其对教育的承诺。
We will maintain regular contact. = 我们将保持定期联系。

虚拟语气:
If we maintained stricter rules, there would be less disorder. = 如果我们执行更严格的规章制度，就会减少混乱。
I wish I had maintained a healthier lifestyle. = 我希望我以前能保持更健康的生活方式。

被动语态:
The equipment is maintained regularly. = 设备定期维护。
The peace was maintained by the intervention of the United Nations. = 和平是由联合国的干预维持的。

疑问句:
How do you maintain your weight? = 你如何保持你的体重？
Can we maintain this level of production? = 我们能维持这个生产水平吗？

否定句:
The government failed to maintain order. = 政府未能维持秩序。
He couldn't maintain his balance and fell. = 他无法保持平衡，摔倒了。
