reordains:/ˌriːɔːrˈdeɪnz/| vt.|重新任命为圣职人员；再次授予圣职|To ordain someone again, especially a priest or minister.|The bishop reordained the priest after the scandal had been resolved. 主教在丑闻解决后重新任命了这位牧师。|近义词：reappoint, reinstate|反义词：defrock, unordain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reordains priests every year. 他每年都重新任命牧师。
The church reordains deserving candidates. 教堂重新任命合格的候选人。


一般过去时:
The bishop reordained him after the investigation. 主教在调查后重新任命了他。
They reordained the minister following his reinstatement.  他们在牧师恢复职位后重新任命了他。


现在完成时:
The church has reordained several priests this decade. 教堂在本十年中重新任命了几位牧师。
He has reordained many times throughout his career. 他在他的职业生涯中多次重新被任命。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal, they had reordained him once. 在丑闻之前，他们已经重新任命过他一次。
The church had reordained him before the new rules were implemented. 在新规实施之前，教堂已经重新任命了他。


将来时:
