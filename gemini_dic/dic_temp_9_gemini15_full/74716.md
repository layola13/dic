freemasons:/ˈfriːmeɪsən/| n. |共济会会员；共济会员|Members of the Freemasons, a fraternal organization with a complex system of rituals, symbols, and degrees of membership.  Freemasons trace their origins back to medieval stonemasons' guilds, but the modern organization has evolved significantly.  They emphasize brotherly love, relief, and truth.  However, the organization is surrounded by secrecy and conspiracy theories. |例句：He's a Freemason and a respected member of the community. 他是共济会会员，也是社区里一位受人尊敬的成员。|例句：Many myths and legends surround the Freemasons and their secret rituals. 许多神话和传说围绕着共济会及其秘密仪式。|近义词：Mason (informal)|反义词：无
