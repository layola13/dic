junipers:/ˈdʒuːnɪpərz/| n. |杜松属植物；杜松树| Evergreen shrubs or trees of the genus Juniperus,  characterized by needle-like or scale-like leaves and berry-like cones.  They are known for their aromatic wood and berries, often used in gin and other products.|例句：The hillside was covered with junipers, their bluish-green foliage contrasting with the brown earth.|近义词：cedar, pine (in some contexts)|反义词：无
