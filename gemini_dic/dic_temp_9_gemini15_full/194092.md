self-image:/ˈsɛlf ɪmɪdʒ/| n. |自我形象，自我认知|The way a person sees and thinks about themselves, including their appearance, abilities, and personality.  It's a subjective perception that can be influenced by various factors. |例句：He has a positive self-image and believes in his abilities. 他拥有积极的自我形象，并相信自己的能力。

例句：Her self-image was damaged after the accident. 事故之后她的自我形象受到了损害。


近义词：self-perception, self-concept, self-esteem (partially), body image (related but more specific)

反义词：None directly opposite, but a severely negative self-image could be contrasted with a healthy self-image.  


词性:名词
