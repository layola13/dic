preserver:/prɪˈzɜːrvər/| n. |保护者；保存者；腌渍人|A preserver is a person or thing that protects something from harm or decay.  It can also refer to someone who preserves food, typically through methods like pickling or canning.|例句：The museum curator is a preserver of history.  博物馆馆长是历史的守护者。|例句：He's a skilled preserver of jams and jellies. 他是一位熟练的果酱和果冻制作人。|近义词：protector, guardian, conserver |反义词：destroyer, vandal, spoiler
