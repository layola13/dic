dearling:/ˈdɪrlɪŋ/| n. |亲爱的，宝贝儿 |A term of endearment, often used to address a loved one.  It expresses affection and intimacy. |例句：My darling, I love you so much. 亲爱的，我非常爱你。|例句：He called his wife "my darling" during the phone call. 他在电话里称他的妻子为“我的亲爱的”。|近义词：sweetheart, darling, honey, love |反义词：N/A
