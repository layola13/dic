/ˈtiːtʃər/| n. |老师，教师 |A teacher is a person who teaches, especially in a school.  教师是一个教书的人，尤其是在学校里。|  My teacher is very kind. 我的老师非常善良。 |instructor, educator, tutor |student|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The teacher teaches English. = 老师教英语。
Teachers often give homework. =老师经常布置作业。


一般过去时:
The teacher taught us history yesterday. = 老师昨天教我们历史。
She taught at this school for ten years. = 她在这所学校教了十年书。


现在完成时:
I have had many good teachers. = 我有很多好老师。
She has taught here since 2000. = 自2000年以来，她一直在這裡任教。


过去完成时:
The students had already finished their homework before the teacher arrived. = 在老师到达之前，学生们已经完成了作业。
He had taught at several schools before he came here. = 他来这里之前曾在几所学校教过书。


将来时:
She will teach a new class next semester. = 下学期她将教一个新班级。
They will be teaching online next week. = 他们下周将进行在线教学。


虚拟语气:
If I were a teacher, I would be more patient. = 如果我是老师，我会更有耐心。
I wish I had been a better teacher. = 我希望我以前能成为一名更好的老师。


被动语态:
The class was taught by a guest lecturer. = 这堂课是由一位客座讲师讲授的。
The lesson is being taught using interactive methods. = 这节课正在使用互动式教学方法进行教学。


疑问句:
Is your teacher strict? = 你的老师严格吗？
What subjects does your teacher teach? = 你的老师教什么科目？


否定句:
He is not a good teacher. = 他不是一个好老师。
She didn't teach math last year. = 去年她没有教数学。
