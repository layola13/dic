hed:/hed/| n.| 标题；头；头版新闻 | a headline; the head of something;  a prominent news story on the front page of a newspaper. |例句：The hed of the article was "Local Woman Wins Lottery".  这篇文章的标题是“当地女子赢得彩票”。|例句：The newspaper's hed focused on the political scandal. 报纸的头版新闻关注的是政治丑闻。|近义词：heading, title, headline |反义词：body (of text), conclusion
