词源:
The word "renaissance" comes from the Old French word *renaissance*, which itself comes from the Latin verb *renasci*, meaning "to be reborn" or "to be revived."  The prefix "re-" indicates again, and "nasci" is related to "nascor" (to be born). Therefore, the word literally means "rebirth."
