roi:/ˌɑːrˈoʊaɪ/ | n. |投资回报率 |Return on investment; a performance measure used to evaluate the efficiency or profitability of an investment or compare the efficiency of a number of different investments.  It is computed by dividing the net profit (or loss) after taxes from an investment by the amount invested. |Example:  The company's ROI on the new marketing campaign was impressive. 这家公司新营销活动的投资回报率令人印象深刻。|近义词：return, yield, profitability |反义词：loss, deficit
