foreknow:/fɔːrˈnoʊ/| vt. |预知，预见|To know beforehand; to have knowledge of something before it happens.  It implies a supernatural or insightful understanding, rather than simply a prediction based on evidence.|例句：The prophet foreknew the coming of the Messiah. 先知预知了弥赛亚的到来。

例句：She somehow foreknew that her flight would be delayed. 她不知何故预知到她的航班会延误。


近义词：predict, foresee, anticipate, previse

反义词：misjudge, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foreknows the future. 他预知未来。
They foreknow the outcome of the election. 他们预知了选举的结果。


一般过去时:
The oracle foreknew the king's death. 神谕预知了国王的死。
She foreknew her friend's betrayal. 她预知了朋友的背叛。


现在完成时:
Have you foreknown this disaster? 你预知过这场灾难吗？
They have foreknown the consequences of their actions. 他们预知了他们行为的后果。


过去完成时:
The witch had foreknown the prince's fate. 女巫预知了王子的命运。
He had foreknown the storm's approach. 他预知了风暴的来临。


将来时:
I will foreknow the result of the experiment. 我将预知实验的结果。
They will foreknow the winner of the race. 他们将预知比赛的获胜者。(Note:  Future tense usage is less common, as foreknowing implies a present or prior knowledge.)


虚拟语气:
If I had foreknown the danger, I would have stayed home. 如果我预知了危险，我就会待在家里。
I wish I had foreknown the outcome. 我真希望我预知了结果。


被动语态:
The future cannot be foreknown. 未来无法被预知。
The consequences were not foreknown. 后果没有被预知。


疑问句:
Did she foreknow the earthquake? 她预知了地震吗？
Could he have foreknown the accident? 他能预知那场事故吗？


否定句:
I did not foreknow the difficulty. 我没有预知到困难。
They could not have foreknown the consequences. 他们不可能预知到后果。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "foreknow".  The word itself is relatively uncommon in modern English.
