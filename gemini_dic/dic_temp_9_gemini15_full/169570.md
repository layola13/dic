prophesied:/ˈprɒfəsaɪzd/| /ˈprɑːfəsaɪzd/| v.| 预言，预示 | To have prophesied something means to have made a prediction or a statement about what will happen in the future, often based on supernatural or divine insight.  |The fortune teller prophesied a long and happy life for her.  算命先生预言她将拥有漫长而幸福的一生。

The ancient oracles prophesied the downfall of the empire. 古代神谕预言了帝国的衰落。


近义词：foretold, predicted, foreseen

反义词：None directly opposite, but  "denied," "disproved" could be considered antonyms in the context of a prophecy's accuracy.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prophesies the end of the world. 他预言世界末日。
She prophesies great success for the company. 她预言公司将会取得巨大成功。


一般过去时:
The prophet prophesied the coming of a great leader. 先知预言一位伟大领袖的到来。
They prophesied rain for the next day. 他们预言第二天会下雨。


现在完成时:
The seer has prophesied many events that have come true. 预言家预言了许多已经实现的事件。
She has prophesied doom for us unless we change our ways. 除非我们改变方式，否则她已经预言了我们的厄运。


过去完成时:
The elders had prophesied a famine before the drought began. 长老们在旱灾开始之前就预言了饥荒。
They had prophesied the king's death many years ago. 他们多年前就预言了国王的死。


将来时:
The fortune teller will prophesy your future.  算命先生会预言你的未来。
They will prophesy a great victory for their team. 他们将预言他们的球队会取得巨大胜利。


虚拟语气:
If he had prophesied correctly, we would have been prepared. 如果他预言正确，我们就会做好准备。
I wish I had prophesied the stock market crash. 我真希望我预言到了股市崩盘。


被动语态:
The coming of the storm was prophesied by the weatherman.  暴风雨的来临被气象员预言了。
The victory was prophesied by many before the game.  比赛前许多人都预言了胜利。


疑问句:

否定句:
The soothsayer did not prophesy any good news. 占卜者没有预言任何好消息。
He did not prophesy the earthquake. 他没有预言地震。
