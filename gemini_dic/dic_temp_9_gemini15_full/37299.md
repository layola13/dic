commandment:/kəˈmændmənt/| n. |诫命；戒律 |A commandment is a divine or authoritative order or instruction.  It's often associated with religious or moral rules.  The Ten Commandments are a famous example. |例句：The Ten Commandments are a fundamental part of the Judeo-Christian faith. 十诫是犹太教和基督教信仰中的一个基本部分。

例句：Following the commandments is essential for a righteous life. 遵守诫命对于过正直的生活至关重要。


近义词：precept, injunction, mandate, rule |反义词：disobedience, transgression, violation |词性：名词


词源：源自古法语 commandement，意为“命令”，由 commander（命令）派生而来。  Commander 最终源自拉丁语 mandare，意为“命令，委托”。
