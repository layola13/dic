encompassed:/ɪnˈkʌmpəst/ | vt. | 包含；包含；围绕 | To include or contain something completely; to surround or encircle. | The study encompassed a wide range of topics. 这项研究包含了广泛的主题。  The mountains encompassed the valley. 群山环绕着山谷。 | include, contain, embrace, surround | exclude, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project encompasses several different tasks. = 这个项目包含几个不同的任务。
Her interests encompass art, music, and literature. = 她的兴趣包括艺术、音乐和文学。


一般过去时:
The treaty encompassed all the major points of contention. = 该条约包含了所有主要的争议点。
The fog encompassed the entire city. = 浓雾笼罩了整个城市。


现在完成时:
The museum has encompassed a vast collection of artifacts. = 这个博物馆收藏了大量的文物。
Our efforts have encompassed both prevention and treatment. = 我们的努力涵盖了预防和治疗两方面。


过去完成时:
By the time the meeting ended, the discussion had encompassed all the relevant issues. = 会议结束时，讨论已经涵盖了所有相关问题。
They had encompassed the entire island in their search. = 他们在搜寻中已经包围了整个岛屿。


将来时:
The new policy will encompass all aspects of environmental protection. = 新政策将涵盖环境保护的各个方面。
The upcoming exhibition will encompass works from various artists. = 即将举行的展览将展出不同艺术家的作品。


虚拟语气:
If the project encompassed all our suggestions, it would be perfect. = 如果项目包含我们所有的建议，那就完美了。
I wish the plan encompassed a more detailed budget. = 我希望计划包含更详细的预算。


被动语态:
The entire town was encompassed by a thick blanket of snow. = 整个城镇被厚厚的积雪覆盖。
Many different fields are encompassed by this research. = 这项研究包含了许多不同的领域。


疑问句:
Does the course encompass all the necessary skills? = 这门课程是否涵盖所有必要的技能？
What aspects of the problem does this solution encompass? = 这个解决方案包含了问题的哪些方面？


否定句:
The agreement did not encompass all the details. = 协议没有包含所有细节。
The report did not encompass the latest findings. = 报告没有包含最新的研究结果。
