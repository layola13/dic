indaba:/ɪnˈdɑːbə/| n.|印巴会议；非正式会议；商讨|A meeting or conference, especially one held in southern Africa to discuss matters of importance.  The term often implies a gathering with a specific purpose, usually involving discussion, negotiation, and resolution of issues.|例句：The tribal leaders held an indaba to discuss the land dispute. 部落首领举行了一次印巴会议来讨论土地纠纷。

例句：The company executives convened an indaba to strategize their marketing campaign. 公司高管召开了一次印巴会议来策划他们的营销活动。

近义词：conference, meeting, assembly, summit, powwow

反义词：None directly applicable;  the antonym would depend on the context (e.g., if the indaba is for conflict resolution, then "conflict" could be considered an indirect antonym).

词性：名词
