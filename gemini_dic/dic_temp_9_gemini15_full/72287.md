一般现在时:
The flotsam drifts aimlessly on the ocean. = 漂浮物漫无目的地漂浮在海洋上。
Flotsam often contains valuable items. = 漂浮物经常包含有价值的物品。

一般过去时:
The storm scattered the flotsam across the beach. = 暴风雨将漂浮物散落在海滩上。
Fishermen collected the flotsam from the sea. = 渔民们从海里收集漂浮物。

现在完成时:
The coast guard has recovered much of the flotsam. = 海岸警卫队已经找回了大部分漂浮物。
They have seen flotsam in various shapes and sizes. = 他们已经看到各种形状和大小的漂浮物。

过去完成时:
Before the storm hit, the flotsam was barely visible. = 暴风雨来临之前，漂浮物几乎不可见。
The ship had already lost its flotsam before sinking. = 船只沉没之前就已经失去了漂浮物。

将来时:
The tide will carry the flotsam further out to sea. = 潮水会把漂浮物带到更远的海里。
They will try to clean up all the flotsam on the shore. = 他们将努力清理海岸上的所有漂浮物。

虚拟语气:
If there was no flotsam, the ocean would be cleaner. = 如果没有漂浮物，海洋就会更干净。
I wish the flotsam would disappear overnight. = 我希望漂浮物一夜之间消失。
