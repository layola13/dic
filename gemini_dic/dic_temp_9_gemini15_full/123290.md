monogamy:/məˈnɒɡəmi/ | n. | 一夫一妻制；专一 | The practice or state of having a sexual relationship with only one partner.  Monogamy is a common social institution in many cultures, though its prevalence varies widely. | Example:  Many people believe in monogamous relationships. 很多人相信一夫一妻制的关系。


Example:  The study examined the success rates of monogamous versus polygamous marriages. 这项研究考察了一夫一妻制婚姻与一夫多妻制婚姻的成功率。


近义词：fidelity,exclusivity


反义词：polygamy,polyandry


词性：名词
