pooling:/ˈpuːlɪŋ/| n. |池化；汇集；合并|The process of combining multiple inputs into a single output, often used in various contexts such as data analysis, resource management, and image processing.  For example, in machine learning, pooling reduces the dimensionality of feature maps while retaining important information. |Example: Pooling layers in convolutional neural networks reduce computational complexity and improve robustness to small variations in the input.  池化层在卷积神经网络中降低了计算复杂度并提高了对输入中微小变化的鲁棒性。|近义词：combining, merging, aggregating |反义词：separating, dispersing, distributing|名词
