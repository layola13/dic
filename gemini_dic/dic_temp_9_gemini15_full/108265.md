launderability:/ˌlɔːndərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可洗性，耐洗性 | The ability of a fabric or garment to withstand repeated washing without significant damage or deterioration in appearance or quality.  It considers factors like shrinkage, colorfastness, and overall durability after laundering. | Example:  The high launderability of this cotton shirt makes it a great choice for everyday wear.  这件棉衬衫具有很高的可洗性，非常适合日常穿着。|近义词：washability, cleanability | 反义词：non-washable, fragile
