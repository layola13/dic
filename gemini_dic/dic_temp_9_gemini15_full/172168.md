pursue:/pərˈsuː/| vt.|追求，从事，追捕|to follow or chase someone or something|He is pursuing his dream of becoming a doctor. 他正在追求成为一名医生的梦想。|follow, chase, seek|abandon, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pursue my studies diligently. = 我努力学习。
She pursues a healthy lifestyle. = 她追求健康的生活方式。

一般过去时:
They pursued the thief through the streets. = 他们追捕小偷穿过街道。
He pursued a career in engineering. = 他从事工程事业。

现在完成时:
I have pursued this goal for many years. = 我多年来一直追求这个目标。
She has pursued justice tirelessly. = 她不知疲倦地追求正义。

过去完成时:
He had pursued his passion for art since childhood. = 他从小就追求他对艺术的热情。
They had pursued the matter for months before finding a solution. = 他们花了几个月时间才找到解决方法。

将来时:
I will pursue my education further. = 我将继续深造。
She will pursue her dreams relentlessly.  = 她将不懈地追求她的梦想。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pursue my ambitions more aggressively. = 如果我是你，我会更积极地追求我的抱负。
I wish I had pursued a different career path. = 我真希望我选择了一条不同的职业道路。

被动语态:
The thief was pursued by the police. = 小偷被警察追捕。
The matter is being pursued by the authorities. = 此事正在由当局调查。

疑问句:
What are you pursuing these days? = 你最近在追求什么？
What career path are you planning to pursue? = 你计划从事什么职业？

否定句:
He did not pursue the opportunity. = 他没有抓住机会。
She chose not to pursue her legal claim. = 她选择不继续她的法律诉求。


PHRASE->
pursue a goal = 追求目标
pursue a career = 从事职业
pursue an interest = 追求兴趣
pursue justice = 追求正义
pursue happiness = 追求幸福

词源:
From Old French *pursuir, from Latin prosequi (“to follow up, prosecute”), from pro- (“forward”) + sequi (“to follow”).
