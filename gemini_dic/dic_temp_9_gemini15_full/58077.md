drooled:/druːld/| v.|垂涎；流口水|To let saliva flow from the mouth, often involuntarily, especially due to sleepiness, illness, or the sight or smell of appealing food.  |例句：The baby drooled all over his bib.  婴儿把口水流了他整个围嘴上。

例句：He drooled over the delicious cake. 他垂涎欲滴地看着那块美味的蛋糕。|近义词：slobbered, slavered|反义词：None specifically antonymous, but could be contrasted with actions implying control over saliva.
