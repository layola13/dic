colada:/koˈlɑːdə/| n.| 科拉达（一种鸡尾酒）| A colada is a type of cocktail, most famously the piña colada. It typically includes rum, cream of coconut, and pineapple juice.  | Example:  I ordered a piña colada at the beach bar. 我在海滩酒吧点了一杯菠萝科拉达。 |近义词：cocktail, mixed drink |反义词: (无直接反义词，可根据语境选择反义词，例如soft drink) |名词

词源:
The word "colada" comes from the Spanish word meaning "strained" or "filtered," referring to the process of straining the ingredients to create the smooth texture of the drink.  The most famous colada, the piña colada, adds "piña" (pineapple) to the name.
