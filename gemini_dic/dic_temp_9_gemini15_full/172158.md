His pursiveness in the face of overwhelming odds finally paid off. 他面对巨大的困难依然坚持不懈，最终取得了成功。

词源:
The word "pursiveness" is derived from the adjective "pursive," which in turn comes from the verb "pursue."  The suffix "-ness" adds the quality or state of being.  The original meaning of "pursue"  relates to chasing or following something, which has evolved to encompass the determined pursuit of a goal, sometimes even to the point of stubbornness.
