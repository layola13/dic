repicture:/ˌriːˈpɪktʃər/| vt.|再描绘；重新想象；重新构想|To picture again; to form a new mental image of.  It implies a re-evaluation or a change in perspective from a previous understanding.|例句：I tried to repicture the scene in my mind, but the details were hazy. 我试图在我的脑海中重新描绘这场景，但细节模糊不清。

例句：The artist decided to repicture the landscape from a different angle.  艺术家决定从不同的角度重新描绘这幅风景画。


近义词：reimagine, revisualize, reconceive|反义词：forget, misrepresent|词性:动词
