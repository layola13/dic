detering: /dɪˈtɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 制止；阻止；威慑 |  Preventing someone from doing something or making something less likely to happen.  It often implies discouraging someone through fear or the threat of unpleasant consequences. |

例句：

The harsh prison sentences are intended to deter potential criminals. = 严厉的监禁是为了震慑潜在的罪犯。

The high price of the car deterred many potential buyers. = 这辆车的价格很高，吓退了许多潜在的买家。


近义词：discouraging, preventing, inhibiting, discouraging, dissuading

反义词：encouraging, inciting, stimulating


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The government's new policy deters illegal immigration. = 政府的新政策阻止了非法移民。

Harsh punishments deter many people from committing crimes. = 严厉的惩罚阻止了许多人犯罪。


一般过去时:

The threat of war deterred the aggressor from attacking. = 战争的威胁阻止了侵略者的进攻。

His stern warning deterred the children from playing near the road. = 他严厉的警告阻止了孩子们在路边玩耍。


现在完成时:

Increased security measures have deterred many potential attacks. = 加强的安全措施已经阻止了许多潜在的袭击。

The new law has deterred many businesses from polluting the environment. = 新法律阻止了许多企业污染环境。


过去完成时:

The heavy rain had deterred many people from attending the outdoor concert. = 大雨已经阻止了许多人参加户外音乐会。

His fear of failure had deterred him from pursuing his dreams. = 他对失败的恐惧已经阻止了他追求梦想。


将来时:

The new security system will deter potential thieves. = 新的安全系统将阻止潜在的小偷。

Higher taxes will deter investment in the country. = 更高的税收将阻止对该国的投资。


虚拟语气:

If the penalties were stricter, it would deter more crime. = 如果惩罚更严格，它将阻止更多的犯罪。

I wish the threat of punishment would deter them from their destructive behavior. = 我希望惩罚的威胁能阻止他们破坏性的行为。


被动语态:

Potential criminals are deterred by the strong police presence. = 强有力的警力震慑了潜在的罪犯。

Many people are deterred from visiting the area by the high crime rate. = 高犯罪率阻止了许多人参观该地区。


疑问句:

Will the new law deter crime effectively? = 新法律能否有效地阻止犯罪？

Does the fear of punishment deter people from breaking the rules? = 对惩罚的恐惧是否能阻止人们违反规定？


否定句:

The lack of consequences did not deter him from repeating his mistakes. = 后果的缺乏并没有阻止他重蹈覆辙。

Unfortunately, the warnings didn't deter the vandals. = 不幸的是，警告并没有阻止破坏分子。

deter the spread of: 阻止…的蔓延
