sandworm:/ˈsændwɜːrm/| n. |沙虫，海虫|A sandworm is a type of marine worm that lives in the sand on the seabed.  They are often segmented and can be quite long. Some species are used as bait for fishing. |例句：The fisherman dug up several sandworms for bait. 渔夫挖出几条沙虫作诱饵。

The children found a large sandworm on the beach. 孩子们在海滩上发现了一条大沙虫。


近义词：lugworm (某些种类), marine worm |反义词：无
