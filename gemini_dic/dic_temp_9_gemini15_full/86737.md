USAGE->
This word is an interjection, and as such doesn't conjugate through tenses.  The following examples show how it is used in different sentence structures.

Simple Sentence:
Hello, my name is John. = 你好，我叫约翰。

Sentence with a Question:
Hello, can I help you? = 你好，我能帮你吗？


PHRASE->
Hello there = 你好(更亲切的表达)

Say hello to... = 代我向...问好
Hello again = 你好，又见面了
Hello stranger = 你好，陌生人


Note:  The provided format requested a conjugation through tenses, but interjections like "hello" do not conjugate.  The USAGE section above demonstrates its use in various sentence types.  There are no antonyms for an interjection like "hello."
