meetings:/ˈmiːtɪŋz/ | n. |会议，集会 |Meetings are a common way for teams to collaborate.  会议是团队协作的常见方式。|近义词：conferences, sessions, gatherings |反义词： adjournments, separations


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We have regular meetings every week. = 我们每周定期开会。
The team holds meetings to discuss projects. = 团队举行会议讨论项目。

一般过去时:
The meeting ended late last night. = 会议昨晚很晚才结束。
We had a productive meeting yesterday. = 我们昨天开了一个富有成效的会议。

现在完成时:
We have had several meetings about this issue. = 我们已经就这个问题开了几次会。
They have finished all the meetings for this week. = 他们已经完成了本周所有的会议。

过去完成时:
The meeting had already started when I arrived. = 我到达时会议已经开始了。
They had completed the agenda before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经完成了议程。

将来时:
We will have a meeting next Monday. = 我们下周一将举行会议。
The board will be holding a meeting to discuss the budget. = 董事会将举行会议讨论预算。

虚拟语气:
It would be helpful if we had more meetings. = 如果我们开更多会，将会有帮助。
I wish we had scheduled more time for the meetings. = 我希望我们为会议安排更多时间。

被动语态:
The meeting was chaired by the CEO. = 会议由首席执行官主持。
The agenda for the meeting was distributed earlier. = 会议议程较早前已分发。

疑问句:
When is the next meeting? = 下次会议是什么时候？
Where will the meeting be held? = 会议将在哪里举行？

否定句:
We didn't have a meeting today. = 我们今天没有开会。
There was no meeting scheduled for this afternoon. = 下午没有安排会议。


PHRASE->
attend a meeting: 参加会议
hold a meeting: 举行会议
schedule a meeting: 安排会议
call a meeting: 召集会议
cancel a meeting: 取消会议
