half-dying: /ˈhæfˌdaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 半死的；奄奄一息的 |  Describes something or someone that is very weak and close to death.  It implies a lingering process rather than sudden death. |


例句：

The half-dying plant needed immediate watering.  = 奄奄一息的植物需要立即浇水。

The half-dying dog whimpered softly. =  半死的狗低声呜咽。


近义词：

dying, moribund, expiring, fading, weakening


反义词：

thriving, flourishing, robust, healthy


词性：形容词
