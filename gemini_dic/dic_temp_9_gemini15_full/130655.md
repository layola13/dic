nonadvantageously: /ˌnɒnˌædvænˈteɪdʒəsli/ | adv. | 不利地，无益地 |  Unfavorably; without advantage.  It describes something done in a way that doesn't provide any benefit or is even detrimental. |


例句：

The project was managed nonadvantageously, leading to significant cost overruns.  = 项目管理不利，导致成本严重超支。

He approached the negotiation nonadvantageously, resulting in a poor outcome. = 他以不利的方式进行谈判，导致结果不佳。


近义词：unprofitably, disadvantageously, unfavorably, detrimentally

反义词：advantageously, beneficially, profitably


词性：副词
