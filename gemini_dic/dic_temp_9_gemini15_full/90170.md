hotlist:/ˈhɑːtlɪst/ | n. | 热门清单，优先处理清单 | A list of items or tasks that require immediate attention or priority.  It often implies urgency and importance.  | Example: The CEO's hotlist included launching the new product and addressing the PR crisis.  首席执行官的优先处理清单包括推出新产品和解决公关危机。 |近义词：priority list, urgent list, top priority items |反义词：back burner items, low priority list | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project manager maintains a hotlist of critical tasks. = 项目经理维护一份关键任务的优先处理清单。
This app shows you a hotlist of trending news stories. = 这个应用程序向你展示热门新闻故事的清单。


一般过去时:
The team reviewed the hotlist before the meeting. = 团队在会议前审查了优先处理清单。
