commentates:/ˈkɒmənˌteɪts/| v.|评论；解说；作解说员|To provide spoken commentary on an event, typically a sporting event or broadcast.|例句：The commentator expertly described the action as it unfolded on the field. 解说员熟练地描述了球场上正在发生的动作。  例句：She commentates on political events for a major news network. 她为一家主要的新闻网络评论政治事件。|近义词：narrates, describes, explains|反义词：remains silent, keeps quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commentates on football matches every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都为足球比赛做解说。
She commentates on current affairs for the BBC. = 她为英国广播公司评论时事。

一般过去时:
He commentated on the Olympics last year. = 他去年为奥运会做了解说。
She commentated on the election results. = 她对选举结果做了评论。

现在完成时:
He has commentated on hundreds of games. = 他已经为数百场比赛做过解说。
She has commentated on this topic many times. = 她已经多次评论过这个话题。

过去完成时:
He had commentated on the match before the interview. = 他在接受采访之前已经对比赛进行了评论。
She had commentated on the situation before the press conference. = 她在新闻发布会之前已经对局势做出了评论。

将来时:
He will commentate on the World Cup final. = 他将为世界杯决赛做解说。
She will commentate on the upcoming presidential debate. = 她将对即将到来的总统辩论进行评论。

虚拟语气:
If he commentated on the game, more people would watch. = 如果他为比赛做解说，就会有更多人观看。
I wish she commentated more often. = 我希望她更频繁地进行评论。

被动语态:
The game was commentated on by a well-known sportscaster. = 这场比赛由一位著名的体育播音员进行了解说。
The event was commentated on live by several experts. = 该活动由几位专家现场进行了解说。

疑问句:
Who will commentate on the match tonight? = 今晚谁将为比赛做解说？
Does he usually commentate on this type of event? = 他通常会对这种类型的事件进行评论吗？

否定句:
He did not commentate on the controversial decision. = 他没有评论有争议的决定。
She does not usually commentate on political matters. = 她通常不评论政治事务。
