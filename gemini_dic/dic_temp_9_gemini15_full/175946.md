rationally:/ˈræʃənəli/| adv.|理性地；合理地|In a rational manner; in a way that is based on reason and logic, rather than emotions or feelings.  It implies a careful consideration of facts and evidence before making a decision or forming an opinion.|例句：He rationally assessed the situation before making a decision. 他在做决定之前理性地评估了形势。
