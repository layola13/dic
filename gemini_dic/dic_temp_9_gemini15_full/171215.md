psychosomatic:/ˌsaɪkoʊsəˈmætɪk/| adj.|身心（性）的；身心失调的|Relating to or denoting the interaction between mind and body, especially in causing illness.|例句：Her psychosomatic symptoms were a manifestation of her stress.  她的身心症状是她压力的表现。|例句：The doctor believes her illness is psychosomatic. 医生认为她的病是身心失调造成的。|近义词：psychophysical, somatopsychic|反义词：physical (in the sense of purely physical, not influenced by the mind)
