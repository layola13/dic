rachis:/ˈreɪkɪs/| n. |轴，穗轴；羽轴|The main axis of a compound structure, such as a spike of flowers or a feather.  It refers to the central stem of a compound inflorescence (a cluster of flowers) or the shaft of a feather from which the barbs extend.|Example: The rachis of the wheat head bore many grains. 小麦穗轴上长着许多麦粒。 The rachis of the feather was strong and flexible. 羽毛的羽轴坚固而灵活。|近义词：axis, shaft, stem|反义词：branch (in the context of branching structures)|词性：名词
