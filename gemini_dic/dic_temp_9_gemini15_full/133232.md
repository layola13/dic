nonexclusions:/ˌnɒnɪkˈskluːʒənz/| n. |非排除；不予排除的事物|Things that are not excluded or exempted.  The term often appears in legal or contractual contexts, referring to items, situations, or individuals that are not subject to a specific exclusion clause.|例句：The contract specified nonexclusions for certain types of damage.  这份合同规定了某些类型的损害不予排除。
The list of nonexclusions is quite extensive.  不予排除的清单相当广泛。|近义词：inclusions, exceptions (in the sense of what is *not* excepted)|反义词：exclusions, exceptions (in the sense of what *is* excepted)|名词
