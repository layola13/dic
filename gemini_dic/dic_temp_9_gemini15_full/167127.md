presuspiciousness:  /ˌpriːsəˈspɪʃəsnəs/ | n. | 事先的怀疑；预先的猜疑 |  A feeling of suspicion or distrust that exists before any evidence is presented. It implies a preemptive sense of doubt or apprehension.  | The presuspiciousness of the detective led him to investigate further, even though there was little initial evidence.  侦探的预先怀疑促使他进一步调查，即使最初证据很少。| suspicion, distrust, apprehension | trust, confidence, belief | 名词
