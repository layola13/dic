shunts:/ʃʌnts/| n. v.|分流；转换；岔路；(铁路)调车线|To divert or switch (something, especially a fluid) from one course or channel to another.  It can also refer to a device or passage that diverts something.  As a noun, it refers to the act of shunting or the thing used for shunting.|例句：The doctor decided to shunt the blood flow away from the damaged area.医生决定将血液从受损区域引流出去。

The railway worker skillfully shunted the carriages onto the siding.铁路工人熟练地将车厢调到岔线上。

近义词：divert, switch, bypass, redirect|反义词：direct, concentrate, focus|名词，动词
