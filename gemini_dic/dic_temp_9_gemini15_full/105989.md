knacks:/næks/ | n. |技巧；窍门；天赋；癖好 | a skill or ability to do something easily and well; a clever trick; a peculiar habit or trait. |例句：He has a knack for languages. 他有语言天赋。 He picked up the knack of it quickly. 他很快就掌握了诀窍。 |近义词：skill, talent, aptitude, trick, ability |反义词：inability, incompetence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has many useful knacks. = 他有很多有用的技巧。
She possesses a knack for making friends. = 她有交朋友的诀窍。

一般过去时:
He had a knack for solving puzzles. = 他以前擅长解谜。
She quickly picked up the knack of knitting. = 她很快学会了编织的技巧。

现在完成时:
I have developed a knack for cooking. = 我已经培养出了烹饪的技巧。
He has lost the knack for playing the piano. = 他已经失去了弹钢琴的技巧。

过去完成时:
She had already mastered the knack of juggling before the performance. = 演出之前她已经掌握了杂耍的技巧。
He had never developed a knack for public speaking. = 他从未培养出公开演讲的技巧。

将来时:
I will learn the knack of photography. = 我将学习摄影技巧。
She will surely develop a knack for coding. = 她肯定能培养出编程的技巧。

虚拟语气:
If I had a knack for painting, I would be an artist. = 如果我有绘画的天赋，我就会成为艺术家。
I wish I had a knack for writing. = 我希望我有写作的天赋。

被动语态:
The knack of making pottery is easily learned. = 制陶的技巧很容易学会。
The knack for playing chess is not easily acquired. = 下国际象棋的技巧不容易获得。

疑问句:
Do you have a knack for baking? = 你有烘焙的技巧吗？
What is his knack for fixing cars? = 他修理汽车的诀窍是什么？

否定句:
I don't have a knack for math. = 我没有数学天赋。
He didn't possess the knack for playing the violin. = 他没有演奏小提琴的技巧。


PHRASE->
a knack for:  对…有天赋/诀窍
have a knack for:  擅长于…
pick up a knack:  掌握技巧
lose the knack:  失去技巧
the knack of: …的诀窍
