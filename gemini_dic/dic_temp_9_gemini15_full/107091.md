ladettes:/læˈdɛts/| n.|辣妹，打扮时髦的年轻女子|A ladette is a young woman who behaves in a boisterous and often irresponsible manner, typically associated with excessive drinking and partying.  The term often carries negative connotations, suggesting a lack of refinement or sophistication.|Example:  The ladettes were causing a disturbance in the bar.  那些辣妹在酒吧里闹事。|近义词：party girl, wild child,  (informal)  bird|反义词：prude, wallflower
