kneading: /niːdɪŋ/ | v. | 揉捏；搓揉 | To work dough or clay into a uniform mixture with the hands.  | Example: She kneaded the dough until it was smooth and elastic. 她揉面团直到它光滑有弹性。|近义词：mixing, molding, shaping | 反义词：None directly opposite, but actions like 'leaving alone' could be considered opposite in the sense of not manipulating.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kneads the bread dough every morning. = 他每天早上都揉面包面团。
She kneads clay to create sculptures. = 她揉捏粘土来创作雕塑。

一般过去时:
The baker kneaded the dough before baking. = 面包师在烘焙前揉了面团。
I kneaded the clay until it was soft. = 我揉捏粘土直到它变软。

现在完成时:
She has kneaded the dough for ten minutes. = 她已经揉面团十分钟了。
He has kneaded several batches of bread today. = 他今天已经揉了好几批面包了。

过去完成时:
The chef had kneaded the dough before adding the ingredients. = 厨师在添加配料之前已经揉好面团了。
We had kneaded the clay for hours before it became workable. = 我们揉捏粘土几个小时后它才变得易于操作。

将来时:
I will knead the dough after I finish chopping vegetables. = 我切完蔬菜后会揉面团。
She will knead the clay tomorrow for her art class. = 她明天将为她的艺术课揉捏粘土。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would knead the dough more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把面团揉得更彻底。
I wish I had kneaded the clay longer; it's too brittle. = 我希望我揉捏粘土的时间更长一些；它太脆了。

被动语态:
The dough was kneaded by the machine. = 面团是由机器揉的。
The clay was kneaded until it was perfectly smooth. = 粘土被揉捏直到完全光滑。

疑问句:
Have you kneaded the dough yet? = 你揉面团了吗？
Did he knead the clay before firing it? = 他在烧制粘土之前揉捏了吗？

否定句:
She didn't knead the dough enough; it's too tough. = 她揉面团不够；它太硬了。
They hadn't kneaded the clay properly, so it cracked. = 他们没有正确地揉捏粘土，所以它裂开了。
