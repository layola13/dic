reconceives:/ˌriːkənˈsiːvz/| vt.|重新构想，重新设想|To think about or plan something again, especially in a new or different way.|例句：The company is reconceiving its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司正在重新构想其营销策略，以瞄准更年轻的人群。例句：The artist reconceived the painting, adding vibrant colors and new elements.艺术家重新构想了这幅画，添加了鲜艳的色彩和新的元素。|近义词：reimagine, rethink, redesign|反义词：maintain, preserve
