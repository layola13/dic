meltingly:/ˈmɛltɪŋli/| adv.| 熔化地；逐渐地；温柔地 |Describes something that is melting or happening gradually and softly, often in a pleasant or appealing way.  It suggests a slow, gentle process of transformation or decline.|例句：The chocolate melted away meltingly in the sun. 巧克力在阳光下慢慢融化。The snow meltingly disappeared under the spring sun. 春日的阳光下，雪慢慢融化消失了。|近义词：gradually, softly, gently, tenderly|反义词：abruptly, suddenly, harshly, roughly|副词
