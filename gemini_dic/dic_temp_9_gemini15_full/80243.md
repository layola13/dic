goethite:/ˈɡoʊθaɪt/| n. |褐铁矿|A common iron oxide-hydroxide mineral,  a hydrous form of iron(III) oxide.  It is an important ore of iron and a significant component of many soils and sediments. Its chemical formula is FeO(OH).|Goethite is a major component of bog iron ore.|近义词：limonite (a general term for iron oxides and hydroxides)|反义词：无
