docu-drama: /ˈdɒkjuːˌdrɑːmə/| n. |纪实剧|A documentary drama is a film or television program that combines documentary techniques with dramatic storytelling.  It uses real-life events or situations as its basis, but may also include fictionalized elements, such as dialogue or scenes, to enhance the narrative and emotional impact.  The goal is often to present a factual story in an engaging and accessible way, sometimes with the aim of raising awareness or prompting discussion about a particular issue.|Example:  The recent docu-drama about the Chernobyl disaster was both gripping and informative. 这部关于切尔诺贝利灾难的纪实剧既扣人心弦又 informative.|近义词: docudrama, factual drama|反义词: fiction, fantasy
