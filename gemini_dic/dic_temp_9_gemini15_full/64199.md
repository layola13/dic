equiaxe:/ˈiːkwiæks/| adj.| 等轴的；等边的 |Equiaxe refers to something having equal axes or dimensions, especially in a crystallographic context.  It describes a crystal system where the three crystallographic axes are of equal length and intersect at right angles. | Example:  The mineral displayed equiaxe crystal habit.  这种矿物呈现等轴晶习性。|近义词：isometric, isodiametric|反义词：anisometric
