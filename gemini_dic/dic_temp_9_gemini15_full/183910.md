reseparating:/ˌriːˈsɛpəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |再分离；重新分离|The act of separating again or anew;  to separate something that has already been separated.  It implies a previous separation.|例句：After the storm, the rescuers were busy reseparating families that had been temporarily separated.  暴风雨过后，救援人员忙于将暂时分离的家庭重新团聚。(Note: The example shows the opposite action, which highlights the meaning.  True reseparation would be a second separation of something already separated.)  The scientist is reseparating the components of the mixture to analyze them individually. 科学家正在重新分离混合物的成分以单独分析它们。|近义词：re-divide, separate again, disunite again|反义词：unite, combine, merge|动词
