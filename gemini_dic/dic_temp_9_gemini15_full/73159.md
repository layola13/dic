forbearer:/fɔːrˈbeərə(r)/|/ˈfɔːrˌbeərər/| n. |祖先；前辈；忍耐者 |A forbear is an ancestor, typically one several generations back.  The word also refers to someone who shows patience or restraint. |例句：My forbears were farmers for generations. 我的祖先世世代代都是农民。
He showed great forbearance in the face of provocation. 面对挑衅，他表现出极大的忍耐力。|近义词：ancestor, predecessor, forefather, forbearant |反义词：descendant, successor, impatient
