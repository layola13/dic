noncomplacency: /ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪsənsi/ | n. | 不自满；不满足；警惕性 |  A state of not being complacent; a vigilant awareness of potential problems or dangers.  It emphasizes the importance of remaining proactive and avoiding self-satisfaction. | Example:  The company's continued success is due in large part to its culture of noncomplacency.  这家公司的持续成功很大程度上归功于其不满足现状的文化。

|近义词: vigilance, alertness, watchfulness, cautiousness |反义词: complacency, self-satisfaction, smugness |
