insane:/ɪnˈseɪn/ | adj. | 疯狂的；精神错乱的；荒谬的 |  Describes someone who is mentally ill, or something that is extremely foolish or unreasonable.  |  He was declared insane after the incident. 他在那件事故后被宣布为精神错乱。 The plan was completely insane.  这个计划完全是疯狂的。 |  mad, crazy, deranged, lunatic | sane, rational, sensible, reasonable |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is insane. = 他疯了。
The plan seems insane to me. = 这个计划在我看来很疯狂。


一般过去时:
He was insane. = 他曾经疯了。
She acted insane yesterday. = 她昨天行为疯狂。


现在完成时:
He has been insane for many years. = 他多年来一直精神错乱。
They have acted insane since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，他们的行为一直很疯狂。


过去完成时:
He had been insane before the treatment. = 在治疗之前，他一直精神错乱。
She had acted insane before anyone realized it. = 在任何人意识到之前，她就已经行为疯狂了。


将来时:
He will be insane if he continues like this. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会疯的。
The situation will become insane if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会变得非常糟糕。


虚拟语气:
If he were insane, he wouldn't be responsible for his actions. = 如果他疯了，他就不用对他自己的行为负责。
I wish he weren't so insane. = 我希望他不那么疯狂。


被动语态:
He was declared insane by the court. = 他被法院宣布为精神错乱。
The idea was considered insane by most people. = 大多数人认为这个想法很疯狂。


疑问句:
Is he insane? = 他疯了吗？
Does this sound insane to you? = 这听起来对你来说很疯狂吗？


否定句:
He is not insane. = 他没有疯。
The idea is not insane, just unconventional. = 这个想法并不疯狂，只是不寻常。


PHRASE->
drive someone insane = 使某人发疯
plea of insanity = 精神错乱的抗辩
insane asylum (old-fashioned) = 精神病院
insanely jealous = 极度嫉妒
insane amount of work = 难以置信的工作量
