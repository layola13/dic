sharpened:/ˈʃɑːrpənd/ | adj. | 磨尖的；清晰的；敏锐的 | Made sharp or sharper; having a keen edge or point.  It can also describe something made more precise or intense. | 例句：The sharpened knife sliced through the tomato easily.  磨尖的刀很容易就切开了西红柿。

The sharpened focus of the camera captured every detail. 相机清晰的焦点捕捉到了每一个细节。


近义词：honed, pointed, keen, acute  | 反义词：dull, blunt, vague, obtuse | 词性：形容词
