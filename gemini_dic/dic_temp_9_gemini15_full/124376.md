Motivelessness can be a symptom of various psychological conditions.|例句：His motivelessness puzzled the investigators. 他的缺乏动机让调查人员感到困惑。

例句：The crime was characterized by a chilling motivelessness. 这起犯罪的特点是令人不寒而栗的缺乏动机。
