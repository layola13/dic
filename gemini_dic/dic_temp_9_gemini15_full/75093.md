frills:/frɪlz/| n.|褶边，装饰；不必要的东西 |Unnecessary details or decorations; something elaborate but unnecessary.  |例句：The dress was simple, without any frills. 这件衣服很简单，没有任何褶边。

例句：She cut out all the frills from her presentation to make it clearer. 她删掉了演示文稿中所有不必要的内容，使之更清晰。


近义词：ornaments, embellishments, flourishes, extras, fuss

反义词：simplicity, plainness, austerity


词源:  From Middle English *frillen "to crisp, curl," from Old French *friller "to crisp, curl," of uncertain origin, possibly from a Germanic source.
