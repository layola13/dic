It can also refer to a specific neighborhood or district, sometimes with a distinct ethnic or cultural identity.|例句：The mahala was a maze of narrow, winding streets and dilapidated buildings.  贫民窟是一片狭窄蜿蜒的街道和破败建筑物的迷宫。|近义词：slum, ghetto, shantytown|反义词：upscale neighborhood, affluent area
