irrigation:/ˌɪrɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |灌溉；冲洗|The artificial application of water to land to assist in the production of crops.  Irrigation is crucial for agriculture in arid and semi-arid regions.|例句：The farmer relies on irrigation to water his crops during the dry season.  这个农民依靠灌溉在旱季给庄稼浇水。|例句：The doctor recommended irrigation to clean the wound.  医生建议用灌溉的方法清洗伤口。|近义词：watering, inundation|反义词：desiccation, dehydration|名词

词源:
From Latin *irrigatio, from irrigare "to water," from ir- "in" + rigare "to water," from rivus "stream."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *rei- "to flow."
