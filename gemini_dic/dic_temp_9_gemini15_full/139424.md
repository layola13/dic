obsessionally:/əbˈsɛʃənəli/ | adv. | 困扰地；着魔般地；执迷不悟地 |  Characterized by or exhibiting an obsession; persistently preoccupied with or fixated on something.  | He checked the locks obsessionally, unable to shake the feeling that someone was after him. 他反复检查锁，无法摆脱有人在追他这种感觉。 | persistently, compulsively, continually | calmly, rationally, indifferently | 副词
