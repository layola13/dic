flintlocks: /ˈflɪntˌlɒks/ | n. | 火锁枪；燧发枪 | A firearm whose ignition system uses a piece of flint striking steel to produce sparks that ignite gunpowder.  | Example:  The museum displayed a collection of antique flintlocks.  博物馆展出了一些古董火锁枪。  The soldier carefully primed his flintlock before firing. 士兵在射击前仔细地装填了他的火锁枪。| 近义词：firelock | 反义词：modern firearm
