misprints:/ˈmɪsprɪnts/ | n. | 印刷错误，笔误 | Errors in printed text, resulting from mistakes during the printing process or from typos.  They can range from simple spelling errors to more significant problems affecting the meaning. | Example: The book was full of misprints, making it difficult to read. 这本书充满了印刷错误，很难阅读。  The numerous misprints in the document detracted from its credibility.  文件中大量的印刷错误降低了它的可信度。|近义词：typographical errors, errors, mistakes | 反义词：accuracy, correctness | 名词
