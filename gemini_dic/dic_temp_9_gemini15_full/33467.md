chutzpas:/ˈhʊtspəs/| n. | 厚颜无耻；胆大妄为；鲁莽；大胆 |  Audacious, excessive self-assurance, especially when displayed in a brazen or impudent way.  It often implies a disregard for social norms or the feelings of others. |  He had the chutzpah to ask for a raise after being late every day. 他厚着脸皮在每天迟到后要求加薪。


She showed incredible chutzpah in challenging the CEO's decision. 她大胆挑战首席执行官的决定，展现了令人难以置信的胆量。| 近义词：audacity, nerve, gall, temerity, effrontery | 反义词：timidity, shyness, diffidence | 名词


词源：来自意第绪语chutzpe，源于希伯来语חוצפה (ḥutspah)，意思是“厚颜无耻”。
