disband:/dɪsˈbænd/| vt.|解散，遣散|To officially end the existence of (an organization, group, etc.)|The band decided to disband after their final concert. 乐队决定在最后一场音乐会后解散。|近义词：dismiss, dissolve, break up|反义词：assemble, gather, form|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army disbands after the war. = 战争结束后军队解散。
The club disbands at the end of the year. = 俱乐部在年底解散。

一般过去时:
The committee disbanded after completing its work. = 委员会完成工作后解散了。
They disbanded the protest group. = 他们解散了抗议团体。

现在完成时:
The group has disbanded after many years of success. = 该团体在多年成功后已解散。
The company has disbanded its research team. = 公司已经解散了其研究团队。

过去完成时:
The team had disbanded before the season ended. = 赛季结束前，球队已经解散了。
The organization had disbanded long before I joined. = 我加入之前，该组织早就解散了。

将来时:
The project team will disband once the project is complete. = 项目完成后，项目团队将解散。
The government will disband the militia. = 政府将解散民兵。

虚拟语气:
If they hadn't disbanded the team, they might have won the championship. = 如果他们没有解散球队，他们可能就赢得了冠军。
I wish they hadn't disbanded the band so quickly. = 我真希望他们不要这么快解散乐队。

被动语态:
The club was disbanded due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，俱乐部被解散了。
The army was disbanded after the treaty was signed. = 条约签署后，军队被解散了。

疑问句:
Will the team disband at the end of the season? = 球队会在赛季结束时解散吗？
Did the group disband peacefully? = 该团体和平解散了吗？

否定句:
The company didn't disband despite the financial difficulties. = 尽管面临财务困难，公司并没有解散。
They refused to disband the union. = 他们拒绝解散工会。


PHRASE->
disband a group = 解散一个团体
disband an army = 解散一支军队
disband a committee = 解散一个委员会
disband a team = 解散一个团队
disband a meeting = 结束会议(非正式用法)
