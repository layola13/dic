scarping:/ˈskɑːrpɪŋ/| n. |陡坡；削坡；剥蚀；|The process of cutting or wearing away a surface, especially a steep slope or cliff. It can refer to natural processes like erosion or human-made activities like road construction. |例句：The scarpling of the mountainside was visible from miles away.  山坡上的陡坡从几英里外就能看到。|例句：The road construction involved significant scarpling to create a stable surface.  道路建设涉及大量的削坡以创造稳定的路面。|近义词：erosion, cutting, excavation, degradation |反义词：building up, accretion, deposition |名词
