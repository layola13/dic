partial:/ˈpɑːrʃəl/ /ˈpɑːrʃəl/| adj.|部分的，不完全的；偏袒的，偏爱的|Relating to or affecting only a part of something.|The building is only partially completed. 这座建筑物只完成了部分。|近义词：incomplete, fractional, biased|反义词：complete, total, whole, impartial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is only partially responsible. = 他只负部分责任。
The solution is partially effective. = 解决方案部分有效。

一般过去时:
She was partially blind. = 她部分失明。
The project was partially funded. = 项目获得了部分资金。


现在完成时:
They have partially restored the old house. = 他们已经部分修复了老房子。
I have partially understood the instructions. = 我已经部分理解了说明。


过去完成时:
He had partially finished the painting before he ran out of paint. = 他在油漆用完之前已经部分完成了绘画。
The team had partially completed the report by the deadline. = 团队在截止日期前已部分完成了报告。


将来时:
We will partially automate the process next year. = 我们明年将部分自动化这个流程。
The company will partially relocate its headquarters. = 公司将部分迁移其总部。


虚拟语气:
If the plan were partially successful, we would be happy. = 如果计划部分成功，我们会很高兴。
I wish the damage were only partially irreversible. = 我希望损坏只是部分不可逆转的。


被动语态:
The task was partially completed by the team. = 任务由团队部分完成。
The damage was partially repaired after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，损坏部分得到了修复。


疑问句:

否定句:
The building is not partially damaged; it's completely destroyed. = 这座建筑物并非部分受损；它是完全被摧毁了。
