minyan:/mɪˈnjɑːn/| n. |弥尼延（犹太教规定的祷告人数）|A minyan is a quorum of ten adult Jews required for certain religious services in Judaism.  Without a minyan, many Jewish prayers and rituals cannot be performed. |例句：We needed one more person to make a minyan for the morning prayers. 我们还需要一个人才能凑够弥尼延，进行早祷。

例句：The small synagogue struggled to gather a minyan every Shabbat. 这家小犹太教堂每个安息日都难以凑够弥尼延。

近义词：quorum (法定人数)

反义词：None (没有合适的反义词)

词性:名词
