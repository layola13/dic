prespecifies:/ˌpriːˈspesɪfaɪz/| vt.|预先规定；预先指定 | To specify beforehand; to determine or stipulate in advance.  The term implies a prior action of defining or setting parameters before a process begins. |例句：The contract prespecifies the delivery date. 合同预先规定了交货日期。

The researcher prespecified the inclusion criteria for the study. 研究人员预先规定了该研究的纳入标准。


近义词：predetermine, predetermine, specify in advance, set beforehand, establish beforehand


反义词： leave unspecified, leave open, improvise


词性：动词
