notability:/ˌnoʊtəˈbɪləti/ | n. |显著性；著名；值得注意 |The quality of being worthy of notice; importance; fame.  It refers to the state of being notable or the degree to which something is notable.  This can apply to people, events, or things. |例句：The notability of the event was undeniable.  这件事的重要性是不容否认的。|例句：His notability in the field of physics is well-established. 他在物理学领域的声望是公认的。|近义词：importance, significance, prominence, renown, fame |反义词：insignificance, obscurity, anonymity |词性：名词
