drupe:/druːp/| n.|核果；浆果|A drupe is a type of fruit that has a fleshy outer part and a hard shell (or stone) surrounding a seed.  Examples include peaches, cherries, plums, and olives. |Example: The peach is a delicious drupe.  桃子是一种美味的核果。  The olive is another example of a drupe often used in cooking. 橄榄是另一种常用于烹饪的核果。|近义词：stone fruit, berry (in a broader sense)|反义词： pome (a fruit with a fleshy core, like an apple)|名词
