词源:
The term "mother-of-pearl" is a direct translation of the French "mère-de-perle," with "mère" meaning "mother" and "perle" meaning "pearl."  The name reflects the belief that pearls are formed within the nacreous lining of the shell, making the shell itself the "mother" of the pearls.
