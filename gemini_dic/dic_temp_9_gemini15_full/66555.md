exhibiting:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪŋ/| v. | 展览；展示；表现；显露 | Showing or displaying something publicly.  The act of presenting something for public view. It can also refer to displaying a particular quality or characteristic. | 例句：The museum is exhibiting a collection of ancient artifacts. = 博物馆正在展出一批古代文物。 The child is exhibiting signs of anxiety. =  孩子表现出焦虑的迹象。|近义词：displaying, showcasing, showing, demonstrating, revealing |反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The gallery is exhibiting modern art. = 画廊正在展出现代艺术。
He is exhibiting great talent in music. = 他在音乐方面展现出非凡的天赋。

一般过去时:
They exhibited their paintings at the local art fair. = 他们在当地艺术博览会上展出了他们的画作。
She exhibited remarkable courage in the face of danger. = 面对危险，她表现出非凡的勇气。

现在完成时:
The artist has exhibited his work in several galleries. =这位艺术家已经在几家画廊展出过他的作品。
He has exhibited a strong commitment to his community. = 他一直对他的社区表现出强烈的责任感。

过去完成时:
The museum had exhibited the painting for several months before it was sold. = 这幅画在卖出之前，博物馆已经展出了几个月。
She had exhibited signs of illness before collapsing. = 她晕倒之前已经表现出生病的迹象。

将来时:
They will be exhibiting their new designs at the fashion show. = 他们将在时装秀上展出他们的新设计。
He will exhibit more confidence as he gains experience. = 随着经验的积累，他会表现出更大的自信。

虚拟语气:
If they exhibited more empathy, they would have better relationships. = 如果他们表现出更多同情心，他们就会有更好的关系。
I wish the company exhibited more transparency in its operations. = 我希望公司在运营方面更加透明。

被动语态:
The artwork was exhibited in a prestigious gallery. = 这件艺术品在一个著名的画廊展出。
His skills were exhibited throughout his impressive career. = 他的技能在他的辉煌职业生涯中得到了充分展示。

疑问句:
Is the school exhibiting any new educational technology? = 学校是否正在展示任何新的教育技术？
Are you exhibiting any symptoms of the illness? = 你是否表现出任何疾病症状？

否定句:
The company is not exhibiting any signs of slowing down. = 公司没有表现出任何放缓的迹象。
He did not exhibit any interest in the job offer. = 他对这份工作机会没有表现出任何兴趣。
