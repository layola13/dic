needily:/ˈniːdɪli/| adv.|贫困地；急需地；可怜地 |In a needy manner; in a state of need.  It describes an action or state performed or existing because of a great need.|The family lived needily, barely able to afford food.|近义词：desperately, urgently, poorly|反义词：affluently, richly, comfortably
