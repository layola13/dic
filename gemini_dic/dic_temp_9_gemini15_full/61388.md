emble:/ˈembləm/ | n. |象征；标志；徽章 | An emblem is a symbol that represents something else, often an organization, idea, or quality.  It can be a picture, object, or design.  |The Olympic rings are an emblem of international sportsmanship. 奥运五环是国际体育精神的象征。|The eagle is the emblem of the United States. 鹰是美国的象征。|近义词：symbol, sign, badge |反义词：N/A
