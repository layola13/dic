omosternum: /ˌoʊməˈstɜːrnəm/ | n. |胸骨柄|The omosternum is the uppermost segment of the sternum, also known as the manubrium.  It articulates with the clavicles and the first two ribs.  It's a crucial part of the skeletal structure of the thorax, providing attachment points for important muscles and ligaments. |The omosternum provides structural support for the upper chest.|近义词：manubrium sterni|反义词：无
