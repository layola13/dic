release:/rɪˈliːs/ | vt. n. | 释放；发行；发布；解除；让步 | To set free from restraint, confinement, or obligation; to give out or make available; to discharge or let go. |例句：The prisoner was released from jail. 囚犯被释放出狱了。

例句：The company released a new product last week. 公司上周发布了一款新产品。


近义词：liberate, discharge, launch, publish, cede | 反义词：confine, restrain, imprison, withhold, retain | 词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He releases a new album every year. 他每年发行一张新专辑。
The company releases its financial reports quarterly. 公司每季度发布财务报告。

一般过去时:
They released the hostages unharmed. 他们释放了未受伤的人质。
The movie was released in theaters last month. 这部电影上个月在影院上映。

现在完成时:
She has released three novels so far. 到目前为止，她已经出版了三部小说。
The government has released a statement on the matter. 政府就此事发表声明。

过去完成时:
He had released the bird before I arrived. 我到之前他已经放飞了那只鸟。
The news had been released to the public before the press conference. 新闻在记者招待会之前就已经发布给公众了。

将来时:
They will release the new software next month. 他们下个月将发布新软件。
The band will release their new single on Friday. 乐队将在周五发行他们的新单曲。

虚拟语气:
If they released the prisoners, there would be fewer crimes. 如果他们释放囚犯，犯罪就会减少。
I wish they had released the information sooner. 我希望他们早点发布信息。

被动语态:
The prisoners were released from prison. 囚犯被释放出狱。
The new album will be released next week. 新专辑将于下周发行。

疑问句:
When will the new game be released? 新游戏什么时候发行？
Has the report been released yet? 报告发布了吗？

否定句:
They did not release any information to the press. 他们没有向媒体发布任何信息。
The company has not yet released its earnings report. 公司尚未发布其收益报告。
