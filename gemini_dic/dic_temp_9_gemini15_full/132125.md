noncrystallizable: /ˌnɒnkrɪstəˈlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 不可结晶的 | Unable to be crystallized; amorphous.  This term describes substances that do not readily form a crystalline structure upon solidification or cooling.  They lack the ordered, repeating three-dimensional arrangement of atoms or molecules characteristic of crystalline materials. |


例句：

Some polymers are noncrystallizable and exist as amorphous solids.  一些聚合物是不可结晶的，以无定形固体的形式存在。

The noncrystallizable fraction of the sugar solution was difficult to separate. 糖溶液中不可结晶的部分很难分离。


近义词：amorphous, glassy, uncrystallizable

反义词：crystallizable, crystalline


词性：形容词
