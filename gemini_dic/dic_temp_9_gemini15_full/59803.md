ecrasement:/eˈkrɑːsmɑ̃/| n.|压碎；粉碎；崩溃|The act of crushing or smashing something; a collapse or breakdown.  It can refer to a physical crushing or a figurative collapse (e.g., of a system or hopes).|例句：The ecrasement of the car was complete after the accident.  车祸后汽车完全被压碎了。|The ecrasement of his dreams left him feeling despondent.  梦想的破灭让他感到沮丧。|近义词：crushing, smashing, pulverizing, collapse, breakdown|反义词：construction, building, elevation, uplift
