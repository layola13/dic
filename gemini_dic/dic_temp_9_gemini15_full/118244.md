mercerize:/ˈmɜːrsəraɪz/| vt.| 使丝光, 丝光处理 |To treat (cotton, etc.) with alkali to improve its strength, luster, and dye affinity.  This process involves treating cotton fibers with a solution of sodium hydroxide under tension.  The treatment causes the fibers to swell and become smoother, resulting in a stronger, more lustrous fabric with improved dye uptake. |Example: The fabric was mercerized to enhance its sheen and durability.  丝光处理过的布料增强了光泽和耐用性。|近义词：treat (with alkali), finish, refine |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be leaving the fabric untreated.
