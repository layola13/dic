nonepicurean: /ˌnɒnˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ | adj. | 非享乐主义的，禁欲的 | Not given to the pursuit of pleasure; austere.  This word describes someone who does not prioritize sensual pleasure or luxury.  They may lead a simple life, abstaining from excessive indulgence. |Example:  He led a nonepicurean life, focusing on spiritual pursuits rather than material comforts. 他过着一种非享乐主义的生活，专注于精神追求而不是物质享受。


Example:  Her nonepicurean diet consisted mainly of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. 她的非享乐主义饮食主要由水果、蔬菜和全谷物组成。


近义词: ascetic, abstemious, austere, spartan


反义词: epicurean, hedonistic, self-indulgent


词性: adjective
