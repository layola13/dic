ourang:/ˈɔːræŋ/ | n. |猩猩| Orangutan, a large arboreal ape native to the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra.  Orangutans are known for their intelligence and solitary nature. |Example: The orangutan swung gracefully through the trees.  这只猩猩优雅地荡过树枝。|近义词：ape, primate |反义词：无
