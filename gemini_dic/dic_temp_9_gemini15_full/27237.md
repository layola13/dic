calmucks:/ˈkælmʌks/| n. |卡尔梅克人；卡尔梅克族人 |The word "Calmucks" is an obsolete and now considered offensive term for the Kalmyk people, a Mongolic ethnic group primarily inhabiting the Republic of Kalmykia in Russia. The term is derived from a historical exonym and carries a negative connotation due to its association with colonialist and racist views.  Its usage should be avoided entirely in favor of "Kalmyk." |The Calmucks were known for their horsemanship and nomadic lifestyle. = 卡尔梅克人以其骑术和游牧生活方式而闻名。|Kalmyk, Oirat |(no direct antonym, as it is a proper noun)|名词
