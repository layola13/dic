overtrawl:/ˈoʊvərtɔːl/| v.|过度拖网捕捞|To fish with a trawl net excessively or illegally, often resulting in damage to the seabed and unsustainable depletion of fish stocks.|例句：The fishing vessel was fined for overtrawling in protected waters. 这艘渔船因在保护水域过度拖网捕捞而被罚款。 The study showed that overtrawling has led to a significant decline in the local fish population. 研究表明，过度拖网捕捞导致当地鱼类数量大幅下降。|近义词：overfishing, excessive trawling|反义词：sustainable fishing, responsible fishing
