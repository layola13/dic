floats:/floʊts/| /fləʊts/ | v. |漂浮；浮动；(想法等)浮现；|To remain on or just above the surface of a liquid without sinking; to drift or move gently on water or in the air.|The boat floats on the lake.  这艘船漂浮在湖面上。


The balloon floats in the sky. 气球漂浮在空中。


近义词：drift, hover, bob


反义词：sink,submerge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wood floats on water. 木头漂浮在水上。
The boat floats gently down the river. 船缓缓地顺流而下。


一般过去时:
The leaf floated down the stream. 叶子顺流漂下。
The balloon floated away. 气球飘走了。


现在完成时:
The boat has floated down the river for hours. 船已经在河上漂流了好几个小时。
The cork has floated to the surface. 软木塞已经浮到水面上了。


过去完成时:
The raft had floated away before we could reach it.  在我们到达之前，木筏已经漂走了。
The debris had floated downstream for miles.  残骸已经顺流漂流了好几英里。


将来时:
The ice will float on the water. 冰将会浮在水上。
The leaves will float on the pond. 叶子将会漂浮在池塘上。


虚拟语气:
If the ship were lighter, it would float. 如果船更轻，它就会漂浮。
If only the boat floated better! 要是船漂得更好就好了！


被动语态:
The boat was floated gently to the shore. 船被轻轻地漂到岸边。
The logs were floated down the river. 木头被顺流而下。


疑问句:
Does the ice float? 冰会漂浮吗？
Will the balloon float? 气球会漂浮吗？


否定句:
The rock doesn't float. 石头不会漂浮。
The wood didn't float because it was too heavy. 木头因为太重而不会漂浮。


PHRASE->
float on (继续) | float away (飘走) | float in (漂浮在...) | float out (漂浮出来) |  float down (漂流而下)


词源:
源自古英语 *flōtan,  与古高地德语 flozzan, 古诺尔斯语 flota  同源，意为“漂浮”。  最终来源可能与PIE *pleu-  (to flow, swim)相关。
