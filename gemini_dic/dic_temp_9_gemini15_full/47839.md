decayer:/dɪˈkeɪər/| n. |衰变者；腐烂物；衰减器|A decayer is something that causes decay or decomposition, or someone or something that undergoes decay.  It can refer to a particle in physics, an organism in biology, or a process of deterioration.|例句：In particle physics, a muon is a type of decayer. 在粒子物理学中，μ子是一种衰变粒子。The old wooden fence was a haven for various decayers.  旧木栅栏是各种腐烂物的藏身之处。|近义词：decomposer, disintegrator, deteriorator |反义词：preserver, protector, stabilizer
