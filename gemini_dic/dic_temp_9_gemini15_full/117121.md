meandered:/miˈændəd/| v.|蜿蜒而行，漫无目的地闲逛 | To follow a winding course.  To proceed or act in a winding or indirect way. |例句：The river meandered through the valley.  这条河蜿蜒穿过山谷。

The path meandered through the woods. 小路蜿蜒穿过树林。

近义词：wander, ramble, wind, twist, stray |反义词：straighten, proceed directly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river meanders through the valley. = 这条河蜿蜒穿过山谷。
He meanders through life without a clear goal. = 他漫无目的地生活，没有明确的目标。


一般过去时:
The river meandered through the valley. = 这条河蜿蜒穿过山谷。
We meandered through the streets, enjoying the sights. = 我们漫步在街道上，欣赏着景色。


现在完成时:
The river has meandered through this valley for centuries. = 这条河几个世纪以来一直蜿蜒流淌在这山谷中。
We have meandered along this path many times. = 我们已经多次沿着这条路蜿蜒而行。


过去完成时:
After we had meandered for hours, we finally found our way back. = 我们漫无目的地走了几个小时后，终于找到了回家的路。
They had meandered through the market before buying anything. = 他们在买东西之前在市场里闲逛了一阵子。


将来时:
We will meander through the countryside tomorrow. = 我们明天将在乡间漫步。
The river will continue to meander its way to the sea. = 这条河将继续蜿蜒流向大海。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would meander through the museum. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们就会在博物馆里漫步。
I wish I could meander through the fields of wildflowers. = 我希望我能漫步在野花田里。


被动语态:
The path is not often meandered because it is so overgrown. = 这条路很少有人走，因为它长满了杂草。 (This example is less natural; the passive voice is not commonly used with this verb.)
The route was meandered by the hikers, leading them to an unexpected view. = 徒步旅行者沿着蜿蜒的小路走，意外地看到了美丽的景色。(This example is more about the route itself being winding, not a passive action.)


疑问句:
Does the road meander much further? = 这条路还会蜿蜒延伸很远吗？
Did they meander through the forest on their way home? = 他们回家途中是否在森林里闲逛？


否定句:
The road does not meander; it goes straight to the city. = 这条路不蜿蜒，它直通城市。
They didn't meander; they walked directly to their destination. = 他们没有闲逛；他们直接走到目的地。
