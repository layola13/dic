cantons:/ˈkæntənz/| n.|州；行政区；（瑞士的）州；（中国某些地区的）县|A canton is a self-governing state, region, or district, especially one of the states of Switzerland.  The term can also refer to a type of administrative division in other countries, particularly in China.|例句：Switzerland is a confederation of cantons. 瑞士是一个由各州组成的联邦。|The Swiss cantons enjoy considerable autonomy. 瑞士各州享有相当大的自治权。|近义词：state, province, region, district|反义词：nation, country (in the sense of a unified whole)
