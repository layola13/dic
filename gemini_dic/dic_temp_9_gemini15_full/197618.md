shepherdling:/ˈʃɛpərdlɪŋ/| n. |牧羊少年，年轻牧羊人 | A young shepherd; a boy who tends sheep.  The word combines "shepherd" with the diminutive suffix "-ling," indicating smallness or youth. | Example: The shepherdling carefully guided his flock across the stream. 牧羊少年小心翼翼地带领他的羊群穿过小溪。|近义词：young shepherd, herdsboy |反义词：experienced shepherd, old shepherd
