indoctrinates:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪts/ | vt. |灌输；教唆；向…灌输教条 | To teach (someone) to accept a set of beliefs uncritically.  | 例句：The cult indoctrinates its members to believe that only they are right.  邪教灌输其成员相信只有他们是正确的。

The government attempts to indoctrinate its citizens with patriotic ideas. 政府试图向公民灌输爱国思想。


近义词：brainwash, proselytize, inculcate,  | 反义词：educate (in a critical, open-minded way), enlighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He indoctrinates his children with his own political views. 他向他的孩子们灌输他自己的政治观点。
The school indoctrinates students with conservative values. 学校向学生灌输保守的价值观。


一般过去时:
The teacher indoctrinated the students with the party line. 老师向学生灌输了党的路线。
The cult indoctrinated its followers with a warped worldview. 邪教用扭曲的世界观灌输其追随者。


现在完成时:
They have indoctrinated the young generation with false information. 他们已经向年轻一代灌输了虚假信息。
The regime has indoctrinated its people for decades. 该政权几十年来一直在向其人民灌输思想。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, the monarchy had indoctrinated the population for centuries. 在革命之前，君主制已经灌输了几个世纪的人民。
The church had indoctrinated the villagers with its beliefs for generations. 教堂世世代代向村民灌输其信仰。


将来时:
The new government will indoctrinate the populace with its ideology. 新政府将向民众灌输其意识形态。
They will indoctrinate the children with their own religious beliefs. 他们将向孩子们灌输他们自己的宗教信仰。


虚拟语气:
If they indoctrinated the students with critical thinking skills, they would be better citizens. 如果他们向学生灌输批判性思维能力，他们将成为更好的公民。
I wish they hadn't indoctrinated him with such hateful ideas. 我真希望他们没有向他灌输如此仇恨的思想。


被动语态:
The students were indoctrinated with a biased history. 学生们被灌输了有偏见的歷史。
The population is being indoctrinated through state-controlled media. 人口正在通过国家控制的媒体接受灌输。


疑问句:
Was the population indoctrinated against foreign ideas? 人口是否被灌输了反对外国思想的观念？
Are the children being indoctrinated with religious dogma? 孩子们是否正在被灌输宗教教条？


否定句:
They did not indoctrinate the children with their political beliefs. 他们没有向孩子们灌输他们的政治信仰。
The school does not indoctrinate students; it encourages critical thinking. 学校不会向学生灌输思想；它鼓励批判性思维。


PHRASE->
indoctrinate into:  灌输…到…
indoctrinate with: 用…灌输
indoctrinate by: 通过…灌输
indoctrinate against: 灌输反对…
indoctrinate someone in: 向某人灌输…


词源:
The word "indoctrinate" comes from the Latin words "in-" (into) and "doctrina" (teaching, doctrine).  It thus literally means "to teach into."
