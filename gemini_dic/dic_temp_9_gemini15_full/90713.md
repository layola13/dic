humidifier:/ˈhjuːmɪdaɪzər/ | n. |加湿器 | A device that adds moisture to the air.  Humidifiers are used to increase the humidity in a room, often during dry winter months. | Example:  We bought a humidifier to combat the dry air in our apartment. = 我们买了一个加湿器来对抗公寓里干燥的空气。

The humidifier in my bedroom helps me sleep better. = 我卧室里的加湿器帮助我睡得更好。|近义词：moisturizer (in some contexts) |反义词：dehumidifier |名词

词源:
The word "humidifier" is a combination of "humid," meaning moist or damp, and the suffix "-ifier," which indicates a device or agent that produces something.  "Humid" ultimately comes from the Latin word "humidus," meaning "moist" or "wet."
