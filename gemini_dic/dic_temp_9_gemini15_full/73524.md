foreshorten: /ˌfɔːrˈʃɔːrtn/| vt.|  缩短；透视缩短；使显得较短 | To make something appear shorter than it actually is, especially by representing it in perspective.  This usually refers to the visual effect of perspective in art and photography, where objects further away appear smaller and compressed. | 例句：The artist foreshortened the figure's legs to create a sense of depth.  艺术家缩短了人物的腿部以营造深度感。

The perspective foreshortened the distant buildings. 透视使远处的建筑物看起来缩短了。


近义词：shorten, abbreviate, compress, contract | 反义词：elongate, extend, lengthen
