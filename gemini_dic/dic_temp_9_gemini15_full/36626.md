collectivizes: /kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 集体化；使集体所有 | To organize (a country or industry) on the basis of collective or state ownership and control.  The process of transforming a system or industry into one controlled by a collective. |

例句：

The Soviet Union collectivized agriculture in the 1930s. = 苏联在20世纪30年代对农业进行了集体化。

The government attempted to collectivize the fishing industry, but met with resistance from independent fishermen. = 政府试图将渔业集体化，但遭到了独立渔民的抵制。


近义词：nationalize, socialize

反义词：privatize, individualize

词性：动词
