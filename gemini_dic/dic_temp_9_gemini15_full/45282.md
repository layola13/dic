cultural:/ˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 文化的；文明的 | Relating to the ideas, customs, and social behaviour of a society.  | Example: The museum showcases the rich cultural heritage of the region. 这个博物馆展示了该地区丰富的文化遗产。  |近义词：civilizational, societal | 反义词：uncivilized, barbaric

WORD ORIGIN->
The word "cultural" is derived from the Latin word "cultura," meaning "cultivation," "tilling," or "refinement."  Over time, its meaning evolved to encompass the cultivation of the mind and spirit, and eventually the intellectual and artistic achievements of a civilization or people.
