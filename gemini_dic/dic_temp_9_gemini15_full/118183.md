mentioners: /mɛnʃənərz/ | n. |提及者，说起的人|Those who mention something;  people who bring something up in conversation or writing.  It refers to the individuals or entities that make a mention of something. |例句：The mentioners of the scandal were all anonymous. 提及这件丑闻的人都是匿名的。 The mentioners in the report were careful to cite their sources. 报告中提及此事的人谨慎地引用了他们的消息来源。|近义词：speakers, referrers, namers|反义词：concealers, silencers|名词
