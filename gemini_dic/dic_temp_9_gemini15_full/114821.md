mameluke:/ˈmæməluːk/| n. |马穆鲁克（穆斯林奴隶兵）|A Mameluke was a slave soldier, originally a Turkish or other non-Arab Muslim, who served in the armies of Egypt and other parts of the Middle East.  They often rose to positions of power.  The term also refers to their descendants.|The Mamelukes were a powerful military force in Egypt for centuries.|近义词：slave soldier, mercenary|反义词：master, civilian
