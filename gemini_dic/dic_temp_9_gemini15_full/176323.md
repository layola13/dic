re-endow: /ˌriːɪnˈdaʊ/ | vt. | 重新赋予；再次捐赠 | To give or provide something again or anew, especially a quality, right, or property.  | 例句：The university decided to re-endow the history department with a substantial grant. = 大学决定向历史系再次捐赠一笔可观的款项。

例句：The government plans to re-endow the national museum with additional funding for renovations. = 政府计划再次拨款给国家博物馆用于翻修。


近义词：reinstate, re-grant, re-equip, replenish


反义词：deprive, divest


词性：动词
