endomysium:/ˌɛndoˈmɪziəm/| n. |肌内膜|A thin sheath of connective tissue surrounding each individual muscle fiber. It is the innermost layer of the three layers of connective tissue that surround skeletal muscle. It plays a vital role in supporting and nourishing the muscle fibers, allowing for efficient contraction and relaxation.|例句：The endomysium provides structural support and facilitates nutrient exchange to individual muscle fibers. 肌内膜为单个肌纤维提供结构支持并促进营养交换。|近义词：sarcolemma (细胞膜)|反义词：epimysium (肌外膜)
