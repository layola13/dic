quislings:/ˈkwɪzlɪŋz/| n.|通敌者；卖国贼|A quisling is a person who betrays their country by collaborating with an enemy occupying force.  The term is derived from Vidkun Quisling, a Norwegian politician who collaborated with the Nazis during World War II.|例句：He was branded a quisling for his collaboration with the enemy.|近义词：traitor, collaborator, turncoat|反义词：patriot, loyalist
