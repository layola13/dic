lunula:/ˈljuːnjuːlə/|/ˈluːnjuːlə/| n. |小月形，半月痕|The lunula is the whitish, half-moon shaped area at the base of the nail.  It's the visible part of the nail matrix, where new nail cells are formed.|例句：The lunula of my thumbnail is quite prominent. 我的拇指指甲的半月痕非常明显。|例句：The size and shape of the lunula can vary from person to person. 半月痕的大小和形状因人而异。|近义词：half-moon, crescent|反义词：无
