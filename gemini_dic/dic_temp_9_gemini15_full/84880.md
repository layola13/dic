hangnail:/ˈhæŋneɪl/| n.|倒刺|A piece of skin that has become detached from the cuticle at the base of a fingernail or toenail, often painful and easily caught.|例句：I have a painful hangnail on my thumb. 我拇指上有个疼的倒刺。|例句：She carefully trimmed the hangnail to prevent it from catching on things. 她仔细地修剪了倒刺，以免它钩到东西。|近义词：agnail|反义词:无
