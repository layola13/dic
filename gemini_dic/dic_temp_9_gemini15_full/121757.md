mispurchasing:/ˌmɪspɜːrtʃeɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 误购，买错 | The act of buying something incorrectly, mistakenly, or unintentionally.  This could involve buying the wrong item, a faulty item, or an item at an inflated price due to a mistake. | Example:  He regretted his mispurchasing of the faulty washing machine. 他后悔买了那台有故障的洗衣机。|近义词：wrong purchase, erroneous purchase, mistaken purchase | 反义词：wise purchase, judicious purchase
