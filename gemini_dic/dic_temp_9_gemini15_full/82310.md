grouser:/ˈɡraʊzər/| n.|抱怨者，牢骚满腹的人|A person who complains frequently and habitually.  Someone who grumbles or complains a lot.|例句：He's a real grouser; he's always complaining about something. 他是个十足的抱怨者；他总是抱怨一些事情。|例句：Don't be such a grouser; try to look on the bright side. 不要总是抱怨；尝试从积极的方面看待事物。|近义词：complainer, grumbler, whiner, malcontent|反义词：optimist, Pollyanna


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grousers about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
She grousers constantly about her job. = 她不断抱怨她的工作。

一般过去时:
He groused about the long lines yesterday. = 他昨天抱怨排队时间太长。
She groused to her friends about her boss. = 她向朋友们抱怨她的老板。

现在完成时:
He has groused about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题几周了。
She has groused repeatedly about the lack of resources. = 她一再抱怨资源匮乏。

过去完成时:
He had groused about the project before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始他就已经抱怨这个项目了。
She had groused endlessly about the unfair treatment before she finally quit. = 在她最终辞职之前，她没完没了地抱怨不公平待遇。


将来时:
He will grouse about the noise again tomorrow. = 他明天还会抱怨噪音。
She will grouse incessantly about the new policy. = 她会没完没了地抱怨新政策。


虚拟语气:
If he knew how to fix it, he wouldn't grouse so much. = 如果他知道如何解决，他就不会抱怨这么多。
I wish she wouldn't grouse all the time. = 我希望她不要总是抱怨。


被动语态:
His complaints are often groused about by others. = 他的抱怨经常被其他人议论。 (This is a less common usage)
The new rules were groused about by the employees. = 新规章制度受到了员工的抱怨。(This is a less common usage)


疑问句:
Does he grouse frequently? = 他经常抱怨吗？
Why does she grouse so much? = 她为什么抱怨这么多？


否定句:
He doesn't grouse often. = 他不经常抱怨。
She didn't grouse at all yesterday. = 她昨天一点都没抱怨。


PHRASE->
a chronic grouser =  一个惯常抱怨的人
a professional grouser = 一个职业抱怨者 (figurative)
a perpetual grouser = 一个总是抱怨的人
grouser's attitude = 抱怨者的态度
grouser's complaint = 抱怨者的抱怨
