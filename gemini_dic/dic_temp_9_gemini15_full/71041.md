first-aider:/ˈfɜːrstˈeɪdə(r)/| n. |急救员，急救护理人员|A person trained to give first aid.  A first-aider is someone who has been trained to provide emergency medical assistance before professional help arrives.  They are equipped with basic knowledge and skills to handle various injuries and illnesses.|例句：The first-aider quickly assessed the victim's injuries and provided immediate treatment. 急救员迅速评估了受害者的伤势并提供了及时的治疗。

The first-aider applied a pressure bandage to stop the bleeding. 急救员用加压绷带止血。


近义词：emergency medical technician (EMT), paramedic |反义词：无
