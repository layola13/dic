non-metallic: /ˌnɑːnˌmɛtælɪk/ | adj. |非金属的| Not made of metal; relating to or characteristic of a non-metal.  Non-metallic materials lack the characteristic properties of metals, such as conductivity and malleability. |

The non-metallic minerals are becoming increasingly valuable.  非金属矿物越来越有价值。

The non-metallic component of the composite material provides insulation.  复合材料的非金属成分提供绝缘。


近义词: non-metal, unmetallic

反义词: metallic


词性: adjective
