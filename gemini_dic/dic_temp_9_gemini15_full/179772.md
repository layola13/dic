reevacuated:/ˌriːˈvækjuˌeɪtɪd/| v. |重新疏散，再次撤离 |To evacuate again; to move people from a place to safety a second time.  |Example: After the initial evacuation, the residents were reevacuated due to a sudden surge in the wildfire.  重新疏散后，由于野火突然加剧，居民们再次撤离。|近义词：relocate, remove, displace |反义词：settle, occupy


USAGE->

They reevacuate the area every hurricane season. = 他们每年飓风季节都会重新疏散该地区。
The government reevacuates citizens from dangerous zones. = 政府会将公民从危险区域重新疏散。
