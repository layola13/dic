/keɪ/ | det. pron. |什么，哪一个|  'What' is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or ideas. It can also be used in exclamations and relative clauses.| What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？| which, which one| nothing, none| pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you already done before he arrived? = 他到达之前你已经做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built there? = 那里正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he meant. = 他没有说出他的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ……怎么样？
What if...? = 如果……将会怎样？
What for? = 为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
