lamer:/ˈleɪmər/| n. |蹩脚的人；无能的人；失败者 |A lamer is someone who is considered incompetent or unskilled, often in a humorous or informal context.  They might be clumsy, make poor decisions, or fail repeatedly at a task. The term often carries a playful or teasing tone rather than outright insult. | Example: "He's such a lamer; he couldn't even build a simple campfire."  他真是个笨蛋，连个简单的篝火都生不起来。|近义词：loser, klutz, buffoon, incompetent |反义词：pro, expert, ace
