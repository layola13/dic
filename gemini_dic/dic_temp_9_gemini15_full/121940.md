missile:/ˈmɪsəl/ | n. |导弹，飞弹 | A self-propelled guided explosive weapon that can be launched from land, sea, or air.  | Example: The country tested a new long-range missile. 这个国家测试了一种新型远程导弹。 The missile struck its target with deadly accuracy. 导弹以致命的精确度击中了目标。 |近义词：rocket, projectile | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The missile flies at supersonic speed. = 这枚导弹以超音速飞行。
He launches missiles regularly. = 他定期发射导弹。


一般过去时:
The missile hit the target. = 导弹击中了目标。
They launched several missiles last night. = 他们昨晚发射了几枚导弹。


现在完成时:
They have launched many missiles this year. = 今年他们已经发射了许多导弹。
The military has developed a new missile defense system. = 军方已经研发出了一种新型导弹防御系统。


过去完成时:

将来时:
They will launch the missile tomorrow. = 他们明天将发射导弹。

虚拟语气:
