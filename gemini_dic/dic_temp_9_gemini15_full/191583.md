schoolboys:/ˈskuːlˌbɔɪz/| n. |男学生们，小学生们|schoolboys refers to a group of boys who attend school. The term is commonly used to refer to younger boys, typically those in primary or secondary school.  However, it can be used more broadly to refer to any group of boys attending school at any level.|例句：The schoolboys were playing football in the playground. 男孩子们在操场上踢足球。
The schoolboys were punished for their bad behavior.  这些男学生因为不良行为受到了惩罚。|近义词：school children, pupils, students (in a broader sense)|反义词：schoolgirls
