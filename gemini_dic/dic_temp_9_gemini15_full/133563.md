nonfissility: /ˌnɒnˈfɪsɪlɪti/ | adj. | 不可裂变性，不易裂解性 | Incapable of undergoing nuclear fission.  The property of not being able to undergo nuclear fission, which is the splitting of an atomic nucleus into two or more lighter nuclei. This is often used in the context of nuclear materials. | Example:  The nonfissility of this isotope makes it unsuitable for use in nuclear weapons.  这个同位素的不可裂变性使其不适合用于核武器。 | 近义词:  infissile, unfissile | 反义词: fissile, fissionable | 词性: adjective
