documentarian:/ˌdɒkjuˈmentəriən/| n. |纪录片制作人；纪录片导演|A person who makes documentary films.  Documentarians often spend years researching and filming their subjects.|例句：Ken Burns is a renowned documentarian known for his historical documentaries.  肯·伯恩斯是一位以其历史纪录片而闻名的著名纪录片制作人。|例句：The young documentarian spent five years embedded with a remote tribe for her latest film.这位年轻的纪录片制作人为了她的最新电影，花了五年时间与一个偏远的部落一起生活。|近义词：filmmaker, documentary director |反义词：fiction writer, novelist
