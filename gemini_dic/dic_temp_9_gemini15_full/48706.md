defibrinate:/diːˈfaɪbrɪneɪt/| vt.|去除纤维蛋白|To remove fibrin from (a substance, especially blood).  This process is often used in preparing blood products for transfusion, removing the clotting agent fibrin to prevent coagulation. |The blood was defibrinated before being used in the transfusion.  血液在输血前被除去了纤维蛋白。


The donated blood needs to be defibrinated to prevent clotting. 捐献的血液需要去除纤维蛋白以防止凝结。|近义词：deproteinate, defibrinogenate |反义词：fibrinate
