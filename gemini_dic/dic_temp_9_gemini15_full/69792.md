fern:/fɜːrn/| n. |蕨类植物|A fern is a flowerless plant that has feathery or leafy fronds and reproduces by spores. Ferns are found in a wide range of habitats, from tropical rainforests to temperate forests.  They are often used in gardens and landscaping for their ornamental value.|The ferns in the garden were lush and green.|近义词：pteridophyte|反义词:flowering plant
