re-accepting:/ˌriːəkˈsɛptɪŋ/| v. |重新接受，再次接受 |The process of re-accepting a previously rejected application or proposal.  It implies a previous rejection followed by a change of decision.|例句：After reviewing the revised proposal, the committee decided to re-accept it.  委员会在审查了修改后的提案后，决定重新接受它。


例句：The university is re-accepting applications from students who were initially rejected due to a clerical error. 大学正在重新接受那些由于文书错误而最初被拒绝的学生的申请。


近义词：accept again, readmit, reinstate |反义词：reject, refuse, decline |动词
