escribed:/ɪˈskraɪbd/| adj.|外切的；外接的 | Described in geometry, a circle or polygon is escribed to another polygon if it is tangent to one of the sides of the polygon and the extensions of the other sides.  In other words, it touches one side internally and the extensions of the other sides externally.  |例句：The escribed circle is tangent to one side of the triangle and the extensions of the other two sides.  外切圆与三角形的一条边相切，并与另外两条边的延长线相切。


The escribed circle of a triangle is larger than the inscribed circle. 三角形的外接圆大于内切圆。


近义词：exscribed, tangential(在某些几何上下文中) | 反义词：inscribed


词源：来源于拉丁语“exscribere”，意为“to write out; to copy out”。  几何学上的含义是后来发展出来的，基于其与内切圆（inscribed）的相对关系。  前缀"ex-"表示"外部的"，而"scribe"与"写"、"画"有关，因此引申出"外接"的含义。
