marveled:/ˈmɑːvəld/ | v. |惊叹，惊奇 |To be filled with wonder or astonishment; to feel or express great surprise or admiration. |例句：I marveled at the beauty of the sunset. 我惊叹于日落的美丽。|例句：We marveled at her skill on the piano. 我们惊叹于她精湛的钢琴技巧。|近义词：wondered, admired, amazed |反义词：ignored, disregarded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I marvel at the complexity of the human brain. = 我惊叹于人脑的复杂性。
They marvel at the speed of modern technology. = 他们惊叹于现代科技的速度。


一般过去时:
She marveled at the intricate details of the painting. = 她惊叹于绘画的复杂细节。
We marveled at the size of the giant redwood tree. = 我们惊叹于巨型红杉树的大小。


现在完成时:
I have marveled at the resilience of nature many times. = 我多次惊叹于大自然的韧性。
They have marveled at the artist's talent throughout his career. = 在他整个职业生涯中，他们一直惊叹于这位艺术家的才能。


过去完成时:
Before seeing the Grand Canyon, I had never marveled at such a breathtaking sight. = 在看到大峡谷之前，我从未惊叹于如此令人惊叹的景象。
Having completed the marathon, they marveled at their own endurance. = 完成马拉松后，他们惊叹于自己的耐力。


将来时:
We will marvel at the progress in space exploration in the coming decades. = 在未来几十年里，我们将惊叹于太空探索的进步。
She will marvel at the beauty of the ancient ruins. = 她将惊叹于古代遗址的美丽。


虚拟语气:
If I had seen the aurora borealis, I would have marveled at its beauty. = 如果我看到北极光，我就会惊叹于它的美丽。
I wish I could marvel at the wonders of the universe more often. = 我希望我能更频繁地惊叹于宇宙的奇迹。


被动语态:
The beauty of the stars was marveled at by the astronomers. = 天文学家们惊叹于星星的美丽。
The athlete's performance was marveled at by the entire stadium. = 整个体育场的人都惊叹于这位运动员的表现。


疑问句:
Do you marvel at the ingenuity of human inventions? = 你惊叹于人类发明的独创性吗？
Wouldn't you marvel at the power of nature? = 你难道不会惊叹于大自然的力量吗？


否定句:
He didn't marvel at the politician's speech. = 他对这位政治家的演讲并不感到惊叹。
They did not marvel at the ordinary events of daily life. = 他们对日常生活中普通的事情并不感到惊叹。
