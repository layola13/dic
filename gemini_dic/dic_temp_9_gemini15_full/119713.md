midshipman:/ˈmɪdʃɪpmən/| n. |见习军官，海军学员|A midshipman is a student at a naval academy or a junior officer in a navy.  They are typically in training to become commissioned officers.|例句：He graduated from the Naval Academy as a midshipman. 他从海军学院毕业成为一名见习军官。|例句：The midshipmen were practicing sailing maneuvers. 海军学员们正在练习航海操练。|近义词：cadet, officer candidate|反义词：admiral, captain (in terms of rank)
