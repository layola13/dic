counterclaimants: /ˌkaʊntərˈkleɪmən(t)s/ | n. |反诉原告，反诉方 |Those who file a counterclaim in a lawsuit.  They are essentially plaintiffs in a counter-suit. | Example: The original plaintiff’s case was weakened by the strong counterclaimants' evidence. 原告的案件因反诉方的有力证据而减弱。|近义词:respondents (in the context of a counterclaim), defendants (in the context of their own counter-suit)|反义词:claimants (in the original suit)
