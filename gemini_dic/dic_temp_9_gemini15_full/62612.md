enfolded:/ɪnˈfoʊld/ | vt. | 包裹；包含；围绕 | To wrap or enclose completely; to involve or encompass.  | The mountain range enfolded the valley in a protective embrace.  山脉以保护性的拥抱包裹着山谷。 | The comforting silence enfolded her. 令人欣慰的寂静环绕着她。| encompass, envelop, surround | unravel, expose | 动词
