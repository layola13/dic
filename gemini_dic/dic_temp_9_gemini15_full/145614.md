overconservativeness:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəs/ | n. |过度保守；过分谨慎 |Excessive adherence to traditional methods or established practices; extreme caution or unwillingness to take risks.  This can manifest in various areas, including politics, economics, and social behavior.  It often implies a resistance to change or innovation, sometimes to a degree that hinders progress. |The company's overconservativeness prevented it from embracing new technologies and ultimately led to its decline. 公司的过度保守使其无法采用新技术，最终导致其衰落。|近义词：extreme caution, excessive prudence, undue restraint, rigidity |反义词：radicalism, progressivism, recklessness, daring
