north:/nɔːrθ/|adj., n., adv.|北方；北部；向北|The word "north" indicates the direction opposite to the south, typically considered as the top of a map. It can be used as an adjective, noun, or adverb.|例句：The north wind is blowing. 北风在吹。  The north of England is known for its beautiful countryside. 英格兰北部以其美丽的乡村而闻名。|近义词：northern|反义词：south
