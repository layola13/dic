deforesters:/dɪˈfɒrɪstərz/ | n. |毁林者；采伐森林的人|People who cut down forests.  Deforesters are often criticized for their environmental impact.|例句：The illegal deforesters were finally caught and prosecuted. 非法采伐森林者最终被抓获并起诉。

The rampant activities of deforesters have led to significant habitat loss. 猖獗的毁林活动导致了严重的栖息地丧失。|近义词：loggers, forest clearers|反义词：reforesters, conservationists|词性:名词
