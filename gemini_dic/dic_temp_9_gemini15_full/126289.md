mynheer:/mənˈhɪər/| n. |先生，阁下（荷兰语）|A Dutch term of address for a man, equivalent to "mister" or "sir".  It is an archaic and somewhat formal term, rarely used in modern English. The word reflects the historical influence of Dutch culture in certain regions.|例句：The old woman addressed the gentleman as "Mynheer." 老妇人称呼那位先生为“先生”。|近义词：mister, sir, gentleman |反义词:N/A
