rockingham:/ˈrɒkɪŋəm/| n. adj.|罗金汉姆（地名，陶器）；罗金汉姆式的|Rockingham refers to a type of English earthenware known for its creamy glaze and often featuring decorative designs.  It can also refer to a town in Northamptonshire, England. As an adjective, it describes things related to Rockingham pottery or the town.|例句：The antique shop displayed a beautiful collection of Rockingham pottery.  这家古董店展示了一套精美的罗金汉陶器。|例句：She visited Rockingham, Northamptonshire during her travels in England.  她在英格兰旅行期间参观了北安普顿郡的罗金汉镇。|近义词：earthenware, pottery|反义词：N/A
