deciduity:/ˌdɛsɪˈdjuːɪti/ | n. |落叶性；脱落性；暂时性|The quality or state of being deciduous; the shedding of leaves, antlers, etc., at the end of a growing season.  Deciduity is a characteristic of many plants and animals.  |例句：The deciduity of the trees in autumn creates a beautiful display of color. 秋天树木的落叶形成了美丽的色彩。The high deciduity of the deer's antlers is an adaptation to their environment. 鹿角的高脱落率是它们对环境的一种适应。|近义词：shedding, defoliation |反义词：evergreenness, persistence |名词
