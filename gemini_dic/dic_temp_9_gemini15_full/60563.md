elastic:/ɪˈlæstɪk/| adj., n.|有弹性的；松紧带|Able to stretch and return to the original shape or size; a material that possesses this property.  |例句：This fabric is very elastic. 这块布料很有弹性。
例句：She wore an elastic band in her hair. 她头上戴着橡皮筋。|近义词：flexible, stretchy, resilient|反义词：rigid, inflexible, stiff

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rubber band is elastic. = 这根橡皮筋很有弹性。
This material is highly elastic. = 这种材料非常有弹性。

一般过去时:
The elastic snapped. = 橡皮筋断了。
The band was elastic and stretched easily. = 这条带子很有弹性，很容易拉伸。

现在完成时:
The material has remained elastic despite repeated stretching. = 尽管反复拉伸，这种材料仍然保持弹性。
The elastic has lost some of its elasticity. = 橡皮筋失去了一些弹性。

过去完成时:
The fabric had been elastic before it was damaged. = 这块布料在损坏之前是有弹性的。
The band had stretched too much and lost its elasticity. = 这条带子拉伸过度，失去了弹性。

将来时:
This new material will be more elastic than the old one. = 这种新材料将比旧材料更有弹性。
We expect the elastic to remain functional for many years. = 我们预计这条橡皮筋还能使用很多年。

虚拟语气:
If this were more elastic, it would be more useful. = 如果这个更有弹性，它将更有用。
I wish this material were as elastic as it once was. = 我希望这种材料能像以前一样有弹性。

被动语态:
The fabric is stretched and then released to test its elasticity. = 布料被拉伸然后释放以测试其弹性。
The elastic band was overstretched and broken. = 橡皮筋被过度拉伸而断裂了。

疑问句:
Is this material elastic? = 这种材料有弹性吗？
How elastic is this rubber band? = 这根橡皮筋弹性如何？

否定句:
This material is not elastic at all. = 这种材料完全没有弹性。
The rope is not elastic; it will break if stretched too far. = 这根绳子没有弹性；如果拉得太远就会断。


PHRASE->

elastic properties: 弹性特性


词源:
The word "elastic" is derived from the Greek word "ἐλαστικός" (elastikos), meaning "capable of being drawn out or driven," from "ἐλαύνειν" (elaunein), meaning "to drive, push, or urge."  This reflects the material's ability to stretch and return to its original form.
