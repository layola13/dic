nonexportation: /ˌnɒnˌɛkspɔːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不出口；禁运 | The policy of refusing to export goods, often for political reasons.  This term usually refers to a collective agreement or policy among producers or a government to refrain from exporting certain goods.  | Example: The colonies engaged in nonexportation agreements to pressure the British government. =殖民地采取不出口协议来向英国政府施压。  The nonexportation of timber led to a shortage in the European market. =木材的不出口导致欧洲市场短缺。 | 近义词：embargo, boycott, restriction | 反义词：exportation, free trade | 名词
