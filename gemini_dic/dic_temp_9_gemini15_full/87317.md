herniae:/ˈhɜːniːeɪ/| n. |疝气（复数）|Herniae are protrusions of an organ or part of an organ through the wall of the cavity that normally contains it.  This often involves the intestines protruding through a weakened area in the abdominal wall.  Different types of hernias exist, depending on their location. |The patient was diagnosed with multiple hernias requiring surgical repair.|近义词：protrusions, ruptures |反义词：无
