/laɪk/ | v. | 喜欢，喜爱 | to feel pleasure or enjoyment in something or someone; to find something or someone attractive or pleasing. | I like chocolate. 我喜欢巧克力。 | love, adore, enjoy | dislike, hate, detest | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I like cats. = 我喜欢猫。
She likes to read. = 她喜欢阅读。

一般过去时:
I liked that movie. = 我喜欢那部电影。
They liked playing soccer. = 他们喜欢踢足球。

现在完成时:
I have liked her since childhood. = 从小我就喜欢她。
We have liked living here. = 我们一直喜欢住在这里。

过去完成时:
I had liked her before I met him. = 在我遇见他之前我就喜欢她了。
They had liked the plan before the changes. = 他们在修改之前就喜欢这个计划了。

将来时:
I will like this job, I think. = 我想我会喜欢这份工作的。
They will like the present. = 他们会喜欢这份礼物的。

虚拟语气:
I wish I liked spicy food. = 我希望我喜欢辛辣的食物。
If I liked him, I would tell him. = 如果我喜欢他，我会告诉他。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)  *Like* is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a feeling or preference, not an action done to someone.


疑问句:
Do you like coffee? = 你喜欢咖啡吗？
Does she like swimming? = 她喜欢游泳吗？

否定句:
I don't like broccoli. = 我不喜欢西兰花。
He doesn't like loud music. = 他不喜欢吵闹的音乐。


PHRASE->
like this = 像这样
like that = 像那样
would like to = 想，想要
look like = 看起来像
it's like = 就像
