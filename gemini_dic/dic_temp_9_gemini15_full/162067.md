pre-Carboniferous:/ˌpriːkɑːrbənɪˈfɛrəs/| adj.| 前石炭纪的 | Existing or occurring before the Carboniferous period.  The term refers to the geological time period preceding the Carboniferous period, encompassing various earlier eras and periods.  It's primarily used in geology and paleontology. | Example: Pre-Carboniferous rocks often contain fossils of early plants and invertebrates.  前石炭纪的岩石通常含有早期植物和无脊椎动物的化石。| 近义词: pre-Mississippian (if referring to a specific sub-period) | 反义词: post-Carboniferous | 词性:形容词
