revisionist:/rɪˈvɪʒənɪst/| adj. n. |修正主义者；修正主义的 |  Relating to or characterized by the revision of accepted ideas or beliefs, often with a political or ideological motivation.  A revisionist often reinterprets past events or policies, sometimes to justify present actions or to promote a particular agenda. | 例句：His revisionist history of the war downplayed the role of the army.  他的战争修正主义史轻描淡写了军队的作用。

例句：The professor presented a revisionist interpretation of the Civil War. 教授提出了对内战的一种修正主义解释。


近义词：reinterpretative, re-evaluative, reinterpreting

反义词：orthodox, traditionalist


词性: adjective, noun
