reaffirmation: /ˌriːæfɜːrˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 重申；再次确认；坚定 | The act of reaffirming; a statement that reaffirms something.  It implies a renewed or strengthened commitment to a belief, promise, or position. |例句：The president's speech served as a reaffirmation of his commitment to economic reform.  总统的演讲是对其致力于经济改革的再次确认。

例句：The contract included a reaffirmation of their partnership. 合同中包含了他们合作关系的重申。


近义词：confirmation, reiteration, reassertion, endorsement | 反义词：repudiation, denial, rejection |  词性：名词
