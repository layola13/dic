/liːz/| n.| 许可，允许；租赁；阅读；|The act of giving permission; the state of being permitted; renting or leasing something; the act of reading.  |例句：They granted him lese of the land. 他们准许他租赁那块土地。|近义词：permission, authorization, lease, reading|反义词：prohibition, denial, ban


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives lese to his tenant. = 他允许他的租户。
She leases her property to others. = 她把她的房产出租给别人。


一般过去时:
The landlord gave me lese of the apartment. = 房东允许我租这套公寓。
They leased the building to a large company. = 他们把那栋楼租给了一家大公司。


现在完成时:
We have given lese to several applicants. = 我们已经允许了几位申请人。
They have leased the space for their new office. = 他们已经租好了新办公室的场地。


过去完成时:
She had given lese to him before the contract was signed. = 在合同签署之前，她已经允许他了。
The company had leased the equipment before the project started. = 在项目开始之前，公司已经租赁了设备。


将来时:
They will give lese to the winner of the competition. = 他们将允许比赛的获胜者。
He will lease his car for a short period. = 他将短期租赁他的汽车。


虚拟语气:
If they gave me lese, I would be happy. = 如果他们允许我，我会很高兴。

被动语态:
Lese was given to the students to use the library. = 学生们被允许使用图书馆。

疑问句:

否定句:
