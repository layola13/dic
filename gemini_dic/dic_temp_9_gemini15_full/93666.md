imbues:/ɪmˈbjuːz/| vt.|灌输；使充满；浸染|To fill or permeate (something) with a quality, feeling, or substance.|The sunset imbued the sky with vibrant colors. 夕阳给天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：infuse, instill, imbue with|反义词：deprive, drain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist imbues his paintings with deep emotion.  艺术家在他的画作中灌注了深厚的情感。
This fabric imbues a sense of luxury. 这块布料给人一种奢华的感觉。


一般过去时:
The experience imbued her with a newfound confidence.  这段经历使她获得了新的自信。
The old house was imbued with a sense of history.  这所老房子充满了历史感。


现在完成时:
Years of hardship have imbued her with resilience.  多年的磨难使她变得坚韧。
The teacher has imbued her students with a love of learning.  老师已经给她的学生灌输了对学习的热爱。


过去完成时:
He had imbued the story with a sense of mystery before he told it. 他在讲述故事之前已经赋予了它一种神秘感。
The painting had been imbued with a unique energy by the artist. 画家已经赋予这幅画独特的能量。


将来时:
The upcoming exhibition will imbue the gallery with a vibrant atmosphere.  即将举行的展览将使画廊充满活力。

虚拟语气:
If the music could imbue the room with joy, it would be a miracle. 如果音乐能使房间充满喜悦，那将是一个奇迹。
I wish the poem could imbue the reader with a sense of peace. 我希望这首诗能给读者带来平静的感觉。


被动语态:
The painting was imbued with rich symbolism. 这幅画充满了丰富的象征意义。
The fabric is imbued with a subtle fragrance. 布料散发着淡淡的香味。


疑问句:
Does this experience imbue you with any new insights?  这段经历给你带来了什么新的见解？
What qualities does the author imbue his characters with? 作者赋予他的人物哪些特质？


否定句:
The movie failed to imbue the audience with any sense of excitement. 这部电影未能使观众感到兴奋。
The bland food didn't imbue the meal with any flavor.  清淡的食物并没有使这顿饭有任何味道。
