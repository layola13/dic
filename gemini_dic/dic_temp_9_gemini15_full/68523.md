fanciers:/ˈfænsiərz/| n.|爱好者，鉴赏家|People who are enthusiastic and knowledgeable about a particular subject, often collecting or breeding related items.|例句：He's a fancier of rare stamps. 他是稀有邮票的爱好者。 |近义词：enthusiasts, connoisseurs, collectors|反义词:ignoramus, novice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Bird fanciers carefully select their breeding pairs. = 养鸟爱好者仔细挑选他们的种鸟。
Stamp fanciers often attend auctions to find rare items. = 邮票爱好者经常参加拍卖会寻找稀有物品。

一般过去时:
The cat fanciers judged the cats based on their breed standards. = 猫爱好者根据品种标准对猫进行评判。
Flower fanciers admired the vibrant colors of the roses. = 花卉爱好者欣赏玫瑰鲜艳的颜色。

现在完成时:
She has become a renowned dog fancier over the years. = 多年来，她已成为著名的养狗爱好者。
They have collected many rare orchids, showcasing their expertise as orchid fanciers. = 他们收集了许多稀有的兰花，展示了他们作为兰花爱好者的专业知识。

过去完成时:
Before the show, the pigeon fanciers had meticulously prepared their birds. = 展览前，鸽子爱好者们精心准备了他们的鸽子。
The antique car fanciers had already researched the history of the vehicles before the sale. = 古董车爱好者在拍卖前已经研究了车辆的历史。

将来时:
The fish fanciers will attend the annual aquarium show next month. = 鱼类爱好者们将于下个月参加年度水族馆展览。
Next year, the bird fanciers will be competing for the national championship. = 明年，养鸟爱好者们将角逐全国冠军。

虚拟语气:
If I were a bonsai fancier, I would have a beautiful collection. = 如果我是盆景爱好者，我会拥有一个美丽的收藏。
I wish I were a knowledgeable coin fancier; I could understand the value of these rare pieces. = 我希望我是一个有知识的钱币爱好者；我可以理解这些稀有物品的价值。

被动语态:
The winning entries were judged by experienced orchid fanciers. = 获奖作品由经验丰富的兰花爱好者评判。
The rare butterflies were carefully examined by the insect fanciers. = 稀有的蝴蝶被昆虫爱好者仔细检查。

疑问句:
Are you a fancier of classic cars? = 你是经典汽车爱好者吗？
Is he a serious fancier of antique furniture? = 他是认真的古董家具爱好者吗？

否定句:
I'm not a fancier of spicy food. = 我不喜欢辛辣食物。
She's not a fancier of modern art. = 她不喜欢现代艺术。
