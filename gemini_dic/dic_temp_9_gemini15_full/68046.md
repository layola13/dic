facts:/fæktz/ | n. | 事实，真相 |  Information known to be true, or a piece of information that is known to be true.  | The facts of the case are still unclear.  这起案件的事实仍不清楚。 | truth, reality, details | fiction, falsehood, lie, myth | 名词


USAGE->

The facts support his claim. = 事实支持他的说法。
These facts are undeniable. = 这些事实是不可否认的。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

将来时:
