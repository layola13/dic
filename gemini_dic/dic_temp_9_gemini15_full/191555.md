scholarships: /ˈskɒləʃɪps/ | n. |奖学金 |Financial aid given to students to help pay for their education.  Scholarships are typically awarded based on merit, academic achievement, or financial need. |Many students rely on scholarships to afford college.  许多学生依靠奖学金来支付大学费用。|  Financial aid, grants, bursaries |  Debt, tuition fees |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He receives a scholarship every year. = 他每年都获得奖学金。
She applies for scholarships annually. = 她每年都申请奖学金。


一般过去时:
I won a scholarship to Oxford University. = 我获得了一份牛津大学的奖学金。
He applied for a scholarship but didn't get it. = 他申请了奖学金，但没有获得。


现在完成时:

He has applied for many scholarships but hasn't been successful yet. = 他申请过许多奖学金，但尚未成功。


过去完成时:
Before starting university, she had already secured a scholarship. = 在上大学之前，她已经获得了一份奖学金。

将来时:
I hope to win a scholarship next year. = 我希望明年能获得奖学金。
