camps:/kæmps/| n.|营地，营，阵营|A camp is a place where people live in tents or temporary shelters, often for recreation or a specific purpose.  It can also refer to a group of people sharing a common belief or ideology. |例句：We went camping in the mountains last weekend.  我们上周末去山里露营了。|例句：The two political camps are fiercely opposed to each other. 两个政治阵营激烈地反对彼此。|近义词：encampment, settlement, group |反义词：home, residence
