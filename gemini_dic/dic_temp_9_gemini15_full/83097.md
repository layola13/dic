gussets:/ˈɡʌsɪts/| n.|三角形布块，角撑，嵌块|Reinforcements, typically triangular pieces of fabric, sewn into a garment to strengthen or shape a particular area, such as a pocket, knee, or elbow. They can also add style.|例句：The jeans have gussets at the crotch for added durability. 这条牛仔裤在裤裆处有三角形布块，增加了耐用性。The sail's gussets were reinforced with extra stitching. 帆的角撑用额外的缝线加固了。|近义词：reinforcements, patches, inserts|反义词:weakenings
