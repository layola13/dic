marginalia:/ˌmɑːrdʒɪˈneɪliə/| n. | 页边批注；书边注记 | Notes, comments, or illustrations written in the margins of a book or manuscript.  Marginalia can range from simple annotations to elaborate drawings and diagrams. They provide insight into the reader's thoughts, reactions, and understanding of the text. | Example: The professor's marginalia were extensive, filled with insightful comments and cross-references.  教授的页边批注非常详尽，充满了有见地的评论和交叉引用。 | 近义词：annotations, notes, comments | 反义词：text (in the sense of the main body of the text)
