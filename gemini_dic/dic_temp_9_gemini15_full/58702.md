dumpling: /ˈdʌmplɪŋ/ | n. |饺子；汤圆；类似饺子的食物 | A small ball of dough, often containing meat or vegetables, that is boiled, steamed, or fried.  Dumplings are a popular dish in many cultures, with variations in ingredients and preparation methods. |

例句：

I made a batch of delicious pork dumplings for dinner.  我做了满满一锅美味的猪肉饺子当晚餐。

She enjoyed the steamed dumplings at the dim sum restaurant.  她在点心餐厅享用了美味的蒸饺。


近义词：boil, ravioli, pierogi, wonton


反义词：无


词性：名词


词源:

The word "dumpling" originated in the Middle English word "dumpynge," which first appeared in the 14th century.  It likely derives from the verb "to dump," meaning to drop something heavily, referring to the way the dough is dropped into boiling water during cooking.  The "-ling" suffix indicates a small or diminutive form.  The word's evolution reflects the simple and direct method of preparing this food.
