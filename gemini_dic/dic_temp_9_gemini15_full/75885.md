fundamentalism:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪzəm/ | n. | 原教旨主义；基本主义 |  The belief in and strict adherence to fundamental principles, often interpreted literally and traditionally, especially in religion or politics.  This often leads to a rejection of modern interpretations and a desire to return to perceived earlier purity. |  例句：The rise of religious fundamentalism is a concern in many parts of the world.  世界许多地区宗教原教旨主义的兴起令人担忧。

例句：Political fundamentalism often leads to intolerance and oppression. 政治原教旨主义常常导致不容忍和压迫。


近义词：traditionalism, orthodoxy, purism | 反义词：modernism, liberalism, secularism
