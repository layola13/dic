presentiments:/ˌpreziˈmentmənts/| n. |预感，预兆 |A feeling of something about to happen, especially something bad.  A presentiment is a strong feeling that something, especially something unpleasant, is going to happen. |例句：She had a presentiment of disaster. 她有一种灾难将要发生的预感。


例句：A presentiment of doom hung over the meeting.  一场灾难的预感笼罩着会议。


近义词：foreboding, premonition, hunch, feeling


反义词：certainty, assurance


词性：名词
