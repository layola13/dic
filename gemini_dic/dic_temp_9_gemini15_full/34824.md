cling:/klɪŋ/ | vi. & vt. |粘着；依附；坚持；紧贴|To hold on tightly to something; to adhere to something; to remain attached to something; to persist in something.|例句：The child clung to his mother's leg. 孩子紧紧抓住他母亲的腿。  The climber clung to the rock face. 登山者紧紧抓住岩壁。|近义词：adhere, stick, hold on, grasp|反义词：release, let go, detach


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ivy clings to the wall.  = 爬山虎攀附在墙上。
He clings to his old beliefs. = 他坚持他的旧信仰。


一般过去时:
She clung to the hope of rescue. = 她抱着一线希望获救。
The frightened bird clung to the branch. = 受惊的鸟儿紧紧抓住树枝。


现在完成时:
I have clung to this idea for years. = 我多年来一直坚持这个想法。
He has clung to power despite the criticism. = 尽管受到批评，他仍然 clinging to power.


过去完成时:
She had clung to the raft for hours before being rescued. = 在获救之前，她紧紧抓住木筏几个小时。
They had clung to the hope that he would return. = 他们一直抱有他会回来的希望。


将来时:
I will cling to my principles no matter what. = 无论发生什么，我都会坚持我的原则。
The small boat will cling to the shore during the storm. = 在暴风雨中，小船会紧贴着海岸。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would cling to the opportunity. = 如果我是你，我会抓住这个机会。
I wish I had clung to my dreams instead of giving up. = 我希望我没有放弃而是坚持我的梦想。


被动语态:
The poster was clinging to the wall. = 海报粘在墙上。
The child should be held tightly and not allowed to cling to anything that might cause harm.  = 应该紧紧抱着孩子，不要让他抓住任何可能造成伤害的东西。


疑问句:
Why does the mud cling to your shoes? = 为什么泥巴粘在你的鞋子上？
Does he still cling to that outdated technology? = 他还坚持使用那种过时的技术吗？


否定句:
The paint didn't cling to the damp wood. = 油漆没有粘在潮湿的木头上。
He refused to cling to the past. = 他拒绝执着于过去。


PHRASE->
cling to:  坚持；依附
cling on: 坚持下去
cling together: 紧紧抱在一起
cling to life: 顽强地活着
cling to hope: 怀抱希望
