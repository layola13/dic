precociousness:/prɪˈkoʊʃəsnəs/| n. |早熟；早慧；过早成熟|The state or quality of being precocious; unusually advanced or mature in development, especially mental development, at an early age.|例句：Her precociousness was evident in her sophisticated conversation and intellectual curiosity.  她的早熟体现在她成熟的谈吐和求知欲上。

例句：The child's precociousness in mathematics surprised his teachers.  孩子在数学方面的早熟让他的老师们感到惊讶。


近义词：precosity, forwardness, maturity (in a young person) |反义词：backwardness, immaturity, retardation
