peony:/ˈpiːəni/| n.| 牡丹 | A peony is a flowering plant with large, showy flowers.  They are known for their beautiful and often fragrant blooms.  Many varieties exist, with colors ranging from white and pink to red and yellow. |例句：The garden was filled with the vibrant colors of blooming peonies. 花园里盛开着色彩鲜艳的牡丹花。

The peony bushes were heavy with fragrant blossoms. 牡丹灌木丛上开满了芬芳的花朵。|近义词：rose, flower |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与不那么艳丽的花朵对比)|词性:名词
