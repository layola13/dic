sea-change:/ˈsiːtʃeɪndʒ/ | n. |巨大的变化；彻底的改变 | A sea change is a significant and transformative alteration, often implying a fundamental shift in perspective, circumstances, or conditions. It suggests a profound and sweeping change, like the dramatic alteration of the sea itself.  | Example: The company underwent a sea change after the new CEO took over.  这家公司在新任CEO接手后发生了翻天覆地的变化。 |近义词：transformation, metamorphosis, revolution, upheaval | 反义词：stasis, stagnation, continuity | 名词
