outechoes: /ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊz/ | v. | 超过回声；盖过…的声音；比…更响亮 |  To exceed or surpass in sound; to be louder than.  |

例句：

The roar of the crowd outechoed the band's music. = 人群的喧嚣盖过了乐队的音乐。

The speaker's voice outechoed the whispers in the back of the room. = 演讲者的声音盖过了房间后面窃窃私语的声音。


近义词：overshadow, surpass, exceed, outdo, drown out

反义词：be muted by, be drowned out by (in the sense of being less loud)

词性: 动词
