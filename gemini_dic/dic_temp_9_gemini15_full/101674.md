irreligion:/ˌɪrɪˈlɪdʒən/| n. |不信教；无宗教信仰|The lack of religious belief; the state of not being religious.  Irreligion encompasses a wide range of perspectives, from atheism and agnosticism to secular humanism and indifference to religion. |例句：His irreligion was a source of conflict with his family. = 他的不信教是他和家人冲突的根源。

例句：The rise of irreligion in Western societies has been a subject of much debate. = 西方社会不信教现象的兴起一直是许多争论的话题。
