marathoner:/ˈmærəθɑːnər/| n.|马拉松运动员|A person who runs marathons.  A marathoner needs significant endurance and training.|例句：He's a seasoned marathoner, having completed over twenty races. 他是一位经验丰富的马拉松运动员，已经完成了二十多场比赛。

例句：She trained rigorously to become a competitive marathoner. 她刻苦训练以成为一名有竞争力的马拉松运动员。


近义词：marathon runner, long-distance runner|反义词：None|词性：名词
