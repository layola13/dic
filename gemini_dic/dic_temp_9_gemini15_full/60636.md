elect's: /ɪˈlɛkts/ | v. | 选择；选举；推选 |  This is the contraction of "elect is" or "elect has". It's a verb form.  The meaning depends on the context, generally referring to the act of choosing or selecting someone for a position, often through a vote.  |


例句：The committee elect's a new chair every year. = 委员会每年选举一位新主席。

The president elect's been working hard on his transition plan. = 当选总统一直在努力制定他的过渡计划。


近义词：choose, select, nominate, appoint


反义词：reject, dismiss, refuse


词性：动词 (verb)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The town elects a new mayor every four years. = 这个镇每四年选举一位新市长。
The jury elects its foreman. = 陪审团选举其组长。


一般过去时:
The people elected him president. = 人民选举他为总统。
They elected a new leader yesterday. = 他们昨天选举了一位新领导。


现在完成时:
The school has elected a new principal. = 学校已经选举了一位新校长。
They have elected a new chairperson for this year's conference. = 他们已经选出了今年会议的主席。


过去完成时:
The club had elected a new president before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，俱乐部已经选出了一位新主席。
Before the meeting, they had elected a committee to investigate the matter. = 会议之前，他们已经选出一个委员会来调查此事。


将来时:
The voters will elect a new governor next month. = 选民们下个月将选举一位新州长。
We will elect our class representative next week. = 我们下周将选举我们的班代表。


虚拟语气:
If we elected a different leader, things might be better. = 如果我们选举一位不同的领导人，情况可能会更好。
I wish we had elected someone more experienced. = 我希望我们当初选了一个更有经验的人。


被动语态:
A new president was elected last year. = 去年选举了一位新总统。
The committee will be elected by the board of directors. = 委员会将由董事会选举产生。


疑问句:
Who will be elected mayor? = 谁将当选市长？
When will the new committee be elected? = 新委员会何时选举产生？


否定句:
The candidate was not elected. = 该候选人未当选。
They didn't elect her to the position. = 他们没有选举她担任这个职位。



PHRASE->

be elected president = 当选总统
elect a committee = 选举委员会
