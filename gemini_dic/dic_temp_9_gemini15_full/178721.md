rectangle:/ˈrɛktæŋɡəl/| n.|矩形，长方形|A plane figure with four straight sides and four right angles.  A quadrilateral with all its angles equal to 90 degrees.|The garden is a perfect rectangle.|近义词：quadrangle, oblong|反义词：circle, square


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense information is not applicable.  The usage examples will focus on different sentence structures.

Simple Sentence:
The painting is a rectangle. = 这幅画是一个矩形。
He drew a perfect rectangle. = 他画了一个完美的矩形。


Compound Sentence:
The room is rectangular, and it's quite spacious. = 房间是长方形的，而且相当宽敞。
The field is a rectangle, but it's not a square. = 这个田地是矩形，但不是正方形。


Complex Sentence:
Because the table is rectangular, it fits perfectly in the corner. = 因为桌子是长方形的，所以它完美地适合放在角落里。
Although it looks like a square, it's actually a rectangle. = 虽然它看起来像正方形，但实际上它是一个矩形。


Interrogative Sentence:
Is the shape a rectangle? = 这个形状是矩形吗？
What is the area of the rectangle? = 这个矩形的面积是多少？


Exclamatory Sentence:
What a perfectly formed rectangle! = 多么完美的矩形！
That rectangle is huge! = 那个矩形真大！
