manumission:/ˌmænjuˈmɪʃən/| n. |释放；解放；给予自由|The act of setting someone free from slavery or bondage.  It often implies a formal or legal process.  The term is usually applied to slaves or serfs. |Example: The manumission of the slaves was a momentous occasion.  奴隶的释放是一个重要的时刻。The plantation owner granted manumission to his most loyal servant.  种植园主给予了他最忠诚的仆人自由。|近义词：liberation, emancipation, release |反义词：enslavement, bondage, servitude |名词
