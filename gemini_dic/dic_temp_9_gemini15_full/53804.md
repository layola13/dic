disconcerts:/dɪsˈkənˌsɜːrts/| vt.|使不安；使困惑；使狼狈|To cause (someone) to feel unsettled or confused.|The sudden change in plans disconcerted him.  突然的计划变更让他不安。|The unexpected news disconcerted the entire team.  意外的消息让整个团队都感到困惑。|近义词：unsettle, perturb, confuse, discompose|反义词：calm, reassure, soothe
