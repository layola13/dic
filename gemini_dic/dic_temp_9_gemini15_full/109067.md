legalizing:/ˈliːɡəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使合法化；将…合法化 | The act of making something legal.  It involves the process of removing legal restrictions or prohibitions on an activity, substance, or practice. | Example:  The government is considering legalizing marijuana. 政府正在考虑将大麻合法化。 |近义词：authorizing, sanctioning, permitting | 反义词：outlawing, prohibiting, banning
