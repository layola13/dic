self-mistrust:/ˌsɛlfˈmɪsˌtrʌst/| n.|自我怀疑，缺乏自信|A lack of confidence in one's own abilities or judgment.  It can manifest as self-doubt, insecurity, and a tendency to second-guess oneself. |Example: Her self-mistrust prevented her from applying for the promotion, even though she was qualified.  她的自我怀疑阻止了她申请晋升，即使她有资格。|近义词：self-doubt, lack of confidence, insecurity, diffidence|反义词：self-confidence, self-assurance, self-belief|名词
