corre:/ˈkɔːreɪ/ | v. | 校正，改正；纠正 | To make or set right; to correct. |例句：The printer automatically corrects spelling errors. 打印机自动更正拼写错误。  |例句：He tried to correct his mistake. 他试图纠正他的错误。|近义词：amend, rectify, adjust |反义词：err, worsen

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He corrects his grammar every day. = 他每天都纠正他的语法。
She corrects the mistakes in the manuscript. = 她更正手稿中的错误。

一般过去时:
The teacher corrected the student's paper. = 老师批改了学生的试卷。
I corrected my misunderstanding. = 我纠正了我的误解。

现在完成时:
She has corrected all the errors in the document. = 她已经更正了文件中的所有错误。
He has corrected his behavior. = 他已经改正了他的行为。

过去完成时:
He had corrected the mistake before the boss noticed. = 老板发现之前他已经改正了错误。
They had corrected the data before submitting the report. = 他们在提交报告之前已经修正了数据。


将来时:
I will correct the typo later. = 我稍后会更正这个印刷错误。
She will correct the problem immediately. = 她会立即纠正这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have corrected the error earlier. = 如果我知道的话，我就会早点纠正这个错误。
I wish I had corrected the mistake before sending the email. = 我真希望在发邮件之前能纠正这个错误。

被动语态:
The spelling errors were corrected by the software. = 拼写错误由软件更正。
The mistake will be corrected soon. = 这个错误很快就会被纠正。

疑问句:
Can you correct this sentence for me? = 你能帮我更正这句话吗？
Will the errors be corrected before publication? = 在出版之前会更正错误吗？

否定句:
He did not correct his mistake. = 他没有纠正他的错误。
The system cannot correct this type of error. = 系统无法纠正这种类型的错误。
