dividend:/ˈdɪvɪdend/| n.|股息，红利|A dividend is a payment made by a corporation to its shareholders, usually as a distribution of profits.  It represents a share of the company's earnings. |Example: The company announced a generous dividend payout to its shareholders.  这家公司宣布向股东支付丰厚的股息。

The board of directors declared a quarterly dividend of $0.50 per share. 董事会宣布每股季度股息为0.50美元。|近义词：profit, distribution, payout |反义词：loss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company pays a dividend every quarter. = 公司每季度支付股息。
Investors receive dividends from their investments. = 投资者从投资中获得股息。

一般过去时:
The company paid a large dividend last year. = 公司去年支付了大量的股息。
Shareholders received their dividends on time. = 股东按时收到了他们的股息。

现在完成时:
The company has paid out substantial dividends this year. = 公司今年已支付了巨额股息。
They have consistently received dividends from that stock. = 他们一直从那只股票中获得股息。

过去完成时:
The company had already paid the dividend before the announcement. = 公司在宣布之前已经支付了股息。
Investors had not received their dividends yet. = 投资者尚未收到他们的股息。

将来时:
The company will pay a dividend next month. = 公司下个月将支付股息。
We expect to receive a dividend in the coming year. = 我们预计在来年收到股息。


虚拟语气:
If the company performed better, it would pay a higher dividend. = 如果公司业绩更好，它将支付更高的股息。
I wish the dividend payout was larger. = 我希望股息支付更大一些。


被动语态:
Dividends are paid out to shareholders quarterly. = 股息每季度支付给股东。
The dividend was declared by the board of directors. = 股息由董事会宣布。


疑问句:
What is the dividend payout for this quarter? = 本季度的股息支付是多少？
Will the company pay a dividend this year? = 公司今年会支付股息吗？


否定句:
The company did not pay a dividend last year. = 公司去年没有支付股息。
They haven't received their dividend yet. = 他们还没有收到他们的股息。
