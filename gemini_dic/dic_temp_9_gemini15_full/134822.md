nonministerial:/ˌnɒnˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/| adj.|非部长的；非内阁的|Not relating to or connected with a minister or ministry; not holding ministerial office.  The term typically refers to government employees or officials who are not members of the cabinet or government ministers.|例句：The nonministerial staff are responsible for the day-to-day running of the department.  非部长级工作人员负责部门的日常运作。

例句：Many nonministerial government positions require specialized expertise. 许多非部长级政府职位需要专门的专业知识。


近义词：non-cabinet, subordinate, civil service


反义词：ministerial, cabinet-level
