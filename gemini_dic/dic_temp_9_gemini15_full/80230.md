godzilla:/ɡɒdˈzɪlə/| n. |哥斯拉|A fictional giant monster, a radioactive dinosaur, featured in a series of Japanese films.  It is typically depicted as a destructive force of nature. |Example: Godzilla destroyed Tokyo in the movie.  哥斯拉在电影中摧毁了东京。|近义词：monster, behemoth|反义词：N/A


USAGE->

Godzilla destroys cities. = 哥斯拉摧毁城市。
Godzilla attacks the city. = 哥斯拉攻击城市。


一般过去时:
Godzilla destroyed Tokyo in 1954. = 哥斯拉在1954年摧毁了东京。
Godzilla fought Mothra. = 哥斯拉与摩斯拉战斗。
