codriver:/ˈkoʊˌdraɪvər/ | n. | 副驾驶员，领航员 | A person who sits beside the driver of a vehicle, especially in a race, and helps with navigation or other tasks.  | Example: The codriver expertly navigated the twisting mountain roads. 副驾驶员熟练地驾驭了蜿蜒的山路。  The rally car's codriver called out the upcoming turns. 拉力赛车的副驾驶员报出了即将到来的弯道。 | 近义词：navigator, assistant driver | 反义词：driver
