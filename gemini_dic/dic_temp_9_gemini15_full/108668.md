leaflike:/ˈliːflaɪk/| adj.|叶状的，像叶子的|Resembling a leaf in shape or appearance.  The term is often used to describe the shape or texture of something, not necessarily its biological nature. |例句：The delicate, leaflike petals of the orchid were stunning.  兰花的精致叶状花瓣令人惊叹。|例句：The sculptor created a leaflike design for the metalwork.  雕塑家为金属制品创作了叶状图案。|近义词：foliage-like, frondlike|反义词：needle-like, stem-like
