homolateral:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈlætərəl/ | adj. | 同侧的，位于同一侧的 |  Relating to or affecting the same side of the body.  It refers to structures or processes that are on the same side of a bilateral structure, such as the body. | Example: The homolateral reflexes were tested to assess the integrity of the nervous system.  同侧反射被测试以评估神经系统的完整性。  The homolateral spread of the infection was concerning. 感染的同侧扩散令人担忧。|近义词：ipsilateral |反义词：contralateral
