reimpression:/ˌriːɪmˈprɛʃən/| n. |再版，重印|A new printing of a book or other publication.|The publisher announced a reimpression of the bestselling novel due to high demand.  出版商宣布由于需求量大，将对畅销小说进行再版。| reprint, reissue | first edition |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The publisher reissues books that are in high demand. = 出版商会再版需求量大的书籍。
We reprint articles from our archive regularly. = 我们定期重印我们档案馆的文章。


一般过去时:
They reissued the classic novel last year. = 他们去年再版了这部经典小说。
The company reprinted the brochure after finding a printing error. = 公司在发现印刷错误后重新印刷了宣传册。


现在完成时:
They have reissued several of their most popular titles this year. = 今年他们已经再版了几本最畅销的书。
The museum has reprinted the exhibition catalog because of its popularity. = 博物馆因为展览目录很受欢迎而重新印刷了它。


过去完成时:
They had already reprinted the book before the conference. = 在会议之前他们已经重印了这本书。
The author had reissued his first novel after many years. = 作者多年后重新出版了他的第一部小说。


将来时:
They will reprint the textbook next month. = 他们下个月将重印教科书。
The magazine will reissue its special anniversary edition. = 这本杂志将重新发行其特刊周年纪念版。


虚拟语气:
If they reprinted the book, it would sell out quickly. = 如果他们重印这本书，它会很快售罄。
I wish they had reissued that album; I really wanted a copy. = 我希望他们能重新发行那张专辑；我真的很想要一张。


被动语态:
The book was reprinted several times. = 这本书重印了好几次。
The magazine's article was reissued in an anthology. = 这本杂志的文章被收录在一本选集中重新发行。


疑问句:
Will the book be reprinted? = 这本书会重印吗？
Has the magazine been reissued yet? = 这本杂志已经重新发行了吗？


否定句:
They did not reprint the book this year. = 他们今年没有重印这本书。
The publisher will not reissue the out-of-print novel. = 出版商不会再版绝版的小说。
