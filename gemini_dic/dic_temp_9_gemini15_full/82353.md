growing-up:/ˈɡroʊɪŋ ʌp/| n. adj. |成长；成年；发育|The process of a person developing from a child into an adult, including physical, emotional, and social changes.  It can also refer to the period of time during which this process occurs. |例句：Growing up in a small town shaped her worldview.  在小镇上长大塑造了她的世界观。

例句：His growing-up years were marked by hardship and adversity. 他的成长岁月充满了苦难和逆境。


近义词：development, maturation, adolescence, childhood


反义词：aging (in the sense of decline), infancy


词性：名词，形容词 (作形容词时通常用作复合词的一部分，例如：growing-up pains 成长烦恼)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is growing up so fast. = 他长得真快。
The children are growing up quickly. = 孩子们长得很快。


一般过去时:
She grew up in a big city. = 她在一个大城市长大。
They grew up together as best friends. = 他们一起长大成为最好的朋友。


现在完成时:
I have grown up a lot since I was a child. = 从小到大，我已经成长了很多。
She has grown up into a beautiful young woman. = 她已经长成一个美丽的年轻女子。


过去完成时:
He had grown up before he realized his parents' sacrifices. = 他长大后才意识到父母的牺牲。
By the time she graduated, she had grown up considerably. = 到她毕业的时候，她已经长大了不少。


将来时:
He will grow up to be a great man. = 他将会长大成为一个伟人。
They will grow up in a different world. = 他们将在不同的世界里长大。


虚拟语气:
If I had grown up in a different environment, I would be a different person. = 如果我在不同的环境中长大，我会成为一个不同的人。
I wish I had grown up with more support. = 我希望我成长过程中能得到更多支持。


被动语态:  (Growing up is rarely used in passive voice because it is an intransitive verb in this context.)


疑问句:
Where did you grow up? = 你在哪里长大的？
How did he grow up to be such a successful person? = 他是如何成长为如此成功的人的？


否定句:
He didn't grow up in a happy home. = 他没有在一个幸福的家庭中长大。
She didn't grow up with much privilege. = 她从小并没有享受到多少特权。



PHRASE->
growing-up pains 成长烦恼
growing-up experience 成长经历
growing-up years 成长岁月
growing-up process 成长过程
growing-up period 成长期
