quartering: /ˈkwɔːrtərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 四分之一；住宿；(军队)分营 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "quartering" refers to the act of dividing something into four equal parts, or the condition of being divided into four parts. It can also refer to lodging or accommodation, particularly for soldiers.  In a military context, it refers to the assignment of troops to specific locations for housing or deployment.

As a verb, "quartering" means to divide into four parts, to provide lodging for, or to station (troops) in quarters.


例句:
(Noun - division) The map showed the quartering of the city into four distinct zones.  = 地图显示该城市被划分为四个不同的区域。

(Noun - lodging)  Finding suitable quartering for the large group proved challenging. = 为这个大型团体寻找合适的住所证明具有挑战性。

(Verb - division) The cake was quartered and shared among the four friends. = 蛋糕被分成四份，分给了四个朋友。

(Verb - lodging) The army quartered its soldiers in the local school. = 军队将士兵安置在当地的学校里。
