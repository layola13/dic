laryngoscopist:/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒskəpɪst/ | n. |喉镜检查者，喉镜专家| A laryngoscopist is a medical professional skilled in performing laryngoscopy, the examination of the larynx (voice box) using a laryngoscope.  They are typically otolaryngologists (ENT doctors) or other specialists trained in this procedure. | Example: The laryngoscopist carefully examined the patient's larynx to determine the cause of their voice problems.  喉镜检查者仔细检查了病人的喉咙，以确定他们声音问题的原因。|近义词：laryngologist, ENT specialist |反义词：无
