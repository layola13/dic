newsboy:/ˈnuːzbɔɪ/ | n. |报童|A boy who sells newspapers, typically on the street.|例句：The newsboy shouted the headlines as he ran down the street. 报童一边跑下街一边大声喊着新闻标题。|近义词：newspaper seller, paperboy |反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newsboy sells newspapers every morning. = 报童每天早上卖报纸。
He delivers newspapers to houses on his route. = 他把报纸送到他路线上的各个住家。


一般过去时:
The newsboy sold all his newspapers before noon. = 报童在中午之前卖掉了所有的报纸。
He delivered the news about the big game. = 他送来了关于这场大型比赛的新闻。


现在完成时:
The newsboy has sold many newspapers this week. = 报童本周已经卖了很多报纸。
He has delivered newspapers in this neighborhood for years. = 他在这个街区送报纸已经很多年了。


过去完成时:
The newsboy had already sold out before I got there. = 我到那里之前，报童已经卖完了。
He had delivered all the papers before sunset. = 他在日落之前已经送完了所有的报纸。


将来时:
The newsboy will sell more newspapers tomorrow. = 报童明天会卖出更多报纸。
He will deliver the newspapers by bicycle. = 他将骑自行车送报纸。


虚拟语气:
If the newsboy had a bicycle, he would be faster. = 如果报童有辆自行车，他会更快。
I wish the newsboy would shout louder. = 我希望报童能喊大声点。


被动语态:
The newspapers were sold by the newsboy quickly. = 报纸很快就被报童卖掉了。
The news was delivered by the newsboy promptly. = 新闻被报童及时送达。


疑问句:
Is the newsboy still selling newspapers? = 报童还在卖报纸吗？
Where does the newsboy get his newspapers? = 报童从哪里拿报纸？


否定句:
The newsboy didn't sell many newspapers today. = 报童今天没有卖很多报纸。
He didn't deliver the papers on time. = 他没有按时送报纸。
