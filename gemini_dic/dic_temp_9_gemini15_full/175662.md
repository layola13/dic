ranting:/ˈræntɪŋ/| v. | 咆哮；怒骂；胡言乱语 |  The act of ranting involves speaking in a loud, angry, and uncontrolled manner, often expressing irrational or exaggerated complaints. It often lacks coherent structure and logical argument. | Example: He was ranting and raving about the injustice of it all. 他在为这其中的不公大发雷霆。


Example:  She spent the entire evening ranting about her terrible boss. 她整个晚上都在抱怨她糟糕的老板。


近义词： raving, fulminating, spluttering, declaiming


反义词： whispering, murmuring, calmly speaking


词性: 动词
