glare:/ɡler/| n./v.|怒视，强光|To look angrily or fiercely at someone or something.  A strong, dazzling light.|The driver glared at the other car that had cut him off. 司机怒视着那辆加塞的车。|The sun glared down on us. 太阳强烈地照耀着我们。|近义词：stare, scowl, flash|反义词：smile, beam


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glares at me every time he sees me. 他每次看到我就怒视我。
The sun glares fiercely in the desert.  沙漠中的阳光非常强烈。


一般过去时:
She glared at him after he insulted her. 他侮辱她之后，她怒视着他。
The headlights glared in my eyes. 车前灯晃得我眼睛睁不开。


现在完成时:
He has glared at me all morning. 他整个上午都在怒视我。
The sun has glared relentlessly all day.  太阳一整天都无情地炙烤着。


过去完成时:
She had glared at him before he even spoke.  在他开口之前，她就怒视着他了。
The sun had glared so intensely that the ground was scorching hot. 太阳炙烤得地面滚烫。


将来时:
He will glare at you if you don't apologize. 如果你不道歉，他会怒视你的。
The setting sun will glare off the water. 落日将照射在水面上，闪闪发光。


虚拟语气:
If he glared at me again, I would leave. 如果他再怒视我，我就走。
I wish the sun didn't glare so brightly. 我希望太阳不要那么刺眼。


被动语态:
He was glared at by the angry man. 他被那个愤怒的男人怒视着。
The room was glared at by a bright light. 房间被一盏明亮的灯照得通亮。


疑问句:
Is he glaring at us? 他在怒视我们吗？
Does the sun glare intensely today? 太阳今天很刺眼吗？


否定句:
He didn't glare at me; he just looked away. 他没有怒视我；他只是把脸转开了。
The sun didn't glare that day; it was cloudy. 那天太阳不刺眼；那天是阴天。

a death glare  凶狠的怒视
to glare back  回瞪
