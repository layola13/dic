The term often refers specifically to tennis racquets, but can also encompass badminton racquets, squash racquets, and others.| Example: He bought a new tennis racquet for the upcoming tournament. 他为即将到来的比赛买了一副新的网球拍。|近义词：bat (in some contexts), paddle|反义词：无
