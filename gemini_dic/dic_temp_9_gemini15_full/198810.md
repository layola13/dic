side-show:/ˈsaɪdʃoʊ/| n.| 附属表演；次要事件；无关紧要的事物 | A secondary or subordinate exhibition or performance, often of a less important or sensational nature than the main event. It can also refer to a minor or insignificant event or matter. | 例句：The circus had a side-show featuring a bearded lady. 马戏团有一个以长胡子女士为特色的附属表演。

The political scandal was merely a side-show compared to the economic crisis.  政治丑闻与经济危机相比只是个次要事件。|近义词：extra, adjunct, subsidiary, minor attraction|反义词：main event, centerpiece, principal attraction
