non-testable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛstəbl/| adj.|不可测试的，无法验证的|Not capable of being tested or verified.  This often applies to hypotheses or theories that lack clear, measurable outcomes or are inherently subjective.|例句：The theory is non-testable because it relies on subjective experiences.  该理论是不可测试的，因为它依赖于主观经验。

The hypothesis is non-testable due to the lack of appropriate methodology.  由于缺乏适当的方法，该假设是不可测试的。


近义词：unverifiable, unprovable, immeasurable,  incapable of testing, untestable


反义词：testable, verifiable, provable, measurable
