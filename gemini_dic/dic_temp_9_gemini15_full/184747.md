resumable:/rɪˈzuːməbl/| adj.| 可恢复的；可继续的 | Able to be resumed or continued after interruption.  It implies the ability to pick up where one left off, often after a pause or interruption. |例句：The game is resumable after saving. 游戏保存后可以继续。

The download is resumable if your internet connection drops.  如果你的网络连接中断，下载可以恢复。|近义词：continuable, restartable |反义词：irreversible, irretrievable
