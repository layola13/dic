knacky:/ˈnæki/| adj.|灵巧的；熟练的；有技巧的|Skillful and clever, especially in a practical way.  It often implies a natural talent or aptitude. |例句：He's a knacky carpenter, able to build anything from scratch. 他是一位灵巧的木匠，能够从零开始建造任何东西。

例句：She's got a knacky way of making even the most difficult tasks seem easy. 她有一种巧妙的方法，能让即使是最困难的任务看起来也很容易。


近义词：skillful, adept, dexterous, clever, ingenious, proficient

反义词：clumsy, awkward, inept, unskilled

词性:形容词
