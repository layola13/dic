scrutinised:/ˈskruːtɪnaɪzd/ | v. | 仔细检查；详细审查；细察 | To examine or inspect closely and thoroughly. | The police scrutinised the evidence carefully.  警方仔细审查了证据。|  examined, inspected, investigated, analyzed | overlooked, ignored | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They scrutinise every detail of the contract. = 他们仔细审查合同的每一个细节。
The professor scrutinises the students' essays. = 教授仔细检查学生的论文。


一般过去时:
The judge scrutinised the witness's testimony. = 法官仔细审查了证人的证词。
She scrutinised the map before setting off. = 出发前她仔细查看了地图。


现在完成时:
The committee has scrutinised the proposal thoroughly. = 委员会已经彻底审查了该提案。
He has scrutinised the data for any errors. = 他已经仔细检查了数据是否有任何错误。


过去完成时:
They had scrutinised the documents before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经仔细审查了文件。
She had scrutinised the evidence multiple times. = 她已经多次仔细审查了证据。


将来时:
The auditors will scrutinise the company's accounts. = 审计员将仔细审查公司的账目。
We will scrutinise the results carefully before making a decision. = 我们将在做出决定之前仔细审查结果。


虚拟语气:
If they had scrutinised the data more carefully, they would have found the mistake. = 如果他们更仔细地检查数据，他们就会发现错误。
I wish I had scrutinised the contract more thoroughly. = 我希望我当时更仔细地审查了合同。


被动语态:
The documents were scrutinised by the lawyers. = 文件由律师仔细审查。
The proposal is being scrutinised by the board of directors. = 该提案正在由董事会仔细审查。


疑问句:
Have the investigators scrutinised all the evidence? = 调查人员是否已经仔细审查了所有证据？
Will the committee scrutinise the budget proposal? = 委员会会审查预算提案吗？


否定句:
The police did not scrutinise the suspect's alibi sufficiently. = 警方没有充分审查嫌疑人的不在场证明。
They haven't scrutinised the details properly. = 他们没有正确地仔细检查细节。
