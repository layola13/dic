neurotoxins:/ˌnʊroʊˈtɑːksɪnz/| n. |神经毒素|Neurotoxins are substances that are poisonous or toxic to the nervous system. They can cause a range of effects, from mild discomfort to paralysis and death, depending on the type of neurotoxin, the dose, and the route of exposure.|Example:  Botulinum toxin is a potent neurotoxin that can cause botulism.|近义词: nerve toxins, neuropoisons|反义词:neuroprotectants
