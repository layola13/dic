recanting: /rɪˈkæntɪŋ/ | v. | 撤回；放弃；否认 | To withdraw or retract a statement, belief, or accusation; to renounce or disavow something previously held or asserted.  |例句：The witness recanted his testimony after realizing he had made a mistake.目击者在意识到自己犯了错误后撤回了证词。

He recanted his earlier criticisms of the government. 他撤回了之前对政府的批评。


近义词：retracting, withdrawing, repudiating, disavowing | 反义词：affirming, confirming, upholding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recants his statement every day. 他每天都撤回他的声明。
They recant their beliefs publicly. 他们公开放弃他们的信仰。

一般过去时:
She recanted her confession. 她撤回了她的供词。
The artist recanted his support for the controversial project.艺术家撤回了对这一有争议项目的支持。

现在完成时:
He has recanted his accusations against his former colleague. 他已撤回了对前同事的指控。
She has recanted her previous statements multiple times. 她多次撤回了之前的陈述。

过去完成时:
They had recanted their support before the scandal broke. 在丑闻爆发之前，他们就已经撤回了支持。
The politician had recanted his position on the issue months ago. 这位政治家几个月前就改变了他对这个问题的立场。

将来时:
He will recant his testimony in court tomorrow. 他将在明天的法庭上撤回证词。
She will likely recant her statements under pressure. 她很可能会在压力下撤回她的声明。


虚拟语气:
If he were to recant his confession, the case would be jeopardized. 如果他撤回供词，案件就会受到威胁。
I wish he had recanted his hurtful words sooner. 我真希望他早点收回那些伤人的话。

被动语态:
His testimony was recanted after further investigation. 经过进一步调查，他的证词被撤回。
The statement was quickly recanted by the spokesperson. 该发言人很快撤回了声明。

疑问句:
Will he recant his false accusations? 他会撤回他那些不实的指控吗？
Did she ever recant her earlier statements? 她有没有撤回之前的声明？

否定句:
He refused to recant his statements, despite the evidence. 尽管有证据，但他拒绝撤回他的陈述。
She did not recant her support for the cause, even under intense pressure. 即使在巨大的压力下，她也没有撤回对这项事业的支持。


PHRASE->
recant a confession: 撤回供词
