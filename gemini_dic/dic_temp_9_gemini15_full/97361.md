infracting:/ɪnˈfræktɪŋ/| v.|违反，违犯|The act of breaking a law, rule, or agreement.  To commit an infraction; to violate or infringe upon a law, rule, or right.|例句：The company was infracting copyright laws by using the image without permission.  这家公司未经许可使用该图像，侵犯了版权法。

例句：Driving over the speed limit is infracting traffic regulations. 超速驾驶违反了交通规则。


近义词：violating, breaching, infringing, transgressing|反义词：obeying, complying, conforming|词性:动词
