hedonisms:/ˈhiːdəˌnɪzəmz/| n. |享乐主义；享乐思想 |The pursuit of pleasure and self-indulgence as a way of life.  Hedonism emphasizes the importance of maximizing pleasure and minimizing pain. |例句：His hedonistic lifestyle involved excessive drinking and partying. 他享乐主义的生活方式包括过度饮酒和狂欢。

例句：The philosophy of hedonism is often criticized for its potential to lead to irresponsible behavior. 享乐主义的哲学经常因为其可能导致不负责任的行为而受到批评。


近义词：pleasure-seeking, self-indulgence, sensualism |反义词：asceticism, self-denial, puritanism |名词
