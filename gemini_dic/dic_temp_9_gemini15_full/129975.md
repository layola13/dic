non-Mongolians: /ˌnɑːn ˈmɒŋɡoʊliənz/ | n. |非蒙古人 |People who are not of Mongolian ethnicity or origin.  This term refers to individuals or groups not belonging to the Mongolian people.  The term can be used in a descriptive or sometimes subtly divisive context depending on the situation. |Example: The cultural exchange program included both Mongolians and non-Mongolians.  非蒙古人和蒙古人都参加了文化交流项目。|近义词: non-Mongols, others, foreigners (in a specific context)|反义词: Mongolians|名词
