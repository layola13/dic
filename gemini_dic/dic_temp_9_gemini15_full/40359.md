contractionary:/kənˈstrækʃənəri/| adj.|紧缩的；收缩的；限制性的|Characterized by or causing contraction or reduction, especially of the money supply.  It often refers to economic policies that reduce the money supply, credit availability, or aggregate demand to curb inflation.|例句：The central bank implemented contractionary monetary policy to combat inflation.  中央银行实施紧缩性货币政策以对抗通货膨胀。

例句：The government's contractionary fiscal policy led to a slowdown in economic growth. 政府的紧缩财政政策导致经济增长放缓。


近义词：restrictive, deflationary, austere|反义词：expansionary, stimulative, inflationary|词性:形容词
