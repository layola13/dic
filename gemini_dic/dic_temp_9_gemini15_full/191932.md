scoot:/skuːt/| v. n.|轻快地走；疾驰；滑行；（尤指）骑滑板车| to move quickly and lightly; to ride a scooter|例句：He scooted across the room to answer the phone. 他轻快地穿过房间去接电话。  例句：The children were scooting around on their scooters. 孩子们正骑着滑板车四处滑行。|近义词：dash, zip, whisk, scoot around|反义词：crawl, creep, meander|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scoots to work every day. = 他每天骑滑板车去上班。
She scoots around the park. = 她在公园里滑行。

一般过去时:
I scooted home after school. = 放学后我飞快地跑回家了。
He scooted past me without saying hello. = 他没打招呼就从我身边飞快地过去了。


现在完成时:
They have scooted off to the beach. = 他们已经飞快地去海滩了。
She has scooted through all her work already. = 她已经快速完成了所有工作。


过去完成时:
He had scooted out before I could ask him anything. = 我还没来得及问他什么，他就溜走了。
The birds had scooted away when they saw us. = 鸟儿们看到我们后就飞走了。


将来时:
I will scoot over to your place later. = 我待会儿会很快去你家。
She will scoot to the store to buy some milk. = 她会很快去商店买牛奶。


虚拟语气:
If I had a scooter, I would scoot to school every day. = 如果我有滑板车，我每天都会骑滑板车去上学。
I wish I could scoot faster. = 我希望我能滑得更快。


被动语态: (Scoot doesn't typically use the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb.  A passive construction would be unnatural.)

否定句:

I don't want to scoot in the rain. = 我不想在雨中滑行。
