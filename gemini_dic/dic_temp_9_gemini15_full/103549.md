jogtrot:/ˈdʒɒɡtrɒt/ | v. |慢跑，缓步跑 |To jogtrot means to move at a slow, steady pace, similar to a horse's jogtrot.  It implies a relatively relaxed and continuous movement, not a sprint or a fast pace. |The old horse jogtrotted along the dusty road. 老马沿着尘土飞扬的道路慢跑。  The hikers jogtrotted up the gentle slope.  徒步旅行者沿着缓坡慢跑。|amble, stroll, saunter |sprint, race, dash |动词
