diggers:/ˈdɪɡərz/| n.|挖掘者，挖掘机，(非正式)矿工|People or machines that dig.  The term can refer to individuals using shovels or other hand tools, construction equipment like excavators, or historically, miners.|例句：The diggers worked tirelessly to complete the canal. 挖掘工人们不知疲倦地工作以完成运河的建设。  The gold diggers were searching for fortune in the Klondike.淘金者们在克朗代克寻找财富。|近义词：excavators, miners, delvers|反义词：builders (in a contrasting sense)
