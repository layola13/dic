covertly:/ˈkʌvərtli/ | adv. | 秘密地；暗中地 | secretly or hidden from view;  in a way that is not openly acknowledged. | 例句：The government covertly funded the opposition party. 政府暗中资助了反对党。 | 例句：She covertly watched him from across the room. 她从房间的另一边偷偷地观察着他。|近义词：secretly, stealthily, clandestinely, furtively | 反义词：openly, publicly, overtly
