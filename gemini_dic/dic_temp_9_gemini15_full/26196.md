butt's: /bʌts/ | n. |屁股；末端；烟蒂；目标 |  The fleshy part of the human body that one sits on; the end or bottom of something; the remaining part of a cigarette after it has been smoked; a target or object of ridicule or attack. |


例句：

He fell on his butt. = 他摔在了屁股上。

The butt of the joke was John. =  这个笑话的对象是约翰。


近义词：rear end, bottom, posterior, target, end


反义词：top, beginning, front


词性：名词


词源：

源自古英语 *butte,  可能与古高地德语 *buzza "肿胀" 相关。其含义随着时间的推移而演变，从“厚实的圆形物体”发展到“屁股”。
