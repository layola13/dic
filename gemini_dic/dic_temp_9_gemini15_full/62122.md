enchondroma:/ˌɛnˈkɒndrəʊmə/| n. |软骨瘤|A benign tumor of cartilage, typically occurring in the bones of the hands and feet.  It's usually slow-growing and asymptomatic, often discovered incidentally on X-ray. |The patient presented with an enchondroma in the proximal phalanx of his left thumb.  这位病人左拇指近端指骨出现软骨瘤。| osteochondroma, chondroma | malignant bone tumor |名词
