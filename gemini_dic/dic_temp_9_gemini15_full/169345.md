promptings:/ˈprɒmptɪŋz/ | n. | 提示；鼓动；唆使 | The act of prompting; suggestions or encouragements.  It can refer to the act of giving cues or hints, or to the suggestions themselves.  |例句：His promptings led to the discovery of the truth. 他的提示导致了真相的发现。

例句：The teacher's constant promptings helped the shy student participate.老师不断的提示帮助害羞的学生参与其中。


近义词：suggestions, hints, cues, encouragements, instigations


反义词：discouragement, hindrance, obstruction


词性: 名词
