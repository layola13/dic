demotivating: /ˌdiːˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人失去动机的；令人泄气的 |  Describes something that discourages, disheartens, or makes someone less enthusiastic or motivated.  It implies a reduction in motivation or drive. |

例句：

The constant criticism was demotivating for the team. =  不断的批评使团队士气低落。

His lack of support was incredibly demotivating. = 他的缺乏支持令人极其沮丧。


近义词：discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting, discouraging, depressing, demoralizing

反义词：motivating, inspiring, encouraging, uplifting

词性：形容词
