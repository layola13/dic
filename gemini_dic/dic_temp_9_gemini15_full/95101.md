inane:/ɪˈneɪn/| adj. |愚蠢的；无意义的；无聊的 |lacking sense or meaning; silly.  The conversation was inane and pointless.  |近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, pointless, vapid |反义词：intelligent, meaningful, sensible, significant


例句：

His inane comments bored everyone at the meeting.  他的愚蠢言论让会议上的每个人都感到厌烦。

The movie's plot was completely inane. 这部电影的情节完全是无意义的。


词源:

From Middle English *inanen, from Old French *inan, from Latin inanis "empty, void".  The shift in meaning from "empty" to "silly" likely reflects a perception of emptyheadedness as a characteristic of foolishness.


用法说明:

inane 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，例如言论、想法、行为等。它强调缺乏意义和价值，通常带有轻蔑或不屑的意味。


没有针对inane的特殊语法形式，因为它是一个形容词，其用法遵循形容词的一般语法规则。
