mommies:/ˈmɑːmiz/ | n. | 妈妈们，母亲们 | The plural form of "mommy," a child's term for mother. It refers to multiple mothers.  | Example: The mommies gathered at the park for a playdate.  妈妈们聚集在公园里玩耍。 |近义词: mothers, mums | 反义词: N/A
