inactivating: /ɪnˈæktɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |使无效的；使不活动的；失活的 |  Describes something that renders something else inactive or ineffective.  It often refers to a process or substance that neutralizes or stops the activity of something, such as an enzyme or a biological process. |

例句：
The drug has an inactivating effect on the virus.  这种药物对病毒具有灭活作用。

The high temperature caused the inactivating of the enzyme. 高温导致酶失活。


近义词：deactivating, disabling, neutralizing, inhibiting

反义词：activating, enabling


词性：形容词 (present participle of inactivate)
