/ˈmoʊblɒɡz/ | n. | 手机博客；移动博客 | Mobile blogs; blogs written and accessed via mobile devices.  Moblogs are typically shorter and less formal than traditional blogs. They often include multimedia content like photos and videos. | Example:  Many journalists use moblogs to report breaking news from the field. 许多记者使用手机博客来报道现场突发新闻。 | 近义词: mobile journal, microblog | 反义词:  traditional blog
