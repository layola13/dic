pre-objective:/ˌpriːəbˈdʒɛktɪv/| adj.|预先目标的，先于目标的|Describes something that comes before or precedes the main objective or goal.  It often implies a preparatory or foundational step.|例句：The pre-objective of the research was to gather preliminary data.  这项研究的预先目标是收集初步数据。  The pre-objective phase involved extensive market research. 预先目标阶段包含广泛的市场调研。|近义词：preparatory, preliminary, antecedent|反义词：ultimate, final, consequent
