sippy:/ˈsɪpi/| n.|小杯子；奶嘴杯|A sippy cup is a cup with a lid and spout, designed for young children to drink from without spilling.|例句：My daughter loves her new sippy cup.  我的女儿喜欢她新的奶嘴杯。|例句：He spilled his juice from the sippy cup. 他把果汁从奶嘴杯里洒了。|近义词：training cup, spill-proof cup|反义词：None
