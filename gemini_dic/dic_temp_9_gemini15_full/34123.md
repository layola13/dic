cladding:/ˈklædɪŋ/| n.|包层；外层；护面|Material used to cover a building's exterior or interior walls, or other structures.  It's often used for insulation, weatherproofing, or aesthetic purposes.|The building's modern cladding is made of sleek, metallic panels. 这座建筑物的现代包层是由光滑的金属板制成的。|covering, facing, veneer, sheathing|uncovered, exposed|名词
