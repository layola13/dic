fatality:/fəˈtæləti/| n. |死亡；致命事故；死亡人数 |The death or loss of life resulting from an accident, natural disaster, or other event.  Fatalities refer to the number of deaths in a particular incident or period.|例句：The accident resulted in three fatalities. 这场事故导致三人死亡。

例句：The number of traffic fatalities has decreased this year.  今年交通事故死亡人数减少了。


近义词：death, casualty, mortality

反义词：survival, recovery
