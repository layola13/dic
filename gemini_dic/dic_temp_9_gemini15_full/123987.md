morn:/mɔːrn/| n.|清晨，早晨|Morning is the first part of the day, from sunrise to noon.|例句：The morn broke with a beautiful sunrise. 清晨伴随着美丽的日出而破晓。|近义词：morning, daybreak, dawn|反义词：evening, night


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The morn brings a new hope. = 清晨带来新的希望。
The morn usually starts with birdsong. = 清晨通常以鸟鸣开始。


一般过去时:
Yesterday morn, I woke up early. = 昨晨，我起得很早。
The morn before yesterday was cold. = 前天早晨很冷。


现在完成时:
The morn has brought good news. = 清晨带来了好消息。
Many morns have passed since we last met. = 自从我们上次见面以来，已经过去了许多早晨。


过去完成时:
By the morn, the storm had subsided. = 到早晨，风暴已经平息了。
Before the morn arrived, they had finished their work. = 在早晨到来之前，他们已经完成了工作。


将来时:
Tomorrow morn, we will leave early. = 明晨，我们将早点出发。
The morn will bring sunshine, I hope. = 希望明晨阳光普照。


虚拟语气:
If the morn were to bring rain, we would stay inside. = 如果明晨下雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish the morn would come quickly. = 我希望早晨快点到来。


被动语态:
The morn was greeted by a gentle breeze. = 清晨被轻柔的微风迎接。
The morn is often described as a time of peace and tranquility. = 清晨通常被描述为和平与宁静的时刻。


疑问句:
What time did the morn break? = 清晨几点钟破晓？
What will the morn bring? = 明晨会带来什么？


否定句:
The morn did not bring any good news. = 清晨没有带来任何好消息。
The morn was not as bright as we hoped. = 清晨不像我们希望的那样明亮。


PHRASE->
good morn = 早安 (较为正式)
till morn = 直到早晨
the morn after = 酒后第二天早晨
one morn = 某一个早晨
this morn = 今晨
