hyperplastic:/ˌhaɪpərˈplæstɪk/| adj. |增生的，过度增生的| Relating to or characterized by hyperplasia (the enlargement of an organ or tissue caused by an increase in the reproduction rate of its cells).  It often implies an abnormal increase in cell numbers.|The biopsy revealed hyperplastic changes in the tissue.|近义词：proliferative|反义词：hypoplastic
