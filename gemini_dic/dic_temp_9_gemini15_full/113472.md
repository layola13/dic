lyrebird:/ˈlaɪərˌbɜːrd/| n. |琴鸟|A lyrebird is a type of bird known for its remarkable ability to mimic sounds, including human-made noises. They are found in Australia and are prized for their elaborate tail feathers, particularly in the males, which resemble a lyre. |例句：The male superb lyrebird's mating display is a breathtaking spectacle of mimicry and dance. 雄性丽色琴鸟的求偶表演是模仿和舞蹈的令人叹为观止的景象。|The Albert's lyrebird is another species known for its vocal mimicry. 阿尔伯特琴鸟是另一种以其声音模仿而闻名的物种。|近义词：songbird, bird of paradise|反义词:|无
