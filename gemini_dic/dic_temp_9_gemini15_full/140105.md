off-loading:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪŋ/| /ˌɔːfˈloʊdɪŋ/ | n. vt.|卸载；卸货；转移负担|The act of removing or transferring a load, burden, or responsibility from something or someone.|例句：The workers are off-loading the containers from the ship. 工人们正在从船上卸下集装箱。|例句：She off-loaded her worries onto her friend. 她向朋友倾诉了她的烦恼。|近义词：unloading, transferring, shifting|反义词：loading, taking on


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The port workers offload the cargo daily. = 港口工人每天卸载货物。
They offload the trucks at the warehouse. = 他们在仓库卸下卡车上的货物。

一般过去时:
The team offloaded the project onto the new intern. = 团队将项目交给了新的实习生。
He offloaded all his belongings from the car. = 他把车上的所有行李都卸了下来。

现在完成时:
We have offloaded all the data to the cloud server. = 我们已经将所有数据卸载到云服务器。
They have offloaded the responsibility to a subcontractor.  = 他们已经将责任转移给了一家分包商。

过去完成时:
They had offloaded the heavy boxes before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经卸下了那些沉重的箱子。
She had offloaded her emotional baggage before starting therapy. = 她开始治疗前已经卸下了她的情感包袱。

将来时:
We will offload the software updates tonight. = 我们今晚将卸载软件更新。
The company will offload some of its less profitable divisions. = 公司将剥离一些盈利较低的部门。

虚拟语气:
If we offloaded the extra work, we could meet the deadline. = 如果我们卸掉额外的工作，我们就能按时完成。
I wish I had offloaded the stress earlier. = 我希望我早点卸掉压力。

被动语态:
The packages were offloaded at the distribution center. = 包裹在配送中心被卸下。
The responsibility was offloaded to the junior staff. = 责任被转移给了初级员工。

疑问句:
Have they offloaded the goods yet? = 他们已经卸货了吗？
Where will the containers be offloaded? = 集装箱将在哪里卸货？

否定句:

They did not offload the data before the system crash. = 系统崩溃前他们没有卸载数据。
