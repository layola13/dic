perfectively: /pərˈfektɪvli/ | adv. |完美地，完全地 |Perfectly and completely.  It suggests a state of absolute perfection or completeness.  The word is not commonly used and might be considered a neologism or a less common variant of "perfectly". |例句：The painting was rendered perfectively; every detail was flawlessly executed. 这幅画画得完美无瑕；每一个细节都完美无缺。


The work was perfectively done, leaving no room for improvement. 工作做得完美无缺，没有改进的空间。


近义词：perfectly, flawlessly, completely, ideally |反义词：imperfectly, incompletely, poorly
