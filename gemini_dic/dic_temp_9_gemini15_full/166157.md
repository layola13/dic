prepronounced:/ˌpriːˈpraʊnaʊnst/| adj. |预先发音的；预先宣布的|Already pronounced or announced beforehand.  It refers to something that has been spoken or declared in advance.|The prepronounced warnings saved many lives during the hurricane. 预先发出的警告在飓风期间挽救了许多人的生命。|近义词：announced beforehand, previously stated|反义词：unannounced, unstated
