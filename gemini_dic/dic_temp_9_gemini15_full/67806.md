eyesplice:/ˈaɪˌspaɪs/| n.|眼接 |A splice made by interweaving the strands of two ropes or cables, especially one made without using a special tool.  It's often used in nautical contexts and for joining things like electrical wires. |例句：The sailor expertly made an eyesplice in the frayed rope. 水手熟练地将磨损的绳索进行了眼接。  The electrician used an eyesplice to join the two electrical cables. 电工用眼接法连接了两根电缆。 |近义词：splice, joint, connection |反义词：separation, disconnection |名词
