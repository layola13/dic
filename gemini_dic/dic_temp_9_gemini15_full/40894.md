cookshops:/ˈkʊkʃɒps/| n. |小餐馆；廉价饭馆|A cookshop is a small, inexpensive restaurant or eatery, often serving simple, basic meals.  The term often implies a less formal or refined atmosphere than a restaurant. | Many cookshops in the city offer affordable lunch specials.  许多城里的廉价饭馆提供价格实惠的午餐特价。|restaurant, eatery, cafe|upscale restaurant, fine dining establishment|名词
