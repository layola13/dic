feldspath:/ˈfɛldˌspɑːθ/| n. |长石|Feldspar is a group of rock-forming minerals that are very common in the Earth's crust.  They are used in the production of ceramics, glass, and other materials.  The different types of feldspar are distinguished by their chemical composition and crystal structure.  For example, orthoclase is a common type of feldspar.|The granite countertop is primarily composed of feldspar.|近义词：orthoclase, plagioclase |反义词：无
