runcinate:/ˈrʌnsɪneɪt/| adj.|镰刀状的，弯刀状的|Having a curved, sickle-shaped tooth or margin, like a scythe.  The term is often used in botany to describe leaf margins or the teeth of leaves.|例句：The leaf margin exhibited a distinctive runcinate pattern.  叶缘呈现出独特的镰刀状图案。

The plant's leaves were deeply lobed and runcinate. 这植物的叶子深裂且呈镰刀状。|近义词：sickle-shaped, scythe-shaped|反义词：entire (in botany, referring to an un-toothed leaf margin)
