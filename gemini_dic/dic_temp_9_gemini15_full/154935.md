pester:/ˈpɛstər/| vt.|烦扰；纠缠；不断地打搅|to trouble or annoy (someone) persistently.|例句：The children pestered their mother for sweets.孩子们不断地缠着妈妈要糖果。
例句：He pestered me with questions all evening. 他整个晚上都在用问题烦扰我。|近义词：annoy, bother, harass, nag|反义词：soothe, appease, calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pesters me every day. = 他每天都烦扰我。
They pester their teacher with questions. = 他们用问题烦扰他们的老师。

一般过去时:
She pestered him until he agreed. = 她一直纠缠他直到他同意。
The reporters pestered the celebrity for an interview. = 记者们纠缠着这位名人要采访。

现在完成时:
I have been pestered by telemarketers all morning. = 我整个上午都被电话推销员烦扰了。
They have pestered their parents for a new car. = 他们一直在纠缠他们的父母要一辆新车。

过去完成时:
He had pestered her for weeks before she finally gave in. = 在她最终让步之前，他已经纠缠了她几周了。
The dog had pestered the cat all day before it finally ran away. = 狗整天都纠缠着猫，直到猫最终跑掉了。

将来时:

They will pester their boss for a raise. = 他们会纠缠他们的老板加薪。

虚拟语气:
If he pestered me any more, I would call the police. = 如果他再烦扰我，我就报警。
I wish he wouldn't pester me so much. = 我希望他不那么烦我。

被动语态:
He was pestered by his classmates. = 他被他的同学们烦扰了。
She was pestered with phone calls all day. = 她整天都被电话烦扰了。

疑问句:
Does he often pester you? = 他经常烦扰你吗？
Are they pestering you about the project? = 他们因为那个项目烦扰你吗？

否定句:
He didn't pester her about it. = 他没有因为这件事烦扰她。
They don't pester me anymore. = 他们不再烦扰我了。


PHRASE->
pester someone into doing something = 纠缠某人去做某事
pester someone for something = 纠缠某人要某物
constantly pester = 不断地烦扰
continuously pester = 持续地烦扰
repeatedly pester = 反复地烦扰


词源:
The word "pester" comes from the Middle English word "pestren," which meant "to strike, beat."  This ultimately derives from the Old French word "pestrer," which had a similar meaning.  The sense evolved from physical striking to annoying or bothering.
