oversleep:/ˈoʊvərˌsliːp/ | v. |睡过头，睡懒觉|To sleep longer than intended or necessary.  |例句：I overslept this morning and missed the bus.  今天早上我睡过头了，错过了公共汽车。
I overslept and was late for work. 我睡过头了，上班迟到了。|近义词：sleep in, oversleep oneself|反义词：wake up early, get up early|动词


USAGE->
