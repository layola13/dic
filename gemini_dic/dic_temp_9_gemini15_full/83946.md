half-concealed:/ˌhɑːf kənˈsiːld/ | adj. | 半隐藏的，半遮掩的 | Partially hidden or obscured.  The adjective describes something that is not fully visible or revealed, but is partially hidden or covered. | 例句：The half-concealed figure in the shadows startled her.  阴影中半隐藏的身影把她吓了一跳。


The half-concealed truth was more damaging than a complete lie. 半遮掩的真相比彻头彻尾的谎言更具破坏性。| 近义词：partially hidden, partly obscured, veiled, masked | 反义词：fully revealed, completely exposed, unveiled, open
