presuggest:/prɪˈsʌdʒɛst/| v.|预先建议，提前建议|To suggest something beforehand or in advance.  It implies a suggestion made before a decision is made or an action is taken.|The committee presuggested several alternative solutions before the final decision was made. 委员会在最终决定之前预先建议了几种替代方案。|suggest, propose, recommend|oppose, reject, dissuade|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He presuggests a different approach to the problem. 他预先建议了一种不同的解决问题的方法。
They presuggest modifications to the initial plan. 他们预先建议修改初步计划。
