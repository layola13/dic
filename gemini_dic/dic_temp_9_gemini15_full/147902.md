overrefined:/ˌoʊvərɪˈfaɪnd/| adj.|矫揉造作的；过于精致的；过于细腻的|Excessively refined or polished to the point of being artificial or unnatural.  It suggests a lack of genuineness or naturalness, often implying an excessive focus on superficial details. |例句：Her overrefined manners felt strained and unnatural.  她的举止过于精致，显得做作而不自然。

例句：The overrefined cake lacked the rustic charm of a homemade one.  这块过于精致的蛋糕缺乏自制蛋糕的质朴魅力。


近义词：affected, artificial, pretentious, mannered, elaborate

反义词：natural, simple, rustic, unrefined, crude


词源：over- (prefix indicating excess) + refined (past participle of refine, meaning to purify or improve)  表示过度精致，精炼到过头了。


用法说明：

overrefined通常用来形容人或事物过于精致，以至于显得不自然或做作。它可以用来形容人的举止、语言、艺术品等等。
