residers:/rɪˈzaɪdərz/| n. |居住者，居民|People who live in a particular place.  Residents are synonymous with dwellers and inhabitants.|Example: The residents of the small town are very friendly.  这个小镇的居民非常友好。|The number of residents in the city has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，该市居民数量显著增加。|inhabitants, dwellers, occupants|evacuees, visitors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The residents of this building recycle diligently. = 这栋楼的居民认真地进行垃圾回收。
Many residents enjoy the local park. = 许多居民喜欢当地的公园。

一般过去时:
The residents protested the new highway construction. = 居民抗议新的高速公路建设。
The residents celebrated the town's anniversary. = 居民庆祝了小镇的周年纪念日。

现在完成时:
The residents have complained about the noise pollution. = 居民们已经抱怨噪音污染了。
The residents have adapted to the new regulations. = 居民们已经适应了新的规章制度。

过去完成时:
The residents had already left before the earthquake struck. = 地震来袭之前，居民们已经离开了。
The residents had known about the potential dangers for years. = 居民们多年来一直知道潜在的危险。

将来时:
The residents will vote on the new zoning laws. = 居民们将对新的分区法进行投票。
The residents will receive a notice about the upcoming event. = 居民们将收到关于即将举行的活动的通知。

虚拟语气:
If the residents had known about the flood, they would have evacuated earlier. = 如果居民们知道洪水，他们会更早疏散。
I wish the residents would be more considerate of their neighbors. = 我希望居民们能更体谅他们的邻居。

被动语态:
The residents were informed about the emergency procedures. = 居民们被告知了紧急程序。
The concerns of the residents are being addressed by the city council. = 市议会正在处理居民的担忧。

疑问句:
How many residents live in this area? = 这个地区有多少居民？
Are the residents satisfied with the public services? = 居民们对公共服务满意吗？

否定句:
The residents did not support the proposed development. = 居民们不支持拟议的开发项目。
The residents were not informed of the meeting. = 居民们没有被告知会议。
