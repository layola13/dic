racking:/ˈrækiŋ/| v. adj. n.| 折磨；使痛苦；费力地寻找；堆放；(尤指因过度使用而)疼痛的；| to cause someone a lot of mental or physical suffering; to search intensively and unsuccessfully; the act of racking something; a framework for supporting something.|例句：The constant noise was racking my nerves.  持续的噪音折磨着我的神经。|例句：She spent hours racking her brains for a solution. 她花了几个小时绞尽脑汁寻找解决办法。|近义词：tormenting, agonizing, exhausting, searching |反义词：soothing, comforting, relaxing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is racking his brain to solve the puzzle. = 他正在绞尽脑汁解这个谜题。
The machine is racking up sales. = 这台机器正在创造越来越多的销售额。


一般过去时:
The stress racked her body and mind. = 压力折磨着她的身心。
They racked their brains trying to remember the password. = 他们绞尽脑汁试图回忆密码。


现在完成时:
She has been racking up debts for years. = 她多年来一直欠债累累。
The company has racked up impressive profits this quarter. = 本季度公司获得了令人印象深刻的利润。


过去完成时:
They had racked their brains but still couldn't find the answer. = 他们绞尽脑汁，但仍然找不到答案。
He had racked up significant experience before taking on the new role. = 他在承担新角色之前积累了丰富的经验。


将来时:
The constant worry will rack his health. = 持续的担忧会损害他的健康。
They will rack up more miles on their journey. = 他们在旅途中将会行驶更多的里程。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, it wouldn't have racked me with guilt. = 如果我知道的话，就不会让我这么内疚了。
I wish it hadn't racked my nerves so much. = 我希望它不会让我这么紧张。


被动语态:
His reputation was racked by scandal. = 他的名誉被丑闻所损害。
The shelves were racked with books. = 书架上摆满了书。


疑问句:
Is he racking his brain again? = 他又在绞尽脑汁了吗？
What is racking up the costs? = 是什么导致成本增加？


否定句:
The news didn't rack her nerves as much as she expected. = 这个消息并没有像她预期的那样让她紧张。
They didn't rack up as much debt as they had feared. = 他们没有像他们担心的那样欠下那么多债务。
