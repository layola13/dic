cullender:/ˈkʌləndər/| n.|漏勺，滤网|A kitchen utensil with perforated holes used to drain liquids from food.  |例句：I used a cullender to drain the pasta. 我用漏勺沥干意面。

例句：She rinsed the vegetables in the cullender before cooking.  她在烹饪之前用漏勺冲洗蔬菜。


近义词：sieve, strainer|反义词：None


词源:  From Middle English *colonder, from Old French *colander, from Vulgar Latin *colatorium ("strainer"), from Latin colum ("sieve").  The word's evolution reflects its function as a straining device across different languages.
