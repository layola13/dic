dissocial:/dɪˈsoʊʃəl/ | adj. | 反社会的，不合群的 |  Relating to or characterized by a rejection of or withdrawal from social interaction; anti-social.  The term often implies a deliberate avoidance of social contact, rather than simply being shy or introverted. | Example:  His dissocial behaviour led to his expulsion from school. 他的反社会行为导致他被学校开除。| 近义词: antisocial, unsociable, asocial | 反义词: sociable, gregarious

词源:
The word "dissocial" is formed from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") and the adjective "social".  It's a relatively straightforward combination reflecting the opposite of social behaviour.
