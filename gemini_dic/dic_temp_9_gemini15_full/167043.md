presuggests:/ˌpriːsəˈdʒɛsts/| v.|预示，预告，暗示|To indicate or suggest something before it happens.  It implies a prior indication or hint of something that will follow.|例句：The dark clouds presuggest an impending storm.  乌云预示着即将到来的暴风雨。

例句：Her nervous laughter presuggested that she was hiding something. 她紧张的笑声暗示着她隐瞒了什么。


近义词：foreshadow, foretell, hint at, suggest, indicate


反义词：confirm (in the sense of proving something definitively)


词性：动词
