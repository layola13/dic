login:/ˈlɒɡɪn/ /ˈlɔːɡɪn/| n., v.|登录，登入|The act of entering a secure system, typically using a username and password; to access a computer system or online service by entering a username and password.|例句：Please login to access your account. 请登录以访问您的帐户。  I need to login to check my email. 我需要登录来查看我的邮件。|近义词：sign in, sign on, access|反义词：logout, sign out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I login to my bank account every day. = 我每天登录我的银行账户。
He logs in to check his email several times a day. = 他一天登录几次来查看他的邮件。

一般过去时:
I logged in this morning to check my messages. = 我今天早上登录查看我的消息。
She logged in yesterday to update her profile. = 她昨天登录更新了她的个人资料。

现在完成时:
I have logged in already. = 我已经登录了。
He has logged in several times today. = 他今天已经登录好几次了。


过去完成时:
I had logged in before the meeting started. = 会议开始前我已经登录了。
She had logged in earlier to prepare for the presentation. = 她早些时候已经登录，为演示做准备。


将来时:
I will login later tonight. = 我今晚稍后会登录。
He will login tomorrow to finish his work. = 他明天会登录完成他的工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had logged in earlier, I would have seen the message. = 如果我早点登录，我就看到了那条消息。
I wish I had logged in sooner. = 我希望我早点登录。


被动语态:
The system can be logged into using a username and password. = 该系统可以使用用户名和密码登录。
The account was logged into without authorization. = 该账户未经授权被登录。


疑问句:
Have you logged in yet? = 你登录了吗？
Did he login successfully? = 他登录成功了吗？


否定句:
I couldn't login because I forgot my password. = 我因为忘记密码而无法登录。
She didn't login to the system yesterday. = 她昨天没有登录系统。


PHRASE->
login details = 登录信息
login credentials = 登录凭据
automatic login = 自动登录
failed login attempt = 登录尝试失败
secure login = 安全登录
