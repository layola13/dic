come-home-to-roost:/ˌkʌmˈhoʊm təˈruːst/| v. |恶报应验；自作自受| To suffer the unpleasant consequences of one's own actions.  This idiom implies that a bad deed or a mistake will eventually have negative repercussions for the person who committed it.  The image is of a bird returning to its roost, only to find it has been damaged or destroyed. | Example: His years of dishonesty finally came home to roost when he was caught and sent to prison.  他的多年欺骗行为最终自食恶果，他被抓住并送进了监狱。|近义词：backfire, rebound, recoil|反义词：escape consequences, get away with it|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His bad habits come home to roost eventually. = 他的坏习惯最终会自食恶果。
The consequences of his actions come home to roost. = 他行为的后果最终会报应在他身上。


一般过去时:
His lies came home to roost when the truth was revealed. = 当真相大白时，他的谎言自食恶果。
The consequences of her reckless driving came home to roost when she had an accident. = 她鲁莽驾驶的后果在她发生事故时报应在了她身上。


现在完成时:
His arrogance has come home to roost; he's now unemployed. = 他的傲慢自食了恶果；他现在失业了。
The company's unethical practices have come home to roost, leading to a major scandal. = 公司的不道德行为自食恶果，导致了一场重大丑闻。


过去完成时:
By the time the scandal broke, his misdeeds had come home to roost. = 当丑闻爆发时，他的恶行已经自食恶果了。
Before the election, his past mistakes had come home to roost, hurting his campaign. = 大选之前，他过去的错误已经自食恶果，损害了他的竞选活动。


将来时:
His deceit will come home to roost one day. = 他的欺骗行为总有一天会自食恶果。
Ignoring safety regulations will eventually come home to roost. = 忽视安全条例最终会自食恶果。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't lied, his dishonesty wouldn't have come home to roost. = 如果他没有撒谎，他的不诚实就不会自食恶果了。
I wish his recklessness hadn't come home to roost so harshly. = 我真希望他的鲁莽行为不会如此严厉地报应在他身上。


被动语态:
His past mistakes were seen to come home to roost. = 他的过去错误被认为是自食恶果。
The consequences of their actions were allowed to come home to roost. = 他们行为的后果被允许自食恶果。


疑问句:
Will his actions ever come home to roost? = 他的行为最终会自食恶果吗？
When will the consequences of his lies come home to roost? = 他谎言的后果什么时候会报应在他身上？


否定句:
His lies didn't come home to roost immediately, but they eventually did. = 他的谎言并没有立即自食恶果，但最终还是如此。
He managed to avoid having his misdeeds come home to roost for many years. = 他设法多年避免了他的恶行自食恶果。
