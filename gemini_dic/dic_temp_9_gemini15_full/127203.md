词源:
The word "naturopath" is a modern compound word.  It combines "nature" (from Latin *natura, meaning "nature, birth, course of things") and "-path" (from Greek -παθης,  "-pathēs," meaning "suffering" or, more broadly, related to disease or treatment). Thus, a naturopath is literally someone who treats disease using methods relating to nature.
