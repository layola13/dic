premiss:/ˈprɛmɪs/| n.|前提，假设 |A premiss is a statement or proposition that forms the basis of a reasoning or argument.  It's something assumed to be true in order to draw a conclusion.  |Example: The premiss of his argument was that all men are created equal.  他的论点的前提是人人生而平等。|近义词：assumption, hypothesis, supposition |反义词：conclusion
