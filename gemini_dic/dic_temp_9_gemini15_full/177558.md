rebracing:/ˌriːˈbreɪsɪŋ/| v.|重新支撑；重新加固；|The act of bracing something again, often after damage or weakening.|例句：The engineers are rebracing the bridge after the recent earthquake.  工程师们在地震后正在重新加固桥梁。

例句：The old house needed rebracing before the new roof could be installed. 老房子需要重新加固才能安装新的屋顶。


近义词：reinforcing, strengthening, shoring up|反义词：weakening, undermining|词性:动词
