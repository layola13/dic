classifi:/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|分类，归类|To arrange or organize (things) into categories according to shared qualities or characteristics.|The librarian classified the books by subject.|近义词：categorize, sort, group|反义词：jumble, mix, randomize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He classifies the documents by date. = 他按日期对文件进行分类。
She classifies the students according to their abilities. = 她根据学生的能力对他们进行分类。


一般过去时:
The scientist classified the new species of insect. = 科学家对这种新的昆虫物种进行了分类。
They classified the data into three categories. = 他们将数据分为三类。


现在完成时:
We have classified all the samples for analysis. = 我们已经对所有样本进行了分类以便分析。
She has classified her stamps by country of origin. = 她已按原产国对她的邮票进行了分类。


过去完成时:
The researchers had classified the fossils before the presentation. = 研究人员在演示之前已经对化石进行了分类。
They had classified the information before making their report. = 他们在做报告之前已经对信息进行了分类。


将来时:
They will classify the images using advanced software. = 他们将使用先进的软件对图像进行分类。
She will classify the data according to the new criteria. = 她将根据新的标准对数据进行分类。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would classify these items properly. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会对这些物品进行适当的分类。
It is important that the documents be classified correctly. = 正确分类文件非常重要。


被动语态:
The documents were classified by the secretary. = 文件由秘书进行分类。
The samples are being classified in the laboratory. = 样本正在实验室里进行分类。


疑问句:
How are these plants classified? = 这些植物是如何分类的？
How did they classify the different types of soil? = 他们是如何对不同类型的土壤进行分类的？


否定句:
He didn't classify the information correctly. = 他没有正确地对信息进行分类。
They did not classify the books according to the Dewey Decimal System. = 他们没有按照杜威十进制系统对书籍进行分类。
