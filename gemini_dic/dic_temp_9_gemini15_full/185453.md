retweet:/ˌriːˈtwiːt/| vt. n.|转发（推文）|To re-post (someone else's tweet) on one's own Twitter feed.|例句：I retweeted the article because I thought it was interesting. 我转发了这篇文章，因为我觉得它很有趣。|例句：Her retweet of the controversial statement caused a stir. 她转发有争议的声明引起了轰动。|近义词：repost, share|反义词：delete, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retweets news articles daily. = 他每天都转发新闻文章。
She retweets anything related to her hobby. = 她转发任何与她的爱好相关的东西。

一般过去时:
I retweeted your post yesterday. = 我昨天转发了你的帖子。
They retweeted the company's announcement. = 他们转发了公司的公告。

现在完成时:
She has retweeted over a thousand times this month. = 这个月她已经转发了一千多次了。
He has retweeted all of his friends' posts. = 他已经转发了他所有朋友的帖子。

过去完成时:
I had retweeted the news before I saw your comment. = 在我看你的评论之前，我已经转发了这条新闻。
They had retweeted the video multiple times before it went viral. = 在视频爆红之前，他们已经转发了多次。

将来时:
I will retweet your post if you share mine. = 如果你分享我的帖子，我会转发你的帖子。
She will retweet the announcement tomorrow morning. = 她明天早上会转发这个公告。


PHRASE->
retweet a tweet = 转发一条推文
retweet something = 转发某事
retweet an article = 转发一篇文章
retweet a video = 转发一个视频
retweet a post = 转发一个帖子
