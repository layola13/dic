indignation: /ˌɪndɪɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 义愤；愤怒；愤慨 |  A feeling of anger or annoyance provoked by what is perceived as unfair, unjust, or wrong.  | 例句：His indignation at the injustice was palpable.  他的义愤之情显而易见。

例句：The news filled her with indignation. 这个消息让她义愤填膺。


近义词：anger, resentment, fury, wrath, outrage | 反义词：calmness, composure, indifference, apathy | 词性：名词
