bypasser:/ˈbaɪpæsər/| n.|旁路者，绕行者，旁通者|A person or thing that bypasses something; someone who avoids or circumvents a process or system.|例句：The bypasser chose a shortcut through the park instead of using the main road. 绕行者选择穿过公园的捷径而不是使用主路。  The new software acts as a bypasser for the outdated system. 新软件充当过时系统的旁路程序。|近义词：detourer, circumnavigator, evader|反义词：follower, conformist
