overaccelerating:/ˌoʊvərəkˈsɛləreɪtɪŋ/| v.|超速加速；过度加速|The act of accelerating beyond a safe or recommended speed or rate.  It implies exceeding the limit or proper rate of acceleration, often with negative consequences.|例句：Overaccelerating on a wet road caused the car to skid.  在湿滑的路面上超速行驶导致汽车打滑。

例句：The rocket's overaccelerating caused damage to the engine. 火箭过度加速导致发动机损坏。


近义词：speeding, accelerating excessively, overdriving


反义词：decelerating, slowing down, coasting


词性:动词(现在分词)
