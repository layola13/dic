preillustrate: /ˌpriːˈɪləstreɪt/ | v. | 预先举例说明；预先阐明 | To illustrate beforehand; to explain or clarify something in advance by providing examples.  | The professor decided to preillustrate the complex theory with a simple analogy before diving into the details. 教授决定在深入讲解复杂理论之前，先用一个简单的比喻来预先说明。 | 近义词：preface, foreshadow, prefigure | 反义词：postscript, epilogue
