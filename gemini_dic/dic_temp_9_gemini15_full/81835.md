greying:/ˈɡreɪɪŋ/| adj.| 变灰的；变老的 | Turning gray or grayish; becoming old.  |The greying of his temples showed the passage of time.  他的鬓角变灰了，显示了时间的流逝。| aging, silvering | blackening, young | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hair is greying. = 头发正在变灰。
His beard is greying rapidly. = 他的胡子正在迅速变灰。

一般过去时:
His hair greyed prematurely. = 他的头发过早地变白了。
The once vibrant colors of the painting were greying. = 那幅画曾经鲜艳的色彩正在变灰。

现在完成时:
His hair has greyed considerably since I last saw him. = 自从我上次见到他以来，他的头发已经白了不少。
The fabric has greyed with age and exposure to sunlight. = 由于年龄和日晒，织物已经褪色变灰了。

过去完成时:
The photograph had greyed significantly over the years. = 这张照片多年来已经严重褪色变灰了。
The wood had greyed considerably due to weathering. = 木材由于风化已经严重变灰了。

将来时:
His hair will grey eventually. = 他的头发最终会变灰。
The metal will grey over time due to oxidation. = 金属会由于氧化而随着时间的推移而变灰。

虚拟语气:

I wish my hair wouldn't grey so quickly. = 我希望我的头发不要变白得这么快。

被动语态:
