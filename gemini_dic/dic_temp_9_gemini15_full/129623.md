non-constant:/ˌnɑːnˈkɒnstənt/| adj. |非恒定的，非常数的 |Not constant; changing.  It refers to something that is not fixed or unchanging, but varies or fluctuates. This term is often used in mathematics, computer science, and other fields dealing with variables and functions.|例句：The temperature in this region is non-constant throughout the year.  这个地区的温度全年都不恒定。

The speed of the car is non-constant; it accelerates and decelerates.  这辆车的速度是不恒定的；它加速和减速。


近义词：variable, fluctuating, inconstant, unsteady, changeable

反义词：constant, unchanging, stable, fixed, steady


词性: adjective
