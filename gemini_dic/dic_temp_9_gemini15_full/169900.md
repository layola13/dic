prospectively:/prɒˈspektɪvli/ | adv. | 预期地；将来地；展望未来地 | In a prospective manner; looking forward to the future.  It implies something is expected or planned for the future, not necessarily certain. | 例句：The company is prospectively hiring 100 new employees next year. = 公司预计明年将招聘100名新员工。

The study prospectively followed patients for five years. = 该研究对患者进行了为期五年的前瞻性随访。


近义词：potentially, possibly, expectedly, anticipatorily | 反义词：retrospectively, retrospectively,  actually, currently
