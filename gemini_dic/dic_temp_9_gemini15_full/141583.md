oppressive:/əˈprɛsɪv/| adj. |压迫的；沉重的；令人窒息的 |  Characterized by or causing oppression; unjustly inflicting hardship and suffering.  It can refer to a government, system, or environment that restricts freedom and liberty. It can also describe something that is heavy, suffocating, or burdensome. |

例句：

The oppressive regime silenced all dissent.  | 压迫性政权压制了所有异议。

The oppressive heat made it difficult to work outdoors. | 闷热的天气使得户外工作变得困难。


近义词：tyrannical, tyrannical, authoritarian, burdensome, suffocating, stifling

反义词：liberating, freeing, lenient, gentle, mild
