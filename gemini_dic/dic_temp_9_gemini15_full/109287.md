lemur:/ˈliːmər/| n. |狐猴|A lemur is a primate from the infraorder Lemuriformes, found only on the island of Madagascar.  They are characterized by their relatively large eyes, often nocturnal habits, and a variety of sizes and appearances. Lemurs range from small mouse lemurs to larger species like the indri.|The ring-tailed lemur is known for its distinctive black and white ringed tail. |近义词：primate (broadly)|反义词：None specifically applicable.
