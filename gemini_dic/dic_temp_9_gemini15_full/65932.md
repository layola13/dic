exacerbations:/ˌɛksæsərˈbeɪʃənz/| n. |加剧；恶化；加重|The worsening or intensification of a disease or problem.  Exacerbations refer to periods of increased severity of a chronic condition, often characterized by a flare-up of symptoms.|例句：The patient experienced several exacerbations of their asthma over the winter.  患者在冬天经历了几次哮喘加重。

例句：Recent political events have led to an exacerbation of tensions between the two countries. 近期的政治事件加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。

近义词：aggravations, worsenings, intensifications

反义词：improvements, alleviations, remissions

词性：名词
