lumpfish:/ˈlʌmpfɪʃ/| n. |河豚，圆头鱼|A lumpfish is a type of fish known for its round, plump body and the ability of the male to create a nest of eggs.  They are typically found in the cold waters of the North Atlantic and the Arctic Ocean. They are not a common food fish but are sometimes used for caviar. |例句：The lumpfish, with its gelatinous eggs, is a fascinating creature of the deep.  河豚，带着它的胶状卵，是深海中一种迷人的生物。

The male lumpfish guards its nest of eggs fiercely. 雄性河豚凶猛地守护着它的卵巢。|近义词：cyclopterus (学名)|反义词:|词性:名词
