replay:/ˈriːpleɪ/| n. vt.|重播；重放；再比赛；回应|To play again; to repeat a performance or action; to respond or answer.|例句：The coach decided to replay the game tape. 教练决定重播比赛录像。

例句：He replayed the scene in his mind. 他在脑海中重放了那个场景。


近义词：rebroadcast, repeat, respond, answer|反义词：original, first time, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replay the highlights every night. = 他们每晚重播精彩片段。
We replay the game to analyse mistakes. = 我们重播比赛来分析错误。


一般过去时:
The DJ replayed the popular song. = DJ 重播了那首热门歌曲。
She replayed the conversation in her head. = 她脑子里重放了那段对话。


现在完成时:
They have replayed the match several times. = 他们已经重播了这场比赛几次了。
I have replayed that level of the game five times. = 我已经重玩了那个游戏关卡五次了。


过去完成时:
They had replayed the entire match before the break. = 中场休息前他们已经重播了整场比赛。
She had replayed the message numerous times before understanding it. = 在理解之前，她已经多次重放了这条消息。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
