chromous:/ˈkroʊməs/ | adj. | 铬的，含铬的 |  Relating to or containing chromium.  Chromous compounds are those in which chromium has a +2 oxidation state.  |Example: Chromous chloride is a rare and reactive compound.  氯化亚铬是一种稀有且反应性强的化合物。|近义词：chromium-containing |反义词：chromic (referring to Cr(III) oxidation state)

词源:
The word "chromous" is derived from the element chromium, ultimately from Greek χρῶμα (chrōma), meaning "color," reflecting the variety of colors exhibited by chromium compounds. The suffix "-ous" is a chemical suffix indicating a lower oxidation state of a metal.
