erratic:/ɪˈrætɪk/| adj. |不稳定的；反复无常的；古怪的 |Characterized by lack of consistency, regularity, or uniformity; unpredictable; unstable.  It implies a lack of predictability and often suggests a tendency towards unexpected changes or behavior. |例句：His erratic behavior worried his friends. 他的古怪行为让朋友们很担心。

例句：The erratic weather patterns made farming difficult.  反复无常的天气模式使得农业耕作困难。


近义词：unpredictable, inconsistent, capricious, volatile, irregular |反义词：consistent, predictable, stable, reliable, regular
