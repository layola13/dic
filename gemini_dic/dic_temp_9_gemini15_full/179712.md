reentrench:/ˌriːɪnˈtʃɛntʃ/| vt. |重新筑防御工事；巩固；加强；退守；节省开支 |To strengthen or fortify (a position or system)；to return to a more defensive position; to reduce spending. |例句：The company decided to reentrench its position in the market by lowering prices. 公司决定通过降价来巩固其在市场上的地位。

例句：Facing economic downturn, the government chose to reentrench its budget, cutting spending on non-essential projects. 面对经济衰退，政府选择紧缩预算，削减非必要项目的开支。

近义词：fortify, consolidate, strengthen, retrench

反义词：weaken, expand, loosen
