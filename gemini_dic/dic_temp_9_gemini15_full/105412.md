kidnaped:/ˈkɪdnæpt/| v.|绑架；拐骗|To take a person away illegally by force, usually to demand a ransom.  Kidnapped is the past tense and past participle of kidnap. |例句：The child was kidnapped from his school.  孩子从学校被绑架了。

例句：The police investigated the kidnapping and eventually found the victim unharmed. 警方调查了绑架案，最终找到了毫发无损的受害者。


近义词：abduct, seize, snatch

反义词：release, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kidnaps children for ransom. 他绑架儿童勒索赎金。
They kidnap people and hold them for ransom. 他们绑架人质并索要赎金。


一般过去时:
The police officer kidnapped the suspect. 警官绑架了嫌疑犯。(This implies an illegal act by the officer)
The criminal kidnapped the heiress.  罪犯绑架了女继承人。


现在完成时:
The gang has kidnapped several victims this year.  该团伙今年已经绑架了几名受害者。
They have kidnapped him and are demanding a million dollars. 他们绑架了他，并索要一百万美元。


过去完成时:
The police discovered that the kidnappers had kidnapped the child several hours earlier. 警方发现绑匪数小时前就绑架了孩子。
It turned out that he had kidnapped her before. 原来他以前绑架过她。


将来时:
They will kidnap the witness if they get the chance. 如果他们有机会，他们将会绑架证人。
The police believe they will kidnap more people. 警方认为他们还会绑架更多的人。


虚拟语气:
If they kidnapped the CEO, the company would be in chaos. 如果他们绑架了首席执行官，公司就会陷入混乱。
I wish they hadn't kidnapped my brother.  我希望他们没有绑架我的兄弟。


被动语态:
The victim was kidnapped from her home. 受害者在家中被绑架。
The child has been kidnapped and is being held for ransom. 孩子被绑架了，正被扣为人质索要赎金。


疑问句:
Were they kidnapped at gunpoint? 他们是被用枪指着绑架的吗？
Has anyone been kidnapped recently? 最近有人被绑架了吗？


否定句:
They did not kidnap the ambassador. 他们没有绑架大使。
The police say they haven't kidnapped anyone. 警方说他们没有绑架任何人。
