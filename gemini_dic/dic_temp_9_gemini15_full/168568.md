process:/ˈprɑːses/ /ˈprəʊses/| n. vt.|过程，进程；方法，步骤；处理，加工|The process of learning a new language takes time and effort. 学习一门外语是一个需要时间和精力的过程。|近义词：procedure, method, system|反义词：result, outcome


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory processes thousands of tons of grain each year. = 工厂每年加工数千吨谷物。
The computer processes the data quickly and efficiently. = 电脑快速有效地处理数据。


一般过去时:
They processed the application last week. = 他们上周处理了申请。
The company processed my order quickly. = 公司很快处理了我的订单。


现在完成时:
They have processed all the applications. = 他们已经处理了所有申请。
We have processed the data and are ready to present the results. = 我们已经处理了数据，并准备展示结果。


过去完成时:
They had processed the documents before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前处理好文件。
The chef had already processed the vegetables before starting to cook. = 大厨在开始烹饪前已经处理好蔬菜了。


将来时:
We will process your request as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快处理您的请求。
The company will process the payments next week. = 公司下周将处理付款。


虚拟语气:
If they processed the data correctly, the results would be accurate. = 如果他们正确地处理数据，结果就会准确。
I wish they had processed my application more quickly. = 我希望他们能更快地处理我的申请。


被动语态:
The application was processed quickly. = 申请很快就被处理了。
The data is being processed by the computer. = 数据正在由电脑处理。


疑问句:
How is the application process going? = 申请流程进展如何？
What process are you using to solve the problem? = 你用什么方法解决这个问题？


否定句:
They did not process the order on time. = 他们没有按时处理订单。
The company has not yet processed my application. = 公司尚未处理我的申请。
