nonconjecturable: /ˌnɒnkənˈdʒɛktʃərəbl/ | adj. | 不可推测的，无法猜测的 |  Impossible to conjecture or guess; beyond speculation.  It refers to something that cannot be inferred or deduced from available evidence or knowledge. |


The future of the stock market is largely nonconjecturable.  = 股市的未来在很大程度上是不可预测的。

The cause of the mysterious disappearance remains nonconjecturable. = 神秘失踪的原因仍然无法猜测。


近义词:  unpredictable, indeterminable, inscrutable, unknowable, unfathomable


反义词: predictable, foreseeable, knowable, certain


词性: adjective
