dodgeable:/ˈdɑːdʒəbəl/| adj.| 可躲避的，容易躲开的 | Easily avoided or evaded.  Something that is dodgeable can be skillfully avoided or evaded. | 例句：The boxer was incredibly dodgeable, making it difficult for his opponent to land a punch. 拳击手非常灵活，让对手很难击中他。

The fast-moving car was barely dodgeable.  那辆快速行驶的汽车几乎无法躲避。


近义词：evasive, avoidable, escapable | 反义词：inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable
