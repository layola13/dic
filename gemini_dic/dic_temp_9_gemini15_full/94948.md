in reality:/ɪn riˈælɪti/ |adv.| 事实上，实际上 |In reality, the situation is much more complicated than it seems.  事实上，情况比看起来复杂得多。|近义词：actually, in fact, really, truly |反义词：in theory, hypothetically


USAGE->
一般现在时:
In reality, the project is behind schedule. = 事实上，这个项目进度落后了。
In reality, many people are struggling financially. = 事实上，许多人都在经济上挣扎。


一般过去时:

In reality, the trip was more exhausting than anticipated. = 事实上，这次旅行比预期的更累人。

否定句:

In reality, it was not his fault. = 事实上，这不是他的错。


PHRASE->
There is no phrase specifically using "in reality" as a single, fixed phrase.  The phrase itself functions as an adverbial phrase modifying the clause it appears in.  Its meaning is conveyed through its context.  Examples:

In reality, the situation is dire. (The situation is dire in reality)
In reality, it's not that easy. (It's not that easy in reality)
He thought he was a genius; in reality, he was quite average. (He was quite average in reality)
In reality, her intentions were good. (Her intentions were good in reality)
I thought I'd be rich by 30; in reality, I'm still struggling. (I'm still struggling in reality)
