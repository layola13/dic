communist: /ˈkɒmjʊnɪst/ /ˈkɑːmjʊnɪst/ | n. adj. | 共产主义者；共产主义的 |

英文解析:A communist is a person who believes in or supports communism, a socioeconomic ideology that advocates for a classless society where the means of production are owned communally and controlled by the state or the community as a whole.  The adjective "communist" describes something relating to or characteristic of communism or communists.

例句:

He is a staunch communist and dedicated his life to the cause. 他是一位坚定的共产主义者，并献身于这一事业。

The communist regime controlled all aspects of life. 共产主义政权控制着生活的方方面面。


近义词: socialist (in some contexts), revolutionary, leftist

反义词: capitalist, individualist, conservative


词性: noun, adjective
