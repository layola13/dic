nonindustrial:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdʌstriəl/| adj. |非工业的；非工业化的|Not relating to or connected with industry.  This adjective describes something that is not part of or related to industrial processes, manufacturing, or the production of goods. |例句：The nonindustrial areas of the country are experiencing a population decline.  这个国家的非工业地区人口正在减少。|例句：He prefers a nonindustrial lifestyle, focusing on sustainable agriculture. 他更喜欢非工业化的生活方式，专注于可持续农业。|近义词：rural, agricultural, pre-industrial |反义词：industrial, manufacturing
