enarch:/ˈɛnɑːrk/| n.|首领，统治者|A person who is at the top of a hierarchy or organization, often implying a position of power and influence.  It often carries a connotation of being autocratic or elitist.| The enarch of the corporation made all the final decisions.  这家公司的首领做所有最终决定。|近义词：leader, ruler, chief, boss, autocrat|反义词：follower, subordinate, subject
