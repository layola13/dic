PCSO:/ˈpiːsiːsoʊ/| n.|社区警务官；警察社区支援干事|Police Community Support Officer, a member of the British police force with limited powers, primarily focused on community engagement and crime prevention.|例句：The PCSO patrolled the park regularly. 社区警务官定期巡逻公园。|例句：The PCSOs work closely with local residents to address community concerns. 社区警务官与当地居民密切合作，以解决社区问题。|近义词：police officer, constable|反义词：criminal, offender|名词
