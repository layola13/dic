obtains:/əbˈteɪnz/| vt.|获得，取得，得到|To get or acquire something, especially after making an effort.|例句：She obtained a degree in engineering. 她获得了工程学位。|例句：He obtained a loan from the bank. 他从银行获得了一笔贷款。|近义词：acquire, gain, secure, achieve|反义词：lose, forfeit, relinquish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obtains information from various sources. 他从各种来源获取信息。
She obtains her daily necessities from the local market. 她从当地市场获得日常用品。

一般过去时:
The company obtained a significant profit last year. 公司去年获得了可观的利润。
He obtained a visa after a long wait.  他在长时间等待后获得了签证。

现在完成时:
She has obtained all the necessary documents. 她已经获得了所有必要的证件。
They have obtained funding for their research project. 他们已经为他们的研究项目获得了资金。

过去完成时:
He had obtained a PhD before he started teaching. 他在开始教学之前已经获得了博士学位。
She had obtained a good reputation in her field.  她在她的领域里已经获得了良好的声誉。

将来时:
I hope to obtain a better job next year. 我希望明年能找到一份更好的工作。
They will obtain the results of the test next week. 他们下周将获得测试结果。


虚拟语气:
If he obtained the necessary funding, he would start the project immediately. 如果他获得了必要的资金，他将立即启动项目。
I wish I had obtained a better score on the exam. 我希望我在考试中取得更好的分数。

被动语态:
The information was obtained from reliable sources. 信息来自可靠的来源。
The permit was obtained without any difficulties. 许可证很容易就获得了。

疑问句:
How did he obtain such a large sum of money? 他是如何获得如此大笔钱财的？
Where did she obtain that rare book? 她从哪里获得那本珍稀书籍？

否定句:
He did not obtain the position he applied for. 他没有获得他申请的职位。
They failed to obtain the necessary permits. 他们未能获得必要的许可证。
