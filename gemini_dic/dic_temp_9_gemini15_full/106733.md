kyrie:/ˈkɪəriˌeɪ/| n.|主祷文（基督教礼拜仪式中的祈祷词）|The word "kyrie" is derived from the Greek word κύριε (kýrie), meaning "Lord" or "Master."  In the context of the Mass, it's the opening invocation, typically sung or chanted,  a plea for mercy and grace from God.  It's a key element of the liturgical tradition. |例句：The Kyrie eleison is a powerful and moving part of the Mass.  主祷文是弥撒中一个强有力且动人的部分。
The choir sang the Kyrie beautifully. 唱诗班优美地唱着主祷文。|近义词：prayer, invocation, supplication|反义词：N/A
