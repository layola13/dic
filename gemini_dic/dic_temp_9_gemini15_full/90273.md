households:/ˈhaʊsˌhoʊldz/| n. |家庭；户；家计|Groups of people living together in a single dwelling, usually a family unit.  Households are the basic units of economic and social life, often consisting of related individuals sharing resources and responsibilities. |例句：The survey collected data from 1,000 households across the country.  这项调查收集了来自全国 1000 户家庭的数据。

例句：Many households are struggling to make ends meet in the current economic climate. 在当前的经济环境下，许多家庭都在努力维持生计。


近义词：families, homes, dwellings |反义词：None (it's not a concept with a direct opposite) |词性:名词
