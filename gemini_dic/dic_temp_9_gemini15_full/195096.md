semiclosed:/ˈsemiˌkloʊzd/| adj.|半封闭的，半关闭的|Partially closed or sealed; neither completely open nor completely closed.  It implies a state between fully open and fully closed, often suggesting a degree of control or restriction over access or flow.|例句：The semiclosed system allowed for some air circulation but prevented dust from entering. 半封闭系统允许一定程度的空气流通，但防止灰尘进入。The semiclosed meeting room facilitated discussion while maintaining a degree of privacy. 半封闭的会议室便于讨论，同时保持一定的私密性。|近义词：partially closed, partially sealed, semi-open|反义词：open, closed
