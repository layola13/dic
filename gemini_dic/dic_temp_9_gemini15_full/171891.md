punta:/ˈpʊntɑː/| n.|尖端，顶点；（尤指南美的）海角；（西班牙语）尖端|A punta is a pointed end or tip, often used to describe a geographical feature like a cape or headland, especially in South America. It can also refer to the pointed end of an object.|例句：The punta of the spear was very sharp.  矛尖非常锋利。

例句：The southern punta of the island is a popular tourist destination. 岛屿南端的尖角是热门旅游景点。|近义词：tip, point, apex, peak|反义词：base, bottom
