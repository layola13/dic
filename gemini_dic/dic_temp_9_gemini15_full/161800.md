ppi:/ˈpiːpiː/|/ˌpiːpiːˈaɪ/| n. |每英寸点数；像素密度|Pixels Per Inch, a measure of the resolution of an image, specifically the number of pixels per linear inch.  It refers to the density of pixels on a display screen or a printed image. A higher PPI generally indicates a sharper, clearer image. |Example:  This monitor boasts a high PPI of 2560 x 1440, resulting in stunning image clarity.|近义词：resolution, pixel density|反义词：low resolution
