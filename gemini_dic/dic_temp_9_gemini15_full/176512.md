re-proofed:/ˌriːˈpruːft/ | v. | 再次校对；重新校正 |  To proofread something again. This implies a previous proofing attempt, and suggests a more thorough or careful review than a single proofing. | Example: The manuscript was re-proofed to catch any remaining errors.  手稿被再次校对以捕捉任何剩余的错误。|近义词：reviewed, checked, revised | 反义词：uncorrected, unedited
