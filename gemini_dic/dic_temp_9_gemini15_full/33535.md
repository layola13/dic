cider:/ˈsaɪdər/| n.|苹果酒|An alcoholic beverage made from fermented apple juice.  It can be sweet, dry, or semi-sweet, depending on the fermentation process.|例句：We enjoyed a glass of cider by the fireplace. 我们在壁炉旁享用了一杯苹果酒。

例句：The orchard produces a wide variety of apples, all used to make their famous cider. 这个果园生产各种各样的苹果，所有苹果都用来酿造他们著名的苹果酒。|近义词：apple wine|反义词：hard liquor (for some types)|词性:名词

词源:
The word "cider" comes from the Old French word "cidre," which itself is derived from the Latin word "sicera," meaning "strong drink."  The evolution reflects the spread of cider production and consumption.
