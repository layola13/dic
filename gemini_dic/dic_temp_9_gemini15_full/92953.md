ideological:/ˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|意识形态的；思想体系的|Relating to or characterized by ideology.  Ideological refers to beliefs or ideas that form the basis of a political, economic, or other social system.  It often implies a rigid or inflexible adherence to these beliefs. |例句：The country is deeply divided along ideological lines. 这个国家在意识形态上严重分裂。

例句：His ideological commitment to socialism led him to dedicate his life to social justice. 他对社会主义的坚定信仰促使他将毕生献身于社会正义事业。


近义词：doctrinal, creedal, principled


反义词：practical, pragmatic, realistic
