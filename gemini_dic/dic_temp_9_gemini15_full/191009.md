scam:/skæm/| n. v.|骗局；诈骗；欺诈|a dishonest scheme; a fraud.  A scam is a dishonest plan, especially one used to get money.|例句：He was the victim of a credit card scam. 他是信用卡诈骗的受害者。
例句：They scammed him out of his life savings. 他们骗走了他一生的积蓄。|近义词：fraud, swindle, con, trick, hoax |反义词：honesty, fairness
