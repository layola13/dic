fauvism:/ˈfoʊvɪzəm/| n.|野兽派(绘画风格)|Fauvism is a style of painting with vivid, bold colors and simplified forms.  It was a reaction against Impressionism and aimed to express emotion through color.|The Fauvist paintings of Matisse are characterized by their bold use of color.  马蒂斯的野兽派绘画以其大胆的色彩运用为特征。|Expressionism, Post-Impressionism|Realism, Classicism|名词
