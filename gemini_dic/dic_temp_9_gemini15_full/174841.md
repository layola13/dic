racialism: /ˈreɪʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 种族主义 | The belief that all members of each race possess characteristics or abilities specific to that race, especially so as to distinguish it as inferior or superior to another race or races.  It often involves the discrimination and prejudice against people based on their race or ethnicity. | Example:  His racialism was evident in his derogatory remarks about other ethnic groups.  他的种族主义在他对其他民族的贬损言论中显而易见。 |近义词:racism, racial prejudice, bigotry |反义词: racial equality, tolerance, inclusivity
