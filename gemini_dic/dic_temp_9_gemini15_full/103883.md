jubilee:/ˈdʒuːbɪli/| n. |禧年，庆祝；欢庆 |A jubilee is a period of celebration, especially one marking a special anniversary.  It often refers to a 50th anniversary but can be used for other significant anniversaries. |The city held a jubilee to celebrate its 200th anniversary.  这座城市举行禧年庆祝其建城200周年。|celebration, festivity, anniversary |mourning, sorrow |名词

一般现在时:
The company celebrates its jubilee every year. = 公司每年都庆祝它的禧年。
A jubilee marks a significant milestone. = 禧年标志着一个重要的里程碑。

We had a big jubilee party last year. = 去年我们举办了一个盛大的禧年派对。
