play up to:/pleɪ ʌp tuː/| v.|奉承；讨好；夸张；渲染|To act in a way that is intended to please someone, often in an insincere way.  It can also mean to exaggerate or emphasize something.|例句：He's always playing up to the boss. 他总是奉承老板。

例句：The media played up the scandal. 媒体夸大了丑闻。

近义词：flatter,  fawn on,  exaggerate,  emphasize

反义词：ignore, downplay,  understate

词性:动词
