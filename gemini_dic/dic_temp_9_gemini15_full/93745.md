immature: /ˌɪmɪˈtʃʊr/ | adj. | 不成熟的，幼稚的 |  Lacking the maturity expected of one's age or stage of development.  It refers to a lack of emotional, mental, or social development. | 例句：His immature behavior at the party embarrassed everyone.  他的幼稚行为在派对上让每个人都尴尬。

He made an immature decision to quit his job without having another one lined up. 他做出了一个不成熟的决定，在没有找到另一份工作的情况下就辞职了。


近义词：childish, juvenile, puerile, adolescent, callow | 反义词：mature, adult, grown-up, developed, sophisticated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is immature in his handling of relationships. = 他处理人际关系的方式很幼稚。
Her immature responses to criticism are concerning. = 她对批评的不成熟回应令人担忧。


一般过去时:
He was immature when he was younger. = 他年轻时很幼稚。
Her immature behavior in the meeting was unacceptable. = 她在会议上的幼稚行为是不可接受的。


现在完成时:
He has been immature throughout his life. = 他一生都很幼稚。
She has shown immature tendencies recently. = 她最近表现出不成熟的倾向。


过去完成时:
He had been immature before he learned responsibility. = 在他学会承担责任之前，他一直很幼稚。
She had been immature, which led to the misunderstanding. = 她以前很幼稚，这导致了误解。


将来时:
He will likely be more mature in the future. = 他将来可能会更成熟。
She will hopefully overcome her immature tendencies. = 她有望克服她幼稚的倾向。


虚拟语气:
If he were more mature, he would handle this differently. = 如果他更成熟，他会以不同的方式处理这件事。
I wish she were less immature in her approach. = 我希望她在处理问题时能少一些幼稚。


被动语态:
His actions were seen as immature by many. = 许多人认为他的行为很幼稚。
Her opinions were dismissed as immature and unfounded. = 她的观点被认为幼稚且毫无根据。


疑问句:
Is his behavior immature, or is it something else? = 他的行为是不成熟，还是其他什么原因？
Are her reactions really that immature? = 她的反应真的那么幼稚吗？


否定句:
His actions weren't immature; he just needed time to adjust. = 他的行为并不幼稚；他只是需要时间来适应。
Her response wasn't immature; she was simply stressed. = 她的回应并不幼稚；她只是压力很大。
