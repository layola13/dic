interviewers:/ˌɪntərˈvjuːərz/| n. |面试官；采访者 |People who conduct interviews.  They ask questions to assess a candidate's suitability for a job or to gather information for a news story or research. |Example: The interviewers were impressed by the candidate's experience. 面试官们对这位候选人的经验印象深刻。|近义词：interview panel, questioners |反义词：interviewees
