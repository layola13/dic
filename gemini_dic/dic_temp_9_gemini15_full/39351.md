conjugably: /ˈkɒndʒʊɡəbli/ | adv. | 可结合地；可共轭地 |  Adverb meaning in a way that can be conjugated or combined.  It refers to the ability of something (usually grammatical elements) to be combined or linked together in a grammatical structure. This word is relatively uncommon and primarily used in linguistics and grammatical discussions.


例句：

The suffixes can be conjugably attached to the verb stem.  = 这些后缀可以结合到动词词干上。

These elements are not conjugably linked in this sentence structure. = 在这个句型中，这些成分不能结合在一起。


近义词：  combinably, joinably (less precise, more general)


反义词：  inseparably (opposite in the sense of not being able to be combined)


词性：副词
