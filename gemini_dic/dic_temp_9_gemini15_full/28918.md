carried:/ˈkæriːd/| v.| 携带；运送；支撑；承担 | To transport or convey something from one place to another; to support or bear the weight of something; to sustain or endure something. |例句：He carried a heavy suitcase. 他提着一个沉重的行李箱。

例句：The river carried debris downstream. 河流把碎屑冲向下游。


近义词：conveyed, transported, borne, sustained, endured| 反义词：left, abandoned, dropped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carries a heavy backpack. = 他背着一个沉重的背包。
She carries herself with grace. = 她举止优雅。


一般过去时:
They carried the injured man to safety. = 他们把受伤的人抬到安全的地方。
I carried the groceries into the kitchen. = 我把杂货搬进了厨房。


现在完成时:
The wind has carried the seeds far and wide. = 风把种子吹到很远的地方。
She has carried her responsibilities well. = 她很好地履行了她的责任。


过去完成时:
He had carried the burden for many years. = 他多年来一直承受着重担。
They had carried out the plan successfully. = 他们成功地执行了计划。


将来时:
We will carry the equipment to the campsite. = 我们会把设备搬到营地。
They will carry on with their work tomorrow. = 他们明天会继续他们的工作。


虚拟语气:
If he carried the weight alone, he would be exhausted. = 如果他独自承担重量，他会筋疲力尽。
I wish I had carried out my promise. = 我真希望我履行了我的诺言。


被动语态:
The message was carried by a messenger. = 这条信息是由信使传达的。
The injured player was carried off the field. = 受伤的球员被抬出了赛场。


疑问句:
Does he carry a gun? = 他带枪吗？
What did they carry in the boxes? = 他们箱子里装了什么？


否定句:
He didn't carry out his threat. = 他没有实行他的威胁。
The river does not carry much water during the dry season. = 在旱季，河水流量不大。


PHRASE->
carry on = 继续
carry out = 执行；完成
carry forward = 结转；发扬
carry through = 完成；渡过难关
carry weight = 有影响力；有分量
