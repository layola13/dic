relaxers: /rɪˈlæksərz/ | n. | 舒缓剂；放松剂；（尤指）烫发剂 |  Substances or methods used to relax or straighten hair, or to relieve tension or stress.  They can refer to chemical treatments for hair or methods for stress relief. |

例句：
She uses chemical relaxers to straighten her naturally curly hair. = 她用化学烫发剂来拉直她天生卷曲的头发。

Many people find yoga to be a good relaxer. = 许多人发现瑜伽是一种很好的放松方法。


近义词：relaxants,  stress relievers (for stress relief meaning), straighteners (for hair meaning)

反义词：stressors, tension builders (for stress relief meaning),curlers (for hair meaning)

词性:名词
