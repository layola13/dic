incubate:/ˈɪŋkjuˌbeɪt/|/ˈɪŋkjubeɪt/| vt.  |孵化；培养；酝酿|To incubate means to keep eggs warm so that they hatch, or to keep something at a suitable temperature for development. It can also refer to fostering the growth or development of something. |例句：The hen incubated her eggs for three weeks. 母鸡孵蛋三周。  The scientist incubated the bacteria in a petri dish. 科学家在培养皿中培养细菌。|近义词：hatch, brood, foster, nurture|反义词：destroy, neglect
