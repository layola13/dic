musicology:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒi/|/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɑːlədʒi/| n. |音乐学|The study of music as an academic discipline, encompassing its history, theory, and cultural context.  Musicology involves the scholarly analysis of music from various perspectives, including its social, cultural, and historical influences. |例句：She pursued a doctoral degree in musicology, specializing in the music of the Baroque period. 她攻读音乐学博士学位，专门研究巴洛克时期的音乐。

例句：His musicology research focuses on the relationship between music and social movements in the 20th century. 他的音乐学研究集中在20世纪音乐与社会运动之间的关系。


近义词：music history, music studies|反义词：None|词性：n.
