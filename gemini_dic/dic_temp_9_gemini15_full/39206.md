congestive:/kənˈdʒɛstɪv/ | adj. | 充血的；淤血的；心力衰竭的 | Relating to or characterized by congestion; especially of the blood vessels.  The term often refers to congestive heart failure, a condition where the heart cannot pump enough blood to meet the body's needs, leading to fluid buildup.  | 例句：He suffered from congestive heart failure. 他患有充血性心力衰竭。  | 例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as congestive pneumonia. 医生诊断她的病情为充血性肺炎。 | 近义词：congested, blocked, obstructed | 反义词：uncongested, clear, unobstructed
