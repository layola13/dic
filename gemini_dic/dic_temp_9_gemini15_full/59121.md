dynamicism: /ˈdaɪnəmɪzəm/ | n. |活力；动感；动力；动势|The quality of being dynamic; characterized by constant change, activity, or progress.  It refers to the force that produces movement or change. |例句：The director's dynamicism infused the film with energy.  导演的活力给电影注入了能量。|例句：The company's success is a testament to its dynamicism and innovation.  公司的成功证明了它的活力和创新。|近义词：energy, vitality, vigor, momentum, activism |反义词：stagnation, inertia, passivity, lethargy |名词

词源:
源自希腊语 dynamis ( δύναμις ), 意思是“力量，能力”，加上后缀 -ism ，表示“特性，主义”。因此，dynamicism 指的是一种力量或能量的特性，表现为持续的变化、活动或进步。
