looking:/ˈlʊkɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 看着；寻找；显得 |  Looking is the present participle of the verb "to look". It can function as a verb or an adjective. As a verb, it describes the act of seeing or searching. As an adjective, it describes appearance or condition. | 例句：I am looking at the beautiful sunset. 我正在看美丽的日落。  He is looking for his lost keys. 他正在寻找他丢失的钥匙。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am looking at the birds. = 我正在看鸟。
She is looking for a new job. = 她正在找一份新工作。


一般过去时:
I was looking at the painting. = 我当时正在看那幅画。
He was looking for a solution. = 他当时正在寻找解决办法。


现在完成时:
I have been looking for my glasses all morning. = 我整个上午都在找我的眼镜。
She has been looking forward to this day for weeks. = 她几周来一直期待着这一天。


过去完成时:
I had been looking for a long time before I found it. = 我找了很久才找到它。
They had been looking for a house before they decided to build one. = 他们决定建房子之前一直在找房子。


将来时:
I will be looking for you at the airport. = 我将在机场等你。
They will be looking for a new apartment next month. = 他们下个月将要找一套新公寓。


虚拟语气:
If I were looking for a job, I would apply to that company. = 如果我在找工作，我会申请那家公司。
I wish I were looking better today. = 我希望我今天看起来更好。


被动语态:
The painting is being looked at by many people. = 这幅画正在被很多人观看。
The problem is being looked into by the committee. = 委员会正在调查这个问题。


疑问句:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she looking for? = 她正在找什么？


否定句:
I am not looking for trouble. = 我不想惹麻烦。
He wasn't looking at me. = 他没有看我。


PHRASE->
looking forward to: 期待
looking after: 照顾
looking for: 寻找
looking good: 看起来不错
looking out: 当心


近义词:  seeing, searching, seeking, appearing, seeming


反义词: ignoring, neglecting, overlooking


词性: verb (动词), adjective (形容词)
