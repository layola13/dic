dunging: /ˈdʌŋɪŋ/ | v. |施肥；粪肥；清理粪便 | The act of applying dung (manure) as fertilizer to land.  It can also refer to the process of removing dung from stables or other areas. |例句：The farmer spent the afternoon dunging the fields. 农民花了整个下午给田地施肥。

The stable needs dunging. 马厩需要清理粪便。|近义词：manuring, fertilizing, mucking out |反义词：N/A

USAGE->

They dung the fields regularly. = 他们定期给田地施肥。
He is dunging the stable now. = 他现在正在清理马厩。
