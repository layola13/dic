cloot:/kluːt/| n.|鞋帮；（苏格兰）脚趾；（非正式）笨蛋；|A piece of leather or other material forming the upper part of a shoe or boot; (Scotland) a toe; (informal) a stupid person. |例句：The cloot of his boot was worn.他的靴子鞋帮磨损了。The boy tripped over a cloot. 男孩被脚趾绊倒了。He's a right cloot! 他真是个笨蛋！|近义词：sole (鞋底), heel (鞋跟), (informal) idiot, fool |反义词: genius, smart
