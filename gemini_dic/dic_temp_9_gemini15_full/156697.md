pie's:/paɪz/| n.|派(所有格)|The possessive form of "pie," indicating ownership or belonging.|例句：That's the baker's pie. 那是面包师的派。|例句：The pie's crust was flaky and delicious. 派皮酥脆可口。|近义词: tart's,pastry's|反义词: None


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical forms like other parts of speech.  It only shows possession.  Therefore, tense and voice are not applicable.


PHRASE->
a piece of pie = 一块派
apple pie = 苹果派
cherry pie = 樱桃派
pumpkin pie = 南瓜派
shepherd's pie = 牧羊人派 (a type of pie)
