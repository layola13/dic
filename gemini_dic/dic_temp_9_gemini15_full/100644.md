intolerantnesses:/ɪnˈtɑːlərəntnəsɪz/ | n. |不容忍；不宽容；偏执 |The plural form of intolerance, referring to multiple instances or types of intolerance.  It signifies a lack of tolerance towards different opinions, beliefs, or behaviors.  |例句：His intolerantnesses towards other cultures were evident in his prejudiced remarks.  他的不宽容体现在他对其他文化的偏见言论中。

His intolerantnesses manifested in his refusal to engage with differing viewpoints. 他的不宽容表现在他拒绝与不同观点接触上。


近义词：bigotries, prejudices, biases |反义词：tolerances, acceptances
