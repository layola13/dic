paused:/pɔːzd/ |/pɔːzd/| adj. v. |暂停的；中止的；停顿的|  Describes something that has been temporarily stopped.  It can be used as an adjective to describe a state or as a verb to describe an action. |例句：The movie was paused. = 电影暂停了。


The music paused abruptly. = 音乐突然停顿了。|近义词：stopped, interrupted, suspended|反义词：continued, resumed, started|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The game is paused. = 游戏暂停了。
He pauses frequently during his speech. = 他在演讲中经常停顿。


一般过去时:
She paused before answering. = 她停顿了一下才回答。
The machine paused unexpectedly. = 机器意外地停住了。


现在完成时:
The project has been paused until further notice. = 项目已暂停，直至另行通知。
They have paused several times during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间暂停了好几次。


过去完成时:
He had paused to consider his options. = 他停下来考虑他的选择。
The workers had paused for lunch. = 工人们停下来吃午饭了。


将来时:
The show will be paused for commercials. = 节目将暂停播放广告。
We will pause the discussion until tomorrow. = 我们将把讨论推迟到明天。


虚拟语气:
If I had paused to think, I might have avoided the mistake. = 如果我停下来思考一下，我可能会避免这个错误。
I wish he had paused before speaking. = 我希望他在说话前能停顿一下。


被动语态:
The game was paused by the referee. = 游戏被裁判暂停了。
The recording was paused accidentally. = 录音意外暂停了。


疑问句:
Is the recording paused? = 录音暂停了吗？
Did he pause for breath? = 他停下来喘口气了吗？


否定句:
The music didn't pause. = 音乐没有暂停。
He didn't pause to explain. = 他没有停下来解释。
