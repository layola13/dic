powerhouse:/ˈpaʊərhɑʊs/ | n. |动力源，强国，能量中心，实力雄厚的人或事物 |A powerhouse is a source of great power or energy, either literally or figuratively. It can refer to a building that generates power, a country with significant economic or military strength, or a person or organization with exceptional influence or ability. |例句：The new power plant is a significant powerhouse for the region.  这个新的发电厂是该地区重要的动力源。

例句：She's a powerhouse in the business world, known for her sharp intellect and drive. 她是商界的实力人物，以其敏锐的智力和干劲而闻名。


近义词：giant, dynamo, force, leader |反义词：weakling, lightweight, insignificant
