guvnor:/ˈɡʌvnər/| n. |老板，上司，头儿 |A familiar and informal term for a boss or manager, especially in British English.  It's a shortened and altered form of "governor".|Example: "I'll have to ask the guvnor about that." = 我得问问老板这事儿。|近义词：boss, manager, chief, superior |反义词：subordinate, employee
