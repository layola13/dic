gomorrah:/ɡəˈmɒrə/| n. |所多玛（罪恶之城）|The biblical city of Gomorrah, along with Sodom, is famously described as a place of wickedness and debauchery, destroyed by God for its sins.  It is often used as a metaphor for a place of extreme wickedness or moral corruption.|例句：The city became a modern Gomorrah, rife with crime and vice.  这座城市变成了现代的所多玛，充斥着犯罪和恶习。|The destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah is a cautionary tale about the consequences of sin. 所多玛和蛾摩拉的毁灭是一个关于罪恶后果的警示故事。|近义词：Sodom, Babylon (in the sense of a wicked city)|反义词：paradise, utopia|名词
