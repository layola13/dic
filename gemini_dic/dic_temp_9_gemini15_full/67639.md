extricates:/ˈɛkstrɪkeɪts/| vt.|使解脱；救出；脱险|To free (someone or something) from a constraint or difficulty.|The firefighters extricated the victims from the burning building. 消防队员将受害者从燃烧的建筑物中救了出来。|近义词：liberate, rescue, release|反义词：entangle, trap, ensnare|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extricates himself from difficult situations with ease. 他轻松地摆脱困境。
She extricates her dog from the thorny bush. 她把她的狗从荆棘丛中解救出来。


一般过去时:
The rescuers extricated the trapped miners. 救援人员救出了被困的矿工。
He extricated himself from the complicated legal battle. 他摆脱了复杂的法律纠纷。


现在完成时:
They have extricated the company from financial ruin. 他们已使公司摆脱了财政困境。
She has extricated herself from a toxic relationship. 她已经摆脱了一段有毒的关系。


过去完成时:
The police had extricated the hostages before the deadline. 警方在最后期限前解救了人质。
He had extricated himself from the lie before anyone noticed. 他在任何人注意到之前就摆脱了谎言。


将来时:
