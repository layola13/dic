creepingly:/ˈkriːpɪŋli/| adv. |令人毛骨悚然地，缓慢地，逐渐地 |  Slowly and secretly, in a way that is slightly frightening or unpleasant.  It describes an action or process that happens gradually and often in a disturbing or unsettling manner. |例句：The shadows lengthened creepingly across the floor.  阴影慢慢地爬过地板。 The temperature creepingly rose throughout the day. 温度一整天都在缓慢地上升。|近义词：insidiously, gradually, stealthily, subtly |反义词：quickly, rapidly, openly, obviously
