reinflames:/ˌriɪnˈfleɪmz/| vt.|重新点燃；使复燃；使再次爆发|To ignite or cause to burn again; to revive or rekindle something, especially a conflict or feeling.|例句：The recent events have reinflamed tensions between the two countries. 近期发生的事件加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。

例句：His angry words reinflamed the old argument. 他愤怒的话语重新点燃了旧日的争论。|近义词：reignite, rekindle, exacerbate|反义词：extinguish, quell, calm
