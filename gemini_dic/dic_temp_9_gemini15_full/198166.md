shoreline:/ˈʃɔːrlaɪn/ | n. |海岸线，岸边 |The line where the land meets the sea or a lake.  It refers to the edge of the land bordering a body of water. |例句：The shoreline was littered with debris after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海岸线上布满了碎片。

例句：We walked along the shoreline, enjoying the sunset. 我们沿着海岸线散步，欣赏着日落。


近义词：coastline, beach, waterfront |反义词：inland, interior


词源：shore (n.) + line (n.)  "shore" originates from Old English *scōr, meaning "shore, bank."  "line" comes from Old English līne, meaning "line, cord."  The combination thus literally means "the line of the shore."
