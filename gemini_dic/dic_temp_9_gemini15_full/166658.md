preseparates:/ˌpriːˈsɛpəreɪts/|v.|预先分开，预先分离|To separate beforehand or in advance.|The lab technician preseparates the blood samples before analysis.  实验室技术员在分析之前预先分离血样。|近义词：predivide, separate beforehand|反义词：combine, unite|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preseparates the ingredients before baking. = 他在烘焙前预先准备好配料。
She preseparates the laundry by color. = 她按颜色预先分好要洗的衣服。


一般过去时:
They preseparated the recyclables from the trash. = 他们把可回收垃圾从普通垃圾中预先分开了。
The chef preseparated the herbs for the garnish. = 厨师预先分好了用于装饰的香草。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

She had preseparated the toys for the children's party. = 她已经为孩子们派对预先分好了玩具。
