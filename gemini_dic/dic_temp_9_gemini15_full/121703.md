mispredicting:/ˌmɪspɹɪˈdɪktɪŋ/ | v. | 错误预测，预测错误 |  The act of incorrectly predicting something; failing to accurately foresee an outcome.  | Example: The model was criticized for mispredicting the election results.  模型因错误预测选举结果而受到批评。  The meteorologist was accused of mispredicting the severity of the storm. 气象学家被指责错误预测了风暴的严重程度。 |近义词:  forecasting incorrectly, erring in prediction, getting wrong | 反义词: accurately predicting, correctly forecasting
