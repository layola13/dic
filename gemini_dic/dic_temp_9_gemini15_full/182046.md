remembrancer: /rɪˈmɛmbrənsər/ | n. |纪念者；纪念品；遗物 | A person or thing that serves as a reminder or keeps a memory alive.  It can refer to a person who commemorates an event or person, or an object that serves as a memorial. |例句：The old photograph served as a treasured remembrancer of her childhood. 这张旧照片是她童年珍贵的纪念品。  The museum curator is a dedicated remembrancer of local history. 博物馆馆长是当地历史的忠实纪念者。 |近义词：memorial, reminder, keepsake, souvenir |反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion |词性：名词
