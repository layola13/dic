gulches:/ˈɡʌltʃɪz/| n. |峡谷；沟壑 |A gulch is a steep, narrow ravine or gully, often with a stream running through it. It's typically smaller than a canyon but larger than a ditch.  |例句：The miners discovered gold in the bottom of the gulch.  矿工们在峡谷底部发现了黄金。

The hikers carefully navigated the treacherous gulch.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地穿过险峻的峡谷。|近义词：ravine, gully, canyon, gorge |反义词：plain, plateau
