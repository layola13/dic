insubmergible:/ˌɪnsəˈmɜːrdʒəbəl/| adj.|不可沉没的，不会沉没的| incapable of being submerged or sunk; unsinkable.  The word highlights the inability of something to be submerged or go underwater and remain there. It often implies a robustness and resilience against the forces that would cause sinking.|例句：The ship was designed to be insubmergible, but it still sank. 这艘船的设计是不可沉没的，但它还是沉了。|例句：Despite the storm, the insubmergible spirit of the crew kept them going. 尽管暴风雨来袭，船员们不可战胜的精神让他们坚持了下来。|近义词：unsinkable, indestructible, invulnerable|反义词：submersible, sinkable
