/ˈmeɪbiz/| n.|也许，可能|Maybe is used to express uncertainty or possibility.  It is often used as a response to a question or statement where the speaker is unsure of the answer.|例句：Maybe I'll go to the party. = 也许我会去参加派对。

例句：Maybe it will rain tomorrow. = 也许明天会下雨。

|近义词：perhaps, possibly, perchance|反义词：certainly, definitely, surely|名词
