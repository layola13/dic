cloudlifter:/ˈklaʊdˌlɪftər/| n. |云升器，提升器|A cloud lifter is a piece of audio equipment designed to boost the level of quiet signals, particularly those from low-output microphones, making them louder and clearer.  It essentially acts as a preamplifier, but with features optimized for handling the subtle nuances of quiet audio sources without introducing significant noise. |Example:  The cloud lifter significantly improved the sound quality of my quiet condenser microphone. = 云升器显著提高了我安静的电容麦克风的音质。

The cloud lifter helped to bring out the detail in the quiet acoustic guitar recording. = 云升器帮助突显了安静的原声吉他录音中的细节。 |近义词：preamplifier, signal booster |反义词：attenuator, noise gate
