covers:/ˈkʌvərz/ | n. v. |覆盖物；封面；报道；涵盖 | To place something over or on top of something else; to provide protection or concealment; to report on something in the media; to include or encompass something. |例句：The covers on the bed were soft and warm. 床上的被子柔软温暖。  The magazine covers a wide range of topics. 这本杂志涵盖了广泛的主题。|近义词：includes, encompasses, envelops, reports |反义词：uncovers, reveals, excludes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He covers the table with a cloth. = 他用一块布盖住桌子。
The reporter covers the latest news. = 记者报道最新的新闻。


一般过去时:
She covered her face with her hands. = 她用手捂住了脸。
The newspaper covered the election results. = 报纸报道了选举结果。


现在完成时:
They have covered a lot of ground in their research. = 他们的研究已经取得了很大的进展。
We have covered all the important points in the meeting. = 我们在会议上涵盖了所有重要的要点。


过去完成时:
They had covered the distance before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临前已经走完了这段路程。
She had covered the story before her colleague. = 她比她的同事先报道了这个故事。


将来时:
We will cover the entire city in our survey. = 我们将在调查中覆盖整个城市。
The journalist will cover the upcoming summit. = 记者将报道即将举行的峰会。


虚拟语气:
If I covered the cost, I would be happy to participate. = 如果我承担费用，我很乐意参加。
I wish I had covered myself with a blanket. = 我真希望我用毯子盖住了自己。


被动语态:
The furniture was covered with dust. = 家具上蒙了一层灰尘。
The event was extensively covered by the media. = 这件事被媒体广泛报道。


疑问句:
Does this insurance cover medical expenses? = 这份保险涵盖医疗费用吗？
What topics does the book cover? = 这本书涵盖哪些主题？


否定句:
The insurance policy doesn't cover accidents. = 保险单不承保意外事故。
The report didn't cover the full details. = 报告没有涵盖全部细节。
