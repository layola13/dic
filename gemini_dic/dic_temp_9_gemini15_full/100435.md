intertrading: /ˌɪntərˈtreɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 国际贸易；国际交易 | The act or process of conducting business transactions between countries.  It involves the exchange of goods, services, or capital across international borders. | Example:  Intertrading between China and the US has significantly increased in recent years.  中国和美国之间的国际贸易近年来大幅增加。 | 近义词: international trade, global commerce, cross-border trading | 反义词: domestic trade, local commerce | 名词
