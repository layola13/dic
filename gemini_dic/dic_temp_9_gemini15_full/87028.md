hepatitis:/ˌhɛpəˈtaɪtɪs/| n.|肝炎|Hepatitis is an inflammation of the liver.  It can be caused by various viruses, alcohol abuse, or autoimmune disorders.  |例句：Viral hepatitis is a serious condition that requires medical attention. 病毒性肝炎是一种需要医疗护理的严重疾病。|例句：The doctor suspected hepatitis after reviewing the patient's symptoms.  医生在查看患者的症状后怀疑是肝炎。|近义词：liver inflammation, liver disease|反义词：liver health
