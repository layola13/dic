multimillions:/ˌmʌltiˈmɪljənz/| adj. |数百万的，几百万的|  Describes a quantity in the millions, often implying a very large, unspecified number of millions.  It emphasizes the vastness of the number rather than providing a precise figure.|例句：The company boasts multimillions of dollars in annual revenue. 这家公司拥有数百万美元的年收入。
The project requires multimillions of investment. 该项目需要数百万美元的投资。|近义词：millions, many millions, numerous millions|反义词：a few, handful, thousands
