marinate:/ˈmærɪneɪt/ | vt. | 腌制；浸渍；使充满（某种气氛） | To soak food, typically meat or fish, in a seasoned liquid before cooking, to add flavor and tenderness.  |例句：I marinated the chicken in a lemon and herb marinade for several hours before grilling. 我在烤鸡肉之前用柠檬和香草腌料腌制了几个小时。

例句：The long, slow negotiations marinated the atmosphere with tension.  长时间缓慢的谈判使气氛充满了紧张。


近义词：pickle, soak, steep | 反义词：dry, roast
