pseudobiological:/ˌsuːdoʊbaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj.|伪生物学的；似生物的|Relating to or resembling biology but not truly biological; often used to describe systems or processes that mimic biological systems but lack the fundamental characteristics of life.  It often implies an artificial or synthetic imitation of biological processes.| Example: The researchers developed a pseudobiological model to simulate the spread of a virus.  研究人员开发了一个伪生物模型来模拟病毒的传播。|近义词：artificial, synthetic, simulated, quasi-biological|反义词：genuine, authentic, truly biological
