freemen's:/ˈfriːmɛnz/| n. |自由民的；自由人所属的|The term "freemen's" refers to something belonging to or associated with freemen, individuals who are not bound by servitude or other forms of involuntary obligation.  It often appears in historical contexts referring to guilds, organizations, or properties associated with individuals possessing certain rights and freedoms.|Example: The freemen's guild held a significant amount of political power.  自由民协会拥有相当大的政治权力。  The freemen's land was granted by the king. 国王授予自由民土地。|近义词: independent, liberated |反义词: enslaved, serfdom
