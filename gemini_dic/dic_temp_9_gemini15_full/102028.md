isoimmune:/ˌaɪsoʊɪˈmjuːn/| adj. |同种异体免疫的；同种免疫的 | Relating to immunity against an antigen from the same species.  Isoimmune reactions occur when an individual's immune system reacts against antigens present in the same species, often due to blood type incompatibility or tissue transplantation. |Example:  Rh incompatibility is a classic example of isoimmune hemolytic disease of the newborn.  新生儿溶血病的典型例子就是Rh血型不合。|近义词：alloimmune (though technically distinct)|反义词：autoimmune
