caruncula:/kəˈrʌŋkjʊlə/| n. |小肉阜；(眼)内眦赘皮；(鸟类)肉垂|A small, fleshy protuberance, especially one of the small, reddish elevations at the inner corner of the eye (the caruncle). In birds, it refers to a fleshy outgrowth on the head or neck.  |例句：The infant's eye caruncle was slightly inflamed. = 婴儿的眼内眦赘皮轻微发炎。

The bird displayed a bright red caruncle on its head. = 那只鸟的头上长着一个鲜红色的肉垂。|近义词：protuberance, excrescence, outgrowth|反义词：无
