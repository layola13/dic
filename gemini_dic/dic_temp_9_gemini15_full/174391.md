quiet-down:/ˈkwaɪət daʊn/| v. |安静下来，平静下来 |To become quieter; to make someone or something quieter.  This is a phrasal verb, meaning it's composed of a verb ("quiet") and a particle ("down").  It implies a reduction in noise or activity.|例句：The children quietened down when their mother entered the room. 孩子们在母亲进屋后安静了下来。

The teacher asked the students to quiet down. 老师让学生们安静下来。|近义词：calm down, settle down, be still|反义词：liven up, get excited, become noisy|词性:动词
