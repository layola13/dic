craziest: /ˈkreɪziɪst/ | adj. | 最疯狂的，最古怪的 |  The most extreme form of crazy;  implying the highest degree of madness, eccentricity, or foolishness.  It is the superlative form of "crazy." | 例句：That's the craziest idea I've ever heard!  = 那是听过最疯狂的主意！

That was the craziest party I've ever been to! = 那是我参加过的最疯狂的派对！


近义词：most insane, most mad, most wild, most absurd, most outlandish | 反义词：most sane, most rational, most sensible, most normal


词源：源自于中古英语 "craz,"  meaning "cracked" or "broken," referring to a fractured mind.  This evolved into "crazy," meaning mentally ill or eccentric, and then to its modern usage, including both positive and negative connotations depending on context.
