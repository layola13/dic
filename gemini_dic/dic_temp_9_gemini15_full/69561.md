fellowships:/ˈfɛloʊʃɪps/ | n. |奖学金；研究员职位；协会；友谊 | Fellowships are grants or scholarships that provide financial support to individuals pursuing advanced studies or research.  They can be awarded by universities, governments, or private organizations.  | 例句：He received a fellowship to study abroad. 他获得了一项奖学金去国外留学。

He was awarded a prestigious fellowship at Oxford University. 他获得了牛津大学的著名研究员职位。


近义词：scholarships, grants, bursaries | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
