gateways:/ˈɡeɪtweɪz/| n.| 网关，门户，通道 | A gateway is an entrance, a point of access, or a means of entering or leaving a place or system.  It can be a physical structure like a gate or a more abstract concept like a software interface. | Example: The old stone gateway led to a hidden garden.  古老的石门通向一个隐藏的花园。

The company uses a secure gateway to protect its data. 公司使用安全的网关来保护其数据。|近义词：entrance, access point, portal, interface |反义词：exit, blockage, barrier
