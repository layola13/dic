cleanups:/ˈkliːnʌps/ | n. |清理工作；整顿；清除行动 |The process of cleaning something up, typically a large-scale or significant cleaning effort. It often refers to removing unwanted or harmful substances or materials.  |例句：The environmental cleanups after the oil spill were extensive.  石油泄漏后的环境清理工作非常广泛。

例句：The city initiated a series of cleanups to address the litter problem.  城市启动了一系列清理工作以解决垃圾问题。


近义词：cleanings, clear-ups, removals, purges | 反义词：messes, pollutions, contaminations | 词性: 名词
