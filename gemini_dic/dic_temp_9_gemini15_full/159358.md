poke around:/poʊk əˈraʊnd/ | v. |四处摸索；闲逛；探究 |To search or explore a place casually or informally, often without a specific purpose.  |例句：I poked around in the attic looking for old photos. 我在阁楼里四处翻找旧照片。

例句：He spent the afternoon poking around the antique shops. 他下午在古董店里闲逛。


近义词：rummage, explore, investigate, snoop


反义词：organize, tidy, clean


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I poke around in my garden every day. = 我每天都在我的花园里四处看看。
They poke around the flea market looking for bargains. = 他们在跳蚤市场四处寻找便宜货。


一般过去时:
She poked around the old house, feeling a sense of mystery. = 她在这个旧房子里四处探寻，感到一种神秘感。
We poked around the bookstore for a good hour before finding anything. = 我们在书店里闲逛了一个小时才找到东西。


现在完成时:
I've poked around in this drawer, but I can't find my keys. = 我已经在这个抽屉里翻找过了，但我找不到我的钥匙。
They've poked around the woods several times, but still haven't found the lost dog. = 他们已经好几次在树林里四处寻找了，但仍然没有找到丢失的狗。


过去完成时:
He had poked around the basement before reporting nothing unusual. = 他在报告没有异常情况之前已经搜查过地下室了。
We had poked around the antique shop for hours before we found the perfect vase. = 我们在古董店里逛了好几个小时才找到完美的那个花瓶。


将来时:
I will poke around the library tomorrow looking for books on ancient history. = 我明天会在图书馆里四处寻找关于古代历史的书籍。
They will poke around the town center before deciding where to have dinner. = 他们会在决定在哪里吃晚饭之前在镇中心四处逛逛。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would poke around the museum more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地参观博物馆。
I wish I had poked around the garage more carefully; I might have found my tools. = 我真希望我更仔细地搜查过车库；我本来可能找到我的工具的。

疑问句:

否定句:
