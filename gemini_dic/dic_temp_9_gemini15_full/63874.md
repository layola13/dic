epileptoid:/ˈɛpɪlɛptɔɪd/| adj.|癫痫样的，类癫痫的|Resembling epilepsy or an epileptic seizure in character or symptoms; suggestive of epilepsy.  The term often describes a condition that mimics epilepsy but doesn't have the same underlying cause.|例句：The patient exhibited epileptoid seizures, but EEG testing ruled out true epilepsy.  病人表现出癫痫样发作，但脑电图检查排除了真正的癫痫。|近义词：convulsive, seizure-like|反义词：none specifically, but terms describing absence of seizure activity could be considered antonyms.
