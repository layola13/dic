henpecking:/ˈhenˌpekɪŋ/| n. v.|唠叨；悍妻的责骂|The act of a wife constantly nagging or scolding her husband.|例句：His henpecking wife drove him to drink.  他那唠叨的妻子把他逼得酗酒。  The constant henpecking wore him down.  没完没了的唠叨磨垮了他。|近义词：nagging, scolding, nagging, haranguing|反义词：praising, complimenting
