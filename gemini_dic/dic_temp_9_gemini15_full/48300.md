decreet:/dɪˈkriːt/ | n. |法令；命令；判决 | A formal order or decision, especially one made by a person in authority.  |例句：The king issued a decree banning the sale of alcohol. 国王发布法令禁止出售酒精。|例句：The court issued a decree ordering the defendant to pay damages. 法院发布判决，责令被告赔偿损失。|近义词：edict, ordinance, order, judgment |反义词：request, suggestion
