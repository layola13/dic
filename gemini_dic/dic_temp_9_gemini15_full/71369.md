flagellation:/ˌflædʒəˈleɪʃən/| n. |鞭打；鞭刑；痛斥|The act of whipping or flogging; a severe criticism or denunciation.|例句：The flagellation of the prisoners was a brutal act. 囚犯的鞭打是一件残酷的行为。

例句：His harsh words amounted to a flagellation of her character. 他尖刻的话语是对她品格的痛斥。


近义词：whipping, flogging, scourging, castigation|反义词：praise, commendation, applause|词性：名词
