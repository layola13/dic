nuthatch:/ˈnʌtˌhætʃ/| n. |攀树鸟|A small, active bird that climbs trees headfirst, often searching for insects and nuts. Nuthatches are known for their distinctive behavior of climbing down tree trunks and branches, unlike most birds that only climb upwards.|例句：The nuthatch expertly moved down the tree trunk, searching for insects.  一只攀树鸟熟练地顺着树干向下移动，寻找昆虫。

例句：We saw a red-breasted nuthatch clinging to the underside of a branch. 我们看到一只红胸攀树鸟紧紧地依附在树枝的下面。|近义词：tree creeper|反义词：无|词性：名词
