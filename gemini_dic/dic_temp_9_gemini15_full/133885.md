nongrieving: /ˌnɒnˈɡriːvɪŋ/ | adj. | 未哀伤的；未悲伤的 | Not experiencing grief or sorrow.  The term implies a lack of the expected emotional response to loss or bereavement. It doesn't necessarily mean happiness or indifference, but rather an absence of grief. | Example:  The nongrieving widow surprised everyone with her calm demeanor.  =  这位没有悲伤的寡妇以她平静的态度让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词: unemotional, stoic, apathetic, unmoved, unaffected

反义词: grieving, sorrowful, mournful, heartbroken, bereaved


词性: adjective
