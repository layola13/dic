furniture: /ˈfɜːrnɪtʃər/ | n. | 家具 |  Furniture refers to the movable objects inside a building, such as chairs, tables, beds, desks, etc., that are used to make a room or building functional and comfortable. It encompasses a wide range of items designed for sitting, sleeping, eating, storing, and working.  |  The furniture in her apartment is modern and stylish.  她的公寓里的家具很现代时尚。  The antique furniture was a family heirloom.  这件古董家具是家族传家宝。 |  furnishings, fittings |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：lack of furniture, bareness) | 名词
