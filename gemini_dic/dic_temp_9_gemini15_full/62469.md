endoscopy:/ɛnˈdɒskəpi/| n.|内窥镜检查|The visual examination of the interior of a body cavity or organ using a specialized instrument called an endoscope.  It is a minimally invasive procedure used to diagnose and treat various medical conditions.|例句：The doctor recommended an endoscopy to investigate the source of his abdominal pain. 医生建议他做内窥镜检查以查明腹痛的根源。|近义词：internal examination, laparoscopy (for abdominal cavity)|反义词：external examination
