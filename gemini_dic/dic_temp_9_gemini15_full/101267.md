involucellum:/ˌɪnvɒljuˈsɛləm/| n. |小苞片，小总苞|A small bract, especially one of a whorl of bracts subtending a flower or flower cluster in some plants.  It's often found in the inflorescence of certain plant families.  The involucellum is distinct from the involucre, which is a whorl of bracts subtending a whole inflorescence.|The small involucellum surrounding the flower helped identify the species.|近义词：bractlet, small bract|反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would relate to the absence of such a structure.
