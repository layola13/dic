glassier:/ˈɡlæsiər/| adj.|玻璃似的；光滑的；冰冷的；冷漠的|More like glass; having a smooth, shiny surface like glass.  It can also imply coldness or lack of emotion.|The glassier surface of the lake reflected the setting sun.  湖面玻璃般光滑，反射着落日余晖。

The glassier stare of the stranger made me uncomfortable. 陌生人冰冷的目光让我感到不舒服。|glossy, shiny, glassy, polished|dull, matte, opaque, rough
