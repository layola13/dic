self-degradation:/ˌsɛlfˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| n. | 自我贬低；自我破坏；降解 | The process by which something breaks down or deteriorates, especially through internal processes.  This term often refers to the breakdown of a substance or material due to internal factors, or to a person's negative self-perception and actions that harm themselves. | Example:  The self-degradation of the protein led to its loss of function. 蛋白质的自我降解导致其功能丧失。

The environmental conditions caused the rapid self-degradation of the plastic bag.  环境条件导致塑料袋迅速自我降解。


He displayed a pattern of self-degradation through reckless behavior and substance abuse. 他通过鲁莽的行为和滥用药物表现出自我毁灭的模式。

Her constant self-degradation was a symptom of her deeper unhappiness. 她持续不断的自我贬低是她内心深处不快乐的症状。|近义词：decomposition, deterioration, self-destruction, self-abasement |反义词：self-improvement, self-enhancement, elevation|名词
