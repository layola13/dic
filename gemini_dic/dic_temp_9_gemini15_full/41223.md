corallian:/kɒˈræliən/| adj.|珊瑚的；珊瑚色的|Of or relating to coral.  The term often describes rocks or formations composed of coral. |例句：The corallian reef was teeming with life. 珊瑚礁充满了生机。The building's corallian facade was stunning. 这座建筑的珊瑚色外观令人惊叹。|近义词：coralline|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could include terms describing non-coral materials like "granitic" or "basaltic".
