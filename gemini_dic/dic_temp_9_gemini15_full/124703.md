mowing:/ˈmoʊɪŋ/| v.|割草；修剪；(用割草机)割(草)|The act of cutting grass, typically with a lawnmower.  It can also refer to cutting other vegetation.|例句：He spends his Saturday mornings mowing the lawn. 他星期六上午都在割草。
例句：The farmer is mowing the wheat. 农民正在收割小麦。|近义词：cutting, trimming, reaping|反义词：growing, cultivating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I mow the lawn every week. = 我每周都割草。
She mows the grass in the field. = 她割田里的草。

一般过去时:
He mowed the lawn yesterday. = 他昨天割了草。
They mowed the field with a tractor. = 他们用拖拉机割了田地。

现在完成时:
I have mowed the lawn twice this week. = 我这周已经割了两次草。
She has mowed all the grass in the garden. = 她已经把花园里的草都割完了。

过去完成时:
He had mowed the lawn before it rained. = 下雨之前他已经割了草。
They had mowed the wheat before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经收割了小麦。

将来时:
I will mow the lawn tomorrow. = 我明天会割草。
We will mow the field next week. = 我们下周会割那块地。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would mow the lawn. = 如果我有时间，我会割草。
I wish I had mowed the lawn earlier. = 我真希望我早点割草。

被动语态:
The lawn was mowed yesterday. = 草坪昨天被割了。
The field is being mowed by the farmer. = 田地正在被农民收割。

疑问句:
Do you mow your own lawn? = 你自己割草吗？
Will they mow the field today? = 他们今天会割那块地吗？

否定句:
I didn't mow the lawn last week. = 我上周没有割草。
They won't mow the field tomorrow. = 他们明天不会割那块地。


PHRASE->
lawn mowing = 割草
rotary mowing = 旋转式割草
mowing machine = 割草机
power mowing = 动力割草
side mowing = 边缘修剪

词源:
The word "mowing" comes from the Old English word "mawan," meaning "to mow" or "to cut down (grass, etc.)."  The suffix "-ing" is a present participle suffix, indicating the action of mowing.
