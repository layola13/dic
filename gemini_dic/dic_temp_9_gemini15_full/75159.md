fritters:/ˈfrɪtərz/| n.|油炸小饼，小煎饼|Small pieces of food, such as vegetables or fruit, that have been dipped in batter and fried.  They are often served as a snack or appetizer.|例句：I made some zucchini fritters for dinner.  我晚餐做了些西葫芦油炸小饼。|例句：These onion fritters are delicious! 这些洋葱油炸小饼真好吃！|近义词：pancakes,  fritters|反义词：无

词源:
The word "fritter" as a noun comes from the Old French word "friture," meaning "fried food."  The verb form, meaning "to waste time or money," evolved later and is related to the idea of small pieces being easily wasted.
