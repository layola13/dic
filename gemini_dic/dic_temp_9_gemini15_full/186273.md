rhizophagous:/ˌraɪzˈɒfəɡəs/| adj.| 食根的；吃根的 | Feeding on roots.  Rhizophagous insects are a significant pest in agriculture, damaging crops by consuming their root systems.  |  The rhizophagous beetle larvae decimated the potato crop.  食根甲虫的幼虫摧毁了马铃薯作物。|  root-eating, root-feeding |  (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "folivorous" (leaf-eating) or "granivorous" (seed-eating) in contrast) | adj.
