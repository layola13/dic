leapfrogs:/ˈliːpfrɒɡz/ | v. |跳跃前进；超越；迅速超过 |To surpass or overtake (someone or something) by a large margin, often unexpectedly.  The verb implies a sudden and significant advancement, like someone jumping over obstacles to get ahead. |例句：The small startup leapfrogged its competitors by developing a revolutionary new product.  这家小型创业公司通过开发一款革命性的新产品迅速超越了竞争对手。

例句：The company leapfrogged the competition by adopting cutting-edge technology. 这家公司通过采用尖端技术超越了竞争对手。


近义词：surpass, outstrip, overtake, outpace |反义词：lag behind, fall short
