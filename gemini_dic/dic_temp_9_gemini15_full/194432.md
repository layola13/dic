self-refuted:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈfjutɪd/| adj.|自相矛盾的，自驳的|Self-refuted arguments are statements or claims that, by their very nature, contradict themselves or prove themselves false.  They contain internal inconsistencies that invalidate their conclusion.|Example:  The statement "This statement is false" is a classic example of a self-refuted argument.  这个陈述是假的，就是一个典型的自相矛盾的论证。|近义词：contradictory, inconsistent, paradoxical|反义词：consistent, logical, valid|
