preheat:/priːˈhiːt/| vt.|预热|To heat (something, such as an oven or engine) beforehand to a specified temperature.  |例句：Remember to preheat the oven to 350 degrees before baking the cake.  记住在烘烤蛋糕之前将烤箱预热到350度。

例句：The mechanic preheated the engine before starting the car. 机械师在启动汽车之前预热了发动机。


近义词：warm up, heat up|反义词：cool down

She preheats her car in the winter. = 冬天她会预热她的车。
