overdilate:/ˌoʊvərˈdaɪleɪt/| vt.|过度扩张，膨胀过度|To dilate or expand beyond normal limits.  It often refers to the excessive widening of a blood vessel or body opening.|例句：The medication caused his pupils to overdilate.  药物导致他的瞳孔过度扩张。
例句：The prolonged exposure to the sun caused her capillaries to overdilate, leading to sunburn.  长时间日晒导致她的毛细血管过度扩张，从而导致晒伤。|近义词：overexpand, distend excessively|反义词：constrict, contract
