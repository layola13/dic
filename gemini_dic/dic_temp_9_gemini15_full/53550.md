disbandment:/dɪsˈbændmənt/| n. |解散；解体；遣散|The act of disbanding something, especially a group or organization.  It refers to the formal ending of a group's existence, often implying a planned or official cessation of activities.|例句：The disbandment of the army was a controversial decision.  军队解散是一个有争议的决定。
The band announced their disbandment after their final concert. 乐队在最后一场演唱会后宣布解散。|近义词：dissolution, breakup, termination|反义词：formation, establishment, organization
