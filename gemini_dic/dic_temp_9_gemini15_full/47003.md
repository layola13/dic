dasher:/ˈdæʃər/| n. |搅拌器；雪橇的扶手；快跑的人|A dasher is a utensil used for stirring or mixing, especially in a churn to make butter. It can also refer to the handle on a sled or sleigh, or someone who dashes or runs quickly.|例句：She used a wooden dasher to churn the cream into butter. 她用木制搅拌器把奶油搅成黄油。

The children enjoyed riding on the sled, holding onto the dasher for dear life.孩子们喜欢乘坐雪橇，紧紧抓住扶手。|近义词：mixer, agitator, runner|反义词：None specifically.
