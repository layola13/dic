sizzled:/ˈsɪzəld/| v.|发出嘶嘶声；油炸；兴奋；激动|To make a hissing sound, typically when something is cooking in hot fat or oil.  It can also refer to a feeling of great excitement or energy.|例句：The bacon sizzled in the pan.  培根在锅里发出嘶嘶声。

The crowd sizzled with excitement.  人群兴奋不已。|近义词：sputtered, crackled, fried|反义词：cooled, calmed
