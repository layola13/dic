mother-like: /ˈmʌðərlaɪk/ | adj. |像母亲一样的，慈母般的|  Resembling a mother in her care, tenderness, and protectiveness.  It describes something or someone exhibiting qualities associated with a mother's nurturing and loving nature. |例句：She had a mother-like concern for her students. 她对她的学生有着母亲般的关爱。

例句：The teacher showed a mother-like patience with the slow learners. 老师对学习较慢的学生表现出母亲般的耐心。


近义词：maternal, motherly, nurturing, caring, protective

反义词：cold, uncaring, harsh, neglectful


词性：形容词
