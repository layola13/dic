sentiency:/ˈsɛnʃənsi/| n. |感知能力；有感觉；有知觉 |The ability to feel, perceive, or experience subjectively.  It often implies a capacity for consciousness and awareness.  Sentience is a complex concept and its exact definition is debated among philosophers and scientists. |Example:  The question of animal sentience is a hotly debated topic. 动物的感知能力是一个备受争议的话题。The robot, while sophisticated, lacked sentience.  这个机器人虽然很复杂，但缺乏感知能力。|近义词：awareness, consciousness, feeling, perception |反义词：insentience, unconsciousness
