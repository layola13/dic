ornamented:/ˈɔːrnəmentɪd/ | adj. | 装饰的；装饰华丽的 | Adorned with decorative features; highly decorated.  |例句：The palace was ornamented with intricate carvings.  宫殿装饰着复杂的雕刻。

The cake was beautifully ornamented with flowers and leaves. 蛋糕上装饰着漂亮的花朵和树叶。|近义词：decorated, embellished, adorned, embellished |反义词：plain, undecorated, bare

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The church is ornamented with beautiful stained glass. 这座教堂装饰着美丽的彩色玻璃。
This room is ornamented with antique furniture.  这个房间装饰着古董家具。


一般过去时:
The artist ornamented the vase with delicate floral patterns. 艺术家用精致的花卉图案装饰了花瓶。
They ornamented the Christmas tree with colorful lights and ornaments. 他们用彩灯和装饰品装饰了圣诞树。


现在完成时:
The building has been ornamented with new sculptures. 这座建筑已经装饰了新的雕塑。
She has ornamented her garden with a variety of colorful flowers. 她用各种各样的鲜艳花朵装饰了她的花园。


过去完成时:
The craftsman had ornamented the chest with precious stones before presenting it to the king. 工匠在将箱子献给国王之前，已经用宝石装饰好了它。
The architect had ornamented the cathedral with intricate details before the consecration. 建筑师在教堂祝圣之前，已经用复杂的细节装饰了它。


将来时:
They will ornament the hall with balloons for the party. 他们将在派对上用气球装饰大厅。
The designer will ornament the dress with lace and beads. 设计师将用蕾丝和珠子装饰这件衣服。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ornament the cake with more icing. 如果我有更多的时间，我会用更多的糖霜装饰蛋糕。
If the house were larger, we would ornament it with more paintings. 如果房子更大一些，我们会用更多的绘画来装饰它。


被动语态:
The walls were ornamented with tapestries. 墙壁上装饰着挂毯。
The cake is being ornamented with chocolate shavings. 蛋糕正在用巧克力屑装饰。


疑问句:
Is the building ornamented with any historical artifacts? 这座建筑物是否装饰着任何历史文物？
Are the walls ornamented with paintings? 墙壁上装饰着绘画吗？


否定句:
The room wasn't ornamented with anything special. 房间里没有任何特别的装饰。
The simple dress wasn't ornamented with any jewels. 这件简单的连衣裙没有用任何珠宝装饰。

词源:
From Old French *ornement, from Latin ornamentum "ornament, decoration," from ornare "to equip, furnish, adorn."
