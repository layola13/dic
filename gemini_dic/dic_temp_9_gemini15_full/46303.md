da's:/dæz/| n.|那，那些|That, those.  It's a contraction of "that is" or "that's". It is used informally. |例句：Da's my book. = 那是我的书。 Da's the problem. = 那就是问题。|近义词：that, those |反义词:this, these


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That is my car. = 那是我的车。
That is a beautiful dress. = 那是一件漂亮的裙子。

一般过去时:
That was a great movie. = 那是一部很棒的电影。
That was my favorite song. = 那是我最喜欢的歌。

现在完成时:
That has been a long day. = 今天过得很长。
That has been my biggest challenge. = 那是我面临的最大挑战。

过去完成时:
That had already been decided. = 那已经决定了。
That had been a difficult task. = 那是一项艰巨的任务。

将来时:
That will be interesting. = 那将会很有趣。
That will be a surprise. = 那将是一个惊喜。

虚拟语气:
If that were true, I would be shocked. = 如果那是真的，我会很震惊。
I wish that were possible. = 我希望那是可能的。

被动语态:
That was made by hand. = 那是手工制作的。
That was decided in the meeting. = 那是会议上决定的。

疑问句:
Is that your bag? = 那是你的包吗？
Is that what you meant? = 那是你的意思吗？

否定句:
That isn't right. = 那不对。
That wasn't the answer I expected. = 那不是我预期的答案。


PHRASE->
That's all = 就这样
That's it = 就这样
That's right = 正确的
That's why = 那就是为什么
That's enough = 足够了
