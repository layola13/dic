conferencing:/ˈkɒnfərənsɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnfərənsɪŋ/| n. |会议；电话会议；视讯会议 |The act of holding a conference; a meeting for consultation or discussion.  It can also refer to the technology used to facilitate such meetings.|例句：The company uses video conferencing to connect with its offices around the world.  这家公司使用视频会议来连接世界各地的办事处。

例句：We had a conference call to discuss the new project. 我们进行了电话会议来讨论新项目。


近义词：meeting, consultation, teleconference, videoconference


反义词：separation, solitude


词性:名词
