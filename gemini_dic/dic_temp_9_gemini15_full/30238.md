cellularly:/ˈsɛljələrli/| adv.|细胞地，在细胞水平上|In a manner relating to cells or their activities.  This adverb describes processes or events occurring within or affecting cells.| 例句：The drug acted cellularly to inhibit protein synthesis.  药物在细胞水平上抑制蛋白质合成。


The effects of radiation were studied cellularly.  辐射的影响在细胞水平上进行了研究。|近义词：cell-by-cell, at the cellular level|反义词：holistically, systemically
