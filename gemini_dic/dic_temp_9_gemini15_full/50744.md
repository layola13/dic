derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋˌduː/| n. |勇敢的行为；大胆的行动|brave and daring deeds; valiant exploits.  It is typically used in a playful or slightly ironic way, suggesting a somewhat reckless bravery.|例句：His derring-do saved the day. 他的勇敢行为挽救了局面。

例句：Their derring-do in the face of danger was remarkable. 他们在危险面前的勇敢行为令人瞩目。


近义词：bravery, valor, courage, audacity, daring, intrepidity


反义词：cowardice, timidity, fearfulness


词性：名词
