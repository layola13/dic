figurativeness:/ˌfɪɡjʊˈreɪtɪvnəs/ | n. |比喻性，形象性 |The quality of being figurative; the use of figures of speech.  Figurativeness adds depth and richness to language, making it more engaging and memorable.  |Example: The poem's figurativeness made it incredibly evocative.  这首诗的比喻性使其令人难以忘怀。|近义词：imagery, symbolism, metaphoricalness |反义词：literalness, plainness
