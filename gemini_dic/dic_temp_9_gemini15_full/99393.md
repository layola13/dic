interdifferentiate: /ˌɪntərˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃieɪt/ | v. | 使……互相区分；使……彼此不同 | To distinguish or differentiate between or among.  This word is rarely used and is often considered overly formal or obscure. It implies a reciprocal process of differentiation, where multiple entities are distinguished from one another simultaneously. |  The researcher attempted to interdifferentiate the subtle variations in the two species of butterflies. 研究人员试图区分这两种蝴蝶的细微差异。|  differentiate, distinguish, discriminate |  homogenize, unify | v.
