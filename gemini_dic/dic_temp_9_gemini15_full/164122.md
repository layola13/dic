predispersion:/ˌpriːdɪˈspɜːrʒən/| n. |预分散，预先分散|The process of dispersing something before it is needed or used, often to improve its performance or stability.  It's commonly used in various fields like chemistry, materials science, and engineering to prevent unwanted aggregation or sedimentation. |Example: Predispersion of pigments in the paint improves its color uniformity and application properties. 预先分散颜料可以提高油漆的颜色均匀性和涂布性能。|近义词：predistribution, premixing|反义词：post-dispersion, aggregation|名词
