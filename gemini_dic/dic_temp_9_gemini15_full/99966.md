interorbital:/ˌɪntərˈɔːrbɪtəl/| adj.|眶间的，眼眶间的| Situated or occurring between the eye sockets.  The interorbital distance is an important measurement in some anthropological studies.|例句：The interorbital distance was measured using calipers.  眶间距是用卡尺测量的。

例句：The bird has a narrow interorbital space. 这只鸟的眶间距很窄。


近义词：between-the-eyes (informal)


反义词：intraorbital (within the eye socket)


词性:形容词
