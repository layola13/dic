derailleurs:/ˌdɛreɪˈlɜːrz/| n. |变速器|  A derailleur is a mechanism in a bicycle that shifts the chain between different cogs on the rear cassette and/or chainrings on the front crankset, allowing the rider to change gears.|例句：My bike's derailleur needs adjusting; it's skipping gears. 我的自行车变速器需要调整；它齿轮跳动。|例句：The derailleur is a crucial component for efficient cycling. 变速器是高效骑行的关键部件。|近义词：gear changer, shifter|反义词：无|名词
