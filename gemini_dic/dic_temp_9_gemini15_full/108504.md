layman:/ˈleɪmən/| n. |外行；门外汉；俗人|A layman is a person without professional knowledge or skill in a particular area.  Laymen often need explanations simplified for them to understand complex subjects.|例句：Even a layman can understand this explanation. 即使是外行也能理解这个解释。|例句：The doctor explained the procedure to the layman in simple terms. 医生用简单的术语向外行解释了这个过程。|近义词：amateur, novice, nonprofessional|反义词：expert, professional, specialist|名词
