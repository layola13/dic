corymbosely:/ˌkɒrɪmˈboʊsli/| adv.|伞房花序状地，伞房状地 |In botany, arranged in a corymb.  Describes the arrangement of flowers or inflorescences in a corymb, a flat-topped or slightly rounded cluster of flowers where the individual flower stalks arise from different points on the stem but reach approximately the same height.|The flowers were corymbosely arranged on the plant.  = 这植物的花呈伞房状排列。|近义词：racemosely (总状花序状地), umbelately (伞形花序状地)|反义词：solitarily (单独地)
