nationalism:/ˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |民族主义；国家主义|The belief that one's own nation is superior to all others and the feeling of strong national pride and unity.  It often involves a desire to promote the interests of one's own nation above those of other nations.  Nationalism can manifest in various ways, including political movements, cultural expressions, and economic policies.|例句：The rise of nationalism in the 19th century led to many wars.  十九世纪民族主义的兴起导致了许多战争。|例句：Their nationalism prevented them from cooperating with other countries. 他们的民族主义阻止了他们与其他国家合作。|近义词：patriotism, jingoism |反义词：internationalism, globalism
