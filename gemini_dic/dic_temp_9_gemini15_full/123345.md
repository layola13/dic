Figuratively, something large and impressive that is difficult to change. |例句：Stonehenge is a prehistoric monolith.  巨石阵是一个史前巨石建筑。 The company has become a monolith, resistant to change. 这家公司已经成为一个庞然大物，难以改变。|近义词：monument, colossus, giant |反义词：fragment, piece, small thing |名词
