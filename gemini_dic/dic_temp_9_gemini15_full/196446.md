serrated:/sɛˈreɪtɪd/| adj.|锯齿状的，有锯齿的|Having a jagged or notched edge, like a saw.  The term is often used to describe the edge of a leaf, a tooth, or a tool.|例句：The serrated knife cut through the bread easily.  这把锯齿刀很容易切过面包。

例句：The leaves of the holly bush are serrated. 冬青灌木的叶子是锯齿状的。


近义词：jagged, notched, toothed, indented|反义词：smooth, even


词源:  From the past participle of the verb "serrate," which comes from the Latin word "serratus," meaning "saw-toothed."
