satisfiable:/ˈsætɪsfaɪəbl/| adj.|令人满意的，可满足的| capable of being satisfied; meeting or fulfilling a requirement or expectation.  |例句：The terms of the contract are entirely satisfiable to both parties. 合同条款对双方都完全令人满意。

例句：His explanation was not satisfiable; it left many unanswered questions. 他的解释令人无法满意；留下了许多悬而未决的问题。


近义词：gratifying, pleasing, acceptable, adequate, sufficient

反义词：unsatisfiable, dissatisfactory, unacceptable, inadequate, insufficient

词性:形容词
