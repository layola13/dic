grappling:/ˈɡræpəlɪŋ/| v.|  grapple 的现在分词 | 搏斗；努力克服；抓住； grapple with sth.  |英文解析：To grapple with something means to struggle to understand or deal with it.  It can also mean to hold or seize something firmly. |例句：He was grappling with a difficult problem. 他正在努力解决一个难题。  The firefighters grappled with the burning building. 消防队员与燃烧的建筑物搏斗。|近义词：struggling, wrestling, contending, seizing |反义词：yielding, surrendering
