martialists:/ˈmɑːʃəlɪsts/| n. |武术家；军事专家|People who practice martial arts or military experts.  They are skilled in combat and often have extensive training in various fighting styles.|例句：Many martialists train for years to perfect their techniques. 许多武术家训练多年以完善他们的技术。

例句：The renowned martialists demonstrated their skills at the international competition.  著名的武术家们在国际比赛上展示了他们的技能。


近义词：fighters, combatants, martial artists


反义词：civilian (in the context of military experts)
