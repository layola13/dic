methodistic:/ˌmɛθəˈdɪstɪk/| adj.|循规蹈矩的；按部就班的；一丝不苟的|Methodistic refers to something characterized by or conforming to a methodical or systematic approach; often implying a rigid adherence to rules and procedures.  It suggests a strong emphasis on order, precision, and a lack of spontaneity.|His methodistic approach to cleaning ensured every surface sparkled, but it also took hours.|近义词：systematic, methodical, rigorous, orderly, precise|反义词：haphazard, disorganized, chaotic, haphazard, spontaneous|
