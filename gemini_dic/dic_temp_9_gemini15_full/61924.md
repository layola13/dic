emulated:/ˈɛmjuleɪtɪd/| v. |模仿，效仿，仿真|To imitate or copy something, especially something admirable or successful.  It can also refer to simulating the behavior of a system. |例句：She emulated her favorite author's writing style. 她模仿了她最喜欢的作者的写作风格。|例句：The software emulates the function of a real-time operating system.  该软件模拟实时操作系统的功能。|近义词：imitated, copied, mimicked, simulated |反义词：originated, innovated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emulates his father's business acumen. = 他效仿他父亲的商业敏锐。
The program emulates a mainframe computer. = 该程序模拟大型计算机。

一般过去时:
She emulated the great painters of the Renaissance. = 她模仿文艺复兴时期的大画家。
The engineers emulated the natural processes of photosynthesis. = 工程师们模拟了光合作用的自然过程。

现在完成时:
They have emulated the success of their competitors. = 他们已经效仿了竞争对手的成功。
Scientists have emulated the human brain's neural networks. = 科学家们已经模拟了人脑的神经网络。

过去完成时:
He had emulated his hero's bravery throughout his life. = 他一生都在效仿他英雄的勇气。
The team had emulated the design of the previous model before starting anew. = 在重新开始之前，团队已经模拟了先前模型的设计。

将来时:
She will emulate her sister's success in the medical field. = 她将效仿她姐姐在医学领域的成功。
We will emulate the functionality of the existing system in our new software. = 我们将在我们的新软件中模拟现有系统的功能。

虚拟语气:
If I were to emulate his dedication, I might succeed. = 如果我效仿他的奉献精神，我可能会成功。
I wish I could emulate the grace of a ballerina. = 我希望我能模仿芭蕾舞演员的优雅。

被动语态:
His achievements were emulated by many aspiring entrepreneurs. = 他的成就被许多有抱负的企业家效仿。
The system's performance was emulated by the testing software. = 系统的性能被测试软件模拟。

疑问句:
Does he emulate any particular artist? = 他模仿任何特定的艺术家吗？
Will the new software emulate the features of the old one? = 新软件会模拟旧软件的功能吗？

否定句:
He did not emulate the negative aspects of his role model. = 他没有效仿他榜样的消极方面。
The researchers could not emulate the complex behavior of the ecosystem. = 研究人员无法模拟生态系统的复杂行为。
