incessant: /ɪnˈsɛsənt/ | adj. | 不停的；连续不断的 |  Continuously occurring; without interruption.  | The incessant rain made it impossible to go outside.  不停的雨使得无法外出。| continuous, constant, unending, perpetual | intermittent, occasional, sporadic | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine runs incessantly. = 机器不停地运转。
He incessantly complains about his job. = 他不停地抱怨他的工作。


一般过去时:
The birds sang incessantly all night. = 鸟儿整夜不停地歌唱。
The dog barked incessantly at the stranger. = 狗对陌生人不停地吠叫。


现在完成时:
The rain has been incessant for days. = 雨已经下了好几天了。
She has incessantly pursued her dream. = 她一直坚持不懈地追求她的梦想。


过去完成时:
The noise had been incessant all through the night. = 整个晚上噪音都一直不断。
He had incessantly tried to contact her before she finally replied. = 在她最终回复之前，他一直不停地试图联系她。


将来时:
The construction will be incessant throughout the summer. = 整个夏天施工都会不停地进行。
They will incessantly work to meet the deadline. = 他们将夜以继日地工作以赶上最后期限。


虚拟语气:
If the noise were incessant, I would move. = 如果噪音持续不断，我会搬家。
I wish the rain would stop being incessant. = 我希望雨能停止不停地下。


被动语态:
The city was incessantly bombarded by the enemy. = 城市遭到敌人的持续轰炸。
His achievements were incessantly praised by the media. = 他的成就受到了媒体的持续赞扬。


疑问句:
Is the noise incessant? = 噪音不停吗？
Is her complaining incessant? = 她一直在抱怨吗？


否定句:
The music wasn't incessant; it stopped several times. = 音乐并非持续不断；它停了好几次。
The work wasn't incessant; there were breaks throughout the day. = 工作并非持续不断；白天有休息时间。
