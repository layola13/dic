reequipping:/ˌriːˈɪkwɪpɪŋ/| v.|重新装备；补充设备 | The act of equipping again; supplying with new equipment.  |例句：The army is undergoing a major reequipping program.  军队正在进行一项重大的重新装备计划。
例句：The factory is currently undergoing reequipping to improve efficiency. 工厂目前正在进行重新装备以提高效率。|近义词：refitting, rearming, renovating|反义词：disarming, decommissioning|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reequipping the hospital with new medical devices. = 他们正在为医院配备新的医疗设备。
The company reequips its factories every five years. = 这家公司每五年对其工厂进行一次重新装备。


一般过去时:
The military reequipped its forces with advanced weaponry. = 军方用先进武器重新装备了部队。
They reequipped the kitchen with modern appliances. = 他们用现代电器重新装备了厨房。


现在完成时:
The school has reequipped its science labs with state-of-the-art equipment. = 学校已经用最先进的设备重新装备了它的科学实验室。
The company has reequipped its production line to increase output. = 公司已经重新装备了生产线以提高产量。


过去完成时:
The team had reequipped their vehicles before embarking on the expedition. = 在开始探险之前，团队已经重新装备了他们的车辆。
Before the new project started, the company had already reequipped its offices. = 在新项目启动之前，公司已经重新装备了办公室。


将来时:
The government will reequip the national defense forces next year. = 政府明年将重新装备国防军。
The factory will reequip its assembly line to meet the increased demand. = 工厂将重新装备其装配线以满足日益增长的需求。


虚拟语气:
If they had reequipped the factory earlier, production would have been higher. = 如果他们早点重新装备工厂，产量就会更高。
I wish they had reequipped the lab before the experiments. = 我希望他们在实验之前已经重新装备了实验室。


被动语态:
