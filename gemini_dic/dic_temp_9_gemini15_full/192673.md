scuttles:/ˈskʌtəlz/| n. v.|仓促行动；毁坏；迅速移动；|英文解析：To move quickly, hurriedly, or furtively.  As a noun, it refers to a small, swift running movement; or a pail or bucket.|例句：The ship scuttled after hitting the iceberg.  船撞上冰山后沉没了。|近义词：hurry, rush, dash, scuttlebutt (n.)|反义词：dawdle, delay, procrastinate


USAGE->
