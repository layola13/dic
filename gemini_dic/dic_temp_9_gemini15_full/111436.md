large:/lɑːrdʒ/| adj. |大的，巨大的 | large describes something of considerable size or extent. It can refer to physical dimensions, quantity, or scale.  |例句：The elephant is a large animal. = 大象是一种大型动物。

The house has a large garden. = 这房子有个大花园。|近义词：big, huge, vast, extensive |反义词：small, tiny, little, minute


USAGE->

The project is quite large. = 这个项目相当庞大。

词源:

The word "large" comes from Old French "large," meaning "wide, broad."  This ultimately derives from a Proto-Germanic root.  The evolution reflects the broadening of meaning from simply "wide" to encompassing general size.
