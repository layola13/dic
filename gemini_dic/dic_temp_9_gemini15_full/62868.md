enlace: /enˈlɑːseɪ/ | n. |连结，联系；环节；纽带 | A connection or link between two or more things.  It can also refer to a specific link in a chain or series of events. |例句：The enlace between the two companies is a strong one.  两家公司之间的联系非常紧密。

例句：The final enlace in the chain of events was the signing of the contract.  事件链条中的最后环节是合同的签署。


近义词：connection, link, bond, tie, relationship

反义词：separation, disconnection, disunity


词性：名词
