expressman: /ɪkˈsprɛsmæn/ | n. | 快递员，速递员 | A person whose job is to deliver packages or other items quickly.  | The expressman delivered the package before noon. 快递员在中午之前送来了包裹。 | courier, messenger |  (近义词无明确反义词，可根据语境选择例如：mailman, postman)
