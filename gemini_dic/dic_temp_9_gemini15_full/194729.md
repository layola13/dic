selfies:/ˈsɛlfiːz/| n.|自拍照片，自拍照|A selfie is a photograph that one has taken of oneself, typically one taken with a smartphone or webcam and shared via social media.|例句：She took a selfie with her favorite celebrity. 她和自己最喜欢的名人一起自拍了一张照片。|例句：He posted a selfie on Instagram showing his new haircut. 他在Instagram上发布了一张自拍，展示了他新的发型。|近义词：self-portrait, self-photo|反义词：portrait (taken by someone else)|名词
