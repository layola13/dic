hyalotype:/ˈhaɪəloʊtaɪp/| n. |透明质酸型，透明质酸类型|A hyalotype refers to a specific type or subtype of hyaluronan (hyaluronic acid) based on its molecular weight, structure, and other properties.  Different hyalotypes may have different effects on the body and are used in various applications, such as cosmetics and medicine.  The term is relatively new and not yet widely standardized. |The study focused on identifying different hyalotypes and their impact on skin aging.  这项研究重点在于识别不同的透明质酸类型及其对皮肤老化的影响。|  (近义词缺乏普遍认可的专业术语，因为 hyalotype 本身就是一个新兴术语) | (反义词缺乏普遍认可的专业术语) |名词
