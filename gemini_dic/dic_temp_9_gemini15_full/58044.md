drollest:/ˈdrɒləst/ | adj. |最滑稽的，最古怪的 | Most humorous or amusing in an odd or peculiar way.  It is the superlative form of "droll". |例句：His drollest jokes always leave the audience in stitches. 他最滑稽的笑话总是让观众捧腹大笑。

例句：The drollest character in the novel was the eccentric old librarian. 小说中最古怪的人物是那位古怪的老图书馆员。


近义词：amusing, funny, comical, whimsical, quirky, odd, peculiar

反义词：serious, solemn, dull, boring, uninteresting

词性:形容词
