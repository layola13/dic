fertilized:/ˈfɜːrtlʌɪzd/ /ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 受精的；施肥的 |  Having been fertilized; impregnated.  The adjective describes something that has undergone fertilization, either in a biological or agricultural context. |例句：The fertilized egg began to divide. 受精卵开始分裂。

The fertilized field yielded a bountiful harvest.  施过肥的田地获得了丰收。|近义词：impregnated, inseminated, fecundated |反义词：unfertilized, barren
