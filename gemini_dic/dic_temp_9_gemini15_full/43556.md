crazes:/kreɪz/| n.|狂热；时尚；痴迷|A craze is a very strong enthusiasm for something, especially one that is shared by many people and lasts only for a short time.  It often refers to a fad or a temporary obsession.|例句：The Pokémon craze swept the world in the late 1990s.  上世纪90年代末，口袋妖怪热席卷全球。

例句：There's currently a craze for vintage clothing.  现在复古服装非常流行。|近义词：fad, fashion, mania, obsession|反义词：indifference, apathy|名词
