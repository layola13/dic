preharden:/ˌpriːˈhɑːrdən/| vt.| 预先硬化；预先强化 | To harden something beforehand, often to increase its strength or durability.  |例句：The steel was prehardened before being shaped into tools.  钢材在成型为工具之前进行了预硬化处理。
The concrete was prehardened to withstand the expected load.混凝土预先硬化以承受预期的载荷。|近义词：harden, toughen, strengthen |反义词：soften, weaken
