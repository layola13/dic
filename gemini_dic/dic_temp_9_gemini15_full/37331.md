commence:/kəˈmɛns/ | vt. | 开始，着手 | to begin something | The meeting will commence at 2 p.m.  会议将于下午两点开始。| The ceremony commenced with a prayer. 仪式以祈祷开始。 | begin, start, initiate | cease, end, finish | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program commences at 7 pm. = 节目晚上7点开始。
They commence work early every day. = 他们每天很早就开始工作。

一般过去时:
The game commenced with a coin toss. = 游戏以抛硬币开始。
She commenced her studies last fall. = 她去年秋天开始学习。

现在完成时:
The construction has commenced. = 建筑工程已经开始。
They have commenced negotiations. = 他们已经开始谈判。

过去完成时:
The play had commenced before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，演出已经开始了。
He had commenced his journey before dawn. = 他在黎明前就开始了他的旅程。

将来时:
The concert will commence at 8 o'clock. = 音乐会将在8点开始。
Work on the project will commence next week. = 项目的工作将于下周开始。

虚拟语气:
If he commenced the project earlier, it would be finished by now. = 如果他早点开始这个项目，现在就完成了。
I wish he had commenced the project earlier. = 我希望他早点开始这个项目。

被动语态:
The meeting was commenced by the chairman. = 会议由主席开始。
The project will be commenced next month. = 项目将于下个月开始。

疑问句:
When does the meeting commence? = 会议几点开始？
Has the investigation commenced yet? = 调查已经开始了吗？

否定句:
The game did not commence on time. = 游戏没有按时开始。
They haven't commenced the repairs yet. = 他们还没有开始修理。
