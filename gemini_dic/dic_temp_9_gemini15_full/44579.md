croupade:/kruːˈpɑːd/|/ˈkruːpɑːd/| n. |屈腿腾跃；(马术)屈腿后跃|A croupade is a movement in dressage where the horse bends its hind legs and rises slightly, bringing its forelegs forward. It's a collected and elevated movement. |The horse performed a perfect croupade, showcasing its training and grace.  这匹马表演了一个完美的屈腿腾跃，展现了它的训练和优雅。|capriole, levade|  (无明确反义词，与平稳步态相对)|名词
