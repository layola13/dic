interminable:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/| adj.| 无止境的；漫长的 | seeming to have no end; unending.  |例句：The meeting felt interminable.  会议感觉没完没了。

例句：The journey was interminable. 这段旅程漫长无边。


近义词：endless, unending, ceaseless, perpetual, everlasting | 反义词：brief, short, fleeting


词源：来自拉丁语 *inter- (between) + *minus (less) + *abilis (able). 字面意思是“不可减少的”，引申为“无止境的”。
