buying:/ˈbaɪɪŋ/| v.|购买；收购|The act of purchasing something.  It involves exchanging money or something of value for goods or services.|例句：I am buying a new car next week. = 我下周要买一辆新车。

例句：She is buying groceries at the supermarket. = 她在超市买杂货。


近义词：purchasing, acquiring, obtaining|反义词：selling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I buy groceries every week. = 我每周都买杂货。
They buy their clothes online. = 他们网上买衣服。

一般过去时:
I bought a new phone yesterday. = 我昨天买了一部新手机。
She bought a beautiful dress for the party. = 她买了一件漂亮的裙子参加派对。

现在完成时:
I have bought all the ingredients for the cake. = 我已经买齐了做蛋糕的所有材料。
They have bought a new house in the suburbs. = 他们已经在郊区买了一栋新房子。

过去完成时:
I had bought the tickets before the prices went up. = 价格上涨之前我就买好了票。
She had bought a new car before her old one broke down. = 她的旧车坏掉之前她就买了一辆新车。

将来时:
I will buy a new computer next month. = 我下个月会买一台新电脑。
They will buy a larger house when they have more money. = 他们有钱了就会买一栋更大的房子。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would buy a bigger house. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish I could buy that beautiful painting. = 我真希望我能买下那幅美丽的画。

被动语态:
The car was bought last year. = 这辆车是去年买的。
The tickets are being bought online. = 票正在网上购买。

疑问句:
Are you buying anything today? = 你今天买什么东西吗？
What are you buying at the store? = 你在商店买什么？

否定句:
I didn't buy anything at the mall. = 我在商场什么也没买。
They haven't bought a new car yet. = 他们还没买新车。


PHRASE->
buying power: 购买力
power buying: 集团购买；批量购买
value buying: 价值购买
impulse buying: 冲动购买
online buying: 网上购物
