re-routed:/ˌriːˈraʊtɪd/| v. |重新规划路线，改道| To change the route or course of something, typically a journey, a plan, or a flow of something.|例句：The flight was re-routed due to bad weather.  航班因恶劣天气而改道。|例句：The traffic was re-routed around the accident. 交通绕过事故现场改道行驶。|近义词：diverted, redirected |反义词：maintained, continued


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-route the traffic every rush hour. = 他们每到高峰期就重新规划交通路线。
The company re-routes its shipments to avoid delays. = 公司重新规划其货运路线以避免延误。

一般过去时:
The police re-routed the traffic after the accident. = 事故发生后，警方重新规划了交通路线。
They re-routed the pipeline to avoid the environmentally sensitive area. = 他们将管道改道，避开了环境敏感区域。

现在完成时:
The authorities have re-routed the bus service due to roadworks. = 由于道路施工，当局已重新规划了公交线路。
We have re-routed the network traffic to improve efficiency. = 我们已经重新规划了网络流量以提高效率。

过去完成时:
They had re-routed the hikers before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经为徒步旅行者重新规划了路线。
The airline had re-routed the flight before the passengers boarded. = 在乘客登机前，航空公司已经改签了航班。

将来时:
They will re-route the trains during the construction. = 施工期间，他们将重新规划火车路线。
The system will automatically re-route the calls if the line is busy. = 如果线路繁忙，系统将自动重新规划呼叫路线。


被动语态:
The delivery was re-routed to a different address. = 快递被重新规划路线送往不同的地址。
The traffic flow was re-routed by the police. = 交通流量被警方重新规划了路线。

疑问句:

否定句:
The traffic was not re-routed. = 交通没有重新规划路线。
The company did not re-route the shipment. = 公司没有重新规划货运路线。
