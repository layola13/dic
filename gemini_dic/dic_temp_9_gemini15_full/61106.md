elocutionists:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɪsts/ | n. | 演说家；朗诵家 |  A person skilled in the art of public speaking, especially one who teaches or practices elocution.  Elocutionists focus on clear articulation, vocal projection, and effective delivery of speech. | The elocutionists gave captivating performances.  那些朗诵家们进行了引人入胜的表演。| orator, speaker, lecturer |  mumbler, stammerer | 名词
