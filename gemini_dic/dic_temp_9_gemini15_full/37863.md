compilations:/ˌkɒmpɪˈleɪʃənz/| n. |汇编，编纂；合集 |A compilation is a collection of various things, often texts, songs, or other works, gathered together into a single volume or set.  It implies a process of selecting and arranging the components. |Example: This anthology is a fascinating compilation of poems from the Victorian era. 这本选集是维多利亚时代诗歌的精彩汇编。|近义词：anthology, collection, anthology |反义词：original work, single work
