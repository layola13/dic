intelligentsia: /ˌɪntɛləˈdʒɛnʃə/ | n. |知识分子阶层； интеллигенция | The intelligentsia are the highly educated, often politically active, and usually liberal members of society.  They are typically involved in intellectual pursuits such as writing, teaching, or research.  |例句：The intelligentsia fiercely debated the government's new policies.  知识分子阶层激烈地辩论着政府的新政策。


例句：The country's intelligentsia played a crucial role in the social and political reforms.  该国的知识分子阶层在社会和政治改革中发挥了至关重要的作用。


近义词：intellectuals, academics, literati, elite | 反义词：ignorant, uneducated, common people
