frontage:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒ/| n.|临街面，正面，门面|The length of a building's front that faces a street or other public space.  It can also refer to the land or property facing a street.|例句：The shop has a large frontage, making it highly visible to potential customers. 这家商店有一个很大的门面，使其很容易被潜在顾客看到。

例句：Our house boasts a stunning river frontage. 我们的房子拥有令人惊叹的河边临街面。

近义词：facade, front, face

反义词：rear, back


PHRASE->
street frontage: 街面临街面
water frontage: 水面临水门面
river frontage: 河滨临街面
frontage road:  临街道路
building frontage: 建筑物正面
