dioeciously:/ˌdaɪˈiːʃəsli/| adj. |雌雄异株的；雌雄异体的|Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.  This term is primarily used in botany and zoology to describe plants and animals that have separate sexes.  The opposite of monoecious. |例句：Many flowering plants are dioeciously, meaning that individual plants are either male or female.  许多开花植物是雌雄异株的，这意味着单个植物要么是雄性，要么是雌性。

Many species of holly are dioeciously, requiring both a male and female plant for fruit production.  许多冬青树物种是雌雄异株的，需要雄性和雌性植物才能结果。


近义词：unisexual, gonochoristic |反义词：monoecious, hermaphroditic
