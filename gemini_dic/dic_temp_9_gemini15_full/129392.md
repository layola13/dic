nomenclature:/ˈnoʊmənˌkleɪtʃər/| n. |命名法；术语；专门词汇 |The system of names used in a particular field; a set of terms used in a particular field. |Example: The nomenclature of organic chemistry can be quite complex. 有机化学的命名法相当复杂。|近义词：terminology, vocabulary, system of naming |反义词：无

词源:
From Late Latin *nomenclatura, from nomen "name" + -clatura "act of calling".  The Latin "nomen" is related to the Greek word "onoma" (ὄνομα), meaning name.  "-clatura" is derived from a verb meaning "to call".  Therefore, the word literally means "the act of naming".
