muzzle:/ˈmʌzəl/| n., v.|口套；枪口；压制；阻止|A muzzle is a covering put over the mouth of an animal, especially a dog, to prevent it from biting.  It can also refer to the end of a gun barrel or the act of suppressing speech or expression.|例句：The dog wore a muzzle to prevent it from biting. 这条狗戴着口套以防止它咬人。

The police officer aimed his muzzle at the suspect. 警官用枪口瞄准了嫌疑犯。


近义词：gag, snout, suppress, restrain|反义词：free, release, encourage

词源:

The word "muzzle" comes from the Old French word "museau," meaning "snout" or "mouth."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "musus," also meaning "snout."  The verb sense developed later, reflecting the action of applying a muzzle.
