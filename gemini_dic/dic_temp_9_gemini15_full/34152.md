clam:/klæm/| n. |蛤蜊，蚌；沉默寡言的人 |A clam is a type of shellfish with a shell in two parts.  It is often eaten as food.  The word can also describe someone who is quiet and reserved. |例句：We had steamed clams for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了清蒸蛤蜊。 He was as clammy as ever during the interview. 他在面试期间一如既往地沉默寡言。|近义词：shellfish, bivalve, oyster |反义词：talkative, garrulous
