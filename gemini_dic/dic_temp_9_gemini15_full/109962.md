liberates:/ˈlɪbəreɪts/ | vt. | 解放；释放；使自由 | To set someone or something free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression.  |例句：The army liberated the city from the enemy.  军队解放了这座城市。

The revolution liberated the people from tyranny.  革命使人民摆脱了暴政。


近义词：emancipate, free, release | 反义词：enslave, imprison, confine | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The key liberates the lock. = 钥匙打开了锁。
He liberates his emotions through painting. = 他通过绘画释放自己的情感。


一般过去时:
The soldiers liberated the prisoners of war. = 士兵们解放了战俘。
The discovery liberated new possibilities for research. = 这一发现为研究带来了新的可能性。


现在完成时:
The movement has liberated many women from traditional roles. = 这场运动已经解放了许多妇女摆脱了传统角色。
The new technology has liberated us from repetitive tasks. = 新技术使我们摆脱了重复性任务。


过去完成时:
They had liberated the town before the enemy reinforcements arrived. = 敌人增援到达之前，他们已经解放了该镇。
The scientist had liberated the virus from its host cell. = 科学家已将病毒从宿主细胞中释放出来。


将来时:
The new law will liberate many people from financial hardship. = 新法律将使许多人摆脱经济困境。
The invention will liberate us from the constraints of time and space. = 这项发明将使我们摆脱时间和空间的限制。


虚拟语气:
If only the reforms liberated the people from poverty. = 如果改革能使人民摆脱贫困就好了。
I wish the new system liberated us from bureaucracy. = 我希望新制度能使我们摆脱官僚主义。


被动语态:
The city was liberated by the allied forces. = 这座城市被盟军解放了。
The prisoners were liberated after many years of captivity. = 囚犯们在被囚禁多年后获释。


疑问句:
Will this new policy liberate the market? =这项新政策会解放市场吗？
What factors will liberate the country from this crisis?  什么因素才能使国家摆脱这场危机？


否定句:
The regime did not liberate the people from oppression. = 政权并没有使人民摆脱压迫。
The treaty failed to liberate the occupied territories. = 条约未能解放被占领的领土。


PHRASE->
liberate someone from something = 使某人摆脱某事
liberate oneself from = 从…中解脱出来
mentally liberate = 心理解放
liberate the mind = 解放思想
liberate energy = 释放能量
