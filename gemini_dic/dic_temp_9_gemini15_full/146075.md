overed:/ˈoʊvərd/ | adj. |覆盖的，遮盖的 | Covered means something is placed over or on top of something else, providing a layer or protection. It can also describe something hidden or concealed. |例句：The table was covered with a white tablecloth.  桌子盖着一条白色的桌布。|例句：The mountain was covered in snow.  山上覆盖着积雪。|近义词：concealed, hidden, shrouded, masked |反义词：uncovered, exposed, bare


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blanket covers the bed. = 毛毯盖在床上。
The snow covers the ground. = 雪覆盖着地面。

一般过去时:
She covered her face with her hands. = 她用手捂着脸。
They covered the distance quickly. = 他们很快地走完了这段距离。

现在完成时:
The artist has covered the canvas with paint. = 艺术家已经用颜料覆盖了画布。
We have covered a lot of ground in this discussion. = 我们在这个讨论中已经讨论了很多内容。

过去完成时:
He had covered the wound before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，他已经包扎好了伤口。
She had covered the topic in her previous presentation. = 她之前在她的演示文稿中已经讨论过这个话题。

将来时:
They will cover the expenses. = 他们将支付费用。
The insurance will cover the damage. = 保险将赔偿损失。

虚拟语气:
If I had covered my car, it wouldn't be damaged. = 如果我当时把车遮盖起来，它就不会损坏。
If only I had covered the food, the flies wouldn't have gotten to it. = 要是我当时把食物盖起来就好了，苍蝇就不会吃到它了。

被动语态:
The table is covered with a cloth. = 桌子被一块布盖着。
The field was covered with snow. = 田野被雪覆盖着。

疑问句:
Is the wound covered? = 伤口包扎好了吗？
Is the floor covered in carpet? = 地板铺着地毯吗？

否定句:
The cake isn't covered in icing. = 蛋糕没有覆盖糖霜。
The report doesn't cover that topic. = 报告没有涵盖那个话题。
