ensuingly:/ɛnˈsuːɪŋli/| adv.|随后地，接着地|Following as a consequence or result.  It indicates that something happens as a direct result of a previous event or action.|例句：The rain started, and ensuingly, the picnic was cancelled.  雨开始下了，因此野餐被取消了。


例句：He failed the exam and ensuingly lost his scholarship. 他考试不及格，因此失去了奖学金。


近义词：consequently, therefore, subsequently, accordingly, thereafter|反义词：previously, beforehand, earlier|词性：副词
