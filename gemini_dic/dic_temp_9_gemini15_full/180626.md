regmacarp:/ˈrɛɡməˌkɑːrp/| n. |裂果|A type of dry fruit that breaks open at maturity to release its seeds.  It is characterized by its splitting along the sutures between carpels.  Examples include capsules and follicles. |The regmacarp of the geranium plant splits into several one-seeded compartments.  = 牻牛儿苗属植物的裂果分裂成几个单种子隔室。|近义词：schizocarp, capsule, follicle |反义词：indehiscent fruit
