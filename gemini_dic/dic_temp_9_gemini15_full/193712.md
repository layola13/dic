self-conflict: /ˌsɛlfˈkɒnflɪkt/ | n. | 内心冲突，自我矛盾 | Internal struggle or tension within a person, often involving opposing desires, beliefs, or values.  It can manifest as indecision, anxiety, or even paralysis. | Example:  The artist experienced a deep self-conflict between her desire for recognition and her fear of failure.  艺术家在她渴望得到认可和害怕失败之间经历了深深的内心冲突。


近义词: internal conflict, inner turmoil, mental struggle, ambivalence

反义词: self-acceptance, inner peace, harmony
