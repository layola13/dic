slating:/ˈsleɪtɪŋ/| n. v.|严厉批评；批评；盖板石板；|The act of criticizing severely;  a harsh criticism; the act of covering with slates.|例句：The film received a slating from the critics.  这部电影受到了影评人的严厉批评。

The band's new album got a real slating in the press. 乐队的最新专辑在媒体上受到了猛烈的抨击。|近义词：criticism, condemnation, rebuke, panning|反义词：praise, commendation, acclaim|名词，动词

一般现在时:
The critics are slating his new novel.  评论家们正在严厉批评他的新小说。
She slates his performance every chance she gets.  她抓住一切机会批评他的表现。


一般过去时:
They slated the government's economic policies.  他们批评了政府的经济政策。
The newspaper slated the mayor for his handling of the crisis.  报纸批评市长处理危机的做法。


现在完成时:
The director has slated the film for release next month.  导演已安排这部电影下个月上映。(此处slated为安排的意思，与批评含义不同)
They have slated the project due to insufficient funding. 他们由于资金不足而取消了该项目。(此处slated为取消的意思，与批评含义不同)


过去完成时:
The play had been slated for closure before the unexpected success.  这出戏在意外成功之前已经被安排关闭了。(此处slated为安排的意思，与批评含义不同)
They had already slated the candidate before the scandal broke.  丑闻爆发前，他们就已经批评这位候选人了。


将来时:
The school will slate the new building project for the next financial year. 学校将把新的建筑项目安排在下一个财政年度。(此处slated为安排的意思，与批评含义不同)
They will slate his actions in the upcoming report.  他们将在即将发布的报告中批评他的行为。


虚拟语气:
If they slated the proposal, we would have to reconsider our strategy. 如果他们否决了这个提案，我们就必须重新考虑我们的策略。(此处slated为否决的意思，与批评含义不同)
I wish they hadn't slated my idea so harshly.  我希望他们不要这么严厉地批评我的想法。


被动语态:
His work was slated by many critics. 他的作品受到了许多评论家的批评。
The proposal was slated for rejection.  该提案被否决了。(此处slated为否决的意思，与批评含义不同)


疑问句:
Has the new movie been slated for release yet? 新电影确定上映日期了吗？(此处slated为安排的意思，与批评含义不同)
Will his performance be slated by the critics? 他的表演会受到评论家的批评吗？


否定句:
The project wasn't slated for cancellation.  该项目没有被取消。(此处slated为安排的意思，与批评含义不同)
The teacher didn't slate the student's work.  老师没有批评学生的作业。


PHRASE->
slate for (安排): The meeting is slated for next Tuesday. 会议定于下周二举行。
slate to (安排): The book is slated to be published next year. 这本书计划明年出版。
slate off (取消): The event was slated off due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，活动被取消了。
slate over (覆盖): The roof was slated over with new tiles. 屋顶用新的瓦片覆盖了。
get slated (遭到批评): He got slated for his poor performance. 他因为表现糟糕而遭到批评。


词源:

The word "slating" originates from the Old English word "slæt," meaning a flat piece of stone used for roofing. The sense of "criticism" developed later, likely based on the image of something being sharply and forcefully struck (like a slate on a roof), representing a harsh criticism.
