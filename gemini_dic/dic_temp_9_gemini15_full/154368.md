permafrosts:/ˈpɜːməfrɔːsts/| n. |永久冻土|Permanently frozen subsoil, typically found in high latitudes or high altitudes.  It's characterized by temperatures that remain below 0°C (32°F) for two or more consecutive years.  The frozen ground can contain ice, rocks, and organic matter.|The construction of the pipeline was hampered by the presence of permafrost.|近义词：frozen ground, permanently frozen soil|反义词：unfrozen ground, thawed soil
