casino:/kəˈsiːnoʊ/| n. |赌场，娱乐场 |A casino is a building where people can gamble, such as playing games of chance or skill for money.  It often includes restaurants, bars, and other entertainment options. |例句：He lost a lot of money at the casino. 他在赌场输了很多钱。  The new casino is attracting tourists from all over the world. 新赌场吸引了来自世界各地的游客。|近义词：gambling hall, gaming house |反义词：church, library
