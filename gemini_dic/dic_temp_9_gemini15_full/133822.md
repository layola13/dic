nonglossy: /ˌnɒnˈɡlɒsi/ | adj. | 不光滑的；无光泽的 | Not shiny; dull in appearance.  The surface lacks a sheen or gloss. |  例句：The nonglossy paint provided a matte finish. = 不光泽的油漆提供了哑光效果。

例句：The nonglossy paper felt rough to the touch. = 不光滑的纸摸起来很粗糙。


近义词：matte, dull, flat, lusterless | 反义词：glossy, shiny, lustrous, polished
