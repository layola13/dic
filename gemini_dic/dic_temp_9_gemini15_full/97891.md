inner-city:/ˈɪnərˈsɪti/| adj. n.| 内城的；市中心区 | Relating to or characteristic of the central area of a city, especially one that is densely populated and often characterized by poverty and social problems.  |例句：The inner-city schools are struggling with underfunding. 内城的学校正在努力应对资金不足的问题。|例句：He grew up in the inner city and witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by its residents. 他在内城区长大，亲眼目睹了居民面临的挑战。|近义词：downtown, urban, metropolitan|反义词：suburban, rural
