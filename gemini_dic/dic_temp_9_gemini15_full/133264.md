nonexhibitionism: /ˌnɒnˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 非炫耀主义，不爱出风头 | The avoidance of ostentatious display or behavior; the opposite of exhibitionism.  It is characterized by modesty, reserve, and a reluctance to draw attention to oneself. |  Her quiet demeanor and unassuming nature were a testament to her nonexhibitionism.  她安静的举止和谦逊的天性证明了她不爱出风头。|近义词：modesty, reserve, reticence, self-effacement | 反义词：exhibitionism, ostentation, showmanship | 名词
