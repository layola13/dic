posthetomy:/pɒsˈθɛktəmi/ | n. | 切除术；后切除术 |Surgical removal of the posterior portion of an organ or structure.  This term is often used in specific contexts, such as in ophthalmology or neurosurgery, referring to the removal of a posterior part of the eye or brain structure.  | Example:  The surgeon performed a posthetomy to remove the damaged posterior segment of the eye.  外科医生进行了后切除术以切除受损的眼球后段。| 近义词： resection, excision | 反义词：implantation, augmentation |名词
