manor:/ˈmænər/| n.|庄园；大宅邸；风度；举止|A manor is a large country house with its surrounding lands, often including a village.  It typically belonged to a lord or landowner in the feudal system. The term also refers to a person's manner or behavior.|例句：They lived in a grand manor house on a sprawling estate. 他们住在一个占地广阔的庄园里的一座宏伟的宅邸中。|例句：His quiet manor belied his inner turmoil. 他平静的举止掩盖了他内心的不安。|近义词：estate, mansion, hall, residence|反义词：humble dwelling, shack
