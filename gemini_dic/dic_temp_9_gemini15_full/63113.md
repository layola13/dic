ensigns:/ˈɛnsaɪnz/| n.|旗帜；军衔；标志|A flag or banner, especially a national flag; a commissioned officer of the lowest rank in the navy.|The ship flew its ensign proudly. 这艘船骄傲地飘扬着它的旗帜。  The ensign saluted the admiral.  少尉向海军上将敬礼。|banners, flags, standards| (no direct antonyms, depends on context)|名词
