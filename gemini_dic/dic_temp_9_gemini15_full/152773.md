peccary:/ˈpɛkəri/| n. |西貒|A peccary is a small, pig-like mammal native to the Americas.  They are known for their tusks and their social behavior. Peccaries are generally omnivorous, feeding on a variety of plants and small animals. |The collared peccary is the most common species. |近义词：javelina (a type of peccary)|反义词：无
