exemplifier:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪər/| n. |例证；典范；模范|A person or thing that exemplifies something.  An exemplifier serves as a clear and typical example of a particular quality, concept, or category. |例句：His work is an exemplifier of the highest standards of craftsmanship. 他的作品是最高工艺标准的典范。|近义词：example, illustration, instance, model, prototype|反义词：counter-example, opposite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting exemplifies the artist's unique style. = 这幅画体现了艺术家独特的风格。
This book exemplifies the best of modern literature. = 这本书是现代文学的典范。


一般过去时:
His actions exemplified his commitment to justice. = 他的行为体现了他对正义的承诺。
That incident exemplified the dangers of reckless driving. = 那起事故充分说明了鲁莽驾驶的危险性。


现在完成时:
Her career has exemplified dedication and hard work. = 她的职业生涯体现了奉献和勤奋。
This project has exemplified the power of collaborative effort. = 这个项目体现了合作努力的力量。


过去完成时:
His previous works had exemplified his talent for storytelling. = 他以前的作品已经体现了他讲故事的天赋。
The historical event had exemplified the fragility of peace. = 那次历史事件体现了和平的脆弱性。


将来时:
Future projects will exemplify the company's commitment to sustainability. = 未来的项目将体现公司对可持续发展的承诺。
His upcoming novel will exemplify his mastery of the genre. = 他即将出版的小说将体现他对该类型的掌握。


虚拟语气:
If his actions exemplified true leadership, he would be revered. = 如果他的行为体现了真正的领导力，他将受到尊敬。
If this case exemplified the flaws in the system, changes must be made. = 如果此案体现了系统中的缺陷，则必须进行更改。


被动语态:
The principles of democracy are exemplified in the country's constitution. = 民主原则在该国的宪法中得到了体现。
His achievements are exemplified by numerous awards. = 他的成就得到了无数奖项的体现。


疑问句:
Does this case exemplify the problem adequately? = 这个案例充分体现了这个问题吗？
Which examples best exemplify the theory? = 哪些例子最能说明这个理论？


否定句:
The situation did not exemplify the company's usual efficiency. = 这种情况并没有体现该公司通常的效率。
His behavior did not exemplify the qualities of a good leader. = 他的行为并没有体现一个好领导的素质。


PHRASE->

exemplify a point = 证明一个论点
exemplify a principle = 阐述一个原则
exemplify a trend = 体现一种趋势
