copybook:/ˈkɒpiˌbʊk/ /ˈkɑːpiˌbʊk/| n.|习字簿，范本，楷模|A copybook is a type of book containing examples of handwriting or drawing, often used for practice or instruction.  It can also refer to something that serves as a model or example to be imitated.|例句：The teacher showed the class a copybook of elegant calligraphy.老师给全班同学展示了一本优雅的书法习字簿。

例句：His business plan served as a copybook for other entrepreneurs.他的商业计划书为其他企业家树立了榜样。|近义词：exemplar, model, example|反义词：improper, incorrect, flawed
