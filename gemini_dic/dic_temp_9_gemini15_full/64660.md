escalate:/ˈɛskəleɪt/| vt. vi. n.|升级；加剧；逐步上升|To increase rapidly in intensity, amount, or extent.|The conflict escalated into a full-blown war. 冲突升级为全面战争。|intensify, worsen, aggravate, heighten|de-escalate, reduce, lessen, diminish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation escalates quickly. = 局势迅速升级。
Prices escalate every year. = 物价每年都在上涨。

一般过去时:
Tensions escalated after the attack. = 袭击后紧张局势升级。
The argument escalated into a fight. = 争吵升级为打斗。

现在完成时:
The problem has escalated beyond our control. = 问题已经升级到我们无法控制的地步。
The conflict has escalated significantly in recent weeks. = 近几周冲突显著升级。

过去完成时:
The situation had escalated before the intervention. = 干预之前，局势已经升级。
The violence had escalated to an alarming level. = 暴力活动已经升级到令人担忧的程度。

将来时:
The conflict will escalate if a solution is not found. = 如果找不到解决办法，冲突将会升级。
The cost of living will escalate further next year. = 明年生活成本将进一步上涨。

虚拟语气:
If we don't address the issue, it will escalate. = 如果我们不处理这个问题，它将会升级。
It would be better to address the problem before it escalates further. = 最好是在问题进一步升级之前解决它。

被动语态:
The conflict was escalated by the aggressive actions. = 冲突因侵略行为而升级。
The situation was escalated unnecessarily. = 局势被不必要地升级了。

疑问句:
Will the situation escalate further? = 局势还会进一步升级吗？
How quickly did the violence escalate? = 暴力活动升级得有多快？

否定句:
The situation did not escalate as expected. = 局势没有像预期的那样升级。
We managed to prevent the conflict from escalating. = 我们成功地防止了冲突升级。


PHRASE->
escalate a conflict = 加剧冲突
escalate a dispute = 升级争端
escalate a problem = 升级问题
escalate the war = 升级战争
escalate tensions = 升级紧张局势
