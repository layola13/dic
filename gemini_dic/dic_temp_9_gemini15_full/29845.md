causation:/kɔːˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |因果关系，起因 |The process by which one event, process, or state of affairs (a cause) contributes to the production of another (an effect).  It involves a relationship between cause and effect.  Causation is different from correlation, which simply indicates a statistical relationship between two events. |Example: The causation of the accident is still under investigation. 事故的起因仍在调查中。  The rise in temperature is a direct causation of global warming. 温度上升是全球变暖的直接原因。|近义词：cause, origin, etiology |反义词：effect, result
