propinquity:/prɒˈpɪŋkwɪti/| n. |邻近；亲近；密切关系 |Nearness in space, time, or relationship.  The state of being close together.  It can refer to physical closeness, familial closeness, or even the close timing of events.|例句：The propinquity of their houses facilitated frequent visits. 他们住得近，方便经常来往。

例句：The propinquity of the two events suggests a causal link.  这两件事发生的接近性暗示了因果关系。


近义词：closeness, proximity, nearness, affinity


反义词：distance, remoteness, separation


词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *propinquite, from Old French *propinquite, from Latin propinquitas, from propinquus (“near, neighboring”), from prope (“near”).
