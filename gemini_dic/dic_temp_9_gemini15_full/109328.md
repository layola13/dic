lengthily:/ˈlɛŋθɪli/| adv.|冗长地，详细地|At length means in detail or at great length.  It implies a thorough and extensive explanation or description.  The adverb "lengthily" emphasizes the extended duration or detail of something.|例句：He lengthily explained the complex procedure. 他冗长地解释了这个复杂的程序。

例句：She lengthily described her travel experiences. 她详细地描述了她的旅行经历。
