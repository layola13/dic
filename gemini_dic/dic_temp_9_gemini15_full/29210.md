casinos:/kəˈsiːnoʊz/| n. |赌场；娱乐场 |Establishments that offer gambling activities such as slots, table games, and sometimes other forms of entertainment.  Casinos are often large and lavish, attracting tourists and high-rollers alike.|例句：Many casinos are located in Las Vegas. 许多赌场位于拉斯维加斯。|例句：The casino offers a wide variety of games. 这个赌场提供各种各样的游戏。|近义词：gambling houses, gaming halls |反义词：church, library

词源:
The word "casino" ultimately derives from the Italian word "casa," meaning "house."  Over time, it evolved through various uses to refer to a place of entertainment or amusement, eventually settling on its modern meaning of a gambling establishment.  The Italian word "casa"  itself has Latin origins.
