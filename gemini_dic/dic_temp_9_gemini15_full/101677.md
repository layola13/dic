irreligiosity: /ˌɪrɪˌlɪdʒəˈɒsəti/ | n. | 不信教；无宗教信仰 | The lack of religious belief or observance.  It often implies a rejection or indifference towards religion, rather than simply a lack of belief. |例句：The rise of irreligiosity in many Western countries has been a subject of much debate.  许多西方国家无宗教信仰的兴起一直是许多争论的主题。  |近义词：secularism, atheism, nonbelief |反义词：religiosity, piety
