impractical:/ɪmˈpræktɪkəl/| adj.|不切实际的，不实用的|Not practical; not suitable for practical use; unrealistic.  It refers to something that is difficult or impossible to do or use in a real situation.  |例句：His plan to build a castle in the backyard is completely impractical. 他的计划要在后院建一座城堡完全不切实际。

例句：That solution is impractical given our limited resources. 鉴于我们资源有限，那个方案不切实际。


近义词：unrealistic, unworkable, infeasible, visionary, utopian

反义词：practical, feasible, workable, realistic
