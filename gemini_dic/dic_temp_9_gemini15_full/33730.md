circling:/ˈsɜːrkəlɪŋ/| v. | 盘旋；环绕；围绕 | Moving in a circle or circles; going round and round.  |例句：The birds were circling overhead.  鸟儿在头顶盘旋。
The plane circled the airport before landing.  飞机在着陆前绕机场盘旋。|近义词：revolving, rotating, whirling |反义词：straight, direct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The eagle is circling the mountain. = 老鹰正在山顶盘旋。
The planets are circling the sun. = 行星围绕太阳旋转。


一般过去时:
The helicopter circled the building. = 直升机绕着建筑物盘旋。
The vultures circled the carcass. = 秃鹫盘旋在尸体上空。


现在完成时:
The hawk has been circling for hours. = 鹰已经盘旋了几个小时。
They have circled the block three times. = 他们已经绕街区转了三圈。


过去完成时:
The plane had circled twice before landing. = 飞机着陆前已经盘旋了两次。
After circling the field, he finally found the entrance. = 在绕过田野后，他终于找到了入口。


将来时:
The drone will circle the area for surveillance. = 无人机将环绕该区域进行监视。
The birds will circle the nest before settling down. = 鸟儿会在安定下来之前盘旋在巢穴周围。


虚拟语气:
If the pilot circled lower, we could see better. = 如果飞行员盘旋得更低，我们就能看得更清楚。
I wish the plane circled again, I missed the photo opportunity. = 我真希望飞机再盘旋一次，我错过了拍照的机会。


被动语态:
The building was circled by the police helicopter. = 这座建筑物被警用直升机盘旋包围。
The area is being circled by search and rescue teams. = 该区域正在被搜救队环绕搜索。


疑问句:
Is the aircraft circling the airport? = 飞机是否正在绕机场盘旋？
Are the birds circling the tree? = 鸟儿是否正在树上盘旋？


否定句:
The satellite didn't circle the earth. = 卫星没有绕地球运行。
The car didn't circle the roundabout. = 汽车没有绕环形交叉路口行驶。
