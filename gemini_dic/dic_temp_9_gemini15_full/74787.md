freighters:/ˈfreɪtərz/| n.|货船；货轮|A freighter is a large ship used to carry goods.  It's typically designed for cargo transport rather than passengers. |例句：The freighter arrived at the port carrying containers of manufactured goods. 这艘货轮抵达港口，装载着大量的制造商品。|例句：Many freighters traverse the oceans, transporting essential goods globally. 许多货轮横渡海洋，在全球运输重要的货物。|近义词：cargo ship, merchant vessel|反义词：passenger ship, liner|名词
