grumpiest:/ˈɡrʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最脾气暴躁的，最易怒的|Most irritable or bad-tempered.  It is the superlative form of the adjective "grumpy."|例句：He was the grumpiest person I'd ever met. 他是我见过的最脾气暴躁的人。

例句：The grumpiest dog in the neighborhood finally found a friend.  这条街区最脾气暴躁的狗终于找到了一个朋友。


近义词：crabbiest, surliest, most irritable, most ill-tempered|反义词：cheeriest, happiest, most jovial, most good-natured|词性：形容词


词源:  The word "grumpiest" derives from the Middle English word "grumpen," meaning "to grumble or murmur."  This in turn likely originates from a Germanic root, possibly related to words signifying complaint or displeasure.  The "-iest" suffix indicates the superlative degree, signifying the highest degree of grumpiness.
