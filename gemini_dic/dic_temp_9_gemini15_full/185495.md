reusabilities:/ˌriːjuːzəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可重用性，可重复使用性 | The ability of something to be used again and again.  This concept is crucial in software engineering, where reusable components can significantly reduce development time and costs. | Example: The high reusabilities of the code modules allowed for rapid prototyping.  代码模块的高可重用性允许快速原型设计。  The company emphasized the reusabilities of its products in its marketing campaign. 公司在其营销活动中强调其产品的可重用性。|近义词: repeatability, recyclability |反义词: disposability, single-use |名词
