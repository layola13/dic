hypobromite:/ˌhaɪpəˈbroʊmaɪt/ | n. |次溴酸盐|A salt of hypobromous acid (HOBr).  Hypobromites are typically formed by the reaction of bromine with a base. They are strong oxidizing agents and are used as bleaching agents and disinfectants.|Example: Sodium hypobromite is a common hypobromite used in water treatment. 次溴酸钠是水处理中常用的次溴酸盐。|近义词：bromite (less common)|反义词：无
