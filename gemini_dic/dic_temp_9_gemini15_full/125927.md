mushes:/ˈmʌʃɪz/| n.|糊状物；烂泥；粥|A soft, pulpy mass;  a mixture of ingredients often mashed or blended together.|例句：The baby food was mushes.婴儿食品是糊状的。|例句：The heavy rain turned the garden path into mushes. 大雨把花园小路变成了烂泥。|近义词：mash, pulp, mush|反义词：solid, firm

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cook mushes the potatoes. = 厨师把土豆捣成泥。
He mushes the ingredients together. = 他把配料混合在一起。


一般过去时:
She mushed the bananas for the baby. = 她把香蕉捣成泥给婴儿吃。
