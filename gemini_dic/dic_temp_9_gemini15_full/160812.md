possess: /pəˈzɛs/ | vt. |拥有；具有；支配；控制 | to have or own something; to have a particular quality or feature |近义词：own, have, control |反义词：lack, be without


英文解析:
The verb "possess" means to have or own something. It can also refer to having a particular quality or characteristic.  The word implies a degree of control or ownership, sometimes more than simply "having" something.


例句:

He possesses a vast collection of stamps. = 他拥有一大批邮票。

She possesses a remarkable talent for painting. = 她拥有非凡的绘画天赋。


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He possesses a rare book. = 他拥有一本珍本书。
They possess great strength. = 他们拥有巨大的力量。

一般过去时:
She possessed a beautiful singing voice. = 她拥有美丽的歌声。
He possessed a strong sense of justice. = 他具有强烈的正义感。

现在完成时:
I have possessed this car for ten years. = 我拥有这辆车十年了。
She has possessed that painting since her grandmother's death. = 自她祖母去世以来，她就拥有那幅画了。

过去完成时:
He had possessed the land for generations. = 他世世代代拥有这块土地。
They had possessed the secret for years.  = 他们保守这个秘密多年了。

将来时:
I will possess a new house next year. = 明年我将拥有一栋新房子。
She will possess the necessary skills by the end of the course. = 到课程结束时，她将掌握必要的技能。

虚拟语气:
If I possessed a magic wand, I would grant you three wishes. = 如果我有一根魔杖，我会满足你三个愿望。
I wish I possessed the ability to fly. = 我希望我拥有飞行的能力。

被动语态:
The painting was possessed by a wealthy collector. = 这幅画为一位富有的收藏家所有。
The land was possessed by the king. = 这块土地为国王所有。


疑问句:
Does he possess the necessary qualifications? = 他拥有必要的资格吗？
Do they possess any information about the case? = 他们掌握任何关于此案的信息吗？

否定句:
He does not possess the required skills. = 他不具备所需的技能。
They do not possess the evidence to prove their claim. = 他们没有证据来证明他们的说法。
