ruminator: /ˈruːmɪneɪtər/ | n. |沉思者，反复思考者 | A person who thinks deeply and at length; one who ruminates.  A ruminator often dwells on past events or worries excessively about the future. |He was a ruminator, constantly replaying past conversations in his mind. 他是个沉思者，不断地在脑海中重放过去的谈话。|  contemplator, ponderer,  meditator|  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑与其行为相反的词，例如)  action-taker, impulsive person

一般现在时:
The ruminator considers the problem carefully. = 沉思者仔细考虑这个问题。
He ruminates on his failures every night. = 他每天晚上都在反复思考自己的失败。

一般过去时:
The ruminator pondered the situation for hours. = 沉思者思考了几个小时的情况。
She ruminated on her childhood memories. = 她反复思考她童年的回忆。

现在完成时:
The ruminator has spent years contemplating his life choices. = 沉思者已经花了数年时间思考自己的人生选择。
He has ruminated on this issue for weeks. = 他已经考虑这个问题几周了。

过去完成时:
The ruminator had already spent days ruminating before reaching a decision. = 在做出决定之前，沉思者已经花了几天时间反复思考。
She had ruminated over the letter before responding. = 在回复之前，她已经反复思考过这封信。

将来时:
The ruminator will continue to ponder this complex situation. = 沉思者将继续思考这个复杂的情况。

虚拟语气:
If he were a ruminator, he would understand the complexities involved. = 如果他是个沉思者，他会理解其中的复杂性。
I wish I were a better ruminator to solve this problem. = 我希望我是一个更好的沉思者来解决这个问题。


被动语态:

疑问句:
