processionals:/prəˈseʃənəlz/| n. |游行队伍；（尤指宗教）仪式队伍；进行曲|A group of people or things moving along in a formal or ceremonial way, especially in a religious context.  Also refers to pieces of music played during such processions.|例句：The processionals moved slowly down the aisle.  游行队伍缓缓地沿着过道前进。

The wedding processionals included the bride's family and friends. 婚礼队伍包括新娘的家人和朋友。


近义词：parade, cortege, procession, march


反义词：dispersion, scattering


词性:名词
