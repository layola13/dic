It often implies a lack of courage, strength, or decisiveness typically associated with men.  The term can be considered derogatory and should be used cautiously.|例句：His manlessness was evident in his refusal to stand up for what he believed in.  他的懦弱在他拒绝为自己的信仰挺身而出中显而易见。|近义词：cowardice, effeminacy, weakness, pusillanimity|反义词：manliness, courage, bravery, fortitude
