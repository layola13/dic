makes-up-to:/meɪks ʌp tuː/| phrasal verb|构成，组成；弥补；达到；化妆|This phrase combines the verb "make" with the preposition "up" and the preposition "to", creating a phrasal verb with different meanings depending on the context.  It often implies a gradual process or accumulation.|例句：The small contributions made up to a substantial sum.  小额捐款加起来构成了一笔可观的款项。|近义词：constitutes, comprises, amounts to, compensates for|反义词：detracts from, diminishes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The small details make up to a complete picture. = 细小的细节构成了完整的画面。
Her daily routine makes up to eight hours of work. = 她的日常工作时间长达八小时。


一般过去时:
The team's efforts made up to a successful project. = 团队的努力成就了一个成功的项目。
His apology made up to some extent for his rude behavior. = 他的道歉在一定程度上弥补了他粗鲁的行为。


现在完成时:
The donations have made up to the target amount. = 捐款已达到目标金额。
Her hard work has made up to her lack of experience. = 她的努力弥补了她缺乏经验的不足。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, their sales had made up to their annual quota. = 到年底，他们的销售额已达到年度配额。
Before the deadline, she had made up to the lost time. = 在截止日期前，她弥补了失去的时间。


将来时:
Their combined efforts will make up to a significant achievement. = 他们的共同努力将取得重大成就。
With extra hours, he will make up to the missed work. = 加班后，他会把错过的工作补上。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could make up to the lost time. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们就能弥补失去的时间。
If she had studied harder, she could have made up to the failing grade. = 如果她学习更努力，她本来可以弥补不及格的成绩。


被动语态:
The shortfall was made up to by additional funding. = 资金缺口由额外资金弥补。
The lost time will be made up to by working overtime. = 失去的时间将通过加班来弥补。


疑问句:
How will they make up to the lost revenue? = 他们将如何弥补损失的收入？
What measures will be taken to make up to the damage? = 将采取什么措施来弥补损失？


否定句:
The team couldn't make up to the deadline. = 团队没能赶上截止日期。
His apology didn't make up to the hurt feelings. = 他的道歉并没有弥补受伤的感情。


PHRASE->
make up to somebody (to try to please someone, often in a way that is not sincere):  讨好某人
make up to a total of:  总计达到
make up to lost ground: 弥补损失
make up to the difference: 弥补差额
make up to a standard: 达标
