relubricates:/ˌriːˈluːbrɪkeɪts/| vt. |重新润滑；再润滑|To lubricate again or anew.  The process involves applying lubricant to a machine or mechanism to reduce friction and wear.|例句：The mechanic relubricated the engine after the long journey. 机械师在长途旅行后重新润滑了发动机。 The factory workers relubricated the machines regularly to prevent damage. 工人们定期对机器进行重新润滑以防止损坏。|近义词：reoil, grease, oil|反义词：dry out, dehydrate
