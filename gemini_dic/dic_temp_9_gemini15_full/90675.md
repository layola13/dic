humbling:/ˈhʌmblɪŋ/| adj.|令人谦逊的；使人感到渺小的|causing one to feel humble; making one feel less important or significant.  |例句：The experience was humbling, showing me how much I still have to learn.  这次经历令人谦逊，让我明白自己还有多少东西要学习。

例句：His defeat in the election was a humbling experience.  他在选举中的失败是一次令人谦逊的经历。


近义词：humiliating, chastening, debasing, mortifying

反义词：exalting, elevating, aggrandizing, inflating

词性:形容词
