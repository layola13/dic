/ˈdɪpθɒŋaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 双元音化；使成双元音 | The process of changing a monophthong (a single vowel sound) into a diphthong (a vowel sound with two distinct parts).  This often happens naturally in language evolution.  | Example:  The pronunciation of the vowel in "boat" is diphthongizing in some dialects.  一些方言中“boat”中的元音发音正在双元音化。| 近义词:  glide, shift (in pronunciation) | 反义词:  monophthongizing

He diphthongizes his vowels excessively. = 他过度地把元音双元音化。


一般过去时:
She diphthongized the vowel sound during her performance. = 她在表演中将元音发音双元音化了。
The language evolved, diphthongizing many of its original sounds. = 这种语言进化了，许多原始发音都双元音化了。
