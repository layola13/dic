mitriform:/ˈmaɪtrɪfɔːrm/| adj.|主教冠形的；扁平的，帽状的|Resembling a mitre in shape; shaped like a mitre (a tall, pointed bishop's hat).  The term often describes structures or organs that are shaped like a bishop's mitre, characterized by two lateral lobes or projections that meet at an angle.|The mitriform septum of the heart is a crucial structure. 心脏的帽状隔膜是一个重要的结构。 The flower has a mitriform calyx. 这朵花有主教冠形的萼片。|近义词：mitre-shaped, conical, pyramidal|反义词：flat, planar
