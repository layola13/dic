decolonisation: /ˌdiːkɒlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去殖民化；脫殖民化 | The process of a former colony gaining independence and establishing its own governance and cultural identity, free from the influence of the colonizing power.  It often involves reversing the political, economic, and social effects of colonialism. | Example: The decolonisation of Africa occurred gradually throughout the 20th century.  非洲的去殖民化过程贯穿整个20世纪，逐步进行。

The process of decolonization in India led to significant social and political reforms. 印度的去殖民化进程导致了重大的社会和政治改革。| 近义词：liberation, independence, self-determination | 反义词：colonization, imperialism
