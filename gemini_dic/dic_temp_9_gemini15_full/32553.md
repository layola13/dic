chippers:/ˈtʃɪpərz/| n.|碎屑；切碎机；（尤指）精神饱满的人|a person who is cheerful and energetic; a machine that cuts things into small pieces; small pieces of something.|例句：The chippers were working hard to clear the fallen branches.  碎屑机正努力清理掉落的树枝。  例句：He's always been a chipper fellow, even in difficult times. 他一直是个精神饱满的小伙子，即使在困难时期也是如此。|近义词：cheerful, lively, energetic, grinder, shredder|反义词：depressed, gloomy, lethargic
