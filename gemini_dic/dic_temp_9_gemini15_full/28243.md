capsulitis:/ˌkæpsəˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |关节囊炎|Inflammation of the joint capsule.  This condition is characterized by pain, stiffness, and limited range of motion in the affected joint.  It can affect various joints in the body, such as the shoulder, hip, knee, and wrist. The inflammation is often caused by injury, overuse, or underlying medical conditions. |例句：He suffered from shoulder capsulitis after a fall. 他摔倒后患上了肩关节囊炎。|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with hip capsulitis and prescribed physical therapy. 医生诊断她患有髋关节囊炎，并开了物理治疗的处方。|近义词：arthritis, bursitis|反义词：N/A
