lycotropous: /ˌlaɪkəˈtroʊpəs/ | adj. |  胚珠倒生；下垂胚珠的 |  Referring to an ovule in which the micropyle (the opening of the ovule) points towards the base of the ovary.  This is in contrast to anatropous ovules, where the micropyle points towards the apex of the ovary. | The study of plant embryology revealed that the plant had lycotropous ovules.  | 近义词：resupinate (in some contexts) | 反义词：anatropous, orthotropous
