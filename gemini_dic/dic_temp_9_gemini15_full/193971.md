self-examination:/ˌsɛlfˌɛɡzæmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |自我检查；反省；自省|The act of examining oneself, one's thoughts, feelings, or actions critically.  It involves introspection and self-assessment to identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement. |例句：Regular self-examination is crucial for personal growth. 定期进行自我反省对个人成长至关重要。|例句：The doctor recommended a self-examination for early detection of skin cancer. 医生建议进行自我检查以早期发现皮肤癌。|近义词：introspection, self-analysis, self-reflection, soul-searching |反义词：self-deception, denial
