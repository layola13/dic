polygamist:/pəˈlɪɡəmɪst/| n. |一夫多妻制/一妻多夫制者|A person who practices polygamy, having more than one spouse at the same time.  Polygamists often cite religious or cultural reasons for their lifestyle. |例句：He is a polygamist who has three wives. 他是一个有三个妻子的多妻制者。|例句：The community was known for its acceptance of polygamists.  这个社区以接受多妻制者而闻名。|近义词：polygamist (no true synonym, as it refers to a specific practice)|反义词：monogamist|名词
