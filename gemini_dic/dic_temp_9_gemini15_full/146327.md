overfeminizing: /ˌoʊvərˈfɛmənˌaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |过度女性化，使过于女性化 |  The act of making something or someone excessively feminine, often to the point of caricature or stereotype.  This can apply to objects, roles, or individuals.  It implies a distortion or exaggeration of feminine traits. |

例句：

The marketing campaign was criticized for overfeminizing the product, making it seem unsuitable for a broader audience.  =  市场营销活动因过度女性化产品而受到批评，使其似乎不适合更广泛的受众。


The film's portrayal of female characters was accused of overfeminizing them, reducing their complexity to stereotypical tropes. =  这部电影对女性角色的刻画被指责为过度女性化，将其复杂性简化为刻板印象的比喻。


近义词：effeminizing, feminizing excessively, making excessively girly


反义词：masculinizing, making excessively masculine


词性：动词
