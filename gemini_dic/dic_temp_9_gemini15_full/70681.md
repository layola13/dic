finalised:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzd/| v.| 完成；定稿；最后确定 | To make something final or complete; to put the finishing touches on something. |例句：We finalised the contract last night. 我们昨晚完成了合同。|例句：The plans for the new building have been finalised. 新建筑的计划已经定稿。|近义词：completed, concluded, settled, perfected |反义词：incompleted, unsettled, unfinished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They finalise the report every Friday. = 他们每周五完成报告。
She finalises her work before leaving the office. = 她下班前完成工作。

一般过去时:
He finalised the deal yesterday. = 他昨天完成了这笔交易。
The committee finalised the agenda last week. = 委员会上周敲定了议程。

现在完成时:
We have finalised all the arrangements for the wedding. = 我们已经完成了婚礼的所有安排。
They have finalised the design for the new product. = 他们已经完成了新产品的最终设计。

过去完成时:
She had finalised her presentation before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已经完成了她的演示文稿。
They had finalised the contract by the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前完成了合同。

将来时:
We will finalise the budget by the end of the month. = 我们将在月底前完成预算。
They will finalise the details tomorrow. = 他们明天将敲定细节。

虚拟语气:
If we had finalised the contract earlier, we wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果我们早点完成合同，就不会现在这样了。
I wish I had finalised my report before the deadline. = 我真希望我在截止日期前完成了我的报告。

被动语态:
The contract was finalised last week. = 合同在上周最终确定。
The project will be finalised next month. = 项目将在下个月完成。

疑问句:
Have you finalised your application yet? = 你已经完成你的申请了吗？
When will the agreement be finalised? = 协议什么时候才能最终确定？

否定句:
They haven't finalised their plans yet. = 他们还没有最终确定他们的计划。
We didn't finalise the deal because of some unexpected issues. = 由于一些意外问题，我们没有完成交易。
