puree:/pʊˈreɪ/| n. v.|泥状物；捣碎；将…捣成泥|A puree is a smooth, creamy mixture made by blending or processing food until it has no lumps.  It's often used to describe foods like fruits, vegetables, or beans that have been processed into a smooth consistency.|例句：The baby food is a delicious carrot puree.  婴儿食品是美味的胡萝卜泥。  I pureed the tomatoes for the sauce. 我把西红柿捣碎做酱汁。|近义词：mash, pulp, paste|反义词：chunk, whole


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I puree the vegetables every week. = 我每周都把蔬菜捣成泥。
She purees the soup until it's smooth. = 她把汤捣碎直到它很顺滑。


一般过去时:
