outdanced:/ˌaʊtˈdænst/| v.|胜过…的舞技|To surpass someone in dancing skill or grace; to excel in a dance competition or performance.|例句：She completely outdanced her rivals, winning the competition with her breathtaking performance. 她凭借令人叹为观止的表演彻底击败了她的对手，赢得了比赛。


例句：The seasoned ballerina outdanced the newcomer with effortless grace.  这位经验丰富的芭蕾舞演员以轻松优雅的姿态胜过了新人。


近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, excel


反义词：be outdone by, be surpassed by


词性:动词


USAGE->

He outdances all his competitors. 他在舞蹈技巧上胜过所有竞争对手。
She outdances everyone in the class. 她在班上舞蹈技巧胜过所有人。
