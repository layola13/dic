microchemist:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌkɛmɪst/ | n. | 微量化学家 | A scientist who specializes in microchemistry, the branch of chemistry dealing with very small quantities of substances.  They often work with samples measured in micrograms or nanograms. |  Dr. Lee is a renowned microchemist, known for her work on trace element analysis. 李博士是一位著名的微量化学家，以其在微量元素分析方面的工作而闻名。 |  chemist, analytical chemist | macrochemist (反义词，相对而言)
