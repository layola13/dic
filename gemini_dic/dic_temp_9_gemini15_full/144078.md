outruns:/ˌaʊtˈrʌnz/| v.|超过，跑过，胜过 |To run faster than; to exceed in speed or duration.  |例句：The hare outran the tortoise.  兔子跑过了乌龟。 |例句：Her success outran her expectations. 她的成功超出了她的预期。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outpace, outstrip|反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outruns his opponents in every race. 他在每场比赛中都超过了他的对手。
The river outruns the road for several miles. 这条河流在几英里内都超过了这条路。

一般过去时:
She outran all the other runners in the marathon. 她在马拉松比赛中超过了所有其他跑步者。
The police car outran the getaway vehicle. 警车超过了逃跑的车辆。

现在完成时:
The demand for the product has outrun the supply. 对该产品的需求已经超过了供应。
He has outrun his previous best time in the 100 meters. 他在100米比赛中超过了他之前的最好成绩。

过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the floodwaters had outrun their containment barriers. 当我们到达时，洪水已经超过了它们的围堰。
The thief had outrun the security guards before they could catch him. 小偷在保安抓住他之前已经跑掉了。

将来时:
The company projects that its revenue will outrun its expenses next quarter. 公司预计下个季度其收入将超过支出。
I believe that my hard work will outrun my fears and lead me to success.我相信我的努力将战胜我的恐惧并引领我走向成功。

虚拟语气:
If only I had trained harder, I could have outrun him. 如果我训练更努力的话，我就能超过他了。
If she had started earlier, she would have outrun the storm.如果她早点出发的话，她就超过了暴风雨。

被动语态:
The criminal was outrun by the police. 罪犯被警察超过了。
The deadline was outrun by the project's completion. 项目的完成时间超过了截止日期。
