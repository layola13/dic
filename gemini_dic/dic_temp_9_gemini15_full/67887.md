fabulize:/ˈfæbjʊlaɪz/ | vt. |虚构；编造故事；杜撰 |To create or relate a fable or fictitious story; to invent or embellish a story with fictitious details.  |例句：He fabulized his childhood experiences to make them more exciting. 他虚构了他的童年经历，使它们更刺激。|例句：She fabulized the events of the day, making them sound far more dramatic than they actually were. 她杜撰了当天发生的事情，使它们听起来比实际情况要戏剧化得多。|近义词：invent, fabricate, concoct, fictionalize |反义词：document, report factually, chronicle accurately |动词
