genome:/ˈdʒinoʊm/ | n. |基因组|The complete set of genes or genetic material present in a cell or organism.| 例句：The human genome project aimed to map the entire human genome. 人类基因组计划旨在绘制整个人类基因组图谱。|近义词：genotype, karyotype |反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The genome contains all the genetic information. = 基因组包含所有遗传信息。
Scientists study the genome to understand diseases. = 科学家研究基因组以了解疾病。

一般过去时:
Researchers sequenced the entire genome. = 研究人员测序了整个基因组。
They analyzed the genome for mutations. = 他们分析了基因组中的突变。

现在完成时:
Scientists have mapped many genomes. = 科学家已经绘制了许多基因组图谱。
Researchers have identified several genes within the genome. = 研究人员已经确定了基因组中的几个基因。

过去完成时:
Before the project, they had not sequenced a complete genome. = 在项目之前，他们还没有测序过完整的基因组。
They had already analyzed parts of the genome before the discovery. = 在发现之前，他们已经分析了基因组的部分内容。

将来时:
Scientists will sequence more genomes in the future. = 科学家们将来会测序更多的基因组。
They will use the genome data to develop new treatments. = 他们将利用基因组数据开发新的疗法。

虚拟语气:
If we knew the entire genome, we could cure many diseases. = 如果我们知道整个基因组，我们就能治愈许多疾病。
It is important that the genome be studied carefully. = 基因组必须被仔细研究。

被动语态:

疑问句:
What is the size of the human genome? = 人类基因组的大小是多少？

否定句:
