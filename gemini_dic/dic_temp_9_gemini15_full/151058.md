paraphrasing:/ˈpærəfreɪzɪŋ/ | n. |释义 | the act of expressing the meaning of something written or spoken in different words, often to make it easier to understand.  | 例句：The student's paraphrasing of the complex text was clear and concise.  学生的复杂文本释义清晰简洁。

The teacher asked for a paraphrasing of the poem in simpler language. 老师要求用更简单的语言来解释这首诗。| 近义词：rewording, restating, summarizing | 反义词：quoting (directly)

He is paraphrasing the author's main points. 他正在释义作者的主要观点。
She paraphrases complex ideas effectively. 她有效地解释了复杂的思想。

一般过去时:
I paraphrased the article for my presentation. 我为我的演示文稿解释了这篇文章。
