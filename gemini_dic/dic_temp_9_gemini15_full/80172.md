godfathered:/ˈɡɑːdfɑːðərd/| vt.|担任教父；庇护；支持；|To act as a godfather to; to sponsor or support (someone or something), often in a way that provides protection or advancement.|The Mafia boss godfathered the young man's career in the construction business. 黑帮老大为这个年轻人建筑业的职业生涯提供了庇护。|近义词：sponsor, patronize, protect|反义词：abandon, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He godfathers many young entrepreneurs. 他为许多年轻企业家担任教父。
The company godfathers several charitable organizations. 这家公司资助几家慈善机构。


一般过去时:
The senator godfathered the bill through Congress. 参议员将该法案通过国会。
My uncle godfathered my first business venture. 我的叔叔资助了我的第一个商业项目。


现在完成时:
He has godfathered numerous projects in the city. 他已经为城市中的许多项目提供了支持。
The foundation has godfathered countless artists over the years. 该基金会多年来资助了无数艺术家。


过去完成时:
They had godfathered the project before the funding was secured. 他们在资金到位之前就为该项目提供了支持。
The family had godfathered his education since childhood.  这个家庭从小就资助了他的教育。


将来时:
The CEO will godfather the new initiative.  首席执行官将支持这项新举措。
They will godfather the young artist's exhibition. 他们将资助这位年轻艺术家的展览。


虚拟语气:
If only someone would godfather my startup.  如果有人能资助我的创业公司就好了。
I wish a wealthy patron would godfather my research. 我希望一位富有的赞助人能资助我的研究。


被动语态:
The project was godfathered by a prominent philanthropist. 该项目得到了著名慈善家的资助。
His career was godfathered by a powerful industry figure. 他的职业生涯得到了业内有影响力的人物支持。


疑问句:
Who godfathered this ambitious undertaking? 谁资助了这项雄心勃勃的事业？
Will anyone godfather the young musician's concert tour? 会有人资助这位年轻音乐家的巡回演唱会吗？


否定句:
No one godfathered his attempt to start a business. 没有人资助他创业的尝试。
The organization did not godfather the controversial project. 该组织没有资助这个有争议的项目。


PHRASE->

godfather a career = 扶持一个职业生涯
