fogbound: /ˈfɒɡbaʊnd/ | adj. |被雾笼罩的，被雾阻碍的 |  Surrounded by or enveloped in fog, especially to the extent of hindering movement or visibility.  |例句：The ship was fogbound in the harbor for three days.  这艘船在港口因大雾滞留了三天。

例句：The mountain road was fogbound, making driving extremely dangerous.  山路被大雾笼罩，使得驾驶极其危险。|近义词：mist-shrouded, fog-enveloped, obscured |反义词：clear, cloudless
