regularly:/ˈrɛɡjələrli/ | adv. | 定期地；经常地 | Regularly occurring events happen at fixed intervals.  This adverb indicates that something happens at fixed intervals or frequently.  It emphasizes the consistent nature of an action or occurrence. |例句：I regularly exercise to stay healthy. 我定期锻炼以保持健康。

例句：She regularly visits her grandmother. 她经常去看望她的祖母。


近义词：frequently, often, habitually, consistently | 反义词：irregularly, infrequently, occasionally


词性：副词
