dumping:/ˈdʌmpɪŋ/| n. vt.|倾倒；倾销；抛弃；甩掉|The act of disposing of something, especially waste material, in a careless or hurried way.  It can also refer to the practice of selling goods in a foreign market at a price below their normal value.|例句：They are dumping toxic waste into the river. 他们正在把有毒废物倾倒进河里。|例句：The company was accused of dumping its products at below-cost prices. 这家公司被指控以低于成本价倾销其产品。|近义词：discarding, disposing of, unloading,  offloading|反义词：preserving, storing, keeping, retaining|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He dumps his rubbish in the alley. = 他把垃圾扔在小巷里。
She dumps her old clothes regularly. = 她定期丢弃旧衣服。

一般过去时:
They dumped the broken furniture. = 他们扔掉了坏掉的家具。
He dumped his girlfriend last week. = 他上周甩了他的女朋友。

现在完成时:
They have dumped all the old computers. = 他们已经把所有旧电脑都扔掉了。
She has dumped several boyfriends in the past. = 她过去甩掉了好几个男朋友。

过去完成时:
He had dumped the evidence before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经销毁了证据。
They had dumped the toxic waste illegally for years. = 他们多年来一直非法倾倒有毒废物。

将来时:
We will dump this old sofa tomorrow. = 我们明天将扔掉这旧沙发。
The factory will dump the waste into the sea. = 工厂将把废物倾倒入海。

虚拟语气:
If I had money, I would dump this car. = 如果我有钱，我会把这辆车扔了。
If they knew the consequences, they wouldn't dump the waste. = 如果他们知道后果，他们就不会倾倒废物了。

被动语态:
The waste was dumped illegally. = 废物被非法倾倒。
The old house is being dumped. = 旧房子正在被拆除（引申义）。

疑问句:
Where did they dump the garbage? = 他们把垃圾扔到哪里了？
Will you dump the broken vase? = 你会把破花瓶扔掉吗？

否定句:
He doesn't dump his trash on the street. = 他不会把垃圾扔在街上。
They didn't dump the chemicals properly. = 他们没有正确处理化学品。


PHRASE->
