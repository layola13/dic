round:/raʊnd/ /raʊnd/| n., adj., adv., v.| 圆的；一轮；回合；大约；环绕 |  A round object has a circular shape.  It can also refer to a period of time, a stage in a process, or an act of going around something.  As a verb, it means to move around something, or to make something round. |例句：The table is round.  这桌子是圆的。


The earth is round. 地球是圆的。


近义词：circular, spherical, approximately, about |反义词：square, rectangular, exact, precise |词性：n., adj., adv., v.
