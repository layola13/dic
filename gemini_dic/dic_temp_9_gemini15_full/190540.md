satinwood:/ˈsætɪnˌwʊd/| n. |缎木；黄檀木 |A type of wood with a smooth, satiny surface, often used in furniture making.  It is typically light-colored with a fine grain. Several species of trees produce wood called satinwood, most notably those in the genus *Chloroxylon* and *Zanthoxylum*.|The antique dresser was made of exquisite satinwood.|近义词：polished wood, fine wood|反义词：rough wood, coarse wood
