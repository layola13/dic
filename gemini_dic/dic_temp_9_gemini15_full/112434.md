lotting:/ˈlɒtɪŋ/| n. |分派；分配；抽签决定；批量处理|The process of dividing something into lots or shares, often through a random process such as drawing lots.  It can also refer to the act of assigning tasks or responsibilities.  In computing, it can mean processing data in batches.|例句：The land was undergoing lotting for development. 这块地正在进行地块划分以进行开发。

The students were subjected to lotting for their dorm assignments. 学生们通过抽签决定宿舍分配。

近义词：allocation, distribution, apportionment, assignment|反义词：centralization, consolidation|词性:名词
