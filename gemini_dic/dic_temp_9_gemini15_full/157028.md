pilosity:/ˌpaɪˈlɒsəti/| n. |毛发；多毛；茸毛|The presence or growth of hair, especially on the body.  It refers to the overall hairiness or downy covering of a surface, often used in a scientific or medical context. |例句：The pilosity on his arms was quite noticeable.  他胳膊上的毛发相当明显。

例句：Excessive pilosity can be a symptom of certain hormonal imbalances. 过多的毛发可能是某些荷尔蒙失衡的症状。


近义词：hairiness, downiness, fuzziness

反义词：baldness, glabrousness

词性：名词
