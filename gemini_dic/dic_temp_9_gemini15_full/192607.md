USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  The usage is straightforward; it describes the state of something as being extremely dirty and neglected.

The scungiest room in the house needed a complete overhaul.  这间房子里最脏的房间需要彻底翻修。
He lived in a scungiest shack on the outskirts of town. 他住在城郊一间最脏的棚屋里。

There are no common phrases specifically using "scungiest."  The word itself is relatively informal and rarely used in established phrases.  Instead, it would be used to describe a noun directly: "the scungiest bathroom," "a scungiest alley," etc.
