leaderboards:/ˈliːdəbɔːrdz/ | n. |排行榜，积分榜 |A leaderboard is a list showing the ranked positions of players or teams in a game or competition.  It typically displays scores, points, or other metrics to indicate performance. |Example:  The leaderboard showed that John was in first place. 排行榜显示约翰排在第一位。


The global leaderboard displays the top 100 players worldwide. 全球排行榜显示全球排名前 100 名的玩家。

|近义词：ranking, scorecard, table |反义词：无 |词性：名词
