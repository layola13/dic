floweriest:/ˈflaʊəriɪst/| adj.| 最鲜艳的；最华丽的；最充满花的 | Most full of flowers; most flowery or ornate in style.  It suggests an abundance and perhaps an excessive use of flowers or flowery language. |例句：The floweriest garden in the town was a riot of color. 镇上最鲜艳的花园色彩缤纷。

The floweriest prose I've ever read was almost unbearable. 我读过的最华丽的散文几乎让人难以忍受。|近义词：most flowery, most ornate, most flamboyant |反义词：plainest, simplest, starkest
