incapacitating: /ɪnˈkæpəseɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 使失去能力的；使无行为能力的 |  rendering incapable; disabling.  The incapacitating effects of the disease left him bedridden.  | 近义词：debilitating, disabling, crippling, weakening | 反义词：strengthening, empowering


例句：

The incapacitating pain prevented him from moving.  = 难以忍受的疼痛使他无法动弹。

Her incapacitating fear of heights kept her from climbing the mountain. = 她对高空极度恐惧，让她无法爬山。
