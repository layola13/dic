punkin:/ˈpʌŋkɪn/| n. |南瓜；小家伙|A punkin is a common informal term for a pumpkin. It's often used in a playful or affectionate way, especially in dialects of American English.|例句：He carved a scary face into the punkin. 他在南瓜上刻了一个可怕的脸。|例句：Grandma baked a delicious pie with that big ol' punkin. 奶奶用那个大南瓜烤了一个美味的馅饼。|近义词：pumpkin, gourd|反义词:  无
