refreshable: /rɪˈfreʃəbl/ | adj. | 可刷新的，可更新的 | Able to be refreshed; capable of being renewed or revitalized.  |例句：The software is refreshable, allowing users to update the data easily.  该软件是可刷新的，允许用户轻松更新数据。

The user interface is refreshable, providing a smooth and responsive experience. 用户界面是可刷新的，提供流畅且响应迅速的体验。|近义词：renewable, updatable, reusable |反义词：irreplaceable, unchangeable, permanent |

词源:
The word "refreshable" is derived from the verb "refresh," which comes from the Old French word "refreschir," meaning "to cool, to refresh."  The "-able" suffix indicates capability or possibility.  Therefore, "refreshable" signifies something that can be refreshed or made new.
