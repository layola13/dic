reorder:/ˌriːˈɔːrdər/| vt.|重新排序，改编顺序|To arrange something in a new order.  This often involves rearranging items that were previously in a different sequence. |例句：Please reorder the files by date. 请按日期重新排列文件。

例句：The editor reordered the chapters to improve the narrative flow. 编辑重新排列章节以改善叙事流程。


近义词：rearrange, reorganize, reshuffle|反义词：disorganize, jumble|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reorder the shelves every month. = 他们每月都会重新整理货架。
We reorder supplies when they run low. = 当用品快用完时，我们会重新订购。


一般过去时:
She reordered the photos in her album. = 她重新整理了相册里的照片。
He reordered the paragraphs to make the essay clearer. = 他重新排列了段落，使文章更清晰。


现在完成时:
They have reordered the furniture in the living room. = 他们已经重新布置了客厅的家具。
I have reordered the chapters for better clarity. = 我已经重新排列章节以提高清晰度。


过去完成时:
She had reordered the ingredients before starting to cook. = 在开始烹饪之前，她已经重新整理了配料。
They had reordered the agenda before the meeting began. = 会议开始前，他们已经重新安排了议程。


将来时:
We will reorder the books after the inventory. = 我们将在盘点后重新订购书籍。
They will reorder the schedule once the new information is available. = 一旦新的信息可用，他们将重新安排时间表。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reorder my priorities. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新安排我的优先级。
I wish I had reordered the tasks earlier. = 我希望我早点重新安排任务。


被动语态:
The files were reordered alphabetically. = 文件按字母顺序重新排列。
The schedule is being reordered due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，时间表正在重新安排。


疑问句:
Will you reorder the items according to size? = 你会按大小重新排列物品吗？
Have they reordered the seating arrangements? = 他们已经重新安排座位了吗？


否定句:
He did not reorder the slides before the presentation. = 他在演示前没有重新排列幻灯片。
They will not reorder the products until next week. = 他们直到下周才会重新订购产品。


PHRASE->
reorder points = 重新排序要点
reorder the list = 重新排序列表
reorder the sequence = 重新排序序列
reorder the data = 重新排序数据
reorder the information = 重新排序信息
