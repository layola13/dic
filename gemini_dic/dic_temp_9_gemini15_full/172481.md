pyramidding: /ˌpɪrəˈmɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 金字塔式交易，层层加码 | The act of increasing one's investment in a security by repeatedly buying more of it, often at increasingly higher prices, resembling the shape of a pyramid.  This is a risky practice, particularly in declining markets. | Example:  Pyramidding in the stock market can lead to significant losses if the price drops. = 在股市中层层加码，如果股价下跌，可能会导致重大损失。| 近义词：leveraging, pyramiding investments | 反义词：hedging, diversifying
