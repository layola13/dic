psaltery:/ˈsɔːltəri/| n.|扬琴，七弦琴|A psaltery is a stringed musical instrument, typically plucked with the fingers or a plectrum.  It has a flat body, usually trapezoidal or triangular, and strings stretched across it. The sound is often described as sweet and gentle.|例句：The musician played a beautiful melody on the psaltery.  那位音乐家用扬琴演奏了一首优美的旋律。The ancient psaltery was found during the archaeological dig. 考古挖掘中发现了古老的扬琴。|近义词：zither, lyre, dulcimer|反义词：None
