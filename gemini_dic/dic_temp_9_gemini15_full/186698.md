righteousness:/ˈraɪtʃəsnəs/ | n. | 正义；公正；正直 | Righteousness is the quality of being morally right or justifiable.  It implies conformity to a standard of right conduct.  It often suggests a strong sense of justice and moral uprightness.  The term often carries a connotation of moral superiority or self-righteousness. |例句：He fought for righteousness and justice. 他为正义和公正而战。

例句：Her righteousness was unwavering even in the face of adversity. 即使面对逆境，她的正义感也毫不动摇。


近义词：justice, morality, rectitude, uprightness, probity | 反义词：wickedness, injustice, iniquity, immorality, depravity | 词性：名词
