committal:/kəˈmɪtəl/ | n. | 承诺；委托；拘留；承担义务 | The act of committing or the state of being committed.  This can refer to a promise, a responsibility, a legal process, or a mental state. | Example:  The government's committal to the peace process was widely praised.  政府对和平进程的承诺受到广泛赞扬。

The judge ordered the committal of the defendant to prison.  法官下令将被告监禁。 | 近义词：commitment, pledge, entrustment, confinement | 反义词：release, denial, rejection |  词性：名词
