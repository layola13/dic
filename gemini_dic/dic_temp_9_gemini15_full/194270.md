self-observed:/ˌsɛlf əbˈzɜːrvd/| adj.| 自我观察的，自我觉察的 |Describes something that is observed or noticed by oneself;  pertaining to introspection or self-reflection. It emphasizes the act of observing one's own thoughts, feelings, or behaviors.|例句：Her self-observed anxiety levels increased before exams.  她的自我观察到的焦虑水平在考试前有所增加。  |例句：The self-observed data revealed a pattern of procrastination. 自我观察的数据揭示了一种拖延的模式。|近义词：introspective, self-reflective, self-aware |反义词：unaware, oblivious, unconcerned |形容词
