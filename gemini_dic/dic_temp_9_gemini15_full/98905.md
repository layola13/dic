intension:/ɪnˈtɛnʃən/| n. |意图；目的；含义|The inherent meaning or purpose of something;  a plan or intention.|例句：His intention was to help, not to hinder. 他的意图是帮助，而不是阻碍。|例句：The true intension of the painting remains a mystery. 这幅画的真正含义仍然是一个谜。|近义词：purpose, aim, design, meaning |反义词：accident, chance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His intention is to finish the project by Friday. = 他的意图是在周五之前完成项目。
What is your intention in coming here? = 你来这里的目的是什么？


一般过去时:
Her intention was to surprise him with a gift. = 她的意图是用礼物给他一个惊喜。
What was your intention when you said that? = 你那样说的时候，你的意图是什么？


现在完成时:
His intention has been to help others throughout his life. = 他毕生的意图都是帮助他人。
What has been your intention in this matter? = 在这件事上，你的意图是什么？


过去完成时:
I realised that his intention had been to deceive me all along. = 我意识到他一直以来的意图都是欺骗我。
What had been their intention before the change of plans? = 在计划改变之前，他们的意图是什么？


将来时:
My intention is to travel to Europe next year. = 我打算明年去欧洲旅行。
What will your intention be regarding the upcoming election? = 关于即将到来的选举，你的意图是什么？


虚拟语气:
If my intention had been clear, there would have been less confusion. = 如果我的意图明确，就不会有那么多的混乱。
I wish my intention had been better understood. = 我希望我的意图能被更好地理解。


被动语态:
His intention was misunderstood. = 他的意图被误解了。
The intention behind the action is still being debated. = 行为背后的意图仍在争论中。


疑问句:
What is your intention? = 你的意图是什么？
What was the intention behind his actions? = 他行为背后的意图是什么？


否定句:
It wasn't my intention to upset anyone. = 我无意冒犯任何人。
There was no malicious intention behind her words. = 她的话并没有恶意。


PHRASE->
