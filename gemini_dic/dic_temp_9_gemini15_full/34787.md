climatologies:/ˌklaɪməˈtɒlədʒiz/ | n. |气候学(复数) |The study of climates, including their classification, causes, and effects.  It encompasses various aspects, such as historical climate data analysis, climate modeling, and the impact of climate change. | Climatologies are crucial for understanding long-term weather patterns and predicting future climate scenarios.  气候学对于理解长期天气模式和预测未来气候情景至关重要。|Meteorology, climatography| (No direct antonym, but the opposite concept could be "individual weather events")|名词
