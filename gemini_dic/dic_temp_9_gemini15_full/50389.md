deposable:/dɪˈpoʊzəbəl/| adj.|可免除的；可撤销的；可取代的；可解雇的 | capable of being deposed; removable from office.  It refers to something that can be removed, replaced, or dismissed, often referring to a person's position or testimony.  |例句：The witness's testimony was deemed deposable, meaning it could be challenged in court.  证人的证词被认为是可以撤销的，这意味着它可以在法庭上受到质疑。
The CEO's position is deposable if the board loses confidence. 如果董事会失去信心，首席执行官的职位是可以撤销的。|近义词：removable, replaceable, dischargeable |反义词：irremovable, irreplaceable, indispensable
