exposed:/ɪkˈspoʊzd/ | adj. |暴露的；无掩护的；面临的；揭露的 | Exposed means not covered or protected; open to view or attack; or revealed. It can refer to a physical location, a person's vulnerability, or a secret being revealed. |例句：The exposed wires are a fire hazard.  暴露的电线是火灾隐患。

The child was exposed to the elements.  孩子暴露在风雨中。


近义词：uncovered, unprotected, vulnerable, revealed, unveiled | 反义词：covered, protected, sheltered, concealed, hidden | 词性:形容词
