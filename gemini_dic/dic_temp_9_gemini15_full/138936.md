nutshell:/ˈnʌtʃɛl/| n. |坚果壳；概括；要点|A nutshell is the hard shell of a nut.  In figurative language, it refers to a brief summary or concise explanation. |例句：He explained the whole story in a nutshell. 他扼要地解释了整个故事。

例句：Let me give you the gist of the meeting in a nutshell.  让我简要地告诉你会议要点。


近义词：summary, abstract, synopsis, brief, gist

反义词：detailed explanation, lengthy account, comprehensive analysis

词性:名词
