desubsidizing: /ˌdiːˈsʌbsɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |取消补贴；停止补贴 | The act of removing or reducing subsidies.  It implies a deliberate action to end financial assistance previously provided. |例句：The government is desubsidizing the agricultural sector, leading to higher food prices. 政府正在取消对农业部门的补贴，导致食品价格上涨。

The government's decision to desubsidize fuel has resulted in increased transportation costs.  政府决定取消燃油补贴导致运输成本上升。| 近义词：withdrawing subsidies, ending subsidies, phasing out subsidies | 反义词：subsidizing, supporting, financing |  词性：动词
