The lack of communication between interdivisions led to duplicated efforts and wasted resources. = 部门之间的缺乏沟通导致了工作重复和资源浪费。


近义词：internal divisions, sub-divisions, sections, compartments | 反义词：unification, integration, consolidation | 词性：名词


词源:

* **inter-**:  Prefix meaning "between," "among," or "mutually."  Derived from Latin *inter*.

* **division**: Noun meaning "the act of dividing," "a part or section," or "a disagreement." Derived from Old French *division*, ultimately from Latin *divisio*.
