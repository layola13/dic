rejuvenize:/rɪˈdʒuːvənʌɪz/| vt.|使恢复活力；使年轻；使复兴|To make someone or something look or feel younger, more lively, or more effective.|The spa treatment rejuvenated her skin. 水疗护理使她的皮肤恢复了活力。|近义词：revitalize, refresh, renew, revitalise|反义词：age, deteriorate, decay
