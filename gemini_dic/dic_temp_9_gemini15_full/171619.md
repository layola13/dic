pulpiest:/ˈpʌlpiːst/| adj.| 最多浆的，最柔软的；最煽动的 | Most full of pulp; most soft and juicy; most sensational or inflammatory.  The word describes something with a high pulp content, often referring to fruit, but can also be used figuratively to describe something overly sensationalized or preachy. |例句：The pulpiest orange I've ever tasted was incredibly sweet and juicy. 我吃过最甜最多汁的橙子，非常甜美多汁。

The pulpiest sermons often leave the congregation feeling more agitated than enlightened. 最煽动人心的布道往往让会众感到更加激动不安，而不是受到启迪。


近义词：juiciest, fleshiest (for fruit); sensationalist, inflammatory (figurative) |反义词：dry, hard, insipid (for fruit); factual, objective (figurative) |词性:形容词
