They might be intellectually curious, but also argumentative or obstructive.|例句：He's a real problematist; he always finds something to complain about. 他是个真正的麻烦制造者；他总是能找到一些东西抱怨。|例句：She's a problematist by nature, always questioning the status quo. 她天生就是一个好问的人，总是质疑现状。|近义词：troublemaker, agitator, critic, contrarian|反义词：peacemaker, conformist, appeaser|名词
