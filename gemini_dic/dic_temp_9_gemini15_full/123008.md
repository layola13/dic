monarcho:/məˈnɑːrkoʊ/| n. |君主；统治者 |Relating to or characteristic of a monarch or monarchy.  The word is less common in modern English, often replaced by more specific terms like "king," "queen," "emperor," or "ruler."  It implies a focus on the role or status of being a monarch rather than the person themselves. |例句：The monarcho's reign was marked by both prosperity and unrest.  这位君主的统治时期既有繁荣也有动荡。

The study of monarchology revealed interesting insights into the power dynamics of the era.  对君主制的学习揭示了那个时代权力动态的有趣见解。|近义词：king, queen, emperor, sovereign, ruler |反义词：subject, commoner
