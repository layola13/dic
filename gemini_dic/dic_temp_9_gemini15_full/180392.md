refutation:/ˌrefjuˈteɪʃən/| n. |驳斥；反驳|The act of proving something to be wrong or false; a statement or argument that disproves something.  |例句：His refutation of the theory was based on strong evidence.  他的理论驳斥基于强有力的证据。

例句：The lawyer presented a strong refutation of the witness's testimony. 律师对证人的证词提出了强有力的反驳。


近义词：denial, contradiction, disproof, refutal


反义词：confirmation, verification, corroboration


词性：名词
