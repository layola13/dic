effervescible:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsəbəl/ | adj. | 欢腾的；兴奋的；充满活力的 |  Characterized by or producing effervescence; lively; vivacious; bubbly.  The word describes something that shows great energy and excitement, often in a bubbly or fizzy way, similar to a carbonated drink. |例句：Her effervescible personality brightened the room.  她的热情活泼的性格照亮了房间。

例句：The children were effervescible with excitement before the party.  孩子们在派对前兴奋地欢腾。


近义词：bubbly, vivacious, lively, sparkling, spirited | 反义词：dull, flat, lifeless, apathetic, subdued


词源：From Middle French *effervescible, from Latin effervescere "to boil up, bubble," from ex- "out" + fervere "to boil."  The -ible suffix indicates capability or tendency.
