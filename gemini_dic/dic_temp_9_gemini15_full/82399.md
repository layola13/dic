grubworm:/ˈɡrʌbˌwɜːrm/| n. |蛴螬，地老虎|A larva of certain beetles, especially those that feed on the roots of plants.  They are often pests in gardens and agricultural fields.|例句：The grubworms destroyed the farmer's entire potato crop.  蛴螬毁掉了农民的整个土豆收成。

例句：We found several grubworms in the soil near the damaged plants. 我们在受损植物附近的土壤中发现了几个蛴螬。


近义词：wireworm, larva, insect pest |反义词：无

词源:
The word "grubworm" is a compound word. "Grub" refers to the larva of an insect, particularly one that is considered a pest.  "Worm" refers to a long, slender, limbless invertebrate.  The combined term thus describes the larval stage of certain beetles that resemble worms.
