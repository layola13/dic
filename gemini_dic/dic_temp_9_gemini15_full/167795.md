primes:/praɪmz/| n.|质数，素数|A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that is not a product of two smaller natural numbers.  A natural number greater than 1 that is not prime is called a composite number.|例句：2, 3, 5, and 7 are prime numbers. 2, 3, 5, 和 7 是质数。|例句：Finding large prime numbers is crucial for cryptography. 寻找大质数对于密码学至关重要。|近义词：prime number|反义词：composite number|名词
