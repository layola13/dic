dispel:/dɪˈspɛl/ | vt. |驱散，消除，打消 | To drive away or cause to vanish; to clear away (something, especially a feeling or belief) |例句：The sun dispelled the morning mist.  阳光驱散了早晨的薄雾。

例句：Her explanation dispelled my doubts.  她的解释打消了我的疑虑。


近义词：disperse, dissipate, banish, eliminate | 反义词：gather, accumulate, foster, encourage | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun dispels the fog every morning. = 太阳每天早晨驱散雾霾。
He dispels rumors with facts. = 他用事实来消除谣言。

一般过去时:
The wind dispelled the clouds. = 风驱散了云彩。
She dispelled his fears with a reassuring smile. = 她报以令人安心的微笑，打消了他的恐惧。

现在完成时:
The evidence has dispelled any doubts about his guilt. = 证据已经消除了对他有罪的任何怀疑。
The good news has dispelled my anxieties. = 好消息消除了我的焦虑。

过去完成时:
They had dispelled all the rumors before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经消除了一切谣言。
The explanation had dispelled my confusion. = 解释消除了我的困惑。

将来时:
The rain will dispel the heat. = 雨会驱散暑热。
I hope this will dispel any misunderstandings. = 我希望这能消除任何误解。

虚拟语气:
If only I could dispel this feeling of unease. = 但愿我能消除这种不安的感觉。
I wish I could dispel the darkness in my heart. = 我希望我能驱散我心中的黑暗。

被动语态:
The fog was dispelled by the rising sun. = 雾被升起的太阳驱散了。
His fears were dispelled by her kind words. = 他的恐惧被她友好的话语打消了。

疑问句:
Can we dispel these misunderstandings? = 我们能消除这些误解吗？
Will this medicine dispel the pain? = 这药能止痛吗？

否定句:
Nothing could dispel her sadness. = 什么也无法驱散她的悲伤。
He couldn't dispel the feeling that something was wrong. = 他无法摆脱有什么不对劲的感觉。

词源:
dispel 源自古法语 despeler，其词根是拉丁语的 dis- (分开，除去) + pellere (推，驱赶)。  因此，dispel 字面意思是“把…推开”。
