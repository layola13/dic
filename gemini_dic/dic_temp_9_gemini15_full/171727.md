pump-priming:/pʌmpˈpraɪmɪŋ/| n. | 刺激经济；启动资金；起动性投资 | The act of stimulating an economy, typically by government spending, to encourage further investment and growth.  It's based on the analogy of priming a pump, where a small amount of liquid is initially added to get the pump working efficiently.  | Example: The government implemented a pump-priming program to boost the struggling construction industry.  政府实施了一项刺激计划来提振苦苦挣扎的建筑业。

|近义词：stimulus, boost, injection, kick-start |反义词：austerity, retrenchment |名词
