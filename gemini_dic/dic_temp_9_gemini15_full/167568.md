prewhipped:/priːˈwɪpt/| adj.| 预先搅打好的；预先打发的 |  Already whipped or beaten, typically referring to ingredients like cream or topping. This term is commonly used in recipes and food descriptions to indicate that an ingredient has been whipped beforehand, saving time and effort in the cooking process. | Example: The prewhipped cream made the cake decorating much easier.  预先打发的奶油让蛋糕装饰更容易了。|近义词：pre-beaten, pre-frothed |反义词：unwhipped
