slitters:/ˈslɪtərz/| n.|裂缝制造者；切割机|A person or thing that slits or makes slits.  The term often refers to machines used for cutting or slitting materials.|例句：The factory uses high-speed slitters to cut rolls of paper into smaller widths. 工厂使用高速切割机将纸卷切割成更窄的宽度。  The sharp slitters sliced through the fabric effortlessly.  锋利的切割机毫不费力地切开了织物。|近义词：cutter, slicer, divider|反义词：joiner, mender
