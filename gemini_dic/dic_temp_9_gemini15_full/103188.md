jesuitically:/ˌdʒɛzjuˈɪtɪkli/| adv.|耶稣会式地；狡猾地；诡辩地|In a manner characteristic of the Jesuits; characterized by cleverness or cunning, especially in matters of religion or morality; sophistically.|例句：He argued jesuitically, twisting the facts to suit his purposes. 他诡辩地争论，歪曲事实以符合他的目的。 He answered the question jesuitically, avoiding a direct answer. 他耶稣会式地回答了这个问题，避免了直接回答。|近义词：cunningly, slyly, subtly, sophistically|反义词：honestly, frankly, straightforwardly, openly
