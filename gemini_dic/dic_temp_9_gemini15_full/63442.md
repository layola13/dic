entreats:/ɪnˈtriːts/| v.|恳求；哀求|To ask someone earnestly or beg for something.|例句：She entreated him to stay. 她恳求他留下。|例句：He entreated mercy from the judge. 他向法官恳求宽大处理。|近义词：beg, implore, plead, supplicate|反义词：demand, command, order


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He entreats his father for forgiveness. = 他恳求父亲原谅他。
She entreats her friends for help. = 她恳求朋友们帮助她。


一般过去时:
They entreated the king for clemency. = 他们恳求国王开恩。
He entreated her to reconsider her decision. = 他恳求她重新考虑她的决定。


现在完成时:
She has entreated him repeatedly, but he refuses. = 她一再恳求他，但他拒绝了。
They have entreated the authorities for assistance. = 他们一直在恳求当局提供援助。


过去完成时:
He had entreated her many times before she finally agreed. = 在她最终同意之前，他已经多次恳求她了。
They had entreated the gods for a good harvest. = 他们曾恳求神明赐予丰收。


将来时:
I will entreat him to change his mind. = 我将恳求他改变主意。
She will entreat her boss for a raise. = 她将恳求老板加薪。


虚拟语气:
I would entreat you to reconsider if I were in your place. = 如果我是你，我会恳求你重新考虑。
If I were you, I would entreat them for help. = 如果我是你，我会恳求他们帮忙。


被动语态:
He was entreated to participate in the project. = 他被恳求参与这个项目。
The king was entreated by his people for peace. = 国王被他的子民恳求和平。


疑问句:
Will you entreat him for me? = 你会为我恳求他吗？
Did she entreat him to leave? = 她恳求他离开了吗？


否定句:
He did not entreat her for forgiveness. = 他没有恳求她原谅。
She did not entreat them for help. = 她没有恳求他们帮忙。


PHRASE->
entreat earnestly = 恳切地恳求
entreat humbly = 卑微地恳求
entreat for mercy = 恳求怜悯
entreat with tears = 哭着恳求
entreat someone to do something = 恳求某人做某事
