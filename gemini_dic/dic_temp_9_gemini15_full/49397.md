deliver:/dɪˈlɪvər/| vt. n.|递送；交付；发表；履行；分娩|to take (something) to a person or place; to give a speech or performance; to bring about (a result) or to achieve (a promise); to give birth to a baby |例句：The postman delivered the letter this morning. 邮递员今天早上送来了信。

例句：She delivered a powerful speech at the conference.她在会议上发表了一篇精彩的演讲。


近义词：convey, transmit, present, fulfill, accomplish, give birth to |反义词：receive, accept, fail, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The courier delivers packages every day. = 快递员每天送包裹。
She delivers speeches with great confidence. = 她自信地发表演讲。


一般过去时:
He delivered the goods on time. = 他准时送达了货物。
The doctor delivered the baby safely. = 医生安全地接生了婴儿。


现在完成时:
They have delivered all the orders. = 他们已经送达了所有订单。
She has delivered many inspiring speeches. = 她已经发表了许多鼓舞人心的演讲。


过去完成时:
The company had delivered the product before the deadline. = 公司在截止日期前已交付产品。
She had delivered her baby before the ambulance arrived. = 救护车到达之前，她已经生下了孩子。


将来时:
We will deliver the furniture tomorrow. = 我们明天送家具。
He will deliver a keynote speech at the summit. = 他将在峰会上发表主题演讲。


虚拟语气:
If he delivered the speech, he would receive recognition. = 如果他发表演讲，他将获得认可。
If she had delivered the baby at home, it would have been risky. = 如果她在家里生孩子，那将很危险。


被动语态:
The package was delivered yesterday. = 包裹昨天送到了。
The speech was delivered flawlessly. = 演讲完美无缺地完成了。


疑问句:
When will the goods be delivered? = 货物什么时候送达？
Who delivered the message? = 谁传达了这个信息？


否定句:
He didn't deliver the promise. = 他没有履行承诺。
She didn't deliver the baby herself. = 她没有自己生孩子。
