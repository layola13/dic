nonvigilantness:/ˌnɒnˈvɪdʒɪləntnəs/| n. |疏忽；不警惕；失察|The lack of vigilance; a state of being unwary or inattentive.  It often leads to unforeseen consequences or mistakes.|例句：The accident was a direct result of the driver's nonvigilantness.  事故是司机疏忽大意的直接结果。

例句：Their nonvigilantness allowed the thief to escape unnoticed. 他们的疏忽大意让小偷溜之大吉。|近义词：negligence, carelessness, inattention, heedlessness|反义词：vigilance, attentiveness, watchfulness, carefulness|词性：名词
