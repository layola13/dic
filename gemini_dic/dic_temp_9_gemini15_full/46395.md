daemonic:/dɑɪˈmɒnɪk/ /ˌdiːməˈnɪk/| adj. |魔鬼般的；恶魔似的；邪恶的 |Relating to or characteristic of a demon or demons; fiendish; evil.  The word often implies a powerful, almost supernatural, evil influence, rather than simply wickedness. |例句：The painting had a daemonic energy, a sense of terrifying power emanating from the canvas.  这幅画有一种恶魔般的力量，从画布上散发出一股令人恐惧的力量。

The novel's protagonist is driven by daemonic forces beyond his control.  小说的主人公被其无法控制的恶魔般的力量驱使着。
