imitate:/ˈɪmɪteɪt/| vt.|模仿，仿效|To copy someone or something's behavior or appearance, often in order to entertain or make fun of them.  It can also refer to reproducing something exactly.|例句：The child imitated his father's walk.  孩子模仿他父亲的走路姿势。

例句：She tried to imitate the singer's voice. 她试图模仿那位歌手的声音。


近义词：copy, mimic, simulate, reproduce|反义词：originate, innovate, create|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imitates his teacher's accent. 他模仿老师的口音。
Birds imitate the sounds they hear. 鸟类模仿它们听到的声音。


一般过去时:
The comedian imitated famous politicians. 喜剧演员模仿了著名的政治家。
She imitated her friend's hairstyle. 她模仿了她朋友的发型。


现在完成时:
He has imitated many famous artists. 他已经模仿了许多著名的艺术家。
She has imitated that style for years. 她多年来一直模仿那种风格。


过去完成时:
The student had imitated the teacher's method before the exam. 学生在考试前模仿了老师的方法。
They had imitated the design for their project. 他们为他们的项目模仿了这个设计。


将来时:
She will imitate the famous dancer's moves. 她将模仿这位著名舞蹈家的动作。
He will imitate the sound of a bird. 他将模仿鸟叫声。


虚拟语气:
If I could imitate her singing, I would be famous. 如果我能模仿她的歌声，我就会出名。
I wish I could imitate his painting style. 我真希望我能模仿他的绘画风格。


被动语态:
His style was imitated by many artists. 他的风格被许多艺术家模仿。
The painting was imitated poorly. 这幅画模仿得很糟糕。


疑问句:
Does she imitate anyone famous? 她模仿过什么名人吗？
Who are you imitating? 你在模仿谁？


否定句:
He doesn't imitate others easily. 他不容易模仿别人。
She didn't imitate the painting accurately. 她没有准确地模仿这幅画。
