decant:/dɪˈkænt/ | vt. | 倒出；倾注；（尤指小心地）将…倒入另一个容器 | To pour a liquid gently from one container into another, especially without disturbing the sediment. | 例句：He decanted the wine into a decanter. 他小心地将酒倒入酒壶。 | 例句：The chemist decanted the solution into a clean beaker. 化学家将溶液小心地倒入一个干净的烧杯中。 | 近义词：pour, transfer | 反义词：retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He decants the wine every evening. = 他每天晚上都倒酒。
She decants the perfume into a smaller bottle. = 她把香水倒进一个小瓶子里。

一般过去时:
They decanted the old wine last night. = 他们昨晚倒了陈年老酒。
He carefully decanted the sediment from the bottle. = 他小心地将瓶中的沉淀物倒掉。

现在完成时:
I have decanted the red wine for dinner. = 我已经把红酒倒好准备晚餐了。
She has decanted the essential oil into a dropper bottle. = 她已经将精油倒入滴瓶中了。

过去完成时:
We had decanted the champagne before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我们已经把香槟倒好了。
The scientist had decanted the sample before running the test. = 科学家在进行测试之前已经将样品倒出来了。

将来时:
I will decant the wine when the guests arrive. = 客人到达时，我将倒酒。
She will decant the liquid into a graduated cylinder. = 她将把液体倒入量筒中。

虚拟语气:
If I had a decanter, I would decant the wine. = 如果我有一个酒壶，我会把酒倒进去。
I wish I had decanted the wine earlier. = 我真希望我早点把酒倒好。

被动语态:
The wine was decanted by the sommelier. = 酒是由侍酒师倒的。
The chemicals were carefully decanted into separate containers. = 化学药品被小心地倒入单独的容器中。

疑问句:
Have you decanted the wine yet? = 你已经倒酒了吗？
Will the liquid be decanted before the experiment? = 实验前液体会被倒出来吗？

否定句:
He didn't decant the wine before serving it. = 他上菜前没有倒酒。
She hasn't yet decanted the essential oils. = 她还没有倒精油。
