movability:/ˌmuːvəˈbɪləti/| n. |可移动性，活动性 |The ability to be moved; the state of being movable.  It refers to the ease with which something can be moved or transported.  This can apply to physical objects, as well as to more abstract concepts like ideas or social groups. |例句：The movability of the furniture made rearranging the room easy.  家具的可移动性使得重新布置房间变得容易。

The high movability of the workforce is a key factor in the company's success.  劳动力的高度流动性是公司成功的关键因素。|近义词：mobility, portability, transportability |反义词：immobility, fixity, stability
