eraser:/ɪˈreɪzər/| n.|橡皮擦|A tool used to remove markings made by pencil or pen.|例句：I need an eraser to correct this mistake. 我需要一块橡皮擦来改正这个错误。|近义词：rubber (BrE)|反义词:None


USAGE->

He uses an eraser daily. = 他每天都用橡皮擦。
