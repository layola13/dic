cafetiere:/ˌkæfɪˈtɛər/|/n.|法压壶；咖啡壶|A cafetière is a type of coffee maker that uses pressure to brew coffee.  It typically consists of a glass or metal container with a plunger that is used to separate the coffee grounds from the brewed coffee.|例句：I prefer using a cafetière to make my coffee in the morning. = 我更喜欢用法压壶在早上冲咖啡。|例句：She bought a stylish cafetière as a housewarming gift. = 她买了一个时尚的法压壶作为乔迁礼物。|近义词：French press, coffee press|反义词：drip coffee maker, percolator
