It combines "pre-" (before) and "confided" (entrusted with a secret). | 例句：The preconfided information proved crucial to the success of the mission.  预先告知的信息对任务的成功至关重要。


The information, preconfided to select individuals, remained secret until the official announcement. 预先告知少数人的信息直到官方宣布才公开。


近义词：preinformed, forewarned, previously told | 反义词：uninformed, kept secret, undisclosed | 词性:形容词
