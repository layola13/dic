Centaurs are often depicted as wild and untamed, but some, like Chiron, were wise and skilled teachers. | The centaur galloped across the field.  这匹人马在田野上飞奔。  The statue depicted a centaur battling a lion.  这尊雕像描绘了一名人马与狮子搏斗。|  mythical creature, hybrid |  human (in contrast to the horse aspect)| 名词
