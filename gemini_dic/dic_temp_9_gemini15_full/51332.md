detectable:/dɪˈtɛktəbəl/ | adj. | 可探测的，可察觉的 | Able to be detected or discovered.  Something that is detectable can be found or noticed, often using special tools or methods. | 例句：The leak was barely detectable.  泄漏几乎难以察觉。 The disease is detectable in its early stages.  这种疾病在早期就能检测出来。| 近义词：discoverable, perceivable, noticeable | 反义词：undetectable, imperceptible, indiscernible

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem is detectable with advanced equipment. = 用先进设备可以探测到这个问题。
The scent is detectable even from a distance. =  即使在远处也能闻到这种气味。


一般过去时:
The flaw was detectable only under a microscope. =  只有在显微镜下才能发现这个缺陷。
The signal was detectable throughout the experiment. = 在整个实验过程中都能检测到信号。


现在完成时:
The leak has been detectable for several weeks. = 泄漏已经可以检测到好几个星期了。
The change has been detectable since the new software was installed. = 自从安装了新软件以来，变化就已可以察觉。


过去完成时:
The damage had been detectable before the accident. =  事故发生前就已经可以发现损坏。
The problem had been detectable, but it was ignored. = 这个问题是可以检测到的，但是却被忽略了。


将来时:
The fault will be detectable once the machine is disassembled. = 一旦机器被拆卸，故障就能被检测到。
The effect will be detectable after a few hours. =  几个小时后就能检测到效果。


虚拟语气:
If the defect were detectable earlier, the problem would have been prevented. = 如果缺陷能够早些被发现，问题就可以避免了。
If the signal were more detectable, the data would be more reliable. = 如果信号更容易被检测到，数据就会更可靠。


被动语态:
The contamination was easily detectable. = 污染很容易被检测到。
The changes in the data were detectable by the new algorithm. = 新算法可以检测到数据中的变化。


疑问句:
Is the signal detectable? = 信号可以检测到吗？
Is the error detectable in this system? = 在这个系统中，错误可以检测到吗？


否定句:
The changes were not detectable to the naked eye. = 这些变化肉眼无法察觉。
The radiation was not detectable at that distance. = 在那个距离上，辐射是检测不到的。


PHRASE->
