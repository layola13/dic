osteotomy:/ɒsˈtiɒtəmi/| n. |骨切除术|The surgical operation of cutting a bone.  Osteotomy is a surgical procedure that involves cutting a bone to correct a deformity, improve alignment, or treat a disease.  |Example: The surgeon performed an osteotomy to correct the patient's leg length discrepancy.  外科医生进行了骨切除术来矫正患者的腿长差异。

Example:  The osteotomy was successful in relieving the pressure on the nerve. 骨切除术成功地缓解了神经的压力。


近义词: bone cutting, bone resection |反义词: bone fusion, bone grafting
