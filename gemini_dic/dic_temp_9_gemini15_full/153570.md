pentail:/ˈpɛnteɪl/| n.|山椒鸟属|A pentail is a bird belonging to the family of birds called  the  Dicaeidae,  commonly known as flowerpeckers.  They are small passerine birds characterized by their long, slender tails.  Many species have brightly colored plumage.|The crimson-breasted flowerpecker (Dicaeum ignipectus) is a species of pentail found in Southeast Asia.|近义词:flowerpecker|反义词:无
