magnetosphere:/ˈmæɡnɪtəˌsfɪər/| n. |磁层|The region surrounding a planet, especially the Earth, in which its magnetic field dominates the interplanetary magnetic field.  It is a zone of charged particles trapped by the planet's magnetic field, protecting the planet from solar wind and cosmic rays.|例句：The Earth's magnetosphere protects us from harmful solar radiation. 地球的磁层保护我们免受有害的太阳辐射。

The magnetosphere deflects most of the solar wind, preventing it from stripping away Earth's atmosphere. 磁层偏转了大部分太阳风，防止它剥夺地球的大气层。|近义词：magnetoionic region|反义词：None.
