flexer:/ˈflɛksər/| n. |弯曲者；屈肌|A flexer is a muscle that bends a limb or other part of the body.  It is often used in anatomy to describe muscles that decrease the angle between two bones.|The biceps brachii is a powerful flexer of the elbow.|近义词：bender,  flexor|反义词：extensor
