iriscope:/ˈaɪrɪskoʊp/| n. |虹膜诊断仪|An iriscope is a device used in iridology, a pseudoscientific practice, to examine the iris of the eye and supposedly diagnose health conditions based on its appearance.  The instrument typically uses magnification to allow for a detailed view of the iris.|Example: The iridologist used an iriscope to examine the patient's eyes.|近义词：iridology instrument (though iridology itself is not a scientifically valid practice)|反义词：无
