meiotically:/maɪəʊˈtɪkli/| adj. | 減數分裂地；通過減數分裂地 | Relating to or characteristic of meiosis.  The process of meiosis reduces the number of chromosomes in a cell by half, creating gametes (sex cells). | Example: Meiotically produced gametes are haploid.  減數分裂產生的配子是單倍體的。 The organism reproduces meiotically.  該生物體通過減數分裂繁殖。|近义词：reductionally |反义词：mitotically
