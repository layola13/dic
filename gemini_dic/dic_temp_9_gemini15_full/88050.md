highest: /ˈhaɪɪst/ | adj. | 最高的；最重要的 |  The word "highest" is the superlative form of the adjective "high," indicating the greatest degree or extent of height, importance, or rank.  It describes something that surpasses all others in a particular quality. |例句：The highest mountain in the world is Mount Everest. 世界上最高的山是珠穆朗玛峰。

He achieved the highest honor in his field. 他在他的领域获得了最高的荣誉。


近义词：topmost, supreme, paramount, ultimate | 反义词：lowest, bottom, least


词源:  Old English *hēah, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch hoog and German hoch.  The superlative suffix "-est" is a common Old English inflectional ending.
