double-book:/ˈdʌbəl bʊk/| vt.|双重预订；重复预订|To make a double booking; to book something twice, often unintentionally.  This usually results in a conflict or scheduling problem.|例句：I accidentally double-booked myself for two meetings at the same time. 我不小心把自己同时预订了两次会议。|例句：The restaurant double-booked our reservation, and we had to wait for a table. 餐厅把我们的预订重复预订了，我们不得不等位。|近义词：overbook, rebook|反义词：cancel,unbook|动词
