misasserts:/ˌmɪsæˈsɜːrts/ | vt. |错误地断言；误称|To state something incorrectly or falsely; to misrepresent.|例句：He misasserts the facts in his article. 他在他的文章中错误地陈述了事实。|例句：The witness misasserted the suspect's identity. 证人错误地指认了嫌疑人的身份。|近义词：misrepresent, misstate, falsify|反义词：assert correctly, state accurately
