postcommunist:/ˌpoʊstˈkɒmjʊnɪst/|/ˌpoʊstˈkɑːmjʊnɪst/| adj.|后共产主义的；后共产主义时代的|Relating to or denoting the period or countries following the collapse of communist regimes.  The term often implies a complex transition characterized by economic restructuring, political reforms, and social upheaval.|例句：Many postcommunist countries are struggling with economic instability. 许多后共产主义国家都在努力应对经济不稳定。|例句：The study focuses on the social impact of postcommunist transformations in Eastern Europe. 这项研究侧重于东欧后共产主义转型对社会的影响。|近义词：post-Soviet, former communist|反义词：communist
