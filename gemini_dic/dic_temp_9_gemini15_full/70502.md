filcher:/ˈfɪltʃər/| vt.|偷窃（少量东西）；窃取|To steal (something, especially something of little value).  It implies a petty theft, often surreptitiously.|例句：He filched a cookie from the jar. 他从罐子里偷了一块饼干。


例句：She filched a glance at the exam paper. 她偷偷看了一眼试卷。


近义词：steal, pilfer, purloin, swipe


反义词：give, donate, bestow


词性：动词


词源：源自古英语 *filcan，意为“欺骗，偷窃”。
