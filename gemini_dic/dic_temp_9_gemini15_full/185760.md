reviewed:/rɪˈvjuːd/ | v. | 审查；评论；复习 | To examine or consider something again or in detail, often to assess its quality, accuracy, or completeness.  |例句：The committee reviewed the proposal carefully before making a decision.  委员会在做出决定之前仔细审查了该提案。

例句： I reviewed my notes before the exam. 我在考试前复习了笔记。


近义词：examined, inspected, assessed, considered, appraised | 反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reviews his work daily. 他每天都检查他的工作。
She reviews her lessons regularly. 她定期复习功课。

一般过去时:
They reviewed the film last night. 他们昨晚观看了这部电影。
The teacher reviewed the material with the students. 老师和学生们一起复习了材料。

现在完成时:
I have reviewed the document three times. 我已经审查了这份文件三次。
She has reviewed all the applications. 她已经审查了所有的申请。

过去完成时:
He had reviewed the contract before signing it. 他在签署合同之前已经审查过合同了。
They had reviewed the data multiple times before presenting their findings. 他们在展示他们的发现之前已经多次审查了数据。

将来时:
We will review the project next week. 我们下周将审查该项目。
They will review the evidence before the trial. 他们将在审判前审查证据。

虚拟语气:
If I had reviewed the material, I would have done better on the test. 如果我复习了材料，考试成绩会更好。
It would be helpful if he reviewed his work more carefully. 如果他更仔细地检查他的工作，会有帮助。

被动语态:
The proposal was reviewed by the committee. 该提案由委员会审查。
The manuscript is being reviewed by the editor. 手稿正在由编辑审查。

疑问句:
Have you reviewed the latest data? 你审查了最新的数据了吗？
Will they review the decision again? 他们会再次审查这个决定吗？

否定句:
He did not review his notes before the test. 他考试前没有复习笔记。
They haven't reviewed the contract yet. 他们还没有审查合同。
