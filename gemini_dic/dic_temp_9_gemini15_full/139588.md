obviously:/ˈɑːbviəsli/ | adv. | 明显地，显然地 |Clearly or evidently.  It is easily perceived or understood.|The answer is obviously wrong. 答案显然是错的。
It's obviously a fake.  这显然是假的。|clearly, evidently, plainly, apparently |unobviously, subtly, obscurely|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is obviously a mistake. = 这显然是个错误。
The solution is obviously the best choice. =  这个方案显然是最佳选择。


一般过去时:
It was obviously a difficult situation. =  情况显然很困难。
The results were obviously disappointing. = 结果显然令人失望。


现在完成时:
It has obviously been a long day. =  显然已经很漫长的一天了。
She has obviously improved her skills. = 她显然提高了她的技能。


过去完成时:
It had obviously rained heavily. = 显然下过大雨。
They had obviously miscalculated. = 他们显然计算错误了。


将来时:
It will obviously be a challenge. = 这显然将是一个挑战。
The project will obviously take time. =  这个项目显然需要时间。


虚拟语气:
If it were obviously wrong, I wouldn't do it. = 如果它显然是错的，我不会去做。
I wish it were obviously easier. = 我希望它显然更容易一些。


被动语态:
The error was obviously overlooked. =  这个错误显然被忽略了。
The problem is obviously being addressed. = 这个问题显然正在得到解决。


疑问句:
Isn't it obviously a problem? = 这不是显然是个问题吗？
Is it obviously the right thing to do? = 这显然是正确的事情吗？


否定句:
It is not obviously true. = 这并非显然是真的。
The connection isn't obviously clear. =  联系并非显然清晰。
