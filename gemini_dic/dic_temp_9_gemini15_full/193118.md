secularize:/ˈsɛkjʊləraɪz/ | vt. | 使世俗化；取消教会的管辖 | To remove (something, such as property or a church office) from ecclesiastical or religious control; to make worldly or temporal.  |例句：The government secularized the education system. = 政府使教育体系世俗化。  The monastery was secularized in the 16th century. = 这座修道院在16世纪被世俗化了。 | 近义词：desacralize, profane, worldly | 反义词：sacralize, sanctify | 动词

词源:
