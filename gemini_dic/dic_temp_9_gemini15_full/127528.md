necromancers:/ˈnɛkrəˌmænsərz/| n. |死灵法师；招魂术士|Practitioners of necromancy, the art of communicating with the dead or raising them from the dead.  They are often depicted in fiction as dark and powerful figures.|Example: The necromancers summoned a terrifying army of skeletons to fight for them. 死灵法师召唤了一支可怕的骷髅大军为他们作战。|近义词：conjurers, sorcerers, wizards|反义词：healers, clerics
