sainfoin:/ˈseɪnfoɪn/| n. |圣福因,多年生豆科牧草|A plant of the pea family, cultivated as fodder.  It is a valuable forage crop, especially in drier climates, known for its deep root system and tolerance of drought.  |Sainfoin is a hardy plant that thrives in poor soil conditions.|近义词：clover, alfalfa|反义词：无
