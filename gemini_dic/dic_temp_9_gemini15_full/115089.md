mangabey:/ˈmæŋɡeɪbiː/| n. | 猕猴属动物 | A mangabey is any of several species of Old World monkeys belonging to the genus *Lophocebus*.  They are characterized by their distinctive crests of hair on their heads. | Example:  The researchers observed a group of black mangabeys foraging in the rainforest.  研究人员观察到一群黑冠猕猴在雨林中觅食。


|近义词: |  monkey (general term), primate |
|反义词: | None (no direct antonym) |
