predelinquent:/ˌpriːdɪˈlɪŋkwənt/| adj.|  准违规的；有违规倾向的；潜在违规的 |  Referring to behavior or a person that shows signs of becoming delinquent but hasn't yet committed a delinquent act.  It suggests an early stage or warning sign of future problematic behavior. | Example: The predelinquent teenager's truancy and aggressive behavior worried his parents.  这个有违规倾向的青少年的逃学和侵略性行为让他的父母很担心。|近义词：delinquent-prone, at-risk, potential offender |反义词：law-abiding, well-behaved
