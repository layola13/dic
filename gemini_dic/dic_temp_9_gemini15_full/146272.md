overfaithfully:/ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfəli/| adj.|过分忠诚地；过于忠实地|Excessively or blindly faithful; showing excessive loyalty or devotion.  It implies a level of faithfulness that is potentially detrimental or unreasonable.|例句：He followed the instructions overfaithfully, resulting in a flawed outcome. 他过于忠实地遵循指示，导致结果有缺陷。 He overfaithfully believed his friend's lies. 他过分相信朋友的谎言。|近义词： excessively loyal, blindly devoted, slavishly faithful |反义词：disloyal, unfaithful, skeptical
