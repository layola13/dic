词源:
The word "fettuccine" is derived from the Italian word "fettuccia," meaning "little ribbon."  It's a diminutive form of "fetta," meaning "slice."  Therefore, fettuccine literally translates to "little ribbons" or "small slices," describing the shape of the pasta.
