frame-ups:/ˈfreɪmʌps/| n.|栽赃陷害，嫁祸|The act of falsely accusing someone of a crime or wrongdoing; a plot to incriminate someone.|例句：The police uncovered a frame-up designed to implicate the mayor in a bribery scandal.  警方揭露了一起栽赃陷害，目的是将市长牵扯进贿赂丑闻。|例句：He was the victim of a frame-up, and spent years in prison for a crime he didn't commit. 他是栽赃陷害的受害者，并因此犯下他没有犯下的罪行而坐了多年的牢。|近义词：set-up, conspiracy, plot|反义词：exoneration, vindication|名词
