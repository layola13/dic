messaged:/ˈmɛsɪdʒd/| vt.|发送信息，发短信|To send a message to someone, typically via electronic means.|I messaged him on WhatsApp to confirm the meeting time. 我通过WhatsApp给他发消息确认会议时间。|texted, emailed, contacted|received a message, ignored a message|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I message my friends every day. = 我每天都给我朋友发信息。
He messages his boss regularly. = 他定期给他老板发信息。


一般过去时:
I messaged him yesterday. = 我昨天给他发信息了。
She messaged her parents about her arrival. = 她给她父母发了信息，告诉他们她到了。


现在完成时:
I have messaged him twice today. = 我今天已经给他发了两次信息了。
She has messaged me several times this week. = 她本周已经给我发了好几次信息了。


过去完成时:
I had messaged him before he called. = 我在他打电话之前已经给他发过信息了。
She had messaged her friend before leaving the house. = 她离开家之前已经给她朋友发过信息了。


将来时:
I will message you when I arrive. = 我到了会给你发信息。
He will message her as soon as he gets the news. = 他一得到消息就会给她发信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had his number, I would message him. = 如果我有他的号码，我会给他发信息。
I wish I had messaged her sooner. = 我真希望我早点给她发信息。


被动语态:
He was messaged by his colleague. = 他收到了同事发来的信息。（他被同事发信息了）
The information was messaged to the relevant parties. = 信息已发送给相关人员。（信息被发送给相关人员了）


疑问句:
Did you message him? = 你给他发信息了吗？
Has she messaged you back yet? = 她回复你信息了吗？


否定句:
I didn't message him. = 我没有给他发信息。
She hasn't messaged me yet. = 她还没给我发信息。


PHRASE->
message someone back  回复某人信息
message someone again 再次给某人发信息
leave a message  留言
direct message  私信
text message 短信
