outlive:/ˌaʊtˈlɪv/| vt.|比…活得长；比…寿命长；经历…之后依然存在|To live longer than (someone or something); to survive (someone or something).|例句：He outlived his wife by ten years. 他比妻子多活了十年。

例句：The ancient castle outlived many wars and still stands today. 这座古老的城堡经历了许多战争之后仍然屹立至今。

近义词：survive, outlast

反义词：predecease, die before


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The oak tree outlives the surrounding shrubs.  = 这棵橡树比周围的灌木活得长。
She outlives all her rivals in the business world. =她在商界比所有竞争对手活得久。


一般过去时:
My grandmother outlived all her siblings. = 我祖母比她所有兄弟姐妹都活得长。
The old building outlived several owners. = 那座旧建筑经历了几个业主。


现在完成时:
This company has outlived many economic downturns. = 这家公司已经经历过多次经济衰退。
The tradition has outlived generations. = 这个传统已经延续了几代人。


过去完成时:
The empire had outlived its glory days. = 这个帝国已经过了它辉煌的时期。
He had outlived his reputation. = 他的名声已经不再了。


将来时:
Scientists predict that this species will outlive the next century. = 科学家预测这个物种将存活到下个世纪。
I hope to outlive my enemies. = 我希望比我的敌人活得久。


虚拟语气:
If only I could outlive my current health problems! = 但愿我能战胜我目前的健康问题!
I wish I could outlive this difficult period. = 我希望我能度过这段艰难时期。


被动语态:
The old house was outlived by its inhabitants.  =  这座老房子比它的住户存在的时间短。
His legacy will be outlived by his actions. = 他的遗产将被他的行为所超越。


疑问句:
Will this technology outlive its usefulness? = 这项技术能持续多久？
Does the company think it can outlive the crisis? = 公司认为它能挺过危机吗？


否定句:
The old regime didn't outlive the revolution. = 旧政权没有熬过革命。
Unfortunately, he didn't outlive his illness. = 不幸的是，他没有战胜他的疾病。
