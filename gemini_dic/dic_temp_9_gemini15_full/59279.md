eagle-eyes: /ˈiːɡəl aɪz/ | adj. | 鹰眼般的；目光锐利的；观察力敏锐的 | Having exceptionally keen eyesight or observation skills; very observant.  |例句：With his eagle eyes, he spotted the mistake in the document.  凭借他敏锐的目光，他发现了文件中的错误。

例句：The eagle-eyed security guard noticed the suspicious package.  目光敏锐的保安注意到了可疑包裹。


近义词：sharp-eyed, keen-eyed, observant, perspicacious, vigilant

反义词：blind, unobservant, inattentive, careless


词源:  The term is a compound adjective formed from "eagle," referring to the bird known for its exceptional eyesight, and "eyes," the organs of sight.  It's a figurative expression, applying the eagle's keen vision to a person's observational abilities.
