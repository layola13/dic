clemency:/ˈklɛmənsɪ/ | n. | 宽大；仁慈；慈悲；赦免 |  Leniency or mercy shown toward someone, especially by someone in authority.  It often refers to a judge's decision to show leniency in sentencing a criminal. |

例句：The judge showed clemency, reducing the defendant's sentence.  法官表现出宽大，减轻了被告的刑罚。

例句：The prisoner appealed for clemency. 囚犯请求宽大处理。


近义词：mercy, leniency, pardon, forgiveness, compassion

反义词：severity, harshness, cruelty, rigor


词源：源自于古法语 *clemence，最终源于拉丁语 *clemens，意思是“温和的，仁慈的”。
