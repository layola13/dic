imbalanced:/ɪmˈbælənst/| adj.|不平衡的，失衡的|lacking balance or equilibrium; uneven.|The imbalanced distribution of resources led to social unrest. 资源分配不均导致社会动荡。|近义词：uneven, disproportionate, unequal|反义词：balanced, even, equitable
