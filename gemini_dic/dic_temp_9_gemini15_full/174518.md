quinquina:/kɪnˈkiːnə/| n. |金鸡纳树，金鸡纳霜|A quinquina is the bark of the cinchona tree, which contains quinine and is used to make medicine.  The word is also sometimes used to refer to the medicine itself, particularly a tonic made from it. |Example:  The explorer relied on quinquina to prevent malaria during his journey through the Amazon.  探险家依靠金鸡纳霜来预防他在亚马逊河流域旅行期间感染疟疾。|近义词：cinchona bark|反义词：N/A
