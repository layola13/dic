fourths:/fɔːrθs/| n.|四分之一；四分之一(复数)|The word "fourths" is the plural form of "fourth," referring to one of four equal parts of a whole.  It can also refer to the ordinal number fourth, when discussing multiple instances of something being in fourth place.|例句：She cut the cake into fourths.  她把蛋糕切成了四块。

The pizza was divided into fourths, and each person got a fourth. 比萨饼被分成四块，每人得到四分之一。|近义词：quarters (if referring to divisions)|反义词：wholes|名词
