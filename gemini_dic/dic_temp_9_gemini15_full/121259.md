misevaluate:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljuˌeɪt/| vt.| 误评，错误评估 | To judge the value of something incorrectly.  |例句：The company misevaluated the market demand, leading to significant losses. 公司错误评估了市场需求，导致了巨大的损失。
The appraiser misevaluated the antique chair, significantly undervaluing it. 评估师错误评估了这把古董椅，大大低估了它的价值。|近义词：misjudge, misassess, undervalue, overvalue |反义词：evaluate accurately, assess correctly
