melicotoon:/ˌmɛlɪˈkoʊtuːn/| n.|梅李子，李子蜜饯|A small, round, yellowish-orange fruit, similar to a plum, but with a more tart flavor.  It is also known as a Spanish lime.  The fruit is often used in preserves and jams.|例句：The melicotoons were tart but delicious in the jam. 梅李子在果酱里虽然酸但很好吃。|例句： We bought some melicotoons at the farmers market.我们在农贸市场买了些梅李子。|近义词：plum, lime|反义词：无|名词
