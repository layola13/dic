fasciole:/ˈfæsiəl/ | n. |小册子；束；肌束 | A small booklet or pamphlet; a bundle or cluster; a fascicle (in anatomy, a small bundle of nerve or muscle fibers). |例句：The professor distributed a fasciole summarizing the key concepts. 教授分发了一本小册子，总结了关键概念。
例句：The muscle fibers were arranged in distinct fascioles. 肌纤维排列成明显的肌束。|近义词：pamphlet, booklet, bundle, cluster |反义词：volume (in the sense of a large book)
