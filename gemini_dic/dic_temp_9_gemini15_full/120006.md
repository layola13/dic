millimetre: /ˈmɪlɪmiːtər/ | n. |毫米| A unit of length equal to one thousandth of a metre.  It is commonly used in measuring small distances and dimensions. |The screw is 5 millimetres in diameter.  这颗螺丝的直径是5毫米。|近义词： millimeter (US spelling) |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but concepts like kilometre represent a much larger unit)|

词源:
