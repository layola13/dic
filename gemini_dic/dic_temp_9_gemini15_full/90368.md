how the wind blows:/haʊ ðə wɪnd bloʊz/| phrase | 风向; 命运; 变化无常| This phrase refers to the unpredictable nature of events,  similar to the ever-changing direction of the wind. It implies that things are uncertain and subject to change.  It's often used to describe a situation where the outcome is unknown or beyond one's control.|例句：We'll just have to see how the wind blows. 我们只能走着瞧了。
例句：Nobody knows how the wind blows in politics. 没有人知道政坛的风向。|近义词：the way things go, chance, fate|反义词：predictable, certain, controlled|phrase

USAGE->
一般现在时:
How does the wind blow today? = 今天风往哪个方向吹？
How does the situation look? = 情况看起来如何？


一般过去时:
How did the wind blow yesterday? = 昨天风往哪个方向吹？
How did the negotiations go? = 谈判进行得如何？


现在完成时:
How has the wind blown this week? = 这周风向如何？
How has the project progressed? = 项目进展如何？


过去完成时:
How had the wind blown before the storm? = 暴风雨来临之前风向如何？
How had the company performed before the merger? = 合并之前公司业绩如何？


将来时:
How will the wind blow tomorrow? = 明天风往哪个方向吹？
How will the market react? = 市场将如何反应？


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew in a different direction, we might have succeeded. = 如果风向不同，我们也许会成功。
If the circumstances were different, we would have acted differently. = 如果情况不同，我们会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
(This phrase doesn't typically use passive voice.)


疑问句:
How does the wind blow? = 风往哪个方向吹？
How is the situation unfolding? = 情况是如何发展的？


否定句:
I don't know how the wind blows in this industry. = 我不知道这个行业的形势如何。
It's hard to say how things will turn out. = 很难说事情会如何发展。
