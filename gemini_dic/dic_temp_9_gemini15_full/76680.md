galvanography:/ˌɡælˈvænɒɡrəfi/| n. |电刻术，电铸术|A process of producing relief prints or engravings using electricity.  The process involves using an electric current to etch or deposit metal onto a surface, creating a design that can then be used for printing.|The artist used galvanography to create intricate relief prints of flowers. 电刻术被艺术家用来创作出精美的花卉浮雕版画。|electrotyping, electroengraving|  |n.
