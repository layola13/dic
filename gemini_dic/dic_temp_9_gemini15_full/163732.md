It suggests a prior lack or inadequacy that influences subsequent situations or outcomes.  The term implies that these shortcomings are not newly arising but rather pre-existing conditions.  | Example: The project's predeficiencies in planning led to significant delays and cost overruns.  这个项目的规划前期不足导致了严重的延误和成本超支。 | 近义词: prior shortcomings, pre-existing flaws, initial deficiencies | 反义词: advantages, strengths, assets | 名词
