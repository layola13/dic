It's typically smaller than a stovetop and often used in smaller kitchens, laboratories, or for outdoor cooking. | Example:  I used a hotplate to cook dinner while camping. 我用电热板在露营时做饭。  The chemist heated the solution on a hotplate. 化学家用电热板加热溶液。|近义词：electric burner, heating element |反义词：refrigerator, icebox |名词
