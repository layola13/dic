crocoite:/ˈkrɒkəwaɪt/| n. |铬铅矿|A lead chromate mineral, PbCrO₄, that is typically bright red to orange-red in color and is a valuable ore of chromium.  It is often found in crystalline form and is prized by mineral collectors for its vibrant color and attractive crystals.|The crocoite specimen displayed a brilliant, almost fiery red hue.|近义词：lead chromate|反义词：无
