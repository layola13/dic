reallotment:/ˌriːˈlɒtmənt/ | n. | 再分配；重新分配 | The act of allotting or distributing something again; a second allotment.  It often refers to the redistribution of resources, land, or tasks. | Example: The reallotment of funds to the education sector resulted in improved school facilities.  重新分配给教育部门的资金导致学校设施得到改善。  The company's reallotment of work responsibilities led to increased efficiency. 公司重新分配工作职责提高了效率。|近义词：reassignment, redistribution, reallocation | 反义词：concentration, consolidation

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government reallots funds annually. = 政府每年重新分配资金。
The company reallots tasks based on employee skills. = 公司根据员工技能重新分配任务。

一般过去时:
They reallotted the land to the farmers. = 他们把土地重新分配给了农民。
The committee reallotted the budget items. = 委员会重新分配了预算项目。

现在完成时:
The school has reallotted classroom space to accommodate more students. = 学校已经重新分配了教室空间以容纳更多学生。
The project manager has reallotted resources to address unforeseen challenges. = 项目经理已经重新分配了资源以应对不可预见的挑战。

过去完成时:
They had reallotted the resources before the project started. = 项目开始前，他们已经重新分配了资源。
The company had reallotted the marketing budget after the initial campaign failed. = 首次宣传活动失败后，公司已经重新分配了营销预算。

将来时:
The council will reallot the parking spaces next month. = 理事会下个月将重新分配停车位。
The team will reallot the workload to ensure timely completion. = 团队将重新分配工作量以确保按时完成。

虚拟语气:
If the company reallotted the budget, we could hire more staff. = 如果公司重新分配预算，我们就可以雇佣更多员工。
I wish they had reallotted the tasks more fairly. = 我希望他们更公平地重新分配任务。

被动语态:
The funds were reallotted to different departments. = 资金被重新分配到不同的部门。
The responsibilities were reallotted to better suit the team's skills. = 职责被重新分配以更好地适应团队的技能。

疑问句:

否定句:
The committee did not reallot the funds. = 委员会没有重新分配资金。
The manager refused to reallot the workload. = 经理拒绝重新分配工作量。
