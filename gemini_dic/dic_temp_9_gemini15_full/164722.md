prefaces:/ˈprɛfɪsɪz/ | n. |前言，序言 | Introductions to a book, typically explaining the author's purpose or providing background information.  |The book's prefaces clearly outlined the author's research methodology. 这本书的前言清楚地概述了作者的研究方法。 | introductions, prologues, forewords |  |  noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The author writes a preface for each of his books. = 作者为他的每一本书都写一篇前言。
This preface explains the book's main themes. = 这篇前言解释了本书的主要主题。


一般过去时:
She wrote a heartfelt preface for her grandmother's memoir. = 她为祖母的回忆录写了一篇感人的前言。
The editor added a preface to the new edition. = 编辑在新的版本中添加了一个前言。


现在完成时:
He has written several prefaces for academic publications. = 他已经为几本学术出版物撰写了前言。
The author has finally completed the preface to her long-awaited novel. = 作者终于完成了她期待已久的长篇小说的前言。


过去完成时:
Before publishing the book, she had already written the preface. = 出版这本书之前，她已经写好了前言。
They had carefully reviewed the preface before sending it to the publisher. = 在寄给出版商之前，他们仔细审查了前言。


将来时:
She will write a preface to her new book next week. = 她下周将为她的新书写一篇前言。
The publisher expects the author to finish the preface by Friday. = 出版商期望作者在星期五之前完成前言。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would write a more detailed preface. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会写一篇更详细的前言。
I wish I had written a more engaging preface. = 我希望我写一篇更引人入胜的前言。


被动语态:
The preface was written by a renowned scholar. = 前言是由一位著名的学者撰写的。
The preface is being revised by the editor. = 前言正在由编辑修改。


疑问句:
Did you read the preface before starting the book? = 你在开始看书之前读了前言吗？
Has the preface been approved by the publisher? = 前言已经得到出版商的批准了吗？


否定句:
The book didn't have a preface. = 这本书没有前言。
She didn't write the preface herself. = 这篇前言不是她自己写的。
