lablab: /ˈlæblæb/ | n. |扁豆，非洲豆 | A climbing bean plant (Lablab purpureus) of tropical Africa, widely cultivated for its edible beans and pods.  The plant is also known as hyacinth bean. | Example:  We planted lablab beans in the garden this year. = 今年我们在花园里种了扁豆。  The lablab’s vibrant purple flowers adorned the trellis. = 扁豆鲜艳的紫色花朵装饰着棚架。 | 近义词：hyacinth bean, Egyptian bean | 反义词：无
