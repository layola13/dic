self-assured:/ˌsɛlf əˈʃʊrd/| adj.|自信的，确信的|Having or showing confidence in oneself; self-confident.|The self-assured speaker captivated the audience with his charisma.  自信的演讲者以其魅力征服了观众。|He walked into the room with a self-assured air. 他自信地走进了房间。|confident, assured, self-possessed|insecure, timid, diffident, unsure|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-assured in his abilities. 他对自己的能力很有信心。
She is always self-assured in her presentations. 她在演示中总是很自信。


一般过去时:
He was self-assured during the interview. 他在面试期间表现得很自信。
She was self-assured even when facing challenges.  即使面对挑战，她也依然自信。


现在完成时:
He has been self-assured since he achieved success. 自从他取得成功以来，他就一直很自信。
She has always been self-assured, even as a child. 她从小就一直很自信。


过去完成时:
He had been self-assured before the competition, but he felt nervous afterwards. 比赛前他很自信，但比赛后却感到紧张。
She had been self-assured throughout her career, building a strong reputation.  她职业生涯中一直很自信，建立了良好的声誉。


将来时:
He will be self-assured when he masters the skill. 当他掌握这项技能时，他会充满自信。
She will be more self-assured after completing the training. 完成培训后，她会更有自信。


虚拟语气:
If he were more self-assured, he would take more risks. 如果他更有自信，他会承担更多风险。
I wish I were as self-assured as she is. 我希望我能像她一样自信。


被动语态:
His self-assured demeanor was admired by many. 许多人都钦佩他自信的举止。
Her self-assuredness was evident in her every action.  她的自信在她的每一个行动中都显而易见。(被动语态在此处略显生硬，通常不用被动)


疑问句:
Is he self-assured enough to lead the team? 他有足够的自信来领导团队吗？
Are you feeling self-assured about the exam? 你对考试有信心吗？


否定句:
He was not self-assured in his ability to win. 他对自己获胜的能力没有信心。
She did not feel self-assured at the beginning of her speech. 她演讲开始时并不自信。
