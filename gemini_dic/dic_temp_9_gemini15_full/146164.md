overestimate:/ˌoʊvərˈɛstɪmeɪt/ | vt. | 高估，过高估计 | To estimate (something) too highly; to place too high a value on.  | 例句：He overestimated his abilities. 他高估了自己的能力。 | 例句：They overestimated the cost of the project. 他们高估了项目的成本。 |近义词： exaggerate, overvalue, inflate | 反义词： underestimate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overestimate the market demand. = 他们高估了市场需求。
We overestimate his potential. = 我们高估了他的潜力。


一般过去时:
The manager overestimated the team's capacity. = 经理高估了团队的能力。
She overestimated the time it would take to finish the report. = 她高估了完成报告所需的时间。


现在完成时:
They have overestimated the risks involved. = 他们已经高估了所涉及的风险。
I have overestimated my stamina for this marathon. = 我高估了我跑马拉松的耐力。


过去完成时:
The investors had overestimated the company's profitability. = 投资者已经高估了公司的盈利能力。
We had overestimated the number of attendees for the conference. = 我们已经高估了会议的与会者人数。


将来时:
He will overestimate the difficulty of the exam. = 他会高估考试的难度。
They will overestimate the impact of the new policy. = 他们会高估新政策的影响。


虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't overestimated the amount of food needed for the party. = 我真希望我没有高估聚会需要的食物数量。


被动语态:
The project's cost was overestimated. = 项目的成本被高估了。

疑问句:

否定句:
