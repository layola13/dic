nonnationalization: /ˌnɑːnˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非国有化；非民族化 | The process of transferring ownership or control of something from a national government to private entities or foreign entities.  It is the opposite of nationalization.  This can apply to industries, resources, or assets previously under state control. | Example: The nonnationalization of the railway system led to increased competition and improved services.  非铁路系统的非国有化导致了竞争的加剧和服务的改进。  | 近义词: privatization, deregulation, decentralization | 反义词: nationalization, socialization
