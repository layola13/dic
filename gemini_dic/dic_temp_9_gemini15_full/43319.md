cram:/kræm/| vt. n.|塞满；猛塞；挤满；临时抱佛脚|To force or pack (something) into a small space; to study intensively for a short period before an exam.|例句：He crammed all his clothes into the suitcase. 他把所有的衣服塞进了手提箱。  例句：She crammed for the exam all night. 她为了考试复习了一整夜。|近义词：stuff, pack, jam, swot|反义词：unpack, empty


USAGE->

He crams his backpack with books. = 他把书塞进背包。
They cram the information into their heads. = 他们把信息塞进脑子里。

I crammed for the test last night. = 我昨晚临时抱佛脚准备考试。
