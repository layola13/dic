plonge:/plɔ̃ʒ/| v.| 潜水；投入；沉浸；|to jump or dive into water; to submerge oneself in something.|例句：He plunged into the cold water. 他跳进冰冷的水里。  例句：She plunged herself into her work. 她全身心地投入工作。|近义词：dive, immerse, submerge|反义词：emerge, surface|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plunges into the pool every morning. = 他每天早上都跳进游泳池。
She plunges her hands into the cold water. = 她把手伸进冰冷的水里。


一般过去时:
The diver plunged into the deep ocean. = 潜水员潜入深海。
They plunged into the project with great enthusiasm. = 他们怀着极大的热情投入到这个项目中。


现在完成时:
He has plunged into the world of finance. = 他已经投身金融界。
She has plunged herself into her studies. = 她已经全身心地投入到学习中。


过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had plunged into a deep depression. = 事故发生前，他陷入了深深的沮丧。
They had plunged into debt after buying the house. = 买房后他们负债累累。


将来时:
He will plunge into the icy lake. = 他将跳入冰冷的湖泊。
She will plunge into the challenges ahead. = 她将迎接未来的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If he plunged into the river, he would be in danger. = 如果他跳进河里，他会很危险。
I wish I had plunged into that business opportunity. = 我真希望我当时抓住了那个商机。


被动语态:
The submarine was plunged into the depths. = 潜艇潜入深海。
The country was plunged into chaos after the war. = 战争之后，国家陷入混乱。


疑问句:
Will he plunge into the unknown? = 他会投入未知领域吗？
Did she plunge into the project immediately? = 她是否立刻投入到项目中？


否定句:
He did not plunge into the argument. = 他没有参与那场争论。
She didn't plunge into the icy water. = 她没有跳进冰冷的水里。



PHRASE->
