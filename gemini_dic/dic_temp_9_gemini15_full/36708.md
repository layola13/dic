collision's: /kəˈlɪʒənz/ | n. | 碰撞的；冲突的 |  The possessive form of "collision," indicating something belonging to or related to a collision. It refers to the consequences, aspects, or effects of a collision. |  例句：The collision's impact caused significant damage to both vehicles. 碰撞的影响对两辆车都造成了严重的损坏。


The collision's aftermath was chaotic. 碰撞的后果一片混乱。|  近义词：impact's, crash's, smash's | 反义词：separation's, detachment's | 名词


WORD ORIGIN->

The word "collision" comes from the Middle French word "collision," which in turn comes from the Late Latin word "collisio," a combination of "col-" (together) and "laedere" (to strike, hurt).  This reflects the fundamental meaning of a collision as two or more things striking together.
