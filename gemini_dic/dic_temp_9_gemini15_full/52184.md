diarists:/ˈdaɪərɪsts/| n. |日记作者；记日记者 |People who keep diaries.  They record their daily experiences, thoughts, and feelings in a personal journal. |Example: Many famous diarists have provided invaluable insights into their times. 许多著名的日记作者为他们的时代提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：journalists (if the diary is intended for publication), chroniclers |反义词：None specifically, but the antonym would be someone who avoids self-reflection or writing.
