redivided:/ˌriːdɪˈvaɪdɪd/| v. |再划分，重新分配 |To divide again or differently.  This implies a previous division that is being altered or refined.|The land was redivided after the war. 战后土地重新划分了。 The company redivided its marketing budget to prioritize digital strategies. 公司重新分配了其市场营销预算，以优先考虑数字策略。|reapportion, redistribute|undivided, combined |动词


USAGE->

The teacher redivides the students into smaller groups. = 老师把学生重新分成更小的组。
