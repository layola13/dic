pre-civilization:/ˌpriːˌsɪvɪləˈzeɪʃən/| adj. n.| 前文明的；文明之前的 | Existing or occurring before the development of civilization; relating to the period before the development of civilization.  |例句：Pre-civilization societies relied on hunting and gathering for sustenance.  前文明社会依靠狩猎和采集来维持生计。
例句：The archaeological dig unearthed artifacts from the pre-civilization era. 考古发掘出土了前文明时代的文物。|近义词：prehistoric, primitive, ancient|反义词：civilized, modern


USAGE->
This word functions primarily as an adjective and, less commonly, as a noun.  Therefore, verb conjugations are not applicable.  The usage is primarily descriptive.

PHRASE->
pre-civilization society: 前文明社会
pre-civilization tools: 前文明工具
pre-civilization art: 前文明艺术
pre-civilization settlements: 前文明定居点
pre-civilization era: 前文明时代
