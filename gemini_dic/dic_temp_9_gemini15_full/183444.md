reptilian:/repˈtɪliən/| adj.|爬行动物的；冷血的；阴险的| Relating to reptiles or resembling a reptile, especially in being cold-blooded, slow-moving, or having a scaly skin.  The word often carries connotations of being sinister, cunning, or emotionless.|例句：Snakes and lizards are reptilian creatures.  蛇和蜥蜴是爬行动物。|例句：His reptilian smile sent shivers down my spine. 他阴险的笑容让我不寒而栗。|近义词：scaly, cold-blooded,  (in negative contexts) sinister, cunning,  |反义词：mammalian, warm-blooded, kind, benevolent
