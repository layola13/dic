caulk:/kɔːk/| n. vt.|填塞；堵塞；嵌缝材料|To fill or seal (a crack or gap) with a waterproof substance.|例句：We need to caulk the gaps around the windows to prevent drafts.我们需要用防水材料填塞窗户周围的缝隙以防止漏风。He caulked the seams of the boat.他堵塞了船的缝隙。|近义词：seal, fill, plug|反义词：open, expose
