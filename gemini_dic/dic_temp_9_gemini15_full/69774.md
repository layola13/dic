ferme:/fɛrm/| n.|农场；庄园；（尤指法国的）农庄|A ferme is a farm, especially a small farm, often in France.  It can also refer to a large estate or country house.|例句：They own a charming ferme in the French countryside.他们拥有法国乡下的一座迷人农庄。|例句：The ferme produced its own vegetables and dairy products. 这个农庄生产自己的蔬菜和奶制品。|近义词：farm, estate, ranch|反义词：city, urban area|名词
