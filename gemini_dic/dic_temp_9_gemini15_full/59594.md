ebullioscope:/ɪˌbʌljʊˈskoʊp/| n. |沸点计|An ebullioscope is an instrument used to determine the boiling point of a liquid, often used in determining the purity of alcoholic beverages.  It measures the boiling point elevation caused by dissolved substances.|The ebullioscope showed that the wine had a slightly higher boiling point than expected, indicating the presence of added sugar.|cryoscope, thermometer|—|
