macaroni:/ˌmækəˈroʊni/ | n. |通心粉，意大利面 |A type of pasta made from wheat flour, water, and sometimes eggs, typically shaped into short, hollow tubes. Macaroni is often used in dishes such as macaroni and cheese. |例句：I like to eat macaroni and cheese. 我喜欢吃通心粉和奶酪。|例句：She cooked a big pot of macaroni for the family. 她为家人煮了一大锅通心粉。|近义词：pasta, noodles |反义词：无
