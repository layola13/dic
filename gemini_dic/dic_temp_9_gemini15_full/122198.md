misuse:/ˌmɪsˈjuːs/ | vt. |滥用；误用；错用 | To use something wrongly or inappropriately. |例句：He misused his position to gain personal advantage. 他滥用职权谋取私利。|例句：She misused the company car for personal errands. 她把公司汽车用于私事。|近义词：abuse, mishandle, misapply |反义词：use properly, utilize correctly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They misuse their power. = 他们滥用权力。
He misuses company funds. = 他挪用公司资金。

一般过去时:
She misused the information. = 她误用了这些信息。
The manager misused his authority. = 经理滥用了他的权力。

现在完成时:
They have misused the resources. = 他们已经滥用了资源。
He has misused his trust. = 他辜负了大家的信任。

过去完成时:
They had misused the equipment before the accident. = 事故发生前他们就滥用了设备。
She had misused her influence to get the job. = 她曾利用自己的影响力获得这份工作。

将来时:
He will misuse the opportunity. = 他将会错失良机。(这里 misuse 指的是没有好好利用机会)
They will misuse the data. = 他们将滥用这些数据。

虚拟语气:
If he had not misused the funds, the company would not have failed. = 如果他没有挪用资金，公司就不会倒闭。
I wish they hadn't misused my kindness. = 我真希望他们没有滥用我的好意。

被动语态:
The funds were misused by the manager. = 资金被经理挪用了。
The resources have been misused for years. = 资源多年来一直被滥用。

疑问句:
Did they misuse the equipment? = 他们滥用设备了吗？
How did he misuse the information? = 他是如何误用信息的？

否定句:
He did not misuse the company car. = 他没有滥用公司汽车。
They did not misuse their privileges. = 他们没有滥用特权。
