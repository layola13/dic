relic:/ˈrɛlɪk/| n. |遗物，遗迹；残余|A relic is an object surviving from an earlier time, especially one of historical or cultural interest.  It can also refer to a trace or remnant of something that has disappeared.  |例句：The museum displayed ancient relics from the Roman Empire.  博物馆展出了罗马帝国的古代遗物。

The old building is a relic of a bygone era. 这栋旧建筑是过去时代的遗迹。|近义词：remains, vestige, remnant|反义词：new, modern
