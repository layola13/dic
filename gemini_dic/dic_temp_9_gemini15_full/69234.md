feasibility:/ˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/| n. |可行性，可能性 |The feasibility of the project is questionable.  该项目的可行性令人怀疑。|The feasibility study showed the plan was viable. 可行性研究表明该计划是可行的。|possibility, viability, practicability |impossibility, impracticability|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The feasibility of the plan is high. = 这个计划的可行性很高。
We assess the feasibility of each proposal. = 我们评估每个提案的可行性。

一般过去时:
The feasibility study showed promising results. = 可行性研究显示出令人鼓舞的结果。
They questioned the feasibility of the project last year. = 他们去年质疑了该项目的可行性。


现在完成时:
We have determined the feasibility of the new technology. = 我们已经确定了新技术的可行性。
They have completed the feasibility assessment. = 他们已经完成了可行性评估。


过去完成时:
Before starting, they had already examined the feasibility. = 开始之前，他们已经检查了可行性。
The engineers had doubted the feasibility of the bridge design. = 工程师们曾怀疑桥梁设计的可行性。


将来时:
We will assess the feasibility of the project next week. = 我们下周将评估该项目的可行性。
The committee will discuss the feasibility report tomorrow. = 委员会明天将讨论可行性报告。


虚拟语气:
If the feasibility were higher, we would proceed. = 如果可行性更高，我们将继续进行。
I wish the feasibility study had been more thorough. = 我希望可行性研究更彻底一些。


被动语态:
The feasibility of the proposal was carefully considered. = 该提案的可行性受到了仔细考虑。
The feasibility study is being conducted by a third-party firm. = 可行性研究正在由第三方公司进行。


疑问句:
What is the feasibility of this approach? = 这种方法的可行性如何？
Is the feasibility of the plan realistic? = 该计划的可行性现实吗？


否定句:
The feasibility of the project is not certain. = 该项目的可行性并不确定。
The study showed that the idea was not feasible. = 研究表明这个想法不可行。


PHRASE->

词源:
From  feasible (adjective) + -ibility (suffix).  Feasible itself comes from the Old French *feasible, from Latin *factibilis, from facere "to do, make".  Thus, feasibility literally means "the state of being doable".
