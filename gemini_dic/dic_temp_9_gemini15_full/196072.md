separatists:/ˈsɛpəreɪtɪsts/| n.|分离主义者|People who support the separation of a group of people from a larger body, especially a political entity.  They advocate for the creation of an independent state or region.|例句：The separatists launched a violent campaign for independence. 分离主义者发起了争取独立的暴力运动。

例句：The government is trying to negotiate with the separatists to find a peaceful solution. 政府正试图与分离主义者谈判以寻求和平解决办法。

近义词： secessionists, nationalists (in the context of seeking independence), insurgents (if resorting to violence)

反义词：unionists, integrationists, loyalists

词性：名词
