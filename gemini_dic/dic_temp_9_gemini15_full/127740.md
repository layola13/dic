negotiates:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪts/ | v. | 谈判；协商；达成协议 | To discuss something in order to reach an agreement, especially in business or politics.  |例句：The union is negotiating with management for a better pay deal. 工会正在与管理层谈判以争取更好的薪酬待遇。|例句：They negotiated a ceasefire with the rebels. 他们与叛军谈判达成了停火协议。|近义词：bargain, discuss, confer, mediate |反义词：refuse, reject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee negotiates the budget every year. = 委员会每年都会协商预算。
She negotiates contracts for her company. = 她为公司洽谈合同。

一般过去时:
They negotiated a peace treaty last month. = 他们上个月协商了一份和平条约。
He successfully negotiated the purchase of the property. = 他成功地协商购买了该房产。

现在完成时:
We have negotiated a new trade agreement with China. = 我们已经与中国达成了一项新的贸易协定。
The government has negotiated several important reforms. = 政府已经协商通过了几项重要的改革。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had negotiated the main points of the agreement. = 会议之前，他们已经协商好了协议的主要内容。
She had negotiated a lower price before she bought the car. = 在她买车之前，她已经协商了一个更低的价格。

将来时:
They will negotiate a new contract next week. = 他们下周将协商一份新合同。
The two countries will negotiate a solution to the dispute. = 这两个国家将协商解决这场争端。

虚拟语气:
If they negotiated in good faith, they might have reached an agreement. = 如果他们真诚地谈判，他们也许就能达成协议。
I wish we had negotiated a better deal. = 我希望我们当时能谈到一个更好的交易。

被动语态:
The contract was negotiated by both parties. = 合同是由双方协商达成的。
A ceasefire was negotiated after weeks of fighting. = 激战数周后，停火协议得以达成。

疑问句:
Are they still negotiating the terms of the contract? = 他们还在协商合同条款吗？
Have they negotiated a settlement yet? = 他们已经协商达成和解了吗？

否定句:
They did not negotiate a satisfactory outcome. = 他们没有协商出一个令人满意的结果。
The two sides failed to negotiate a compromise. = 双方未能协商达成妥协。


PHRASE->
negotiate a deal: 谈成一笔交易
negotiate a price: 协商价格
negotiate a settlement: 协商和解
negotiate a contract: 谈判合同
negotiate terms: 协商条款
