hyperconsciousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnʃəsnəs/| /ˌhaɪpərˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/ | n. | 超意识；高度意识 |  A state of heightened awareness and sensitivity beyond normal consciousness;  a state of being intensely aware of one's own thoughts, feelings, and sensations, and also of the environment. It often involves a deep understanding of oneself and the world, sometimes accompanied by spiritual or mystical experiences.  | Example: Through meditation, she achieved a state of hyperconsciousness, allowing her to perceive subtle energies around her.  通过冥想，她达到了超意识状态，让她能够感知周围的细微能量。  | 近义词： heightened awareness, heightened sensitivity, supersensitivity, metaconsciousness | 反义词： unconsciousness, unawareness, insensibility | 名词
