languishingly:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃɪŋli/| adv.|萎靡地，衰弱地，没精打采地 |In a weak or feeble manner; languidly.|例句：The plants languished languidly in the dry soil.  干旱的土壤里，植物萎靡不振地枯萎着。

例句：He looked languishingly at her across the crowded room. 他在拥挤的房间里对她投去渴望而忧郁的眼神。

近义词：weakly, listlessly, languidly, despondently

反义词：vigorously, energetically, enthusiastically

词性：副词
