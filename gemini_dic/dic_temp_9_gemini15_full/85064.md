hard-earned:/ˈhɑːrdˈɜːrnd/ | adj. | 辛辛苦苦挣来的，来之不易的 |  Describes something obtained or achieved after much effort, difficulty, and perseverance. It emphasizes the significant amount of work and struggle involved in acquiring something. |例句：Her hard-earned savings finally allowed her to buy a house.  她辛辛苦苦攒下的钱终于让她买了一套房子。

She received a hard-earned promotion after years of dedication. 她经过多年的努力，终于得到了晋升。（实至名归的晋升）| 近义词：well-deserved, richly deserved, much-deserved | 反义词：easily obtained, effortless, unearned
