hermit:/ˈhɜːrmɪt/| n. |隐士，隐居者 |A hermit is a person who lives alone and apart from society, often for religious reasons or to pursue a solitary life.  Hermits typically live a simple life, often in seclusion in a remote area. |例句：He lived the life of a hermit in a small cabin in the woods. 他在森林里的小木屋里过着隐士般的生活。|例句：Many hermits throughout history have found solace and spiritual growth in isolation. 历史上许多隐士都在独处中找到了慰藉和精神上的成长。|近义词：recluse, ascetic, loner |反义词：socialite, extrovert
