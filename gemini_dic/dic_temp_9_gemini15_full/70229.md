fichu:/fiˈʃuː/| n.|小围巾，装饰性头巾|A fichu is a small, usually triangular scarf or shawl worn around the shoulders or neck, often as a decorative element of a woman's attire.  It's historically significant and has seen revivals in fashion.|例句：She wore a delicate lace fichu with her low-cut gown. 她穿着低胸长袍，搭配一条精致的蕾丝小围巾。

例句：The actress's costume included a charming silk fichu. 女演员的服装包括一条迷人的丝绸小围巾。|近义词：shawl, scarf, kerchief|反义词：无
