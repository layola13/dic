sentimentalize:/ˌsɛntɪmənˈtaɪz/ | vt. | 使感伤；使多愁善感；过分强调感情 | To make something sentimental; to view or treat something in an excessively sentimental manner.  |  例句：Don't sentimentalize the past; learn from it and move on. 不要沉溺于过去的感伤；要从中吸取教训并继续前进。

例句：He sentimentalized his childhood memories, exaggerating their idyllic nature. 他将童年回忆过分美化，夸大了它们的田园诗般的本质。


近义词：romanticize, romanticise, emotionalize

反义词：de-sentimentalize, harden, toughen
