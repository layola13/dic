prioress:/ˈpraɪərəs/| n.|女修道院院长|The prioress is the female superior of a convent or a nunnery, ranking below an abbess.  She is responsible for the spiritual and temporal well-being of the nuns under her care. |例句：The prioress welcomed the new novices to the convent. 女修道院院长欢迎新来的修女来到修道院。|例句：The prioress presided over the daily prayers and ceremonies. 女修道院院长主持每日的祈祷和仪式。|近义词：abbess (for larger communities)|反义词：laywoman|名词
