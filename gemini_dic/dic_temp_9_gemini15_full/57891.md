driftbolt:/ˈdrɪftbəʊlt/| n. |漂流螺栓，木螺栓|A driftbolt is a type of bolt, typically made of wood or metal, used to fasten timbers together, often without a nut and washer. It is driven into place using a hammer or mallet.  It is often used in construction and carpentry. |例句：The carpenter used driftbolts to secure the beams. 木匠用漂流螺栓固定梁木。The old barn was held together with numerous driftbolts.  旧谷仓是用许多漂流螺栓固定在一起的。|近义词：bolt, dowel, pin |反义词：screw (in the sense of a threaded fastener)
