creameries:/ˈkriːməriz/| n. |奶油加工厂；乳品店|A creamery is a place where milk is processed into butter, cheese, and other dairy products. It can also refer to a shop selling dairy products.|例句：The local creamery makes the best ice cream in town. 当地乳品店制作城里最好吃的冰淇淋。
例句：She bought fresh butter from the creamery. 她从乳品店买了新鲜黄油。|近义词：dairy, dairy farm|反义词：无
