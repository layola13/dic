mentees:/ˌmɛnˈtiːz/| n. |被指导者，受指导者 | A mentee is a person who is receiving guidance and support from a mentor.  Mentees benefit from the experience and knowledge of their mentors, often in a professional or personal development context. | Example:  The program pairs experienced professionals with talented mentees to foster career growth.  这个项目将经验丰富的专业人士与有天赋的被指导者配对，以促进职业发展。 |近义词：protégés, apprentices, trainees|反义词：mentors
