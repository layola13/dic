obo:/ˈoʊboʊ/| abbr.|义务债券|Obligation bond; a type of bond issued by a municipality or other government entity, often to finance public projects.  These bonds are backed by the taxing power of the issuer, meaning that if the issuer defaults, bondholders can collect taxes to repay the debt.|例句：The city issued obo's to fund the new school construction.  这个城市发行义务债券来资助新的学校建设。|近义词：municipal bond, revenue bond|反义词：N/A|abbr.
