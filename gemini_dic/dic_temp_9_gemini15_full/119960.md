millenary:/mɪˈlɛnəri/ | n. |千禧年；一千年的；持续一千年的 | A millenary is a period of one thousand years.  It can also refer to something that lasts for a thousand years or commemorates a thousand years. | Example: The celebrations marked the millenary of the city's founding.  这个庆祝活动标志着这座城市建城一千年。 |近义词：millennium, thousand-year period |反义词： N/A
