lay-away:/ˈleɪəweɪ/| n. |分期付款购买；预购；延期付款|A system of purchasing goods where the buyer makes a series of payments over time until the full price is paid, at which point the goods are delivered.  It's a form of retail financing. |例句：I put the bicycle on layaway and will make monthly payments until it's paid off.我把自行车放在分期付款里，并将每月付款直到付清。

例句：The furniture store offers a layaway plan for large purchases.这家家具店为大件商品提供分期付款计划。


近义词：installment plan, payment plan, deferred payment|反义词：cash purchase, immediate purchase|名词
