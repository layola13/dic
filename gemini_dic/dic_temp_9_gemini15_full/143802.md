outorganizes: /ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/ | v. | 超越组织能力；组织得更好 |  To surpass in organizational skills or effectiveness.  This is a relatively rare word, formed by adding the prefix "out-" to the verb "organize." It implies exceeding someone or something else in the ability to organize. |


例句：

The new CEO outorganizes his predecessor in every aspect of the company's operations. = 新任首席执行官在公司运营的各个方面都比他的前任做得更好。

Their meticulous planning outorganizes any attempt to disrupt their project. = 他们细致的计划挫败了任何试图破坏他们项目的企图。


近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outperform, outdo

反义词：disorganize, mismanage,  undermine


词性：动词
