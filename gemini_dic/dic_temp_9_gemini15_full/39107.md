confronte:/kənˈfrʌnt/| vt.|对抗；面对；使面临|To meet (someone) face to face, especially as a challenge; to deal with a difficult situation|例句：He confronted the thief and demanded his money back. 他面对小偷并要求他把钱还回来。

例句：The company confronted a serious financial crisis. 这家公司面临严重的金融危机。

近义词：encounter, face, meet|反义词：avoid, evade, shun|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confronts his fears every day. = 他每天都直面恐惧。
She confronts the challenges with courage. = 她勇敢地面对挑战。


一般过去时:
He confronted the bully in the schoolyard. = 他在学校操场上与恶霸对质。
She confronted her boss about the unfair treatment. = 她向老板质问不公平的待遇。


现在完成时:
He has confronted many obstacles in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多障碍。
She has confronted her past traumas through therapy. = 她通过治疗直面了她过去的创伤。


过去完成时:
He had confronted the problem before, so he knew what to do. = 他以前曾面对过这个问题，所以他知道该怎么做。
She had confronted the evidence before making her decision. = 在做出决定之前，她已经面对了证据。


将来时:
He will confront the issue in the next meeting. = 他将在下次会议上解决这个问题。
She will confront her fears and pursue her dreams. = 她将克服恐惧，追求梦想。


虚拟语气:
If I confronted him, he might apologize. = 如果我去质问他，他可能会道歉。
I wish I had confronted my doubts earlier. = 我希望我早点面对我的疑虑。


被动语态:
The problem was confronted by the team. = 这个问题由团队解决了。
The issue is being confronted by the government. = 政府正在解决这个问题。


疑问句:
How will you confront this difficult situation? = 你将如何应对这个困境？
What strategies will you use to confront the challenges ahead? = 你将使用什么策略来应对未来的挑战？


否定句:
He didn't confront his feelings. = 他没有面对自己的感情。
She didn't confront the difficult truth. = 她没有面对残酷的事实。


PHRASE->
confront a problem = 面对问题
confront a challenge = 面对挑战
confront the enemy = 与敌人对抗
confront reality = 面对现实
confront the truth = 面对真相
