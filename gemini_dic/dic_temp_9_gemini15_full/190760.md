savers:/ˈseɪvərz/| n.|储蓄者；节省者；救星|People who save money regularly, often for a specific purpose such as retirement or a down payment on a house.  They are also people or things that save something from loss or danger. |例句：Many savers are concerned about low interest rates. 许多储蓄者担心低利率。|例句：The firefighters were savers that day, rescuing the trapped family. 消防员那天是救星，救出了被困的家庭。|近义词：investors, economizers, rescuers|反义词：spenders, wasters
