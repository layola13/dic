rediscusses:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌsɪz/| v.|重新讨论，再次讨论 |To discuss something again; to reconsider a topic.|例句：The committee rediscussed the budget after new information emerged.  委员会在出现新信息后重新讨论了预算。|例句：They rediscussed the terms of the contract to ensure everyone agreed. 他们再次讨论了合同条款以确保每个人都同意。|近义词：reconsider, review, re-examine |反义词：conclude, finalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rediscuss the project every week. = 他们每周都重新讨论这个项目。
He rediscusses the problem with his team. = 他与他的团队重新讨论这个问题。


一般过去时:
The board rediscussed the proposal after the initial rejection. = 董事会在最初的提议被拒绝后重新讨论了该提案。
She rediscussed her concerns with her manager. = 她与她的经理重新讨论了她的担忧。


现在完成时:
We have rediscussed the strategy several times. = 我们已经多次重新讨论了这个策略。
They have rediscussed the matter since the last meeting. = 自上次会议以来，他们已经重新讨论了此事。


过去完成时:

The committee had rediscussed the issue multiple times. = 委员会已经多次重新讨论了这个问题。


将来时:
We will rediscuss the topic next week. = 我们下周将重新讨论这个话题。
They will rediscuss the contract amendments. = 他们将重新讨论合同修正案。


虚拟语气:
If they rediscussed the issue, they might find a solution. = 如果他们重新讨论这个问题，他们可能会找到解决方案。
It would be helpful if they rediscussed the details. = 如果他们重新讨论细节，将会很有帮助。

否定句:
