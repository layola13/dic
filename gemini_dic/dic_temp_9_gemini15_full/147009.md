英文解析：

As a noun, "overlap" refers to a portion of something that extends over and covers a part of another thing.  It can also refer to the extent to which two things coincide or share a common area.

As a verb, "overlap" means to extend over and cover a part of something else; to coincide partially in time or space.

例句：

Noun:

The roof tiles overlapped to ensure the house was waterproof. 屋顶瓦片相互重叠以确保房屋防水。

The two companies' product lines show significant overlap. 两家公司的产品线有很大的重叠。


Verb:

The responsibilities of the two jobs overlapped considerably. 这两份工作的职责有相当大的重叠。

The presentation slides overlapped so that I couldn't see what the presenter was talking about.幻灯片重叠，我看不清演示者在说什么。



词源：

From Old English *ofer- “over” + *lapian “to lap, wrap,” from Proto-Germanic *lapjan.
