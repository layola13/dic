pound-down: /paʊndˈdaʊn/ | vt. |猛击；使…下跌；压制；彻底击败| To strike repeatedly and forcefully; to force down in price or value; to suppress or subdue; to defeat decisively. |例句：He pounded down the door in frustration. 他怒气冲冲地猛砸房门。|近义词：hammer down, beat down, crush, suppress |反义词：elevate, uplift, boost

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The workers pound down the stakes into the ground. = 工人们把桩子用力砸进土里。
She pounds down the prices of her goods to attract customers. = 她压低商品价格以吸引顾客。


一般过去时:
The blacksmith pounded down the hot iron. = 铁匠用力锤打着烧红的铁。
The company pounded down the stock price. = 公司压低了股票价格。


现在完成时:
The rain has pounded down all day. = 雨下了一整天。
They have pounded down the opposition. = 他们彻底击败了对手。


过去完成时:
The storm had pounded down the coast before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，暴风雨已经袭击了海岸。
The government had pounded down inflation before the election. = 政府在大选之前已经控制住了通货膨胀。


将来时:
The protesters will pound down the gates of the parliament. = 抗议者将猛砸议会的大门。
The market analysts predict that the stock price will pound down further. = 市场分析师预测股价将会进一步下跌。


虚拟语气:
If he pounded down the door, he would have entered the house. = 如果他砸开了门，他就能进入房子。
If the company had pounded down the prices earlier, they would have sold more products. = 如果公司早点降价，他们就会卖出更多产品。


被动语态:
The door was pounded down by the angry mob. = 那扇门被愤怒的暴民砸坏了。
The prices were pounded down by the intense competition. = 价格被激烈的竞争压低了。


疑问句:
Was the door pounded down by the police? = 门是被警察砸开的吗？
Were the prices pounded down by the economic crisis? = 价格是被经济危机压低的吗？


否定句:
He didn't pound down the door; he used a key. = 他没有砸门，他用钥匙开的门。
The company didn't pound down the prices; they maintained them. = 公司没有压低价格，他们维持了原价。
