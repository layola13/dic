disabuse:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːz/ | vt. |使某人摆脱错误观念；纠正；使醒悟 | To free someone from a misconception or error.  |例句：I had to disabuse him of the notion that he was indispensable. 我不得不让他摆脱那种自己是不可或缺的观念。

例句：She tried to disabuse him of his prejudices. 她试图纠正他的偏见。

近义词：correct, enlighten, undeceive, disillusion | 反义词：mislead, deceive, delude | 动词
