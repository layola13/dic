crashes:/ˈkræʃɪz/ | n. v. |坠毁；碰撞；失败；（系统）崩溃 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "crashes" refers to instances of a sudden and violent collision, a sudden failure of a system, or a sudden drop in something's value. As a verb, it describes the action of colliding violently, failing suddenly, or causing something to collapse.


例句:
The plane crashed into the mountain.  飞机撞上了山。

The stock market crashed in 1929.  1929年股市崩盘了。


近义词:
collapses, impacts, breakdowns, plummets


反义词:
succeeds, functions, thrives, soars


词性:
Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The computer crashes frequently. 电脑经常崩溃。
The market crashes sometimes due to unexpected events. 市场有时由于意外事件而崩溃。


一般过去时:
The program crashed yesterday. 程序昨天崩溃了。
My car crashed into a tree. 我的车撞到了一棵树上。


现在完成时:
The system has crashed three times this week. 这个系统本周已经崩溃了三次。
The price of oil has crashed recently. 最近油价暴跌。


过去完成时:
The server had crashed before we could upload the files.  在我们能够上传文件之前，服务器已经崩溃了。
The economy had crashed before the new government took office. 新政府上任前经济已经崩溃了。


将来时:
The website will crash if too many people try to access it at once. 如果太多人同时试图访问该网站，它将会崩溃。
If you drive so fast, you'll crash the car. 如果你开这么快，你会撞坏车的。


虚拟语气:
If the brakes had failed, the car would have crashed. 如果刹车失灵了，汽车就会坠毁。
I wish the computer hadn't crashed during my presentation. 我真希望我的电脑在演示过程中没有崩溃。


被动语态:
The car was crashed by a drunk driver. 那辆车被一名醉酒司机撞坏了。
The project was crashed due to lack of funding. 项目由于缺乏资金而失败了。


疑问句:
Did the computer crash? 电脑崩溃了吗？
How many times has the website crashed today? 网站今天崩溃了多少次？


否定句:
The plane didn't crash. 飞机没有坠毁。
The system hasn't crashed yet. 系统还没有崩溃。
