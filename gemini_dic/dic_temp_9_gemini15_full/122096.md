mistime:/mɪsˈtaɪm/| vt.| 错过时机；判断时间错误；安排时间不当| to do something at the wrong time or too late; to calculate the timing of something incorrectly |例句：He mistimed his jump and fell into the water. 他跳跃时机不对，掉进了水里。

例句：The company mistimed the launch of its new product, resulting in poor sales. 公司新产品的发布时机不对，导致销量不佳。


近义词：miss,  delay,  undertime,  misjudge

反义词：time,  perfect timing
