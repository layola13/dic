digram:/ˈdaɪɡræm/| n. |二元图；二字母组合|A digram is a two-letter combination, often used in linguistics to represent frequently occurring letter pairs.  It can also refer to a simple diagram showing two things. |Example: The digram "th" is common in English.  这个二元图"th"在英语中很常见。The digram showed the relationship between temperature and pressure. 这个二元图显示了温度和压力之间的关系。|近义词：bigram (more technical), two-letter combination|反义词：N/A
