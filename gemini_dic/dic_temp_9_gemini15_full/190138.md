sanitarist: /ˈsænɪtərɪst/ | n. |公共卫生专家，卫生员 | A sanitarist is a person who is trained and qualified in sanitation, public health, and hygiene.  They work to prevent disease and improve public health through various methods, including inspecting facilities, educating the public, and enforcing regulations. | Example: The sanitarist inspected the restaurant's kitchen for cleanliness and safety violations.  公共卫生专家检查了这家餐馆的厨房卫生和安全违规情况。|近义词：public health officer, health inspector |反义词：polluter, unsanitary person
