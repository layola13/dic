cresset:/ˈkrɛsɪt/| n. |火盆；灯架；（尤指中世纪的）火盆|A cresset is a container, typically of metal, for holding a fire or light, often used outdoors.  Historically, cressets were frequently used as portable light sources, particularly during medieval times. They were often hung from a pole or placed on a stand.|The flickering cresset cast long shadows on the cobblestone street.  中世纪的火盆在鹅卵石街道上投下长长的阴影。|近义词：lantern, brazier, lamp|反义词:darkness
