legolas:/ˈlɛɡoʊlæs/| n. |莱戈拉斯（人物名）|A fictional character in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy world of Middle-earth. He is an elf, a prince of Mirkwood, and a skilled archer.  He is known for his grace, agility, and accuracy with a bow and arrow.  He is a major character in *The Lord of the Rings*.| Legolas skillfully shot the arrow, hitting the target from an incredible distance. 莱戈拉斯熟练地射出箭，从难以置信的距离击中了目标。


He is a loyal companion to Aragorn and Gimli. 他是阿拉贡和金雳忠诚的同伴。|近义词：elf (精灵)|反义词：无
