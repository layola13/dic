resynchronizing:/ˌriːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |重新同步，使重新同步 | The process of bringing something back into synchronization; restoring a system or process to a state of agreement in time or phase.  | Example: The network is currently resynchronizing after a brief outage. 网络在短暂中断后目前正在重新同步。 The two clocks need resynchronizing. 这两块表需要重新同步。 | 近义词：realigning, readjusting, coordinating | 反义词：desynchronizing


USAGE->

The system resynchronizes automatically after a power failure. = 系统在断电后会自动重新同步。
The devices resynchronize their clocks regularly. = 这些设备定期重新同步它们的时钟。

一般过去时:
The engineers resynchronized the network after the storm. = 工程师在暴风雨过后重新同步了网络。
The team resynchronized their schedules to ensure collaboration. = 团队重新同步了他们的日程安排以确保合作。
