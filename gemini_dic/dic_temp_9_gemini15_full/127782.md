neighboring:/ˈneɪbərɪŋ/ | adj. | 邻近的，附近的 |  Adjacent, close by, nearby; situated near or next to something else.  The word implies a relatively close proximity. | 例句：The neighboring town is only a short drive away. = 邻近的小镇开车只需一小会儿就能到。

The neighboring countries signed a trade agreement. = 邻国签署了一项贸易协定。


近义词：adjacent, adjoining, nearby, proximate, contiguous | 反义词：distant, remote, faraway | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The neighboring country is developing rapidly. = 邻国发展迅速。
Our neighboring houses are very similar. = 我们隔壁的房子非常相似。


一般过去时:
The neighboring farm was sold last year. = 邻近的农场去年卖掉了。
The neighboring village hosted a festival. = 邻村举办了一个节日。


现在完成时:
We have visited our neighboring town several times. = 我们已经去过邻镇好几次了。
They have had issues with their neighboring property. = 他们和邻近的房产有过纠纷。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, the neighboring company had already filed for bankruptcy. = 在合并之前，邻近的公司已经申请破产了。
I had never met our neighboring family before last week. = 在上周之前，我从未见过我们隔壁的家人。


将来时:
We will visit our neighboring city next month. = 我们下个月将参观邻近的城市。
The neighboring building will be demolished next year. = 邻近的建筑物将于明年拆除。


虚拟语气:
If we lived in a neighboring state, it would be easier to visit. = 如果我们住在邻州，探访会更容易。
I wish I had known the neighboring family was having a party. = 我真希望我知道隔壁邻居在举办派对。


被动语态:
The neighboring houses were damaged by the storm. = 邻近的房屋被风暴破坏了。
The neighboring park is being renovated. = 邻近的公园正在翻修。


疑问句:
Do you know the neighboring family well? = 你认识隔壁的家人吗？
Have you ever been to the neighboring village? = 你去过邻村吗？


否定句:

They didn't complain about the noise from the neighboring construction. = 他们没有抱怨邻近建筑工地的噪音。


PHRASE->

词源:
neighboring 源自neighbor + -ing。 neighbor的词源追溯到古英语neahgebur，由neah（近）和gebur（居民，农民）构成。 -ing后缀表示形容词，表示“与…相关的”。  因此neighboring的含义是“与邻居相关的”，引申为“邻近的”。
