mangonist:/ˈmæŋɡənɪst/| n.|芒果主义者|A person who strongly advocates for or believes in a particular set of beliefs or practices related to mangoes, often to an excessive or unreasonable degree.  This is a neologism, not a standard dictionary word. It implies an obsession or fanaticism about mangoes.|例句：He's a real mangonist; his house is filled with mango-themed decorations and he only eats mango-flavored foods. 他是个十足的芒果主义者；他的房子里充满了芒果主题的装饰，而且他只吃芒果味的食品。|近义词：enthusiast, fanatic, aficionado |反义词：apath
