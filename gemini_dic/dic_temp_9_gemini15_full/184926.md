retard:/rɪˈtɑːrd/ | v., n., adj. | 阻碍；迟钝的人；智力迟钝的 | To delay or hinder the progress of something; a person with intellectual disability; slow or delayed in development. |例句：The heavy rain retarded the growth of the crops. 大雨阻碍了农作物的生长。The child's development was retarded.  这个孩子的发育迟缓了。|近义词：delay, hinder, impede, obstruct, slow down;  (n.)  disabled person, intellectually challenged person |反义词：accelerate, expedite, hasten, speed up; (n.)  gifted person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain retards the growth of the plants. = 大雨阻碍了植物的生长。
He retards the project's progress. = 他延缓了项目的进度。


一般过去时:
The storm retarded the ship's arrival. = 暴风雨延误了船的到达。
The accident retarded the traffic flow. = 事故阻碍了交通流量。


现在完成时:
The drought has retarded the harvest. = 干旱已经延误了收成。
His illness has retarded his progress in school. = 他的病延误了他的学业。


过去完成时:
The previous delays had retarded the completion of the bridge. = 先前的延误已经延缓了桥梁的完工。
The bad weather had retarded their journey. = 恶劣的天气延误了他们的旅程。


将来时:
The upcoming snowstorm will retard travel. = 即将来临的暴风雪将阻碍出行。
The new regulations will retard economic growth. = 新规将阻碍经济增长。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't intervened, the project would have been retarded. = 如果政府不干预，项目就会被延误。
If he hadn't been ill, his studies wouldn't have been retarded. = 如果他没有生病，他的学习就不会被耽误。


被动语态:
The project was retarded by the lack of funding. = 项目因缺乏资金而被延误。
The construction was retarded by unexpected difficulties. = 建设因意外困难而被延误。


疑问句:
What retarded the development of the technology? = 什么阻碍了这项技术的开发？
Did anything retard the team's performance? = 有什么东西阻碍了团队的表现吗？


否定句:
The new techniques did not retard the production process. = 新技术并没有延缓生产过程。
Nothing retarded the progress of the negotiations. = 没有什么阻碍谈判的进展。
