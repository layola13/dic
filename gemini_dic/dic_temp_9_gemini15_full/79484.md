globalist:/ˈɡloʊbəlɪst/| n. |全球主义者；全球化支持者|A person who supports globalization or believes in a globalized world.  They often advocate for international cooperation and interconnectedness.  The term can be used neutrally, but it is often used pejoratively, implying a lack of national loyalty or a hidden agenda. |例句：Some critics accuse globalists of prioritizing international interests over national sovereignty.  一些批评者指责全球主义者将国际利益置于国家主权之上。

例句：The speaker argued that globalist policies have led to increased economic inequality. 演讲者认为全球主义政策导致了经济不平等的加剧。


近义词：internationalist, cosmopolitan, global citizen |反义词：nationalist, protectionist, isolationist |词性:noun
