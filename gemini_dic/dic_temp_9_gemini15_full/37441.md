commiserately:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtli/| adv.| 同情地；怜悯地 | expressing sympathy or pity | She looked at him commiserately. 她同情地看着他。 |近义词：sympathetically, compassionately, empathetically |反义词：cruelly, unsympathetically


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commiserates with her regularly. = 他经常安慰她。
They commiserate with each other about their bad luck. = 他们互相同情彼此的不幸。


一般过去时:
She commiserated with him on his loss. = 她对他遭受的损失表示同情。
They commiserated with the victims of the flood. = 他们同情洪灾的受害者。


现在完成时:
I have commiserated with many people in my career. = 在我的职业生涯中，我已同情过很多人。
We have commiserated with our friend about his illness. = 我们已经对朋友的病情表示同情。


过去完成时:
She had commiserated with him before he left. = 他离开之前，她已经对他表示同情。
They had commiserated with each other about their problems. = 他们之前已经互相同情彼此的问题。


将来时:
I will commiserate with her when she tells me the news. = 当她告诉我这个消息时，我会安慰她。
They will commiserate with the family after the funeral. = 葬礼结束后，他们将向家人表示慰问。


虚拟语气:

I wish I could commiserate with him more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地安慰他。


被动语态:

否定句:
