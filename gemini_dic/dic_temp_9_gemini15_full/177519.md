reblossoms:/ˌriːˈblɑːsəmz/| v.|再次开花；重新兴旺；复苏|To blossom again; to flourish or thrive anew.  The word implies a renewal or resurgence after a period of dormancy or decline.|例句：After a harsh winter, the garden reblossomed in spring.  严冬过后，花园在春天重新开花。

The roses reblossomed after a period of drought. 经过一段时间的干旱后，玫瑰花又重新盛开。|近义词：reflourish, revive, regenerate|反义词：wither, decay, decline|动词
