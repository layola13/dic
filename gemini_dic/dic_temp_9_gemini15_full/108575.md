leach:/liːtʃ/| v. n.| 过滤；渗出；浸出；淋溶；淘洗；|to wash or dissolve out soluble constituents from a solid (as in leaching minerals from soil)  |例句：The rain leached the nutrients from the soil. 雨水冲走了土壤中的养分。|例句：The color leached out of the fabric after washing. 洗涤后织物的颜色褪去了。|近义词：percolate, seep, filter, drain|反义词：concentrate, saturate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain leaches nutrients from the soil. = 雨水冲刷土壤中的养分。
He leaches the tea leaves to make a weaker brew. = 他冲泡茶叶使其茶水变淡。


一般过去时:
The dye leached out of the clothes in the wash. = 染料在清洗过程中从衣服上褪色了。
They leached the minerals from the ore. = 他们从矿石中提取矿物质。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has leached the topsoil of its nutrients. = 持续的降雨已冲刷掉了表层土壤的养分。
The color has leached out of the painting over time. = 画作的颜色随着时间的推移而褪色了。


过去完成时:
The prolonged drought had leached the soil of its moisture. = 长期的干旱使土壤失去了水分。
The acid rain had leached the nutrients from the forest floor. = 酸雨已冲刷掉了森林地面的养分。


将来时:
The heavy rains will leach the pesticides from the soil. = 大雨将冲刷掉土壤中的农药。
The new cleaning product will not leach color from fabrics. = 新型清洁产品不会使织物褪色。


虚拟语气:
If we used less fertilizer, the nutrients wouldn't leach away so quickly. = 如果我们少用一些化肥，养分就不会流失得这么快。
I wish the dye wouldn't leach out of this fabric so easily. = 我希望这种染料不会这么容易从织物上褪色。


被动语态:
The minerals are leached from the ore using a chemical process. = 矿物是使用化学方法从矿石中提取出来的。
The nutrients were leached from the soil by the heavy rains. = 养分被大雨从土壤中冲刷掉了。


疑问句:
Does the fertilizer leach into the groundwater? = 化肥会渗入地下水吗？
Will the dye leach out if I wash it in hot water? = 如果我用热水洗涤，染料会褪色吗？


否定句:
The fabric doesn't leach color even after repeated washings. = 即使反复清洗，织物也不会褪色。
This type of soil doesn't readily leach nutrients. = 这种类型的土壤不会轻易流失养分。
