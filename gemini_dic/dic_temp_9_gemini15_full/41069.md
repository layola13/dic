copper:/ˈkɒpər/ /ˈkɑːpər/| n. |铜；铜币；铜制器|A chemical element, a reddish-brown metal, that is a good conductor of heat and electricity. It is used in electrical wiring, plumbing, and many other applications.|例句：The statue was made of copper. 这尊雕像是铜制的。|例句：He gave me some coppers for my help. 他给了我一些铜币作为酬谢。|近义词：brass, bronze|反义词：无
