phobia:/ˈfoʊbiə/| n.|恐惧症，憎恶|An extreme or irrational fear of or aversion to something.|例句：She has a phobia of spiders. 她有蜘蛛恐惧症。|近义词：fear, dread, terror|反义词：love, affection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a phobia of snakes. = 他有蛇类恐惧症。
She suffers from a phobia of heights. = 她患有恐高症。


一般过去时:
I overcame my phobia of flying. = 我克服了我的飞行恐惧症。
He didn't have a phobia of water as a child. = 他小时候没有恐水症。


现在完成时:
She has developed a phobia of clowns. = 她已经发展出对小丑的恐惧症。
I have had this phobia since childhood. = 我从小就有这种恐惧症。


过去完成时:
He had already conquered his phobia before the trip. = 在旅行之前他已经克服了他的恐惧症。
She hadn't realised the severity of her phobia until the incident. = 直到那件事发生，她才意识到自己恐惧症的严重性。


将来时:
He will seek therapy to treat his phobia. = 他将寻求治疗来治疗他的恐惧症。
She will likely still have this phobia in the future. = 她将来很可能仍然会有这种恐惧症。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have this phobia, I would travel more. = 如果我没有这种恐惧症，我会旅行更多。
I wish I didn't have a phobia of public speaking. = 我希望我没有公开演讲恐惧症。


被动语态:
Phobias are often treated with therapy. = 恐惧症通常用治疗来治疗。
His phobia was successfully treated. = 他的恐惧症得到了成功的治疗。


疑问句:
Do you have any phobias? = 你有任何恐惧症吗？
What kind of phobia does she have? = 她有什么样的恐惧症？


否定句:
He doesn't have a phobia of dogs. = 他没有犬类恐惧症。
She doesn't suffer from any phobias. = 她没有患有任何恐惧症。
