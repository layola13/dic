pulsive:/ˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|冲动的，易怒的|Impulsive describes someone who acts on sudden urges or feelings, without thinking about the consequences.  It suggests a lack of forethought and control over one's actions.|例句：He made an impulsive decision to quit his job. 他冲动地决定辞职。|例句：Her impulsive nature often got her into trouble. 她的冲动性格常常让她惹上麻烦。|近义词：impatient, rash, impetuous, hasty|反义词：deliberate, thoughtful, cautious, premeditated
