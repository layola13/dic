photophysical:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈfɪzɪkəl/| adj.|光物理的；光物理学的| Relating to the interaction of light with matter, especially the absorption and emission of light by molecules.  This includes processes such as fluorescence, phosphorescence, and absorption.  The study of photophysical processes is crucial in many fields, including chemistry, physics, and materials science.|The photophysical properties of the dye were carefully studied.|近义词：optical, light-related|反义词：N/A
