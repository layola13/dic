jerks:/dʒɜːrks/| n., v.| 笨蛋；急促地拉动；抽动；| A sudden, sharp movement; an abrupt, unpleasant person;  a series of short, quick movements.|例句：He jerked his head towards the door. 他猛地把头转向门。|近义词：idiot, fool, lout, spasm, twitch|反义词：genius, smooth, gentle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often jerks his head when he's nervous. = 他紧张的时候经常猛甩头。
The engine jerks violently when starting. = 发动机启动时剧烈抖动。


一般过去时:
She jerked the door open. = 她猛地拉开了门。
The car jerked to a stop. = 车子猛地停了下来。


现在完成时:
The dog has jerked its leash several times. = 狗已经拉扯了几次它的牵引绳。
He has jerked his knee in the past, causing pain. = 他以前膝盖抽筋过，导致疼痛。


过去完成时:
He had jerked the steering wheel, narrowly avoiding an accident. = 他猛打方向盘，才险险避免了一场事故。
The machine had jerked violently before it finally stopped. = 机器剧烈抖动过之后才最终停止。


将来时:
She will jerk the rope to signal the start of the race. = 她将猛拉绳子以发出比赛开始的信号。
The train will jerk violently as it slows down. = 火车减速时会剧烈抖动。


虚拟语气:
If he jerked the lever, the machine would start. = 如果他拉动操纵杆，机器就会启动。
I wish I hadn't jerked my arm so suddenly. = 我真希望我没有那么突然地猛甩胳膊。


被动语态:
The rope was jerked violently by the strong wind. = 绳子被强风猛烈地拉扯。
The car was jerked forward by the sudden acceleration. = 汽车由于突然加速而猛地向前冲去。


疑问句:
Did he jerk the door open? = 他是不是猛地拉开了门？
Why did the car jerk so suddenly? = 为什么汽车会突然猛冲？


否定句:
He didn't jerk the handle, he turned it smoothly. = 他没有猛拉把手，而是平稳地转动它。
The machine didn't jerk at all during the test. = 机器在测试过程中一点也没有抖动。


PHRASE->
jerk around: 愚弄；戏弄
jerk off: 自慰
jerk someone around: 随意对待某人
jerk reaction: 突然反应
a real jerk: 一个真正的笨蛋
