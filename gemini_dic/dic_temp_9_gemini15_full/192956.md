seceding:/səˈsiːdɪŋ/| v.|脱离，退出，分裂|The act of withdrawing formally from membership of a federation or body, especially a political state.|例句：Several states were seceding from the Union.  几个州正在脱离联邦。|例句：The southern states seceded from the United States in 1861.南方各州于1861年脱离美利坚合众国。|近义词：withdrawing, separating, breaking away|反义词：uniting, joining, merging|动词
