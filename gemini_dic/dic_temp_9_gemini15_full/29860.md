causeless:/ˈkɔːzləs/| adj. |无原因的，无缘无故的 |Without a cause; having no cause or reason.  |例句：His anger seemed causeless. 他的愤怒似乎毫无缘由。|例句：The accident was causeless. 事故是无缘无故发生的。|近义词：groundless, baseless, unfounded, motiveless |反义词：caused, reasoned, justified


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  There are no tenses or voices to consider.  Its usage is primarily in descriptive sentences.

Example Sentences illustrating different grammatical structures:

Declarative Sentence:
The causeless panic spread quickly through the crowd. = 无缘无故的恐慌迅速蔓延到人群中。

Interrogative Sentence:
Was his outburst causeless, or was there a hidden reason? = 他的爆发是无缘无故的，还是有隐藏的原因？

There are no common set phrases directly using "causeless".  The word is generally used as a standalone adjective.
