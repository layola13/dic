confusely:/kənˈfjuːzli/ | adv. | 困惑地；混乱地 | In a confused manner; haphazardly.  The instructions were written confusely, leading to much frustration. | 近义词：chaotically, haphazardly, disjointedly | 反义词：clearly, orderly, systematically


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses.  Its usage is primarily descriptive, modifying verbs or entire clauses.

Example 1: He spoke confusely, and I couldn't understand him. 他说话含糊不清，我听不懂。

Example 2: The papers were piled confusely on the desk. 文件杂乱无章地堆在桌子上。


PHRASE->
There are no common multi-word phrases that include "confusely".  Its usage is typically standalone.
