cracklings:/ˈkræk.lɪŋz/ | n. |油炸猪皮渣，爆裂声 | The crispy, crunchy remnants of pork rind after rendering the fat.  They are often used as a snack or added to dishes for added texture and flavor. | Example: The cracklings were incredibly crispy and flavorful, a perfect accompaniment to the roast pork.  油炸猪皮渣又脆又香，是烤猪肉的完美搭配。  The fire crackled merrily in the hearth.  壁炉里的火发出愉快的噼啪声。|近义词：pork scratchings, crackling bits, crispies |反义词：soggy, soft
