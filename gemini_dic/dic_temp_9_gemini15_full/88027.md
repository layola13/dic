It is usually served in a tall glass filled with ice.|例句：He ordered a whiskey highball. 他点了一杯威士忌高球。  She prefers a vodka highball with lime. 她更喜欢加青柠的伏特加高球。|近义词：long drink, mixed drink|反义词：shot, cocktail (in the sense of a shorter, more complex drink)|名词
