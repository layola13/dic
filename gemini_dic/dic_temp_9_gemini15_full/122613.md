moderns:/ˈmɒdənz/| /ˈmɑːdərnz/ | n. |现代人；现代事物|Modern people, things, or ideas that are characteristic of the present time.  The term often implies a contrast with older, traditional styles or methods.|例句：The museum showcases the works of several modern artists. = 这家博物馆展出了几位现代艺术家的作品。

例句：Modern technology has revolutionized communication. = 现代科技彻底改变了通信方式。


近义词：contemporaries, current, present-day | 反义词：ancients, traditional, classical | 词性:n.
