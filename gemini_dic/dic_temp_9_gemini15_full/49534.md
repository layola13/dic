demarcator:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪtər/| n. |界标，分界线，分隔物 |A demarcator is something that marks or indicates a boundary or limit.  It can be a physical object or an abstract concept that distinguishes one area or thing from another. |例句：The river serves as a natural demarcator between the two countries.  这条河充当了这两个国家之间的天然分界线。

例句：The fence acts as a demarcator between the properties. 这道栅栏充当了房产之间的分界线。


近义词：boundary marker, divider, separator, delimiter


反义词：unifier, connector, joiner


词性:名词
