listens:/ˈlɪsnz/| v.|倾听，聆听 |To pay attention to someone or something in order to hear what is being said or played.|例句：She listens attentively to her teacher. 她专心听老师讲课。|例句：He listens to music every evening. 他每天晚上都听音乐。|近义词：hears, attends to|反义词：ignores, disregards


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He listens to the radio every morning. = 他每天早上都听收音机。
She listens carefully during lectures. = 她在讲座期间认真倾听。

一般过去时:
I listened to the birds singing. = 我听了鸟儿的歌声。
They listened intently to the speaker. = 他们专心致志地听着演讲者。

现在完成时:
I have listened to that song many times. = 我已经听过那首歌很多次了。
She has listened to all of his albums. = 她听过他所有的专辑。

过去完成时:
I had listened to the news before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经听过新闻了。
They had listened to the entire concert before leaving. = 他们离开之前已经听完了整场音乐会。

将来时:
I will listen to your advice. = 我会听你的建议。
She will listen to the new album tonight. = 她今晚将收听新专辑。

虚拟语气:
If I listened more carefully, I would understand better. = 如果我更仔细地听，我会理解得更好。
I wish I had listened to my parents' advice. = 我真希望我听了父母的建议。

被动语态:  (Listen is not usually used in the passive voice)  The passive voice is not commonly used with the verb "listen."

疑问句:
Do you listen to podcasts? = 你听播客吗？
Does he listen to classical music? = 他听古典音乐吗？

否定句:
I didn't listen to what he said. = 我没听他说什么。
She doesn't listen to gossip. = 她不听闲话。


PHRASE->
listen to = 倾听
listen up = 注意听

listen in = 偷听
lend an ear = 倾听（侧重于给予帮助）
