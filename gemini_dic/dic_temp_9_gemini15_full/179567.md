reembarked:/ˌriːɪmˈbɑːrkt/ | v. |重新登船；重新上船 | To embark again; to go aboard a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle after having disembarked. |例句：After a brief stop in port, the passengers reembarked on the cruise ship.  重新在港口短暂停留后，乘客们重新登上了游轮。

例句：The soldiers reembarked their vessel after completing their shore leave. 士兵们完成岸上休假后重新登上了他们的船只。


近义词：reboard, go aboard again | 反义词：disembark, leave | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reembarks every time he visits the island. 他每次访问岛屿都会重新登船。
They reembark the ship at dawn. 他们在黎明时重新登船。


一般过去时:
She reembarked the train after a short break. 她短暂休息后重新登上火车。
The crew reembarked the ship after the storm subsided.  暴风雨平息后，船员们重新登上了船。


现在完成时:
They have reembarked on their journey after a short delay. 他们经过短暂延误后重新开始了旅程。
We have reembarked on the project after a lengthy hiatus. 我们在长时间中断后重新开始了这个项目。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
