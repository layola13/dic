hexametaphosphate:/ˌhɛksəˈmɛtəfɒsfeɪt/| n. |六偏磷酸盐|A salt of hexametaphosphoric acid, a polymeric form of phosphoric acid.  It's used as a food additive (e.g., in processed meats to retain moisture), a water softener, and in various industrial applications. |Example:  The processed cheese contained hexametaphosphate as a preservative.  这种加工过的奶酪含有六偏磷酸盐作为防腐剂。|近义词:sodium hexametaphosphate,  (depending on the specific salt)|反义词:None|名词
