melon: /ˈmɛlən/ | n. |甜瓜；瓜类水果 | A sweet, juicy fruit with a thick rind and usually many seeds.  Melons are typically round or oblong and come in various sizes and colors, including watermelon, cantaloupe, honeydew, and muskmelon. | 例句：I bought a delicious watermelon at the farmer's market. 我在农贸市场买了一个美味的西瓜。

例句：She cut the cantaloupe into small pieces. 她把哈密瓜切成小块。


近义词：cantaloupe, watermelon, honeydew, muskmelon | 反义词：None (no direct antonym) | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "melon" ultimately derives from the Greek word "mêlōn" (μῆλον), meaning "apple."  Over time, through Latin ("melo") and various other languages, the word evolved to its current form, but it retains a connection to the original sense of a round, juicy fruit.
