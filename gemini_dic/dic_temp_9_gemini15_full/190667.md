sauerkrauts:/ˈsaʊərkraʊts/| n. |酸菜(复数)| Sauerkraut is a traditional fermented cabbage dish.  It's typically made by finely shredding cabbage, adding salt, and allowing it to ferment naturally. The resulting dish has a distinctive sour taste and is often served as a side dish or ingredient in other recipes. |The restaurant serves sauerkrauts as a side with their sausages.|近义词：fermented cabbage, pickled cabbage|反义词：sweet cabbage
