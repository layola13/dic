recidivistic:/ˌrɛsɪˈdɪvɪstɪk/| adj.|累犯的；惯犯的；反复犯错的|Relating to or characteristic of recidivism; tending to relapse into criminal behavior.  The study focused on recidivistic tendencies among young offenders.|近义词：relapsed, repeat, habitual|反义词：reformed, rehabilitated


例句:

He displayed recidivistic behavior, repeatedly violating his parole. = 他表现出累犯行为，多次违反假释。

The program aims to reduce recidivistic rates among released prisoners. = 该项目旨在降低释放囚犯的累犯率。


词源:

From recidivism + -istic.  Recidivism itself comes from the Latin verb *recidīvēre* "to fall back, relapse," from *re-* "back" + *cadere* "to fall."
