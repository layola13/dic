continues:/kənˈtɪnjuːz/ | v. |继续，持续 |To carry on or persist in an action or state.  |例句：The rain continues to fall. 雨还在下。|例句：The meeting continues for another hour. 会议还要持续一个小时。|近义词：proceeds, persists, endures |反义词：stops, ceases, ends |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The show continues every night. = 演出每晚继续上演。
He continues to work hard. = 他继续努力工作。

一般过去时:
The music continued until midnight. = 音乐持续到午夜。
She continued to read after dinner. = 她晚饭后继续看书。

现在完成时:
It has continued to rain all day. = 一整天都在下雨。
They have continued their research for years. = 他们多年来一直在进行他们的研究。

过去完成时:
The rain had continued for hours before it finally stopped. = 雨下了好几个小时才最终停下来。
He had continued to work even though he was tired. = 即使他很累，他也继续工作。

将来时:
The concert will continue after the intermission. = 音乐会将在中场休息后继续。
We will continue our journey tomorrow. = 我们明天将继续我们的旅程。

虚拟语气:
If he continued to work hard, he would succeed. = 如果他继续努力工作，他就会成功。
I wish the party continued longer. = 我希望派对能持续更久。

被动语态:
The project will be continued next year. = 该项目将于明年继续进行。
The meeting was continued after a short break. = 会议在短暂休息后继续进行。

疑问句:
Does the movie continue after the commercial break? = 电影在商业广告后还会继续吗？
Will the game continue even if it rains? = 即使下雨，比赛还会继续吗？

否定句:
The show did not continue after the first act. = 演出在第一幕后没有继续。
He did not continue his studies after graduation. = 他毕业后没有继续学习。


PHRASE->
continue to do sth. = 继续做某事

continue as = 继续担任
