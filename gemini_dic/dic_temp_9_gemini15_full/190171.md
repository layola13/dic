sanserifs:/ˈsæn(t)səˌrɪfs/| n.|无衬线字体|Sans-serif typefaces are characterized by the absence of small decorative strokes at the ends of letters.  They are often used in digital contexts due to their clean and modern appearance.|例句：The website uses a sans-serif font for its body text.  网站正文使用无衬线字体。|近义词：plain type, clean type|反义词：serif|名词
