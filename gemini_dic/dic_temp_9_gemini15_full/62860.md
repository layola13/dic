enjoying:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪɪŋ/ | v. | 享受；喜爱；玩得开心 |  The present participle of "enjoy". It describes the act of experiencing pleasure or satisfaction. | 例句：I am enjoying the beautiful scenery. = 我正在享受美丽的景色。

I'm enjoying myself immensely at the party. = 我在派对上玩得很开心。


近义词：savoring, relishing, appreciating | 反义词：disliking, hating, loathing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is enjoying his retirement. = 他正在享受他的退休生活。
They are enjoying the concert. = 他们正在享受音乐会。

一般过去时:
She enjoyed the movie last night. = 她昨晚很喜欢那部电影。
We enjoyed our vacation in Hawaii. = 我们在夏威夷的假期过得很愉快。

现在完成时:
I have enjoyed working here for many years. = 我在这里工作多年，一直很享受。
They have enjoyed many successes in their careers. = 他们在事业上取得了许多成功，并从中受益匪浅。

过去完成时:
She had enjoyed her time in college before she graduated. = 她在大学毕业前度过了愉快的时光。
He had enjoyed learning to play the guitar before he got too busy. = 他在太忙之前，曾很享受学习吉他的过程。

将来时:
We will be enjoying the beach next week. = 我们下周将在海滩上玩得开心。
They will enjoy the benefits of the new program soon. = 他们很快就会享受到新项目的益处。

虚拟语气:
If I had more free time, I would be enjoying hobbies more. = 如果我有更多空闲时间，我会更享受我的爱好。
I wish I were enjoying this meeting more. = 我希望我能更享受这次会议。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)  The concert is being enjoyed by everyone. = 音乐会正受到大家的喜爱。
The beautiful scenery is being enjoyed by the tourists. = 美丽的风景正受到游客们的喜爱。

疑问句:
Are you enjoying the food? = 你喜欢这食物吗？
Is she enjoying her new job? = 她喜欢她的新工作吗？

否定句:
I am not enjoying this weather. = 我不喜欢这个天气。
He wasn't enjoying the party very much. = 他不太享受这个派对。


PHRASE->
enjoy oneself = 玩得开心
enjoy doing sth = 喜欢做某事
enjoy the moment = 享受当下
enjoy a good reputation = 享有盛誉
enjoy good health = 保持健康
