parlourmaid: /ˈpɑːləmeɪd/ | n. | 女仆（尤指负责客厅的） | A female servant, especially one responsible for the parlor or sitting room.  | The parlourmaid brought in the tea.  女仆端来了茶。 The wealthy family employed several parlourmaids.  那个富裕的家庭雇佣了好几个女仆。 | maidservant, servant |  (无明显反义词，可考虑"mistress"，但语境不同) | 名词
