oarless:/ˈɔːrləs/| adj.|无桨的，无船桨的 |Without oars; lacking oars.  The term describes a boat or other watercraft that is without oars, implying a lack of means for propulsion by rowing. |例句：The oarless boat drifted helplessly on the current.  无桨的小船在水流中无助地漂流。  The oarless raft was at the mercy of the wind and waves.  无桨的木筏任凭风浪摆布。|近义词：rowless, unpowered|反义词：oared
