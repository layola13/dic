corolliflorous:/ˌkɒrɒˈlɪflərəs/ | adj. | 具有花冠的；花冠状的 | Having a corolla; relating to or characterized by a corolla.  The term refers to plants where the petals are fused together to form a distinct corolla, a characteristic feature of many flowering plants. | Example:  Many corolliflorous plants attract pollinators with their brightly colored corollas.  许多具有花冠的植物用它们鲜艳的花冠吸引传粉者。 | 近义词：petaloid (referring to petal-like structures) | 反义词：apetalous (lacking petals)
