compaternity:/ˌkɒmpəˈtɜːnɪti/| n. |共同父权；共同父性；兄弟情谊|The word "compaternity" refers to the relationship between people who share a common father, or more broadly, a close bond of brotherhood or shared fatherhood. It suggests a sense of kinship and shared experience stemming from a common paternal lineage.|例句：The study explored the concept of compaternity within the context of ancient tribal societies. 这项研究探讨了古代部落社会中共同父权的概念。|近义词：fraternity, brotherhood, paternity|反义词：estrangement, alienation
