facia-board:/ˈfeɪʃəbɔːrd/| n. |装饰板，檐板|A facia board is a flat, horizontal board that forms the outermost part of a building's eaves or other structural features.  It's often decorative and protects the underlying structure from the elements.  It can be made of various materials, including wood, vinyl, or metal.|The house needed new facia boards to improve its appearance and protect it from the weather.  = 这房子需要新的檐板来改善外观并保护它免受风雨侵蚀。 The builder installed the facia boards carefully to ensure a neat finish. = 建造者仔细安装了檐板以确保整洁的表面。|soffit, trim board|none|名词
