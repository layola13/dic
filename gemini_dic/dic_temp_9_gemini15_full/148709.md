overtook:/ˌoʊvərˈtuːk/|/ˈoʊvəˌtʊk/| vt. |超过；赶上；突然袭击|To go past something or someone that is moving more slowly than you are.  It also implies surpassing in some measure. |例句：The fast car overtook the slower one.  = 快车超过了慢车。


The police overtook the criminals' car. = 警察超过了罪犯的汽车。(这里也暗示着抓捕)

近义词：pass, surpass, outstrip, outpace |反义词：be overtaken by, lag behind


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He overtakes me every day on my way to work. = 他每天上班路上都超过我。
The new technology overtakes previous methods. = 新技术超越了以前的方法。

一般过去时:
I overtook the other runners in the last lap. = 我在最后一圈超过了其他跑步者。
She overtook him in the race. = 她在比赛中超过了他。

现在完成时:
We have overtaken our competitors in market share. = 我们在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。
The company has overtaken its rivals in sales. = 这家公司在销售额上已经超过了竞争对手。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, he had already overtaken me in the race. = 当我到达时，他已经在比赛中超过我了。
They had overtaken the leading team before halftime. = 在中场休息之前，他们已经超过了领先的队伍。

将来时:
I think she will overtake him soon. = 我认为她很快就会超过他。
The new model will overtake the old one in popularity. = 新型号的受欢迎程度将超过旧型号。

虚拟语气:
If he had tried harder, he might have overtaken his opponent. = 如果他更努力一些，他可能就超过了他的对手。
I wish I had overtaken that car earlier. = 我真希望我早点超过那辆车。

被动语态:
He was overtaken by a faster car. = 他被一辆更快的车超过了。
Our team was overtaken in the final minutes of the game. = 我们的队伍在比赛的最后几分钟被超越了。

疑问句:
Did you overtake that truck on the highway? = 你在高速公路上超车了吗？
Has she ever overtaken a world-record holder? = 她曾经超过过世界纪录保持者吗？

否定句:
I didn't overtake him because the road was too narrow. = 我没有超过他，因为路太窄了。
He couldn't overtake the leading cyclist. = 他无法超过领先的自行车选手。


PHRASE->
overtake an opponent = 超过对手
overtake the lead = 取得领先
overtake a crisis = 度过危机
overtake by surprise = 突然袭击
overtake the market = 占据市场
