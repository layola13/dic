couple:/ˈkʌpəl/ | n. |一对，夫妇，少量 | A couple refers to two things or people considered as a unit or pair.  It can also mean a small number, roughly two or three. |例句：He and his wife are a lovely couple. 他和他妻子是一对可爱的夫妇。

例句：I need a couple of days to finish this project. 我需要几天时间来完成这个项目。


近义词：pair, duo, few | 反义词：many, numerous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A couple of birds sing in the tree. = 几只鸟在树上唱歌。
We need a couple of hours to get there. = 我们需要几个小时才能到达那里。

一般过去时:
A couple of students were late for class. = 有几个学生上课迟到了。
They bought a couple of apples at the market. = 他们在市场上买了几个苹果。

现在完成时:
I've met a couple of interesting people this week. = 这周我遇到了一些有趣的人。
We've had a couple of problems with the new software. = 我们在使用新软件时遇到了一些问题。

过去完成时:
They had already had a couple of drinks before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经喝了几杯酒了。
She had seen a couple of movies before choosing this one. = 她看过几部电影后才选择这部。

将来时:
We will need a couple of extra hands to finish the job. = 我们需要多几个人来完成这项工作。
I will meet a couple of clients tomorrow. = 我明天要见几个客户。

虚拟语气:
If I had a couple of extra dollars, I would buy that book. = 如果我有几块钱，我会买那本书。
I wish I had a couple of days off. = 我希望我有几天假。

被动语态:
A couple of houses were damaged in the storm. = 几座房子在暴风雨中受损。
A couple of questions were left unanswered. = 有几个问题没有得到解答。

疑问句:
Do you have a couple of minutes to talk? = 你有几分钟时间谈谈吗？
Would you like a couple of cookies? = 你想吃几块饼干吗？

否定句:
There weren't a couple of chairs available. = 没有几把椅子可用。
I haven't seen a couple of my friends lately. = 我最近没见过几个朋友。



PHRASE->
a couple of = 一对；几个
couple with = 加上；与...结合
couple up = 配对；结合
a young couple = 一对年轻夫妇
married couple = 夫妇


词源:
The word "couple" originates from the Old French word "cupel," meaning "pair" or "two."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "copula," meaning "a bond" or "a link."  The sense of "a pair" developed from the idea of two things being linked or joined together.
