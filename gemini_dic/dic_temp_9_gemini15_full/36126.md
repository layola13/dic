coessentiality: /ˌkoʊɛsɛnʃiˈæləti/ | n. | 共本质性，共存性 | The inherent and inseparable connection between two or more things;  the state of being coessential.  It often implies a necessary relationship where one thing cannot exist without the other. | Example: The coessentiality of faith and works in Christian theology is a subject of ongoing debate.  基督教神学中信仰和行为的共本质性是一个持续争论的话题。| 近义词：interdependence, inseparability, intrinsic connection | 反义词：independence, separability, unrelatedness | 名词
