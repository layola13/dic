sermon:/ˈsɜːrmən/| n. |布道；说教；冗长乏味的训斥 | A sermon is a religious discourse delivered as part of a worship service. It typically involves interpretation of scripture and application of religious teachings to daily life.  | The priest gave a powerful sermon on forgiveness.  牧师作了一篇关于宽恕的有力布道。| lecture, address, preachment | praise, compliment | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pastor delivers a sermon every Sunday. = 牧师每周日都会发表一篇布道。
He gives inspiring sermons. = 他发表令人振奋的布道。

一般过去时:
The preacher gave a long sermon yesterday. = 传道者昨天发表了一篇冗长的布道。

现在完成时:
