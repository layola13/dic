hall:/hɔːl/| /hɑːl/ | n. |大厅，走廊，会堂|A large room or area, often used for meetings, ceremonies, or entertainments.  It can also refer to a corridor or passageway.|例句：The school hall was decorated for the graduation ceremony. 学校大厅为毕业典礼装饰一新。|例句：We walked down the long hall to reach the main office. 我们沿着长长的走廊走到主办公室。|近义词：lobby, auditorium, corridor, passage|反义词：room(in the sense of a small, private room)
