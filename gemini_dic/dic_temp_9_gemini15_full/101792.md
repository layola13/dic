irrevocable: /ɪˈrɛvəkeɪbl/ | adj. | 不可撤销的，不能改变的 |  Irrevocable means incapable of being changed or reversed.  It implies a finality and permanence to a decision or action.  | Example: The judge's decision was irrevocable.  法官的判决是不可撤销的。|近义词：irreversible, final, unchangeable | 反义词：reversible, changeable, alterable

词源:
The word "irrevocable" is derived from the Latin prefix "ir-" (meaning "not") + "revocare" (meaning "to call back" or "to revoke").  Therefore, it literally means "not capable of being called back" or "not capable of being reversed."
