rhizopoda:/raɪˈzɒpədə/| n. |根足纲动物，根足虫|A class of amoeboid protozoa characterized by pseudopodia (false feet) used for locomotion and feeding.  They are typically found in aquatic environments and moist soils.| Example:  Many species of rhizopoda are found in freshwater ponds.  许多根足纲动物物种存在于淡水池塘中。|近义词：amoeba (although amoeba is a genus within Rhizopoda)|反义词：N/A
