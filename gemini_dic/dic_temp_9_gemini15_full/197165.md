shant: /ʃænt/ | v. | 将不，不会 |  A contraction of "shall not," expressing a firm refusal or prohibition. It is primarily used in the first person singular and plural, and is now considered somewhat archaic or dialectal, especially in American English. |  I shan't go to the party. = 我不会去参加派对。  We shan't be late. = 我们不会迟到。 | will not, won't | shall, will | v.
