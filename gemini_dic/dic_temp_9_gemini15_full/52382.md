dicynodont:/ˈdaɪsɪnədɒnt/| n. |二齿兽 |An extinct, quadrupedal, herbivorous or omnivorous therapsid reptile from the Permian and Triassic periods, characterized by a beak-like mouth and two prominent tusks.  |Dicynodonts were among the most successful terrestrial vertebrates of the Permian. |近义词：therapsid (a broader term) |反义词：无
