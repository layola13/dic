half-alike:/ˈhæf əˈlaɪk/| adj. | 半像的，部分相似的 | Partially similar; resembling each other in some respects but not entirely.  | The two paintings are half-alike; they share a similar style but differ in detail. 这两幅画有些相似；它们风格相似，但细节不同。 | similar, alike, comparable | completely different, dissimilar, unlike | 形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  The usage is straightforward: it describes a degree of similarity.  Examples will show its use in different sentence structures.

肯定句:
The twins are half-alike. = 这对双胞胎长得有点像。
The two proposals are half-alike in their approaches. = 这两个提案在方法上有些相似。


否定句:
The brothers are not half-alike in personality. = 这两个兄弟性格并不相似。
The two designs are definitely not half-alike. = 这两个设计完全不像。


疑问句:
Are the two songs half-alike? = 这两首歌相似吗？
Are these two strategies half-alike in their execution? = 这两种策略在执行上相似吗？


比较句:
While not identical, the houses are half-alike in architecture. = 虽然不完全相同，但这两座房子的建筑风格有些相似。
Compared to the original, the copy is half-alike at best. = 与原件相比，复制品充其量只是有点像。
