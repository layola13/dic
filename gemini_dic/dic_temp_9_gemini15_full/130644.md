nonadopters:/ˌnɒnəˈdɒptərz/| n. |未采纳者，不采用者 |Those who have not adopted a particular product, technology, or idea.  Nonadopters represent a significant market segment that companies often try to understand and reach. | Example: The marketing team analyzed the characteristics of nonadopters to better target their campaign.  市场团队分析了未采用者的特征，以便更好地定位其宣传活动。|近义词：refusers, resisters, holdouts |反义词：adopters, users
