pocketful: /ˈpɒkɪtfʊl/ | n. | 一口袋的；满满一袋的 |  A pocketful refers to the contents of a pocket, implying a significant or substantial quantity.  It suggests that the pocket is full or nearly full. | 例句：He had a pocketful of marbles. 他口袋里装满了弹珠。

例句：She pulled a pocketful of coins from her bag. 她从包里掏出一把硬币。


近义词：pockets full of, a handful of (if referring to a specific item)


反义词：pocket empty, a few


词性：名词
