restoke:/riːˈstoʊk/| vt.|重新装满；重新供应；重新点燃|To replenish a supply of something; to rekindle or reignite something.|例句：The fireman had to restoke the fire to keep it burning. 消防员不得不重新燃起火来保持燃烧。

例句：We need to restock the shelves before the customers arrive. 我们需要在顾客到达之前重新进货。


近义词：replenish, refill, reignite|反义词：empty, extinguish|动词
