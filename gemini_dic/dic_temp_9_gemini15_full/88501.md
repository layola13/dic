historiography:/ˌhɪstəriˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |历史编纂学；历史写作方法|The study of the methods used by historians in writing history.  Historiography examines how historians select, interpret, and present historical evidence, and how these choices shape our understanding of the past. It also considers the influence of social, political, and intellectual contexts on historical writing. |例句：His dissertation offered a fascinating analysis of the historiography of the French Revolution. 他的论文对法国大革命的历史编纂学进行了引人入胜的分析。|例句：The historiography surrounding the American Civil War is vast and complex.  围绕美国内战的历史编纂学浩瀚而复杂。|近义词：historical writing, history writing, historical method |反义词：N/A
