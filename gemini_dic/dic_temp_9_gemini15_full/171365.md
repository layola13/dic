published:/ˈpʌblɪʃt/ | adj. v. | 出版了的；发表了的；公布的 |  To make (something written or printed) available to the public; to issue (a book, journal, etc.) for sale or distribution.  |  例句：The book was published last year. 这本书去年出版了。
The newspaper published an article about the accident. 报纸发表了一篇关于事故的文章。|近义词：released, issued, announced |反义词：unpublished, suppressed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company publishes many books every year. = 这家公司每年出版很多书。
He publishes his articles online. = 他在网上发表他的文章。


一般过去时:
They published the results of the study. = 他们公布了研究结果。
She published her first novel at the age of 25. = 她25岁时出版了她的第一部小说。


现在完成时:
She has published several articles on this topic. = 她已经发表了几篇关于这个主题的文章。
The government has published a new report on climate change. = 政府已经发布了一份关于气候变化的新报告。


过去完成时:
He had published two books before he turned 30. = 他30岁之前已经出版了两本书。
The author had published several short stories before his novel became a bestseller. = 在他的小说成为畅销书之前，这位作者已经发表了几篇短篇故事。


将来时:
They will publish the next edition of the magazine next month. = 他们下个月将出版该杂志的下一期。
The university will publish the research findings in a peer-reviewed journal. = 大学将在同行评审期刊上发表研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had published my research earlier, I would have received more recognition. = 如果我早点发表我的研究成果，我会得到更多认可。
I wish I had published that article sooner. = 我真希望我早点发表那篇文章。


被动语态:
The article was published in a prestigious journal. = 这篇文章发表在一本著名的期刊上。
The book is being published next spring. = 这本书将于明年春天出版。


疑问句:
Has the journal published the findings yet? = 期刊已经发表了研究结果了吗？
When will the report be published? = 报告什么时候发表？


否定句:
They did not publish the article because of its controversial content. = 由于文章内容具有争议性，他们没有发表它。
The author has not yet published his manuscript. = 作者尚未发表他的手稿。


PHRASE->
published author: 已出版作品的作者
published works: 已发表的作品
widely published: 广泛发表的
recently published: 最近出版的
self-published: 自费出版的
