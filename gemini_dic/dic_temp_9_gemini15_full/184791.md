resurgent: /rɪˈsɜːrdʒənt/ | adj. | 复苏的；再起的；重新兴起的 |  Rising again after a period of decline or inactivity; experiencing renewed growth or vigor.  | 例句：The resurgent economy is creating new job opportunities.  = 复苏的经济正在创造新的就业机会。

例句：After a period of decline, the company is experiencing a resurgent period of growth. = 在一段时间的衰退之后，该公司正在经历一个复苏的增长时期。


近义词：reviving, reviving, reborn, renewed, recovering, rallying


反义词：declining, fading, waning, diminishing


词性：形容词
