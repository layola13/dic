peripneumonia: /ˌpɛriˌnuˈmoʊniə/ | n. | 胸膜肺炎 |  Inflammation of the lungs and the pleura (the membrane surrounding the lungs).  It is often caused by bacterial or viral infections.  The term is often used in veterinary medicine, particularly referring to contagious bovine pleuropneumonia (CBPP), a serious disease affecting cattle. | The cattle herd suffered a severe outbreak of peripneumonia.  这群牛遭受了严重的胸膜肺炎疫情。 | pleuropneumonia, lung infection | healthy lungs | 名词
