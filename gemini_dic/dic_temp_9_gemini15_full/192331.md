scribing:/ˈskraɪbɪŋ/| n. v.|刻写；记录；书写；描述|The act of writing or drawing, especially carefully or precisely; the process of making a detailed written or drawn record.  It can also refer to the act of describing something in detail.|例句：The meticulous scribing of the ancient text took years.  古代文本的细致刻写花费了数年时间。

The architect spent hours scribing the building plans. 建筑师花了数小时绘制建筑图纸。|近义词：writing, drawing, recording, documenting, describing|反义词：erasing, deleting


USAGE->

He scribes the ancient texts. 他抄写古代文本。
She scribes notes during the lecture. 她在讲座期间做笔记。


一般过去时:
The monk patiently scribed the scriptures.  僧侣耐心地抄写经文。

过去完成时:
