semi-smile:/ˈsɛmiˌsmaɪl/ | adj. | 微笑的，似笑非笑的表情 |  A semi-smile is a subtle, partial smile; it's not a full, broad smile, but a slight curving of the lips that suggests amusement, politeness, or perhaps a hint of something else.  It can be difficult to interpret the exact emotion behind a semi-smile.  | 例句：She offered a semi-smile, neither fully happy nor unhappy. 她报以似笑非笑的表情，既不完全快乐，也不完全不快乐。

She gave him a semi-smile and a slight nod. 她对他报以一丝微笑和轻轻的点头。
