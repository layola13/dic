forced-feeding: /fɔːrstˈfiːdɪŋ/ | n. | 强迫喂食；强行灌输 | The act of feeding someone against their will, often used to describe the forceful ingestion of food or information.  This can be a violent act, often associated with animal cruelty or abuse of vulnerable populations.  | Example: The farmer was accused of forced-feeding his geese to increase their liver size.  = 这位农民被指控强迫喂食他的鹅以增加它们的肝脏大小。  The propaganda campaign was a form of forced-feeding of nationalist ideas. = 这场宣传活动是对民族主义思想的强行灌输。 | 近义词：compulsory feeding, stuffing, indoctrination | 反义词：voluntary feeding, self-feeding
