messiah:/məˈsaɪə/| n. |弥赛亚，救世主 |A person who is expected to bring salvation to their people.  Often used in a religious context, referring to a divinely appointed leader or savior.  The term is most commonly associated with Jewish and Christian beliefs.|例句：Many Jews await the coming of the Messiah.  许多犹太人期盼弥赛亚的到来。

例句：Christians believe Jesus Christ is the Messiah. 基督徒相信耶稣基督是弥赛亚。


近义词：savior, redeemer, liberator

反义词：destroyer, oppressor


词性:名词
