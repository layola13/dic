self-pointed:/ˌsɛlfˈpɔɪntɪd/| adj. | 自指的，自我指向的 |  Describes something that refers to or is directed towards itself; inherently self-referential.  It often implies a circular or recursive nature.  | 例句：The self-pointed argument led to a logical paradox.  自指的论点导致了逻辑悖论。

The self-pointed criticism was surprisingly effective.  这种自我批评出奇地有效。


近义词：reflexive, self-referential, circular

反义词：external, objective, other-directed


词性:形容词
