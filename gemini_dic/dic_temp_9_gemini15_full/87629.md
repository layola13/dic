hew-out: /hjuːˈaʊt/ | vt. | 砍出；凿出；挖掘出 | To shape or form (something) by cutting or chopping away material.  It often implies a laborious and difficult process. | 例句：The sculptor hewed out a magnificent statue from a block of marble.  雕塑家从一块大理石中凿出一尊宏伟的雕像。

The pioneers hewed out a farm from the wilderness.  拓荒者们在荒野中开辟了一个农场。


近义词：carve, chisel, excavate, fashion


反义词：build, construct, create (in the sense of adding material rather than removing it)


词性：动词
