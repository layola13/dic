kruller:/ˈkrʊlər/| n. |油炸圈饼，荷兰甜甜圈|A kruller is a type of fried dough pastry, often twisted or rolled into a ring shape, and typically coated in sugar or cinnamon sugar.  It is similar to a doughnut but often has a more delicate texture and is less dense. |Example:  The bakery sold delicious, warm krullers every morning.  这家面包店每天早上都卖美味的热油炸圈饼。

The krullers were sprinkled with powdered sugar. 这些油炸圈饼撒上了糖粉。|近义词：doughnut, fritter|反义词：无|名词
