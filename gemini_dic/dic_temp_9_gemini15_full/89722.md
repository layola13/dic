hopeless:/ˈhoʊplɪs/ | adj. | 没有希望的；绝望的；无用的 |  Feeling or causing despair; offering no prospect of success or improvement.  |例句：The situation seemed hopeless.  情况似乎毫无希望。

The patient's condition is hopeless. 病人的病情已无望。


近义词：desperate, despairing, futile, pointless, vain | 反义词：hopeful, promising, encouraging


USAGE->
