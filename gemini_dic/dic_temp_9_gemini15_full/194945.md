semi-monthly:/ˈsɛmiˈmʌnθli/| adj.|半月刊的；每月两次的|occurring twice a month.  The word combines "semi-" (meaning half or partly) with "monthly" (meaning occurring once a month).  It describes something that happens two times per month, typically at equal intervals.|例句：The magazine is published semi-monthly. 这本杂志是半月刊。

The company holds semi-monthly meetings.  这家公司每月举行两次会议。|近义词：bi-weekly (though this can also mean every two weeks)|反义词：daily, weekly, monthly|词性:形容词
