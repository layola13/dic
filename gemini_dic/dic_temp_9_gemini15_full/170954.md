pseudoparasitic:/ˌsuːdoʊˌpærəˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. | 类寄生的；似寄生的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an organism that resembles a parasite in some respects but does not exhibit the full parasitic lifestyle.  Such organisms may derive some benefit from their host but do not necessarily harm it.  They might be commensals or even mutualists, depending on the specific relationship. | Example:  The plant exhibits pseudoparasitic behavior, drawing some nutrients from its neighbor but not causing significant damage. = 这植物表现出类寄生的行为，从其邻居那里吸收一些养分，但不会造成重大损害。  |近义词:  quasi-parasitic, semi-parasitic | 反义词:  fully parasitic, non-parasitic | 词性: adjective
