procollectivistic: /ˌproʊkəˈlɛktɪvɪstɪk/ | adj. | 倾向集体主义的；支持集体主义的 |  Characterized by or advocating for procollectivism; emphasizing the importance of the group over the individual.  This term describes a belief system or ideology that prioritizes the needs and goals of the collective over the individual. It contrasts with individualism, which emphasizes individual rights and autonomy. | Example:  The procollectivistic society prioritized community needs above personal desires.  = 倾向集体主义的社会优先考虑社区需求而不是个人愿望。  | 近义词: collectivist, communitarian | 反义词: individualistic, egoistic |
