reactor:/riˈæktər/| n. |反应堆；反应器；反应物|A reactor is a vessel or apparatus in which a chemical reaction or a nuclear reaction takes place.  It can refer to a device for carrying out controlled nuclear fission, or a container used in chemical processes.|Example: The nuclear reactor generates electricity. 核反应堆发电。|Example: The chemical reactor was carefully monitored. 化学反应器受到了仔细的监控。|近义词：vessel, container, apparatus|反义词：None specifically, depends on context.
