gazeful: /ˈɡeɪzfʊl/ | adj. | 注视的；凝视的；眼神深情的 | Full of gaze; expressing or characterized by a fixed, intent look.  Often implies a thoughtful or dreamy quality. |例句：Her gazeful eyes were fixed on the distant horizon.  她的眼神深情地注视着远方的地平线。

例句：The painting depicted a gazeful figure lost in contemplation.  这幅画描绘了一个眼神深情的人物沉思着。


近义词：pensive, contemplative, dreamy, wistful, thoughtful


反义词：unobservant, unseeing, vacant, blank


词性：形容词
