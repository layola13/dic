The river meanders through the valley. = 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。
The path meanders through the forest. = 这条小路蜿蜒穿过森林。


一般过去时:
The river meandered through the valley. = 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。（过去）
The hikers meandered along the trail. = 徒步旅行者沿着小路漫步。（过去）


现在完成时:
The river has meandered for miles. = 这条河已经蜿蜒了几英里。
The hikers have meandered through the woods for hours. = 徒步旅行者已经在树林里漫步了几个小时。


过去完成时:
The river had meandered for centuries before reaching the sea. = 这条河在到达大海之前已经蜿蜒了几个世纪。
They had meandered through the city before finding the museum. = 他们在找到博物馆之前已经在城市里漫步了很久。


将来时:
The river will meander further downstream. = 这条河将继续蜿蜒向下游。
We will meander along the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海滩上漫步。


虚拟语气:
If the river meandered less, it would be easier to navigate. = 如果这条河不那么蜿蜒，航行就会更容易。
I wish the path meandered less, it's so tiring. = 我希望这条路不那么蜿蜒，太累了。


被动语态:
The course of the river is meandered. = 这条河的河道是蜿蜒的。(被动少用)
The path should not be meandered so much. = 这条路不应该这么蜿蜒。(被动少用)


疑问句:
Does the river meander much? = 这条河蜿蜒得很厉害吗？
Does the trail meander through the mountains? = 这条小路蜿蜒穿过山脉吗？


否定句:
The road doesn't meander; it's straight. = 这条路不蜿蜒；它是笔直的。
The stream doesn't meander like the river. = 这条小溪不像那条河那样蜿蜒。
