counterthrusts:/ˈkaʊntəθrʌsts/| n.|反击，反冲|A counterthrust is a forceful action taken in response to an attack or challenge; a strong counterattack or reaction.  It often implies a reciprocal force, pushing back against an initial force. |例句：The army launched a series of counterthrusts to regain lost territory.  军队发动了一系列反击以夺回失地。
The company's counterthrusts to the competitor's new product line were successful.  公司针对竞争对手新产品线的反击取得了成功。|近义词：counterattack, riposte, retort, retaliation|反义词：submission, surrender, capitulation|名词
