reweaves:/ˌriːˈwiːvz/| vt.|重新编织；重新组合|To weave again; to weave differently or anew; to re-create or reorganize something in a new way.|例句：She reweaves the old tapestry, giving it a new life. 她重新编织了那幅旧挂毯，赋予了它新的生命。|例句：The artist reweaves the narrative, adding new layers of meaning.  艺术家重新编织了叙述，增加了新的意义层面。|近义词：reconstruct, reorganize, revamp|反义词：destroy, dismantle
