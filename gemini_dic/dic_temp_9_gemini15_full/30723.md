cerused:/ˈsɜːrəst/| v. |涂以白铅粉；漂白|To cover (wood, etc.) with a mixture of white lead and oil, to give a smooth, opaque white surface.  This process was traditionally used to imitate the appearance of marble or to protect wood from the elements. |The artist cerused the wooden panel before painting the portrait. 艺术家在画肖像之前用白铅粉涂抹了木板。 The old table had been cerused, giving it a creamy white finish.  旧桌子涂上了白铅粉，呈现出乳白色的外观。|  whiten, bleach | darken, stain |动词
