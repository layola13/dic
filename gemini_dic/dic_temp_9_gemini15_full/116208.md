maslin:/ˈmæzlɪn/| n. |混合物；杂烩；各种水果的混合果酱|A maslin is a mixture of various things, often fruits, nuts, or seeds.  It can refer to a mixed fruit preserve or a general mixture. The term often implies a lack of uniformity or a blend of different ingredients.|The gardener created a colorful maslin of flowers in the border.|近义词：mixture, medley,potpourri |反义词：uniformity, singularity
