hearkened:/ˈhɑːrkənd/| v.|倾听，留心，注意|To listen attentively; to give heed.  The word implies a sense of careful and perhaps prolonged listening, often to something significant or important.|例句：The villagers hearkened to the old woman's tales. 村民们倾听着老妇人的故事。
例句：We hearkened to the speaker's every word. 我们仔细聆听着演讲者的每一个字。|近义词：listen, attend, heed|反义词：ignore, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hearkens to the wise counsel of his elders. 他倾听长辈的明智建议。
They hearken to the warnings of the storm. 他们倾听暴风雨的警告。


一般过去时:
She hearkened to the whispers of the wind. 她倾听着风的低语。
The crowd hearkened to the politician's speech. 人群倾听着政治家的演讲。


现在完成时:
I have hearkened to many stories in my life. 我一生中听过许多故事。
We have hearkened to the pleas of the refugees. 我们已经听取了难民的恳求。


过去完成时:
They had hearkened to the rumors for weeks before taking action.  他们在采取行动之前几周一直在倾听谣言。
She had hearkened to the advice of her mentor. 她已经听取了她导师的建议。


将来时:
We will hearken to the judge's verdict. 我们将倾听法官的判决。
They will hearken to the experts' opinion. 他们将倾听专家的意见。


虚拟语气:
If I had hearkened to his advice, I wouldn't be in this mess. 如果我听了他的建议，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had hearkened to my parents' warnings. 我真希望我听了父母的警告。


被动语态:
His words were hearkened to by all present.  在场的所有人都倾听了他的话。
The warnings were hearkened to, but too late.  警告被听到了，但为时已晚。


疑问句:
Did you hearken to what he said? 你有仔细听他说了什么吗？
Will they hearken to reason? 他们会听取道理吗？


否定句:
They did not hearken to the warnings. 他们没有听取警告。
She did not hearken to the pleas for help. 她没有理会求救的呼喊。


PHRASE->
hearken back to: 回溯到...
hearken unto: 倾听（较为正式）
hearken to the call of: 响应...的号召
hearken to the voice of: 倾听...的声音
give heed to: 注意，留心（近义词组）
