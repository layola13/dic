patronymical:/ˌpæt.rəˈnɪm.ɪ.kəl/| adj.| 来源于父系的名字的；源于父名的 | Relating to or denoting a name derived from that of a father or ancestor.  It often refers to a system of naming where surnames are derived from the father's given name. |  The patronymic system is common in many cultures.  | 近义词：inherited, ancestral | 反义词：matronymic


例句：

Many Irish surnames are patronymic, meaning they are derived from the father's name.
许多爱尔兰姓氏是父系姓氏，这意味着它们来源于父亲的名字。


His patronymic surname, "MacDonald," indicates his descent from Donald.
他的父系姓氏“麦克唐纳”表明他是唐纳德的后裔。
