reconsidering:/ˌriːkənˈsɪdərɪŋ/| v. |重新考虑，再考虑 |To think again about a decision or opinion.  It implies a previous consideration and a renewed evaluation.|例句：I'm reconsidering my decision to move to the city. 我正在重新考虑搬到城市的决定。

例句：After hearing his explanation, she reconsidered her initial judgment.  在听了他的解释后，她重新考虑了她最初的判断。


近义词：rethinking, reviewing, re-evaluating |反义词：confirming, finalizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reconsidering his job offer. 他正在重新考虑他的工作机会。
They are reconsidering their travel plans. 他们正在重新考虑他们的旅行计划。


一般过去时:
She reconsidered her options after the meeting. 会议后，她重新考虑了她的选择。
He reconsidered his statement, realizing it was inaccurate. 他重新考虑了他的说法，意识到它是不准确的。


现在完成时:
I have reconsidered the proposal and have some new suggestions. 我已经重新考虑了这个提案，有一些新的建议。
They have reconsidered their position on the issue. 他们已经重新考虑了他们在这个问题上的立场。


过去完成时:
She had reconsidered her initial offer before making the final decision. 在做出最终决定之前，她已经重新考虑了她最初的提议。
He had reconsidered his plans after receiving the bad news. 在收到坏消息后，他已经重新考虑了他的计划。


将来时:
I will reconsider your request tomorrow. 我明天会重新考虑你的请求。
They will reconsider their decision after further investigation. 他们会在进一步调查后重新考虑他们的决定。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reconsider my approach. 如果我是你，我会重新考虑我的方法。
I wish I had reconsidered my investment sooner. 我真希望我早点重新考虑我的投资。


被动语态:
The decision was being reconsidered by the committee. 委员会正在重新考虑这个决定。
His proposal is being reconsidered by the management. 他的提案正在由管理层重新考虑。


疑问句:
Are you reconsidering your offer? 你在重新考虑你的提议吗？
Is the company reconsidering its policy? 公司正在重新考虑其政策吗？


否定句:
He is not reconsidering his decision. 他没有重新考虑他的决定。
They are not reconsidering their plans. 他们没有重新考虑他们的计划。


PHRASE->

reconsidering the evidence  重新考虑证据
reconsidering the strategy 重新考虑策略
