flocking:/ˈflɒkɪŋ/| /ˈflɑːkɪŋ/| n. v.|成群结队；聚集；大量涌入|The act of moving or gathering together in a large group, especially of birds or animals.  It can also refer to the gathering of people.|例句：Birds were flocking to the lake. 鸟儿成群结队地飞向湖边。

The sheep were flocking together for warmth. 羊群聚集在一起取暖。|近义词：gathering, clustering, swarming, congregating|反义词：scattering, dispersing, separating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Birds flock together for warmth. = 鸟儿们聚集在一起取暖。
People flock to the beach on sunny days. = 晴天人们都涌向海滩。

一般过去时:
A large crowd flocked to the concert. = 大批人群涌向音乐会。
The birds flocked to the newly planted trees. = 鸟儿们飞向新种的树木。

现在完成时:

Many tourists have flocked to this scenic spot. = 许多游客涌向这个风景名胜。

过去完成时:
