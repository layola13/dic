prongbuck:/ˈprɒŋbʌk/| n. |叉角羚|A North American antelope with a forked pronghorn.  It's known for its speed and distinctive horns.|Pronghorn antelopes are incredibly fast runners, able to reach speeds of up to 60 mph. 叉角羚是令人难以置信的快速奔跑者，能够达到每小时60英里的速度。|antelope, gazelle|  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "slow animal" could be considered a contextual antonym)|名词
