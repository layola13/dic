manchu:/ˈmæntʃuː/| n. adj.|满族；满语的；满洲的|Manchu refers to the people of Manchuria, their language, or their culture.  The Manchu were a Tungusic people who conquered China and established the Qing Dynasty.  Their language, also called Manchu, belongs to the Tungusic language family.|例句：The Qing Dynasty was ruled by the Manchu. 清朝是由满族统治的。The Manchu language is now largely extinct. 满语现在基本上已经灭绝了。|近义词：Manchurian|反义词：Han (as an opposing ethnic group)
