misassessment:/ˌmɪsəˈsɛsmənt/| n. |误判，错误评估 |A misjudgment or a wrong evaluation of something.  It implies a failure to accurately understand or appreciate the true nature or significance of something. |例句：The company's misassessment of the market led to significant financial losses. 公司对市场的误判导致了严重的经济损失。

The project's failure was a direct result of a serious misassessment of the risks involved.  项目的失败是直接由于对所涉风险的严重误判造成的。|近义词：misjudgment, error, mistake, miscalculation |反义词：accurate assessment, correct evaluation, sound judgment |名词
