flash-out: /ˈflæʃaʊt/ | vt. | 突然显露；突然爆发；迅速清除 | To suddenly reveal or expose something; to erupt suddenly; to quickly remove or eliminate something. | 例句：The truth flashed out during the interrogation.  审讯中真相大白。| 例句：The anger flashed out unexpectedly. 怒火突然爆发。| 近义词：reveal, expose, erupt, burst forth | 反义词：conceal, suppress, hide
