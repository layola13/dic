intermenstrual: /ˌɪntərˈmɛnstruəl/ | adj. | 月经间的；两次月经之间的 | Relating to the period between menstrual periods.  This adjective describes the time between one menstrual cycle and the next.  It's primarily used in medical contexts. | Example:  She experienced intermenstrual bleeding. 她经历了月经间出血。


近义词: intercyclical (in the context of menstruation)


反义词: menstrual (referring to the period itself)


词性: adjective
