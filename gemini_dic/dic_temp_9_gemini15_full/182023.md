remedied:/ˈrɛmədiːd/| v.| 补救；纠正；治疗 | To have corrected or improved a bad situation or problem.  |例句：The government has promised to remedy the situation. 政府已承诺纠正这种情况。

例句：The mechanic remedied the problem with the car's engine. 技工修理好了汽车发动机的故障。


近义词：repaired, corrected, improved, fixed, resolved

反义词：worsened, aggravated, exacerbated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team remedies problems quickly. = 团队快速解决问题。
She remedies any issues promptly. = 她迅速解决任何问题。


一般过去时:
They remedied the leak in the roof. = 他们修补了屋顶的漏洞。
He remedied the situation with a quick solution. = 他用一个快速的解决方案解决了问题。


现在完成时:
We have remedied the software bugs. = 我们已经修复了软件错误。
The company has remedied the customer complaints. = 公司已经解决了客户的投诉。


过去完成时:
They had remedied the damage before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经修复了损坏。
She had remedied the misunderstanding before it escalated. = 在误会升级之前，她已经解决了误会。


将来时:
We will remedy the situation as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快解决这个问题。
They will remedy the defect in the next update. = 他们将在下一次更新中修复缺陷。


虚拟语气:
If they had remedied the problem earlier, the damage would have been less. = 如果他们早点解决这个问题，损失就会小一些。
I wish they had remedied the situation more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地解决这个问题。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
