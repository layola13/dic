dala-dala:/ˈdɑːlə ˈdɑːlə/| n. |达拉达拉（菲律宾公共汽车）|A dala-dala is a type of public transportation in the Philippines, typically a converted van or bus, often overcrowded and characterized by its vibrant painting and decoration.  It's known for its relatively inexpensive fares and extensive reach, serving as a vital mode of transport, especially in rural areas.| Example: I took a dala-dala to get to the market. 我坐达拉达拉去了市场。|近义词： jeepney (in some contexts)|反义词：private car, taxi|名词
