fakers:/ˈfeɪkərz/| n.|骗子，伪装者|People who pretend to be something they are not; imposters.  They deceive others by presenting a false image or identity.|例句：The fakers were eventually exposed for their lies. 那些骗子最终被揭穿了他们的谎言。
The art world is rife with fakers trying to pass off forgeries as originals. 艺术界充斥着试图将赝品冒充为真品的骗子。|近义词：imposters, deceivers, charlatans, frauds|反义词：truth-tellers, genuine people, authentic individuals|名词
