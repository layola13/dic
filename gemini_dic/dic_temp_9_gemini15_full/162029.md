prays:/preɪz/| v.|祈祷，祈求|To ask God or a god for something; to express one's wishes, hopes, or desires to a divine being.|例句：She prays every night before bed. 她每天晚上睡觉前都会祈祷。|例句：They prayed for rain. 他们祈求下雨。|近义词：supplicates, implores, beseeches|反义词：curses, blasphemes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prays every day. = 他每天祈祷。
She prays for her family's safety. = 她为家人的安全祈祷。


一般过去时:
He prayed before the exam. = 他考试前祈祷了。
They prayed for guidance. = 他们祈祷寻求指引。


现在完成时:
She has prayed for years. = 她祈祷了很多年了。
He has prayed for a miracle. = 他一直在祈祷奇迹发生。


过去完成时:
She had prayed for his recovery before he died. = 他死前，她一直为他康复祈祷。
They had prayed for a better life. = 他们一直祈祷过上更好的生活。


将来时:
I will pray for you. = 我会为你祈祷。
We will pray for peace. = 我们将为和平祈祷。


虚拟语气:
I would pray for a solution if I were in your shoes. = 如果我处在你的位置，我会祈祷能找到解决办法。
If I knew how to pray, I would pray. = 如果我知道怎么祈祷，我会祈祷。


被动语态:
Prayers were said for the victims. = 为受害者进行了祈祷。
A prayer was offered for peace. = 献上了祈求和平的祈祷。


疑问句:
Do you pray regularly? = 你经常祈祷吗？
What do you pray for? = 你祈祷什么？


否定句:
He doesn't pray. = 他不祈祷。
She didn't pray that day. = 她那天没有祈祷。



PHRASE->
pray for = 为...祈祷
pray to = 向...祈祷
say a prayer = 做一个祈祷
pray earnestly = 恳切祈祷
answer prayers = 祈祷得到回应
