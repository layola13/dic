rebuilds:/ˌriːˈbɪldz/ | v. |重建；改建；重组 |To construct or create something again after it has been damaged or destroyed.  It implies a more comprehensive process than simply repairing. |例句：The city is rebuilding after the earthquake.  城市在地震后正在重建。
The company is rebuilding its image after the scandal. 公司在丑闻后正在重建其形象。|近义词：reconstruct, renovate, restore |反义词：destroy, demolish, raze |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rebuild houses after natural disasters. = 他们在自然灾害后重建房屋。
The team rebuilds its strategy every season. = 这个团队每个赛季都会重建其策略。

一般过去时:
He rebuilt his business after bankruptcy. = 他在破产后重建了他的事业。
She rebuilt the engine of her car. = 她重建了她汽车的引擎。

现在完成时:
They have rebuilt the bridge since the flood. = 洪水之后，他们已经重建了这座桥。
The government has rebuilt many schools in the region. = 政府已在该地区重建了许多学校。

过去完成时:
They had rebuilt the damaged section of the road before the race. = 比赛前，他们已经重建了受损的路段。
She had rebuilt her confidence after the setback. = 挫折之后，她已经重建了她的信心。

将来时:
We will rebuild the community center next year. = 我们明年将重建社区中心。
They will rebuild the damaged infrastructure. = 他们将重建受损的基础设施。

虚拟语气:
If we had more funds, we would rebuild the entire facility. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们将重建整个设施。
I wish they would rebuild the old theater. = 我希望他们能重建那家旧剧院。

被动语态:
The old building is being rebuilt. = 老建筑正在被重建。
The damaged roads were rebuilt quickly. = 受损的道路很快就被重建了。

疑问句:
Are they rebuilding the stadium? = 他们正在重建体育馆吗？
Will the houses be rebuilt soon? = 房屋很快就会重建吗？

否定句:
They did not rebuild the damaged building. = 他们没有重建受损的建筑物。
The company will not rebuild its factory. = 公司不会重建它的工厂。
