exits:/ˈɛksɪts/| n. |出口；退场；(动词exit的第三人称单数现在式)|The ways out of a place; the act of leaving a place or situation; the third-person singular present tense of the verb "exit".|例句：The emergency exits are clearly marked.紧急出口标志清晰可见。

There are two exits from the building. 这栋楼有两个出口。


近义词：outputs, ways out, departures |反义词：entries, entrances


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exits the stage. = 他退出了舞台。
The train exits the station. = 火车驶离车站。


一般过去时:
She exited the room quietly. = 她悄悄地离开了房间。
The actor exited stage left. = 演员从舞台左侧退场。


现在完成时:
He has exited the building. = 他已经离开了大楼。
The show has exited its run. = 演出已经结束。


过去完成时:
They had exited the theater before the final scene. = 他们在最后一幕之前就离开了剧院。
The plane had exited the runway. = 飞机已经驶离跑道。


将来时:
She will exit the building shortly. = 她很快就会离开大楼。
The flight will exit the gate in 10 minutes. = 飞机将在十分钟后离开登机口。


虚拟语气:
If he exited now, he would miss the show. = 如果他现在离开，他就会错过演出。
I wish I had exited the meeting earlier. = 我真希望我早点离开会议。


被动语态:
The room is exited through that door. = 房间可以通过那扇门出去。
The building is exited by many people every day. = 每天有很多人离开这栋楼。


疑问句:
Where does he exit? = 他从哪里出去？
How do you exit the game? = 你怎么退出游戏？


否定句:
He didn't exit the room. = 他没有离开房间。
She will not exit the building. = 她不会离开大楼。


PHRASE->
