confirmative: /kənˈfɜːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 确认的；证实的 | Providing confirmation or verification; tending to confirm.  It emphasizes the act of confirming something. |例句：The results of the test provided confirmative evidence of his innocence.  测试结果证实了他的清白。

例句：His testimony offered confirmative details about the crime scene.  他的证词提供了关于犯罪现场的确凿细节。


近义词：confirmatory, corroborative, supportive, validating


反义词：disconfirming, refuting, contradicting, invalidating


词性：形容词
