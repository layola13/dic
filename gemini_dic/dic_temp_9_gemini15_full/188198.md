rowboats:/ˈroʊboʊts/| n. |划艇；小划船|A small boat propelled by oars.  Rowboats are typically used for recreational purposes on lakes, rivers, and calm seas.|例句：We rented a rowboat and spent the afternoon rowing on the lake. 我们租了一艘划艇，在湖上划船度过了下午。|例句：The children enjoyed their rowboat ride on the pond. 孩子们喜欢在池塘里划船。|近义词：dinghy, skiff|反义词：motorboat, sailboat|名词
