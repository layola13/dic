herbivores:/ˈɜːrbɪvɔːrz/|/ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːrz/| n. |食草动物|Animals that feed on plants. Herbivores play a crucial role in the ecosystem by controlling plant populations and transferring energy from plants to other trophic levels.  |Example: Cows, sheep, and deer are all herbivores.  奶牛、绵羊和鹿都是食草动物。

Elephants are large herbivores that inhabit various ecosystems. 大象是居住在各种生态系统中的大型食草动物。|近义词：plant-eaters, grazers |反义词：carnivores, omnivores
