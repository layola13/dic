outwrenched: /ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃt/ | v. | 扭转；用力扭开；强迫(某人)说出 |  To wrench or twist something violently out of shape or from its position; to forcibly extract information or a confession from someone. |


例句：

The door handle was outwrenched from its frame. = 门把手被暴力地从框架上扭了下来。

The police outwrenched a confession from the suspect. = 警察强迫嫌疑人招供。


近义词：wrenched, twisted, forced, extorted


反义词： gently removed, eased, coaxed


词性: 动词
