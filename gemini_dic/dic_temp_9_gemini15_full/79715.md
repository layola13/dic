glucosuria:/ˌɡluːkoʊˈsʊəriə/| n. |糖尿；尿糖症|The presence of glucose in the urine, typically indicating diabetes mellitus or other metabolic disorders. Glucosuria is a condition characterized by abnormally high levels of glucose in the urine.  It is often a symptom of uncontrolled diabetes, where the kidneys are unable to reabsorb all the glucose filtered from the blood. Other causes can include pregnancy, stress, and certain medications.  |Example:  The doctor ordered a urine test to check for glucosuria.医生为了检查尿糖症开了尿检。  The patient's glucosuria was a clear indication of his poorly controlled diabetes. 病人的尿糖症清楚地表明了他的糖尿病控制不佳。|近义词：glycosuria|反义词：No direct antonym, but the absence of glucose in the urine could be described as  "normal urinary glucose levels" or "absence of glucosuria".|名词
