picturing: /ˈpɪktʃərɪŋ/ | v. |描绘，想象，拍摄 | To form a mental image of; imagine.  To represent in a picture; depict. To take a picture or pictures of. |例句：I'm picturing a beautiful beach. 我正在想象一个美丽的沙滩。

I'm picturing myself winning the lottery. 我正在想象自己中了彩票。


近义词：imagining, envisioning, depicting, portraying, photographing | 反义词：ignoring, forgetting, overlooking | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I picture myself as a successful entrepreneur. = 我把自己想象成一个成功的企业家。
She pictures her future with a loving family. = 她想象着自己未来有一个充满爱的家庭。


一般过去时:
He pictured the scene vividly in his mind. = 他在他的脑海中生动地描绘了这个场景。
They pictured the accident happening right before their eyes. = 他们想象到事故就发生在他们眼前。


现在完成时:
I have pictured this moment countless times. = 我无数次地想象过这一刻。
She has pictured her life in a different country many times. = 她多次想象过自己在另一个国家的生活。


过去完成时:
He had pictured a different outcome before the meeting. = 会议之前他已经想象过不同的结果。
They had pictured a peaceful retirement before the unexpected job loss. = 在意外失业之前，他们曾想象过一个平静的退休生活。


将来时:
I will picture myself relaxing on a tropical beach. = 我将想象自己在一个热带海滩上放松。
She will picture the moment she receives her award. = 她将想象自己获得奖项的那一刻。


虚拟语气:
If I pictured my success more clearly, I would work harder. = 如果我能更清晰地想象我的成功，我会更努力工作。
I wish I could picture a better future for my children. = 我希望我能为我的孩子们描绘一个更美好的未来。


被动语态:
The event was pictured in many newspapers. = 许多报纸都刊登了这次活动的图片。
The scene is pictured beautifully in the novel. = 小说中对这个场景的描绘非常美丽。


疑问句:
How do you picture your ideal vacation? = 你如何想象你理想中的假期？
What does she picture when she thinks of happiness? = 当她想到幸福时，她在想象什么？


否定句:
I cannot picture myself living in a big city. = 我无法想象自己住在繁华的大都市。
He didn't picture the consequences of his actions. = 他没有预料到自己行为的后果。
