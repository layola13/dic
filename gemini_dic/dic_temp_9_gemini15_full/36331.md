coifed:/kɔɪft/| adj. v.|梳理头发的；发型精心打理的| having one's hair neatly arranged.  The term often implies a stylish or sophisticated hairstyle.|例句：She had her hair coifed for the wedding.  她为婚礼精心梳理了头发。

例句：The actress arrived with her hair perfectly coifed.  这位女演员到达时，头发梳理得一丝不苟。


近义词：styled, groomed, dressed (hair)
反义词：unkempt, untidy, disheveled

词性:  adj. & v. (过去分词作形容词)
