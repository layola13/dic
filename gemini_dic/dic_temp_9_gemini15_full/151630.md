particle:/ˈpɑːrtɪkəl/|n.|粒子，微粒；极小量；语素|A particle is a minute piece of matter.  It can also refer to a very small amount of something, or a word that has a grammatical function but little or no independent meaning.|例句：Dust particles filled the air.  空气中充满了灰尘颗粒。|例句：He only ate a particle of the cake. 他只吃了一小块蛋糕。|近义词：grain, speck, bit, element|反义词：whole, entirety
