monasticize:/məˈnæstɪsaɪz/| vt. |使过着隐修生活；使具有僧侣的特性|To cause (someone) to adopt a monastic life or way of living; to give (something) the characteristics of a monastery.|The remote island was monasticized by the influx of religious hermits.  偏远的岛屿由于宗教隐士的涌入而变得像修道院一样。|He attempted to monasticize his life after a spiritual awakening. 他在灵性觉醒后试图过上隐修生活。|近义词：secularize(反义词)|动词
