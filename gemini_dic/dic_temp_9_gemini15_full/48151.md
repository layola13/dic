decoloration:/ˌdiːkʌləˈreɪʃən/ | n. |脱色；漂白；褪色 |The removal of color from something.  This can refer to the process of removing color from a substance, such as fabric or hair, or the resulting loss of color.  |例句：The decoloration of the fabric was caused by prolonged sun exposure.  织物的褪色是由于长时间日晒造成的。

The decoloration of the solution indicated the completion of the chemical reaction.  溶液的脱色表明化学反应已完成。


近义词：discoloration, bleaching, fading, paling |反义词：coloration, dyeing
