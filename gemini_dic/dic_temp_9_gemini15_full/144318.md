outsprinting: /ˌaʊtˈsprɪntɪŋ/ | v. | 快速超越；跑赢 |  The act of sprinting faster than someone else; surpassing someone in a sprint.  |  例句：He won the race by outsprinting his opponent in the final stretch. 他在最后冲刺阶段超过对手赢得了比赛。


例句：The cheetah outsprinted the gazelle. 猎豹跑赢了瞪羚。


近义词：outpacing, outrunning, overtaking


反义词：being outsprinted


词性：动词
