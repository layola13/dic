reaper:/ˈriːpər/ | n. | 收割者；死神；收割机 | A person or machine that reaps;  Death personified.  |例句：The farmer used a reaper to harvest his wheat.  = 农民用收割机收割小麦。

例句：The grim reaper came for him unexpectedly. = 死神出乎意料地带走了他。

近义词：harvester, mower, death, grim reaper | 反义词：sower (播种者)
