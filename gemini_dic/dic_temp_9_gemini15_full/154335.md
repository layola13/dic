perjuring:/ˈpɜːrdʒəriŋ/| v.|伪证；作伪证|The act of committing perjury; willfully giving false testimony under oath.|例句：He was accused of perjuring himself in court. 他被指控在法庭上作伪证。|例句：Perjuring oneself in a court of law can lead to serious consequences. 在法庭上作伪证会导致严重的后果。|近义词：lying under oath, giving false testimony, committing perjury|反义词：telling the truth, being truthful
