dnas:/ˈdiːɛneɪz/ | n. | 脱氧核糖核酸 (DNA) | Deoxyribonucleic acid, a self-replicating material present in nearly all living organisms as the main constituent of chromosomes. It is the carrier of genetic information. | Example:  The scientists analyzed the dnas to identify the species. 科学家们分析了DNA来识别物种。 |近义词: genetic material, genome |反义词: None
