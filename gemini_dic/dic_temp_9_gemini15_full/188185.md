roves:/roʊvz/| v.|漫游；巡游；四处搜寻|To travel or wander over an area, especially without a fixed destination.|例句：The lion roves the African savanna.  狮子在非洲大草原上漫游。|例句：The police roved the streets looking for the suspect. 警察在街上四处搜寻嫌疑犯。|近义词：wander, roam, ramble|反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roves the countryside. = 他在乡间漫游。
She roves the city streets at night. = 她晚上在城市街道上漫游。

一般过去时:
They roved through the forest. = 他们漫游在森林里。
The explorers roved across the uncharted territory. = 探险家们穿越了未知的领土。

现在完成时:
The nomads have roved these lands for centuries. = 游牧民族几个世纪以来一直在这些土地上游牧。
She has roved across many countries in search of adventure. = 她为了寻找冒险而游历了许多国家。

过去完成时:
They had roved for days before they found shelter. = 他们流浪了好几天才找到住所。
The cattle had roved freely before the fences were built. = 在围栏建好之前，牛群自由自在地漫游。

将来时:
We will rove the coast this summer. = 今年夏天我们将漫游海岸。
The explorers will rove the Amazon rainforest next year. = 探险家们明年将漫游亚马逊雨林。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would rove the world. = 如果我有时间，我会周游世界。
I wish I could rove the mountains like I used to. = 我真希望我能像以前一样在山里漫游。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The area is rarely roved by tourists. = 这个地区很少有游客光顾。
The land has been roved by countless generations. = 这片土地被无数代人游历过。

疑问句:
Does he often rove the fields? = 他经常在田野里漫游吗？
Will they rove the desert alone? = 他们会独自漫游沙漠吗？

否定句:
He doesn't rove aimlessly; he has a purpose. = 他漫游并非漫无目的；他是有目标的。
They didn't rove far from the village. = 他们没有远离村庄。


PHRASE->
rove about = 漫游，四处游荡
rove at large = 自由漫游
rove around = 到处闲逛
rove over = 巡视，漫游于…之上
rove through = 穿过，漫游于…之中
