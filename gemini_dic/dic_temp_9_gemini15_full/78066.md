It is usually found in hydrothermal veins associated with other lead and antimony sulfides.  The mineral is named after the Greek words "geo" (earth) and "chronos" (time), referring to its association with other minerals that are time markers in geological formations. |Geocronite crystals are often found in clusters and exhibit a dark gray to black color. |近义词：None readily available; it's a specific mineral. |反义词：None directly applicable.
