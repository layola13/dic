screecher:/ˈskriːtʃər/| n. |尖声叫喊者；刺耳的声音；（尤指鸟类的）鸣叫声|A screecher is a person or thing that makes a loud, harsh, high-pitched sound, especially a bird.  It can refer to a person who screams or shouts in a shrill voice, or to a sound itself.|例句：The screecher disturbed the quiet of the night.  尖锐的叫声扰乱了夜晚的宁静。 The old car's brakes made a terrible screeching sound. 那辆旧车的刹车发出可怕的尖锐声响。|近义词：shouter, screamer, shriller|反义词：whisperer, murmurer
