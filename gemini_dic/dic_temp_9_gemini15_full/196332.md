serializing:/ˈsɪəriəlaɪzɪŋ/| v.|序列化，串行化|The process of converting an object into a stream of bytes to store it or transmit it over a network.|例句：We need to serialize the data before sending it to the server. 我们需要在将数据发送到服务器之前对其进行序列化。|近义词：marshaling, encoding|反义词：deserializing
