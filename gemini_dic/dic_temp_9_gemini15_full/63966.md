episodes:/ˈɛpɪsoʊdz/| n.|情节；片段；事件|A series of connected events forming a story; a single event or period of time in a series.|例句：The final episode of the series was very emotional.  这部剧的最后一集非常感人。|例句：Several episodes of violence occurred in the city last week. 上周该市发生了好几起暴力事件。|近义词：installment, incident, occurrence|反义词：conclusion, entirety


USAGE->
