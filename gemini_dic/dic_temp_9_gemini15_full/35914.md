cocreating:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtɪŋ/| v.|共同创造，协同创作|The act of creating something together, typically involving collaboration and shared responsibility.  It emphasizes the joint effort and mutual contribution in the creative process. |例句：We are co-creating a new marketing strategy that incorporates input from all departments. = 我们正在共同制定一个新的营销策略，其中包含来自所有部门的意见。

例句：The artists are co-creating a mural that reflects the community's diverse culture. = 艺术家们正在共同创作一幅反映社区多元文化的壁画。


近义词：collaborating, cooperating, working together, joint creation


反义词：creating independently, working alone, solo creation


词性：动词
