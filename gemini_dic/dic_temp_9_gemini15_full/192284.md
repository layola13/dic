screenwriting:/ˈskriːnˌraɪtɪŋ/| n. |编剧，电影剧本创作|The art and craft of writing scripts for films, television, or other visual media.  It involves creating a story with characters, dialogue, and visual descriptions that will translate effectively to the screen. |Example:  She honed her screenwriting skills by studying classic films and working on independent projects.  她通过学习经典电影和参与独立项目磨练了自己的编剧技巧。|近义词：scriptwriting, screenplay writing|反义词：film directing, film acting
