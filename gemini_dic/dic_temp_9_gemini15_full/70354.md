friendly:/ˈfrɛndli/ | adj. |友好的，友善的 | kind, warm, and pleasant |  例句：He gave me a friendly smile. 他给了我一个友好的微笑。

例句：She was very friendly to everyone. 她对每个人都很友好。


近义词：amiable, cordial, affable | 反义词：unfriendly, hostile, unfriendly, cold


词源:  The word "friendly" originates from the Old English word "frēondlīċ," which combines "frēond" (friend) and the suffix "-līċ" (like, similar to).  This reflects its direct meaning of being "like a friend".
