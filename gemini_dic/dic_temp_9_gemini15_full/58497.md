duelers:/ˈduːələrz/| n. |决斗者，争论者 |People engaged in a duel or argument.  Those who fight a duel are called duelers. They might also be involved in a heated debate or argument. | Example: The two duelers faced each other, pistols drawn.  两个决斗者面对面站着，拔出了手枪。

The duelers engaged in a fierce verbal battle.  这两个争论者进行了一场激烈的言语交锋。|近义词：combatants, antagonists, disputants |反义词：peacemakers, mediators
