firelit:/ˈfaɪərˌlɪt/| adj. |火照亮的，炉火映照的|Lit by fire; illuminated by firelight.  The word combines "fire" and the past participle "lit" (from "light"). It describes a scene or object illuminated by the warm glow of a fire.|例句：The firelit room was cozy and inviting.  火光照亮的房间舒适而温馨。


The firelit hearth provided a warm glow on a cold night.  炉火映照的壁炉在寒冷的夜晚提供了温暖的光芒。|近义词：fire-lit, illuminated, lighted|反义词：dark, shadowed
