proboscidian:/proʊˈbɑːsɪdiən/| n. |长鼻动物；有长鼻的|An animal with a long trunk or proboscis, especially an elephant.|Elephants are proboscidian mammals.|近义词：elephantine (referring to elephants)|反义词：None specifically, but words implying short noses could be considered antonyms in a loose sense.
