re-address:/ˌriːˈædrɛs/| vt.|重新考虑；再次处理；重新写地址；对…再作答复|To consider something again, often with a different approach; to deal with something again; to write a new address on something; to respond to something again.|例句：We need to re-address the issue of funding. 我们需要重新考虑资金问题。例句：The company decided to re-address its marketing strategy.  公司决定重新制定其营销策略。|近义词： reconsider, review, revise, rethink|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-address the problem every week. = 他们每周都重新处理这个问题。
He re-addresses the envelope with the correct address. = 他用正确的地址重新写了信封。


一般过去时:
She re-addressed the letter to the correct recipient. = 她将信件重新寄给了正确的收件人。
The committee re-addressed the concerns of the community. = 委员会再次处理了社区的担忧。


现在完成时:
They have re-addressed the budget several times. = 他们已经几次重新调整了预算。
We have re-addressed the shortcomings of the previous plan. = 我们已经重新解决了先前计划的不足之处。


过去完成时:
He had re-addressed the email before sending it. = 他在发送电子邮件之前已重新编辑了它。
After the review, they had re-addressed the safety concerns. = 审查之后，他们重新考虑了安全问题。


将来时:
We will re-address the issue at the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上重新讨论这个问题。
The company will re-address its customer service policy. = 公司将重新制定其客户服务政策。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-address the report. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新审阅这份报告。
It is vital that we re-address this problem immediately. = 我们必须立即重新解决这个问题至关重要。


被动语态:
The proposal was re-addressed by the board. = 该提案被董事会重新审议。
The concerns of the public were re-addressed by the government. = 公众的担忧得到了政府的重新处理。


疑问句:
Will the company re-address the complaints? = 公司会重新处理这些投诉吗？
When will the matter be re-addressed? = 这件事什么时候会被重新处理？


否定句:
They did not re-address the underlying causes of the problem. = 他们没有重新解决问题的根本原因。
The committee refused to re-address the issue. = 委员会拒绝重新处理这个问题。
