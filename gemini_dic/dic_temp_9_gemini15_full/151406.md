parnassian:/pɑːrˈnæsɪən/| adj. |帕纳萨斯山的；完美的；典雅的；|Relating to or characteristic of Mount Parnassus in Greece, which was associated with the Muses and poetry; hence, suggesting lofty or elevated style, often implying artificiality or prettiness.  It often describes poetry or art that is highly polished and technically skillful but lacks genuine feeling or originality.|例句：His Parnassian poems, while technically brilliant, lacked emotional depth. 他的帕纳萨斯风格的诗歌，虽然技巧精湛，却缺乏情感深度。  例句：The painting, though Parnassian in its meticulous detail, felt somewhat cold and distant. 这幅画，虽然在精细的细节上具有帕纳萨斯风格，但却感觉有些冷淡疏远。|近义词：classical, elegant, refined, highbrow |反义词：vulgar, common, crude, uninspired
