madeleines:/ˈmæd(ə)lɛnz/ | n. | 马德琳蛋糕 | Small shell-shaped sponge cakes, typically made with butter, eggs, flour, and sugar.  They are often baked in special shell-shaped molds. | Example:  She served delicate madeleines with afternoon tea. 她用精致的马德琳蛋糕搭配下午茶。 |近义词：shell-shaped cakes, sponge cakes |反义词：无
