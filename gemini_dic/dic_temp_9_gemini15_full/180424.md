regally:/ˈriːɡəli/| adj.|帝王般地；堂皇地；高贵地 | In a regal manner; in a way that is fit for a king or queen; splendidly; impressively.  |例句：She walked regally down the aisle. 她迈着帝王般的步伐走过过道。

例句：The palace stood regally on the hill. 这座宫殿雄伟地矗立在山上。


近义词：royally, majestically, grandly, splendidly, impressively


反义词：meanly, humbly, shabbily, poorly


词源:  From regal + -ly.  "Regal" itself comes from the Old French *regal*, from Latin *regalis*, from *rex*, "king".  Thus, the suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "regal" into an adverb describing how something is done.
