scalings:/ˈskeɪlɪŋz/ | n. | 比例；缩放；规模；等级 | The process of adjusting the size or scale of something, often in a digital or graphical context.  It can also refer to the relative sizes or proportions of things.  | 例句：The image underwent several scalings before it was deemed suitable for printing. 图片在被认为适合打印之前经过了几次缩放。

The scalings of the map were inaccurate, leading to miscalculations. 地图的比例不准确，导致计算错误。


近义词：sizing, scaling up, scaling down, resizing, proportions | 反义词：None directly opposite, but "uniformity" could be considered a contrasting concept in some contexts. | 词性：名词
