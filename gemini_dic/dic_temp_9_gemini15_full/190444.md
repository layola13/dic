sashimi:/səˈʃiːmi/| n. |生鱼片| Thin slices of raw fish, often served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.  It's a popular dish in Japanese cuisine.|例句：I ordered a platter of sashimi for dinner. 我点了个生鱼片拼盘当晚餐。|例句：The sashimi was incredibly fresh and delicious. 这生鱼片非常新鲜美味。|近义词：raw fish, sushi (though sushi often includes rice)|反义词：cooked fish
