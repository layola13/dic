dromon:/ˈdrɒmən/| n. |轻型战船，快速帆船|A dromon was a type of fast, maneuverable warship used in the Byzantine Empire and other Mediterranean powers from the 7th to the 13th centuries.  It was characterized by its speed,  relatively shallow draft, and often its use of both sails and oars.  Dromons played a crucial role in naval warfare during this period.|The Byzantine dromons were instrumental in maintaining control of the Mediterranean.|近义词：warship, galley|反义词：无
