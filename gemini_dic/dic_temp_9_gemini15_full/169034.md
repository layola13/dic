proglacial: /proʊˈɡleɪʃəl/ | adj. | 冰川前的；冰川作用形成的 | Relating to or denoting the area or processes immediately beyond the limits of a glacier.  This area is often characterized by features shaped by glacial activity, such as meltwater streams, lakes, and deposits of sediment. |


例句：

The proglacial lake is fed by meltwater from the glacier. = 冰川前的湖泊由冰川融水补给。

Proglacial deposits often contain valuable minerals. = 冰川前沉积物通常含有宝贵的矿物。


近义词：glacier-fed, glacial, ice-marginal

反义词：None directly opposite, but words suggesting the opposite of glacial influence, such as "interglacial," could be considered contextual opposites.

词性：形容词
