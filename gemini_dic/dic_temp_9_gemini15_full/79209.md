glandiferous:/ɡlænˈdɪfərəs/| adj. | 结实的；多橡实的；结果实的 | Bearing acorns or nuts; nut-bearing.  The term refers to plants that produce nuts or acorns.  | 例句：The glandiferous oak trees provided ample food for the squirrels.  结实的橡树为松鼠提供了充足的食物。

The glandiferous forest was home to many animals that relied on the nuts for sustenance.  结果实的森林是许多依靠坚果生存的动物的家园。


近义词：nut-bearing, acorn-bearing | 反义词：sterile, barren


词源:  From Latin *glandi- (from glans "acorn") + -ferous ("bearing," "producing").  The word combines the Latin word "glans" (acorn) with the suffix "-ferous," meaning "bearing" or "producing."
