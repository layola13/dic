reconsider:/ˌriːkənˈsɪdə(r)/| vt.|重新考虑，再考虑|To think again about a decision, plan, or belief, often in order to change it.|例句：I urge you to reconsider your decision. 我强烈建议你重新考虑你的决定。|例句：After much deliberation, she decided to reconsider her resignation.经过深思熟虑，她决定重新考虑辞职。|近义词：rethink, review, re-evaluate|反义词：confirm, finalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconsiders his options every day. 他每天都重新考虑他的选择。
They reconsider their strategy regularly. 他们定期重新考虑他们的策略。


一般过去时:
She reconsidered her offer after receiving a better one.  她在收到更好的报价后重新考虑了她的报价。
He reconsidered his plans for the weekend. 他重新考虑了他周末的计划。


现在完成时:
I have reconsidered my position on this matter. 我已经重新考虑了我对这件事的立场。
They have reconsidered their initial proposal. 他们已经重新考虑了他们最初的提议。


过去完成时:
He had reconsidered his decision before he announced it.  在他宣布之前，他已经重新考虑了他的决定。
She had reconsidered her options before making a final choice.  她在做出最终选择之前已经重新考虑了她的选择。


将来时:
I will reconsider my offer if you can provide additional information. 如果你能提供更多信息，我会重新考虑我的提议。
They will reconsider the project next week. 他们下周将重新考虑这个项目。


虚拟语气:
I would reconsider if I had more information. 如果我有更多信息，我会重新考虑。
If I were you, I would reconsider. 如果我是你，我会重新考虑。


被动语态:
The proposal was reconsidered by the committee.  这个提案被委员会重新考虑了。
The decision should be reconsidered. 这个决定应该被重新考虑。


疑问句:
Will you reconsider your decision? 你会重新考虑你的决定吗？
Should we reconsider our approach? 我们应该重新考虑我们的方法吗？


否定句:
He refused to reconsider his stance. 他拒绝重新考虑他的立场。
They will not reconsider their offer. 他们不会重新考虑他们的报价。
