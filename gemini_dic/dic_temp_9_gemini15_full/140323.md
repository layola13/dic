ogres:/ˈoʊɡərz/ | n. |食人魔，怪物 |A mythical creature, typically depicted as a large, hideous, and often cannibalistic being.  They are frequently found in folklore and fantasy stories. |例句：The ogre captured the princess and locked her in his tower. 食人魔抓走了公主，把她锁在塔里。 |例句：Fairy tales often feature brave knights fighting fearsome ogres. 童话故事经常出现勇敢的骑士与可怕的食人魔战斗。|近义词：monster, giant, beast, fiend |反义词：fairy, angel, pixie
