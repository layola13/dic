ligated:/ˈlaɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. v. |结扎的；绑扎的；连接的 | Past participle of "ligate".  It describes something that has been tied or bound off, often in a surgical context.  It can also refer to the act of connecting or binding things together. |

The surgeon ligated the artery to stop the bleeding. = 外科医生结扎动脉以止血。

The two companies are now ligated through a joint venture. = 两家公司现在通过合资企业联系在一起。

反义词：untied, unbound, disconnected, separated
