periled:/ˈperɪld/| adj.|危险的，处于危险中的| exposed to or threatened with danger or harm; endangered.|例句：The hikers were periled by the sudden storm. 登山者遭遇突发暴风雨而陷入危险。|例句：The company's future is periled by the economic downturn. 公司的未来因经济衰退而岌岌可危。|近义词：endangered, threatened, jeopardized|反义词：safe, secure, protected|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship is periled by the storm. = 这艘船正遭受暴风雨的威胁。
His career is periled by his recent mistakes. = 他最近犯的错误危及了他的事业。


一般过去时:
The village was periled by the flood. = 村庄遭受洪水威胁。
Our plans were periled by the unexpected delay. = 意外的延误使我们的计划陷入了危险。


现在完成时:
The environment has been periled by pollution. = 环境已经被污染所威胁。
His reputation has been periled by the scandal. = 他的名誉因丑闻而受损。


过去完成时:
The expedition had been periled by a lack of supplies. = 探险队由于缺乏补给而陷入了危险。
The project had been periled by poor management. = 该项目由于管理不善而岌岌可危。


将来时:
The mission will be periled by the lack of funding. = 这项任务将因缺乏资金而面临危险。
The negotiations will be periled by the mistrust between the parties. = 双方之间的不信任将危及谈判。


虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, his life wouldn't have been periled. = 如果他更小心一些，他的生命就不会有危险。
If the dam were to break, the entire town would be periled. = 如果水坝决堤，整个城镇都将面临危险。


被动语态:
The city was periled by the earthquake. = 城市遭受地震威胁。
His health was periled by his unhealthy lifestyle. = 他不健康的生活方式危及了他的健康。


疑问句:
Is the project periled by the delay? = 项目因为延误而有危险吗？
Are the hikers periled by the wild animals? = 登山者受到野生动物威胁吗？


否定句:
The mission was not periled by the bad weather. = 这项任务没有因为恶劣天气而陷入危险。
His reputation is not periled by the accusations. = 他的名誉没有因为指控而受损。


PHRASE->
seriously periled = 严重受威胁
greatly periled = 极度危险
imminently periled = 迫在眉睫的危险
periled by = 受…威胁
periled situation = 危险的局面
