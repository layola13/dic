prepasting:/ˌpriːˈpeɪstɪŋ/| n. |预先粘贴，预先裱糊|The act of pasting something in advance of its intended use or application.  It often involves preparing materials or surfaces beforehand for a later pasting process. For example, prepasting wallpaper before hanging it. |Example: The prepasting of the wallpaper saved considerable time during the installation.  预先裱糊墙纸节省了安装过程中的大量时间。|近义词：pre-application, preliminary pasting |反义词：post-pasting
