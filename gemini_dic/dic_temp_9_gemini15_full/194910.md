semi-illuminated: /ˌsɛmiɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半照明的，部分照亮的 | Partially illuminated; dimly lit.  The adjective describes a state where light is present but not fully illuminating the subject. It suggests a subdued or incomplete illumination.  | 例句：The semi-illuminated hallway cast long shadows on the walls. = 半明半暗的走廊在墙上投下长长的影子。

The moon cast a semi-illuminated glow on the landscape. = 月亮给风景投下朦胧的光辉。 | 近义词：partially lit, dimly lit, shadowy, penumbral | 反义词：brightly lit, fully illuminated, brilliantly lit
