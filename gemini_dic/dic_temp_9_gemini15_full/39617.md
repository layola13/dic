consented:/kənˈsɛntɪd/ | v. | 同意，准许 | To give permission or agree to something. |例句：She consented to the marriage. 她同意了这桩婚事。|近义词：agreed, approved, permitted |反义词：refused, objected, denied |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consents to the plan. = 他同意这个计划。
They consent to our request. = 他们同意了我们的请求。


一般过去时:
She consented to my proposal. = 她同意了我的建议。
He consented to the terms of the contract. = 他同意了合同的条款。


现在完成时:
They have consented to the changes. = 他们已经同意了这些改变。
She has consented to participate in the study. = 她已经同意参与这项研究。


过去完成时:
He had consented to the deal before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经同意了这笔交易。
They had consented to his request earlier that day. = 他们当天早些时候已经同意了他的请求。


将来时:
She will consent to the interview next week. = 她下周将同意接受采访。
He will consent to the operation if the doctor recommends it. = 如果医生建议，他会同意手术。


虚拟语气:
If she consented, we could proceed. = 如果她同意，我们就可以继续了。
I wish he had consented to my plan. = 我希望他同意我的计划。


被动语态:
The request was consented to by the board. = 董事会同意了这项请求。(较少用被动语态)
His proposal was consented to after much deliberation. = 经过长时间的考虑后，他的提议得到了批准。(较少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Will they consent to our terms? = 他们会同意我们的条件吗？
Did she consent to the operation? = 她同意手术了吗？


否定句:
She did not consent to the search. = 她不同意搜查。
They refused to consent to the agreement. = 他们拒绝同意这项协议。


PHRASE->
consent to = 同意…
give consent = 给予同意
with one's consent = 征得某人的同意
without consent = 未经同意
obtain consent = 获得同意
