fritillary: /ˈfrɪtɪlˌɛəri/ | n. |贝母属植物；棋盘花；（某些蝴蝶的）一种|

英文解析：A fritillary can refer to a plant of the genus Fritillaria, known for their checkered or mottled flowers, or to a butterfly of the family Nymphalidae, often displaying vibrant colors and patterns.  The name likely derives from the checkered pattern resembling a chessboard (fritillus meaning "dice box" in Latin).
