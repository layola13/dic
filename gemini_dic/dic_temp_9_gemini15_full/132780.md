nondualistically:/ˌnɒnˈdʒuːəlɪstɪkli/| adv.|非二元论地，超越二元对立地 |  Adverb describing a way of thinking or acting that does not recognize or rely on dualistic distinctions (such as subject/object, mind/body, good/evil). It emphasizes interconnectedness and unity. | 例句：He approached the problem nondualistically, seeing the seemingly opposing forces as interconnected aspects of a single reality. 他以非二元论的方式处理这个问题，将看似对立的力量视为单一现实的相互关联的方面。

例句：She views the world nondualistically, recognizing the inherent interconnectedness of all things. 她以非二元论的视角看待世界，认识到万物之间内在的相互关联。


近义词：holistically, integrally, unitarily, monistically


反义词：dualistically, dichotomously, separately


词性：副词
