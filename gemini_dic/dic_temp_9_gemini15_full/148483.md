overstride:/ˌoʊvərˈstraɪd/| v.|跨大步走；迈大步；超过限度|To stride or step over; to exceed the limits of.  It implies taking steps that are longer than normal, often with an implication of haste or carelessness. |例句：He overstrode the line, resulting in a foul. 他跨过了线，导致犯规。

例句：The horse overstrode its gait, almost stumbling.  这匹马步伐过大，差点绊倒。


近义词：overstep, exceed, surpass, outstrip

反义词：understep, understride (not a common word)
