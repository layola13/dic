outspurt:/ˈaʊtspɜːrt/| n. |突然涌出；爆发| A sudden forceful outburst or emission.  It often refers to a sudden release of liquid, gas, or emotion.|例句：There was a sudden outspurt of blood from the wound.  伤口突然喷涌出鲜血。|例句：Her anger manifested as an outspurt of furious words.她的愤怒表现为一阵愤怒的言语爆发。|近义词：burst, gush, eruption|反义词：trickle, seep, gradual release|名词
