halving:/ˈhævɪŋ/| n. v.|减半；对半分割|The act of dividing something into two equal parts.  It can be used as a noun referring to the act of halving or as a verb meaning to divide something in half.|例句：The halving of the workforce led to increased workloads.  员工人数减半导致工作量增加。 The cake was halved before being served. 蛋糕被切成两半后才上桌。|近义词：dividing, splitting, bisecting|反义词：doubling


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He halves the apple. 他把苹果切成两半。
She halves her workload. 她把工作量减半。

一般过去时:
They halved the recipe. 他们把食谱的用量减半了。
He halved the pizza. 他把比萨饼切成两半。

现在完成时:
We have halved our expenses this year. 我们今年已经把开支减半了。
She has halved the sugar in the recipe. 她已经把食谱里的糖减半了。

过去完成时:
They had halved the budget before the project started. 项目开始前他们已经把预算减半了。
She had halved the ingredients before realizing she needed more. 她意识到需要更多材料之前已经把材料减半了。

将来时:
We will halve the portion sizes. 我们将会把分量减半。
He will halve the distance by taking the shortcut. 他将通过走捷径来缩短距离一半。

虚拟语气:
If I halved the recipe, it would be enough for two people. 如果我把食谱的用量减半，就足够两个人吃了。
I wish I had halved the ingredients earlier. 我真希望我早点把材料减半。

被动语态:
The cake was halved by the chef. 蛋糕被厨师切成两半了。
The budget was halved by the company. 预算被公司减半了。

疑问句:
Did you halve the potatoes? 你把土豆切成两半了吗？
Will you halve the apples? 你会把苹果切成两半吗？

否定句:
He didn't halve the bread. 他没有把面包切成两半。
They didn't halve the ingredients. 他们没有把材料减半。


PHRASE->

halve the cost = 降低成本一半
