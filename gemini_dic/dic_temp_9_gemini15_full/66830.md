expatiate: /ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/ | vt. |详述；详谈；详细解释 | To speak or write at length or in detail.  |例句：He expatiated on the virtues of his new invention. 他详细阐述了他新发明的优点。

例句：The professor expatiated on the complexities of quantum physics for over an hour. 教授花了超过一个小时详细讲解量子物理的复杂性。


近义词：elaborate, elucidate, detail, enlarge upon, dwell on

反义词：summarize, abridge, condense


词性:动词
