mass-observe:/ˈmæs ɒbˈzɜːrv/| vt.|大规模观察，大规模监测|To observe or monitor something on a large scale, often using automated systems and data analysis techniques.|例句：The researchers mass-observed the migration patterns of birds using radar technology.  研究人员利用雷达技术对鸟类的迁徙模式进行了大规模观测。


例句：The government is mass-observing social media activity to detect potential threats. 政府正在大规模监控社交媒体活动以检测潜在威胁。


近义词：monitor extensively, broadly survey, comprehensively observe


反义词：ignore, overlook, neglect


词性:动词
