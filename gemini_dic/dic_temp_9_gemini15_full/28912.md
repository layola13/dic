carriages:/ˈkærɪdʒɪz/| n.|车厢；四轮马车；运输工具|A carriage is a compartment in a train, or a horse-drawn vehicle.|例句：The train carriages were crowded with passengers. 火车车厢挤满了乘客。


The antique carriage was beautifully restored. 这辆古董马车经过了精美的修复。|近义词：coach, wagon, vehicle|反义词：无
