interpollinate:/ˌɪntərˈpɒləneɪt/| vt.|异花授粉|The transfer of pollen from the flower of one plant to the flower of another plant of the same species.|例句：Bees are important agents of interpollination in many plant species. 蜜蜂是许多植物物种异花授粉的重要媒介。
例句：Wind can also facilitate interpollination, particularly in grasses and other wind-pollinated plants. 风也能促进异花授粉，尤其是在禾本科和其他风媒植物中。|近义词：cross-pollinate|反义词：self-pollinate|动词
