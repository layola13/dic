secularizer:/ˈsɛkjʊləraɪzər/ | n. | 世俗化者；使世俗化的人或事物 | A person or thing that secularizes.  Someone or something that removes something from religious or spiritual influence. | The government acted as a secularizer, separating church and state.  政府扮演着世俗化的角色，将教会和国家分离。|近义词：laicizer, rationalizer |反义词：sacralizer, spiritualizer
