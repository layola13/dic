re-repeat:/ˌriːrɪˈpiːt/| v.|重复，反复|To repeat something again; to say or do something again.|He re-repeated the instructions to make sure everyone understood. 他再次重复了指示以确保每个人都理解。|repeat, reiterate|omit, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-repeats the question every day. 他每天都重复这个问题。
They re-repeat the experiment to verify the results. 他们重复实验以验证结果。

一般过去时:
She re-repeated her answer for clarity. 她为了清楚起见重复了她的回答。
He re-repeated the joke, hoping for a better reaction. 他又讲了一遍笑话，希望能得到更好的反应。

现在完成时:
I have re-repeated myself several times. 我已经重复过好几次了。
She has re-repeated the same mistake. 她犯了同样的错误。

过去完成时:
He had re-repeated the message before the meeting. 他在会议前又重复了一遍信息。
They had re-repeated the experiment, still getting the same result. 他们重复了实验，仍然得到相同的结果。

将来时:
I will re-repeat the instructions tomorrow. 我明天会再次重复说明。
She will re-repeat the performance next week. 她下周将再次演出。

虚拟语气:
If I had to, I would re-repeat the entire process. 如果必须的话，我会重复整个过程。
I wish I hadn't re-repeated that embarrassing story. 我真希望我没有重复那个令人尴尬的故事。

被动语态:
The instructions were re-repeated for emphasis. 说明为了强调而被重复了一遍。
The experiment will be re-repeated under different conditions. 实验将在不同的条件下重复进行。

疑问句:
Will you re-repeat the question, please? 请您重复一下问题好吗？
Should I re-repeat the experiment? 我应该重复实验吗？

否定句:
I won't re-repeat myself again. 我不会再重复了。
He didn't re-repeat the information. 他没有重复信息。
