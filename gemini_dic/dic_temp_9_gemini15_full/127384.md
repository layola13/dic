neap:/niːp/| n.|小潮，低潮|The lowest tide in a lunar month, occurring when the sun, Earth, and moon are at right angles to each other.  Neap tides have a smaller tidal range than spring tides.|例句：The neap tide was so low that we could walk far out into the bay. 这场小潮水位很低，我们能走到海湾深处。|例句：We waited for the neap tide to go out before venturing across the sandbar. 我们等到小潮退去后才冒险穿过沙洲。|近义词：low tide|反义词：spring tide
