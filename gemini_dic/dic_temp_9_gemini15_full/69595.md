felwort:/ˈfɛlˌwɔːrt/| n. |黑药属植物|A plant of the genus *Gentiana*, typically having a bitter root and used medicinally.  Many species are known as gentians.  The name derives from the plant's use in treating bile problems ("fell" in Middle English could refer to bile or gall). |The felwort was traditionally used to treat various ailments.  |近义词：gentian|反义词：无
