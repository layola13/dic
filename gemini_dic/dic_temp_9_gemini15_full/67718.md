exuscitate: /ɪkˈsʌsɪteɪt/ | vt. | 使复活；使苏醒；复兴 | To revive or restore to life or consciousness; to reanimate.  The word is rarely used in modern English and is considered archaic.  It implies a more complete restoration than simply reviving someone. | 例句：The ancient myth tells of a powerful sorcerer who could exuscitate the dead. = 古代神话讲述了一个能够使死者复活的强大巫师的故事。


The doctor attempted to exuscitate the patient after the accident, but unfortunately, it was too late. = 医生试图在事故后使病人复活，但不幸的是，已经太晚了。


近义词：revive, resuscitate, reanimate | 反义词：kill, extinguish, destroy | 动词
