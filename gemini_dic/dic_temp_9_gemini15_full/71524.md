flapjacks:/ˈflæpˌdʒæks/ | n. | 薄烤饼，燕麦饼 | A flapjack is a type of thick pancake or griddle cake, often made with oats, butter, sugar, and syrup.  They are typically baked rather than fried. | Example:  I made a batch of delicious flapjacks for breakfast this morning.  今天早上我做了一批美味的薄烤饼当早餐。|近义词：pancake, griddle cake | 反义词：无
