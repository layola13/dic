ponders:/ˈpɒndərz/ /ˈpɑːndərz/| vt.|沉思，考虑，琢磨|To think deeply or carefully about something.|He pondered the question for a long time before answering. 他在回答之前长时间地思考了这个问题。|She pondered her options carefully. 她仔细考虑了她的选择。|consider, contemplate, reflect|decide, act, ignore|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ponders the meaning of life. 他正在思考生命的意义。
She ponders her next move in the game.  她在思考游戏中的下一步行动。

一般过去时:
He pondered the problem all night. 他整夜都在思考这个问题。
She pondered the implications of her decision. 她仔细考虑了她决定的影响。

现在完成时:
I have pondered this question for weeks. 我已经思考这个问题好几个星期了。
They have pondered the matter and reached a conclusion. 他们已经仔细考虑了这个问题并得出了结论。

过去完成时:
He had pondered the consequences before he acted.  在他采取行动之前，他已经考虑过后果。
She had pondered the offer for days before accepting. 她接受之前已经考虑了几天这个提议。

将来时:
I will ponder your suggestion carefully. 我会仔细考虑你的建议。
She will ponder the matter before making a decision. 她会在做决定之前仔细考虑这件事。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would ponder the matter more thoroughly. 如果我有时间，我会更彻底地考虑这个问题。
I wish I had pondered the consequences before I spoke. 我希望在我说话之前我已经考虑过后果。

被动语态:
The question was pondered by the committee for hours.  这个问题被委员会考虑了几个小时。
The proposal was pondered by the board before being rejected.  这个提案在被否决之前被董事会仔细考虑过。

疑问句:
What are you pondering? 你在思考什么？
What were they pondering at the meeting?  他们在会议上思考什么？

否定句:
He didn't ponder the risks involved. 他没有考虑所涉及的风险。
She didn't ponder the consequences of her actions. 她没有考虑她行为的后果。
