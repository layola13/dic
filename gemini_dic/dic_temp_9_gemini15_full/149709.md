palaeographers:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfərz/| n. |古文字学家|Scholars who study ancient writings, especially their scripts and the history they reveal.  They decipher and interpret ancient texts, providing insights into past cultures and societies. |The palaeographers carefully examined the ancient manuscript, deciphering its faded script. 古文字学家仔细检查了古代手稿，破译了其褪色的文字。  The team of palaeographers published a groundbreaking study on the evolution of the Latin alphabet.  古文字学家团队发表了一项关于拉丁字母演变的开创性研究。 | epigraphers, codicologists |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps historians focusing on other primary sources) |名词
