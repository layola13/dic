confidingly:/kənˈfaɪdɪŋli/| adv. | 悄悄地；秘密地；信任地 | In a confiding manner; as if sharing a secret.  It suggests intimacy and trust. |例句：She leaned closer and whispered confidingly, "I have a secret to tell you." 她靠得更近，悄悄地、秘密地耳语道：“我有个秘密要告诉你。”

例句：He confidingly shared his deepest fears with his therapist. 他信任地与他的治疗师分享了他最深层的恐惧。


近义词：secretly, privately, trustingly, intimately

反义词：openly, publicly, blatantly


词性: 副词
