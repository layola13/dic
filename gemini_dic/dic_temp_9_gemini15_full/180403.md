regainable:/rɪˈɡeɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可恢复的，可重新获得的 | Capable of being regained or recovered.  Something that is regainable can be gotten back after being lost or taken away. |  例句：His lost health is regainable with proper treatment.  他的健康状况可以通过适当的治疗恢复。

例句：The stolen jewels are hopefully regainable.  失窃的珠宝有希望找回。


近义词：recoverable, retrievable, restorable

反义词：irrecoverable, irretrievable, unrecoverable

词性:形容词
