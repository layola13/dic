optography:/ɒpˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |视力检查法；验光法|The process of recording images using light, especially in the context of vision testing or ophthalmology.  Optography often refers to techniques used to assess visual acuity and other aspects of visual function.|Example: The optometrist used optography to determine the patient's refractive error.  验光师使用视力检查法来确定病人的屈光不正。|近义词：ophthalmography, vision testing |反义词：无
