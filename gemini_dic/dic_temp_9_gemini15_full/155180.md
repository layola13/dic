pewee:/ˈpiːwiː/| n.|美洲的一种小型鸣禽|A small North American flycatcher of the genus Contopus, typically having a plain brown back and a whitish breast.  The pewee is known for its distinctive call, a plaintive "pe-wee".|The pewee perched on a branch, singing its melancholic song.  这只啄木鸟栖息在树枝上，唱着它忧郁的歌。|wood pewee, least flycatcher|  (no direct antonym)|名词
