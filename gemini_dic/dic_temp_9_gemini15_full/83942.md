half-coaxingly:/ˌhæf ˈkoʊksɪŋli/ | adv. | 半哄半劝地，半强迫地 |  Describes an action performed with a mixture of coaxing (gentle persuasion) and a hint of compulsion or force. It suggests a gentle but insistent approach. |例句：He half-coaxingly persuaded her to try the new dish. 他半哄半劝地让她尝试新菜。

例句：She half-coaxingly urged him to finish his homework. 她半哄半劝地催促他完成作业。


近义词：persuasively, coaxingly, urgingly, pressingly


反义词： forcefully, commandingly, roughly


词性：副词
