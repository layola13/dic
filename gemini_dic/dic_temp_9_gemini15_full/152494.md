paying:/ˈpeɪɪŋ/| v.|支付；给予；注意；值得；有报酬的|The act of giving money or something else of value in exchange for goods or services. It can also refer to giving attention or effort to something.|例句：I'm paying for the dinner. 我在付晚餐的钱。

He is paying close attention to the lecture. 他正在认真听讲座。|近义词：spending, giving, attending to, rewarding|反义词: receiving, neglecting, ignoring

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am paying my bills on time every month. = 我每个月都按时支付我的账单。
She is paying attention to the details of the project. = 她正在关注项目的细节。


一般过去时:
They paid for the groceries with a credit card. = 他们用信用卡支付了杂货的费用。
He paid close attention to the instructions. = 他仔细听了指示。


现在完成时:
I have paid all my debts. = 我已经还清了所有的债务。
She has been paying for her education herself. = 她一直自己支付学费。


过去完成时:
He had paid the rent before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经付了房租。
They had been paying for the house for ten years before they finally owned it. = 他们在最终拥有房子之前已经支付了十年的房租。


将来时:
I will pay you back tomorrow. = 我明天会还你钱。
She will be paying her tuition fees next semester. = 下学期她将支付她的学费。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would pay for a trip to Europe. = 如果我有更多钱，我会支付去欧洲旅行的费用。
I wish I could pay off all my student loans. = 我希望我能还清所有学生贷款。


被动语态:
The bills are paid monthly. = 账单每月支付。
The employees are being paid fairly. = 员工得到公平的报酬。


疑问句:
Are you paying cash or credit? = 你付现金还是信用卡？
Is she paying attention in class? = 她上课认真听讲吗？


否定句:
He didn't pay for the damage. = 他没有赔偿损失。
She wasn't paying attention to what I said. = 她没有注意我说的话。


PHRASE->
paying attention: 注意力
paying off: 还清；取得成功
paying back: 偿还
paying the price: 付出代价
paying your dues: 尽义务；付出努力
