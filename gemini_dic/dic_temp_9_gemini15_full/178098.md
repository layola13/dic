reclusiveness:/rɪˈkluːsɪvnəs/| n. |隐居；孤僻；离群索居|The state or habit of living in seclusion and often avoiding the company of other people.  It often implies a preference for solitude and a shyness or aversion to social interaction.|His reclusiveness was a source of much speculation among his neighbors.  他的隐居让邻居们有很多猜测。|His reclusiveness prevented him from forming close relationships. 他的孤僻使他无法建立亲密的关系。|sociability, seclusion, isolation|extroversion, sociability
