rowling:/ˈroʊlɪŋ/ | v. |滚动；翻滚；发出隆隆声 | to move along a surface by turning over and over | The ball was rolling down the hill. 球滚下了山。The thunder was rolling in the distance. 远处传来隆隆的雷声。 | 近义词： tumbling, rolling, revolving | 反义词：stopping, halting

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river rolls steadily towards the sea. = 这条河稳稳地流向大海。
She rolls her eyes whenever he speaks. = 每当他说话时，她都会翻白眼。


一般过去时:
The stone rolled down the mountain. = 石头滚下山了。
He rolled the dough out on the table. = 他把面团擀在桌子上。


现在完成时:
The snow has rolled up into huge drifts. = 雪已经堆积成巨大的雪堆。
The ball has rolled under the sofa. = 球滚到沙发底下了。


过去完成时:
The car had rolled over several times before coming to a stop. = 汽车翻滚了几次才停下来。
She had rolled the dice before I could even see them. = 我还没看到她就掷骰子了。


将来时:
The ball will roll down the hill. = 球会滚下山。
They will roll out the red carpet for the VIPs. = 他们会为贵宾铺上红地毯。


虚拟语气:
If I rolled the dice, I might win. = 如果我掷骰子，我可能会赢。
I wish I had rolled the ball further. = 我真希望我把球滚得更远。


被动语态:
The dough was rolled out thinly. = 面团被擀得很薄。
The barrels were rolled into the warehouse. = 木桶被滚进了仓库。


疑问句:
Will the ball roll down the hill? = 球会滚下山吗？
Does she roll her eyes at you often? = 她经常对你翻白眼吗？


否定句:
The car didn't roll down the hill. = 车没有滚下山。
He doesn't roll his sleeves up when he works. = 他工作时不卷起袖子。
