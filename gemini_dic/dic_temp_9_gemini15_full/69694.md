fenian:/ˈfɛniən/| n. |芬尼安人；爱尔兰共和兄弟会成员|A member of a secret Irish republican organization, active in the 19th century, that aimed to overthrow British rule in Ireland.  The Fenian Brotherhood was dedicated to achieving Irish independence through armed rebellion.  The name is derived from the ancient Irish warriors known as Fianna.|The Fenians launched several unsuccessful uprisings against British rule. |近义词：Irish republican, nationalist |反义词：British loyalist
