reoutfitted:/ˌriˈaʊtfɪtɪd/ | v. |重新装备；翻新；重新配备 |  To equip or furnish (something) again or anew.  This usually implies a significant upgrade or replacement of existing equipment or furnishings. | 例句：The army reoutfitted its soldiers with the latest combat gear.  军队为士兵们重新配备了最新的作战装备。

The old factory was reoutfitted with modern machinery. 老工厂安装了现代化机器并进行了翻新。


近义词：refit, re-equip, renovate, refurbish | 反义词：strip, dismantle
