preindependent: /ˌpriːɪndɪˈpɛndənt/ | adj. |  独立之前的；在独立之前存在的 |  Existing or occurring before independence.  The word combines the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") with "independent". It describes something that happened or existed in the period leading up to a nation or region gaining independence. |


例句：

The preindependent era saw significant social and political upheaval.  = 独立前的时代经历了重大的社会和政治动荡。

Preindependent customs influenced the nation's post-independence development. = 独立前的习俗影响了国家独立后的发展。


近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding


反义词：postindependent


词性:形容词
