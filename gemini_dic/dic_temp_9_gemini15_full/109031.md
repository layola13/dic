leg-pulling:/ˈlɛɡˌpʊlɪŋ/| n.|开玩笑，戏弄，愚弄|The act of teasing or playing a joke on someone, often in a good-natured way.  It involves tricking someone into believing something untrue, usually for amusement.|例句：He's always leg-pulling his younger brother. 他总是捉弄他弟弟。

例句：Stop that leg-pulling; it's not funny anymore.  别再开玩笑了，一点都不好笑。|近义词：teasing, joking, kidding, prank, hoax|反义词：seriousness, sincerity
