satiety:/səˈtaɪəti/| n. |饱腹感；满足感；厌腻|A feeling of being completely satisfied, especially after eating a lot of food.  It also refers to a feeling of being completely satisfied with something, to the point of being bored or disgusted.|例句：After the enormous Thanksgiving meal, a feeling of satiety settled over everyone.  感恩节丰盛的晚餐过后，每个人都感到饱腹感。

例句：The constant repetition of the same task led to satiety and a lack of motivation.  重复同样的任务导致厌倦和缺乏动力。


近义词：fullness, repletion, satisfaction, surfeit

反义词：hunger, appetite, craving, dissatisfaction
