chided:/tʃaɪdɪd/| v.|责备，训斥|To scold or rebuke someone gently or mildly.|例句：The teacher chided the student for being late.老师因为学生迟到而责备了他。|例句：My mother chided me for not cleaning my room. 我妈妈责备我没有打扫我的房间。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scold, admonish|反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often chides his children for their bad behavior. 他经常因为孩子们的坏行为而责备他们。
She chides her employees gently when they make mistakes. 当她的员工犯错时，她会温和地责备他们。


一般过去时:
The manager chided the employee for missing the deadline. 经理因为员工错过截止日期而责备了他。
My father chided me for my poor grades.  我父亲因为我的糟糕成绩而责备我。


现在完成时:
She has chided him several times for his carelessness. 她已经多次因为他的粗心大意而责备他了。
They have chided the team for their lack of effort. 他们因为团队缺乏努力而责备了他们。


过去完成时:
He had chided her before, so she was already prepared for his criticism. 他之前已经责备过她了，所以她已经准备好接受他的批评了。
The teacher had chided the class for their disruptive behavior before starting the lesson. 老师在开始上课之前已经因为班级的扰乱行为而责备了他们。


将来时:
I will chide him if he does that again. 如果他再这样做，我会责备他。
She will likely chide her son for his poor choices. 她很可能会因为儿子的糟糕选择而责备他。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't chide him so harshly. 如果我是你，我不会那么严厉地责备他。
I wish I hadn't chided her so severely. 我真希望我没有那么严厉地责备她。


被动语态:
He was chided for his inappropriate comments. 他因为不恰当的评论而受到责备。
The children were chided for their noisy play. 孩子们因为吵闹的玩耍而受到责备。


疑问句:
Did she chide you for being late? 她因为你迟到而责备你了吗？
Will he chide me for forgetting his birthday? 他会因为我忘记了他的生日而责备我吗？


否定句:
She didn't chide him for his mistake this time. 这次她没有因为他犯的错误而责备他。
I won't chide you if you try your best. 如果你尽力了，我不会责备你。


PHRASE->

chide someone into submission 责备某人直到服从
chide and scold 责备和训斥
