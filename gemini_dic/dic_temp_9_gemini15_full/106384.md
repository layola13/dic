konseal:/kənˈsiːl/| vt.|隐藏；遮掩；隐瞒|To hide or keep secret; to prevent from being seen or known.|The artist concealed the flaw in the painting by cleverly painting over it.  艺术家巧妙地将画布上的瑕疵遮掩起来。|The government tried to conceal the truth about the scandal. 政府试图隐瞒丑闻的真相。|hide, disguise, mask, secrete|reveal, expose, disclose|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He conceals his true feelings. = 他隐藏自己的真实感受。
They conceal their wealth from the tax authorities. = 他们向税务机关隐瞒自己的财富。

一般过去时:
She concealed the evidence. = 她隐藏了证据。
He concealed the truth from his parents. = 他向父母隐瞒了真相。

现在完成时:
They have concealed their intentions. = 他们一直隐瞒着他们的意图。
She has concealed her identity for years. = 她多年来一直隐瞒自己的身份。

过去完成时:
He had concealed the letter before the police arrived. = 在警察到来之前，他已经把信藏起来了。
They had concealed their plans for a long time. = 他们很久以前就隐瞒了他们的计划。

将来时:
She will conceal the documents from the public. = 她将向公众隐瞒这些文件。
They will conceal their activities from the media. = 他们将向媒体隐瞒他们的活动。

虚拟语气:
If he concealed the truth, he would be in trouble. = 如果他隐瞒真相，他就会有麻烦。
I wish I had concealed my feelings better. = 我真希望我当初能更好地隐藏自己的感情。

被动语态:
The truth was concealed from the public. = 真相被隐瞒了公众。
The documents were concealed in a safe. = 文件被藏在一个保险箱里。

疑问句:
Did he conceal the money? = 他是否藏起了钱？
Why did she conceal her age? = 她为什么隐瞒自己的年龄？

否定句:
He did not conceal his anger. = 他没有掩饰他的愤怒。
She did not conceal her surprise. = 她没有掩饰她的惊讶。
