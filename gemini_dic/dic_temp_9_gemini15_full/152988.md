pedobaptist:/ˈpiːdoʊˌbæptɪst/| n. |为婴儿施洗的人；婴儿洗礼论者|A person who believes in the practice of baptizing infants.  This is a theological term referring to those who believe that infant baptism is a valid Christian sacrament.  The practice is distinct from credobaptism, where baptism is only administered to those who profess faith. |Example:  Some denominations are pedobaptist, while others are credobaptist.  一些教派是婴儿洗礼论者，而另一些则是信徒洗礼论者。|近义词：infant baptist, believer in infant baptism|反义词：credobaptist
