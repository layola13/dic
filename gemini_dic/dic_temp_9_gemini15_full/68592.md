fantasia:/fænˈteɪziə/| n.|幻想曲；幻想；奇幻作品|A fantasia is a musical composition, often for orchestra, that is characterized by its free and imaginative form, often without a strict adherence to traditional structures.  It allows for improvisation and exploration of musical ideas.  It can also refer to a fanciful or imaginative creation, particularly in literature or art.| 例句：The orchestra played a breathtaking fantasia on a theme by Bach. 乐团演奏了一首令人惊叹的巴赫主题幻想曲。


The film was a beautiful fantasia of light and color. 这部电影是一部美丽的光影幻想。|近义词：fantasy, improvisation, daydream|反义词：reality, fact, realism|名词
