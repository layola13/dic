self-fitting: /ˌsɛlfˈfɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 自适应的，自调节的 |  Describes something that adjusts or adapts itself automatically to fit a particular situation or requirement.  It implies a process of automatic adjustment without external intervention. | Example: The software boasts a self-fitting algorithm that optimizes performance based on user needs.  这个软件拥有一个自适应算法，可以根据用户的需求优化性能。|近义词：self-adjusting, adaptive, automatic | 反义词：manual, fixed, static |
