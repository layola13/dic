moisturize:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪz/| vt.|滋润，保湿|To make (something, especially skin) moist; to apply a moisturizer to.|例句：I moisturize my face every morning. 我每天早上都给脸部保湿。|例句：The lotion helps to moisturize dry skin. 这款乳液有助于滋润干燥的皮肤。|近义词：hydrate, condition, lubricate|反义词：dry out, dehydrate
