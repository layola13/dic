populist:/ˈpɒpjʊlɪst/ /ˈpɑːpjʊlɪst/| n. adj.|民粹主义者；民粹主义的|A populist is a person who appeals to or represents the concerns of ordinary people.  A populist movement or ideology often champions the interests of the common people against the elite. |例句：The candidate ran a populist campaign, promising to cut taxes and reduce government spending.  这位候选人发起了一场民粹主义运动，承诺减税和减少政府支出。

例句：The populist party gained significant support from rural voters.  民粹主义政党获得了农村选民的大力支持。


近义词：demagogue, rabble-rouser|反义词：elitist
