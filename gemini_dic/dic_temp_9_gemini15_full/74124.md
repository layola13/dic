foulest: /ˈfaʊləst/ | adj. | 最肮脏的；最恶劣的；最邪恶的 |  The most unpleasant, wicked, or offensive.  It is the superlative form of "foul". | 例句：The foulest language imaginable was used in the argument.  这场争吵中使用了你能想象到的最粗俗的语言。


The foulest stench ever encountered filled the air.  空气中弥漫着有史以来最难闻的气味。

近义词：filthiest, dirtiest, most wicked, most vile, most corrupt | 反义词：cleanest, purest, most virtuous, most innocent


词源:  Old English *fūl, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vuil and German faul ‘rotten, foul’.  The sense development is from ‘rotten’ to ‘dirty’ and then to ‘morally bad’.
