esculapius:/ɛsˈkjuːleɪpiəs/| n. |阿斯克勒庇俄斯（希腊神话中的医药之神）|The ancient Greek god of medicine and healing, often depicted with a staff and snake.  He is the ancestor of the medical profession and his symbol, the Rod of Asclepius (a single snake entwined around a staff), is still used in medicine today.|Example:  The symbol of Esculapius, the snake-entwined staff, is recognized worldwide as a symbol of medicine.  阿斯克勒庇俄斯，一条蛇缠绕着权杖的象征，在全世界都被认为是医学的象征。|近义词：Apollo (in some contexts)|反义词：无
