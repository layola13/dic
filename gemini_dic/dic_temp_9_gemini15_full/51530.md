detruncation:/diːtrʌŋˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 去截短；恢复完整 | The act of removing a truncated part or restoring something to its original, complete form.  It often refers to restoring data or information that has been cut off or shortened.  For example, in database management, detruncation might involve recovering lost data from a truncated table. | Example: The detruncation of the historical records was a painstaking process.  去截短的历史记录的恢复是一个费力的过程。 The detruncation of the image file restored the missing corner. 图片文件的恢复完整性恢复了缺失的角。 | 近义词: restoration, recovery, reconstruction | 反义词: truncation, abbreviation, shortening |  名词
