druids:/ˈdruːɪdz/| n.|德鲁伊教祭司；德鲁伊|Members of the ancient Celtic priesthood in Gaul, Britain, and Ireland, who practiced religious rites, divination, and medicine, often associated with nature worship and reverence for oak trees.|例句：The druids performed rituals in sacred groves. 德鲁伊祭司在神圣的树林中举行仪式。|近义词：priest, shaman, sorcerer|反义词:None
