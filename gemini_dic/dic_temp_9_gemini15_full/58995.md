duumvir: /ˈduːmvaɪər/ | n. | 二人执政官；二人委员会 | A Roman official holding one of two positions in a board or magistracy.  The term often refers to officials with joint authority, especially in Roman local government.  | Example: The duumviri oversaw the construction of the new aqueduct.  二人执政官负责监督新的渡槽的建设。 | 近义词：magistrate, official, commissioner | 反义词：monarch, dictator
