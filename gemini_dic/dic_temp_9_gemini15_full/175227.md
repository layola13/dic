railers:/ˈreɪlərz/| n.|栏杆；抱怨者；（火车）车厢|Railings, or barriers, are commonly found along walkways, staircases, or balconies to prevent falls.  They can also refer to people who constantly complain or grumble. In a railway context, it can refer to railway carriages.|例句：The old wooden railings were rotten and needed replacing.  那些旧木栏杆腐烂了，需要更换。|近义词：guardrails, banisters, complainers, grumblers|反义词：openings, supporters
