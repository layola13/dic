hymnology:/haɪˈmɒnəlɒdʒi/| n. |赞美诗学；圣歌学 |The study of hymns, their history, authors, and theological significance.  It encompasses the analysis of hymn texts, music, and their cultural context. |例句：He is a professor of hymnology, specializing in the hymns of the 18th century. 他是赞美诗学教授，专门研究18世纪的赞美诗。|例句：Her research in hymnology revealed fascinating connections between religious and social history. 她在赞美诗学方面的研究揭示了宗教史和社会史之间引人入胜的联系。|近义词：hymn studies, hymn research |反义词：无
