followers:/ˈfɑːləʊərz/ /ˈfɒləʊərz/| n.|追随者，粉丝|People who follow someone or something, especially on social media.  Those who admire and support a particular person, cause, or ideology.|例句：The influencer has millions of followers on Instagram. 这个网红在Instagram上有数百万粉丝。

例句：His followers eagerly awaited his next speech.  他的追随者们热切地等待着他的下一次演讲。


近义词：fans, disciples, adherents, subscribers

反义词：leaders, opponents


词性：名词
