over-reducing: /ˌoʊvər rɪˈduːsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度还原；过度简化 |  The act of reducing something excessively, often leading to simplification or loss of detail or nuance.  It implies going too far in the process of reduction. This term is often used in contexts like chemical reactions, data analysis, or artistic representation.


例句：

Over-reducing the complex data set led to inaccurate conclusions.  过度简化复杂的数据集导致了不准确的结论。

The painting suffered from over-reducing, losing much of its original texture and depth.  这幅画由于过度简化而失去了许多原始的纹理和深度。


近义词：oversimplifying, simplifying excessively, minimizing excessively

反义词：overcomplicating, elaborating, expanding


词性：动词 (present participle of over-reduce)
