inapplicably:/ˌɪnæˈplɪkəbli/| adv.|不适用地；无关紧要地|In a manner that is not applicable or relevant.|The rule is inapplicably in this situation because of the unique circumstances.|近义词：irrelevantly, inappropriately|反义词：appropriately, applicably


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" does.  The usage is solely about how it modifies a verb or clause.

Example 1:
The law was inapplicably applied to this case. = 这项法律在这个案件中并不适用。

Example 2:
The instructions were inapplicably vague. = 指示语含糊不清，无法适用。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "inapplicably."  Its usage is generally standalone within a sentence to describe how something is not applicable.
