overanalysis:/ˌoʊvərəˈnæləsɪs/| n. |过度分析，过度解读|The act of analyzing something excessively or to an unnecessary degree, often leading to inaccurate or misleading conclusions.  It involves focusing on minor details while neglecting the bigger picture. |例句：His overanalysis of the poem prevented him from appreciating its overall beauty.  他的过度解读诗歌妨碍了他欣赏其整体美感。|近义词：overinterpretation, overthinking, hyperanalysis |反义词：cursory analysis, superficial examination |名词
