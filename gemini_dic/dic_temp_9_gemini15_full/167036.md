presuffrages:/ˈpriːˌsʌfrɪdʒɪz/| n.|提前投票；预先投票|Votes cast before the official election day.  They are often used in situations where voters may not be able to vote on the official election day due to various reasons such as travel or illness.|例句：The presuffrages showed a strong lead for the incumbent candidate.  提前投票显示现任候选人领先。

例句：Many soldiers cast their presuffrages before deploying overseas. 许多士兵在海外部署前进行了提前投票。|近义词：advance voting, early voting|反义词：post-election count, final count|名词
