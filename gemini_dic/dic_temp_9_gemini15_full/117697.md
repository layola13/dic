melamine:/ˈmɛləmɪn/| n. |三聚氰胺|Melamine is a chemical compound used in the production of plastics, adhesives, and resins. It is a hard, white, crystalline powder that is highly resistant to heat and chemicals. However, it is toxic if ingested in large quantities.|例句：The scandal involved the illegal addition of melamine to infant formula.  三聚氰胺非法添加到婴儿配方奶粉的丑闻。

例句：Melamine resin is used to make durable tableware. 三聚氰胺树脂用于制造耐用的餐具。|近义词：resin, polymer|反义词：无
