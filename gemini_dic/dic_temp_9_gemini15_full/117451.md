meditatist:/ˈmɛdəteɪtɪst/| n. |沉思者，冥想者 |A meditatist is a person who practices meditation regularly.  They engage in the practice of focusing their mind on a particular object, thought, or activity to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm state.|例句：The experienced meditatist found inner peace through daily practice.  这位经验丰富的冥想者通过每日练习找到了内心的平静。The retreat center attracted many meditatists seeking spiritual enlightenment.  这个静修中心吸引了许多寻求精神启蒙的冥想者。|近义词：contemplative, mystic, yogi |反义词：agitator, busybody
