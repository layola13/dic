disbarring: /dɪsˈbɑːrɪŋ/ | v. |  除名；取消…的律师资格 |  The act of preventing someone, especially a lawyer, from practicing their profession.  It implies a formal process of removal, often due to misconduct. |


例句：

The lawyer was disbarred for unethical conduct. =  这名律师因不道德行为而被取消律师资格。

The judge ordered the disbarring of the attorney. = 法官下令取消该律师的执业资格。


近义词：debar, disallow, disqualify


反义词：admit, license, reinstate


词性：动词
