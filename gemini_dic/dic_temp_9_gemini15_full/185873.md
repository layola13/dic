revoked:/rɪˈvoʊkt/ | vt. |撤销；废除；取消 | To make something officially invalid.  To take away a privilege, license, or right. |例句：The court revoked his driving license. 法院吊销了他的驾照。

例句：His membership was revoked after he violated the club's rules.  因为他违反了俱乐部的规定，他的会员资格被取消了。


近义词：cancel, annul, rescind, repeal |反义词：grant, bestow, confirm, ratify |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government revokes licenses regularly. = 政府定期吊销执照。
She revokes her earlier statement. = 她撤回了之前的声明。


一般过去时:
They revoked the contract last week. = 他们上周撤销了合同。
The judge revoked his parole. = 法官撤销了他的假释。


现在完成时:
The company has revoked its offer. = 公司已经撤回了它的报价。
He has revoked his permission to use the image. = 他已经撤销了他使用该图片的许可。


过去完成时:
The court had revoked his license before the accident. = 事故发生前，法院已经吊销了他的驾照。
She had revoked her promise before he could act on it.  = 他还没来得及采取行动，她就反悔了。


将来时:
The council will revoke the planning permission. = 理事会将撤销规划许可。
They will revoke his membership if he continues to misbehave. = 如果他继续行为不端，他们将取消他的会员资格。


虚拟语气:
If the judge revoked the sentence, he would be free. = 如果法官撤销判决，他就会自由了。
I wish they had revoked the unfair rule. = 我希望他们已经撤销了那个不公平的规定。


被动语态:
His license was revoked due to speeding. = 他的驾照因超速被吊销。
The contract was revoked after a dispute between the parties. = 双方发生争执后，合同被撤销了。


疑问句:
Will the judge revoke the order? = 法官会撤销命令吗？
Has the university revoked his scholarship? = 大学是否撤销了他的奖学金？


否定句:
The government did not revoke the law. = 政府没有废除该法律。
They haven't revoked the ban yet. = 他们还没有取消禁令。


PHRASE->
revoke a license = 吊销执照
revoke a decree = 撤销法令
revoke a will = 撤销遗嘱
revoke an order = 撤销命令
revoke a privilege = 取消特权


词源:
From Middle English *revoken, from Old French revoquer, from Latin revocare (“to call back”), from re- (“back”) + vocare (“to call”).
