disobeyer:/ˌdɪsoʊˈbeɪər/| n. |不服从者，违抗者|A person who disobeys rules, laws, or orders.  Someone who refuses to comply with authority or instructions.|例句：The disobeyer was punished for his actions.  不服从者因其行为而受到惩罚。
The rebellious teenager was a known disobeyer of school rules.  那个叛逆的青少年是出了名的违反校规的人。|近义词：rebel, lawbreaker, transgressor, defiant|反义词：obedient, compliant, follower
