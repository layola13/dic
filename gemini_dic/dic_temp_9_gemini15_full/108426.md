lawsonia:/lɔːˈsoʊniə/| n.|指甲花属植物；海娜；散沫花|Lawsonia is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lythraceae, commonly known as henna.  The most well-known species is Lawsonia inermis, whose leaves are used to produce a reddish-brown dye for staining skin, hair, and fingernails. |The henna dye from Lawsonia inermis is used in many cultures for body art and hair coloring.|近义词：henna (as a dye)|反义词：无
