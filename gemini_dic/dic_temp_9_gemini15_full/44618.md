crowdsources:/ˈkraʊdˌsɔːrsɪz/ | vt. |众包，通过大众的力量来完成 | To obtain (information, input, or services) by enlisting the participation of a large number of people, typically via the internet.  |例句：The company crowdsources its marketing campaigns by inviting its customers to submit ideas. 这家公司通过邀请客户提交创意来众包其营销活动。

例句：We crowdsourced the design of our new logo, receiving hundreds of submissions from our online community. 我们通过众包的方式设计新标志，从我们的在线社区收到了数百份投稿。


近义词：outsource, solicit, collect


反义词：centralize, manage internally


词性:动词
