clamoring:/ˈklæmərɪŋ/| v.|喧闹；强烈要求；吵闹 |  to make a loud and confused noise; to demand something loudly | 例句：The crowd was clamoring for the band to play another song. = 人群吵着要乐队再演奏一首歌。

The protesters were clamoring for political reform. = 抗议者强烈要求政治改革。

近义词： shouting, yelling, demanding, protesting | 反义词： whispering, murmuring, acquiescing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children are clamoring for attention. = 孩子们吵着要人注意他们。
The audience is clamoring for an encore. = 观众强烈要求再来一首。

一般过去时:
The crowd clamored for justice. = 人群大声疾呼要求正义。
They clamored for a change in leadership. = 他们强烈要求更换领导人。

现在完成时:
The public has clamored for stricter gun control laws. = 公众一直强烈要求更严格的枪支管制法。
The fans have been clamoring for a reunion tour. = 粉丝们一直在强烈要求重聚巡演。

过去完成时:
The villagers had clamored for years for better roads. = 村民们多年来一直强烈要求修建更好的道路。
They had clamored for a response, but received none. = 他们一直强烈要求得到回应，但没有得到任何回应。

将来时:
The students will clamor for better facilities next year. = 学生们明年将强烈要求更好的设施。
They will clamor for a reduction in tuition fees. = 他们将强烈要求降低学费。

虚拟语气:
If they clamored louder, maybe they would have been heard. = 如果他们喊得更大声，也许他们会被听到。
I wish they hadn't clamored so much during the performance. = 我真希望他们在演出期间不要吵闹得这么厉害。

被动语态:
The government was clamored at for a response to the crisis. = 政府因对危机的回应而遭到强烈要求。
New policies are being clamored for by the citizens. = 公民们正强烈要求新的政策。

疑问句:
Is the crowd clamoring for something? = 人群是在强烈要求什么吗？
Were they clamoring for his resignation? = 他们是在强烈要求他辞职吗？

否定句:
The people didn't clamor for change. = 人们没有强烈要求改变。
The students did not clamor for a new building. = 学生们没有强烈要求建造新楼。


PHRASE->
clamor for change = 要求改变

clamor to be heard = 要求被听到
clamor against injustice = 抗议不公正
public clamor = 公众的强烈要求

词源:
The word "clamoring" comes from the Middle English word "clameren," which means "to cry out loudly."  This, in turn, is derived from the Old French word "clamer," ultimately having roots in the Latin word "clamare," meaning "to shout" or "to cry out."
