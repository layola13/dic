mailbox: /ˈmeɪlˌbɑːks/ | n. |邮箱，信箱 | A container, typically a box, for receiving mail.  It is usually located outside a building or residence. | Example:  I checked the mailbox this morning and found a letter from my aunt.  今天早上我查看了邮箱，发现一封来自我阿姨的信。 | Near synonyms: postbox, letterbox | Antonyms: N/A
