interesting:/ˈɪntrəstɪŋ/ | adj. |有趣的，令人感兴趣的 |  Interesting describes something that captures attention and holds one's curiosity. It evokes a feeling of fascination or engagement.  | The book was interesting from beginning to end. 这本书从头到尾都很有趣。| captivating, fascinating, engaging | boring, dull, uninteresting | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The movie is interesting. = 这部电影很有趣。
I find this topic interesting. = 我觉得这个话题很有趣。

一般过去时:
The lecture was interesting. = 讲座很有趣。
The story was very interesting. = 这个故事非常有趣。

现在完成时:
I have found many interesting things in the museum. = 我在博物馆里发现了很多有趣的东西。
We have had an interesting journey. = 我们有一段有趣的旅程。

过去完成时:
I had already read many interesting books before I started this one. = 在我开始读这本书之前，我已经读过许多有趣的书了。
They had discussed some interesting ideas before the meeting. = 他们会在会议之前讨论一些有趣的想法。

将来时:
The conference will be interesting. = 会议将会很有趣。
I think the trip will be interesting. = 我认为这次旅行会很有趣。

虚拟语气:
It would be interesting to see what happens next. = 看接下来会发生什么将会很有趣。
I wish the meeting had been more interesting. = 我希望会议更有趣一些。

被动语态:
The results were found to be interesting. = 结果被发现很有趣。
The experiment was deemed interesting by the scientists. = 实验被科学家认为很有趣。


疑问句:
Is the new project interesting? = 新项目有趣吗？
Do you find this subject interesting? = 你觉得这个主题有趣吗？


否定句:
The film wasn't very interesting. = 这部电影不太有趣。
I didn't find the presentation interesting at all. = 我一点也不觉得这个演示有趣。
