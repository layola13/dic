reestimation:/ˌriːˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |重新估计，再估价 | The act of estimating something again, often after new information has become available.  It involves revising an initial estimate based on updated data or circumstances. | Example:  The project's reestimation resulted in a higher budget. 项目的重新评估导致预算增加。 |近义词：reappraisal, reassessment |反义词：initial estimation, first assessment |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reestimates the project cost annually. = 公司每年都会重新评估项目成本。
We reestimate the damage after the storm. = 我们在暴风雨过后重新评估了损失。


一般过去时:
They reestimated the time needed for the journey. = 他们重新评估了旅途所需的时间。
The engineer reestimated the structural integrity of the bridge. = 工程师重新评估了桥梁的结构完整性。


现在完成时:
The team has reestimated the project timeline several times. = 团队已经多次重新评估了项目时间表。
They have reestimated the value of their assets. = 他们已经重新评估了其资产的价值。


过去完成时:
After reviewing the new data, they had reestimated the risk. = 在审查了新数据后，他们重新评估了风险。
Before making a final decision, the committee had reestimated the potential benefits. = 在做出最终决定之前，委员会重新评估了潜在的益处。


将来时:
We will reestimate the budget once we receive the updated figures. = 一旦我们收到更新后的数据，我们将重新评估预算。
The consultant will reestimate the project completion date. = 顾问将重新评估项目的完成日期。


虚拟语气:
If we had more information, we would reestimate the outcome. = 如果我们有更多信息，我们将重新评估结果。
It would be wise to reestimate the risks involved before proceeding. = 在继续之前，重新评估所涉及的风险将是明智之举。


被动语态:
The cost of the repairs was reestimated after the inspection. = 检查后，维修费用进行了重新评估。
The project's timeline is being reestimated due to unforeseen delays. = 由于不可预见延误，项目时间表正在重新评估中。


疑问句:
Will the project be reestimated before the next meeting? = 下次会议前项目是否会重新评估？
When will the damages be reestimated? = 什么时候会重新评估损失？


否定句:
The company did not reestimate the project's cost. = 公司没有重新评估项目的成本。
They did not reestimate the impact of the change. = 他们没有重新评估该变化的影响。


PHRASE->
reestimation of costs = 成本重新估算
reestimation of risks = 风险重新评估
reestimation of damages = 损失重新评估
reestimation of time = 时间重新估算
reestimation of resources = 资源重新估算
