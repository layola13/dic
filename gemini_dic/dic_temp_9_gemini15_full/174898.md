raconteurs:/rækɒnˈtɜːrz/| n. |说故事的人，擅长讲故事的人 | A raconteur is someone who is skilled at telling stories, often in a captivating and entertaining way. They are typically articulate, engaging, and have a knack for weaving a compelling narrative.  | He was a natural raconteur, captivating his audience with his witty anecdotes. 他是个天生的说故事高手，他妙趣横生的轶事迷住了听众。  | storyteller, narrator, anecdotist | listener, quiet person
