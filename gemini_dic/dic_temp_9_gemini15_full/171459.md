puffer:/ˈpʌfər/| n. |膨胀物；(尤指)河豚；膨胀的衣服|A puffer is something that puffs or swells, especially a type of fish (the pufferfish) that inflates its body as a defense mechanism.  It can also refer to a type of jacket or coat that is inflated with down or synthetic filling. |例句：The pufferfish inflated itself to appear larger and scare away predators.  河豚膨胀自己以显得更大，吓跑捕食者。

例句：She wore a stylish puffer jacket to keep warm in the winter. 她穿着一件时尚的羽绒服以抵御冬天的寒冷。|近义词：inflater, blower (for the action); down jacket, padded jacket (for the garment)|反义词：deflator (for the action); thin coat (for the garment)|
