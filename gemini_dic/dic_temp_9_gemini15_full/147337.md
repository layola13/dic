overmourning:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnɪŋ/| n.|过度悲伤，悲痛过度|Excessive grief or mourning.  It often implies that the grieving process is unhealthy or prolonged, hindering the individual's ability to move on.|例句：Her overmourning for her lost son prevented her from rebuilding her life.  她的儿子去世后，她过度悲伤，以至于无法重建自己的生活。|近义词：excessive grief, prolonged mourning, unhealthy grieving|反义词：acceptance, resilience, moving on
