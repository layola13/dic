ernest:/ˈɜːrnɪst/| adj.|认真的；诚挚的；严肃的 |Serious and sincere; showing deep feeling.  |例句：He spoke with earnest conviction. 他带着诚挚的信念说话。|例句：She had an earnest desire to help. 她有强烈的愿望想要帮助。|近义词：serious, sincere, grave, solemn |反义词：frivolous, flippant, insincere, casual
