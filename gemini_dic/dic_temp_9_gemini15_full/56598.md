donations:/doʊˈneɪʃənz/| n.|捐赠；捐款|The act of giving money, goods, or services to a cause, charity, or person.  Donations can be made in various forms, including cash, goods, or time.|例句：The charity relies heavily on public donations.  这家慈善机构严重依赖公众捐款。

例句：Many people made donations after the earthquake.  地震后许多人捐款。


近义词：contributions, gifts, offerings, alms

反义词：withholdings, retentions


词性：名词
