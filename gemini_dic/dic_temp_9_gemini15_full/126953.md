narratory:/ˈnærəˌtɔːri/| adj. |叙事的，讲述故事的| Relating to or characteristic of narration or a narrator.  It describes something that tells a story or is involved in the act of storytelling.|The film had a strong narratorial voice.|近义词：narrative, storytelling |反义词：descriptive (in some contexts)
