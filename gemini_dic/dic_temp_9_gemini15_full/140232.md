officiousness:/əˈfɪʃəsnəs/| n. | 爱管闲事；多管闲事；自以为是；傲慢 | The excessive eagerness to tell people what to do, especially in a way that is annoying or unwanted.  It often implies a lack of genuine authority or expertise. |  His officiousness was irritating; he constantly offered unsolicited advice. 他爱管闲事，令人讨厌；他不断地提供不请自来的建议。

The officiousness of the security guard made the visitors feel unwelcome.  保安的盛气凌人让游客感到不受欢迎。|  pretentiousness, meddlesomeness, bossiness |  unobtrusiveness, humility, deference | 名词
