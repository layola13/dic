pre-Buddhist: /ˌpriːˈbʊdɪst/ | adj. | 佛教之前的，前佛教时代的 | Existing or occurring before the rise of Buddhism.  Relating to the period or culture before the spread of Buddhism in a particular region. |

例句：

Pre-Buddhist art in India often featured fertility goddesses and animal motifs.  = 印度的佛教前艺术作品通常以生育女神和动物图案为特色。

The archaeological findings reveal much about pre-Buddhist religious practices in Southeast Asia. = 考古发现揭示了东南亚佛教前宗教习俗的许多信息。


近义词：ante-Buddhist, pre-Buddhistic

反义词：post-Buddhist


词性:形容词
