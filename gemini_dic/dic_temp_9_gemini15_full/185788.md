revirescence:/ˌriːvɪˈrɛsəns/| n. |复苏；再生；恢复生机|The renewed growth of plants after a period of dormancy or decline; a return to vigor or activity after a period of decline.  It often refers to the renewed growth of plants in spring, but can also be used metaphorically to describe a renewal of energy, interest, or activity.|例句：The garden showed signs of revirescence after the spring rains. 春雨过后，花园呈现出复苏的景象。

例句：The company experienced a revirescence after the new CEO took over. 新任首席执行官上任后，公司焕发了生机。

近义词：rejuvenation, regeneration, renewal, resurgence|反义词：decline, decay, deterioration, withering|词性:名词
