sanguifier:/ˈsæŋɡwɪfaɪər/| n. |血液生成物，造血剂 |A substance or agent that produces or stimulates the production of blood.  The term is largely obsolete and rarely used in modern medical terminology.|Example:  While "sanguifier" was once used to describe substances believed to increase blood production, modern medicine uses more precise terms.  |近义词：hematopoietic agent, blood former|反义词：blood thinner (indirectly, as it opposes blood production)|名词
