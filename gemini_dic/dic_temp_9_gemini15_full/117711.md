melancholic:/ˈmɛləŋkɑːlɪk/ | adj. |忧郁的，愁闷的 |Feeling or expressing pensive sadness, typically with no obvious cause.  It suggests a thoughtful or wistful sadness, often with a hint of sweetness or romanticism. |例句：He was in a melancholic mood, staring out the window at the rain. 他心情忧郁，望着窗外的大雨出神。

例句：The melancholic melody of the song perfectly captured the film's somber tone. 这首歌忧郁的旋律完美地捕捉了电影阴郁的基调。


近义词：sad, gloomy, depressed, wistful, pensive, moody

反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful, merry, buoyant
