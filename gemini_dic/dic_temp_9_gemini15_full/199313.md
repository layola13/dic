silviculture:/ˈsɪlvɪkʌltʃər/| n. |森林栽培学；造林学|The science and practice of controlling the establishment, growth, composition, health, and quality of forests and woodlands to meet the diverse needs and values of landowners and society.|例句：Silviculture practices vary depending on the species of tree and the desired outcome. 森林栽培实践因树种和预期结果而异。|近义词：forestry, forest management|反义词： deforestation
