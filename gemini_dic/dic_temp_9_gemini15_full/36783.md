colobuses:/ˈkɒləbəsɪz/| n. |疣猴属动物|Colobus monkeys are a genus of Old World monkeys characterized by their long limbs, long tails, and often black and white fur.  They are arboreal and are found in Africa.|  The colobuses in the sanctuary were thriving.  保护区里的疣猴非常繁荣。|  guenons, monkeys|  apes|名词
