redoubles:/rɪˈdʌbəlz/ | v. |加倍；使加倍；再做一遍 | To double again; to repeat with increased intensity or force.  |例句：The company redoubled its efforts to meet the deadline. 公司加倍努力以赶上最后期限。|例句：She redoubled her efforts after the setback.  她在遭受挫折后加倍努力。|近义词：increase, intensify, multiply |反义词：decrease, lessen, diminish |动词
