USAGE->
This word is a possessive form and doesn't have verb conjugations like other parts of speech.  It shows possession and therefore, doesn't follow typical tense structures.  Instead, its usage depends on the context of the sentence.

Example 1:  Ethernet's speed is impressive.  以太网的速度令人印象深刻。
Example 2: We need to check Ethernet's cable connection. 我们需要检查以太网的电缆连接。
