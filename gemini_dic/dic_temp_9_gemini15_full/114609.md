malefactors:/ˈmælɪfæktərz/ | n. |作恶者，罪犯 |People who do evil deeds, especially those who commit crimes.  Malefactors are individuals who engage in harmful or illegal activities, often with malicious intent.  They are responsible for causing harm or damage to others or society. |例句：The malefactors were finally brought to justice.  作恶者最终受到了法律的制裁。

The police are searching for the malefactors responsible for the bank robbery. 警方正在搜寻负责银行抢劫案的罪犯。|近义词：criminals, offenders, culprits, wrongdoers |反义词：benefactors, philanthropists, do-gooders |名词
