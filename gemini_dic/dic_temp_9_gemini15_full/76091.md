furrow:/ˈfʌroʊ/| n. vt.|犁沟；皱纹；皱眉|A furrow is a long, narrow trench in the ground, especially one made by a plough.  It can also refer to a wrinkle or crease in the skin, especially on the forehead, or the act of making such a crease. |例句：The farmer used a plough to create deep furrows in the field. 农民用犁在田里挖出深深的犁沟。

The worry etched deep furrows on his face.  忧虑在他脸上刻下深深的皱纹。


近义词：groove, wrinkle, crease, wrinkle, frown |反义词：smoothness

词源:
