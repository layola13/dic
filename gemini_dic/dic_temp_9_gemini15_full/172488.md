pyramidize:/ˈpɪrəmɪdaɪz/| vt.|  使成金字塔形；使成锥形 | To arrange or shape something into a pyramid or pyramid-like structure.  The process involves stacking or arranging elements in a progressively smaller manner, culminating in a pointed apex.  This can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts. |例句：The builders pyramidized the stones to create a majestic monument.  建造者将石头堆成金字塔状，创造了一座雄伟的纪念碑。

The company pyramidized its organizational structure, resulting in a single CEO at the top.  这家公司将其组织结构建成金字塔形，顶部只有一个首席执行官。

|近义词：conicalize,  taper,  point|反义词：flatten,  level,  spread out| 动词
