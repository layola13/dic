pluteal:/ˈpluːtiəl/| adj.| 羽状的；幼体海胆的 | Relating to the pluteus, the larval stage of certain echinoderms (such as sea urchins and starfish), characterized by its ciliated bands and bilateral symmetry.  | Example: The pluteal larva of the sea urchin is a fascinating example of developmental biology.  幼体海胆的羽状幼虫是发育生物学中一个引人入胜的例子。|近义词：larval (in the context of echinoderm development)|反义词：adult (in the context of echinoderm development)
