evince:/ɪˈvɪns/| vt.|显示；表明；证明|To reveal the presence of (a quality or feeling); to show clearly.  The evidence evinced his guilt.  |His nervousness evinced his anxiety about the upcoming exam.|reveal, demonstrate, manifest, show|conceal, hide, mask|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
His smile evinces his happiness. = 他的笑容显示了他的快乐。
The data evinces a clear trend. = 数据显示了一个清晰的趋势。

一般过去时:
Her actions evinced her true character. = 她的行为显示了她真正的性格。
The study evinced a correlation between the two variables. = 这项研究表明这两个变量之间存在相关性。

现在完成时:
Recent events have evinced a growing concern about climate change. = 近期的事件表明人们对气候变化越来越担忧。
The investigation has evinced significant irregularities. = 调查显示存在严重的违规行为。

过去完成时:
The previous research had evinced the need for further investigation. = 先前的研究已经表明需要进一步调查。
His earlier behavior had evinced a lack of seriousness. = 他之前的行为表明他缺乏认真。

将来时:
Future studies will evince the long-term effects of this policy. = 未来的研究将显示这项政策的长期影响。
The results of the experiment will evince the effectiveness of the new treatment. = 实验结果将显示新疗法的有效性。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence evinced his innocence, he would be released. = 如果证据表明他是无辜的，他就会被释放。
If the data had evinced a different trend, we would have drawn different conclusions. = 如果数据显示不同的趋势，我们会得出不同的结论。

被动语态:
His guilt was evinced by the evidence. = 他的罪行被证据所表明。
The need for reform was evinced by the public outcry. = 公众的强烈抗议表明了改革的必要性。

疑问句:
Does his behavior evince any signs of illness? = 他的行为是否显示出任何疾病的迹象？
What does this data evince about consumer behavior? = 这些数据显示了关于消费者行为的什么信息？

否定句:
The study did not evince any significant differences between the two groups. = 这项研究没有显示两组之间有任何显著差异。
His words did not evince any remorse. = 他的话语没有表现出任何悔恨。
