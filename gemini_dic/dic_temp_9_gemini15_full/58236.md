druidish: /ˈdruːɪdɪʃ/ | adj. | 德鲁伊式的，像德鲁伊一样的 |  Relating to or characteristic of Druids or Druidism; suggesting the mysterious or magical qualities associated with Druids.  The word evokes a sense of ancient wisdom, nature worship, and mystical practices. |

The ancient stones held a certain druidish aura, hinting at forgotten rituals.  =  古老的石头散发着某种德鲁伊式的氛围，暗示着被遗忘的仪式。

His druidish pronouncements were filled with cryptic symbolism and allusions to nature. = 他德鲁伊式的声明充满了神秘的象征主义和对自然的暗示。


近义词：pagan, mystical, occult, shamanistic


反义词：conventional, mundane, ordinary, secular


词性:形容词
