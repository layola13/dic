dermatitis:/ˌdɜːrməˈtaɪtɪs/|/n.|皮炎，皮肤炎|Inflammation of the skin.  It can be caused by various factors, including allergies, irritants, infections, or underlying medical conditions. Symptoms can vary depending on the cause and type of dermatitis, but often include redness, itching, swelling, and rash.|  She developed contact dermatitis after handling poison ivy. 她接触毒藤后患上了接触性皮炎。|eczema, inflammation|healthy skin.|名词
