pulsating:/ˈpʌlseɪtɪŋ/| adj.|搏动的；跳动的；悸动的|Characterized by or exhibiting pulsation; throbbing; beating rhythmically.|The pulsating rhythm of the music filled the room.  音乐的跳动节奏充满了整个房间。The pulsating light of the beacon warned sailors of danger. 指示灯跳动的灯光警告水手危险。| throbbing, beating, vibrating|still, static, constant|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The heart is pulsating steadily. = 心脏在稳定地跳动。
The light pulsates rhythmically. = 灯光有节奏地跳动。

一般过去时:
The light pulsated rapidly. = 灯光快速地跳动。
My head pulsated with pain. = 我的头部因疼痛而跳动。


现在完成时:
The pain has been pulsating all day. = 疼痛已经持续跳动了一整天。
The city has pulsated with energy for centuries. = 这个城市几个世纪以来一直充满活力。


过去完成时:
The wound had pulsated before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，伤口一直在跳动。
The throbbing had pulsated all night long. = 那跳动感持续了一整夜。


将来时:

The city will pulsate with excitement during the festival. = 节日期间，城市将充满兴奋。


虚拟语气:
