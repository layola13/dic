paediatricians:/ˌpiːdiˈætrɪʃənz/| n. |儿科医生| Physicians specializing in the medical care of children.  They diagnose and treat illnesses, injuries, and developmental issues in infants, children, and adolescents. | Example:  Many paediatricians recommend regular check-ups for children. 许多儿科医生建议定期为儿童进行检查。  The paediatrician diagnosed the child with pneumonia. 儿科医生诊断孩子患有肺炎。|近义词：child specialists, pediatric physicians|反义词：gerontologist (specialist in aging)
