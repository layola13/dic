copy:/ˈkɒpi/ /ˈkɑːpi/| vt. n.| 复制，抄写；副本，拷贝 | To reproduce something exactly, usually in writing or printing.  It can also refer to the reproduced item itself. | 例句：He copied the text from the book. 他从书上抄写了这段文字。

例句：She made a copy of the document. 她复印了这份文件。


近义词：duplicate, reproduce, replicate | 反义词：originate, create


词性:动词，名词
