pan: /pæn/ | n. v. | 平底锅；全面批评；淘金盘；<非正式>全景；泛指 |

英文解析:  A pan is a shallow, wide, and usually round container, typically made of metal, used for cooking. It can also refer to a  shallow container used for washing gold (gold pan) or a comprehensive criticism (pan a movie).  In informal usage, it can mean a wide view or panorama (a panoramic view). As a verb, it means to criticize severely.


例句:
She fried the eggs in a frying pan. = 她用平底锅煎鸡蛋。

He panned the new restaurant's food. = 他严厉批评了这家新餐厅的食物。


近义词: (as a noun)  skillet, frying pan, dish, container; (as a verb) criticize, condemn, trash


反义词: (as a verb) praise, commend, applaud


词性: noun, verb


词源:  From Old French *pane, from Latin patena ("shallow dish").
