obsessional:/əbˈsɛʃənəl/ | adj. | 痴迷的；强迫性的 | Characterized by or relating to obsession; compulsively preoccupied with something.  The word highlights a persistent and often distressing preoccupation. |  His obsessional need for cleanliness made it difficult for him to maintain relationships. 他对清洁的痴迷让他难以维持人际关系。 Her obsessional thoughts about her work prevented her from relaxing. 她对工作的痴迷想法使她无法放松。 | obsessive, compulsive | detached, indifferent | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is obsessional about his diet. = 他对饮食很痴迷。
She is obsessional about her appearance. = 她对自己的外表很痴迷。

一般过去时:
He was obsessional about collecting stamps. = 他过去痴迷于集邮。
She was obsessional about keeping her house spotless. = 她过去痴迷于保持房子一尘不染。

现在完成时:
He has been obsessional about fitness for years. = 他多年来一直痴迷于健身。
She has been obsessional about her career since college. = 从大学起，她就一直痴迷于她的事业。

过去完成时:
He had been obsessional about his weight before he started exercising. = 在他开始锻炼之前，他一直很在意自己的体重。
She had been obsessional about her grades throughout high school. = 整个高中时期，她都非常在意自己的成绩。

将来时:
He will be obsessional about his new project. = 他将痴迷于他的新项目。
She will be obsessional about finishing the marathon. = 她将痴迷于完成马拉松比赛。

虚拟语气:
If he were less obsessional, he would be happier. = 如果他不那么痴迷，他会更快乐。
If she weren't so obsessional about work, she'd have more time for her family. = 如果她对工作没有那么痴迷，她就会有更多时间陪伴家人。

被动语态:
His behavior was described as obsessional. = 他的行为被描述为痴迷的。
Her need for control was considered obsessional by her therapist. = 她的控制欲被她的治疗师认为是痴迷的。

疑问句:
Is he obsessional about anything in particular? = 他对什么特别痴迷吗？
Is she obsessional about cleanliness? = 她对清洁很痴迷吗？

否定句:
He is not obsessional about his work. = 他对工作并不痴迷。
She is not obsessional about material possessions. = 她并不痴迷于物质财富。
