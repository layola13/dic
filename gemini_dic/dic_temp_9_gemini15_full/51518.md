detrital:/dɪˈtraɪtəl/| adj.|碎屑的；由碎屑组成的|Consisting of or derived from fragments of rock or other material.|The sandstone is a detrital sedimentary rock, formed from the accumulation of sand grains.|近义词：fragmental, clastic|反义词：in situ, autochthonous

Etymology:
The word "detrital" comes from the Latin word "detritus," meaning "worn away," "rubbish," or "waste."  The suffix "-al" is added to form an adjective.
