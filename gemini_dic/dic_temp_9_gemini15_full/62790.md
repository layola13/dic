engrosser:/ɪnˈɡrɔːsər/ | n. | 专利权买断者；大量收购者；抄写员；(尤指中世纪) 囤积居奇者 | A person who buys up large quantities of something, especially to control its price; a person who makes copies of documents.  | Example:  The engrosser bought up all the available wheat, driving up the price for consumers.  囤积居奇者买光了所有可获得的小麦，导致消费者价格上涨。 |近义词：monopolizer, speculator, copyist | 反义词：distributor, retailer
