lurkers:/ˈlɜːrkərz/| n. |潜伏者，潜藏者，暗中观察者 |People who participate in online communities but rarely or never contribute. They read posts and discussions without actively engaging.  |Example: The forum had many lurkers who never posted their own opinions.  这个论坛有很多潜伏者，他们从未发表过自己的意见。|近义词：observers, watchers, onlookers |反义词：participants, contributors, active members |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Many lurkers silently observe the discussions. = 许多潜伏者默默地观察着讨论。
The lurker reads the posts without commenting. = 潜伏者阅读帖子而不评论。


一般过去时:
Several lurkers were present at the online meeting. = 几位潜伏者出席了在线会议。
He was a lurker for years before finally posting. = 他潜伏多年后才最终发帖。


现在完成时:
She has been a lurker on this forum for months. = 她在这个论坛潜伏了几个月了。
They have lurked on various online communities. = 他们潜伏在各种在线社区。


过去完成时:
Before joining the discussion, he had been a lurker for a long time. = 在加入讨论之前，他潜伏了很长时间。
The user had lurked on the site for years before revealing their identity. = 该用户在透露身份之前，在这个网站潜伏了多年。


将来时:
He will continue to be a lurker for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，他将继续潜伏。
More lurkers will likely join the community over time. = 随着时间的推移，可能有更多潜伏者加入这个社区。


虚拟语气:
If I were a lurker, I wouldn't be able to contribute to the community. = 如果我是个潜伏者，我就无法为社区做出贡献。
I wish I hadn't been a lurker for so long. = 我真希望自己没有潜伏这么久。


被动语态:
The discussions were observed by many lurkers. = 许多潜伏者观察了这些讨论。
The comments were read by the lurker without reply. = 潜伏者阅读了这些评论，但没有回复。


疑问句:
Are there many lurkers in this chat room? = 这个聊天室里有很多潜伏者吗？
Was he a lurker when he first joined the group? = 他第一次加入小组时是潜伏者吗？


否定句:
He is not a lurker; he actively participates. = 他不是潜伏者；他积极参与。
She wasn't just a lurker; she was secretly gathering information. = 她不仅仅是潜伏者；她是在秘密收集信息。
