computably:/kəmˈpjuːtəbli/ | adj. | 可计算的，能计算的 |  Able to be computed; capable of being calculated or determined by a computational process.  This term often refers to the ability of a function or problem to be solved by a Turing machine or an equivalent computational model within a finite amount of time. | Example:  The function is computably enumerable.  这个函数是可枚举的。 | 近义词： calculable, determinable | 反义词：uncomputable, undecidable
