It implies a lack of flexibility and an overemphasis on order and conformity. | 例句：The overregimenting of the school stifled creativity and individuality among the students. 学校的过度严厉管制扼杀了学生的创造力和个性。

The overregimenting of the army led to low morale and high desertion rates.  军队过度军事化管理导致士气低落和逃兵率高。


近义词：overcontrolling, micromanaging, overmanaging, authoritarian, dictatorial | 反义词：laissez-faire, permissive, lenient, flexible
