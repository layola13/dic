frustrations:/ˌfrʌˈstreɪʃənz/ | n. | 挫折；令人沮丧的事 | Feelings of anger and annoyance caused by being unable to do something.  |例句：She expressed her frustrations about the slow progress of the project. 她表达了她对项目进展缓慢的沮丧。

例句：His frustrations stemmed from a lack of opportunities. 他的挫折源于缺乏机会。


近义词：disappointments, setbacks, annoyances, irritations | 反义词：successes, achievements, satisfactions | 词性：名词
