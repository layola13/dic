gorging:/ˈɡɔːrdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 狼吞虎咽；暴食 | To eat greedily and to excess.  |例句：He was gorging himself on chocolates. 他正在狼吞虎咽地吃巧克力。
例句：The birds were gorging on the berries. 鸟儿们正贪婪地啄食浆果。|近义词：stuffing, devouring, glutting |反义词：starving, dieting

一般现在时:
He gorges on pizza every Friday. = 他每个星期五都狼吞虎咽地吃披萨。
They gorge themselves on sweets after dinner. = 他们晚饭后暴食甜食。

一般过去时:
She gorged herself on ice cream last night. = 她昨晚暴食冰淇淋。
The dog gorged on the leftover steak. = 那条狗狼吞虎咽地吃掉了剩下的牛排。

现在完成时:
He has gorged himself on fast food all week. = 他整个星期都在暴食快餐。
We have gorged on delicious pastries at the bakery. = 我们在面包店里狼吞虎咽地吃了美味的糕点。

过去完成时:
They had gorged themselves before the race started. = 比赛开始前他们就已经吃饱了。
She had gorged on candy before realizing how much sugar she had consumed. = 她吃了一堆糖果后才意识到自己吃了多少糖。

词源:
The word "gorge" ultimately derives from the Old French word "gorge," meaning "throat" or "gullet." This reflects the image of someone stuffing their throat with food.  The verb "gorge" evolved from this meaning to describe the act of eating excessively.
