Often used to describe arguments or actions that misuse or misinterpret legal terminology and procedures to achieve illegitimate aims.  These arguments are typically based on misunderstandings, misinterpretations, or deliberate distortions of the law. |例句：He presented a pseudolegal argument to avoid paying taxes. 他提出了一个似是而非的法律论点来逃避纳税。


例句：The defendant's pseudolegal claims were dismissed by the court.  被告的伪法律主张被法院驳回。
