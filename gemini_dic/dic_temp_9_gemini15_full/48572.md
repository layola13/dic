defaults:/dɪˈfɔːlts/| n.|默认值，缺省值，默认设置|The settings that a system or application uses automatically unless the user specifies otherwise.  They are pre-selected options. |例句：The defaults for the printer are set to black and white printing.  打印机的默认设置为黑白打印。

例句：The browser's default search engine is Google. 浏览器的默认搜索引擎是谷歌。


近义词：standards, presets, presettings, assumptions|反义词：customizations,overrides, preferences


词源:  The word "default" comes from the Old French word "defaut," meaning "failure," "fault," or "lack."  Over time, its meaning shifted to refer to the pre-selected option that is used when no other choice is made.
