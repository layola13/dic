pulled:/pʊld/ | v. |拉，拽，拔出 | To exert force on (something) so as to cause movement towards oneself.  It can also imply a forceful or sudden movement. |例句：He pulled the door open. 他拉开了门。

He pulled a muscle lifting weights. 他举重时拉伤了肌肉。|近义词：drew, tugged, dragged |反义词：pushed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pull the weeds from my garden every week. = 我每周都从花园里拔草。
She pulls her hair back in a ponytail. = 她把头发扎成马尾辫。

一般过去时:
He pulled the trigger and fired the gun. = 他扣动了扳机，开枪了。
The horse pulled the cart along the road. = 马拉着车沿路走。

现在完成时:
I have pulled all the weeds in the garden. = 我已经把花园里的杂草都拔掉了。
She has pulled a prank on her friend. = 她捉弄了她的朋友。

过去完成时:
He had pulled the rope before the weight fell. = 在重物落下之前，他已经拉了绳子。
They had pulled several all-nighters to finish the project. = 为了完成项目，他们熬了好几个通宵。

将来时:
I will pull the car over to the side of the road. = 我将把车停靠在路边。
They will pull together to overcome this challenge. = 他们将团结起来克服这个挑战。

虚拟语气:
If I pulled that lever, the machine would start. = 如果我拉动那个杠杆，机器就会启动。
I wish I had pulled myself together sooner. = 我希望我早点振作起来。

被动语态:
The tooth was pulled by the dentist. = 牙齿被牙医拔掉了。
The wagon was pulled by two strong oxen. = 这辆马车由两头强壮的牛拉动。

疑问句:
Did you pull the plug on the washing machine? = 你拔掉了洗衣机的插头吗？
Who pulled the fire alarm? = 谁拉响了火警警报？

否定句:
I didn't pull any punches in my speech. = 我在演讲中没有手下留情。
She didn't pull her weight on the project. = 她在这个项目中没有尽力。
