hostesses:/ˈhoʊstəsɪz/ | n. |女主人；女服务员；空姐|Female attendants who receive and entertain guests or perform other duties, such as serving food and drinks.  They can be found in various settings, from restaurants and hotels to airlines.|例句：The hostesses at the wedding reception were very gracious. 婚礼招待会上的女主人非常热情。|例句：The flight hostesses served drinks and snacks to the passengers. 空姐给乘客送饮料和点心。|近义词：hostesses, waitresses, stewardesses|反义词：guests, patrons
