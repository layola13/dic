inappropriateness: /ˌɪnəˈproʊpriətəsnəs/ | n. | 不适当；不合适 | The quality or state of being inappropriate; unsuitability.  It refers to something that is not suitable or proper for a particular situation, time, or place. |  例句：The inappropriateness of his joke offended many guests.  他的笑话不合适，冒犯了许多客人。


例句：The inappropriateness of her attire was evident at the formal dinner.  她在正式晚宴上的穿着明显不合适。


近义词：unsuitability, impropriety, unfitness, inadequacy


反义词：appropriateness, suitability, fitness, propriety


词性: noun
