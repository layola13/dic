puddles:/ˈpʌdəlz/| n.|水坑，积水|Small collections of water accumulated on a surface, typically after rain.|例句：Children were splashing in the puddles after the rain. 雨后孩子们在水坑里玩水。|近义词：pools, ponds|反义词:dryness


USAGE->

There are many puddles in the street. = 街上有许多水坑。
The rain forms puddles on the pavement. = 雨水在人行道上形成了水坑。


一般过去时:
Puddles formed in the low-lying areas. = 水坑在低洼地区形成。
