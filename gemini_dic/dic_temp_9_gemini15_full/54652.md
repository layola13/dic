disobedience:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiəns/| n. |不服从；违抗；违逆|The act or state of refusing to obey or comply.  It often implies a deliberate refusal to follow rules, orders, or authority.  |例句：His disobedience led to his suspension from school.  他的不服从导致他被学校停学。

例句：The children's disobedience resulted in a loss of privileges. 孩子们的违抗导致他们失去了特权。


近义词：defiance, insubordination, rebellion, noncompliance |反义词：obedience, compliance, submission |词性：名词
