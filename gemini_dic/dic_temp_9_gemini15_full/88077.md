highlighting:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | v. n. | 突出显示；强调 | The act of making something stand out or more noticeable.  It can refer to the process of emphasizing something or making it visually prominent. |例句：The report highlighting the risks of climate change. 这份报告突出了气候变化的风险。


例句：She is highlighting the key points of the presentation. 她正在强调演示文稿的要点。


近义词：emphasizing, stressing, underscoring, accentuating | 反义词：downplaying, obscuring, minimizing | 词性: v. n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are highlighting the importance of education. = 他们正在强调教育的重要性。
He highlights the key findings in his report. = 他在他的报告中突出了主要发现。

一般过去时:
She highlighted the problem in the meeting. = 她在会议上强调了这个问题。
The teacher highlighted the most important words on the board. = 老师在黑板上标注了最重要的单词。

现在完成时:
We have highlighted the need for change. = 我们已经强调了改变的必要性。
They have highlighted the successes of the program. = 他们已经强调了该项目的成功之处。

过去完成时:
He had highlighted the dangers before the expedition. = 他在远征之前就已经强调了危险性。
She had highlighted the inconsistencies in the data. = 她已经强调了数据中的不一致之处。

将来时:
They will highlight the new features in the next presentation. = 他们将在下一次演示中重点介绍新功能。
We will highlight the importance of teamwork. = 我们将强调团队合作的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If I were to write a report, I would highlight the main findings. = 如果我要写一份报告，我会突出主要发现。
I wish I had highlighted that point earlier. = 我希望我早点强调那一点。

被动语态:
The risks were highlighted in the final report. = 风险在最终报告中得到了强调。
The importance of safety is being highlighted by the company. = 公司正在强调安全的重要性。

疑问句:
What are you highlighting in your presentation? = 你在你的演示文稿中强调什么？
Which points should I highlight in my essay? = 我应该在我的论文中强调哪些要点？

否定句:
He did not highlight the negative aspects of the project. = 他没有突出该项目的负面方面。
The report failed to highlight the potential benefits. = 该报告未能突出潜在的好处。
