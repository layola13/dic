illumin:/ɪˈluːmɪn/| vt. |照亮；阐明；启迪| to light up; to make something clear or understandable; to inspire or enlighten someone intellectually or spiritually |例句：The moon illuminated the path. 月亮照亮了道路。  The professor's lecture illuminated the complex topic. 教授的讲座阐明了这个复杂的话题。|近义词：light up, enlighten, clarify, elucidate |反义词：obscure, darken, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun illuminates the earth. = 太阳照亮地球。
The artist illuminates his canvas with vibrant colors. = 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩照亮了他的画布。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
