half-closed: /ˌhɑːfˈkloʊzd/ | adj. | 半闭的，半开的 | Partially closed; not fully open.  |例句：The door was half-closed, letting in a sliver of light. 门半掩着，透进一丝光线。

The eyes were half-closed in sleep.  眼睛半睁着，像是睡着了。


近义词：partially closed, ajar, slightly open | 反义词：wide open, fully open, closed


词源:  The word is a compound of "half" (meaning one of two equal parts) and "closed" (past participle of the verb "to close").  It reflects a state where something is in a transitional phase between being open and being completely closed.
