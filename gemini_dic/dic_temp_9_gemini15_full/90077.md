hostess:/ˈhoʊstəs/ | n. |女主人；女招待；空姐 | A woman who receives guests; a woman employed to serve guests in a restaurant or bar; a flight attendant on an aircraft. |例句：She was a gracious hostess, making all her guests feel welcome. 她是一位亲切的女主人，让所有客人都感到宾至如归。

例句：The hostess at the restaurant seated us at a table by the window. 餐厅的女招待把我们安排在靠窗的桌子旁。


近义词：host, waitress, stewardess, flight attendant |反义词：guest
