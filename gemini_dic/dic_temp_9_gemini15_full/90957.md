hurrahed:/hʊˈrɑːd/| v.|欢呼；高呼万岁|To shout "hurrah".  It's a past tense form of "hurrah".|例句：The crowd hurrahed when the winning team appeared.  人群在获胜队伍出现时欢呼起来。
The soldiers hurrahed for their commanding officer. 士兵们为他们的指挥官欢呼。|近义词：cheered, shouted, applauded|反义词：booed, hissed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hurrah for the president. = 他们为总统欢呼。
We hurrah when our team wins. = 当我们队获胜时，我们欢呼。


一般过去时:
The crowd hurrahed wildly. = 人群疯狂地欢呼。
He hurrahed with delight. = 他高兴地欢呼。


现在完成时:
They have hurrahed for every goal scored. = 他们为每一个进球都欢呼。
The fans have hurrahed throughout the game. = 粉丝们在整场比赛中都在欢呼。


过去完成时:
They had hurrahed before the announcement was made. = 他们在宣布之前就欢呼了。
The audience had hurrahed enthusiastically. = 观众们热情地欢呼了。


将来时:
They will hurrah when the hero arrives. = 英雄到来时，他们会欢呼。
The supporters will hurrah loudly. = 支持者们会大声欢呼。


虚拟语气:
If they won, they would hurrah. = 如果他们赢了，他们就会欢呼。
I wish they would hurrah for the team. = 我希望他们会为球队欢呼。


被动语态:
The victory was hurrahed by everyone. = 胜利受到了所有人的欢呼。
(Note: Passive voice is less common with this verb.)


疑问句:
Did they hurrah? = 他们欢呼了吗？
Will they hurrah for the new leader? = 他们会为新领导人欢呼吗？


否定句:
They did not hurrah. = 他们没有欢呼。
They wouldn't hurrah for the opposition. = 他们不会为反对派欢呼。


PHRASE->
There are limited common phrases using "hurrahed" as it's primarily a past tense verb.  The emphasis is on the act itself, rather than phrasal verbs.  Examples could be constructed, but aren't standard expressions.  For example:  "Hurrahed their hearts out" (欢呼得非常卖力).
