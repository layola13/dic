iridioscope:/ɪˌrɪdiˈoʊskoʊp/| n. |虹膜镜|An instrument used to examine the iris of the eye, often to detect certain diseases or conditions.  It typically involves illuminating the iris and magnifying it for detailed observation.|The doctor used an iridioscope to examine the patient's iris for signs of glaucoma.|近义词：iriscope|反义词：无
