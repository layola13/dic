manifold:/ˈmænɪfoʊld/| adj., n.|多样的；多方面的；歧管|


英文解析:
As an adjective, "manifold" means having many different forms or types.  As a noun, it refers to a pipe or tube with several branches, or in mathematics, a type of space.

例句:
The possibilities are manifold. = 可能性多种多样。

The software offers manifold advantages. = 该软件具有诸多优势。


近义词:
numerous, various, multiple, diverse, complex

反义词:
single, simple, uniform, unvaried


词性:
adjective, noun
