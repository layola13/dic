dissociable:/dɪˈsoʊʃəbl/| adj.| 可分离的；可解离的；不相容的 | Capable of being separated or disconnected;  easily separated or disassociated;  incompatible.  It implies a lack of connection or integration between things or ideas. | Example: The two concepts are dissociable in theory, but in practice they often overlap.  理论上这两个概念是可以分离的，但在实践中它们经常重叠。

The effects of the drug are dissociable from its side effects.  这种药物的疗效与其副作用是可以区分的。|近义词：separable, detachable, distinct, independent |反义词： inseparable, integrated, unified, connected
