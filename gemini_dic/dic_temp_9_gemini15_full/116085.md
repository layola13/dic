martyrdom:/ˈmɑːrtərdəm/| n. |殉道；殉难；受苦；苦行|The state or fact of being a martyr; the act of being put to death or enduring great suffering on behalf of a belief, principle, or cause.  It often implies a courageous and unwavering commitment to one's beliefs, even in the face of death or severe hardship. |例句：He faced martyrdom with unwavering faith. 他以坚定不移的信念面对殉道。

例句：Her quiet endurance of her illness could be considered a form of martyrdom. 她默默忍受疾病，这可以被看作一种殉道的行为。


近义词：sacrifice, self-sacrifice, suffering, persecution

反义词：betrayal, cowardice, surrender
