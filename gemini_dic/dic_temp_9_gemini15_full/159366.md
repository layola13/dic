pokerish:/ˈpoʊkərɪʃ/| adj.|像扑克脸的；冷漠的；不露声色的|Resembling a poker face; impassive; emotionless.  It suggests a deliberate attempt to conceal one's feelings, often implying coldness or indifference. |例句：He kept a pokerish expression throughout the interrogation. 他在整个审讯过程中都保持着冷漠的表情。


例句：Her pokerish demeanor hid a storm of emotions inside. 她冷漠的态度掩盖了她内心的情感风暴。


近义词：impassive, stoic, unemotional, impassive, expressionless


反义词：expressive, emotional, demonstrative


词性：形容词
