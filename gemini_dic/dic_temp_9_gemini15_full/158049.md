plash:/plæʃ/| n. v.|飞溅；泼溅声|The sound of water splashing against the rocks was soothing. 水拍打岩石的声音很舒缓。|近义词：splash, splosh, spatter|反义词：drip, trickle

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water splashes against the rocks. = 水拍打着岩石。
He splashes water on his face. = 他把水泼在脸上。

一般过去时:
The rain splashed against the window. = 雨水拍打着窗户。
She splashed paint on the canvas. = 她把颜料泼在画布上。

现在完成时:
The children have splashed in the pool all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在游泳池里玩水。
He has splashed mud all over his trousers. = 他把泥巴溅到了裤子上。

过去完成时:
The car had splashed mud all over my dress.  = 汽车已经把泥巴溅到了我的裙子上。
They had splashed paint on the wall before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经把油漆泼到墙上了。

将来时:
The waves will splash against the shore. = 海浪将拍打着海岸。
I will splash some cold water on my face. = 我会在脸上泼些冷水。

虚拟语气:
If it rained, the water would splash onto the window. = 如果下雨，水就会溅到窗户上。
I wish the children hadn't splashed so much mud. = 我真希望孩子们不要溅那么多泥。

被动语态:
The walls were splashed with paint. = 墙壁被泼上了油漆。
The car was splashed with mud. = 汽车被溅上了泥巴。

疑问句:
Did the water splash on your clothes? = 水溅到你的衣服上了吗？
Will the waves splash over the rocks? = 海浪会拍打过岩石吗？

否定句:
The rain didn't splash against the windows. = 雨水没有拍打窗户。
He didn't splash the water on purpose. = 他不是故意泼水的。


PHRASE->
splash of color: 一抹色彩
make a splash: 引人注目；大出风头
splash screen: 欢迎画面
splash down: (航天器)溅落
splash out: 挥霍；大肆花费
