interposes:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊz/| vt.|插入；介入；打断；提出（异议）|To come between; to intervene; to interrupt.  It implies a temporary intrusion or interruption, often to mediate or object.|例句：He interposed himself between the two fighting men. 他介入两个打架的人中间。|例句：She interposes objections to the plan. 她对这个计划提出异议。|近义词：intervene, interrupt, mediate|反义词：ignore, neglect, allow
