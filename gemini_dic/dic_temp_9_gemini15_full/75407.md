fructose:/ˈfruːktoʊs/| n. |果糖 |A simple ketonic monosaccharide found in many plants, especially fruits; a type of sugar.  It is sweeter than sucrose (table sugar).| Example: Fruits like apples and bananas are rich in fructose. 苹果和香蕉富含果糖。  Many processed foods contain high fructose corn syrup. 许多加工食品含有高果糖玉米糖浆。|近义词：levulose, fruit sugar |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
