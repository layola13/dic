flit:/flɪt/| v.|快速移动，飞逝，掠过|to move swiftly and lightly; to pass quickly|The hummingbird flitted from flower to flower.  蜂鸟在花丛中飞来飞去。|The years flitted by.  岁月飞逝。|dart, flutter, whisk, zip|stay, remain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The butterfly flits among the flowers. 蝴蝶在花丛中飞舞。
Birds flit between the branches. 鸟儿在树枝间飞掠。

一般过去时:
A shadow flitted across the wall. 一道影子掠过墙壁。
The thought flitted through my mind. 这个念头一闪而过我的脑海。

现在完成时:
The years have flitted by so quickly. 岁月飞逝得如此之快。
Ideas have flitted through my head all day.  我整天脑子里都在胡思乱想。

过去完成时:
Before I knew it, the hours had flitted past.  不知不觉中，几个小时就过去了。
The opportunity had flitted by before I could seize it.  机会稍纵即逝，我未能抓住。

将来时:
The birds will flit through the air tomorrow. 明天鸟儿将会在空中飞舞。
I think that the time will flit by quickly. 我认为时间会飞逝而过。

虚拟语气:
If only I could flit away from all my troubles.  但愿我能逃离我所有的烦恼。
I wish I could flit through time and see the future.  我希望我能穿越时空，看到未来。

被动语态:
(Not commonly used in passive voice, as it implies active, swift movement)  (由于它暗示着主动、快速的运动，所以很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Does the hummingbird flit quickly? 蜂鸟飞得快吗？
Did the thought flit through your mind? 这个念头闪过你的脑海了吗？


否定句:
The bird did not flit away. 鸟没有飞走。
The image did not flit across my mind.  那个影像没有在我脑海中闪过。
