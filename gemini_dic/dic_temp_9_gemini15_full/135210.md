nonostentation: /ˌnɒnˌɒsteɪnˈteɪʃən/ | n. |不炫耀，不张扬|The act or practice of not showing off or boasting; modesty; unpretentiousness.  It suggests a deliberate avoidance of ostentation, a conscious choice to be humble and understated.|例句：Her nonostentation was a refreshing change from the usual displays of wealth among her peers.  她的不炫耀与同龄人中常见的炫富形成了令人耳目一新的对比。

例句：The simplicity of the design reflected the architect's commitment to nonostentation.  设计的简洁体现了建筑师对不张扬的承诺。


近义词：modesty, humility, unpretentiousness, simplicity, restraint


反义词：ostentation, showiness, flamboyance, conspicuous consumption


词性：名词
