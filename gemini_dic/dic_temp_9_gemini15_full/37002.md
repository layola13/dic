columbine:/ˈkʌləmbaɪn/| n. |翠雀花；(尤指)赤雀花 |A columbine is a type of flowering plant with spurred petals.  They are known for their delicate and often brightly colored flowers. The name is often associated with the common columbine (Aquilegia vulgaris).|The garden was ablaze with vibrant columbines.|近义词：wildflower,  flower|反义词：无
