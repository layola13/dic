redowa:/ˈrɛdəʊə/| n. |雷多瓦舞曲|A Redowa is a lively, moderately fast waltz in 3/4 time, characterized by a distinctive rhythm and a flowing, graceful style.  It originated in Bohemia in the early 19th century and became popular throughout Europe and beyond.|The dancers gracefully moved to the rhythm of the redowa.|近义词：waltz, mazurka|反义词：无
