distress:/dɪˈstrɛs/| n. vt.|痛苦，忧虑；危难；使痛苦；使忧虑|The noun "distress" refers to extreme anxiety, sorrow, or pain;  a state of suffering or great difficulty. As a verb, "distress" means to cause someone or something to suffer extreme anxiety, sorrow, or pain.  |例句：The news of the accident caused her great distress.  事故的消息让她非常痛苦。

The storm distressed the small fishing boats. 暴风雨使那些小渔船受损。|近义词：anguish, suffering, affliction, misery, trouble |反义词：comfort, ease, relief, joy
