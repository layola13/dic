reopen:/ˌriˈoʊpən/ | vt. |重新开放，重新开始 |To open something again after it has been closed.  |例句：The store reopened after renovations. 商店装修后重新开业了。|例句：They decided to reopen the investigation. 他们决定重新开始调查。|近义词：restart, resume |反义词：close, shut down |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop reopens tomorrow. = 商店明天重新开业。
They reopen the case every year. = 他们每年都重新审理此案。

一般过去时:
The museum reopened last week. = 博物馆上周重新开放了。
She reopened the old wound with her words. = 她的话重新揭开了旧伤疤。

现在完成时:
They have reopened the investigation into the accident. = 他们已经重新调查了这起事故。
The school has reopened after the fire. = 学校大火后重新开学了。

过去完成时:
The business had reopened before I arrived. = 我到达之前，生意已经重新开始了。
They had reopened the discussion several times before reaching a conclusion. = 他们达成结论之前已经几次重新讨论了。

将来时:
The restaurant will reopen next month. = 餐厅下个月重新开业。
The government will reopen the border soon. = 政府很快就会重新开放边境。

虚拟语气:
If they reopened the factory, they would create many jobs. = 如果他们重新开办工厂，将会创造许多就业机会。
I wish they would reopen the library. = 我希望他们能重新开放图书馆。

被动语态:
The road was reopened after the repairs. = 道路维修后重新开放了。
The case is being reopened due to new evidence. = 由于新的证据，案件正在重新审理。

疑问句:
Will the school reopen in September? = 学校会在九月重新开学吗？
When will the bridge be reopened? = 大桥什么时候重新开放？

否定句:
The shop did not reopen until next week. = 商店直到下周才重新开业。
They have not reopened the investigation yet. = 他们还没有重新开始调查。
