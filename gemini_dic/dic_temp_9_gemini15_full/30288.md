/sɛn/ | n. |分，厘；分币|A cent is a unit of currency equal to one hundredth of a dollar.|例句：The price is 10 cents. 价格是10美分。|近义词：penny|反义词：dollar


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A cent is a small amount of money. = 一分钱是一小笔钱。
How many cents do you have? = 你有多少分钱？


一般过去时:
He paid five cents for the candy. = 他花了五分钱买糖果。
She gave me a few cents for the tip. = 她给了我几美分的小费。


现在完成时:
I have saved many cents in my piggy bank. = 我在我的储钱罐里存了很多分钱。
Have you ever seen a two-cent stamp? = 你见过两分钱的邮票吗？


过去完成时:
I had already spent all my cents before I got to the store. = 我到商店之前已经把所有的分钱都花光了。
They had saved enough cents to buy a new toy. = 他们已经攒够了足够的钱买一个新玩具。


将来时:
I will have many cents by the end of the week. = 到周末我将会有很多分钱。
Will the price go up a few cents next year? = 明年价格会上涨几美分吗？


虚拟语气:
If I had a cent, I would buy a candy. = 如果我有一分钱，我会买糖果。
I wish I had more cents to donate. = 我希望我有更多钱可以捐赠。


被动语态:
A cent is often used as a small unit of currency. = 分币通常用作货币的小单位。
The price was decreased by one cent. = 价格降低了一分钱。


疑问句:
How many cents are there in a dollar? = 一美元有多少分？
How much does this cost in cents? = 这个用分钱算多少钱？


否定句:
I don't have any cents left. = 我一分钱都没剩下了。
He didn't give me enough cents for the bus. = 他没有给我足够的钱坐公共汽车。
