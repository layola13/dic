phantoms:/ˈfæntəmz/| n.|幽灵，幻影，幻象|A phantom is something that appears to exist but is not real, or a ghost.  It can refer to a ghostly apparition, a figment of the imagination, or something elusive and hard to define.|例句：The flickering lights created strange phantoms in the shadows. 闪烁的灯光在阴影中创造出奇异的幻影。

例句：He dismissed their concerns as mere phantoms of his imagination. 他认为他们的担忧只是他想象中的虚幻之物。|近义词：ghost, apparition, illusion, specter|反义词：reality, substance, truth|名词
