potpourris:/ˌpɒpʊˈriːz/| n. |香料混合物；杂烩；混合体 |A potpourri is a mixture of dried, usually fragrant, flowers, leaves, and spices.  It can also refer to a collection of diverse things. |例句：The vase held a delightful potpourri of dried roses and lavender.  花瓶里装着玫瑰和薰衣草的混合香料。

例句：Her new book is a potpourri of essays on various topics. 她的新书是关于各种主题的杂文集。


近义词：mixture, medley, assortment, collection

反义词：uniformity, homogeneity


词性：名词

词源:
From French *pot-pourri*, literally "rotten pot," from *pot* "pot" + *pourri* "rotten."  The original meaning referred to a stew of leftover meats and vegetables, later evolving to refer to the fragrant mixtures of dried flowers and spices.
