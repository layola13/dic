kennedy:/ˈkɛnɪdi/ | n. |肯尼迪（姓氏）|The surname Kennedy, of Irish origin, is associated with a prominent American political family.  The name is derived from the Gaelic name "Ó Cinnéide," meaning "descendant of Cinnéid."  Many famous Kennedys, including John F. Kennedy, a former US President, have significantly shaped American history. |The Kennedy family has a long and influential history in American politics. |近义词: (as a surname)  None directly equivalent |反义词: (Not applicable as a surname)|名词
