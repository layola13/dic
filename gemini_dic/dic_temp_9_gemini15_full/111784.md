loft-bed:/lɒft bed/| n.|阁楼床，高架床|A loft bed is a bed that is raised high off the ground, often with storage space underneath.  This design is popular in small spaces to maximize floor area.|例句：My son loves his loft bed; he has plenty of room to play underneath.|近义词：high bed, elevated bed|反义词：low bed, ground bed

词源:

The word "loft" originates from the Old English word "loft," meaning "air," "sky," or "upper room."  Over time, it came to refer to a space under a roof, often used for storage.  Combining "loft" with "bed" creates a descriptive term for a bed elevated to utilize the vertical space.
