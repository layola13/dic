ostleress:/ˈɒstlərɛs/ | n. | 女马夫，女照料马匹的人 | A woman who attends to horses in a stable.  | The ostleress carefully groomed the prize-winning stallion. 女马夫仔细地梳理了获奖种马。 |  groom, stablehand |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
