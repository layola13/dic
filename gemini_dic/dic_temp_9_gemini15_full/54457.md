disinhume:/dɪsɪnˈhjuːm/| vt.|掘出（通常指埋葬的东西）；重新启用；使复苏|To dig up (something buried, especially a body); to resume use of something; to revive or restore something from a state of neglect or inactivity.|例句：The archaeologists disinhumed ancient artifacts from the tomb. 考古学家们从坟墓中挖掘出古代文物。
例句：The company disinhumed its old marketing strategy after a period of decline. 公司在经历一段时间的衰退后重新启用其旧的营销策略。|近义词：exhume, unearth, resurrect|反义词：bury, inter, entomb
