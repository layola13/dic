dragonish:/ˈdræɡənɪʃ/| adj.|像龙的；龙形的；凶猛的|Resembling a dragon; having the characteristics of a dragon; fierce or frightening.  The word suggests a likeness to a dragon, often emphasizing its fierceness or mythical qualities, rather than a literal transformation.|例句：He had a dragonish glare. 他怒目而视，像龙一样。

例句：The dragonish creature roared and breathed fire.  那个龙形的生物咆哮着喷火。


近义词：draconic, reptilian, fearsome, terrifying|反义词：gentle, mild, amiable, peaceful
