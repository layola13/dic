infesters: /ɪnˈfɛstərz/ | n. | 侵扰者；寄生虫；有害生物 | Those who or that which infests.  A pest that infests a place or thing. | 例句：The house was infested with rats. 这房子里老鼠成灾了。

The swarms of locusts were terrible infesters, devastating the crops.  蝗虫群是可怕的害虫，毁坏了庄稼。|近义词：pests, parasites, invaders |反义词：benefactors, protectors

一般现在时:
The rats infest the old house. = 老鼠侵扰着那栋旧房子。
These insects infest the garden. = 这些昆虫侵扰着花园。

一般过去时:
Cockroaches infested the kitchen. = 蟑螂侵扰了厨房。
The area was infested with mosquitoes last summer. = 去年夏天，这个地区蚊子成灾。

现在完成时:
The garden has been infested with aphids all summer. = 整个夏天蚜虫都侵扰着花园。
The building has been infested by termites for years. = 这栋建筑物多年来一直被白蚁侵扰。

过去完成时:
The farmer had already noticed that his crops had been infested before the harvest. = 农民在收割前就已经注意到他的庄稼受到了侵害。
The house had been infested with mice for months before they discovered it. = 在他们发现之前，老鼠已经侵扰了房子好几个月了。

将来时:
The experts predict that the area will be infested with locusts next year. = 专家预测明年该地区将遭受蝗灾。
If we don't take action, the pests will infest our entire farm. = 如果我们不采取行动，害虫将会侵扰我们的整个农场。

虚拟语气:
If the house were not infested with insects, we would move in. = 如果房子没有被昆虫侵扰，我们会搬进去。
I wish the garden were not infested with weeds. = 我希望花园没有杂草丛生。

被动语态:
The building was infested with ants. = 这栋楼被蚂蚁侵扰了。
The field was infested by a plague of grasshoppers. = 田地被蝗虫灾害侵扰了。

疑问句:
Is the house infested with insects? = 这房子被昆虫侵扰了吗？
Are the crops infested with pests? = 庄稼被害虫侵扰了吗？

否定句:
The house is not infested with bugs. = 这房子没有被虫子侵扰。
The farm wasn't infested with rats. = 农场没有被老鼠侵扰。

词源:
The word "infester" derives from the verb "infest," which in turn comes from the Latin word "infestare," meaning "to attack," "to assail," or "to beset."  The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or doer of the action.  Therefore, an "infester" is something that performs the action of infesting.
