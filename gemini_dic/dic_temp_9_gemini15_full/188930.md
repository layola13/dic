ruthlessly:/ˈruːθləsli/| adv.|无情地；残酷地；冷酷地|In a way that shows no pity or compassion; cruelly.|例句：He ruthlessly pursued his ambition. 他残酷地追求他的野心。|例句：The company ruthlessly cut costs. 公司无情地削减成本。|近义词：mercilessly, cruelly, brutally, pitilessly|反义词：mercifully, compassionately, kindly
