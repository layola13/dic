overdebate:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪt/| v.|过度争论，喋喋不休地争论|To debate or discuss something excessively or to the point of being unproductive.  It implies that the discussion has gone on for too long and hasn't yielded any useful results.|例句：They overdebated the minor details of the contract, delaying the signing process. 他们对合同的细枝末节争论不休，延误了签署流程。


例句：The committee overdebated the budget allocation, leading to a standstill. 委员会对预算分配争论不休，导致了僵局。|近义词：overdiscuss, excessively argue, belabor, quibble|反义词：concisely discuss, briefly debate, agree readily.
