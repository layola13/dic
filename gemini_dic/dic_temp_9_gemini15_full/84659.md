handbill:/ˈhændbɪl/| n. |传单，小广告 |A handbill is a small printed advertisement or notice that is distributed by hand.  It is typically used to announce an event, promote a product or service, or disseminate information. |Example:  He distributed handbills advertising the upcoming concert. 他分发了宣传即将举行的音乐会的传单。


Example: The shop owner put up handbills announcing a sale. 店主张贴了传单，宣布促销。|近义词：flyer, leaflet, circular |反义词：None.
