inspection:/ɪnˈspɛkʃən/ | n. | 检查；视察；检阅 | The act of officially examining something to check its quality, condition, or safety.  It involves a careful and thorough examination to identify any problems or defects. |例句：The building passed its final inspection. 这座建筑物通过了最终检查。

例句：A thorough inspection revealed several safety hazards.  彻底检查发现了几个安全隐患。


近义词：examination, survey, review, scrutiny | 反义词：neglect, oversight


词源：源自于古法语“inspeccion”，最终来自拉丁语“inspectio”，由“inspicere”（仔细看）构成，由“in”（进入）和“specere”（看）组成。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inspector conducts regular inspections. = 检查员定期进行检查。
This factory undergoes frequent inspections. = 这个工厂经常进行检查。


一般过去时:
The mechanic performed a detailed inspection of the engine. = 机械师对发动机进行了详细检查。
They conducted a safety inspection last week. = 他们上周进行了一次安全检查。


现在完成时:
The team has completed the inspection of the bridge. = 团队已经完成了对桥梁的检查。
They have not yet finished the final inspection. = 他们尚未完成最终检查。


过去完成时:
The engineers had already completed the pre-flight inspection. = 工程师们已经完成了飞行前的检查。
Before the accident, they had not had a recent inspection. = 事故发生前，他们没有进行过最近的检查。


将来时:
We will conduct a thorough inspection tomorrow. = 我们明天将进行彻底检查。
The authorities will perform an inspection next month. = 当局下个月将进行检查。


虚拟语气:
If we had done a proper inspection, we would have found the problem earlier. = 如果我们进行了适当的检查，我们就能早点发现问题。
I wish we had a more thorough inspection before purchase. = 我希望我们在购买前进行更彻底的检查。


被动语态:
The equipment was inspected yesterday. = 设备昨天进行了检查。
The building is being inspected for structural damage. = 正在检查建筑物的结构损坏情况。


疑问句:
Has the inspection been completed? = 检查完成了吗？
When will the inspection take place? = 检查将在何时进行？


否定句:
The inspection did not reveal any significant problems. = 检查没有发现任何重大问题。
They did not conduct a proper inspection. = 他们没有进行适当的检查。
