nove:/ˈnɒvəl/| n. |小说，长篇故事|A novel is a long fictional narrative, typically written in prose.|Example:  She's writing a historical novel set in 18th-century England. 她正在写一部以18世纪英国为背景的历史小说。|近义词：story, tale, narrative |反义词：poem, short story
