disfurniture: /ˌdɪsˈfɜːrnɪtʃər/ | n. | 去除家具；搬走家具 | The act of removing furniture from a place.  It implies a deliberate and often complete removal of furnishings.  | Example: The disfurniture of the apartment was complete before the new tenants moved in.  公寓在新的租户搬进来之前已经完全清空了家具。 | 近义词：defurnishing, removal of furniture | 反义词：furnishing

一般现在时:
The landlord disfurnishes apartments before new tenants arrive. = 房东在新的租户到达之前会清空公寓的家具。
They disfurnish the office every Friday. = 他们每个星期五都会清空办公室的家具。


一般过去时:
He disfurnished his old house before selling it. = 他在卖掉旧房子之前清空了里面的家具。
The movers disfurnished the warehouse efficiently. = 搬运工高效地清空了仓库的家具。


现在完成时:
They have disfurnished the entire building. = 他们已经清空了整栋楼的家具。
She has disfurnished her bedroom to repaint the walls. = 她已经清空了她的卧室以便重新粉刷墙壁。


过去完成时:
The previous tenants had disfurnished the apartment before we moved in. = 我们搬进来之前，之前的租户已经清空了公寓的家具。
They had disfurnished the shop before the renovation began. = 在装修开始之前，他们已经清空了商店的家具。


将来时:

The company will disfurnish the old office before relocating. = 公司会在搬迁之前清空旧办公室的家具。


虚拟语气:
