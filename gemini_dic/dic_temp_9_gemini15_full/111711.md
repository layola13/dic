loco:/ˈloʊkoʊ/ | adj. | 疯狂的；失去理智的；迷失方向的 | crazy; insane; deranged; out of one's mind;  unhinged;  The man was loco after the accident. 事故后，那个人疯了。|近义词：insane, crazy, mad, demented, unbalanced | 反义词：sane, rational, sensible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is loco. = 他疯了。
She seems a little loco. = 她看起来有点疯。


一般过去时:
He was loco with grief. = 他因悲伤而发疯。
They went loco when they won the lottery. = 他们中了彩票后欣喜若狂。


现在完成时:
He has gone loco. = 他疯了。
She has been acting loco lately. = 她最近行为古怪。


过去完成时:
He had gone loco before the incident. = 在事故发生前他就已经疯了。
They had been loco for a long time. = 他们疯了很久了。


将来时:
He will go loco if he fails the exam. = 如果考试不及格，他会疯的。
She will probably go loco with excitement. = 她可能会兴奋得发疯。


虚拟语气:
If I were loco, I would do that. = 如果我疯了，我会那样做。
I wish I weren't loco. = 我希望我没有疯。


被动语态:
(Loco is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is he loco? = 他疯了吗？
Are you loco? = 你疯了吗？


否定句:
He is not loco. = 他没疯。
She doesn't seem loco. = 她看起来不疯。
