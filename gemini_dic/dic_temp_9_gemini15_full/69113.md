favellas:/fəˈvɛləz/| n.|贫民窟|Informal settlements or shantytowns, typically located on the outskirts of large cities in Brazil and other countries in Latin America.  They are characterized by substandard housing, inadequate infrastructure, and high levels of poverty and crime.|Example: The favelas of Rio de Janeiro are known for their vibrant culture and challenging living conditions.  里约热内卢的贫民窟以其充满活力的文化和艰苦的生活条件而闻名。|近义词：shantytown, slum, squatter settlement|反义词：affluent neighborhood, upscale community
