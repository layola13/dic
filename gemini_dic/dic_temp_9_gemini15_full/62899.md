enlightens:/ɪnˈlaɪtnz/ | vt. |启发；阐明；教化 |To illuminate, to inform, to instruct, to make something clearer or easier to understand. It implies a process of revealing something previously unknown or unclear.  |The professor's lecture enlightened the students on the complexities of quantum physics.  教授的讲座使学生们了解了量子物理学的复杂性。|illuminates, clarifies, elucidates |confuses, obscures, bewilders|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun enlightens the world. = 太阳照亮世界。
This book enlightens readers on the subject of ancient history. = 这本书启发了读者对古代历史的了解。


一般过去时:
The teacher enlightened the students on the importance of hard work. = 老师向学生们阐明了努力工作的重要性。
The experience enlightened me about the realities of life. = 这段经历让我了解了生活的现实。


现在完成时:
Reading this book has enlightened me greatly. = 阅读这本书让我受益匪浅。
His speech has enlightened the public on the issue of climate change. = 他的演讲使公众了解了气候变化问题。


过去完成时:
She had been enlightened by her travels abroad. = 她从国外的旅行中受益匪浅。
They had already been enlightened about the dangers before the project began. = 项目开始前，他们就已经了解了其中的危险。


将来时:
This new technology will enlighten us on the mysteries of the universe. = 这项新技术将帮助我们揭开宇宙的奥秘。
The upcoming conference will enlighten participants about the latest research. = 即将举行的会议将使与会者了解最新的研究成果。


虚拟语气:
If only I had been enlightened earlier! = 要是我早点明白就好了！
I wish the article had enlightened me more on the topic. = 我希望这篇文章能更多地阐明该主题。


被动语态:
The students were enlightened by the guest speaker. = 学生们受到了客座演讲者的启发。
The audience was enlightened by the professor's explanation. = 观众被教授的解释所启发。


疑问句:
Will this new evidence enlighten the jury? = 这项新证据会让陪审团明白吗？
Does this explanation enlighten you on the problem? = 这个解释能让你明白这个问题吗？


否定句:
The explanation didn't enlighten me at all. = 这个解释我一点也不明白。
His words didn't enlighten her on the situation. = 他的话并没有让她了解情况。
