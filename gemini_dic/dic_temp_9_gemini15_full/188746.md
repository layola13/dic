runnion:/ˈrʌnjən/| n. |溃烂；脓疮；流脓的伤口| A runnion is a sore or ulcer that discharges pus.  The term is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  It suggests a foul and festering wound.|例句：The beggar's leg was a festering runnion.  = 乞丐的腿上是一个溃烂流脓的伤口。  The old wound became a painful runnion. = 旧伤变成了一个疼痛的溃烂。|近义词：sore, ulcer, abscess, fester, boil|反义词：heal, healthy skin
