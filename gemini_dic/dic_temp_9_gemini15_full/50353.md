depolishing: /ˌdiːˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 去抛光；再抛光 | The process of removing a previous polish, often to correct imperfections or prepare a surface for a new polish.  It involves removing a layer of material to achieve a smoother, more even finish. | Example: The jeweler performed a depolishing to remove scratches from the bracelet before re-polishing it. = 珠宝匠在重新抛光手镯之前进行了去抛光处理，以去除划痕。


近义词：re-polishing, refinishing, smoothing

反义词：polishing
