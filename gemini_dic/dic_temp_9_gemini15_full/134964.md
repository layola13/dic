These individuals may be residing in the country legally or illegally, and their status can vary depending on their immigration status and the laws of the host country. | Example: The new immigration policy affects nonnationals living in the country.  新的移民政策影响居住在该国的外国人。  The government is providing assistance to nonnationals seeking asylum. 政府正在向寻求庇护的外国人提供援助。|近义词：foreigners, aliens, expatriates |反义词：nationals, citizens |名词
