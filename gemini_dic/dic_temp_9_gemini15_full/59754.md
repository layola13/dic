economical:/ˌɛkəˈnɒmɪkl/ /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪkl/ | adj. |节约的；经济的；合算的 |  Relating to or involving the use of minimum resources to achieve maximum results.  It suggests efficiency and avoidance of waste.  | 例句：This car is very economical on fuel. 这辆车非常省油。

This small apartment is more economical than the large house. 这间小公寓比大房子更经济实惠。

近义词：thrifty, frugal, inexpensive, cost-effective | 反义词：wasteful, extravagant, expensive, prodigal
