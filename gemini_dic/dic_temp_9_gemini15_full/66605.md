exigent:/ˈɛksɪdʒənt/| adj.|紧急的；要求高的；苛刻的|demanding or urgent; requiring immediate action or attention; characterized by urgency or pressure.|The exigent circumstances required immediate action. 紧急的情况需要立即采取行动。|The job demands an exigent employee. 这份工作需要一位要求高的员工。|urgent, pressing, critical, crucial|unimportant, leisurely, relaxed, calm

词源:
From Middle French *exigent, from Latin exigent-, present participle of exigere "to demand, require, drive out," from ex- "out" + agere "to drive, do, act".  The meaning shifted from "demanding" to "urgent" over time.
