heroical:/hɪˈroʊɪkəl/ | adj. | 英勇的；英雄的；壮烈的 |  Descriptive of a person, action, or quality that is characteristic of a hero; showing great courage.  It implies a noble and admirable quality, often in the face of danger or adversity. |


例句：

His heroical defense of the city saved countless lives. = 他英勇地保卫城市，拯救了无数人的生命。

The heroical efforts of the firefighters prevented the fire from spreading. = 消防员英勇的努力阻止了火势蔓延。


近义词：valiant, courageous, brave, gallant, intrepid

反义词：cowardly, timid, pusillanimous


词源:

The word "heroical" comes from the Middle English word "heroik," which in turn originates from the Old French "heroïque" and ultimately from the Greek "hērōikos," meaning "of or belonging to a hero."  The Greek word "hērōs" means "hero."
