pseudonarcotic: /ˈsuːdoʊnɑːrˈkɒtɪk/ | n. | 伪麻醉药，类似麻醉药的药物 | A substance that mimics the effects of a narcotic but is not a true narcotic; often used to describe drugs with similar effects to opioids but with a different chemical structure.  | Example:  Some cough syrups contain pseudonarcotics to suppress coughs.  某些止咳糖浆含有伪麻醉药来抑制咳嗽。 |近义词：opioid agonist (部分情况下), analgesic (如果具有镇痛作用) |反义词：narcotic antagonist
