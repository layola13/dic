hydro:/ˈhaɪdroʊ/| /ˈhaɪdrəʊ/ |prefix|水，氢|Combining form meaning "water" or "hydrogen".  It is used as a prefix in many scientific terms.|例句：Hydroelectric power plants use the energy of moving water to generate electricity. 水力发电厂利用流水能量发电。例句：Hydrochloric acid is a strong acid used in many industrial processes. 盐酸是一种用于许多工业过程的强酸。|近义词：aqueous, watery|反义词：anhydrous
