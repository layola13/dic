co-owns:/koʊˈoʊnz/| v.|共有，共同拥有|To jointly own something.  This implies a shared ownership, often with equal rights and responsibilities.|例句：My brother and I co-own a small business. = 我哥哥和我共同拥有一家小企业。

例句：They co-own a beautiful vineyard in Tuscany. = 他们在托斯卡纳共同拥有一个美丽的葡萄园。


近义词：jointly owns, shares ownership of, possesses together|反义词：solely owns, individually owns|词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They co-own a house in the city. = 他们在城里共同拥有一栋房子。
We co-own a boat and share the costs. = 我们共同拥有一艘船并分担费用。

一般过去时:
They co-owned a restaurant for ten years. = 他们共同经营了一家餐厅十年。
We co-owned a car but sold it last year. = 我们以前共同拥有一辆车，但去年卖掉了。

现在完成时:
They have co-owned the company since 2000. = 自2000年以来，他们一直共同拥有这家公司。
We have co-owned this land for generations. = 我们世世代代共同拥有这块土地。

过去完成时:
They had co-owned the property before the divorce. = 离婚前他们共同拥有这处房产。
We had co-owned the business before my partner retired. = 我的合伙人退休前我们共同经营这家企业。

将来时:
They will co-own a new studio next year. = 他们明年将共同拥有一个新的工作室。
We will co-own a holiday home in the future. = 我们将来会共同拥有一处度假屋。

虚拟语气:
If they co-owned a farm, they would be self-sufficient. = 如果他们共同拥有一个农场，他们将自给自足。
I wish we co-owned a yacht, sailing the seven seas. = 我希望我们共同拥有一艘游艇，航行七大洋。

被动语态: (The verb "co-own" is rarely used passively.)  A passive construction would be awkward and unnatural.


疑问句:
Do they co-own the building? = 他们共同拥有那栋建筑物吗？
Will you co-own the project with them? = 你会和他们一起拥有这个项目吗？

否定句:
They do not co-own the business anymore. = 他们不再共同拥有这家企业了。
We did not co-own the apartment; he owned it solely. = 我们没有共同拥有那套公寓；他独自拥有。


PHRASE->
co-own a business = 共同经营企业
co-own property = 共同拥有房产
co-own a vehicle = 共同拥有车辆
co-own an asset = 共同拥有资产
co-own intellectual property = 共同拥有知识产权
