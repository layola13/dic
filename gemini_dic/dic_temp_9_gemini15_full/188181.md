rove:/roʊv/| v.|漫游，流浪；搜寻；调查|to travel around without any definite plan; to search or explore widely|例句：The nomads rove across the vast plains.  游牧民族在广阔的平原上游荡。

The detectives roved the city searching for clues.  侦探们在城市里四处搜寻线索。


近义词：wander, roam, ramble, explore|反义词：stay, remain, settle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roves the countryside every summer. 他每年夏天都在乡下漫游。
They rove the streets looking for trouble. 他们在街上游荡寻找麻烦。


一般过去时:
She roved through Europe for a year. 她游历欧洲一年。
We roved the forest, searching for mushrooms. 我们在森林里漫游，寻找蘑菇。


现在完成时:
He has roved across three continents. 他已经游历了三大洲。
They have roved the internet looking for information. 他们一直在网上搜索信息。


过去完成时:
She had roved extensively before settling down. 在定居之前，她已经广泛地旅行过。
They had roved the city for hours before finding the address.  他们在城里找了几个小时才找到地址。


将来时:
We will rove the coast next summer. 我们明年夏天将在沿海地区漫游。
He will rove the library searching for old books. 他将漫游图书馆寻找旧书。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would rove the world. 如果我有时间，我会周游世界。
I wish I could rove freely without a care. 我希望我能自由自在地漫游，不用操心。


被动语态:
The countryside is roved by many tourists every year.  每年都有许多游客在乡下漫游。

疑问句:

否定句:
He doesn't rove aimlessly; he always has a purpose. 他不是漫无目的地游荡；他总是有目的的。
They didn't rove far from home. 他们没有离家太远。
