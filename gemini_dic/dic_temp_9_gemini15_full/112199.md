loony:/ˈluːni/| adj., n.|疯子；古怪的；疯狂的 |A loony is a person who is considered crazy or eccentric.  The word can be used informally and sometimes carries a slightly humorous connotation.  However, it can also be considered offensive depending on the context.|例句：He's a bit of a loony, always talking about aliens. 他有点疯疯癫癫的，总是谈论外星人。

He acted like a complete loony. 他行为举止像个十足的疯子。|近义词：crazy, mad, insane, eccentric, wacky, daft|反义词：sane, rational, normal, sensible|词性:形容词，名词
