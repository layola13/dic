railing: /ˈreɪlɪŋ/ | n. |栏杆，围栏 | A railing is a barrier, typically made of wood, metal, or other material, that is used to prevent people or things from falling from a height or to mark a boundary.  It's often found along stairs, balconies, decks, and bridges. |例句：The railing around the balcony was made of wrought iron.  阳台上的栏杆是用锻铁制成的。

The children were warned to stay away from the railing. 孩子们被警告不要靠近栏杆。


近义词：banister, balustrade, guardrail |反义词：opening, gap
