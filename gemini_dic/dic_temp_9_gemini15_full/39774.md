consomme:/ˈkɒnsɒm/ /ˈkɑːnsɑːmeɪ/| n.|清汤，浓缩清汤|A consommé is a rich, clear broth made by clarifying a stock with egg whites. It's often served as a first course in formal meals.|The restaurant is famous for its rich consommé.|近义词：broth, bouillon|反义词：stew
