recon:/ˈrɛkən/ | v. |重新考虑，和解，调解 | To think over again; to consider carefully; to reconcile; to rebuild  |例句：I need to recon my plans for the weekend. 我需要重新考虑我周末的计划。|例句：The two warring factions finally agreed to recon.  交战的两派最终同意和解。|近义词：reconsider, reconcile, rebuild |反义词：disagree, estrange


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconsiders his decision every day. 他每天都重新考虑他的决定。
They reconcile their differences through dialogue.  他们通过对话化解了分歧。

一般过去时:
She reconsidered her options before making a choice.  她在做出选择之前重新考虑了她的选择。
The two countries finally reconciled after years of conflict.  这两个国家在多年的冲突后最终和解了。

现在完成时:
He has reconsidered his position on the matter. 他已经重新考虑了他在这个问题上的立场。
They have reconciled and are now friends again. 他们已经和解，现在又是朋友了。

过去完成时:
She had reconsidered her offer before accepting it.  她在接受之前已经重新考虑了她的提议。
The warring factions had reconciled before the peace treaty was signed.  交战各方在和平条约签署之前已经达成和解。

将来时:
I will reconsider my answer after I've had more time to think. 我会在有更多时间思考之后重新考虑我的答案。
They will reconcile their differences through mediation. 他们将通过调解来解决他们的分歧。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reconsider my actions. 如果我是你，我会重新考虑我的行为。
If they had reconciled earlier, things would have been better. 如果他们早点和解，情况就会好得多。

被动语态:
His proposal was reconsidered by the committee. 他的提案被委员会重新考虑了。
The differences between them were reconciled by a third party. 他们之间的分歧由第三方调解解决了。

疑问句:
Will you reconsider your decision? 你会重新考虑你的决定吗？
Have they reconciled yet? 他们已经和解了吗？

否定句:
He did not reconsider his decision, even after the arguments.  即使在争论之后，他也没有重新考虑他的决定。
They have not yet reconciled after the argument.  争吵之后他们还没有和解。
