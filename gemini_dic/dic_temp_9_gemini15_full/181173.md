reinherit:/ˌriːɪnˈhɛrɪt/| vt. |重新继承；再次继承|To inherit something again, especially after a previous inheritance has been lost or depleted.|The family reinherited the estate after a lengthy legal battle.  这个家族在经过漫长的法律诉讼后重新继承了这片地产。
He reinherited his grandfather's fortune after a court ruling. 他在法院判决后再次继承了他祖父的财产。|近义词：inherit (again), reacquire|反义词：disinherit, forfeit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinherit the property every year. = 他们每年都重新继承这处房产。
She reinherits her grandmother's jewelry. = 她继承了她祖母的珠宝。


一般过去时:
He reinherited the title after his cousin's death. = 他在堂兄死后重新继承了头衔。
The company reinherited the patent following a successful lawsuit. = 公司在赢得诉讼后重新获得了专利权。


现在完成时:

She has reinherited the throne after the previous ruler's abdication. = 在前任统治者退位后，她重新继承了王位。


过去完成时:
The heir had reinherited the estate before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，继承人已经重新继承了这片地产。
They had reinherited the land only to lose it again to taxes. = 他们重新继承了这片土地，却再次因为税收而失去它。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
