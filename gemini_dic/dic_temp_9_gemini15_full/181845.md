reloan:/ˌriːˈloʊn/ | v. | 再贷款，重新贷款 | To lend money again, especially to someone who has already borrowed money.  | Example: The bank decided to reloan the funds to a more reliable borrower.  银行决定将资金重新贷给一个更可靠的借款人。 | 近义词：refinance, relend | 反义词：repay, recall loan


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank reloans money to its existing clients regularly. = 银行定期向现有客户重新贷款。
They reloan funds to support small businesses. = 他们重新贷款以支持小型企业。


一般过去时:
The company reloaned the assets last quarter. = 公司上季度重新贷款了资产。
He reloaned the money at a higher interest rate. = 他以更高的利率重新贷款了这笔钱。


现在完成时:
The bank has reloaned millions of dollars this year. = 银行今年已重新贷款数百万美元。
We have reloaned the property to a new buyer. = 我们已将房产重新贷款给新的买家。


过去完成时:
They had reloaned the money before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经重新贷款了这笔钱。
She had reloaned her house several times. = 她已经几次重新抵押她的房子了。


将来时:
We will reloan the capital next month. = 我们下个月将重新贷款资本。
The government will reloan funds to the affected areas. = 政府将向受灾地区重新贷款资金。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funds, we would reloan them immediately. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们会立即重新贷款。
I wish I had reloaned the property sooner. = 我真希望我早点把房产重新贷款。


被动语态:
The money was reloaned to a different client. = 这笔钱被重新贷给了不同的客户。
The funds are being reloaned to support infrastructure projects. = 这些资金正在被重新贷款以支持基础设施项目。


疑问句:
Will the bank reloan the money? = 银行会重新贷款吗？
Can we reloan the property to a new owner? = 我们能把房产重新抵押给新的业主吗？


否定句:
The bank did not reloan the money due to credit risk. = 由于信用风险，银行没有重新贷款。
They won't reloan the funds without proper collateral. = 没有适当的抵押品，他们就不会重新贷款。
