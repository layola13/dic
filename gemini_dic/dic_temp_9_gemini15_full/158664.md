plodding:/ˈplɒdɪŋ/ | adj. | 沉闷的；单调乏味的；步履蹒跚的 |  characterized by slowness and lack of energy; laborious and monotonous.  |例句：The work was plodding and repetitive. 这项工作沉闷乏味且重复。

例句：He walked with a plodding gait. 他步履蹒跚地走着。


近义词：tedious, laborious, monotonous, wearisome, trudging

反义词：exciting, stimulating, invigorating, brisk, lively


词源：源自中古英语 *plodden，意为“缓慢而沉重地走”。  最终源自原始日耳曼语 *platjan，意思是“拍打”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The work is plodding and repetitive. = 这项工作沉闷乏味且重复。
He plods along the road every day. = 他每天沿着这条路缓慢地走着。


一般过去时:
The hiker plodded through the mud. = 徒步旅行者在泥泞中艰难跋涉。
She plodded through her homework. = 她艰难地完成了作业。


现在完成时:
They have plodded through many challenges. = 他们已经克服了许多挑战。
He has plodded along in his career for years. = 他在职业生涯中默默耕耘多年。


过去完成时:
They had plodded for hours before reaching the summit. = 他们在到达山顶之前已经跋涉了几个小时。
She had plodded through the entire book before bedtime. = 她在睡前已经读完了整本书。


将来时:
We will plod through the snow tomorrow. = 我们明天将在雪地里艰难跋涉。
He will plod on regardless of the obstacles. = 不管遇到什么障碍，他都会继续前进。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be so plodding, I wouldn't have started. = 如果我知道会这么乏味，我就不会开始了。
I wish the work wasn't so plodding. = 我希望这项工作不要这么乏味。


被动语态:
The journey was plodded through by the determined traveler. = 这位意志坚定的旅行者艰难地完成了旅程。
The task was plodded through slowly but surely. = 这项任务虽然缓慢，但最终还是完成了。


疑问句:
Is the pace of this project plodding? = 这个项目的进度缓慢吗？
Does he always plod along like that? = 他总是这样缓慢地走吗？


否定句:
The work wasn't plodding at all; it was quite exciting. = 这项工作一点也不乏味；它相当令人兴奋。
She didn't plod through her tasks; she completed them efficiently. = 她并没有艰难地完成任务；她高效地完成了它们。
