prognoses:/ˈprɒɡnoʊsiːz/| n. |预后，预测 |The plural form of "prognosis," referring to multiple predictions or forecasts, especially of the likely course of a disease or situation.  It often involves considering various factors and potential outcomes.|例句：The doctors offered differing prognoses for the patient's recovery.  医生们对病人的康复给出了不同的预后。|例句：The economic prognoses for the next year are uncertain.  明年经济的预测是不确定的。|近义词：forecasts, predictions, projections |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "certainties" or "guarantees" if the prognoses are uncertain. |名词
