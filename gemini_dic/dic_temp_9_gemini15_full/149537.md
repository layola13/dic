page-turner:/ˈpeɪdʒˌtɜːrnər/ | n. |引人入胜的书；让人爱不释手的书 |A page-turner is a book that is so exciting and interesting that you want to keep reading it.  It's captivating and hard to put down. |例句：That new thriller is a real page-turner; I couldn't put it down until I finished it. 那本新的惊悚小说真是引人入胜，我一口气读完了。  The novel was such a page-turner that I finished it in just two days. 这部小说非常引人入胜，我两天就看完了。|近义词：gripping read, captivating book, compelling story |反义词：dull book, boring read | n.
