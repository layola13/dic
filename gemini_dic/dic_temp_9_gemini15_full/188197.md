rowboat:/ˈroʊboʊt/| n. |划艇，小划子| A small boat propelled by oars.  |例句：We rented a rowboat and spent the afternoon on the lake. 我们租了一艘划艇，在湖上度过了下午。|近义词：dinghy, skiff|反义词：motorboat, sailboat|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rows the rowboat across the lake. = 他划着小船穿过湖。
They row their rowboat every Sunday. = 他们每周日都划他们的划艇。


一般过去时:
She rowed the rowboat to the shore. = 她划着小船到岸边。
We rowed the rowboat for hours. = 我们划了几个小时的船。


现在完成时:
I have rowed this rowboat many times. = 我已经划过这条船很多次了。
They have rowed to the island. = 他们已经划船到岛上了。


过去完成时:
He had rowed the rowboat before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经划过小船了。
We had rowed for miles before we saw land. = 我们划了好几英里才看到陆地。


将来时:
We will row the rowboat tomorrow. = 我们明天将划船。
She will row the rowboat to the other side. = 她会划船到对面去。


虚拟语气:
If I had a rowboat, I would go fishing. = 如果我有一艘划艇，我会去钓鱼。
I wish I had a rowboat to explore the lake. = 我希望我有一艘划艇去探索湖泊。


被动语态:
The rowboat was rented by tourists. = 这艘划艇被游客租走了。
The rowboat is being repaired. = 这艘划艇正在修理中。


疑问句:
Is that a rowboat? = 那是划艇吗？
Whose rowboat is this? = 这是谁的划艇？


否定句:

They didn't use the rowboat today. = 他们今天没有用划艇。
