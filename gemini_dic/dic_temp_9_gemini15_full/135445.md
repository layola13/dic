nonpersuasively:/ˌnɒnpəˈsweɪsɪvli/ | adv. | 不具说服力地；无力地 | In a way that fails to persuade or convince.  It describes an action or communication that lacks the power to influence or sway someone's opinion or behavior. | 例句：He argued nonpersuasively, losing the debate. 他论证无力，输掉了辩论。

He presented his case nonpersuasively, and the judge was unconvinced. 他不具说服力地陈述了他的案情，法官没有被说服。


近义词：unconvincingly, ineffectively, weakly, feebly

反义词：persuasively, convincingly, powerfully
