equable:/ˈɛkwəbəl/| adj.|公平的；平静的；稳定的；温和的|Characterized by evenness of temper; calm; unvarying.|The climate here is remarkably equable, with mild temperatures year-round.|近义词：calm, steady, stable, even-tempered|反义词：volatile, erratic, uneven, tempestuous


例句：

He possessed an equable temperament, rarely losing his composure. 他拥有平静的性情，很少失去镇定。

The company enjoyed an equable growth rate for several years. 这家公司多年来保持着稳定的增长速度。


词源:

From Latin *aequabilis, from aequus ("equal") + -abilis ("-able").  The word essentially means "capable of being equal" or "even".


用法说明:


equable 主要用作形容词，描述人的性格或事物状态。


描述人的性格时，指性格平和、不易动怒，情绪稳定。


描述事物状态时，指状态稳定、均匀，没有大的波动。


例如：


an equable climate 温和的气候


an equable distribution of resources 资源的均衡分配


an equable temper 平静的脾气


an equable personality 平静的个性


The teacher maintained an equable demeanor throughout the chaos.  老师在混乱中保持着平静的举止。


The company's profits showed an equable increase over the past decade. 公司的利润在过去十年里稳步增长。
