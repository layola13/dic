oberon:/ˈoʊbərɒn/| n. |奥伯龙（妖精王）|Oberon is the king of the fairies in Shakespeare's A Midsummer Night's Dream and other works of fiction.  He is often depicted as a powerful and sometimes mischievous figure.  The name is also used for various things, including a programming language and a moon of Uranus.|Example:  Oberon, the fairy king, orchestrated the chaotic events of the play. 奥伯龙，妖精之王，策划了剧中混乱的事件。|近义词:fairy king, sprite king|反义词:None|名词
