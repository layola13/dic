jump-on-board:/ˌdʒʌmp ɒn ˈbɔːrd/| phrasal verb|加入，参与，欣然接受|To enthusiastically join a project, activity, or group.|例句：I'm excited to jump on board this new project. 我很高兴加入这个新项目。

例句：Many investors jumped on board after hearing about the company's success. 许多投资者在听说该公司成功后纷纷加入。


近义词：join, participate, engage, embrace, sign up


反义词：withdraw, opt out, decline, refuse


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They jump on board new initiatives readily. = 他们欣然加入新的倡议。
We jump on board every opportunity to improve. = 我们抓住每一个改进的机会。


一般过去时:
She jumped on board the marketing campaign immediately. = 她立即加入了营销活动。
He jumped on board the new project last week. = 他上周加入了新的项目。


现在完成时:
They have jumped on board the sustainability program. = 他们已经加入了可持续发展计划。
We have jumped on board this exciting venture. = 我们已经加入了这个激动人心的项目。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had jumped on board the fundraising effort. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经加入了筹款工作。
By the time the meeting ended, everyone had jumped on board. = 到会议结束时，每个人都加入了。


将来时:
I will jump on board the research team next month. = 我下个月将加入研究团队。
They will jump on board the new initiative as soon as possible. = 他们将尽快加入新的倡议。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would jump on board immediately. = 如果我有时间，我会立即加入。
I wish I could jump on board, but I'm too busy. = 我真希望我能加入，但我太忙了。


被动语态:
The new recruits were quickly jumped on board. = 新招募的人员很快就被吸收了。
The new strategy was jumped on board by the team. = 团队欣然接受了新的战略。


疑问句:
Will you jump on board our cause? = 你会加入我们的事业吗？
Are they going to jump on board the project? = 他们会加入这个项目吗？


否定句:
He didn't jump on board the project because he was busy. = 他因为太忙而没有加入该项目。
She chose not to jump on board the initiative. = 她选择不加入这项倡议。
