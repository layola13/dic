prediscountable:/ˌpriːdɪˈskaʊntəbl/| adj.| 可预先计算的，可预先折扣的 |  Able to be discounted or calculated in advance.  This word is a blend of "pre" (before), "dis" (reverse/removal), and "countable" (able to be counted). It implies that a value or amount can be determined and potentially reduced before a final calculation or transaction.  | The investment's prediscountable value made it attractive to investors.  投资的可预先计算价值使其对投资者具有吸引力。 |  precalculable, estimable |  unpredictable, incalculable |  形容词
