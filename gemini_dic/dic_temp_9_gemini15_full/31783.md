checkered:/ˈtʃɛkərd/| adj.|方格图案的；多变的；变幻莫测的；有污点的|Having a pattern of squares of different colors, typically red and black or black and white; marked by periods of varied fortune; marked by both good and bad experiences; stained or spotted.|例句：He wore a checkered shirt. 他穿着一件格子衬衫。
She had a checkered past. 她有过一段多事之秋的过去。


近义词：varied, variegated, mottled, spotted, diverse, complicated


反义词：plain, uniform, consistent, uneventful


词源：源自古法语“echequier”，意为“棋盘”，最终源自古日耳曼语词根。  The word ultimately derives from Old French *echequier*, meaning "chessboard," which in turn comes from Germanic roots relating to chess.
