examined:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnd/ | vt. | 检查；仔细查看；审问 | To inspect or investigate something carefully; to question someone formally. |例句：The doctor examined the patient carefully. 医生仔细检查了病人。|例句：The police examined the suspect for hours. 警察审问了嫌疑犯数小时。|近义词：inspected, investigated, scrutinized |反义词：ignored, overlooked

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist examines the data every day. = 科学家每天检查数据。
They examine the evidence carefully before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前仔细检查证据。

一般过去时:
The detective examined the crime scene. = 侦探检查了犯罪现场。
She examined the painting with a magnifying glass. = 她用放大镜仔细检查了这幅画。

现在完成时:
The committee has examined all the proposals. = 委员会已经审查了所有的提案。
Have you examined the contract thoroughly? = 你仔细检查过合同了吗？

过去完成时:
The jury had examined all the evidence before reaching a verdict. = 陪审团在做出判决之前已经审查了所有证据。
They had examined the documents several times before submitting their report. = 他们在提交报告之前已经检查了几次文件。

将来时:
The professor will examine the students on the material next week. = 教授下周将考查学生们掌握的材料。
We will examine the results of the experiment carefully. = 我们将仔细检查实验结果。


被动语态:
The patient was examined by the doctor. = 病人被医生检查了。
The suspect was examined by the police. = 嫌疑人被警方审问了。

疑问句:

Will the judge examine the witness? = 法官会审查证人吗？

否定句:
The police didn't examine the scene properly. = 警方没有正确检查现场。
He hasn't examined the evidence carefully enough. = 他没有足够仔细地检查证据。
