lick:/lɪk/| vt. n.|舔，轻扫；少量；尝试；| to pass the tongue over the surface of (something)|例句：The cat licked its paw. 猫舔着它的爪子。|例句：He gave the cake a quick lick. 他快速舔了一口蛋糕。|近义词：lap, taste, swipe|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He licks the ice cream. = 他舔着冰淇淋。
The dog licks its bowl clean. = 狗舔干净了它的碗。

一般过去时:
She licked the stamp. = 她舔了邮票。
The child licked the lollipop. = 孩子舔着棒棒糖。

现在完成时:
The dog has licked all the ice cream. = 狗已经把所有的冰淇淋都舔光了。
I have licked my wounds after the defeat. = 我在失败后舔舐着伤口。

过去完成时:
He had licked the envelope before he mailed it. = 他在寄信之前已经舔过信封了。
She had licked her fingers before she started eating. = 她开始吃饭之前已经舔过手指了。

将来时:
I will lick the plate clean. = 我会把盘子舔干净。
The cat will lick its fur. = 猫会舔它的毛。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would lick my wounds and try again. = 如果我是你，我会舔舐伤口，然后重试。
I wish I could lick my troubles away. = 我希望我能把我的烦恼舔掉。

被动语态:
The stamp was licked before it was stuck on the envelope. = 邮票在贴到信封上之前被舔过了。
The ice cream was quickly licked clean. = 冰淇淋很快就被舔干净了。

疑问句:
Did he lick the spoon? = 他舔了勺子吗？
Will she lick the plate? = 她会舔盘子吗？

否定句:
He didn't lick the ice cream. = 他没有舔冰淇淋。
She didn't lick the envelope. = 她没有舔信封。
