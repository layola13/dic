regimen:/ˈredʒɪmən/| n.|制度；养生之道；疗养法；管理方法|A regulated system or plan, especially a systematic plan or course of treatment.  It often refers to a prescribed course of medical treatment, diet, or exercise. |例句：She followed a strict regimen of exercise and diet. 她遵循着严格的运动和饮食制度。|例句：The doctor prescribed a new regimen of medication. 医生开了一种新的药物疗法。|近义词：system, plan, program, course|反义词：chaos, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor outlines a new regimen for the patient. = 医生为病人制定了一个新的治疗方案。
He follows a strict fitness regimen. = 他遵循严格的健身制度。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

She has maintained a consistent exercise regimen throughout the year. = 她全年都保持着规律的锻炼计划。

过去完成时:

She had followed a healthy eating regimen for years before she fell ill. = 在生病之前，她多年来一直坚持健康的饮食习惯。

将来时:
