paracone:/ˈpærəˌkoʊn/| n. |锥状体，副锥体|A paracone is a cusp (pointed projection) on the upper molars of some mammals, specifically located on the buccal (cheek-side) surface.  It's one of several cusps that contribute to the complex morphology of mammalian teeth.  Paracones are important in mammalian paleontology and evolutionary studies as their presence, shape, and position can help determine phylogenetic relationships between different species.|The paracone is a prominent feature on the upper molars of many carnivores.|近义词：cusp, cone (in a broader sense)|反义词：无
