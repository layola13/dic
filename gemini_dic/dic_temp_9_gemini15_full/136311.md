nonrepudiable: /ˌnɒnˌrɛpjʊˈdiːəbl/| adj. |不可否认的，不可抵赖的 | incapable of being denied or rejected as genuine.  It refers to something that cannot be denied or disavowed by the party that is responsible for it. This is often used in the context of digital signatures and authentication, ensuring that the sender cannot later deny having sent a message or made a transaction. |  The digital signature on this document is nonrepudiable, ensuring its authenticity.  这个文件的数字签名是不可否认的，确保其真实性。  The contract includes a nonrepudiable clause, preventing either party from denying their agreement. 合同包含一个不可否认的条款，防止任何一方否认他们的协议。|  undeniable, irrefutable, incontestable | repudiable, deniable |

词源:
The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not"), the verb "repudiate" (meaning "to reject, disown, or deny"), and the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of").  Therefore, "nonrepudiable" means "not capable of being repudiated" or "not capable of being denied."
