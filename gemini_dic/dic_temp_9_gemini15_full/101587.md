irradiate:/ɪˈreɪdieɪt/| vt.|照射；辐射；使充满活力|To expose to radiation; to emit rays of light or other radiation; to spread or fill with something (e.g., light, energy, or influence).|例句：The sun irradiates the earth with light and heat. 太阳以光和热照射地球。

The artist's work irradiates joy and optimism.  这位艺术家的作品充满快乐和乐观。|近义词：beam, radiate, illuminate|反义词：obscure, darken
