raves:/reɪvz/| v. n.| 狂喜；热烈赞扬；胡言乱语 | To talk in a confused or excited way, often because of drugs or mental illness; to praise enthusiastically.  |例句：He was raving about the concert. 他对这场音乐会赞不绝口。The patient was raving deliriously. 病人神志不清地胡言乱语。|近义词：rant, rave about, praise, extol|反义词：criticize, condemn, pan


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He raves about his new car. = 他对他的新车赞不绝口。
They rave about the food at that restaurant. = 他们对那家餐馆的食物赞不绝口。


一般过去时:
She raved about the movie all night. = 她整晚都在兴奋地谈论那部电影。
He raved and shouted until he collapsed. = 他又喊又叫直到倒下。


现在完成时:
The critics have raved about her latest novel. = 批评家们对她的最新小说赞不绝口。
He has raved about the benefits of this new technology. = 他一直对这项新技术的益处赞不绝口。


过去完成时:
They had raved about the band's performance before the show even started. = 演出甚至还没开始，他们就已经对乐队的表演赞不绝口了。
She had raved for hours before finally falling asleep. = 她胡言乱语了几个小时才最终睡着。


将来时:
The audience will rave about the singer's performance. = 观众将会对歌手的表演赞不绝口。
He will probably rave if he doesn't get his way. = 如果他不遂心愿，他可能会胡言乱语。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would rave about this scenery. = 如果他在这里，他会对这风景赞不绝口。
I wish I hadn't raved so much yesterday. = 我真希望我昨天不要说得那么激动。


被动语态:
Her performance was raved about by critics. = 她的表演受到了评论家的盛赞。
His ideas were raved about by his colleagues. = 他的想法受到了同事们的热烈赞扬。


疑问句:
Does he often rave about his work? = 他经常对他的工作赞不绝口吗？
Was she raving when she said that? = 她当时说这话的时候是不是在胡言乱语？


否定句:
He didn't rave about the food, he actually disliked it. = 他并没有对食物赞不绝口，事实上他不喜欢它。
She didn't rave, she simply expressed her opinion calmly. = 她没有胡言乱语，她只是平静地表达了她的意见。
