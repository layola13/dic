procrastinated:/prəˈkræstɪneɪtɪd/| v.|拖延，延误|To delay or postpone something.|He procrastinated on his assignment until the last minute. 他拖延作业到最后一分钟。|近义词：delay, postpone, defer|反义词：expedite, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I procrastinate when I feel overwhelmed. = 当我感到不知所措时，我会拖延。
He procrastinates on his studies regularly. = 他经常拖延学业。

一般过去时:
I procrastinated on that project for weeks. = 我拖延那个项目好几个星期了。
She procrastinated and missed the deadline. = 她拖延了，错过了截止日期。

现在完成时:
I have procrastinated on this task all day. = 我整天都在拖延这项任务。
They have procrastinated so much that the project is behind schedule. = 他们拖延得太多，以至于项目进度落后了。

过去完成时:
I had procrastinated so much that I had to work all night to finish. = 我拖延了太多，不得不通宵工作才能完成。
She had procrastinated on her studies, resulting in a poor grade. = 她拖延了学业，导致成绩很差。

将来时:
I will procrastinate no more! = 我不会再拖延了！
He knows he will procrastinate again if he doesn't start now. = 他知道如果现在不开始，他会再次拖延。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't procrastinated, I would have finished on time. = 如果我没有拖延，我就按时完成了。
I wish I hadn't procrastinated on that important task. = 我真希望我没有拖延那项重要的任务。

被动语态:
The project was procrastinated on until it was too late. = 该项目被拖延到太迟了。
The decision was procrastinated, leading to further complications. = 决定被拖延，导致进一步的复杂化。

疑问句:
Did you procrastinate on your homework? = 你拖延作业了吗？
Why did he procrastinate on such an important project? = 他为什么拖延如此重要的项目？

否定句:
I did not procrastinate; I completed the task on time. = 我没有拖延；我按时完成了任务。
She did not procrastinate and finished her work ahead of schedule. = 她没有拖延，提前完成了工作。


PHRASE->
procrastinate on = 拖延…
procrastinate about = 犹豫是否…
procrastinate with = 用…来拖延时间
avoid procrastinating = 避免拖延
stop procrastinating = 停止拖延
