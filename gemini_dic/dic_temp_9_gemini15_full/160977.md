postages:/ˈpoʊstɪdʒɪz/| n.|邮资；邮费|The cost of sending something by post.  It includes the price of stamps or other postage methods.|例句：The postage for this package is quite high. 这个包裹的邮费相当高。

例句：We need to calculate the total postage before shipping the goods. 我们需要在发货前计算总邮费。


近义词：postage fees, mailing costs, postal charges|反义词：无


词性：名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The postage costs ten dollars. = 邮费是十美元。
Postage varies depending on the weight and destination. = 邮费取决于重量和目的地。


一般过去时:
The postage cost five dollars last year. = 去年邮费是五美元。
The postage was included in the price. = 邮费包含在价格里。


现在完成时:
The company has already paid the postage. = 公司已经支付了邮费。
Have you calculated the postage yet? = 你算过邮费了吗？


过去完成时:
They had already affixed the postage stamps before they mailed the letter. = 他们寄信之前已经贴好了邮票。
I had underestimated the postage cost. = 我低估了邮费。


将来时:
The postage will be higher next year. = 明年邮费将会更高。
I will pay the postage when I receive the package. = 我收到包裹时会支付邮费。


虚拟语气:
If the postage were cheaper, I would send more packages. = 如果邮费更便宜，我会寄更多包裹。
I wish the postage wasn't so expensive. = 我希望邮费不要这么贵。


被动语态:
The postage is usually paid by the sender. = 邮费通常由发件人支付。
The postage was incorrectly calculated. = 邮费计算错误。


疑问句:
How much is the postage? = 邮费是多少？
Does the price include postage? = 价格包含邮费吗？


否定句:
The package didn't include postage. = 包裹不包含邮费。
I haven't paid the postage yet. = 我还没有支付邮费。
