nexuses:/ˈnɛksəsɪz/| n. |连接点；中心；关系网 | A nexus is a connection or series of connections linking two or more things.  It often refers to a central or crucial point of connection.  The plural form, "nexuses," indicates multiple such connections or points. | Example: The city served as a nexus of trade and culture.  这个城市是贸易和文化的中心。  The various incidents formed a nexus of suspicion.  各种事件形成了一个疑点网络。|近义词：connection, link, hub, center, core |反义词：separation, isolation, disconnect |名词
