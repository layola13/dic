moses: /ˈmoʊzɪz/ | n. | 摩西 | The biblical prophet who led the Israelites out of Egypt.  Moses is a significant figure in Judaism, Christianity, and Islam, known for receiving the Ten Commandments from God on Mount Sinai. He is revered as a lawgiver, prophet, and leader. | Example: Moses parted the Red Sea. 摩西分开红海。 |近义词: leader, prophet | 反义词:  N/A
