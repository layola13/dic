herbivore:/ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːr/| n. |食草动物|An animal that feeds on plants.|例句：Cows are herbivores.  奶牛是食草动物。|例句：Rabbits are a type of herbivore. 兔子是一种食草动物。|近义词：plant-eater, grazer|反义词：carnivore, omnivore|名词
