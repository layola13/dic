repacker:/ˌriːˈpækər/| n. |重新包装者，重新包装机|A person or machine that repacks something.  Someone whose job is to repack goods, often for resale or shipment.  A machine used for repackaging items. |例句：The repacker carefully sorted the items before repackaging them into smaller boxes.  重新包装者在将物品重新包装到较小的箱子之前，仔细地对物品进行了分类。 The factory uses a high-speed repacker to process thousands of items daily.  这家工厂使用高速重新包装机每天处理数千件物品。|近义词：re-packager, repacking machine|反义词：N/A
