hybridization:/ˌhaɪbrɪdəˈzeɪʃən/| n. |杂交；混合；杂种|The process of combining different varieties or species of organisms to create a hybrid.  This can refer to plants, animals, or even the mixing of different types of DNA.  It often involves combining desirable traits from different parents. |Example:  The hybridization of wheat varieties led to increased crop yields.  杂交小麦品种导致了作物产量的增加。


The hybridization of two distinct musical styles created a unique sound.  两种截然不同的音乐风格的混合创造了一种独特的音效。|近义词：crossbreeding, interbreeding, mixing|反义词：purebreeding, inbreeding
