pierces:/pɪərsɪz/| vt.|刺穿；穿透；渗透；触动；理解|To make a hole through something with a pointed object.  It can also refer to a sharp sensation or a sudden, strong feeling.|例句：The needle pierced her skin.  针刺破了她的皮肤。

例句：His words pierced her heart.  他的话深深刺痛了她的心。

近义词：penetrate, puncture, perforate |反义词：seal, close, mend
