scalped:/skælp/ | vt. n. |剥头皮；诈取；敲竹杠 |To remove the scalp from (a person), typically as a trophy or punishment.  Also, to cheat or swindle someone out of their money. |例句：The settlers scalped their enemies in battle.  殖民者在战斗中给他们的敌人剥头皮。 He felt scalped by the high price of the car. 他觉得这辆车的价格太高，被敲竹杠了。|近义词：flay, fleece, swindle, cheat |反义词：protect, benefit


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They scalp tickets outside the stadium. 他们在体育场外倒卖门票。
He scalps customers regularly. 他经常敲诈顾客。


一般过去时:
The warriors scalped their victims.  勇士们给他们的受害者剥了头皮。
The salesman scalped me on the deal.  那个销售员在交易中敲诈了我。

He has scalped hundreds of people. 他已经敲诈了数百人。
