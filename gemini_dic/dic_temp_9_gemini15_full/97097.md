infester:/ɪnˈfɛstər/| n.  |侵扰者；感染者|A person or thing that infests a place or thing.  Someone or something that spreads widely and causes damage or harm.| The house was infested with rats. 这房子里老鼠成灾了。The area is infested with mosquitoes. 这个地区蚊子很多。|近义词：pest, plague, parasite|反义词：benefactor, cleanser


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The insects infest the garden. = 昆虫侵扰着花园。
The rats infest the old building. = 老鼠侵扰着那座旧建筑。


一般过去时:
The termites infested the wooden beams. = 白蚁侵蚀了木梁。
Locusts infested the fields last year. = 去年蝗虫侵扰了田地。


现在完成时:
The garden has been infested with aphids all summer. = 整个夏天蚜虫都侵扰着花园。
The ship has been infested by rats during the voyage. = 船在航行期间被老鼠侵扰了。


过去完成时:
The farmer realized that his crops had been infested by a fungus before he harvested them. = 农民在收割之前才意识到他的庄稼已经被真菌侵害了。
The city had been infested with crime for years before the new police chief arrived. = 在新警察局长到来之前，这座城市多年来一直被犯罪困扰。


将来时:
Unless we take action, the pests will infest our crops next season. = 除非我们采取行动，否则害虫将在下个季节侵害我们的庄稼。
If we don't clean the house, insects will infest it soon. = 如果我们不打扫房子，昆虫很快就会侵扰它。


虚拟语气:
If the area were not infested with mosquitoes, we would spend more time outdoors. = 如果这个地区没有蚊子侵扰，我们会花更多时间在户外。
If the house had not been infested with termites, it would still be standing. = 如果房子没有被白蚁侵蚀，它现在可能还在。


被动语态:
The house was infested by rodents. = 这房子被啮齿动物侵扰了。
The forest was infested with disease. = 森林被疾病侵害了。


疑问句:
Is the area infested with snakes? = 这个地区有蛇侵扰吗？
Is the house infested with cockroaches? = 这房子里蟑螂成灾了吗？


否定句:
The area is not infested with poisonous spiders. = 这个地区没有毒蜘蛛侵扰。
The building is not infested with insects. = 这栋楼没有昆虫侵扰。


PHRASE->

infested with:  充满……的；为……所侵扰的
heavily infested:  严重受……侵扰的
become infested: 开始受……侵扰
quickly infested:  迅速受……侵扰
infested area:  受……侵扰的地区
