laminitis:/ˌlæmɪˈnaɪtɪs/| n. |蹄叶炎|Inflammation of the laminae of the hoof in horses and other ungulates, causing severe pain and lameness.  It is characterized by inflammation of the sensitive laminae of the hoof, which attach the hoof wall to the underlying bone.  This inflammation causes severe pain and lameness. The condition can be caused by various factors, including overfeeding, concussion, and bacterial infections.  Without prompt treatment, laminitis can lead to serious complications, including rotation of the coffin bone and even euthanasia.|The horse was diagnosed with laminitis and required immediate veterinary care.  这匹马被诊断出患有蹄叶炎，需要立即进行兽医治疗。|founder,  |healthy hooves|名词
