词源:
The word "nonutilitarian" is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "utilitarian," which derives from the noun "utility" (usefulness or practical value) and ultimately from the Latin word "utilis" (useful).  Therefore, "nonutilitarian" literally means "not useful."
