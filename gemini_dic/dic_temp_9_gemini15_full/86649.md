hellebore: /ˈhɛləbɔːr/ | n. |嚏根草，圣诞玫瑰 | A genus of poisonous flowering plants in the buttercup family, known for their attractive but toxic flowers.  Hellebores are often cultivated as ornamentals, especially during the winter months.  Some species are used medicinally, but caution is advised due to their toxicity. | Example: The gardener carefully handled the hellebores, aware of their poisonous nature. 园丁小心地处理嚏根草，知道它们的毒性。  The Christmas rose is a type of hellebore. 圣诞玫瑰是一种嚏根草。 | 近义词：winter rose (for some species) | 反义词： (no direct antonym, but plants with non-toxic properties could be considered contrasting) | 名词
