lobotomizes:/ləˈbɒtəmaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 脑叶切除术 | To perform a lobotomy on (someone).  This surgical procedure involves severing connections in the brain's prefrontal cortex, and was once used to treat mental illness, though it is now largely considered unethical and obsolete due to its severe side effects. | Example:  The doctors lobotomized the patient in an attempt to alleviate his severe depression. = 医生对病人进行了脑叶切除手术，试图减轻他的严重抑郁症。  The controversial procedure lobotomized thousands before its dangers became widely known. = 在其危险性被广泛认知之前，这种有争议的程序对数千人进行了脑叶切除手术。| 近义词：incise (the brain), sever (connections in the brain) | 反义词： (no direct antonym, as it's a specific surgical procedure) | 动词
