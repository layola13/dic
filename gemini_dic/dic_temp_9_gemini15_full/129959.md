non-member:/ˈnɑːnˌmɛmbər/| adj. n. |非会员，非成员|  A person or entity that does not belong to a particular group, organization, or club.  It describes someone who is not a member of a specific organization or group. |例句：He is a non-member of the golf club, so he has to pay a higher fee to play. 他不是高尔夫俱乐部的会员，所以他必须支付更高的费用才能打球。

He attended the meeting as a non-member observer. 他以非会员观察员的身份参加了会议。|近义词：outsider, non-affiliate |反义词：member
