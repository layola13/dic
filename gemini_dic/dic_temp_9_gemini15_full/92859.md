id'ed:/aɪd/| v. |识别，鉴定|Past participle of "identify".  It means to have identified something or someone.  |例句：The suspect was quickly id'ed by the witness. 嫌疑人很快就被目击者认出来了。|例句：The police id'ed the stolen car. 警方查明了被盗车辆。|近义词：identified, recognized, determined|反义词：misidentified, unrecognized|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They identify the problem quickly. = 他们很快地识别出了问题。
We identify the suspects through the security footage. = 我们通过安全录像认出了嫌疑人。

一般过去时:
The police identified the thief. = 警察认出了小偷。
She identified her attacker in a police lineup. = 她在警方的识别阵容中认出了袭击者。

现在完成时:
They have identified the cause of the accident. = 他们已经查明了事故的原因。
Scientists have identified a new species of bird. = 科学家们已经发现了一种新的鸟类。

过去完成时:
The detective had identified the killer before the arrest. = 侦探在逮捕之前已经认出了凶手。
She had identified the painting as a forgery. = 她已经认定这幅画是赝品。

将来时:
They will identify the winner tomorrow. = 他们明天将确定获胜者。
We will identify the problem and provide a solution. = 我们将找出问题并提供解决方案。

虚拟语气:
If they identified the problem earlier, they could have avoided the crisis. = 如果他们早点发现问题，就能避免这场危机。
I wish I had identified the error sooner. = 我希望我早点发现错误。

被动语态:
The problem was quickly identified by the team. = 问题很快就被团队发现了。
The suspect was identified by a fingerprint match. = 嫌疑人通过指纹比对被确认身份。

疑问句:
Have they identified the missing child? = 他们找到失踪的孩子了吗？
Can you identify the man in the photograph? = 你能认出照片里的那个人吗？

否定句:
They didn't identify the correct suspect. = 他们没有认出正确的嫌疑人。
I couldn't identify the voice on the phone. = 我无法辨认电话里的声音。
