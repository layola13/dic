/ɡɔːrs/ | n. |峡谷，山沟 | A narrow, steep-sided valley, typically one with a stream running through it. Gorges are often formed by erosion.  |例句：The hikers traversed a deep gorge.  徒步旅行者穿越了一个深深的峡谷。
|例句：The river carved a magnificent gorge through the mountains. 这条河在山脉中雕刻出一个壮丽的峡谷。|近义词：ravine, canyon, chasm |反义词：plain, plateau
