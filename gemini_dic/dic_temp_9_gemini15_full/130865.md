nonarchitects:/ˌnɑːnˈɑːrkɪtɛkts/ | n. |非建筑师 |People who are not architects.  Those lacking professional architectural training or licensure. |Example:  The nonarchitects on the committee offered valuable insights from a user's perspective.  委员会中的非建筑师们从用户的角度提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词:laypeople, nonprofessionals, amateurs|反义词:architects
