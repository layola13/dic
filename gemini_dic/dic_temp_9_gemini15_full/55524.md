distributable:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 可分配的，可发布的 | Capable of being distributed; able to be shared or given out.  | Example: This software is distributable under the GNU license.  这个软件可以在GNU许可证下分发。  The company has a large amount of distributable profit this year.  公司今年有大量的可分配利润。 |近义词：shareable, disseminable | 反义词：unavailable, unshareable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software is distributable freely. = 这个软件可以免费分发。
This profit is distributable to shareholders. = 这笔利润可以分配给股东。


一般过去时:
The document was distributable only to authorized personnel. = 该文件只能分发给授权人员。
The goods were distributable across the country. = 货物可在全国范围内分发。


现在完成时:
The updates have been distributable since yesterday. = 更新自昨天起即可分发。
The profits have been distributable to employees for the last five years. = 过去五年来，利润一直可以分发给员工。


过去完成时:
The information had been distributable before the meeting. = 会议之前信息就已经可以分发了。
The resources had been distributable to all teams by the end of the project. = 项目结束时，资源已经可以分配给所有团队了。


将来时:
The new version will be distributable next month. = 新版本将于下个月分发。
The bonuses will be distributable after the year-end review. = 年终考核后将发放奖金。


虚拟语气:
If the software were distributable, it would be widely adopted. = 如果软件可分发，它将会被广泛采用。
I wish the report were distributable to everyone. = 我希望报告可以分发给所有人。


被动语态:
The files are being distributable to all users. = 文件正在分发给所有用户。
The funds are being distributable among the departments. = 资金正在各部门之间分配。


疑问句:
Is this software distributable to the public? = 这个软件可以公开分发吗？
Are the resources distributable among all projects? = 资源可以分配给所有项目吗？


否定句:
The data is not distributable without authorization. = 未经授权不得分发数据。
This information was not distributable to the press. = 此信息不得分发给媒体。
