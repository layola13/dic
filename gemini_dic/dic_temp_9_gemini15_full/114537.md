malapert:/ˈmæləpɜːrt/| adj.|放肆的；无礼的；冒失的；轻率的|Impudent or impertinent; cheeky.  Characterized by a bold or impudent disregard of others; saucy.|例句：He was a malapert young man who often contradicted his elders. 他是个放肆的年轻人，经常顶撞长辈。


例句：The malapert child refused to apologize for his rude behavior. 这个放肆的孩子拒绝为他粗鲁的行为道歉。


近义词：impertinent, impudent, cheeky, saucy, forward


反义词：respectful, polite, courteous, deferential


词性：形容词
