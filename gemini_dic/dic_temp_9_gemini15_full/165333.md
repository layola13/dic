preinvitations:/ˌpriːˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |预先邀请；发出邀请的准备工作 |Preliminary invitations sent before formal invitations are issued.  These might be informal inquiries or expressions of interest to gauge attendance before a formal invitation is sent.  |Example:  The preinvitations for the gala were sent out six weeks in advance to gauge interest.|近义词：advance notice, preliminary inquiries|反义词：formal invitations


USAGE->

Preinvitations are sent out to gauge interest. = 预先邀请被发出以评估兴趣。
They send preinvitations before the official invitations. = 他们在正式邀请函之前发出预先邀请函。
