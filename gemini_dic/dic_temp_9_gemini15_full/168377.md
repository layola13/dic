proacademic: /ˌproʊˌækəˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 专业学术的，过于学术的 |  Describes something relating to or characteristic of professional academics or academic institutions; often implies an excessive focus on academic matters, sometimes to the detriment of other aspects.  | 例句：His proacademic approach to teaching made the subject seem dry and inaccessible to many students.  他的教学方式过于学术化，使得许多学生觉得枯燥乏味且难以理解。  | 近义词：scholarly, academic, pedantic | 反义词：practical, applied, non-academic
