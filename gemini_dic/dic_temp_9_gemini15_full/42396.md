counterchange:/ˈkaʊntərtʃeɪndʒ/| n. |对调；交换；图案互换|The reciprocal interchange of colours or patterns in a design, typically in weaving or heraldry.  It refers to a design where two or more colours or patterns are interwoven or juxtaposed in such a way that they appear to be reversed or mirrored on different parts of the design. For example, a fabric might show red on a blue background in one area and blue on a red background in another.|The tapestry showed a beautiful counterchange of gold and crimson threads.|interchange, reversal, inversion|uniformity, consistency|名词
