quoter:/ˈkwəʊtər/ | n. | 引用者，报价者 | A person who quotes or cites something.  Someone who gives a quotation of prices. | Example: The quoter for the project underestimated the costs.  这个项目的报价者低估了成本。|近义词：citer, pricer | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The quoter provides accurate pricing. = 报价者提供准确的定价。
He quotes prices daily. = 他每天报价。


一般过去时:
The quoter submitted the estimate last week. = 报价者上周提交了估价。
She quoted a price for the service. = 她报了一个服务的价钱。


现在完成时:
The quoter has already sent the quotation. = 报价者已经发送了报价。
They have quoted several projects this month. = 这个月他们已经报了好几个项目的价。


过去完成时:
The client had already received the quoter's proposal. = 客户已经收到了报价者的提案。
Before the meeting, they had quoted several options. = 会议之前，他们已经报了几个方案的价。


将来时:
The quoter will send the final quote tomorrow. = 报价者明天将发送最终报价。
He will quote the price after reviewing the documents. = 他会在审核文件后报价。


虚拟语气:
If the quoter were more experienced, the estimate would be more accurate. = 如果报价者更有经验，估价会更准确。
I wish the quoter had provided a more detailed breakdown. = 我希望报价者能提供更详细的明细。


被动语态:
The prices were quoted by the supplier. = 价格由供应商报价。
The project was quoted at a surprisingly low price. = 该项目报了一个令人惊讶的低价。


疑问句:
Who is the quoter for this project? = 这个项目的报价者是谁？
What price was quoted for the repairs? = 维修报价是多少？


否定句:
The quoter did not include all the costs. = 报价者没有包括所有成本。
He didn't quote a price for the additional work. = 他没有为额外的工作报价。
