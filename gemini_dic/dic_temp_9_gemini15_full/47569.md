debarkation:/ˌdiːbɑːrˈkeɪʃən/| n. |下船；下飞机；卸货 |The act of disembarking from a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  It refers to the process of leaving a vessel or aircraft, usually at the end of a journey. |例句：The debarkation of passengers was delayed due to bad weather.  乘客的下船由于恶劣天气而延误。
例句：The debarkation process at the airport was smooth and efficient. 机场的下飞机流程顺利高效。|近义词：disembarkation, landing, unloading |反义词：embarkation, boarding, loading |
