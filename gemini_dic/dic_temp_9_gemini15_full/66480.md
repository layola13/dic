exergue:/ˈɛksɜːɡ/ | n. |题词；铭文；(硬币或奖章上的)边缘题记 | A short inscription, motto, or saying placed on a monument, coin, medal, or the like, often to provide a concise explanation or commentary.  It is typically placed at the bottom or side of the main text or image. | Example: The exergue of the coin bore the date of its minting. 这枚硬币的题词刻着它的铸造日期。|近义词：inscription, motto, legend |反义词：main text, body
