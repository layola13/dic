reharmonize:/ˌriːˈhɑːrmənaɪz/| vt.|重新和声化，重新配和弦|To change the harmony of (a musical passage) by substituting different chords while retaining the original melody.|例句：The composer decided to reharmonize the classical piece to give it a more modern feel.  作曲家决定重新为这首古典乐曲配和弦，以使其更具现代感。

例句：The jazz musician reharmonized the standard, adding unexpected chords that surprised the audience.  这位爵士乐音乐家重新为这首标准曲目配和弦，加入了出乎意料的和弦，令观众大吃一惊。


近义词：re-chord, rearrange, revise|反义词：leave unchanged, maintain original harmony|动词
