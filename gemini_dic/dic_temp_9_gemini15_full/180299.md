refrigeration:/ˌrɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən/| n. |冷藏；制冷|The process of making and maintaining something at a low temperature, typically to preserve food.  It involves the removal of heat from a confined space. |例句：Proper refrigeration is essential for keeping food fresh.  正确的冷藏对于保持食物新鲜至关重要。|例句：The invention of refrigeration revolutionized food storage. 制冷的发明彻底改变了食品储存方式。|近义词：cooling, chilling, freezing|反义词：heating, warming|名词
