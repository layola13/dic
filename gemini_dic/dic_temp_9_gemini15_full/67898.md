facades:/fəˈsɑːdz/ | n. | 外观；正面；门面 |  The outward appearance of something, especially a building, that may conceal the true nature or condition.  It can also refer to a false outward show. | The building's facades were beautifully restored. 这座建筑的正面经过了美丽的修复。 The politician maintained a facade of calm despite the crisis. 尽管发生了危机，这位政治家仍然保持着平静的外表。 | fronts, exteriors, masks | realities, truths | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The building has beautiful facades. = 这栋建筑有美丽的正面。
These facades hide a decaying interior. = 这些外观掩盖着腐朽的内部。


一般过去时:
The old facades were torn down. = 旧的正面被拆除了。
The facades of the shops looked very old. = 商店的正面看起来很旧。


现在完成时:
They have restored the facades of many old buildings. = 他们已经修复了许多旧建筑的正面。
The city has invested heavily in preserving its historic facades. = 这个城市大力投资于保护其历史建筑的正面。


过去完成时:
They had already repaired the facades before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经修好了正面。
The facades had been neglected for many years. = 正面多年来一直被忽视。


将来时:
They will renovate the building's facades next year. = 他们明年将翻新这栋建筑的正面。
The facades will be illuminated at night. = 正面将在晚上被照明。


虚拟语气:
If the facades were restored, the building would look much better. = 如果正面得到修复，这座建筑会看起来好得多。
I wish the facades were more attractive. = 我希望正面更吸引人。


被动语态:
The facades are being cleaned. = 正面正在清洗。
The facades were damaged by the storm. = 正面被暴风雨破坏了。


疑问句:
What style are the facades of the houses? = 这些房子的正面是什么风格？
Are the facades original or added later? = 正面是原始的还是后来添加的？


否定句:
The facades are not well-maintained. = 正面维护得不好。
The facades do not reflect the building's true age. = 正面并没有反映出建筑的真实年龄。


PHRASE->
