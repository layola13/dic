plectrums:/ˈplɛktrəmz/ | n. |拨片；琴拨 |  A plectrum is a small, thin piece of a hard material, such as plastic, bone, or metal, used to pluck the strings of a stringed instrument, such as a guitar or ukulele.  | Example: He used a plectrum to play a fast-paced flamenco guitar solo. 他用拨片演奏了一段节奏快速的弗拉明戈吉他独奏。 |近义词：pick | 反义词：无
