magnetizer: /ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzər/ | n. | 磁化器；磁化装置 | A device or substance that magnetizes something.  It can refer to a tool used to induce magnetism in materials or a material with strong magnetic properties used to magnetize others. | Example: The technician used a powerful magnetizer to align the magnetic domains in the hard drive.  技术人员使用强大的磁化器来调整硬盘中的磁畴。  | 近义词: magnetiser (British spelling) | 反义词: demagnetizer
