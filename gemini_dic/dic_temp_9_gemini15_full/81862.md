gridlocked:/ˈɡrɪdˌlɑːkt/ | adj. |交通堵塞的；僵局的；停滞不前的 |  Describes a situation, typically traffic, where movement is completely stopped or severely restricted due to congestion.  It can also refer to a political or social situation where no progress can be made due to conflicting interests. |例句：The city was gridlocked during rush hour.  = 高峰期城市交通严重堵塞。

The negotiations were gridlocked, with neither side willing to compromise. = 谈判陷入僵局，双方都不愿妥协。|近义词：blocked, congested, stalled, deadlocked |反义词：flowing, moving freely, unblocked


USAGE->
