redacteur:/ˈrɛdəktər/| n.|编辑，撰稿人|A redacteur is a person who edits or writes for a publication, especially a newspaper or magazine.  They are responsible for preparing written material for publication, often involving fact-checking, rewriting, and ensuring clarity and style.|例句：The redacteur meticulously checked the article for factual accuracy before submitting it for publication.  这位编辑在提交文章出版前仔细核实了文章的准确性。|近义词：editor, writer, journalist, subeditor |反义词：reader, audience
