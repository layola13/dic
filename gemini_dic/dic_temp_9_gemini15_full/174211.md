quenchable: /ˈkwɛntʃəbl/ | adj. | 可熄灭的；可抑制的；可满足的 | capable of being quenched or extinguished; able to be satisfied or suppressed.  | The fire was easily quenchable with a bucket of water.  这把火很容易用一桶水扑灭。|  extinguishable, suppressible | unquenchable, insatiable


USAGE->

The fire is quenchable with water. = 这场火可以用水扑灭。
This thirst is quenchable with a cold drink. = 这口渴可以用冷饮解渴。

一般过去时:

Her anger was quenchable after a long talk. = 她长时间谈话后怒气消了。

过去完成时:
