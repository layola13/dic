diaphote:/ˈdaɪəfoʊt/| n. |透射光，透射照片|A diaphote is a photograph taken using transmitted light, meaning the light passes through the subject before reaching the camera lens. This technique is often used for translucent objects or to capture internal structures.  | Example:  The scientist used a diaphote to study the intricate vein patterns of a leaf.  科学家用透射照片来研究一片叶子的复杂叶脉图案。|近义词：transmitted light photograph, transparency |反义词：opaque photograph
