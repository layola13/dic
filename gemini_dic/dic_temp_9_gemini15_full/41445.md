cornflour:/ˈkɔːrnflɑːr/| n. |玉米淀粉|Cornflour is a fine powder made from maize (corn) that is used as a thickening agent in cooking. It is a common ingredient in many recipes, particularly in sauces, soups, and puddings.  It's also sometimes used as a dusting powder for food. |The recipe calls for two tablespoons of cornflour to thicken the sauce. 这个食谱需要两汤匙玉米淀粉来使酱汁变稠。|starch,  maize starch |flour, wheat flour|
