savories:/ˈseɪvəriːz/| n.|美味咸味食品；开胃菜|Savories are dishes that are salty or spicy, rather than sweet.  They are often served as appetizers or snacks.|例句：The menu featured a selection of delicious savories, including cheese straws and mini quiches. 菜单上有各种美味的咸味食品，包括奶酪面包条和迷你蛋挞。

例句：We enjoyed a variety of savories before the main course.  我们在主菜前享用各种各样的开胃菜。|近义词：appetizers, hors d'oeuvres, snacks|反义词：sweets, desserts|词性:n.
