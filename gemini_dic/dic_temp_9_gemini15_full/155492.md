pheromones:/ˈferəmoʊnz/ | n. |信息素 |Chemical substances produced and released into the environment by animals, especially insects, affecting the behavior or physiology of others of the same species.  These scents can trigger various responses, such as attraction, alarm, or territorial marking.  |Example:  Many insects rely on pheromones to attract mates.  许多昆虫依靠信息素来吸引配偶。


The release of pheromones by the queen bee influences the behavior of the worker bees.  蜂王释放的信息素会影响工蜂的行为。|近义词：hormones, scents, signals |反义词：N/A
