orangeat:/ˈɔːrɪnˌdʒæt/| n. |橘子酱；橘子果酱 |Orangeat is a sweet preserve made from oranges, typically used as a flavoring agent in desserts and drinks.  It often contains pieces of candied orange peel.| Example: The cake was flavored with orangeat and a hint of cinnamon.  这个蛋糕用橘子酱和一点肉桂调味。|近义词：marmalade (if including peel) |反义词：无
