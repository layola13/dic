platinocyanic: /ˌplætɪnoʊsaɪˈænɪk/ | adj. | 铂氰的 |  Relating to or containing platinocyanides.  This adjective describes compounds containing the platinocyanide ion, [Pt(CN)₄]²⁻, which involves platinum bonded to four cyanide ligands.  These compounds often exhibit interesting optical properties, like fluorescence.

例句：

The experiment involved the synthesis of a new platinocyanic compound.  实验涉及一种新型铂氰化合物的合成。

The brilliant fluorescence of the platinocyanic crystals was striking. 铂氰晶体的明亮荧光非常引人注目。


近义词：platinic (referring to platinum in a higher oxidation state, not directly synonymous but related in terms of platinum chemistry)


反义词：无直接反义词，可根据语境考虑与不含铂氰的物质相关的形容词。


词性：形容词
