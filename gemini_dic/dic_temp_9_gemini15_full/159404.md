polariscope:/pəˈlærɪskoʊp/| n. |偏光镜；偏光仪|An instrument used to examine substances in polarized light, often to identify crystalline structures or determine the optical properties of materials.|例句：The geologist used a polariscope to analyze the rock sample. 地质学家用偏光镜来分析岩石样本。

The chemist employed a polariscope to study the birefringence of the crystal. 化学家用偏光镜研究晶体的双折射。|近义词：polarimeter, polarizing microscope |反义词:None
