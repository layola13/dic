quizzical:/ˈkwɪzɪkəl/| adj.| 好奇的；疑问的；玩味的；古怪的 | Expressing or showing curiosity or puzzlement.  It often suggests a slightly amused or skeptical curiosity. |例句：He gave me a quizzical look. 他给了我一个好奇的眼神。

例句：Her quizzical expression suggested she doubted my story. 她那疑问的表情暗示她怀疑我的故事。


近义词：curious, questioning, inquisitive, puzzled, skeptical, dubious

反义词：certain, sure, confident, unsurprised


词源：From quiz + -ical.  "Quiz" itself has uncertain origins, possibly from a slang term meaning "to question or examine."
