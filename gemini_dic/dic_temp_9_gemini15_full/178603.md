recounting:/rɪˈkaʊntɪŋ/| v. n.|复述，叙述，重新计算|The act of telling or describing something again, or the process of counting something again.  It can refer to a narrative account or a numerical recalculation.|例句：He gave a vivid recounting of his adventures in the Amazon. 他生动地讲述了他亚马逊河探险的经历。

例句：The recounting of the votes revealed a surprising outcome.  重新计票揭示了一个令人惊讶的结果。|近义词：narrating, relating, describing, recount, retelling, recalculating|反义词：forgetting, omitting, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recounts his childhood memories often. = 他经常回忆他的童年往事。
She recounts the events of the day to her diary. = 她把当天发生的事情记在日记里。

一般过去时:
He recounted his experiences in detail. = 他详细地叙述了他的经历。
She recounted the story to her friends. = 她向朋友们复述了这个故事。

现在完成时:
He has recounted this story many times. = 他已经多次讲述过这个故事。
She has recounted the events leading up to the accident. = 她已经叙述了导致事故发生的一系列事件。

过去完成时:
He had recounted the story before anyone else had a chance. = 在其他人有机会之前，他已经讲完了这个故事。
She had recounted her experiences to the police. = 她已经向警方讲述了她的经历。

将来时:
He will recount the events at the meeting tomorrow. = 他将在明天讲述会议上的事情。
She will recount her trip to the mountains in her blog. = 她将在博客上讲述她去山上的旅行。

虚拟语气:
If he recounted the truth, people would believe him. = 如果他讲述真相，人们就会相信他。
I wish she had recounted the incident more clearly. = 我希望她能更清晰地讲述这件事。

被动语态:
The story was recounted by several witnesses. = 这个故事被几个证人讲述了。
The results were recounted due to irregularities. = 由于出现不规则现象，结果被重新计算。

疑问句:
Will he recount the entire incident? = 他会把整个事件都讲述出来吗？
How accurately did she recount the events? = 她对事件的叙述有多准确？

否定句:
He did not recount the entire story. = 他没有讲述整个故事。
She refused to recount what she had seen. = 她拒绝讲述她所看到的事情。



PHRASE->

a firsthand recounting = 第一手的叙述

a biased recounting = 有偏见的叙述
