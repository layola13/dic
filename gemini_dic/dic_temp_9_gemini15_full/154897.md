pesage:/pəˈsɑːʒ/| n.|过磅；称重；重量；(尤指邮件的)重量费|The act of weighing something, especially goods or mail; the weight of something, especially mail.  It can also refer to the fee charged for weighing something, particularly mail.|例句：The pesage of the package was surprisingly high.  包裹的重量费出奇地高。

例句：The customs officer checked the pesage of the luggage. 海关官员检查了行李的重量。


近义词：weighing, weight, postage(for mail)

词性:名词
