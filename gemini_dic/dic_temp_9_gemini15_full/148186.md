overshadow:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊ/| vt.|使黯然失色；使相形见绌；遮蔽；笼罩|To make (something) seem less important or noticeable.  To cast a shadow over.|例句：His great achievements were overshadowed by the scandal.他的伟大成就被丑闻掩盖了。

The storm clouds overshadowed the sun.暴风雨的乌云遮蔽了太阳。|近义词：eclipse, obscure, dwarf, dim|反义词：highlight, illuminate, enhance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new building overshadows the old church. = 新建筑使古老的教堂黯然失色。
Her success overshadows her past mistakes. = 她的成功掩盖了她过去的错误。


一般过去时:
The bad news overshadowed our celebration. = 坏消息使我们的庆祝活动黯然失色。
The tall trees overshadowed the small cottage. = 高大的树木遮蔽了小别墅。


现在完成时:
Recent events have overshadowed the earlier debate. = 最近发生的事件使早些时候的辩论黯然失色。
His arrogance has overshadowed his talent. = 他的傲慢掩盖了他的才能。


过去完成时:
The scandal had overshadowed his career before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，丑闻就已经损害了他的事业。
The previous success had been overshadowed by the recent failure. = 先前的成功已经被最近的失败所掩盖。


将来时:
The upcoming election will overshadow all other political news. = 即将到来的选举将使所有其他政治新闻黯然失色。
The new project will overshadow the previous one. = 新项目将使之前的项目相形见绌。


虚拟语气:
If only the positive aspects weren't overshadowed by the negative ones. = 如果积极方面没有被消极方面所掩盖就好了。
I wish my accomplishments weren't overshadowed by my mistakes. = 我希望我的成就不会被我的错误所掩盖。


被动语态:
His achievements were overshadowed by a series of unfortunate events. = 他的成就被一系列不幸的事件所掩盖。
The small town was overshadowed by the massive skyscraper. = 小镇被巨大的摩天大楼遮蔽了。


疑问句:
Does his fame overshadow his talent? = 他的名声是否掩盖了他的才能？
Will the new stadium overshadow the old one? = 新体育场会使旧体育场黯然失色吗？


否定句:
The good news did not overshadow the bad news. = 好消息并没有掩盖坏消息。
His failures did not overshadow his successes. = 他的失败并没有掩盖他的成功。


PHRASE->
overshadowed by = 被…所掩盖
overshadowing figure = 举足轻重的人物
overshadowing presence = 强大的存在感
overshadowing influence = 影响力巨大
overshadowed with = 被…笼罩着
