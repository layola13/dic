intervisit:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪt/| v.|互访，互相拜访|To visit each other; to make reciprocal visits.|例句：The two families intervisited frequently during the summer. 两个家庭在夏天经常互相拜访。|例句：The delegations intervisited to foster better understanding. 代表团互访以增进相互了解。|近义词：reciprocate visits, exchange visits|反义词：avoid contact, remain isolated
