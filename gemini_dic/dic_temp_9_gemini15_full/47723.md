decadents:/ˈdɛkədənts/| n. adj.|颓废者；颓废的；耽于享乐的|A person or group of people who are decadent.  The adjective describes something characterized by moral or cultural decline. |例句：The decadent aristocracy lived a life of luxury and excess.  = 颓废的贵族过着奢侈放纵的生活。

The decadent society was riddled with corruption and moral decay. = 颓废的社会充斥着腐败和道德沦丧。|近义词：degenerate, depraved, dissolute|反义词：ascetic, virtuous, moral
