moles:/moʊlz/| n.|摩尔；鼹鼠；痣；|A mole is a unit of measurement in chemistry, representing a specific number of atoms or molecules (approximately 6.022 x 10^23). It can also refer to a small burrowing mammal, or a dark spot on the skin.  |Example:  The chemist calculated the number of moles in the solution. 化学家计算了溶液中的摩尔数。  The gardener set traps to catch the moles damaging his lawn. 园丁设下陷阱来捕捉破坏他草坪的鼹鼠。|近义词：unit (for the chemistry definition), shrew (for the animal definition), blemish (for the skin definition)|反义词：无
