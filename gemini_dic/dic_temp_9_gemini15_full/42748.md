/ˈkʌntri/| n. |国家；乡村；故乡 |A country is a nation with its own government and boundaries.  A country can also refer to a rural area. |例句：China is a large country. 中国是一个大国。

例句：We spent our vacation in the countryside. 我们在乡下度过了假期。|近义词：nation, state, land |反义词：city, town

The country produces a lot of rice. = 这个国家生产大量的稻米。
Many people live in the country. = 许多人住在乡下。


一般过去时:
He visited his home country last year. = 他去年访问了他的祖国。
