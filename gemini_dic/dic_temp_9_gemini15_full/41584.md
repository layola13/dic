corporealization: /kɔːrˌpɔːriəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 实体化；物质化 | The process of becoming corporeal or material; giving a physical form to something abstract or spiritual.  It often involves the embodiment of something previously intangible. | 例句：The artist's corporealization of abstract concepts was striking. 艺术家对抽象概念的实体化令人印象深刻。


The novel explored the corporealization of the protagonist's anxieties. 这部小说探讨了主人公焦虑的物质化。 | 近义词：materialization, embodiment, incarnation | 反义词：dematerialization, abstraction
