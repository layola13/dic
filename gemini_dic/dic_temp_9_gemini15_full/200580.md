slaughtered:/ˈslɔːtəd/ | vt. |屠宰；残杀；大量杀害 |To kill (animals) for food; to kill violently and in large numbers.  |例句：The farmer slaughtered the pig for the winter feast. 农民为过冬的盛宴宰杀了猪。|例句：Thousands were slaughtered in the war.  战争中成千上万的人被屠杀。|近义词：butcher, massacre, kill |反义词：save, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer slaughters the chickens every Friday. = 农民每星期五都宰杀鸡。
He slaughters his opponents in debate. = 他在辩论中痛击对手。


一般过去时:
They slaughtered the cattle yesterday. = 他们昨天屠宰了牛。
The army slaughtered the civilians. = 军队屠杀了平民。


现在完成时:
The butcher has slaughtered all the pigs. = 屠夫已经宰杀了所有的猪。
The rebels have slaughtered many innocent people. = 反叛者已经杀害了许多无辜的人。


过去完成时:
They had slaughtered all the sheep before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经宰杀了所有的羊。
The soldiers had slaughtered hundreds of people before the city fell. = 城池陷落之前，士兵们已经屠杀了数百人。


将来时:
The farmer will slaughter the turkeys for Thanksgiving. = 农民将为感恩节宰杀火鸡。
They will slaughter their enemies without mercy. = 他们将毫不留情地屠杀他们的敌人。


虚拟语气:
If they had slaughtered the animals humanely, it would have been better. = 如果他们人道地宰杀了动物，那就好了。
I wish they hadn't slaughtered so many innocent people. = 我真希望他们没有屠杀那么多无辜的人。


被动语态:
The chickens were slaughtered early this morning. = 鸡是在今天早上早些时候被宰杀的。
Many civilians were slaughtered during the conflict. = 冲突期间许多平民被屠杀。


疑问句:
Were the animals slaughtered according to regulations? = 动物是否按照规定被宰杀？
How many people were slaughtered in the massacre? = 在那次大屠杀中，有多少人被杀害？


否定句:
They did not slaughter the sheep yesterday. = 他们昨天没有宰杀羊。
The soldiers refused to slaughter the prisoners of war. = 士兵拒绝屠杀战俘。
