commendations:/ˌkɑːmənˈdeɪʃənz/ | n. | 称赞；嘉奖；推荐 | Expressions of praise or approval; an official expression of praise or approval.  | The team received commendations for their outstanding work. 团队因其杰出工作而受到表彰。 | praise, accolades, tributes, citations | criticism, condemnation, rebuke | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives many commendations for his bravery. = 他因勇敢而获得许多表彰。
The company gives commendations to its top performers. = 公司给予表现最佳的员工表彰。

一般过去时:
She received a commendation for her outstanding service. = 她因其杰出服务而获得表彰。
The soldiers were given commendations for their heroism. = 这些士兵因其英勇事迹而获得表彰。

The project has earned many commendations for its innovative design. = 该项目因其创新设计而赢得了许多表彰。

过去完成时:

将来时:
