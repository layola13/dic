forearm:/ˈfɔːrɑːm/| n.|前臂|The part of the arm between the elbow and the wrist.  It is the section of the upper limb extending from the elbow to the wrist and contains the radius and ulna bones.|例句：He broke his forearm in the accident. 他在事故中摔断了前臂。

例句：She flexed her forearm, showing off her muscles. 她弯曲前臂，炫耀她的肌肉。


近义词：lower arm


反义词：upper arm


词性：名词
