dumple:/ˈdʌmpl/| n.| 饺子；(尤指英国的)油炸面团；|A dumpling is a small ball of dough, often filled with meat or vegetables, that is cooked by boiling, steaming, or frying.  In British English, it often refers specifically to a fried ball of dough.|例句：She made delicious pork dumplings for dinner. 她做了美味的猪肉饺子当晚餐。|例句：He ordered a plate of fried dumplings as a side dish. 他点了一盘油炸饺子作为配菜。|近义词：boil, steam, fry|反义词：无
