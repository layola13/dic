highjacked:/ˈhaɪdʒækt/ | v. | 劫持；篡改；盗用 | To seize control of (a vehicle, especially an aircraft, or other conveyance) by force.  It can also refer to the unauthorized use or alteration of something, often in a digital context. |例句：The plane was highjacked by terrorists.  飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。

The hackers highjacked the company's website. 黑客劫持了公司的网站。|近义词：seize, commandeer, hijack |反义词：release, relinquish
