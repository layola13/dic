phantomship:/ˈfæntəmʃɪp/| n. |幽灵船；虚构的船只；不存在的船只 |A phantomship is a ship that is believed to exist only in legend or folklore, often associated with supernatural events or unexplained disappearances. It can also refer to a ship that is registered but does not actually exist, or a company that exists only on paper.  |例句：The sailors swore they saw a phantomship sailing on the horizon. 水手们发誓他们在地平线上看到一艘幽灵船。

The legend of the Flying Dutchman is a famous example of a phantomship.  飞翔的荷兰人传说是一个著名的幽灵船例子。|近义词：ghost ship, phantom vessel |反义词：real ship, existing vessel
