lyceum:/laɪˈsiːəm/| n.|讲习所；博物馆；剧院；文理学院|A lyceum is a public building, often a hall, used for lectures, concerts, or other public meetings.  Historically, it was a type of school or institution for advanced learning. |例句：The town's lyceum hosted a series of lectures on ancient history.  这个镇的讲习所举办了一系列关于古代历史的讲座。


例句：The new lyceum offers a wide range of courses in science and the humanities. 新的文理学院提供各种科学和人文学科课程。


近义词：institute, academy, hall, auditorium|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
