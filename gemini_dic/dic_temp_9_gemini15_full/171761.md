punchcard:/ˈpʌntʃkɑːrd/ | n. | 打孔卡片 | A punch card is a piece of stiff paper that contains digitally recorded data represented by the presence or absence of holes in predefined positions.  These cards were used extensively in early data processing and computing. | Example: The old computer system used punch cards to input data.  老式计算机系统使用打孔卡输入数据。|近义词：hollerith card, data card |反义词：None
