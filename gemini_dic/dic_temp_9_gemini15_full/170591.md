pseudo-cultural:/ˌsuːdəʊˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. |伪文化的；似是而非的文化；假文化的 |  Relating to or characteristic of something that superficially resembles a genuine culture but lacks its authenticity or depth.  It often implies a manufactured or commercialized imitation of cultural elements, lacking genuine cultural significance or understanding. | 例句：The resort offered a pseudo-cultural experience with staged traditional dances and overpriced handicrafts.  这个度假村提供了一种伪文化的体验，有编排好的传统舞蹈和价格过高的工艺品。


例句：The band's music, while popular, was criticized for its pseudo-cultural appropriation of indigenous rhythms.  这支乐队的音乐虽然流行，但因其对土著节奏的伪文化挪用而受到批评。


近义词：artificial, contrived, simulated, affected, ersatz


反义词：authentic, genuine, real, traditional, original

词性:形容词
