recompute:/ˌriːkəmˈpjuːt/| vt.|重新计算，再计算|To compute again; to calculate again.|例句：We need to recompute the figures to ensure accuracy.我们需要重新计算这些数字以确保准确性。

例句：The software automatically recomputes the results after each data entry.该软件在每次数据输入后会自动重新计算结果。


近义词： recalculate, recalibrate, refigure|反义词：compute initially, calculate originally|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recompute the budget every quarter. = 他们每季度重新计算预算。
We recompute the trajectory after each sensor reading. = 我们在每次传感器读数后重新计算轨迹。
