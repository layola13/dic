jamb:/dʒæm/| n.| 门框，窗框；阻塞物 | A jamb is the vertical side-piece of a doorway or window frame.  It can also refer to something that obstructs or jams. | Example: The door wouldn't close because the jamb was warped. 门关不上是因为门框变形了。The traffic jam blocked the road. 交通堵塞阻塞了道路。|近义词：frame, doorpost, lintel | 反义词：opening
