/pɛst/| n.|害虫，瘟疫|a destructive insect or other animal that attacks crops, food, livestock, etc.|例句：The farmer sprayed pesticide to control the pests in his field.  这位农民喷洒农药来控制田里的害虫。|近义词：vermin, parasite|反义词:beneficial insect
