reimprint:/ˌriːˈɪmprɪnt/| vt.|再印；重新铭刻；再次留下深刻印象|To print again; to impress again; to leave a strong impression again.  The book was reimprinted due to high demand.  |近义词：reprint, restamp, re-etch|反义词：erase, obliterate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reimprint the logo on every product. = 他们在每个产品上重新印制标志。
The company reimprints its marketing materials annually. = 公司每年都会重新印刷营销材料。


一般过去时:
The publisher reimprinted the bestseller last month. = 出版商上个月重新印刷了这本畅销书。

现在完成时:
