homesickness:/ˈhoʊmsɪknəs/ | n. |思乡病，想家 |A feeling of intense longing for home and family while one is away from them.  This feeling is often accompanied by sadness, anxiety, and even depression. |例句：She felt intense homesickness after only a week away at college. 她去大学才一周就感到强烈的思乡之情。

例句：The long trip intensified his homesickness. 漫长的旅行加剧了他的思乡之情。


近义词：nostalgia, longing for home | 反义词：contentment, comfort

Homesickness often affects students studying abroad.  = 思乡之情常常影响在国外学习的学生。
He feels homesickness whenever he is away from his family. = 他无论何时离开家人都会感到思乡。

My homesickness subsided after a few weeks. = 几周后，我的思乡之情减轻了。

词源:
The word "homesickness" is a compound word formed from "home" and "sick".  "Sick" in this context doesn't mean physically ill, but rather mentally distressed or longing.  The word evolved naturally from the feeling itself.
