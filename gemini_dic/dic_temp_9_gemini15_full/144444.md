outswim:/aʊtˈswɪm/| vt.|超过…游泳，游过…|To swim faster or further than (someone or something).|例句：He outswam all the other competitors in the race. 他在比赛中超过所有其他选手。

例句：The strong current nearly outswam her.  强劲的洋流差点把她冲走。

近义词：surpass, outpace, exceed|反义词：be outswum by|词性：动词
