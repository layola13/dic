feminizes:/ˈfɛmɪnaɪzɪz/| vt.|使女性化；使具有女性特征 | To make or become more feminine in appearance or character.  |例句：The new dress feminizes her figure. 这件新裙子使她的身材显得更女性化了。|例句：He feminizes his voice when he speaks to children. 他跟孩子们说话时会把声音弄得更女性化。|近义词：effeminate, womanize|反义词：masculinize
