eryngo:/ɛˈrɪŋɡoʊ/ | n. |海蓬子，海防风 | A plant of the carrot family, with blue-purple flowers and edible root.  It is also known as sea holly.  The roots and stems can be candied or used in salads. | Example: The candied eryngo was a delightful treat.  (蜜饯海防风是一种美味的甜点。)  The chef used eryngo in his innovative salad. (厨师在他的创新沙拉中使用了海防风。) | 近义词：sea holly | 反义词：无
