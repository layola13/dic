increasable: /ɪnˈkriːsəbl/ | adj. | 可增加的，可增长的 | Capable of being increased.  Something that is increasable can be made larger, greater, or more numerous. | The company's profits are increasable through strategic marketing. 公司的利润可以通过战略营销来增加。The available memory is increasable by adding more RAM. 可用的内存可以通过添加更多内存来增加。 |  expandable, augmentable |  decreasable


USAGE->

The production capacity is increasable. = 生产能力是可以增加的。
This software's features are increasable. = 这个软件的功能是可以增加的。
