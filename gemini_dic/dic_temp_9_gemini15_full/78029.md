gentrifying:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪɪŋ/| v. |使中产阶级化；改善（贫民区）|The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it conforms to middle-class taste. This often leads to displacement of lower-income residents.|例句：The neighborhood is rapidly gentrifying, pushing out long-term residents.  = 这个街区正在迅速地中产阶级化，迫使长期居住的居民搬走。|例句：The city's efforts to gentrify the downtown area have been met with mixed reactions. = 城市改善市中心区域的努力收到了褒贬不一的回应。|近义词：improving, upgrading, renovating|反义词：deteriorating, slumming, decaying|动词
