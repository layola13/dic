chatted:/ˈtʃætɪd/| v.| 聊天；闲聊 | To talk in a friendly informal way. |例句：We chatted for hours about our childhood memories. 我们聊了几个小时关于我们童年的回忆。|例句：They chatted happily over coffee. 他们一边喝咖啡一边愉快地聊天。|近义词：conversed, gossiped, talked |反义词：remained silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chat every day. = 他们每天聊天。
We chat online frequently. = 我们经常在线聊天。

一般过去时:
I chatted with my friend yesterday. = 我昨天和我的朋友聊天了。
They chatted about the movie after the show. = 演出结束后他们聊了聊电影。

现在完成时:
We have chatted for two hours. = 我们已经聊了两个小时了。
She has chatted with him several times. = 她已经和他聊过几次了。

过去完成时:
They had chatted before the meeting started. = 会议开始之前他们已经聊过了。
I had chatted with her on the phone before I saw her. = 我在见到她之前已经和她通了电话。

将来时:
I will chat with him tomorrow. = 我明天会和他聊天的。
We will chat online later tonight. = 我们今晚稍后会在线聊天。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would have chatted with you longer. = 如果我有时间，我会和你聊更久的。
I wish I had chatted with her before she left. = 我希望在她离开之前我已经和她聊过了。

被动语态:
The issue was chatted about extensively. =  这个问题被广泛讨论了。 (This is less common for "chat")
The problem was chatted through. = 这个问题通过聊天解决了。(This usage is idiomatic)


疑问句:
Did you chat with him? = 你和他聊了吗？
Have they chatted yet? = 他们聊过了吗？

否定句:
I didn't chat with anyone today. = 我今天没有和任何人聊天。
They haven't chatted recently. = 他们最近没有聊天。


PHRASE->
chat away = 滔滔不绝地聊天
chat up = 搭讪
chat someone up = 勾搭某人
have a chat = 聊聊天
catch up (with a chat) = 聊聊近况
