defrock:/dɪˈfrɒk/| vt.|剥夺圣职；除掉…的圣职；|To remove someone from their position or status as a member of the clergy.  The act of stripping a priest or other clergyman of his or her religious office. |例句：The priest was defrocked for misconduct.  这位牧师因行为不端被剥夺了圣职。

例句：He was defrocked after admitting to a series of financial irregularities. 他承认了一系列财务违规行为后被撤职。


近义词：unfrocking,  disrobe (in a figurative sense)


反义词：ordain, consecrate


词性：动词
