customer:/ˈkʌstəmə(r)/| n. |顾客，客户 |A person who buys goods or services from a shop or business.  |例句：The customer complained about the poor service.  顾客抱怨服务差。|例句：We value our loyal customers. 我们重视我们的忠实顾客。|近义词：client, patron, buyer |反义词：seller, vendor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The customer buys a new phone. = 顾客买了一部新手机。
The customer wants a refund. = 顾客想要退款。

一般过去时:
The customer bought a new car last week. = 顾客上周买了一辆新车。
The customer asked for a discount. = 顾客要求打折。

现在完成时:
The customer has already paid for the goods. = 顾客已经付了货款。
The customer has complained several times. = 顾客已经投诉过几次了。

过去完成时:
The customer had already left when I arrived. = 我到达时顾客已经离开了。
The customer had complained before, so we offered a solution. = 顾客之前就抱怨过，所以我们提供了一个解决方案。

将来时:
The customer will receive a confirmation email. = 顾客将会收到一封确认邮件。
The customer will visit our store next week. = 顾客下周将访问我们的商店。

虚拟语气:
If the customer were satisfied, he would recommend us. = 如果顾客满意，他会推荐我们。
I wish the customer had understood the instructions. = 我希望顾客已经理解了说明。

被动语态:
The customer was served promptly. = 顾客得到了迅速的服务。
The customer's request was fulfilled. = 顾客的要求得到了满足。

疑问句:
Is the customer happy with the product? = 顾客对产品满意吗？
Does the customer need any assistance? = 顾客需要帮助吗？

否定句:
The customer didn't like the color. = 顾客不喜欢这种颜色。
The customer wasn't satisfied with the service. = 顾客对服务不满意。


PHRASE->
customer service: 顾客服务
customer satisfaction: 顾客满意度
customer loyalty: 顾客忠诚度
potential customer: 潜在客户
valued customer: 尊贵的顾客
