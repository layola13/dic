enforces:/ɪnˈfɔːrsɪz/ | vt. |强制执行；实施；加强 |To compel observance of or compliance with (a law, rule, etc.).  |例句：The government enforces strict environmental regulations.  政府强制执行严格的环境法规。|例句：The police enforced the law by arresting the suspect. 警方逮捕嫌疑犯以执行法律。|近义词：implement, execute, uphold |反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school enforces a strict dress code. = 学校强制执行严格的着装规范。
The police enforce traffic laws daily. = 警察每天执行交通法规。

一般过去时:
The judge enforced the contract. = 法官强制执行了合同。
They enforced the rules with a firm hand. = 他们铁腕执行规则。

现在完成时:
The government has enforced new tax laws. = 政府已经实施了新的税法。
The company has enforced its policy on workplace safety. = 公司已经执行了其关于工作场所安全的政策。

过去完成时:
The court had enforced the previous ruling. = 法院已经执行了之前的判决。
Before the incident, they had enforced a no-tolerance policy. = 事发之前，他们一直执行零容忍政策。

将来时:
The authorities will enforce the new regulations next month. = 当局将于下个月实施新规。
The school will enforce its new policy starting next semester. = 学校将从下学期开始执行新的政策。

虚拟语气:
If the government enforced stricter laws, crime rates might decrease. = 如果政府实施更严格的法律，犯罪率可能会下降。
I wish they enforced the rules more consistently. = 我希望他们更一致地执行规则。

被动语态:
The law was enforced by the police. = 这项法律由警方执行。
The new rules are being enforced throughout the company. = 新规正在公司范围内执行。

疑问句:
Does the company enforce a dress code? = 公司是否强制执行着装规范？
How will they enforce this difficult regulation? = 他们将如何执行这项难以执行的规定？

否定句:
The school doesn't enforce that rule anymore. = 学校不再执行那条规定了。
The government failed to enforce the environmental protection laws effectively. = 政府未能有效地执行环境保护法。
