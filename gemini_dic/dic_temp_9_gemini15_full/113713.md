macrobenthic:/ˌmækroʊˈbɛnθɪk/| adj.|大型底栖的|Relating to or denoting organisms, especially animals, living in the bottom of a sea, lake, or other body of water, that are visible to the naked eye.  These organisms play a crucial role in benthic ecosystems, influencing nutrient cycling and sediment stability.|例句：Macrobenthic communities are significantly affected by pollution. 大型底栖生物群落受污染影响很大。

例句：The study focused on the distribution of macrobenthic invertebrates in the estuary.  这项研究的重点是河口大型底栖无脊椎动物的分布。


近义词：benthic, bottom-dwelling|反义词：pelagic, planktonic|词性:形容词
