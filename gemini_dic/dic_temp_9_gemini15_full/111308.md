liturgiologist:/ˌlɪtərˈdʒiːɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |礼仪学家，礼拜仪式学者 | A liturgiologist is a scholar who studies liturgy, the form and order of a religious service or rite.  They analyze the historical development, theological meaning, and practical application of liturgical practices. |  Dr. Smith, a renowned liturgiologist, presented a fascinating paper on the evolution of the Eucharist. 史密斯博士，一位著名的礼仪学家，发表了一篇关于圣餐演变的精彩论文。 |  liturgical scholar, ritualist |  (无明确反义词)
