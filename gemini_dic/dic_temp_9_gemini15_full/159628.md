polluting:/pəˈluːtɪŋ/| adj.|污染的；弄脏的| causing pollution or damage to the environment.  This describes something that is in the process of causing pollution. |例句：The polluting factory was shut down.  这家污染严重的工厂被关闭了。|例句：The polluting effects of the oil spill were devastating.  漏油的污染效应是毁灭性的。|近义词：contaminating, tainting, fouling|反义词：purifying, cleansing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The factory is polluting the river. = 工厂正在污染河流。
The car is polluting the air. = 汽车正在污染空气。

一般过去时:
The company polluted the lake last year. = 公司去年污染了湖泊。
The storm polluted the beach with debris. = 暴风雨把沙滩弄脏了。


现在完成时:
We have been polluting the environment for decades. = 我们几十年来一直在污染环境。
They have polluted the air with harmful chemicals. = 他们用有害化学物质污染了空气。


过去完成时:
They had polluted the water before anyone noticed. = 他们在任何人注意到之前就已经污染了水。
The industry had polluted the land for years. = 这个行业多年来一直在污染土地。


将来时:
The new power plant will pollute the atmosphere. = 新发电厂将污染大气。
If we don't act, we will pollute the oceans further. = 如果我们不采取行动，我们将进一步污染海洋。


虚拟语气:
If the factory continued polluting, the river would be destroyed. = 如果工厂继续污染，河流将会被破坏。
It's crucial that we stop polluting our planet. = 停止污染我们的星球至关重要。


被动语态:
The river is being polluted by industrial waste. = 河流正在被工业废物污染。
The air was polluted by the nearby factory. = 空气被附近的工厂污染了。


疑问句:
Is the factory still polluting the river? = 工厂还在污染河流吗？
What is polluting the water supply? = 什么东西正在污染供水？


否定句:
The company isn't polluting the environment anymore. = 公司不再污染环境了。
We didn't pollute the lake; it was a natural occurrence. = 我们没有污染湖泊；这是自然现象。
