dressily:/ˈdrɛsɪli/| adj. |穿着讲究地；盛装地| In a way that is elegantly or smartly dressed.  The word implies a deliberate and noticeable effort in one's attire. |例句：She dressed herself dressily for the wedding. 她盛装出席婚礼。

例句：The guests arrived dressily, showcasing their finest attire. 客人们盛装出席，展示了他们最精美的服饰。


近义词：elegantly, smartly, stylishly, fashionably


反义词：sloppily, shabbily, carelessly


词性:形容词
