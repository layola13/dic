sandaled:/ˈsændəld/ | adj. | 穿凉鞋的；饰有凉鞋图案的 | Wearing sandals; adorned with a sandal design.  |例句：The children were sandaled and ready for the beach. 孩子们穿着凉鞋，准备去海滩。

例句：The designer's new collection featured a sandaled motif on many of the dresses. 设计师的新系列在许多连衣裙上都饰有凉鞋图案。
