marjoram:/ˈmɑːrdʒərəm/| n. |马郁兰|A sweet-smelling herb used in cooking, belonging to the mint family.  It has small, oval leaves and is used fresh or dried.|例句：She added a sprig of marjoram to the stew to enhance its flavor. 她往炖菜里加了一枝马郁兰来增强香味。

例句：The aroma of roasted vegetables and marjoram filled the kitchen. 烤蔬菜和马郁兰的香味充满了厨房。


近义词：oregano, thyme, savory|反义词：无|词性:noun
