citational:/saɪˈteɪʃənəl/| adj.|引证的；引用的；提及的|Relating to or involving citation; characterized by or based on citations.|The essay had a strong citational basis, relying heavily on established research. 这篇文章有很强的引证基础，严重依赖已有的研究。|近义词：referential, quoted, cited|反义词：original, independent

一般现在时:
The article is highly citational in nature. 这篇文章本质上是高度引证性的。
His work is citational of many previous theories. 他的作品引用了许多以前的理论。


一般过去时:
The professor's lecture was heavily citational. 教授的讲座大量引用了他人的观点。
The student's paper was largely citational, lacking original analysis. 学生的论文很大程度上是引用的，缺乏原创性分析。


现在完成时:
His latest book has been highly citational in academic circles. 他的最新著作在学术界已被高度引用。
The research has become increasingly citational in recent years. 近年来，这项研究越来越被引用。


过去完成时:
The article had been citational of earlier works on the subject. 这篇文章已经引用了关于该主题的早期著作。
Before publication, the manuscript had been heavily citational, requiring significant revision. 在出版之前，手稿大量引用了其他资料，需要进行大量的修改。

虚拟语气:
If the paper were more citational, it would be more convincing. 如果这篇论文更注重引用，它会更有说服力。
I wish my dissertation were less citational and more original. 我希望我的论文少一些引用，多一些原创性。


被动语态:
The ideas presented in the paper were heavily citational.  论文中提出的观点大量引用了其他资料。
His work is often citational of classical literature. 他的作品经常引用古典文学。


疑问句:
Is the author's argument primarily citational or analytical? 作者的论点主要是引用的还是分析性的？
Is this paper too citational for a doctoral dissertation? 这篇论文对于博士论文来说是不是引用过多？


否定句:
The student's essay was not sufficiently citational. 学生的论文引用不足。
The article was not merely citational; it presented a novel argument. 这篇文章不仅仅是引用，它还提出了一个新的论点。
