skydives:/ˈskaɪdaɪvz/| v.|跳伞|To jump from an aircraft and fall through the air, typically using a parachute.|例句：He skydives every weekend for the thrill. 他每个周末跳伞寻求刺激。|近义词：parachutes|反义词:None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skydives regularly. = 他定期跳伞。
She skydives with her friends. = 她和朋友一起跳伞。

一般过去时:
I skydived for the first time last year. = 我去年第一次跳伞。
They skydived over the mountains. = 他们飞越山脉跳伞。

现在完成时:
He has skydived over 100 times. = 他跳伞超过100次了。
She has never skydived before. = 她以前从未跳伞过。

过去完成时:
I had never skydived before I went to New Zealand. = 在我去新西兰之前我从未跳伞过。
They had already skydived twice that day. = 那天他们已经跳伞两次了。

将来时:
I will skydive next month. = 我下个月将要跳伞。
We will skydive in tandem. = 我们将双人跳伞。

虚拟语气:
If I had the courage, I would skydive. = 如果我有勇气，我会跳伞。
I wish I could skydive. = 我希望我能跳伞。

被动语态:
The parachute was skydived from the plane. (This usage is unusual and grammatically awkward.  The passive voice is not typically used with this verb.)=降落伞从飞机上被投下。(这种用法不常见且语法上别扭。这个动词通常不用被动语态。)
Skydiving is often done in groups. = 跳伞通常是集体进行的。


疑问句:
Have you ever skydived? = 你曾经跳过伞吗？
Will she skydive tomorrow? = 她明天会跳伞吗？

否定句:
I don't skydive. = 我不跳伞。
She didn't skydive last weekend. = 她上周末没跳伞。
