labyrinths:/ˈlæbərɪnθs/ | n. | 迷宫；复杂局面 | A labyrinth is a complex network of interconnecting passages or paths, especially one that is difficult to navigate.  It can also refer to a confusing or complicated situation. |例句：The ancient city was a labyrinth of narrow streets. 古城是一座纵横交错的狭窄街道的迷宫。

The investigation led us down a labyrinth of legal technicalities.  调查把我们带入了一系列复杂的法律技术细节的困境中。|近义词：maze, tangle, network, complexity |反义词：straight path, simplicity
