incriminations:/ˌɪnkrɪmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 罪名指控；控告；罪证 | The act of accusing someone of a crime or wrongdoing; the state of being accused.  It often involves presenting evidence to support the accusation. |例句：The incriminations against him were based on circumstantial evidence.  他对他的指控是基于间接证据。

The incriminations led to a lengthy and costly trial.  这些指控导致了一场漫长而昂贵的审判。


近义词：accusations, charges, indictments, allegations

反义词：exculpations, exonerations


词源：源自拉丁语“incriminare”，意为“控告，指控”。  “in-”表示“进入，进入”， “criminare”源于“crimen”，意为“罪行”。
