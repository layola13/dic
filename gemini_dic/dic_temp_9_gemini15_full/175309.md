rais:/reɪz/| vt.  |举起；提高；抚养；筹集；引起|to lift something to a higher position; to increase the amount, level, or value of something; to bring up children; to collect money for a cause; to cause something to happen |例句：He raised his hand to ask a question. 他举手提问。
The company raised its prices by 10%. 公司将价格提高了10%。

例句：She raised three children on her own. 她独自抚养了三个孩子。


例句：The charity is trying to raise money for a new hospital.  慈善机构正在努力为新建医院筹集资金。


例句：His actions raised concerns among the public. 他的行为引起了公众的担忧。


近义词：lift, elevate, increase, rear, collect, arouse, evoke| 反义词：lower, decrease, depress, suppress|动词
