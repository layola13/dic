irreverent:/ɪˈrevərənt/| adj.| 不敬的；无礼的；轻率的 | Showing a lack of respect for people or things that are generally considered sacred or important.  |例句：His irreverent remarks about the president caused a stir. 他对总统的不敬言论引起了一阵骚动。

例句：The comedian's irreverent humor pushed the boundaries of good taste.  这位喜剧演员不敬的幽默突破了良好趣味的界限。


近义词：disrespectful, impious, flippant, sacrilegious

反义词：respectful, reverent, pious, devout


词源:
The word "irreverent" comes from the Old French word "irreverent," which itself is derived from the Latin word "irreverens."  "Ir-" is a negative prefix, "rever" relates to "reverence" (deep respect), and "-ent" is an adjectival suffix.  Therefore, the word literally means "not reverent."
