fjord:/fjɔːrd/| n. |峡湾|A long, narrow, deep inlet of the sea between high cliffs, as in Norway or Scotland.  Fjords are formed by glacial erosion. |The Norwegian fjords are breathtakingly beautiful.  挪威峡湾美得令人窒息。|The cruise ship sailed through a stunning fjord.  游轮驶过一个令人惊叹的峡湾。|inlet, sound|sea, ocean|
