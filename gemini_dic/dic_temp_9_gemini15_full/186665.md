right-angled: /ˈraɪtˈæŋɡld/ | adj. | 直角的 | Having an angle of 90 degrees.  A right-angled triangle has one angle of 90 degrees.  The term is commonly used in geometry and mathematics to describe shapes and angles. |

例句：

The triangle is right-angled. = 这个三角形是直角三角形。

The corner of the room is right-angled. = 房间的角落是直角的。


近义词：rectangular (when referring to shapes), perpendicular


反义词：obtuse-angled, acute-angled


词源:

* **right:**  From Old English *riht*, meaning "straight, correct, just."  In this context, it refers to a straight angle, a 90-degree angle.

* **angled:** From "angle," ultimately deriving from the Latin *angulus*, meaning "corner, angle."
