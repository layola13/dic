/ˌpriːtərˈplɜːrfɛkt/ | adj. | 过去完成时 |  The preterpluperfect tense is a verb tense used to describe an action that was completed before another action in the past. It is formed using the past perfect tense ("had" + past participle).  It indicates an action that happened earlier than another past action.  |


例句：

Before she went to the party, she had finished her homework.  = 在她去参加聚会之前，她已经完成了作业。

He had already eaten dinner when his friends arrived. = 当他的朋友们到达时，他已经吃过晚饭了。


近义词：prior, antecedent (for the action),  earlier (for time)


反义词： subsequent, later (for time),  following (for the action)


词性：形容词 (describing the tense)
