hint-at: /hɪnt æt/ | v. | 暗示，提示 | To suggest or imply something indirectly; to allude to something subtly.  |例句：Her silence hinted at her disapproval.  她的沉默暗示了她的不满。

Her nervous laughter hinted at underlying anxiety. 她紧张的笑声暗示了潜在的焦虑。


近义词：suggest, imply, insinuate, allude to, intimate


反义词：state explicitly, declare openly


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hints at trouble ahead. 他暗示前方有麻烦。
She hints at her dissatisfaction with her job. 她暗示对工作的不满。


一般过去时:
He hinted at a possible solution. 他暗示了一个可能的解决方案。
She hinted at her plans for the future. 她暗示了她未来的计划。


现在完成时:
They have hinted at changes in management. 他们暗示了管理层的变动。
She has hinted at wanting a promotion. 她暗示想要升职。


过去完成时:
He had hinted at his resignation weeks before. 他几周前就暗示过他要辞职。
She had hinted at the problems she was facing. 她暗示了她面临的问题。


将来时:
He will hint at his intentions later. 他稍后会暗示他的意图。
She will hint at the surprise party. 她会暗示惊喜派对。


虚拟语气:
If he hinted at the truth, I would believe him. 如果他暗示真相，我会相信他。
I wish she had hinted at her unhappiness sooner. 我希望她早点暗示她的不快乐。


被动语态:
The article hinted at the scandal. 这篇文章暗示了丑闻。（注意：hint at 通常不用于被动语态，此例为特殊情况）
His dissatisfaction was hinted at in his letter. 他的不满在他的信中有所暗示。（此例更符合被动语态表达）


疑问句:
What are you hinting at? 你在暗示什么？
Is he hinting at something important? 他在暗示什么重要的事情吗？


否定句:
He didn't hint at any problems. 他没有暗示任何问题。
She didn't hint at knowing the secret. 她没有暗示知道这个秘密。


PHRASE->
hint at something: 暗示某事
drop a hint: 顺便提及；暗示
give someone a hint: 给某人提示
take a hint: 领会暗示
subtle hint:  细微的暗示
