re-practicing:/ˌriːˈpræktɪsɪŋ/ | v. |反复练习，再练习 |The act of practicing something again, often to improve skill or mastery.  It implies a previous attempt at practicing. |例句：She's re-practicing her piano sonata for the upcoming recital. = 她正在为即将到来的独奏会反复练习她的钢琴奏鸣曲。


例句：He is re-practicing his presentation for the important meeting. = 他正在为重要的会议反复练习他的演讲。


近义词：rehearsing, reviewing, drilling


反义词： neglecting, abandoning



USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are re-practicing their dance routine. = 他们在反复练习他们的舞蹈套路。
We are re-practicing the song for the choir performance. = 我们正在为合唱表演反复练习这首歌。

一般过去时:
She re-practiced her lines for the play. = 她为戏剧反复练习台词。
They re-practiced the experiment to verify the results. = 他们重复实验以验证结果。

现在完成时:
I have re-practiced the guitar solo several times. = 我已经反复练习了吉他独奏几次。
He has re-practiced his speech since the last presentation. = 自上次演讲以来，他已经反复练习了他的演讲。

过去完成时:
They had re-practiced the scene before the director arrived. = 导演到达之前，他们已经反复排练了这个场景。
She had re-practiced the song many times before the audition. = 在试镜之前，她已经多次反复练习了这首歌。

将来时:
We will re-practice the code before deploying it. = 我们将在部署代码之前反复练习。
They will re-practice the play for the final performance. = 他们将为最终演出反复练习戏剧。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-practice my French. = 如果我有更多时间，我会反复练习我的法语。
If she were to compete, she would re-practice her routine. = 如果她要参加比赛，她会反复练习她的套路。

被动语态:
The dance routine was re-practiced many times. = 舞蹈套路被反复练习了很多次。

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not re-practice the scales. = 他没有反复练习音阶。
They did not re-practice the scene before the performance. = 他们在演出前没有反复练习那个场景。
