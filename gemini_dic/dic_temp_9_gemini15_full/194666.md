self-unworthinesses:/sɛlf ʌnˈwɜːðinəsɪz/|/ˈsɛlf ʌnˈwɜːrðɪnɪsiz/| n. |自我价值感缺失；自卑感（复数）|The plural form of "self-unworthiness," referring to multiple instances or aspects of feeling unworthy of oneself.  It encompasses feelings of inadequacy, low self-esteem, and a lack of self-worth.  This term often describes a complex of negative self-perceptions. |例句：Her self-unworthinesses stemmed from years of criticism and neglect.  她的自卑感源于多年的批评和忽视。


例句：The therapy aimed to address her various self-unworthinesses and help her build self-esteem.  治疗的目标是解决她的各种自卑感，并帮助她建立自信。


近义词：feelings of inadequacy, low self-esteem, lack of self-worth, inferiority complex, self-doubt, self-deprecation


反义词：self-acceptance, self-love, self-esteem, self-confidence, self-worth


词性:名词 (复数)
