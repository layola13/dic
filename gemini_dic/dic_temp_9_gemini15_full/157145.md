pine:/paɪn/| n. v.|松树；渴望；消瘦|A pine tree is a type of evergreen coniferous tree.  To pine for something is to yearn for it deeply.  To pine away is to gradually waste away, typically through illness or grief.|例句：The wind whistled through the pines. 风在松树间呼啸而过。
He pined for his lost love. 他渴望他的失恋。|近义词：yearn, long for, languish|反义词：thrive, flourish
