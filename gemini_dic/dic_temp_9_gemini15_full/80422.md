gona:/ˈɡɒnə/| /ˈɡɑːnə/ | adj. | 将要的，快要…的 | Going to,  a contraction of "going to," indicating future intention or prediction.  It expresses a plan, prediction, or something that is likely to happen. |  例句：I'm gonna go to the store. = 我要去商店。  | 例句：It's gonna rain. = 要下雨了。| 近义词：going to, about to | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am going to eat. = 我打算吃东西。
She is going to sing. = 她打算唱歌。


一般过去时:
I was going to leave. = 我本来打算离开。
They were going to help. = 他们本来打算帮忙。


现在完成时:
I have been going to do it. = 我一直打算做这件事。
She has been going to call. = 她一直打算打电话。


过去完成时:
I had been going to finish. = 我本来打算完成。
They had been going to visit. = 他们本来打算拜访。


将来时:
I will be going to travel. = 我将要去旅行。
He will be going to study. = 他将要去学习。


虚拟语气:
If I were going to go, I would tell you. = 如果我打算去，我会告诉你。
I wish I were going to see them. = 我希望我能够去看他们。


被动语态:
It was going to be finished. = 它本来要被完成。
The project was going to be canceled. = 这个项目本来要被取消。


疑问句:
Are you going to help me? = 你打算帮我吗？
Is she going to come? = 她打算来吗？


否定句:
I am not going to eat. = 我不打算吃东西。
They are not going to go. = 他们不打算去。


PHRASE->
gonna get = 将要得到
gonna be = 将会是
gonna do = 将要做
gonna see = 将要看到
gonna have = 将会有
