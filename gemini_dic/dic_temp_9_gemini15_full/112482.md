lounged:/laʊndʒd/| v.|懒洋洋地坐或躺；闲逛；|to relax in a comfortable position, often for an extended period|She lounged on the sofa, reading a book. 她懒洋洋地躺在沙发上看书。 He lounged around the house all afternoon. 他整个下午都在家里闲逛。|rested, relaxed, reclined|worked, hurried, rushed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lounges by the pool every afternoon. 他每天下午都在泳池边休息。
They lounge around the house all day on weekends. 他们周末整天在家里闲逛。

一般过去时:
I lounged on the beach all day yesterday. 我昨天一整天都在海滩上休息。
She lounged by the fireplace, sipping wine. 她靠着壁炉，一边品酒一边休息。

现在完成时:
He has lounged around all day and accomplished nothing. 他整天无所事事地闲逛，什么也没做成。
We have lounged on the patio for hours, enjoying the sun. 我们在露台上享受阳光，已经休息了好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
She had lounged in the sun for hours before she got sunburned.  她在晒太阳好几个小时后才晒伤。
They had lounged around all morning before starting their project. 他们在开始项目之前整个上午都在闲逛。

将来时:
I will lounge by the pool tomorrow. 我明天将在泳池边休息。
We will lounge on the beach all day next weekend. 我们下周末将在海滩上休息一整天。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would lounge on the beach all day. 如果我有更多时间，我会整天在海滩上休息。
I wish I could lounge by the pool right now. 我真希望现在能躺在泳池边休息。

被动语态:  (Lounged is rarely used in passive voice because it implies an active action.)  This section is omitted.


疑问句:

否定句:
