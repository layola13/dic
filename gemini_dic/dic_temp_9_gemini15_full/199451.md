simultaneous:/ˌsɪmlˈteɪniəs/| adj.|同时发生的，同时存在的|happening or existing at the same time.  It emphasizes the occurrence of two or more events without any noticeable interval between them.|例句：The two events were simultaneous. 这两件事同时发生。

例句：The explosions were simultaneous. 爆炸是同时发生的。


近义词：concurrent, coincident, concomitant|反义词：sequential, successive


USAGE->
