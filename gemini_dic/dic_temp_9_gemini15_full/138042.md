It encompasses aspects of social democracy, environmental consciousness, egalitarianism, and a strong welfare state.  The concept also acknowledges the diversity within the Nordic region and avoids generalizations. | The conference explored various Nordicities, highlighting both commonalities and differences among the five nations.  | 近义词:  Nordic characteristics, Scandinavian features | 反义词:  Southern European traits,  non-Nordic cultures | 名词
