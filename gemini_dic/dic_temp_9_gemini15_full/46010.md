cybernauts:/ˈsaɪbərnɔːts/| n. |网络用户，网民|A cybernaut is a person who spends a lot of time on the internet, especially exploring its various aspects.  They are active participants in the online world, engaging in various activities such as browsing, communicating, and creating content.|Example:  Many cybernauts use social media platforms to connect with friends and family. 许多网民使用社交媒体平台与朋友和家人联系。|近义词：netizen, internet user, online user|反义词：offline user
