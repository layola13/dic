multiplex:/ˈmʌltiplɛks/| n. adj. v.|多路复用；多联放映；多工；多路传送|A multiplex is a system or device that combines multiple signals into one for transmission or processing. It can refer to a cinema with multiple screens, a system for transmitting multiple signals over a single cable, or a computer system capable of handling multiple tasks simultaneously. |例句：The new multiplex cinema has twelve screens. 这个新的多厅影院有十二个屏幕。  The company uses multiplex technology to transmit data efficiently. 这家公司使用多路复用技术来高效传输数据。|近义词：multichannel, complex, compound|反义词：simplex
