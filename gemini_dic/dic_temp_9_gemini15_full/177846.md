receptary:/rɪˈsɛptəri/| n.|药房；调剂室；处方笺；药剂库|A receptary is a place where medicines are prepared or dispensed, or a place where prescriptions are kept.  It can also refer to a book containing recipes or formulas, especially for medicines.|例句：The old apothecary kept his receptary locked away.  老药剂师把他的药方本锁了起来。|近义词：pharmacy, dispensary, apothecary|反义词: N/A
