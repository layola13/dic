pausing:/ˈpɔːzɪŋ/|/ˈpɔːzɪŋ/| v.|暂停；停顿；迟疑 |The act of stopping temporarily; a brief interruption.  It can also refer to a moment of hesitation or delay. |例句：He paused before answering the question. 他在回答问题之前停顿了一下。

例句：The music paused unexpectedly. 音乐意外地停顿了。


近义词：stopping, halting, hesitating, delaying

反义词：continuing, proceeding, rushing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pauses before speaking. 他在说话前停顿一下。
She pauses frequently during her speech. 她在演讲中经常停顿。


一般过去时:
He paused to catch his breath. 他停下来喘口气。
She paused and looked around. 她停下来环顾四周。


现在完成时:
He has paused several times already. 他已经停顿了好几次了。
She has paused for thought. 她停下来思考了。


过去完成时:
He had paused before he realized his mistake. 他在意识到自己的错误之前已经停顿了。
She had paused to consider the consequences. 她停下来考虑后果了。


将来时:
He will pause to take a picture. 他将会停下来拍照。
She will pause for a moment of reflection. 她将会停顿片刻进行反思。


虚拟语气:
If he paused, he might reconsider. 如果他停下来，他可能会重新考虑。
I wish she had paused before speaking. 我希望她在说话前停顿一下。


被动语态:
The music was paused by the DJ. 音乐被DJ暂停了。
The performance was paused due to technical difficulties. 表演由于技术问题而暂停了。


疑问句:
Did he pause before answering? 他在回答之前停顿了吗？
Why did she pause in the middle of her sentence? 她为什么在句子中间停顿？


否定句:
He didn't pause for a moment. 他没有停顿片刻。
She didn't pause to think about it. 她没有停下来思考这件事。

词源:
From the Old French word *pauser*, meaning "to stop, rest," ultimately derived from the Latin word *pausare*, which means "to cause to stop,"  related to the Latin word *pausa*, meaning "a stop, pause."
