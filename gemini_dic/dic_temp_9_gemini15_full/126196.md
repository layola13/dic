/ɛmˈviː/ | n. | 音乐录影带 | Music video; a short film created to promote a song.  It often features the artist performing the song, alongside visual elements that complement the lyrics and theme. | Example: The band released a new MV for their latest single. 乐队为他们最新的单曲发布了一个新的MV。 | 近义词: music video, promotional video | 反义词: N/A
