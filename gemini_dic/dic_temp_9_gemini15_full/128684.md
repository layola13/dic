nictitate:/ˈnɪktɪteɪt/| v.|瞬目；眨眼；(鸟类) 第三眼睑闭合|To close or partially close the eyes quickly; to wink.  The term can also refer to the action of a bird's nictitating membrane. |例句：He nictitated in surprise. 他惊讶地眨了眨眼。


例句：The owl nictitated slowly, its third eyelid sweeping across its eye. 猫头鹰缓慢地瞬目，它的第三眼睑扫过它的眼睛。


近义词：blink, wink, flicker|反义词：stare, gape


词源:  From Latin *nictitāre, frequentative of *nictere "to wink," of unknown origin.  The connection to the nictitating membrane in birds and other animals is due to the resemblance of the action to a wink.
