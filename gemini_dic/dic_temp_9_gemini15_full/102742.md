jank:/dʒæŋk/| n. adj. v.|破烂货，劣质品；（俚语）差劲的，不流畅的；使运转不流畅|英文解析：Jank can refer to something of poor quality, malfunctioning, or generally undesirable. As an adjective, it describes something that is jerky, unstable, or unreliable. As a verb, it means to cause something to malfunction or run poorly.|例句：The old car was a real jank; it constantly broke down. 这辆旧车真是个破烂货；它经常抛锚。  The game ran jank on my older computer.  我的旧电脑上游戏运行得很卡。|近义词：crap, junk, rubbish, buggy, glitchy|反义词：high-quality, smooth, reliable, excellent
