parson:/ˈpɑːrsn/| n. |牧师，教区牧师 |A parson is a member of the clergy, especially a priest in the Church of England, who is in charge of a parish.  |The parson gave a heartfelt sermon on Sunday.  这位牧师在周日作了一场发自肺腑的布道。|clergyman, priest, minister |layperson, parishioner |名词
