scooter:/ˈskuːtər/ | n. |小型摩托车，滑板车 | A small motorized two-wheeled vehicle, or a child's toy with a standing platform and handlebars propelled by pushing one's feet off the ground.  | 例句：He rides his scooter to work every day. 他每天骑滑板车上班。 | 例句：The children were playing with their scooters in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩滑板车。 | 近义词：motorbike (for motorized scooters), skateboard (for non-motorized scooters) | 反义词：car, bicycle (depending on context) | 名词

词源:
The word "scooter" ultimately derives from the Middle English word "scoot," meaning "to move swiftly or suddenly," which is of uncertain origin.  The sense of a vehicle came later, reflecting the swift movement associated with the device.
