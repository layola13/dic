downsampled:/ˈdaʊnˌsæmpld/ | v. | 降采样，下采样 | To reduce the sampling rate of a digital signal, typically by reducing the number of samples. This is often done to reduce the size of a file or to improve processing speed.  | Example: The image was downsampled to reduce its file size.  图像被降采样以减小其文件大小。 |近义词:resampled, subsampled | 反义词:upsampled
