mesoarium:/ˌmɛzoʊˈɛəriəm/| n.| 系膜 | The mesoarium is a double fold of peritoneum that connects the ovaries to the broad ligament of the uterus in females.  It is analogous to the mesentery in the intestines. The mesoarium provides support and contains blood vessels and nerves supplying the ovaries. |Example: The surgeon carefully dissected the mesoarium to avoid damaging the ovarian vessels.  外科医生小心地解剖系膜，避免损伤卵巢血管。|近义词：mesentery (in a broader sense)|反义词：无|名词
