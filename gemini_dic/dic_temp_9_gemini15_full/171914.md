pupation:/pjuˈpeɪʃən/| n. |蛹期，化蛹|The process of transformation from a larva to a pupa, particularly in insects.  It refers to the stage of an insect's life cycle where it is enclosed in a pupa or chrysalis before emerging as an adult. |例句：The pupation of the monarch butterfly is a remarkable transformation.  帝王蝶的蛹期是一个显著的蜕变过程。

The caterpillar underwent pupation before emerging as a butterfly.  毛毛虫在化蛹后变成蝴蝶。
