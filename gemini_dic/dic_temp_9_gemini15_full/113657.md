machines:/məˈʃiːnz/ | n. |机器，机械 |A machine is a device that uses power to do work.  It typically consists of interconnected parts that work together to perform a specific function.  Examples include cars, computers, and washing machines. |例句：The factory uses many machines to produce cars.  这家工厂使用许多机器来生产汽车。|例句：Modern machines are incredibly complex. 现代机器极其复杂。|近义词：engines, mechanisms, apparatus |反义词：hand tools, manual labor
