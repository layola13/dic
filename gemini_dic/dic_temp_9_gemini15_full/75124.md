frisked:/frɪskt/ | vt. |搜身，摸索| To pass one's hands over (a person's body) to search for concealed weapons or contraband.  It often implies a quick and superficial search.|例句：The police officer frisked the suspect before placing him in the patrol car. 警官在把嫌疑犯送进巡逻车之前对他进行了搜身。


The security guard frisked my bag at the entrance. 安保人员在入口处搜查了我的包。


近义词：search, pat down| 反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police officer frisks suspects regularly. = 警官定期搜查嫌疑人。
He frisks his pockets for his keys. = 他摸索着口袋找钥匙。


一般过去时:
They frisked him before letting him go. = 他们在放他走之前搜了他的身。
She frisked her purse for her phone. = 她翻找她的钱包找手机。


现在完成时:
The officers have frisked everyone entering the building. = 警官们已经搜查了所有进入大楼的人。
I have frisked my pockets but I can't find my wallet. = 我已经摸遍了口袋，但找不到我的钱包。


过去完成时:
They had frisked all the passengers before the plane took off. = 飞机起飞前，他们已经搜查了所有乘客。
She had frisked her bag twice before realizing her passport was missing. = 她翻找了两次包才意识到护照丢了。


将来时:
The security personnel will frisk everyone entering the concert. = 安保人员将搜查所有进入音乐会的人。
I will frisk my bag before I leave the house. = 我离开家之前会搜查我的包。


虚拟语气:
If they frisked him thoroughly, they might have found the weapon. = 如果他们彻底搜查了他，他们可能会找到武器。
I wish they had frisked me more carefully; I lost my wallet. = 我真希望他们更仔细地搜查我；我的钱包丢了。


被动语态:
He was frisked by the police. = 他被警察搜了身。
The passengers were frisked before boarding the plane. = 乘客在登机前被搜查了。


疑问句:
Were you frisked at the airport? = 你在机场被搜身了吗？
Did they frisk all the participants? = 他们搜查了所有参与者吗？


否定句:
They did not frisk the suspect properly. = 他们没有正确地搜查嫌疑人。
The guard didn't frisk my bag. = 警卫没有搜查我的包。
