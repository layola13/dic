calorimotor:/ˌkæləriˈmoʊtər/ | n. |量热器，热量计 | A calorimotor is a device used to measure the heat generated or absorbed during a chemical or physical process.  It's often used in chemistry and physics experiments to determine the heat of reaction, specific heat, or heat capacity. | Example: The researchers used a highly sensitive calorimotor to measure the heat released during the catalytic reaction.  研究人员使用高度灵敏的量热器来测量催化反应过程中释放的热量。|近义词：calorimeter |反义词：N/A
