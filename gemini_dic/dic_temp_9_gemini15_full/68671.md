farawayness:/ˈfɑːrəweɪnəs/| n. |遥远感，疏远感 |The feeling of being distant or remote, either physically or emotionally.  It can refer to a sense of remoteness in time, space, or relationship. |例句：The farawayness of the mountains filled her with a sense of peace.  群山的遥远感带给她一种平静的感觉。


The farawayness in his eyes suggested he was lost in thought. 他眼神中的遥远感暗示着他陷入了沉思。|近义词：remoteness, distance, alienation, isolation |反义词：closeness, proximity, intimacy
