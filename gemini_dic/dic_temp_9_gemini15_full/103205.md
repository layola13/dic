jetfoils:/ˈdʒɛtˌfɔɪlz/| n. |水翼船|A jetfoil is a type of high-speed hydrofoil vessel that uses submerged foils to lift the hull out of the water at higher speeds, reducing drag and increasing efficiency.  This allows for significantly faster travel than conventional boats. |例句：The jetfoil sliced through the water, leaving a white wake in its path. 这艘水翼船破浪前进，留下白色的尾迹。

The jetfoil ride was smoother than I expected, even in choppy seas.  水翼船的乘坐比我预期的要平稳，即使在波涛汹涌的海面上也是如此。|近义词：hydrofoil, high-speed craft |反义词：slow boat, conventional boat
