flavonoid:/ˈfleɪvənɔɪd/| n. |类黄酮|Flavonoids are a group of naturally occurring polyphenolic compounds found in plants. They are known for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties and are associated with various health benefits.  They are widely distributed in fruits, vegetables, and other plant-based foods. |Example:  Many studies have shown that a diet rich in flavonoids is associated with a reduced risk of heart disease.|近义词：polyphenol|反义词：无
