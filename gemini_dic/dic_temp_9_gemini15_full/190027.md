sandglass:/ˈsændˌɡlæs/| n. |沙漏|A sandglass is a device used to measure time, consisting of two glass bulbs connected by a narrow neck, with sand flowing from the upper bulb to the lower bulb at a regulated rate.  It's a simple, visual timer.  |Example: The sandglass had almost run out, signifying the end of the game.|近义词：hourglass, timer|反义词：stopwatch (in terms of precision)
