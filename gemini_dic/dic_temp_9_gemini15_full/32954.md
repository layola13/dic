choragic:/kəˈreɪdʒɪk/| adj.| 赞助戏剧的；与戏剧赞助有关的 | Relating to a choregus, a wealthy Athenian citizen who financed the chorus in dramatic competitions.  The term often describes buildings or monuments associated with such sponsorship. | The choragic monument of Lysicrates is a well-preserved example of Athenian architecture.  |近义词：dramatic, theatrical |反义词：nondramatic, non-theatrical
