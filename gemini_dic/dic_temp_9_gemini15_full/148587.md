overtaxation:/ˌoʊvərˌtæksˈeɪʃən/| n. |过度征税；税负过重|The imposition of excessive taxes.  Overtaxation can stifle economic growth and lead to social unrest.|例句：The high taxes imposed on small businesses led to widespread overtaxation and business closures. 高额的企业税导致了普遍的过度征税和企业倒闭。
例句：Many citizens feel that the current level of taxation constitutes overtaxation. 许多公民认为目前的税收水平构成了过度征税。|近义词：excessive taxation, heavy taxation|反义词：undertaxation, tax relief|词性 n.
