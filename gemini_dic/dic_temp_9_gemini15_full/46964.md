darting:/ˈdɑːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 快速移动的；闪动的；敏捷的 |  Moving or glancing quickly; characterized by quick, sudden movements.  |例句：The child's darting eyes followed the butterfly.  孩子的眼睛快速地追随着蝴蝶。

例句：A darting pain shot through his leg. 一阵剧痛迅速地穿过他的腿。


近义词：flickering, flashing, quick, rapid, swift | 反义词：slow, sluggish, deliberate, steady


词源:  Derived from the noun "dart," meaning a small, pointed missile thrown or shot. The adjective "darting" describes something moving with the speed and suddenness of a thrown dart.  The word's origins trace back to Old English *daert, from Proto-Germanic *daurtiz.
