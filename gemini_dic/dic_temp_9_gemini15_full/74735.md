freestone:/ˈfriːstoʊn/| adj. | 易剥皮的（水果）；（石头）易于凿刻的 |  A freestone is a type of fruit, especially a peach or plum, whose flesh readily separates from the stone or pit.  It can also refer to a type of stone that is easy to cut or work with. |例句：The peaches were freestone, making them easy to eat.  这些桃子是易剥皮的，很容易吃。  The sculptor chose freestone for its ease of carving.  雕刻家选择易于雕刻的石头。|近义词：easily separable, easily worked |反义词：clingstone (for fruit)  difficult to work (for stone)
