freaking:/ˈfriːkɪŋ/| adj. adv.|该死的，非常，极其|英文解析:A mild swear word used to emphasize a feeling or situation.  It's a milder alternative to "fucking."  It can function as an adjective or an adverb.|例句：That movie was freaking awesome! = 那部电影真是太棒了！

That test was freaking hard. = 那次考试太难了。|近义词：damn, incredibly, awfully, extremely|反义词： mildly, slightly
