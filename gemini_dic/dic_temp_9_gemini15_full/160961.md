post-treatment: /ˌpoʊstˈtriːtmənt/ | n. | 后续治疗，术后治疗 | Treatment given after the primary treatment has been completed.  It often aims to manage side effects, prevent recurrence, or improve the long-term outcome. | Example: The post-treatment rehabilitation program was crucial for his recovery.  后续康复计划对他康复至关重要。 |近义词：follow-up treatment, aftercare, subsequent treatment | 反义词：pre-treatment, pretreatment
