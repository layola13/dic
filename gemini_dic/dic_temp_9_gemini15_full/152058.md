patentees:/ˌpætənˈtiːz/| n. |专利权人，专利获得者|The people who have been granted a patent.  Those who hold a patent. |例句：The patentees of the new software are seeking to enforce their intellectual property rights. 新软件的专利权人正在寻求执行他们的知识产权。


例句：The company is in dispute with the patentees over the use of their invention.  这家公司与专利权人就其发明的使用权存在争议。


近义词：patent holders, inventors (if they also hold the patent)


反义词：patent infringers


词性: 名词
