chutney:/ˈtʃʌtni/| n. |酸辣酱，奇特尼酱 |A chutney is a type of condiment, typically sweet, sour, and spicy, made from fruits, vegetables, or spices.  It is often used as an accompaniment to Indian food. |例句：The mango chutney was a delicious addition to the curry.  芒果酸辣酱为咖喱增添了美味。

例句：We served the chicken tikka masala with a selection of chutneys. 我们用各种酸辣酱搭配鸡肉丁卡萨。

近义词：relish, sauce, condiment
反义词：无


词源:  The word "chutney" originates from the Hindi word "chatni," which itself derives from the Sanskrit word "chatana," meaning "to lick." This reflects the traditional way people would eat the sauce—by licking it.
