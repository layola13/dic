inflammation:/ˌɪnfləˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 炎症 | The body's response to injury or infection, characterized by redness, swelling, heat, and pain.  It is a complex process involving the immune system. | Example: The inflammation in his knee joint made it difficult to walk.  他的膝盖关节炎症使行走困难。The doctor prescribed medication to reduce the inflammation. 医生开了药来消炎。|近义词： swelling, redness, irritation | 反义词：healing, resolution


USAGE->

Inflammation is a natural response to injury. = 炎症是对损伤的自然反应。
The wound shows signs of inflammation. = 伤口显示出炎症迹象。


一般过去时:
