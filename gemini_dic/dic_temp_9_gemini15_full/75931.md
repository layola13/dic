fungicide:/ˈfʌŋɡɪsaɪd/| n. |杀菌剂|A chemical substance used to kill fungi.  Fungicides are used in agriculture to control fungal diseases in crops and also in the home to prevent mold and mildew. |Example:  The farmer sprayed a fungicide on his crops to prevent blight.  农民在农作物上喷洒了杀菌剂以防止枯萎病。


The gardener applied a fungicide to combat the powdery mildew on her roses. 园丁使用了杀菌剂来对抗玫瑰上的白粉病。|近义词：antifungal, mycocide|反义词：fungistat (fungistatic agent)
