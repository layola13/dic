metopomancy:/ˌmɛtəˈpɔːmənsi/| n. |观天象占卜术|Divination by observing the weather, atmospheric phenomena, or meteorological conditions.  It is an ancient form of divination relying on interpreting signs in the sky, clouds, wind, and other atmospheric conditions to predict the future or gain insight.  The practice is rooted in the belief that the sky reflects the earthly realm and its events.|The ancient Greeks practiced metopomancy, interpreting cloud formations to predict the outcome of battles.|近义词：meteorology (in a divinatory sense), augury, divination|反义词：N/A
