cataract:/ˈkætərækt/| n.|白内障；瀑布；大洪水|A cataract is a medical condition in which the lens of the eye becomes cloudy, resulting in impaired vision.  It can also refer to a large waterfall or a sudden, overwhelming flood.  |例句：He had to undergo surgery to remove a cataract from his left eye. 他不得不接受手术以切除左眼的白内障。
The Niagara Falls is a spectacular cataract.尼亚加拉瀑布是一个壮观的瀑布。|近义词：waterfall (for the water feature), clouding (for the eye condition)|反义词：clear vision (for the eye condition), trickle (for the water feature)
