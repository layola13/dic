relicensures:/ˌriːˈlaɪsənʃərz/| n. |再许可；重新许可|The process of granting a new license for something that was previously licensed.  This often involves transferring ownership or rights.| Example: The software company is considering relicensures of its older products to a new open-source license.|近义词：re-licensing, renewed licensing|反义词：license revocation, termination of license|



USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company relicensures its software annually. = 公司每年重新许可其软件。
He relicensures the music for each new project. = 他为每个新项目重新许可音乐。

一般过去时:
They relicensured the property last year. = 他们去年重新许可了该房产。
The artist relicensured his artwork for a wider distribution. = 该艺术家重新许可了他的艺术作品以进行更广泛的分发。


现在完成时:
The firm has relicensured several products this year. = 公司今年已经重新许可了几种产品。
She has relicensured the song for use in a film. = 她已经重新许可这首歌用于电影中。


过去完成时:
They had relicensured the patent before the lawsuit began. = 在诉讼开始之前，他们已经重新许可了专利。
He had relicensured the software before the merger. = 在合并之前，他已经重新许可了软件。


将来时:

The organization will relicensure its trademark soon. = 该组织很快将重新许可其商标。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
