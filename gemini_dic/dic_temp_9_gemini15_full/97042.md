infeeble: /ɪnˈfiːbəl/ | v. |使衰弱；使虚弱 | To weaken or enfeeble; to make feeble.  |The illness infeebled him considerably.  疾病使他相当虚弱。 The long journey infeebled the travelers. 漫长的旅程使旅行者们精疲力尽。| weaken, debilitate, enervate | strengthen, invigorate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The illness infeeble him. = 疾病使他虚弱。
Hard work infeeble her body and mind. = 辛勤的工作使她身心疲惫。

一般过去时:
The heat infeebled the soldiers. = 炎热的天气使士兵们虚弱。
The long march infeebled the army. = 漫长的行军使军队疲惫不堪。

现在完成时:
The drought has infeebled the crops. = 干旱已经使庄稼枯萎。
Years of illness have infeebled him. = 多年的疾病已经使他虚弱。

过去完成时:
The long siege had infeebled the city's defenses. = 长期的围困已经削弱了城市的防御能力。
Several days without food had infeebled the survivors. = 好几天没吃东西已经使幸存者们虚弱不堪。

将来时:
Lack of sleep will infeeble his performance. = 睡眠不足将会影响他的表现。
The continuing stress will infeeble her immune system. = 持续的压力会削弱她的免疫系统。

虚拟语气:
If the treatment had been delayed, it would have infeebled him further. = 如果治疗推迟了，它会使他更加虚弱。
If she continued to neglect her health, it would infeeble her. = 如果她继续忽视她的健康，它会使她虚弱。

被动语态:
His body was infeebled by the disease. = 他的身体被疾病削弱了。
The army was infeebled by disease and famine. = 军队因疾病和饥荒而元气大伤。

疑问句:
Will this medication infeeble him further? = 这种药物会使他更加虚弱吗？
Does prolonged exposure to the sun infeeble the skin? = 长时间暴露在阳光下会使皮肤变弱吗？

否定句:
The medication did not infeeble him. = 这种药物并没有使他虚弱。
The exercise regimen did not infeeble him; it strengthened him. = 锻炼计划并没有使他虚弱；反而使他强壮了。

词源:
The word "infeeble" is derived from the Old French word "enfeeblir," which itself comes from the Old French "feble" meaning "weak." The prefix "in-" intensifies the meaning, similar to the prefix "en-".
