exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ | vt. |驱魔，去除(坏事)| to drive out or remove (an evil spirit) from a person or place; to get rid of something unpleasant or unwanted |The priest exorcised the demon from the possessed child.  牧师将附在孩子身上的恶魔驱逐了出去。|近义词：expel, banish, eliminate |反义词：invoke, summon


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He exorcises evil spirits from haunted houses. 他驱逐闹鬼房子里的恶灵。
The priest exorcises demons regularly.  牧师定期驱魔。


一般过去时:
The shaman exorcised the illness from the village.  萨满驱除了村庄的疾病。
They exorcised the bad memories from their minds. 他们驱除了脑海中的坏记忆。


现在完成时:
