distinguishment: /dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃmənt/ | n. | 卓越；杰出；区别；辨别 | The act of distinguishing; the state of being distinguished; a mark of distinction.  It refers to the act of recognizing or noting differences between things, or to something that sets someone or something apart as exceptional. | 例句：Her work shows great distinction and originality.  她的作品展现了极大的独特性和原创性。

Her distinction in the field of medicine is well-deserved.  她在医学领域的成就当之无愧。


近义词：difference, distinction, discrimination, identification, recognition, separation, differentiation

反义词：similarity, resemblance, likeness, indistinguishability


词性：名词
