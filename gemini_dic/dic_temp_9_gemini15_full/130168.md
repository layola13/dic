non-revealing: /ˌnɒn rɪˈviːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不泄露的；不揭示的 | Not revealing or disclosing something, especially secrets or information.  | The study used non-revealing pseudonyms to protect the identities of participants. 这项研究使用了不泄露身份的假名来保护参与者的身份。|近义词：concealing, undisclosed, hidden, obscure |反义词：revealing, explicit, transparent
