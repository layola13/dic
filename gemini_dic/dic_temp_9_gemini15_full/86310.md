It describes something done or expressed using the Hebrew language or in a style reminiscent of Hebrew culture.| The poem was written hebraically, using ancient Hebrew poetic forms.|近义词：in Hebrew|反义词：in a non-Hebrew manner


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense conjugation examples are not applicable.  Its usage is primarily descriptive.

Example 1:

Example 2:
He spoke hebraically to the rabbi. = 他用希伯来语和拉比说话。
