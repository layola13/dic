groing:/ˈɡroʊɪŋ/|/ˈɡrəʊɪŋ/| adj.| 生长的；增长的；越来越…的 | Growing pains are a common experience for teenagers.  青少年成长的烦恼是常见的经历。|The company is experiencing a period of rapid growing. 公司正经历着快速增长的时期。| growing, developing, increasing | shrinking, decreasing, declining | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The plant is growing well. = 这棵植物长得很好。
The economy is growing steadily. = 经济稳步增长。


一般过去时:
The child was growing rapidly. = 孩子长得很快。
The population was growing at an alarming rate. = 人口以惊人的速度增长。


现在完成时:
The company has been growing since its inception. = 自公司成立以来，它一直在发展壮大。
The tree has grown taller. = 这棵树长高了。


过去完成时:
The business had been growing for years before it went public. = 这家公司上市前已经发展壮大多年了。
The plant had grown significantly since last spring. = 自去年春天以来，这棵植物已经长得很大了。


将来时:
The city will be growing in the coming years. = 未来几年，这座城市将会发展壮大。
The population is expected to grow rapidly. = 预计人口将迅速增长。


虚拟语气:
If the economy were growing, we would see more jobs. = 如果经济增长，我们将看到更多就业机会。
I wish my garden were growing faster. = 我希望我的花园长得更快些。


被动语态:
The problem is being grown more serious. = 问题变得越来越严重。
The food is being grown organically. = 这些食物是有机种植的。


疑问句:
Is your business growing? = 你的生意在发展吗？
Are the flowers growing well? = 花长得好吗？


否定句:
The plant isn't growing very well. = 这棵植物长得不太好。
The economy isn't growing as quickly as expected. = 经济增长速度不如预期。


PHRASE->

growing number: 不断增长的数量
growing season: 生长期
growing evidence: 越来越多的证据
