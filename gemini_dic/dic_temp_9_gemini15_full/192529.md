scrutinous:/ˈskruːtɪnəs/| adj.|细致的；彻底的；严密的|Characterized by close examination and careful scrutiny; painstakingly thorough.|The police conducted a scrutinous investigation into the crime.  警方对这起犯罪进行了细致的调查。|The professor gave a scrutinous review of my paper. 教授对我的论文进行了严密的审查。|thorough, meticulous, painstaking|superficial, cursory, perfunctory


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It describes a noun.
