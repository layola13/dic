cesareans:/sɪˈzəriən/| n.|剖腹产|The surgical delivery of a baby through an incision in the mother's abdomen and uterus.|例句：She had a cesarean section because of complications during labor. 她因为分娩并发症而进行了剖腹产。|近义词：C-section, abdominal delivery|反义词：vaginal delivery
