infects:/ɪnˈfɛkts/ | vt. | 传染；感染；侵害 | To contaminate or affect (a person, animal, or plant) with a disease or infectious agent.  | 例句：The virus infects millions of people each year.  病毒每年感染数百万人。| 例句：The bacteria infected the wound, causing a serious infection.  细菌感染了伤口，导致严重的感染。| 近义词：contaminates, contaminates, affects | 反义词：cures, heals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The virus infects many people. = 病毒感染许多人。
This bacteria infects the lungs. =这种细菌感染肺部。


一般过去时:
The flu infected her last week. = 她的流感感染了她上周。
The disease infected the entire village. = 这种疾病感染了整个村庄。


现在完成时:
The disease has infected thousands of people. = 这种疾病已经感染了成千上万的人。
The fungus has infected the entire crop. = 真菌已经感染了整片庄稼。


过去完成时:
The infection had already infected him before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，感染已经感染了他。
They had infected the water supply before anyone noticed. = 他们在任何人注意到之前已经污染了供水。


将来时:

Experts predict the parasite will infect a wider area. = 专家预测这种寄生虫将感染更广的区域。

被动语态:

疑问句:

PHRASE->

词源:
From Old French *infecter, from Latin *infectare, frequentative of Latin *inficere "to stain, defile, taint," from in- "in, into" + ficere "to make, do," related to facere "to make, do."  The sense shifted from “to taint” to “to infect.”
