一般现在时:
The land is denuded of its vegetation. = 这片土地上的植被已被剥夺。
The mountain is denuded by years of erosion. = 这座山由于多年的侵蚀而变得荒芜。

一般过去时:
The forest was denuded by the logging company. = 这片森林被伐木公司砍伐殆尽。
The storm denuded the trees of their leaves. = 暴风雨剥光了树叶。

现在完成时:
The hillside has been denuded by overgrazing. = 山坡因过度放牧而变得荒芜。
The area has been denuded of its natural resources. = 该地区已被剥夺了其自然资源。

过去完成时:
The land had been denuded before the conservation efforts began. = 在保护工作开始之前，这片土地就已经荒芜了。
The river had been denuded of its fish by pollution. = 河流由于污染而鱼类绝迹。

将来时:
The deforestation will denude the mountains of their trees. = 滥砍滥伐将使山上的树木消失殆尽。
Climate change will denude many coastal areas. = 气候变化将使许多沿海地区变得荒芜。

虚拟语气:
If the forest were denuded, the animals would lose their habitat. = 如果森林被破坏，动物就会失去栖息地。
I wish the landscape weren't so denuded. = 我希望这片土地不要如此荒芜。

被动语态:
The forest was denuded by a severe fire. = 森林被一场大火烧毁殆尽。
The land has been denuded by unsustainable farming practices. = 这片土地因不可持续的农业耕作方式而变得荒芜。

疑问句:
Is the forest denuded? = 森林是否已被破坏？
Has the area been denuded by mining? = 该地区是否因采矿而变得荒芜？

否定句:
The forest is not denuded yet. = 森林还没有被破坏。
The area hasn't been completely denuded. = 该地区尚未完全荒芜。
