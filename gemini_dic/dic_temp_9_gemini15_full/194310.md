self-peace:/sɛlf piːs/| n.| 自我平和，内心的平静 |a state of inner calmness and tranquility achieved through self-reflection and acceptance.  It emphasizes finding peace within oneself, independent of external factors. |例句：After years of struggle, she finally found her self-peace.  多年挣扎后，她终于找到了内心的平静。|例句：Practicing mindfulness can help cultivate self-peace.  练习正念有助于培养内心的平静。|近义词：inner peace, serenity, tranquility, composure |反义词：anxiety, stress, turmoil, unrest |名词

词源:

The term "self-peace" is a modern compound word.  "Self" refers to oneself, one's own being or identity. "Peace" denotes a state of tranquility, quietness, and freedom from disturbance. The combination signifies a state of inner harmony and calmness derived from within oneself.  There isn't a specific historical etymology as it's a relatively recent term.
