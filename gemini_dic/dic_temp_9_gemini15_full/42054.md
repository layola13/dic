cosying: /ˈkɒziɪŋ/ | v. | 变得舒适；使舒适；依偎 | The act of making or becoming cozy.  It often implies a feeling of warmth, comfort, and intimacy. | 例句：They were cosying up by the fire. = 他们依偎在火炉旁取暖。  She cosying herself up in a warm blanket. = 她用温暖的毯子裹住自己。| 近义词： snuggling, cuddling, nestling | 反义词： shivering, freezing

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are cosying up on the sofa. = 他们在沙发上依偎着。
We cosy up with a good book and hot chocolate. = 我们抱着好书和热巧克力取暖。


一般过去时:
She cosied up to the fireplace. = 她依偎在壁炉旁。
They cosied down for the night. = 他们舒适地躺下睡觉了。


现在完成时:
We have cosied up in front of the fire all evening. = 我们整个晚上都依偎在火炉前。
She has cosied herself up in bed with a book. = 她舒服地躺在床上看书。


过去完成时:
They had cosied down before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经舒适地躺下了。
We had cosied up by the fire before the power went out. = 电力中断之前，我们已经依偎在火炉旁了。


将来时:
We will cosy up by the fire tonight. = 我们今晚将在壁炉旁取暖。
They will cosy down after a long day's work. = 他们将在辛苦一天的工作后舒适地休息。


虚拟语气:
If it were colder, we would cosy up by the fire. = 如果天气更冷，我们会依偎在火炉旁。
I wish I could cosy up with a good book right now. = 我希望我现在能抱着好书舒适地休息。


被动语态:
The room was cosied up with soft lighting and comfortable furniture. = 房间里柔和的灯光和舒适的家具营造了温馨舒适的氛围。(被动语态较少用在此词)
The blankets were cosied around the child. = 毛毯裹住了孩子。(被动语态较少用在此词)


疑问句:
Are they cosying up by the fire? = 他们正在壁炉旁依偎吗？
Will you cosy up with me tonight? = 你今晚想和我依偎在一起吗？


否定句:
They didn't cosy up to each other. = 他们没有互相依偎。
We couldn't cosy up because it was too hot. = 我们无法依偎，因为太热了。
