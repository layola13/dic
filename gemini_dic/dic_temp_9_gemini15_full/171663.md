pulsion:/ˈpʌlʒən/| n. |驱动力；冲动；本能 |A driving force; an impulse; an instinct.  It often refers to a powerful urge or drive, often subconscious. |例句：The pulsion to create was strong within her. 她内心有着强烈的创作冲动。

例句：His aggressive behavior was driven by a deep-seated pulsion for dominance. 他的侵略性行为源于一种根深蒂固的支配欲。


近义词：drive, impulse, urge, instinct, compulsion

反义词：inhibition, restraint, control

词性：名词


词源：From Latin *pulsus, past participle of pellere "to push, drive".  The word reflects the sense of being "driven" or "pushed" by an internal force.
