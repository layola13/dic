eed:/iːd/| n.|需要，需求|The need for change is urgent.  改变的需求迫在眉睫。|近义词：requirement, demand, necessity |反义词：sufficiency, abundance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He needs a new car. = 他需要一辆新车。
They need more time to finish the project. = 他们需要更多时间来完成项目。


一般过去时:
She needed help yesterday. = 她昨天需要帮助。
We needed to leave early. = 我们需要早点离开。


现在完成时:
I have needed a vacation for months. = 我已经需要休假几个月了。
They have needed assistance since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，他们就需要帮助。


过去完成时:
He had needed the money before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前就需要这笔钱。
She had needed a doctor's appointment for weeks. = 她已经需要预约医生好几个星期了。


将来时:
I will need your help tomorrow. = 我明天需要你的帮助。
They will need more information before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前需要更多信息。


虚拟语气:
If I needed help, I would ask you. = 如果我需要帮助，我会求助于你。
She wished she had needed more time to study. = 她希望她有更多时间学习。


被动语态:
The project needs to be finished by Friday. = 项目需要在周五之前完成。
Help is needed immediately. = 需要立即提供帮助。


疑问句:
Do you need anything? = 你需要什么吗？
What does he need? = 他需要什么？


否定句:
I don't need your help. = 我不需要你的帮助。
They didn't need to rush. = 他们不需要匆忙。


PHRASE->
in need of = 需要...
the need for = 对...的需求
meet the needs = 满足需求
have a need to = 需要做...
feel the need = 感到有必要
