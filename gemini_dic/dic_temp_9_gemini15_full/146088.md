overeducation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |过度教育 |The phenomenon of individuals possessing educational qualifications exceeding the requirements of their current job or career path.  It often refers to situations where someone with a postgraduate degree works in a job that could be adequately filled by someone with a bachelor's degree or less.  This can lead to underemployment, dissatisfaction, and a sense of wasted potential. | Example:  Many graduates experience overeducation, finding themselves underemployed in jobs that don't utilize their advanced skills.  许多毕业生都经历了过度教育，发现自己被低估了，在工作中没有利用到他们的高级技能。|近义词：overqualification,credential inflation |反义词：undereducation,lack of education|名词
