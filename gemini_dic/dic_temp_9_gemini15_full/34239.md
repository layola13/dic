clans:/klænz/| n.|氏族；家族；宗族|A clan is a group of families or individuals who are related to each other, often sharing a common ancestor and a strong sense of identity and loyalty.  They often have shared traditions, customs, and a hierarchical structure.|The Scottish Highlands were home to many powerful clans. 苏格兰高地是许多强大氏族的家园。|The Johnson clan gathered for their annual reunion. 约翰逊家族聚集在一起参加他们的年度聚会。|tribe, family, kin|nation, individual|名词
