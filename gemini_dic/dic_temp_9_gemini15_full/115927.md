marquessates:/ˈmɑːkwɪseɪts/| n. |侯爵领地，侯爵爵位|The plural form of marquessate, referring to the lands or territories ruled by a marquess.  It can also refer to the rank or dignity of a marquess.|The vast marquessates of the old empire were gradually broken up into smaller states.|近义词：lordship, principality|反义词:  None directly opposite, but could consider terms implying lesser rank like barony.
