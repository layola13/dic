misdirects:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪrekt/| vt.|误导，使误入歧途|To guide or lead someone or something wrongly; to cause someone to have a false understanding or belief.|The misleading advertisement misdirected many consumers.  这个误导性的广告误导了许多消费者。|The faulty map misdirected us for miles. 这张错误的地图让我们走了好几英里的冤枉路。|guide, mislead, deceive|direct, clarify, enlighten|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misdirects his anger at innocent people. 他把怒气错发泄到无辜的人身上。
The company misdirects its resources on unprofitable projects. 这家公司将资源错误地投入到无利可图的项目上。


一般过去时:
The faulty instructions misdirected the workers. 错误的指示误导了工人。
She misdirected her efforts, leading to failure. 她努力的方向错了，导致失败。


现在完成时:

His actions have misdirected the investigation. 他的行为已经误导了调查。
