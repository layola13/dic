phytology:/faɪˈtɒlədʒi/| n. |植物学|The scientific study of plants, including their physiology, structure, genetics, ecology, distribution, classification, and economic importance.  Phytology encompasses a broad range of sub-disciplines, from the study of individual cells to the analysis of entire ecosystems. |The phytology course covered a wide range of topics, from plant cell structure to the impact of climate change on plant distribution. 植物学课程涵盖了广泛的主题，从植物细胞结构到气候变化对植物分布的影响。|botany, plant science|zoology| n.
