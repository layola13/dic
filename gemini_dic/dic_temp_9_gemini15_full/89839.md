hornito:/ɔːrˈniːtoʊ/| n. |小火山锥，寄生火山锥 |A small, parasitic volcanic cone formed by lava that erupts from the side of a larger volcano.  Hornitos are typically smaller and less explosive than the main volcano. They often have a relatively simple, symmetrical shape. |Example:  The flanks of the volcano were dotted with numerous hornitos.  这座火山侧面布满了许多小火山锥。


The numerous hornitos on the volcano's slopes indicated recent volcanic activity.  火山斜坡上众多的小火山锥表明最近有火山活动。

|近义词：parasite cone, cinder cone (depending on composition)|反义词：main volcano, stratovolcano|
