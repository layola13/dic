semipatriots:/ˈsemiˌpeɪtriəts/| n.|半爱国者，伪爱国者|A person who shows only lukewarm or conditional patriotism; someone who claims patriotism but whose actions or beliefs contradict this claim.  They may support their country in some ways but oppose certain policies or actions. |例句：Some argue that those who criticize their government's foreign policy are not necessarily semipatriots, but rather engaged citizens.|近义词：quasi-patriot, conditional patriot, lukewarm patriot|反义词：patriot, nationalist
