pedagogies:/ˌpɛdəˈɡɑːdʒiz/ | n. | 教育学；教学法；教学方法 | The plural form of pedagogy, referring to the theories and practices of teaching.  It encompasses various approaches, methods, and philosophies related to education. | Example:  The conference explored diverse pedagogies in early childhood education.  这次会议探讨了幼儿教育中不同的教学法。 |近义词：teaching methods, educational approaches, instructional strategies |反义词： (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with "ineffective teaching methods")
