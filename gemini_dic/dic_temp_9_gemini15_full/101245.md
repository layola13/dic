invocate: /ˈɪnvəkeɪt/ | vt. | 恳求；祈求；援引；调用 | To invoke something is to call upon it for assistance, support, or as an example.  It can refer to calling upon a deity, a law, a principle, or a process. |

例句：

The shaman invoked the spirits to heal the sick.  = 萨满祭司祈求神灵治愈病人。

The lawyer invoked the principle of self-defense in his closing argument. = 律师在结案陈词中援引了自卫原则。


近义词：appeal to, call upon, cite, summon, plead for

反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They invoke the gods for help. = 他们祈求神灵帮助。
She invokes her rights under the constitution. = 她援引宪法赋予她的权利。


一般过去时:
He invoked his right to remain silent. = 他行使了保持沉默的权利。
The judge invoked the relevant statute. = 法官援引了相关的法令。


现在完成时:
She has invoked the help of her family. = 她已经求助于她的家人。
They have invoked the emergency powers. = 他们已经动用了紧急权力。


过去完成时:
He had invoked the name of God before he died. = 他死前曾呼唤过上帝的名。
The lawyer had invoked previous case law to support his argument. = 律师曾援引先前的案例法来支持他的论点。


将来时:
They will invoke the contract to settle the dispute. = 他们将援引合同来解决纠纷。
She will invoke the power of veto. = 她将行使否决权。


虚拟语气:
If I needed help, I would invoke the assistance of my friends. = 如果我需要帮助，我会请求朋友们的帮助。
I wish I could invoke a miracle to solve this problem. = 我希望我能创造奇迹来解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The law was invoked to justify the decision. = 该法律被援引来证明该决定的合理性。
The emergency powers were invoked to restore order. = 紧急权力被启用以恢复秩序。


疑问句:
What clause did they invoke in the agreement? = 他们在协议中援引了哪一条款？
What precedents will the court invoke? = 法院将援引哪些先例？


否定句:
He did not invoke the right of appeal. = 他没有行使上诉权。
The defendant did not invoke any relevant precedents. = 被告没有援引任何相关的先例。


PHRASE->

invoke a curse = 降下诅咒
invoke the name of = 祈求…的名义
invoke a law = 援引法律
invoke a process = 调用一个进程
invoke a function = 调用一个函数

词源:

From Old French *invoquer, from Latin invocare "to call upon," from in- "upon" + vocare "to call."
