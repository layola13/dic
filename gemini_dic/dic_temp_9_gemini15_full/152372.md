pavage:/pəˈvɑːʒ/ | n. |铺路，路面；铺砌物 | Pavement; a paved surface.  The process of laying paving stones or other materials to create a paved surface. |例句：The new paving on the driveway looks great. 新铺的车道路面看起来很棒。

例句：The road's poor paving led to several accidents.  这条路的路面质量差导致了几起事故。


近义词：pavement, surfacing, paving stones


反义词：unpaved, rough ground


词源：源自古法语 *paver,  意思是“铺设”，最终源自拉丁语 *paviare,  意思是“用石头铺设”。
