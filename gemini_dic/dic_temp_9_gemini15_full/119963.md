millennialism:/mɪˈleniəlɪzəm/| n. |千禧年说；千年论；期盼千年王国|The belief that a divinely ordained millennium, or thousand-year reign of Christ, is imminent or will occur at a specific time.  This belief often involves apocalyptic or utopian expectations.|例句：Some interpretations of millennialism predict a catastrophic event preceding the millennium.|近义词：millenarianism, chiliasm|反义词：secularism
