modulates:/ˈmɒdjʊleɪts/ /ˈmɑːdjʊleɪts/ | vt. | 调制；调节；缓和；改变音调 | To modulate something means to adjust or regulate it, often to make it smoother or more gradual.  It can also refer to varying the pitch or tone of one's voice. |例句：The singer modulated her voice to a softer tone. 歌手把声音调低了。

The musician skillfully modulated the intensity of the music. 音乐家熟练地调节了音乐的强度。


近义词：adjust, regulate, temper, vary | 反义词：intensify (in some contexts)
