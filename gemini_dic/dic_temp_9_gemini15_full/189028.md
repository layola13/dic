sabbaticals:/sæˈbætɪkəl/| n. |学术休假；安息年 | A sabbatical is a period of leave granted to a university teacher or other worker, usually to pursue research or other professional development.  |  Many professors take a sabbatical every seven years to conduct research. 许多教授每七年休一次学术假进行研究。
She's on sabbatical this semester.  她这个学期正在休学术假。|  leave of absence, time off | work, employment |
