drywall:/ˈdraɪwɔːl/| n. |石膏板，纸面石膏板 |A building material consisting of a gypsum core encased in thick paper, used for interior walls and ceilings.  |例句：The drywall in the new addition needs to be painted. 新建部分的石膏板需要粉刷。|例句：We used drywall instead of plaster for the walls. 我们用石膏板代替灰泥做墙。|近义词：plasterboard, gypsum board |反义词：brick, concrete
