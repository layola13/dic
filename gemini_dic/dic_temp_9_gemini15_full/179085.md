redepreciates:/ˌriːdɪˈpriːʃieɪts/| v.|重新贬值；再次贬值 |To decrease in value again after a previous increase or period of stability.  The verb implies a prior appreciation followed by a subsequent devaluation.|例句：After a brief period of appreciation, the currency redepreciated sharply.  货币在短暂升值后迅速贬值。 The company's stock redepreciated after the initial surge. 公司股票在最初飙升后再次贬值。|近义词：depreciate, devalue, decline|反义词：reappreciate, appreciate, increase
