nonathlete: /ˌnɑːnˈæθliːt/ | n. | 非运动员 | A person who does not participate in competitive sports or athletics.  Someone who is not an athlete. | Example:  He's a nonathlete, preferring books to basketball. 他不是运动员，更喜欢看书而不是篮球。  |近义词: non-sportsperson, amateur (in the sense of not professional), layman  | 反义词: athlete, sportsman, sportswoman |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a nonathlete. = 他不是运动员。
She isn't a nonathlete; she plays tennis professionally. = 她不是非运动员；她职业打网球。

一般过去时:
He was a nonathlete in high school. = 他高中时不是运动员。
They were nonathletes before joining the running club. = 他们在加入跑步俱乐部之前都不是运动员。

现在完成时:
He has remained a nonathlete throughout his life. = 他一生都不是运动员。
She has always been a nonathlete, preferring academics to sports. = 她一直都不是运动员，更喜欢学术而不是体育。

过去完成时:

将来时:
He will likely remain a nonathlete. = 他很可能仍然不是运动员。
She will probably continue to be a nonathlete, focusing on her studies. = 她可能会继续做个非运动员，专注于学业。

虚拟语气:
If he were a nonathlete, he wouldn't understand the pressure of competition. = 如果他不是运动员，他就不会理解竞争的压力。
I wish I were a nonathlete so I could have more free time. = 我希望我不是运动员，这样我就可以有更多空闲时间。

被动语态:
