contextualises:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪz/ | v. |语境化；使具有语境；解释语境 | To place (something) within its context; to interpret or explain something in terms of its context.  |  The essay contextualises the poem within the social and political climate of its time. 这篇文章将这首诗置于其当时的社会和政治环境中进行解读。|近义词：situate, frame, interpret | 反义词：decontextualize
