homies:/ˈhoʊmiz/| n.|哥们儿，朋友|A close friend or companion, especially a male one. The term often implies a strong bond and shared experiences.|例句：He and his homies were always getting into trouble.他和他的哥们总是惹麻烦。|例句：My homies and I are going to the game tonight.我和我的哥们今晚要去看比赛。|近义词：buddies, pals, friends, mates|反义词：enemies, rivals
