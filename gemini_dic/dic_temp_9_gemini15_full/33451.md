churns:/tʃɜːrnz/| v.|搅拌；翻腾；剧烈震动；焦虑不安|To stir or shake something vigorously; to turn or rotate rapidly; to be in a state of agitation or turmoil.|例句：The washing machine churns the clothes. 洗衣机搅动衣服。 The sea churns violently during a storm.暴风雨期间大海剧烈翻腾。|近义词：agitate, stir, swirl, toss|反义词：still, calm, peaceful

USAGE->

The farmer churns the butter. = 农夫在搅拌黄油。
The river churns with the strong current. = 河水因急流而翻腾。

一般过去时:
She churned the milk into butter. = 她把牛奶搅拌成黄油。
