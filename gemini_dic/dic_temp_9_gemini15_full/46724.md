dandelions:/ˈdændɪlaɪən/| n. |蒲公英|A dandelion is a common wildflower with bright yellow flowers that turn into fluffy white seed heads.  They are known for their ability to spread their seeds easily by the wind.|The children blew the dandelion seeds into the wind.|近义词：weed (when considered undesirable)|反义词：cultivated flower (in a garden setting)|名词
