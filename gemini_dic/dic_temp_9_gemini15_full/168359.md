pro-Vietnamese: /ˌproʊ ˌviːətˈnæmiːz/ | adj. | 亲越的；支持越南的 |  Favoring or supporting Vietnam or its people.  This term usually implies political or ideological support.  It can refer to individuals, policies, or movements. | 例句：His pro-Vietnamese stance during the war was controversial. 他在战争期间亲越的立场颇具争议。


The term is a compound word, combining "pro-" (meaning "in favor of," "supporting") with "Vietnamese" (referring to Vietnam or its people).


近义词：pro-Vietnamese (no perfect synonym, but similar sentiments could be expressed with phrases like):  supportive of Vietnam,  in favor of Vietnam,  sympathetic to Vietnam


反义词：anti-Vietnamese, hostile to Vietnam


词性: adjective
