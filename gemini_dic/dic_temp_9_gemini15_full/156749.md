piercer:/ˈpɪərsər/| n. v.|穿孔者；穿孔器；穿刺器|A piercer is a person who performs piercings, or a tool used for piercing.  It can also refer to something that pierces.|例句：She went to a professional piercer to get her ears pierced. 她去了一家专业的穿孔店穿耳洞。  The ice pick served as a makeshift piercer for the experiment.  冰锥在实验中充当临时穿刺器。|近义词：puncher, perforator|反义词：repairer, mender
