offensive:/əˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|冒犯的；攻击性的；令人不快的|Causing someone to feel deeply hurt, angry, or upset;  attacking or intending to attack.  It can also describe something unpleasant or disagreeable.|例句：His comments were deeply offensive to many people. = 他的评论深深地冒犯了许多人。

例句：The smell from the garbage was offensive. = 垃圾散发出的气味令人作呕。


近义词：insulting,  provocative,  disagreeable,  unpleasant,  abhorrent|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, inoffensive, complimentary|词性：adjective
