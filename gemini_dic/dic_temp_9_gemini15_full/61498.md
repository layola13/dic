embroidery:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri/ | n. | 刺绣；绣花 | The art or process of decorating fabric or other materials with needlework.  It often involves using a needle and thread to create patterns or images.  | Example:  The tablecloth featured exquisite embroidery. 这块桌布饰有精美的刺绣。


The embroidery on her dress was stunning. 她衣服上的刺绣令人惊艳。| needlework, needlepoint | plain weaving | n.
