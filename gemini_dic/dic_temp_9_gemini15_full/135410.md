nonperpendicularly:/ˌnɒnˌpɜːrpɛnˈdɪkjʊləli/| adv.|非垂直地，不正交地|Not at a right angle; not perpendicular.  This adverb describes something that is not at a 90-degree angle to something else.|例句：The lines intersected nonperpendicularly.  =  这些线非垂直相交。

The two planes were positioned nonperpendicularly to each other. = 这两架飞机彼此位置不正交。|近义词：obliquely, askew, at an angle|反义词：perpendicularly
