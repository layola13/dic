greasiest:/ˈɡriːsiəst/| adj.|最油腻的，最油污的 |Most covered or saturated with grease; extremely oily or greasy.  |例句：The greasiest spoon in the kitchen was used to stir the frying pan. 厨房里最油腻的勺子被用来搅拌煎锅。

例句：That's the greasiest burger I've ever eaten! 这是我吃过的最油腻的汉堡！


近义词：oiliest, fattiest, slickest |反义词：driest, cleanest


词源：源自 grease (油脂) + -iest (最高级后缀)  The word "grease" itself comes from Old French "graisse," meaning "fat."
