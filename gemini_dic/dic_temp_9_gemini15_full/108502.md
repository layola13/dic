laying-up: /ˈleɪɪŋ ʌp/ | v. |  搁置；保养；储藏；（尤指因伤病）退出比赛 |


英文解析：The verb "laying-up" refers to several actions, often involving putting something aside or preparing something for later use.  In a sporting context, it usually means withdrawing from a competition due to injury.  In a manufacturing or maintenance context, it signifies preparing equipment for storage or repair.  It can also simply mean storing something away.


例句：

The mechanic is laying up the engine for winter. = 技工正在为冬天保养发动机。

He had to lay up for several weeks after the accident. = 事故后他不得不休息几周。

The company is laying up its excess inventory. = 公司正在储藏多余的库存。


近义词： storing, putting away,  shelving,  (in sports) withdrawing


反义词： using,  continuing (in sports),  activating


词性：动词
