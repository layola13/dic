harikari: /ˌhɑːrɪˈkɑːri/ | n. | 切腹自杀；(尤指日本武士的)自杀 |  The act of disemboweling oneself, especially by a samurai in Japan, as a form of ritual suicide.  This term is often used incorrectly and should be replaced with seppuku. |  The samurai performed harakiri to atone for failure.  武士为了赎罪而切腹自杀。


|近义词: seppuku, suicide |反义词: life, survival |词性: noun
