hectocotylized: /ˌhɛktoʊˈkɒtɪlaɪzd/ | adj. | 具交接腕的；具有雄性交接腕的 | Modified (especially of a cephalopod's arm) to transfer sperm.  This usually refers to a specialized arm in male cephalopods (like octopuses and squids) used for transferring sperm during mating. | Example: The hectocotylized arm of the octopus is a fascinating example of sexual dimorphism.  这条章鱼的交接腕是性二态性的一个迷人例子。|近义词：modified, specialized | 反义词：unmodified, normal
