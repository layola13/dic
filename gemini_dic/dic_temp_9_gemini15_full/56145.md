documentations:/ˌdɒkjuːmənˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 文件，文档，文献 |  The act of providing documents or the documents themselves that provide evidence or information.  It refers to the collection of documents that support or explain something. | Example: The project requires thorough documentations of all the processes.  这个项目需要对所有流程进行彻底的记录。  The documentations were submitted on time. 文件已按时提交。|近义词：records, papers, archives | 反义词：lack of evidence, absence of proof | 名词
