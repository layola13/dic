misfits:/ˈmɪsfɪts/| n.|不适合的人；不协调的事物；不合格的人|People or things that are not suitable for a particular purpose or group, or that do not fit in with others.  They often feel out of place or excluded. |例句：He felt like a misfit in the prestigious law firm. 他感觉自己在这个著名的律师事务所格格不入。

例句：The mismatched furniture looked like a collection of misfits.  这些不配套的家具看起来像一堆不协调的东西。


近义词：outsiders, outcasts, anomalies, oddballs, eccentrics

反义词：fits, regulars, conformists


词性:名词
