doffing:/ˈdɒfɪŋ/| v.|脱掉；脱帽致敬|The act of removing something, especially a hat or clothing.  It often implies a respectful or ceremonial gesture. |例句：The soldier stood at attention, performing a doffing of his cap. 士兵立正站好，脱帽致敬。

例句：He finished his work and proceeded with the doffing of his overalls. 他完成了工作，然后脱掉工作服。


近义词：removing, taking off, shedding


反义词：donning, putting on


词性:动词
