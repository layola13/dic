phototoxicity:/ˌfoʊtoʊtɒkˈsɪsəti/| n. |光毒性 |The toxic effect of a substance on a living organism following exposure to light, particularly ultraviolet (UV) light.|  Some drugs can cause phototoxicity, leading to skin reactions when exposed to sunlight.|近义词：photosensitivity, light sensitivity|反义词：photoprotection
