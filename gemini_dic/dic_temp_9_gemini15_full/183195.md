reprepare:/ˌriːprɪˈpeər/| vt.|重新准备，再次准备|To prepare something again or differently.  It implies a previous preparation that was insufficient, unsatisfactory, or needs modification.|例句：We had to reprepare the presentation after the client requested changes. 我们在客户要求更改后不得不重新准备演示文稿。

例句：The chef had to reprepare the dish after the first attempt was deemed unsatisfactory.  厨师第一次尝试失败后不得不重新制作这道菜。


近义词：revise, redo, remake, prepare again,  review


反义词：neglect, abandon, leave undone


词性：动词
