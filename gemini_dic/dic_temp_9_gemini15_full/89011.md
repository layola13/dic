hollowed:/ˈhɒləʊd/ /ˈhɑːloʊd/ | v. |掏空的；使凹陷；使空虚；使沮丧|To make a hole in something, or to become empty or depressed.|The wind hollowed out the cave over centuries.  风在几个世纪里掏空了这个洞穴。

The constant criticism hollowed her out emotionally.  持续不断的批评使她情绪低落。

|近义词：excavate, empty, depress |反义词：fill, solid,  uplift |动词
