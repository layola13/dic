inventive:/ɪnˈvɛntɪv/| adj. | 富有创造力的；有发明才能的 |  Characterized by the power or process of inventing; showing inventiveness; imaginative.  |  He's a very inventive engineer, always coming up with new solutions. 他是一位非常有创造力的工程师，总是能想出新的解决方案。 | creative, innovative, original | unimaginative, uncreative | 形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inventive in his approach to problem-solving. = 他解决问题的方法很有创造力。
She is an inventive writer, crafting unique and compelling stories. = 她是一位富有创造力的作家，创作出独特而引人入胜的故事。


一般过去时:
The ancient Egyptians were inventive in their use of building materials. = 古埃及人在建筑材料的使用上很有创造力。
Leonardo da Vinci was incredibly inventive, designing machines centuries ahead of their time. = 达芬奇非常有创造力，设计出比他那个时代早几个世纪的机器。


现在完成时:
Scientists have been inventive in finding new ways to treat diseases. = 科学家们在寻找治疗疾病的新方法方面取得了创造性的成果。
She has been inventive in her marketing strategies, leading to significant sales growth. = 她在市场营销策略方面很有创造力，这导致了显著的销售增长。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the printing press, people had been inventive in reproducing texts. = 在印刷机发明之前，人们在复制文本方面很有创造力。
They had been inventive in their attempts to escape, but ultimately failed. = 他们曾尝试过各种有创造性的逃生方法，但最终失败了。


将来时:
Engineers will be inventive in developing sustainable energy solutions. = 工程师们将在开发可持续能源解决方案方面富有创造力。
We believe that future generations will be even more inventive than we are. = 我们相信，后代将比我们更有创造力。


虚拟语气:
If he were more inventive, he could find a better solution. = 如果他更有创造力，他就能找到更好的解决方案。
I wish I were more inventive in my cooking. = 我希望我的烹饪更有创造力。


被动语态:
New solutions are being invented to address climate change. = 正在发明新的解决方案来应对气候变化。
Innovative ideas are constantly being invented in the tech industry. = 科技行业不断涌现出创新想法。


疑问句:
Is he inventive enough for this project? = 他在这个项目中足够有创造力吗？
Are there any inventive ways to reduce waste? = 有什么创造性的方法可以减少浪费吗？


否定句:
His approach to the problem wasn't very inventive. = 他解决问题的方法不太有创造力。
The design was not particularly inventive. = 这个设计并没有特别有创造力。


PHRASE->
inventive mind  富有创造力的头脑
inventive spirit  创新精神
inventive solutions  创造性的解决方案
highly inventive  非常有创造力的
an inventive approach  一种创造性的方法

词源:
源自动词invent (发明)，加上形容词后缀-ive。 invent 来自拉丁语invenire (找到，发现)，由in- (进入) + venire (来)构成。
