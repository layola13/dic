safeguards:/ˈseɪfɡɑːrdz/ | n. | 保护措施；安全措施 | Measures or actions taken to protect something from harm or damage.  |  The company has implemented strict safeguards to protect its data. 这家公司实施了严格的安全措施来保护其数据。 The government introduced new safeguards to prevent future accidents. 政府出台了新的安全措施以防止未来的事故。 | precautions, protections, defenses | dangers, risks, threats | 名词
