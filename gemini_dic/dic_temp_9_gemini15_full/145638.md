overconsumption: /ˌoʊvər kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. |过度消费；消费过度 | Excessive consumption of goods and services, often beyond what is needed or sustainable.  This can lead to environmental damage, economic instability, and personal debt. | Example: The overconsumption of resources is a major threat to the planet.  过度消费资源是对地球的主要威胁。The overconsumption of fast fashion contributes to textile waste. 快时尚的过度消费导致纺织品浪费。|近义词: extravagance, excess, wastefulness | 反义词: conservation, frugality, thrift
