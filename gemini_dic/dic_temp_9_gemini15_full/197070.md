shambling:/ˈʃæmblɪŋ/| adj. |蹒跚的，摇摇晃晃的 |Moving in a clumsy or unsteady way; shuffling.  It implies a lack of coordination and often suggests weakness or fatigue. |例句：He walked with a shambling gait, his shoulders slumped. 他步履蹒跚，肩膀下垂。

例句：The zombie shambled towards the terrified villagers.  僵尸摇摇晃晃地走向惊恐的村民。


近义词：shuffling, stumbling, tottering, unsteady, awkward

反义词：graceful, elegant, poised, steady, agile

词性：形容词
