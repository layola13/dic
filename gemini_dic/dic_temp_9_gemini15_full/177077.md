reappeal:/ˌriːəˈpiːl/| vt.|再次恳求；重新提出上诉|To make a fresh appeal; to appeal again.|例句：He decided to reappeal the court's decision. 他决定对法院的判决再次提出上诉。|例句：She will reappeal to the higher court. 她将向高级法院再次上诉。|近义词：reiterate, reapply|反义词：withdraw, concede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reapplies for the job every year. = 他每年都重新申请这份工作。
They regularly reappeal to the higher authority. = 他们定期向更高的权威机构提出上诉。


一般过去时:
She reappealed the unfair dismissal. = 她对不公平的解雇再次提出上诉。
The lawyer reapplied for a postponement. = 律师再次申请延期。


现在完成时:
He has reapplied for the scholarship three times. = 他已经三次重新申请奖学金了。
They have reappealed the case to a higher court. = 他们已经将此案上诉到更高一级法院。


过去完成时:
She had reappealed before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，她已经再次上诉了。
They had reapplied for the permit several times. = 他们已经几次重新申请许可证了。


将来时:
He will reappeal the judge's ruling. = 他将对法官的裁决再次提出上诉。
They will reapply for the grant next year. = 他们明年将重新申请拨款。


虚拟语气:
If he were to reappeal, he would need new evidence. = 如果他要再次上诉，他需要新的证据。
I wish I had reapplied for that position. = 我真希望我重新申请了那个职位。


被动语态:
The case was reappealed by the defendant. = 此案被告再次上诉。
The application was reapplied for by the applicant. = 申请人再次申请。


疑问句:
Will they reappeal the decision? = 他们会对这个决定再次上诉吗？
Did he reapply for the job after being rejected? = 他被拒绝后是否重新申请了这份工作？


否定句:
He did not reappeal the court's ruling. = 他没有对法院的裁决再次上诉。
She did not reapply for the position. = 她没有重新申请这个职位。


PHRASE->
reappeal a case = 再次上诉一个案件
reappeal a decision = 再次上诉一个决定
reappeal for consideration = 再次请求考虑
reappeal to the higher court = 向上级法院再次上诉
reappeal the judgment = 再次上诉判决
