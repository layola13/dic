housewright:/ˈhaʊsˌraɪt/| n. |房屋木匠，木工|A housewright is a skilled carpenter who specializes in building or repairing houses.  They are often involved in all aspects of the construction process, from framing to finishing. |The housewright expertly crafted the intricate window frames.  这位房屋木匠巧妙地制作了复杂的窗框。|carpenter, joiner|mason, plumber|名词
