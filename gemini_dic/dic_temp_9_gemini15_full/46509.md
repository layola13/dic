daisies:/ˈdeɪziːz/| n.|雏菊，紫菀|A daisy is a type of flower with white petals and a yellow center.  Daisies are often associated with innocence and purity.|例句：The field was covered in a carpet of daisies.  田野里铺满了雏菊。|例句：She picked a single daisy and tucked it behind her ear. 她摘了一朵雏菊别在耳后。|近义词：flowers, wildflowers|反义词：无
