chapped:/tʃæpt/| adj.|干裂的，裂开的|Having cracks, as from dryness or cold.  Describes skin that is dry, cracked, and sore.|例句：My lips are chapped from the cold wind.  我的嘴唇被寒风吹裂了。|例句：The gardener's hands were chapped and rough. 园丁的手又干又裂。|近义词：cracked, fissured, split|反义词：smooth, supple


USAGE->

His hands are chapped and sore. = 他的手干裂疼痛。
