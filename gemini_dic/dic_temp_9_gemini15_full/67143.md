/ɪkˈspoʊʒəl/ | n. | 展览；揭露；曝光；显露 | The act of exposing something to view or making it known.  It can refer to a public display, a revelation of something hidden, or the act of being subjected to something (like the elements). | 例句：The museum's new exposal of Impressionist paintings is highly anticipated.  博物馆新展出的印象派绘画作品备受期待。

The exposal of the politician's corruption scandal led to his resignation.  这位政客的腐败丑闻被曝光后，他被迫辞职。


|近义词：exhibition, display, revelation, disclosure, exposure | 反义词：concealment, secrecy, hiding |  名词
