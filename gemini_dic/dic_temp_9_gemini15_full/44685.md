crucifying: /ˈkruːsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 钉死在十字架上；极度折磨；严厉批评 |  The act of fastening someone to a cross as a form of execution; to inflict great suffering on; to severely criticize. |


例句：

They were crucifying him for his mistakes. = 他们因他的错误而严厉批评他。

The media is crucifying the politician for his scandal. = 媒体因他的丑闻而严厉批评这位政治家。


近义词：torturing, tormenting, agonizing, castigating, lambasting


反义词：praising, commending, applauding


词源：

From Middle English *crucifien, from Old French crucifiger, from Latin crucifigere, from crux "cross" + figere "to fix, fasten".
