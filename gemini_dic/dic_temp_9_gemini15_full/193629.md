self-biography:/ˌsɛlfbaɪˈɒɡrəfi/|/ˈsɛlfbaɪˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |自传 |An autobiography is a detailed account of a person's life, written by that person.  It typically covers significant events, relationships, and personal reflections.  |Example:  She wrote a compelling self-biography detailing her rise from poverty to success. 她写了一本引人入胜的自传，详细描述了她从贫困到成功的历程。|近义词：autobiography, life story |反义词：biography (written by someone else)
