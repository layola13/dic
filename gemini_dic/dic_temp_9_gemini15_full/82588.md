guardhouse:/ˈɡɑːrdhaʊs/|/ˈɡɑːrdˌhaʊs/| n. |警卫室；岗哨；值班室 |A small building where guards are stationed, often for security or protection.  It typically contains facilities for the guards, such as sleeping quarters, restrooms, and storage. |例句：The soldiers were stationed in the guardhouse at the entrance to the base.  士兵驻扎在基地入口处的警卫室里。

The guardhouse was brightly lit throughout the night. 警卫室整夜灯火通明。|近义词：guard post, sentry box, watchtower |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with places lacking security.
