/ˈsɪnɑːn/| n. |近义词|反义词|名词

中文翻译：同义词；近义词

英文解析：A word or phrase that has the same or nearly the same meaning as another word or phrase.  It's often used in discussions of vocabulary and language.

例句：

"Happy" is a sinon for "joyful".  "快乐"是"喜悦"的同义词。

The words "big" and "large" are sinon. "大"和"巨大的"是同义词。


近义词：equivalent, synonym, parallel

反义词：antonym


词性：名词
