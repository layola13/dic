cross-trainer:/ˈkrɒsˌtreɪnər/| n.|交叉训练器；椭圆机|A piece of exercise equipment that simulates both running and walking, providing a low-impact cardiovascular workout.|例句：I use a cross-trainer three times a week to improve my cardiovascular fitness. 我一周用三次交叉训练器来提高我的心血管健康。  She prefers the cross-trainer to the treadmill because it's easier on her knees. 她更喜欢交叉训练器而不是跑步机，因为它对她的膝盖更友好。|近义词：elliptical machine, elliptical trainer|反义词：None.
