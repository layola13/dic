how-to: /ˈhaʊ tuː/ | n. | 方法，指南，教程 | A how-to guide or manual provides instructions on how to perform a task or achieve a result.  It often involves a step-by-step process.  | Example:  I found a great how-to video on YouTube for fixing my leaky faucet. = 我在YouTube上找到一个关于修理我漏水的龙头的好教程视频。


Example: This how-to book teaches you how to bake delicious cakes. = 这本指导书教你如何烘焙美味的蛋糕。


近义词：guide, manual, tutorial, instruction, directions | 反义词：无
