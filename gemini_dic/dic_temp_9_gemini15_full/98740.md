insurrectionary: /ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɛri/ | adj. | 叛乱的；起义的 | Relating to or characteristic of an insurrection; rebellious.  |例句：The insurrectionary forces seized control of the capital.  叛乱部队占领了首都。
The government swiftly suppressed the insurrectionary movement. 政府迅速镇压了叛乱运动。|近义词：rebellious, revolutionary, seditious |反义词：loyal, obedient, submissive |
