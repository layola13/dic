词源:
The word "irresilient" is formed by adding the negative prefix "ir-" to the adjective "resilient".  "Resilient" comes from the Latin word "resilio," meaning "to leap back," "spring back," or "rebound."  Therefore, "irresilient" literally means "not able to leap back" or "not able to rebound."
