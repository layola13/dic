diuretic:/ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪk/| n. adj.|利尿剂；利尿的|A diuretic is a substance that promotes the excretion of urine.  It increases the flow of urine. |例句：The doctor prescribed a diuretic to help reduce his swelling.  医生开了利尿剂来帮助减轻他的肿胀。|近义词：diuretic agent, urinary stimulant|反义词：antidiuretic
