point-out:/pɔɪnt aʊt/ | vt. | 指出；指明；强调 | To draw attention to something or someone.  |例句：The teacher pointed out my mistake in the essay. 老师指出了我论文中的错误。 |例句：He pointed out the dangers of smoking. 他指出了吸烟的危害。| 近义词：indicate, highlight, emphasize, show | 反义词：ignore, overlook, neglect | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He points out the flaws in the design. = 他指出了设计中的缺陷。
She points out the importance of punctuality. = 她指出了准时的重要性。

一般过去时:
The guide pointed out the historical landmarks. = 导游指出了历史地标。
He pointed out the errors in my calculations. = 他指出了我计算中的错误。


现在完成时:
She has pointed out the inconsistencies in the report. = 她已经指出了报告中前后矛盾的地方。
They have pointed out the need for more funding. = 他们已经指出了对更多资金的需求。


过去完成时:
He had pointed out the problem several times before. = 他之前已经多次指出过这个问题了。
She had pointed out the risks involved in the project. = 她已经指出了该项目中涉及的风险。


将来时:
He will point out the key features of the product. = 他将指出产品的关键特性。
She will point out the best route to take. = 她将指出最佳路线。


虚拟语气:
If he pointed out the mistake earlier, we could have avoided it. = 如果他早点指出错误，我们就能避免它了。
I wish he had pointed out the problem sooner. = 我希望他早点指出这个问题。


被动语态:
The mistake was pointed out by the teacher. = 老师指出了错误。
The need for reform was pointed out by many experts. = 许多专家指出了改革的必要性。


疑问句:
Can you point out where I made a mistake? = 你能指出我哪里犯错了？
Will you point out the way to the station? = 你能指明去车站的路吗？


否定句:
He didn't point out the obvious flaw in the plan. = 他没有指出计划中明显的缺陷。
She didn't point out any problems during the presentation. = 她没有在演示过程中指出任何问题。


PHRASE->
point out a fact = 指出一个事实
point out the difference = 指出差异
point out the significance = 指出重要性
point out the problem = 指出问题
point out the solution = 指出解决方案
