pcb:/ˌpiːsiːˈbiː/|/ˈpiːsiːˈbiː/| n.|印刷电路板|Printed Circuit Board.  A PCB is a thin sheet of insulating material, usually fiberglass, with copper conductive pathways printed on it. These pathways connect various electronic components, creating an electrical circuit. PCBs are used in almost all electronic devices, from smartphones to computers.|例句：The technician replaced the faulty PCB in the computer.  技术员更换了电脑中故障的印刷电路板。|近义词：circuit board, printed wiring board|反义词：无
