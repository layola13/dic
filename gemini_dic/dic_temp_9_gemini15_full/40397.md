contraflow:/ˈkɒntrəfləʊ/| n.|逆向行驶，逆行车道|A system where traffic is allowed to flow in the opposite direction to normal, usually to alleviate congestion or during emergencies.  |例句：The authorities implemented a contraflow system to ease traffic congestion during the marathon.  当局实施了逆向行驶系统以缓解马拉松期间的交通拥堵。|例句：Due to the accident, a contraflow was put in place on the highway. 由于事故，高速公路上实施了逆向行驶。|近义词：reverse flow, opposite flow|反义词：normal flow, unidirectional flow|名词

词源:
The word "contraflow" is a compound word formed from "contra-" (meaning "against" or "opposite") and "flow" (referring to the movement of traffic).  Therefore, it literally means "against the flow" or "opposite flow".
