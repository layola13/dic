chaunter:/ˈtʃɔːntər/| n. |吟唱者；念经者；（尤指）领唱者|A chaunter is a person who chants, especially a leader of a chant.  They often lead religious chants or are part of a choir. |例句：The chaunter led the congregation in a rhythmic prayer.  吟唱者带领会众做节奏感强的祈祷。The lead chaunter's voice carried above the others. 领唱者的声音盖过了其他人。|近义词：chanter, singer, leader|反义词：listener
