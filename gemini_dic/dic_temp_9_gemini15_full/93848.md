immobilises:/ɪˈmɒbəlaɪz/ | vt. |使固定；使不动；使瘫痪 | To make something or someone unable to move.  This verb typically refers to making something or someone immobile, either physically or figuratively. |例句：The accident immobilised his car. 事故使他的车无法移动。

The surgeon immobilised the patient's broken leg with a cast. 外科医生用石膏固定了病人的骨折腿。


近义词：disable, paralyze, cripple, restrain, fix, secure | 反义词：mobilize, activate, free, release | 词性：动词
