haemoconcentration:/ˌhiːməʊˌkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/| n. |血液浓缩|The increase in the concentration of blood cells and other blood constituents resulting from a decrease in the plasma volume. This can occur due to dehydration, burns, or prolonged diarrhea.|Example:  Severe dehydration can lead to haemoconcentration, increasing the risk of thrombosis.  严重的脱水会导致血液浓缩，增加血栓形成的风险。|近义词：blood thickening, plasma reduction|反义词：haemodilution
