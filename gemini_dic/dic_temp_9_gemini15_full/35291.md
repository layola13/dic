clypeastroid:/ˈklaɪpiˌæstrɔɪd/| n. |海胆类动物|A clypeastroid is a type of sea urchin belonging to the order Clypeasteroida, characterized by their flattened, disc-like or heart-shaped bodies.  They are often found in shallow, sandy or muddy marine environments.| Example:  The fossil record shows a diverse range of clypeastroid species.  化石记录显示种类繁多的海胆类动物。  |近义词：sea urchin (泛指)|反义词：N/A
