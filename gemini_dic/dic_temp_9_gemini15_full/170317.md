proves:/pruːvz/| v.|证明；证实|To demonstrate the truth or existence of (something) by evidence or argument.|例句：The evidence proves his guilt.  证据证明他有罪。|例句：The experiment proved his theory correct.  实验证明他的理论是正确的。|近义词：demonstrates, shows, establishes, verifies|反义词：disproves, refutes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence proves his innocence. = 证据证明了他的清白。
This experiment proves the theory. = 这个实验证明了这个理论。


一般过去时:
The test proved the hypothesis. = 测试证明了假设。
The results proved inconclusive. = 结果证明是不确定的。


现在完成时:
Research has proved the link between smoking and cancer. = 研究已经证明了吸烟和癌症之间的联系。
She has proved herself to be a capable leader. = 她已经证明自己是一位有能力的领导者。


过去完成时:
They had proved their loyalty to the crown. = 他们已经向王室证明了他们的忠诚。
It had proved to be a difficult challenge. = 这已被证明是一个艰难的挑战。


将来时:
This new data will prove invaluable. = 这些新数据将被证明是无价的。
Further testing will prove the drug's effectiveness. = 进一步的测试将证明该药物的有效性。


虚拟语气:
If the theory proved correct, it would revolutionize the field. = 如果理论被证明是正确的，它将彻底改变这个领域。
I wish the experiment had proved successful. = 我希望实验已经成功了。


被动语态:
His guilt was proved beyond a reasonable doubt. = 他的罪行已在合理的怀疑之外得到证实。
The theory was proved incorrect by later experiments. = 后来实验证明该理论是错误的。


疑问句:
Does this evidence prove anything? = 这证据能证明什么吗？
Will this experiment prove our hypothesis? = 这个实验会证明我们的假设吗？


否定句:
The study did not prove the hypothesis. = 这项研究没有证明假设。
The experiment failed to prove the theory. = 实验未能证明该理论。


PHRASE->
prove oneself = 证明自己
prove to be = 被证明是
prove conclusively = 确凿地证明
prove beyond a doubt = 毫无疑问地证明
prove a point = 证明一个论点
