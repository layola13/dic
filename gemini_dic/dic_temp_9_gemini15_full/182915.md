repetitions:/ˌrepəˈtɪʃənz/| n. |重复，反复|The act of repeating something; the state of being repeated.  It can refer to instances of something being repeated, or the act of repeating itself.|例句：The repetitions in the poem created a hypnotic effect. 这首诗歌中的重复创造了一种催眠效果。

例句：There were too many repetitions in his speech; it lacked originality.他的演讲中重复太多，缺乏原创性。


近义词：repetitions, reiterations, recurrences | 反义词：uniqueness, originality


词源:  From the Latin "repetitio," meaning "a repeating," from "repetere," meaning "to repeat,"  formed from the prefix "re-" (again) and "petere" (to seek, to strive for).
