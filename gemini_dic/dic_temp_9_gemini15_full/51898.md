dharna:/ˈdɑːrnə/| n. |静坐抗议；静坐示威|A dharna is a form of non-violent protest where a group of people sit-in at a particular place to express their grievances or demands.  It is often used in India and other parts of South Asia. |例句：The farmers staged a dharna outside the parliament building to protest against the new agricultural laws.  农民们在议会大厦外举行了静坐抗议，以抗议新的农业法。|近义词：sit-in, protest, demonstration|反义词：compliance, obedience|名词
