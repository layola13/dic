marinade:/ˌmærɪˈneɪd/ | n., v. |腌料；腌制 |  A liquid mixture, typically of oil, vinegar, herbs, and spices, in which meat or fish is marinated.  |例句：The chicken was marinated in a lemon and herb marinade for several hours.  鸡肉在柠檬和香草腌料中腌制了几个小时。

例句：I'm going to marinade the steak before grilling it. 我要在烤之前腌制牛排。


近义词：pickle, brine, sauce | 反义词：None


词性: 名词和动词
