eponymous:/ɪˈpɒnɪməs/ | adj. | 以人名命名的；同名的 |  Bearing the same name as a person, thing, or place.  | Example:  The eponymous hero of the novel is a complex and flawed character. 这部小说的同名主人公是一个复杂而有缺陷的人物。|近义词：namesake | 反义词：anonymous
