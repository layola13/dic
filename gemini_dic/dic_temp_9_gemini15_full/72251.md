florin:/ˈflɒrɪn/| n. |佛罗林（旧时的货币单位）|A florin is a former monetary unit of several European countries, including the Netherlands, Austria, and Italy.  It's also historically been used in other parts of the world.  The value and subdivisions varied depending on the country and time period. |The Dutch guilder was divided into 100 cents, and each guilder contained 2½ florins. |guilder, shilling, mark |pound, dollar |名词
