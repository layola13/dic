night-and-day:/naɪt ænd deɪ/| adj. |日夜不停地；整天；非常|Continuously; all the time; constantly; incessantly.|例句：He worked night and day to finish the project. 他日夜不停地工作以完成项目。|例句：The baby cried night and day. 婴儿日夜哭闹。|近义词：constantly, incessantly, continuously |反义词：occasionally, intermittently

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They work night and day. = 他们日夜工作。
The machine runs night and day. = 这台机器日夜运转。

一般过去时:
We studied night and day for the exam. = 我们为考试日夜学习。
The factory operated night and day. = 工厂日夜运作。

现在完成时:
She has been working night and day to meet the deadline. = 她为了赶期限一直日夜工作。
They have been running the business night and day. = 他们一直日夜经营着这个生意。

过去完成时:
They had been working night and day before the launch. = 在发布之前，他们已经日夜工作了。
She had been studying night and day before the important test. = 她在重要的考试之前，已经日夜学习了。

将来时:
We will work night and day to complete this task. = 我们将日夜工作以完成这项任务。
He will be running the marathon night and day. = 他将日夜参加马拉松比赛。(This example is less common, but illustrates future continuous)


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would work night and day. = 如果我有更多时间，我会日夜工作。
If they were more dedicated, they would work night and day. = 如果他们更尽职尽责，他们会日夜工作。

被动语态:
The project was completed by workers who toiled night and day. =  日夜工作的工人完成了这个项目。
The machine is operated night and day by the staff. = 机器由工作人员日夜操作。

疑问句:
Do they work night and day? = 他们日夜工作吗？
Does the factory operate night and day? = 工厂日夜运作吗？

否定句:
We don't work night and day. = 我们不日夜工作。
The shop doesn't open night and day. = 这家店不日夜营业。
