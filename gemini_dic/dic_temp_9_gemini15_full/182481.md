renouncing: /rɪˈnaʊnsɪŋ/ | v. | 放弃；否认；拒绝；宣布放弃 |  Giving up or rejecting something, especially a claim, right, or belief.  | 例句：He is renouncing his claim to the throne. 他正在放弃他对王位的继承权。 | 近义词：abandoning, relinquishing, forswearing, repudiating | 反义词：claiming, embracing, accepting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renounces his citizenship every year. 他每年都放弃他的公民身份。
She renounces violence in all its forms. 她宣布放弃一切形式的暴力。


一般过去时:
He renounced his faith after the war. 战争后他放弃了他的信仰。
She renounced her inheritance to pursue her dreams. 她放弃了继承权去追求她的梦想。


现在完成时:
He has renounced his past life. 他已经放弃了他过去的生活。
She has renounced all ties to her former life. 她已经与她以前的生活断绝了一切联系。


过去完成时:
He had renounced his position before the scandal broke. 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经辞去了职务。
She had renounced her belief in God years before. 多年前她就放弃了对上帝的信仰。


将来时:
He will renounce his title next year. 他明年将放弃他的头衔。
She will renounce her membership of the club. 她将辞去俱乐部会员资格。


虚拟语气:
If he renounced his ambition, he would be happier. 如果他放弃了他的野心，他会更快乐。
I wish I had renounced my bad habits sooner. 我希望我早点改掉我的坏习惯。


被动语态:
His citizenship was renounced by him. 他的公民身份被他放弃了。
His claim to the property was renounced by the court. 法院宣布放弃他对该房产的所有权。


疑问句:
Will he renounce his position? 他会放弃他的职位吗？
Has she renounced her beliefs? 她放弃了她的信仰吗？


否定句:
He did not renounce his right to appeal. 他没有放弃他的上诉权。
She will not renounce her love for him. 她不会放弃对他的爱。
