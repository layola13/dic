petiolate: /ˈpɛtiəleɪt/ | adj. | 有叶柄的；具叶柄的 | Having a petiole; relating to or possessing a petiole.  A petiole is the stalk that joins a leaf blade to the stem.  | Example: The petiolate leaves of the maple tree are easily identifiable.  枫树的叶柄叶很容易辨认。 The plant has large, petiolate leaves. 这植物有大的叶柄叶。|近义词：stalked|反义词：sessile (无柄的)


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Instead, it describes nouns.

Example sentences illustrating its use:

A petiolate leaf: 一片有叶柄的叶子
The petiolate structure of the plant: 植物的叶柄结构
