laparocele:/ˈlæpəroʊˌsiːl/| n. |腹壁疝|A laparocele is a type of hernia that occurs in the abdominal wall. It's a protrusion of abdominal contents through a defect or weakness in the abdominal wall muscles.  Laparocele can vary in size and location, and may or may not cause symptoms.  Severe cases can lead to bowel obstruction or strangulation, requiring immediate surgical intervention.|The patient presented with a painful laparocele in the right lower quadrant.|近义词：abdominal hernia, ventral hernia|反义词：无
