词源:

The word "indigestion" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") + "digestion" (the process of digesting food).  Therefore, it literally means "not digesting."  "Digestion" itself has roots in the Latin word "digerere," meaning "to distribute, separate, dissolve."
