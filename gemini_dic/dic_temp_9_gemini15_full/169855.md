proseminary:/proʊˈsɛmɪneri/| n. |预备研讨会；预备课程|A preparatory seminar, typically for graduate students, often focusing on research methods or a specific field of study. It's a smaller, more focused seminar than a full-fledged seminary.  |例句：The graduate program began with a proseminary on qualitative research methods.  研究生课程以关于定性研究方法的预备研讨会开始。|近义词：preparatory course, introductory seminar, workshop|反义词：advanced seminar, graduate-level course
