multisegmental: /ˌmʌltiˈsɛɡmɛntəl/ | adj. | 多节的，多段的 |  Consisting of or divided into many segments or sections.  This term is often used in fields like anatomy, linguistics, and engineering to describe structures or processes that are composed of multiple distinct parts. |

例句：

The multisegmental spine allows for a wide range of motion.  = 多节的脊柱允许更大的活动范围。

The multisegmental pipeline transports oil across several states. = 多段的管道输送石油跨越多个州。


近义词：polysegmental, multipartite, segmented


反义词：unsegmented, single-segment, monolithic


词源:

* **multi-**:  from Latin *multus*, meaning "many" or "much".

* **segment-**: from Latin *segmentum*, meaning "a piece cut off," from *secare*, "to cut."

* **-al**: a suffix forming adjectives, meaning "relating to" or "characterized by."
