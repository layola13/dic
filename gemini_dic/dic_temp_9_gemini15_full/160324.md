poop's: /puːps/ | n. | 粪便；大便 |  The informal term for feces.  It's often used in a childish or humorous context.  | 例句：The dog left a poop in the garden. = 狗在花园里拉了一泡屎。 |近义词：feces, excrement, stool, dung | 反义词：无 | 名词
