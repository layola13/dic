great:/ɡreɪt/| adj.|伟大的，极好的，巨大的|excellent; of considerable size, extent, or intensity.|例句：She's a great singer. 她是一位伟大的歌手。  It's a great honor to meet you. 见到您是我的荣幸。|近义词：wonderful, excellent, superb, immense, huge|反义词：terrible, awful, small, insignificant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake is great. = 蛋糕很好吃。
The view from the mountain is great. = 山上的景色很棒。

一般过去时:
The party was great. = 派对很棒。
The movie we saw last night was great. = 我们昨晚看的电影很棒。

现在完成时:
Things have been great since I moved. = 自从我搬家以来，一切都很顺利。
We have had a great time. = 我们玩得很开心。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had a great car. = 我真希望我有一辆好车。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
