reinstatement: /ˌriːɪnˈsteɪtmənt/ | n. |恢复职位；恢复原状；复职 | The act of restoring someone or something to their former position or state.  It implies a previous removal or suspension. |例句：The reinstatement of the wrongfully dismissed employee was a victory for justice.  被冤枉解雇的员工复职是正义的胜利。

例句：After a period of suspension, the company announced the reinstatement of its CEO.  停职一段时间后，公司宣布其首席执行官复职。


近义词：restoration, resumption, reinstallation, reappointment |反义词：dismissal, removal, termination, discharge |词性:noun
