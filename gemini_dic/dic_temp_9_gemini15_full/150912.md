paralleling:/ˈpærəleɪlɪŋ/| v.| 平行；与…相似；与…同时发生 |  The act of being parallel or running parallel; resembling or corresponding to something; occurring at the same time. |  例句：The two rivers are paralleling each other. 这两条河彼此平行。

例句：Her career paralleled his in many ways. 她的职业生涯在许多方面与他相似。


近义词：corresponding, resembling, coinciding, simultaneous

反义词：diverging, contrasting, differing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road parallels the river. = 这条路与河流平行。
His work parallels mine in many aspects. = 他的工作在许多方面与我的相似。

一般过去时:
The two events paralleled each other closely. = 这两件事彼此紧密相关。
His career paralleled hers for many years. = 他的职业生涯和她的一样持续了很多年。

现在完成时:
The company's growth has paralleled the expansion of the market. = 公司的增长与市场的扩张同步。
Recent developments have paralleled earlier predictions. = 最近的发展与早先的预测相符。

过去完成时:
The artist's earlier work had paralleled the styles of the Impressionists. = 这名艺术家早期的作品与印象派画家的风格相似。
His actions had paralleled those of his predecessors. = 他的行为与他的前任相似。

将来时:
The project will parallel the ones completed last year. = 该项目将与去年完成的项目类似。
Our efforts will parallel those of the other team. = 我们的努力将与其他团队的努力同步进行。

虚拟语气:
If his career paralleled mine, we would be friends. = 如果他的职业生涯与我的相似，我们会成为朋友。
I wish my life paralleled the life of a successful entrepreneur. = 我希望我的生活能像一个成功的企业家一样。

被动语态:
The two lines were paralleled in the drawing. = 这两条线在图纸中是平行的。
His achievements were paralleled by few. = 很少有人能与他的成就相比。

疑问句:
Does his work parallel mine? = 他的工作与我的相似吗？
Do these events parallel each other chronologically? = 这些事件在时间上是平行的吗？

否定句:
The two roads do not parallel each other. = 这两条路不平行。
His views did not parallel the mainstream opinion. = 他的观点与主流观点不符。


PHRASE->
run parallel to = 与…平行
in parallel with = 与…同时
parallel development = 并行开发
parallel processing = 并行处理
parallel lines = 平行线

词源:
From Middle English *paralel, from Old French *paralel, from Latin *parallelus, from Greek παράλληλος (parállēlos), from παρά (pará) "beside" + ἀλλήλων (allēlōn) "one another".
