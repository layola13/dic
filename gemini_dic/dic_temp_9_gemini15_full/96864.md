词源:
The word "inexhausted" is formed from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "exhausted".  "Exhausted" comes from the verb "exhaust," ultimately deriving from the Old French word "exhauster"  (to empty, to drain), which itself has Latin roots.
