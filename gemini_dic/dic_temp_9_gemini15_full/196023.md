sentinels:/ˈsɛntɪnəlz/| n.|哨兵；守卫；守护者| A sentinel is a person or thing that stands guard or keeps watch.  Sentinels are often used in military contexts to protect against attack, but the term can also be used more broadly to refer to anything that acts as a warning or protector. |例句：The soldiers stood as sentinels throughout the night.  士兵整夜都站岗放哨。

The old lighthouse served as a sentinel against the treacherous rocks.古老的灯塔作为警示危险礁石的守护者。|近义词：guard, watchman, protector, guardian|反义词：attacker, invader
