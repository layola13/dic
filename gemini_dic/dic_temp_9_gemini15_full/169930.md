prosternum: /ˈproʊstɜːrnəm/ | n. |前胸骨|The prosternum is the anteriormost sternal segment in some insects.  It is the first segment of the sternum, located at the front of the thorax.  It can be fused with other sternal segments or highly modified depending on the insect species.  |The prosternum of the beetle was unusually large.|近义词：anterior sternum (in insects)|反义词：无
