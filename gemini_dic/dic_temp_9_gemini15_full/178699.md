recrudesced: /ˌriːˈkruːdɛst/ | v. | 复发；恶化；重新出现 | To break out again; to reappear after a period of inactivity; to become worse or more severe. | 例句：The old wound recrudesced after he strained it. = 他拉伤旧伤后，旧伤复发了。  The symptoms of the disease recrudesced after a period of remission. = 疾病的症状在缓解期后再次出现。| 近义词：reappear, relapse, resurface, worsen | 反义词：improve, abate, subside, heal |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound recrudesces slowly. = 伤口慢慢复发。
His anger recrudesces frequently. = 他的怒火经常复燃。

一般过去时:
The infection recrudesced after a week of treatment. = 感染在一周的治疗后复发了。
His old habits recrudesced after he lost his job. = 他失业后，旧习复发了。

现在完成时:
The problem has recrudesced despite our efforts. = 尽管我们努力了，问题还是复发了。
His prejudice has recrudesced in recent years. = 近年来他的偏见又死灰复燃了。

过去完成时:
The disease had recrudesced before they realized it. = 他们意识到之前，疾病已经复发了。
The conflict had recrudesced several times before the peace treaty was signed. = 在签署和平条约之前，冲突已经几次复发了。

将来时:
The symptoms may recrudesce if he doesn't follow the doctor's orders. = 如果他不遵守医生的嘱咐，症状可能会复发。
The violence is likely to recrudesce if the underlying issues are not addressed. = 如果不解决根本问题，暴力很可能卷土重来。

虚拟语气:
If he were to overexert himself, the injury might recrudesce. = 如果他过度用力，伤势可能会复发。
I wish the old arguments wouldn't recrudesce. = 我希望旧的争论不会再起。

被动语态:
The disease was known to have recrudesced in several patients. = 已知该疾病在几名患者中复发。
The conflict has been reported to have recrudesced in the region. = 据报道，该地区的冲突再次爆发。

疑问句:
Will the symptoms recrudesce after the surgery? = 手术后症状会复发吗？
Has the problem recrudesced since the last meeting? = 自上次会议以来，问题是否复发了？

否定句:
The infection did not recrudesce after the antibiotic treatment. = 感染在抗生素治疗后没有复发。
Thankfully, the pain did not recrudesce. = 谢天谢地，疼痛没有复发。
