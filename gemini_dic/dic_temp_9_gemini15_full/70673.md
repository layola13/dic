finaglers:/fɪˈnæɡlərz/| n.|欺骗者，耍花招的人 | Someone who uses tricks or deceptions to achieve their goals.  They often manipulate situations or people to get what they want. |The finaglers managed to get extra funding for their project through some questionable accounting practices.  那些欺骗者通过一些可疑的会计手段设法为他们的项目获得了额外资金。|近义词：trickster, swindler, con artist, manipulator |反义词：honest person, truthful person


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He finagles his way into exclusive parties. = 他耍花招混进独家派对。
They finagle discounts from vendors. = 他们向供应商讨价还价。

一般过去时:
