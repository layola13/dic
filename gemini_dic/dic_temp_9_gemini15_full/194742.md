selfs:/selfz/| n.|自身，自己(复数)|The word "selfs" is the plural form of "self," though rarely used in modern English.  It refers to one's own selves or individual identities.  Its usage is largely archaic or poetic, often found in older texts.|例句：The multiple selves within a single person can be a source of conflict.  (一个人内心的多个自我可能成为冲突的根源。)  The different selves of the artist were reflected in his diverse works. (艺术家的不同自我反映在他的各种作品中。) |近义词：selves, oneself(s) |反义词: others, other people.
