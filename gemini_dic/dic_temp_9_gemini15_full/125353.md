multilobar:/ˌmʌltiˈloʊbɑːr/| adj. |多叶的；多叶状的 | Having or relating to multiple lobes.  The term is frequently used in medical contexts, particularly in reference to organs with lobes, such as the lungs, liver, or brain.  | Example: The patient presented with multilobar pneumonia.  患者表现为多叶性肺炎。|近义词: multilobular, polylobar |反义词: unilober, unilobar

词源:
