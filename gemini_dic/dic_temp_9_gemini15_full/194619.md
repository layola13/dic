self-tempted:/selfˈtɛmptɪd/| adj.| 自我诱惑的，自己引诱自己的 |  Describes someone who is tempted by their own desires or impulses, often leading to self-destructive behavior. It suggests a deliberate act of giving in to temptation rather than being externally influenced. | Example: He felt self-tempted to abandon his diet and eat the entire cake. 他感到自己忍不住要放弃节食，吃掉整个蛋糕。|近义词：self-indulgent, self-destructive|反义词：self-controlled, disciplined|
