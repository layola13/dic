obliviousnesses:/əˈblɪviəsnəsɪz/| n.| 漠视；健忘；疏忽；不注意 | The plural form of obliviousness, referring to multiple instances of being unaware or unmindful.  It signifies repeated instances of inattention or forgetfulness. | Example:  The obliviousnesses of the management led to a series of costly mistakes. 管理层的多次疏忽导致了一系列代价高昂的错误。 |近义词：unawarenesses, inattentions, neglects |反义词：attentivenesses, mindfulnesses
