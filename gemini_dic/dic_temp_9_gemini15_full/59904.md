edema:/ɪˈdiːmə/| n.|水肿，浮肿|Abnormal accumulation of fluid in interstitial spaces of tissues.  It causes swelling.  Edema can be localized or generalized, and can be caused by various factors such as heart failure, kidney disease, or allergies.|例句：The patient presented with significant edema in his lower extremities.  这位病人下肢严重水肿。|例句：Allergic reactions can sometimes cause facial edema. 过敏反应有时会导致面部水肿。|近义词：swelling, puffiness, dropsy|反义词：dehydration
