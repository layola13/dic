skinbound: /ˈskɪnbaʊnd/ | adj. | 皮肤紧绷的；皮肤干燥的；被束缚的 |  Covered or bound by skin; parched and dry.  It can also figuratively describe someone constrained or restricted. | 例句：The old man's skin was skinbound and cracked from years of sun exposure.  = 老人的皮肤因多年的日晒而干燥开裂。


The desert traveler felt skinbound by the relentless heat and lack of water.  =  沙漠旅行者感到被无情的酷暑和缺水所束缚。| 近义词：parched, dry, constrained, restricted, bound | 反义词：hydrated, supple, free, liberated |
