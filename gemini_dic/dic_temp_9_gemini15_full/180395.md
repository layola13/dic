refuted:/rɪˈfjuːtɪd/ | v. |驳斥；反驳；否认| To prove (a statement or theory) to be wrong or false; to deny the truth or accuracy of something. |例句：The scientist refuted the claim that the Earth is flat.  科学家驳斥了地球是平的这一说法。

例句：His arguments were completely refuted by new evidence. 他的论点被新的证据完全驳斥了。


近义词：contradicted, denied, disproved, rebutted | 反义词：confirmed, verified, substantiated, supported |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refutes the accusations against him. = 他驳斥了针对他的指控。
The study refutes previous findings. = 这项研究驳斥了之前的研究结果。


一般过去时:
She refuted his argument with compelling evidence. = 她用令人信服的证据驳斥了他的论点。
The lawyer refuted the witness's testimony. = 律师驳斥了证人的证词。


现在完成时:
Scientists have refuted the theory of spontaneous generation. = 科学家们已经驳斥了自然发生论。
They have refuted all the charges leveled against them. = 他们已经驳斥了所有针对他们的指控。


过去完成时:
He had refuted similar claims in previous publications. = 他之前在出版物中驳斥过类似的主张。
The defense had refuted the prosecution's key witness. = 防御方已经驳斥了检方的关键证人。


将来时:
She will refute the allegations in her upcoming speech. = 她将在即将发表的演讲中驳斥这些指控。
The expert will refute the flawed methodology of the study. = 专家将驳斥该研究有缺陷的方法论。


虚拟语气:
If he had more evidence, he would refute the accusations more effectively. = 如果他有更多证据，他就能更有效地驳斥这些指控。
I wish I could refute the rumors circulating about me. = 我希望我能驳斥关于我的谣言。


被动语态:
His theory was refuted by subsequent research. = 他的理论被后续研究驳斥了。
The argument was refuted by several experts. = 这一论点被几位专家驳斥了。


疑问句:
How can we refute such a widespread misconception? = 我们如何才能驳斥这种普遍的误解？
Has anyone refuted this claim before? = 之前有人驳斥过这一说法吗？


否定句:
He could not refute the evidence presented against him. = 他无法驳斥针对他的证据。
They failed to refute the accusations convincingly. = 他们未能令人信服地驳斥这些指控。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "refute" comes from the Latin word "refutare," meaning "to repulse, drive back," which in turn stems from "re-" (back) and "futare" (to strike, push).  The original meaning involved physically pushing back, and the semantic shift to "disprove" occurred over time.
