intercomparison:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmpərɪzn/ | n. |相互比较；对比；对照 |The act of comparing two or more things to find similarities and differences.  It often involves a systematic and objective evaluation of data or results from different sources. |例句：The intercomparison of the two methods showed significant discrepancies.  两个方法的相互比较显示出显著的差异。


例句：The study included an intercomparison of results from different laboratories. 这项研究包括了来自不同实验室的结果的相互比较。


近义词：comparison, contrast, collation, benchmarking


反义词：N/A  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与“独立评估”或“单独分析”相对)


词性：名词
