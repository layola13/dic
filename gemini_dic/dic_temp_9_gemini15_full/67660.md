extruction:/ɪkˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. | 提取；萃取；抽出；挤压 | The process of removing something from a substance.  This can refer to the physical removal of a material from a larger mass, or the separation of a component from a mixture using chemical or physical means.  |例句：The extraction of oil from the ground is a complex process.  从地下开采石油是一个复杂的过程。

例句：The extraction of caffeine from coffee beans is a common industrial process. 从咖啡豆中提取咖啡因是一个常见的工业过程。


近义词：removal, separation, extraction, derivation | 反义词：insertion, addition, incorporation | 词性: 名词
