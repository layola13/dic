/ˈhʌlərz/| n. |去壳机；脱粒机|A huller is a machine used to remove the hulls or outer coverings from grains, seeds, or nuts.  It's commonly used in agriculture and food processing to prepare grains for consumption or further processing.  |例句：The rice huller separated the grain from the husk efficiently.  这台碾米机有效地将谷粒与谷壳分离。

例句：The farmer used a huller to remove the shells from the peanuts before selling them. 农民在出售花生之前用脱壳机去掉了花生壳。
