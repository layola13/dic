rhapsodies:/ˈræpsədiz/| n. |狂想曲；热情洋溢的表达 |A rhapsody is a literary or musical piece characterized by a wildly emotional, unrestrained, and often ecstatic expression.  It's typically a composition that is free-flowing and lacks a strict formal structure.  Rhapsodies often express intense feelings or ideas in a spontaneous and exuberant manner.|例句：Her speech was a rhapsody of praise for the team's achievements.  她的演讲是对团队成就的热情洋溢的赞美。

The composer's latest work is a stunning rhapsody for piano and orchestra. 作曲家的最新作品是一首令人惊叹的钢琴与管弦乐队的狂想曲。


近义词：ecstasy, effusion, outburst, outpouring |反义词：monotony, restraint, calmness, composure |词性:名词
