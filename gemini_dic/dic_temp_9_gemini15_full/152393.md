pavian:/pæˈviːən/| n.|狒狒|A large, strong, and often aggressive monkey with a dog-like face and powerful jaws, inhabiting Africa and Arabia.|例句：The troop of pavians moved swiftly through the savanna.  一群狒狒迅速穿过稀树草原。

例句：We observed a large male pavian guarding his harem. 我们观察到一只大型雄性狒狒守护着它的妻妾。


近义词：baboon (近义词，但baboon通常指较大的狒狒种类)|反义词：None (没有合适的反义词)|词性:名词
