cedar's:/ˈsiːdər z/| n. |雪松的；雪松树的 |The possessive form of "cedar," referring to something belonging to or associated with a cedar tree.  It indicates ownership or relationship. |例句：The cedar's scent filled the air.  雪松的香味充满了空气。 |例句：The cedar's branches reached towards the sky. 雪松的枝条伸向天空。|近义词：pine's (松树的), juniper's (杜松的) |反义词:  N/A
