limation:/ˌlɪmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|消除；去除；终止|The elimination of poverty is a major goal. 消除贫困是一个主要目标。|近义词：removal, eradication, exclusion |反义词：inclusion, retention, preservation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company is implementing a plan for the elimination of waste. = 公司正在实施一项消除浪费的计划。
Elimination of errors is a key part of quality control. = 消除错误是质量控制的关键部分。


一般过去时:
The team celebrated the elimination of their rivals from the tournament. = 球队庆祝他们在比赛中淘汰了对手。
The doctor discussed the elimination of the infection with the patient. = 医生与病人讨论了感染的消除。


现在完成时:
The new policy has resulted in the elimination of many bureaucratic obstacles. = 新政策导致许多官僚障碍被消除。
Scientists have achieved significant progress in the elimination of diseases. = 科学家们在疾病消除方面取得了重大进展。


过去完成时:
The judge had ordered the elimination of certain evidence from the trial. = 法官已下令从审判中消除某些证据。
The team had already planned for the elimination of potential problems. = 球队已经计划好了消除潜在的问题。


将来时:
The government will implement measures for the elimination of pollution. = 政府将实施措施以消除污染。
They will discuss strategies for the elimination of hunger in the coming months. = 他们将在未来几个月讨论消除饥饿的策略。


虚拟语气:
If we eliminated all the unnecessary steps, the process would be much more efficient. = 如果我们取消所有不必要的步骤，这个过程将会更高效。
I wish they would consider the elimination of those harmful chemicals. = 我希望他们会考虑消除那些有害的化学物质。


被动语态:
The problem was solved by the elimination of the faulty component. = 问题通过消除故障部件得到了解决。
Many species are threatened by habitat elimination. = 许多物种都受到栖息地消失的威胁。


疑问句:
What is the best method for elimination of this weed? = 消除这种杂草的最佳方法是什么？
What measures are being taken for the elimination of traffic congestion? = 正在采取什么措施来消除交通拥堵？


否定句:
The plan did not lead to the elimination of the problem. = 该计划并没有导致问题的解决。
The company failed to achieve the elimination of waste as promised. = 公司未能实现如期消除浪费的目标。

词源:
源自拉丁语 *elimināre，由ex-（出，外）+ līmen（门槛）构成，字面意思是“把…从门槛上移开”，引申为“除去，排除”。
