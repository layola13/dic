preconsent: /ˌpriːkənˈsɛnt/ | n. | 预先同意；预先许可 |  Prior consent; agreement given in advance.  It often refers to consent obtained before a procedure or research begins, ensuring participants understand the implications before involvement.  | Example:  The study required preconsent from all participants before data collection commenced.  = 这项研究要求在数据收集开始之前获得所有参与者的预先同意。

The researcher obtained preconsent from the subjects. = 研究人员获得了受试者的预先同意。 |近义词：prior agreement, advance approval, preliminary consent | 反义词：post-consent, subsequent approval | 名词
