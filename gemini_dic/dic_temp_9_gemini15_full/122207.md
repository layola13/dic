misutilization: /ˌmɪsˌjuːtɪləˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |误用，滥用 | The improper or wrong use of something.  It implies a misuse that is harmful or inefficient. |例句：The misutilization of company funds led to a financial crisis. 公司资金的滥用导致了金融危机。

The misutilization of antibiotics contributes to antibiotic resistance. 抗生素的滥用导致抗生素耐药性。|近义词：abuse, misuse, maltreatment |反义词：proper use, correct application |名词
