rewears:/riːˈwɛrz/| v.|重新穿;再次穿着|To wear (something) again.  This word is a relatively new formation, not found in standard dictionaries, but understandable from its component parts.|例句：I rewear my favorite jeans at least twice a week. = 我一周至少穿两次我最喜欢的牛仔裤。|例句：She often re-wears dresses to different events. = 她经常把同一件衣服穿去不同的场合。|近义词：reuse, wear again|反义词：discard, throw away, dispose of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She re-wears her clothes often. = 她经常重新穿她的衣服。
He re-wears his suits for business meetings. = 他把西装重新穿去商务会议。


一般过去时:
I re-wore that dress to the party. = 我把那件衣服重新穿去参加派对。
They re-wore their costumes for the second performance. = 他们把戏服重新穿去参加第二次演出。


现在完成时:
She has re-worn that sweater several times this month. = 这个月她已经把那件毛衣重新穿过几次了。
They have re-worn their hiking boots on multiple occasions. = 他们已经多次重新穿他们的徒步旅行靴。


过去完成时:
He had re-worn his lucky shirt before the game. = 在比赛之前，他已经重新穿上了他的幸运衫。
She had re-worn the same dress to two weddings. = 她已经把同一件衣服重新穿去参加过两次婚礼。


将来时:
I will re-wear this outfit for the next event. = 我将把这套衣服重新穿去参加下一次活动。
They will re-wear their graduation gowns for the family photoshoot. = 他们将重新穿上毕业礼服进行家庭拍摄。


虚拟语气:
If I had a nicer dress, I would re-wear it. = 如果我有一件更漂亮的衣服，我会重新穿它。
If she had time, she would re-wear her favorite outfit. = 如果她有时间，她会重新穿上她最喜欢的衣服。


被动语态:  (This verb is rarely, if ever, used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:

否定句:
