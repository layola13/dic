openers:/ˈoʊpənərz/ | n. | 开场白；开瓶器；（对话等的）起始部分 |  Something that opens or begins something else,  such as a conversation, a performance, or a container.  It can refer to the first words spoken in a conversation, the initial act in a play, or a tool used to open something. | 例句：He used a clever opener to start the conversation. 他用了一个巧妙的开场白来开始谈话。

The bartender showed off his impressive array of bottle openers. 酒保炫耀了他令人印象深刻的开瓶器收藏。


近义词：introductions, beginnings, initiators, starters | 反义词：closers, conclusions, endings
