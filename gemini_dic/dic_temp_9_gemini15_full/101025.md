invasion:/ɪnˈveɪʒən/| n.|入侵，侵略，侵犯|The unwelcome intrusion of something into a place or situation.|The Norman invasion of England in 1066 changed the course of history.  诺曼人1066年入侵英格兰改变了历史进程。The invasion of privacy is a serious concern. 侵犯隐私是一个严重的问题。|occupation, incursion, raid|withdrawal, retreat, liberation|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army invades the country. = 军队入侵该国。
Pollution invades our environment. = 污染侵入了我们的环境。

一般过去时:
The Vikings invaded Britain. = 维京人入侵了不列颠。
The pests invaded the garden. = 害虫侵入了花园。

现在完成时:
The insects have invaded the house. = 昆虫已经入侵了房子。
Foreign companies have invaded the local market. = 外国公司已经入侵了当地市场。

过去完成时:
The enemy had invaded the city before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，敌人已经入侵了城市。
The disease had invaded his body before he sought medical attention. = 他寻求医疗救助之前，疾病已经侵入了他的身体。

将来时:
The enemy will invade our territory next week. = 敌人下周将入侵我们的领土。
The weeds will invade the lawn if we don't take action. = 如果我们不采取行动，杂草将会入侵草坪。

虚拟语气:
If the enemy invaded, we would fight back. = 如果敌人入侵，我们将反击。
I wish the mosquitoes hadn't invaded my room. = 我真希望蚊子没有入侵我的房间。

被动语态:
The country was invaded by the enemy forces. = 该国被敌军入侵了。
The garden was invaded by weeds. = 花园被杂草入侵了。

疑问句:
Was the country invaded by a foreign power? = 该国是被外国势力入侵的吗？
Have the pests invaded our crops? = 害虫入侵了我们的庄稼吗？

否定句:
The army did not invade the neighboring country. = 军队没有入侵邻国。
The disease did not invade his vital organs. = 疾病没有入侵他的重要器官。
