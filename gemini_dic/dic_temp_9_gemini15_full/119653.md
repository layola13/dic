middleman: /ˈmɪdlˌmæn/ | n. |中间人，中间商 | A person who acts as an intermediary between two other people or groups, especially in business.  | Example: The art dealer acted as a middleman between the artist and the buyer.  这位艺术品经销商充当了艺术家和买家之间的中间人。|近义词: intermediary, go-between, agent, broker | 反义词: principal, direct participant

词源:
The word "middleman" is a compound word formed from "middle" and "man".  "Middle" indicates the intermediary position, and "man" refers to the person fulfilling that role.  The term's usage reflects its origins in business and trade, where individuals acted as intermediaries to connect buyers and sellers.
