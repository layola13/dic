equivocate:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪt/ | vt. |含糊其辞；支吾其词；模棱两可地说 | To use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.  |例句：He equivocated when asked about his involvement in the scandal. 他被问及卷入丑闻一事时闪烁其词。

例句：The politician's response was full of equivocation, leaving the public confused.  这位政治家的回应含糊其辞，使公众感到困惑。


近义词：prevaricate, hedge, obfuscate, tergiversate, straddle


反义词：clarify, elucidate, state clearly


词性:动词
