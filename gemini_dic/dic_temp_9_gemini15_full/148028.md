overromanticize:/ˌoʊvərroʊˈmænṭɪsaɪz/| vt.|过分浪漫化；对…怀有不切实际的浪漫幻想|To exaggerate the romantic aspects of something; to view something through rose-tinted glasses.|例句：He overromanticized their relationship, ignoring the frequent arguments and disagreements. 他过分浪漫化了他们的关系，忽略了频繁的争吵和分歧。

例句：Don't overromanticize the past; it wasn't always perfect. 不要过分美化过去；它并不总是完美的。


近义词：idealize, romanticize excessively, sentimentalize


反义词：be realistic about, be pragmatic about


词性:动词
