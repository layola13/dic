guile:/ɡaɪl/| n.| 狡猾；奸诈；欺骗；诡计 | Cunning, deceit, or trickery.  It often involves cleverness and skill in deceiving others. | 例句：He used his guile to win the argument. 他用他的狡猾赢得了这场辩论。

例句：Her guile was evident in her charming but manipulative behavior.  她的狡猾在她迷人但具有操纵性的行为中显而易见。


近义词：craft, cunning, deception, trickery, shrewdness, artifice

反义词：honesty, frankness, sincerity, ingenuousness


词源：古英语 *gīl,  meaning "fraud, deceit".  Related to Old High German *gīlī "guile".
