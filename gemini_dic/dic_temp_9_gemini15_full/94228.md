imperially: /ɪmˈpɪəriəli/ | adv. | 帝王般地；专制地；以帝国的方式 | In a manner characteristic of an empire or emperor; with imperial authority or power.  |例句：He ruled imperially, brooking no dissent. 他以帝王般的方式统治，不容许任何异议。 |例句：The laws were enforced imperially, with little regard for local customs. 这些法律以专制的方式执行，几乎不考虑当地习俗。|近义词：royally, regally, autocratically |反义词：democratically, republicanly
