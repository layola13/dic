jalapenos:/ˌdʒæləˈpeɪnoʊz/| n. |墨西哥辣椒，哈拉比诺辣椒|A type of small, hot chili pepper, typically green, with a pungent flavor. They are widely used in Mexican cuisine.|例句：I added some jalapenos to my chili for extra spice. 我在我的辣椒里加了一些墨西哥辣椒来增加辣味。

例句：The pizza was topped with jalapenos and pepperoni. 比萨饼上面放着墨西哥辣椒和意大利辣香肠。


近义词：chili peppers, jalapeños (itself is often used as both singular and plural)

反义词：sweet peppers (e.g., bell peppers)


词源:The word "jalapeño" is derived from the Nahuatl word "xalāpan," which refers to the city of Xalapa in Veracruz, Mexico, where the peppers are believed to have originated.  The suffix "-eno" is a common Spanish suffix indicating origin or belonging to a place.
