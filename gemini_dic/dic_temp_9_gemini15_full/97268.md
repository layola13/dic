inflows:/ˈɪnfloʊz/ | n. |流入；资金流入；涌入 |The movement of something into a place, especially money into a country or organization.  |例句：The company experienced significant inflows of cash after the successful product launch. 公司在产品成功发布后经历了大量的现金流入。|例句：Heavy inflows of tourists overwhelmed the small town. 大量的游客涌入使这个小镇不堪重负。|近义词：influx, influxion, inflow, accession |反义词：outflows, outflow, efflux


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river experiences regular inflows of fresh water from the melting glaciers. = 这条河定期从融化的冰川获得淡水流入。
Significant capital inflows are boosting the nation's economy. = 大量的资本流入正在推动国家的经济增长。

一般过去时:
Last year's inflows of foreign investment were higher than expected. = 去年外国投资流入超过预期。
The dam stopped the inflows of floodwater into the city. = 水坝阻止了洪水流入城市。

现在完成时:
There have been substantial inflows of refugees into the country this year. = 今年已有大量难民涌入该国。
The company has seen consistent inflows of revenue since implementing the new marketing strategy. = 自从实施新的营销策略以来，公司一直有稳定的收入流入。

过去完成时:
Before the storm, there had been minimal inflows of water into the reservoir. = 在暴风雨来临之前，水库的进水量极少。
By the end of the quarter, the project had already received significant inflows of funding. = 到季度末，该项目已经获得了大量的资金流入。


将来时:
We anticipate increased inflows of tourists during the summer months. = 我们预计夏季几个月游客数量将会增加。
The government projects larger inflows of tax revenue next year. = 政府预计明年税收收入将会增加。

虚拟语气:
If there were greater inflows of investment, the region would prosper. = 如果有更多投资流入，该地区将会繁荣。
I wish there were more inflows of talented workers into the industry. = 我希望这个行业能有更多有才华的工人涌入。

被动语态:
The reservoir is filled by inflows from several mountain streams. = 水库是由几条山间溪流的流入而充满的。
The economy was negatively impacted by the sudden halt of foreign inflows. = 经济受到了外国资金流入突然停止的负面影响。

疑问句:
What are the main inflows of water into this lake? = 这湖的主要水源是什么？
Are there any significant inflows of capital expected in the near future? = 近期是否有预期的大量资本流入？

否定句:
