pitcher:/ˈpɪtʃər/| n. |投手；水罐；大水壶 | A pitcher is a person who throws the ball in baseball.  It can also refer to a container for liquids, typically with a handle and a spout. |例句：He's the best pitcher on the team. 他是队里最好的投手。

The pitcher of water was empty. 水罐空了。|近义词：jug, carafe (for containers); hurler (for baseball)|反义词：batter (in baseball)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pitches for the Yankees. = 他为扬基队投球。
She pours water from the pitcher. = 她从水壶里倒水。

一般过去时:
