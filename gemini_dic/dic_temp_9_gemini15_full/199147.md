silica:/ˈsɪlɪkə/| n. |二氧化硅，硅石|Silicon dioxide, a compound of silicon and oxygen, that occurs abundantly in nature as quartz and in various other minerals.  It is used in the manufacture of glass, ceramics, and abrasives.|例句：Glass is primarily made of silica.  玻璃主要由二氧化硅制成。|例句：Many gemstones are composed of silica in various crystalline forms. 许多宝石是由各种结晶形式的二氧化硅组成的。|近义词：quartz, silicon dioxide|反义词：无
