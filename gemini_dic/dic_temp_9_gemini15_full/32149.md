chew:/tʃuː/| vt. |咀嚼；细想；考虑|To crush and grind with the teeth.|He chewed his food thoroughly before swallowing. 他细嚼慢咽地吃东西。|The problem chewed at his mind.  这个问题困扰着他。|bite, masticate|swallow, gulp|verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chews his food slowly. = 他慢慢地咀嚼食物。
She chews gum all day. = 她整天嚼口香糖。

一般过去时:
He chewed the gum until it lost its flavor. = 他嚼口香糖直到它失去味道。
She chewed on the problem all night. = 她整晚都在思考这个问题。

现在完成时:
I have chewed my nails for years. = 我啃指甲已经很多年了。
He has chewed through the entire rope. = 他已经咬断了整条绳子。

过去完成时:
He had chewed all the food before the waiter came. = 服务员来之前他已经把所有食物都嚼完了。
She had chewed on the idea for weeks before deciding. = 她考虑这个主意好几个星期才决定。

将来时:
I will chew on this proposal before I make a decision. = 我会在做决定之前仔细考虑这个提案。
They will chew the cud after their long journey. = 长途跋涉后，他们会反刍。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had chewed on that investment opportunity before rejecting it. = 我真希望在拒绝那个投资机会之前，我好好考虑过。
