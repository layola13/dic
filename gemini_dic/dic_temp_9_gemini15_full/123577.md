monstruous:/ˈmɒnstrəs/ /ˈmɑːnstrəs/ | adj. |巨大的；可怕的；畸形的；极坏的|extremely large or great; abnormally large; shockingly bad or excessive; of or resembling a monster;  |例句：The monstrous waves crashed against the shore. 巨浪拍打着海岸。

The monstrous task took him years to complete. 这项艰巨的任务花了他数年时间才完成。


近义词：huge, enormous, gigantic, terrible, awful, dreadful, atrocious|反义词：tiny, minuscule, insignificant, wonderful, delightful, pleasant

词源：源自于古法语monstrueux，最终源于拉丁语monstrum，意为“怪物，预兆”。
