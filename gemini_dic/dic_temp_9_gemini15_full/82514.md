gsm:/dʒiːɛsˈɛm/ | n. | 每秒千位字节；全球移动通讯系统 |  Global System for Mobile communications, a standard developed for second-generation (2G) digital cellular technology. It is used for text messaging and voice calls on mobile phones.  | Example: My phone uses GSM technology. 我的手机使用GSM技术。 | 近义词: 2G, cellular network | 反义词: N/A
