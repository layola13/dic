lily-pads:/ˈlɪliˌpædz/| n.|睡莲叶，莲叶|The flat, round leaves of a water lily, that float on the surface of the water.  Lily-pads are often used as a habitat for various aquatic animals.|例句：The frogs sat on the lily-pads, basking in the sun. 青蛙坐在睡莲叶上，晒着太阳。

The ducks were swimming amongst the lily-pads. 鸭子在睡莲叶之间游泳。|近义词：water lily leaves, water-lily pads|反义词：无
