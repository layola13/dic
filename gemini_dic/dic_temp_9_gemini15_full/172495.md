pyramid:/ˈpɪrəmɪd/| n.|金字塔；锥体；(等级制度的)尖端|A pyramid is a structure with a square or triangular base and sloping sides that meet in a point at the top.  It is often a monumental structure.  The term can also refer to something shaped like a pyramid, or a hierarchical structure with a few people at the top and many at the bottom.|例句：The Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.  吉萨大金字塔是古代世界七大奇迹之一。

The company's organizational structure is a pyramid, with the CEO at the top. 这家公司的组织结构是一个金字塔，首席执行官位于顶端。|近义词：cone, spire, apex|反义词：base, foundation|名词
