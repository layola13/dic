retorting:/rɪˈtɔːrtɪŋ/| v.| 反驳；回嘴；反击 |  The act of replying sharply or angrily to a remark or accusation.  |例句：He spent the rest of the evening retorting to every point his opponent made. 他花了剩下的晚上反驳对手提出的每一项论点。

例句：She retorted angrily to his accusations. 她愤怒地反驳了他的指控。


近义词：rebutting, replying sharply, answering back, countering

反义词：agreeing, conceding, acquiescing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retorts to every criticism. = 他反驳每一个批评。
She retorts with a witty remark. = 她以一句俏皮话反驳。


一般过去时:
He retorted sharply to the insult. = 他尖锐地反驳了侮辱。
She retorted that she wasn't responsible. = 她反驳说她不负责任。


现在完成时:
He has retorted to all the accusations against him. = 他已经反驳了所有针对他的指控。
She has retorted with evidence to support her claims. = 她已经用证据反驳来支持她的说法。


过去完成时:
He had retorted before I could even speak. = 我还没来得及说话，他就已经反驳了。
She had retorted with a sarcastic comment. = 她已经用一句讽刺的评论反驳了。


将来时:
He will retort with a strong defense. = 他将会以强有力的辩护来反驳。
She will retort by quoting the law. = 她将通过引用法律来反驳。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would retort to that statement. = 如果他在的话，他会反驳那句话。
I wish I had retorted more effectively. = 我希望我当时能更有效地反驳。


被动语态:
His arguments were retorted against effectively. = 他的论点被有效地反驳了。
Her statement was retorted by several speakers. = 她的声明被几位发言人反驳了。


疑问句:
How did he retort to the question? = 他如何反驳那个问题？
Will she retort to the challenge? = 她会反驳这个挑战吗？


否定句:
He didn't retort to my accusation. = 他没有反驳我的指控。
She didn't retort, but merely sighed. = 她没有反驳，只是叹了口气。


PHRASE->
retort angrily = 愤怒地反驳
retort sarcastically = 讽刺地反驳
retort sharply = 尖锐地反驳
retort with a smile = 微笑着反驳
retort defensively = 防御性地反驳

词源:
From the Old French *retorter, from the Latin *re- + torquēre "to twist," "to turn."  The original sense referred to the twisting or turning of something, such as in a retort (a type of laboratory vessel). The figurative sense of "to reply sharply" developed later.
