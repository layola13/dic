self-valuations:/ˌsɛlfˌvæljuˈeɪʃənz/| n. |自我评价，自我评估 |The process of individuals assessing their own skills, abilities, and worth.  It often involves reflecting on past performance, identifying strengths and weaknesses, and setting goals for future development. |例句：The company requires all employees to complete annual self-valuations. 公司要求所有员工每年都要进行自我评价。

例句：Her self-valuations revealed a surprising lack of self-confidence despite her many accomplishments.  她的自我评价显示出尽管她取得了许多成就，但却出人意料地缺乏自信。


近义词：self-assessments, appraisals, self-assessments, self-analysis |反义词：external evaluation, peer review |词性:名词
