entwine:/ɪnˈtwaɪn/ | vt. | 缠绕；纠缠；交织 | To twist or twine together; to interweave or interconnect.  |例句：The ivy entwined itself around the old oak tree.  常春藤缠绕在古老的橡树上。
The two stories entwined throughout the novel. 这两个故事在小说中交织在一起。 |近义词：intertwine, interweave, braid, twist |反义词：separate, disentangle
