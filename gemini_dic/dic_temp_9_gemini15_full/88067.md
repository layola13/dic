highlandry:/ˈhaɪləndri/| n. |高地生活；高地习俗；高地人；高地文化|The way of life, customs, and culture of people living in highland regions.  Highlandry often involves unique traditions, practices, and social structures adapted to the mountainous terrain and climate.|Example: The museum showcased a fascinating exhibit on the highlandry of the Scottish Highlands.  博物馆展示了关于苏格兰高地高地人生活的迷人展览。|近义词：highland culture, mountain life, pastoralism|反义词：lowland life, urban life|名词
