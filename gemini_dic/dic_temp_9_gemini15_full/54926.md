disposited:/dɪˈspəʊzɪt/ | v. |处理，处置；安排；倾向于|To dispose of something means to get rid of it, often by throwing it away or destroying it.  It can also mean to arrange or settle something, or to have a particular tendency or inclination.|例句：The company disposed of its waste responsibly.  公司负责任地处理了它的废物。

The judge disposed of the case quickly.  法官迅速处理了此案。|近义词：dispose, get rid of, arrange, settle|反义词：retain, keep, accumulate|动词


USAGE->

将来时:
