sinusitis:/ˌsaɪnəˈsaɪtɪs/| n. |鼻窦炎|Inflammation of the sinuses.  Sinusitis is a common condition that causes inflammation and swelling in the tissue lining the sinuses.|例句：My sinusitis is acting up again; I need to see a doctor.  我的鼻窦炎又发作了；我需要去看医生。|近义词：sinus infection, rhinosinusitis |反义词：None
