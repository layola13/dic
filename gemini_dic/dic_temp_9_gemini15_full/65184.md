ethologic:/ˌeθəˈlɒdʒɪk/|/ˌiːθəˈlɒdʒɪk/| adj.|行为学的；伦理学的|Relating to the scientific study of animal behavior.  Ethology focuses on observable behaviors in natural settings and their evolutionary and ecological significance. |Example:  Ethologic studies of primate behavior have revealed complex social structures. 行为学对灵长类动物行为的研究揭示了复杂的社会结构。|近义词:behavioral, biological|反义词:N/A
