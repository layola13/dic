reornament:/ˌriːˈɔːrnəmənt/ | v. | 再装饰，重新装饰 | To decorate or adorn again; to redecorate.  The word is a combination of "re-" (again) and "ornament" (to decorate). It implies a second or renewed decoration, often after a period of disrepair or a change in style. | Example:  They decided to reornament the entire house after the renovation. 他们决定装修后重新装饰整栋房子。| 近义词：redecorate, refurbish, renovate | 反义词：strip, dismantle
