mother-ships:/ˈmʌðərʃɪps/ | n. |母舰，旗舰 | A large ship that carries and supports smaller ships or aircraft.  It serves as a base of operations and resupply for smaller vessels. |例句：The aircraft carrier served as the mother-ship for the fighter jets.  这艘航空母舰充当战斗机的母舰。

例句：The expedition used a large research vessel as a mother-ship, launching smaller boats for exploration. 探险队使用一艘大型研究船作为母舰，发射小型船只进行探险。


近义词：flagship, command ship, base ship |反义词：None.
