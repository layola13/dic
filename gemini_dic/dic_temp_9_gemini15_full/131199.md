nonbureaucratic:/ˌnɒnbjʊərəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. |非官僚的；效率高的|Not characterized by bureaucracy; efficient and informal.  This adjective describes systems, processes, or organizations that avoid the complexities and inefficiencies often associated with bureaucracy. It emphasizes simplicity, speed, and directness. | Example: The company prides itself on its nonbureaucratic approach to problem-solving, enabling quick responses to customer needs.  这家公司以其非官僚的解决问题方法而自豪，能够快速响应客户的需求。|近义词：efficient, streamlined, uncomplicated, informal |反义词：bureaucratic, cumbersome, inefficient, complex
