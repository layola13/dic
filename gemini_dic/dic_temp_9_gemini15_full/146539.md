overglamourizing: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |过度美化；过分渲染 |  To exaggerate the glamour or attractiveness of something; to present something in an excessively glamorous or idealized way. |例句：The advertisement overglamourized the product, making it seem far better than it actually was.  广告对产品进行了过度美化，使其看起来比实际情况好得多。

例句：The movie overglamourizes the life of a rock star, ignoring the struggles and hardships. 这部电影过分美化了摇滚明星的生活，忽略了其中的挣扎和苦难。


近义词：romanticizing, idealizing, embellishing, aggrandizing


反义词：understating, downplaying, minimizing


词性：动词
