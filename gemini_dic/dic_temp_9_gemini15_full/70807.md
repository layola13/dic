fingers: /ˈfɪŋɡərz/ | n. |手指|Fingers are the five slender, jointed parts at the end of a human hand, used for grasping, feeling, and manipulating objects.  They are essential for dexterity and fine motor skills. |例句：She pointed at the map with her fingers. 她用手指指着地图。

例句：I hurt my fingers while chopping vegetables. 切菜时我伤到了手指。


近义词：digits (formal), toes (on feet) |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|词性：名词
