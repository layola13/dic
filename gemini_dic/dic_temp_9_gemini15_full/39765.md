consolidating: /kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |巩固；加强；合并 |  The process of making something stronger or more solid; combining things into a single, more effective unit. |例句：The company is consolidating its operations in order to become more efficient. 公司正在整合其业务以提高效率。

例句：The government is consolidating its power. 政府正在巩固其权力。


近义词：strengthen, reinforce, unify, merge, combine | 反义词：weaken, divide, separate, fragment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is consolidating his position in the company. 他正在巩固他在公司的地位。
They are consolidating their resources. 他们正在整合他们的资源。


一般过去时:
She consolidated her notes after the lecture. 讲座结束后，她整理了笔记。
The two companies consolidated their businesses last year. 这两家公司去年合并了业务。


现在完成时:
We have consolidated our market share. 我们已经巩固了我们的市场份额。
They have consolidated their debt. 他们已经整合了他们的债务。


过去完成时:
He had consolidated his power before the rebellion. 他在叛乱之前巩固了他的权力。
The team had consolidated their strategy before the game. 球队在比赛前巩固了他们的策略。


将来时:
The government will consolidate its control over the region. 政府将巩固其对该地区的控制。
The company will consolidate its operations next year. 公司明年将整合其业务。


虚拟语气:
If we consolidated our efforts, we could achieve our goal. 如果我们整合我们的努力，我们就能实现我们的目标。
I wish we had consolidated our resources earlier. 我希望我们早点整合资源。


被动语态:
The accounts were consolidated at the end of the year. 这些账户在年底合并了。
The two departments are being consolidated. 这两个部门正在合并。


疑问句:
Are they consolidating their assets? 他们正在整合资产吗？
Is the company consolidating its branches? 公司正在整合其分公司吗？


否定句:
He did not consolidate his notes. 他没有整理笔记。
The company is not consolidating its operations at this time. 公司目前没有整合其业务。


PHRASE->
consolidate power: 巩固权力
consolidate gains: 保持优势
consolidate debt: 整合债务
consolidate learning: 巩固学习
consolidate resources: 整合资源

词源:
From Latin *consolidare, from com- "together" + solidus "solid".
