personalising: /ˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使个性化；定制；个人化 |  The act of making something more personal or unique to an individual.  This often involves tailoring something to fit a specific person's preferences, needs, or characteristics. | 例句：The website allows you to personalise your homepage with your favourite news sources. = 网站允许你用你最喜欢的新闻来源个性化你的主页。


The company is personalising its marketing campaigns to target specific customer segments. = 公司正在个性化其营销活动以瞄准特定客户群体。


近义词：customising, tailoring, individualising, adapting | 反义词：generalising, standardising


词性：动词


词源:  The word "personalising" is derived from the noun "person" and the suffix "-alising".  "-al" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns (personal), and "-ise" is a verb-forming suffix meaning "to make" or "to become".  Therefore, personalising means "to make personal" or "to give a personal character to".
