deformity:/dɪˈfɔːrməti/| n. |畸形，变形|A deformity is a condition where a part of the body is not formed or shaped correctly.  It can result from genetic factors, birth defects, accidents, or diseases.  The severity of a deformity can range from minor to severe, affecting both physical function and appearance.|例句：The child was born with a heart deformity.  这个孩子天生患有心脏畸形。

例句：His facial deformity was a result of a childhood accident. 他的面部畸形是童年事故造成的。


近义词：malformation, defect, abnormality |反义词：normality, perfection
