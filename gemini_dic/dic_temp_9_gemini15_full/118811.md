metamorphist:/mɛtəˈmɔːrfɪst/| n. |变形者；蜕变者；变质学家|A metamorphist is someone who undergoes or facilitates metamorphosis, either literally (like a butterfly) or figuratively (like someone undergoing a significant personal transformation).  The term can also refer to a specialist in metamorphic rocks. |例句：The caterpillar is a metamorphist, transforming into a beautiful butterfly.  毛毛虫是变形者，它会变成美丽的蝴蝶。

The artist, a true metamorphist, constantly reinvented his style.这位艺术家，一位真正的变形者，不断地重塑着自己的风格。|近义词：transformer, changer, alchemist (figuratively)|反义词：stagnant, unchanging
