outsing:/aʊtˈsɪŋ/| vt.|胜过（歌唱）；比…唱得好|To surpass in singing; to sing better than.  |例句：She outsang all the other contestants in the singing competition. 她在这场歌唱比赛中胜过所有其他选手。
|例句：The young soprano outsang the veteran tenor in the duet. 年轻的女高音在二重唱中胜过了资深男高音。|近义词：outperform, excel, surpass|反义词：be outsung by, be surpassed by|动词
