responsibilities:/ˌrɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlətiz/|/n.|责任；职责；义务|The state or fact of having a duty to deal with something or of having control over someone.|My responsibilities include managing the team and meeting deadlines. 我的职责包括管理团队和按时完成工作。|近义词：duties, obligations, tasks|反义词：privileges, freedoms|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your responsibilities in this project? = 你在这个项目中的职责是什么？
What responsibilities does this job entail? = 这份工作包含哪些责任？


一般过去时:
What were your responsibilities in your previous role? = 你在之前的职位上有什么职责？
What responsibilities did she have at the company? = 她在那家公司有什么责任？


现在完成时:
What responsibilities have you undertaken so far? = 到目前为止你承担了哪些责任？
What responsibilities has the team completed? = 团队完成了哪些责任？


过去完成时:
What responsibilities had you completed before the deadline? = 在截止日期之前你完成了哪些责任？
What responsibilities had they finished before the project ended? = 在项目结束之前他们完成了哪些责任？


将来时:
What will your responsibilities be in the new position? = 在新的职位上你的职责将会是什么？
What responsibilities will the company assign to him? = 公司会指派他哪些责任？


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I could take on more responsibilities. = 如果我有更多的时间，我可以承担更多的责任。
I wish I had fewer responsibilities. = 我希望我的责任少一些。


被动语态:
Many responsibilities were delegated to the junior staff. = 许多责任被委托给了初级员工。
The tasks were divided according to responsibilities. = 任务根据责任进行了划分。


疑问句:
Whose responsibilities are these? = 这些是谁的责任？
What are the responsibilities of a team leader? = 团队领导的责任是什么？


否定句:
He doesn't have many responsibilities at the moment. = 他目前没有多少责任。
She wasn't responsible for that aspect of the project.  = 她不负责项目的那个方面。


PHRASE->
take responsibility for = 对…负责
