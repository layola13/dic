dermaptera:/dɜːrˈmæptərə/| n.|革翅目昆虫|Dermaptera is an order of insects characterized by their cerci, which are forceps-like appendages at the end of their abdomen.  They are also known as earwigs.  They are mostly nocturnal and omnivorous, feeding on plants, insects, and decaying organic matter.|例句：Many species of dermaptera are beneficial to gardeners because they prey on other insects.|近义词：earwig (common name)|反义词: N/A
