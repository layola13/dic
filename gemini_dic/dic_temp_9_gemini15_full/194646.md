self-treated:/ˌsɛlfˈtriːtɪd/| adj.|自行治疗的，自我疗法的| Treated by oneself, without professional medical help.  |例句：He engaged in self-treated methods for his back pain, using over-the-counter pain relievers and hot compresses. 他采用自我疗法治疗背痛，使用非处方止痛药和热敷。|例句：Self-treated wounds can sometimes become infected if not properly cleaned and cared for. 自行处理的伤口如果没有得到适当的清洁和护理，有时会感染。|近义词：self-medicated, home-treated |反义词：professionally treated, medically treated
