quashing: /ˈkwɒʃɪŋ/ | /ˈkwɑːʃɪŋ/ | v. | 镇压；取消；驳回 |  The act of suppressing or putting an end to something, especially a rebellion, protest, or legal proceeding.  |

例句：

The police quickly quashed the riot. = 警方迅速平息了暴乱。

The judge quashed the conviction. = 法官撤销了定罪。


近义词：suppressing, crushing, overturning, annulling, voiding

反义词：upholding, confirming, supporting, endorsing


词源:

源自古法语 *quacher，意为“to crush, to quell”。  最终源自古高地德语 *wachan，意为“to shake, to totter”。
