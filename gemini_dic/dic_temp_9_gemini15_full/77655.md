gelded:/ˈɡɛldɪd/| adj. v.|阉割的；去势的；无力化的 | Castrated; deprived of power or vitality.  |The gelded horse was calm and docile.  阉割后的马很平静温顺。|  The project felt gelded after the funding was cut.  在资金被削减后，这个项目感觉无力了。|castrated, emasculated, neutered| potent, powerful

The farmer gels his horses every year. = 农民每年都阉割他的马。
He gels his stallion to prevent it from breeding. = 他阉割他的种马以防止它繁殖。
