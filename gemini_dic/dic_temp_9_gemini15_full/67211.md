expressiveness:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪvnəs/| n. |表现力；表达能力|The ability to convey meaning, feeling, or emotion effectively.  It refers to the skill and power of expressing oneself clearly and effectively, whether through language, art, music, or other means.  The degree to which something effectively conveys meaning or emotion.|例句：Her expressiveness in her writing was remarkable.  她的写作表现力非凡。

The painting lacked expressiveness. 这幅画缺乏表现力。|近义词：eloquence, expressivity, articulation, communication|反义词：inarticulateness, incommunicativeness, dullness, blandness|词性:名词
