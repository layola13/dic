recommendative: /ˌrɛkəˈmɛndətɪv/ | adj. | 建议性的，推荐性的 |  Relating to or characterized by recommendation; expressing a recommendation.  This adjective describes something that suggests or implies a recommendation.  It focuses on the act of recommending rather than the thing being recommended. |


例句：

The recommendative tone of the letter made it clear that the candidate was highly favored.  = 这封信中建议的语气清楚地表明该候选人非常受青睐。

His recommendative remarks were instrumental in securing the funding. = 他的推荐性言论对获得资金至关重要。


近义词：suggestive, advisory, recommending, commendatory


反义词：disapproving, condemnatory, discouraging


词性: adjective
