quenched:/kwɛntʃt/| vt. adj.| 淬火；熄灭；解渴；抑制 | to satisfy (a thirst) or (a feeling): to extinguish or cool suddenly, especially by immersion in water or other liquid; to suppress or restrain (a feeling or reaction) |  The firefighters quickly quenched the blaze. 消防队员迅速扑灭了大火。


The cold water quenched his thirst. 冷水解了他的渴。


近义词：extinguish, slake, suppress, satisfy | 反义词：ignite, inflame, stimulate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He quenches his thirst with a glass of water. 他用一杯水解渴。
The factory quenches steel to make it stronger. 工厂用淬火法使钢铁更坚固。


一般过去时:
She quenched her thirst with a cool drink. 她喝了凉爽的饮料解渴。
The blacksmith quenched the hot iron in water. 铁匠将烧红的铁淬入水中。


现在完成时:
They have quenched the fire successfully. 他们成功地扑灭了大火。
The heat has been quenched by the rain.  雨水已经消除了酷暑。


过去完成时:
The firefighters had quenched the fire before the building collapsed. 在大楼倒塌之前，消防员已经扑灭了大火。
He had quenched his thirst before starting his journey. 他在开始旅程之前已经解渴了。


将来时:
The new system will quench the demand for energy. 新系统将满足对能源的需求。
They will quench the flames with a fire extinguisher. 他们将用灭火器扑灭火焰。


虚拟语气:
If only I could quench my thirst! 要是我能解渴就好了！
If he had quenched the fire sooner, the damage would have been less. 如果他早点扑灭大火，损失就会少一些。


被动语态:
The fire was quickly quenched by the firefighters. 大火很快就被消防队员扑灭了。
His anger was quenched by her kind words. 他的怒火被她温柔的话语平息了。


疑问句:
Can you quench this burning desire? 你能压制住这种强烈的欲望吗？
Has the thirst been quenched? 渴解了吗？


否定句:
He couldn't quench his intense curiosity. 他无法压制住他强烈的求知欲。
The rain failed to quench the drought.  雨水未能缓解干旱。

词源:

quench源自古英语*cwencan，意思是“熄灭，扼杀”。  它与古高地德语*cwanchan以及古诺尔斯语kweka有着共同的日耳曼语词根。  这些词都与“压碎，压扁”的概念有关，暗示了熄灭火焰或抑制情绪的行动。
