interdependently:/ˌɪntərˈdɪpɛndəntli/| adv.|相互依赖地，互相依赖地|In a way that shows mutual dependence.  Things or people that are interdependent rely on each other.  |例句：The success of the project depends on the team members working interdependently.  这个项目的成功取决于团队成员之间的相互合作。


例句：The economies of the world are increasingly interdependent. 全球经济日益相互依赖。


近义词：mutually, reciprocally, conjointly


反义词：independently, separately, autonomously


词性：副词
