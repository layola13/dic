darling:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/|/ˈdɑːrliŋ/| n./adj.|亲爱的；宝贝儿|A term of endearment used to address someone loved or cherished.  It can be used as a noun or an adjective, and its level of formality depends heavily on context.|例句：My darling, I love you so much. 亲爱的，我非常爱你。

例句：That darling little puppy is so cute. 那只可爱的狗狗真可爱。


近义词：sweetheart, love, honey, dear, beloved|反义词：enemy, foe, stranger


词性：Noun, Adjective


USAGE->

He calls his wife darling every day. 他每天都叫他妻子亲爱的。
She considers her cat her darling. 她把她的猫视为宝贝。


一般过去时:

She has cherished her darling pet since it was a kitten. 自从它还是一只小猫以来，她就一直珍爱她的宠物。
