directorial:/ˌdɪrɛkˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|导演的；指导的；管理的|Relating to or characteristic of a director, especially a film or theatre director.  It can also refer to the manner or style of a director.|例句：The film's directorial style was highly innovative and visually stunning. 这部电影的导演风格极具创新性和视觉冲击力。

例句：Her directorial debut was a critical and commercial success. 她的导演处女作获得了评论和商业上的成功。


近义词：managing, supervisory, guiding, leading


反义词：subordinate, following, unmanaged


词性:形容词
