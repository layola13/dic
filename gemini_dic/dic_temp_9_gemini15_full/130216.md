non-sensory: /ˌnɒnˈsɛnsəri/ | adj. |非感觉的，非感官的 | Not involving or perceived by the senses.  It refers to things that are not experienced through sight, hearing, smell, taste, or touch. | Example:  The concept of infinity is a non-sensory experience.  无限的概念是一种非感官体验。  | 近义词： abstract, intangible, intellectual | 反义词：sensory, perceptible
