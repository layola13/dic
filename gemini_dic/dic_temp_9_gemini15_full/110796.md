linkages:/ˈlɪŋkɪdʒɪz/| n. |联系，关联；连接装置；遗传连锁| Connections or relationships between things; a system of links or connections; (genetics) the tendency for genes located close together on a chromosome to be inherited together. |例句：The linkages between the two companies are very strong.  两家公司之间的联系非常紧密。|例句：The car's linkages were worn out. 这辆车的连接装置磨损了。|近义词：connections, relationships, links, associations |反义词：separations, disconnections
