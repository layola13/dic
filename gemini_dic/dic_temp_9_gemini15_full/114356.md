major-leagues:/ˈmeɪdʒər liːɡz/| n.|美国职业棒球大联盟|The term "major leagues" typically refers to Major League Baseball (MLB), the highest professional level of baseball in the United States and Canada.  It is characterized by its highly competitive nature, high salaries for players, and extensive media coverage.  The term can also be used more generally to refer to the top professional level of any sport.|例句：He dreamed of playing in the major leagues. 他梦想在职业棒球大联盟打球。|例句：She's a major-leagues player in the world of finance. 她在金融界是顶级高手。|近义词：professional leagues, top leagues|反义词：minor leagues, amateur leagues|名词
