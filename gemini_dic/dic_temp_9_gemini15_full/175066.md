radiometry:/reɪdiˈɒmɪtri/| n. |辐射测量学|The science and practice of measuring radiant energy.  Radiometry deals with the measurement of electromagnetic radiation, including its power, intensity, and spectral distribution.  It's crucial in various fields like astronomy, remote sensing, and optical engineering.|Example:  The researchers used advanced radiometry techniques to analyze the star's energy output.  研究人员使用先进的辐射测量技术来分析恒星的能量输出。|近义词：radiation measurement, radiant flux measurement|反义词：None specifically, but concepts like "qualitative light analysis" could be considered contrasting.|名词
