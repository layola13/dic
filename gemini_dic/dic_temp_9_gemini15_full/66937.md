expenseless: /ɪkˈspɛnsləs/ | adj. | 无费用的；免费的 | Free from expense; costing nothing.  The word is rarely used and often considered archaic.  It implies a complete lack of cost, not just a low cost. |例句：The promised expenseless trip turned out to involve hidden costs.  承诺的免费旅行结果却包含隐藏的费用。


近义词：free, gratis, costless, without charge | 反义词：expensive, costly, pricey
