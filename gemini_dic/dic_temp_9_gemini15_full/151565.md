part-work:/ˈpɑːrtwɜːrk/| n. |分期付款；分期工作；分期出版物|A system of payment or work that is divided into installments.  It often refers to publications released in installments,  or projects completed in stages.|例句：The encyclopedia is sold as a part-work. 这套百科全书以分期付款的方式出售。|例句：The construction project was completed as a part-work over several years. 建设项目分阶段完成，历时数年。|近义词：installment plan, serial publication|反义词：one-off payment, single project|名词
