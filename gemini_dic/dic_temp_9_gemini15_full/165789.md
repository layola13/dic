preobliged:/ˌpriːˈɒblɑːdʒd/| adj.| 预先感谢的；预先感激的 |  This word is a less common, more formal, and somewhat archaic synonym for "pre-thanked" or expressing gratitude in advance. It implies an obligation has already been acknowledged before the act of kindness or help has occurred.  | Example: I am preobliged for your kind assistance. 我预先感谢您的帮助。|近义词：pre-thanked, grateful in advance |反义词：ungrateful, unthankful |词性：形容词
