pannier:/ˈpænɪər/| n.|自行车车筐；背篼；提篮|A pannier is a large basket, typically one of a pair, carried on the back of a pack animal or bicycle.  They are often used for carrying goods or luggage.|例句：She loaded her panniers with supplies for the journey. 她把旅行用品装进了她的车筐。|例句：The donkey carried two panniers laden with firewood. 这头驴驮着两筐柴火。|近义词：basket, hamper, creel|反义词：none.
