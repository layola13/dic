savour:/ˈseɪvər/| vt. n.|品味；欣赏；享受；回味|To taste or smell something with great enjoyment.  To enjoy something slowly and fully.|例句：She savoured the delicious chocolate. 她细细品味着美味的巧克力。

例句：He savoured the moment of victory. 他尽情享受胜利的时刻。


近义词：relish, enjoy, appreciate, relish


反义词：dislike, despise, abhor


词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I savour the coffee every morning. = 我每天早晨都细细品味咖啡。
They savour every bite of the meal. = 他们细细品味每一口饭菜。


一般过去时:
She savoured the wine slowly. = 她慢慢地品味着葡萄酒。
We savoured the quiet atmosphere of the countryside. = 我们享受着乡村宁静的氛围。


现在完成时:
I have savoured many delicious meals in my life. = 我一生中品尝过许多美味佳肴。
She has savoured the fruits of her hard work. = 她享受着辛勤劳动的成果。


过去完成时:
He had savoured the victory before the awards ceremony. = 在颁奖典礼之前，他已经回味过胜利的喜悦。
They had savoured the peace and quiet before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们享受过片刻的宁静。


将来时:
We will savour the beautiful sunset tonight. = 我们今晚将欣赏美丽的日落。
They will savour the freedom of their retirement. = 他们将享受退休的自由。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would savour every moment. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会细细品味每一刻。
I wish I could savour this moment forever. = 我希望我能永远享受这一刻。


被动语态:

否定句:
