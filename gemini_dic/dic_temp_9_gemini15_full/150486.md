pantisocracy:/ˌpæntiˈsɒkrəsi/ | n. | 平均主义；人人平等的社会制度 | A system of government where all citizens share power equally, often associated with utopian ideals.  It implies a society characterized by complete equality in wealth and power.  The term combines "pan" (all) and "isocracy" (equal rule). | Example: The early socialist thinkers often envisioned a pantisocracy as the ultimate form of social justice.  早期社会主义思想家常常将平均主义设想为社会正义的最终形式。| 近义词：egalitarianism, communism (in some interpretations) | 反义词：oligarchy, autocracy, plutocracy |
