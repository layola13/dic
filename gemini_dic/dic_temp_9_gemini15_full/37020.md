columnist:/ˈkɑːləmnɪst/| n. |专栏作家|A columnist is a writer who regularly contributes articles or columns to a newspaper, magazine, or website.  Columnists often offer opinions or commentary on a particular topic or range of topics.  They develop a following based on their writing style and viewpoints.|Example:  The columnist's latest article on political corruption was widely read and discussed.  这位专栏作家关于政治腐败的最新文章被广泛阅读和讨论。|近义词：writer, commentator, essayist|反义词：reader, editor
