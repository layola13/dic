prioritization: /ˌpraɪˌɔːrɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 优先级排序，优先考虑 | The act of prioritizing; the process of assigning priorities.  It involves determining the relative importance of tasks or items and arranging them in order of importance. | 例句：The prioritization of tasks is crucial for effective time management.  任务的优先级排序对于有效的时间管理至关重要。


例句：Careful prioritization of projects ensured the company met its deadlines.  对项目的仔细优先级排序确保了公司按时完成任务。


近义词：ranking, ordering, preference, scheduling


反义词：randomization, neglecting


词性：名词
