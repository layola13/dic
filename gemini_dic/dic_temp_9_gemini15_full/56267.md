dogfight:/ˈdɒɡfaɪt/|/ˈdɔːɡfaɪt/| n. v.|激烈的空战；争吵；扭打|A dogfight is a close-range air battle between two or more aircraft.  It can also refer to a fierce quarrel or a rough fight.|例句：The two fighter jets engaged in a fierce dogfight above the city.  两架战斗机在城市上空进行了一场激烈的空战。

The children got into a terrible dogfight over a toy.孩子们为了一个玩具大打出手。|近义词：air battle, brawl, scuffle|反义词：peaceful negotiation, amicable discussion|词性:Noun, Verb
