drollish:/ˈdrɒlɪʃ/ | adj. |滑稽的，古怪有趣的|  Amusingly odd or whimsical;  slightly humorous in an understated or dry way.  It suggests a quiet, subtle humor rather than boisterous laughter. |例句：He had a drollish way of telling stories, often with unexpected twists. 他讲述故事的方式很古怪有趣，经常有出人意料的转折。

He gave a drollish smile and said nothing. 他诡异一笑，什么也没说。


近义词：whimsical, quirky, funny, amusing, comical | 反义词：serious, solemn, humorless, dull


词性：形容词
