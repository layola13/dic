skydiving:/ˈskaɪˌdaɪvɪŋ/| n.|跳伞运动|The activity of jumping from an aircraft and falling through the air before opening a parachute.  It's an extreme sport that requires training and specific equipment.|例句：He went skydiving for his 40th birthday. 他40岁生日那天去跳伞了。|例句：Many people find skydiving to be a thrilling and exhilarating experience.|许多人认为跳伞是一种令人兴奋和刺激的体验。|近义词：parachuting|反义词:None specifically.
