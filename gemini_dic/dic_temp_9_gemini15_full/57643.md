draw:/drɔː/ /drɑː/| vt. n.|绘画，拉，吸引，提取|To produce a picture or diagram by making lines on a surface with a pen, pencil, etc.; to pull or drag something; to attract or pull something towards something else; to obtain or extract information or a conclusion.  |例句：She drew a beautiful picture of a bird. 她画了一幅美丽的鸟儿图画。

例句：The artist drew inspiration from nature.  这位艺术家从大自然中汲取灵感。

近义词：sketch, paint, attract, pull, extract|反义词：push, repel,  repress

词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I draw every day. = 我每天都画画。
The artist draws inspiration from nature. = 这位艺术家从大自然中汲取灵感。

一般过去时:
She drew a beautiful portrait. = 她画了一幅美丽的肖像。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

He had drawn the water from the well. = 他已经从井里打水了。

将来时:

否定句:
