polypiparous:/ˌpɒlɪˈpɪpərəs/| adj.| 多胚的；多胎的 | Producing or containing many embryos or offspring.  The term is primarily used in zoology, particularly in reference to organisms capable of producing multiple offspring from a single fertilization event.  | Example: Some species of insects exhibit polypiparous reproduction.  某些昆虫种类表现出多胚繁殖。 | 近义词: multiparous, prolific | 反义词: uniparous
