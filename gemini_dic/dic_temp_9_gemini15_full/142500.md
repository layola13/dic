osmoses:/ɒzˈməʊsiːz/ | n. |渗透作用(复数) | The processes by which substances pass through a semipermeable membrane, driven by differences in concentration.  Osmosis refers to the movement of water across a membrane; other processes, such as dialysis, involve the movement of solutes. |例句：The experiments demonstrated the different rates of osmoses in various solutions. 实验表明各种溶液的渗透速率不同。

例句：The biologist studied the osmoses occurring within plant cells. 生物学家研究了植物细胞内发生的渗透作用。


近义词：diffusions (in some contexts) | 反义词：N/A | 词性:noun
