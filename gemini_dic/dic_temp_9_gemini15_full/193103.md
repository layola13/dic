sects:/sektz/| n.|宗派；教派；派别；（昆虫的）类；|A sect is a relatively small group of people with somewhat different religious beliefs from those of a larger group to which they belong.  It can also refer to a faction or subdivision within a larger group, not necessarily religious.|例句：Many different sects of Buddhism exist in Tibet.  西藏存在许多不同的佛教宗派。|例句：The political party is divided into several warring sects. 这个政党分裂成几个互相争斗的派别。|近义词：faction, group, denomination, school|反义词：mainstream, majority
