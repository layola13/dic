magicked:/ˈmædʒɪkt/| vt.|施魔法；变戏法|To perform magic or create something seemingly by magic.|例句：The magician magicked a rabbit out of a hat. 魔术师从帽子里变出一只兔子。
例句：She magicked a delicious meal from a few simple ingredients. 她用简单的食材变出一顿美味佳肴。|近义词：enchanted, conjured, bewitched|反义词：destroyed, ruined.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He magics a coin from thin air. 他凭空变出一枚硬币。
She magics up a stunning dress for the ball. 她变出一件令人惊艳的舞会礼服。

一般过去时:
The wizard magicked the dragon away. 巫师用魔法让龙消失了。
He magicked a smile onto her face. 他变出一个笑容在她脸上。

现在完成时:
They have magicked the old house into a beautiful home. 他们把旧房子变成了美丽的住宅。
She has magicked away all the dishes. 她用魔法把所有盘子都收起来了。

过去完成时:
The fairy had magicked the prince into a frog. 仙女已经把王子变成了青蛙。
Before I knew it, she had magicked a cake. 我还没反应过来，她就变出了一个蛋糕。

将来时:
The magician will magic a dove from his sleeve. 魔术师将从袖子里变出一只鸽子。
She will magic a solution to the problem. 她将变出一个解决问题的办法。

虚拟语气:
If I could magic away all my problems, I would. 如果我能用魔法消除我所有的问题，我会这么做的。
I wish I could magic myself to Paris. 我希望我能施魔法把自己送到巴黎。

被动语态:
The rabbit was magicked out of the hat. 兔子是从帽子里变出来的。
A beautiful garden was magicked into existence overnight. 一夜之间，一个美丽的庭院凭空出现。

疑问句:
How did he magic that coin? 他是怎么变出那枚硬币的？
Can you magic us some more food? 你能变出更多食物吗？

否定句:
He couldn't magic the lock open. 他无法用魔法打开锁。
She didn't magic the car away. 她没有把车变走。


PHRASE->
magic away:  使...消失
magic up: 变出；突然拿出
magic into:  使...变成
magicked out of: 从...变出来
magicked into existence:凭空出现
