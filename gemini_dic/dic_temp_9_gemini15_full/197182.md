shapable: /ˈʃeɪpəbəl/ | adj. | 可塑的，易于成形的 |  capable of being shaped or molded.  This adjective describes something that can be easily changed or formed into a different shape or form.  It often implies a degree of malleability or flexibility. |


例句：

Clay is a shapable material. = 粘土是一种易于成形的材料。

The young minds are highly shapable. = 年轻的心灵非常容易塑造。


近义词：malleable, moldable, pliable, ductile, adaptable


反义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding, intractable


词性：形容词
