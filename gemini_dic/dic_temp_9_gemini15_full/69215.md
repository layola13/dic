fearfully:/ˈfɪrfəli/| adv.|可怕地，害怕地，非常地|In a fearful manner; extremely.  It describes something done in a way that shows fear or apprehension, or it can be used to emphasize the degree of something.|例句：He looked fearfully around the dark room. 他害怕地环顾着昏暗的房间。

例句：She was fearfully tired after the long journey. 长途旅行后，她累坏了。|近义词：terribly, awfully, frightfully|反义词：bravely, confidently, calmly|副词
