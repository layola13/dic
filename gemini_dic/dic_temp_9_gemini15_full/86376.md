hederaceous:/ˌhɛdərˈeɪʃəs/| adj.| 攀援状的；蔓生的；类似常春藤的 | Relating to or resembling ivy; climbing or creeping.  The term often describes plants with trailing or climbing habits, similar to those of ivy.  |例句：The hederaceous plant climbed the wall, its tendrils clinging tightly.  这株攀援状的植物攀上了墙，它的卷须紧紧地抓住墙面。

例句： Many hederaceous species are found in temperate forests. 许多蔓生的物种在温带森林中被发现。


近义词：climbing, creeping, trailing, scandent


反义词：erect, upright

词源:  From Latin *hederaceus, from *hederā (ivy) + -aceus (-aceous suffix indicating resemblance).  The suffix "-aceous" indicates a resemblance to or belonging to a particular type.
