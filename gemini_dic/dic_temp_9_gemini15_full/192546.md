scuffing:/ˈskʌfɪŋ/| n. |拖着脚走，擦伤，磨损|The act of scraping or dragging one's feet along the ground;  a mark or damage caused by rubbing or scraping.|例句：The children were scuffing their feet on the pavement. 孩子们在人行道上拖着脚走。


例句：The scuffing on the leather shoes showed signs of wear. 皮鞋上的磨损痕迹显示出磨损的迹象。


近义词：dragging, scraping, abrasion, wearing


反义词：polishing, cleaning


词性：名词
