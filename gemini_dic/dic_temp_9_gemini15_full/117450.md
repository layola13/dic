meditations:/ˌmɛdəˈteɪʃənz/| n. |冥想；沉思；思考 |The act of meditating; deep thought or reflection.  Meditations often involve focusing on a specific object, thought, or activity to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm state. |例句：His daily meditations helped him to manage stress.  他的每日冥想帮助他缓解压力。

例句：The book contains a series of meditations on the nature of reality. 这本书包含一系列关于现实本质的沉思。


近义词：contemplations, reflections, musings |反义词：distractions, diversions
