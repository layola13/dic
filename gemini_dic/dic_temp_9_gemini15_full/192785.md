seaman:/ˈsiːmən/| n.|水手，海员|A person who works on a ship, especially one who is not an officer.|例句：He was a seasoned seaman, having sailed the seven seas. 他是一位经验丰富的水手，航行了七大洋。  例句：The seaman skillfully navigated the stormy waters.  这位水手熟练地驾驭着暴风雨的海面。|近义词：sailor, mariner, sailorman|反义词：passenger, landlubber
