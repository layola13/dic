cowrote:/koʊˈroʊt/| v.|合著，共同撰写|To write something together with another person or people.  It implies a collaborative effort where both parties contribute significantly to the final product.|例句：She cowrote a bestselling novel with her sister. 她和姐姐合著了一本畅销小说。

例句：The two authors cowrote a groundbreaking article on climate change.两位作者合著了一篇关于气候变化的开创性文章。


近义词：coauthored, collaborated on, jointly wrote

反义词：solely wrote, authored alone
