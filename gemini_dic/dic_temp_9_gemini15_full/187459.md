role-player:/ˈroʊlˌpleɪər/ | n. | 角色扮演者 | A person who participates in role-playing, either in a game or a therapeutic setting.  | Example:  He's a skilled role-player, able to convincingly inhabit different characters. 他是一位熟练的角色扮演者，能够令人信服地扮演不同的角色。 | 近义词: role-player, actor, impersonator  | 反义词: observer, spectator | 名词


USAGE->

The role-player takes on a new persona. = 角色扮演者扮演了一个新的角色。
She role-plays different characters in her therapy sessions. = 她在治疗中扮演不同的角色。


一般过去时:
The role-player expertly portrayed a villain. = 角色扮演者巧妙地扮演了一个反派。
