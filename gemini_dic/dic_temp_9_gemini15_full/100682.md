intra-linguistic: /ˌɪntrəˈlɪŋɡwɪstɪk/ | adj. |语言内部的，语言学内部的 | Relating to or occurring within a single language or linguistic system.  It refers to phenomena, processes, or comparisons that stay within the boundaries of one language, without considering cross-linguistic influences or comparisons. | Example:  Intra-linguistic variation refers to differences within a single language, such as dialects or sociolects.  语言内部差异指的是单一语言内部的差异，例如方言或社会方言。 | 近义词: internal, intralanguage | 反义词: interlinguistic, cross-linguistic
