eventuality:/ˌiːvɛnˈtʃuːəlɪti/| n. |可能性；可能发生的事；结果 | A possible event; something that may happen.  The plan takes into account all eventualities. |The possibility of war was a frightening eventuality.  战争的可能性是一个可怕的可能性。|可能性，可能的结果|improbability, unlikelihood|certainty, inevitability|名词


例句：

We must consider all possible eventualities before making a decision.
我们必须在做决定之前考虑所有可能的情况。

The company has contingency plans in place to deal with any eventuality.
该公司已经制定了应急计划，以应对任何可能发生的情况。


近义词：

possibility, probability, chance, contingency


反义词：

certainty, inevitability
