emergents:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənts/| n. |涌现的事物；新兴事物；(植物)从水中冒出的植物|Things that are newly arising or becoming prominent;  plants that emerge from the water.|Example: The rise of social media is one of the most significant emergents of the 21st century.  21世纪社交媒体的兴起是21世纪最显著的新兴事物之一。|Example: The emergent vegetation of the swamp included various water lilies and reeds. 沼泽中新出现的植被包括各种睡莲和芦苇。|近义词：novelties, up-and-comers, newcomers|反义词：established things, old-timers, veterans|名词
