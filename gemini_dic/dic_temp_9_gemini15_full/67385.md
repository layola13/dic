exteriorly:/ɪkˈstɪəriəli/ | adv. | 外部地；外表上 | Adverb describing something done or existing on the outside or surface.  It refers to the outward appearance or external aspect of something. | The house looked dilapidated exteriorly, but the interior was surprisingly modern.  房子外表看起来破旧不堪，但内部却出奇地现代化。 The painting was beautifully executed exteriorly, but the canvas was damaged.  这幅画的外部绘制精美，但画布却受损了。 | outwardly, superficially | internally, intrinsically | 副词
