rebreak:/ˌriːˈbreɪk/| vt.|再次破坏，重断，再打断|To break again or anew; to break once more.  This word is formed by the prefix "re-" meaning "again" and the verb "break".|例句：The vase was repaired, but it rebroke when it fell from the shelf again. 花瓶修好了，但它又从架子上掉下来摔碎了。
The truce rebroke after only a week.  停战协议仅仅维持了一周就再次破裂了。|近义词：break again, fracture anew, shatter again|反义词：repair, mend, fix
