digesting:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪŋ/| v.|消化；领会；整理|The process of breaking down food into a form that the body can absorb and use.  It also refers to the mental process of understanding and absorbing information.|例句：My stomach is digesting the large meal I just ate. =我的胃正在消化我刚吃的大餐。
The professor is digesting the research findings before writing the report. = 教授在撰写报告之前正在消化研究结果。|近义词：absorbing, assimilating, processing|反义词：rejecting, expelling


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I digest my food slowly. = 我慢慢消化食物。
She digests information quickly. = 她很快就能理解信息。

一般过去时:
I digested the entire book in a week. = 我在一周内读完了整本书。
He digested the news with difficulty. = 他费力地消化了这个消息。

现在完成时:
I have digested all the chapters. = 我已经消化了所有章节。
She has digested the implications of the study. = 她已经理解了这项研究的含义。

过去完成时:
I had digested the main points before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经理解了要点。
They had digested the information before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经消化了信息。

将来时:
I will digest this report tomorrow. = 我明天将消化这份报告。
She will digest the new regulations carefully. = 她会仔细消化新的规章制度。

虚拟语气:
If I digested the instructions more carefully, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我更仔细地阅读说明书，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had digested the information better before the exam. = 我希望我在考试前能更好地理解信息。


被动语态:
The food is being digested in the stomach. = 食物正在胃里消化。
The information was digested by the team before the presentation. = 信息在演示文稿之前由团队消化。


疑问句:
Are you digesting the information properly? = 你正在正确地消化信息吗？
How long does it take your body to digest food? = 你的身体需要多长时间才能消化食物？


否定句:
I cannot digest dairy products. = 我不能消化乳制品。
He didn't digest the implications of the contract. = 他没有理解合同的含义。


PHRASE->

digest food = 消化食物
digest information = 吸收信息
digest a book = 细读一本书
digest the news = 理解新闻
digest the facts = 理清事实
