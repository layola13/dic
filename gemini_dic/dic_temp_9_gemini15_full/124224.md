mosquito:/məˈskiːtoʊ/| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect with a long proboscis, the female of which sucks the blood of animals.|Example:  The mosquito bite caused a large, itchy welt.  蚊子叮咬导致一个又大又痒的肿块。|近义词：gnat, insect|反义词：N/A
