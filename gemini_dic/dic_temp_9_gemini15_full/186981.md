risked:/rɪskt/| v.|冒风险，冒险|To expose to danger or loss; to take a chance on.  |例句：He risked his life to save the child. 他冒着生命危险救了孩子。
例句：She risked losing her job by speaking out against her boss. 她冒着丢掉工作的风险公开反对老板。|近义词：ventured, jeopardized, endangered|反义词：secured, protected, guaranteed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He risks his health by smoking. = 他吸烟危害健康。
They risk their lives daily in their jobs. = 他们在工作中每天都冒着生命危险。


一般过去时:
I risked everything to start my own business. = 我冒着一切风险创办了自己的公司。
She risked a fine by parking illegally. = 她因违章停车而冒着被罚款的风险。


现在完成时:
He has risked his reputation by associating with them. = 他与他们交往，冒着毁坏自己名誉的风险。
She has risked everything for love. = 她为了爱情，已经冒险了一切。


过去完成时:

They had risked their lives to escape. = 他们曾冒着生命危险逃脱。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
I would risk my life for you. = 我会为了你而冒生命危险。

被动语态:
