ferryman:/ˈfɛriˌmæn/| n.|摆渡人，渡船工|A ferryman is a person who operates a ferry, a boat that carries people or goods across a body of water.  They are often associated with folklore and mythology.|例句：The ferryman rowed the boat across the river. 摆渡人划船渡过河。|例句：The old ferryman knew every ripple in the river. 老船夫熟悉河里的每一个涟漪。|近义词：boatman, transporter|反义词：passenger
