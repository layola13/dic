foreseeability: /ˌfɔːrˈsiːəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可预见性 | The extent to which something can be anticipated or foreseen.  It is a crucial concept in legal contexts, particularly in negligence and contract law.  |  The foreseeability of the risk is a key factor in determining liability. 可预见性风险是决定责任的关键因素。 The foreseeability of the accident was low, given the unusual circumstances.  鉴于不寻常的情况，事故的可预见性很低。 | predictability, anticipation | unforeseeability, unpredictability | 名词
