ospray:/ˈɒspreɪ/| n.|鱼鹰|A large bird of prey with a white head and brown back, that catches fish.  It is also known as a fish hawk or fish eagle.|例句：The osprey dived gracefully into the water and emerged with a wriggling fish.  鱼鹰优雅地潜入水中，叼着一尾扭动的鱼浮出水面。

例句：We saw an osprey nest high in a tall tree overlooking the river. 我们看到一只鱼鹰在高高的树上筑巢，俯瞰着河流。


近义词：fish hawk, fish eagle|反义词：无|词性:名词

词源:
The word "osprey" comes from the Old French word "osprei," which in turn is derived from the Latin word "avis piscatorius," meaning "fishing bird."  The word has been used in English since at least the 14th century.
