reorients:/ˌriːˈɔːriɛnts/ | vt. |重新定向，使重新定向 | To change the direction or focus of something or someone; to cause someone to adopt a new perspective or attitude. |例句：The company reoriented its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic.  公司调整了其营销策略以针对更年轻的人群。
The experience reoriented his priorities in life.  这段经历改变了他的人生目标。|近义词：redirect, refocus, realign |反义词：misdirect, disorient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher reorients the students' attention to the blackboard. = 老师重新将学生的注意力吸引到黑板上。
He reorients his business strategy every year. = 他每年都会调整他的商业策略。

一般过去时:
The map reoriented me to my current location. = 地图让我重新定位了我的当前位置。
She reoriented her career path after having children. = 她生孩子后重新规划了自己的职业道路。

现在完成时:
The company has reoriented its products to meet customer demands. = 公司已经调整了产品以满足客户需求。
They have reoriented their thinking about the project. = 他们已经改变了对项目的看法。

过去完成时:
He had reoriented his life goals before he met his wife. = 他在遇到妻子之前已经改变了人生目标。
The government had reoriented its foreign policy after the war. = 战争后政府已经调整了外交政策。

将来时:
The company will reorient its marketing campaign next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度调整其营销活动。
She will reorient her priorities after completing her studies. = 完成学业后，她将重新调整自己的优先级。

虚拟语气:
If the company reoriented its strategy, it might be more successful. = 如果公司调整战略，可能会更成功。
I wish I had reoriented my career path earlier. = 我真希望我早点重新规划职业道路。

被动语态:
The students were reoriented to the new curriculum. = 学生们被重新引导到新的课程中。
His beliefs were reoriented by his travels abroad. = 他在国外的旅行改变了他的信仰。

疑问句:
Will the company reorient its focus on the international market? = 公司会将重点重新放在国际市场上吗？
How can we reorient the public's perception of the issue? = 我们如何才能改变公众对这个问题的看法？

否定句:
The company did not reorient its marketing strategy. = 公司没有调整其营销策略。
He refused to reorient his thinking on the matter. = 他拒绝改变他对这件事的看法。
