jailors:/ˈdʒeɪlərz/| n. |狱卒，看守|A jailor is a person who guards prisoners in a jail.|例句：The jailors were strict but fair. 狱卒们严厉但公平。 The jailors conducted a thorough search of the cell. 狱卒们对牢房进行了彻底搜查。|近义词：warder, keeper, gaoler|反义词：prisoner
