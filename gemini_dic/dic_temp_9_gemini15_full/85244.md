/ˈhɑːrməl/| n. |危害；损害；伤害|Harm is any physical or psychological damage to a person or thing. It can be intentional or unintentional, and can range from minor injuries to serious, life-threatening harm.|例句：The storm caused considerable harm to the crops.  暴风雨对庄稼造成了相当大的损害。|例句：His words caused her great harm. 他的话对她造成了很大的伤害。|近义词：damage, injury, hurt, detriment |反义词：benefit, advantage, help


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pollution harms the environment. = 污染危害环境。
Smoking harms your health. = 吸烟有害健康。


一般过去时:
The accident harmed his reputation. = 那次事故损害了他的名誉。
The fire harmed the building. = 大火损害了建筑物。


现在完成时:
The scandal has harmed the company's image. = 这场丑闻损害了公司的形象。
Years of neglect have harmed the bridge's structure. = 多年的忽视损害了桥梁的结构。


过去完成时:
The previous management had harmed the company's financial stability. =  之前的管理层损害了公司的财务稳定性。
The drought had harmed the crops before the rain came. = 在下雨之前，干旱已经损害了庄稼。


将来时:
The new policy will harm small businesses. = 新政策将损害小型企业。
Further deforestation will harm the ecosystem. = 进一步的森林砍伐将损害生态系统。


虚拟语气:
If we continue this way, we will harm the environment. = 如果我们继续这样下去，我们将损害环境。
I wish the storm hadn't harmed my garden. = 我真希望暴风雨没有损害我的花园。


被动语态:
The crops were harmed by the frost. = 庄稼被霜冻损害了。
The company's reputation was harmed by the negative publicity. = 公司的声誉因负面宣传而受损。


疑问句:
Did the incident harm anyone? = 这件事伤害到任何人了吗？
Will the changes harm the project? = 这些改变会损害项目吗？


否定句:
The medicine didn't harm her. = 这药没有伤害她。
The rain didn't harm the plants. = 雨没有伤害植物。


PHRASE->
do harm to = 损害…
cause harm = 造成伤害
come to harm = 受害
harmful to = 对…有害的
without harm = 安然无恙
