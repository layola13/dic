mukluk: /ˈmʌklʌk/ | n. |雪地靴 | A type of waterproof boot traditionally worn by Inuit and other Arctic peoples, typically made from sealskin or other animal hide.  Mukluks are known for their softness, warmth, and insulation. |例句：She wore warm mukluks to keep her feet dry and warm in the snow. 她穿着温暖的雪地靴来保持双脚在雪地里干燥和温暖。


例句：The mukluk's thick sole provided excellent traction on the icy ground.  这双雪地靴厚厚的鞋底在冰冷的地面上提供了极好的抓地力。

近义词：snow boot, winter boot

反义词：sandal, flip-flop
