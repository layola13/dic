psychodrama:/ˈsaɪkəʊˈdrɑːmə/| n. |心理剧疗法|A type of psychotherapy in which patients role-play their problems and conflicts in a group setting, aiming to gain insight and improve emotional functioning.|Example: The therapist used psychodrama to help the patient confront their fear of public speaking.|近义词：role-playing therapy, group therapy|反义词：individual therapy
