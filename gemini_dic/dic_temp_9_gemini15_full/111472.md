It is often implemented during periods of high demand or system instability. | 例句：The city experienced widespread load-sheddings due to the extreme heatwave. = 由于极端热浪，该市经历了大范围的限电。


例句：Frequent load-sheddings disrupted businesses and daily life in the region. = 频繁的限电扰乱了该地区的商业和日常生活。
