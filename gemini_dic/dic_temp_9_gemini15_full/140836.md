on-sufferance:/ˌɒnˈsʌfərəns/| adj.|容忍的，勉强允许的|Existing or permitted only by tolerance or indulgence, not by right or law.  It implies a temporary or conditional permission, often subject to revocation.|例句：He was living on sufferance in his brother's house. 他勉强住在哥哥的家里。  The tenants remained on sufferance after their lease expired.  租约到期后，租客勉强继续居住。|近义词：tolerated, permitted, allowed (but with a stronger sense of reluctance)|反义词：by right, legally, officially|形容词
