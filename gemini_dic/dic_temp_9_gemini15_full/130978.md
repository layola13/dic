nonastronomical: /ˌnɒnˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkl/ | adj. |非天文上的；非天文学的 | Not relating to astronomy or the physical universe beyond Earth.  This adjective signifies that something is unrelated to celestial bodies, space, or the cosmos. It emphasizes a terrestrial or earthly focus. |例句：The project's budget, while large, was nonastronomical in the context of national spending.  这个项目的预算虽然很大，但在国家支出的大背景下却不算天文数字。


The increase in the price of bread is a nonastronomical issue, unlike the expansion of the universe. 面包价格上涨是一个非天文问题，不像宇宙的膨胀。


近义词：terrestrial, earthly, mundane, worldly |反义词：astronomical, cosmic, celestial

词性：形容词
