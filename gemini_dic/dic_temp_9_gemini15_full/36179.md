coffinless:/ˈkɒfɪnləs/| adj. |无棺材的；不使用棺材的 | Without a coffin.  The term often implies a burial or cremation practice that doesn't involve a traditional coffin.  | Example:  The body was laid to rest in a coffinless burial.  尸体被安葬在一个没有棺材的墓穴里。|近义词:uncoffined |反义词:coffined
