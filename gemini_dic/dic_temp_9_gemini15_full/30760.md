例句：There have been several cessations in the ongoing construction project due to inclement weather.  由于恶劣天气，正在进行的建筑项目已经中断了好几次。|近义词：halts, stops, pauses, breaks, interruptions|反义词：continuations, commencements, beginnings|名词
