hylaeosaurus:/ˌhaɪliːəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |禽龙属（一种已灭绝的恐龙）|A genus of extinct ornithopod dinosaur from the Early Cretaceous period.  Hylaeosaurus is known from relatively incomplete remains, making its exact classification and appearance somewhat uncertain.  It was characterized by bony plates and spikes along its back, suggesting some form of armour. | The discovery of *Hylaeosaurus* was crucial to the early understanding of dinosaurs. | 近义词: (no direct synonyms, as it's a specific genus) | 反义词: (no direct antonyms)
