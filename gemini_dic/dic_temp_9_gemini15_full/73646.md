forfeited:/ˈfɔːrfɪtɪd/ | v. |丧失；被没收；放弃 | To lose or give up something as a penalty for wrongdoing or failure to fulfill an obligation.  |例句：He forfeited his right to vote. 他丧失了他的投票权。 |例句：The team forfeited the game due to insufficient players.  由于队员不足，该队放弃了比赛。|近义词：lose, relinquish, surrender, waive |反义词：gain, acquire, retain, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He forfeits his possessions every time he gambles. = 他每次赌博都会输掉自己的财产。
She forfeits her turn if she doesn't answer correctly. = 如果她答错，她就失去了轮流的机会。


一般过去时:
They forfeited the match because of bad behaviour. = 由于行为不端，他们输掉了比赛。
The judge forfeited his license for reckless driving. = 法官因鲁莽驾驶而被吊销驾照。


现在完成时:
He has forfeited all his savings through bad investments. = 他因不良投资而损失了所有积蓄。
The company has forfeited its reputation due to scandal. =  该公司因丑闻而名誉扫地。


过去完成时:
She had forfeited her place in the competition before the final round. = 在决赛之前，她已经失去了在比赛中的位置。
The player had forfeited several games before he finally won. = 在他最终获胜之前，这位选手已经输掉了几场比赛。


将来时:
If he continues to break the rules, he will forfeit his membership. = 如果他继续违反规定，他将被取消会员资格。
They will forfeit their deposit if they cancel the contract. = 如果他们取消合同，他们将失去押金。


虚拟语气:
If he had played better, he would not have forfeited the match. = 如果他打得更好，他就不会输掉比赛。
If she hadn't cheated, she wouldn't have forfeited her scholarship. = 如果她没有作弊，她就不会失去奖学金。


被动语态:
His property was forfeited by the government. = 他的财产被政府没收了。
The prize was forfeited because of his disqualification. = 由于他被取消资格，奖品被没收了。


疑问句:
Will he forfeit his rights if he doesn't pay his taxes? = 如果他不交税，他会失去他的权利吗？
Has she forfeited her chance to win the competition? = 她已经失去赢得比赛的机会了吗？


否定句:
He did not forfeit his position despite the allegations. = 尽管有指控，但他并没有失去他的职位。
She did not forfeit her rights to appeal the decision. = 她并没有失去上诉的权利。


PHRASE->

词源:

The word "forfeited" comes from the Old French word "forfaire," which literally means "to do wrong" or "to commit a fault."  This evolved through Middle English to its modern form, retaining the sense of losing something as a consequence of wrongdoing or failure.
