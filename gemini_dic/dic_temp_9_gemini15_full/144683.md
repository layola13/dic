outyielding: /ˌaʊtˈjiːldɪŋ/ | v. | 超过产量；产量高于 | Producing or yielding more than something else.  |例句：This new variety of wheat is outyielding all others in the region.  这种新型小麦的产量超过该地区所有其他品种。

例句：The solar farm is outyielding its projected energy output by 15%.  这个太阳能农场的发电量超过了预计的15%。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, surpassing in yield, outproducing


反义词：underproducing, underyielding


词性:动词
