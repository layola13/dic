lamellate:/ˈlæməleɪt/| adj.|层状的；片状的；薄板状的|Having thin, flat layers or plates.  The structure is characterized by a series of parallel plates or layers. |例句：The mineral displayed a distinct lamellate structure under the microscope.  显微镜下，这种矿物呈现出明显的层状结构。


例句：The onion's lamellate structure is easily visible when sliced. 洋葱的层状结构切开后很容易看到。


近义词：layered, stratified, foliated, platy


反义词：massive, homogenous, unlayered


词性：形容词
