enhances:/ɪnˈhɑːnsɪz/| vt. |增强，提高，改进 |To improve the quality, value, or attractiveness of something. |例句：The new software enhances the user experience. 新软件增强了用户体验。

The painting enhances the beauty of the room. 这幅画增强了房间的美感。


近义词：improve, better, amplify, boost |反义词：impair, worsen, diminish, reduce |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The filter enhances the image quality. = 滤镜增强了图像质量。
Regular exercise enhances physical health. = 定期锻炼增强了身体健康。

一般过去时:
The presentation enhanced the company's image. = 此演示文稿提升了公司的形象。
The new lighting enhanced the atmosphere of the restaurant. = 新的灯光增强了餐厅的气氛。

现在完成时:
The renovations have enhanced the value of the property. = 装修提升了房产的价值。
Recent advancements have enhanced our understanding of the disease. = 近期的进步增强了我们对这种疾病的了解。

过去完成时:
They had enhanced security measures before the event. = 他们在活动前加强了安全措施。
She had enhanced her skills through years of practice. = 她通过多年的练习提升了自己的技能。

将来时:
The new technology will enhance productivity. = 新技术将提高生产力。
The upcoming marketing campaign will enhance brand awareness. = 即将来临的营销活动将增强品牌知名度。

虚拟语气:
If we used better materials, it would enhance the product's durability. = 如果我们使用更好的材料，它将增强产品的耐用性。
I wish the software enhanced the graphics more effectively. = 我希望软件能更有效地增强图形效果。

被动语态:
The building's appearance was enhanced by the new landscaping. = 建筑的外观因新的景观设计而得到提升。
The company's reputation has been enhanced by its recent success. = 公司的声誉因其最近的成功而得到提升。

疑问句:
How can we enhance customer satisfaction? = 我们如何提高客户满意度？
What strategies can enhance employee morale? = 哪些策略可以提高员工士气？

否定句:
The poor lighting did not enhance the mood of the party. = 昏暗的灯光并没有增强派对的气氛。
The changes did not enhance the overall design. = 这些改变并没有增强整体设计。
