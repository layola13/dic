reprehender:/ˌrɛprɪˈhɛndər/| n. |责备者；批评者 | One who reproves or criticizes.  A reprehended is someone who has been reproved or criticized. | The reprehended student quietly accepted his punishment.  被责备的学生默默地接受了他的惩罚。|近义词：critic, censor, reprover |反义词：praiser, flatterer


USAGE->

The reprehended employee was unfairly targeted. 被责备的员工受到了不公平的对待。

Passive Voice:
His actions were reprehended by the board. 他的行为受到了董事会的谴责。


Negative Sentence:
He was not a reprehender by nature; he preferred to offer encouragement. 他天性并非批评者；他更喜欢给予鼓励。
