kayaks:/ˈkaɪæks/| n.|皮划艇|A kayak is a small, narrow boat for one or two people, propelled by a double-bladed paddle.  Kayaks are used for recreation, sport, and transportation.|例句：He rented a kayak and explored the coastline. 他租了一艘皮划艇并探索了海岸线。

例句：We went kayaking on the lake. 我们在湖上划皮划艇。


近义词：canoe (but canoes are typically paddled with a single-bladed paddle)|反义词:None|词性:名词
