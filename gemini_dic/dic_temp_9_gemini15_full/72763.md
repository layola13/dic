foghorn:/ˈfɔːɡhɔːrn/| n. |雾号，汽笛|A foghorn is a horn, usually on a ship or lighthouse, that produces a loud, resonant sound to warn of danger in foggy conditions.|The foghorn blared, a deep, mournful sound cutting through the thick fog.  雾号发出低沉、悲鸣的声音，穿过浓雾。|The foghorn warned ships of the nearby cliffs. 雾号警告船只附近有悬崖。|siren, horn|—|
