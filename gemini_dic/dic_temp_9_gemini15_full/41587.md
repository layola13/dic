英文解析:The plural form of "corporealness," referring to the quality or state of being corporeal;  the nature of being physical or material, as opposed to spiritual or immaterial. It emphasizes the multiple aspects or instances of physical existence.


例句：

The artist's work focuses on the diverse corporealnesses of the human form across different cultures. = 艺术家的作品关注不同文化中人类形态的多样物质性。

The philosopher contrasted the fleeting nature of spiritual experience with the enduring corporealnesses of the physical world.= 哲学家对比了精神体验的短暂性和物质世界持久存在的物质性。


近义词：materiality, physicality, tangibility, embodiment


反义词：spirituality, immateriality, intangibility
