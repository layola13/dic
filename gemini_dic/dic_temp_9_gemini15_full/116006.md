marshland:/ˈmɑːrʃlænd/ | n. |沼泽地，湿地 | Marshland is a type of wetland characterized by low-lying areas with waterlogged soil and vegetation adapted to wet conditions. It often supports a diverse ecosystem of plants and animals. | 例句：The birds nested in the marshland.  鸟儿在沼泽地筑巢。

例句：We walked through the marshland, careful not to disturb the wildlife. 我们小心地穿过沼泽地，以免打扰野生动物。


近义词：wetland, bog, swamp, fen

反义词：highland, upland, dry land

词性：名词
