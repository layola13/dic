compared:/kəmˈpɛərd/| v. |比较，对照| To examine (two or more objects, ideas, people, etc.) in order to discover their similarities or differences.  It is often used in the past participle form ("compared to" or "compared with").|例句：Compared to last year, sales are up 10%. 与去年相比，销售额增长了10%。

例句：When compared with other candidates, she stands out. 与其他候选人相比，她脱颖而出。


近义词：contrasted, contrasted with, juxtaposed, likened


反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, unlike


词性:动词
