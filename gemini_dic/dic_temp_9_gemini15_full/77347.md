gaudy:/ˈɡɔːdi/| adj.|俗丽的；花哨的；过于艳丽的 | excessively bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless.  |例句：The gaudy colors of the carnival costumes were dazzling.  嘉年华服装的俗丽色彩令人眼花缭乱。
The room was decorated in a gaudy style with too much bright colors and patterns. 房间装饰风格俗丽，色彩和图案过于鲜艳。|近义词：flashy, showy, ostentatious, flamboyant |反义词：tasteful, understated, subtle, muted
