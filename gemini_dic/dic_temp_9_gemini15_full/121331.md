misgauges:/mɪsˈɡeɪdʒ/ | vt. | 误判；错误估计；对…估计错误 | To judge or estimate incorrectly; to miscalculate.  | Example: The general misgauged the enemy's strength and suffered a devastating defeat.  将军错误估计了敌人的实力，遭受了惨重的失败。|近义词：misjudge, underestimate, miscalculate | 反义词：accurately assess, correctly gauge
