integrated:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 综合的；完整的；一体化的 | Combining or coordinating different things into a whole.  It suggests a unified system where different parts work together seamlessly. | Example: The integrated circuit revolutionized the electronics industry.  集成电路彻底改变了电子工业。  The integrated marketing campaign targeted multiple demographics. 综合营销活动针对多个目标群体。 |近义词: unified, combined, holistic | 反义词: segregated, separate, fragmented


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school integrates art and science into its curriculum. = 学校把艺术和科学融入其课程中。
This system integrates seamlessly with our existing software. = 这个系统与我们现有的软件无缝集成。


一般过去时:
They integrated the new data into their analysis. = 他们将新数据整合到他们的分析中。
The company integrated its operations across different countries. = 公司整合了其在不同国家的业务。


现在完成时:
We have integrated several new features into the latest version. = 我们已将多个新功能集成到最新版本中。
The team has successfully integrated the two departments. = 团队已成功地整合了这两个部门。


过去完成时:
They had already integrated the feedback before the final release. = 在最终发布之前，他们已经整合了反馈意见。
The project had integrated all the necessary components by the deadline. = 该项目在截止日期前已整合所有必要的组件。


将来时:
We will integrate the new technology into our products next year. = 我们明年将把这项新技术整合到我们的产品中。
The company will integrate its supply chain for greater efficiency. = 公司将整合其供应链以提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If we integrated all the data, we would have a clearer picture. = 如果我们整合所有数据，我们将会有更清晰的画面。
It would be better if the system integrated more smoothly. = 如果系统集成得更流畅，那就更好了。


被动语态:
The new system is being integrated into the network. = 新系统正在被集成到网络中。
The software was integrated with the hardware successfully. = 软件已成功地与硬件集成。


疑问句:
Is the new software integrated with the existing system? = 新软件与现有系统集成了吗？
How is the data integrated into the report? = 数据是如何整合到报告中的？


否定句:
The two systems are not integrated. = 这两个系统没有集成。
The company did not integrate its marketing efforts effectively. = 公司没有有效地整合其营销工作。
