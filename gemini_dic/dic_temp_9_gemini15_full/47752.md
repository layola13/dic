decalling:/dɪˈkæliŋ/| v.| 脱胶；去除涂层 |The process of removing a decal or adhesive film from a surface.  Decalling involves carefully separating the decal from its backing and then applying it to a desired surface.|例句：The technician is de-calling the old labels from the product packaging. 技术人员正在从产品包装上撕掉旧标签。

The artist spent hours meticulously de-calling the intricate design. 艺术家花了数小时仔细地撕下复杂的图案。|近义词：removing, peeling, stripping |反义词：applying, adhering, sticking |动词
