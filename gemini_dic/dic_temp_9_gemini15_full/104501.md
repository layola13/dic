kale-seed:/ˈkeɪlsiːd/| n. |羽衣甘蓝种子|Kale seed refers to the seeds of the kale plant (Brassica oleracea var. sabellica), a leafy green vegetable.  These seeds are used for propagating kale plants, either by home gardeners or commercially. They are a source of nutrients and can be consumed, although this is less common than consuming the leaves.|例句：I planted kale seeds in my garden last spring. = 我去年春天在我的花园里种了羽衣甘蓝种子。


例句：The farmer used high-quality kale seeds to ensure a bountiful harvest. = 农民使用了优质的羽衣甘蓝种子以确保丰收。|近义词：kale planting material, Brassica oleracea seeds|反义词：无|词性：名词
