itch:/ɪtʃ/| n./v.|发痒；渴望|An unpleasant feeling on the skin that makes you want to scratch.  It can also refer to a strong desire or urge.|例句：I have an itch on my arm.  我的胳膊痒。|例句：He had an itch to travel the world. 他渴望周游世界。|近义词：tingle, scrape, yearn|反义词：numbness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mosquito bite itches. = 蚊子叮咬的地方痒。
My skin itches all over. = 我全身都痒。


一般过去时:
The wool sweater itched. = 羊毛衫很痒。
My arm itched terribly last night. = 昨晚我的胳膊痒得厉害。


现在完成时:
I've itched all day. = 我痒了一整天了。
He's itched so much he's broken the skin. = 他挠得厉害，都挠破皮了。


过去完成时:
The rash had itched for days before I saw the doctor. = 我看医生之前，疹子已经痒了好几天了。
I had itched so much my skin was raw. = 我挠得厉害，皮肤都溃烂了。


将来时:
That rash will itch for several days. = 那疹子会痒几天。
I think this will itch more later. = 我想这过会儿会更痒。


虚拟语气:
If it itched less, I wouldn't scratch it so much. = 如果不那么痒，我就不会挠那么多了。
I wish this mosquito bite didn't itch so much. = 我真希望这个蚊子叮咬的地方没那么痒。


被动语态:
The skin is itched by the insect bite. = 皮肤被昆虫叮咬后发痒了。
His arms were itched raw by the poison ivy. = 他的胳膊被毒藤弄得又痒又破。


疑问句:
Does this fabric itch? = 这布料会痒吗？
Does your rash still itch? = 你的疹子还痒吗？


否定句:
This shirt doesn't itch. = 这件衬衫不痒。
My skin doesn't itch anymore. = 我的皮肤不再痒了。


PHRASE->
have an itch to do sth. = 渴望做某事
scratch an itch = 挠痒
an itch to travel = 旅行的渴望
have the travel itch = 有旅行的冲动
a skin itch = 皮肤痒
