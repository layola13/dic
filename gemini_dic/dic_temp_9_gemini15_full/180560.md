regiments:/ˈredʒɪmənts/ | n. |团，兵团 |A regiment is a military unit, typically comprising several battalions.  It's a large organized body of soldiers. |The regiment marched proudly through the city.  这个团骄傲地穿过城市行进。 The new recruits were assigned to the 1st Infantry Regiment. 新兵被分配到第一步兵团。 |battalion, brigade | (反义词缺乏直接对应，可考虑使用smaller unit 或 individual soldiers) |名词
