on-off:/ˈɒnˌɒf/ /ˈɑːnˌɑːf/| adj.|  开关的；断续的；间歇的 |  Describes something that can be switched on and off, or that operates intermittently.  It can also describe a relationship that is unstable or characterized by periods of closeness and distance. | Example: The on-off switch is located on the back of the device.  这个开关位于设备的背面。The on-off relationship between them was exhausting. 他们之间忽冷忽热的关系令人筋疲力尽。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, fluctuating |反义词：constant, continuous, steady
