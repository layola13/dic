frizzly:/ˈfrɪzli/| adj.|卷曲的，蓬松的|Having a frizzy texture; characterized by many small curls or waves.|Her hair was frizzly after the rain.  她的头发雨后变得蓬松卷曲。|The wool was incredibly frizzly. 这羊毛非常蓬松卷曲。|curly, kinky, wavy|straight, smooth
