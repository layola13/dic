emblazoner:/ɪmˈbleɪzənər/ | n. | 装饰者；装饰工；(纹章)绘纹章者 | One who emblazons; one who decorates something conspicuously.  An emblazoner is a person skilled in decorating surfaces with bright and noticeable designs, often heraldic devices. | The skilled emblazoner meticulously painted the family crest onto the shield.  熟练的装饰工细致地将家族纹章绘制到盾牌上。| decorator, ornamenter | obliterator, eraser | 名词
