housework:/ˈhaʊsˌwɜːrk/| n. |家务劳动|The tasks involved in maintaining a household, such as cleaning, cooking, and laundry.|例句：I hate doing housework, especially cleaning the bathroom.  我讨厌做家务，尤其是清洁浴室。|例句：After a long day at work, the last thing I want to do is housework.  工作了一整天后，我最不想做的就是家务。|近义词：chores, domestic duties|反义词：leisure, recreation

USAGE->

She does housework every day. = 她每天都做家务。
He helps with the housework on weekends. = 他周末帮忙做家务。
