词源:
The word "mishmash" is of uncertain origin, but it's likely a blend of words reflecting its meaning of a confused mixture.  One theory suggests it might be related to Middle English "mes"—meaning "mixture"—or from a similar-sounding Yiddish word.  The exact etymology remains debated.
