reptilelike:/ˈrɛptaɪlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像爬行动物的；爬行动物似的|Resembling a reptile in form or characteristics.|The dinosaur's reptilelike scales were impressive.|近义词：reptilian, saurian|反义词：mammalian, avian


例句:

The creature's reptilelike skin was rough and scaly. = 那生物像爬行动物一样的皮肤粗糙而有鳞片。

His reptilelike movements were slow and deliberate. = 他像爬行动物一样的动作缓慢而谨慎。
