preconsider:/ˌpriːkənˈsɪdə(r)/| vt.|预先考虑，预想|To think about or consider something beforehand.  It implies careful thought and planning before an action or decision.|例句：We preconsidered the potential risks before embarking on the project. 我们在着手这个项目之前预先考虑了潜在的风险。

例句：She preconsidered all possible scenarios before making her presentation.  她在做演讲之前预先考虑了所有可能的情况。


近义词：anticipate, contemplate, foresee, plan for

反义词：neglect, overlook, disregard

词性：动词
