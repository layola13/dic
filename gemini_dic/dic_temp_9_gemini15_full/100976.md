invading:/ɪnˈveɪdɪŋ/| v.|入侵，侵略，侵犯|To enter a place by force with an army; to spread into or affect something in a harmful way.|例句：The enemy army invaded the country. 敌军入侵了该国。
The weeds are invading the garden. 杂草正在侵入花园。|近义词：attack, conquer, overrun|反义词：defending, protecting, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army is invading the neighboring country. = 军队正在入侵邻国。
The weeds are invading my garden. = 杂草正在入侵我的花园。

一般过去时:
The Vikings invaded England in the 8th century. = 维京人在8世纪入侵了英格兰。
The disease invaded his body quickly. = 疾病迅速侵袭了他的身体。

现在完成时:
The insects have invaded the entire house. = 昆虫已经入侵了整个房子。
Fear has invaded her heart. = 恐惧已经侵入了她的内心。

过去完成时:
The country had been invaded several times before. = 这个国家之前已经被入侵过好几次了。
The rumor had invaded the town before the truth was known. = 在真相大白之前，谣言已经传遍了整个城镇。

将来时:
They will invade the city at dawn. = 他们将在黎明时分入侵该城市。
The cold weather will invade the region soon. = 冷天气很快就会侵袭该地区。

虚拟语气:
If they invaded, we would fight back. = 如果他们入侵，我们将反击。
If the virus invaded my system, I would be very sick. = 如果病毒入侵我的系统，我会病得很重。

被动语态:
The country was invaded by the enemy forces. = 该国被敌军入侵了。
The town was invaded by tourists. = 城镇被游客挤满了。


疑问句:
Is the army invading our territory? = 军队是否正在入侵我们的领土？
Are the pests invading our crops? = 害虫是否正在侵害我们的庄稼？

否定句:
The enemy did not invade the capital. = 敌人没有入侵首都。
The cold weather did not invade the region this year. = 今年寒冷天气没有侵袭该地区。
