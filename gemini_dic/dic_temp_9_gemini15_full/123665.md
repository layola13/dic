mooches:/ˈmuːtʃɪz/| v.| 蹭吃蹭喝；游手好闲；懒散地闲逛 |To obtain something, especially money or food, by sponging or begging.  To live by sponging on others. To spend time idly. |例句：He mooches around all day doing nothing. 他整天游手好闲，什么也不做。

He mooched a cigarette from his friend. 他向朋友蹭了一根烟。


近义词：sponge, bum, freeload, loaf, idle|反义词：work, contribute, earn
