crocodile:/ˈkrɒkədaɪl/|/ˈkrɑːkədaɪl/| n. |鳄鱼|A large reptile that lives in rivers and lakes in warm parts of the world.  Crocodiles are known for their powerful jaws and sharp teeth.|例句：The crocodile lurked in the murky water, waiting for its prey.  那条鳄鱼潜伏在浑浊的水中，等待着它的猎物。|例句：We saw a huge crocodile basking in the sun on the riverbank. 我们看到一条巨大的鳄鱼在河岸上晒太阳。|近义词：alligator|反义词：None|名词
