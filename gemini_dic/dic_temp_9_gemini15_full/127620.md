needled:/ˈniːdəld/ | adj., v. |  针刺的；烦扰的；刺激的 |  (adj.) Pricked with a needle; (v.) To prick or irritate with a needle; to annoy or provoke persistently. | 例句：The fabric felt needled. = 这布料感觉像被针扎过。  He was needled into making a decision. = 他被激怒而不得不做决定。|近义词：(adj.)  pricked, stung, irritated; (v.)  provoked, irritated, annoyed | 反义词：(adj.) smooth, unpricked; (v.) soothed, calmed
