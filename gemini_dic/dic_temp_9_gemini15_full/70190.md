fibrinogenous: /ˌfaɪbrɪˈnɒdʒənəs/ | adj. | 纤维蛋白原生成的；产生纤维蛋白原的 |  Relating to or producing fibrinogen.  Fibrinogen is a protein that is essential for blood clotting.  A fibrinogenous substance is one that contributes to the formation of fibrin, the insoluble protein that forms blood clots. |


例句：

The liver plays a fibrinogenous role in the blood clotting process.  肝脏在血液凝固过程中起着纤维蛋白原生成的作用。

The study investigated the fibrinogenous properties of a novel synthetic compound.  该研究调查了一种新型合成化合物的纤维蛋白原生成特性。


近义词：fibrinogenic (less common)


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与抑制凝血相关的词语，例如anticoagulant (抗凝剂)。


词性：形容词
