courtship:/ˈkɔːtʃɪp/| n. |求爱；求婚；恋爱阶段|The process of courting or wooing someone, typically before marriage.  It involves establishing a romantic relationship and demonstrating commitment. |例句：Their courtship was a whirlwind romance. = 他们的求爱是一场旋风般的浪漫。|例句：The long courtship finally ended in marriage. = 漫长的求爱最终以结婚告终。|近义词：wooing, dating, romance, pursuit|反义词：estrangement, separation, divorce
