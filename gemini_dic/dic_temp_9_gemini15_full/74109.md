fougade:/fuˈɡɑːd/| n. | 矿井巷道中的爆破孔；爆破；(旧)一种小型迫击炮 | A fougade is a small, shallow mine or charge of explosives placed in a mine or other confined space to break up rock or other material.  Historically, it also referred to a type of small mortar.  |Example: The miners used fougade blasting to create new tunnels in the hard rock. 矿工们使用爆破孔爆破来在坚硬的岩石中开凿新的隧道。|近义词：mine, blast, charge |反义词：N/A
