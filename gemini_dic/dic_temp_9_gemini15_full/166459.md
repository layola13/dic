prerevised:/ˌpriːrɪˈvaɪzd/| adj.| 预先修改的；修订前的 |  Describes something that has been revised before the current or final version. It implies that further revisions might be necessary.  | Example: The prerevised manuscript contained several errors.  预先修改的手稿包含几个错误。 |近义词: preliminary, earlier, earlier version|反义词: final, revised, perfected
