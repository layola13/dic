reballots:/ˌriːˈbæləts/| n. |复选；重新投票|The process of voting again, often to resolve a dispute or clarify a previous vote.  This term is less common than "revote" or "second ballot".|例句：After a tie, reballots were held to determine the winner.  = 平局之后，进行了复选以确定获胜者。

例句：The controversial decision led to calls for reballots in several districts. = 有争议的决定导致了几个地区的重新投票呼吁。


近义词：revote, second ballot, recount


反义词：final vote, conclusive vote


词性：名词
