judith:/ˈdʒuːdɪθ/| n. |朱迪思|A female given name of Hebrew origin, meaning "woman of Judea" or "Jewess".  It is a biblical name, associated with the courageous and strong Judith in the Apocrypha, who single-handedly saved her city from the Assyrian army.
