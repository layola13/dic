massaged:/məˈsɑːʒd/| v.|按摩；揉捏；篡改|To rub or knead (a part of the body) with the hands, especially to relieve pain or stiffness.  It can also refer to manipulating something subtly to change its appearance or meaning.|例句：She massaged her aching shoulders. 她按摩了她酸痛的肩膀。|例句：He massaged the figures to make the results look better. 他篡改了数据，使结果看起来更好。|近义词：rubbed, kneaded, manipulated|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite of relieving pain would be causing pain.
