freesia:/ˈfriːziə/| n. |小苍兰|A freesia is a plant with sweet-smelling flowers, often used in bouquets.  They come in various colors, including white, yellow, orange, pink, purple, and red. |例句：The freesia's delicate fragrance filled the room.  小苍兰的芬芳充满了整个房间。

例句：She received a beautiful bouquet of freesias for her birthday.  她收到了一个美丽的生日小苍兰花束。


近义词：iris (虹膜), gladiolus (剑兰)


反义词：无
