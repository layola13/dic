infix: /ˈɪnfɪks/ | n. | 中缀符, 中缀运算符 | An infix is a symbol or operator that is placed between the two operands on which it operates.  Infix notation is the standard way of writing mathematical expressions, where the operator is placed between the operands (e.g., 2 + 2). | Example: In the expression "2 + 2", "+" is the infix operator. |近义词: operator (in this context) |反义词: prefix, postfix
