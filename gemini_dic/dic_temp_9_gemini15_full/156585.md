pickoff:/ˈpɪkɔːf/| vt. n.|盗垒，摘除，拣出|To steal a base in baseball by throwing the ball to a teammate who tags the runner before he reaches the base.  Also, to remove something undesirable; to select something from a group.|例句：The pitcher attempted a pickoff throw to first base. 投手试图向一垒传球盗垒。

The gardener picked off the withered flowers. 园丁摘掉了枯萎的花朵。

近义词：steal (baseball), remove, select |反义词：leave, keep, retain |动词
