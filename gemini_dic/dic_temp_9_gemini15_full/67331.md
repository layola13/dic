extemporizers:/ɪkˈstempərˌaɪzərz/ | n. |即兴演讲者；临场发挥者 |People who speak or perform without preparation.  They are skilled at delivering speeches or performances spontaneously and effectively. |Example: The conference featured several accomplished extemporizers who captivated the audience with their insightful and engaging presentations.  那些会议上的一些熟练的即兴演讲者以其富有洞察力和引人入胜的演讲吸引了观众。|近义词：improvisers, ad-libbers |反义词：scripted speakers, prepared speakers
