converging:/kənˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 汇聚的；趋同的 | Coming together from different directions; tending to meet at a point.  | Example: The converging lines of the perspective drawing created a sense of depth.  透视画中汇聚的线条营造出一种深度感。  The converging opinions of the experts led to a unified recommendation.专家的意见趋于一致，从而得出了统一的建议。 |近义词：meeting, merging, uniting | 反义词：diverging, separating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rivers are converging near the delta. = 河流在三角洲附近汇合。
Their opinions are converging on a single point. = 他们的意见正在趋于一致。


一般过去时:
The paths converged at the old oak tree. = 小路在古老的橡树下汇合。
The two companies converged their efforts on the new project. = 两家公司将他们的努力集中在新项目上。


现在完成时:
The evidence has converged to support the theory. = 证据已经汇聚在一起支持这一理论。
Many research studies have converged on the same conclusion. = 许多研究都得出了相同的结论。


过去完成时:
The roads had converged before reaching the city center. = 道路在到达市中心之前就已经汇合了。
All the arguments had converged on a single, overwhelming point. = 所有论点都汇聚在一个压倒性的要点上。


将来时:
The two forces will converge on the battlefield tomorrow. = 两军将于明日在战场上汇合。
The opinions of the panel will likely converge during the discussion. = 专家组的意见可能会在讨论中趋于一致。


虚拟语气:
If the lines converged, it would form a triangle. = 如果这些线汇合，它将形成一个三角形。
If the two cultures converged, a new society might be formed. = 如果两种文化融合，可能会形成一个新的社会。


被动语态:
The opinions were converged into a consensus. = 意见最终达成共识。
The two streams were converged by a dam. = 两条溪流被水坝汇合。


疑问句:
Where do the roads converge? = 道路在哪里汇合？
Do their views converge on this matter? = 他们在这个问题上的观点是否一致？


否定句:
The lines do not converge at a single point. = 这些线不在单点汇合。
Their opinions did not converge on the issue. = 他们在这个问题上的意见并不一致。
