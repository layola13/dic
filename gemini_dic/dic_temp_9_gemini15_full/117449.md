meditation:/ˌmɛdəˈteɪʃən/| n. |冥想，沉思 |The practice of focusing your mind on a particular object or thought, typically in order to achieve a state of relaxation or heightened awareness.  冥想是一种专注于特定物体或思想的精神练习，通常是为了达到放松或提高意识的状态。| I practice meditation every morning to reduce stress. 我每天早上练习冥想以减轻压力。| contemplation, reflection, mindfulness | action, activity | noun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I meditate every day. = 我每天冥想。
She meditates before important meetings. = 她在重要的会议之前冥想。

一般过去时:
He meditated for an hour yesterday. = 他昨天冥想了一个小时。
They meditated on the meaning of life. = 他们沉思生命的意义。

现在完成时:
I have meditated for years. = 我已经冥想多年了。
She has never meditated before. = 她以前从未冥想过。

过去完成时:

They had meditated for hours before reaching a decision. = 他们冥想了几个小时才做出决定。

将来时:
