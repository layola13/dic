conger:/ˈkɒŋɡər/ /ˈkɑːŋɡər/ | n. |康吉鳗，海鳗 |A conger is a large, eel-like fish that lives in the sea.  Congers are typically found in burrows on the seabed. They are known for their long, slender bodies and powerful jaws. |例句：The fisherman caught a huge conger eel. 渔夫钓到了一条巨大的康吉鳗。

The conger eel slithered through the rocks. 康吉鳗在岩石间蜿蜒游动。|近义词：eel (a more general term)|反义词：无
