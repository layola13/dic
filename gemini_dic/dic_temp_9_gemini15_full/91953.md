hypermodern:/ˈhaɪpərmɒdən/| adj. |超现代的，后现代的 | Extremely modern or advanced; often implying a reaction against or a development beyond modernism.  It suggests a style that goes beyond the simple functionality of modernism and incorporates elements of irony, playfulness, and eclecticism. |例句：The building's hypermodern design incorporated both cutting-edge technology and traditional materials. 这座建筑的超现代设计融合了尖端技术和传统材料。

例句：His hypermodern paintings blend abstract forms with pop culture imagery. 他的超现代绘画将抽象形式与流行文化图像融合在一起。


近义词：ultramodern, avant-garde, postmodern, contemporary


反义词：traditional, classical, antiquated, old-fashioned


词性:形容词
