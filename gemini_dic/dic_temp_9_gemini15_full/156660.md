USAGE->

一般现在时:
The book contains many beautiful pictures. = 这本书包含许多美丽的图片。
These pictures depict scenes from the war. = 这些图片描绘了战争中的场景。


一般过去时:
She showed me pictures of her family. = 她给我看了她家人的照片。
We took many pictures at the party. = 我们在派对上拍了很多照片。


现在完成时:
I have seen these pictures before. = 我以前见过这些照片。
Have you finished looking at the pictures? = 你看完照片了吗？


过去完成时:
I had already seen the pictures before she showed them to me. = 在她给我看照片之前，我已经看过了。
They had taken many pictures before the battery died. = 电池没电之前他们已经拍了很多照片。


将来时:
We will take pictures at the wedding. = 我们将在婚礼上拍照。
I will show you the pictures later. = 我稍后会给你看照片。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better camera, I would take better pictures. = 如果我有一台更好的相机，我会拍出更好的照片。
I wish I had taken more pictures. = 我真希望我拍更多照片。


被动语态:
The pictures were taken by a professional photographer. = 这些照片是由专业摄影师拍摄的。
The pictures are being displayed in the gallery. = 这些照片正在画廊展出。


疑问句:
Are there any pictures in this folder? = 这个文件夹里有什么照片吗？
What pictures did you like best? = 你最喜欢哪些照片？


否定句:

I didn't take any pictures. = 我没有拍任何照片。
