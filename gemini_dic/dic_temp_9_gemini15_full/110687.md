lineages: /ˈlɪniɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 血统；家系；世系 |  The word "lineages" refers to lines of descent from an ancestor; a sequence of ancestors and their descendants.  It often implies a continuous and unbroken line of inheritance, whether genetic, familial, or cultural. |  例句：The study traced the lineages of several prominent families. 这项研究追溯了几个显赫家族的血统。

例句：Researchers are studying the lineages of different strains of the virus. 研究人员正在研究不同病毒菌株的世系。


近义词：ancestry, descent, pedigree, family line, genealogy | 反义词：None (Antonyms are not directly applicable as it describes a concept, not a quality that has a direct opposite) | 词性：名词
