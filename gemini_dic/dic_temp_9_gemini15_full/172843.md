quadrupedal: /kwɑːˈdruːpɛdəl/ | adj. | 四足的，用四足行走的 | Having four feet.  |例句：Many mammals are quadrupedal, using all four limbs for locomotion.  许多哺乳动物是四足动物，用四肢行走。

例句：The baby giraffe wobbled on its quadrupedal legs, trying to keep its balance. 小长颈鹿摇摇晃晃地用四条腿站着，努力保持平衡。


近义词：four-footed, tetrapodal | 反义词：bipedal
