例句：The chef's numerous persnicketinesses delayed the opening of the restaurant.  这位厨师的诸多吹毛求疵耽误了餐厅的开业。|近义词：fastidiousness, meticulousness, fussiness, over-criticalness|反义词：sloppiness, carelessness, laxness|词性：noun
