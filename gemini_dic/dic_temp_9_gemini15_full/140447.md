oks:/oʊks/| interj.|好，行，知道了|Expression of agreement or acknowledgement.  It's a shortened, informal version of "okay".|例句：Oks, I'll do it. 好吧，我去做。
例句：Oks, see you later. 行，待会儿见。|近义词：okay, alright, all right, fine|反义词:no, nope


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am okay with that. = 我同意。
It is okay to leave now. = 现在离开是可以的。


一般过去时:
It was okay, but not great. = 还不错，但不是很好。
Everything was okay until the end. = 一切都好，直到最后。


现在完成时:
It has been okay so far. = 到目前为止还不错。
Everything has been okay since then. = 从那以后一切都很顺利。


过去完成时:
It had been okay until the accident. = 在事故发生之前一切都很好。
I had thought it was okay to go. = 我以为去是可以的。


将来时:
It will be okay. = 一切都会好起来的。
Everything will be okay in the end. = 最后一切都会好起来的。


虚拟语气:
It would be okay if you helped me. = 如果你能帮我，那就太好了。
I wish it was okay to leave early. = 我希望可以早点离开。


被动语态:
It was decided that it would be okay. = 决定这是可以的。
It is considered okay to disagree. = 不同意被认为是可以的。


疑问句:
Is it okay to ask a question? = 我可以问个问题吗？
Is it okay if I leave early? = 我早点走可以吗？


否定句:
It's not okay to cheat. = 作弊是不对的。
It wasn't okay to treat her like that. = 那样对待她是不对的。


PHRASE->
Okay then = 那么好吧
Okay, I get it = 好吧，我明白了
It's okay to... = 可以...
That's okay = 没关系
Okay, bye = 好吧，再见
