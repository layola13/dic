misallotment:/ˌmɪs.əˈlɒt.mənt/ | n. | 分配错误，错误分配 | The misallocation of resources led to project delays. 资源分配错误导致项目延误。|近义词：maldistribution, misappropriation |反义词：proper allocation, correct distribution


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company misallocates funds every year. = 公司每年都会错误分配资金。
This project suffers from misallotment of resources. = 这个项目遭受资源分配错误的困扰。


一般过去时:
They misallotted the budget last quarter. = 他们上个季度错误分配了预算。
The government misallotted funds for the new infrastructure project. = 政府错误地分配了新基础设施项目的资金。


现在完成时:
The department has misallotted its resources repeatedly. = 该部门反复错误分配资源。
The company has misallotted a significant portion of its marketing budget. = 公司错误分配了很大一部分市场营销预算。


过去完成时:

The committee had misallotted the budget before the project even started. = 项目开始之前，委员会就已经错误分配了预算。


将来时:

The board predicts that the project will suffer from misallotment of funds in the future. = 董事会预测该项目未来将遭受资金错误分配的困扰。


虚拟语气:
If they had not misallotted the funds, the project would have been successful. = 如果他们没有错误分配资金，项目就会成功。
I wish they hadn't misallotted the resources to less important tasks. = 我希望他们没有将资源错误分配给不太重要的任务。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
