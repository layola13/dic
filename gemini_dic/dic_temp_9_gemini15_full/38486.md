conchologist:/ˌkɒŋkəˈlɒdʒɪst/| n. |贝壳学家|A person who studies mollusks, especially their shells.|例句：The conchologist carefully examined the intricate spiral of the conch shell.  贝壳学家仔细检查了海螺壳复杂的螺旋结构。

例句：Many conchologists are also passionate collectors of shells from around the world. 许多贝壳学家也是来自世界各地的贝壳的热情收藏家。


近义词：malacologist (though malacology is a broader field encompassing the study of all mollusks, not just shells)  贝类学家（虽然贝类学是一个更广泛的领域，包括所有软体动物的研究，而不仅仅是贝壳）


反义词：无


词性：名词
