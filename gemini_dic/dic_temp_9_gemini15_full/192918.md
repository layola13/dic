seaweeded:/ˈsiˌwiːdɪd/| adj.|海藻覆盖的；长满海藻的|Covered with seaweed.|The rocks were seaweeded and slippery.|近义词： seaweed-covered |反义词:clean, bare.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rocks are seaweeded. = 岩石上长满了海藻。
The boat is seaweeded. = 船上长满了海藻。
