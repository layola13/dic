podocarp:/ˈpɒdəkɑːrp/ | n. |罗汉松属植物 | Podocarps are coniferous evergreen trees or shrubs, belonging to the family Podocarpaceae. They are characterized by their distinctive seed cones, often fleshy and berry-like.  Many species are cultivated as ornamentals for their attractive foliage and form. | Example:  The garden featured several magnificent podocarps, their dark green foliage providing a striking contrast to the brighter blooms. = 花园里种植了几棵壮观的罗汉松，它们深绿色的叶子与鲜艳的花朵形成了鲜明的对比。 |近义词: conifer, evergreen |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用deciduous tree作为对比) |  n.
