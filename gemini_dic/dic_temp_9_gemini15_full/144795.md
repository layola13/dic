over-flowed:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊd/| v.|溢出，泛滥|The verb "overflowed" is the past tense of "overflow," meaning to flow over the brim or edge of a container; to exceed the capacity of something; to be so full as to run over.|例句：The river overflowed its banks after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。|例句：My inbox overflowed with emails. 我的收件箱里邮件堆积如山。|近义词：inundate, flood, deluge|反义词：underflow, drain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river overflows its banks during the rainy season. = 雨季河水会漫过河岸。
The cup overflows when you pour too much milk. = 当你倒入太多牛奶时，杯子会溢出来。


一般过去时:
The bathtub overflowed because I forgot to turn off the faucet. = 因为我忘了关水龙头，浴缸溢出来了。
The river overflowed last night, causing widespread flooding. = 昨晚河水泛滥，造成了大范围的洪水。


现在完成时:
The river has overflowed several times this year. = 今年河水已经泛滥好几次了。
The bucket has overflowed, spilling water all over the floor. = 水桶溢出来了，水洒了一地。


过去完成时:
The basement had overflowed before the firefighters arrived. = 消防员到达之前，地下室就已经积水了。
The dam had overflowed, leading to a catastrophic flood. = 水坝已经决堤，导致了灾难性的洪水。


将来时:
The river will overflow if it rains heavily again. = 如果再次下大雨，河水将会泛滥。
If we don't fix the leak, the sink will overflow tomorrow. = 如果我们不修理漏水的地方，水槽明天就会溢出来。


虚拟语气:
If the cup had not overflowed, we would have had enough milk. = 如果杯子没有溢出来，我们就足够牛奶了。
I wish the river hadn't overflowed and damaged the crops. = 我希望河水没有泛滥并损坏庄稼。


被动语态:
The streets were overflowed by the rising tide. = 街道被上涨的潮水淹没了。
The reservoir was overflowed due to the unexpected rainfall. = 由于意外的降雨，水库溢满了。


疑问句:
Did the river overflow last night? = 河水昨晚泛滥了吗？
Has the sink overflowed again? = 水槽又溢出来了？


否定句:
The river did not overflow this year. = 今年河水没有泛滥。
The container did not overflow even though it was almost full. = 容器即使几乎满了也没有溢出来。
