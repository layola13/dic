doubtlessly:/ˈdaʊtɪslɪ/ | adv. |无疑地，肯定地 | without any doubt; certainly.  The adverb "doubtlessly" emphasizes certainty and leaves no room for doubt. It's often used to express strong conviction or to eliminate any possibility of questioning a statement. |例句：Doubtlessly, the new policy will improve the company's efficiency. = 无疑，这项新政策将会提高公司的效率。
He doubtlessly knew the answer. = 他无疑知道答案。|近义词：certainly, undoubtedly, undeniably, surely, inevitably |反义词：doubtfully, questionably, possibly, perhaps
