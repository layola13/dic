larkspur: /ˈlɑːrkspɜːr/| n. |飞燕草|A tall plant with colorful flowers, often blue, purple, or pink.  It's known for its spurred petals.  Larkspurs are popular garden flowers and some species are poisonous.|The larkspur in her garden was in full bloom.  她花园里的飞燕草盛开了。|  delphinium,  consolida |  n/a
