gaggling:/ˈɡæɡlɪŋ/| n. |嘎嘎叫声；（尤指鹅的）成群喧闹 |The sound of geese making a lot of noise, typically in a group. |例句：The gaggling of geese filled the morning air.  鹅的嘎嘎叫声充满了清晨的空气。|近义词：chattering, squawking, cackling |反义词:silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The geese are gaggling loudly. = 鹅们大声地嘎嘎叫。
Many birds are gaggling near the lake. = 许多鸟儿在湖边嘎嘎叫。

一般过去时:
The geese gaggled all night. = 鹅们整夜嘎嘎叫。
I heard the ducks gaggling yesterday. = 昨天我听到鸭子在嘎嘎叫。

现在完成时:
The geese have been gaggling since dawn. = 鹅们从黎明开始就一直嘎嘎叫。
The ducks have gaggled incessantly. = 鸭子一直在不停地嘎嘎叫。

过去完成时:
The geese had gaggled for hours before they finally settled down. = 鹅们在最终安静下来之前已经嘎嘎叫了几个小时。
The birds had gaggled loudly before the storm broke. = 在暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们大声地嘎嘎叫。
