nonconsolidated:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪd/| adj.|未合并的，未巩固的|Not consolidated; separate and uncombined.  This term often refers to financial assets, geological formations, or data. |例句：The nonconsolidated accounts showed a loss for the quarter.  未合并的报表显示本季度亏损。

例句：The geologist studied the nonconsolidated sedimentary rocks. 地质学家研究了未固结的沉积岩。


近义词：unconsolidated, separate, fragmented, loose

反义词：consolidated, unified, combined, integrated

词性: adjective
