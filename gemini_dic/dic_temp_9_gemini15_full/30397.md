centimeter: /ˈsɛntɪmiːtər/ | n. |厘米 |A unit of length equal to one hundredth of a meter.  It is a unit in the metric system. |The table is 150 centimeters long. 这张桌子长150厘米。  The insect is only a few centimeters long. 这昆虫只有几厘米长。|millimeter, inch |mile, kilometer |名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  The usage revolves around its function as a unit of measurement.
