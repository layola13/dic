istic: /ˈɪstɪk/ | adj. |  …的；具有…性质的；属于…的 |  Suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to," "characterized by," or "of the nature of."  It is often used to create adjectives from nouns or roots. | 例句：Artistic creations often reflect the artist's emotions. 艺术创作往往反映了艺术家的情感。  The characteristic features of the disease include fever and rash.  这种疾病的特征包括发烧和皮疹。 | 近义词：characteristic, typical, relating to | 反义词：uncharacteristic, atypical
