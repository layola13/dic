outscent:/ˈaʊtˌsɛnt/| vt.|胜过…的香味|To surpass in scent or fragrance.|The lilies outscent all the other flowers in the garden. 百合花的香味胜过花园里所有其他的花。|近义词：outdo, surpass, excel|反义词：underwhelm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roses outscent the lilies. = 玫瑰花的香味胜过百合花。
This perfume outscent all the others. = 这款香水比所有其他的都香。


一般过去时:
The lavender outscent the jasmine yesterday. = 昨天薰衣草的香味胜过茉莉花。
That old perfume outscent the new one. = 那款旧香水比新款更香。


现在完成时:
This flower has outscent all the others in the competition. = 这朵花在比赛中胜过了所有其他的花。
The new fragrance has outscent its predecessor. = 新香型已经超过了它的前身。


过去完成时:
The garden had outscent the nearby fields before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，花园的香味已经胜过了附近的田野。
The roses had outscent the carnations by the time the judges arrived. = 等评委到达时，玫瑰花的香味已经胜过了康乃馨。


将来时:
The jasmine will outscent the roses in the spring. = 春天茉莉花的香味将胜过玫瑰花。
This new scent will outscent all others on the market. = 这种新的香味将胜过市场上的所有其他香味。


虚拟语气:
If only this perfume outscent the others! = 但愿这款香水能胜过其他的！
I wish this rose would outscent the lilies. = 我希望这朵玫瑰的香味能胜过百合花。


被动语态:
The lilies were outscent by the roses. = 百合花的香味被玫瑰花胜过了。(很少用被动语态)
The other flowers were outscent by the jasmine. = 其他花朵的香味被茉莉花胜过了。(很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Does this perfume outscent the other one? = 这款香水比另一款更香吗？
Will the new fragrance outscent the old one? = 新香型会比旧香型更香吗？


否定句:
This flower doesn't outscent the others. = 这朵花的香味没有胜过其他的。
The lilies did not outscent the roses. = 百合花的香味没有胜过玫瑰花。


PHRASE->
outscent the competition = 在竞争中胜出
outscent the market = 胜过市场上的所有产品
outscent the rest = 胜过其他所有
outscent the room = 香味充满房间
outscent everything else = 胜过其他所有事物
