meanest:/ˈmiːnɪst/| adj.|最卑鄙的，最吝啬的，最刻薄的|The adjective "meanest" is the superlative form of "mean," meaning unkind, cruel, or stingy.  It describes the highest degree of meanness.  It can refer to a person's character, actions, or even objects (in a figurative sense).|例句：He was the meanest person I've ever known. 他是我认识的最卑鄙的人。

He told the meanest joke at the party. 他在派对上讲了一个最刻薄的笑话。|近义词：cruelest, nastiest, most unkind, most stingy|反义词：kindest, gentlest, most generous
