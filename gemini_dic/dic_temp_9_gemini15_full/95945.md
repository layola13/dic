incube:/ɪnˈkjuːb/| vt.|孵化；酝酿；培养|To incubate means to keep eggs warm until they hatch, or to keep something developing slowly.|例句：The hen incubated her eggs for three weeks.  母鸡孵蛋三周。|例句：The company is incubating a new product. 公司正在酝酿一款新产品。|近义词：hatch, brood, foster|反义词：destroy, neglect
