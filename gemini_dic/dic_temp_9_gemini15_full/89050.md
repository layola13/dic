holograph:/ˈhɒləɡræf/| n.|全息照片；亲笔信；亲笔签名|A holograph is a document entirely written in the handwriting of its author.  It is often used in reference to a will or other legal document written and signed by the testator.  The term also refers to a three-dimensional image created by holography.|例句：The will was declared invalid because it wasn't a holograph.遗嘱因并非亲笔书写而被宣告无效。

The museum displayed a stunning holograph of a dinosaur. 博物馆展出了一幅令人惊叹的恐龙全息图。|近义词：autograph, handwritten document|反义词：typed document, printed document
