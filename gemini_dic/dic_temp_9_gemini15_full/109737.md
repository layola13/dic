levered:/ˈlɛvərd/ | adj. | 杠杆化的；负债的 |  Having a high level of debt in relation to equity.  This term is commonly used in finance to describe a company or investment that uses significant debt financing.  |  例句：The levered buyout was highly successful.  = 这项杠杆收购非常成功。

The company's highly levered balance sheet poses a significant risk. =  这家公司高度杠杆化的资产负债表构成了重大风险。


近义词：indebted, highly geared | 反义词：unlevered, debt-free | 词性：形容词
