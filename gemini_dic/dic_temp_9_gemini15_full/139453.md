obsoletion:/ˌɒbsəˈliːʃən/| /ˌɑːbsəˈliːʃən/ | n. |淘汰；废弃；过时 |The process of becoming obsolete; the state of being obsolete.  It refers to the gradual decline in the use of something until it is no longer used or needed. This can apply to technology, practices, ideas, or even words. |例句：The obsoletion of the old system was a gradual process. 旧系统的淘汰是一个循序渐进的过程。

The rapid obsoletion of technology requires constant adaptation.  技术的快速淘汰需要不断适应。


近义词：depreciation, obsolescence, outdating,  disuse,  discarding |反义词：innovation, modernization,  renewal,  upgrade
