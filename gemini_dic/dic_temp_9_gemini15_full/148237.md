oversimplify:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/ | vt. |过分简化；简单化 | To make something simpler than it really is, often to the point of inaccuracy or misrepresentation.  |  He oversimplified the complex political situation, leading to a misunderstanding of the key issues. 他过分简化了复杂的政治局势，导致对关键问题的误解。 | simplify, reduce | complicate, elaborate | 动词
