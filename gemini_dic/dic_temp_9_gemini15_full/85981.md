headships:/ˈhɛdʃɪps/| n.|领导职位；首长职务；领导能力|The position or duties of a head, especially of a school or department.  It often refers to the responsibilities and authority associated with a leadership role.|例句：The headship of the department was offered to Professor Smith.  史密斯教授被提名担任系主任。

例句：She showed exceptional headship in guiding the team through the crisis.她在带领团队度过危机时展现了非凡的领导能力。


近义词：leadership, directorship, superintendence, management

反义词：subordination, followership


词性：名词
