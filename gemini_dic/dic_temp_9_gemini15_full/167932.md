printed-out: /ˈprɪntɪd aʊt/ | adj. | 打印出来的 |  Describes something that has been printed from a computer or other digital device.  It implies a tangible, physical copy resulting from a digital process. |


例句：

I have a printed-out copy of the report. = 我有一份打印出来的报告。

The printed-out instructions were easy to follow. = 打印出来的说明书很容易理解。


近义词：printed, hard copy


反义词：digital, onscreen


词性：形容词
