roe:/roʊ/| n.|鱼卵；精子；（某些植物的）种子；|Fish eggs, especially those of a fish used for food; the mass of eggs of a fish.  It can also refer to the sperm of male animals, and the seeds of some plants.|例句：Salmon roe is a delicacy in many cultures.  鲑鱼卵在许多文化中是一种美味佳肴。|例句：The male fish releases its roe into the water. 雄性鱼类将精子释放到水中。|近义词：spawn, eggs, milt (for sperm)|反义词：N/A|
