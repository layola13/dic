new-found:/ˈnuːfaʊnd/| adj.|新发现的，新获得的|Newly discovered or acquired.  The adjective "new-found" emphasizes the recent nature of the discovery or acquisition.|例句：He celebrated his new-found freedom. 他庆祝他新获得的自由。

例句：Her new-found confidence was evident in her presentation. 她新获得的自信在她演示中显而易见。


近义词：newly discovered, recently acquired, fresh


反义词：long-established, long-held, familiar


词性：形容词
