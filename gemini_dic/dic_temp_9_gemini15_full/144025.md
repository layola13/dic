outreigns:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnz/| v.|统治，凌驾于；超过|to reign or rule over; to surpass or exceed in power, influence, or extent.|例句：The king outreigns all other figures in the kingdom. 国王统治着王国中所有其他人物。|例句：Her influence outreigns that of any other member of the team. 她的影响力超过了团队中任何其他成员。|近义词：rule, govern, dominate, surpass, exceed|反义词：obey, submit, be subordinate to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The queen outreigns the entire country. = 女王统治着整个国家。
His talent outreigns that of his peers. = 他的才能超过了他的同龄人。

一般过去时:
The emperor outreigned his predecessors. = 皇帝的统治时间超过了他的前任。
Her beauty outreigned all others at the ball. = 在舞会上，她的美貌胜过所有人。

现在完成时:
The dynasty has outreigned for centuries. = 这个王朝已经统治了几个世纪。
His influence has outreigned geographical boundaries. = 他的影响力已经超越了地域界限。

过去完成时:
The empire had outreigned many smaller kingdoms. = 帝国已经统治了许多较小的王国。
The old regime had outreigned for decades before the revolution. = 旧政权在革命前已经统治了几十年。

将来时:
The new leader will outreign his opponents. = 新领导人将战胜他的对手。
This technology will outreign all previous methods. = 这项技术将超越所有以前的方法。

虚拟语气:
If he were to outreign his rivals, he would be a great king. = 如果他能战胜他的对手，他将成为一位伟大的国王。
I wish my influence could outreign the negativity. = 我希望我的影响力能够战胜消极情绪。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively.)  The kingdom was outreigned by a powerful emperor. = 王国被一位强大的皇帝统治着。
The previous record was outreigned by the new athlete. =  之前的纪录被新的运动员打破了。(此处outreigned更贴切的翻译是“打破”或“超过”)


疑问句:
Does the current government outreign the people's will? =  现任政府是否凌驾于人民的意志之上？
Will his ambition ever outreign his common sense? = 他的野心最终是否会战胜他的常识？

否定句:
The small nation did not outreign its powerful neighbor. = 小国没有凌驾于其强大的邻国之上。
Her efforts did not outreign the challenges. = 她的努力未能克服挑战。


PHRASE->
outreigns all others = 超越所有其他
outreigns in influence = 在影响力方面胜过
outreigns in power = 在权力方面胜过
outreigns in importance = 在重要性方面胜过
outreigns the competition = 胜过竞争
