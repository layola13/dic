dubiousness:/ˈduːbiəsnəs/| n. |怀疑；可疑；不确定|The dubiousness of his claims led to further investigation.  他的说法令人怀疑，导致了进一步的调查。| uncertainty, suspicion, doubt | certainty, confidence, trust |名词


例句:

Her dubiousness about the project's success was evident in her hesitant tone.  她对项目能否成功的怀疑，从她犹豫的语气中显而易见。


The dubiousness of the evidence led the jury to acquit the defendant.  证据的可疑性导致陪审团宣告被告无罪。


近义词:

uncertainty, skepticism, questionableness, doubtfulness


反义词:

certainty, confidence, assurance, trust


词源:

From dubious + -ness.  源自dubious（可疑的）加上后缀-ness（表示性质或状态）。  Dubious itself comes from the Latin dubitare, meaning "to doubt".  Dubious本身来自拉丁语dubitare，意思是“怀疑”。
