friendly:/ˈfrɛndli/| adj.|友好的，友善的|Characterized by or exhibiting friendliness; amiable; sociable.  It describes a person, place, or situation that is pleasant, kind, and welcoming.|例句：She gave me a friendly smile. 她对我友善地笑了笑。|例句：The atmosphere in the classroom was friendly and relaxed. 教室里的气氛友好而轻松。|近义词：amiable, amicable, cordial, genial, pleasant|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, unfriendly, antagonistic
