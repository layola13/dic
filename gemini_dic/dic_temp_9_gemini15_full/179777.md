reevaluates:/ˌriːɪˈvæljuˌeɪts/| vt.|重新评估；再评价|To reconsider and assess (something) again.|The company reevaluates its marketing strategy every quarter.  公司每季度都会重新评估其营销策略。|They reevaluated the situation after receiving new information. 他们在收到新信息后重新评估了形势。|assess, reassess, reconsider|accept, approve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reevaluates his life choices every year. 他每年都会重新评估自己的人生选择。
The committee reevaluates the budget regularly. 委员会定期重新评估预算。

一般过去时:
She reevaluated her career path after a year of working.  她工作一年后重新评估了自己的职业道路。
The judge reevaluated the evidence presented.  法官重新评估了提交的证据。

现在完成时:
They have reevaluated the project's feasibility. 他们已经重新评估了项目的可行性。
The government has reevaluated its environmental policies. 政府已经重新评估了其环境政策。

过去完成时:
After the accident, he had reevaluated his priorities in life. 事故发生后，他重新评估了自己的人生优先事项。
They had reevaluated the risks before making a decision. 他们在做出决定之前已经重新评估了风险。

将来时:
She will reevaluate her options after considering all the factors. 她考虑所有因素后将重新评估自己的选择。
The team will reevaluate the data next week.  团队下周将重新评估数据。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reevaluate my decision. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新评估我的决定。
It is important that they reevaluate the plan before proceeding. 在继续之前，他们重新评估计划很重要。

被动语态:
The proposal was reevaluated by the board of directors.  该提案由董事会重新评估。
The cost of the project was reevaluated due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，项目的成本被重新评估。

疑问句:
Will the company reevaluate its pricing strategy? 公司会重新评估其定价策略吗？
Have they reevaluated the safety procedures? 他们已经重新评估安全程序了吗？

否定句:
He did not reevaluate his position despite the new evidence. 尽管有新的证据，他也没有重新评估自己的立场。
The committee did not reevaluate the budget this year. 委员会今年没有重新评估预算。
