heatwave:/ˈhiːtweɪv/| n. |热浪，酷暑|A period of unusually hot weather, typically lasting for days or weeks.|例句：The heatwave caused widespread power outages.  热浪导致了大范围的停电。|例句： We are experiencing a severe heatwave this summer. 今年夏天我们正在经历一场严重的热浪。|近义词：heat spell, hot spell|反义词：cold snap, freeze
