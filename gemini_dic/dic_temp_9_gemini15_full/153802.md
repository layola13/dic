perca:/ˈpɜːrkə/| n.|鲈鱼|Perca is a genus of freshwater fish in the family Percidae, commonly known as perches.  They are characterized by their spiny fins and elongated bodies.  Many species are popular game fish. |例句：The lake is known for its abundance of perch.  这个湖以盛产鲈鱼而闻名。|近义词：pike, bass|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other types of fish.
