niqab:/nɪˈkɑːb/| n. |尼卡布面罩；面纱|A niqab is a veil worn by some Muslim women that covers the face, leaving only the eyes visible.  It is often worn in conjunction with a hijab.|例句：She wore a niqab that only revealed her eyes. 她戴着只露出眼睛的尼卡布面罩。|例句：The niqab is a subject of much debate in many Western countries. 尼卡布在许多西方国家是一个备受争议的话题。|近义词：veil, face veil, burqa (although burqa is a more encompassing garment)|反义词：None directly, but could be considered the opposite of revealing clothing.
