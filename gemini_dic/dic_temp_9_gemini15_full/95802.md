inconvertible:/ˌɪnkənˈvɜːrtəbl/ | adj. |不可兑换的；不可转换的 |  Incapable of being converted or changed into something else;  unable to be exchanged for something else of equal value, typically currency.  | 例句：The currency is inconvertible into foreign exchange.  这种货币不能兑换成外汇。

The gold standard made the currency inconvertible. 金本位制使得货币不可兑换。


近义词：irredeemable, nonconvertible, unchangeable


反义词：convertible, exchangeable


词源:  The word "inconvertible" is formed from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "convertible," which itself derives from the verb "convert," ultimately from the Latin verb "convertire," meaning "to turn around" or "to change."  The sense of exchange is a later development.
