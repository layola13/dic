private:/ˈpraɪvɪt/|/ˈpraɪveɪt/| adj.|私人的；私密的；秘密的| Belonging to or affecting a particular person or group of people, and not shared with others; not public.  It can also refer to something kept secret or confidential. |例句：This is private property; you can't enter without permission. 这块地是私人的；未经许可你不能进入。
例句：She shared some private details of her life with her close friend. 她与她的密友分享了她生活的一些私密细节。|近义词：personal, confidential, secret, secluded|反义词：public, open, common, shared
