sighed:/saɪd/| v.|叹息，叹气|to exhale audibly, especially expressing sadness, relief, or tiredness.|She sighed deeply, her shoulders slumping. 她深深地叹了口气，肩膀也垮了下来。|groaned, whispered, murmured|laughed, cheered|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sighs every time he thinks about his lost love. = 他每次想起失去的爱人都会叹气。
She sighs contentedly after a long bath. = 洗完热水澡后她舒心地叹了口气。


一般过去时:
He sighed with relief when he heard the good news. = 当他听到好消息时，他如释重负地叹了口气。
She sighed sadly as she watched the sunset. = 她看着日落，悲伤地叹了口气。

过去完成时:
