collectivize:/kəˈlektɪvaɪz/| vt.|集体化；使公有化|To bring under the control of a collective, especially to change from private to state ownership or control.|The government decided to collectivize agriculture.  政府决定将农业集体化。|The Soviet Union collectivized its farms in the 1930s. 苏联在20世纪30年代对农场进行了集体化。|nationalize, socialize|privatize, individualize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collectivize the land. = 他们将土地集体化。
The government collectivizes the industry. = 政府将工业集体化。


一般过去时:
The country collectivized its farms. = 这个国家将它的农场集体化了。
The Soviet Union collectivized its agriculture. = 苏联将它的农业集体化了。


现在完成时:
The government has collectivized most of the industries. = 政府已经将大部分工业集体化了。
Many countries have collectivized their resources. = 许多国家已经将它们的资源集体化了。


过去完成时:
They had collectivized the land before the revolution. = 他们在革命前已经将土地集体化了。
The people had collectivized their efforts to fight the drought. = 人们为了对抗干旱已经将他们的努力集体化了。


将来时:
The state will collectivize the banks. = 国家将银行集体化。
They will collectivize the transportation system. = 他们将交通系统集体化。


虚拟语气:
If the government collectivized the healthcare system, it might be more efficient. = 如果政府将医疗体系集体化，它可能会更高效。
I wish they had not collectivized the farms. = 我希望他们没有将农场集体化。


被动语态:
The farms were collectivized in the 1950s. = 农场在20世纪50年代被集体化了。

疑问句:

否定句:
