nonconscientiously:/ˌnɒnkɒnʃiˈenʃəsli/| adv. |不认真地；不谨慎地；漫不经心地 |Acting without moral principle or sense of responsibility; carelessly.  |例句：He nonconscientiously completed the assignment, leading to numerous errors. 他不认真地完成了作业，导致许多错误。


例句：She approached her studies nonconscientiously, resulting in poor grades. 她漫不经心地对待学习，导致成绩很差。


近义词：carelessly, negligently, recklessly, thoughtlessly, perfunctorily


反义词：conscientiously, carefully, diligently, meticulously


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".
* **conscientious**: adjective meaning "wishing to do what is right, especially to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly".  This word itself derives from "conscience" (a person's moral sense of right and wrong).

用法说明:

This adverb modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.  It emphasizes a lack of care, thoroughness, or moral consideration in the action.  It's often used to describe actions done in a haphazard or irresponsible manner.


USAGE->

This adverb is not typically used in specific grammatical tenses in the way that verbs are.  Its usage is dependent on the verb it modifies.  Here are examples demonstrating its use in different sentence structures:


简单句: He worked nonconscientiously. 他工作马虎。

复合句: Because he was tired, he completed the task nonconscientiously. 因为他累了，所以他草率地完成了任务。

疑问句: Did she act nonconscientiously in that situation? 在那种情况下，她是否行为不端？

否定句: He did not act nonconscientiously; he was simply overwhelmed. 他并非行为不端；他只是不堪重负。

PHRASE->

There aren't common idiomatic phrases directly using "nonconscientiously". Its meaning is typically conveyed within the sentence structure itself.  However, phrases that convey similar meanings can be used:

* in a slipshod manner: 以粗制滥造的方式
* with a lack of care: 不小心
* halfheartedly: 半心半意地
* without a second thought: 毫不犹豫地
* carelessly and recklessly: 粗心大意且鲁莽地
