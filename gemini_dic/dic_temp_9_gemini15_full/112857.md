lucklessly:/ˈlʌklislɪ/| adj. | 不幸地，倒霉地 |  Unlucky; without luck.  It describes an event or situation marked by bad luck or misfortune. |例句：He lucklessly tripped and fell, breaking his arm. 他不幸绊倒摔倒，摔断了胳膊。

例句：She lucklessly missed the winning lottery number by one digit.  她不幸只差一位数就错过了彩票中奖号码。


近义词：unluckily, unfortunately, unhappily, disastrously |反义词：luckily, fortunately, happily, successfully |词性：形容词
