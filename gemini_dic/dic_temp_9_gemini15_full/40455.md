contravener:/kənˈtreɪvənər/| n. |违反者，违规者 |A contravener is a person who violates a law, rule, or agreement.  They are someone who acts against or disobeys established regulations or norms. |Example: The contravener was fined for exceeding the speed limit.  违规者因超速被罚款。 The company, a known contravener of environmental regulations, faced severe penalties.  这家公司一贯违反环境法规，面临严厉处罚。|近义词：offender, violator, transgressor |反义词：conformist, observer
