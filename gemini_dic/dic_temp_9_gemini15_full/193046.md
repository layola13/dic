secrete:/sɪˈkriːt/ | vt. |分泌；隐藏；隐瞒 | To produce and release a substance from a cell or gland; to keep something secret. |例句：The glands secrete hormones into the bloodstream.  腺体将荷尔蒙分泌到血液中。|例句：She secreted the money in a safe place. 她把钱藏在一个安全的地方。|近义词：excrete, hide, conceal |反义词：reveal, divulge |动词

The body secretes various hormones. = 身体分泌各种荷尔蒙。
The company secretes its financial information. = 这家公司隐瞒其财务信息。
