drawplate:/ˈdrɔːpleɪt/ | n. |拉丝板，拉拔模|A drawplate is a steel plate with a series of precisely sized holes used to reduce the diameter of wire or rod by pulling it through the holes.  The process is called drawing. | The jeweler used a drawplate to refine the silver wire to the perfect thickness. 珠宝匠用拉丝板将银线细化到完美的厚度。  The factory uses drawplates to manufacture different gauges of wire. 工厂使用拉丝板制造不同规格的线材。|die, plate| (no direct antonym, as it describes a specific tool)|名词
