oceanariums:/ˌoʊʃəˈneriəmz/| n. |海洋馆|A large building with tanks of seawater where many different kinds of sea animals are kept and shown to the public.  An oceanarium is a facility dedicated to the exhibition and conservation of marine life. It typically features large tanks holding diverse aquatic species. |The children loved the oceanarium; they spent hours watching the dolphins and sharks.孩子们非常喜欢海洋馆；他们花了几个小时观看海豚和鲨鱼。|aquarium, marine park|desert, land.
