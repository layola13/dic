nips:/nɪps/| n. |轻捏；少量；小吃；(尤指乳房的)轻捏；|A quick light pinch;  a small amount of something; small pieces of food, often sweet; a gentle squeeze, especially of breasts. |例句：She gave the baby's cheek a gentle nip. 她轻轻捏了一下婴儿的脸颊。


例句：He took a nip of his whiskey. 他喝了一口威士忌。


近义词：pinch, bite, snack, nibble


反义词：large amount, feast


词性:名词


USAGE->
