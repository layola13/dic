carbonating:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 充气；使碳酸化 | To impregnate (a liquid, especially water) with carbon dioxide, making it fizzy.  | Example: The machine is carbonating the soda water. 这台机器正在给苏打水充气。  The restaurant carbonated their own beverages. 这家餐厅自己生产碳酸饮料。 | 近义词：fizzing, effervescing | 反义词：deflating, de-carbonating | 动词

一般现在时:
The factory constantly carbonates the drinks. = 工厂不断地给饮料充气。
She carbonates her own water at home. = 她在家自己给水充气。

一般过去时:
They carbonated the lemonade before the party. = 他们在派对前给柠檬水充气。
He carbonated the sparkling wine. = 他给起泡酒充气。

现在完成时:

They have carbonated several batches of drinks today. = 他们今天已经充了几批饮料的气。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
If we had a carbonation machine, we would carbonate our own drinks. = 如果我们有碳酸化机器，我们会自己给饮料充气。
I wish I could carbonate this juice easily. = 我希望我可以轻松地给这种果汁充气。

被动语态:
The drinks are carbonated in a special tank. = 饮料在一个特殊的罐子里充气。
The water is being carbonated by a high-pressure pump. = 水正在通过高压泵充气。

疑问句:
Are they carbonating the beer now? = 他们现在正在给啤酒充气吗？
Is the soda carbonated naturally or artificially? = 苏打水是天然碳酸的还是人工碳酸的？

否定句:
They don't carbonate the juice; they buy it pre-carbonated. = 他们不给果汁充气；他们买的是预先充好气的。
We didn't carbonate the water because we ran out of CO2. = 我们没有给水充气，因为二氧化碳用完了。
