elytrum: /ˈɛlɪtrəm/ | n. |鞘翅 | The hardened forewing of a beetle, serving as a protective covering for the delicate hindwings.  | Example: The beetle's elytra were a shiny, iridescent green.  这只甲虫的鞘翅是闪亮的虹绿色。 |近义词: wing case, tegmen (in some insects) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑hindwing作为功能上的对比) | 名词
