USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river curves gently. = 河水缓缓地弯曲。
The road curves sharply to the right. = 道路急转弯向右。

一般过去时:
The ball curved beautifully. = 球漂亮地旋转着。
The path curved around the hill. = 小路绕过小山丘弯曲。

现在完成时:
The road has curved several times. = 这条路已经弯曲了好几次。
The river has curved dramatically since the flood. = 自洪水以来，河道已经发生了巨大的弯曲。

过去完成时:
The road had curved sharply before reaching the bridge. = 在到达桥梁之前，道路已经急转弯了。
The path had curved unexpectedly, leading us astray. = 小路出乎意料地弯曲，导致我们迷路了。

将来时:
The road will curve dramatically ahead. = 前方道路将急剧弯曲。
The river will curve further downstream. = 河流在下游将进一步弯曲。

虚拟语气:
If the road curved less, it would be easier to drive. = 如果道路弯曲程度较小，驾驶起来会更容易。
I wish the path curved less steeply. = 我希望这条小路不要弯曲得那么陡峭。

被动语态:
The road was curved to avoid the swamp. = 道路被弯曲以避开沼泽。
The path was cleverly curved to enhance the view. = 小路巧妙地弯曲以增强视野。

疑问句:
Does the road curve sharply here? = 这条路在这里急转弯吗？
Where does the river curve most dramatically? = 河流在哪里弯曲得最厉害？

否定句:
The road doesn't curve much. = 这条路弯曲不多。
The path didn't curve unexpectedly. = 小路没有出乎意料地弯曲。
