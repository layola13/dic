overbilling:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪlɪŋ/ | n. |过度收费，超额收费 |The act of charging someone more than the actual cost of a service or product.  This can be intentional or unintentional, often due to errors in billing systems or deliberate fraud. |Example: The customer complained about the overbilling on their phone bill. 客户抱怨他们的电话账单被多收费了。|近义词：overcharging, excessive billing |反义词：underbilling
