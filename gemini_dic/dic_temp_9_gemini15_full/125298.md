multigauge: /ˈmʌltiɡeɪdʒ/ | n. | 多功能测量仪，多表计 | A multigauge is an instrument that measures and displays multiple parameters simultaneously.  It's commonly used in vehicles and industrial settings to monitor various aspects of a system's performance. | Example: The pilot consulted the multigauge to check the engine temperature, fuel level, and altitude.  飞行员查看多功能测量仪以检查发动机温度、油量和高度。 |近义词：multi-meter, indicator panel | 反义词：单一测量仪 (single gauge)
