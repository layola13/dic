retaliation:/ˌrɪtæliˈeɪʃən/| n. |报复；反击|The act of retaliating; returning a similar injury, wrong, or insult.  It often implies a deliberate and proportionate response to a perceived attack or injustice.|例句：The bombing was in retaliation for the assassination of the president.  这次爆炸是对总统遇刺的报复。|例句：Her harsh words were met with immediate retaliation. 她尖刻的话语遭到了立即的反击。|近义词：revenge, reprisal, retribution|反义词：forgiveness, pardon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company retaliates against unfair competition. = 这家公司对不正当竞争采取报复行动。
He retaliates whenever he feels insulted. = 他每当感到受辱时就会进行报复。


一般过去时:
The government retaliated with sanctions. = 政府以制裁作为报复。
She retaliated by spreading rumors. = 她通过散布谣言进行报复。


现在完成时:
They have retaliated for years against their rivals. = 他们多年来一直在报复他们的竞争对手。
The team has retaliated strongly after losing the first game. = 在输掉第一场比赛后，球队强势反击。


过去完成时:
He had retaliated before anyone realized what had happened. = 在任何人意识到发生了什么之前，他已经进行了报复。
The country had retaliated by severing diplomatic ties. = 该国通过断绝外交关系进行了报复。


将来时:
The country will retaliate against any further aggression. = 国家将对任何进一步的侵略采取报复行动。
We will retaliate if our demands are not met. = 如果我们的要求得不到满足，我们将进行报复。


虚拟语气:
If they attacked us, we would retaliate immediately. = 如果他们攻击我们，我们将立即进行报复。
I wish I hadn't retaliated so harshly. = 我真希望我没有那样严厉地进行报复。


被动语态:
The attack was seen as a retaliatory measure. = 这次袭击被视为报复措施。
Retaliation was swift and decisive. = 报复迅速而果断。


疑问句:
Will the government retaliate? = 政府会进行报复吗？
How will they retaliate against the attack? = 他们将如何报复这次袭击？


否定句:
The company did not retaliate against the negative review. = 该公司没有对负面评价进行报复。
He chose not to retaliate despite the provocation. = 尽管受到了挑衅，他还是选择不进行报复。


PHRASE->
in retaliation for = 作为对……的报复
