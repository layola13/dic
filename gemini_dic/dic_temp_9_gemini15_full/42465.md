counterfeit:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪt/ | adj., n., v. | 伪造的；假冒的；赝品；伪造；假冒 |  To make an illegal copy of something in order to deceive; a copy that is intended to deceive. |例句：The police seized a large quantity of counterfeit money.  警方查获了大量伪钞。

The painting was a counterfeit; it wasn't a genuine Picasso. 这幅画是赝品；它不是毕加索的真迹。


近义词：fake, forged, spurious, imitation | 反义词：genuine, authentic, real


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He counterfeits signatures. 他伪造签名。
They counterfeit banknotes. 他们伪造钞票。


一般过去时:
The artist counterfeited famous paintings.  这位艺术家伪造了名画。
The forger counterfeited the document. 伪造者伪造了文件。


现在完成时:
The police have uncovered a large counterfeit operation. 警方已经发现了一个大型伪造犯罪团伙。
They have counterfeited thousands of dollars in fake bills.  他们伪造了数千美元的假钞。


过去完成时:
He had counterfeited the documents before the police arrived. 在警方到达之前，他已经伪造了文件。
The fraudsters had counterfeited the seals before the contract was signed. 骗子在合同签署之前就已经伪造了印章。


将来时:
They will counterfeit more money to fund their operation. 他们将伪造更多钱来资助他们的行动。
She will counterfeit the official documents for her plan. 她将伪造官方文件以实现她的计划。


虚拟语气:
If he counterfeited the evidence, he would go to jail. 如果他伪造证据，他就会坐牢。
If she had counterfeited the passport, she would have been arrested. 如果她伪造了护照，她就会被逮捕。


被动语态:
The counterfeit money was quickly detected. 伪钞很快就被发现了。
The painting was discovered to be a counterfeit. 这幅画被发现是赝品。


疑问句:
Did he counterfeit the documents? 他是否伪造了文件？
Were the banknotes counterfeited? 这些钞票是伪造的吗？


否定句:
He did not counterfeit the signature. 他没有伪造签名。
The company does not counterfeit products. 这家公司不伪造产品。


PHRASE->
counterfeit money: 伪钞
counterfeit goods: 假货
counterfeit documents: 伪造文件
counterfeit drugs: 假药
counterfeit passport: 假护照

词源:
源自中古英语 *counterfete，来自古法语 contrefait，意思是“模仿；复制”。  “contre-” 表示“对抗；反对”，而 “fait” 是“做”的意思。  因此，其本义是“做成相反的；模仿”。
