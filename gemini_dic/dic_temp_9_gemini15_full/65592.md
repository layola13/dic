eval:/ɪˈvæljuˌeɪt/ | vt. |评估；评价；估价 |To form an idea of the amount, number, or value of something.  |例句：The teacher will eval the students' work. 老师将评估学生的作业。|例句：We need to eval the risks before we proceed. 我们需要在继续之前评估风险。 |近义词：assess, appraise, judge |反义词：ignore, neglect |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He evaluates the situation carefully. = 他仔细评估了形势。
She evaluates her employees' performance regularly. = 她定期评估员工的表现。

一般过去时:
They evaluated the project's success. = 他们评估了项目的成功。
The committee evaluated the proposals. = 委员会评估了这些提案。

现在完成时:
We have evaluated all the options. = 我们已经评估了所有选项。
She has evaluated the impact of the policy. = 她已经评估了这项政策的影响。

过去完成时:
They had evaluated the data before making a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经评估了数据。
The team had evaluated the risks prior to the launch. = 团队在启动之前已经评估了风险。

将来时:
We will evaluate the results next week. = 我们下周将评估结果。
She will evaluate the students' essays tomorrow. = 她明天将评估学生的论文。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would evaluate the proposal more thoroughly. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更彻底地评估该提案。
I wish I had evaluated the risks more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地评估了风险。

被动语态:
The proposal will be evaluated by the committee. = 该提案将由委员会评估。
The project's success was evaluated by independent experts. = 项目的成功由独立专家评估。

疑问句:
How will the project be evaluated? = 项目将如何评估？
Have they evaluated the consequences? = 他们评估了后果吗？

否定句:
They did not evaluate the potential risks. = 他们没有评估潜在的风险。
We have not yet evaluated the full impact. = 我们尚未评估全部影响。
