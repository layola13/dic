confirmors:/kənˈfɜːrmərz/ | n. | 证实者；确认者 | Those who confirm or verify something.  They provide evidence or testimony to support a claim or statement.  | Example: The judge asked for additional confirmors to corroborate the witness's testimony.  法官要求提供更多证实者来证实证人的证词。|近义词：verifiers, validators, witnesses |反义词：deniers, refuters
