encode:/ɪnˈkoʊd/ | vt. |编码，加密 | To convert information or data into a code, especially for transmission or storage.  | Example: The message was encoded before being sent.  信息在发送前被编码了。|近义词：cipher, encrypt |反义词：decode, decrypt
