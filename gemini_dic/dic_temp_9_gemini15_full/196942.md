shaftless:/ʃæftləs/| adj. | 无轴的，不带轴的 | Lacking a shaft; without a shaft.  This term is often used to describe machines or tools that operate without a central rotating shaft.  | Example: The shaftless mixer is easy to clean because there are no moving parts to disassemble.  无轴搅拌机易于清洁，因为没有需要拆卸的活动部件。 | 近义词：shaft-free, axel-less | 反义词：shafted
