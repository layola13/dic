havener:/ˈheɪvənər/| n. |避难港居民；港湾居民；避风港管理者|A person who lives in or manages a haven or safe harbor.  The term is not widely used and often appears in specialized contexts, particularly historical or literary ones.|例句：The havener guided the small fishing boats into the sheltered cove.  避风港的管理者引导小型渔船进入避风的港湾。|近义词：harbor master, port warden|反义词：None specifically contrasting.
