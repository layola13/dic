prediscover:/ˌpriːdɪˈskʌvər/| vt.|重新发现，再次发现|To discover something again or anew; to find something that was known but had been forgotten or lost.|例句：The archaeologists prediscovered ancient ruins buried under the sand. 考古学家重新发现了埋在沙子下的古代遗址。
例句：After years of searching, she prediscovered her lost diary.  多年寻找后，她重新发现了丢失的日记。|近义词：rediscover, find again|反义词：lose, forget
