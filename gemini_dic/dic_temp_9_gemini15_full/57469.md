draftiness:/ˈdræftinəs/| n.|通风不良；漏风；寒气；气流|The state or quality of being drafty; the presence of drafts of air.  It often implies an unpleasant chilliness or discomfort due to cold air entering a space. |例句：The old house suffered from a terrible draftiness. 这栋老房子通风很糟糕。

例句：The window's draftiness made the room feel cold. 窗户漏风使房间感觉很冷。


近义词：drowsiness, chilliness, coldness, airiness


反义词：airtightness, insulation, warmth
