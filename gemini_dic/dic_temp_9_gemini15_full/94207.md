imperfection:/ˌɪmpərˈfɛkʃən/| n. |不完美；缺点；瑕疵 |The state or quality of being imperfect; a flaw or defect.  Imperfections are common in life and can be sources of both frustration and growth. |例句：The painting has a few minor imperfections, but overall, it's beautiful. 这幅画有一些细微的瑕疵，但总的来说很漂亮。

例句：Despite its imperfections, the system works reasonably well.  尽管这个系统有缺陷，但它运行得还算不错。


近义词：flaw, defect, blemish, fault, shortcoming |反义词：perfection


词源：  From Middle French *imperfection, from Latin *imperfectio, from imperfectus (see imperfect).
