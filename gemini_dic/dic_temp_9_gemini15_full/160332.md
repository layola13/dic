poor-house:/ˈpʊrˌhaʊs/| n. |济贫院，贫民院 |A poorhouse is a publicly funded institution providing shelter and support for the poor and destitute.  It's a historical term, often associated with harsh conditions. |例句：In the 19th century, many poor families relied on the poorhouse for survival. = 在19世纪，许多贫困家庭依靠济贫院生存。

例句：The conditions in the poorhouse were deplorable, with overcrowding and inadequate food. = 济贫院的条件很糟糕，人满为患，食物也不足。


近义词：workhouse, almshouse, pauper institution |反义词：mansion, palace


词性：名词

词源:
The word "poorhouse" is a compound word combining "poor" (meaning impoverished or needy) and "house" (meaning a dwelling).  Its etymology reflects its function as a house for the poor.  The term emerged during the period when such institutions were common, primarily in the 18th and 19th centuries.
