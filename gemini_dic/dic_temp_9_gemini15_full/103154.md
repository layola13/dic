jersey:/ˈdʒɜːrsi/|n.|球衣；毛线衫；泽西岛|A jersey is a close-fitting knitted shirt, typically worn by sports players.  It can also refer to a type of soft knitted fabric or to the island of Jersey.|例句：He wore a red jersey with the team logo. 他穿着印有球队标志的红色球衣。

例句：She knitted a warm jersey for her son. 她为儿子织了一件暖和的毛衣。|近义词：sweater, shirt, top|反义词：coat, jacket
