dog-paddle:/ˈdɒɡˌpædəl/|/ˈdɔːɡˌpædəl/| v.|狗刨式游泳|To swim using a method where the arms and legs move alternately in a circular motion, similar to the paddling motion of a dog.  This is a relatively inefficient and unsophisticated swimming style.|例句：The children were dog-paddling in the shallow end of the pool. 孩子们在游泳池的浅水区狗刨式游泳。

例句：He dog-paddled frantically to reach the shore. 他拼命地狗刨式游泳以到达岸边。|近义词：paddle, sprawl (in water)|反义词：swim (efficiently), freestyle, breaststroke|动词
