polychoral:/ˈpɒliˌkɔːrəl/| adj. |多声部的，多合唱队的 | Relating to or consisting of several choirs singing together or alternately.  The term often implies a complex interplay of musical lines and textures.  | Example:  The composer's polychoral motet showcased the virtuosity of the four choirs.  作曲家的多声部经文歌展现了四个合唱队的精湛技艺。 |近义词：multichoral, polyphonic |反义词：monochoral, unison
