carbohydrate:/ˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪt/| n. |碳水化合物|A carbohydrate is any of a large group of organic compounds occurring in foods and living tissues and including sugars, starch, and cellulose. They contain hydrogen and oxygen in the same ratio as water (2:1) and typically can be broken down to release energy in the animal body.|例句：Carbohydrates are an important source of energy for the body.  碳水化合物是人体重要的能量来源。|例句：Many fruits are rich in carbohydrates. 许多水果富含碳水化合物。|近义词：saccharide, sugar|反义词：protein, fat
