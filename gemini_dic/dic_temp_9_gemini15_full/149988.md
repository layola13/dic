palmigrade:/ˈpæl.mɪ.ɡreɪd/| adj. |掌行的；用手掌行走的|  Relating to or characterized by palmigrade locomotion, in which the animal walks on its palms, with its digits spread out.  This is in contrast to digitigrade (walking on toes) and plantigrade (walking on soles).| Example:  Lemurs are known for their palmigrade locomotion. 狐猴以其掌行运动而闻名。|近义词: plantigrade (in some contexts), manus-grade |反义词: digitigrade
