mycologies:/maɪˈkɒlədʒiz/ | n. |真菌学 |The scientific study of fungi, including their genetic and biochemical properties, their taxonomy and their use to humans.  |例句：Mycologists are studying the effects of climate change on fungal populations. 真菌学家正在研究气候变化对真菌种群的影响。|例句：Advances in mycologies have led to new antibiotics and other medicines. 真菌学方面的进展已经导致了新的抗生素和其他药物的出现。|近义词：fungology |反义词：无
