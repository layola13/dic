nerve:/nɜːrv/ | /nɝv/| n. | 神经；胆量；勇气 |  A nerve is a bundle of fibers that transmits impulses from the brain to other parts of the body, or from one part of the body to another.  It can also refer to courage or boldness. |  例句：The dentist numbed the nerve before drilling. 牙医在钻牙之前先麻醉了神经。  He showed great nerve in facing the angry crowd. 他面对愤怒的群众表现出极大的勇气。 | 近义词：nerve fiber, nerve cell,  courage, bravery, boldness | 反义词：cowardice, fear, timidity |  名词
