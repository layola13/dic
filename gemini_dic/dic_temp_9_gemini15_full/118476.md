mesis:/ˈmiːsɪs/ | n. | 呕吐；反胃 | The feeling of nausea and the urge to vomit.  It can be caused by various factors, including illness, food poisoning, or motion sickness. | Example:  The smell of the fish caused me a fit of mesis.  (鱼腥味让我一阵反胃。) | 近义词：vomiting, nausea, retching | 反义词：appetite, well-being |  名词
