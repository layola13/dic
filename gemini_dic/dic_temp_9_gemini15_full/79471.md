gloating:/ˈɡloʊtɪŋ/|/ˈɡləʊtɪŋ/| adj. |幸灾乐祸的；沾沾自喜的 |Expressing or feeling great pleasure at one's own success or another's misfortune.  |例句：He couldn't hide his gloating smile after winning the competition. 他在赢得比赛后无法掩饰他幸灾乐祸的笑容。

例句：Her gloating remarks about her promotion were very insensitive. 她关于自己升职的幸灾乐祸的言论非常不敏感。


近义词：smug, self-satisfied, triumphant, complacent

反义词：sympathetic, empathetic, compassionate
