dharnas:/ˈdɑːrnɑːz/| n. |静坐示威|A dharna is a form of protest in which a group of people sit-in at a place to show their opposition to something.  It is often a non-violent demonstration. |例句：The farmers held a dharna outside the parliament building to protest against the new agricultural laws.  农民在议会大厦外举行静坐示威，抗议新的农业法。|近义词：sit-in, protest, demonstration |反义词：compliance, obedience
