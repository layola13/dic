restore:/rɪˈstɔːr/| vt.|恢复，修复，重建|To return something to its former state or condition.|例句：The museum is working to restore the damaged painting. 博物馆正在努力修复受损的画作。|近义词：rehabilitate, recover, reinstate|反义词：destroy, damage, ruin

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They restore antique furniture. = 他们修复古董家具。
The government restores the old building. = 政府修复了那座旧建筑。


一般过去时:
He restored his old car to its former glory. = 他把他的旧车修复到了以前的辉煌状态。
The team restored the power after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，团队恢复了电力。


现在完成时:
They have restored the damaged bridge. = 他们已经修复了损坏的桥梁。
She has restored her health after a long illness. = 她经过长期疾病后恢复了健康。


过去完成时:
They had restored the church before the anniversary. = 他们在周年纪念日之前修复了教堂。
She had restored her confidence before the presentation. = 她在演示之前恢复了信心。


将来时:
They will restore the park next year. = 他们明年将修复公园。
The company will restore the system after the update. = 公司将在更新后恢复系统。


虚拟语气:
If they had more money, they would restore the old house. = 如果他们有更多钱，他们就会修复老房子。
I wish they would restore the old tradition. = 我希望他们能恢复古老的传统。


被动语态:
The painting was restored by a skilled artist. = 这幅画是由一位技艺娴熟的艺术家修复的。
The system is being restored after the power outage. = 断电后系统正在恢复中。


疑问句:
Can they restore the data? = 他们能恢复数据吗？
Will the building be restored to its original state? = 这座建筑物会恢复到原来的状态吗？


否定句:
They couldn't restore the damaged files. = 他们无法恢复损坏的文件。
The mechanic didn't restore the engine properly. = 技工没有正确地修复发动机。
