crisscross:/ˈkrɪskrɒs/| /ˈkrɪskrɔːs/| adj., v., n.| 交叉的；交错的；画叉； |To intersect or lie in a pattern of intersecting lines; a pattern of intersecting lines.|例句：The streets crisscrossed the town. 街道纵横交错地穿过小镇。  The threads were crisscrossed to create a strong fabric. 线交叉编织以形成坚固的织物。|近义词：intertwine, intersect, cross|反义词：parallel, straight
