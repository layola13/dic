prehandle: /priːˈhændl/ | vt. |预处理，预先处理 | To prehandle something means to deal with or process it before the main process begins.  It often involves preparing or modifying data or materials to make the subsequent process more efficient or effective. | 例句：The data needs to be prehandled before it can be analyzed. 数据在分析之前需要预处理。  The engineers prehandled the materials to improve the quality of the final product. 工程师预先处理了材料以提高最终产品的质量。 | 近义词：preprocess, prepare, condition | 反义词：postprocess
