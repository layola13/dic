hyperoxygenized:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒənaɪzd/ | adj. | 高氧化的；过度充氧的 |  Containing or having been subjected to an abnormally high level of oxygen.  This term often refers to situations where tissues or blood have more oxygen than is typically considered normal or healthy.  | 例句：The hyperoxygenized blood flowed swiftly to the damaged tissue. 高氧化的血液迅速流向受损组织。 The patient received hyperoxygenized air to improve their breathing. 病人吸入高氧空气以改善呼吸。 | 近义词：oversaturated (with oxygen), superoxygenated | 反义词：hypoxic, anoxic
