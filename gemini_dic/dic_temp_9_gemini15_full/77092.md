garrot:/ɡæˈrɒt/| n. vt.|勒死；绞杀；|The act of strangling someone, typically with a wire or cord around the neck.|例句：The prisoner was garrotted by his captors. 囚犯被他的绑匪勒死了。|近义词：strangle, throttle, suffocate|反义词：resuscitate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He garrots his victims. = 他勒死他的受害者。
They garrot the chickens before cooking. = 他们在烹饪前把鸡勒死。


一般过去时:
The assassin garrotted the politician. = 刺客勒死了这位政治家。
The thief garrotted the security guard. = 小偷勒死了保安。


现在完成时:
The police have garrotted the suspect. = 警方已经勒死了嫌疑犯。(This usage is less common and may imply a legal execution, not a murder.)
They have garrotted the animal humanely. = 他们已经仁慈地勒死了动物。


过去完成时:

He had garrotted the bird before it could escape. = 他在鸟逃走之前已经勒死了它。
