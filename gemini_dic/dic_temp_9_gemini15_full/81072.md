graining:/ˈɡreɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 纹理，木纹；颗粒结构 | The imitation of wood grain on a surface.  It refers to the process of creating or reproducing the appearance of wood grain on a surface, typically using paint or other materials.  The pattern and texture produced by this process. | Example: The graining on the cabinet doors looks very realistic.  橱柜门上的木纹看起来非常逼真。


The graining technique added depth and richness to the painted furniture.  这种木纹技巧为油漆家具增添了深度和质感。|  texturing, veining | smoothness | n.
