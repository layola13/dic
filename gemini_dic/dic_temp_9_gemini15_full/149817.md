paleography:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfi/| n. |古文字学|The study of ancient writing systems and handwriting.  It involves the analysis of the physical characteristics of writing, such as script, handwriting style, and the materials used.  Paleographers aim to identify the author, date, and geographical origin of documents. |The paleography of the manuscript reveals it was written in the 12th century.|近义词：paleology, diplomatics|反义词:None
