purporting:/pərˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 声称；意图；假装 |  to claim or allege something; to imply or suggest something; to profess or pretend to be something.  |  The document purporting to be a royal decree was a forgery.  （声称是皇家法令的文件是伪造的。） The study purports to show a link between diet and cancer. (这项研究声称显示了饮食与癌症之间的联系。) | claiming, alleging, professing | denying, disproving, refuting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is purporting to be a doctor. = 他声称自己是一名医生。
The company purports to offer the best service. = 公司声称提供最好的服务。

一般过去时:
She purported to be a princess. = 她假装是位公主。
They purported to have solved the problem. = 他们声称已经解决了这个问题。

现在完成时:
The article has purported to expose a political scandal. = 这篇文章声称揭露了一起政治丑闻。
They have purported to be experts in the field. = 他们自称是该领域的专家。

过去完成时:
He had purported to know the answer. = 他声称知道答案。
She had purported to be innocent. = 她声称自己是无辜的。

将来时:
They will purport to have new evidence. = 他们将会声称有新的证据。
The report will purport to explain the situation. = 这份报告将会声称解释了这种情况。

虚拟语气:
If he purported to be honest, I would trust him. = 如果他声称诚实，我会相信他。
I wish he hadn't purported to be an expert. = 我真希望他没有自称是专家。

被动语态:
The painting was purported to be a masterpiece. = 这幅画被声称是杰作。
The information is purported to be confidential. = 这些信息据称是机密的。

疑问句:
Does the document purport to be authentic? = 这份文件声称是真的吗？
What does the letter purport to say? = 这封信声称说了什么？

否定句:
The study does not purport to offer a definitive answer. = 这项研究并没有声称提供最终答案。
He did not purport to understand the situation. = 他并没有声称理解这种情况。


PHRASE->
purport to do something = 声称要做某事
purport to be something = 声称是某物
purport to have done something = 声称做过某事
purport to know something = 声称知道某事
purporting to be = 声称是
