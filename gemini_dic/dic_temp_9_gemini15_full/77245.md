gastromalacia:/ˌɡæstrəʊməˈleɪʃə/| n. |胃软化|Softening of the stomach tissue.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including inflammation, infection, or ischemia (reduced blood flow). Gastromalacia can lead to symptoms such as abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, and digestive disturbances.  It is not a commonly used term and often requires further specification regarding the cause and location within the stomach.|The patient presented with severe abdominal pain and was diagnosed with gastromalacia due to a severe infection.|  |  |n.
