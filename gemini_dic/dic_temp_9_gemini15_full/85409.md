harvest:/ˈhɑːrvɪst/| n. & v.| 收获；收割 | The gathering of ripe crops; the season when crops are gathered.  Also, the result or product of one's work or efforts. |例句：The harvest was plentiful this year. 今年收成丰厚。
The farmer began harvesting his wheat. 农民开始收割小麦。|近义词：reap, gather, glean |反义词：sowing, planting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Farmers harvest their crops in the autumn. = 农民在秋天收割庄稼。
She harvests the benefits of her hard work. = 她收获着她努力工作的成果。


一般过去时:
They harvested the corn last week. = 他们上周收割了玉米。
We harvested a large amount of apples from the orchard. = 我们从果园里收获了大量的苹果。


现在完成时:
The farmers have harvested all the rice. = 农民们已经收割了所有的稻米。
She has harvested the rewards of her dedication. = 她已经收获了她奉献的回报。


过去完成时:
They had harvested the wheat before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经收割了小麦。
The company had harvested significant profits in the previous quarter. = 公司在上个季度已经获得了可观的利润。


将来时:
We will harvest the grapes next month. = 我们下个月将收割葡萄。
They will harvest the data and analyze the results. = 他们将收集数据并分析结果。


虚拟语气:
If we had harvested the crops earlier, we wouldn't have lost so much. = 如果我们早点收割庄稼，我们就不会损失那么多。
I wish I had harvested more tomatoes last summer. = 我希望去年夏天我多收割一些西红柿。


被动语态:
The crops were harvested by machine. = 庄稼是用机器收割的。
The data is being harvested and processed. = 数据正在被收集和处理。


疑问句:
When will you harvest the potatoes? = 你什么时候收土豆？
Have they harvested the soybeans yet? = 他们已经收割大豆了吗？


否定句:
They didn't harvest the apples because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他们没有收苹果。
We haven't harvested the entire field yet. = 我们还没有收割整个田地。
