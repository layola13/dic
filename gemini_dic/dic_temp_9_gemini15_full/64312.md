equivocatingly: /ɪˌkwɪˈvɒkɪteɪŋli/ | adv. | 模棱两可地；含糊其辞地 |  Speaking or acting in a way that allows for more than one interpretation, often intentionally misleading.  | 例句：He answered equivocatingly, avoiding a direct response. 他含糊其辞地回答，避免直接回应。  He responded equivocatingly, leaving me unsure of his intentions. 他含糊其辞地回应，让我不确定他的意图。| 近义词：ambiguously, vaguely, evasively, uncertainly | 反义词：clearly, directly, explicitly, unequivocally |  词性: 副词
