goatlike: /ˈɡoʊtlaɪk/ | adj. | 山羊似的；顽固的 | Resembling a goat in appearance or behavior; stubborn or headstrong. |例句：The goatlike creature leaped across the chasm.  那只山羊似的生物跃过了峡谷。

The child, with a goatlike stubbornness, refused to eat his vegetables.  那个孩子，像山羊一样固执，拒绝吃蔬菜。

近义词：capricious, headstrong, stubborn, obstinate | 反义词：docile, obedient, compliant


词源：
The word "goatlike" is a straightforward compound word formed from "goat" and the suffix "-like."  "Goat" comes from Old English *gāt, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gaitō. The suffix "-like" is a common English suffix meaning "resembling" or "having the qualities of."  Thus, "goatlike" literally means "like a goat."

用法说明:
goatlike 主要用作形容词，描述人或事物像山羊一样。它可以指外貌，例如山羊般的胡须或角，也可以指行为，例如固执、顽固或任性。
