hymnologist:/ˌhɪmˈnɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |赞美诗学家，圣歌研究专家 | A hymnologist is a scholar who studies hymns, their history, authorship, theology, and musical settings.  They often work in theological settings, archives, or universities. |  Dr. Smith, a renowned hymnologist, gave a lecture on the evolution of hymnody in the Protestant church. 史密斯博士，一位著名的赞美诗学家，做了一个关于新教教会圣歌演变的讲座。 |  hymnographer, hymn writer |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
