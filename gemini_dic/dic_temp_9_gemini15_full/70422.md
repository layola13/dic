fighting:/ˈfaɪtɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|战斗，打架；奋斗；好斗的|The act of engaging in a physical or non-physical battle or struggle.  It can refer to a physical fight, a competition, or a metaphorical struggle.|例句：The two boxers were fighting fiercely. 这两个拳击手在激烈地搏斗。

例句：They are fighting for their rights. 他们在为争取自己的权利而斗争。|近义词：battling, struggling, competing, wrestling|反义词：peace, harmony, cooperation
