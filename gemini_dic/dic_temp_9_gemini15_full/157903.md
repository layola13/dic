plaited:/ˈpleɪtɪd/ | adj. v. | 编织的；编成的； | Woven or braided together.  The word typically refers to hair, but can also describe other materials. | 例句：Her hair was plaited into two long braids.  她的头发编成了两条长辫子。

例句：The artisan skillfully plaited the reeds to create a sturdy basket.  工匠熟练地编织芦苇，制作了一个结实的篮子。


近义词：braided, interwoven, intertwined | 反义词：unbraided, loose


词源:  From Old English *plehtan, meaning "to plait, braid, weave".  The word shares roots with words in other Germanic languages describing similar actions.
