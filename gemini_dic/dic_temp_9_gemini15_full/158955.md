plus-minus:/ˈplʌs ˈmaɪnəs/ | adj. n. |正负，加减；大约 |  Describes a quantity or value that is approximately a certain amount, allowing for a small variation above or below that amount.  It can also refer to the mathematical symbols "+" and "-". |例句：The temperature is plus-minus two degrees. 温度大约是正负二度。 The final score was 10 plus-minus 2 points. 最终得分大约是10分上下2分。|近义词：approximately, roughly, about |反义词：precisely, exactly |词性: adjective, noun
