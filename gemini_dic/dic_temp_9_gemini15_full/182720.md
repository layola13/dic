repairable:/rɪˈpeərəbl/| adj.| 可修复的，可修理的 | Capable of being repaired; able to be fixed.  | The damage to the car was repairable.  这辆车的损坏是可以修复的。|  fixable, mendable | irreparable, unfixable |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The phone is repairable. = 这部手机是可以修理的。
This machine is repairable with the right tools. = 用合适的工具，这台机器是可以修理的。

一般过去时:
The vase was repairable before it was dropped. = 花瓶在掉落之前是可以修复的。
The damage was repairable, but it cost a lot. = 损坏是可以修复的，但是费用很高。

现在完成时:
The computer has been repairable since the technician arrived. = 自从技师到达后，电脑就可以修理了。
The broken chair has been repairable for a week. = 这把坏椅子已经可以修理一周了。


过去完成时:
The engine had been repairable until the explosion. = 引擎在爆炸前是可以修复的。
The bridge had been repairable before the earthquake struck. = 地震发生前，这座桥是可以修复的。


将来时:
The car will be repairable after the parts arrive. = 零件到达后，汽车就可以修理了。
The roof will be repairable once the storm passes. = 一旦暴风雨过去，屋顶就可以修理了。

虚拟语气:
If the damage were repairable, we would fix it ourselves. = 如果损坏是可以修复的，我们会自己修理。
I wish the tear in my clothes were repairable. = 我希望我衣服上的撕裂是可以修复的。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
