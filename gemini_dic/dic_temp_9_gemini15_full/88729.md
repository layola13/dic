hobnobbing:/ˈhɒbnɒbɪŋ/ | v. | 闲聊；攀谈；应酬 |  To hobnob means to spend time in a friendly way with someone, especially someone important or influential.  It often implies informal socializing and networking. |  例句：The politicians were hobnobbing at the party, exchanging pleasantries and making connections.  政客们在聚会上闲聊，互相寒暄，建立联系。

例句：They spent the evening hobnobbing with celebrities. 他们整个晚上都在和名人们闲聊。


近义词：chatting, socializing, mingling, networking, schmoozing


反义词：isolating, withdrawing, shunning


词性：动词
