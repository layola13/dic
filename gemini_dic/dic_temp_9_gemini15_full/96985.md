infantilistic:/ˌɪnfænˈtɪlɪstɪk/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的| characterized by or exhibiting the characteristics of a very young child, especially in a way that is inappropriate for one's age.  This often implies a regression to immature behaviors or thinking. It suggests a lack of maturity and responsibility.|例句：His infantilistic behavior at the meeting surprised everyone. 他在会议上的幼稚行为让所有人都很惊讶。 His refusal to accept responsibility for his actions is infantilistic. 他拒绝为自己的行为负责，这很幼稚。|近义词：childish, immature, puerile, babyish|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up
