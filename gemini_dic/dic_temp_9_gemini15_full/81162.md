granddaddies:/ˈɡrændˌdædiz/| n.|爷爷们；老家伙们|A plural form of granddad, referring to multiple grandfathers or, informally, to old or outdated things.|例句：My granddaddies used to tell me stories about the old west.  我的爷爷们过去常常给我讲关于美国西部的故事。  例句：Those old computers are real granddaddies. 那些旧电脑真是老古董。|近义词：grandfathers, old timers, old fogeys|反义词：youths, youngsters
