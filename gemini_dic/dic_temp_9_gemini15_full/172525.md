pyrites:/ˈpaɪraɪts/| n.| 黄铁矿 | Iron disulfide (FeS2), a common mineral that often appears as brassy yellow crystals. It is a sulfide mineral and is often mistaken for gold due to its appearance.  Pyrites are important sources of sulfur and iron. | Example:  The miners discovered a vein of glittering pyrites.  矿工们发现了一条闪闪发光的黄铁矿脉。


|近义词: iron sulfide, fool's gold |反义词:|  |词性:n.|
