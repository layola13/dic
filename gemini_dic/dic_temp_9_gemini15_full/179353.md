redraft:/ˌriːˈdræft/| vt.|重新起草；修改；再写|To write or type something again, making changes to improve it or to correct mistakes.|例句：The contract needs to be redrafted to reflect the new agreement.  合同需要重新起草以反映新的协议。

例句：She redrafted her essay several times before submitting it.  她在提交论文之前修改了好几次。


近义词：rewrite, revise, amend

反义词：finalize, complete


词性:动词
