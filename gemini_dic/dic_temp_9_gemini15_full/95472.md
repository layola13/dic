incites:/ɪnˈsaɪts/ | vt. |煽动；激起；激励 | To encourage or stir up (violent or unlawful behavior).  It can also mean to provoke or stimulate (a feeling or reaction).|例句：The speaker's words incited the crowd to riot.  演讲者的言辞煽动了人群暴乱。

例句：His aggressive behavior incited a fight. 他的侵略性行为引发了一场争斗。


近义词：provoke, instigate, rouse, spur, stimulate | 反义词：calm, soothe, pacify, quell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He incites violence through his speeches. = 他通过演讲煽动暴力。
The advertisement incites consumers to buy the product. = 广告煽动消费者购买该产品。

一般过去时:
The rebels incited a revolution. = 反叛者煽动了一场革命。
Her words incited anger in the room. = 她的言辞激起了房间里的怒火。

现在完成时:
His actions have incited much controversy. = 他的行为已引起许多争议。
They have incited fear among the population. = 他们已在民众中煽动恐惧。

过去完成时:
He had incited unrest before the election. = 大选前他已经煽动了骚乱。
The rumors had incited panic in the town. = 谣言已经在镇上引发了恐慌。

将来时:
They will incite a rebellion if their demands are not met. = 如果他们的要求得不到满足，他们将煽动叛乱。
His speech will incite strong emotions. = 他的演讲将会激起强烈的感情。

虚拟语气:
If he incited a riot, he would be arrested. = 如果他煽动暴乱，他将被逮捕。
I wish he hadn't incited such hatred. = 我真希望他没有煽动如此大的仇恨。

被动语态:
The crowd was incited to violence by the protesters. = 抗议者煽动人群暴力。
The riot was incited by irresponsible reporting. = 暴乱是由不负责任的报道煽动的。

疑问句:
What incited the fight? = 什么引发了这场争斗？
Did his words incite any action? = 他的话煽动了什么行动吗？

否定句:
He did not incite violence; he merely expressed his opinion. = 他没有煽动暴力；他只是表达了他的意见。
The police prevented him from inciting a riot. = 警方阻止了他煽动暴乱。


PHRASE->
incite to riot  煽动暴乱
incite violence 煽动暴力
incite hatred 煽动仇恨
incite rebellion 煽动叛乱
incite fear 煽动恐惧

词源:
incites 源自拉丁语动词incitare，意思是“to urge on, spur, incite”。  前缀in-表示“into”或“towards”，词根citare意思是“to call, summon, excite”。
