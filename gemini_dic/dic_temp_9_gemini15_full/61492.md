embroiderer:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdə(r)ər/| n. |刺绣者，绣工|A person who embroiders.  An embroiderer skillfully uses needle and thread to create intricate designs on fabric.|例句：My grandmother was a skilled embroiderer, her work was renowned in the village.  我的祖母是一位技艺精湛的刺绣者，她的作品在村里享有盛名。

例句：The museum showcased the work of several renowned embroiderers from around the world.  博物馆展出了来自世界各地几位著名刺绣家的作品。


近义词：needleworker, seamstress (if female)


反义词：None (antonyms are not applicable for this noun)


词性：Noun
