cultivate:/ˈkʌltɪveɪt/| vt. |耕作；培养；陶冶；发展|To prepare and use land for growing crops; to try to develop and improve something.|例句：Farmers cultivate the land to grow food. 农民耕作土地以种植粮食。 They are cultivating a new strain of rice. 他们正在培育一种新的水稻品种。|近义词：grow, develop, foster, nurture|反义词：neglect, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cultivates his garden every weekend. = 他每个周末都耕种他的花园。
She cultivates a positive attitude. = 她培养积极的态度。


一般过去时:
They cultivated that land for years. = 他们耕种那块土地多年了。
He cultivated a friendship with his colleague. = 他与同事培养了友谊。


现在完成时:
We have cultivated a strong relationship with our clients. = 我们与客户建立了牢固的关系。
She has cultivated her singing talent for many years. = 她多年来一直培养她的歌唱才能。


过去完成时:
They had cultivated the field before the rain came. = 雨来之前他们已经耕种了田地。
She had cultivated her public image carefully. = 她已经仔细地培养了自己的公众形象。


将来时:
We will cultivate more organic vegetables next year. = 明年我们将种植更多的有机蔬菜。
He will cultivate his skills through practice. = 他将通过练习来培养他的技能。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cultivate a hobby. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会培养一个爱好。
It is vital that we cultivate a culture of respect. = 至关重要的是，我们应该培养一种尊重的文化。


被动语态:
The land was cultivated by the farmers. = 这块土地是由农民耕种的。
His talent was cultivated by his teacher. = 他的天赋是由他的老师培养的。


疑问句:
Do you cultivate any plants in your garden? = 你在花园里种植任何植物吗？
How do you cultivate patience? = 你如何培养耐心？


否定句:
They did not cultivate the land properly. = 他们没有正确地耕种土地。
She does not cultivate friendships easily. = 她不容易与人建立友谊。
