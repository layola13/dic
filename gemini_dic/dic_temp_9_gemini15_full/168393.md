proadvertising:/ˌproʊˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ/| n. |正面宣传，积极广告宣传|The act of promoting a product, service, or idea in a positive and favorable light, often emphasizing its benefits and downplaying potential drawbacks.  It's a deliberate attempt to create a positive perception and encourage favorable attitudes towards the advertised subject.|Example: The company launched a proadvertising campaign focusing on the environmental benefits of its new product.  公司发起了一场正面宣传活动，重点关注其新产品的环保效益。|近义词：positive advertising, favorable publicity, brand advocacy|反义词：negative advertising, smear campaign, bad publicity|名词
