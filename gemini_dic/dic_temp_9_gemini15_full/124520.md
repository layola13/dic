mountainboard:/ˈmaʊntɪnˌbɔːrd/ | n. | 山地滑板 | A mountainboard is a type of all-terrain board similar to a snowboard or skateboard, but designed for off-road use on rough terrain like mountains, hills, and trails.  It typically features larger wheels than a skateboard and a more robust construction to withstand the impact of uneven surfaces. | Example: He took his mountainboard up the steep mountain trail. 他沿着陡峭的山路骑着山地滑板往上走。  |近义词：all-terrain board, off-road board | 反义词：skateboard (in terms of terrain suitability)
