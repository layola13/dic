re-parked:/ˌriːˈpɑːrkt/ | v. | 重新停车 | To park a vehicle again, often in a different location.  This implies a previous parking event. | 例句：After realizing he was blocking the driveway, he re-parked his car further down the street. = 意识到他挡住了车道后，他把车重新停在了更远处的街上。  He re-parked his bicycle to avoid the puddle. = 他把自行车重新停放以避开水坑。 | 近义词：relocate (a vehicle), reposition | 反义词：unpark


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-parks his car every day. = 他每天都重新停车。
They re-park their bikes after lunch. = 他们午餐后重新停放自行车。

一般过去时:
She re-parked the car because it was blocking the fire exit. = 她把车重新停放，因为它挡住了消防通道。
He re-parked his motorcycle to be closer to the entrance. = 他把摩托车重新停放，以靠近入口。

现在完成时:
I have re-parked the car three times today. = 我今天已经三次重新停车了。
They have re-parked the van to avoid the construction zone. = 为了避开施工区域，他们已经把货车重新停放了。

过去完成时:

She had re-parked her scooter several times before finding a suitable spot. = 在找到合适的地方之前，她已经好几次重新停放了她的踏板车。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
