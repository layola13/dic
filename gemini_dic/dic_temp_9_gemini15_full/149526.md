paganism:/ˈpeɪɡənɪzəm/| n. |异教；多神教|The belief and worship of a multitude of gods and goddesses, typically involving rituals and practices connected to nature and the cycles of the seasons.  It encompasses a wide range of beliefs and practices that existed before and alongside the rise of major religions like Christianity and Islam.  Many modern pagan traditions draw inspiration from ancient practices, but are distinct from them. |例句：The ancient Romans practiced a form of paganism that involved the worship of many gods and goddesses.  古代罗马人信奉一种涉及崇拜许多神灵的异教。

例句：Many modern pagan traditions incorporate elements of nature worship and earth-based spirituality. 许多现代异教传统融合了自然崇拜和大地精神的元素。


近义词：polytheism, idolatry, heathenism|反义词：monotheism
