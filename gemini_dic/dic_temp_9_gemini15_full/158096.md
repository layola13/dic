plasticity: /plæˈstɪsəti/ | n. |可塑性，柔韧性，展性|The ability of a material to be molded or shaped.  It also refers to the ability of the brain or nervous system to adapt and change throughout life.|

例句：

The plasticity of clay allows sculptors to create intricate forms.  粘土的可塑性使雕塑家能够创作出复杂的造型。

The brain's remarkable plasticity allows it to recover from injury. 大脑非凡的可塑性使其能够从损伤中恢复。


近义词：malleability, flexibility, adaptability, ductility

反义词：rigidity, inflexibility, immutability
