hallmarked:/ˈhɔːlmɑːkt/| v. adj. | 打上印记；具有…的特征 | To mark (an article) with an official mark guaranteeing its quality or genuineness; to be characteristic of. |例句：The silver was hallmarked by the Goldsmiths' Company. 这银器是金匠公司打上印记的。  The film is hallmarked by its stunning visuals. 这部电影以其令人惊叹的视觉效果为特色。|近义词：stamped, characterized, distinguished |反义词：unmarked, undistinguished


USAGE->

This design hallmarks the company's products. = 这种设计是该公司产品的标志。
