have:/hæv/ /həv/ | vt. & aux. v. | 有；拥有；吃；喝；进行；经历 |  To possess, own, or hold something; to experience or undergo something; to take or receive something (food or drink); to cause or allow something to happen. |

例句：I have a new car. = 我有一辆新车。

例句：We had a great time at the party. = 我们在派对上玩得很开心。


近义词：possess, own, obtain, experience, undergo

反义词：lack, lose, forfeit


词性: 动词 (及物动词和助动词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have a dog. = 我有一只狗。
She has a beautiful dress. = 她有一件漂亮的裙子。

一般过去时:
I had a headache yesterday. = 我昨天头痛。
They had a meeting this morning. = 他们今天早上开会了。

现在完成时:
I have finished my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
She has lived in London for ten years. = 她已经在伦敦住了十年了。

过去完成时:
I had already eaten before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经吃过了。
They had never seen such a beautiful sunset. = 他们从未见过如此美丽的日落。

将来时:
I will have finished the project by Friday. = 我将在星期五之前完成这个项目。
She will have left by the time you arrive. = 你到达的时候她已经离开了。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would travel the world. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I had a bigger house. = 我希望我有一座更大的房子。

被动语态:
The work was done by John. = 这项工作是由约翰完成的。
The cake was eaten by the children. = 蛋糕被孩子们吃掉了。


疑问句:
Do you have any questions? = 你有什么问题吗？
Does she have a car? = 她有车吗？


否定句:
I do not have a car. = 我没有车。
She does not have time to help. = 她没有时间帮忙。

PHRASE->
have to = 必须
have got = 有 (非正式)
have a look = 看一看
have a try = 尝试一下
have fun = 玩得开心


词源:
The word "have" originates from the Old English verb *habban, which is related to other Germanic words meaning "to hold" or "to possess."  Its ultimate origins trace back to Proto-Indo-European roots associated with grasping or seizing.
