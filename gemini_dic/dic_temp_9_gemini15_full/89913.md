/ɔːrs/| n.|马|A large, strong, plant-eating animal with four legs and a long tail, used for riding, carrying loads, or pulling vehicles.|例句：The horse galloped across the field. 那匹马飞奔过田野。|近义词： steed, pony, gelding|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse runs in the field. = 马在田野里奔跑。
The horses pull the cart. = 马拉车。

一般过去时:
The horse ran away. = 马跑掉了。
The farmer rode the horse. = 农民骑着马。

现在完成时:
I have ridden that horse before. = 我以前骑过那匹马。
They have owned that horse for many years. = 他们拥有那匹马很多年了。

过去完成时:
The horse had already left before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，马已经离开了。
He had never ridden a horse before that day. = 在那天之前，他从未骑过马。

将来时:
I will ride a horse tomorrow. = 我明天将骑马。
She will train her horse for the competition. = 她将为比赛训练她的马。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would ride it every day. = 如果我有一匹马，我每天都会骑它。
I wish I could ride a horse. = 我希望我能骑马。

被动语态:
The horse was ridden by the child. = 这匹马被孩子骑着。
The horses are trained for racing. = 马匹被训练用于赛马。

疑问句:
Is that your horse? = 那匹马是你的吗？
How many horses do they have? = 他们有多少匹马？

否定句:
I don't own a horse. = 我不拥有马。
They didn't see any horses. = 他们没有看到任何马。
