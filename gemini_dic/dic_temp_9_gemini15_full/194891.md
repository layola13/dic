semi-evening: /ˈsɛmiˈiːvnɪŋ/ | adj. | 半晚上的，傍晚的 |  Describes a time period that is between late afternoon and the beginning of the evening, often associated with a transition period. It implies a time that's neither fully daytime nor fully nighttime. |例句：We had a semi-evening stroll along the beach. 我们在海滩上散了一段傍晚的步。
We enjoyed a semi-evening cocktail at the bar.  我们在酒吧享用了一杯傍晚的鸡尾酒。| 近义词：late afternoon, early evening, dusk | 反义词：midday, noon, midnight


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms.  Therefore, verb tenses, passive voice, etc., are not applicable.  Its usage is limited to descriptive sentences.
