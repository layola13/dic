rippingly:/ˈrɪpɪŋli/| adv.|极好地；极快地；极度地|Extremely well; extremely fast; intensely.  The adverb "rippingly" intensifies the adjective "ripping," which itself means excellent or exciting.  It suggests a degree of excitement or excellence that surpasses the ordinary. |例句：The band played rippingly, electrifying the crowd. 乐队演奏得极好，点燃了全场。

例句：The car accelerated rippingly down the track.  这辆车在赛道上飞速行驶。


近义词：amazingly, wonderfully, incredibly, spectacularly, breathtakingly


反义词：terribly, awfully, dreadfully


词性：副词
