latus:/ˈleɪtəs/| n.|侧边，阔度；焦点弦|Latin for "side" or "broad," referring to a line segment. In geometry, it specifically denotes the latus rectum of a conic section (parabola, ellipse, hyperbola), which is a chord through a focus parallel to the directrix.  It's also used in botany to describe the breadth of a leaf or other plant part.|例句：The latus rectum of the parabola is four times the focal length.  抛物线的焦点弦是焦距的四倍。|近义词：width, breadth, chord|反义词：narrowness, thinness
