precompressed:/ˌpriːkəmˈprɛst/| adj.|预压缩的；预先压缩的|Already compressed; compressed beforehand.  This term often refers to data or files that have been compressed before being stored or transmitted.|例句：The precompressed data file reduced the download time significantly. 预压缩的数据文件大大减少了下载时间。

例句：The precompressed air tanks are ready for use. 预压缩的气罐已准备好使用。


近义词：compressed, compacted|反义词：uncompressed, expanded|词性：形容词
