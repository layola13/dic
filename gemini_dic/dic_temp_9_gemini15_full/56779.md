dopiness:/ˈdoʊpinɪs/ | n. |愚笨；迟钝；昏昏欲睡的状态|A state of being dull, sluggish, or sleepy; a lack of alertness or intelligence.  It often implies a lack of energy and mental sharpness.|例句：The afternoon sun induced a state of dopiness.  下午的阳光让人昏昏欲睡。

例句：His speech was marked by a certain dopiness, lacking clarity and focus.他的演讲显得有些迟钝，缺乏清晰度和重点。

近义词：stupor, lethargy, sluggishness, dullness, drowsiness

反义词：alertness, sharpness, vivacity, energy

词性:名词
