refuge:/ˈrefjuːdʒ/| n. |避难所；庇护；避难 |a place of shelter or protection from danger or hardship|The refugees found refuge in a nearby village. 难民在一个附近的村庄找到了避难所。|shelter, sanctuary, asylum|danger, exposure, persecution|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mountain offers refuge from the storm.  山为暴风雨提供了避难所。
This small town serves as a refuge for artists. 这个小镇为艺术家们提供了一个避难所。


一般过去时:
They sought refuge in the church during the bombing. 轰炸期间，他们在教堂寻求庇护。
The family found refuge in a deserted cabin.  这家人在一个废弃的小屋里找到了避难所。


现在完成时:
Many have sought refuge from the war-torn country.  许多人已经逃离饱受战争蹂躏的国家寻求避难。
She has found refuge in her faith during difficult times.  在困难时期，她在信仰中找到了慰藉。


过去完成时:
Before the storm hit, they had already sought refuge in a cave. 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经躲进山洞寻求庇护了。
The animals had found refuge in the forest before the hunters arrived.  猎人到来之前，动物们已经躲进了森林里。


将来时:
They will seek refuge in the mountains if the fighting continues. 如果战斗继续，他们将逃往山区寻求庇护。
If the floodwaters rise, we will need to find refuge on higher ground.  如果洪水上涨，我们需要在高地寻找避难所。


虚拟语气:
If I had known about the storm, I would have sought refuge earlier. 如果我知道暴风雨来临，我就会早点寻求庇护。
I wish I could find refuge from this constant stress. 我希望我能从这种持续的压力中解脱出来。


被动语态:
Refuge was offered to the displaced people by the Red Cross. 红十字会向流离失所的人们提供了庇护。
The injured soldiers were given refuge in the field hospital. 受伤的士兵在野战医院得到了庇护。


疑问句:
Where can we find refuge from the approaching tornado? 我们在哪里可以找到避难所来躲避即将到来的龙卷风？
Is there any refuge available for the homeless?  是否有任何机构可以为无家可归者提供庇护？


否定句:
They found no refuge from the relentless sun.  他们在无情的阳光下找不到任何庇护。
There was no refuge from the noise and chaos of the city.  在城市喧嚣和混乱中，没有可以躲避的地方。
