petaled:/ˈpetəld/| adj.|有花瓣的；长有花瓣的|Having petals.  The word describes a flower or flower part possessing petals.  It emphasizes the presence and often the number or arrangement of petals.|例句：The rose is a petaled flower.  玫瑰花是有花瓣的花。

The tulip's brightly petaled bloom attracted the bees.  郁金香鲜艳的花瓣吸引了蜜蜂。


近义词：petalous (more formal)


反义词：apetalous (lacking petals)


词性：形容词
