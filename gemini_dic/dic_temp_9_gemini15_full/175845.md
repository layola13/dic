rasterise:/ˈræstəraɪz/| vt.|光栅化，栅格化|To convert an image or graphic from a vector format to a raster format, consisting of pixels.  This process is essential for displaying images on screens or printing them, as screens and printers work with pixels.|例句：The software rasterises the vector image before displaying it on the screen.  软件在屏幕上显示矢量图像之前将其光栅化。|近义词：rasterize, pixelate|反义词：vectorize
