chromium:/ˈkroʊmiəm/ | n. |铬 |Chromium is a hard, brittle, greyish-white metal.  It is a transition metal and is found in the Earth's crust.  It is used in many alloys, especially stainless steel, to improve their hardness and corrosion resistance. |Example: Stainless steel contains chromium, making it resistant to rust.  不锈钢含有铬，使其耐锈蚀。|近义词: chrome (less formal)|反义词: N/A
