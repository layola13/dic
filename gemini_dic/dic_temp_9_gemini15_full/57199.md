down-the-road:/ˌdaʊn ðə ˈroʊd/| adj. | 将来的；未来的 |  In the future; at some time in the future.  This phrase describes something that will happen or be relevant at a later, unspecified time.  |例句：We're planning a big expansion down the road.  我们计划将来大规模扩张。

例句：Down the road, we might consider hiring more staff.  将来，我们可能会考虑雇佣更多员工。


近义词：future, eventual, prospective, forthcoming | 反义词：present, immediate, current | 词性：形容词
