friskers:/ˈfrɪskərz/ | n. | 搜查者；嬉戏者 | A person who frisks or searches someone.  Also refers to someone who playfully jumps or runs about. |例句：The friskers searched everyone entering the building. 搜查者搜查了进入大楼的每一个人。The children were like friskers, running and laughing in the park. 孩子们像是在嬉戏，在公园里跑来跑去，咯咯地笑着。|近义词：searcher, player, jumper |反义词：inspector (in a formal context)
