pieta:/ˈpiːɛtɑː/|/ˈpiːeɪtɑː/| n. |圣母怜子像|A depiction of the Virgin Mary holding the dead body of Jesus Christ.  It often emphasizes Mary's sorrow and grief. The subject matter has been depicted in art throughout history, most famously in Michelangelo's sculpture.|Example:  Michelangelo's Pieta is one of the most famous works of Renaissance art.  米开朗基罗的圣母怜子像文艺复兴时期最著名的作品之一。

The Pieta in St. Peter's Basilica is a powerful and moving image.  圣彼得大教堂中的圣母怜子像是一幅有力而动人的图像。|近义词：depiction of mourning, Madonna and Child (in the context of death)|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to joy or celebration instead of sorrow.
