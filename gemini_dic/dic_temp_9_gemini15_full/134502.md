nonleguminous: /ˌnɑːnˈlɛɡjumɪnəs/ | adj. | 非豆科的 | Not belonging to the Leguminosae (or Fabaceae) family of plants.  This adjective describes plants that do not possess the characteristics of legumes, such as having pods containing seeds. |  Example:  Many nonleguminous plants rely on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.  许多非豆科植物依靠菌根真菌来吸收养分。|近义词：non-legume,  n.a.(not applicable in some contexts)|反义词：leguminous
