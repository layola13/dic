nontraveller: /ˈnɒnˈtrævələ(r)/ | n. |非旅行者，不旅行的人 | A person who does not travel, especially for leisure.  This term emphasizes the lack of travel as a significant aspect of the person's lifestyle. |Example:  He's a nontraveller; he prefers to stay home and read. 他是个不爱旅行的人；他更喜欢待在家里看书。 |近义词：stay-at-home, homebody |反义词：traveler, globetrotter | 名词
