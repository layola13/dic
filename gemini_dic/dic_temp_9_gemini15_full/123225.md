monocotyl:/ˈmɒnəʊkɒtɪl/| n. |单子叶植物|A monocotyl is a flowering plant that has one cotyledon (embryonic leaf) in its seed.  Monocots are a large and diverse group of plants that include grasses, lilies, orchids, and palms. They are characterized by several features, including parallel leaf venation, fibrous root systems, and flower parts in multiples of three. |Example:  Grasses, such as wheat and rice, are examples of monocots.  小麦和水稻是单子叶植物的例子。|近义词:monocotyledon |反义词:dicot, dicotyledon
