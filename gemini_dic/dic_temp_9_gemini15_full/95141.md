inapprehensive:/ˌɪnæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 不焦虑的；无忧虑的；满不在乎的 | Not feeling or showing anxiety or apprehension.  The word suggests a calm and relaxed state, often to the point of being unconcerned about potential problems or dangers. |  He was surprisingly inapprehensive about the upcoming surgery. 他对即将到来的手术出奇地平静。She faced the challenge with an inapprehensive demeanor. 她以一种满不在乎的态度面对挑战。|近义词：calm, composed, unconcerned, nonchalant, placid |反义词：anxious, apprehensive, worried, concerned, nervous | 词性：形容词
