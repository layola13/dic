reconsolidated: /ˌriːkənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 重新巩固的；重新合并的 |  Having been consolidated again; strengthened or unified anew.  | The reconsolidated company is now stronger and more profitable. 重新合并后的公司现在更强大，盈利能力也更强。 | consolidated, strengthened, unified | fragmented, weakened, divided | adjective
