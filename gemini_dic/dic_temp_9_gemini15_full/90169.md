hotlink:/ˈhɑːtˌlɪŋk/| n. v.|热链接，盗链| A hotlink is a hyperlink that directly links to an image or other file on another website, without permission.  This can overload the server of the original website and is generally considered unethical.  As a verb, it means to create such a link.|例句：He hotlinked the image from my website without my consent. 他未经我同意就从我的网站盗链了图片。|近义词：direct link, unauthorized link|反义词：direct download, local copy
