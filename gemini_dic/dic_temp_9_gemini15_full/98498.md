instant:/ˈɪnstənt/ | adj., n. | 立即的；瞬间；瞬间；片刻 |  A moment in time; something that happens very quickly.  It can also refer to a product that is prepared quickly. |例句：The instant I saw her, I knew. = 我一看到她就知道了。  He needs instant gratification. = 他需要即时满足。|近义词：immediate, prompt, quick, moment |反义词：delayed, slow, gradual


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instant coffee tastes good. = 速溶咖啡味道不错。
This instant messaging app is very popular. = 这个即时通讯应用非常流行。


一般过去时:
The cake was ready in an instant. = 蛋糕瞬间就做好了。
I felt an instant connection with him. = 我立刻和他产生了联系。


现在完成时:
I have had many instant noodles for lunch. = 我午饭吃了很多方便面。
She has finished the work in an instant. = 她瞬间完成了工作。


过去完成时:
I had already made an instant decision. = 我已经做出了一个迅速的决定。
By the instant he arrived, the party had ended. = 他到达时，聚会已经结束了。


将来时:
I will send you an instant message when I arrive. = 我到达后会立即给你发信息。
The problem will be solved in an instant. = 问题将立即得到解决。


虚拟语气:
If I had an instant solution, I would tell you. = 如果我有一个立即的解决方案，我会告诉你。
I wish I had an instant way to solve this problem. = 我希望我有一个立即解决这个问题的方法。


被动语态:
The problem was solved instantly. = 问题得到了立即解决。
Instant coffee is often made with hot water. = 速溶咖啡通常用热水冲泡。


疑问句:
Is there an instant solution to this problem? = 这个问题有立即的解决方案吗？
Do you prefer instant coffee or freshly brewed coffee? = 你喜欢速溶咖啡还是现磨咖啡？


否定句:

The answer was not instant. = 答案不是立即的。
