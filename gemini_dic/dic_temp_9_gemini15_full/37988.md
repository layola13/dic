complicating:/ˈkɑːmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/|/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |使复杂化的；复杂的|Making something more complicated or difficult.  This word is the present participle of the verb "complicate".|例句：The new regulations are complicating the process significantly. 新规章使流程复杂化了很多。

例句：His constant interruptions were complicating the meeting. 他不断的打断使会议变得复杂。


近义词：intricating, perplexing, confusing, embroiling


反义词：simplifying, clarifying, easing


词性：形容词
