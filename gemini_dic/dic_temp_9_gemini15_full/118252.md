merchandised:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzd/ | v. | 商品化；进行商品销售 | To treat (something) as a commodity to be bought and sold; to promote and sell (goods) | The company merchandised its new product aggressively.  这家公司积极地推销其新产品。|The museum merchandised its collection of artifacts. 这个博物馆将它的文物藏品商品化了。 | market, commercialize, retail |  de-merchandised (反义词，但并非完全反义，而是指停止商品化)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They merchandise their products effectively. = 他们有效地推销他们的产品。
The store merchandises a wide variety of goods. = 这家商店销售各种各样的商品。


一般过去时:
The company merchandised its new line of clothing successfully. = 公司成功地推销了其新的服装系列。
They merchandised their products at a trade show. = 他们在贸易展销会上推销他们的产品。


现在完成时:
They have merchandised their brand successfully in the global market. = 他们在全球市场上成功地推销了他们的品牌。
The company has merchandised its products through various channels. = 公司通过各种渠道推销其产品。


过去完成时:
By the time the event started, they had already merchandised all their stock. = 在活动开始之前，他们已经卖光了所有的库存。
Before the launch, they had carefully merchandised the product. = 在发布之前，他们已经仔细地策划了产品的销售。


将来时:
They will merchandise their new product at the upcoming expo. = 他们将在即将到来的博览会上推销他们的新产品。
The company will merchandise its products online and offline. = 公司将在线上和线下推销其产品。


虚拟语气:
If they had merchandised their product differently, they might have sold more. = 如果他们采用不同的方式推销产品，可能会卖出更多。
I wish they had merchandised their products more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地推销他们的产品。


被动语态:
The new product was heavily merchandised during the holiday season. = 新产品在节日期间进行了大力度的推销。
Their products are merchandised through various retail outlets. = 他们的产品通过各种零售店进行销售。


疑问句:

否定句:
