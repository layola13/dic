lyic:/ˈlɪrɪk/| n.|抒情诗；歌词|A lyric is a poem that expresses personal emotions or feelings, often in a song-like manner.  It's characterized by its musicality and subjective viewpoint.|Example:  The song's lyrics were deeply moving. 这首歌的歌词非常感人。|近义词：verse, song, poem|反义词：epic (in terms of scope and style)|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lyrics express deep sadness. = 歌词表达了深深的悲伤。
These lyrics are very catchy. = 这些歌词非常朗朗上口。


一般过去时:
The lyrics described a lost love. = 歌词描述了一段失去的爱。
