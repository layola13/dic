pythagoreanism:/ˌpaɪθæɡəˈriːənɪzəm/| n. |毕达哥拉斯主义，毕达哥拉斯学派|The philosophical and religious system of Pythagoras and his followers, emphasizing mathematics, music, and mysticism.  It involved a strict code of conduct and a belief in the transmigration of souls.  |Example: Pythagoreanism profoundly influenced the development of Greek mathematics and philosophy. 毕达哥拉斯主义深刻地影响了希腊数学和哲学的发展。|近义词：Pythagoreanism (no true synonyms, but related concepts include Neoplatonism)|反义词：empiricism (as an opposing philosophical approach)|名词
