skived:/skɪvd/| v.| 逃避；偷懒；旷课 | To avoid work or a duty, especially by pretending to be ill.  This often implies a deliberate avoidance rather than genuine inability. |例句：He skived off work to go to the football match. 他逃工去看足球比赛了。
He skived school to play video games. 他逃学去玩电子游戏了。


近义词：evade, dodge, shirk, bunk off, play truant|反义词：work diligently, attend faithfully
