improviso:/ɪmˈprɒvɪzoʊ/| n. v.|即兴表演；即兴创作|The act of composing, performing, or speaking spontaneously without previous preparation.  It can refer to a musical piece, a speech, a dramatic performance, or any other creative work done without planning.|例句：The pianist gave a stunning *improviso* at the concert.  这位钢琴家在音乐会上进行了一场令人惊叹的即兴演奏。

例句：She delivered a brilliant *improviso* on the topic of climate change. 她就气候变化问题发表了一篇精彩的即兴演讲。


近义词：extemporization, improvisation, impromptu


反义词：rehearsal, preparation, premeditation


词性: Noun & Verb
