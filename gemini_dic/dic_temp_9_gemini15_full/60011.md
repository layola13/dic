editress:/ˈɛdɪtrəs/| n.|女编辑，女主编|A woman who edits written material for publication.  This term is now largely considered archaic or dated, with "editor" being the preferred gender-neutral term.|  She was a highly respected editress for a leading fashion magazine. 她是一位备受尊敬的时尚杂志女编辑。| editor, female editor |  (no direct antonym)| 名词

The editress edits the manuscript carefully. = 女编辑仔细地编辑手稿。
She reviews articles submitted by the writers. = 她审查作家提交的文章。
