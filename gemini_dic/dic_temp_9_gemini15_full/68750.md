farmstead:/ˈfɑːrmˌsted/ | n. |农场住宅；农庄 | A group of farm buildings with the farmhouse and other structures clustered together.  A farmstead is the central area of a farm, typically including the main house, barns, stables, and other outbuildings. | Example: The old farmstead was surrounded by fields of golden wheat.  古老的农庄被金黄色的麦田环绕着。 The family has lived on this farmstead for generations. 这个家族世世代代都居住在这个农庄上。 | 近义词：farm, homestead, ranch | 反义词：city, metropolis
