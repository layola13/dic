dilly:/ˈdɪli/| n. |令人愉快的事物；好东西；拖延| A dilly is something delightful or enjoyable; something excellent or wonderful.  It can also refer to the act of delaying or procrastinating. |例句：That new car is a real dilly!  那辆新车真是太棒了！

例句：He kept dilly-dallying, so he missed the deadline. 他一直拖延，所以错过了截止日期。|近义词：delight, treat, marvel, procrastination|反义词：disappointment, unpleasantness, promptness
