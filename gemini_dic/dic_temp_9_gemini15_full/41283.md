cordials:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl/| n. |甜酒；兴奋剂；兴奋剂； cordial drinks|  A cordial is a sweet, typically alcoholic, liqueur. They are often fruit-flavored and served as an after-dinner drink or as a mixer in cocktails.  |例句：She offered her guests a selection of cordials after dinner. 晚饭后，她给客人提供了各种甜酒。|例句：The bartender mixed a delicious cocktail using a variety of cordials. 酒保用各种甜酒调制了一款美味的鸡尾酒。|近义词：liqueur, aperitif|反义词：None specifically.

词源:
