drugster: /ˈdrʌɡstər/ | n. |瘾君子，吸毒者 |A person who uses illegal drugs, especially habitually.  This term often carries a negative connotation, implying a significant level of addiction and potentially problematic behavior. | Example: The drugster was arrested for possession of heroin.  这个瘾君子因持有海洛因而被捕。 |近义词：junkie, addict, user |反义词：N/A
