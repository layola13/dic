/piːsiːpiː/ | n. | 氯胺酮；PCP | Phencyclidine, commonly known as PCP or angel dust, is a dissociative anesthetic drug. It is a powerful hallucinogen that can cause a range of effects, from euphoria and hallucinations to paranoia, aggression, and violence.  Its use is illegal in most countries. | Example: The police found evidence of PCP use at the crime scene.  警方在犯罪现场发现了氯胺酮使用的证据。|近义词：angel dust, sernyl | 反义词：无  | 名词
