homothallism:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈθælɪzəm/| n. | 单性生殖；同宗配合 | The ability of a single organism to reproduce sexually without the need for a partner, typically through self-fertilization.  This is common in many fungi and some plants. | Example:  Many fungal species exhibit homothallism, allowing them to reproduce even in the absence of other individuals.  许多真菌物种表现出单性生殖，即使在没有其他个体的情况下也能繁殖。|近义词: self-fertilization, autogamy |反义词: heterothallism
