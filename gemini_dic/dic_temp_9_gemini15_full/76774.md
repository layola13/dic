gamester:/ˈɡeɪmstər/| n. |赌徒，游戏玩家|A person who habitually gambles or plays games, especially for money.  A gamester is someone who is heavily involved in gambling, often to the point of addiction or recklessness. They may risk significant amounts of money in pursuit of winning. |He was a notorious gamester, losing his entire fortune at the card table. 他是个臭名昭著的赌徒，在牌桌上输掉了全部家当。|gambler, player|hard worker, investor|名词
