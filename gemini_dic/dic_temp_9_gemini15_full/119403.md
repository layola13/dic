microfibre:/ˈmaɪkroʊfaɪbər/| n. |超细纤维 |A synthetic fiber made of extremely fine filaments, often used in cleaning cloths and clothing because of its absorbency and softness.  It's typically made from polyester or a blend of polyester and nylon.|例句：The microfiber cloth easily wiped away the dust.  超细纤维布轻松擦掉了灰尘。  I love the softness of my microfiber towels. 我喜欢我的超细纤维毛巾的柔软度。|近义词：microfiber (US spelling), ultrafine fiber |反义词:coarse fiber
