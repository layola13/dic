maclurea: /mækˈluːriə/ | n. | 柘树属植物 | Maclurea is a genus of plants in the family Moraceae,  containing only one species, Maclurea tinctoria, commonly known as fustic or old fustic.  It is a medium-sized tree native to the tropical regions of the Americas,  known for its yellowish wood used as a dye.  | The vibrant yellow dye extracted from maclurea tinctoria was historically important for dyeing fabrics. | 近义词: fustic, old fustic | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其他染料对比) | 名词
