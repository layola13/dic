side-effects:/ˈsaɪdɪˈfekts/ | n. |副作用，副反应 | Unwanted or unpleasant effects of a drug or medical treatment.  These effects can range from mild to severe.  |例句：Many medications have side effects, such as nausea or drowsiness. 许多药物都有副作用，例如恶心或嗜睡。

Many patients experienced unpleasant side effects from the new drug.许多病人服用这种新药后出现了令人不快的副作用。|近义词：adverse effects, adverse reactions, unwanted effects |反义词：intended effects, desired effects |名词
