cliquey:/ˈkliːki/ | adj. | 排外的；小圈子内的；拉帮结派的 |  Describes a group of people who are exclusive and tend to socialize only with each other, often excluding others.  It suggests a sense of secrecy and potentially unkindness towards outsiders. | The office felt cliquey; several employees seemed to always eat lunch together, never inviting anyone else.  办公室气氛很排外；一些员工总是聚在一起吃午饭，从不邀请其他人。|  exclusive, insular, in-group, close-knit | inclusive, open, welcoming | 形容词
