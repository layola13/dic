hydrocarbons:/ˈhaɪdrəʊkɑːrbənz/ | n. | 碳氢化合物 | Compounds consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon.  They are the principal constituents of petroleum and natural gas. Hydrocarbons are classified by the arrangement of the carbon atoms into families such as alkanes, alkenes, alkynes, and aromatic hydrocarbons. | Example:  Fossil fuels, such as coal, oil, and natural gas, are primarily composed of hydrocarbons. 矿物燃料，例如煤炭、石油和天然气，主要由碳氢化合物组成。 |近义词:organic compounds|反义词:inorganic compounds|名词
