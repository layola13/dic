doormen:/ˈdɔːrmən/| n.|门卫，看门人|A doorman is a person employed to guard or attend the entrance of a building, such as a hotel or apartment building. They often assist guests with luggage and provide other services.|Example: The doorman greeted us warmly and helped us with our bags. 门卫热情地迎接我们，并帮我们提行李。|近义词：concierge, attendant, guard|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "resident" or "guest".
