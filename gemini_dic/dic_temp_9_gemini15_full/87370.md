heroner:/ˈhɛrənər/| n.|苍鹭|A heron is a large wading bird with long legs and a long neck.  Herons are known for their patience and skill in hunting fish and other aquatic animals.|Example: The heron stood motionless in the shallow water, patiently waiting for its prey.  一只苍鹭一动不动地站在浅水中，耐心地等待着猎物。|近义词：crane, egret|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑体型小的鸟类，如sparrow)|名词
