sclerodermic:/ˌsklɛrəˈdɜːrmɪk/| adj.|硬皮病的；硬皮病样的|Relating to or characteristic of scleroderma, a chronic disease causing hardening and thickening of the skin.  |例句：The patient presented with sclerodermic changes in her fingers.  这位病人手指出现了硬皮病样的变化。

例句：The doctor suspected a sclerodermic condition based on the patient's symptoms.  医生根据病人的症状怀疑是硬皮病。


近义词：sclerotic (in the context of skin hardening)


反义词：None directly opposite, but terms describing soft, supple skin could be considered indirect antonyms.


词性:形容词
