kitchenware:/ˈkɪtʃənˌwɛr/ | n. |厨具，厨房用具 | Kitchenware refers to the tools, utensils, and equipment used in a kitchen for cooking, preparing, and serving food.  This includes items like pots, pans, knives, cutting boards, dishes, and other related items. |  She bought a new set of kitchenware for her new apartment. 她为她的新公寓买了一套新的厨具。  The kitchenware store had a wide selection of items. 这家厨具店有很多商品。| cookware, utensils,  cookery |  (无明确反义词，可考虑用"non-kitchen items"之类的表达) | 名词
