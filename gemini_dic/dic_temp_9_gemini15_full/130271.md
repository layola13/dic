non-subject:/ˈnɒn ˈsʌbdʒɛkt/| adj.|非主语的；非主观的；非主题的|Not relating to the subject or topic; not involving a grammatical subject.  It often describes something that is not the main focus or topic of discussion or consideration.|例句：The article contained a lot of non-subject material. 这篇文章包含许多与主题无关的内容。|近义词：irrelevant, extraneous, unrelated |反义词：central, primary, subject
