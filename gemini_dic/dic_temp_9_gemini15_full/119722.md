midstream:/ˈmɪdstriːm/| adj. n.|中途的；中流的；|英文解析：in or at the middle of a stream, river, or process.  In the middle of something; halfway through.|例句：The project was abandoned midstream. = 项目中途被放弃了。

The canoe capsized midstream.  = 独木舟在河中间倾覆了。|近义词：midway, halfway, in progress|反义词：at the beginning, at the end
