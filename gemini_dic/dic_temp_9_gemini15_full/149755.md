palate:/ˈpælət/ | n. |味觉；上颚；调色板 |The palate is the roof of the mouth, and it plays a crucial role in tasting and appreciating flavors. It also refers to a person's sense of taste and the range of flavors they can discern.  Additionally, it can refer to a range of colors used by an artist. |例句：He has a refined palate and can distinguish subtle nuances in wine. 他拥有精致的味觉，能够辨别葡萄酒的细微差别。

例句：The artist used a vibrant palate of colors in her painting.艺术家在她的绘画中使用了鲜艳的色彩。


近义词：roof of the mouth (for anatomical sense), taste (for sense of taste), palette (for artist's range of colors) |反义词：None directly applicable.


词源：From Old French *palat, from Latin palatum, meaning "roof of the mouth".  The sense of "taste" developed from the anatomical sense, as the palate is where taste buds are located. The sense of "range of colors" is a later metaphorical extension.
