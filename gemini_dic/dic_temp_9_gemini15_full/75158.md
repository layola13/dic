frittering:/ˈfrɪtərɪŋ/| v.|挥霍，浪费（时间、金钱等）|To waste time, money, or opportunities in a careless or foolish way.|例句：He frittered away his inheritance on gambling. 他把继承来的财产都挥霍在赌博上了。
例句：Don't fritter away your time on unimportant things. 不要把时间浪费在不重要的事情上。|近义词：squandering, wasting, dissipating|反义词：saving, conserving, accumulating|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fritters away his money on useless things. = 他把钱浪费在无用的东西上。
She fritters her time on social media. = 她把时间浪费在社交媒体上。


一般过去时:
They frittered away the entire afternoon playing video games. = 他们整个下午都浪费在玩电子游戏上。
He frittered his savings on a frivolous purchase. = 他把积蓄花在一个轻率的购买上。


现在完成时:
I have frittered away most of my vacation. = 我已经浪费了大部分假期。
She has frittered away her chance for promotion. = 她已经浪费了她升职的机会。


过去完成时:
He realized he had frittered away his youth on meaningless pursuits. = 他意识到他把青春浪费在了毫无意义的追求上。
They regretted that they had frittered away their resources. = 他们后悔浪费了他们的资源。


将来时:

She fears she will fritter away her talent. = 她担心她会浪费掉自己的才能。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't frittered away his money, he would be rich now. = 如果他没有挥霍他的钱，他现在就会很富有。
I wish I hadn't frittered away so much time. = 我真希望我没有浪费这么多时间。


被动语态:
His inheritance was frittered away on gambling. = 他的遗产被挥霍在赌博上了。
The opportunity was frittered away due to negligence. = 由于疏忽，机会被浪费了。

否定句:
He didn't fritter away his time; he used it wisely. = 他没有浪费时间；他明智地利用了它。
She doesn't fritter away her earnings; she saves them carefully. = 她不挥霍她的收入；她仔细地储蓄着。
