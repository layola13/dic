plaster:/ˈplæstər/| n., v.|灰泥；膏药；石膏；涂抹；敷贴|

英文解析：A plaster is a substance used to cover and protect a wound or injury. It can also refer to a material used for building or repairing surfaces. As a verb, it means to cover something with plaster.

例句：
The plaster on his arm was starting to peel off.  = 他胳膊上的膏药开始脱落了。

The walls were covered with a thick layer of plaster. = 墙壁上覆盖着一层厚厚的灰泥。


近义词：bandage, stucco, cement, apply, spread

反义词：remove, uncover

词性：名词，动词
