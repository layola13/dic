marksmanship:/ˈmɑːrksmənʃɪp/ | n. |  射击技术；准确性 | The skill of shooting accurately, especially with a gun or bow and arrow.  It encompasses accuracy, precision, and control. |  His marksmanship was legendary; he never missed a target.  他的射击技术非常出名；他从未失手过。  The competition judged marksmanship and speed.  比赛评判的是射击技术和速度。 | accuracy, precision, skill | inaccuracy, clumsiness | 名词
