intranets:/ˈɪntrənɛts/| n. |内联网，企业内部网|A private network accessible only to authorized users within an organization.  It's used for internal communication, file sharing, and application access.|例句：Our company uses an intranet to share documents and communicate internally. = 我们公司使用内联网来共享文档和进行内部沟通。|例句：The intranet provides employees with access to company news and policies. = 内联网为员工提供访问公司新闻和政策的途径。|近义词：internal network, corporate network|反义词：internet, extranet|名词
