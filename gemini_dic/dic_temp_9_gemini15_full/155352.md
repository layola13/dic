pharmacology:/ˌfɑːrməˈkɒlədʒi/ | n. | 药理学 | The study of drugs and their effects on the body.  It encompasses the origin, properties, and effects of drugs, as well as their therapeutic uses and interactions. | Pharmacology is a complex field that requires a strong understanding of chemistry, biology, and medicine.  |近义词：pharmaceutics |反义词：无

词源:
pharmacology comes from Greek roots:  "pharmakon" (φάρμακον), meaning "drug" or "medicine," and "-logia" (-λογία), meaning "study of."  Therefore, pharmacology literally means "the study of drugs."
