portapotty:/ˈpɔːrtəˌpɑːti/| n. |临时厕所；便携式厕所 |A porta-potty is a temporary, portable toilet often used at outdoor events or construction sites.  It's typically made of plastic and has a built-in tank for waste.| 例句：The festival organizers provided plenty of porta-potties for the large crowd.  节日组织者为众多人群提供了充足的临时厕所。

The porta-potties were overflowing after the concert.  音乐会结束后，临时厕所都满了。|近义词：portable toilet, temporary restroom |反义词：permanent restroom, indoor toilet
