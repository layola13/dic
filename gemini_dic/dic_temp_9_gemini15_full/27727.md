cangue:/ˈkæŋɡ/| n. |木枷；颈夹|A cangue is a large wooden board with a hole for the neck, used as a punishment in some Asian countries.  It was typically placed around a prisoner's neck, restricting their movement and making them vulnerable to public scorn.  The weight and size of the cangue varied. |The criminal was forced to wear a heavy cangue for days.  犯人被迫戴着沉重的木枷好几天。|pillory, stocks|freedom, liberty|名词
