dimetrodon:/ˈdaɪmɛtrədɒn/| n. |二齿兽|Dimetrodon is an extinct genus of synapsids that lived during the Permian period.  It is often mistaken for a dinosaur, but it actually predates the dinosaurs.  It is characterized by its large sail-like structure on its back, which is believed to have been used for thermoregulation. | Example:  The Dimetrodon's sail was a striking feature.  二齿兽的帆状物是一个显著的特征。  The fossil of the Dimetrodon was found in Texas. 在德克萨斯州发现了二齿兽的化石。|近义词: synapsid (更广泛的分类)|反义词:  None (extinct genus)
