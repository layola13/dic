deepeningly:/ˈdiːpənɪŋli/ | adv. |日益加深地，越来越深地 | Increasingly deeply or intensely.  The adverb "deepeningly" describes something that is becoming more profound, intense, or serious over time. |例句：The crisis deepened, deepeningly affecting the global economy. 危机日益加深，越来越严重地影响着全球经济。
The relationship between the two countries is deepeningly strained. 两国之间的关系日益紧张。|近义词：increasingly, progressively, gradually, relentlessly |反义词：superficially, lightly, shallowly |词性:副词
