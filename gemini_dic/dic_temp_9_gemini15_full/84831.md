hang-glider:/ˈhæŋˌɡlaɪdər/| n. |滑翔伞；滑翔伞飞行员 |A hang glider is an aircraft without an engine, consisting of a large kite-like wing supported by a frame, on which a pilot sits or lies.  It relies on air currents and the pilot's skill to stay aloft.| 例句：He took a hang-gliding lesson and soared through the air. 他上了一节滑翔伞课，在空中翱翔。

He is an experienced hang-glider, known for his daring flights. 他是一位经验丰富的滑翔伞飞行员，以其大胆的飞行而闻名。|近义词：glider |反义词：airplane, helicopter
