infesting: /ɪnˈfestɪŋ/ | v. | 侵扰；寄生；蔓延 | To spread to or over in large numbers and cause damage or harm.  |例句：The house was infested with rats. 这房子鼠患严重。 The garden was infested with weeds. 花园里杂草丛生。| 近义词：overrunning, swarming, plaguing | 反义词：clearing, purifying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The insects are infesting the crops. = 昆虫正在侵害庄稼。
Pests are infesting our garden. = 害虫正在侵扰我们的花园。

一般过去时:
Rats infested the old building. = 老建筑里老鼠成灾。
The area was infested with mosquitoes last summer. = 去年夏天这个地区蚊子很多。

现在完成时:
The farm has been infested with locusts. = 农场蝗虫成灾了。
The house has been infested by termites for years. = 这房子多年来一直被白蚁侵蚀。

过去完成时:
The ship had been infested with cockroaches before it reached port. = 这艘船在到达港口之前就已经蟑螂成灾了。
Before the treatment, the area had been infested with ticks. = 在治疗之前，该地区一直有大量壁虱。

将来时:
If we don't act now, the disease will infest the whole region. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，疾病将会蔓延整个地区。
The weeds will infest the garden if we don't pull them out. = 如果我们不拔掉杂草，杂草就会蔓延整个花园。

虚拟语气:
If the field were infested with weeds, the harvest would be poor. = 如果田地里长满了杂草，收成就会很差。
I wish this house weren't infested with ants. = 我真希望这房子里没有蚂蚁。

被动语态:
The area was infested by a swarm of bees. = 该地区被一群蜜蜂侵扰。
The crops were infested with aphids. = 庄稼蚜虫成灾。

疑问句:
Is the house infested with termites? = 这房子有白蚁吗？
Are the plants infested with any diseases? = 植物感染了什么疾病吗？

否定句:
The area is not infested with snakes. = 这个地区没有蛇。
The orchard wasn't infested with pests this year. = 果园今年没有害虫。
