dynamited:/ˈdaɪnəmaɪtɪd/| v. | 用炸药炸毁；使爆炸 | To destroy something using dynamite.  |The building was dynamited by the terrorists.  恐怖分子用炸药炸毁了那座建筑物。|  blast, explode | repair, construct | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dynamite the old bridge. = 他们炸毁了那座旧桥。
The miners dynamite the rocks to get minerals. = 矿工们用炸药炸开岩石以获取矿物。


一般过去时:
The demolition crew dynamited the building. = 拆迁队炸毁了那栋建筑物。
They dynamited the mountain to build a road. = 他们炸山修路。


现在完成时:
They have dynamited several buildings in the city. = 他们已经炸毁了该市的几座建筑物。
The construction company has dynamited the rock to build a tunnel. = 建设公司已经炸掉了岩石来修建隧道。


过去完成时:

The police discovered that the thieves had dynamited the safe. = 警察发现小偷已经炸开了保险箱。

The engineers will dynamite the cliff to widen the road. = 工程师们将炸掉悬崖以拓宽道路。


虚拟语气:
If they dynamited the dam, the city would be flooded. = 如果他们炸毁水坝，城市就会被淹没。
I wish they hadn't dynamited that historical building. = 我真希望他们没有炸毁那座历史建筑。


被动语态:
