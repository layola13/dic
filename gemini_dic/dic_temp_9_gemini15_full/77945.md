genomes:/ˈdʒinoʊmz/ | n. |基因组|The complete set of genes or genetic material present in a cell or organism.  A genome includes both the genes and the non-coding sequences of the DNA.| 例句：Scientists are studying the human genome to understand the causes of diseases. 科学家们正在研究人类基因组以了解疾病的成因。  The genomes of different species can be compared to understand evolutionary relationships. 不同物种的基因组可以进行比较，以了解进化关系。 |近义词：genetic material, DNA, karyotype |反义词：无
