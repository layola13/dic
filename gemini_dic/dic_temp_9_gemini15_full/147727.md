overprinting:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪntɪŋ/ | n. |套印，重印|The process of printing one color or image over another, already printed color or image.  This technique is often used to create richer colors or special effects. For example, in the printing of banknotes, overprinting helps to prevent counterfeiting.|例句：The intricate design on the stamp was achieved through skillful overprinting. 这枚邮票上复杂的图案是通过熟练的套印技术实现的。

The artist used overprinting to add depth and texture to their artwork.艺术家使用套印来增加作品的深度和质感。|近义词：superimposing, layering|反义词：underprinting (less common)
