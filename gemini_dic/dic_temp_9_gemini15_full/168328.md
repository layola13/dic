pro-Renaissance: /ˌproʊˌrɛnəˈsɑːns/ | adj. | 前文艺复兴的；文艺复兴之前的 |  Referring to the period of art and culture immediately preceding the Italian Renaissance, typically encompassing the late medieval period.  It signifies a transitional phase characterized by evolving artistic and intellectual trends that foreshadowed the Renaissance. | Example:  Pro-Renaissance art often shows a blend of Gothic and classical elements.  前文艺复兴时期的艺术作品通常融合了哥特式和古典元素。 | 近义词: Late Medieval, pre-Renaissance | 反义词: Renaissance, Post-Renaissance
