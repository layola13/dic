myrtaceous:/mɜːrˈteɪʃəs/| adj.|桃金娘科的；桃金娘属的|Belonging to or characteristic of the myrtle family (Myrtaceae), a large family of flowering plants that includes many shrubs and trees with aromatic leaves and often showy flowers. The term often implies the presence of fragrant oils or a particular leaf structure.|Example: The air was filled with the distinctive, myrtaceous scent of eucalyptus.|近义词：myrtle-like|反义词：无
