preexecuting: /ˌpriːˈɛɡzɪkjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先执行；预先处理 |  The act of performing an action or task before another, typically as a preparation or prerequisite. This term is often used in the context of computer programming or system administration where certain operations need to be completed before the main process begins.  | Example: The script preexecutes a series of commands to set up the necessary environment variables before launching the application.  这个脚本在启动应用程序之前预先执行一系列命令来设置必要的环境变量。 | 近义词: pre-processing,  preparing,  initializing | 反义词: postexecuting, finalizing | 动词
