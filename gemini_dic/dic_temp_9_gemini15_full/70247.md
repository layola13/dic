fictionalizations:/ˌfɪkʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |虚构化；虚构作品|The act of making something fictional; the process of creating fictional versions of real events or people.  It often involves adapting or reinterpreting real-life stories or characters for a narrative purpose.  Plural form of fictionalization.|例句：The movie is a loose fictionalization of actual events. 这部电影是对真实事件的松散虚构。  The novel offers a compelling fictionalization of the author's childhood. 这部小说对作者的童年进行了引人入胜的虚构描写。|近义词：dramatization, adaptation, romanticization|反义词：factual representation, documentary, reality|名词
