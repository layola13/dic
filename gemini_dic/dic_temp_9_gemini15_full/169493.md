propagates:/ˈprɒpəɡeɪts/|/ˈprɑːpəɡeɪts/| vt. |传播；繁殖；增殖 |To spread or transmit something, especially information, widely.  To reproduce or multiply. |例句：The rumor propagated quickly through the town.  谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。
The plant propagates by seeds. 这植物靠种子繁殖。|近义词：disseminate, spread, multiply, reproduce |反义词：suppress, stifle, extinguish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fire propagates rapidly in dry conditions. = 在干燥的环境下，火势迅速蔓延。
The company propagates its brand through social media. = 公司通过社交媒体推广其品牌。


一般过去时:
The news propagated throughout the country within hours. = 消息在几个小时内传遍了全国。
The farmer propagated new varieties of roses. = 农民繁殖了新品种的玫瑰。


现在完成时:
The virus has propagated widely across the globe. = 病毒已在全球广泛传播。
The gardener has successfully propagated several rare orchids. = 园丁成功地繁殖了几种稀有的兰花。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the printing press, information had propagated slowly. = 在印刷术发明之前，信息的传播速度很慢。
By the time the authorities intervened, the false news had already propagated extensively. = 当当局介入时，假新闻已经广泛传播了。


将来时:
Scientists predict that the disease will propagate rapidly in warmer climates. = 科学家预测该疾病在较温暖的气候下会迅速传播。
The company plans to propagate its new product line through targeted advertising. = 公司计划通过定向广告宣传其新的产品线。


虚拟语气:
If the rumor had not propagated so quickly, the damage would have been less severe. = 如果谣言没有传播得那么快，损失就不会那么严重。
If we propagated these ideas more effectively, we would see better results. = 如果我们更有效地传播这些理念，我们将看到更好的结果。


被动语态:
The idea was propagated through various channels. = 这个想法通过各种渠道传播。
New plant varieties are propagated through cuttings. = 新的植物品种通过扦插繁殖。


疑问句:
How does this plant propagate? = 这种植物是如何繁殖的？
How is the misinformation being propagated? = 虚假信息是如何传播的？


否定句:

The seeds failed to propagate in the poor soil. = 种子在贫瘠的土壤中未能繁殖。
