earwax:/ˈɪrwæks/| n. |耳垢，耳屎|A yellowish waxy substance secreted in the ear canal, protecting the eardrum from dust and other foreign particles.|例句：Excessive earwax can lead to hearing problems.  过多的耳垢会导致听力问题。

例句：The doctor removed the impacted earwax from his ear. 医生为他清除了堵塞的耳垢。


近义词：cerumen


反义词：无


词性：名词
