dancingly: /ˈdænsɪŋli/ | adv. | 舞蹈般地，轻快地，活泼地 | In a manner suggesting dancing; lightly, playfully, or sprightly. | 例句：She moved dancingly across the room. 她轻快地穿过房间。 The leaves danced dancingly in the breeze. 叶子在微风中轻盈地舞蹈。 | 近义词：lightly, playfully, sprightly, gracefully | 反义词：heavily, clumsily, awkwardly | 副词
