digitalise: /ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪz/ | vt. |数字化；使…电子化 | To convert information into a digital format.  This often involves scanning documents, images, or audio/video into a computer system for storage or processing. | 例句：The library is in the process of digitalising its entire collection. 图书馆正在对其所有藏品进行数字化处理。

例句：We need to digitalise our workflows to improve efficiency. 我们需要将我们的工作流程数字化以提高效率。


近义词：digitize, computerize


反义词：dematerialize (in the sense of making something less accessible or tangible)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They digitalise their records every year. = 他们每年都将他们的记录数字化。
The company digitalises all incoming documents. = 公司将所有传入的文件数字化。

一般过去时:
She digitalised her old photos last week. = 她上周将她的旧照片数字化了。

现在完成时:

词源:  The word "digitalise" is a back-formation from "digital," which itself comes from "digit" (a finger or toe, and by extension, a numeral).  The "-ise" suffix is a common verb-forming element, indicating the process of making something digital.
