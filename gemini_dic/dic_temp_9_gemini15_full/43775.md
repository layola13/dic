cremain:/krɪˈmeɪn/| v.| 焚烧遗体；火化 |To cremate a body; to burn a dead body to ashes.  |例句：His remains were cremated after the funeral. 他的遗体在葬礼后被火化了。  |近义词：incinerate, burn |反义词：bury, inter
