rhaphides:/ˈræfɪdiːz/| n. |针晶束，针状结晶体 | Bundles of needle-shaped crystals, typically calcium oxalate, found in the cells of some plants.  These crystals can cause irritation if ingested. | Example:  The microscopic analysis revealed the presence of numerous rhaphides in the plant tissue.  显微分析显示植物组织中存在许多针晶束。 |近义词：crystal bundles, acicular crystals |反义词：无
