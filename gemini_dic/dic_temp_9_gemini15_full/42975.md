coves:/koʊvz/ | n. |小海湾，海湾 |A cove is a small, sheltered bay or inlet, typically with a curved shore.  It is often characterized by its secluded and peaceful nature. |例句：The small fishing boats were sheltered in the quiet cove.  这些小渔船停泊在宁静的小海湾里。

The secluded cove was a perfect spot for a picnic.  这个僻静的小海湾是野餐的理想场所。

|近义词：bay, inlet, creek |反义词：open sea, ocean
