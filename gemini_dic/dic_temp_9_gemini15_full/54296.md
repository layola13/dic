disgusting: /dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋ/ | adj. |令人作呕的，令人厌恶的 | causing a feeling of strong dislike or disgust; extremely unpleasant. |

例句：

The smell in the garbage can was disgusting.  = 垃圾桶里的气味令人作呕。

That movie was absolutely disgusting; I couldn't finish watching it. = 那部电影简直令人厌恶，我都没看完。


近义词：repulsive, nauseating, sickening, offensive, loathsome, revolting

反义词：pleasant, delightful, appealing, attractive

词源:

disgusting源于动词 disgust，加上形容词后缀 -ing 构成。disgust 本身来自古法语 desgust,  由  des- (表示“去掉”，“剥夺”) 和古法语gust  (表示“味觉”， “喜好”) 构成。  因此，disgust 本身的意思是“使失去味觉”，引申为“使厌恶”。
