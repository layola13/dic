gunfights:/ˈɡʌnfaɪts/| n. |枪战|A gunfight is a fight in which guns are used.  It often implies a relatively short, intense exchange of gunfire between two or more individuals or groups.|Example: The film climaxed with a spectacular gunfight in the dusty streets.|近义词：shootout, firefight, gun battle|反义词：peaceful resolution, negotiation
