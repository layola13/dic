penalty:/ˈpɛnəlti/| n.|惩罚，处罚|A penalty is a punishment imposed for a violation of a rule or law.|例句：He received a penalty for speeding. 他因超速而受到处罚。|近义词：punishment, sanction, fine|反义词：reward, prize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The penalty for speeding is a fine. = 超速的处罚是罚款。
What is the penalty for cheating on the exam? = 考试作弊的惩罚是什么？


一般过去时:
The judge imposed a heavy penalty on the criminal. = 法官对罪犯处以重罚。
What penalty did he receive for his actions? = 他因自己的行为受到了什么处罚？


现在完成时:
The team has received several penalties this season. = 这个赛季球队已经受到了几次处罚。
He has already paid the penalty for his mistakes. = 他已经为自己的错误付出了代价。


过去完成时:
The team had already incurred several penalties before halftime. = 中场休息前，球队已经受到了几次处罚。
They had paid the penalty before the trial even began. = 审判甚至开始之前，他们就已经付出了代价。


将来时:
The player will receive a penalty if he fouls again. = 如果该球员再次犯规，将受到处罚。
What penalty will they face if they violate the contract? = 如果他们违反合同，将面临什么处罚？


虚拟语气:
If he had not committed the crime, he would not have faced the penalty. = 如果他没有犯罪，他就不会面临惩罚。
I wish I hadn't committed that mistake; I wouldn't have to pay the penalty now. = 我真希望我没有犯那个错误；我现在就不必为此付出代价了。


被动语态:
A penalty was imposed for breaking the rules. = 违反规章制度将受到处罚。
The penalty for littering will be strictly enforced. = 乱扔垃圾的处罚将得到严格执行。


疑问句:
What is the penalty for parking here? = 在这里停车的处罚是什么？
What penalty can I expect to receive? = 我可以预期会受到什么处罚？


否定句:
There is no penalty for late submission. = 延迟提交没有处罚。
He did not receive any penalty for his behavior. = 他因为自己的行为没有受到任何处罚。
