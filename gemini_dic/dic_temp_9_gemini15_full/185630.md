reverberate:/rɪˈvɜːrbəreɪt/| v.|回响，反响，震动|To continue in a series of echoes; resound.  To be felt or experienced as a continuing effect. |例句：The sound of the explosion reverberated through the valley. 爆炸声在山谷中回荡。 The impact of the news reverberated throughout the company.  这个消息的影响在整个公司引起了反响。|近义词：echo, resonate, resound, vibrate|反义词：fade, die away


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The church bells reverberate across the town. 教堂的钟声在镇上回荡。
His words reverberate with the audience.  他的话语在听众中引起共鸣。


一般过去时:
The thunder reverberated across the sky. 雷声在空中回荡。
The news of the scandal reverberated throughout the nation. 丑闻的消息在全国引起强烈反响。


现在完成时:
The effects of the earthquake have reverberated throughout the region. 地震的影响已经波及整个地区。
The singer's performance has reverberated in my mind. 歌手的表演在我的脑海中久久回荡。


过去完成时:
The criticisms had reverberated through the department before the meeting. 批评在会议前就已经在部门里引起反响。
The decision had reverberated long after it was made.  这个决定在做出很久以后依然影响深远。


将来时:
The announcement will reverberate across the globe.  这项声明将在全球引起反响。
The concert's impact will reverberate for years to come. 音乐会的余波将在未来几年持续影响。


虚拟语气:
If the news were to reverberate as expected, the stock market would plummet. 如果消息像预期那样引起反响，股市就会暴跌。
I wish my achievements would reverberate through the ages. 我希望我的成就能够流芳百世。


被动语态:
The message was reverberated across the social media.  这条消息在社交媒体上广泛传播。
His ideas were reverberated by many scholars. 他的思想得到了许多学者的认同和传播。


疑问句:
Will the impact of the policy reverberate throughout society?  这项政策的影响会在整个社会引起反响吗？
Does the artist's work reverberate with viewers? 艺术家的作品引起观众共鸣吗？


否定句:
The speech did not reverberate with the audience.  演讲没有引起听众的共鸣。
The sound did not reverberate across the open field.  声音没有在空旷的田野里回荡。

词源:
From Latin *re- "back" +  verberāre "to strike, beat".  The original sense was of a repeated striking or beating, leading to the sense of echoing sound.
