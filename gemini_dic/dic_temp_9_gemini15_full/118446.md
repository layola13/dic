mescal:/mɛsˈkæl/| n. |梅斯卡尔酒 |A distilled alcoholic beverage made from the agave plant, particularly in Mexico.  It is often smoky in flavor and differs from tequila in its production methods and the types of agave used.|  This mescal has a wonderful smoky flavor. 这杯梅斯卡尔酒有着令人愉悦的烟熏味。|The mescal was served with lime and salt. 梅斯卡尔酒配上青柠和盐一起上桌。|tequila, pulque|  (无直接反义词，可考虑non-alcoholic beverages)|名词
