part and parcel: /pɑːrt ænd ˈpɑːrsəl/ | n. |不可分割的一部分；重要组成部分| An essential or integral part of something.  It emphasizes the inseparability of the components. |例句：Regular exercise is part and parcel of a healthy lifestyle.  规律的锻炼是健康生活方式不可分割的一部分。

例句：Volunteering is part and parcel of our company culture. 志愿服务是我们公司文化不可分割的一部分。


近义词：integral part, essential element, vital component, key ingredient

反义词：extraneous element, unimportant aspect, insignificant detail

词性: 名词短语
