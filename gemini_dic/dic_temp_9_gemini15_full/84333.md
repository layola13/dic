/ˈhælfər/| n. |半数；一半的人；半价|A halfer is a person or thing that is half of something else, often used informally.  It can refer to half a price, half a portion, or half the number of people. The term is less common than "half" but can be used in informal contexts.|例句：They sold the tickets for half the price, a real halfer!他们以半价出售门票，真是便宜！|近义词：half, half-price, fifty percent|反义词：whole, full price, entirety|
