displayers:/dɪˈspleɪərz/ | n. | 显示器；展示者 | A displayer is a device or person that displays something.  It can refer to a screen showing information, a stand showcasing products, or a person presenting something. | Example: The museum uses interactive displayers to engage visitors. 博物馆使用互动显示器来吸引游客。  The art displayers carefully arranged the paintings. 艺术展示者小心地摆放了绘画作品。 |近义词：exhibitors, showcases, monitors, screens | 反义词：concealers, hiders | 名词
