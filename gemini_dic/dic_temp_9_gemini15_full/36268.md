cohabitants:/koʊˈhæbɪtənts/ | n. |同居者，共同居住者 | People who live together, especially a couple who are not married.  |例句：The cohabitants shared the household chores equally. 同居者平等地分担家务。

例句：The survey questioned cohabitants about their living arrangements.  调查询问了同居者关于他们的居住安排。


近义词：companions, housemates, partners (if romantic) | 反义词：strangers, individuals living separately


词源:
co- (together) + habitant (inhabitant, dweller).  The word evolved from the verb "to inhabit," reflecting the shared living space.
