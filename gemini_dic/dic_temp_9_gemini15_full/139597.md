ocarina:/ˌɒkəˈriːnə/| n. |奥卡丽娜（一种陶土吹奏乐器）|A small, egg-shaped wind instrument made of clay or ceramic, played by blowing across a hole and covering finger holes to produce different notes.  It is known for its sweet, flute-like sound.|例句：He played a beautiful melody on his ocarina. 他用他的奥卡丽娜吹奏了一首优美的旋律。|近义词：flute, whistle (in some contexts)|反义词：None.
