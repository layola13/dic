name-plate:/ˈneɪmpleɪt/| n. |铭牌；姓名牌 | A name-plate is a small plate bearing a person's or company's name, often attached to a door, desk, or other item.  |Example:  He proudly displayed his new name-plate on his office door. 他自豪地将他的新铭牌展示在他的办公室门上。  |近义词：sign, label, plaque |反义词：无
