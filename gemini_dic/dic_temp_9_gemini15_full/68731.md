farmed:/fɑːrmd/ | v. |耕种；养殖；经营农场|  To cultivate land for crops or raise livestock.  It implies the systematic and organized management of agricultural land or animals for profit or sustenance.| 例句：They farmed the land for generations. 他们世世代代耕种这片土地。

They farmed salmon in large tanks. 他们在大型水箱中养殖鲑鱼。


近义词：cultivated, raised, cultivated, operated (a farm) | 反义词：abandoned (a farm), neglected (land)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They farm wheat and corn. = 他们种植小麦和玉米。
The farmer farms his land diligently. = 这位农民勤勉地耕种他的土地。

一般过去时:
We farmed that field last year. = 我们去年耕种了那块田地。
My grandfather farmed this land for fifty years. = 我祖父耕种这片土地五十年了。

现在完成时:
They have farmed this land for centuries. = 他们世世代代耕种这片土地。
She has farmed organically for many years. = 她多年来一直从事有机农业。

过去完成时:
They had farmed the land before the drought. = 干旱之前他们已经耕种了这片土地。
The family had farmed that area for generations. = 这个家庭世世代代都在那个地区务农。

将来时:
We will farm a larger area next year. = 我们明年将耕种更大的面积。
They will farm sustainably in the future. = 他们将来会进行可持续农业耕作。


虚拟语气:
If I farmed my own land, I would grow organic vegetables. = 如果我耕种自己的土地，我会种植有机蔬菜。
I wish I farmed, I'd have more time in nature. = 我希望自己从事农业耕作，那样我就能有更多时间亲近大自然。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
