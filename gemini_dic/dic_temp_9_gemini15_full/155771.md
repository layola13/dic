phonogram:/ˈfoʊnəˌɡræm/|/ˈfəʊnəˌɡræm/| n. | 语音符号；音标；表音文字 | A phonogram is a written symbol representing a speech sound or phoneme, often used in teaching reading and spelling.  It can refer to a single letter, a group of letters, or a symbol representing a sound.  | Example: The letter 'a' is a simple phonogram; 'sh' is a digraph phonogram. 语音符号'a'是一个简单的音标；'sh'是一个双字母音标。|近义词：grapheme, symbol |反义词：logogram (a symbol representing a word)
