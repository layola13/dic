microfilmed:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlmd/ | v. | 缩微胶卷拍摄；将…缩微胶卷化 | To photograph (documents, etc.) on microfilm.  |例句：The historical documents were microfilmed for preservation.  历史文件被缩微胶卷拍摄以作保存。|例句：The library microfilmed all its old newspapers. 图书馆将其所有旧报纸都缩微胶卷化了。|近义词： filmed, photographed, recorded |反义词：enlarged, expanded
