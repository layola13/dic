preinspecting: /ˌpriːɪnˈspektɪŋ/ | v. | 预先检查；事先检验 |  The act of inspecting something beforehand.  It implies a thorough examination before the main inspection or use. | 例句：The engineers were preinspecting the bridge for structural weaknesses before opening it to traffic.  工程师们在桥梁通车前对其进行预先检查，以查找结构缺陷。

The building inspector is preinspecting the new construction to ensure it meets all safety codes. 建筑检查员正在对新建筑进行预先检查，以确保其符合所有安全规范。


近义词：pre-examining, surveying, reviewing, checking beforehand | 反义词：post-inspecting, reviewing after use
