maxing:/ˈmæksɪŋ/ | v. | 最大化；达到最大值 |  To reach or cause to reach a maximum level or extent.  |  例句：He's maxing out his credit cards. 他正在透支信用卡。|  例句：The company is maxing out its production capacity. 公司正在发挥其最大的生产能力。| 近义词：optimizing, maximizing | 反义词：minimizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are maxing out their potential. = 他们在最大限度地发挥潜力。
We max out our resources every month. = 我们每个月都用尽了我们的资源。


一般过去时:
The athlete maxed out his performance during the competition. = 运动员在比赛中发挥了最佳水平。
She maxed out her savings account to buy a house. = 她用光了储蓄账户的钱买了一栋房子。


现在完成时:
He has maxed out his phone's storage. = 他已经用光了手机的存储空间。
They have maxed out their credit limits. = 他们已经用完了他们的信用额度。


过去完成时:
I had maxed out my energy before the marathon started. = 马拉松开始之前，我已经耗尽了精力。
The server had maxed out its processing power before the crash. = 服务器在崩溃之前已经达到了其最大的处理能力。


将来时:
We will max out our productivity next quarter. = 下个季度我们将使我们的生产力达到最大值。
The engineer predicts that the system will max out under heavy load. = 工程师预测该系统在重负载下将达到最大值。


虚拟语气:
If I maxed out my training, I would perform better. = 如果我把训练做到极致，我会表现得更好。
I wish I had maxed out my investment earlier. = 我真希望我早点把投资做到最大化。


被动语态:
The system was maxed out due to the high traffic. = 由于流量过大，系统达到了最大值。
The server's capacity was maxed out, leading to service disruption. = 服务器的容量已达到最大值，导致服务中断。


疑问句:
Are you maxing out your study time? = 你是否充分利用了学习时间？
Has the factory maxed out its production for the year? = 工厂今年的产量是否已经达到最大值？


否定句:
We didn't max out our budget this year. = 今年我们没有用完预算。
They didn't max out their potential in the project. = 他们在项目中没有充分发挥潜力。
