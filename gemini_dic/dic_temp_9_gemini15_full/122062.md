mistemper:/mɪsˈtɛmpər/| v.|脾气暴躁，使…脾气暴躁，(金属)回火不当|To spoil the temper of (metal) by overheating; to put (metal) into a state of bad temper; to make or become irritable or bad-tempered.|例句：The blacksmith mistempered the sword, making it brittle.  铁匠回火不当，使剑变得易碎。|例句：His mistempered nature led to many arguments. 他暴躁的脾气导致了很多争吵。|近义词：irritate, anger, exasperate,  spoil (in the context of metal)|反义词：soothe, calm, appease, temper (in the context of metal)
