capitalist:/ˈkæpɪtəlɪst/| n. |资本家；资本主义者；资本主义拥护者 |A capitalist is a person who owns capital, such as factories, machines, and other resources used to produce goods and services. They typically invest their capital to make a profit.  |Example:  He's a successful capitalist who built his empire from the ground up. 他是一位从零开始建立自己商业帝国的成功资本家。|近义词： entrepreneur, tycoon, magnate |反义词： socialist, communist


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The capitalist invests in the stock market. = 这位资本家投资股市。
Capitalists often seek to maximize profits. = 资本家通常寻求利润最大化。


一般过去时:
The capitalist sold his company last year. = 这位资本家去年卖掉了他的公司。
Many capitalists lost money during the recession. = 许多资本家在经济衰退期间损失了钱财。


现在完成时:
The capitalist has accumulated a vast fortune. = 这位资本家已经积累了巨额财富。
She has been a successful capitalist for over two decades. = 她已经是一位成功的资本家超过二十年了。


过去完成时:
The capitalist had already invested heavily before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，这位资本家已经进行了巨额投资。
They had been capitalists for generations. = 他们世世代代都是资本家。


将来时:
The capitalist will expand his business into new markets. = 这位资本家将把他的业务扩展到新的市场。
Capitalists will likely face increasing regulation. = 资本家可能会面临越来越严格的监管。


虚拟语气:
If I were a capitalist, I would invest in renewable energy. = 如果我是个资本家，我会投资可再生能源。
I wish I had the capital of a successful capitalist. = 我希望我有像一个成功的资本家那样的资本。


被动语态:
The company was bought by a wealthy capitalist. = 这家公司被一位富有的资本家收购了。
Capitalism is often criticized for its inequalities. = 资本主义经常因为其不平等而受到批评。


疑问句:
Is he a capitalist? = 他是资本家吗？
Are capitalists responsible for social inequality? = 资本家应该为社会不平等负责吗？


否定句:

Many people are not capitalists; they are employees. = 许多人不是资本家；他们是雇员。
