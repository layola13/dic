lodgings:/ˈlɒdʒɪŋz/ | n. |住所，住宿处 | Rooms or a place to sleep, especially rented ones.  Lodgings are temporary accommodations, often simpler than a hotel. | Example:  I found some cheap lodgings near the university. 我在大学附近找到了一些便宜的住所。  She secured lodgings for the summer. 她为夏天找到了住处。 |近义词：accommodations, rooms, quarters |反义词：home (in the sense of permanent residence)
