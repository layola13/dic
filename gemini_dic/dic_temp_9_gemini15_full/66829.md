expatiated:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪtɪd/ | v. |详述；详细解释；滔滔不绝地谈论 | To speak or write at length on a subject.  |例句：He expatiated on the virtues of his new invention. 他详细阐述了他新发明的优点。

例句：She expatiated at length about her travels in Italy. 她滔滔不绝地谈论她在意大利的旅行经历。


近义词：elaborate, detail, enlarge upon, dwell on, discourse on | 反义词：summarize, condense, abridge


词源:  From Late Latin *expatiatus, past participle of expatiari "to wander," from ex- "out" + spatiari "to walk, stroll," from spatium "space, extent."  The sense development is from "to wander in speech" to "to speak at length."
