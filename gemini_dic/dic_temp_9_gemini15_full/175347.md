rake-up: /reɪk ʌp/ | vt. | 搜集；挖掘；重新提起；责备 | To gather or collect something, often something scattered or unpleasant; to bring up or discuss something again, especially something unpleasant from the past; to reprimand or scold. | 例句：He raked up the leaves in the yard. 他把院子里的树叶耙了起来。  The journalist raked up the politician's past scandals.  记者挖出了这位政治家的过去丑闻。 | 近义词：gather, collect, unearth, dredge up, reprimand | 反义词：ignore, overlook, forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rakes up the leaves every autumn. = 他每年秋天都耙树叶。
She rakes up old grievances with her sister. = 她老是和她姐姐重提旧怨。

一般过去时:
They raked up the debris after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们清理了残骸。
He raked up my mistakes from years ago. = 他翻出了我几年前犯的错误。

现在完成时:
She has raked up all the evidence against him. = 她已经收集了所有对他不利证据。
They have raked up the past again and it's causing problems. = 他们又重提过去，这导致了一些问题。

过去完成时:
He had raked up the old argument before I could intervene. = 我还没来得及插手，他就又把旧账翻出来了。
She had raked up all the necessary documents before the meeting. = 她在会议前已经收集好了所有必要的文档。

将来时:
They will rake up the garden tomorrow. = 他们明天将整理花园。
He will rake up the past again if you provoke him. = 如果你激怒他，他会再次重提过去。


虚拟语气:
If he raked up all the evidence, he would win the case. = 如果他收集所有证据，他就能赢得官司。
I wish he hadn't raked up that old argument again. = 我真希望他不要再重提那个老话题了。

被动语态:
The leaves were raked up by the gardener. = 叶子被园丁耙起来了。
The old scandals were raked up by the media. = 那些旧丑闻被媒体重新翻了出来。

疑问句:
Will they rake up the old disagreements? = 他们会重提旧的不愉快吗？
Have they raked up any new evidence? = 他们收集到什么新的证据了吗？


否定句:
He didn't rake up the past during the conversation. = 他在谈话中没有提及过去。
She didn't rake up any problems at the meeting. = 她在会议上没有提出任何问题。
