flashly:/ˈflæʃli/ | adv. | 炫耀地；闪耀地；短暂地 |  In a showy or ostentatious manner; with a sudden bright flash of light; for a short time. |例句：He dressed flashily, hoping to impress everyone. 他穿着炫耀地，希望能给每个人留下深刻印象。|例句：The lightning flashed flashily across the night sky.  闪电在夜空中炫耀地闪烁。|近义词：showily, ostentatiously, brilliantly, briefly |反义词：subduedly, modestly, quietly
