highwaymen:/ˈhaɪweɪmən/| n.|公路强盗，拦路抢劫者|A highwayman was a robber who attacked travelers on roads, especially during the 17th and 18th centuries in England. They were often on horseback and carried weapons.  |Example:  The highwayman ambushed the stagecoach and stole all the passengers' valuables.  公路强盗伏击了驿马车，偷走了所有乘客的贵重物品。

The notorious highwayman Dick Turpin terrorized the countryside.臭名昭著的公路强盗迪克·特平让乡村陷入恐怖之中。|近义词：bandit, robber, brigand|反义词：policeman, lawman
