second-hander:/ˈsekəndˌhændər/| adj. n.|二手货；二手车；转卖人|A second-hander is something that has been previously owned or used.  It can refer to a person who buys and sells used goods, or the goods themselves. |例句：He bought a second-hander car. 他买了一辆二手车。

He's a second-hander, always buying and selling used goods. 他是个二手货贩子，总是买卖二手货。|近义词：used, pre-owned, secondhand|反义词：new, unused
