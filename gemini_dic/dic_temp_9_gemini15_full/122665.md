modularization:/ˌmɒdjuːləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 模块化；模块化设计 | The process of dividing a system or project into smaller, independent modules. This approach enhances code reusability, maintainability, and testability.  | Example: The modularization of the software allowed for easier updates and bug fixes.  模块化软件使得更新和修复错误更容易。|  decomposition, compartmentalization | integration, unification |
