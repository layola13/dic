nonminerals: /ˌnɒnˈmɪnərəlz/ | n. |非矿物 | Nonminerals are substances that are not minerals.  They lack one or more of the defining characteristics of a mineral, such as being naturally occurring, inorganic, solid, having a definite chemical composition, and having an ordered atomic arrangement.  Examples include wood, plastic, and coal. | Example:  Organic materials like wood and shells are nonminerals.  有机材料如木材和贝壳是非矿物质。


近义词: non-mineral substances, nonmetallic materials


反义词: minerals


词性: noun
