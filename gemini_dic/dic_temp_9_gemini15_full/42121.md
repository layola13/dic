cottolene:/ˈkɒtəliːn/ | n. | 植物油，人造奶油 | A brand name of vegetable shortening, now used generically to refer to any vegetable shortening.  It's a solid, white, fat substitute often used in baking and frying. | Example:  The recipe calls for one cup of cottolene.  这个食谱需要一杯植物油。 |近义词：vegetable shortening, lard (though lard is animal fat) | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with butter or olive oil.
