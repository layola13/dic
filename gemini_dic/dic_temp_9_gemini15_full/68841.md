fashion:/ˈfæʃən/| n. v.|时尚；流行；方式；风尚|The prevailing custom, manner, or style; a popular trend.|例句：The fashion industry is constantly evolving. 时尚产业不断发展。  The fashion of the times is to be informal. 当今的时尚是随意。|近义词：style, trend, vogue|反义词：antiquated, outdated|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This fashion is very popular. = 这种时尚非常流行。
What's the latest fashion in shoes? = 最新的鞋子流行趋势是什么？


一般过去时:
That fashion was popular in the 1980s. = 那种时尚在20世纪80年代很流行。
People followed that fashion blindly. = 人们盲目地追随那种时尚。


现在完成时:
Fashion has changed dramatically in recent years. = 近年来时尚发生了巨大的变化。
Have you followed the latest fashion trends? = 你关注最新的时尚潮流了吗？


过去完成时:
The fashion had already changed before she realized it. = 在她意识到之前，时尚已经变了。
They had followed that fashion for years. = 他们追随那种时尚已经很多年了。


将来时:
This fashion will be very popular next year. = 这种时尚明年将会非常流行。
What will be the next big fashion trend? = 下一个主要的时尚潮流将会是什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would follow the latest fashion. = 如果我有更多钱，我会追随最新的时尚。
I wish I could afford those fashionable clothes. = 我希望我能买得起那些时尚的衣服。


被动语态:
This fashion is widely copied. = 这种时尚被广泛模仿。
The new fashion was quickly adopted by young people. = 新时尚很快被年轻人所接受。


疑问句:
What's in fashion this season? = 这个季节流行什么？
Is this style still in fashion? = 这种风格还在流行吗？


否定句:
That dress is not in fashion anymore. = 那件衣服不再流行了。
She doesn't care about fashion. = 她不关心时尚。


PHRASE->
in fashion: 流行
out of fashion: 过时
high fashion: 高级时装
fast fashion: 快时尚
fashion show: 时装秀


词源:
The word "fashion" comes from the Old French word *facon*, meaning "making, doing, act," and ultimately from the Latin word *facere*, meaning "to do" or "to make."  The evolution reflects a shift from the act of making or shaping something to the resulting style or trend.
