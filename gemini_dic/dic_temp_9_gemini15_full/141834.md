orca:/ˈɔːrkə/| n. |逆戟鲸，虎鲸 |A large, powerful marine mammal belonging to the dolphin family, known for its intelligence and social behavior.  Orcas are apex predators, meaning they are at the top of the food chain. They are found in all oceans of the world.|例句：The orca breached the surface, its powerful body glistening in the sunlight.  逆戟鲸跃出水面，它强壮的身体在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：A pod of orcas was spotted hunting seals near the coast.一群逆戟鲸被发现正在海岸附近猎杀海豹。|近义词：killer whale|反义词：无|词性：名词
