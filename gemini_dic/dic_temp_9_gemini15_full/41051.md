coping:/ˈkoʊpɪŋ/ | v. n. |应对，处理；克服；(建筑)盖板 | To manage successfully; to deal with.  |例句：She's coping well with the pressure of her new job. 她正在很好地应对新工作的压力。
例句：He found coping with the loss of his wife extremely difficult. 他发现难以应对失去妻子的痛苦。

词性: 动词, 名词


近义词: managing, dealing with, overcoming, handling, tackling

反义词: failing, succumbing, giving up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He copes with stress by exercising. = 他通过锻炼来应对压力。
They cope well under pressure. = 他们在压力下应对良好。


一般过去时:
She coped admirably with the crisis. = 她出色地应对了危机。
We coped with the problem by working together. = 我们通过合作解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
He has coped with many challenges in his life. = 他一生中应对过许多挑战。
She has coped successfully with her illness. = 她成功地应对了她的疾病。


过去完成时:
They had coped with similar situations before. = 他们以前应对过类似的情况。
He had coped with the loss of his job before finding a new one. = 他在找到新工作之前已经应对了失业的打击。


将来时:
She will cope with the situation as best she can. = 她将尽其所能应对这种情况。
We will cope with whatever challenges arise. = 无论出现什么挑战，我们都会应对。


虚拟语气:
If I had better coping mechanisms, I would be less stressed. = 如果我有更好的应对机制，我的压力就会小一些。
I wish I knew how to cope with this better. = 我希望我知道如何更好地应对这种情况。


被动语态:
The problem was coped with effectively. = 这个问题得到了有效的解决。(注意：coping作被动语态使用频率较低)
The crisis is being coped with by the emergency services. = 紧急服务部门正在应对这场危机。(注意：coping作被动语态使用频率较低)


疑问句:
How do you cope with your anxiety? = 你如何应对你的焦虑？
How are they coping with the difficult situation? = 他们如何应对困境？


否定句:
He couldn't cope with the workload. = 他无法应付工作量。
She didn't cope well with the criticism. = 她没有很好地应对批评。


PHRASE->
coping mechanism = 应对机制
coping skills = 应对技巧
coping strategy = 应对策略
coping with stress = 应对压力
cope with = 应对，处理


词源:
源自古英语 *copian，意思是“交换，交易”。  其含义演变到“与…打交道”再到“应对”。
