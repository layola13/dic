overfeminized: /ˌoʊvərˈfemɪnaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度女性化的 | excessively feminized; characterized by an extreme emphasis on traditionally feminine traits or roles.  This term often implies a negative connotation, suggesting an artificial or unhealthy exaggeration of femininity.  | 例句：The marketing campaign was criticized for its overfeminized portrayal of women.  市场营销活动因其对女性的过度女性化刻画而受到批评。

The movie's plot felt overfeminized, reducing complex characters to stereotypes. 电影的情节感觉过于女性化，将复杂的角色简化为刻板印象。


近义词：effeminate (for men), overly delicate, excessively girly, hyperfeminine | 反义词：masculine, androgynous, tomboyish


词源:  The word is a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively or to an excessive degree) and "feminized" (the past participle of the verb "feminize," meaning to make or become feminine).  "Feminine" itself derives from Latin "femina" (woman).
