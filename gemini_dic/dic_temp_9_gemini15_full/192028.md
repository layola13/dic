scorpionidea:/ˌskɔːrpiənˈiːdiə/| n. |蝎子目|The order Scorpionidea is a group of arachnids that includes scorpions.  They are characterized by their segmented bodies, eight legs, a pair of pedipalps modified into claws, and a venomous tail ending in a stinger.  They are found in diverse habitats worldwide, except Antarctica. |例句：The study focused on the venom composition of several species within the Scorpionidea order.  这项研究侧重于蝎子目中几种蝎子的毒液成分。
