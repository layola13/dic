lymphy: /ˈlɪmfi/ | adj. | 淋巴的；淋巴液的 |  Characterized by or relating to lymph or the lymphatic system.  The term often describes a condition or appearance suggestive of lymphatic involvement, such as swelling or pallor. | 例句：The patient presented with lymphy swelling in the neck.  患者颈部出现淋巴肿胀。


例句：The biopsy showed lymphy tissue consistent with lymphoma. 活检显示淋巴组织与淋巴瘤一致。


近义词：lymphatic (more formal)


反义词：None directly opposite, but words describing the opposite of lymphatic swelling (e.g.,  uninflamed, normal) could be considered contextual antonyms.


词性：形容词
