quarreled: /ˈkwɒrəld/ /ˈkwɔːrəld/ | v. | 争吵；吵架 | To have an angry argument or disagreement. |例句：They quarreled loudly in the street.  他们在大街上大声争吵。

例句：The brothers quarreled over their inheritance. 兄弟俩为遗产争吵。


近义词：argued, bickered, squabbled, contended, wrangled | 反义词：agreed, harmonized


词源:  The word "quarreled" originates from the Old French word "quarreler," meaning "to quarrel." This, in turn, is derived from the Old French word "querre," meaning "quarrel," which is possibly related to the Old Norse word "kverra," meaning "a complaint, a grievance".
