hull:/hʌl/| n. |船体；外壳；（谷物等的）壳；|The outer shell of a ship, aircraft, or other structure.  The husk or outer covering of a seed, fruit, or nut. |例句：The ship's hull was damaged in the storm.  = 这艘船的船体在风暴中受损。

The nuts were difficult to crack because of their hard hulls. = 因为坚硬的外壳，这些坚果很难敲开。


近义词：shell, casing, frame, body |反义词：content, interior
