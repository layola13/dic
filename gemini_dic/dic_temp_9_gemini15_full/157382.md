piping-hot: /ˈpaɪpɪŋ hɑːt/| adj. |滚烫的，热气腾腾的 | Extremely hot; freshly cooked or made.  The adjective "piping" here intensifies the meaning of "hot," suggesting a very high degree of heat, often with visible steam or heat.  | 例句：The pizza was piping hot when it arrived.  送达时披萨热气腾腾的。

The coffee was piping hot and delicious. 咖啡滚烫且美味。


近义词：scalding, boiling, steaming, blazing


反义词：cold, lukewarm, tepid


词性：形容词
