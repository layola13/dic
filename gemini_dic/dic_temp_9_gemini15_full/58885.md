duratives:/ˈdʒʊərətɪvz/| n.|持续性动作或状态|Words or phrases that describe actions or states that continue over a period of time.  Duratives are often contrasted with punctual or instantaneous events.|Example:  The rain continued for hours. (持续性动作)|近义词：continuatives, protracted events|反义词：punctuals, instantaneous events|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is studying English. = 他正在学习英语。
She works at a bank. = 她在银行工作。


一般过去时:
They were playing football. = 他们正在踢足球。
He lived in London for five years. = 他在伦敦住了五年。


现在完成时:
I have been working here for ten years. = 我在这里工作十年了。
She has known him since childhood. = 她从孩童时代就认识他了。


过去完成时:
He had been waiting for an hour before she arrived. = 她到达之前他已经等了一个小时了。
They had lived there for years before they moved. = 他们在那儿住了很多年才搬家。


将来时:
I will be working late tonight. = 今晚我将工作到很晚。
They will be living in Paris next year. = 他们明年将住在巴黎。


虚拟语气:
If I were studying harder, I would pass the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我会通过考试。
I wish I were living in a warmer climate. = 我希望我住在一个气候更温暖的地方。


被动语态:
The house was being built for six months. = 这房子建造了六个月。
The project has been running for two years. = 这个项目已经运行了两年。


疑问句:
How long have you been learning Spanish? = 你学习西班牙语多久了？
Were they playing tennis when you called? = 你打电话时他们正在打网球吗？


否定句:
I wasn't sleeping. = 我没有睡觉。
They haven't been feeling well recently. = 他们最近身体不太舒服。


PHRASE->
for hours = 好几个小时
all day = 整天
for a long time = 很长时间
the whole week = 整周
since morning = 从早上开始
