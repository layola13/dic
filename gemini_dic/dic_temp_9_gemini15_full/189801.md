salvageable:/ˈsæl.vɪ.dʒə.bəl/ | adj. | 可抢救的；可挽回的 |  capable of being saved or repaired; retrievable from a bad situation.  | The damaged car is salvageable. 这辆受损的汽车是可以修复的。|  recoverable, retrievable, repairable | irretrievable, irreparable, unrecoverable
