exclusion:/ɪkˈskluʒən/| n. |排除；排斥；不包括|The act of excluding or the state of being excluded.  It refers to the deliberate omission or prevention of something or someone from a group, process, or place. |例句：The exclusion of women from the study skewed the results.  这项研究排除了女性，导致结果出现偏差。|例句：His exclusion from the team was a great disappointment. 他被排除在球队之外，非常失望。|近义词：omission, rejection, barring, expulsion|反义词：inclusion, admittance


USAGE->

The club's rules dictate the exclusion of members under 18. =  俱乐部的规则规定不允许18岁以下的成员加入。
Exclusion from the competition is a serious penalty. =  被排除在比赛之外是一种严重的惩罚。
