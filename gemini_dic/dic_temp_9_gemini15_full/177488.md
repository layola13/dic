rebirth:/ˌriːˈbɜːrθ/ | n. |重生；复兴；复活 |The process of being born again; a new beginning; revival.  |例句：The city experienced a rebirth after the war. 战后，这座城市经历了重生。|例句：The rebirth of interest in classical music is encouraging.  对古典音乐兴趣的复兴令人鼓舞。|近义词：renewal, regeneration, resurrection |反义词：death, demise, extinction |名词
