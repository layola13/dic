keelman:/ˈkiːlmən/| n. |平底船船长；船底工；（尤指煤船的）船长|A keelman is the captain or a crew member of a keelboat, especially one that carries coal.  The term often refers to those who worked on coal barges on rivers and canals.|例句：In the 18th century, keelmen played a vital role in transporting coal throughout Britain. = 在18世纪，平底船船长在英国各地运输煤炭方面发挥了至关重要的作用。

例句：The keelman skillfully navigated the narrow canal. = 船底工熟练地驾驶着狭窄的运河。


近义词： bargeman, collier (in the context of coal transport)|反义词：None specifically applicable|词性：名词
