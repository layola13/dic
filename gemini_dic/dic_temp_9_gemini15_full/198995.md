sigla:/ˈsɪɡlə/ | n. |缩写；缩略词；铭文|A sigla is a shortened form of a name, word, or phrase, often used as an abbreviation. It can also refer to an inscription or legend.  | 例句：NATO is a sigla for the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. 北约是北大西洋公约组织的缩写。 The sigla on the coin indicated its year of minting. 硬币上的铭文标明了它的铸造年份。|近义词：abbreviation, acronym, initialism |反义词：full form, complete name |名词
