dodge-out: /dɑːdʒ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 躲避；逃避；躲闪出去 | To quickly move away from something to avoid it.  This phrasal verb implies a sudden and skillful evasion, often to avoid a collision or confrontation. | 例句：He dodged out of the way just in time to avoid the speeding car. 他及时躲闪开来，避开了飞驰的汽车。

He dodged out of the meeting before the boss could ask him any difficult questions. 他在老板问他任何难题之前就溜出了会议。


近义词：evade, avoid, sidestep, shun | 反义词：confront, face, encounter
