moiety:/ˈmɔɪəti/| n. |一部分，半份 |A moiety is one of two equal or approximately equal parts of something.  It often refers to a portion, share, or part of a whole, not necessarily exactly half.  |例句：Each received a moiety of the inheritance.  每人继承了一半遗产。  The experiment involved testing a moiety of the participants. 实验包括测试一部分参与者。|近义词：portion, part, share, fraction |反义词：whole, entirety, totality |名词
