gryphon:/ˈɡrɪfɒn/|/ˈɡrɪfɑːn/| n. |狮鹫|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.  Gryphons are often depicted as guardians or symbols of power and strength. They are frequently featured in heraldry and mythology.|The gryphon perched atop the castle wall, a majestic guardian of the kingdom.|近义词：griffin|反义词：无
