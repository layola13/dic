shuffle:/ˈʃʌfəl/| vt. vi. n.|洗牌；拖着脚走；混乱；|to push your feet along the ground without lifting them fully, or to move in this way:  to mix cards or papers thoroughly: a confused mixture or disorganized state|例句：He shuffled his feet nervously. 他紧张地拖着脚走。例句：The magician shuffled the cards expertly. 魔术师熟练地洗牌。|近义词：drag, jumble, mess|反义词：order, organize
