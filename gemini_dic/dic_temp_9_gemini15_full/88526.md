hit-and-runs:/ˈhɪt ænd rʌnz/| n.|肇事逃逸；打带跑|A hit-and-run is an incident, typically a traffic accident, in which a vehicle collides with a person or another vehicle and the driver leaves the scene without providing assistance or identifying themselves.  It can also refer to a similar action in other contexts, such as a fight or an attack.|例句：The police are investigating a hit-and-run accident that occurred last night. 警方正在调查昨晚发生的一起肇事逃逸事故。

例句：He was charged with hit-and-run after leaving the scene of the accident.  事故发生后，他因肇事逃逸而被指控。


近义词：肇事逃逸，逃逸事故，逃之夭夭|反义词：承担责任，留下帮助
