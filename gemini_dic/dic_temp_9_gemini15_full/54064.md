disease:/dɪˈziːz/| n. |疾病，病，病害 |A disease is an abnormal condition affecting the body of an organism.  It is often characterized by specific signs and symptoms and is not simply a result of physical injury. Diseases can be caused by various factors including infections, genetic defects, environmental factors, and lifestyle choices. |例句：Heart disease is a leading cause of death in many countries.  心脏病是许多国家的主要死因。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with a rare disease. 医生诊断她患有罕见疾病。


近义词：illness, ailment, sickness, malady |反义词：health, wellness
