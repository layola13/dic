silentest: /ˈsaɪləntɪst/ | adj. | 最安静的，最沉默的 |  The superlative form of "silent," indicating the highest degree of silence or quiet. It describes something or someone that is quieter than anything else.  | 例句：The silentest room in the house was the library.  =  房子里最安静的房间是图书馆。 The silentest moment of the performance came just before the final note. = 演出中最安静的时刻出现在最后一音符奏响之前。 | 近义词：stillest, quietest, most hushed | 反义词：loudest, noisiest
