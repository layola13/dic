dendrology:/dɛnˈdrɑːlədʒi/ | n. |树木学，林学 |The scientific study of trees, including their classification, identification, distribution, and ecology.  |Example:  Dendrology is a fascinating field of study for those interested in the natural world. 树木学对于那些对自然界感兴趣的人来说是一个引人入胜的研究领域。 |近义词：silviculture, arboriculture |反义词：无
