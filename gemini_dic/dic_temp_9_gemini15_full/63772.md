epics:/ˈɛpɪks/| n.|史诗；史诗般的作品|A long narrative poem, often recounting heroic deeds or events of great importance.  It typically involves gods, heroes, and mythical creatures.|Example: Homer's "Iliad" and "Odyssey" are classic examples of epics.  荷马的《伊利亚特》和《奥德赛》是史诗的经典之作。|近义词：saga, epic poem, heroic tale|反义词：short story, anecdote
