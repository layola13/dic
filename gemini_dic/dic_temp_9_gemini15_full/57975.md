drive-bies:/ˈdraɪvbaɪz/| n. |驾车枪击案|A drive-by shooting is a crime where someone in a moving vehicle shoots a gun at people or property.  It's often associated with gang violence and is characterized by the speed and lack of direct confrontation.|例句：The police are investigating a series of drive-bies in the neighborhood.  警方正在调查该社区发生的一系列驾车枪击案。|近义词：ambush, attack (from a vehicle)|反义词：peaceful encounter, planned attack (not from a vehicle)|名词
