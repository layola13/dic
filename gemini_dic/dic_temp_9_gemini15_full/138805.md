numpty:/ˈnʌmptɪ/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋 |A numpty is a foolish or stupid person.  The term is informal and often used humorously, though it can be considered mildly offensive.  It's primarily used in British English. |例句：He's such a numpty; he can't even change a tire. 他真是个笨蛋，连换个轮胎都不会。  Don't be such a numpty! 不要这么傻！|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, twit, dope |反义词：genius, intellect, mastermind |名词
