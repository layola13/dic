scarifier:/ˈskærɪfaɪər/| n. | 耙地机；刺破器；起绒机 | A scarifier is a machine or tool used to break up or roughen a surface.  This can be the surface of the ground (in agriculture), a material like asphalt (in road construction), or fabric (in textile manufacturing).  It achieves this by using sharp teeth, tines, or blades. | Example: The farmer used a scarifier to prepare the field for planting.  农民用耙地机准备播种田地。


The road crew employed a scarifier to remove the old asphalt before repaving.  道路施工队在重新铺设沥青之前用起绒机去除旧沥青。

| 近义词：cultivator, breaker, roughener | 反义词：smoother, leveler | 名词
