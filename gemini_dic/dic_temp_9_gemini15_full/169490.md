propagandizing: /ˌprɒpəˈɡændaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈprɑːpəɡændaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 宣传；进行宣传 | The act of spreading ideas or beliefs, often in a biased or misleading way, to promote a political cause or point of view.  |  The government was accused of propagandizing its citizens to support the war.  政府被指控向其公民宣传以支持战争。  |  promoting, publicizing, advertising | countering, refuting, debunking |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are propagandizing their new product. = 他们正在宣传他们的新产品。
The party propagandizes its ideology relentlessly. = 这个党不遗余力地宣传其意识形态。


一般过去时:
The regime propagandized its successes to the people. = 该政权向人民宣传其成功之处。
They propagandized heavily before the election. = 他们在选举前进行了大规模的宣传。


现在完成时:
The company has been propagandizing its environmentally friendly policies. = 这家公司一直在宣传其环保政策。
They have propagandized this idea for years. = 他们多年来一直在宣传这个想法。


过去完成时:
The media had propagandized the public before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，媒体已经对公众进行了宣传。
The government had propagandized the benefits of the new law for months. = 政府已经宣传了几个月新法律的好处。


将来时:
They will propagandize their candidate heavily in the coming weeks. = 未来几周他们将大力宣传他们的候选人。
The organization will propagandize its cause through social media. = 该组织将通过社交媒体宣传其事业。


虚拟语气:
If they propagandized more effectively, they might win more support. = 如果他们更有效地进行宣传，他们可能会赢得更多支持。
I wish they hadn't propagandized so much misinformation. = 我真希望他们没有宣传这么多虚假信息。


被动语态:
The public was propagandized into believing the false claims. = 公众被宣传相信了那些虚假说法。
The new policy is being propagandized through various channels. = 新政策正在通过各种渠道进行宣传。


疑问句:
Are they propagandizing their political agenda? = 他们在宣传他们的政治议程吗？
How effectively are they propagandizing their views? = 他们宣传其观点的效率如何？


否定句:
They did not propagandize their views openly. = 他们没有公开宣传他们的观点。
The company does not propagandize unethical practices. = 该公司不宣传不道德的行为。


PHRASE->
propagandizing the masses = 向大众宣传
propagandizing a cause = 宣传一个事业
propagandizing through media = 通过媒体宣传
propagandizing effectively = 高效地宣传
propagandizing the truth = 宣传真相 (虽然这词组本身有点矛盾，但可以表达试图以宣传方式呈现真相的意思)
