oophorectomy:/uːpəˈrɛktəmi/| n. |卵巢切除术|The surgical removal of one or both ovaries.  This procedure is often performed to treat ovarian cancer, endometriosis, or other gynecological conditions.  It can also be a preventative measure in some cases. |例句：She underwent a bilateral oophorectomy to reduce her risk of ovarian cancer. 她接受了双侧卵巢切除术以降低患卵巢癌的风险。

例句：The doctor recommended a unilateral oophorectomy after discovering a benign cyst on one ovary. 医生在发现一个卵巢上的良性囊肿后，建议进行单侧卵巢切除术。


近义词：ovariectomy |反义词：ovary transplantation (卵巢移植)


词性：名词
