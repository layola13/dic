poke:/poʊk/| /pəʊk/ | vt. n.|戳，捅；拨弄；探出；（非正式）轻轻推；|to push or prod gently with a finger or pointed object; to investigate or inquire discreetly;  to extend or protrude slightly|例句：He poked the fire to make it burn brighter. 他拨弄火堆让它烧得更旺。|例句：She poked her head around the corner. 她探出头来。|近义词：prod, jab, nudge, stick|反义词：pull back, withdraw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pokes the bear with a stick. = 他用棍子戳熊。
She pokes her nose into everyone's business. = 她爱管闲事。


一般过去时:
I poked the cake to see if it was done. = 我戳了戳蛋糕看看是否烤好了。
He poked around in the attic looking for old toys. = 他在阁楼里四处翻找旧玩具。


现在完成时:
She has poked the fire several times. = 她已经多次拨弄火堆了。
He has poked his head out of the window several times. = 他已经好几次探头出窗外了。


过去完成时:
I had poked around in the drawer before I found the key. = 我在抽屉里翻找了一阵子才找到钥匙。
They had poked the sleeping dog before it woke up. = 他们戳了戳睡着的狗，然后它醒了。


将来时:
I will poke the cake to check if it's ready. = 我会戳一下蛋糕看看是否烤好了。
He will poke his head out to see what’s happening. = 他会探出头看看发生了什么事。


虚拟语气:
If I poked the bear, it would attack me. = 如果我戳熊，它会攻击我。
If I had poked the cake earlier, I would have known it was done. = 如果我早点戳一下蛋糕，我就知道它烤好了。


被动语态:
The fire was poked by the old man. = 火堆是被老人拨弄的。
The sleeping dog was poked by the child. = 睡着的狗是被孩子戳的。


疑问句:
Did you poke the sleeping cat? = 你戳睡着的猫了吗？
Are you poking fun at me? = 你在取笑我吗？


否定句:
Don't poke the sleeping dog. = 不要戳睡着的狗。
I didn't poke her; she fell. = 我没有戳她；她自己摔倒了。
