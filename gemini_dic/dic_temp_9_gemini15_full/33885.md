英文解析：A circumstance is a fact or event that makes a situation the way it is.  Circumstances can be favorable or unfavorable, and they often influence outcomes.  It is usually used in the plural form.

例句：

Under the circumstances, I think we should postpone the meeting.  在这种情况下，我认为我们应该推迟会议。

The success of the project depends on a number of favorable circumstances.  项目的成功取决于许多有利的因素。
