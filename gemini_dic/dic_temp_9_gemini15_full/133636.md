nonforfeiture:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːfɪtʃər/| n. | 保全权；不丧失权；失效权 | The right to retain a policy's cash value even if premiums are not paid.  This right protects the policyholder from losing their investment in the policy. | Example:  The nonforfeiture provision allowed him to keep the accumulated cash value despite missing several premium payments. = 保全条款使他即使错过了几次保费支付也能保留累积的现金价值。|近义词：retention right, right to surrender value |反义词：forfeiture, lapse
