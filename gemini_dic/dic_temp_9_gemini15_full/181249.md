reinstalling:/ˌriːɪnˈstɔːlɪŋ/ | v. |重新安装 |The act of installing something again.  This usually refers to software or hardware that has been removed or malfunctioned. |例句：I'm reinstalling the operating system because my computer is running slowly. = 我正在重新安装操作系统，因为我的电脑运行缓慢。

例句：After the virus attack, we had to reinstall all the software on the network. = 病毒攻击后，我们不得不重新安装网络上的所有软件。


近义词：re-installing, reinstalling again, setting up again, putting back


反义词：uninstalling, removing


词性:动词 (现在分词)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reinstalling the software. = 他正在重新安装软件。
They are reinstalling the application. = 他们正在重新安装应用程序。

一般过去时:
She reinstalled the game last night. = 她昨晚重新安装了游戏。
We reinstalled the driver after updating it. = 我们更新驱动程序后重新安装了它。

现在完成时:
I have reinstalled the program three times. = 我已经重新安装了这个程序三次。
They have reinstalled the operating system successfully. = 他们已经成功地重新安装了操作系统。

过去完成时:
He had reinstalled the software before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经重新安装了软件。
She had reinstalled the drivers before attempting to print. = 她尝试打印之前已经重新安装了驱动程序。

将来时:
I will reinstall the application tomorrow. = 我明天将重新安装该应用程序。
They will reinstall the software after the backup is complete. = 备份完成后，他们将重新安装软件。

虚拟语气:
If I had a backup, I would reinstall the system. = 如果我有备份，我会重新安装系统。
It would be better if you reinstalled the drivers. = 如果你重新安装驱动程序会更好。

被动语态:
The software is being reinstalled by the technician. = 该软件正在由技术人员重新安装。
The operating system was reinstalled after the hard drive failure. = 硬盘故障后，操作系统被重新安装了。

疑问句:

否定句:
