compatibleness:/kəmˌpætəˈbɪlnəs/ | n. |兼容性，相容性 |The degree to which two or more things are able to exist or function together effectively.  It refers to the ability of things to work well together without conflict or difficulty. |例句：The compatibleness of the software and hardware is crucial for optimal performance.  软件和硬件的兼容性对于最佳性能至关重要。

The compatibleness of their personalities made them a great team.  他们性格上的相容性使他们成为一个伟大的团队。


近义词：compatibility, congruity, accord, harmony |反义词：incompatibility, discord, conflict
