self-satisfied: /ˌselfˈsætɪsfaɪd/ | adj. | 自满的，沾沾自喜的 |  Feeling or showing excessive pride in oneself or one's achievements.  It suggests a complacency that prevents further improvement or growth. |


例句：

He became self-satisfied after his promotion and stopped working hard. 他升职后变得自满，不再努力工作了。

Her self-satisfied smile irritated everyone in the room. 她自鸣得意的笑容激怒了房间里的每个人。


近义词：complacent, smug, conceited, self-important, arrogant

反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, self-critical


词性：形容词
