semifloret:/ˈsemiˌflɔːrɛt/| n. |小花，半花 |A small individual flower, especially one of the florets composing a composite flower head.  A semifloret is a small, individual flower that is part of a larger flower head, particularly in plants of the Asteraceae family (e.g., sunflowers, daisies). It often lacks a fully developed calyx. |Example: The sunflower head is composed of numerous tiny semiflorets.  向日葵花头由许多细小的花组成。|近义词：floret, floweret |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite of a large, solitary flower.
