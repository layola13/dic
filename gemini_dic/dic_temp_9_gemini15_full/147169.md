overloyalty: /ˌoʊvərˈlɔɪəlti/ | n. |过度忠诚；盲目忠诚 |Excessive or uncritical loyalty.  It implies a loyalty that is misplaced, potentially harmful, or detrimental to one's own interests or well-being.  It often involves sacrificing one's own judgment or needs for the sake of another person or cause, even when it's unreasonable or harmful. |

例句：His overloyalty to the company led him to accept unreasonable demands. 他的过度忠诚导致他接受了不合理的要求。

例句：Blind overloyalty can prevent people from seeing the flaws in their leaders or organizations.  盲目的过度忠诚会阻止人们看到领导或组织的缺陷。


近义词：blind loyalty, unquestioning loyalty, excessive devotion, fanaticism

反义词：disloyalty, betrayal, skepticism, critical thinking


词性: 名词
