expensed:/ɪkˈspɛnst/ | v. | 作为支出记入账簿；报销 | To record an expense in accounting records.  This usually involves deducting the cost from revenue or allocating it to a specific budget. | Example:  The company expensed the travel costs. = 公司将差旅费用记入账簿。 The employee expensed their lunch. = 员工报销了午餐费用。 |近义词：charged, debited, booked | 反义词：credited, reimbursed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company expenses all travel costs. = 公司将所有差旅费用记入账簿。
They expense their marketing materials regularly. = 他们定期将营销材料记入账簿。


一般过去时:
She expensed the conference registration fee. = 她报销了会议注册费。
We expensed the new software purchase. = 我们报销了新软件的购买费用。


现在完成时:
He has expensed all his receipts this month. = 他本月已将所有收据报销。
The department has expensed a significant amount on training. = 该部门在培训方面已支出大量资金。


过去完成时:
They had expensed the project costs before the audit. = 审计之前，他们已将项目成本记入账簿。
The accountant had expensed all the invoices by the end of the quarter. = 会计在季度末已将所有发票记入账簿。


将来时:
We will expense the hotel bills upon our return. = 我们回来后将报销酒店账单。
She will expense the airfare next week. = 她下周将报销机票费用。


虚拟语气:
If we expensed this now, we would have a clearer budget. = 如果我们现在就记入这笔账，我们的预算就会更清晰。
I wish we had expensed the repairs earlier. = 我希望我们早点报销维修费用。


被动语态:
The client's expenses were expensed to the project. = 客户的费用已记入项目账目。
All travel expenses are expensed to the appropriate department. = 所有差旅费用都记入相应的部门账目。


疑问句:
Have you expensed the stationery yet? = 你报销文具了吗？
Were the consulting fees expensed correctly? = 咨询费记账正确了吗？


否定句:
They did not expense the entertainment costs. = 他们没有报销娱乐费用。
The company doesn't expense personal phone calls. = 公司不报销私人电话费用。
