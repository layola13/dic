frying:/ˈfraɪɪŋ/| v.|油煎，油炸|The act of cooking food in hot oil or fat.  It involves submerging or partially submerging food in hot oil until it is cooked through and often browned.|例句：I'm frying some potatoes for dinner. 我正在煎一些土豆当晚餐。

例句：He fried the eggs until they were crispy. 他把鸡蛋煎到酥脆。


近义词：sautéing, pan-frying, deep-frying|反义词：boiling, steaming, baking|动词
