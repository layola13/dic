phenologist:/fɪˈnɒlədʒɪst/| n. |物候学家|A phenologist is a scientist who studies phenology, the timing of natural events in plant and animal life cycles, such as the first flowering of a plant or the first appearance of a particular insect.  They observe and record these events to understand how climate change and other environmental factors affect the timing of these natural occurrences. |The phenologist meticulously recorded the date of the first bloom of each species. 物候学家一丝不苟地记录了每种植物的第一次开花日期。|ecologist, naturalist| (无明确反义词)|名词
