filipinos:/ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnoʊz/| n. |菲律宾人（复数）|The term "Filipinos" refers to the citizens of the Philippines, an archipelago in Southeast Asia.  It encompasses a diverse population with various ethnic backgrounds and languages, united by a shared history and culture.|例句：Many Filipinos migrated to the United States in search of better opportunities. 许多菲律宾人移居美国寻找更好的机会。
There are millions of Filipinos working abroad.  数百万菲律宾人在国外工作。|近义词：Philippine nationals, Filipinos (singular: Filipino)|反义词：无
