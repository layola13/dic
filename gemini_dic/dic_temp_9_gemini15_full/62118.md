enchiladas:/ˌɛntʃɪˈlɑːdəz/ | n. |墨西哥卷饼|Mexican food consisting of corn tortillas filled with meat, cheese, or other ingredients, covered in sauce, and baked.  Enchiladas are a popular dish in Mexican cuisine, known for their savory flavors and versatility. They can be made with various fillings and sauces, offering a wide range of taste experiences.|例句：For dinner tonight, we're having enchiladas. 今晚的晚餐是墨西哥卷饼。

例句：My favorite restaurant makes amazing chicken enchiladas. 我最喜欢的餐厅做出的鸡肉墨西哥卷饼非常棒。


近义词：burritos, tacos (depending on the filling and preparation)|反义词：无|词性：名词
