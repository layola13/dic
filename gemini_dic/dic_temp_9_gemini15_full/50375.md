deportation:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. |驱逐出境，递解出境 |The act of deporting a foreigner from a country.  The process of being officially forced to leave a country and return to one's home country. |例句：The deportation of illegal immigrants is a controversial issue.  非法移民的驱逐出境是一个有争议的问题。

例句：His deportation was a result of his criminal activities. 他的驱逐出境是由于他的犯罪活动造成的。


近义词：expulsion, removal, extradition | 反义词：immigration, admission

Deportation happens frequently in many countries. = 许多国家经常发生驱逐出境事件。
The government deports illegal immigrants regularly. = 政府定期驱逐非法移民。


一般过去时:

They were deported back to their home country. = 他们被遣返回自己的祖国。


现在完成时:

The government has recently stepped up deportations. = 政府最近加大了驱逐出境的力度。


过去完成时:
He had already been deported before the trial. = 在审判之前，他已经遭到驱逐出境了。
The refugees had been deported several times before finding asylum. = 难民在找到庇护之前已经被驱逐出境好几次了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't committed that crime, he wouldn't have faced deportation. = 如果他没有犯下那项罪行，他就不会面临驱逐出境。

被动语态:
Many people are deported each year. = 每年都有许多人被驱逐出境。
The decision to deport him was heavily criticized. = 驱逐他的决定受到了强烈批评。


疑问句:
What are the grounds for deportation? = 驱逐出境的理由是什么？

否定句:

词源:
From "de-" (away from) + "port" (carry) + "-ation" (the act of).  The word ultimately derives from the Latin verb *portare* ("to carry").  The prefix "de-" indicates removal or separation.
