paint-by-numbers: /ˈpeɪnt baɪ ˈnʌmbərz/ | n. | 数字绘画，按图索骥 | A painting method where numbered areas on a canvas correspond to numbered paints, guiding the painter to create a picture.  It is a simplified form of painting intended for beginners or those seeking a relaxing creative activity. | Example: My daughter enjoys paint-by-numbers kits as a relaxing hobby.  我的女儿喜欢数字绘画套装作为一种轻松的爱好。|近义词: numbered painting, pre-printed canvas painting |反义词: freehand painting, abstract painting | 名词
