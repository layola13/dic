cliffsides:/ˈklɪfsʌɪdz/| n.|悬崖边，悬崖峭壁|The sides of a cliff; steep, rocky slopes.  Cliffsides often feature dramatic scenery and unique ecosystems.|例句：The climbers carefully navigated the treacherous cliffsides.攀登者小心地绕过危险的悬崖峭壁。

例句：The wildflowers bloomed in vibrant colors along the cliffsides.野花在悬崖边上五颜六色地盛开。

近义词：cliff edges, precipices, escarpments|反义词：plains, lowlands
