defragmenter:/ˌdiːˈfræɡmɛntər/ | n. | 磁盘碎片整理程序 | A computer program that reorganizes files on a hard drive to improve performance.  It works by moving fragmented files into contiguous blocks of space, reducing the time it takes the computer to access them. | Example:  I ran the defragmenter last night to speed up my computer.  昨晚我运行了磁盘碎片整理程序以加快电脑速度。|近义词: disk optimizer, file organizer | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，但可以理解为“碎片化”的相反)
