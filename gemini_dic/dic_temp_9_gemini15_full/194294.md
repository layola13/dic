self-oriented:/ˈsɛlf ˈɔːriɛntɪd/| adj. |以自我为中心的；自我导向的 |Self-oriented individuals prioritize their own needs and desires above others'.  They may be less considerate of others' feelings and perspectives. |例句：He's become increasingly self-oriented since his promotion.  他升职后变得越来越以自我为中心了。

He adopted a self-oriented approach to problem-solving, neglecting the team's input. 他采取了一种以自我为中心的解决问题的方法，忽略了团队的意见。|近义词：egoistic, egocentric, narcissistic, self-centered |反义词：altruistic, selfless, considerate, other-oriented


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is self-oriented in his decision-making. = 他在做决定时是以自我为中心的。
They are self-oriented and rarely consider others' viewpoints. = 他们以自我为中心，很少考虑别人的观点。


一般过去时:
She was self-oriented in her youth, but matured later. = 她年轻时很以自我为中心，但后来成熟了。
He was self-oriented during the negotiations, leading to a deadlock. = 他在谈判中以自我为中心，导致了僵局。


现在完成时:
He has always been self-oriented, prioritizing personal gain above all else. = 他一直以来都以自我为中心，把个人利益放在首位。
She has become more self-oriented since the accident. = 自从那次事故后，她变得更加以自我为中心了。


过去完成时:
He had been self-oriented throughout his career, rarely seeking collaboration. = 他在整个职业生涯中都以自我为中心，很少寻求合作。
They had already been self-oriented before the project started, hindering team cohesion. = 项目开始前他们就已经很以自我为中心了，阻碍了团队的凝聚力。


将来时:

They will probably continue to be self-oriented in their approach to the task. = 他们可能会继续以自我为中心的方式来处理这项任务。


虚拟语气:

I wish she weren't so self-oriented; it makes collaboration difficult. = 我希望她不要那么以自我为中心；这使得合作变得困难。


被动语态:
