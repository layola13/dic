lanugo:/ləˈnuːɡoʊ/|/lənˈuːɡoʊ/| n. |绒毛；胎毛|Fine, soft hair covering the body of a fetus and sometimes a newborn infant.  It is usually shed before or shortly after birth.|Example: The newborn baby was covered in a fine layer of lanugo.  这个新生儿身上覆盖着一层细细的绒毛。|近义词：down, fuzz|反义词：hair (coarse hair)
