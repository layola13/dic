reallocates:/ˌriːˈæləkeɪts/ | vt. |重新分配，再分配 |To allocate (something) again or anew.  This usually refers to memory allocation in computer science, but can apply to other resources as well. |例句：The operating system reallocates memory as needed. 操作系统根据需要重新分配内存。

例句：The company reallocated its budget to prioritize research and development. 公司重新分配了预算，优先考虑研发。


近义词：redistribute, reallot | 反义词：concentrate, hoard | 词性：动词
