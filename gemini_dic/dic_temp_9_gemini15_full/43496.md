crating:/ˈkreɪtɪŋ/| n. |装箱；制作箱子；箱子|The act of packing something into a crate or the process of making crates.  It can also refer to the crates themselves, especially when used collectively.|例句：The crating of the fragile sculptures required extreme care.  装运易碎雕塑需要格外小心。 The crating process was slow and laborious. 制作箱子的过程缓慢而费力。|近义词：boxing, packing, packaging|反义词：unpacking, unpacking


USAGE->

They are crating the furniture for shipment. = 他们正在打包家具准备运输。
He crates apples for market every week. = 他每周都把苹果装箱销往市场。
