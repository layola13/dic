flaxseeds:/ˈflæksˌsiːdz/| n. |亚麻籽|The seeds of the flax plant, rich in fiber and omega-3 fatty acids, often consumed for their health benefits.  They are also known as linseeds.|例句：I add flaxseeds to my yogurt every morning for extra fiber.  = 我每天早上在酸奶里加亚麻籽来增加纤维。

例句：The recipe calls for ground flaxseeds to help bind the ingredients. = 这个食谱需要磨碎的亚麻籽来帮助粘合配料。
