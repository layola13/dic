remonstrances:/rɪˈmɒnstrənsɪz/| n.|抗议；反对；规劝|The act of remonstrating; a protest or complaint.  It often involves earnest and serious objections.|例句：Despite the public's remonstrances, the government proceeded with the unpopular policy. 尽管公众提出了抗议，政府还是继续推行不受欢迎的政策。|例句：Her remonstrances against his reckless driving fell on deaf ears. 她对他鲁莽驾驶的规劝石沉大海。|近义词：protests, objections, complaints, reprimands|反义词：acquiescence, approval, consent|名词
