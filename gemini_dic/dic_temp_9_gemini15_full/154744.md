persuading:/pərˈsweɪdɪŋ/| v. |劝说；说服 |The act of influencing someone to do or believe something.  It implies using reason, logic, or emotional appeals to change their mind or actions. |例句：He spent hours persuading his father to let him buy a car. 他花了几个小时劝说父亲让他买一辆车。

例句：The salesperson was very persuasive, and I ended up buying the product.  销售员非常有说服力，我最终买了那个产品。


近义词：convincing, influencing, coaxing, inducing

反义词：dissuading, deterring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is persuading his friend to join the team. 他正在劝说他的朋友加入团队。
She persuades her clients to invest in her business. 她劝说她的客户投资她的生意。


一般过去时:
They persuaded the manager to change his decision. 他们说服经理改变了他的决定。
He persuaded me to go to the party. 他说服我去参加派对。


现在完成时:
I have persuaded him to reconsider his offer. 我已经说服他重新考虑他的提议。
She has persuaded many people to donate to charity. 她已经说服很多人捐款给慈善机构。


过去完成时:
They had persuaded the committee to approve the project before the meeting.  他们在会议前已经说服委员会批准了该项目。
He had persuaded her to leave before the storm arrived.  暴风雨来临之前，他已经说服她离开了。


将来时:
I will persuade him to help us. 我将说服他帮助我们。
She will persuade the board to invest in new technology. 她将说服董事会投资新技术。


虚拟语气:
If I could persuade him, I would. 如果我能说服他，我会的。
I wish I could persuade her to change her mind. 我希望我能说服她改变主意。


被动语态:
He was persuaded to change his plans. 他被说服改变了他的计划。
The audience was persuaded by her speech. 观众被她的演讲说服了。


疑问句:
Can you persuade him to come? 你能说服他来吗？
Will she be persuaded to cooperate? 她会被说服合作吗？


否定句:
I couldn't persuade him to attend the meeting. 我无法说服他参加会议。
She wasn't persuaded by his arguments. 她没有被他的论点说服。


PHRASE->
persuade someone to do something: 说服某人做某事
persuade someone of something: 使某人相信某事
persuade someone into doing something: 诱导某人做某事
persuade someone out of doing something:  劝说某人不要做某事
be persuaded by something: 被某事说服


词源:
源自古法语 *persuader，来自拉丁语 persuadere，意为“劝说，说服”。  persuadere 由 per- (完全地) + suadere (劝说)构成。
