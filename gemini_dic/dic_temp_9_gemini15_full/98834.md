intelligential:/ˌɪntɛləˈdʒɛntʃəl/| adj.|智力的，才智的| Relating to or characteristic of intelligence; intellectual.  The word is rarely used and considered somewhat awkward or overly formal.  It's better to use more common synonyms.|The intelligential capacity of the human brain is remarkable. 人类大脑的智力能力非凡。|近义词：intellectual, mental, cognitive|反义词：unintelligent, stupid, dull
