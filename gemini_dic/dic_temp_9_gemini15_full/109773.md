levitated:/ˈlɛvɪteɪtɪd/ | v. |使漂浮；使悬浮 | To cause (something) to rise and float in the air, especially by supernatural means.  |例句：The magician levitated the assistant on stage. 魔术师让助手在舞台上漂浮起来。

例句：The yoga instructor claims she can levitate small objects. 瑜伽教练声称她可以使小物體漂浮。


近义词：float, hover, suspend | 反义词：ground, drop
