regalia:/rɪˈɡeɪliə/| n.|皇家徽章；盛装；装饰品|The word "regalia" refers to the emblems or insignia of royalty, or any splendid or impressive array of objects. It can also refer to ceremonial clothing or decorations.|例句：The king's regalia was on full display during the coronation. 国王的皇家徽章在加冕典礼上充分展示。The band's regalia was dazzling.乐队的盛装令人眼花缭乱。|近义词：insignia, emblems, finery, ornaments|反义词：plainness, simplicity
