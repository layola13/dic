reviv:/rɪˈvaɪv/| vt.|使复苏；使复活；使恢复生机|To restore to life or consciousness; to bring back to a former condition; to renew or refresh.|The rain revived the wilting plants. 雨水使枯萎的植物恢复了生机。  The doctor revived the unconscious patient. 医生使昏迷的病人苏醒过来。|近义词：reanimate, revitalize, resurrect|反义词：kill, destroy, extinguish|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revives the old traditions. = 他使古老的传统复兴。
The medicine revives his spirits. = 这药使他精神振作。

一般过去时:
The rain revived the dying flowers. = 雨水使快枯萎的花儿恢复了生机。
She revived after a long illness. = 她大病一场后恢复了健康。

现在完成时:
The company has revived its fortunes. = 这家公司已经恢复了元气。
They have revived the old custom. = 他们恢复了旧习俗。

过去完成时:
He had revived the company before he retired. = 他退休前使公司恢复了元气。
She had revived her spirits before the meeting. = 会议前她已恢复了精神。

将来时:
The government will revive the economy. = 政府将使经济复苏。
This treatment will revive him. = 这种治疗会使他恢复健康。

虚拟语气:
