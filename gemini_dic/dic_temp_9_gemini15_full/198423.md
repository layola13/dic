shred:/ʃred/ | n. v. |碎片；撕碎；大量减少 | A shred is a small piece of something that has been torn or cut off.  To shred is to tear or cut something into small pieces. |例句：The old letter was torn into shreds. 这封旧信被撕成了碎片。 He shredded the documents before throwing them away. 他在扔掉文件之前把它们撕碎了。 |近义词：fragment, scrap, tear, rip |反义词：whole, intact


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shreds the vegetables for the salad. = 他把蔬菜切碎做沙拉。
The machine shreds paper efficiently. = 这台机器高效地碎纸。

一般过去时:
She shredded the documents yesterday. = 她昨天撕碎了文件。

The wind has shredded the leaves from the trees. = 风把树叶吹得粉碎了。

过去完成时:
