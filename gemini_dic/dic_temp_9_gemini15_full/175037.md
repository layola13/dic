radioenzymatic:/ˌreɪdioʊɛnzaɪˈmætɪk/| adj. |放射酶的；用放射性同位素标记的酶进行的| Relating to or using radioenzymatic assays, a type of assay that uses radioactive isotopes to measure the activity of enzymes.  This technique involves labeling a substrate with a radioactive isotope and then measuring the amount of radioactive product formed by the enzyme. | The radioenzymatic assay was used to measure the concentration of dopamine in the brain tissue.  放射酶测定法被用来测量脑组织中多巴胺的浓度。|  暂无近义词 | 暂无反义词 | adjective
