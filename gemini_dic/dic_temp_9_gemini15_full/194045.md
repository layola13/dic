self-glorious:/ˌselfˈɡlɔːriəs/ | adj. | 自负的；虚荣的 | Excessively proud of oneself; vain.  It implies an excessive and often unwarranted pride in one's achievements or qualities. |例句：His self-glorious boasting alienated his friends.  他的自吹自擂使朋友们疏远了他。

例句：She delivered a self-glorious speech about her accomplishments. 她发表了一篇关于她成就的自吹自擂的演讲。


近义词：vain, egotistical, narcissistic, conceited, boastful, self-important

反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, self-effacing


词源:  The word is a combination of "self" and "glorious."  "Glorious" comes from the Old French "glorieux," ultimately from the Latin "gloriosus," meaning "full of glory." The prefix "self-" emphasizes that the glory is attributed to oneself.
