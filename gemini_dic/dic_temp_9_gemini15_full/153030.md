peel:/piːl/ |/pɪl/| n./vt.|剥皮；果皮；外皮|To remove the skin or outer covering of something, especially fruit or vegetables.  Also refers to the skin or outer covering itself.|例句：I peeled the orange before eating it.  我吃橘子之前剥了皮。

例句：The peel of the banana was brown and spotted. 香蕉皮又棕又斑点。


近义词：pare, skin, shuck| 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to add a covering. | 词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peels potatoes every night. 他每晚都削土豆皮。
She peels an apple with a knife. 她用刀削苹果皮。


一般过去时:
I peeled the garlic yesterday. 我昨天剥了蒜。
They peeled the oranges for the party. 他们为聚会剥了橘子。


现在完成时:
I have peeled all the carrots. 我已经把所有胡萝卜都削皮了。
She has peeled the potatoes for the soup. 她已经为做汤削好了土豆皮。


过去完成时:
He had peeled the onions before I arrived. 我到达之前他已经剥好了洋葱。
They had peeled the apples before making the pie.  他们在做馅饼之前已经削好了苹果皮。


将来时:
I will peel the bananas tomorrow. 我明天会削香蕉皮。
She will peel the potatoes for the stew. 她会为炖菜削土豆皮。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would peel all the vegetables. 如果我有更多的时间，我会把所有蔬菜都削皮。
I wish I had peeled the potatoes earlier. 我真希望我早点把土豆削皮。


被动语态:
The potatoes were peeled by the chef. 土豆是由厨师削皮的。
The apples are being peeled for the dessert. 苹果正在被削皮，准备做甜点。


疑问句:
Have you peeled the potatoes yet? 你削土豆皮了吗？
Did he peel the oranges for juice? 他削橘子做果汁了吗？


否定句:
I didn't peel the onions. 我没有剥洋葱。
She hasn't peeled the potatoes yet. 她还没削土豆皮。


PHRASE->
peel off: 脱落；剥落
peel away: 剥离；逐渐消失
peel back: 揭开；揭露
sun-peel: 晒伤脱皮
peel and stick:  撕开粘贴


词源:
The word "peel" comes from the Old English word "pylan," meaning "to skin" or "to flay."  This ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic roots related to ideas of stripping or uncovering.
