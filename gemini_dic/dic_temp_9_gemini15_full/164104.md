predismissal: /ˌpriːdɪˈsmɪsəl/ | adj. | 解雇前的；解雇之前的 | Relating to the period before dismissal from employment.  It often describes events, actions, or circumstances that occur before someone is fired or let go from their job. |  例句：Predismissal warnings were given to several employees.  =好几名员工收到了解雇前的警告。


例句：The company conducted a predismissal performance review. = 公司进行了解雇前的业绩评估。


近义词：pre-termination, prior to dismissal


反义词：post-dismissal


词性：形容词
