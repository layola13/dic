midst:/mɪdst/| n. |中间，当中 |The word "midst" means the middle of something, especially a group of people or things, or a period of time.  It suggests being surrounded or enveloped. |例句：He found himself in the midst of a raging storm. 他发现自己身处一场猛烈的暴风雨之中。


例句：In the midst of all the chaos, she remained calm. 在一片混乱之中，她保持冷静。


近义词：middle, center, among, amongst


反义词：outside, periphery, edge


词源: 古英语 *midd,  "middle" +  -st (suffix indicating location)
