coptic:/ˈkɒptɪk/| adj. n.|科普特人的；科普特语的；科普特教的|Relating to the Copts, their language, or their church.  The word refers to the Christian community in Egypt and their language and culture which trace their origins back to the early Christians in Egypt.|例句：Coptic art is known for its unique style.  科普特艺术以其独特的风格而闻名。The museum houses a significant collection of Coptic artifacts. 这个博物馆收藏了大量的科普特文物。|近义词：Egyptian (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but terms referring to other religions or cultures could be considered contrastive.
