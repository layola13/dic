sautéed:/soʊˈteɪd/| adj.|  煎炒的；爆炒的 |  Cooked quickly in a small amount of fat over relatively high heat.  This cooking method typically involves tossing the food frequently to ensure even browning and cooking. | 例句：The sautéed mushrooms added a delicious earthy flavor to the pasta.  煎炒的蘑菇为意大利面增添了美味的泥土气息。
The sautéed vegetables were perfectly crisp-tender. 爆炒的蔬菜恰到好处地脆嫩。

近义词：stir-fried, pan-fried, fried

反义词：boiled, steamed, baked
