manning:/ˈmænɪŋ/| v.|人员配备，担任…职务，操纵|The act of providing personnel for a task or position; the process of operating or controlling something, especially a ship or weapon.|例句：The captain is manning the ship. 船长正在掌舵。  The factory is currently manning three shifts. 工厂目前安排了三个班次的人员。|近义词：staffing, crewing, operating|反义词：unmanned, unattended

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are manning the phones all day. = 他们整天都在接电话。
He mans the reception desk every morning. = 他每天早上都在前台值班。

一般过去时:
The crew manned the lifeboats. = 船员们操作救生艇。
She manned the cash register for ten years. = 她当了十年收银员。

现在完成时:
They have manned the border post for months. = 他们已经驻守边境哨所好几个月了。
We have manned the project since its inception. = 自从项目启动以来，我们一直负责这个项目。


过去完成时:
They had manned the defenses before the enemy arrived. = 敌人到达之前，他们已经做好防御准备了。
The firefighters had manned the hoses for hours before the fire was contained. = 消防员们在控制住火势之前已经操作水龙带好几个小时了。


将来时:
They will man the new factory next week. = 他们下周将负责新工厂的运营。
We will man the help desk during the conference. = 我们将在会议期间负责帮助台。


虚拟语气:
If we had manned the station properly, the incident would not have happened. = 如果我们当时正确地安排了人员值守，就不会发生事故了。
If they manned the lifeboats more quickly, more people could have been saved. = 如果他们更快地操作救生艇，就能挽救更多的人。


被动语态:
The ship was manned by a crew of twenty. = 这艘船由二十名船员操作。
The project is being manned by a team of experienced engineers. = 该项目由一支经验丰富的工程师团队负责。


疑问句:
Is the factory manned 24/7? = 工厂是否全天候有人值守？
Who manned the controls during the emergency? = 紧急情况下是谁在操作控制台？


否定句:
The office wasn't manned during the lunch break. = 午休时间办公室无人值守。
They didn't man the checkpoints effectively. = 他们没有有效地控制检查站。
