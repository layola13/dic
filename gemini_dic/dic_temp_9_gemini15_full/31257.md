chaotic:/keɪˈɒtɪk/ | /ˈkeɪɑːtɪk/ | adj. |混乱的，无序的 |Characterized by a lack of order or predictability; wildly disordered.  The situation is chaotic and unpredictable.  The aftermath of the storm left the city in a chaotic state. |近义词：disordered, turbulent, tumultuous, anarchic |反义词：orderly, organized, calm, peaceful
