funders:/ˈfʌndərz/| n. |资助者，提供资金者 |People or organizations that provide financial support for a project, event, or institution.  |例句：The university relies heavily on its private funders for research grants.  大学在很大程度上依靠其私人资助者提供研究经费。
|例句：Several funders have committed to supporting the new community center.  几位资助者已承诺支持新的社区中心。|近义词：sponsors, benefactors, patrons, investors |反义词：recipients, beneficiaries


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The funders provide annual grants. = 资助者提供年度拨款。
These funders support innovative projects. = 这些资助者支持创新项目。

一般过去时:
The funders approved the budget last week. = 资助者上周批准了预算。
Several funders withdrew their support. = 几位资助者撤回了他们的支持。

现在完成时:
The funders have already committed significant funds. = 资助者已经承诺了大量资金。
The organization has secured funding from various funders. = 该组织已获得来自各个资助者的资金。

过去完成时:
Before the project started, the funders had reviewed the proposal. = 在项目开始之前，资助者已经审查了提案。
The team had already contacted potential funders by the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，团队已经联系了潜在的资助者。

将来时:

We expect the funders will increase their contributions. = 我们预计资助者将增加他们的捐款。

虚拟语气:
If the funders were more generous, the project would succeed. = 如果资助者更慷慨一些，项目就会成功。
I wish the funders had provided more detailed feedback. = 我希望资助者能提供更详细的反馈。

被动语态:

The grant application is currently being reviewed by the funders. = 拨款申请目前正在由资助者审查。

疑问句:
