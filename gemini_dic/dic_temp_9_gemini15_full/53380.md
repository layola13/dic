disagreeableness:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəbəlnəs/ | n. | 不愉快；令人不快；不和谐 | The unpleasant or offensive quality of something or someone; a lack of agreeableness.  It refers to the tendency to be uncooperative, critical, and antagonistic. |  The disagreeableness of the weather forced us to postpone the picnic. 天气不好，我们不得不推迟野餐。  His constant disagreeableness made him unpopular with his colleagues. 他总是令人不快，同事们都不喜欢他。| agreeableness, unpleasantness, antagonism | agreeableness, pleasantness, harmony
