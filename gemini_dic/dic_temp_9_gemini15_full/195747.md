semitones:/ˈsɛmɪˌtoʊnz/| n. |半音|A semitone is the smallest interval used in Western music.  It is the distance between two adjacent notes on a piano keyboard.  Semitones are also known as half steps.  The term is often used in discussions of musical scales, intervals, and intonation.|The song modulates up a semitone at the bridge.这首歌在桥段提升了一个半音。|The guitarist bent the string a semitone to create a bluesy effect. 吉他手弯弦一个半音以创造布鲁斯效果。|近义词：half step|反义词：whole tone, whole step
