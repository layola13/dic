similitudes:/sɪmɪˈlɪtjuːdz/| n.|相似之处，类似点|Similarities or points of resemblance.  The essay explored the similitudes between the two literary works.|近义词：similarities, resemblances, analogies, parallels|反义词：differences, dissimilarities, contrasts|名词


例句：

The judge noted the similitudes between the two cases. = 法官注意到这两个案件的相似之处。

There are striking similitudes in their writing styles. = 他们的写作风格有惊人的相似之处。
