dude:/duːd/| n.|家伙，哥们儿|A man, typically a young man, informal term of address.|例句：Hey dude, what's up?  嘿，哥们儿，怎么了？|例句：That dude is really cool.  那个家伙真的很酷。|近义词：guy, fellow, man, pal|反义词：N/A
