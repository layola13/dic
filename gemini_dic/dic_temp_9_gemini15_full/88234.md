hindoo:/ˈhɪnduː/| n. |印度人；印度教徒|A person of Indian origin, especially a follower of Hinduism.  The term is now considered outdated and potentially offensive.  It was commonly used in the past, but its colonial and sometimes derogatory connotations make it inappropriate in modern usage.|例句：The old book referred to the people of India as "Hindoos".  = 那本旧书称印度人为“印度人”。

例句：Using the term "hindoo" is considered offensive by many. = 许多人认为使用“hindoo”一词具有冒犯性。


近义词：Indian, Hindu (Hindu is preferred and more accurate for religious affiliation) |反义词：None specifically, but terms like "Westerner" or other regional identifiers offer contrast. |词性：名词
