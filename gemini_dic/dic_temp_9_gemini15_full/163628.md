predator:/ˈpredətər/| n. |掠食者，捕食者 | An animal that naturally preys on others.  A predator hunts, kills, and eats other animals for food.  They are often at the top of the food chain. | Example: Lions are apex predators in the African savanna.  狮子是非洲大草原上的顶级掠食者。

The shark is a fearsome predator of the ocean depths.  鲨鱼是海洋深处可怕的掠食者。


近义词：hunter, carnivore, beast of prey
反义词：prey
