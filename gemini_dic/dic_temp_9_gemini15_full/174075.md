quasisovereign:/ˈkwɑːziˌsɒvrɪn/ | adj. | 准主权的，似主权的 |  Having or exercising some but not all the powers of a sovereign state; possessing limited sovereignty.  This term often describes entities that have significant autonomy but are still subject to the ultimate authority of a larger governing body. | Example: The autonomous region enjoys a quasisovereign status, managing its internal affairs but remaining under the umbrella of the federal government.  = 该自治区享有准主权地位，管理其内部事务，但仍处于联邦政府的管辖之下。 | 近义词：semi-sovereign, self-governing, autonomous | 反义词：completely sovereign, subordinate, dependent
