regeneration's: /ˌriːdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |再生；复兴；更新；重建 |The process of regenerating or being regenerated.  It's the possessive form of "regeneration".| 例句：The city's regeneration project aims to revitalize the downtown area.  城市的重建项目旨在振兴市中心地区。

The town's regeneration is a long-term process requiring significant investment.  这个小镇的复兴是一个需要大量投资的长期过程。


近义词：renewal, revival, rebirth, reformation | 反义词：destruction, decay, decline | 词性：名词 (possessive form)
