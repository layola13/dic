interdental: /ˌɪntərˈdɛntl/ | adj. | 牙齿间的；介于牙齿之间的 | Relating to or situated between the teeth.  | Example:  Interdental brushing is crucial for preventing gum disease.  (牙缝刷对于预防牙龈疾病至关重要。)

The word "interdental" is formed from the prefix "inter-" meaning "between" and the word "dental" relating to teeth.


近义词：interproximal


反义词：None directly opposite, but words implying "not between the teeth" could be used contextually.  e.g.,  lingual (tongue-side), buccal (cheek-side)


词性：形容词
