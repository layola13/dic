chastened:/ˈtʃeɪs(ə)nd/| adj.| 惩戒的；悔过的；谦卑的 | humbled and subdued, especially as a result of punishment or humiliation.  |例句：After his public rebuke, he felt chastened and ashamed.  他的公开斥责之后，他感到羞愧和悔恨。|例句：The chastened tone of his letter suggested remorse. 他信中悔恨的语气表明了他悔过之意。|近义词： humbled, subdued, repentant, contrite |反义词： arrogant, proud, boastful, unrepentant
