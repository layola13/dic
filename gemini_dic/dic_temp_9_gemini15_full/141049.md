onlookers:/ˈɒnˌlʊkərz/ | n. |旁观者，围观者 | People who watch something without getting involved.  They are passive observers of an event. |例句：The onlookers watched in horror as the building collapsed. 旁观者们惊恐地看着大楼倒塌。

例句：Many onlookers gathered to watch the street performer.许多旁观者聚集在一起观看街头表演者。


近义词：spectators, observers, witnesses | 反义词：participants, actors
