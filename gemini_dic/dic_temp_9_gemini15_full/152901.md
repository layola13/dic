peddling:/ˈped(ə)lɪŋ/| v.|兜售，叫卖，推销|The act of selling goods, especially in a small-scale or informal way;  to promote or disseminate (an idea or information) persistently or forcefully.|例句：He was peddling his wares on the street corner. 他在街角兜售他的商品。

例句：They're peddling a dangerous ideology. 他们在兜售一种危险的意识形态。


近义词：hawking, vending, trafficking, promoting, pushing


反义词：buying, purchasing


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peddles his paintings at the weekend market. = 他在周末市场兜售他的画作。
She peddles false information online. = 她在网上散布虚假信息。


一般过去时:
The salesman peddled his products door-to-door. = 销售员挨家挨户推销他的产品。
They peddled their ideas to the committee. = 他们向委员会推销他们的想法。


现在完成时:
He has been peddling his wares for years. = 他兜售他的商品已经好多年了。
She has successfully peddled her book to several publishers. = 她已成功地将她的书推销给了几家出版社。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal, he had been peddling his influence for years. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他多年来一直在滥用他的影响力。
They had peddled false promises to the voters. = 他们曾向选民许下虚假的承诺。


将来时:
He will be peddling his crafts at the upcoming fair. = 他将在即将到来的集市上兜售他的工艺品。
She will continue peddling her conspiracy theories. = 她将继续散播她的阴谋论。


虚拟语气:
If he peddled his wares more aggressively, he would make more money. = 如果他更积极地兜售他的商品，他就能赚更多钱。
If she hadn't peddled false information, the situation wouldn't be so dire. = 如果她没有散布虚假信息，情况就不会这么糟糕。


被动语态:
His products are peddled across the country. = 他的产品在全国各地销售。
The false rumors were peddled by the media. = 这些谣言是被媒体散布的。


疑问句:
What is he peddling today? = 他今天在卖什么？
Are they still peddling their outdated ideas? = 他们还在兜售他们过时的想法吗？


否定句:
He doesn't peddle illegal drugs. = 他不贩卖毒品。
She wouldn't peddle her influence for personal gain. = 她不会为了个人利益而滥用她的影响力。


PHRASE->
peddle influence: 滥用影响力
peddle rumors: 散布谣言
peddle drugs: 贩卖毒品
peddle lies: 散布谎言
peddle one's wares: 兜售商品


词源:
The word "peddle" ultimately comes from the Old French word "pedaler," meaning "to travel on foot,"  suggesting the act of a traveling merchant going from place to place selling goods.  The sense shifted to the act of selling itself over time.
