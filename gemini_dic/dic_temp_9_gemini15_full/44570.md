crouched:/kraʊtʃt/| v.|蹲下，蜷缩|To lower one's body close to the ground by bending the legs.  It implies a posture of concealment, readiness, or humility.|例句：The cat crouched low to the ground, ready to pounce. 猫低低地蹲在地上，准备猛扑。


例句：She crouched behind the bush, trying to hide from the rain. 她蹲在灌木丛后面，试图躲雨。


近义词：squat, stoop, bend, hunker


反义词：stand, rise, straighten


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crouches behind the wall. 他蹲在墙后。
She often crouches to examine small plants. 她经常蹲下检查小植物。

一般过去时:
He crouched behind the tree to avoid being seen. 他躲在树后蹲着，以免被看见。
The thief crouched low to the ground. 小偷低着头蹲在地上。

现在完成时:
The dog has crouched by the fireplace all afternoon. 这只狗整个下午都蹲在壁炉旁。
She has crouched down to tie her shoelaces. 她蹲下系鞋带。

过去完成时:
He had crouched there for hours before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，他已经在那里蹲了几个小时。
The bird had crouched in its nest. 鸟儿已经蜷缩在巢里。

将来时:
He will crouch down to pick up the dropped coin. 他将蹲下捡起掉落的硬币。
They will crouch behind the barricade. 他们将躲在路障后面蹲着。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would crouch and wait for my prey. 如果我是猫，我会蹲下等待猎物。
I wish I could crouch as low as a frog. 我希望我能像青蛙一样蹲得那么低。

被动语态:
(The verb "crouch" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is he crouching behind the car? 他是否躲在车后蹲着？
Why are they crouching in the shadows? 他们为什么躲在阴影里蹲着？

否定句:
He didn't crouch down to avoid being seen. 他没有蹲下躲避。
She didn't crouch low enough to be hidden. 她蹲得不够低，无法躲藏。


PHRASE->
crouch down: 蹲下
crouch low: 蹲得很低
crouch in fear: 害怕地蹲着
crouch behind something: 躲在...后面蹲着
crouch over something: 弯腰俯身在...上面


词源:
The word "crouch" ultimately derives from the Old English word *crucian, meaning "to bend or crook."  This shares roots with other Germanic words denoting bending or curving.  The modern spelling and pronunciation have evolved over time through Middle English.
