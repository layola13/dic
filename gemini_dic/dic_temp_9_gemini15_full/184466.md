restacked:/ˌriːˈstækt/| v.|重新堆放，重新码放 |To stack something again, often in a different way or order.  |例句：The librarian restacked the books after the students had finished borrowing them. 图书管理员在学生借书完毕后重新整理了书籍。

例句：After the storm, we had to restack all the fallen wood. 风暴过后，我们不得不重新堆放所有倒下的木头。


近义词：rearranged, reorganized, restacked, repiled|反义词：scattered, dispersed
