pronotal:/proʊˈnoʊtəl/ | adj. |前胸的；属于前胸的 | Relating to or denoting the pronotum.  The term is primarily used in entomology (the study of insects) to describe the dorsal (top) surface of the first thoracic segment of an insect. | Example: The pronotal shield of the beetle was brightly colored.  这只甲虫的前胸背板颜色鲜艳。 The insect's pronotal structure was unusual.  这只昆虫的前胸结构很不寻常。| 近义词：thoracic (in a broader sense) | 反义词：无
