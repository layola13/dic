realizing: /ˈriːəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 意识到；实现；了解；获得 |  Understanding and becoming aware of something.  The act of making something real or actual. |


例句：

I'm realizing the importance of saving money. = 我正在意识到储蓄的重要性。

She's realizing her dream of becoming a doctor. = 她正在实现她成为医生的梦想。


近义词：understanding, recognizing, perceiving, achieving, accomplishing


反义词：ignoring, overlooking, neglecting


词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I am realizing my mistake. = 我正在意识到我的错误。
He realizes the gravity of the situation. = 他意识到情况的严重性。


一般过去时:
I realized my mistake too late. = 我太迟才意识到我的错误。
She realized she had left her keys at home. = 她意识到她把钥匙忘在家里了。


现在完成时:
I have realized the importance of hard work. = 我已经意识到努力工作的重要性。
They have realized their ambitions. = 他们已经实现了他们的抱负。


过去完成时:
I had realized my error before he pointed it out. = 他指出我的错误之前，我已经意识到了。
She had realized the danger before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，她已经意识到了危险。


将来时:
I will realize my dream one day. = 我总有一天会实现我的梦想。
They will realize the consequences of their actions. = 他们将会意识到他们行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If I had realized the truth earlier, I would have acted differently. = 如果我早点意识到真相，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had realized the potential risks involved. = 我真希望我当时意识到了潜在的风险。


被动语态:
The importance of the project was realized by the team. = 这个项目的重要性被团队意识到了。
The potential of the technology is being realized gradually. = 这项技术的潜力正在逐渐被实现。


疑问句:
Do you realize what you've done? = 你意识到你做了什么了吗？
Does he realize the consequences of his actions? = 他意识到他行为的后果了吗？


否定句:
I didn't realize how late it was. = 我没有意识到已经这么晚了。
He didn't realize the significance of the meeting. = 他没有意识到这次会议的重要性。



PHRASE->

realizing a dream: 实现梦想
realizing a goal: 实现目标
realizing the potential: 意识到潜力
realizing the importance of: 意识到……的重要性
realizing an ambition: 实现一个雄心壮志
