dolomitic: /ˌdɒləˈmɪtɪk/ | adj. | 白云石的；含白云石的 | Containing or consisting of dolomite.  Dolomite is a sedimentary carbonate rock composed primarily of the mineral dolomite, CaMg(CO3)2.  The term "dolomitic" describes rocks, soils, or other materials that have a significant dolomite content. |  The soil in this region is highly dolomitic, affecting the types of plants that can grow.  这地区的土壤富含白云石，影响了可以生长的植物种类。 The dolomitic limestone was quarried for use in construction.  这种含白云石的石灰岩被开采用于建筑。 | calcareous, magnesian | siliceous (in contrast)
