reignited: /ˌriːˈaɪtɪd/ | v. | 重新点燃；使复燃；重新激起 |

英文解析:To reignite something means to cause it to start burning or to become active again after a period of inactivity.  It can refer to literal fires or to metaphorical flames, such as passions, conflicts, or interests.

例句:

The discovery of new evidence reignited the debate about the politician's guilt. = 新证据的发现重新燃起了关于这位政治家有罪的辩论。

The campfire, nearly extinguished by the rain, was reignited with dry kindling. = 几乎被雨水熄灭的营火，用干柴重新燃了起来。


近义词: rekindle, revive, reawaken, inflame, renew

反义词: extinguish, quench, suppress, stifle


词性: 动词
