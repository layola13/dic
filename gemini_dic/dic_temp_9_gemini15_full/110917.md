lipochrin: /ˈlɪpəkrɪn/ | n. |脂褐素|A yellowish-brown pigment found in the cells of various tissues, especially in the aging brain and heart.  It is a complex mixture of lipid and protein. Lipofuscin is considered a marker of cellular aging and oxidative stress.|例句：The accumulation of lipofuscin in the brain is associated with age-related cognitive decline. 脑中脂褐素的积累与年龄相关的认知能力下降有关。  The presence of lipofuscin in heart muscle cells can indicate cellular damage. 心肌细胞中脂褐素的存在可能表明细胞损伤。|近义词：age pigment, ceroid|反义词：无|名词
