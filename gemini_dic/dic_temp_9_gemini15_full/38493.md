conchyliologist:/ˌkɒŋkiːliˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 贝类学家 | A conchyliologist is a scientist who studies mollusks, particularly their shells.  They are experts in the classification, identification, and collection of shells. | The conchyliologist meticulously cataloged the newly discovered snail shell.  这位贝类学家一丝不苟地编目了新发现的蜗牛壳。| malacologist, shell collector |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
