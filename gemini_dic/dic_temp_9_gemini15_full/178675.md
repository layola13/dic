recriminalized:/ˌriːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/| v. |重新定为犯罪；再次定罪 |To make something a crime again after it was no longer considered a crime.  This often refers to actions or behaviors that were previously decriminalized or legalized. |The state recriminalized the possession of small amounts of marijuana.  这个州重新将少量持有大麻定为犯罪。

The government recriminalized certain types of online gambling. 政府重新将某些类型的在线赌博定为犯罪行为。|近义词：re-illegalize, re-prohibit |反义词：decriminalize, legalize
