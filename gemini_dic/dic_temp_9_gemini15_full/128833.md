nightstands:/ˈnaɪtˌstændz/| n. |床头柜|A small table placed beside a bed, typically used for holding a lamp, books, and other personal items.|例句：I placed my book and a glass of water on the nightstand before going to sleep. 我睡觉前把书和一杯水放在床头柜上。|近义词：bedside table|反义词:None
