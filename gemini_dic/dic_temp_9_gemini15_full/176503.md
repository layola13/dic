re-probed:/ˌriːˈproʊbd/ | v. | 再探查；重新调查 | To investigate or examine something again.  |例句：The police re-probed the scene of the crime after new evidence emerged.  警方在出现新的证据后重新调查了犯罪现场。

例句：The scientists re-probed the data to ensure accuracy. 科学家们重新探查了数据以确保准确性。| 近义词：re-examine, reinvestigate, review | 反义词：ignore, neglect | 动词
