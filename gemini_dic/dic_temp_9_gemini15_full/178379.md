reconfronting:/ˌriːkənˈfrʌntɪŋ/| v.|再次面对；重新对抗 |To confront again; to face or deal with (a problem, situation, etc.) again.|例句：The witnesses were reconfronting their attacker in court. 目击者们在法庭上再次面对他们的袭击者。|例句：The government is reconfronting the economic crisis with a new set of policies. 政府正在用一套新的政策再次应对经济危机。|近义词：re-encounter, rechallenge|反义词：avoid, evade
