decentralizing: /ˌdiːˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 分散化，权力下放 | The act of distributing or transferring power or authority away from a central location or authority to more local or regional entities.  It often involves delegating decision-making power and responsibilities to lower levels of an organization or system. | 例句：The company is decentralizing its operations to improve efficiency and responsiveness to local markets. 公司正在分散其业务，以提高效率并更好地响应当地市场需求。


例句：The government is decentralizing its education system, giving more autonomy to individual schools. 政府正在将教育体系下放，赋予各学校更大的自主权。


近义词：deconcentrating, distributing, dispersing


反义词：centralizing, concentrating
