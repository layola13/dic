lauding:/ˈlɔːdɪŋ/| v.|赞扬，赞美|To praise highly; to extol.|例句：The critics lauded his performance as brilliant. 批评家们高度赞扬了他的精彩表演。|例句：The company lauded its employees' hard work. 公司赞扬了员工的辛勤工作。|近义词：praising, extolling, commending, applauding|反义词：condemning, criticizing, censuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They laud her achievements every year. = 他们每年都赞扬她的成就。
We laud the efforts of our volunteers. = 我们赞扬志愿者的努力。


一般过去时:
The mayor lauded the firefighters for their bravery. = 市长赞扬了消防员的勇敢。
The teacher lauded the student's exceptional essay. = 老师称赞学生的优秀论文。


现在完成时:
The media has lauded the athlete's impressive victory. = 媒体一直赞扬这位运动员令人印象深刻的胜利。
She has been lauded for her contributions to science. = 她因对科学的贡献而受到赞扬。


过去完成时:
The crowd had lauded the singer's performance before the encore. = 在返场之前，人群就已经赞扬了歌手的演出。
They had lauded the project's success even before its completion. = 甚至在项目完成之前，他们就已经赞扬了它的成功。


将来时:
The president will laud the efforts made to combat climate change. = 总统将赞扬为应对气候变化所做的努力。
We will laud the winner of the competition at the ceremony. = 我们将在仪式上赞扬比赛的获胜者。


虚拟语气:
If they had worked harder, they would have been lauded for their efforts. = 如果他们工作更努力，他们的努力就会受到赞扬。
I wish I had been lauded for my accomplishments. = 我希望我的成就得到赞扬。


被动语态:
His work was lauded by many experts in the field. = 他的作品受到该领域许多专家的称赞。
The new policy is being lauded as a step in the right direction. = 新政策被誉为朝着正确方向迈出的一步。


疑问句:
Will the team be lauded for their success? = 球队会因为他们的成功而受到赞扬吗？
Are they going to laud her for her bravery? = 他们会因为她的勇敢而赞扬她吗？


否定句:
They did not laud his efforts, despite his hard work. = 尽管他努力工作，他们并没有赞扬他的努力。
The manager didn't laud the employees' performance. = 经理没有赞扬员工的表现。

deservedly lauded = 应得的赞扬
