grandparents:/ˈɡrændˌpeərənts/| n. |祖父母，外祖父母 |Grandparents are the parents of one's parents. They play an important role in many families, offering love, support, and wisdom to their grandchildren. |例句：My grandparents lived with us when I was a child.  我的祖父母在我小时候和我们住在一起。|例句：I spent many happy summers visiting my grandparents in the countryside. 我在乡下度过了许多快乐的暑假，去看望我的祖父母。|近义词：ancestors, elders |反义词：grandchildren
