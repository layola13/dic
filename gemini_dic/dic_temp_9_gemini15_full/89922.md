horse-whip:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪp/| n. v.|马鞭；用马鞭抽打|A horsewhip is a whip used for riding or driving horses.  It can also be used as a weapon. |例句：The farmer used a horsewhip to guide his horses. 农夫用马鞭引导他的马匹。

例句：He was beaten with a horsewhip. 他被马鞭抽打。


近义词：whip, lash

反义词：None (antonyms are not applicable for this word in its primary meaning)

词性: noun, verb
