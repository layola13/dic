hypersplenic: /ˌhaɪpərˈspliːnɪk/ | adj. |脾功能亢进的| Relating to or characteristic of hypersplenism; exhibiting an abnormally increased splenic activity.  It describes a condition where the spleen is overactive, removing blood cells from circulation at an excessive rate. This can lead to low blood counts (anemia, thrombocytopenia, and leukopenia).| The patient presented with hypersplenic symptoms including fatigue, pallor, and easy bruising.|近义词: splenomegalic (if referring to enlarged spleen)|反义词:  There isn't a direct antonym, but the opposite would be a normally functioning spleen.
