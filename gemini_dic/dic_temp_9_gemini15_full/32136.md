chevrette:/ʃəˈvret/| n.|母山羊；小山羊；年轻女子|A young female goat; sometimes used figuratively to refer to a young woman.|例句：The farmer proudly showed off his prize-winning chevrette.  这位农民自豪地展示了他获奖的母山羊。|例句：She was called a "little chevrette" by her friends. 她的朋友们称她为“小姑娘”。|近义词：kid, nanny goat (for the goat meaning), lass, girl (for the figurative meaning)|反义词：buck (male goat), old woman (for the figurative meaning)
