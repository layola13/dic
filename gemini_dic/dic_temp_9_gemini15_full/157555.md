pitbull:/ˈpɪtˌbʊl/| n.|比特犬，皮特犬|A pitbull is a type of dog, specifically a dog of the Pit Bull Terrier family, known for its muscular build and strong jaw.  They are often associated with a controversial reputation due to their strength and potential for aggression, although responsible ownership can mitigate these risks.  The term "pit bull" is often used loosely and can refer to several breeds.|例句：The pitbull bounded playfully towards its owner. 比特犬兴高采烈地朝它的主人跑去。

例句：Owning a pitbull requires responsible training and socialization. 拥有比特犬需要负责任的训练和社会化。|近义词：Pit Bull Terrier, American Pit Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier|反义词:无直接反义词，但可以考虑体格较小，性格温顺的犬种，如：Chihuahua, Poodle|名词
