preaching:/ˈpriːtʃɪŋ/| n. v.|布道；说教；宣扬|The act of delivering a sermon or lecture, especially one on a religious topic; the act of promoting or advocating a particular idea or cause.  |例句：The preacher's preaching was inspiring.  =  牧师的布道令人鼓舞。

The constant preaching about healthy eating became tiresome. =  不断的说教健康饮食变得令人厌烦。|近义词：sermonizing, lecturing, advocating, propagating |反义词：silence, ignoring


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He preaches every Sunday. = 他每周日都布道。
She preaches the importance of education. = 她宣扬教育的重要性。

一般过去时:
The priest preached a powerful sermon. = 牧师做了一次有力的布道。
He preached against violence. = 他谴责暴力。

现在完成时:
He has preached to thousands of people. = 他已经向成千上万的人布道。
She has preached this message for years. = 她多年来一直在宣扬这个信息。

过去完成时:
He had preached the gospel for many years before retiring. = 他退休前已经传福音很多年了。
They had preached the same message repeatedly. = 他们反复宣扬同样的信息。

将来时:
He will preach at the cathedral tomorrow. = 他明天将在教堂布道。
She will preach tolerance and understanding. = 她将宣扬宽容和理解。

虚拟语气:
If he preached more often, more people would attend. = 如果他更经常布道，会有更多人参加。
I wish he would stop preaching at me. = 我希望他能停止对我进行说教。

被动语态:
The sermon was preached with great passion. = 布道充满激情地进行。
The importance of peace was preached throughout the region. = 和平的重要性在整个地区都被宣扬。

疑问句:
Does he preach regularly? = 他经常布道吗？
What is she preaching about? = 她正在宣扬什么？

否定句:
He doesn't preach hate. = 他不宣扬仇恨。
She didn't preach to the unconverted. = 她没有向未改信者布道。


PHRASE->

preaching to the choir:对知情者说教 (To preach to people who already agree with you)
preaching from the pulpit:在讲台上布道
fire and brimstone preaching:地狱烈火般的布道（指充满谴责的布道）
preaching to the converted:对已经信服的人说教
hear someone's preaching: 听某人说教
