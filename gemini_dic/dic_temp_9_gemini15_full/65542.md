euthanatising: /ˌjuːθəˈneɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 安乐死 | The act of killing someone painlessly, especially to relieve suffering.  | Example: The debate surrounding euthanatising terminally ill patients is complex and emotionally charged.  安乐死绝症患者的辩论复杂且充满情感。| 近义词：mercy killing, assisted suicide | 反义词：life support, treatment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are euthanatising the injured animal. = 他们正在对受伤的动物实施安乐死。
He euthanises animals regularly at the shelter. = 他定期在收容所对动物实施安乐死。


一般过去时:
The vet euthanised the dog. = 兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死。
They euthanised the horse after its injury. = 他们在这匹马受伤后对其进行了安乐死。


现在完成时:
The family has euthanised their beloved pet. = 这个家庭已经对他们心爱的宠物进行了安乐死。
She has euthanised many animals throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经对许多动物进行了安乐死。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
