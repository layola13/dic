protozoans:/ˌproʊtəˈzoʊənz/ | n. |原生动物 |Single-celled eukaryotic microorganisms that are typically found in water or moist environments. They are diverse in form and function, and some are pathogenic.  |Example:  Many protozoans are harmless, but some can cause serious diseases.  许多原生动物是无害的，但有些会引起严重的疾病。|近义词：protists (a broader term)|反义词：metazoans (multicellular animals)
