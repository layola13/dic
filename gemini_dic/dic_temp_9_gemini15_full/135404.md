nonpermissiveness:/ˌnɒnpəˈmɪsɪvnəs/ | adj. | 不允许；不容忍；压制 | The state or quality of not being permissive; the act of not allowing or tolerating something.  It implies a restrictive or controlling environment where freedom of expression or action is limited.  | Example: The nonpermissiveness of the school environment stifled creativity among the students.  学校环境的压制扼杀了学生们的创造力。 | 近义词：intolerance, restrictiveness, authoritarianism | 反义词：permissiveness, tolerance, liberalism |  形容词
