presentific: /ˌpreˈzɛntɪfaɪk/ | adj. |  似科学的；貌似科学的；伪科学的 |  Appearing scientific or based on science, but actually not genuinely so; often used pejoratively to describe claims or methods that mimic the appearance of scientific rigor without possessing its substance.  | 例句：His arguments, while sounding presentific, lacked any real empirical evidence. 他的论点虽然听起来像科学的，但却缺乏任何真正的经验证据。


例句：The company's marketing campaign used presentific language to convince customers of the product's efficacy. 该公司的营销活动使用了似是而非的科学语言来让顾客相信该产品的功效。


近义词：pseudo-scientific, quasi-scientific, unscientific, specious


反义词：scientific, factual, verifiable, valid

词性：形容词
