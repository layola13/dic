installing:/ɪnˈstɔːlɪŋ/ | v. |安装，设置|The act of putting software or hardware into place and making it ready to use.  It involves setting up the necessary configurations and dependencies.|例句：They are installing a new air conditioning system. 他们正在安装一个新的空调系统。


例句：I'm installing a new game on my computer. 我正在我的电脑上安装一个新游戏。


近义词：fitting, setting up, implementing | 反义词：removing, uninstalling, deleting

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He installs software every week. 他每周都安装软件。
She installs new apps on her phone. 她会在她的手机上安装新的应用程序。


一般过去时:
I installed the program yesterday. 我昨天安装了这个程序。
They installed the new printer last Friday. 他们上周五安装了新打印机。


现在完成时:
We have installed the operating system. 我们已经安装了操作系统。
She has installed several security programs. 她已经安装了几个安全程序。


过去完成时:
They had installed the software before the meeting. 他们在会议之前已经安装了软件。
He had installed the drivers before starting the game. 他在开始游戏之前已经安装了驱动程序。


将来时:
I will install the updated drivers tomorrow. 我明天将安装更新的驱动程序。
They will install the new system next week. 他们下周将安装新系统。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would install that program. 如果我有更多时间，我会安装那个程序。
I wish I had installed the antivirus software earlier. 我希望我早点安装了杀毒软件。


被动语态:
The software was installed successfully. 软件成功安装。
The new system is being installed now. 新系统正在安装中。


疑问句:
Are you installing the new software? 你正在安装新软件吗？

否定句:
