hollandish:/ˈhɒləndɪʃ/| adj.|荷兰式的；荷兰风格的|Of or relating to Holland or the Netherlands; characteristic of Holland or its people.  The term often refers to artistic styles, particularly in painting, associated with the Dutch Golden Age.|例句：The painting exhibited a distinct Hollandish style, characterized by its realistic portrayal of everyday life. 这幅画展现出独特的荷兰风格，其特点是写实地描绘日常生活。

The still life was clearly Hollandish in its meticulous detail and use of light. 这幅静物画在精细的细节和光线的运用上明显具有荷兰风格。|近义词：Dutch, Netherlandish|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be applied depending on the context (e.g., if describing an artistic style, the opposite might be abstract or impressionistic).|
