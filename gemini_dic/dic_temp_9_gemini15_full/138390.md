notory:/ˈnoʊtəri/ | n. |公证人；(尤指美国)公证员| A notary public is an official who is authorized to witness and certify signatures and documents.|The notary public certified the signature on the document. 公证员证明了文件上的签名。|近义词：public notary, commissioner of oaths|反义词：无
