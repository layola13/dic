loucher:/ˈluːtʃər/| v.|斜视，瞟|To look sideways or obliquely; to squint.  The word often implies a sly or suspicious glance.|例句：He louched at the woman across the room. 他斜眼看着房间对面的那个女人。
He louched suspiciously at the closed door. 他鬼鬼祟祟地斜眼看着那扇关着的门。|近义词：squint, glance, peer, ogle|反义词：stare straight
