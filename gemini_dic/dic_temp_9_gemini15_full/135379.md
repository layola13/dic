nonperformers:/ˌnɒn pərˈfɔːrməz/ | n. | 未达标者；表现不佳者 | People or things that fail to meet expectations or achieve desired results.  They may be underperforming employees, inefficient systems, or unsuccessful products. | Example: The company decided to lay off several nonperformers in order to improve overall productivity.  这家公司决定解雇几名表现不佳的员工以提高整体生产力。|近义词：underachievers, failures, slackers | 反义词：high performers, top achievers, stars
