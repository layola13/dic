reoccupied:/ˌriːˈɒkjʊpaɪd/ | adj. |重新占据的；重新使用的 |  Occupied again; used again after a period of not being used.  |Example: The abandoned building was reoccupied by squatters.  废弃的建筑物被棚户区居民重新占据。  |近义词:reclaimed, reused, repurposed |反义词:vacant, unoccupied, abandoned |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory is reoccupied by a new company. = 这家工厂被一家新公司重新占据。
This space is reoccupied for storage. = 这个空间被重新用于储存。


一般过去时:
The army reoccupied the territory last year. = 军队去年重新占领了该领土。
The old house was reoccupied by its former owner. = 这座老房子被以前的主人重新占据。


现在完成时:
The government has reoccupied the island. = 政府已经重新占领了该岛屿。
The building has been reoccupied since last month. = 这座建筑物从上个月起就被重新使用了。


过去完成时:
They had reoccupied the building before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经重新占领了那座建筑物。
The city had been reoccupied by the rebels by the time the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达时，这座城市已经被叛军重新占领。


将来时:
The company will reoccupy the office building next month. = 公司下个月将重新占据办公大楼。
The land will be reoccupied for agricultural purposes. = 这块土地将被重新用于农业用途。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy were to reoccupy the city, we would be in serious trouble. = 如果敌人重新占领这座城市，我们将面临严重的麻烦。
I wish the government had not reoccupied that area so quickly. = 我真希望政府没有那么快就重新占领那个地区。


被动语态:
The land was reoccupied by the local farmers. = 这块土地被当地农民重新占据。
The building is being reoccupied after extensive renovations. = 这座建筑物经过大规模翻修后正在重新使用。


疑问句:
Was the house reoccupied after the fire? = 火灾后房子重新被占据了吗？
Will the area be reoccupied in the near future? = 该地区会在不久的将来重新被占据吗？


否定句:
The abandoned mine was not reoccupied. = 废弃的矿井没有被重新占据。
The building has not been reoccupied since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，这座建筑物一直没有被重新使用。
