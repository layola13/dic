schoolfellow:/ˈskuːlˌfɛloʊ/|/ˈskuːlˌfeɪləʊ/| n. | 同学，校友 | A schoolfellow is a person who attends the same school as you.  |  I haven't seen my old schoolfellows for years.  我已经好几年没见过我的老同学了。| classmate, schoolmate |  enemy, opponent | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is my schoolfellow. 他是我的同学。
They are schoolfellows. 他们是同学。


一般过去时:
He was my schoolfellow. 他是我的同学（过去）。
We were schoolfellows in high school. 我们高中时是同学。


现在完成时:
I have known him since we were schoolfellows. 从我们还是同学起我就认识他了。
They have been schoolfellows for many years. 他们做了多年的同学。


过去完成时:
I had known him before we became schoolfellows.  在我们成为同学之前我就认识他了。
They had been schoolfellows for five years before they graduated. 他们在毕业前做了五年的同学。


将来时:
He will still be my schoolfellow next year. 他明年仍然是我的同学。
We will be schoolfellows for another year. 我们还会再做一年的同学。


虚拟语气:
If I had known he was my schoolfellow, I would have talked to him more. 如果我知道他是我的同学，我会和他多聊聊。
I wish I had been his schoolfellow. 我真希望我是他的同学。


被动语态:
He was chosen as the monitor by his schoolfellows. 他被他的同学们选为班长。
She was admired by her schoolfellows for her intelligence. 她因她的聪明才智而受到同学们的敬佩。


疑问句:
Is he your schoolfellow? 他是你的同学吗？
Were they schoolfellows? 他们那时是同学吗？


否定句:
He is not my schoolfellow. 他不是我的同学。
They were not schoolfellows. 他们不是同学。
