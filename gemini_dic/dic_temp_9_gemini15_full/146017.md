overdrain: /ˌoʊvərˈdreɪn/ | v. |过度排水；过度引流 | To drain excessively; to drain beyond what is necessary or desirable.  |例句：Overdraining the soil can lead to desertification. 过度排水会导致沙漠化。

例句：The farmer overdrained his fields, resulting in a poor harvest. 农民过度排水田地，导致收成不好。


近义词：over-irrigate (if referring to water), deplete, exhaust


反义词：underdrain, retain moisture


词性:动词

词源:  The word "overdrain" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively or to a great extent) and the verb "drain" (meaning to remove liquid from something).  Its meaning is a straightforward extension of the meaning of its component parts.
