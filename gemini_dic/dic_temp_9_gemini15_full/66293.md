excoriating: /ɪkˈskɔːrieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 严厉批评的；苛刻的；极度痛苦的 | Severely critical; causing intense suffering.  | The critic delivered an excoriating review of the play, leaving no aspect untouched.  评论家对这部戏进行了严厉的批评，没有放过任何一个方面。 | scathing, blistering, withering, harsh | lenient, mild, gentle
