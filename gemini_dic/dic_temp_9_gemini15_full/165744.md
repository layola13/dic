prenegotiations:/ˌpriːnɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃənz/| n. |预先谈判；谈判前的磋商|Preliminary discussions or negotiations before formal negotiations begin.  These discussions often involve exploring the possibilities of an agreement and identifying potential sticking points. |例句：The prenegotiations were crucial in setting the stage for a successful contract. 预先谈判对于成功签订合同至关重要。

例句：The two companies engaged in extensive prenegotiations before signing the merger agreement. 两家公司在签署合并协议之前进行了广泛的预先谈判。


近义词： preliminary talks, prior discussions, advance negotiations

反义词：final negotiations, concluding talks


词性:名词
