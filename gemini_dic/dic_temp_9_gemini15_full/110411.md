like a house on fire: /laɪk ə haʊs ɒn faɪər/ | adj. phrase | 非常快地；非常成功地 |  This idiom describes something progressing or being done very rapidly and successfully. It implies a sense of uncontrolled, energetic activity. The imagery comes from a house engulfed in flames, burning rapidly and intensely. | 例句：The new business is doing like a house on fire. 这家新公司发展得非常迅速。

The project is going like a house on fire; we'll finish ahead of schedule.  这个项目进展神速；我们将提前完成。| 近义词：rapidly, quickly, successfully, booming, flourishing | 反义词：slowly, sluggishly, unsuccessfully, failing
