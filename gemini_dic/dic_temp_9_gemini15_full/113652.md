machined:/məˈʃiːnd/| adj. v.| 机加工的；用机器制造的 |Processed or created using a machine.  |例句：The machined parts were incredibly precise.  机加工零件非常精确。

The engine block is machined from a solid piece of aluminum.  发动机缸体是由整块铝材机加工而成的。|近义词：manufactured, fabricated, engineered |反义词：handmade, handcrafted
