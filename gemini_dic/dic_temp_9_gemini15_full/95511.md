inclining:/ɪnˈklaɪnɪŋ/| v. adj.|倾斜的；倾向的；易于…的 |To be inclined towards something means to feel a preference for it, or to be likely to do it.  It can also describe something that is leaning or sloping. |例句：The tower is inclining dangerously.  塔身正危险地倾斜着。

The evidence inclines me to believe his story. 证据使我倾向于相信他的说法。


近义词：leaning, sloping, tending, predisposed, prone |反义词：vertical, upright, straight, resisting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inclining his head. = 他正在低下头。
She inclines towards modern art. = 她倾向于现代艺术。

一般过去时:
The tree inclined in the wind. = 树在风中倾斜。
He inclined to agree with me. = 他倾向于同意我的意见。

现在完成时:
The tower has inclined further since the last inspection. = 自上次检查以来，塔身倾斜得更厉害了。
She has always inclined towards a career in medicine. = 她一直倾向于从事医学事业。

过去完成时:
The building had inclined noticeably before the earthquake. = 地震前建筑物已经明显倾斜了。
He had inclined to believe the rumors before he saw the evidence. = 在他看到证据之前，他倾向于相信那些谣言。

将来时:
The leaning tower will incline further if no action is taken. = 如果不采取行动，倾斜的塔将会倾斜得更厉害。
I believe she will incline towards accepting the offer. = 我相信她会倾向于接受这个提议。

虚拟语气:
If the ground were softer, the fence would incline more easily. = 如果地面更软，栅栏会更容易倾斜。
I wish the building didn't incline so much. = 我希望建筑物不要倾斜得这么厉害。

被动语态:
The road was inclined at a sharp angle. = 这条路以一个锐角倾斜。
His opinion is inclined to be ignored. = 他的意见往往被忽略。

疑问句:
Does the building incline to the right or the left? = 建筑物向右倾斜还是向左倾斜？
Does she incline towards this political party? = 她倾向于哪个政党？

否定句:
The tower does not incline significantly. = 塔身没有明显倾斜。
He doesn't incline to violence. = 他不喜欢暴力。
