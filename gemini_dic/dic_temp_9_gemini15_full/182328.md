renascence:/rɪˈnæsns/| n. |文艺复兴；复兴；新生 |The period in European civilization immediately following the Middle Ages, characterized by a renewed interest in classical art, literature, and philosophy.  It marked a transition from the medieval world to the modern world. |The Renaissance saw a flourishing of art and literature. 文艺复兴时期艺术和文学蓬勃发展。|The city is experiencing a cultural renascence.  这座城市正在经历一场文化复兴。|resurgence, revival, rebirth |decline, downfall |名词
