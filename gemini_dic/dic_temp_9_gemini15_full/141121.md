ontologist:/ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n.| 本体论者，本体学家 | A person who studies ontology.  An ontologist is concerned with the nature of being, existence, or reality, as well as the basic categories of being and their relations. They often work in fields like philosophy, computer science (knowledge representation and reasoning), and information science. | Example: The ontologist carefully analyzed the relationships between different concepts in the knowledge base. 本体学家仔细分析了知识库中不同概念之间的关系。| 近义词：metaphysician, philosopher (in relevant fields) | 反义词：None specifically, but someone who denies the importance of ontology could be considered an implicit antonym. | 名词
