misspends:/ˌmɪsˈspendz/| vt.|滥用；浪费；挥霍|To use (money, time, etc.) unwisely or wastefully.|例句：He misspends his money on gambling. 他把钱都花在了赌博上。|例句：She misspends her time on trivial matters. 她把时间浪费在琐事上。|近义词：waste, squander, fritter away|反义词：save, conserve, invest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misspends his salary every month. = 他每个月都挥霍他的薪水。
They misspend their time playing video games. = 他们把时间浪费在玩电子游戏上。

一般过去时:
He misspent his inheritance on a yacht. = 他把继承的遗产挥霍在游艇上了。
She misspent years chasing an impossible dream. = 她把好几年时间浪费在一个不可能实现的梦想上。

现在完成时:
He has misspent most of his savings. = 他已经挥霍了他大部分的积蓄。
She has misspent her youth on meaningless pursuits. = 她把青春浪费在了毫无意义的事情上。

过去完成时:
He had misspent all his money before the trip. = 旅行前他已经把所有的钱都挥霍光了。
She realised she had misspent her time studying the wrong subject. = 她意识到她把时间浪费在了学习错误的科目上。

将来时:
He will misspend his bonus on a luxury car. = 他会把奖金挥霍在一辆豪华车上。
They will misspend their summer vacation sleeping. = 他们会把暑假时间浪费在睡觉上。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't misspent his money, he would be rich now. = 如果他没有挥霍他的钱，他现在就会很富有。
I wish I hadn't misspent my youth. = 我真希望我没有挥霍我的青春。

被动语态:
His money was misspent on frivolous things. = 他的钱被浪费在了琐碎的事情上。
Time can be misspent on unproductive activities. = 时间可以被浪费在没有成效的活动上。

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't misspend his inheritance.  = 他没有挥霍他的遗产。
She doesn't misspend her time; she's very efficient. = 她没有浪费时间；她非常高效。
