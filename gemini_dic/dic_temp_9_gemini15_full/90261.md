housed:/haʊzd/| v. adj.|居住的；容纳的；被安置的|To provide housing or shelter for; to contain or accommodate;  past tense and past participle of "house".|例句：The family is housed in a comfortable apartment. 这个家庭住在一套舒适的公寓里。|近义词：lodged, accommodated, sheltered|反义词：homeless, unhoused


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum houses a vast collection of artifacts. = 博物馆收藏了大量的文物。
The building houses many different businesses. = 这座建筑物容纳了许多不同的企业。


一般过去时:
They housed the refugees in temporary shelters. = 他们把难民安置在临时住所里。
The old barn housed the farm animals. = 旧谷仓里住着农场动物。


现在完成时:
The government has housed thousands of people affected by the disaster. = 政府已为成千上万受灾民众提供了住房。
The company has housed its servers in a secure data center. = 公司已将其服务器安置在安全的數據中心。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, they had housed all the livestock in the barn. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已将所有牲畜安置在谷仓里。
The family had housed several foster children over the years. = 多年来，这个家庭收养过好几个寄养儿童。


将来时:
The new development will house hundreds of families. = 新开发项目将容纳数百个家庭。
The company plans to house its operations in a new building next year. = 公司计划明年将业务迁入新大楼。


虚拟语气:
If we had more space, we could house more guests. = 如果我们有更多空间，我们就能容纳更多客人。
I wish we could house all the homeless animals in the city. = 我希望我们能够收容城市里所有无家可归的动物。


被动语态:
The refugees were housed in temporary camps. = 难民被安置在临时营地里。
The data is housed on a secure server. = 数据存储在一个安全的服务器上。


疑问句:
Where are the animals housed? = 动物们住在哪里？
How many people are housed in this building? = 这栋建筑里住着多少人？


否定句:
The hotel could not house all the guests. = 旅馆无法容纳所有客人。
The small apartment could not house their large family. = 这套小公寓无法容纳他们大家庭。


PHRASE->
be housed in = 位于…；被安置在…
house prices = 房价
housing shortage = 住房短缺
public housing = 公共住房
affordable housing = 可负担住房
