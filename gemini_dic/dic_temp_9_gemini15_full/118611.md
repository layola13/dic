messaging:/ˈmɛsɪdʒɪŋ/ | n. |信息传递；消息服务；发短信 |The act of sending and receiving messages, often electronically.  It encompasses various methods of communication, from text messages to instant messaging and email. |例句：Instant messaging is a popular form of messaging.  即时通讯是一种流行的信息传递方式。

例句：The company uses a sophisticated messaging system to communicate with its customers.  这家公司使用一套复杂的消息系统与客户沟通。


近义词：communication, correspondence, dispatching | 反义词：silence, non-communication


词源:  Derived from the noun "message" (itself from Old French *message, from Old Latin *mittere "to send"), combined with the suffix "-ing," indicating the act of doing something.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are messaging each other regularly. = 我们定期互相发信息。
He messages his friends every day. = 他每天都给他的朋友发短信。


一般过去时:
They messaged their parents about their arrival. = 他们给父母发了信息告知到达情况。
She messaged him to confirm the meeting. = 她给他发信息确认会议。


现在完成时:
I have messaged him several times, but he hasn't replied. = 我给他发了好几次信息，但他还没回复。
She has been messaging her friends all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在给朋友发信息。


过去完成时:
They had messaged before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经发过信息了。
He had messaged her several times before she finally answered. = 他给她发了好几次信息，她才终于回复。


将来时:
I will message you when I arrive. = 我到了会给你发信息。
We will be messaging throughout the day to coordinate. = 我们一整天都会发信息来协调工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had his number, I would message him. = 如果我有他的号码，我会给他发信息。
I wish I could message him right now. = 我真希望现在能给他发信息。


被动语态:
The message was messaged to all employees. = 这条信息被发送给了所有员工。(This sentence is slightly awkward.  Passive voice is less common with "messaging".)
The information is being messaged via email. = 信息正在通过电子邮件发送。(Again, slightly awkward)


疑问句:
Are you messaging anyone right now? = 你现在正在给谁发信息？
Have you messaged him yet? = 你给他发信息了吗？


否定句:
I didn't message him yesterday. = 我昨天没有给他发信息。
They haven't messaged back yet. = 他们还没有回复信息。
