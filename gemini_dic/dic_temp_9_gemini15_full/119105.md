metrologist:/ˌmɛtrɒˈlɒdʒɪst/| n. |计量学家，计量员|A metrologist is a scientist or expert who specializes in metrology, the science of measurement.  They are involved in developing, maintaining, and improving measurement standards and techniques.  Metrologists work in various fields, including manufacturing, engineering, and scientific research, ensuring the accuracy and reliability of measurements.|例句：The metrologist calibrated the instruments to ensure accurate readings.  计量学家校准了仪器以确保读数准确。


The company hired a metrologist to verify the accuracy of their scales. 公司聘请了一位计量学家来验证其秤的准确性。|近义词：measurement expert, calibration specialist|反义词：无
