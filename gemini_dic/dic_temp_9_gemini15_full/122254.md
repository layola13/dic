mitering:/ˈmaɪtərɪŋ/| n. v.|斜接，斜角连接|The act of joining two pieces of material at an angle, typically 45 degrees, to form a miter joint.|例句：The carpenter carefully performed the mitering of the picture frame. 木匠仔细地进行了画框的斜接。The mitering of the wood created a seamless corner. 木材的斜接创造了一个无缝的角落。|近义词：beveling, joining|反义词：butting
