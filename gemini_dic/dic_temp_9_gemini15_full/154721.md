perspirant:/ˈpɜːrspərənt/| n. |止汗剂| A cosmetic or medicinal preparation applied to the skin to reduce perspiration.  It typically contains antiperspirant ingredients that temporarily block sweat glands.| Example:  She applies perspirant every morning to stay dry throughout the day. 她每天早上都涂抹止汗剂，以保持一天的干爽。|近义词：antiperspirant|反义词：None directly opposite, but perhaps "sweat enhancer" (though this doesn't exist as a common term).|名词

词源:
The word "perspirant" is derived from the verb "perspire" (to sweat) and the suffix "-ant," which indicates an agent or substance that performs the action.  Therefore, a perspirant is something that relates to or affects perspiration.
