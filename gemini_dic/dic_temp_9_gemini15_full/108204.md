laudable:/ˈlɔːdəbl/| adj.|值得赞扬的；令人钦佩的|Deserving praise and commendation; commendable.  It refers to actions, qualities, or efforts that are worthy of approval and admiration.  The word implies a high degree of moral or ethical value.|例句：Her dedication to the project was laudable.  她的奉献精神值得赞扬。

例句：His efforts to help the homeless are truly laudable. 他的努力帮助无家可归的人是真正值得赞扬的。


近义词：commendable, praiseworthy, admirable, meritorious, exemplary

反义词：blameworthy, reprehensible, deplorable, shameful


词性：形容词
