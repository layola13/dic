compatriot:/kəmˈpeɪtriət/| n. |同胞，同国人 | A fellow citizen or national of a country.  | Example:  He fought alongside his compatriots for the liberation of their country. 他与他的同胞们并肩作战，争取国家的解放。
|近义词: countryman, fellow citizen |反义词: foreigner, alien |
