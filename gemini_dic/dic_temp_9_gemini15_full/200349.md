/skus/ | n. |库存单位, 存货单位|Stock Keeping Unit; a unique identification number assigned to each distinct product or service that a company sells.  It is used for inventory management and tracking. | Example: The warehouse manager needs to track the SKUs for all incoming and outgoing shipments.  仓库管理员需要跟踪所有进出货物的库存单位。|近义词：item, product, inventory item|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the SKU of this item? = 这个商品的库存单位是什么？
How many SKUs do we have in stock? = 我们有多少库存单位的商品在库存中？


一般过去时:
What was the SKU of the returned product? = 退回产品的库存单位是什么？
How many SKUs did we sell last month? = 上个月我们卖出了多少库存单位的商品？


现在完成时:
How many SKUs have we sold this year? = 今年我们卖出了多少库存单位的商品？
What SKUs have been discontinued? = 哪些库存单位的商品已经停产了？


过去完成时:

How many SKUs had been damaged before the shipment? = 装运前有多少库存单位的商品受损了？

虚拟语气:
If we had more SKUs, we would have higher sales. = 如果我们有更多库存单位的商品，我们的销售额就会更高。
I wish we knew the SKUs of all our competitors' products. = 我希望我们知道所有竞争对手产品的库存单位。


被动语态:
