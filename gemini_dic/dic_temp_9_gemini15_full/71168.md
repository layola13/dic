fissiparous:/ˈfɪsɪpərəs/| adj.|分裂的；离心倾向的；易分裂的| tending to cause or undergo division or fragmentation.  The term often refers to political or social groups splitting into factions.| The fissiparous tendencies within the party led to its ultimate downfall.  这个党派内部的分裂倾向导致了最终的垮台。|近义词：divisive, schismatic, fragmentation-prone|反义词：unified, cohesive, integral
