nitrates:/ˈnaɪtreɪts/| n. |硝酸盐|Nitrate salts are chemical compounds containing the nitrate ion (NO₃⁻). They are commonly found in fertilizers, explosives, and food preservatives.  Nitrates are also naturally occurring in the environment.  High levels of nitrates in drinking water can be harmful to human health.|The farmer used nitrates to fertilize his crops.|近义词：nitric salts|反义词：无
