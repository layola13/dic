mares:/ˈmɛrz/| n.|母马，母驴|A female horse.|The farmer's mares foaled last spring.|近义词：female horse, jenny (female donkey)|反义词：stallion

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mares graze in the pasture. = 母马们在牧场上吃草。
Several mares are pregnant. = 几匹母马怀孕了。


一般过去时:

The old mare died last winter. = 那匹老母马去年冬天死了。
