grateful:/ˈɡreɪtfl/| adj.|感激的，感谢的|Feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness or benefits received.|例句：I am grateful for your help. 我感谢你的帮助。|例句：She expressed her grateful appreciation for the gift. 她表达了她对这份礼物的感激之情。|近义词：thankful, appreciative, indebted|反义词：ungrateful, unappreciative


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am grateful for your kindness. = 我感激你的好意。
They are grateful to have such supportive friends. = 他们很感激有这样支持他们的朋友。


一般过去时:
I was grateful for the opportunity. = 我很感激这次机会。
She was grateful that he had helped her. = 她很感激他帮助了她。


现在完成时:
I have been grateful for their support throughout the years. = 多年来我一直很感激他们的支持。
We have been grateful for the chance to learn from you. = 我们一直很感激有机会向你学习。


过去完成时:
I had been grateful for his guidance before he left. = 在他离开之前，我一直很感激他的指导。
They had been grateful for the timely assistance. = 他们一直很感激及时的帮助。


将来时:
I will be grateful for any help you can offer. = 我将感激你提供的任何帮助。
They will be grateful to know that you are safe. = 他们会很高兴知道你平安无事。


虚拟语气:
I would be grateful if you could reply soon. = 如果你能尽快回复，我会很感激。
It would be grateful to have your support. = 有你的支持将会非常感激。


被动语态:
His help was gratefully received. = 他的帮助得到了衷心的感谢。
Their efforts were gratefully acknowledged. = 他们的努力得到了衷心的认可。


疑问句:
Are you grateful for what they did? = 你感激他们所做的事情吗？
Isn't she grateful for the second chance? = 她难道不感激第二次机会吗？


否定句:
I am not grateful for his interference. = 我并不感激他的干涉。
They were not grateful for the unexpected criticism. = 他们并不感激突如其来的批评。


PHRASE->
feel grateful = 感到感激
be grateful to sb. = 感激某人
a grateful heart = 一颗感恩的心
express gratitude = 表达感激之情
show gratitude = 表示感激
