selden:/ˈsɛldəm/| adv.|很少，难得|Rarely; infrequently; on few occasions.| Seldom do I see him these days.  这些日子我很少见到他。|often, frequently, usually|always, constantly|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seldom visits his grandparents. = 他很少去看望他的祖父母。
She seldom eats meat. = 她很少吃肉。

一般过去时:
They seldom went to the cinema. = 他们很少去看电影。
We seldom argued. = 我们很少吵架。

现在完成时:
I have seldom felt so happy. = 我很少感到如此快乐。
She has seldom been ill. = 她很少生病。

过去完成时:
I had seldom seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我很少见过如此美丽的日落。
He had seldom traveled abroad. = 他很少出国旅行。

将来时:
We will seldom have time to relax. = 我们将来很少有时间放松。
They will seldom visit us. = 他们将来很少会来看我们。


虚拟语气:
If I had money, I would seldom worry. = 如果我有钱，我很少会担心。
I wish I seldom had to work overtime. = 我希望我很少需要加班。


被动语态:
This problem is seldom discussed. = 这个问题很少被讨论。
This event is seldom mentioned. = 这件事很少被提及。


疑问句:
Do you seldom go out? = 你很少出去吗？
Does he seldom come to work on time? = 他很少准时上班吗？


否定句:
He doesn't seldom come to see me. (This is grammatically awkward; use "He often comes to see me" instead). 他经常来看我。(这句话在语法上很别扭；应该用“他经常来看我”代替)。
She is not seldom happy. (This is grammatically awkward; use "She is often happy" instead). 她经常很开心。(这句话在语法上很别扭；应该用“她经常很开心”代替)。
