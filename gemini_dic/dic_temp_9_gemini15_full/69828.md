ferret:/ˈfɛrɪt/ | n. v. |雪貂；搜寻；挖掘 | A small, slender, long-bodied mammal with reddish-brown fur, used for hunting rabbits and rats.  Also, to search or hunt for something persistently. |例句：The ferret darted into the rabbit hole. 雪貂窜进了兔洞。
They ferreted out the truth after a long investigation. 经过长时间的调查，他们查明了真相。 |近义词：polecat, weasel (n.);  investigate, search (v.) |反义词：hide (v.)
