outblossoms: /ˌaʊtˈblɑːsəmz/ | v. | 超过；胜过；比…更繁盛 | To surpass in beauty, growth, or achievement.  |例句：The roses outblossomed all the other flowers in the garden.  玫瑰花比花园里其他所有的花都长得茂盛。


The wildflowers outblossomed the cultivated varieties this year. 今年野花比人工栽培的品种长得更茂盛。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outshine, outstrip | 反义词：underperform, lag behind | 词性：动词

词源:
The word "outblossoms" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than," "surpassing") and the verb "blossoms" (meaning to produce flowers).  It's a relatively straightforward derivation reflecting the action of one thing exceeding another in flowering or flourishing.
