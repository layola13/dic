eschatologize:/ˌɛskætəˈlɒdʒaɪz/ | vt. |阐述末世论；对…作末世论解释 | To interpret or explain something in terms of eschatology (the part of theology concerned with the final events in the history of the world, especially the second coming of Christ and the last judgment).  |例句：He eschatologized the current political climate, seeing it as a sign of the impending apocalypse. 他用末世论的观点解释当前的政治气候，认为这是迫在眉睫的末日预兆。

例句：Some theologians eschatologize historical events to find parallels with biblical prophecy. 一些神学家用末世论的观点解释历史事件，以寻找与圣经预言的相似之处。


近义词：interpret, analyze, explain (in terms of eschatology) | 反义词： N/A | 词性：动词
