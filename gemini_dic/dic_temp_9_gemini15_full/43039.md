cowhearted:/ˈkaʊˌhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|胆小的，懦弱的|Cowhearted describes someone who lacks courage and is easily frightened.  It suggests a timid and fearful disposition.|He was too cowhearted to confront the bully.|近义词：timid, cowardly, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, bold
