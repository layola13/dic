docudrama:/ˈdɒkjuːˌdrɑːmə/| n. |纪实剧 |A docudrama is a film or television program that presents a factual story, often a historical event, in a dramatic way.  It blends documentary-style techniques with dramatic storytelling, using actors to portray real people and events.  While aiming for historical accuracy, creative license is often taken to enhance the narrative. |Example: The docudrama "Apollo 13" dramatizes the events surrounding the near-disaster of the Apollo 13 mission.  |近义词：documentary drama, historical drama |反义词：fiction, pure fiction |名词
