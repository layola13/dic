redismissals: /ˌriːdɪsˈmɪsəlz/ | n. | 再解雇；重新解雇 | The act of dismissing someone again after they have been previously dismissed.  This often implies a reinstatement followed by a second dismissal.  It suggests a more serious offense or a failure to meet expectations after a prior chance. | Example: The redismissals of several employees sparked outrage among the workforce.  再解雇几名员工激起了员工的愤怒。The company's redismissals were met with protests from the union. 公司的再次解雇遭到了工会的抗议。|近义词：re-dismissals, second dismissals |反义词：reinstatements, hirings |名词
