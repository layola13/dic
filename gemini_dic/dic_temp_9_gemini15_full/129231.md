/nɒktɪlɪˈɔːnɪd/ | n. | 蝙蝠科动物 | A noctilionid is any member of the bat family Noctilionidae, which includes only two species: the fishing bat (Noctilio leporinus) and the lesser fishing bat (Noctilio albiventris).  These bats are characterized by their unique adaptations for catching fish. |  The fishing bat uses its large feet and specialized claws to capture fish from the water's surface. | 近义词: fishing bat (less precise) | 反义词:  (no direct antonym,  consider "terrestrial mammal") | 名词
