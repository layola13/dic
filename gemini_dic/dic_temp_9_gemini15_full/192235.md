scrawniness:/ˈskrɔːnɪnəs/| n. |瘦弱；骨瘦如柴；憔悴|Extreme thinness; a state of being very thin and weak, often suggesting poor health or lack of nutrition.|The scrawniness of the stray dog was heartbreaking.|近义词：emaciation, thinness, gauntness|反义词：obesity, corpulence, stoutness
