forenotice:/ˈfɔːrˌnoʊtɪs/| n. |预告；预先通知|Prior notification or warning of something.|The company gave us forenotice of the price increase.  公司提前通知了我们价格上涨的消息。|Prior notice, advance warning|No prior notice.| noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We give forenotice of all major changes. = 我们会预先通知所有重大变化。
The company gives forenotice to its employees. = 公司会提前通知其员工。


一般过去时:
They gave us forenotice of the meeting. = 他们提前通知了我们会议。
The school gave forenotice of the holiday. = 学校提前通知了假期。


现在完成时:

The management has given forenotice about the restructuring. = 管理层已经预先通知了重组事宜。


过去完成时:
