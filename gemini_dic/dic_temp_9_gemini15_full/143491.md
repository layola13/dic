outgleaming:/ˌaʊtˈɡliːmɪŋ/| v.|胜过；超过（在光彩或辉煌方面）|To surpass in brightness or splendor; to excel.|The newly renovated building outgleamed all the others on the street. 新装修的建筑物的光彩胜过街上的其他建筑物。|近义词：outshine, outshine, surpass, excel|反义词：be eclipsed by, be overshadowed by
