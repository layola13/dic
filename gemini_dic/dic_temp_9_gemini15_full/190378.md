sari:/ˈsɑːriː/ | n. |纱丽，印度女子长袍 | A sari is a long piece of unstitched cloth, typically five to nine yards in length, that is draped around the body in various styles.  It is a traditional garment worn by women in the Indian subcontinent. |  She wore a beautiful silk sari to the wedding. 她穿着一条美丽的丝绸纱丽去参加婚礼。  |  dhoti (for men),  dress, gown |  — |  n.
