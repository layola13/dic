mainsail:/ˈmeɪnˌseɪl/| n. |主帆|The main sail is the largest sail on a sailing vessel, typically located on the main mast.|例句：The captain adjusted the mainsail to catch the wind.船长调整主帆以捕捉风力。

The mainsail is the principal sail of a sailing vessel, usually attached to the main mast. It's crucial for propulsion and maneuvering.  Its size and shape significantly impact the boat's performance. |近义词：principal sail, largest sail|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with smaller sails like jib or spinnaker.

词源:
