sclerodermata:/ˌsklɛrəʊˈdɜːrmətə/| n. |硬皮病（皮肤硬化症）|A chronic autoimmune disease characterized by hardening and tightening of the skin and connective tissues.|Example:  She was diagnosed with scleroderma, a debilitating disease affecting her skin and internal organs. 她被诊断患有硬皮病，这是一种使她的皮肤和内脏受损的衰弱性疾病。|近义词：systemic sclerosis|反义词：无
