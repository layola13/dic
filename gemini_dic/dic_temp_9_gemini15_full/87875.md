hiding:/ˈhaɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 躲藏；隐藏；隐瞒 | To put or keep something or someone out of sight; to conceal; to keep secret.  | 例句：The cat is hiding under the table.  猫躲在桌子底下。

The children were hiding from the rain. 孩子们躲雨。


近义词：concealing, secreting, concealing, sheltering | 反义词：revealing, showing, exposing | 词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hides his feelings well. 他很擅长隐藏自己的感情。
They are hiding in the bushes. 他们在灌木丛中躲藏。


一般过去时:
She hid the letter in a drawer. 她把信藏在抽屉里。
He hid behind a tree. 他躲在一棵树后面。


现在完成时:
They have hidden the evidence. 他们已经藏匿了证据。
She has been hiding her true identity. 她一直在隐藏她的真实身份。


过去完成时:
He had hidden the money before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，他已经藏起了钱。
They had been hiding for days before they were found. 他们在被发现之前已经躲藏了几天。


将来时:
She will hide the present until his birthday. 她会把礼物藏起来直到他的生日。
They will be hiding in the shadows.  他们将躲藏在阴影中。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would hide my feelings. 如果我是你，我会隐藏我的感情。
I wish I had hidden the truth sooner. 我真希望我早点隐瞒真相。


被动语态:
The truth was hidden from him. 真相被他隐瞒了。
The treasure has been hidden for centuries. 宝藏已被隐藏了几个世纪。


疑问句:
Where is he hiding? 他藏在哪里？
Why are you hiding your face? 你为什么遮住脸？


否定句:
He didn't hide his disappointment. 他没有掩饰他的失望。
They wouldn't hide the truth from us. 他们不会对我们隐瞒真相。

词源:
源自古英语 *hyd-an，意为“隐藏”。  This root is related to words like "hide" (as in animal hide) and "hood".
