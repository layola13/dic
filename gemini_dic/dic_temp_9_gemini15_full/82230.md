grouches:/ˈɡraʊtʃɪz/| n.|脾气坏的人；抱怨的人|A grouch is a person who is habitually grumpy, irritable, and complains frequently.  They often express dissatisfaction and negativity.|He's such a grouch; he complains about everything. 他是个爱抱怨的人，什么都抱怨。|  The old man was a real grouch, always scowling and muttering to himself.  那个老头是个十足的脾气坏的人，总是愁眉苦脸，自言自语。| grumbler, complainer, curmudgeon | cheerful person, optimist | 名词
