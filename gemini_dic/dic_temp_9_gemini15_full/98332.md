insinew: /ˈɪnsaɪnjuː/ | n. |肌腱；腱；筋 | A strong, fibrous connective tissue that connects muscle to bone.  It is composed of collagen fibers arranged in parallel bundles, giving it great tensile strength.  Insinews allow muscles to exert force on bones, enabling movement. | Example: The surgeon carefully repaired the severed insinew in his hand.  外科医生仔细地修复了他手中断裂的肌腱。|近义词: tendon, sinew |反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与“脆弱的组织”对比)|名词
