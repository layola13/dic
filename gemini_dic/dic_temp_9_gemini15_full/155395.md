phased-out: /feɪzd aʊt/ | adj. | 逐步淘汰的；逐渐取消的 | Gradually removed or eliminated over a period of time.  | The old system has been phased out in favor of a new one. 旧系统已被逐步淘汰，取而代之的是一个新系统。 |近义词：eliminated, discontinued, scrapped |反义词：introduced, implemented


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is phasing out its older models. = 公司正在逐步淘汰其旧型号产品。
They are phasing out the use of plastic bags. = 他们正在逐步淘汰塑料袋的使用。


一般过去时:
The government phased out the tax on fuel. = 政府取消了燃油税。
The school phased out the old curriculum. = 学校逐步淘汰了旧的课程。


现在完成时:
They have phased out the inefficient machinery. = 他们已经淘汰了效率低下的机械。
We have phased out the use of harmful chemicals. = 我们已经逐步淘汰了有害化学品的使用。


过去完成时:
The factory had phased out the outdated equipment before the inspection. = 工厂在检查之前已经淘汰了过时的设备。
By the time the new law was passed, they had phased out the dangerous practice. = 新法通过时，他们已经停止了危险的作法。


将来时:
The company will phase out the product next year. = 公司将于明年淘汰该产品。
They will phase out the old software gradually. = 他们将逐步淘汰旧软件。


虚拟语气:
If they phased out the polluting factory, the air quality would improve. = 如果他们关闭污染工厂，空气质量将会改善。
I wish they had phased out the harmful pesticides sooner. = 我希望他们早点淘汰有害农药。


被动语态:
The old cars were phased out by the new regulations. = 旧车因新规而被淘汰。
The inefficient system is being phased out. = 效率低下的系统正在被逐步淘汰。


疑问句:
When will the outdated technology be phased out? = 过时的技术何时会被淘汰？
Have they phased out the unpopular policy yet? = 他们已经取消不受欢迎的政策了吗？


否定句:
The company hasn't phased out its older products yet. = 公司尚未淘汰其旧产品。
They didn't phase out the program as quickly as planned. = 他们的计划没有那么快地取消该项目。


PHRASE->
phase out a product = 逐步淘汰产品
phase out a program = 逐步取消计划
phase out a system = 逐步淘汰系统
phase out a policy = 逐步取消政策
phase out a process = 逐步淘汰流程
