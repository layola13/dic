chimes:/tʃaɪmz/ | n. v. |钟声；悦耳的铃声；（钟等的）鸣响；发出悦耳的声音 |  The sound of bells ringing; a set of bells; pleasant sounds; to ring, as bells do; to sound pleasantly | 例句：The church chimes rang out at noon. 中午教堂的钟声响了。The gentle chimes of the music box were soothing. 音乐盒轻柔的铃声很抚慰人心。|近义词：bells, peals, tinkling,  | 反义词：silence, hush


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The clock chimes every hour. = 钟每小时报时。
The wind chimes chime softly in the breeze. = 风铃在微风中轻轻地响着。


一般过去时:
The bells chimed merrily at the wedding. = 婚礼上钟声欢快地鸣响。
The old clock chimed twelve times. = 旧钟敲响了十二下。


现在完成时:
The church bells have chimed throughout the day. = 教堂的钟声整天都在响。
The wind chimes have chimed a beautiful melody. = 风铃奏出了优美的旋律。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
