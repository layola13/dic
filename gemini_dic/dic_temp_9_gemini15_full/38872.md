confer:/kənˈfɜːr/| vt. |授予，给予；协商，商讨；比较|To grant or bestow something, especially an honor or privilege. To have a discussion or consultation. To compare.|例句：The university conferred an honorary degree upon him. 大学授予他荣誉学位。|例句：Let's confer about the best way to proceed. 让我们协商一下最好的前进方式。|近义词：bestow, grant, discuss, consult|反义词：withhold, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They confer regularly with their colleagues. = 他们定期与同事协商。
The committee confers awards annually. = 委员会每年颁发奖项。

一般过去时:
The president conferred with his advisors before making a decision. = 总统在做决定之前与顾问们进行了协商。
She conferred the medal on the brave soldier. = 她把奖章授予这位勇敢的士兵。

现在完成时:
The university has conferred many honorary degrees this year. = 今年大学已经授予了许多荣誉学位。
They have conferred about the project for hours. = 他们就这个项目协商了几个小时。

过去完成时:
He had conferred with his lawyer before going to court. = 他在去法院之前已经与律师协商过了。
The committee had conferred the prize before the ceremony began. = 委员会在仪式开始之前已经颁发了奖项。

将来时:
We will confer on the matter tomorrow. = 我们明天将就此事进行协商。
The board will confer the title of "Employee of the Year". = 董事会将授予“年度员工”的称号。

虚拟语气:
If they conferred more often, they might reach a better solution. = 如果他们更频繁地协商，他们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish they had conferred with me before making the decision. = 我希望他们在做决定之前能和我协商一下。

被动语态:
The award was conferred upon her last night. = 奖项昨晚授予了她。
The matter will be conferred upon by the committee. = 此事将由委员会讨论。

疑问句:
Will they confer about the budget cuts? = 他们会讨论预算削减吗？
Did the committee confer the prize? = 委员会颁发了奖项吗？

否定句:
They did not confer with the stakeholders. = 他们没有与利益相关者协商。
The university will not confer the degree without proper verification. = 大学如果没有适当的验证，将不会授予学位。
