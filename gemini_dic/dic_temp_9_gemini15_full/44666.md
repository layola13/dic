crucibles:/ˈkruːsəbəlz/| n.|坩埚；严峻考验|A crucible is a container, typically made of heat-resistant material, used for melting and heating substances at high temperatures.  It can also refer to a severe test or trial. |例句：The scientist carefully poured the molten metal into the crucible.  科学家小心地将熔化的金属倒入坩埚中。|例句：The war proved to be a crucible that forged a strong national identity.  战争证明是一场锻造强大民族认同感的严峻考验。|近义词：container, test, trial, ordeal|反义词：comfort, ease, haven
