phony:/ˈfoʊni/| adj.|假的；虚伪的；矫揉造作的；冒牌的|Something that is phony is not genuine or real; it is fake or artificial. It can also refer to someone who is insincere or deceitful.  |例句：His apology seemed phony.  他的道歉听起来很虚伪。

例句：That diamond ring is phony; it's just cubic zirconia.  那枚钻戒是假的；只是锆石。


近义词：fake, artificial, sham, counterfeit, insincere, hypocritical|反义词：genuine, authentic, real, sincere, truthful|词性：adj.
