mis-frames:/ˈmɪsfreɪmz/ | v. |曲解，误解，歪曲|To misunderstand or misrepresent the meaning or significance of something.  It often implies a deliberate or unintentional distortion of facts or context.|例句：The media mis-framed the politician's statement, making it seem more controversial than it was.  媒体曲解了这位政治家的声明，使其显得比实际情况更具争议性。|  例句：He mis-framed the argument, leading to a heated debate. 他曲解了论点，导致了激烈的辩论。|近义词：misinterpret, misrepresent, distort|反义词：clarify, explain accurately, understand correctly|动词
