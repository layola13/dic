leopards:/ˈlepərdz/| n. |豹|A large feline mammal with a tawny coat marked with black spots.  Leopards are known for their agility, stealth, and powerful build. They are found in Africa and Asia.|例句：The leopard silently stalked its prey through the tall grass.  豹子悄悄地穿过高高的草地潜伏着猎物。
例句：We saw a leopard sunning itself on a rock. 我们看到一只豹子在岩石上晒太阳。|近义词：panther|反义词：无
