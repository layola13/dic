history:/ˈhɪstəri/| n.|历史，历史学|The study of past events, particularly in human affairs.  It encompasses political, social, economic, and cultural developments.|例句：We learned about ancient Roman history in class. 我们在课上学习了古罗马历史。|例句：The history of art is a fascinating subject.  艺术史是一个引人入胜的学科。|近义词：annals, chronicle, record|反义词：future, present
