prefinance:/ˈpriːfaɪnæns/| n. |预先融资，预付融资 |The act of providing funding before a project or undertaking begins.  It often involves securing financing in advance of actual needs to ensure sufficient capital is available when required. |例句：The company secured prefinance for the new factory through a loan from a private equity firm. = 这家公司通过私募股权公司的贷款获得了新工厂的预先融资。  The prefinance allowed them to start construction immediately. = 预先融资使他们能够立即开始建设。|近义词：advance funding, upfront financing, pre-funding|反义词：post-financing, delayed financing|名词
