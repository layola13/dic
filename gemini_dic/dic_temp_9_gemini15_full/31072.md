chamoix:/ʃæmɪ/| n.|羚羊皮，仿羚羊皮|A chamois is a type of soft leather traditionally made from the skin of a chamois (a type of antelope), now often made synthetically.  It is known for its softness, absorbency, and ability to polish. |例句：He used a chamois to clean the car. 他用羚羊皮擦拭汽车。

例句：She bought a chamois leather cloth to polish her silver. 她买了一块羚羊皮擦布来擦拭她的银器。


近义词：shammy (leather), suede (similar texture but different origin)|反义词：None directly, but coarse materials could be considered antonyms in terms of texture.|词性:noun
