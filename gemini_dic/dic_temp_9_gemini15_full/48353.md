decrypts:/dɪˈkrɪpts/ | vt. | 解密；破译 | To decrypt something means to convert it from a coded form into an understandable form.  This usually refers to digital information, but can also apply to codes and ciphers. | Example: The police were able to decrypt the encrypted message and arrest the criminals.  警方能够破译加密信息并逮捕罪犯。 The software decrypts the data before displaying it. 软件在显示数据之前对其进行解密。 |近义词：decode, decipher | 反义词：encrypt, encode | 动词
