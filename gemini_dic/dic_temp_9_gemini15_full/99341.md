interdebating: /ˌɪntərˈdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 相互辩论；互相争论 |  The act of engaging in a debate or discussion with another person or group.  It implies a reciprocal exchange of arguments and counterarguments.  The word is less common than "debating" and emphasizes the mutual nature of the discussion. | 例句：The two candidates spent the entire evening interdebating on the issue of tax reform. = 两位候选人整个晚上都在就税制改革问题进行辩论。  The students were interdebating the merits of different philosophical approaches. = 学生们正在互相辩论不同哲学方法的优缺点。 | 近义词：debating, arguing, discussing, disputing | 反义词：agreeing, concurring
