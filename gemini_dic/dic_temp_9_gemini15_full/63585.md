envisagement:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒmənt/| n. |展望；设想；构想|The act of envisaging; the formation of a mental image of something not yet existing or experienced.  It often implies a detailed and careful consideration of possibilities.|例句：The architect's envisagement of the new building was breathtaking. 建筑师对新建筑的设想令人叹为观止。
例句：The company's envisagement for the future includes expansion into new markets. 公司对未来的设想包括拓展新市场。|近义词：conception, vision, imagining, projection|反义词：neglect, disregard|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government envisages a bright future for the country. = 政府设想国家拥有光明的未来。
His envisagement of the project is quite detailed. = 他对项目的设想相当详细。


一般过去时:
They envisaged a successful outcome of the negotiations. = 他们设想谈判会有圆满的结果。
The artist envisaged a world of peace and harmony. = 艺术家设想了一个和平和谐的世界。


现在完成时:
She has envisaged many scenarios for her future career. = 她已经为她未来的职业生涯设想了许多方案。
We have envisaged the potential challenges and developed strategies to overcome them. = 我们已经预想到了潜在的挑战，并制定了克服这些挑战的策略。


过去完成时:
They had envisaged a much simpler process before encountering the unforeseen difficulties. = 在遇到不可预见的困难之前，他们设想了一个更简单的流程。
The team had envisaged a quicker turnaround time for the project. = 该团队曾设想项目的周转时间更快。


将来时:
They will envisage a more sustainable approach to their business practices. = 他们将设想一种更可持续的商业行为方法。
The company will envisage significant growth in the coming years. = 公司预计未来几年将取得显著增长。


虚拟语气:
If they had envisaged the risks involved, they might have acted differently. = 如果他们预想到了所涉及的风险，他们可能会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had envisaged the consequences of my actions. = 我希望我已经预想到了我行为的后果。


被动语态:
A brighter future was envisaged by the committee. = 委员会设想了一个更光明的未来。
A detailed plan was envisaged for the project implementation. = 为项目实施制定了详细的计划。


疑问句:
What do you envisage happening next? = 你设想接下来会发生什么？
What future do they envisage for the company? = 他们设想公司将来的发展前景如何？


否定句:
They did not envisage such a drastic change in the market. = 他们没有预料到市场会发生如此剧烈的变化。
He did not envisage the difficulties he would encounter. = 他没有预料到他会遇到的困难。
