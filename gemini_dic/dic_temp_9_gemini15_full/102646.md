jam-packed:/dʒæmˈpækt/| adj. |挤满的；塞满的；非常拥挤的 | Extremely full or crowded; filled to capacity.  The adjective "jam-packed" implies a degree of tightness and congestion beyond simply being full. |例句：The concert was jam-packed; we could barely move.  这场音乐会挤满了人，我们几乎动弹不得。

The train was jam-packed with commuters during rush hour.  高峰时段，火车上挤满了通勤者。


近义词：crowded, full, overflowing, congested, packed, crammed

反义词：empty, sparse, uncrowded, vacant


词性:形容词
