mountaintop:/ˈmaʊntɪntɑːp/ | n. | 山顶，山峰 | The highest point of a mountain.  A summit. | 例句：The view from the mountaintop was breathtaking.  山顶的景色令人叹为观止。

The climbers finally reached the mountaintop after days of arduous climbing. 登山者经过数日艰苦攀登后终于到达了山顶。


近义词：summit, peak, apex | 反义词：base, bottom, valley


词源：mountain (山) + top (顶部)  The word is a simple compound word, combining the noun "mountain" with the noun "top" to describe the highest point of a mountain.
