deprivations:/ˌdɛprɪˈveɪʃənz/| n.|剥夺；匮乏；丧失|The state of lacking or being deprived of something, especially something essential or necessary.  It often refers to a lack of basic necessities like food, shelter, or education.|例句：The children suffered from severe deprivations during the war.  战争期间孩子们遭受了严重的剥夺。|例句：Years of deprivations have left her emotionally scarred.  多年的匮乏使她留下了情感创伤。|近义词：lack, want, scarcity, deficiency|反义词：abundance, affluence, plenty|名词
