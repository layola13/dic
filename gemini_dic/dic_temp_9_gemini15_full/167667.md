prickle:/ˈprɪkəl/| n., v.| 刺；刺痛；发怒 | A prickle is a small, sharp point, like a thorn or a tiny sting.  It can also refer to a tingling or stinging sensation. As a verb, it means to cause this sensation. |例句：The rose bush had many prickles.  这棵玫瑰丛有很多刺。

The sweater felt prickly against my skin. 这件毛衣穿在我身上感觉扎扎的。

He felt a prickle of fear. 他感到一丝恐惧。

The news pricked his conscience. 这消息刺痛了他的良心。

近义词：thorn, spine, sting, tingling, itch |反义词：smoothness, calmness
