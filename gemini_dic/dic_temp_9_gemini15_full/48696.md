defiantness:/dɪˈfaɪəntnəs/| n. |蔑视；反抗；挑战性|The quality or state of being defiant; a bold resistance to authority or opposition.  It implies a refusal to yield or comply, often accompanied by a show of contempt or hostility.|例句：Her defiantness in the face of overwhelming odds was inspiring.  她面对巨大的困难依然反抗的精神令人鼓舞。


例句：The defiantness of the protesters surprised the police.  抗议者的反抗态度让警察大吃一惊。

近义词：rebelliousness, resistance, opposition, insubordination

反义词：compliance, obedience, submissiveness, acquiescence

词性：名词
