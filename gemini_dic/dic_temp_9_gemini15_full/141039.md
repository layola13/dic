online:/ˌɒnˈlaɪn/ /ˈɑːnlaɪn/| adj., adv., n.|在线的；在线地；在线服务|Existing or operating on the internet.  An online activity takes place on the internet, using a computer or other device connected to the network.|例句：I bought the book online. 我在网上买了这本书。
例句：She's online now. 她现在在线。

近义词：virtual, web-based, internet-based|反义词：offline, disconnected
