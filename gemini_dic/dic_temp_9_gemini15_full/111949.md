lolls:/lɒlz/| v.|懒洋洋地坐或躺；(使)松弛；|To sit, lie, or hang down in a relaxed or lazy way; to cause to become loose or slack.|例句：The dog lolls in the sun.  狗狗懒洋洋地晒着太阳。


The old man lolls in his armchair.  老人懒洋洋地坐在扶手椅上。| lounge, slump, sag| taut, tense|动词

词源:
The word "loll" ultimately derives from Old English *lollen, meaning "to hang loosely."  Its development reflects a sense of relaxed hanging or drooping.
