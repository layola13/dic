nonadjustable: /ˌnɒnəˈdʒʌstəbl/ | adj. | 不可调节的，不可调整的 |  Not capable of being adjusted or changed.  Something that is nonadjustable cannot be altered or modified in any way. |


例句：

The rent is nonadjustable for the duration of the lease. = 在租赁期内，租金是不可调整的。

This setting is nonadjustable; you cannot change it. = 此设置不可调整；你无法更改它。


近义词：fixed, inflexible, unalterable, immutable

反义词：adjustable, modifiable, changeable


词源:

* **non-**:  A prefix meaning "not" or "without."
* **adjustable**: An adjective derived from the verb "adjust," meaning "to change something slightly to make it better or more suitable."  "Adjustable" implies the capability of being changed or modified.
