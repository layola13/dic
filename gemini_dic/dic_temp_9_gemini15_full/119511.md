microphyte:/ˈmaɪkroʊfaɪt/| n. |微型植物，小型植物 |A microphyte is a small plant, especially an alga or other aquatic plant.  It is often microscopic in size and forms part of the phytoplankton.  |Example:  Many microphytes thrive in the nutrient-rich waters of estuaries.  许多微型植物在营养丰富的河口水中茁壮成长。|近义词:  small plant, alga, phytoplankton (if referring to aquatic microphytes) |反义词: macrophyte
