pyramid:/ˈpɪrəmɪd/| n. |金字塔；锥体；叠锥形物 | A pyramid is a solid geometric figure whose base is a polygon and whose sides are triangles that meet at a common point (the apex).  Pyramids can be found in nature and are often used in architecture and design.  The ancient Egyptian pyramids are a prime example of this structure. |例句：The Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.  吉萨大金字塔是古代世界七大奇迹之一。

例句：The data is presented in a pyramid structure, with the most important information at the top. 数据以金字塔结构呈现，最重要的信息位于顶部。


近义词：cone (锥体),  cone-shaped object (锥形物体)

反义词：None (没有明确的反义词)


词源:  From Late Latin *pyramis, from Greek πυραμίς (pyramís), possibly from πυρ (pyr) "fire" +  a suffix of uncertain origin; perhaps related to the fiery appearance of the sun's rays. The word may have originally referred to a fire-shaped object.
