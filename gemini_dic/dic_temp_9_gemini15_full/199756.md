sires:/ˈsaɪərz/| n.|父亲们；祖先；始祖|The word "sires" is the plural form of "sire," meaning a male parent, especially of an animal,  or a male ancestor. It can also be used figuratively to refer to the originator or creator of something.|例句：The proud sires watched their sons compete in the race.  骄傲的父亲们看着他们的儿子们在赛跑中竞争。|The stallions are the sires of many champion racehorses.  这些种马是许多冠军赛马的父亲。|近义词：fathers, ancestors, progenitors|反义词：offspring, descendants
