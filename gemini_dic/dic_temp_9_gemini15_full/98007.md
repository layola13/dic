inoperation:/ɪn ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən/| adj.|运转中；在操作中；有效；|In operation means functioning, working, or being in use.  It describes something that is currently active and performing its intended purpose.|例句：The new system is now in operation. 新系统现已投入运行。

The factory has been in operation for over 50 years. 这家工厂已经运营了50多年了。

|近义词：functioning, running, active, effective |反义词：out of operation, inactive, broken, defunct |词性：形容词
