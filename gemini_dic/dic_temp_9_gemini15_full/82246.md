groundbreaker:/ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkər/| n. |开拓者；创新者；具有开创性的事物|A groundbreaker is a person or thing that makes a significant new discovery or achievement, especially one that changes the way things are done.  It refers to someone or something that is innovative and pioneering.|例句：Marie Curie was a groundbreaker in the field of science.居里夫人是科学领域的开拓者。

The new software is a groundbreaker in the industry. 这款新软件是业界的开拓性产品。|近义词：pioneer, innovator, trailblazer, revolutionary|反义词：follower, imitator, traditionalist
