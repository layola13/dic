lithological:/ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|岩性的；岩石学的| relating to the lithology of rocks; the physical characteristics of a rock.  The study of lithological characteristics helps geologists understand the formation and history of rocks.|例句：The lithological analysis revealed a high proportion of sandstone in the formation. 岩性分析表明该地层中砂岩比例很高。

The lithological map showed a clear boundary between the two rock types. 岩性图显示了两种岩石类型之间的清晰界限。|近义词：petrographic, petrological|反义词：N/A

词源:
From  lithos (λίθος) (Greek for "stone") + -logical (from -logy, meaning "the study of").  It combines the Greek root for "stone" with the suffix "-logical," indicating a relationship to the study of stones or rocks.
