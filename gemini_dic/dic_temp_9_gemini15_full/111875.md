logoed:/ˈloʊɡoʊd/| adj.|印有标志的；带有商标的|Bearing a logo; adorned with a company's or organization's emblem.|例句：The logoed shirts were a popular giveaway at the conference.  印有标志的衬衫在会议上很受欢迎。|例句：She proudly wore her logoed jacket, showing her support for the team. 她自豪地穿着印有队徽的夹克，表达了她对球队的支持。|近义词：branded, marked, imprinted|反义词：unbranded, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company logoed all of its products. = 公司在其所有产品上都印上了标志。
He logoed his new design on the t-shirt. = 他在新设计的T恤衫上印上了他的标志。

一般过去时:
They logoed the hats before shipping them. = 他们在发货前在帽子上印上了标志。
The artist logoed each painting with a small signature. = 艺术家在每幅画上都印上了一个小小的签名。

现在完成时:
The marketing team has logoed all the marketing materials. = 营销团队已经将所有营销材料都印上了标志。
She has logoed every single item in her store. = 她已经在她商店里的每一件商品上都印上了标志。

过去完成时:
They had logoed the brochures before the meeting started. = 他们在会议开始之前就已经在宣传册上印上了标志。
The company had logoed the packaging long before the product launch. = 公司早在产品发布之前就在包装上印上了标志。

将来时:
We will logo all the new products next week. = 我们下周将在所有新产品上印上标志。
The designer will logo the bags with a unique pattern. = 设计师将在包上印上独特的图案。

虚拟语气:
If they logoed the product differently, it might sell better. = 如果他们采用不同的方式在产品上印上标志，它可能会卖得更好。
I wish they had logoed the shirts with a more subtle design. = 我希望他们在衬衫上印上更精细的设计。

被动语态:
The merchandise was logoed by a professional printing company. = 商品是由一家专业的印刷公司印上标志的。
All the promotional items are being logoed for the upcoming event. = 所有促销用品正在为即将到来的活动印上标志。

疑问句:
Has the product been logoed yet? = 产品已经印上标志了吗？
Are they going to logo the packaging with the new logo? = 他们打算用新的标志在包装上印上标志吗？

否定句:
The company did not logo the pens because of a printing error. = 公司由于印刷错误而没有在笔上印上标志。
They haven't logoed the bags yet; they're waiting for the final design. = 他们还没有在包上印上标志；他们正在等待最终的设计。
