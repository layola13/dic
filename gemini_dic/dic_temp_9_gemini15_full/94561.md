impotent:/ˈɪmpəʊtənt/ | adj. |无力的；无效的；虚弱的 | Lacking power or ability; weak.  It can refer to physical weakness, lack of effectiveness, or inability to act. |例句：He felt impotent in the face of such overwhelming grief. 他面对如此巨大的悲伤感到无力。

例句：The medicine proved impotent against the disease. 这药对这种病无效。


近义词：powerless, ineffective, weak, feeble

反义词：powerful, effective, strong, potent


词源:  From Old French *impotent, from Latin impotens, from in- "not" + potens "powerful".
