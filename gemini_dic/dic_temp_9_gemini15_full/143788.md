outmuscles:/ˌaʊtˈmʌs(ə)l/| vt.|胜过，超过(尤指力量)|To surpass or overcome (someone or something), especially in terms of strength or power. |例句：The incumbent outmuscled his challenger in the debate.  现任者在辩论中胜过了他的挑战者。|例句：The new technology outmuscles the old system in terms of efficiency. 新技术在效率方面超过了旧系统。|近义词：outpower, overpower, surpass, exceed|反义词：be outmatched, be overpowered


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outmuscles his opponents in every match. 他在每一场比赛中都胜过对手。
The company outmuscles its competitors in the market. 这家公司在市场上胜过了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outmuscled her brother in the arm wrestling contest. 她在一场臂力比赛中胜过了她的哥哥。
The team outmuscled their rivals to win the championship.  这支队伍以强大的实力战胜对手，赢得了冠军。

现在完成时:
He has outmuscled many competitors throughout his career. 他在职业生涯中已经胜过了许多竞争对手。
Our company has outmuscled the others in securing this contract. 我们公司在获得这份合同方面胜过了其他公司。

过去完成时:
They had outmuscled their opponents before the final round. 在决赛之前，他们就已经胜过了对手。
The government had outmuscled the opposition to pass the bill. 政府已经压制了反对派，通过了法案。

将来时:
She will outmuscle her opponent in the upcoming election. 她将在即将到来的选举中击败她的对手。
We believe our product will outmuscle the competition in terms of innovation. 我们相信我们的产品在创新方面将胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outmuscle his rivals. 如果他训练更努力，他就能战胜对手。
If the company had invested more, it would have outmuscled its competitors. 如果公司投入更多资金，它就能战胜竞争对手。

被动语态:
His opponent was outmuscled in the final match. 他的对手在决赛中被击败了。
The old system was outmuscled by the new technology. 旧系统被新技术淘汰了。

疑问句:
Did she outmuscle him in the weightlifting competition?  她在举重比赛中战胜他了吗？
Will our company outmuscle the others in the bidding process? 我们公司会在竞标过程中胜过其他公司吗？

否定句:
He didn't outmuscle his opponent in the first round. 他在第一轮没有战胜他的对手。
The smaller company couldn't outmuscle the larger corporation. 这家较小的公司无法战胜这家较大的公司。
