nicotiana:/ˌnɪkəˈtiːənə/| n. |烟草属植物|Nicotiana is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.  The genus includes many species, some of which are cultivated for their leaves, which are used to produce tobacco.  The plants are generally known for their trumpet-shaped flowers and often have a strong, distinctive scent.|Example: Many varieties of Nicotiana are grown as ornamentals for their attractive flowers.  许多品种的烟草因其美丽的花朵而被种植为观赏植物。|近义词：tobacco plant (在特定语境下)|反义词：无
