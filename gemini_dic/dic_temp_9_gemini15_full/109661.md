词源:
From Modern Latin *leukaemia, from Greek λευκός (leukós, "white") + αἷμα (haima, "blood").  The "-emia" suffix indicates a blood condition.  The spelling "leukaemia" is more common in British English, while "leukemia" is more common in American English.
