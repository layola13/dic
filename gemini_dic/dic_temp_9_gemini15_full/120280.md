miniaturist:/ˌmɪniətʃərɪst/| n. |微型画家；细微之处专家|A miniaturist is an artist who creates miniature paintings, especially portrait miniatures.  They are skilled in meticulous detail and often work in watercolor or enamel on a small scale, typically ivory or vellum.  |例句：The museum is showcasing a remarkable collection of works by 18th-century miniaturists.  这个博物馆正在展出18世纪微型画家的杰出作品集。

例句：His attention to detail is that of a true miniaturist, even in his larger works. 他对细节的关注如同真正的微型画家一样，即使在他较大的作品中也是如此。|近义词：miniature painter, artist, illustrator|反义词：muralist (mural painter), large-scale artist|词性：名词
