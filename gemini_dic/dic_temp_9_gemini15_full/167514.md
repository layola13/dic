previously:/ˈpriːviəsli/| adv.|先前地，以前地，早先|Previously, I worked as a teacher.|以前，我做过老师。|近义词：earlier, formerly, before|反义词：later, subsequently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He usually arrives previously to the meeting. = 他通常在会议之前到达。
She previously attends all the meetings. = 她以前参加过所有的会议。


一般过去时:
They previously lived in London. = 他们以前住在伦敦。
I previously had a different job. = 我以前有份不同的工作。


现在完成时:
He has previously mentioned this issue. = 他以前曾提到过这个问题。
She has previously worked with this team. = 她以前曾与这个团队合作过。


过去完成时:
I had previously informed him of the changes. = 我以前已经通知他这些变化了。
They had previously discussed the project. = 他们以前已经讨论过这个项目了。


将来时:
He will previously have finished the report. = 他将在之前完成报告。（This implies a prior action before a future event.  The phrasing is a bit awkward in simple future tense.  It's more common to use present perfect or past perfect in this context.)
She will previously have made the arrangements. = 她将在之前做好安排。（Similar to the above, a more natural phrasing would be present perfect or past perfect.)


虚拟语气:
If I had previously known, I would have acted differently. = 如果我以前知道的话，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had previously understood the implications. = 我希望我以前理解了这些含义。


被动语态:
The problem was previously addressed by the committee. = 这个问题以前已被委员会处理过。
The report was previously submitted to the board. = 报告以前已提交给董事会。


疑问句:
Had you previously met him? = 你以前见过他吗？
Were you previously aware of the situation? = 你以前知道这种情况吗？


否定句:
I had not previously considered that option. = 我以前没有考虑过那个选择。
She did not previously work in this field. = 她以前不从事这个领域的工作。


PHRASE->
as previously mentioned = 如前所述

not previously known =  先前未知
