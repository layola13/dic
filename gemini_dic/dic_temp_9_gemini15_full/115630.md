marching:/ˈmɑːrtʃɪŋ/| v. |行进，前进，游行|The act of walking with a steady, rhythmic step, often in a group.  It can refer to military processions, parades, or simply walking purposefully. |例句：The soldiers were marching in formation.  士兵们正列队行进。

The band was marching down the street. 乐队正在街上游行。|近义词：walking, parading, proceeding, advancing|反义词：standing still, halting, retreating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are marching in the parade. = 他们在游行队伍中行进。
The soldiers march every morning. = 士兵们每天早上都行军。

一般过去时:
We marched for miles that day. = 那天我们行军了好几英里。
The protesters marched to the government building. = 抗议者们游行到政府大楼。

现在完成时:
They have marched for peace many times. = 他们多次为和平游行。
The army has marched across the desert. = 军队已经穿越沙漠行军。

The soldiers had marched all night. = 士兵们整夜都在行军。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
