overbill:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪl/| vt.|超额收费；开出过高的账单|To charge (someone) more than is actually due; to send an invoice for a higher amount than the actual cost.|例句：The hotel overbilled me for the room service.  酒店房间服务费向我多收了钱。|例句：The contractor overbilled the client by $5,000. 承包商向客户多收了5000美元。|近义词：overcharge, inflate, exaggerate|反义词：underbill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The restaurant overbills customers regularly. = 这家餐馆经常向顾客超额收费。
They overbill for their services. = 他们为他们的服务收取过高的费用。


一般过去时:
The mechanic overbilled me for the repairs. = 修理工在维修方面向我多收了钱。
The company overbilled its clients last quarter. = 上个季度，该公司向客户超额收费。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
