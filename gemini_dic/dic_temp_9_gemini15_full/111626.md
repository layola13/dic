locality:/ləʊˈkælɪti/| n. |地方；位置；地区 |A locality is a particular area or place. It refers to a specific geographic location, often smaller than a city or town, and can be characterized by its unique features, environment, or community.  |例句：The police are investigating the locality where the crime occurred.  警方正在调查犯罪发生的地点。

例句：The unique plants and animals of this locality are under threat. 这个地区独特的动植物面临威胁。


近义词：area, region, district, neighborhood, place, vicinity |反义词：widespread, universal, global


词源:  The word "locality" comes from the Latin word "localitas," meaning "localness" or "belonging to a place."  This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "locus," meaning "place."  The suffix "-ity" is a common English suffix that forms nouns denoting a quality or state.
