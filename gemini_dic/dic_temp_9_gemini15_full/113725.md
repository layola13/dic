macrocystis: /ˌmækrəˈsɪstɪs/ | n. |巨藻属 | A genus of large brown algae (seaweeds) found in cold, nutrient-rich waters, known for their long, buoyant stipes and extensive kelp forests.  They are among the largest and fastest-growing organisms in the world. | Example: Macrocystis pyrifera, commonly known as giant kelp, forms extensive underwater forests that provide habitat for a variety of marine life. 巨藻，俗称巨型海带，形成广阔的海底森林，为各种海洋生物提供栖息地。|近义词: kelp (general term)|反义词: None (no direct antonym)|名词
