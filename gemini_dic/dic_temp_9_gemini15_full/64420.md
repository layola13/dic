ergonomics:/ˌɜːɡəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. |人体工程学 |The applied science of equipment design, as for the workplace, intended to maximize productivity by minimizing operator fatigue and discomfort. 人体工程学是一门应用科学，它致力于设计设备（例如工作场所的设备），通过最大限度地减少操作员的疲劳和不适来最大限度地提高生产力。|Example:  Good ergonomics in the workplace can reduce the risk of repetitive strain injuries. 良好的工作场所人体工程学可以降低重复性劳损的风险。|近义词：human factors, human engineering |反义词：None
