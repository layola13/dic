dumb-asses:/ˈdʌmˈæsɪz/| n. |笨蛋们，傻瓜们 |A derogatory term referring to a group of stupid or foolish people.  It's a plural form combining "dumb" and "ass."  The word "ass" is a vulgar term for a donkey, often used as a derogatory term for a person. |例句：Those dumb-asses ruined the whole project.  那些笨蛋毁了整个项目。


Those dumb-asses don't understand the instructions. 那些笨蛋不明白指示。


近义词：idiots, morons, fools, jackasses |反义词：geniuses, intellectuals, wise men
