procity: /prəˈsɪsəti/ | n. |互惠性，相互性|Reciprocity refers to the practice of exchanging things with others for mutual benefit, especially privileges, concessions, or information.  It implies a two-way relationship where actions or benefits are exchanged.  It often involves a sense of fairness and balance. |例句：The treaty was based on the principle of reciprocity. 这项条约是基于互惠的原则。

The success of the project depends on reciprocity between the participating organizations.  这个项目的成功取决于参与组织之间的互惠。|近义词：mutuality, interdependence, exchange,  reciprocality |反义词：unilateralism, one-sidedness
