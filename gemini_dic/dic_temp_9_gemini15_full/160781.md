positioner:/pəˈzɪʃənər/ | n. |定位器，定位装置 | A device or system used to accurately place or hold something in a desired position.  It can refer to mechanical devices used in manufacturing,  electronic components in circuits, or even software algorithms. | Example: The robotic arm uses a precise positioner to weld the car parts together.  机器人手臂使用精确的定位器将汽车零件焊接在一起。|近义词：placer, locator, adjuster | 反义词：displacer, remover
