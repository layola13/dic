mesmerizers:/ˈmɛzməraɪzərz/| n.|催眠者；迷住者|People or things that mesmerize.  Someone or something that mesmerizes is captivating or enchanting, holding someone's attention completely.|The street performer's mesmerizing act captivated the entire crowd. 街头艺人的催眠表演迷住了全场观众。|近义词：hypnotists, captivators, enchanters|反义词：bore, repellant
