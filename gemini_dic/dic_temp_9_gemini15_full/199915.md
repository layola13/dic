sixty:/ˈsɪksti/ | adj. |六十，六十个|Sixty is a cardinal number representing the quantity of sixty. It is used to quantify objects, people, or abstract concepts.  It is also used in expressions of time and measurement. | He is sixty years old. 他六十岁了。 There are sixty minutes in an hour. 一小时有六十分钟。|近义词：60, threescore|反义词：zero, none
