lexicologies:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːlədʒiz/ | n. |词典学；词汇学 |The study of dictionaries and lexicography. It encompasses the principles and practices involved in compiling, editing, and using dictionaries. Lexicologies also explores the history and evolution of dictionaries, as well as their role in language and culture. |Example:  Her dissertation focused on the lexicologies of early modern English dictionaries.  她的论文重点研究了早期现代英语词典的词典学。|近义词：lexicography, dictionary studies |反义词：无
