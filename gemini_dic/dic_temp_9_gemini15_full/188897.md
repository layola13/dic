rustled:/ˈrʌs(ə)ld/| v.|沙沙作响，发出沙沙声 |To make a soft, crackling sound like that of dry leaves or silk.|The leaves rustled in the breeze.  树叶在微风中沙沙作响。The silk dress rustled as she moved.  她一动，丝绸衣服就发出沙沙声。|whispered, murmured|silent, quiet|动词

The leaves rustle in the wind. = 树叶在风中沙沙作响。
