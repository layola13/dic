guillemots:/ˈɡɪləmɒts/| n. |海雀属鸟类|A guillemot is any of several species of alcids, seabirds characterized by their black and white plumage, upright posture, and excellent swimming abilities. They are found in the northern hemisphere, nesting on cliffs and rocky shores.  Guillemots are known for their diving skills and their fish-based diet.|Example:  The cliffs were teeming with guillemots, their white breasts contrasting sharply with the dark rock.  悬崖上挤满了海雀，它们白色的胸脯与黑暗的岩石形成鲜明对比。|近义词：auk, murre |反义词：无
