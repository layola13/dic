post-axial:/ˌpoʊstˈæksɪəl/| adj.|轴后(的)|Situated or occurring beyond the axis of a limb or body part.  Describes structures located on the little finger or toe side of the hand or foot. |The post-axial border of the hand is the ulnar side (little finger side). 手的轴后缘是小指侧。 The sixth digit is a post-axial polydactyly.第六指是轴后多指畸形。|近义词：ulnar (for hand), fibular (for foot)|反义词：pre-axial
