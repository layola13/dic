commercialists: /kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪsts/ | n. | 商业主义者；唯利是图者 | People primarily concerned with profit and commerce, often at the expense of other considerations. They may prioritize financial gain over ethical concerns, social responsibility, or environmental protection.  |  The commercialists pushed for the deal, ignoring the potential environmental damage. 商业主义者们推动达成这笔交易，无视潜在的环境破坏。 |近义词：mercantilists, profiteers, businesspeople (in a negative context) |反义词：altruists, idealists, philanthropists
