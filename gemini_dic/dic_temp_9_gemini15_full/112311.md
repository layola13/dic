lordliness:/ˈlɔːdlɪnəs/ | n. | 尊贵；高傲；傲慢 | The quality or state of being lordly;  a lofty or haughty manner; arrogance.  It suggests a superior attitude and often implies condescension or disdain.  |例句：His lordliness was evident in his condescending tone.  他的尊贵在他的居高临下的语气中显而易见。

例句：The lordliness of his manner alienated many of his former friends. 他高傲的态度疏远了他许多以前的朋友。


近义词：haughtiness, arrogance, condescension, superiority, pride


反义词：humility, modesty, meekness, deference


词源：源于古英语 *hlāfordlīċnes,  来自 *hlāford (lord) + -līċnes (ness).  The word reflects the social hierarchy and power dynamics of feudal societies.
