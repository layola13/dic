It means strong, healthy, and vigorous; able to withstand hardship.  |  The robustous oak tree weathered the storm. 这棵强壮的橡树经受住了暴风雨的考验。  | 近义词：robust, sturdy, strong, vigorous | 反义词：weak, frail, delicate
