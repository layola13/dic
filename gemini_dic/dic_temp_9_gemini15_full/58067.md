droners:/ˈdroʊnərz/| n.|无人机操作员；嗡嗡作响的人；沉闷乏味的人|People who operate drones;  also refers to someone who drones on and on in a monotonous way, or something that makes a droning sound.|例句：The droners were carefully piloting their drones over the disaster area. 无人机操作员正在灾区小心地操控他们的无人机。 The lecture was so boring, it was filled with droners.  这演讲太无聊了，全是些让人昏昏欲睡的人。|近义词：UAV operators, bores, hummers|反义词：lively speakers, engaging presenters
