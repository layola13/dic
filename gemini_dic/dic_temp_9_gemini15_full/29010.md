cartloads:/ˈkɑːrtloʊdz/| n.|车载货物；大量的|A large quantity of something, typically transported in carts.  It implies a significant volume.|例句：They brought cartloads of sand to the construction site.  他们把车载的沙子运到了建筑工地。

例句：The volunteers hauled cartloads of donations to the charity. 志愿者们把大量的捐赠物运送到慈善机构。


近义词：loads, quantities, heaps, masses


反义词：少量 a small amount


词性：名词
