forsake:/fərˈseɪk/ | vt. |放弃；抛弃；遗弃 | To abandon or leave entirely; to renounce or give up.  |例句：He forsook his family and his country. 他抛弃了他的家人和祖国。

例句：She had to forsake her career to raise her children. 她不得不放弃事业来抚养孩子。


近义词：abandon, desert, relinquish, renounce, quit | 反义词：retain, keep, cherish, embrace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forsakes his principles. 他放弃了他的原则。
They forsake their homeland. 他们抛弃了他们的家园。


一般过去时:
She forsook her dreams. 她放弃了她的梦想。
He forsook his old friends. 他抛弃了他的老朋友。


现在完成时:
I have forsaken my bad habits. 我已经改掉了我的坏习惯。
They have forsaken their faith. 他们已经放弃了他们的信仰。


过去完成时:
She had forsaken her ambition. 她已经放弃了她的雄心壮志。
He had forsaken his family long ago. 他很久以前就抛弃了他的家人。


将来时:
I will forsake my comfortable life. 我将放弃我舒适的生活。
They will forsake their old ways. 他们将放弃他们旧有的方式。


虚拟语气:
If I were to forsake my job, I would be financially ruined. 如果我放弃工作，我会经济破产。
I wish I had not forsaken my studies. 我真希望我没有放弃我的学业。


被动语态:
His principles were forsaken. 他的原则被抛弃了。
His family was forsaken by him. 他的家人被他抛弃了。


疑问句:
Would you forsake your friends for wealth? 你会为了财富而抛弃你的朋友吗？
Will they forsake their beliefs? 他们会放弃他们的信仰吗？


否定句:
He did not forsake his duty. 他没有放弃他的职责。
She would not forsake her child. 她不会抛弃她的孩子。


PHRASE->
forsake all others = 抛弃所有其他人
forsake hope = 放弃希望
forsake oneself = 放纵自己
forsake the world = 隐居
forsake one's faith = 放弃信仰


词源:
Old English *forsacan, from for- (“completely”) + sacan (“to strive, contend, dispute”)  The original sense was to renounce, deny, or reject something.  Over time, the meaning shifted to the more common understanding of abandoning or leaving something or someone behind.
