naught:/nɔːt/ | n. adj. | 零；无价值之物；坏蛋；顽童 |  nothing; zero; worthless; mischievous child | The experiment came to naught.  实验以失败告终。 | 近义词：nothing, zero, zilch | 反义词：something, everything, value


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is naught but a troublemaker. = 他不过是个捣乱分子。
The sum of naught and one is one. = 零加一等于一。


一般过去时:
His efforts came to naught. = 他的努力付诸东流。
The plan was naught from the start. = 这个计划从一开始就毫无价值。


现在完成时:
All my work has come to naught. = 我所有的努力都白费了。
Their hopes have been brought to naught. = 他们的希望破灭了。


过去完成时:
The negotiations had come to naught before he arrived. = 他到达之前，谈判就已经破裂了。
Everything they had worked for had come to naught. = 他们为之奋斗的一切都化为乌有。


将来时:
This plan will come to naught if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，这个计划将会失败。
All their efforts will come to naught. = 他们所有的努力都将白费。


虚拟语气:
If he had tried harder, it wouldn't have come to naught. = 如果他更努力一些，就不会失败了。
I wish my efforts hadn't come to naught. = 我真希望我的努力没有白费。


被动语态:
His hopes were brought to naught by the news. = 这个消息使他的希望破灭了。
Their plans were reduced to naught by the storm. = 他们的计划被暴风雨摧毁了。


疑问句:
Is this naught but a dream? = 这仅仅是一个梦吗？
Is all this effort for naught? = 所有的努力都白费了吗？


否定句:
It is not naught; it has some value. = 它并非一无是处；它有一定的价值。
He is not naught but a good boy. = 他不仅仅是个好孩子。


PHRASE->
come to naught =  失败；落空
set at naught = 蔑视；忽视
bring to naught = 使失败；使无效
make naught of = 不理会；忽视
think naught of = 不在乎；轻视
