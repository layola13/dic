infotainments:/ˌɪnfoʊˈteɪnmənts/| n. |信息娱乐 |Information and entertainment combined, typically in media.  This term refers to content that blends factual information with entertainment value, aiming to inform and entertain simultaneously. For example, a news program with humorous segments or a documentary with engaging storytelling techniques would be considered infotainment. |Example:  The news channel's new morning show is a prime example of infotainment, blending news headlines with lighthearted interviews and comedic sketches.  = 新闻频道的早间新节目是信息娱乐的典型例子，它将新闻头条与轻松愉快的采访和喜剧小品融合在一起。


The rise of infotainment is partly attributed to increased competition among news providers, where the need to attract a wider audience necessitates a move away from purely hard news.  = 信息娱乐的兴起部分归因于新闻提供商之间竞争的加剧，吸引更广泛受众的需求使得单纯的硬新闻报道逐渐减少。|近义词：edutainment, entertainment,  educational entertainment |反义词：pure news, hard news
