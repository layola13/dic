gopro:/ˈɡoʊproʊ/| n. |GoPro相机；GoPro公司|A GoPro is a small, rugged action camera manufactured by GoPro, Inc.  It's known for its ability to capture high-quality video and photos in extreme conditions.  The brand name also refers to the company itself.| Example: I mounted my GoPro to my helmet and filmed the entire mountain bike ride. 我把GoPro装在头盔上，拍下了整个山地自行车骑行过程。|近义词：action camera, sports camera|反义词：N/A|名词
