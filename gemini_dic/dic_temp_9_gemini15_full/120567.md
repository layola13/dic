mired:/ˈmaɪərd/| adj.| 困在…中；深陷于…；泥泞的 |  Stuck or entangled in something, typically something unpleasant or undesirable; covered in mud. | 例句：The project is mired in delays. 项目进展受阻。 The car was mired in mud. 汽车陷进了泥里。 |近义词：bogged down, stuck, hampered, entrapped |反义词：free, unhindered, clear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is mired in bureaucracy. = 这个项目深陷官僚主义之中。
His reputation is mired in scandal. = 他的名声深陷丑闻之中。


一般过去时:
The troops were mired in the swamp. = 部队陷入了沼泽。
The negotiations were mired in disagreements. = 谈判因为分歧而陷入僵局。


现在完成时:
The economy has been mired in recession for years. = 经济多年来一直深陷衰退之中。
The investigation has been mired in controversy. = 调查一直争议不断。


过去完成时:
The company had been mired in debt for many years before it finally went bankrupt. = 公司在最终破产前多年来一直债务缠身。
The writer had been mired in writer's block for months before completing his novel. = 作家在完成小说之前，几个月都处于创作瓶颈期。


将来时:
The construction project will be mired in legal challenges for months. = 建设项目几个月内都将面临法律挑战。
Unless the issues are resolved promptly, the negotiations will be mired in further disputes. = 除非迅速解决这些问题，否则谈判将陷入进一步的争议。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't intervened, the country would have been mired in civil war. = 如果政府不干预，这个国家就会陷入内战。
If he hadn't been so stubborn, he wouldn't have been mired in this predicament. = 如果他不那么固执，他就不会陷入这种困境。


被动语态:
The investigation was mired in secrecy. = 调查被蒙上了一层神秘面纱。
The discussions were mired by conflicting interests. = 讨论因利益冲突而受阻。


疑问句:
Is the company still mired in financial difficulties? = 公司仍然深陷财务困境吗？
Are the peace talks mired in irreconcilable differences? = 和谈是否因无法调和的分歧而陷入僵局？


否定句:
The project was not mired in delays, it was completed on time. = 项目没有延误，而是按时完成的。
His career was not mired in scandal, he maintained a clean reputation. = 他的职业生涯没有受到丑闻的影响，他保持着良好的声誉。


PHRASE->
be mired in debt  = 负债累累
be mired in controversy = 陷入争议
mired in difficulties = 陷入困境
mired in poverty = 贫困潦倒
mired in uncertainty = 陷入不确定性
