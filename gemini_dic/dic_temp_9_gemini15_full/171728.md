pump-up: /pʌmp ʌp/ | vt. n.|  使兴奋；使充满活力；充气；(肌肉)增大 | To fill something with air or liquid, or to make someone feel more energetic or confident. |  例句：He pumped up his bicycle tires before the race. 他在比赛前给自行车轮胎充气。  |  例句：The coach gave a pump-up speech to motivate the team. 教练发表了一番鼓舞人心的讲话来激励球队。 | 近义词：inflate, energize, boost, inspire | 反义词：deflate, discourage, depress | 动词
