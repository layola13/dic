mispronunciation:/ˌmɪsprəˌnʌnsɪˈeɪʃən/ | n. |发音错误，误读|The act of pronouncing a word incorrectly; an instance of incorrect pronunciation.|例句：His mispronunciation of the word "nuclear" caused some confusion.  他的“核”字发音错误引起了一些混乱。

例句：The student's frequent mispronunciations hindered her communication. 学生频繁的误读妨碍了她的交流。


近义词：incorrect pronunciation, malapropism | 反义词：correct pronunciation


词源: mis- (错误地) + pronunciation (发音)  The prefix "mis-" indicates something done wrongly or badly.  "Pronunciation" comes from the verb "pronounce," meaning to articulate words.  Therefore, "mispronunciation" literally means "incorrect pronunciation."
