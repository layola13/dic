sciascope:/ˈsaɪəskoʊp/| n. |阴影镜，暗箱|A sciascope is an optical device that projects images onto a screen using a pinhole or lens, similar to a camera obscura. It's often used for educational or entertainment purposes to demonstrate optical principles.|例句：The children were fascinated by the sciascope's ability to project images of the outside world onto the wall. 孩子们对阴影镜能够将外部世界投射到墙上的能力感到着迷。|近义词：camera obscura,  pinhole camera|反义词：None|名词
