psychoanalysing: /ˌsaɪkoʊənəˈlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 心理分析 | The process of interpreting thoughts, feelings, and behaviors from a psychoanalytic perspective.  It involves exploring the unconscious mind to understand the root causes of psychological issues. | 例句：The therapist spent several sessions psychoanalysing the patient's dreams.  治疗师花了几个疗程来分析病人的梦。

The therapist is psychoanalysing her patient's childhood experiences to understand their current anxieties. 治疗师正在分析病人的童年经历以了解他们目前的焦虑。


近义词：analyzing, interpreting, probing


反义词：ignoring, overlooking


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He psychoanalyses his patients carefully. 他仔细地分析他的病人。
She psychoanalyses her own behaviour regularly. 她定期分析自己的行为。


一般过去时:
Freud psychoanalysed many famous patients. 弗洛伊德分析过许多著名的病人。
The doctor psychoanalysed the patient's symptoms. 医生分析了病人的症状。


现在完成时:

She has psychoanalysed the text for hidden meanings. 她已经分析了文本中隐藏的含义。


过去完成时:
The analyst had psychoanalysed the patient for several years. 分析师已经分析了病人好几年了。
He had psychoanalysed her dreams before reaching a conclusion. 他在得出结论之前分析了她的梦。
