hungriest:/ˈhʌŋɡriəst/| adj.| 最饥饿的 | Most hungry; experiencing the strongest feeling of hunger.  | Example: After a long hike, I was the hungriest person in the group.  徒步旅行后，我是那群人中最饿的一个。|近义词：starving, famished, ravenous | 反义词：full, satiated
