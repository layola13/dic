emplacement:/ˈæmplɛsmənt/ | n. | 位置；部署；安置 | The emplacement of the artillery was crucial to the battle's success.  火炮的部署对战斗的成功至关重要。| 近义词：position, location, placement, site | 反义词：removal, evacuation | 名词


英文解析：The word "emplacement" refers to a position or location, especially one that has been prepared or designed for a specific purpose, such as the placement of military equipment or machinery. It often implies a carefully chosen and prepared site.

例句：

The emplacement of the new statue was a matter of much debate. 新雕像的安置问题引起了很多争论。

The enemy's tank emplacements were heavily fortified. 敌人的坦克阵地设防严密。


词源：

The word "emplacement" comes from the Old French word "emplace" meaning "to place" or "to set in place".  The suffix "-ment" is a common noun-forming suffix in English and French, indicating a state, action, or result.  Therefore, "emplacement" literally means "the act of placing" or "the place where something is placed".
