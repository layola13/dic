mayapan:/maɪəˈpɑːn/| n. |玛雅潘|Mayapan was a large Maya city in the Yucatán Peninsula.  It was a significant center of power during the Late Postclassic period (1200-1450 AD).  The city's decline is attributed to various factors, including internal conflict, drought, and possibly disease.|例句：Mayapan served as the last major political center of the Maya civilization before the Spanish Conquest. 玛雅潘在西班牙征服之前是玛雅文明的最后一个主要政治中心。|近义词：Chichén Itzá (similar in scale and importance)|反义词：无|名词
