lutenist:/ˈluːtənɪst/| n. |鲁特琴演奏家|A lutenist is a musician who plays the lute, a stringed instrument.  Lutenists were particularly prominent in the Renaissance and Baroque periods. |The lutenist played a beautiful solo during the concert.  鲁特琴演奏家在音乐会上演奏了一首优美的独奏。|musician, guitarist| (no direct antonym)|名词
