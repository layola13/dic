multielements:/ˌmʌltiˈɛləmənts/| n. |多元素，多种成分|A substance or system composed of multiple elements.  This term is often used in fields like chemistry, materials science, and computer science to describe systems with multiple components. For example, a multielement alloy contains multiple metallic elements. |例句：The new alloy is a multielement material with enhanced strength and corrosion resistance.  这种新型合金是一种具有增强强度和耐腐蚀性的多元素材料。|例句：The software uses a multielement approach to data analysis. 该软件采用多元素方法进行数据分析。|近义词：composite, mixture, compound|反义词：single-element, pure
