shiny:/ˈʃaɪni/ | adj. | 光亮的；闪亮的；崭新的 | Having a bright, shining surface.  The word implies a reflection of light, often suggesting smoothness and newness. |例句：The new car had a shiny coat of paint. 这辆新车漆得光亮闪闪。

例句：Her shoes were so shiny they reflected the light.  她的鞋子光亮得能反射光线。


近义词：glossy, bright, lustrous, gleaming, polished | 反义词：dull, matte, tarnished, drab

词源:  The word "shiny" originates from the Old English word "scīnian," meaning "to shine."  This word has Germanic roots, related to words in other Germanic languages signifying brightness or shining.
