logotype:/ˈloʊɡoʊtaɪp/|/ˈlɒɡəʊtaɪp/| n. |标识；商标；徽标 |A logotype is a design or lettering that represents a company, organization, or brand.  It's often a stylized version of the company's name.  It's distinct from a logo, which can be a symbol or image. |Example: Coca-Cola's script lettering is a famous logotype.|近义词：logo, trademark, brand mark|反义词：None
