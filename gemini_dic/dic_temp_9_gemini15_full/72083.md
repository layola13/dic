floated:/ˈfloʊtɪd/ | v. |漂浮；浮动；传播；提出；发行 | to rest on or move on the surface of a liquid without sinking; to move gently and smoothly on air or water; to drift; to be or become widespread |例句：The balloon floated gently in the breeze. 气球轻轻地飘荡在微风中。
The proposal floated by the committee was rejected. 委员会提出的提案被否决了。

近义词：drift, hover, sail, circulate, propose  | 反义词：sink, plunge, submerge | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaf floats on the water.  = 叶子漂浮在水上。
The rumours float around the office. = 谣言在办公室里流传。

一般过去时:
The boat floated down the river. = 船顺流而下。
He floated the idea of a new project. = 他提出了一个新项目的想法。

现在完成时:
The news has floated across the globe. = 这则新闻已经传遍全球。
The company has floated its shares on the stock exchange. = 该公司已将其股票在证券交易所上市。

过去完成时:
The raft had floated several miles downstream before we found it. = 我们找到木筏之前，它已经在下游漂流了好几英里。
The rumours had floated around for weeks before they were confirmed. = 谣言在得到证实之前已经流传了好几周。


将来时:
The balloons will float beautifully in the sky. = 气球将在天空中漂浮得非常漂亮。
The company plans to float its IPO next year. = 公司计划明年进行首次公开募股。


虚拟语气:
If the boat floated, we could cross the river. = 如果船能漂浮起来，我们就能过河。
I wish my worries floated away like clouds. = 我希望我的烦恼像云一样飘走。

被动语态:
The proposal was floated by the chairman. = 该提案是由主席提出的。
The shares were floated on the stock market last week. = 股票上周在股市上市。

疑问句:
Does the wood float on water? = 木头会浮在水上吗？
Will the new product float successfully in the market? = 新产品会在市场上成功上市吗？

否定句:
The boat didn't float; it sank. = 船没有漂浮，它沉了。
The committee refused to float the controversial proposal. = 委员会拒绝提出有争议的提案。


PHRASE->
float an idea = 提出一个想法
float a loan = 申请贷款
float a company = 上市公司
float on air = 轻飘飘地
float through the air = 在空中飘荡
