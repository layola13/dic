centenarianism:/ˌsɛntɪˈnɛəriənɪzəm/| n. | 百岁老人现象；百岁老人文化 | The state or condition of being a centenarian;  the collective characteristics, experiences, and societal impact of centenarians.  It can also refer to the study of centenarians and the factors contributing to their longevity. | Example:  The study explored centenarianism, focusing on lifestyle factors and genetic predispositions.  百岁老人现象的研究侧重于生活方式因素和遗传倾向。  | 近义词: longevity studies, gerontology (in part) | 反义词:  premature death, infant mortality |  词性：名词
