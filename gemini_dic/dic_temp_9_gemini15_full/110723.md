liner:/ˈlaɪnər/| n. |班轮；衬里；内衬|A liner is a large passenger ship or an aircraft that travels regularly on a particular route.  It can also refer to a material used as a lining or coating.|例句：The ocean liner sailed across the Atlantic. 这艘班轮横渡大西洋。

The coat has a warm liner. 这件外套有一个保暖的内衬。|近义词：ship, vessel, lining, coating |反义词：None specifically.  The antonym depends on the context (e.g., for "lining," an antonym might be "exterior").
