slattern:/ˈslætən/| n.|邋遢的女人，不洁净的女人|A slattern is a woman who is habitually untidy or dirty in her appearance or habits.  The term carries a strong negative connotation, suggesting not only a lack of cleanliness but also a lack of care and self-respect.|The slattern sat on the park bench, her clothes stained and her hair unkempt.|近义词：slut, sloven, dowdy|反义词：neat, tidy, meticulous|名词
