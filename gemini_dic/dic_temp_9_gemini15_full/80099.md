gobbing:/ˈɡɒbɪŋ/ | v. | 吐口水；猛扔；大量涂抹 | The act of spitting or throwing something forcefully; also, the act of applying something thickly or abundantly. |例句：He was gobbing at the street. 他在街上吐口水。
The painter was gobbing the paint onto the canvas. 画家正把颜料大量涂抹在画布上。|近义词：spitting, splattering, daubing |反义词：carefully applying, delicately placing.
