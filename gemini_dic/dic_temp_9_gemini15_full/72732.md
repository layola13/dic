foes:/foʊz/| n.|敌人，对手|People or things that are opposed to or hostile to each other.  Foes can be individuals, groups, nations, or even abstract concepts.|例句：The two countries have been foes for centuries.  这两个国家几个世纪以来一直是敌人。|例句：The hero faced many foes on his quest.  这位英雄在寻宝过程中面临许多敌人。|近义词：enemies, adversaries, opponents|反义词：friends, allies
