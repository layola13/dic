quasi-necessary: /ˈkwɑːziˌnɛsəseri/ | adj. | 准必要的，近乎必要的 | Almost but not quite necessary; essential in a limited sense.  It implies a condition that is highly desirable or advantageous, though not strictly obligatory. |  例句：Regular exercise is quasi-necessary for maintaining good health.  规律的锻炼对于保持健康来说几乎是必要的。  |  例句：While not strictly required, a strong understanding of statistics is quasi-necessary for success in data science. 虽然不是严格要求，但对统计学的深入理解对于数据科学的成功来说几乎是必要的。| 近义词：essential, vital, important, crucial, highly desirable | 反义词：unnecessary, optional, dispensable
