ingratitude:/ɪnˈɡrætɪtjuːd/| n. |忘恩负义；不知好歹|The lack of gratitude; unwillingness to acknowledge benefits received.  It is a feeling or expression of not being thankful for kindness or help received. |例句：Her ingratitude towards her benefactors shocked everyone.  她的忘恩负义令所有帮助过她的人都感到震惊。

例句：His complete ingratitude after all she’d done for him was unforgivable. 他在她为他做了那么多之后表现出的完全的忘恩负义是不可原谅的。


近义词：ungratefulness, thanklessness, unappreciativeness|反义词：gratitude, thankfulness, appreciation|词性:名词
