sedilia:/sɪˈdɪliə/| n.|座位；（教堂里的）席位；（尤指教堂合唱团的）长椅| A sedilia is a set of seats, typically three, built into the wall of a church, usually in the sanctuary near the altar, for the clergy.  They are often elaborately carved and decorated.|Example: The sedilia in the medieval church were beautifully carved with floral motifs.  中世纪教堂里的席位用精美的花卉图案雕刻装饰。|近义词：seats, pews, stalls|反义词：standing room
