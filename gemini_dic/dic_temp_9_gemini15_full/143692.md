outleap:/ˈaʊtliːp/| v.|跳出，超越，超过|To jump or spring out; to surpass or exceed.|例句：The rabbit outleaped the fox. 兔子跳过了狐狸。 The company's profits outleaped expectations. 公司的利润超出了预期。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, transcend|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outleaps the hurdles with ease. 他轻松地跳过了障碍物。
The sales figures outleap those of last year. 销售额超过了去年。


一般过去时:
The athlete outleaped his rivals in the final jump. 运动员在最后一次跳跃中超过了他的对手。
The demand outleaped the supply. 需求超过了供应。


现在完成时:
The stock price has outleaped all predictions. 股价已经超过了所有预测。
She has outleaped her previous best performance. 她已经超越了她之前的最佳成绩。


过去完成时:
The team had outleaped their opponents in the first half. 球队在上半场就超过了对手。
He had outleaped his own expectations. 他已经超出了自己的预期。


将来时:
The company expects its profits to outleap last year's figures. 公司预计其利润将超过去年的数字。
We believe that technology will outleap the current limitations. 我们相信技术将超越目前的局限性。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outleaped his opponent. 如果他训练更刻苦，他可能就超过了他的对手。
If only I had outleaped my fears. 要是我能克服我的恐惧就好了。


被动语态:
His record was outleaped by a younger athlete. 他的纪录被一位年轻的运动员打破了。
The expectations were outleaped by the actual results. 预期被实际结果所超越。


疑问句:
Did he outleap the fence? 他跳过围栏了吗？
Will their performance outleap last year’s? 他们的表现会超过去年吗？


否定句:
He didn't outleap the barrier. 他没有跳过障碍。
The project didn't outleap the budget. 项目没有超出预算。
