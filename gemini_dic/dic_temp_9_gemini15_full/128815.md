nightlight:/ˈnaɪtlaɪt/| n.|夜灯|A small light left on at night, especially in a child's bedroom, to provide a faint illumination and reassurance.|例句：My son always needs a nightlight in his room.  我的儿子房间里总需要一盏夜灯。|例句：The soft glow of the nightlight calmed the baby. 夜灯柔和的光线让婴儿平静下来。|近义词：bedside lamp (if larger), dim light|反义词：bright light, sunlight|名词
