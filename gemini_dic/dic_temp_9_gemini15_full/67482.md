extra:/ˈɛkstrə/| adj., n., adv. |额外的；附加的；特级的；额外的东西；|英文解析: exceeding the usual, expected, or necessary amount or degree.  As an adjective, it describes something beyond the usual or standard quantity or quality. As a noun, it refers to something added or given in addition to what is usual or expected. As an adverb, it means to a greater extent than usual. |例句:  We need to buy extra food for the party. = 我们需要为派对买些额外的食物。

The movie had extra scenes not shown in the theatrical release. = 这部电影有一些在影院上映时没有放映的额外场景。


近义词:additional, supplementary, surplus, bonus |反义词:ordinary, usual, regular, standard
