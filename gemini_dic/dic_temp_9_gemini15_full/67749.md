eyebaths:/ˈaɪbæθz/ | n. |洗眼器；眼浴 | Eye baths are containers filled with a solution used to cleanse or soothe the eyes.  They are often used to relieve eye irritation or remove foreign objects. | Example:  She used an eyebath to rinse out the irritating dust from her eyes. 她用洗眼器冲洗掉眼睛里刺激性的灰尘。 |近义词：eye wash (less formal), eye rinse |反义词：无
