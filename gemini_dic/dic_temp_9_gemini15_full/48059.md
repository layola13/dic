declamator:/ˌdekləˈmeɪtər/| n. |朗诵者；演说家；慷慨激昂的演说者|A declamator is a person who speaks or recites something, especially in a dramatic or rhetorical manner.  They often deliver speeches or dramatic readings with emphasis and expression. |例句：The declamator's voice boomed across the hall.  朗诵者的声音在大厅里回荡。|例句：He was a powerful declamator, known for his passionate delivery. 他是一位充满力量的朗诵者，以其充满激情的表达而闻名。|近义词：orator, speaker, reciter, elocutionist |反义词：listener, audience
