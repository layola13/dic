fedoras:/fɛˈdɔːrəz/| n. |软呢帽|A fedora is a soft felt hat with a creased crown and a narrow brim.  It's typically associated with men's fashion, though women also wear them. |Example: He wore a stylish fedora tilted at a rakish angle. 他戴着一顶时尚的软呢帽，斜戴着。


Example:  She completed her outfit with a classic black fedora. 她用一顶经典的黑色软呢帽完成了她的装扮。


近义词：soft hat, felt hat
反义词：hard hat, top hat


词源:The word "fedora" is named after the 1890 play *Fedora* by Victorien Sardou, in which actress Sarah Bernhardt wore a similar hat.  The style became popularized in the early 20th century.
