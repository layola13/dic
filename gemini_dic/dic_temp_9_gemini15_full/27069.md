calfskin:/ˈkæfskɪn/| n. |小牛皮|Leather made from the skin of a calf. It is known for its softness, suppleness, and fine grain.  Calfskin is often used in the production of high-quality leather goods, such as shoes, handbags, and jackets.| Example: These shoes are made of genuine calfskin. 这双鞋是用真小牛皮做的。|近义词：kidskin, leather|反义词：无|名词
