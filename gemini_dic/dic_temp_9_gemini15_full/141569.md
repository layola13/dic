oppositionary: /ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 反对的；对抗的 | Relating to or characterized by opposition.  Someone who is oppositionary is actively against something or someone.  |  例句：His oppositionary stance on the new policy caused considerable friction.  他的反对新政策的立场引起了相当大的摩擦。


例句：The oppositionary movement gained momentum as more people joined their cause. 反对运动随着越来越多的人加入他们的事业而获得了动力。


近义词：antagonistic, adversarial, contrary, resistant


反义词：supportive, cooperative, agreeable, compliant


词性：形容词
