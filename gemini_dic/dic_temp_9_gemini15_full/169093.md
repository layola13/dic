progressor: /prəˈɡresər/ | n. |进步者；发展者；倡导者 | A person or thing that progresses or causes progress.  Someone or something that promotes advancement or development. |例句：He is a progressor in the field of renewable energy. 他是可再生能源领域的进步者。

例句：The new software is a progressor for the company's efficiency.  这个新软件提高了公司的效率。


近义词：pioneer, innovator, developer, promoter |反义词：regressor, obstructionist, reactionary

词性: 名词
