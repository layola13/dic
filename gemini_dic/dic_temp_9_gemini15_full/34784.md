climatography: /ˌklaɪməˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |气候学；气候记录|The scientific study of climates, including their classification, description, and analysis.  It often involves the creation of climate maps and charts to visualize climate data. |The climatography of the region shows a distinct pattern of wet and dry seasons.  该地区的 气候学显示出明显的旱季和雨季模式。| meteorology, climatology | (无明确反义词，可考虑反义词为对特定气候的否定描述)|名词
