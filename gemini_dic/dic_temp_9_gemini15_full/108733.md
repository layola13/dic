leapfrog:/ˈliːpfrɒɡ/| /ˈliːpfrɔːɡ/| n. vi. vt.|跳蛙游戏；超越；飞跃式发展|

英文解析:
As a noun, "leapfrog" refers to a children's game where players jump over each other.  As a verb, it means to surpass or overtake someone or something, often rapidly.  It can also describe a process of development that advances rapidly by skipping intermediate stages.

例句:
The children played leapfrog in the park. = 孩子们在公园里玩跳蛙游戏。

The company leapfrogged its competitors with its innovative technology. = 这家公司凭借其创新技术超越了竞争对手。


近义词:
surpass, overtake, outstrip, transcend, bypass


反义词:
lag behind, fall short, underperform


词性:
Noun, Verb


USAGE->

过去完成时:
