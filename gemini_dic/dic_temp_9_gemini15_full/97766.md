inition:/ˌɪnɪˈʃən/ | n. | 开始；启动；创立 | The act of beginning something; the start or commencement of something.  It can also refer to the process of initiating something or someone into a group or activity. | Example: The initiation of the project was delayed due to funding issues.  项目的启动因资金问题而被推迟。 The initiation ceremony for the new members was quite impressive. 新成员的入会仪式非常令人印象深刻。|近义词：beginning, commencement, start, inauguration | 反义词：conclusion, end, termination | 名词
