shaky:/ˈʃeɪki/ | adj. | 颤抖的；不稳定的；不可靠的 |  Describes something that is unsteady, trembling, or likely to fail.  It can refer to physical objects, actions, or situations. |例句：His hands were shaky as he held the glass. 他拿着杯子的手在颤抖。

例句：The shaky bridge looked unsafe. 那座摇摇晃晃的桥看起来不安全。


近义词：trembling, unsteady, unstable, rickety, wavering, weak, unreliable, flimsy

反义词：steady, stable, firm, strong, reliable, solid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old house is shaky. = 这栋老房子摇摇晃晃。
His voice is shaky with emotion. = 他的声音因激动而颤抖。


一般过去时:
My hands were shaky after the marathon. = 马拉松之后我的手一直在抖。
The ground felt shaky during the earthquake. = 地震时地面感觉摇晃。


现在完成时:
The building has been shaky since the storm. = 自从暴风雨以来，这栋建筑物就一直摇摇晃晃。
My confidence has been shaky lately. = 最近我的信心很脆弱。


过去完成时:
The ladder had been shaky before it collapsed. = 梯子在倒塌之前就已经摇晃了。
The evidence had been shaky from the beginning. = 从一开始证据就不可靠。


将来时:
The bridge will be shaky after the flooding. = 洪水过后，桥会变得摇晃。
I think the market will be shaky next year. = 我认为明年市场将会动荡不安。


虚拟语气:
If the table were shaky, I wouldn't put my drink on it. = 如果桌子摇晃，我不会把我的饮料放在上面。
I wish the ground weren't so shaky. = 我希望地面不要这么晃。


被动语态:
The table was shaken by the earthquake. = 桌子被地震震动了。(注意：此句中shaky是形容词，被动语态是shake的被动形式)
The old building was considered shaky and unsafe. = 这栋旧建筑被认为摇摇晃晃且不安全。(注意：此句中shaky是形容词，是被动语态的补充说明)


疑问句:
Is the chair shaky? = 椅子摇晃吗？
Is your handwriting shaky today? = 你今天的字迹抖吗？


否定句:
The foundation is not shaky. = 地基不晃。
The evidence was not shaky; it was conclusive. = 证据并非不可靠；它是确凿的。

词源:
The word "shaky" originates from the Middle English word "shaken," the past participle of the verb "shake."  The sense of being unsteady or trembling is inherent in the root verb.  The "-y" suffix adds an adjectival quality, meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
