misnurture:/ˌmɪsˈnɜːrtʃər/ | n. |教养不良；养育不当 | The act of bringing up or educating badly;  poor upbringing or training. It implies neglect, lack of proper guidance, or harmful influence during childhood development, leading to negative consequences in personality, behavior, or character. | Example:  His misnurture led to a life of crime.  他的教养不良导致了他的一生犯罪。| 近义词：malnutrition, ill-breeding, bad upbringing | 反义词：good upbringing, proper nurturing, excellent parenting | 名词
