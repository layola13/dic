haematoporphyrin:/ˌhiːmætəˈpɔːrfɪrɪn/| n. |血卟啉|A porphyrin derivative found in blood and other tissues; used in photodynamic therapy.  It's a photosensitizing agent that absorbs light and produces reactive oxygen species, killing cancer cells.|Example: Haematoporphyrin derivative (HpD) is a photosensitizer used in photodynamic therapy for treating various cancers.|近义词：porphyrin (a broader term)|反义词：无
