float:/floʊt/ /fləʊt/| v. n. |漂浮；浮动；飘动；（使）浮起；游荡；(股票)波动 |To move or be suspended on or near the surface of a liquid or gas; to rise to the top of a liquid; to be light enough to stay on the surface of a liquid.  |例句：The boat floated gently down the river.  船轻轻地漂流而下。
The wood floats on water. 木头漂在水上。|近义词：drift, hover, bob|反义词：sink, submerge|动词,名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The balloon floats in the air. 气球飘在空中。
The leaves float on the water. 叶子漂浮在水上。

一般过去时:
The boat floated downstream. 船顺流而下漂流。
The cork floated on the water. 软木塞漂浮在水上。

现在完成时:
The log has floated down the river. 木头已经顺流而下漂流了。
The debris has floated ashore. 残骸已经漂到岸边了。

过去完成时:
The boat had floated away before we could reach it. 我们还没来得及到达，船就已经漂走了。
The leaves had floated to the bottom of the pool. 叶子已经沉到了池底。

将来时:
The raft will float down the river. 木筏将顺流而下漂流。
The balloon will float high above the clouds. 气球将飘在云层之上。

虚拟语气:
If I had a boat, I would float down the river. 如果我有一艘船，我就会顺流而下漂流。
If the wood were lighter, it would float. 如果木头更轻，它就会漂浮。

被动语态:
The boat was floated down the river by the current. 船被水流冲向下游。
The raft was floated to the shore. 木筏被冲到岸边。

疑问句:
Does the wood float? 这木头会漂浮吗？
Will the balloon float? 气球会漂浮吗？

否定句:
The rock doesn't float. 石头不会漂浮。
The heavy box didn't float.  沉重的箱子没有漂浮起来。
