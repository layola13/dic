词源:

The word "schizophrenics" is derived from "schizophrenia," which is a combination of the Greek words "schizo-" (meaning "split") and "phren-" (meaning "mind").  The suffix "-ics" denotes "relating to" or "suffering from". Thus, the word refers to people afflicted with the condition.
