overthwart:/ˌoʊvərˈθwɔːrt/ | vt. | 阻挠；反对；使受挫折 | To obstruct or oppose (someone or something).  To cross or lie across. | 例句：The strong headwinds overthwarted their attempt to reach the island. 强烈的逆风阻碍了他们到达岛屿的尝试。

The ambitious project was overthwarted by a lack of funding.  雄心勃勃的项目由于缺乏资金而受阻。|近义词：hinder, impede, obstruct, thwart | 反义词：aid, assist, facilitate, help |
