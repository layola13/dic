heater:/ˈhiːtər/| n. |加热器，暖气机 |A device that produces heat, often used to warm a room or building.  |Example: We turned on the heater to warm up the cold room. 我们打开暖气机来暖和寒冷的房间。|近义词：radiator, warmer |反义词：cooler, air conditioner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heater warms the room efficiently. = 加热器有效地温暖了房间。
The heaters run constantly during winter. = 冬天加热器持续运转。


一般过去时:
The heater broke down last winter. = 加热器去年冬天坏了。
We used the space heater to dry our clothes. = 我们用暖风机烘干衣服。


现在完成时:
The heater has been working perfectly all season. = 加热器整个季节都运行良好。
