prattle:/ˈprætəl/| v. n.| 闲聊；喋喋不休；婴幼儿语 | To talk at length in a foolish or inconsequential way; to babble; the act of prattling; childish or foolish talk. |例句：The children prattled on about their day.孩子们喋喋不休地谈论着他们的一天。

The old woman prattled incessantly about her ailments. 老妇人没完没了地唠叨着自己的病痛。


近义词：babble, chatter, jabber, ramble |反义词：silence, quiet, thoughtful speech


USAGE->

He prattles incessantly about his achievements. 他没完没了地吹嘘自己的成就。
They prattle away happily during their tea break. 他们在茶歇期间快乐地闲聊。


一般过去时:
She prattled on about her trip to Europe. 她喋喋不休地谈论着她去欧洲的旅行。
