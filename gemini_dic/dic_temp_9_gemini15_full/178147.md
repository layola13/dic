recognizers:/ˈrekəɡnaɪzərz/ | n. |识别器，识别系统|A recognizer is a system or device that identifies or recognizes something, such as speech, patterns, or faces.  It uses algorithms and data to make these identifications.|Example: The software uses advanced speech recognizers to transcribe audio files accurately.  这个软件使用先进的语音识别器来准确地转录音频文件。|近义词：identifier, detector, analyzer |反义词：obfuscator
