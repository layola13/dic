perigean:/pɛrɪˈdʒiːən/ | adj. | 近地点的；近月点的 | Of or relating to the perigee.  The term refers to the point in the orbit of a celestial body (like a satellite or the Moon) that is nearest to the Earth. | Example: The perigean spring tide is expected to cause significant coastal flooding. 近地点的春潮预计将导致严重的沿海洪水。 | 近义词: periapsis (more general term for closest approach to a focus) | 反义词: apogean
