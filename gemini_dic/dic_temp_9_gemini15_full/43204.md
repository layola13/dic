crabfaced:/ˈkræbfeɪst/| adj.| 脾气暴躁的；面容凶恶的 | Having a bad-tempered or surly expression.  The term suggests a face resembling that of a crab, implying a pinched, scowling, or irritable appearance. |例句：He looked crabfaced when I told him the news. 他听到这个消息后，脸色很难看。

例句：The crabfaced old woman glared at the children playing in the park.  那个面容凶恶的老妇人怒视着在公园里玩耍的孩子们。


近义词：sullen, grumpy, surly, ill-tempered, cross, displeased


反义词：cheerful, pleasant, amiable, good-natured, happy


词性:形容词
