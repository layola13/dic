mountebank:/ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋk/| n. |江湖郎中，骗子，卖弄学问的人 |A mountebank is a person who sells quack medicines or performs tricks to attract attention, often using exaggerated claims or deceptive practices.  They are essentially charlatans or swindlers.  |Example: The mountebank sold his "miracle cure" to a crowd of gullible onlookers.  江湖郎中向一群轻信的旁观者兜售他的“奇迹疗法”。|近义词：charlatan, quack, imposter, fraud, swindler |反义词：legitimate practitioner, honest person |名词
