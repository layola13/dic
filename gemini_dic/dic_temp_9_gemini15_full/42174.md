coughing:/ˈkɒfɪŋ/ /ˈkɔːfɪŋ/| v.|咳嗽|The act of coughing; a forceful expulsion of air from the lungs.  It is often a symptom of illness or irritation of the respiratory tract.|例句：He was coughing continuously throughout the night. 他整夜都在不停地咳嗽。|例句：The dust made me start coughing. 灰尘呛得我开始咳嗽。|近义词：spluttering, hacking|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He coughs every morning. 他每天早上都咳嗽。
She coughs frequently during allergy season. 过敏季节她经常咳嗽。


一般过去时:
I coughed all night long. 我咳嗽了一整夜。

现在完成时:
I have coughed so much, I have a sore throat. 我咳嗽得太多了，嗓子都疼了。

过去完成时:
