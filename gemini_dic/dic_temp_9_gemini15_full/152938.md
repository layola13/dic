pediatrics'/piːdiˈætrɪks/| n. |儿科；小儿科医学|The branch of medicine dealing with the care of children.  It encompasses the medical, surgical, and developmental aspects of a child's health.|例句：She specializes in pediatrics. 她专攻儿科。|例句：The hospital has a new pediatrics ward. 这家医院有一个新的儿科病房。|近义词：pediatric medicine|反义词：geriatrics (老年医学)
