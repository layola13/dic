funiculars:/ˈfjuːnɪkjʊlərz/ | n. |缆车，索道 |A funicular is a cable railway typically used on steep inclines, often in mountainous areas, to transport passengers or goods. It uses a cable and counterweight system to move cars up and down the slope. |例句：The funiculars in San Francisco offer stunning views of the city. 旧金山的缆车提供令人惊叹的城市景观。  The mountain resort uses funiculars to transport skiers to the top of the slopes.  山区度假村使用缆车将滑雪者运送到山顶。|近义词：cable railway, incline railway |反义词：stairway, elevator (in some contexts)
