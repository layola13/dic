hagiologist:/ˌheɪdʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |圣徒传记作者；圣徒学者|A hagiologist is a scholar who studies the lives of saints and the writing of hagiographies.  |The hagiologist spent years researching the life of Saint Catherine of Alexandria.|近义词：hagiographer|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who critiques or debunks religious figures.
