operatic:/ˌɒpəˈrætɪk/| /ˌɑːpəˈrætɪk/ | adj. | 歌剧的；歌剧式的；夸张的 | Relating to opera; having the characteristics of opera.  The term can also describe something excessively dramatic or theatrical. | 例句：The singer's operatic voice filled the hall.  这位歌手歌剧般的声音充满了大厅。

The performance was operatic in its intensity.  这场演出的强度堪比歌剧。


近义词：theatrical, dramatic, melodramatic | 反义词：subdued, understated, realistic


词源:  Derived from "opera" + "-atic," a suffix forming adjectives. "Opera" itself originates from the Italian word "opera," meaning "work," specifically a musical work.
