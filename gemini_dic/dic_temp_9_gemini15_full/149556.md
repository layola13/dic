pagina:/ˈpædʒɪnə/| n.|页，版面|A pagina is a single side of a leaf of paper in a book or other document.  It's also used to refer to a screen or a web page.|例句：The important information is on page 10. 重要的信息在第10页。|例句：This webpage has many paginas of information. 这个网页有很多页的信息。|近义词：page, leaf, sheet|反义词：N/A

词源:
