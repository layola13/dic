pixel:/ˈpɪksəl/| n. |像素|A pixel is the smallest unit of a digital image.  It is a single point of color in a display or image.  Pixels are arranged in a grid to create the complete picture.  The more pixels an image has, the higher its resolution and the sharper the image appears.|例句：The image quality depends heavily on the number of pixels. 图片质量很大程度上取决于像素数量。

例句：High-resolution displays use millions of pixels to create sharp, detailed images. 高分辨率显示器使用数百万像素来创建清晰、细致的图像。|近义词：dot, point|反义词：None|名词
