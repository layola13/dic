lithic:/ˈlɪθɪk/| adj.|石器的；与石头有关的 |Relating to stone, especially in archaeology, referring to the Stone Age or stone tools.|The lithic industry of the Paleolithic period is characterized by sophisticated toolmaking techniques.  旧石器时代的石器工业的特点是精湛的工具制造技术。|近义词：petrous, stony|反义词：non-lithic
