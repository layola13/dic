multiframe: /ˈmʌltiˌfreɪm/ | n. |多帧图像；多帧画面|A multiframe image or display consists of multiple individual frames or images combined to form a larger or more complex whole.  This is common in animation, video, and some types of image processing. For example, a multiframe image could show the different phases of a process or a sequence of events. |Example: The animator created a multiframe animation to illustrate the character's complex movements. 动画师制作了一个多帧动画来演示角色的复杂动作。|近义词：composite image, multiple-frame image |反义词：single-frame image |名词
