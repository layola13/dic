onomatopoeias: /ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːəz/ | /ˌɑːnəˌmætəˈpiːəz/ | n. |拟声词；象声词 | Words that imitate the sounds they describe.  Onomatopoeia is the process of creating words that sound like what they describe.  These words are often used to add vividness and realism to writing and speech. | Example: The bees buzzed lazily around the flowers. 蜜蜂懒洋洋地嗡嗡地飞舞在花朵周围。The sizzling bacon filled the kitchen with its delicious aroma.  滋滋作响的培根让厨房充满了美味的香气。 |近义词：sound words, imitative words |反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds chirp merrily in the morning. = 鸟儿在早晨快乐地鸣叫。
The clock ticks steadily. = 时钟稳稳地走动。


一般过去时:
The owl hooted in the night. = 猫头鹰在夜里发出叫声。
The thunder crashed loudly. = 雷声巨响。


现在完成时:
The rain has pattered against the window all day. = 雨水整天都淅淅沥沥地打在窗户上。
The wind has howled fiercely throughout the night. = 风在整个晚上猛烈地呼啸。


过去完成时:
The dog had barked incessantly before I finally fell asleep. = 我最终睡着之前，狗一直在不停地叫。
The machine had clattered loudly before it finally stopped. = 机器发出很大的哐当声后才最终停止。


将来时:
The fireworks will boom spectacularly in the evening. = 烟花将在晚上壮观地轰鸣。
The train will whistle as it approaches the station. = 火车接近车站时会鸣笛。


虚拟语气:
If the storm were to roar, we'd stay inside. = 如果暴风雨咆哮，我们就待在屋里。
I wish the waves weren't crashing so loudly. = 我希望海浪不要这么大声地拍打。


被动语态:
The song was hummed softly by the singer. = 歌曲被歌手轻声哼唱。
The door was slammed shut. = 门被砰地关上了。


疑问句:
Does the engine sputter when it starts? = 发动机启动时是否会发出噼啪声？
Did you hear the cat meow? = 你听到猫叫了吗？


否定句:
The leaves didn't rustle in the still air. = 在静止的空气中，树叶没有沙沙作响。
The baby didn't gurgle happily today. = 宝宝今天没有高兴地咯咯笑。
