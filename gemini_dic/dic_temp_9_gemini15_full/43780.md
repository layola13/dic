cremated:/krɪˈmeɪtɪd/| v. (过去分词)|火化的；被火化的|To cremate someone means to burn their body after they have died, usually as a part of a funeral ceremony.  The ashes are then often kept in an urn by the family.|例句：Her grandmother was cremated and her ashes scattered at sea.  她奶奶被火化了，骨灰撒在了海里。

例句：After the funeral service, his body was cremated. 葬礼仪式结束后，他的遗体被火化了。

近义词：incinerated, burned

反义词：buried, entombed

词性：动词（过去分词）
