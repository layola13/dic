/ˈkaʊntərˌfɔːlər/| n.|反向跌落者；（尤指）股票价格反向下跌者|A counter-faller is someone or something that falls in the opposite direction to what is expected.  This term is most commonly used in the context of financial markets, referring to a stock or asset that increases in value when the overall market is falling. |Example:  Despite the overall market downturn,  that tech stock proved to be a counter-faller, increasing in value by 10%.  尽管整体市场下跌，那只科技股却成为反向跌落者，价值上涨了10%。|近义词：contrarian, outlier|反义词：follower, conformist|名词
