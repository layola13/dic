goldcrest:/ˈɡoʊldkrɛst/ | n. |金冠戴菊 |A small, active bird with a bright yellow crest on its head. It is known for its distinctive call and its habit of foraging for insects among the branches of trees.  |例句：The goldcrest is the smallest bird in Britain.  金冠戴菊是英国最小的鸟类。|例句：We spotted a goldcrest flitting through the branches of the pine tree. 我们看到一只金冠戴菊在松树枝间飞舞。|近义词：kinglet (a general term)|反义词：无 |名词
