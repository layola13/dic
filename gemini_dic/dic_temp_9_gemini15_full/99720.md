interlude:/ˈɪntərluːd/| n.|插曲；间奏；幕间剧|A short period of time between two events or activities, or a short piece of music played between the parts of a longer piece.|例句：The comedian provided a brief interlude between the main acts.  喜剧演员在主要节目之间提供了简短的插曲。|例句：There was a musical interlude before the second act began. 第二幕开始前有一段音乐间奏。|近义词：intermission, interval, break|反义词：continuation, sequel
