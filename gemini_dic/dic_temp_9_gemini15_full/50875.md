desensitizer:/ˌdiːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzər/| n. |脱敏剂；减敏剂 |A desensitizer is a substance or process that reduces or eliminates sensitivity to a stimulus, such as pain, allergies, or emotional responses.  It can be a chemical agent, a therapy, or a technique. | Example: The dentist applied a desensitizing agent to the exposed dentin to reduce tooth sensitivity. 牙医在暴露的牙本质上涂抹了脱敏剂以减轻牙齿敏感。|近义词：anesthetic, analgesic, hypo-allergenic substance|反义词：sensitizer
