/ˌoʊvərˈflaʊərɪŋ/| n. |过度使用面粉；面粉用量过多|The act of using too much flour in a recipe, resulting in a heavy or dry final product.  Overflouring can lead to tough baked goods. |The cake was ruined by overflouring.  这块蛋糕因为面粉用量过多而被毁了。|underflouring|  (无明确反义词，可使用underflouring表示面粉用量不足)|名词
