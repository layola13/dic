headcount:/ˈhɛdkaʊnt/| n. |员工人数，人数统计|The number of people employed by a company or organization, or the number of people present at a particular event.  It often refers to a formal count of employees, used for payroll, budgeting, or workforce planning.|例句：The company's headcount has increased by 10% this year.  今年公司的员工人数增加了10%。|例句：We need to take a headcount before we leave the building.  我们离开大楼前需要清点人数。|近义词：personnel count, employee count, staff count|反义词：N/A
