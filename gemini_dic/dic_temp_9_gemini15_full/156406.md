phytogenesis:/ˌfaɪtoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs/| n. |植物发生，植物起源|The origin and development of plants.  The study of phytogenesis explores how plants evolved and diversified over millions of years.  This field often overlaps with paleobotany and evolutionary biology. | Example:  The phytogenesis of flowering plants is a complex and fascinating area of research. 植物开花植物的起源是一个复杂而迷人的研究领域。|近义词:plant evolution, plant development |反义词:N/A
