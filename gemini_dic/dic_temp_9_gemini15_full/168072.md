priviest:/ˈpraɪvɪəst/| adj.| 最私密的，最隐秘的 | Most private; most secret.  The word is the superlative form of "private". It suggests a level of secrecy and privacy exceeding that implied by simply "private". |例句：He confided his priviest thoughts to his diary. 他把他最私密的想法倾诉在他的日记里。
She shared her priviest secrets with her closest friend. 她与她最亲密的朋友分享了她最私密的秘密。|近义词：most confidential, most secret, most intimate |反义词：public, open, exposed


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and therefore doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  Adjectives do not have tenses.  They describe nouns.
