fatigued:/fəˈtiːɡd/ | adj. |疲惫的，精疲力竭的 | feeling extremely tired, usually because of hard work or lack of sleep.  |例句：After a long day of hiking, I felt completely fatigued.  长途跋涉一天后，我感到精疲力竭。

例句：She was fatigued from working overtime every day. 她每天加班工作，感到很疲惫。


近义词：tired, exhausted, weary, drained, worn out |反义词：energetic, refreshed, revitalized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is fatigued after the marathon. 他马拉松后很疲惫。
They are fatigued from their demanding jobs.  他们因要求严格的工作而感到疲惫。


一般过去时:
I was fatigued after the long flight. 长途飞行后我感到疲惫。
She was fatigued and went to bed early. 她很疲惫，早早就睡了。


现在完成时:
I have been fatigued all week. 我整个星期都很疲惫。
They have been fatigued since they started the project.  自从他们开始这个项目以来，他们就一直很疲惫。


过去完成时:
I had been fatigued for days before I finally took a break. 在我最终休息之前，我已经疲惫了好几天了。
They had been fatigued for weeks and needed a vacation. 他们已经疲惫了好几个星期，需要休假了。


将来时:
I will be fatigued after the meeting. 会议结束后我会很疲惫。
They will be fatigued by the end of the day. 到今天结束时他们会很疲惫。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't worked so hard, I wouldn't be so fatigued. 如果我不那么努力工作，我就不会这么疲惫了。
I wish I weren't so fatigued. 我希望我不那么疲惫。


被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)  The workers were fatigued by the intense workload. 工人们因繁重的工作而感到疲惫。（此句被动语态更强调工作对工人的影响）
His body was fatigued from overtraining. 他的身体因过度训练而疲惫不堪。


疑问句:
Are you fatigued? 你累了吗？
Is he fatigued from the journey? 他旅途劳累吗？


否定句:
I am not fatigued at all. 我一点也不累。
She is not fatigued, despite the long walk. 尽管走了很远的路，她并不疲惫。


PHRASE->
extreme fatigue 极度疲劳
mental fatigue 精神疲劳
muscle fatigue 肌肉疲劳
chronic fatigue 慢性疲劳
fatigue fracture 疲劳骨折
