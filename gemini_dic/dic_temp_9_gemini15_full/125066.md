mulish: /ˈmjuːlɪʃ/ | adj. | 倔强的；固执的；顽固的 |  Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.  |  例句：He was mulish in his refusal to apologize. 他倔强地拒绝道歉。

例句：Her mulish determination to finish the project, even when ill, impressed everyone.  她即使生病也要完成项目的倔强决心给每个人留下了深刻的印象。


近义词：obstinate, stubborn, intractable, headstrong, inflexible

反义词：yielding, compliant, amenable, flexible, docile


词源：源自于mule（骡子），骡子以其固执和难以驾驭而闻名，因此形容词mulish用来形容人具有类似骡子的固执特性。
