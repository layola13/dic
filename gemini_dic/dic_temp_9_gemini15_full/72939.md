fondue:/ˈfɒn.duː/ | n. |奶酪火锅；巧克力火锅|A fondue is a dish in which pieces of food are dipped into a hot sauce or cheese.  It's often a communal meal, enjoyed by several people dipping their food into the shared pot.|Example: We had a delicious cheese fondue for dinner last night.  昨晚我们吃了美味的奶酪火锅。|近义词：dip, melt|反义词： N/A
