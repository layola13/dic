haemangioblastoma: /ˌhiːmænˌdʒiːoʊblæsˈtoʊmə/ | n. | 血管母细胞瘤 | A rare, benign tumor of the central nervous system (brain and spinal cord) that arises from haemangioblasts, cells that can differentiate into both blood vessels and blood cells.  These tumors are typically slow-growing and can cause symptoms depending on their size and location. |  The patient was diagnosed with a cerebellar haemangioblastoma.  这位病人被诊断出患有小脑血管母细胞瘤。  |  |  | n.
