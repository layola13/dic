/aʊd/ | pron.| 那个；哪些；什么 |  "What" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, ideas, or actions.  |  What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？ | which,  | this |  pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What time is it now? = 现在几点？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you eaten today? = 你今天吃了什么？
What has she accomplished in her career? = 她在事业上取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you called me? = 你打电话给我之前做了什么？
What had they already discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you reading? = 你在读什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没有说他想要什么。


PHRASE->
what's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
what if = 如果…怎么样？
what about = ……怎么样？
no matter what = 无论如何
what for = 为了什么
