hypnotizes:/ˈhɪpnətaɪzɪz/| vt.|催眠；迷住；强烈吸引|To put (someone) into a hypnotic state; to fascinate or strongly attract.  |例句：The magician hypnotized the volunteer with a swinging watch.  魔术师用一个摆动的怀表催眠了志愿者。


例句：The beauty of the sunset hypnotized her.  日落的美丽深深地吸引了她。


近义词：mesmerize, fascinate, enthrall, captivate|反义词：bore, repel


USAGE->

The magician hypnotizes his audience every night. = 魔术师每晚都催眠他的观众。
The music hypnotizes me. = 这音乐迷住了我。


一般过去时:
