gravitated:/ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪd/| v.|趋向，被吸引|to move toward or be drawn to something.|The discussion gravitated towards the topic of climate change.  讨论逐渐转向气候变化的话题。|He gravitated towards the group of friends playing basketball. 他被吸引到打篮球的那群朋友那里。|近义词：tend, incline, drift|反义词：avoid, shun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
People gravitate towards things they find interesting. = 人们趋向于他们感兴趣的事物。
The ball gravitates towards the center of the Earth. = 球体受地球中心引力作用而趋向地球中心。


一般过去时:
She gravitated towards a career in medicine. = 她选择了医学作为自己的职业。
The conversation gravitated towards politics. = 谈话逐渐转向了政治。


现在完成时:
He has gravitated towards a more minimalist lifestyle. = 他已经逐渐过上了更加极简的生活方式。
Many young people have gravitated towards online communities. = 许多年轻人已经转向了在线社区。


过去完成时:
Before the party, she had gravitated towards the quiet corner. = 聚会前，她已经倾向于安静的角落。
By the end of the meeting, the discussion had gravitated towards a compromise. = 到会议结束时，讨论已经转向了妥协。


将来时:
I think he will gravitate towards a leadership role. = 我认为他会倾向于领导角色。
With the new policy, more investors will gravitate towards renewable energy. = 随着新政策的出台，更多的投资者将倾向于可再生能源。


虚拟语气:
If I were to choose, I would gravitate towards the simpler option. = 如果让我选择，我会倾向于更简单的选择。
If she had followed her heart, she would have gravitated towards art. = 如果她跟随自己的内心，她本会转向艺术。


被动语态:
The conversation was gravitated towards a more serious tone. = 谈话被引导向了更严肃的基调。(虽然"gravitate"本身不常用于被动语态，此句说明被动语态的可能性)
The attention was gravitated towards the most pressing issue. = 注意力被吸引到了最紧迫的问题上。(同样，"gravitate"本身不常用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Where do you gravitate towards in your free time? = 你空闲时间喜欢往哪儿去？
Towards what kind of work do you gravitate? = 你倾向于从事哪种类型的工作？


否定句:
He didn't gravitate towards the noisy crowd. = 他不喜欢嘈杂的人群。
She didn't gravitate towards the popular opinion. = 她并不趋向于大众观点。


PHRASE->
gravitate towards = 趋向于
gravitate to = 被吸引到
gravitate around = 围绕...而转
gravitate in = 向...集中
gravitate upon = 依赖于
