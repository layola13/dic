placations: /ˌpleɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 安抚；抚慰；劝解 | The act of appeasing or conciliating someone; the attempt to make someone less angry or hostile.  |例句：Her placations were unsuccessful; he remained furious.  她的安抚无效；他仍然很生气。

例句：The government's placations to the protesters did little to quell the unrest.  政府对抗议者的安抚措施几乎没有平息骚乱。


近义词： appeasement, conciliation, pacification, propitiation | 反义词： provocation, antagonism, aggravation


词性：名词
