roup:/ruːp/| n.|群，组，团|A group is a number of people or things that are located close together or are considered or classed together.|例句：A group of children were playing in the park.一群孩子在公园里玩耍。|近义词：cluster, bunch, collection|反义词：individual, single


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds form a group. = 鸟儿们组成一群。
My friends and I are a study group. = 我的朋友们和我组成一个学习小组。

一般过去时:
A large group gathered outside the building. = 一大群人聚集在建筑物外面。
The students formed a group to work on the project. = 学生们组成一个小组来完成项目。

现在完成时:
They have formed a support group for cancer patients. = 他们已经为癌症患者组建了一个支持小组。
She has joined a hiking group. = 她加入了一个远足小组。

过去完成时:
Before the concert, a large group had already assembled. = 音乐会之前，一大群人已经聚集在一起了。
The team had been a group for many years. = 这个团队已经是一个团队很多年了。

将来时:
A new group will be formed next week. = 下周将组建一个新的团队。
We will meet as a group tomorrow. = 我们明天将作为一个团队见面。

虚拟语气:
If they were a group, they would be more powerful. = 如果他们是一个团队，他们会更有力量。
I wish we had a larger group to help. = 我希望我们有一个更大的团队来帮忙。

被动语态:
The students were grouped into teams. = 学生们被分成小组。
The items were grouped by category. = 这些物品按类别分组。

疑问句:
What group are you in? = 你在哪一组？
How many people are in your group? = 你小组里有多少人？

否定句:
They are not a cohesive group. = 他们不是一个有凝聚力的团队。
He didn't want to be part of that group. = 他不想成为那个团队的一员。
