ceremonially:/ˌserɪˈməʊniəli/ | adj. | 隆重地；仪式地；正式地 |  In a ceremonial manner; with ceremony.  It suggests an act performed with formality and often with a sense of tradition or importance. | 例句：The treaty was ceremonially signed by both leaders. = 这项条约由两位领导人隆重签署。


The new king was ceremonially crowned. = 新国王进行了隆重的加冕仪式。


近义词：formally, officially, ritually, solemnly | 反义词：informally, casually, informally
