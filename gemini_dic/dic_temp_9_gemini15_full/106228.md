knotweed:/ˈnɒtˌwiːd/ | n. | 蓼属植物 | A plant of the genus Polygonum, many species of which are weeds.  Knotweed is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively.  Some species are invasive and difficult to eradicate. | Example: Japanese knotweed is a highly invasive species that can cause significant damage to property.  日本虎杖是一种极具侵略性的物种，会对财产造成重大损害。


The dense growth of knotweed choked the drainage ditch.  蓼属植物的茂密生长阻塞了排水沟。| 近义词:weed, invasive plant | 反义词: (no direct antonym,  but "desirable plant" or "native plant" could be considered contrastive) | 名词
