overgeneralizing:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 概括过度；过分概括 | To draw broad conclusions from limited evidence; to make generalizations that are too inclusive or inaccurate.  | Example: He's overgeneralizing based on a few isolated incidents. 他根据少数孤立事件做出了过度概括。  |近义词：generalizing excessively, stereotyping, making sweeping statements | 反义词：being specific, detailing, differentiating
