confesses:/kənˈfɛsɪz/| vt.|承认，坦白|To admit to be true or to be the case, typically with reluctance.|He confessed to the crime. 他承认了罪行。|She confessed her love for him. 她承认爱他。|admit, acknowledge, avow|deny, conceal, hide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confesses his mistakes every day. = 他每天都承认自己的错误。
She confesses her feelings to her friend. = 她向朋友倾诉了自己的感情。

一般过去时:
The thief confessed to the police. = 小偷向警察认罪了。
He confessed that he had lied. = 他承认自己撒谎了。

现在完成时:
He has confessed his guilt to the court. = 他已向法庭承认了自己的罪行。
She has confessed all her secrets to her diary. = 她已经把她所有的秘密都写在日记里了。

过去完成时:
He confessed that he had already known the truth. = 他承认自己早就知道真相了。
She confessed that she had stolen the money. = 她承认自己偷了钱。

将来时:
He will confess his wrongdoing to his family. = 他将向家人承认自己的过错。
She will confess everything to the authorities. = 她会向当局坦白一切。

虚拟语气:
If he confessed, he would feel better. = 如果他坦白了，他会感觉好一些。
I wish he confessed his feelings to me. = 我希望他能向我坦白他的感情。

被动语态:
His guilt was confessed to the judge. = 他的罪行被供认给了法官。
The truth was confessed by the witness. = 真相被证人承认了。

疑问句:
Will he confess his crimes? = 他会承认他的罪行吗？
Did she confess her secret? = 她承认了她的秘密吗？

否定句:
He didn't confess his involvement in the accident. = 他没有承认自己参与了这场事故。
She refused to confess her mistake. = 她拒绝承认自己的错误。
