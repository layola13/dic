garnetiferous:/ˌɡɑːrnɪˈtɛrəfərəs/| adj.|含石榴石的|Containing or yielding garnets.  The term refers to rocks or minerals that contain garnet.  |例句：The garnetiferous schist was rich in metamorphic minerals.  含石榴石的片岩富含变质矿物。

The garnetiferous gneiss displayed a distinctive red coloration due to the abundance of garnet crystals.富含石榴石的片麻岩由于石榴石晶体的丰富而呈现出独特的红色。|近义词：garnet-bearing|反义词：garnet-free
