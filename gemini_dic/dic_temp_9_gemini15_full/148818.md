overventuresome: /ˌoʊvərˈvɛntʃərˌsəm/ | adj. | 过于冒险的，鲁莽的 | Excessively adventurous; rash.  It implies a recklessness or a disregard for potential consequences. |  His overventuresome expedition into the uncharted jungle resulted in his being lost for weeks.  他的过于冒险的探险进入未开发的丛林导致他失踪数周。 | 近义词：reckless, rash, foolhardy, impetuous | 反义词：cautious, prudent, careful, timid
