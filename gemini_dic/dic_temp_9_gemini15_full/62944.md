enneagram:/ˈɛniəˌɡræm/| n. |九型人格，九型性格图 |A system of personality typing that describes nine interconnected personality types, or "enneatypes."  Each type is characterized by a core belief, a set of fears and motivations, and common patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving.  It is often used for self-discovery, personal growth, and understanding relationships. |The enneagram helped me understand my own motivations and those of others. |近义词：personality typology, character system |反义词：None.
