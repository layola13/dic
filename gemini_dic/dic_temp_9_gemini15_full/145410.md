overcensoriousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsəˈrɪəsnɪsəz/| n. |过度审查；过分挑剔；吹毛求疵|The excessive tendency to censor or criticize; an instance of this.  This word highlights the extreme and potentially negative aspect of censoriousness. It suggests a level of criticism that goes beyond reasonable or necessary limits.|例句：His overcensoriousnesses stifled creativity and open communication within the team.  他的过度审查扼杀了团队中的创造力和开放沟通。

The constant overcensoriousnesses of the school administration led to widespread student resentment. 学校管理层的不断过度审查导致学生普遍不满。|近义词：hypercriticism, excessive criticism, nitpicking, faultfinding |反义词：tolerance, leniency, acceptance


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "too much," "excessive," or "beyond."
* **censorious**: adjective meaning "severely critical," "fault-finding."  This itself derives from:
    * **censor**:  a person who examines material for objectionable content.  Ultimately from Latin *censor* (examiner of the census, later an official with power to control public morals).
    * **-ious**: adjective suffix.
* **-ness**: noun suffix indicating a state, quality, or condition.
* **-es**: plural suffix (for nouns).


用法说明：

由于overcensoriousnesses是一个罕见的词，它主要用于学术写作或对特定现象的详细分析。它不适合日常口语。  例句中展示了其在描述过度审查行为或批评态度时的用法。  由于其冗长且较为正式，更简洁的表达方式通常更受青睐，例如"excessive criticism" 或 "hypercriticism"。

该词不适用各种时态变化，因为它是一个名词，描述的是一种状态或行为。  它不具有动词、形容词等其他词性。
