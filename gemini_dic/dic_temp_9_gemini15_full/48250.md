decorates:/ˈdɛkəreɪts/| vt.|装饰，装扮|To add decoration to something, making it more attractive.|例句：She decorates her apartment with colorful paintings. 她用色彩鲜艳的画装饰她的公寓。|例句：They decorated the Christmas tree with ornaments. 他们用装饰品装饰圣诞树。|近义词：adorn, embellish, ornament|反义词：strip, despoil, ruin|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She decorates her room every year. = 她每年都装饰她的房间。
He decorates cakes for a living. = 他靠装饰蛋糕为生。

一般过去时:
They decorated the house for the party. = 他们为派对装饰了房子。
We decorated the Christmas tree last night. = 我们昨晚装饰了圣诞树。

现在完成时:
She has decorated the entire house. = 她已经装饰了整座房子。
They have decorated the cake beautifully. = 他们把蛋糕装饰得很漂亮。

过去完成时:
He had already decorated the room before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经装饰好了房间。
They had decorated the hall for the wedding ceremony. = 他们已经为婚礼装饰好了大厅。

将来时:
I will decorate my apartment next weekend. = 我下周末将装饰我的公寓。
We will decorate the cake with fresh flowers. = 我们将用鲜花装饰蛋糕。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would decorate the whole house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会装饰整个房子。
I wish I had decorated the cake better. = 我真希望我把蛋糕装饰得更好。

被动语态:
