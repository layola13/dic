prince:/prɪns/| n. |王子，亲王| A male member of a royal family, especially the son of a king or queen.  A prince is often heir to the throne or holds a significant position within a monarchy. |Example: The prince charmed the people with his kind demeanor.  王子以其和蔼可亲的态度赢得了民心。|近义词：royal, nobleman|反义词：pauper, commoner
