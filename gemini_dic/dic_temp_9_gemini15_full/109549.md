lesson:/ˈlesən/ | n. |教训，课程，功课 | a period of teaching or learning; something learned |例句：The lesson today is about grammar. 今天的课程是关于语法的。|近义词：class,lecture,instruction |反义词：distraction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lesson starts at 9 am. = 课程9点开始。
This lesson is very difficult. = 这节课很难。


一般过去时:
The lesson was about history. = 课程是关于历史的。
We learned a lot in yesterday's lesson. = 我们在昨天的课上学到了很多东西。


现在完成时:
I have finished my lesson. = 我已经完成了我的功课。
We have learned three lessons this week. = 这周我们已经上了三节课。


过去完成时:
I had finished my lesson before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经完成了我的功课。
They had studied the lesson before the test. = 考试前他们已经学习过这节课了。


将来时:
The lesson will start soon. = 课马上就要开始了。
We will have a lesson on physics tomorrow. = 明天我们将有一节物理课。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have learned the lesson. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能学会这节课了。
I wish I had understood the lesson better. = 我希望我更好地理解了这节课。


被动语态:
The lesson was taught by Mr. Smith. = 这节课是由史密斯先生讲授的。
The lesson is being taught now. = 现在正在上课。


疑问句:
What is the lesson about? = 这节课讲的是什么？
What lesson are we learning today? = 我们今天上什么课？


否定句:
The lesson wasn't interesting. = 这节课没意思。
I didn't understand the lesson. = 我没理解这节课。


PHRASE->
learn a lesson = 吸取教训
teach a lesson = 教训某人
a valuable lesson = 宝贵的教训
a hard lesson = 刻骨铭心的教训
life lesson = 人生经验
