excavates:/ˈɛkskəveɪts/ | vt. |挖掘；发掘；开凿 |To remove earth or other material from (a site) to expose something buried or hidden, or to make a hole or tunnel.  |例句：Archaeologists are excavating the site to find ancient artifacts. 考古学家正在挖掘遗址以寻找古代文物。
例句：The workers excavated a tunnel under the river. 工人们在河底下挖掘了一条隧道。|近义词：dig, unearth, uncover |反义词：bury, conceal, fill
