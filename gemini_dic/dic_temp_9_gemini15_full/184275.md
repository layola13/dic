resorted:/rɪˈzɔːrtɪd/| v.|求助，诉诸，凭借|To have recourse to; turn to for help or refuge.|例句：Desperate, she resorted to begging.  绝望之下，她开始乞讨。|例句：They resorted to violence to settle the dispute.  他们诉诸暴力来解决争端。|近义词：turn to,  have recourse to|反义词：ignored, neglected

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resorts to violence when angry. = 他生气时会诉诸暴力。
She resorts to prayer for comfort. = 她求助于祈祷以获得安慰。

一般过去时:
They resorted to bribery to win the contract. = 他们为了赢得合同而行贿。
The company resorted to layoffs due to financial difficulties. = 由于财务困难，该公司采取了裁员措施。

现在完成时:
He has resorted to drastic measures to solve the problem. = 他已经采取了严厉措施来解决这个问题。
She has resorted to medication to manage her anxiety. = 她一直依靠药物来控制她的焦虑。

过去完成时:
They had resorted to illegal activities before being caught. = 他们被捕前曾从事非法活动。
The government had resorted to censorship to suppress dissent. = 政府曾采取审查制度来压制异见。

将来时:
If all else fails, we will resort to legal action. = 如果其他方法都失败了，我们将诉诸法律行动。
They will resort to drastic measures if the situation worsens. = 如果情况恶化，他们将采取严厉措施。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would never resort to such tactics. = 如果我是他，我绝不会采取这种策略。
I wish they hadn't resorted to lying. = 我真希望他们没有撒谎。

被动语态:
Violence was resorted to as a last resort. = 暴力作为最后手段被使用了。
Legal action was resorted to after all other options failed. = 在所有其他选择都失败后，诉诸了法律行动。

疑问句:
Did they resort to any illegal means? = 他们采取了任何非法手段吗？
What methods did she resort to in order to succeed? = 为了成功，她采取了什么方法？

否定句:
He didn't resort to violence despite the provocation. = 尽管受到挑衅，他并没有诉诸暴力。
She didn't resort to any unfair tactics during the competition. = 在比赛中，她没有采取任何不公平的手段。
