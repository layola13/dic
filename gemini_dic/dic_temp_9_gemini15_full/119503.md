microphotograph:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈfoʊtəɡræf/| n. |显微照片，显微摄影|A photograph taken through a microscope, showing a greatly magnified image of a tiny subject.| 例句：The microphotograph revealed the intricate structure of the cell. 这张显微照片揭示了细胞复杂的结构。

The microphotograph showed clear evidence of bacterial contamination. 这张显微照片清楚地显示了细菌污染的证据。


近义词：micrograph, magnified image|反义词：macrophotograph
