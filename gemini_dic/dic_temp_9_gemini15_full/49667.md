demises:/dɪˈmaɪz/ | n. |死亡；终止；让与|The noun "demise" refers to a death, especially of a person in a position of authority, or to the end of something's existence. It can also refer to the transfer of property or title by will or inheritance.|例句：The demise of the company was sudden and unexpected.  公司的倒闭是突然而意外的。|例句：The king's demise left the kingdom in turmoil. 国王的去世使王国陷入混乱。|近义词：death, decease, end, expiration |反义词：birth, beginning, commencement |名词
