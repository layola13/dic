nonvehicular:/ˌnɒnˈvɪhɪkjʊlə(r)/| adj.|非车辆的，非机动的|Not relating to or involving vehicles.  This adjective describes things, activities, or spaces that are not associated with or used by vehicles.|The city is planning to expand its nonvehicular pathways.  这个城市正计划扩展其非机动车道。

The nonvehicular area is reserved for pedestrians and cyclists. 非机动车区域仅供行人和骑车者使用。


近义词：pedestrian, non-motorized

反义词：vehicular, motorized


词性：形容词
