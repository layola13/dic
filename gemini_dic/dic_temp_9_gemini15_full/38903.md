confessional:/kənˈfɛʃənəl/ | n. |忏悔室；忏悔；自白 | A confessional is a private booth or enclosed space in a church where a person confesses their sins to a priest.  It can also refer to a public confession or self-revelation.  | Example: The priest listened patiently in the confessional.  牧师在忏悔室里耐心地听着。 The singer's memoir was a powerful confessional. 歌手的回忆录是一部强有力的自白。 |近义词：confession, disclosure, revelation |反义词：secrecy, concealment |名词
