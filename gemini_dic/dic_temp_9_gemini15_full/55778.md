diversions:/daɪˈvɜːrʒənz/ | n. |消遣；转移；改道 | Activities that distract or entertain; a turning aside from a course or purpose.  |例句：Reading is a pleasant diversion for me. 阅读对我来说是一种愉快的消遣。

例句：The unexpected detour caused a significant diversion in our travel plans.  意外的绕路使我们的旅行计划发生了重大改变。


近义词：pastimes, amusements, distractions, detours | 反义词：focus, concentration, direct route


词源:  From Old French *diversioun, from Latin *diversiōnem (nominative *diversiō), from Latin *divertere "to turn aside," from dis- "apart" + vertere "to turn."
