nonbibulousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪbjʊləsnəsɪz/| n.|非嗜酒性；不酗酒；不爱喝酒的特性|The plural form of "nonbibulousness," referring to the state or quality of not being bibulous (fond of or addicted to alcoholic drinks).  It denotes a lack of fondness for or abstinence from alcoholic beverages.| Example:  The nonbibulousnesses of the monks were evident in their austere lifestyle.  僧侣们不嗜酒的特性在他们简朴的生活方式中显而易见。|近义词:abstinence, sobriety, temperance|反义词: bibulousness, inebriety, dipsomania
