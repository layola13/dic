The patient cast up his dinner. 病人把晚饭吐了出来。


例句：The accountant cast up the figures at the end of the day. 会计在一天结束时计算了数字。

例句：Debris was cast up on the beach after the hurricane. 飓风过后，沙滩上堆积着碎片。


近义词：throw away, vomit, compute, accumulate, pile up|反义词：retain, keep down, subtract, disperse, scatter|动词
