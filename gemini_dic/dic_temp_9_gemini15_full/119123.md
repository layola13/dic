metropolitans:/ˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtənz/| n. |大主教；大都市人；主要都市居民|A metropolitan is a bishop of a major city,ranking above other bishops.  It can also refer to people living in or characteristic of a large city. |例句：The metropolitan addressed the congregation.  大主教向会众讲话。

The metropolitans of the country are experiencing rapid growth. 这个国家的各大都市正在经历快速发展。|近义词：archbishop, citizen, urbanite|反义词：rural resident, villager
