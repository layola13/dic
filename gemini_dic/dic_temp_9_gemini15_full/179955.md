referral: /rɪˈfɜːrəl/ | n. | 推荐，介绍；转诊 |

英文解析：The act of referring someone or something; a person who is referred.  It can refer to recommending someone for a job, referring a patient to a specialist, or mentioning something to someone.

例句：

I got the job through a referral from a friend. = 我通过朋友的推荐得到了这份工作。

The doctor gave me a referral to a cardiologist. = 医生给我开了转诊单，让我去看心脏病专家。


近义词：recommendation, suggestion, introduction, forwarding

反义词：rejection, refusal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He often receives referrals from his previous clients. = 他经常收到以前客户的推荐。
She regularly gives referrals to local businesses. = 她定期向当地企业推荐客户。

一般过去时:
I received a referral for a new job last week. = 我上周收到一份新工作的推荐。
The doctor gave him a referral to a specialist. = 医生给他开了转诊单，让他去看专科医生。

现在完成时:
She has received many referrals this month. = 她这个月收到了很多推荐。
They have given several referrals to the charity. = 他们已经向慈善机构推荐了几个人。

过去完成时:
He had already received several referrals before the interview. = 在面试之前他已经收到了几份推荐。
The patient had been given a referral to a different hospital. = 患者已经转诊到另一家医院。

将来时:

被动语态:

疑问句:
