buttes:/bjuːts/| n.|平顶山，孤山|An isolated hill with steep sides and a flat top, especially one formed of mesas.|例句：The buttes rose dramatically from the flat desert floor.  平顶山从平坦的沙漠地面上戏剧性地升起。
例句：We saw several impressive buttes during our hike.  我们在徒步旅行中看到了一些令人印象深刻的平顶山。|近义词：mesa, hill, mound|反义词：valley, plain
