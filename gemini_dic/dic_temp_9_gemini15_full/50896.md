desertion:/dɪˈzɜːrʃən/| n. |遗弃；抛弃；逃亡；擅离职守 |The act of abandoning or leaving a person, place, or thing.  It often implies a sense of neglect or betrayal.  The context determines the specific meaning. |例句：The desertion of his post led to his court-martial.  他的擅离职守导致了他被军事法庭审判。

例句：His wife filed for divorce citing his emotional desertion. 他的妻子以他情感上的遗弃为由申请离婚。


近义词：abandonment, forsaking, abandonment, defection |反义词：loyalty, faithfulness, commitment |词性：名词

词源:
The word "desertion" comes from the Old French word "desercion," which in turn originates from the Latin word "desertio," meaning "abandonment" or "forsaking."  The Latin word is derived from the verb "deserere," meaning "to abandon," "to forsake," or "to leave."
