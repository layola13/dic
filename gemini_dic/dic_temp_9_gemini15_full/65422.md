euphoria's:/juːˈfɔːriəz/| n.| 狂喜的；欣快的；极度快乐的 | The feeling of intense happiness and well-being.  It's often associated with a feeling of being elated, joyful, and carefree.  The possessive form indicates belonging to or relating to euphoria. |例句：Her euphoria's contagious; everyone around her felt happy.  她的狂喜具有感染力；周围的人都感到快乐。

例句：The euphoria's short-lived nature was a stark contrast to the initial excitement. 狂喜的短暂性与最初的兴奋形成了鲜明对比。|近义词：ecstasy, bliss, rapture, exhilaration|反义词：depression, misery, despair, anguish|名词
