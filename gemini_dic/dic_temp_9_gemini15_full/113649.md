machine-tooled:/məˈʃiːn tuːld/ | adj. | 机床加工的；用机床制造的 |  Describes objects or parts that have been shaped or finished using machine tools. This implies a high degree of precision and accuracy, often associated with mass production or industrial manufacturing. |例句：The engine parts were machine-tooled to exacting specifications.  引擎部件是用机床加工到精确的规格。

The gears in the clock were machine-tooled for smooth operation.  钟表里的齿轮是用机床加工的，以便平稳运行。


近义词：machined, mechanically manufactured, precisely crafted | 反义词：hand-tooled, handcrafted, handmade | 词性: adjective
