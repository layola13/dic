mercurous:/ˈmɜːrkjʊrəs/| adj.| 汞的；亚汞的 | Relating to or denoting the univalent mercury ion (Hg⁺) or its compounds.  Mercurous compounds contain mercury in the +1 oxidation state, existing as Hg₂²⁺ dimers.  They are often less toxic than mercuric compounds.| Example: Mercurous chloride was once used as a medicine. 氯化亚汞曾被用作药物。|近义词：mercurous;  |反义词：mercuric
