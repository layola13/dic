containerization: /ˌkənˌteɪnəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |集装箱化，容器化 | The process of packaging software and its dependencies into a single unit (a container), to ensure that it runs consistently across different computing environments. This simplifies deployment, scaling, and management of applications. | Example: Containerization has revolutionized software deployment, making it easier to move applications between different servers and cloud platforms.  集装箱化彻底改变了软件部署，使应用程序更容易在不同的服务器和云平台之间移动。


近义词：virtualization, packaging  | 反义词：decontainerization | 词性: n.
