compiling:/kəmˈpaɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 编译，汇编 | To compile something, such as a program or a report, means to gather together information from different sources and put it into a single, organized form.  It often involves transforming source code into machine-readable instructions. | The programmer spent hours compiling the code before running the program. 程序员花了数小时编译代码然后才运行程序。The editor is compiling the articles for the next issue of the magazine. 编辑正在为杂志的下一期汇编文章。 | assembling, collecting |  decompiling | 动词


USAGE->

He compiles data for his research every day. 他每天都为他的研究收集数据。
