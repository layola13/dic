newsy's:/ˈnuːziːz/| adj.|新闻的；爱说闲话的；爱打听消息的|Full of news; given to spreading news or gossip.  The word is often used informally and can sometimes imply a slightly negative connotation, suggesting excessive interest in gossip or trivial matters.|例句：The newsy website reported on the latest celebrity scandal. 这个爱报道新闻的网站报道了最新的名人丑闻。


例句：She's a bit newsy; she always knows what's going on in the neighborhood. 她有点爱打听消息；她总是知道附近发生了什么。|近义词：gossipy, chatty, informative|反义词：uninformative, secretive, quiet
