galleons:/ˈɡælɪən/| n.|大帆船，大型多桅帆船|A large sailing ship with three or more masts, typically used by European powers from the 15th to 18th centuries for trade and warfare.|例句：Spanish galleons once sailed the oceans carrying gold and silver from the New World. 西班牙的大帆船曾经航行在大洋上，从新世界运载黄金和白银。|例句：The pirate captain dreamed of capturing a richly laden galleon. 海盗船长梦想捕获一艘装满财宝的大帆船。|近义词：carrack, caravel|反义词：small boat, dinghy
