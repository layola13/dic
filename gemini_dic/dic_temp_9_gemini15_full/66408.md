executions:/ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃənz/ | n. | 处决；执行；实行 | The act of carrying out or putting into effect a plan, order, or legal sentence, especially the killing of a person as a legal punishment.  It can also refer to the performance or completion of a task or process. |例句：The executions were carried out at dawn. = 处决是在黎明时分进行的。

例句：The successful execution of the plan required careful coordination. = 成功执行该计划需要仔细协调。


近义词：exterminations, killings, implementations, performances | 反义词：acquittals, pardons, suspensions


词源：源自于古法语“execucion”,最终源自于拉丁语“exsecutio”,由“ex-”(out)和“sequi”(to follow)构成，表示“following out”或“carrying out”。
