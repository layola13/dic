radioassay:/ˈreɪdioʊˈeɪseɪ/| n. |放射性分析法|The measurement of radioactivity in a sample to determine the amount of a specific substance present.  This technique is often used in medical and environmental science. |Example: Radioassay was used to determine the level of radioactive iodine in the patient's thyroid.  放射性分析法被用来测定病人甲状腺中放射性碘的含量。|近义词：radioimmunoassay, isotopic assay |反义词:None
