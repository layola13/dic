overstepped:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛpt/ | vt. |超越；逾越；越过界限 | To go beyond the limits of (something), especially in a way that is considered inappropriate or unacceptable.  |例句：He overstepped his authority by ordering the arrest without a warrant. 他未经授权就下令逮捕，越权了。

例句：She overstepped the boundaries of professional conduct by disclosing confidential information. 她泄露了机密信息，违反了职业行为规范。


近义词：transgress, exceed, infringe, violate | 反义词：respect, adhere to, comply with | 词性:动词
