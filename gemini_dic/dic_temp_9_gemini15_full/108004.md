lasso: /ˈlæsoʊ/ | v. n. |套索；用套索套住 | To catch or secure (something, especially an animal) with a lasso.  A lasso is a rope with a noose used for catching animals. | The cowboy lassoed the runaway calf. 牛仔用套索套住了逃跑的小牛。 He used a lasso to catch the horse. 他用套索套住了马。 | 近义词： snare, rope | 反义词：release, free


USAGE->

He lassos the steer every day. 他每天用套索套住那头牛。
She lassos wild horses for a living. 她以捕捉野马为生。


一般过去时:
The rancher lassoed the bull. 牧场主用套索套住了公牛。
They lassoed the runaway horse. 他们套住了逃跑的马。

词源:
The word "lasso" comes from the Spanish word "lazo," meaning "noose" or "loop."  The Spanish word ultimately derives from the Latin word "laqueus," meaning "noose" or "snare."
