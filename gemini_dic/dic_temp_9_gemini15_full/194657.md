self-undoing: /ˌsɛlf ˈʌnˈduːɪŋ/ | adj. | 自毁的；导致自身失败的 |  Something that is self-undoing is likely to cause its own failure or ruin. It describes actions, plans, or systems that contain inherent flaws leading to their own destruction.  |


例句：His arrogance proved to be his self-undoing. = 他的傲慢最终导致了他的失败。

His self-undoing stemmed from a lack of planning. = 他的失败源于缺乏计划。


近义词：self-defeating, counterproductive, destructive, ruinous


反义词：self-preserving, beneficial, constructive, helpful


词性：形容词
