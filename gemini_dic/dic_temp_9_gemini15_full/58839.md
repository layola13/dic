duplexing:/ˈduːpleksɪŋ/| n. |双工；双向通信|The process of transmitting and receiving data simultaneously in both directions.  This is commonly used in telecommunications and networking to enable two-way communication over a single channel.  For example, a full-duplex connection allows for simultaneous talking and listening. | Example:  The new communication system uses duplexing to improve efficiency.  新的通讯系统使用双工来提高效率。  The radio uses half-duplexing, meaning only one party can transmit at a time. 这部无线电使用半双工，这意味着一次只能有一方发送信号。|近义词：two-way communication, simultaneous transmission and reception|反义词：simplexing (单工)|名词
