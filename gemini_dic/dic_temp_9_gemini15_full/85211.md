harkened:/ˈhɑːkənd/| v.|倾听，留心，注意|To listen attentively; to give heed.|The villagers harkened to the old storyteller's tales.|listened, attended, heeded|ignored, disregarded, overlooked|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harkens to the advice of his elders. = 他倾听长辈的建议。
They always harken to the news. = 他们总是留心新闻。

一般过去时:
I harkened to the sound of the wind. = 我倾听了风的声音。
She harkened to her intuition. = 她倾听了自己的直觉。


现在完成时:
We have harkened to the warnings. = 我们已经听取了警告。
They have harkened to the call of adventure. = 他们已经响应了冒险的号召。


过去完成时:
He had harkened to the rumors before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经听取了谣言。
She had harkened to the advice, but it was too late. = 她已经听取了建议，但是太晚了。


将来时:
They will harken to the judge's ruling. = 他们将倾听法官的裁决。
I will harken to your pleas. = 我将倾听你的请求。


虚拟语气:
If I had harkened to his warning, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我听了他的警告，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had harkened to my parents' advice. = 我真希望我听了父母的建议。


被动语态:
His words were harkened to by the crowd. = 他的话受到了人群的倾听。
The warnings were not harkened to. = 警告没有被重视。


疑问句:
Did you harken to what he said? = 你听了他说的话吗？
Will they harken to our demands? = 他们会理会我们的要求吗？


否定句:
They did not harken to the siren's call. = 他们没有理会海妖的呼唤。
He refused to harken to reason. = 他拒绝听从道理。


PHRASE->
harken back to = 回顾，追溯到
harken to = 倾听，留心
harken unto = 倾听（较为正式）
harken after = 追求，追寻
harken forth = 宣告，宣布


词源:
The word "harkened" comes from the Old English word "heorcnian," meaning "to listen attentively."  This further derives from the Proto-Germanic "*haurkōnjan,"  ultimately connecting to a root meaning "to hear."
