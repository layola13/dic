jesuit:/ˈdʒɛzuɪt/| n. |耶稣会士；耶稣会会员|A member of the Society of Jesus, a Roman Catholic religious order founded by Ignatius of Loyola in 1540.  Jesuits are known for their intellectual rigor, missionary work, and educational institutions.|例句：Many famous universities were founded by Jesuits. 许多著名的大学是由耶稣会士创办的。|近义词：Jesuit priest, member of the Society of Jesus|反义词：None specifically.
