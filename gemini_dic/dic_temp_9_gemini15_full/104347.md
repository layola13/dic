juxtapose:/ˈdʒʌk.stə.poʊz/| vt.|并置，并列|To place or deal with close together for contrasting effect.|The exhibition juxtaposes Picasso's early and late works.|近义词：contrast, compare|反义词：separate, isolate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He juxtaposes modern art with classical sculpture. = 他将现代艺术与古典雕塑并置。
The artist juxtaposes bright colours with muted tones. = 这位艺术家将鲜艳的色彩与柔和的色调并置。


一般过去时:
She juxtaposed the two images to highlight their differences. = 她将两幅图像并列以突出它们的差异。
The writer juxtaposed the characters' contrasting personalities. = 作者将人物性格的对比并置。
