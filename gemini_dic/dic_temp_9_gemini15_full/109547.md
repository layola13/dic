lessness:/ˈlɛsnəs/| n.|缺乏；不足；无；无能|The state or quality of being without or lacking something; absence.  It is usually used as a suffix added to nouns or adjectives to form abstract nouns.|例句：His speech was filled with aimlessness and hopelessness.  他的演讲充满了漫无目的和绝望。

例句：The carelessness of the driver caused the accident. 司机的粗心导致了事故。


近义词：lack, absence, deficiency, want|反义词：abundance, presence, sufficiency


词源：源自古英语的 *lēasness，由 *lēas（“空虚的，缺乏的”）和后缀 -ness（表示状态或性质）构成。
