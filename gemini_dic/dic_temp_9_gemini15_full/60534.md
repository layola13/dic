elaiometer:/ˌeɪliˈɒmɪtə(r)/| n. |油量计，油分计 |An instrument for measuring the amount of oil in a substance, especially seeds.  It typically measures the percentage of oil by weight or volume. |The elaiometer showed that the sunflower seeds contained a high percentage of oil.|近义词：oil meter, oleometer |反义词：无
