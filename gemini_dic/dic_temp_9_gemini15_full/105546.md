kimchi:/ˈkɪmtʃi/| n.|泡菜|A traditional Korean dish made from fermented vegetables, usually napa cabbage and Korean radish, with a variety of seasonings including gochugaru (Korean chili powder), garlic, ginger, jeotgal (fermented seafood), and gochujang (Korean chili paste).  It's known for its spicy and sour flavor.| 例句：Kimchi is a staple food in Korean cuisine. 泡菜是韩国菜的主食。

例句：I love the spicy kick of kimchi. 我喜欢泡菜辛辣的味道。


近义词：pickled vegetables, fermented vegetables| 反义词：None specifically, but dishes without fermentation or spice could be considered opposites in terms of flavor profile.|词性: 名词
