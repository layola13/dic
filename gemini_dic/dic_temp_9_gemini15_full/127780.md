neighborhood:/ˈneɪbərhʊd/| n. |街区，邻里，附近地区 |A neighborhood is a district or area of a town or city, especially one with particular characteristics.  It refers to the people living in a particular area, considered as a community. |例句：We live in a quiet neighborhood. 我们住在一个安静的街区。

例句：The neighborhood association is organizing a cleanup.  邻里协会正在组织一次清理活动。


近义词：vicinity, area, district, locality |反义词：distance, remoteness


词源:  The word "neighborhood" comes from the Old English "neahgebur" which is a compound of "neah" (near) and "gebur" (neighbor, dweller).  Over time,  "gebur" evolved into "neighbor," and the suffix "-hood" was added, indicating state or condition, resulting in "neighborhood."
