freelancers:/ˈfriːˌlænsərz/| n.|自由职业者们|People who work for themselves and are not employed by a company.|例句：Many freelancers find it difficult to secure consistent work. 许多自由职业者发现很难获得稳定的工作。  例句：The platform connects businesses with freelancers in various fields. 该平台将企业与各个领域的自由职业者联系起来。|近义词：independent contractors, self-employed individuals|反义词：employees, staff


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Freelancers often work from home. = 自由职业者通常在家工作。
Many freelancers use online platforms to find clients. = 许多自由职业者使用在线平台寻找客户。


一般过去时:
She worked as a freelancer for several years. = 她做了几年自由职业者。
He freelanced for a marketing agency last year. = 他去年为一家营销机构做过自由职业。


现在完成时:
I have freelanced since graduating from college. = 我从大学毕业后就一直在做自由职业。
She has completed several successful projects as a freelancer. = 她作为自由职业者已经完成了几个成功的项目。


过去完成时:
Before starting his own business, he had freelanced for many companies. = 在创业之前，他曾为许多公司做过自由职业。
They had freelanced together before they decided to form a partnership. = 在决定组建合伙公司之前，他们曾一起做过自由职业。


将来时:
I will be freelancing full-time next year. = 我明年将全职做自由职业。
She will freelance for a tech company during the summer. = 暑假期间她将为一家科技公司做自由职业。


虚拟语气:
If I were a freelancer, I would have more flexibility. = 如果我是自由职业者，我会更有弹性。
I wish I had started freelancing sooner. = 我真希望我早点开始做自由职业。


被动语态:
Freelancers are often overlooked in traditional employment statistics. = 自由职业者在传统的就业统计中经常被忽视。
Many projects are completed by freelancers worldwide. = 全世界许多项目都是由自由职业者完成的。


疑问句:
Are you a freelancer? = 你是自由职业者吗？
How many freelancers work for this company? = 有多少自由职业者为这家公司工作？


否定句:
He is not a freelancer; he's a full-time employee. = 他不是自由职业者；他是一名全职员工。
They do not hire freelancers for this type of project. = 他们不为这种类型的项目雇佣自由职业者。
