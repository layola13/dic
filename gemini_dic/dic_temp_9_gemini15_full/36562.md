USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bridge collapses under heavy traffic.  桥梁在车流过重下倒塌。
The economy sometimes collapses due to mismanagement.  经济有时会因为管理不善而崩溃。

一般过去时:
The old house collapsed during the storm.  老房子在暴风雨中倒塌了。
The company collapsed after losing its major client. 公司在失去主要客户后倒闭了。

现在完成时:
The roof has collapsed after years of neglect. 屋顶多年失修后倒塌了。
The government's plan has collapsed due to public opposition. 政府的计划由于公众反对而失败了。

过去完成时:
The dam had collapsed before the rescue teams arrived.  救援队到达之前，水坝已经崩塌了。
The negotiations had collapsed before they could reach a compromise.  在他们能够达成妥协之前，谈判就已经破裂了。

将来时:
The building will collapse if it isn't repaired.  如果这座建筑物不进行维修，它将会倒塌。
The market will likely collapse if the interest rates rise sharply.  如果利率急剧上升，市场可能会崩溃。

虚拟语气:
If the bridge had been maintained properly, it wouldn't have collapsed. 如果桥梁得到适当的维护，它就不会倒塌了。
If the government hadn't intervened, the economy would have collapsed. 如果政府不干预，经济就会崩溃。

被动语态:
The old mine was collapsed by the recent flooding.  老矿井被最近的洪水冲垮了。
The deal was collapsed due to unforeseen circumstances. 这笔交易由于不可预见的情况而告吹。

疑问句:
Did the structure collapse during the storm?  结构在暴风雨中倒塌了吗？
Has the government's new policy caused the market to collapse? 政府的新政策导致市场崩溃了吗？

否定句:
The building didn't collapse despite the strong winds.  尽管风很大，建筑物却没有倒塌。
The talks didn't collapse, and an agreement was reached. 会谈没有破裂，并且达成了协议。
