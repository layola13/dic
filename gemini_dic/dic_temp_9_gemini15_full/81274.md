granola:/ɡrəˈnoʊlə/ | n. |格兰诺拉麦片|A breakfast cereal made from rolled oats, nuts, seeds, and sometimes dried fruit, often sweetened and baked.| 例句：I usually start my day with a bowl of granola and yogurt. 我通常以一碗格兰诺拉麦片和酸奶开始我的一天。

例句：This granola is so delicious that I could eat it all day long. 这格兰诺拉麦片太好吃了，我可以整天吃它。
