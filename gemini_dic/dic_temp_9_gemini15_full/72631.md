flyless:/ˈflaɪləs/| adj. |不会飞的，无飞行的能力的|Lacking the ability to fly.|例句：The kiwi is a flyless bird.  几维鸟是一种不会飞的鸟。|例句：Many island birds have evolved to be flyless due to lack of predators. 由于缺乏捕食者，许多岛屿鸟类已经进化成不会飞的。|近义词：flightless, wingless|反义词：flying, winged


USAGE->

This insect is flyless. = 这只昆虫不会飞。

The bird has become flyless. = 这只鸟已经不会飞了。
This island's birds have evolved to be flyless. = 这个岛上的鸟类已经进化成不会飞的了。
