hygroscope:/ˈhaɪɡroʊskoʊp/ | n. |吸湿器，测湿计 | An instrument for measuring the humidity of the air or other gases.  It typically works by detecting changes in the length or weight of a material that absorbs moisture. |The hygroscope showed a significant increase in humidity after the rain.  吸湿器显示雨后湿度显著增加。| hygrometer, psychrometer |  (None readily available as a direct antonym;  perhaps "instrument measuring dryness" could be considered a functional opposite) |名词
