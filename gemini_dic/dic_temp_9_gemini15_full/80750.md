gospelize:/ˈɡɒspəlaɪz/ | vt. | 传播福音；使…福音化 | To spread the gospel; to convert or influence in accordance with Gospel principles. | Example:  The missionaries tried to gospelize the remote village. 传教士试图使偏远村庄福音化。  He attempted to gospelize his friends with his newfound faith. 他试图用他新发现的信仰来感化他的朋友。| 近义词：evangelize, proselytize | 反义词：secularize
