catcalling:/ˈkætˌkɔːlɪŋ/| n. | 咸猪手，街头骚扰 | The act of shouting or whistling at a stranger, typically a woman, in a sexually suggestive and often offensive way.  It's a form of harassment that objectifies and degrades the person being targeted. | Example:  The woman was subjected to catcalling while walking home from work.  这个女人下班回家时受到了咸猪手的骚扰。|近义词：street harassment, harassment, heckling |反义词：respect, courtesy
