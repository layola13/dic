microsite:/ˈmaɪkroʊsaɪt/| n. |微型网站，专题网站|A microsite is a small, self-contained website that is usually part of a larger website.  It is often created for a specific campaign, product, or event.  Microsites are designed to be focused and easy to navigate, providing a streamlined user experience. |Example: The company created a microsite to promote its new product launch.  这家公司创建了一个微型网站来推广其新产品发布。

The company launched a dedicated microsite for its holiday sale. 这家公司为其假日促销活动启动了一个专门的微型网站。|近义词：mini-site, landing page |反义词：main website, full website
