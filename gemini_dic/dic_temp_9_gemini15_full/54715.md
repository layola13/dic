disownment:/dɪsˈoʊnmənt/| n. | 否认；撇清关系；不承认；弃绝 | The act of disowning someone or something; repudiation; renunciation.  It implies a formal or deliberate rejection of a previous claim, relationship, or responsibility. | Example: The disownment of his son caused the old man great pain.  老人的儿子被逐出家门让他非常痛苦。 The disownment of her past actions was a necessary step for her rehabilitation.  否认她过去的所作所为是她康复的必要步骤。|近义词：repudiation, renunciation, rejection |反义词：acceptance, acknowledgment, adoption
