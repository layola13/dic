converters:/kənˈvɜːrtərz/| n.|转换器，变换器|A device or program that changes data from one form to another.  For example, a converter might change an image file from JPG to PNG, or convert electrical energy into another form of energy.|例句：The audio converter changed the MP3 file into a WAV file.  音频转换器将MP3文件转换为WAV文件。
The power converter transforms AC electricity into DC electricity.  电源转换器将交流电转换为直流电。|近义词：transformer, transducer, adapter|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be described by the verb "to revert"|名词
