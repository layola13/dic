outpowers:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərz/| vt.|胜过；比…强大|To surpass in power or strength.|例句：His strength outpowers mine.他的力气比我大。|例句：The new engine outpowers its predecessor. 新引擎比它的前身更强大。|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outweighs|反义词：is outpowered by, is inferior to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun outpowers the moon in brightness.  太阳的光芒胜过月亮。
Her determination outpowers her fear.  她的决心战胜了她的恐惧。


一般过去时:
The army outpowered the rebels.  军队战胜了叛军。
His argument outpowered hers in the debate.  在辩论中，他的论点胜过她。


现在完成时:
Technology has outpowered human limitations in many areas.  技术已经在许多领域超越了人类的局限性。
She has outpowered her rivals in the race for promotion.  在晋升的竞争中，她已经胜过了她的竞争对手。


过去完成时:
The evidence had outpowered the defendant's alibi.  证据已经证实了被告的不在场证明是假的。
They had outpowered their opponents in the previous match.  他们在之前的比赛中已经战胜了对手。


将来时:
The new software will outpower the existing system. 新软件将胜过现有系统。
His influence will outpower theirs in the future. 他的影响力将来会超过他们。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outpowered his opponent. 如果他训练更刻苦，他也许就能战胜对手。
I wish my skills outpowered my weaknesses. 我希望我的技能能战胜我的弱点。


被动语态:
Their team was outpowered by the opponents. 他们的队伍被对手击败了。
The old system was outpowered by the new technology. 旧系统被新技术超越了。


疑问句:
Does his influence outpower the president's? 他的影响力能超过总统吗？
Does this engine outpower the previous model? 这台引擎的动力比之前的型号强吗？


否定句:
The small country could not outpower the larger nation.  这个小国无法战胜这个大国。
His arguments did not outpower the evidence presented.  他的论点无法驳倒所提出的证据。


PHRASE->
outpower completely  完全胜过
outpower significantly  显著胜过
outpower in terms of... 在...方面胜过
outpower by a wide margin  大幅度胜过
outpower effortlessly  毫不费力地胜过
