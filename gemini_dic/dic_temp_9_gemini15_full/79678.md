glovelike:/ˈɡlʌvˌlaɪk/| adj.|像手套的；像手套一样的|Resembling a glove in shape, texture, or fit.  The term implies a close fit or covering, similar to how a glove fits a hand.|例句：The tight-fitting bodysuit felt glovelike against her skin.  这件紧身衣穿在她身上感觉像手套一样贴身。|例句：The sculptor created a glovelike casing for the delicate artifact. 雕塑家为这件精美的文物制作了一个像手套一样的保护套。|近义词：fitting, snug, close-fitting|反义词：loose, baggy, ill-fitting
