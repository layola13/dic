preoccupiedly:/ˌpriːˈɒkjupaɪdli/|/ˌpriːˈɑːkjʊpeɪdli/| adv.|心不在焉地，全神贯注地|In a preoccupied manner; absorbed in thought or other matters.  Showing signs of being preoccupied.|例句：He stared preoccupiedly out of the window, lost in thought. 他心不在焉地望着窗外，陷入沉思。

例句：She ate her dinner preoccupiedly, barely noticing the conversation around her. 她心不在焉地吃着晚餐，几乎没注意到周围的谈话。


近义词：absentmindedly, thoughtfully, distractedly


反义词：attentively, attentively, keenly


词性:副词
