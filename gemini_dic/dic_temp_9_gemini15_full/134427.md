nonirradiated: /ˌnɒnɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未经照射的，未受辐射的 | Not exposed to ionizing radiation.  This term is often used in the context of food, medical supplies, or other materials that could be sterilized or altered by radiation. | Example:  The nonirradiated spices retain their full flavor. 未经照射的香料保留了其全部风味。  The hospital uses only nonirradiated surgical instruments. 医院只使用未经照射的手术器械。 | 近义词：untreated, unsterilized (in specific contexts) | 反义词：irradiated
