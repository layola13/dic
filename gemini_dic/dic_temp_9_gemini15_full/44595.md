crowbar:/ˈkraʊbɑːr/| n. |撬棍；杠杆| A crowbar is a strong metal bar, usually with a curved end, used for levering or prying things open or apart.  It's a simple but powerful tool often used in construction, demolition, and other tasks requiring forceful separation of objects. |例句：He used a crowbar to pry open the jammed door. 他用撬棍撬开了卡住的门。

例句：The mechanic used a crowbar to remove the stubborn bolt. 技工用撬棍撬掉了那个顽固的螺栓。


近义词：lever, pry bar, wrecking bar|反义词：None specifically, but tools for joining or fastening could be considered opposites in function.|词性：noun
