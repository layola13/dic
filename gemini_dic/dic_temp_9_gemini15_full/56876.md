dose:/doʊs/| n.|剂量，份量|The amount of a medicine or drug taken at one time.  It can also refer to a quantity of something else.|例句：The doctor prescribed a dose of antibiotics twice a day.医生开了每天两次抗生素的剂量。|例句：She gave the plant a dose of fertilizer.她给植物施了一剂肥料。|近义词：portion, quantity, amount|反义词：overdose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor gives me a dose of medicine every morning. = 医生每天早上给我一剂药。
A single dose of this medicine is enough. = 单剂量的这种药就足够了。


一般过去时:
She took a dose of painkillers after the surgery. = 她手术后服用了一剂止痛药。
He received a large dose of radiation during treatment. = 他在治疗期间接受了大量的辐射。


现在完成时:
I have already taken my dose of medication. = 我已经服用了我的药。
The patient has received several doses of the serum. = 病人已经接受了几剂血清。


过去完成时:
He had already taken his dose before the doctor arrived. = 医生到达之前，他已经服药了。
She had received a large dose of bad news that day. = 那天她收到了很多坏消息。


将来时:
I will take my dose before bedtime. = 我睡前会服药。
The patient will receive another dose of chemotherapy tomorrow. = 病人明天将接受另一剂化疗。


虚拟语气:
If I took a larger dose, I might feel better. = 如果我服用更大的剂量，可能会感觉好些。
I wish I hadn't taken such a large dose. = 我真希望我没有服用这么大的剂量。


被动语态:
The medicine was administered in small doses. = 药物以小剂量服用。
The patient was given a dose of antibiotics intravenously. = 病人静脉注射了一剂抗生素。


疑问句:
What is the recommended dose for this medication? = 此药物的推荐剂量是多少？
How many doses are left in the bottle? = 瓶子里还剩多少剂量？


否定句:
He did not take his dose of medication. = 他没有服用他的药。
She hasn't yet received her dose of the vaccine. = 她还没有接种疫苗。



PHRASE->
lethal dose = 致死剂量
toxic dose = 毒性剂量
therapeutic dose = 治疗剂量
overdose = 过量服用
maintenance dose = 维持剂量
