chairlift:/ˈtʃɛrˌlɪft/| n. |缆车，吊椅|A chairlift is a type of aerial lift used to transport people uphill, typically in mountainous areas for skiing or other recreational activities.  It consists of a series of chairs suspended from a continuously moving cable. |例句：The chairlift took us to the top of the mountain in about ten minutes. 缆车大约十分钟就把我们送到了山顶。

The children enjoyed the scenic ride on the chairlift. 孩子们喜欢乘坐缆车欣赏风景。

|近义词：cable car, ski lift |反义词：None  |词性:名词
