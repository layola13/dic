shredding: /ʃˈredɪŋ/ | n. v. |撕碎；切碎；粉碎；销毁；(数据)粉碎|

英文解析:The act of tearing or cutting something into small pieces; the process of destroying something by cutting it into small pieces, often for security or disposal purposes.  It can also refer to the act of reducing something to fragments or small pieces.  In a data security context, it refers to the irreversible destruction of data.

例句:
He was shredding documents before the meeting. 他在会议前正在销毁文件。

The machine is shredding the paper into tiny strips. 这台机器正在把纸张切成细条。


近义词: tearing, ripping, cutting, destroying, pulverizing, disintegrating

反义词: preserving, saving, protecting, assembling, restoring


词性: Noun and Verb
