go-and:/ɡoʊænd/| phrasal verb| 去做…；快去… |This is a phrasal verb, not a single word.  It combines the verb "go" with the particle "and" to create a more forceful or urgent command. It implies immediate action. |例句：Go and get me a glass of water! = 快去给我倒杯水！ Go and see a doctor if you're feeling unwell. = 如果你感觉不舒服，就去看看医生。|近义词：go get, hurry up and|反义词: stay, remain.


USAGE->
This phrasal verb doesn't have tense variations in the same way a regular verb does. The tense is determined by the verb "go".

一般现在时:
Go and fetch the mail. = 去取邮件。
Go and tell him the news. = 去告诉他这个消息。

一般过去时:
I went and saw the movie. = 我去看电影了。
He went and apologized to her. = 他去向她道歉了。

现在完成时:
I have gone and broken my phone. = 我把手机弄坏了。
She has gone and spilled the coffee. = 她把咖啡洒了。

过去完成时:
I had gone and spent all my money. = 我把所有的钱都花光了。
They had gone and missed the train.  = 他们错过了火车。

将来时:
I will go and visit my grandma tomorrow. = 我明天去看望奶奶。
He will go and buy some groceries. = 他要去买些杂货。

虚拟语气:
If I had gone and asked him, he would have helped me. = 如果我去问他，他会帮助我的。
I wish I had gone and seen the exhibition. = 我真希望我去看了展览。

被动语态:
(This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it is a command or instruction.)


疑问句:
(Not typically used in a question format.  Instead, you would ask a question about the action that follows "go and")  Will you go and help him? = 你会去帮他吗？


否定句:
Don't go and tell anyone. = 别告诉任何人。
I didn't go and complain about it. = 我没有去抱怨这件事。


PHRASE->
Go and get it! = 去拿！
Go and see for yourself. = 自己去看吧。
Go and have a rest. = 去休息一下。
Go and relax! = 去放松一下！
Go and study! = 去学习！
