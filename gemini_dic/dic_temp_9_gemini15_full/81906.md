grifter:/ˈɡrɪftər/| n. |骗子，行骗者 |A grifter is a person who engages in petty or confidence tricks to swindle people out of money or other valuables.  They are often skilled at deception and manipulation. |例句：He was a smooth-talking grifter who conned elderly people out of their life savings. 他是个油嘴滑舌的骗子，骗取了老年人的毕生积蓄。

例句：The police caught the grifter red-handed trying to sell fake jewelry.  警方当场抓住这个骗子试图出售假珠宝。


近义词：con artist, swindler, scammer, hustler, cheat |反义词：honest person, philanthropist, benefactor
