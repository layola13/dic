reexchanging: /ˌriːɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | v. | 再交换；重新交换 | The act of exchanging something again;  a repeated exchange. This word is formed by the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "exchanging." It implies a second or further instance of exchanging items or information.  | Example: Reexchanging the gifts proved to be more complicated than anticipated.  重新交换礼物证明比预期的更复杂。 |近义词: re-trading,  interchanging again |反义词: keeping, retaining |动词
