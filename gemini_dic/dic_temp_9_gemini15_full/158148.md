platformer:/ˈplætˌfɔːrmər/ | n. | 平台游戏 | A video game in which the player controls a character that moves across platforms to reach a goal.  These games often involve jumping, running, and overcoming obstacles. | Example:  Super Mario Bros. is a classic example of a platformer.  超级马里奥兄弟是平台游戏的经典之作。  |近义词:jump'n'run game |反义词:  None specifically;  the antonym would depend on the contrasted genre (e.g., RPG, FPS).|名词
