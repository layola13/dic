reprioritizations:/ˌriːˌpraɪˈɔːrɪtaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 重新排序；重新确定优先级 | The reprioritizations of tasks led to a more efficient workflow.  重新确定任务的优先级导致工作流程更高效。|近义词：reorganizations, re-evaluations |反义词：prioritization (without re-)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reprioritizes projects based on market demand. 公司根据市场需求重新确定项目的优先级。
We reprioritize our tasks every Monday morning. 我们每个星期一早上都会重新安排我们的任务优先级。


一般过去时:
They reprioritized the budget after the unexpected expenses. 意外支出后，他们重新调整了预算的优先级。
The team reprioritized their goals following the new strategy.  团队在新战略指导下重新设定了目标的优先级。


现在完成时:
Management has reprioritized several key initiatives this year. 管理层今年已重新确定了几项关键举措的优先级。
We have reprioritized our efforts to focus on customer satisfaction. 我们已经重新调整了工作重点，以专注于客户满意度。


过去完成时:
They had reprioritized the research before the funding was secured. 他们在获得资金之前就已经重新确定了研究的优先级。
The project manager had reprioritized tasks to meet the deadline. 项目经理已经重新安排了任务的优先级以满足截止日期。


将来时:
We will reprioritize our resources once the new data is available.  一旦新的数据可用，我们将重新分配资源的优先级。
The board will reprioritize the company's investments next quarter. 董事会将在下个季度重新确定公司投资的优先级。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would reprioritize the less urgent tasks. 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新安排不太紧急的任务的优先级。
It is essential that we reprioritize our spending to align with the new goals. 至关重要的是，我们必须重新调整支出优先级以符合新目标。


被动语态:
The project was reprioritized due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，项目重新确定了优先级。
Many tasks were reprioritized after the crisis. 危机过后，许多任务的优先级都被重新调整了。


疑问句:
Will the project be reprioritized next month? 下个月项目会重新确定优先级吗？
Have the tasks been reprioritized according to the new schedule? 任务是否已根据新时间表重新确定优先级？


否定句:
The team did not reprioritize their work despite the setbacks. 尽管遇到挫折，团队并没有重新调整工作优先级。
We will not reprioritize the project unless we receive additional funding. 除非我们获得额外资金，否则我们不会重新确定项目的优先级。
