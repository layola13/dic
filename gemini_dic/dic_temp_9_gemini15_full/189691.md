salp:/sælp/| n.|海樽，樽海鞘|A salp is a free-swimming marine invertebrate belonging to the class Thaliacea.  They are barrel-shaped and transparent, often found in large colonies. They are filter feeders, consuming phytoplankton.|Example: The ocean current carried a large swarm of salps towards the shore.  海洋洋流把一大群海樽带到了岸边。|近义词：sea squirt (though not strictly a synonym, as sea squirts are sessile)|反义词：N/A
