cheater:/ˈtʃiːtər/| n.|作弊者，欺骗者|A person who cheats.  Someone who acts dishonestly in order to gain an advantage.|例句：He was caught cheating on the exam. 他考试作弊被抓住了。 He's a cheater in love. 他是感情骗子。|近义词：deceiver, fraud, swindler|反义词：honest person, truthful person|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cheats on his exams. = 他考试作弊。
She cheats at cards. = 她打牌作弊。


一般过去时:
He cheated on the test last week. = 他上周考试作弊了。
They cheated their way to success. = 他们通过欺骗获得成功。


现在完成时:
He has cheated on every test this semester. = 这学期他每次考试都作弊。
She has cheated me out of my money. = 她骗走了我的钱。


过去完成时:
He had cheated on the quiz before the teacher noticed. = 在老师注意到之前，他已经在测验中作弊了。
They had cheated their partners for years. = 他们已经欺骗他们的合作伙伴多年了。


将来时:
He will cheat on the next exam if he gets the chance. = 如果有机会，他会下次考试作弊。
She will cheat again if she isn't caught. = 如果她不被抓住，她还会再次作弊。


虚拟语气:
If he cheated, he would fail the class. = 如果他作弊，他就会不及格。
I wish he hadn't cheated on me. = 我真希望他没有欺骗我。


被动语态:
He was caught cheating. = 他被抓到作弊了。
The students were accused of cheating. = 学生们被指控作弊。


疑问句:
Did he cheat on the exam? = 他考试作弊了吗？
Is she a cheater? = 她是个骗子吗？


否定句:
He didn't cheat on the test. = 他考试没有作弊。
She wouldn't cheat, would she? = 她不会作弊，是吗？
