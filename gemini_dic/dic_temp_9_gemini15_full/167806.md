priming:/praɪmɪŋ/ | n. v. | 准备；启动；预先准备；启动；预处理 |

英文解析：The act of preparing something for use or action, or the state of being prepared.  It can refer to preparing something for a specific purpose, or to the activation of a particular response or idea through prior exposure.  In psychology, it refers to the implicit memory effect in which exposure to one stimulus influences the response to another stimulus.

例句：

The mechanic primed the engine before starting it.  = 机械师在启动发动机之前对其进行了预热。

The teacher primed the students for the upcoming exam. = 老师为即将到来的考试给学生们做了准备。

近义词：preparation, activation, initiation, preconditioning

反义词：deactivation, inhibition, suppression


词性：noun, verb
