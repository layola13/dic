commonest:/ˈkɒmənɪst/ /ˈkɑːmənɪst/ | adj. | 最常见的；最普通的 | Most frequent or widespread.  It indicates the superlative degree of common. |例句：The commonest cause of death is heart disease. 最常见的死因是心脏病。

例句：The commonest type of tree in this forest is the oak.  这片森林里最常见的树木是橡树。


近义词：most common, most frequent, prevalent, usual


反义词：rarest, least common, unusual, exceptional

词性：形容词
