reencloses:/ˌriːɪnˈkloʊzɪz/| v. |重新封闭；重新装入信封|To enclose again; to put back into an envelope or container.  The term implies a prior enclosing action. |例句：He reenclosed the letter with a more detailed explanation. 他用更详细的解释重新装入信件。

例句：The company reenclosed the package after discovering a missing item. 公司在发现缺少一件物品后重新包装了包裹。


近义词：repackage, reseal, re-envelope

反义词：open, unseal


词性：动词
