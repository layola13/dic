phraseogram:/ˈfreɪzoʊɡræm/ | n. | 短语图；词组图解 | A phraseogram is a visual representation of a phrase or idiom, often used in language learning or to illustrate grammatical structures.  It typically uses images or symbols to represent the meaning of the words in the phrase, making it easier to understand, especially for visual learners or those learning a new language. | Example: A phraseogram for "raining cats and dogs" might show cats and dogs falling from the sky.  | 近义词: idiom chart, phrase illustration | 反义词:  (no direct antonym)
