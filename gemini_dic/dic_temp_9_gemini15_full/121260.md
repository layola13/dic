misevaluated:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljuːeɪtɪd/| adj.| 评估错误的，误判的 |  To have wrongly assessed the value or importance of something.  This often implies a significant error in judgment. | Example: The company's stock was misevaluated, leading to significant losses for investors.  这家公司的股票被错误评估，导致投资者遭受重大损失。 |近义词：misjudged, underestimated, overestimated, mispriced |反义词：accurately evaluated, correctly assessed
