nonsupervisory:/ˌnɑːnˈsuːpərvaɪzəri/| adj. |非监督的，非管理的 | Not involving or requiring supervision.  This adjective describes employees, positions, or tasks that do not involve overseeing or managing others. |例句：The nonsupervisory employees received a bonus for their hard work.  非管理层员工因其辛勤工作而获得奖金。

例句：The nonsupervisory role focuses primarily on individual tasks.  非管理职位主要侧重于个人任务。


近义词：unsupervised, subordinate, rank-and-file


反义词：supervisory, managerial, overseeing
