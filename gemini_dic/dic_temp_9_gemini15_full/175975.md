ratting:/ˈrætiŋ/| n. v.|告密；背叛；破坏；（老鼠）侵扰|The act of informing on someone or something, often secretly or dishonestly;  the act of betraying someone or something; damaging or destroying something; infestation of rats.|例句：The ratting on his colleagues cost him his job. 告发同事让他丢了工作。|例句：The old house was suffering from serious ratting.  这座老房子遭到严重的鼠患。|近义词：informing, betraying, denouncing, sabotaging|反义词：loyalty, faithfulness, support|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ratting on his friends. = 他正在告发他的朋友们。
They are ratting out the secrets of the company. = 他们正在泄露公司的秘密。

一般过去时:
She ratted on her brother to the police. = 她向警方告发了她的哥哥。
The workers ratted on the management's illegal activities. = 工人们告发了管理层的非法活动。


现在完成时:
He has ratted on me several times. = 他已经告发我几次了。
They have ratted out the entire operation. = 他们已经泄露了整个行动计划。


过去完成时:
The thief had ratted on his accomplices before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷已经告发了同伙。
She had ratted out the plan before anyone realized it. = 在任何人意识到之前，她已经泄露了计划。


将来时:
I think he will rat on us if he's caught. = 我认为如果他被抓住了，他会告发我们。
They will rat out their boss if they lose their jobs. = 如果他们失去工作，他们会告发他们的老板。


虚拟语气:
If he had not ratted on us, we would have succeeded. = 如果他没有告发我们，我们就会成功了。
I wish he hadn't ratted out our secret. = 我真希望他没有泄露我们的秘密。


被动语态:
He was ratted on by his own brother. = 他被自己的兄弟告发了。
The information was ratted out to the press. = 信息被泄露给了媒体。


疑问句:
Did he rat on you? = 他告发你了吗？
Was the information ratted out intentionally? = 信息是被故意泄露的吗？


否定句:
He didn't rat on his friends, despite the pressure. = 尽管有压力，他也没有告发他的朋友们。
They didn't rat out the company's secrets. = 他们没有泄露公司的秘密。


PHRASE->
rat on someone = 告发某人
rat out someone = 告发某人（通常指更严重的背叛）
a rat race = 激烈的竞争
like a rat = 偷偷摸摸地
smell a rat = 觉得事情不对劲
