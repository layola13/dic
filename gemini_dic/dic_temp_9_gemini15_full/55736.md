dived:/daɪvd/| v.|潜水；俯冲；迅速下降|To plunge or thrust oneself into water; to descend rapidly.|The children dived into the swimming pool.孩子们跳入游泳池。|He dived for cover when he heard the gunshot. 听到枪声，他一头扎进掩体。|plunged, plunged into, plummeted|climbed, ascended


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dives into the pool every morning. 他每天早上都跳进游泳池。
She dives deep to find pearls. 她潜入深海寻找珍珠。

一般过去时:
They dived into the ocean to explore a shipwreck. 他们潜入大海探索沉船。
The bird dived swiftly to catch the fish. 鸟儿迅速俯冲捕捉鱼。

现在完成时:
I have dived in this lake many times. 我在这个湖里潜过很多次。
She has never dived from such a high cliff. 她从未从这么高的悬崖上跳过。

过去完成时:
He had dived several times before he found the treasure. 他潜了几次才找到宝藏。
They had already dived before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前他们已经潜过水了。

将来时:
We will dive in the Caribbean next summer. 我们明年夏天将在加勒比海潜水。
The plane will dive sharply to avoid the mountain. 飞机将急剧俯冲以避开山峰。

虚拟语气:
If I could dive, I would explore the coral reef. 如果我会潜水，我会去探索珊瑚礁。
I wish I had dived deeper to see the dolphins. 我真希望我潜得更深一点看看海豚。

被动语态:
The submarine was dived deep into the ocean. 潜艇潜入深海。
The treasure was dived for by many adventurers. 许多冒险家都去寻找宝藏。

疑问句:
Did you dive yesterday? 你昨天潜水了吗？
Have they ever dived in the sea? 他们曾在海里潜水吗？

否定句:
He didn't dive into the cold water. 他没有跳进冰冷的水里。
They haven't dived yet. 他们还没潜水呢。
