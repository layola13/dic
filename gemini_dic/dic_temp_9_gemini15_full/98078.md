inquiries:/ɪnˈkwaɪəriːz/| n.|询问；查询；调查|Questions or requests for information.|The customer service department received numerous inquiries about the product recall.  客户服务部门收到了许多关于产品召回的询问。  |Many inquiries were made about the missing documents. 许多关于丢失文件的询问都被提出来了。|questions, queries, investigations|answers, responses|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We make inquiries about the new policy. = 我们就新政策进行询问。
They regularly receive inquiries from potential customers. = 他们定期收到潜在客户的咨询。


一般过去时:
He made several inquiries before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前做了几次询问。
She inquired about the availability of tickets. = 她询问了票的供应情况。


现在完成时:
We have received many inquiries regarding the upcoming event. = 我们已经收到了许多关于即将到来的活动的询问。
They have made inquiries into the matter. = 他们已经对这件事进行了调查。


过去完成时:
She had made inquiries about the job before applying. = 在申请之前，她已经就这份工作进行了咨询。
They had received few inquiries prior to the advertising campaign. = 在广告宣传之前，他们收到的询问很少。


将来时:
We will make inquiries about the flight schedule. = 我们将就航班时刻表进行询问。
They will be making inquiries about the property’s history. = 他们将对该房产的历史进行调查。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would make more inquiries. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做更多调查。
I wish I had made inquiries sooner. = 我希望我早点进行询问。


被动语态:
Inquiries were made about the incident. = 关于此事进行了询问。
The matter is being investigated following inquiries from the public. = 在公众的询问之后，此事正在调查中。


疑问句:
Are there any inquiries about this project? = 关于这个项目有什么疑问吗？
Do you have any further inquiries? = 你还有什么其他疑问吗？


否定句:
They did not make any inquiries before purchasing the car. = 他们在买车之前没有进行任何询问。
We received no inquiries during the holiday season. = 我们在假期期间没有收到任何询问。
