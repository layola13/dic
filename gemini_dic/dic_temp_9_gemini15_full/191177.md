scarf:/skɑːrf/| n. |围巾，头巾 | A piece of fabric worn around the neck or head for warmth or decoration.  |例句：She wore a warm wool scarf to protect herself from the cold. 她戴着一条温暖的羊毛围巾来御寒。|例句：He tied a silk scarf around his head. 他在头上系了一条丝巾。|近义词：shawl, muffler |反义词：无

词源:
The word "scarf" comes from the Old Norse word "skarfa," meaning "to cover" or "to wrap".  It's related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
