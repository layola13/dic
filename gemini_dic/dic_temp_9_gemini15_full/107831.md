larcenous:/ˈlɑːrsənəs/| adj.| 偷窃的；有偷窃倾向的 | given to or characterized by theft; thievish.  |The larcenous employee embezzled thousands of dollars from the company.  那个有偷窃倾向的员工从公司贪污了数千美元。|近义词：thieving, pilfering, kleptomaniac (if a compulsion)|反义词：honest, upright, virtuous
