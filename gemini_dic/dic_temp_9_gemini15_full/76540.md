gallantly:/ˈɡæləntli/| adv. |勇敢地；潇洒地；体贴地 |In a brave and noble manner; courteously and gallantly.  |例句：He gallantly rescued the child from the burning building. 他勇敢地从燃烧的建筑物中救出了孩子。

例句：He gallantly offered his seat to the elderly woman. 他体贴地把座位让给了那位老妇人。


近义词：bravely, courageously, chivalrously, courteously


反义词：cowardly, cravenly, discourteously


词源：源自古法语 *galant,  最终源于拉丁语 *gallus "cock," referring to the bird's proud bearing.  The meaning shifted to denote a brave and chivalrous man, then to actions displaying such qualities.
