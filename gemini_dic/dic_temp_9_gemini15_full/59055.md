dwell:/dwel/| v.|居住，沉思，详述 |To live in a place; to think deeply about something; to discuss something at length.|例句：They dwell in a small cottage in the countryside. 他们住在乡下的一间小别墅里。  They dwelt on the problem for hours. 他们花了几个小时思考这个问题。|近义词：reside, inhabit, ponder, contemplate, elaborate|反义词：depart, leave, neglect, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dwells in a small apartment. = 他住在一间小公寓里。
We dwell on the past too much. = 我们对过去想得太多了。


一般过去时:
She dwelt in the city for many years. = 她在这个城市住了很多年。
They dwelt upon the details of the plan. = 他们详细阐述了计划的细节。


现在完成时:
He has dwelt in this house since childhood. = 他从孩提时代就住在这所房子里。
We have dwelt on this topic sufficiently. = 我们已经充分讨论了这个话题。


过去完成时:
She had dwelt in that city before moving to London. = 在搬到伦敦之前，她住过那个城市。
They had dwelt on the issue for days before reaching a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经考虑这个问题好几天了。


将来时:
They will dwell in a new house next year. = 他们明年将住进新房子。
We will dwell on the implications of this research. = 我们将详细探讨这项研究的意义。


虚拟语气:
If I dwelt in a castle, I would be very happy. = 如果我住在城堡里，我会非常高兴。
I wish I could dwell on this beautiful scenery longer. = 我真希望我能更长时间地欣赏这美丽的风景。


被动语态:
The matter was dwelt upon extensively in the report. = 报告中广泛讨论了这个问题。
The issue is being dwelt on by the committee. = 委员会正在仔细考虑这个问题。


疑问句:
Where do you dwell? = 你住在哪里？
What aspects of the problem are you dwelling on? = 你正在考虑这个问题的哪些方面？


否定句:
They do not dwell on their past mistakes. = 他们不会沉湎于过去的错误。
We did not dwell on the minor details. = 我们没有详述那些细枝末节。


PHRASE->
dwell on/upon = 仔细考虑；详述
dwell in/at = 居住于
dwell among = 生活在...之中
dwell with = 与...同住；仔细考虑
dweller = 居民
