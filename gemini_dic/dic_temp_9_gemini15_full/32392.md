chiller:/ˈtʃɪlər/| n.| 冷却器；使人感到害怕的人或事物 | A chiller is a machine used to cool liquids or gases, often used in industrial processes or for air conditioning.  It can also refer to something or someone that is frightening or unsettling. | 例句：The factory uses a large chiller to cool the production line.  工厂使用大型冷却器来冷却生产线。| 例句：The suspense in the movie made it a real chiller. 这部电影中的悬念使它成为一部真正令人毛骨悚然的电影。|近义词：refrigerator (for cooling liquids), coolant, thriller (for something frightening)|反义词：heater (opposite function), comforter (opposite effect)
