chibouk:/tʃɪˈbuːk/ | n. |水烟袋；烟管 |A long-stemmed pipe used for smoking tobacco, especially in the Middle East and North Africa.  It often has a long, flexible tube and a bowl for the tobacco.|Example: He puffed contentedly on his chibouk, watching the sunset. 他舒舒服服地抽着他的水烟袋，看着日落。|近义词：hookah, narghile, shisha pipe |反义词：None.
