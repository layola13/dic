prez:/prɛz/| n.|总统，主席|Shortened form of "president".  It is an informal term.|Example: The prez gave a speech. 总统发表了演讲。|近义词：president, chief|反义词：subordinate, underling|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prez approves the budget. = 总统批准了预算。
The prez attends many meetings. = 总统参加了许多会议。

一般过去时:
The prez signed the bill. = 总统签署了法案。
The prez visited the affected area. = 总统访问了受灾地区。

现在完成时:
The prez has announced a new policy. = 总统已经宣布了一项新政策。
The prez has served two terms. = 总统已经任职两届。

过去完成时:
The prez had already left when the news broke. = 消息传出时，总统已经离开了。
The prez had completed his speech before the questions began. = 在提问开始之前，总统已经完成了他的演讲。

将来时:
The prez will address the nation tomorrow. = 总统明天将向全国发表讲话。
The prez will travel to Europe next month. = 总统下个月将前往欧洲。

虚拟语气:
If the prez were here, he would settle the dispute. = 如果总统在这里，他会解决这场争端的。
I wish the prez would take action. = 我希望总统采取行动。

被动语态:
The budget was approved by the prez. = 预算得到了总统的批准。
The decision was made by the prez. = 决定是由总统做出的。

疑问句:
Is the prez coming today? = 总统今天会来吗？
Will the prez meet with the delegation? = 总统会和代表团会面吗？

否定句:
The prez did not veto the bill. = 总统没有否决该法案。
The prez hasn't commented on the situation. = 总统还没有就此事发表评论。
