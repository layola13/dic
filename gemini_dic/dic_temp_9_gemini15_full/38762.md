condones:/kənˈdoʊnz/| vt.|宽恕；纵容；默许|to accept or allow (something that is considered wrong or bad) to continue|The school cannot condone bullying. 学校不能容忍欺凌行为。|近义词：tolerate, overlook, excuse|反义词：condemn, denounce, censure

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He condones his son's bad behavior. = 他纵容他儿子的不良行为。
The government condones corruption. = 政府默许腐败。

一般过去时:
They condoned the violence. = 他们宽恕了暴力行为。
She condoned his lies. = 她原谅了他的谎言。

现在完成时:
We have condoned this behavior for too long. = 我们已经容忍这种行为太久了。
The company has condoned unethical practices. = 公司一直在纵容不道德的行为。

过去完成时:
He had condoned her actions before, but not this time. = 他以前曾原谅过她的行为，但这次不行。
The court had condoned similar actions in the past. = 法院过去曾默许过类似的行为。

将来时:
They will condone such actions no longer. = 他们将不再容忍此类行为。
The principal will not condone cheating on exams. = 校长不会纵容考试作弊。

虚拟语气:
If they condoned this, there would be chaos. = 如果他们纵容这个，就会造成混乱。
I wish they wouldn't condone such behavior. = 我希望他们不要纵容这种行为。

被动语态:
Such behavior should not be condoned. = 这种行为不应该被容忍。
His actions were condoned by his superiors. = 他的行为受到了上司的纵容。

疑问句:
Does the school condone this type of behavior? = 学校是否容忍这种行为？
Will the authorities condone this illegal activity? = 当局会纵容这项非法活动吗？

否定句:
We do not condone violence. = 我们不容忍暴力。
The judge did not condone the defendant's actions. = 法官没有宽恕被告的行为。
