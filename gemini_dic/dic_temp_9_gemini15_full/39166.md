congeal:/kənˈdʒiːl/| vt. vi. |凝固；冻结；使凝结|To change from a fluid to a solid state, usually by cooling; to become thick or stiff.|The jelly began to congeal in the bowl. 果冻开始在碗里凝固。|The blood congealed quickly in the cold air. 血在寒冷的空气中迅速凝固。|近义词：solidify, thicken, harden|反义词：melt, liquefy, thaw|
