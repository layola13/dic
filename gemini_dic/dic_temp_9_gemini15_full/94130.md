impeder:/ɪmˈpiːdə(r)/| n. v.|阻碍者；妨碍物；阻碍；妨碍|A person or thing that impedes something.  Something that delays or prevents progress.|The heavy traffic impeded our journey. 交通堵塞阻碍了我们的旅程。|The constant rain impeded the construction work.  持续的雨水阻碍了施工工作。|近义词：obstacle, hindrance, obstruction, deterrent|反义词：facilitator, aid, help|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rocks impede the flow of the river. = 岩石阻碍了河水的流动。
Noise impedes concentration. = 噪音妨碍集中注意力。


一般过去时:
The storm impeded our progress. = 暴风雨阻碍了我们的进展。
