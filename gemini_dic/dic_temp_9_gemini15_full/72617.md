flyboat:/ˈflaɪboʊt/ | n. |飞行船，水上飞机 |A flyboat is a type of aircraft that can take off and land on water.  It combines features of both an airplane and a boat.  |例句：The flyboat glided smoothly across the lake before rising into the air. = 飞行船在湖面上平稳地滑行，然后升空。The old flyboat was a popular tourist attraction. = 那艘老式飞行船是热门的旅游景点。|近义词：seaplane, hydroplane|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept might be a land-based aircraft.

词源:
The word "flyboat" is a compound word, combining "fly" (meaning to move through the air) and "boat" (a vessel for water travel).  It reflects the dual nature of the aircraft, capable of both flying and floating on water.
