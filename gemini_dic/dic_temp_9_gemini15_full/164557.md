preevolutionist: /ˌpriːˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst/ | n. |进化论之前的思想家，进化论前的人|A person who held views on the origin and development of life that predate the theory of evolution by natural selection.  Such views might include creationism, catastrophism, or other theories that did not involve the mechanism of natural selection. | Example:  Many preevolutionists believed in the immutability of species. 许多进化论之前的思想家相信物种不变性。| 近义词: pre-Darwinian, creationist (in some contexts) | 反义词: evolutionist
