rowed:/roʊd/| v.|划船；使…前进|To propel (a boat) through water using oars.  Also, to move something by rowing.|例句：They rowed across the lake.他们划船渡过了湖。

They rowed the boat swiftly to shore.他们迅速地划船到岸边。


近义词：oared, propelled|反义词：None specifically, but "to be stationary" is an antonym to the action.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rows every morning. 他每天早上都划船。
She rows the boat skillfully. 她熟练地划船。

一般过去时:
I rowed the boat yesterday. 我昨天划船了。
We rowed across the river. 我们划过了河。

现在完成时:
They have rowed across the ocean. 他们已经划船横渡了海洋。
She has rowed many times in her life. 她一生划过很多次船。

过去完成时:
He had rowed for hours before he reached the island. 他到达岛屿前已经划了好几个小时的船。
They had rowed many miles before they found shelter. 他们找到避难所之前已经划了很多英里。

将来时:
We will row to the island tomorrow. 我们明天将划船去岛上。
They will row in the competition next week. 他们下周将参加比赛划船。


虚拟语气:
If I had a boat, I would row across the lake. 如果我有一艘船，我会划船穿过湖。
I wish I could row better. 我希望我能更好地划船。

被动语态:
The boat was rowed by a skilled oarsman. 这艘船是由一位熟练的划船手划的。
The race was rowed in difficult conditions. 比赛在恶劣的条件下进行。

疑问句:
Did you row yesterday? 你昨天划船了吗？
Have they ever rowed a dragon boat? 他们曾经划过龙舟吗？

否定句:
I didn't row today. 我今天没有划船。

词源:
The word "rowed" comes from the Old English word "rōwan," meaning "to row."  This word is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
