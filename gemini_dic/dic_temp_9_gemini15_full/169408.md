pronotum:/ˈproʊnoʊtəm/| n. |前胸背板|The dorsal surface of the prothorax of an insect.  It is the top part of the first segment of the thorax, often forming a distinct plate-like structure.  The pronotum can vary greatly in shape, size, and texture, providing useful characteristics for insect identification.| The beetle's pronotum was brightly colored.  这只甲虫的前胸背板颜色鲜艳。|  shield, plate|  (无明确反义词，可考虑与其他胸部结构相对比)| 名词
