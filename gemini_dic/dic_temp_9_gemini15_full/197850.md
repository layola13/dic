ship-to-shore:/ʃɪp tu ʃɔːr/| adj. n.|船到岸的，船岸间的|Relating to or denoting communication or transport between a ship and the shore.  It often refers to the transfer of goods, people, or information between a vessel and land.|例句：The ship-to-shore communication system failed during the storm.  暴风雨期间船岸通信系统失灵了。The ship-to-shore transfer of cargo was delayed. 货物的船岸转移被耽搁了。|近义词：shore-to-ship, vessel-to-shore|反义词：shore-to-ship
