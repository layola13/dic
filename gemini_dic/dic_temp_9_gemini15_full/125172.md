multibuilding: /ˌmʌltiˈbɪldɪŋ/ | n. |多层建筑；多栋建筑物 | A complex of multiple buildings, often connected or in close proximity.  It refers to a group of buildings functioning as a single unit or sharing common resources. |例句：The new multibuilding campus houses all the university's departments. 新建的多栋建筑组成的校园容纳了大学的所有系。


The multibuilding complex provides a variety of amenities for its residents.  这个多层建筑群为其居民提供了各种便利设施。|近义词：building complex, multi-structure complex |反义词：single building, standalone building
