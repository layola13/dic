devoto: /dɪˈvoʊtoʊ/ | n. | 献身者；信徒；热心崇拜者 | A devoted follower or admirer; someone who is intensely dedicated to a cause, person, or ideal.  | 例句：She was a devoted follower of the guru. 她是一位虔诚的古鲁信徒。


He is a devoted husband and father. 他是一位尽职的丈夫和父亲。


近义词：devotee, enthusiast, adherent, disciple | 反义词：apathy, indifference, skeptic


词源:  From Latin *devovere "to vow, consecrate," from de- "completely" + vovere "to vow." The noun developed from the sense of someone who has made a vow or is completely dedicated.
