doctorate:/ˈdɒktərət/ /ˈdɑːktərət/ | n. | 博士学位；博士论文 | A doctorate is the highest academic degree awarded by a university.  It usually involves years of advanced study and the completion of a significant research project or dissertation. | She received her doctorate in physics from Harvard. 她获得了哈佛大学的物理学博士学位。 He is currently writing his doctorate thesis. 他目前正在撰写他的博士论文。 | PhD, degree | undergraduate degree, bachelor's degree | 名词
