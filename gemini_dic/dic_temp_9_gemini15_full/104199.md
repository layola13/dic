It often involves the overthrow of a previous government through a coup d'état.  Juntas typically suspend democratic processes and rule through authoritarian means. | Example: The military junta seized power after a bloody coup.  军事政变后，军政府夺取了政权。  The junta's rule was characterized by repression and human rights abuses.  军政府的统治以镇压和侵犯人权为特征。 |近义词: military dictatorship, ruling council, governing body |反义词: democracy, civilian government |名词
