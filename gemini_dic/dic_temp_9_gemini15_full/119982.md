millet:/ˈmɪlɪt/| n. |小米；谷子|A small-seeded cereal grain that is grown in warm regions and used as food for humans and animals. It is a hardy crop that can tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.|例句：Millet is a staple food in many parts of Africa. 小米是非洲许多地区的主食。|例句：The birds were feeding on the millet seeds. 鸟儿正在吃小米种子。|近义词：sorghum, grain|反义词：无
