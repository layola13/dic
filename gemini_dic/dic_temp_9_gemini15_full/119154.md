mewler:/ˈmjuːlər/| n. | 哀鸣者；(尤指猫) 喵喵叫的人|A mewler is someone who mews, especially a cat, or someone who cries or complains in a whining way.  The term often carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting a persistent and annoying sound. |例句：The mewler kept me awake all night.  那个不停喵喵叫的家伙让我整夜睡不着觉。|The incessant mewling of the baby irritated everyone.  婴儿持续不断的哭闹声激怒了每个人。|近义词：whiner, complainer, crybaby|反义词：calm person, stoic
