hectors:/ˈhɛktərz/| v.| 欺压；恐吓；训斥 | to bully or harass someone in a loud and aggressive manner.  |例句：He was always hectoring his younger brother. 他总是欺压他的弟弟。|例句：Don't hector me; I'm trying to concentrate. 别训斥我；我正努力集中注意力。|近义词：bully, browbeat, intimidate |反义词：soothe, comfort, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hectors his employees daily. = 他每天都欺压他的员工。
She hectors her children about their chores. = 她总是唠叨她的孩子们去做家务。


一般过去时:
He hectored me into agreeing to his plan. = 他强迫我同意他的计划。
The manager hectored the staff about meeting deadlines. = 经理训斥员工没有按时完成工作。


现在完成时:
He has hectored his way to the top. = 他靠欺压别人爬到了顶峰。
She has hectored him relentlessly for weeks. = 她已经连续几周无情地训斥他了。


过去完成时:
He had hectored her for years before she finally stood up to him. = 在她最终反抗他之前，他已经训斥了她好几年了。
They had hectored the witnesses into changing their testimony. = 他们之前曾恐吓证人改变证词。


将来时:
He will hector you if you don't agree. = 如果你不同意，他会训斥你。
She will likely hector him about his grades again. = 她很可能会再次因为他的成绩而训斥他。


虚拟语气:
If he hectored me again, I would leave. = 如果他再次恐吓我，我会离开。
I wish he wouldn't hector me so much. = 我希望他不要再这样训斥我了。


被动语态:
He was hectored into submission. = 他被恐吓到屈服。
The students were hectored by the teacher for being late. = 学生们因为迟到而被老师训斥。


疑问句:
Does he often hector people? = 他经常恐吓别人吗？
Is she hectoring you again? = 她又在恐吓你吗？


否定句:
He doesn't hector his friends. = 他不欺压他的朋友。
She didn't hector him about his mistakes. = 她没有因为他的错误而训斥他。


PHRASE->
hector someone into doing something =  强迫某人做某事
hector and bully = 欺压和恐吓
constantly hector =  постоянно ругать
relentlessly hector =  无情地训斥
to hector someone about something =  因为某事而训斥某人
