fibrilla:/fɪˈbrɪlə/| n. |细纤维；肌原纤维|A fibril is a small, thread-like structure, especially one found in muscle tissue or connective tissue.  It's a component of larger structures like fibers.  Fibrils are often composed of protein filaments arranged in a parallel manner. | Example:  Muscle fibers are made up of many smaller fibrils. 肌纤维是由许多更小的细纤维组成的。|近义词：fibril, filament |反义词：N/A
