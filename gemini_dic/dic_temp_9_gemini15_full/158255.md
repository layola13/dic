play games:/pleɪ ɡeɪmz/| v. |玩游戏|To engage in games or recreational activities for amusement or competition.|例句：The children love to play games after school. 孩子们放学后喜欢玩游戏。|例句：We played board games all evening. 我们整个晚上都在玩棋盘游戏。|近义词：play, gamble, frolic|反义词：work, study


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They play games every day. = 他们每天都玩游戏。
We play video games on weekends. = 我们周末玩电子游戏。

一般过去时:
She played games with her friends yesterday. = 她昨天和朋友们一起玩游戏。

现在完成时:
