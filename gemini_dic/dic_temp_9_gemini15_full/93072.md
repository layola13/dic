idolizes:/ˈaɪdəlaɪzɪz/| vt.|崇拜；盲目追捧；|To admire and respect someone very much, often more than is realistic.  It often implies an unrealistic or excessive admiration. |例句：She idolizes her older sister. = 她崇拜她姐姐。

例句：Many young people idolize famous singers and actors. = 许多年轻人崇拜着著名的歌手和演员。


近义词：worship, revere, admire, venerate|反义词：despise, scorn, disdain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He idolizes the basketball star. = 他崇拜这位篮球明星。
She idolizes her teacher. = 她崇拜她的老师。


一般过去时:
I idolized that band when I was younger. = 我年轻的时候崇拜那个乐队。
They idolized the famous author. = 他们崇拜那位著名的作家。


现在完成时:
She has idolized this artist for many years. = 她多年来一直崇拜这位艺术家。
He has idolized that superhero since childhood. = 他从小就崇拜那个超级英雄。


过去完成时:
I had idolized him before I met him in person. = 在我亲自见到他之前，我一直崇拜他。
They had idolized the singer before her scandals came out. = 在她丑闻曝光之前，他们一直崇拜这位歌手。


将来时:
She will continue to idolize him, no matter what happens. = 不管发生什么，她都会继续崇拜他。
They will likely idolize this new musician for years to come. = 未来几年，他们很可能会崇拜这位新音乐家。


虚拟语气:
If I were to meet my idol, I would be speechless. = 如果我见到我的偶像，我会说不出话来。
I wish I could idolize someone worthy of my admiration. = 我希望我能崇拜一个值得我敬佩的人。


被动语态:
The singer is idolized by millions of fans. = 这位歌手被数百万粉丝崇拜。
That actor was idolized by many during his prime. = 那位演员在巅峰时期受到许多人的崇拜。


疑问句:
Does she idolize any particular celebrity? = 她崇拜任何特定的名人吗？
Who do you idolize the most? = 你最崇拜谁？


否定句:
He doesn't idolize anyone blindly. = 他不会盲目崇拜任何人。
She doesn't idolize pop stars. = 她不崇拜流行歌星。


PHRASE->
盲目崇拜(blindly idolize)
公开崇拜(openly idolize)
秘密崇拜(secretly idolize)
过度崇拜(excessively idolize)
停止崇拜(cease to idolize)
