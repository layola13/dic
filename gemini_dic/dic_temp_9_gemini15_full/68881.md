fasteners:/ˈfæsnərz/| n. |紧固件，扣件 | Devices used to fasten or secure objects together.  They include things like screws, bolts, zippers, buttons, hooks, and clasps.  | Example: The furniture was held together with strong fasteners. 这家具是用结实的紧固件固定在一起的。|近义词：clasps, closures, attachments |反义词：unfasteners (not a common word, the opposite action is described using verbs like "unfasten" or "detach")
