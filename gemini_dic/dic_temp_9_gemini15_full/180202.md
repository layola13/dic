refortifying:/ˌriːfɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 再强化；重新巩固 |  The act of strengthening or reinforcing something again.  It implies a previous strengthening effort that needs further enhancement. | Example: The army is refortifying its defenses along the border.  军队正在沿边境重新巩固防御工事。 | 近义词：reinforcing, strengthening, bolstering | 反义词：weakening, undermining
