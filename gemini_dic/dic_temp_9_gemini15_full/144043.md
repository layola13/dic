outrighter:/ˈaʊtraɪtər/| n. |支架，支撑物；独木舟的平衡木；（尤指）强烈支持者|A device used to provide stability or support, especially on a boat or other structure.  It often refers to a stabilizing beam or structure extending outward from a boat's hull.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe someone who strongly supports a particular cause or person.|例句：The outriggers on the canoe kept it stable in the rough water.  独木舟上的平衡木使它在汹涌的水中保持稳定。


例句：He is an outrighter of the new political party. 他是这个新政党的坚定支持者。|近义词：stabilizer, support, proponent, advocate|反义词：opponent, detractor
