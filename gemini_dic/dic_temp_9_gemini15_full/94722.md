impropriator:/ɪmˈprɒprɪeɪtə(r)/| n. |不当的占用者；非法占有者 |A person who wrongly appropriates something, especially land or property.  An impropriator is someone who takes possession of something that doesn't rightfully belong to them, often without legal justification. This often involves land or property, but it can extend to other things as well. |The impropriator was finally brought to justice after years of evading the law.  非法占有者最终在逃亡多年后被绳之以法。|近义词：usurper, trespasser, squatter |反义词： rightful owner, legitimate possessor |名词
