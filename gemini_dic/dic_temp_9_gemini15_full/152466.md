pay-out:/ˈpeɪaʊt/| n. v.|支付，支出，分红，奖金 |The act of paying out money, especially a large sum.  A distribution of profits to shareholders. |例句：The company announced a generous pay-out to its employees. 公司宣布向员工支付丰厚的奖金。 The annual pay-out to shareholders was substantial.  股东的年度分红相当可观。|近义词：payment, disbursement, dividend, bonus |反义词：income, receipt, deposit|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company pays out dividends quarterly. = 公司每季度支付股息。
They pay out bonuses based on performance. = 他们根据业绩发放奖金。


一般过去时:
The lottery paid out a million dollars. = 彩票支付了一百万美元。
They paid out all their savings on the house. = 他们把所有的积蓄都花在了房子上。


现在完成时:
The government has paid out billions in aid. = 政府已经支付了数十亿美元的援助款项。
We have paid out a significant amount this month. = 这个月我们已经支付了一大笔款项。


过去完成时:
They had paid out all their money before the trip. = 他们在旅行前已经花光了所有的钱。
The company had paid out its profits before the scandal broke. =丑闻爆发前，公司已经支付了利润。


将来时:
The insurance company will pay out on the claim. = 保险公司将支付索赔款项。
They will pay out the winnings next week. = 他们将在下周支付奖金。


虚拟语气:
If the company was successful, they would pay out a larger bonus. = 如果公司成功了，他们将支付更高的奖金。
I wish they would pay out the money quickly. = 我希望他们能尽快付钱。
