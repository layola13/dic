psychological:/ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌsaɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 心理学的；心理的 | Relating to the mind and its functions; mental.  It can refer to mental processes, emotions, and behaviors. |  His psychological state was fragile after the accident. 他在事故后心理状态很脆弱。 The book explored the psychological impact of war. 这本书探讨了战争的心理影响。 | mental, emotional, psychiatric | physical, physiological | 形容词

词源:

The word "psychological" is derived from the Greek words "psyche" (ψυχή), meaning "soul," "mind," or "spirit," and "logos" (λόγος), meaning "word," "reason," or "study."  Therefore, "psychological" literally means "relating to the study of the mind."
