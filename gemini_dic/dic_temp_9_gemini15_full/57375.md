doyens:/ˈdɔɪɛnz/| n.|权威人士；专家；领导者|A doyens is a person who is highly respected and influential in a particular field, often due to their long experience and expertise.  They are considered leaders and experts in their domain.|例句：She is a doyen of the fashion industry. 她是一位时尚界的权威人士。

例句：The doyen of the art world opened the new gallery. 艺术界的权威人士为新的画廊举行了开幕仪式。


近义词：expert, authority, leader, master, veteran


反义词：novice, beginner, amateur, newcomer


词性:名词
