flyby:/ˈflaɪbaɪ/| n. |飞越；飞掠；近距离飞越|A flyby is a close passage of a spacecraft past a planet or other celestial body.  It is often used to gather data and images.  A flyby does not involve orbiting the body. |例句：The spacecraft performed a successful flyby of Jupiter.  航天器成功飞越了木星。

The probe's flyby of Mars provided valuable scientific data.  探测器飞掠火星提供了宝贵的科学数据。


近义词：flypast, flyover|反义词：orbit, landing|词性：名词
