re-coining:/ˌriːˈkɔɪnɪŋ/| v. |重新铸造；重新创造(词语)；重新使用(词语)|The act of creating new words or phrases, or using existing words or phrases in new and innovative ways.  It often involves altering the meaning or usage of an existing term. |例句：The poet's re-coining of everyday words gave them new, poetic meaning.  诗人对日常词汇的重新创造赋予了它们新的诗意含义。

The band's re-coining of classic rock songs was controversial but ultimately successful. 乐队对经典摇滚歌曲的重新演绎是有争议的，但最终获得了成功。|近义词：recasting, reinventing, repurposing|反义词：maintaining, preserving, retaining (original meaning)|动词
