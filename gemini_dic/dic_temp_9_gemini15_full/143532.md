outgrowth:/ˈaʊtɡroʊθ/| n.|生长结果，副产品，自然结果|A natural consequence or result of something.|The company's success is an outgrowth of years of hard work.  这家公司的成功是多年努力的结果。|近义词：result, consequence, product|反义词：cause, origin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant's outgrowth is rapid. = 植物的生长很快。
This problem is an outgrowth of poor planning. = 这个问题是糟糕的计划导致的。

一般过去时:
The outgrowth of the project was unexpected. = 项目的结果是出乎意料的。
His anger was an outgrowth of his frustration. = 他的愤怒是他沮丧的结果。

现在完成时:
The success has been an outgrowth of dedication and perseverance. = 成功是奉献和毅力的结果。
The current situation is an outgrowth of past mistakes. = 目前的情况是过去错误的结果。

过去完成时:
The argument had been an outgrowth of misunderstandings. = 争论是误解的结果。
The damage had been an outgrowth of neglect. = 损坏是忽视的结果。

将来时:
The new policy will be an outgrowth of the current review. = 新政策将是当前审查的结果。
His future success will be an outgrowth of his hard work. = 他未来的成功将是他努力工作的结果。

虚拟语气:
If we had planned better, this wouldn't have been an outgrowth of our failure. = 如果我们计划得更好，这就不应该是我们失败的结果。
If he had worked harder, his success would have been a greater outgrowth. = 如果他更努力工作，他的成功将会更大。

被动语态:
The company's growth was considered an outgrowth of effective management. = 公司的增长被认为是有效管理的结果。
This problem is seen as an outgrowth of poor communication. = 这个问题被认为是不良沟通的结果。

疑问句:
Is this a natural outgrowth of the process? = 这是这个过程的自然结果吗？
What was the outgrowth of their collaboration? = 他们合作的结果是什么？

否定句:
This isn't an outgrowth of intentional actions. = 这不是故意行为的结果。
That wasn't an outgrowth of the plan. = 那不是计划的结果。


PHRASE->
a natural outgrowth = 自然的结果
an outgrowth of = ...的结果
a logical outgrowth = 合乎逻辑的结果
a direct outgrowth = 直接的结果
an unexpected outgrowth = 意想不到的结果
