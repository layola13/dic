programmed:/ˈproʊɡræmd/ | adj. v. | 程序化的；预先编程的；计划好的 |  Something that is programmed is controlled by a computer program or has been planned in advance.  |例句：The robot is programmed to follow a specific set of instructions.  这个机器人被编程为遵循一组特定的指令。

The washing machine has a programmed cycle for different fabrics. 这台洗衣机有针对不同织物的预设程序。


近义词：prearranged, planned, scheduled, automated | 反义词：improvised, spontaneous, unplanned


词性: Adjective (adj.), Verb (v.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The computer programs the robot's movements. = 计算机编程控制机器人的动作。
The system automatically programs the lights to turn on at sunset. = 系统自动编程使灯光在日落时自动开启。


一般过去时:
I programmed the VCR to record the show. = 我设定录像机录制节目。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
