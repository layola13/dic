pulvinulus:/pʌlvɪˈnjuːləs/| n. |小垫；小枕；（植物）小叶枕|A pulvinulus is a small cushion-like structure, often found in plants at the base of a leaflet or leaf, allowing for movement.  It's a small swelling or pad. In botany, it specifically refers to the swollen base of a leaflet or petiole, enabling the leaf to move in response to stimuli. |Example: The pulvinuli at the base of the leaves allowed the plant to fold its leaflets at night.  植物叶片基部的叶枕使植物能够在夜间折叠叶片。|近义词：cushion, pad, swelling |反义词：N/A
