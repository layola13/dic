five-spice: /ˈfaɪvˌspaɪs/ | n. |五香粉| A blend of five spices, typically including star anise, Sichuan peppercorns, cloves, cinnamon, and fennel seeds.  This blend is commonly used in Chinese cooking to add a complex and savory flavor profile.  The exact proportions of each spice can vary depending on the recipe and regional preferences. | The five-spice powder added a wonderful depth of flavor to the duck. 五香粉为鸭子增添了美妙的香味。 The recipe called for a generous amount of five-spice powder. 食谱需要大量的五香粉。|近义词：Chinese spice blend, mixed spice |反义词：无
