nonconfiscations:/ˌnɑːnˌkɒnfɪˈskeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非没收；不没收 | The absence of confiscations; instances or acts of not confiscating.  This word is relatively rare and often appears in legal or economic contexts. It describes situations where property or assets are not seized by authorities. | Example: The government's policy of nonconfiscations encouraged foreign investment. 政府的不没收政策鼓励了外国投资。 | 近义词: non-seizures, abstentions from confiscation | 反义词: confiscations, seizures
