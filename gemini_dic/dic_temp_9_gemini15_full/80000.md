go-karts:/ˈɡoʊkɑːrts/ | n. |小型赛车，卡丁车 |A go-kart is a small, open-wheeled motor vehicle designed for racing or recreational use.  They are typically powered by a small gasoline engine or an electric motor. |例句：My son loves racing go-karts at the local track. = 我儿子喜欢在当地的赛道上驾驶卡丁车比赛。

例句：We rented go-karts for an hour and had a blast. = 我们租了卡丁车玩了一个小时，玩得很开心。


近义词：kart, mini-racing car |反义词：  (无直接反义词，可考虑大型赛车等作为对比)|词性: 名词
