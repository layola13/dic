reaccompanying:/ˌriːəˈkʌmpənɪŋ/| v.|再次陪伴，重新陪伴 |To accompany again; to go with someone or something again.  |例句：After a brief separation, they were reaccompanying each other on their travels.  再次分开后，他们在旅行中再次陪伴彼此。  |例句：The band reaccompanied the singer for the encore.乐队为返场演出再次为歌手伴奏。|近义词：rejoining, accompanying again|反义词：leaving, abandoning


USAGE->

The musicians reaccompany the singer during the second act. 音乐家们在第二幕中再次为歌手伴奏。
