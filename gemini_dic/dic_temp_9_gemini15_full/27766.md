canneries:/ˈkænəriːz/| n. |罐头厂（复数）|Factories where food, especially fish or fruit, is canned.  Canneries are often located near sources of the raw materials they process, such as fishing ports or agricultural regions.  They employ various processes including cleaning, preparing, canning, and labeling the products.| Many canneries along the coast were closed due to overfishing.  许多沿海的罐头厂由于过度捕捞而关闭了。
