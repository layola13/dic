skin-diving:/ˈskɪnˌdaɪvɪŋ/| n. |潜水（不用潜水设备）|Skin-diving is a popular water sport that involves swimming underwater without the use of scuba gear.  Skin divers typically use only a mask, snorkel, and fins.  |例句：He enjoys skin-diving in the clear waters of the Caribbean. 他喜欢在大西洋清澈的海水中潜水。

例句：They went skin-diving and saw many colorful fish.  他们去潜水，看到了许多色彩鲜艳的鱼。|近义词：snorkeling, free diving|反义词：scuba diving
