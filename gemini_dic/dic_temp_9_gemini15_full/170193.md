protostelic:/ˌproʊtoʊˈstɛlɪk/| adj.| 原生中柱的 | Relating to or denoting a type of stele in which the central vascular tissue is a solid core of xylem surrounded by a ring of phloem and a pericycle.  It represents a relatively simple and primitive type of stelar organization found in some vascular plants. | The protostele of this fern is characteristically haplostelic.  这个蕨类的原生中柱具有单中柱的典型特征。|近义词:haplostelic(单中柱的), actinostelic(星状中柱的)|反义词:siphonostele(管状中柱的), eustele(真中柱的)
