inaugurative:/ɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪtɪv/| adj. |就职的；开幕的；创立的 |Marking the beginning of something new.  The inaugural speech of a president, for example, marks the start of their term.  The word often implies importance or significance. |例句：The inaugural address was broadcast live to a global audience.  就职演说向全球观众现场直播。

例句：This year’s festival is an inaugural event. 今年的节日是开幕活动。


近义词：initial, introductory, opening, first


反义词：final, concluding, closing


词性:形容词
