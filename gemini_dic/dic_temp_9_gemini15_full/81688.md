greenfish: /ˈɡriːnˌfɪʃ/ | n. |绿鱼，绿色的鱼| A fish that is green in color.  The term is not a specific species but a descriptive term. |例句：I saw a greenfish swimming in the shallows. 我看到一条绿色的鱼在浅水里游泳。  The aquarium contained several exotic greenfish. 水族馆里有一些奇特的绿鱼。|近义词：green-colored fish, verdant fish|反义词：redfish, blackfish
