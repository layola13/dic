postcard:/ˈpoʊstkɑːrd/| n. |明信片|A postcard is a piece of thick paper or thin cardboard, typically rectangular, that is sent through the post without an envelope.  It usually has a picture on one side and space for a message on the other.|I sent a postcard to my family from Paris. 我从巴黎寄了一张明信片给我的家人。|greeting card, picture card|letter, email|名词
