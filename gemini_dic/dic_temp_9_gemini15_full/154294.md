The word suggests a sense of continuous, often inescapable, recurrence. |  The peristrephic nature of history often leads to the repetition of past mistakes.  历史的循环往复常常导致人们重复过去的错误。 The peristrephic cycle of life, death, and rebirth is a central theme in many mythologies.  生死轮回的循环往复是许多神话中的核心主题。 | 近义词：cyclical, recurrent, repetitive, rotary | 反义词：linear, progressive, unidirectional |  adj.
