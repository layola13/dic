invaders:/ɪnˈveɪdərz/| n.|入侵者，侵略者|People or things that invade a place or country.  They are often associated with aggression and hostility.|例句：The invaders were quickly defeated by the local army.  入侵者很快就被当地军队击败了。

例句：Alien invaders threatened to destroy the planet. 外星入侵者威胁要摧毁地球。


近义词：conquerors, aggressors, intruders, raiders

反义词：defenders, protectors, liberators


词性:名词
