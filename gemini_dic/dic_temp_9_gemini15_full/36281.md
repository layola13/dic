coheading:/ˈkoʊˌhediŋ/ | n. | 副标题，小标题 | A secondary heading that provides further detail or clarification under a main heading.  It's often used in documents, reports, or articles to organize information hierarchically. | Example: The main heading was "Project Overview," and the co-heading was "Budget Allocation."  = 主要标题是“项目概述”，副标题是“预算分配”。|近义词：subheading, subtitle | 反义词：main heading, title
