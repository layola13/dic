inauguratory:/ɪˈnɔːɡjʊrəˌtɔːri/| adj.|就职的；开幕的；开创性的| relating to or marking the beginning of something new.|The president delivered an inaugural address.总统发表就职演说。|近义词：initial, opening, commencing |反义词：final, concluding, closing
