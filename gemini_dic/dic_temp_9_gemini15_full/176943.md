realized:/ˈriːəlaɪzd/| v. |意识到，实现，了解|To understand a situation, fact, or feeling fully; to achieve something that you were hoping to do.| I suddenly realized my mistake. 我突然意识到我的错误了。| understand, comprehend, grasp | ignored, overlooked |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He realizes his potential. 他意识到他的潜力。
She realizes the importance of education. 她意识到教育的重要性。

一般过去时:
I realized I was wrong. 我意识到我错了。
They realized their dream. 他们实现了他们的梦想。

现在完成时:
We have realized the benefits of this program. 我们已经意识到了这个项目的益处。
She has realized her ambition to become a doctor. 她已经实现了成为一名医生的抱负。

过去完成时:
He had realized his mistake before he spoke. 他在说话之前已经意识到自己的错误了。
They had realized the danger before it was too late. 他们在为时已晚之前意识到了危险。

将来时:
I will realize my goals. 我将实现我的目标。
We will realize the importance of teamwork. 我们将会意识到团队合作的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If I had realized the consequences, I would not have done it. 如果我意识到了后果，我就不会那样做了。
I wish I had realized sooner. 我希望我早点意识到。

被动语态:
The potential of the project was realized. 项目的潜力得到了实现。
The benefits of the new technology are being realized. 新技术的益处正在被实现。

疑问句:
Do you realize the risks involved? 你意识到其中涉及的风险吗？
Has he realized his mistake yet? 他意识到自己的错误了吗？

否定句:
I didn't realize how late it was. 我没有意识到已经这么晚了。
He didn't realize the gravity of the situation. 他没有意识到局势的严重性。
