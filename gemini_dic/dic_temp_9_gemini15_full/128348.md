neutralized:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzd/ | vt. | 使无效；使中和；抵消；压制 | To make something ineffective or harmless; to counteract or balance the effect of something. | 例句：The antidote neutralized the poison.  解毒剂中和了毒药。

The army neutralized the enemy's attack.  军队挫败了敌人的进攻。


近义词：counteracted, cancelled out, offset, deactivated | 反义词：activated, intensified, exacerbated | 词性：动词
