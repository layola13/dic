preresemblances: /ˌpriːrɪˈzɛmblənsɪz/ | n. | 预先相似之处，先兆相似 |  Plural of preresemblance.  Refers to similarities or resemblances that exist before something else happens or is observed.  It implies a foreshadowing or anticipation of a later, more complete resemblance.  The word is relatively uncommon. |


例句：

The early sketches showed preresemblances to the final painting, indicating the artist's clear vision from the outset.  早期草图显示出与最终绘画的预先相似之处，表明艺术家从一开始就具有清晰的视野。

The initial test results displayed some preresemblances to the predicted outcome, prompting further investigation. 初步测试结果显示出与预期结果的一些预先相似之处，促使进一步调查。


近义词：precursors, forerunners, anticipations, foreshadowings


反义词：discrepancies, differences, dissimilarities


词性：名词
