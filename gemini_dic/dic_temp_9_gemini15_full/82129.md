grokking:/ˈɡrɑːkɪŋ/| v. |彻底理解，掌握；直觉地理解|To understand something intuitively and thoroughly.  It implies a deep, almost intuitive grasp of the subject matter.|例句：After weeks of study, I finally grokked the concept of quantum physics.  经过几周的学习，我终于彻底理解了量子物理的概念。

例句：She grokked the situation immediately, knowing exactly what to do. 她立即理解了情况，知道该怎么做。


近义词：understand, grasp, comprehend, fathom, get,  master

反义词：misunderstand, misconceive, fail to grasp
