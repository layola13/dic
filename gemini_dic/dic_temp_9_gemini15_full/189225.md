sacristan:/ˈsækrɪstən/| n.|司库；圣器保管员|A sacristan is a person who is responsible for the care of a church and its sacred objects.  They often assist the priest or minister during services. |The sacristan carefully polished the chalice before the mass. 司库在弥撒前仔细擦拭了圣杯。 The sacristan unlocked the vestry for the priest. 司库为牧师打开了圣器室。| sexton, verger |  |名词
