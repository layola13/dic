freebooted:/ˌfriːˈbuːtɪd/| v.|免费获取（软件或服务）, 盗版 |To obtain (software or services) without paying for them; to pirate.  The term often implies a more casual or opportunistic approach to unauthorized access than "piracy."| 例句：He freebooted the software instead of buying a license. 他免费获取了软件而不是购买许可证。 They freebooted the premium features of the streaming service. 他们免费使用了流媒体服务的付费功能。|近义词：pirate, pilfer, steal |反义词：purchase, subscribe, license
