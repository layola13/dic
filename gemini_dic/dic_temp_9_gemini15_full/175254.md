railwayman:/ˈreɪlweɪmæn/| n. |铁路工人，铁路职员|A railwayman is a person who works for a railway company, often in a manual or operational role.  The term can encompass various jobs, from track maintenance to train operation. |He was a railwayman for over thirty years, retiring with a generous pension. 他在铁路工作了三十多年，退休时领到了一笔丰厚的退休金。|railroad worker, railway employee|businessman, office worker|名词
