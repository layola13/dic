justify:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ/ | vt. |证明…是正当的；为…辩护；调整（文字）使其对齐 | To show or prove to be right or reasonable.  To align or arrange (text) so that the lines are even on both sides. |例句：He tried to justify his actions. 他试图为他的行为辩护。 |例句：The text was justified to the left margin. 文本左对齐。|近义词：vindicate, defend, rationalize, align |反义词：condemn, refute, misalign


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He justifies his actions with his beliefs. = 他用他的信仰来为他的行为辩护。
She justifies her decision based on the facts. = 她根据事实来证明她的决定是正确的。

一般过去时:
The court justified the judge's decision. = 法院证明了法官的判决是正确的。
He justified his lateness by saying there was traffic. = 他解释说路上堵车来解释他迟到的原因。

现在完成时:
They have justified their investment in the project. = 他们已经证明了他们对这个项目的投资是合理的。
She has justified her actions to her superiors. = 她已经向她的上司解释了自己的行为。


过去完成时:
He had justified his actions before the hearing began. = 在听证会开始之前，他已经为自己的行为辩护了。
They had justified their decision with a detailed report. = 他们用一份详细的报告证明了他们的决定是正确的。


将来时:
She will justify her claims with evidence. = 她将用证据来证明她的说法。
He will justify his absence from the meeting. = 他将解释他缺席会议的原因。


虚拟语气:
If he could justify his actions, he would feel better. = 如果他能证明自己的行为是正确的，他会感觉更好。
I wish I could justify my spending habits. = 我希望我能证明我的消费习惯是合理的。


被动语态:
His actions were justified by the circumstances. = 他的行为由于当时的处境而被认为是合理的。
The decision was justified by the board of directors. = 该决定得到了董事会的认可。


疑问句:
How can you justify such behavior? = 你怎么能为这种行为辩护呢？
Does this evidence justify the conclusion? = 这证据能证明结论的正确性吗？


否定句:
He couldn't justify his absence. = 他无法解释他缺席的原因。
The evidence did not justify a conviction. = 证据不足以定罪。


PHRASE->
justify oneself = 为自己辩护
justify the means = 为方法辩护
justify the end = 为目的辩护
fully justify = 完全证明
justify the expense = 证明花费合理
