ghosting's:/ˈɡoʊstɪŋz/ | n. |幽灵般消失；突然中断联系的行为 |The act of ending a personal relationship with someone by abruptly ceasing all communication.  This is often done without explanation or warning. |Example:  His ghosting's left her feeling confused and hurt.  他的突然消失让她感到困惑和受伤。 |近义词:  disappearing act, silent treatment, ditching |反义词:  open communication, honest breakup
