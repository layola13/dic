jobcentre: /ˈdʒɒbˌsɛntər/ | n. |就业中心|A public agency that provides job search assistance and other employment-related services to unemployed individuals.  It helps people find jobs, provides training and advice, and may offer benefits information.|Example: He went to the jobcentre to register as unemployed. 他去就业中心登记失业。|近义词：employment agency, employment office |反义词: N/A

She visits the jobcentre regularly. = 她定期去就业中心。

He registered at the jobcentre last week. = 他上周在就业中心注册。
