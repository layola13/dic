This term is less common and often used in specific contexts like biology or ecology, referring to the mechanisms an organism uses to survive and thrive.|例句：The animal's self-conservations instincts kicked in when it sensed danger.  = 动物在察觉到危险时启动了自我保护本能。

The plant's self-conservations mechanisms helped it survive the drought. = 植物的自我保存机制帮助它度过了干旱。|近义词：self-preservation, survival instincts, self-protection|反义词：self-destruction, self-sacrifice|名词
