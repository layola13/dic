inburst: /ˈɪnbɜːrst/ | n. | 突然涌入；爆发；突发 | A sudden, large influx or outburst.  It often refers to a sudden increase in something, like data, emotions, or activity. | 例句：The inburst of tourists overwhelmed the small town.  游客的突然涌入使这个小镇不堪重负。 | 例句：There was a sudden inburst of anger from the audience.  观众席上突然爆发出一阵怒火。| 近义词：influx, surge, outburst, eruption | 反义词：decline, decrease, dwindling |  名词
