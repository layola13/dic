re-inflating:/ˌriːɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新充气；使重新膨胀 | The act of inflating something again. This usually refers to something that has previously been deflated or partially deflated, such as a tire, balloon, or even an economy.  |例句：The mechanic is re-inflating the flat tire. 机械师正在给瘪了的轮胎充气。

The company is trying to re-inflate the struggling economy. 公司正试图使低迷的经济复苏。 |近义词：re-inflate, pump up,  replenish (in the context of an economy) |反义词：deflating, puncturing
