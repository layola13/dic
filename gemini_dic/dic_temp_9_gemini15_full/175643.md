ransacked:/ˈrænsækt/| vt.|洗劫；彻底搜查|To search a place thoroughly and violently, especially in order to steal things.  It implies a chaotic and destructive search.|例句：The burglars ransacked the house, leaving everything in a mess.  窃贼洗劫了这所房子，把一切都弄得乱七八糟。

例句：The police ransacked the suspect's apartment looking for evidence.  警方彻底搜查了嫌疑人的公寓以寻找证据。


近义词：plunder, loot, sack, pillage

反义词：organize, tidy, restore
