overcatch:/ˈoʊvərˌkætʃ/ | v. | 过度捕捞 | The act of catching more fish than is sustainable, often leading to depletion of fish stocks and ecological damage.  | Example:  The overfishing of cod in the North Atlantic has led to a dramatic decline in the population.  过度捕捞北大西洋的鳕鱼导致其数量急剧下降。 |近义词：overfishing, excessive fishing | 反义词：sustainable fishing, responsible fishing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overcatch tuna in the Pacific Ocean. = 他们在太平洋过度捕捞金枪鱼。
Fishermen often overcatch during peak spawning season. = 渔民在产卵高峰期经常过度捕捞。


一般过去时:
The fishing fleet overcaught the salmon last year. = 渔船去年过度捕捞了鲑鱼。
They overcaught the quota by 20%. = 他们超额捕捞了20%。


现在完成时:

The fishing industry will likely overcatch if profits remain high. = 如果利润仍然很高，渔业很可能会过度捕捞。


虚拟语气:
