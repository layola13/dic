hambone:/ˈhæmˌboʊn/| n.|火腿骨；(非正式)傻瓜|A piece of bone from a ham.  The term is also used informally as a term of endearment or insult, similar to "dummy" or "fool".|例句：He gnawed on the hambone. 他啃着火腿骨。

例句：Don't be such a hambone! 别那么傻！


近义词：ham shank, bone (in the literal sense), dummy, fool


反义词：N/A (as a food item;  as an insult, the opposite would be someone intelligent or clever)


词性:名词
