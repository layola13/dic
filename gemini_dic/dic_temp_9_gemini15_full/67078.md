exploitation:/ˌɛksploɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|剥削；开发；利用；运用 |The act of using something in a way that helps you unfairly.  It can refer to the unfair treatment of workers, the overuse of natural resources, or the manipulation of a situation for personal gain. |例句：The exploitation of workers in sweatshops is a serious human rights issue.  血汗工厂对工人的剥削是一个严重的人权问题。

例句：The company's exploitation of its employees led to widespread discontent. 公司对员工的剥削导致了广泛的不满。


近义词：abuse, misuse, oppression, use, development |反义词：fairness, justice, protection, conservation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company exploits its workers. = 公司剥削其工人。
She exploits every opportunity. = 她利用每一个机会。


一般过去时:
They exploited the natural resources. = 他们开发了自然资源。
He exploited his position to gain advantage. = 他利用自己的职位谋取私利。


现在完成时:
They have exploited the oil fields for decades. = 几十年来，他们一直在开采油田。
We have exploited the new technology to improve efficiency. = 我们已经利用新技术提高了效率。


过去完成时:
They had exploited the workers before the law was passed. = 在法律通过之前，他们就已经剥削工人了。
The company had exploited the loophole in the regulations. = 公司已经利用了规章制度中的漏洞。


将来时:
They will exploit the new market. = 他们将开发新的市场。
He will exploit his talents to the fullest. = 他将充分发挥自己的才能。


虚拟语气:
If they exploited the resources responsibly, the environment would be better. = 如果他们负责任地开发资源，环境就会更好。
I wish they hadn't exploited the workers so ruthlessly. = 我真希望他们没有如此残酷地剥削工人。


被动语态:
The workers were exploited by the factory owner. = 工人们被工厂老板剥削了。
The resources are being exploited at an unsustainable rate. = 资源正在以不可持续的速度被开发。


疑问句:
Are they exploiting the workers? = 他们正在剥削工人吗？
Is this company exploiting its market position? = 这家公司正在利用其市场地位吗？


否定句:
The company does not exploit its employees. = 公司不剥削其员工。
They did not exploit the situation for personal gain. = 他们没有利用这种情况谋取私利。
