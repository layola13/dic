scarecrowy: /ˈskeəkreʊi/ | adj. | 稻草人似的；吓人的；令人毛骨悚然的 | Resembling a scarecrow; looking thin, gaunt, and frightening.  |例句：The old house had a scarecrowy appearance, with its broken windows and overgrown garden.  那栋老房子看起来像个稻草人，窗户破了，花园里杂草丛生。

The scarecrowy figure in the field frightened the children. 田地里稻草人似的景象吓坏了孩子们。


近义词：frightening, spooky, creepy, gaunt, ragged,  thin


反义词：robust, healthy, plump, well-fed


词性: 形容词
