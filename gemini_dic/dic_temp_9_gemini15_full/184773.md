resupply:/ˌriːsəˈplaɪ/| vt. n.| 再供应；补充供应 | To supply again; replenish.  |例句：The troops were resupplied by air. 空中为部队进行了物资补充。

The outpost needs a resupply of food and ammunition. 前哨站需要补充食物和弹药。|近义词：replenish, restock, refill |反义词：deplete, exhaust


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They resupply the store daily. = 他们每天都给商店补充货品。
We resupply the troops regularly. = 我们定期为军队补充物资。

一般过去时:
The team resupplied the climbers with water and food. = 该团队为登山者补充了水和食物。
They resupplied the base camp yesterday. = 他们昨天为大本营补充了物资。

现在完成时:
The army has resupplied its troops several times. = 军队已经多次为其部队补充了物资。
We have resupplied the warehouse with new products. = 我们已经用新产品补充了仓库的库存。

过去完成时:
They had resupplied the station before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经为该站补充了物资。
The soldiers had resupplied their weapons before the battle. = 士兵们在战斗前已经补充了武器弹药。

将来时:
We will resupply the remote village next week. = 我们下周将为偏远村庄补充物资。
The company will resupply the shelves tomorrow. = 公司明天将补充货架上的商品。

虚拟语气:
If we had resupplied the ship earlier, it wouldn't have run out of fuel. = 如果我们早点为船只补充燃料，它就不会燃料耗尽了。
I wish they had resupplied the hospital with medicine. = 我希望他们已经为医院补充了药品。

被动语态:
The outpost was resupplied by helicopter. = 前哨站由直升机进行了物资补充。
The shelves are resupplied every morning. = 货架每天早上都会补充商品。

疑问句:
Will the expedition be resupplied before they reach the summit? = 探险队在到达山顶之前会得到物资补充吗？
When was the last time the station was resupplied? = 该站上次补充物资是什么时候？

否定句:
They did not resupply the troops in time. = 他们没有及时为军队补充物资。
The store hasn't been resupplied yet. = 商店还没有补充商品。
