insulation:/ˌɪnsʊˈleɪʃən/| n. |绝缘，隔热，隔音|The material's ability to prevent the passage of heat, electricity, or sound.|The house needs better insulation to keep it warm in winter.|近义词：isolation, protection|反义词：conduction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The insulation protects the wires from short circuits. = 绝缘层保护电线免受短路。
Good insulation keeps the house warm in winter. = 良好的隔热措施使房屋在冬天保持温暖。

一般过去时:
The old insulation was damaged by the storm. = 旧的绝缘材料被暴风雨损坏。
They added extra insulation to the attic last year. = 他们去年在阁楼上增加了额外的隔热材料。

现在完成时:
We have installed new insulation in the walls. = 我们已经在墙内安装了新的隔热材料。
They have improved the building's insulation significantly. = 他们已经显著改善了建筑物的隔热性能。

过去完成时:
The pipes had frozen because the insulation had failed. = 由于绝缘失效，管道已经冻结。
Before the renovation, the house had poor insulation. = 翻新之前，这栋房子隔热性能很差。

将来时:

They will need more insulation to meet the energy efficiency standards. = 他们需要更多隔热材料才能达到节能标准。

虚拟语气:
If we had better insulation, our energy bills would be lower. = 如果我们有更好的隔热材料，我们的能源账单就会更低。
I wish the insulation in my apartment was better. = 我希望我公寓的隔热性能更好。

被动语态:
