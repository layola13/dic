forgeries:/ˈfɔːrdʒəriːz/| n.|伪造品；伪造行为|Counterfeit items, such as fake documents, money, or artwork, created with the intent to deceive.  The act of creating such forgeries is also referred to as forgery.|例句：The police uncovered a ring of forgeries involving hundreds of thousands of dollars in counterfeit banknotes.  警方破获了一个涉及数十万美元假钞的伪造案。

例句：The art expert quickly identified the painting as a forgery. 艺术专家很快认出这幅画是赝品。


近义词：counterfeits, imitations, fabrications, shams

反义词：originals, genuines, authentic


词性:名词
