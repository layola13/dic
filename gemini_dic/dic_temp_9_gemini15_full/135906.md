nonpsychoanalytically:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkoʊˌænəˈlɪtɪkli/| adv.|非精神分析地|Not in accordance with or employing the methods or principles of psychoanalysis.  This adverb indicates an approach or perspective that avoids the theoretical framework and interpretive techniques of psychoanalysis.|例句：The therapist approached the patient's problems nonpsychoanalytically, focusing on behavioral techniques instead. 治疗师以非精神分析的方法处理病人的问题，转而专注于行为疗法。


The study was conducted nonpsychoanalytically, emphasizing empirical data over subjective interpretations.  这项研究以非精神分析的方式进行，强调实证数据而非主观解释。|近义词：behaviorally, empirically, objectively|反义词：psychoanalytically
