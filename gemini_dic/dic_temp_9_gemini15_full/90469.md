hubbub:/ˈhʌbʌb/| n. |喧嚣，嘈杂声 |A hubbub is a loud noise caused by a lot of people talking or shouting at the same time. It suggests a chaotic and confusing situation. |例句：The announcement was drowned out by a hubbub of excited voices.  公告被一阵兴奋的喧嚣声盖住了。|例句：There was a tremendous hubbub in the marketplace. 集市上喧嚣一片。|近义词：commotion, uproar, clamor, din, tumult|反义词：silence, quiet, calm, peace
