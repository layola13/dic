revetoed:/riˈvoʊtəd/ | v. |否决(过去式和过去分词) |  To reject (a proposal, bill, etc.) formally.  This is the past tense and past participle of the verb "to veto".|

例句：The president vetoed the bill. = 总统否决了该法案。

The senate revetoed the proposed amendment. = 参议院否决了提议的修正案。


近义词：rejected, overruled, quashed, disallowed

反义词：approved, passed, accepted

词性：动词(过去式和过去分词)
