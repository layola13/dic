inventories:/ˈɪnvəntɔːriz/ | n. |存货；清单；详细目录 | A detailed list of items such as property, goods in stock, or the contents of a building.  |例句：The company took inventory of its supplies at the end of the year.  公司在年底清点库存。|例句：The museum has a detailed inventory of all its artifacts.  博物馆对其所有文物都有详细的清单。|近义词：stock, supplies, list, catalog |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company inventories its stock monthly. = 公司每月盘点库存。
We inventory the warehouse every quarter. = 我们每季度清点仓库。

一般过去时:
They inventoried the entire building after the fire. = 火灾后他们清点整个建筑物的物品。
The team inventoried the damaged goods. = 团队清点了受损的货物。

现在完成时:
The store has inventoried all its new products. = 商店已清点所有新产品。
We have inventoried the supplies for the upcoming project. = 我们已经清点了即将开展的项目的物资。

过去完成时:
They had inventoried the warehouse before the audit. = 审计之前他们已经清点过仓库。
The accountant had inventoried all the assets before submitting the report. = 会计在提交报告之前已经清点过所有资产。

将来时:
We will inventory the equipment next week. = 我们下周将清点设备。
They will inventory the shop's stock before closing. = 他们将在关门前清点商店的库存。

虚拟语气:
If we had inventoried the goods earlier, we would have avoided the shortage. = 如果我们早点清点货物，我们就不会缺货了。
It's crucial that we inventory all the materials before the construction starts. = 在施工开始之前，我们必须清点所有材料至关重要。

被动语态:
The warehouse was inventoried last month. = 仓库上个月进行了清点。
The inventory was completed by the end of the day. = 清点工作在当天结束前完成。

疑问句:
Has the inventory been completed? = 清点工作完成了吗？
When will the inventory be conducted? = 什么时候进行清点？

否定句:
They did not inventory the storage room. = 他们没有清点储藏室。
The company has not yet inventoried its new shipment. = 公司尚未清点其新到的货物。
