The children avoided stepping in the cowpats. 孩子们避免踩到牛粪。

词源:
The word "cowpats" is a compound word. "Cow" refers to the animal, and "pats" is a diminutive of "pat," meaning a small lump or mass.  Therefore, "cowpats" literally means "small lumps of cow dung."
