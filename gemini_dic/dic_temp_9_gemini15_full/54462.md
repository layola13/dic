disintegrates:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| v.| 瓦解；崩解；衰变 | To break down or decay into smaller parts; to lose cohesion or unity.  |例句：The old building is slowly disintegrating.  这栋旧建筑正在慢慢地崩塌。
The empire disintegrated into smaller kingdoms.  帝国瓦解成了许多较小的王国。


近义词：decay, decompose, crumble, fall apart, break down
反义词：integrate, coalesce, unite, consolidate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old bridge disintegrates slowly over time. = 这座旧桥随着时间的推移慢慢地崩塌。
The empire disintegrates into smaller kingdoms. = 帝国瓦解成许多较小的王国。


一般过去时:
The ancient monument disintegrated after the earthquake. = 这座古代纪念碑在地震后崩塌了。
The team disintegrated after their star player left. = 球队在他们的明星球员离开后瓦解了。


现在完成时:
The ancient civilization has disintegrated over centuries. = 古代文明已在几个世纪中瓦解。
The evidence has disintegrated due to the heat. = 证据由于高温而分解了。


过去完成时:
The building had disintegrated before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，建筑物已经崩塌了。
The relationship had disintegrated long before the argument. = 在争吵很久之前，这段关系就已经瓦解了。


将来时:
The rock face will disintegrate further with the next rainfall. = 岩石表面在下一次降雨后将进一步崩解。
The treaty will disintegrate if they don't cooperate. = 如果他们不合作，条约将会瓦解。


虚拟语气:
If the material disintegrated, the experiment would fail. = 如果材料崩解，实验就会失败。
I wish the situation hadn't disintegrated so quickly. = 我真希望情况没有这么快恶化。


被动语态:
The statue was disintegrated by the acid rain. = 雕像被酸雨腐蚀了。
The evidence was disintegrated by the fire. = 证据被大火烧毁了。


疑问句:
Will the material disintegrate under high pressure? = 材料在高压下会崩解吗？
Is the team disintegrating due to internal conflict? = 球队是因为内部冲突而瓦解的吗？


否定句:
The plan did not disintegrate despite the setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，计划并没有瓦解。
The building did not disintegrate even after the storm. = 即使在暴风雨过后，建筑物也没有倒塌。

nuclear disintegration = 核裂变


词源:
dis- (否定或加强意义的前缀) + integr- (完整的，整体的) + -ate (动词后缀)  The word ultimately derives from the Latin *integer*, meaning "whole" or "untouched".  The prefix "dis-" indicates a reversal or separation of the whole.
