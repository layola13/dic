rounding:/ˈraʊndɪŋ/| n. v.|四舍五入；圆形；圆化；|The process of approximating a number to a specified degree of accuracy by removing the least significant digits and adjusting the remaining digits accordingly.  It involves increasing or decreasing the last retained digit depending on the value of the discarded digit(s).|例句：The rounding of the final score to the nearest whole number resulted in a tie.|近义词：approximation, estimation|反义词：precision, exactness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We round the numbers to the nearest ten. = 我们将数字四舍五入到最接近的十位数。
She rounds up her expenses every month. = 她每个月都会把她的开支四舍五入。


一般过去时:
They rounded the figures down to simplify the report. = 他们将数字向下取整以简化报告。
He rounded the corners of the painting. = 他将画的角弄圆了。


现在完成时:
We have rounded the results to two decimal places. = 我们已将结果四舍五入到小数点后两位。
She has rounded off the sharp edges of the stone. = 她已经磨平了石头锋利的边缘。


过去完成时:
They had rounded the sum to the nearest dollar before presenting it. = 他们汇报之前已将总额四舍五入到最接近的美元。
He had rounded the corners of the table to make it safer for children. = 他已经将桌子的角弄圆以使它对孩子更安全。


将来时:
We will round the figures up to the next hundred. = 我们将把数字向上取整到下一个百位数。
She will round off the edges of the wood before painting it. = 她会在油漆之前将木头的边缘磨圆。


虚拟语气:
If we rounded the figures up, the profit would look better. = 如果我们把数字向上取整，利润看起来会更好。
I wish I had rounded the corners more carefully. = 我希望我当时能更仔细地把角磨圆。


被动语态:
The numbers were rounded to the nearest integer. = 这些数字被四舍五入到最接近的整数。
The edges of the sculpture are being rounded by the artist. = 雕塑的边缘正在被艺术家磨圆。


疑问句:
Should we round the percentage up or down? = 我们应该向上还是向下取整百分比？
Are the corners of the design rounded or sharp? = 设计的角是圆的还是尖的？


否定句:
We did not round the figures; we kept them exact. = 我们没有四舍五入这些数字；我们保留了它们的精确值。
She didn't round the edges of the picture frame. = 她没有把相框的边缘弄圆。


PHRASE->
round up: 四舍五入 (增加到最接近的整数)
round down: 五舍六入 (减少到最接近的整数)
round off: 使圆滑；使完美；完成
round number: 整数
to the nearest: 四舍五入到最接近的
