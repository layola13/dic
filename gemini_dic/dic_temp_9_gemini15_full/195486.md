semiorthodoxly:/ˌsɛmiˈɔːrθədɒksli/| adv.| 半正统地，部分正统地 |  In a manner that is partly orthodox and partly unorthodox; showing a blend of traditional and unconventional beliefs or practices.  |例句：He approached the problem semiorthodoxly, using both established methods and some novel techniques. 他以半正统的方式处理这个问题，既使用了既定的方法，也使用了一些新技术。


例句：The artist's work was described as semiorthodoxly modern, blending classical forms with contemporary sensibilities.  这位艺术家的作品被描述为半正统的现代风格，将古典形式与当代审美融合在一起。


近义词：partially traditionally, somewhat conventionally


反义词：unorthodoxly, radically, unconventionally


词性：副词
