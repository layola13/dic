malaria:/məˈleəriə/| n.|疟疾|A disease caused by a parasite that is transmitted to humans through the bites of infected mosquitoes.|例句：Many people in tropical regions suffer from malaria. 热带地区很多人患有疟疾。  例句：The doctor confirmed that he had malaria. 医生确诊他患有疟疾。|近义词：malarial fever|反义词：无
