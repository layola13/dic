graveyards:/ˈɡreɪvjɑːrdz/ | n. | 墓地，坟场 | A graveyard is a consecrated ground used for burying the dead.  It often contains tombstones or markers to identify the deceased. | Example: The old graveyard was overgrown with weeds.  这个古老的墓地长满了杂草。 |近义词：cemetery, burial ground | 反义词：None
