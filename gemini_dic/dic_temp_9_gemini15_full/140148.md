offcuts:/ˈɒfˌkʌts/ | n. |边角料，剩余物|Pieces of material, such as wood, cloth, or metal, that are left over after the main part has been used.  These are often considered waste but can be reused or recycled. |例句：The carpenter carefully stored the offcuts of wood for future projects.  木匠小心地将木头边角料储存起来以备将来使用。


The factory recycles its metal offcuts. 工厂回收其金属边角料。|近义词：scraps, remnants, leftovers, waste|反义词：main pieces, whole pieces
