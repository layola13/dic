characterising: /ˈkærəktəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 表征；具有…的特征；描绘…的特性 |  Describing the distinctive features or qualities of someone or something.  The act of characterizing is the process of identifying and highlighting the key aspects that define something. |

例句：
The novel is characterised by its vivid descriptions of nature. = 这部小说以其生动的自然描写为特色。

The artist's work is characterised by bold colours and abstract forms. =  这位艺术家的作品以大胆的色彩和抽象的形式为特色。


近义词：describing, depicting, portraying, defining, identifying

反义词：misrepresenting, obscuring, ignoring


词性：动词

一般现在时:
The artist characterises her subjects with great sensitivity. = 艺术家以极大的敏感性来刻画她的主题。
This period is characterised by rapid technological advancement. =  这个时期以快速的科技进步为特征。

一般过去时:
He characterised the situation as extremely dangerous. = 他将这种情况描述为极其危险。
The historian characterised the reign of the emperor as a time of prosperity. = 历史学家将皇帝的统治时期描述为繁荣时期。

现在完成时:
She has characterised the role of women in society with great accuracy. = 她已经非常准确地刻画了妇女在社会中的角色。
Researchers have characterised the new virus as highly contagious. = 研究人员已经将这种新型病毒描述为具有高度传染性。

过去完成时:
The critic had characterised the play as a masterpiece before the premiere. = 批评家在首映式之前就把这部戏描述为杰作。
Scientists had characterised the bacteria before developing the vaccine. = 科学家在研制疫苗之前已经对细菌进行了表征。

将来时:
She will characterise the main character as a complex and flawed individual. = 她将把主人公描绘成一个复杂而有缺陷的个体。
The report will characterise the economic situation as unstable. = 这份报告将把经济形势描述为不稳定。

虚拟语气:
If he characterised the situation differently, the outcome might have been better. = 如果他对情况的描述有所不同，结果可能会更好。
I wish she had characterised the events more objectively. = 我希望她能更客观地描述这些事件。

被动语态:
The painting was characterised by its use of light and shadow. = 这幅画以其对光影的运用为特色。
The experiment was characterised by careful methodology. =  这个实验以其严谨的方法论为特色。

疑问句:
How would you characterise the current political climate? = 你如何形容当前的政治气候？
How is the disease characterised in its early stages? = 在疾病的早期阶段，它是如何表现出来的？

否定句:
The report did not characterise the situation accurately. = 这份报告并没有准确地描述情况。
He failed to characterise the emotional complexity of the character. = 他未能刻画出人物的情感复杂性。
