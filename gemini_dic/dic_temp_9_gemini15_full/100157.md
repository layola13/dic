/ˈɪntərˌeɪtər/| n. |评分者间 |Inter-rater reliability refers to the degree of agreement among raters. It is a measure of the consistency of ratings given by different raters.  High inter-rater reliability indicates that the raters are consistent in their judgments, while low inter-rater reliability suggests that there is significant disagreement among the raters.  |Example: The study demonstrated high inter-rater reliability, with the two raters agreeing on 90% of the scores.  这项研究显示评分者间具有高度信度，两位评分者在90%的得分上达成一致。|近义词：rater agreement, scorer agreement|反义词：inter-rater unreliability
