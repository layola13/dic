illicit: /ɪˈlɪsɪt/ | adj. | 非法的，不正当的 |  Forbidden by law, rules, or custom; illegal.  |例句：The illicit drug trade is a major problem. 非法毒品交易是一个主要问题。

例句：He was involved in illicit activities. 他参与了非法活动。


近义词：illegal, unlawful, prohibited, forbidden, contraband, illegitimate

反义词：legal, lawful, permitted, allowed, legitimate


词性:形容词
