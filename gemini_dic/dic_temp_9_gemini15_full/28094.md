capita:/ˈkæpɪtə/| n.|人均，每人|per head; for each person;  The term "per capita" indicates a calculation done for each individual in a group. It's often used in statistics related to income, consumption, or other metrics distributed across a population.| 例句：The per capita income in this city is relatively high. = 这个城市的平均收入相对较高。| 例句：The per capita consumption of coffee has increased in recent years. = 近年来人均咖啡消费量有所增加。|近义词：per head, per person|反义词：aggregate, total
