paroxysmal: /pærˈɒksɪzməl/ | adj. |阵发性的，发作性的 | occurring in sudden, sharp, and brief attacks or fits; intermittent.  | 例句：He suffered from paroxysmal nocturnal dyspnea. 他患有阵发性夜间呼吸困难。 | 例句：The child experienced paroxysmal coughing fits.  孩子经历了阵发性咳嗽发作。| 近义词：intermittent, spasmodic, convulsive | 反义词：constant, continuous, steady

词源:
From Greek *paroxysmos ("exacerbation"), from *para- ("beside") + *oxys ("sharp").  The word reflects the sudden and sharp nature of the attacks or fits it describes.
