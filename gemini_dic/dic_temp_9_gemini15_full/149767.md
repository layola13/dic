palatine:/ˈpælətaɪn/| adj. n.|上颚的；腭的；上颚的；皇宫的；王宫的|Relating to the palate (the roof of the mouth).  Also, historically relating to the Palatine Hill in Rome, the site of several imperial palaces, hence suggesting imperial or regal status.|The palatine tonsils are located at the back of the throat. 上颚扁桃体位于喉咙后部。The Palatine Hill was the center of ancient Roman power.  帕拉丁山是古罗马权力的中心。|近义词：palatal,  (for regal sense) imperial, royal|反义词：sublingual (for anatomical sense)
