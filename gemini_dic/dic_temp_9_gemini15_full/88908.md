hold-fort:/hoʊld fɔːrt/| n. phr.|坚守阵地；据守要塞|To defend a position, often against overwhelming odds; to maintain one's position or stance resolutely.  |例句：The soldiers held the fort against the enemy's relentless attacks. 士兵们顽强抵抗敌人的猛烈攻击，坚守阵地。  |例句：Despite the criticism, she held the fort and defended her ideas. 尽管受到批评，她仍然坚持自己的立场，捍卫自己的想法。|近义词：defend, withstand, maintain, persevere |反义词：surrender, retreat, concede, abandon |词组
