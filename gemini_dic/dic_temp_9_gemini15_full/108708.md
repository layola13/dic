leaned:/liːnd/| v.|倚靠，倾斜，倾向|To incline or bend from a vertical position; to rely on or favor something. |例句：He leaned against the wall. 他靠在墙上。|例句：She leaned towards the progressive political views. 她倾向于进步的政治观点。|近义词：inclined, slanted, depended on |反义词：stood straight, upright


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leans against the tree. = 他靠在树上。
She leans towards modern art. = 她倾向于现代艺术。

一般过去时:
He leaned against the wall. = 他靠在墙上。
She leaned over to whisper in his ear. = 她俯身在他耳边低语。

现在完成时:
He has leaned on his friends for support. = 他一直依靠朋友的支持。
She has leaned towards a more conservative approach. = 她已经倾向于采取更为保守的方法。

过去完成时:
He had leaned on his cane for years. = 他多年来一直依靠拐杖。
She had leaned heavily on her family during the difficult times. = 在困难时期，她非常依赖她的家人。


将来时:
He will lean on his experience to succeed. = 他将依靠他的经验来取得成功。
She will lean towards a different strategy this time. = 这次她将倾向于采用不同的策略。

虚拟语气:
If he leaned any further, he would fall. = 如果他再倾斜一点，他就会摔倒。
I wish she leaned more towards compromise. = 我希望她更倾向于妥协。

被动语态:
The tower leaned dangerously. = 塔楼危险地倾斜了。(塔楼被危险地倾斜了)
The decision was leaned heavily on his advice. = 这个决定很大程度上依赖于他的建议。(这个决定被很大程度上依赖于他的建议)

疑问句:
Does he lean to the left or the right? = 他倾向于左派还是右派？
Why did she lean on you for help? = 她为什么依靠你帮忙？

否定句:
He didn't lean on anyone for help. = 他没有依靠任何人帮忙。
She didn't lean towards any particular political party. = 她不倾向于任何特定的政党。


PHRASE->
lean on: 依靠
lean towards: 倾向于
lean back: 向后倾斜
lean forward: 往前倾斜
lean in: 凑近
