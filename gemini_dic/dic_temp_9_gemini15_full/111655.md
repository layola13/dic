locators:/ˈloʊkeɪtərz/ | n. |定位器，查找器，指示器 | Devices or methods used to find the location of something.  Locators can range from simple tools like maps and compasses to sophisticated technologies like GPS systems and radar. They are essential in various fields, from navigation and surveying to search and rescue operations. | Example: The GPS locators helped rescuers quickly find the lost hikers.  救援人员用GPS定位器很快找到了迷路的徒步旅行者。|近义词：finders, trackers, identifiers |反义词：obscurers, concealers
