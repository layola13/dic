complexifying: /kəmˈplɛksɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 使复杂化；使变得更复杂 |  Making something more complex or intricate.  | The rapid advancement of technology is complexifying our lives. 技术的快速发展正在使我们的生活复杂化。 |  complicating, intricating | simplifying, simplifying | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is complexifying the tax system. = 政府正在使税收制度复杂化。
This new software is complexifying the workflow. = 这个新软件使工作流程复杂化。


一般过去时:
The new regulations complexified the process significantly. = 新规章大大复杂化了这个流程。
He complexified the problem by adding unnecessary details. = 他通过添加不必要的细节使问题复杂化。


现在完成时:
They have complexified the situation beyond repair. = 他们已经把情况弄得无法挽回地复杂化了。
Recent events have complexified the international relations. = 最近的事件使国际关系复杂化。


过去完成时:
She had complexified the instructions to the point of incomprehensibility. = 她已经把说明弄得难以理解地复杂化了。
The company had complexified its organizational structure unnecessarily. = 公司已经不必要地使它的组织结构复杂化。


将来时:
The proposed changes will complexify the already cumbersome system. =  拟议的变更将使已经繁琐的系统更加复杂。
The new rules will complexify the application process. = 新规将使申请流程复杂化。


虚拟语气:
If we implemented this plan, it would complexify the operations considerably. = 如果我们实施这个计划，它将使运营相当复杂化。
I wish they hadn't complexified the design so much. = 我希望他们没有把设计弄得这么复杂。


被动语态:
The project was complexified by unforeseen circumstances. =  该项目因不可预见的情况而复杂化。
The issue has been complexified by political interference. =  这个问题因政治干预而复杂化。


疑问句:

否定句:

We tried not to complexify the problem unnecessarily. = 我们尽量避免不必要地使问题复杂化。
