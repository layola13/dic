noncharacteristic: /ˌnɒnkærəktəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非典型的；不具特征的；非特征性的 | Not typical or characteristic of something; unusual or unexpected.  | The sudden outburst was noncharacteristic of her usual calm demeanor.  她突然爆发的脾气和她通常平静的举止很不相符。 | typical, characteristic, usual | atypical, characteristic, usual |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This behavior is noncharacteristic of him. = 这种行为对他来说是不典型的。
Her calmness is noncharacteristic in this stressful situation. = 在这种压力很大的情况下，她的冷静是不寻常的。


一般过去时:
His anger was noncharacteristic of his previous behavior. = 他当时的愤怒与他之前的行为不符。
The mistake was noncharacteristic for such a careful worker. = 对这样一个细心的工人来说，这个错误是不寻常的。


现在完成时:
This has been noncharacteristic of the team's performance this season. = 这个赛季球队的表现一直不符合他们的典型水平。
That reaction has been noncharacteristic of her throughout the whole process. = 在整个过程中，她的反应一直不符合她的常态。


过去完成时:
His outburst had been noncharacteristic of his usual quiet nature. = 他之前的爆发与他平时安静的性格不相符。
Her hesitation had been noncharacteristic of her decisive personality. = 她的犹豫与她果断的个性不相符。


将来时:
This behavior will be noncharacteristic of him in the future. = 他未来的行为将与他以往不同。
Her response will likely be noncharacteristic of her usual frankness. = 她的回应可能与她通常的坦率不同。


虚拟语气:
If his behavior were noncharacteristic, we would investigate. = 如果他的行为不典型，我们会调查。
If her reaction had been noncharacteristic, we would have been surprised. = 如果她的反应不典型，我们会感到惊讶。


被动语态:
His actions were considered noncharacteristic by his colleagues. = 他的行为被同事们认为是不典型的。
Her silence was found to be noncharacteristic of her personality. = 人们发现她的沉默与她的个性不相符。


疑问句:
Is his behavior noncharacteristic? = 他的行为不典型吗？
Is this reaction noncharacteristic of her? = 这种反应对她来说不典型吗？


否定句:
His actions were not noncharacteristic; they were quite typical. = 他的行为并非不典型；而是相当典型的。
Her response was not noncharacteristic; she often reacts that way. = 她的反应并非不典型；她经常那样反应。
