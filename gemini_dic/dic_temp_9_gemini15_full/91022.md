husher:/ˈhʌʃər/| n.| 安静的人；使安静的人 | A person who makes others be quiet.  Someone employed to keep order and quiet, often in a public place. |例句：The usher was a husher, quickly silencing the noisy audience.  那个引导员是个让人安静的人，他迅速地让吵闹的观众安静下来。


The security guard acted as a husher, keeping the protesters from shouting.  保安扮演着维持秩序的角色，阻止抗议者喊叫。

近义词：silencer, pacifier, quieter
反义词：agitator, troublemaker

词性:名词
