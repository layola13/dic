flying:/ˈflaɪɪŋ/ | adj. |飞行的；飞翔的；流动的；迅速的 |  Describes something that is in the air and moving through the air, or something that moves very quickly.  |例句：The flying colours were beautiful.  飘扬的旗帜很漂亮。

例句：The flying saucer was sighted over the desert.  不明飞行物在沙漠上空被发现。


近义词：soaring, soaring, airborne, swift, rapid |反义词：stationary, grounded, slow


词源: Old English *fleogan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vliegen and German fliegen.  The -ing suffix forms the present participle, which is often used as an adjective.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird is flying high above the trees. = 鸟儿在树梢上空飞翔。
The plane is flying to London. = 飞机正在飞往伦敦。


一般过去时:
The kite flew high in the sky. = 风筝在空中飞得很高。
The birds flew south for the winter. = 鸟儿飞到南方过冬。


现在完成时:

He has flown many times before. = 他以前飞过很多次了。


过去完成时:
The bird had flown away before I could see it. = 我还没看到鸟就飞走了。
They had flown to Paris before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前飞到了巴黎。


将来时:
The rocket will fly to the moon next year. = 火箭明年将飞往月球。

疑问句:

否定句:
