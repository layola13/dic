fathering: /ˈfɑːðəriŋ/ | v. | 抚养；当父亲；产生；促成 | The act of being a father; raising or nurturing children; contributing to the creation or development of something. |例句：He is proud of his role in fathering three successful children. 他为抚养三个成功的孩子而自豪。  |例句：The company's innovative approach to marketing is fathering a new era of sales growth. 公司创新的营销方法正在促成一个新的销售增长时代。 |近义词：parenting, nurturing, fostering, begetting, engendering |反义词：neglecting, abandoning
