overseasons:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːzən/| adj.|海外的，国外的|Beyond the seas; in or to a foreign country.|例句：Many overseas students choose to study in the UK. 许多海外学生选择在英国学习。|近义词：foreign, international, abroad|反义词：domestic, local
