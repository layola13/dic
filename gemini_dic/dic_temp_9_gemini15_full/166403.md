preresolving:/ˌpriːˈrɛzɒlvɪŋ/| v. |预先解决；预先确定|The act of resolving something beforehand; determining something in advance.  It often refers to resolving issues or conflicts before they escalate or become problems.  This word is primarily used in technical contexts, particularly in computer science and networking.|


例句：

The system's preresolving of IP addresses significantly improved its performance.  = 系统预先解析IP地址显著提高了其性能。

Preresolving potential conflicts is crucial for successful project management. = 预先解决潜在冲突对于成功的项目管理至关重要。


近义词：predetermining, preempting, anticipating, resolving beforehand


反义词：postponing, neglecting, ignoring


词性：动词
