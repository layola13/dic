re-phase:/ˌriːˈfeɪz/| vt.|重新表达；改述；重新安排阶段|To express something again in different words; to restate.  It can also refer to the rearranging of phases or stages in a process.|例句：Could you re-phase that sentence?  It's a bit confusing.  = 你能重新表达一下那个句子吗？有点令人困惑。


例句：The project timeline needs to be re-phased to accommodate the delays. = 项目时间表需要重新安排阶段以适应延误。


近义词：reword, restate, paraphrase, reformulate


反义词：leave unchanged, maintain the same


词性：动词
