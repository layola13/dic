non-irritant: /ˌnɒnˈɪrɪtənt/ | adj. | 不刺激的，无刺激性的 | Not causing irritation or inflammation.  A substance that does not irritate the skin or other tissues. | 例句：This lotion is a non-irritant formula, suitable for sensitive skin.  这款乳液配方温和不刺激，适合敏感肌肤使用。


This cream is a non-irritant and is safe for use on babies.  这款面霜不刺激，对婴儿使用安全。

近义词：mild, soothing, gentle, harmless | 反义词：irritant, caustic, harsh, abrasive


词性:形容词

词源:  The word is formed from "non-" (meaning "not") + "irritant" (meaning "causing irritation").  Irritant itself comes from the verb "irritate," ultimately deriving from the Latin word "irritare," meaning "to provoke, excite, or irritate."
