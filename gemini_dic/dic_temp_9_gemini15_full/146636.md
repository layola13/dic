overharasses: /ˌoʊvərˈhærəs/ | vt. |过度骚扰；过度烦扰 | To harass excessively or repeatedly.  This word combines "over-" (excessively) with "harasses" (the present tense form of the verb "to harass"). It implies a level of harassment beyond what is considered acceptable or tolerable.  |例句：The company was accused of overharassing its employees with excessive emails and unreasonable demands. 公司被指控过度骚扰员工，发送过多的电子邮件并提出不合理的要求。

The constant demands from her boss led to her feeling overharassed.  她老板不断的要求让她感到过度被骚扰。|近义词：persecutes, torments, pester, badger,  |反义词：soothe, comfort, appease,  |词性:动词
