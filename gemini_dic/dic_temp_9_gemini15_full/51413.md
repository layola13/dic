deterred:/dɪˈtɜːrd/ | v. |阻止；威慑；使却步 | To prevent (someone) from doing something or prevent (something) from happening.  |例句：The threat of punishment deterred him from committing the crime. 惩罚的威胁阻止了他犯罪。

例句： Heavy fines deterred many drivers from speeding.  高额罚款阻止了许多司机超速。


近义词：prevent, discourage, inhibit, dissuade | 反义词：encourage, incite, stimulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The threat of war deters aggression. = 战争的威胁阻止了侵略。
High prices deter many customers. = 高价阻止了许多顾客。


一般过去时:
The rain deterred us from going for a walk. = 雨阻止了我们去散步。
The potential risks deterred investors. = 潜在的风险吓退了投资者。


现在完成时:
Nothing has deterred her from achieving her goals. = 没有什么能阻止她实现目标。
The harsh criticism has deterred many from entering the field. = 严厉的批评阻止了许多人进入该领域。


过去完成时:
Previous failures had deterred him from trying again. = 之前的失败阻止了他再次尝试。
The negative reviews had deterred many potential buyers. = 负面评论吓退了许多潜在买家。


将来时:
Higher taxes will deter investment. = 更高的税收将阻止投资。
Stricter regulations will deter companies from polluting. = 更严格的法规将阻止公司污染环境。


虚拟语气:
If the punishment were harsher, it might deter more criminals. = 如果惩罚更严厉，可能会阻止更多罪犯。
I wish the threat of punishment would deter them from violence. = 我希望惩罚的威胁能阻止他们暴力行为。


被动语态:
Many potential investors were deterred by the high risks. = 许多潜在投资者被高风险吓退了。
He was deterred from applying by the complicated application process. = 复杂的申请流程阻止了他申请。


疑问句:
What will deter them from continuing this harmful practice? = 什么才能阻止他们继续这种有害的做法？
What factors deterred you from accepting the job offer? = 哪些因素阻止了你接受这份工作机会？


否定句:
Nothing could deter her from pursuing her dreams. = 没有什么能阻止她追求梦想。
The lack of funding did not deter the researchers from their work. = 资金不足并没有阻止研究人员的工作。


PHRASE->
deter from = 阻止...做...
deterrence to = 对...的威慑
a strong deterrent = 强有力的威慑
deterrent effect = 威慑作用
means of deterrence = 威慑手段

词源:
From Old French deterrer, from Latin deterrēre "to frighten away, scare off," from de- "away" + terrēre "to frighten."
