eyewink:/ˈaɪwɪŋk/| n. v.|眨眼；暗示；眨眼示意|A quick, brief closure of the eyelid.  It can be a deliberate gesture, often used to communicate nonverbally, or involuntary, as in a reflex.|例句：She gave me a playful eyewink. 她朝我俏皮地眨了眨眼。

例句：The eyewink signaled his agreement.  这眨眼示意了他的同意。

近义词：blink, wink, glance|反义词：stare, gape

词性：名词，动词
