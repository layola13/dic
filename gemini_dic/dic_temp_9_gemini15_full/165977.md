preparticipation: /ˌpriːpɑːrˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 参与前的；赛前 |  The act or process of preparing for participation, especially in a sporting event or activity. It often involves medical screenings and assessments to ensure fitness for participation. | 例句：Preparticipation physical examinations are mandatory for all athletes in this league. =  在这个联盟中，所有运动员都必须进行赛前体检。 | 例句：The preparticipation screening process identified a potential heart condition in one of the candidates. = 参与前的筛选过程发现了一名候选人潜在的心脏疾病。 | 近义词：preparation, screening, assessment | 反义词：postparticipation
