sedater:/səˈdeɪtər/| n. |镇静剂；使镇静的人或事物 | A sedater is a substance or thing that calms or soothes. It can refer to a medication used to reduce anxiety or agitation, or to a person or thing that has a calming effect.  |例句：The doctor prescribed a sedater to help her sleep. 医生开了镇静剂帮助她睡眠。 |近义词：tranquilizer, calmative, soother |反义词：stimulant, excitant
