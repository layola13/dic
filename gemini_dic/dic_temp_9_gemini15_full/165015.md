prehesitates:/ˌpriːˈhezɪteɪts/| v. |犹豫不决地开始；迟疑地做|To begin to do something hesitantly or with uncertainty.  It combines the prefix "pre-" (before) with "hesitates," indicating an action of hesitation that precedes another action.|例句：He prehesitated before answering the difficult question. 他在回答那个难题之前犹豫了一下。

例句：She prehesitated, then cautiously opened the door. 她犹豫了一下，然后谨慎地打开了门。


近义词：falters, wavers, hesitates, delays


反义词：rushes, plunges, acts decisively


词性:动词
