in-luck: /ɪnˈlʌk/ | adj. | 幸运的，走运的 | Having good fortune; experiencing unexpected good luck.  |例句：I was in luck; I found a $20 bill on the sidewalk.  我真幸运；我在人行道上捡到一张20美元的钞票。


例句：She was in luck because she got the job she wanted. 她很幸运，因为她得到了她想要的工作。


近义词：fortunate, lucky, blessed | 反义词：unlucky, unfortunate, jinxed
