pensions:/ˈpɛnʃənz/ | n. |养老金；退休金 |Regular payments made to someone who has retired from work.  |例句：She receives a generous pension from her former employer. 她从之前的雇主那里领取丰厚的养老金。|例句：Many people rely on their pensions for their income in retirement. 许多人在退休后依靠养老金来维持生计。|近义词：retirement benefits, annuity |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives a pension every month. = 他每月领取养老金。
The company provides pensions to its employees. = 公司为其员工提供养老金。


一般过去时:
She received a lump-sum pension payment. = 她收到一笔养老金一次性付款。
He collected his pension from the post office. = 他从邮局领取了他的养老金。


现在完成时:
I have received my pension for ten years. = 我已经领取养老金十年了。
They have been paying into their pensions since they started working. = 从他们开始工作以来，他们一直在缴纳养老金。


过去完成时:
He had already drawn his pension before he passed away. = 他去世前已经领过了养老金。
She had received several pension increases during her retirement. = 在她退休期间，她已经获得了几次养老金增长。


将来时:
He will start receiving his pension next year. = 他明年将开始领取养老金。
She will likely outlive her pension savings. = 她很可能会比她的养老金储蓄活得更久。


虚拟语气:
If I had a larger pension, I would travel more. = 如果我的养老金更多，我会多旅行。
I wish I had a more generous pension plan. = 我希望我有一个更慷慨的养老金计划。


被动语态:
Pensions are paid monthly by the government. = 养老金由政府每月支付。

疑问句:

否定句:
