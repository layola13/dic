laryngitis:/ˌlærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs/| n. |喉炎|Inflammation of the larynx (voice box), resulting in hoarseness or loss of voice.  It is often caused by viral infections, but can also be caused by overuse, irritation, or allergies.|例句：She developed laryngitis after shouting at a football match. 她在一场足球比赛中大喊大叫后得了喉炎。

例句：The singer suffered from acute laryngitis and had to cancel his concert.  这位歌手患上了急性喉炎，不得不取消了他的演唱会。


近义词：pharyngitis (if affecting both larynx and pharynx)

反义词：None (a condition, not something with a direct opposite)

词性：Noun
