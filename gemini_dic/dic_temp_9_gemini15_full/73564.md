forested:/ˈfɔːrɪstɪd/ | adj. | 森林覆盖的，长满树木的 | Covered with forests; abounding in forests.  |例句：The region is heavily forested. 这个地区树木茂密。  |例句：The forested hills were a beautiful sight.  树木繁茂的山丘景色优美。|近义词：wooded, wooded, sylvan |反义词：treeless, barren


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The area is forested. = 这个地区是森林覆盖的。
The hills are heavily forested. = 这些山丘树木茂密。

一般过去时:
The land was forested before the logging. = 在砍伐森林之前，这片土地是森林覆盖的。
The valley was densely forested a century ago. = 一个世纪前，这个山谷树木茂密。

现在完成时:
The region has become forested since the reforestation project. = 自从重新造林项目以来，该地区已经变得树木茂密了。
Much of the land has been forested naturally over time. = 随着时间的推移，大部分土地已自然成林。

过去完成时:
The area had been forested for centuries before it was cleared. = 这片地区在被清理之前已经覆盖着森林几个世纪了。
The slopes had been densely forested until the recent wildfire. = 直到最近的野火发生之前，山坡上都覆盖着茂密的森林。

将来时:
They will reforest the area, making it forested again. = 他们将重新造林，使该地区再次覆盖森林。
The project aims to make the barren land forested in the next decade. = 该项目旨在在未来十年内使这片贫瘠的土地变成森林覆盖的土地。

虚拟语气:
If the area were forested, it would provide more habitat for wildlife. = 如果该地区覆盖着森林，它将为野生动物提供更多栖息地。
I wish this land were forested again, like it was in the past. = 我希望这片土地能像过去一样再次被森林覆盖。

被动语态:
The land was forested by nature. = 这片土地是自然形成的森林。
The area is being forested through a government initiative. = 通过政府的倡议，该地区正在进行造林。

疑问句:
Is the mountain forested? = 这座山是森林覆盖的吗？
Are the surrounding areas forested? = 周围地区是森林覆盖的吗？

否定句:
The plains are not forested. = 平原上没有森林。
That part of the country is not heavily forested. = 那部分国家树木并不茂密。
