gormand:/ˈɡɔːrmənd/| n. |贪吃者，食客|A person who eats excessively or voraciously.  A gourmand is often connoisseur of food, appreciating fine dining and culinary experiences.|例句：He's a gourmand who enjoys trying new restaurants and cuisines. 他是个美食家，喜欢尝试新的餐馆和菜系。|例句：The gourmand savored each bite of the exquisite meal.  这位美食家细细品味着这顿精致的饭菜。|近义词：glutton, epicure, food lover|反义词：ascetic, abstemious
