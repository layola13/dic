nonteacher: /ˈnɑːnˌtiːtʃər/ | n. | 非教师；非教育工作者 | A person who is not a teacher.  This term is often used to contrast with teachers, highlighting individuals who do not hold a teaching position or are not involved in formal education. | Example:  The nonteachers were invited to the school's fundraising event.  非教师被邀请参加学校的筹款活动。|近义词：layperson, non-educator, layperson (in education) |反义词：teacher, educator, instructor
