planishing:/ˈplænɪʃɪŋ/ | n. v. |平整；压光；锤光 |The process of smoothing or flattening a metal surface by hammering or rolling.  It's used to improve the surface finish and remove imperfections. |例句：The sheet metal was planished to a mirror finish.  钣金被压光到镜面效果。 The artisan carefully planished the silver, removing all the scratches. 工匠仔细地锤光银器，去除了所有的划痕。 |近义词：smoothing, flattening, burnishing |反义词：roughening, pitting
