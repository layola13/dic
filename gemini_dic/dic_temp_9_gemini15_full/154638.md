persists:/pərˈsɪsts/| v.|持续，坚持，执意|To continue to exist or occur; to continue doing something despite difficulties or opposition.|例句：The pain persists despite the medication.  疼痛尽管服药仍持续存在。|例句：He persists in his denial of any wrongdoing. 他坚持否认自己有任何不当行为。|近义词：continues, endures, remains, perseveres|反义词：ceases, stops, ends


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem persists. = 问题持续存在。
He persists in his efforts. = 他坚持他的努力。


一般过去时:
The pain persisted throughout the night. = 疼痛持续了一整夜。
She persisted in her studies despite the difficulties. = 尽管有困难，她仍然坚持学习。


现在完成时:
The drought has persisted for months. = 干旱已经持续了好几个月了。
He has persisted in his belief despite the evidence. = 尽管有证据，他仍然坚持自己的信念。


过去完成时:
The symptoms had persisted for weeks before he sought medical attention. = 在他寻求医疗救助之前，症状已经持续了好几个星期了。
They had persisted in their attempts to reach the summit, despite the bad weather. = 尽管天气恶劣，他们仍然坚持尝试到达山顶。


将来时:
The cold weather will persist into next week. = 冷天气将持续到下周。
She will persist in her efforts to find a solution. = 她将坚持努力寻找解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If he persisted, he might succeed. = 如果他坚持下去，他可能会成功。
I wish he would persist in his studies. = 我希望他能坚持学习。


被动语态:
The issue was persistently ignored. = 这个问题一直被忽视。
The belief was persistently challenged. = 这一信念一直受到质疑。(被动语态中"persist"本身较少用被动式)


疑问句:
Does the problem persist? = 问题持续存在吗？
Will his efforts persist? = 他的努力会持续吗？


否定句:
The symptoms did not persist. = 症状没有持续。
He did not persist in his attempts. = 他没有坚持他的尝试。


PHRASE->
persist in doing sth = 坚持做某事
persist with sth = 坚持某事
persistently ignore = 持续忽略
persistently challenge = 持续挑战
to persist with one's goals = 坚持自己的目标
