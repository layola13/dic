embryogenic:/ˌɛmbrɪəʊˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 胚胎发生的；生胚的 | Relating to or capable of producing embryos.  The term describes the ability of a cell, tissue, or organ to develop into an embryo.  This capacity is crucial in plant tissue culture and reproductive biology. | Example:  The embryogenic callus produced numerous plantlets.  胚胎发生的愈伤组织产生了大量小植株。


The embryogenic potential of the tissue was high. 该组织的胚胎发生潜力很高。


近义词:embryonal, developmental | 反义词:sterile, infertile
