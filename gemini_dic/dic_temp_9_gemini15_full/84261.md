half-stocking:/ˈhæfˌstɒkɪŋ/| n.|半筒袜|A stocking that only covers the foot and lower leg, not extending to the thigh.  It's a shorter version of a full stocking. |例句：She wore half-stockings with her Mary Janes. 她穿着半筒袜和玛丽珍鞋。|例句：The dancer's half-stockings showed off her elegant ankles. 舞者的半筒袜展现了她优雅的脚踝。|近义词：ankle sock, sockette|反义词：full stocking, thigh-high stocking
