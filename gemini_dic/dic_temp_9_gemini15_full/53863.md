discontinuations:/ˌdɪsˌkɒntɪnjuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 中断；中止；停止；废除 | The discontinuations of the product line led to job losses. 产品线的停止生产导致了失业。|近义词：cessations, terminations, suspensions |反义词：continuations,resumptions
