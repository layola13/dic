flagman:/ˈflæɡmən/| n. |旗手，信号员 |A person who uses flags to give signals, especially to control traffic or guide trains.  |例句：The flagman signaled the train to stop. 旗手示意火车停止。|例句：The construction flagman guided traffic around the road closure. 建筑工地的旗手引导车辆绕过封闭的路段。|近义词：signalman, signaler |反义词：N/A


USAGE->

The flagman signals the train's departure. = 旗手示意火车出发。
The flagman directs traffic flow. = 旗手指挥交通流量。
