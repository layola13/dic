salt-and-pepper:/ˈsɔːlt ænd ˈpepər/| adj. |黑白相间的；盐和胡椒味的|Describes something having a mixture of black and white or grey colours, resembling the appearance of salt and pepper.  It can also describe food seasoned with salt and pepper.|例句：The cat has a salt-and-pepper coat.  这只猫的毛是黑白相间的。

例句：The steak was seasoned with salt and pepper. 这块牛排用盐和胡椒调味。


近义词：grey, grizzled, mottled (for colours); seasoned (for food)
反义词：monochromatic (for colours); unseasoned (for food)

词性：形容词
