plagiate:/ˈpleɪdʒeɪt/| vt.|剽窃，抄袭|To steal and pass off (the ideas or words of another) as one's own; use (another's production) without crediting the source.|例句：He plagiarized his essay from the internet. 他从网上抄袭了他的论文。

例句：The student was expelled for plagiarizing a paper.  这名学生因抄袭论文而被开除。


近义词：copy, steal, appropriate, crib, pirate|反义词：originate, create, invent|动词
