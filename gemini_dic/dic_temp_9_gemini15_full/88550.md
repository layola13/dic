hitch-hike:/ˈhɪtʃhaɪk/| v.|搭便车|To travel by getting free rides in someone else's vehicle.|He hitch-hiked across the country. 他搭便车横穿了全国。|travel, ride, bum a ride|stay put, walk.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hitchhike every summer. = 他们每年夏天都搭便车旅行。
She hitchhikes to school every day. = 她每天搭便车去上学。

一般过去时:
We hitchhiked to the festival last year. = 我们去年搭便车去参加节日。
He hitchhiked from London to Paris. = 他从伦敦搭便车到了巴黎。

现在完成时:
I have hitchhiked across three continents. = 我已经搭便车穿越了三个大洲。
She has never hitchhiked before. = 她以前从未搭过便车。

过去完成时:
They had hitchhiked for days before reaching the coast. = 他们搭便车走了好几天才到达海岸。
He had hitchhiked most of the way before his car broke down. = 他的车坏掉之前，他大部分路程都是搭便车走的。

将来时:
We will hitchhike to the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将搭便车去海滩。
They will hitchhike across the country next year. = 他们明年将搭便车穿越全国。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I wouldn't hitchhike. = 如果我有车，我不会搭便车。
I wish I hadn't hitchhiked alone at night. = 我真希望我晚上没有独自搭便车。

被动语态:
Many miles were hitchhiked during the journey. = 在旅途中搭乘了许多便车。
The route was often hitchhiked by tourists. = 这条路线经常被游客搭便车走。

疑问句:
Have you ever hitchhiked before? = 你以前搭过便车吗？
Did they hitchhike all the way to California? = 他们一路搭便车到加利福尼亚了吗？

否定句:
We didn't hitchhike; we took a bus. = 我们没有搭便车；我们坐公共汽车。
She doesn't like to hitchhike. = 她不喜欢搭便车。


PHRASE->
hitchhike across = 搭便车横穿
hitchhike to = 搭便车到
hitchhike from = 搭便车从...出发
hitchhike around = 搭便车环游
hitchhike for = 搭便车去（某个目的）
