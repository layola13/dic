repudiating:/rɪˈpjuːdieɪtɪŋ/| v.| 拒绝；否认；抛弃 |  To refuse to accept, acknowledge, or be associated with.  To reject or disown. |例句：He is repudiating the allegations against him. 他正在否认针对他的指控。

例句：The company repudiated the contract after discovering fraudulent activity. 公司在发现欺诈行为后拒绝对合同负责。


近义词：reject, deny, disown, renounce, disavow

反义词：accept, acknowledge, endorse, affirm

词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repudiates his past actions. 他否认他过去的所作所为。
She repudiates the agreement. 她拒绝接受协议。

一般过去时:
They repudiated the treaty. 他们否决了条约。
He repudiated his family's legacy. 他抛弃了他家族的遗产。

现在完成时:
She has repudiated her earlier statements. 她已经否认了她之前的陈述。
He has repudiated all responsibility for the incident. 他已经否认对该事件负有任何责任。

过去完成时:
They had repudiated the offer before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前已经拒绝了提议。
She had repudiated her beliefs before converting to another religion.  她皈依其他宗教之前已经放弃了她之前的信仰。

将来时:
He will repudiate the charges against him. 他将否认对他提出的指控。
The government will repudiate the agreement if certain conditions are not met. 如果不满足某些条件，政府将否决该协议。

虚拟语气:
If he repudiated the deal, the company would suffer. 如果他拒绝这笔交易，公司将会受损。
I wish she hadn't repudiated our friendship. 我真希望她没有拒绝我们的友谊。

被动语态:
The contract was repudiated by the company. 合同被公司拒绝对其负责。
His claims were repudiated by the court. 他的说法被法院驳回。

疑问句:
Will he repudiate the evidence? 他会否认证据吗？
Does she repudiate her own family? 她是否否认自己的家人？

否定句:
He did not repudiate the offer. 他没有拒绝提议。
She does not repudiate her commitment. 她没有否认她的承诺。


PHRASE->
repudiate a claim: 驳斥说法
repudiate a debt: 拒付债务
repudiate a treaty: 废除条约
repudiate an agreement: 撕毁协议
repudiate one's heritage: 抛弃自己的传统
