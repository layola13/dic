radio-protection:/ˈreɪdioʊprəˈtɛkʃən/| n. |辐射防护，防辐射|The protection of people, animals, and the environment from the harmful effects of ionizing radiation.  This involves various methods to minimize exposure, such as shielding, distance, and time limitation.|例句：The radio-protection measures in the nuclear power plant are very strict. 核电站的辐射防护措施非常严格。|例句：Adequate radio-protection is crucial during medical imaging procedures. 在医学影像检查过程中，充分的辐射防护至关重要。|近义词：radiation shielding, radiation safety|反义词：radiation exposure
