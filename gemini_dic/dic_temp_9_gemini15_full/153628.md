pentecostalists:/ˌpɛn.təˈkɒs.təl.ɪsts/| n. |五旬节教派信徒|Adherents of Pentecostalism, a renewal movement within Protestant Christianity emphasizing spiritual gifts such as speaking in tongues (glossolalia), divine healing, and prophecy.|Many Pentecostalists believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit, evidenced by speaking in tongues.  许多五旬节教派信徒相信圣灵的洗礼，其证据是说方言。|近义词：charismatic Christians, evangelical Christians|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with those of other Christian denominations.
