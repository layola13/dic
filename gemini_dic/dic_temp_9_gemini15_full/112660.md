lower:/ˈloʊər/ /ˈləʊə(r)/ | adj., v., n.|较低的；降低；下议院|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "lower" means situated or placed below something else; less in amount, degree, or rank.  As a verb, it means to move something or someone downwards; to reduce something in amount, degree, or intensity. As a noun, it refers to the lower house of a legislature, such as the House of Commons in the UK or the House of Representatives in the US.

例句:
The lower shelf is full of books. = 下层的架子上全是书。

The company decided to lower its prices. = 公司决定降低价格。


近义词:
(adj.) inferior, subordinate, lesser, reduced
(v.) decrease, reduce, diminish, lessen

反义词:
(adj.) higher, upper, superior, greater
(v.) raise, increase, elevate, augment


词性:
adj., v., n.
