perinatally:/ˌpɛrɪˈneɪtəli/ | adv. |围产期地，围产期；出生前后 | Relating to the period around the time of birth.  This encompasses the time before birth (prenatal), during birth (intrapartum), and shortly after birth (postnatal). |The child was exposed to a toxin perinatally, leading to developmental problems.  孩子在围产期接触到毒素，导致发育问题。|  around the time of birth,  during the perinatal period |  preconception,  post-adolescence | 副词
