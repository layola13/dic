robber:/ˈrɑːbər/| n. |强盗，抢劫犯 |A robber is a person who steals from others, often using force or threats.  Robbers typically target valuables such as money, jewelry, or other possessions.  |例句：The robber was caught by the police after a high-speed chase.  这个强盗在高速追逐后被警方抓获。

The robber escaped with a large amount of cash.  这个强盗带着一大笔现金逃跑了。|近义词：thief, bandit, burglar |反义词：victim, benefactor


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The robber steals money from banks. = 强盗从银行偷钱。
He robs people on the street. = 他在街上抢劫路人。

一般过去时:
The robber stole a valuable necklace. = 强盗偷了一条珍贵的项链。
They robbed the museum last night. = 他们昨晚抢劫了博物馆。

现在完成时:
The police have arrested the robber. = 警方已经逮捕了强盗。
He has robbed several stores in the area. = 他已经抢劫了该地区几家商店。

过去完成时:
The police had already arrived when the robber escaped. = 强盗逃跑时，警察已经到了。
They had robbed the bank before the alarm sounded. = 警报响起之前，他们已经抢劫了银行。

将来时:
The robber will likely face a long prison sentence. = 这个强盗很可能会面临漫长的监禁。
They will rob another store next week. = 他们下周将抢劫另一家商店。

虚拟语气:
If I were a robber, I would be very careful. = 如果我是个强盗，我会非常小心。
I wish the robber hadn't hurt anyone. = 我希望强盗没有伤害任何人。

被动语态:
The bank was robbed last night. = 银行昨晚被抢劫了。
The robber was apprehended by the police. = 强盗被警方逮捕了。

疑问句:
Was it a robber who stole the car? = 偷车的是强盗吗？

否定句:
