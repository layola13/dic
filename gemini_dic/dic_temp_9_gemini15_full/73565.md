forester:/ˈfɒrɪstə(r)/| /ˈfɔːrɪstər/| n. |护林员，林务官|A forester is a person who works in the management and care of forests.  They are responsible for protecting and preserving forest resources, including trees, wildlife, and soil.  Foresters often work for government agencies or private companies. |例句：The forester planted new trees to replace those lost in the fire.  护林员种植新树以取代火灾中损失的树木。


The forester surveyed the damage after the storm.  暴风雨过后，护林员勘察了损失情况。|近义词：woodsman, gamekeeper |反义词：None directly applicable.  |词性 n.
