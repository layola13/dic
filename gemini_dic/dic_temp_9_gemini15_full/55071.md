disregarding:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/ | v. | 不顾；漠视；忽略 | Ignoring or neglecting; treating something as unimportant.  |例句：He disregarded the warnings and continued driving. 他不顾警告继续开车。|例句：She disregarded his advice and did what she wanted. 她不顾他的建议做了她想做的事。|近义词：ignoring, neglecting, overlooking, dismissing |反义词：heeding, considering, respecting

一般现在时:
He disregards the rules. 他无视规则。
They disregard my opinions. 他们不顾我的意见。

一般过去时:
She disregarded the doctor's advice. 她不理会医生的建议。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
He had disregarded the earlier warnings, resulting in the accident. 他之前无视了警告，导致了事故。

将来时:

被动语态:

否定句:
