pretentiousnesses:/prɪˈtɛnʃəsnəsɪz/ | n. |炫耀，自命不凡；矫揉造作 |The plural form of pretentiousness, referring to multiple instances or types of ostentatious or affected behavior.  It signifies excessive displays of importance, knowledge, or culture, often to impress others. |例句：Her pretentiousnesses, from her overly elaborate vocabulary to her affected air of superiority, alienated her colleagues.  她的炫耀，从她过于华丽的词汇到她故作高傲的神情，都疏远了她的同事。  The various pretentiousnesses on display at the art exhibition were frankly tiresome.  艺术展览上展示的各种矫揉造作，坦白说令人厌烦。|近义词：affectations, ostentations, airs, pomposities |反义词：humility, modesty, simplicity
