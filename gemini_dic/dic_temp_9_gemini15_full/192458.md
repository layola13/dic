scrubstone:/ˈskrʌbstəʊn/| n.|粗糙的石头，磨石|A rough, coarse-grained stone, often used for scrubbing or sharpening.  The term often implies a stone found naturally, rather than manufactured.|例句：The old woman used a scrubstone to clean her pots and pans.  这位老妇人用磨石来清洗她的锅碗瓢盆。
The gardener used a scrubstone to sharpen his shears. 园丁用磨石来磨他的剪刀。|近义词：grindstone, whetstone, sharpening stone|反义词：polished stone, smooth stone|名词
