ingurgitate:/ɪŋˈɡɜːrɡɪteɪt/| vt.|吞咽；狼吞虎咽|To swallow greedily or in large amounts.  The word implies a forceful and rapid swallowing, often suggesting a lack of refinement or excessive haste.|例句：He ingurgitated the entire pizza in under five minutes. 他在五分钟内狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整个披萨。

例句：The thirsty traveler ingurgitated the water from the stream. 口渴的旅行者贪婪地喝着溪水。


近义词：gulp, swallow, devour, guzzle|反义词：spit out, reject, refuse
