gittern:/ˈɡɪtərn/| n. |吉特恩琴（一种中世纪的拨弦乐器）|A gittern is a medieval plucked stringed instrument, similar in shape to a lute but typically smaller and with a pear-shaped body.  It was popular in Europe from the 12th to the 15th centuries. |Example: The minstrel played a lively tune on his gittern.  吟游诗人用他的吉特恩琴演奏了一首欢快的曲子。|近义词：lute, cittern |反义词：无
