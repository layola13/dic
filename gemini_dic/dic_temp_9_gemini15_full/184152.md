resited:/rɪˈsaɪtɪd/| v.|重新安置；重新提及；再次陈述|To place or establish again; to mention or state again.|例句：The company resited its headquarters to a more central location.  公司将其总部迁至更中心的位置。|例句：The witness resited his earlier testimony.  证人再次陈述了他的早期证词。|近义词：relocated, repositioned, reiterated, restated|反义词：removed, relocated (in the opposite direction)
