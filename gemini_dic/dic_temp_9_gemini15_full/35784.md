cockatoo:/ˌkɒkəˈtuː/| n. |凤头鹦鹉|A cockatoo is a type of parrot that is known for its large crest of feathers on its head. They are often brightly colored and are popular as pets.  |Example: The cockatoo screeched loudly when it was startled.  这只凤头鹦鹉受惊吓时大声尖叫。|近义词：parrot (general term)|反义词：无
