fall for:/fɔːl fɔːr/| phrasal verb| 迷恋；上当；受骗 |To be strongly attracted to someone; to be tricked or deceived. |例句：I fell for him the moment I saw him. 我一看到他就爱上他了。

例句：He fell for the scam and lost all his money. 他上了骗局的当，损失了所有钱财。


近义词：be attracted to, be infatuated with, be deceived by, be tricked by |反义词：resist, ignore, see through
