lavolta:/ləˈvɒltə/ | n. |  旋转舞，快步舞 | A lively, whirling dance of Italian origin, characterized by rapid turns and changes of direction.  It was particularly popular during the Renaissance. | Example: The dancers performed a dazzling lavolta, their movements a blur of motion.  舞者们表演了一支令人眼花缭乱的旋转舞，他们的动作像一团模糊的运动。| 近义词：volta, galliard | 反义词：slow dance, minuet
