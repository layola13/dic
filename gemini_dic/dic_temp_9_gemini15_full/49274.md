deliberates:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪts/| v.|仔细考虑；认真商讨|To think about or discuss something carefully and in detail before making a decision.|The committee deliberates on the proposed budget before voting.|近义词：consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss|反义词：rush, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jury deliberates on the evidence. = 陪审团正在仔细考虑证据。
She deliberates before making important decisions. = 她做重要决定前都会仔细考虑。


一般过去时:
The council deliberated for hours before reaching a decision. = 理事会经过数小时的讨论才做出决定。
They deliberated the matter carefully. = 他们仔细地考虑了这件事。


现在完成时:
The court has deliberated and reached a verdict. = 法院已经经过审议并做出了裁决。
We have deliberated the pros and cons of the proposal. = 我们已经仔细权衡了这项提案的利弊。


过去完成时:
After they had deliberated, they announced their findings. = 他们经过商议后，宣布了他们的调查结果。
The committee had deliberated for days before voting. = 委员会在投票前已经商讨了几天。


将来时:
The judge will deliberate before passing sentence. = 法官将在判刑前进行审议。
The board will deliberate on the merger next week. = 董事会下周将对合并案进行商议。


虚拟语气:
If the jury deliberated more carefully, they might reach a different verdict. = 如果陪审团更仔细地审议，他们可能会得出不同的裁决。
I wish the committee had deliberated more thoroughly. = 我希望委员会能更彻底地进行商议。


被动语态:
The matter was deliberated extensively by the committee. = 这件事由委员会进行了广泛的讨论。
The proposal is being deliberated by the board. = 董事会正在对提案进行审议。


疑问句:
Has the committee deliberated on the issue yet? = 委员会已经对这个问题进行审议了吗？
Will the court deliberate before sentencing? = 法院在判刑前会进行审议吗？


否定句:
The judge did not deliberate long before making a ruling. = 法官在做出裁决前并没有长时间的考虑。
They did not deliberate the consequences of their actions. = 他们没有仔细考虑他们行为的后果。
