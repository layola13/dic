itinerantly:/ɪˈtɪnərəntli/| adv. | 巡回地；旅程地；四处奔波地 | In a way that involves traveling from place to place, usually for work.  |例句：The band toured itinerantly, playing in small towns across the country. 乐队巡回演出，在全国各地的小城镇演出。  The artist lived itinerantly, finding inspiration in different landscapes.  这位艺术家四处奔波生活，在不同的风景中寻找灵感。|近义词：peripatetically, erratically, nomadically |反义词：sedentarily, statically

词源:
