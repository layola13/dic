intermedial: /ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəl/ | adj. | 中间的；中级的；媒介的 | Relating to or denoting a stage or position that is intermediate between two others.  It often refers to something that bridges or connects different media or forms.  | Example: The artist's work is considered intermedial, blending painting and digital animation.  艺术家作品被认为是媒介的，融合了绘画和数字动画。 |近义词: intermediate, mediating, transitional | 反义词: initial, final, primary, ultimate
