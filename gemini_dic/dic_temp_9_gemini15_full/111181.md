lithographed:/lɪˈθɑːɡræft/ | adj. | 石版印刷的；用石版印刷术制作的 | Printed from a lithographic stone or plate.  The process involves drawing or writing on a specially prepared stone, then using that stone to print multiple copies. |例句：The poster was beautifully lithographed in vibrant colors. 这张海报是用鲜艳的色彩精美石版印刷的。
The antique book contained lithographed illustrations. 这本古书包含石版印刷的插图。


近义词：printed,  offset-printed (if referring to a specific printing method)


反义词：hand-drawn, hand-painted (if contrasting with the printing method)


词性：形容词
