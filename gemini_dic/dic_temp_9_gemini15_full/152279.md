patsies:/ˈpæsiːz/| n.|傻瓜，受骗者，牺牲品|A patsy is someone who is easily tricked or manipulated.  They are often naive, gullible, or easily intimidated.|例句：He was used as a patsy in the robbery. 他在抢劫案中被当作替罪羊。

例句：They made him a patsy, taking advantage of his trusting nature. 他们利用他容易相信的本性，把他当作了傻瓜。


近义词：fool, dupe, sucker, victim, chump

反义词：manipulator, mastermind, con artist


词性: 名词
