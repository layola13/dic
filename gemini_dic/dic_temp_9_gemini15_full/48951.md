degarnish:/diːˈɡɑːrnɪʃ/| vt.|去除装饰；除去…的装饰|To remove decorations or garnish from something.|The chef degarnished the plate before serving it.  厨师在端上菜之前去掉了盘子上的装饰。|近义词：remove, strip, undecorate|反义词：garnish, decorate


USAGE->

The chef degarnishes the dishes before plating. = 厨师在摆盘前去除菜肴的装饰。
They degarnish the cakes with simple icing. = 他们用简单的糖霜去除蛋糕上的装饰。
