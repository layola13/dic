non-topographical:/ˌnɒnˌtɒpəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. |非地形学的；非地形的| Not related to or based on the features of a particular area of land.  This adjective describes something that does not concern the physical geography or surface features of a place. It is the opposite of topographical. |例句：The study focused on non-topographical factors influencing the spread of the disease. 这项研究侧重于影响疾病传播的非地形因素。

The analysis used non-topographical data, such as population density and socioeconomic status.  分析使用了非地形数据，例如人口密度和社会经济地位。


近义词：non-geographic, a-topographical


反义词：topographical, geographic


词性：形容词
