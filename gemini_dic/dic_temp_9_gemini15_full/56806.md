The Dorian mode is a musical scale or mode characterized by a characteristic sound often described as austere, majestic, or serious.|例句：The concert featured a piece in the Dorian mode.  这场音乐会演奏了一首多利亚调式的作品。The ruins are a testament to the Dorian civilization. 这些遗址是多利亚文明的证明。|近义词：Greek, austere, majestic|反义词：frivolous, light, playful
