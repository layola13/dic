clearness:/ˈklɛːrnəs/ | n. |清晰度，明朗，明白 |The clarity of the explanation was impressive. 解释的清晰度令人印象深刻。|近义词：clarity, lucidity, transparency |反义词：ambiguity, obscurity, vagueness


词源:  源自于古英语 *clǣrnes,  由 *clǣr (清晰的) + -nes (名词后缀)构成。


用法说明:

clearness 主要用作名词，表示清晰、明了的状态或程度，可以指视觉、听觉、思维等方面的清晰度。


例句:

1. The clearness of the water allowed us to see the fish swimming below. 水的清澈让我们可以看到下面游动的鱼。

2.  The professor's explanation lacked clearness; many students were confused. 教授的解释缺乏清晰度；许多学生感到困惑。

3. The clearness of his intentions was evident in his actions. 他的意图的明确性在他的行动中显而易见。

4.  There was a surprising clearness in the air after the storm. 暴风雨过后，空气出奇地清新。

5. The picture lacked clearness due to the poor lighting.  由于灯光不好，照片缺乏清晰度。


近义词:

clarity, lucidity, transparency, distinctness, perspicuity


反义词:

ambiguity, obscurity, vagueness, confusion, murkiness
