long-haul: /lɒŋ hɔːl/ /lɔːŋ hɔːl/ | adj. n. | 长途的；长途运输 |  Relating to or involving a long distance, especially in travel or transportation.  |例句：The long-haul flight to Australia was tiring.  澳大利亚的长途航班令人疲惫。

例句：She works in long-haul trucking. 她从事长途卡车运输工作。|近义词：long-distance, extended, far-reaching |反义词：short, brief, local |词性:形容词，名词
