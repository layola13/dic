cambrian:/kæmˈbriən/| adj. n.|寒武纪的；寒武纪时期|Of or relating to the Cambrian period, the first period of the Paleozoic Era, lasting from about 541 to 485.4 million years ago, characterized by the sudden appearance of diverse and complex life forms in the fossil record.| The Cambrian explosion is a significant event in the history of life. 寒武纪生命大爆发是生命史上的一个重要事件。  |Precambrian|Postcambrian|adj. n.
