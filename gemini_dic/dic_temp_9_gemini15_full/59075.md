dwindling: /ˈdwɪndəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 逐渐减少的；衰退的 | Gradually decreasing in size, amount, or strength.  | The dwindling supply of oil is causing prices to rise.  石油供应减少导致价格上涨。 | decreasing, diminishing, fading, shrinking | increasing, growing, expanding | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The population of the town is dwindling. = 这个小镇的人口正在减少。
His energy is dwindling as the day goes on. = 他一天天能量越来越少。


一般过去时:
The company's profits dwindled last year. = 公司去年的利润减少了。
Her enthusiasm dwindled as the project progressed. = 随着项目的进展，她的热情减退了。


现在完成时:
Our savings have dwindled significantly. = 我们的积蓄已经大幅减少。
The number of students in the class has dwindled this semester. = 这个学期班上的学生人数减少了。


过去完成时:
By the end of the journey, their supplies had dwindled to almost nothing. = 到旅程结束时，他们的补给几乎消耗殆尽。
His hope had dwindled after several failed attempts. = 经过几次失败的尝试后，他的希望破灭了。


将来时:
The resources will dwindle unless we find new sources. = 除非我们找到新的资源，否则资源将会枯竭。
His patience will dwindle if we don't get this done quickly. = 如果我们不快点完成这件事，他的耐心就会耗尽。


虚拟语气:
If the funding dwindled, the project would be cancelled. = 如果资金减少，项目就会被取消。
I wish my patience wouldn't dwindle so quickly. = 我希望我的耐心不会这么快就耗尽。


被动语态:
The forest was dwindling due to deforestation. = 森林由于砍伐森林而日渐减少。
The resources are being dwindled by overuse. = 资源因过度使用而减少。


疑问句:
Is the company's market share dwindling? = 公司的市场份额是否正在减少？
Are your funds dwindling? = 你的资金是否正在减少？


否定句:
The support for the candidate did not dwindle despite the negative press. = 尽管有负面新闻，候选人的支持率并没有下降。
The quality of the products did not dwindle over time. = 产品的质量并没有随着时间的推移而下降。


PHRASE->
a dwindling number = 越来越少的数量
dwindling resources = 逐渐减少的资源
a dwindling population = 逐渐减少的人口
dwindling supplies = 逐渐减少的供应
dwindling funds = 逐渐减少的资金
