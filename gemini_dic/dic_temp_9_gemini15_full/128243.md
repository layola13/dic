neurolinguist:/ˌnʊrəʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst/| n. |神经语言学家|A neurolinguist is a scientist who studies the neural mechanisms in the brain that control the comprehension, production, and acquisition of language.  They investigate how the brain processes language, including areas such as speech perception, language comprehension, language production, and the neural basis of language disorders.  |Example: The neurolinguist presented her findings on the brain regions involved in bilingual language processing.  神经语言学家展示了她关于参与双语语言处理的大脑区域的研究结果。|近义词: psycholinguist, cognitive scientist|反义词: N/A
