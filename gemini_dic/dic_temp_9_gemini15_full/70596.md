filly:/ˈfɪli/| n.|小母马；年轻女子|A young female horse, especially one that is less than four years old.  The term can also be used figuratively to refer to a young woman, often implying youthfulness and energy.|例句：The filly won the race easily. 这匹小母马轻松赢得了比赛。

例句：She's a spirited filly, full of life. 她是个充满活力的年轻女子。|近义词：mare (for the literal meaning), girl, young woman|反义词：stallion (male horse), old woman
