despatcher:/dɪˈspætʃər/ | n. |调度员，发送者 | A person who sends or forwards something, especially goods or messages; a person who coordinates the activities of others, such as vehicles or personnel.  |The despatcher quickly routed the emergency call to the nearest ambulance. 调度员迅速将紧急呼叫转接到最近的救护车。 The despatcher ensured all packages were correctly labeled and sent. 发送者确保所有包裹都贴上了正确的标签并被发送。| dispatcher, sender | receiver | 名词
