futurologist:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɒlədʒɪst/| n. |未来学家|A futurologist is a person who studies and predicts future trends and developments, often in areas such as technology, society, and the environment. They use various methods, including data analysis, trend extrapolation, and scenario planning, to forecast potential future scenarios.  |Many futurologists believe that technology will continue to transform society in profound ways.  许多未来学家认为技术将继续以深刻的方式改变社会。|social scientist, prognosticator, prophet |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 pessimist  if focusing on predictions) |名词
