microfilming:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlmɪŋ/| n. v.|缩微胶卷拍摄；缩微胶卷制作|The process of photographing documents or other materials onto microfilm.  This reduces the size of the original material significantly, making storage and retrieval easier. |例句：The library uses microfilming to preserve old newspapers. 图书馆用缩微胶卷拍摄来保存旧报纸。

The historical archives are undergoing extensive microfilming. 历史档案馆正在进行大规模的缩微胶卷拍摄。

近义词：microphotography, miniaturization |反义词：enlargement, magnification
