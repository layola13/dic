historian:/hɪˈstɔːriən/ | n. |历史学家|A historian is a person who studies and writes about history. Historians analyze past events, interpret their significance, and construct narratives to understand the past.|例句：Many historians believe that the event was a turning point in history. 许多历史学家认为这一事件是历史的转折点。|例句：She is a renowned historian specializing in ancient Rome. 她是一位专门研究古罗马的著名历史学家。|近义词：chronicler, scholar (of history)|反义词：N/A|名词
