/leɪmz/| adj.| 无聊的；乏味的；差劲的 | lame means boring, uninteresting, or unsatisfactory. It can also describe something that is weak or ineffective.  | The movie was so lame.  这部电影太无聊了。| His excuse was lame. 他的借口很牵强。 | interesting, exciting | exciting, great, superb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The comedian's jokes are lame. = 这个喜剧演员的笑话很无聊。
This plan seems lame to me. = 这个计划在我看来很糟糕。


一般过去时:
His attempt to fix the car was lame. = 他修理汽车的尝试很失败。
The party was incredibly lame. = 派对非常无趣。


现在完成时:
I've found his explanations lame for years. = 多年来我一直觉得他的解释很无力。
We have had some lame experiences recently. = 我们最近经历了一些糟糕的体验。


过去完成时:
I had already realized his argument was lame. = 我已经意识到他的论点很薄弱。
She had thought the presentation was lame before it even began. = 在演示开始之前，她就觉得演示很糟糕。


将来时:
The next album will be lame, I think. = 我认为下一张专辑会很糟糕。
That idea will be lame, for sure. = 那主意肯定很差劲。


虚拟语气:
If his excuse was less lame, I might believe him. = 如果他的借口不那么牵强，我可能会相信他。
If the presentation wasn't so lame, we'd have been interested. = 如果演示不那么糟糕，我们会感兴趣的。


被动语态:
His performance was considered lame by the critics. = 他的表演被评论家认为很糟糕。
The project was deemed lame and was canceled. = 该项目被认为很糟糕，并被取消了。


疑问句:
Isn't this idea lame? = 这个主意是不是很糟糕？
Don't you think the movie was lame? = 你不觉得这部电影很无聊吗？


否定句:
The party wasn't lame; it was actually quite fun. = 派对并不无聊；事实上它相当有趣。
His explanation wasn't lame at all; it was quite convincing. = 他的解释一点也不牵强；它相当令人信服。


PHRASE->
