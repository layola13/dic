/ˌnɑːnˈwɛstərn/ | adj. | 非西方的 | Relating to or characteristic of countries or cultures outside of the West (generally considered to include Europe and North America).  This term is often used in academic and political contexts to discuss cultures, histories, and perspectives different from those traditionally dominant in Western discourse.  The definition of "West" itself can be debated and is often contested. | Example:  The course explores non-Western perspectives on globalization. 这门课程探讨了对全球化的非西方视角。  The conference featured scholars from various non-Western nations.  此次会议邀请了来自各个非西方国家的学者。| 近义词: non-European, Eastern, Southern (in some contexts), developing (sometimes, but potentially inaccurate and problematic) | 反义词: Western, Occidental
