dongola:/dɒŋˈɡoʊlə/ | n. |东戈拉皮革；东戈拉鞋|A type of strong, durable leather originally made in Dongola, Sudan. It is known for its fine grain and water resistance.|例句：His boots were made of high-quality dongola leather.  他的靴子是用优质东戈拉皮革制成的。|近义词：morocco leather, calfskin|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun, so verb conjugations are not applicable.  The usage focuses on its descriptive properties as a type of leather.
