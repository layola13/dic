economizer:/ˈɪkənəmaɪzər/ | n. |节约器，省油器 | A device or system designed to reduce consumption of a resource, typically energy or fuel.  It often refers to devices that improve efficiency. |Example: The new economizer on the boiler significantly reduced fuel consumption.  新的锅炉节约器大大降低了燃料消耗。|近义词：saver, conserver |反义词：waster, squanderer
