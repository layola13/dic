hyperoestrogenism:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈɛstrədʒənɪzəm/ | n. |雌激素过多症 | A condition characterized by excessively high levels of estrogen in the body.  This can result from various factors, including tumors, certain medications, or liver disease.  Symptoms can vary widely depending on the cause and severity but may include irregular menstrual cycles, breast tenderness, weight gain, and mood changes. |  She was diagnosed with hyperoestrogenism after experiencing prolonged irregular bleeding. 她经历了长时间的不规则出血后被诊断为雌激素过多症。 The doctor suspects a hormonal imbalance causing her hyperoestrogenism. 医生怀疑是荷尔蒙失衡导致了她的雌激素过多症。|  estrogen excess, high estrogen levels | estrogen deficiency, hypoestrogenism | 名词
