fixatifs:/fiksəˈtiːfs/| n.| 定型剂；固定剂 |Fixatifs are substances used to fix or set something, typically in art or science.  In art, they are used to preserve artwork, preventing colors from fading or running. In microscopy, they are used to preserve tissue samples for examination. | 例句：The artist used a fixative spray to protect the watercolor painting.  艺术家用定型喷雾来保护水彩画。


The photographer employed a fixative solution to preserve the developed negatives. 摄影师用定影液来保存冲洗后的底片。|近义词：preservative, stabilizer |反义词：solvent (in some contexts)
