durative:/ˈdʒʊərətɪv/| adj.|持续的，持久的|lasting for a considerable time; continuous.|The durative aspect of the verb describes an action that continues over a period of time.  持续体动词描述的是一个持续一段时间内发生的动作。|近义词：continuous, prolonged, enduring|反义词：fleeting, momentary, transient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is working on a long-term project. = 他正在从事一个长期项目。
She always drinks tea in the morning. = 她总是早上喝茶。


一般过去时:
They lived in London for five years. = 他们在伦敦住了五年。
We played football for two hours. = 我们踢了两个小时的足球。


现在完成时:
I have been studying English for three years. = 我学习英语三年了。
She has worked in this company since 2010. = 她从2010年就在这家公司工作了。


过去完成时:
He had been waiting for her for an hour before she arrived. = 她到达之前他已经等了她一个小时了。
They had lived in Paris for ten years before moving to New York. = 他们在搬到纽约之前在巴黎住了十年。


将来时:
We will be staying at the hotel for a week. = 我们将在酒店住一个星期。
She will be working on the project until next month. = 她将一直工作到下个月。


虚拟语气:
If I had had more time, I would have stayed longer. = 如果我有更多时间，我就会待久一点。
I wish I had been able to travel more when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候能够多旅行一些。


被动语态:
The bridge was being constructed for two years. = 这座桥梁建造了两年。
The problem has been discussed for several meetings. = 这个问题已经在几次会议上讨论过了。


疑问句:
How long have you been learning French? = 你学习法语多久了？
How long did the meeting last? = 会议持续了多久？


否定句:
The rain didn't last long. = 雨没有下很久。
He wasn't working on the project for very long. = 他并没有在这个项目上工作很久。


PHRASE->
durative aspect = 持续体
durative action = 持续性动作
durative verb = 持续性动词
durative event = 持续性事件
long-durative = 长期持续的
