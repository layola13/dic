lessons:/ˈlɛsən(z)/| n.|课程，教训，功课|instruction given to a person to improve their knowledge or skill in a particular subject; something learned from an experience.|例句：The teacher gave us a lesson on grammar.老师给我们上了一堂语法课。|例句：I learned a valuable lesson from my mistakes.我从我的错误中吸取了宝贵的教训。|近义词：class, lecture, instruction, tutorial|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher gives lessons every day. = 老师每天上课。
He teaches English lessons. = 他教授英语课程。


一般过去时:
She gave a piano lesson yesterday. = 她昨天上了一堂钢琴课。
We learned our lessons well. = 我们把功课学得很扎实。


现在完成时:
I have learned many lessons in life. = 我在生活中学习了很多教训。
She has given hundreds of lessons this year. = 今年她已经上了数百节课。


过去完成时:
I had already finished my lessons before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经完成了我的功课。
