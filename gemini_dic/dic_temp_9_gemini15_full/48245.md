decontrols:/ˌdiːkənˈtrəʊlz/ | v. |取消管制；放松管制 | To remove or reduce government controls or regulations on a particular industry or activity.  | Example: The government decontrolled the airline industry, leading to increased competition and lower fares.  政府取消了对航空业的管制，导致竞争加剧，票价下降。 |近义词：deregulate, liberalize | 反义词：regulate, control | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government decontrols the energy market. = 政府取消对能源市场的管制。
They decontrol prices regularly. = 他们定期取消价格管制。


一般过去时:
The president decontrolled the oil industry last year. = 总统去年取消了石油行业的管制。
The authorities decontrolled the market in 2005. = 当局在2005年取消了市场管制。


现在完成时:
The government has decontrolled several sectors of the economy. = 政府已经取消了经济的几个部门的管制。
They have decontrolled the telecommunications industry.  = 他们已经取消了电信行业的管制。


过去完成时:
They had decontrolled the banking sector before the crisis. = 危机爆发前，他们已经取消了银行业的管制。
The country had decontrolled its media before the election. = 该国在大选前已经取消了媒体管制。


将来时:

They will decontrol the internet soon. = 他们很快就会取消互联网管制。


虚拟语气:
