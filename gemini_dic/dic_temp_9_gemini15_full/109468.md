lepidoted:/ˈlɛpɪdoʊtɪd/| adj.| 有鳞的；布满鳞片的 | covered with scales or scale-like structures.  The word describes a surface texture characterized by small, overlapping scales or scale-like formations, resembling those found on reptiles or fish. |例句：The lepidoted skin of the snake made it difficult to grip.  这条蛇有鳞的皮肤很难抓住。

The lepidoted leaves of the plant shimmered in the sunlight.  植物有鳞的叶子在阳光下闪闪发光。

近义词：scaly, squamous

反义词：smooth, glabrous

词性：形容词
