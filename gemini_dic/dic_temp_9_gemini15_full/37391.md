commenter:/ˈkɑːmɛntər/| n.|评论者，评论员|A person who makes comments, especially online.  Someone who posts comments on a website, blog, or social media platform.|Example: The commenter left a scathing review of the new movie.  评论者对这部新电影留下了尖刻的评论。|近义词：critic, reviewer, annotator|反义词：author (in the context of creating content, not commenting on it)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The commenter posts insightful remarks daily. = 这位评论员每天都发表有见地的评论。
That commenter always shares her opinions. = 那位评论员总是分享她的观点。


一般过去时:
The commenter wrote a detailed critique yesterday. = 评论者昨天写了一篇详细的评论。
He commented on the article last week. = 他上周对这篇文章发表了评论。


现在完成时:
The commenter has already responded to several questions. = 评论者已经回答了几个问题。
She has consistently provided helpful feedback. = 她一直提供有益的反馈。


过去完成时:
The commenter had already expressed his dissatisfaction before the meeting. = 在会议之前，评论者已经表达了他的不满。
They had posted numerous comments before the article was updated. = 在文章更新之前，他们已经发布了大量的评论。


将来时:

He will likely address the concerns raised in the comments section. = 他可能会解决评论区中提出的担忧。


虚拟语气:
If the commenter were more respectful, the discussion would be more productive. = 如果评论者更有礼貌，讨论会更有成效。
I wish the commenter had been more constructive in their criticism. = 我希望评论者在批评中更有建设性。
