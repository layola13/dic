screen-print:/ˈskriːnˌprɪnt/| n. vt.|丝网印刷；晒版印刷|A method of printing in which ink is pressed through a screen onto a surface.  The screen is typically made of silk or synthetic fabric, and the design is created by blocking out areas of the screen.|例句：We used screen-print to create custom t-shirts for the event. 我们用丝网印刷为活动制作了定制T恤。

例句：The artist uses screen-printing to reproduce his artwork on fabric.  这位艺术家用丝网印刷将他的作品复制到织物上。


近义词：silk-screen printing, serigraphy|反义词：无
