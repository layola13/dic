promoters:/prəˈmoʊtərz/ | n. | 推广者；促进者；启动子 |

英文解析：A promoter is a person who promotes something, such as a product, service, or event.  In genetics, a promoter is a region of DNA that initiates transcription of a particular gene.

例句：

The company hired promoters to distribute flyers at the music festival.  这家公司雇佣了推广员在音乐节上分发传单。

The new marketing campaign relies heavily on social media promoters. 新的营销活动严重依赖社交媒体推广者。


近义词：advocates, sponsors, boosters, (in genetics) enhancers

反义词：inhibitors, suppressors, detractors
