preadjustable: /ˌpriːəˈdʒʌstəbl/ | adj. | 可预先调整的，可预先设置的 | Capable of being adjusted beforehand; preset.  This refers to something that can be set or configured in advance, before its actual use or operation.  | 例句：The preadjustable thermostat allowed me to set the temperature before I even got home. = 可预先调整的恒温器让我在回家之前就能设置好温度。

The camera's preadjustable settings ensured consistent image quality. = 相机的预设设置确保了图像质量的一致性。


近义词：pre-set, preset, configurable, adjustable in advance

反义词：fixed, unadjustable, inflexible


词性：形容词
