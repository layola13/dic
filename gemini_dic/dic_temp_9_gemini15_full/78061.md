geocentric:/ˌdʒioʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|地心说的；以地球为中心的|Relating to or denoting a system of astronomy that regards the earth as the center of the universe.|The geocentric model of the universe placed the Earth at the center, with the sun, moon, and stars revolving around it.|近义词：earth-centered|反义词：heliocentric
