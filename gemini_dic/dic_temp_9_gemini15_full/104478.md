kaizen:/kaɪˈzɛn/| n.|改进，改善，持续改进|Continuous improvement; a Japanese philosophy emphasizing incremental changes and improvements in business and daily life.  It focuses on making small, ongoing changes to processes, products, or services to improve efficiency and quality.|Our company implemented a kaizen program to streamline our workflow.  我们的公司实施了精益改进计划以简化我们的工作流程。|improvement, betterment, optimization|stagnation, deterioration, regression|名词
