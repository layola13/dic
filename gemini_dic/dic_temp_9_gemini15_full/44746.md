crumbled:/ˈkrʌmbəld/ | v. | 崩溃；碎裂；瓦解 | to break into small pieces; to fall apart; to disintegrate  |例句：The old building crumbled to dust.  这栋旧建筑变成了一堆尘土。

The cake crumbled when I tried to cut it. 我试图切蛋糕时，它碎了。


近义词：disintegrated, collapsed, broke apart, fell apart | 反义词：solidified, strengthened, unified


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old bridge crumbles slowly.  = 这座旧桥在慢慢地崩塌。
The empire crumbles under the weight of its own corruption. = 这个帝国在自身腐败的重压下土崩瓦解。


一般过去时:
The ancient walls crumbled during the earthquake. = 古代城墙在地震中倒塌了。
His dreams crumbled before his eyes. = 他的梦想在他眼前破灭了。


现在完成时:
The empire has crumbled into dust. = 这个帝国已经化为乌有。
The building has crumbled after years of neglect. = 这栋建筑由于多年的忽视而倒塌了。


过去完成时:
The old house had crumbled before they could renovate it. = 在他们能翻新之前，这座老房子就已经倒塌了。
His confidence had crumbled after repeated failures. = 在屡次失败后，他的信心崩溃了。


将来时:
The cliff will crumble into the sea eventually. = 这悬崖最终会崩塌到海里。
The regime will crumble under the pressure of the rebellion. = 政权将在叛乱的压力下崩溃。


虚拟语气:
If the foundation was weak, the building would have crumbled. = 如果地基薄弱，这座建筑就会倒塌。
I wish my hopes hadn't crumbled so easily. = 我真希望我的希望没有这么轻易地破灭。


被动语态:
The cake was crumbled into the filling. = 蛋糕被弄碎了，拌入了馅料中。
The empire was crumbled by internal strife. = 这个帝国被内部冲突瓦解了。


疑问句:
Did the building crumble? = 建筑物倒塌了吗？
Has the support for the government crumbled? = 对政府的支持瓦解了吗？


否定句:
The structure didn't crumble under the strain. = 结构在压力下没有倒塌。
The negotiations did not crumble despite the disagreements. = 尽管存在分歧，谈判并没有破裂。


PHRASE->
