overpatriotically: /ˌoʊvərˌpætəriˈɑːtɪkli/ | adv. | 过度爱国地，极端爱国地 | Excessively patriotic; to a degree exceeding normal or reasonable patriotism. |


例句：

He spoke overpatriotically, ignoring the flaws in his country's policies. = 他言辞过激地表达爱国主义，无视本国政策中的缺陷。

The protestors displayed overpatriotically fervent nationalism. = 抗议者表现出极端狂热的民族主义。


近义词：ultra-patriotically, excessively patriotically, jingoistically


反义词：unpatriotically, apathetically (towards one's country)


词性：副词


词源：

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a great degree. (来自古英语 *ofer,  表示“超过，在之上”)
* **patriotic**:  adj. relating to patriotism (来自法语 *patriotique, 来自拉丁语 *patrius,  表示“祖国的，本国的”)
* **-ally**: suffix forming adverbs from adjectives. (来自古法语 *-alment,  表示“方式，状态”)


因此，overpatriotically 的字面意思是“过度地爱国地”。
