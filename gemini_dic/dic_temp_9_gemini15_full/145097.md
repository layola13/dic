overappraised:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪzd/| adj.| 估价过高的；评价过高的 |  To have placed a higher value on something than it is actually worth, either in terms of monetary value or intrinsic worth. This can apply to assets, skills, or even people. | The antique chair was overappraised at auction, selling for far less than the initial estimate.  古董椅子在拍卖会上估价过高，售价远低于最初的估价。|近义词：overvalued, overrated, overpriced|反义词：undervalued, underrated
