catechisation:/ˌkætɪkɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |宗教教义问答；教理问答；教导|The act of instructing in the principles of a religion, especially by means of question and answer.  This often refers to religious education, particularly within the Christian tradition. |例句：The catechisation of children is an important part of their religious upbringing.  儿童的宗教教理问答是其宗教教养的重要组成部分。|例句：The priest conducted a thorough catechisation session, covering various aspects of the faith.  牧师进行了一次彻底的教理问答课程，涵盖了信仰的各个方面。|近义词：religious instruction, indoctrination, theological education |反义词：secularization, atheism
