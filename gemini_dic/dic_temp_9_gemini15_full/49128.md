deisms:/ˈdiːɪzəm/| n.|泛神论；自然神论|Belief in a God who does not intervene in the universe.|Deism is a belief system that emphasizes reason and observation over revelation and religious dogma.  Many Deists believe in a creator God, but they do not believe that God actively intervenes in human affairs or answers prayers.|近义词：natural religion, theism (in some contexts)|反义词：atheism, polytheism
