/moʊ/| n. |摩尔|A unit of measurement for the amount of a substance, equal to the amount of substance that contains as many elementary entities (atoms, molecules, ions, electrons, etc.) as there are atoms in 12 grams of carbon-12.|例句：One mole of water contains 6.02 x 10^23 molecules.|近义词：|反义词:|
