igneous:/ˈɪɡniəs/| adj.|火成的，喷发的|Formed by the solidification of molten rock (magma or lava).  Igneous rocks are one of the three main rock types, along with sedimentary and metamorphic rocks.|例句：Granite is a common type of igneous rock. 花岗岩是一种常见的火成岩。|例句：The volcanic eruption created a vast field of igneous rock. 火山爆发形成了广阔的火成岩地带。|近义词：volcanic, magmatic|反义词：sedimentary, metamorphic
