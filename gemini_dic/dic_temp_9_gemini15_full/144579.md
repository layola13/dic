outwatch:/ˈaʊtwɒtʃ/| n.|守夜，值夜班|The act of watching or guarding something outdoors, especially at night.|例句：The farmer did an outwatch to protect his crops from thieves.  农民守夜以保护庄稼免遭小偷。|近义词：vigil, watch, patrol|反义词：inwatch


USAGE->

They outwatch the borders regularly. = 他们定期巡逻边界。
