hemimorphic:/ˌhɛmiˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 半形的；半晶形的 |  Having only one kind of crystallographic polarity; possessing dissimilar ends.  A hemimorphic crystal has faces or poles that are not symmetrically arranged on both ends.  | Example: The crystal exhibited hemimorphic symmetry.  这个晶体表现出半晶形对称性。|近义词：polar | 反义词：holohedral
