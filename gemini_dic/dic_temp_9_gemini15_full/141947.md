ordovician:/ˌɔːrdəˈvɪʃən/| n. |奥陶纪 |The Ordovician Period is a geologic period and system that extends from 485.4 million to 443.8 million years ago. It is the second period of the Paleozoic Era.  |The Ordovician Period saw the diversification of many marine life forms. |近义词:Paleozoic Era |反义词:None
