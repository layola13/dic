oligarchs:/ˈɒlɪɡɑːks/|/ˈɑːləɡɑːrks/| n. |寡头政治统治者；寡头政治集团；掌握巨大权势的富豪|A small group of powerful people who control a country or organization.  They often gained their wealth through connections with the government and exploit their power for personal gain.  |例句：The country is ruled by a small group of oligarchs. 这个国家由一小撮寡头统治。

例句：Several oligarchs have been sanctioned for their involvement in the conflict.  一些寡头因为参与冲突而受到制裁。


近义词：tycoons, magnates, plutocrats

反义词：the common people, the masses


词源：From Ancient Greek ὀλιγαρχία (oligarchia), from ὀλίγος (oligos) "few" + ἄρχω (archo) "to rule".  The term literally means "rule by the few".
