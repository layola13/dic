insulin: /ˈɪnsəlɪn/ | n. |胰岛素|A hormone produced in the pancreas by the islets of Langerhans that regulates the amount of glucose in the blood.  The lack of insulin causes a form of diabetes. |例句：The doctor prescribed insulin injections to manage his diabetes.  医生开了胰岛素注射剂来控制他的糖尿病。|近义词：None (unique hormone)|反义词：None (no direct opposite)|名词
