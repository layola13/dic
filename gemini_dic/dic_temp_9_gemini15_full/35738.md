cochairperson:/ˈkoʊtʃɛrpɜːrsən/ | n. |共同主席；联合主席 | A person who shares the responsibilities and duties of a chairperson with another person.  They jointly lead a meeting, organization, or committee. | Example:  The conference will be co-chaired by Dr. Smith and Professor Jones.  这次会议将由史密斯博士和琼斯教授共同主持。|近义词：joint chair, co-president |反义词：sole chair, single chairperson
