fast-tracking:/ˈfæstˌtrækɪŋ/| n. v.|快速推进；优先处理|The process of accelerating the progress of something, typically a project or a person's career.|例句：The company is fast-tracking the development of its new product. 公司正在快速推进其新产品的开发。|例句：He was fast-tracked to a senior management position. 他被快速提升到了高级管理职位。|近义词：expediting, accelerating, prioritizing|反义词：delaying, postponing, hindering|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fast-track the most promising employees. = 他们快速提拔最有前途的员工。
We are fast-tracking the project to meet the deadline. = 我们正在加快项目进度以赶上截止日期。


一般过去时:
The government fast-tracked the legislation. = 政府快速通过了这项立法。
They fast-tracked him through the training program. = 他们让他快速完成了培训项目。


现在完成时:
They have fast-tracked several projects this year. = 今年他们已经快速推进好几个项目了。
The company has fast-tracked her career since she joined. = 自她加入公司以来，公司就一直在快速提升她的职业生涯。


过去完成时:
They had fast-tracked the research before the conference. = 他们在会议之前已经快速完成了研究。
She had fast-tracked her way to the top of the company. = 她已经快速地晋升到了公司的高层。


将来时:
We will fast-track the approval process. = 我们将加快审批流程。
They will fast-track the new employee into the team. = 他们将让新员工快速融入团队。


虚拟语气:
If we fast-tracked the process, we could finish earlier. = 如果我们加快流程，我们可以早点完成。
I wish they had fast-tracked my application. = 我希望他们能加快我的申请流程。


被动语态:
The project was fast-tracked to completion. = 该项目被快速推进直至完成。
He was fast-tracked into the leadership program. = 他被快速选拔进入领导力项目。


疑问句:
Will they fast-track the proposal? = 他们会快速推进这个提案吗？
Are they fast-tracking the new recruit? = 他们正在快速培养新员工吗？


否定句:
They did not fast-track the less important projects. = 他们没有快速推进那些不太重要的项目。
We cannot fast-track the process without compromising quality. = 我们不能在不影响质量的情况下加快流程。
