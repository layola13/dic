patrones:/pəˈtroʊniz/| n.|模式；榜样；保护人；主顾；赞助人|Patterns, models, or examples to be followed.  A person who gives support or protection to another. A customer or client of a business. |例句：The company's success is a pattern for others to follow. 这家公司的成功是其他公司效仿的榜样。 The artist found a patron who funded his work.这位艺术家找到了一位资助他作品的赞助人。|近义词：model, example, sponsor, client, customer|反义词：opponent, adversary
