saltpetre:/ˈsɔːltpiːtər/| n. |硝石|Saltpetre is a naturally occurring mineral composed of potassium nitrate (KNO₃). It has historically been used in gunpowder, fertilizers, and food preservation.  |Example:  Gunpowder is a mixture of charcoal, sulfur, and saltpetre. 火药是木炭、硫磺和硝石的混合物。|近义词：potassium nitrate, niter|反义词：无
