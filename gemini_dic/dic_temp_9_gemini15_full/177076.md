reapparel:/ˌriːəˈpærəl/| vt.|重新穿衣；更换服装|To clothe again; to dress anew.  This word is relatively rare and primarily found in literary contexts.|例句：After the accident, the paramedics reappareled the injured man in warm blankets.  在事故之后，医护人员用温暖的毯子重新给受伤男子穿衣。|例句：She reappareled herself in a more formal gown for the evening reception.  她换上了一件更正式的礼服参加晚上的招待会。|近义词：redress, reclothe, re-dress|反义词：undress, disrobe|动词
