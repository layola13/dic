childs:/tʃaɪldz/| n. |孩子的(复数)|The plural of "child", referring to more than one child.  It is the possessive form of "child", indicating belonging to a child. |例句：The childs' toys were scattered all over the floor.  孩子们 的玩具散落在地上。

例句：These are the childs' drawings. 这些是孩子的画。


近义词：children's (possessive plural)


反义词：adult's (possessive singular)


词性：名词（复数所有格）
