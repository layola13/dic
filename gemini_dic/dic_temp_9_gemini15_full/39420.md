connaturalize:/kənˈnætʃərəlaɪz/| vt.|使自然化；使合乎自然；使适应|To make something seem natural or inherent; to integrate or assimilate something into a natural or normal state.|例句：The artist connaturalized the fantastical elements of the story into a believable narrative. 艺术家将故事中虚幻的元素融入了一个可信的叙事中。

例句：The long residency helped her connaturalize to the local culture. 长期居住帮助她适应了当地文化。


近义词：naturalize, acclimatize, assimilate, integrate|反义词：alienate, estrange, separate


词性：动词
