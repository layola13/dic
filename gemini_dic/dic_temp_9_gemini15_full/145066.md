overanalyses: /ˌoʊvərəˈnælɪsiːz/ | n. |过度分析；过分解读| The act of analyzing something excessively or to an unnecessary degree, often leading to misinterpretations or unfounded conclusions.  It implies an excessive focus on details, potentially missing the bigger picture. |例句：Her overanalyses of his every word led to unnecessary conflict.  她的过度解读他说的每一句话导致了不必要的冲突。  His overanalyses of the data obscured the simple truth. 他对数据的过度分析掩盖了简单的真相。|近义词：overinterpretation, overthinking, excessive scrutiny|反义词：cursory examination, superficial analysis|名词
