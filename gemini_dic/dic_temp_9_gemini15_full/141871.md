orchidologist:/ˌɔːrkɪˈdɒlədʒɪst/| n. |兰科植物学家|A scientist who studies orchids.  Orchidologists are experts in the taxonomy, biology, ecology, and cultivation of orchids. |例句：The orchidologist identified the rare orchid species.这位兰科植物学家鉴定了这种稀有的兰花品种。 |例句：Many orchidologists contribute to conservation efforts for endangered orchid species. 许多兰科植物学家为濒危兰花物种的保护工作做出了贡献。|近义词：orchid expert, botanist (with specialization in orchids)|反义词：无
