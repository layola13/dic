fort:/fɔːrt/| n.|堡垒，要塞|A fort is a strong building or group of buildings used for defending a place against attack.  It is typically fortified with walls, towers, and other defensive structures.|The soldiers took refuge in the fort during the attack.  士兵们在袭击中躲进了堡垒。|stronghold, fortress|open area, undefended place
