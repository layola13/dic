cleare:/klɪər/| adj.|清晰的，明亮的，清澈的|Clear means free from clouds, mist, or obstructions; easily seen or heard; easily understood; free from doubt or confusion.  It can also describe liquids that are transparent and free from impurities. |例句：The sky is clear tonight. 今晚的天空很晴朗。
The explanation was clear and concise. 解释清晰简洁。|近义词：transparent, lucid, distinct, unambiguous|反义词：cloudy, murky, obscure, ambiguous


USAGE->

The water is clear. = 水很清澈。
The instructions are clear. = 指示很清晰。

一般过去时:
