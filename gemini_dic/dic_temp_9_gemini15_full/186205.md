rheumatology:/ˌruːməˈtɒlədʒi/|/ˌruːməˈtɑːlədʒi/| n. |风湿病学|The branch of medicine dealing with the diagnosis and treatment of rheumatic diseases.  It encompasses a wide range of conditions affecting joints, muscles, and connective tissues. |例句：She consulted a rheumatologist for her persistent joint pain. 她因为持续的关节疼痛去看了一位风湿病学家。|例句：Advances in rheumatology have led to improved treatments for arthritis. 风湿病学的进步导致了关节炎治疗的改善。|近义词：arthritis (as a specific disease),  joint disease|反义词：无
