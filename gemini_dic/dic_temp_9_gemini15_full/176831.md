readvise:/ˌriːˈædvɑɪz/ | vt. | 再建议，重新建议 | To advise again; to give advice again.  |例句：We readvised him to reconsider his decision. 我们再次建议他重新考虑他的决定。|近义词：reconsider, advise again, suggest again |反义词:  discourage, dissuade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They readvise their clients regularly. = 他们定期地再次建议他们的客户。
The company readvises its investment strategy annually. = 这家公司每年都会重新评估其投资策略。


一般过去时:
The manager readvised the team on the new project. = 经理再次就新项目向团队提出了建议。
She readvised him to seek professional help. = 她再次建议他寻求专业帮助。


现在完成时:
They have readvised the board on the matter several times. = 他们已经就此事多次再次向董事会提出建议。
The doctor has readvised her to take the medication. = 医生再次建议她服用药物。


过去完成时:

The lawyer had readvised his client on the legal implications. = 律师已经再次就法律影响向他的客户提出建议。
