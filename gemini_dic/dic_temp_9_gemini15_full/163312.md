preconfiguration:/ˌpriːkənˈfɪɡjəreɪʃən/| n. |预配置，预先配置 |The process of configuring something beforehand, typically a system or device, to a specific set of parameters or settings.  This saves time and effort later, as the settings are already in place.  |例句：The server's preconfiguration included setting up the necessary databases and user accounts. 服务器的预配置包括设置必要的数据库和用户帐户。

The network device arrived with its preconfiguration already complete. 网络设备到达时，其预配置已经完成。|近义词：setup, initial configuration, presetting |反义词：postconfiguration, manual configuration |名词
