/ˈwʌns/ | adv. | 一次；曾经 | Once, I lived in London. |  once more, again, previously | never, always | adv.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She eats ice cream once a week. = 她每周吃一次冰淇淋。
He visits his grandmother once a month. = 他每月去看望祖母一次。


一般过去时:
I saw her once at the party. = 我在派对上见过她一次。
They went to the beach once last summer. = 他们去年夏天去过一次海滩。


现在完成时:
I have been to Paris once. = 我去过巴黎一次。
She has seen that movie once before. = 她以前看过那部电影一次。


过去完成时:
I had met him once before the conference. = 在会议之前我见过他一次。
They had visited the museum once earlier that year. = 那一年早些时候他们去过博物馆一次。


将来时:
I will see you once next week. = 我下周会见你一次。
She will go to the doctor once next month. = 她下个月会去看一次医生。


虚拟语气:
If I had a million dollars, I would travel the world once. = 如果我有一百万美元，我会环游世界一次。
I wish I could visit Rome once. = 我希望我能去罗马一次。


被动语态:
The prize was awarded once a year. = 奖项每年颁发一次。
This event is held once every five years. = 此活动每五年举行一次。


疑问句:
Have you been to the cinema once this month? = 这个月你去过电影院一次吗？
Did you ever see him once? = 你曾经见过他一次吗？


否定句:
I have never been to that restaurant once. = 我从未去过那家餐厅一次。
She didn't go to school once that week. = 她那周一次都没去上学。


PHRASE->
at once = 立即
all at once = 突然
once in a while = 偶尔
once upon a time = 从前
once and for all = 一劳永逸
