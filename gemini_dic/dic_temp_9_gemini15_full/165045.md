preidentify:/ˌpriːaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| vt.|预先识别，预先鉴定|To identify something beforehand or in advance.  It implies identifying something before a more formal or complete identification process. |例句：The system will preidentify potential risks before they become major problems.  系统会在潜在风险演变成重大问题之前预先识别它们。

例句：The detective tried to preidentify the suspect from the blurry security footage.  侦探试图从模糊的监控录像中预先识别嫌疑人。


近义词：foresee, anticipate, pre-determine,  predict


反义词：misidentify, overlook, miss


词性: verb
