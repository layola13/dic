jager:/ˈjɑːɡər/| n. |猎手；(尤指)猎人；耶格麦芽酒| A jäger is a hunter, especially one who hunts game.  The word is also used as a brand name for a type of German liqueur. |例句：He was a skilled jäger, able to track his prey for miles. 他是一位熟练的猎手，能够追踪猎物数英里。  The bar served a wide selection of jägermeister. 酒吧供应各种耶格麦芽酒。|近义词：hunter, huntsman, sportsman |反义词：prey, game
