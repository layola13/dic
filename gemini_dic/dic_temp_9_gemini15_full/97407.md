infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/| v.|侵犯，违反|To encroach upon; to violate; to trespass.  It usually refers to breaking a law or rule, or interfering with someone's rights or property.|例句：They infringed copyright law by reproducing the image without permission. 他们未经许可复制图像，侵犯了版权法。|例句：His actions infringed on her privacy.他的行为侵犯了她的隐私。|近义词：violate, trespass, encroach, contravene|反义词：obey, respect, uphold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He infringes the law. = 他违反了法律。
They infringe on our rights. = 他们侵犯了我们的权利。

一般过去时:
She infringed the copyright. = 她侵犯了版权。
He infringed the rules of the game. = 他违反了比赛规则。

现在完成时:
They have infringed my patent. = 他们侵犯了我的专利。
We have infringed the agreement. = 我们违反了协议。

过去完成时:
He had infringed the contract before the lawsuit. = 在诉讼之前，他已经违反了合同。
They had infringed the rules many times. = 他们已经多次违反了规定。

将来时:
She will infringe the regulations if she continues. = 如果她继续下去，她将违反规章制度。
They will infringe upon our territory. = 他们将侵犯我们的领土。

虚拟语气:
If he infringed the law, he would be punished. = 如果他违反法律，他将受到惩罚。
I wish they hadn't infringed my privacy. = 我希望他们没有侵犯我的隐私。

被动语态:
The law was infringed. = 法律被侵犯了。
His rights were infringed upon. = 他的权利受到了侵犯。

疑问句:
Did they infringe the agreement? = 他们是否违反了协议？
Does this action infringe any laws? = 这一行为是否侵犯了任何法律？

否定句:
He did not infringe any rules. = 他没有违反任何规则。
We did not infringe on their property. = 我们没有侵犯他们的财产。
