pressure-cooked:/ˈprɛʃər kʊkt/ | adj. | 高压锅烹制的；快速烹制的 | Cooked under pressure in a pressure cooker.  This method significantly reduces cooking time while retaining nutrients and flavor. | 例句：The pressure-cooked chicken was incredibly tender. 高压锅烹制的鸡肉非常嫩。

例句：I prefer pressure-cooked beans for their faster cooking time. 我更喜欢高压锅烹制的豆子，因为它们烹饪时间更快。


近义词：speed-cooked, quickly cooked, rapidly cooked

反义词：slow-cooked, simmered
