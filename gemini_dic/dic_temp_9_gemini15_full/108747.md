learn:/lɜːrn/ /lɝːn/| vt. vi.|学习，学会，获悉|to acquire knowledge or skill in (a subject or activity) by study, experience, or being taught.|例句：I am learning English. 我正在学习英语。

例句：He learned to ride a bike when he was five. 他五岁的时候学会了骑自行车。


近义词：study, acquire, master|反义词：forget, unlearn|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I learn English every day. = 我每天学习英语。
She learns to play the piano. = 她学习弹钢琴。

一般过去时:
I learned a lot from that experience. = 我从那次经历中学到了很多。
They learned to swim last summer. = 他们去年夏天学会了游泳。

现在完成时:
I have learned a lot about history. = 我已经学到了很多关于历史的知识。
She has learned to speak French fluently. = 她已经学会了流利地说法语。

过去完成时:
I had learned to drive before I went to college. = 我上大学之前已经学会了开车。
They had learned the lesson before the test. = 考试前他们已经学到了教训。

将来时:
I will learn to code next year. = 我明年将学习编程。
She will learn Spanish in college. = 她将在大学学习西班牙语。

虚拟语气:
If I learned harder, I would pass the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能通过考试。
I wish I had learned more about that topic. = 我希望我曾经对那个话题了解更多。

被动语态:
The lesson was learned by all the students. = 所有学生都学到了这个教训。
New techniques are being learned by the employees. = 员工们正在学习新的技术。

疑问句:
What are you learning at school? = 你在学校学习什么？
Did you learn anything useful today? = 你今天学到什么有用的东西了吗？

否定句:
I didn't learn anything new today. = 我今天没有学到任何新东西。
She doesn't learn easily. = 她不容易学会东西。


PHRASE->
learn by heart = 记住，背诵
learn from = 向...学习
learn about = 了解
learn to = 学会
learn of = 听说
