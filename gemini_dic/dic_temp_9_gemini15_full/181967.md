remastered:/ˌriːˈmæstərd/ | adj. | 重制版；重新制作的 |  Describes something, typically audio or video, that has been improved or enhanced in quality through a process of re-mastering.  This often involves updating the sound or picture quality using modern technology. | Example:  The remastered album sounds incredible.  = 重制版专辑听起来棒极了。 The remastered film version is available on Blu-ray. = 重新制作的电影版本可以在蓝光光盘上观看。| 近义词：re-edited, enhanced, improved | 反义词：original, unedited, raw
