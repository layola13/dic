protogram:/ˈproʊtəˌɡræm/| n. |原型图，原型程序，原型 |A protogram is a preliminary or initial version of a program, design, or other work, serving as a model or template for later development.  It's often a simplified or incomplete representation of the final product, used to test concepts and functionalities. |Example: The team created a protogram to test the core mechanics of the game before embarking on full development.  原型团队在开始全面开发之前创建了一个原型程序来测试游戏的核心机制。|近义词：prototype, archetype, model, draft |反义词：final product, finished version
