grouped:/ɡruːpt/ | adj. | 分组的；成群的 |  Combined into a group or groups.  | Example: The students were grouped by ability.  学生们按能力分组。  The sheep were grouped together in the field.羊群在田野里聚集在一起。|近义词：clustered, assembled, collected | 反义词：scattered, dispersed, separated |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The items are grouped by color. = 这些物品按颜色分组。
The students are grouped into four classes. = 学生们被分成四个班级。


一般过去时:
The data was grouped according to age. = 数据按年龄分组。
The participants were grouped randomly. = 参与者被随机分组。


现在完成时:
The files have been grouped into folders. = 文件已被分组到文件夹中。
The results have been grouped by category. = 结果已按类别分组。


过去完成时:
The tasks had been grouped before the meeting. = 任务在会议前已分组。
The evidence had been grouped by the investigators. = 证据已被调查人员分组。


将来时:
The images will be grouped by theme. = 图片将按主题分组。
The participants will be grouped into teams. = 参与者将被分成小组。


虚拟语气:
If the data were grouped differently, the results would be different. = 如果数据分组方式不同，结果也会不同。
I wish the items were grouped more logically. = 我希望这些物品能更合乎逻辑地分组。


被动语态:
The students are grouped by their teachers. = 学生由老师分组。
The data is grouped using a specific algorithm. = 数据使用特定算法分组。


疑问句:
How are the students grouped? = 学生们是如何分组的？
Are the items grouped by size or weight? = 这些物品是按大小还是重量分组的？


否定句:
The tasks were not grouped effectively. = 任务分组效率不高。
The data is not grouped in a meaningful way. = 数据分组方式没有意义。
