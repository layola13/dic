skiagraphy:/ˈskaɪæɡrəfi/| n. |X光摄影术，X光照片|The process of producing images of internal body structures using X-rays; also refers to the resulting image.  This technique uses X-rays to create a shadow image of the internal structures of the body onto a photographic plate or digital sensor.|例句：The skiagraphy revealed a fracture in his leg.  X光摄影显示他腿部骨折。|例句：The doctor studied the skiagraphy carefully to diagnose the problem. 医生仔细研究X光照片以诊断问题。|近义词：radiography, X-ray|反义词:N/A
