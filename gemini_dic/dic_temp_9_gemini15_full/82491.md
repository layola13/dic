grunters:/ˈɡrʌntərz/| n. |咕哝的人；猪；(非正式)抱怨者|People or animals that grunt;  informally, complainers.  The word refers primarily to pigs because of their characteristic sound, but can also be used figuratively to describe people who grumble or complain. |例句：The grunters in the pen were jostling for position at the trough. 猪圈里的猪为了抢占食槽的位置而互相推搡。 The meeting was full of grunters complaining about the new policies. 会议上充满了抱怨新政策的人。|近义词：growlers, grumblers, complainers, whiners|反义词：praisers, complimenters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pigs grunt loudly. = 猪大声地哼哼叫。
He grunts in response to my question. = 他哼哼唧唧地回答我的问题。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
