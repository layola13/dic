It often implies a return to an organized state. |例句：After the busy lunch rush, the waiter spent an hour reracking the dishes.  午餐高峰期过后，服务员花了一个小时重新摆放盘子。


The process of reracking the bowling balls took longer than expected.  重新摆放保龄球的时间比预期的要长。
