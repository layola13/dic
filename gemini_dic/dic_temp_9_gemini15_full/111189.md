lithographs:/ˈlɪθəˌɡræfs/ | n. |平版印刷术；平版印刷品 |A lithograph is a picture printed from a stone or metal plate with a smooth surface.  The image is created by drawing or painting on the plate with a greasy substance, which then accepts ink.  |例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of lithographs by renowned artists.  这个博物馆拥有众多著名艺术家创作的令人惊叹的平版印刷品收藏。


The gallery exhibited several rare lithographs from the early 20th century. 画廊展出了20世纪早期的一些珍稀平版印刷品。|近义词：print, graphic |反义词：original painting (depending on context)
