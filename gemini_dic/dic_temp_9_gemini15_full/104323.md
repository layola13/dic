jutes:/dʒuːts/| n. |黄麻纤维；黄麻布|Jutes refers to the strong fibers obtained from the jute plant (Corchorus capsularis and Corchorus olitorius), primarily used to make burlap, hessian cloth, and other coarse fabrics.  It can also refer to the fabrics made from these fibers.|例句：The sacks were made of strong jutes.  麻袋是用结实的黄麻布做的。|例句：The rough texture of the jutes was ideal for the floor covering. 黄麻布粗糙的纹理非常适合用作地板覆盖物。|近义词：burlap, hessian|反义词：silk, linen (in terms of fineness)
