death: /dɛθ/ | n. |死亡；逝世；死亡人数 |The end of life; the state of being dead.  It can also refer to the process of dying or the cause of death. |例句：His death was a great loss to his family.  他的去世对他的家人来说是巨大的损失。
例句：The cause of death was determined to be heart failure. 死因被确定为心力衰竭。|近义词： demise, decease, expiration, passing |反义词： life, birth
