multispecies:/ˌmʌltiˈspiːʃiːz/| adj.|多物种的；多种物种的|Relating to or involving many different species.  This term is often used in ecology, biology, and conservation to describe systems or studies that consider multiple species interacting. |例句：A multispecies approach to conservation is crucial for maintaining biodiversity. 多物种保护方法对于维持生物多样性至关重要。

例句：The researchers conducted a multispecies study of the coral reef ecosystem. 研究人员对珊瑚礁生态系统进行了多物种研究。


近义词：polyspecific, multi-species (the hyphenated version is also common)


反义词：monotypic, single-species


词性：形容词
