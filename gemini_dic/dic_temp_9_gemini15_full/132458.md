nondepressing: /ˌnɑːn.dɪˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 不令人沮丧的，令人振奋的 | Not causing sadness or depression; uplifting.  The opposite of depressing. | 例句：The news was surprisingly nondepressing.  这个消息令人意外地振奋。


The positive atmosphere at work was nondepressing and encouraging. 工作中的积极氛围令人振奋且鼓舞人心。


近义词：uplifting, cheering, encouraging, inspiring, heartening, optimistic

反义词：depressing, disheartening, discouraging, dispiriting, gloomy, pessimistic

词性：形容词
