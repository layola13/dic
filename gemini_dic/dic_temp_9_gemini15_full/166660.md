preseparation: /ˌpriːˌsepəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |预分离；预先分离 | The act or process of separating something beforehand or in preparation for something else.  It often refers to a preparatory step in a larger process, like separating components before a chemical reaction or dividing tasks before a project. | Example:  The preseparation of the ingredients ensured a smoother baking process. 预先分离配料确保了烘焙过程更顺利。 The preseparation of data made the analysis much easier. 数据预先分离使得分析更容易。 | 近义词：preliminary separation, prior separation, advance separation | 反义词：postseparation, final separation | 词性：名词
