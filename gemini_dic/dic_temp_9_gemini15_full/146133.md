overemployed:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪd/| adj.|过度就业的；超负荷工作的|Having more employment than one can comfortably handle; working multiple jobs or excessive hours.|例句：She's overemployed, juggling two full-time jobs and a freelance gig. 她过度就业，同时兼顾两份全职工作和一份自由职业。|例句：He felt overemployed and stressed due to the demanding nature of his two jobs. 由于两份工作要求很高，他感到过度就业且压力很大。|近义词：overworked, overburdened, stressed|反义词：underemployed, unemployed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overemployed this semester. = 这个学期他过度就业了。
They are overemployed and struggling to balance their work and personal lives. = 他们过度就业，难以平衡工作和生活。


一般过去时:
She was overemployed last year, working three jobs simultaneously. = 去年她过度就业，同时做三份工作。
