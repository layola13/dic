imbank:/ɪmˈbæŋk/| vt.|筑堤；筑堤围绕；把…筑堤围起来|To enclose or protect with a bank or embankment; to form a bank or embankment around.|例句：They embanked the river to prevent flooding. 他们筑堤以防止洪水。|例句：The city was embanked to protect it from the sea.  这座城市被筑堤以保护它免受海水的侵袭。|近义词：embankment, dike, levee|反义词：demolish, destroy.
