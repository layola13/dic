inthrong:/ɪnˈθrɒŋ/| n. |人群，人群中|A large crowd of people.  The word suggests a dense, moving mass of people.|例句：The streets were thronged with people celebrating the victory. 街道上挤满了庆祝胜利的人群。
例句：She pushed her way through the throng to reach the front. 她挤过人群走到前面。|近义词：crowd, multitude, mass|反义词：solitude, emptiness|名词
