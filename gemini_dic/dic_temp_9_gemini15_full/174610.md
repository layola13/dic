quitting: /ˈkwɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 退出；放弃；停止；辞职 | The act of ceasing or stopping something; giving up; resigning from a job.  |

例句：

He's quitting his job to travel the world. = 他要辞职去环游世界。

She's quitting smoking after years of trying. = 她尝试多年后终于戒烟了。


近义词： stopping, ceasing, abandoning, resigning, leaving


反义词： continuing, persisting, starting, beginning


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is quitting his job next month. = 他下个月要辞职。
She is quitting smoking cold turkey. = 她正在强行戒烟。

一般过去时:
He quit his job last week. = 他上周辞职了。
She quit smoking a year ago. = 她一年前戒烟了。

现在完成时:
He has quit smoking three times. = 他戒烟三次了。
She has quit her job and started her own business. = 她辞职了，自己创业了。

过去完成时:
He had quit his job before he traveled. = 他旅行前已经辞职了。
She had quit smoking before she got pregnant. = 她怀孕前已经戒烟了。

将来时:
He will quit his job when he finds a new one. = 他找到新工作后就会辞职。
She will quit smoking next year. = 她明年将会戒烟。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would quit my job. = 如果我是他，我会辞职。
I wish I had quit smoking sooner. = 我真希望我早点戒烟。

被动语态: (Quitting itself is not usually used in passive voice.  The action of being made to quit could be expressed differently)
His contract was terminated; he was essentially made to quit. = 他的合同被终止了；他基本上是被迫辞职的。
The company forced him to quit. = 公司强迫他辞职。

疑问句:
Are you quitting your job? = 你要辞职吗？
Is she quitting smoking? = 她要戒烟吗？

否定句:
He isn't quitting his job. = 他不会辞职。
She isn't quitting smoking yet. = 她还没戒烟。


PHRASE->
quit smoking = 戒烟
quit drinking = 戒酒
quit your job = 辞职
quit complaining = 不要抱怨
quit messing around = 别胡闹
