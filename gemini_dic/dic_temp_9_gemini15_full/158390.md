pleased:/pliːzd/| adj.|高兴的；愉快的；满意的|Feeling or showing pleasure and satisfaction.|I am pleased to meet you. 我很高兴认识你。| delighted, gratified, satisfied| displeased, unhappy, dissatisfied|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pleased with his new job. 他对他的新工作很满意。
She is pleased to help others. 她乐于助人。


一般过去时:
I was pleased with the results of the exam. 我对考试的结果很满意。
They were pleased to see their friends. 他们很高兴见到他们的朋友。


现在完成时:
We have been pleased with the service. 我们一直对这项服务感到满意。
She has been pleased with her progress. 她对自己的进步感到满意。


过去完成时:
He had been pleased with his performance until he saw the final score. 他一直对他自己的表现很满意，直到他看到最终分数。
They had been pleased to hear the good news before the unfortunate accident. 在不幸事故发生之前，他们听到这个好消息感到很高兴。


将来时:
I will be pleased to see you again soon. 我很快就能再次见到你，我会很高兴的。
They will be pleased with the gift. 他们会喜欢这份礼物的。


虚拟语气:
I would be pleased if you could help me. 如果你能帮我，我会很高兴的。
She would have been pleased if she had known. 如果她知道的话，她会很高兴的。


被动语态:
The customer was pleased by the quick response. 客户对快速的回应感到满意。
He was pleased to be invited to the party. 他很高兴被邀请参加聚会。


疑问句:
Are you pleased with your work? 你对你的工作满意吗？
Is she pleased with the present? 她对这份礼物满意吗？


否定句:
I am not pleased with your behavior. 我对你的行为不满意。
He was not pleased to hear the news. 他听到这个消息并不高兴。


PHRASE->
be pleased to do sth. 乐意做某事
