interlocutors:/ɪnˈtɜːləkjuːtərz/ | n. |对话者，谈话者 |Participants in a conversation or dialogue.  They are the people who are talking to each other. |Example: The interlocutors agreed on a compromise.  对话双方达成了一致。
The two interlocutors engaged in a lively debate.  两位对话者进行了热烈的辩论。|近义词：conversationalists, speakers, participants |反义词：listeners (in this specific context)
