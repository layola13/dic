foal:/foʊl/ | n. | 小马驹，雏马 | A young horse, especially one less than one year old.  | 例句：The foal playfully kicked at its mother's legs.  小马驹顽皮地踢着母马的腿。

The farmer carefully watched over the newborn foal. 农民仔细照看着刚出生的小马驹。| 近义词：colt (雄性小马), filly (雌性小马) | 反义词：stallion (种马), mare (母马)
