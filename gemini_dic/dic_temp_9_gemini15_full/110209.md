life-saving: /ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/ | adj. | 救命的，挽救生命的 |  Able to save a person's life; preventing death.  | 例句：The doctor performed a life-saving operation.  医生进行了一次救命手术。

| 例句：She received life-saving treatment after the accident.  她在事故后接受了救命治疗。| 近义词：vital, crucial, essential | 反义词：lethal, deadly, fatal |  词性：形容词
