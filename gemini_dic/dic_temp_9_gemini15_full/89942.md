horselike:/ˈhɔːrslaɪk/| adj.|像马的；马似的|Resembling a horse in appearance or characteristics.  The adjective "horselike" describes something that possesses qualities similar to a horse, either in physical appearance or behavior.|The horselike creature galloped across the plains.  = 那匹像马一样的生物在平原上飞奔。 The sculpture had a horselike grace. = 雕塑有一种马一般的优雅。|equine, steedlike|humanlike, catlike|
