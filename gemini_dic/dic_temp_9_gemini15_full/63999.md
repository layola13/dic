epistle:/ɪˈpɪsəl/| n. |书信；长信；（尤指）正式信函 | A formal or official letter, especially a long one.  |He received an epistle from his estranged brother. 他收到他疏远的兄弟寄来的一封信。| letter, missive | informal note, email | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes an epistle every week. 他每周写一封信。
She sends epistles to her pen pals. 她给她的笔友寄信。


一般过去时:
He wrote an epistle to the king. 他给国王写了一封信。
She sent an epistle to her family. 她给她的家人寄了一封信。


现在完成时:
He has written many epistles in his life. 他一生写过许多信。
She has sent several epistles this month. 这个月她已经寄了好几封信了。


过去完成时:
He had written the epistle before the messenger arrived. 信使到达之前，他已经写好了信。
She had sent the epistle before the deadline. 她已在截止日期前寄出了信。


将来时:
He will write an epistle tomorrow. 他明天将写一封信。
She will send the epistle next week. 她下周将寄出这封信。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would write a long epistle. 如果我有时间，我会写一封长信。
I wish I had written an epistle to thank her. 我真希望我写过一封信来感谢她。


被动语态:
The epistle was written in elegant calligraphy. 这封信是用优雅的书法写的。
The epistle was sent by registered mail. 这封信是挂号邮件寄出的。


疑问句:
Have you received the epistle yet? 你收到信了吗？
Did he write the epistle himself? 这封信是他自己写的吗？


否定句:
He didn't write an epistle. 他没有写信。
She hasn't sent the epistle yet. 她还没寄信。
