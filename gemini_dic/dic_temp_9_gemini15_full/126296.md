myodynamics:/ˌmaɪoʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/| n.|肌动力学|The study of the mechanics of muscle movement and function.  It encompasses the forces generated by muscles, the movement of bones and joints, and the control of these movements by the nervous system.  This field integrates aspects of anatomy, physiology, biomechanics, and neuroscience. |例句：Research in myodynamics is crucial for understanding and treating musculoskeletal disorders. 肌动力学的研究对于理解和治疗肌肉骨骼疾病至关重要。


例句：Advanced knowledge of myodynamics is essential for physical therapists.  高级肌动力学知识对于物理治疗师来说至关重要。


近义词：muscle mechanics, biomechanics of muscle, motor control


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“肌肉无力”或“运动障碍”相关的词语。

词性:名词
