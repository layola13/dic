pondered:/ˈpɒndəd/ /ˈpɑːndərd/| v.|仔细考虑，沉思|To think deeply and carefully about something.|He pondered the question for a long time before answering. 他在回答之前仔细考虑了很久这个问题。|considered, contemplated, reflected|ignored, disregarded, overlooked|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ponders the meaning of life. = 他思考着生命的意义。
She ponders her next move in the game. = 她仔细思考着游戏中的下一步行动。


一般过去时:
I pondered the implications of her words. = 我仔细思考了她话语的含义。
They pondered the problem for hours. = 他们思考这个问题几个小时。


现在完成时:
She has pondered this decision for weeks. = 她已经考虑这个决定好几个星期了。
He has pondered the possibilities and chosen the best option. = 他权衡了各种可能性，并选择了最佳方案。


过去完成时:
I had pondered the situation for days before making a decision. = 我在做决定之前已经考虑了几天的情况。
They had pondered the offer before rejecting it. = 他们仔细考虑了这个提议之后才拒绝它。


将来时:
I will ponder your suggestion carefully. = 我会仔细考虑你的建议。
She will ponder the matter before responding. = 她会在回应之前仔细考虑这件事。


虚拟语气:
If I had pondered the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我当时仔细考虑了后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had pondered the risks before investing. = 我希望在投资之前我仔细考虑了风险。


被动语态:
The problem was pondered by the committee for many weeks. = 这个问题被委员会考虑了好几个星期。
The decision was carefully pondered before being announced. = 在宣布之前，这个决定被仔细考虑过了。


疑问句:
Have you pondered the alternatives? = 你考虑过其他的选择吗？
Did they ponder the risks involved? = 他们是否考虑了其中的风险？


否定句:
He did not ponder the consequences of his actions. = 他没有考虑自己行为的后果。
She didn't ponder the implications of the agreement. = 她没有考虑协议的含义。


PHRASE->
ponder over = 仔细考虑
ponder a question = 思考一个问题
ponder the possibilities = 权衡各种可能性
ponder the implications = 仔细思考含义
ponder the future = 思考未来
