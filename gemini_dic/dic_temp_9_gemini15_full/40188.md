contentment:/kənˈtɛntmənt/ | n. |满足感，知足 | a feeling of happiness and satisfaction.  It suggests a state of being pleased and at peace with one's situation. | Example:  She found contentment in her simple life. 她在她简单的生活中找到了满足感。  He expressed contentment with his current job. 他表达了他对目前工作的满意。|近义词：satisfaction, gratification, peace of mind |反义词：dissatisfaction, discontent, unhappiness | 名词
