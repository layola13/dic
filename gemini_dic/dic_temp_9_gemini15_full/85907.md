headiest:/ˈhedɪst/| adj.| 最浓烈的；令人陶醉的；最令人兴奋的；最鲁莽的 | Most intoxicating; strongest or most potent; most exciting or exhilarating; most reckless.  |例句：The headiest wine I've ever tasted. 我喝过的最烈的酒。

The headiest days of his career were spent traveling the world. 他职业生涯中最令人兴奋的日子是环游世界。


近义词：intoxicating, potent, exhilarating, heady, reckless | 反义词：mild, weak, sober, cautious

词源:  Derived from "head," referring to the intoxicating effect of alcohol or strong emotions, making one feel lightheaded or dizzy.  The -iest suffix denotes the superlative degree, indicating the highest or most intense form.  The original sense is connected to the feeling of being "heady" –  dizzy or lightheaded from strong drink or excitement.
