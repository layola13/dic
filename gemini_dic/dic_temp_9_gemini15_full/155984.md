photography:/fəˈtɒɡrəfi/ /fəˈtɑːɡrəfi/| n. |摄影术，摄影；照片|The art, process, or practice of creating lasting images by recording light.  Photography involves capturing images using a camera, either digitally or through film.  It encompasses various techniques, styles, and subjects.|例句：He has a passion for landscape photography. 他热爱风景摄影。

例句：The exhibition showcased stunning examples of wildlife photography.  展览展示了令人惊叹的野生动物摄影作品。


近义词：picture-taking, image-making, photo-taking

反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable to a process)

词性：名词
