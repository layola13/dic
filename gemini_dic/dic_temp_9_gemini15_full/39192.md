congenialness:/kənˈdʒiːniəlˌnɛs/ | n. |宜人；亲切；和谐 | The congenialness of the atmosphere made the meeting a success.  气氛的融洽使会议取得了成功。|近义词：compatibility, agreeableness, harmony |反义词：discord, incompatibility, hostility |名词

英文解析:The quality of being pleasant, agreeable, and friendly; a feeling of being comfortable and at ease.  It describes a situation or environment that is suitable and enjoyable.

例句:

The congenialness of the small town made it a perfect place to raise a family. 小镇的宜人氛围使其成为养育家庭的理想之地。

The team's success was partly due to the congenialness among its members. 团队的成功部分归功于成员之间的融洽关系。


词源:  Derived from the adjective "congenial," which comes from the Latin "con-" (together) and "genialis" (born with, relating to birth).  Originally, it meant "of the same nature or kind," but evolved to its current meaning of pleasant and agreeable.
