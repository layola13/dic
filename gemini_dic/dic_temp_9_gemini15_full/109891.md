liaison: /liˈeɪzɒn/ /liˈeɪzən/ | n. |联络，联系；联络员；调情|

英文解析:A liaison is a person who acts as a connection between people or groups; a close or romantic relationship, especially a secret one;  a connection or link between two things.

例句:

He acted as a liaison between the police and the community. 他充当了警方和社区之间的联络人。

The two departments maintain a close liaison. 这两个部门保持着密切的联系。


近义词: connection, link, contact, intermediary, intermediary, affair

反义词: separation, disconnection, isolation


词源: From Old French *liaison, from lier "to bind, tie".  The word's meaning evolved from the literal sense of "binding" or "linking" to the figurative sense of connection and then to the specific meaning of a romantic liaison.
