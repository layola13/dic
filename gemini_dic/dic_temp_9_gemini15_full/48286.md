decrease:/dɪˈkriːs/| v., n.|减少，降低；减少量 | To become or make less; a reduction in size, amount, number, or degree. |例句：The company's profits decreased last year.  公司去年的利润下降了。|例句：There has been a significant decrease in crime rates. 犯罪率显著下降了。|近义词：reduce, diminish, lessen |反义词：increase, augment, grow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature decreases in the evening. = 晚上温度下降。
Demand for the product decreases steadily. = 对该产品的需求持续下降。


一般过去时:
The population decreased significantly after the war. = 战后人口显著下降。
His weight decreased by ten pounds. = 他的体重减轻了十磅。


现在完成时:
The number of students has decreased this semester. = 本学期学生人数减少了。
The company's market share has decreased dramatically. = 公司的市场份额急剧下降。


过去完成时:
The value of the property had decreased before they sold it. = 他们在出售房产之前，房产的价值已经下降了。
The number of birds had decreased sharply before the conservation efforts began. = 在保护工作开始之前，鸟类的数量已经急剧下降。


将来时:
The price of oil will decrease next year. = 明年油价将会下降。
The demand for this product is expected to decrease in the coming months. = 预计未来几个月对该产品的需求将会下降。


虚拟语气:
If the government decreased taxes, the economy would improve. = 如果政府降低税收，经济就会改善。
I wish the noise would decrease. = 我希望噪音能小一些。


被动语态:
The budget was decreased by ten percent. = 预算减少了百分之十。
The volume has been decreased to a comfortable level. = 音量已降低到舒适的水平。


疑问句:
Did the number of accidents decrease last month? = 上个月事故数量减少了吗？
Has the cost of living decreased recently? = 最近生活成本下降了吗？


否定句:
The company's sales did not decrease last quarter. = 公司上季度的销售额没有下降。
The pollution levels have not decreased significantly. = 污染水平没有显著下降。


PHRASE->
decrease in =  在…方面减少
decrease by = 减少了…
a decrease of = 减少了…
a sharp decrease = 急剧下降
a gradual decrease = 逐渐下降
