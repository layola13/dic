obeyable: /oʊˈbeɪəbl/ | adj. | 可服从的，顺从的 |  Capable of being obeyed; submissive.  | The rules are clearly stated and obeyable. 这些规则表达清晰，易于遵守。 |  obedient, compliant, docile |  rebellious, defiant, disobedient | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog is obeyable. = 这条狗很听话。
The instructions are obeyable. = 这些指示是可以遵守的。

一般过去时:
The child was obeyable yesterday. = 孩子昨天很听话。
The soldiers were obeyable to their commander. = 士兵们服从他们的指挥官。

现在完成时:
The horse has always been obeyable. = 这匹马一直很温顺。
The students have been obeyable throughout the semester. = 学生们整个学期都很听话。

过去完成时:
The dog had been obeyable before the accident. = 事故发生前，这条狗一直很听话。
The employees had been obeyable until the new manager arrived. = 直到新经理到来之前，员工们都很听话。

将来时:
The puppy will be obeyable with proper training. = 小狗经过适当的训练后会很听话。
The new system will be obeyable by all staff. = 新系统将可被所有员工遵守。

虚拟语气:
If the rules were obeyable, there would be fewer problems. = 如果规则易于遵守，问题就会少一些。
I wish the instructions were more obeyable. = 我希望这些指示更容易遵守。

被动语态:
The law must be obeyed. (obeyable is not typically used in passive voice) = 法律必须遵守。(obeyable通常不用于被动语态)
The rules should be made more obeyable. = 规则应该更易于遵守。

疑问句:
Is the order obeyable? = 这个命令可以遵守吗？
Are the regulations obeyable by everyone? = 这些规章制度每个人都能遵守吗？

否定句:
The dog is not obeyable. = 这条狗不听话。
The instructions are not easily obeyable. = 这些指示不容易遵守。


PHRASE->
easily obeyable = 容易遵守的
highly obeyable = 非常听话的
generally obeyable = 通常易于服从的
relatively obeyable = 相对容易服从的
reasonably obeyable = 相当容易服从的
