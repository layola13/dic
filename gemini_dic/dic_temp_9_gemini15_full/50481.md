depressions:/dɪˈprɛʃənz/ | n. | 抑郁症；萧条；洼地；凹陷 | A state of low mood and aversion to activity that can affect a person's thoughts, behavior, feelings, and sense of well-being.  It can also refer to a period of economic decline or a low area in the ground. |例句：She suffered from severe depressions after the loss of her job. 她失业后患上了严重的抑郁症。

例句：The Great Depression of the 1930s had a devastating impact on the global economy. 20世纪30年代的大萧条对全球经济产生了毁灭性影响。|近义词：sadness, downturn, slump, recession, hollow |反义词：elation, happiness, boom, prosperity, elevation
