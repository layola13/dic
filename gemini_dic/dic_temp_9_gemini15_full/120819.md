misattribute: /ˌmɪsæˈtrɪbjuːt/ | vt. | 误归于；错归于 | To attribute something to the wrong cause, person, or source.  | 例句：He misattributed the quote to Shakespeare, when it was actually written by Marlowe. 他错误地将这句名言归于莎士比亚，而实际上它是马洛写的。

例句：The historian misattributed the painting to a lesser-known artist.  这位历史学家错误地将这幅画归于一位鲜为人知的艺术家。


近义词：misapply, misassign, wrongly attribute


反义词：correctly attribute, rightly ascribe


词性:动词
