outpracticed: /ˌaʊtˈpræktɪst/ | v. |  超越，技艺胜过 |  To surpass someone in skill or practice through superior training or experience.  |  例句：The young prodigy outpracticed all his competitors, securing the championship title.  年轻的神童通过更优异的训练和经验，技艺胜过所有竞争对手，获得了冠军头衔。


例句：She had been outpracticed by her opponent in the final match.  在决赛中，她被对手的熟练技艺超越了。


近义词：outskill, outmaneuver, surpass, exceed


反义词：be outdone by, be surpassed by


词性：动词
