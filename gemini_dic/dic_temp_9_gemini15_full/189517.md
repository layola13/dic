salama:/səˈlaːmə/| n.|平安；问候语；健康|A word of greeting or farewell, meaning "peace" or "health" in Swahili and other related languages. It's often used as a general greeting, similar to "hello" or "goodbye".|Example: Salama!  = Peace! / Hello!/Goodbye!|近义词：hello, goodbye, peace, health|反义词：war, illness


USAGE->

I wish you salaama. = 我祝你平安。
He sends his salaama to you. = 他向你问好。


一般过去时:
She said salaama before leaving. = 她离开前说了声平安。
They exchanged salaamas at the market. = 他们在市场上互相问候。
