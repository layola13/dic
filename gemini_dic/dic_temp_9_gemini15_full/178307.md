reconciles: /ˈrɛkənˌsaɪlz/ | vt. | 使和解；调和；使一致；使顺从 | To restore friendly relations between. To make (things that seem different) compatible. | 近义词：settle, resolve, harmonize | 反义词：estrange, alienate


英文解析：
The verb "reconcile" means to restore friendly relations between people or groups after a disagreement or conflict.  It can also mean to make things compatible or consistent that at first appear to be different or contradictory.  The word implies a process of bringing together opposing elements to create harmony or agreement.


例句：

After a long argument, they finally reconciled.
经过长时间的争吵，他们最终和解了。

The two accounts of the incident did not reconcile.
这两起事件的描述并不一致。
