hematologist:/ˌhiːməˈtɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |血液学家|A hematologist is a medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the blood, bone marrow, and lymphatic system.  |例句：My doctor referred me to a hematologist to investigate my low blood count. 我的医生把我转介给一位血液学家来调查我的低血球计数。  例句：The hematologist explained the treatment options for my leukemia. 血液学家解释了我白血病的治疗方案。|近义词：blood specialist, hemotologist |反义词:N/A
