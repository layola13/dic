resistless:/rɪˈzɪstləs/| adj.|无法抗拒的，不可抵挡的|incapable of being resisted; overwhelming; irresistible.  The temptation was resistless.|例句：The temptation to eat the entire cake was resistless.  吃掉整个蛋糕的诱惑是无法抗拒的。


例句：The beauty of the sunset was resistless. 日落的美丽是无法抗拒的。


近义词：irresistible, compelling, overpowering, overwhelming|反义词：resistible, controllable, avoidable
