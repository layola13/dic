disproportion's:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənz/ | n. |不成比例，不相称 |The disproportionate allocation of resources led to widespread inequality. 资源分配不均导致了普遍的不平等。 |近义词： imbalance, disparity |反义词：proportion, balance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The distribution of wealth shows a disproportion.  财富分配显示出一种不成比例。
This area suffers from a disproportion of resources. 这个地区资源分配不均。


一般过去时:
There was a disproportion in the number of men and women attending the conference. 参加会议的男女比例失调。
A disproportion of funding was given to the less successful projects.  资金分配不均，给了那些不太成功的项目。


现在完成时:
There has been a disproportion in the representation of different ethnic groups in the media.  媒体对不同种族群体的代表性一直存在比例失调。
A disproportion of attention has been paid to minor issues.  过多的关注被给予了次要问题。


过去完成时:
Before the reforms, there had been a significant disproportion in income levels. 改革前，收入水平存在严重的不均衡。
By the end of the war, a disproportion of the population had been displaced.  到战争结束时，人口比例严重失调。


将来时:
There will be a disproportion in the age demographics in the coming years.  未来几年，人口年龄结构将出现比例失调。
Further investment will correct the disproportion of resources between the two departments. 进一步的投资将纠正两个部门之间资源分配的不均衡。


虚拟语气:
If there were no disproportion in access to education, more people would succeed. 如果教育机会均等，更多的人会成功。
I wish there were no disproportion in income between the rich and poor.  我希望贫富之间的收入差距不存在。


被动语态:
The disproportion in the budget was highlighted by the auditor.  审计员强调了预算中的比例失调。
The disproportion in the sample was noted in the research report. 研究报告中指出了样本中的比例失调。


疑问句:
Is there a disproportion in the representation of women in politics?  女性在政治中的代表性是否失调？
What accounts for the disproportion in the test scores?  考试成绩的差异是什么原因造成的？


否定句:
There is no disproportion in the size of the two groups.  这两个群体的大小没有差异。
The study found no disproportion in the results between the two experimental groups.  研究发现两组实验结果之间没有差异。
