reforested:/ˌriːˈfɒrɪstɪd/| adj. |重新造林的；再造林的|Having been replanted with trees after deforestation.  The reforested area is now home to a variety of wildlife.|近义词：replanted, afforested|反义词：deforested


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The area is reforested every year. = 这个地区每年都进行重新造林。
This region is reforested with native species. = 这个区域正用本地物种进行重新造林。


一般过去时:
They reforested the mountainside last year. = 他们去年对山坡进行了重新造林。
The government reforested millions of acres. = 政府重新造林数百万英亩土地。


现在完成时:
The community has reforested a significant portion of the land. = 社区已经对很大一部分土地进行了重新造林。
We have reforested the damaged areas successfully. = 我们已经成功地对受损区域进行了重新造林。


过去完成时:
They had reforested the area before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经对该地区进行了重新造林。
The company had reforested the land before selling it. = 那家公司在出售土地之前已经对其进行了重新造林。


将来时:
They will reforest the cleared land next spring. = 他们将在明年春天对清理后的土地进行重新造林。
The project will reforest a large expanse of desert. = 该项目将对大片沙漠进行重新造林。


虚拟语气:
If we had reforested earlier, the erosion would have been less severe. = 如果我们早些进行重新造林，水土流失就不会那么严重。
It's important that the area be reforested as soon as possible. = 尽快对该地区进行重新造林非常重要。


被动语态:
The land was reforested by the local volunteers. = 这片土地由当地志愿者重新造林。
The hills were reforested after the devastating fire. = 毁灭性火灾之后，山丘被重新造林。


疑问句:
Has the damaged forest been reforested yet? = 受损的森林已经重新造林了吗？
Will the government reforest the area this year? = 政府今年会对该地区进行重新造林吗？


否定句:
The area wasn't reforested properly. = 该地区没有进行适当的重新造林。
They did not reforest the land after mining operations. = 采矿作业后，他们没有对土地进行重新造林。
