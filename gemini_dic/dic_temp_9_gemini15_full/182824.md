repeated:/rɪˈpiːtɪd/| adj. |重复的，反复的|Repeated actions can lead to muscle memory.  重复的动作可以形成肌肉记忆。 The repeated delays frustrated everyone. 反复的延误让每个人都感到沮丧。|近义词：recurring, reiterated, duplicated|反义词：unique, original


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine repeats the process automatically. = 机器自动重复这个过程。
He repeats the same mistake every time. = 他每次都犯同样的错误。


一般过去时:
She repeated the question for clarity. = 她为了清楚起见重复了这个问题。
They repeated the experiment with different variables. = 他们用不同的变量重复了实验。


现在完成时:
He has repeated his success in the competition. = 他在比赛中再次取得成功。
She has repeated the same phrase many times. = 她已经重复说了很多遍同样的话。


过去完成时:
He had repeated the instructions several times before they understood. = 他在他们理解之前已经重复了几次说明。
They had repeated the experiment numerous times without success. = 他们已经无数次重复实验，但没有成功。


将来时:
She will repeat the performance next week. = 她下周将再次演出。
He will repeat the exercise until he gets it right. = 他会重复练习直到做对为止。


虚拟语气:
If I repeated the mistake, I would be punished. = 如果我再犯同样的错误，我会受到惩罚。
I wish I hadn't repeated myself so much. = 我真希望我没有那样重复自己。


被动语态:
The experiment was repeated by another team. = 实验被另一个团队重复进行了。
The instructions were repeated clearly for everyone to understand. = 说明被清楚地重复了一遍，以便每个人都能理解。


疑问句:
Did you repeat what I said? = 你重复我说的话了吗？
Will you repeat that again, please? = 请你再说一遍好吗？


否定句:
He did not repeat the error. = 他没有重复那个错误。
She refused to repeat the lie. = 她拒绝重复那个谎话。


PHRASE->
repeated attempts = 反复尝试

repeated exposure = 反复接触
repeatedly stated = 反复说明
