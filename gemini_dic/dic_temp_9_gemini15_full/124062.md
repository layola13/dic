morphologies:/mɔrˈfɑːlədʒiz/| n. |形态学；形态；词形变化|The study of the forms and structures of things.  It can refer to the forms of words (in linguistics), the forms of organisms (in biology), or the forms of geological features (in geology).|例句：The morphologies of the two species are remarkably similar. 这两个物种的形态非常相似。

例句：Linguistic morphology studies the internal structure of words. 语言形态学研究词的内部结构。


近义词：forms, structures, shapes|反义词：无
