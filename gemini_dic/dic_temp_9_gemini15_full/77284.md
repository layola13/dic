gate:/ɡeɪt/| n. v.|大门；关卡；门；途径|A gate is a structure, typically made of wood, metal, or other materials, used to close off an opening in a fence, wall, or other barrier. It can also refer to a point of entry or exit, or a stage in a process.|例句：He opened the gate and let the dog out. 他打开大门，让狗出去。The airport gate is number 23. 机场登机口是23号。|近义词：entrance, doorway, opening|反义词：barrier, obstruction
