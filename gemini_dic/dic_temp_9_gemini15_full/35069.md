closeting: /ˈklɒzɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 私下隐瞒（性取向等） | The act of keeping one's homosexuality or other sexual orientation secret, often from family, friends, or the public.  It involves presenting a false public persona to avoid social stigma or discrimination. |  He lived a life of closeting, fearing rejection from his conservative family. 他过着隐瞒性取向的生活，害怕被保守的家人拒绝。


  Many LGBTQ+ individuals engage in closeting due to societal pressures. 许多LGBTQ+人士因为社会压力而选择隐瞒。


近义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing, masking | 反义词：outing, revealing, disclosing
