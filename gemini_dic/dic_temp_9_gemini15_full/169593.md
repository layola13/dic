prophets:/ˈprɒfɪts/| n. |预言家，先知 | A prophet is a person regarded as divinely inspired to speak or interpret the will of God.  They often foretell future events. |例句：Many ancient cultures revered their prophets and sought their guidance.  许多古代文化都尊敬他们的先知并寻求他们的指导。

例句：The prophet Isaiah foretold the coming of the Messiah. 先知以赛亚预言了弥赛亚的到来。


近义词：seer, visionary, oracle, foreteller |反义词：denier, skeptic
