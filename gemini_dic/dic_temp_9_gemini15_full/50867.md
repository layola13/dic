desensationalizing: /ˌdiːˌsɛnˈseɪʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |淡化耸人听闻的报道；去除煽动性；使不那么轰动 | To remove the sensational or exciting aspects of something; to make something less sensational. |  The news report was desensationalized to avoid causing undue panic. 新闻报道经过淡化处理，避免引起不必要的恐慌。|近义词：de-emphasizing, downplaying, toning down |反义词：sensationalizing, exaggerating, hyping up
