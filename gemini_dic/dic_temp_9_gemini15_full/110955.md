liquate:/ˈlɪkweɪt/| vt.| 使熔化；使液化 | To make (something solid) liquid; to melt.  |例句：Heat the butter until it liquates. 把黄油加热直到它熔化。|例句：The high temperature liquated the metal. 高温使金属熔化。|近义词：melt, fuse, liquefy |反义词：solidify, freeze, harden
