prankish:/ˈpræŋkɪʃ/| adj.|恶作剧的，顽皮的|Inclined to play pranks; mischievous.  Characterized by or given to pranks.|例句：His prankish behavior got him into trouble.  他的恶作剧行为让他惹上了麻烦。

例句：The prankish children filled the teacher's desk with balloons. 顽皮的孩子们用气球填满了老师的桌子。


近义词：mischievous, playful, playful, roguish, impish


反义词：serious, well-behaved, docile
