cornstalk:/ˈkɔːrnstɔːk/| n. |玉米秸秆；玉米杆|The stalk of a maize plant, consisting of the stem and leaves.  It is often used as animal feed or for other purposes after the corn has been harvested. |例句：The farmer used the cornstalks to mulch his garden.  农民用玉米秸秆覆盖他的花园。|例句：After harvesting, the cornstalks were left in the field to decompose. 收获后，玉米秸秆留在田里腐烂。|近义词：maize stalk, corn stem |反义词：None
