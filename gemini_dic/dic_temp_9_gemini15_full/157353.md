pipemouth:/ˈpaɪpmaʊθ/| n. |管嘴鸟；吸管嘴|A pipemouth is a type of bird characterized by its long, slender bill, often used to probe for insects in crevices or under bark.  These birds are generally small and inconspicuous.|Example: The tiny pipemouth flitted among the branches, its long bill searching for insects.  管嘴鸟在树枝间飞舞，它长长的喙寻找着昆虫。|近义词：暂无直接近义词，可根据语境用其他描述鸟类的词语代替|反义词：暂无直接反义词
