overculls: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌlz/ | v. |过度猎杀，过度捕杀 | To kill or remove (animals, especially game animals) in excessive numbers, often beyond what is sustainable.  This often leads to population decline or even extinction.  | Example:  The overculling of deer led to an imbalance in the ecosystem.  过度捕杀鹿导致生态系统失衡。  The government's policy of overculling endangered species is highly controversial. 政府过度捕杀濒危物种的政策极具争议。 |近义词：overhunt, overharvest, decimate | 反义词：conservation, protection
