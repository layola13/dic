deliber:/dɪˈlɪbər/| v. adj.|仔细考虑；深思熟虑；故意；蓄意|To think about or discuss something carefully, especially before making a decision.  It can also mean to do something intentionally.|例句：She deliberated for a long time before making her decision. 她做了长时间的深思熟虑才做出决定。
The jury deliberated for several hours before reaching a verdict. 陪审团在达成判决前商议了数小时。|近义词：consider, ponder, contemplate, meditate|反义词：impulsive, hasty, rash|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deliberates before speaking. 他说话前会仔细考虑。
They deliberate on the matter every week. 他们每周都会仔细讨论这件事。


一般过去时:
She deliberated carefully before signing the contract.  她在签合同之前仔细斟酌。
The committee deliberated for hours, but couldn't reach a decision. 委员会商讨了数小时，但仍无法达成一致。


现在完成时:
We have deliberated the proposal thoroughly. 我们已经彻底讨论了这个提案。
They have deliberated on this issue many times. 他们已经多次讨论过这个问题。


过去完成时:
He had deliberated for days before making his proposal. 他在提出建议之前已经考虑了几天。
They had deliberated the options before choosing one. 他们在选择一个方案之前已经仔细考虑了其他方案。


将来时:
The council will deliberate on the new budget next week. 理事会下周将讨论新的预算。
She will deliberate with her team before answering. 她会和团队商议后再回答。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would deliberate further. 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步考虑。
I wish I had deliberated more carefully. 我希望我当时考虑得更仔细一些。


被动语态:
The matter was deliberated extensively by the board.  董事会对这件事进行了广泛的讨论。
The proposal will be deliberated at the next meeting.  该提案将在下次会议上进行讨论。


疑问句:
Did they deliberate before acting? 他们在行动之前是否经过深思熟虑？
Will the court deliberate on the case tomorrow? 法院明天会审理此案吗？


否定句:
He didn't deliberate his actions. 他没有深思熟虑自己的行为。
They did not deliberate the consequences. 他们没有考虑后果。
