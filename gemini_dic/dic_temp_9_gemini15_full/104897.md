keeps:/kiːps/ | v. | 保持；继续；保存；经营 | To continue to have or possess something.  |  She keeps a diary. 她写日记。(持续拥有并使用日记)  He keeps his promises. 他信守诺言。(持续履行承诺) | continues, maintains, preserves | loses, abandons, relinquishes | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He keeps his room clean. = 他保持他的房间干净。
She keeps a pet dog. = 她养了一只宠物狗。


一般过去时:
The rain kept falling all day. = 雨整整下了一天。
They kept working until late. = 他们一直工作到很晚。


现在完成时:
She has kept the secret for years. = 她保守这个秘密多年了。
He has kept fit by exercising regularly. = 他通过定期锻炼保持健康。


过去完成时:
I had kept the money in a safe place. = 我把钱放在安全的地方了。
They had kept quiet about the incident. = 他们对这件事一直保持沉默。


将来时:
He will keep trying until he succeeds. = 他会一直尝试直到成功。
We will keep you informed. = 我们会随时通知你。


虚拟语气:
If he kept his promise, she would be happy. = 如果他信守诺言，她会很高兴。
I wish he kept his word. = 我希望他遵守诺言。


被动语态:
The secret was kept by all involved. = 这个秘密被所有相关人员保守。
The old building is kept in good condition. = 这座旧建筑保养得很好。


疑问句:
Does he keep a journal? = 他写日记吗？
What does she keep in her bag? = 她的包里装着什么？


否定句:
He doesn't keep late hours. = 他不熬夜。
She didn't keep her appointment. = 她没有遵守约会。



PHRASE->
keep in touch = 保持联系
keep up with = 跟上
keep on = 继续
keep away from = 远离
keep an eye on = 注意；照看

词源:
源自古英语 *cēpan，意思是“to hold, keep, protect”。  与keep相关的词在日耳曼语系中广泛存在，体现了其古老的词源。
