christophany:/krɪˈstɒfəni/ | n. |基督显现 |The manifestation of Christ, especially in his miracles or his divine glory.  A christophany is a theological term referring to an appearance or manifestation of Christ, often in a miraculous or glorious way. It can encompass various events in the Bible and Christian tradition where Christ's presence is revealed.  | Example: The Gospel accounts describe several christophanies, such as the transfiguration on the mountain.  基督教福音书中描述了几次基督显现，例如在山上显圣。|近义词：theophany, epiphany |反义词：absence, concealment |名词
