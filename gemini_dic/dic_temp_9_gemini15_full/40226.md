contextualized:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪzd/ | adj. |语境化的；情境化的 |  Having been placed within a context; understood or interpreted in relation to its context.  This adjective describes something that has been considered and understood within its specific environment, circumstances, or background. It emphasizes the importance of the surrounding information in comprehending the meaning or significance of something. | Example: The study provided a contextualized analysis of the poem, considering its historical and social background.  这项研究对这首诗进行了语境化的分析，考虑到了它的历史和社会背景。 |近义词：situated, relevant, pertinent |反义词：decontextualized, isolated, abstract
