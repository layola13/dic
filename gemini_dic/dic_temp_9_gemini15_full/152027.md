patchoulis:/pætʃəˈluːs/ | n. |广藿香，坡里香 |A strong-smelling, dark brown essential oil obtained from the leaves of the patchouli plant, used in perfumes and incense.  广藿香是一种从广藿香植物的叶子中提取的强烈气味的深棕色精油，用于香水和熏香。|The perfume contained a base note of patchouli. 这款香水含有广藿香基调。|近义词：pogostemon |反义词：无
