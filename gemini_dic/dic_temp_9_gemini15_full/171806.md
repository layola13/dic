punctuation:/ˌpʌŋktʃuˈeɪʃən/ | n. |标点符号|Punctuation refers to the marks used in writing or printing to separate sentences and clauses and to clarify meaning.  It includes commas, periods, question marks, exclamation points, colons, semicolons, and more.  Proper punctuation is essential for clear communication.|Example: The correct punctuation in this sentence ensures clarity.  这个句子的正确标点确保了清晰度。|近义词：marks, symbols |反义词：None
