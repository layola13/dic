over-fluent: /ˌoʊvərˈfluːənt/ | adj. | 过于流利的；过于流畅的 | excessively fluent; characterized by an excessive fluency that may seem unnatural or insincere.  |  His speech was over-fluent, almost glib; it lacked substance. 他的演讲过于流利，几乎油嘴滑舌，缺乏实质内容。  |近义词： glib, voluble, articulate (but excessively so) | 反义词： hesitant, halting, stammering, inarticulate
