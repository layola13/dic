deprivals:/dɪˈpraɪvəlz/| n.|剥夺；丧失；匮乏|The state of being deprived of something essential or desirable.  It often refers to the lack of necessities like food, water, or freedom.  The plural form emphasizes multiple instances of deprivation or a collection of deprivations.|例句：The prisoners suffered terrible deprivals during their imprisonment. 囚犯们在狱中遭受了可怕的剥夺。

The long drought led to widespread deprivals of water and food.  长时间的干旱导致了广泛的缺水和食物匮乏。|近义词：loss, lack, want, absence|反义词：abundance, plenty, fulfillment|名词
