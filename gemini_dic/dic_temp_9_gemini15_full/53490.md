disarticulator:/ˌdɪsɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪtər/| n. |脱臼器，关节分离器|A device used to separate or disarticulate bones or joints, often used in anatomical studies or surgical procedures.  It can also refer to something that causes disarticulation.|例句：The anthropologist used a disarticulator to carefully separate the bones of the ancient skeleton. 人类学家使用脱臼器仔细地分离古代骨骼。

例句：The surgeon employed a specialized disarticulator to facilitate the hip replacement surgery. 外科医生使用专门的脱臼器来促进髋关节置换手术。|近义词：disarticulating instrument, joint separator|反义词：articulator, joiner|
