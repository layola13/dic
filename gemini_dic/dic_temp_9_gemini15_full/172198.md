pus:/pʌs/| n.|脓，腐液|A thick yellowish or greenish fluid consisting of dead white blood cells and bacteria, formed at the site of an infection.|The wound was filled with pus.|近义词：matter, suppuration|反义词:clean, healthy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The wound produces pus. = 伤口产生脓液。
Pus contains dead white blood cells. = 脓液包含死亡的白细胞。

一般过去时:
The infection produced a lot of pus. = 感染产生了大量的脓液。
The doctor drained the pus from the abscess. = 医生排出了脓肿里的脓液。

现在完成时:
The infection has produced a significant amount of pus. = 感染已经产生了大量的脓液。
He has had pus in his wound for days. = 他的伤口几天都有脓液了。

过去完成时:
The pus had already formed before he saw the doctor. = 在他去看医生之前脓液已经形成了。
By the time he arrived, the wound had filled with pus. = 当他到达的时候，伤口已经充满了脓液。


将来时:
The doctor expects the wound to produce more pus. = 医生预计伤口会产生更多的脓液。
They predict the infection will result in pus formation. = 他们预测感染会导致脓液形成。


虚拟语气:
If the wound were infected, it would produce pus. = 如果伤口感染了，它就会产生脓液。
I wish the pus would drain more quickly. = 我希望脓液能更快地排出。


被动语态:
The pus was drained from the wound. = 脓液从伤口排出。
The abscess was treated to remove the pus. = 脓肿经过治疗以清除脓液。


疑问句:
Is there pus in the wound? = 伤口里有脓液吗？
Does the infection usually produce pus? = 感染通常会产生脓液吗？


否定句:
The wound doesn't contain pus. = 伤口不含脓液。
There was no pus in the incision. = 切口里没有脓液。
