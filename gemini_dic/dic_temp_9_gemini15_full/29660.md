catgut:/ˈkætɡʌt/| n. |琴弦；猫肠线|A strong cord made from the intestines of sheep, goats, or other animals, formerly used for strings of musical instruments and surgical sutures.  Now often made synthetically.|例句：The old violin had catgut strings. 这把旧小提琴用的是猫肠线琴弦。

例句：The surgeon used catgut to stitch the wound. 外科医生用猫肠线缝合伤口。

近义词：gut string, surgical suture


反义词：无


词性：名词
