landfalls:/ˈlændˌfɔːlz/| n. |陆地；登陆点；首次看见陆地|The sighting of land after a sea voyage; the act of making land after a sea voyage.|例句：The ship made landfall at dawn.  船在黎明时分登陆。

例句：After weeks at sea, the landfall was a welcome sight.  在海上航行了数周后，看到陆地令人欣慰。

近义词：arrival, touchdown, reaching shore

反义词：departure, setting sail
