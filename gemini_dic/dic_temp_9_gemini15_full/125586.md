multitudes:/ˈmʌltɪtʃuːdz/| n.|众多；大量；群众|A large number of people or things.  It often implies a vast or overwhelming quantity.|例句：Multitudes of people gathered to witness the parade.  数不清的人聚集在一起观看游行。

例句：The multitudes were captivated by the speaker's charisma.  听众们被演讲者的魅力所吸引。


近义词：crowds, masses, throngs, hordes, swarms

反义词：few, handful, minority


词源:  From Middle English *multitude, from Old French multitude, from Latin multitūdō, from multus "many" + -tūdō "-ness, state of being".
