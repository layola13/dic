goos:/ɡuːs/| n.|鹅|A goose is a large water bird with a long neck, short legs, and webbed feet.  Geese are known for their honking and their ability to fly in formation.|例句：We saw a flock of geese flying south for the winter. 我们看到一群鹅向南飞去过冬。|例句：The farmer raises geese for their meat and eggs. 这位农民饲养鹅是为了获取它们的肉和蛋。|近义词：gander (雄鹅)|反义词：无
