outstreet:/ˈaʊtstrit/| n.|偏僻的街道，偏远地区|A less-traveled or less-developed street, often located outside the main commercial or residential areas of a town or city.  It can also refer to a remote area or the outskirts.|例句：They lived on an outstreet, far from the city center. =他们住在远离市中心的偏僻街道上。|例句：The outstreet was dimly lit and deserted. =这条偏僻的街道灯光昏暗，而且空无一人。|近义词：backstreet, side street, byway, lane,  outskirts|反义词：main street, downtown, central area
