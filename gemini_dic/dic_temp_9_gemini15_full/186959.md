rips:/rɪps/| n. v.|撕裂；裂口；猛烈抨击| To tear something; a tear or opening; a strong criticism.|例句：The strong wind ripped the tent. 大风撕裂了帐篷。|例句：There's a rip in my jeans. 我的牛仔裤破了一个洞。|近义词：tear, split, lacerate, criticize |反义词：mend, repair, sew


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rips the paper. = 他撕碎了纸。
The wind rips the sails. = 风撕裂了帆。

一般过去时:
She ripped her dress on a nail. = 她的裙子被钉子划破了。
The car ripped through the fence. = 车子冲破了栅栏。

现在完成时:
The dog has ripped my book. = 狗撕坏了我的书。
The storm has ripped the roof off the house. = 暴风雨把房顶掀掉了。

过去完成时:
He had ripped the letter before I saw it. = 我看到之前他已经撕碎了信。
The thief had ripped open the safe. = 小偷已经撬开了保险箱。

将来时:
The strong wind will rip the tree down. = 大风会把树刮倒。
I think the fabric will rip easily. = 我认为这布料很容易撕破。

虚拟语气:
If I pulled harder, the fabric would rip. = 如果我再用力拉，布料就会撕破。
I wish I hadn't ripped my favorite shirt. = 我真希望我没有撕坏我最喜欢的衬衫。

被动语态:
The clothes were ripped during the wash. = 衣服在清洗过程中被撕破了。
The painting was ripped from the wall. = 画被从墙上撕了下来。

疑问句:
Did the wind rip the roof? = 风把屋顶掀了吗？
Has anyone ripped the pages of this book? = 有人撕了这本书的页码吗？

否定句:
He didn't rip the shirt intentionally. = 他不是故意撕坏衬衫的。
The fabric didn't rip easily. = 这布料不容易撕破。


PHRASE->
rip off: 敲竹杠；欺骗

rip through: 迅速穿过；横冲直撞
rip something up: 撕碎；撕毁
rip into someone: 严厉批评某人
