deanery:/ˈdiːnəri/| n.|教长管区；教长住所|The area of responsibility of a dean, typically a group of parishes under his/her jurisdiction. It can also refer to the official residence of a dean.|例句：The deanery includes several churches in the surrounding villages. 教长管区包括周边村庄的几座教堂。|例句：The new dean moved into the deanery last week. 新任教长上周搬进了教长住所。|近义词：diocese (larger area), rectory (priest's residence)|反义词：N/A|名词

词源:
From Old French *deainerie, from *deain (dean) + -erie (suffix indicating place or office).  Ultimately derived from Latin decanus ("leader of ten"). The term originally referred to the jurisdiction of a dean (a senior church official), later extending to include the dean's residence.
