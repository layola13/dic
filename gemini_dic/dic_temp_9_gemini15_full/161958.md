prang:/præŋ/| v.| 撞击；轻微碰撞；发出尖锐的声音 | To strike or collide with something, usually lightly; to make a sharp, ringing sound. |例句：The car pranged into a lamppost.  汽车撞到了一根灯柱上。
The cymbals pranged loudly.  钹发出响亮的声响。|近义词：bump, scrape, clang, crash |反义词：glide, float
