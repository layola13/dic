reavowing: /ˌriːəˈvaʊɪŋ/ | v. | 再次声明；重申；重新宣誓 | To state or affirm something again; to renew a vow or promise.  |  例句：After the scandal, the politician reavowed his commitment to public service.  在丑闻之后，这位政客再次声明了他对公共服务的承诺。


例句：She reavowed her love for him, despite everything that had happened.  尽管发生了所有的事情，她还是重申了她对他的爱。


近义词：reaffirm, reaffirming, restate, reiterate, reassert | 反义词：recant, deny, retract


词性：动词
