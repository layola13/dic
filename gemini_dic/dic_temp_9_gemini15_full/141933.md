ordinates:/ˈɔːrdɪnəts/ | n. | 坐标；纵坐标 | Coordinates are pairs of numbers that specify the location of a point on a plane.  坐标是指定平面上点位置的一对数字。|近义词：coordinates, abscissa,  applicate|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The coordinates of the point are (2, 3). = 这个点的坐标是 (2, 3)。
What are the coordinates of the origin? = 原点的坐标是什么？


一般过去时:
The cartographer plotted the coordinates of the island. = 绘图员标出了岛屿的坐标。
We found the coordinates of the treasure. = 我们找到了宝藏的坐标。


现在完成时:
They have determined the coordinates of the earthquake's epicenter. = 他们已经确定了地震震中的坐标。
Scientists have accurately mapped the coordinates of the galaxies. = 科学家们已经精确地绘制了星系的坐标图。


过去完成时:
Before launching the rocket, engineers had already calculated the precise coordinates. = 在发射火箭之前，工程师们已经计算出了精确的坐标。
The team had verified the coordinates before proceeding. = 团队在继续之前已经验证了坐标。


将来时:
The GPS will display the exact coordinates of your location. = GPS 将显示您位置的精确坐标。

虚拟语气:
