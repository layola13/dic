errantly:/ˈɛrəntli/| adv.| 错误地，迷失方向地 |  In a mistaken or incorrect way; wrongly.  It implies a deviation from what is correct, expected, or intended.  |例句：He errantly believed that the earth was flat. 他错误地认为地球是平的。

例句：The hikers errantly strayed from the marked trail.  徒步旅行者错误地偏离了标记的小路。


近义词：wrongly, incorrectly, mistakenly, erroneously

反义词：correctly, accurately, rightly

词性:副词
