multicastings:/ˌmʌltiˈkæstɪŋz/| n.|多播，多点广播|The act of sending a single message to multiple recipients simultaneously over a network.  This is a common technique used in network communication for efficiently distributing data to a large number of clients.|例句：The server performed multicastings to distribute the video stream to all connected clients. = 服务器执行多播以将视频流分发到所有连接的客户端。

例句：Efficient multicastings are crucial for delivering live streaming content to a large audience. = 高效的多播对于向大量观众提供直播内容至关重要。


近义词：broadcasting, distribution, dissemination|反义词：unicasting, point-to-point communication|名词
