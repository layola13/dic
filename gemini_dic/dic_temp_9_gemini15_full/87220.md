heremite:/ˈhɛrmɪt/| n. |隐士，隐居者 |A hermit is a person who lives alone, usually in a secluded place, often for religious reasons.  They are typically characterized by their solitary lifestyle and detachment from society. |例句：He abandoned his worldly possessions and became a hermit in the mountains. 他放弃了世俗的财产，成为了一名山中的隐士。

例句：Many hermits lived in caves and subsisted on simple food. 许多隐士住在山洞里，靠简单的食物生存。


近义词：recluse, ascetic, solitary

反义词：socialite, extrovert
