reconsultation:/ˌriːkɒnsʌlˈteɪʃən/ | n. |复诊；再次咨询；重新商讨 | The act of consulting again; a second or further consultation.  It often involves revisiting a previous issue or problem. |例句：The doctor scheduled a reconsultation to review the patient's progress.  医生安排了复诊以审查病人的病情进展。

例句：After the initial meeting, a reconsultation was deemed necessary to address outstanding concerns. 初次会议后，认为需要再次咨询以解决未决问题。


近义词：re-examination, review, second opinion, follow-up consultation | 反义词：initial consultation, first consultation | 词性：名词
