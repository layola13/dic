count-coup:/kaʊnt kuːp/|n.|政变，夺权|A count coup refers to a sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government.  It often involves the overthrow of a leader or government by force. |例句：The military staged a count coup and overthrew the democratically elected president.  军队发动政变，推翻了民选总统。|近义词：overthrow, putsch, rebellion, revolution|反义词：peaceful transition, democratic election|名词
