nondestruction:/ˌnɒn dəˈstrʌkʃən/| adj.|无损的，非破坏性的|Not causing damage or destruction.|The nondestructive testing ensured the integrity of the bridge.  无损检测确保了桥梁的完整性。|destructive, damaging|constructive, beneficial|形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  Instead, we will show how it's used in different sentence structures.


In a simple sentence:
The nondestructive testing method is preferred.  无损检测方法是首选。

In a complex sentence:
Because of the nondestructive nature of the scan, we could examine the artifact without harming it. 由于扫描的无损特性，我们可以检查文物而不会损坏它。

In a sentence with a passive voice:
The components were inspected using a nondestructive technique. 零件使用无损技术进行了检查。

In a sentence with an adverbial modifier:
Surprisingly, a completely nondestructive approach was found.  令人惊讶的是，找到了一种完全无损的方法。

In a comparative sentence:
Nondestructive testing is far superior to destructive testing in many applications. 在许多应用中，无损检测远优于破坏性检测。
