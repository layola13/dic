featherlessness: /ˈfɛðərləsnəs/ | n. | 无毛；缺乏羽毛 | The absence of feathers.  This term is often used in zoology and evolutionary biology to describe the characteristic lack of feathers in certain animals, particularly in contrast to birds.  | Example: The featherlessness of humans is a key distinguishing feature from birds. 人类的无毛是与鸟类的一个关键区别特征。| 近义词：lack of feathers, hairlessness (if referring to hair) | 反义词：feathered, hairy (if referring to hair)
