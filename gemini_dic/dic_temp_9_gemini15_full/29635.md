caterer:/ˈkeɪtərər/ | n. | 承办宴会者；饮食供应者 | A caterer is a person or company that provides food and drinks for events such as parties, weddings, and corporate functions. They handle all aspects of food preparation, service, and sometimes even decorations and rentals. |  The wedding caterer provided a delicious three-course meal. 婚礼的餐饮承办商提供了一顿美味的三道菜。  | provider, supplier, vendor |  (无明显的反义词，可考虑 self-catering (自己做饭)) | 名词

词源:
