refilming:/ˌriːˈfɪlmɪŋ/ | n. | 重拍；重新拍摄 | The act of filming something again, often with changes to the script, cast, or director.  This is often done to improve a film's quality, correct errors, or adapt it to a new audience. | Example: The movie received negative reviews, leading to its refilming. 这部电影收到负面评价，导致了它的重拍。 |近义词: reshooting, remaking | 反义词: original filming
