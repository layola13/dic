nonconscription:/ˌnɒnkənˈskrɪpʃən/| n. |非征兵制；免除兵役|The absence of compulsory military service; a system where individuals are not required to serve in the armed forces.|Example:  Many countries have adopted nonconscription, relying instead on volunteer armed forces. 许多国家已经采用非征兵制，而是依靠志愿军。|近义词：volunteerism, exemption from conscription |反义词：conscription, compulsory military service
