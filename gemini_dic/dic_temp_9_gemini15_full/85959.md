headphones:/ˈhɛdfoʊnz/ | n. |耳机 |A pair of headphones is a device that covers or is placed over the ears to allow the listener to privately hear audio. They typically consist of two transducers, one for each ear, and a connecting headband or cord. |例句：I use headphones when I listen to music on the bus. 我在公共汽车上听音乐时会戴耳机。

例句：She bought a pair of noise-canceling headphones to block out distractions. 她买了一副降噪耳机来消除干扰。


近义词：earphones, headset | 反义词：N/A
