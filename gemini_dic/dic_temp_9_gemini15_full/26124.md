bustee:/bʌsˈtiː/| n.|贫民窟；棚户区 |A bustee is a densely populated, unplanned, and often impoverished urban settlement, characterized by substandard housing and a lack of basic amenities.  Bustees are typically found in developing countries. |例句：Many families live in overcrowded bustees lacking basic sanitation. 许多家庭居住在缺乏基本卫生设施的拥挤贫民窟里。|例句：The government is trying to improve living conditions in the bustees. 政府正试图改善贫民窟的生活条件。|近义词：slum, shantytown, squatter settlement |反义词：suburb, upscale neighborhood
