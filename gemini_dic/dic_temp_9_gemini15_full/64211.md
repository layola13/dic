equiform:/ˈɛkwɪfɔːrm/| adj.|等形的，形状相同的|Having the same form or shape.  The term often refers to  similar shapes in different organisms, suggesting a common ancestry or convergent evolution.|The equiform wings of butterflies and moths suggest convergent evolution.|近义词：similar, isomorphic, homomorphic|反义词： dissimilar, heteromorphic|形容词
