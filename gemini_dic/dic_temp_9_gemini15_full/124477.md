mouldability's: /ˌmoʊldəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可塑性（名词所有格） | The capacity of something to be molded or shaped.  It refers to the ease with which a substance can be shaped or altered.  This word is the possessive form of "mouldability," indicating possession or belonging. |


例句：The clay's mouldability is excellent, allowing for intricate designs.  = 粘土的可塑性极佳，可以制作精细的设计。

The material's mouldability was a key factor in its selection for the project. = 材料的可塑性是项目选择它的关键因素。


近义词：formability, malleability, plasticity


反义词：inflexibility, rigidity, inflexibility


词性: Noun (possessive)
