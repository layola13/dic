hustings:/ˈhʌstɪŋz/| n.|选举会场；政治集会|A hustings is a public meeting held during an election campaign, at which candidates answer questions from the audience.|例句：The candidates faced tough questions at the hustings.  候选人们在选举集会上受到了尖锐的质问。|近义词：election meeting, political rally, campaign meeting|反义词：private meeting
