nonconvertible: /ˌnɒnkənˈvɜːtəbl/ | adj. |不可兑换的，不能转换的 |  Not able to be converted or exchanged for something else, especially currency.  This term typically refers to a currency that cannot be exchanged for another currency at a fixed or floating rate.  It also applies to assets or obligations that cannot be easily changed into another form. |

例句：

The currency is nonconvertible, meaning it cannot be freely exchanged for other currencies. =  这种货币不可兑换，这意味着它不能自由兑换成其他货币。

This bond is nonconvertible into common stock. = 这张债券不能转换为普通股。


近义词： inconvertible, irredeemable, unchangeable, fixed


反义词：convertible, exchangeable, transferable


词源:

* **non-**:  Prefix meaning "not."
* **convert**: From Latin *convertire*, meaning "to turn around, change."  This root also gives us words like "conversion," "converted," and "converter."
* **-ible**: Suffix forming adjectives meaning "able to be" or "capable of being."


USAGE->
(由于nonconvertible是形容词，因此不适用时态变化。以下列举了不同句式例句)


肯定句:
The agreement is nonconvertible. = 这项协议是不可更改的。
That asset is nonconvertible into cash. = 那项资产无法转换成现金。

否定句:
The currency is not nonconvertible; it can be exchanged. = 这种货币并非不可兑换；它可以兑换。
The contract isn't nonconvertible; it allows for modifications. = 这份合同并非不可更改；它允许修改。

疑问句:
Is the investment nonconvertible? = 这项投资不可兑换吗？
Are these bonds nonconvertible? = 这些债券不可兑换吗？

条件句:
If the currency remains nonconvertible, trade will be hampered. = 如果货币仍然不可兑换，贸易将受到阻碍。
Unless the contract is nonconvertible, we can renegotiate terms. = 除非合同不可更改，否则我们可以重新协商条款。
