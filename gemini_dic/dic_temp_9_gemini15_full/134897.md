nonmulched:/ˌnɑːnˈmʌltʃt/ | adj. |未覆盖覆盖物的；未覆盖护根物的 |Not covered with mulch.  Mulch is a material (such as straw, leaves, or wood chips) spread around plants to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.  Therefore, "nonmulched" describes soil or plants that lack this protective covering. |The nonmulched garden beds were quickly overrun with weeds.|近义词：unmulched |反义词：mulched
