regionalisms:/ˌriːdʒənəlɪzəm/| n.| 方言；区域习语；地方特色 | Words or phrases used in a particular region, often differing from standard language.  These can include pronunciation, vocabulary, and grammar. | Example: "The use of 'y'all' is a common regionalism in the Southern United States."  "使用 'y'all' 是美国南部常见的方言。" | 近义词：dialect, provincialism, localism | 反义词：standard language, universal language
