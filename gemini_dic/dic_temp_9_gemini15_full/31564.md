chaseable: /ˈtʃeɪsəbl/ | adj. | 可追捕的，可追逐的 |  Describes something that can be chased or pursued.  It implies that the object of the chase is capable of being caught or apprehended. | Example: The playful kitten was very chaseable.  那只顽皮的小猫非常容易追逐。 The thief, despite his speed, was chaseable. 那个小偷，尽管他跑得很快，但还是可以追到的。 | 近义词：pursuable, trackable, huntable | 反义词：uncatchable, elusive, untraceable |  词性:形容词
