quasi-jocundly:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈdʒɒkəndli/| adv.|近乎愉快地，略带喜悦地|Approaching or suggesting jocundity (cheerfulness or merriment) but not fully or genuinely so.  It implies a somewhat restrained or subdued cheerfulness.|He spoke quasi-jocundly about his recent promotion, a hint of anxiety underlying his words. 他近乎愉快地谈论了他最近的升职，言语中透露出一点焦虑。|近义词：cheerfully, merrily, brightly, lightheartedly |反义词：sadly, gloomily, dejectedly, morosely
