reclaim:/rɪˈkleɪm/| vt. n.|回收；开垦；要求归还；|To take back something that was yours; to recover or regain possession of something; to make (land) usable again.|例句：The government is planning to reclaim the land for housing development. 政府计划收回这块土地用于住宅开发。  例句：He reclaimed his stolen bicycle. 他取回了被盗的自行车。|近义词：recover, retrieve, regain|反义词：abandon, relinquish, surrender

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reclaim recyclable materials. = 他们回收可回收材料。
The farmer reclaims the land. = 农民开垦这块土地。

一般过去时:
She reclaimed her lost purse. = 她找回了丢失的钱包。
The company reclaimed its investment. = 公司收回了投资。

现在完成时:
We have reclaimed a significant amount of land. = 我们已经开垦了大量的土地。
They have reclaimed their rights. = 他们已经收回了自己的权利。

过去完成时:
He had reclaimed his inheritance before his death. = 他在他死前已经收回了他的遗产。
They had reclaimed the lost city from the jungle. = 他们已经从丛林中收回了失落的城市。

将来时:
They will reclaim the contaminated area. = 他们将清理受污染的地区。
She will reclaim her belongings from the storage unit. = 她将从储物单元取回她的物品。

虚拟语气:
If I could reclaim my youth, I would. = 如果我能找回我的青春，我会的。
I wish I could reclaim the lost time. = 我希望我能找回失去的时间。

被动语态:
The land was reclaimed for agricultural use. = 这块土地被开垦用于农业用途。
The stolen goods were reclaimed by the police. = 被盗的货物被警方追回。

疑问句:
Can we reclaim the damaged goods? = 我们能收回损坏的货物吗？
Will they reclaim the land for building? = 他们会收回这块土地用于建筑吗？

否定句:
He did not reclaim his lost property. = 他没有取回他丢失的财产。
They could not reclaim the stolen car. = 他们无法追回被盗的汽车。
