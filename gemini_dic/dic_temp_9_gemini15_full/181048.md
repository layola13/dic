reincluded:/ˌriːɪnˈkluːdɪd/| v. adj.|重新包含，再次包含 | To include again or anew; to include something that had been previously omitted or removed.  |例句：The amendments reincluded the previously deleted clause.  修正案重新加入了先前删除的条款。


例句：After reviewing the feedback, the team reincluded the feature in the software update. 在审查了反馈后，团队在软件更新中重新加入了该功能。


近义词：reinserted, added back, restored


反义词：excluded, omitted, removed


词性:动词(过去分词)
