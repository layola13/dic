navigational:/ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/| adj.|导航的；航行的；航海的| Relating to navigation or the process of navigating.  It describes something connected with the act of directing a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle from one place to another. |例句：The navigational system guided the ship through the storm.  导航系统引导船只穿越风暴。

例句：The plane's navigational instruments were malfunctioning.  飞机的导航仪器出现故障。


近义词：nautical, seafaring, guiding


反义词：unguiding, directionless


词性:形容词
