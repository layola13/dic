laminarite:/ˈlæmɪnəraɪt/| n. |层状矿物；层状岩石|A sedimentary rock composed of thin layers, often of plant origin.  Laminarite is typically formed from compressed layers of algae or other plant material.  It can be a valuable source of information about past environments. |例句：The geologists found several layers of laminarite in the ancient seabed. 地质学家们在古代海床上发现了数层层状矿物。


The discovery of laminarite in this formation indicates a shallow-water environment in the past.  在这个地层中发现层状矿物表明过去存在浅水环境。|近义词：stratified rock, layered rock |反义词：massive rock, unstratified rock
