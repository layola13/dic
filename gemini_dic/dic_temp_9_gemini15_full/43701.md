crediting-to:/ˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ tuː/| v. |归功于，记入…的贷方|Attributing something to a particular source or person.  The act of giving credit or acknowledgment to someone or something.|The success of the project is crediting to the team's hard work. 项目的成功归功于团队的辛勤工作。|近义词：ascribing to, attributing to, assigning to|反义词：debiting from, blaming on


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are crediting the extra work to overtime pay. = 我们把额外的工作记入加班费。
They credit this achievement to their collaboration. = 他们把这项成就归功于他们的合作。


一般过去时:
The company credited the increased profits to improved marketing. = 公司将利润增长归功于改进的营销策略。
She credited her success to her hard work and perseverance. = 她把她的成功归功于她的努力和毅力。


现在完成时:
We have credited all the expenses to the correct accounts. = 我们已经将所有费用记入正确的账户。
They have credited the discovery to a young scientist. = 他们已将这项发现归功于一位年轻的科学家。


过去完成时:
They had credited the funds to the wrong account before realizing the mistake. = 他们在意识到错误之前已将资金记入了错误的账户。
She had credited her inspiration to a dream she'd had. = 她已将她的灵感归功于她做过的梦。


将来时:
We will credit the payment to your account tomorrow. = 我们明天将款项记入您的账户。
They will credit the innovation to the research team. = 他们将把这项创新归功于研究团队。


虚拟语气:
If they had worked harder, they would have credited the success to their efforts. = 如果他们更努力工作，他们就会把成功归功于他们的努力。
If we credited the problem to a lack of resources, we might find a solution. = 如果我们将问题归因于资源匮乏，我们可能会找到解决方案。


被动语态:
The extra expenses were credited to the research and development budget. = 额外的支出记入了研发预算。
The invention was credited to a team of engineers. = 这项发明归功于一个工程师团队。


疑问句:
To whom will you credit this accomplishment? = 你将把这项成就归功于谁？
To what factor do you credit your success? = 你把你的成功归功于什么因素？


否定句:
The bank didn't credit the payment to my account. = 银行没有将付款记入我的账户。
We cannot credit this achievement to a single person. = 我们不能将这项成就归功于一个人。


PHRASE->
crediting to one's account = 记入某人的账户
crediting to experience = 归功于经验
crediting to hard work = 归功于辛勤工作
crediting to luck = 归功于运气
crediting to innovation = 归功于创新
