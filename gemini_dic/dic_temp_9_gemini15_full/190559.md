satirized:/ˈsætəraɪzd/| v. |讽刺；挖苦；嘲弄 |To satirize something is to use humor, irony, exaggeration, or ridicule to expose and criticize folly or vice, particularly in the context of contemporary politics and other topical issues.  The goal is often to inspire change or at least raise awareness. |The cartoon satirized the politician's recent gaffe.  这幅漫画讽刺了这位政治家最近的失误。

The novel satirizes the excesses of consumerism. 这部小说讽刺了消费主义的过度。


近义词：mock, ridicule, lampoon, caricature, parody | 反义词：praise, commend, flatter
