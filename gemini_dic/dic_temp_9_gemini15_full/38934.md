confide:/kənˈfaɪd/| vt.|吐露；信托；委托|To tell someone something secret or personal, trusting them not to repeat it.  To entrust someone with a secret.|例句：She confided her troubles to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的烦恼。

例句：He confided the task to his most reliable employee. 他把这项任务托付给了他最可靠的员工。|近义词：entrust, reveal, disclose|反义词：conceal, hide, suppress|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I confide in my diary every night. = 我每天晚上都向我的日记倾诉。
She confides her deepest fears to her therapist. = 她向她的治疗师倾诉她最深层的恐惧。

一般过去时:
He confided his secret to me last night. = 他昨晚向我吐露了他的秘密。
She confided in her sister about the difficult situation. = 她向她姐姐倾诉了这个困境。

现在完成时:
I have confided in you because I trust you. = 我向你吐露心事是因为我相信你。
She has confided her plans to several close friends. = 她已经向几个密友透露了她的计划。

过去完成时:
He had confided his worries to his father before the trip. = 在旅行之前，他已经向父亲倾诉了他的担忧。
She had confided in her journal for years before seeking professional help. = 在寻求专业帮助之前，她多年来一直向日记倾诉。


将来时:
I will confide in him about my concerns. = 我将向他倾诉我的担忧。
She will confide her secret to her husband soon. = 她很快就会向她的丈夫吐露她的秘密。


虚拟语气:
If I had a trusted friend, I would confide in them. = 如果我有一个值得信赖的朋友，我会向他们倾诉。
I wish I could confide in someone about this problem. = 我希望我能向某人倾诉这个问题。


被动语态:
The secret was confided to only a few people. = 这个秘密只被少数人知道。
His concerns were confided to his closest advisor. = 他的担忧被告知了他最亲密的顾问。


疑问句:
In whom do you confide your secrets? = 你向谁倾诉你的秘密？
Would you confide in a stranger with your personal problems? = 你会向陌生人倾诉你的个人问题吗？


否定句:
He didn't confide in anyone about his anxieties. = 他没有向任何人倾诉他的焦虑。
She refused to confide in her colleagues about the project's setbacks. = 她拒绝向同事们倾诉项目中的挫折。


PHRASE->
confide in someone = 向某人吐露心事
confide a secret = 泄露一个秘密
confide one's feelings = 吐露某人的感受
confide one's troubles = 倾诉某人的烦恼
confide something to someone = 向某人透露某事

词源:
From Old French confier, from Latin confīdēre "to trust," from com- "together" + fidēre "to trust."
