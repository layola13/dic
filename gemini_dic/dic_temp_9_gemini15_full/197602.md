词源：

Shenyang is a transliteration of the Mandarin Chinese name 沈阳 (Shěnyáng).  "沈" (Shěn) refers to the Shen clan, a prominent family in the area's history. "阳" (Yáng) means "sun" or "south-facing slope."  The name likely reflects the location and historical significance of the Shen clan in the area.
