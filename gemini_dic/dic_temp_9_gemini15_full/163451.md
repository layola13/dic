precontend: /ˌpriːkənˈtɛnd/ | v. | 预先争论；预先竞争 | To argue or compete beforehand; to engage in preliminary contention.  | The two teams precontended over the rules before the game even began.  两个队在比赛开始前就预先就规则争论了起来。 |  contest, dispute, debate | concede, agree | 动词

The rivals precontend for market share. = 对手们预先争夺市场份额。
