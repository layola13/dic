calamities:/kəˈlæmətiz/| n. |灾难，灾祸 | 大规模的灾难或不幸事件；一系列不幸事件。 Calamities can be natural disasters, accidents, or other unfortunate events that cause significant harm or suffering. |例句：The earthquake caused widespread calamities.  地震造成了广泛的灾难。

The series of calamities left the family devastated. 一系列的灾难使这个家庭遭受了毁灭性的打击。


近义词：catastrophes, disasters, misfortunes, mishaps |反义词：blessings, good fortune, prosperity |词性:名词
