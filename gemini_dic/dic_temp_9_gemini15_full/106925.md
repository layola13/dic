lacewings:/ˈleɪsˌwɪŋz/| n.|草蛉|A lacewing is an insect with delicate, lacy-veined wings. They are beneficial insects in gardens because their larvae are voracious predators of aphids and other small pests.|例句：The gardener was delighted to see lacewings in her garden, as they help control the aphid population. 花园管理员很高兴在她的花园里看到草蛉，因为它们有助于控制蚜虫的数量。  The delicate lacewings danced in the evening light.  精致的草蛉在暮色中飞舞。|近义词：neuropteran|反义词:N/A
