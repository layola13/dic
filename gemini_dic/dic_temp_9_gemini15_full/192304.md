screwing: /ˈskruːɪŋ/ | n. v. | 螺旋，拧紧；性交；欺骗 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "screwing" refers to the act of fastening something with a screw.  It can also, more informally and often vulgarly, refer to sexual intercourse. As a verb, it means to fasten something using a screw, or to twist or turn something forcefully.  It can also be used informally (and often vulgarly) to mean to cheat or exploit someone.


例句:
The screwing of the lightbulb into the socket was surprisingly difficult. = 把灯泡拧进灯座里出奇地困难。

He admitted to screwing the company out of thousands of dollars. = 他承认欺骗公司，骗取了数千美元。
