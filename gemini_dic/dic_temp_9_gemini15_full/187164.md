roadrunner: /ˈroʊdˌrʌnər/ | n. |走鹃|A fast-running bird of the cuckoo family, especially the greater roadrunner (Geococcyx californianus), found in the southwestern United States and Mexico.  It is known for its speed and ability to catch and eat snakes and lizards.|例句：The roadrunner darted across the desert floor, its long legs a blur.  走鹃快速穿过沙漠地面，长长的腿像一道模糊的影子。|近义词：cuckoo (in a broader sense), ground cuckoo|反义词：None specifically, but words implying slowness could be considered antonyms.|名词
