shaping:/ˈʃeɪpɪŋ/| v. n. |塑造；形成；影响；形状；样子 |To give a particular form or shape to something.  |例句：The sculptor is shaping the clay into a beautiful bird.  雕塑家正在把粘土塑造成一只美丽的鸟。

例句：The wind shaped the sand dunes into fantastic forms. 风把沙丘塑造成奇异的形状。


近义词：molding, forming, fashioning, creating, influencing| 反义词：destroying, ruining, demolishing | 动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is shaping the dough into rolls. 他正在把面团揉成面包卷。
The wind is shaping the trees. 风正在塑造树木的形状。


一般过去时:
She shaped the clay into a pot. 她把泥土塑造成一个罐子。
The experience shaped his character. 那次经历塑造了他的性格。


现在完成时:
They have shaped their lives according to their beliefs. 他们已经根据自己的信仰塑造了人生。
The artist has shaped many masterpieces.这位艺术家已经塑造了许多杰作。


过去完成时:
He had shaped his argument carefully before the debate. 在辩论之前，他已经仔细地构思了他的论点。
The wind had shaped the landscape over centuries.几个世纪以来，风塑造了这片土地的地貌。


将来时:
She will shape the future of the company. 她将塑造公司的未来。
The new policy will shape the way we work. 新政策将改变我们的工作方式。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would shape it better. 如果我有更多的时间，我会把它做得更好。
I wish I could shape my destiny. 我希望我能主宰自己的命运。


被动语态:
The clay was shaped by the potter. 粘土被陶工塑造。
The policy is being shaped by public opinion. 政策正在受到公众舆论的影响。


疑问句:
How are you shaping up? 你最近情况如何？（进展如何？）
What is shaping the narrative of this story?  是什么塑造了这个故事的叙述？


否定句:
He didn't shape the wood properly. 他没有正确地雕刻木材。
The government failed to shape a coherent policy. 政府未能制定出一项连贯的政策。


PHRASE->
Shape up or ship out!  (俚语)  要么改进，要么滚蛋！
In shape  (adj.)  身体健康，状态良好
Out of shape (adj.)  身体状况不佳
Shape shifter (n.)  变化莫测的人或事物
Take shape (v.)  成形；显现


词源:
Old English *sċeap, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch schaap and German Schaf ‘sheep,’ reflecting the original sense of ‘form,’ ‘figure.’  The modern meaning developed from the idea of something having a particular form or figure.  The verb meaning 'to give shape to' is a later development.
