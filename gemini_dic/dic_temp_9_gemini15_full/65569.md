evac:/ˈɛvæk/| vt.|撤离，疏散|To evacuate is to remove (someone) from a place of danger to a safer place.  It implies a planned and organized removal, often in response to an emergency. |例句：The authorities evacuated the residents before the hurricane hit.  当局在飓风来临之前疏散了居民。
例句：They had to evacuate the building because of a fire. 他们不得不因火灾而撤离大楼。|近义词：remove, clear,  |反义词：invade, occupy.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government evacuates citizens during natural disasters. = 政府在自然灾害期间疏散公民。
We evacuate the building during fire drills. = 我们在消防演习期间撤离大楼。

一般过去时:
The police evacuated the area after the bomb threat. = 炸弹威胁后，警方疏散了该地区。
They evacuated the school because of a gas leak. = 由于煤气泄漏，他们疏散了学校。

现在完成时:
They have evacuated all the patients from the hospital. = 他们已经将所有病人从医院疏散。
The authorities have evacuated the coastal areas. = 当局已经疏散了沿海地区。

过去完成时:
They had evacuated the village before the flood reached it. = 在洪水到达村庄之前，他们已经疏散了村庄。
The residents had evacuated their homes before the earthquake struck. = 地震发生前，居民们已经撤离了家园。

将来时:
We will evacuate the building if there is a fire. = 如果发生火灾，我们将疏散大楼。
The city will evacuate its citizens if the volcano erupts. = 如果火山爆发，城市将疏散其市民。

虚拟语气:
If there were a fire, we would evacuate immediately. = 如果发生火灾，我们将立即撤离。
It would be wise to evacuate if the situation worsens.  = 如果情况恶化，撤离将是明智之举。

被动语态:
The town was evacuated overnight. = 该镇一夜之间被疏散。
The building is being evacuated now. = 大楼现在正在被疏散。

疑问句:
Will the residents be evacuated? = 居民会被疏散吗？
Have they evacuated the area yet? = 他们已经疏散该地区了吗？

否定句:
They did not evacuate the building quickly enough. = 他们没有及时疏散大楼。
The residents were not evacuated in time. = 居民们没有及时撤离。
