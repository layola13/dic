imbizo:/ɪmˈbɪzoʊ/| n. |聚会；集会；会议|A traditional Zulu meeting, typically held outdoors, where community members gather to discuss important issues.  It is a forum for dialogue, consensus-building, and decision-making. |例句：The community held an imbizo to discuss the new development plans. 社区举行了一次聚会来讨论新的发展计划。
The imbizo lasted for hours, with many people sharing their opinions.  这次聚会持续了几个小时，许多人都分享了他们的意见。|近义词：meeting, gathering, assembly, conference|反义词：dispersion, scattering
