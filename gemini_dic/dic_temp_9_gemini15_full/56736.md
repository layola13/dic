doormat:/ˈdɔːrmæt/ | n. |擦鞋垫，门垫 | A mat placed outside a door to wipe shoes on.  It can also refer to someone who is easily taken advantage of. | Example:  He wiped his muddy shoes on the doormat. 他把泥泞的鞋子擦在门垫上。  She's such a doormat, always letting people walk all over her. 她真是个受气包，总是让人摆布。 |近义词：mat, welcome mat, rug (in some contexts) |反义词：bully, strong person
