sacrifice:/ˈsækrɪfaɪs/| n. vt.|牺牲；祭品；祭祀|The act of giving up something valued for the sake of something else regarded as more important;  an offering made to a deity.|例句：He made a great sacrifice for his family. 他为了家庭做出了巨大的牺牲。

例句：The ancient Romans offered sacrifices to their gods. 古罗马人向他们的神灵献祭。


近义词：offering, immolation, oblation|反义词：gain, profit, acquire|词性：Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sacrifices his time for his work. = 他为工作牺牲了他的时间。
She sacrifices her comfort for her children. = 她为了孩子牺牲了自己的舒适。


一般过去时:
They sacrificed a goat to the gods. = 他们向神献祭了一只山羊。

现在完成时:

They have sacrificed their free time to help others. = 他们牺牲了自己的空闲时间来帮助他人。


过去完成时:
They had sacrificed everything they owned to start a new life. = 他们为了开始新的生活牺牲了一切财产。
He had sacrificed his dreams to support his family. = 他为了养家糊口牺牲了自己的梦想。

虚拟语气:
