husbandage:/ˈhʌzbəndɪdʒ/| n.| 耕作；农事；管理；节俭；婚姻生活 | The careful and skillful management of resources, especially in farming or business;  marriage.  | Example: The success of the farm depended on good husbandage.  这家农场的成功取决于良好的经营管理。


Example:  Their long and happy husbandage was a testament to their enduring love. 他们漫长而幸福的婚姻生活证明了他们永恒的爱。 | 近义词：management, stewardship, husbandry, economy | 反义词：wastefulness, extravagance, mismanagement | 词性：名词
