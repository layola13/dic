grumbler:/ˈɡrʌmblər/| n.|抱怨者，牢骚满腹的人|A person who frequently complains or grumbles.  |例句：He's a grumbler, always finding fault with everything. 他是个抱怨者，总是对所有事情吹毛求疵。|例句：The old grumbler complained about the noise from the street.  那个爱抱怨的老人抱怨街上的噪音。|近义词：complainer, whiner, fault-finder |反义词：optimist, complaisant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grumbles about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
She grumbles constantly about her job. = 她不断抱怨她的工作。

一般过去时:
He grumbled about the long wait. = 他抱怨漫长的等待。
She grumbled to herself about the unfairness of it all. = 她自言自语地抱怨这一切的不公平。

现在完成时:
He has grumbled about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题几周了。
She has grumbled repeatedly about the lack of support. = 她一再抱怨缺乏支持。

过去完成时:
He had grumbled for years before he finally took action. = 他抱怨了好几年才最终采取行动。
She had grumbled throughout the entire meeting. = 她在整个会议期间都在抱怨。

将来时:
He will grumble if you are late again. = 如果你再次迟到，他会抱怨。
She will probably grumble about the extra work. = 她可能会抱怨额外的工作。

虚拟语气:
If he grumbled less, people would like him more. = 如果他少抱怨一些，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't grumble so much. = 我希望她不要抱怨那么多。

被动语态:
His complaints are often grumbled about by others. = 他的抱怨经常被别人抱怨。 (This is a less common usage)
The decision was grumbled about by many employees. = 许多员工对这个决定表示不满。


疑问句:
Does he often grumble? = 他经常抱怨吗？
Why is she grumbling today? = 她今天为什么抱怨？

否定句:
He didn't grumble about the food. = 他没有抱怨食物。
She doesn't usually grumble. = 她通常不抱怨。


PHRASE->
a grumbling stomach = 咕噜作响的肚子
a grumbling voice = 低沉抱怨的声音
a grumbling crowd =  抱怨的人群
grumbling discontent =  不满的抱怨
to grumble and groan = 抱怨和呻吟
