disaggregation: /ˌdɪsˌæɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |分解；分散；细分 | The process of separating something into its constituent parts.  It can refer to breaking down large datasets into smaller, more manageable units, or to the separation of a larger organization into smaller, independent units. |


例句：

The disaggregation of the data revealed some interesting patterns. 数据的分解揭示了一些有趣的模式。

The company's disaggregation into smaller subsidiaries improved efficiency.  公司分解成较小的子公司提高了效率。


近义词：decomposition, subdivision, breakdown, fragmentation, decentralization


反义词：aggregation, consolidation, unification, integration


词性：名词
