physiography:/ˌfɪziˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |自然地理学；地貌学 |The scientific study of the physical features of the earth and other planets.  It involves the description and interpretation of landforms, their distribution, and their origins. Physiography is closely related to geology, geomorphology, and climatology. | Example:  The physiography of the region is characterized by rolling hills and fertile valleys.  这个地区的自然地理特征以连绵起伏的丘陵和肥沃的山谷为特征。|近义词：geomorphology, physical geography |反义词：N/A
