ownest: /ˈoʊnɪst/ | adj. | 最自己的；最亲密的 |  This word is a superlative form of "own," indicating the highest degree of possession or intimacy. It is rarely used in modern English and often considered archaic or poetic.  It implies a deep sense of belonging or personal connection. | Example:  The ownest thoughts of my heart, I shared with him.  (我把我内心最私密的想法与他分享了。) | 近义词: dearest, most intimate, most personal | 反义词:  least personal, alien, foreign |  词性: 形容词


USAGE->
This word is archaic and doesn't have common usage across different tenses.  Examples would be limited to poetic or archaic contexts.  The following examples use "own" instead of "ownest" for practical demonstration of tenses.
