It's often associated with age and weathering, giving the object a distinctive appearance.  It can be desirable, adding value and character. | The bronze statue had developed a beautiful green patine over the centuries.  这尊青铜雕像经过几个世纪的岁月，已经形成了一层美丽的绿色铜锈。| patina, verdigris|  (反义词较少，因其描述的是一种状态，而非特定性质)|名词
