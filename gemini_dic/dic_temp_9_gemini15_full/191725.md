sciatically:/saɪˈætɪkli/| adv.| 坐骨神经痛地，患坐骨神经痛地 |  Describes something done or experienced because of sciatica.  It indicates the pain or discomfort is related to sciatica. |She moved sciatically, wincing with each step. 她因为坐骨神经痛而移动，每一步都龇牙咧嘴。|  painfully, awkwardly | comfortably, easily | 副词
