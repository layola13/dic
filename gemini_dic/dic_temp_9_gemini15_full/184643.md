restriction:/rɪˈstrɪkʃən/| n. |限制；约束；规定|A limitation or a rule that controls how something can be done or used.  It can refer to a physical constraint, a legal limit, or a self-imposed boundary.|例句：There are restrictions on the amount of alcohol you can buy.  你所能购买的酒的量是有限制的。|例句：Speed restrictions are in place on this road. 这条路上有限速规定。|近义词：limitation, constraint, regulation, restraint |反义词：freedom, liberty, openness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government imposes many restrictions on businesses. = 政府对企业施加许多限制。
This rule places restrictions on what we can do. = 这条规则限制了我们能做什么。


一般过去时:
There were restrictions on travel during the pandemic. = 大流行期间对旅行有限制。
The company imposed new restrictions on employee access. = 公司对员工的访问权限实施了新的限制。


现在完成时:
They have imposed new restrictions on data privacy. = 他们已经对数据隐私实施了新的限制。
The city has lifted many of the restrictions. = 该市已经取消了许多限制。


过去完成时:
Before the announcement, there had been few restrictions on gatherings. = 在公告发布之前，对集会几乎没有限制。
They had already implemented restrictions before the storm hit. = 在风暴来临之前，他们就已经实施了限制。


将来时:
They will introduce stricter restrictions next month. = 他们下个月将实行更严格的限制。
The new law will impose restrictions on advertising. = 新法将对广告施加限制。


虚拟语气:
If there were no restrictions, we could travel anywhere. = 如果没有限制，我们就可以去任何地方旅行。
I wish there were fewer restrictions on our work. = 我希望我们工作上的限制少一些。


被动语态:
Many activities were restricted due to the bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，许多活动都被限制了。
Access to the building is restricted by security measures. = 安全措施限制了进入大楼的权限。


疑问句:
Are there any restrictions on bringing food into the concert? = 带食物进入音乐会有什么限制吗？
What restrictions apply to this visa? = 这张签证有什么限制？


否定句:
There are no restrictions on the number of people attending. = 参加人数没有限制。
This software has no restrictions on its usage. = 这个软件的使用没有限制。
