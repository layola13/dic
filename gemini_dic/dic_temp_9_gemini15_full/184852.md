resynthesis:/ˌriːˈsɪnθəsɪs/| n. |再合成；重新合成 |The process of synthesizing something again, often from different components or with a new approach.  It implies a rebuilding or recomposition from previously existing materials or information. |例句：The artist's resynthesis of traditional techniques with modern materials produced stunning results.  艺术家将传统技法与现代材料重新合成，产生了令人惊叹的结果。
例句：The scientist's resynthesis of the compound resulted in a more stable form. 科学家对该化合物的重新合成产生了更稳定的形式。|近义词：recomposition, reconstruction, re-creation|反义词：analysis, decomposition|名词
