double-glazed: /ˈdʌbəlɡleɪzd/ | adj. | 双层玻璃的；双层玻璃窗的 |  Having two panes of glass with an air or gas space between them to provide better insulation.  | Example:  The double-glazed windows helped to keep the house warm in winter.  双层玻璃窗有助于在冬天保持房屋温暖。


The house features double-glazed windows throughout.  整栋房子都装有双层玻璃窗。 |  近义词：insulated, thermally efficient | 反义词：single-glazed


词源:

* **double:**  From Old English *dubbel, from Proto-Germanic *dubl-.  Meaning "two" or "twice."
* **glazed:** From Middle English *glased, past participle of *glasen, from Old English *glæsian, "to furnish with glass," from *glæs, "glass."


用法说明:

double-glazed 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，通常是窗户 (windows) 或门 (doors)。 它描述了由两块玻璃板和中间的空气或气体层组成的结构，这提高了隔热性和隔音性。
