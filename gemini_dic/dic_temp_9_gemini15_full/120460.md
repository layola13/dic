minorite:/maɪˈnɒrɪti/ | n. |少数民族，少数派 | A group of people who are different from the larger group in a country or area, especially in terms of race, religion, or language.  They often face discrimination and lack political power. | Example:  The government is trying to protect the rights of minorities. 政府正在努力保护少数民族的权利。|近义词：minority group, small group |反义词：majority
