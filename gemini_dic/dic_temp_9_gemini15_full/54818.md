dispersement:/dɪˈspɜːrsmənt/| n.| 分配；散布；支付 | The act of dispersing or distributing something, such as funds, goods, or people.  It often implies a wide spread or scattering. |例句：The dispersement of funds to the various departments was delayed.  资金向各部门的分配被推迟了。

例句：The dispersement of seeds ensured the plant's survival. 种子的散播确保了植物的生存。


近义词：distribution, dissemination, allocation, disbursement |反义词：concentration, accumulation, gathering

词性：名词

词源:
From the verb "disperse" (to scatter or spread widely),  which is itself from the Latin "dispersus," the past participle of "dispergere," meaning "to scatter," formed from the prefix "dis-" (apart) and "spargere" (to scatter).  The suffix "-ment" indicates the action or process.
