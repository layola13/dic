hubcap:/ˈhʌbkæp/| n. |轮毂盖，车轮盖 | A hubcap is a decorative metal or plastic cover that fits over the wheel hub of a car or other vehicle.  It protects the wheel hub and often adds a stylistic element to the vehicle's appearance. | Example:  He lost a hubcap driving over a pothole. 他开车经过一个坑时弄丢了一个轮毂盖。  |近义词：wheel cover |反义词：无
