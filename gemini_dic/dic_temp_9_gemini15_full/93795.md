immersed:/ɪˈmɜːrst/| v. adj.|沉浸的；专心的；使沉浸|To be completely surrounded by something, or to be deeply involved in something.|例句：She was completely immersed in her work. 她完全沉浸在工作中。|近义词：engrossed, absorbed, enveloped|反义词：detached, superficial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He immerses himself in his studies every evening. = 他每天晚上都专心致志地学习。
They immerse the fabric in dye before drying it. = 他们在晾干织物之前将其浸入染料中。


一般过去时:
She immersed herself in the fascinating novel. = 她沉浸在那本引人入胜的小说中。
The archaeologists immersed the artifact in a special solution. = 考古学家将文物浸泡在一种特殊的溶液中。


现在完成时:
He has immersed himself in the project for months. = 他已经投入这个项目几个月了。
They have immersed the seeds in water to promote germination. = 他们已经将种子浸泡在水中以促进发芽。


过去完成时:
She had immersed herself in the research before writing the paper. = 在写论文之前，她已经全身心地投入到研究中。
The team had immersed the components in a cleaning solution. = 团队已经将组件浸泡在清洁溶液中。


将来时:
He will immerse himself in the new challenge. = 他将全身心地投入到新的挑战中。
They will immerse the sculptures in protective coating. = 他们将把雕塑浸泡在保护涂层中。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would immerse myself in learning a new language. = 如果我有更多时间，我会全身心地学习一门外语。
I wish I could immerse myself in a good book right now. = 我真希望我现在能沉浸在一本好书中。


被动语态:
The fabric was immersed in dye for several hours. = 织物浸泡在染料中数小时。
The components are being immersed in a cleaning solution. = 组件正在被浸泡在清洁溶液中。


疑问句:
Was he immersed in his work when you called him? = 你打电话给他时，他正专心工作吗？
Are the vegetables immersed in boiling water? = 蔬菜是浸在沸水中吗？


否定句:
She didn't immerse herself in the details of the report. = 她没有深入研究报告的细节。
They didn't immerse the parts in the correct solution. = 他们没有将零件浸泡在正确的溶液中。
