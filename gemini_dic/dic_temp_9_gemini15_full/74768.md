freeze-dryer:/ˈfriːzˌdraɪər/| n. |冷冻干燥机|A freeze-dryer is a machine that removes water from a product after it has been frozen, using a process called freeze-drying or lyophilization.  This process preserves the product by preventing the formation of ice crystals that would damage its structure. |例句：The laboratory uses a freeze-dryer to preserve sensitive biological samples. 实验室使用冷冻干燥机来保存敏感的生物样本。|例句：We need a high-capacity freeze-dryer to process the large batch of fruit. 我们需要一台大容量冷冻干燥机来处理一大批水果。|近义词：lyophilizer|反义词：None|名词
