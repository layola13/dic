nondivisively:/ˌnɒnˈdɪvaɪzɪvli/ | adv. | 不分裂地；和谐地；团结一致地 | In a way that does not cause division or disagreement.  It emphasizes unity and cooperation. | The team worked nondivisively to achieve their common goal.  团队齐心协力地实现了他们的共同目标。 |  harmoniously, unitedly, collaboratively | divisively, controversially, discordantly | 副词


USAGE->

This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  The usage is consistent across different sentence types.

Affirmative Sentence:
They approached the problem nondivisively, finding a solution that satisfied everyone.  他们以不分裂的方式处理这个问题，找到了一个让每个人都满意的解决方案。

Negative Sentence:
The issue wasn't handled nondivisively; instead, it led to further conflict.  这个问题处理得并不和谐；相反，它导致了进一步的冲突。


Question Sentence:
Did they resolve the dispute nondivisively? 他们是否以不分裂的方式解决了争端？

Exclamative Sentence:
How nondivisively they worked together! 他们合作得多么和谐！


PHRASE->

This word is not typically used in common phrases.  Its meaning is best understood in its standalone adverbial usage.
