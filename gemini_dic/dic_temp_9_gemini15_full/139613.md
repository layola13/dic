occasionate:/əˈkeɪʒəneɪt/ | vt. |引起，导致；是…的原因 | to cause something to happen, especially something bad or unpleasant | The heavy rain occasionated flooding in the town. 大雨导致了该镇的洪水。  His carelessness occasioned the accident. 他的粗心导致了事故。|近义词：cause, bring about, give rise to |反义词：prevent, avoid, avert |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud noise occasionates a headache. = 噪音引起头痛。
This policy occasionates many problems. =这项政策导致许多问题。

一般过去时:
The storm occasionated widespread power outages. =暴风雨导致了大范围的停电。
His recklessness occasionated a serious car accident. = 他的鲁莽导致了一起严重的交通事故。

现在完成时:
His actions have occasionated much controversy. =他的行为已经引起了很多争议。
The drought has occasionated a severe water shortage. =干旱已经导致了严重的缺水。

过去完成时:
The faulty wiring had occasionated the fire. = 坏的线路已经导致了火灾。
Her negligence had occasionated the project's failure. =她的疏忽已经导致了项目的失败。

将来时:
The new law will occasionate significant changes in the industry. =新法将导致该行业发生重大变化。
The upcoming election will occasionate much political debate. =即将到来的选举将引发大量的政治辩论。

虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, he would not have occasionated the accident. =如果他更小心一点，就不会导致那场事故了。
If the company had planned better, it would not have occasionated so many problems. =如果公司计划得更好，就不会导致这么多问题了。

被动语态:
The damage was occasionated by the earthquake. =破坏是由地震造成的。
The delay was occasionated by unforeseen circumstances. =延误是由不可预见的情况造成的。

疑问句:
What occasioned the dispute? =是什么引起了这场争端？
What occasioned your sudden departure? =是什么导致你突然离开？

否定句:
The good weather did not occasion any problems. =好天气没有造成任何问题。
His explanation did not occasion any further questions. =他的解释没有引起任何进一步的问题。


PHRASE->
occasionate a problem = 引起问题
occasionate difficulties = 导致困难
occasionate debate = 引起争论
occasionate a delay = 导致延误
occasionate a crisis = 导致危机
