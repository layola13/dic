rappelling:/ræˈpɛlɪŋ/| v.|绳降；速降|The act of descending a steep slope or cliff by using a rope.|例句：We went rappelling down the cliff face. 我们沿着悬崖峭壁进行了绳降。
例句：The experienced climbers were rappelling with ease. 经验丰富的登山者轻松地进行了绳降。|近义词：abseiling|反义词：ascending
