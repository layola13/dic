self-sways:/selfˈsweɪz/| v.|自我摇摆，自我飘忽不定，犹豫不决 |The word "self-sways" is not a standard dictionary entry. It's a compound word formed by combining "self" and "sways."  It describes a state of internal conflict or indecision, where one's own thoughts and feelings pull in different directions, leading to wavering or uncertainty.  It implies a lack of firm conviction or a sense of being tossed about by internal forces.|例句：He self-sways between accepting the job offer and pursuing his own business. 他在接受工作邀请和自己创业之间犹豫不决。|近义词：hesitates, vacillates, fluctuates, wavers|反义词：resolute, decisive, unwavering
