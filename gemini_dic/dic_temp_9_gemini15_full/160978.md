postal:/ˈpoʊstl/| adj.|邮政的；邮寄的|Relating to the system of delivering mail.|The postal service is responsible for delivering letters and packages. 邮政服务负责投递信件和包裹。|近义词：mail, post office|反义词：electronic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The postal service delivers mail daily. = 邮政服务每天投递邮件。
He sends postal money orders regularly. = 他定期邮寄汇款。


一般过去时:
They sent the package via postal service. = 他们通过邮政服务寄送包裹。
She received a postal card from her friend. = 她收到朋友寄来的一张明信片。


现在完成时:
