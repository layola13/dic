dysfunction:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃən/| n. |功能障碍；机能障碍；故障；失调 |The inability of a system or organ to function normally.  It often implies a failure or impairment of a process or system. |例句：The team's dysfunction led to missed deadlines and poor performance. 团队的功能障碍导致了错过最后期限和糟糕的表现。

例句：His emotional dysfunction stemmed from childhood trauma. 他的情感障碍源于童年创伤。


近义词：malfunction, impairment, failure, breakdown, disorder |反义词：function, proper operation, efficiency
