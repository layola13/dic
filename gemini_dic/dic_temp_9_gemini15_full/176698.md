reactionally:/riˈækʃənəli/| adv.|  反应性地；因反应而；出于反应|  In a manner relating to or resulting from a reaction. It suggests an action or behavior is a direct response to something else, often impulsively or without much forethought. |例句：He reacted emotionally and reactionally to the news. 他对这个消息做出了情绪化和反应性的回应。  |近义词：reactively, impulsively, spontaneously|反义词：deliberately, thoughtfully, proactively

例句：She reactionally slammed the door shut after the argument.  她争吵后反应性地猛地关上了门。
