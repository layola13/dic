multicircuit:/ˈmʌltiˈsɜːrkɪt/| adj.|多电路的，多回路的|Having or involving more than one circuit.|The multicircuit breaker protects the entire system from overload.|近义词：multiloop, polycircuit|反义词：single-circuit


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  The usage focuses on its descriptive nature within sentences.

Example sentences illustrating its use:


A multicircuit board is required for this complex electronic device. = 这个复杂的电子设备需要一块多电路板。

The engineers designed a multicircuit system to enhance redundancy and reliability. = 工程师们设计了一个多电路系统以增强冗余性和可靠性。
