nosocomial:/ˌnəʊsəˈkoʊmiəl/ | adj. |医院的；医院感染的 | Originating in a hospital.  Relating to or occurring in a hospital.  Specifically referring to infections acquired in a hospital.  | The patient developed a nosocomial infection after surgery.  这位病人术后感染了医院感染。 | hospital-acquired, healthcare-associated |  healthy, sterile |  形容词
