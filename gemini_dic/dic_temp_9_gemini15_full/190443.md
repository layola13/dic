sashes:/ˈsæʃɪz/| n.| 佩带, 腰带；窗扇 | A sash is a long strip of cloth worn around the waist or over the shoulder, often as part of a uniform or ceremonial dress. It can also refer to a window sash, the framework holding the panes of glass. |例句：The bride wore a beautiful sash.新娘穿了一条美丽的腰带。The window sashes were painted white. 窗扇被漆成了白色。|近义词：belt, ribbon, band|反义词：None.
