haemorrhaged: /ˈhɛməˌrɪdʒd/ | v. | 大出血，大量流血 | To lose a large amount of blood in a short time, typically as a result of an injury or medical condition. | The patient haemorrhaged profusely after the accident.  病人事故后大量出血。|  bleed heavily, gush |  stop bleeding, clot |动词
