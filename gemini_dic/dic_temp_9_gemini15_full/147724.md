overpriced:/ˌoʊvərˈpraɪst/| adj. |价格过高的，定价过高的 |The adjective "overpriced" describes something that costs more than it is worth or more than is considered reasonable. It implies that the price is excessively high relative to the quality, value, or market price of the item or service.  |例句：That restaurant is overpriced; the food wasn't even that good. 那家餐馆价格过高；食物甚至不怎么样。

The hotel room was overpriced for what it offered.  酒店房间的价格与其提供的服务不相符，价格过高。|近义词：expensive, costly, exorbitant, dear |反义词：cheap, inexpensive, affordable, bargain
