possess:/pəˈzɛs/ | vt. |拥有；具有；支配；控制 | to have or own something, or to have a particular quality | He possesses a large fortune. 他拥有巨额财富。  She possesses great intelligence. 她非常聪明。|近义词：own, have, control | 反义词：lack, be without


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He possesses a rare talent. = 他拥有罕见的才能。
They possess several properties in the city. = 他们在城里拥有几处房产。


一般过去时:
She possessed a strong will. = 她意志坚定。
He possessed a remarkable collection of stamps. = 他拥有一个非凡的邮票收藏。


现在完成时:
I have possessed this watch for twenty years. = 我拥有这块手表二十年了。
They have possessed that land for generations. = 他们世世代代拥有那块土地。


过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had possessed a vibrant personality. = 事故发生前，他性格开朗。
She had possessed the painting for many years before selling it. = 在卖掉之前，她拥有那幅画很多年了。


将来时:
He will possess even greater wealth in the future. = 他将来会拥有更大的财富。
They will possess the necessary skills for the job. = 他们将具备胜任这份工作所需的技能。


虚拟语气:
If I possessed a magic wand, I would grant you your wish. = 如果我有一根魔杖，我会满足你的愿望。
I wish I possessed the ability to fly. = 我希望我有飞翔的能力。


被动语态:
The land was possessed by the king. = 这块土地为国王所有。
The house is possessed by a strange energy. = 这房子充满了奇怪的能量。


疑问句:
Does he possess the necessary qualifications? = 他具备必要的资格吗？
What qualities do you possess that make you suitable for this job? = 你有什么特质使你适合这份工作？


否定句:
He does not possess the required experience. = 他不具备所需的经验。
She did not possess the courage to tell the truth. = 她没有勇气说出真相。
