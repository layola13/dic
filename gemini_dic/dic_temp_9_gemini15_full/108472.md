lay low: /leɪ loʊ/ | phrasal verb | 隐藏；保持低调；避免惹麻烦 | To keep a low profile; to avoid attention or trouble. | 例句：After the robbery, the thieves decided to lay low for a while.  抢劫之后，小偷们决定暂时低调行事。


例句：The witness decided to lay low after testifying in court.  证人在法庭作证后决定保持低调。


近义词：hide, conceal, keep a low profile, lie low, keep quiet


反义词：show off, flaunt, be prominent, attract attention


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lay low to avoid being caught. = 他们为了避免被抓住而保持低调。
We lay low during the storm. = 我们在暴风雨期间保持低调。


一般过去时:
The suspect laid low after the crime. = 犯罪嫌疑人在犯罪后保持低调。
He laid low in the mountains for several months. = 他在山里躲藏了几个月。


现在完成时:
They have laid low since the scandal broke. = 自从丑闻爆发以来，他们就一直保持低调。
She has laid low to protect her family. = 她为了保护家人而一直保持低调。


过去完成时:
They had laid low for weeks before the police found them. = 警察找到他们之前，他们已经躲藏了好几个星期了。
He had laid low all summer, avoiding his creditors. = 他整个夏天都躲藏起来，躲避债主。


将来时:
They will lay low until the situation improves. = 他们会保持低调，直到情况好转。
We will lay low and wait for the right moment. = 我们会保持低调，等待合适的时机。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would lay low for a while. = 如果我是你，我会暂时保持低调。
I wish I had laid low instead of arguing with him. = 我真希望当时我没有和他争吵，而是保持低调。


被动语态: (Lay low doesn't typically take a passive voice.)


疑问句:
Should we lay low until the police investigation is over? = 我们应该在警方调查结束之前保持低调吗？
Are they laying low to avoid the media? = 他们为了躲避媒体而保持低调吗？


否定句:
They didn't lay low; they went out partying. = 他们没有保持低调；他们出去狂欢了。
She wouldn't lay low; she wanted to confront the issue head-on. = 她不会保持低调；她想正面应对这个问题。


PHRASE->
lay low for a while = 暂时保持低调
lay low until... = 直到...才保持低调
lay low to avoid... = 为了避免...而保持低调
lay low after... = 在...之后保持低调
lay low and wait = 保持低调并等待
