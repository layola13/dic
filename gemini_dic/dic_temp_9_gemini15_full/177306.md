reassuming:/ˌriːəˈsuːmɪŋ/| v. |重新承担；再次担任；重新开始|  The act of resuming or beginning again something that was interrupted or stopped. It implies a return to a previous state or activity. |例句：After a brief hiatus, he is reasuming his duties as CEO.  在短暂中断后，他重新担任首席执行官的职务。

例句：She is reasuming her painting after a long break.  她休息很久后重新开始绘画。


近义词：resuming, restarting, recommencing |反义词：abandoning, ceasing, stopping


词性：动词
