misgoverns:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnz/| vt.|施政不善；治理不当|To govern badly or unfairly; to administer or manage inefficiently or corruptly.|例句：The country has been misgoverned for decades, leading to widespread poverty and inequality.  这个国家几十年来治理不善，导致了普遍的贫困和不平等。|例句：The company was misgoverned by its CEO, resulting in significant financial losses. 这家公司由其首席执行官治理不善，导致了巨大的经济损失。|近义词：maladminister, mismanage,  rule badly|反义词：govern well, administer effectively, manage efficiently|动词
