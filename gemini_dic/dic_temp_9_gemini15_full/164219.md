predominance:/prɪˈdɒmɪnəns/|/ˈprɛdəmənəns/| n. |优势；支配；卓越；主导地位 |The state or fact of being predominant; superiority in numbers, power, status, or influence.|The predominance of one political party led to a lack of diverse viewpoints.  一个政党的优势地位导致缺乏多样化的观点。

The predominance of evidence pointed to his guilt.  证据的优势表明他有罪。


近义词：superiority, supremacy, ascendancy, dominance, mastery|反义词：subordination, inferiority, minority
