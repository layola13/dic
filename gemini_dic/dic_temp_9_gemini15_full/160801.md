posits:/ˈpɒzɪts/ /ˈpɑːzɪts/| vt.|假定；断定；提出；认为|To put forward as a fact or proposition; to assume as a basis; to suggest or propose.|The theory posits that the universe began with a Big Bang.  该理论假定宇宙起源于大爆炸。|He posits a connection between poverty and crime. 他认为贫困和犯罪之间存在联系。|assume, propose, suggest, assert|deny, refute, contradict|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist posits a new theory. = 科学家提出一个新的理论。
She posits that the results are significant. = 她认为结果是显著的。


一般过去时:
He posited a hypothesis for the experiment. = 他为实验提出了一个假设。
They posited a different explanation for the phenomenon. = 他们对这一现象提出了不同的解释。


现在完成时:
Researchers have posited several explanations for the disease. = 研究人员已经提出了几种关于这种疾病的解释。
Scientists have posited that climate change is accelerating. = 科学家们已经提出气候变化正在加速。


过去完成时:
They had posited that the reaction would be exothermic before conducting the experiment. = 在进行实验之前，他们已经假定反应会放热。
She had posited a different solution to the problem earlier. = 她之前已经提出了一个不同的解决方案。


将来时:
The professor will posit a new model in his lecture. = 教授将在他的讲座中提出一个新的模型。
They will posit that the findings are inconclusive. = 他们将认为这些发现是不确定的。


虚拟语气:
If he posited a different scenario, the outcome might be different. = 如果他假设不同的情况，结果可能会不同。
I wish he had posited a more realistic solution. = 我希望他提出了一个更现实的解决方案。


被动语态:
A new theory was posited by the researchers. = 研究人员提出了一种新的理论。
Different explanations have been posited for the phenomenon. = 对这一现象已经提出了不同的解释。


疑问句:
What did the author posit in his book? = 作者在他的书中提出了什么？
What hypothesis did they posit for their research? = 他们为他们的研究提出了什么假设？


否定句:
The study did not posit a causal relationship. = 这项研究没有假定因果关系。
He didn't posit any concrete evidence to support his claim. = 他没有提出任何具体的证据来支持他的说法。


PHRASE->

posit a solution = 提出一个解决方案
