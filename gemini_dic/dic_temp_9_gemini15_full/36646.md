collects:/kəˈlɛkts/|v.|收集，采集|To gather or bring together.  It implies accumulating items of the same kind or type.|例句：She collects stamps. 她收集邮票。

例句：The museum collects ancient artifacts. 这个博物馆收集古代文物。


近义词：gather, accumulate, assemble, amass

反义词：disperse, scatter, distribute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collects coins. = 他收集硬币。
She collects antique furniture. = 她收集古董家具。

一般过去时:
They collected donations for charity. = 他们为慈善机构募集捐款。
He collected all the scattered papers. = 他收集了所有散落的纸张。

现在完成时:
I have collected many different types of seashells. = 我已经收集了许多不同种类的贝壳。
She has collected enough evidence to prove her point. = 她已经收集到足够的证据来证明她的观点。

过去完成时:
Before the exhibition, they had collected all the paintings. = 展览前，他们已经收集了所有的画作。
By the time the storm hit, he had collected all the outdoor furniture. = 暴风雨来临之前，他已经把所有的户外家具都收起来了。

将来时:
We will collect the children from school. = 我们将从学校接孩子们。
He will collect the package tomorrow. = 他明天将取包裹。

虚拟语气:
If I collected enough money, I would buy a new car. = 如果我攒够钱，我会买一辆新车。
I wish I had collected more data for my research. = 我真希望我为我的研究收集了更多数据。

被动语态:
The data is collected automatically. = 数据是自动收集的。
The donations were collected by volunteers. = 捐款是由志愿者收集的。

疑问句:
Does he collect anything special? = 他收集什么特别的东西吗？
What kind of books does she collect? = 她收集什么类型的书？

否定句:
He doesn't collect stamps anymore. = 他不再收集邮票了。
She didn't collect the package yesterday. = 她昨天没有取包裹。
