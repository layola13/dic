mercy-killings:/ˈmɜːrsi ˈkɪlɪŋz/| n. |安乐死；仁慈的杀戮|The act of killing someone painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable and painful disease.  This term is often used in the context of euthanasia, where a doctor or other medical professional ends the life of a patient to alleviate unbearable suffering.|例句：The debate surrounding mercy killings is complex and deeply emotional.  围绕安乐死的辩论复杂且充满情感。

例句：Mercy killings are illegal in many countries, but some people believe they should be legalized under specific circumstances.  在许多国家，安乐死是非法的，但有些人认为在特定情况下应该合法化。


近义词：euthanasia, assisted suicide, mercy death|反义词：murder, homicide, manslaughter|词性:noun
