glamourised:/ˈɡlæməraɪzd/| v. |使魅力四射；美化；渲染；理想化 | To make something seem more attractive, exciting, or glamorous than it really is.  |The film glamourised the life of a spy. 这部电影美化了间谍的生活。 |近义词：romanticized, idealized, romanticized, embellished |反义词：demythologized, denigrated
