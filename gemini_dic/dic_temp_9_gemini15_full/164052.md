predisclosing: /ˌpriːdɪˈskloʊzɪŋ/ | v. | 预先披露；提前公开 |  The act of disclosing information before it is officially or typically revealed. This often involves sharing information in advance of a formal announcement or event.  |例句：The company engaged in predisclosing its financial results to selected investors. 公司对特定投资者提前披露了财务业绩。

例句：Predisclosing sensitive information can lead to market manipulation. 提前披露敏感信息可能导致市场操纵。


近义词：pre-releasing, preannouncing, leaking, divulging


反义词：concealing, withholding, suppressing, keeping secret


词性:动词
