fragmenting:/ˈfræɡmɛntɪŋ/| v.|碎片化；分裂；分割|The process of breaking something into smaller pieces or parts.  It can refer to physical objects, data, or even social structures. |例句：The hard drive is fragmenting, causing slow performance.  硬盘正在碎片化，导致性能缓慢。
The company is fragmenting into smaller, independent units. 这家公司正在分裂成更小的独立部门。|近义词：shattering, dividing, splitting, breaking up|反义词：unifying, integrating, consolidating|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The software is fragmenting the large file. = 软件正在将大型文件分割。
The empire is fragmenting into smaller kingdoms. = 帝国正在分裂成较小的王国。


一般过去时:
The vase fragmented when it fell to the floor. = 花瓶掉到地上摔碎了。
The ancient civilization fragmented over centuries. = 古代文明经过几个世纪而分裂。


现在完成时:
The hard drive has fragmented significantly. = 硬盘已经严重碎片化。
The political landscape has fragmented since the election. = 自选举以来，政治格局已经四分五裂。


过去完成时:
The system had fragmented before the backup was initiated. = 系统在备份启动之前就已经碎片化了。
The community had fragmented long before the conflict began. = 在冲突开始很久以前，社区就已经分裂了。


将来时:
The glacier will fragment as the temperature rises. = 随着温度升高，冰川将会崩裂。
The market will likely fragment further with the introduction of new technologies. = 随着新技术的引入，市场可能会进一步碎片化。


虚拟语气:
If the data were fragmented, recovery would be difficult. = 如果数据碎片化了，恢复将很困难。
I wish the project hadn't fragmented so badly. = 我真希望项目没有这么严重地支离破碎。


被动语态:
The image was fragmented by the impact. = 图像被撞击碎片化了。
The land was fragmented by the construction of the highway. = 土地被高速公路的建设分割了。


疑问句:
Is the database fragmenting? = 数据库是否正在碎片化？
Are the social groups fragmenting? = 社会群体是否正在分裂？


否定句:
The glass didn't fragment; it remained intact. = 玻璃没有碎裂；它保持完整。
The team didn't fragment despite the disagreements. = 尽管存在分歧，团队并没有分裂。
