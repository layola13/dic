prequalifying: /ˌpriːˈkwɑːlɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 预选的；预先资格审查的 |  Determining eligibility before full application.  The process involves assessing candidates based on preliminary criteria to filter out those who clearly don't meet the minimum requirements. This saves time and resources by focusing on more promising applicants. |

例句：

The prequalifying stage of the loan application requires you to submit your credit score and income verification. = 贷款申请的预选阶段需要您提交您的信用评分和收入证明。

The company uses a prequalifying questionnaire to screen job applicants. = 公司使用预选问卷来筛选求职者。


近义词： preliminary, initial, screening


反义词： final, conclusive


词性：形容词
