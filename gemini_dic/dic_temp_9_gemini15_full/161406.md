postulation:/ˌpɒstʃʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 假定；假设；提出；要求 | The act of postulating; an assumption or proposition.  It can refer to the act of putting forward a theory or suggestion, or the theory or suggestion itself. | Example: The scientist's postulation of a new theory revolutionized the field.  这位科学家的新理论假设彻底改变了该领域。

The postulation of a new particle is still under investigation. 对一种新的粒子的假设仍在调查中。 |近义词：assumption, hypothesis, proposition, supposition |反义词：proof, verification, confirmation |  词性：名词
