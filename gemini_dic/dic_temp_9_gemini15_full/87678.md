The faces can be any polygon, as long as there are six of them. |Example: A cube is a type of hexahedron.  一个立方体是一种六面体。|近义词: six-sided solid, polyhedron (more general)|反义词:  None (it's a category, not a specific shape with a direct opposite)|名词
