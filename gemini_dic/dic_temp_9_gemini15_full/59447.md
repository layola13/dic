earthworms:/ˈɜːθwɜːmz/| n. |蚯蚓|Earthworms are segmented worms that live in the soil. They are important for soil health because they help to aerate the soil and improve its drainage. |The gardener added earthworms to the soil to improve its fertility. 园丁往土壤里添加蚯蚓以提高肥力。|nightcrawlers, angleworms|None|noun
