coordinate: /koʊˈɔːrdɪnət/ | n., v. | 坐标；协调；使协调 |

英文解析:As a noun, a coordinate refers to one of a set of numbers that determine the position of a point, line, or plane.  As a verb, it means to bring the different parts of something into a consistent relationship.  It can also mean to work together effectively.


例句(Noun):

The coordinates of the treasure are 34°N, 118°W.  宝藏的坐标是北纬34度，西经118度。

We need to find the exact coordinates of the building before we start construction. 我们需要在开始建设之前找到建筑物的精确坐标。


例句(Verb):

The team worked together to coordinate their efforts. 团队通力合作以协调他们的工作。

It's important to coordinate your schedule with your colleagues'.  协调你的日程安排与同事们的是很重要的。


近义词: (Noun) position, location; (Verb) harmonize, synchronize, integrate, collaborate


反义词: (Verb) disorganize, clash, conflict


词源: From Latin *coordinatus, past participle of coordinare "to arrange together," from com- "together" + ordinare "to arrange," from ordo "order."
