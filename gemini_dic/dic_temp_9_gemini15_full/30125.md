ceding:/ˈsiːdɪŋ/| v.|放弃，割让，让步|The act of giving something up or surrendering something to someone else.  It often implies a reluctant relinquishing of something that is valuable or important. |例句：The company ceded control of the project to its partner. 公司将项目的控制权让给了合作伙伴。

例句：After much negotiation, they ceded to his demands.经过多次协商，他们最终同意了他的要求。


近义词：yielding, relinquishing, surrendering, giving up|反义词：claiming, retaining, keeping, seizing


词源：源自古法语 *cedier，意为“给予，让步”，最终源于拉丁语 *cedere，意为“走，退却，让步”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cedes his rights to the property. 他放弃了他对这处房产的权利。
The government cedes control to the local authorities. 政府将控制权让渡给地方当局。


一般过去时:
She ceded her position to a younger colleague. 她把自己的职位让给了更年轻的同事。
The king ceded the territory to the neighboring country. 国王将领土割让给了邻国。


现在完成时:
They have ceded their land to developers. 他们已将土地让给了开发商。
The company has ceded market share to its competitors. 这家公司已经将其市场份额让给了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
He had ceded his authority before the crisis. 在危机爆发前，他已经放弃了自己的权力。
The empire had ceded vast territories by the end of the war. 到战争结束时，帝国已经割让了大片领土。


将来时:
She will cede her power to her successor. 她将把权力移交给她的继任者。
The company will likely cede some market share next year. 公司明年可能会失去一些市场份额。


虚拟语气:
If he ceded his claim, the conflict would end. 如果他放弃他的要求，冲突就会结束。
I wish he had ceded to reason instead of anger. 我希望他当时能顺从理性而不是愤怒。


被动语态:
The land was ceded to the new government. 这块土地被割让给了新政府。
Control of the company was ceded to the shareholders. 公司的控制权被让渡给了股东。


疑问句:
Will they cede to pressure from the opposition? 他们会屈服于反对派的压力吗？
Has the government ceded any territory recently? 政府最近是否割让了任何领土？


否定句:
He refused to cede his power. 他拒绝放弃他的权力。
They would not cede an inch of ground. 他们寸步不让。
