individualization: /ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |个性化；个体化；个人化 | The process of becoming or making something individual; the adaptation of something to the needs of an individual.  It emphasizes the unique characteristics and needs of a single person or thing. |例句：The school encourages individualization of learning to cater to diverse student needs. 学校鼓励个性化学习以满足不同学生的需要。

例句：The company's marketing strategy focuses on product individualization to attract customers. 该公司的营销策略侧重于产品个性化以吸引顾客。


近义词：personalization, customization, differentiation | 反义词：standardization, uniformity
