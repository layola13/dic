progressive:/prəˈɡrɛsɪv/ | adj. |进步的；先进的；渐进的 |  Adjective describing something that is developing or improving gradually, or that supports such development.  It can also refer to a political or social philosophy advocating for gradual change and reform. |例句：He holds progressive views on social issues. 他对社会问题持有进步的观点。

例句：The company has a progressive approach to employee training. 这家公司对员工培训采取了先进的方法。


近义词：advanced, forward-looking, modern, liberal

反义词：conservative, reactionary, regressive, traditional


词源:  From Middle French *progressif, from Latin progressus, past participle of progredi "to advance," from pro- "forward" + gradi "to step."
