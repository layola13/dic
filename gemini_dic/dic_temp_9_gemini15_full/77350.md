gaudiness:/ˈɡɔːdinɪs/| n. |俗丽，炫耀，过分艳丽 | Excessive or tasteless showiness of ornament.  It suggests an overabundance of bright colors, flashy decorations, or excessive ornamentation that is considered vulgar or lacking in good taste. |The gaudiness of the decorations was overwhelming.  那装饰的俗丽令人难以忍受。|The gaudy colours clashed horribly.  那些俗丽的颜色互相冲突得厉害。|近义词：ostentation, flashiness, flamboyance, vulgarity |反义词：simplicity, elegance, subtlety, refinement
