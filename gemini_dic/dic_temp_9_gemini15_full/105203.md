keying:/ˈkiːɪŋ/ | n. v. |按键；键入；调谐；关键；兴奋；|The act of pressing keys on a keyboard or similar device; the process of inputting data or information by pressing keys;  the act of tuning or adjusting a musical instrument;  a crucial element or factor;  a state of intense excitement or exhilaration. |例句：The keying of the data took several hours.  数据录入花费了几个小时。|例句： The pianist carefully keying the delicate melody.  钢琴家仔细地弹奏着精致的旋律。 |近义词：typing, inputting, tuning, adjusting |反义词：outputting, deleting
