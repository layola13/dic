quasi-discriminating: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 准歧视的，似歧视的 |  Approaching or resembling discrimination; exhibiting characteristics suggestive of, but not fully meeting the criteria for, discrimination.  It implies a subtle or indirect form of discrimination, often unintentional or difficult to prove. |


例句：

The company's hiring practices, while not overtly discriminatory, were deemed quasi-discriminating due to the disproportionate number of male applicants selected.  公司的招聘措施虽然并非公开歧视，但由于男性申请者被选中的人数不成比例，因此被认为是准歧视行为。


The study revealed a quasi-discriminating effect of the new policy on low-income families.  这项研究揭示了新政策对低收入家庭的准歧视性影响。


近义词： indirectly discriminatory, subtly biased, implicitly unfair


反义词：  nondiscriminatory, impartial, unbiased


词性：形容词
