concentrator: /ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtər/ /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtər/ | n. |精选器；浓缩器；精矿机；聚精会神者 | A device or process that concentrates something, typically a substance or energy.  For example, a device that increases the concentration of an ore or a machine that increases the concentration of a particular substance.  It can also refer to a person who is very focused and dedicated to a task. |


例句：

The gold concentrator processed the ore efficiently.  = 淘金精选器高效地处理了矿石。

The new solar concentrator increased energy output by 20%. = 新型太阳能聚光器使能量输出提高了20%。


近义词：concentrator,  refiner,  purifier


反义词：disperser,  diluter


词性：名词
