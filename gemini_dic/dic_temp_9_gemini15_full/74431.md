francisco:/frænˈsɪskoʊ/| Proper Noun |弗朗西斯科，旧金山|A Spanish given name, and the name of a city in California.  It is derived from the name Francis, ultimately from the Roman name Franciscus.|例句：My friend's name is Francisco. 我的朋友名叫弗朗西斯科。

例句：San Francisco is a beautiful city. 旧金山是一个美丽的城市。

近义词：Francis (given name), San Francisco (city)
反义词：None (as a proper noun)
词性：Proper Noun
