matting:/ˈmætɪŋ/| n.|席子；垫子；缠绕物|Woven or plaited material used as a floor covering, or for other purposes such as wrapping.  It can also refer to the process of covering something with matting.|The floor was covered with a thick matting.|近义词：rug, carpeting, covering|反义词：bare
