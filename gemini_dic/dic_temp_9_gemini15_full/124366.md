motivation: /ˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 动机；动力；积极性 | The reason or reasons one has for acting or behaving in a particular way.  It can refer to the internal drive to achieve something or the external factors that influence one's actions. | Example:  His motivation for studying hard was to get a scholarship. 他努力学习的动力是为了获得奖学金。

His lack of motivation led to poor performance at work. 他缺乏动力导致工作表现不佳。|近义词：incentive, drive, impulse, stimulus |反义词：apathy, inertia, reluctance


USAGE->

What motivates you to work so hard? = 什么激励你如此努力工作？
His motivation stems from a deep desire to help others. = 他的动力源于强烈的助人愿望。

虚拟语气:
