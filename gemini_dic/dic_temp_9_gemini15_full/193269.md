seeds:/siːdz/| n.|种子；萌芽；起源|The seeds of doubt were sown in his mind. 怀疑的种子在他心中播下。|近义词：germs, kernels, origins|反义词：fruits, results


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The farmer plants seeds every spring. = 农民每年春天都播种。
These seeds need sunlight to germinate. = 这些种子需要阳光才能发芽。


一般过去时:
She scattered the seeds across the field. = 她把种子撒在田里。
We planted sunflower seeds last summer. = 我们去年夏天种了向日葵种子。


现在完成时:
The gardener has already sown the seeds. = 园丁已经播种了。
Have you collected all the seeds yet? = 你已经收集好所有的种子了吗？


过去完成时:
They had planted the seeds before the rain came. = 下雨之前他们已经播种了。
The birds had eaten most of the seeds. = 鸟儿已经吃了大部分的种子。


将来时:
We will sow the seeds next week. = 我们下周将播种。
They will harvest the seeds in the autumn. = 他们将在秋季收获种子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more seeds, I would plant a bigger garden. = 如果我有更多的种子，我会种一个更大的花园。
I wish I had planted the seeds earlier. = 我希望我早点播种。


被动语态:
The seeds were planted by the farmer. = 种子是由农民种植的。
The seeds are being watered regularly. = 种子正在定期浇水。


疑问句:
What kind of seeds are these? = 这是什么种类的种子？
Where did you get these seeds? = 你从哪里弄到这些种子的？


否定句:

We don't have enough seeds for the whole field. = 我们没有足够的种子来播种整个田地。
