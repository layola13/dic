combativeness:/ˌkɑːmˈbætɪvnəs/|/ˌkɒmˈbætɪvnəs/| n. |好斗性；好战性；好胜心 |A disposition or tendency to fight or be aggressive.  It refers to a readiness or eagerness to engage in conflict or competition. |例句：His combativeness surprised everyone.  他的好斗性让每个人都吃了一惊。

例句：The boxer displayed remarkable combativeness in the ring.  这位拳击手在拳台上展现了非凡的好斗性。


近义词：aggression, belligerence, pugnacity, hostility, contentiousness

反义词：passivity, docility, submissiveness, meekness, amiability


词源:  Derived from the word "combative," which itself comes from the Old French word "combatif," meaning "ready to fight."  The suffix "-ness" is added to create the noun form, indicating a state or quality.
