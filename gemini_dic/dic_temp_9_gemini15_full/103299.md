jibes:/dʒaɪbz/| v. n.|嘲笑；符合；一致；|To make fun of someone; to be in agreement or harmony.  Also, a nautical term referring to the boom of a sailing ship.|The comedian jibed at the politician's expense.  The two accounts of the event jibe. The ship's jibes were damaged in the storm. |近义词：taunt, mock, jeer, agree, correspond|反义词：praise, compliment, disagree, conflict|动词，名词
