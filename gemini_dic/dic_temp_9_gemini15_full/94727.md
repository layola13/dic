improsperous:/ɪmˈprɒspərəs/ | adj. | 不景气的；不成功的；贫穷的 | Not prosperous; unsuccessful; poor.  The word suggests a lack of financial success and often implies a state of decline or hardship. |例句：The once-thriving town is now improverished and improsperous.  曾经繁荣的小镇现在贫穷落魄。

The improsperous farmer lost his entire crop to a late frost.  这个不景气的农民因为晚霜而损失了全部收成。


近义词：unsuccessful, failing, impoverished, poor, bankrupt, destitute | 反义词：prosperous, successful, thriving, flourishing, affluent, wealthy
