overreaches:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃɪz/| v.| 权限过大；越权；超出范围；力所不及 |To exceed one's authority or limits; to try to do too much.  |例句：The government's attempt to control the media overreached its authority. 政府试图控制媒体的行为已超越其权限。  The company overreached in its expansion plans and suffered financially. 该公司在扩张计划中做得过火，最终遭受了经济损失。|近义词：exceed, trespass, overstep, go too far |反义词：underestimate, restrain, limit

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company overreaches its market. = 这家公司市场拓展过度。
He often overreaches in his statements. = 他说话经常言过其实。


一般过去时:
The dictator overreached in his attempts to control the population. = 独裁者试图控制人民的企图最终失败了。
The lawyer overreached during the cross-examination. = 律师在盘问过程中太过火了。


现在完成时:
The team has overreached in its ambitions this season. = 本赛季球队野心勃勃，最终却力不从心。
She has overreached herself by taking on too many projects. = 她承担了太多的项目，最终力不从心。


过去完成时:
They had overreached in their investment strategy and lost a lot of money. = 他们的投资策略过于激进，最终损失惨重。
The general had overreached his authority by ordering the attack without permission. = 将军未经允许就下令进攻，这已经越权了。


将来时:

He will overreach if he continues to work at this pace. = 如果他继续以这种速度工作，他将会筋疲力尽。


虚拟语气:

If he hadn't overreached, he would have won the debate. = 如果他没有言过其实，他就能赢得辩论。


被动语态:

The proposal was deemed to have overreached the scope of the project. = 该提案被认为超出了项目的范围。


疑问句:

否定句:
The manager didn't overreach in his delegation of tasks. = 经理在任务分配方面并没有做得过火。
They didn't overreach in their fundraising efforts. = 他们的筹款工作并没有做得过火。
