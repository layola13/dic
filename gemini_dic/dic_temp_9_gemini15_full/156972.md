pillaged:/ˈpɪlɪdʒd/| v.|洗劫，掠夺，抢劫 |To violently rob and take goods from a place, especially during a war.  |例句：The invading army pillaged the city, leaving it in ruins. 侵略军洗劫了这座城市，把它夷为平地。

例句：The museum was pillaged by thieves, and many artifacts were lost forever.  博物馆被盗贼洗劫一空，许多文物永远丢失了。


近义词：plunder, loot, ransack, despoil |反义词：protect, conserve, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pillage villages for food and supplies. = 他们洗劫村庄以获取食物和补给。
The pirates pillage ships on the high seas. = 海盗在公海上洗劫船只。


一般过去时:
The Vikings pillaged the coastal towns. = 维京人洗劫了沿海城镇。
The army pillaged the enemy's capital. = 军队洗劫了敌人的首都。


现在完成时:
The rebels have pillaged several towns in the region. = 叛军已经洗劫了该地区几个城镇。
The bandits have pillaged many homes. = 强盗已经洗劫了许多房屋。


过去完成时:
The villagers had been pillaged before the soldiers arrived. = 士兵到达之前，村民们已经被洗劫一空了。
The city had already been pillaged by the time the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达时，城市已经被洗劫一空了。


将来时:
The invaders will pillage the countryside. = 侵略者将洗劫乡村。
They will pillage the city if we don't surrender. = 如果我们不投降，他们将洗劫这座城市。


虚拟语气:
If they had pillaged the town, they would have found treasure. = 如果他们洗劫了小镇，他们会发现宝藏。
I wish they hadn't pillaged the village. = 我真希望他们没有洗劫那个村庄。


被动语态:
The town was pillaged by the invading army. = 这个小镇被侵略军洗劫了。
Many homes were pillaged during the riot. = 暴乱期间许多房屋被洗劫一空。


疑问句:
Were the villages pillaged by the enemy? = 村庄是被敌人洗劫的吗？
Has the city been pillaged? = 城市被洗劫了吗？


否定句:
The soldiers did not pillage the town. = 士兵们没有洗劫这座城镇。
The army did not pillage the civilian population. = 军队没有洗劫平民百姓。
