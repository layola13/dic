overprotecting: /ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度保护的 | Excessively protective; shielding someone from any potential harm or challenge, often to the detriment of their development and independence.  |The overprotecting parents kept their child from playing outdoors.|近义词：oversheltering, excessively cautious, hyper-protective |反义词：neglectful, permissive, laissez-faire |形容词
