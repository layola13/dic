perichete:/ˈpɛrɪkiːt/| n. | 长尾小鹦鹉 |A small, often brightly colored parrot with a long tail.  Perichetes are known for their ability to mimic sounds and their playful nature.  | Example: The perichete perched on the branch, its vibrant feathers gleaming in the sunlight.  这只长尾小鹦鹉栖息在树枝上，它鲜艳的羽毛在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：parakeet, lovebird |反义词：无
