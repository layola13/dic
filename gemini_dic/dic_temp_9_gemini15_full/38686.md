condensations:/ˌkɒndənˈseɪʃənz/ | n. | 冷凝(现象)，浓缩 | The process by which a gas changes into a liquid.  It often refers to the formation of water droplets from water vapor.  The term can also refer to the act of making something more concise or shorter. | 例句：The condensation on the window made it difficult to see.  窗户上的冷凝水使得难以看清。| 例句：The professor provided a condensation of the lengthy research paper. 教授对冗长的研究论文进行了浓缩。|近义词：condensing,liquefaction,abridgment,summary |反义词：evaporation, expansion
