pro-Nigerians: /ˌproʊ naɪˈdʒɪəriənz/ | adj. n. | 支持尼日利亚人的；拥护尼日利亚的；尼日利亚支持者 |  This term refers to individuals or groups who advocate for the interests and well-being of Nigerians. It can describe policies, movements, or people expressing support for Nigeria or its citizens.  The term itself is a compound word combining "pro-" (meaning "in favor of" or "supporting") and "Nigerians" (citizens of Nigeria).  There is no direct antonym, as opposition would be expressed through different terms depending on the context (e.g., anti-Nigerian government, etc.).


例句：

Many pro-Nigerians groups actively campaign for better governance in the country. 许多支持尼日利亚人的团体积极为国家更好的治理而奔走。

The pro-Nigerians sentiment was high during the recent FIFA World Cup. 在最近的国际足联世界杯期间，支持尼日利亚人的情绪很高。


近义词： pro-Nigeria, Nigerian supporters, Nigeria advocates


反义词：  (No direct antonym;  opposition would be expressed differently depending on the context, for example: anti-government, anti-Nigerian policies)


词性:  形容词(修饰名词) 和名词 (指人或群体)
