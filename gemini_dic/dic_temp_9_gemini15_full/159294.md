pointalism:/ˈpɔɪntəlɪzəm/| n. |点彩画法 |A painting technique in which small, distinct dots of color are applied in patterns to form an image.  The overall impression of color and form is created by the viewer's eye mixing the colors from a distance. |The painting is a striking example of pointillism, with its vibrant dots of color. 这幅画是点彩画法的突出例子，色彩鲜艳的点十分醒目。|impressionism, divisionism |abstract painting,  brushstroke painting| 名词
