moths:/mɒθs/| n.|飞蛾|A moth is a nocturnal insect with wings, closely related to butterflies. Moths are generally active at night, unlike butterflies, which are usually diurnal.  They often have duller colours than butterflies and are attracted to light.|例句：The moths were fluttering around the lamp. 飞蛾在灯周围飞舞。

例句：I saw several moths flying near the porch light last night. 昨晚我看到几只飞蛾在门廊灯附近飞舞。


近义词：butterfly (though with key differences), insect


反义词：无


词性:名词
