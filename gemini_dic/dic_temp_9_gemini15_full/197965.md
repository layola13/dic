shivering: /ˈʃɪvərɪŋ/ | adj. |颤抖的，发抖的 | Trembling or shaking with cold, fear, or excitement.  |例句：She was shivering with cold. 她冻得发抖。|例句：He stood shivering in the doorway. 他颤抖地站在门口。|近义词：trembling, shaking, quivering |反义词：calm, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is shivering. = 他在发抖。
The leaves are shivering in the wind. = 叶子在风中颤抖。

一般过去时:
She shivered all night. = 她整夜都在发抖。
The dog shivered after its bath. = 洗完澡后，狗直发抖。

现在完成时:
I have been shivering since I got out of the pool. = 我下水后就一直发抖。
The child has shivered all day because of the fever. = 孩子因为发烧一整天都在发抖。

过去完成时:
He had shivered for hours before anyone noticed. = 好几个小时他才被人注意到一直在发抖。
She had shivered so much that her teeth were chattering. = 她抖得牙齿都打颤了。


将来时:
I think I will be shivering by the time we get to the top of the mountain. = 我想我们爬到山顶的时候我会冷得发抖。
The baby will shiver if you don't put a blanket on her. = 如果你不给宝宝盖上毯子，她会发抖的。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have a coat, I would be shivering. = 如果我没有外套，我会冷得发抖。
If she had worn warmer clothes, she wouldn't have shivered so much. = 如果她穿得暖和些，就不会抖得那么厉害了。

被动语态:
(Shivering is rarely used in the passive voice,  as it describes a state rather than an action performed on something.)


疑问句:
Are you shivering? = 你在发抖吗？
Is the dog shivering because it's cold? = 狗是因为冷才发抖的吗？

否定句:
He wasn't shivering at all. = 他一点也不冷。
She didn't shiver despite the icy wind. = 尽管寒风凛冽，她却没有发抖。


PHRASE->
shivering cold = 冻得发抖
shivering with fear = 吓得发抖
shivering with excitement = 兴奋得发抖
a shivering voice = 颤抖的声音
to shiver with laughter = 笑得发抖
