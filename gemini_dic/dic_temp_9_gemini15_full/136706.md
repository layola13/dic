nonseizure: /ˈnɒnˈsiːʒə(r)/ | adj. |非癫痫的，无癫痫发作的 |  Describing a state or event that is not a seizure;  lacking seizure activity.  The term is primarily used in the medical field to describe periods or conditions free from epileptic seizures. | Example:  The patient experienced a period of nonseizure activity after medication adjustment.  = 服药调整后，病人经历了一段无癫痫发作的时期。  The EEG showed a nonseizure pattern. =脑电图显示无癫痫发作模式。 | 近义词: seizure-free, afebrile (in the context of fever-related seizures) | 反义词: seizure-prone, epileptic


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It describes a state or condition. Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable.  Examples below show its usage in different sentence structures.


肯定句:
He had a long period of nonseizure activity. = 他有一段长时间的无癫痫发作期。
The doctor confirmed the nonseizure nature of the event. = 医生确认该事件并非癫痫发作。


否定句:
The patient did not experience any nonseizure intervals. = 病人没有经历任何无癫痫发作的间歇期。
It was not a nonseizure episode; it was a clear seizure. = 这不是非癫痫发作事件；这是一次明显的癫痫发作。


疑问句:
Was that a nonseizure event? = 那是一次非癫痫发作事件吗？
Are there any periods of nonseizure activity recorded? = 有记录任何无癫痫发作的时期吗？


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "nonseizure".  The term is highly specific to the medical field.
