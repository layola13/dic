outstudied:/ˌaʊtˈstʌdid/| v.| 被超越学习，被超过学习 | To surpass someone in studying; to study more than someone else.  |例句：He outstudied his classmates and topped the exam. 他比他的同学们学习更努力，考试得了第一名。  I was completely outstudied by my rival.  我的竞争对手在学习上远远超过了我。|近义词：surpassed, excelled, exceeded |反义词：understudied, lagged behind |动词
