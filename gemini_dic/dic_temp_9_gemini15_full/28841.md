carpels:/ˈkɑːrpəlz/ | n. | 心皮；雌蕊 | The carpels are the female reproductive organs of a flower, consisting of the stigma, style, and ovary.  They enclose and protect the ovules. | Example: The flower had three carpels, each containing several ovules. 这朵花有三个心皮，每个心皮包含几个胚珠。 |近义词：pistils (有时可互换使用，但pistil通常指整朵花的雌蕊，而carpel指单个的单位) | 反义词：stamens (雄蕊) | 名词
