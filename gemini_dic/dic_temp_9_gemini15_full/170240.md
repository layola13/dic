protruded:/proˈtruːdɪd/ | v. | 突出，伸出，隆起 | To extend beyond or above a surface; to stick out.  | The broken bone protruded through the skin.  断裂的骨头穿透了皮肤。 | The handle protruded from the bag.  把手从包里伸出来。 | jut out, stick out, bulge | recede, withdraw |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He protrudes his chin when he's angry. = 他生气时会把下巴往前伸。
The branch protrudes from the wall. = 树枝从墙上伸出来。

一般过去时:
His eyes protruded slightly after the accident. = 事故后他的眼睛略微突出。
She protruded her tongue playfully. = 她顽皮地伸出舌头。

现在完成时:
The tumor has protruded significantly in recent weeks. = 近几周肿瘤明显突出。
His stomach has protruded since he gained weight. = 他发胖后肚子就凸出来了。

过去完成时:
The nail had protruded from the wood before I hammered it in. = 在我把它钉进去之前，钉子已经从木头上突出来了。
The wire had protruded from the insulation, causing a short circuit. = 电线已经从绝缘层中突出，导致短路。

将来时:
The new building will protrude significantly from the skyline. = 新建筑将从天际线上显著突出。
His jaw will probably protrude if he keeps eating so fast. = 如果他继续这么快地吃东西，他的下巴可能会突出。

虚拟语气:
If his eyes protruded any further, he'd need medical attention. = 如果他的眼睛再突出一些，他就需要医疗护理。
I wish my knowledge of this subject didn't protrude so noticeably. = 我希望我对这个主题的了解不会如此明显地突出。


被动语态:
His ribs were protruded by the impact of the collision. = 他的肋骨被碰撞的影响挤压出来了。
The details were protruded in the report. = 报告中突出了细节。

疑问句:
Does his belly protrude too much? = 他的肚子是不是太突出了？
Does that wire protrude from the casing? = 那根电线是不是从外壳里伸出来了？

否定句:
His jaw didn't protrude noticeably. = 他的下巴没有明显突出。
The handle didn't protrude from the box. = 手柄没有从盒子里伸出来。
