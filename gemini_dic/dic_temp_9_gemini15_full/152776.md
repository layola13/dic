peck:/pek/| n. vt.|啄；轻啄；少量；亲吻|A peck is a quick light stroke with a beak or a similar pointed object. It can also refer to a small amount of something or a light kiss.|例句：The bird pecked at the seeds.  那只鸟啄食种子。  例句：She gave him a peck on the cheek. 她在他的脸颊上轻轻一吻。|近义词：tap, nibble, kiss|反义词：gorge, devour


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The chicken pecks at the grain. = 小鸡啄食谷物。
He pecks at his food. = 他没精打采地吃东西。


一般过去时:
The bird pecked at the window. = 鸟啄了窗户一下。
She pecked a quick kiss on his cheek. = 她在他脸颊上快速地亲了一下。


现在完成时:
The hen has pecked all the seeds. = 母鸡已经啄食了所有的种子。
He has pecked away at the problem all day. = 他整天都在努力解决这个问题。


过去完成时:
The woodpecker had pecked a hole in the tree. = 啄木鸟已经在树上啄了个洞。
She had pecked at her food before the meeting. = 会议前她吃了一点东西。


将来时:
The chick will peck at the crumbs. = 小鸡会啄食面包屑。
He will peck at the keyboard all night. = 他会整晚敲打键盘。


虚拟语气:
If the bird pecked at the fruit, it would fall. = 如果鸟啄了水果，它就会掉下来。
I wish he would stop pecking at me. = 我希望他能别再挑我的毛病了。


被动语态:
The seeds were pecked by the birds. = 种子被鸟啄食了。
The problem is being pecked at by the committee. = 委员会正在努力解决这个问题。


疑问句:
Does the bird peck at the fruit? = 鸟啄水果吗？
Did he peck at his food? = 他吃东西了吗？（指吃得很少）


否定句:
The bird didn't peck at the seeds. = 鸟没有啄食种子。
She didn't peck at his offer. = 她没有接受他的提议（指轻率地拒绝）。


PHRASE->
peck order: 啄食顺序；等级制度
peck at something: 啄；少量地吃
peck and choose: 挑挑拣拣
a peck of troubles: 一大堆麻烦
peck on the cheek: 在脸颊上轻吻
