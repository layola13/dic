dizzying:/ˈdɪziɪŋ/| adj.|令人头晕目眩的；令人眼花缭乱的；使人晕眩的 |Making one feel unsteady or dizzy; causing a feeling of disorientation or confusion due to rapid or excessive change or stimulation.  |The dizzying heights of the skyscraper made me feel queasy. 摩天大楼令人眩晕的高度让我感到恶心。 The dizzying array of choices overwhelmed her.  琳琅满目的选择让她不知所措。| overwhelming, stunning, breathtaking, bewildering | steady, calm, clear, sober |
