mussite:/ˈmʌsaɪt/ | n. |穆萨石，一种矿物 | Mussite is a relatively rare mineral belonging to the pyroxene group.  It's a variety of aegirine-augite, a sodium-rich clinopyroxene. Its chemical composition varies, but it generally contains significant amounts of sodium, iron, and aluminum. |Mussite crystals are typically dark green to black in color and have a prismatic habit. It's found in alkaline igneous rocks and pegmatites. |近义词：aegirine-augite, clinopyroxene |反义词：无
