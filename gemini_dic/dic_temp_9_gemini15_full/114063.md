magnetize:/ˈmæɡnətaɪz/| vt.| 使磁化；吸引；迷住 | To cause (a substance) to become magnetic.  To strongly attract or fascinate. |例句：The iron bar was magnetized by the strong magnet.  铁棒被强磁铁磁化了。

例句：Her charisma magnetized the audience. 她的魅力吸引了观众。


近义词：attract, charm, fascinate, enthrall

反义词：demagnetize, repel


词性:动词


USAGE->
