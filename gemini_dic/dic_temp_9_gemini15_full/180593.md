registrars:/ˈrɛdʒɪstrɑːrz/ | n. | 注册员；注册官；登记员 |  A registrar is an official who is responsible for keeping records, especially official records, or for registering people or things.  They often work in government offices, universities, or other institutions. | Example: The university registrars are responsible for managing student records. 大学注册员负责管理学生档案。

The registrar confirmed my enrollment. 注册员确认了我的入学。


近义词: recorder, clerk, officer

反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym)

词性: Noun
