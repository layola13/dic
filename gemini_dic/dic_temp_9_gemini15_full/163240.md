preconceived:/ˌpriːkənˈsiːvd/| adj. | 预先构成的，预先设想的 | Formed or conceived beforehand; existing before an experience or event.  It implies a judgment or opinion formed before having sufficient evidence. |例句：He approached the problem with preconceived notions about its solution. 他带着对解决方法的预先设想来处理这个问题。


例句：Her preconceived ideas about the country were completely wrong. 她对那个国家的预想完全错了。


近义词：preconceived, preformed, preplanned, predetermined, prejudged


反义词：unbiased, open-minded, objective


词性：形容词
