overpeopling:/ˌoʊvərˈpiːplɪŋ/ | n. | 人口过剩；过度拥挤 | The act or process of populating an area to an excessive degree, resulting in overcrowding and strain on resources.  It refers to a situation where the population density is too high for the available resources and infrastructure to support. | Example:  The overpeopling of the city led to housing shortages and increased traffic congestion.  城市人口过剩导致住房短缺和交通拥堵加剧。| 近义词：overpopulation, overcrowding | 反义词：underpopulation, depopulation | 名词
