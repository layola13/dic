machine-guns:/məˈʃiːnɡʌnz/ | n. | 机枪 |  Automatic weapons that fire many bullets rapidly.  They are typically used in warfare and are known for their high rate of fire and devastating effect. | Example: The soldiers used machine-guns to suppress enemy fire.  士兵们用机枪压制敌人的火力。


The enemy's machine-guns kept us pinned down. 敌人的机枪压制住了我们。|近义词：automatic weapons, rapid-fire guns |反义词：handgun, rifle (depending on context - these are less automatic and less high-capacity)
