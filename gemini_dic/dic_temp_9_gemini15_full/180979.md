reimplantation:/ˌriːɪmplænˈteɪʃən/| n. |再植；再植术|The surgical procedure of reattaching a severed body part, such as a tooth, limb, or organ, to its original location.  It involves restoring blood supply and nerve function to the reattached part.|例句：The successful reimplantation of his severed finger was a testament to modern surgical techniques.  他的断指成功再植是现代外科技术的证明。

The reimplantation surgery was long and complex, requiring the meticulous work of a skilled surgical team. 再植手术漫长而复杂，需要一个熟练的外科团队一丝不苟的工作。|近义词：reattachment, transplantation, re-grafting |反义词：excision, removal, amputation
