outlasted:/ˌaʊtˈlæstɪd/| v. |持续时间比…长；比…经久耐用|To last longer than; to survive or endure longer than.  The verb implies a contest or comparison of duration.|例句：The old house outlasted its owners. 这座老房子比它的主人存活的时间更长。

The ancient civilization outlasted many of its contemporaries.  这个古老的文明比许多同时代的文明存活的时间更长。|近义词：survive, endure, outlive, persist|反义词：perish, expire, cease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outlasts his opponents in every debate. 他在每一次辩论中都比他的对手坚持得更久。
The engine outlasts its warranty period.  这台引擎的使用寿命超过了保修期。


一般过去时:
The empire outlasted several emperors. 这个帝国比好几位皇帝的统治时间都长。
The old oak tree outlasted the storm.  这棵古老的橡树挺过了暴风雨。


现在完成时:
Their marriage has outlasted many of their friendships. 他们的婚姻比他们许多友谊都持续的时间长。
This material has outlasted all expectations.  这种材料的使用寿命超出了所有预期。


过去完成时:
The castle had outlasted numerous sieges.  这座城堡经受住了无数次围攻。
She had outlasted all her rivals in the business.  她在事业上比所有竞争对手都坚持得更久。


将来时:
We hope this technology will outlast its competitors. 我们希望这项技术能够比竞争对手更持久。
This building will outlast many generations. 这座建筑将持续许多代。


虚拟语气:
If only my car could outlast another five years.  但愿我的车还能再用五年。
If the bridge had been built better, it might have outlasted the flood. 如果这座桥建造得更好，它也许就能挺过洪水。


被动语态:
The original design was outlasted by newer models.  最初的设计被更新的型号所取代（暗示更新型号更持久）。
His reputation has been outlasted by his deeds. 他的事迹使他的名声得以流传（暗示事迹更持久）。


疑问句:
Will this battery outlast the trip?  这块电池够用完整个旅程吗？
Did the bridge outlast the earthquake?  这座桥挺过了地震吗？


否定句:
The old car didn't outlast the winter.  那辆旧车没挺过冬天。
His ambition didn't outlast his resources.  他的野心没能持续到他的资源耗尽。
