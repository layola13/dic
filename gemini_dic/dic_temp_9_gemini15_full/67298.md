exspoliation:/ˌɛkspɔɪˈleɪʃən/| n.|掠夺；剥削；破坏；耗尽|The act of plundering or despoiling, especially the systematic removal of valuable resources from a territory or people.  It often implies a violation of rights and a disregard for the well-being of those affected. |例句：The exspoliation of the natural resources left the land barren. 掠夺自然资源使土地变得贫瘠。 The exspoliation of the indigenous population led to widespread poverty and suffering. 对原住民的剥削导致了普遍的贫困和苦难。|近义词：plunder, pillage, exploitation, depletion, spoliation|反义词：conservation, preservation, enrichment
