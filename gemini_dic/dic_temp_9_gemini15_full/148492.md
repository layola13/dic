overstriven:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɪvən/| adj. | 过分努力的；过于费力的；矫揉造作的 | Excessively strained or forced; overly elaborate or artificial.  It suggests an attempt that has gone too far, resulting in something unnatural or ineffective. |例句：His overstriven attempt at humor fell flat.  他的过于费力的幽默尝试适得其反。

His overstriven prose lacked natural grace. 他的过于刻意的散文缺乏自然的优雅。


近义词：strained, forced, affected, labored, artificial, pretentious


反义词：natural, effortless, spontaneous, simple, understated

词性：形容词
