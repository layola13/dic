dissent:/dɪˈsent/| v.|不同意，异议|To disagree with something, especially a decision or opinion.  |例句：The judge dissented from the majority opinion.  法官对多数意见表示异议。|例句：Several members of the board dissented from the proposal.  董事会中有几名成员对该提案表示异议。|近义词：disagree, object, oppose|反义词：agree, concur, assent


dissent-from:/dɪˈsent frɒm/| v.|不同意…；对…持异议|To express disagreement with or opposition to something.  This is a phrasal verb,  emphasizing the *object* of the disagreement.|例句：I dissent from the committee's conclusion on this matter. 我对委员会在此事上的结论表示异议。|例句：Many scientists dissent from the theory of global warming. 许多科学家对全球变暖理论持异议。|近义词：object to, disagree with, take exception to|反义词：agree with, concur with, assent to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dissents from the ruling. = 他对裁决表示异议。
They dissent from the proposed changes. = 他们反对拟议的更改。


一般过去时:
She dissented from the majority decision. = 她对大多数人的决定表示异议。
The lawyer dissented from the judge's interpretation. = 律师对法官的解释表示异议。


现在完成时:
He has dissented from company policy on several occasions. = 他多次对公司政策表示异议。
They have dissented from the party line on this issue. = 他们在这个问题上与党内路线相左。


过去完成时:
She had dissented from the plan before it was finalized. = 在计划最终确定之前，她就表示反对。
They had dissented from the proposal before the vote. = 他们在投票前就对提案表示反对。


将来时:
He will dissent from the verdict if he believes it's unjust. = 如果他认为判决不公正，他会表示异议。
They will dissent from the decision if it harms the environment. = 如果该决定会损害环境，他们将表示反对。


虚拟语气:
If I were on the jury, I would dissent from a guilty verdict. = 如果我是陪审员，我会对有罪判决表示异议。
I wish I could dissent from this unfair policy, but I can't. = 我真希望我能反对这项不公平的政策，但我不能。


被动语态:
His dissent from the decision was noted. = 他对决定的异议已被记录在案。
The proposed changes were dissented from by many members. = 许多成员对拟议的更改表示反对。


疑问句:
Does he dissent from the government's position? = 他是否反对政府的立场？
Will they dissent from the proposed legislation? = 他们会反对拟议的法案吗？


否定句:
He did not dissent from the court's ruling. = 他没有对法院的裁决表示异议。
They did not dissent from the agreement. = 他们没有反对该协议。


PHRASE->
dissent from a decision = 对一项决定表示异议
dissent from a view = 对一种观点表示异议
dissent from the majority = 与大多数人意见相左
dissent from the norm = 与常态相悖
dissent from the established order = 反对既定秩序
