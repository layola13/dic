disutilize:/dɪsˈjuːtəlaɪz/| vt.|滥用；误用；未能充分利用|To use something inefficiently or to fail to make full use of something.  The term implies a misuse or waste of potential.|例句：The company disutilized its talented workforce by assigning them menial tasks. 公司滥用其人才，指派他们做一些琐碎的任务。

例句：They disutilized the new software, failing to exploit its advanced features. 他们未能充分利用新软件，没有发挥其高级功能。


近义词：misuse, misapply, waste, squander

反义词：utilize, optimize, maximize


词性：动词
