pistil:/ˈpɪstəl/| n. |雌蕊|The pistil is the female reproductive part of a flower, consisting of the stigma, style, and ovary.  It receives pollen during pollination and develops into the fruit containing seeds.|例句：The pistil of the lily was clearly visible in the center of the flower. 百合花的雌蕊在花朵中央清晰可见。The pistil is essential for the plant's reproduction. 雌蕊对植物的繁殖至关重要。|近义词：carpel (sometimes used interchangeably)|反义词：stamen (male reproductive part)|名词
