jailbreaking:/ˈdʒeɪlˌbreɪkɪŋ/ | v. |越狱（指破解iOS设备或其他设备的操作系统限制）|The act of circumventing the security restrictions imposed by the manufacturer on a device, typically a smartphone or tablet, to gain root access and modify its operating system.  This often involves installing unauthorized software or applications. |Example: He jailbroke his iPhone to install custom themes and apps. 他越狱了他的iPhone来安装自定义主题和应用程序。|近义词：hacking, unlocking, bypassing|反义词：securing, locking down|
