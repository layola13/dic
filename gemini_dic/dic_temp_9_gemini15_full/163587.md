precultures:/ˈpriːkʌltʃərz/| n.|前培养物；预培养物|Cultures prepared in advance, often used to inoculate another culture or to grow microorganisms under specific conditions before transferring them to a different medium or environment.|The precultures were incubated overnight to ensure sufficient cell density before inoculation into the bioreactor.  预培养物在接种到生物反应器之前过夜培养，以确保足够的细胞密度。|近义词：starter cultures, inocula|反义词：main cultures, final cultures
