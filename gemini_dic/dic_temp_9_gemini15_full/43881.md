crestfallenness: /ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlən.nəs/ | n. |沮丧；灰心；垂头丧气 | The state of being dejected or depressed in spirits, especially as a result of disappointment or failure.  It implies a feeling of dejection and loss of spirit, often accompanied by a sense of humiliation or defeat. |例句：Her crestfallenness was evident in her slumped posture and downcast eyes.  她的沮丧从她无精打采的姿势和低垂的眼睛中显而易见。

例句：The team's crestfallenness after their crushing defeat was palpable.  球队惨败后的沮丧之情溢于言表。


近义词：dejection, despondency, discouragement, disappointment, dejection, low spirits, gloom, melancholy

反义词：elation, exhilaration, jubilation, triumph, cheerfulness

词性: 名词
