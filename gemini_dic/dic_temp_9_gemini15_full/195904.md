sensemaking:/ˈsɛnsˌmeɪkɪŋ/| n. |意义建构；理解；释义 |The process by which people give meaning to their experiences.  It involves selecting, organizing, and interpreting information to create a coherent understanding of a situation. |Example:  The team spent hours in sensemaking, trying to understand the market research data.  团队花了数小时进行意义建构，试图理解市场调研数据。|近义词：interpretation, comprehension, understanding |反义词：misunderstanding, confusion |名词
