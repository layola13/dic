lubricants:/ˈluːbrɪkənts/ | n. |润滑剂，润滑油 | Substances that reduce friction between surfaces in contact.  Lubricants are used in a wide variety of applications, from engines to machinery to everyday household items. | Example:  The mechanic added more lubricants to the engine. 机械师给发动机添加了更多的润滑油。

The car needs new lubricants. 这辆车需要新的润滑油。|近义词：oils, greases, lubricants,  |反义词：abrasives|名词

词源:
From the verb "lubricate,"  which comes from the Latin "lubricus" meaning "slippery."  The suffix "-ant" indicates an agent or thing that performs the action.
