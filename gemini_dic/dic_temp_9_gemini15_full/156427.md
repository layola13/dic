phytopathologist:/ˌfaɪtoʊpæˈθɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |植物病理学家|A scientist who studies plant diseases.  Phytopathologists investigate the causes of plant diseases, their development, and methods for controlling them. They often work in agricultural settings, helping farmers prevent crop losses.|Example: The phytopathologist identified the fungus responsible for the blight affecting the potato crop. 植物病理学家确定了导致马铃薯作物枯萎的真菌。|近义词：plant pathologist|反义词：无
