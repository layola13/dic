nullifidian:/nʌlɪˈfɪdiən/ | adj. | 虚无主义者；否认信仰的 |  A person who denies or disbelieves in the existence of God or any supreme being; an atheist or agnostic.  The term often implies a rejection of all religious and spiritual beliefs. | Example:  He was labeled a nullifidian by the religious community for his outspoken atheism. 他因公开的无神论而被宗教界贴上虚无主义者的标签。|近义词：atheist, agnostic, infidel, unbeliever |反义词：theist, believer, devotee
