reknocked:/riːˈnɒkt/| v.|再次敲门，重新敲门|To knock again or repeatedly.|He re-knocked, louder this time. 他又敲了敲门，这次声音更大。|knocked, tapped|opened|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-knocks the door every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就重新敲门。
They re-knock on the door until someone answers. 他们不断敲门直到有人回应。

一般过去时:
She re-knocked after hearing no response. 她没听到回应后又敲了敲门。
The delivery man re-knocked because no one was home. 送货员因为没人在家所以又敲了敲门。

现在完成时:
He has re-knocked several times but still no answer. 他已经敲了好几次门了，但还是没有人回应。
Have they re-knocked since the first time?  他们自从第一次敲门后又敲过门了吗？


过去完成时:
I realized I had re-knocked too hard. 我意识到我敲门敲得太用力了。
They said they had re-knocked before leaving. 他们说他们在离开之前又敲过门。

将来时:
I will re-knock if nobody answers the door. 如果没有人开门，我会再敲一次门。
She will re-knock tomorrow morning. 她明天早上会再次敲门。


虚拟语气:
If I had re-knocked, she might have heard me. 如果我再敲一次门，她也许会听到。
I wish I had re-knocked before leaving. 我真希望我在离开之前再敲一次门。

被动语态:
The door was re-knocked on repeatedly. 门被反复敲击。
The door was re-knocked on by the postman. 门被邮递员再次敲响。

疑问句:
Did he re-knock after the first attempt? 他第一次尝试后又敲了吗？
Has she re-knocked yet? 她又敲门了吗？

否定句:
He didn't re-knock after the first time. 他第一次之后没有再次敲门。
She hadn't re-knocked before I left. 我离开之前她没有再次敲门。


PHRASE->
re-knock on the door:  再次敲门
re-knock the door repeatedly: 反复敲门
re-knock at the door: 在门上再次敲击
re-knock with urgency: 紧急地再次敲门
re-knock politely: 礼貌地再次敲门
