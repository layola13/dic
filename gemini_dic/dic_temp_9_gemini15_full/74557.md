fraudster:/ˈfrɔːd.stər/ | n. | 欺诈者，骗子 | A person who commits fraud.  Someone who illegally obtains money or property by deception. | The fraudster was eventually caught and sentenced to prison.  这个骗子最终被抓住并被判入狱。  |近义词: swindler, con artist, cheat, imposter |反义词: honest person, benefactor
