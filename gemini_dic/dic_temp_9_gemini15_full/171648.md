pulsatilla:/ˌpʌlsəˈtɪlə/| n. |脉冲星，(植物)银莲花属植物 | Pulsatilla refers to a genus of flowering plants in the buttercup family, Ranunculaceae, commonly known as pasqueflowers.  They are known for their bell-shaped, often hairy flowers.  The name also sometimes refers to a type of variable star that pulsates in brightness. |The pasqueflower, *Pulsatilla vulgaris*, is a beautiful spring bloom. = 脉冲星，银莲花属植物，*毛茛科*植物，通常被称为*复活节花*，以其钟形、通常多毛的花朵而闻名。|近义词：pasqueflower, windflower |反义词：无
