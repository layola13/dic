rescind:/rɪˈsɪnd/| vt.|撤销；废除；取消|To make void; cancel; revoke.  The action of rescinding something previously enacted or agreed upon.|The company decided to rescind its offer of employment. 公司决定撤销其录用通知。

The court rescinded the previous ruling. 法院撤销了之前的判决。|revoke, annul, cancel, repeal|uphold, maintain, enforce|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government rescinds unfair laws. = 政府废除不公平的法律。
He rescinds his previous statement. = 他撤回了他之前的声明。

一般过去时:
They rescinded the contract. = 他们取消了合同。
The judge rescinded the order. = 法官撤销了命令。

现在完成时:
The company has rescinded its offer. = 公司已经撤回了它的报价。
She has rescinded her application. = 她已经撤回了她的申请。

过去完成时:
They had rescinded the agreement before the meeting. = 他们会议之前就已经取消了协议。
He had rescinded his promise before realizing his mistake. = 他在意识到自己的错误之前就已经收回了承诺。

将来时:
The school will rescind the student's suspension. = 学校将撤销学生的停学处分。
They will rescind the policy next year. = 他们明年将废除这项政策。

虚拟语气:
If the court had rescinded the ruling earlier, this wouldn't have happened. = 如果法院早些撤销裁决，这件事就不会发生了。
I wish they had rescinded the contract before I signed it. = 我希望他们在我在合同上签字之前就取消了它。

被动语态:
The decision was rescinded by the board. = 该决定被董事会撤销了。
The order was rescinded due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，该命令被撤销了。

疑问句:
Will the contract be rescinded? = 合同会被撤销吗？
Has the offer been rescinded? = 报价已经被撤销了吗？

否定句:
The company did not rescind the contract. = 公司没有取消合同。
They refused to rescind their decision. = 他们拒绝撤回他们的决定。


PHRASE->
rescind a contract = 撤销合同
rescind an agreement = 撤销协议
rescind a decision = 撤销决定
rescind an order = 撤销命令
rescind a law = 废除法律


词源:
From Latin *rescindĕre, from re- + scindĕre "to cut, cleave."  The sense development is from "to cut apart" to "to cut off, cancel."
