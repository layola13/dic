rustier:/ˈrʌstiər/| adj.|更生锈的，更腐蚀的|More rusty; showing greater signs of rust or decay.  The tools in the shed were rustier than I remembered. 车库里的工具比我记得的还要生锈。|近义词：more corroded, more weathered,  oxidized|反义词：shinier, newer, cleaner


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The old tools are rustier than the new ones. = 旧工具比新工具更生锈。
This metal is rustier now than it was last year. = 这个金属现在比去年更生锈了。

一般过去时:
The car was rustier after the winter. = 冬天过后，汽车更生锈了。
The fence was rustier in some places than others. = 围栏有些地方比其他地方更生锈。

现在完成时:
The tools have become rustier since we last used them. = 自从我们上次使用工具以来，它们变得更生锈了。
The pipes have grown rustier over the years. = 这些管道多年来变得越来越生锈。

过去完成时:
The tools had become rustier before we cleaned them. = 我们清洁工具之前，它们已经变得更生锈了。
The ship's hull had grown rustier than expected by the time it reached port. = 当船到达港口时，船体比预期的更生锈了。


将来时:
The car will be rustier if it's left outside all winter. = 如果汽车整个冬天都放在外面，它会更生锈。
The iron gate will become rustier over time if not properly maintained. = 如果铁门没有得到适当的维护，它会随着时间的推移而变得更生锈。

虚拟语气:
If the tools were less rustier, they would be easier to use. = 如果工具不那么生锈，它们就更容易使用了。（这句略有不自然，更常用的表达是：If the tools weren't so rusty...）
I wish my car wasn't so rustier. = 我希望我的车不要这么生锈。

被动语态:
The pipes were left to become rustier. = 管子被任其生锈。
The tools were found to be rustier than anticipated. = 发现工具比预期的更生锈。

疑问句:
Is this part rustier than that part? = 这个部件比那个部件更生锈吗？
Are the tools getting rustier? = 工具越来越生锈了吗？

否定句:
The tools aren't rustier than I thought. = 工具没有我想象的那么生锈。
The car isn't as rustier as it looks. = 汽车不像看起来那么生锈。


PHRASE->
There is no common phrase directly using "rustier".  The adjective is typically used in descriptive sentences.  However, we can construct phrases:

a rustier appearance = 更生锈的外观
more rustier than before = 比以前更生锈
become increasingly rustier = 变得越来越生锈
a rustier old car = 一辆更生锈的老车
noticeably rustier = 明显更生锈
