odiousnesses:/ˈoʊdiəsnəsəz/| n.|令人厌恶的事物；可憎的行为|The odiousnesses of the regime were well documented.  暴政的令人憎恶的行径已被充分记录。|近义词：abominations, horrors, detestations|反义词：delights, pleasures|名词


英文解析：The plural form of "odiousness,"  referring to the state or quality of being odious; extremely unpleasant; repulsive.  It denotes multiple instances or aspects of something hateful or offensive.


例句：

The odiousnesses of war are many and varied.  战争的令人厌恶之处多种多样。

His odiousnesses were finally exposed, leading to his downfall. 他的恶行最终被揭露，导致了他的垮台。


词源：

Derived from the adjective "odious" + "-ness" (suffix indicating a state or quality) + "-es" (plural suffix).  "Odious" itself comes from the Latin word "odiōsus," meaning "hateful," from "odi," meaning "I hate."
