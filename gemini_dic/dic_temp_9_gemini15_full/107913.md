larval:/ˈlɑːrvəl/| adj.| 幼虫的；幼体的 | Relating to or characteristic of a larva.  Larval stage refers to the immature stage of an insect's life cycle before it becomes an adult. | 例句：The larval stage of the butterfly is a caterpillar. 蝴蝶的幼虫期是毛毛虫。

例句：The researchers observed the larval development of the fruit fly. 研究人员观察了果蝇的幼虫发育。


近义词：juvenile, immature, pupal (if referring to the stage before the pupa)


反义词：adult, mature


词性:形容词
