free-falls:/ˈfriːfɔːlz/| v.|自由落体；暴跌；突然下降 |To fall freely under the force of gravity, without any restraint or support.  It can also refer to a sharp and sudden decline in something, such as prices or values.|例句：The skydiver free-fell for several seconds before opening his parachute. 跳伞运动员在打开降落伞前自由落体了几秒钟。

The stock market free-fell after the unexpected news.  股市在意外消息公布后暴跌。|近义词：plummet, plunge, tumble,  drop|反义词：ascend, rise, soar, climb|
