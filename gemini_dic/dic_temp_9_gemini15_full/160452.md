populate:/ˈpɒpjʊleɪt/ /ˈpɑːpjʊleɪt/| vt.|居住于；填充；使充满|to fill a place with people or animals; to add content to something.|例句：Many immigrants populated the new city. 许多移民居住在这个新城市。

例句：The museum is populated with artifacts from ancient civilizations. 这个博物馆里陈列着来自古代文明的文物。


近义词：inhabit, fill, stock, colonize|反义词：depopulate, empty, evacuate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds populate the forest. = 鸟儿栖息在森林里。
They populate the database with new information. = 他们用新信息填充数据库。

一般过去时:
The pioneers populated the western territories. = 拓荒者们居住在西部地区。
They populated the spreadsheet with sales figures. = 他们用销售数据填充电子表格。


现在完成时:
Immigrants have populated this area for generations. = 多年来，移民们居住在这个地区。
The team has populated the website with images and text. = 团队已经用图片和文字填充了网站。

过去完成时:

They had populated the document with data before submitting it. = 他们在提交文档之前已经用数据填充了它。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The website was not populated with the correct information. = 网站没有填充正确的信息。


PHRASE->

词源:
populate源于拉丁语populāre，意思是“to people”，其词根是populus，意思是“people”。
