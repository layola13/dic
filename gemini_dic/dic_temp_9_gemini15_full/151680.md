partitioner:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənər/ | n. | 分区器，分区程序 | A partitioner is a component or algorithm that divides data into partitions.  It is commonly used in distributed systems and databases to distribute data across multiple nodes or machines for parallel processing or efficient storage.  Different partitioners employ various strategies, such as hashing, range partitioning, or round-robin, to achieve balanced distribution and optimize performance. | Example: The database uses a consistent hashing partitioner to distribute the data evenly across the cluster.  数据库使用一致性哈希分区器将数据均匀地分布在集群中。  The Hadoop framework offers various partitioners to optimize data processing. Hadoop框架提供各种分区器来优化数据处理。 |近义词：data distributor, data splitter | 反义词：aggregator (in the context of data processing) |  n.
