lactational:/lækˈteɪʃənəl/| adj.|泌乳的，哺乳期的|Relating to or involving lactation (the secretion of milk).  The word describes anything connected to the production and secretion of milk from the mammary glands.|例句：Lactational amenorrhea is a temporary absence of menstruation during breastfeeding.  哺乳期闭经是哺乳期暂时性月经停止。

例句：The study focused on lactational changes in maternal behavior. 这项研究的重点是母乳喂养期间母性行为的变化。


近义词：mammary, milk-producing

反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable, but terms related to absence of lactation could be considered, such as "non-lactating" or "post-lactational")
