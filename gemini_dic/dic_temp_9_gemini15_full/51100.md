desperateness:/ˈdɛspəreɪtnəs/ | n. |绝望；孤注一掷；不顾一切 |The feeling of being desperate; a state of extreme urgency or need.  It often implies a lack of hope and a willingness to take extreme risks. |例句：His desperateness led him to commit a crime. 他的绝望导致他犯罪了。

例句：Driven by desperateness, she took a reckless gamble.  出于绝望，她孤注一掷。


近义词：desperation, hopelessness, urgency, extremity |反义词：calmness, hopefulness, serenity, composure |词性：名词
