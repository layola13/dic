macramé:/ˈmækrəmeɪ/| n. |马克兰工艺，编结工艺 |A form of textile-making using knotting techniques.  It is often used to create wall hangings, plant holders, and other decorative items.  Macramé is characterized by its intricate patterns and textures created through various knotting methods.| Example:  She made a beautiful macramé wall hanging for her living room. 她为她的客厅做了一个漂亮的马克兰挂毯。|近义词：knotting, weaving, crocheting |反义词：N/A
