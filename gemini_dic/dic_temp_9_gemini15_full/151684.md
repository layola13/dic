partitionist's: /ˌpɑːrˈtɪʃənɪsts/ | n. | 分裂主义者的 | A supporter of partition, especially the division of a country into separate political units.  A person who advocates for the division of a larger entity into smaller, more independent parts. | 例句：The partitionist's arguments focused on the historical grievances and cultural differences between the two regions. 分裂主义者的论点集中在两个地区之间的历史冤仇和文化差异上。  The partitionist's plan called for a complete separation of the north and south. 分裂主义者的计划要求完全分开南北。 | 近义词：separatist, secessionist, autonomist | 反义词：unificationist, integrationist | 名词
