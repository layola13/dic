modes:/moʊdz/|ˈməʊdz/| n.|模式，方式，方法|The word "modes" refers to a way or manner in which something exists or is experienced. It can also refer to a particular way of operating or functioning.|例句：There are several modes of transportation available.  有多种交通工具可用。|例句：The machine operates in different modes. 这台机器以不同的模式运行。|近义词：methods, ways, manners, styles|反义词：chaos, disorder
