self-appraisal:/ˌsɛlf əˈpreɪzl/| n.|自我评价，自我评估|An evaluation of oneself and one's performance, often used in a professional context for career development or performance reviews.  It involves identifying strengths, weaknesses, achievements, and areas for improvement.|例句：My self-appraisal highlighted my strong communication skills but also revealed a need to improve my time management. 我的自我评价突出了我强大的沟通能力，但也揭示了我需要改进时间管理。|例句：Employees are required to complete a self-appraisal before their annual performance review. 员工在年度绩效考核前必须完成自我评价。|近义词：self-assessment, self-evaluation|反义词：external evaluation, peer review|名词
