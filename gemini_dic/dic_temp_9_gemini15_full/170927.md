pseudomythical:/ˌsuːdoʊˈmɪθɪkəl/| adj. |似神话的，半神话的 | Seemingly or partly mythical; having the characteristics of a myth but not entirely based on fact or legend.  It often refers to something that blends elements of myth and reality, or that is presented as mythical but may have a basis in historical or cultural fact. | Example: The story of the founding of the city is pseudomythical, blending historical events with legendary accounts of heroic deeds.  这个城市建立的故事是似神话的，它将历史事件与英雄事迹的传说故事融合在一起。 |近义词：semi-mythical, legendary, quasi-mythical | 反义词：factual, historical, real


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in the example.  It modifies nouns.
