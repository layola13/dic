qualifiedly:/ˈkwɒlɪfaɪdli/| adv.|有条件地；部分地；合格地|With qualifications or reservations; to a certain extent but not completely.  It implies some limitation or condition. |例句：He qualifiedly endorsed the plan, expressing some concerns about its feasibility. 他有条件地认可了该计划，表达了对其可行性的担忧。

例句：She qualifiedly agreed to the proposal, suggesting a few modifications. 她有条件地同意了这个提议，提出了一些修改建议。
