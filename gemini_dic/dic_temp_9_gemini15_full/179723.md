reenumerating: /ˌriːɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新编号，重新列举 | The act of numbering or listing again. It implies a previous numbering or listing that is being revised or replaced.  | Example: The librarian is reenumerating the books after the recent reorganization of the shelves. 图书管理员正在重新编号书籍，因为书架最近进行了重新整理。 | 近义词：renumbering, relisting, recataloging | 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be simply "numbering" or "listing" without a prior enumeration.
