rotifer:/ˈroʊtɪfər/ | n. | 轮虫 | A microscopic aquatic invertebrate animal with a ciliated crown, which it uses for feeding and locomotion.  They are found in various freshwater, marine, and even moist soil habitats. | Example:  The researchers observed a variety of rotifers in the pond water sample.  研究人员在池塘水样本中观察到各种轮虫。

词源:
