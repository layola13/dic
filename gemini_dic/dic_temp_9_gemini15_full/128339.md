neutralise: /ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/ | vt. | 中和；使无效；抵消 | To make something harmless or ineffective.  |例句：The antidote neutralised the poison. 解毒剂中和了毒药。|例句：The army neutralised the enemy's attack.  军队挫败了敌人的进攻。|近义词：counteract, cancel out, offset |反义词：intensify, enhance, exacerbate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The antidote neutralises the poison. = 解毒剂中和毒药。
The government neutralises the opposition's influence. = 政府削弱了反对派的势力。


一般过去时:
The acid neutralised the alkali. = 酸中和了碱。
The police neutralised the bomb threat. = 警方化解了炸弹威胁。


现在完成时:
The medication has neutralised the infection. = 药物已经抑制了感染。
They have neutralised the negative effects of the crisis. = 他们已经消除了危机的负面影响。


过去完成时:
She had neutralised the threat before anyone noticed. = 她在他人注意到之前就消除了威胁。
The engineers had neutralised the faulty circuit. = 工程师们已经使故障电路失效。


将来时:
The new policy will neutralise the competition. = 新政策将抵消竞争。
We will neutralise the harmful substances in the water. = 我们将中和水中的有害物质。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have neutralised the problem. = 如果我们早点行动，就能解决这个问题。
If they had more resources, they could neutralise the attack. = 如果他们有更多资源，就能抵御攻击。


被动语态:
The threat was neutralised by the quick action of the police. = 警方迅速行动，化解了威胁。
The negative effects were neutralised by the positive feedback. = 正面反馈抵消了负面影响。


疑问句:
Can we neutralise the effects of climate change? = 我们能中和气候变化的影响吗？
How can we neutralise the opposition's propaganda? = 我们如何才能抵消反对派的宣传？


否定句:
The treatment did not neutralise the virus. = 该疗法没有清除病毒。
The measures failed to neutralise the economic downturn. = 这些措施未能阻止经济衰退。


PHRASE->
neutralise the threat = 消除威胁
neutralise the enemy = 消灭敌人
neutralise the acid = 中和酸
neutralise the effects = 消除影响
neutralise the opposition = 削弱反对派


词源:
From French *neutraliser*, from *neutre* "neutral" + *-iser*  verb-forming suffix.  The ultimate origin is Latin *neuter* "neither," from *ne* "not" + *uter* "which (of two)."
