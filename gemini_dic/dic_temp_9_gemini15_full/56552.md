domineeringly:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋli/ | adv. | 专横地；盛气凌人地 | In a domineering manner; tyrannically.  The boss spoke domineeringly to his employees.  | 例句：He acted domineeringly, expecting everyone to obey his commands immediately. 他专横地行事，期望每个人立即服从他的命令。

She spoke domineeringly, silencing any dissent. 她盛气凌人地说话，压制了任何异议。| 近义词：authoritatively, imperiously, tyrannically, overbearingly | 反义词：submissively, humbly, meekly
