cowinner: /ˈkoʊwɪnər/ | n. | 共同获胜者 | A person who wins a prize or competition together with another or others.  | Example:  She was a cowinner of the Nobel Prize in Literature. 她与他人共同获得了诺贝尔文学奖。| 近义词: joint winner, shared winner | 反义词: sole winner, loser


USAGE->

The team are cowinners of the championship. = 这个团队是锦标赛的共同获胜者。
