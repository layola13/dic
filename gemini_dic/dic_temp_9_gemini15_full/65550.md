euthanize:/ˈjuːθənaɪz/| vt. |安乐死；对…实施安乐死 |To kill (a terminally ill or injured animal) painlessly.  This usually involves veterinary intervention and is done to prevent further suffering.|例句：The vet had to euthanize the dog after it was hit by a car.  兽医不得不对被车撞到的狗实施安乐死。

The farmer decided to euthanize the injured cow to end its suffering. 农场主决定对受伤的奶牛实施安乐死以结束它的痛苦。|近义词：put down, dispatch,  kill (mercifully) |反义词：save, treat, cure |动词
