chaster:/ˈtʃeɪstər/ | adj. |贞洁的，纯洁的；朴素的|  Chaste means free from obscenity; decent; pure.  It often refers to sexual purity, but can also describe something simple and unadorned. |例句：She led a chaste life, avoiding all forms of sexual activity. 她过着贞洁的生活，避免一切形式的性行为。

She preferred a chaste style of dress, avoiding anything too flashy or revealing. 她喜欢朴素的穿着风格，避免任何过于花哨或暴露的服装。|近义词：pure, virtuous, modest, unadorned |反义词：immodest, impure, lewd, licentious |
