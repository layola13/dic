imperiality: /ɪmˌpɪəriˈæləti/ | n. | 帝国统治；帝国主义；帝王气派 | The quality or state of being imperial; dominance or power asserted by an empire or emperor.  It can refer to the policies and practices of an empire, the ideology that supports empire, or the cultural expressions of imperial power.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting oppression and exploitation. | Example: The historian analyzed the lingering effects of imperiality on the colonized nation.  历史学家分析了帝国统治对被殖民国家的持久影响。  | 近义词：imperialism, dominion, hegemony | 反义词：colonial subjugation, liberation, independence |  词性：名词
