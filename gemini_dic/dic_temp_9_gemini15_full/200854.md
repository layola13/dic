slimmed:/slɪmd/| v.|使变苗条；精简；削减 |To make or become thinner or smaller.  It can refer to a person losing weight, a company reducing costs, or a document being made shorter. |例句：She slimmed down after joining a gym. 她加入健身房后瘦了。|例句：The company slimmed its workforce to cut costs. 公司精简员工以削减成本。|近义词：thinned, reduced, streamlined |反义词：fattened, enlarged, expanded
