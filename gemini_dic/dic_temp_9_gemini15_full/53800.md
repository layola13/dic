disconcertingly:/dɪskənˈsɜːrtɪŋli/ | adv. |令人不安地，令人困惑地 |In a way that causes one to feel unsettled or confused.  It describes something that is unsettling or disturbing.  |例句：The silence after his announcement was disconcertingly long.  他的声明后，沉默的时间长到令人不安。


例句：The ease with which he lied was disconcertingly casual. 他撒谎的轻松程度令人不安地随意。


近义词：unsettlingly, disturbingly, unnervingly, oddly, strangely


反义词：comfortingly, reassuringly, calmingly
