gratifies:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪz/| vt.|使满足；使高兴；酬谢|To give pleasure or satisfaction to (someone).  It implies fulfilling a desire or need. |例句：His success gratifies his parents.  他的成功使他的父母感到欣慰。

例句：The gift greatly gratified her. 这份礼物让她非常高兴。

近义词：pleases, satisfies, delights, fulfills

反义词：disappoints, dissatisfies, frustrates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news gratifies me. = 这个消息让我很高兴。
Her hard work gratifies her teacher. = 她的努力工作使她的老师感到欣慰。

一般过去时:
The concert gratified the audience. = 音乐会让观众很满意。
The good results gratified the scientist. = 良好的结果让科学家很满意。

现在完成时:
Their achievements have gratified the whole team. = 他们的成就使整个团队感到欣慰。
This success has greatly gratified me. = 这次成功让我非常高兴。

过去完成时:
The completed project had gratified the manager. = 已完成的项目让经理很满意。
The positive feedback had gratified the writer. =  积极的反馈让作家很满意。

将来时:
The new policy will gratify many citizens. = 新政策将使许多市民满意。
Winning the award will greatly gratify her. = 赢得奖项将让她非常高兴。

虚拟语气:
If only I could finish the project, it would gratify me. = 如果我能完成这个项目就好了，那会让我很满足。
I wish this news would gratify my parents. = 我希望这个消息能让我父母高兴。

被动语态:
He was gratified by the compliments. = 他对这些赞美感到很高兴。
The team was gratified by the positive outcome. = 团队对积极的结果感到满意。

疑问句:
Does this outcome gratify you? = 这个结果让你满意吗？
Will this decision gratify the shareholders? = 这个决定会让股东们满意吗？

否定句:
The results did not gratify the researcher. = 结果没有让研究人员满意。
The explanation did not gratify the customer. = 解释没有让顾客满意。


PHRASE->
gratify oneself = 自我满足
gratify a desire = 满足愿望
gratify one's ambition = 实现抱负
gratify one's curiosity = 满足好奇心
gratify a need = 满足需求


词源:
From Latin *gratificare, from gratus "pleasing, agreeable" + facere "to make, do."  The word ultimately traces back to Proto-Indo-European *gʷṛ̥- "to love, desire."
