英文解析:  A peculiarism is a distinctive or unusual feature, characteristic, or habit. It often refers to something that is strange or odd, but not necessarily negative. It can apply to an individual's personality, a group's culture, or even a particular aspect of something.

例句:

The region's peculiarism is its unique dialect.  这个地区独特的方言是它的特殊之处。

His peculiarism for wearing mismatched socks was well-known among his friends. 他穿不配套袜子的怪癖在他朋友中是出了名的。


词源:  Derived from the adjective "peculiar," meaning "strange," "odd," or "unusual," which in turn comes from the Late Latin word "peculiaris," meaning "one's own," "private," or "belonging to a particular person or thing."  The suffix "-ism" denotes a characteristic, doctrine, or practice.
