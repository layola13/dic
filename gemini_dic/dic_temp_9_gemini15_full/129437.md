noms:/nɒmz/| n. |名字；名称|The word "noms" is a slang term, primarily used in informal settings, referring to names.  It's a shortened, playful version of "names."|例句：What are the noms of the participants? = 参与者的名字是什么？

例句：Can you tell me the noms on the guest list? = 你能告诉我宾客名单上的名字吗？

近义词：names, appellations

反义词：无
