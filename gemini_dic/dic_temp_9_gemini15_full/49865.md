demotions: /dɪˈmoʊʃənz/ | n. | 降职；贬黜 | The act of demoting someone to a lower rank or position; a reduction in rank or status.  |  例句：His demotions were a result of his consistent poor performance. 他的降职是因为他持续糟糕的表现。


例句：The company announced several demotions after a period of financial difficulty. 公司在经历一段时间的财务困难后宣布了几次降职。


近义词：downgradings, reductions in rank, demotions


反义词：promotions, advancements, elevations


词性:名词
