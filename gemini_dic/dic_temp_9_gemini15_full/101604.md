irrebuttable: /ˌɪrɪˈbʌtəbl/ | adj. | 不可辩驳的，无可否认的 | Incapable of being refuted or disproved; undeniable.  | The evidence against him was irrebuttable, leading to his conviction.  证据确凿，导致了他被定罪。| undeniable, incontestable, irrefutable | refutable, disputable, debatable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence presented is irrebuttable. = 提交的证据是无可辩驳的。
This conclusion is irrebuttable. = 这个结论是不可辩驳的。


一般过去时:
The judge found the evidence irrebuttable. = 法官认定证据是不可辩驳的。
His arguments were irrebuttable. = 他的论点是无可辩驳的。


现在完成时:
Scientists have presented irrebuttable proof of climate change. = 科学家们已经提供了气候变化的无可辩驳的证据。
The prosecution has presented irrebuttable evidence of his guilt. = 控方已经提供了证明其有罪的无可辩驳的证据。


过去完成时:
By the time the trial ended, the evidence against him had become irrebuttable. = 到审判结束时，针对他的证据已变得无可辩驳。
They had gathered irrebuttable evidence before making the accusation. = 他们在提出指控之前已经收集了无可辩驳的证据。


将来时:
The findings of the investigation will be irrebuttable. = 调查结果将是无可辩驳的。
Future research will likely make this conclusion irrebuttable. = 未来的研究很可能会使这个结论变得无可辩驳。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were irrebuttable, the case would be closed. = 如果证据是无可辩驳的，案件就会结案。
I wish the evidence against my client were irrebuttable.  我希望针对我委托人的证据是无可辩驳的。(这个例子中，irrefutable更符合语境，因为说话人希望证据对委托人有利，但事实上并非如此。)


被动语态:
The facts were considered irrebuttable by the jury. = 事实被陪审团认为是无可辩驳的。
His arguments were deemed irrebuttable by the court. = 他的论点被法庭认为是无可辩驳的。


疑问句:
Is the evidence truly irrebuttable? = 证据真的无可辩驳吗？
Are the claims made in the report irrebuttable? = 报告中提出的说法是无可辩驳的吗？


否定句:
The defendant's claims were not irrebuttable. = 被告的主张并非无可辩驳。
The evidence presented was not entirely irrebuttable. = 提交的证据并非完全无可辩驳。



PHRASE->
irrebuttable evidence = 不可辩驳的证据
irrebuttable proof = 不可辩驳的证据
irrebuttable conclusion = 不可辩驳的结论
irrebuttable case = 确凿的案情
irrebuttable argument = 无可辩驳的论点


词源:

* **ir-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **rebut**: verb meaning "to refute" (from Old French *rebuter,  from *re- "back" + *buter "to strike, push")
* **-able**: suffix meaning "capable of being"


因此，irrebuttable字面意思是“不能被反驳的”。
