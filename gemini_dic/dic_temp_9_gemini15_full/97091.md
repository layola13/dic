infest:/ɪnˈfɛst/| vt.|侵扰；充满（害虫等）|to invade and occupy in large numbers; to overrun.|The house was infested with rats. 这房子里老鼠成灾。  The garden was infested with weeds. 花园里杂草丛生。|近义词：overrun, plague, swarm|反义词：eradicate, cleanse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rats infest the old house. = 老鼠侵扰着那座旧房子。
Fleas infest my dog. = 跳蚤侵扰着我的狗。

一般过去时:
The ship was infested with cockroaches. = 那艘船曾被蟑螂侵扰。
Locusts infested the fields last year. = 去年蝗虫侵扰了田地。

现在完成时:
The area has been infested with mosquitoes all summer. = 整个夏天这个地区都被蚊子侵扰了。
The building has been infested with termites for years. = 这栋建筑物多年来一直被白蚁侵扰。

过去完成时:
The farmer found that the crop had been infested by insects before harvest. = 农民发现庄稼在收割前已被虫害侵扰。
The house had been infested with mice for months before we bought it. = 我们买下这房子之前，它已经被老鼠侵扰了好几个月了。

将来时:
If we don't take action, the pests will infest our crops next year. = 如果我们不采取行动，明年害虫将会侵扰我们的庄稼。
Experts predict that the insects will infest the region next spring. = 专家预测昆虫将在明年春天侵扰该地区。

虚拟语气:
If the garden weren't infested with weeds, it would look much nicer. = 如果花园里没有杂草丛生，它看起来会漂亮得多。
I wish my apartment wasn't infested with ants. = 我真希望我的公寓里没有蚂蚁。

被动语态:
The fields were infested by the locust swarm. = 田地被蝗虫群侵扰了。
The house is being infested by termites. = 这房子正在被白蚁侵扰。

疑问句:
Is the house infested with insects? = 这房子里是否有很多昆虫？
Are the crops infested with pests? = 庄稼是否受到害虫侵扰？

否定句:
The area is not infested with snakes. = 这个地区没有蛇。
The hotel was not infested with bugs. = 这家酒店没有虫子。


PHRASE->
be infested with =  充满…（害虫等）

infestation of insects =  昆虫侵扰
control infestation = 控制虫害
