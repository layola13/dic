nabob:/ˈneɪbɒb/| n.|土豪；巨富；达官贵人|A very wealthy, influential person, especially one who has made their fortune in the East.  The term often implies ostentation and a somewhat arrogant or condescending manner.|例句：He lived the life of a nabob, surrounded by luxury and servants. 他过着土豪般的生活，周围环绕着奢侈品和仆人。
例句：The retired businessman, having amassed a considerable fortune, became a true nabob in his community.  这位退休的商人积累了可观的财富，成为了他所在社区名副其实的巨富。|近义词：magnate, tycoon, plutocrat|反义词：pauper, beggar
