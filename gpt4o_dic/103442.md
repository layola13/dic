Here’s a detailed analysis of the word "what":

### Word: What
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to inquire about information concerning someone or something.

### Example Usage:
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"  
  **Translation**: "你叫什么名字？"

### Synonyms:
- which
- that

### Antonyms:
- (none)

### Usage in Different Tenses:
#### Simple Present:
- **Question**: "What do you want for dinner?"  
  **Translation**: "你晚饭想吃什么？"

#### Present Continuous:
- **Question**: "What are you doing now?"  
  **Translation**: "你现在在做什么？"

#### Present Perfect:
- **Question**: "What have you done?"  
  **Translation**: "你做了什么？"

#### Simple Past:
- **Question**: "What did he say?"  
  **Translation**: "他说了什么？"

#### Future Simple:
- **Question**: "What will you do tomorrow?"  
  **Translation**: "你明天要做什么？"

### Phrases and Idioms:
- **Examples**:
  - "What's up?"  
    **Translation**: "怎么了？/ 你好吗？"
  - "What the hell!"  
    **Translation**: "搞什么鬼！"

- **Idioms**:
  - "what if"  
    **Translation**: "如果...会怎么样"
  - "what for"  
    **Translation**: "为了什么"
  - "no matter what"  
    **Translation**: "无论如何"
  - "what's more"  
    **Translation**: "而且；更重要的是"
  - "what about"  
    **Translation**: "...怎么样"
  - "so what"  
    **Translation**: "那又怎样"

### Negative Sentences:
- **Example**: "I don't know what to say."  
  **Translation**: "我不知道该说什么。"

### Interrogative Sentences:
- **Question**: "What time is it?"  
  **Translation**: "现在几点了？"

This comprehensive overview captures the usage, forms, and contexts of the word "what."
