### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Antonyms
- None

### Usage

#### Simple Present
- *Questions*:
  - What do you want for dinner?
    - 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- *Questions*:
  - What are you doing now?
    - 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- *Questions*:
  - What have you done?
    - 你做了什么？

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- *Questions*:
  - What have you been doing all day?
    - 你一整天都在做什么？

#### Simple Past
- *Questions*:
  - What did he say?
    - 他说了什么？

#### Past Continuous
- *Questions*:
  - What were you doing when I called?
    - 我打电话时你在做什么？

#### Past Perfect
- *Questions*:
  - What had happened before I arrived?
    - 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- *Questions*:
  - What had you been doing before the accident?
    - 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

#### Simple Future
- *Questions*:
  - What will you do tomorrow?
    - 你明天要做什么？

#### Future Continuous
- *Questions*:
  - What will you be doing this time tomorrow?
    - 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

#### Future Perfect
- *Questions*:
  - What will you have accomplished by next year?
    - 到明年你会完成什么？

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- *Questions*:
  - What will you have been doing for ten years?
    - 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

#### Subjunctive Mood
- *Examples*:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it.
    - 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

#### Passive Voice
- *Examples*:
  - What was decided at the meeting?
    - 会议上决定了什么？

#### Interrogative Sentences
- *Questions*:
  - What time is it?
    - 现在几点了？

#### Negative Sentences
- *Examples*:
  - I don't know what to say.
    - 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases

#### Everyday Expressions
- **Phrase**: What's up?
  - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

#### Idioms
- **Phrase**: What if
  - **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样

- **Phrase**: What for
  - **Translation**: 为了什么

- **Phrase**: No matter what
  - **Translation**: 无论如何

- **Phrase**: What’s more
  - **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是

- **Phrase**: What about
  - **Translation**: ...怎么样

- **Phrase**: So what
  - **Translation**: 那又怎样
