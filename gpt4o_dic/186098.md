Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis

- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么 (in Chinese)

### Definition
"What" is primarily used to inquire about information regarding a person, object, or situation. It serves as a fundamental interrogative term in English.

### Example
- **Sentence**: What is your name?  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- None listed.

### Usage

#### Simple Present
- **Questions**:
  - What do you want for dinner?  
    **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What are you doing now?  
    **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What have you done?  
    **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Past Tenses
- **Simple Past**:
  - What did he say?  
    **Translation**: 他说了什么？

- **Past Continuous**:
  - What were you doing when I called?  
    **Translation**: 我打电话时你在做什么？

#### Future Tenses
- **Simple Future**:
  - What will you do tomorrow?  
    **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
  **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?  
  **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- What time is it?  
  **Translation**: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Example**: I don't know what to say.  
  **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases and Idioms

#### Common Phrases
- **What’s up?**  
  **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  
- **What the hell!**  
  **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **what if**: 如果...会怎么样
- **what for**: 为了什么
- **no matter what**: 无论如何
- **what's more**: 而且；更重要的是
- **what about**: ...怎么样
- **so what**: 那又怎样

This analysis captures the diverse uses and contexts of "what," showcasing its functional versatility in English.
