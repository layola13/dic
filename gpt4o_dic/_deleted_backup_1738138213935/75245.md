以下是英文单词“what”的详细解析：

### Basic Information
- **Word**: **what**
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to inquire information regarding something or someone.
- **Example**: What is your name?

### Synonyms and Antonyms
- **Synonyms**: which, that
- **Antonyms**: (none listed)

### Usage Examples by Tense
#### Simple Present
- **What is your name?**  
  翻译: 你叫什么名字？
- **What do you want for dinner?**  
  翻译: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **What are you doing now?**  
  翻译: 你现在在做什么？
- **What is she reading at the moment?**  
  翻译: 她这会儿在读什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **What have you done?**  
  翻译: 你做了什么？
- **What has she achieved so far?**  
  翻译: 到目前为止她取得了什么成就？

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **What have you been doing all day?**  
  翻译: 你一整天都在做什么？
- **What has he been working on lately?**  
  翻译: 他最近一直在做什么？

#### Simple Past
- **What did he say?**  
  翻译: 他说了什么？
- **What happened yesterday?**  
  翻译: 昨天发生了什么事？

#### Past Continuous
- **What were you doing when I called?**  
  翻译: 我打电话时你在做什么？
- **What was she thinking about?**  
  翻译: 她当时在想什么？

#### Past Perfect
- **What had happened before I arrived?**  
  翻译: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？
- **What had they discussed before the meeting?**  
  翻译: 会议前他们讨论了什么？

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **What had you been doing before the accident?**  
  翻译: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
- **What had they been discussing for so long?**  
  翻译: 他们一直在讨论什么？

#### Simple Future
- **What will you do tomorrow?**  
  翻译: 你明天要做什么？
- **What will the weather be like next week?**  
  翻译: 下周天气会怎样？

#### Future Continuous
- **What will you be doing this time tomorrow?**  
  翻译: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
- **What will they be discussing at the meeting?**  
  翻译: 会议上他们会讨论什么？

#### Future Perfect
- **What will you have accomplished by next year?**  
  翻译: 到明年你会完成什么？
- **What will have changed by then?**  
  翻译: 到那时会有什么变化？

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **What will you have been doing for ten years?**  
  翻译: 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？
- **What will she have been studying by graduation?**  
  翻译: 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **If I knew what to do, I would do it.**  
  翻译: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。
- **I wish I knew what was going on.**  
  翻译: 我希望我知道发生了什么。

#### Passive Voice
- **What was decided at the meeting?**  
  翻译: 会议上决定了什么？
- **What is being done to solve the problem?**  
  翻译: 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **What time is it?**  
  翻译: 现在几点了？
- **What are you talking about?**  
  翻译: 你在说什么？

#### Negative Sentences
- **I don't know what to say.**  
  翻译: 我不知道该说什么。
- **She didn't tell me what she wanted.**  
  翻译: 她没有告诉我她想要什么。

### Common Phrases and Idioms
#### Examples
- **What's up?**  
  翻译: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
- **What the hell!**  
  翻译: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **what if**: 如果...会怎么样
- **what for**: 为了什么
- **no matter what**: 无论如何
- **what's more**: 而且；更重要的是
- **what about**: ...怎么样
- **so what**: 那又怎样

这个解析提供了“what”这个单词的多种用法、例句和相关短语，帮助理解在不同语境下的应用。
