下面是对英文单词“what”的详细解析：

### Word: What

- **Phonetics:** /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech:** Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation:** 什么
- **Definition:** 用于询问信息关于某人或某事。

#### Examples:
- **Sentence:** What is your name?
  - **Translation:** 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms:
- which
- that

#### Antonyms:
- (无明显反义词)

### Usage:

1. **Simple Present:**
   - What do you want for dinner?
     - 你晚饭想吃什么？

2. **Present Continuous:**
   - What are you doing now?
     - 你现在在做什么？

3. **Present Perfect:**
   - What have you done?
     - 你做了什么？

4. **Present Perfect Continuous:**
   - What have you been doing all day?
     - 你一整天都在做什么？

5. **Simple Past:**
   - What did he say?
     - 他说了什么？

6. **Past Continuous:**
   - What were you doing when I called?
     - 我打电话时你在做什么？

7. **Past Perfect:**
   - What had happened before I arrived?
     - 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

8. **Past Perfect Continuous:**
   - What had you been doing before the accident?
     - 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

9. **Simple Future:**
   - What will you do tomorrow?
     - 你明天要做什么？

10. **Future Continuous:**
    - What will you be doing this time tomorrow?
      - 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

11. **Future Perfect:**
    - What will you have accomplished by next year?
      - 到明年你会完成什么？

12. **Future Perfect Continuous:**
    - What will she have been studying by graduation?
      - 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

13. **Subjunctive Mood:**
    - If I knew what to do, I would do it.
      - 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

14. **Passive Voice:**
    - What is being done to solve the problem?
      - 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

15. **Interrogative Sentences:**
    - What time is it?
      - 现在几点了？

16. **Negative Sentences:**
    - I don't know what to say.
      - 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases:

- **Examples:**
  - What's up?
    - 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  
- **Idioms:**
  - what if: 如果...会怎么样
  - what for: 为了什么
  - no matter what: 无论如何
  - what's more: 而且；更重要的是
  - what about: ...怎么样
  - so what: 那又怎样

这个解析涵盖了单词“what”的发音、用法、例句，以及相关短语和成语，能够帮助更好地理解和使用这个词。
