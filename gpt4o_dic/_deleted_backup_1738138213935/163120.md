以下是对英文单词“precolor”的详细解析：

**Word**: precolor  
**Phonetics**: /ˈpriːˌkʌlər/  
**Part of Speech**: verb (transitive)  
**Translation**: 预染  
**Definition**: To apply a color to a material, such as fabric or hair, before the main coloring process.  
**Example**: Before dyeing the fabric, we decided to precolor it for better results.  
**Synonyms**: base color, undercoat  
**Antonyms**: none  

**Usage**:
1. **Simple Present**:  
   - **Question**: What do you use to precolor the fabric?  
   - **Translation**: 你用什么来预染这块布料？
  
2. **Present Continuous**:  
   - **Question**: Are you precoloring the hair before applying the final dye?  
   - **Translation**: 你在给头发预染吗，然后再上最后的染发剂？
  
3. **Simple Past**:  
   - **Question**: Did you precolor the material yesterday?  
   - **Translation**: 你昨天预染了材料吗？
  
4. **Future Simple**:  
   - **Question**: Will you precolor the canvas before starting the painting?  
   - **Translation**: 你会在开始画画之前给画布预染吗？

**Phrases**:
- **What’s the process for precoloring?**  
  - **Translation**: 预染的过程是什么？
  
- **Precoloring is essential for achieving depth in color**.  
  - **Translation**: 预染对于实现颜色的深度至关重要。

**Context**: Precoloring is often used in various industries, such as textiles and hairdressing. In the textile industry, it helps improve color vibrancy and depth. In hairdressing, precoloring can prepare the hair for more even and lasting color application.
