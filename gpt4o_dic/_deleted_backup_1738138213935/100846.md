Here’s a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Basic Information
- **Word**: What
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？
- **Sentence**: What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Usage in Different Tenses
1. **Simple Present**:
   - Example: What is your name?  
   - Translation: 你叫什么名字？

2. **Present Continuous**:
   - Example: What are you doing now?  
   - Translation: 你现在在做什么？

3. **Present Perfect**:
   - Example: What have you done?  
   - Translation: 你做了什么？

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**:
   - Example: What have you been doing all day?  
   - Translation: 你一整天都在做什么？

5. **Simple Past**:
   - Example: What did he say?  
   - Translation: 他说了什么？

6. **Past Continuous**:
   - Example: What were you doing when I called?  
   - Translation: 我打电话时你在做什么？

7. **Past Perfect**:
   - Example: What had happened before I arrived?  
   - Translation: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**:
   - Example: What had you been doing before the accident?  
   - Translation: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

9. **Simple Future**:
   - Example: What will you do tomorrow?  
   - Translation: 你明天要做什么？

10. **Future Continuous**:
    - Example: What will you be doing this time tomorrow?  
    - Translation: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

11. **Future Perfect**:
    - Example: What will you have accomplished by next year?  
    - Translation: 到明年你会完成什么？

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**:
    - Example: What will you have been doing for ten years?  
    - Translation: 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.
  - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?
  - **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Question**: What time is it?
  - **Translation**: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Example**: I don't know what to say.
  - **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Phrases**:
  - What’s up?  
    - Translation: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  - What the hell!  
    - Translation: 搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms**:
  - What if: 如果...会怎么样
  - What for: 为了什么
  - No matter what: 无论如何
  - What’s more: 而且；更重要的是
  - What about: ...怎么样
  - So what: 那又怎样

This comprehensive analysis provides a thorough understanding of the word "what" in various contexts, tenses, and usages.
