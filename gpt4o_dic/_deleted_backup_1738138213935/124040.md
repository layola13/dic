Here's a detailed breakdown of the English word "what":

### Morphemes of "what"
- **Word:** what
- **Phonetics:** /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech:** Pronoun (pron.), Adjective (adj.), Adverb (adv.), Conjunction (conj.)
- **Translation:** 什么

### Definition
"What" is primarily used to inquire about information regarding persons or objects.

### Example
- **Sentence:** What is your name?
- **Translation:** 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- (none)

### Usage in Different Tenses
#### Simple Present
- **Question:** What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation:** 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Question:** What are you doing now?
  - **Translation:** 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Question:** What have you done?
  - **Translation:** 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Question:** What did he say?
  - **Translation:** 他说了什么？

#### Future Simple
- **Question:** What will you do tomorrow?
  - **Translation:** 你明天要做什么？

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example:** If I knew what to do, I would do it.
  - **Translation:** 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- **Example:** What was decided at the meeting?
  - **Translation:** 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Question:** What time is it?
  - **Translation:** 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Example:** I don't know what to say.
  - **Translation:** 我不知道该说什么。

### Common Phrases
- **Phrase:** What's up?
  - **Translation:** 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
- **Phrase:** What the hell!
  - **Translation:** 搞什么鬼！

### Idioms
- **What if:** 如果...会怎么样
- **What for:** 为了什么
- **No matter what:** 无论如何
- **What's more:** 而且；更重要的是
- **What about:** ...怎么样
- **So what:** 那又怎样

This comprehensive analysis highlights "what" as a crucial term for asking questions and eliciting information in various linguistic contexts.
