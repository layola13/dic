Here is a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
"What" is used to ask for information regarding someone or something.

### Example
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- None

### Usage

#### Simple Present
- **Questions**: 
  - "What is your name?" (Translation: 你叫什么名字？)
  - "What do you want for dinner?" (Translation: 你晚饭想吃什么？)

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What are you doing now?" (Translation: 你现在在做什么？)
  - "What is she reading at the moment?" (Translation: 她这会儿在读什么？)

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**: 
  - "What have you done?" (Translation: 你做了什么？)
  - "What has she achieved so far?" (Translation: 到目前为止她取得了什么成就？)

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What have you been doing all day?" (Translation: 你一整天都在做什么？)
  - "What has he been working on lately?" (Translation: 他最近一直在做什么？)

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**: 
  - "What did he say?" (Translation: 他说了什么？)
  - "What happened yesterday?" (Translation: 昨天发生了什么事？)

#### Past Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What were you doing when I called?" (Translation: 我打电话时你在做什么？)
  - "What was she thinking about?" (Translation: 她当时在想什么？)

#### Past Perfect
- **Questions**: 
  - "What had happened before I arrived?" (Translation: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？)
  - "What had they discussed before the meeting?" (Translation: 会议前他们讨论了什么？)

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What had you been doing before the accident?" (Translation: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？)
  - "What had they been discussing for so long?" (Translation: 他们一直在讨论什么？)

#### Simple Future
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will you do tomorrow?" (Translation: 你明天要做什么？)
  - "What will the weather be like next week?" (Translation: 下周天气会怎样？)

#### Future Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (Translation: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？)
  - "What will they be discussing at the meeting?" (Translation: 会议上他们会讨论什么？)

#### Future Perfect
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will you have accomplished by next year?" (Translation: 到明年你会完成什么？)
  - "What will have changed by then?" (Translation: 到那时会有什么变化？)

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will you have been doing for ten years?" (Translation: 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)
  - "What will she have been studying by graduation?" (Translation: 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**: 
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (Translation: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
  - "I wish I knew what was going on." (Translation: 我希望我知道发生了什么。)

#### Passive Voice
- **Examples**: 
  - "What was decided at the meeting?" (Translation: 会议上决定了什么？)
  - "What is being done to solve the problem?" (Translation: 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**: 
  - "What time is it?" (Translation: 现在几点了？)
  - "What are you talking about?" (Translation: 你在说什么？)

#### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**: 
  - "I don't know what to say." (Translation: 我不知道该说什么。)
  - "She didn't tell me what she wanted." (Translation: 她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

### Phrases

#### Common Expressions
- **Examples**: 
  - "What's up?" (Translation: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - "What the hell!" (Translation: 搞什么鬼！)

#### Idioms
- **Examples**: 
  - "what if" (Translation: 如果...会怎么样)
  - "what for" (Translation: 为了什么)
  - "no matter what" (Translation: 无论如何)
  - "what's more" (Translation: 而且；更重要的是)
  - "what about" (Translation: ...怎么样)
  - "so what" (Translation: 那又怎样)

This comprehensive analysis covers the various uses and forms of the word "what" in English, along with relevant examples and translations.
