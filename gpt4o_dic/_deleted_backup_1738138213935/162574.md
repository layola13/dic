### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

#### Definition
"**What**" is used to ask for information about someone or something, seeking clarification or specifics.

#### Examples
- **Inquiry**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)

#### Synonyms
- Which
- That

#### Antonyms
- None specified

### Usage by Tense

#### Simple Present
- **Questions**:
  - "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What are you doing now?" (你现在在做什么？)

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**:
  - "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)

#### Past Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What were you doing when I called?" (我打电话时你在做什么？)

#### Past Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - "What had happened before I arrived?" (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What had they been discussing for so long?" (他们一直在讨论什么？)

#### Simple Future
- **Questions**:
  - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)

#### Future Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What will they be discussing at the meeting?" (会议上他们会讨论什么？)

#### Future Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - "What will have changed by then?" (到那时会有什么变化？)

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What will she have been studying by graduation?" (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

#### Subjunctive Mood
- If I knew **what** to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
- I wish I knew **what** was going on. (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

#### Passive Voice
- **Questions**:
  - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**:
  - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)

#### Negative Sentences
- "I don't know **what** to say." (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases

#### Common Examples
- "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
- "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

#### Idioms
- "What if" (如果...会怎么样)
- "What for" (为了什么)
- "No matter what" (无论如何)
- "What's more" (而且；更重要的是)
- "What about" (...怎么样)
- "So what" (那又怎样)
