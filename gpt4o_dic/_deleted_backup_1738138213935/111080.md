Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Detail
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Example
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- **Related Words**: which, that

### Usage

#### 1. Simple Present
- **Questions**:
  - What do you want for dinner? (你晚饭想吃什么？)
  
#### 2. Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What are you doing now? (你现在在做什么？)

#### 3. Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What have you done? (你做了什么？)

#### 4. Present Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What have you been doing all day? (你一整天都在做什么？)

#### 5. Simple Past
- **Questions**:
  - What did he say? (他说了什么？)

#### 6. Past Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What were you doing when I called? (我打电话时你在做什么？)

#### 7. Past Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What had happened before I arrived? (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

#### 8. Past Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What had you been doing before the accident? (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)

#### 9. Simple Future
- **Questions**:
  - What will you do tomorrow? (你明天要做什么？)

#### 10. Future Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What will you be doing this time tomorrow? (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

#### 11. Future Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have accomplished by next year? (到明年你会完成什么？)

#### 12. Future Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have been doing for ten years? (到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)

#### 13. Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

#### 14. Passive Voice
- **Examples**:
  - What was decided at the meeting? (会议上决定了什么？)

#### 15. Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**:
  - What time is it? (现在几点了？)
  
#### 16. Negative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases
- **Examples**:
  - **Phrase**: What's up?  
    - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

- **Idioms**:
  - **Phrase**: what if  
    - **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
  - **Phrase**: what for  
    - **Translation**: 为了什么
  - **Phrase**: no matter what  
    - **Translation**: 无论如何
  - **Phrase**: what's more  
    - **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是
  - **Phrase**: what about  
    - **Translation**: ...怎么样
  - **Phrase**: so what  
    - **Translation**: 那又怎样

This analysis provides a comprehensive overview of the different usages, contexts, and related phrases for the word "what."
