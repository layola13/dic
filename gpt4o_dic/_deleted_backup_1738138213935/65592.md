### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to inquire about information regarding someone or something.
- **Example**: What is your name?
- **Synonyms**: which, that
- **Antonyms**: None

#### Usage in Different Tenses:

1. **Simple Present**:
   - **Example**: What is your name? (你叫什么名字？)
   - **Example**: What do you want for dinner? (你晚饭想吃什么？)

2. **Present Continuous**:
   - **Example**: What are you doing now? (你现在在做什么？)
   - **Example**: What is she reading at the moment? (她这会儿在读什么？)

3. **Present Perfect**:
   - **Example**: What have you done? (你做了什么？)
   - **Example**: What has she achieved so far? (到目前为止她取得了什么成就？)

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**:
   - **Example**: What have you been doing all day? (你一整天都在做什么？)
   - **Example**: What has he been working on lately? (他最近一直在做什么？)

5. **Simple Past**:
   - **Example**: What did he say? (他说了什么？)
   - **Example**: What happened yesterday? (昨天发生了什么事？)

6. **Past Continuous**:
   - **Example**: What were you doing when I called? (我打电话时你在做什么？)
   - **Example**: What was she thinking about? (她当时在想什么？)

7. **Past Perfect**:
   - **Example**: What had happened before I arrived? (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)
   - **Example**: What had they discussed before the meeting? (会议前他们讨论了什么？)

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**:
   - **Example**: What had you been doing before the accident? (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)
   - **Example**: What had they been discussing for so long? (他们一直在讨论什么？)

9. **Simple Future**:
   - **Example**: What will you do tomorrow? (你明天要做什么？)
   - **Example**: What will the weather be like next week? (下周天气会怎样？)

10. **Future Continuous**:
    - **Example**: What will you be doing this time tomorrow? (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)
    - **Example**: What will they be discussing at the meeting? (会议上他们会讨论什么？)

11. **Future Perfect**:
    - **Example**: What will you have accomplished by next year? (到明年你会完成什么？)
    - **Example**: What will have changed by then? (到那时会有什么变化？)

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**:
    - **Example**: What will you have been doing for ten years? (到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)
    - **Example**: What will she have been studying by graduation? (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

13. **Subjunctive Mood**:
    - **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
    - **Example**: I wish I knew what was going on. (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

14. **Passive Voice**:
    - **Example**: What was decided at the meeting? (会议上决定了什么？)
    - **Example**: What is being done to solve the problem? (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

15. **Interrogative Sentences**:
    - **Example**: What time is it? (现在几点了？)
    - **Example**: What are you talking about? (你在说什么？)

16. **Negative Sentences**:
    - **Example**: I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)
    - **Example**: She didn't tell me what she wanted. (她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

#### Phrases and Idioms:

- **Common Expressions**:
    - **Phrase**: What's up? (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
    - **Phrase**: What the hell! (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
    - **Idiom**: what if (如果...会怎么样)
    - **Idiom**: what for (为了什么)
    - **Idiom**: no matter what (无论如何)
    - **Idiom**: what's more (而且；更重要的是)
    - **Idiom**: what about (...怎么样)
    - **Idiom**: so what (那又怎样)

This comprehensive analysis illustrates the versatility and utility of the word "what" in English language usage.
