以下是对英文单词“what”的详细解析：

### Word Details
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
“what”用于询问有关某人或某事的信息。

### Example
- **Sentence**: What is your name?  
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- None

### Usage
#### Simple Present
1. **Question**: What do you want for dinner?  
   **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
1. **Question**: What are you doing now?  
   **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
1. **Question**: What have you done?  
   **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Present Perfect Continuous
1. **Question**: What have you been doing all day?  
   **Translation**: 你一整天都在做什么？

#### Simple Past
1. **Question**: What did he say?  
   **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Past Continuous
1. **Question**: What were you doing when I called?  
   **Translation**: 我打电话时你在做什么？

#### Past Perfect
1. **Question**: What had happened before I arrived?  
   **Translation**: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

#### Past Perfect Continuous
1. **Question**: What had you been doing before the accident?  
   **Translation**: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

#### Simple Future
1. **Question**: What will you do tomorrow?  
   **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

#### Future Continuous
1. **Question**: What will you be doing this time tomorrow?  
   **Translation**: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

#### Future Perfect
1. **Question**: What will you have accomplished by next year?  
   **Translation**: 到明年你会完成什么？

#### Future Perfect Continuous
1. **Question**: What will she have been studying by graduation?  
   **Translation**: 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

#### Subjunctive Mood
1. **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
   **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

#### Passive Voice
1. **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?  
   **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

#### Interrogative Sentences
1. **Question**: What time is it?  
   **Translation**: 现在几点了？

#### Negative Sentences
1. **Example**: I don't know what to say.  
   **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
#### Common Expressions
1. **Phrase**: What's up?  
   **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
2. **Phrase**: What the hell!  
   **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
1. **Idiom**: what if  
   **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
2. **Idiom**: what for  
   **Translation**: 为了什么
3. **Idiom**: no matter what  
   **Translation**: 无论如何
4. **Idiom**: what's more  
   **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是
5. **Idiom**: what about  
   **Translation**: ...怎么样
6. **Idiom**: so what  
   **Translation**: 那又怎样

这个详细的解析展示了“what”在各种语法结构中的用法及其多重含义，便于理解和应用。
