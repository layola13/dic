**Word Analysis: "what"**

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Example Usage:
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"
- **Translation**: "你叫什么名字？"

### Synonyms:
- which
- that

### Antonyms:
- None provided.

### Usage in Different Tenses:
- **Simple Present**:
  - Question: "What do you want for dinner?"
  - Translation: "你晚饭想吃什么？"

- **Present Continuous**:
  - Question: "What are you doing now?"
  - Translation: "你现在在做什么？"

- **Present Perfect**:
  - Question: "What have you done?"
  - Translation: "你做了什么？"

- **Simple Past**:
  - Question: "What did he say?"
  - Translation: "他说了什么？"

- **Future Simple**:
  - Question: "What will you do tomorrow?"
  - Translation: "你明天要做什么？"

### Subjunctive Mood:
- Example: "If I knew what to do, I would do it."
- Translation: "如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。"

### Passive Voice:
- Example: "What was decided at the meeting?"
- Translation: "会议上决定了什么？"

### Interrogative Sentences:
- Question: "What time is it?"
- Translation: "现在几点了？"

### Negative Sentences:
- Example: "I don't know what to say."
- Translation: "我不知道该说什么。"

### Phrases:
- **Examples**:
  - Phrase: "What's up?"
    - Translation: "怎么了？/ 你好吗？"
  - Phrase: "What the hell!"
    - Translation: "搞什么鬼！"

- **Idioms**:
  - Phrase: "what if"
    - Translation: "如果...会怎么样"
  - Phrase: "what for"
    - Translation: "为了什么"
  - Phrase: "no matter what"
    - Translation: "无论如何"
  - Phrase: "what's more"
    - Translation: "而且；更重要的是"
  - Phrase: "what about"
    - Translation: "...怎么样"
  - Phrase: "so what"
    - Translation: "那又怎样"

This comprehensive overview highlights the multifaceted uses of the word "what" in English, illustrating its versatility in questioning, defining, and expressing circumstances.
