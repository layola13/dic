Here’s a detailed analysis of the English word "what."

### Word Overview
- **Word**: What
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
"What" is primarily used to ask for information regarding someone or something, indicating an inquiry.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"
- **Translation**: "你叫什么名字？"

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Antonyms
N/A

### Usage in Different Tenses

1. **Simple Present**
   - Questions:
     - "What do you want for dinner?"
     - **Translation**: "你晚饭想吃什么？"

2. **Present Continuous**
   - Questions:
     - "What are you doing now?"
     - **Translation**: "你现在在做什么？"

3. **Present Perfect**
   - Questions:
     - "What have you done?"
     - **Translation**: "你做了什么？"

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**
   - Questions:
     - "What have you been doing all day?"
     - **Translation**: "你一整天都在做什么？"

5. **Simple Past**
   - Questions:
     - "What did he say?"
     - **Translation**: "他说了什么？"

6. **Past Continuous**
   - Questions:
     - "What were you doing when I called?"
     - **Translation**: "我打电话时你在做什么？"

7. **Past Perfect**
   - Questions:
     - "What had happened before I arrived?"
     - **Translation**: "在我到达之前发生了什么事？"

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**
   - Questions:
     - "What had you been doing before the accident?"
     - **Translation**: "事故发生前你一直在做什么？"

9. **Simple Future**
   - Questions:
     - "What will you do tomorrow?"
     - **Translation**: "你明天要做什么？"

10. **Future Continuous**
    - Questions:
      - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?"
      - **Translation**: "明天这个时候你会在做什么？"

11. **Future Perfect**
    - Questions:
      - "What will you have accomplished by next year?"
      - **Translation**: "到明年你会完成什么？"

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**
    - Questions:
      - "What will you have been doing for ten years?"
      - **Translation**: "到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？"

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**:
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it."
  - **Translation**: "如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。"

### Passive Voice
- **Examples**:
  - "What was decided at the meeting?"
  - **Translation**: "会议上决定了什么？"

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**:
  - "What time is it?"
  - **Translation**: "现在几点了？"

### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - "I don't know what to say."
  - **Translation**: "我不知道该说什么。"

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)
  
- **Idioms**:
  - "What if" (如果...会怎么样)
  - "What for" (为了什么)
  - "No matter what" (无论如何)
  - "What's more" (而且；更重要的是)
  - "What about" (...怎么样)
  - "So what" (那又怎样)
