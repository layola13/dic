Here's a detailed analysis of the word **"what"**:

### Basic Information
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
**"What"** is primarily used to ask for information about someone or something, serving as a key interrogative word in English.

### Examples
- **Simple Present**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)
- **Present Continuous**: "What are you doing now?" (你现在在做什么？)
- **Present Perfect**: "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)
- **Simple Past**: "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)
- **Simple Future**: "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
(There are no direct antonyms for "what" as it serves as an interrogative.)

### Usage in Different Tenses
- **Present Perfect Continuous**: "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)
- **Future Perfect**: "What will you have accomplished by next year?" (到明年你会完成什么？)

### Subjunctive Mood Examples
- "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
- "I wish I knew what was going on." (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

### Passive Voice Examples
- "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)
- "What is being done to solve the problem?" (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

### Interrogative Sentences
- "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)
- "What are you talking about?" (你在说什么？)

### Negative Sentences
- "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)
- "She didn't tell me what she wanted." (她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

### Phrases & Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - "What if" (如果...会怎么样)
  - "What for" (为了什么)
  - "No matter what" (无论如何)
  - "What's more" (而且；更重要的是)
  - "What about" (...怎么样)
  - "So what" (那又怎样)
