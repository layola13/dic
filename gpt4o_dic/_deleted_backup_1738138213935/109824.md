Certainly! Here’s a detailed lexical analysis of the word "what":

### Word Overview
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun (pron.), Adjective (adj.), Adverb (adv.), Conjunction (conj.)
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **General Example**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)

### Synonyms
- **Similar Words**: which, that

### Usage in Different Tenses
1. **Simple Present**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)

2. **Present Continuous**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What are you doing now?" (你现在在做什么？)

3. **Present Perfect**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)

5. **Simple Past**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)

6. **Past Continuous**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What were you doing when I called?" (我打电话时你在做什么？)

7. **Past Perfect**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What had happened before I arrived?" (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What had you been doing before the accident?" (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)

9. **Simple Future**
   - **Questions**: 
     - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)

10. **Future Continuous**
    - **Questions**: 
      - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

11. **Future Perfect**
    - **Questions**: 
      - "What will you have accomplished by next year?" (到明年你会完成什么？)

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**
    - **Questions**: 
      - "What will you have been doing for ten years?" (到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**: 
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

### Passive Voice
- **Examples**: 
  - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**: 
  - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)

### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**: 
  - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases and Idioms
1. **Everyday Phrases**:
   - "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
   - "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

2. **Idioms**:
   - "what if" (如果...会怎么样)
   - "what for" (为了什么)
   - "no matter what" (无论如何)
   - "what's more" (而且；更重要的是)
   - "what about" (...怎么样)
   - "so what" (那又怎样)

This analysis encapsulates the various uses and meanings of the word "what" in English, providing comprehensive context and examples for its application.
