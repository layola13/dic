### Word Analysis: "what"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Example Usage
- **Question**: What is your name?  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms
- which
- that

#### Antonyms
- None

#### Usage by Tense

- **Simple Present**:
  - *Question*: What do you want for dinner?  
    *Translation*: 你晚饭想吃什么？

- **Present Continuous**:
  - *Question*: What are you doing now?  
    *Translation*: 你现在在做什么？

- **Present Perfect**:
  - *Question*: What have you done?  
    *Translation*: 你做了什么？

- **Past Simple**:
  - *Question*: What did he say?  
    *Translation*: 他说了什么？

- **Future Simple**:
  - *Question*: What will you do tomorrow?  
    *Translation*: 你明天要做什么？

#### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - What's up? — 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  - What the hell! — 搞什么鬼！

- **Idiomatic Expressions**:
  - what if — 如果...会怎么样
  - what for — 为了什么
  - no matter what — 无论如何
  - what's more — 而且；更重要的是
  - what about — ...怎么样
  - so what — 那又怎样

#### Additional Usage
- **Subjunctive Mood**:
  - *Example*: If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
    *Translation*: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

- **Passive Voice**:
  - *Example*: What was decided at the meeting?  
    *Translation*: 会议上决定了什么？

- **Negative Sentences**:
  - *Example*: I don't know what to say.  
    *Translation*: 我不知道该说什么。

This structured analysis provides a comprehensive understanding of the word "what," illustrating its versatility and usage in different contexts.
