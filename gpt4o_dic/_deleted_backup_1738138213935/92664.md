### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Example Usage
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

- (none applicable)

### Usage in Different Tenses

#### Simple Present
- **Questions**: 
  - "What do you want for dinner?" 
    - *Translation*: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What are you doing now?" 
    - *Translation*: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - "What have you accomplished?"
    - *Translation*: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**:
  - "What did he say?"
    - *Translation*: 他说了什么？

#### Simple Future
- **Questions**:
  - "What will you do tomorrow?" 
    - *Translation*: 你明天要做什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - "What time is it?" 
    - *Translation*: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - "I don't know what to say."
    - *Translation*: 我不知道该说什么。
