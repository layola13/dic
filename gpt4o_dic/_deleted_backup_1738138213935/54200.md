### Detailed Analysis of the Word "disfancy"

| **Category**          | **Details**                                                                 |
|------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Word**              | disfancy                                                                   |
| **Phonetics**         | /dɪsˈfænsi/                                                                |
| **Part of Speech**    | verb                                                                       |
| **Translation**       | 不再喜欢；失去兴趣                                                          |
| **Definition**        | To lose interest in or cease to find enjoyment in something.               |
| **Example**           | She disfancied her old hobbies after moving to a new city.                 |
| **Synonyms**          | lose interest, tire of, grow weary of                                      |
| **Antonyms**          | fancy, enjoy
