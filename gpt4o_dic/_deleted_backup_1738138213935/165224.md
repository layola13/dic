Here’s a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Overview
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
"What" is primarily used to inquire about information regarding someone or something. It functions in various contexts, forming questions or clauses in sentences.

### Example Sentence
- **Example**: What is your name? (Translation: 你叫什么名字？)

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Usage
*What* can be used in various tenses and sentence structures:

- **Simple Present**:
  - Question: What do you want for dinner? (你晚饭想吃什么？)

- **Present Continuous**:
  - Question: What are you doing now? (你现在在做什么？)

- **Present Perfect**:
  - Question: What have you done? (你做了什么？)

- **Present Perfect Continuous**:
  - Question: What have you been doing all day? (你一整天都在做什么？)

- **Simple Past**:
  - Question: What did he say? (他说了什么？)

- **Past Continuous**:
  - Question: What were you doing when I called? (我打电话时你在做什么？)

- **Past Perfect**:
  - Question: What had happened before I arrived? (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

- **Past Perfect Continuous**:
  - Question: What had you been doing before the accident? (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)

- **Simple Future**:
  - Question: What will you do tomorrow? (你明天要做什么？)

- **Future Continuous**:
  - Question: What will you be doing this time tomorrow? (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

- **Future Perfect**:
  - Question: What will you have accomplished by next year? (到明年你会完成什么？)

- **Future Perfect Continuous**:
  - Question: What will she have been studying by graduation? (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

- **Subjunctive Mood**:
  - Example: I wish I knew what was going on. (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

- **Passive Voice**:
  - Example: What was decided at the meeting? (会议上决定了什么？)

- **Interrogative Sentences**:
  - Question: What time is it? (现在几点了？)

- **Negative Sentences**:
  - Example: I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - What's up? (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - What the hell! (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - what if (如果...会怎么样)
  - what for (为了什么)
  - no matter what (无论如何)
  - what’s more (而且；更重要的是)
  - what about (...怎么样)
  - so what (那又怎样)
