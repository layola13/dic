Here's a detailed analysis of the word "what":

### Word Analysis
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Example
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- None listed.

### Usage
#### Simple Present
- **Questions**:
  - What do you want for dinner?
    - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What are you doing now?
    - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What have you done?
    - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**:
  - What did he say?
    - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Future Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have accomplished by next year?
    - **Translation**: 到明年你会完成什么？

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Expressions**:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it.
    - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Phrases
#### Common Expressions
- **What's up?**
  - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
- **What the hell!**
  - **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **what if**
  - **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
- **what for**
  - **Translation**: 为了什么
- **no matter what**
  - **Translation**: 无论如何
- **what's more**
  - **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是
- **what about**
  - **Translation**: ...怎么样
- **so what**
  - **Translation**: 那又怎样
