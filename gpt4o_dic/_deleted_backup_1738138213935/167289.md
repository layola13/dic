### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Example Sentence
- **Usage**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)

#### Synonyms
- which
- that

#### Antonyms
- None

### Usage in Various Tenses

1. **Simple Present**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)

2. **Present Continuous**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What are you doing now?" (你现在在做什么？)

3. **Present Perfect**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)

5. **Simple Past**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)

6. **Past Continuous**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What were you doing when I called?" (我打电话时你在做什么？)

7. **Past Perfect**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What had happened before I arrived?" (在我到达之前发生了什么？)

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What had you been doing before the accident?" (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)

9. **Simple Future**
   - **Questions**:
     - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)

10. **Future Continuous**
    - **Questions**:
      - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

11. **Future Perfect**
    - **Questions**:
      - "What will you have accomplished by next year?" (到明年你会完成什么？)

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**
    - **Questions**:
      - "What will she have been studying by graduation?" (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

13. **Subjunctive Mood**
    - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

14. **Passive Voice**
    - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)

15. **Interrogative Sentences**
    - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)

16. **Negative Sentences**
    - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)

### Common Phrases and Idioms

#### Examples
- **What's up?** (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
- **What the hell!** (搞什么鬼！)

#### Idioms
- **what if** (如果...会怎么样)
- **what for** (为了什么)
- **no matter what** (无论如何)
- **what's more** (而且；更重要的是)
- **what about** (...怎么样)
- **so what** (那又怎样)

This comprehensive breakdown of "what" illustrates its versatility in English, showing its various uses across different grammatical structures.
