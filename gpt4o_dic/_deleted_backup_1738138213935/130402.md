### Detailed Analysis of the Word "Non-visualized"

**Word:** non-visualized  
**Phonetics:** /nɒn ˈvɪʒ.ʊ.aɪzd/  
**Part of Speech:** Adjective  
**Translation:** 非视觉化的  

**Definition:** Refers to concepts, ideas, or information that are not represented visually or that cannot be easily visualized. Often used in contexts such as data, thoughts, or experiences that lack a clear visual representation.

**Example:** The non-visualized concepts in the theory made it challenging to comprehend fully.

**Synonyms:** abstract, conceptual, intangible  
**Antonyms:** visualized, observable, concrete
