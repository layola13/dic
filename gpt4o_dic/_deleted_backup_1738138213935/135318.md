### Detailed Analysis of the English Word "What"

- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

#### Definition
"What" is used to ask for information about someone or something, making it one of the most common interrogative words in English.

#### Examples
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？
  
#### Synonyms
- which
- that

#### Antonyms
- None applicable

### Usage

#### 1. **Simple Present**
- **Questions**:
  - What do you want for dinner?
    - 你晚饭想吃什么？
  
#### 2. **Present Continuous**
- **Questions**:
  - What are you doing now?
    - 你现在在做什么？

#### 3. **Present Perfect**
- **Questions**:
  - What have you done?
    - 你做了什么？

#### 4. **Present Perfect Continuous**
- **Questions**:
  - What have you been doing all day?
    - 你一整天都在做什么？

#### 5. **Simple Past**
- **Questions**:
  - What did he say?
    - 他说了什么？

#### 6. **Past Continuous**
- **Questions**:
  - What were you doing when I called?
    - 我打电话时你在做什么？

#### 7. **Past Perfect**
- **Questions**:
  - What had happened before I arrived?
    - 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

#### 8. **Past Perfect Continuous**
- **Questions**:
  - What had you been doing before the accident?
    - 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

#### 9. **Simple Future**
- **Questions**:
  - What will you do tomorrow?
    - 你明天要做什么？

#### 10. **Future Continuous**
- **Questions**:
  - What will you be doing this time tomorrow?
    - 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

#### 11. **Future Perfect**
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have accomplished by next year?
    - 到明年你会完成什么？

#### 12. **Future Perfect Continuous**
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have been doing for ten years?
    - 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

#### 13. **Subjunctive Mood**
- **Examples**:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it.
    - 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。
  
#### 14. **Passive Voice**
- **Examples**:
  - What was decided at the meeting?
    - 会议上决定了什么？

#### 15. **Interrogative Sentences**
- **Questions**:
  - What time is it?
    - 现在几点了？

#### 16. **Negative Sentences**
- **Examples**:
  - I don't know what to say.
    - 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
- **Common Examples**:
  - "What's up?" 
    - 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
    
- **Idioms**:
  - "What if" 
    - 如果...会怎么样
  - "What for" 
    - 为了什么
  - "No matter what" 
    - 无论如何
  - "What's more" 
    - 而且；更重要的是
  - "What about" 
    - ...怎么样
  - "So what" 
    - 那又怎样

This comprehensive overview of "what" covers its use across various grammatical contexts and common phrases, helping to understand its versatility in the English language.
