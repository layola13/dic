### 详细解析英文单词 "prefinish"

**word:** prefiniš  
**phonetics:** /ˈprɛfɪnɪʃ/  
**part of speech:** verb  
**translation:** 预完成/预处理  

**definition:**  
To treat or prepare a surface prior to final finishing, often used in woodworking and manufacturing contexts.

**example:**  
"The carpenter decided to prefinish the cabinets before installation."

**synonyms:**  
- prepare  
- pre-treat  
- stage  

**antonyms:**  
- finish  
- complete  
- finalize
