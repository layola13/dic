### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Example
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- (None identified)

### Usage in Different Tenses

- **Simple Present**:
  - **Q**: What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

- **Present Continuous**:
  - **Q**: What are you doing now?
  - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

- **Present Perfect**:
  - **Q**: What have you done?
  - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

- **Present Perfect Continuous**:
  - **Q**: What have you been doing all day?
  - **Translation**: 你一整天都在做什么？

- **Simple Past**:
  - **Q**: What did he say?
  - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

- **Past Continuous**:
  - **Q**: What were you doing when I called?
  - **Translation**: 我打电话时你在做什么？

- **Past Perfect**:
  - **Q**: What had happened before I arrived?
  - **Translation**: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

- **Past Perfect Continuous**:
  - **Q**: What had you been doing before the accident?
  - **Translation**: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

- **Simple Future**:
  - **Q**: What will you do tomorrow?
  - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

- **Future Continuous**:
  - **Q**: What will you be doing this time tomorrow?
  - **Translation**: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

- **Future Perfect**:
  - **Q**: What will you have accomplished by next year?
  - **Translation**: 到明年你会完成什么？

- **Future Perfect Continuous**:
  - **Q**: What will she have been studying by graduation?
  - **Translation**: 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example**: "If I knew what to do, I would do it."
- **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- **Example**: "What was decided at the meeting?"
- **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Q**: What time is it?
- **Translation**: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Example**: "I don't know what to say."
- **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
- **Common Expressions**:
  - **Phrase**: "What's up?"
  - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

- **Idioms**:
  - **Phrase**: "What if"
  - **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
  - **Phrase**: "What for"
  - **Translation**: 为了什么
  - **Phrase**: "No matter what"
  - **Translation**: 无论如何
  - **Phrase**: "What's more"
  - **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是
  - **Phrase**: "What about"
  - **Translation**: ...怎么样
  - **Phrase**: "So what"
  - **Translation**: 那又怎样

This comprehensive analysis of "what" illustrates its versatility and importance in English communication, as well as its usage across various grammatical contexts.
