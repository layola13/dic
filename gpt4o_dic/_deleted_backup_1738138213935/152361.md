以下是对英文单词“paused”的详细解析：

### "paused"

- **Phonetics**: /pɔːzd/
- **Part of Speech**: verb (past tense of pause)
- **Translation**: 暂停
- **Definition**: To stop or cause to stop temporarily, often during an activity.

### Usage Examples

- **Simple Present**: 
  - "I pause the video to take notes." (我暂停视频来记笔记。)
  
- **Present Continuous**: 
  - "She is pausing to think." (她在暂停思考。)
  
- **Simple Past**: 
  - "He paused before answering." (他在回答前暂停了一下。)

- **Present Perfect**: 
  - "They have paused the discussion." (他们暂停了讨论。)

### Synonyms

- Temporarily stop
- Halt
- Break

### Antonyms

- Continue
- Proceed
- Go on

### Phrases and Idioms

- **Example**: 
  - "Take a pause." (稍作停顿。)
  
- **Idiom**: 
  - "Pause for thought." (停下来思考一下。)

### Contextual Use

- **In Music/Media**: When listening to music or watching a video, one might say, "I paused the movie at a suspenseful moment."
- **In Conversations**: People often pause to gather their thoughts, such as, "I paused for a moment to consider my response."

整体来说，"paused" 是一个常用的动词，适用于多种上下文，表达某种暂时停止的状态。
