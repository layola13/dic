### Detailed Analysis of the Word "What"

**Word:** what  
**Phonetics:** /wɑːt/  
**Part of Speech:** pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction  
**Translation:** 什么  

**Definition:**  
"What" is used to inquire about information regarding someone or something. It serves as a prompt for clarification, specifics, or details.

---

### Examples in Context:

- **General Example:** 
  - **Usage:** What is your name?  
    **Translation:** 你叫什么名字？

---

### Synonyms:
- which
- that

### Antonyms:
- none

---

### Usage Across Tenses:

- **Simple Present:**
  - What do you want for dinner?  
    Translation: 你晚饭想吃什么？

- **Present Continuous:**
  - What are you doing now?  
    Translation: 你现在在做什么？

- **Present Perfect:**
  - What have you done?  
    Translation: 你做了什么？

- **Present Perfect Continuous:**
  - What have you been doing all day?  
    Translation: 你一整天都在做什么？

- **Simple Past:**
  - What did he say?  
    Translation: 他说了什么？

- **Past Continuous:**
  - What were you doing when I called?  
    Translation: 我打电话时你在做什么？

- **Past Perfect:**
  - What had happened before I arrived?  
    Translation: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

- **Past Perfect Continuous:**
  - What had you been doing before the accident?  
    Translation: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

- **Simple Future:**
  - What will you do tomorrow?  
    Translation: 你明天要做什么？

- **Future Continuous:**
  - What will you be doing this time tomorrow?  
    Translation: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

- **Future Perfect:**
  - What will you have accomplished by next year?  
    Translation: 到明年你会完成什么？

- **Future Perfect Continuous:**
  - What will you have been doing for ten years?  
    Translation: 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

---

### Subjunctive Mood:
- If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
  Translation: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

---

### Passive Voice:
- What was decided at the meeting?  
  Translation: 会议上决定了什么？

---

### Interrogative Sentences:
- What time is it?  
  Translation: 现在几点了？

---

### Negative Sentences:
- I don't know what to say.  
  Translation: 我不知道该说什么。

---

### Phrases and Idioms:

**Common Phrases:**
- What's up?  
  Translation: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
- What the hell!  
  Translation: 搞什么鬼！

**Idioms:**
- what if: 如果...会怎么样
- what for: 为了什么
- no matter what: 无论如何
- what's more: 而且；更重要的是
- what about: ...怎么样
- so what: 那又怎样

This overview encapsulates various ways "what" can be employed in the English language, demonstrating its versatility as a word essential for inquiry and conversation.
