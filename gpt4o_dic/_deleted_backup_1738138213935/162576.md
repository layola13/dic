### Detailed Analysis of the Word "What"

#### Basic Information
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to inquire about the nature or identity of an object, person, or concept.

#### Example
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms
- which
- that

#### Antonyms
- None

### Usage in Different Tenses

1. **Simple Present**
   - **Question**: What do you want for dinner?
   - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？
   
2. **Present Continuous**
   - **Question**: What are you doing now?
   - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

3. **Present Perfect**
   - **Question**: What have you done?
   - **Translation**: 你做了什么？
   
4. **Present Perfect Continuous**
   - **Question**: What have you been doing all day?
   - **Translation**: 你一整天都在做什么？

5. **Simple Past**
   - **Question**: What did he say?
   - **Translation**: 他说了什么？
   
6. **Past Continuous**
   - **Question**: What were you doing when I called?
   - **Translation**: 我打电话时你在做什么？

7. **Past Perfect**
   - **Question**: What had happened before I arrived?
   - **Translation**: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？
   
8. **Past Perfect Continuous**
   - **Question**: What had you been doing before the accident?
   - **Translation**: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

9. **Simple Future**
   - **Question**: What will you do tomorrow?
   - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？
   
10. **Future Continuous**
    - **Question**: What will you be doing this time tomorrow?
    - **Translation**: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

11. **Future Perfect**
    - **Question**: What will you have accomplished by next year?
    - **Translation**: 到明年你会完成什么？

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**
    - **Question**: What will she have been studying by graduation?
    - **Translation**: 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

13. **Subjunctive Mood**
    - **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.
    - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

14. **Passive Voice**
    - **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?
    - **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

15. **Interrogative Sentences**
    - **Question**: What are you talking about?
    - **Translation**: 你在说什么？

16. **Negative Sentences**
    - **Example**: I don't know what to say.
    - **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Common Phrases and Idioms

#### Examples
- **Phrase**: What's up?
  - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  
- **Phrase**: What the hell!
  - **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **Phrase**: what if
  - **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
  
- **Phrase**: what for
  - **Translation**: 为了什么
  
- **Phrase**: no matter what
  - **Translation**: 无论如何
  
- **Phrase**: what's more
  - **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是
  
- **Phrase**: what about
  - **Translation**: ...怎么样
  
- **Phrase**: so what
  - **Translation**: 那又怎样

This comprehensive breakdown provides a clear understanding of the word "what," its applications, and its role in English language structure.
