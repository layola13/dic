以下是单词“what”的详细解析：

### Basic Information
- **Word:** what
- **Phonetics:** /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech:** Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation:** 什么

### Definition
“what”用于询问关于某人或某事的信息。

### Example
- **Sentence:** What is your name?
  
### Synonyms and Antonyms
- **Synonyms:** which, that
- **Antonyms:** None

### Usage
#### Simple Present
- **Example:** What do you want for dinner?  
  **Translation:** 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Example:** What are you doing now?  
  **Translation:** 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Example:** What have you done?  
  **Translation:** 你做了什么？

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Example:** What have you been doing all day?  
  **Translation:** 你一整天都在做什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Example:** What did he say?  
  **Translation:** 他说了什么？

#### Past Continuous
- **Example:** What were you doing when I called?  
  **Translation:** 我打电话时你在做什么？

#### Past Perfect
- **Example:** What had happened before I arrived?  
  **Translation:** 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **Example:** What had you been doing before the accident?  
  **Translation:** 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

#### Simple Future
- **Example:** What will you do tomorrow?  
  **Translation:** 你明天要做什么？

#### Future Continuous
- **Example:** What will you be doing this time tomorrow?  
  **Translation:** 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

#### Future Perfect
- **Example:** What will you have accomplished by next year?  
  **Translation:** 到明年你会完成什么？

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **Example:** What will you have been doing for ten years?  
  **Translation:** 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example:** If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
  **Translation:** 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

#### Passive Voice
- **Example:** What was decided at the meeting?  
  **Translation:** 会议上决定了什么？

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **Example:** What time is it?  
  **Translation:** 现在几点了？

#### Negative Sentences
- **Example:** I don't know what to say.  
  **Translation:** 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
#### Examples
- **Phrase:** What's up?  
  **Translation:** 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

#### Idioms
- **Idioms:**  
  - What if (如果...会怎么样)  
  - What for (为了什么)  
  - No matter what (无论如何)  
  - What's more (而且；更重要的是)  
  - What about (...怎么样)  
  - So what (那又怎样)  

这个解析帮助你更好地理解“what”的含义、用法及其在不同句型中的应用。
