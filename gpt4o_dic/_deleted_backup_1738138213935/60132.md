### Word Analysis: "effectivenesses"

- **Word**: effectivenesses
- **Phonetics**: /ɪˈfɛktɪvnəsɪz/
- **Part of Speech**: noun (plural)
- **Translation**: 效率 (复数形式)
  
- **Definition**: The state or quality of being effective; the degree to which something is successful in producing a desired result.

- **Example**: The effectivenesses of the training programs varied significantly across different departments.

- **Synonyms**: efficiency, potency, efficacy
- **Antonyms**: ineffectiveness, inefficiency, failure
