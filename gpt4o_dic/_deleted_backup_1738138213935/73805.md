Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information regarding someone or something.

### Examples:
- **General Example**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)

### Synonyms:
- which
- that

### Antonyms:
- (None)

### Usage

#### Simple Present
- **Questions**: 
  - "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What is she reading at the moment?" (她这会儿在读什么？)

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**:
  - "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)

#### Past Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What was she thinking about?" (她当时在想什么？)

#### Past Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - "What had happened before I arrived?" (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What had they been discussing for so long?" (他们一直在讨论什么？)

#### Simple Future
- **Questions**:
  - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)

#### Future Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

#### Future Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - "What will you have accomplished by next year?" (到明年你会完成什么？)

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - "What will she have been studying by graduation?" (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**:
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

#### Passive Voice
- **Examples**:
  - "What is being done to solve the problem?" (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**:
  - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)

#### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases

#### Common Examples
- "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
- "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

#### Idioms
- "what if" (如果...会怎么样)
- "what for" (为了什么)
- "no matter what" (无论如何)
- "what's more" (而且；更重要的是)
- "what about" (...怎么样)
- "so what" (那又怎样)

This comprehensive overview captures the versatility of "what" in various contexts, showcasing its importance in English communication.
