### Context on Technologism
**Technologism** often reflects a mindset where technology is viewed as a panacea for various societal problems, sometimes leading to neglect of ethical, social, or cultural dimensions. It can drive debates about the role of technology in education, policy-making, and how it shapes human interactions.
