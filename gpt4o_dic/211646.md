### Detailed Analysis of the Word "What"

#### Basic Information
- **Word:** what
- **Phonetics:** /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech:** pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation (Chinese):** 什么

#### Definition
"What" is primarily used to ask for information regarding an object, person, idea, or situation. It can structure questions that seek clarification or details.

#### Examples
- **Question:** What is your name?
  - **Translation:** 你叫什么名字？
  
- **Question:** What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation:** 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Synonyms
- which
- that

#### Antonyms
- None specified.

#### Usage in Different Tenses

1. **Simple Present**
   - **Question:** What do you want for dinner?
   - **Translation:** 你晚饭想吃什么？

2. **Present Continuous**
   - **Question:** What are you doing now?
   - **Translation:** 你现在在做什么？

3. **Present Perfect**
   - **Question:** What have you done?
   - **Translation:** 你做了什么？

4. **Simple Past**
   - **Question:** What did he say?
   - **Translation:** 他说了什么？
  
5. **Future Simple**
   - **Question:** What will you do tomorrow?
   - **Translation:** 你明天要做什么？

#### Special Uses

- **Subjunctive Mood**
  - **Example:** If I knew what to do, I would do it.
  - **Translation:** 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

- **Passive Voice**
  - **Example:** What was decided at the meeting?
  - **Translation:** 会议上决定了什么？

- **Interrogative Sentences**
  - **Question:** What time is it?
  - **Translation:** 现在几点了？

- **Negative Sentences**
  - **Example:** I don't know what to say.
  - **Translation:** 我不知道该说什么。

#### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases:**
  - **Phrase:** What's up?
    - **Translation:** 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  - **Phrase:** What the hell!
    - **Translation:** 搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms:**
  - **Phrase:** what if
    - **Translation:** 如果...会怎么样
  - **Phrase:** no matter what
    - **Translation:** 无论如何
  - **Phrase:** what's more
    - **Translation:** 而且；更重要的是

This encapsulated overview of 'what' highlights its multifaceted use in English, showcasing its significance in everyday conversation and various grammatical structures.
