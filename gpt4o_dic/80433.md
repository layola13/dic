### Context and Importance
Gondolin is significant in Tolkien's legendarium, representing an idealized world of beauty and culture that ultimately meets a tragic end. Its story is highlighted in "The Silmarillion," emphasizing themes of hope, loss, and heroism. The city serves as a backdrop for major events in the narratives, including tales of the Elves and their struggles against darkness.
