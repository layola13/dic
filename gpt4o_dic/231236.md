### 详细解析英文单词 {undermist}

**word:** undermist  
**phonetics:** /ˈʌndərmɪst/  
**part_of_speech:** noun  

**translation:** 地雾（或低雾）

**definition:** A term used to describe a layer of mist or fog that appears near the ground, often obscuring visibility and creating atmospheric effects.

**example:** The valley was shrouded in a dense undermist, making it difficult to see the landscape.

**synonyms:** fog, haze, mist  
**antonyms:** clarity, brightness, visibility

#### Usage

- **simple_present:**
  - **Sentence:** The undermist covers the fields in the early morning.
  - **Translation:** 清晨，地雾覆盖了田野。
  
- **present_continuous:**
  - **Sentence:** The undermist is rolling in from the river.
  - **Translation:** 地雾正从河边涌来。
  
- **simple_past:**
  - **Sentence:** The undermist enveloped the city last night.
  - **Translation:** 昨晚，地雾笼罩了城市。
  
- **future_simple:**
  - **Sentence:** The undermist will settle in the valley by dusk.
  - **Translation:** 到黄昏时，地雾将会在山谷中弥漫。

#### Phrases and Idioms

- **examples:**
  - **Phrase:** "The scene was like a painting with the undermist."
  - **Translation:** "这幕景象如同一幅画，上面笼罩着地雾。"

- **idioms:**
  - **Phrase:** "In the undermist of uncertainty."
  - **Translation:** "在不确定的地雾中。"
