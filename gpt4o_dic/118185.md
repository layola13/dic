### Word Analysis: "what"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun (pron.), Adjective (adj.), Adverb (adv.), Conjunction (conj.)
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

---

#### Examples:
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

---

### Synonyms:
- **which**
- **that**

### Antonyms:
- None

---

### Usage in Various Tenses:

1. **Simple Present**:
   - **Question**: "What do you want for dinner?"
     - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

2. **Present Continuous**:
   - **Question**: "What are you doing now?"
     - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

3. **Present Perfect**:
   - **Question**: "What have you done?"
     - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

4. **Past Tenses**:
   - **Simple Past**: "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)
   - **Past Continuous**: "What were you doing when I called?" (我打电话时你在做什么？)
   - **Past Perfect**: "What had happened before I arrived?" (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

5. **Future Tenses**:
   - **Simple Future**: "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)
   - **Future Continuous**: "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

6. **Subjunctive Mood**:
   - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

7. **Passive Voice**:
   - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)

8. **Interrogative Sentences**:
   - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)

9. **Negative Sentences**:
   - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)

---

### Common Phrases and Idioms:

- **Everyday Phrases**:
  - "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - "what if" (如果...会怎么样)
  - "what for" (为了什么)
  - "no matter what" (无论如何)
  - "what's more" (而且；更重要的是)
  - "what about" (...怎么样)
  - "so what" (那又怎样)

---
