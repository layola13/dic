### 单词详解: subframes

- **word**: "subframes"
- **phonetics**: /ˈsʌbfreɪmz/
- **part_of_speech**: noun
- **translation**: 子框架
- **definition**: Smaller frames or sections that fall under a larger framework, often used in various contexts such as structural design, software architecture, or media encoding.
- **example**: "The video is divided into several subframes to improve processing efficiency."
- **synonyms**: components, segments
- **antonyms**: whole, entirety

#### 详细解析

- **usage**: 
  - In **architecture**: Subframes can refer to smaller structural units within a building's framework, contributing to stability while allowing for modular construction.
  - In **software**: It may refer to segments of code or functions operating within a larger software environment or application.
  - In **media**: Used in video and image processing, where subframes help manage quality and performance during playback.
