### Context
Glanderous refers primarily to the disease known as glanders, which is caused by the bacterium *Burkholderia mallei*. It typically affects horses but can be transmitted to humans, leading to severe respiratory illness, among other symptoms. This term is used in veterinary medicine and discussions regarding zoonotic diseases.
