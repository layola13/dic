### Context and Usage
The term "multidisc" is commonly encountered in the realms of video gaming and data storage. In gaming, it often signifies titles that require multiple discs to accommodate additional content, graphics, or gameplay features, reflecting a larger and more complex game design. In data storage, a multidisc system may refer to setups that utilize several disks for significant data capacity or redundancy.
