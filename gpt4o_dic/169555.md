Here’s a detailed analysis of the word "what":

### Properties of the Word "What"

- **Word**: What
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: *What is your name?*  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Usage Patterns

#### 1. **Simple Present Tense**
   - *What do you want for dinner?*  
     **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### 2. **Present Continuous Tense**
   - *What are you doing now?*  
     **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### 3. **Present Perfect Tense**
   - *What have you done?*  
     **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### 4. **Simple Past Tense**
   - *What did he say?*  
     **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### 5. **Future Tense**
   - *What will you do tomorrow?*  
     **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

### Mood Usage
- **Subjunctive Mood**: 
  - *If I knew what to do, I would do it.*  
    **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice Examples
- *What was decided at the meeting?*  
  **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- *What time is it?*  
  **Translation**: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- *I don't know what to say.*  
  **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
- **Common Phrases**:
  - *What's up?*  
    **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  - *What the hell!*  
    **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms**:
  - *What if* — 如果...会怎么样
  - *What for* — 为了什么
  - *No matter what* — 无论如何
  - *What's more* — 而且；更重要的是
  - *What about* — ...怎么样
  - *So what* — 那又怎样

This comprehensive structure highlights the various contexts in which "what" can be used, offering a clear understanding of its functions in English.
