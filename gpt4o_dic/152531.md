### Word Analysis: "what"

**Phonetics:** /wɑːt/

**Part of Speech:**  
- Pronoun (pron.)
- Adjective (adj.)
- Adverb (adv.)
- Conjunction (conj.)

**Translation:** 什么

**Definition:**  
Used to ask for information about someone or something.

**Example:**  
*What is your name?*  
（你叫什么名字？）

**Synonyms:**  
- which
- that

**Antonyms:**  
- None

### Usage Examples

#### Simple Present
- **Question:** What do you want for dinner?  
  **Translation:** 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Question:** What are you doing now?  
  **Translation:** 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Question:** What have you done?  
  **Translation:** 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Question:** What did he say?  
  **Translation:** 他说了什么？

#### Future Simple
- **Question:** What will you do tomorrow?  
  **Translation:** 你明天要做什么？

### Phrases and Idioms

#### Common Phrases
- **What's up?**  
  *Translation:* 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  
- **What the hell!**  
  *Translation:* 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **what if**  
  *Translation:* 如果...会怎么样
  
- **what for**  
  *Translation:* 为了什么
  
- **no matter what**  
  *Translation:* 无论如何
  
- **what's more**  
  *Translation:* 而且；更重要的是

- **what about**  
  *Translation:* ...怎么样

- **so what**  
  *Translation:* 那又怎样

### Additional Structures

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example:** If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
  *Translation:* 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

#### Passive Voice
- **Example:** What was decided at the meeting?  
  *Translation:* 会议上决定了什么？

#### Negative Sentences
- **Example:** I don't know what to say.  
  *Translation:* 我不知道该说什么。
