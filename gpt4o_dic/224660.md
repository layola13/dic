### Detailed Analysis of the Word "What"

- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun (pron.), adjective (adj.), adverb (adv.), conjunction (conj.)
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Examples:
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"
- **Translation**: "你叫什么名字？"

#### Synonyms:
- which
- that

#### Antonyms:
- None listed

### Usage:

#### Simple Present:
- **Question**: "What do you want for dinner?"
- **Translation**: "你晚饭想吃什么？"

#### Present Continuous:
- **Question**: "What are you doing now?"
- **Translation**: "你现在在做什么？"

#### Present Perfect:
- **Question**: "What have you done?"
- **Translation**: "你做了什么？"

#### Present Perfect Continuous:
- **Question**: "What have you been doing all day?"
- **Translation**: "你一整天都在做什么？"

#### Simple Past:
- **Question**: "What did he say?"
- **Translation**: "他说了什么？"

#### Past Continuous:
- **Question**: "What were you doing when I called?"
- **Translation**: "我打电话时你在做什么？"

#### Past Perfect:
- **Question**: "What had happened before I arrived?"
- **Translation**: "在我到达之前发生了什么事？"

#### Past Perfect Continuous:
- **Question**: "What had you been doing before the accident?"
- **Translation**: "事故发生前你一直在做什么？"

#### Simple Future:
- **Question**: "What will you do tomorrow?"
- **Translation**: "你明天要做什么？"

#### Future Continuous:
- **Question**: "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?"
- **Translation**: "明天这个时候你会在做什么？"

#### Future Perfect:
- **Question**: "What will you have accomplished by next year?"
- **Translation**: "到明年你会完成什么？"

#### Future Perfect Continuous:
- **Question**: "What will you have been doing for ten years?"
- **Translation**: "到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？"

#### Subjunctive Mood:
- **Example**: "If I knew what to do, I would do it."
- **Translation**: "如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。"

#### Passive Voice:
- **Example**: "What was decided at the meeting?"
- **Translation**: "会议上决定了什么？"

#### Interrogative Sentences:
- **Question**: "What time is it?"
- **Translation**: "现在几点了？"

#### Negative Sentences:
- **Example**: "I don't know what to say."
- **Translation**: "我不知道该说什么。"

### Phrases:

#### Common Examples:
- **Phrase**: "What's up?"
  - **Translation**: "怎么了？/ 你好吗？"

- **Phrase**: "What the hell!"
  - **Translation**: "搞什么鬼！"

#### Idioms:
- **Phrase**: "what if"
  - **Translation**: "如果...会怎么样"

- **Phrase**: "what for"
  - **Translation**: "为了什么"

- **Phrase**: "no matter what"
  - **Translation**: "无论如何"

- **Phrase**: "what's more"
  - **Translation**: "而且；更重要的是"

- **Phrase**: "what about"
  - **Translation**: "...怎么样"

- **Phrase**: "so what"
  - **Translation**: "那又怎样"

This comprehensive analysis illustrates the versatile nature of the word "what," showcasing its various usages in different contexts along with relevant idiomatic expressions.
