### Additional Context
Mildred Dresselhaus (1930-2017) was a prominent figure in her field, often referred to as the "queen of carbon science" for her extensive research on graphene and carbon nanotubes. Her work significantly advanced understanding in nanotechnology and materials science, and she was a mentor to many in STEM fields, particularly encouraging women in science.
