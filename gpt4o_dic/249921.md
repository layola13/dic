Here's a detailed解析 for the word "what":

### Word Details
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Example
- **Sentence**: What is your name?  
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- [None listed]

### Usage Examples
#### Simple Present
- **Question**: What do you want for dinner?  
  **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Question**: What are you doing now?  
  **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Question**: What have you done?  
  **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Question**: What did he say?  
  **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Future Simple
- **Question**: What will you do tomorrow?  
  **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
  **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?  
  **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Question**: What time is it?  
  **Translation**: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Example**: I don't know what to say.  
  **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
#### Common Expressions
- **Phrase**: What's up?  
  **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

#### Idiomatic Expressions
- **Phrase**: what if  
  **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
- **Phrase**: no matter what  
  **Translation**: 无论如何
