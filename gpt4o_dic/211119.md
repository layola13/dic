### substyle

- **word**: substyle
- **phonetics**: /ˈsʌbstaɪl/
- **part_of_speech**: noun
- **translation**: 子风格
- **definition**: A category or variant within a larger style, often having its own distinct characteristics or features.
- **example**: The designer introduced a new substyle that combines elements of vintage and modern aesthetics.
- **synonyms**: variant, subtype, category
- **antonyms**: main style, original style
