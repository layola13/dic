### Additional Context
"Preneuralgic" is a term primarily used in medical contexts, especially in neurology. It refers to the symptoms or pain that occur before the onset of neuralgia, which is typically characterized by sharp, severe pain along the distribution of a nerve. Understanding these precursor symptoms can aid in timely diagnosis and treatment.
