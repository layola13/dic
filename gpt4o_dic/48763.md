### 英文单词 **"defix"** 解析

#### 单词基本信息
- **单词**: defix
- **音标**: /dɪˈfɪks/
- **词性**: 动词 (verb)
- **翻译**: 固定；使不动
- **定义**: 1. To make something fixed or immovable.  
         2. To concentrate or direct one's attention on something.

#### 例句
1. **简单现在时**:  
   - "He defixes the poster on the wall."  
     *他固定墙上的海报。*
   
2. **进行时**:  
   - "She is defixing her gaze on the painting."  
     *她正全神贯注地盯着那幅画。*
   
3. **完成时**:  
   - "They have defixed the position of the statue."  
     *他们已经固定了雕像的位置。*
