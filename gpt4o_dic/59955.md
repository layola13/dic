### 英文单词解析: edict

**基本信息**  
- **单词**: edict  
- **音标**: /ˈiːdɪkt/  
- **词性**: 名词  

**翻译**: 法令，公告  

**定义**:  
An edict is an official order or proclamation issued by a person in authority, typically used in a governmental or legal context.

**例句**:  
- The king issued an edict to promote peace in the kingdom.  
- An edict from the government changed the law regarding public gatherings.  

**同义词**:  
- decree  
- proclamation  
- mandate  

**反义词**:  
- suggestion  
- recommendation  

**用法**:
- **在法律和政府文件中**: Often found in formal contexts where official decisions are made.
- **正式声明**: Can relate to announcements made by religious or organizational leaders.

**短语和习惯用语**:  
- **Royal edict**: 指由国王或皇室成员发布的法令。  
- **Public edict**: 指针对公众发布的正式公告。  

这种形式代表着对公共事务的正式声明，具有法律效力，必须执行。
