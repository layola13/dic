### English Word Analysis: Precondense

**Word**: precondense  
**Phonetics**: /ˌpriː.kənˈdɛns/  
**Part of Speech**: verb  
**Translation**: 预浓缩（类似于“在浓缩之前的状态”）  
**Definition**: To reduce something in volume or concentration before a final stage of condensation.  

**Example**: Scientists must precondense the gas before cooling it to liquid form.  

**Synonyms**: condense, compress  
**Antonyms**: expand, dilute
