### Context

"Wolfishly" is an adverb that draws its meaning from the qualities often associated with wolves—greediness, ferocity, and a predatory demeanor. Its usage typically invokes imagery of intense desire or voraciousness, making it applicable in various contexts, especially in describing hunger or eagerness.
