### Additional Context
PACS stands for Picture Archiving and Communication System, and it plays a crucial role in modern healthcare by enabling the efficient management of medical images like X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans. By digitizing these images, PACS improves accessibility, facilitates easier sharing among medical professionals, and enhances the overall workflow within medical facilities.
