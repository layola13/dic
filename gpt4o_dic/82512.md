### Word Analysis: "what"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Examples
- **General Usage**: 
  - "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)
  - "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)

#### Synonyms
- Which
- That

#### Usage by Tense

1. **Simple Present**:
   - "What do you want to do?" (你想做什么？)
  
2. **Present Continuous**:
   - "What are you doing now?" (你现在在做什么？)

3. **Present Perfect**:
   - "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**:
   - "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)

5. **Simple Past**:
   - "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)

6. **Past Continuous**:
   - "What were you thinking about?" (你在想什么？)

7. **Past Perfect**:
   - "What had happened?" (发生了什么事？)

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**:
   - "What had you been doing?" (你一直在做什么？)

9. **Simple Future**:
   - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)

10. **Future Continuous**:
    - "What will you be doing tomorrow?" (明天你会在做什么？)

11. **Future Perfect**:
    - "What will you have accomplished?" (你将完成什么？)

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**:
    - "What will you have been doing?" (你将已经在做什么？)

13. **Subjunctive Mood**:
    - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

14. **Passive Voice**:
    - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)

15. **Interrogative Sentences**:
    - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)

16. **Negative Sentences**:
    - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)

#### Phrases and Idioms

- **Common Phrases**:
  - "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - "What if" (如果...会怎么样)
  - "What for" (为了什么)
  - "No matter what" (无论如何)
  - "What's more" (而且；更重要的是)
  - "What about" (...怎么样)
  - "So what" (那又怎样)

This comprehensive breakdown provides a clear understanding of the word "what" in various contexts, tenses, and usages.
