Here’s a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Basic Information
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Usage
#### Simple Present
- **What do you want for dinner?**
  - 种翻译: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **What are you doing now?**
  - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **What have you done?**
  - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **What have you been doing all day?**
  - **Translation**: 你一整天都在做什么？

#### Simple Past
- **What did he say?**
  - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Past Continuous
- **What were you doing when I called?**
  - **Translation**: 我打电话时你在做什么？

#### Past Perfect
- **What had happened before I arrived?**
  - **Translation**: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **What had you been doing before the accident?**
  - **Translation**: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

#### Simple Future
- **What will you do tomorrow?**
  - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

#### Future Continuous
- **What will you be doing this time tomorrow?**
  - **Translation**: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

#### Future Perfect
- **What will you have accomplished by next year?**
  - **Translation**: 到明年你会完成什么？

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **What will you have been doing for ten years?**
  - **Translation**: 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.
  - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?
  - **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **What time is it?**
  - **Translation**: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Example**: I don't know what to say.
  - **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
#### Common Phrases
- **What's up?**
  - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

#### Idioms
- **What if**: 如果...会怎么样
- **What for**: 为了什么
- **No matter what**: 无论如何
- **What's more**: 而且；更重要的是
- **What about**: ...怎么样
- **So what**: 那又怎样

This breakdown provides a comprehensive understanding of the word "what" in various contexts and usages.
