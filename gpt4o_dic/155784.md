以下是对英文单词“what”的详细解析：

### Phonological Information
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: 
  - Pronoun
  - Adjective
  - Adverb
  - Conjunction

### Translation
- **中文**: 什么

### Definition
- **Meaning**: 用于询问有关某人或某事的信息。

### Example Sentence
- **English**: What is your name?
- **中文**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- (无)

### Usage Examples
#### Simple Present
- **Questions**:
  - What do you want for dinner?
  - 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What are you doing now?
  - 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What have you done?
  - 你做了什么？

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What have you been doing all day?
  - 你一整天都在做什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**:
  - What did he say?
  - 他说了什么？

#### Past Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What were you doing when I called?
  - 我打电话时你在做什么？

#### Past Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What had happened before I arrived?
  - 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What had you been doing before the accident?
  - 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

#### Simple Future
- **Questions**:
  - What will you do tomorrow?
  - 你明天要做什么？

#### Future Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What will you be doing this time tomorrow?
  - 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

#### Future Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have accomplished by next year?
  - 到明年你会完成什么？

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What will she have been studying by graduation?
  - 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it.
  - 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

#### Passive Voice
- **Examples**:
  - What was decided at the meeting?
  - 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**:
  - What time is it?
  - 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - I don't know what to say.
  - 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
- **Examples**:
  - What's up? (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - What the hell! (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - what if (如果...会怎么样)
  - what for (为了什么)
  - no matter what (无论如何)
  - what's more (而且；更重要的是)
  - what about (...怎么样)
  - so what (那又怎样)
