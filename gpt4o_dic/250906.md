Here’s a detailed breakdown of the English word "what":

### Word Profile
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
"What" is used to ask for information about someone or something, or it can express a clarification or surprise in a statement.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Usage Patterns

#### Simple Present
- **Question**: What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Question**: What are you doing now?
  - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Question**: What have you done?
  - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Question**: What did he say?
  - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Future Simple
- **Question**: What will you do tomorrow?
  - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - **What’s up?**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  - **What the hell!**: 搞什么鬼！
  
- **Idioms**:
  - **what if**: 如果...会怎么样
  - **what for**: 为了什么
  - **no matter what**: 无论如何
  - **what's more**: 而且；更重要的是
  - **what about**: ...怎么样
  - **so what**: 那又怎样

### Contextual Uses
- **Interrogative Sentences**: Used to formulate questions.
  - *Example*: What time is it?
    - **Translation**: 现在几点了？

- **Negative Sentences**: Can function in the negative to indicate a lack of knowledge.
  - *Example*: I don't know what to say.
    - **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

- **Subjunctive Mood**: Expresses hypothetical situations.
  - *Example*: If I knew what to do, I would do it.
    - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

This summary of "what" illustrates its versatility and importance in English communication across various contexts.
