Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis

- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation (Chinese)**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: What is your name?  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？
  
- **Sentence**: What do you want for dinner?  
  **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Usage in Different Tenses

#### Simple Present
- **Question**: What is your name?  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？
- **Question**: What do you want for dinner?  
  **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Question**: What are you doing now?  
  **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？
- **Question**: What is she reading at the moment?  
  **Translation**: 她这会儿在读什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Question**: What have you done?  
  **Translation**: 你做了什么？
- **Question**: What has she achieved so far?  
  **Translation**: 到目前为止她取得了什么成就？

#### Simple Past
- **Question**: What did he say?  
  **Translation**: 他说了什么？
- **Question**: What happened yesterday?  
  **Translation**: 昨天发生了什么事？

#### Future Tenses
- **Simple Future**: What will you do tomorrow?  
  **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？
  
- **Future Continuous**: What will you be doing this time tomorrow?  
  **Translation**: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

### Questions and Statements
- **Interrogative Sentences**: 
  - What time is it?  
    **Translation**: 现在几点了？
    
  - What are you talking about?  
    **Translation**: 你在说什么？

- **Negative Sentences**: 
  - I don't know what to say.  
    **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。
    
  - She didn't tell me what she wanted.  
    **Translation**: 她没有告诉我她想要什么。

### Phrases
- **Common Expressions**:
  - **Phrase**: What's up?  
    **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

  - **Phrase**: What the hell!  
    **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms**:
  - **Phrase**: what if  
    **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
  
  - **Phrase**: what for  
    **Translation**: 为了什么

### Summary
The word "what" serves as a versatile tool for inquiring about information in various contexts, useful in forming questions in multiple tenses and situations. Its usage spans casual inquiries to more complex structures, making it essential in both spoken and written English.
