### Context:
"Auld Lang Syne" is a Scots-language song traditionally sung to bid farewell to the old year at the stroke of midnight on New Year's Eve. The phrase "auld lang syne" itself translates to "old long since" or "for old times' sake," evoking feelings of nostalgia and remembrance.
