### 单词解析：reingulf

#### 基本信息
- **Word**: reingulf
- **Phonetics**: /ˌriːɪnˈɡʌlf/
- **Part of Speech**: verb
- **Translation**: 重新吞没，重新淹没

#### 定义
**Reingulf** 指再次吞没或淹没某物，通常用于描述某种事物在之前已经被吞没后再次被包含在内的情况。

#### 例句
- **Example**: "After the flood waters receded, the valley was gradually reingulfed by the rising river."  
  **Translation**: "洪水退去后，山谷逐渐被上涨的河水重新吞没。"

#### 相关词汇
- **Synonyms**: submerge, engulf, inundate
- **Antonyms**: uncover, free, release

#### 用法
1. **简单现在时**:
   - **Sentence**: "The landscape reingulfs the town every few years."  
   - **Translation**: "每隔几年，景观就会重新吞没那个小镇。"

2. **过去时**:
   - **Sentence**: "The village was reingulfed during the last storm."  
   - **Translation**: "在上次暴风雨中，那个村庄再次被吞没。"

3. **未来时**:
   - **Sentence**: "If the rains continue, the area will be reingulfed."  
   - **Translation**: "如果雨水持续，该地区将会再次被吞没。"

#### 短语与习语
- **Phrase Example**: 
  - "The valley will reingulf the old ruins after the next storm."  
  - **Translation**: "下次暴风雨后，山谷将再次吞没那些古老的遗迹。"

这种用法强调了与自然灾害相关的再发生，带有一定的文学色彩。
