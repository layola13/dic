### Word Analysis: "what"

#### Basic Information
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Example
- **Sentence**: "What is your name?"
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms
- which
- that

#### Antonyms
- (No direct antonyms)

---

### Usage Examples

#### Simple Present
- **Question**: "What do you want for dinner?"
  - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？
  
#### Present Continuous
- **Question**: "What are you doing now?"
  - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Question**: "What have you done?"
  - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Question**: "What did he say?"
  - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Simple Future
- **Question**: "What will you do tomorrow?"
  - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

---

### Advanced Usage

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Example**: "If I knew what to do, I would do it."
  - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **Question**: "What time is it?"
  - **Translation**: 现在几点了？

---

### Phrases

#### Common Expressions
- **What's up?**
  - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
- **What the hell!**
  - **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **What if**: 如果...会怎么样
- **What for**: 为了什么
- **No matter what**: 无论如何
- **What's more**: 而且；更重要的是

---

This analysis captures the versatility of "what," illustrating both its grammatical applications and common usage in everyday English.
