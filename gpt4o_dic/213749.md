Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Profile: What

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Question**: What is your name?  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Antonyms
*(No direct antonyms)*

### Usage by Tense

- **Simple Present**:
  - Question: What do you want for dinner?  
    Translation: 你晚饭想吃什么？

- **Present Continuous**:
  - Question: What are you doing now?  
    Translation: 你现在在做什么？

- **Present Perfect**:
  - Question: What have you done?  
    Translation: 你做了什么？

- **Present Perfect Continuous**:
  - Question: What have you been doing all day?  
    Translation: 你一整天都在做什么？

- **Simple Past**:
  - Question: What did he say?  
    Translation: 他说了什么？

- **Past Continuous**:
  - Question: What were you doing when I called?  
    Translation: 我打电话时你在做什么？

- **Past Perfect**:
  - Question: What had happened before I arrived?  
    Translation: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

- **Past Perfect Continuous**:
  - Question: What had you been doing before the accident?  
    Translation: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

- **Simple Future**:
  - Question: What will you do tomorrow?  
    Translation: 你明天要做什么？

- **Future Continuous**:
  - Question: What will you be doing this time tomorrow?  
    Translation: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

- **Future Perfect**:
  - Question: What will you have accomplished by next year?  
    Translation: 到明年你会完成什么？

- **Future Perfect Continuous**:
  - Question: What will she have been studying by graduation?  
    Translation: 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

### Subjunctive Mood
- Example: If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
  Translation: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- Example: What was decided at the meeting?  
  Translation: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- Question: What time is it?  
  Translation: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- Example: I don't know what to say.  
  Translation: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases and Idioms

- **Common Phrases**:
  - "What's up?"  
    Translation: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  - "What the hell!"  
    Translation: 搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms**:
  - "What if"  
    Translation: 如果...会怎么样
  - "What for"  
    Translation: 为了什么
  - "No matter what"  
    Translation: 无论如何
  - "What's more"  
    Translation: 而且；更重要的是
  - "What about"  
    Translation: ...怎么样
  - "So what"  
    Translation: 那又怎样

This structured overview provides a comprehensive understanding of the word "what," including its grammatical roles, usage in various contexts, and relevant phrases.
