### 词汇详解：enumerators

- **word**: "enumerators"
- **phonetics**: /ɪˈnjuːməˌreɪtərz/
- **part_of_speech**: noun
- **translation**: 计数员，列举者
- **definition**: Individuals who count or list items systematically, often used in surveys or data collection.

#### 示例
- **example**: "The enumerators collected data from various households during the census."
- **translation**: "计数员在普查期间从不同家庭收集数据。"

#### 相关词汇
- **synonyms**: "counters", "registrars"
- **antonyms**: None specific
