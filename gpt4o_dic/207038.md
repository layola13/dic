Here’s a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to inquire about information pertaining to someone or something.

### Examples in Context
- **General Example**: What is your name? (你叫什么名字？)

### Synonyms and Antonyms
- **Synonyms**: which, that
- **Antonyms**: None applicable

### Usage Examples in Different Tenses

1. **Simple Present**
   - **Question**: What do you want for dinner?  
     **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

2. **Present Continuous**
   - **Question**: What are you doing now?  
     **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

3. **Present Perfect**
   - **Question**: What have you done?  
     **Translation**: 你做了什么？

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**
   - **Question**: What have you been doing all day?  
     **Translation**: 你一整天都在做什么？

5. **Simple Past**
   - **Question**: What did he say?  
     **Translation**: 他说了什么？

6. **Past Continuous**
   - **Question**: What were you doing when I called?  
     **Translation**: 我打电话时你在做什么？

7. **Past Perfect**
   - **Question**: What had happened before I arrived?  
     **Translation**: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**
   - **Question**: What had you been doing before the accident?  
     **Translation**: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

9. **Simple Future**
   - **Question**: What will you do tomorrow?  
     **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

10. **Future Continuous**
    - **Question**: What will you be doing this time tomorrow?  
      **Translation**: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

11. **Future Perfect**
    - **Question**: What will you have accomplished by next year?  
      **Translation**: 到明年你会完成什么？

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**
    - **Question**: What will you have been doing for ten years?  
      **Translation**: 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

### Subjunctive Mood and Passive Voice Examples
- **Subjunctive Mood**:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

- **Passive Voice**:
  - What was decided at the meeting? (会议上决定了什么？)

### Interrogative and Negative Sentences
- **Interrogative**:  
  - What time is it? (现在几点了？)

- **Negative Sentences**:  
  - I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - What’s up? (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - What the hell! (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - What if (如果...会怎么样)
  - What for (为了什么)
  - No matter what (无论如何)
  - What’s more (而且；更重要的是)
  - What about (…怎么样)
  - So what (那又怎样)

This structured breakdown provides a comprehensive overview of the word "what," illustrating its various uses, grammatical aspects, and contextual applications.
