### Additional Context
"Novellike" describes works or styles that evoke the essence of novels, often indicating a complexity and richness in narrative, setting, or character development. It can be applied to various forms of storytelling beyond traditional novels, such as films, plays, or even non-fiction that employs storytelling techniques typical of novel writing.
