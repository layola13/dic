### Additional Context
"Untangible" is a less common variant of "intangible," which is more widely used. It describes things that cannot be physically touched or quantified, often relating to abstract concepts like feelings, ideas, or advantages that lack physical presence. The term reflects the essence of many emotional and cultural phenomena that are essential yet cannot be directly measured or owned.
