### Contextual Overview

Etherealism is often associated with artistic movements where an emphasis on lightness, transparency, and spirituality is prevalent, enabling artists and thinkers to convey emotions that touch the sublime. This concept is deeply rooted in philosophical discussions about the nature of reality and perception, contrasting with more materialistic viewpoints. Etherealism finds expressions in various art forms, including literature, painting, and music, fostering an appreciation for the delicate and intangible aspects of existence.
