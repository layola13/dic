### Overview

**Nondeodorants** are products designed for personal hygiene that do not have the chemical fragrances or masking scents typically found in deodorants. These are often preferred by those with sensitive skin, allergies, or those who prefer a more natural approach to personal care.

#### Context and Usage
- **Example Context**: Nondeodorants may include unscented soaps, lotions, and other skin care products that minimize skin reactions.
- **Why People Use Them**: Some individuals react negatively to the synthetic fragrances used in traditional deodorants, leading them to seek out alternatives that are gentler on the skin.
