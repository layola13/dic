### Context
The name "Gulbenkian" is most often associated with the Gulbenkian Foundation, a prominent cultural institution based in Lisbon, Portugal, which reflects the legacy of its founder Calouste Gulbenkian, an Armenian oil tycoon and philanthropist. The foundation plays a significant role in promoting arts, science, and education, and is home to a vast collection of art, including works from various historical periods.
