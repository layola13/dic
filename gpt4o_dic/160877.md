### Context and Explanation

The term **"post-climax"** is primarily used in literary and cinematic contexts, referring to the segment of a story that follows its climax. This phase is crucial as it elaborates on the aftermath of the climax, wrapping up character arcs and resolving conflicts. It serves to provide the audience with a sense of closure or anticipation for future developments.

### Usage in Literature and Film

Understanding the post-climax part of a narrative helps in analyzing how stories express themes and character evolution. It is essential for writers to craft this phase effectively to maintain audience engagement and satisfaction.
