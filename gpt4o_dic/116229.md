### Context:
The Masorites were instrumental in the development of the Masoretic Text (MT), which is the authoritative Hebrew text of the Jewish Bible. Their work included adding vowel markings, cantillation signs, and notes regarding the pronunciation and interpretation of the scriptures, ensuring the texts were preserved accurately over centuries.
