Here’s a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

### Definition
"What" is used to ask for information about someone or something, often to seek clarification or specifics.

### Examples
- **Question**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？
  
- **Question**: What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Usage in Different Tenses
- **Simple Present**:
  - **Q**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

- **Present Continuous**:
  - **Q**: What are you doing now?
  - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？
  
- **Present Perfect**:
  - **Q**: What have you done?
  - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

- **Simple Past**:
  - **Q**: What did he say?
  - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

- **Future Simple**:
  - **Q**: What will you do tomorrow?
  - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

### Mood and Voice
- **Subjunctive Mood**:
  - **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.
  - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。
  
- **Passive Voice**:
  - **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?
  - **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Question**: What time is it?
  - **Translation**: 现在几点了？
  
- **Question**: What are you talking about?
  - **Translation**: 你在说什么？

### Negative Sentences
- **Example**: I don't know what to say.
  - **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。
  
- **Example**: She didn't tell me what she wanted.
  - **Translation**: 她没有告诉我她想要什么。

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - **Phrase**: What's up?
    - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
    
  - **Phrase**: What the hell!
    - **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms**:
  - **Idiom**: What if
    - **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样
    
  - **Idiom**: What's more
    - **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是

This detailed breakdown covers the multifaceted uses and implications of the word "what" in the English language.
