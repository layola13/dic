Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word: "what"
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么 (shénme)
  
### Definition
- **Meaning**: "What" is used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Question**: What is your name?
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？
- **Question**: What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

### Synonyms
- **Related Words**: which, that

### Antonyms
- **None Provided**

### Usage in Different Tenses
1. **Simple Present**:
   - What are you doing now? (你现在在做什么？)
   - What has she achieved so far? (到目前为止她取得了什么成就？)

2. **Present Continuous**:
   - What is she reading at the moment? (她这会儿在读什么？)

3. **Present Perfect**:
   - What have you done? (你做了什么？)

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**:
   - What have you been doing all day? (你一整天都在做什么？)

5. **Simple Past**:
   - What did he say? (他说了什么？)

6. **Past Continuous**:
   - What were you doing when I called? (我打电话时你在做什么？)

7. **Past Perfect**:
   - What had happened before I arrived? (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

8. **Future Tenses**:
   - What will you do tomorrow? (你明天要做什么？)
   - What will they be discussing at the meeting? (会议上他们会讨论什么？)

### Subjunctive Mood
- If I knew what to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

### Passive Voice
- What was decided at the meeting? (会议上决定了什么？)
- What is being done to solve the problem? (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

### Interrogative Sentences
- What time is it? (现在几点了？)
- What are you talking about? (你在说什么？)

### Negative Sentences
- I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)
- She didn't tell me what she wanted. (她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - What's up? (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - What the hell! (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - what if (如果...会怎么样)
  - what for (为了什么)
  - no matter what (无论如何)
  - what's more (而且；更重要的是)
  - what about (...怎么样)
  - so what (那又怎样)
