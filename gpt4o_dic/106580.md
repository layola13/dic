### Detailed Analysis of the Word "what"

#### Basic Information
- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

#### Definition
"What" is used to ask for information regarding someone or something. It can also introduce clauses or be used in expressions.

#### Example Sentences
- **Inquiry**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)
- **Request**: "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)

#### Synonyms and Antonyms
- **Synonyms**: which, that
- **Antonyms**: None directly applicable.

#### Usage in Different Tenses
1. **Simple Present**: 
   - "What is your favorite color?" (你最喜欢的颜色是什么？)
   - "What do you want?" (你想要什么？)
   
2. **Present Continuous**: 
   - "What are you doing right now?" (你现在在做什么？)
   - "What is she working on?" (她正在做什么？)

3. **Present Perfect**: 
   - "What have you accomplished?" (你取得了什么成就？)
   - "What has happened?" (发生了什么？)

4. **Present Perfect Continuous**: 
   - "What have you been working on?" (你一直在做什么？)
   - "What has he been studying?" (他一直在学习什么？)

5. **Simple Past**: 
   - "What did you see?" (你看到了什么？)
   - "What happened yesterday?" (昨天发生了什么？)

6. **Past Continuous**: 
   - "What were you thinking about?" (你在想什么？)
   - "What was happening at that moment?" (那时候发生了什么？)

7. **Past Perfect**: 
   - "What had you done before the meeting?" (会议前你做了什么？)
   - "What had they decided by then?" (到那时他们决定了什么？)

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**: 
   - "What had you been doing before I arrived?" (我到达之前你一直在做什么？)
   - "What had they been discussing?" (他们一直在讨论什么？)

9. **Simple Future**: 
   - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)
   - "What will happen next?" (接下来会发生什么？)

10. **Future Continuous**: 
    - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)
    - "What will they be talking about?" (他们会讨论什么？)

11. **Future Perfect**: 
    - "What will you have accomplished by next year?" (到明年你将完成什么？)
    - "What changes will have occurred?" (会发生什么变化？)

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**: 
    - "What will you have been doing for ten years?" (到时候你将做这件事十年了？)
    - "What will she have been studying by graduation?" (到毕业时她将学习专业多久了？)

13. **Subjunctive Mood**: 
    - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我会去做。)
    - "I wish I knew what was going on." (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

14. **Passive Voice**: 
    - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)
    - "What is being done about it?" (对此正在采取什么措施？)

15. **Interrogative Sentences**: 
    - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)
    - "What are you talking about?" (你在说什么？)

16. **Negative Sentences**: 
    - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)
    - "She didn't tell me what she wanted." (她没有告诉我想要什么。)

#### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - "What if?" (如果...会怎么样)
  - "What for?" (为了什么)
  - "No matter what." (无论如何)
  - "What's more." (而且；更重要的是)
  - "What about?" (...怎么样)
  - "So what?" (那又怎样)

This comprehensive breakdown of "what" covers its various forms and contexts, showcasing its versatility in the English language.
