### 词汇解析: "unscrambling"

---

- **单词**: unscrambling
- **音标**: /ʌnˈskræmblɪŋ/
- **词性**: 动词，名词（现在分词形式）
- **翻译**: 解码；重新排列

#### 定义
指将混杂或不规则的内容整理成有序或可理解的形式，通常指对字母、单词或信息的重组以恢复原始状态。

#### 示例
- **句子**: "The teacher gave us a task of unscrambling the letters to form a meaningful word."
- **翻译**: "老师给我们布置了一项任务，让我们解码字母形成一个有意义的单词。"

#### 同义词
- reordering
- decoding
- rearranging

#### 反义词

- mixing

#### 用法
- **动词形式**:
  - **简单现在时**: "I unscramble the letters daily."
  - **现在进行时**: "They are unscrambling the words right now."
  - **简单过去时**: "She unscrambled the puzzle last night."
  - **将来时**: "We will unscramble the codes tomorrow."

- **名词形式**:
  - "Unscrambling of the text requires patience and skill."
  - "The unscrambling process can be challenging."

#### 短语

  - "The activity involved unscrambling phrases."
