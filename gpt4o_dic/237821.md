Here's a detailed analysis of the word "what":

### What
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Example Usage:
- **Question**: What is your name?
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- which
- that

### Antonyms
- (None listed)

### Grammar and Usage
#### Simple Present
- **Questions**:
  - What do you want for dinner? (你晚饭想吃什么？)

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What are you doing now? (你现在在做什么？)

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What have you done? (你做了什么？)

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What have you been doing all day? (你一整天都在做什么？)

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**:
  - What did he say? (他说了什么？)

#### Past Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What were you doing when I called? (我打电话时你在做什么？)

#### Past Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What had happened before I arrived? (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What had you been doing before the accident? (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)

#### Simple Future
- **Questions**:
  - What will you do tomorrow? (你明天要做什么？)

#### Future Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What will you be doing this time tomorrow? (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

#### Future Perfect
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have accomplished by next year? (到明年你会完成什么？)

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**:
  - What will you have been doing for ten years? (到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
  - I wish I knew what was going on. (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

#### Passive Voice
- **Examples**:
  - What was decided at the meeting? (会议上决定了什么？)
  - What is being done to solve the problem? (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**:
  - What time is it? (现在几点了？)
  - What are you talking about? (你在说什么？)

#### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)
  - She didn't tell me what she wanted. (她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

### Phrases
#### Common Expressions
- **What's up?**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
- **What the hell!**: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **What if**: 如果...会怎么样
- **What for**: 为了什么
- **No matter what**: 无论如何
- **What's more**: 而且；更重要的是
- **What about**: ...怎么样
- **So what**: 那又怎样

This comprehensive breakdown of "what" shows its versatility in different contexts and grammatical structures, making it a fundamental word in English communication.
