**Word**: denization  
**Phonetics**: /ˌdɛnɪˈzeɪʃən/  
**Part of Speech**: noun  
**Translation**: 归化（指外国人获得某国公民权）  
**Definition**: The act of granting the status of a denizen to a foreigner, which is a form of partial naturalization. It allows a foreigner certain rights within a country without full citizenship.
