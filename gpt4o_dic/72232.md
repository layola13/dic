### Additional Context

Floriculture is a branch of horticulture that involves the cultivation of flowering plants for gardens, as well as for floristry, general landscaping, and cultivation for floral design. The floricultural industry plays a significant role in both local economies and global markets due to its contributions to aesthetics and ecological balance.
