Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Phonetic Details
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/

### Part of Speech
- **Categories**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction

### Translation
- **Chinese**: 什么

### Definition
- **Use**: "What" is used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Sentence**: What is your name?
  
### Synonyms
- **Similar Words**: which, that

### Antonyms
- **None Available**

### Usage in Different Tenses
- **Simple Present**:
  - What do you want for dinner? (你晚饭想吃什么？)

- **Present Continuous**:
  - What are you doing now? (你现在在做什么？)

- **Present Perfect**:
  - What have you done? (你做了什么？)

- **Present Perfect Continuous**:
  - What have you been doing all day? (你一整天都在做什么？)

- **Simple Past**:
  - What did he say? (他说了什么？)

- **Past Continuous**:
  - What were you doing when I called? (我打电话时你在做什么？)

- **Past Perfect**:
  - What had happened before I arrived? (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

- **Past Perfect Continuous**:
  - What had you been doing before the accident? (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)

- **Simple Future**:
  - What will you do tomorrow? (你明天要做什么？)

- **Future Continuous**:
  - What will you be doing this time tomorrow? (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

- **Future Perfect**:
  - What will you have accomplished by next year? (到明年你会完成什么？)

- **Future Perfect Continuous**:
  - What will you have been doing for ten years? (到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)

### Subjunctive Mood
- If I knew what to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

### Passive Voice
- What is being done to solve the problem? (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

### Interrogative Sentences
- What time is it? (现在几点了？)

### Negative Sentences
- I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases
- **Common Expressions**:
  - What's up? (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - What the hell! (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - what if (如果...会怎么样)
  - what for (为了什么)
  - no matter what (无论如何)
  - what's more (而且；更重要的是)
  - what about (...怎么样)
  - so what (那又怎样)

This comprehensive overview outlines the various aspects of "what," showcasing its versatility and fundamental role in English communication.
