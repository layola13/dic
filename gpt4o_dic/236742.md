### 单词解析: unpannel

#### 基本信息
- **Word**: unpannel
- **Phonetics**: /ʌnˈpæn.əl/
- **Part of Speech**: verb (rare usage)
- **Translation**: 去掉面板; 解除某个面板的封闭状态
- **Definition**: To remove or dismantle a panel, often referring to the removal of a covering or structure.

#### 例句
- **Example**: "The technician had to unpannel the device to fix the issue."
  - **Translation**: "技术人员必须拆除设备的面板才能修复问题。"

#### 同义词
- **Synonyms**: remove, dismantle, disassemble
- **Antonyms**: panel, assemble, install

#### 用法
- **Simple Present**: 
  - "They unpanel the wall to inspect the wiring." 
    - (他们拆去墙面的面板以检查电线。)
  
- **Past Tense**: 
  - "She unpanneled the door to make repairs."
    - (她拆下门的面板进行修理。)

- **Future Tense**: 
  - "I will unpanel the cabinet next week." 
    - (我下周会拆除橱柜的面板。)

#### 相关短语
- **Phrases**: 
  - "Unpanel a wall" (拆去墙面)
  - "Unpanel a device" (拆除设备的面板)

### 其他信息
该词在日常英语中使用较少，主要见于专业领域或技术文献中。
