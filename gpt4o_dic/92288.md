**Word Analysis: "hyphenating"**

- **Phonetics**: /ˈhaɪ.fən.eɪ.tɪŋ/
- **Part of Speech**: verb (present participle of "hyphenate")
- **Translation**: 连字符连接 (在中文中可理解为连字符)
- **Definition**: The act of joining two words or parts of words with a hyphen (-) to create a compound word or to clarify meaning.
- **Example**: "The company specializes in hyphenating words for clarity."

- **Synonyms**: connecting, joining, linking
- **Antonyms**: separating, disconnecting

### Usage
- **Simple Present**: 
  - "I hyphenate certain adjectives." (我连接某些形容词。)
- **Present Continuous**: 
  - "They are hyphenating the project names." (他们正在连接项目名称。)
- **Simple Past**: 
  - "She hyphenated the terms in her report." (她在报告中连接了术语。)

### Phrases & Idioms
- **Examples**:
  - **"Hyphenate these two words."** (请将这两个词连接起来。)
  - **"Why are you hyphenating?"** (你为什么要连字符？)

Hyphenating is commonly used in English to ensure clarity in writing, particularly in compound modifiers or when avoiding ambiguity.
