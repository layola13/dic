### Word Analysis: "What"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Examples:
- **Sentence**: What is your name?  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms:
- Which
- That

#### Antonyms:
- [None]

### Usage:

#### Simple Present:
- **Question**: What do you want for dinner?  
  **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous:
- **Question**: What are you doing now?  
  **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect:
- **Question**: What have you done?  
  **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past:
- **Question**: What did he say?  
  **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Simple Future:
- **Question**: What will you do tomorrow?  
  **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

#### Passive Voice:
- **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?  
  **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

#### Negative Sentences:
- **Example**: I don't know what to say.  
  **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases:

#### Common Expressions:
- **Phrase**: What's up?  
  **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

#### Idioms:
- **Idiom**: What if  
  **Translation**: 如果...会怎么样  
- **Idiom**: What's more  
  **Translation**: 而且；更重要的是  
- **Idiom**: So what  
  **Translation**: 那又怎样  

This comprehensive breakdown of "what" illustrates its versatility in various contexts, aiding language learners in grasping its usage effectively.
