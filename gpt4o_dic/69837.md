- **Chemistry Domain**: Ferric refers specifically to iron ions with a +3 charge, contrasting with ferrous, which describes +2 charge iron.
- **Applications**: Ferric compounds are widely used in various industries, including pigments, water treatment, and steel manufacturing.
- **Biological Importance**: Ferric ions play a crucial role in biological systems, especially in oxygen transport and various enzymatic reactions.
