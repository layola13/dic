Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis: "what"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么 (shén me)
  
### Definition
"What" is used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Example Sentence
- "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Antonyms
- None

### Usage

#### Simple Present
- **Questions**: 
  - "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What are you doing now?" (你现在在做什么？)

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions**: 
  - "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)

#### Simple Past
- **Questions**: 
  - "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)

#### Past Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What were you doing when I called?" (我打电话时你在做什么？)

#### Past Perfect
- **Questions**: 
  - "What had happened before I arrived?" (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)

#### Past Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What had you been doing before the accident?" (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)

#### Simple Future
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)

#### Future Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)

#### Future Perfect
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will you have accomplished by next year?" (到明年你会完成什么？)

#### Future Perfect Continuous
- **Questions**: 
  - "What will she have been studying by graduation?" (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**: 
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)

#### Passive Voice
- **Examples**: 
  - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)

#### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions**: 
  - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)

#### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**: 
  - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)

### Phrases and Idioms

#### Common Phrases
- "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
- "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

#### Idioms
- "What if" (如果...会怎么样)
- "What for" (为了什么)
- "No matter what" (无论如何)
- "What's more" (而且；更重要的是)
- "What about" (...怎么样)
- "So what" (那又怎样)

This comprehensive breakdown highlights the various contexts and uses of the word "what," demonstrating its versatility in English language communication.
