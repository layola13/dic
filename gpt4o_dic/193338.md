以下是对英文单词“what”的详细解析：

### Word Analysis: "what"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun (pron.), Adjective (adj.), Adverb (adv.), Conjunction (conj.)
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **General Example**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)

### Synonyms and Antonyms
- **Synonyms**: which, that
- **Antonyms**: None specified.

### Usage

#### Simple Present
- **Examples**:
  - **Question**: "What do you want for dinner?" (你晚饭想吃什么？)
  - **Question**: "What is your name?" (你叫什么名字？)

#### Present Continuous
- **Examples**:
  - **Question**: "What are you doing now?" (你现在在做什么？)
  - **Question**: "What is she reading at the moment?" (她这会儿在读什么？)

#### Present Perfect
- **Examples**:
  - **Question**: "What have you done?" (你做了什么？)
  - **Question**: "What has she achieved so far?" (到目前为止她取得了什么成就？)

#### Present Perfect Continuous
- **Examples**:
  - **Question**: "What have you been doing all day?" (你一整天都在做什么？)
  - **Question**: "What has he been working on lately?" (他最近一直在做什么？)

#### Simple Past
- **Examples**:
  - **Question**: "What did he say?" (他说了什么？)
  - **Question**: "What happened yesterday?" (昨天发生了什么事？)

#### Past Continuous
- **Examples**:
  - **Question**: "What were you doing when I called?" (我打电话时你在做什么？)
  - **Question**: "What was she thinking about?" (她当时在想什么？)

#### Past Perfect
- **Examples**:
  - **Question**: "What had happened before I arrived?" (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)
  - **Question**: "What had they discussed before the meeting?" (会议前他们讨论了什么？)

#### Future Tenses
- **Simple Future**:
  - "What will you do tomorrow?" (你明天要做什么？)
- **Future Continuous**:
  - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)
  
#### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples**:
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
  - "I wish I knew what was going on." (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

#### Passive Voice
- **Examples**:
  - "What was decided at the meeting?" (会议上决定了什么？)
  - "What is being done to solve the problem?" (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - "What time is it?" (现在几点了？)
  - "What are you talking about?" (你在说什么？)

### Negative Sentences
- **Examples**:
  - "I don't know what to say." (我不知道该说什么。)
  - "She didn't tell me what she wanted." (她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - "What's up?" (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - "What the hell!" (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idiomatic Expressions**:
  - "What if" (如果...会怎么样)
  - "What for" (为了什么)
  - "No matter what" (无论如何)
  - "What's more" (而且；更重要的是)
  - "What about" (...怎么样)
  - "So what" (那又怎样)

这种详尽的解析显示了“what”在英语中的多种用法以及它在提问中的重要性。
