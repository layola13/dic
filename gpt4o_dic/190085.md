### Context and Cultural Significance
The term "sangeet" plays a significant role in Indian weddings, where it serves not only as an entertainment event but also as an opportunity for families to come together and celebrate the union in a more relaxed setting. During a sangeet, various performances are often seen, including traditional dances, music plays, and sometimes even skits, fostering a sense of joy and unity among attendees.
