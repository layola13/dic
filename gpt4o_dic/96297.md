### Context

Indigeneity relates to the original inhabitants of a region and encompasses aspects of their culture, identity, and historical experiences. It is often used in discussions about cultural preservation, rights, and recognition of indigenous populations worldwide. Understanding indigeneity is crucial in the context of globalization, as it highlights the importance of sustaining traditional cultures and practices in the face of external influences.
