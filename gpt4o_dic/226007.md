### Word Analysis: "what"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么

#### Definition
Used to inquire for information about someone or something.

#### Example Sentence
- "What is your name?"
- Translation: 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms
- which
- that

#### Antonyms
- (None listed)

### Usage in Different Tenses

**Simple Present**
- Questions:
  - "What do you want for dinner?" 
    - Translation: 你晚饭想吃什么？

**Present Continuous**
- Questions:
  - "What are you doing now?"
    - Translation: 你现在在做什么？

**Present Perfect**
- Questions:
  - "What have you done?"
    - Translation: 你做了什么？

**Present Perfect Continuous**
- Questions:
  - "What have you been doing all day?"
    - Translation: 你一整天都在做什么？

**Simple Past**
- Questions:
  - "What happened yesterday?"
    - Translation: 昨天发生了什么事？

**Past Continuous**
- Questions:
  - "What were you doing when I called?"
    - Translation: 我打电话时你在做什么？

**Past Perfect**
- Questions:
  - "What had happened before I arrived?"
    - Translation: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

**Past Perfect Continuous**
- Questions:
  - "What had you been doing before the accident?"
    - Translation: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

**Simple Future**
- Questions:
  - "What will you do tomorrow?"
    - Translation: 你明天要做什么？

**Future Continuous**
- Questions:
  - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?"
    - Translation: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

**Future Perfect**
- Questions:
  - "What will you have accomplished by next year?"
    - Translation: 到明年你会完成什么？

**Future Perfect Continuous**
- Questions:
  - "What will she have been studying by graduation?"
    - Translation: 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

**Subjunctive Mood**
- Examples:
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it."
    - Translation: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

**Passive Voice**
- Examples:
  - "What was decided at the meeting?"
    - Translation: 会议上决定了什么？

**Interrogative Sentences**
- Questions:
  - "What time is it?"
    - Translation: 现在几点了？

**Negative Sentences**
- Examples:
  - "I don't know what to say."
    - Translation: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases and Idioms

#### Common Phrases
- "What's up?"
  - Translation: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
- "What the hell!"
  - Translation: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- "what if" 
  - Translation: 如果...会怎么样
- "what for" 
  - Translation: 为了什么
- "no matter what" 
  - Translation: 无论如何
- "what's more" 
  - Translation: 而且；更重要的是
- "what about" 
  - Translation: ...怎么样
- "so what" 
  - Translation: 那又怎样

This breakdown offers a comprehensive overview of the word "what," demonstrating its versatility across different usages and contexts.
