### Word Analysis: "what"

**Phonetics**: /wɑːt/

**Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction

**Translation**: 什么

**Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

**Example**: What is your name?

**Synonyms**: which, that

**Antonyms**: None listed

---

### Usage Across Tenses

- **Simple Present**:
  - _What is your name?_ (你叫什么名字？)
  - _What do you want for dinner?_ (你晚饭想吃什么？)

- **Present Continuous**:
  - _What are you doing now?_ (你现在在做什么？)
  - _What is she reading at the moment?_ (她这会儿在读什么？)

- **Present Perfect**:
  - _What have you done?_ (你做了什么？)
  - _What has she achieved so far?_ (到目前为止她取得了什么成就？)

- **Present Perfect Continuous**:
  - _What have you been doing all day?_ (你一整天都在做什么？)
  - _What has he been working on lately?_ (他最近一直在做什么？)

- **Simple Past**:
  - _What did he say?_ (他说了什么？)
  - _What happened yesterday?_ (昨天发生了什么事？)

- **Past Continuous**:
  - _What were you doing when I called?_ (我打电话时你在做什么？)
  - _What was she thinking about?_ (她当时在想什么？)

- **Past Perfect**:
  - _What had happened before I arrived?_ (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)
  - _What had they discussed before the meeting?_ (会议前他们讨论了什么？)

- **Past Perfect Continuous**:
  - _What had you been doing before the accident?_ (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)
  - _What had they been discussing for so long?_ (他们一直在讨论什么？)

- **Simple Future**:
  - _What will you do tomorrow?_ (你明天要做什么？)
  - _What will the weather be like next week?_ (下周天气会怎样？)

- **Future Continuous**:
  - _What will you be doing this time tomorrow?_ (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)
  - _What will they be discussing at the meeting?_ (会议上他们会讨论什么？)

- **Future Perfect**:
  - _What will you have accomplished by next year?_ (到明年你会完成什么？)
  - _What will have changed by then?_ (到那时会有什么变化？)

- **Future Perfect Continuous**:
  - _What will you have been doing for ten years?_ (到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)
  - _What will she have been studying by graduation?_ (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

### Subjunctive Mood
- _If I knew what to do, I would do it._ (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
- _I wish I knew what was going on._ (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

### Passive Voice
- _What was decided at the meeting?_ (会议上决定了什么？)
- _What is being done to solve the problem?_ (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

### Interrogative Sentences
- _What time is it?_ (现在几点了？)
- _What are you talking about?_ (你在说什么？)

### Negative Sentences
- _I don't know what to say._ (我不知道该说什么。)
- _She didn't tell me what she wanted._ (她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

---

### Phrases

- **Examples**:
  - _What's up?_ (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - _What the hell!_ (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**:
  - _what if_ (如果...会怎么样)
  - _what for_ (为了什么)
  - _no matter what_ (无论如何)
  - _what's more_ (而且；更重要的是)
  - _what about_ (...怎么样)
  - _so what_ (那又怎样)

This overview encapsulates the various usages and meanings of "what," making it a versatile term in English.
