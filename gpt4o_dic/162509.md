### Context about "preaggravates"

"Preaggravates" combines the prefix "pre-" meaning "before" and "aggravates," which refers to making something worse or more severe. This term is often used to describe actions that lead to an escalation of problems before they would naturally become more severe, highlighting a proactive instead of reactive behavior.
