Here's a detailed analysis of the English word "what":

### Word Analysis: "what"
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun (pron.), Adjective (adj.), Adverb (adv.), Conjunction (conj.)
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **General Example**: "What is your name?" 
  - **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms and Antonyms
- **Synonyms**: which, that
- **Antonyms**: None listed

### Usage
#### Tenses and Sentence Forms
1. **Simple Present**:
   - "What do you want for dinner?" 
     - Translation: 你晚饭想吃什么？
  
2. **Present Continuous**:
   - "What are you doing now?" 
     - Translation: 你现在在做什么？
  
3. **Present Perfect**:
   - "What have you done?" 
     - Translation: 你做了什么？
  
4. **Present Perfect Continuous**:
   - "What have you been doing all day?" 
     - Translation: 你一整天都在做什么？

5. **Simple Past**:
   - "What did he say?" 
     - Translation: 他说了什么？
  
6. **Past Continuous**:
   - "What were you doing when I called?" 
     - Translation: 我打电话时你在做什么？

7. **Past Perfect**:
   - "What had happened before I arrived?" 
     - Translation: 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

8. **Past Perfect Continuous**:
   - "What had you been doing before the accident?" 
     - Translation: 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

9. **Simple Future**:
   - "What will you do tomorrow?" 
     - Translation: 你明天要做什么？

10. **Future Continuous**:
    - "What will you be doing this time tomorrow?" 
      - Translation: 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

11. **Future Perfect**:
    - "What will you have accomplished by next year?" 
      - Translation: 到明年你会完成什么？

12. **Future Perfect Continuous**:
    - "What will you have been doing for ten years?" 
      - Translation: 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？

13. **Subjunctive Mood**:
    - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." 
      - Translation: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

14. **Passive Voice**:
    - "What was decided at the meeting?" 
      - Translation: 会议上决定了什么？

15. **Interrogative Sentences**:
    - "What time is it?" 
      - Translation: 现在几点了？

16. **Negative Sentences**:
    - "I don't know what to say." 
      - Translation: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases
#### Common Expressions
- **What’s up?** 
  - Translation: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  
- **What the hell!** 
  - Translation: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms
- **What if**: 如果...会怎么样
- **What for**: 为了什么
- **No matter what**: 无论如何
- **What’s more**: 而且；更重要的是
- **What about**: ...怎么样
- **So what**: 那又怎样

This analysis encapsulates the multifaceted uses and meanings of "what," showcasing its versatility in English language contexts.
