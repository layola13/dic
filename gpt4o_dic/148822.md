### Contextual Understanding

"Overviewing" typically refers to the process of looking over or summarizing material. It’s used in various contexts, particularly in academic and professional settings where summarizing key points or data is essential for clarity and comprehension. Consider it synonymous with "reviewing," but it emphasizes the broad perspective rather than in-depth detail.
