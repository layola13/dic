### Additional Context
**Superprofits** often arise in situations where a company has a significant competitive advantage, such as unique technology, patent protections, or in markets where supply is limited. This concept is essential in discussions about market economics and business strategy, as it highlights discrepancies between anticipated profit margins and actual profits.
