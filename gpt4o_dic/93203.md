Here's a detailed analysis of the word "what":

### General Information
- **Word:** What
- **Phonetics:** /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech:** Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation (Chinese):** 什么
- **Definition:** Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Example
- **Sentence:** What is your name?
- **Translation:** 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Antonyms
(None provided)

### Usage by Tense

#### Simple Present
- **Questions:**
  - What do you want for dinner?  
    Translation: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous
- **Questions:**
  - What are you doing now?  
    Translation: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect
- **Questions:**
  - What have you done?  
    Translation: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past
- **Questions:**
  - What did he say?  
    Translation: 他说了什么？

### Subjunctive Mood
- **Examples:**
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
    Translation: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice
- **Examples:**
  - What was decided at the meeting?  
    Translation: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences
- **Questions:**
  - What time is it?  
    Translation: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences
- **Examples:**
  - I don't know what to say.  
    Translation: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases and Idioms

#### Common Phrases
- **What's up?**  
  Translation: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  
- **What the hell!**  
  Translation: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idiomatic Expressions
- **What if:** 如果...会怎么样
- **What for:** 为了什么
- **No matter what:** 无论如何
- **What's more:** 而且；更重要的是
- **What about:** ...怎么样
- **So what:** 那又怎样

This comprehensive overview provides insights into the usage, structure, and expressions associated with the word "what."
