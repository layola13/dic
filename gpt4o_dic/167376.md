Here's a detailed analysis of the word "what":

### General Information
- **Word**: What
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

### Examples
- **Example Sentence**: "What is your name?"
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

### Synonyms
- Which
- That

### Antonyms
- None listed

### Usage in Context

1. **Simple Present**:
   - **Question**: "What do you want for dinner?"
   - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

2. **Present Continuous**:
   - **Question**: "What are you doing now?"
   - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

3. **Present Perfect**:
   - **Question**: "What have you done?"
   - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

4. **Simple Past**:
   - **Question**: "What did he say?"
   - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

5. **Simple Future**:
   - **Question**: "What will you do tomorrow?"
   - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

6. **Interrogative Sentences**:
   - **Question**: "What time is it?"
   - **Translation**: 现在几点了？

7. **Negative Sentences**:
   - **Example**: "I don’t know what to say."
   - **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases and Idioms
- **Common Phrases**:
  - "What's up?" - 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
  - "What the hell!" - 搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms**:
  - "What if" - 如果...会怎么样
  - "What for" - 为了什么
  - "No matter what" - 无论如何
  - "What's more" - 而且；更重要的是
  - "What about" - ...怎么样
  - "So what" - 那又怎样

### Additional Usage
- **Subjunctive Mood**:
  - "If I knew what to do, I would do it." - 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。
  
- **Passive Voice**:
  - "What was decided at the meeting?" - 会议上决定了什么？

This detailed breakdown highlights the versatility and utility of "what" in various contexts of the English language.
