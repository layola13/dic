- **Word**: interavailabilities
- **Phonetics**: /ˌɪntər.əˌveɪ.ləˈbɪl.ɪ.tiz/

#### 定义
"Interavailabilities" refers to the state of mutual availability or compatibility where different entities (such as systems, schedules, or resources) can coexist or work together efficiently.

1. "The interavailabilities of different software platforms can determine how easily they integrate."
   - 这些不同软件平台的互通可用性会影响它们的集成难易度。

2. "In project planning, understanding the interavailabilities of team members ensures effective collaboration."
   - 在项目规划中，了解团队成员的互通可用性可以确保高效合作。
