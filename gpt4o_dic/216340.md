Here's a detailed analysis of the word **"what"**:

| **Attribute**          | **Details**                                      |
|-----------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Word**              | what                                             |
| **Phonetics**         | /wɑːt/                                          |
| **Part of Speech**    | Pronoun, Adjective, Adverb, Conjunction        |
| **Translation**       | 什么                                           |
| **Definition**        | Used to ask for information about someone or something. |
| **Example**           | What is your name?                              |
| **Synonyms**          | which, that                                     |
| **Antonyms**          | None                                            |

### Usage in Different Tenses

- **Simple Present**
  - Question: What is your name? (你叫什么名字？)
  - Question: What do you want for dinner? (你晚饭想吃什么？)
  
- **Present Continuous**
  - Question: What are you doing now? (你现在在做什么？)
  - Question: What is she reading at the moment? (她这会儿在读什么？)
  
- **Present Perfect**
  - Question: What have you done? (你做了什么？)
  - Question: What has she achieved so far? (到目前为止她取得了什么成就？)

- **Present Perfect Continuous**
  - Question: What have you been doing all day? (你一整天都在做什么？)
  - Question: What has he been working on lately? (他最近一直在做什么？)

- **Simple Past**
  - Question: What did he say? (他说了什么？)
  - Question: What happened yesterday? (昨天发生了什么事？)
  
- **Past Continuous**
  - Question: What were you doing when I called? (我打电话时你在做什么？)
  - Question: What was she thinking about? (她当时在想什么？)

- **Past Perfect**
  - Question: What had happened before I arrived? (在我到达之前发生了什么事？)
  - Question: What had they discussed before the meeting? (会议前他们讨论了什么？)

- **Past Perfect Continuous**
  - Question: What had you been doing before the accident? (事故发生前你一直在做什么？)
  - Question: What had they been discussing for so long? (他们一直在讨论什么？)

- **Simple Future**
  - Question: What will you do tomorrow? (你明天要做什么？)
  - Question: What will the weather be like next week? (下周天气会怎样？)

- **Future Continuous**
  - Question: What will you be doing this time tomorrow? (明天这个时候你会在做什么？)
  - Question: What will they be discussing at the meeting? (会议上他们会讨论什么？)

- **Future Perfect**
  - Question: What will you have accomplished by next year? (到明年你会完成什么？)
  - Question: What will have changed by then? (到那时会有什么变化？)

- **Future Perfect Continuous**
  - Question: What will you have been doing for ten years? (到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？)
  - Question: What will she have been studying by graduation? (到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？)

### Subjunctive Mood
- Example: If I knew what to do, I would do it. (如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。)
- Example: I wish I knew what was going on. (我希望我知道发生了什么。)

### Passive Voice
- Example: What was decided at the meeting? (会议上决定了什么？)
- Example: What is being done to solve the problem? (正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？)

### Interrogative Sentences
- Question: What time is it? (现在几点了？)
- Question: What are you talking about? (你在说什么？)

### Negative Sentences
- Example: I don't know what to say. (我不知道该说什么。)
- Example: She didn't tell me what she wanted. (她没有告诉我她想要什么。)

### Common Phrases and Idioms
- **Examples**
  - Phrase: What's up? (怎么了？/ 你好吗？)
  - Phrase: What the hell! (搞什么鬼！)

- **Idioms**
  - Phrase: what if (如果...会怎么样)
  - Phrase: what for (为了什么)
  - Phrase: no matter what (无论如何)
  - Phrase: what's more (而且；更重要的是)
  - Phrase: what about (...怎么样)
  - Phrase: so what (那又怎样)

This comprehensive overview of "what" should provide you with a clear understanding of its uses and nuances in English.
