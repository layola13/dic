### Detailed Analysis of the Word "Restages"

**Word:** restages  
**Phonetics:** /ˌriːˈsteɪdʒɪz/  
**Part of Speech:** verb (present tense, third person singular)  
**Translation:** 重新上演  
**Definition:** The act of staging something again or re-enacting a performance or production.  

**Example:** The theater company restages the classic play every summer.

**Synonyms:**  
- revives  
- re-enacts  
- reintroduces  

**Antonyms:**  
- cancels  
- halts  

### Usage

**Simple Present:**  
- **Example:** The company restages popular shows regularly.  
  **Translation:** 该公司定期重新上演受欢迎的剧目。

**Present Continuous:**  
- **Example:** They are restaging the performance due to popular demand.  
  **Translation:** 由于需求旺盛，他们正在重新上演这场表演。

**Simple Past:**  
- **Example:** Last year, the group restaged an old favorite.  
  **Translation:** 去年，该团体重新上演了一部老经典。

**Future Simple:**  
- **Example:** Next month, they will restage the musical.  
  **Translation:** 下个月，他们将重新上演这部音乐剧。

### Contextual Usage

- **In Theater:** Often used when a production is revived for new audiences or for anniversaries of the original performance.
- **In Arts:** Can refer to art exhibitions or performances being set up again in a different context.

### Example Phrases

- **"The director decided to restage the successful show in a larger venue."**  
  **Translation:** 导演决定在一个更大的场馆重新上演这场成功的演出。

- **"After ten years, the festival finally restages the beloved classic."**  
  **Translation:** 十年后，节日终于重新上演了这部备受喜爱的经典。

This comprehensive breakdown provides a clear understanding of the term "restages" and its correct usage in different contexts.
