### 英文单词解析 - "what"

- **Word**: what
- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to ask for information about someone or something.

#### Examples:
- **Sentence**: What is your name?  
  **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms:
- which
- that

#### Antonyms:
- (无)

### Usage in Different Tenses

- **Simple Present**:
  - What do you want for dinner?  
    你晚饭想吃什么？

- **Present Continuous**:
  - What are you doing now?  
    你现在在做什么？

- **Present Perfect**:
  - What have you done?  
    你做了什么？

- **Simple Past**:
  - What did he say?  
    他说了什么？

- **Future Simple**:
  - What will you do tomorrow?  
    你明天要做什么？

### Mood and Voice

- **Subjunctive Mood**:
  - If I knew what to do, I would do it.  
    如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

- **Passive Voice**:
  - What was decided at the meeting?  
    会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative and Negative Sentences

- **Interrogative Sentences**:
  - What time is it?  
    现在几点了？

- **Negative Sentences**:
  - I don't know what to say.  
    我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases and Idioms

- **Common Phrases**:
  - What's up?  
    怎么了？
  - What the hell!  
    搞什么鬼！

- **Idioms**:
  - what if - 如果...会怎么样
  - what for - 为了什么
  - no matter what - 无论如何
  - what's more - 而且；更重要的是
  - what about - ...怎么样
  - so what - 那又怎样

This comprehensive analysis provides various contexts and uses of the word "what," illustrating its versatility in English.
