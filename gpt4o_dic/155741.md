### Word Analysis: "what"

- **Phonetics**: /wɑːt/
- **Part of Speech**: pronoun, adjective, adverb, conjunction
- **Translation**: 什么
- **Definition**: Used to request information regarding someone or something.

#### Example Sentence:
- **English**: What is your name?
- **Translation**: 你叫什么名字？

#### Synonyms:
- which
- that

#### Antonyms:
- (none applicable)

### Usage Examples

#### Simple Present:
- **Question**: What do you want for dinner?
  - **Translation**: 你晚饭想吃什么？

#### Present Continuous:
- **Question**: What are you doing now?
  - **Translation**: 你现在在做什么？

#### Present Perfect:
- **Question**: What have you done?
  - **Translation**: 你做了什么？

#### Simple Past:
- **Question**: What did he say?
  - **Translation**: 他说了什么？

#### Future Simple:
- **Question**: What will you do tomorrow?
  - **Translation**: 你明天要做什么？

### Subjunctive Mood:
- **Example**: If I knew what to do, I would do it.
  - **Translation**: 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。

### Passive Voice:
- **Example**: What was decided at the meeting?
  - **Translation**: 会议上决定了什么？

### Interrogative Sentences:
- **Question**: What time is it?
  - **Translation**: 现在几点了？

### Negative Sentences:
- **Example**: I don't know what to say.
  - **Translation**: 我不知道该说什么。

### Phrases

#### Common Expressions:
- **Phrase**: What's up?
  - **Translation**: 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

- **Phrase**: What the hell!
  - **Translation**: 搞什么鬼！

#### Idioms:
- **What if**: 如果...会怎么样
- **What for**: 为了什么
- **No matter what**: 无论如何
- **What's more**: 而且；更重要的是
- **What about**: ...怎么样
- **So what**: 那又怎样

This comprehensive breakdown illustrates the versatility and multiple uses of "what" in English, providing context through examples and idiomatic phrases.
