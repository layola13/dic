### Context and Usage
The idiom "live high on the hog" originates from American English and reflects a lifestyle characterized by comfort and indulgence. It suggests enjoying the finer things in life—often seen as a reward after hard work or success.

This phrase can be used in various contexts, often related to wealth, promotions, or fortunate circumstances.
