### Additional Context

"Odds and ends" is commonly used in informal contexts to refer to small, miscellaneous items that don't fit into a particular category but still require attention or organization. It's a useful expression in everyday conversations when discussing tasks or belongings that may not seem significant individually but collectively may require some organization or resolution.
