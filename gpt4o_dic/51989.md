### 英文单词 "diaglyph" 详细解析

#### 1. 单词信息
- **单词**: diaglyph
- **音标**: /ˈdaɪəɡlɪf/
- **词性**: 名词 (Noun)
- **翻译**: 凹雕, 阴刻
- **定义**: A type of engraving or carving where the design is cut into the surface, creating a sunken or indented image. This is the opposite of "relief" carving, where the design is raised from the surface.

#### 2. 例句
- **例句**: The ancient Egyptians often used diaglyphs in their stone carvings to create intricate designs.
  - **翻译**: 古埃及人经常在石雕中使用凹雕技术来创作复杂的设计。

#### 3. 同义词
- **同义词**: intaglio, sunken carving
  - **intaglio**: 凹版雕刻，通常用于指在宝石或金属上雕刻的凹雕艺术。
  - **sunken carving**: 凹陷雕刻，与diaglyph意义相近。
