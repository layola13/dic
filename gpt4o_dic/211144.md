### Additional Context

**Subsyndicates** are often used in financial contexts, particularly in investment banking or real estate projects, where a main syndicate (a group of investors or lenders) may delegate specific tasks or areas of investment to smaller groups, known as subsyndicates. This structure helps in managing risks and pooling expertise from different sectors.
